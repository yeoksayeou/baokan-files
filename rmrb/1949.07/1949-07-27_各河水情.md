Title: River Water Conditions
Author:
Date: 1949-07-27
Page: 2

Due to continuous overcast and rainy weather since the 24th, rivers in eastern and central Hebei are generally rising; the water levels in rivers in southern Hebei are stable. A summary is as follows:

　　Bei Canal: The water level is trending upward. At 6:00 AM on the 26th, the Tongxian water level was 20.80 meters.

　　Luan River: The water level is trending upward. At 3:00 PM on the 25th, the Luanxian water level was 24.13 meters.

　　Chaobai River: The water level is slightly rising. At 6:00 AM on the 26th, the Suzhou village water level was 26.23 meters.

　　Hutuo River: The river water rose sharply. At 3:00 AM on the 25th, the Huangbizhuang water level reached 100.79 meters, only about two decimeters away from the highest flood level in history. The water level has now receded. At 8:00 AM on the 26th, the water level was 100.00 meters.

　　Zhuolong River: The water level is slightly rising. At 6:00 AM on the 23rd at Huagang, the water level was 21.82 meters.

　　Yongding River: The water level is stable. At 7:00 AM on the 26th, the Sanjiadian water level was 103.06 meters.

　　South Canal: The water level is trending downward. At 6:00 PM on the 23rd, the Linqing water level was 28.80 meters.

　　Fuyang River: The water level is stable. At 7:00 PM on the 23rd at Xingmuzhai, the water level was 29.90 meters.

　　Zhang River: The water level is stable. At 6:00 PM on the 23rd at Mashenmiao, the water level was 48.63 meters.

### 各河水情

1949-07-27
第2版()
专栏：

　　各河水情
    冀东、冀中地区因二十四日以来连日阴雨，各河普涨；冀南各河水势平稳，兹概述如下：
    北运河——水势趋涨，二十六日六时通县水位２０．８０公尺。
    滦河——水势趋涨，二十五日十五时，滦县水位２４．１３。
    潮白河——水势微涨，二十六日六时苏庄水位２６．２３。
    滹沱河——河水猛涨，二十五日三时黄壁庄，水位曾达１００．７９，距离历年最高洪水位仅二公寸许，现水势已回落，廿六日八时水位１００．００。
    潴龙河——水势微涨，滑岗二十三日六时水位２１．８２。
    永定河——水势平稳，二十六日七时三家店水位１０３．０６。
    南运河——水势趋落，二十三日十八时临清水位２８．８０。
    滏阳河——水势平稳，邢穆宅二十三日十九时水位２９．９０。
    漳河——水势平稳，马神庙二十三日十八时水位４８．６３。
