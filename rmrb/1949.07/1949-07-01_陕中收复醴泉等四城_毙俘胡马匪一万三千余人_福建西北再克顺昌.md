Title: Shaanxi Central Recovers Liquan and Four Other Cities; Over 13,000 Enemy Troops Killed or Captured; Northwest Fujian Recaptures Shunchang
Author:
Date: 1949-07-01
Page: 3

Xinhua News Agency, Xi'an, 30th: The People's Liberation Army's First Field Army recovered the four county towns of Liquan, Qianxian, Xingping, and [Unclear Characters] west of Xi'an and north and south of the Wei River on the 27th and 28th. The defending enemy fled westward. These county towns were previously liberated in mid-May by the PLA forces advancing into the Jinghe River Valley and north and south of the Wei River. At the beginning of this month, the Hu-Ma bandit troops gathered the strength of four army corps to counterattack, attempting to retake Xi'an, and the aforementioned county towns were occupied. After fierce counterattacks by the PLA, more than 13,000 enemy troops were killed, wounded, or captured.

Xinhua News Agency, Northern Fujian Front, 30th: A certain unit of the People's Liberation Army advancing into Fujian re-captured Shunchang County town on the 23rd. The defending enemy, the puppet police brigade, consisting of more than 200 men, fled westward towards JiangLe. We ambushed and annihilated one of their companies en route, and wiped out the puppet township local armed forces, capturing a total of 138 enemy soldiers, and seizing 52 rifles and carbines, and 2 light machine guns. Shunchang is located southwest of Jian'ou, and was briefly liberated on May 20th.

### 陕中收复醴泉等四城　毙俘胡马匪一万三千余人  福建西北再克顺昌

1949-07-01
第3版()
专栏：

　　陕中收复醴泉等四城
  　毙俘胡马匪一万三千余人
    福建西北再克顺昌
    【新华社西安三十日电】人民解放军第一野战军二十七、二十八两日收复西安以西、渭河南北的醴泉、乾县、兴平、＠①＠②四个县城，守敌西逃。按上述县城曾于五月中为进军泾渭河谷及渭河南北的解放军所解放。本月初，胡马匪军纠集四个兵团的兵力反扑，企图重占西安，上述县城被侵占。经解放军猛烈反击，毙伤俘敌一万三千余名。
    【新华社闽北前线三十日电】挺进福建的人民解放军某部，二十三日再度攻占顺昌县城，守敌伪保警大队二百余西向将乐逃窜。我在途中围歼其一连，并歼灭伪乡镇地方武装，共俘敌一百三十八名，缴长短枪五十二枝，轻机枪两挺。顺昌在建瓯西南，五月二十日曾一度解放。
