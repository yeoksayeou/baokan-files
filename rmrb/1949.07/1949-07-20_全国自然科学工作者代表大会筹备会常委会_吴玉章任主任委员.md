Title: Preparatory Committee Standing Committee of the National Natural Science Workers Representative Congress: Wu Yuzhang Appointed as Chairman

Author:
Date: 1949-07-20
Page: 2

    Preparatory Committee Standing Committee of the National Natural Science Workers Representative Congress
    Wu Yuzhang Appointed as Chairman
    [Ping City News] The Standing Committee of the Preparatory Committee of the First National Natural Science Workers Representative Congress of China met yesterday (the 19th) and elected Wu Yuzhang as Chairman, Liang Xi, He Cheng, Zeng Zhaolun and five others as Vice-Chairmen, and Yan Jici as Secretary-General. According to the actual needs of the preparatory work, the meeting decided to establish a planning committee and organizational, publicity, general affairs and other working bodies, and designated special personnel to be responsible for promoting the generation of representatives in various regions and the establishment of branches. The Standing Committee has completed the sorting out of the Preparatory Committee's articles of association.

### 全国自然科学工作者代表大会筹备会常委会  吴玉章任主任委员

1949-07-20
第2版()
专栏：

    全国自然科学工作者代表大会筹备会常委会
    吴玉章任主任委员
    【平市讯】中华全国第一次自然科学工作者代表大会筹备会常务委员会昨（十九）日集会，推选吴玉章为主任委员，梁希、贺诚、曾昭抡等五人为副主任委员，严济慈为秘书长。会议根据筹备工作的实际需要决定设立计划委员会及组织、宣传、总务等工作机构，并确定专人负责推动各地区代表产生及筹组分会事宜。常委会已完成筹备会简章的整理工作。
