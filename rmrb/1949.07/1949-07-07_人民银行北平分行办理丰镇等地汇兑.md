Title: People's Bank of Beiping Branch Handles Remittances to Fengzhen and Other Places
Author: Yu
Date: 1949-07-07
Page: 2

　　People's Bank of Beiping Branch Handles Remittances to Fengzhen and Other Places
　　[Our Newspaper] Remittances between Beiping and Fengzhen, Jining, Yuxian, Yanggao on the Ping-Sui Railway line, and Wangjiajing in Jizhong, will be open from the 7th. The Beiping Branch of the People's Bank of China's Business Department will handle remittances to Fengzhen and Jining, and the South City Office of the bank will handle remittances to Yuxian, Yanggao, and Wangjiajing. The exchange rate is 6 per mille for Fengzhen and Jining; 8 per mille for Yuxian and 5 per mille for Wangjiajing. (Yu)

### 人民银行北平分行办理丰镇等地汇兑
宇
1949-07-07
第2版()
专栏：经济新闻

　　人民银行北平分行办理丰镇等地汇兑
    【本报讯】北平与平绥线之丰镇、集宁、蔚县、阳高及冀中之王家井五地之汇兑于七日起开放。人民银行北平分行营业部承作丰镇、集宁两地者，该行南城办事处承作蔚县、阳高、王家井三地者，汇率除蔚县千分之八、王家井千分之五外余均千分之六。　（宇）
