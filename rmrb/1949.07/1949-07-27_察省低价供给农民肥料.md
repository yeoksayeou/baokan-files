Title: Chahar Province Provides Low-Priced Fertilizer to Farmers
Author:
Date: 1949-07-27
Page: 2

(Chahar Provincial People's Government, Agricultural Extension Department)

Chahar Province, in order to help the masses solve fertilizer difficulties and increase production, has specially sold more than 160,000 jin of sodium nitrate and fertilizer powder from enemy and puppet materials at low prices to the masses in Huailai Xin'anbao, Xuanhua Shalingzi, and Zhuolu, for application in paddy fields and vegetable gardens. It is estimated that the area that can be fertilized is more than 10,000 mu, and the autumn harvest can increase the production of fine grains by more than 500,000 jin.

### 察省低价供给农民肥料

1949-07-27
第2版()
专栏：

　　察省低价供给农民肥料
    【察哈尔讯】察省为帮助群众解决肥料困难，提高产量，特将接收敌伪物资中之硝酸钠、肥田粉十六万余斤低价推销给怀来新保安、宣化沙岭子及涿鹿等地群众，施用于稻田及蔬菜园中。预计可施面积万余亩，秋后能增产细粮五十余万斤。　　　　　　　　　　　　（察哈尔省人民政府农业处推广科）
