Title: Youth Delegation Leaves Harbin for Abroad
Author:
Date: 1949-07-29
Page: 2

Xinhua News Agency, Harbin, 28th. The Chinese Democratic Youth Delegation, which is attending the Second World Democratic Youth Congress and the International Youth Day, left Harbin by train on the 27th after a three-day stay. During their stay in Harbin, 150,000 young people in Harbin held a gathering, and the Shenyang and Harbin basketball teams played friendly matches with the delegation's basketball team.

### 青年代表团　离哈出国

1949-07-29
第2版()
专栏：

　　青年代表团
  　离哈出国
    【新华社哈尔滨二十八日电】出席世界民主青年第二次代表大会和国际青年节的中国民主青年代表团在哈尔滨停留三天后，二十七日乘车离哈出国。该团留哈期间，哈尔滨青年十五万人曾举行集会，沈、哈篮球队并曾与该团篮球队作友谊比赛。
