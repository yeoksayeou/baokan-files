Title: Viet Minh Army's Half-Year Achievements: 76,000 French Troops Eliminated, Over 100 French Strongholds Captured and Destroyed
Author:
Date: 1949-07-22
Page: 3

　　Viet Minh Army's Half-Year Achievements
    76,000 French Troops Eliminated
    Over 100 French Strongholds Captured and Destroyed
    [Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, 21st] TASS, Sydney, 19th: Reports received here concerning the Viet Minh Army's achievements in the first half of this year state that, from January to June this year, the Viet Minh Army killed or wounded over 76,000 French troops and captured 103 French strongholds. During the same period, the Viet Minh Army and guerrillas destroyed 37 French strongholds.

### 越盟军半年战果　歼法军七万六千　攻克摧毁法军据点百余处

1949-07-22
第3版()
专栏：

　　越盟军半年战果
    歼法军七万六千
    攻克摧毁法军据点百余处
    【新华社北平二十一日电】塔斯社悉尼十九日讯：此间收到的报道今年上半年越盟军战果的消息称，在今年一月至六月的时期内，越盟军毙伤法军七万六千余名，攻克法军据点一百零三处。同一期间，越盟军与游击队摧毁了法军据点三十七处。
