Title: Telegram of Congratulations from the International Organization of Journalists
Author:
Date: 1949-07-14
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Prague, 13th -- The Secretariat of the International Organization of Journalists sent a telegram congratulating the plenary meeting of the preparatory committee of the All-China Association of Journalists, held today in Beiping. The telegram reads as follows:

Preparatory Committee of the All-China Association of Journalists, Beiping:

The Secretariat of the International Organization of Journalists extends brotherly congratulations to the representatives of Chinese journalists who are holding a meeting to establish a new and progressive association of journalists and wishes you success. The International Organization of Journalists represents honest and peace-loving journalists from twenty-two countries, and we hope that your association will soon become a member of this international organization.

Secretary-General of the International Organization of Journalists
Hronek

Executive Secretary Dr. Kransky (signed)

### 国际记者协会贺电

1949-07-14
第1版()
专栏：

　　国际记者协会贺电
    【新华社布拉格十三日电】国际记者协会秘书处电贺今日于北平举行的中华全国新闻工作者协会筹备会的全体会议。电文如下：
    北平中华全国新闻工作者协会筹备委员会：
    国际记者协会秘书处谨向举行会议以建立新的进步的新闻工作者协会的中国新闻工作者的代表们致兄弟的贺忱，并祝你们成功。国际记者协会代表二十二国正直的与爱好和平的新闻工作者，我们希望你们的协会不久便可以成为这一国际组织的成员。
    国际记者协会秘书长
　　赫罗涅克
　　执行秘书　克兰斯基博士（签字）
