Title: Four Hundred Kinds of Soviet Literature and Art Books to be Published This Year
Author:
Date: 1949-07-17
Page: 3

Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, 16th: Moscow dispatch: The Soviet State Literature and Art Book Publishing Bureau has approved a new publishing plan for 1950. The plan stipulates that approximately four hundred different novels will be published this year, with a total printing of 37.1 million copies. This quantity is fifteen times that of the pre-war year of 1940. The Literature and Art Department of the bureau will publish a thirty-five volume collection of works by the great proletarian writer Gorky and many new works by Soviet writers next year. Works by writers from various allied republics and people's democracies will also be published in large quantities. In addition, the publication of collected works by Dreiser, Balzac, and Romain Rolland will begin. The State Literature and Art Book Publishing Bureau is continuing to expand its scale of work. The literature and art books published by the bureau during the post-war five-year plan will reach approximately 108 million copies by the end of 1950.

### 苏联文艺书籍今年将出版四百种

1949-07-17
第3版()
专栏：

    苏联文艺书籍
    今年将出版四百种
    【新华社北平十六日电】莫斯科讯：苏联国家文艺书籍出版局已批准了一九五零年新的出版计划。计划规定今年共出版约四百种不同的小说创作，共刊印三千七百一十万册。这个数量相当于战前一九四零年的十五倍。该局文艺部明年将出版伟大无产阶级作家高尔基的三十五卷文集和苏维埃作家的许多新作品。各加盟共和国与人民民主国家作家的著作，也将大量出版。此外，并将开始出版德莱塞、巴尔扎克和罗曼罗兰的文集。国家文艺书籍出版局正在继续扩大工作规模，该局在战后五年计划期间所出版的文艺书籍，到一九五零年底将达一亿另八百万册左右。
