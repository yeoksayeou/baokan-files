Title: Commemorating the Great "August 1st" Army Day: People's Liberation Army in Various Regions Actively Preparing
Author:
Date: 1949-07-28
Page: 1
 
　　Commemorating the Great "August 1st" Army Day
　　The People's Liberation Army in Various Regions Actively Preparing
    [Xinhua Broadcasting Station, Beiping Broadcast] Beiping, 27th—News: The birthday of the Chinese People's Liberation Army, "August 1st," is approaching. The People's Liberation Army in various regions is preparing to commemorate this great festival. The headquarters of the Second Field Army of the People's Liberation Army recently issued a circular to all its subordinate units to hold flag dedication ceremonies and army-people get-togethers on "August 1st" to publicize the history of the Chinese Communist Party's arduous struggle for the liberation of the Chinese people, the glorious traditions of the Chinese People's Liberation Army, and the great victory of the Liberation War. The Political Department of the East China Military Region and the Third Field Army of the People's Liberation Army issued a circular to its subordinate units, stating that in commemorating "August 1st," they should convene meetings of combat heroes and combat models; they should educate the troops to understand the history of struggle, glorious traditions, and characteristics of the Chinese revolution of the People's Liberation Army. The Political Department of the North China Military Region of the Chinese People's Liberation Army decided to hold the "Exhibition of Historical Relics of the Chinese People's War of Resistance Against Japan and the War of Liberation" in Beiping from August 1st to 10th. In its instructions for commemorating "August 1st," the Northeast Administrative Committee requested governments at all levels to earnestly check and improve the work of giving preferential treatment to the families of martyrs and revolutionary soldiers; all agencies and organizations in various regions should offer congratulations to the People's Liberation Army on "August 1st."

### 纪念伟大“八一”建军节　各地人民解放军正积极筹备

1949-07-28
第1版()
专栏：

　　纪念伟大“八一”建军节
  　各地人民解放军正积极筹备
    【北平新华广播电台广播】北平二十七号消息：中国人民解放军的生日“八一”快要到来，各地人民解放军，正在筹备纪念这一个伟大的节日。人民解放军第二野战军司令部，最近通令所属各部队，在“八一”举行爱旗典礼和军民联欢大会，宣传中国共产党为中国人民解放而艰苦奋斗的历史，中国人民解放军的光荣传统，以及解放战争的伟大胜利。人民解放军华东军区和第三野战军政治部，通令所属各单位，在纪念“八一”中，要召集战斗英雄、战斗模范大会；要教育部队了解人民解放军的奋斗历史、光荣传统和中国革命的特点。中国人民解放军华北军区政治部，决定从八月一号到十号，在北平举行“中国人民抗日解放战争史迹影展”。东北行政委员会，在纪念“八一”的指示中，要求各级政府，认真检查做好优待烈士和革命军人家属的工作；各地机关团体在“八一”这一天，都应该向解放军庆贺。
