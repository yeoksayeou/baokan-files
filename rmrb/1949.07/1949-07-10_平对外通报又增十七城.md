Title: Beijing Announces Opening of Telegraph Service to Seventeen More Cities

Author:

Date: 1949-07-10
Page: 2

Beijing Telegraph Bureau

　　【Beijing News】The Beijing Telegraph Bureau announced on the 9th that from late June to the 8th of this month, telegraph services to seventeen more cities have been opened to the outside world. These seventeen cities are: Wuxue, Xiaogan, and Echeng in Hubei Province; Jianyang, Jian'ou, Shaxian, Yangkou, Shunchang, and Xiayang in Fujian Province; Weixian, Tai'an, Ziyang, Jining, Chengyang, and Yantai in Shandong Province; Yanggao in Shanxi Province; and Jinxi in Jiangxi Province, and all will accept commercial telegrams.
　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　(Beijing Telegraph Bureau)

### 平对外通报又增十七城

1949-07-10
第2版()
专栏：

　　平对外通报又增十七城
    【平市讯】北平电信局九日发表：自六月下旬至本月八日，北平对外又有十七个城市的电报开放了。这十七个城市是：湖北省的武穴、孝感、鄂城，福建省的建阳、建瓯、沙县、洋口、顺昌、峡阳，山东省的潍县、泰安、滋阳、济宁、城阳、烟台，山西省的阳高及江西省的金溪，并且都收拍商报。
    　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　（北平电讯局）
