Title: Shanghai Rice, Flour, and Cooking Oil Prices Generally Fall
Author: 
Date: 1949-07-21
Page: 1

　　Shanghai Rice, Flour and Cooking Oil
    Prices Generally Fall
    [Broadcast from the Beiping Xinhua Broadcasting Station] Shanghai, 20th – Rice prices in Shanghai have fallen. Since last week, Shanghai's market experienced fluctuations in grain prices caused by unscrupulous merchants driving up prices. Following the large-scale sales by the Shanghai Grain Trading Company, rice prices have fallen in the last two days from 60,000 yuan at the peak to 48,000 yuan. Other products such as flour and cooking oil have also fallen by 1,000 to 3,000 yuan.

### 上海米面食油  普遍跌价

1949-07-21
第1版()
专栏：

　　上海米面食油
    普遍跌价
    【北平新华广播电台广播】上海二十日消息：上海米价下跌。上海市面从上星期以来，由于奸商哄抬引起的粮价波动，经过上海粮食贸易公司的大量抛售以后，两天以来，米价已经从前几天高涨时的六万元，下跌到四万八千元。其他面粉、食油等，也都下跌了一千元到三千元。
