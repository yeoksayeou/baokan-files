Title: Youth Performing Arts Troupe to Leave Today for World Youth Conference and Festival; Literary and Art Congress Holds Farewell Banquet
Author: Bo Sheng
Date: 1949-07-22
Page: 1
Youth League Central Committee

　　Sends off Youth Delegation to Foreign Countries
　　Vice Chairman Zhou and Others Attend
　　[Ping City News] The Central Committee of the China New Democratic Youth League held a farewell party at No. 2 Yuhe Bridge yesterday (21st) at 5:00 PM to see off the Chinese Democratic Youth Delegation attending the Second World Democratic Youth Congress and the International Youth Festival. Guests included Vice Chairman Zhou, Li Lisan of the All-China Federation of Trade Unions, Bo Yibo of the North China Bureau, Zhang Youyu, Deputy Mayor of Beiping, Xu Bing, Deputy Minister of the United Front Work Department of the CPC Central Committee, Xiao Ming of the Preparatory Committee of the Municipal Federation of Trade Unions, Zhang Xiuyan of the Municipal Women's Committee, Zhang Xiaomei, Xu Liqun of the Municipal Youth Committee, Yang Bozhen and other comrades. All representatives entered the venue in neat uniforms and wearing embroidered badges. Feng Wenbin, Secretary of the Central Committee of the Youth League, delivered a speech, encouraging everyone to promote the struggle of the Chinese people abroad, learn from the experiences of the Soviet Union and other new democratic countries in Eastern Europe, establish contacts, unite the broad masses of democratic youth around the world, and strive for lasting world peace and people's democracy. He emphasized that the delegation should accept the instructions given by Vice Chairman Zhou and Lu Dingyi, Minister of the Propaganda Department of the CPC Central Committee, a few days ago, and be modest, prudent, and avoid arrogance and impetuosity. Liao Chengzhi, head of the delegation, pointed out that the delegation must consciously feel the great responsibility, and therefore must consciously strengthen the discipline and collectivity of action. Mr. Wu Han pointed out that this trip abroad by the delegation represents the new democratic China led by the Communist Party, and will be warmly welcomed by democratic youth all over the world. Therefore, every member of the delegation should pay attention to their personal words and actions. Qian Sanqiang, based on his own experience attending the World Congress for the Defense of Peace, pointed out that without the leadership of the Communist Party, the Chinese people and Chinese youth would not have today's glory. Xie Bangding of the All-China Student Federation and Xiao Hua, deputy head of the delegation, also spoke. After the meeting, there was a dinner and recreational programs. REC>
　　Attending World Youth Conference and Youth Festival
　　Youth Performing Arts Troupe to Leave Today
　　Literary and Art Congress Holds Farewell Banquet
　　[Reported by our newspaper] Guo Moruo, Mao Dun, Zhou Yang, chairman and vice-chairmen of the National Literary and Art Congress, and heads of various delegations, on behalf of all representatives of the Literary and Art Congress, hosted a banquet at Xinlu Chun Restaurant at 12:00 noon yesterday (21st) to entertain the Chinese Youth Performing Arts Troupe, which will set out for Hungary today to participate in the Second World Youth Festival. Over 60 people attended, including Liao Chengzhi, Chen Jiakang, and Li Bozhao. At the banquet, Mao Dun, on behalf of the Literary and Art Congress, wished the departing Youth Performing Arts Troupe a victorious success. He hoped that they would not only represent the youth of China, but also represent the victorious departure of China's literary and art army, bringing the spirit of the Literary and Art Congress abroad, bringing China's good works to other countries for observation, and learning more things from other countries to bring back. He said that after they return to China, a larger grand meeting will be held to welcome them.
　　Subsequently, Li Bozhao, head of the departing performing arts troupe, delivered a reply. She said that this trip abroad will certainly be based on the principles and spirit of the Literary and Art Congress, and strive to express it through actions, and will never fail to live up to the care of all the representatives of the Literary and Art Congress. During the banquet, everyone raised their glasses to celebrate, filled with youthful joy.
　　　　　　　
　　(Bo Sheng)

### 出席世界青年大会及青年节　青年文工团今出国  文代大会设宴欢送
柏生
1949-07-22
第1版()
专栏：

　　青年团中委会
  　欢送出国青年代表团
  　周副主席等均往参加
    【平市讯】中国新民主主义青年团中央委员会为欢送出席世界民主青年第二次代表大会及国际青年节的中国民主青年代表团，昨（二十一）日下午五时在御河桥二号举行欢送会。来宾有周副主席、全国总工会李立三、华北局薄一波、北平市副市长张友渔、中共中央统战部副部长徐冰、市总工会筹委会萧明、市妇委张秀岩，张晓梅、市青委许立群、杨伯箴等同志。全体代表身着整齐服装，佩带绣花胸章，整队入场。青年团中央委员会书记冯文彬同志首先致词，勉励大家到国外要宣传中国人民的奋斗事迹，学习苏联与东欧各新民主主义国家的经验，建立联系，团结全世界广大民主青年，为争取世界持久和平与人民民主而努力，并着重指出代表团要接受日前周副主席与中共中央宣传部长陆定一同志所给予的指示，谦虚谨慎、戒骄戒躁。代表团团长廖承志同志指出代表团要自觉地感到责任重大，因此必须自觉地加强行动的纪律性与集体性。吴晗先生指出代表团这次出国，是代表以共产党为领导的新民主主义的中国，必会被全世界民主青年所热烈欢迎，因此，代表团每个团员要注意个人的言语行动。钱三强先生以他本人出席世界拥护和平大会的经验，指出没有共产党的领导，中国人民及中国青年就不会有今天的光荣。全国学联谢邦定、代表团副团长萧华同志也都讲了话。会后聚餐，并有游艺节目。REC>
　　出席世界青年大会及青年节
  　青年文工团今出国
    文代大会设宴欢送
    【本报讯】全国文代大会正副主席郭沫若、茅盾、周扬及各代表团团长，代表文代大会全体代表于昨（二十一）日上午十二时，假新陆春饭店设宴招待于今天首途出国赴匈牙利参加世界第二次青年节的中国青年文工团。出席廖承志、陈家康、李伯钊等六十余人。席上，茅盾代表文代会预祝出国青年文工团的胜利成功。希望他们不但是代表中国的青年，同时代表中国的文艺军胜利出国，把文代大会的精神带到国外去，把中国好的作品带去与他国观摩。并多学习各国的东西带回来。等回国后将要开更大的一次盛会欢迎他们。
    随后出国文工团团长李伯钊代表致答词。她说此次出国一定本着文代大会的方针和精神，努力以行动表现出来，决不辜负文代大会全体代表们的关怀。席间大家举杯相庆，充满了年青的欢愉。　　　　　　　
　　（柏生）
