Title: Preparatory Committee Members for the First National Science Conference of China Arrive in Beiping One After Another
Author:
Date: 1949-07-03
Page: 2

[Beiping City News] The Preparatory Committee for the First National Science Conference of China will hold its first plenary session of the preparatory committee members in Beiping on the 11th of this month. Preparatory committee members from various places have arrived in Beiping one after another. They include Yin Zanxun, Ren Meie, Zhou Lisan, Yao Keli, Feng Zefang, Yang Zhongjian, Liu Yinong, and Yu Wu from Nanjing; Wang Ganchang, Wang [Unidentified character], Bei Shizhang, Cai Banghua, and Su Buqing from Hangzhou; Xu Weiying, Gao Huimin, and Yang Zhou from Shijiazhuang; and Gao Shangyin, Zeng Zhaoan, and Zeng Shengzhi from Wuchang.

### 中华全国第一次科学会议　筹备委员陆续抵平

1949-07-03
第2版()
专栏：

　　中华全国第一次科学会议
  　筹备委员陆续抵平
    【平市讯】中华全国第一次科学会议筹备委员会将于本月十一日在北平开第一次全体筹备委员会议，各地筹备委员已陆续抵平。计有从南京来之尹赞勋、任美锷、周立三、姚克力、冯泽芳、杨锺健、刘伊农、虞鋈，从杭州来之王淦昌、王＠①、贝时璋、蔡邦华、苏步青，从石家庄来之徐纬英、高惠民、杨舟，从武昌来之高尚荫，曾昭安、曾省之等人。
    字库未存字注释：
    　＠①原字王加进
