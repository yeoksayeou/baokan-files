Title: Literary and Art Congress Sends Condolences Upon Hearing of Dimitrov's Death
Author:
Date: 1949-07-05
Page: 1

　　Literary and Art Congress Sends Condolences Upon Hearing of Dimitrov's Death
[Ping City News] On the third day of the National Literary and Art Congress, during the proceedings, all the representatives were informed of the sad news of the death of Dimitrov, the outstanding leader of the international labor movement and General Secretary of the Bulgarian Communist Party. All stood up in mourning amidst the strains of mourning music. It was unanimously decided to send a telegram of condolence in the name of the Congress. The original text of the telegram is as follows: To the Council of Ministers of the People's Republic of Bulgaria:
　　We are deeply saddened to learn of the death of Comrade Dimitrov, the outstanding leader of the international communist movement and the great patriot and internationalist. We express our deepest condolences in this telegram.
　　National Congress of Literary and Art Workers of China
　　July 4, 1949

### 惊闻季米特洛夫逝世文代大会特致电吊唁
中华全国文学艺术工作者代表大会
1949-07-05
第1版()
专栏：

　　惊闻季米特洛夫逝世文代大会特致电吊唁
    【平市讯】全国文代大会第三日，大会进行中，全体代表闻悉国际劳工运动卓越的领袖，保加利亚共产党总书记季米特洛夫病逝的噩耗，一致在哀乐声中起立悼念。并一致决议用大会名义通电吊唁。该电原文如下：保加利亚人民民主共和国部长会议：
    惊悉卓越的国际共产主义的领袖，伟大的爱国主义者与国际主义者季米特洛夫同志病逝，至深哀悼，特电致唁。
    中华全国文学艺术工作者代表大会
    一九四九年七月四日
