Title: 11th Day of the Literary and Art Congress: Adoption of the Draft Constitution of the National Federation of Literary and Art Circles; All Representatives Pay Tribute to Mr. Wen Yiduo on the Third Anniversary of His Death
Author: Bai Sheng
Date: 1949-07-15
Page: 3

The 11th Day of the Literary and Art Congress

Adoption of the Draft Constitution of the National Federation of Literary and Art Circles

All Representatives Pay Tribute to Mr. Wen Yiduo on the Third Anniversary of His Death

[Our Newspaper's Report] Yesterday (the 14th) was the eleventh day of the National Literary and Art Congress, presided over by Chairman Yang Hansheng. The meeting discussed the Constitution (Draft) of the All-China Federation of Literary and Art Circles and the Regulations (Draft) for the Election of the National Committee of the Federation of Literary and Art Circles. Tributes were also paid to Mr. Wen Yiduo on the third anniversary of his death.

At the beginning of the meeting, Sha Kefu, Secretary of the Congress, reported on the drafting process of the Draft Constitution of the National Federation of Literary and Art Circles. After heated and careful discussions and revisions by all the representatives, it was immediately put to a vote and passed.

The Chairman reported that the 15th was the third anniversary of Mr. Wen Yiduo's death, and because the Congress would be holding group discussions on the 15th, the Chairman proposed holding a memorial service in advance. All representatives stood up together and observed a moment of silence amidst mournful music. The Chairman also read aloud the Congress's letter of condolence to Mr. Wen Yiduo's wife, Ms. Gao Zhen. (See separate article)

During the meeting, representatives from the Pingju (Beijing Opera) circles of Beiping City and the Beiping Federation of Teachers and Staff of Primary and Secondary Schools presented flags and speeches.

[Another Report] Yesterday at 5:00 PM, nine units, including the North China Bureau of the CPC Central Committee, the North China People's Government, the North China Military Region, the Beiping Municipal Committee of the CPC, the Beiping Municipal People's Government, the Beiping Military Control Commission, the Beiping Municipal Branch of the China Democratic League, the Preparatory Committee of the Beiping Municipal Branch of the Revolutionary Committee of the Kuomintang, and the Beiping Municipal Rectification Committee of the Chinese Peasants' and Workers' Democratic Party, held a cocktail party today at 5:00 PM to entertain all the representatives of the National Congress of Literary and Art Workers. Beiping Mayor Ye Jianying delivered a welcoming speech on behalf of the nine units, and Mao Dun delivered a response on behalf of all the representatives. The Korean Cultural Troupe, which participated in the performances of the Literary and Art Congress, also performed dances and songs to add to the festivities.

[Another Report] The Congress will hold group discussions today, and each delegation will discuss the list of candidates separately.

　　　　　　　　(Bai Sheng)

### 文代大会第十一日　通过全国文联章程草案  全体代表为闻一多先生死难三周年致哀
柏生
1949-07-15
第3版()
专栏：

　　文代大会第十一日
  　通过全国文联章程草案
    全体代表为闻一多先生死难三周年致哀
    【本报讯】全国文代大会昨天（十四日）为第十一日，阳翰笙主席。会上讨论了中华全国文学艺术界联合会章程（草案）及选举文联全国委员会条例（草案）。并为闻一多先生死难三周年致哀。
    会议首由大会秘书长沙可夫报告全国文联章程草案草拟经过。经全体代表热烈慎重商讨与修正后，当即表决通过。
    主席报告十五日为闻一多先生死难三周年纪念，因大会十五日进行小组讨论，主席提议提前举行悼念。全体代表一致起立在哀乐声中默哀。主席并朗读通过大会致闻一多夫人高真先生的慰问信。（见另文）
    会议进行中并有北平市评剧界代表及北平中小学教联献旗、献词。
    【又讯】昨日下午五时，中共中央华北局、华北人民政府、华北军区、中共北平市委会、北平市人民政府、北平军管会、民盟北平市支部、国民党革命委员会北平市分会筹委会、农工民主党北平市整委会等九个单位，今天下午五点钟举行鸡尾酒会，宴请全国文学艺术工作者代表大会全体代表，由北平市市长叶剑英代表这九个单位致欢迎词，茅盾代表全体代表致答词。并有参加文代会演出的朝鲜文工团表演舞蹈、歌曲等节目助兴。
    【又讯】大会今日小组讨论，各代表团将分别讨论候选人名单。
    　　　　　　　　（柏生）
