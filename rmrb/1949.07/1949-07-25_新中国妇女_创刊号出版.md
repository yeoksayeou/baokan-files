　　Publication of the Inaugural Issue of "New Chinese Women"
    [This Newspaper's Report] The inaugural issue of the monthly magazine "New Chinese Women" was published on July 20th. This magazine is edited and published by the "New Chinese Women Press" and distributed by Xinhua Bookstores in various regions. The "New Chinese Women Press" is under the Propaganda and Education Department of the All-China Democratic Women's Federation. It is a comprehensive women's publication that guides the current women's movement, points out current women's issues, introduces revolutionary theory, provides various general knowledge, and exchanges experiences in women's work from various regions. The table of contents of the inaugural issue includes: Stalin's "On Working Women," and the resolution of the First National Women's Congress of China – "The Resolution on the Current Tasks of the Chinese Women's Movement," etc.

### “新中国妇女”创刊号出版

1949-07-25
第1版()
专栏：

　　“新中国妇女”创刊号出版
    【本报讯】“新中国妇女”月刊创刊号已于七月二十日出版。该刊是由“新中国妇女社”编辑和出版，由各地新华书店负责发行。“新中国妇女社”隶属于中华全国民主妇女联合会宣传教育部。它是一个指导当前妇女运动，指出当前妇女问题，介绍革命理论，提供各种常识，交流各地妇女工作经验的综合性妇女刊物。创刊号要目有：斯大林的“论劳动妇女”，中国第一次全国妇女代表大会的决议——“中国妇女运动当前任务的决议”等。
