Title: Celebrating Soviet Navy Day: Vasilevsky Issues Order, Saluting Troops and Shipbuilders for Strengthening Naval Power
Author:
Date: 1949-07-26
Page: 1

　Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, July 25th: Moscow dispatch: Soviet Marshal Vasilevsky, Minister of the Armed Forces of the Soviet Union, issued the following order on the 24th, celebrating Soviet Navy Day: Comrades soldiers and non-commissioned officers of the Army and Navy! Comrades lieutenants, captains, colonels, and generals of the Army and Navy!

　　Today, the Soviet people and their armed forces celebrate Soviet Navy Day and review the achievements of Soviet sailors and shipbuilders in fulfilling the tasks set by our leader and teacher, Comrade Stalin.

　　On Navy Day, I salute you and congratulate you.

　　I wish all personnel of the Soviet Navy and workers of the shipbuilding industry new achievements in further strengthening the naval power of our great motherland.

　　To celebrate Soviet Navy Day, I order: Today, July 24th, warships in Leningrad, Kronstadt, Tallinn, Baltiysk, Sevastopol, Odessa, Murmansk, Onega, Arkhangelsk, Vladivostok, Port Arthur, Sovetskaya Gavan, Petropavlovsk-Kamchatsky, Baku, Khabarovsk, Izmail, and Pinsk shall fire a twenty-gun salute.

　　Long live the Soviet Navy! Long live the Soviet Armed Forces! Long live our Soviet motherland! Long live the glorious Communist Party of the Soviet Union! Long live our leader and teacher, the great Comrade Stalin!

　　[Signed] Vasilevsky, Soviet Marshal, Minister of the Armed Forces of the Soviet Union.

### 庆祝苏联海军节 华西列夫斯基颁发命令  向加强海军力量的部队和造船工人致敬

1949-07-26
第1版()
专栏：
 　庆祝苏联海军节
   华西列夫斯基颁发命令
   向加强海军力量的部队和造船工人致敬
    【新华社北平二十五日电】莫斯科讯：苏联武装部队部部长、苏联元帅华西列夫斯基于二十四日颁发庆祝苏联海军节的命令如下：陆、海军战士、士官同志们！陆、海军尉官、校官、将军同志们！
    今天，苏维埃人民和他们的武装部队庆祝苏联海军节，并检阅苏联海员们和造船工人们在执行我们的领袖与导师斯大林同志所规定的任务中所获得的成就。
    在海军节，我向你们致敬和道贺。
    祝苏联海军部队全体人员和造船工业工人在更进一步地加强我们伟大祖国的海军力量上，获得新的成就。
    为了庆祝苏联海军节，我命令：今天，七月二十四日，在列宁格勒、喀隆斯塔德、塔林、波罗的斯克、悉瓦斯托波尔、敖德萨、摩尔曼斯克、宛加、亚尔干日尔、海参威、旅顺港、苏维埃次卡亚加凡、彼得罗巴夫罗夫斯克、堪察加、巴库、哈巴罗夫斯克、伊兹海尔及平斯克地的军舰，齐鸣礼炮二十响。
    苏维埃海军万岁！苏联武装部队万岁！我们的苏维埃祖国万岁！光荣的苏联共产党万岁！我们的领袖和导师、伟大的斯大林同志万岁！
    苏联武装部队部部长、苏联元帅华西列夫斯基（签字）。
