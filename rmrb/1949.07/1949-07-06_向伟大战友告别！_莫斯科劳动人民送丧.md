Title: Bidding Farewell to the Great Comrade-in-Arms! Moscow Working People Hold Funeral

Author:
Date: 1949-07-06
Page: 1

　　Bidding Farewell to the Great Comrade-in-Arms!
  　Moscow Working People Hold Funeral
    Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, 4th: TASS, Moscow: On July 3rd, the working people of Moscow held a funeral for Dimitrov, the outstanding leader of the international workers' movement. Mourning flags were hung diagonally on buildings in the Soviet capital. Moscow, the entire Soviet Union, and all progressive humanity deeply mourn this heavy loss together.

    This is the final farewell. Dimitrov's relatives and friends gathered around the coffin. At 10:00 AM, Malenkov, Beria, Kaganovich, Mikoyan, Shvernik, Bulganin, Suslov, Ponomarenko, and Popov carefully carried the coffin out onto the street and placed it on a gun carriage. The funeral procession then set off for the Belorussky Railway Station. Behind the coffin was a stream of people holding flowers. Gorky Street and its neighboring small streets were crowded with tens of thousands of working people, paying their last respects to this heroic fighter for the great cause of Lenin and Stalin, and for the cause of communism.

    At 10:50 AM, the procession approached the Belorussky Railway Station, with the Soviet and Bulgarian national flags, bordered in black, hanging on the front of the station. The coffin was taken off the gun carriage, carried past the honor guard, onto the platform, and onto the hearse. Dimitrov's body was carefully placed in a carriage covered with red cloth, black cloth, and decorated with a blue border.

    At 11:05 AM, the locomotive whistle sounded low, and the hearse started. Moscow bid farewell to the unforgettable Dimitrov.

    This outstanding leader of the international workers' movement, the loyal fighting comrade and friend of the great Stalin, will forever live in the hearts of the Soviet people, and will inspire future generations to create sacred achievements for the cause of communism.

    The Soviet government delegation, the Moscow workers' delegation, the Bulgarian government and Bulgarian Communist Party delegation, as well as Dimitrov's relatives and friends, accompanied the hearse from Moscow to the Bulgarian capital, Sofia.

### 向伟大战友告别！　莫斯科劳动人民送丧

1949-07-06
第1版()
专栏：

　　向伟大战友告别！
  　莫斯科劳动人民送丧
    【新华社北平四日电】塔斯社莫斯科讯：七月三日，莫斯科劳动人民为国际工人阶级运动杰出的领袖季米特洛夫送丧。苏联首都建筑物上斜挂着丧旗。莫斯科、全苏联以及全体进步人类，一同对这个沉重的损失深表哀悼。
    这是最后的送别。季米特洛夫亲属及朋友围聚着灵柩。上午十时，马林科夫、贝利亚、卡冈诺维奇、米高扬、什维尔尼克、布尔加宁、苏斯洛夫、波诺马林科、波波夫极其谨慎地将灵柩抬出街上，并安放在炮车上。出殡行列即向白俄罗斯火车站出发。灵柩后面为手执鲜花的人流。高尔基大街及其邻近的小街上，汇集着成千成万的劳动人民，向这位列宁、斯大林伟大事业和共产主义事业的英勇斗士致最后的敬意。
    上午十时五十分，行列行近白俄罗斯火车站，车站正面悬挂有镶着黑边的保苏两国国旗。灵柩被抬出炮车，从仪仗队面前抬过，抬向月台，抬向殡车。季米特洛夫的遗体被小心地安放在覆以红布、黑布及饰以青边的车厢上。
    上午十一时五分，火车头汽笛低鸣，殡车开动。莫斯科与永志不忘的季米特洛夫告别了。
    这位国际工人阶级运动的杰出领袖、伟大斯大林的忠实战斗同志与朋友，将永远活在苏联人民的心上，并将感召后代子孙为共产主义的事业而创造神圣的功绩。
    苏联政府代表团、莫斯科工人代表团、保政府及保共代表团以及季米特洛夫的亲属朋友，都护随殡车离莫斯科前往保加利亚首都索非亚。
