Title: Nanjing Commemorates "July 1st"
Author:
Date: 1949-07-03
Page: 2

　　Nanjing Commemorates "July 1st"
    [This newspaper's report] Nanjing People's Broadcasting Station broadcasted on the 2nd: The Nanjing Municipal Committee of the Chinese Communist Party held a Party members' meeting on "July 1st" to commemorate the 28th anniversary of the founding of the Chinese Communist Party. More than 4,000 Party members participated. Chairman Chen Xiuliang pointed out that the victory of the Party in the past 28 years was due to the correct leadership of Chairman Mao and the Central Committee, and the result of the heroic struggle of all Party members in connecting with the masses and uniting the people. Comrade Chen called on all Party members in the city to commemorate the 28th anniversary of the Party, to earnestly guard against arrogance, and to earnestly study the theory and policies of Marxism-Leninism in order to overcome the difficulties that still exist and complete more and greater construction tasks.

### 南京纪念“七一”

1949-07-03
第2版()
专栏：

　　南京纪念“七一”
    【本报讯】南京人民广播电台二日广播：中共南京市委会在“七一”举行党员大会，纪念中国共产党成立二十八周年，参加的党员有四千多人。主席陈修良指出二十八年党的胜利，是由于毛主席和中央领导的正确和全体党员联系群众和团结人民英勇斗争的结果。陈同志号召全市党员为了纪念党的二十八周年，必需力戒骄傲，认真的学习马列主义的理论和政策，以克服目前还存在着的困难，完成更多更大的建设任务。
