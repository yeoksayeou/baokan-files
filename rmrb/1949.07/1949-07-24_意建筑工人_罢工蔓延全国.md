Title: Italian Construction Workers' Strike Spreads Nationwide
Author:
Date: 1949-07-24
Page: 3

Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, July 23rd: TASS, Rome, July 22nd: The Italian General Confederation of Labor's press office issued a communique stating that the construction workers' strike demanding wage increases has spread to all provinces of Italy, the Venice region, Sicily, and Sardinia. Ansa news agency acknowledged that in the regions where workers' living conditions are the most difficult, such as Apulia and Lucania, the participation of construction workers in the strike has reached 90% to 95%.

### 意建筑工人　罢工蔓延全国

1949-07-24
第3版()
专栏：

　　意建筑工人
  　罢工蔓延全国
    【新华社北平二十三日电】塔斯社罗马二十二日讯：意大利总工会新闻处发表公报称，建筑工人要求增加工资的罢工，已蔓延至意大利的一切省份、威尼斯区域、西西里及萨丁尼亚。安莎通讯社承认，在劳工生活情况最艰苦的区城如亚蒲利亚、路坎尼亚，建筑工人参加罢工的已达百分之九十至百分之九十五。
