Title: Tonight's Program on Beiping Xinhua Radio Station
Author:
Date: 1949-07-07
Page: 1

　　Beiping Xinhua Radio Station's Program for Tonight
    [From Our Newspaper] Beiping Xinhua Radio Station's speech program at 19:50 tonight: (1) Commander-in-Chief Zhu De of the Chinese People's Liberation Army will broadcast to the nation in commemoration of "July 7th"; (2) Rebroadcasting of the recording of Comrade Zhou Enlai, Deputy Director of the Standing Committee of the Preparatory Committee of the New Political Consultative Conference, reading the declaration of the parties and groups of the Preparatory Committee of the New Political Consultative Conference in commemoration of the 12th anniversary of the "July 7th" Anti-Japanese War. At 19:30, during the women's program, the broadcast will include: (1) "Women in the Liberated Areas Support the Army's Advance"; (2) The All-China Democratic Women's Federation calls for a "Letter Writing" campaign to support the army. At 22:20, the cultural program will broadcast the joint performance of "Yellow River Cantata" and other anti-Japanese war songs by the Anti-Enemy Drama Society, the People's Art Troupe, the North China First, Second, and Third Art Troupes, and the Youth Drama Society.

### 北平新华广播电台今晚节目

1949-07-07
第1版()
专栏：

　　北平新华广播电台今晚节目
    【本报讯】北平新华广播电台今晚十九点五十分演讲节目：（一）中国人民解放军朱总司令为纪念“七七”向全国广播；（二）重播新政治协商会议筹备会常务委员会副主任周恩来同志宣读新政治协商会议筹备会各党派各团体为纪念“七七”抗日战争十二周年宣言录音。十九点三十分妇女时间，播送：（一）“解放区妇女支援大军向前进”；（二）中华全国民主妇女联合会号召劳军发起“一封信”运动。二十二点二十分文艺节目，播送抗敌剧社、人民文工团、华北第一、二、三文工团、青年剧社联合演唱“黄河大合唱”和其他抗战歌曲。
