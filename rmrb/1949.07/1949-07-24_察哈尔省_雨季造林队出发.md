Title: Chahar Province: Afforestation Team Sets Off for Rainy Season

Author:
Date: 1949-07-24
Page: 1

　　Chahar Province
  　Afforestation Team Sets Off for Rainy Season
    [Chahar News] In order to complete the task of planting ten million trees during the rainy season, the Chahar Provincial People's Government organized two afforestation teams, totaling seventeen people, who set off on the 8th of this month for the Datong Yuhe area and the Xuanhua Yanghe River bank to assist local government work. The expected duration is one month.

### 察哈尔省　雨季造林队出发

1949-07-24
第1版()
专栏：

　　察哈尔省
  　雨季造林队出发
    【察哈尔讯】察哈尔省人民政府为完成雨季植树一千万棵的任务，特组织了两个造林队共十七人，于本月八日分赴大同御河地区及宣化洋河沿岸协助地方政府工作。预期为一个月。
