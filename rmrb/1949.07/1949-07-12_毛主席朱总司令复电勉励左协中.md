Title: Chairman Mao and Commander-in-Chief Zhu Send a Reply Encouraging Zuo Xiezhong
Author:
Date: 1949-07-12
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, Beiping, 11th. General Zuo Xiezhong of the former Kuomintang 22nd Army and others accepted the plan for a peaceful resolution of the army. Chairman Mao and Commander-in-Chief Zhu sent a reply today to commend and encourage them. The telegram is as follows:

　　General Zuo Xiezhong and all officers and soldiers of the former Kuomintang 22nd Army: We have received your circular telegram. It is extremely gratifying that your general and others led your troops to accept the peaceful solution, so that your army and the people around Yulin celebrate their liberation. We hope that you will strive to unite the troops, strengthen training, transform the relationship between officers, soldiers, and civilians, and strive to participate in the great task of liberating the Northwest.

　　　Mao Zedong, Zhu De
　　　July 11, 1949

### 毛主席朱总司令复电勉励左协中

1949-07-12
第1版()
专栏：

　　毛主席朱总司令
  　复电勉励左协中
    【新华社北平十一日电】前国民党二十二军左协中将军等接受和平解决该军方案，毛主席朱总司令本日复电奖勉。电文如下：
    左协中将军及前国民党第二十二军全体官兵们：接读通电，贵将军等率部接受和平解决方案，使贵军及榆林一带人民咸庆解放，极为欣慰。尚望努力团结部队，加强整训，改造官兵、军民关系，为参加西北解放斗争的伟大任务而奋斗。
    　　　毛泽东　朱德
    　　一九四九年七月十一日
