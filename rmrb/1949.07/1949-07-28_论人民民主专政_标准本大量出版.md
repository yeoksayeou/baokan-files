Title: Standard Edition of "On the People's Democratic Dictatorship" Published in Large Quantities
Author:
Date: 1949-07-28
Page: 2

　　“Standard Edition of "On the People's Democratic Dictatorship" Published in Large Quantities
[This Newspaper's Report] After the Xinhua Bookstore in Beiping initially printed 50,000 copies of the finally proofread standard edition of "On the People's Democratic Dictatorship," nearly 40,000 copies were sold in the cities of Peiping and Tianjin alone in less than half a month. A second edition of 50,000 copies is now being rushed into print. Printing plates have also been successively sent to the Northeast, East China, Northwest, and Central China regions.

### “论人民民主专政”标准本大量出版

1949-07-28
第2版()
专栏：

　　“论人民民主专政”标准本大量出版
    【本报讯】“论人民民主专政”最后校正的标准本新华书店在北平初版印出五万册后，不到半个月，仅在平、津二市，即已销出四万册，现已继续赶印第二版五万册。东北、华东、西北、华中各地纸型亦已陆续寄出。
