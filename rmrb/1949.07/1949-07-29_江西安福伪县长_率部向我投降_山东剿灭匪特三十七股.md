Title: Jiangxi Anfu Puppet County Magistrate Leads Troops to Surrender; Shandong Annihilates 37 Bandit-Spy Gangs
Author:
Date: 1949-07-29
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Central China Front, 28th: On the 24th at dawn, a unit of the People's Liberation Army surrounded the puppet county government and part of the Baojing (security police) regiment of Anfu County, Jiangxi, in Yixi, more than 40 miles north of the city. The puppet county magistrate, Yan Jizhi, and more than 330 officers and soldiers of the Baojing regiment, along with 308 rifles and pistols, surrendered to the People's Liberation Army.

Xinhua News Agency, Jinan, 28th: People's local armed forces in various newly liberated areas of Shandong Province are actively suppressing bandits and spies to consolidate the security of the rear. In the three months of April, May, and June, a total of 37 armed bandit-spy gangs were annihilated, totaling 842 people, of whom 578 were captured alive, 21 were killed, and 243 bandits and spies registered for surrender. We seized 3 mortars, 3 light machine guns, 28 Thompson and carbine submachine guns, 683 rifles and pistols, and a portion of other military supplies.

### 江西安福伪县长　率部向我投降　山东剿灭匪特三十七股

1949-07-29
第1版()
专栏：

　　江西安福伪县长
  　率部向我投降
  　山东剿灭匪特三十七股
    【新华社华中前线二十八日电】人民解放军某部二十四日拂晓，在赣西安福城北四十余里伊溪，将该县伪县府及保警团一部包围，伪县长晏继志及保警团官兵三百三十余人，携同长短枪三○八支全部向解放军投降。
    【新华社济南二十八日电】山东省新解放区各地人民地方武装积极清剿匪特，巩固后方治安。四、五、六三个月中，共歼灭武装匪特三十七股，共八百四十二名，其中生俘五百七十八名，击毙二十一名，匪特自首登记者二百四十三名。我缴获迫击炮三门，轻机枪三挺，汤姆式、卡宾等冲锋枪二十八支，长短枪六百八十三支，及其他军用品一部。
