Title: *Labor Daily* Publishes Editorial Congratulating the Opening of the World Federation of Trade Unions Congress
Author:
Date: 1949-07-02
Page: 3
Xinhua News Agency, Beiping, 1st: Moscow Dispatch: The Soviet Union's trade union newspaper, *Labor Daily*, published an editorial on June 29th congratulating the opening of the Second Congress of the World Federation of Trade Unions in Milan, stating: "The inauguration of this congress is a significant and major event in the lives of working people worldwide."

*Labor Daily* emphasized: "The World Federation of Trade Unions has steadfastly implemented the resolutions of the First Congress and has been fighting to meet the urgent needs of the working class. The World Federation of Trade Unions has always occupied a glorious position in the democratic camp, and it is an active fighter for people's peace and freedom and for democracy and progress."

Referring to the attempts by the enemies of working-class unity to form a scab international to oppose the World Federation of Trade Unions, *Labor Daily* stated: "This malicious act of betraying the interests of the working people is doomed to failure. Progressive workers around the world know that only by uniting their forces can they protect their vital interests, their rights, and their social benefits."

After pointing out the measures taken by imperialist countries to suppress democracy and the frenzied arms race, *Labor Daily* stated: "The working people of all countries, in mobilizing their forces to protect their economic and social interests and to repel the enemies of peace and progress, look to the Soviet Union with hopeful eyes. The Soviet Union has no crises or unemployment and is confidently advancing towards the victory of communism. The achievements of the Soviet people bring joy to working people around the world and strengthen their confidence in the struggle for their vital interests, for a better future, and for peace and democracy."

### 劳动报发表社论　祝贺世界工联代表大会开幕

1949-07-02
第3版()
专栏：

　　劳动报发表社论
  　祝贺世界工联代表大会开幕
    【新华社北平一日电】莫斯科讯：苏联职工会机关报劳动报于六月二十九日发表社论，祝贺在米兰开幕的世界工联第二届代表大会称：这一代表大会的揭幕，是全世界劳动人民生活中显著的重大事件。
    劳动报着重指出：世界工联一直坚守不渝地实行着第一届表代大会的决议，并为满足工人阶级迫切的需要而进行着斗争。世界工联在民主阵营中一直占着光荣的地位，它是争取人民的和平与自由和争取民主与进步的积极的战士。
    论及工人阶级团结的敌人成立工贼国际以反对世界工联的企图时，劳动报声称：这种背叛劳动人民利益的恶行，是注定了要失败的。全世界进步工人深知，只有团结他们的力量，他们才能保护他们切身的利益，他们的权利和他们的社会权益。
    劳动报于指出帝国主义国家压制民主的措施及疯狂的军备竞赛后声称：世界各国的劳动人民，在动员他们的力量来保护他们经济与社会的利益和击退和平与进步的敌人当中，以期望的眼光注视着苏联。苏联没有危机和失业，而且正满怀信心地向共产主义的胜利前进。苏联人民的成就，给全世界劳动人民带来了欢欣，并加强了他们为切身利益、为更美好的前途、为和平与民主而斗争的信心。
