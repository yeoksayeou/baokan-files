Title: Northeast Anshan Iron: First Batch of 60,000 Jin Transported to Beiping

Author: 
Date: 1949-07-05
Page: 2

　　Economic News

　　Northeast Anshan Iron
  　First Batch of 60,000 Jin Transported to Beiping
    [Our Dispatch] The first batch of iron transported from the Northeast after liberation arrived in Beiping on the evening of the 2nd, totaling 60,000 jin, and was unloaded yesterday. In order to supply factories in Beiping, Tianjin, Baoding and other places with raw materials, the Pingjin Coal and Iron Company purchased the first batch of 500 tons of iron in Anshan, Northeast China. Most of it has now been transported to Tianjin, and part of it will be transported to Beiping successively. In the future, depending on the iron demand in North China, further purchases will be made in Anshan. (Zhong)

### 东北鞍山铁　首批六万斤运平

1949-07-05
第2版()
专栏：经济新闻

　　东北鞍山铁
  　首批六万斤运平
    【本报讯】解放后，由东北运来之首批铁于二日晚抵平，计六万斤，已于昨日卸车。平津煤铁公司为供给平津保等地工厂原料，在东北鞍山购妥首批铁五百吨，现已大部运津，其中一部将陆续运平。今后视华北需铁情形，继续在鞍山洽购。　（仲）
