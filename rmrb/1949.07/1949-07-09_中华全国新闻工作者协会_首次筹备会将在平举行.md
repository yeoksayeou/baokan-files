Title: All-China Journalists Association First Preparatory Meeting to be Held in Beiping
Author:
Date: 1949-07-09
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, Beiping, July 7th. The first preparatory committee of the All-China Journalists Association will be held in Beiping on the 13th of this month. The association was initiated in May of this year by four groups: the China Liberated Areas Journalists Association, the China Youth Journalists Society, the Beiping Journalists Symposium, and the Tianjin Journalists Conference. At that time, it was agreed to first convene a national congress of journalists, and then establish the All-China Journalists Association at the meeting. For the convenience of preparation, the four groups jointly drafted a list of candidates for preparatory committee members and standing preparatory committee members, and on the 11th of last month, jointly telegraphed the journalists of newspapers, news agencies, and radio stations in the five major central cities of Beiping, Shanghai, Hankou, Shenyang, and Xi'an, as well as the journalists of newspapers and news agencies of the First Field Army, Second Field Army, Third Field Army, and Fourth Field Army of the People's Liberation Army, suggesting that they discuss it and propose amendments. After more than 20 days of back-and-forth telegrams, the opinions of all parties have been finally consolidated. The list has been amended and passed. The four initiating news groups issued a notice on the 7th of this month, scheduling the first preparatory committee meeting to be held at the Xinhua Broadcasting Station in Beiping on the 13th of this month. The amended and approved list of preparatory committee members and standing preparatory committee members is as follows:

	(I) Preparatory Committee Members: Wang Kangzhi, Wang Yunsheng, Shi Ximin, Tian Fang, Bao Zhijing, Li Chunqing, Li Pu, Li Huang, Wu Wentao, Wu Lengxi, Meng Qiujiang, Jin Zhonghua, Zhou Qinyue, Shao Zonghan, Lin Zhongyi, Fan Changjiang, Hu Qiaomu, Hu Yuzhi, Hu Jiwei, Hong Sisi, Pu Xixiu, Xu Ying, Xu Zhucheng, Xu Maijin, Hai Leng, Chen Kehan, Chen Hanbo, Mei Yi, Zhang Panshi, Huan Xiang, Lu Yi, Qiao Mu, Peng Zigang, Yun Yiqun, Yang Gang, Yang Geng, Liao Chengzhi, Liao Jingdan, Liao Mosha, Zhao Chaogou, Xiong Fu, Deng Gang, Deng Tuo, Liu Baiyu, Liu Simu, Liu Zunqi, Sa Kongliao, Gu Zhizhong.

	(II) Standing Preparatory Committee Members: Fan Changjiang, Hu Qiaomu, Hu Yuzhi, Zhang Panshi, Huan Xiang, Yun Yiqun, Liao Chengzhi, Liu Zunqi, Sa Kongliao.

### 中华全国新闻工作者协会　首次筹备会将在平举行

1949-07-09
第1版()
专栏：

　　中华全国新闻工作者协会
  　首次筹备会将在平举行
    【新华社北平七日电】中华全国新闻工作者协会第一次筹备委员会将于本月十三日在北平举行。该会系中国解放区新闻记者联合会、中国青年记者学会、北平新闻工作者座谈会、平津新闻工作会议等四团体于本年五月发起。当时议定首先召集全国新闻工作者代表大会，然后在会上产生中华全国新闻工作者协会，为便于筹备起见，该四团体共同草拟了一个筹备委员和常务筹备委员的候选人名单，于上月十一日，联名电告北平、上海、汉口、沈阳、西安等五大中心城市的报社、通讯社、广播电台的新闻工作者，与人民解放军第一野战军、第二野战军、第三野战军、第四野战军的报社、通讯社的新闻工作者，建议他们予以讨论，并提出修正意见。经过二十多天的往返电商，各方意见业已最后集中，名单经修正通过，原发起之四新闻团体乃于本月七日发出通知，定期于本月十三日假北平新华广播电台举行第一次筹备委员会。修正通过之筹备委员与常务筹备委员名单如下：
