Title: People in Various Parts of Italy Sign Petitions Opposing the Ratification of the Aggression Pact
Author:
Date: 1949-07-06
Page: 3

Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, 5th – TASS reports: The delegation of people from various parts of Italy petitioning against the ratification of the North Atlantic Treaty is delivering an increasing number of peace petitions to the National Committee of People's Petitions in Rome. Delegations from the Marche and Umbria regions in central Italy have submitted petitions with over 320,000 signatures. According to preliminary statistics, in Florence, the number of people who have signed the petition has exceeded 274,000.

### 意各地人民签名请愿　反对批准侵略公约

1949-07-06
第3版()
专栏：

　　意各地人民签名请愿
  　反对批准侵略公约
    【新华社北平五日电】塔斯社讯：意大利各地人民反对批准北大西洋公约请愿代表团，正以愈来愈多的和平请愿书送达罗马人民请愿全国委员会。意大利中部马彻斯地区与乌姆布里地区的代表团，送来有三十二万多人签名的请愿书。另据初步统计，在佛罗伦萨，在请愿书上签名的已超过二十七万四千人。
