Title: Circular from the Chinese People's Revolutionary Military Commission: Prohibition of Abuse and Unauthorized Production of Military Flags and Emblems
Author:
Date: 1949-07-27
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Peiping, 26th: The Chinese People's Revolutionary Military Commission has issued the following circular regarding the prohibition of abuse and unauthorized production of the military flags and emblems of the Chinese People's Liberation Army:

　　The military flag and emblem of the Chinese People's Liberation Army, formulated by this Commission, represent the dignity of the military force of our people's motherland and the glory of our People's Liberation Army in defending the motherland and protecting the people. These military flags and emblems can only be used and worn by the established units of the People's Liberation Army and their active-duty soldiers. All military flags and emblems shall be strictly manufactured by the logistics departments at or above the level of major military regions and field armies of the People's Liberation Army in accordance with the prescribed standards. Private manufacturers are prohibited from manufacturing and selling them without authorization, and it is strictly forbidden to use the military flag and emblem patterns as ordinary signs and designs, so as not to affect military discipline and appearance. This circular is hereby issued.

　　　　Chinese People's Revolutionary Military Commission, July 18, 1949

### 中国人民革命军事委员会通令　禁止滥用滥制军旗军徽

1949-07-27
第1版()
专栏：

　　中国人民革命军事委员会通令
  　禁止滥用滥制军旗军徽
    【新华社北平二十六日电】中国人民革命军事委员会顷为禁止滥用滥制中国人民解放军军旗军徽事发布通令如下：
    本会制订的中国人民解放军军旗军徽系表示我人民祖国武力的尊严，及我人民解放军捍卫祖国保护人民的光荣。此项军旗、军徽，只有人民解放军建制部队及其在职军人才能使用佩带。军旗军徽一律由人民解放军各大军区一级及各野战军一级之后勤部严格遵照规定标准制造，禁止私人厂商擅自制造贩卖，并严禁以军旗军徽式样用作普通标记图案，以免影响军纪军容。特引通令。
  　　　　中国人民革命军事委员会　一九四九年七月十八日
