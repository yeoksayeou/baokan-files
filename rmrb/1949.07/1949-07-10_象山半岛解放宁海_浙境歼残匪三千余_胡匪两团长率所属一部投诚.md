Title: Liberation of Ninghai on the Xiangshan Peninsula; Over 3,000 Remnant Bandits Eliminated in Zhejiang Province; Two Bandit Regiment Commanders of the Hu Faction Lead a Portion of Their Subordinates in Surrender
Author:
Date: 1949-07-10
Page: 1
　　Xinhua News Agency, Hangzhou, July 9th: A unit of the People's Liberation Army, in suppressing the remnant Kuomintang bandit troops entrenched in the Xiangshan Peninsula in eastern Zhejiang, liberated Ninghai County on the Xiangshan Peninsula on the 5th of this month, successively eliminating parts of the enemy's Eighty-seventh Army's 222nd Division, 220th Division, and Traffic Police Headquarters.
　　Xinhua News Agency, Hangzhou, July 9th: Armed forces of various military sub-districts under the Zhejiang Military Region of the Chinese People's Liberation Army, in June, successively cleared out over 3,470 remnant Kuomintang bandit armed reactionary forces in Yuyao, Shaoxing, Zhuji, Jinhua, Lanxi, Linhai, Huangyan, Tonglu, Jiande and other counties, including over 1,900 bandits under the command of Cao Jiazheng, commander of the Bandit Zhejiang Second Anti-Communist Headquarters, who surrendered to the Liberation Army. A total of one mortar, one 60mm gun, 73 light and heavy machine guns, and over 1,260 long and short guns were seized.
　　Xinhua News Agency, Xi'an, July 9th: Wang Ping, regimental commander of the 367th Regiment of the 123rd Division, and Wang Zongyi, regimental commander of the 84th Regiment of the 28th Division, both of the Hu Zongnan bandit troops, led a portion of their subordinates to surrender to the Liberation Army at the end of June.

### 象山半岛解放宁海　浙境歼残匪三千余  胡匪两团长率所属一部投诚

1949-07-10
第1版()
专栏：

　　象山半岛解放宁海
  　浙境歼残匪三千余
    胡匪两团长率所属一部投诚
    【新华社杭州九日电】人民解放军一部进剿盘踞浙东象山半岛的国民党匪军残部，于本月五日解放象山半岛上的宁海县城，先后歼敌八十七军二二二师、二二○师和交警总队各一部。
    【新华社杭州九日电】中国人民解放军浙江军区所属各军分区武装部队，六月份先后肃清余姚、绍兴、诸暨、金华、兰溪、临海、黄岩、桐庐、建德等县国民党匪军残余反动武装三千四百七十余人，其中包括向解放军投诚的匪浙江第二反共司令部司令曹家政等股一千九百余人。共缴获迫击炮一门，六○炮一门，轻重机枪七十三挺，长短枪一千二百六十余枝。
    【新华社西安九日电】胡宗南匪部一百二十三师三百六十七团团长汪平、二十八师八十四团团长王宗义，于六月底先后率所部一部向解放军投诚。
