Title: Hebei Government Treatment Team Helps Establish Four County Hospitals in Eastern Hebei
Author:
Date: 1949-07-19
Page: 2

    Hebei Government Treatment Team
    Helps Establish Four County Hospitals in Eastern Hebei
    ▲ The Hebei Government Health Department's medical team, during its two months of work in the Fifteenth District of Eastern Hebei, vaccinated over 39,000 people, administered over 19,000 preventative injections, and treated over 1,800 people; it also restored and established four county hospitals in Fengnan, Zunhua, and other counties.

### 华北人民政府治疗队  帮冀东建县医院四所

1949-07-19
第2版()
专栏：

    华北人民政府治疗队
    帮冀东建县医院四所
    ▲华北人民政府卫生部医疗工作队，在冀东十五分区工作两月来，共种痘三万九千余人，防疫注射一万九千余人，治疗一千八百余人；恢复与建立了丰南、遵化等县立医院四所。
