Title: Telegram of Congratulations from the Korean Literary Artists General League to the Literary Congress

Author:
Date: 1949-07-13
Page: 2

Korean Literary Artists General League
Telegram of Congratulations to the Literary Congress
To the representatives of the First All-China Congress of Literary and Art Workers, and to all the representatives of literary and art workers of China:

You are the warriors united around Mr. Mao Zedong, the leader of the Chinese people, wielding the weapon of inheriting the long and splendid Chinese cultural traditions, and striving to create a free and prosperous New China.

We extend our warmest congratulations to you!

Central Standing Committee and all literary and art workers of the Korean Literary Artists General League
Chairman of the Central Standing Committee of the Korean Literary Artists General League Han Xueye
July 6, 1949

To all literary and art worker representatives of the First All-China Congress of Literary and Art Workers:

Today, your unity is not only a powerful driving force for creating a free and prosperous New China, but also a tremendous force for consolidating the friendship and peace of the people of the world and the vigorous progress of international democratic culture. On behalf of 1.3 million members, I sincerely congratulate and pay my respects.

Chairman of the Central Committee of the Korea-Soviet Culture Association Li Giyong

### 北朝鲜文艺总同盟给文代大会的贺电

1949-07-13
第2版()
专栏：

　　北朝鲜文艺总同盟
    给文代大会的贺电
    第一届全中国文学艺术工作者代表大会、全中国文学艺术工作者代表各位：
    你们是团结于中国人民的领袖毛泽东先生的周围，举起继承悠久灿烂的中国文化传统的武器，为创建自由富强的新中国而奋斗的勇士。
    谨向你们致亲热的祝贺！
    北朝鲜文学艺术总同盟中央常务委员会及全体文艺工作者
    北朝鲜文学艺术总同盟中央常务委员会委员长　韩雪野
  　一九四九年七月六日
    第一届全中国文学艺术工作者代表大会全体文学艺术工作者代表各位：
    今天你们的团结一致，不只是为创建自由富强的新中国强有力的一个原动力，同时为巩固全世界人民的亲善与和平，国际民主文化活泼进展一支巨大力量，兹谨代表一三零万会员忠心祝贺并致敬意。
  　朝苏文化协会中央委员会委员长　李箕永
