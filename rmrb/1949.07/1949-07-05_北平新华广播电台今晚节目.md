Title: Tonight's Program on Beiping Xinhua Broadcasting Station
Author:
Date: 1949-07-05
Page: 1

　　Beiping Xinhua Broadcasting Station Tonight's Program
    [This newspaper] Beiping Xinhua Broadcasting Station's speech program from 19:50 to 20:10 tonight will feature a speech by historian and writer Mr. Guo Moruo on "Realizing the Democratization of Japan." From 19:30 to 19:50, during the workers' time, the program "Let's All Get Involved in Establishing Trade Unions" will be broadcast. From 22:20 to 22:50, the literary and art program will feature the North China People's Art Troupe performing "Ode to New China" and other instrumental music programs.

### 北平新华广播电台今晚节目

1949-07-05
第1版()
专栏：

　　北平新华广播电台今晚节目
    【本报讯】北平新华广播电台今晚十九点五十分到二十点十分演讲节目，由历史学家和作家郭沫若先生演讲“实现日本民主化”。十九点三十分到十九点五十分职工时间，播送“大家动手办工会”。二十二点二十分到二十二点五十分文艺节目，由华北人民文工团演播“新中国颂”和其他器乐节目。
