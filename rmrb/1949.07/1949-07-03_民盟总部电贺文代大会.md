Title: China Democratic League Headquarters Sends Congratulatory Telegram to Literary and Art Congress

Author:

Date: 1949-07-03
Page: 1

　　China Democratic League Headquarters Sends Congratulatory Telegram to Literary and Art Congress
    [Our Newspaper's Dispatch] The Provisional Working Committee of the China Democratic League Headquarters sent a telegram yesterday (2nd) to congratulate the National Literary and Art Congress, stating: "At this moment, on the eve of the complete collapse of the Kuomintang reactionaries, it is of extremely significant that so many gentlemen from the liberated areas and the Kuomintang-controlled areas are gathering in Beiping for such an unprecedentedly grand congress." The telegram also stated: "Now that the whole of China is about to enter a new period of construction, and you happen to be joining forces here, we believe that, under the leadership of the Chinese Communist Party, all of you will get closer to the people and delve deeper into the masses, exchange experiences, and pave a broad path for the New Democratic literary and art movement, and add a brilliant color to the literary scene of the new China."

### 民盟总部电贺文代大会

1949-07-03
第1版()
专栏：

　　民盟总部电贺文代大会
    【本报讯】中国民主同盟总部临时工作委员会于昨（二）日电贺全国文代大会，电称：“当这国民党反动派整个崩溃的前夕，许多从解放区和国统区来的先生们在北平召集这样一个盛况空前的大会，我们认为实在是有非常重大的意义。”又称：“现在整个中国快要步入新的建设时期，而你们恰好在这里会师，我们相信各位先生一定能在中共领导之下，更加靠拢人民深入群众，互相交换经验，为新民主主义的文艺运动辟一坦途，为新中国的文坛放一异彩。”
