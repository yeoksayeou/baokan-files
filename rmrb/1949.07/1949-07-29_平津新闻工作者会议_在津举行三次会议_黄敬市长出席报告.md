Title: Tianjin-Beijing Newspaper Workers Conference Held Three Meetings in Tianjin; Mayor Huang Jing Attended and Reported
Author: 
Date: 1949-07-29
Page: 2

　　Tianjin-Beijing Newspaper Workers Conference
  　Held Three Meetings in Tianjin
  　Mayor Huang Jing Attended and Reported
    [Tianjin Electric] The Tianjin-Beijing Newspaper Workers Conference held its third meeting at 3:00 PM yesterday (the 28th) at the Tianjin Municipal Government Reception Office. Thirty-seven newspaper workers from Tianjin and Beijing newspaper offices, news agencies, radio stations, and other units attended. During the meeting, Mayor Huang Jing of Tianjin was invited to report on the changes in industry and commerce, the handling of labor-capital relations, the coordination between public and private industry and commerce in solving raw material and sales problems, as well as taxation and foreign exchange issues in Tianjin since its liberation six months ago. Subsequently, the business relations of various newspapers were discussed. The meeting adjourned at 6:00 PM.

### 平津新闻工作者会议　在津举行三次会议　黄敬市长出席报告

1949-07-29
第2版()
专栏：

　　平津新闻工作者会议
  　在津举行三次会议
  　黄敬市长出席报告
    【天津电】平津新闻工作会议，于昨（二十八日）下午三时在天津市政府交际处举行第三次会议。到平津各报社、通讯社、广播电台等单位新闻工作者三十七人。会中邀请天津市黄敬市长报告天津解放半年来的工商业变化、劳资关系的处理、公营和私营工商业的配合解决原料与销路的情形、以及税收、外汇等问题。嗣即讨论各报的业务关系。至六时散会。
