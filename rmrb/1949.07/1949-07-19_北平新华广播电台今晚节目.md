Title: Tonight's Program on Beiping Xinhua Broadcasting Station
Author:
Date: 1949-07-19
Page: 1

[This News] Tonight's lecture program on Beiping Xinhua Broadcasting Station from 19:50 to 20:10 will broadcast a recording of the artist Jiang Feng's lecture: "The Art Movement in the Liberated Areas." From 19:30 to 19:50, the All-China Federation of Trade Unions will present "Workers' Time," broadcasting: (1) An introduction to the National Trade Union Work Conference; (2) An introduction to a short commentary from the second issue of the Workers' Daily - "Trade Unions Must Stand Firm on the Working Class's Position and Beware of Capitalists' Instigation." The arts and entertainment program from 22:20 to 22:50 will feature an introduction to Korean music.

### 北平新华广播电台今晚节目

1949-07-19
第1版()
专栏：

    北平新华广播电台今晚节目
    【本报讯】北平新华广播电台今晚十九点五十分到二十点十分演讲节目，播送美术家江丰讲：“解放区的美术运动”录音。十九点三十分到十九点五十分，由中华全国总工会主办的职工时间，播送：（一）介绍全国工会工作会议；（二）介绍工人日报第二期的一篇短评——工会要站稳工人阶级立场，当心资本家的挑拨。二十二点二十分到二十二点五十分文艺节目，朝鲜音乐介绍。
