Title: Beiping Post Office Expands Parcel Post Area
Author:
Date: 1949-07-28
Page: 2

　　Beiping Post Office
  　Expands Parcel Post Area
    [Beiping City News] The Beiping Postal Administration and its subordinate bureaus will begin accepting ordinary parcels destined for the following areas within the Shanghai postal district starting July 27th: Tianjiaqiao, Qingpu, Zhujiajiao, Jinshan, (Zhu Jing), Minhang, Chuansha Nanhui, Zhangyan, Dongxian (Nanqiao), Jiangwan, Wusong, Baoshan, Gaoqiao Town, Jiading, Huaihe, Taicang, Xinzhuang, Tuanpu, Dachang, Nanbu Town, Shatou, Anting, Zhang Liantang, Qiaozhen, Huangdu, Luodian, Xi Dun, Datuan, Sijing, Miaozhen, Chongming, Haimen, Qidong; and the following areas within the Zhejiang postal district: Yongjia (Wenzhou), Pingyang, Yueqing, Ruian, Wenling, Chumen, Yuhuan, Gu'aotou, Qingtian, Huian, Weiping, Suian, Gangkou, Jiande. They will also accept insured parcels destined for the following areas in Jiangsu Province: Nanjing, Puzhen, Pukou, Tangshan Town, Shangxinhe, Wuxi, Zhenjiang, Suzhou, Danyang, Changzhou, Jiangyin, Jintan, Yixing, Qi Shuwei, Liyang, Wujiang, Changshu, Shengze, Xu Shuguan, Xiashu, Xinfeng, Gaozi, Longze, Heqiao, Benniu, Henglin, Zhenze, Lobei, Rongxiang.
  　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　(Beiping Postal Administration)

### 北平邮局　扩大通邮包地区

1949-07-28
第2版()
专栏：

　　北平邮局
  　扩大通邮包地区
    【平市讯】北平邮政管理局及所管辖各局自七月二十七日起开始收寄上海邮区之田家桥，青浦，朱家角，金山，（洙泾），闵行，川沙南汇，张堰，东贤（南桥），江湾，吴淞，宝山，高桥镇，嘉定，怀河，太仓，莘庄，团浦，大场，南堡镇，沙头，安亭，章练塘，桥镇，黄渡，罗店，茜墩，大团，泗泾，庙镇，崇明，海门，启东，浙江邮区之永嘉（温州），平阳，乐清，瑞安，温岭，楚门，玉环，古鳌头，青田，会安，威坪，遂安，港口，建德等地普通包裹。并收寄江苏省南京，浦镇，浦口，汤山镇，上新河，无锡，镇江，苏州，丹阳，常州，江阴，金坛，宜兴，戚墅埝，溧阳，吴江，常熟，盛泽，许墅关，下蜀，辛丰，高资，龙泽，和桥，奔牛，横林，震泽，洛北，荣巷等地保价包裹。
  　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　（北平邮政管理局）
