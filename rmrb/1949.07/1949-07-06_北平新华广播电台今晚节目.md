Title: Tonight's Program on Beiping Xinhua Radio Station
Author:
Date: 1949-07-06
Page: 1

　　Beiping Xinhua Radio Station Tonight's Program
    [Our Newspaper] Tonight from 19:50 to 20:10, the speech program will feature Zhou Enlai, Deputy Director of the Standing Committee of the Preparatory Committee of the New Political Consultative Conference, reading the declaration of various parties and groups of the Preparatory Committee of the New Political Consultative Conference in commemoration of the 12th anniversary of the "July 7th" Anti-Japanese War (Beiping People's Broadcasting Station will broadcast simultaneously). From 19:30 to 19:50, the youth program will broadcast "Youths Who Persisted in Guerrilla Warfare Behind Enemy Lines During the War of Resistance." From 22:20 to 22:50, the cultural program will feature the People's Art Troupe Military Band broadcasting "Soviet Military Music."

### 北平新华广播电台今晚节目

1949-07-06
第1版()
专栏：

　　北平新华广播电台今晚节目
    【本报讯】北平新华广播电台今晚十九点五十分到二十点十分演讲节目，新政治协商会议筹备会常务委员会副主任周恩来宣读新政治协商会议筹备会各党派各团体为纪念“七七”抗日战争十二周年宣言（北平人民广播电台同时转播）。十九点三十分到十九点五十分青年时间，播送“在抗战中坚持敌后斗争的青年们”。二十二点二十分到二十二点五十分文艺节目，由人民文工团军乐队播送“苏联军乐”。
