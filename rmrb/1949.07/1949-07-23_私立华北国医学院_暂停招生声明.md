Title: Private North China College of Chinese Medicine Suspends Enrollment Announcement
Author:
Date: 1949-07-23
Page: 1

Private North China College of Chinese Medicine
  　Suspension of Enrollment Announcement
    Our college is private and has not yet completed registration. Enrollment is suspended until the registration is approved. The enrollment advertisement published on the 17th of this month is hereby withdrawn. We hereby declare.

### 私立华北国医学院　暂停招生声明

1949-07-23
第1版()
专栏：

　　私立华北国医学院
  　暂停招生声明
    本院系私立，尚未办理立案，在立案未批准前暂停招生。本月十七日所登招生广告自行撤销，特此声明。
