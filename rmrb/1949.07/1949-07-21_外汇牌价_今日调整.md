Title: Foreign Exchange Rates Adjusted Today
Author:
Date: 1949-07-21
Page: 2
Economic News

　　Foreign Exchange Rates Adjusted Today
　　[This newspaper's dispatch] The Bank of China's foreign exchange rates will be revised as follows starting today: US dollar at 1 to 1800 yuan (People's currency, same below), British pound at 1 to 4800 yuan, and Hong Kong dollar at 1 to 270 yuan.

### 外汇牌价　今日调整

1949-07-21
第2版()
专栏：经济新闻

　　外汇牌价　今日调整
    【本报讯】中国银行外汇牌价今日起改订如下：美钞一比一八○○元（人民券，下同），英镑一比四八○○元，港币一比二七○元。
