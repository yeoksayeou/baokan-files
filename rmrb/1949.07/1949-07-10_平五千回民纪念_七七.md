Title: Five Thousand Hui People in Beiping Commemorate the "July 7th Incident"
Author:
Date: 1949-07-10
Page: 1

　　Beiping Xinhua Broadcasting Station broadcast: Beiping, July 9th -- More than five thousand Hui people in Beiping held a commemorative meeting for the "July 7th Incident" on the afternoon of the 5th. Yang Jingren, the Hui representative of the Preparatory Committee of the New Political Consultative Conference, pointed out in his speech at the meeting: "The liberation of the Hui nationality is inseparable from the liberation of the people of the whole country." He said: "Only with the Communist Party can all ethnic groups have the hope of true liberation; history proves that if the Hui nationality wants complete liberation, it must follow the Communist Party and follow the people's leader, Chairman Mao."

### 平五千回民纪念“七七”

1949-07-10
第1版()
专栏：

　　平五千回民纪念“七七”
    【北平新华广播电台广播】北平九日消息：北平市五千多回民，五日下午举行纪念“七七”大会。新政协筹备会回族代表杨静仁，在大会讲话，指出：“回回民族的解放，和全国人民的解放是分不开的。”他说：“有了共产党，各民族才有了真正解放的希望；历史证明，回回民族要求彻底解放，必须跟着共产党走，跟着人民领袖毛主席走。”
