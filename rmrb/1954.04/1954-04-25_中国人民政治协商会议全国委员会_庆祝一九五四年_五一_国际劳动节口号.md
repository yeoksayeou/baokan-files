Title: Slogans of the National Committee of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference in Celebration of the 1954 "May Day" International Labor Day
Author:
Date: 1954-04-25
Page: 1

    Slogans of the National Committee of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference in Celebration of the 1954 "May Day" International Labor Day
    I. Long live the "May Day" International Labor Day, the festival of solidarity for the working people of the world!
    II. Long live world peace!
    III. Resolve all international disputes through peaceful negotiation! All countries that desire peace and people who love peace, unite to strive for a further relaxation of international tensions!
    IV. Safeguard peace and security in Asia! Restore peace in Indochina, oppose U.S. imperialism's intervention and expansion of the war in Indochina! Strive for a peaceful resolution of the Korean question, oppose U.S. imperialism's conspiracies to undermine peace and unification in Korea! Oppose U.S. imperialism's organization of military blocs and establishment of a network of military bases in Asia!
    V. Prohibit atomic weapons, hydrogen weapons, bacteriological weapons and other weapons for mass destruction! Substantially reduce the armaments and armed forces of all countries!
    VI. Develop friendly cooperation and economic and trade relations between the people of all countries!
    VII. Pay tribute to the people of all countries who are fighting for peace and democracy! Long live the united world camp of peace, democracy and socialism headed by the Soviet Union!
    VIII. Pay tribute to the great Soviet people who are victoriously carrying out communist construction! Long live the Soviet Union, the model for our construction and the strong bulwark of world peace! The great friendship between the Chinese and Soviet peoples is the most powerful guarantee for safeguarding peace in the Far East and the world. Long live the unbreakable friendly alliance between China and the Soviet Union!
    IX. Pay tribute to the working people of all People's Democratic countries who are victoriously carrying out socialist construction!
    X. Pay tribute to the heroic Korean people who have achieved great victory in the war against aggression! Wish the Korean people new achievements in the cause of restoring and developing the national economy and striving for the peaceful reunification of the motherland!
    XI. Pay tribute to the people of Vietnam, Cambodia and Laos who are fighting heroically against imperialist aggression and for national independence!
    XII. Pay tribute to the Japanese people who are fighting for national independence and democratic freedom! Oppose U.S. imperialism's transformation of Japan into a military base and the revival of Japanese militarist forces! Develop trade relations and cultural exchanges between the Chinese and Japanese peoples!
    XIII. Pay tribute to the German people who are fighting for the establishment of a unified, independent, democratic and peaceful Germany! Oppose U.S. imperialism's policy of dividing Germany and reviving militarist forces in West Germany! Consolidate peace and security in Europe!
    XIV. Pay tribute to the people of colonies and dependent countries who are fighting for national independence and freedom!
    XV. Long live the friendship between the Chinese people and the people of Great Britain, the United States and France in the struggle to ease international tensions and safeguard peace in the Far East and the world!
    XVI. Long live the friendship between the Chinese people and the people of India, Indonesia, Burma, Pakistan and other Asian countries!
    XVII. Long live the friendship of the people of the Pacific region in their struggle for peace!
    XVIII. Celebrate the great victory of the Chinese people's struggle to resist U.S. aggression and aid Korea! Pay tribute to the glorious Chinese People's Volunteers who have made great contributions in the struggle to resist U.S. aggression and aid Korea!
    Commanders and fighters of the Chinese People's Volunteers! Resolutely abide by the Korean Armistice Agreement, maintain high vigilance, and prevent any enemy attacks and sabotage activities!
    XIX. Oppose U.S. imperialism's occupation of our territory of Taiwan!
    XX. Pay tribute to the People's Liberation Army, Navy and Air Force, public security forces, people's police and militia who are defending the security of the motherland and socialist construction!
    Commanders and fighters of the Chinese People's Liberation Army! Strive to learn from the advanced military science and technology of the Soviet Union, improve political and cultural standards, strengthen physical fitness, improve discipline, and strive to build a modern national defense force!
    XXI. The people of the whole country unite and strive to realize the state's general task during the transition period! Strive to gradually realize the state's socialist industrialization and the socialist transformation of agriculture, handicrafts, and capitalist industry and commerce!
    XXII. Study the theories of Marxism-Leninism, learn from the experience of socialist construction in the Soviet Union, enhance socialist consciousness, and strive for the victory of socialist construction and socialist transformation!
    XXIII. The people of the whole country unite more closely around the Chinese Communist Party and the Central People's Government, further consolidate the worker-peasant alliance, further consolidate the great unity of the people of the whole country, unite as one, work hard, and build our country into a great socialist country!
    XXIV. The people of the whole country unite and strive to realize the basic tasks of the state's first five-year plan! Concentrate major forces on developing heavy industry, establishing the foundation for national industrialization and national defense modernization; correspondingly cultivate construction talent, develop the transportation industry, light industry, agriculture and commerce; systematically promote the cooperativization of agriculture and handicrafts, continue to transform capitalist industry and commerce, correctly bring into play the positive role of individual agriculture, handicrafts and capitalist industry and commerce; ensure that the proportion of socialist components in the national economy steadily increases, and ensure that the people's material and cultural living standards are gradually improved on the basis of developing production!
    XXV. The people of the whole country continue to carry forward the glorious tradition of hard struggle, increase production, practice strict economy, obey the state plan, and ensure the gradual realization of the state's socialist industrialization!
    XXVI. The people of the whole country unite and strive to comprehensively fulfill and over fulfill the state construction plan for 1954!
    XXVII. All workers, male and female, engineering and technical personnel, and staff members! Strengthen labor discipline, launch labor competitions, launch technical innovation movements, and increase labor productivity to create more wealth for society!
    XXVIII. Workers, engineering and technical personnel, and staff members of factories and mines! Strive to increase new products, increase the quantity and quality of products, save raw materials and reduce costs!
    XXIX. Workers, engineering and technical personnel and staff members in basic construction departments! Further strengthen the planning and organization of basic construction work, strengthen design work and exploration work, strengthen construction management, ensure project quality, accelerate project progress, and reduce construction and installation costs!
    XXX. Workers, technicians and staff in geological exploration departments! Strive to complete exploration tasks, improve exploration quality, expand geological surveys in a planned way, strengthen scientific research, and strive to rapidly meet the country's needs for resources!
    XXXI. Workers, engineers, technicians and staff in the transport sector! Strive to improve transport, speed up the turnover of vehicles and vessels! Improve transport quality, reduce transport costs, build new lines, strengthen old lines, and develop the transport industry!
    XXXII. Leaders of state-owned factories, mines, enterprises, basic construction and transportation sectors! Rely on the masses of workers, engineering and technical personnel to improve the technical level and management level of enterprises; strengthen planning management, implement job plans, establish and improve the responsibility system; strengthen technical leadership, strengthen safety production measures, strengthen financial management, and ensure comprehensive and balanced completion and over fulfillment of the various indicators of the national plan!
    XXXIII. Trade union workers! Unite all workers, engineering and technical personnel and staff with socialist ideas, organize labor competitions, organize rationalization proposals, promote advanced experience, strengthen labor discipline, strengthen safety and health work in industrial and mining transportation enterprises and construction sites, care for workers' lives, and help workers improve their political, cultural and technical levels to ensure the completion of the national economic construction plan!
    XXXIV. Rural work cadres and all comrades who serve the countryside! Continuously raise the socialist consciousness of the peasant masses; actively and steadily develop agricultural producers' cooperatives and mutual aid teams, actively develop credit cooperation, strive to improve the business of supply and marketing cooperatives, patiently and enthusiastically help individual farmers, and promote the socialist transformation of agriculture; summarize production experience, promote increased production measures, care for peasants' lives, and consolidate the worker-peasant alliance!
    XXXV. Male and female farmers! Participate in mutual aid and cooperative organizations, improve farming techniques, increase yield per unit area, increase production of grain, cotton, oil crops and other cash crops according to the state's plan, actively sell surplus grain, cotton and other agricultural and sideline products to the state according to the state's needs, supply more meat, eggs and vegetables, and strive to support the country's industrial construction and realize the socialist transformation of agriculture!
    XXXVI. Members of agricultural producers' cooperatives! Members of mutual aid teams! Strengthen unity, work actively, improve production efficiency, and set an example of patriotism and increased production! Further consolidate and improve mutual aid and cooperative organizations, and take the initiative to unite and help individual farmers!
    XXXVII. Workers and staff of state farms and tractor stations! Learn agricultural machinery technology well, improve business management, increase yield per unit area, reduce production costs, help farmers improve production technology, and give full play to the exemplary role of socialist agriculture!
    Agricultural science and technology workers! Go deep into the countryside, combine your scientific and technological knowledge with the production experience of farmers, and help farmers develop production!
    XXXVIII. Workers, engineers, technicians, staff and laborers participating in water conservancy work! Complete water conservancy projects, develop farmland water conservancy, do a good job in drought prevention, flood control, and waterlogging prevention, overcome disasters, and strive for a bumper harvest in agriculture and the full utilization of water conservancy resources!
    XXXIX. Forestry workers, technicians and forest farmers! Improve harvesting and transportation operations, ensure the supply of timber, and strive to over fulfill the production plan! Better protect forests and afforest, and strive to develop the country's afforestation cause!
    XL. Male and female herders! Workers of state-owned ranches! Technicians and staff! Improve livestock breeding methods, improve livestock breeds, prevent and control livestock diseases, protect and cultivate grasslands, gradually develop mutual aid and cooperation, and strive to increase livestock and increase livestock products!
    XLI. Fishery workers and male and female fishermen! Improve fishing and fish farming methods, gradually develop mutual aid and cooperation, and strive to develop fishery production!
    XLII. Salt workers and male and female salt farmers! Strive to increase salt production and improve salt quality!
    XLIII. Male and female boat people! Strive to develop water transport and facilitate the exchange of goods!
    XLIV. Male and female handicraft workers and craftsmen! Gradually develop handicraft cooperation according to the principle of voluntariness, respect teachers and love apprentices, improve technology, improve management, improve quality, reduce costs, and strive to supply the needs of the people!
    XLV. Workers in state-owned commerce, workers in supply and marketing cooperatives and consumer cooperatives! Vigorously carry out the exchange of goods between urban and rural areas, improve business management, reduce the cost of commodity circulation, and better serve production and consumers!
    XLVI. Clerks of private commercial stores! Unite with capitalists, improve business under the leadership of state-owned commerce, and strive to promote the exchange of goods between urban and rural areas!
    XLVII. Staff members of state organs! Improve political consciousness and professional skills, implement state policies, abide by state laws and discipline, abide by work systems, work actively, continuously improve work efficiency, constantly maintain and strengthen close ties with the masses of people, promote the spirit of criticism and self-criticism, constantly overcome shortcomings and errors in work, oppose bureaucratic work styles, guard against arrogance and impatience, and serve the people wholeheartedly and serve socialist construction!
    XLVIII. Educators! Improve the quality of teaching work, educate youth and children with advanced scientific knowledge, socialist ideas, love of labor and a spirit of abiding by discipline, and strive to cultivate builders of socialist society!
    Childcare workers! Care for children and strive to improve childcare work!
    XLIX. Scientific workers! Learn from advanced Soviet science, strengthen the connection between research work and production, actively train cadres, popularize scientific knowledge, and give play to the role of scientific work in national construction!
    L. Medical and health workers! Strive to do a good job in urban and industrial and mining sanitation work, go deep into the countryside to prevent and treat diseases for the masses, improve women's and children's health care, strengthen the unity of new and old medicine, develop mass health undertakings, and strive to improve people's health!
    Sports workers! Develop mass sports movements and strive to improve people's health!
    LI. Literary and art workers! Go deep into real life, improve artistic accomplishment, strive for artistic practice, and create more excellent literary and artistic works that educate the people with socialist spirit!
    LII. Young men and women! Students! Respond to Chairman Mao's call for "good health, good study, and good work," exercise, learn scientific knowledge, improve political standards, strengthen labor concepts, consciously abide by discipline, and better serve production and serve the motherland!
    LIII. Women across the country! Improve political awareness, improve production and work capacity, actively participate in national construction undertakings, and strive to further realize women's equal rights with men in political, economic, cultural, social and family life!
    LIV. Industrialists and businessmen! Obey the leadership of the People's Government and the state-owned economy, strive to manage enterprises that are beneficial to the national economy and the people's livelihood, do a good job in the business entrusted by the state and carry out the exchange of goods between urban and rural areas; abide by state laws and regulations, actively improve business management, and gradually embark on the track of state capitalism and state planning!
    LV. Religious believers! Carry forward the patriotic spirit and actively participate in the struggle against imperialist aggression and to defend peace!
    LVI. Members of various democratic parties and people's organizations! Actively participate in national construction work and give play to the role of the people's democratic united front in socialist construction and socialist transformation!
    LVII. Strengthen the unity, friendship and mutual assistance between all nationalities, and strive together to oppose imperialist aggression and build a great motherland! Promote national regional autonomy, strive to develop the economy, culture, education and medical and health work in minority areas, and gradually improve the lives of the people of all nationalities!
    LVIII. Overseas Chinese unite, carry forward the spirit of mutual assistance and mutual support, safeguard their legitimate rights and interests, and love the motherland!
    LIX. Complete elections at all levels, hold people's congresses at all local levels well, greet the National People's Congress, further consolidate the people's democratic system, and promote the country's socialist construction!
    LX. Long live the great unity of the people of the whole country!
    LXI. Long live the great unity of the people of the world!
    LXII. Long live the great and invincible Marxist-Leninist doctrine!
    LXIII. Long live the great People's Republic of China!
    LXIV. Long live the great Chinese Communist Party!
    LXV. Long live Chairman Mao Zedong, the great leader of the Chinese people!

### 中国人民政治协商会议全国委员会  庆祝一九五四年“五一”国际劳动节口号

1954-04-25
第1版()
专栏：

    中国人民政治协商会议全国委员会
    庆祝一九五四年“五一”国际劳动节口号
    一、全世界劳动人民团结的节日——“五一”国际劳动节万岁！
    二、世界和平万岁！
    三、用和平协商的方式解决一切国际争端！一切愿意和平的国家和爱好和平的人民联合起来，争取进一步缓和国际紧张局势！
    四、维护亚洲的和平和安全！恢复印度支那的和平，反对美帝国主义干涉和扩大印度支那战争！争取朝鲜问题的和平解决，反对美帝国主义破坏朝鲜和平和统一的阴谋！反对美帝国主义在亚洲组织军事集团和建立军事基地网！
    五、禁止原子武器、氢武器、细菌武器和其他大规模毁灭性武器！大量裁减各国的军备和武装部队！
    六、发展各国人民间的友好合作和经济贸易关系！
    七、向世界各国为和平、民主而奋斗的人民致敬！以苏联为首的团结一致的世界和平民主社会主义阵营万岁！
    八、向正在胜利地进行共产主义建设的伟大的苏联人民致敬！我国建设的榜样、世界和平的坚强堡垒苏联万岁！中苏两国人民的伟大友谊是保卫远东及世界和平的最有力的保证。中国和苏联的牢不可破的友好同盟万岁！
    九、向正在胜利地进行社会主义建设的各人民民主国家的劳动人民致敬！
    十、向在反侵略战争中取得伟大胜利的英雄的朝鲜人民致敬！祝朝鲜人民在恢复和发展国民经济及争取祖国和平统一的事业中得到新的成就！
    十一、向反对帝国主义侵略、争取民族独立而英勇斗争的越南、高棉和寮国的人民致敬！
    十二、向争取民族独立和民主自由的日本人民致敬！反对美帝国主义把日本变为军事基地和复活日本军国主义势力！发展中日两国人民之间的贸易关系和文化交流！
    十三、向为争取建立统一、独立、民主、和平的德国而奋斗的德国人民致敬！反对美帝国主义分裂德国并使西德军国主义势力复活的政策！巩固欧洲的和平与安全！
    十四、向争取民族独立和自由的殖民地和附属国的人民致敬！
    十五、中国人民和英、美、法三国人民在为缓和国际紧张局势、维护远东和世界和平而斗争中的友谊万岁！
    十六、中国人民和印度、印度尼西亚、缅甸、巴基斯坦及其他亚洲各国人民的友谊万岁！
    十七、太平洋区域各国人民在为和平事业而斗争中的友谊万岁！
    十八、庆祝中国人民抗美援朝斗争的伟大胜利！向在抗美援朝斗争中建立了伟大功勋的光荣的中国人民志愿军致敬！
    中国人民志愿军的指挥员和战斗员们！坚决遵守朝鲜停战协定，提高警惕，防止敌人的任何袭击和破坏活动！
    十九、反对美帝国主义侵占我国领土台湾！
    二○、向保卫祖国安全和社会主义建设的中国人民解放军陆、海、空军，公安部队，人民警察和民兵致敬！
    中国人民解放军的指挥员和战斗员们！努力学习苏联先进军事科学和技术，提高政治和文化水平，增强体质，提高纪律，为建设现代化的国防军而奋斗！
    二一、全国人民一致努力，为实现国家在过渡时期的总任务而奋斗！为逐步实现国家的社会主义工业化，逐步实现国家对农业、对手工业和对资本主义工商业的社会主义改造而奋斗！
    二二、学习马克思列宁主义的理论，学习苏联社会主义建设的经验，提高社会主义的觉悟，争取社会主义建设和社会主义改造的胜利！
    二三、全国人民更加紧密地团结在中国共产党和中央人民政府周围，进一步巩固工农联盟，进一步巩固全国人民的大团结，万众一心，努力奋斗，把我们的国家建设成为一个伟大的社会主义国家！
    二四、全国人民一致努力，为实现国家第一个五年计划的基本任务而奋斗！集中主要力量发展重工业，建立国家工业化和国防现代化的基础；相应地培养建设人材，发展交通运输业、轻工业、农业和商业；有步骤地促进农业、手工业的合作化，继续进行对资本主义工商业的改造，正确地发挥个体农业、手工业和资本主义工商业的积极作用；保证国民经济中社会主义成份的比重稳步增长，保证在发展生产的基础上逐步提高人民物质生活和文化生活的水平！
    二五、全国人民继续发扬艰苦奋斗的光荣传统，增加生产，厉行节约，服从国家计划，保证逐步实现国家的社会主义工业化！
    二六、全国人民一致努力，争取全面完成和超额完成一九五四年的国家建设计划！
    二七、全体男女工人们、工程技术人员们和职员们！加强劳动纪律，开展劳动竞赛，开展技术革新运动，提高劳动生产率，为社会创造更多的财富！
    二八、工厂矿山的工人们、工程技术人员们和职员们！为努力增加新产品、提高产品数量和质量、节约原料材料和降低成本而奋斗！
    二九、基本建设部门的工人们、工程技术人员
  和职员们！进一步加强基本建设工作的计划性和组织性，加强设计工作和勘察工作，加强施工管理，保证工程质量，加速工程进度，降低建筑和安装的成本！
    三○、地质勘探部门的工人们、技术人员们和职员们！努力完成勘探任务，提高勘探质量，有计划地扩大地质普查，加强科学研究，为迅速满足国家建设的资源需要而奋斗！
    三一、交通运输部门的工人们、工程技术人员们和职员们！努力改进交通运输工作，加速车辆和船舶的周转！提高运输质量，降低运输成本，建设新线路，加强旧线路，发展交通运输事业！

    三三、工会工作者们！以社会主义思想团结全体工人、工程技术人员和职员，组织劳动竞赛，组织合理化建议，推广先进经验，加强劳动纪律，加强工矿交通企业和工地的安全卫生工作，关心工人生活，帮助工人提高政治、文化和技术水平，保证完成国家经济建设计划！
    三四、农村工作干部和一切为农村服务的同志们！不断提高农民群众的社会主义觉悟；积极地、稳步地发展农业生产合作社和互助组，积极发展信用合作，努力改进供销合作社的业务，耐心地热情地帮助单干农民，促进农业的社会主义改造；总结生产经验，推广增产措施，关心农民生活，巩固工农联盟！

    三六、农业生产合作社社员们！互助组的组员们！加强团结，积极劳动，提高生产效率，树立爱国增产的先进榜样！进一步巩固和提高互助合作组织，主动团结和帮助单干农民！

    农业科学技术工作者们！深入农村，把自己的科学技术知识和农民的生产经验结合起来，帮助农民发展生产！
    三八、参加水利工作的工人们、工程技术人员们、职员们和民工们！完成水利工程，发展农田水利，做好防旱、防洪、防涝的工作，战胜灾害，为争取农业的丰收和充分利用水利资源而努力！
    三九、林业工人们、技术人员们和林农们！改进采伐运输作业，保证木材供应，争取超额完成生产计划！更好地护林造林，为发展国家造林事业而努力！
    四○、男女牧民们！国营牧场的工人们！技术人员们和职员们！改善牲畜饲养方法，改良牲畜品种，防治牲畜疾病，保护和培育草原，逐步发展互助合作，为增殖牲畜和增加畜产品而努力！
    四一、渔业工人们和男女渔民们！改进捕鱼和养鱼方法，逐步发展互助合作，为发展渔业生产而努力！
    四二、盐业工人们和男女盐民们！为增加盐的产量、提高盐的质量而努力！
