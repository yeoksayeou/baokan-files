Title: Korean Delegation Visits Taiyuan
Author:
Date: 1954-04-05
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Taiyuan, April 4th: A portion of the Korean People's Delegation to China, led by head of delegation Kim Ung-gi, and accompanying art troupe members, arrived in Taiyuan City at 4:00 PM on the 3rd, accompanied by Ding Xilin, Vice Minister of the Ministry of Culture of the Central People's Government.

Those welcoming them at the station included Wang Shiying, Vice Chairman of the Shanxi Provincial Consultative Committee; Shi Jiyan, Vice Chairman of the Shanxi Branch of the Resist America, Aid Korea Association; Chi Biqing, Standing Committee Member of the Shanxi Provincial Committee of the Chinese Communist Party; Zeng Baotang, Deputy Commander of the Shanxi Military Region of the Chinese People's Liberation Army; Wei Fengqi, Secretary-General of the Shanxi Provincial People's Government; Cao Huanwen, Vice Chairman of the Taiyuan City Consultative Committee; Li Xiaolin, Vice Chairman of the Taiyuan City Branch of the Resist America, Aid Korea Association; Ma Guishu, Deputy Secretary of the Taiyuan City Committee of the Chinese Communist Party; Yue Weifan, Deputy Mayor of the Taiyuan City People's Government, and 1,500 representatives from all sectors of the people.

As the special train carrying the delegation slowly entered the station, the solemn military music and the cheers of over a thousand welcomers resounded on the platform. The delegation and art troupe members received bouquets presented by 260 young female students amidst applause and cheers. Yang Lijie, a female student from Shanxi Normal College who had participated in the comfort delegation to Korea, warmly embraced Choi Bun-ok, a female tank driver and member of the delegation. The Shanxi Provincial People's Broadcasting Station also broadcasted the news of the distinguished guests' arrival to the people of the entire province.

A grand welcoming rally was held on the station square. Wang Shiying, Vice Chairman of the Shanxi Provincial Consultative Committee, delivered a speech on behalf of the people of the entire province, extending a warm welcome to the delegation. Head of Delegation Kim Ung-gi also spoke at the welcoming rally.

In the evening, the delegation and art troupe members attended a welcoming banquet.

### 朝鲜访华团到太原

1954-04-05
第1版()
专栏：

    朝鲜访华团到太原
    【新华社太原四日电】以金应基团长为首的朝鲜人民访华代表团部分团员和随行艺术团人员，在中央人民政府文化部副部长丁西林陪同下，三日下午四时到达太原市。
    到车站欢迎的，有山西省协商委员会副主席王世英，山西省抗美援朝分会副主席史纪言，中共山西省委常务委员池必卿，中国人民解放军山西军区副司令员曾保堂，山西省人民政府秘书长卫逢祺，太原市协商委员会副主席曹焕文，太原市抗美援朝分会副主席李晓林，中共太原市委副书记麻贵书，太原市人民政府副市长岳维藩和各界人民代表共一千五百人。
    当载着代表团的专车徐徐进站时，月台上庄严的军乐声和一千多欢迎者的欢呼声响成一片。代表团和艺术团人员在掌声和欢呼声中接受了二百六十个青年女学生的献花。曾经参加赴朝慰问团去过朝鲜的山西师范学院女学生杨立杰和代表团团员女坦克司机崔粉玉热烈拥抱。山西省人民广播电台也将贵宾们光临的消息传送给全省人民。
    车站广场上举行了隆重的欢迎大会。山西省协商委员会副主席王世英代表全省人民致词，对代表团表示热烈欢迎。金应基团长也在欢迎会上讲了话。
    晚间，代表团和艺术团人员出席了欢迎晚会。
