Title: Korean People's Delegation to China warmly welcomed in Xi'an
Author:
Date: 1954-04-09
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Xi'an: Part of the Korean People's Delegation to China and members of the art troupe accompanying the delegation, led by Head of Delegation Kim Ung-gi and accompanied by Ding Xilin, Deputy Minister of Culture of the Central People's Government, arrived in Xi'an at 9:40 AM on the 8th. Xu Zhe, Counselor of the Embassy of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea in China, also accompanied them. Those who went to the station to welcome them included the vice-chairmen of the Northwest, Shaanxi Province, and Xi'an City branches of the Resist-America Aid-Korea General Federation, heads of the Northwest Bureau of the CPC Central Committee, the Northwest Administrative Committee, and the Shaanxi Province and Xi'an City People's Governments, representatives of the local garrison, responsible persons of various democratic parties and people's organizations, Losang, Director of the Panchen Lama's Office in Xi'an, Liu Jiyao, combat hero of the Taking of Mount Hua, Liu Yunting, model family member of a volunteer soldier who went to Korea with the Third Delegation to Comfort the Korean People, and representatives from all ethnic groups and all walks of life, totaling more than 2,000 people.

    After the delegation members alighted from the train, the welcoming people stepped forward to shake hands warmly, and 260 Young Pioneers presented flowers to the delegation members in salute. A storm of applause and cheers resounded.

    Subsequently, a grand welcoming ceremony was held at the Xi'an Railway Station by people from all walks of life in Xi'an. Ke Zhongping, Vice Chairman of the Northwest Resist-America Aid-Korea General Federation, extended a warm welcome to the Korean people's representatives on behalf of the people of all ethnic groups in the Northwest. Amidst warm applause, Head of Delegation Kim Ung-gi delivered a speech, saying: We feel infinitely honored to be able to visit the Northwest region, which became the central base area during the Chinese Communist Party's leadership of the War of Resistance Against Japan. He also expressed his gratitude for the welcome from the people of Xi'an.

    That afternoon, the delegation and art troupe members, accompanied by Fang Zhongru, Mayor of Xi'an City, and others, toured the city of Xi'an and the Drum Tower, a Ming Dynasty building.

    That evening, the Northwest Resist-America Aid-Korea General Federation and three other organizations jointly hosted a banquet at the People's Mansion to entertain the distinguished guests.

### 朝鲜人民访华代表团在西安受到热烈欢迎

1954-04-09
第1版()
专栏：

    朝鲜人民访华代表团在西安受到热烈欢迎
    据新华社西安电：朝鲜人民访华代表团部分团员和随代表团前来的艺术团人员，在金应基团长率领下，由中央人民政府文化部副部长丁西林陪同，在八日上午九点四十分到达西安。朝鲜民主主义人民共和国驻我国大使馆参赞徐哲也陪同前来。前往车站欢迎的有西北、陕西省、西安市抗美援朝总分会、分会副主席，中共中央西北局、西北行政委员会、陕西省和西安市人民政府首长，当地驻军代表和各民主党派、各人民团体负责人，西藏班禅驻西安办事处处长罗桑，智取华山的战斗英雄刘吉尧，参加第三届赴朝慰问团赴朝慰问的模范志愿军家属刘云亭和各族各界代表共两千多人。
    代表团人员下车后，欢迎的人们上前热情握手，二百六十名少年先锋队队员向代表团人员献花致敬，暴风雨般的掌声和欢呼声响成一片。
    接着，在西安车站西安各界人民举行了盛大的欢迎仪式。西北抗美援朝总分会副主席柯仲平代表西北各族各界人民向朝鲜人民代表表示热烈的欢迎。在热烈的掌声中，金应基团长致词，他说：我们能够访问在中国共产党领导抗日战争时期中成为中心根据地的西北地区，感到无限的光荣。并对西安人民的欢迎表示感谢。
    当日下午，代表团及艺术团人员由西安市人民政府市长方仲如等陪同，游览了西安市容和明代的建筑物——鼓楼。
    当日晚，由西北抗美援朝总分会等四团体在人民大厦联合设宴招待贵宾。
