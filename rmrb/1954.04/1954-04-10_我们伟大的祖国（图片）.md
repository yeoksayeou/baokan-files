    Our Great Motherland
    The Fushun Power Plant expansion project is one of the important construction projects carried out in our country with the assistance of the Soviet Union. Upon completion of the entire project, it will supply more electricity to meet the needs of factories and mines in the Northeast region. This is the appearance of the Fushun Power Plant under expansion.

### 我们伟大的祖国（图片）

1954-04-10
第1版()
专栏：

    我们伟大的祖国
    抚顺发电厂扩建工程是我国在苏联援助下进行的重要建设工程之一。全部工程竣工后，将以更多的电力供应东北区厂矿的需用。这是正在扩建中的抚顺发电厂的外貌。
