Title: The Ministry of Agriculture Sends Working Groups to Inspect Spring Plowing in Various Regions
Author:
Date: 1954-04-04
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, April 2nd—The Ministry of Agriculture of the Central People's Government has dispatched working groups to various regions to inspect spring plowing work. The working groups, led by responsible cadres from the Ministry of Agriculture, began to travel in batches to North China, Northeast China, East China, Central South China, Southwest China, and Northwest China from mid-February. They, together with local agricultural department cadres, went deep into counties, districts, villages, as well as state-owned farms and agricultural production cooperatives to understand the situation of spring plowing production. At present, the working groups conducting inspections in Northeast China, Northwest China, Central South China, and Southwest China have respectively reported their preliminary findings to the Ministry of Agriculture. The working groups in various regions are continuing to actively assist local agricultural departments in promoting spring plowing work.

### 中央农业部派工作组检查各地春耕工作

1954-04-04
第1版()
专栏：

    中央农业部派工作组检查各地春耕工作
   【新华社二日讯】中央人民政府农业部派工作组分赴各地检查春耕工作。各工作组由农业部负责干部分别率领，从二月中旬开始陆续分批前往华北、东北、华东、中南、西南、西北等区，会同当地农业部门干部，深入县、区、村以及国营农场、农业生产合作社了解春耕生产情况。目前，在东北、西北、中南、西南等地进行检查的工作组，已分别将初步了解情况报告农业部。各地工作组仍在继续积极协助当地农业部门推动春耕工作。
