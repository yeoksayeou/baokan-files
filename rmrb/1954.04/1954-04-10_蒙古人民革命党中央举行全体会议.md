Title: Central Committee of the Mongolian People's Revolutionary Party Holds Plenary Session
Author:
Date: 1954-04-10
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Ulan Bator, April 6th (TASS): The Central Committee of the Mongolian People's Revolutionary Party recently held a plenary session. The plenary session discussed Tsedenbal's report on measures to further develop animal husbandry and increase livestock product productivity.

The plenary session also discussed organizational matters. As the Prime Minister of the Mongolian People's Republic, Tsedenbal has relatively heavy duties. At his request, the plenary session relieved him of his post as General Secretary of the Central Committee of the Mongolian People's Revolutionary Party and approved Damba as the First Secretary of the Central Committee.

### 蒙古人民革命党中央举行全体会议

1954-04-10
第4版()
专栏：

    蒙古人民革命党中央举行全体会议
    【新华社九日讯】据塔斯社乌兰巴托六日讯：蒙古人民革命党中央委员会最近举行了全体会议。全体会议讨论了泽登巴尔关于进一步发展畜牧业和提高牲畜产品生产率的措施的报告。
    全体会议还讨论了组织问题。泽登巴尔作为蒙古人民共和国总理，职务比较繁重，全体会议根据他的请求，解除了他所担任的蒙古人民革命党中央委员会总书记的职务，并批准达姆巴为中央委员会第一书记。
