Title: Indonesian People Support Resolutions of the Indonesian Communist Party Congress
Author:
Date: 1954-04-01
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Jakarta, 30th dispatch from TASS: The resolutions of the Fifth Congress of the Indonesian Communist Party (PKI) have received widespread response from the working people of Indonesia.

    A mass rally was held in Solo, the largest city in Central Java, a city that has always played a major role in the Indonesian people's national liberation movement. D.N. Aidit, General Secretary of the Central Committee of the Indonesian Communist Party, delivered a report on the work of the Party Congress at the meeting. He elaborated in particular on the significance of the upcoming parliamentary elections and called on the working people of Central Java to vote for the Communist Party and other democratic organizations.

    Aarons, the representative of the Australian Communist Party, also spoke at the meeting. He said that the Australian working people fully support Indonesia's claim to West New Guinea (West Irian).

    The大会 passed a resolution supporting the Indonesian Communist Party's program.

    Mass rallies were also held in Semarang, Bandung and other cities.

### 印尼人民拥护印尼共产党代表大会的决议

1954-04-01
第4版()
专栏：

    印尼人民拥护印尼共产党代表大会的决议
   【新华社三十日讯】塔斯社雅加达二十七日讯：印尼共产党第五次代表大会的决议获得印尼劳动人民广泛的响应。
    在爪哇中部最大的城市梭罗——这个在印度尼西亚人民民族解放运动中一向起着重大作用的城市，举行了一个群众大会。印尼共产党中央委员会总书记艾地在会上作了关于党代表大会的工作报告。他特别详细地论述了关于将要到来的议会选举的意义，并号召爪哇中部劳动人民投共产党和其他民主组织的票。
    在会上讲话的还有澳大利亚共产党代表阿隆斯。他说：澳大利亚劳动人民充分支持印度尼西亚对新畿内亚西部（西伊里安）的要求。
    大会通过了一项支持印尼共产党党纲的决议。
    三宝垄、万隆和其他城市也同样举行了群众大会。
