Title: Chinese and Korean Delegations Hold Separate Press Conferences
Author:
Date: 1954-04-29
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, Geneva, April 27th: The Delegation of the People's Republic of China held its first press conference for Chinese and foreign journalists at the Palais des Nations at 5:30 PM on the 27th. Huang Hua, the spokesperson for the Chinese Delegation, briefed the journalists present on the proceedings of the meeting on the 27th. He gave a particularly detailed account of the speech by Nam Il, Minister of Foreign Affairs and Chief Representative of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea delegation, and the proposal he put forward regarding the restoration of Korea's national unification and the holding of free all-Korea elections. Huang Hua also answered questions from the journalists.
    Xinhua News Agency, Geneva, April 27th: The Delegation of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea held a press conference at the Maison de la Presse here at 5:50 PM on the 27th. Kim Myong-gui, the spokesperson for the delegation, read out the proposal made by Foreign Minister Nam Il in his speech at the meeting on the 27th regarding the restoration of Korea's national unification and the holding of free all-Korea elections. This proposal has attracted great attention from journalists here. The spokesperson also answered questions from the journalists.

### 我国代表团和朝鲜代表团  分别举行记者招待会

1954-04-29
第1版()
专栏：

    我国代表团和朝鲜代表团
    分别举行记者招待会
    【新华社日内瓦二十七日电】中华人民共和国代表团二十七日下午五时三十分在国联大厦举行了对中国记者和外国记者的第一次招待会。中国代表团发言人黄华在会上向到会的记者介绍了二十七日会议进行情形。特别详细地介绍了朝鲜民主主义人民共和国代表团首席代表南日外务相的发言和他提出的关于恢复朝鲜的国家统一和举行全朝鲜自由选举的方案。黄华并回答了记者们提出的问题。
    【新华社日内瓦二十七日电】朝鲜民主主义人民共和国代表团二十七日下午五点五十分在此间记者之家举行记者招待会。代表团发言人金明龟宣读了南日外务相在二十七日会上发言所提出的关于恢复朝鲜的国家统一和举行全朝鲜自由选举的方案。这个方案引起此间记者极大的重视。发言人并答复了记者们提出的问题。
