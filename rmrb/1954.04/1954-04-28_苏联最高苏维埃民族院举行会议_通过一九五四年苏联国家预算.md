Title: Soviet of Nationalities of the Supreme Soviet of the USSR Holds Meeting, Passes 1954 Soviet National Budget
Author:
Date: 1954-04-28
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Moscow: According to TASS, the Soviet of Nationalities of the Supreme Soviet of the USSR held consecutive meetings on the morning of the 23rd and the afternoon of the 24th to discuss the 1954 Soviet national budget, and concluded the discussion on the 1954 Soviet national budget at the meeting on the 26th.

The Chairman of the Council of Ministers of the USSR, G.M. Malenkov, participated in the meeting on the 26th and delivered a lengthy speech. When he appeared on the rostrum, the entire venue erupted in thunderous applause. His speech was frequently interrupted by applause.

A total of thirty-five representatives from various Union Republics and Autonomous Republics of the USSR spoke at the meeting during the discussion of the 1954 Soviet national budget. The Minister of Finance of the USSR, Zverev, delivered a concluding speech. He pointed out that the representatives who spoke during the discussion unanimously approved the draft budget. During the discussion, the representatives also spoke of the shortcomings in the work of various sectors of the national economy and the Ministry of Finance, and proposed ways to eliminate these shortcomings and raise all activities to a higher level. Zverev said that the Council of Ministers of the USSR has reviewed the recommendations of the Budget Committee of the Soviet of Nationalities. The government agreed to the amendments proposed by the Budget Committee and considered it appropriate to adopt these amendments. Zverev then spoke about the suggestions made by the representatives during the discussion regarding increasing appropriations beyond the budgeted amounts for individual items. He pointed out that the Council of Ministers of the USSR will carefully consider these suggestions and adopt corresponding resolutions.

The meeting then adopted the 1954 Soviet national budget and the budget law. The budget revenue was 572.5423 billion rubles, and the expenditure was 562.8019 billion rubles, with revenue exceeding expenditure by more than 9.74 billion rubles.

The meeting of the Soviet of Nationalities also approved the decree of the Presidium of the Supreme Soviet of the USSR on transferring the Crimean Oblast of the Russian Federation to the Ukrainian Republic, as well as other decrees, and passed a law on the approval of these decrees.

The Soviet of Nationalities of the Supreme Soviet of the USSR is scheduled to hold a joint meeting with the Soviet of the Union on the 27th.

### 苏联最高苏维埃民族院举行会议  通过一九五四年苏联国家预算

1954-04-28
第1版()
专栏：

    苏联最高苏维埃民族院举行会议
    通过一九五四年苏联国家预算
    据新华社讯：据塔斯社莫斯科讯：苏联最高苏维埃民族院在二十三日上午和二十四日下午连续举行会议，讨论一九五四年苏联国家预算，并在二十六日会议上结束了关于一九五四年苏联国家预算的讨论。
    苏联部长会议主席格·马·马林科夫参加了二十六日的会议，并发表了长篇演说。当他在主席台上出现时，全场响起了暴风雨般的掌声。他的演说不断为掌声所打断。
    在讨论一九五四年苏联国家预算时在会上发言的共有苏联各加盟共和国和自治共和国的三十五个代表。最后由苏联财政部部长兹维列夫作了总结发言。他指出：在讨论中发言的代表们都一致赞成这个预算草案。在讨论过程中，代表们还谈到了国民经济各部门和财政部工作中的缺点，并提出了消灭这些缺点和把一切活动提高到更高的水平的办法。兹维列夫说，苏联部长会议已审议了民族院预算委员会的建议。政府同意预算委员会提出的修正，认为通过这些修正是适宜的。兹维列夫接着谈到了在讨论中代表们提出的关于在个别事项上可以超过预算规定的数额增加拨款的建议。他指出，苏联部长会议将仔细考虑这些建议并将通过相应的决议。
    会议接着通过了一九五四年苏联国家预算和预算法。预算收入为五千七百二十五亿四千二百三十万卢布，支出为五千六百二十八亿零一百九十万卢布，收入超过支出九十七亿四千多万卢布。
    民族院会议还批准了苏联最高苏维埃主席团关于将俄罗斯联邦的克里米亚州划归乌克兰共和国的命令以及其他法令，并通过了一项关于批准这些法令的法律。
    苏联最高苏维埃民族院定于二十七日和联盟院举行联席会议。
