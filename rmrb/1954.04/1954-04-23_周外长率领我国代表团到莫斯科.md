Title: Premier Zhou Leads Chinese Delegation to Moscow
Author:
Date: 1954-04-23
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, April 22nd. The Delegation of the People's Republic of China to the Geneva Conference, led by Zhou Enlai, Chief Representative and Minister of Foreign Affairs, arrived in Moscow by plane on the afternoon of the 21st.

Welcoming them at the airport were Molotov, First Deputy Chairman of the Council of Ministers and Minister of Foreign Affairs of the Soviet Union, Kuznetsov, Deputy Minister of Foreign Affairs, Yasnov, Chairman of the Moscow City Soviet, Fedlin, Director of the Far Eastern Department of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs, Zhukov, Director of the Protocol Department, and Kolesnikov, Commander of the Moscow City Defense.

Zhang Wentian, Representative of the Chinese Delegation to the Geneva Conference, Deputy Minister of Foreign Affairs and Ambassador to the Soviet Union, and embassy staff also welcomed them at the airport.

### 周外长率领我国代表团到莫斯科

1954-04-23
第1版()
专栏：

    周外长率领我国代表团到莫斯科
    【新华社莫斯科二十二日电】出席日内瓦会议的中华人民共和国代表团，在首席代表、外交部部长周恩来率领下，在二十一日下午乘飞机抵达莫斯科。
    在机场欢迎的有苏联部长会议第一副主席兼外交部部长莫洛托夫、外交部副部长库兹涅佐夫、莫斯科市苏维埃主席雅斯诺夫、外交部远东司司长费德林、交际司司长茹科夫和莫斯科市城防司令科列斯尼科夫等人。
    我国出席日内瓦会议代表团代表、外交部副部长兼驻苏联大使张闻天和使馆人员也到机场欢迎。
