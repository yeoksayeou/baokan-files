Title: Scottish Miners' Union Delegation Arrives in Beijing to Attend "May Day" Observances
Author:
Date: 1954-04-26
Page: 1

    Invited to Attend "May Day" Observances
    Scottish Miners' Union Delegation Arrives in Beijing
    [Xinhua News Agency, April 25th] At the invitation of the National Committee of the Chinese Coal Miners' Union, the Scottish Miners' Union delegation has arrived in Beijing by plane on the afternoon of the 25th to participate in the "May Day" observances in our country.
    Those who went to the airport to welcome them included: Li Jiebo, Secretary of the Secretariat of the All-China Federation of Trade Unions; Jin Zhifu, member of the Presidium of the All-China Federation of Trade Unions and Chairman of the National Committee of the Chinese Coal Miners' Union; Jin Ziming and Fang Ming, Deputy Directors of the International Liaison Department of the All-China Federation of Trade Unions, and others.
        The list of the Scottish Miners' Union delegation is as follows:
    Head of Delegation: Abraham Moffat, President of the Scottish Miners' Union.
    Members: Magnus Anderson Fearny, Executive Committee Member of the Scottish Miners' Union; Samuel Copeland, President of the Scottish Colliery Drivers and Allied Workers' Union;
          Robert Flares Younger, Technical Safety Inspector of the Scottish Miners' Union;
          Joseph O'Neill, Miner;
          John Hanlon, Miner;
          Andrew Mitchell, Miner.

### 应邀参加“五一”节观礼  苏格兰矿工工会代表团到京

1954-04-26
第1版()
专栏：

    应邀参加“五一”节观礼
    苏格兰矿工工会代表团到京
    【新华社二十五日讯】应中国煤矿工会全国委员会的邀请，前来我国参加“五一”节观礼的苏格兰矿工工会代表团，已在二十五日下午乘飞机到达北京。
    前往机场欢迎的有：中华全国总工会书记处书记李颉伯，中华全国总工会主席团委员、中国煤矿工会全国委员会主席金直夫，中华全国总工会国际联络部副部长金子明、方明等。
        苏格兰矿工工会代表团名单如下：
    团长  亚伯拉罕·莫法特苏格兰矿工工会主席。
    团员  马格纳斯·安德逊·费尔尼  苏格兰矿工工会执行委员。塞缪尔·柯普兰苏格兰矿坑司机与同业工人工会主席。
          罗伯特·弗拉雷斯·杨格  苏格兰矿工工会技术安全检查员。
          约瑟夫·奥尼尔  矿工。
          约翰·汉隆  矿工。
          安德鲁·密契尔  矿工。
