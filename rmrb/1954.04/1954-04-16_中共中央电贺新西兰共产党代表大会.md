Title: CPC Central Committee Sends Congratulatory Message to New Zealand Communist Party Congress
Author:
Date: 1954-04-16
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, April 15th: The Central Committee of the Chinese Communist Party has sent a telegram of congratulations to the New Zealand Communist Party Congress, which is scheduled to be held on April 17th. The telegram reads as follows:

To the National Committee of the New Zealand Communist Party and to all the delegates of the New Zealand Communist Party Congress, comrades:

The Central Committee of the Chinese Communist Party extends its brotherly and warm congratulations to the Congress and wishes you further achievements in the struggle to defend world peace and the interests of the working people. Congratulations on the success of the Congress!

The Central Committee of the Chinese Communist Party

### 中共中央电贺新西兰共产党代表大会

1954-04-16
第1版()
专栏：

    中共中央电贺新西兰共产党代表大会
    【新华社十五日讯】新西兰共产党代表大会定于四月十七日举行，中国共产党中央委员会特致电祝贺。电文如下：新西兰共产党全国委员会并转新西兰共产党代表大会全体代表同志们：
    中国共产党中央委员会谨向大会致以兄弟般的热烈祝贺，祝贺你们在保卫世界和平与保卫劳动人民利益的斗争中获得进一步的成就。祝贺大会的成功！中国共产党中央委员会
