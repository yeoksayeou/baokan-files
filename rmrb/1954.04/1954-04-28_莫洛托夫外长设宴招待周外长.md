Title: Molotov Hosts Banquet in Honor of Foreign Minister Zhou

Author: 
Date: 1954-04-28
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Geneva, April 26th. Soviet Foreign Minister V.M. Molotov hosted a banquet on the 26th to honor Zhou Enlai, Minister of Foreign Affairs of the People's Republic of China.

Zhang Wentian, Wang Jiaxiang, and Li Kenong, Vice Ministers of Foreign Affairs of the People's Republic of China, Wang Bingnan, Secretary-General of the Delegation of the People's Republic of China to the Geneva Conference, and advisors Shi Zhe and Qiao Guanhua also attended the banquet.

From the Soviet side, those present included A.A. Gromyko, V.V. Kuznetsov, Fedlin, P.F. Yudin, A.A. Soldatov, V.A. Fekhenko, and D.A. Zhukov.

The banquet proceeded in a warm and friendly atmosphere.

### 莫洛托夫外长设宴招待周外长

1954-04-28
第1版()
专栏：

    莫洛托夫外长设宴招待周外长
    【新华社日内瓦二十六日电】苏联外交部部长维·米·莫洛托夫二十六日举行宴会招待中华人民共和国外交部部长周恩来。
    中华人民共和国外交部副部长张闻天、王稼祥、李克农，中华人民共和国出席日内瓦会议代表团秘书长王炳南，顾问师哲、乔冠华也出席了宴会。
    苏联方面出席的有安·阿·葛罗米柯，瓦·瓦·库兹涅佐夫，费德林，帕·费·尤金，阿·阿·索尔达托夫，弗·阿·费金科，德·阿·茹科夫。
    宴会在热情、友好空气中进行。
