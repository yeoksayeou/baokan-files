Title: Joint Meeting of the Soviet Supreme Soviet of the Union and the Soviet of Nationalities Elects Presidium of the Supreme Soviet and Approves List of Soviet Government

Author:
Date: 1954-04-29
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, April 28th: According to a TASS dispatch from Moscow: The Soviet Supreme Soviet of the Union and the Soviet of Nationalities held their final joint meeting on the 27th in the Moscow Kremlin. The meeting elected the Presidium of the Supreme Soviet of the USSR and formed the Soviet government.

    Soviet Supreme Soviet Deputy Puzanov, representing the party groups and the council of elders of the Soviet Supreme Soviet of the Union and the Soviet of Nationalities, submitted to the meeting a proposal, approved by the Central Committee of the Communist Party of the Soviet Union, regarding the composition of the Presidium of the Supreme Soviet of the USSR.

    Voroshilov was unanimously elected Chairman of the Presidium of the Supreme Soviet of the USSR. The meeting also elected sixteen vice-chairmen representing all the Union Republics of the USSR. Begov was elected Secretary of the Presidium, and fifteen other deputies were elected as members of the Presidium of the Supreme Soviet of the USSR.

    Meeting Chairman Lacis then announced that, at the request of the Supreme Soviet of the USSR, Soviet Supreme Soviet Deputy Malenkov had presented a new list of the Soviet government, approved by the Central Committee of the Communist Party of the Soviet Union. After reading Malenkov's statement regarding the new list of the government, Lacis said that the party groups and the council of elders of the Soviet of the Union and the Soviet of Nationalities had discussed and approved the new list of the government.

    The joint meeting of the Soviet Supreme Soviet of the Union and the Soviet of Nationalities then unanimously adopted the new list of the Council of Ministers of the USSR. Malenkov was appointed Chairman of the Council of Ministers of the USSR, Molotov as First Deputy Chairman of the Council of Ministers and Minister of Foreign Affairs, Marshal of the Soviet Union Bulganin as First Deputy Chairman of the Council of Ministers and Minister of Defense, Kaganovich as First Deputy Chairman of the Council of Ministers, Mikoyan as Deputy Chairman of the Council of Ministers and Minister of Trade, Saburov as Deputy Chairman of the Council of Ministers and Chairman of the State Planning Committee, Pervukhin, Tevosyan, and Kosygin as Deputy Chairmen of the Council of Ministers, and Malyshev as Deputy Chairman of the Council of Ministers and Minister of Medium Machine Building Industry. The meeting also appointed ministers for various ministries of the USSR.

    When the chairman announced that the meeting had unanimously elected the Presidium of the Supreme Soviet of the USSR and approved the new list of the Soviet government, the hall resounded with thunderous applause.

    The first session of the Fourth Supreme Soviet of the USSR was then declared closed.

### 苏联最高苏维埃联盟院和民族院联合举行会议  选出苏联最高苏维埃主席团并批准苏联政府名单

1954-04-29
第1版()
专栏：

    苏联最高苏维埃联盟院和民族院联合举行会议
    选出苏联最高苏维埃主席团并批准苏联政府名单
    【新华社二十八日讯】据塔斯社莫斯科讯：苏联最高苏维埃联盟院和民族院二十七日在莫斯科克里姆林宫联合举行末次会议。会议选出了苏联最高苏维埃主席团，并组成了苏联政府。
    苏联最高苏维埃代表普扎诺夫代表苏联最高苏维埃联盟院和民族院的党组和年长代表委员会，向会议提出了一项经苏联共产党中央委员会批准的关于组成苏联最高苏维埃主席团的建议。
    伏罗希洛夫被一致选为苏联最高苏维埃主席团主席。会议并选出代表苏联所有加盟共和国的十六名副主席。别哥夫被选为主席团秘书，另有十五名代表被选为苏联最高苏维埃主席团委员。
    会议主席拉齐斯接着宣布，根据苏联最高苏维埃的要求，苏联最高苏维埃代表马林科夫提出了一份经苏联共产党中央委员会批准的苏联政府的新名单。在宣读了马林科夫关于政府的新名单的声明以后，拉齐斯说，联盟院和民族院的党组和年长代表委员会已经讨论并通过了政府的新名单。
    苏联最高苏维埃联盟院和民族院联席会议接着一致通过了苏联部长会议的新名单。马林科夫被任命为苏联部长会议主席，莫洛托夫为部长会议第一副主席兼外交部部长，苏联元帅布尔加宁为部长会议第一副主席兼国防部部长，卡冈诺维奇为部长会议第一副主席，米高扬为部长会议副主席兼贸易部部长，萨布罗夫为部长会议副主席兼国家计划委员会主席，别尔乌辛、捷沃西安和柯西金为部长会议副主席，马利歇夫为部长会议副主席兼中型机器制造工业部部长。会议还任命了苏联各部部长。
    当主席宣布会议已一致选出苏联最高苏维埃主席团并批准苏联政府的新名单时，会场中响起了暴风雨般的掌声。
    第四届苏联最高苏维埃第一次会议至此宣告闭幕。
