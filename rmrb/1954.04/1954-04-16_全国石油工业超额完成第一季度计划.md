Title: National Petroleum Industry Exceeds First Quarter Plan

Author: 
Date: 1954-04-16
Page: 1

    The national petroleum industry has exceeded its first quarter production plan. The total industrial output value completed 111.9 percent of the plan. Among the major products, crude oil completed 104 percent of the plan; gasoline 100.5 percent; kerosene 103.2 percent; diesel 109 percent; lubricating oil 117.1 percent; sulfur 105.4 percent. Compared with the same period last year, the smallest increase in major products over the same period last year was 37 percent, and the largest reached 155.4 percent.

### 全国石油工业超额完成第一季度计划

1954-04-16
第1版()
专栏：

    全国石油工业超额完成第一季度计划
    【本报讯】全国石油工业超额完成第一季生产计划。工业总产值完成了计划的百分之一一一点九。各项主要产品计原油完成了计划的百分之一○四；汽油一○○点五；煤油一○三点二；柴油一○九；润滑油一一七点一；硫?一○五点四。与去年同期比较，在主要产品中超过去年同期最少的为百分之三十七；最多的达百分之一五五点四。
