Title: Gui Sui in Inner Mongolia to be Renamed "Hohhot"
Author:
Date: 1954-04-23
Page: 3

Xinhua News Agency:

    Gui Sui, the capital of the Inner Mongolia Autonomous Region, will be renamed Hohhot. In accordance with the spirit of the State Council's "Instructions on Handling Titles, Place Names, Monuments, and Plaques and Couplets with Discriminatory or Insulting Nature Towards Ethnic Minorities" and the opinions of people from all ethnic groups and sectors, and with the approval of the State Council of the Central People's Government, the People's Government of the Autonomous Region will rename "Gui Sui" to "Hohhot" starting from April 25th. Gui Sui leans against the Daqing Mountains in the north. Hohhot means blue city in Mongolian.

### 内蒙古归绥将改名“呼和浩特”

1954-04-23
第3版()
专栏：

    内蒙古归绥将改名“呼和浩特”
    内蒙古自治区首府归绥将改名呼和浩特。自治区人民政府根据政务院“关于处理带有歧视或侮辱少数民族性质的称谓、地名、碑碣、匾联的指示”精神和各族各界人民的意见并报请中央人民政府政务院批准，自四月二十五日起，将“归绥”改称“呼和浩特”。归绥北倚大青山，呼和浩特即蒙语蓝色的城市。  （新华社）
