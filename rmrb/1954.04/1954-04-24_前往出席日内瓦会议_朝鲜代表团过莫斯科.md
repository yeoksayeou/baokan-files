Title: North Korean Delegation Heads to Geneva Conference, Passes Through Moscow
Author:
Date: 1954-04-24
Page: 1

    Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, April 23rd: TASS Moscow, April 22nd: The government delegation of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea, headed by Minister of Foreign Affairs Nam Il, passed through Moscow on the 22nd on its way to Geneva to attend the conference.

    The following were at the airport to see them off: Soviet Deputy Minister of Foreign Affairs Kuznetsov, Director of the Far East Department Fedlin, Director of the Protocol Department Zhukov, Deputy Chairman of the Moscow City Soviet Bobrovnikov, and Commander of the Moscow City Defense, Major General Kolesnikov.

    Also at the airport to see them off were: Lin Hai, Ambassador Extraordinary and Plenipotentiary of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea to the Soviet Union, and personnel from the Embassy of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea in Moscow.

### 前往出席日内瓦会议  朝鲜代表团过莫斯科

1954-04-24
第1版()
专栏：

    前往出席日内瓦会议
    朝鲜代表团过莫斯科
    【新华社二十三日讯】塔斯社莫斯科二十二日讯：以朝鲜民主主义人民共和国外务相南日为首的朝鲜民主主义人民共和国政府代表团在赴日内瓦出席会议途中，于二十二日经过莫斯科。
    在机场送行的有：苏联外交部副部长库兹湼佐夫，远东司司长费德林，交际司司长茹科夫，莫斯科市苏维埃副主席波布罗尼科夫，莫斯科市城防司令科列斯尼科夫少将。
    在机场送行的还有：以朝鲜民主主义人民共和国驻苏联特命全权大使林海为首的朝鲜民主主义人民共和国驻莫斯科大使馆人员。
