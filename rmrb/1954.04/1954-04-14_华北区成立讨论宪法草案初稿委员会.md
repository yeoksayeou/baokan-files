Title: North China Region Establishes Constitution Draft Initial Draft Discussion Committee
Author:
Date: 1954-04-14
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, 13th. The North China level and various provinces and cities are actively preparing and conducting discussions on the initial draft of the Constitution of the People's Republic of China. The North China Administrative Committee held its 47th administrative meeting on April 6, specifically to study the issue of the North China level and organs and organizations discussing the initial draft of the Constitution. The meeting decided to establish a Constitution Draft Initial Draft Discussion Committee, with Zhang Su as the director. The committee also established an office to guide and liaise with the five groups that have been divided for discussion.

Beijing, Shanxi, and Tangshan City have also successively established Constitution Draft Initial Draft Discussion Committees and have begun discussions.

### 华北区成立讨论宪法草案初稿委员会

1954-04-14
第1版()
专栏：

    华北区成立讨论宪法草案初稿委员会
    【新华社十三日讯】华北级和各省、市正积极准备和进行对中华人民共和国宪法草案初稿的讨论。华北行政委员会四月六日举行了第四十七次行政会议，专门研究了华北级和机关、团体讨论宪法草案初稿的问题。会议决定成立讨论宪法草案初稿委员会，由张苏任主任。委员会并设办公室，负责指导和联系已划分好的五个小组进行讨论。
    北京、山西、唐山市也已先后成立了讨论宪法草案初稿委员会，并已开始进行讨论。
