Title: The Hungarian People are Advancing Along the Road of Socialist Construction (Pictures)
Author:
Date: 1954-04-04
Page: 4

    The Hungarian People are Advancing Along the Road of Socialist Construction
    Top left: The "Tisza River" Sluice under construction. After completion, its water storage will be able to irrigate 120,000 hectares of land.
    Bottom left: A village doctor is examining the children of farmers.
    Bottom: The first blast furnace of the Stalin Metallurgical United Enterprise, which has been completed and put into production.

### 匈牙利人民沿着社会主义建设的道路前进（图片）

1954-04-04
第4版()
专栏：

    匈牙利人民沿着社会主义建设的道路前进
    左上：正在建筑中的“的萨河”水闸。建成以后，它的蓄水将能灌溉十二万公顷的土地。
    左下：乡村医生在为农民的孩子检查身体。
    下：已经建成并投入生产的斯大林冶金联合企业的第一高炉。
