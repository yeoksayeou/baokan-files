Title: Laos Establishes Committee for the Defense of World Peace
Author:
Date: 1954-04-29
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Laos, 27th: According to the Vietnam News Agency, Laos held a peace conference on April 15th. Attending this historic meeting were 180 representatives from all levels, sectors, religious groups, and ethnic groups from the liberated areas and enemy-occupied areas across the country.

After listening to the report by the Vice-Chairman of the Lao Free National United Front, the representatives engaged in heated discussions. During the discussions, they thoroughly exposed the imperialists' conspiracy to expand their aggressive war and expressed extreme indignation at the US imperialists' intensified interference in the Indochina war.

The conference then passed a resolution expressing full confidence in the growing world peace movement and expressing full support for the statement made by the Minister of Foreign Affairs of the Lao Resistance Government in March, protesting against the United States' direct interference in the Indochina war.

The conference also passed telegrams of respect to the World Peace Council, the Vietnam Committee for the Defense of World Peace, and the Cambodian Committee for the Defense of World Peace.

The conference formally elected the Lao Committee for the Defense of World Peace and issued a letter to the people of the whole country, calling on the people of the whole country to further strengthen their ranks, carry out the struggle against the imperialist aggressors to win the independence and peace of the motherland, and contribute to the cause of safeguarding world peace.

### 寮国成立保卫世界和平委员会

1954-04-29
第4版()
专栏：

    寮国成立保卫世界和平委员会
    【新华社二十八日讯】据越南通讯社寮国二十七日讯：寮国在四月十五日举行了和平大会。参加这次历史性会议的有来自全国各解放区和敌占区的各阶层、各界、各宗教团体和各民族的代表一百八十人。
    代表们在听取了寮国自由民族统一战线副主席的报告以后，展开了热烈的讨论。在讨论中，他们彻底揭发了帝国主义者扩大侵略战争的阴谋，并对美帝国主义者加紧扩大干涉印度支那战争表示极大的愤慨。
    大会接着通过决议：对于日益壮大的世界和平运动表示充分信心，并表示全力支持三月间寮国抗战政府外交部部长抗议美国直接干涉印度支那战争的声明。
    大会还通过了向世界和平理事会、越南保卫世界和平委员会以及高棉保卫世界和平委员会致敬电。
    大会正式选出了寮国保卫世界和平委员会，并发表了告全国人民书，号召全国人民进一步紧密自己的队伍，进行反对帝国主义侵略者的斗争，以赢得祖国的独立与和平，并对保障世界和平的事业作出贡献。
