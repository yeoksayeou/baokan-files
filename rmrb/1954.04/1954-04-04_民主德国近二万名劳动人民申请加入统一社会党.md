Title: Nearly 20,000 Working People in the German Democratic Republic Apply to Join the Socialist Unity Party
Author:
Date: 1954-04-04
Page: 4

    Xinhua News Agency, Berlin, 3rd. Ebert, member of the Political Bureau of the Central Committee of the Socialist Unity Party of Germany and Mayor of Greater Berlin, announced while chairing the meeting of the Fourth Party Congress on March 31st that a total of 19,157 workers and laboring farmers had applied to join the Party during the preparations for the Fourth Party Congress. At the same time, in order to greet the Fourth Party Congress, the working people of the Republic submitted a total of 131,123 production pledges, expressing their determination to make the greatest efforts to increase production, reduce costs, and complete production plans ahead of schedule.

### 民主德国近二万名劳动人民申请加入统一社会党

1954-04-04
第4版()
专栏：

    民主德国近二万名劳动人民申请加入统一社会党
   【新华社柏林三日电】德国统一社会党中央委员会政治局委员、大柏林市市长艾柏特在第四次代表大会三月三十一日的会议上担任主席时宣布，在筹备党第四次代表大会期间，共有一万九千一百五十七名工人与劳动农民申请入党。同时，共和国各地劳动人民为了迎接党第四次代表大会，共提出了十三万一千一百二十三份生产保证书，表示决心为提高生产、降低成本和提前完成生产计划而作最大的努力。
