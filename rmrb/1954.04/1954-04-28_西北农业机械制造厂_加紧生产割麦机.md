Title: Northwest Agricultural Machinery Factory Speeds Up Production of Wheat Harvesters
Author:
Date: 1954-04-28
Page: 2

Northwest Agricultural Machinery Factory
Speeds Up Production of Wheat Harvesters

The workers of the Northwest Agricultural Machinery Factory manufactured twelve horse-drawn wheat harvesters for the farmers in March, exceeding the production plan by 20 percent. In April, the factory will manufacture thirty horse-drawn wheat harvesters. Currently, the workers are working hard on production.

### 西北农业机械制造厂  加紧生产割麦机

1954-04-28
第2版()
专栏：

    西北农业机械制造厂
    加紧生产割麦机
    西北农业机械制造厂工人三月份给农民赶制马拉割麦机十二部，超额百分之二十完成了生产计划。四月份该厂将制造出三十部马拉割麦机。目前，工人正紧张生产中。
