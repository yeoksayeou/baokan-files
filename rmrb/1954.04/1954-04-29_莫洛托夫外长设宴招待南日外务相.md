Title: Foreign Minister Molotov Hosts Banquet in Honor of Foreign Minister Nam Il
Author:
Date: 1954-04-29
Page: 1
(Xinhua News Agency, Geneva, April 27th) TASS, Geneva, April 27th: Soviet Foreign Minister V.M. Molotov held a banquet on the 27th to honor Nam Il, Foreign Minister of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea.
    The representatives and advisors of the delegation of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea to the Geneva Conference who attended the banquet were Bai Nan-un, Ki Sok-bok, Chang Chun-san, Kim Taek-yong, Kim Myong-gu, Jeon Dong-heok, and Bang Seung-jik.
    The Soviet side was represented by A.A. Gromyko, V.V. Kuznetsov, S.P. Suzdalev, Fedelin, V.A. Fegin, and D.A. Zhukov.
    The banquet took place in a warm and friendly atmosphere.

### 莫洛托夫外长设宴招待南日外务相

1954-04-29
第1版()
专栏：

    莫洛托夫外长设宴招待南日外务相
    【新华社二十八日讯】塔斯社日内瓦二十七日讯：苏联外交部部长维·米·莫洛托夫在二十七日举行宴会，招待朝鲜民主主义人民共和国外务相南日。
    出席宴会的有参加日内瓦会议的朝鲜民主主义人民共和国代表团代表和顾问白南云、奇石福、张春山、金泽泳、金明求、田东赫、方承直。
    苏联方面出席宴会的有安·阿·葛罗米柯、瓦·瓦·库兹涅佐夫、斯·普·苏兹达列夫、费德林、弗·阿·费金科、德·阿·茹科夫。
    宴会在热烈友好的气氛中进行。
