Title: Polish Journalist Kornecki Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1954-04-29
Page: 4

    Xinhua News Agency, April 28th—According to the 1954 implementation plan of the Sino-Polish Cultural Cooperation Agreement, Polish journalist Roman Kornecki, editor of the Polish United Workers' Party organ "New Road," arrived in Beijing by plane this afternoon to visit China and attend the "May 1st" celebrations.

### 波兰记者柯尔奈茨基到京

1954-04-29
第4版()
专栏：

    波兰记者柯尔奈茨基到京
    【新华社二十八日讯】根据中波文化合作协定一九五四年执行计划来我国访问并参加“五一”节观礼的波兰记者柯尔奈茨基·罗曼，在今天下午乘飞机抵达北京。柯尔奈茨基是波兰统一工人党机关刊物“新路”的编辑。
