Title: North and Central Vietnamese People's Armed Forces Intercept Enemy Motorized Group and Convoy
Author:
Date: 1954-04-21
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, April 20th: According to the Vietnam News Agency on the 19th, on the 16th, the North Vietnamese People's Armed Forces intercepted the enemy's Fifth Motorized Group in Duyen Ha district, 18 kilometers northwest of Thai Binh, the capital of Thai Binh province. Preliminary reports indicate that the People's Armed Forces annihilated 200 enemy soldiers.

After destroying 22 enemy vehicles on Highway 19 in the Pleiku area on the 12th, the People's Armed Forces of Central Vietnam intercepted a French military convoy on the highway 50 kilometers east of Pleiku, destroying 31 enemy vehicles.

### 北越和中越人民武装  截击敌军机动团和运输队

1954-04-21
第1版()
专栏：

    北越和中越人民武装
    截击敌军机动团和运输队
    【新华社二十日讯】据越南通讯社十九日讯：十六日，北越人民武装在太平省省会太平西北十八公里的沿河县，截击敌军的第五机动团，据初步消息，人民武装歼灭敌军二百名。
    中越南部人民武装十二日在波来古地区的第十九号公路上击毁敌军汽车二十二辆后，又在波来古以东五十公里处的公路上，截击一支法军运输队，击毁敌军汽车三十一辆。
