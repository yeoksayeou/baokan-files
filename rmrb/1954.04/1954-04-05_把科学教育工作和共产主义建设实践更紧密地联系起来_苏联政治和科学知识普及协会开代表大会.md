Title: More Closely Link Scientific Education Work with the Practice of Communist Construction: Soviet Society for the Dissemination of Political and Scientific Knowledge Holds Congress
Author:
Date: 1954-04-05
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency, April 4th: According to TASS, the Second Congress of the Soviet Society for the Dissemination of Political and Scientific Knowledge opened on the 2nd in the Column Hall of the Trade Union Building in Moscow. Nearly seven hundred delegates and many guests attended the congress, including famous scientists, social celebrities, writers, artists, advanced workers, and agricultural workers.

The congress was presided over by Academician Vvedensky. The congratulatory message from the Central Committee of the Communist Party of the Soviet Union was read out at the meeting (full text to be published separately).

Subsequently, the Vice-Chairman of the Society, Academician Labutev of the Academy of Agricultural Sciences, gave a report on the work of the Society. He said: Since its establishment in 1947, the Society now has 300,000 members, including 13,500 scientific workers, 90,000 teachers, 19,000 agronomists, 24,000 doctors, and approximately 3,000 advanced industrial and agricultural workers. The Society also has 2,000 group members – scientific and cultural education organizations, state organs, mass organizations, industrial enterprises, and collective farms. The main activity of the Society is to hold lectures. In 1953, the Society held more than one million lectures, with an audience of 92 million people. There are more than 15,000 lecture halls in factories, collective farms, and district central cities. The content of the reports includes: issues of history, philosophy, and political economics; the foreign and domestic policies of the Communist Party and the government; science and technology, literature, and art. The Society has printed more than 4,000 reports as separate pamphlets, with a total circulation of 172.2 million copies. The intellectuals of the People's Democracies have expressed great interest in the activities of the Society. They are learning from us and organizing mass science popularization groups.

The congress is continuing.

### 把科学教育工作和共产主义建设实践更紧密地联系起来  苏联政治和科学知识普及协会开代表大会

1954-04-05
第4版()
专栏：

    把科学教育工作和共产主义建设实践更紧密地联系起来
    苏联政治和科学知识普及协会开代表大会
    【新华社四日讯】据塔斯社消息：苏联政治和科学知识普及协会第二次代表大会二日在莫斯科的工会大厦圆柱大厅开幕。参加大会的有将近七百个代表和许多来宾，其中有著名的科学家、社会名流、作家、艺术家、先进工人和农业工作者等。
    大会由弗维杰恩斯基院士主持。会上宣读了苏联共产党中央委员会的贺词（全文另发）。
    接着，协会副主席、农业科学院院士拉布捷夫就协会的工作作了报告。他说：协会自一九四七年成立以来，到现在已经拥有三十万会员。其中有一万三千五百个科学工作者、九万个教师、一万九千个农艺师、二万四千个医生、约三千个先进的工农业工作者。协会还拥有二千个团体会员——科学和文化教育组织、国家机关和群众团体、工业企业和集体农庄。协会的主要活动是举办报告会。一九五三年，协会共举办了一百多万次报告会，听众达九千二百万人。在工厂、集体农庄和区中心城市中有一万五千多个演讲厅。报告的内容包括：历史、哲学和政治经济学问题，共产党和政府的外交和国内政策，科学和技术、文学和艺术等方面的问题。协会已把四千多个报告印成单行本，共印发了一亿七千二百万份。人民民主国家的知识分子对于协会的活动表示巨大的兴趣。他们向我们学习，组织了群众性的知识普及团体。
    大会在继续进行中。
