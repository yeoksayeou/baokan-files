Title: Soong Ching-ling Donates Royalties to Aid Korean Orphans; Park Chung-hee Sends Letter to Soong Ching-ling Expressing Gratitude
Author:
Date: 1954-04-11
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, 10th: Park Chung-hee, Chairman of the Korean War Disaster Relief Committee, wrote a letter to Soong Ching-ling, Chairman of the Chinese People's National Committee for the Defence of Children, on March 1st, expressing gratitude for Chairman Soong Ching-ling's righteous act of donating 52.8 million yuan in royalties from her book "Struggling for a New China" last August to Korean orphans who suffered from war disasters. In her letter, Park Chung-hee said that your sincere love and assistance have encouraged the Korean mothers who stood up in the war and increased their confidence in striving for new victories in restoring the post-war people's economy.

### 宋庆龄捐助稿费救济朝鲜孤儿  朴正爱致函宋庆龄表示感谢

1954-04-11
第1版()
专栏：

    宋庆龄捐助稿费救济朝鲜孤儿
    朴正爱致函宋庆龄表示感谢
    【新华社十日讯】朝鲜战争灾民救济委员会委员长朴正爱在三月一日写信给中国人民保卫儿童全国委员会主席宋庆龄，对宋庆龄主席在去年八月将她的著作“为新中国奋斗”的稿费五千二百八十万元人民币捐赠给遭受战争灾害的朝鲜孤儿的义举表示感谢。朴正爱在信中说，你的诚挚的爱和援助，鼓舞了在战争中站立起来的朝鲜母亲，并增加了她们在进行恢复战后人民经济中争取新的胜利的信心。
