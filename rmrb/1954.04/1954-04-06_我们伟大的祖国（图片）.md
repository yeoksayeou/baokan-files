    Our Great Motherland
    Relevant leading organs in various regions are actively strengthening their leadership and assistance to agricultural production cooperatives. This is a farmland water conservancy surveying team from the Yunnan Provincial Department of Agriculture and Forestry's Water Conservancy Bureau conducting systematic water conservancy surveys in Shangsuan Township, Jinning County (location of the China-Soviet Friendship Agricultural Production Cooperative), in order to solve water conservancy problems and enable them to achieve even greater harvests this year.

### 我们伟大的祖国（图片）

1954-04-06
第1版()
专栏：

    我们伟大的祖国
    各地有关领导机关积极加强对农业生产合作社的领导和帮助。这是云南省农林厅水利局农田水利测量队在晋宁县上蒜乡（中苏友好农业生产合作社所在地）进行系统的水利测量，以便解决水利问题，使他们能够在今年获得更大的丰收。
