Title: British Communist Party's 23rd National Congress Continues; Passes Resolution Calling on British People to Strive for the Banning of Weapons of Mass Destruction
Author:
Date: 1954-04-20
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency, London, 19th: The 23rd National Congress of the British Communist Party continued its session on April 18th, according to TASS.

    At the morning session on the 18th, delegates continued to discuss how to strengthen the united action of the working class and jointly struggle for peace and for the vital interests of the working people. They also introduced the work experience of local Party organizations in implementing the principle of collective leadership and strengthening the Party's role among the masses.

    A. Bains and James Klugmann, members of the Party Executive Committee, reported on the Party's work in ideological, cultural, and political education, as well as future tasks.

    The meeting passed a resolution on the issue of rent. The resolution pointed out: According to the rent bill drafted by the government, British slums are to be preserved forever, while at the same time making eight million British families pay an additional 80 million pounds in rent annually. The Congress expressed its firm protest against this. The resolution also called for a broad mass movement to oppose rent increases.

    At the end of the morning session, a congratulatory telegram from William Z. Foster, Chairman of the National Committee of the Communist Party of the United States, to the Congress was read.

    At the afternoon session on the 18th, the Party's tasks in agriculture were discussed, and delegates continued to exchange experiences in organizing joint action in the struggle for peace, for the establishment of a European collective security system, and against the arming of West Germany.

    Moss, Secretary of the National Committee of the British Communist Youth League, spoke at the meeting, requesting the Party to pay more attention to and assist the Communist Youth League organizations.

    At the end of the afternoon session on the 18th, the Congress unanimously passed a special resolution on banning weapons of mass destruction. The resolution said:

  “We call on all the British people to launch a broad movement and fight to achieve the following goals: negotiations between major powers; an agreement between major powers on banning the use of atomic and hydrogen weapons and establishing strict international control and inspection systems; the abolition of American air bases in Britain; and the withdrawal of American troops.”

    The Congress continues to receive congratulatory telegrams from fraternal parties in Canada, France, Italy, Australia, and other countries.

### 英国共产党第二十三届代表大会继续举行  通过决议号召英国人民争取禁用大规模毁灭性武器

1954-04-20
第4版()
专栏：

    英国共产党第二十三届代表大会继续举行
    通过决议号召英国人民争取禁用大规模毁灭性武器
    【新华社十九日讯】　据塔斯社伦敦讯：英国共产党第二十三届代表大会于四月十八日继续举行会议。
    在十八日上午的会议上，代表们继续讨论了如何加强工人阶级的一致行动、共同为和平及为劳动人民的切身利益而斗争的问题，并介绍了各地党组织在贯彻实行集体领导的原则以及加强党在群众中的作用的工作经验。
    党执行委员会委员艾·班斯及詹姆斯·克卢格曼分别报告了党在思想工作、文化工作及政治教育工作方面的工作情况及今后的任务。
    会议通过了一项关于房租问题的决议。决议指出：根据政府拟定的房租法案，英国的贫民窟要永远保存下去，而同时要使八百万户英国家庭每年多付出八千万英镑的房租。代表大会对此表示坚决抗议。决议并号召展开一个广泛的群众性运动来反对提高房租。
    在上午会议结束时，宣读了美国共产党全国委员会主席威廉·福斯特代表美国共产党致大会的贺电。
    十八日下午的会议上，讨论了党在农业方面的任务，代表们并继续就在争取和平、争取建立欧洲集体安全体系以及反对武装西德的斗争中组织联合行动的工作交换了经验。
    英国共产主义青年团全国委员会书记摩斯在会上发言，要求党更多地关心与帮助共产主义青年团组织。
    十八日下午会议结束时，大会一致通过一项关于禁用大规模毁灭性武器的特别决议。决议说：
  “我们号召全体英国人民展开一个广泛的运动，为争取达到下列目的而斗争：大国间举行谈判；大国在禁止使用原子武器和氢武器以及建立严格的国际管制和检查制度方面达成协议；取消美国在英国的空军基地；撤退美国驻军。”
    大会继续收到加拿大、法国、意大利、澳大利亚及其他一些国家兄弟党的贺电。
