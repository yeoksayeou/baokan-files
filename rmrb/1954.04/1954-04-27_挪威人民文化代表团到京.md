Title: Norwegian People's Cultural Delegation Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1954-04-27
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, April 26th: The Norwegian People's Cultural Delegation, invited by the Chinese People's Institute of Foreign Affairs, arrived in Beijing by plane on the 26th. The delegation consists of twelve people, led by the head of the delegation, Gutorm Gjessing, Professor of Ethnology and Archeology at the University of Oslo, and the deputy head, Ragnar Forbech, Pastor and member of the World Peace Council. Welcoming them at the airport were Zhou Gengsheng, Acting President of the Chinese People's Institute of Foreign Affairs, Vice Presidents Chen Hansheng and Qian Duansheng, Jin Ziming, Deputy Director of the International Liaison Department of the All-China Federation of Trade Unions, Fang Ming, Vice Chairman of the National Committee of the China Education Trade Union, Zhang Jin, Deputy Director of the Preparatory Committee of the China Construction Trade Union, and figures from the cultural and religious circles, including Hong Shen, Zheng Zhenduo, Hua Junwu, and Jiang Yizhen.

Also present at the airport to greet them was Mr. Golan, the Norwegian government's representative for establishing diplomatic relations with China.

The list of the Norwegian People's Cultural Delegation is as follows:

Head: Gutorm Gjessing, Professor of Ethnology and Archeology at the University of Oslo
Deputy Head: Ragnar Forbech, Pastor, Member of the World Peace Council
Members: Ragnar Solheim, Lawyer, Chairman of the Chinese Committee of the Norway-People's Democratic Countries Cultural Association
            Mrs. Ellis Bonnevie-Hansen, Doctor, Chairman of the Oslo Maternal and Child Health Organization
            Anna Margrete Ruudholm, Painter
            Finn Faaberg, Painter, Teacher at the National Academy of Art
            Gunnar Eliassen, Paper Mill Worker
            Halvor Særum, Construction Worker
            Hans Jacob Nilsen, Director of the Oslo People's Theatre
            Otto Ottesen, Middle School Teacher
            Odd Stefan Dalgard, Student, President of the Student Union of the University of Oslo
Secretary of the Delegation: Olav Dalgard, Writer, Actor

### 挪威人民文化代表团到京

1954-04-27
第1版()
专栏：

    挪威人民文化代表团到京
    【新华社二十六日讯】应中国人民外交学会邀请前来我国访问的挪威人民文化代表团一行十二人由团长古托姆·耶辛、副团长拉格纳·福贝克率领，在二十六日乘飞机到达北京。前往机场欢迎的有中国人民外交学会代会长周鲠生，副会长陈翰笙、钱端升，中华全国总工会国际联络部副部长金子明，中国教育工会全国委员会副主席方明，中国建筑工会筹备委员会副主任张进和文化界、宗教界人士洪深、郑振铎、华君武、蒋翼振等人。
    到机场迎接的还有挪威政府驻我国的建交谈判代表高兰。
