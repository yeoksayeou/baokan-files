Title: Polish General R. Kerzemiere appointed as Polish member of the Neutral Nations Supervisory Commission

Author:
Date: 1954-04-08
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, April 7th: Warsaw, April 6th (Polish Press Agency): The government of the Polish People's Republic has appointed General R. Kerzemiere as the Polish member of the Neutral Nations Supervisory Commission, and recalled Major General M. Unglowski, the former Polish member of the Neutral Nations Supervisory Commission, for other assignments. On April 5, General Kerzemiere left Warsaw for Korea.

### 波兰任克尔泽米埃尔将军为中立国监察委员会波兰委员

1954-04-08
第1版()
专栏：

    波兰任克尔泽米埃尔将军为中立国监察委员会波兰委员
    【新华社七日讯】波兰通讯社华沙六日讯：波兰人民共和国政府任命勒·克尔泽米埃尔将军为中立国监察委员会波兰委员，并召回中立国监察委员会前任波兰委员米·翁格洛夫斯基少将，另有任用。四月五日，克尔泽米埃尔将军离华沙前往朝鲜。
