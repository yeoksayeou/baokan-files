Title: Chinese Trade Union Representatives Left Beijing Yesterday to Attend "May Day" in Hungary and Bulgaria
Author:
Date: 1954-04-25
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, April 24th: At the invitation of the Central Council of Hungarian Trade Unions and the Central Council of Bulgarian Trade Unions, the delegation of the All-China Federation of Trade Unions to Hungary and Bulgaria to attend the "May Day" celebrations left Beijing by plane this morning, April 24th, for Budapest and Sofia.

Those who went to the airport to see them off included Dong Xin, Secretary of the Secretariat of the All-China Federation of Trade Unions, and Jin Ziming, Deputy Director of the International Liaison Department.

Hungarian Ambassador to China Subek, First Secretary Csatordayi, and Bulgarian Chargé d'Affaires ad interim to China Grigorov also went to the airport to see them off.

The list of the delegation to Hungary is as follows:

　　Head of the Delegation: Xi Bingwu, Executive Committee Member of the All-China Federation of Trade Unions, Chairman of the Shanxi Provincial Federation of Trade Unions.
　　Members: Liu Yechen, Chairman of the Trade Union of the State-owned Nanchang Power Plant.
　　　　　　Wang Meigong (female), Fine Yarn Spinner at Jinan Renfeng Cotton Mill, National Model Worker of the Textile Industry.
　　　　　　Chen Baoyu (female), Doffer in the Preparation Workshop of Liaodong Textile Factory, Northeast Regional Model Worker.

The list of the delegation to Bulgaria is as follows:

　　Li Shuying (female), Alternate Executive Committee Member of the All-China Federation of Trade Unions, Deputy Director of the Light Industry Working Committee.

### 到匈牙利和保加利亚参加“五一”  我国工会代表昨天离北京

1954-04-25
第1版()
专栏：

    到匈牙利和保加利亚参加“五一”
    我国工会代表昨天离北京
    【新华社二十四日讯】中华全国总工会应匈牙利工会中央理事会及保加利亚总工会中央理事会的邀请，派往匈牙利及保加利亚参加“五一”节观礼的代表团，已在二十四日晨乘飞机离京飞往布达佩斯和索非亚。
    前往机场欢送的有中华全国总工会书记处书记董昕和国际联络部副部长金子明等。
    匈牙利人民共和国驻我国大使苏贝克、一等秘书察托尔道伊和保加利亚人民共和国驻我国使馆临时代办格利郭洛夫等也到机场欢送。
    赴匈牙利代表团名单如下：
    团长  席炳午  中华全国总工会执行委员、山西省工会联合会主席。

          劳动模范。
