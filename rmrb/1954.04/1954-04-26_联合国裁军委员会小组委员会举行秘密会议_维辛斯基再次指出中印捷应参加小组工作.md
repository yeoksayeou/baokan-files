Title: UN Disarmament Commission Subcommittee Holds Secret Meeting; Vyshinsky Reiterates that China, India, and Czechoslovakia Should Participate in the Subcommittee's Work
Author:
Date: 1954-04-26
Page: 4
　
    Xinhua News Agency, New York, April 25th: According to TASS, the United Nations Disarmament Commission Subcommittee held a secret meeting on April 23rd. After the meeting, the chairman of the Subcommittee, Canadian representative Johnson, told reporters that the next meeting of the Subcommittee will be held in London on May 13th.

    Soviet representative A.Y. Vyshinsky issued a statement to reporters after the meeting, outlining his remarks at the Subcommittee meeting: "The Soviet Union believes that the current composition of the Subcommittee is unsatisfactory. The Soviet representative, when discussing the composition of the Subcommittee, reminded the Disarmament Commission that we strongly advocate the participation of representatives from the People's Republic of China, India, and Czechoslovakia in the Subcommittee. The United States, Britain, France, and some other countries refused to adopt the Soviet proposal, which has created serious difficulties for the Subcommittee in carrying out its task of reaching an agreement on the reduction of armaments and the prohibition of atomic weapons. These difficulties arise because the Subcommittee is composed on a one-sided basis.

    "It must be noted that the Disarmament Commission rejected the Soviet proposal to include countries such as the People's Republic of China, India, and Czechoslovakia in the Subcommittee, countries that have ample reason to be considered the most relevant to the issue of disarmament. The Soviet Union still believes that for the Subcommittee to work successfully, its members should include the aforementioned three countries, namely: the People's Republic of China, India, and Czechoslovakia. To this end, the Soviet delegation reserves the right to raise the issue of the composition of the Subcommittee again."

### 联合国裁军委员会小组委员会举行秘密会议  维辛斯基再次指出中印捷应参加小组工作

1954-04-26
第4版()
专栏：

    联合国裁军委员会小组委员会举行秘密会议
    维辛斯基再次指出中印捷应参加小组工作
    【新华社二十五日讯】据塔斯社纽约二十四日讯：联合国裁军委员会小组委员会四月二十三日举行秘密会议。会后，小组委员会主席、加拿大代表约翰逊向记者说，小组委员会下次会议将于五月十三日在伦敦举行。
    苏联代表安·扬·维辛斯基会后对记者发表了他在小组委员会会议上的声明，声明说：“苏联认为小组委员会现有的成员是不能令人满意的。苏联代表曾经在讨论小组委员会成员问题时提醒裁军委员会注意，我们力主中华人民共和国代表、印度代表和捷克斯洛伐克代表参加小组委员会。美国、英国、法国和其他一些国家拒绝采纳苏联提出的建议，这就使小组委员会在执行它所担当的要在缩减军备和禁止原子武器问题上达成协议这一任务时产生严重的困难，其所以会产生这种困难，是因为这一小组委员会是在片面的基础上组成的。
    “必须注意到这样的情况，即：裁军委员会拒绝了苏联提出的使中华人民共和国、印度、捷克斯洛伐克这样一些国家参加小组委员会的建议，而这些国家是有充分理由被认为是与裁军问题最有关系的国家的。苏联仍旧认为：小组委员会要胜利地进行工作，其成员应该包括上述三个国家，即：中华人民共和国、印度和捷克斯洛伐克。为此，苏联代表团保留重新提出关于小组委员会成员问题的权利。”
