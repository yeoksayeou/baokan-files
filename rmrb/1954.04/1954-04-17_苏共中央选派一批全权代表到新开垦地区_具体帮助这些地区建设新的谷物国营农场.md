Title: The CPSU Central Committee Selects a Group of Plenipotentiary Representatives to New Reclamation Areas to Specifically Assist These Areas in Building New Grain State Farms

Author:

Date: 1954-04-17
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, 16th. TASS, Moscow, 14th: The Central Committee of the Communist Party of the Soviet Union has selected a group of plenipotentiary representatives to the reclamation areas of Kazakhstan, Siberia, the Urals, and the Volga River Basin to specifically assist the Party organizations and Soviets in these areas to organize and build new grain state farms within the time limits specified by the government (1954-1955). On average, there will be one plenipotentiary representative for every four to five new grain state farms.

Before their departure, the plenipotentiary representatives studied the work experience of many advanced collective farms and state farms in the Moscow region, visited the new agricultural machinery displayed especially for them in the machinery and equipment exhibition hall of the Soviet Agricultural Exhibition, and visited the local building material samples used for constructing machine tractor stations, state farms, and collective farms from various regions displayed at the All-Union Building Exhibition. They also listened to a report by Comrade Yulkin, Minister of State Farms of the Russian Federation, on the basic principles of organizing new state farms, and an academic lecture by Academician Lysenko, President of the All-Union Lenin Academy of Agricultural Sciences, on how to obtain a bumper harvest of spring wheat on newly reclaimed land in Siberia and Kazakhstan.

### 苏共中央选派一批全权代表到新开垦地区  具体帮助这些地区建设新的谷物国营农场

1954-04-17
第4版()
专栏：

    苏共中央选派一批全权代表到新开垦地区
    具体帮助这些地区建设新的谷物国营农场
    【新华社十六日讯】据塔斯社莫斯科十四日讯：苏联共产党中央委员会选派了一批全权代表到哈萨克、西伯利亚、乌拉尔和伏尔加河流域等开垦地区，具体帮助这些地区的党组织和苏维埃在政府所指定的期限（一九五四—一九五五年）内组织和建设新的谷物国营农场。平均每四到五个新的谷物国营农场就将有全权代表一名。
    全权代表们在出发前研究了莫斯科州许多先进集体农庄和国营农场的工作经验，参观了苏联农业展览会机器设备陈列室特为他们展出的新的农业机器，以及全苏建筑展览会上展览的各地用于建筑机器拖拉机站、国营农场和集体农庄的本地建筑材料的样品。他们还听了俄罗斯联邦国营农场部部长尤尔金关于组织新的国营农场的基本原则的报告和全苏列宁农业科学院院长李森科院士关于在西伯利亚和哈萨克新开垦的土地如何获得春小麦丰收的学术演讲。
