Title: Comrade Khrushchev's Sixtieth Birthday: Telegram of Congratulations from the CPSU Central Committee and the USSR Council of Ministers

Author:

Date: 1954-04-18
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, April 17th: TASS Moscow, April 17th: The Central Committee of the Communist Party of the Soviet Union and the Council of Ministers of the USSR sent a telegram to N.S. Khrushchev congratulating him on his sixtieth birthday. The full text of the congratulatory telegram is as follows: Comrade Nikita Sergeyevich Khrushchev:

    As you celebrate your sixtieth birthday, the Central Committee of the Communist Party of the Soviet and the Council of Ministers of the USSR cordially congratulates you, a loyal student of Lenin and a comrade-in-arms of Stalin, an outstanding leader of the Communist Party and the Soviet state, and a glorious son of the working people.

    The Communist Party and the Soviet people highly value the contributions you have made in the struggle to dedicate your entire life to the cause of communism.

    In the harsh years of the civil war, in the peaceful socialist construction, in the difficult years of the Great Patriotic War, and today, in the peaceful period of the struggle to build a communist society in our country, in all posts of Party and state work, you have contributed all your strength to promote the great victory of the Soviet people, improve their welfare, and build communism with your inexhaustible energy, fortitude, and adherence to principles.

    Our friend and comrade, our dear Nikita Sergeyevich, we heartily wish you good health and wish you continued long and fruitful work for the happiness of the Soviet people and our country, and for the cause of communism.
    The Central Committee of the Communist Party of the Soviet Union
    The Council of Ministers of the USSR

### 赫鲁晓夫同志六十寿辰  苏共中央和苏联部长会议致电祝贺

1954-04-18
第1版()
专栏：

    赫鲁晓夫同志六十寿辰
    苏共中央和苏联部长会议致电祝贺
    【新华社十七日讯】塔斯社莫斯科十七日讯：苏联共产党中央委员会和苏联部长会议致电尼·谢·赫鲁晓夫，祝贺他的六十岁寿辰。贺电全文如下：尼基塔·谢尔盖耶维奇·赫鲁晓夫同志：
    欣逢你的六十岁寿辰，苏联共产党中央委员会和苏联部长会议热诚地祝贺你，列宁的忠实学生和斯大林的战友，共产党和苏维埃国家卓越的领袖，劳动人民光荣的儿子。
    共产党和苏维埃人民非常珍视你在把全部生命贡献给共产主义事业的奋斗中所建树的功绩。
    在严酷的内战年代里，在和平的社会主义建设中，在伟大的卫国战争的艰苦年代里，以及在今天、争取在我国建成共产主义社会的斗争的和平时期里，你在党和国家工作的一切岗位上，以你无穷尽的精力、刚毅和忠于原则的性格，贡献出你的全部的力量来促进苏联人民伟大的胜利，增进他们的福利以及建设共产主义。
    我们的朋友和同志，我们亲爱的尼基塔·谢尔盖耶维奇，我们衷心地祝贺你身体健康，并祝你继续长期为苏联人民和我们国家的幸福，为共产主义事业作出有成绩的工作。苏联共产党中央委员会苏联部长会议
