Title: Beside the Fires of War (Picture)
Author: Famichev
Date: 1954-04-04
Page: 6

　　Beside the Fires of War
    USSR Famichev
    News report: The United States is actively interfering in the war in Indochina, forcing France to suffer huge losses in this "living burial war" that only benefits American monopoly capitalists.
    This is the trick of "aid," leisurely warming oneself on the sidelines; this is the all-too-common "friendship" that places others in a burning situation.
    An American says to an old Turkish farmer: "Hey, old man, would you like to receive American aid?"
    The old Turkish farmer replies: "Maybe next time! This year's harvest is really too bad."
          (Originally published in the Soviet "Izvestia" on March 28, 1954)

### 在战争篝火之旁（图片）
法米契夫
1954-04-04
第6版()
专栏：

　　在战争篝火之旁
    苏联  法米契夫
    报纸消息：  美国积极干涉印度支那战争，迫使法国在这个只是对美国垄断资本家有利的“体葬战争”中蒙受巨大的损失。
    这就是“援助”的洋把戏，自己在一旁悠然自得地取暖，这就是司空见惯的“友谊”，把别人置于焚身的境地。
    美国人对土耳其老农说：“喂，老头，你愿意得到美国的援助吗？”
    土耳其老农答道：“下次再说吧！今年的收成确实太坏了。”
          （原载一九五四年三月二十八日苏联“消息报”）
