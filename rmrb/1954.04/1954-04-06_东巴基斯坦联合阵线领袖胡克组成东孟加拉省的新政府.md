Title: United Front Leader Huk of East Pakistan Forms New Government of East Bengal Province
Author: 
Date: 1954-04-06
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency, Karachi, 5th—It is reported that Fazlul Huq, the leader of the East Pakistan United Front, who defeated the ruling Muslim League of Pakistan by obtaining an overwhelming majority in the East Pakistan Constituent Assembly elections, has formed the new government of East Bengal Province. Huq and the ministers of the new government were sworn in at Dhaka on the 3rd.

The newly elected United Front members of the East Pakistan Constituent Assembly also held a meeting on the 2nd, and unanimously elected Huq as the leader of the United Front members of the East Pakistan Constituent Assembly. Huq said during the East Pakistan campaign that the current Pakistan Constituent Assembly should be dissolved and a new assembly should be elected to replace it.

### 东巴基斯坦联合阵线领袖胡克组成东孟加拉省的新政府

1954-04-06
第4版()
专栏：

    东巴基斯坦联合阵线领袖胡克组成东孟加拉省的新政府
    【新华社五日讯】喀喇蚩消息：在东巴基斯坦制宪议会选举中，以获取压倒多数票击败了巴基斯坦执政党回教联盟的东巴基斯坦联合阵线领袖法兹卢尔·胡克已组成了东孟加拉省的新政府。胡克和新政府的部长们三日在达卡举行了就职宣誓。
    新选出的东巴基斯坦制宪议会的联合阵线议员并于二日举行集会，一致推选胡克为东巴基斯坦制宪议会联合阵线议员团的领袖。胡克曾在东巴基斯坦竞选中说：目前的巴基斯坦制宪议会应该解散，并选出一个新的议会来代替它。
