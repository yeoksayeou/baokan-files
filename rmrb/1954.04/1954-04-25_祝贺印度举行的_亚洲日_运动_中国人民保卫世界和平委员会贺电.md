Title: Congratulations to India on the "Asian Day" Movement: Congratulatory Telegram from the Chinese People's Committee for World Peace
Author:
Date: 1954-04-25
Page: 1

    Congratulations to India on the "Asian Day" Movement
    Congratulatory Telegram from the Chinese People's Committee for World Peace
    (Xinhua News Agency, April 24th) The All-India Peace Council is scheduled to hold the "Asian Day" movement on April 25th. The Chinese People's Committee for World Peace sends a telegram of congratulations. The telegram reads as follows: The Chinese People's Committee for World Peace and all the Chinese people extend warm respect to your council and all the Indian people for the "Asian Day" movement you are about to hold, and sincerely congratulate the success of the "Asian Day" movement. It is hoped that the people of China and India will unite closely and strive unremittingly for greater achievements in the common cause of defending world and Asian peace.
    The Chinese People's Committee for World Peace, April 24, 1954

### 祝贺印度举行的“亚洲日”运动  中国人民保卫世界和平委员会贺电

1954-04-25
第1版()
专栏：

    祝贺印度举行的“亚洲日”运动
    中国人民保卫世界和平委员会贺电
    【新华社二十四日讯】全印和平理事会定于四月二十五日举行“亚洲日”运动，中国人民保卫世界和平委员会特去电祝贺。电文如下：中国人民保卫世界和平委员会并全中国人民对贵会与全体印度人民所要举行的“亚洲日”运动，谨致热烈的敬意，并诚挚地祝贺“亚洲日”运动的成功。希望中印两国人民紧密团结，为保卫世界与亚洲和平的共同事业中获得更大成就而奋斗不懈。
    中国人民保卫世界和平委员会  一九五四年四月二十四日
