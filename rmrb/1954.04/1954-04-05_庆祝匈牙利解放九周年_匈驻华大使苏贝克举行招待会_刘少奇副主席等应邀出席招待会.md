Title: Celebrating the Ninth Anniversary of Hungary's Liberation, Hungarian Ambassador to China, Subek, Holds Reception. Vice Chairman Liu Shaoqi and Others Invited to Attend.
Author:
Date: 1954-04-05
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, April 4th: On April 4th, the glorious ninth anniversary of the liberation of Hungary by the Soviet Army, the Ambassador of the Hungarian People's Republic to China, Subek, held a reception at the Hungarian Embassy at 7:00 PM.

Invited to attend were: Liu Shaoqi, Vice Chairman of the Central People's Government; Guo Moruo and Deng Xiaoping, Vice Premiers of the Government Administration Council; Shen Junru, President of the Supreme People's Court; Zhang Zongxun, Deputy Chief of the General Staff of the People's Revolutionary Military Commission; Xiao Hua, Deputy Director of the General Political Department; Wu Xiuquan, Vice Minister of Foreign Affairs; heads of various departments of the Central People's Government; leaders of various democratic parties and people's organizations; figures from cultural and artistic circles; labor models; combat heroes; and Wu Han, Deputy Mayor of the Beijing Municipal People's Government, and others.

Also invited were diplomatic envoys and diplomatic officials from various countries to China.

More than 400 people attended the reception.

The reception proceeded in a warm and friendly atmosphere. During the event, the hosts and guests repeatedly toasted to the health of Chairman Mao Zedong, the great leader of the Chinese people, and Rakosi, the leader of the Hungarian people.

Mao Zedong, Chairman of the Central People's Government of the People's Republic of China, and Premier Zhou Enlai, sent telegrams to István Dobi, Chairman of the Presidium of the Hungarian People's Republic, and Imre Nagy, Chairman of the Council of Ministers, on the 2nd, respectively, congratulating them on the ninth anniversary of Hungary's liberation.

### 庆祝匈牙利解放九周年  匈驻华大使苏贝克举行招待会  刘少奇副主席等应邀出席招待会

1954-04-05
第1版()
专栏：

    庆祝匈牙利解放九周年
    匈驻华大使苏贝克举行招待会
    刘少奇副主席等应邀出席招待会
   【新华社四日讯】四月四日为光荣的苏联军队解放匈牙利九周年纪念日，匈牙利人民共和国驻我国大使苏贝克特在四日下午七时在匈牙利大使馆举行招待会。
    应邀出席的有：中央人民政府副主席刘少奇，政务院副总理郭沫若、邓小平，最高人民法院院长沈钧儒，人民革命军事委员会副总参谋长张宗逊、总政治部副主任萧华，外交部副部长伍修权，以及中央人民政府各部门首长，各民主党派和各人民团体的负责人，文化艺术界人士，劳动模范，战斗英雄和北京市人民政府副市长吴晗等。
    应邀出席的还有各国驻华使节和外交官员。
    出席招待会的共有四百多人。
    招待会在热烈和友好的空气中进行。席间，宾主不断为中国人民的伟大领袖毛泽东主席和匈牙利人民的领袖拉科西的健康而干杯。
    中华人民共和国中央人民政府毛泽东主席和周恩来总理，曾在二日分别致电匈牙利人民共和国主席团主席伊斯特万·道比和部长会议主席伊姆雷·纳吉，祝贺匈牙利解放九周年。
