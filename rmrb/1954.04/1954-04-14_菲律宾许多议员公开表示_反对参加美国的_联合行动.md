Title: Many Philippine Congressmen Publicly Express Opposition to Participating in the U.S.'s "Joint Action"
Author:
Date: 1954-04-14
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Manila, 13th: Many influential congressmen in the Philippines have recently publicly expressed their opposition to the U.S.'s attempt to drag the Philippines into the "Pacific Pact" and to the Philippines' participation in the "joint action" to interfere in the Indochina War. United Press International reported that "their reason for opposition is that participation in the pact ('Pacific Pact') will expose the Philippines to serious war risks."

Senator Recto, one of the leaders of the ruling Philippine Nationalist Party, said on the 9th regarding the U.S.'s efforts to force the Philippines to participate in the "joint action" to interfere in the Indochina War: "I will fight against this action inside and outside Congress." Recto believes that the people of Indochina have the right to decide their own form of government. He said, "This is not the business of the Filipinos." Recto also expressed concern about the war threats contained in the U.S.'s "Pacific Pact" organization plan.

Kuenko, chairman of the Foreign Affairs Subcommittee of the Philippine House of Representatives, and committee member Corps also expressed their opposition on the 10th to any measures that would involve the Philippines in the Indochina War. Senate Liberal Party leader Paredes and Democratic Party Senator Cabili also expressed opposition to the Philippines joining the "Pacific Pact."

### 菲律宾许多议员公开表示  反对参加美国的“联合行动”

1954-04-14
第4版()
专栏：

    菲律宾许多议员公开表示
    反对参加美国的“联合行动”
    【新华社十三日讯】马尼拉消息：菲律宾有势力的国会议员多人最近公开表示反对美国企图把菲律宾拉进“太平洋公约”，反对菲律宾参加干涉印度支那战争的“联合行动”。合众社说，“他们反对的理由是：参加该约（‘太平洋公约’）将使菲律宾受到严重的战争危险”。
    菲律宾执政党国民党的领袖之一、参议员雷克托九日就美国力图强制菲律宾参加干涉印度支那战争的“联合行动”一事发表谈话说：“我将在国会内外进行反对这一行动的斗争。”雷克托认为，印度支那人民有权利决定自己的政府形式。他说：“这不是菲律宾人的事”。雷克托又对美国的“太平洋公约”组织计划所包含的战争威胁表示忧虑。
    菲律宾众议院外交小组委员会主席库恩柯和委员科普斯，也在十日表示反对足以使菲律宾介入印度支那战争的一切措施。参议院自由党领袖帕雷德斯和民主党参议员卡比利都表示反对菲律宾参加“太平洋公约”。
