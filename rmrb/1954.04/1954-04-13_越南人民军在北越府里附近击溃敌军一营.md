Title: Vietnamese People's Army Annihilates Enemy Battalion Near Phu Ly, North Vietnam
Author:
Date: 1954-04-13
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, Hanoi, April 11th:

    According to the Vietnam News Agency on April 11th, on April 7th, the Vietnamese People's Army attacked the 2nd Battalion of the 5th Regiment of the French invading Foreign Legion, stationed 3 kilometers south of Phu Ly, the capital of Ha Nam province, on Highway 1. After a fierce battle, the People's Army completely annihilated this enemy battalion, killing and capturing 230 enemy soldiers. On the 4th, the People's Army eliminated an enemy stronghold 12 kilometers southeast of Nam Dinh, the capital of Nam Dinh province.
    
    Meanwhile, from April 3rd to 9th, the North Vietnamese People's Armed Forces used landmines to destroy 10 enemy vehicles on Highways 1, 10, and 21. On April 1st, the People's Armed Forces also destroyed two enemy trains on the Haiphong-Hanoi railway.

### 越南人民军在北越府里附近击溃敌军一营

1954-04-13
第1版()
专栏：

    越南人民军在北越府里附近击溃敌军一营
    【新华社十二日讯】据越南通讯社十一日讯：四月七日，越南人民军进攻驻扎在河南省省会府里以南三公里、第一号公路上的法国侵略军外籍军团第五团第二营。经过激烈的战斗，人民军完全击溃了这一营敌军，毙、俘敌军二百三十名。四日，人民军拔除了位于南定省省会东南十二公里处的一个敌军据点。
    同时，四月三日到九日，北越人民武装在第一号、第十号和第二十一号公路上用地雷炸毁了敌军汽车十辆。人民武装并在四月一日在海防到河内的铁路上炸毁敌军火车两列。
