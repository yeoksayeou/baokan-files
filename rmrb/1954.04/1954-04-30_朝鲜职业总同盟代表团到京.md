Title: Delegation of the General Federation of Trade Unions of Korea Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1954-04-30
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency Report:

    Delegation of the General Federation of Trade Unions of Korea Arrives in Beijing
    According to Xinhua News Agency: Invited by the All-China Federation of Trade Unions to come to our country to participate in the "May Day" celebrations, the delegation of the General Federation of Trade Unions of Korea, a group of five people led by head of the delegation Kim Hyong-seop, arrived in Beijing by train on the morning of the 29th.
    List of the delegation of the General Federation of Trade Unions of Korea:
    Head of the delegation: Kim Hyong-seop, Chairman of the Central Committee of the Korean Electrical Workers' Trade Union
    Deputy Head of the delegation: Kang Bong-ki, Chairman of the Central Committee of the Korean Chemical and Building Materials Workers' Trade Union
    Members: Lee Hwang-ok, Chairman of the Pyonganbuk-do Committee of the Korean Light Industry Workers' Trade Union
        Song Young-geun, Labor Hero, Train Driver
        Choi Byeong-ryul, Instructor of the International Department of the Central Committee of the General Federation of Trade Unions of Korea

### 朝鲜职业总同盟代表团到京

1954-04-30
第4版()
专栏：

    朝鲜职业总同盟代表团到京
    据新华社讯：应中华全国总工会邀请前来我国参加“五一”节观礼的朝鲜职业总同盟代表团一行五人在团长金炯爕率领下，于二十九日早晨乘火车到达北京。
    朝鲜职业总同盟代表团名单：
    团长　金炯爕　朝鲜电气劳动者职业同盟中央委员会委员长
    副团长　姜凤麒　朝鲜化学及建筑器材劳动者职业同盟中央委员会委员长
    团员　李黄玉　朝鲜轻工业劳动者职业同盟平安北道委员会委员长
        宋永根　劳动英雄，火车司机
        崔秉律　朝鲜职业总同盟中央委员会国际部指导员
