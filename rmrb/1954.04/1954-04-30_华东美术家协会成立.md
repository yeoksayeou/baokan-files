Title: East China Artists Association Established
Author:
Date: 1954-04-30
Page: 3

Xinhua News Agency

    East China Artists Association Established
    The inaugural meeting of the East China Artists Association was held in Shanghai from April 21st to 27th. More than 80 painters, sculptors, arts and crafts artists, and printmakers from all over East China attended. The meeting elected Lai Shaoqi, Liu Kaiqu, Huang Binhong, Feng Zikai, Mi Gu, Chen Yanqiao, and more than 50 others to form the council of the East China Artists Association.

### 华东美术家协会成立

1954-04-30
第3版()
专栏：

    华东美术家协会成立
    华东美术家协会成立大会从四月二十一日到二十七日在上海举行，出席的有华东各地画家、雕塑家、工艺美术家和版画家等八十多人。大会选出赖少其、刘开渠、黄宾虹、丰子恺、米谷、陈烟桥等五十多人组成华东美术家协会理事会。
                          （新华社）
