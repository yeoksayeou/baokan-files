Author:
Date: 1954-04-02
Page: 1

    Our Great Motherland
    In order to meet the needs of passengers and freight transportation on the steep slope between Chongqing's Caiyuanba Station and Lianglukou after the opening of the Chengdu-Chongqing Railway, Chongqing has built a cable car line with a total length of 141 meters. Two Soviet electric cable cars run in opposite directions, carrying 20,000 passengers and 500 tons of cargo every day.

### 我们伟大的祖国（图片）

1954-04-02
第1版()
专栏：

    我们伟大的祖国
    重庆市为适应成渝铁路通车后重庆市菜园坝车站到两路口之间陡坡上行人和货运的需要，兴建了一条全长一百四十一公尺的缆车道，上下对开两辆苏联电车型缆车，每天可载运二万个乘客和五百吨货物。
