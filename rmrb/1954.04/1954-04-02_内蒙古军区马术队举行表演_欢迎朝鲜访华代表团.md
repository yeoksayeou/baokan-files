Title: Inner Mongolia Military Region Equestrian Team Holds Performance to Welcome North Korean Delegation to China
Author:
Date: 1954-04-02
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, Gui Sui, April 1st. The Equestrian Team of the Inner Mongolia Military Region of the Chinese People's Liberation Army held an equestrian performance in Gui Sui City on the morning of March 31st to welcome the North Korean People's Delegation to China headed by Kim Ung-gi.

    At the beginning of the performance, the Mongolian cavalry warriors participating in the performance greeted the delegation with the highest etiquette of the cavalry - the "knife-throwing salute." They performed a total of six programs including horseback slashing, obstacle jumping, polo performance competition, light cavalry horse racing, light riding performance, and horseback shooting, including sixty-four items such as "Salute to the Delegation" and "Building a Sky Bridge." The soldiers' highly mobile, flexible, brave, and decisive performances won prolonged applause from the delegation. At the end of the performance, the performers accepted flowers from the delegation.

### 内蒙古军区马术队举行表演  欢迎朝鲜访华代表团

1954-04-02
第1版()
专栏：

    内蒙古军区马术队举行表演
    欢迎朝鲜访华代表团
    【新华社归绥一日电】中国人民解放军内蒙古军区马术队，三月三十一日上午在归绥市举行马术表演，欢迎以金应基团长为首的朝鲜人民访华代表团。
    表演开始时，参加表演的蒙族骑兵战士以骑兵的最高礼节——“抛刀礼”迎接了代表团。他们共表演了乘马斩劈、障碍超越、马球表演赛、轻骑赛马、轻乘表演、马上射击等六个节目，包括“向代表团致敬”、“搭天桥”等六十四个项目，战士们高度的机动、灵活、勇猛、果敢的表演，博得了代表团人员经久不息的掌声。表演结束，表演者接受了代表团的献花。
