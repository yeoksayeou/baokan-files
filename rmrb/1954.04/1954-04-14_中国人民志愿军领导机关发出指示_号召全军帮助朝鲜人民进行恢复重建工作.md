Title: Chinese People's Volunteer Army Leadership Issues Instructions, Calling on the Entire Army to Help the Korean People with Restoration and Reconstruction Work
Author:
Date: 1954-04-14
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Pyongyang, April 13th: The leadership of the Chinese People's Volunteer Army recently issued instructions to the entire army regarding assisting the Korean people in their restoration and reconstruction efforts. The instructions called upon the entire army to cherish the interests of the Korean people a hundredfold and to further strengthen the friendship and unity with the Korean people in future assistance to the Korean people's reconstruction efforts.

The instructions stipulate that the focus of assisting the Korean people in their restoration and reconstruction work should be: helping to complete water conservancy projects near the stationed areas that would most benefit the Korean people; to the extent possible, making every effort to help the people in the stationed areas with spring plowing, tree planting, summer hoeing, and autumn harvesting, and striving to help the Korean people realize the Korean government's call of "not wasting an inch of land"; to help the people in the stationed areas build houses, settle down and produce, and help local Korean governments build urgently needed public places.

The instructions require all units and organs of the entire army to attach importance to this work. In the construction of various projects, it is necessary to ensure the quality of the projects, to make the various buildings strong and durable, in accordance with the requirements of the Korean people, and to save materials and cherish tools.

### 中国人民志愿军领导机关发出指示  号召全军帮助朝鲜人民进行恢复重建工作

1954-04-14
第1版()
专栏：

    中国人民志愿军领导机关发出指示
    号召全军帮助朝鲜人民进行恢复重建工作
    【新华社平壤十三日电】中国人民志愿军领导机关最近向全军发出了关于帮助朝鲜人民进行恢复与重建工作的指示，号召全军百倍爱护朝鲜人民的利益，并在今后帮助朝鲜人民恢复建设的工作中进一步密切与朝鲜人民的友谊团结。
    指示规定帮助朝鲜人民进行恢复建设工作的重点为：帮助完成驻区附近能使朝鲜人民获益最大的水利工程；在可能的条件下，尽力帮助驻区人民进行春耕、植树、夏锄、秋收等劳动，尽力帮助朝鲜人民实现朝鲜政府“不荒废一寸土地”的号召；要帮助驻地人民修建房屋、安家生产，帮助朝鲜地方政府修建急需的公共场所等。
    指示要求全军的各部队、各机关重视这一工作。各项工程在施工中要保证工程质量，使各种建筑物坚固耐久，合乎朝鲜人民的要求，并要做到节省器材和爱护工具。
