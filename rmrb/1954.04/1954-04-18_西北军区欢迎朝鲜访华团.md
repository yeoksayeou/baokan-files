Title: Northwest Military Region Welcomes Korean Delegation to China
Author:
Date: 1954-04-18
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, Lanzhou, April 17th: The Northwest Military Region of the Chinese People's Liberation Army held a grand welcoming ceremony on the morning of the 16th to warmly welcome the Korean People's Delegation to China, headed by Kim Ung-gi, and the accompanying art troupe.

    Peng Shaohui, Deputy Commander of the Northwest Military Region, delivered a welcoming speech on behalf of all commanders and fighters of the Northwest Military Region.

    After a reply speech by head of the delegation Kim Ung-gi, the Korean People's Delegation to China received flowers presented by the Northwest Military Region of the Chinese People's Liberation Army amidst applause and cheers, and exchanged flags with the Northwest Military Region of the Chinese People's Liberation Army.

    After the welcoming meeting, the Korean National Art Theatre and the National Classical Art Theatre jointly performed wonderful programs.

### 西北军区欢迎朝鲜访华团

1954-04-18
第1版()
专栏：

    西北军区欢迎朝鲜访华团
    【新华社兰州十七日电】中国人民解放军西北军区在十六日上午举行盛大欢迎会，热烈欢迎以金应基团长为首的朝鲜人民访华代表团和随行的艺术团。
    西北军区副司令员彭绍辉代表西北军区全体指挥员战斗员致欢迎词。
    在金应基团长致答词后，朝鲜人民访华代表团在掌声和欢呼声中接受了中国人民解放军西北军区的献花，并和中国人民解放军西北军区相互献旗。
    欢迎大会结束后，朝鲜国立艺术剧场和国立古典艺术剧场综合演出了精彩节目。
