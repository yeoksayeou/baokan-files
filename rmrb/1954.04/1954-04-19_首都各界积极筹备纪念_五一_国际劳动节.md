Title: All Circles in the Capital Actively Prepare to Celebrate "May Day" International Labor Day
Author:
Date: 1954-04-19
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency report: All circles in the capital are actively preparing to celebrate "May Day" International Labor Day. The "May Day" International Labor Day Celebration Preparatory Committee of Beijing Municipality has been established and is now actively carrying out various preparatory work.

    Many factories and mines in the capital are rushing to produce various charts and models marking the increasing improvement of production levels, preparing to participate in the parade. The North China Agricultural Machinery General Factory is rushing to produce models such as the double-wheeled double-furrow plow.

    The capital's art troupe of several thousand people is also preparing to participate in the parade. The China Youth Art Theater is rushing to make signboards explaining their past performances, preparing to participate in the festival parade. Workers are busily decorating Tiananmen Square and the reviewing stands. Many spotlights and amplifiers have been installed in Tiananmen Square. Beijing's Qianmen Railway Station is also being renovated to welcome foreign guests who will come to China to attend the "May Day" celebrations. Major theaters and troupes in the capital are rehearsing various wonderful dramas and songs and dances, preparing to stage them around "May Day."

    At present, workers in various factories and mines in the capital have launched a labor emulation campaign to welcome "May Day." They will use practical actions of overfulfilling the state production plan to welcome the festival of the working people of the world. Workers at the Soviet Exhibition Center construction site are preparing to install the gilded iron tower with the red five-pointed star on the central hall of the Soviet Exhibition Center before "May Day" as a gift for "May Day."

### 首都各界积极筹备纪念“五一”国际劳动节

1954-04-19
第1版()
专栏：

    首都各界积极筹备纪念“五一”国际劳动节
    据新华社讯：首都各界积极筹备纪念“五一”国际劳动节。北京市庆祝“五一”国际劳动节筹备委员会已经成立，现正积极进行各项筹备工作。
    首都许多厂矿正在赶制各种标明生产水平日益提高的图表和模型，准备参加游行。华北农业机械总厂正赶制着双轮双铧犁等模型。
    首都数千人的文艺大队也正在做参加游行的准备，中国青年艺术剧院正赶制说明他们过去演出情况的标语牌，准备参加节日游行。工人们正紧张地装饰天安门和观礼台，很多聚光灯、扩音器已在天安门广场安置起来。北京的前门车站也正在进行修饰，准备欢迎来我国参加“五一”观礼的外宾。首都各大剧院、剧团正排练各种精彩的戏剧和歌舞，准备在“五一”前后上演。
    现在首都各厂矿的工人们，已掀起了迎接“五一”的劳动竞赛热潮，他们要以超额完成国家生产计划的实际行动，来迎接全世界劳动人民的节日。苏联展览馆工地上的工人准备在“五一”节前，把苏联展览馆中央大厅上的红五星镀金铁塔安装起来，作为对“五一”节的献礼。
