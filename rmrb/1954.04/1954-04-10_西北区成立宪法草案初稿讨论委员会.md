Title: Northwest District Establishes Draft Constitution Initial Draft Discussion Committee
Author:
Date: 1954-04-10
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Xi'an, 9th: The Northwest Administrative Committee, at its 35th administrative meeting held on the 6th, discussed the issue of organizing responsible persons from government organs, democratic parties, and people's organizations at the Northwest District, Shaanxi Province, and Xi'an city levels to discuss the initial draft of the Constitution of the People's Republic of China. The meeting decided to concentrate the personnel participating in the discussion and divide them into several groups for discussion. The meeting approved the establishment of the Draft Constitution Initial Draft Discussion Committee, which is specifically responsible for guiding the discussion.

### 西北区成立宪法草案初稿讨论委员会

1954-04-10
第1版()
专栏：

    西北区成立宪法草案初稿讨论委员会
    【新华社西安九日电】西北行政委员会在六日举行的第三十五次行政会议上，讨论了组织西北区、陕西省、西安市一级各政府机关、各民主党派、各人民团体负责人讨论中华人民共和国宪法草案初稿的问题。会议决定将参加讨论的人员集中在一起，分成若干小组进行讨论。会议通过成立了宪法草案初稿讨论委员会，专门负责指导讨论的进行。
