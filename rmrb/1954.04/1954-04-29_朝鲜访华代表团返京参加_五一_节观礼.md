Title: Korean Delegation Returns to Beijing to Attend "May Day" Celebrations

Author:
Date: 1954-04-29
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency:
    The Korean People's Delegation, headed by Kim Ung-ki, and the accompanying art troupe, accompanied by Chu Tunan, Director of the Central People's Government Committee for Eliminating Illiteracy, and Ding Xilin, Vice Minister of Culture, returned to Beijing on the morning of the 28th from Tianjin by special train to attend the "May Day" celebrations.
    More than 500 people went to the station to welcome them, including Chen Shutong, Vice Chairman of the National Committee of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference and Vice Chairman of the China People's Anti-American Aggression and Aid Korea General Association, and Qi Yanming, Director of the General Office of the Central People's Government.
    Also present to welcome them were Choi Il, Ambassador of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea to China, Han Sorya, a well-known Korean writer, and Zheng Shengyan, Deputy Secretary-General of the Asian and Pacific Regions Peace Liaison Committee.

### 朝鲜访华代表团返京参加“五一”节观礼

1954-04-29
第4版()
专栏：

    朝鲜访华代表团返京参加“五一”节观礼
    据新华社讯：以金应基为首的朝鲜人民访华代表团和随行艺术团，由中央人民政府扫除文盲工作委员会主任委员楚图南、文化部副部长丁西林陪同，在二十八日上午从天津乘专车回到北京，参加“五一”节观礼。
    前往车站欢迎的，有中国人民政治协商会议全国委员会副主席、中国人民抗美援朝总会副主席陈叔通，中央人民政府办公厅主任齐燕铭等共五百多人。
    前往欢迎的，还有朝鲜民主主义人民共和国驻我国大使崔一，朝鲜著名作家韩雪野，亚洲及太平洋区域和平联络委员会副秘书长郑圣彦。
