Title: Central Meteorological Observatory Issues Gale Warning
Author:
Date: 1954-04-20
Page: 1

    Xinhua News Agency, 19th [dateline not given]—The Central Meteorological Observatory issued a gale warning at 8:00 PM on April 19th: It is expected that in the next 24 hours, the northern part of the South China Sea and the Taiwan Strait will experience northerly winds of around force 6. Tonight, the Yellow Sea and the Bohai Sea will still have northerly winds of force 5-6. On the 20th, the wind force will gradually weaken to around force 4-5.

### 中央气象台发布大风消息

1954-04-20
第1版()
专栏：

    中央气象台发布大风消息
    【新华社十九日讯】　中央气象台四月十九日下午八时发布大风消息：预计未来二十四小时内，南海北部和台湾海峡有六级左右的偏北风。今晚黄海、渤海仍有五、六级的偏北风。二十日风力将逐渐减弱到四五级左右。
