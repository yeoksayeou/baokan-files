Title: Southwest Region and Chongqing Municipality, Central South Region and Wuhan Municipality Organize Leading Officials from Government Organizations and Groups to Discuss the Initial Draft of the Constitution
Author:
Date: 1954-04-17
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, April 16th—Leading officials from government organizations and groups at the Southwest and Central South regional levels, and the Chongqing and Wuhan municipal levels, have begun or are preparing to discuss the initial draft of the Constitution of the People's Republic of China. The Southwest Administrative Committee, at its chairman's office meeting on April 10th, studied the issue of organizing leading officials from Southwest-level government organs, democratic parties, and people's organizations to discuss the initial draft of the Constitution. The meeting decided on the composition and timing of the discussion groups for the initial draft of the Constitution. From the 14th, leading officials from Southwest region-level government organs, democratic parties, and people's organizations have begun reading relevant documents. In addition, more than 140 leading officials from Chongqing municipality-level government organs and groups have also been divided into seven groups and began reading and discussing the initial draft of the Constitution from the 16th.

The government organs, democratic parties, and people's organizations at the Central South regional level and Wuhan Municipality are actively preparing to conduct discussions on the initial draft of the Constitution of the People's Republic of China. The Wuhan Municipal People's Representative Conference Consultative Committee convened a meeting of responsible persons from relevant units on the afternoon of April 15th, specifically to study the issue of organizing discussions on the initial draft of the Constitution. The meeting decided to establish an office for discussing the initial draft of the Constitution, responsible for guiding the discussions conducted by people from all walks of life in Central South region-level and Wuhan municipality-level organs.

### 西南区及重庆市、中南区及武汉市  组织机关团体负责人讨论宪法草案初稿

1954-04-17
第1版()
专栏：

    西南区及重庆市、中南区及武汉市
    组织机关团体负责人讨论宪法草案初稿
    【新华社十六日讯】西南、中南区一级和重庆、武汉市一级机关、团体的负责人，已开始或正准备对中华人民共和国宪法草案初稿的讨论。西南行政委员会在四月十日举行的主席办公会议上，研究了西南一级机关和民主党派、人民团体的负责人讨论宪法草案初稿的问题。会议决定了讨论宪法草案初稿的编组和时间。十四日起，西南区一级机关、民主党派、人民团体的负责人已开始阅读有关文件。此外，重庆市一级机关、团体的负责人一百四十多人，也已分编为七组，从十六日起开始对宪法草案初稿的阅读和讨论。
    中南区一级和武汉市的政府机关、各民主党派、各人民团体积极准备进行对中华人民共和国宪法草案初稿的讨论。武汉市各界人民代表会议协商委员会四月十五日下午召集各有关单位负责人会议，专门研究了组织讨论宪法草案初稿的问题。会议决定成立讨论宪法草案初稿办公室，负责指导中南区一级和武汉市一级机关各界人士讨论的进行。
