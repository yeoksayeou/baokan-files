Title: CPC Central Committee Sends Congratulatory Message to British Communist Party Congress
Author:
Date: 1954-04-16
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, April 15th: The Central Committee of the Communist Party of China sent a telegram of congratulations to the 23rd National Congress of the Communist Party of Great Britain, which is scheduled to open on April 16th. The text of the telegram is as follows:

To Comrade Pollitt, Executive Committee of the Communist Party of Great Britain, and to all the delegates of the 23rd National Congress of the Communist Party of Great Britain, dear comrades:

The Central Committee of the Communist Party of China warmly congratulates the convocation of the 23rd National Congress of the Communist Party of Great Britain. We wish the congress success and congratulate you on achieving further successes in the struggle for world peace and for the democratic rights of the British people.

          The Central Committee of the Communist Party of China

### 中共中央电贺英国共产党代表大会

1954-04-16
第1版()
专栏：

    中共中央电贺英国共产党代表大会
    【新华社十五日讯】英国共产党第二十三届全国代表大会定四月十六日召开，中国共产党中央委员会特致电祝贺。电文如下：英国共产党执行委员会亲爱的波立特同志转英国共产党第二十三届全国代表大会全体代表同志们：
    中国共产党中央委员会热烈祝贺英国共产党第二十三届全国代表大会的召开。祝贺大会的成功，祝贺你们在争取世界和平和争取英国人民民主的斗争中获得更进一步的成就。
          中国共产党中央委员会
