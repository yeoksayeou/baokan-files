Title: Government Administration Council Holds 212th Meeting
Author:
Date: 1954-04-10
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, April 8th—The 212th meeting of the Government Administration Council of the Central People's Government was held on the afternoon of the 8th. The meeting heard and approved the "Report on the National Middle School Education Conference" and the "Basic Summary of National General Education and Teacher Training Education Work in 1953 and Tasks for 1954" presented by Dong Chuncai, Deputy Minister of Education of the Central People's Government, and adopted instructions on improving and developing middle school education. The meeting also approved various appointment and removal proposals.

### 政务院举行第二一二次会议

1954-04-10
第1版()
专栏：

    政务院举行第二一二次会议
    【新华社八日讯】中央人民政府政务院第二百一十二次政务会议在八日下午举行。会议听了和批准了中央人民政府教育部董纯才副部长所作的“关于全国中学教育会议的报告”和“全国普通教育与师范教育工作一九五三年的基本总结和一九五四年的任务”，通过了关于改进和发展中学教育的指示。会议并通过了各项任免案。
