Title: Chinese Trade Union Delegation Arrives in Moscow
Author:
Date: 1954-04-19
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, 17th. TASS reports from Moscow on the 17th: The delegation of the All-China Federation of Trade Unions, led by the head of the delegation, Du Yanqing, arrived in Moscow by plane on the 17th at the invitation of the All-Union Central Council of Trade Unions to participate in the May Day celebrations in the Soviet Union. Greeting them at the airport were L. Solovyov, Secretary of the All-Union Central Council of Trade Unions, and heads of various industrial trade union central committees.

### 我国工会代表团到莫斯科

1954-04-19
第1版()
专栏：

    我国工会代表团到莫斯科
    【新华社十八日讯】据塔斯社莫斯科十七日讯：应全苏工会中央理事会的邀请到苏联参加“五一”节观礼的中华全国总工会代表团，在团长杜延庆率领下，已于十七日乘飞机到达莫斯科。前往机场欢迎的有全苏工会中央理事会书记列·索洛维耶夫和各产业工会中央委员会的负责人等。
