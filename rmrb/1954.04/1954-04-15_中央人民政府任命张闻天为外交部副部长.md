Title: The Central People's Government Appoints Zhang Wentian as Vice Minister of Foreign Affairs
Author:
Date: 1954-04-15
Page: 1

    Xinhua News Agency, 14th: Chairman Mao Zedong of the Central People's Government approved Premier Zhou Enlai's proposal from the State Council on the 14th and appointed Zhang Wentian as Vice Minister of Foreign Affairs of the Central People's Government. Zhang Wentian will continue to serve concurrently as the Ambassador of the People's Republic of China to the Union of Soviet Socialist Republics. ### 中央人民政府任命张闻天为外交部副部长

1954-04-15
第1版()
专栏：

    中央人民政府任命张闻天为外交部副部长
    【新华社十四日讯】中央人民政府毛泽东主席在十四日批准政务院周恩来总理的提请，任命张闻天为中央人民政府外交部副部长。张闻天仍兼中华人民共和国驻苏维埃社会主义共和国联盟大使。唐山钢厂炼钢车间订出增产节约计划
