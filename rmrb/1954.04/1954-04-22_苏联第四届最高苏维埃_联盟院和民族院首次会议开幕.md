Title: Opening of the First Sessions of the Soviet Union's Fourth Supreme Soviet, the Union Council and the Nationalities Council
Author:
Date: 1954-04-22
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, April 21st: The first session of the Union Council of the Fourth Supreme Soviet of the Soviet Union opened at 2:00 PM on April 20th in the Moscow Kremlin. Among the delegates attending the meeting were prominent public figures, advanced workers and collective farmers, as well as renowned scientists, writers, and teachers. Also present were diplomatic missions from various countries, journalists, and many other guests.

When the leaders of the Soviet Communist Party and the government, Malenkov, Molotov, Voroshilov, Khrushchev, Kaganovich, Saburov, Pervukhin, Suslov, Shatalin, and Kirichenko, appeared on the government rostrum, the delegates greeted them with prolonged applause.

The opening of the meeting was presided over by A. Kizulin, a representative of the Supreme Soviet of the Soviet Union and a Doctor of Agricultural Sciences. He pointed out that the first session of the Fourth Supreme Soviet of the Soviet Union was being held at a time of great significance. The great Soviet country is entering a period of great flourishing development. The elections to the Supreme Soviet on March 14th were another vivid demonstration of the great unity of the Soviet Communist Party, the government, and the Soviet people. Through this election, the Soviet people have approved the policies, work, and plans of the Communist Party and expressed their firm determination to continue contributing all their creative energies to the great cause of building communism in the Soviet state.

The meeting elected A. Volkov as Chairman of the Union Council. N. Kalchenko, A. Lebedev, V. Mzhavanadze, and Omarova were elected as Vice-Chairmen.

The meeting adopted the following agenda:
    (1) Election of the Credentials Committee of the Union Council.
    (2) Election of the Standing Committee of the Union Council.
    (3) Approval of the decrees formulated by the Presidium of the Supreme Soviet of the Soviet Union.
    (4) Election of the Presidium of the Supreme Soviet of the Soviet Union.
    (5) Formation of the government - the Council of Ministers of the Soviet Union.
    (6) Approval of the Soviet Union's national budget for 1954.

The meeting also elected the Credentials Committee, the Legislative Committee, the Budget Committee, and the Foreign Affairs Committee of the Union Council.

A joint session of the Union Council and the Nationalities Council was held at 10:00 AM on April 21st.

Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, April 20th: The first session of the Nationalities Council of the Fourth Supreme Soviet of the Soviet Union opened at 4:00 PM on April 20th in the Moscow Kremlin. Representatives from the Soviet Union's constituent republics, autonomous republics, national districts, and autonomous regions attended the meeting. Also present were public representatives from Moscow, personnel from foreign diplomatic missions, and Soviet and foreign journalists.

When Voroshilov, Bulganin, Mikoyan, and Shvernik took their seats on the rostrum, the audience greeted them with enthusiastic applause.

Professor O. Kirchenstein, Deputy Director of the Latvian Academy of Sciences and representative of the Nationalities Council of the Supreme Soviet of the Soviet Union, delivered the opening address. He said that the Communist Party's wise national policy has united the people of all nationalities in our country into a close-knit family and forged an unbreakable friendship among them, which is the source of strength and glory of the Soviet multi-ethnic state. The Soviet state is advancing rapidly. We are proud of our achievements in industry, agriculture, and culture, but we should not be satisfied with these achievements. We must achieve increasingly remarkable successes in the construction of communism.

The meeting then elected V. Latsis as Chairman of the Nationalities Council, and N. Avkhimovich, P. Komarov, M. Sultanova, and P. Tychyna as Vice-Chairmen.

The meeting adopted the following agenda:
    (1) Election of the Credentials Committee of the Nationalities Council.
    (2) Election of the Standing Committee of the Nationalities Council.
    (3) Approval of the decrees formulated by the Presidium of the Supreme Soviet of the Soviet Union.
    (4) Election of the Presidium of the Supreme Soviet of the Soviet Union.
    (5) Formation of the government - the Council of Ministers of the Soviet Union.
    (6) Approval of the Soviet Union's national budget for 1954.

The meeting also elected the Credentials Committee, the Legislative Committee, the Budget Committee, and the Foreign Affairs Committee of the Nationalities Council.

### 苏联第四届最高苏维埃  联盟院和民族院首次会议开幕

1954-04-22
第1版()
专栏：

    苏联第四届最高苏维埃
    联盟院和民族院首次会议开幕
    【新华社二十一日讯】据塔斯社莫斯科讯：苏联第四届最高苏维埃联盟院第一次会议，二十日下午二时在莫斯科克里姆林宫开幕。参加会议的代表中有著名的公众领袖，先进的工人和集体农民，以及著名的科学家、作家和教师。到会的还有各国外交使团、新闻记者和其他许多来宾。
    当苏联共产党和政府的领袖马林科夫、莫洛托夫、伏罗希洛夫、赫鲁晓夫、卡冈诺维奇、萨布罗夫、别尔乌辛、苏斯洛夫、夏塔林和基里钦科在政府席上出现时，与会代表们报以经久不息的掌声。
    会议由苏联最高苏维埃代表、农业科学博士亚·基祖林主持开幕。他指出，苏联第四届最高苏维埃第一次会议是在一个具有重大意义的时候举行的。伟大的苏维埃国家正在进入一个伟大的蓬勃发展的时期。三月十四日最高苏维埃的选举，是苏联共产党、政府和苏联人民伟大团结的又一次生动的表现。通过这次选举，苏联人民批准了共产党的政策、工作和计划，并表示坚决要为在苏维埃国家内建设共产主义的伟大事业继续贡献出他们的全部创造力量。
    会议选出亚·沃尔科夫为联盟院主席。尼·卡尔钦科、亚·列别杰夫、瓦·穆热瓦纳泽和奥马罗娃当选为副主席。
    会议通过议程如下：
    （一）选举联盟院资格审查委员会。
    （二）选举联盟院常务委员会。
    （三）批准苏联最高苏维埃主席团所制订的法令。
    （四）选举苏联最高苏维埃主席团。
    （五）组成政府——苏联部长会议。
    （六）批准苏联一九五四年国家预算。
    会议并选出了联盟院资格审查委员会、立法委员会、预算委员会和外交委员会。
    联盟院和民族院二十一日上午十时举行联席会议。
    【新华社二十一日讯】据塔斯社莫斯科二十日讯：苏联第四届最高苏维埃民族院第一次会议，二十日下午四时在莫斯科克里姆林宫开幕。参加会议的有苏联各加盟共和国、自治共和国、民族区和自治州的代表。到会的还有莫斯科的公众代表、各国外交使团人员以及苏联和外国记者。
    当伏罗希洛夫、布尔加宁、米高扬和什维尔尼克在主席台就座时，全场热烈鼓掌欢迎。
    苏联最高苏维埃民族院代表、拉脱维亚科学院副院长奥·基尔亨斯登教授致开幕词。他说，共产党英明的民族政策把我国各族人民团结成一个紧密的家庭，锻炼了他们之间的牢不可破的友谊，这种友谊是苏维埃多民族国家的力量和光荣的源泉。苏维埃国家正在迅速地前进。我们为我们在工业、农业和文化方面的成就感到骄傲，但是我们不应该以这些成就为满足，我们要在共产主义建设中取得日新月异的成就。
    会议接着选出维·拉齐斯为民族院主席，尼·阿夫希莫维奇、帕·科马罗夫、马·苏尔塔诺夫娃和巴·蒂奇纳为副主席。
    会议通过议程如下：
    一、选举民族院资格审查委员会。
    二、选举民族院常务委员会。
    三、批准苏联最高苏维埃主席团所制订的法令。
    四、选举苏联最高苏维埃主席团。
    五、组成政府——苏联部长会议。
    六、批准苏联一九五四年国家预算。
    会议并选出了民族院资格审查委员会、立法委员会、预算委员会和外交委员会。
