Title: Chinese Women's Federation Delegation to Romania
Author:
Date: 1954-04-27
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, April 26th: At the invitation of the Romanian Democratic Women's Federation, the delegation of the All-China Democratic Women's Federation, sent to Romania to participate in the "May Day" celebrations, departed from Beijing by plane for Bucharest on the 26th.

Those who went to the airport to see them off included Lu Cui, Minister of the International Work Department of the All-China Democratic Women's Federation, and Qi Yun, Deputy Minister, among others.

The wife of Romanian Ambassador to China, Guo Zuowen, and First Secretary, Mrs. Meng Tiannu, also went to the airport to see them off.

After participating in the "May Day" celebrations, the delegation will conduct a short visit and tour in Romania, and exchange working experience with Romanian women.

The list of the delegation is as follows:

Head of the delegation: Sun Wenshu, Director of the Secondary Normal Education Department of the Ministry of Education of the Central People's Government.

Members: Luo Yun, Director of the Tianjin Democratic Women's Federation
          Dong Bian, Deputy Director of New China Women's Magazine
          Lu Qiongying, Head of the Propaganda Section of the International Work Department of the All-China Democratic Women's Federation

### 我妇联代表团去罗马尼亚

1954-04-27
第1版()
专栏：

    我妇联代表团去罗马尼亚
    【新华社二十六日讯】中华全国民主妇女联合会应罗马尼亚民主妇女联合会的邀请，派往罗马尼亚参加“五一”节观礼的代表团已在二十六日乘机离京飞往布加勒斯特。
    前往机场欢送的有中华全国民主妇女联合会国际工作部部长陆璀、副部长戚云等。
    罗马尼亚人民共和国驻我国大使郭佐文夫人和一等秘书孟天努夫人也到机场欢送。
    代表团在参加“五一”节观礼后将在罗马尼亚进行短期的访问、参观，并与罗马尼亚妇女交流工作经验。
    代表团名单如下：
    团长：孙文淑中央人民政府教育部中等师范教育司司长
    团员：罗云天津市民主妇女联合会主任
          董边　新中国妇女社副社长
          卢琼英　中华全国民主妇女联合会国际工作部宣传科科长
