Title: SED Congress Continues; Ulbricht Delivers Concluding Remarks on the Discussion of the Central Report
Author:
Date: 1954-04-06
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Berlin, April 5th: The Fourth Congress of the Socialist Unity Party of Germany (SED) continued on April 3rd and 4th, during which the summary report of the Party Central Committee was discussed and adopted.

At the morning session on the 3rd, speeches were delivered by P. Heini, a railway worker and labor hero; Modrow, Secretary of the Free German Youth League of Berlin; P. Ferner, member of the Central Committee of the SED; E. Honnecker, Chairman of the Central Committee of the Free German Youth League, and others. In his speech, Ferner emphasized the need to strengthen the unified action of the German working people, as this is the main condition for winning the struggle against Adenauer's war policy and for the peaceful and democratic reunification of Germany. Walter Friedrich, President of the German Academy of Sciences, addressed the congress on behalf of the scientists of the German Democratic Republic and was warmly welcomed.

At the evening session on the 3rd, a delegation of heavy industry workers congratulated the congress and pledged that heavy industry workers were determined to fight under the leadership of the SED to implement the new policies of the Party and the government, to strengthen the German Democratic Republic, and to further improve the lives of the working people. Female labor hero Nolte, Lauscher, Chairman of the State Planning Commission of the German Democratic Republic, Ziller, Secretary of the Central Committee of the SED, and J. Becher, Minister of Culture of the German Democratic Republic, also spoke at the meeting.

At the meeting on the 4th, the discussion on the summary report of the Central Committee concluded. A total of eighty-three people spoke, and Walter Ulbricht, First Secretary of the Central Committee of the SED, delivered concluding remarks. He pointed out that the German Democratic Republic plays a leading role in the German people's struggle for peaceful reunification and stated that the German Democratic Republic would spare no effort to consolidate its friendship with the Soviet Union and tirelessly pursue a policy of peace. He emphasized that, in addition to the "General Treaty" and the "European Defense Community," there is another path, which is the path to peaceful reunification of Germany proposed by the Soviet Union, the path currently being taken by the German Democratic Republic.

At the meetings on the 3rd and 4th, representatives of fraternal parties, including the Bulgarian Communist Party, the Austrian Communist Party, the Albanian Labor Party, the Greek Communist Party, the Finnish Communist Party, the Belgian Communist Party, and the Spanish Communist Party, congratulated and addressed the congress. The congress also received a congratulatory telegram from the People's Party of Iran.

### 德国统一社会党代表大会继续举行  乌布利希就中央报告的讨论作了总结发言

1954-04-06
第4版()
专栏：

    德国统一社会党代表大会继续举行
    乌布利希就中央报告的讨论作了总结发言
    【新华社五日讯】据塔斯社柏林讯：德国统一社会党第四次代表大会四月三日和四日继续进行，会上讨论并通过了党中央委员会的总结报告。
    在三日上午会议上发言的有铁路工人、劳动英雄普·海尼，柏林自由德国青年联盟书记莫德罗夫，德国统一社会党中央委员会委员普·费尔纳，自由德国青年联盟中央委员会主席埃·昂纳克等。费尔纳在发言中强调必须加强德国劳动人民的一致行动，因为这是反对阿登纳的战争政策、争取和平和在民主的基础上重新统一德国的斗争取得胜利的主要条件。德国科学院院长华·腓特烈在会上代表德意志民主共和国的科学家向大会致贺，受到了热烈的欢迎。
    在三日晚间会议上，重工业工人代表团向代表大会致贺，并保证：重工业工人决心在德国统一社会党的领导下，为实现党和政府的新方针、为加强德意志民主共和国的力量和进一步改善劳动人民的生活而奋斗。女劳动英雄诺尔特、德意志民主共和国国家计划委员会主席劳施纳、德国统一社会党中央委员会书记齐勒、德意志民主共和国文化部部长约·贝希尔等也在会上发言。
    在四日会议上，结束了关于中央委员会总结报告的讨论，发言的共有八十三人，最后由德国统一社会党中央委员会第一书记瓦尔特·乌布利希作总结发言。他指出，德意志民主共和国在德国人民为和平统一而进行的斗争中起着主导作用，并表示，德意志民主共和国将竭力巩固和苏联的友谊，不倦地执行和平政策。他着重指出，除了“一般性条约”和“欧洲防务集团”以外，还有另外一条道路，这就是苏联建议的和平统一德国的道路，德意志民主共和国目前所走的道路。
    在三日和四日会议上，向大会祝贺和讲话的有：保加利亚共产党、奥地利共产党、阿尔巴尼亚劳动党、希腊共产党、芬兰共产党、比利时共产党和西班牙共产党等兄弟党的代表。大会并收到了伊朗人民党拍来的贺电。
