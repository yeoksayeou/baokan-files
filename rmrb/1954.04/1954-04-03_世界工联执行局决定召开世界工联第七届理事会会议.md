Title: World Federation of Trade Unions Executive Bureau Decides to Convene the 7th Session of the World Federation of Trade Unions General Council

Author: 
Date: 1954-04-03
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Vienna, April 2nd. TASS, Vienna, April 1st: The Executive Bureau of the World Federation of Trade Unions (WFTU) has decided to convene the 7th Session of the WFTU General Council. The decision states:

    The 7th Session of the WFTU General Council is scheduled to be held on November 25, 1954. The agenda of the meeting is:

    1. The international situation and the implementation of the resolutions of the Third World Trade Union Congress;

    2. The Charter of Workers' Trade Union Rights and launching a worldwide movement to defend and obtain trade union and democratic rights;

    3. Audit Committee report;

    4. Other matters.

    The Executive Bureau has accepted the invitation of the Central Council of Polish Trade Unions and decided that the 7th Session of the WFTU General Council will be held in Warsaw.

### 世界工联执行局决定召开世界工联第七届理事会会议

1954-04-03
第4版()
专栏：

    世界工联执行局决定召开世界工联第七届理事会会议
    【新华社二日讯】塔斯社维也纳一日讯：世界工会联合会执行局决定召开世界工联第七届理事会会议。决定说：
    世界工联第七届理事会定于一九五四年十一月二十五日召开，会议议程是：
    一、国际形势和世界工会第三次代表大会决议的执行情况；
    二、工人工会权利章程和展开一个保卫和获得工会与民主权利的世界规模的运动；
    三、审核委员会的报告；
    四、其他。
    执行局已接受波兰工会中央理事会的邀请，决定世界工联第七届理事会在华沙举行。
