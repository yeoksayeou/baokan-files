Title: Capital Establishes Draft Constitution First Draft Discussion Committee
Author:
Date: 1954-04-19
Page: 1

　　Xinhua News Agency, 18th: All circles in the capital are enthusiastically discussing the first draft of the Constitution of the People's Republic of China. On April 5th, Beijing established the Draft Constitution First Draft Discussion Committee of the People's Republic of China, with ten members, chaired by Zhang Youyu, Deputy Mayor of the Beijing Municipal People's Government. More than 230 people from the Beijing Municipal People's Government, responsible cadres from urban and suburban districts, heads of democratic parties, heads of people's organizations, and representatives from cultural, educational, and business circles have formed sixteen groups and have begun discussions successively since the 8th and 9th.

### 首都成立宪法草案初稿讨论委员会

1954-04-19
第1版()
专栏：

　　首都成立宪法草案初稿讨论委员会
    【新华社十八日讯】首都各界正热烈讨论中华人民共和国宪法草案初稿。四月五日，北京市成立了中华人民共和国宪法草案初稿讨论委员会，有委员十人，由北京市人民政府副市长张友渔负责主持。北京市人民政府、城郊各区党政负责干部、各民主党派、各人民团体负责人、文教界、工商界等共二百三十余人，组成了十六个小组，自八、九两日起已陆续开始讨论。
