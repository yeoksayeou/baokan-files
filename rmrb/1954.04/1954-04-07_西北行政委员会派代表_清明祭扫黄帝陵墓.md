Title: Northwest Administrative Committee Sends Representatives to Sweep Tombs at the Yellow Emperor's Mausoleum for Qingming Festival
Author:
Date: 1954-04-07
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Xi'an, 6th: On April 5th, Qingming Festival, the Northwest Administrative Committee and the Shaanxi Provincial People's Government sent representatives to Huangling County, Shaanxi Province, to sweep the tomb of the great ancestor of the Chinese nation, Xuanyuan Yellow Emperor. Ancient cypresses in the mausoleum stand tall and majestic in the spring sunshine. At 8:30 in the morning, a public memorial ceremony was held, presided over by Han Zhao'e, member of the Northwest Administrative Committee and Vice Chairman of the Shaanxi Provincial People's Government, and attended by Huo Zile, Deputy Secretary-General of the Northwest Administrative Committee, Liu Xianzeng, Deputy Director of the Cultural and Education Department of the Shaanxi Provincial People's Government, and others. Representatives from all walks of life in Huangling County and about 1,000 nearby farmers also participated in the public memorial. After the public memorial, the participants circled the mausoleum to pay their respects and then went to the Xuanyuan Yellow Emperor Temple at the foot of the mountain for a memorial service. On the morning of the 5th, residents near Huangling, as in previous years, successively visited the mausoleum and brought back cypress branches from the mausoleum as souvenirs.

### 西北行政委员会派代表  清明祭扫黄帝陵墓

1954-04-07
第1版()
专栏：

    西北行政委员会派代表
    清明祭扫黄帝陵墓
    【新华社西安六日电】　四月五日清明节，西北行政委员会和陕西省人民政府派代表到陕西省黄陵县祭扫中华民族伟大祖先轩辕黄帝陵墓。陵园内古柏参天，在春天的阳光下，显得庄严美丽。上午八时半，举行公祭仪式，由西北行政委员会委员、陕西省人民政府副主席韩兆鹗主祭，西北行政委员会副秘书长霍子乐、陕西省人民政府文教厅副厅长刘宪曾等陪祭。参加公祭的还有黄陵县各界代表和附近农民约一千多人。公祭后，与祭人员并绕陵一周致敬，又到山麓下轩辕黄帝庙举行祭奠。五日早晨，黄陵附近居民和往年习惯一样，陆续到陵墓前参谒，并带回陵上柏枝留作纪念。
