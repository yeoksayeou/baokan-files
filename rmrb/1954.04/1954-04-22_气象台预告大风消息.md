Title: Weather Forecast: Gale Warning
Author:
Date: 1954-04-22
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, April 21st—The Central Meteorological Observatory issued a gale warning at 8:00 PM on April 21st: It is expected that from noon tomorrow (the 22nd), there will be level 5-6 southwest winds in the central and southwestern parts of the Inner Mongolia Autonomous Region, the Northeast Plain, the Bohai Sea area, and the Shandong Peninsula.

It is expected that from tomorrow evening, there will be northerly winds of around level 6 in the northwestern part of the Inner Mongolia Autonomous Region.

### 气象台预告大风消息

1954-04-22
第1版()
专栏：

    气象台预告大风消息
    【新华社二十一日讯】中央气象台二十一日下午八时发布大风消息：预计从明日（二十二日）中午起，内蒙自治区的中部和西南部、东北平原、渤海地区和山东半岛将有五、六级的西南风。
    预计明日傍晚起，内蒙自治区的西北部将有六级左右的偏北风。
