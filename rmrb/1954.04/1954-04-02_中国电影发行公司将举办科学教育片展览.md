Title: China Film Distribution Company to Hold Science Education Film Exhibition
Author: 
Date: 1954-04-02
Page: 3

Cultural Briefs

    China Film Distribution Company will hold a science education film exhibition in six major cities including Beijing, Shenyang, Shanghai, Hankou, Chongqing, and Xi'an in April and May. The purpose is to popularize scientific knowledge and cultivate the public's interest in science.
    These scientific short films are extremely rich in content, including thirteen types such as "Zoo," "Science and Technology (First Collection)," "Li Xikui Shunting Method," "Sports and Health," "How to Achieve High Yield Cotton," "Hao Jianxiu Work Method," "Solar and Lunar Eclipses," "Safe Use of Electricity at Home," "Painless Childbirth," "Soil and Water Conservation," "Eradicating Rice Pests - Rice Stem Borer," "Eliminating Locusts," and "Prevention and Treatment of Anthrax."

### 中国电影发行公司将举办科学教育片展览

1954-04-02
第3版()
专栏：文化简讯

    中国电影发行公司将举办科学教育片展览
    中国电影发行公司四五月间将在北京、沈阳、上海、汉口、重庆、西安等六大城市举行科学教育片展览，普及科学知识，培养群众热爱科学的兴趣。
    这些科学短片，内容极为丰富，包括“动物园”、“科学与技术（第一辑）”、“李锡奎调车法”、“体育与健康”、“怎样丰产棉花”、“郝建秀工作法”、“日食和月食”、“家庭安全用电”、“无痛分娩”、“水土保持”、“根治水稻害虫——三化螟”、“消灭飞蝗”、“防治炭疽病”等十三种。
