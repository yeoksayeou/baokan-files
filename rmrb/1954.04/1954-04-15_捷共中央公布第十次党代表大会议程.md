Title: CPC Central Committee of Czechoslovakia Announces Agenda for the Tenth Party Congress
Author: 
Date: 1954-04-15
Page: 4

    CPC Central Committee of Czechoslovakia Announces Agenda for the Tenth Party Congress
    [Xinhua News Agency, Prague, April 14] The "Red Rights Newspaper," the organ of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of Czechoslovakia, published a resolution of the Party Central Committee on the 12th. The resolution announced that the Tenth Party Congress will be held on June 11 this year. The agenda of the congress is as follows:
    I. Sapototsky delivers the opening speech;
    II. Report on the work of the CPC Central Committee and the future tasks of the Party - Reporter: First Secretary of the Central Committee Secretariat, Novotny;
    III. Report of the Central Inspection Committee - Reporter: Chairman of the Central Inspection Committee, Shtitka;
    IV. Instructions on formulating the national economic development plan for 1955 and a short-term plan for further increasing agricultural production in the next two to three years - Reporter: Siroky;
    V. Report on the revision of the Party Constitution - Reporter: Secretary of the Central Committee Secretariat, Pasek;
    VI. Election of the Party Central Committee and the Central Inspection Committee.
    The resolution also stipulated the method for electing delegates to the congress.

### 捷共中央公布第十次党代表大会议程

1954-04-15
第4版()
专栏：

    捷共中央公布第十次党代表大会议程
    【新华社布拉格十四日电】捷克斯洛伐克共产党中央机关报“红色权利报”十三日刊载了党中央委员会十二日的一项决议。决议宣布在今年六月十一日召开第十次党代表大会，代表大会的议程如下：
    一、萨波托斯基致开幕词；
    二、关于捷共中央的工作和党的今后任务的报告——报告人：中央委员会书记处第一书记诺沃提尼；
    三、中央检查委员会的报告——报告人：中央检查委员会主席什蒂特卡；
    四、关于制订发展一九五五年国民经济计划和最近两三年内进一步提高农业生产的短期计划的指示——报告人：西罗基；
    五、修改党章的报告——报告人：中央委员会书记处书记帕歇克；
    六、选举党中央委员会和中央检查委员会。
    决议还规定了大会代表的选举办法。
