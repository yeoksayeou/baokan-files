Title: PLA Troops Stationed in Zhejiang Shot Down and Damaged One Chiang Kai-shek Bandit Plane Each
Author:
Date: 1954-04-10
Page: 1

    Xinhua News Agency, Nanjing, 8th. The People's Liberation Army troops stationed in Zhejiang shot down and damaged one American-made Chiang Kai-shek bandit F-47 fighter plane each on March 18th, which had intruded into the airspace over Zhejiang. The shot-down enemy plane crashed into the sea near Tiandao Island in southeastern Zhejiang.
    Prior to this incident, the People's Liberation Army troops stationed in Zhejiang shot down one Chiang Kai-shek bandit plane each on February 1st and 9th.

### 人民解放军驻浙江的部队又击落击伤蒋匪飞机各一架

1954-04-10
第1版()
专栏：

    人民解放军驻浙江的部队又击落击伤蒋匪飞机各一架
    【新华社南京八日电】中国人民解放军驻浙江的部队在三月十八日又击落和击伤窜入我浙江上空的美造蒋匪F—四七型战斗机各一架。被击落的敌机坠入浙东南田岛附近海中。
    在这次事件以前，人民解放军驻浙江的部队曾在二月一日和九日，先后击落蒋匪飞机各一架。
