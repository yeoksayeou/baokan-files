Title: The Korean People's Visiting Delegation Visits and Performs in Gui Sui

Author:
Date: 1954-04-01
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Gui Sui, March 31st. The Korean People's Visiting Delegation, headed by Kim Ung-gi, arrived in Gui Sui on the 28th and has met with representatives from all ethnic groups in the Inner Mongolia Autonomous Region and the general public of Gui Sui. Over the past two days, the delegation's accompanying art troupe has given three visiting performances. Some members of the delegation and the art troupe visited the Inner Mongolia Institute of Animal Husbandry and Veterinary Medicine and the famous Xilitu Zhao Temple in Gui Sui. On the 30th, all members of the delegation held discussions separately with representatives of farmers and herdsmen from rural areas and grasslands, model workers from Gui Sui, and representatives of youth and women from all ethnic groups. Delegation member and "Hero of the Republic" Liu Wanshu also gave a report to 50,000 citizens of Gui Sui. After the meeting, the art troupe gave an open-air performance for the audience. During the visits, reports, and performances, the delegation was warmly welcomed by people from all ethnic groups and all walks of life in the Inner Mongolia Autonomous Region and Gui Sui. When the representatives visited the Inner Mongolia Institute of Animal Husbandry and Veterinary Medicine, "Hero of the Republic" Liu Wanshu and model fighter Cui Fenyu were lifted up several times by the enthusiastic students.

### 朝鲜人民访华代表团  在归绥访问和演出

1954-04-01
第1版()
专栏：

    朝鲜人民访华代表团
    在归绥访问和演出
   【新华社归绥三十一日电】以金应基团长为首的朝鲜人民访华代表团二十八日到达归绥后，已和内蒙古自治区各族各界代表、归绥市广大市民见面。两天来，代表团随行艺术团已作了三次访问演出。代表团部分团员和艺术团人员参观了内蒙古畜牧兽医学院和归绥市著名的席勒图召庙。三十日，代表团全体团员分头与来自农村和草原的农、牧民代表、归绥市模范工人、各族青年、妇女代表举行了座谈会。代表团团员、“共和国英雄”柳万洙并向归绥市五万市民作了报告。会后，艺术团又向听众作了露天演出。在参观、报告、演出等活动中，代表团受到内蒙古自治区和归绥市各族各界人民的热烈欢迎。当代表们参观内蒙古畜牧兽医学院时，“共和国英雄”柳万洙和模范战斗员崔粉玉好几次被热情的同学们抬了起来。
