Title: Yan'an Agencies, Troops, and Citizen Representatives Pay Respects to "April 8th" Martyrs
Author:
Date: 1954-04-09
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Yan'an Telegram: On April 8th, more than 400 representatives from Yan'an agencies, troops, and citizens went to the "April 8th" Martyrs Cemetery to pay respects, commemorating the eighth anniversary of the martyrdom of Wang Ruofei, Qin Bangxian, Ye Ting, Deng Fa, Huang Qisheng, and other martyrs. Representatives from the Second Detachment of the Second General Sub-delegation of the National People's Delegation to Comfort the People's Liberation Army, currently in Yan'an, also participated in the memorial ceremony. At 10:00 AM, the ceremony began with mournful music. The chief officiants, Zhang Hanwu, Deputy Head of the Second Detachment of the Second General Sub-delegation of the National People's Delegation to Comfort the People's Liberation Army, and Zhao Guoqing, Deputy Secretary of the Yan'an Local Committee of the Chinese Communist Party, offered incense, sacrificial offerings, libations, and wreaths, and read the memorial address. In their speeches, Zhang Hanwu and Guo Changnian, Deputy Commissioner of the Yan'an Commissioner's Office, recounted the circumstances of the deaths of Wang Ruofei, Qin Bangxian, Ye Ting, Deng Fa, Huang Qisheng, and other martyrs with deep sorrow. They called on all cadres and soldiers to conscientiously study the resolutions of the Fourth Plenary Session of the Seventh Central Committee of the Chinese Communist Party and the general line of the state during the transition period, maintain revolutionary vigilance and the spirit of hard struggle, and work hard to do all tasks well and build our country.

### 延安机关、部队和市民代表祭奠“四八”烈士

1954-04-09
第1版()
专栏：

    延安机关、部队和市民代表祭奠“四八”烈士
    据新华社延安电：延安各机关、部队和市民代表四百多人八日前往“四八”烈士陵园祭奠，纪念王若飞、秦邦宪、叶挺、邓发、黄齐生等烈士殉难八周年。现在延安的全国人民慰问人民解放军代表团第二总分团第二分团的代表也参加了祭奠。上午十时，祭奠仪式在哀乐声中开始。由主祭人全国人民慰问人民解放军代表团第二总分团第二分团副团长张汉武和中共延安地方委员会副书记赵国卿上香、献馔、奠酒、献花圈、读祭文。张汉武和延安专员公署副专员郭长年在讲话中，都以沉痛的心情追述了王若飞、秦邦宪、叶挺、邓发、黄齐生等烈士遇难的经过。他们号召全体干部和战士认真学习中国共产党七届四中全会的决议和国家在过渡时期的总路线，保持革命警惕和坚苦奋斗的精神，努力做好各项工作，建设我们的国家。
