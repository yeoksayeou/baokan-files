Title: Mongolian Cycling Team Enters Our Country En Route to Beijing
Author:
Date: 1954-04-23
Page: 1

    Xinhua News Agency, Gui Sui, April 22nd: At the invitation of the Physical Culture and Sports Commission of the Central People's Government of our country, the Ulaanbaatar-Beijing Cycling Team of the Mongolian People's Republic, which is visiting our country and participating in the "May 1st" celebrations, arrived at the border of the Xilin Gol League of the Inner Mongolia Autonomous Region of our country on the afternoon of the 21st, after a long journey of 1,100 li, led by Dambi, Vice Chairman of the Mongolian National Sports Commission.
    Those who went to welcome them included: Huang Zhong, member and Deputy Secretary-General of the Physical Culture and Sports Commission of the Central People's Government, Khasabatar, Deputy Director of the Physical Culture and Sports Commission of the Inner Mongolia Autonomous Region People's Government, Bu He, Deputy Director of the Cultural Affairs Bureau of the Inner Mongolia Autonomous Region People's Government, and more than 100 representatives of all ethnic groups and people from all walks of life in the local area.
    After all the members of the Cycling Team of the Mongolian People's Republic arrived at the border of our country, they shook hands warmly with the welcomers and accepted gifts from them.
    On the morning of the 22nd, the Mongolian Cycling Team, accompanied by the welcomers, continued to cycle towards Beijing.

### 蒙古自行车远足队  进入我国境前来北京

1954-04-23
第1版()
专栏：

    蒙古自行车远足队
    进入我国境前来北京
    【新华社归绥二十二日电】应我国中央人民政府体育运动委员会的邀请，前来我国访问并参加“五一”节观礼的蒙古人民共和国乌兰巴托—北京自行车远足队，在蒙古国家体育运动委员会副主席丹毕率领下，经过一千一百华里的长途行程，已在二十一日下午到达我国内蒙古自治区锡林郭勒盟国境。
    前往欢迎的有：中央人民政府体育运动委员会委员、副秘书长黄中，内蒙古自治区人民政府体育运动委员会副主任喀萨巴塔尔，内蒙古自治区人民政府文化事业管理局副局长布赫和当地各族、各界人民代表共一百多人。
    蒙古人民共和国自行车远足队全体队员到达我国国境后，与欢迎者亲切握手，并接受欢迎者的献礼。
    蒙古自行车远足队二十二日上午在欢迎者的陪同下，继续骑自行车向北京前进。
