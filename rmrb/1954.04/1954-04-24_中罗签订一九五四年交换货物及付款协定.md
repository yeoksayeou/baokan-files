Title: China and Romania Sign 1954 Goods Exchange and Payment Agreement
Author:
Date: 1954-04-24
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, April 24th. After friendly negotiations, the government representatives of the People's Republic of China and the Romanian People's Republic signed the China-Romania 1954 Goods Exchange and Payment Agreement in Bucharest on the 19th.

The agreement stipulates that the goods exchanged will be more extensive than in 1953. China will export cotton, jute, non-ferrous metals, various products needed by the food industry, and silk to Romania. Romania will export petroleum products, chemical raw materials, cotton cloth, and industrial products to China.

The agreement was signed by A. Bârlădeanu, Minister of Foreign Trade of the Romanian People's Republic, on the Romanian side, and Wang Youping, Ambassador Extraordinary and Plenipotentiary of the People's Republic of China to the Romanian People's Republic, on the Chinese side.

### 中罗签订一九五四年交换货物及付款协定

1954-04-24
第1版()
专栏：

    中罗签订一九五四年交换货物及付款协定
    【新华社二十四日讯】中华人民共和国和罗马尼亚人民共和国的政府代表经过友好的谈判后，于十九日在布加勒斯特签订了中罗一九五四年交换货物及付款协定。
    协定规定所交换的货物比一九五三年更为广泛。中国将向罗马尼亚输出棉花、黄麻、有色金属、食品工业所需要的各种产品和绸缎等。罗马尼亚将向中国输出石油产品、化工原料、棉布和工业品等。
    在协定上签字的，罗马尼亚方面是罗马尼亚人民共和国对外贸易部部长亚·巴尔勒德亚努，中国方面是中华人民共和国驻罗马尼亚人民共和国特命全权大使王幼平。
