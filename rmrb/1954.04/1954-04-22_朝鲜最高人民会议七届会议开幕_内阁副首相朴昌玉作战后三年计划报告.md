Title: The 7th Session of the Supreme People's Assembly of North Korea Opens; Vice Premier Pak Chang-ok of the Cabinet Reports on the Post-War Three-Year Plan

Author:
Date: 1954-04-22
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Pyongyang, April 21st: According to the Korean Central News Agency, the 7th Session of the Supreme People's Assembly of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea opened in Pyongyang on the 20th. Deputies from all over the country attended the meeting. Premier of the Cabinet, Marshal Kim Il-sung, Vice Premiers of the Cabinet Choe Yong-gon, Pak Chang-ok, Kim Il, Choe Chang-ik, Hong Myung-hui, Jeong Il-ryong, Park Ui-wan, and ministers of the Cabinet also attended the meeting.

The meeting unanimously adopted the following agenda:
    1. On the Three-Year Plan for the Rehabilitation and Development of the People's Economy of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea from 1954 to 1956; 2. On the Summary of the Implementation of the State Budget of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea from 1950 to 1953 and the State Budget for 1954; 3. On the Approval of the Decrees of the Standing Committee of the Supreme People's Assembly of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea.

Vice Premier of the Cabinet and Chairman of the State Planning Commission, Pak Chang-ok, delivered a report on the draft of the Three-Year Plan for the Rehabilitation and Development of the People's Economy of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea from 1954 to 1956, which was warmly welcomed by all attendees.

### 朝鲜最高人民会议七届会议开幕  内阁副首相朴昌玉作战后三年计划报告

1954-04-22
第4版()
专栏：

    朝鲜最高人民会议七届会议开幕
    内阁副首相朴昌玉作战后三年计划报告
    【新华社平壤二十一日电】据朝鲜中央通讯社讯：朝鲜民主主义人民共和国最高人民会议第七届会议二十日在平壤开幕。出席会议的有来自全国各地的议员。内阁首相金日成元帅，内阁副首相崔庸健、朴昌玉、金一、崔昌益、洪命熹、郑一龙、朴义琓和内阁各相也出席了会议。
    会议一致通过了如下的议程：
    一、关于朝鲜民主主义人民共和国一九五四年到一九五六年人民经济恢复发展的三年计划；二、关于朝鲜民主主义人民共和国一九五零年到一九五三年国家预算执行情况总结及一九五四年的国家预算；三、关于批准朝鲜民主主义人民共和国最高人民会议常任委员会政令。
    内阁副首相兼国家计划委员会委员长朴昌玉就第一项议程作了关于朝鲜民主主义人民共和国一九五四年到一九五六年人民经济恢复发展的三年计划草案的报告，受到了全体到会者的热烈欢迎。
