Title: Korean Delegation Leaves Chongqing for Chengdu
Author:
Date: 1954-04-10
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency dispatch:

The entire Korean People's Delegation, led by Deputy Heads of Delegation Li Yong-ho and Li Dong-ying, and the entire members of the Korean People's Army Ensemble, accompanied by Chu Tu-nan, Director of the Central People's Government Committee for the Elimination of Illiteracy, left Chongqing by special train at 5 p.m. on the 9th for a visit to Chengdu after a five-day visit in Southwest China.

During their five-day visit in Chongqing, the Korean People's Delegation had extensive meetings with people from all walks of life in Chongqing and received a warm welcome. The delegation extended their regards to the wounded and sick of the Chinese People's Liberation Army and the Chinese People's Volunteers, as well as the families of the Volunteers' martyrs and military personnel; they also visited the famous iron and steel plant and the newly built power plant. The Korean People's Army Ensemble gave wonderful performances in the past few days, attended by more than 47,000 people.

[Another dispatch] Deputy Head of the Korean People's Delegation, Li Dong-ying, and other representatives visited the families of Chinese People's Volunteers martyrs and military personnel in the Southwest Region and Chongqing City on the 7th. The consolation meeting was held in the auditorium of the Chongqing Municipal People's Representative Conference Consultative Committee. More than 300 family members of the Volunteers’ martyrs and military personnel attended the consolation meeting. The representatives received flowers from representatives of the martyrs' families and military personnel in warm applause.

After Luo Shigao, Deputy Mayor of Chongqing, delivered a speech on behalf of the martyrs' families and military personnel in the Southwest Region and Chongqing City, Deputy Head Li Dong-ying delivered a message of consolation. He first extended cordial greetings to the families of the Volunteers' martyrs and military personnel, and said: The great achievements made by your sons, husbands, and brothers on the soil of Korea will never be erased from Korean history; we will cherish this noble friendship as we cherish our own eyes, and will continue to strengthen the noble friendship between the peoples of China and Korea.

After Li Dong-ying's speech, the representatives presented flowers to the representatives of the martyrs' families and military personnel. Representatives of the Volunteers’ martyrs' families and military personnel, Liu Yushun, father of Liu Xingwen, a second-class hero and first-class meritorious worker of the Volunteers, and Yi Dengying, father of Yi Yi, a first-class meritorious worker of the Volunteers, expressed warm welcome and heartfelt gratitude for the warm visit and cordial consolation of the Korean People's Delegation in their reply speeches. They also stated that they would continue to take the lead and set an example in practical work, learn from the brave and hardworking spirit of the Korean people, build our beautiful socialist motherland, and more actively assist the Korean people in their recovery and reconstruction work.

### 朝鲜访华团离开重庆去成都

1954-04-10
第1版()
专栏：

    朝鲜访华团离开重庆去成都
    据新华社讯：以李永镐和李东英副团长为首的朝鲜人民访华代表团来西南访问的全体团员和朝鲜人民军协奏团全体团员，在中央人民政府扫除文盲工作委员会主任委员楚图南陪同下，在重庆进行了五天多的访问活动后，在九日下午五时乘专车去成都访问。
    朝鲜人民访华代表团在重庆五天多的访问中，和重庆各界人民作了广泛的会见，受到热诚的欢迎。代表团慰问了中国人民解放军和中国人民志愿军伤病员以及志愿军烈士家属和军人家属；还参观了著名的钢铁厂和新建的电力厂。朝鲜人民军协奏团几天来作了精彩的演出，观看演出的有四万七千多人。
      【又讯】朝鲜人民访华代表团副团长李东英和代表等多人曾在七日慰问了西南区和重庆市的中国人民志愿军烈士家属和军人家属。慰问大会在重庆市各界人民代表会协商委员会大礼堂举行。出席慰问大会的志愿军烈属、军属有三百多人。代表们在热烈的掌声中接受了烈属、军属代表的献花。
    重庆市人民政府副市长罗士高代表西南区和重庆市的烈属、军属致词后，继由李东英副团长致慰问词。他首先向志愿军烈属、军属致以亲切的慰问，并说：你们的儿子、丈夫和兄弟在朝鲜土地上所建立的丰功伟绩，将在朝鲜历史上永不磨灭；我们将像爱护自己的眼珠一样爱护这种高贵的友谊，并将继续加强中朝两国人民高贵的友谊。
    李东英致词后，代表们向烈属、军属代表献花。志愿军烈属、军属代表，志愿军二级英雄、一等功臣刘兴文烈士的父亲刘玉顺和志愿军一等功臣易毅的父亲易登瀛在致答词中，对朝鲜人民访华代表团的热情访问和亲切慰问都表示热烈的欢迎和衷心的感谢。他们并表示：今后将继续在实际工作中起带头和模范作用，学习朝鲜人民勇敢勤劳的精神，来建设我们美好的社会主义祖国，和更积极地援助朝鲜人民恢复和重建工作。
