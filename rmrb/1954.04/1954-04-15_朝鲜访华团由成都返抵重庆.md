Title: Korean Delegation Returns to Chongqing from Chengdu
Author:
Date: 1954-04-15
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency: The members of the Korean People's Delegation to China and the Korean People's Army Ensemble, after a four-day visit to Chengdu in Southwest China, returned to Chongqing from Chengdu on the 14th.

During their visit to Chengdu, the delegation met extensively with people from all walks of life in Chengdu and received a warm welcome. The delegation visited revolutionary disabled soldiers who were studying and recuperating, as well as family members of martyrs and military personnel of the Chinese People's Volunteer Army. The delegation also held discussions with workers, youth, women, agricultural, forestry and water conservancy workers, model agricultural laborers, and agricultural science workers from Sichuan Province and Chengdu. The delegation also reported to more than 30,000 people from all walks of life in Chengdu on the great victories and achievements of the Korean people in the just war to defend their homeland and in the work of restoration and reconstruction. The Korean People's Army Ensemble also gave a wonderful performance for the people of Chengdu.

### 朝鲜访华团由成都返抵重庆

1954-04-15
第1版()
专栏：

    朝鲜访华团由成都返抵重庆
    据新华社讯：朝鲜人民访华代表团访问西南的团员和朝鲜人民军协奏团，在成都进行了四天的访问活动后，十四日由成都返抵重庆。
    代表团在成都访问期间，和成都市各界人民作了广泛的会见，受到热烈的欢迎。代表团慰问了在学习和休养中的革命残废军人以及中国人民志愿军烈士家属和军人家属。代表团还和四川省、成都市的工人、青年、妇女、农林和水利工作者、农业劳动模范、农业科学工作者举行了座谈。代表团还向成都市各界三万多人报告了朝鲜人民在保卫祖国的正义战争中和恢复重建工作中的伟大胜利和成就。朝鲜人民军协奏团也向成都人民作了精彩的演出。
