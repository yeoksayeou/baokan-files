Title: Our Great Motherland (Picture)
Author:
Date: 1954-04-13
Page: 1

    Our Great Motherland
    Farmers in various places are appropriately increasing the production of oil crops to supply the needs of the country and the farmers. The rape plants in Hanshou County, Hunan Province, are growing very well and a bumper harvest is expected. In this rich field of rape, the members of the Tong Dezheng Mutual Aid Team in Hanshou County are tidying up the ditches in the fields to protect the growth of the crops.

### 我们伟大的祖国（图片）

1954-04-13
第1版()
专栏：

    我们伟大的祖国
    各地农民都在适当地增产油料作物，以供应国家和农民的需要。湖南省汉寿县一带的油菜长得很好，可望丰收。在这一片丰盛的油菜田里，汉寿县童德正互助组的组员正在田间整理沟道，保护作物的成长。
