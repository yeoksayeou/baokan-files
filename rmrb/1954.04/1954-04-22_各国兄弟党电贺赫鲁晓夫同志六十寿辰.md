Title: Fraternal Parties of Various Countries Send Congratulatory Telegrams on Comrade Khrushchev's Sixtieth Birthday
Author:
Date: 1954-04-22
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, April 20th: According to TASS, on the occasion of the sixtieth birthday of N.S. Khrushchev, First Secretary of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of the Soviet Union, the Communist Party of the Soviet Union continues to receive congratulatory telegrams from Communist and Workers' Parties of various countries, including: the Polish United Workers' Party, the Czechoslovak Communist Party, the Romanian Workers' Party, the Hungarian Working People's Party, the Bulgarian Communist Party, the Socialist Unity Party of Germany, the Mongolian People's Revolutionary Party, and the Finnish Communist Party. The telegrams congratulate him on his good health and wish him continued creative work for the happiness of the Soviet people, for the victory of Communism, and for the peace and friendship of all peoples.

### 各国兄弟党电贺赫鲁晓夫同志六十寿辰

1954-04-22
第4版()
专栏：

    各国兄弟党电贺赫鲁晓夫同志六十寿辰
    【新华社二十日讯】据塔斯社莫斯科讯：在苏联共产党中央委员会第一书记尼·谢·赫鲁晓夫六十岁寿辰之际，苏联共产党继续收到各国共产党和工人党的贺电，其中有：波兰统一工人党、捷克斯洛伐克共产党、罗马尼亚工人党、匈牙利劳动人民党、保加利亚共产党、德国统一社会党、蒙古人民革命党和芬兰共产党等。贺电中祝贺他身体健康，并祝他在为苏联人民的幸福、为共产主义的胜利、为各国人民的和平与友谊方面继续作出创造性的工作。
