Title: Korean Workers' Party Central Committee Issues Slogans Celebrating "May Day"
Author:
Date: 1954-04-24
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency, Pyongyang, April 22nd: The Central Committee of the Korean Workers' Party issued slogans on the 20th to celebrate the 1954 International Labor Day.

In its slogans, the Central Committee of the Korean Workers' Party extended national thanks to the people of the Soviet Union, the People's Republic of China, and other people's democratic countries for their enormous assistance to the post-war reconstruction of Korea; extended warm congratulations to the great Soviet people who are victoriously engaged in communist construction, to the fraternal Chinese people who have achieved great results in socialist construction, and to the people of all people's democratic countries who are victoriously engaged in the cause of socialist construction; extended fraternal congratulations to the people of colonial, semi-colonial, and dependent countries who are fighting for the national independence, freedom, and peace of their countries; and extended warm congratulations to all peace-loving people around the world who are waging a resolute struggle for lasting world peace, friendship among peoples, and against warmongers. The slogans wished for the eternal friendship and solidarity between the people of the Soviet Union, China, and the people of the people's democratic countries, and the Korean people.

The slogans called on all workers, farmers, and intellectuals of the Republic to strive to complete and overfulfill the three-year plan for the post-war recovery and development of the national economy, and to promote the peaceful reunification and prosperous development of the fatherland; called on the Korean People's Army to heighten its vigilance, defend the fruits of the people's labor, and further consolidate and strengthen the combat friendship and unity with the fraternal Chinese People's Volunteers; and called on all the people of the Republic to further strengthen the work of cherishing and supporting the Korean People's Army and the Chinese People's Volunteers, to fight against special agents and saboteurs, assassins, to give full play to creativity, to extensively launch patriotic saving campaigns, and to further strengthen the worker-peasant alliance, and to consolidate and strengthen the ideological unity and solidarity of all the Korean people.

The Central Committee of the Korean Workers' Party also called on the working class and people in the southern half of the Republic to further launch a struggle for national salvation, to oppose the American occupiers and the Syngman Rhee traitorous bandit gang, and to rise up to fight for the peaceful reunification and independence of the fatherland.

### 朝鲜劳动党中央发布庆祝“五一”节口号

1954-04-24
第4版()
专栏：

    朝鲜劳动党中央发布庆祝“五一”节口号
    【新华社平壤二十二日电】朝鲜劳动党中央委员会在二十日发布庆祝一九五四年“五一”国际劳动节的口号。
    朝鲜劳动党中央委员会在口号中向给予朝鲜战后恢复建设事业以巨大援助的苏联、中华人民共和国和各人民民主国家人民致以全民性的感谢；向胜利地进行共产主义建设的伟大的苏联人民，向在社会主义建设中获得巨大成果的兄弟般的中国人民，向胜利地进行社会主义建设事业的各人民民主国家人民致以热烈的祝贺；向为争取自己祖国的民族独立、自由与和平而斗争的殖民地、半殖民地及附属国的人民致以兄弟般的祝贺；向为世界持久和平、各国人民之间的友谊和反对战争挑拨者而进行坚决斗争的全世界爱好和平的人民致以热烈祝贺。口号中祝苏联、中国和各人民民主国家人民与朝鲜人民之间永恒的友谊团结万岁。
    口号中号召共和国全体工人、农民、知识分子为完成和超额完成战后人民经济恢复发展的三年计划而斗争，促进祖国的和平统一与繁荣发展；号召朝鲜人民军提高警惕，保卫人民的劳动成果，进一步巩固和加强与兄弟般的中国人民志愿军的战斗友谊和团结；并号召共和国全体人民进一步加强爱护和支援朝鲜人民军和中国人民志愿军的工作，和特务分子及破坏、暗害分子作斗争，发挥创造性，广泛展开爱国节约运动，进一步加强工农联盟，巩固和加强全体朝鲜人民思想上的统一和团结。
    朝鲜劳动党中央委员会并号召共和国南半部的工人阶级和人民进一步展开救国斗争，反对美国占领者和李承晚卖国匪帮，奋起争取祖国的和平统一与独立。
