Our Great Motherland
The 2.1 million mu of wheat planted by Guangdong farmers last winter have generally yielded a bumper harvest, with the provincial average increasing by 10% compared to last year. This is a picture of members of the First Agricultural Production Cooperative of Tongjing Township, Xinhui County, Guangdong Province, selecting wheat seeds in the abundant wheat fields, preparing to expand the wheat planting area this winter.

### 我们伟大的祖国（图片）

1954-04-26
第1版()
专栏：

    我们伟大的祖国
    广东农民在去年冬季种植的二百一十万亩小麦，普遍获得丰收，全省平均比去年增产一成。这是广东省新会县桐井乡第一农业生产合作社的社员在丰收的麦田里挑选麦种，准备今冬扩大小麦的种植面积。
