Title: Soviet, Mongolian, French, Italian and Other Countries' Trade Union Representatives Arrive in Beijing to Attend "May Day" Celebrations
Author:
Date: 1954-04-29
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency dispatch: At the invitation of the All-China Federation of Trade Unions, the Soviet Trade Union Delegation, the Head of the Trade Union Delegation of the Mongolian People's Republic, the French General Confederation of Labor Delegation, and the Italian General Confederation of Labor Delegation arrived in Beijing by plane on the afternoon of the 28th to participate in the "May Day" celebrations in our country. The list of the Soviet Trade Union Delegation is as follows:

    Head: V.I. Krestyaninov, Chairman of the Moscow City Trade Union Council, Deputy to the Supreme Soviet of the Soviet Union;
    Members: M.V. Serov, Chairman of the Central Committee of the Foreign Trade Workers' Union;
    V.G. Kucherova, Deputy Foreman of the Ivanovo Milanke United Factory, Deputy to the Supreme Soviet of the Soviet Union;
    V.G. Brazhenov, Chief Driver of the Moscow-Sogirovo Locomotive Depot, Deputy to the Supreme Soviet of the Soviet Union;
    A.G. Dyusin, Staff Member of the All-Union Central Council of Trade Unions;
    Staff: V.P. Shakovich, Staff Member of the All-Union Central Council of Trade Unions;
    V.V. Sigayev, Translator;
    T.G. Smirnov, Translator. Head of the Trade Union Delegation of the Mongolian People's Republic:

    Rob Sanglavtang, Secretary of the Central Committee of the Mongolian People's Republic Education Workers' Union.
    List of the French General Confederation of Labor Delegation:

    Head: Henri Raynaud, Secretary of the French General Confederation of Labor;
    Members: Pierre Degeure, General Secretary of the French Construction Union Federation;
    Jean Moréas, Secretary of the Federation of Trade Unions for Theater and Film Workers;
    René Laisse, Secretary of the Rhône Federation of Trade Unions;
    Marguerite Vigouk (female), Member of the Executive Bureau of the Seine Metalworkers' Union Federation.
    List of the Italian General Confederation of Labor Delegation:

    Head: Varni Avido, Secretary of the Bologna Trade Union Committee;
    Secretary: Tangelo Luzi, Secretary of the Naples Trade Union Committee;
    Members: Trenden Bruno, Director of the Economic Department of the Italian General Confederation of Labor;
    Manacoda Maleo Aligiro, Leader of the Italian Independent Trade Union of Educators;
    Dosio Andre, Deputy Secretary of the National Federation of Metallurgical Workers' Unions;
    Alini Walter, Member of the Executive Committee of the Milan Trade Union Committee.

### 应邀参加“五一”节观礼  苏蒙法意等国工会代表到京

1954-04-29
第4版()
专栏：

    应邀参加“五一”节观礼
    苏蒙法意等国工会代表到京
    据新华社讯：应中华全国总工会的邀请前来我国参加“五一”节观礼的苏联工会代表团、蒙古人民共和国工会代表团团长、法国总工会代表团和意大利总工会代表团，在二十八日下午乘飞机到达北京。苏联工会代表团名单：
