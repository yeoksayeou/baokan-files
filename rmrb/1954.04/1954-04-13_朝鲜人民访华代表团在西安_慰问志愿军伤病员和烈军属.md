Title: Korean People's Delegation to China in Xi'an Visits and Comforts Volunteer Army Wounded and Sick and Families of Martyrs

Author:

Date: 1954-04-13
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency Dispatch: Part of the members of the Korean People's Delegation to China, on the afternoon of the 11th, visited and comforted the Chinese People's Volunteer Army wounded and sick recuperating in the Xi'an area and the families of the Chinese People's Volunteer Army martyrs and military families in Shaanxi Province and Xi'an City. At the meeting to comfort the recuperating members of the Chinese People's Volunteer Army, delegation leader Kim Ung-gi and deputy leader Kim Dal-hyun delivered speeches, expressing cordial greetings to the wounded and sick. The representatives also personally visited the wards to offer their regards. At the meeting to comfort the families of the Chinese People's Volunteer Army martyrs and military families, delegation secretary-general Kang Hou-bong and five delegation members extended cordial greetings to more than two hundred families of martyrs and military families who attended the meeting.

### 朝鲜人民访华代表团在西安  慰问志愿军伤病员和烈军属

1954-04-13
第1版()
专栏：

    朝鲜人民访华代表团在西安
    慰问志愿军伤病员和烈军属
    据新华社讯：朝鲜人民访华代表团部分团员，十一日下午分别慰问了在西安地区休养的中国人民志愿军伤病员和陕西省、西安市的中国人民志愿军烈士家属和军人家属。在慰问中国人民志愿军休养员的大会上，金应基团长和金达铉副团长致词，对伤病员们表示亲切慰问。代表们并亲赴病房进行慰问。在慰问中国人民志愿军烈士家属和军人家属的大会上，代表团书记长康候凤和五位团员向到会的二百多烈属、军属进行了亲切慰问。
