Title: The Vietnamese People's Army Shot Down Three More Enemy Planes
Author:
Date: 1954-04-30
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, 29th Dispatch: According to the Vietnam News Agency on the 28th, the Vietnamese People's Army recently shot down three more enemy planes around Dien Bien Phu, including two B-26 type aircraft. The People's Army also repelled two companies of enemy troops advancing from the Hang Gang stronghold in the southern part of Dien Bien Phu with the support of three tanks, killing and wounding many enemy soldiers.

### 越南人民军又击落敌机三架

1954-04-30
第1版()
专栏：

    越南人民军又击落敌机三架
    【新华社二十九日讯】据越南通讯社二十八日讯：最近，越南人民军又在奠边府周围击落敌机三架，其中有两架是B—26型飞机。人民军并击退在三辆坦克支援下自奠边府南部据点航岗出犯的两连敌军，毙伤敌军甚多。
