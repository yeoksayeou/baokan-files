    Our Great Motherland
    Shandong University pays great attention to the training of teaching staff. All teaching units have formulated specific training plans and conduct regular inspections. This is a scene of Professor Du Zuodong of the Organic Chemistry Teaching Group of the Chemistry Department of the university guiding an assistant teacher in conducting a vacuum distillation experiment from Soviet teaching materials.

### 我们伟大的祖国（图片）

1954-04-04
第1版()
专栏：

    我们伟大的祖国
    山东大学很注意培养师资工作。各教学单位都订出了具体培养计划，并进行定期检查。这是该校化学系有机化学教学小组杜作栋教授指导助教做苏联教材中真空蒸馏实验的情形。
