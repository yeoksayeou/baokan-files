Title: The Vietnam People's Army Annihilated More Than 900 Enemy Soldiers on the Right Bank of the Red River in One Week
Author:
Date: 1954-04-07
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, April 6th: According to Vietnam News Agency on April 5th: In the week from March 21st to 27th, the Vietnam People's Army fought 36 battles in the Red River's right bank region of the Third Interzone in North Vietnam, killing, wounding, and capturing 919 enemy soldiers, and seizing 98 enemy light and heavy machine guns, 4 mortars and recoilless rifles, and 170 rifles.

### 越南人民军在红河右岸一周歼敌九百多名

1954-04-07
第1版()
专栏：

    越南人民军在红河右岸一周歼敌九百多名
    【新华社六日讯】　据越南通讯社五日讯：在三月二十一日到二十七日的一周中，越南人民军在北越第三联区的红河右岸地区作战三十六次，毙、伤、俘敌军九百一十九名，缴获敌军轻重机枪九十八挺、迫击炮和无后坐力炮四门、步枪一百七十支。
