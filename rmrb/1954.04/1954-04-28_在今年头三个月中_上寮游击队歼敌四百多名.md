Title: In the First Three Months of This Year, the Upper Laos Guerrilla Force Annihilated Over 400 Enemy Troops

Author:

Date: 1954-04-28
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Laos, 26th: According to the Vietnam News Agency, the Laotian Resistance Government issued a communique on the achievements of the Upper Laos guerrillas in the first three months of this year, stating: In the first three months of this year, the Upper Laos guerrillas engaged in 56 battles of various sizes, killing, wounding, and capturing 474 enemy troops, including seven officers at the rank of lieutenant and the entire command personnel of the Kasi stronghold garrison. They seized one enemy mortar, 72 machine guns, 48 rifles, five radio stations, three tons of ammunition, and many other military equipment.

The communique stated that during this period, in Vientiane Province alone, the guerrillas eliminated ten enemy strongholds (including Ban Xi, Kasi, and Muang Kieu strongholds) and forced the enemy to withdraw from eight strongholds. As a result, the guerrilla base in this province expanded to the banks of the Mekong River.

### 在今年头三个月中  上寮游击队歼敌四百多名

1954-04-28
第1版()
专栏：

    在今年头三个月中
    上寮游击队歼敌四百多名
    【新华社二十七日讯】据越南通讯社寮国二十六日讯：寮国抗战政府发表上寮游击队在今年头三个月中的战绩公报说：在今年头三个月中，上寮的游击队共进行了大小五十六次战斗；毙、伤、俘敌军四百七十四名，其中有尉级军官七名以及卡西据点守敌的全体指挥人员；缴获敌军迫击炮一门、机枪七十二挺、步枪四十八支、电台五部、弹药三吨和其他许多军事装备。
    公报说，在这一时期中，单在万象省，游击队就拔除了敌军据点十处（其中包括版奚、卡西、芒沟等据点），并迫使敌军撤离据点八处；结果，使这个省的游击基地扩大到了湄公河岸。
