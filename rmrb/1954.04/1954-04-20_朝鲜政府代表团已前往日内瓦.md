Title: Korean Government Delegation Leaves for Geneva
Author:
Date: 1954-04-20
Page: 1

    Xinhua News Agency, Pyongyang, 19th: The Korean Central News Agency reported on the 19th that the government delegation of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea, headed by Foreign Minister Nam Il, has left Pyongyang for Geneva to participate in the Geneva Conference on the Korean question, which was decided to be convened at the Berlin Four Foreign Ministers Conference held from January 25th to February 18th this year.

### 朝鲜政府代表团已前往日内瓦

1954-04-20
第1版()
专栏：

    朝鲜政府代表团已前往日内瓦
      【新华社平壤十九日电】　朝鲜中央通讯社十九日讯：为了参加在今年一月二十五日到二月十八日举行的柏林四国外长会议决定召开的关于朝鲜问题的日内瓦会议，以南日外务相为首席代表的朝鲜民主主义人民共和国政府代表团已经离开平壤，前往日内瓦。
