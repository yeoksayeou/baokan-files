Title: Baoji-Chengdu Railway Uses Mechanical Track Laying (Photos)
Author: Liu Shilin
Date: 1954-04-17
Page: 2

    Baoji-Chengdu Railway Uses Mechanical Track Laying
    Track laying is being carried out intensively on the Baoji-Chengdu Railway. Using machines to lay tracks not only saves one hundred laborers per kilometer, but also ensures production safety and reduces labor intensity.
                            Xinhua News Agency reporter Liu Shilin photographed
    The Baoji-Chengdu Railway began trial use of mechanical track laying in mid-March this year. This is a crane loading the spiked rails onto a train, ready to be transported to the line for laying.
                          Xinhua News Agency reporter Liu Shilin photographed

### 宝成铁路使用机械铺轨（图片）
刘诗临
1954-04-17
第2版()
专栏：

    宝成铁路使用机械铺轨
    宝成铁路正在紧张地进行铺轨。用机器铺轨不但每一公里能节省一百个劳动力，还保证了生产的安全减轻了劳动强度。
                            新华社记者　刘诗临摄
    宝成铁路已从今年三月中旬开始试用机械铺轨。这是起重机正在把钉好了的钢轨装车，准备运到线路上去铺设。
                          新华社记者　刘诗临摄
