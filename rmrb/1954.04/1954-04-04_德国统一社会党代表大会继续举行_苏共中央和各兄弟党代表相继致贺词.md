Title: The Socialist Unity Party of Germany Congress Continues; Representatives of the CPSU Central Committee and Fraternal Parties Deliver Congratulatory Messages
Author:
Date: 1954-04-04
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Berlin, March 3rd: The Fourth Congress of the Socialist Unity Party of Germany continued its sessions on April 1st and 2nd.

On the first day of April, the manager of the state-owned "Lenna" chemical factory in the German Democratic Republic, Schirmer, and the First Secretary of the Suhl District Committee of the Socialist Unity Party of Germany, Ferber, spoke on increasing production and enhancing vigilance to ensure the smooth implementation of the new guidelines, respectively. The Minister of Justice of the German Democratic Republic, Benjamin, and the member of the Political Bureau of the Central Committee of the Socialist Unity Party of Germany, Stoff, also spoke. Stoff called on party members to strengthen political education for the working people in his speech. On the second day of the meeting, Alternate Member of the Political Bureau of the Central Committee of the Socialist Unity Party of Germany, Mückenberger, emphasized the importance of consolidating the worker-peasant alliance when talking about the party's work in the countryside.

The parties of the working class of various countries extended congratulations to the Fourth Congress of the Socialist Unity Party of Germany. At the meeting on the 1st, Mikoyan, member of the Presidium of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of the Soviet Union, read the congratulatory telegram on behalf of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of the Soviet Union and delivered a speech. Ji Pengfei, the representative of the Communist Party of China, also read the congratulatory telegram from the Central Committee of the Communist Party of China at the conference. During these two days of meetings, representatives from the Polish United Workers' Party, the French Communist Party, the Hungarian Working People's Party, the Italian Communist Party, the Romanian Workers' Party, and the Communist Party of Germany also delivered congratulatory messages to the congress. In addition, congratulatory telegrams from the Central Committee of the Korean Workers' Party, the Central Committee of the Vietnam Workers' Party, and the Central Guidance Department of the Japanese Communist Party were also read at the congress.

### 德国统一社会党代表大会继续举行  苏共中央和各兄弟党代表相继致贺词

1954-04-04
第4版()
专栏：

    德国统一社会党代表大会继续举行
    苏共中央和各兄弟党代表相继致贺词
   【新华社三日讯】据塔斯社柏林消息：德国统一社会党第四次代表大会于四月一日和二日继续举行会议。
    在四月一日的会议上，民主德国国营“伦那”化学工厂的经理席尔默和德国统一社会党苏尔区委会第一书记费尔伯分别就增加生产和提高警惕，以保证新方针的顺利执行作了发言。民主德国司法部部长本雅明和德国统一社会党中央委员会政治局委员斯多夫也作了发言。斯多夫在发言中号召党员加强进行对劳动人民的政治教育。在二日的会议上，德国统一社会党中央委员会政治局候补委员穆肯柏格尔在谈到党在乡村的工作时强调了巩固工农联盟的重要性。
    各国工人阶级的政党在会上都向德国统一社会党第四次代表大会致贺。在一日的会议上，苏联共产党中央委员会主席团委员米高扬代表苏联共产党中央委员会宣读了贺电，并讲了话。中国共产党代表姬鹏飞也在大会上宣读了中国共产党中央委员会的贺电。在这两天的会议上，还有波兰统一工人党、法国共产党、匈牙利劳动人民党、意大利共产党、罗马尼亚工人党和德国共产党的代表，都向大会致了贺词。此外，在大会上，还宣读了朝鲜劳动党中央委员会、越南劳动党中央委员会和日本共产党中央指导部的贺电。
