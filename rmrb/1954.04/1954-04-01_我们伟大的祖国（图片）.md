Title: Our Great Motherland (Picture)
Author:
Date: 1954-04-01
Page: 1

    Our Great Motherland
    The Red Star Collective Farm in Nanyuan District, Beijing has begun spring plowing. This year, the farm's land area has expanded to more than 12,000 mu, and it is preparing to plant crops such as cotton, vegetables, corn, and millet. This is a tractor from the Nanyuan Tractor Station, pulling an eight-bottom plow to cultivate the land for the farm.

### 我们伟大的祖国（图片）

1954-04-01
第1版()
专栏：

    我们伟大的祖国
    北京市南苑区红星集体农庄开始春耕了。今年农庄的土地面积已经扩大到一万二千多亩，准备播种棉花、蔬菜、玉米和谷子等作物。这是南苑拖拉机站的拖拉机，正拖着八铧犁为农庄耕地。
