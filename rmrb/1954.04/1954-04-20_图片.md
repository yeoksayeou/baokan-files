    Part of the Korean People's Delegation and Art Troupe arrived in Guihua City on March 28th, and held a discussion with representatives of families of revolutionary martyrs, military personnel, and model workers of the Chinese People's Volunteers in Guihua City on the 31st. The picture shows Kim Jik-hyun (center), a member of the Korean People's Delegation, a coal mine tunneling worker from Awuji Coal Mine, and a labor hero, signing autographs for Li Suzhen (right), a family member of a Chinese People's Volunteer model worker, and Wan Xiuying (Hui nationality) (left), a family member of a serviceman, as a memento.
                        Xinhua News Agency Reporter Zhang Jie Photo

    On March 31st, some members of the Korean People's Delegation held a discussion with representatives of families of revolutionary martyrs, military personnel, and model workers of the Chinese People's Volunteers in Guihua City. This is a group photo of Choi Bun-ok (center), a member of the Korean People's Delegation and a female tank driver of the Korean People's Army, and representatives of families of revolutionary martyrs, military personnel, and model workers of the Chinese People's Volunteers who participated in the discussion.
