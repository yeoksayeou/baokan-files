Title: Vietnamese People's Armed Forces Actively Strike at Enemy Troops
Author:
Date: 1954-04-23
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, April 22nd: According to Vietnam News Agency, the Vietnamese People's Armed Forces are actively attacking enemy troops in various parts of Vietnam.

　　On April 18th, the People's Armed Forces of Ding Province in North Vietnam ambushed enemy troops 5 kilometers south of Bui Chu, wiping out two enemy companies and seizing many weapons and ammunition. On the 16th, the People's Armed Forces of Thai Binh Province annihilated 200 enemy troops of the 5th Mobile Group in Yanhe County. At the same time, they intercepted another unit of the enemy's 5th Mobile Group in Phan Thon and Du Me of the same county, wiping out more than 100 enemy troops.

　　In South Vietnam, in the ten days up to April 1st, the People's Armed Forces in Cao Lanh County, Long Chu Sa Province, removed and forced the enemy to withdraw from 29 strongholds and watchtowers, thereby expanding the guerrilla base in Cao Lanh County. In March, the People's Armed Forces in Soc Trang Province annihilated more than 100 enemy troops and destroyed three armored vehicles and three cars.

　　On April 4th, the People's Armed Forces in the area south of Hue, Thua Thien Province in Central Vietnam, destroyed a French military train.

### 越南人民武装积极打击敌军

1954-04-23
第1版()
专栏：

    越南人民武装积极打击敌军
    【新华社二十二日讯】据越南通讯社讯：越南人民武装在越南各地积极出击，打击敌军。
    四月十八日，北越南定省的人民武装在裴朱以南五公里的地方伏击敌军，歼灭敌军两个连，还缴获许多武器、弹药。十六日，太平省的人民武装在沿河县歼灭第五机动团的敌军二百名的同时，又在这一县藩村和度美截击敌军第五机动团的另一支部队，歼灭敌军一百多名。
    南越的人民武装在到四月一日为止的十天中，在龙朱沙省高岭县拔除和迫使敌人撤离了二十九个据点和了望塔，从而扩大了高岭县的游击基地；人民武装三月份在朔庄省歼灭敌军一百多名，击毁装甲车和汽车各三辆。
    四月四日，中越的人民武装在承天省顺化以南地区，击毁法军火车一列。
