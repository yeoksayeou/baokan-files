Title: The Government Administration Council Holds its 214th Meeting
Author:
Date: 1954-04-23
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, April 22nd: The 214th meeting of the Central People's Government Administration Council was held on the afternoon of the 22nd. The meeting heard and approved the report by Guo Moruo, Director of the Culture and Education Committee of the Government Administration Council, on the National Conference on Cultural and Educational Work, and the report by Shen Yanbing, Minister of the Ministry of Culture of the Central People's Government, on the Fourth National Cultural Work Conference. In addition, the meeting approved various appointment and removal cases and cases to be submitted to the Central People's Government Committee for approval.

### 政务院举行第二一四次会议

1954-04-23
第1版()
专栏：

    政务院举行第二一四次会议
    【新华社二十二日讯】中央人民政府政务院第二百一十四次政务会议在二十二日下午举行。会议听了和批准了政务院文化教育委员会郭沫若主任所作的关于全国文教工作会议的报告和中央人民政府文化部沈雁冰部长所作的关于全国第四次文化工作会议的报告。此外，会议通过了各项任免案和提请中央人民政府委员会批准任命案。
