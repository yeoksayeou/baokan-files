    The Duck Bridge River irrigation project in Yulin County, Guangxi Province, is one of the largest irrigation projects currently in Guangxi Province. After the completion of the entire project, it can protect more than 40,000 mu of land from drought. This is a full view of the 8-meter-high and 41-meter-long dam built on the upper reaches of the Duck Bridge River.
    Xinhua News Agency draft, photo by Zhao Huanggang
