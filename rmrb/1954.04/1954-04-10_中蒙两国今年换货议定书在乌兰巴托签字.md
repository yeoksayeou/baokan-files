Title: China and Mongolia Sign This Year's Barter Protocol in Ulaanbaatar
Author:
Date: 1954-04-10
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Ulaanbaatar, April 9th: The 1954 China-Mongolia Goods Exchange Protocol was signed in Ulaanbaatar on April 7th.

    The protocol stipulates that the goods to be mutually supplied by the two countries in 1954 will be further increased compared to 1953. The People's Republic of China will supply the Mongolian People's Republic with silk, leather goods, dried fruits, fresh fruits, and other goods, while the Mongolian People's Republic will supply China with livestock, hides, and various livestock products.

    Signing the protocol on behalf of the People's Republic of China was Li Zheren, Vice Minister of Foreign Trade, and on behalf of the Mongolian People's Republic was P. Batar, Minister of Trade.

    Attending the signing ceremony were Chen Ying, Chargé d'Affaires ad interim of the Embassy of the People's Republic of China in the Mongolian People's Republic, Lubsan, Deputy Prime Minister of the Mongolian People's Republic, Idanjab, Deputy Minister of Foreign Affairs, and Ochirbat, the Mongolian Ambassador to China currently in Ulaanbaatar, as well as all members of the Chinese and Mongolian trade delegations and many senior officials of the Mongolian Ministry of Trade.

### 中蒙两国今年换货议定书在乌兰巴托签字

1954-04-10
第1版()
专栏：

    中蒙两国今年换货议定书在乌兰巴托签字
    【新华社九日讯】乌兰巴托消息：一九五四年中蒙两国交换货物议定书于四月七日在乌兰巴托签字。
    议定书规定一九五四年两国相互供应的货物，比一九五三年又有进一步的增加。中华人民共和国将供给蒙古人民共和国绸缎、皮革制品、干果、水果和其他货物，蒙古人民共和国将供给中国牲畜、皮毛和各种牲畜产品。
  在议定书上签字的，中华人民共和国方面是对外贸易部副部长李哲人，蒙古人民共和国方面是贸易部部长普雷文·巴特尔。
    参加签字仪式的有中华人民共和国驻蒙古人民共和国大使馆临时代办陈应、蒙古人民共和国副总理鲁布桑、外交部副部长伊丹甲布和现在乌兰巴托的蒙古驻我国大使奥其尔巴特以及中蒙两国贸易代表团全体团员和蒙古贸易部高级官员多人。
