Title: Invited to Visit Our Country, Mongolian Cycling Team Sets Off
Author:
Date: 1954-04-21
Page: 1

    Xinhua News Agency, Ulaanbaatar, April 20th: The Mongolian People's Republic cycling team, invited by the Sports Commission of the Central People's Government of our country to visit, set off by bicycle from Sukhbaatar Square in Ulaanbaatar on April 15th to come to Beijing. The expedition team consists of thirteen people and is led by Danbi, Vice Chairman of the Mongolian National Sports Commission.
    More than 600 people, including representatives from Mongolian youth groups and sports organizations, went to Sukhbaatar Square to welcome the Mongolian cycling expedition team. Personnel from our embassy in Mongolia also went to see them off.

### 应邀来我国访问  蒙古自行车队出发

1954-04-21
第1版()
专栏：

    应邀来我国访问
    蒙古自行车队出发
    【新华社二十日讯】乌兰巴托消息：应我中央人民政府体育运动委员会邀请前来我国访问的蒙古人民共和国自行车远足队，已在四月十五日从乌兰巴托的苏赫—巴托尔广场乘自行车出发，前来北京。远足队由十三人组成，由蒙古国家体育运动委员会副主席丹毕率领。
    前往苏赫—巴托尔广场欢送蒙古自行车远足队的，有蒙古青年团体和体育组织的代表等六百余人。我驻蒙大使馆人员也前往欢送。
