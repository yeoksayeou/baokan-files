Title: Delegation to Germany, Romania, Poland, and Czechoslovakia Departs

Author:
Date: 1954-04-27
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, April 26th: At the invitation of the Free German Trade Union Federation, the Central Council of the General Confederation of Trade Unions of Romania, the Central Council of the Polish Trade Unions, and the Central Council of the Czechoslovak Trade Unions, the delegation of the All-China Federation of Trade Unions, which will attend the "May Day" International Labor Day celebrations in the German Democratic Republic, Romania, Poland, and Czechoslovakia, departed from Beijing by plane on the morning of the 25th to Berlin, Bucharest, Warsaw, and Prague.

Those who went to the airport to see them off included Dong Xin, Secretary of the Secretariat of the All-China Federation of Trade Unions, and Jin Ziming, Deputy Director of the International Liaison Department.

Personnel from the embassies of the German Democratic Republic and the Romanian People's Republic in China also went to the airport to see them off.

### 我赴德、罗、波、捷代表团启程

1954-04-27
第1版()
专栏：

    我赴德、罗、波、捷代表团启程
    【新华社二十六日讯】中华全国总工会应自由德国工会联合会、罗马尼亚总工会中央理事会、波兰工会中央理事会及捷克斯洛伐克工会中央理事会的邀请，前往民主德国、罗马尼亚、波兰及捷克斯洛伐克参加“五一”国际劳动节观礼的代表团，已在二十五日晨乘机离京飞往柏林、布加勒斯特、华沙和布拉格。
    前往机场欢送的有中华全国总工会书记处书记董昕和国际联络部副部长金子明等。
    德意志民主共和国和罗马尼亚人民共和国驻我国使馆人员也到机场欢送。
