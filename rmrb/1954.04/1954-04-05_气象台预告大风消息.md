Title: Weather Forecast: Gale Warning
Author:
Date: 1954-04-05
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, April 4th—The Central Meteorological Observatory issued a gale warning at 8:00 PM on the 4th: It is expected that Inner Mongolia Autonomous Region will have northerly winds of about force 6 tonight. It is expected that tomorrow Inner Mongolia, North China, Northeast China, Bohai Sea and the areas around the Bohai Sea will have northerly winds of about force 6. The gale area will move from northwest to southeast.

### 气象台预告大风消息

1954-04-05
第1版()
专栏：

    气象台预告大风消息
    【新华社四日讯】中央气象台在四日下午八时发布大风消息：预计今晚内蒙古自治区将有六级左右的偏北风，预计明日内蒙古、华北、东北、渤海和渤海四周地区将有六级左右的偏北风，大风区域由西北向东南移动。
