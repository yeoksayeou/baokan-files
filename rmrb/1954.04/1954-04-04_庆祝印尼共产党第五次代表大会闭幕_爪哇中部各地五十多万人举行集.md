Title: Celebrating the Closing of the Fifth National Congress of the Indonesian Communist Party; More Than 500,000 People Hold Rallies in Various Parts of Central Java
Author:
Date: 1954-04-04
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency, Jakarta, April 2nd: TASS Jakarta, April 1st telegram: Indonesian newspapers published a statement by Aidit, General Secretary of the Indonesian Communist Party, on March 31. Aidit recently visited Central Java and returned to Jakarta on March 30. The statement said: Surabaya, Malang, and Solo held rallies to celebrate the closing of the Fifth National Congress of the Indonesian Communist Party. More than 500,000 workers, peasants, and intellectuals who participated in the congress unanimously supported the new party program and election manifesto of the Indonesian Communist Party.

### 庆祝印尼共产党第五次代表大会闭幕  爪哇中部各地五十多万人举行集

1954-04-04
第4版()
专栏：

    庆祝印尼共产党第五次代表大会闭幕
    爪哇中部各地五十多万人举行集会
   【新华社二日讯】塔斯社雅加达一日电：印尼各报三月三十一日刊载了印度尼西亚共产党总书记艾地所发表的声明。艾地最近访问了爪哇中部，于三月三十日返抵雅加达。声明说：泗水、玛琅和梭罗都举行了庆祝印尼共产党第五次代表大会闭幕的大会。参加大会的五十多万工人、农民和知识分子，一致拥护印尼共产党的新党纲和竞选宣言。
