Title: Peace Advocates Conference of Seine Department, France Sends Letter to Bidault Demanding the Establishment of Peace in Indochina
Author:
Date: 1954-04-28
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, April 26th, TASS, Paris, April 25th: The Peace Advocates Conference of Seine Department, France, was held in Paris. The two main items on the conference agenda were: opposing the "European Defense Community" and striving for a peaceful resolution of the Indochina issue.

The meeting on April 24th was presided over by Posson, Honorary Chairman of the Seine Department Committee of the Radical Socialist Party. Jacques Madaule gave a report on the situation of the peace movement in Seine Department. He said: The main task of peace advocates is to oppose the "European Defense Community" and oppose the revival of German militarism. He said: Forty-four of the fifty-seven National Assembly members in Seine Department have formally expressed their opposition to ratifying the Paris Treaty, and fifteen of the twenty Republican Senate members oppose ratifying the Paris Treaty.

Madaule called for greater efforts to strive for an immediate cessation of the Vietnam War and a peaceful resolution of the Indochina conflict.

Reserve officer Ville-Polaire, some drama and film artists, and Academician of the Academy of Medicine, Bouguignon, all vehemently condemned the "European Army" plan.

The meeting continued.

Xinhua News Agency, April 27th, TASS, Paris: The Peace Advocates Conference of Seine Department, France, continued on the 25th. At the meeting, industrial workers, professors, teachers, artists, and veterans all denounced the continuation of the Indochina War. They demanded that the French delegation attending the Geneva Conference take all measures to peacefully resolve the Indochina issue. They also protested the government's preparation to have the parliament approve the Bonn Treaty and the Paris Treaty, which are opposed by the majority of the French people.

The conference wrote a letter to the French Minister of Foreign Affairs, representing millions of people in Seine Department with various beliefs, demanding the establishment of peace in Indochina.

The conference also passed a resolution condemning the "European Army" plan, reiterating that peace advocates will continue to wage unremitting struggle against the revival of German militarism.

### 法国塞纳省和平拥护者会议  致函皮杜尔要求在印度支那建立和平

1954-04-28
第4版()
专栏：

    法国塞纳省和平拥护者会议
    致函皮杜尔要求在印度支那建立和平
    【新华社二十六日讯】塔斯社巴黎二十五日讯：法国塞纳省和平拥护者会议在巴黎举行，会议的两项主要议程是：反对“欧洲防务集团”，争取和平解决印度支那问题。
    四月二十四日的会议由激进社会党塞纳省委员会名誉主席波逊主持。雅克·马道尔就塞纳省和平运动的情况作了一个报告。他说：和平拥护者的主要任务是反对“欧洲防务集团”，反对德国军国主义复活。他说：塞纳省的五十七个国民议会议员中有四十四人正式表示反对批准巴黎条约，二十个共和国参议院议员中有十五个反对批准巴黎条约。
    马道尔号召以更大的努力来争取立刻停止越南战争并且和平解决印度支那的冲突。
    后备军官维尔—波雷、一些戏剧与电影艺术工作者、医学科学院院士布吉尼翁等都猛烈地斥责“欧洲军”计划。
    会议继续举行。
   【新华社二十七日讯】据塔斯社巴黎讯：法国塞纳省和平拥护者会议在二十五日继续举行。会上，产业工人、教授、教师、艺术家和退伍军人都痛斥继续印度支那战争。他们要求出席日内瓦会议的法国代表团采取一切措施来和平解决印度支那问题。他们还抗议政府准备要议会批准遭到大多数法国人民反对的波恩条约和巴黎条约。
    会议写了一封信给法国外交部长，代表塞纳省几百万抱着各种信仰的人民要求在印度支那建立和平。
    会议还通过了一项决议，谴责“欧洲军”计划，重申和平拥护者要继续为反对德国军国主义复活而进行不懈的斗争。
