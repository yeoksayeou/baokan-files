Title: Chinese Academy of Sciences Holds Memorial Meeting for Mr. Liang Siyong
Author:
Date: 1954-04-21
Page: 3

    The Chinese Academy of Sciences held a memorial meeting on the 18th for Mr. Liang Siyong, a famous Chinese archaeologist and deputy director of the Institute of Archaeology of the Chinese Academy of Sciences.
    More than 300 people attended the memorial meeting, including heads of various research institutes of the Chinese Academy of Sciences, representatives from the All-China Federation of Natural Science Specialized Societies, the Ministry of Higher Education of the Central People's Government, and the Ministry of Culture, all staff members of the Institute of Archaeology of the Chinese Academy of Sciences, professors and students majoring in archaeology from the Department of History of Peking University, and friends of Mr. Liang Siyong. Guo Moruo, President of the Chinese Academy of Sciences, also attended the memorial meeting.
    Tao Menghe, Vice President of the Chinese Academy of Sciences, delivered a eulogy, saying: Mr. Liang Siyong was an outstanding and enthusiastic scholar in Chinese archaeology. His selfless and arduous work in academics over the past 30 years has made many important contributions to the cause of Chinese archaeology. After the founding of New China, Mr. Liang Siyong, despite his long illness and physical weakness, still led archaeological work with great enthusiasm and achieved many accomplishments. Mr. Liang Siyong's spirit of dedication to the scientific cause of the motherland will always be a force inspiring Chinese scientific workers to move forward.
    Historian Yin Da introduced Mr. Liang Siyong's academic activities and achievements throughout his life at the meeting. Zheng Zhenduo, Qian Duansheng, Pei Wenzhong, and Su Bingqi, friends of Mr. Liang Siyong during his lifetime, spoke at the meeting successively.
    Finally, Mr. Liang Sicheng, a representative of Mr. Liang Siyong's family, spoke at the meeting. He expressed his deep gratitude to the responsible comrades of the Chinese Academy of Sciences for their warm care and condolences during Mr. Liang Siyong's illness.
                          (According to Xinhua News Agency)

### 中国科学院举行梁思永先生纪念会

1954-04-21
第3版()
专栏：

    中国科学院举行梁思永先生纪念会
    中国科学院在十八日举行我国著名考古学家中国科学院考古研究所副所长梁思永先生纪念会。
    参加纪念会的有中国科学院各研究所负责人、中华全国自然科学专门学会联合会、中央人民政府高等教育部、文化部等单位的代表、中国科学院考古研究所全体工作人员、北京大学历史系考古专业的教授和学生、梁思永先生生前友好等三百多人。中国科学院院长郭沫若也参加了纪念会。
    中国科学院副院长陶孟和致悼辞说：梁思永先生是中国考古学界的一位卓越的热情的学者，他三十年来在学术上的忘我的艰辛的工作，对中国考古事业作出了许多重要的贡献。新中国成立后，梁思永先生虽然久病，身体衰弱，但仍然以极大热情领导考古工作，并有了很多成就。梁思永先生这种热爱祖国科学事业的献身的精神，将永远成为鼓舞中国科学工作者前进的力量。
    历史学家尹达在会上介绍了梁思永先生生平的学术活动和成就。梁思永先生生前友好郑振铎、钱端升、裴文中、苏秉琦先后在会上讲话。
    最后，梁思永先生家属代表梁思成先生在会上讲话。他对中国科学院负责同志在梁思永先生患病期间所给予的热情的关怀和慰问表示深切的感谢。
                          （据新华社讯）
