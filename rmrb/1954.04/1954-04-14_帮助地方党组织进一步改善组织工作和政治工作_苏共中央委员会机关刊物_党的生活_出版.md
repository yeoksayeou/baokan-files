Title: "Party Life," the Organ of the CPSU Central Committee, Published to Help Local Party Organizations Further Improve Organizational and Political Work
Author:
Date: 1954-04-14
Page: 4

    Helping Local Party Organizations Further Improve Organizational and Political Work
    "Party Life," the Organ of the CPSU Central Committee, Published
    [Moscow, 13th Dispatch] Reporter Li He reports: "Party Life," the organ of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of the Soviet Union, has recently been published. The inaugural issue publishes essays and communications on the Party's organizational and propaganda work, editorials, short commentaries, assistance for reporters and propagandists, and answers to readers' questions. The editorial states: "The purpose of publishing 'Party Life' is to help local Party organizations further improve the Party's organizational work and political work. The basic task of the publication is to systematically explain the most important issues of the policies of the Communist Party and the Soviet government, to explore pressing issues in Party building, to introduce good experiences in the Party's organizational and propaganda work, and to criticize shortcomings in the practical work of individual Party organizations. The publication will regularly publish answers to questions for Party secretaries, reporters, and propagandists, reviews of essays, reviews of local publications, and answers to questions from Communist Party members. The most important condition for the publication to fulfill the above tasks is the active cooperation of the broad masses of Communist Party members."

### 帮助地方党组织进一步改善组织工作和政治工作  苏共中央委员会机关刊物“党的生活”出版

1954-04-14
第4版()
专栏：

    帮助地方党组织进一步改善组织工作和政治工作
    苏共中央委员会机关刊物“党的生活”出版
    【本报莫斯科十三日专电】记者李何报道：苏联共产党中央委员会机关刊物“党的生活”新近出版了。创刊号发表了党的组织工作和宣传工作的论文和通讯，社论，短评，对报告员宣传员的帮助，读者问题解答。社论说：“党的生活”出版的目的，是为了帮助地方党组织进一步改善党的组织工作和党的政治工作。刊物的基本任务在于有系统地说明共产党和苏维埃政府的政策的最重要问题，探讨党的建设的迫切问题，介绍党的组织工作和宣传鼓动工作的好经验，批评个别党组织的实际工作中的缺点。刊物上将定期登载供给党组织的书记、报告员和宣传员的答疑，评介论文，地方刊物述评和共产党员问题解答。使得刊物能负起上述任务的最主要条件，是广大的共产党员对它的积极合作。”
