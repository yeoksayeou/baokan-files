Title: Malenkov and Others Watch Performance by Russian Rural Artists; Kosygin and Others Awarded the Order of Lenin and the Order of the Red Banner of Labor
Author: 
Date: 1954-04-04
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency dispatch: The final grand performance of the Russian Federal Rural Amateur Art Festival was held at the Bolshoi Theatre in Moscow on the 1st. Malenkov, Molotov, Khrushchev, Voroshilov, Bulganin, Kaganovich, Shvernik, Shatalin, Kosygin, and Malyshev attended the performance. The performance featured 1,500 outstanding singers, musicians, and dancers from rural areas across the Russian Federation, who presented many wonderful musical and dance programs that were warmly welcomed by the audience.

Xinhua News Agency dispatch: Voroshilov, Chairman of the Presidium of the Supreme Soviet of the USSR, awarded a group of senior cadres with various Soviet medals in the Kremlin on March 29.

The following individuals were awarded the Order of Lenin for their contributions to the Soviet state: Kosygin, Deputy Chairman of the Council of Ministers of the USSR; Kuzmin, Minister of the Iron and Steel Industry of the USSR; Sokolov, Chairman of the State Construction Committee of the Council of Ministers of the USSR; Stepanov, First Deputy Minister of the Machine Building Industry of the USSR; Raizer, First Deputy Minister of the Construction of the USSR; and Khramov and Maksarev, senior officials of the machine building industry. The Order of Lenin was also awarded in celebration of their 50th birthdays.

The following individuals were awarded the Order of the Red Banner of Labor for their contributions to the Soviet state: Buzin, First Deputy Minister of Finance of the USSR; Fomin, Deputy Minister of Transport and Heavy Machine Building Industry of the USSR; Kutchumov, Deputy Minister of Agriculture of the USSR; Poskonev, Deputy Minister of Finance of the USSR; and Smirnov, Deputy Minister of Trade of the USSR. The Order of the Red Banner of Labor was also awarded in celebration of their 50th birthdays.

Kirov, Minister of Education of the Russian Federation and President of the Academy of Educational Sciences of the Russian Federation, was awarded the Order of the Red Banner of Labor in recognition of his contributions to the Soviet state in the field of national education and in celebration of his 60th birthday. After the awards ceremony, Kirov, on behalf of the recipients, expressed his sincere gratitude to the Party and the government for the awards they had received.

Voroshilov concluded by offering warm congratulations to the award recipients, wishing them good health and further achievements in their work.

### 马林科夫等观看俄罗斯农村艺术家表演  柯西金等荣获列宁勋章和劳动红旗勋章

1954-04-04
第4版()
专栏：

    马林科夫等观看俄罗斯农村艺术家表演
    柯西金等荣获列宁勋章和劳动红旗勋章
    据新华社讯：俄罗斯联邦农村业余艺术会演，一日在莫斯科大剧院举行了最后一次盛大的表演会。马林科夫、莫洛托夫、赫鲁晓夫、伏罗希洛夫、布尔加宁、卡冈诺维奇、什维尔尼克、夏塔林、柯西金、马利歇夫都出席了表演会。参加这次演出的有来自俄罗斯联邦各地农村的优秀歌唱家、音乐家和舞蹈家等一千五百人，演出了许多精彩的音乐、舞蹈节目，受到观众热烈的欢迎。
    据新华社讯：苏联最高苏维埃主席团主席伏罗希洛夫，三月二十九日在克里姆林宫以苏联各种勋章分别授予一批高级干部。
    由于对苏维埃国家的功绩而荣获列宁勋章的有：苏联部长会议副主席柯西金、苏联钢铁工业部部长库兹明、苏联部长会议国家建设委员会主席索科洛夫、苏联机器制造工业部第一副部长斯捷潘诺夫、苏联建造部第一副部长拉伊泽尔、苏联机器制造工业部门的高级官员赫拉莫夫和马克萨雷夫。授予列宁勋章还为了庆祝他们的五十岁寿辰。
    由于对苏维埃国家的功绩而荣获劳动红旗勋章的有：苏联财政部第一副部长布津、苏联运输和重型机器制造工业部副部长弗明、苏联农业部副部长库楚莫夫、苏联财政部副部长波斯科诺夫、苏联贸易部副部长斯米尔诺夫。授予劳动红旗勋章还为了庆祝他们的五十岁寿辰。
    为了表扬俄罗斯联邦教育部部长兼俄罗斯联邦教育科学院院长凯罗夫在国民教育方面对苏维埃国家的功绩，并庆祝他的六十岁寿辰，授给他劳动红旗勋章。授勋完毕，凯罗夫代表受勋者表示衷心感谢党和政府给他们的奖励。
    伏罗希洛夫最后向受勋者热烈祝贺，祝他们身体健康并在工作中取得进一步的成就。
