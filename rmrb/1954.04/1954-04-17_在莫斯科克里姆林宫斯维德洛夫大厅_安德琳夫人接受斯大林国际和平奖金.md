Title: Madame Andreen Receives Stalin International Peace Prize at Sverdlov Hall, Moscow Kremlin
Author:
Date: 1954-04-17
Page: 4

(Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, 16th) According to TASS Moscow: On April 15th, the Stalin International Peace Prize award ceremony for "Strengthening International Peace" was held in the Sverdlov Hall of the Kremlin, to award the Stalin International Peace Prize for "Strengthening International Peace" to Madame Andrea Andreen, a distinguished Swedish public figure and one of the bravest fighters against the threat of war.

Representatives of many mass organizations in Moscow, advanced workers of industrial enterprises, scientists, doctors, writers, and artists attended the award ceremony.

Academician Dmitri Skobeltsyn, Chairman of the Stalin International Peace Prize Committee for "Strengthening International Peace," congratulated Madame Andrea Andreen on receiving this noble award. He paid tribute to Madame Andreen as one of the bravest peace fighters and presented her with the certificate and the gold medal of the Stalin International Peace Prize for "Strengthening International Peace."

Subsequently, Nikolai Tikhonov, Chairman of the Soviet Peace Committee, representatives of various Soviet people's groups, and Eva Palmer, a member of the Swedish National Peace Committee, warmly congratulated Madame Andreen. The conference also read a congratulatory telegram sent by Howard Fast from New York.

In her reply, Madame Andreen expressed her deep gratitude for being awarded this honor. She emphasized the great significance of the peace movement in defending world peace and international friendship, and believed that the time would eventually come when war becomes impossible. She said that this is a new era in human history, and it is our hope. Let us fight together for peace.

### 在莫斯科克里姆林宫斯维德洛夫大厅  安德琳夫人接受斯大林国际和平奖金

1954-04-17
第4版()
专栏：

    在莫斯科克里姆林宫斯维德洛夫大厅
    安德琳夫人接受斯大林国际和平奖金
    【新华社十六日讯】据塔斯社莫斯科讯：四月十五日，在克里姆林宫斯维德洛夫大厅举行了“加强国际和平”斯大林国际奖金授奖典礼，以“加强国际和平”斯大林国际奖金授予瑞典杰出的公众领袖、反对战争威胁的最勇敢的战士之一安德烈·安德琳夫人。
    参加授奖典礼的有莫斯科许多群众组织的代表、工业企业的先进工人、科学家、医生、作家和艺术工作者。
    “加强国际和平”斯大林国际奖金委员会主席德米特里·斯科贝尔琴院士祝贺安德烈·安德琳夫人荣获这个崇高的奖赏。他向作为最勇敢的和平战士之一的安德琳夫人致敬，并授予她奖状和“加强国际和平”斯大林国际奖金的金质奖章。
    接着，苏联拥护和平委员会主席尼古拉·吉洪诺夫、苏联人民各团体的代表和瑞典全国和平委员会委员伊娃·帕麦尔都向安德琳夫人热烈祝贺。大会并宣读了霍华德·法斯特自纽约拍来的贺电。
    安德琳夫人致答辞时对授予她以这个荣誉表示深切的感谢。她着重指出和平运动对保卫世界和平和国际友谊有重大的意义，并相信战争成为不可能的时候终会到来。她说，这是人类历史的新纪元，是我们的希望。让我们共同为和平而斗争。
