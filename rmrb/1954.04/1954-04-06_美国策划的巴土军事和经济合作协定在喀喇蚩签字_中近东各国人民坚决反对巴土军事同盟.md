Title: U.S.-Engineered Pakistan-Turkey Military and Economic Cooperation Agreement Signed in Karachi; Peoples of Middle Eastern Countries Resolutely Oppose Pakistan-Turkey Military Alliance
Author:
Date: 1954-04-06
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, 3rd: Under the intensified planning of the United States, Pakistan and Turkey formally signed a five-year military and economic cooperation agreement in Karachi on the 2nd, further linking Pakistan with the U.S. aggressive bloc.

The Associated Press reported that the agreement stipulates that it will automatically remain in effect every five years unless either party notifies its termination. Pakistani Foreign Minister Zafrulla Khan said at a press conference after the signing of the agreement that the two countries are planning to hold negotiations and exchange military missions "as soon as possible" regarding the establishment of a joint military staff organization.

Xinhua News Agency, 5th: TASS Beirut, 4th: "Al- تلغراف " reports that the Farmers' Union of the Beqaa Valley in Lebanon recently held a meeting attended by 5,000 representatives. The meeting condemned the Pakistan-Turkey military alliance and the plan to establish a federation of Arab countries, and demanded the rejection of foreign "aid" given under conditions that affect national sovereignty. The meeting demanded the expulsion of the U.S. delegation representatives stationed in Lebanon implementing the so-called Truman "Point Four Program."

Xinhua News Agency, 5th: TASS Beirut: Students and people across Lebanon are fiercely opposing the authorities' killing of students on March 27 who held a demonstration against the Pakistan-Turkey agreement.

Students in many schools in Beirut, Tripoli, Sidon, and other cities in Lebanon have been on strike for a week. The striking students are demanding the resignation of the government and the punishment of those who shot at the students.

"Al Nahar" reported that students in Sidon have also announced a hunger strike, demanding the resignation of the government, the release of arrested students, and the reinstatement of students who were expelled for participating in the strike. The newspaper said that 109 prominent lawyers have lodged a protest with the government, expressing their opposition to shooting at demonstrating students.

The funeral of Hassan Abu Ismail, a student who died during the demonstration, was held in Beirut on March 29, attended by thousands of students and citizens. The Beirut newspaper "Al Islaah" pointed out that the funeral turned into a demonstration against imperialist military agreements and aggressive alliances that threaten the national independence and sovereignty of Arab countries.

Meanwhile, Syrian students are supporting the protest movement of Lebanese students. The Lebanese newspaper "Al- تلغراف " reported that students at Syrian University and Damascus middle schools went on strike on March 29 to protest the shooting of demonstrating students in Beirut. Students in Damascus also organized mass demonstrations that day, chanting slogans against military blocs and imperialist treaties.

Demonstrations were also held in Homs, with demonstrators holding signs with slogans such as "Long live the Lebanese people's struggle for freedom!" "Down with the imperialist joint defense plan and the Turkish-Pakistani alliance!"

Xinhua News Agency, 5th: According to the Indian News Service on the 4th: Pir Maanki Sharif, Chairman of the All-Pakistan Peace Committee and leader of the opposition in the Northwest Frontier Province Legislative Assembly, told "Lahore Daily" that the people of East Pakistan have expressed clear opposition to U.S. military "aid" to Pakistan. Sharif said that he opposes all agreements and "aid" that bring war to Pakistan. Sharif demanded the dissolution of the current Pakistan Constituent Assembly and the holding of new elections.

Maulana Bhashani, Chairman of the East Pakistan People's Muslim League, said when talking about U.S. military "aid" to Pakistan, that the parties forming the United Front in East Pakistan have agreed to support a resolution against U.S. military "aid" to Pakistan at the first meeting of the East Pakistan Constituent Assembly after its re-election.

Mian Iftikharuddin, leader of the Azad Pakistan Party, also condemned the Pakistani government for accepting U.S. military "aid" on March 30.

### 美国策划的巴土军事和经济合作协定在喀喇蚩签字  中近东各国人民坚决反对巴土军事同盟

1954-04-06
第4版()
专栏：

    美国策划的巴土军事和经济合作协定在喀喇蚩签字
    中近东各国人民坚决反对巴土军事同盟
    【新华社三日讯】在美国加紧策划下，巴基斯坦和土耳其已于二日在喀喇蚩正式签定为期五年的军事和经济合作协定，从而使巴基斯坦和美国侵略集团发生了进一步的联系。
    美联社消息说，协定规定每隔五年如无任何一方通知结束，该协定将自动继续有效。巴基斯坦外交部长查弗鲁拉汗在协定签字后的记者招待会上说，两国正计划“及早”举行关于成立联合军事参谋机构的谈判和交换军事使团。
    【新华社五日讯】据塔斯社贝鲁特四日讯：“电讯报”报道，黎巴嫩贝卡盆地农民联合会最近举行了会议，参加的有五千名代表。会议谴责了巴基斯坦—土耳其军事同盟和建立阿拉伯国家的联合邦的计划，并要求拒绝在影响国家主权的条件下给予的外国“援助”。会议要求驱逐驻在黎巴嫩的执行所谓杜鲁门“第四点计划”的美国代表团代表。
    【新华社五日讯】据塔斯社贝鲁特讯：黎巴嫩全国各地学生和民众激烈反对当局于三月二十七日枪杀举行反对巴土协定示威游行的学生。
    在黎巴嫩首都贝鲁特、的黎波里、赛义达及其他城市的许多学校的学生已经罢课一星期。罢课的学生要求政府辞职、并惩罚开枪射击学生的人。
    “晨报”报道，赛义达学生并已宣布举行绝食罢课，要求政府辞职，释放被捕学生，恢复因参加罢课而被开除的学生的学籍。该报说，一百零九名著名律师已向政府提出抗议，表示反对开枪射击举行示威的学生。
    在示威时殉难的学生哈桑·阿鲍·伊斯迈尔的葬礼于三月二十九日在贝鲁特举行，参加送葬的有数千名学生和市民。贝鲁特“伊斯拉报”指出，这个葬礼变成了一个反对威胁阿拉伯国家民族独立和主权的帝国主义军事协定和侵略同盟的示威。
    同时，叙利亚的学生对黎巴嫩学生的抗议运动进行支援。黎巴嫩“电讯报”报道，叙利亚大学和大马士革中学的学生于三月二十九日举行罢课，抗议向贝鲁特示威学生开枪的行为。大马士革学生当天还组织了群众示威游行，示威者高呼反对军事集团和帝国主义条约的口号。
    荷姆斯也举行了示威游行，示威者举着上写“黎巴嫩人民争取自由的斗争万岁！”“打倒帝国主义的共同防御计划和土巴联盟！”等口号的标语牌。
    【新华社五日讯】据印度新闻处四日报道：全巴基斯坦和平委员会主席、西北边省立法会议反对派领袖毕尔·曼基·沙里夫对“拉合尔日报”说，东巴基斯坦人民对于美国给巴基斯坦以军事“援助”，已表示了明确的反对意见。沙里夫说，他反对把战争带给巴基斯坦的所有的协定和“援助”。沙里夫要求解散目前的巴基斯坦制宪议会，并重新举行选举。
    东巴基斯坦人民回教联盟主席巴沙尼在谈到美国给巴基斯坦的军事“援助”时说，东巴基斯坦组成联合阵线的各党派，都已同意将在东巴基斯坦制宪议会改选后的第一次会议上支持反对美国给巴基斯坦以军事“援助”的决议。
    自由巴基斯坦党领袖米安·伊夫蒂卡鲁丁也在三月三十日谴责了巴基斯坦政府接受美国军事“援助”。
