Title: German Writers Leonhard and Uhse Visit China

Author:

Date: 1954-04-28
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Berlin, April 27th: German Writers’ Union Council member and German writers Ludwig Renn and Bodo Uhse flew to China on the morning of the 25th. According to the 1954 implementation plan of the Sino-German Cultural Cooperation Agreement, they will be visiting China for two months.

Representatives from the German Writers’ Union, the German Association for Foreign Cultural Relations, and the Chinese Embassy in the German Democratic Republic were at the airport to see them off.

Ludwig Renn is one of Germany’s veteran revolutionary writers. His famous works include the novels "War" and "After the War," which describe World War I. Bodo Uhse is a revolutionary anti-fascist writer and author of novels such as "Mercenaries and Soldiers," "Our Sons," and "Patriots."

### 德作家雷恩、乌塞来我国访问

1954-04-28
第1版()
专栏：

    德作家雷恩、乌塞来我国访问
    【新华社柏林二十七日电】德国作家联盟理事会理事、德国作家卢德韦希·雷恩和鲍多·乌塞二十五日晨乘飞机赴中国。他们根据中德文化合作协定一九五四年执行计划，将在中国作两个月的访问。
    到机场欢送的有德国作家联盟、德国对外文化联络协会和我国驻德意志民主共和国大使馆的代表。
    卢德韦希·雷恩是德国革命的老作家之一，他的名著有描写第一次世界大战的“战争”和“战后”等长篇小说。鲍多·乌塞是革命的反法西斯作家，他著有“雇佣兵与士兵”、“我们的儿子”和“爱国者”等小说。
