Title: Korean Delegation Arrives in Lanzhou from Xi'an; People from All Walks of Life in Southwest China Warmly See Off Korean Friends

Author:

Date: 1954-04-17
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency report:
    Part of the Korean People's Delegation to China, led by Chief Kim Ung-gi, and accompanying art troupe members arrived in Lanzhou from Xi'an on the 15th.

    Greeting them at the station were Deng Baoshan, Chairman of the Gansu Provincial People's Representative Conference Consultative Committee and the Gansu Branch of the China Resistance to America and Aid Korea Association; Zhang Desheng, Secretary of the CPC Gansu Provincial Committee; Peng Shaohui, Deputy Commander of the Northwest Military Region of the Chinese People's Liberation Army; Liao Hansheng, Deputy Political Commissar; Huo Weide, Vice Chairman of the Gansu Provincial People's Government; and representatives from all walks of life, totaling two thousand people.

    A grand welcoming rally was held at the station. Deng Baoshan delivered a speech on behalf of the people of all ethnic groups in Gansu Province, warmly welcoming the arrival of the delegation. Chief Kim Ung-gi delivered a return speech, expressing his gratitude for the welcome from the people of all ethnic groups.

    In the evening, the Gansu Provincial People's Representative Conference Consultative Committee and the Gansu Branch of the China Resistance to America and Aid Korea Association held a grand banquet to welcome the delegation.

    Xinhua News Agency report:
    The Korean People's Delegation to China visiting the Southwest region, headed by Deputy Chief Li Yong-ho and Deputy Chief Li Dong-young, left Chongqing by special ship for Wuhan at 10:00 a.m. on the 15th.

    The day before the delegation left Chongqing, the Southwest General Branch of the Resistance to America and Aid Korea Association held a grand send-off meeting. After the send-off meeting, He Long, Secretary of the Southwest Bureau of the CPC Central Committee, Vice Chairman of the Southwest Administrative Committee, and Commander of the Southwest Military Region of the Chinese People's Liberation Army, hosted a banquet to bid farewell to the delegation.

### 朝鲜访华团由西安到达兰州  西南各界热烈欢送朝鲜友人

1954-04-17
第1版()
专栏：

    朝鲜访华团由西安到达兰州
    西南各界热烈欢送朝鲜友人
    据新华社讯：以金应基团长为首的朝鲜人民访华代表团部分团员和随行艺术团人员，在十五日上午由西安到达兰州。
    到车站欢迎的，有甘肃省各界人民代表会议协商委员会、甘肃省抗美援朝分会主席邓宝珊，中共甘肃省委书记张德生，中国人民解放军西北军区副司令员彭绍辉、副政治委员廖汉生，甘肃省人民政府副主席霍维德，以及各界代表两千人。
    车站上举行了隆重的欢迎大会。邓宝珊代表甘肃省各族人民致词，热烈欢迎代表团的来临。金应基团长致答词，对各族人民的欢迎表示感谢。
    晚间，甘肃省各界人民代表会议协商委员会和甘肃省抗美援朝分会举行盛大宴会，欢宴代表团。
    据新华社讯：以李永镐副团长和李东英副团长为首访问西南区的朝鲜人民访华代表团十五日上午十时，乘专轮离重庆赴武汉。
    代表团离开重庆的前一天，西南抗美援朝总分会举行了盛大的欢送会。欢送会后，中共中央西南局书记、西南行政委员会副主席、中国人民解放军西南军区司令员贺龙并设宴欢送代表团。
