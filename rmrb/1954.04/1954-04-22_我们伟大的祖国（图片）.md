Author:
Date: 1954-04-22
Page: 1

Our Great Motherland
After entering spring, continuous heavy snows fell in the eastern pastoral areas of Xilin Gol League in Inner Mongolia Autonomous Region, and a large number of livestock were trapped. The Central People's Government sent planes to airdrop grain and fodder to support herdsmen in fighting disasters and protecting livestock. This is the plane arriving over the pastoral area, preparing to airdrop.

### 我们伟大的祖国（图片）

1954-04-22
第1版()
专栏：

    我们伟大的祖国
    内蒙古自治区锡林郭勒盟东部牧区入春后连降大雪，大批牲畜被困。中央人民政府派飞机空投粮食饲料，支援牧民抗灾保畜。这是飞机到达牧区上空，准备空投。
