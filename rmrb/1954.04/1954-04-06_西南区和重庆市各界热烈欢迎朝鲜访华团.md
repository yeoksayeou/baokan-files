Title: Southwest Region and Chongqing City Enthusiastically Welcome the North Korean Delegation

Author:
Date: 1954-04-06
Page: 1

The Southwest Region and Chongqing City Enthusiastically Welcome the North Korean Delegation

[Chongqing, April 5th (report from our newspaper)] Part of the North Korean People's Delegation to China, headed by Deputy Leaders Li Yong-ho and Li Dong-ying, and the North Korean People's Army Ensemble, accompanied by Chu Tu-nan, Director of the Central People's Government's Committee for Eliminating Illiteracy, arrived in Chongqing at 2:00 PM on the 4th by special steamer. Li Feng-jiu, First Secretary of the Embassy of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea in China, also accompanied them. More than 3,000 representatives from all ethnic groups and all sectors of the people in the Southwest Region and Chongqing City held a grand welcoming ceremony at the Liberation Monument in the center of Chongqing City to welcome the heroic North Korean people's representatives who had traveled tirelessly to visit the Southwest.

The Southwest Sub-Branch of the Resist-America, Aid-Korea General Association hosted a banquet at 12:00 noon on the 5th to entertain the members of the North Korean People's Delegation to China and all members of the North Korean People's Army Ensemble. Li Feng-jiu, First Secretary of the Embassy of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea in China, was also invited to attend the banquet. Those present were responsible people and representatives from the CPC Central Committee Southwest Bureau, the Southwest Administrative Committee, the Southwest Military Region, the CPC Chongqing Municipal Committee, the Municipal People's Government, and various democratic parties and people's organizations. Qiu Shao-hua, the younger brother of the special-class hero of the Chinese People's Volunteers, Qiu Shao-yun; Liu Yu-shun, the father of martyr Liu Xing-wen; Yi Pei-xiu, the father of hero Yi Cai-xue; Huang Zai-ting, the father of Huang Jia-fu; and Gan Yu-zhen, the wife of Tian Wen-fu, also attended the banquet. Ren Bai-ge, Vice Chairman of the Southwest Sub-Branch of the Resist-America, Aid-Korea General Association, delivered a speech at the beginning of the banquet, expressing a warm welcome. Song Ren-qiong, First Deputy Secretary of the CPC Central Committee Southwest Bureau, and Wang Wei-zhou, Vice Chairman of the Southwest Administrative Committee, spoke successively, welcoming the North Korean People's Delegation to China to the Southwest. Deputy Leader Li Yong-ho then delivered a reply.

At 4:00 PM on the 5th, He Long, Vice Chairman of the Southwest Administrative Committee, received all the representatives of the North Korean People's Delegation to China who came to visit the Southwest and Jiang Zhe-yuan, the leader of the North Korean People's Army Ensemble.

At 6:30 PM on the 5th, people from all walks of life in the Southwest Region and Chongqing City held a grand welcoming meeting in the auditorium of the Southwest Administrative Committee to warmly welcome all the representatives of the North Korean People's Delegation to China who came to visit the Southwest and all members of the North Korean People's Army Ensemble. He Long, Xiong Kewu, Liu Wenhui, Wang Weizhou, Song Renqiong, Lu Han, and Deng Xihou, Vice Chairmen of the Southwest Administrative Committee; Zhang Ziyi, Zhou Xing, and Yu Jiangzhen, members of the Southwest Administrative Committee; Li Da, Deputy Commander of the Southwest Military Region, Wang Xinting, Deputy Political Commissar; Cao Diqiu, Mayor of Chongqing; Ren Baige, Vice Chairman of the Southwest Sub-Branch of the Resist-America, Aid-Korea General Association, and more than 4,500 responsible people and cadres from various agencies, troops, and organizations in Chongqing City attended the welcoming meeting. The welcoming meeting began with the majestic national anthems of Korea and China, after which flowers were presented to Deputy Leaders Li Yong-ho and Li Dong-ying. Ren Bai-ge, Vice Chairman of the Southwest Sub-Branch of the Resist-America, Aid-Korea General Association, delivered a welcoming speech, warmly welcoming the North Korean People's Delegation to China to visit the Southwest. Deputy Leader Li Yong-ho then delivered a reply, expressing his gratitude and respect for the welcome from the people of the Southwest. At the welcoming meeting, the North Korean People's Delegation to China presented flags to the Southwest Administrative Committee, the Southwest Sub-Branch of the Resist-America, Aid-Korea General Association, the Southwest Office of the All-China Federation of Trade Unions, the Southwest Working Committee of the China New Democratic Youth League, and the Southwest Working Committee of the All-China Democratic Women's Federation, respectively. The above units also presented banners to the North Korean People's Delegation to China. After the meeting, the North Korean People's Army Ensemble performed wonderful programs.

### 西南区和重庆市各界热烈欢迎朝鲜访华团

1954-04-06
第1版()
专栏：

    西南区和重庆市各界热烈欢迎朝鲜访华团
   【本报重庆五日电】以副团长李永镐和李东英为首的朝鲜人民访华代表团部分团员和朝鲜人民军协奏团，由中央人民政府扫除文盲工作委员会主任委员楚图南陪同，在四日下午二时乘专轮到达重庆。朝鲜民主主义人民共和国驻我国大使馆一等秘书李凤九也陪同前来。西南区和重庆市各族各界人民代表三千多人在重庆市中心解放碑举行了盛大的欢迎仪式，欢迎英雄的朝鲜人民代表不辞劳苦地来到西南访问。
    西南抗美援朝总分会在五日十二时设宴招待朝鲜人民访华代表团团员和朝鲜人民军协奏团全体人员。朝鲜民主主义人民共和国驻我国大使馆一等秘书李凤九也应邀出席了宴会。出席作陪的有中共中央西南局、西南行政委员会、西南军区、中共重庆市委会、市人民政府及各民主党派、各人民团体的负责人和各界代表。中国人民志愿军特等功臣邱少云烈士的弟弟邱少华，刘兴文烈士的父亲刘玉顺，英雄易才学的父亲易培修，黄家富的父亲黄再廷，田文富的妻子甘玉珍，也出席了宴会。西南抗美援朝总分会副主席任白戈在宴会开始时致词表示热烈欢迎。中共中央西南局第一副书记宋任穷、西南行政委员会副主席王维舟相继讲话，欢迎朝鲜人民访华代表团来到西南。接着，李永镐副团长致答词。
    五日下午四时，西南行政委员会副主席贺龙接见朝鲜人民访华代表团来西南访问的全体代表和朝鲜人民军协奏团领队姜哲渊。
    五日下午六时半，西南区和重庆市各界人民，在西南行政委员会大礼堂举行盛大欢迎会，热烈欢迎朝鲜人民访华代表团来西南访问的全体代表和朝鲜人民军协奏团全体人员。西南行政委员会副主席贺龙、熊克武、刘文辉、王维舟、宋任穷、卢汉、邓锡侯，西南行政委员会委员张子意、周兴、于江震，西南军区副司令员李达、副政委王新亭，重庆市长曹荻秋、西南抗美援朝总分会副主席任白戈以及重庆市各机关、部队、团体负责人和干部四千五百余人出席了欢迎大会。欢迎会在雄壮的朝中两国国歌声中开始后，大会向李永镐、李东英副团长献花。西南抗美援朝总分会副主席任白戈致欢迎词，热烈欢迎朝鲜人民访华代表团来西南访问。李永镐副团长接着致答词，他对西南人民的欢迎表示感激和敬意。欢迎会上，朝鲜人民访华代表团分别向西南行政委员会、西南抗美援朝总分会、中华全国总工会西南办事处、中国新民主主义青年团西南工作委员会、中华全国民主妇女联合会西南工作委员会献旗。上述各单位也分别将锦旗献给朝鲜人民访华代表团。会后，朝鲜人民军协奏团演出了精采节目。
