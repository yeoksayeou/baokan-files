Title: Telegram of Tribute to Frédéric Joliot-Curie, Chairman of the World Peace Council
Author:
Date: 1954-04-21
Page: 1

    To Chairman Joliot-Curie of the World Peace Council:
    Today, as we hold a rally in Beijing, China, to celebrate the fifth anniversary of the World Peace Movement, please allow us to express our highest respect and warmest congratulations to you and the World Peace Council under your leadership. Over the past five years, the World Peace Movement has achieved significant accomplishments. The Korean armistice and the convocation of the Berlin Four Foreign Ministers Conference are major hallmarks of these achievements. As the Geneva Conference is about to convene, the Chinese people are willing to work with the people of the world to strive tirelessly for the Geneva Conference to achieve good results, further ease international tensions, and ensure peace in Asia and the world.
    People from all walks of life in the capital of China, celebrating the fifth anniversary of the World Peace Movement
    April 20, 1954

### 给世界和平理事会主席约里奥—居里致敬电

1954-04-21
第1版()
专栏：

    给世界和平理事会主席约里奥—居里致敬电世界和平理事会约里奥—居里主席：
    今天我们在中国北京举行庆祝世界和平运动五周年大会，请让我们向您及您领导的世界和平理事会表示崇高的敬意和热烈的祝贺。五年来世界和平运动获得了重大的成就。朝鲜停战和柏林四国外长会议的召开是这一重大成就的主要标志。当此日内瓦会议即将召开之际，中国人民愿与全世界人民一起，为日内瓦会议能获致良好结果，进一步缓和国际紧张局势，确保亚洲及全世界的和平而奋斗不懈。
    中国首都各界人民庆祝世界和平运动五周年大会
    一九五四年四月二十日
