Title: Northeast Petroleum Factory No. 2 Restoration Project Nearing Completion (Photos)
Author:
Date: 1954-04-25
Page: 2

    Northeast Petroleum Factory No. 2 Restoration Project Nearing Completion
    The restoration project for the second set of "retorts" and its auxiliary production equipment for manufacturing crude oil at Northeast Petroleum Factory No. 2 was basically completed at the end of March and will be test-run in production this "May Day." The entire restoration project for the crude oil equipment has been underway successively and will be put into production separately by the end of this year.

    The main equipment for manufacturing crude oil at Northeast Petroleum Factory No. 2 - the retort - will be test-run in production this "May Day." Xinhua News Agency reporter Jia Chengbin photo

    This is a component of a Soviet-style ball mill. Xinhua News Agency reporter Jia Chengbin photo

    After the complete restoration of Northeast Petroleum Factory No. 2, it will not only produce a large amount of crude oil, but also a large amount of ammonium sulfate solution for the manufacture of fertilizer. This is the ammonium sulfate absorption tower to be restored. Xinhua News Agency reporter Jia Chengbin photo

### 东北石油二厂恢复工程即将完成（图片）

1954-04-25
第2版()
专栏：

    东北石油二厂恢复工程即将完成
    东北石油二厂制造原油的第二部“干馏炉”及其辅助生产设备恢复工程，已于三月末基本结束，将在今年“五一”试车生产。原油设备的全部恢复工程已陆续施工，并将分别在今年年底投入生产。
    东北石油二厂制造原油的主要设备——干馏炉，将在“五一”试车生产。  新华社记者　贾承滨摄
    这是苏联式球磨机的部件。  新华社记者　贾承滨摄
    东北石油二厂全部工程恢复后，不仅生产大量原油，而且生产大量的硫铵溶液，供制造肥田粉之用。这是将要恢复的硫铵吸收塔。　新华社记者　贾承滨摄
