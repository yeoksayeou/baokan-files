Title: People from All Walks of Life in the Capital Hold a Grand Meeting to Warmly Celebrate the Fifth Anniversary of the World Peace Movement
Author:
Date: 1954-04-21
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, April 20th: A grand meeting of people from all walks of life in the capital to celebrate the fifth anniversary of the World Peace Movement was solemnly held on the afternoon of the 20th.

    Among those attending the meeting were: Guo Moruo, Chairman of the China Peace Committee; Chen Shutong, Vice Chairman; Standing Committee members Shen Junru, Shen Yanbing, Shao Lizi, Ma Yinchu, Zhang Naiqi, Xu Deheng, Xu Baoju, Huang Yanpei, Deng Yingchao, Liu Ningyi, Liao Chengzhi, Xiao San, Luo Longji; Nie Zhen, Chairman of the North China General Branch of the China Peace Committee; Wu Han, Vice Chairman; and Zeng Zhaolun, Vice Chairman of the Beijing Branch.

    Also attending the meeting were: Cai Tingkai, Zhang Bojun, Li Dequan, Liu Guanyi, Lu Cui and others, all members of the World Peace Council.

    Han Xueye, member of the World Peace Council and Chairman of the Korean National Committee for the Support of Peace; Zheng Shengyan (Korea), Kozhevnikov (Soviet Union), Kameda Togo (Japan), and Wantureri (Chile), Deputy Secretaries-General of the Asian and Pacific Regions Peace Liaison Committee, also attended the meeting.

    More than 1,000 representatives from various democratic parties and people's organizations also attended the meeting.

    Chen Shutong, Vice Chairman of the China Peace Committee, delivered the opening speech. He said: The World Peace Movement has experienced tremendous development and achievements in the past five years. In order to celebrate the development and achievements of the peace movement over the past five years, the World Peace Council has specifically suggested that all countries hold commemorations on this day of the year.

    Guo Moruo, Chairman of the China Peace Committee, reported on the achievements of the World Peace Movement over the past five years.

    The conference unanimously adopted a congratulatory telegram to Chairman Joliot-Curie of the World Peace Council.

    After the meeting, the China Youth Art Theater performed the drama "Steel Transport Soldiers".

### 首都各界人民举行大会  热烈庆祝世界和平运动五周年

1954-04-21
第1版()
专栏：

    首都各界人民举行大会
    热烈庆祝世界和平运动五周年
    【新华社二十日讯】首都各界人民庆祝世界和平运动五周年大会，在二十日下午隆重举行。
    出席大会的有：中国人民保卫世界和平委员会主席郭沫若、副主席陈叔通，常务委员沈钧儒、沈雁冰、邵力子、马寅初、章乃器、许德珩、许宝驹、黄炎培、邓颖超、刘宁一、廖承志、萧三、罗隆基，中国人民保卫世界和平委员会华北总分会主席聂真、副主席吴晗，北京市分会副主席曾昭抡等。
    出席大会的还有：世界和平理事会理事蔡廷锴、章伯钧、李德全、刘贯一、陆璀等。
    世界和平理事会理事、朝鲜拥护和平全国民族委员会委员长韩雪野，亚洲及太平洋区域和平联络委员会副秘书长郑圣彦（朝鲜）、柯热夫尼科夫（苏联）、龟田东伍（日本）、万徒勒里（智利）等也出席了大会。
    出席大会的还有各民主党派、各人民团体代表共一千多人。
    中国人民保卫世界和平委员会副主席陈叔通致开会词。他说：五年来世界和平运动曾有巨大的发展与成就，世界和平理事会为了庆祝五年来和平运动的发展与成就，特建议各国在今年的今天举行纪念。
    中国人民保卫世界和平委员会主席郭沫若在会上报告了五年来世界和平运动的成就。
    大会一致通过了致世界和平理事会约里奥—居里主席的致敬电。
    会后，中国青年艺术剧院演出了话剧“钢铁运输兵”。
