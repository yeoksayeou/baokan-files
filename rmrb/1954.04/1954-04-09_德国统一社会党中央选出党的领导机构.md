Title: Socialist Unity Party of Germany Central Committee Elects Party Leadership

Author:
Date: 1954-04-09
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Berlin, April 8th (TASS dispatch): The newly elected Central Committee of the Socialist Unity Party of Germany held a meeting on April 8th, presided over by Wilhelm Pieck, where the leadership organs of the Socialist Unity Party of Germany were elected.

Friedrich Ebert, Otto Grotewohl, Hermann Matern, Fred Oelsner, Wilhelm Pieck, Heinrich Rau, Karl Schirdewan, Willi Stoph, and Walter Ulbricht were elected as members of the Politburo of the Central Committee of the Socialist Unity Party of Germany. Erich Honecker, Bruno Leuschner, Erich Mückenberger, Alfred Neumann, and Herbert Warnke were elected as alternate members of the Politburo.

Walter Ulbricht was elected as First Secretary of the Central Committee. Erich Mückenberger, Fred Oelsner, Karl Schirdewan, Paul Wandel, and Gerhard Ziller were elected as Secretaries of the Central Committee.

Hermann Matern, Hertha Geffke, Max Sens, Ernst Jarthunke, Günter Tänzer, Elli Hempelt, Herbert Warnke, Otto Holz, and Otto Seppke were elected as members of the Central Control Commission of the Party. Heinrich Modzick, Richard Lielmann, Thomas Mrochen, Hermann Fischer, and Erich Amand were elected as alternate members of the Control Commission.

### 德国统一社会党中央选出党的领导机构

1954-04-09
第4版()
专栏：

    德国统一社会党中央选出党的领导机构
    【新华社八日讯】据塔斯社柏林八日讯：德国统一社会党新选举出来的中央委员会八日在威廉·皮克主持下举行了会议，会上选出德国统一社会党的领导机构。
    弗雷德里希·艾柏特、奥托·格罗提渥、赫尔曼·马特恩、弗雷德·厄斯纳、威廉·皮克、亨利希·劳、卡尔·希德万、威利·斯多夫、瓦尔特·乌布利希当选为德国统一社会党中央委员会政治局委员。埃里希·昂纳克、布伦诺·劳施纳、埃里希·穆肯柏格尔、艾尔弗雷德·诺曼、赫伯特·瓦恩克当选为政治局候补委员。
    瓦尔特·乌布利希当选为中央委员会第一书记。埃里希·穆肯柏格尔、弗雷德·厄斯纳、卡尔·希德万、保罗·汪戴尔、格哈德·齐勒当选为中央委员会书记。
    赫尔曼·马特恩、赫尔塔·盖夫凯、马克斯·森斯、恩斯特·亚尔敦基尔希、根特·滕纳、艾利·亨佩尔、赫伯特·维托尔茨、奥托·霍尔兹、奥托·塞普凯当选为党的中央监察委员会委员。亨利希·莫兹勒、理夏德·利尔曼、托马斯·姆罗亨、赫尔曼·费希尔、埃里希·阿曼特当选为监察委员会候补委员。
