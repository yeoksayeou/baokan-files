Title: German Trade Union Delegation Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1954-04-27
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, April 26th: The trade union delegation from the German Democratic Republic, invited by the All-China Federation of Trade Unions to participate in the “May 1st” Labor Day celebrations in our country, arrived in Beijing by plane at 2:00 PM on April 26th. Those who went to the airport to welcome them included: Li Jiebo, Secretary of the Secretariat of the All-China Federation of Trade Unions; Zhao Guoqiang, Chairman of the Preparatory Committee of the China Metal and Smelting Workers' Union; Xiang Minghua, Vice Chairman of the National Committee of the China Postal and Telecommunications Workers' Union; Chen Yu, member of the Executive Committee of the All-China Federation of Trade Unions; and Jin Ziming and Fang Ming, Deputy Directors of the International Liaison department of the All-China Federation of Trade Unions.

Also present at the airport to welcome the delegation were personnel from the Embassy of the German Democratic Republic in China.

The delegation list is as follows:

Head of Delegation: Heinz Meier, Member of the Presidium of the Joint Executive Committee of the Free German Trade Union Federation, Chairman of the Printing and Paper Workers' Union.
Members: Frieda Hoffmann, Labor Hero, worker at the Radeberg Saxonia Factory.
        Willi Freihof, Labor Hero, boiler worker at the state-owned Buna Chemical Plant.
Translator: Doris Wetterhahn

### 德工会代表团到京

1954-04-27
第1版()
专栏：

    德工会代表团到京
    【新华社二十六日讯】应中华全国总工会邀请前来我国参加“五一”节观礼的德意志民主共和国工会代表团已于二十六日下午二时乘飞机到达北京。前往机场欢迎的有：中华全国总工会书记处书记李颉伯、中国五金冶炼工会筹备委员会主席赵国强、中国邮电工会全国委员会副主席向明华、中华全国总工会执行委员会委员陈宇及中华全国总工会国际联络部副部长金子明、方明。
    前往机场迎接的还有德意志民主共和国驻我国大使馆人员。
    代表团名单如后：
    团长：海恩茨·迈尔　自由德国工会联合会联合执行委员会主席团委员，印刷与纸张工人工会主席
    团员：弗里达·霍夫曼　劳动英雄，拉德堡萨克森尼亚工厂工人
          韦利·弗莱霍夫　劳动英雄，国营布纳化学工厂锅炉工人
