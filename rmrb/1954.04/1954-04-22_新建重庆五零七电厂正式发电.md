Title: Newly Built Chongqing 507 Power Plant Formally Generates Electricity
Author:
Date: 1954-04-22
Page: 1

(Our newspaper's Chongqing dispatch from the 20th) The first mechanized thermal power plant in Southwest China – the 507 Power Plant – after one year and four months of construction and installation work, officially commenced production on April 20th. A ribbon-cutting ceremony to celebrate the commencement of electricity generation was held that afternoon at 3:00 PM. He Long, Secretary of the Southwest Bureau of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of China and Vice Chairman of the Southwest Administrative Committee, Song Renqiong, First Deputy Secretary of the Southwest Bureau and Vice Chairman of the Southwest Administrative Committee, along with guests from Party, government, and military organs, factories, and mines in Chongqing, as well as over 3,000 workers from the entire plant, attended the conference.

At the beginning of the conference, Li Hao, Director of the 507 Power Plant, first reported on the construction process, followed by a speech by Comrade He Long. Comrade He Long first extended congratulations to all the workers of the power plant on behalf of the Southwest Bureau of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of China and the Southwest Administrative Committee. He said: The successful construction of the 507 Power Plant is another victory in China's socialist industrialization and a major contribution to promoting the industrial development of Chongqing. This is the result of the selfless labor of all the workers, the enthusiastic support of the people of the whole country, and the selfless assistance of the Soviet Union. He especially expressed his heartfelt gratitude to the Soviet experts who participated in the construction of the plant, and through the Soviet experts, to the Communist Party of the Soviet Union, the Soviet government, and the Soviet people. He called on all workers to raise their class consciousness and technical level under the glorious light of the General Line, conscientiously learn from the advanced experience of the Soviet Union, ensure safe production, and strive to realize the country's socialist industrialization and overfulfill the state plan. Next, the head of the Soviet expert group, Ye Shikefu, spoke at the meeting. In his speech, he pointed out that the electricity generation of the 507 Power Plant is a vivid example of the great friendship between the people of China and the Soviet Union. He wished the power plant safe and economical operation after it started operation. Also speaking at the meeting were Li Da, Deputy Commander of the Southwest Military Region, Cao Diqiu, Secretary of the Chongqing Municipal Committee of the Communist Party of China and Mayor of Chongqing, and labor models from the power plant construction site. At the meeting, a congratulatory telegram from Chen Yu, Minister of the Central Ministry of Fuel Industry, to all the workers of the plant was also read. The Southwest Electric Power Administration and representatives of the power plant workers presented banners, flowers, and gifts to the Soviet experts. Finally, the conference unanimously adopted a letter of respect to Chairman Mao.

At 4:40 PM, Comrade He Long cut the ribbon in the turbine workshop, and the 507 Power Plant was declared officially in operation. After the 507 Power Plant is put into production, it will increase Chongqing's power generation capacity by 1.3 times.

### 新建重庆五零七电厂正式发电

1954-04-22
第1版()
专栏：

    新建重庆五零七电厂正式发电
    【本报重庆二十日电】西南第一座机械化火力发电厂——五零七电厂，经过一年零四个月的建筑安装工作，在四月二十日正式开工生产，并在当天下午三时，举行庆祝发电剪彩典礼大会。中共中央西南局书记、西南行政委员会副主席贺龙，西南局第一副书记、西南行政委员会副主席宋任穷和重庆市党、政、军机关和厂矿企业来宾及全厂职工三千多人参加了大会。
    大会开始，首由五零七电厂厂长李浩报告了建厂的经过，继由贺龙同志讲话。贺龙同志首先代表中共中央西南局、西南行政委员会向电厂全体职工致贺。他说：五零七电厂修建成功是我国社会主义工业化中的又一胜利，是推动重庆市工业发展的一个重大贡献。这是由于全体职工忘我劳动，全国人民热烈支援和苏联无私援助的结果。他特别向参加建厂的苏联专家，并通过苏联专家向苏联共产党、苏联政府和人民致以衷心的谢意。他号召全体职工在总路线的光辉照耀下，提高阶级觉悟和技术水平，认真学习苏联先进经验，保证安全生产，为实现国家社会主义工业化，为超额完成国家计划而努力。接着，苏联专家组长叶世科夫在会上讲话。他在讲话中指出：五零七电厂的发电是中苏两国人民伟大友谊的鲜明例证，他预祝电厂开工以后安全地经济地运转。在会上相继讲话的有：西南军区副司令员李达，中共重庆市委书记、重庆市市长曹荻秋和电厂工地劳动模范等。会上还宣读了中央燃料工业部部长陈郁给全厂职工的贺电。西南电业管理局和电厂职工代表并向苏联专家献旗、献花、献礼。最后大会一致通过向毛主席的致敬电。
    下午四点四十分钟，贺龙同志在透平车间剪彩，五零七电厂宣告正式开工生产。五零七电厂投入生产后，将使重庆市发电能力提高一点三倍。
