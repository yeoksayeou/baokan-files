Title: Vietnamese People's Armed Forces Attack the Eastern Suburbs of Da Nang
Author:
Date: 1954-04-22
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, April 21st: According to Vietnam News Agency on the 20th, on the evening of April 14th, the people's armed forces in the central Vietnamese province of Quang Nam attacked the eastern suburbs of Da Nang, destroying two enemy military strongholds - My Khe and Tan Thai, eliminating 105 enemy soldiers. The people's armed forces also destroyed eight enemy amphibious vehicles and damaged a bridge connecting these two strongholds and Da Nang.

### 越南人民武装袭击岘港东郊

1954-04-22
第1版()
专栏：

    越南人民武装袭击岘港东郊
    【新华社二十一日讯】据越南通讯社二十日讯：四月十四日晚上，中越广南省的人民武装袭击岘港东郊，拔除敌军据点两处——美溪和新太，歼灭敌军一百零五名。人民武装并击毁敌军水陆两用车辆八辆，破坏联系这两据点和岘港的桥梁一座。
