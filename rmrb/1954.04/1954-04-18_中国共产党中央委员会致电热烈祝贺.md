Title: Central Committee of the Communist Party of China Sends Warm Congratulations
Author:
Date: 1954-04-18
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, April 17th: The Central Committee of the Communist Party of China sent a telegram congratulating Nikolai Khrushchev, First Secretary of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of the Soviet Union, on his sixtieth birthday. The text of the telegram is as follows:

Comrade N.S. Khrushchev:

Dear Comrade Khrushchev, on the occasion of your sixtieth birthday, the Central Committee of the Communist Party of China extends to you, an outstanding leader of the Communist Party of the Soviet Union and the Soviet people, a loyal fighter who has tirelessly struggled and made outstanding contributions to the great cause of the Lenin-Stalin Party, our warm and fraternal congratulations, and wishes you good health.

The Central Committee of the Communist Party of China
April 15, 1954

### 中国共产党中央委员会致电热烈祝贺

1954-04-18
第1版()
专栏：

    中国共产党中央委员会致电热烈祝贺
    【新华社十七日讯】中国共产党中央委员会致电祝贺苏联共产党中央委员会第一书记尼·谢·赫鲁晓夫六十寿辰。电文如下：尼·谢·赫鲁晓夫同志：
    亲爱的赫鲁晓夫同志，在您六十寿辰之际，中国共产党中央委员会谨向您、苏联共产党和苏联人民的卓越领导者，为列宁斯大林党的伟大事业不倦斗争并有了卓越贡献的忠诚战士致以热烈的、兄弟般的祝贺，并祝您身体健康。中国共产党中央委员会
      一九五四年四月十五日
