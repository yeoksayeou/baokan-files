Title: Bulgarian Sculptor Funev Returns Home
Author:
Date: 1954-04-23
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, April 22nd: Ivan Zenov Funev, a famous Bulgarian sculptor, who was invited to visit China according to the 1953 executive plan of the Sino-Bulgarian Cultural Agreement, has returned home by plane on the 21st. During his visit to China, Ivan Funev visited and toured Beijing, Datong, Luoyang, Guangzhou, Hangzhou, Shanghai, Nanjing and other places, held many discussions with Chinese artists, and engaged in creative work.

Before his departure, Hong Shen, Director of the Bureau of Foreign Cultural Relations of the Cultural and Educational Committee of the Central People's Government, Xiao Chuanjiu, Professor of the East China Branch of the Central Academy of Fine Arts, and sculptor Zhang Songhe, among others, went to the airport to see him off.

Also at the airport to see him off was Grigorov, Chargé d'Affaires ad interim of the Embassy of the People's Republic of Bulgaria in China.

### 保加利亚雕塑家富涅夫返国

1954-04-23
第1版()
专栏：

    保加利亚雕塑家富涅夫返国
    【新华社二十二日讯】根据中保文化协定一九五三年执行计划应邀前来我国访问的保加利亚名雕塑家伊凡·泽诺夫·富涅夫，已在二十一日乘机返国。伊凡·富涅夫在中国访问和参观了北京、大同、洛阳、广州、杭州、上海、南京等地，和我国美术家举行了多次座谈，并进行了创作。
    临行时，中央人民政府政务院文化教育委员会对外文化联络事务局局长洪深、中央美术学院华东分院教授萧传玖和雕塑家张松鹤等都到机场欢送。
    在机场欢送的还有保加利亚人民共和国驻华大使馆临时代办格利郭洛夫。
