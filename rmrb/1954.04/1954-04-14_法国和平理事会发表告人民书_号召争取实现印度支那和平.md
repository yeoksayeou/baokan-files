Title: French Peace Council Issues Appeal to the People, Calling for Efforts to Achieve Peace in Indochina
Author:
Date: 1954-04-14
Page: 4

    Xinhua News Agency, Paris, April 7th: "L'Humanité" published an appeal to the French people from the Presidium of the French National Peace Council. The appeal stated that the main task of the French people currently is: to seek peace in Indochina and to reject the Bonn and Paris war treaties.
    The Presidium of the French National Peace Council decided to hold a demonstration in Paris on June 12th and 13th to defend peace and French independence. Delegations of peace fighters from all provinces of France will participate in this demonstration.

### 法国和平理事会发表告人民书  号召争取实现印度支那和平

1954-04-14
第4版()
专栏：

    法国和平理事会发表告人民书
    号召争取实现印度支那和平
    【新华社讯】塔斯社巴黎七日讯：“人道报”刊载了法国全国和平理事会主席团的告法国人民书。告法国人民书说：法国人民当前的主要任务是：在印度支那求得和平并且拒绝波恩和巴黎战争条约。
    法国全国和平理事会主席团决定于六月十二日和十三日在巴黎举行保卫和平和法国独立的示威游行。参加这次示威游行的，将有来自全法国各省的和平战士代表团。
