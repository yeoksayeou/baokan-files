Title: Soviet "Pravda" Reporter Comments on Foreign Minister Zhou Enlai's Speech
Author: 
Date: 1954-04-30
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, April 29th - "Pravda" reporters Zhukov, Pulishevsky, and Lasagin, in their Geneva dispatch "The Korean Question Awaits Resolution," commented on Foreign Minister Zhou Enlai's speech at the April 28th meeting, saying: This is a profound and powerful speech, resonating with the voices of hundreds of millions of awakened Asian people. Reading this speech, one feels noble and stirring emotions, the joy for a great people who have won independence and freedom through resolute struggle, and who have made their country truly great in a short period of time. The voice of this great country is resounding with such power on the international stage.

### 苏联“真理报”记者  评周恩来外长的发言

1954-04-30
第1版()
专栏：

    苏联“真理报”记者
    评周恩来外长的发言
    【新华社莫斯科二十九日电】“真理报”记者茹科夫、普利歇夫斯基、拉萨金在日内瓦通讯“朝鲜问题等待着解决”中，谈到周恩来外长在二十八日会议上的发言说：这是一篇深刻的、有力的演说，演说中响着亿万觉醒了的亚洲人民的声音。读着这篇演说，你会感受到高尚的、激动的感情，感受到为在坚强的斗争中获得了独立和自由，并在短期内使自己国家成为真正伟大国家的伟大人民而欢欣的感情。这个伟大国家的声音在国际舞台上以如此的力量震响着。
