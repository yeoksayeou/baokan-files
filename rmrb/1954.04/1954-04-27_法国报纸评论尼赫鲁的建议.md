Title: French Newspapers Comment on Nehru's Proposals
Author:
Date: 1954-04-27
Page: 4

    Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, April 26th: TASS, Paris, April 25th: The "Paris Radical News" commented on Indian Prime Minister Nehru's call for a cessation of hostilities in Indochina and non-interference in the country's internal affairs, saying that Nehru's proposals "bring a new element" to the Indochina issue. The newspaper emphasized that Paris reacted favorably to the proposal to cease fire in Indochina. "Le Monde" published an editorial on Nehru's proposals, saying that the aim of the proposals is "to prevent the war from expanding and to reduce the danger of this war turning into a world war."

### 法国报纸评论尼赫鲁的建议

1954-04-27
第4版()
专栏：

    法国报纸评论尼赫鲁的建议
    【新华社二十六日讯】塔斯社巴黎二十五日讯：印度总理尼赫鲁号召停止印度支那战火和不干涉这个国家的内政，“巴黎激进新闻”为此评论道，尼赫鲁的建议给印度支那问题“带来新的因素”。报纸着重说，巴黎对号召在印度支那停火的主张，反应良好。“世界报”就尼赫鲁的建议发表社论说，这个建议的目的是在于“使战争不致扩大和减少这个战争变为世界大战的危险”。
