Title: People of Xi'an and Chengdu Warmly Welcome the North Korean Delegation

Author:
Date: 1954-04-12
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency report: On the afternoon of April 9th, people from all walks of life in Xi'an held a welcome rally to warmly welcome representatives of the Korean People's Delegation to China who were visiting the Northwest, as well as all members of the two art troupes.

Among those attending the welcome rally were Ma Mingfang, Third Secretary of the Northwest Bureau of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of China, Ma Wenrui, Deputy Secretary, Zhang Zhizhong, Vice Chairman of the Northwest Administrative Committee, Ke Zhongping and Li Furen, Vice Chairmen of the Northwest Branch of the Resist America, Aid Korea General Association, and more than 1,000 people from all walks of life in the Northwest Region, Shaanxi Province, and Xi'an City.

Li Furen, Vice Chairman of the Northwest Branch of the Resist America, Aid Korea General Association, delivered a welcome speech, warmly welcoming the Korean People's Delegation to China. Head of the delegation, Kim Ung-gi, delivered a reply speech, expressing deep gratitude for the welcome from the people of the Northwest.

At the welcome rally, the Korean People's Delegation to China presented banners to the Northwest Administrative Committee, the Northwest Branch of the Resist America, Aid Korea General Association, the Northwest Working Committee of the All-China Federation of Trade Unions, the Northwest Working Committee of the New Democratic Youth League of China, and the Northwest Working Committee of the All-China Democratic Women's Federation; the aforementioned units also presented five banners to the Korean People's Delegation to China. After the meeting, the Korean National Art Theatre and the National Classical Art Theatre gave opening performances.

Xinhua News Agency report: Members of the Korean People's Delegation to China visiting the Southwest, led by Deputy Heads Li Yongho and Li Dongying, and the Korean People's Army Ensemble arrived in Chengdu on a special train on the morning of the 10th. More than 3,000 people from all walks of life in Sichuan Province and Chengdu City held a welcoming ceremony at the station.

On the evening of the 10th, people from all walks of life in Sichuan Province and Chengdu City held a grand welcome meeting. Among those attending the welcome meeting were Li Jingquan, Secretary of the Sichuan Provincial Committee of the Communist Party of China, Chairman of the Sichuan Provincial People's Government, and Political Commissar of the Sichuan Military Region of the Chinese People's Liberation Army, Li Dazhang, First Deputy Secretary of the Sichuan Provincial Committee of the Communist Party of China and Vice Chairman of the Sichuan Provincial People's Government, Yan Hongyan, Vice Chairman of the Joint Office of the Liaison Committee of the People's Representatives Conferences from Eastern Sichuan, Southern Sichuan, Western Sichuan, and Northern Sichuan, and more than 1,000 people from all walks of life in Sichuan Province and Chengdu City. At the welcome meeting, Yan Hongyan delivered a welcome speech. Deputy Head Li Yongho delivered a reply speech. He said that the heroic feats established on the Korean front by the Chinese People's Volunteer Army warriors represented by Huang Jiguang, Hu Xiudao, Qiu Shaoyun, Huang Jiafu, and Tan Bingyun will never be forgotten by the Korean people; the noble internationalist aid given to us by the Chinese people will forever shine in our history. After the meeting, the Korean People's Army Ensemble performed wonderful programs.

### 西安成都两市人民热烈欢迎朝鲜访华团

1954-04-12
第1版()
专栏：

    西安成都两市人民热烈欢迎朝鲜访华团
    据新华社讯：西安市各界人民九日下午举行欢迎大会，热烈欢迎朝鲜人民访华代表团部分来西北访问的代表和两个艺术团的全体人员。
    出席欢迎大会的有中共中央西北局第三书记马明方、副书记马文瑞，西北行政委员会副主席张治中，西北抗美援朝总分会副主席柯仲平、李敷仁以及西北区、陕西省、西安市各界人士一千多人。
    西北抗美援朝总分会副主席李敷仁致欢迎词，热烈欢迎朝鲜人民访华代表团。金应基团长致答词，对西北人民的欢迎表示深深的感谢。
    欢迎会上，朝鲜人民访华代表团向西北行政委员会、西北抗美援朝总分会、中华全国总工会西北工作委员会、中国新民主主义青年团西北工作委员会、中华全国民主妇女联合会西北工作委员会献旗；上述各单位也分别将五面锦旗献给朝鲜人民访华代表团。会后，朝鲜国立艺术剧场和国立古典艺术剧场作揭幕演出。
    据新华社讯：以副团长李永镐和李东英为首的朝鲜人民访华代表团访问西南的团员和朝鲜人民军协奏团，在十日上午乘专车到达成都。四川省及成都市各界人士三千多人在车站举行了欢迎仪式。
    十日晚，四川省和成都市各界人民举行了盛大欢迎会。参加欢迎大会的有中共四川省委会书记、四川省人民政府主席、中国人民解放军四川军区政治委员李井泉，中共四川省委员会第一副书记、四川省人民政府副主席李大章，川东、川南、川西、川北各界人民代表会议协商委员会联合办事处副主席阎红彦，以及四川省和成都市各界人士一千多人。欢迎会上，阎红彦致欢迎词。李永镐副团长致答词。他说，以黄继光、胡修道、邱少云、黄家富、谭炳云等为代表的中国人民志愿军勇士们，在朝鲜前线所建立的丰功伟迹，朝鲜人民永远不会忘记；中国人民给予我们的高尚的国际主义援助将永远辉耀在我们的历史上。会后，朝鲜人民军协奏团演出了精采节目。
