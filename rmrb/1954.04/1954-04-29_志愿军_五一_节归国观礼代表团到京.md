Title: Volunteer Army "May 1st" Labor Day Observation Delegation Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1954-04-29
Page: 4

    Xinhua News Agency: The Chinese People's Volunteer Army "May 1st" Labor Day observation delegation, consisting of over ninety people and led by General Zhang Nansheng, Deputy Director of the Political Department of the Chinese People's Volunteer Army, arrived in Beijing today. Those who went to the station to welcome the delegation included Xu Baoju and Luo Longji, Standing Committee members of the Chinese People's Anti-American and Aid-Korea General Association, Liu Guanyi, the Secretary-General, Gan Siqi, Deputy Director of the General Political Department of the Central People's Revolutionary Military Commission, Liu Qiren, Secretary-General, Zhu Zaoguan, Deputy Director of the General Office, representatives from various democratic parties, representatives from various people's organizations, Zhang Panshi, Fourth Deputy Secretary of the North China Bureau of the Communist Party of China, Wu Han, Deputy Mayor of Beijing, as well as literary and art workers, students, and others.
    Also present at the station to welcome the delegation were Choi Il, Ambassador of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea to China, and embassy staff.
    The representatives of the delegation include commanders, combatants, political workers, and logistics workers from various branches of the Chinese People's Volunteer Army who have made outstanding contributions in the struggle to resist U.S. aggression and aid Korea and defend world peace. Among them are many well-known heroes and models, including Wang Yanlin, a second-class hero who once commanded a squad in a battle to continuously capture five enemy positions, destroy twenty-eight enemy fire points, and annihilate more than two hundred and seventy enemy soldiers; Pan Changyi, a second-class hero who was wounded several times but continued to fight in the counterattack on "Cross" Hill in June last year and used a machine gun to cover his comrades in taking the main peak; Wang Wenfan, an excellent machine gun shooter and second-class hero who killed more than one hundred and fifty enemies in the Battle of North Mountain of Shixian Cave; Wang Daqiang, the company commander of the anti-aircraft artillery company who was awarded first-class merit for his company's downing and damaging seventy-one enemy aircraft in more than two years of air combat; Su Zhiming, a driver who safely drove more than 50,000 kilometers and was awarded first-class merit; and Chen Renhui, a seventeen-year-old female nurse who rescued seven wounded soldiers under artillery fire and was awarded second-class merit. The representatives returned home with boundless excitement and joy to participate in the "May 1st" Labor Day celebrations. Second-class hero Wang Wenfan said: We want to personally pay tribute to the industrial and agricultural labor models who have completed and exceeded the national plan, to the people of the whole country who are working hard to build the motherland, and bring more good news of the motherland's construction to our comrades in Korea.

### 志愿军“五一”节归国观礼代表团到京

1954-04-29
第4版()
专栏：

    志愿军“五一”节归国观礼代表团到京
    据新华社讯：中国人民志愿军“五一”节归国观礼代表团九十多人，在团长、中国人民志愿军政治部副主任张南生将军率领下，今日到达北京。前往车站欢迎的有中国人民抗美援朝总会常务委员许宝驹、罗隆基，秘书长刘贯一，中央人民政府人民革命军事委员会总政治部副主任甘泗淇，秘书长刘其人，办公厅副主任朱早观，各民主党派代表，各人民团体代表，中国共产党华北局第四副书记张磐石，北京市副市长吴晗，以及文艺工作者、学生等多人。
    到车站欢迎的还有朝鲜民主主义人民共和国驻我国大使崔一和使馆人员。
    代表团代表中有中国人民志愿军各兵种、各部队在抗美援朝、保卫世界和平的斗争中有卓越功绩的指挥员、战斗员、政治工作人员和后勤工作人员。其中并有许多著名的英雄和模范，有曾在某次战斗中指挥一个班连续攻占敌军五个阵地、摧毁敌军二十八个火力点，歼灭敌军二百七十多名的二级英雄王彦林；有在去年六月反击“十字架”山战斗中，数次负伤仍坚持战斗，并以机枪掩护战友夺下主峰的二级英雄潘昌义；有在石岘洞北山战斗中杀敌一百五十多人的优秀机枪射手、二级英雄王文范；有荣立一等功的高射炮连连长王大强，他所领导的连队在两年多对空作战中曾击落和击伤七十一架敌机；有安全行车五万多公里、荣立一等功的司机苏志铭；有在炮火中抢救出七名伤员的十七岁女护士、荣立二等功的陈仁惠等。代表们怀着无限兴奋和喜悦的心情归国来参加“五一”节观礼，二级英雄王文范说：我们要亲自向完成和超额完成国家计划的工业和农业劳动模范，向辛勤建设祖国的全国人民致敬，并把更多的祖国建设的好消息带给在朝鲜的战友。
