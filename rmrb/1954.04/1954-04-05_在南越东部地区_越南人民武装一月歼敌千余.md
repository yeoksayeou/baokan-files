Title: In the Eastern Region of South Vietnam, Vietnamese People's Armed Forces Eliminate Over a Thousand Enemies in One Month
Author:
Date: 1954-04-05
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, 4th dispatch: According to the Vietnam News Agency, the South Vietnam Eastern Region Command of the Vietnamese People's Army issued a communique stating that in the month ending March 15th, the people's armed forces in the South Vietnam Eastern Region conducted a total of 131 battles; uprooted and forced the enemy to withdraw from 53 strongholds; eliminated 1,722 enemy soldiers, including two captains and several sergeants; destroyed 40 enemy vehicles, 5 gunboats, and 15 gasoline tank cars. In addition, 239 puppet soldiers defected to the People's Army.

### 在南越东部地区  越南人民武装一月歼敌千余

1954-04-05
第1版()
专栏：

    在南越东部地区
    越南人民武装一月歼敌千余
    【新华社四日讯】据越南通讯社讯：越南人民军南越东部联区司令部发表公报说，在截至三月十五日为止的一个月中，南越东部地区的人民武装共作战一百三十一次；拔除和迫使敌人撤退据点五十三个；歼灭敌军一千七百二十二名，其中有上尉二名和军士若干名；击毁敌军汽车四十辆、炮艇五艘和汽油十五车厢。此外并有二百三十九名伪军向人民军投诚。
