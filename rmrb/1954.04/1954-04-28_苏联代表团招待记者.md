Title: Soviet Delegation Hosts Journalists
Author:
Date: 1954-04-28
Page: 1
　　Xinhua News Agency, April 27th, TASS Geneva, April 27th: After the conclusion of the first session of the Geneva Foreign Ministers' Conference, the press office of the Soviet delegation held a press conference at the Palais des Nations to host journalists from the Soviet Union and other countries. Approximately two hundred journalists attended the press conference.
　　The Director of the Press Department of the Soviet Ministry of Foreign Affairs, L.F. Ilyichev, reported to the journalists on the preliminary negotiations between the Soviet Minister of Foreign Affairs, V.M. Molotov, and the British Foreign Secretary, Anthony Eden. He said that the Soviet proposal regarding the issue of the conference chairman was adopted during the negotiations. Therefore, at the first meeting, following the proposal of the American delegation, it was decided that the Foreign Minister of Thailand, the Soviet Minister of Foreign Affairs, and the British Foreign Secretary would take turns serving as the chairman of the conference.
　　Following this, the journalists were informed about the proceedings of the first meeting of the Foreign Ministers' Conference. It was specifically mentioned that organizational issues were discussed at the first meeting.

### 苏联代表团招待记者

1954-04-28
第1版()
专栏：

    苏联代表团招待记者
    【新华社二十七日讯】塔斯社日内瓦二十七日讯：在日内瓦外长会议第一次会议结束之后，苏联代表团新闻处在国联大厦举行记者招待会，招待苏联及其他国家的新闻记者。出席记者招待会的记者有二百人左右。
    苏联外交部新闻司司长勒·弗·伊利切夫向记者报告了关于苏联外交部部长维·米·莫洛托夫和英国外交大臣安·艾登预先进行谈判的情况。他说，在谈判过程中苏联提出的关于会议主席问题的建议被采纳了。因此，在第一次会议上，按照美国代表团的建议，决定由泰国外交部部长、苏联外交部部长、英国外交大臣轮流担任会议主席。
    接着，向记者们报告了外长会议第一次会议的经过。特别告诉记者的是：在第一次会议上讨论了有关组织的问题。
