Title: Our Great Motherland (Picture)
Author:
Date: 1954-04-29
Page: 1

    Our Great Motherland
    Workers in various regions are launching a technical innovation movement to complete production tasks ahead of schedule and welcome the "May 1st" International Labor Day. This is Zhao Bingtang, a veteran lathe operator at the state-owned Shanghai Machine Tool Plant, who, influenced by the spirit of Wang Chonglun's technical innovation, has worked hard to improve his operations and techniques. He completed his April production task in ten working days and two hours, and is now striving to complete two months' worth of tasks in one month.

### 我们伟大的祖国(图片）

1954-04-29
第1版()
专栏：

    我们伟大的祖国
    各地工人正开展技术革新运动，提前完成生产任务，迎接“五一”国际劳动节。这是国营上海机床厂老车工赵炳堂在王崇伦技术革新精神的影响下，努力改进操作和技术，以十个工作日零两小时完成四月份的生产任务，现在正为一个月完成两个月任务而努力。
