Title: CPI Central Committee Calls on Indian People to Support the People of French India
Author:
Date: 1954-04-18
Page: 4

    Xinhua News Agency, Delhi, 16th. TASS reports from Delhi on the 16th: The Central Committee of the Communist Party of India has issued an appeal to the people of the country, calling on the Indian people to support the struggle of the people of French India.
    The appeal to the people of the country salutes the people of French India who are waging a heroic struggle to liberate themselves from imperialist rule. The appeal points out that despite the cruel persecution of patriots since 1947, the people of these territories have intensified their struggle to rejoin India.
    The appeal emphasizes that the French authorities are using the local administration as a transit base to transport reinforcements and weapons to Indochina to carry out wars of aggression.

### 印共中央号召印度人民支援法属印度人民

1954-04-18
第4版()
专栏：

    印共中央号召印度人民支援法属印度人民
    【新华社十六日讯】据塔斯社德里十六日讯：印度共产党中央委员会发表告全国人民书，号召印度人民支持法属印度人民的斗争。
    告全国人民书向正在为了从帝国主义统治下求得解放而进行英勇斗争的法属印度人民致敬。告全国人民书指出，尽管一九四七年以来，爱国者遭受了残酷的迫害，这些属地的人民还是加紧进行了争取重新归并于印度的斗争。
    告全国人民书着重指出，法国当局正在将本地治理作为一个转运基地，把援军和武器运送到印度支那去进行侵略战争。
