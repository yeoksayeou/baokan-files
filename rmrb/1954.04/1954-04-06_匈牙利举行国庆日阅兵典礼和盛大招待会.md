Title: Hungary Holds National Day Military Parade and Grand Reception
Author:
Date: 1954-04-06
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Budapest, April 5th: According to TASS, the Hungarian people celebrated the 9th anniversary of the liberation of Hungary by the Soviet army on April 4th.

　　On the morning of the 4th, a military parade was held in Stalin Square, Budapest. On the reviewing stand were: members of the Political Bureau and secretaries of the Central Committee of the Hungarian Working People's Party, headed by First Secretary of the Central Committee of the Hungarian Working People's Party, Rákosi; the heads of the Hungarian government, headed by Chairman of the Council of Ministers, Nagy; and Chairman of the Presidium of the Hungarian People's Republic, Dobi, and others.

　　On the grandstand were: advanced workers in Hungarian industry and agriculture, famous Hungarian scientists and cultural workers, Soviet Ambassador to Hungary, Kiselyov, and diplomatic envoys from various countries to Hungary.

　　Hungarian Minister of National Defense, Lieutenant General I. Bata, reviewed the troops and delivered a speech.

　　On the evening of the 4th, the Presidium of the Hungarian People's Republic and the Hungarian Council of Ministers held a grand reception in the National Assembly building to celebrate the 9th anniversary of the liberation.

　　Celebration meetings were also held in factories, institutions, villages, and schools across the country.

### 匈牙利举行国庆日阅兵典礼和盛大招待会

1954-04-06
第4版()
专栏：

    匈牙利举行国庆日阅兵典礼和盛大招待会
    【新华社五日讯】据塔斯社布达佩斯讯：四月四日，匈牙利人民庆祝苏联军队解放匈牙利九周年。
      四日早晨，布达佩斯斯大林广场上举行了阅兵典礼。在检阅台上的有：以匈牙利劳动人民党中央委员会第一书记拉科西为首的党中央委员会政治局委员和书记处书记，以部长会议主席纳吉为首的匈牙利政府首长，和匈牙利人民共和国主席团主席道比等人。
    在观礼台上的有：匈牙利工农业的先进工作者、著名的匈牙利科学家和文化工作者、苏联驻匈牙利大使基谢列夫和各国驻匈牙利外交使节。
    匈牙利国防部部长伊·巴塔中将检阅了部队，并发表了演说。
    四日晚间，匈牙利人民共和国主席团和匈牙利部长会议在国民议会大厦举行了庆祝解放九周年的盛大招待会。
    全国各地的工厂、机关、村庄和学校也纷纷举行了庆祝会。
