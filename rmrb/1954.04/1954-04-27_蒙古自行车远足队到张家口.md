Title: Mongolian Cycling Expedition Arrives in Zhangjiakou
Author: 
Date: 1954-04-27
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Zhangjiakou, April 26th: The Mongolian Ulaanbaatar-Beijing cycling expedition, led by Dambi, Vice Chairman of the Mongolian National Sports Commission, has passed through China's vast Inner Mongolian grasslands and arrived in Zhangjiakou City at 11:30 a.m. on April 26th.

Welcoming the team outside Pingmen in the suburbs of Zhangjiakou City were Guo Zhen, Deputy Director of the Preparatory Committee of the Zhangjiakou Municipal People's Government Sports Commission, and a cycling team of 20 male and female athletes representing Zhangjiakou City. Accompanying the Mongolian cycling team were Khasabatar, Vice Chairman of the Sports Commission of the People's Government of the Inner Mongolia Autonomous Region, and Buhe, Deputy Director of the Cultural Affairs Bureau of the People's Government of the Inner Mongolia Autonomous Region.

Also present to welcome the team was Demidavag, Third Secretary of the Embassy of the People's Republic of Mongolia in China.

More than 500 representatives from all walks of life in Zhangjiakou City held a welcoming ceremony on Jiefang Avenue. Many local Party and government officials personally attended the welcoming ceremony, and Young Pioneers presented flowers to the distinguished guests who had come from afar. Wang Shaowen, Mayor of Zhangjiakou City People's Government, delivered a warm welcome and sincere greetings to the cycling expedition of the People's Republic of Mongolia on behalf of the people of the city. Dambi expressed his gratitude on behalf of all the team members and the sports community of the People's Republic of Mongolia.

That evening, the Mongolian cycling team also attended a welcome banquet. Khasabatar, on behalf of the Sports Commission of the People's Government of the Inner Mongolia Autonomous Region and all the athletes of the Inner Mongolia Autonomous Region, presented a Hada, a silver bowl, and a Mongolian edition of the Selected Works of Mao Zedong to Dambi.

The Mongolian cycling expedition will continue cycling towards Beijing on the morning of the 27th.

### 蒙古自行车远足队到张家口

1954-04-27
第1版()
专栏：

    蒙古自行车远足队到张家口
    【新华社张家口二十六日电】由蒙古国家体育运动委员会副主席丹毕率领的蒙古乌兰巴托—北京自行车远足队，已经通过我国辽阔的内蒙草原，在四月二十六日上午十一时三十分到达张家口市。
    在张家口市郊平门外欢迎的，有张家口市人民政府体育运动委员会筹备会副主任郭真，张家口市男女运动员代表二十人组成的自行车队等。陪同蒙古自行车队来到的，有我国内蒙古自治区人民政府体育运动委员会副主席喀萨巴塔尔和内蒙古自治区人民政府文化事业管理局副局长布赫。
    前往欢迎的还有蒙古人民共和国驻我国大使馆三等秘书德米达瓦格等。
    张家口市各界代表五百多人在解放大街举行了欢迎会。许多当地党政机关的负责人都亲自出席了欢迎会，少年先锋队员把鲜花献给远道来的贵宾。张家口市人民政府市长王绍文代表全市人民向蒙古人民共和国自行车远足队致热烈的欢迎和亲切的慰问，丹毕代表全队人员和蒙古人民共和国体育界表示谢意。
    当晚，蒙古自行车队还参加了欢迎宴会。喀萨巴塔尔代表内蒙古自治区人民政府体育运动委员会和内蒙古自治区全体运动员向丹毕献哈达、银碗和蒙文版毛泽东选集。
    蒙古自行车远足队将在二十七日上午继续骑自行车向北京前进。
