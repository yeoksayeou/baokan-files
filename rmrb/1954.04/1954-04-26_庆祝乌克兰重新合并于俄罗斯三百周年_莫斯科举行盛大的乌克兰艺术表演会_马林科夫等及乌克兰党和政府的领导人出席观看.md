Title: Celebrating the 300th Anniversary of Ukraine's Reunification with Russia: Grand Ukrainian Art Performance Held in Moscow; Malenkov and Other Leaders, as well as Leaders of the Ukrainian Party and Government, Attend
Author: 
Date: 1954-04-26
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, 25th: According to TASS, to celebrate the 300th anniversary of Ukraine's reunification with Russia, Ukrainian artists held a grand Ukrainian art performance at the Bolshoi Theatre in Moscow on the 24th. The following attended the performance: Malenkov, Khrushchev, Voroshilov, Bulganin, Kaganovich, Mikoyan, Saburov, Pervukhin, Shvernik, Kirichenko, Suslov, Pospelov, Shatalin, Korotchenko, Chairman of the Presidium of the Supreme Soviet of the Ukrainian Republic, Kalchenko, Chairman of the Council of Ministers of the Ukrainian Republic, and other leaders of the Ukrainian Party and government. Many representatives attending the ongoing session of the Supreme Soviet of the Soviet Union and literary and art workers also attended the performance.

The program included a solemn hymn "Glory to the Communist Party" performed by a joint choir, beautiful Ukrainian songs praising the Soviet motherland and the friendship of all nationalities, Ukrainian folk dances "Metlitsa" and "Hutsulka," solos and duets from operas by Ukrainian and Russian composers, and a choral suite performed by the Ukrainian Republic National Song and Dance Ensemble. The Ukrainian artists' wonderful performance was warmly welcomed by the audience.

### 庆祝乌克兰重新合并于俄罗斯三百周年  莫斯科举行盛大的乌克兰艺术表演会  马林科夫等及乌克兰党和政府的领导人出席观看

1954-04-26
第4版()
专栏：

    庆祝乌克兰重新合并于俄罗斯三百周年
    莫斯科举行盛大的乌克兰艺术表演会
    马林科夫等及乌克兰党和政府的领导人出席观看
    【新华社二十五日讯】据塔斯社莫斯科讯：为庆祝乌克兰重新合并于俄罗斯三百周年，乌克兰的艺术家们二十四日在莫斯科的大剧院举行了盛大的乌克兰艺术表演会。出席表演会的有：马林科夫、赫鲁晓夫、伏罗希洛夫、布尔加宁、卡冈诺维奇、米高扬、萨布罗夫、别尔乌辛、什维尔尼克、基里钦科、苏斯洛夫、波斯伯洛夫、夏塔林、乌克兰共和国最高苏维埃主席团主席科罗特钦科、乌克兰共和国部长会议主席卡尔钦科和乌克兰党和政府的其他领导人。许多参加正在举行的苏联最高苏维埃会议的代表和文艺工作者也出席了表演会。
    表演节目有联合合唱团演出的庄严的颂歌“光荣属于共产党”、歌颂苏维埃祖国和各族人民的友谊的优美的乌克兰歌曲、乌克兰民族舞蹈“麦特里查”和“古楚尔卡”、乌克兰和俄罗斯作曲家的歌剧中的独唱和对唱，以及乌克兰共和国国家歌舞团演出的合唱组曲。乌克兰艺术家的精彩表演受到观众的热烈欢迎。
