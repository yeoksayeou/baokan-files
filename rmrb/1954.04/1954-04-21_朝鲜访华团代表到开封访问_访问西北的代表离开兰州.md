Title: Korean Delegation Visits Kaifeng; Delegation Visiting Northwest Departs Lanzhou
Author:
Date: 1954-04-21
Page: 1

    Xinhua News Agency Report: Part of the members of the Korean People's Delegation to China, headed by Vice Head Li Yong-ho and Li Dong-young, and the Korean People's Army Ensemble, accompanied by Chu Tu-nan, Director of the Committee for Eliminating Illiteracy of the Central People's Government, arrived in Kaifeng on the afternoon of the 19th at 1 p.m. by special train, after concluding their visit and performance in various places in the Southwest, passing through Wuhan on the afternoon of the 18th.
    Those who went to the station in Henan Province and Kaifeng City to welcome them included: Liu Jixue, Wang Yizhai, and Ren Zhiming, Vice Chairmen of the Henan Provincial People's Representative Conference Consultative Committee; Ji Wenfu, Chairman of the Henan Provincial Branch of the China People's Committee to Resist America and Aid Korea; Yue Ming, Vice Chairman; Jia Xinzhai, Vice Chairman of the Henan Provincial People's Government; Yang Weiping, First Deputy Secretary of the Henan Provincial Committee of the Chinese Communist Party; Liu Gang, Third Deputy Secretary; Chen Bingzhi, Chairman of the Kaifeng City Branch of the China People's Committee to Resist America and Aid Korea; Jiang Xin, Mayor of the Kaifeng Municipal People's Government; Wang Quanguo, Director of the Political Department of the Henan Military Region of the Chinese People's Liberation Army; responsible persons of various democratic parties and people's organizations in Henan Province; and representatives of people from all walks of life, totaling more than 1,000 people. A grand welcoming ceremony was held at the station. Wang Yizhai, Vice Chairman of the Henan Provincial People's Representative Conference Consultative Committee, delivered a welcoming speech, expressing heartfelt gratitude and welcome to the glorious envoys of the heroic Korean people for visiting Kaifeng. Vice Head Li Yong-ho delivered a reply speech expressing his gratitude. At 5 p.m., the Henan Province and Kaifeng City People's Representative Conference Consultative Committee and the Henan Province and Kaifeng City Branches of the China People's Committee to Resist America and Aid Korea hosted a banquet to entertain the distinguished guests.
    Xinhua News Agency Report: Part of the members of the Korean People's Delegation to China, headed by Head Kim Ung-gi, and the accompanying art troupe, after making a four-day visit in Lanzhou, departed by special train at 8 p.m. on the 18th to visit Baoding.
    Those who went to the station to see them off included: Deng Baoshan, Chairman of the Gansu Provincial People's Representative Conference Consultative Committee and the Gansu Provincial Branch of the China People's Committee to Resist America and Aid Korea; heads of the Gansu Province and Lanzhou City People's Government; representatives of local troops; responsible persons of various democratic parties and people's organizations; and representatives of all ethnic groups and all sectors of society, totaling 4,000 people.

### 朝鲜访华团代表到开封访问  访问西北的代表离开兰州

1954-04-21
第1版()
专栏：

    朝鲜访华团代表到开封访问
    访问西北的代表离开兰州
    据新华社讯：以副团长李永镐和李东英为首的朝鲜人民访华代表团部分团员和朝鲜人民军协奏团，在结束了西南各地访问演出之后，由中央人民政府扫除文盲工作委员会主任委员楚图南陪同，于十八日下午经过武汉，十九日下午一时乘专车到达开封。
    河南省及开封市前往车站欢迎的有：河南省各界人民代表会议协商委员会副主席刘积学、王毅斋、任芝铭，河南省抗美援朝分会主席嵇文甫、副主席岳明，河南省人民政府副主席贾心斋，中共河南省委第一副书记杨蔚屏、第三副书记刘刚，开封市抗美援朝分会主席陈冰之，开封市人民政府市长姜鑫，中国人民解放军河南军区政治部主任王全国，河南省各民主党派、各人民团体负责人以及各界人民代表共有一千多人。车站上举行了隆重的欢迎仪式。河南省各界人民代表会议协商委员会副主席王毅斋致欢迎词，对英雄的朝鲜人民的光荣使者来到开封访问表示衷心的感谢和欢迎。李永镐副团长致答词表示感谢。下午五时，河南省、开封市各界人民代表会议协商委员会和河南省、开封市抗美援朝分会设宴招待贵宾们。
    据新华社讯：以金应基团长为首的朝鲜人民访华代表团部分团员和随行艺术团，在兰州作了四天访问活动后，已在十八日下午八点乘专车去保定访问。
    前往车站欢送的，有甘肃省各界人民代表会议协商委员会、甘肃省抗美援朝分会主席邓宝珊，甘肃省和兰州市人民政府首长，当地驻军代表，各民主党派、各人民团体负责人和各族各界代表共四千人。
