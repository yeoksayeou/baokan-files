Title: Egypt's New Government Formed
Author:
Date: 1954-04-24
Page: 4

International Briefs

    Egypt's "Revolutionary Committee" announced on the 17th that, according to the "Revolutionary Committee's" decision, Egyptian President General Naguib appointed Deputy Prime Minister Lieutenant Colonel Gamal Abdel Nasser as Prime Minister and entrusted him with organizing the new government. Nasser announced the list of the new government personnel on the morning of the 18th, including Hassan Marei as Minister of Commerce and Industry, Guindi Abdel Malek as Minister of Supply, Mohamed Awad Mohamed as Minister of Education, and Abdel Hamid El Sherif as Minister of Finance. Lieutenant Colonel Abdel Latif Baghdad, who served as Minister of the Army and Navy in the previous government, was reassigned as Minister of Municipal and Rural Affairs, and Lieutenant Colonel Husseini Shafei was appointed as Minister of the Army and Navy. Lieutenant Colonel Hassani Ibrahim was appointed as Minister of State in charge of Presidential Affairs. Personnel in other departments such as the Ministry of Foreign Affairs, the Ministry of Interior, the Ministry of National Guidance, the Ministry of Agriculture, the Ministry of Justice, and the Ministry of Social Affairs remained unchanged.

### 埃及新政府组成

1954-04-24
第4版()
专栏：国际简讯

    埃及新政府组成
    埃及“革命委员会”十七日宣布，根据“革命委员会”的决议，埃及总统纳吉布将军任命副总理加麦尔·阿卜杜勒·纳赛尔中校为总理并委托他组织新政府。纳赛尔于十八日上午宣布了新政府人员的名单，其中包括商业和工业部部长哈桑·马雷、供应部部长古英迪·阿卜杜勒·马列克、教育部部长穆罕默德·阿瓦德·穆罕默德和财政部部长阿卜杜勒·哈米德·艾尔·薛里夫。在上届政府中担任陆海军部部长的阿卜杜勒·拉蒂夫·巴格达迪空军中校改任市政与农村事务部部长，陆海军部部长由侯赛尼·夏费中校担任。哈萨尼·易卜拉欣空军中校担任负责总统事务的国务部部长。外交部、内政部、国家指导部、农业部、司法部和社会工作部等其他部门的人员未作更动。
