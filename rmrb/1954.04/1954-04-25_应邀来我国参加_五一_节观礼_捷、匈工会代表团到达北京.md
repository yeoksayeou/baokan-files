Title: Czechoslovak and Hungarian Trade Union Delegations Arrive in Beijing to Attend “May Day” Observances in China

Author: 
Date: 1954-04-25
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency: Beijing—At the invitation of the All-China Federation of Trade Unions, the Czechoslovak Trade Union Delegation and the Hungarian Trade Union Delegation, which will attend the “May Day” celebrations in China, arrived in Beijing by plane at 2:00 p.m. on the 24th. Xu Zhi-zhen, Secretary of the Secretariat of the All-China Federation of Trade Unions, and others were present to welcome them.

The list of the Czechoslovak Trade Union Delegation is as follows:

	Head of Delegation: Gustav Hnilicka, Secretary of the Central Council of Czechoslovak Trade Unions.

	Members: Julius Slabey, lathe operator, recipient of the Order of the Czechoslovak Republic. Member of the Czechoslovak Committee for the Defense of Peace.
		Karel Kral, editor of the Central Council of Trade Unions.
	Secretary: Jaroslav Richterbraun.

The list of the Hungarian Trade Union Delegation is as follows:

	Head of Delegation: András Jénes, Member of the Presidium of the Central Council of Hungarian Trade Unions.

	Member: Zoltán Szabó, Chairman of the Medical Workers' Trade Union.

### 应邀来我国参加“五一”节观礼  捷、匈工会代表团到达北京

1954-04-25
第1版()
专栏：

    应邀来我国参加“五一”节观礼
    捷、匈工会代表团到达北京
    据新华社讯：应中华全国总工会邀请前来我国参加“五一”节观礼的捷克斯洛伐克工会代表团和匈牙利工会代表团，在二十四日午后二时乘飞机到达北京。前往欢迎的有中华全国总工会书记处书记许之桢等。

                洛伐克工会中央理事会书记。

                捷克斯洛伐克共和国勋章。捷克
