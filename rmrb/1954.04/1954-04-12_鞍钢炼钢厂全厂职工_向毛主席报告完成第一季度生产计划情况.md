Title: Anshan Iron and Steel Works Steelmaking Plant All Employees Report to Chairman Mao on the Completion of the First Quarter Production Plan

Author:

Date: 1954-04-12
Page: 1

    Anshan Iron and Steel Works Steelmaking Plant All Employees
    Report to Chairman Mao on the Completion of the First Quarter Production Plan
    (Xinhua News Agency Dispatch) The entire staff of the Anshan Iron and Steel Company's Steelmaking Plant wrote a letter to Chairman Mao on April 10th, reporting on the completion of the first quarter production plan.
    In the letter, the employees first reported the good news to Chairman Mao that they had completed the national plan for March three days ahead of schedule and the first quarter production task eight days ahead of schedule. The steel production in the first quarter exceeded the national plan by 10.2 percent, the qualification rate increased by 0.52 percent compared with the plan, and the cost was reduced by more than 6 billion yuan. The steel grades tasks all completed the national plan. In particular, the output of high-quality steel exceeded the plan every month for three months. In terms of daily output, taking the average daily output in the fourth quarter of last year as 100, the average daily steel output in March this year has reached 110.6. The furnace bottom unit area utilization coefficient averaged 100 in the fourth quarter of last year, and 110.6 in March this year. The employees concluded in the letter: "All employees of our factory will never be arrogant, continue to work hard, strengthen furnace maintenance, consolidate the regular labor competition, learn from the advanced experience of the Soviet Union, and strive to produce more and better high-quality steel."

### 鞍钢炼钢厂全厂职工  向毛主席报告完成第一季度生产计划情况

1954-04-12
第1版()
专栏：

    鞍钢炼钢厂全厂职工
    向毛主席报告完成第一季度生产计划情况
    据新华社讯：鞍山钢铁公司炼钢厂全体职工，四月十日写信向毛主席报告完成第一季度生产计划的情况。
    职工们在信中首先向毛主席报告了他们已提前三天完成三月份国家计划和提前八天完成第一季度生产任务的喜讯。第一季度的产钢量较国家计划超过百分之一十点二，合格率较计划提高百分之零点五二，降低成本六十多亿元。钢种任务都完成了国家计划。特别是优质钢的产量，三个月中月月都超过了计划。在日产量方面，如以去年第四季度的平均日产量为一百，今年三月份平均日产钢量已达到了一百一十点六。炉底单位面积利用系数，去年第四季度平均为一百，今年三月份为一百一十点六。职工们最后在信中说：“我们厂全体职工绝不骄傲，继续努力，加强炉体维护，巩固经常化的劳动竞赛，学习苏联先进经验，为生产更多更好的优质钢而奋斗。”
