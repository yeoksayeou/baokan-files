Title: Chinese People's Institute of Foreign Affairs Hosts Banquet for Norwegian People's Cultural Delegation
Author:
Date: 1954-04-29
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, April 27th: The acting president of the Chinese People's Institute of Foreign Affairs, Zhou Gengsheng, held a banquet at the Peace Hotel on the 27th to welcome the Norwegian People's Cultural Delegation, which was invited to visit our country.

Professor Jessing, head of the Norwegian People's Cultural Delegation, Reverend Fobbe, deputy head, and all members of the delegation attended the banquet. Also present at the banquet were Chen Hansheng, Hu Yuzhi, and Qian Duansheng, vice presidents of the Chinese People's Institute of Foreign Affairs; Liu Guanyi, Secretary-General of the Chinese People's Committee for World Peace; Jin Ziming, Deputy Director of the International Liaison Department of the All-China Federation of Trade Unions; and figures from the literary, medical, and religious circles, including Hong Shen, Zheng Zhenduo, Fang Shishan, and Jiang Yizhen.

Zhou Gengsheng delivered a speech at the beginning of the banquet, extending a warm welcome to the Norwegian People's Cultural Delegation. He pointed out that the people of China and Norway have long enjoyed a friendship, which has further developed since the founding of the People's Republic of China. He concluded by saying that the Norwegian people and the Chinese people both love peace, and the visit of the Norwegian People's Cultural Delegation to China signifies the enhancement of mutual understanding and friendship between the two peoples.

Head of Delegation Jessing and Deputy Head Fobbe also delivered speeches at the banquet. Jessing said that the Norwegian delegation is composed of people with different political and religious beliefs and different professions, but they all came to visit New China with the same feeling. He expressed that after the delegation returns home, they will introduce what they have seen and heard in China to the Norwegian people, as a contribution to the world peace movement.

The banquet proceeded in a friendly atmosphere.

### 中国人民外交学会  欢宴挪威人民文化代表团

1954-04-29
第4版()
专栏：

    中国人民外交学会
    欢宴挪威人民文化代表团
    【新华社二十七日讯】中国人民外交学会代会长周鲠生于二十七日在和平宾馆举行宴会，欢迎应邀来我国访问的挪威人民文化代表团。
    挪威人民文化代表团团长耶辛教授、副团长福贝克牧师及代表团全体团员都参加了宴会。出席宴会作陪的有中国人民外交学会副会长陈翰笙、胡愈之、钱端升，中国人民保卫世界和平委员会秘书长刘贯一、中华全国总工会国际联络部副部长金子明和文艺界、医学界、宗教界人士洪深、郑振铎、方石珊、蒋翼振等人。
    周鲠生在宴会开始时致词，对挪威人民文化代表团表示热烈欢迎。他指出：中挪两国人民长久存在着友谊，这个友谊自从中华人民共和国成立以来就有了进一步的发展。他最后说：挪威人民和中国人民同样爱好和平，挪威人民文化代表团的访问中国，意味着中挪两国人民的相互了解和友谊的增进。
    耶辛团长和福贝克副团长也在宴会上先后发表演说，耶辛说，挪威代表团是由不同政治和宗教信仰、不同职业的人组成的，但是都抱着同样的心情来访问新中国。他表示，代表团回国后，将在中国的所见所闻，介绍给挪威人民，以此作为对世界和平运动的贡献。
  宴会在友好气氛中进行。
