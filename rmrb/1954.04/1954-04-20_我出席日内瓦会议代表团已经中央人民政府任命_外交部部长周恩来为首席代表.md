Title: Central People's Government Appoints Delegation to Geneva Conference, Foreign Minister Zhou Enlai as Chief Representative
Author:
Date: 1954-04-20
Page: 1

    Xinhua News Agency, 19th: Chairman Mao Zedong of the Central People's Government has appointed Foreign Minister Zhou Enlai as the Chief Representative of the People's Republic of China delegation to the Geneva Conference, and Vice Foreign Ministers Zhang Wentian, Wang Jiaxiang, and Li Kenong as representatives.

### 我出席日内瓦会议代表团已经中央人民政府任命  外交部部长周恩来为首席代表

1954-04-20
第1版()
专栏：

    我出席日内瓦会议代表团已经中央人民政府任命
    外交部部长周恩来为首席代表
    【新华社十九日讯】　中央人民政府毛泽东主席已经任命外交部部长周恩来为出席日内瓦会议的中华人民共和国代表团首席代表，外交部副部长张闻天、王稼祥、李克农为代表。
