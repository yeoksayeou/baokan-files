Title: Korean Delegation Arrives in Geneva
Author:
Date: 1954-04-26
Page: 1

    Xinhua News Agency, Geneva, April 25th: Nam Il, Minister of Foreign Affairs and chief representative of the government delegation of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea attending the Geneva Conference, arrived in Geneva on the 23rd at 10:00 AM local time. Bai Nan-un and Ki Sok-bok, representatives of the delegation, arrived with him.
    Further dispatch: When the government delegation of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea attending the Geneva Conference passed through Berlin on the 22nd, Lothar Bolz, Deputy Prime Minister and Minister of Foreign Affairs of the German Democratic Republic, Paul Wandel, Secretary of the Central Committee of the Socialist Unity Party, and Georg Henke, State Secretary of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs, went to the airport to welcome them. Soviet Ambassador to the German Democratic Republic, Semyonov, Chinese Ambassador to the German Democratic Republic, Ji Pengfei, and diplomatic envoys of various People's Democratic countries also welcomed them at the airport.
    That evening, Bolz, the Minister of Foreign Affairs of the German Democratic Republic, hosted a banquet to entertain the Korean delegation. The Soviet and Chinese ambassadors to Germany were also invited to the banquet.

### 朝鲜代表团到达日内瓦

1954-04-26
第1版()
专栏：

    朝鲜代表团到达日内瓦
    【新华社日内瓦二十五日电】　出席日内瓦会议的朝鲜民主主义人民共和国政府代表团首席代表外务相南日已于二十三日当地时间上午十时抵达日内瓦。同来的有代表团代表白南云与奇石福。
    又讯：出席日内瓦会议的朝鲜民主主义人民共和国政府代表团于二十二日过柏林时，赴机场欢迎的有德意志民主共和国副总理兼外交部长洛塔·博尔茨，统一社会党中央委员会书记保罗·汪戴尔，外交部国务秘书格奥克·韩克等。苏联驻德意志民主共和国大使谢麦诺夫，中华人民共和国驻德意志民主共和国大使姬鹏飞，和各人民民主国家驻德使节也到机场欢迎。
    当晚，德意志民主共和国外交部长博尔茨设宴招待朝鲜代表团，苏、中两国驻德大使也应邀出席了宴会。
