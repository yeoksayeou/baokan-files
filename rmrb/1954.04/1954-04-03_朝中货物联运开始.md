Title: China-North Korea Joint Cargo Transportation Begins
Author:
Date: 1954-04-03
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Pyongyang, April 2nd: According to the Korean Central News Agency, in accordance with the Sino-Korean border railway agreement signed on the basis of the International Railway Passenger and Freight Transport Agreement, cargo transportation between North Korea and China began on April 1st.

The stations designated by North Korea to handle cargo transportation include 28 stations such as Sinuiju, Manpo, Namyang, Pyongyang, Hamhung, and Chongjin.

The commencement of joint cargo train services between North Korea and China will further enhance the friendship between the people of the two countries, promote cooperation and development in transportation between the two countries, and help further develop mutually beneficial cooperation in the economic and cultural fields between North Korea and the Soviet Union and other People's Democratic countries.

With the commencement of joint cargo transportation between North Korea and China, North Korea will also handle cargo transportation with fraternal countries such as the Soviet Union, Czechoslovakia, Poland, Hungary, the German Democratic Republic, Romania, Bulgaria, the Mongolian People's Republic, and Albania, which are parties to the International Railway Passenger and Freight Transport Agreement.

Korean railways officially joined the International Railway Passenger and Freight Transport Agreement as a member of the international railway network on August 1, 1953.

### 朝中货物联运开始

1954-04-03
第1版()
专栏：

    朝中货物联运开始
    【新华社平壤二日电】据朝鲜中央通讯社讯：根据以国际铁路旅客和货物联运协定为基础而签订的中朝国境铁路协定，朝鲜和中国之间从四月一日开始货物联运。
    朝鲜方面指定办理货物联运的车站有：新义州、满浦、南阳、平壤、咸兴、清津等二十八个站。
    朝鲜和中国之间联运货物列车的开行，将进一步增强朝中两国人民的友谊，促进两国间交通运输事业的合作和发展，并有助于进一步发展朝鲜和苏联及其他人民民主国家间经济文化的互助合作。
    随着朝鲜和中国之间货物联运的开始，朝鲜和参加国际铁路旅客和货物联运协定的苏联、捷克斯洛伐克、波兰、匈牙利、德意志民主共和国、罗马尼亚、保加利亚、蒙古人民共和国、阿尔巴尼亚等兄弟国家，也将办理货物联运。
    朝鲜铁路是在一九五三年八月一日正式作为国际铁路网的一员参加国际铁路旅客和货物联运协定的。
