Title: Mongolian Cycling Team Arrives at Nankou
Author:
Date: 1954-04-29
Page: 4

    Xinhua News Agency, April 28th: The Mongolian People's Republic's Ulaanbaatar-Beijing cycling expedition team, after braving the rain and passing through the treacherous Badaling and Juyongguan, arrived at Nankou Town, 110 li from Beijing, at 5 p.m. on the 28th.
    After starting from Zhangjiakou City, the Mongolian cycling expedition team passed through Xuanhua City, Shacheng, and other places, and was warmly welcomed by the local people. Along the way, villagers and townspeople poured water on the roads to reduce wind and sand, making it easier for the cycling expedition team to travel.
    The Mongolian cycling expedition team is scheduled to embark on the final leg of the Ulaanbaatar-Beijing route on the 29th, and will arrive in Beijing on the afternoon of the same day.

### 蒙古自行车队到达南口

1954-04-29
第4版()
专栏：

    蒙古自行车队到达南口
    【新华社二十八日讯】蒙古人民共和国乌兰巴托—北京自行车远足队，冒雨通过险峻的八达岭和居庸关等地后，已于二十八日下午五时到达离北京一百一十华里的南口镇。
    蒙古自行车远足队从张家口市起程后，路经宣化市、沙城等地，曾受到当地人民热烈欢迎。沿途各地村镇人民，为了减少风沙飞扬，使自行车远足队便于行驶，纷纷用水浇泼道路。
    蒙古自行车远足队定于二十九日踏上乌兰巴托—北京最后一段路程，并将于同日下午到达北京。
