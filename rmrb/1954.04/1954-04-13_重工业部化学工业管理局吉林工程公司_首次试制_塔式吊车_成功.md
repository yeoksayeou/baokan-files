Title: Jilin Engineering Company of the Chemical Industry Administration Bureau of the Ministry of Heavy Industry Successfully Trials "Tower Crane" for the First Time
Author: Dahai
Date: 1954-04-13
Page: 2

    Jilin Engineering Company of the Chemical Industry Administration Bureau of the Ministry of Heavy Industry
    Successfully Trials "Tower Crane" for the First Time
    Workers of the Jilin Engineering Company of the Chemical Industry Administration Bureau of the Ministry of Heavy Industry, after more than fifty days of effort, successfully trial-manufactured a "tower crane" for the first time. This crane is more than 30 meters high and can lift about two tons each time. With this crane for high-altitude transportation, the difficulties of transporting building materials and mechanical equipment at high altitudes are overcome. (Dahai) (Attached picture)

### 重工业部化学工业管理局吉林工程公司  首次试制“塔式吊车”成功
大海
1954-04-13
第2版()
专栏：

    重工业部化学工业管理局吉林工程公司
    首次试制“塔式吊车”成功
    中央人民政府重工业部化学工业管理局吉林工程公司职工，经过五十多天的努力，首次试制“塔式吊车”成功。这种吊车高达三十多公尺，每次能举重两吨左右。进行高空运输，有了这种吊车，就克服了高空运输建筑材料和机械设备的困难。　（大海）（附图片）
