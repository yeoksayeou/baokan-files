Title: Chinese Trade Union Delegation Departs for Soviet Union to Attend May Day Celebrations
Author:
Date: 1954-04-17
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, April 16th:
Upon the invitation of the All-Union Central Council of Trade Unions, the Chinese Trade Union Delegation to the Soviet Union to attend the May Day celebrations departed from Beijing by plane this morning, April 16th, bound for Moscow. Those who went to the airport to see them off included Xu Zhizhen and Liu Ziku, Secretaries of the All-China Federation of Trade Unions, and Jin Ziming, Deputy Director of the International Liaison Department, among others. The delegation list is as follows:

团长 (Tuánzhǎng): Head of Delegation: Du Yanqing, Member of the Presidium of the All-China Federation of Trade Unions, Director of the Northwest Working Committee of the All-China Federation of Trade Unions.
秘书 (Mìshū): Secretary: Wu Ping, Deputy Director of the Women Workers Department of the All-China Federation of Trade Unions.

团员 (Tuányuán): Members:
	Liu Yun, Alternate Member of the Executive Committee of the All-China Federation of Trade Unions, Vice Chairman of the Guangxi Provincial Federation of Trade Unions.
	Peng Tao, Member of the Chongqing Municipal Federation of Trade Unions.
	Shen Han, Vice Chairman of the Shanghai General Trade Union.
	Ren Zihe, Vice Chairman of the Gansu Provincial Federation of Trade Unions.
	Wang Xiangsheng, Director of the Academic Affairs Office of the Cadre School of the All-China Federation of Trade Unions.
	Hou Che, Director of the North China Private Enterprise Working Committee of the All-China Federation of Trade Unions.
	Tang Liyan, Section Chief of the Steel Foundry of the Dalian Sino-Soviet Shipbuilding Company, who successfully cast anchor chains.
	Wang Chonglun, Youth Planner of the Tool Workshop of the Mechanical General Plant of Anshan Iron and Steel Company, Anshan Special-Grade Labor Model, who created tools such as the "Universal Tool Tire."
	Huang Baomei (female), Fine Spinning Operator of the State-owned Shanghai No. 17 Cotton Textile Factory, National Textile Industry Labor Model, Outstanding Worker of the Hao Jianxiu Work Method.
	Cai Liancheng, Steelmaking Worker of Tangshan Steel Plant, Tangshan Labor Model, Creator of the Top and Bottom Blowing Converter Steelmaking Method.
