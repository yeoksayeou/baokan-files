Title: Part of the Korean Delegation to China Visits Gui Sui Then Goes to Taiyuan
Author:
Date: 1954-04-03
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, Gui Sui, April 2nd: Part of the Korean People's Delegation to China, headed by leader Kim Ung-gi, and members of the art troupe, left Gui Sui at 9:00 PM on the 1st to visit Taiyuan and other places, after a five-day visit to Gui Sui.

Those who went to the station to see them off included Wang Zaitian and Sun Lanfeng, vice-chairmen of the Preparatory Committee of the Inner Mongolia Autonomous Region Consultative Committee; Hu Zhaoheng, vice-chairman of the Inner Mongolia Autonomous Region Anti-US and Aid-Korea General Branch; Su Qianyi, deputy secretary of the Inner Mongolia Branch Bureau of the CPC Central Committee; Yang Zhilin, vice-chairman of the People's Government of the Inner Mongolia Autonomous Region; Liu Huaxiang, deputy commander of the Inner Mongolia Military Region of the Chinese People's Liberation Army; Ruan Muhan, mayor of Gui Sui; and leaders of various people's organizations and representatives from all ethnic groups and walks of life, totaling more than 2,000 people.

During the delegation's visit to Gui Sui, the city was filled with the profound friendship between the Chinese and Korean peoples. Wherever members of the delegation appeared, crowds of people rushed to shake hands and embrace them. The delegation watched the equestrian performance of the Inner Mongolia Military Region Equestrian Team of the Chinese People's Liberation Army, visited the Inner Mongolia Animal Husbandry and Veterinary College and the famous Xilitu Zhao Temple in Gui Sui, and held discussions with representatives of workers, farmers, herdsmen, youth, women, as well as family members of revolutionary martyrs, military personnel, and model workers in Gui Sui. The accompanying art troupe gave wonderful performances to nearly 60,000 people of all ethnic groups in the autonomous region.

Before leaving, the delegation was invited to attend the reception performance of the Inner Mongolia National Song and Dance Troupe and a grand banquet held by the People's Government of the Inner Mongolia Autonomous Region. The People's Government of the Inner Mongolia Autonomous Region, the Inner Mongolia Military Region of the Chinese People's Liberation Army, and various people's organizations also presented the Korean People's Delegation to China with valuable gifts from the Mongolian people, such as Hada scarves, silver bowls, silver chopsticks, ethnic costumes, and Mongolian versions of the Selected Works of Mao Zedong.

### 朝鲜访华代表团部分团员  访问归绥后去太原

1954-04-03
第1版()
专栏：

    朝鲜访华代表团部分团员
    访问归绥后去太原
    【新华社归绥二日电】以金应基团长为首的朝鲜人民访华代表团部分团员和艺术团人员，在归绥市进行了五天访问后，已于一日下午九时离开归绥到太原等地访问。
    到车站欢送的，有内蒙古自治区协商委员会筹备委员会副主席王再天、孙兰峰，内蒙古自治区抗美援朝总分会副主席胡昭衡，中共中央内蒙古分局副书记苏谦益，内蒙古自治区人民政府副主席杨植霖，中国人民解放军内蒙古军区副司令员刘华香，归绥市人民政府市长阮慕韩，以及各人民团体负责人，各族各界代表等共二千余人。
    代表团在归绥访问期间，全市洋溢着中朝两国人民深厚的友谊。凡是有代表团人员出现的地方，都有成群的人争着和他们握手拥抱。代表团观看了中国人民解放军内蒙古军区马术队的马术表演，参观了内蒙古畜牧兽医学院和归绥市著名的席勒图召庙，和全区工人、农民、牧民、青年、妇女以及归绥市志愿军烈属、军属和功臣家属代表举行了座谈。随行艺术团向自治区近六万各族人民作了精彩的表演。
    临行前，代表团应邀出席了内蒙古民族歌舞团的招待演出大会和内蒙古自治区人民政府举行的盛大宴会。内蒙古自治区人民政府、中国人民解放军内蒙古军区和各人民团体，并向朝鲜人民访华代表团赠送蒙族人民贵重的礼物哈达、银碗、银筷、民族服装及蒙文版毛泽东选集等礼品。
