The Emerging Industrial City - Huangshi

With the development of the motherland's economic construction, a brand-new industrial city - Huangshi, on the right bank of the middle reaches of the Yangtze River, is growing at an amazing speed. With the development of industry, the urban area is becoming more and more beautiful. Now, six wide avenues stretching in all directions have been built here, of which Huangshi Avenue is as wide as 30 meters. Beautiful new buildings such as workers' dormitories, theaters, hospitals, schools, bookstores, and state-owned stores have also appeared in this new city.

### 新兴的工业城市——黄石市（图片）
柯善文　严震南
1954-04-19
第2版()
专栏：

    新兴的工业城市——黄石市
    随着祖国经济建设的发展，长江中游的右岸，一座崭新的工业城市——黄石市，以惊人的速度成长起来。随着工业的发展，市区日益美丽。现在这里已建成了六条四通八达的宽阔大道，其中的黄石大道宽达三十公尺。工人宿舍、剧院、医院、学校、书店、和国营商店等漂亮的新建筑物也都在这座新城市里出现了。
