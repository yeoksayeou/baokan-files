Title: General Headquarters of the Vietnam People's Army Issues Communiqué on Dien Bien Phu Victory: 5,000 Enemy Troops Eliminated in Two Offensives
Author:
Date: 1954-04-16
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, April 15th: According to a Vietnam News Agency dispatch on April 14th: The General Headquarters of the Vietnam People's Army issued a communiqué on April 12th regarding the battle of Dien Bien Phu, stating: The People's Army launched its first offensive against the French forces at Dien Bien Phu in mid-March, completely destroying the northern defense line of the French army's Dien Bien Phu fortified group. Subsequently, the People's Army continued to intensify the siege, seriously threatening the enemy's airfield and repelling multiple enemy incursions. By the end of March, the People's Army launched its second offensive against the enemy forces at Dien Bien Phu, removing four hills east of Muong Thanh and two strongholds west of the airfield.

The communiqué stated: According to incomplete statistics, the People's Army eliminated 5,000 enemy troops in these two offensives, accounting for approximately two-fifths of the total enemy force at Dien Bien Phu (the original enemy force at Dien Bien Phu was 12,000). The majority of these eliminated enemy troops were European and African soldiers.

In addition, the People's Army shot down and destroyed a total of 50 enemy aircraft, destroyed 5 American-made tanks, and destroyed 7 gasoline and ammunition depots, and seized a large quantity of weapons and ammunition.

The communiqué concluded by saying that since the People's Army began its offensive, the enemy has dispatched two additional paratrooper battalions to reinforce the Dien Bien Phu garrison.

Xinhua News Agency, April 15th: According to a Vietnam News Agency dispatch on April 14th: On April 4th, the Vietnamese People's Armed Forces, two kilometers west of Hai Duong city, the capital of Hai Duong province, used landmines to destroy a French military train traveling on the Hai Phong-Hanoi railway line, including two carriages full of weapons, ammunition, and troops. On the 6th, the People's Armed Forces entered a station three kilometers west of Hai Duong, destroying one enemy locomotive and six carriages full of weapons and troops.

### 越南人民军总司令部发表奠边府战绩公报  在两次攻势中共歼灭敌军五千人

1954-04-16
第1版()
专栏：

    越南人民军总司令部发表奠边府战绩公报
    在两次攻势中共歼灭敌军五千人
    【新华社十五日讯】据越南通讯社十四日讯：越南人民军总司令部十二日就奠边府的战斗发表公报说：人民军在三月中旬对奠边府法军发动第一次攻势，完全摧毁了法军奠边府集团据点的北部防线。以后，人民军继续加紧围困，严重的威胁着敌军的机场，并击退了多次出犯的敌军。到三月底，人民军对奠边府敌军发动了第二次攻势，拔除了芒清以东敌军的四个小丘以及机场以西的两处据点。
    公报说：据不完全的统计，人民军在这两次攻势中共歼灭敌军五千人，约占奠边府全部敌军的五分之二（奠边府原有敌军一万二千人）。这些被歼的敌军大部是欧非籍军队。
    此外，人民军共击落和击毁敌军飞机五十架，击毁美制坦克五辆，击毁汽油库和弹药库七座，并缴获大批武器和弹药。
    公报最后说，自从人民军开始发动攻势以来，敌人已经加派两个伞兵营增援奠边府守军。
    【新华社十五日讯】据越南通讯社十四日讯：四月四日，越南人民武装在海阳省省会海阳以西二公里处，用地雷炸毁行驶在海防—河内铁路线上的法军火车一列，其中有满载武器、弹药和军队的车厢两节。六日，人民武装进入位于海阳以西三公里处的一个车站，击毁敌军机车一辆、满载武器和军队的车厢六节。
