Title: Neutral Nations Supervisory Commission Polish Member General Krzemień Passes Through Beijing En Route to Korea
Author:
Date: 1954-04-13
Page: 1

    Neutral Nations Supervisory Commission Polish Member
    General Krzemień Passes Through Beijing En Route to Korea
    [Xinhua News Agency, April 12th] General Krzemień (formerly transliterated as Le. Ke'erzhi'aier), the Polish member of the Neutral Nations Supervisory Commission, arrived in Beijing from Warsaw by plane on the 9th and departed for Korea on the 12th.
    Those who welcomed and saw him off included: Jiang Keding, Acting Director of the Foreign Liaison Department of the People's Revolutionary Military Commission, and Wang Tuo, Deputy Director of the Reception Department of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs.
    Kiryluk, the Ambassador of the Polish People's Republic to China, and Li Zhongyi, Counselor of the Embassy of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea, also welcomed and saw him off.

### 中立国监察委员会波兰委员  克谢敏将军过北京去朝鲜

1954-04-13
第1版()
专栏：

    中立国监察委员会波兰委员
    克谢敏将军过北京去朝鲜
    【新华社十二日讯】中立国监察委员会波兰委员克谢敏（前译勒·克尔泽米埃尔）将军已在九日从华沙乘飞机到达北京，十二日动身去朝鲜。
    前往迎、送的有：人民革命军事委员会对外联络处代处长蒋克定，外交部交际处副处长王拓等。
    波兰人民共和国驻华大使基里洛克和朝鲜民主主义人民共和国大使馆参赞李忠翼等也前往迎、送。
