Title: Central Meteorological Observatory Forecasts Frost in Areas Affected by Strong Winds
Author:
Date: 1954-04-19
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, April 18th. The Central Meteorological Observatory issued a strong wind advisory at 8:00 PM on April 18th: It is expected that in the next 24 hours, the central and southern parts of Inner Mongolia Autonomous Region, the Northeast Plain, Rehe Province, North China, the middle and lower reaches of the Huai River, the areas around Dongting Lake and Poyang Lake, and the sea near the mouth of the Yangtze River will experience northerly winds of around force 6. The Bohai Sea and Yellow Sea will experience northerly winds of around force 8. It is predicted that in the areas affected by the strong winds, except for the areas around Dongting Lake and Poyang Lake, the minimum temperature will drop by about 10 degrees Celsius within 48 hours, and there will be frost after the wind stops.

### 中央气象台预告  大风所到地区将有霜冻

1954-04-19
第1版()
专栏：

    中央气象台预告
    大风所到地区将有霜冻
    【新华社十八日讯】中央气象台四月十八日下午八时发布大风消息：预计未来二十四小时内，内蒙古自治区的中部和南部、东北平原、热河省、华北地区、淮河中下游、洞庭湖和鄱阳湖四周的地区、长江口附近的海面，有六级左右的偏北风，渤海海面、黄海海面有八级左右的偏北风。预计大风所到地区，除洞庭湖和鄱阳湖四周地区外，四十八小时内最低气温将降低摄氏十度左右，风停以后，将有霜冻。
