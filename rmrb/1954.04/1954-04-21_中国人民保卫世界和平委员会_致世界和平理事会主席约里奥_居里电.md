Title: Chinese People's Committee for World Peace Sends Telegram to Frédéric Joliot-Curie, Chairman of the World Peace Council
Author:
Date: 1954-04-21
Page: 1

    Chinese People's Committee for World Peace
    Sends Telegram to Frédéric Joliot-Curie, Chairman of the World Peace Council

    To Mr. Frédéric Joliot-Curie, Chairman of the World Peace Council:

    On the occasion of the fifth anniversary of the World Peace Movement, the Chinese People's Committee for World Peace, on behalf of the entire Chinese people, extends warm congratulations to you and the World Peace Council under your leadership. We also express our sincere respect for your significant efforts in the cause of defending peace.

    The Chinese People's Committee for World Peace and the entire Chinese people will always support your and the World Peace Council's call, devote our loyalty to the cause of peace, and tirelessly strive to expand and consolidate the unity of the people of Asia and the world, and to achieve further relaxation of international tensions.

    Chinese People's Committee for World Peace
    April 20, 1954

### 中国人民保卫世界和平委员会  致世界和平理事会主席约里奥—居里电

1954-04-21
第1版()
专栏：

    中国人民保卫世界和平委员会
    致世界和平理事会主席约里奥—居里电世界和平理事会主席约里奥—居里先生：
    当此世界和平运动五周年纪念日，中国人民保卫世界和平委员会特代表全中国人民向您及您领导的世界和平理事会，致以热烈祝贺，并对您在保卫和平事业中所作的重大努力，表示诚恳的敬意。
    中国人民保卫世界和平委员会和全体中国人民将永远支持您及世界和平理事会的号召，为和平事业而竭其忠诚，并将扩大和巩固亚洲与全世界的人民的团结、为争取国际紧张局势的进一步缓和而努力不懈。
    中国人民保卫世界和平委员会    一九五四年四月二十日
