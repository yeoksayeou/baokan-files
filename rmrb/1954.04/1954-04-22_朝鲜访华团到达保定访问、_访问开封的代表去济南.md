Title: Korean Delegation Visits Baoding, Representatives Visiting Kaifeng Go to Jinan
Author:
Date: 1954-04-22
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, Baoding, April 21st: The head of the Korean People's Delegation to China, Kim Ung-gi, and the deputy head, Kim Tal-hyon, leading some members of the delegation and the art troupe accompanying the delegation, arrived in Baoding on a special train on the morning of the 21st, accompanied by Ding Xilin, Deputy Minister of Culture of the Central People's Government of China.

Xu Zhe, Counselor of the Embassy of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea in China, also accompanied them.

The distinguished guests were warmly welcomed by more than three thousand people at the station. More than one hundred people, including the heads of the Hebei Provincial Committee of the Chinese Communist Party, the Provincial People's Government, the Hebei Military Region of the People's Liberation Army, and various people's organizations, came to the station to welcome them. Geng Changsuo, the famous labor model who first established agricultural production cooperatives in our country, and Gao Guizhen, a model of supporting the army and giving preferential treatment to families of soldiers and martyrs who participated in the third delegation to Korea to comfort the troops, and family members of volunteer army heroes, also came from afar to greet them.

A grand welcoming rally was held in the station square, which was decorated with the flags of Korea and China and colorful flags. Ma Guorui, Vice Chairman of the Hebei Provincial People's Representative Conference Consultative Committee, delivered a speech expressing the warm welcome of the people of the province to the distinguished guests. Head of Delegation Kim Ung-gi delivered a speech of thanks.

According to Xinhua News Agency: Some members of the Korean People's Delegation to China, headed by Deputy Heads Li Yong-ho and Li Dong-young, and the Korean People's Army Orchestra, after a two-day visit to Kaifeng, took a special train to Jinan at 11 p.m. on the 20th.

### 朝鲜访华团到达保定访问、  访问开封的代表去济南

1954-04-22
第1版()
专栏：

    朝鲜访华团到达保定访问、
    访问开封的代表去济南
    【新华社保定二十一日电】朝鲜人民访华代表团团长金应基和副团长金达铉率领部分团员和随代表团前来的艺术团人员，在我中央人民政府文化部副部长丁西林陪同下，于二十一日上午乘专车到保定访问。
    朝鲜民主主义人民共和国驻我国大使馆参赞徐哲也陪同前来。
    贵宾们在车站受到三千多人的热烈欢迎。中共河北省委员会、省人民政府、人民解放军河北军区及各人民团体的负责人一百余人都到车站欢迎。我国最先创办农业生产合作社的著名劳动模范耿长锁和参加第三届赴朝慰问团的拥军优属模范、志愿军功臣家属高桂珍也远道赶来迎接。
    隆重的欢迎大会在悬挂着朝、中国旗和彩旗的车站广场上举行。河北省各界人民代表会议协商委员会副主席马国瑞致词表示全省人民对贵宾的热烈欢迎，金应基团长致词表示感谢。
    据新华社讯：以副团长李永镐和李东英为首的朝鲜人民访华代表团部分团员和朝鲜人民军协奏团，在开封进行了两天的访问后，已于二十日晚上十一时乘专车赴济南。
