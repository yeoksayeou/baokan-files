Title: Declaration of the Standing Committee of the World Peace Council on Atomic Weapons
Author:
Date: 1954-04-03
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, Vienna, 2nd: TASS reported from Vienna on the 1st that the Standing Committee of the World Peace Council meeting passed a declaration on atomic weapons.
The declaration said: The demand to prohibit atomic weapons, put forward in the Stockholm Appeal, which is supported by hundreds of millions of men and women, has today become the demand of the people of all countries.
The infinite power of scientific discovery is not intended to destroy mankind and instantly destroy thousands of years of human labor, but to find ways to relieve mankind's current suffering and help mankind achieve a better life.
Declaring atomic warfare illegal is not only necessary, but also possible. This can be achieved by concluding international agreements to ban all kinds of radioactive weapons and radioactive poisons. An international system of investigation and supervision should and can be established.
The people of all countries must immediately demand that their governments conclude agreements prohibiting all kinds of weapons of mass destruction.

### 世界和平理事会常委会关于原子武器的宣言

1954-04-03
第1版()
专栏：

    世界和平理事会常委会关于原子武器的宣言
    【新华社二日讯】据塔斯社维也纳一日讯：世界和平理事会常务委员会会议通过了关于原子武器的宣言。
    宣言说：受到亿万男女拥护的斯德哥尔摩宣言中提出的禁止原子武器的要求，今天已经成为各国人民的要求。
    科学发现无穷无尽的力量，不是为了要毁灭人类以及转瞬间毁灭人类几千年的劳动成果，而是为了要找到解除人类目前的痛苦，和帮助人类获得更美满的生活的方法。
    宣布原子战争为非法，不仅是必要的，而且是可能的。这可以通过缔结国际协定禁止各种放射性武器和放射性毒物的办法来实现。一种国际调查和监督的制度，是应该而且可以建立的。
    各国人民必须立刻要求他们的政府缔结协定，禁止各种大规模毁灭性的武器。
