Title: The Soviet Union and Hungary Sign 1954 Barter Protocol; Romania Signs Trade and Payment Agreements with Denmark and Turkey Respectively
Author:
Date: 1954-04-19
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency dispatch: Following friendly negotiations, the Soviet Ministry of Foreign Trade and the Hungarian trade delegation signed the 1954 Soviet-Hungarian Barter Protocol in Moscow on the 7th.

    The exchange of goods stipulated in the protocol exceeds both the exchange volume stipulated for this year in the long-term trade agreement and the trade volume of 1953. The Soviet Union will supply Hungary with cotton, coke, iron ore, ferroalloys, timber, fertilizers, industrial equipment, agricultural machinery, automobiles, ball bearings, and other goods needed by the Hungarian national economy. Hungary will supply the Soviet Union with seagoing and inland river vessels, hoisting and transport equipment, industrial equipment, hogs, textiles, and other daily necessities.

    The People's Republic of Romania and Denmark recently signed a trade and payment agreement. According to this agreement, Denmark will export fish, condensed milk, lard, margarine, seeds, medicines, cotton yarn, and other goods to Romania. The People's Republic of Romania will export tractors, chemicals, construction timber, petroleum products, medicinal plants, glass, glassware, and some agricultural products to Denmark.

    Representatives of the People's Republic of Romania and Turkey signed a Romanian-Turkish trade and payment agreement in Ankara on April 5. According to the agreement, Turkey will export fish, olives, citrus fruits, rice, seeds, cotton, flax, light leather, and raw materials needed for the tanning industry to Romania. Romania will export tractors and agricultural machinery, industrial equipment (including machine tools), chemicals, petroleum, timber, woodenware, paper, cement, window glass, and glassware, etc., to Turkey.

### 苏联和匈牙利签订一九五四年换货议定书  罗马尼亚分别和丹麦土耳其签订贸易及付款协定

1954-04-19
第4版()
专栏：

    苏联和匈牙利签订一九五四年换货议定书
    罗马尼亚分别和丹麦土耳其签订贸易及付款协定
    据新华社讯：苏联对外贸易部和匈牙利贸易代表团在进行了友好的谈判后，于七日在莫斯科签订了一九五四年苏匈换货议定书。
    议定书规定的换货额比长期贸易协定为今年规定的换货额和一九五三年的贸易额都大。苏联将供给匈牙利以棉花、焦炭、铁矿砂、铁合金、木材、肥料、工业设备、农业机器、汽车、球轴承和匈牙利国民经济所需要的其他货品。匈牙利将供给苏联以航海船只和内河船只、起重设备和运输设备、工业设备、毛猪、纺织品和其他日用品。
    罗马尼亚人民共和国和丹麦最近签订了贸易及付款协定。根据这项协定，丹麦将向罗马尼亚输出鱼、炼乳、猪油、人造牛油、种籽、药品、棉纱和其他货品。罗马尼亚人民共和国将向丹麦输出拖拉机、化学品、建筑木材、石油产品、药用植物、玻璃、玻璃器具和一些农产品。
    罗马尼亚人民共和国和土耳其的代表于四月五日在安卡拉签订了罗土贸易及付款协定。根据协定规定：土耳其将向罗马尼亚输出鱼、橄榄、柑橘、大米、种籽、棉花、亚麻、轻皮和制革工业所需的原料。罗马尼亚将向土耳其输出拖拉机和农业机器、工业设备（包括工作母机）、化学制品、石油、木材、木器、纸、水泥、窗户玻璃和玻璃器具等。
