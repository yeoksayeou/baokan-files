Title: Japanese Workers Set "May Day" Central Slogan: Oppose the U.S.-Japan "Mutual Defense Assistance Agreement"
Author:
Date: 1954-04-06
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Tokyo, 5th. News: The Central Unified "May Day" Labor Day Conference Executive Committee, formed by Japanese labor union organizations to celebrate the 1954 "May Day" Labor Day, decided on the 1st to make opposing the U.S.-Japan "Mutual Defense Assistance Agreement" and rearmament the central slogan of this year's "May Day." Other slogans decided by the Executive Committee included defending freedom and democracy, raising wages, opposing heavy taxes, and establishing a minimum wage system.

### 日本工人定出“五一”中心口号  反对美日“共同防御援助协定”

1954-04-06
第4版()
专栏：

    日本工人定出“五一”中心口号
    反对美日“共同防御援助协定”
    【新华社五日讯】东京消息：日本各工会组织为庆祝一九五四年“五一”劳动节而组成的中央统一“五一”劳动节大会执行委员会，在一日决定以反对美日“共同防御援助协定”和重整军备为今年“五一”节的中心口号。在执行委员会决定的其他口号中，包括保卫自由和民主、提高工资、反对重税、建立最低工资制。
