Title: Vietnamese People's Armed Forces Destroy Enemy Train Bound for Hanoi

Author: 
Date: 1954-04-25
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, April 24th: According to Vietnam News Agency on April 23rd, the Vietnamese People's Armed Forces recently used landmines to destroy a French military train traveling on the railway line from Haiphong to Hanoi, twelve kilometers east of the capital of Hai Duong Province. It was loaded with weapons and ammunition supplied by the United States to the French colonialists.

### 越南人民武装  炸毁驶往河内的敌火车一列

1954-04-25
第1版()
专栏：

    越南人民武装
    炸毁驶往河内的敌火车一列
    【新华社二十四日讯】据越南通讯社二十三日讯：越南人民武装最近在距离海阳省省会以东十二公里的地方，用地雷炸毁行驶在海防到河内的铁路线上的法军火车一列，其中满载美国供给法国殖民者的武器和弹药。
