Title: Chinese Delegation Arrives in Geneva; Foreign Minister Zhou Enlai Issues Written Statement at Airport
Author: 
Date: 1954-04-26
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Geneva, April 25th: The Chief Representative of the People's Republic of China Delegation to the Geneva Conference, Foreign Minister Zhou Enlai, along with delegation representatives, Vice Foreign Ministers Zhang Wentian, Wang Jiaxiang, Li Kenong, and delegation advisors, arrived in Geneva by plane on the 24th at 3:30 PM local time.

Those welcoming at the airport included: Alfred Zinder, Director of the Political Affairs Division of the Swiss Federal Government's Political Department; Robert Morris, Chief of Protocol of the Political Department; Charles Duboulet, President of the Geneva State Government; Albert Dussaix, Deputy Mayor of Geneva; and other government officials.

Also welcoming at the airport was Feodor F. Molochkov, the Soviet Union's Envoy to Switzerland. Kuznetsov, the Soviet Union's Delegation Representative to the Geneva Conference and Deputy Foreign Minister, who arrived in Geneva on the 23rd, and Yudin, the Ambassador to China, were also present at the airport to greet them.

Also welcoming at the airport were: Nam Il, Foreign Minister and Chief Representative of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea delegation, who arrived in Geneva on the 23rd, along with representatives Baek Nam-un, Ki Seok-bok, and advisor Jang Chun-san; Czechoslovak Envoy to Switzerland, Svoboda; Hungarian Envoy to Switzerland, Kutash; First Secretary of the Polish Embassy in Switzerland, Kaspisiak; Chargé d'Affaires of the Romanian Embassy in Switzerland, Stoffel; and Chargé d'Affaires of the Bulgarian Embassy in Switzerland, Ivanov.

Also welcoming at the airport were Feng Xuan, the People's Republic of China's Envoy to Switzerland, Wen Pengjiu, Consul General in Geneva, and all staff members of the embassy and consulate. Wang Bingnan, Secretary-General of the Chinese delegation, and delegation advisors, who arrived in Geneva some days ago, were also present at the airport to greet them. Foreign Minister Zhou Enlai shook hands with the welcoming personnel after disembarking from the plane.

Foreign Minister Zhou Enlai issued the following written statement at the airport:

    The Geneva Conference is about to be held. This conference will discuss the peaceful settlement of the Korean issue and the restoration of peace in Indochina. If these two pressing Asian issues can be resolved, it will be conducive to safeguarding peace in Asia and further easing international tensions.

    Peace-loving people and nations around the world will closely watch the progress of the Geneva Conference and eagerly hope for its success. The Chinese people have the same expectations for this conference.

    The delegation of the People's Republic of China is participating in this conference with sincerity. We believe that the joint efforts of the participants and the common desire to consolidate peace will provide the possibility of resolving the aforementioned pressing Asian issues.

    I would like to take this opportunity to extend my greetings to the citizens of Geneva and the entire Swiss people!

Xinhua News Agency, Berlin, April 25th: The delegation of the People's Republic of China to the Geneva Conference, led by Chief Representative, Foreign Minister Zhou Enlai, flew to Geneva via Berlin on the morning of the 24th.

Those welcoming at the airport included: Prime Minister Grotewohl of the German Democratic Republic, Deputy Prime Minister and Foreign Minister Bolz, Deputy Prime Minister Schultz, Politburo member of the Central Committee of the Socialist Unity Party of Germany Oesner, Central Committee Secretary Wandel, Mayor of Greater Berlin Ebert, Minister of Education Labus, Minister of Light Industry Feldmann, Minister of Justice Benjamin, State Secretary of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs Hanke, Director of the Government Office Geyer, Director of the President's Private Office Wünsche, and other officials.

Also welcoming at the airport were the Ambassador Extraordinary and Plenipotentiary of the Soviet Union to the German Democratic Republic, Soviet High Commissioner to Germany Semyonov, Deputy Commissioner Miroshnichenko and other Soviet officials stationed in Germany.

Also present to welcome were: Ji Pengfei, Ambassador of the People's Republic of China to the German Democratic Republic and all diplomatic personnel of the embassy, Hungarian Ambassador Sáfárkó, Bulgarian Ambassador Kostov, Albanian Minister Lulo, Chargé d'Affaires of the Polish Embassy Daodin, Chargé d'Affaires of the Czechoslovak Embassy Lafargue, Chargé d'Affaires of the Romanian Embassy Mantu, and Commercial Representative of Finland to the German Democratic Republic, Hekila, etc.

After disembarking from the plane, Foreign Minister Zhou Enlai had breakfast with Prime Minister Grotewohl, senior government officials, and diplomatic envoys from various countries. The delegation left Berlin for Geneva by plane at 11:30 a.m.

### 我国代表团到达日内瓦  周恩来外长在机场发表书面声明

1954-04-26
第1版()
专栏：

    我国代表团到达日内瓦
    周恩来外长在机场发表书面声明
    【新华社日内瓦二十五日电】中华人民共和国出席日内瓦会议代表团首席代表、外交部部长周恩来，偕代表团代表、外交部副部长张闻天、王稼祥、李克农和代表团顾问等一行，在二十四日当地时间下午三时半乘飞机到达日内瓦。
    在机场欢迎的有：瑞士联邦政府政治部政务司司长艾尔弗雷德·琴德尔，政治部礼宾处处长罗伯·莫里斯，日内瓦州政府主席查尔斯·杜博莱，日内瓦副市长艾伯特·杜梭，以及其他政府官员等。
    在机场欢迎的还有：苏联驻瑞士公使费奥多·弗·莫洛奇科夫。二十三日抵日内瓦的苏联出席日内瓦会议代表团代表、外交部副部长库兹涅佐夫和驻华大使尤金等也在机场迎接。
    在机场欢迎的还有：二十三日抵日内瓦的朝鲜民主主义人民共和国代表团首席代表南日外务相，代表白南云、奇石福和顾问张春山，捷克斯洛伐克驻瑞士公使斯沃波达，匈牙利驻瑞士公使库塔什，波兰驻瑞士公使馆一等秘书卡斯皮夏克，罗马尼亚驻瑞士使馆代办斯托斐尔，保加利亚驻瑞士使馆代办伊凡诺夫等。
    在机场欢迎的还有中华人民共和国驻瑞士公使冯铉，驻日内瓦总领事温朋久和使领馆全体人员。日前抵日内瓦的中国代表团秘书长王炳南和代表团顾问等也在机场迎接。周恩来外长下机后与欢迎人员一一握手。
    周恩来外长在机场发表书面声明如下：
    日内瓦会议就要举行了。这个会议将要讨论和平解决朝鲜问题和恢复印度支那和平问题。亚洲这两个迫切的问题，如果能够获得解决，将有利于保障亚洲的和平，并进一步缓和国际的紧张局势。
    全世界爱好和平的人民和国家都将密切地注视着日内瓦会议的进展，并热烈地期望着会议的成功。中国人民对于这个会议有着同样的期待。
    中华人民共和国代表团抱着诚意来参加这个会议。我们相信，参加会议者的共同努力和对于巩固和平的共同愿望，将会提供解决上述亚洲迫切问题的可能。
    我愿意趁着这个机会向日内瓦市民及全体瑞士人民致意！
    【新华社柏林二十五日电】出席日内瓦会议的中华人民共和国代表团，在首席代表、外交部部长周恩来率领下，在二十四日上午乘飞机经柏林转赴日内瓦。
    到机场欢迎的有：德意志民主共和国总理格罗提渥、副总理兼外交部长博尔茨、副总理舒尔茨、德国统一社会党中央委员会政治局委员厄斯纳、中央委员会书记汪戴尔、大柏林市长艾柏特、教育部长拉布斯、轻工业部长费德曼、司法部长本雅明、外交部国务秘书韩克、政府办公厅主任盖耶、总统私人办公厅主任温舍以及其他官员。
    到机场欢迎的还有苏联驻德意志民主共和国特命全权大使、苏联驻德高级专员谢麦诺夫、副专员米罗什尼钦科等苏联驻德官员。
    前往欢迎的还有：中华人民共和国驻德意志民主共和国大使姬鹏飞和大使馆全体外交人员、匈牙利大使夏法朗柯、保加利亚大使科斯托夫、阿尔巴尼亚公使卢洛、波兰使馆代办道定、捷克斯洛伐克使馆代办拉法格、罗马尼亚使馆代办曼图及芬兰驻德意志民主共和国商务代表赫基拉等。
    周恩来外长下飞机后和格罗提渥总理、政府高级官员及各国外交使节等共进早餐。代表团在上午十一时半乘飞机离柏林赴日内瓦。
