Title: Weather Bureau Forecasts Gale Warnings
Author:
Date: 1954-04-16
Page: 1
    Xinhua News Agency, 15th. The Central Weather Bureau issued a gale warning at 8:00 PM on the 15th: It is expected that tonight and tomorrow, the central part of the Inner Mongolia Autonomous Region and the western half of Rehe Province will still have northerly winds of about force six. It is expected that tomorrow the Northeast Plain and the eastern half of Rehe Province will also have gales of about force six. The wind direction will change from southerly to northerly.

### 气象台预告大风消息

1954-04-16
第1版()
专栏：

    气象台预告大风消息
    【新华社十五日讯】中央气象台十五日下午八时发布大风消息：预计今夜及明日，内蒙古自治区中部、热河省西半部仍将有六级左右的偏北风。预计明日东北平原及热河省东半部也将有六级左右的大风。风向偏南转偏北。
