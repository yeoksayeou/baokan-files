Our Great Motherland
In order to expand production and support national industrial construction, the peasants of Sanjuzi Village, San District, Chengde County, Rehe Province, are preparing to open up 150 mu of paddy fields on the bank of the Lao Niu River in the district, which can increase grain production by 130,000 jin. This is a survey of the terrain by agricultural science surveyors and district and village cadres in Chengde County.

### 我们伟大的祖国（图片）

1954-04-03
第1版()
专栏：

    我们伟大的祖国
    热河省承德县三区三咀子村农民为了扩大生产，支援国家工业建设，准备在该区老牛河岸开辟稻田一百五十亩，可以增加十三万斤粮食。这是承德县农业科测量人员和区、村干部在测量地形。
