Title: Japanese People Actively Prepare to Participate in the World Peace Assembly
Author:
Date: 1954-04-25
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency report:

    People throughout Japan are holding peace rallies, demanding the easing of international tensions, and are actively preparing to participate in the World Peace Assembly proposed by the World Peace Council to be held this year.
    The All-Hokkaido Peace Congress was held in Sapporo on the 10th and 11th of this month. 1,500 representatives from all over Hokkaido participated in the congress. The representatives reported on the development of the peace movement in various places, especially the movement to ban the use of atomic weapons. These reports showed that the people of Hokkaido, even housewives and children, are dedicating their efforts to fight for peace. A girl in Asahikawa City independently collected 1,500 signatures calling for a ban on the use of atomic weapons. After the representatives finished their reports, the congress enthusiastically discussed the issues of easing international tensions and supporting the World Peace Assembly, and elected the All-Hokkaido representatives to the Japanese delegation to the World Peace Assembly.
    In Kyoto, Honshu, under the call of World Peace Council members Ikuo Oyama, and Kinkazu Saionji, activities to prepare for participation in the World Peace Assembly have also been widely launched, and preparations are being made to hold the Western Japan Peace Assembly in May. Currently, organizations participating in the preparatory work include the Kyoto Regional Council of the General Council of Trade Unions of Japan, the Kyoto Regional Headquarters of the National Railway Workers' Union, the Kyoto Federation of the Japan-China Friendship Association, the Japan-Soviet Union Friendship Association, the Federation of Women's Groups, and the Kaijin Association, among many other groups.
    Osaka, Yokohama, Chiba, Hyogo, and other places are also actively carrying out diverse activities to defend peace and ease international tensions, such as holding peace rallies and collecting signatures to ban atomic weapons.

### 日本人民积极筹备参加世界性和平集会

1954-04-25
第4版()
专栏：

    日本人民积极筹备参加世界性和平集会
    据新华社讯：日本全国各地人民纷纷举行和平集会，要求缓和国际紧张局势，并积极准备参加由世界和平理事会建议在今年举行的世界性和平集会。
    全北海道和平大会已于十日、十一日两天在札幌市举行。参加大会的有全北海道各地代表一千五百人。代表们报告了各地和平运动，特别是要求禁止使用原子武器的运动的发展情况。这些报告表明：北海道各地人民、即使是家庭妇女和儿童也都在为争取和平而献出自己的力量。旭川市有一个女孩子独自征集了一千五百个要求禁止使用原子武器的签名。大会在代表报告完毕后，热烈地讨论了缓和国际紧张局势和拥护世界性和平集会的问题，并选出了全北海道出席世界性和平集会的日本代表。
    在本州的京都，在世界和平理事会理事大山郁夫、西园寺公一等人的号召下，也广泛地展开了筹备参加世界性和平集会的活动，并准备在五月举行西部日本和平集会。现在参加筹备工作的有日本工会总评议会京都地方评议会、国营铁道工会京都地方本部、日中友好协会京都联合会、日苏亲善协会、妇女团体联合会及海神会等许多团体。
    大阪、横滨、千叶、兵库等地也都在积极展开举行和平集会、禁止原子武器的签名等多种多样性的保卫和平与缓和国际紧张局势的活动。
