Title: Weather Forecast Predicts Strong Winds
Author:
Date: 1954-04-18
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, 17th: The Central Meteorological Observatory issued a strong wind advisory at 8:00 PM on the 17th: It is expected that on the night of the 17th and the 18th, the southern and central parts of the Inner Mongolia Autonomous Region, northern North China, Rehe Province, Shandong Peninsula, the lower reaches of the Yellow River, the Bohai Sea and its coastal areas, and the Yellow Sea will still experience northerly winds of around force six. It is expected that on the 18th, the north bank of the Huai River and the East China Sea will also experience northerly winds of around force six.

### 气象台预告大风消息

1954-04-18
第1版()
专栏：

    气象台预告大风消息
    【新华社十七日讯】中央气象台十七日下午八时发布大风消息：预计十七日夜和十八日，内蒙古自治区的南部和中部、华北北部、热河省、山东半岛、黄河下游、渤海海面和沿岸、黄海海面，仍将有六级左右的偏北风。预计十八日淮河北岸和东海海面，也将有六级左右的偏北风。
