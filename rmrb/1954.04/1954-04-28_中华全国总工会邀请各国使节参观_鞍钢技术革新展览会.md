Title: All-China Federation of Trade Unions Invites Foreign Envoys to Visit "Anshan Iron and Steel Works Technological Innovation Exhibition"
Author:
Date: 1954-04-28
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, April 27th: The All-China Federation of Trade Unions invited envoys from various countries to China to visit the "Anshan Iron and Steel Works Technological Innovation Exhibition" at 3:00 PM on the 27th.

    Prior to the visit, Liu Ningyi, Vice-Chairman of the All-China Federation of Trade Unions, extended a warm welcome to the guests. Dong Xin, Secretary of the Secretariat of the All-China Federation of Trade Unions, introduced the main content of the exhibition. The guests, accompanied by Liu Ningyi, Dong Xin, and others, visited various sections of the exhibition.

    In addition, Han Xueye, head of the Asian and Pacific Regions Peace Liaison Committee and Chairman of the Korean National Committee for the Defense of Peace, and the “Pravda” correspondent in Beijing, and many others, also visited the exhibition at the same time.

### 中华全国总工会邀请各国使节参观“鞍钢技术革新展览会”

1954-04-28
第1版()
专栏：

    中华全国总工会
    邀请各国使节参观“鞍钢技术革新展览会”
    【新华社二十七日讯】中华全国总工会在二十七日下午三时邀请各国驻华使节参观了“鞍钢技术革新展览会”。
    参观前，中华全国总工会副主席刘宁一向来宾们表示了热烈的欢迎。中华全国总工会书记处书记董昕介绍了展览会的主要内容。来宾们在刘宁一、董昕等陪同下，参观了展览会的各个部分。
    又：亚洲及太平洋区域和平联络委员会负责人，朝鲜拥护和平全国民族委员会委员长韩雪野及苏联“真理报”驻北京记者等多人，也于同时参观了展览会。
