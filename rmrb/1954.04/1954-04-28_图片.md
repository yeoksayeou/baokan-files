Author: Ke Shanwen
Date: 1954-04-28
Page: 2

The Hanyang Sleeper Anti-Corrosion Plant of the Ministry of Railways of the Central People's Government will have all its main machinery and equipment installed by April this year, and will strive to put it into trial production on the "May Day" holiday. The picture shows workers carrying out the final inspection of the huge sleeper-making pot in the steaming room to ensure a successful test run on "May Day".
