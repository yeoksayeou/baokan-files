Title: Hungarian Mathematician Pál Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1954-04-29
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency: Hungarian mathematician Dúlán Pál, Academician of the Hungarian Academy of Sciences, who was invited to China for academic visits according to the Sino-Hungarian Cultural Cooperation Agreement, arrived in Beijing by train on the 27th. Dúlán Pál has written seventy works and has twice won the Kossuth Prize.

### 匈牙利数学家巴尔到京

1954-04-29
第4版()
专栏：

    匈牙利数学家巴尔到京
    据新华社讯：根据中匈文化合作协定应邀来我国作学术访问的匈牙利数学家杜澜·巴尔于二十七日乘火车到达北京。杜澜·巴尔是匈牙利科学院院士，有著作七十种，曾两度获得科苏特奖金。
