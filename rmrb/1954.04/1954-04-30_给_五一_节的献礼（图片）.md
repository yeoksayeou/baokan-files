    Gifts for the "May Day" Festival
    The newly installed generator at the Tianjin No. 1 Power Plant will generate electricity on "May Day." Here, workers are inspecting the installation quality of the generator's speed control system to ensure safe power transmission during generation.
    Xinhua News Agency reporter Yue Guofang photo
    Workers at the Xi'an No. 2 Power Plant are engaged in a safety production competition to welcome the "May Day" International Labor Day. Shi Jinduo, leader of the third shift in the boiler workshop and a Communist Party member, is discussing and establishing a competition plan with his team members. Xinhua News Agency reporter Bai Ying photo
    Workers at the Refractory Materials Factory of Benxi Iron and Steel Company modified four friction presses before "May Day". This machine replaces the manual production process of refractory brick forming and basically eliminates the phenomenon of refractory brick section cracks. This is the scene of workers producing refractory bricks.
    Xinhua News Agency reporter Jia Chengbin photo
    The average doffing downtime of the spinning workshop of the No. 2 Spinning Department of the state-owned Shanghai No. 2 Cotton Textile Factory was about 40 seconds last year. Now, in order to increase production, the Yi Shijuan group has proposed that the average downtime per machine per stop should not exceed 35 seconds. This is the scene of Yi Shijuan doffing.
    Xinhua News Agency reporter Chen Zhiping photo

### 给“五一”节的献礼（图片）

1954-04-30
第2版()
专栏：

    给“五一”节的献礼
    天津第一发电厂新安装好的发电机将在“五一”发电。这是工人们正在检查发电机调速系统的安装质量，以保证发电时安全送电。
    新华社记者　岳国芳摄
    西安第二发电厂职工正在展开安全生产竞赛，迎接“五一”国际劳动节。锅炉车间第三班班长、共产党员石金铎正和组员一起讨论订立竞赛计划。　新华社记者　白媖摄
    本溪钢铁公司耐火材料厂的工人在“五一”节前改装了四台摩擦压力机。这个机器代替了用手工生产的工序——耐火砖成型，并且基本上消灭了耐火砖断面裂纹的现象。这是工人在生产耐火砖的情形。
    新华社记者　贾承滨摄
    国营上海第二棉纺织厂二纺部细纱车间去年的落纱停台时间平均四十秒左右。现在，裔式娟小组为了提高产量，提出全组平均每次每台停车时间不超过三十五秒。这是裔式娟正在落纱的情形。　新华社记者　陈之平摄
