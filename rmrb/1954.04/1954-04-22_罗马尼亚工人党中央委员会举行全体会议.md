Title: Romanian Workers' Party Central Committee Holds Plenary Session
Author:
Date: 1954-04-22
Page: 4

    Xinhua News Agency, Bucharest, April 20th: TASS reports that the Central Committee of the Romanian Workers' Party held a plenary session on the 19th. The agenda of the meeting was as follows: 1. The implementation of the resolutions of the August 1953 plenary session of the Central Committee of the Romanian Workers' Party and measures concerning the implementation of the 1954 national plan and national budget; 2. Several issues concerning the Party's organization.

    The plenary session heard and approved the reports made by M. Constantinescu, D. Petrescu, and G. Apostol on the first item on the agenda. The meeting heard and approved the speech made by Gheorghe Gheorghiu-Dej on the second item on the agenda.

    The plenary session resolved: 1. The Secretariat of the Central Committee of the Romanian Workers' Party shall be composed of four persons not holding positions in the government. The Secretariat shall be responsible for the Party's day-to-day work. The work of the Secretariat shall be guided by the Political Bureau of the Central Committee. 2. Adopt Gheorghe Gheorghiu-Dej's proposal to abolish the position of General Secretary of the Party Central Committee and establish the position of First Secretary of the Central Committee.

    The plenary session elected G. Apostol as First Secretary of the Central Committee, and N. Ceaușescu, M. Dalea, and J. Fazekas as Secretaries of the Central Committee. The plenary session elected N. Ceaușescu and A. Drăghici as alternate members of the Political Bureau of the Central Committee, J. Fazekas as a member of the Central Committee, and M. Mujic and M. Roșianu as alternate members of the Central Committee.

    The plenary session decided to convene the Second Congress of the Romanian Workers' Party on October 30th.

### 罗马尼亚工人党中央委员会举行全体会议

1954-04-22
第4版()
专栏：

    罗马尼亚工人党中央委员会举行全体会议
    【新华社二十一日讯】塔斯社布加勒斯特二十日讯：罗马尼亚工人党中央委员会十九日举行全体会议。会议议程如下：一、罗马尼亚工人党中央委员会一九五三年八月全体会议决议的执行情况和关于执行一九五四年的国家计划和国家预算的措施；二、关于党的组织的若干问题。
    全体会议听取并批准了米·康斯坦丁尼斯库、杜·佩特雷斯库和格·阿波斯托尔就第一项议程所作的报告。会议听取并批准了乔治乌—德治就第二项议程所作的发言。
    全体会议决议：一、罗马尼亚工人党中央委员会书记处由不在政府中担任工作的四人组成。书记处负责党的日常工作。书记处的工作由中央委员会政治局指导。二、通过乔治乌—德治关于取消党中央委员会总书记职位和设立中央委员会第一书记职位的建议。
    全体会议选举格·阿波斯托尔为中央委员会第一书记，尼·奇阿乌西斯库、米·达列亚和雅·法泽卡斯为中央委员会书记。全体会议选举尼·奇阿乌西斯库和亚·德勒吉奇为中央委员会政治局候补委员，雅·法泽卡斯为中央委员会委员，米·穆日克和米·罗山亚努为中央委员会候补委员。
    全体会议决定在十月三十日召开罗马尼亚工人党第二次代表大会。
