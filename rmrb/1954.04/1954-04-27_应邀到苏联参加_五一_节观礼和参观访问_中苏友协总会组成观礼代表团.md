Title: In Response to Invitation to Attend "May Day" Parade and Visit the Soviet Union, Sino-Soviet Friendship Association Forms Delegation for Parade Observation

Author:

Date: 1954-04-27
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency: The Sino-Soviet Friendship Association, at the invitation of the Soviet Society for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries, has organized a delegation consisting of representatives from the Sino-Soviet Friendship Association, the People's Liberation Army, women, youth, culture, art, science, politics, and economics to attend the "May Day" parade and visit the Soviet Union. The delegation will also introduce the achievements of New China in political, economic, and cultural construction to the Soviet people.

The Sino-Soviet Friendship Association held a tea party on the 26th to bid farewell to the delegation.

Those attending the farewell tea party included Wu Yuzhang, Shen Junru, and Guo Moruo, vice-presidents of the Sino-Soviet Friendship Association, directors of the Sino-Soviet Friendship Association in Beijing, and over one hundred people from cultural, artistic, scientific, political, and economic circles in the capital, including Chen Shutong, Shen Yanbing, Ma Yinchu, Shao Lizi, Cai Tingkai, Hu Yuzhi, and Wu Youxun.

Vasikov, Chargé d'Affaires ad interim of the Soviet Embassy, and Shanfero, temporary representative of the Soviet Society for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries in China, also attended the farewell tea party.

At the tea party, Wu Yuzhang, Guo Moruo, Shen Yanbing, Cao Mengjun, Jin Xiang, and others successively delivered speeches of farewell, wishing the delegation a successful completion of its mission.

Vasikov, Chargé d'Affaires ad interim of the Soviet Embassy, also delivered a farewell speech at the meeting, warmly wishing the delegation a successful visit to the Soviet Union.

Qian Junrui, head of the delegation of the Sino-Soviet Friendship Association to the Soviet Union to attend the "May Day" parade, delivered a speech on behalf of all members. He said that the delegation would certainly live up to the trust of the Sino-Soviet Friendship Association and people from all walks of life, strive to learn from the advanced experience and academic achievements of the Soviet Union, express the heartfelt gratitude of the Chinese people to the great assistance of the Soviet Union, introduce the situation of the Chinese people's various struggles to realize the socialist industrialization of the country, and further strengthen the great friendship between the peoples of China and the Soviet Union.

The delegation of the Sino-Soviet Friendship Association to the Soviet Union to attend the "May Day" parade is scheduled to leave the country by plane on the morning of the 27th.

### 应邀到苏联参加“五一”节观礼和参观访问  中苏友协总会组成观礼代表团

1954-04-27
第1版()
专栏：

    应邀到苏联参加“五一”节观礼和参观访问
    中苏友协总会组成观礼代表团
    据新华社讯：中苏友好协会总会应苏联对外文化协会的邀请，特组织包括中苏友好协会、人民解放军、妇女、青年、文化、艺术、科学、政治、经济等方面代表的代表团，赴苏联参加“五一”节观礼和参观、访问；并向苏联人民介绍新中国政治、经济及文化建设各方面的成就。
    中苏友好协会总会在二十六日举行茶会欢送。
    参加欢送茶会的，有中苏友好协会副会长吴玉章、沈钧儒、郭沫若，中苏友好协会总会理事会在京理事以及首都文化、艺术、科学、政治、经济等方面人士陈叔通、沈雁冰、马寅初、邵力子、蔡廷锴、胡愈之、吴有训等一百多人。
    苏联大使馆临时代办华司考、苏联对外文化协会驻华临时代表善菲罗等，也出席了欢送茶会。
    在茶会上，吴玉章、郭沫若、沈雁冰、曹孟君、金湘等相继致词欢送，预祝代表团胜利地完成任务。
    苏联大使馆临时代办华司考也在会上致词欢送，他热烈预祝代表团访苏成功。
    中苏友好协会总会赴苏联参加“五一”节观礼代表团团长钱俊瑞代表全体团员致答词。他表示：代表团一定不负中苏友好协会总会及各界人民的委托，努力学习苏联的先进经验和学术上的先进成就，并向苏联人民表达我国人民对苏联伟大援助的衷心感谢，介绍我国人民为实现国家的社会主义工业化所进行的各项斗争的情况，进一步加强中苏两国人民的伟大友谊。
    中苏友好协会总会赴苏联参加“五一”节观礼代表团定于二十七日早晨乘飞机出国。
