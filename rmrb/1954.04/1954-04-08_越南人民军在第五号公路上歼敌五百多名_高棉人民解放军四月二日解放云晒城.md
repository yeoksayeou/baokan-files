Title: Vietnam People's Army Annihilates Over 500 Enemy Troops on Highway 5; Khmer People's Liberation Army Liberates Yunxiao City on April 2nd
Author:
Date: 1954-04-08
Page: 1

    Xinhua News Agency, April 7th: According to the Vietnam News Agency on the 5th, since the Vietnam People's Army launched a series of offensives on Highway 5 in mid-March, it has annihilated 503 enemy troops, including one European-African company; destroyed more than 30 vehicles of various types; and seized 6 mortars, 2 rocket launchers, 7 machine guns, 47 automatic rifles, 209 rifles, 2 radio transceivers, and more than two tons of ammunition.
    Xinhua News Agency, April 7th: According to the Vietnam News Agency, the Cambodian Resistance Government issued a communique on the 6th, stating that the Khmer People's Liberation Army liberated Yunxiao City on April 2nd. The communique stated: On March 31st, the Khmer People's Liberation Army surrounded Yunxiao City, a French military stronghold located 100 kilometers northeast of Shangding City, the capital of Shangding Province in northeastern Cambodia. On April 1st, the enemy dispatched a mobile force from Bassac in Lower Laos, attempting to rescue Yunxiao. However, this reinforcement was intercepted by the Khmer People's Liberation Army. As a result, five enemy vehicles were destroyed, and the remaining enemies fled in disarray. The People's Liberation Army then launched an attack on Yunxiao on the 2nd and liberated the city on the same day.

### 越南人民军在第五号公路上歼敌五百多名   高棉人民解放军四月二日解放云晒城

1954-04-08
第1版()
专栏：

    越南人民军在第五号公路上歼敌五百多名
    高棉人民解放军四月二日解放云晒城
   【新华社七日讯】据越南通讯社五日讯：越南人民军从三月中旬向第五号公路发动一系列的进攻以来，已歼灭敌军五百零三名，包括一个欧非连；击毁各种车辆三十多辆；缴获迫击炮六门、火箭筒二具、机枪七挺、自动步枪四十七支、步枪二百零九支、无线电收发报机二架和两吨多弹药。
    【新华社七日讯】据越南通讯社讯：高棉抗战政府六日发表公报说，高棉人民解放军在四月二日解放了云晒城。公报说：高棉人民解放军在三月三十一日包围了位于高棉东北部上丁省省会上丁东北一百公里的法军据点云晒城。四月一日，敌人曾从下寮巴色赶派了一支机动部队，企图援救云晒。但是，这支援军遭到高棉人民解放军的截击。结果，敌军汽车五辆被击毁，余敌溃逃。人民解放军遂于二日对云晒发动攻击，并于当日解放该城。
