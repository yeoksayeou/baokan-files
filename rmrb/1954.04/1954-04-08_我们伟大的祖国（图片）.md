Our Great Motherland
The Beijing Electric Power School is the best-equipped of the nine electric power schools in the country, and it is training a large number of intermediate-level electric power technicians. This is a third-year student in the Electrical Machinery Department listening to the teacher explain the ventilation equipment of a DC motor.
