    Our Great Motherland
    The successful self-production of tungsten light bulbs by workers at the state-owned Shanghai Bulb Factory is a very important contribution to our country's national defense industry, transportation industry, and the people's daily lives. This is a scene of two engineers from the state-owned Shanghai Bulb Factory inspecting self-produced tungsten light bulbs.

### 我们伟大的祖国（图片）

1954-04-25
第1版()
专栏：

    我们伟大的祖国
    国营上海灯泡厂职工自制钨丝灯泡成功，对我国的国防工业、交通工业及人民的日常生活都是很重要的贡献。这是国营上海灯泡厂的二位工程师正在检验自制钨丝灯泡的情形。
