Title: German Writers Usse and Renn Arrive in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1954-04-29
Page: 4

    Xinhua News Agency Dispatch: German Democratic Republic writers Bodo Uhse and Ludwig Renn, who were invited to visit our country under the 1954 executive plan of the Sino-German Cultural Cooperation Agreement, arrived in Beijing by plane on the 27th. Bodo Uhse is a member of the Council of the Writers' Union of the German Democratic Republic and editor-in-chief of "Aufbau" magazine. Ludwig Renn is an academician of the Academy of Arts of the German Democratic Republic.

### 德国作家乌塞、雷恩到京

1954-04-29
第4版()
专栏：

    德国作家乌塞、雷恩到京
    据新华社讯：根据中德文化合作协定一九五四年执行计划应邀来我国访问的德意志民主共和国作家鲍多·乌塞和卢德韦希·雷恩二人，于二十七日乘飞机到达北京。鲍多·乌塞是德意志民主共和国作家联盟理事会理事、“建设”杂志总编辑，卢德韦希·雷恩是德意志民主共和国艺术科学院院士。
