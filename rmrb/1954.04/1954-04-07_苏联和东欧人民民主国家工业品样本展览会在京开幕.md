Title: Soviet and Eastern European People's Democratic Countries Industrial Product Sample Exhibition Opens in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1954-04-07
Page: 1

    [This newspaper dispatch] (From our own correspondent) The Soviet Union and Eastern European People's Democratic Countries Industrial Product Sample Exhibition, jointly organized by the import companies affiliated with the Ministry of Foreign Trade of the Central People's Government, opened on April 5th at the Beijing Youth Palace. This exhibition displays drawings, photographs, specifications, and product introduction magazines for more than 40,000 products, including machine tools, transportation, agriculture, mining and hoisting machinery, instruments, hardware, steel, telecommunications equipment, chemical raw materials, and general merchandise. There are also some samples of chemical raw materials, oils, dyes, etc. Through this exhibition, the various industrial construction departments in our country will further understand the specifications and models of industrial products from various brother countries, which will be conducive to the development of trade between our country and the Soviet Union and Eastern European People's Democratic Countries.
    During the exhibition, industrial construction departments from various regions that need to order goods from abroad will send people to visit successively. On the first day of the exhibition's opening, more than 200 engineers and cadres responsible for handling foreign orders from factories, mines, and other grassroots ordering units from all over the country visited the exhibition. The exhibition is scheduled to close on April 24.

### 苏联和东欧人民民主国家工业品样本展览会在京开幕

1954-04-07
第1版()
专栏：

    苏联和东欧人民民主国家工业品样本展览会在京开幕
    【本报讯】　中央人民政府对外贸易部所属各进口公司联合举办的苏联及东欧各人民民主国家工业产品样本展览会，于四月五日在北京青年宫开幕。这次展览会展出有工作母机，交通、农业、矿山起重等方面的各种机械，仪器、五金、钢材、电讯器材，及化工原料和百货等四万多种产品的图样、照片、规格说明书和介绍产品的杂志，并有部分化工原料、油类、染料等样品。通过这次展览，我国各工业建设部门将进一步了解各兄弟国家工业产品的规格、型号等情况，有利于我国和苏联及东欧各人民民主国家之间贸易的开展。
    展览期间，各地需要向国外订货的工业建设部门将陆续派人前来参观。在展览会开幕的第一天，就有从全国各个地区的厂矿等基层订货单位派来的工程师及负责办理国外订货的干部等二百多人进行了参观。展览会定四月二十四日闭幕。
