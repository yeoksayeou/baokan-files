Title: World Federation of Trade Unions Executive Bureau Meeting Concludes; Resolutions Passed on Reports by Saillant, Liu Changsheng, and Grassi
Author:
Date: 1954-04-01
Page: 4

    Xinhua News Agency, Vienna, March 31st: The Executive Bureau meeting of the World Federation of Trade Unions (WFTU) concluded on March 28th.
    The meeting adopted the "May Day" International Labor Day appeal to the working people of the world, and passed resolutions on the report by WFTU General Secretary Louis Saillant, "The International Situation and the Implementation of the Resolutions of the Third World Trade Union Congress," the report by Liu Changsheng, "The Struggle to Achieve Recognition and Implementation of the Basic Principles of Defending Trade Union Rights and the Preparations for the 1954 'May Day' International Labor Day," and the report by Luigi Grassi, "The Activities and Tasks of the Various Trade Union Internationals."

### 世界工会联合会执行局会议闭幕  就赛扬、刘长胜、葛拉西的报告通过决议

1954-04-01
第4版()
专栏：

    世界工会联合会执行局会议闭幕
    就赛扬、刘长胜、葛拉西的报告通过决议
    【新华社三十一日讯】塔斯社维也纳讯：世界工会联合会执行局会议于三月二十八日闭幕。
    会议通过了“五一”国际劳动节告世界劳动人民书，并就世界工联总书记路易·赛扬的报告“国际形势与世界工会第三次代表大会决议的执行情况”刘长胜的报告“争取承认和履行保卫工会权利的基本原则的斗争和一九五四年‘五一’国际劳动节的筹备工作”和路易吉·葛拉西的报告“各工会国际的活动与任务”通过了决议。
