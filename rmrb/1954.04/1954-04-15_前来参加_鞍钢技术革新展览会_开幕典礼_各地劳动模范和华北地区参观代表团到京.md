Title: Labor Models from Various Regions and North China Visiting Delegation Arrive in Beijing to Attend the Opening Ceremony of the "Anshan Iron and Steel Technology Innovation Exhibition"
Author:
Date: 1954-04-15
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, April 14th -- Eight labor models from various regions, including Zhang Mingshan, have arrived in Beijing to attend the opening ceremony of the "Anshan Iron and Steel Technology Innovation Exhibition." These labor models are advanced individuals with significant innovations in technological reform, including Tang Liyan, foreman of the steel casting workshop of Dalian Sino-Soviet Shipbuilding Company and labor model of Lushun-Dalian City; Wang Chonglun, youth planer of the Machine General Factory of Anshan Iron and Steel Company who created the "universal tool tire" and special-class labor model of Anshan City; Cai Liancheng, steelmaking worker of Tangshan Steel Plant who improved steelmaking operating methods and labor model of Tangshan City; Zhang Mingshan, vice-director of the small rolling mill of Anshan Iron and Steel Company who successfully created the "anti-surround disc" and special-class labor model of Anshan City; Liu Zuwei, young assistant engineer who successfully researched the automatic separation protection device for power plant overload and special-class labor model of Anshan City; Huang Rongchang, carpenter of Chongqing 101 Steel Plant who transformed the manual carpentry workshop into a fully mechanized production line and labor model of the plant; Fu Jingwen, deputy section chief of the production section of Wuhan 201 Plant who made significant achievements in promoting the assembly line method and special-class labor model of Wuhan City; Zhu Shunyu, deputy foreman of the turbine trial manufacturing workshop of Shanghai General Machinery Factory who continuously creates and improves tools and labor model of the plant.

Xinhua News Agency, April 14th -- The North China visiting delegation of the "Anshan Iron and Steel Technology Innovation Exhibition," consisting of more than 200 people, arrived in Beijing on the 14th. Among these representatives, in addition to some provincial and municipal Chinese Communist Party organizations and trade union cadres, most are outstanding workers and technicians from factories and mining enterprises, including Wang Kaishan, technician of the Second Power Plant of Taiyuan Power Bureau who has made significant achievements in learning from the advanced experience of the Soviet Union; Zhang Qiyuan, worker of Tangshan Kailuan Machinery Factory who created several advanced tools and labor model of Tangshan City; and Wei Ya, deputy director and engineer of Tangshan Steel Plant and labor model of Hebei Province.

During their stay in Beijing, in addition to attending the opening ceremony of the exhibition, the North China visiting delegation will also organize representatives to visit and study the experience of technological innovation of Anshan Iron and Steel, while reading documents.

### 前来参加“鞍钢技术革新展览会”开幕典礼  各地劳动模范和华北地区参观代表团到京

1954-04-15
第1版()
专栏：

    前来参加“鞍钢技术革新展览会”开幕典礼
    各地劳动模范和华北地区参观代表团到京
      【新华社十四日讯】应邀前来参加“鞍钢技术革新展览会”开幕典礼的各地劳动模范张明山等八人先后到达北京。这些劳动模范都是技术革新中有重大创造的先进人物，其中有大连中苏造船公司铸钢厂工段长、旅大市劳动模范唐立言，创造“万能工具胎”的鞍山钢铁公司机械总厂青年刨工、鞍山市特等劳动模范王崇伦，改进炼钢操作方法的唐山钢厂炼钢工人、唐山市劳动模范蔡连城；还有创造“反围盘”成功的鞍山钢铁公司小型轧钢厂副厂长、鞍山市特等劳动模范张明山，研究发电厂过负荷自动分离保护装置成功的青年助理工程师、鞍山市特等劳动模范刘祖威，将手工操作的木工房改变成全面机械化生产的重庆一零一钢铁厂木工、厂劳动模范黄荣昌，推行流水作业法有重大成就的武汉市二零一厂生产科副科长、武汉市特等劳动模范傅景文，不断创造和改进工具的上海市通用机器厂汽轮试造车间副工段长、厂劳动模范朱顺馀等。
    【新华社十四日讯】“鞍钢技术革新展览会”华北地区参观代表团二百多人于十四日到达北京。这些代表中，除部分省市级中国共产党组织和工会干部外，大部分是厂矿企业中优秀的工人和技术人员，其中有学习苏联先进经验有重大成就的太原电业局第二发电厂技师王凯山，有创造数种先进工具的唐山开滦机器厂工人、唐山市劳动模范张起元，还有唐山钢厂副厂长兼工程师、河北省劳动模范魏亚等。
    华北地区参观代表团在北京期间，除参加展览会开幕典礼大会外，并将组织代表一面参观，一面阅读文件，学习鞍钢技术革新的经验。
