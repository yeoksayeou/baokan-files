Title: Supreme Soviet of the USSR Joint Session of the Two Chambers Passes Resolution Instructing Malenkov to Present New Government List
Author:
Date: 1954-04-23
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, 22nd – TASS reports: The joint session of the Union Council and the National Council of the Fourth Supreme Soviet of the USSR was held on the morning of the 21st in the Moscow Kremlin.

Leaders of the Communist Party and the government of the Soviet Union, Malenkov, Molotov, Khrushchev, Voroshilov, Bulganin, Kaganovich, Mikoyan, Saburov, Pervukhin, Shvernik, Kirichenko, Suslov, Pospelov, and Shatalin, were warmly applauded as they took their seats on the government platform.

A. Zverev, Minister of Finance of the USSR, delivered a report on the 1954 State Budget of the USSR at the meeting.

Following Zverev’s report, a statement by G.M. Malenkov, Chairman of the Council of Ministers of the USSR, regarding the resignation of the Soviet government to the Supreme Soviet of the USSR was read at the meeting. The statement said: In accordance with the Soviet Constitution, and considering that the Supreme Soviet of the USSR has raised the issue of forming the Soviet government, the Council of Ministers of the USSR believes that its term has expired and hereby submits its resignation. (Note: According to the Soviet Constitution, the new Supreme Soviet of the USSR organizes the Soviet government at its joint session of the two chambers.)

Soviet Supreme Soviet Deputy Kirichenko then spoke at the meeting regarding Malenkov's statement. He pointed out: The Soviet government has effectively led the country. The best evaluation of the government's activities is the achievements made in various sectors of the national economy. The Soviet Union's industry, transportation, agriculture, science, and culture are continuously developing. The election of the Supreme Soviet of the USSR held on March 14 this year demonstrated the Soviet people's warm support for the policies of the Communist Party and the Soviet government, and demonstrated the unbreakable unity of the Communist Party, the Soviet government, and all the Soviet people.

Soviet Supreme Soviet Deputies Kapitonov and Mustafayev then spoke at the meeting. They unanimously endorsed the activities of the Soviet government and requested the Supreme Soviet of the USSR to approve them. Kapitonov said: The Soviet government's loyal service to the country, to the working class, collective farmers, and Soviet intellectuals, has earned the warm love and boundless gratitude of the working people. Mustafayev said: During the period since the first session of the Third Supreme Soviet of the USSR, all of the multifaceted activities of the Soviet government have consistently aligned with the fundamental interests of the Soviet working people.

The joint session finally unanimously passed a resolution regarding the statement of G.M. Malenkov, Chairman of the Council of Ministers of the USSR.

The joint session of the Union Council and the National Council of the Supreme Soviet of the USSR concluded.

Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, 22nd – TASS reports: The joint session of the Union Council and the National Council of the Supreme Soviet of the USSR on the 21st passed the following resolution regarding the statement of G.M. Malenkov, Chairman of the Council of Ministers of the USSR:

    1. The Supreme Soviet of the USSR accepts the statement of G.M. Malenkov, Chairman of the Council of Ministers of the USSR, regarding the resignation of the Soviet government to the Supreme Soviet of the USSR, approves the activities of the Council of Ministers of the USSR, and instructs Comrade G.M. Malenkov, Chairman of the Council of Ministers of the USSR, to submit a new list of the Soviet government to the Supreme Soviet of the USSR.

    2. The current Council of Ministers of the USSR is instructed to continue in office until the Supreme Soviet of the USSR approves the new list of the Soviet government at this session.

### 苏联最高苏维埃两院联席会议通过决议  指令马林科夫提出政府新名单

1954-04-23
第1版()
专栏：

    苏联最高苏维埃两院联席会议通过决议
    指令马林科夫提出政府新名单
    【新华社二十二日讯】据塔斯社莫斯科讯：苏联第四届最高苏维埃联盟院和民族院，二十一日上午在莫斯科克里姆林宫举行了联席会议。
    苏联共产党和政府的领袖马林科夫、莫洛托夫、赫鲁晓夫、伏罗希洛夫、布尔加宁、卡冈诺维奇、米高扬、萨布罗夫、别尔乌辛、什维尔尼克、基里钦科、苏斯洛夫、波斯伯洛夫、夏塔林在政府席上就座时，全场热烈鼓掌欢迎。
    苏联财政部部长阿·兹维列夫在会上作了关于一九五四年苏联国家预算的报告。
    兹维列夫报告后，会上宣读了苏联部长会议主席马林科夫关于苏联政府向苏联最高苏维埃提出辞职的声明。声明说：根据苏联宪法，同时鉴于苏联最高苏维埃已经提出组成苏联政府的问题，苏联部长会议认为它的任期已满，特提出辞职（按：根据苏联宪法，新届苏联最高苏维埃在其两院联席会议上组织苏联政府）。
    苏联最高苏维埃代表基里钦科接着就马林科夫的声明在会上讲了话。他指出：苏联政府已经对国家进行了有效的领导。对政府活动的最好的评价就是国民经济各部门所取得的成就。苏联的工业、运输业、农业、科学和文化正在不断地发展着。今年三月十四日举行的苏联最高苏维埃选举，表明了苏联人民热烈拥护共产党和苏联政府的政策，表明了共产党、苏联政府和全体苏联人民的牢不可破的团结。
    苏联最高苏维埃代表卡皮托诺夫和穆斯塔法耶夫接着在会上讲了话。他们一致赞同苏联政府的活动，并要求苏联最高苏维埃加以批准。卡皮托诺夫说：苏联政府对国家，对工人阶级、集体农民和苏维埃知识分子的忠诚服务，获得了劳动人民的热烈爱戴和无限感激。穆斯塔法耶夫说：自从苏联第三届最高苏维埃第一次会议以来的这段时期中，苏联政府的全部的多方面的活动都始终是符合苏联劳动人民的根本利益的。
    联席会议最后就苏联部长会议主席马林科夫的声明一致通过了一项决议。
    苏联最高苏维埃联盟院和民族院联席会议至此结束。
    【新华社二十二日讯】据塔斯社莫斯科讯：苏联最高苏维埃联盟院和民族院二十一日联席会议，就苏联部长会议主席格·马·马林科夫的声明通过决议如下：
    一、苏联最高苏维埃接受苏联部长会议主席马林科夫关于苏联政府向苏联最高苏维埃提出辞职的声明，批准苏联部长会议的活动，并指令苏联部长会议主席马林科夫同志向苏联最高苏维埃提出苏联政府的新名单。
    二、指令现在的苏联部长会议继续任职，直到苏联最高苏维埃本次会议批准苏联政府的新名单的时候为止。
