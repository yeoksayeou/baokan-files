Title: Reducing Prices is a Consistent Policy of the Soviet Party and Government (Picture)
Author:
Date: 1954-04-10
Page: 4

    Reducing Prices is a Consistent Policy of the Soviet Party and Government
    In Moscow clothing stores, the prices of coats, women's outerwear, hats, and other sewn products have been reduced by 10 to 15 percent.
    In Moscow tea shops, tea prices have been reduced by 10 percent, and coffee and cocoa prices have been reduced by 15 percent.

### 减低物价是苏联党和政府的一贯政策（图片）

1954-04-10
第4版()
专栏：

    减低物价是苏联党和政府的一贯政策
    在莫斯科服装商店里，大衣、女式外衣、帽子和其他缝制品的价格减低百分之十到十五。
    在莫斯科茶叶商店里，茶叶价格减低百分之十，咖啡和可可价格减低百分之十五。
