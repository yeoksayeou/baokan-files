Title: Vietnamese Trade Union Delegation Arrives in Beijing

Author: 
Date: 1954-04-27
Page: 1

　　Invited to Attend "May Day" Celebrations
　　Vietnamese Trade Union Delegation Arrives in Beijing
　　[Xinhua News Agency, April 25th] The trade union delegation of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam, invited by the All-China Federation of Trade Unions to attend the "May Day" celebrations in our country, arrived in Beijing by train on the 25th.
　　Those who went to welcome them included: Li Jiebo, Secretary of the Secretariat of the All-China Federation of Trade Unions; Jin Ziming, Deputy Director of the International Liaison Department of the All-China Federation of Trade Unions; and Hao Tongsheng, Deputy Director of the Finance Department.
　　Chen Yu, Secretary-General of the Asian-Australasian Liaison Bureau of the World Federation of Trade Unions, also went to welcome them.
　　Fan Ping, Second Secretary of the Embassy of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam in China, also went to welcome them.
　　The list of the Vietnamese Trade Union Delegation is as follows:
　　Head of Delegation: Vu Huy Tinh, Member of the Standing Committee of the Vietnam General Confederation of Labor
　　Members: Nguyen Cong Thanh, Member of the Standing Committee of the Trade Union Federation of a certain Interzone
　　　　　Nguyen Hoang, Worker at the B-4 Factory in Binh Dinh Province, Labor Model
　　　　　Pham Thi Minh Thu (female), Worker at the Hoang Huu Nam Factory in Thai Nguyen Province, Labor Model
　　　　　Nguyen Nhu Que, Intellectual Worker, Labor Model

### 越南工会代表团到京

1954-04-27
第1版()
专栏：

    应邀参加“五一”节观礼
    越南工会代表团到京
    【新华社二十五日讯】应中华全国总工会的邀请，前来我国参加“五一”节观礼的越南民主共和国工会代表团，已在二十五日乘火车到达北京。
    前往欢迎的有：中华全国总工会书记处书记李颉伯、中华全国总工会国际联络部副部长金子明、财务部副部长郝桐生。
    世界工联亚澳联络局秘书长陈宇也前往欢迎。
    前往欢迎的还有越南民主共和国驻华大使馆二等秘书范平。
    越南工会代表团名单如下：
    团长：武辉静　越南总工会常务委员会委员
    团员：阮公澄　某联区的工会联合会常务委员会委员
