Title: Telegram of Congratulations from the Asian and Pacific Regions Peace Liaison Committee
Author:
Date: 1954-04-25
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, April 24th: The Asian and Pacific Regions Peace Liaison Committee sent a telegram to the All-India Peace Council regarding the "Asia Day" movement held in India. The telegram reads:

To the All-India Peace Council:

    The "Asia Day" movement launched by your council, calling on all the people of India, and the various demands for maintaining peace in Asia to be submitted to the Geneva Conference, not only conform to the peaceful aspirations of all the people of India, but also to the peaceful aspirations of all the people of Asia. We believe that your call will win warm support from the people of all Asia and the people of the world.

    Congratulations on the success of the Indian people in this movement!

    Asian and Pacific Regions Peace Liaison Committee, April 24, 1954

### 亚洲及太平洋区域和平联络委员会贺电

1954-04-25
第1版()
专栏：

    亚洲及太平洋区域和平联络委员会贺电
    【新华社二十四日讯】亚洲及太平洋区域和平联络委员会关于印度举行“亚洲日”运动致电全印和平理事会，电文如下：全印和平理事会：
    贵会号召全体印度人民所举行的“亚洲日”运动和将向日内瓦会议发出的维护亚洲和平的各项要求，不仅符合全体印度人民的和平愿望，也符合全体亚洲人民的和平愿望。我们相信你们的这一号召将赢得全亚洲人民以及全世界人民的热烈的拥护与支持。
    祝贺印度人民在此运动中的成功！
    亚洲及太平洋区域和平联络委员会  一九五四年四月二十四日
