Title: Our Great Motherland (Picture)
Author: 
Date: 1954-04-20
Page: 1

Our great motherland
    The spring sweeping preparations at the Huaibei Salt Field are well done. The salt production task for March was overfulfilled by March 25th, exceeding the salt production figures for mid-April of last year. This is a picture of salt excavation by the Shengangdong Sanwei team of the Tainan Field in the Huaibei Salt Field.

### 我们伟大的祖国（图片）

1954-04-20
第1版()
专栏：

    我们伟大的祖国
    淮北盐场的春扫准备工作做得好。三月份的产盐任务到三月二十五日止已超额完成，超过去年四月中旬的产盐数字。这是淮北盐场台南场深港东三圩小组刳盐情况。
