Title: Message of Greetings from the CPC Central Committee to the SED Congress
Author:
Date: 1954-04-03
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Berlin, 2nd. The representative of the Chinese Communist Party, Ji Pengfei, read out the congratulatory message from the Central Committee of the Chinese Communist Party to the congress at the morning session of the Fourth Congress of the Socialist Unity Party of Germany on the 1st.

The message said: The Central Committee of the Chinese Communist Party extends fraternal congratulations to the Fourth Congress of the Socialist Unity Party of Germany.

Under the assistance of the Soviet Union, the Socialist Unity Party of Germany has led the German people in establishing the German Democratic Republic, achieved tremendous success in eliminating the remnants of fascism, rapidly restoring war wounds, developing the national economy, and improving the material and cultural life of the people, and laid a solid foundation for the cause of striving to establish a unified, independent, and peace-loving democratic Germany.

The convening of the Fourth Congress of the Socialist Unity Party of Germany will enable the Socialist Unity Party to achieve further successes in leading the construction of the German Democratic Republic and in uniting the entire German people in the struggle to establish a unified, independent, democratic, and peace-loving Germany.

### 中共中央致德统一社会党代表大会的贺词

1954-04-03
第4版()
专栏：

    中共中央致德统一社会党代表大会的贺词
    【新华社柏林二日电】在德国统一社会党第四次代表大会一日上午的会议上，中国共产党代表姬鹏飞向大会宣读了中国共产党中央委员会的贺词。
    贺词说：中国共产党中央委员会谨向德国统一社会党第四次代表大会致以兄弟般的祝贺。
    德国统一社会党在苏联的援助下领导德国人民建立了德意志民主共和国，在肃清法西斯残余势力、迅速恢复战争创伤、发展国民经济和提高人民的物质和文化生活上获得了巨大的成就，并为争取建立统一、独立与爱好和平的民主德国的事业奠定了巩固的基础。
    德国统一社会党第四次代表大会的召开，将使统一社会党在领导德意志民主共和国的建设事业中，在团结全德人民建立一个统一、独立、民主与爱好和平的德国的斗争中，获得进一步的成就。
