Title: Togliatti Speaks on Soviet Government Proposal: Proposal will further clarify the situation and eliminate all excuses for warmongers

Author: 
Date: 1954-04-06
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Rome, April 4th: TASS reports that Italian Communist Party General Secretary Togliatti spoke to a reporter from "Corriere della Sera" on the Soviet government's March 31st note to the governments of France, Britain, and the United States.

    Togliatti said: The Soviet proposal is very timely. Everyone hopes that it will not only greatly help to further ease international tensions, but also specifically bring a new period to international relations.

    Togliatti pointed out: The imperialists' "Cold War" policy made another war seem inevitable, even imminent. But now there is a new path. This path has no danger of a new war and leads to the establishment of new relations between the people of all countries on the basis of understanding and cooperation. The Berlin Four-Power Foreign Ministers' Conference was the first step in moving in this correct direction. The current Soviet proposal will further clarify the situation, eliminate the final obstacles, and refute all the arguments of the followers of the war policy. The significance of this proposal lies in the fact that it provides a concrete opportunity to finally abandon the path to world division and war.

    Togliatti finally said: We hope that the Soviet proposal will lay the foundation for mutual agreement, mutual trust, and peaceful international policies. But it must be remembered: hope alone is not enough. The people should make their rulers aware of and obey their will.

### 陶里亚蒂就苏联政府的建议发表谈话  这一建议将进一步澄清局势消除战争制造者的一切藉口

1954-04-06
第4版()
专栏：

    陶里亚蒂就苏联政府的建议发表谈话
    这一建议将进一步澄清局势消除战争制造者的一切藉口
   【新华社五日讯】据塔斯社罗马四日讯：意大利共产党总书记陶里亚蒂就苏联政府三月三十一日致法、英、美三国政府的照会向“国家晚报”记者发表谈话。
    陶里亚蒂说：苏联的建议是很合时宜的。每个人都希望它不仅会大大有助于进一步缓和国际紧张局势，而且能具体地为国际关系带来一个新的时期。
    陶里亚蒂指出：帝国主义者的“冷战”政策使得另一次战争似乎是不可避免的，甚至是近在眼前的。但是，现在有一条新的道路。这条路不会有新战争的危险，而且是导向各国人民在谅解和合作的基础上建立新的关系。柏林四国外长会议是向这个正确的方向前进的第一步。而苏联目前的建议就会进一步澄清局势，消除最后的障碍，并且推翻战争政策的追随者的一切论据。这个建议的意义在于它提供了最后放弃通往世界分裂和战争的道路的具体机会。
    陶里亚蒂最后说：我们希望苏联的建议会为互相协议、互相信任与和平的国际政策奠定基础。但是，必须记住：光是希望是不够的。人民应该使他们的统治者知道而且服从他们的意志。
