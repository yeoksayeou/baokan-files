Title: Our Great Motherland (Picture)
Author:
Date: 1954-04-14

    Our Great Motherland
    A youth work section composed of young workers has been established at the construction site of the Soviet Exhibition Hall in Beijing. They are responsible for the construction of the open-air theater. This young worker is driving a bulldozer to level the site.

### 我们伟大的祖国（图片）

1954-04-14
第1版()
专栏：

    我们伟大的祖国
    北京苏联展览馆工地成立了由青年工人组成的青年工段。他们负责修建露天剧场的工程。这位青年工人正驾驶着推土机进行场地铲平工作。
