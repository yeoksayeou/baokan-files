Title: Burma Forbids American Planes Carrying French Troops from Flying Over Burmese Territory
Author:
Date: 1954-04-26
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, London, April 25th, TASS reported: Reuters reported that Burma has forbidden American planes carrying French troops to Indochina from flying over Burmese territory.

### 缅甸不许载运法军的美机飞越缅甸领土

1954-04-26
第4版()
专栏：

    缅甸不许载运法军的美机飞越缅甸领土
    【新华社二十五日讯】塔斯社伦敦二十四日讯：路透社报道，缅甸不许载运法国军队前往印度支那的美国飞机飞越缅甸领土。
