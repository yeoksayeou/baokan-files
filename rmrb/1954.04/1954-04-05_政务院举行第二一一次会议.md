Title: The Government Administration Council Holds its 211th Meeting
Author:
Date: 1954-04-05
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, April 4th: The 211th meeting of the Central People's Government Administration Council was held on the afternoon of the 1st. The meeting heard and approved the report made by Li Lisan, Minister of the Central People's Government Labor Department, on the summary of past work and suggestions for improving future work. The meeting also approved various appointments and removals.

### 政务院举行第二一一次会议

1954-04-05
第1版()
专栏：

    政务院举行第二一一次会议
   【新华社四日讯】中央人民政府政务院第二百一十一次政务会议在一日下午举行。会议听了和批准了中央人民政府劳动部李立三部长所作的关于过去工作总结及改进今后工作意见的报告。会议并通过了各项任免案。
