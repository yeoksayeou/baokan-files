Title: East China and Shanghai Circles Begin Discussing Draft Constitution

Author: 
Date: 1954-04-21
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, Shanghai, 20th - Leading members of organizations at the East China level and of various organizations, democratic parties, and people's organizations in Shanghai have begun discussing the initial draft of the Constitution of the People's Republic of China. On April 11th, the East China level organizations and Shanghai established a Draft Constitution Discussion Committee, with Chen Yi as the director and Pan Hannian, Sheng Pihua, Wu Kejian, Liu Jiping, and Shen Zhiyuan as deputy directors. The committee has an office responsible for guidance and liaison matters.

More than 600 representatives from all aspects of society, including organizations at the East China level, various organizations in Shanghai, local organizations of democratic parties, people's organizations, cultural, educational, scientific and technological circles, ethnic minorities, and religious circles, are participating in the discussion of the initial draft of the Constitution, and they are organized into 20 groups. From the 14th, each group began to read the initial draft of the Constitution and related documents; in the two discussion sessions that have been held, people from all walks of life have spoken enthusiastically and have conducted serious discussions on the initial draft of the Constitution, word by word and sentence by sentence.

### 华东和上海各界开始讨论宪法草案初稿

1954-04-21
第1版()
专栏：

    华东和上海各界开始讨论宪法草案初稿
    【新华社上海二十日电】华东一级机关和上海市各机关、各民主党派及各人民团体负责人，已开始讨论中华人民共和国宪法草案初稿。四月十一日，华东一级机关和上海市成立了宪法草案初稿讨论委员会，由陈毅任主任，潘汉年、盛丕华、吴克坚、刘季平、沈志远任副主任。委员会设有办公室，负责指导和联系等事宜。
    参加宪法草案初稿讨论的有华东一级机关，上海市各机关，各民主党派地方组织，各人民团体，文化、教育、科学技术界，少数民族和宗教界等各方面代表人士六百多人，共编为二十个小组。从十四日起，各小组开始阅读宪法草案初稿和有关文件；在已经举行的两次讨论会上，各界人士发言都很踊跃，对宪法草案初稿逐字逐句进行了认真的讨论。
