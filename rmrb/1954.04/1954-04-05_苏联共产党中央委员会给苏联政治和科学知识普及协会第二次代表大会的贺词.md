Title: Message of Greetings from the Central Committee of the Communist Party of the Soviet Union to the Second Congress of the All-Union Society for the Dissemination of Political and Scientific Knowledge
Author:
Date: 1954-04-05
Page: 4

The Central Committee of the Communist Party of the Soviet Union congratulates the Second Congress of the All-Union Society for the Dissemination of Political and Scientific Knowledge, and congratulates this vanguard of the Soviet intelligentsia that disseminates knowledge to the people. The Soviet intelligentsia is faithfully serving its people and serving the great cause of communist construction.

The All-Union Society for the Dissemination of Political and Scientific Knowledge holds more than one million lectures on various types of knowledge every year, making a great contribution to the communist education of the working people, and helping the Communist Party and the Soviet government to solve a historical task more quickly: to make all workers and peasants cultured and educated people.

The Second Congress of the All-Union Society for the Dissemination of Political and Scientific Knowledge is being held under the circumstances that the entire Soviet people, under the leadership of the Communist Party, are victoriously realizing the most important task—to greatly increase the supply of food and industrial products to the residents within two or three years. Therefore, the various organizations of the All-Union Society for the Dissemination of Political and Scientific Knowledge and the entire Soviet intelligentsia are faced with a lofty task: to link scientific and educational work more closely with the practice of communist construction, and to do everything possible and use all knowledge to provide collective farm members and staff of machine tractor stations and state farms with the necessary help in the struggle for a new and great upsurge in agriculture.

The Central Committee of the Communist Party of the Soviet Union believes that the All-Union Society for the Dissemination of Political and Scientific Knowledge, which has 300,000 members, will gloriously complete a sacred task—to bring the immortal doctrines of Marx, Engels, Lenin, and Stalin and the latest achievements of modern science and technology to the people, and to widely promote the advanced experience of industrial and agricultural innovators, in order to further strengthen the national strength of our socialist country.

Long live our glorious Soviet intelligentsia!
Long live our great Soviet people—the builders of communism!
		The Central Committee of the Communist Party of the Soviet Union
          (Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, 2nd [of April] via TASS)

### 苏联共产党中央委员会给苏联政治和科学知识普及协会第二次代表大会的贺词

1954-04-05
第4版()
专栏：

    苏联共产党中央委员会给苏联政治和科学知识普及协会第二次代表大会的贺词
    苏联共产党中央委员会祝贺苏联政治和科学知识普及协会第二次代表大会，祝贺这个向人民传播知识的苏维埃知识分子的先头部队。苏维埃知识分子在忠实地为自己的人民服务，为伟大的共产主义建设事业服务。
    苏联政治和科学知识普及协会每年举办一百余万次关于各种知识的讲座，为劳动人民的共产主义教育事业作出伟大的贡献，帮助共产党和苏联政府更快地解决一项历史任务：使所有工人和农民成为有文化的和受过教育的人。
    苏联政治和科学知识普及协会第二次代表大会是在这种情况下召开的，即全体苏联人民在共产党的领导下胜利地实现着最重要的任务——在两三年内大大提高对居民的食品和工业品的供应。因此，苏联政治和科学知识普及协会的各个组织和全体苏维埃知识分子面临着一项崇高的任务：把科学教育工作和共产主义建设的实践更加紧密地联系起来，竭尽一切力量并运用一切知识，在争取农业的新的巨大高涨方面，给集体农庄庄员、机器拖拉机站和国营农场的工作人员以必要的帮助。
    苏联共产党中央委员会相信：拥有三十万会员的苏联政治和科学知识普及协会将光荣地完成一项神圣的任务——把马克思、恩格斯、列宁、斯大林的不朽学说和现代科学与技术的最新成就带给人民，广泛地推广工业和农业革新者的先进经验，以进一步加强我们社会主义国家的国力。
    我们光荣的苏维埃知识分子万岁！
    我们伟大的苏联人民——共产主义的建设者万岁！
        苏联共产党中央委员会
          （新华社据塔斯社莫斯科二日电）
