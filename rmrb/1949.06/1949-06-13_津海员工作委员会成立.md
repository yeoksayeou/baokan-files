Title: Tianjin Seafarers' Work Committee Established
Author:
Date: 1949-06-13
Page: 2

　　Xinhua News Agency, Tianjin, 12th. The Tianjin Seafarers' Work Committee has been established recently and is currently preparing for the establishment of the Seafarers' Union. The members of the committee will include seafarers on coastal ships, workers on Haihe barges and inland river small steamers, as well as employees of Tianjin's shipping companies.

### 津海员工作委员会成立

1949-06-13
第2版()
专栏：

　　津海员工作委员会成立
    【新华社天津十二日电】津市海员工作委员会已于日前成立，现正进行海员工会的筹备事宜。该会会员将包括来往沿海轮船的海员、海河驳船与内河小火轮的职工、以及津市各轮船公司职工等。
