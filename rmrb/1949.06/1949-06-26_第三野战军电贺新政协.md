Title: Third Field Army Cables Congratulations to the New Political Consultative Conference
Author:
Date: 1949-06-26
Page: 1

　　Xinhua News Agency, Shanghai, 25th: The entire commanders and fighters of the Third Field Army of the Chinese People's Liberation Army and the East China Military Region sent a telegram congratulating the establishment of the preparatory committee of the New Political Consultative Conference. The telegram stated:

　　All comrades of our East China Military Region and the Third Field Army are celebrating the establishment of the preparatory committee of the New Political Consultative Conference with unparalleled excitement, and wholeheartedly support all resolutions of the conference. We are determined to respond to the call of Chairman Mao and Commander-in-Chief Zhu, continue to fight bravely, cooperate with fraternal corps and all the people's forces, annihilate the remaining enemies, liberate all of China, and greet the victorious convocation of the New Political Consultative Conference. We vow to always faithfully defend the New Political Consultative Conference and the democratic coalition government that will be established.

### 第三野战军电贺新政协

1949-06-26
第1版()
专栏：

　　第三野战军电贺新政协
    【新华社上海二十五日电】中国人民解放军第三野战军及华东军区全体指战员致电祝贺新政治协商会议筹备会成立，电称：
    我华东军区、第三野战军全体同志谨以无比兴奋的心情，来庆祝新政协筹备会的成立，衷心拥护大会一切决议。我们决心响应毛主席、朱总司令的号召，继续奋勇战斗，配合兄弟兵团及一切人民力量，全歼残敌，解放全中国，来迎接新政协会议的胜利召集，并誓愿永远忠实捍卫新政协及将成立的民主联合政府。
