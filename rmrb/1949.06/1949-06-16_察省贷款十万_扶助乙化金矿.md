Title: Chahar Province Loans 100,000 to Support Yihua Gold Mine
Author:
Date: 1949-06-16
Page: 2
Economic News

　　Chahar Province Loans 100,000
  　To Support Yihua Gold Mine
    [This newspaper reports] There are 20 gold mines in Yihua County, Nankou Special District, Chahar Province. They were shut down due to destruction by the Chiang Kai-shek bandit gang. After our army liberated the area, restoration of mining and smelting has begun. The Nankou Office of the People's Bank of Chahar Province has provided a loan of 100,000 yuan to support their rapid resumption of production for the use of our mechanical industry.

### 察省贷款十万　扶助乙化金矿

1949-06-16
第2版()
专栏：经济新闻

　　察省贷款十万
  　扶助乙化金矿
    【本报讯】南口专区乙化县有廿处金矿，曾因遭受蒋匪帮破坏而停工，我军解放该区后，已着手恢复采冶，人民银行察省分行南口办事处特予贷款十万元，扶植其迅速恢复生产，以供我机械工业生产之用。
