Title: Qixin Cement Sales are Booming

Author:

Date: 1949-06-08
Page: 2

　　Qixin Cement Sales are Booming
    [Tangshan Electric Dispatch] With the assistance of the People's Government, the Qixin Cement Company in Tangshan is experiencing booming sales. In May, cement production reached 15,519 tons, exceeding the original plan by 3,519 tons. Since April, all of the company's monthly cement production has been exclusively sold by the Tianjin Trade Corporation. Sales in April reached 13,320 tons. In mid-May, another 16,859 tons were sold, and the sales range has expanded from the Northeast and North China regions to East China and the Central Plains. The phenomenon of stockpiles of goods like mountains that existed before liberation is no longer present.

### 启新洋灰销路畅旺

1949-06-08
第2版()
专栏：

　　启新洋灰销路畅旺
    【唐山电】唐山启新洋灰公司，在人民政府扶助下，产品销路畅旺，五月份出产洋灰一万五千五百一十九吨，较原计划超出三千五百一十九吨。该公司自四月份起，每月出产洋灰全部由天津贸易总公司包销，四月份销出量达到一万三千三百二十吨。五月中又销出一万六千八百五十九吨，运销范围也由东北、华北各地伸展到华东与中原地区。解放前存货堆积如山的现象已不再存在。
