Title: Revision of Foreign Exchange Rates

Author:
Date: 1949-06-06
Page: 2

　　Revised Foreign Exchange Rates

[Our Newspaper] Effective today (June 6th), the revised foreign exchange rates are: US dollar 1 to People's Currency 1100 yuan, British pound 1 to People's Currency 2880 yuan, Hong Kong dollar 1 to People's Currency 180 yuan.

### 国外汇兑改订牌价

1949-06-06
第2版()
专栏：

　　国外汇兑改订牌价
    【本报讯】国外汇兑今（六日）起改订牌价为美钞一比人民券一一○○元，英磅一比人民券二八八○元，港币一比人民券一八○元。
