Title: Commemorating "July 1st" and "July 7th": Revolutionary University Holds Preparatory Meeting
Author: Wang Hong
Date: 1949-06-30
Page: 2

　　Commemorating "July 1st" and "July 7th"
  　Revolutionary University Holds Preparatory Meeting
    [Ping City News] The North China People's Revolutionary University held a preparatory meeting on the evening of the 28th to commemorate "July 1st" and "July 7th." It was decided that the entire schedule for the commemoration would be seven days. In addition to holding commemorative meetings on "July 1st" and "July 7th" respectively, where responsible comrades from the university will introduce the significance of "July 1st," the history of the Communist Party of China, the historical facts and combat experience of the "July 7th" Anti-Japanese War, commemorative evening parties, symposiums, and ball games will be held in various departments and classes. Propaganda teams will be organized to conduct propaganda in nearby villages and towns, and the school's cultural troupe will also perform the famous play "Wang Xiuluan" for nearby residents.
    (Wang Hong)

### 纪念“七一”“七七”　革大开筹备会
王红
1949-06-30
第2版()
专栏：

　　纪念“七一”“七七”
  　革大开筹备会
    【平市讯】华北人民革命大学于二十八日晚召开纪念“七一”、“七七”筹备会，决定纪念大会的全部日程为七天。除于“七一”、“七七”分别召开纪念大会，由校内负责同志介绍“七一”的意义，中国共产党党史、“七七”对日抗战的史实及斗争经验外，并将在各部班分别举行纪念晚会、座谈会及球类比赛，组织宣传队到附近各村镇进行宣传，本校文工团亦将为附近居民演出名剧“王秀鸾”。
    （王红）
