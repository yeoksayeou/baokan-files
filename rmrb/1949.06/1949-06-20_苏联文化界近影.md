    Recent Developments in Soviet Cultural Circles

    On May 12th, the Soviet Writers' Union organized a literary evening entitled "Striving for World Peace" at the Hall of Columns in Moscow. The evening was opened by Konstantin Fedin, a Stalin Prize laureate. He reported on the powerful movement of all progressive forces worldwide striving for peace and opposing war. Prominent Soviet writers and poets such as Nikolai Vydal, Mikhail Isakovsky, Alexei Surkov, Stepan Shchipachev, and others read their works calling for peace and exposing the instigators of a new war.
    　　　　　×　　　　　　　×　　　　　　　×
    A new documentary film "May Day," chronicling the grand celebration of May Day in Moscow this year, premiered in Moscow on May 16th. The film was produced by the Central Documentary Film Studio.
    　　　　　×　　　　　　　×　　　　　　　×
    Moscow recently published Vasily Dorogin's "Sovereignty in Soviet State Law." The book critiques bourgeois theories of sovereignty, pointing out that the strength and vitality of sovereignty in Soviet state law is based on the moral and political unity of Soviet society, and that "the socialist economic system and the socialist ownership of the means of production constitute the solid material foundation of Soviet state sovereignty." According to a TASS dispatch, the book is the first jurisprudential study in Soviet jurisprudence to analyze the doctrine of national sovereignty from a Marxist-Leninist perspective.
    　　　　×　　　　　　×　　　　　　×
    The Young Pioneers Publishing House recently published "This is America!" The book includes political essays, sketches, and travelogues about the United States by Gorky, Mayakovsky, Zhukov, and others.

### 苏联文化界近影

1949-06-20
第4版()
专栏：

　　苏联文化界近影
    苏联作家协会于五月十二日在莫斯科的圆柱大厅组织了“争取全世界和平”文学晚会。晚会由斯大林奖金获得者康斯坦金·裴定主持开幕。他报告了全世界一切进步力量争取和平反对战争的强大运动。苏联名作家和诗人尼古拉·维达尔，米海尔·伊萨科夫斯基，阿列克塞·苏尔科夫，斯梯潘·谢巴切夫…等人，曾在会上宣读他们号召争取和平及揭露新战争挑拔者的作品。
    　　　　　×　　　　　　　×　　　　　　　×
    记载莫斯科今年庆祝五一节盛况的新文献片“五一节”，已于五月十六日在莫斯科献映。该片系文献片中央制片厂出品。
    　　　　　×　　　　　　　×　　　　　　　×
    莫斯科最近出版了瓦西里·多罗金所著“苏维埃国家法中的主权”一书。该书批判了资产阶级的主权学说，指明了苏维埃国家法中的主权的力量和活力是以苏维埃社会的道德、政治的统一为基础的，“社会主义的经济制度和社会主义的生产手段所有制，组成了苏维埃国家主权的稳固的物质基础”。据塔斯社电讯称，该书是苏联法学以马列主义观点分析国家主权学说的第一本法学研究书。
    　　　　×　　　　　　×　　　　　　×
    青年先锋队出版局最近出版了“这就是美国！”一书。该书包括高尔基、玛雅科夫斯基、茹科夫等人所撰关于美国的政论、速写及旅行记等。
