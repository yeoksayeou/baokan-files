Title: Shanghai Garrison Headquarters Clears, Registers, and Returns Private Cars Requisitioned by Bandit Troops
Author:
Date: 1949-06-03
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Shanghai, 2nd. Before the liberation of Shanghai, the Kuomintang bandit troops forcibly requisitioned thousands of private cars. Many of them were damaged or burned by the bandit troops. In order to alleviate the suffering of the people, the Shanghai Garrison Headquarters of the People's Liberation Army has stipulated the following methods for clearing, registering, and returning the cars: 1. Car owners who have identified the location of their cars can register with the Garrison Headquarters with their car documents. After verification, the car will be returned upon bail, and the car owner will publish a statement in the newspaper; 2. Car owners who have not yet found the location of their cars can submit a written report to the Garrison Headquarters, specifying the car's license plate and engine number. Once found, the owner will be notified to retrieve it immediately.
