Title: Warrior's Poem: Shell

Author:

Date: 1949-06-08
Page: 4

　　Warrior's Poem
  　Shell
    The shell is pointed at both ends,
    It emits a puff of smoke as it leaves.
    Asked it: "Where are you going?"
    It said: "To Jiangnan!"
　 
　　The Bullet's Words
　
    My body is two inches long,
    Five brothers stand in a row;
    My head is sharp and bright,
    The brave warrior carries me everywhere,
　
　　Warrior Wu Jinqiang
　
    "What to do in Jiangnan?"
    "To find the major war criminals!
    When the war criminals see me,
    Their lives will be hard to keep!"
　
  　Warrior Wang De
　
    If I see the reactionary army,
    I will automatically load into the chamber;
    If you ask me what I'm going to do?
    I will annihilate the enemy completely.

### 战士的诗　炮弹

1949-06-08
第4版()
专栏：

　　战士的诗
  　炮弹
    炮弹两头尖，
    临走冒股烟。
    问它：“何处去？”
    它说：“下江南！”
　 
　　子弹的话
　
    我的身子二寸长，
    弟兄五个连一行；
    我的脑袋尖又亮，
    勇士带我走四方，
　
　　战士吴金强
　
    “江南干什么？”
    “去找大战犯！
    战犯见了我，
    活命难上难！”
　
  　战士王德
　
    要是看见反动军，
    我便自动上了膛；
    你要问我干什么？
    我叫敌人消灭光。
