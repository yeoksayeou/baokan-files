Title: North China Higher Education Committee Holds First Meeting to Discuss Academic System and Other Issues
Author: Jin Feng
Date: 1949-06-07
Page: 1

　　North China Higher Education Committee
  　Holds First Meeting to Discuss Academic System and Other Issues
    [Reported by this newspaper] The North China Higher Education Committee held its first meeting yesterday (June 6th) at the Six Nations Hotel. The following 42 committee members attended: Dong Biwu, Zhang Xiruo, Zhou Yang, Ma Xulun, Li Da, Xu Deheng, Zeng Zhaolun, Wu Han, Guo Moruo, Wu Yuzhang, Xu Teli, Ma Yinchu, Fan Wenlan, Cheng Fangwu, Deng Chumin, Zhang Zhirang, Tang Yongtong, Zheng Zhenduo, Qian Duansheng, Lan Gongwu, Yang Xiufeng, Ye Qisun, Chen Daisun, Lu Zhiwei, Zhang Dongsun, Lei Jieqiong, Li Jinxi, Xu Beihong, Li Zong'en, Yan Jici, Pei Wenzhong, Chao Zhefu, Yu Li, Liu Ding, Le Tianyu, Zhou Zezhao, Shen Tilan, Huang Songling, Zhang Zonglin, Zhang Guofan, Yu Dafu, and Feng Naichao. At the meeting, Chairman and Vice-Chairman Dong Biwu and Zhang Xiruo explained the significance and tasks of the establishment of the Higher Education Committee. Vice-Chairman Zhou Yang and Committee Member Huang Songling of the Higher Education Committee reported on the progress of work since the takeover of universities in the Pingjin area. Subsequently, opinions were widely exchanged and discussions were very enthusiastic on the university academic system, curriculum reform, management of private universities, autumn enrollment, training and allocation of graduates from various universities this term, and other tasks. The meeting unanimously agreed to authorize the Standing Committee to continue studying the issues discussed and to form various specialized committees separately, and submit them to the second meeting to be held soon for formal approval, in order to facilitate the work.
    　　　　(Jin Feng)

### 华北高等教育委员会　首次集会讨论学制等问题
金凤
1949-06-07
第1版()
专栏：

　　华北高等教育委员会
  　首次集会讨论学制等问题
    【本报讯】华北高等教育委员会昨（六日）在六国饭店举行第一次会议，到会董必武、张奚若、周扬、马叙伦、李达、许德珩、曾昭抡、吴晗、郭沫若、吴玉章、徐特立、马寅初、范文澜、成仿吾、邓初民、张志让、汤用彤、郑振铎、钱端升、蓝公武、杨秀峰、叶企荪、陈岱荪、陆志韦、张东荪、雷洁琼、黎锦熙、徐悲鸿、李宗恩、严济慈、裴文中、晁哲甫、于力、刘鼎、乐天宇、周泽昭、沈体兰、黄松龄、张宗麟、张国藩、俞大绂、冯乃超等四十二位委员。会上正副主任委员董必武、张奚若说明了高等教育委员会成立的意义与任务。高等教育委员会副主任委员周扬与黄松龄委员，分别报告平津各大学接管以来工作进行情况。继就大学学制、课程改革、私立大学之管理、秋季招生、本期各大学毕业生训练与分配等工作，广泛交换意见，讨论甚为热烈。会议一致同意授权常委会对所讨论诸问题继续研究并分别筹组各种专门委员会，提交将于最近召开的第二次会议正式通过，以利工作进行。
    　　　　（金凤）
