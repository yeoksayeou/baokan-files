Title: Hebei Central Elementary and Secondary Education Delegation Arrives in Beiping
Author: Song Shaozong
Date: 1949-06-15
Page: 2

　　Hebei Central Elementary and Secondary Education Delegation Arrives in Beiping
    [Beiping News] The Hebei Central Elementary and Secondary Education Delegation has arrived in Beiping. This delegation was decided upon by the Education Department of the Hebei Central Administrative Office and is composed of education section chiefs from the 9th and 11th Special Districts, education section chiefs from subordinate counties, normal school principals or teachers, principals of complete primary schools, and experienced primary school teachers. The first group of 93 people arrived in Beiping from Baoding on the morning of June 8th. On the 9th, they set off in groups to visit the No. 1 and No. 2 affiliated schools of the Normal University, the affiliated primary school of the Beijing Normal University, and the Beijing Normal University itself. (Song Shaozong, et al.)

### 冀中小学教育参观团抵平
宋绍宗
1949-06-15
第2版()
专栏：

　　冀中小学教育参观团抵平
    【平市讯】冀中小学教育参观团抵平。该团系由冀中行政公署教育处决定由九、十一两专区教育科长、所属各县教育科长、师范校长或教师、完小校长及有经验的小学教师所组成。首批九十三人，已于六月八日上午由保抵平。九日分组出发，参观了师大附一、附二、北师附小和北师等校。　　　　（宋绍宗等）
