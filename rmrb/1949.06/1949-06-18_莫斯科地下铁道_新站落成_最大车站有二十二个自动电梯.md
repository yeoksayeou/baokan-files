Title: Moscow Subway New Station Completed: Largest Station Has Twenty-Two Escalators
Author:
Date: 1949-06-18
Page: 3

　　Moscow Subway
  　New Station Completed
  　Largest Station Has Twenty-Two Escalators
    [Xinhua News Agency, Peiping, 16th] TASS Moscow: The six new stations of the Moscow subway line along the Kursk Railway Station to Kremlin Square route are nearing completion. These stations will be magnificent underground palaces decorated with various types of marble and granite. Currently, over fifty tunnels are being excavated along the underground ring railway line. In four locations, the tunnels will pass under the Moscow River. Moscow's new underground ring railway will encircle the central area of Moscow for over twenty kilometers and will connect with all existing subway lines. The new railway will have twelve stations. The stations of this underground ring railway are more convenient and spacious than those of other subway lines. The largest station is the Komsomolskaya Station, which will have twenty-two escalators. Once the construction of Moscow's new underground ring railway is completed, Moscow will have forty-one subway stations.

### 莫斯科地下铁道　新站落成　最大车站有二十二个自动电梯

1949-06-18
第3版()
专栏：

　　莫斯科地下铁道
  　新站落成
  　最大车站有二十二个自动电梯
    【新华社北平十六日电】塔斯社莫斯科讯：沿库尔斯克火车站到克里姆林广场路线的莫斯科地下铁道的六个新站，即将落成。这些站将是以各色各样大理石和花岗石装置的宏大的地下宫。现在沿着地下环行铁道线，开掘五十多个隧道。在四个地方，隧道要从莫斯科河底下经过。莫斯科新的地下环行铁道将环绕莫斯科中心地区二十多公里，并将与现在一切地下铁道联接起来。新铁道将有十二个车站。这一地下环行铁道的车站，比其他地下铁道的车站更加方便和宽敞。最大的车站是共产主义青年团车站，将有二十二个自动电梯。新莫斯科地下环行铁道的建设一旦完成，将使莫斯科拥有四十一个地下车站。
