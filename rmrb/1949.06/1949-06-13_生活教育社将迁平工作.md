Title: The Life Education Society Will Move Its Work to Beiping
Author:
Date: 1949-06-13
Page: 2

　　【Beiping News】The directors of the Life Education Society in Beiping met at the Taimiao yesterday morning (the 12th) at 9:00 AM. Present were Fang Yu Yan, Zhang Zonglin, Tao Hong, Zhang Zhirang, Tian Han, An E, Cao Mengjun, Du Junhui, Cheng Jinwu, Cao Jianpei, and others, ten people in total. The meeting decided to move the Life Education Society from Shanghai to Beiping, prepare for the publication of the Life Education Society's journal and the complete works of Mr. Tao Xingzhi; and will hold a memorial for the third anniversary of Mr. Tao Xingzhi's death in July.

### 生活教育社将迁平工作

1949-06-13
第2版()
专栏：

　　生活教育社将迁平工作
    【平市讯】在平之生活教育社理事于昨（十二日）上午九时在太庙集会。到方与严、张宗麟、陶宏、张志让、田汉、安娥、曹孟君、杜君慧、程今吾、曹建培等十人。会中决定将生活教育社由上海迁平，筹备出版生活教育社社刊及陶行知先生全集；并将于七月举行陶行知先生逝世三周年纪念。
