Title: Mexican Progressive Youth Association to Hold Congress
Author:
Date: 1949-06-12
Page: 3
　　
　　Mexican Progressive Youth Association
  　To Hold Representative Congress
    Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, 11th: A dispatch from the Polish Press Agency in Mexico: The "Popular Newspaper" recently published a draft declaration for the Third National Congress of the Mexican Progressive Youth Association, to be held in mid-June. The declaration emphasizes the association's understanding of the serious threats to Mexican sovereignty, but also understands that the forces supporting peace are stronger than the forces of aggression, both in Mexico and abroad. The declaration calls on the youth of Mexico to unite under the banner of peace and strive for a happy future, and to advance under the banner of the World Federation of Democratic Youth.

### 墨进步青年协会将开代表大会

1949-06-12
第3版()
专栏：

　　墨进步青年协会
  　将开代表大会
    【新华社北平十一日电】波兰通讯社墨西哥讯：“大众报”顷发表将在六月中旬举行的墨西哥进步青年协会第三次全国代表大会的宣言草案。宣言强调协会了解墨西哥主权所受到的严重威胁，可是也了解拥护和平的力量在墨西哥和国外都比侵略势力强大。宣言号召墨西哥全国青年在和平的旗帜下团结起来，为争取幸福的将来而奋斗，并在世界民主青年联盟的旗帜下前进。
