Title: US, British, and French Foreign Ministers Continue Secret Talks
Author:
Date: 1949-06-03
Page: 3

　　US, British, and French Foreign Ministers Continue Secret Talks

Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, 2nd: Paris dispatch: After the Soviet Union rejected the proposals made by the US, Britain, and France on the issue of German unification at the Foreign Ministers' Conference, the foreign ministers and their advisors from the US, Britain, and France held secret talks on May 31st. According to the United States Information Service, the meeting "roughly discussed the prospects of the current Foreign Ministers' Conference." The US, British, and French proposals were submitted to the Foreign Ministers' Conference on May 28th. Before submitting them, the three foreign ministers also held secret talks on the morning of the same day.

### 美英法外长续秘密会商

1949-06-03
第3版()
专栏：

　　美英法外长续秘密会商
    【新华社北平二日电】巴黎讯：当苏联在外长会议上拒绝了美英法所提关于德国统一问题的建议之后，美英法三国外长及其顾问于五月三十一日举行秘密会商。据美国新闻处称，此次会商“大致系讨论现行外长会议之展望”问题。美英法建议系于五月二十八日向外长会议提出，在提出之前，该三国外长亦曾于同日上午举行秘密会谈。
