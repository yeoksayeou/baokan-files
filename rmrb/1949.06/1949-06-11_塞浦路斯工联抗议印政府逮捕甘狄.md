Title: Cyprus Workers' Confederation Protests Indian Government's Arrest of Gandhi
Author:
Date: 1949-06-11
Page: 3

　　Cyprus Workers' Confederation
  　Protests Indian Government's Arrest of Gandhi
    Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, June 10th: A dispatch from Cyprus reports that the Cyprus Labour Federation, affiliated with the World Federation of Trade Unions, sent a telegram to the United Nations on the 8th, protesting the Indian government's arrest last month of Gandhi, the acting General Secretary of the All India Trade Union Congress. The Cyprus Labour Federation also requested the United Nations to take immediate action to secure the release of Dange, the chairman of the All India Trade Union Congress, who was arrested previously.

### 塞浦路斯工联抗议印政府逮捕甘狄

1949-06-11
第3版()
专栏：

　　塞浦路斯工联
  　抗议印政府逮捕甘狄
    【新华社北平十日电】塞浦路斯讯：参加世界工联的塞浦路斯劳工联合会八日致电联合国，抗议印度政府于上月逮捕全印职工大会代理总书记甘狄。塞浦路斯劳工联合会并要求联合国立即采取行动，设法释放前此被捕的全印职工大会主席丹吉。
