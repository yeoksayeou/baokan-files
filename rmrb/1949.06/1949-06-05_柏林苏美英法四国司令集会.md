Title: Berlin's Four-Power Meeting of Soviet, US, British, and French Commanders
Author:
Date: 1949-06-05
Page: 3

　　Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, 4th. Reports from various sources: The four commanders of the Soviet Union, the United States, Britain, and France in Berlin held their first meeting in fifty weeks on the afternoon of the 3rd. The content of the discussion was not announced. The meeting was convened at the initiative of the Soviet commander in Berlin, Kotikov, and was held at the headquarters of the French military government.

### 柏林苏美英法四国司令集会

1949-06-05
第3版()
专栏：

　　柏林苏美英法四国司令集会
    【新华社北平四日电】各方报道：柏林苏美英法四国司令于三日下午举行五十个星期以来的首次会议，讨论内容未经宣布。会议系在柏林苏军司令柯提柯夫倡议之下召集，在法国军政府总部举行。
