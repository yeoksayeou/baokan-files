Title: Standing Committee of the World Congress of Advocates of Peace, representing hundreds of millions of people, advocates that major powers maintain agreements and democratically resolve the German question.
Author:
Date: 1949-06-06
Page: 3
　　Xinhua News Agency, Beiping, 5th: Polish News Agency, Warsaw, 4th: The Standing Committee of the World Congress of Advocates of Peace, representing hundreds of millions of people who participated in the congress, sent a declaration to the foreign ministers of the four countries at the Paris Conference. The committee emphasized that all countries wish to see major powers maintain agreements, which will be able to resolve all international problems. The declaration mentioned the enormous sacrifices made by the Allied countries in the war, expressing the desire to see the foreign ministers' meeting resolve the German question on the basis of genuine democratization of Germany.
　　Xinhua News Agency, Beiping, 5th: Reports from various sources: The foreign ministers of the Soviet Union, the United States, Britain, and France held a second secret meeting on the afternoon of the 4th to continue discussing the Soviet and American proposals on the Berlin issue, with US Secretary of State Acheson serving as chairman. As was the case on the 3rd, the foreign ministers of the United States, Britain, and France held a separate meeting before the four-country secret meeting on the 4th.

### 和大常委会代表数亿人民  主张大国保持协议  民主解决德国问题

1949-06-06
第3版()
专栏：

　　和大常委会代表数亿人民
    主张大国保持协议  民主解决德国问题
    【新华社北平五日电】波兰通讯社华沙四日讯：世界拥护和平大会常设委员会，代表参加该大会的数亿人民以宣言一件致送巴黎会议四国外长。委员会强调，各国愿见大国之间保持协议，这将能够解决一切国际问题。宣言提起盟国在大战中所付出的巨大牺牲，表示愿见外长会议在德国真正民主化的基础上解决德国问题。
    【新华社北平五日电】各方报道：苏美英法四国外长于四日下午举行第二次秘密会议，继续讨论苏美两国关于柏林问题的建议，由美国务卿艾奇逊任主席。和三日的情形一样，在四日举行四国的秘密会议之前，美英法外长曾举行了单独的会议。
