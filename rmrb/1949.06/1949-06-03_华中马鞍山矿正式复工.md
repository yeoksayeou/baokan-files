Title: Hua Zhong Ma'anshan Mine Officially Resumes Production
Author:
Date: 1949-06-03
Page: 1

　　Hua Zhong Ma'anshan Mine Officially Resumes Production
    [Broadcast by Beiping Xinhua Broadcasting Station] Nanjing, 2nd – The Ma'anshan Branch Mine of the Hua Zhong Mining Bureau officially resumed production on June 1st. This mine specializes in the production of iron sulfide. In the past, the mine's highest monthly output was 12,000 tons. From May 3rd to the end of the month, the mine's workers assisted the military representatives in taking over the work and prepared for the resumption of production and planned the resumption of production. After discussions by all employees, it has been approved by the Military Control Commission.

### 华中马鞍山矿正式复工

1949-06-03
第1版()
专栏：

　　华中马鞍山矿正式复工
    【北平新华广播电台广播】南京二日消息：华中矿务局马鞍山分矿，六月一日正式复工。该矿专门制造硫化铁，过去该矿最高的月产量是一万二千吨，从五月三日到月的该矿的职工就一面协助军事代表办理接管工作，一面进行复工准备、复工计划，经过全体职工讨论后，已经军管会批准。
