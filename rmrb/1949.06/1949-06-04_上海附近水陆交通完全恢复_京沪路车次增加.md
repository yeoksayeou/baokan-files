Title: Water and Land Transportation Near Shanghai Fully Restored; Number of Trains on Beijing-Shanghai Line Increased
Author:
Date: 1949-06-04
Page: 3

　　[Broadcast by Beiping Xinhua Broadcasting Station] Shanghai, 3rd - The Shanghai and Wusong County ferry services of the Shanghai Ferry Company began operating on the 2nd. So far, water and land transportation near Shanghai has been fully restored.
　　[Xinhua News Agency, Shanghai, 3rd] After the entire Beijing-Shanghai Railway was cleared, the number of train services increased daily. On May 31st, the number of trains traveling west from Shanghai increased to twelve, a threefold increase compared to May 28th (the day after Shanghai's liberation). On the same day, the number of trains traveling east from Nanjing increased to ten, a fivefold increase compared to May 28th.

### 上海附近水陆交通完全恢复　京沪路车次增加

1949-06-04
第3版()
专栏：

　　上海附近水陆交通完全恢复
  　京沪路车次增加
    【北平新华广播电台广播】上海三日消息：上海市轮渡公司的上海、吴淞县班轮，从二日起开始航行。到目前为止，上海市附近的水陆交通，已经完全恢复。
    【新华社上海三日电】京沪路全线畅通后，行车班次日有增加。五月三十一日从上海西行车增至十二班，比二十八日（上海解放之次日）增加两倍，同日从南京东行车增至十班，比二十八日增加四倍。
