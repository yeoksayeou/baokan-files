Title: Shanghai Democratic Figures Zhang Lan and Others Arrive in Beiping Yesterday; CCP Representatives Lin Boqu and Others Go to the Station to Welcome Them
Author:
Date: 1949-06-25
Page: 1

    Shanghai Democratic Figures Zhang Lan and Others Arrive in Beiping Yesterday
    CCP Representatives Lin Boqu and Others Go to the Station to Welcome Them
    [Xinhua News Agency, Beiping, 24th] Shanghai democratic figures Zhang Lan, Shi Liang, Luo Longji, Guo Chuntao, Wang Baozhen and others arrived in Beiping at 4 pm today.  CCP Central Committee representatives Lin Boqu, Dong Biwu, Li Weihan, democratic figures Li Jishen, Shen Junru, Guo Moruo, Zhang Bojun, Tan Pingshan, Peng Zemin, Zhang Xiruo, Zhou Xinmin, Chen Mingshu, Li Dequan, Deng Chumin, Chen Qiyuan, and representatives of the All-China Democratic Women's Federation Kang Keqing, Shen Zijiu, Qu Mengjue and others went to the station to welcome them.

### 上海民主人士张澜等昨抵平  中共代表林伯渠等赴站欢迎

1949-06-25
第1版()
专栏：

    上海民主人士张澜等昨抵平
    中共代表林伯渠等赴站欢迎
    【新华社北平二十四日电】上海民主人士张澜、史良、罗隆基、郭春涛、王葆真等一行，于今下午四时抵平。中共中央代表林伯渠、董必武、李维汉，民主人士李济深、沈钧儒、郭沫若、章伯钧、谭平山、彭泽民、张奚若、周新民、陈铭枢、李德全、邓初民、陈其瑗，及全国民主妇联代表康克清、沈兹九、区梦觉等前往车站欢迎。
