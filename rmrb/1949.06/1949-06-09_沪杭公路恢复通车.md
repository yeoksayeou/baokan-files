Title: Shanghai-Hangzhou Highway Reopens to Traffic
Author:
Date: 1949-06-09
Page: 1

　　Shanghai-Hangzhou Highway Reopens to Traffic
    [Xinhua News Agency, Shanghai, 8th] The Shanghai-Hangzhou Highway was reopened to traffic on the 4th. The eleven bridges on the road that were destroyed by the fleeing Kuomintang bandit troops have been repaired.

### 沪杭公路恢复通车

1949-06-09
第1版()
专栏：

　　沪杭公路恢复通车
    【新华社上海八日电】沪杭公路已于四日起恢复通车。该路上被国民党匪军窜逃时所破坏的十一座桥梁已抢修完竣。
