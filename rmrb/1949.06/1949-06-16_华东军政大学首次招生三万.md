Title: East China Military and Political University Enrolls 30,000 for the First Time
Author:
Date: 1949-06-16
Page: 1

　　Xinhua News Agency, Shanghai, 15th: East China Military and Political University has decided to enroll 30,000 students for the first time in order to meet the needs of revolutionary development and cultivate a large number of military, political, economic, and cultural work cadres. General Chen Yi serves concurrently as the president of the university, with campuses located in Nanjing and Suzhou. The university is divided into undergraduate and preparatory courses. The undergraduate courses are divided into military affairs, politics, culture, finance and economics, medical services, and other technical arms specialties. The university will set up enrollment offices in Shanghai, Suzhou, Zhenjiang, Wuxi, Changshu, Hangzhou, Ningbo, Jinhua, Jiaxing, Wenzhou and other places to conduct enrollment. The study time at the university is tentatively set at half a year for the preparatory course and one and a half years for the undergraduate course. The school provides food, lodging, clothing, and learning materials for the enrolled students. Graduates who pass the examination at the end of the study period will be recommended by the school to work in the People's Liberation Army or local governments, depending on the circumstances.

### 华东军政大学首次招生三万

1949-06-16
第1版()
专栏：

　　华东军政大学首次招生三万
    【新华社上海十五日电】华东军政大学决定首次招收学员三万名，以适应革命发展需要，大量培养军事、政治、经济、文化工作干部。该校校长由陈毅将军兼任，校址分设南京、苏州。该校分本科、预科。本科分军事、政治、文化、财政经济、医务等科及其他技术兵种特科。该校将分别在上海、苏州、镇江、无锡、常熟、杭州、宁波、金华、嘉兴、温州等地设立招生办事处，进行招生。该校学习时间预科暂定半年，本科暂定一年半。入学学员的膳宿、服装、学习用品均由学校供给。学习期满成绩及格者，由校方酌情介绍到人民解放军或地方政府工作。
