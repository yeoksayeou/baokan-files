Title: Huang Yanpei and Others Arrive in Shanghai; Vice Mayor Pan and Others Welcome Them at the Station
Author:
Date: 1949-06-27
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, Shanghai, June 26th—Huang Yanpei, Ma Yinchu, Chen Shutong, Sheng Pihua, Bao Dasan, Deng Yingchao, Zhang Qinqiu, Deng Yuzhi, and other members of the Preparatory Committee of the New Political Consultative Conference arrived in Shanghai on the 25th. Pan Hannian, Vice Mayor of the Shanghai People's Government, Sha Qianli, Deputy Secretary-General, Zhang Hanfu, Director of the Foreign Affairs Office, Xia Yan, Deputy Director of the Cultural and Educational Management Committee of the Military Control Commission, and representatives of democratic parties such as Leng Yuqiu and Hu Juewen from the China Democratic National Construction Association and the Chinese Vocational Education Society, as well as Zhang Yun and Huang Jingwen from women's circles, were at the station to welcome them.

### 黄炎培等抵沪　潘副市长等到站欢迎

1949-06-27
第1版()
专栏：

　　黄炎培等抵沪
  　潘副市长等到站欢迎
    【新华社上海二十六日电】新政治协商会议筹备委员黄炎培、马寅初、陈叔通、盛丕华、包达三、邓颖超、张琴秋、邓裕志等一行，已于二十五日到达上海。上海人民市政府副市长潘汉年、副秘书长沙千里、外事处长章汉夫，军管会文化教育管理委员会副主任夏衍，民主建国会、中华职教社等民主党派代表冷御秋、胡厥文，妇女界章蕴、黄静汶等，都到车站欢迎。
