Title: Shanghai-Hangzhou Railway Now Open to Traffic; Shanghai Customs Resumes Operations
Author:
Date: 1949-06-16
Page: 1

　　Shanghai-Hangzhou Railway Now Open to Traffic
    Shanghai Customs Resumes Operations
    [Xinhua News Agency, Shanghai, 15th] The Shanghai-Hangzhou Railway, which was severely damaged by the Kuomintang bandit troops, has been opened to traffic on the 12th after day and night repairs by the railway staff. Currently, only a few large iron bridges between Songjiang and Fengjing have not been completed, and temporary ferry transportation is used. Now it is possible to reach Hangzhou from Shanghai on the same day, with two trains running up and down each day.
    [Broadcast by Xinhua Broadcasting Station, Beiping] Shanghai, 14th: The Shanghai Jianghai Customs has resumed operations after being taken over by the Military Control Commission. From the 4th to the 8th of this month, it collected a total of 120 million yuan in taxes (People's currency), and seized a batch of smuggled goods on the British merchant ship Shengjing.

### 沪杭铁路业已通车  沪江海关恢复工作

1949-06-16
第1版()
专栏：

　　沪杭铁路业已通车
    沪江海关恢复工作
    【新华社上海十五日电】曾被国民党匪军严重破坏的沪杭铁路，经该路员工日夜抢修，已于十二日通车。现仅淞江与枫泾间几座大铁桥尚未完工，暂用轮船接运办法。现自上海至杭州当天即可到达，每日开行上下行列车各二次。
    【北平新华广播电台广播】上海十四号消息：上海江海关经军管会接管以后，已经恢复工作，从本月四号到八号，一共收入了税款人民币一万万二千万元，并且在英商盛京轮上查获了私货一批。
