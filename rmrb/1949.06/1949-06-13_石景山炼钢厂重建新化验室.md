Title: Shijingshan Steel Plant Rebuilds New Laboratory
Author:
Date: 1949-06-13
Page: 2

　　Shijingshan Steel Plant Rebuilds New Laboratory
    [Report from this newspaper] After the fire destroyed the laboratory of the Shijingshan Iron and Steel Plant, the workers of the plant automatically processed and rushed to work. Now, three brand-new laboratories have been rebuilt. The plan to produce iron on "July 1st" can be achieved on schedule. (Chi)

### 石景山炼钢厂重建新化验室

1949-06-13
第2版()
专栏：

　　石景山炼钢厂重建新化验室
    【本报讯】石景山钢铁厂自化验室失火焚毁后，经该厂职工自动加工赶活，现已重新建好三间崭新的化验室，“七一”出铁的计划，当可按期做到。（炽）
