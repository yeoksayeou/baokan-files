Title: Tonight's Program on Beiping Xinhua Broadcasting Station
Author:
Date: 1949-06-24
Page: 1

    Beiping Xinhua Broadcasting Station Tonight's Program
    [This newspaper's report] Beiping Xinhua Broadcasting Station's lecture program from 19:50 to 20:10 tonight will be given by Zhang Pei, former Major General Director of the Research Department and concurrently Dean of Academics of the Kuomintang Army Mechanized School, and former Lieutenant General Director of the Armored Tactics Department and concurrently Director of the General Officer Class of the Kuomintang Army University. The international affairs program from 21:15 to 21:30 will introduce the New Democratic countries of Eastern Europe. The arts program from 22:20 to 22:50 will feature the Beiping Youth Art Troupe singing six songs: 1. Support Chairman Mao, 2. Unite and Advance, 3. Crossing the Yangtze River, 4. Vanguard of the Train, 5. Worker Liberation, 6. I Love New China.

### 北平新华广播电台今晚节目

1949-06-24
第1版()
专栏：

    北平新华广播电台今晚节目
    【本报讯】北平新华广播电台今晚十九点五十分到二十点十分演讲节目，由前国民党陆军机械化学校研究处少将处长兼教务长，前国民党陆军大学机甲战术系中将主任兼将官班主任章培演讲。二十一点十五分到二十一点三十分国际时事节目，介绍东欧的新民主主义国家。二十二点二十分到二十二点五十分文艺节目，由北平市青年文艺工作团演唱六支歌曲：一、拥护毛主席，二、团结前进，三、过长江，四、列车的先锋，五、工人解放，六、我爱新中国。
