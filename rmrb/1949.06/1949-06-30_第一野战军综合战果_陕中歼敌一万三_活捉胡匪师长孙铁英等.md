Title: Comprehensive Battle Results of the First Field Army: 13,000 Enemies Annihilated in Central Shaanxi, Including the Capture of Bandit Commander Sun Tieying
Author:
Date: 1949-06-30
Page: 1

　　Comprehensive Battle Results of the First Field Army
  　13,000 Enemies Annihilated in Central Shaanxi
  　Including the Capture of Bandit Commander Sun Tieying
    [Broadcast from Beiping Xinhua Broadcasting Station] Front Lines in Central Shaanxi, 29th: The Headquarters of the People's Liberation Army First Field Army announced the comprehensive battle results of annihilating the Hu (referring to Hui Muslims) and Ma (referring to Ma Bufang's forces) bandit troops from the beginning of June to the 25th: A total of 13,181 enemies were annihilated, including 9,771 enemy troops killed or wounded. It has been verified that Han Youlu, commander of the Ma bandit's 248th Division, and Ma Qing, commander of the 503rd Regiment of the 168th Division, were killed. Hu bandit Sun Tieying, commander of the 165th Division, and 3,410 other enemy officers and soldiers were captured alive. In addition, 368 enemy troops revolted. A total of 47 artillery pieces, 184 light and heavy machine guns, 1,516 rifles and carbines, more than 2,460 artillery shells, more than 147,000 bullets of various types, and a portion of other military supplies were seized.

### 第一野战军综合战果　陕中歼敌一万三　活捉胡匪师长孙铁英等

1949-06-30
第1版()
专栏：

　　第一野战军综合战果
  　陕中歼敌一万三
  　活捉胡匪师长孙铁英等
    【北平新华广播电台广播】陕中前线二十九日消息：人民解放军第一野战军司令部公布六月初旬到二十五日歼灭胡马匪军的综合战果：共计歼灭敌人一万三千一百八十一名，其中打死打伤敌军九千七百七十一名，已经查明打死马匪二百四十八师师长韩有禄，和一百六十八师五百零三团团长马清，活捉胡匪一百六十五师师长孙铁英等以下敌军官兵三千四百一十名。此外，敌军起义的有三百六十八名。缴获各种炮四十七门，轻重机枪一百八十四挺，步马枪等一千五百一十六支，各种炮弹两千四百六十多发，各种子弹十四万七千多发，和其他军用物资一部。
