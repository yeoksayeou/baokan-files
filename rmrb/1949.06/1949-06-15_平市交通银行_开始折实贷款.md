Title: Traffic Bank of Ping City Begins Loaning Based on Real Value

Author:
Date: 1949-06-15
Page: 2

The Traffic Bank of Ping City
　　Begins Loaning Based on Real Value
　　[This Newspaper] Ping City Traffic Bank concluded its general industrial and mining loans in early June, and began focusing on supporting production through methods of "real value" and "fixed order." On the 13th, it handled the first real value loan for a private enterprise. The loan recipient was the Dazhong Dyeing and Weaving Factory, with a loan amount of RMB 3 million. Repayment is calculated based on the standard physical unit of the Savings Department of the People's Bank of Peiping Branch, with a term of four months and a monthly interest rate of seven per mille. The purpose is to purchase six more looms to meet the needs of expanded production.
　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　(Yu)

### 平市交通银行　开始折实贷款

1949-06-15
第2版()
专栏：

　　平市交通银行
  　开始折实贷款
    【本报讯】平市交通银行于六月初结束一般工矿放款，采用折实和定货两种方式重点扶植生产后，十三日对私营企业办理了第一笔折实贷款。贷款户是大众染织厂，款额人民币三百万元，借还均以人民银行北平分行储蓄部标准实物单位计算，期限四个月，月息七厘，用途系添购织布机六台，以应扩大生产之需。 　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　（宇）
