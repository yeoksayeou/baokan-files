Folk Method for Treating Aphids

Add one jin of tobacco leaf scraps to ten jin of cold water, and soak for a day and a night; separately, slowly add one and a half jin of lime to ten jin of cold water, stirring to dissolve. Filter the residue from each solution, then mix the two solutions together, add another forty jin of cold water and mix well. Use a sprayer to spray or use a bowl or basin to hold the water and dip the cotton plants.

(Zhaoyang)

### 治蚜土法
朝阳
1949-06-22
第2版()
专栏：

    治蚜土法
    烟叶末一斤加冷水十斤，泡一天一夜；另用石灰一斤半，徐徐加入冷水十斤搅拌溶化，各滤去渣子，然后把两种水混合一起，再加入冷水四十斤拌匀，用喷雾器喷或用碗盆盛水沾棉棵均可。           
        （朝阳）
