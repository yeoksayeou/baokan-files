Title: Soviet and American Foreign Ministers Leave France to Return Home
Author:
Date: 1949-06-23
Page: 3

    Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, 22nd: Reports from various sources indicate that Soviet Foreign Minister Vyshinsky left Paris on the 21st to return home. U.S. Secretary of State Acheson flew to Washington on the night of the 20th.

### 苏美外长离法返国

1949-06-23
第3版()
专栏：

    苏美外长离法返国
    【新华社北平二十二日电】各方报道：苏外长维辛斯基已于二十一日离巴黎返国。美国务卿艾奇逊，则已于二十日夜即飞往华盛顿。
