Title: Henan Daily and Jiangxi Daily Launched Successively
Author:
Date: 1949-06-09
Page: 1

Henan Daily was launched on June 1st. The Xinhua News Agency Henan Branch was also established at the same time. Furthermore, after the Central Plains Bureau of the CPC Central Committee and the Central Plains Military Region were abolished, the Central Plains Daily ceased publication on May 31st. Jiangxi Daily was launched on the 7th. The original Nanchang News ceased publication after 13 issues.

### 河南日报江西日报先后创刊

1949-06-09
第1版()
专栏：

　　河南日报江西日报先后创刊
    河南日报于六月一日创刊。新华社河南省分社也同时成立。又中共中央中原局与中原军区撤销后，中原日报已于五月三十一日终刊。江西日报已于七日创刊，原南昌新闻已于出版十三期后停刊。
