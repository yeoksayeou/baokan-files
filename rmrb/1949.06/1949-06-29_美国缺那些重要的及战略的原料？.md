Title: What Important and Strategic Raw Materials Does the United States Lack?
Author:
Date: 1949-06-29
Page: 3

Answering the Question of the Times

　　What Important and Strategic Raw Materials Does the United States Lack?
    Reply to the 6th Group of the 5th Team of the Railway Department Study Class of the Military Commission:
    The strategic and important raw materials that the United States lacks are: antimony, chromium, coconut shell charcoal, manganese, manila fiber, mercury, marble, nickel, crystal, quinine, rubber, silk, tin, tungsten, aluminum, asbestos, cork, graphite, cowhide, iodine, opium, kapok, platinum, carbolic acid, wool, vanadium, Toluene, etc.
    　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　——Editor

### 美国缺那些重要的及战略的原料？

1949-06-29
第3版()
专栏：时事问答

　　美国缺那些重要的及战略的原料？
    复军委铁道部学习班第五中队第六组：
    美国所缺少的战略的及重要原料，为：锑、铬、椰子壳炭、锰、孟尼拉纤维，水银、云石、镍、水晶、金鸡纳霜、橡皮、丝、锡、钨、铝、石绵、软木、石墨、牛皮、碘、鸦片、树丝棉、白金、石炭酸、羊毛、钒、Ｔｏｌｏｎｌ等。
