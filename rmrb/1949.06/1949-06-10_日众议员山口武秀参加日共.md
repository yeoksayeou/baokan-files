Title: Japanese Diet Member Takehide Yamaguchi Joins the Japanese Communist Party
Author:
Date: 1949-06-10
Page: 3

　　Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, 9th: According to a Reuters dispatch from Tokyo: Japanese Diet member and former Labor-Farmer Party member Takehide Yamaguchi announced on the 6th that he had joined the Japanese Communist Party. As a result, the JCP's seats in the House of Representatives have increased from 35 to 36.

### 日众议员山口武秀参加日共

1949-06-10
第3版()
专栏：

　　日众议员山口武秀参加日共
    【新华社北平九日电】据路透社东京讯：日本众议员、前工农党党员山口武秀六日宣布他已加入日本共产党。因此，日共在众院的议席已由三十五席增至三十六席。
