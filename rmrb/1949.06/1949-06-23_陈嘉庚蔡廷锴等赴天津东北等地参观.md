Title: Chen Jiageng, Cai Tingkai, and Others Visit Tianjin and Northeast China
Author:
Date: 1949-06-23
Page: 1
    Chen Jiageng, Cai Tingkai, and Others to Visit Tianjin and Northeast China

    [Ping City News] Chen Jiageng and Cai Tingkai, members of the Standing Committee of the Preparatory Committee of the New Political Consultative Conference, left Ping for Tianjin on the afternoon of the 22nd. After visiting various factories and municipal construction in Tianjin, Mr. Chen will proceed to various places in Northeast China for inspection, and then return to Peiping. Those traveling with Mr. Chen include Zhuang Mingli, representative of the Preparatory Committee of the New Political Consultative Conference and overseas Chinese democrat, as well as overseas Chinese businessmen Wang Yuting and Zhang Chuming.

### 陈嘉庚蔡廷锴等赴天津东北等地参观

1949-06-23
第1版()
专栏：

    陈嘉庚蔡廷锴等
    赴天津东北等地参观
    【平市讯】新政治协商会议筹备会常务委员陈嘉庚、蔡廷锴于二十二日下午离平赴津。陈氏在津参观各工厂及市政建设后，即转赴东北各地考察，然后返回北平。与陈氏同行者尚有新政治协商会议筹备会代表华侨民主人士庄明理及侨商王雨亭、张楚鸣等。
