Title: Taiyuan People's Bank Issues Agricultural Loans to Suburbs
Author:
Date: 1949-06-01
Page: 2

　　Taiyuan People's Bank Issues Agricultural Loans to Suburbs
    [Taiyuan Electric] The Taiyuan Branch of the People's Bank of China has issued 2.5 million yuan in production loans to the suburbs of the city. The purpose of this loan is to help suburban farmers quickly restore and develop agricultural production, a large part of which will be used to purchase livestock and build water conservancy projects. The loan is due to be repaid by the autumn at the latest, and can be repaid in full or in installments. The interest rate is seven percent per month. When making the loan, the Taiyuan Branch will directly send people to the districts and villages to cooperate with the district government in issuing the loans during the organization of production. Currently, the bank dispatched eight loan officers on May 23rd, each carrying funds to the respective districts.

### 太原人民银行发放郊区农贷

1949-06-01
第2版()
专栏：经济新闻

　　太原人民银行发放郊区农贷
    【太原电】中国人民银行太原分行向市郊区发放二百五十万元生产贷款。此项贷款目的在于帮助郊区农民很快恢复与发展农业生产，其中将有大部贷款用于购买牲口与兴修水利。贷款最迟在秋后归还，归还时整借整还或整借零还均可，利息为月息七厘。贷款时由太原分行直接派人到区村协同区政府在组织生产中进行发放。现该行已于五月二十三日派出办理贷款员八人，分别携款到达各区。
