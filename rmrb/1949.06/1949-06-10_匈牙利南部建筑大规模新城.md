Title: Large-Scale New City Being Built in Southern Hungary
Author:
Date: 1949-06-10
Page: 3

　　Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, 9th: A new city is under construction north of Mohács, a city on the southern border of Hungary. The scale of this project is unprecedented in Hungary. A highway is being built between Békés and Mohács in southern Hungary, and the railway will also be extended to the new city.

### 匈牙利南部建筑大规模新城

1949-06-10
第3版()
专栏：

　　匈牙利南部建筑大规模新城
    【新华社北平九日电】布达佩斯讯：匈牙利南部边境莫哈克斯城之北正在建设新城。其工程之浩大，在匈牙利尚属空前。匈南部贝克斯与莫哈克斯间公路正在建筑中，铁路也将延长至新城。
