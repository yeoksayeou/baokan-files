Title: Pushkin's 150th Anniversary of Birth Celebrated Enthusiastically by People Across the Soviet Union. Evening Parties and Concerts Held in Various Places
Author:
Date: 1949-06-08
Page: 3
Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, 7th: Moscow News: June 6th marked the 150th anniversary of the birth of the great Russian poet Pushkin, and people across the Soviet Union enthusiastically commemorated this festival. On the 5th, Moscow held a mass commemorative meeting in the sun-drenched Pushkin Square, attended by representatives of Moscow workers, staff, and intellectuals, delegations from various Soviet republics, and foreign writers. Nikolai Tikhonov, Deputy Secretary-General of the Soviet Writers' Union, delivered a speech saying: Pushkin expressed the spirit and soul of the Russian people, and his artistic genius will forever be our glory. The people of all ethnic groups in the Soviet Union cherish his name.

　　Commemorative evenings and concerts were held in Leningrad, Kiev, Riga, Tallinn, and many other cities and towns in the Soviet Union. More than 700 reciters and singers in various places recited and sang Pushkin's works. The new film "Pushkin," produced to commemorate the 150th anniversary of this great poet's birth, has been screened in various places since the 6th.

### 普希金百五十周年诞辰　全苏人民热烈纪念  各地都举行了晚会和音乐会

1949-06-08
第3版()
专栏：

  　普希金百五十周年诞辰　全苏人民热烈纪念
    各地都举行了晚会和音乐会
    【新华社北平七日电】莫斯科讯：六月六日是伟大的俄罗斯诗人普希金一百五十周年诞辰纪念日，全苏人民都热烈纪念这个节日。五日，莫斯科在阳光普照的普希金广场上举行了群众纪念大会，参加的有莫斯科工人职员和知识分子的代表、苏联各共和国代表团及外国作家。苏联作家协会副总书记吉洪诺夫曾发表讲演说：普希金表现了俄罗斯人民的心灵与灵魂，他的艺术天才永远是我国的光荣，苏联各族人民都珍爱他的名字。
    在列宁格勒、基辅、里加、塔林以及苏联其他许多城乡，都举行了纪念晚会和音乐会。各地共有七百多名朗诵家和歌手朗诵和歌唱普希金的作品。为纪念这一伟大诗人一百五十周年诞辰而摄制的新片“普希金”，六日起已在各地放映。
