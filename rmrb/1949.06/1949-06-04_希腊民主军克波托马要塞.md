Title: Greek Democratic Army Captures the Key Fortress of Potoma
Author:
Date: 1949-06-04
Page: 3

Xinhua News Agency, Peiping, 3rd: Free Greece Radio broadcast: A communique from the headquarters of the Greek Democratic Army forces operating in the Grammos Mountains states: After eleven hours of fierce fighting on May 30th, the Democratic Army captured the strong defensive position of the Royalist Fascist army – the Potoma Fortress. Many enemies were annihilated and much war materiel was captured.

### 希腊民主军克波托马要塞

1949-06-04
第3版()
专栏：

　　希腊民主军克波托马要塞
    【新华社北平三日电】自由希腊电台广播：活动于格拉姆斯山区的希腊民主军部队司令部公报称：民主军于五月三十日经十一小时激烈战斗之后，占领保皇法西斯军强固的防务阵地——波托马要塞。歼敌和缴获战利品甚多。
