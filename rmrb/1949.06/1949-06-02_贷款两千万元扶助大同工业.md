Title: Loan of 20 Million Yuan to Support Datong Industry
Author:
Date: 1949-06-02
Page: 2

　　Economic News

　　Loan of 20 Million Yuan to Support Datong Industry
　　[Chahar Electric Dispatch] In order to support Datong in rapidly restoring and developing industrial production, the Chahar Branch of the People's Bank of China recently allocated a loan of 20 million yuan to Datong enterprises. In addition, it has been decided to issue small production loans of 2 million to 3 million yuan to citizens who have production conditions but lack funds. An investigation is currently underway and the loans will be issued soon.

### 贷款两千万元扶助大同工业

1949-06-02
第2版()
专栏：经济新闻

　　贷款两千万元扶助大同工业
    【察哈尔电】为扶助大同迅速恢复与发展工业生产，中国人民银行察哈尔分行，近拨给大同企业贷款两千万元。此外，对有生产条件而缺乏资金的市民，决定发放二百万至三百万元的小本生产贷款。现正进行调查，不久即可发放。
