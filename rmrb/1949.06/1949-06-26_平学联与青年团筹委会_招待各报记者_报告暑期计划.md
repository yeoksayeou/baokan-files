Title: Pingxue Lian and Youth League Preparatory Committee Host Newspaper Reporters to Report on Summer Plans
Author:
Date: 1949-06-26
Page: 2

　　Pingxue Lian and Youth League Preparatory Committee
  　Host Newspaper Reporters to Report on Summer Plans
    [Our Newspaper's Report] The Beiping City Student Union and the Youth League Preparatory Committee hosted reporters from various newspapers at 3:00 PM yesterday (the 25th) to report on summer work plans and to discuss reporting and communication issues related to youth learning during the summer. The basic spirit of the summer work plan is: (1) To adapt to the learning needs of the vast number of young students, to meet their learning aspirations as much as possible, and to enable everyone to develop their own revolutionary outlook on life through learning, work, and various activities. (2) To fully promote the spirit of democratic unity. Participation in learning or work is based on the conscious and voluntary participation of the vast number of students. Free research, discussion, and questioning are encouraged. (3) To strive for lively, vivid, and diverse content.
    Another report: The summer youth study garden is scheduled for registration from June 27th to July 2nd. Students from various schools can register with the school's student union. The registration fee is set at twenty yuan.

### 平学联与青年团筹委会　招待各报记者　报告暑期计划

1949-06-26
第2版()
专栏：

　　平学联与青年团筹委会
  　招待各报记者　报告暑期计划
    【本报讯】北平市学生联合会、青年团筹委会于昨日（二十五）下午三时招待各报记者，报告暑期工作计划，并商讨暑期青年学习的报导与通讯联系问题。暑期工作计划的基本精神是：（一）适应广大青年同学的学习要求，尽可能满足其学习愿望，使大家在学习、工作及各种活动中锻炼自己的革命人生观。（二）充分发扬民主团结的精神。参加学习或工作，都是建立在广大同学自觉自愿的基础上。提倡自由研究、讨论与质疑。（三）内容力求活泼生动，多种多样。
    又讯：暑期青年学园订于六月二十七日至七月二日为报名期，各校同学可向该校学生会报名，规定报名费二十元。
