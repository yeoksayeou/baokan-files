Title: Second Tianjin-Beiping News Work Conference: Comrade Peng Zhen Attends and Speaks
Author:
Date: 1949-06-15
Page: 1

　　Second Tianjin-Beiping News Work Conference
  　Comrade Peng Zhen Attends and Speaks
    [This Newspaper’s Dispatch] The second Tianjin-Beiping News Work Conference was held at Xihua Hall, Beiping Municipal Government, on the afternoon of the 14th. More than 30 news workers from various newspapers, news agencies, and radio stations in Tianjin and Beiping attended. At this conference, Comrade Peng Zhen, Secretary of the Beiping Municipal Committee of the Communist Party of China, reported to the attendees on the work of the Beiping Municipal Committee of the Communist Party of China since the liberation of Beiping and the work plan for the recent period. He also put forward three requirements for the news work in Beiping: prudence, truthfulness, and timeliness. The conference exchanged views on the issue of preparing for the National News Work Conference.

### 平津新闻工作二次会议　彭真同志莅会讲话

1949-06-15
第1版()
专栏：

　　平津新闻工作二次会议
  　彭真同志莅会讲话
    【本报讯】平津新闻工作会议于十四日下午假北平市政府西花厅举行第二次会议。到平津各报社、通讯社、广播电台等单位新闻工作者三十余人。这次会议邀请中共北平市委会书记彭真同志向到会者报告了中共北平市委会在北平解放以来的工作情况和最近时期的工作计划，并对北平的新闻工作提出了三项要求，即谨慎、真实和适时。会议对筹备全国新闻工作会议问题，曾交换意见。
