Title: All-China Federation of Trade Unions Decides to Convene National Railway Delegates Conference on July 1st to Prepare for the Restoration of the National Railway Trade Union
Author:
Date: 1949-06-11
Page: 1

　　All-China Federation of Trade Unions Decides
  　To Convene National Railway Delegates Conference on July 1st
  　To Prepare for the Restoration of the National Railway Trade Union
    [Special Dispatch from This Newspaper] The All-China Federation of Trade Unions, accepting the suggestion of the Northeast Railway Workers' General Union, has decided to convene a National Railway Workers' Provisional Representative Conference on "July 1st" to prepare for the restoration of the National Railway Trade Union. The original text of the decision is as follows: The All-China Federation of Trade Unions' decision on preparing for the restoration of the National Railway Trade Union: Fully agrees with the Northeast Railway Workers' General Union's suggestion to convene a National Railway Provisional Representative Conference to prepare for the restoration of the National Railway Trade Union, and makes the following specific decisions: (1) The National Railway Workers' Provisional Representative Conference will be held in Beiping on July 1st of this year. (2) The main agenda of the Provisional Representative Conference is: to discuss the issue of the responsibility system in railway work, the issue of democratization of factory management, to formulate a draft of the National Railway Trade Union Constitution, and to elect a preparatory committee. (3) The number of representatives shall be determined by the local railway general trade unions or their preparatory committees, with one representative elected for every 5,000 employees, and directly subordinate units of the Ministry of Railways with fewer than 5,000 employees may also elect one representative. (4) The elected representatives should include heads of local railway general trade unions or their preparatory committees, labor heroes, model workers, and other prestigious outstanding employees; (5) Representatives from various regions should arrive in Beiping before June 25th to report on the situation and exchange experiences before the meeting; (6) After receiving the notice, the railway trade unions or their preparatory committees in various regions must make full preparations and write the status and opinions on railway construction, production, operations, and employee welfare, wages, education, publicity, organization, and other issues into written materials and bring them with the representatives.
  　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　June 10th

### 中华全国总工会决定　七一召开全国铁代会　筹备恢复铁路总工会

1949-06-11
第1版()
专栏：

　　中华全国总工会决定
  　七一召开全国铁代会
  　筹备恢复铁路总工会
    【本报特讯】中华全国总工会接受东北铁路职工总会的建议，决定于“七一”召开全国铁路职工临时代表会议，筹备恢复全国铁路总工会，决定原文如下：中华全国总工会关于筹备恢复全国铁路总工会的决定：完全同意东北铁路职工总会关于召开全国铁路临时代表会议，筹备恢复全国铁路总工会的建议，并作如下之具体决定：（一）定于本年七月一日在北平召开全国铁路职工临时代表会议。（二）临时代表会议的主要议程为：讨论铁路工作中的负责制问题，工厂管理民主化问题，制定全国铁路总工会章程草案，选举筹备委员会。（三）代表名额由各地铁路总工会或其筹委会，按职工五千人推选代表一人，五千人以下之铁道部直属单位亦得推选代表一人。（四）推选的代表中，应包括各地铁路总工会或其筹委会负责人、劳动英雄、模范工作者及其他有威信的优秀职工等；（五）各地代表应于六月二十五日前到达北平，以便于开会前汇报情况，交换经验；（六）各地铁路工会或其筹委会于接获通知后，务须充分准备，将有关铁路建设、生产、业务及职工福利、工资、教育、宣传、组织等问题的状况和意见，写成书面材料，交各地代表带来。
  　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　六月十日
