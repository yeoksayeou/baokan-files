Title: Da Zhonghua Record Company Records Songs from Liberated Areas
Author:
Date: 1949-06-05
Page: 1

[North China Xinhua Broadcasting Station Broadcast] Shanghai, 4th – The Da Zhonghua Record Company, taken over by the Shanghai Military Control Commission's Cultural and Educational Management Committee's News and Publication Office, resumed work on the 3rd of this month. On the first day, they recorded seven types of records of songs from the liberated areas, namely: "The Sky in the Liberated Area," "The Army Marches Forward," "Sing of Victory," "Chiang Kai-shek's Soldiers are a Complete Mess," "Salute to the People's Liberation Army," "Twisting Towards New China," and "Happy Countryside." These seven records will be broadcast on various radio stations. Today, they are preparing to continue recording two records, "Brave Team" and "Great March."

### 大中华唱片厂灌制解放区歌曲

1949-06-05
第1版()
专栏：

　　大中华唱片厂灌制解放区歌曲
    【北平新华广播电台广播】上海四日消息：上海市军管会文化教育管理委员会新闻出版处接管的大中华唱片厂，已经在本月三日恢复工作。第一天已经灌制解放区歌曲唱片七种，就是：“解放区的天”、“军队向前进”、“唱胜利”、“蒋家兵呀一团糟”、“向人民解放军致敬”、“扭向新中国”、“快乐的农村”，这七种唱片就要在各广播电台播送，今天准备继续灌制“勇敢队”、“大进军”等两张唱片。
