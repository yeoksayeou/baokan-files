Title: On the Eve of the Bandit Gang's Demise in Shanghai: Huang Jingwu and Others Murdered; Bodies Discovered
Author:
Date: 1949-06-16
Page: 1

　　On the Eve of the Bandit Gang's Demise in Shanghai
  　Huang Jingwu and Others Murdered
  　Bodies Discovered
    [Xinhua News Agency, Shanghai, 15th] Democratic figures Huang Jingwu, Zeng Wei, Yu Jian, and others were murdered by the Kuomintang bandit gang on the eve of Shanghai's liberation. Their bodies have now been discovered. Huang Jingwu was a member of the China Democratic League and the second son of Huang Yanpei, one of the leaders of the Democratic League. He previously served as the secretary of the Foreign Relations Committee of the Democratic League and a member of the Shanghai Municipal Branch Committee. Zeng Wei was a member of the Organization Committee of the Democratic League and the Deputy Minister of the Organization Department of the Chinese Peasants' and Workers' Democratic Party Central Committee, as well as the person in charge of the Shanghai Municipal Branch. Yu Jian was the person in charge of civil movement work at the Shanghai Municipal Party Department of the Chinese Peasants' and Workers' Democratic Party.

### 沪匪帮覆灭前夕　杀害黄竞武等　尸体已被发现

1949-06-16
第1版()
专栏：

　　沪匪帮覆灭前夕
  　杀害黄竞武等
  　尸体已被发现
    【新华社上海十五日电】民主人士黄竞武、曾伟、余健等三人于上海解放前夕为国民党匪帮杀害，现尸体已被发现。黄竞武为中国民主同盟盟员，民主同盟领导者之一黄炎培的次子，曾任民盟对外关系委员会秘书、上海市支部委员等职。曾伟为民盟组织委员会委员，并为农工民主党中央组织部副部长及上海市支部负责人。余健为农工民主党上海市党部民运工作负责人。
