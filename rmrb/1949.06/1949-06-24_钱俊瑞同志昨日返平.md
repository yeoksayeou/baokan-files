Title: Comrade Qian Junrui Returned to Beiping Yesterday
Author: 
Date: 1949-06-24
Page: 1

    Comrade Qian Junrui Returned to Beiping Yesterday
    [This newspaper dispatch] Comrade Qian Junrui, Secretary-General of the Chinese Delegation to the World Peace Congress, returned to Beiping at 8:00 PM on the 23rd.
