Title: The Pingjin Southward Work Group Has Arrived in Wuhan
Author:
Date: 1949-06-02
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Hankou, 1st. The Pingjin Universities and Colleges Teachers and Students Southward Work Group, consisting of Professor Sheng Chenghua and 55 others, arrived in Wuhan on the 25th of last month to prepare for participation in the takeover work. Pan Zinian and Wang Lanxi, the two ministers of the Cultural Takeover Department of the Wuhan Military Control Commission, made a special trip to welcome them on the afternoon of the 27th and gave them encouragement.

### 平津南下工作团已到达武汉

1949-06-02
第1版()
专栏：

　　平津南下工作团已到达武汉
    【新华社汉口一日电】平津各校师生南下工作团盛澄华教授等五十六人，已于上月二十五日到达武汉，准备参加接管工作。武汉市军管会文化接管部潘梓年和王阑西两部长，特于二十七日下午前往欢迎，并加勉励。
