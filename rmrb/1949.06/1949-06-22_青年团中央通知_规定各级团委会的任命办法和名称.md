Title: Central Committee of the Youth League Notification Regarding Appointment Methods and Names of Youth League Committees at All Levels
Author:
Date: 1949-06-22
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, Peiping, June 20th. The Central Committee of the China New Democratic Youth League issued a notification on June 15th, stipulating the appointment methods and names of the Youth League work committees at all levels. The full text of the notification is as follows:

    (1) The convening of the First National Congress of the China New Democratic Youth League in April of this year formally announced the establishment of the China New Democratic Youth League. All regions that have not yet established a Youth League, or have not yet elected leadership organizations of the Youth League at all levels through representative congresses from the bottom up, should establish Youth League work committees to carry out the work of establishing the League in their respective regions.

    (2) The appointment methods for members of the New Democratic Youth League work committees at all levels are stipulated as follows: The members of the Youth League work committees of all regions, large provinces, and cities equivalent to large provinces shall be directly appointed by the Central Committee of the Youth League; the members of the Youth League work committees at all levels below the region and province shall be approved and appointed by the next higher level of the Youth League committee, and the two higher levels of the Youth League committee shall be notified for the record.

    (3) The names of the New Democratic Youth League committees at all levels are stipulated as follows:

    I. Regional level: Should be called the China New Democratic Youth League ×× (e.g., Northeast, Northwest) Committee. Abbreviated as Youth League ×× Committee.

    II. Provincial or Regional Party Committee level: Should be called the China New Democratic Youth League ×× Province (e.g., Henan, Shanxi) Committee, abbreviated as Youth League ×× Provincial Committee, or the China New Democratic Youth League ×× Region (e.g., Shaanbei Region, Bohai Region) Committee, abbreviated as Youth League ×× Regional Committee.

    III. Municipal level: Directly-administered municipalities should be called the China New Democratic Youth League ×× Municipality Committee; municipalities under the jurisdiction of a province (region) should be called the China New Democratic Youth League ×× Province (Region) ×× Municipality Committee. Both are uniformly abbreviated as Youth League ×× Municipal Committee.

    IV. Prefecture Committee level: Should be called the China New Democratic Youth League ×× Province (Region) ×× (e.g., Sanbian, Donghai) Local Committee. Abbreviated as Youth League ×× Prefecture Committee.

    V. County level: Should be called the China New Democratic Youth League ×× Province (Region) ×× County Committee. Abbreviated as Youth League ×× County Committee.

    VI. District level: Should be called the China New Democratic Youth League ×× County, No. × (number) District Committee. Abbreviated as Youth League ×× County × District Committee.

### 青年团中央通知  规定各级团委会的任命办法和名称

1949-06-22
第1版()
专栏：

    青年团中央通知
    规定各级团委会的任命办法和名称
    【新华社北平二十日电】中国新民主主义青年团中央委员会于六月十五日发出通知，规定各级青年团工作委员会的任命办法和名称。通知全文如下：
    （一）本年四月中国新民主主义青年团第一次全国代表大会的召开，即已正式宣告中国新民主主义青年团的成立。凡尚未建团，或尚未自下而上经过各级代表大会选举产生各级团的领导机构之地区，均应建立青年团的工作委员会，开展各该地区的建团工作。
    （二）各级新民主主义青年团工作委员会委员的任命办法规定如下：凡各地区、大省及相当于大省的市青年团工作委员会，由青年团中央委员会直接任命之；地区及省以下的各级青年团工作委员会委员，均由上一级团委批准与任命，上两级团委备案。
    （三）各级新民主主义青年团委员会的名称规定如下：
    一、区域：应称中国新民主主义青年团××（如东北、西北）委员会。简称青年团××团委。
    二、省级或区党委级：应称中国新民主主义青年团××省（如河南、山西）委员会，简称青年团××省委或中国新民主主义青年团××区（如陕北区、渤海区）委员会，简称青年团××区团委。
    三、市级：直属市应称中国新民主主义青年团××市委员会，省（区）属市，应称中国新民主主义青年团××省（区）××市委员会。简称统为青年团××市委。
    四、地委级：应称中国新民主主义青年团××省（区）××（如三边、东海）地方委员会。简称青年团××地委。
    五、县级：应称中国新民主主义青年团××省（区）××县委员会。简称青年团××县委。
    六、区级：应称中国新民主主义青年团××县第×（数字）区委员会。简称青年团××县×区委。
