Title: Polish United Workers' Party Sends Greetings to American Communist Leader
Author:
Date: 1949-06-07
Page: 3

　　Xinhua News Agency, Peiping, 5th: Polish News Agency dispatch: The Warsaw Committee of the Polish United Workers' Party recently sent a telegram to the New York Committee of the Communist Party of the United States, extending its respects to Foster (Chairman of the Communist Party of the United States), Dennis (General Secretary), outstanding fighters in defending world peace and social justice, and their ten comrades, and expressing their shared concerns. The telegram stated that the Polish working class believes that your heroic stance in defending the cause of justice will unite the American working masses to fight for the consolidation of peace and international friendly cooperation.

### 波统一工人党电美共领袖致敬

1949-06-07
第3版()
专栏：

　　波统一工人党电美共领袖致敬
    【新华社北平五日电】波兰通讯社讯：波兰统一工人党华沙委员会顷致电美国共产党纽约委员会，向保卫世界和平和社会正义的杰出战士福斯特（美共主席）、但尼斯（总书记）和他们的十位同志致敬，并表示与他们休戚相关。电文说，波兰工人阶级相信你们保卫正义事业的英勇立场，将使美国的劳动群众团结起来，为巩固和平与国际的友爱合作而斗争。
