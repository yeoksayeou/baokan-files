Title: Four Hundred Remnants on Hengsha Island Surrender to Our Army with Weapons
Author:
Date: 1949-06-12
Page: 1

　　Xinhua News Agency, Shanghai, 11th: The remnant enemy of the Kuomintang Temporary First Army, 11th Division, 31st Regiment, 3rd Battalion (excluding the machine gun company) and a platoon from the communications company, totaling 424 officers and soldiers, led by Captain Zhang Jianchao and Shen Jun at 5:00 PM on the 6th, surrendered to the People's Liberation Army stationed in Wusong aboard the civilian ship "Dalian," bringing with them 240 rifles, 15 light machine guns, 20-odd pistols, 2 carbines, and 5 Thompson submachine guns.

### 横沙岛残敌四百携械向我军投降

1949-06-12
第1版()
专栏：

　　横沙岛残敌四百携械向我军投降
    【新华社上海十一日电】闲踞崇明岛南横沙岛之残敌国民党暂一军十一师第三十一团三营全部（缺机枪连）及通讯连之一个排，总计官兵四百二十四人，于六日下午五时由上尉连长张坚超、沈钧率领，乘民轮“大连号”向驻守吴淞之人民解放军投诚，携来步枪二百四十枝，轻机枪十五挺，短枪二十余枝，卡宾枪二枝，汤姆枪五枝。
