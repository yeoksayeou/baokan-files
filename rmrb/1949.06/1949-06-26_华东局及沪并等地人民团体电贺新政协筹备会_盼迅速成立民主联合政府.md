Title: East China Bureau and Shanghai and Other Local People's Organizations Telegraph Congratulations to the New CPPCC Preparatory Committee, Hoping for the Rapid Establishment of a Democratic Coalition Government
Author:
Date: 1949-06-26
Page: 1
　
　　East China Bureau and Shanghai and Other Local People's Organizations Telegraph Congratulations to the New CPPCC Preparatory Committee, Hoping for the Rapid Establishment of a Democratic Coalition Government
    [This Newspaper's Comprehensive Report] Since the establishment of the New CPPCC Preparatory Committee, all parties have telegraphed the committee to express their joy and support. The joint congratulatory telegram from the East China Bureau of the CPC Central Committee and the Shanghai Municipal Committee of the CPC stated: "We wholeheartedly support all resolutions of the New CPPCC Preparatory Committee and warmly welcome the rapid convocation of a new Political Consultative Conference to establish a national democratic coalition government." The Shanghai Federation of Trade Unions Preparatory Committee stated that it vowed to support the soon-to-be-established Central People's Government with actions. The Shanghai Student Federation's congratulatory telegram said: "We cheer for the New CPPCC" and expressed "the need to strengthen unity and do a good job of serving the South with a hundred times more determination!"
    People's organizations from all walks of life in Taiyuan City recently telegraphed the Preparatory Committee to express their congratulations. The congratulatory telegrams from Party, government, and military organs in Taiyuan jointly stated: We vow to unite the people of the entire region, develop production in Taiyuan, and eliminate the remaining bandits and secret agents as a gift to the New CPPCC.
    People from all walks of life in Zhangjiakou City celebrated the establishment of the CPPCC Preparatory Committee and held a representative symposium on the 22nd. During the meeting, representatives from all walks of life unanimously stood up to pay their respects to Chairman Mao and Commander-in-Chief Zhu.
    When the news reached Dali in Shaanxi Province, workers, students, and citizens from all walks of life were extremely delighted and expressed the hope that a democratic coalition government would be established soon.

### 华东局及沪并等地人民团体电贺新政协筹备会  盼迅速成立民主联合政府

1949-06-26
第1版()
专栏：

　　华东局及沪并等地人民团体电贺新政协筹备会
    盼迅速成立民主联合政府
    【本报综合报导】新政协筹备会成立以来，各方纷纷致电该会表示欣喜与拥护。中共中央华东局、中共上海市委的联合贺电中说：“我们竭诚拥护新政协筹备会的一切决议，热烈欢迎迅速召开新的政治协商会议，成立全国民主联合政府。”上海市总工会筹委会表示誓以行动来拥护即将成立的中央人民政府。上海市学联的贺电说：“我们为新政协欢呼”，并表示“要加强团结，以百倍于前的毅力，把南下服务工作作好！”
    太原市各界人民团体顷致电筹备会致贺，太原党政军各机关贺电中共同表示：誓将以团结全区人民，发展太原生产，肃清残余匪特作为新政协的献礼。
    张家口市各界人民庆祝政协筹备会的成立，特于二十二日举行代表座谈会，会议进行中，各界代表曾一致起立向毛主席朱总司令致敬。
    消息传至陕中大荔，工人、学生及各界市民莫不欣喜异常，并表示希望民主联合政府早日成立。
