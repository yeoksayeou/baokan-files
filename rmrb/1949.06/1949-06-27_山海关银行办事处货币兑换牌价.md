Title: Shanhai Pass Bank Office Currency Exchange Rate
Author:
Date: 1949-06-27
Page: 2

　　Shanhaiguan Bank Office Currency Exchange Rate
    [From Our Newspaper] The joint office of the People's Bank of China and the Northeast Bank in Shanhaiguan has changed its exchange rate, effective from the 25th of this month, to one yuan of Renminbi equaling 160 yuan of Northeast or Great Wall currency.

### 山海关银行办事处货币兑换牌价

1949-06-27
第2版()
专栏：

　　山海关银行办事处货币兑换牌价
    【本报讯】中国人民银行、东北银行山海关联合办事处自本月二十五日起，牌价改为人民券一元兑换东北券或长城券一百六十元。
