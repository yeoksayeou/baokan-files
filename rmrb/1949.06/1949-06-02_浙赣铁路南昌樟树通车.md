Title: Zhejiang-Jiangxi Railway, Nanchang-Zhangshu Section, Opens to Traffic

Author:

Date: 1949-06-02
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Nanchang, 31st - The Nanchang to Zhangshu section of the Zhejiang-Jiangxi Railway was repaired and opened to traffic on the 25th.

### 浙赣铁路南昌樟树通车

1949-06-02
第1版()
专栏：

　　浙赣铁路南昌樟树通车
