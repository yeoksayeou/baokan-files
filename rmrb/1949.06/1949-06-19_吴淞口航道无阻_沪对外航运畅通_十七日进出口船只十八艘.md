Title: Wusongkou Channel Clear; Shanghai's Foreign Shipping Unimpeded; Eighteen Import and Export Ships on the 17th
Author:
Date: 1949-06-19
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Shanghai, 18th. Shanghai's foreign shipping is open and unimpeded. Shanghai shipping circles dispatched three ships on the 16th and 17th to test the waters near Wusongkou, and the results have confirmed that there are no obstacles in the channel. On the 17th alone, Shanghai saw thirteen ships imported and five ships exported.

### 吴淞口航道无阻　沪对外航运畅通　十七日进出口船只十八艘

1949-06-19
第1版()
专栏：

　　吴淞口航道无阻
  　沪对外航运畅通
  　十七日进出口船只十八艘
    【新华社上海十八日电】上海对外航运畅通无阻。上海航业界于十六、十七两日派船三艘在吴淞口附近往返试航结果，已证实航道毫无障碍。十七日一天内，上海进口船只达十三艘，出口船五艘。
