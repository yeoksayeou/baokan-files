Title: North China People's Government Decides to Establish Beiping College of Political Science and Law; Xie Juezai is Chairman of the Preparatory Committee
Author:
Date: 1949-06-10
Page: 1

【Ping City News】In order to cultivate judicial talents, the North China People's Government has decided to establish Beiping College of Political Science and Law. An order was issued on June 7th, appointing Xie Juezai as the Chairman of the Preparatory Committee of Beiping College of Political Science and Law. Shen Junru, Zhang Zhirang, Wang Zhixiang, Li Da, Chen Jinkun, He Sijing, Li Mu'an, Yang Shaoxuan, Zuo Zonglun, Guan Shixiong, Xu Zexin, Wu Yuheng, Chen Chuangang, Lü Fu, Jia Qian, Wang Feiran, Wang Xiaoyi, Chen Shouyi, and Yu Liechen were appointed as members, with Xie Juezai, Zuo Zonglun, Guan Shixiong, Chen Chuangang, Chen Shouyi, and Yu Liechen designated as standing committee members.

### 华北人民政府决定创办北平政法学院　谢觉哉为筹委会主任委员

1949-06-10
第1版()
专栏：

　　华北人民政府决定创办北平政法学院　谢觉哉为筹委会主任委员
    【平市讯】华北人民政府为培养司法人材，决定创办北平政法学院。并于六月七日发出命令，派谢觉哉为北平政法学院筹备委员会主任委员，沈钧儒、张志让、王之相、李达、陈瑾昆、何思敬、李木庵、杨绍萱、左宗纶、关世雄、许泽新、吴昱恒、陈传纲、吕复、贾潜、王斐然、王笑一、陈守一、于烈辰为委员，并指定谢觉哉、左宗纶、关世雄、陈传纲、陈守一、于烈辰为常务委员。
