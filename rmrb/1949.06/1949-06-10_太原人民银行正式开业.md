Title: Taiyuan People's Bank Officially Opens

Author:

Date: 1949-06-10
Page: 2

Economic News

　　Taiyuan People's Bank Officially Opens
    [Taiyuan Electric] Since entering Taiyuan City, the Taiyuan Branch of the People's Bank of China has been engaged in exchanging counterfeit gold yuan coupons, issuing production self-help loans, managing silver dollar pricing, establishing a local currency market, and taking over bureaucratic capital banks; after active preparations, it officially opened on the first of this month.

### 太原人民银行正式开业

1949-06-10
第2版()
专栏：经济新闻

　　太原人民银行正式开业
    【太原电】中国人民银行太原分行自进入太原城后，除兑换伪金圆券，发放生产自救贷款，管理白洋计价行使，建立本币市场，接管官僚资本银行等工作外；经过积极筹备，已于本月一日正式开业。
