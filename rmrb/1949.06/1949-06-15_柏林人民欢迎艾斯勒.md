Title: People of Berlin Welcome Eisler
Author:
Date: 1949-06-15
Page: 3

　　Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, 14th: The Polish Press Agency, Berlin: The people of Berlin, under the auspices of the Socialist Unity Party, held a mass meeting to warmly welcome the German anti-fascist leader Eisler, who recently returned to his homeland. At the meeting, Eisler delivered a speech stating that he hated the German war criminals of yesterday and the American warmongers of today. He expressed his gratitude to the Polish government and the Polish people for their assistance, saying that any attempt to revise the Polish border was a crime against peace. Gendritski, Chairman of the Berlin Socialist Unity Party Committee, announced that Eisler had been accepted into the Socialist Unity Party.

### 柏林人民欢迎艾斯勒

1949-06-15
第3版()
专栏：

　　柏林人民欢迎艾斯勒
    【新华社北平十四日电】波兰通讯社柏林讯：柏林人民在统一社会党主持下召开群众大会，热烈欢迎新近返回祖国的德国反法西斯领袖艾斯勒。会上艾斯勒发表演说称，他憎恨昨日的德国战犯与今日的美国战争贩子。他表示感谢波兰政府和波兰人民给他的援助，他说任何修改波兰边界的企图，都是危害和平的罪行。柏林统一社会党党委会主席詹德里茨基宣布艾斯勒已被接受加入统一社会党。
