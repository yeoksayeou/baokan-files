Title: We welcome the following manuscripts
Author:
Date: 1949-06-09
Page: 1

　　We welcome the following manuscripts:
    I. Essays and sketches with guiding and educational significance.
    II. News reports and reports that are both substantive and vividly written; essays and features that tell a story or portray a character.
    III. Theater reviews, film reviews, book reviews, or analyses and commentaries on a particular work.
    IV. Reports from workers, students, soldiers, and cadres about their opinions and experiences in discussing a certain issue in their studies, as well as specific accounts of how their thinking has been improved and their lives changed through study.
    V. Fairy tales, popular science pieces, theoretical studies and explorations, and problem-solving answers.
    VI. For all of the above manuscripts, we hope they will be short, within 1,500 characters.
    VII. Current affairs cartoons, story paintings, woodcuts, and comic strips depicting production, support for the front, and the War of Liberation.

　　　　Supplement to this newspaper

### 我们欢迎下列文稿

1949-06-09
第1版()
专栏：

　　我们欢迎下列文稿
    一、具有指导、教育意义的杂文、随笔。
    二、有内容写得又生动的通讯、报告；写一个故事、写一个人物的散文、特写。
    三、剧评、影评、书评或对某一篇作品的分析、评介。
    四、工人、学生、战士，干部学习中，对某一问题讨论的意见、心得，以及从学习中如何提高了思想、改变了生活的具体过程的报导。
    五、童话，科学小品，理论的学习、探讨及问题解答。
    六、以上文稿，均望写短小些，在千五百字以内。
    七、时事漫画，描写生产、支前、解放战争的故事画、木刻、连环画。
    　　　　本报副刊启
