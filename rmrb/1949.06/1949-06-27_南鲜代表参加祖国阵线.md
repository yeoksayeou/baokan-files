Title: South Korean Representatives Participate in Fatherland Front
Author:
Date: 1949-06-27
Page: 3

　　Xinhua News Agency, Pyongyang, 25th: Representatives from the Korean Industrial Revival Association, Li Tingwan (transliteration), and the Korean People's Republican Party, Park Gunseok (both transliterations), recently arrived in Pyongyang from South Korea to participate in the founding conference of the Korean Democratic Fatherland United Front. Li Tingwan told reporters that American monopoly capitalists have seized control of 97 percent of South Korea's industry. Li Tingwan emphasized that every righteous Korean is rising up to fight for the independence and unification of the fatherland, and believes that supporting the Democratic Fatherland Front is a powerful weapon to strive for the withdrawal of American troops and to overthrow the Syngman Rhee "government."

### 南鲜代表参加祖国阵线

1949-06-27
第3版()
专栏：

　　南鲜代表参加祖国阵线
    【新华社北平二十五日电】塔斯社平壤讯：朝鲜工业复兴协会代表李廷晚（译音）及朝鲜人民共和党代表朴君席（以上两名均译音）最近自南朝鲜抵平壤，参加朝鲜民主祖国联合阵线成立大会的工作。李廷晚告记者称：美国垄断资本家已攫得南朝鲜工业百分之九十七的控制权。李廷晚着重指出：每个正义的朝鲜人都起来为祖国的独立统一而斗争，并认为支持民主祖国阵线是争取美军撤退与打倒李承晚“政府”的强有力武器。
