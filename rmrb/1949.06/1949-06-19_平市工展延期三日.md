Title: The Ping City Industrial Exhibition Extended by Three Days
Author:
Date: 1949-06-19
Page: 2

　　The Ping City Industrial Exhibition Extended by Three Days
    [Our Newspaper] Since the opening of the Beiping City Industrial Exhibition, in addition to the enthusiastic visits from local municipal organizations, groups, schools, and citizens, various regions have dispatched visiting delegations. Nine visiting delegations from eastern Hebei, Tianjin, Zhangjiakou, Hebei-Luyu, Taiyue, Shijiazhuang, central Hebei, Jinzhou, and Shandong have successively come to Ping to visit. As of yesterday, the number of visitors has reached over 90,000. The initial daily attendance of 3,000 has increased to 6,000, and even exceeded 10,000. Originally scheduled to close on the 20th, it has now been decided, at the request of all sectors, to extend the exhibition for three days, closing on the 23rd.

### 平市工展延期三日

1949-06-19
第2版()
专栏：

　　平市工展延期三日
    【本报讯】北平市工业展览会开幕以来，除本市机关团体学校市民纷纷前往参观外，各地均派出参观团并陆续有冀东、天津、张家口、冀鲁豫、太岳、石家庄、冀中、锦州、山东等九个参观团，来平参观。至昨日止参观者已达九万余人。由开始每天三千人增至每天六千人以至万余人。原定二十日闭幕，现经各界要求，决定延期三日，至二十三日闭幕。
