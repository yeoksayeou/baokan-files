Title: Revolutionary University to Summarize Theoretical Study Next Week
Author: Dang Ziqiang
Date: 1949-06-13
Page: 2

　　Revolutionary University to Summarize Theoretical Study Next Week
　　[Ping City News] The first stage of study at Revolutionary University—Marxist-Leninist theoretical study—will conclude this week, and a summary will begin next week. The summary includes three parts: 1. Theoretical test; 2. Ideological summary; 3. Group evaluation. The summary is tentatively scheduled for about ten days, with the steps being arrangement from top to bottom, and summarization from bottom to top (individual summary, up to group, class, department, and school-wide summary).
　　　　　　　　　(Dang Ziqiang)

### 革大下周总结理论学习
党自强
1949-06-13
第2版()
专栏：

　　革大下周总结理论学习
    【平市讯】革大学习的第一个阶段——马列主义理论学习于本周结束，下周即开始总结。总结内容有三：一、理论测验；二、思想总结；三、小组鉴定。总结时间暂定十天左右，步骤是由上而下的布置，由下而上的总结（个人总结，直到小组、班、部、全校总结）。　　　　　
　　　　　　　　　（党自强）
