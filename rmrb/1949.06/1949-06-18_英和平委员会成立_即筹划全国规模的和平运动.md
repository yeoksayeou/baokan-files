Title: British Peace Committee Established; Planning National-Scale Peace Movement
Author:
Date: 1949-06-18
Page: 3

　　Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, 17th: TASS, London: The British Peace Committee announced its establishment in London on June 15th. The writer Crowther, who recently led the British delegation to the Paris World Peace Congress, was appointed chairman. The purpose of the British Peace Committee is to help develop and, where necessary, initiate activities to defend peace. Prominent figures participating in the committee include Zilliacus (Member of Parliament), Bernal (Member of Parliament), Pritt (Member of Parliament), Elizabeth Allen, John Wood (miner), McDougall, and Allen Euston (minister). The committee is already planning a national-scale peace movement. In the coming weeks, meetings, conferences, and demonstrations will be held in many important cities. The renowned French Catholic scholar Elder Beaulier is scheduled to arrive in London on June 16th to deliver speeches at several large gatherings across the country. The Peace Congress to be held in London this October will be a milestone in the British peace movement. It will be held for two days with the participation of over a thousand representatives.

### 英和平委员会成立　即筹划全国规模的和平运动

1949-06-18
第3版()
专栏：

　　英和平委员会成立
  　即筹划全国规模的和平运动
    【新华社北平十七日电】塔斯社伦敦讯：英国和平委员会已于六月十五日在伦敦宣布成立，以最近率领英国代表团参加巴黎世界和平大会的作家克劳瑟任主席。英国和平委员会的宗旨，是在帮助开展并在必要的地方发动保卫和平的活动。参加该委员会的著名人士，包括齐里亚库斯（国会议员）、柏纳尔（国会议员）、普里特（国会议员）、伊利沙柏·艾伦，约翰·伍德（矿工），麦克杜加尔和艾伦·伊欧斯东（牧师）。委员会已在筹划全国规模的和平运动。在未来数周中，许多重要城市中将举行会议、大会和示威。著名法国天主教学者鲍利尔长老，定于六月十六日抵达伦敦，向全国各地的某些大集会发表演说。今年十月间要在伦敦举行的和平代表大会。将是英国和平运动的里程碑。该会将举行两日，有一千名以上的代表参加。
