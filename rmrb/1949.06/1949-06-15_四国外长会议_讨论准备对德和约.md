Title: Four-Power Foreign Ministers Conference Discusses Preparing a Peace Treaty with Germany
Author:
Date: 1949-06-15
Page: 3

Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, 14th: TASS, Paris – The Council of Foreign Ministers of the Soviet Union, the United States, Britain, and France held a plenary session (open meeting) on June 12th to discuss the third item on the agenda: the issue of preparing a peace treaty with Germany. The meeting was chaired by British Foreign Secretary Bevin and lasted for four and a half hours. Afterwards, the foreign ministers held a closed-door meeting with restricted audiences.

### 四国外长会议　讨论准备对德和约

1949-06-15
第3版()
专栏：

　　四国外长会议
  　讨论准备对德和约
    【新华社北平十四日电】塔斯社巴黎讯：苏美英法外长会议于六月十二日举行全体会议（公开会议），讨论会议的第三项议程：准备对德和约问题，会议由英外相贝文任主席，历时四小时半。其后，各外长又举行限制听众的秘密会议。
