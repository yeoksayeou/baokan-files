Title: Telegraphic Transfers Now Available Between Beiping and Tianjin, Nanjing, and Hankou

Author:

Date: 1949-06-29
Page: 2

　　Telegraphic Transfers Now Available Between Beiping and Tianjin, Nanjing, and Hankou
    [Beiping News] The Beiping Postal Administration has now opened telegraphic transfer services with Tianjin, Nanjing, Hankou, and Taiyuan. The remittance limit is temporarily set at 200,000 yuan per transaction. Telegraphic transfer services to other cities in the liberated areas will be launched successively after consultations, to benefit the public. (Beiping Postal Administration Public Service Group)

### 平与津京汉并互通电汇

1949-06-29
第2版()
专栏：

　　平与津京汉并互通电汇
    【平市讯】北平邮政管理局现已与天津、南京、汉口、太原四地互通电汇，汇款限额每次暂定二十万元，至解放区其他各城市，俟洽妥后，亦将陆续开办电汇，以利公众。（北平邮政管理局公众服务组）
