Title: Eisler Elected to German People's Council

Author:
Date: 1949-06-02
Page: 3

Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, 1st - Reports from various sources: German anti-fascist leader Eisler has been unanimously elected as a member of the People's Council by over two thousand delegates of the Third German People's Congress. After regaining his freedom on May 27, Eisler traveled from England to Prague by plane on May 31, before returning to his homeland via another route, to fight for a democratic, peaceful, and unified Germany.

### 艾斯勒当选德人民委会委员

1949-06-02
第3版()
专栏：

　　艾斯勒当选德人民委会委员
    【新华社北平一日电】各方报道：德国反法西斯领袖艾斯勒已由第三届德国人民代表大会二千余代表一致选举为人民委员会委员。艾氏于五月二十七日恢复自由后，三十一日由英国乘飞机前往布拉格，转道回返祖国，为争取民主、和平、统一的德国而斗争。
