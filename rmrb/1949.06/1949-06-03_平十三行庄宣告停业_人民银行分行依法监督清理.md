Title: Thirteen Ping-Based Banks Declare Closure; People's Bank Branch Supervises Liquidation According to Law
Author:
Date: 1949-06-03
Page: 2
　　
　　【Report from this newspaper】Among the private banks and money shops in Ping City that failed to register and verify capital before the deadline set by the Beijing Branch of the People's Bank of China were: Zhengtai, Datong, and Dasheng Banks; Zhenda, Yu Daheng, Yisheng, Yuchanghou, Wanyichang, and Puli Money Shops; and Yufeng, Longyu, Daquan, Huichang, and Yuanli exchange businesses. Among them, with the exception of Dasheng Bank, which has downsized its operations, applied for, and submitted capital verification to continue operating as a money shop, the remaining thirteen banks and shops, including Zhengtai, all declared closure yesterday (the 2nd). The Beijing Branch of the People's Bank of China immediately implemented supervision and liquidation in accordance with the liquidation measures for closed banks and shops. Furthermore, yesterday (the 2nd), the Banking and Money Shop Association was notified to inform all relevant banks and shops to cease bill exchange starting today (the 3rd).
　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　(Yu)

### 平十三行庄宣告停业　人民银行分行依法监督清理

1949-06-03
第2版()
专栏：经济新闻

　　平十三行庄宣告停业
  　人民银行分行依法监督清理
    【本报讯】平市私营银钱业于中国人民银行北平分行办理登记截止前未进行登记验资之行庄计有：正太、大同、大生三银行。振大、余大亨、义生、裕长厚、万义长、普利六钱庄及裕丰、隆裕、大泉、汇昌、源利等兑换业。其中除大生银行紧缩机构，已呈请并缴验资金改为钱庄继续营业外，其余正太等十三家行庄一律于昨（二日）起宣告停业，中国人民银行北平分行即依照停业行庄清理办法，实施监督清理。并于昨日（二日）通知银钱两业公会转知各该行庄限于今（三日）起停止票据交换。　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　（宇）
