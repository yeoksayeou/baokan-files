Title: Pakistan Trade Union Demands Release of Union Leaders
Author:
Date: 1949-06-07
Page: 3

　　Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, 5th: TASS, Karachi dispatch: The Pakistan Times reports that the acting chairman of the Pakistan Federation of Trade Unions, Faiz, has submitted a memorandum to the government demanding the release of all trade union leaders from prison and the re-employment of all workers dismissed for trade union activities. The memorandum states that genuine workers' organizations are facing relentless suppression throughout Pakistan.

### 巴基斯坦工联要求释放工会领袖

1949-06-07
第3版()
专栏：

　　巴基斯坦工联要求释放工会领袖
    【新华社北平五日电】塔斯社喀剌蚩讯：巴基斯坦时报载称：巴基斯坦职工会联合会代理主席费兹已向政府提出备忘录，要求自监狱中释放全部职工会领袖，并重新雇用全部因工会活动而被开除的工人。备忘录称：真正的工人团体，在巴基斯坦各地正遭遇着无情的压制。
