Title: People's Bank of Zhangyuan Branch Grants 50 Million to Support Industry
Author:
Date: 1949-06-05
Page: 2
Economic News

　　People's Bank of Zhangyuan Branch
  　Grants 50 Million to Support Industry
    [This Newspaper Dispatches] In order to develop urban industrial production and regulate the turnover of funds in the market, the Zhangjiakou Branch of the People's Bank of China has vigorously absorbed deposits and applied them to production undertakings that are beneficial to the national economy and the people's livelihood, supporting the restoration and development of industry. As of early May, a total of more than 50 million yuan had been loaned, of which 43.2 million yuan was loaned to 22 state-owned industrial enterprises. This included two soap factories, five flour mills, one paper mill, two oil mills, two printing houses, two leather and felt saddle factories, one cooperative iron industry, two construction companies, three shoe factories, one straw hat factory, and three state-owned transportation companies, accounting for 74 percent of the total loan amount.
    A total of 15 million yuan was loaned to 191 private industrial enterprises. These included 73 flour mills, 93 leather, rawhide, and tanning industries, 5 felt hat factories, 5 vermicelli factories, 4 iron industries, 3 brick and tile kilns, and 8 shoe-making and soap-making industries, accounting for 26 percent of the total loan amount.
    　　　　　　　　　　　(Communication Group of the Business Office of the People's Bank of Zhangjiakou City)

### 人民银行张垣分行　放款五千万扶植工业

1949-06-05
第2版()
专栏：经济新闻

　　人民银行张垣分行
  　放款五千万扶植工业
    【本报讯】中国人民银行张市分行为了发展城市工业生产，调剂市面资金周转，以大力吸收存款，运用于有利国计民生的生产事业上去，扶植了工业恢复与发展。至五月上旬，共贷款五千余万元，其中公营工业二十二家共贷款四千三百二十万元。包括肥皂业二家，面粉业五家，造纸业一家，榨油业二家，印刷业二家，制革毡鞍业二家，合作铁工业一家，建筑业二家，制鞋三家，草帽业一家，公营运输业三家，共占贷款总数百分之七十四。
    私营工业一九一家，共贷一千五百万元。计有面粉业七十三家，皮裘、粗皮、制革业九十三家，毡帽五家，制粉条业五家，铁工业四家，砖瓦窑业三家，制鞋、造胰等业八家，占贷款总数之百分之二十六。
    　　　　　　　　　　　（中国人民银行张市营业处通信小组）
