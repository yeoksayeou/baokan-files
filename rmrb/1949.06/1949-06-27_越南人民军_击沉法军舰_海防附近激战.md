Title: Vietnamese People's Army Sinks French Warship; Fierce Fighting Near Haiphong
Author:
Date: 1949-06-27
Page: 3

Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, June 26th: Hanoi dispatch: According to a French military communiqué of the 24th, Vietnamese war activity had "somewhat increased" in the past week. In the Haiphong area, a key region for the French in northern Vietnam, the Vietnamese People's Army attacked a French mobile unit's stronghold. In southern Vietnam, the People's Army engaged in three significant battles with the French army. On the Aistri River near Saigon, the French deployed naval support. However, one French warship struck a mine and sank. The crew narrowly avoided capture only with air cover.

Furthermore, according to Agence France-Presse: The Vietnamese People's Army attacked a French military convoy on the Sino-Vietnamese border on the 18th, destroying five or six trucks.

### 越南人民军　击沉法军舰　海防附近激战

1949-06-27
第3版()
专栏：

　　越南人民军
  　击沉法军舰
  　海防附近激战
    【新华社北平二十六日电】河内讯：据二十四日法军公报透露，上周间越南战事有“某些增加”。在越北法心腹地区海防附近，越人民军曾攻击法军别动队的据点。在南越，人民军曾与法军发生三次重要的战斗。在西贡附近的挨斯吐里河，法方曾出动海军助战。但法舰一艘中水雷沉没。舰上人员仅在空军掩护下始免于充当俘虏。
    又据法新社讯：越人民军曾于十八日袭击中越边境的法军运输队，毁卡车五、六辆。
