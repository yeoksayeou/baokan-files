Title: This Year Returning to Grammos: Greek Democratic Army Annihilates 20,000 Enemies
Author:
Date: 1949-06-19
Page: 3

Xinhua News Agency, Beiping, 18th: The Free Greece News Agency reports that the Greek Democratic Army Headquarters issued a special order to the soldiers, non-commissioned officers, officers, commanders, and political commissars fighting in Grammos on the 16th, the anniversary of the Battle of Grammos. The order stated: A year ago, the royalist fascists boasted that they would defeat the Greek Democratic Army in Grammos. However, the Democratic Army carried out brilliant mobile warfare in Vissa and defeated the enemy. This year, the Democratic Army has returned to Grammos, inflicting 20,000 casualties on the enemy, and firmly maintaining its positions.

### 今年重回格拉姆斯　希民主军歼敌两万

1949-06-19
第3版()
专栏：

　　今年重回格拉姆斯
  　希民主军歼敌两万
    【新华社北平十八日电】自由希腊通讯社报道：十六日为格拉姆斯战役一周年纪念，希腊民主军总司令部发表致在格拉姆斯作战的战士、士官、军官、司令员和政治委员的特别命令。命令声称：在一年以前，保皇法西斯夸口说他们将在格拉姆斯击败希腊民主军。然而民主军在维萨作了光辉的运动战，并击败了敌人。今年民主军重回了格拉姆斯，给予敌两万人的损失，并坚固地保持阵地。
