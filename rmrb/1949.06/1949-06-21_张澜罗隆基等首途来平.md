Title: Zhang Lan, Luo Longji and Others First Depart for Beiping

Author:

Date: 1949-06-21
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Shanghai, 20th. Democratic figures Zhang Lan, Luo Longji, Shi Liang, Guo Chuntao, Wang Baozhen and others took a special train on the evening of the 18th, first departing for Beiping.

Nanjing People's Radio, 20th broadcast. Democratic figures remaining in Shanghai, including Zhang Lan, Luo Longji, Shi Liang, Guo Chuntao, Wang Baozhen and ten others, came to Nanjing from Shanghai on the morning of the 19th. Today they will take the Jinpu Railway train to Beiping to participate in the preparatory meeting of the New Political Consultative Conference. Yesterday afternoon, Mayor Liu Bocheng held a special banquet to welcome Zhang Lan and others. Mr. Zhang Lan, Luo Longji, and Shi Liang will also participate in the Fourth Plenary Session of the China Democratic League to be held in Beiping.

### 张澜罗隆基等首途来平

1949-06-21
第1版()
专栏：

    张澜罗隆基等首途来平
    【新华社上海二十日电】民主人士张澜、罗隆基、史良、郭春涛、王葆真等于十八日晚十一时乘专车首途赴平。
    【南京人民电台二十日广播】留在上海的民主人士张澜、罗隆基、史良、郭春涛、王葆真等十一人，十九号上午从上海来南京。今天搭津浦路车去北平参加新政协筹备会。昨天下午刘伯承市长，特设宴为张澜等洗尘。张澜、罗隆基、史良三位先生并将参加民盟在北平召开的四中全会。
