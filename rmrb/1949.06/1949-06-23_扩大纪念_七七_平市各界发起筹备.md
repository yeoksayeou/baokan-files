Title: Expanding Commemoration of "July 7th" Incident: Preparations Initiated by All Circles in Beiping

Author:

Date: 1949-06-23
Page: 2

Expanding Commemoration of "July 7th" Incident
Preparations Initiated by All Circles in Beiping

[Report from this newspaper] In order to initiate an expanded commemoration of the "July 7th" Anniversary, the CPC Beiping Municipal Committee, the Preparatory Committee of the Beiping Municipal Branch of the Revolutionary Committee of the Chinese Kuomintang, the Beiping Municipal Branch of the China Democratic League, the Beiping Municipal Military Control Commission, the Beiping Municipal People's Government, the Preparatory Committee of the Beiping Municipal Federation of Trade Unions, the Preparatory Committee of the Beiping Municipal Committee of the China New Democratic Youth League, the Beiping Municipal Student Federation, and the Women's Federation, totaling nine units, held a meeting yesterday (the 22nd) in the West Flower Hall of the Municipal Government. It was resolved to invite 35 units, including Party, government, military, and people's organizations, to form a preparatory committee, which is scheduled to be formally established at 9:00 AM on the 24th in the West Flower Hall of the Municipal Government.

### 扩大纪念“七七”  平市各界发起筹备

1949-06-23
第2版()
专栏：

    扩大纪念“七七”
    平市各界发起筹备
    【本报讯】中国共产党北平市委会，中国国民党革命委员会北平市分会筹委会，中国民主同盟北平市支部，北平市军管会，北平市人民政府，北平市职工总会筹委会，中国新民主主义青年团北平市筹委会，北平市学联、妇联等九单位，为发起扩大纪念“七七”纪念日，昨（二十二）日在市府西花厅开会，决议邀请党政军民各团体等三十五单位组成筹备委员会，并定于二十四日晨九时在市府西花厅正式成立。
