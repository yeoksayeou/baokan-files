Title: Four Foreign Ministers Issue Communiqué Announcing Meeting Progress
Author:
Date: 1949-06-17
Page: 3

Xinhua News Agency, Beiping, 16th: TASS, Paris: On June 15th, the four foreign ministers issued the following communiqué, stating: At the secret meetings held in recent days, the four foreign ministers reviewed the issue of the Austrian peace treaty and the temporary agreement on the German situation. In order to study the various proposals put forward by the representatives, the meeting on the 15th decided to adjourn. The next meeting of the four foreign ministers will be held at 3:30 pm on June 16th. The meeting will be a closed session.

### 四国外长发布公报　宣布会议进程

1949-06-17
第3版()
专栏：

　　四国外长发布公报
    宣布会议进程
    【新华社北平十六日电】塔斯社巴黎讯：六月十五日四国外长发表下述公报，宣称：四国外长在最近几日来所举行的秘密会议上，审查了对奥和约以及关于德国形势的暂时协议的问题。为研究代表们所提出之各种建议起见，十五日的会议决定展期。四国外长的下次会议将于六月十六日下午三时半举行。会议将为秘密会议。
