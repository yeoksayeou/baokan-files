Title: People's Bank of China's Xi'an Branch Commences Remittance Exchange with Tianjin and Shijiazhuang Yesterday

Author:

Date: 1949-06-27
Page: 1

[Xi'an Xinhua Broadcasting Station broadcasted on the 26th] Xi'an News: The People's Bank of China's Xi'an Branch began remittance exchange with Tianjin and Shijiazhuang yesterday. Remittance exchange between the branch and major cities such as Shanghai, Nanjing, Jinan, Hankou, Kaifeng, and Beiping can also be gradually started within three to five days.

### 人民银行西安分行昨起与津石通汇

1949-06-27
第1版()
专栏：

　　人民银行西安分行昨起与津石通汇
    【西安新华广播电台二十六日广播】西安消息：人民银行西安分行，从今天起开始和天津、石家庄两地通汇。该行和上海、南京、济南、汉口、开封、北平等各大都市的通汇，在三五天以内，也可以陆续开始。
