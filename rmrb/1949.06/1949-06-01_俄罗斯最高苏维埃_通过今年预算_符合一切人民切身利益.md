Title: Russian Supreme Soviet Passes This Year's Budget, Conforming to the Vital Interests of All the People
Author:
Date: 1949-06-01
Page: 3

Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, May 30th: Moscow dispatch: The Supreme Soviet of the Russian Soviet Socialist Federal Republic (the largest of the sixteen republics of the Soviet Union) held its third session from the 24th to the 26th in the Kremlin, and unanimously approved the 1949 state budget of the Russian Federal Republic, with a total income of 51.803 billion rubles and a total expenditure of 51.57 billion rubles, with income exceeding expenditure by 233 million rubles. At the meeting, the Republic's Minister of Finance, Savronov, on behalf of the Council of Ministers, presented a report on the draft of the 1949 Republic's state budget and a report on the execution of the 1947-1948 budget. The Supreme Soviet made a detailed review, discussion and revision of these two reports.

　　The 1949 budget draft reflects the rapid post-war economic and cultural development of the Russian Federal Republic. Both income and expenditure in the budget draft are more than double that of the pre-war year of 1940. Expenditures for cultural and social services account for more than 70 percent of the total budget. The number of students will increase by 1.5 million compared to last year, bringing the total to 18.47 million, the number of libraries will increase to 12,319, the number of rural clubs will increase to 13,190, and the number of cinemas will increase to 20,000, which is more than double the number of pre-war cinemas. Hospitals and hospital staff in urban and rural areas will also increase significantly. During the discussion of this budget draft, the representatives of the Supreme Soviet unanimously pointed out that the 1949 budget guarantees the requirements stipulated in the national economic plan for the further development of the Republic's economy and culture, and fully conforms to the vital interests of all the people of the Russian Federation.

　　The Russian Supreme Soviet meeting also approved the decrees issued by the Presidium of the Supreme Soviet during the two sessions.

### 俄罗斯最高苏维埃　通过今年预算　符合一切人民切身利益

1949-06-01
第3版()
专栏：

　　俄罗斯最高苏维埃
  　通过今年预算
  　符合一切人民切身利益
    【新华社北平三十日电】莫斯科讯：俄罗斯苏维埃社会主义联邦共和国（苏联十六个加盟共和国中最大的共和国）最高苏维埃从二十四日到二十六日在克里姆林宫举行第三次会议，一致通过了一九四九年俄罗斯联邦共和国国家预算，计收入总数五百一十八亿零三百万卢布，支出总数五百一十五亿七千万卢布，收入超过支出两亿三千三百万卢布。会议先由共和国财政部长萨伏罗诺夫代表部长会议提出关于一九四九年共和国国家预算草案的报告、及一九四七到一九四八年预算执行的报告。最高苏维埃对这两个报告作了详尽的审查、讨论和修正。
    一九四九年的预算草案，反映出俄罗斯联邦共和国战后在经济和文化上的飞速发展。预算草案中的收入和支出，都比战前一九四○年增加了一倍多。用于文化和社会服务的支出，占全部预算的百分之七十强。学生的数目将比去年增加一百五十万人，总数将增至一千八百四十七万，图书馆将增加到一万二千三百一十九所，农村俱乐部将增加到一万三千一百九十所，电影院增加到两万家，将比战前的电影院多一倍，城乡的医院和医院人员也将大大增加。最高苏维埃的代表们在讨论这一预算草案时，都一致指出：一九四九年的预算，保证了为进一步发展共和国经济与文化的国民经济计划所规定的要求，并完全符合于俄罗斯联邦一切人民的切身利益。
    俄罗斯最高苏维埃会议并批准了最高苏维埃主席团在两次会议期间所颁发的命令。
