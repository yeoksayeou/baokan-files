Title: Standing Committee's First Meeting Decides to Temporarily Establish Four Working Bodies
Author:
Date: 1949-06-20
Page: 1

　　Standing Committee's First Meeting
  　Decides to Temporarily Establish Four Working Bodies
    [Xinhua News Agency, Beiping, 19th] In accordance with Article 6 of the Organizational Rules of the Preparatory Committee of the New Political Consultative Conference, the Standing Committee, at its first meeting held at 8:00 PM on June 16th, decided to temporarily establish four working bodies: the Secretariat, the General Affairs Office, the Reception Office, and the Press Office. Liang Airan was appointed as Director of the Secretariat, Zhou Zijian as Director of the General Affairs Office, Shen Bochun as Director of the Reception Office, and Huan Xiang as Director of the Press Office.

### 常委会首次会　决定暂设四项工作机构

1949-06-20
第1版()
专栏：

　　常委会首次会
  　决定暂设四项工作机构
    【新华社北平十九日电】依据新政协筹备会组织条例第六条之规定，常务委员会于六月十六日下午八时举行的第一次会议上，决定暂设秘书处、庶务处、招待处、新闻处等四项工作机构，并以梁蔼然为秘书处处长，周子健为庶务处处长，申伯纯为招待处处长，宦乡为新闻处处长。
