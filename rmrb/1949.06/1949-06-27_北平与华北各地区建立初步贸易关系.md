Title: Preliminary Trade Relations Established Between Beiping and Various Regions of North China
Author:
Date: 1949-06-27
Page: 2

　　Xinhua News Agency, Beiping, June 26th. Representative delegations from eastern Hebei, central Hebei, Taiyue, Hebei-Shandong-Henan, Shijiazhuang and other regions, who came to Beiping to visit the industrial exhibition, have established preliminary direct trade relations with Beiping city after exchanging views with relevant departments in Beiping on the issue of material exchange. Various regions have ordered a batch of goods from Beiping, including 12,800 bolts of cloth, as well as soap, matches, socks, and other items. More than 10 million jin of coarse and fine grains supplied to Beiping along the PingSui Railway have also arrived. In the future, trade relations between Beiping and various parts of North China will gradually develop, and the exchange of materials between urban and rural areas will become increasingly active.

### 北平与华北各地区建立初步贸易关系

1949-06-27
第2版()
专栏：

　　北平与华北各地区建立初步贸易关系
    【新华社北平二十六日电】来平参观工业展览的冀东、冀中、太岳、冀鲁豫、石家庄等地区的代表团，经与北平有关部门交换关于物资交流问题意见后，已与平市建立了初步的直接贸易关系。各地区均已向平市订购货物一批，包括布匹一万二千八百匹及肥皂、火柴、袜子等。平绥沿线供应平市之粗细粮一千余万斤亦已到达。今后北平与华北各地的贸易关系将逐步开展，城乡物资交流将日趋活跃。
