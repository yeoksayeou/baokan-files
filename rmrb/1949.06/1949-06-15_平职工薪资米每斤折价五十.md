Title: Tianjin Workers' Salaries Valued at 50 Yuan Per Jin of Millet
Author:
Date: 1949-06-15
Page: 2

　　[This newspaper reports] Tianjin Workers' Salaries Valued at 50 Yuan Per Jin of Millet
　　[This Newspaper's Report] The Tianjin Municipal Federation of Trade Unions and other relevant departments have assessed and the North China People's Government has approved that the millet price conversion for the salaries of employees in various organs and enterprises in Tianjin for the first half of June will be issued at 50 yuan per jin.

### 平职工薪资米每斤折价五十

1949-06-15
第2版()
专栏：

　　平职工薪资米每斤折价五十
    【本报讯】六月份上半月平市各机关、企业部门职工薪资小米折价，经市总工会等有关部门评定，华北人民政府批准，按照每斤五十元发给。
