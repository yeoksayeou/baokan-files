Title: Ping-Shi Phone Call
Author: Tang Jiaju
Date: 1949-06-29
Page: 2

　　Ping-Shi Phone Call
    [Ping City News] At 7:40 PM on June 27th, the construction of the Ping-Shi line was completed. At 8:30 PM, the first official phone call was made, with clear and loud sound. (Tang Jiaju)

### 平石通话
唐家炬
1949-06-29
第2版()
专栏：

　　平石通话
    【平市讯】六月二十七日下午七时四十分，平石间的线路架设完结，八点三十分正式通话，声音清晰宏亮。（唐家炬）
