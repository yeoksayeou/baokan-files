Title: Ping City Industrial Exhibition, Third Day: Twenty-Two Agencies and Organizations Visit
Author:
Date: 1949-06-09
Page: 2

　　Ping City Industrial Exhibition, Third Day: Twenty-Two Agencies and Organizations Visit
    [Reported by this newspaper] Yesterday was the third day of the Ping City Industrial Exhibition. There were 4,769 individual visitors. Group visitors included the CCP Beijing Municipal Committee, the Preparatory Committee of the New Democratic Youth League, the Ministry of Agriculture of the North China People's Government, and 22 units including the staff training class of the Municipal Federation of Trade Unions.
     (End)

### 平市工展第三日　廿二机关团体前往参观

1949-06-09
第2版()
专栏：

　　平市工展第三日　廿二机关团体前往参观
    【本报讯】平市工业展览会昨为第三日，个人观众四千七百六十九人，团体参观者有中国共产党北平市委会，新民主主义青年团筹委会，华北人民政府农业部，市职工总会职工训练班等二十二个单位。　
     （鸣）
