Title: Fishery Administration Workers Recover Seven Sunken Bandit Ships
Author:
Date: 1949-06-07
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, Beiping, June 6th: More than fifty workers from the former Kuomintang Administrative Yuan's Post-War Rehabilitation Affairs Committee, Fishery Administration Office and Ship Repair Factory, are actively salvaging ships sunk into the river by the Kuomintang bandit gang. From May 31st to June 4th, in five days, they have already salvaged seven fishing boats from the former Fishery Administration Office, including the Yu Jing and Yu Yue. The carrying capacity of each of these ships is 200 tons. In addition, a tugboat was also recovered.

### 渔业管理处工人　捞出匪帮沉船七艘

1949-06-07
第1版()
专栏：建设新上海

　　渔业管理处工人　捞出匪帮沉船七艘
    【北平新华广播电台广播】上海六日消息：前伪行政院善后事业保管委员会渔业管理处、船舶修理厂的五十多个工人，积极打捞被国民党匪帮沉入江中的船只，从五月三十一日到六月四日五天内，已经打捞出前渔业管理处的渔京、渔粤等七艘渔船，以上各船的载重量是二百吨，另外还捞起了一艘拖船。
