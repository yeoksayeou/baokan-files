Title: Ba Dou Shan Uprising Naval Officers and Soldiers Intensify Study to Enhance Understanding
Author:
Date: 1949-06-01
Page: 1

　　【North Beiping Xinhua Broadcasting Station Broadcast】 Nanjing, 31st dispatch: The former Kuomintang Navy Second Fleet officers and soldiers who participated in the People's Liberation Army in the Ba Dou Shan Uprising are gradually enhancing their understanding of the cause of the people's liberation. After studying, they realized that only by standing on the side of the people and serving the people can they build a people's navy and establish a democratic, independent, free, and prosperous New China. They are now intensifying their study of the People's Liberation Army's three major democratic systems (political, military, and economic) and excellent work style, striving to build a people's navy.

### 笆斗山起义海军官兵　加紧学习提高认识

1949-06-01
第1版()
专栏：

　　笆斗山起义海军官兵
  　加紧学习提高认识
    【北平新华广播电台广播】南京三十一日消息：在笆斗山起义参加人民解放军的前国民党海军第二舰队全体官兵，对于人民解放事业的认识逐渐提高，他们经过学习以后，认识到只有站在人民方面为人民服务，才能建设人民的海军，才能建立民主独立自由富强的新中国。现在他们正在加紧学习人民解放军的政治、军事、经济等三大民主制度和优良作风，为建设人民的海军而努力。
