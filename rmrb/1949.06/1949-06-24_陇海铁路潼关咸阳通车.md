Title: Longhai Railway: Tongguan to Xianyang Open to Traffic
Author:
Date: 1949-06-24
Page: 1
    [Broadcast by Beiping Xinhua Broadcasting Station] Xi'an, 23rd—The five bridges, culverts and tunnels on the Tongguan-to-Xianyang section of the Longhai Railway and the Xianyang-to-Tongchuan branch line, including the Wei River, Ba River, and Jing River bridges, all of which were destroyed by the Hu bandits, have been successively completed and officially opened to traffic by the 23rd of this month, thanks to the efforts of the staff at each station.

### 陇海铁路潼关咸阳通车

1949-06-24
第1版()
专栏：

    陇海铁路潼关咸阳通车
    【北平新华广播电台广播】西安二十三日消息：陇海铁路潼关到咸阳段和咸阳到铜川支线上，所有被胡匪破坏的渭河、灞河、泾河等五座桥梁、孔道和涵洞，在各站员工的努力抢修下，本月二十三日以前，已经先后完工，正式通车。
