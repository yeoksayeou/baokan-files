Title: Hua Bei People's Government Councillor An's Remains Buried
Author: 
Date: 1949-06-26
Page: 1
　　
[This Newspaper News] Mr. An Zhiren, Councillor of the North China People's Government, passed away on the 21st of this month due to illness. His remains were buried at 7:30 AM on the 23rd in the public cemetery belonging to the North China People's Hospital.

### 华北人民政府安参议遗体已安葬

1949-06-26
第1版()
专栏：

　　华北人民政府安参议遗体已安葬
    【本报讯】华北人民政府参议安宅仁先生，于本月二十一日病逝，其遗体于二十三日七时半安葬于华北人民医院所属之公墓。
