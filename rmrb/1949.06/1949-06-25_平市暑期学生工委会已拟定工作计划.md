Title: Ping City Summer Student Work Committee Formulates Work Plan
Author:
Date: 1949-06-25
Page: 2

    Ping City Summer Student Work Committee Formulates Work Plan
    [Ping City News] The Summer Student Work Committee, organized by the Preparatory Committee of the New Democratic Youth League of Peiping and the Student Union, has formulated a summer work plan. It is now scheduled to hold a joint meeting of团支部 secretaries of major middle schools and student union chairmen of various units of the Student Union at 8:00 a.m. on the 26th at the National Art College Auditorium to discuss the steps of the work.
    In addition, the committee is scheduled to receive the press at the City League Preparatory Committee on East Chang'an Street at 3:00 p.m. on the 25th to report on the summer work plan and discuss the connection issues of youth work reporting and communication in the future.

### 平市暑期学生工委会已拟定工作计划

1949-06-25
第2版()
专栏：

    平市暑期学生工委会已拟定工作计划
    【平市讯】北平市新民主主义青年团筹委会及学联组织之暑期学生工作委员会已拟定了暑期工作计划。现定于二十六日上午八时在国立艺专礼堂举行各大中学团支部书记及学联各单位学生会主席联席会议，讨论工作的进行步骤。
    又该会定于二十五日下午三时在东长安街市团筹委会招待新闻界，报告暑期工作计划，及商讨今后青年工作报导及通讯之联系问题。
