Title: Indian Authorities Suppress Demonstrating Students; World Student Union Protests
Author:
Date: 1949-06-22
Page: 3

    Indian Authorities Suppress Demonstrating Students
    World Student Union Protests
    [Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, 21st] Prague Dispatch: The World Student Union recently protested to the Indian Ambassador in Prague regarding the Indian authorities' brutal suppression of students. During the conversation with the Indian Ambassador, the representatives of the World Student Union listed the facts of the Indian police's brutal suppression of student demonstrations.

### 印当局镇压示威学生  世界学联提出抗议

1949-06-22
第3版()
专栏：

    印当局镇压示威学生
    世界学联提出抗议
    【新华社北平二十一日电】布拉格讯：世界学联最近为印度当局对学生采取残酷镇压事，向驻布拉格的印度大使提出抗议。在与印度大使谈话中，世界学联代表列举印度警察对学生示威游行的残酷镇压的事实。
