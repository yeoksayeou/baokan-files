Title: Passed Two Provisional Motions; "July 7th" Various Units Jointly Publish Commemorative Document; All Delegates Rose to Pay Respects to Chairman Mao

Author:
Date: 1949-06-20
Page: 1

　　Passed Two Provisional Motions
　　“July 7th” Various Units Jointly Publish Commemorative Document
    All Delegates Rose to Pay Respects to Chairman Mao
　　[Xinhua News Agency, Beiping, 19th] The Preparatory Committee of the New Political Consultative Conference passed two provisional motions before its closing session this afternoon. Chairman Mao Zedong proposed that on July 7th of this year, the various parties, groups, and units participating in the Preparatory Committee of the New Political Consultative Conference jointly issue a commemorative document, which was unanimously approved. Prior to this provisional motion, representatives such as Deng Chumin, Xu Deheng, and Shen Junru proposed that all delegates rise and pay respects to the leader of the Chinese people, Chairman Mao Zedong, and the Commander-in-Chief of the Chinese People's Liberation Army, Zhu De. All delegates immediately rose unanimously, and the enthusiastic applause lasted for a long time.

### 通过两项临时动议　“七七”各单位联名发表纪念文件  全体代表起立向毛主席致敬

1949-06-20
第1版()
专栏：

　　通过两项临时动议
  　“七七”各单位联名发表纪念文件
    全体代表起立向毛主席致敬
    【新华社北平十九日电】新政治协商会议筹备会第一届全体会议在本日下午闭幕前，通过了两项临时动议。毛泽东主席提议在本年七月七日由参加新政治协商会议筹备会的各党派各团体各单位联名发表纪念文件，当经全体一致通过。在此项临时动议前，邓初民、许德珩、沈钧儒等代表提议全体代表起立向中国人民领袖毛泽东主席和中国人民解放军朱德总司令致敬。全体代表当即一致起立，热烈的掌声经久不息。
