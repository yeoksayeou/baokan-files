Title: Shen Junru and Others Telegraph Zhang Lan, Hoping to Be Informed of Travel Date
Author:
Date: 1949-06-02
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, 1st — Shen Junru, Zhang Bojun, Huang Yanpei, Zhang Dongsun and other members of the China Democratic League, along with all members of the League Headquarters Temporary Working Committee, sent a telegram on the 31st to welcome Zhang Lan, Chairman of the China Democratic League, as well as Luo Longji, Shi Liang, Guo Zeshen, and others to Beiping. The telegram stated: "We deeply admire your efforts in leading League affairs while being confined to Shanghai for so long. Now that Shanghai has been liberated, please come north together to preside over the Fourth Plenary Session, discuss the League's future decisions, we especially send this telegram to welcome you and hope to be informed of your travel date."

### 沈钧儒等电张澜盼告行期

1949-06-02
第1版()
专栏：

　　沈钧儒等电张澜盼告行期
    【新华社北平一日电】中国民主同盟沈钧儒、章伯钧、黄炎培、张东荪诸氏暨民盟总部临时工作委员会全体同人三十一日致电欢迎民盟主席张澜暨罗隆基、史良、郭则沉等来平。电称：“诸同志久困沪滨，领导盟务，殊为敬佩。上海既告解放，请即连袂北来，主持四中全会，商订本盟今后决策，特电欢迎，并盼电告行期。”
