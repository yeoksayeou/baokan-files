Title: Delegation to World Federation of Trade Unions Congress Passes Through Shenyang En Route to Foreign Country
Author:
Date: 1949-06-08
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Shenyang, 7th. The Chinese delegation of eighteen members, headed by the leader Liu Ningyi, which is to attend the Second Congress of the World Federation of Trade Unions, passed through Shenyang by special train on the evening of the 2nd en route to a foreign country. When the special train carrying the delegation passed through Shenyang, the Standing Committee members of the Northeast Federation of Trade Unions and the Shenyang Municipal Federation of Trade Unions all went to the station to see them off. The delegation departed from Beiping on the 1st. The entire delegation consists of twenty-six members, and eight members including Zhang Like, who are in Shenyang, had already gone to Harbin one day earlier to plan for matters relating to going abroad.

### 出席世界工联代表大会　我代表团过沈出国

1949-06-08
第1版()
专栏：

　　出席世界工联代表大会　我代表团过沈出国
    【新华社沈阳七日电】出席世界职工联盟第二次代表大会的中国代表团一行十八人，在团长刘宁一率领下，于二日晚乘专车过沈出国。代表团所乘专车过沈时，东北总工会及沈阳市总工会各常委，均亲至车站送行。代表团系于一日由北平出发。代表团全部团员共二十六人，其在沈团员张立克等八人已先一日赴哈尔滨筹划出国事宜。
