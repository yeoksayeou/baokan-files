　　Hero Li Guisheng Leads Women to Work Hard and Accomplish Much
    [Chahar News] Li Guisheng, director of the Women's Federation of Jiangjiagou Village, Second District, Tang County, has won three honorary titles: "Labor Hero," "Production Model," and "Model Worker." This year, after the spring began, she actively led women in production, mobilizing all 56 young and middle-aged women in the village to participate in production, and organized 38 to join the hoeing group. They can do anything: paving fields, collecting manure, carrying manure, carrying water, hoeing wheat, harrowing land, etc.
    　　　　　　　　　　(Zhang Xishui, Zhang Mingwen, Zhen Ji)

### 英雄李贵生领导拨工干活多
张希水　张明文　甄吉
1949-06-01
第2版()
专栏：妇女生产剪彩

　　英雄李贵生领导拨工干活多
    【察哈尔讯】唐县二区江家沟村妇联会主任李贵生，曾获得“劳动英雄”、“生产模范”“模范工作者”三种光荣称号，今年入春后，她积极领导妇女生产，把全村五十六个青壮妇女都发动起来参加生产，组织了三十八名参加拨工组。垫圈、起粪、担粪、担水、锄麦、耠地等什么也能干。
    　　　　　　　　　　（张希水、张明文、甄吉）
