Title: With the Help of the People's Government, People in the Suburbs of Tianjin Rebuild New Homes
Author:
Date: 1949-06-30
Page: 2

Xinhua News Agency, Tianjin, 29th. More than 8,000 disaster-stricken people in the suburbs of Tianjin, whose houses were demolished by the Kuomintang bandit troops before the liberation of Tianjin, have been rebuilding their new homes with the help of loans from the People's Government. From mid-May to early June, the People's Government, through the Tianjin Municipal People's Bank, issued interest-free loans totaling more than 75 million yuan. Currently, more than 2,100 new houses have been completed, and approximately 300 more are expected to be completed by the end of the month.

### 得人民政府帮助　津郊人民重建新居

1949-06-30
第2版()
专栏：

　　得人民政府帮助
  　津郊人民重建新居
    【新华社天津二十九日电】天津解放前房屋遭国民党匪军拆毁的市郊八千余灾民，在人民政府贷款帮助下纷纷重建新居。自五月中旬至六月初，人民政府共经津市人民银行发放无利贷款七千五百余万元。现新建房屋已完成二千一百余间，尚有约三百余间月底可完成。
