Title: Actively Revitalize Yangtze River Shipping: Shanghai Establishes Private Ship Guidance Committee
Author:
Date: 1949-06-15
Page: 3

　　Actively Revitalize Yangtze River Shipping
  　Shanghai Establishes Private Ship Guidance Committee
    [Xinhua News Agency, Shanghai, 14th] In order to rapidly revitalize Yangtze River shipping, the Shipping Department of the Shanghai Military Control Commission's Financial and Economic Takeover Committee has established a guidance committee for private shipping and a guidance committee for ship salvage and repair. The former is currently investigating the number and condition of private ships in this port, other ports, and those that have been sunk. The latter has already guided the salvage of ships such as "Zhonglian," "Haiyun," "Yifeng," "Jiangchang," "Shengli," "Shengmin," "Shenghui," and "Shengquan," as well as six large fishing boats. They are currently salvaging and preparing to salvage seven ships including the "Nanjing" and "Hongda," two warships, and fishing boats.

### 迅速活跃长江航运　沪成立私营船只指导委会

1949-06-15
第3版()
专栏：

　　迅速活跃长江航运
  　沪成立私营船只指导委会
    【新华社上海十四日电】上海军管会财经接管委员会航运处为迅速活跃长江航运，特成立对于私营航业的指导委员会和船舶打捞修理指导委员会。现前者正着手调查私营船只在本埠、外埠以及被沉没的只数和情况。后者已指导捞起“中连”、“海云”、“益丰　”、“江昌”、“生黎”、“生民”、“生辉”、“生权”等船及六艘大渔船。现正打捞和准备打捞“南京”、“鸿大”等七艘轮船，两艘军舰，及渔船等。
