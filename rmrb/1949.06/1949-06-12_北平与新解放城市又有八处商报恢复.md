Title: More Commercial Telegrams Resume in Beiping and Newly Liberated Cities
Author:
Date: 1949-06-12
Page: 2

　　[Ping City News] The Beiping Telecommunications Bureau has resumed commercial telegram services to and from eight cities from the 9th to the 11th: Dadukou, Dongliu, and Zhide in Anhui Province; Nanxiang in Jiangsu Province; Daye and Shihuiyao in Hubei Province; Puqi in Hubei Province; and Yongkang in Zhejiang Province. Please notify all sectors.

### 北平与新解放城市又有八处商报恢复

1949-06-12
第2版()
专栏：

　　北平与新解放城市又有八处商报恢复
    【平市讯】北平电信局于九日至十一日又恢复了安徽省的大渡口、东流、至德、江苏省的南翔，湖北省的大冶、石灰窑、蒲圻，及浙江省的永康等八处城市收发商报，请各界注意。
