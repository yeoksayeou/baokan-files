Title: Chen Qihan Appointed Commander of Jiangxi Military Region, Yang Chengwu Concurrently Commander of Tianjin Garrison

Author:
Date: 1949-06-30
Page: 1

　　Xinhua News Agency, Nanchang, 29th: The Jiangxi Military Region of the People's Liberation Army has been ordered to be established. The Military Commission of the Chinese People's Revolutionary Committee has appointed Chen Qihan as Commander, Chen Zhengren as Political Commissar, and Yang Guofu as Deputy Commander. Commander Chen and others reported for duty on the 25th.
　　
　　Beijing Xinhua Broadcasting Station broadcast: Tianjin, 28th: The Military Commission of the Chinese People's Revolutionary Committee has appointed Yang Chengwu concurrently as Commander of the Tianjin Garrison, and Wang Shiying as Deputy Commander. General Yang Chengwu and others have reported for duty.

### 陈奇涵任江西军区司令员  杨成武兼天津警备司令

1949-06-30
第1版()
专栏：

　　陈奇涵任江西军区司令员
    杨成武兼天津警备司令
    【新华社南昌二十九日电】人民解放军江西军区，已奉令成立。中国人民革命军事委员会任命陈奇涵为司令员，陈正人为政治委员，杨国夫为副司令员。陈司令员等已于二十五日到职视事。
    【北平新华广播电台广播】天津二十八日消息：中国人民革命军事委员会任命杨成武兼天津警备司令，王世英为副司令。杨成武将军等已经到职工作。
