Title: Liulihe Cement Plant Completes More Than Half of Three-Month Production Plan in One Month
Author:
Date: 1949-06-01
Page: 2
　　Liulihe Cement Plant

　　Three-Month Production Plan More Than Half Completed in One Month

　　[Ping City News] The Liulihe Cement Plant planned to produce 10,000 tons of cement in three months. Due to the establishment of a new labor attitude among the workers, in the first month after starting work, from April 15th to May 15th, the output of cement was more than 5,718 tons, exceeding the average expected monthly output by 71.5 percent. At this rate of production efficiency, the three-month plan can be completed in just over fifty days.

### 琉璃河水泥厂　三个月生产计划一月完成一半多

1949-06-01
第2版()
专栏：

　　琉璃河水泥厂
  　三个月生产计划一月完成一半多
    【平市讯】琉璃河水泥厂计划在三个月内生产水泥一万吨，由于工人们建立了新的劳动态度，在开工后的第一个月从四月十五日到五月十五日，就出产了水泥五千七百一十八吨多，超过平均预计月产量的百分之七十一点五。按照这样的生产效率，三个月的计划只须五十多天就可全部完成。
