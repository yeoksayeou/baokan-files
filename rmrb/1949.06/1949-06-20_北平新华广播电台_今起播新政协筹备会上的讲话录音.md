Title: Beiping Xinhua Broadcasting Station to Broadcast Recordings of Speeches from the New Political Consultative Conference Preparatory Meeting Starting Today
Author:
Date: 1949-06-20
Page: 1

　　Beiping Xinhua Broadcasting Station
　　To Broadcast Recordings of Speeches from the New Political Consultative Conference Preparatory Meeting Starting Today
    [This newspaper dispatch] Beiping Xinhua Broadcasting Station will broadcast the opening address by Vice Chairman Zhou Enlai and the recorded speeches of Chairman Mao Zedong, Commander-in-Chief Zhu De, Mr. Li Jishen, Mr. Shen Junru, Mr. Guo Moruo, Mr. Chen Shutong, and Mr. Chen Jiageng at the opening ceremony of the New Political Consultative Conference Preparatory Meeting tonight; the broadcast will be split into four days, starting tonight. The station will broadcast the opening address by Vice Chairman Zhou Enlai and the speech of Chairman Mao from 19:50 to 20:10 tonight. The speeches of Commander-in-Chief Zhu De and Mr. Li Jishen and the other six speakers will be broadcast at the same time on the 21st, 22nd, and 23rd. Starting today, the station will change its broadcasting schedule. Today's loop program will be broadcast from 19:30 to 19:50, featuring the All-China Democratic Women's Federation discussing "Introducing Two Problems Resolved by the North China Women's Representative Conference." The arts and culture program will air from 22:20 to 22:50, featuring the North China Military Region Political Department Military Band performing military songs, a total of seven songs:
　　(1) The Red Flag Song; (2) New Soldiers' March; (3) Cavalry March; (4) Ode to Heroes; (5) Military Song Medley One; (6) Military Song Medley Two; (7) Military Song Medley Three.
　　Xinhua News Agency Correction
    In the fourth section of Feng Wenbin's concluding remarks at the Youth League Congress (published on page two of this newspaper on the 17th): "In five years, we will increase the existing 10 percent of industry to 30 percent," should be corrected to "Within ten to fifteen years, we will increase the existing 10 percent of industry to 30 to 40 percent."
    In the fourth paragraph of the draft decision on the establishment of the National Railway Working Committee of the Youth League (published on page one of this newspaper on the 18th), "and limited to the end of the month to establish the railway branch bureau..." should be corrected to "In the old liberated areas, limited to the end of July (gradually established in the new areas), establish the railway branch bureau..." We hereby make this correction.

### 北平新华广播电台　今起播新政协筹备会上的讲话录音

1949-06-20
第1版()
专栏：

　　北平新华广播电台
  　今起播新政协筹备会上的讲话录音
    【本报讯】北平新华广播电台今天晚上播送新的政治协商会议筹备会开幕典礼上周恩来副主席的开幕词和毛泽东主席、朱德总司令、李济深先生、沈钧儒先生、郭沫若先生、陈叔通先生、陈嘉庚先生等七人的讲话录音片；从今天晚上起分四天播送。该台今晚十九时五十分起到二十点十分，播送周恩来副主席的开幕词和毛主席的讲话。朱德总司令和李济深先生等六个人的讲话。将在二十一、二十二、二十三三天同时间分别播送。该台从今天起，改变播音时间，今天的轮回节目，从十九点三十分到十九点五十分，播送中华全国民主妇女联合会主讲的“介绍华北妇女代表会议解决的两个问题”，从二十二点二十分到二十二点五十分文艺节目，今天的文艺节目是人民解放军华北军区政治部军乐队的军歌演奏，一共演奏七支歌曲：
    （一）红旗歌；（二）新战士进行曲；（三）骑兵进行曲；（四）英雄赞；（五）军歌联奏之一；（六）军歌联奏之二；（七）军歌联奏之三。
　　新华社更正
    冯文彬在青年团代表大会上的结论稿（载十七日本报二版）第四节中：“在五年后，我们要将现有的占百分之十的工业，提高到占百分之三十”，应改为“在十年至十五年之内，我们要将现有的占百分之十的工业，提高到占百分之三十到四十”。
    青年团建立全国铁道工作委员会的决定稿（载十八日本报一版）第四段中，“并限于月底以前把铁道分局……”，应改为“在老解放区，限于七月底以前（在新区逐渐建立），把铁道分局……”。特此更正。
