Title: French-Soviet Association Congress Opens in Paris
Author:
Date: 1949-06-07
Page: 3

　　Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, June 6th: TASS, Paris—The fourth congress of the French-Soviet Association opened here on the 4th. The meeting is being held under the slogan "Win Peace by Strengthening French-Soviet Friendship" and is chaired by Curie and General Petit.

### 法苏协会代表大会在巴黎揭幕

1949-06-07
第3版()
专栏：

　　法苏协会代表大会在巴黎揭幕
    【新华社北平六日电】塔斯社巴黎讯：法苏协会第四届代表大会于四日在此间开幕。会议在“以加强法苏友谊来赢得和平”的口号下举行，由居里和柏蒂将军担任主席。
