Title: Anglo-Soviet Trade Friendship Conference Receives Broad Popular Support
Author:
Date: 1949-06-11
Page: 3

　　Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, 10th: TASS, London: The Conference for Peace, Trade, and Friendship with the Soviet Union, scheduled to be held in London on June 12, has now received the broadest support from British progressives. According to the "Workers' Daily": the conference represents approximately two million people. The delegates and observers already presumed for the conference have reached 440, including representatives from 29 trade union committees, representatives from 10 local trade union committees, 41 cooperative societies, and representatives from many trade union branches and other organizations.

### 英对苏贸易友好大会获得广大人民的支持

1949-06-11
第3版()
专栏：

　　英对苏贸易友好大会获得广大人民的支持
    【新华社北平十日电】塔斯社伦敦讯：定于六月十二日在伦敦举行的对苏和平贸易与友好会议，现已获得英进步人士的最广泛支持。据“工人日报”称：该会议约代表二百万人民。已被推定的大会代表与观察员已达四百四十名，其中包括二十九个工会委员会的代表，十个工会地方委员会的代表，四十一个合作社及许多工会分会与其他团体的代表。
