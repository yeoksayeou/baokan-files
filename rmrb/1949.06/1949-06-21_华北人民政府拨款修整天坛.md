Title: The North China People's Government Allocates Funds to Repair the Temple of Heaven
Author: Ji Qing
Date: 1949-06-21
Page: 2

The North China People's Government Allocates Funds to Repair the Temple of Heaven
[This newspaper's dispatch] The Financial Department of the North China People's Government has allocated 3.8 million People's Currency to repair ancient buildings in Beiping, such as the Imperial College and the Temple of Heaven, as repair expenses. The Cultural Relics Arrangement Committee has now formulated a repair plan and work will begin soon. (Ji Qing)

### 华北人民政府拨款修整天坛
纪清
1949-06-21
第2版()
专栏：

    华北人民政府拨款修整天坛
    【本报讯】华北人民政府财政部为修整北平国子监、天坛等处古代建筑，特拨款人民券三百八十万元，做为整修费。现已由文物整理委员会拟定修整计划，不日即可动工。      （纪清）
