Title: Peiping Xinhua Broadcasting Station to Add New Programs Starting on the 20th

Author:
Date: 1949-06-17
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Peiping, 16th - The Peiping Xinhua Broadcasting Station will add Taiwanese news, Chaozhou news, Cantonese news, Japanese news, rotating programs for workers, youth, and women, as well as lecture, art, and entertainment programs starting on the 20th of this month. Broadcast time will increase from twice to four times a day, from nine to twelve hours a day. The newly scheduled broadcast times and program schedules are as follows:

First Broadcast:
6:00 to 6:10 AM　Opening Music, Report on Morning Programs
6:10 to 6:25 AM　Japanese News
6:25 to 6:40 AM　Taiwanese News
6:40 to 6:55 AM　Chaozhou News
6:55 to 7:10 AM　Cantonese News
7:10 to 7:25 AM　Mandarin News (First Broadcast)
7:25 to 7:40 AM　Comprehensive Report
7:40 to 7:50 AM　Music
7:50 to 8:00 AM　Preview of the Day's Programs, Time Check
8:00 to 8:30 AM　(Entertainment Program - Sundays and Holidays Only)

Second Broadcast:
12:30 to 12:35 PM　Opening Music
12:35 to 12:50 PM　Mandarin News (Second Broadcast)
12:50 to 1:00 PM　Report on Tonight's Programs, Time Check

Third Broadcast:
1:30 to 4:30 PM　News Program for Field Armies

Fourth Broadcast:
6:00 to 6:10 PM　Opening Music, Report on Tonight's Programs
6:10 to 6:15 PM　Records (South China Music)
6:15 to 6:30 PM　Taiwanese News
6:30 to 6:45 PM　Chaozhou News
6:45 to 7:00 PM　Cantonese News
7:00 to 7:05 PM　Records (Japanese Music)
7:05 to 7:20 PM　Japanese News
7:20 to 7:30 PM　Records (National Music)
7:30 to 7:50 PM　Rotating Programs for Workers, Youth, and Women
7:50 to 8:10 PM　Lecture Program
8:10 to 8:30 PM　Entertainment Program
8:30 to 8:50 PM　Mandarin News (Third Broadcast)
8:50 to 9:00 PM　Comprehensive Report (City Construction, Production Reports, etc.)
9:00 to 9:15 PM　Commentary
9:15 to 9:30 PM　International Affairs
9:30 to 10:00 PM　English News
10:00 to 10:20 PM　Mandarin News (Fourth Broadcast)
10:20 to 10:50 PM　Art and Literature Program
10:50 to 11:00 PM　Preview of Tomorrow's Programs, Time Check
　　　　　　　　　　Concluding Music
11:00 to 12:00 PM　Recorded News

The station's wavelengths are 29.24 meters, 10260 kHz; 33.2 meters, 9040 kHz; 40 meters, 7500 kHz; 42 meters, 7100 kHz; 49.2 meters, 6096 kHz; 441 meters, 680 kHz.
