Title: Albania Prepares for Parliamentary Elections
Author:
Date: 1949-06-01
Page: 3

　　Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, 30th. The Albanian News Agency reports: Preparations for the elections of the Albanian People's Assembly are actively underway, with the organizations of the Democratic Front and the Trade Unions playing an important role in this preparation. Many groups from women's organizations go to the countryside to explain to rural women the importance of the upcoming elections for consolidating the people's regime. Trade Unions have organized meetings in various enterprises. The Albanian working people wholeheartedly support the candidates of the Democratic Front.

### 阿尔巴尼亚筹备选举议会

1949-06-01
第3版()
专栏：

　　阿尔巴尼亚筹备选举议会
    【新华社北平三十日电】阿尔巴尼亚通讯社报道：阿尔巴尼亚人民议会的选举，正在积极筹备中，民主阵线和职工会的组织在这一筹备工作中起着重要的作用。妇女团体的许多小组，到农村去向农村妇女解释即将举行的选举对巩固人民政权的重要性。职工会在各个企业中组织了会议。阿尔巴尼亚劳动人民都衷心地拥护民主阵线的候选人。
