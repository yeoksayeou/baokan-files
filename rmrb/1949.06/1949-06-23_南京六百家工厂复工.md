Title: Six Hundred Factories in Nanjing Resume Production
Author:
Date: 1949-06-23
Page: 1

    Six Hundred Factories in Nanjing Resume Production
    Thanks to the massive loans and support from the People's Government, more than six hundred of the two thousand three hundred private factories in Nanjing have resumed production. The production volume is gradually increasing, and sales are growing day by day. After receiving assistance from the trade company, Youheng Flour Mill and Pufeng Flour Mill's May production exceeded the original plan by more than double.

### 南京六百家工厂复工

1949-06-23
第1版()
专栏：

    南京六百家工厂复工
    南京私营的二千三百多家工厂，由于人民政府大量贷款扶植，已经有六百多家复工。生产量逐渐提高，销路一天比一天增加。有恒面粉厂和普丰面粉厂得到贸易公司扶助以后，五月份的生产量已经超过了原定计划一倍以上。
