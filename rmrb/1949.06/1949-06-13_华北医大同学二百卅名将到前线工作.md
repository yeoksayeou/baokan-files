Title: 230 Students from North China Medical University to Work on the Front Lines
Author: Run Cang, Ruo Feng
Date: 1949-06-13
Page: 2

　　North China Medical University
  　230 Students to Work on the Front Lines

    [This newspaper dispatch] This newspaper reports that the fourth, fifth, sixth, seventh, and eighth graduating classes of North China Medical University have graduated simultaneously in recent days. Of these 230 people's doctors, more than 80 percent will work on the front lines, and the first group has already departed on the 6th of this month. (Run Cang, Ruo Feng)

### 华北医大同学二百卅名将到前线工作
润苍　若风
1949-06-13
第2版()
专栏：

　　华北医大
  　同学二百卅名将到前线工作
    【本报讯】华北医大四、五、六、七、八共五期同学，已于日前同时毕业。这二百三十名人民医生中，有百分之八十以上的同志将到前线工作，本月六日首批已出发。　　　　　　　　　　　（润苍、若风）
