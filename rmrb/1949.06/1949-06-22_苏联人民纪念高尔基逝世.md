Title: Soviet People Commemorate Gorky's Death
Author:
Date: 1949-06-22
Page: 3
Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, June 21st: Moscow dispatch: Throughout the Soviet Union, grand gatherings were widely held on the 18th to commemorate the thirteenth anniversary of the death of the great writer Maxim Gorky. Factories, schools, and workers' and soldiers' clubs organized reports, symposiums, evening parties, and exhibitions. The Pravda published the poem "Contemporary Great Man" by the poet Aleksei Surkov, Izvestia published "The Founder of Socialist Literature," and the Komsomolskaya Pravda published "Fighter for Peace and Democracy" to commemorate this literary giant.

### 苏联人民纪念高尔基逝世

1949-06-22
第3版()
专栏：

    苏联人民纪念高尔基逝世
    【新华社北平二十一日电】莫斯科讯：苏联全国各地于十八日广泛地举行大会，纪念伟大作家高尔基逝世十三周年。工厂、学校、工人和军队的俱乐部都组织了报告、座谈会、晚会和展览会。真理报刊载了诗人列克雪·苏尔科夫所作的“当代伟人”，消息报发表了“社会主义文学的缔造者”，共产主义青年团真理报则登载了“和平与民主的斗士”，来忆念此一代文豪。
