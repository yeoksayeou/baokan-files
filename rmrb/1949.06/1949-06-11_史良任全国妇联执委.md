Title: Shi Liang Appointed Executive Committee Member of the All-China Women's Federation
Author:
Date: 1949-06-11
Page: 1

　　Shi Liang Appointed Executive Committee Member of the All-China Women's Federation
    [Xinhua News Agency, Beiping, 10th] At the fifth meeting of its Standing Committee held on the 9th, the All-China Democratic Women's Federation passed a resolution to elect Shi Liang as a member of the Executive Committee. This is a supplement based on the number of Executive Committee members reserved when the First National Congress of Chinese Women elected the Executive Committee.

### 史良任全国妇联执委

1949-06-11
第1版()
专栏：

　　史良任全国妇联执委
    【新华社北平十日电】中华全国民主妇女联合会在九日举行的第五次常务委员会上，通过选举史良为该会执行委员。这是根据中国妇女第一次全国代表大会选举该会执行委员时所留名额而补充的。
