Title: Huang Yanpei and Others Travel to Shanghai on Official Business
Author:
Date: 1949-06-22
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, 21st—Following the conclusion of the first plenary session of the Preparatory Committee of the New Political Consultative Conference, Preparatory Committee members Huang Yanpei, Hu Ziying, Ma Yinchu, Wu Yaozong, Wang Shao'ao, Xu Guangping, Lin Handa, Chen Shutong, Sheng Pihua, Bao Dasan, Zhang Bojun, Wu Gengmei, Deng Yingchao, Zhang Qinqiu, Deng Yuzhi, and others, totaling a group of fifteen, departed from Beiping at 8:00 PM on the 21st via special train to Shanghai to attend to official business. Upon completion of their tasks, they will return to Beiping to participate in the preparatory work for the new Political Consultative Conference.

### 黄炎培等因公赴沪

1949-06-22
第1版()
专栏：

    黄炎培等因公赴沪
    【新华社北平二十一日电】新政治协商会议筹备会首届全体会议闭幕后，筹备委员黄炎培、胡子婴、马寅初、吴耀宗、王绍鏊、许广平、林汉达。陈叔通、盛丕华、包达三、张＠伯、吴羹梅、邓颖超、张琴秋、邓裕志等一行于二十一日下午八时由平乘专车赴沪，料理公务，事毕，仍将返平参加新的政治协商会议的筹备工作。
