Title: British Railway Workers Continue Strike Against New Timetable
Author:
Date: 1949-06-09
Page: 3

　　British Railway Workers Continue Strike
　　Against New Timetable
    Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, 8th: TASS, London: The Sunday strike by railway workers against the new timetable is expanding. On the 5th, the third Sunday strike, no on-duty workers on the Scottish railways reported for work. Representatives of 800 railway workers in eastern England have also decided to join the fourth Sunday strike on the 12th.

### 英铁路工人继续罢工　反对新行车时间表

1949-06-09
第3版()
专栏：

　　英铁路工人继续罢工
  　反对新行车时间表
    【新华社北平八日电】塔斯社伦敦讯：铁路工人为反对新的行车时间表而举行的星期日罢工正在扩展中。五日是第三次星期日罢工，苏格兰铁路的值班工人都没有上工。英国东部八百名铁路工人的代表，也决定将于十二日参加第四次星期日罢工。
