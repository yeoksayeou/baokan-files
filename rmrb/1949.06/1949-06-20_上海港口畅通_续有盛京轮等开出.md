Title: Shanghai Port Open, Shengjing and Other Ships Continue to Depart
Author:
Date: 1949-06-20
Page: 3
　
　　Shanghai Port Open
  　Shengjing and Other Ships Continue to Depart
    [Beiping Xinhua Broadcasting Station Broadcast] Shanghai, 19th—Ocean shipping from Shanghai is now unobstructed. The Taikoo Company's Shengjing departed from Shanghai for Hong Kong on the afternoon of the 18th. An American ship departed from Tianjin and is expected to arrive in Shanghai on the 19th. Yesterday, ships departing from Shanghai included the Fuxing No. 2 and the Renmin Jiang'an No. 7, among others.

### 上海港口畅通　续有盛京轮等开出

1949-06-20
第3版()
专栏：

　　上海港口畅通
  　续有盛京轮等开出
    【北平新华广播电台广播】上海十九日消息：上海海洋线航运，现在已经通行无阻。太古公司盛京轮十八日下午从上海开往香港，美轮一艘从天津开出，十九日可以到达上海，昨天从上海出口的船只，有复兴二号和人民江安号等七艘。
