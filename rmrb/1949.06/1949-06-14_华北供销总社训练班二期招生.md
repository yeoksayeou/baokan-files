Title: North China General Supply and Marketing Cooperative Training Class Second Enrollment

Author: Ru

Date: 1949-06-14
Page: 2

North China General Supply and Marketing Cooperative Training Class Second Enrollment

[Our Newspaper] The first class of 430 graduates from the cooperative training class organized by the North China General Supply and Marketing Cooperative has been assigned to various regions to begin work. The second class is now open for enrollment. This class will mainly train specialized personnel in cotton inspection to meet the needs of establishing cotton production and sales cooperatives.

### 华北供销总社训练班二期招生

1949-06-14
第2版()
专栏：

　　华北供销总社训练班二期招生
    【本报讯】华北供销合作总社主办之合作训练班第一期毕业学员四百三十人，已分配至各地区开始工作。现第二期开始招生，此期主要培养棉花检别的专门人材，以应建立棉花产销合作社之需要。
    　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　（儒）
