Title: China Property Insurance Company Entrusts Postal Savings and Remittance Bureau to Handle Business
Author: Yu
Date: 1949-06-19
Page: 2

Economic News

　　China Property Insurance Company
  　Entrusts Postal Savings and Remittance Bureau to Handle Business
    [Our Newspaper] In order to expand its business, China Property Insurance Company Limited has entrusted the Municipal Postal Savings and Remittance Bureau, the South City Office of the People's Bank of China, and the Bank of Communications to act as agents for the company's insurance business. The Bank of Communications has already underwritten two fire insurance policies.
　（Yu）

### 中国产物保险公司　托储汇局代办业务
宇
1949-06-19
第2版()
专栏：经济新闻

　　中国产物保险公司
  　托储汇局代办业务
    【本报讯】中国产物股份保险公司为扩展业务，特委托本市邮政储金汇业局、人民银行南城办事处、交通银行代理该公司办理保险业务，其中交通银行并已承保火险两笔。　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　
　（宇）
