Title: Hankou People's Bank Has Established Remittance Services with Beijing, Tianjin, Shanghai, and Other Places
Author:
Date: 1949-06-30
Page: 1

　　Xinhua News Agency, Hankou, 29th: Since its opening on the first of this month, the Hankou Branch of the People's Bank of China has been developing its various businesses. As of the 25th, it has absorbed various deposits totaling 2.717 billion yuan in Renminbi and issued industrial loans of 288.82 million yuan in Renminbi. It has exchanged 79,400 silver dollars, more than 60 taels of gold, and more than 24,000 US dollars and Hong Kong dollars. Remittance services are now available in nine locations including Shanghai, Tianjin, Beijing, Xuzhou, Zhengzhou, Kaifeng, Xinyang, Jiujiang, and Nanchang. The bank is currently in contact with sixteen other locations including Nanjing, Hangzhou, and Qingdao, and remittance services will be available there soon.

　　Furthermore, it is reported that the Hankou Jiaotong Bank plans to establish an office in the Shihuiyao industrial area southeast of Wuhan in the near future to handle industrial deposits, loans, and other related business.

### 汉口人民银行已与平津沪等地通汇

1949-06-30
第1版()
专栏：

　　汉口人民银行已与平津沪等地通汇
    【新华社汉口二十九日电】中国人民银行汉口分行自本月一日开业以来，各项业务均有开展，截至二十五日，吸收各种存款共达人民币二十七亿一千七百余万元，发放出工业贷款人民币二亿八千八百余万元。兑入银元七万九千四百余枚，黄金六十余两，美钞及港币二万四千余元。通汇地点已有上海、天津、北平、徐州、郑州、开封、信阳、九江、南昌等九处；现该行正与南京、杭州、青岛等十六处进行联系，不日亦可通汇。
    又讯：汉口交通银行拟于最近在武汉东南的工业区石灰窑设立办事处，办理工业存放款及其他有关业务。
