Title: Shanghai Merchants Steam Navigation Bureau and Others Loan Wages to Employees
Author:
Date: 1949-06-03
Page: 1
　
　　[Broadcast by Beiping Xinhua Broadcasting Station] Shanghai, 2nd – The Shanghai Military Control Commission's Finance and Economics Takeover Committee Shipping Department, in order to quickly stabilize the lives of staff, has decided to temporarily loan wages. The units taken over by the department, including the China Merchants Steam Navigation Bureau, China Barge Transportation Company, Shanghai Navigation Administration Bureau, Pudong Shipyard, and Taiwan Navigation Company Shanghai Branch, have decided to temporarily loan RMB 2,000 to all employees to maintain their livelihoods before wage and salary standards are set. Those with particularly difficult living conditions can continue to borrow. It is also stipulated that employees who were dismissed and unemployed before liberation for carrying out anti-retreat and ship-guarding struggles will enjoy the same treatment. Appropriate care will also be given to the families in Shanghai of employees who have gone to the Kuomintang-controlled areas.

### 上海招商局等借给职工工资

1949-06-03
第1版()
专栏：

　　上海招商局等借给职工工资
    【北平新华广播电台广播】上海二日消息：上海军管会财政经济接管委员会航运处为了迅速安定职工人员生活，决定暂借工资。该处所接管的招商局、中华驳运公司、上海航政局、浦东造船厂、台湾航行公司上海分公司等单位，在职工人员工资薪水还没有规定以前，决定一律暂借人民币两千元，维持职工生活，生活特别困难的，还可以续借；并且规定在解放以前，因为进行反撤退斗争和护船斗争被开除失业的职工，都享有同样待遇；对于已经去国民党统治区的职工人员留沪家属，也给予适当照顾。
