Title: Eastern Zhejiang: We Capture Linhai; Enemy Troops of 4,000 Surrender
Author:
Date: 1949-06-25
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Hangzhou, 24th. The People's Liberation Army liberated Linhai County in eastern Zhejiang on the evening of the 15th. The defending enemy, Qi Yongnian's three regiments, totaling 3,900+ men, surrendered to us.

### 浙东我克临海守敌四千人投诚

1949-06-25
第1版()
专栏：

    浙东我克临海守敌四千人投诚
    【新华社杭州二十四日电】人民解放军十五日晚解放浙江东部的临海县城，守敌戚永年部三个团三千九百余人向我投诚。
