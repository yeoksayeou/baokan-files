Title: He Xiangning and Others Send Telegram of Condolences to Soong Ching Ling
Author:
Date: 1949-06-07
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, Beiping, 6th. Leading figures of the Beiping women's circle, He Xiangning, Cai Chang, Deng Yingchao, Li Dequan, and Xu Guangping, today jointly sent a telegram of condolences to Ms. Soong Ching Ling. The telegram reads: To Ms. Soong Ching Ling, care of the Shanghai Municipal Government:

　　We were once forced to separate and struggle together for freedom and democracy. We have always been concerned about and miss you. Now that Shanghai belongs to the people, and national liberation is about to arrive, our common hope will soon be fully realized. We send our condolences and respect.

　　He Xiangning, Cai Chang, Deng Yingchao, Li Dequan, Xu Guangping
  　　　　June 6th

### 何香凝等电慰宋庆龄

1949-06-07
第1版()
专栏：

　　何香凝等电慰宋庆龄
    【新华社北平六日电】北平妇女界领袖何香凝、蔡畅、邓颖超、李德全、许广平，今日联名致电慰问宋庆龄女士，电文如下：上海市政府转孙夫人宋庆龄先生：
    我们曾被迫分隔两地，共同为自由民主奋斗，我们对你的进退与怀念，无时或已，现在上海已为人民所有，全国解放即将来到，我们共同的希望不久即可完全实现。谨电慰问，并致敬意。
    何香凝、蔡畅、邓颖超、李德全、许广平
  　　　　六月六日
