Title: British Groups and Trade Unions Support Peaceful Trade with the Soviet Union, Requesting Attendance at National Conference
Author:
Date: 1949-06-01
Page: 3
Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, 30th - London: The organizers of the Peace, Trade, and Friendship with the Soviet Union Conference announced on May 28th that the conference is gaining support from across the country. Two weeks prior, the organizers had already received requests from twelve national organizations and numerous local groups to attend the conference. The South Wales branch of the National Union of Mineworkers and its twenty-four trade union committees, thirty-five branches of the Amalgamated Engineering Union, and thirty cooperative groups have announced their preparation to send representatives to the conference.

### 英各地团体工会　拥护对苏和平贸易请示出席全国会议

1949-06-01
第3版()
专栏：

　　英各地团体工会
  　拥护对苏和平贸易请示出席全国会议
    【新华社北平三十日电】伦敦讯：对苏和平、贸易及友好大会的组织者于五月二十八日宣称：大会正得到全国各地的拥护。还在两星期以前，大会的组织者就已经接到十二个全国性团体和许多地方团体出席大会的请求。全国矿工联合会南威尔斯分会及其二十四个工会委员会、机器工人工会的三十五个分会及三十个合作社团体已宣布准备派遣代表出席大会。
