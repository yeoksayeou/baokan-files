Title: Indexed Savings Launched in Beijing, Shanghai, and Hangzhou
Author:
Date: 1949-06-25
Page: 1

    Xinhua News Agency dispatch: People's Banks and the Bank of China in Shanghai, Nanjing, and Hangzhou have successively launched indexed savings, which have been warmly supported by citizens. In Shanghai, the Bank of China received deposits from 8,003 households, totaling over 158,000 units, in the three days from the 14th to the 16th. In Nanjing, the People's Bank of China, along with entrusted branches of the Bank of China and the Farmers Bank, have seen daily increases in deposits of indexed savings since the 20th. On the 21st alone, a total of over 15,000 units were deposited. The People's Bank of Hangzhou also launched indexed savings on the 20th. The bank is currently in discussions with the Hangzhou Power Plant and the Post Office regarding collective savings.

### 京沪杭举办折实储蓄

1949-06-25
第1版()
专栏：

    京沪杭举办折实储蓄
    【本报讯】综合新华社电：上海、南京、杭州三市的人民银行与中国银行等已先后举办折实储蓄，并已得到市民的热烈拥护。沪市中国银行十四日至十六日三天中即已存入八千零三户十五万八千余单位。南京人民银行以及委托的中国银行和农民银行等三处，自二十日起，所举办的折实储蓄，存入单位日有增加，二十一日一天内，共存入一万五千余单位。杭州市人民银行，亦于二十日举办折实储蓄。该行正与杭市发电厂、邮局等洽商集体储蓄。
