Title: Bank of Communications in Tianjin Supports Private Ironworks
Author:
Date: 1949-06-01
Page: 1

In order to support iron industry production and supply the needs of construction, the Tianjin Bank of Communications, on the 28th of last month, loaned 7.8 million yuan each to the private Tianxing Ironworks and Baokou Iron Factory in the form of orders. The term is 50 days, with a monthly interest rate of 1.2% in kind. On the 31st, an additional 10 million yuan was loaned to the two factories for temporary working capital. After obtaining the loans, the two factories purchased 500 tons of iron materials in bulk from the Kailuan Mining Bureau, which is sufficient for three months of production.

### 天津交通银行扶助私营铁工厂

1949-06-01
第1版()
专栏：

　　天津交通银行扶助私营铁工厂
    【本报讯】天津交通银行为扶助铁业生产，以供应生产建设需要，上月二十八日以定货方式，贷给私营天兴制铁所和宝口铁工厂各七百八十万元，期限五十天，实物月息一分二厘，三十一日再贷给临时周转资金两厂共一千万元。该两厂借得款后已从开滦矿务局整批购入铁料五百吨，足供三个月生产之用。
