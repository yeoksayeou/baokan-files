Title: Provisional Measures for Handling Beggars in North China Region: Implementation Temporarily Postponed Except in Beijing and Tianjin
Author:
Date: 1949-06-07
Page: 2

[This City News] The North China People's Government has issued a notice regarding the implementation of the "Provisional Measures for Handling Beggars in the North China Region" promulgated on May 21st, deciding that the measures will be implemented first in Beijing and Tianjin, while implementation in other areas will be temporarily postponed. During implementation, special attention should be paid to quickly organizing beggars for production or implementing work-relief programs, in order to minimize financial expenditures as much as possible. The measures will be promoted in other areas after experience is gained in Beijing and Tianjin.

### 华北区处理乞丐暂行办法　除平津外暂缓执行

1949-06-07
第2版()
专栏：

　　华北区处理乞丐暂行办法　除平津外暂缓执行
    【本市讯】华北人民政府顷对执行五月二十一日颁布之“华北区处理乞丐暂行办法”发出通知，决定该办法先在平、津两地进行，其他各地暂缓执行。在执行中，应特别注意迅速组织乞丐生产或以工代赈，以求尽量减少财政开支，俟平、津两地取得经验后，再行推广。
