Title: Tanggu Fourth Salt Production Area Sees Significant Output Increase in May
Author:
Date: 1949-06-09
Page: 2
Economic News

　　Tanggu Fourth Salt Production Area Sees Significant Output Increase in May
    [Our dispatch] The four salt production areas under the Tanggu Salt Affairs Administration, namely Tanggu, Dagu, Xinhe, and Denggu, produced a total of 2,984,866 dan of salt in May. Among these, public salt fields (185 fu) produced 1,887,810 dan; private salt fields (98 fu) produced 1,097,056 dan. This represents an increase of more than double the output compared to April (1,487,156 dan).

### 塘沽四盐产区　五月份产量大增

1949-06-09
第2版()
专栏：经济新闻

　　塘沽四盐产区　五月份产量大增
    【本报讯】塘沽盐务管理局所属塘沽、大沽、新河、邓沽等四个盐区，五月份共产盐二九八四八六六担。内公滩一八五付、产盐一八八七八一○担；私滩九八付，产盐一○九七○五六担。较四月份（一四八七一五六担）增加产量一倍强。
