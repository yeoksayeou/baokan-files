Title: Wuhan Municipal Trade Union Preparatory Committee Established
Author:
Date: 1949-06-15
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, Hankou, 14th. Representatives from various industries in Wuhan held a meeting on the 11th and unanimously approved the establishment of the Wuhan Municipal Trade Union Preparatory Committee. Zhang Pinghua, Secretary of the Wuhan Municipal Committee of the Chinese Communist Party, Cai Shubin, Zhang Jinshen, Liao Siguang, Chen Dongshan, Huang Minwei, Jiang Haoran, Liu Shi, Chen Huanying, Li Qixin, Deng Xiang, Yuan Daohua, and twenty-five others were elected as members of the preparatory committee. Telegrams were also sent to Chairman Mao, Commander-in-Chief Zhu, and all commanders and fighters of the Fourth Field Army to pay their respects.

### 武汉市职工总会筹委会成立

1949-06-15
第1版()
专栏：

　　武汉市职工总会筹委会成立
    【新华社汉口十四日电】武汉各业职工于十一日举行代表会议，一致通过成立武汉市职工总会筹备委员会。选出中共武汉市委书记张平化及蔡树彬、张金深、廖似光、陈东山、黄民伟、江浩然、刘实、陈焕英、李启新、邓祥、袁道华等二十五人为筹备委员会委员。并分别通电向毛主席、朱总司令及第四野战军全体指战员致敬。
