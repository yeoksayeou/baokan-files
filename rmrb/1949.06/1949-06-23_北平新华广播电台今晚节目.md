Title: Tonight's Program on Beiping Xinhua Broadcasting Station
Author:
Date: 1949-06-23
Page: 1

    Beiping Xinhua Broadcasting Station Tonight's Program
    [This newspaper reported] Beiping Xinhua Broadcasting Station will broadcast Mr. Ma Sicong's violin performance of new works including the Suiyuan Suite, Pastoral, and Shaman Dance in tonight's arts and culture program. These three pieces are violin solos based on Suiyuan folk music, North China folk songs, and the dance music of Mongolian shamanic festivals. The composer, Mr. Ma Sicong, will personally perform them on Beiping Xinhua Broadcasting Station starting at 22:20 tonight, with piano accompaniment by piano expert Mr. Wang Muli. In addition, the station will broadcast recordings of speeches by Mr. Chen Shutong and Mr. Chen Jiageng at the opening ceremony of the Preparatory Committee of the New Political Consultative Conference during the speech program from 19:50 to 20:00 tonight. The rotation program from 19:30 to 19:50 tonight is Women's Time, broadcasting "The Participation of Rural Women in Agricultural Production in Liberated Areas of China."

### 北平新华广播电台今晚节目

1949-06-23
第1版()
专栏：

    北平新华广播电台今晚节目
    【本报讯】北平新华广播电台今晚文艺节目，播送马思聪先生小提琴演奏绥远组曲、牧歌、跳神等新作，这三支曲是根据绥远民间音乐、华北民谣风格和蒙古民间打鬼跳神节日的舞踏音乐写成的小提琴独奏曲，由作者马思聪先生在今晚二十二点二十分起，亲自在北平新华广播电台演奏，并由钢琴专家王慕理先生钢琴伴奏。另外，该台今晚十九点五十分起到二十点演讲节目，播送陈叔通先生和陈嘉庚先生在新政治协商会议筹备会开幕典礼上讲话的录音。十九点三十分起到十九点五十分轮回节目，今晚是妇女时间，播送“中国解放区农村妇女参加农业生产的情形。”
