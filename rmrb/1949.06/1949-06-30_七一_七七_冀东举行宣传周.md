Title: "July 1st" "July 7th" Propaganda Week Held in Eastern Hebei
Author:
Date: 1949-06-30
Page: 2
　　
　　"July 1st" "July 7th"
  　Propaganda Week Held in Eastern Hebei
    [Eastern Hebei News] The Eastern Hebei District and Tangshan City's Party, government, military, and civilian sectors have jointly formed a preparatory committee to commemorate "July 1st" and "July 7th." It was decided to hold a commemorative week from "July 1st" to "July 7th." Internal Party meetings will be held on "July 1st" for commemoration. A city-wide commemorative meeting and parade will be held in Tangshan on "July 7th." A propaganda campaign will be launched without affecting production. Active preparations are currently underway.

### “七一”“七七”冀东举行宣传周

1949-06-30
第2版()
专栏：

　　“七一”“七七”
  　冀东举行宣传周
    【冀东讯】冀东区和唐山市党政军民各界联合组成了纪念“七一”“七七”的筹备委员会，决定从“七一”到“七七”举办纪念周，“七一”党内开会纪念，唐山“七七”召开全市纪念大会并游行示威，在不影响生产的原则下，展开宣传运动。现正积极筹备中
