Title: Central Committee of the Youth League Sends Telegram to Congratulate Revolutionary University Youth League Congress
Author:
Date: 1949-06-14
Page: 1

　　Youth League Central Committee
  　Sends Telegram to Congratulate Revolutionary University Youth League Congress
    [Reported by Our Newspaper] The Central Committee of the China New Democratic Youth League sent a telegram on the 12th of this month to congratulate the Revolutionary University Youth League Congress. The telegram stated: Congratulations to the new League members of your school on joining the League. It is hoped that all of you League members will diligently study Marxism-Leninism and Mao Zedong Thought, play an exemplary role in your studies, be ready at any time to take on work in various positions, and establish a very close relationship with all the students in the school, unite them, and guide them to strengthen their studies under the guidance of teachers, and cultivate themselves into excellent revolutionary cadres. We wish you all the best!

### 青年团中委会　电贺革大团员大会

1949-06-14
第1版()
专栏：

　　青年团中委会
  　电贺革大团员大会
    【本报讯】中国新民主主义青年团中央委员会于本月十二日电贺革命大学青年团大会，电文称：祝贺你校新团员入团，希望你们全体团员，努力学习马列主义、毛泽东思想，在学习上起模范作用，随时准备到各个岗位上去担负工作；并与全校同学建立十分密切的联系，团结他们，引导他们在师长指导之下加强学习，培养自己成为优秀的革命干部。祝你们努力！
