Title: Four Foreign Ministers Hold Another Secret Meeting
Author:
Date: 1949-06-16
Page: 3

　　Four Foreign Ministers
    Hold Another Secret Meeting
    [Xinhua News Agency, Beiping, 15th] TASS, Paris: On June 14th, the Foreign Ministers of the Soviet Union, the United States, Britain, and France held a secret meeting, chaired by Soviet Foreign Minister Vyshinsky. Only the chief delegates were present. The next meeting will be held on June 15th.

### 四国外长  再度秘密会议

1949-06-16
第3版()
专栏：

　　四国外长
    再度秘密会议
    【新华社北平十五日电】塔斯社巴黎讯：六月十四日，苏美英法外长举行秘密会议，由苏外长维辛斯基任主席，仅有各主要代表出席。下次会议将于六月十五日举行。
