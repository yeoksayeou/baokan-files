Title: Large Quantities of Rice and Oil Shipped to Shanghai

Author:

Date: 1949-06-10
Page: 1

To supply the people of the city with essential living materials, the Wanbei Trade Corporation has already shipped its first batch of 7,709 bags of rice (200 jin per bag) and 4,839 dan of oil (100 jin per dan) to Shanghai. The second batch of supplies is now about to depart.

### 大批米、油运沪

1949-06-10
第1版()
专栏：

  　大批米、油运沪
    皖北贸易总公司为供应城市人民的生活资料，已将收购之首批大米七千七百零九包（每包二百市斤）及油类四千八百三十九担（每担百斤）运往上海。现在第二批物资又将启运。
