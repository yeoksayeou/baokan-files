Title: Ping City Industrial Exhibition Opens Today
Author:
Date: 1949-06-06
Page: 2

　　Ping City Industrial Exhibition Opens Today
    [Reported by This Newspaper] The North Ping City Industrial Exhibition, sponsored by the Industry and Commerce Bureau of the North Ping City People's Government, held a preview yesterday in Zhongshan Park. Ninety-four units participated in the exhibition, including the state-owned People's Paper Mill, Glass Instrument Factory, Industrial Experiment Institute, North China Agricultural Machinery Factory, and private enterprises such as Zhenbei Tannery Company and Synthetic Chemical Factory. The exhibited industrial products include machinery, paper, leather, weaving and dyeing, pharmaceuticals, enamel, matches, cigarettes, soap, flour, electrical materials, porcelain, and dozens of other types. Among them, the tractor and various agricultural machines exhibited by the North China Agricultural Machinery Factory attracted the most attention, with many people gathering around to watch. The opening ceremony is scheduled to be held at Lai Jin Yu Xuan at 9:00 AM today, and the official exhibition will begin at 2:00 PM in the afternoon. (Kai)

### 平市工展今开幕

1949-06-06
第2版()
专栏：

　　平市工展今开幕
    【本报讯】北平市人民政府工商局所主办之北平市工业展览会，昨日在中山公园举行预展。参加展览者有国营人民造纸厂、玻璃仪器厂，工业试验所、华北农业机器厂，私营之振北制革公司、合成化学工厂等九十四个单位。展览之工业品包括机器、造纸、制革、织染、制药、搪瓷、火柴、卷烟、肥皂、面粉、电料、瓷业等数十种。其中以华北农业机器厂所展览之拖拉机与各种农业机器最引人注意，围观者甚多。该会定今日上午九时在来今雨轩举行开幕典礼，下午二时起正式展览。（恺）
