Title: Second Fleet Naval Personnel Who Revolted Reply to People's Naval Academy, Willing to Cooperate in Building the New Chinese Navy
Author:
Date: 1949-06-12
Page: 1

　　The Second Fleet Naval Personnel Who Revolted
  　Reply to People's Naval Academy
  　Willing to Cooperate in Building the New Chinese Navy
    Xinhua News Agency, Nanjing, June 11th: The entire personnel of the former Kuomintang Navy Second Fleet, who revolted on the Nanjing section of the Yangtze River on April 23rd, sent a telegram on the 5th of this month to the Chinese People's Naval Academy and Deng Zhaoxiang, the former captain of the "Chongqing" cruiser, and all the ship's personnel, expressing their willingness to study diligently the working system and military building thought of the People's Army under the leadership of the Chinese Communist Party, Chairman Mao, and Commander-in-Chief Zhu, and to strive together for the establishment of a strong Chinese People's Navy and a New Democratic China.

### 起义海军第二舰队员兵　电复人民海军学校　愿协力建设新中国海军

1949-06-12
第1版()
专栏：

　　起义海军第二舰队员兵
  　电复人民海军学校
  　愿协力建设新中国海军
    【新华社南京十一日电】四月二十三日在南京江面上起义的前国民党海军第二舰队全体员兵，于本月五日电复中国人民海军学校及前“重庆”号巡洋舰舰长邓兆祥及该舰全体员兵，表示愿在中国共产党与毛主席、朱总司令领导下，努力学习人民军队的工作制度和建军思想，共同为建立强大的中国人民海军和新民主主义的中国而奋斗。
