Title: Grammos Mountains Entirely Liberated, Democratic Army of Greece Completely Expels Remaining Enemies

Author:

Date: 1949-06-14
Page: 3

　　Grammos Mountains
  　Entirely Liberated
  　Democratic Army of Greece Completely Expels Remaining Enemies
    Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, 13th: According to a broadcast from the Free Greece Radio Station, after two months of fighting, the Grammos Mountains in the northern Pindus Mountains were entirely liberated by the Greek Democratic Army on the 8th. The remaining troops of the royalist fascist army in the area have been completely expelled. In Salonica, small units of the Democratic Army are intensifying their attacks on the royalist fascist gendarmerie and police.

### 格拉姆斯山区　全部解放　希民主军全部逐出残敌

1949-06-14
第3版()
专栏：

　　格拉姆斯山区
  　全部解放
  　希民主军全部逐出残敌
    【新华社北平十三日电】据自由希腊电台广播：经过两个月战斗后，平都斯山北部的格拉姆斯山区，八日已为希腊民主军全部解放，该区保皇法西斯军的残余部队已被完全逐出。在萨罗尼加，民主军的小队正加紧打击保皇法西斯的宪兵和警察。
