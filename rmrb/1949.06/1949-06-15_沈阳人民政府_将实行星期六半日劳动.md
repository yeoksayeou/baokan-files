Title: Shenyang People's Government to Implement Half-Day Labor on Saturdays
Author:
Date: 1949-06-15
Page: 3

　　Shenyang People's Government
  　　Will Implement Half-Day Labor on Saturdays
    [Xinhua News Agency, Shenyang, 14th] In order to respond to the call of the Northeast Bureau of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of China to strengthen the labor concept of cadres and staff, the Shenyang Municipal People's Government has decided to implement a half-day labor system on Saturdays. Since the 11th of this month, 480 municipal government personnel have formed a labor brigade. The brigade is divided into twelve companies, each with forty people, organized by bureau and division, with the bureau chiefs, division chiefs, secretary directors, and section chiefs serving as brigade commander, company commander respectively. On the afternoon of the 11th (Saturday), each company went to factories, farms, parks, and other places to participate in labor.

### 沈阳人民政府　将实行星期六半日劳动

1949-06-15
第3版()
专栏：

　　沈阳人民政府
  　将实行星期六半日劳动
    【新华社沈阳十四日电】沈阳市人民政府为了响应中共中央东北局加强干部和职员的劳动观念的号召，决定实行星期六半日劳动制。自本月十一日起市府人员已有四百八十人组成劳动大队。该大队下分十二个中队，每中队四十人，以局、处为单位组成，大队长、中队长均由局长、处长、秘书主任及科长分别担任。十一日（星期六）下午，各中队已分别到工厂、农场、公园等参加劳动。
