Title: Calcutta Prison Political Prisoners Riot
Author:
Date: 1949-06-11
Page: 3

Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, 10th. According to Agence France-Presse: Political prisoners in Calcutta prison, unable to endure the abuse of the Indian authorities, rioted on the 8th. The Indian authorities gathered 300 policemen and 200 prison guards to suppress the riot. Gunfire continued for more than an hour before ceasing.

### 加尔各答监狱政治犯暴动

1949-06-11
第3版()
专栏：

　　加尔各答监狱
  　政治犯暴动
    【新华社北平十日电】据法新社讯：加尔各答监狱里的政治犯，因不堪印度当局的虐待，于八日发生暴动。印当局纠集警察三百名、狱卒二百名实行弹压，枪声历一小时余始止。
