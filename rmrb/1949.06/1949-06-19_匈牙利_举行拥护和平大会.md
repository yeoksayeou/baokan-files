Title: Hungary Holds Peace Conference
Author:
Date: 1949-06-19
Page: 3

　　Hungary
  　Holds Peace Conference
    [Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, 18th] TASS, Budapest: The Hungarian National Peace Conference was held on the 17th. Participants included more than 500 representatives from organizations such as the Hungarian National Committee of Cultural Workers, the Trade Union Council, the Hungarian Democratic Women's League, the Hungarian Youth League, the Laboring Peasants and Agricultural Workers' League, the Free Fighters League, the Protestant and other churches, and the Association of Science and Art Workers. More than 200 of the representatives were women. The conference solemnly opened in the Parliament Hall, which was decorated with slogans such as "Defend Peace!", "Down with the Warmongers!", "Long Live the Soviet Union, Our Liberator and Defender of World Peace!", and "Long Live Stalin, the Great Leader of the Peace Camp!". Amidst stormy applause, the conference elected Rakosi as honorary chairman. Losonczy, a member of the Hungarian National Committee of Cultural Workers and a delegate to the World Peace Congress, reported on "Hungary in the Struggle for Peace".

### 匈牙利　举行拥护和平大会

1949-06-19
第3版()
专栏：

　　匈牙利
  　举行拥护和平大会
    【新华社北平十八日电】塔斯社布达佩斯讯：匈牙利全国拥护和平大会于十七日举行。参加者有匈牙利文化工作者全国委员会、职工会理事会、匈牙利民主妇女联盟、匈牙利青年联盟、劳动农民与农业工人联盟、自由战士联盟、新教及其他教会、科学与艺术工作者协会等团体的五百余名代表。全体代表中有二百余名为妇女代表。大会系在饰着“保卫和平！”“打倒战争贩子！”“我们的解放者，世界和平的保卫者——苏联万岁！”“和平阵营底伟大领袖斯大林万岁！”等标语的议会大厅中，隆重开幕。大会在暴风雨般的掌声中通过选举拉科西为大会名誉主席。由匈牙利文化工作者全国委员会委员、出度世界拥护和平大会的代表洛逊齐报告“在为和平而斗争中的匈牙利”。
