Title: Shanghai Deputy Mayor Pan Hannian Visits Yan Huiqing, Zhang Lan, and Others
Author:
Date: 1949-06-03
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Shanghai, 2nd — Pan Hannian, Deputy Mayor of the Shanghai People's Government, visited Yan Huiqing, director of the Shanghai Municipal Temporary Relief Committee, on the 29th, expressing his gratitude for his enthusiasm for wartime relief work and extending his regards. On the same day, Deputy Mayor Pan also visited and extended his regards to democratic figures Zhang Lan, Luo Longji, and Shi Liang, who remained in Shanghai.

### 沪副市长潘汉年慰问颜惠庆张澜等

1949-06-03
第1版()
专栏：

　　沪副市长潘汉年慰问颜惠庆张澜等
    【新华社上海二日电】上海人民市政府副市长潘汉年二十九日走访沪市临时救济委员会主任颜惠庆，对其热心于战时救济工作，表示感谢，并致慰问。同日，潘副市长并走访和慰问留沪民主人士张澜、罗隆基、史良三氏。
