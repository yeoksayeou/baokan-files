Title: French Intellectuals Wire Foreign Ministers' Conference, Condemn US and British Crimes of Arming Germany and Japan, Oppose Treating Peace Treaties as Waste Paper
Author:
Date: 1949-06-18
Page: 3

　　Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, 17th. Polish News Agency, Paris: The French National Committee of Intellectuals in Support of Peace recently sent a telegram to the Foreign Ministers' Conference as follows: "When this problem, on which the future of peace depends, is on the agenda of the conference, we believe it is our duty to raise the words of the World Peace Congress declaration. The declaration says: 'Whoever considers peace treaties as a pile of waste paper, rejects proposals for disarmament and arms themselves to the teeth, exposes themselves as aggressors.'" The letter condemns acts aimed at arming Germany and Japan and expresses the hope that the Foreign Ministers will resolve the issues they are discussing in the spirit of the principles that constitute the basis of the wartime Allied treaties, and in the spirit of the United Nations Charter.

### 法知识分子电外长会议　谴责美英武装德日罪行  反对把和平条约当作废纸

1949-06-18
第3版()
专栏：

　　法知识分子电外长会议
  　谴责美英武装德日罪行
    反对把和平条约当作废纸
    【新华社北平十七日电】波兰通讯社巴黎消息：法国知识分子拥护和平全国委员会顷致电外长会议如下：“当这个和平前途所系的问题列在会议议程上的时候，我们认为我们有责任提起世界和平大会宣言里说的话。宣言说：谁认为和平条约是一堆废纸，拒绝解除军备的建议而且武装到牙齿，谁就暴露了他们自己是侵略者”。该信谴责旨在武装德国和日本的行为，并表示希望外长们按照构成战时盟国条约之基础的诸原则的精神，按照联合国宪章的精神，解决他们所讨论的问题。
