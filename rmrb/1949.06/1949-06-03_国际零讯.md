International News in Brief

　　An Italian Communist Party campaign to promote its party newspaper recently achieved great success. Two weeks after the start of the campaign, the circulation of "l'Unità" Milan edition increased from 150,000 to 300,000 copies. The Rome edition also increased by 30,000 copies. In Bologna and other places, the circulation of "l'Unità" tripled.
　　　　　　　　×　　　　　×　　　　　×
　　The Polish United Workers' Party has fourteen party newspapers, with a total circulation of two million copies. The Polish version of the Cominform's journal "For Lasting Peace, For People's Democracy" sells 110,000 copies.
　　　　　　　　×　　　　　×　　　　　×
　　Professor Spitzer, an associate professor at the University of Oregon in the United States, was dismissed by the university president on the "charge" of "following the Communist line" for publishing a letter in a chemistry journal endorsing Lysenko's theory of genetics.

### 国际零讯

1949-06-03
第4版()
专栏：国际零讯

　　国际零讯
    意大利共产党不久前发动的一个推销党报运动，获得巨大成绩。在运动开始两周以后，团结报米兰版销数即由十五万份增至三十万份。而罗马版也增销三万份。在波伦亚等地，团结报销路增加了三倍。
    　　　　　　　　×　　　　　×　　　　　×
    波兰统一工人党有十四个党报，销路已达两百万份。情报局机关杂志“争取持久和平与人民民主”波兰文版，销十一万份。

    美国俄勒冈大学副教授斯皮泽，因为在化学杂志上发表了一封信，赞同李森科在遗传学上的理论，竟被该校校长以“走共产党路线”的“罪名”解聘。
