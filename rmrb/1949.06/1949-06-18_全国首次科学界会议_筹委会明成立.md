Title: Preparatory Committee for the First National Scientific Conference to be Established Tomorrow
Author:
Date: 1949-06-18
Page: 1

【Ping City News】Preparatory work for the first National Scientific Conference is actively underway. As the people's liberation cause is about to be fully completed and economic construction is about to begin, all sectors are eagerly hoping to convene a national scientific conference to discuss various issues related to the coordination of science with production and construction. In mid-May, the China Science Society, the China Society of Natural Sciences, the China Association of Scientific Workers, and the Northeast Natural Science Research Association jointly initiated the organization of a promotion association. The association has held four meetings in Beiping to discuss the preparations for the first National Scientific Conference and has nominated more than 200 preparatory committee members. As the preparatory committee members are scattered in Beiping, Tianjin, Shanghai, Nanjing, Hong Kong, and Wuhan, it is difficult to gather them all at once. It has been decided to convene the preparatory committee members who can arrive on June 19th to establish a preparatory committee and organize a temporary working committee to expedite the preparations. A plenary meeting of the preparatory committee members is scheduled for July 10th.

### 全国首次科学界会议　筹委会明成立

1949-06-18
第1版()
专栏：

　　全国首次科学界会议
  　筹委会明成立
    【平市讯】中华全国第一次科学界会议筹备工作正积极进行中。由于人民解放的事业迅将全面完成，经济建设即行展开，各方面都切望召集一个全国性的科学界会议，来研讨科学与生产建设配合的各项问题。五月中曾由中国科学社、中华自然科学社、中国科学工作者协会、东北自然科学研究会四团体发起组织促进会。该会已在北平举行会议四次，讨论全国第一次科学界会议的筹备问题，并推定筹备委员二百余人。因筹委散在平、津、沪、宁、香港、武汉各地，一时不易到齐，乃决定先于六月十九日召集能赶到的筹备委员，成立筹备会，组织临时干事会，以便迅速进行筹备。并订于七月十日举行筹委全体会议。
