Title: Hubei, Lüda Party, Government, Military, and People Send Telegrams to Congratulate the Establishment of the Preparatory Committee of the New Political Consultative Conference
Author:
Date: 1949-06-24
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, Hankou, 23rd—The Hubei Provincial People's Government, the Hubei Provincial Committee of the Chinese Communist Party, and the Hubei Military Region of the Chinese People's Liberation Army jointly sent a telegram on the 21st to congratulate the preparatory committee of the New Political Consultative Conference. The telegram stated: Most of Hubei has been liberated, and in the future, we will do our best to support the People's Liberation Army in continuing to advance and strive to restore and develop production to celebrate the success of the conference.

In addition, the Hubei Provincial Branch of the China Democratic League, the Hankou City Branch, the Hubei Provincial Branch of the Chinese Peasants' and Workers' Democratic Party, the Wuhan Branch of the China Democratic National Construction Association, and democratic figures Zhang Nanxian, Li Shucheng, Li Fanyi, Li Xiping, Geng Bozhao, Liu Jun, Zhou Jie, Jiang Bingling, and others also sent telegrams to congratulate the establishment of the preparatory committee of the New Political Consultative Conference.

Xinhua News Agency, Dalian, 23rd—The Lüda Administrative Committee and eight people's organizations sent a telegram to the preparatory committee of the New Political Consultative Conference, expressing warm congratulations. The telegram reads as follows:

To Chairman Mao Zedong and all representatives of the Standing Committee of the Preparatory Committee of the New Political Consultative Conference: We are delighted to learn that the preparatory committee of the New Political Consultative Conference has been established in Beiping. We firmly believe that a truly democratic and united New Political Consultative Conference will soon open, and a brand-new, strong, and truly People's Democratic Republic of China will soon arrive. From then on, Chinese history will enter a bright new era. The million people of Lüda are extremely excited and encouraged and sincerely congratulate you.

Lüda has been liberated from the enslavement of Japanese寇 since the "August 15th" Soviet troops were dispatched. Under the friendly assistance of the Soviet garrison and the leadership of the Chinese Communist Party, we have overcome various difficulties and have been able to engage in the restoration and development of New Democratic economic, cultural, and other undertakings. In the future, we must serve wholeheartedly and diligently under the leadership of the unified national people's democratic united government.

### 湖北、旅大党政军民  电贺新政协筹备会成立

1949-06-24
第1版()
专栏：

    湖湖北、旅大党政军民
    电贺新政协筹备会成立
    【新华社汉口二十三日电】湖北省人民政府、中共湖北省委会、中国人民解放军湖北军区二十一日联名电贺新政协筹备会，电称：湖北已经大部解放，今后将以尽力支援人民解放军继续前进，努力恢复和发展生产事业，来祝贺大会的成功。
    另中国民主同盟湖北省支部、汉口市支部、中国农工民主党湖北省支部、民主建国会武汉分会及民主人士张难先、李书城、李范一、李西平、耿伯钊、刘俊、周杰、江炳灵等亦分别致电祝贺新政协筹备会成立。
    【新华社大连二十三日电】旅大行政委员会暨八个人民团体致电新政治协商会议筹备会，表示热烈祝贺。电文如下：
    新政协筹备会常务委员会毛泽东主席并全体代表：欣闻新政治协商会议筹备会在平成立，我们深信一个真正民主团结的新政治协商会议不久即将开幕，一个崭新的、强盛的、名符其实的中华人民民主共和国不久即将到来，中国的历史从此将要进入一个光明的新时代，我旅大百万人民万分兴奋鼓舞，忠诚祝贺。
    旅大自“八一五”苏联出兵从日寇奴役下解放出来，在苏联驻军友谊协助和中国共产党领导下，克服种种困难，得以从事新民主主义经济、文化等事业的恢复和发展，今后更当在全国统一的人民民主联合政府领导之下，全心全意服务努力。
