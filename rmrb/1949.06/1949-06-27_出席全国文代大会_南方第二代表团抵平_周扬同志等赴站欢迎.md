Title: Southern Second Delegation to National Literary Congress Arrives in Beiping; Comrade Zhou Yang and Others Greet Them at the Station
Author: Bo Sheng
Date: 1949-06-27
Page: 1
　　
　　To Attend the National Literary Congress
  　Southern Second Delegation Arrives in Beiping
  　Comrade Zhou Yang and Others Greet Them at the Station
    [Our Dispatch] The Southern Second Delegation to the National Literary Congress arrived in Beiping yesterday (26th) at 7:30 PM from Shanghai. The delegation is led by the head of the delegation Feng Xuefeng, deputy head Chen Baichen, and Secretary-General Yao Shixiao, including representatives Ba Jin, Chen Wangdao, Jin Yi, Tang Tao, Chen Zizhan, Lu Ling, Xu Jie, Wu Zuxiang, Luo Sun, Chen Zhongfan, Fang Lingru, Yuan Shuipai, Liu Kaiqu, Chen Yanqiao, Pang Xunqin, Ni Yide, Shu Mo, Sun Shen, Jiang Dingxian, Shen Zhibai, Mei Lanfang, Xiong Foxi, Ying Yunwei, Zhao Dan, Shen Fu, Zhou Xinfang, Wu Weiyun, Feng Yidai, Li Jianwu, Lü Junqiao, and others, totaling seventy-seven people. Zhou Yang, Mao Dun, Ding Ling, Ouyang Yuqian, Ai Qing, Tian Han, Feng Naichao, Hong Shen, Ke Zhongping, Zhou Wen, Cheng Yanqiu, Hu Feng, Ma Sicong, Zhong Jingwen, and other members of the preparatory committee of the Literary Congress and people from literary and art circles in Beiping went to the station to welcome them. All the representatives stayed at the Liuxiang Hotel last night.
    (Bo Sheng)

### 出席全国文代大会　南方第二代表团抵平　周扬同志等赴站欢迎
柏生
1949-06-27
第1版()
专栏：

　　出席全国文代大会
  　南方第二代表团抵平
  　周扬同志等赴站欢迎
    【本报讯】出席全国文化大会的南方第二代表团，昨（二十六）日下午七时半由沪抵平。该团由正团长冯雪峰、副团长陈白尘、秘书长姚时晓率领，到代表巴金、陈望道、靳以、唐韬、陈子展、路翎、许杰、吴组湘、罗荪、陈中凡、方令儒、袁水拍、刘开渠、陈烟桥、庞薰琴、倪贻德、舒模、孙慎、江定仙、沈知白、梅兰芳、熊佛西、应云卫、赵丹、沈浮、周信芳、吴蔚云、冯亦代、李健吾、吕君樵等七十七人。文代大会筹备会及在平文艺界人士周扬、茅盾、丁玲、欧阳予倩、艾青、田汉、冯乃超、洪深、柯仲平、周文、程艳秋、胡风、马思聪、钟敬文等均前往车站欢迎。全部代表昨晚即下榻留香饭店。
    （柏生）
