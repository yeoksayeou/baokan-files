Title: Tonight's Program on Beiping Xinhua Broadcasting Station
Author:
Date: 1949-06-25
Page: 1

    Beiping Xinhua Broadcasting Station Tonight's Program
    [This Newspaper News] The Beiping Xinhua Broadcasting Station's rotational program from 19:30 to 19:50 tonight is the Youth Time sponsored by the Central Propaganda Department of the China New Democratic Youth League, introducing the All-China Students' Federation. From 22:20 to 22:50, the arts program will introduce Shaanbei minor tunes, performed by the North China University Art Troupe No. 2 with selections from the Yangko opera "Everyone Likes It." From 19:50 to 20:10, the lecture program will feature Mr. Wang ＠瑛, Director of Public Health Education at the Beiping First Hygiene Office, speaking on "Prevention of Summer Gastrointestinal Infectious Diseases." From 21:15 to 21:30, the international affairs program will introduce the economic construction of the New Democratic countries.

### 北平新华广播电台今晚节目

1949-06-25
第1版()
专栏：

    北平新华广播电台今晚节目
    【本报讯】北平新华广播电台今晚十九点三十分到十九点五十分轮回节目，是由中国新民主主义青年团中央宣传部主办的青年时间，介绍中华全国学生联合会。二十二点二十分到二十二点五十分文艺节目，介绍陕北＠调，由华北大学文工二团播演秧歌剧“大家喜欢”中的选曲。十九点五十分到二十点十分演讲节目，由北平第一卫生事务所公共卫生教育主任王＠瑛先生主讲：“夏天胃肠传染病的预防”。二十一点十五分到二十一点三十分国际时事节目，介绍新民主主义国家的经济建设。
