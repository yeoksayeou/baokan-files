Title: The Representatives' Conference of Primary and Secondary School Teachers and Staff in Beiping Resumes Yesterday
Author:
Date: 1949-06-13
Page: 2

Beiping News: The Representatives' Conference of Primary and Secondary School Teachers and Staff in Beiping, after adjourning on the 5th, resumed yesterday (the 12th), with 268 representatives attending. At 8:00 AM, the Preparatory Committee of the Beiping Municipal New Democratic Youth League, the Preparatory Committee of the Democratic Youth Federation, and Wenquan Middle School presented banners to the conference. Subsequently, the conference discussed the "Draft Charter of the Beiping Municipal Joint Association of Primary and Secondary School Teachers and Staff" and the "Draft Election Law for the First Executive Committee of the Beiping Municipal Joint Association of Primary and Secondary School Teachers and Staff". By 4:00 PM, both drafts were fully approved. Following this, Xue Chengye presided over the nomination of candidates for the Executive Committee, resulting in a total of 119 nominations. The meeting will continue today (the 13th), and after group deliberations and discussions, 45 Executive Committee members and 10 alternate Executive Committee members are scheduled to be elected by secret ballot.

### 平中小学教职员代表大会昨复会

1949-06-13
第2版()
专栏：

　　平中小学教职员代表大会昨复会
    【平市讯】平市中小学教职员代表大会五日休会后，已于昨（十二）日复会到代表二百六十八人。上午八时本市新民主主义青年团筹委会、民主青联筹委会及温泉中学向大会献旗。旋即讨论“北平市中小学教职员联合会章程草案”及“北平市中小学教联第一届执行委员选举法草案”，至下午四时，两草案获得全部通过。继由薛成业主持推选执委候选人，共提出一百一十九名。会议今（十三）日继续举行，拟经过小组酝酿讨论后，即用无记名投票选举执行委员四十五人，候补执行委员十人。
