Title: People's Bank of Ping and its Offices Increase Remittance Points such as Taiyuan

Author: 
Date: 1949-06-03
Page: 2

The People's Bank of Ping and its Offices
	Increase Remittance Points such as Taiyuan
	[Our dispatch] The People's Bank of Beiping Branch started remittances with Taiyuan on the 1st. In addition, the branch and its East, West, and South offices have added a total of nineteen remittance points including Jinan, as follows: Business Department of the Branch: Taiyuan (exchange rate 8 per mille), Jinan (15 per mille), Tanggu (3 per mille), Caozhou (10 per mille), Changzhi (7 per mille). South City Office: Handan (6 per mille), Xingtai (5.5 per mille), Changzhi (7 per mille), Hengshui (5 per mille), Nangong (5 per mille), Linqing (5 per mille), Lixian (10 per mille), Shanhaiguan (10 per mille), Dingxing (3 per mille), Pingshan (7 per mille). East City Office: Shanhaiguan (exchange rate as above), Zhangjiakou (4 per mille), Baoding (3 per mille). West City Office: Shanhaiguan (exchange rate as above).
	[Another dispatch] The Jiaotong Bank of this city and its Mentougou office, in order to facilitate the transfer of funds for public and private industrial and mining enterprises, began handling remittances with Tianjin on the 1st.　　　　　　　　　　(Yu)

### 平人民银行暨各办事处　增加太原等通汇点

1949-06-03
第2版()
专栏：

　　平人民银行暨各办事处
  　增加太原等通汇点
    【本报讯】人民银行北平分行于一日起开始与太原通汇，此外该行暨东、西、南三办事处亦增加济南等通汇点共十九处如下：分行营业部：太原（汇率千分之八）济南（千分之十五）塘沽（千分之三）曹州（千分之十）长治（千分之七）。南城办事处：邯郸（千分之六）邢台（千分之五点五）长治（千分之七）衡水（千分之五）南宫（千分之五）临清（千分之五）蠡县（千分之十）山海关（千分之十）定兴（千分之三）平山（千分之七）。东城办事处：山海关（汇率同前）张家口（千分之四）保定（千分之三）。西城办事处：山海关（汇率同前）。
    【又讯】本市交通银行暨该行门头沟办事处，为便利公私工矿企业调拨款项，于一日起开始办理与天津通汇事宜。　　　　　　　　　　（宇）
