Title: Shanghai Xinhua Bookstore Sells Over 10,000 Books on First Day; Life Bookstore and Others Reopen

Author:

Date: 1949-06-10
Page: 1

　　Xinhua News Agency, Shanghai, 9th. The Shanghai branch of Xinhua Bookstore began operations on the 5th. On that day, two temporary sales outlets sold more than 12,000 books, among which Chairman Mao's "On New Democracy" and "On Coalition Government" were the most popular. The bookstore is now rushing to print works by Chairman Mao and responsible comrades of the CCP Central Committee, as well as various policy documents of the CCP.

　　In other news: The Life Bookstore, Xinzhi Bookstore, and Dushu Publishing House, which were forced to close down due to oppression by the Kuomintang reactionaries, also reopened on the 5th. The three bookstores have merged to form the Shanghai United Publishing and Distribution Office, and more than 40 new books printed during the shutdown have already gone on sale.

### 沪新华书店第一天售书逾万册　生活书店等已复业

1949-06-10
第1版()
专栏：

　　沪新华书店第一天售书逾万册
  　生活书店等已复业
    【新华社上海九日电】新华书店上海分店五日开始营业。当天两个临时门市部就卖出书籍一万二千余册，其中以毛主席所著“新民主主义论”、“论联合政府”等书最为畅销。该店现正赶印毛主席和中共中央负责同志的著作及中共各种政策文件等书。
    又讯：受国民党反动派压迫而停业的生活书店、新知书店、读书出版社三书店，也在五日复业。三书店合并组成上海联合发行所，停业期间印就的新书四十余种已经开始出售。
