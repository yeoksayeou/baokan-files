Title: Youth League Central China Working Committee Established; Wuhan Municipal Working Committee Also Begins Work
Author:
Date: 1949-06-13
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Hankou, 12th—The Central Committee of the China New Democratic Youth League has decided to establish the Central China Regional Working Committee of the China New Democratic Youth League, and has appointed comrades Song Yiping, Zhou Hui, Hu Keshi, Yang Zejiang, Li Zhizhou, and Zhu Yujin as members, with Song Yiping as secretary, Zhou Hui as first deputy secretary, and Hu Keshi as second deputy secretary. More than one hundred youth work cadres transferred from the Northeast, Tianjin, and the Central Plains have arrived in Wuhan in recent days. Under the direct leadership of the Central China Bureau of the CPC Central Committee, they are launching youth movements and Youth League work in the Central China region. In order to unify and strengthen the leadership of the Wuhan youth movement and advance the work of building the League, the Central China Working Committee has also decided to organize the Wuhan Municipal Working Committee of the New Democratic Youth League, with Song Yiping concurrently serving as secretary and Li Zhi as deputy secretary. The committee has now successively dispatched twelve working groups to factories and schools to carry out propaganda and education work, encourage working youth and intellectual youth to actively participate in the takeover and restoration of production, and organize political and cultural study movements for young people, help establish trade unions and improve student unions; at the same time, on this basis, carry out planned and step-by-step League building work.

### 青年团华中工委会成立　武汉市工委会亦开始工作

1949-06-13
第1版()
专栏：

　　青年团华中工委会成立
  　武汉市工委会亦开始工作
    【新华社汉口十二日电】中国新民主主义青年团中央委员会已决定成立中国新民主主义青年团华中区工作委员会，并指派宋一平、周惠、胡克实、杨泽江、黎智、李止舟、朱语今等同志为委员，以宋一平任书记，周惠任第一副书记，胡克实任第二副书记。该会从东北、平津、中原调集的青年工作干部百余人，均已于日前抵武汉，在中共中央华中局直接领导下，展开华中区青年运动与青年团工作。为了统一与加强武汉青年运动的领导与推进建团工作，华中工作委员会并决定组织新民主主义青年团武汉市工作委员会，宋一平兼书记，黎智任副书记。该会现已陆续派出十二个工作组分赴工厂、学校，展开宣传教育工作，鼓励劳动青年与知识青年积极参加接管与恢复生产工作，并组织青年的政治文化学习运动，帮助建立职工会与健全学生会；同时在此基础上展开有计划有步骤的建团工作。
