Title: Commemorating "July 1st": North China Postal Administration to Issue Commemorative Stamps
Author:
Date: 1949-06-02
Page: 2

　　Commemorating "July 1st"
　　North China Postal Administration to Issue Commemorative Stamps
　　[Our Dispatch] In order to commemorate the 28th anniversary of the founding of the Chinese Communist Party, the North China Postal Administration has decided to issue commemorative stamps on "July 1st". There are two sets of commemorative stamps, five types in each set, all exquisitely printed with Chairman Mao's image. They are currently being printed and will be available on "July 1st".

### 纪念“七一”　华北邮政总局将发纪念邮票

1949-06-02
第2版()
专栏：

　　纪念“七一”
  　华北邮政总局将发纪念邮票
    【本报讯】华北邮政总局为了纪念中共成立二十八周年，决定在“七一”发行纪念邮票。纪念邮票共有两套，每套五种，票面精印毛主席像。现正赶印，“七一”即可发行。
