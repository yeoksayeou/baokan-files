Title: Nanjing Railway Workers'慰问团 Visits Shanghai to Express Sympathy to Workers from All Routes
Author: 
Date: 1949-06-02
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, Nanjing, 1st. The Preparatory Committee of the Nanjing District Workers' Union of the Nanjing-Shanghai Railway organized a慰问团to Shanghai on the "Shanghai Liberation" train on May 30th to express their sympathy to all railway workers and introduce to them the situation of the Nanjing District railway workers assisting the People's Government in taking over and restoring production and transportation.

### 南京铁路职工慰问团赴沪慰问各路职工

1949-06-02
第1版()
专栏：

　　南京铁路职工慰问团赴沪慰问各路职工
    【新华社南京一日电】京沪路南京区职工会筹委会，组织京沪路南京区职工慰问团，于五月三十日乘“上海解放号”列车赴上海慰问各铁路职工，并向他们介绍南京区铁路职工协助人民政府接管及恢复生产、运输等情形。
