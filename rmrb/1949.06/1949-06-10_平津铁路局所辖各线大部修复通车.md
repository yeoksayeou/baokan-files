Title: Major Railways Under the Tianjin-Pukou Railway Bureau Restored and Open to Traffic
Author: 
Date: 1949-06-10
Page: 1

[Broadcast by Beiping Xinhua Broadcasting Station] Tianjin, 9th—It is reported that the damaged railways and bridges within the Tianjin-Pukou Railway Bureau’s jurisdiction have been almost entirely restored. More than 1,600 kilometers of railway are now open to traffic, accounting for 88 percent of the total length. A total of 149 bridges have been repaired or are nearing completion, accounting for more than 90 percent of the total. The speed of trains on various lines within the jurisdiction has been increased. The speed between Tianjin and Beiping reaches a maximum of 80 kilometers per hour, and between Tianjin and Shanhaiguan, it is 65 kilometers per hour.

### 平津铁路局所辖各线大部修复通车

1949-06-10
第1版()
专栏：

　　平津铁路局所辖各线大部修复通车
    【北平新华广播电台广播】天津九日消息：平津铁路管理局辖区内各线毁坏的铁路、桥梁等，已经接近全部修复。现在已经通车的铁路有一千六百多公里，占全线的百分之八十八。已经修好和快要修好的桥梁，一共有一百四十九座，占总数的百分之九十以上。现在该辖区内各线行车速度都已经提高，平津间的行车速度，每点钟最高达到八十公里，天津山海关间是六十五公里。
