Title: Eighteen Shanghai Fishing Boats Salvaged
Author:
Date: 1949-06-27
Page: 1

　　Xinhua News Agency, Shanghai, 26th: The eighteen fishing boats that were sunk in the Huangpu River by the Kuomintang bandit troops before the liberation of Shanghai have all been salvaged. This is the result of twenty days of joint efforts by the Shanghai Military Control Commission's Ship Repair Factory, the Fisheries Management Office, and the workers. The Ship Repair Factory is now mobilizing all workers to rush repairs, and it is expected that all of them will be repaired within a week and fishing will be resumed.

　　In other news: The China Shipbuilding Technology Workers Association was established in Shanghai on the 20th to exchange shipbuilding experience and promote production and construction.

### 上海十八艘渔轮打捞出水

1949-06-27
第1版()
专栏：

　　上海十八艘渔轮打捞出水
    【新华社上海二十六日电】上海解放前被国民党匪军在黄浦江内凿沉的十八艘渔轮，已全部打捞出水。这是上海军管会船舶修理厂及渔管处及工人经二十天共同努力打捞的结果。现船舶修理厂正动员全体工人进行抢修，预计一周内即可全部修好，恢复捕鱼。
    又讯：为交换船舶修造经验，推进生产建设，中国船舶修造技术工作者协会于二十日在上海成立。
