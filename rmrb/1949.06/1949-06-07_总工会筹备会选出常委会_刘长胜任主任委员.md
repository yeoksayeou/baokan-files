Title: Preparatory Committee of the Federation of Trade Unions Elects Standing Committee; Liu Changsheng Appointed Chairman

Author:

Date: 1949-06-07
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Shanghai, June 6th: The Shanghai Federation of Trade Unions Preparatory Committee held its first preparatory meeting on the 3rd, with 128 committee members present. The meeting elected Rao Shushi, Chen Yi, Liu Xiao, Liu Changsheng, Zhu Junxin, Zhou Bingkun, Han Wucheng, Wang Chunhui, He Xiufeng, Zhou Guoxiang, Zhang Qi, Wang Ruowang, Tang Guifen, Wang Keqin, Li Jiaqi, Mao Qihua, Shen Yu, Wang Zhongyi, Ji Kang, Fan Xiaofeng, Yu Jingcheng, Yang Guangming, Ni Fusheng, Yang Chunlin, Li Yongxian, Shen Mo, Lou Caibao, Wu Jie, Chen Gongqi, Chen Zhilian, Shen Xiangsheng, Xu Binggen, Wang Alin, Chen Xingsheng, Ma Xiaodi, and Ouyang Zirun, totaling 36 people, as members of the Standing Committee of the Shanghai Federation of Trade Unions, thus establishing the Standing Committee. Liu Changsheng was appointed Chairman. The Standing Committee has ten departments: Organization, Culture and Education, Production, Wages, Welfare, Youth Workers, Women Workers, Private Enterprises, Public Utilities, and Discipline, as well as two sections: Secretariat and General Affairs, and one Research Office.

### 总工会筹备会选出常委会　刘长胜任主任委员

1949-06-07
第1版()
专栏：建设新上海

　　总工会筹备会选出常委会　刘长胜任主任委员
    【新华社上海六日电】上海总工会筹备委员会于三日举行首次筹备会议，到会筹委一百二十八人。会议选出饶漱石、陈毅、刘晓、刘长胜、朱俊欣、周炳坤、韩武成、王春晖、何秀凤、周国祥、张祺、王若望、汤桂芬、王克勤、李家齐、毛齐华、沈毓、王中一、纪康、范小凤、余敬成、杨光明、倪福生、杨春霖、李咏贤、沈默、娄才宝、吴杰、陈公琪、陈之连、沈翔声、许炳根、王阿林、陈杏生、马小弟、欧阳祖润等三十六人为上海总工会常务委员，成立常务委员会。刘长胜任主任委员。常委会下设组织、文教、生产、工资、福利、青工、女工、私营企业、公用事业、纠察等十部，秘书、总务两处及一研究室。
