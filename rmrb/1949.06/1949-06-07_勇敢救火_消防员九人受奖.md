Title: Brave Firefighters Honored for Putting Out Fire, Nine Receive Awards
Author:
Date: 1949-06-07
Page: 2

　　Brave Firefighters Honored for Putting Out Fire, Nine Receive Awards
    [This Newspaper Reports] On the 3rd of this month, a warehouse of a certain unit in the suburbs of Beiping caught fire. Upon hearing the news, the fire brigade rushed to the scene and bravely went forward to put out the fire, regardless of their own safety. In particular, the firefighters Yu Wenshen and others from the First, Fourth, and Eighth Squadrons fully demonstrated their exemplary role. The Public Security Bureau has issued a commendation order and awarded each of the nine exemplary firefighters a shirt as encouragement.
    　　　　　　(Ji)

### 勇敢救火　消防员九人受奖

1949-06-07
第2版()
专栏：

　　勇敢救火　消防员九人受奖
    【本报讯】本月三日，平郊某部仓库失火，消防队闻讯赶到，奋不顾身，冒火勇敢向前施救，尤以第一、四、八中队队员于文申等九人，充分发挥了模范作用。公安局特传令嘉奖，并赠奖九位模范消防员衬衣各一件，以示鼓励。
    　　　　　　（迹）
