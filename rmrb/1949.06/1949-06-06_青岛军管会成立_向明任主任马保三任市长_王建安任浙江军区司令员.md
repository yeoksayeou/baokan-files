Title: Qingdao Military Control Commission Established; Xiang Ming Appointed Director, Ma Baosan Appointed Mayor; Wang Jian'an Appointed Commander of Zhejiang Military Region
Author:
Date: 1949-06-06
Page: 1

　　Qingdao Military Control Commission Established
  　Xiang Ming Appointed Director, Ma Baosan Appointed Mayor
    Wang Jian'an Appointed Commander of Zhejiang Military Region
    [Xinhua News Agency, Jinan, 5th] The Qingdao Military Control Commission and the People's Government of Qingdao City were established on the 2nd. The Shandong Military Region of the People's Liberation Army appointed Xiang Ming as the Director of the Military Control Commission, and Lai Keke and Tan Xilin as Deputy Directors. The Shandong Provincial People's Government appointed Ma Baosan as the Mayor of Qingdao City. The takeover work has now begun. Furthermore, the Qingdao Garrison Command was also established on the same day and its subordinate garrison troops have entered the urban area to perform their duties.
    [Xinhua News Agency, Hangzhou, 5th] The Zhejiang Military Region of the Chinese People's Liberation Army was established in Hangzhou a few days ago. The Commander of the Military Region is Wang Jian'an, the Political Commissars are Tan Zhenlin, Tan Qilong, and Ji Pengfei (concurrently Director of the Political Department), and the Chief of Staff is Li Yingxi.

### 青岛军管会成立　向明任主任马保三任市长  王建安任浙江军区司令员

1949-06-06
第1版()
专栏：

　　青岛军管会成立
  　向明任主任马保三任市长
    王建安任浙江军区司令员
    【新华社济南五日电】青岛市军管会及人民市政府已于二日成立。人民解放军山东军区任命向明为军管会主任，赖可可、谭希林为副主任。山东省人民政府任命马保三为青岛市市长。现各项接管工作已开始。又：青岛市警备司令部亦已于同日奉命成立，所属卫戍部队已进入市区执行职务。
    【新华社杭州五日电】中国人民解放军浙江省军区已于日前在杭州成立。军区司令员为王建安，政治委员为谭震林、谭启龙、姬鹏飞（兼政治部主任），参谋长为李迎希。
