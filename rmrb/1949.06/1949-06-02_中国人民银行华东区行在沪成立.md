Title: The East China Regional Branch of the People's Bank of China Established in Shanghai
Author:
Date: 1949-06-02
Page: 1

　　The East China Regional Branch of the People's Bank of China Established in Shanghai
    [Xinhua News Agency, Shanghai, 1st] The East China Regional Branch of the People's Bank of China was established in Shanghai on the 30th. Zeng Shan is the manager and Chen Mu is the deputy manager. The Shanghai Branch of the People's Bank of China was also established on the same day. Chen Mu concurrently serves as the manager, and Xie Shoufu and Lu Dungen are the deputy managers. The East China Regional Branch of the People's Bank of China has jurisdiction over branches in the three provinces of Jiangsu, Zhejiang, and Anhui, and the two cities of Beijing and Shanghai.

### 中国人民银行华东区行在沪成立

1949-06-02
第1版()
专栏：

　　中国人民银行华东区行在沪成立
    【新华社上海一日电】中国人民银行华东区行已于三十日在沪成立。由曾山任经理、陈穆任副经理。人民银行上海分行亦同日成立，经理由陈穆兼任，副经理为谢寿夫、卢钝根。人民银行华东区行所辖范围包括苏浙皖三省及京沪两市的分支行。
