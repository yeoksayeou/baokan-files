Title: Shanghai Mayor Chen Yi and Others Invite Prominent Figures from All Walks of Life to a Forum

Author:
Date: 1949-06-19
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Shanghai, June 18th: Shanghai Mayor Chen Yi, Shanghai Municipal Party Committee Secretary Rao Shushi, and others invited prominent figures from all walks of life in Shanghai, including Zhang Yuanji, Yan Huiqing, Tang Wenzhi, Cai Yuanpei's widow, Yu Huanzheng, Zhu Kezhen, Wu Youxun, Tao Menghe, Chen Wangdao, and Mao Yisheng, to a forum on the 15th. Yan and Tang were absent due to illness. At the meeting, opinions were widely exchanged on issues such as developing industrial production while also taking into account rural land reclamation, dredging rivers, developing water conservancy, restoring transportation, providing relief to and organizing unemployed people in Shanghai to go to the interior to participate in industrial and agricultural production, as well as current Shanghai's public security and educational transformation.

### 上海陈毅市长等　邀各界名流座谈

1949-06-19
第1版()
专栏：

　　上海陈毅市长等
  　邀各界名流座谈
    【新华社上海十八日电】上海市长陈毅、中共上海市委书记饶漱石等于十五日邀集上海各界耆老名流张元济、颜惠庆、唐文治、蔡元培夫人、俞寰澄、竺可桢、吴有训、陶孟和、陈望道、茅以升等举行座谈会。颜、唐两氏因病未到。会上对于发展工业生产并兼顾农村开荒，疏浚河道，发展水利，恢复交通，救济与组织上海失业者到内地去参加工业与农业生产，以及对当前上海的治安与教育改造等问题，广泛地交换了意见。
