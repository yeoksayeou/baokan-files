Title: North Korean Factories Increase Production and Practice Economy, Second Quarter Plan Completed Ahead of Schedule
Author:
Date: 1949-06-16
Page: 3

　　North Korean Factories
    Increase Production and Practice Economy
    Second Quarter Plan Completed Ahead of Schedule
    [Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, 15th] TASS, Pyongyang dispatch: North Korean factories have successively completed the second quarter production plans ahead of schedule. The Hasillun (transliteration) Paper Factory in North Hamgyong Province completed the second quarter production plan on June 4th. The Pyongyang Textile Factory and the Xinmang (transliteration) railway workers also completed the plan on June 6th. The brick factory in the Sangge (transliteration) area near Pyongyang also completed the plan on June 8th. The brick factory in Shadong (transliteration), the Pyongyang tobacco factory, and others have all completed the production plans for the first half of the year ahead of schedule. At the same time, various factories carried out economy campaigns. Cement factory workers have saved 660,000 Korean won.

### 北鲜各工厂  增产节约  第二季计划提前完成

1949-06-16
第3版()
专栏：

　　北鲜各工厂
    增产节约
    第二季计划提前完成
    【新华社北平十五日电】塔斯社平壤讯：北朝鲜各工厂纷纷提前完成第二季生产计划。咸镜北道的哈西伦（译音）造纸工厂，已于六月四日完成第二季生产计划。平壤的纺织工厂与新芒（译音）的铁道工人，也在六月六日完成了计划。平壤附近的桑哥（译音）地方的造砖工厂，也于六月八日完成了计划。在沙东（译音）的造砖工厂和平坏的烟草工厂等，也都提前完成了上半年的生产计划。各工厂同时进行了节约运动。水泥工厂工人已节省了朝鲜币六十六万元。
