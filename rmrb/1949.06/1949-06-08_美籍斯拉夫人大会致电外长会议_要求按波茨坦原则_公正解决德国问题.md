Title: American Slavic Congress Sends Telegram to Foreign Ministers' Conference Demanding Fair Resolution of the German Question According to Potsdam Principles
Author:
Date: 1949-06-08
Page: 3

Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, 7th. Polish News Agency dispatch: The American Slavic Congress has recently sent a telegram to the Paris Foreign Ministers' Conference, requesting them to "make the greatest effort to reach a peaceful agreement on the German question on the basis of the Yalta and Potsdam principles." The Congress expressed alarm at the resurgence of industrial magnates responsible for Nazi war crimes in West Germany and demanded the democratization of West Germany.

### 美籍斯拉夫人大会致电外长会议　要求按波茨坦原则  公正解决德国问题

1949-06-08
第3版()
专栏：

　　美籍斯拉夫人大会致电外长会议
      要求按波茨坦原则  公正解决德国问题
    【新华社北平七日电】波兰通讯社消息：美籍斯拉夫人大会顷致电巴黎外长会议，要求他们“作最大的努力，在雅尔塔与波茨坦原则的基础上达到关于德国问题的和平协议”。大会对应负纳粹战争罪行之责的工业大亨在西德重新掌权表示惊惧，并要求将西德民主化。
