Title: Tonight's Program on Beiping Xinhua Broadcasting Station
Author:
Date: 1949-06-27
Page: 1

　　Beiping Xinhua Broadcasting Station Tonight's Program
    [This Newspaper's News] Beiping Xinhua Broadcasting Station's lecture program from 19:50 to 20:10 tonight will feature Mr. Qian Sanqiang, a professor in the Physics Department of Tsinghua University, speaking on "Soviet Education and Scientific Research." From 19:30 to 19:50, the women's program will introduce the experience of the Lüda Nursery. From 22:20 to 22:50, the arts program will feature a segmented performance of the famous historical Peking opera "Hong Niangzi" by the Peking Opera Experimental School of the North China Cultural and Art Work Committee. From 20:50 to 21:00, a comprehensive report will be broadcast—the enthusiastic support of the people in Xi'an, Kaifeng, Suiyuan, Inner Mongolia, Harbin, and the Lushun-Dalian area for the establishment of the Preparatory Committee of the New Political Consultative Conference.

### 北平新华广播电台今晚节目

1949-06-27
第1版()
专栏：

　　北平新华广播电台今晚节目
    【本报讯】北平新华广播电台今晚十九点五十分到二十点十分演讲节目，由清华大学物理系教授钱三强先生讲“苏联的教育与科学研究”。十九点三十分到十九点五十分妇女时间，介绍旅大托儿所的经验。二十二点二十分到二十二点五十分文艺节目，由华北文化艺术工作委员会平剧实验学校分段演唱著名历史平剧“红娘子”。二十点五十分到二十一点播送一篇综合报导——西安、开封、绥远、内蒙古、哈尔滨和旅顺大连地区人民兴高采烈地拥护新政治协商会议筹备会成立的情形。
