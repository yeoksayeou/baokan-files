Title: Private Banking and Finance Registration in Beiping Closes Yesterday: 51 Banks and Firms Present Capital of Over 133 Million
Author:
Date: 1949-06-01
Page: 2

[Reported by this newspaper] The registration and capital verification of private banks and financial firms handled by the Beiping Branch of the People's Bank of China concluded yesterday (May 31st) upon deadline. The banks that registered and presented their capital included: Jin Cheng, Da Lu, Yan Ye, Zhong Nan, China Industrial, China Agricultural and Industrial, Zhong Fu, Da Zhong, Xinhua Commercial, Guo Hua, Shanghai, Zhejiang Xingye, United Commercial, Beiping Commercial, Ju Xing Cheng, and 15 others. The Qian Zhuang (traditional Chinese banks) included: Da De Tong, Da De Heng, Heng Tai, Heng Xing, Qian Xing, Ji Xing, De Run Xing, Tong Yuan Xiang, Bao Sheng, Hou Sheng, Fu Sheng, Fu Cheng, Fu Shun De, Ren Chang, Ren Fa Gong, Yong Tai Gong, Yong Tai Cheng, Yong Zeng He, Rong Chang Hou, Quan Ju Hou, Yu Xing Zhong, Yi Ju, Ju Yi, Chun He, Qi Ming, Ping Yi, Ji Tong, San Ju Yuan, Jin Hui Feng, Bao Feng Sheng, Xin Cheng Ji, Jun Xing He, Xiang Rui Xing, and 33 others. The exchange businesses: Jin Hong Yu, Zhong Yuan, and Yong Zeng, three in total. The total amount of capital verified was 133,510,000 yuan.

　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　(Yu)

### 平银钱业登记昨日截止　五十一行庄呈验资金一亿三千余万

1949-06-01
第2版()
专栏：

　　平银钱业登记昨日截止
  　五十一行庄呈验资金一亿三千余万
    【本报讯】中国人民银行北平分行所办理之私营银钱业登记验资，昨日（三十一日）期满截止。进行登记并呈验资金之银行有：金城、大陆、盐业、中南、中国实业、中国农工、中孚、大中、新华商业、国华、上海、浙江兴业、联合商业、北平商业、聚兴诚等十五家及钱庄：大德通、大德恒、恒泰、恒兴、谦兴、济兴、德润兴、同元祥、宝生、厚生、福生、福成、福顺德、仁昌、仁发公、永泰公、永泰成、永增合、荣长厚、全聚厚、裕兴中、义聚、聚义、春和、启明、平易、济通、三聚源、晋汇丰、宝丰盛、信成记、峻兴和、祥瑞兴等三十三家。兑换业：晋鸿裕、中源、永增三家，共收验资本金总额一亿三千三百五十一万元。
    　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　（宇）
