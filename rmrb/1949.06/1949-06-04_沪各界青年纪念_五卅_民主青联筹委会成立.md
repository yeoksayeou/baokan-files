Title: Shanghai's Various Circles of Youth Commemorate "May Thirtieth" - Preparatory Committee for the Democratic Youth Federation Established
Author:
Date: 1949-06-04
Page: 3

Xinhua News Agency, Shanghai, 3rd. Shanghai's youth representatives from all walks of life held a "May Thirtieth" commemoration meeting at Jiaotong University on the afternoon of the 1st. Two thousand two hundred people attended, including workers, students, professional youth, military youth, scientific workers, young teachers, and representatives of the YMCA. A presidium of fifteen people, including Li Chang, Dai Baitao, and Fan Changjiang, presided. Mayor Chen Yi and Liu Changsheng, representative of the All-China Federation of Trade Unions, attended and spoke. Mayor Chen encouraged everyone in his speech to strive forward for the construction tasks of the new China and new Shanghai. Mayor Chen put forward three major current tasks: eliminate the remaining enemies, establish revolutionary order, protect the people's democratic freedom, and restore production. Labor youth should organize trade unions to support their own interests, strive to realize the policy of developing production, prospering the economy, taking into account both public and private interests, and benefiting both labor and capital, and become model workers and labor heroes. The task of intellectual youth is to establish their own organizations, unite all democratic youth, resume classes, unite teachers and students, study and master the laboring people's new outlook on life and Mao Zedong Thought, study and master industrial and agricultural production knowledge and various scientific knowledge and technologies, and strive forward for the cause of new democracy, world lasting peace, and the people's democratic cause. Li Chang of the Central Committee of the Communist Youth League put forward four major styles to Shanghai's 600,000 youth: combining a calm mind with fiery emotions, democratic unity, humble learning, and arduous simplicity, and encouraged young comrades and students to work hard for the construction of the new Shanghai and the new China. The Shanghai Student Union, the China Technology Association and other three groups presented flags to the Central Committee of the Communist Party of China and all commanders and fighters of the Third Field Army, which were accepted by Mayor Chen Yi and General Zhang Dingcheng on behalf of them. The meeting approved the establishment of the Preparatory Committee of the Shanghai Democratic Youth Federation, nominated Li Chang and other twenty-eight people as preparatory committee members, and sent telegrams of respect to Chairman Mao Zedong and Commander-in-Chief Zhu De.

### 沪各界青年纪念“五卅”　民主青联筹委会成立

1949-06-04
第3版()
专栏：

　　沪各界青年纪念“五卅”
  　民主青联筹委会成立
    【新华社上海三日电】上海市各界青年代表于一日下午在交通大学举行“五卅”纪念大会。到会工人、学生、职业青年、部队青年、科学工作者、青年教师、基督教青年代表等共二千二百人。由李昌、戴白韬、范长江等十五人为主席团，陈毅市长及全国总工会代表刘长胜均到会讲话。陈市长在讲话中鼓励大家为新中国新上海的建设任务而努力前进。陈市长提出当前三大任务：肃清残余敌人、建立革命秩序、保护人民民主自由、恢复生产。劳动青年要组织拥护自己利益的工会，努力实现发展生产、繁荣经济、公私兼顾、劳资两利的方针，要成为模范工作者与劳动英雄。知识青年的任务为成立自己的团体，团结一切民主青年，恢复上课，团结师生，学习和掌握劳动人民的新人生观与毛泽东思想，学习和掌握工业农业生产知识及各种科学知识与技术，为新民主主义事业，世界持久和平与人民民主事业而努力前进。青年团中央委员会李昌向上海六十万青年提出冷静的头脑与火热的感情结合、民主团结、虚心学习与艰苦扑素四大作风，勉励青年同志与同学们为建设新上海、新中国而努力。上海学生联合会、中国技术协会等三团体分别向中共中央及第三野战军全体指战员献旗，当由陈毅市长及张鼎丞将军代表分别接受。大会通过成立上海市民主青年联合会筹备委员会，推定李昌等二十八人为筹备委员，并电毛泽东主席、朱德总司令致敬。
