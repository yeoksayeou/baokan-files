Title: Four Foreign Ministers' Meeting: Three Secret Discussions on the Berlin Issue
Author:
Date: 1949-06-08
Page: 3

　　Four Foreign Ministers' Meeting: Three Secret Discussions on the Berlin Issue
    Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, 7th. Reports from various sources: The Four Foreign Ministers' Meeting of the Soviet Union, the United States, Britain, and France has entered its third week. On June 6th, the four foreign ministers held their third secret meeting to continue discussions on the Soviet and American proposals regarding the Berlin issue, chaired by French Foreign Minister Schuman. Prior to the four-nation meeting, the foreign ministers of the United States, Britain, and France held a separate consultation at the French Foreign Ministry.

### 四国外长会议　三次秘密讨论柏林问题

1949-06-08
第3版()
专栏：

　　四国外长会议　三次秘密讨论柏林问题
    【新华社北平七日电】各方报道：苏美英法四国外长会议已进入第三周。六月六日，四国外长举行第三次秘密会议，继续讨论苏联与美国关于柏林问题的建议，由法外长舒曼任主席。在举行四国会议之前，美英法外长曾在法国外交部举行单独会商。
