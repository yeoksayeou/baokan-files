Title: Trade Union Office Runs Workers' Political School
Author:
Date: 1949-06-06
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Nanjing, June 4th. The Nanjing Office of the All-China Federation of Trade Unions has been approved by the Nanjing Municipal People's Government to establish a workers' political school to recruit employed and unemployed workers to cultivate backbones for production and construction. The school's enrollment quota is tentatively set at 1,500 to 2,000 people. In the last week of last month, 457 people reported, of whom 437 have been admitted and reported to the school.

### 总工会办事处办工人政治学校

1949-06-06
第1版()
专栏：

　　总工会办事处办工人政治学校
    【新华社南京四日电】中华全国总工会南京办事处已呈准南京市人民政府举办工人政治学校，招收在业与失业工人，培养生产建设的骨干。该校招收名额暂定为一千五百至二千人。上月底一周内已有四百五十七人报告，其中四百三十七人已被录取并报到入学。
