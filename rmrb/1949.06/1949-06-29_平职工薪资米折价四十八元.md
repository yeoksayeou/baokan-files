Title: Peiping Workers' Salary Millet Equivalent Priced at Forty-Eight Yuan
Author:
Date: 1949-06-29
Page: 2

　　Peiping Workers' Salary Millet Equivalent Priced at Forty-Eight Yuan
    [From Our Newspaper] Yesterday morning, the Finance Department of the North China People's Government convened relevant departments such as the Municipal Federation of Trade Unions to discuss and determine the millet equivalent price for the second half of this month for workers' salaries in the Peiping area, which will be issued at forty-eight yuan per catty.

### 平职工薪资米折价四十八元

1949-06-29
第2版()
专栏：

　　平职工薪资米折价四十八元
    【本报讯】本月下半月北平区职工薪资小米折价，昨日上午经华北人民政府财政部召集市总工会等各有关部门商订，按每斤四十八元发给。
