Title: U.S. Grain Prices Continue to Fall

Author:

Date: 1949-06-19
Page: 3

　　U.S. Grain Prices
  　Continue to Fall
    [Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, 18th] Associated Press, New York, 15th: According to the wholesale price index published by the Dun & Bradstreet statistical office, U.S. grain prices fell by an average of five points last week, only two points away from falling to the lowest point in twenty-eight months, created on February 8th of last year.

### 美粮食价格　继续跌落

1949-06-19
第3版()
专栏：

　　美粮食价格
  　继续跌落
    【新华社北平十八日电】美联社纽约十五日讯：根据邓·布列得斯特统计所发表的批发物价指数，美国粮食价格在上星期平均跌落五分，只差两分便降到在去年二月八日造成的二十八个月来的最低点了。
