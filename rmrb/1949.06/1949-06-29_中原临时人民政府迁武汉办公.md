Title: Central Plains Provisional People's Government Moves to Wuhan to Conduct Business
Author:
Date: 1949-06-29
Page: 1

　　Central Plains Provisional People's Government Moves to Wuhan to Conduct Business
    [Xinhua News Agency, Hankou, 28th] In order to facilitate its leadership work, the Central Plains Provisional People's Government has moved from Kaifeng to Wuhan and began operations on the 25th.

### 中原临时人民政府迁武汉办公

1949-06-29
第1版()
专栏：

　　中原临时人民政府迁武汉办公
    【新华社汉口二十八日电】中原临时人民政府为便于领导工作，已由开封迁抵武汉，并于二十五日开始办公。
