Title: Current Exchange Rate for Real Value Savings

Author: 
Date: 1949-06-09
Page: 2

　　Current Exchange Rate for Real Value Savings
    The Savings Department of the People's Bank of China, Beiping Branch, announces that the standard real commodity unit exchange rate for today (i.e., the 9th) for real value savings is 252.29 yuan.
