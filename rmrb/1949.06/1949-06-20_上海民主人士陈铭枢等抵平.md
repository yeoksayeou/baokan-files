Title: Democratic Figure Chen Mingshu and Others from Shanghai Arrive in Beiping
Author:
Date: 1949-06-20
Page: 3

Xinhua News Agency, Beiping, 19th: Democratic figure Chen Mingshu and a group of fourteen people arrived in Beiping on the afternoon of the 18th from Shanghai via Tianjin.

### 上海民主人士陈铭枢等抵平

1949-06-20
第3版()
专栏：

　　上海民主人士陈铭枢等抵平
    【新华社北平十九日电】上海民主人士陈铭枢等一行十四人已于十八日下午由上海经天津抵平。
