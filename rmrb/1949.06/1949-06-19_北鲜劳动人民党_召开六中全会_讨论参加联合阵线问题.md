Title: North Korean Workers' Party Holds Sixth Plenary Session to Discuss Joining the United Front
Author:
Date: 1949-06-19
Page: 3

　　North Korean Workers' Party
  　Holds Sixth Plenary Session
  　Discussing the Issue of Participating in the United Front
    [Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, 17th] Pyongyang News: According to the Workers' Daily, the organ of the North Korean Workers' Party, the North Korean Workers' Party recently held the Sixth Plenary Session, where it listened to and discussed Kim Il-sung's report on the Workers' Party's participation in the founding congress of the All-Korea Democratic Fatherland United Front. The meeting elected the representatives of the North Korean Workers' Party to attend the congress.

### 北鲜劳动人民党　召开六中全会　讨论参加联合阵线问题

1949-06-19
第3版()
专栏：

　　北鲜劳动人民党
  　召开六中全会
  　讨论参加联合阵线问题
    【新华社北平十七日电】平壤讯：据朝鲜劳动人民党机关报劳动新闻报道：北朝鲜劳动人民党日前召开六中全会，听取和讨论了金日成关于劳动人民党参加全朝鲜民主祖国联合阵线成立大会的报告。会议选出了北朝鲜劳动人民党参加该大会的代表。
