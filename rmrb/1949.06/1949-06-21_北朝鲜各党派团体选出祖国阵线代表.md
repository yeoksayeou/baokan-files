Title: North Korean Parties and Organizations Elect Representatives to Fatherland Front
Author:
Date: 1949-06-21
Page: 3

Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, 20th: The Korean Central News Agency reports that all political parties and public organizations in North Korea are actively participating in the work of forming the United Democratic Fatherland Front. At meetings held by the central committees of various political parties and public organizations, the specific tasks of establishing the United Democratic Fatherland Front were discussed, and representatives were elected to attend the inaugural meeting of the United Democratic Fatherland Front.

### 北朝鲜各党派团体选出祖国阵线代表

1949-06-21
第3版()
专栏：

    北朝鲜各党派团体选出祖国阵线代表
    【新华社北平二十日电】朝鲜中央电讯社消息：北朝鲜的一切政党和公共团体，都积极参加了组成民主祖国联合阵线的工作。在各政党中央委员会以及各公共团体举行的会议上，都曾讨论过关于成立民主祖国联合阵线的具体任务，并选出了参加民主祖国联合阵线成立大会的代表。
