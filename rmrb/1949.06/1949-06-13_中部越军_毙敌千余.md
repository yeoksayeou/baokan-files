Title: Central Vietnam Army Kills Over 1,000 Enemy Troops
Author:
Date: 1949-06-13
Page: 3

　　Central Vietnam Army
  　Kills Over 1,000 Enemy Troops
    Xinhua News Agency, Peiping, 12th: Vietnam Radio broadcast: During the battles in the Dinh Te Li Tian area of Central Vietnam from February 9th to May 19th, the Vietnamese army killed 1,200 French troops, destroyed 20 trucks, 1 locomotive, 10 freight cars, 1 seventy-five millimeter cannon, 4 mortars, 8 machine guns, and seized some spoils of war.

### 中部越军　毙敌千余

1949-06-13
第3版()
专栏：

　　中部越军
  　毙敌千余
    【新华社北平十二日电】越南电台广播：二月九日至五月十九日期间，在中越的丁特利田地区战斗中，越南军计击毙法军一千二百名，击毁卡车二十辆，火车头一辆，货车十辆，七五公厘炮一门，迫击炮四门，机枪八挺，并缴获若干战利品。
