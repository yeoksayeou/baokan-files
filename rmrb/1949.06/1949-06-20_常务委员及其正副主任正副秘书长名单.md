Title: List of Standing Committee Members and their Heads, Deputy Heads, Secretary-General, and Deputy Secretaries-General

Author:

Date: 1949-06-20
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Peiping, 19th -- According to Articles 4 and 5 of the Organizational Regulations of the Preparatory Committee of the New Political Consultative Conference (CPPCC), the list of Standing Committee members of the Preparatory Committee of the New CPPCC, as well as its Chairman, Vice-Chairmen, Secretary-General, and Deputy Secretaries-General, is as follows:

I. Standing Committee Members:

　　Mao Zedong, Zhu De, Li Jishen, Li Lisan, Shen Junru, Shen Yanbing, Zhou Enlai, Lin Boqu,
　　Ma Xulun, Ma Yinchu, Ulanhu (Yun Ze), Zhang Bojun, Zhang Lan, Zhang Xiruo, Guo Moruo,
　　Chen Shutong, Chen Jiageng, Huang Yanpei, Cai Tingkai, Cai Chang, Tan Pingshan (listed in order of number of strokes in their surnames)

II. Chairman and Vice-Chairmen of the Standing Committee:

　　Chairman: Mao Zedong
　　Vice-Chairmen: Zhou Enlai, Li Jishen, Shen Junru, Guo Moruo, Chen Shutong

III. Secretary-General: Li Weihan

　　Deputy Secretaries-General: Yu Xinqing, Shen Tilan, Zhou Xinmin, Lian Guan, Huan Xiang, Sun Qimeng, Qi Yanming
　　Yan Baohang, Luo Shuzhang (listed in order of number of strokes in their surnames)

专栏：

　　常务委员及其正副主任正副秘书长名单

    毛泽东、朱德、李济深、李立三、沈钧儒、沈雁冰、周恩来、林伯渠、
    马叙伦、马寅初、乌兰夫、（云泽）、章伯钧、张澜、张奚若、郭沫若、
    陈叔通、陈嘉庚、黄炎培、蔡廷锴、蔡畅、谭平山（以姓氏笔划多少为序）

    主任、毛泽东
    副主任、周恩来、李济深、沈钧儒、郭沫若、陈叔通
