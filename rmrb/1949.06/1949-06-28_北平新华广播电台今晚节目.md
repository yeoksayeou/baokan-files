Title: Tonight's Program on Beiping Xinhua Broadcasting Station
Author:
Date: 1949-06-28
Page: 1

　　Beiping Xinhua Broadcasting Station Tonight's Program

    [This Dispatch] Beiping Xinhua Broadcasting Station's speech program from 19:50 to 20:10 this evening will feature Mr. Wang Pingyang, Chief of the Engineering Department of the North China Electric Power Company under the Public Enterprise Department of the North China People's Government, speaking on "General Overview of the Work of the North China Electric Power Grid." The workers' program from 19:30 to 19:50 will introduce how workers across the country are welcoming "July 1st" with practical actions on the production front. The arts program from 22:20 to 22:50 will feature the Combat Drama Troupe of the First Field Army of the People's Liberation Army performing four new songs including "The Army Marches Forward," "The People's Liberation Army and the People are One Family," "The Great March," and "Singing of Chairman Mao," as well as a "Northwest Folk Song Medley." The commentary program from 21:00 to 21:15 will broadcast Pravda's response to a reader's question, "Can the Peace Camp Crush the Plans of the Warmongers?"

### 北平新华广播电台今晚节目

1949-06-28
第1版()
专栏：

　　北平新华广播电台今晚节目
    【本报讯】北平新华广播电台今晚十九点五十分到二十点十分演讲节目，由华北人民政府公营企业部华北电业公司工程处处长王平洋先生讲“华北电业电力网工作概况”。十九点三十分到十九点五十分职工节目，介绍全国工人在生产战线上以实际行动迎接“七一”的情形。二十二点二十分到二十二点五十分文艺节目，由人民解放军第一野战军战斗剧社演播“军队向前进”，“解放军老百姓是一家人”，“大进军”，“歌唱毛主席”等四支新歌曲和“西北民歌联奏”。二十一点到二十一点十五分评论节目，播送真理报答复读者所提出的问题“和平阵营能否粉碎战争贩子底计划”。
