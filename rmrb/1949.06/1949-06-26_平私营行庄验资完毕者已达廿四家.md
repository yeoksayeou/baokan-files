Title: Twenty-four Private Banks and Firms in Beiping Have Completed Capital Verification
Author:
Date: 1949-06-26
Page: 2

[This News Agency] Beiping: Twenty-four private banks and firms in Beiping have now completed their capital verification.
    [Report from Our Newspaper] Yesterday (the 25th), ten more private banks and firms in Beiping, including Qianxing, Dadeheng, Dedetong, Fucheng, Chunhe, Dalu, Pingyi, Derunxing, Jitong, and Baofengsheng, submitted their second installment of capital to the Beiping Branch of the People's Bank of China for verification. As of the 25th, a total of twenty-four firms have fully paid up their capital, and approximately twenty-four or five firms are close to completing full payment.
　　　　　　　　　　
　　　(Yu)

### 平私营行庄验资完毕者已达廿四家

1949-06-26
第2版()
专栏：

　　平私营行庄验资完毕者已达廿四家
    【本报讯】平市私营行庄昨日（二十五）续有谦兴、大德恒、大德通、福成、春和、大陆、平易、德润兴、济通、宝丰盛等十家向中国人民银行北平分行缴验二次应缴资金。至二十五日止，全部缴验资金者已有二十四家，接近全部缴验者有二十四、五家。　　　　　　　　　　
　　　（宇）
