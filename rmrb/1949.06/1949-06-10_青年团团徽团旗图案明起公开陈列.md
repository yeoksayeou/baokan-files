Title: Youth League Emblem and Flag Designs to be Publicly Displayed Starting Tomorrow
Author:
Date: 1949-06-10
Page: 1

　　Youth League Emblem and Flag Designs to be Publicly Displayed Starting Tomorrow
    [This newspaper's dispatch] The Review Committee for the Emblem, Flag, and Song of the China New Democratic Youth League held its first meeting the day before yesterday (the 8th) and decided to publicly display the submitted designs for the Youth League emblem and flag at 17 East Chang'an Street for five days starting from the 11th, welcoming people from all walks of life to visit and offer opinions. The deadline for submissions of lyrics for the Youth League song, as well as designs for the Youth League flag and emblem, has been extended to the 25th of this month, and the deadline for song scores has been extended to July 5th. It is reported that the committee plans to reward the authors of the selected Youth League emblem, song lyrics, and song scores with 200 jin of millet each, and the author of the selected Youth League flag design with 100 jin.

### 青年团团徽团旗图案明起公开陈列

1949-06-10
第1版()
专栏：

　　青年团团徽团旗图案明起公开陈列
    【本报讯】中国新民主主义青年团团徽、团旗、团歌审订委员会前日（八日）开第一次会议，决定将应征之团徽、团旗图案自十一日起在东长安街十七号公开陈列五天，欢迎各界人士参观提出意见。并将征求团歌歌词、团旗及团徽图案截止日期，延至本月二十五日，团歌曲谱截止日期，延至七月五日。闻该会拟对于将来中选之团徽、团歌歌词、团歌曲谱作者，各赠小米二百斤，团旗作者赠一百斤为酬。
