Title: All-China Federation of Trade Unions Sends Congratulatory Message to All India Trade Union Congress, Protests Unreasonable Persecution by Indian Government
Author: 
Date: 1949-06-03
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, June 2nd.

The All-China Federation of Trade Unions has telegraphed congratulations to the All India Trade Union Congress representatives' conference held in Bombay. The original telegram is as follows:

To Comrade Chhedi, Chairman of the All India Trade Union Congress Annual Conference, and to all All India Trade Union Representatives:

We have learned from telegrams that the All India Trade Union Congress Annual Conference was held in Bombay on the 28th of last month and successfully concluded on the 29th. We extend our warm congratulations! We express our strong indignation and protest against the various persecutions you have suffered from the Indian government, and the police's barbaric obstruction of yesterday's conference, resulting in one representative being arrested and six injured. We firmly believe that no persecution can stop the unity and awakening of the Indian working class.

　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　All-China Federation of Trade Unions
　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　June 1st

### 全国总工会电贺全印工代会　抗议印政府无理迫害

1949-06-03
第1版()
专栏：

　　全国总工会电贺全印工代会
  　抗议印政府无理迫害
    【新华社北平二日电】中华全国总工会顷电在孟买举行的全印职工大会代表大会致贺，原电如下：
    全印职工大会年会主席柴狄亚并转全印职工代表们：
    由电讯获悉全印职工大会年会于上月二十八日在孟买召开，并已于二十九日胜利闭幕，我们敬致热烈的祝贺！对于你们遭受印度政府的种种迫害，昨日大会又被警察蛮横阻扰，致代表被捕一人、受伤六人，我们表示强烈的愤慨抗议，我们深信：任何迫害都不能阻止印度工人阶级的团结和觉醒。
    　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　中华全国总工会
    　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　六月一日
