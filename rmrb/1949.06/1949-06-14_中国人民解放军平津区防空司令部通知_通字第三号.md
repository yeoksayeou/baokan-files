Title: Notice from the Air Defense Headquarters of the Pingjin Area of the Chinese People's Liberation Army, Tong Zi No. 3
Author:
Date: 1949-06-14
Page: 1

　　Notice from the Air Defense Headquarters of the Pingjin Area of the Chinese People's Liberation Army
  　Tong Zi No. 3
    June 14th
    1. The municipality of Ping (now Beijing) will test all electric sirens at 11:30 AM today (the 14th). Citizens are requested not to be alarmed upon hearing the sound.
    2. The sound emitted by the test siren will be a single long tone. Please avoid mistaking it for an air raid warning signal. This notice is hereby given.

### 中国人民解放军平津区防空司令部通知　通字第三号

1949-06-14
第1版()
专栏：

　　中国人民解放军平津区防空司令部通知
  　通字第三号
    六月十四日
    一、今（十四）日上午十一时半平市试验各电动警报器，希市民闻声勿惊。
    二、试验警报器所发出之音响为一长声，切防与空袭警报讯号误会，特此通知。
