Title: The First Municipal People's Education Center of Beiping to Hold Industrial Exhibition
Author:
Date: 1949-06-27
Page: 2
　　
　　The First Municipal People's Education Center of Beiping
　　To Hold Industrial Exhibition
　　[Beiping News] In order to allow the general public to have a deeper understanding of the state of industrial products in this city, and to welcome July 7th, the First Municipal People's Education Center of Beiping will hold an industrial product exhibition. The collection of industrial products has now begun. The time for registration or telephone contact is from 9:00 AM to 9:00 PM, and the address is outside the Drum Tower.

### 平市第一民教馆将举办工业展览

1949-06-27
第2版()
专栏：

　　平市第一民教馆
  　将举办工业展览
    【平市讯】北平市立第一人民教育馆为使一般群众对本市工业品状况有更深刻的了解，迎接七七，将举办工业生产品展览。现已开始集工业生产品。前征往登记或通电接洽时间为上午九时至下午九时，地址在地外鼓楼。
