Title: Indian Police Beat Attending Representatives; Trade Union Congress Lodges Strong Protest
Author:
Date: 1949-06-03
Page: 3

　　Xinhua News Agency, Peiping, 1st -- Bombay Dispatch: The All-India Trade Union Congress recently issued a statement protesting the beating of worker representatives attending the conference by Indian fascist police. The beating occurred before the conference began on the morning of the 29th. Police attacked a group of worker representatives in front of the Trade Union Congress hall building, beating them severely with batons. One representative was arrested and six were injured. After the incident, police densely deployed around the hall of the conference venue, and the surrounding urban area was also full of police.

### 印警殴打与会代表职工大会严重抗议

1949-06-03
第3版()
专栏：

　　印警殴打与会代表职工大会严重抗议
    【新华社北平一日电】孟买讯：全印职工代表大会顷发表声明，抗议印度法西斯警察殴打参加会议的职工代表。殴打发生在二十九日上午的会议举行之前，警察在代表大会会厅大楼门前袭击一群工人代表，以警棍猛殴他们。代表一名被捕、六名被殴伤。事件发生后，警察复在会场的大楼四周密布警察，附件的市区内，也遍布警察。
