Title: North China People's Government Decides to Convene Agricultural Production Conference
Author:
Date: 1949-06-21
Page: 1
    North China People's Government Decides to Convene Agricultural Production Conference

    [Ping City News] The North China People's Government has decided to convene a regional agricultural production conference on July 10 to summarize the agricultural production in the first half of the year and discuss various issues in future agricultural production. On the 20th of this month, telegrams were sent to all administrative offices and provincial and municipal governments to immediately prepare materials regarding the completion of agricultural production plans, drought resistance and sowing, labor mutual aid, production leadership, sideline business conditions, farmers' purchasing power and means of production, and to submit opinions and plans for future production development to the Agricultural Department of the North China People's Government by the end of the month, so that they can be discussed at the meeting.

### 华北人民政府决定  召开农业生产会议

1949-06-21
第1版()
专栏：

    华北人民政府决定
    召开农业生产会议
    【平市讯】华北人民政府决定于七月十日召开全区农业生产会议，总结前半年的农业生产及讨论今后农业生产中的各种问题。本月二十日已分电各行署、省、市政府立即准备关于农业生产计划完成情形、抗旱播种、劳动互助、生产领导、副业情况、农民购买力与生产资料等材料，并提出今后生产发展的意见和计划，于月底前报送华北人民政府农业部，以便届时提出研讨。
