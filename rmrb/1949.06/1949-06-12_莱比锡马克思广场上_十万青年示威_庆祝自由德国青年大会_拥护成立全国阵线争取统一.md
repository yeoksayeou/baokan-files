Title: Tens of Thousands of Young People Demonstrate on Marx Square in Leipzig, Celebrating the Free German Youth Congress and Supporting the Establishment of a National Front for Unification

Author: 
Date: 1949-06-12
Page: 3

　　Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, 11th: TASS reports: Leipzig held a Youth Festival after the closing of the Third Congress of the Free German Youth League. The Youth Festival began with a demonstration of 120,000 people, including representatives of the Free German Youth League, students from various states and Berlin in the Eastern Zone, and youth delegations from West Germany. The ranks of the demonstrators headed straight for the huge Marx Square, where the grand event celebrating the Third Congress of the Free German Youth was held. The congress began with the singing of the World Democratic Youth Anthem. The chairman of the Free German Youth League, Erich Honecker, spoke first. He said: "We pledge all our efforts to make German youth stand in the ranks of the world's peaceful forces, headed by the socialist country, the Soviet Union. We are fully determined to support the National Front fighting for Germany's unification and independence." The congress warmly welcomed the Soviet youth representative, Yastaven, who assured the audience in his speech that German democratic youth could always regard Soviet youth as reliable friends. Wilhelm Pieck, representing the Presidium of the German People's Council, congratulated the democratic youth of Germany, pointing out that the sympathy of progressive democratic forces around the world for the Free German Youth League encourages German youth to contribute all their strength to consolidate the democratic system and strive for German unification and a peace treaty. Jexter Demis, the representative of the World Federation of Democratic Youth, congratulated the congress on behalf of the 55 million youth of the Federation, saying that the Free German Youth League was invited to send representatives to the Second World Youth Congress to be held in Budapest on August 14th. The participants warmly welcomed this news. The congress closed in the singing of the Democratic Youth Anthem.

### 莱比锡马克思广场上　十万青年示威　庆祝自由德国青年大会  拥护成立全国阵线争取统一

1949-06-12
第3版()
专栏：

　　莱比锡马克思广场上
  　十万青年示威
  　庆祝自由德国青年大会   
    拥护成立全国阵线争取统一
    【新华社北平十一日电】塔斯社讯：莱比锡在自由德国青年联盟第三届代表大会闭幕后举行青年节。青年节以十二万人的示威游行开始，参加游行者有自由德国青年联盟代表、东占区各州和柏林的学生以及西德的青年代表团。游行者的行列直趋巨大的马克思广场，庆祝自由德国青年第三届代表大会的盛会就在广场上举行。大会开始时唱世界民主青年曲。自由德国青年联盟主席昂纳克首先发言。他说：“我们誓以一切努力使德国青年站在以社会主义国家苏联为首的全世界和平力量的行列中。我们具有充分决心，支持为德国的统一与独立而奋斗的全国阵线。”大会热烈欢迎苏联青年代表亚斯达文，他在演说时向听众保证：德国民主青年可以永远把苏联青年作为可靠的友人。皮克代表德国人民委员会主席团向德国的民主青年致贺，他指出：全世界进步的民主力量对自由德国青年联盟的同情，鼓励德国青年贡献其一切力量，来巩固民主制度，争取德国统一和缔结和约。世界民主青年联盟代表杰克斯特·戴米斯代表该盟五千五百万青年向大会致贺，他说：邀请自由德国青年联盟选派代表参加八月十四日在布达佩斯举行的世界青年第二届代表大会。与会者热烈欢迎这一消息。大会在民主青年曲的歌声中闭幕。
