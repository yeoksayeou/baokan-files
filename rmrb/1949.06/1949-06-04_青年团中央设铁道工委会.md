Title: Central Committee of the Communist Youth League Establishes Railway Work Committee
Author:
Date: 1949-06-04
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Peiping, 3rd. In order to unify and strengthen the leadership of the national railway youth work, the Central Committee of the China New Democratic Youth League has established the "National Railway Work Committee of the China New Democratic Youth League," and appointed Lu Ping, Yue Zhijian, Jia Yi, Zhang Yongwei, and Li Xiebo as members, with Lu Ping and Yue Zhijian as the director and deputy director respectively. The Work Committee began its work at the Ministry of Railways on June 1st. It will focus on the Tangshan, Changxindian, and Beijing East Railway Stations under the Tianjin Railway Administration, as well as the headquarters of the Ministry of Railways.

### 青年团中央设铁道工委会

1949-06-04
第1版()
专栏：

　　青年团中央设铁道工委会
    【新华社北平三日电】为了统一和加强领导全国铁道青年工作，中国新民主主义青年团中央委员会特成立“中国新民主主义青年团全国铁道工作委员会”，指定陆平、岳志坚、贾义、张勇为、李颉伯等五人为委员；并以陆平、岳志坚二人为正副主任。该工作委员会已于六月一日在铁道部开始办公。并将以平津铁路管理局的唐山、长辛店、北平东站及铁道部本部为工作重点。
