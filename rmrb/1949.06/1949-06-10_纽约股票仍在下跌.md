Title: New York Stocks Continue to Fall
Author:
Date: 1949-06-10
Page: 3

Xinhua News Agency, Beiping, 8th. Reuters, New York reports: New York stocks continue to fall. Stocks for hardware, film, oil, aircraft, railways, automobiles and other major commodities all plummeted on the 6th, falling by several cents to several dollars per share. Some observers on Wall Street admit that the reason for the stock decline is the fear among stockholders that the current sudden drop in consumer purchasing power will continue.

### 纽约股票仍在下跌

1949-06-10
第3版()
专栏：

　　纽约股票仍在下跌
    【新华社北平八日电】据路透社纽约讯：纽约的股票仍在下跌中。五金、胶片、油类、飞机、铁路、汽车和其他主要商品的股票，六日均告暴跌，每股分别跌落几分至几元美金。华尔街一些观察家承认：此次股票下跌的原因，是股票持有人害怕目前消费者购买力的突然下降，要继续下去。
