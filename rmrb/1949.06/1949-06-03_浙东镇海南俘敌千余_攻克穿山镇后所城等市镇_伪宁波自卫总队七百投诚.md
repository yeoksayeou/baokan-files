Title: Zhejiang East Town of Zhenhai Captures Over 1,000 Enemies; Conquers Chuanshan Town, Housuo City and Other Towns; 700 from the Puppet Ningbo Self-Defense Force Surrender
Author:
Date: 1949-06-03
Page: 1
　　
Xinhua News Agency, Hangzhou, 2nd—The People's Liberation Army liberating Zhenhai, Zhejiang East, captured over 1,000 Kuomintang army soldiers of the 87th Army who fled south along the coast from Zhenhai in Chuanshan Town, Housuo City and other places south of Zhenhai in the three days from May 26th to 28th, and occupied many towns such as Chuanshan Town and Housuo City.

[Another Dispatch] Over 700 personnel of the puppet Ningbo Self-Defense Force surrendered to the People's Liberation Army in Dongqian Lake and Hanling, more than 30 li southeast of Ningbo, on May 30th. They brought with them 35 light and heavy machine guns and over 670 long and short guns.

### 浙东镇海南俘敌千余　攻克穿山镇后所城等市镇　伪宁波自卫总队七百投诚

1949-06-03
第1版()
专栏：

　　浙东镇海南俘敌千余
  　攻克穿山镇后所城等市镇
  　伪宁波自卫总队七百投诚
    【新华社杭州二日电】解放浙东镇海的人民解放军于五月二十六日至二十八日的三天中，在镇海以南的穿山镇、后所城等地，俘获由镇海沿海岸南逃的国民党军八十七军一千余名，并攻占穿山镇、后所城等市镇多处。
    【又讯】伪宁波自卫总队七百余人，五月三十日在宁波东南三十余里的东钱湖、韩岭两地向人民解放军投诚。携来轻重机枪三十五挺、长短枪六百七十余支。
