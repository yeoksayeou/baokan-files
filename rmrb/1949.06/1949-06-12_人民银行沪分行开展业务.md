Title: People's Bank of China Shanghai Branch Commences Operations
Author:
Date: 1949-06-12
Page: 1

The Shanghai Branch of the People's Bank of China is actively commencing operations. The Treasury Department officially opened for business on the 4th, collecting national taxes, with current daily tax revenue exceeding 60 million yuan. The Business Department handles deposit and loan, remittance, and other services, with deposits absorbed already reaching over 6 billion yuan. Domestic remittances for interbank transfers with Ping, Tianjin, Beijing, Hangzhou and other places will begin within the week.

### 人民银行沪分行开展业务

1949-06-12
第1版()
专栏：

　　人民银行沪分行开展业务
    中国人民银行上海分行，刻正积极开展业务。公库部于四日起正式营业，代收国税，现每天有六千余万元的税款收入。业务部办理存放款、汇兑等业务，吸收之存款数额已达六十余亿元，国内汇兑周内即可开始办理与平、津、京、杭等地的通汇。
