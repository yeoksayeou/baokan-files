Title: Order of the North China People's Government: Suimeng District Changed to Suiyuan Province; Yang Zhilin Appointed as Provincial Chairman
Author:
Date: 1949-06-15
Page: 1

　　Order of the North China People's Government
  　Suimeng District Changed to Suiyuan Province
  　Yang Zhilin Appointed as Provincial Chairman
    [Ping City News] The North China People's Government issued an order on June 13th, deciding that Suimeng District will be renamed Suiyuan Province from today, and the Suimeng Government will be changed to the Suiyuan Provincial People's Government. The order also appointed Yang Zhilin, the former Chairman of the Suimeng Government, as the Chairman of the Suiyuan Provincial People's Government.

### 华北人民政府命令　绥蒙区改绥远省　杨植霖任省主席

1949-06-15
第1版()
专栏：

　　华北人民政府命令
  　绥蒙区改绥远省
  　杨植霖任省主席
    【平市讯】华北人民政府于六月十三日发出命令，决定绥蒙区自即日起改称绥远省，绥蒙政府改为绥远省人民政府，并任命原绥蒙政府主席杨植霖为绥远省人民政府主席。
