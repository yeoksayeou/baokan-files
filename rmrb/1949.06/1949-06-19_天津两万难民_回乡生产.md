Title: Tianjin 20,000 Refugees Return Home to Production
Author:
Date: 1949-06-19
Page: 2

　　Tianjin 20,000 Refugees
  　Return Home to Production
    [Xinhua News Agency, Tianjin, 17th] The Tianjin Municipal People's Government Civil Affairs Bureau has been sending refugees from the war back to their hometowns for production since January 27th. As of the end of May, a total of more than 21,000 people have been sent. Among them, more than 6,800 people received travel expenses from the People's Government, totaling more than 100,000 yuan in Renminbi.

### 天津两万难民　回乡生产

1949-06-19
第2版()
专栏：

　　天津两万难民
  　回乡生产
    【新华社天津十七日电】津市人民政府民政局，自一月二十七日开始遣送战争中的难民返乡生产，截至五月底，已共遣送二万一千余人。其中从人民政府领得路费者达六千八百余人。共发人民币十万余元。
