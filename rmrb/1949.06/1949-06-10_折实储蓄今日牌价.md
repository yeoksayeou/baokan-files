Title: Value of Commodity-Based Savings Today

Author:

　　Commodity-Based Savings Rate Today
    The standard commodity unit price for commodity-based savings at the Savings Department of the People's Bank of Peiping Branch today (i.e., the 10th) is 249.62 yuan.
