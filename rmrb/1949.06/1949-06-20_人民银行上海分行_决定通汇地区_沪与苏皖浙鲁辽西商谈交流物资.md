Title: People's Bank of China Shanghai Branch Decides on Remittance Area; Shanghai Discusses and Exchanges Goods with Jiangsu, Anhui, Zhejiang, Shandong, Liaoning and Western Provinces
Author:
Date: 1949-06-20
Page: 1

　　People's Bank of China Shanghai Branch
  　Decides on Remittance Area
  　Shanghai Discusses and Exchanges Goods with Jiangsu, Anhui, Zhejiang, Shandong, Liaoning and Western Provinces
    [North China New China Broadcasting Station Broadcast] Shanghai, 19th - The Shanghai Branch of the People's Bank of China has decided to handle remittance business from the 21st in more than 50 cities along the Beijing-Shanghai, Shanghai-Hangzhou, Tianjin-Pukou, and Beijing-Hankou railways, and along the lower reaches of the Yangtze River. Remittance is temporarily limited to mail transfer and draft transfer. Telegraphic transfer can also be restored in the short term.
    [Xinhua News Agency, Shanghai, 19th] The preparatory office of the Shanghai Municipal Trading Corporation, in order to connect trade in various regions, has been exchanging supply and demand information with representatives from trading companies in Jiangsu, Anhui, Zhejiang, Shandong, Liaoning, and the western regions who have come to Shanghai in recent days, preparing to exchange goods from various regions in a planned way.

### 人民银行上海分行　决定通汇地区　沪与苏皖浙鲁辽西商谈交流物资

1949-06-20
第1版()
专栏：

　　人民银行上海分行
  　决定通汇地区
  　沪与苏皖浙鲁辽西商谈交流物资
    【北平新华广播电台广播】上海十九日消息：人民银行上海分行决定从二十一日起办理京沪、沪杭、津浦、平汉等铁路沿线，和长江下游沿岸五十多个城市的汇兑业务，通汇暂时限于信汇和票汇，电汇在短期内也可以恢复。
    【新华社上海十九日电】上海市贸易总公司筹备处为沟通各地贸易，连日与苏、皖、浙、鲁、辽西等地贸易公司派来上海的代表，交换供需情况，准备有计划地交流各地物资。
