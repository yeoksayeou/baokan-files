Title: Zhangjiakou City Redeems Gold and Silver Ornaments
Author:
Date: 1949-06-02
Page: 2
Economic News

　　Zhangjiakou City Redeems Gold and Silver Ornaments
　　[Chahar Telegram] In order to help gold and silver ornament businesses change their profession, the Zhangjiakou branch of the Chahar Provincial People's Bank has redeemed gold and silver according to the preferential redemption methods stipulated by the North China People's Government. From the 20th to the 26th of last month, a total of twelve gold and silver ornament businesses in the city, except for two that did not redeem, have all redeemed and are preparing to change their profession. A total of more than thirty taels of gold and more than six thousand five hundred and ninety-six taels of silver were redeemed.

### 张市收兑金银饰物

1949-06-02
第2版()
专栏：经济新闻

　　张市收兑金银饰物
    【察哈尔电】察省人民分行张市营业处，为帮金银饰物业转业，特根据华北人民政府规定的优待收兑办法，收兑金银。自上月二十日至二十六日六天中，全市金银饰物业共十二家，除两家未兑换外，余皆已兑换筹备转业。计共兑黄金三十两余，白银六千五百九十六两余。
