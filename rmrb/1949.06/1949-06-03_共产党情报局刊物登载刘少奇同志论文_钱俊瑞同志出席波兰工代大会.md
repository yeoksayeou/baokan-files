Title: Communist Information Bureau Publication Publishes Comrade Liu Shaoqi's Thesis; Comrade Qian Junrui Attends Polish Labor Congress
Author:
Date: 1949-06-03
Page: 1

　　Communist Information Bureau Publication Publishes Comrade Liu Shaoqi's Thesis
　　Comrade Qian Junrui Attends Polish Labor Congress

　　【Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, 2nd dispatch】 Bucharest dispatch: The Communist and Workers' Parties Information Bureau's publication "For Lasting Peace and People's Democracy" in its tenth issue of this year (May 15th), under the title "The Victory of Democratic Forces in China," summarized the brilliant achievements of the Chinese People's Liberation Army up to the liberation of Hangzhou. The eleventh issue of the publication (June 1st) published "On Internationalism and Nationalism" written by Liu Shaoqi, one of the leaders of the Chinese Communist Party.
　　【Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, 2nd dispatch】 Warsaw dispatch from the Polish Press Agency: After attending the Ninth Congress of the Czechoslovak Communist Party, Qian Junrui, representative of the Chinese Communist Party, arrived in Warsaw on May 30th to attend the Polish Trade Union Congress.

### 共产党情报局刊物登载刘少奇同志论文　钱俊瑞同志出席波兰工代大会

1949-06-03
第1版()
专栏：

　　共产党情报局刊物登载刘少奇同志论文
  　钱俊瑞同志出席波兰工代大会
    【新华社北平二日电】布加勒斯特讯：共产党与工人党情报局刊物“争取持久和平与人民民主”本年第十期（五月十五日），以“中国民主力量的胜利”为题，综述中国人民解放军载至解放杭州时为止的辉煌成就。该刊第十一期（六月一日）登载了中国共产党领袖之一刘少奇著的“论国际主义与民族主义”。
    【新华社北平二日电】波兰通讯社华沙讯：中国共产党代表钱俊瑞于参加捷克斯洛伐克共产党第九届代表大会之后，五月三十日抵华沙，参加波兰职工会代表大会。
