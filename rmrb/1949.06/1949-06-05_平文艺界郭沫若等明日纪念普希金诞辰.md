Title: Literary and Art Circles in Beiping, Including Guo Moruo, to Commemorate Pushkin's Birthday Tomorrow

Author:

Date: 1949-06-05
Page: 1

　　[This News Report] Tomorrow (the 6th) is the 150th anniversary of the birth of the great Russian poet Pushkin. Literary and art figures in Beiping, including Guo Moruo, Mao Dun, Zheng Zhenduo, Tian Han, Zhou Yang, Ke Zhongping, Hu Feng, Sha Kefu, He Luting, Cao Jinghua, Ma Sicong, Ai Qing, Li Bozhao, Ge Baoquan, Li Guangtian, Feng Zhi, Zang Kejia, etc., are preparing to hold a memorial meeting tomorrow with speeches, recitations, music and other programs.
　　Further news: Poet Xiao San has already set out a few days ago to attend the Soviet Union's 150th anniversary commemoration of Pushkin's birth upon invitation.

### 平文艺界郭沫若等明日纪念普希金诞辰

1949-06-05
第1版()
专栏：

　　平文艺界郭沫若等明日纪念普希金诞辰
    【本讯报】明日（六日）为俄国大诗人普希金一百五十周年诞辰纪念，在平文艺界人士郭沫若、茅盾、郑振铎、田汉、周扬、柯仲平、胡风、沙可夫、贺绿汀、曹靖华、马思聪、艾青、李伯钊、戈宝权、李广田、冯至、臧克家等筹备于明日举行纪念会、定有讲演、朗诵、音乐等节目。
    又讯：诗人萧三已于日前起程，应邀参加苏联普希金一百五十周年诞辰纪念会。
