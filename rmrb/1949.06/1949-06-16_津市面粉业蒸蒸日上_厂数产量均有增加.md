Title: Tianjin Flour Industry Thriving: Number of Factories and Output Both Increase
Author:
Date: 1949-06-16
Page: 2

　　Tianjin Flour Industry Thriving
  　Number of Factories and Output Both Increase
    [Tianjin Electric] Under the support of the People's Government, the flour industry in Tianjin has gradually resumed production. All twenty-one flour mills had resumed operation by March, and three new factories, Xin Ya, Zhong He, and Fu Long, were added in May. Thanks to the People's Government's assistance in resolving raw material issues, the output of each factory has gradually increased. The total flour production in the city in May was more than 425,000 bags, an increase of more than 30% compared to April.

### 津市面粉业蒸蒸日上　厂数产量均有增加

1949-06-16
第2版()
专栏：

　　津市面粉业蒸蒸日上
  　厂数产量均有增加
    【天津电】天津市的面粉业在人民政府扶植下，逐渐恢复生产。二十一家面粉厂，三月份已经全部复工，五月份又新增加了新亚、中和、福隆三家。由于人民政府帮助解决了原料问题，各厂产量逐渐增加，五月份全市面粉总生产量是四十二万五千多袋，比四月份的产量增加了百分之三十以上。
