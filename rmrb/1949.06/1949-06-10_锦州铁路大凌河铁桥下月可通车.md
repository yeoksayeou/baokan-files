Title: Jinzhou Railway Daling River Iron Bridge to Open to Traffic Next Month
Author:
Date: 1949-06-10
Page: 1

(Broadcast by Beiping Xinhua Broadcasting Station) News from western Liaoning on the 9th: The Jinzhou Railway Daling River Iron Bridge engineering team began constructing a permanent iron bridge on April 1st. The bridge is 845 meters long and requires 67 steel beams. At the beginning of the work, the engineering office's trade union convened a meeting of workers to draft a plan. At the meeting, the workers firmly stated that they would overcome difficulties to complete the task and established a merit evaluation committee. After the meeting, a competition was launched. So far, ten bridge piers and nine pier foundations have been completed. It is expected to be repaired and open to traffic around July 15th.

### 锦州铁路大凌河铁桥下月可通车

1949-06-10
第1版()
专栏：

　　锦州铁路大凌河铁桥下月可通车
    【北平新华广播电台广播】辽西九日消息：锦州铁路大凌河铁桥工程队，四月一日开始修建永久性的铁桥，该桥全长八百四十五公尺，需要钢梁六十七根，工作刚开始的时候，工程处的工会就召集工友开会草拟计划，会上工友们都坚决表示要克服困难完成任务，并且成立了评功委员会，会后就展开竞赛，到现在为止，已经修好了十个桥墩和九个墩基，预计在七月十五日左右修复通车。
