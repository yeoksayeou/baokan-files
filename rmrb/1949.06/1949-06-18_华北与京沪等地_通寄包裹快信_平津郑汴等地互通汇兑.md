Title: North China, Shanghai and Other Places to Send Packages and Express Letters; Tianjin, Zhengzhou, Kaifeng and Other Places to Exchange Money Orders

Author: 
Date: 1949-06-18
Page: 1

　　North China and Jinghu (Nanjing-Shanghai) and other places
  　Can send packages and express letters
  　Tianjin, Zhengzhou, Kaifeng and other places can mutually exchange money orders
    [Reported by this newspaper] The North China Postal Administration has now issued an order to all post offices in the region that, from the date of receiving the order, they can start sending small packages not exceeding 500 cubic centimeters to the newly liberated areas in the south of the Yangtze River. And according to traffic conditions, it is designated to the following 14 bureaus in Jiangsu Province: Nanjing, Shanghai, Zhenjiang, Danyang, Wuxi, Suzhou, Zhenru Railway Station, Benniu, Wujin, Hushu Pass, Kunshan, Nanxiang, Songjiang, and Fengjing. The following 4 bureaus in Anhui Province: Anqing, Wuhu, Dangtu, and Datong. The following 15 bureaus in Zhejiang Province: Hangzhou, Jiaxing, Shaoxing, Yiwu, Xiaoshan, Linpu, Zhuji, Jinhua, Ningbo, Jiashan, Changan, Linping, Jianqiao, Lanxi, and Yuyao. The following 2 bureaus in Jiangxi Province: Nanchang and Jiujiang. The following 14 bureaus in Hubei Province: Hankou, Wuchang, Hanyang, Guangshui, Huayuan, Xiaogan, Huangpi, Wuxue, Qichun, Shihuiyao, Huangshigang, Huanggang, Echeng, and Xindi, to start sending ordinary packages weighing less than 5 kilograms. The Tianjin and other bureaus can also send express mail to the above places. In the future, for every newly liberated area in the south of the Yangtze River, in addition to immediately accepting ordinary and registered mail from that area, small packages of 500 cubic centimeters or less can also be accepted immediately. For Changsha and Yueyang in Hunan Province, Fuzhou and Xiamen in Fujian, Guangzhou and Sanshui in Guangdong Province, and Yongning and Cangwu in Guangxi Province, once they are liberated, ordinary packages weighing less than 5 kilograms and express mail can be sent immediately. After all traffic is restored, large packages weighing more than 10 kilograms will be mutually exchanged successively.
    In addition, the North China Postal Administration and the Henan Provincial Postal Administration have agreed to decide that the Beijing Postal Administration, Postal Savings and Remittance Bureau, Tianjin Postal Administration, Postal Savings and Remittance Bureau, and Taiyuan, Heze, Puyang, Zhuxian, Caoxian, Dingtao, Xingtai, Anyang, Xinxiang, Jiaozuo, Linqing, Handan, Daming, Hengshui, Yangcheng, Baoding, Cangxian, and Shijiazhuang and other 22 bureaus and Kaifeng, Zhengzhou, Luoyang, Yuxian, Xiangcheng, Xuchang, Luohe, Zhumadian, Xinyang, Huaiyang, Gongxian, Shangqiu, Zhuji, Zhoujiakou, Nanyang and other 15 post offices in Henan Province will mutually exchange postal remittances starting from July 1. The minimum remittance limit is 1,000 yuan, and the maximum is 50,000 yuan.

### 华北与京沪等地　通寄包裹快信　平津郑汴等地互通汇兑

1949-06-18
第1版()
专栏：

　　华北与京沪等地
  　通寄包裹快信
  　平津郑汴等地互通汇兑
    【本报讯】华北邮政总局现已通令全区各邮局，自接令之日起开始与江南新解放地区均可通寄重五百公分之小型包裹。并按交通条件，指定与江苏省之南京、上海、镇江、丹阳、无锡、苏州、真如车站、奔牛、武进、浒墅关、昆山、南翔、松江、枫泾等十四局。安徽省之安庆、芜湖、当涂、大通等四局。浙江省之杭州、嘉兴、绍兴、义乌、萧山、临浦、诸暨、金华、宁波、嘉善、长安、临平、笕桥、兰溪、余姚等十五局。江西省之南昌、九江两局。湖北省之汉口、武昌、汉阳、广水、花园、孝感、黄陂、武穴、蕲春、石灰窑、黄石港、黄冈、鄂城、新堤等十四局，开始通寄重五公斤以下之普通包裹。平津等局并可对以上各地寄送快递邮件。今后对江南每解放一地除马上可收寄该地之普通与挂号邮件外，并可立即收寄五百公分以下之小型包裹。对湖南省之长沙、岳阳，福建之福州、厦门，广东省之广州、三水，广西省之邕宁、苍梧等地，俟一解放则可马上通寄重五公斤以下之普通包裹及快递邮件。俟交通全部恢复后即陆续增加互通十公斤以上之大包裹。
    又华北邮政总局与河南省邮政管理局商妥，决定北平邮政管理局、邮政储金汇业局、天津邮政管理局、邮政储金汇业局及太原、菏泽、濮阳、筑先、曹县、定陶、邢台、安阳、新乡、焦作、临清、邯郸、大名、衡水、阳城、保定、沧县、石家庄等二十二局与河南省之开封、郑州、洛阳、禹县、襄城、许昌、漯河、驻马店、信阳、淮阳、巩县、商邱、朱集、周家口、南阳等十五邮局于七月一日开始互通邮政汇兑。汇兑限额最少者一千元，最多者五万元不等。
