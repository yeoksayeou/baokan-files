Title: More Locations in Beiping Open for Telegraph Service
Author:
Date: 1949-06-24
Page: 2

(Beiping News) The Beiping Telecommunications Bureau announced on the 22nd that in the past ten days, telegraph services from Beiping have been opened to fifteen more cities. These fifteen cities are: Nanxun in Zhejiang, Pucheng, Shaowu, and Nanping in Fujian, Yulin and Sanyuan in Shaanxi, Nancheng and Lichuan in Jiangxi, Guangshui in Hubei, Xinpu in Jiangsu, Lianghe in Songjiang Province, Queshan, Luoshan, Jigongshan, and Huangchuan in Henan. Business telegrams can also be sent and received. The public is advised to take note and utilize these services.

### 北平通报地点又增加十五处

1949-06-24
第2版()
专栏：

    北平通报地点又增加十五处
    【平市讯】北平电信局二十二日发表：在最近十天内，北平对外又有十五个城市的电报开放了。这十五个城市是：浙江南浔，福建浦城、邵武、南平，陕西榆林、三原，江西南城、黎川，湖北广水，江苏新浦，松江省的亮河，河南确山、罗山、鸡公山、潢川，并且都收拍商报，请各界人士注意利用。
