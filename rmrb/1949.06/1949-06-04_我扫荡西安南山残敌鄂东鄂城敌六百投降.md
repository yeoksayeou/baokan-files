Title: We Wipe Out Remnants in the Southern Mountains of Xi'an; 600 Enemy Troops Surrender in Echeng, Eastern Hubei
Author:
Date: 1949-06-04
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Xi'an, 3rd: The People's Liberation Army, sweeping up the remaining enemy forces in the southern mountains of Xi'an, annihilated four platoons of the enemy's 69th Army, 84th Division, south of Yinjiahui Town at Dayukou on May 29th. In addition to those killed and wounded, more than 130 bandits, including a battalion commander and below, were captured.
    
Xinhua News Agency, Hankou, 3rd: Liu Wenguang, deputy head of the puppet Pacification Corps in Echeng, eastern Hubei, led the first and third battalions of the corps, a total of more than 600 men, to surrender to the People's Liberation Army on May 31st.

### 我扫荡西安南山残敌鄂东鄂城敌六百投降

1949-06-04
第1版()
专栏：

　　我扫荡西安南山残敌鄂东鄂城敌六百投降
    【新华社西安三日电】向西安南山扫荡残敌的解放军，五月二十九日在引驾回镇以南的大峪口歼灭敌六十九军八十四师的四个排，除毙伤外，俘匪营长以下一百三十余名。
    【新华社汉口三日电】湖北东部鄂城伪绥靖团副团长刘文光，率该团一、三两营共六百余人，于五月三十一日向解放军投降。
