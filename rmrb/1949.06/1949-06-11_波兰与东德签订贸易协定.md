Title: Poland and East Germany Sign Trade Agreement
Author:
Date: 1949-06-11
Page: 3

　　Xinhua News Agency, Beiping, 10th: The trade agreement between the Soviet-occupied zone of Germany and Poland was signed on June 8th, according to a Polish news agency report. The term is from 1949 to 1950. The Soviet-occupied zone of Germany will supply Poland with machinery and industrial equipment worth a total of 30 million US dollars. Poland will supply the Soviet-occupied zone of Germany with coal, textiles, cast iron, metals, food, and other industrial products.

### 波兰与东德签订贸易协定

1949-06-11
第3版()
专栏：

　　波兰与东德签订贸易协定
    【新华社北平十日电】波兰通讯社讯：德境苏占区与波兰之间的贸易协定，业于六月八日签字。期限从一九四九年到一九五○年。德境苏占区将供给波兰以机器与工业装备，总值三千万美元。波兰将供给德境苏占区以煤、纺织品、铸铁、金属、食品和其他工业品。
