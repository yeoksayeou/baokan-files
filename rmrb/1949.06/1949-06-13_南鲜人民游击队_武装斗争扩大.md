Title: South Korean People's Guerrilla Force Expands Armed Struggle
Author:
Date: 1949-06-13
Page: 3

　　South Korean People's Guerrilla Force
  　Expands Armed Struggle
    [Xinhua News Agency, Pyongyang, 11th] TASS, Pyongyang, 11th: The armed struggle of the South Korean people against the Li Chengwan (Syngman Rhee) puppet government is expanding daily. Guerrillas are active in Chungcheongbuk-do and Jeollabuk-do, and are engaged in fierce battles with the puppet constabulary in areas such as Digin (transliteration, same below), Chungdu, Oksam, Daesu, Danhae, and Sucheon. In the Daesu area, the guerrillas occupied a residential area after the battle.

### 南鲜人民游击队　武装斗争扩大

1949-06-13
第3版()
专栏：

　　南鲜人民游击队
  　武装斗争扩大
    【新华社北平十二日电】塔斯社平壤十一日讯：南朝鲜人民反对李承晚伪政府的武装斗争，日益扩大。游击队在忠清北道与全罗北道活动，并与伪警备队在狄金（译音，下同）、忠都、玉森、台苏、丹海与苏川等地区进行激战。在台苏地区，游击队在作战以后占领一居民区。
