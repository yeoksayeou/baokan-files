Title: Re-establishing the Four-Power Allied Kommandatura in Berlin: All Decisions Must Be Agreed Upon Unanimously
Author:
Date: 1949-06-10
Page: 3

    Re-establishing the Four-Power Allied Kommandatura in Berlin
  　Any Decision Must Be Agreed Upon Unanimously
    The second part of Vyshinsky's speech pointed out that the Soviet Union suggests re-establishing the Four-Power Allied Kommandatura in Berlin. This is necessary in order to coordinate measures for the administration of all of Berlin and to ensure the normal life of the entire city. In order to achieve coordination and ensure normal life in Berlin, the Soviet Union suggests handing over the vast majority of issues to the City Council and the municipal government, so as to limit the powers of the Four-Power Allied Kommandatura. The issues falling entirely under the jurisdiction of the Four-Power Allied Kommandatura would only include regulating how the provisional constitution of 1946 is observed, supervising the imprisonment of war criminals convicted by the Nuremberg International Tribunal, certain public security issues, and economic and administrative issues whose resolution involves measures taken by the occupying authorities.
    Vyshinsky concluded: The Soviet delegation maintains the following opinion, that according to Article 3 of the 1946 regulations of the Four-Power Allied Kommandatura in Berlin, all decisions of the Kommandatura must be adopted unanimously. All issues under the jurisdiction of the Four-Power Allied Kommandatura must have a unanimously agreed decision. Unanimous agreement is required in approving decrees of the Berlin City Council and the city-wide government. Unanimous agreement is required in discussing protests against decisions made by Berlin local authorities – the City Council and the municipal government. Only by approving these decisions with unanimous agreement can the normal life of Berlin be guaranteed.

### 重建柏林四国司令部　任何决定必须一致协议

1949-06-10
第3版()
专栏：

    重建柏林四国司令部
  　任何决定必须一致协议
    维辛斯基发言的第二部分指出，苏联建议重建柏林的四国司令部。为了协调柏林全市管理上的措施，并保证整个柏林的正常生活，这是必要的。为了实现协调和保证柏林正常生活，苏联建议把极大部分问题交市议会和市政府管辖，以限制四国司令部的职权。在完全归四国司令部管辖的问题中，只包括管制一九四六年临时宪法之如何被遵守，监督经努连堡国际法庭判决之战犯的监禁，某些关系公安的问题以及那些在解决时牵连占领当局所采取之措施的经济性质和行政性质的问题。
    维辛斯基末称：苏联代表团仍保持下述意见，即按照柏林四国司令部一九四六年条例第三条的规定，四国司令部的各项决定，都必须一致通过。凡属四国司令部管辖的问题，都必须有一致协议的决定。在批准柏林市议会和全市范围政府的法令时，必须一致协议。在讨论对柏林地方当局——市议会和市政府的决定的抗议时，必须一致协议。只有用一致协议批准这些决定的办法，才能保证柏林的正常生活。
