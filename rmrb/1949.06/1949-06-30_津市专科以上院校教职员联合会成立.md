Title: Tianjin Municipality College and University Faculty and Staff Union Established
Author:
Date: 1949-06-30
Page: 2
　　
　　Tianjin Municipality College and University
  　Faculty and Staff Union Established
    [Tianjin Electric] The first representative assembly of faculty and staff of colleges and universities in Tianjin was held on the 26th and 27th for two consecutive days. More than 100 faculty and staff representatives from ten units including Nankai University and Peiyang University attended. After discussion, the conference adopted the "Articles of Association of the Tianjin Municipality College and University Faculty and Staff Union" and its tasks, and elected 19 executive committee members including Zhang Guofan and Xiao Caiyu, formally establishing the Tianjin Municipality College and University Faculty and Staff Union. The conference also passed a telegram to Chairman Mao, Commander-in-Chief Zhu, and all commanders and fighters of the People's Liberation Army to pay their respects, expressing their willingness to strengthen unity and work together for the cultural and educational construction of New Democracy.

### 津市专科以上院校教职员联合会成立

1949-06-30
第2版()
专栏：

　　津市专科以上院校
  　教职员联合会成立
    【天津电】津市专科以上院校教职员第一届代表大会于二十六、二十七连续举行两日，出席南开大学、北洋大学等十单位教职员代表一百余人。大会经讨论后，通过了“天津市专科以上院校教职员联合会章程”及任务，并选出执行委员张国藩、萧采瑜等十九人，正式成立天津市专科以上院校教职员联合会。大会并通过向毛主席、朱总司令及人民解放军全体指战员通电致敬，表示愿加强团结，共同为新民主主义的文化教育建设而努力。
