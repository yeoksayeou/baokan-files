Title: Tan Kah Kee and Others Arrive in Shenyang
Author:
Date: 1949-06-30
Page: 1

　　Xinhua News Agency, Shenyang, 29th -- Overseas Chinese democratic personage Tan Kah Kee and his party of five, who came to Northeast China for a visit, arrived in Shenyang on the morning of the 25th. Lin Feng, Chairman of the Northeast Administrative Committee, Vice Chairman Gao Chongmin, and Shenyang Mayor Zhu Qiwen and others greeted them at the station.

### 陈嘉庚等到达沈阳

1949-06-30
第1版()
专栏：

　　陈嘉庚等到达沈阳
    【新华社沈阳二十九日电】来东北参观的华侨民主人士陈嘉庚等一行五人，已于二十五日上午到达沈阳。东北行政委员会主席林枫、副主席高崇民、沈阳市长朱其文等都到车站观迎。
