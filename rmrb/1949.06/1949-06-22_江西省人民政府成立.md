Title: Jiangxi Provincial People's Government Established
Author:
Date: 1949-06-22
Page: 1

    Xinhua News Agency, Nanchang, 21st: The Jiangxi Provincial People's Government was established on June 17th. The Chinese Revolutionary Military Commission appointed Shao Shiping as the Chairman of the province, and Fan Shiren and Fang Zhichun as Vice-Chairmen, all of whom have taken office and are performing their duties.

### 江西省人民政府成立

1949-06-22
第1版()
专栏：

    江西省人民政府成立
    【新华社南昌二十一日电】江西省人民政府已于六月十七日成立。中国革命军事委员会任命邵式平为省主席，范式人、方志纯为副主席，均已到职视事。
