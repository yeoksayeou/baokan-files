Title: Tonight's Program on Beiping Xinhua Broadcasting Station
Author:
Date: 1949-06-30
Page: 1

　　Beiping Xinhua Broadcasting Station Tonight's Program
    [This newspaper's dispatch] Beiping Xinhua Broadcasting Station's speech program from 19:50 to 20:10 tonight will feature Minister Teng Daiyuan of the Railway Department of the Chinese People's Revolutionary Military Commission broadcasting to the entire nation. The art and literature program from 22:20 to 22:50 will feature the People's Art Troupe performing "Introduction to Soviet Songs," with renowned vocalist Du Yijia performing the Volga Boatmen's Song, among others. The revolving program from 19:30 to 19:50 tonight will be the Women's Time program sponsored by the All-China Democratic Women's Federation, introducing the International Democratic Women's Federation. The international affairs segment from 21:15 to 21:30 will broadcast (1) the opening of the founding congress of the Korean Democratic Fatherland United Front; (2) the latest news about the Chinese delegation attending the Second Congress of the World Federation of Trade Unions.

### 北平新华广播电台今晚节目

1949-06-30
第1版()
专栏：

　　北平新华广播电台今晚节目
    【本报讯】北平新华广播电台今晚十九点五十分到二十点十分演讲节目，由中国人民革命军事委员会铁道部滕代远部长对全国广播。二十二点二十分到二十二点五十分文艺节目，由人民文工团演播“苏联歌曲介绍”，并有著名声乐家杜矢甲独唱伏尔加船夫曲等。十九点三十分到十九点五十分轮回节目，今晚是由中华全国民主妇女联合会主办的妇女时间，介绍国际民主妇女联合会。二十一点十五分到二十一点三十分国际时事，播送（一）朝鲜民主祖国联合阵线成立大会开幕；（二）出席世界职工联盟第二届代表大会的中国代表团近况。
