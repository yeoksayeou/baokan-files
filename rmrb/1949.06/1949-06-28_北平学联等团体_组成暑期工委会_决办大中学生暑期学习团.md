Title: Peiping Student Federation and Other Groups Form Summer Work Committee; Decide to Organize Summer Study Groups for University and Middle School Students
Author:
Date: 1949-06-28
Page: 2

　　Peiping Student Federation and Other Groups
  　Form Summer Work Committee
  　Decide to Organize Summer Study Groups for University and Middle School Students
    [Peiping News] Students in Peiping are enthusiastically responding to the All-China Student Federation's notice regarding summer study work. The Peiping Student Federation, in coordination with the Preparatory Committee of the New Democratic Youth League of Peiping and other organizations and groups, has formed the "Summer Student Work Committee." The committee has decided to organize summer study groups for university and middle school students during this summer vacation, and to establish youth study gardens in five districts throughout the city, in order to unite the broad masses of students in collective study. The committee also plans to mobilize a large number of students to participate in workers' education work.

### 北平学联等团体　组成暑期工委会　决办大中学生暑期学习团

1949-06-28
第2版()
专栏：

　　北平学联等团体
  　组成暑期工委会
  　决办大中学生暑期学习团
    【平市讯】北平学生热烈响应全国学联关于暑期学习工作的通知。北平市学联已配合新民主主义青年团北平市筹委会等机关、团体组成了“暑期学生工作委员会”。该会决定在今年暑假期间，举办大中学生暑期学习团，及在全市分五区举办青年学园，以团结广大同学进行集体学习。该会并拟发动大批同学参加工人教育工作。
