Title: South Korean Guerrillas Occupy Three Cities Including Dengsen
Author:
Date: 1949-06-14
Page: 3
　　
　　South Korean Guerrillas
  　Occupy Three Cities Including Dengsen

    Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, 13th: The Korean Central News Agency reports: South Korean guerrillas are engaged in fierce fighting with Syngman Rhee's police in Jeolla Nam-do and Gyeongsang Buk-do. One guerrilla force occupied Dengsen County (all county names are transliterated). Another guerrilla force occupied Muan County and Gimcheon County. In Danyang County, guerrillas attacked a police detachment, seizing a large number of weapons and ammunition. In other areas, guerrillas systematically destroyed police stations and cut off telephone communications. The Syngman Rhee puppet government treats peaceful residents with brutal means, and in May, 490 patriots were shot in Jeolla Nam-do.

### 南鲜游击队　占领登森等三城

1949-06-14
第3版()
专栏：

　　南鲜游击队
  　占领登森等三城
    【新华社北平十三日电】朝鲜中央通讯社报道：南朝鲜游击队正与李承晚警察在全罗南道与庆尚北道进行激战。一支游击队占领登森（县名均译音，下同）县。另一支游击队占领木安县和基姆森县。在丹阳县，游击队攻击警察队，激获武器和弹药甚多。在其他地区，游击队有系统地捣毁警察所，并切断电话交通。李承晚傀儡政府以残暴的手段对待和平居民，五月份中全罗南道有四百九十名爱国者被枪毙。
