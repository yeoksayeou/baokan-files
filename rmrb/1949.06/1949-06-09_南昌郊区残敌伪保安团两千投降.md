Title: Two Thousand Remnants of the Enemy and Puppet Security Regiment Surrender in the Suburbs of Nanchang
Author:
Date: 1949-06-09
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Nanchang, 8th. After the liberation of Nanchang, more than 2,000 remnants of the four puppet security regiments who had scattered into the suburbs surrendered to the People's Liberation Army. Among them was the puppet Jiangxi Provincial Security Ninth Regiment, which surrendered to the Liberation Army led by its commander and deputy commander, Wu Youyuan and Luo Yangxian.

### 南昌郊区残敌伪保安团两千投降

1949-06-09
第1版()
专栏：

　　南昌郊区残敌伪保安团两千投降
    【新华社南昌八日电】南昌解放后，流窜在郊区的四个伪保安团共两千余人向人民解放军投降。其中整团投降者有伪江西省保安第九团，该团在其正副团长吴幼元、罗样贤率领下向解放军投降。
