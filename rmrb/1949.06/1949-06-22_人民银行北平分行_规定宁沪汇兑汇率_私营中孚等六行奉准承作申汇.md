Title: People's Bank of Beiping Branch Stipulates Nanjing-Shanghai Exchange Rate; Six Private Banks, Including Zhongfu, Approved to Handle Shanghai Remittances
Author: (宇)
Date: 1949-06-22
Page: 2

    People's Bank of Beiping Branch
    Stipulates Nanjing-Shanghai Exchange Rate
    Six Private Banks, Including Zhongfu, Approved to Handle Shanghai Remittances
    [This Newspaper Reports] Starting yesterday (the 21st), the Beiping Branch of the People's Bank of China began handling Shanghai-Nanjing remittances, with exchange rates ranging from 20/1000 to 50/1000, to be determined temporarily based on the nature of the remittance. For each remittance, the telegraph fee is 1,350 yuan for ordinary cases, and doubled for urgent cases.
    Further Dispatch: Six private banks in Beiping, namely Zhongfu, Shanghai, Jin Cheng, Juxingcheng, Zhejiang Xingye, and Xinhua, also received notification yesterday (the 21st) that they are permitted to freely handle remittances to Shanghai, with exchange rates ranging from 25/1000 to 50/1000.

### 人民银行北平分行  规定宁沪汇兑汇率  私营中孚等六行奉准承作申汇

1949-06-22
第2版()
专栏：

    人民银行北平分行
    规定宁沪汇兑汇率
    私营中孚等六行奉准承作申汇
    【本报讯】中国人民银行北平分行于昨日（二十一日）起，承办沪宁汇兑，汇率千分之二十至千分之五十，根据汇款性质临时决定。每笔汇款，普通者收电费一千三百五十元，加急者加倍。
    又讯：平私营中孚、上海、金城、聚兴诚、浙江兴业、新华等六银行，亦于昨日（二十一日）奉到准许自由通汇通知，正式承作申汇，汇率千分之二十五至五十不等。            
          （宇）
