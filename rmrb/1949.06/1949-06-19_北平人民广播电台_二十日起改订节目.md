Title: Beiping People's Broadcasting Station Revises Program Schedule Starting on the 20th

Author:
Date: 1949-06-19
Page: 1

　　Beiping People's Broadcasting Station
  　Revises Program Schedule Starting on the 20th
    Beiping People's Broadcasting Station will revise its program schedule starting on the 20th as follows:
    Morning:
    7:00 to 7:10 AM　Opening Music, Report on Morning Programs, Peking Opera
    7:10 to 7:25 AM　Relay of the First News Broadcast from Beiping Xinhua Broadcasting Station
    7:25 to 7:40 AM　First Local News
    7:40 to 8:00 AM　Morning Music
    8:00 to 8:15 AM　Time Announcement, Report on Market Conditions
    8:15 to 8:30 AM　Peking Opera, Preview of Noon and Evening Programs, Closing Music, Break
    Noon:
    12:20 to 12:35 PM　Opening Music, Report on Noon Programs, Peking Opera
    12:35 to 12:50 PM　Relay of the Second News Broadcast from Beiping Xinhua Broadcasting Station
    12:50 to 1:00 PM　Cantonese Opera
    1:00 to 1:30 PM　Time Announcement, Storytelling and Local Folk Music
    1:30 to 1:40 PM　Preview of Evening Programs, Closing Music, Break
    Evening:
    5:50 to 6:00 PM　Opening Music, Report on Evening Programs, Western Music
    6:00 to 6:20 PM　Time Announcement, Second Local News
    6:20 to 6:35 PM　Popular Science Talk (Monday, Tuesday, Wednesday, Thursday, Friday), Listener Service (Saturday, Sunday)
    6:35 to 6:50 PM　Youth Time (Monday, Wednesday, Thursday, Saturday, Sunday), Political Common Knowledge (Tuesday, Friday)
    6:50 to 7:10 PM　Entertainment Activities (Monday, Wednesday, Friday, Sunday), New Song Practice (Tuesday, Thursday, Saturday)
    7:10 to 7:30 PM　Workers' Time
    7:30 to 7:50 PM　Children's Time (Monday, Wednesday, Friday, Sunday)
　　Women's Time (Tuesday, Thursday, Saturday)
    7:50 to 8:10 PM　Peking Opera
    8:10 to 8:30 PM　Commentary, Voice of the Municipality
    8:30 to 9:30 PM　Relay of Beiping Xinhua Broadcasting Station Programs
    9:30 to 9:40 PM　Literary Ramblings (Monday, Tuesday, Wednesday, Thursday, Friday), Cantonese Opera (Saturday, Sunday)
    9:40 to 10:00 PM　Preview of Tomorrow's Programs, Western Music Closing Music, Break
    (Every Saturday, a Weekend Evening Party begins at 9:40 PM)
    The station's wavelengths are 353 meters, 850 kilocycles; 222 meters, 1350 kilocycles.
　　Important Correction
    In yesterday's newspaper, on page one, in the news article "Symposium on New Legislation Issues," the characters "Wang Ming" were omitted after "headed by the person in charge of the Legal Committee of the CCP Central Committee" in the fourth line. We hereby solemnly make this correction. Also, the fifth character in the thirteenth line from the bottom, "总" (zǒng, "total"), should be "纲" (gāng, "outline"). This is also corrected.
    Additionally, according to the "Errata Sheet for the Implementing Rules of the Trademark Registration Method of the North China Region" from the General Office of the North China People's Government: In the first article, second line, of the Implementing Rules, "Provisional Measures for Trademark Registration" should be "Trademark Registration Measures" (as published on page one of the 16th in this newspaper).

### 北平人民广播电台　二十日起改订节目

1949-06-19
第1版()
专栏：

　　北平人民广播电台
  　二十日起改订节目
    北平人民广播电台从二十日起改订节目如下：
    早晨：
    七点到七点十分　开始曲　报告早晨节目　平剧
    七点十分到七点二十五分　转播北平新华广播电台第一次新闻
    七点二十五分到七点四十分　第一次本市新闻
    七点四十分到八点　早晨的音乐
    八点到八点十五分　报时　报告行情
    八点十五分到八点三十分　平剧　预告中午和晚间节目　终了曲　体息
    中午：
    十二点二十分到十二点三十五分　开始曲　报告中午节目　平剧
    十二点三十五分到十二点五十分　转播北平新华广播电台第二次新闻
    十二点五十分到十三点　粤曲
    十三点到十三点三十分　报时　故事讲述和地方杂曲
    十三点三十分到十三点四十分　预告晚间节目　终了曲　休息
    晚间：
    十七点五十分到十八点　开始曲　报告晚间节目西乐
    十八点到十八点二十分　报时　第二次本市新闻
    十八点二十分到十八点三十五分　通俗科学讲话（星期一、二、三、四、五）　听众服务（星期六、日）
    十八点三十五分到十八点五十分　青年时间（星期一、三、四、六、日）政治常识（星期二、五）
    十八点五十分到十九点十分　文娱活动（星期一、三、五、日）　新歌练习（星期二、四、六）
    十九点十分到十九点三十分　职工时间
    十九点三十分到十九点五十分　儿童时间（星期一、三、五、日）
　妇女时间（星期二、四、六）
    十九点五十分到二十点十分　平剧
    二十点十分到二十点三十分　言论　市政之声
    二十点三十分到二十一点三十分　转播北平新华广播电台节目
    二十一点三十分到二十一点四十分　文艺漫谈（星期一、二、三、四、五）　粤曲（星期六、日）
    二十一点四十分到二十二点　预告明天节目　西乐终了曲　休息
    （每逢星期六从二十一点四十分开始周末晚会）
    该台波长三五三公尺，八五○千周；二二二公尺，一三五○千周。
　　重要补正
    昨日本报一版“座谈新立法问题”新闻第四行“首由中共中央法律委员会负责人”下脱漏“王明”二字，特此郑重补正。又：倒数第十三行第五字“总”应为“纲”，一并更正。
    又据华北人民政府秘书厅“华北区商标注册办法及其施行细则勘误表”中称：施行细则第一条第二行“商标注册暂行办法”，应为“商标注册办法”（载十六日本报第一版）。
