Title: Czechoslovakian National Trade Union Protests Indian Government's Crimes
Author:
Date: 1949-06-04
Page: 3

　　Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, 3rd. Prague Dispatch: The Czechoslovakian National Trade Union, representing its three million members, sent a telegram to the United Nations on May 30th, protesting the Indian government's imprisonment of hundreds of Indian progressive people.

### 捷全国职工会抗议印政府罪行

1949-06-04
第3版()
专栏：

　　捷全国职工会抗议印政府罪行
    【新华社北平三日电】布拉格讯：捷克斯洛伐克全国职工会顷代表其三百万会员，于五月三十日致电联合国。抗议印度政府囚禁数百印度进步人民。
