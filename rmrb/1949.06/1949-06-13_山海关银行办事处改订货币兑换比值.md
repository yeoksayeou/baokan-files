Title: Shanhaiguan Bank Office Revises Currency Exchange Rate
Author:
Date: 1949-06-13
Page: 2
　
　　Shanhaiguan Bank Office Revises Currency Exchange Rate
    [From Our Newspaper] The joint office of the Northeast Bank and the People's Bank of China in Shanhaiguan adjusted its exchange rates on the 11th, changing the rate to 1 yuan of Renminbi for 150 yuan of Northeast and Great Wall currency.

### 山海关银行办事处改订货币兑换比值

1949-06-13
第2版()
专栏：

　　山海关银行办事处改订货币兑换比值
    【本报讯】东北银行、中国人民银行山海关联合办事处，自十一日起，调整牌价，改为人民券壹元兑换东北、长城币壹百伍拾元。
