Title: Polish President Receives Qian Junrui
Author:
Date: 1949-06-05
Page: 3

　　Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, 4th: Polish News Agency, Warsaw Dispatch: Polish President Berrut received Saiyang, Secretary-General of the World Federation of Trade Unions, and Qian Junrui, Dean of Studies of China's North China University, on the 2nd.
