Title: Tonight's Program on Beiping Xinhua Broadcasting Station
Author:
Date: 1949-06-26
Page: 1

[This News] Tonight's program on Beiping Xinhua Broadcasting Station:
    From 7:50 PM to 8:10 PM, a lecture program by Mr. Liang Sicheng, Professor of the Department of Architecture at Tsinghua University, on "Urban Traffic Safety Issues." From 7:30 PM to 7:50 PM, "Worker's Time," broadcasting the current tasks of the North China Workers' Movement. From 10:20 PM to 10:50 PM, an arts and culture program featuring the North China Military Region Political Department's Anti-Enemy Drama Troupe performing "Poor People Turn Over," "Welcome Field Army," and "Singing of the Liberated Areas." From 9:15 PM to 9:30 PM, international current affairs, introducing the New Democratic Czechoslovakia.

### 北平新华广播电台今晚节目

1949-06-26
第1版()
专栏：

　　北平新华广播电台今晚节目
    【本报讯】北平新华广播电台今晚十九点五十分到二十点十分演讲节目，由清华大学建筑系教授梁思成先生讲“城市交通安全问题”。十九点三十分到十九点五十分职工时间，播送华北职工运动的当前任务。二十二点二十分到二十二点五十分文艺节目，由华北军区政治部抗敌剧社演唱穷人翻身谣、欢迎野战军、歌唱解放区。二十一点十五分到二十一点三十分国际时事，介绍新民主主义的捷克。
