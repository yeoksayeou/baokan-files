Title: Paul Robeson Sings for Polish Workers
Author:
Date: 1949-06-05
Page: 3

　　Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, 5th: Warsaw - Famous American black singer Paul Robeson arrived in Warsaw on the 30th of last month to attend the Polish Trade Union Congress. Robeson sang many famous black songs and famous songs from the Soviet Union, China, England, Italy, and Jewish traditions to a crowd of ten thousand people. He said that he was very happy to sing for the Polish workers who are building a better future.

　　Robeson is scheduled to fly to Moscow on the 4th and will rush back to New York to testify for the twelve American communist leaders who are being "tried".

### 黑人歌王罗伯逊为波兰工人歌唱

1949-06-05
第3版()
专栏：

　　黑人歌王罗伯逊为波兰工人歌唱
    【新华社北平五日电】华沙讯：著名美国黑人歌王罗伯逊于上月三十日到达华沙出席波兰职工会代表大会。罗伯逊曾在一万人的大会上歌唱了许多黑人名歌和苏、中、英、意及犹太的著名歌曲。他说他很高兴为正在建设更好的未来的波兰工人而歌唱。
    罗伯逊定于四日飞往莫斯科，并将赶回纽约，为十二个受“审判”的美共领袖作证。
