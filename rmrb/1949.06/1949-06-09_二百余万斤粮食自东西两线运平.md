Title: Over Two Million Catties of Grain Shipped from East and West Lines to Beiping
Author:
Date: 1949-06-09
Page: 2

　　Economic News

　　Over Two Million Catties of Grain Shipped from East and West Lines to Beiping
    [Report from this newspaper] The North China Trading Company shipped grain from the Northeast and Ping-Sui Railway lines to the Beiping Trading Company, and another 2.34 million catties arrived on the 7th and 8th. Among them, 1.5 million catties were shipped from Fengzhen, Chaigoubao and other places along the Ping-Sui Railway, including wheat, millet, sorghum, buckwheat, etc. 840,000 catties were shipped from the Northeast, all of which was millet. (Yu)

### 二百余万斤粮食自东西两线运平

1949-06-09
第2版()
专栏：经济新闻

　　二百余万斤粮食自东西两线运平
    【本报讯】华北贸易总公司由东北及平绥线运拨北平贸易公司之食粮，七、八两日又到达二百三十四万斤。其中有一百五十万斤是由平绥线丰镇、柴沟堡等地运来的，粮种包括有小麦、小米、红粮、荞麦等。有八十四万斤是由东北运来的，全部是谷子。（宇）
