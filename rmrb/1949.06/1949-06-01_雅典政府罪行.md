Title: Crimes of the Athens Government
Author:
Date: 1949-06-01
Page: 3
　　
    Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, 29th: The Free Greece News Agency reports: The terrible atrocities committed by the Greek Royalist fascists against the Macedonians continue. In the past few days alone, 7,000 Macedonians have suffered the bloody terror and persecution of the Royalist fascists. At the command of American Ambassador Grady and Military Mission Chief Follet, the Royalist fascists are imprisoning, torturing, and murdering women, children, and the elderly.

### 雅典政府罪行

1949-06-01
第3版()
专栏：

　　雅典政府罪行
    【新华社北平二十九日电】自由希腊通讯社讯：希腊保皇法西斯对马其顿人所施的可怕的暴行仍在继续中。过去数日内，就有七千名马其顿人遭受保皇法西斯的血腥恐怖与迫害。按照美国大使格拉第和军事使团团长佛力特的命令，保皇法西斯囚禁、拷打和杀害妇女、儿童和老年人。
