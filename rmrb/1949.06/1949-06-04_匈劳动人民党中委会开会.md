Title: Hungarian Working People's Party Central Committee Meeting

Author:

Date: 1949-06-04
Page: 3

　　Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, 3rd. Budapest dispatch: The Hungarian Working People's Party Central Committee held a plenary session in Budapest on May 31st. The meeting heard a report from Party General Secretary Rákosi on the results of the parliamentary elections and the political situation, and a report from Party Central Committee Secretary Knób about the party's constitution.

### 匈劳动人民党中委会开会

1949-06-04
第3版()
专栏：

　　匈劳动人民党中委会开会
    【新华社北平三日电】布达佩斯讯：匈牙利劳动人民党中央委员会全会于五月三十一日在此间开幕。会议听取党总书记拉科西关于议会选举结果及政治形势的报告，和党中央委员会书记克诺巴兹关于入党规章的报告。
