Title: Indonesian Democratic Front Issues Declaration, Strongly Condemns Hatta-Dutch Agreement, Calls on People to Persist in Struggle
Author:
Date: 1949-06-02
Page: 3
Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, June 1st: According to Dutch newspaper reports from TASS: Indonesian People's Guerrilla forces, currently persisting in the anti-imperialist struggle, recently distributed leaflets in the Yogyakarta and Solo areas of Central Java, announcing that the Indonesian Democratic Front opposes the anti-people agreement signed by the Hatta group with the Netherlands in May. The declaration denounces the agreement as a transaction between reactionaries. The Democratic Front urges the Indonesian people to resolutely carry out the struggle to win complete independence and freedom for Indonesia.

### 印尼民主阵线发表宣言　严斥哈达荷帝协议号召人民坚持斗争

1949-06-02
第3版()
专栏：

　　印尼民主阵线发表宣言
  　严斥哈达荷帝协议号召人民坚持斗争
    【新华社北平一日电】据塔斯社引荷兰报纸的消息：正在坚持反帝斗争的印尼人民游击队，最近在中爪哇日惹和梭罗地区散发传单，宣布印尼民主阵线反对哈达集团于五月间与荷兰签订的反人民协议。宣言斥责该协议为反动派之间的交易。民主阵线促印尼人民坚决进行争取印尼完全独立自由的斗争。
