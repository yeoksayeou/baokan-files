Title: Preparations for the Shanghai Committee of the Greater Shanghai Youth League Under Way
Author:
Date: 1949-06-09
Page: 1

　　Xinhua News Agency, Shanghai, 8th. The Central Committee of the Chinese New Democratic Youth League, in order to rapidly launch league-building work in Shanghai, has decided to establish the Shanghai Working Committee of the Chinese New Democratic Youth League. It has also appointed Li Chang, Dai Baitao, Zhang Ben, Fan Xiaofeng, and thirteen others as members, with Li Chang and Zhang Ben serving as the secretary and deputy secretary, respectively. The committee began operations on June 5th.

### 大上海青年团沪工委会办公

1949-06-09
第1版()
专栏：

　　大上海青年团沪工委会办公
    【新华社上海八日电】中国新民主主义青年团中央委员会为了在上海迅速展开建团工作，决定成立中国新民主主义青年团上海工作委员会；并指定李昌、戴白韬、张本、范小凤等十三人为委员，以李昌、张本分任正副书记。该会已于六月五日开始办公。
