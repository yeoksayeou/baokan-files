Title: Yesterday, Dragon Boat Festival, Markets Closed as Usual

Author:

Date: 1949-06-02
Page: 2

　　Yesterday, Dragon Boat Festival
  　Markets Closed as Usual
    [This newspaper dispatch] Yesterday (the 1st) was the Dragon Boat Festival according to the lunar calendar. Most markets in this city, including those for grain, oil, wine, and cotton cloth, were closed for the day as per usual practice. Trading will resume as normal today (the 2nd). (Yu)

### 昨端阳节　市场循旧例休市

1949-06-02
第2版()
专栏：

　　昨端阳节
  　市场循旧例休市
    【本报讯】昨日（一日）为农历端阳节，本市粮食、油酒、棉布等市场大多循旧例休市一日，今日（二日）照常交易。（宇）
