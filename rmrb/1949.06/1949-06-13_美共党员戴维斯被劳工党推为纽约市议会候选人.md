Title: CPUSA Member Davis Nominated by Labor Party as Candidate for New York City Council
Author:
Date: 1949-06-13
Page: 3

Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, 10th: TASS, New York - Labor Party Chairman Marcantonio announced on the 9th that Communist Davis and Labor Party member Connolly would be the Labor Party's candidates in the New York City Council elections. Marcantonio said that in nominating Communist Davis as a candidate, the Labor Party urged voters to fight for the full political rights of the Communist Party and all minority parties. The Worker Daily declared that the re-nomination of Communist Davis, along with Connolly, as Labor Party candidates is a matter of national significance. Davis is a black leader. Throughout the United States, every black person knows that he is a man who fights to abolish the hateful racial segregation system.

### 美共党员戴维斯被劳工党推为纽约市议会候选人

1949-06-13
第3版()
专栏：

　　美共党员戴维斯被劳工党推为纽约市议会候选人
    【新华社北平十日电】塔斯社纽约讯：美国劳工党主席马肯托尼奥九日宣布：共产党员戴维斯和劳工党员康诺利为劳工党在纽约市议会选举中的候选人。马肯托尼奥说：劳工党在推定共产党员戴维斯为候选人时，敦促选民为共产党和一切少数党派的充分政治权利而斗争。工人日报宣称：共产党员戴维斯与康诺利一起再次被推定为劳工党的候选人，是一件含有全国意义的事情。戴维斯是黑人领袖。在美国全国，每一个黑人都知道他是为取消可恨的种族隔离制而斗争的一个人。
