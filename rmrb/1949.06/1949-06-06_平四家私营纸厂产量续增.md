Title: Production Continues to Increase at Four Private Paper Mills in Ping

Author:
Date: 1949-06-06
Page: 2

　　Production Continues to Increase at Four Private Paper Mills in Ping
　　[This Newspaper's Dispatch] The private Yongtai, Yihe, Chuqi, and Beiping paper mills, and the joint public-private Daxing Paper Mill in Ping City, saw continued increases in production in May. Total production of printing paper reached 9,057 reams, an increase of 311 reams compared to April. The Yongtai factory is currently adding steaming equipment to prepare for using raw materials for papermaking, to solve the difficulty of a lack of waste paper.
    　　　（Ming）

### 平四家私营纸厂产量续增

1949-06-06
第2版()
专栏：

　　平四家私营纸厂产量续增
    【本报讯】平市私营永泰、义和、初起，北平四家造纸厂及公私合营的大兴造纸厂，五月份产量续见增加，总计生产片艳纸九千零五十七令，较四月份增加三百十一令。永泰厂现正添置蒸球设备，准备用生料造纸，解决废纸缺乏的困难。
    　　　（鸣）
