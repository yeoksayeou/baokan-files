Title: Over 600 Outstanding Youths at China University Hold Separate Admission Ceremonies; Central Committee of the Youth League Sends Congratulatory Letter
Author:
Date: 1949-06-16
Page: 2

　　Over 600 Outstanding Youths at China University
  　Hold Separate Admission Ceremonies
  　Central Committee of the Youth League Sends Congratulatory Letter
    [Our Newspaper's Dispatch] After more than a month of political study and ideological remolding, the political awareness of all students at North China University has generally improved, and many erroneous ideas and old burdens have been cleared away. They have requested to join the organization of the Chinese New Democratic Youth League. More than 40 percent of the 11,266 young students in the university have applied to join the League. The China University League Committee has officially approved the first batch of more than 660 outstanding youths to join the League. Admission ceremonies were held separately in each department. At 5:00 PM the day before yesterday (the 14th), a portion of them held a grand admission ceremony in the auditorium of the 5th and 6th District Teams of the First Branch of China University; Comrade Yang Shu, representative of the Central Committee of the Youth League, and Comrade Cheng Fangwu, Vice President and Secretary of the China University Party Committee, attended the meeting and delivered speeches.
    [Our Newspaper's Dispatch] The Central Committee of the Chinese New Democratic Youth League sent a letter on the 14th to congratulate the new League members of China University. The letter stated: "Congratulations to the new League members of your school on joining the League. It is hoped that you will strive to study Marxism-Leninism and Mao Zedong Thought, and become models in study. Use your exemplary role in work and study to gain the trust of your classmates, so that our League truly becomes an organization that unites the masses and is supported by the masses."

### 华大六百余优秀青年　分别举行入团仪式　青年团中委会致函祝贺

1949-06-16
第2版()
专栏：

　　华大六百余优秀青年
  　分别举行入团仪式
  　青年团中委会致函祝贺
    【本报讯】华北大学全校同学经过一个多月的政治学习、思想改造，政治觉悟程度普遍提高，清除了许多错误思想、陈旧包袱，纷纷要求参加中国新民主主义青年团的组织。在全校一万一千二百六十六个青年同学中有百分之四十以上的人申请入团。华大校团委已正式批准六百六十余位优秀青年首批入团。分别在各部举行入团仪式。前天（十四日）下午五时有一部分在华大一部五六区队大礼堂举行隆重的入团仪式；团中央代表杨述同志及华大党委书记成仿吾副校长均莅会讲话。
    【本报讯】中国新民主主义青年团中央委员会十四日致函祝贺华大新团员入团，函称：祝贺你校新团员入团，希望你们努力学习马列主义、毛泽东思想，并成为学习中的模范。以自己在工作和学习中的模范作用，取得同学的信任，使我们的团真正成为团结群众、为群众所拥护的组织。
