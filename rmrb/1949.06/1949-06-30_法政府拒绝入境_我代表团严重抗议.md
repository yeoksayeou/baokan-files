Title: French Government Refuses Entry; Our Delegation Lodges Strong Protest
Author:
Date: 1949-06-30
Page: 3
Xinhua News Agency, Peiping, June 29th: The Polish News Agency reports: Forty delegates representing three million textile and garment workers from eleven countries attended the recent founding congress of the International Textile and Garment Workers' Department held in Lyon, France. The congress decided to establish its permanent headquarters in Warsaw and elected Delisa Nosi, president of the Italian Textile Workers' Union, as chairman of the Executive Committee, Mravieva, chairman of the Soviet Textile Workers' Union, and Auber, general secretary of the French Textile Workers' Union, as vice-chairmen, and Borski, vice-chairman of the Central Council of Polish Trade Unions, as secretary. The other eleven executive committee members include representatives from Romania, the Netherlands, Germany, China, India, Africa, and South America. The congress also adopted an open letter to textile workers and the regulations of the department.

Another news item: The Chinese delegation attending the congress was unable to participate due to the French government's unreasonable refusal of entry. The Chinese delegation has lodged a strong protest with the French government.

### 法政府拒绝入境　我代表团严重抗议

1949-06-30
第3版()
专栏：

　　法政府拒绝入境
  　我代表团严重抗议
    【新华社北平二十九日电】波兰通讯社报道：来自十一个国家、代表三百万纺织与缝纫工人的四十个代表，参加了最近在法国里昂召开的国际纺织与缝纫业职工部的成立大会。大会决定以华沙为永久会址，并选举意大利纺织工人工会主席德里沙·诺斯任执行委员会主席，苏联纺织工人工会主席姆拉维也瓦及法国纺织工人工会总书记奥伯任副主席，波兰职工会中央理事会副主席布尔斯基任书记，其他十一个执行委员包括罗马尼亚、荷兰、德国、中国、印度、非洲与南美之代表。大会又通过了告纺织业职工书与该职工部的章程。
    又息：出席大会的中国代表团，因法政府无理拒绝入境，未能与会。中国代表团已向法政府提出严重抗议。
