Title: Don't Be Alarmed by Artillery Testing

Author:

Date: 1949-06-14
Page: 1

Don't Be Alarmed by Artillery Testing

The ordnance factory under the Enterprise Department of the North China People's Government will be testing artillery near Lugou Bridge for the entire day today (14th). Residents of Beijing and surrounding areas are requested not to be alarmed by the sound.

　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　Enterprise Department Office

### 试炮勿惊

1949-06-14
第1版()
专栏：

　　试炮勿惊
    华北人民政府企业部所属兵工厂今（十四）日在芦沟桥附近试炮一整天，望我平市及其附近地区居民，闻声勿惊。
    　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　企业部办公室
