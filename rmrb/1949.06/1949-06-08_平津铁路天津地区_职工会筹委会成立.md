Title: Tianjin Area Preparatory Committee of the Pingjin Railway Trade Union Established
Author:
Date: 1949-06-08
Page: 2

Xinhua News Agency, Tianjin, 7th. The Tianjin area of the Pingjin Railway held a representative conference of employees in Tianjin at the end of May. At the meeting, the Preparatory Committee of the Pingjin Railway Tianjin Area Trade Union was established, and Wang Xiaoci, Zheng Bingwen, and 23 others were elected as preparatory committee members. The Tianjin area of the Pingjin Railway includes all factories, sections, stations, and offices from Beicang in the west to Jinxi River and Tanggu in the east, and Duliu in the south, as well as various directly affiliated units of the Ministry of Railways in Tianjin, with a total of more than 9,800 employees.

### 平津铁路天津地区　职工会筹委会成立

1949-06-08
第2版()
专栏：

　　平津铁路天津地区　职工会筹委会成立
    【新华社天津七日电】平津铁路天津地区职工代表大会于五月下旬在津市召开。会上成立了平津铁路天津地区职工会筹委会，选出王孝慈、郑秉文等二十五人为筹备委员。平津铁路天津地区包括西起北仓，东至金溪河、塘沽，南到独流的各厂、段、站、所，及铁道部在津各直属单位，职工共九千八百余人。
