Title: Hua University Prepares to Form Faculty and Staff Union
Author:
Date: 1949-06-17
Page: 2

(Ping City Dispatch) To strengthen unity, improve welfare, and better organize cultural and recreational activities, the more than 1,800 faculty and staff members (including all service personnel, cooks, and workers) of North China University have decided to form a Faculty and Staff Union. An eight-member preparatory committee has been formed to carry out preparatory work, and the Union will be formally established in the shortest possible time. (Hua University Secretary Office)

### 华大筹组教职联

1949-06-17
第2版()
专栏：

　　华大筹组教职联
    【平市讯】华北大学教员、职员（包括所有服务员、炊事员、工人等）共一千八百余人，为了加强团结，增进福利，更好的组织文化娱乐等，决定筹组教职员联合会，并推出教职员八人组成筹备委员会，进行筹备工作，于最短时间即可正式成立。　（华大秘书室）
