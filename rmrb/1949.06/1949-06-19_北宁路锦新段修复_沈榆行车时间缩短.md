Title: Jin-Xin Section of Beining Railway Repaired: Shenyang-Yushu Travel Time Reduced
Author:
Date: 1949-06-19
Page: 1

　　Jin-Xin Section of Beining Railway Repaired
  　Shenyang-Yushu Travel Time Reduced
    [Xinhua News Agency, Shenyang, 18th] The Jinzhou to Xinmin section of the Beining Railway has been repaired. Now, trains can travel directly from Shenyang to Shanhaiguan, shortening the journey by three hours compared to the previous route via Xinlitun and Yixian. In addition, the Dahushan to Liaoyuan line, specifically the Dahushan to Xinlitun section, has also been repaired and opened to traffic.

### 北宁路锦新段修复　沈榆行车时间缩短

1949-06-19
第1版()
专栏：

　　北宁路锦新段修复
  　沈榆行车时间缩短
    【新华社沈阳十八日电】北宁路锦州到新民段已经修复，现列车自沈阳直达山海关，较过去绕行新立屯、义县，可缩短三小时。另，大虎山至辽源线大虎山到新立屯段亦已修复通车。
