Title: Participating Nations of Aggression Pact Conduct Important Negotiations; US Imperialism Seeks to Control All Military Power
Author:
Date: 1949-06-19
Page: 3

　　Participating Nations of Aggression Pact
  　Conduct Important Negotiations
  　US Imperialism Seeks to Control All Military Power
    Xinhua News Agency, Peiping, 18th: TASS, Brussels, 17th: The Front newspaper reports: Important negotiations are currently being held among the signatory nations of the aggressive North Atlantic Pact. The American representative in the so-called North Atlantic Pact Defense Committee has submitted a note insisting on the necessity of concentrating the overall command of all military strategic actions of all North Atlantic nations in Washington.

### 参加侵略公约各国　进行重要商谈　美帝要总揽各国军权

1949-06-19
第3版()
专栏：

　　参加侵略公约各国
  　进行重要商谈
  　美帝要总揽各国军权
    【新华社北平十八日电】塔斯社布鲁塞尔十七日讯：阵线报报道：侵略性的北大西洋公约诸签字国之间，刻正举行重要商谈。所谓北大西洋公约防御委员会中的美国代表，已经提出照会，有坚持必要把所有北大西洋国家的一切军事战略行动的总指挥权集中在华盛顿。
