Title: Tangshan Delegates Head to Beiping to Attend National Railway Workers' Conference
Author:
Date: 1949-06-27
Page: 1

　　To Attend the National Railway Workers' Conference
  　Tangshan Delegates Head to Beiping for the First Time
    [Tangshan Electric Dispatch] Tangshan Railway Factory and Tangshan area railway stations and sections' worker representatives, including Li Yongshun and two others who will participate in the National Railway Workers' Conference, departed for Beiping on the 24th.

### 出席全国铁路职工会议　唐山代表首途来平

1949-06-27
第1版()
专栏：

　　出席全国铁路职工会议
  　唐山代表首途来平
    【唐山电】唐山铁路工厂及唐山地区铁路各站段参加全国铁路职工会议的职工代表李永顺等三人已于二十四日首途赴平。
