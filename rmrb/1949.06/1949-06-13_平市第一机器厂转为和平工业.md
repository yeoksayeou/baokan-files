Title: The First Machine Factory in Beiping Transforms to Peaceful Industry
Author:
Date: 1949-06-13
Page: 2

Xinhua News Agency, Beiping, June 11th—The city's First Machine Factory, which manufactured munitions before liberation, began converting to peaceful industry in June, manufacturing various machines for construction industries. The factory's June production plan has been set, including contracting for five automatic coal feeders ordered by the Tianjin China Textile Company Machinery Factory, repairing twenty existing old machines, manufacturing parts such as drills, and completing the unfinished work from April and May. The riveting workshop is currently being renovated to expand the scope of production.

### 平市第一机器厂转为和平工业

1949-06-13
第2版()
专栏：

　　平市第一机器厂转为和平工业
    【新华社北平十一日电】解放前制造军火的本市第一机器厂，六月份开始转为和平工业，制造各种建设工业的机器。该厂六月份的生产计划已订出，计承包天津中纺公司机器厂所定的五部自动给煤机，修理原有旧机器二十部，制造钻子等零件，并完成四、五月份未完成的工作。现厂内正修盖铆工房，准备扩大生产范围。
