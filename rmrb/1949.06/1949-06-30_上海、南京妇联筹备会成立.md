Title: Shanghai and Nanjing Women's Federation Preparatory Committees Established
Author:
Date: 1949-06-30
Page: 3

Xinhua News Agency, Shanghai, June 29th: Shanghai's women representatives from all walks of life gathered on the 26th to celebrate Shanghai's liberation and to establish the Shanghai Democratic Women's Federation Preparatory Committee. More than 2,300 representatives attended, including female workers, female students, professional women, and housewives. Mayor Chen Yi, Vice-Chairman of the All-China Democratic Women's Federation Deng Yingchao, and Xu Guangping all attended the meeting. In his speech, Mayor Chen Yi pointed out that women's issues are part of the entire people's revolutionary movement and also part of the construction of the New China. Building a New China cannot be done in a day or two, and everyone must persistently and actively overcome difficulties and do it step by step. Deng Yingchao said that among the 134 preparatory committee members of the new CPPCC, there are 18 women representatives, which shows that the political status of Chinese women has been unprecedentedly improved. This is the result of the struggle of Chinese women together with the people of the whole country. She called on Shanghai women to form a strong organization and work with people from all walks of life in Shanghai to eliminate the remaining reactionary forces in Shanghai and restore production. Xu Guangping called on Shanghai women to participate in production and strengthen their studies in her speech. Female worker representative Tang Guifen called on female workers in her speech to strengthen unity and study, and to step up production to accelerate the construction of the New China. The meeting unanimously passed a resolution to form the Shanghai Democratic Women's Federation Preparatory Committee, composed of 87 people including Shi Liang, Zhang Yun, Tang Guifen, and Ge Yang, and sent telegrams of respect to Chairman Mao, Commander-in-Chief Zhu, the CPPCC Preparatory Committee, and the All-China Democratic Women's Federation.
	Also, Nanjing News: The Nanjing Democratic Women's Federation Preparatory Committee was established on the 15th. Chen Xiuliang, Sha Yiyin, and other 17 people are members of the preparatory committee.

### 上海、南京妇联筹备会成立

1949-06-30
第3版()
专栏：

　　上海、南京　妇联筹备会成立
    【新华社上海二十九日电】上海市各界妇女代表于二十六日集会，庆祝上海解放，并成立上海民主妇女联合会筹备会。到会女工、女学生、职业妇女、家庭妇女等代表二千三百余人。陈毅市长、中华全国民主妇女联合会副主席邓颖超、许广平均到会参加。陈市长在讲话中指出：妇女问题是整个人民革命运动的一部分，也是新中国建设的一部分。建设新中国不是一两天能做好的，大家要坚韧地、积极地克服困难，一步步做去。邓颖超说，新政协一百三十四个筹备委员中有妇女代表十八人，这说明中国妇女的政治地位已空前提高。这是中国妇女与全国人民在一起斗争的结果。她号召上海妇女组成强大的团体，与上海各界人民一起，为肃清上海残余反动势力及恢复生产而努力。许广平在讲话中号召上海妇女参加生产，加强学习。女工代表汤桂芬在讲话中号召女工们除加强团结和学习外，更要加紧生产，加速新中国的建设。会议一致通过由史良、章蕴、汤桂芬、戈扬等八十七人组成上海民主妇女联合会筹备会，并致电毛主席、朱总司令及新政协筹备会、中华全国民主妇女联合会致敬。
    又南京讯：南京市民主妇女联合会筹委会已于十五日成立。由陈修良、沙轶因等十七人为筹备委员。
