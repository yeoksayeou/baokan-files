Title: Zhengzhou Railway Workers Preparatory Committee Responds to Restoration of the All-China Railway Federation
Author:
Date: 1949-06-15
Page: 2

　　Zhengzhou Railway Workers Preparatory Committee Responds to Restoration of the All-China Railway Federation
　　[This newspaper dispatch] The Northeast Railway Workers' General Union's proposal to convene a national railway workers' representative meeting to prepare for the restoration of the All-China Railway Federation has been responded to by the Zhengzhou Railway Federation of Trade Unions Preparatory Committee in a telegram on June 13th. The telegram stated that more than 25,000 workers in Zhengzhou unanimously agreed to this proposal with extreme excitement and will send representatives to participate on time.

### 郑州铁路职工筹委响应恢复全国铁总

1949-06-15
第2版()
专栏：

　　郑州铁路职工筹委响应恢复全国铁总
    【本报讯】东北铁路职工总会关于召开全国铁路职工代表会，筹备恢复全国铁路总工会的提议，郑州铁路总工会筹委会六月十三日来电响应，电文称，郑州二万五千余职工以极端兴奋的心情一致同意这一提议，并将选派代表准时参加。
