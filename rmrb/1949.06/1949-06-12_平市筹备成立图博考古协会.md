Title: Preparations underway in Beiping to establish the Library, Museum, and Archaeology Association
Author: Baisheng
Date: 1949-06-12
Page: 2

　　Beiping is Preparing to Establish the Library, Museum, and Archaeology Association
    [This Newspaper's Dispatch] Experts in library science, museum science, and archaeology in Beiping held a symposium at the Beiping Library yesterday (the 11th) at 2:00 PM to discuss preparations for establishing the "Association of Library, Museum, and Archaeological Workers." More than ten people attended, including Zheng Zhenduo and Pei Wenzhong. Ma Wei and five others were selected to be responsible for drafting the charter and other matters. It was decided to convene a preparatory meeting on the 23rd or 24th of this month.
　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　(Baisheng)

### 平市筹备成立图博考古协会
柏生
1949-06-12
第2版()
专栏：

　　平市筹备成立图博考古协会
    【本报讯】北平市图书馆学、博物馆学及考古学专家于昨（十一）日下午二时在北平图书馆举行座谈会商讨筹备成立“图博考古工作者协会”。出席者有郑振铎、裴文中等十余人，推出马卫等六人负责起草章程等事宜，决定在本月二十三日或二十四日召开筹备大会。
    　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　（柏生）
