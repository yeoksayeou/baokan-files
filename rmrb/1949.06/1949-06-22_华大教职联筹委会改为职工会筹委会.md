Title: Preparatory Committee of the Huabei University Faculty and Staff Union Changed to Preparatory Committee of the Trade Union
Author: Yu Piao
Date: 1949-06-22
Page: 2

    Huabei University's Preparatory Committee of the Faculty and Staff Union Changed to the Preparatory Committee of the Trade Union
    [Ping City News] Since the establishment of the Preparatory Committee of the Faculty and Staff Union at Huabei University, everyone has been enthusiastic and actively deliberating. All 150+ faculty and staff members of the Second Division proposed to the Preparatory Committee that the Huabei University Faculty and Staff Union be changed to the Huabei University Trade Union. After discussion, the Preparatory Committee accepted this proposal and has now changed the Preparatory Committee of the Faculty and Staff Union to the Preparatory Committee of the Trade Union.
        (Yu Piao)

### 华大教职联筹委会改为职工会筹委会
余飘
1949-06-22
第2版()
专栏：

    华大教职联筹委会改为职工会筹委会
    【平市讯】华北大学自筹组教职员联合会以来，大家情绪热烈，纷纷酝酿，第二部全体教职员一百五十余人向筹委会提议，请把华北大学教职员联合会改为华北大学职工会。筹委会经过讨论，接受了此项提议，现已将教职员联合会筹委会改为职工会筹委会。        （余飘）
