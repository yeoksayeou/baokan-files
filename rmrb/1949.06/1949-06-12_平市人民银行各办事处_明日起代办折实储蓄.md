Title: Ping City People's Bank Branches to Handle Savings Deposits Indexed to Commodity Units Starting Tomorrow
Author:
Date: 1949-06-12
Page: 2
　　Ping City People's Bank Branches

　　To Handle Savings Deposits Indexed to Commodity Units Starting Tomorrow
　　[This newspaper reports] The Savings Department of the People's Bank of Beiping Branch, in order to develop savings business and facilitate employees and citizens in various districts of the city to handle savings deposits indexed to commodity units conveniently near their locations, has specially entrusted the four branches of the Beiping Branch in Tongxian (inside Tongxian City), Dongcheng (on Wangfujing Street), Xicheng (on Xidan North Street), and Nancheng (outside Chongwenmen Street), as well as the four sub-branches of Dongdan, Dongsi, Gulou Street, and Xidan, to begin handling the business of savings deposits indexed to commodity units on behalf of the department starting tomorrow (the 13th).
　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　(Yu)

### 平市人民银行各办事处　明日起代办折实储蓄

1949-06-12
第2版()
专栏：

　　平市人民银行各办事处
  　明日起代办折实储蓄
    【本报讯】人民银行北平分行储蓄部，为开展储蓄业务和便利市内各区职工、市民就近办理折实储蓄，特委托北平分行通县（在通县城内）、东城（在王府井大街）、西城（在西单北大街）、南城（在崇文门外大街）四个办事处暨东单、东四、鼓楼大街、西单等四个分理处于明日（十三日）起开始代理该部办理折实储蓄业务。
   　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　（宇）
