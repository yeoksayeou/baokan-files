　　Shanghai Cultural Briefs

　　(1) The China Children's Literature Writers Association held a work discussion meeting on the 12th. Li Chucai, Shi Yanbing, Shen Baiying, Jin Jin and other 54 people attended. Dai Baitao, Director of the Education Department of the Shanghai Municipal Government, attended and delivered a speech. He pointed out that children's publications should focus on: cultivating children's love for the people and serving the people; love for the country, resolutely opposing imperialist aggression against China and traitors; love for labor, opposing unearned income and other correct concepts.

　　(2) The "Era" magazine, which has been published for nine years, has now been changed to a semi-monthly publication, specializing in publishing the works of Marx, Engels, Lenin, and Stalin, introducing the experience of socialist construction in the Soviet Union, and reporting on the life of the Soviet people. The Liberation issue of the magazine (ie, Volume 10 of the 9th year) was published on the 12th. The Era Publishing House, which publishes the magazine, has also been reorganized to be operated by Chinese employees of the publishing house, and will continue to translate and compile literary and theoretical books in large quantities. (Xinhua News Agency, Shanghai, 15th)

### 上海文化短讯

1949-06-17
第4版()
专栏：

　　上海文化短讯
    （一）中国儿童读物作者协会十二日举行工作讨论会，到会李楚才、施雁冰、沈百英、金近等五十四人。由上海市政府教育处戴白韬处长出席讲话，他指出今后儿童读物应当注重：培养儿童爱人民，为人民大众服务；爱国家，坚决反对帝国主义侵略中国和卖国贼；爱劳动，反对不劳而获等正确的观念。
    （二）已出版九年的“时代”杂志，现已改成半月刊，专门刊载马、恩、列、斯的论著，介绍苏联社会主义建设的经验，报道苏联人民的生活情况。该刊解放号（即第九年第十卷）已于十二日出版。刊印该杂志的时代出版社，也已改由该社中国职员经营，继续大批编译文艺理论书籍。　（新华社上海十五日电）
