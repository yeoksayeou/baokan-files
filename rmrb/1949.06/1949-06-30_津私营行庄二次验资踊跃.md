Title: Tianjin Private Banks and Money Shops Actively Participate in Second Capital Verification

Author: 
Date: 1949-06-30
Page: 2

　　Tianjin Private Banks and Money Shops Actively Participate in Second Capital Verification
    [Tianjin News] The second phase of private banks and money shops in Tianjin registering and verifying their capital with the People's Bank has been more enthusiastic than the first phase. The deadline for the second phase of registration and verification is the end of this month. As of the 28th, 81 private banks and money shops in the city have registered with the People's Bank, including 17 banks such as Shanghai Bank and Dalu Bank, and 64 money shops such as Yuanji, Housheng, and Juhuachang. Only a small number have not yet registered.

### 津私营行庄二次验资踊跃

1949-06-30
第2版()
专栏：

　　津私营行庄二次验资踊跃
    【天津讯】津市私营行庄第二期向人民银行登记验资情况，较第一期为踊跃。第二期登记验资应于本月底截止，截至二十八日，本市私营行庄向人民银行登记者已有八十一家，其中银行有上海、大陆等十七家；钱庄有元吉、厚生、聚华昌等六十四家。未登记者为数寥寥。
