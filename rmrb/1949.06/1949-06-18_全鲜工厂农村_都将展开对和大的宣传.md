Title: Extensive Propaganda on the World Peace Congress to be Launched in Factories and Rural Areas Throughout Korea

Author:
Date: 1949-06-18
Page: 3

　　Factories and Rural Areas Throughout Korea
  　Will Launch Extensive Propaganda on the World Peace Congress
    [Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, 16th] Pyongyang dispatch: The third meeting of the Korean National Peace Committee was recently held here. At the meeting, the Korean representative Park Chung-hee, who participated in the World Peace Congress, gave a report. The meeting approved the launch of extensive propaganda throughout Korea regarding the work and decisions of the World Peace Congress. The plan stipulates that lectures on the situation of the World Peace Congress will be held in major industrial sectors, construction sites, rural areas, educational departments and other institutions in Pyongyang in the near future.

### 全鲜工厂农村　都将展开对和大的宣传

1949-06-18
第3版()
专栏：

　　全鲜工厂农村
  　都将展开对和大的宣传
    【新华社北平十六日电】平壤讯：全朝鲜拥护和平委员会第三次会议，顷于此间举行。会议首由参加世界拥护和平大会的朝鲜代表朴廷懿作报告。会议批准在全朝鲜展开对世界拥护和平大会的工作和决定的广泛宣传。该计划规定最近将在平壤的重要企业部门、建筑场所、农村、教育部门和其他机关举行报告世界拥护和平大会情况的讲演会。
