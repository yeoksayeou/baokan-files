Title: Bank of China in Beiping Adjusts Gold and Silver Prices Yesterday
Author:
Date: 1949-06-23
Page: 2

    The Bank of China in Beiping adjusted its purchasing prices for gold, silver, and silver dollars yesterday (22nd) as follows: Gold at 50,000 yuan per tael, silver at 450 yuan per tael, silver dollars (each) - large head at 500 yuan, small head at 450 yuan, and miscellaneous foreign coins at 400 yuan. (Yu)

### 平中国银行昨调整金银牌价

1949-06-23
第2版()
专栏：

    平中国银行昨调整金银牌价
    【本报讯】平中国银行收兑金、银、银元牌价，昨日（二十二日）起调整为：黄金每两五万元，白银每两四百五十元，银元（枚）大头五百元，小头四百五十元，杂洋四百元。            （宇）
