Title: Huainan Coal Mine Produced 90,000 Tons of Coal in May
Author:
Date: 1949-06-15
Page: 3

　　Huainan Coal Mine Produced 90,000 Tons of Coal in May
    [Xinhua News Agency, Shanghai, Fourteenth] The Huainan Coal Mine has completed its May coal production plan of 90,000 tons, exceeding the plan by 7,400 tons. The mine's June production plan has been determined by the mine's production management committee to increase to 120,000 tons.

### 淮南煤矿五月份产煤九万吨

1949-06-15
第3版()
专栏：

　　淮南煤矿五月份产煤九万吨
    【新华社上海十四日电】淮南煤矿五月份产煤九万吨的计划已完成，并超过计划七千四百吨。六月份该矿生产计划已经该矿生产管理委员会确定增加为十二万吨。
