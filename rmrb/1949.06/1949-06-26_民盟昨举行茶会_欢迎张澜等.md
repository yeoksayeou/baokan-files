Title: China Democratic League Holds Tea Party to Welcome Zhang Lan and Others
Author:
Date: 1949-06-26
Page: 1

　　China Democratic League Holds Tea Party
  　Welcoming Zhang Lan and Others
    [Ping City News] Yesterday (the 25th) at 7:00 PM, the Provisional Working Committee of the Headquarters of the China Democratic League, the Beijing Branch of the China Democratic League, and the Guangming Daily jointly held a tea party at the Beijing Hotel to welcome Zhang Lan, Luo Longji, Shi Liang, and others who arrived in Ping from Shanghai the day before. Shen Junru, Zhang Bojun, Wu Han, Zhang Lan, Luo Longji, Shi Liang, and Wang Baozhen all spoke at the meeting.

### 民盟昨举行茶会　欢迎张澜等

1949-06-26
第1版()
专栏：

　　民盟昨举行茶会
  　欢迎张澜等
    【平市讯】昨日（二十五日）下午七时，中国民主同盟总部临时工作委员会、民盟北平市支部及光明日报社在北京饭店联合举行茶会，欢迎前日由沪抵平的张澜、罗隆基、史良等。会上沈钧儒、章伯钧、吴晗、张澜、罗隆基、史良、王葆真均曾讲话。
