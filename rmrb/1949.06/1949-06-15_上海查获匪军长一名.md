Title: Shanghai Arrests a Bandit Army Commander

Author:

Date: 1949-06-15
Page: 1

　　Xinhua News Agency, Shanghai, 14th. Liu Bingzhe, commander of the Kuomintang bandit army's 28th Army, hid in Shanghai after the bandit army's defeat in Shanghai. On the 10th, he attempted to escape to Taiwan aboard the Shengjing ship, but was apprehended by the Shanghai People's Municipal Government Public Security Bureau. The bandit 28th Army was completely annihilated in the great annihilation battle north of Guangde at the end of April. Bandit Liu himself escaped at that time and fled to Shanghai.

### 上海查获匪军长一名

1949-06-15
第1版()
专栏：

　　上海查获匪军长一名
    【新华社上海十四日电】国民党匪军二十八军军长刘秉哲，于上海匪军覆没后藏匿上海市内，十日企图搭盛京号轮船逃往台湾，当被上海人民市政府公安局查获。按匪二十八军系于四月底在广德以北地区大歼灭战中被全部歼灭，刘匪本人当时漏网逃至上海。
