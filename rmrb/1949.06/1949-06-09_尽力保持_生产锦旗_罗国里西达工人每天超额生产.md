Title: Strive to Maintain the "Production Banner": Romanian Resita Workers Exceed Production Quotas Daily
Author:
Date: 1949-06-09
Page: 3

　　Strive to Maintain the "Production Banner"
  　Romanian Resita Workers Exceed Production Quotas Daily
    [Xinhua News Agency, Beiping, 8th] Romanian News Agency dispatch: The workers of Resita, an industrial center in southwestern Romania, and winners of the "Production Banner," are now striving to maintain their banner, achieving huge over-production every day. For example, the stoker workers exceeded the May production plan by 4.7 percent, while improving the quality of castings. The rolling mill workers exceeded the production quota by 20.6 percent. Workers in the electric motor department accepted the challenge from the power plant workers' competition, guaranteeing a 20 percent increase in production. Workers at the Red Steel Plant guarantee to exceed the June production plan by 7 percent, increase labor productivity by 4 percent, and reduce fuel consumption. Workers at the Northern Tesh agricultural machinery and tractor manufacturing center have launched a socialist competition challenge to machinery centers across the country, while putting forward specific guarantees.

### 尽力保持“生产锦旗”　罗国里西达工人每天超额生产

1949-06-09
第3版()
专栏：

　　尽力保持“生产锦旗”
  　罗国里西达工人每天超额生产
    【新华社北平八日电】罗马尼亚通讯社讯：罗马尼亚西南部工业中心里西达的工人们，即“生产锦旗”的获得者，现在尽力保持他们的锦旗，每天都在获得巨大的超额生产。例如，司炉工人超过五月份生产计划百分之四点七，同时改进了铸锉的质量。辗金属工人超过生产定额百分之二十点六。电动机部门的工人接受动力厂工人的竞赛挑战，保证增加生产百分之二十。红色炼钢厂的工人保证超过六月生产计划百分之七，提高劳动生产率百分之四，并减少消耗燃料。农业机器与拖拉机制造中心北部特什的工人向全国各机器业中心提出了社会主义竞赛的挑战，同时提出了具体的保证。
