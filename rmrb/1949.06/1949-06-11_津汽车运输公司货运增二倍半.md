Title: Tianjin Automotive Transportation Company Increases Freight Volume by Two and a Half Times
Author:
Date: 1949-06-11
Page: 2

Tianjin Electric - Tianjin Automotive Transportation Company Achieves Significant Success in Business. The company averages 23 vehicles per day; the total monthly mileage reached over 95,000 kilometers, and the freight volume increased from over 40,000 ton-kilometers in April to over 148,900 ton-kilometers. At the same time, vehicle management has been strengthened, and a dedicated person responsibility system has been implemented, greatly reducing the number of vehicle damages. The company's vehicles frequently travel to Baoding, Shijiazhuang, Hengshui, Nangong, Jinan, Cangxian, and other places, with the main task of promoting the exchange of materials between urban and rural areas.

### 津汽车运输公司货运增二倍半

1949-06-11
第2版()
专栏：

　　津汽车运输公司
  　货运增二倍半
    【天津电】天津市汽车运输公司业务获得显著成绩。该公司平均每天出车二十三辆；全月共行车九万五千多公里，货运由四月份的四万多吨公里增加到十四万八千九百多吨公里。同时并加强了车辆管理，实行专人负责制，车辆损坏次数大减。该公司车辆经常来往保定、石家庄、衡水、南宫、济南、沧县等地，主要任务是促进城乡间的物资交流。
