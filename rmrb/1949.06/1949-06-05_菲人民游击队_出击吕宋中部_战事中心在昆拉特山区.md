Title: Philippine People's Guerrilla Force Launches Attacks in Central Luzon; Center of Fighting in the Kunlat Mountains
Author:
Date: 1949-06-05
Page: 3

　　Philippine People's Guerrilla Force
  　Launches Attacks in Central Luzon
  　Center of Fighting in the Kunlat Mountains
    [Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, 4th] According to a Reuters dispatch from Manila: In recent weeks, the Philippine People's Guerrilla Force has engaged in fierce fighting with the Philippine government army in Central Luzon. In order to contain the puppet government's army's offensive on the border between Nueva Ecija and Pangasinan provinces, the guerrillas launched attacks in the aforementioned two provinces and the provinces of Bulacan and Laguna at the end of May, with intense fighting. According to Philippine government sources, the current center of fighting is in the Kunlat Mountains in eastern Nueva Ecija province.

### 菲人民游击队　出击吕宋中部　战事中心在昆拉特山区

1949-06-05
第3版()
专栏：

　　菲人民游击队
  　出击吕宋中部
  　战事中心在昆拉特山区
    【新华社北平四日电】据路透社马尼拉讯：最近数周内，菲律宾人民游击队在吕宋中部对菲政府军展开激战。为了牵制傀儡政府军队对蕊瓦伊西哈与班邦加省边界的进攻，游击队曾于五月底在上述两省和布拉干、拉古拉等省出击，战斗甚烈。据菲政府方面消息，现战事中心在蕊瓦伊西哈省东部的昆拉特山区。
