　　North China Highway Bureau Transportation Company Handles Public and Private Freight Transportation
　　[Tianjin Electric] In order to facilitate the exchange of goods between urban and rural areas, the Transportation Company directly under the North China Highway Transportation Bureau has established the First Freight Transit Station to handle the storage, transit, and combined transportation of public and private goods. At present, in addition to the original Tianjin-Cangxian-Dexian line, Tianjin-Baoding-Shijiazhuang line, the company has also added lines from Tianjin via Hengshui to Ji-Fei-Nangong, from Tianjin to Tanggu, and branch lines from Cangxian to Dexian, Cangxian to Hengshui, and Cangxian to Jiucheng.

### 华北公路局运输公司经办公私货运

1949-06-02
第2版()
专栏：

　　华北公路局运输公司经办公私货运
    【天津电】华北公路运输总局直属运输公司为了便利城乡物资的交流，已经成立第一货物转运站，办理公私货物的保存转运联运等业务。目前该公司营业路线除了原有的天津、沧县、德县线，天津、保定、石家庄线以外，并且加开从天津经过衡水到冀肥南宫，从天津到塘沽等线，和沧县到德县、沧县到衡水、沧县到旧城等支线。
