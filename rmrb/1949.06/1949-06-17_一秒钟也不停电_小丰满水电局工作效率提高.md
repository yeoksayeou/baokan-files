Title: No Power Outage for Even a Second: Xiaofengman Hydroelectric Bureau Improves Work Efficiency
Author:
Date: 1949-06-17
Page: 3

　　No Power Outage for Even a Second
  　Xiaofengman Hydroelectric Bureau Improves Work Efficiency
    [Broadcast by Beiping Xinhua Broadcasting Station] Jilin, 16th: Since the Northeast Xiaofengman Hydroelectric Bureau launched a competition on April 2nd, the work efficiency of all departments has generally improved. At the beginning of the competition, the power plant put forward the slogan of ensuring no power outage for even a second, and no accidents have occurred up to the beginning of June. In terms of dam construction, both quantity and quality have improved. Before the competition, 580 people averaged 300 cubic meters of concrete per day. After the competition, the amount gradually increased. By May 7th, it had increased to 486 cubic meters, and by May 28th, 651 people poured 764.4 cubic meters of concrete.

### 一秒钟也不停电　小丰满水电局工作效率提高

1949-06-17
第3版()
专栏：

　　一秒钟也不停电
  　小丰满水电局工作效率提高
    【北平新华广播电台广播】吉林十六号消息：东北小丰满水电局，从四月二号展开竞赛以来，各部门工作效率普遍提高。发电厂在竞赛开始的时候，就提出了保证一秒钟不停电的口号，一直到六月初，没有发生过一次事故。筑堤方面，数量和质量都提高了。竞赛以前，五百八十人平均每天打混凝土三百立方米，竞赛以后逐步提高，到五月七号，就增加到四百八十六立方米，到二十八号，六百五十一人打了混凝土七百六十四·四立方米。
