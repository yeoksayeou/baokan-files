Title: Taiyuan and Shanghai Can Now Talk by Phone
Author:
Date: 1949-06-04
Page: 1

　　Taiyuan and Shanghai Can Now Talk by Phone
    [Taiyuan Dispatch] The Taiyuan Telegraph Bureau has recently connected long-distance telephone lines with Shanghai and will open telephone services shortly. Telegrams sent to Shijiazhuang have also been received by the bureau. Taiyuan can now communicate by telegram and telephone with major cities nationwide, including Hong Kong and the newly liberated Wuhan, Xi'an, and other places.

### 太原上海即可通话

1949-06-04
第1版()
专栏：

　　太原上海即可通话
    【太原电】太原电报局，顷已与上海长途电话接通，不日即开放通话。发往石家庄电报，该局亦已收受；太原与全国大都市通报、通话者，有香港及新近解放之武汉、西安等处。
