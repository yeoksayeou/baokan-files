Title: Remnants of Bandits by Poyang Lake Surrender to Us One After Another
Author:
Date: 1949-06-14
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Nanjing, 13th: Remnants of the bandits under Liu Ruming's 68th Army, who had fled to the vicinity of Poyang Lake, as well as local Kuomintang teams from various counties in northern Jiangxi, are surrendering to the local People's Liberation Army one after another. In the past month or so, over 800 enemy remnants have surrendered, turning in weapons including two mortars, twenty-two light and heavy machine guns, over 1,400 rifles, as well as radio stations, flamethrowers, etc.

### 鄱阳湖畔残匪　纷纷向我投诚

1949-06-14
第1版()
专栏：

　　鄱阳湖畔残匪
  　纷纷向我投诚
    【新华社南京分社十三日电】流窜至鄱阳湖畔的残匪刘汝明部六十八军及赣北各县国民党地方团队，纷纷向当地解放军投诚。月余以来投降的残敌已达八百余人，交出的武器有迫击炮二门，轻重机枪二十二挺，步枪千四百余支及电台、喷火器等。
