Title: Jiangxi's Jing'an and Xiushui Areas Annihilate 400 Enemies, Capture Bandit Security Commander and Column Commander Each

Author:
Date: 1949-06-29
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, Nanchang, 28th: A unit of the People's Liberation Army captured Xu Shunan, Commissioner of Jiujiang and concurrently Security Commander of the Kuomintang, along with 180 others in the battle in the Qiujiajie area of Jing'an, northwest of Nanchang, on the 23rd, seizing over 180 guns. When bandit Xu fled from Jiujiang with the Kuomintang local armed forces, he extensively destroyed bridges and looted more than 10,000 shi of grain, attempting to resist the People's Liberation Army stubbornly.

Furthermore: On the afternoon of the 19th, the People's Liberation Army annihilated 231 bandits from the Xiang-E-Gan Column near Xiushui County in northwestern Jiangxi, capturing the column's Major General Commander Mao Fuyi alive, seizing 446 rifles, eight light machine guns, and one grenade launcher.

### 江西靖安修水两地歼敌四百　俘匪保安司令纵队司令各一

1949-06-29
第1版()
专栏：

　　江西靖安修水两地歼敌四百
  　俘匪保安司令纵队司令各一
    【新华社南昌二十八日电】人民解放军某部二十三日在南昌西北的靖安邱家街地区战斗中，活捉国民党九江专员兼保安司令徐树南以下一百八十名，缴枪一百八十余支。徐匪自九江率国民党地方武装逃窜时，曾大肆破坏桥梁，并劫走粮食万余石，妄图顽抗人民解放军。
    又：人民解放军十九日下午三时在赣西北修水县附近歼匪湘鄂赣纵队二百三十一人，活捉该纵队少将司令卯抚夷，缴获步枪四四六支，轻机枪八挺，掷弹筒一个。
