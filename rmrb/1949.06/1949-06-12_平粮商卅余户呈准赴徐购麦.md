Title: More Than 30 Grain Merchants in Ping City Approved to Purchase Wheat in Xu

Author:
Date: 1949-06-12
Page: 2

　　More Than 30 Grain Merchants in Ping City Approved to Purchase Wheat in Xu
    [This News] More than 30 grain merchants in Ping City have been approved by the Municipal Bureau of Industry and Commerce to purchase new wheat in the Xuzhou area and have been issued purchase certificates. (Zhong)

### 平粮商卅余户呈准赴徐购麦

1949-06-12
第2版()
专栏：

　　平粮商卅余户呈准赴徐购麦
