Title: Four Foreign Ministers Issue Communiqué; Hold Secret Meeting
Author:
Date: 1949-06-19
Page: 3

　　Xinhua News Agency, Peiping, 18th: TASS, Paris: The four Foreign Ministers issued a communiqué on the 16th, declaring that the Soviet, American, British, and French Foreign Ministers held a secret meeting today to discuss the German and Austrian issues discussed at the previous meeting. The four Foreign Ministers will hold their next secret meeting on the 19th.

### 四外长发表公报　举行秘密会议

1949-06-19
第3版()
专栏：

　　四外长发表公报
  　举行秘密会议
    【新华社北平十八日电】塔斯社巴黎讯：十六日四国外长发表公报，宣称：苏、美、英、法外长会议今日举行秘密会议，讨论了前次会议讨论过的德奥问题。四外长将于十九日举行下一次秘密会议。
