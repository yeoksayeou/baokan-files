Title: Beijing and Shanghai Delegations Head North to Attend the National Congress of Literary and Art Workers
Author: 
Date: 1949-06-25
Page: 1

[Nanjing Radio, 24th] The Shanghai delegation of sixty-five members, led by delegation head Feng Xuefeng, left Shanghai for Nanjing at 8:00 AM yesterday to attend the National Congress of Literary and Art Workers. They boarded the Jingpu train north at 8:00 PM that same day. The Nanjing delegation attending the congress also traveled to Beiping on the same train.

### 出席全国文代会  京沪代表团北上

1949-06-25
第1版()
专栏：

    出席全国文代会
    京沪代表团北上
    【南京二十四日广播】出席中华全国文艺工作者代表大会的上海代表团六十五人，由团长冯雪峰率领昨日上午八点钟从上海来南京；当天下午八点钟就乘京浦车北上。出席大会的南京代表团也同车去北平。
