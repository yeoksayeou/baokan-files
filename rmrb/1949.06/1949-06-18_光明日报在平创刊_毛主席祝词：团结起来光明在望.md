Author:

Date: 1949-06-18

　　Guangming Daily Launched in Beiping
  　Chairman Mao's Message: Unite, the Future is Bright
    【Xinhua News Agency, Beiping, 17th】Guangming Daily, the organ of the China Democratic League, was launched in Beiping on the 16th. The inaugural issue contains congratulatory messages from the heads of various democratic parties and non-party democratic figures. Chairman Mao Zedong of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of China's congratulatory message reads: "Unite, the future is bright." The newspaper's inaugural editorial, titled "Unite to Build a New Democratic China," stated: "To build a new democratic China, we demand unity in organization and consistency in action. We may have different thoughts, different opinions, and different styles. But when different thoughts, opinions, and styles are transformed into actions, we must seek consistency." Zhang Bojun serves as the newspaper's president, Hu Yuzhi as the editor-in-chief, Lin Zhongyi as the general manager, and Sa Kongliao as the secretary-general. It is published daily in broadsheet format with four pages.

### 光明日报在平创刊　毛主席祝词：团结起来光明在望

1949-06-18
第2版()
专栏：

　　光明日报在平创刊
  　毛主席祝词：团结起来光明在望
    【新华社北平十七日电】中国民主同盟机关报光明日报十六日在北平创刊。创刊号载有各民主党派负责人及无党派民主人士祝词。中共中央毛泽东主席祝词为“团结起来，光明在望”。该报在题为“团结一致建设民主新中国”的发刊词中称：要建设民主新中国，“在组织方面我们要求团结，在行动方面我们要求一致。我们可以有不同的思想，不同的见解，不同的作风。但是当不同的思想、见解、作风化为行动的时候，却必须求得一致。”该报由章伯钧任社长，胡愈之任总编辑，林仲易任总经理，萨空了任秘书长，日出对开四版。
