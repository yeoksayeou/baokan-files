Title: Savings Deposits Indexed to Real Value Today's Exchange Rate

Author: 
Date: 1949-06-17
Page: 2

Economic News

　　Savings Deposits Indexed to Real Value Today's Exchange Rate
    The Savings Department of the People's Bank of Beiping Branch announces that today's (June 17th) standard unit exchange rate for savings deposits indexed to real value is 244.13 yuan.
