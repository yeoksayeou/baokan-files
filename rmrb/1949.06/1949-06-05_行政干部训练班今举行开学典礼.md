Title: Administrative Cadre Training Class to Hold Opening Ceremony Today
Author:
Date: 1949-06-05
Page: 1

　　Administrative Cadre Training Class to Hold Opening Ceremony Today
    [Report from this newspaper] The Administrative Cadre Training Class, organized by the North China People's Government, has already commenced at the end of May. The main content of the class is political training. The official opening ceremony is scheduled to be held at the Zhongshan Park Music Hall on the evening of June 5th. Vice Chairman Lan Gongwu and Comrade Xie Juezai will deliver reports at that time.

### 行政干部训练班今举行开学典礼

1949-06-05
第1版()
专栏：

　　行政干部训练班今举行开学典礼
    【本报讯】华北人民政府举办的行政干部训练班已于五月底先后开学，该班学习内容主要为政治训练，兹定于六月五日晚在中山公园音乐堂正式举行开学典礼，届时并有蓝公武副主席、谢觉哉同志做报告。
