Title: Shanghai, Nanjing, and Other Places Solemnly Commemorate Teachers' Day
Author:
Date: 1949-06-09
Page: 3

Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, 8th. Teachers in primary, secondary, and higher education institutions in Shanghai, Nanjing, Wuhan, Jinan, and other cities held grand commemorative meetings on "June 6th," Teachers' Day. Shanghai held a commemorative meeting attended by more than 1,500 teacher representatives from various schools. Mayor Chen Yi, Deputy Mayor Wei Que, and others spoke at the meeting. The meeting also resolved to organize the Shanghai Municipal United Association of Primary and Secondary School Teachers and Staff. Over two hundred professors from Nanjing Central University, along with students, staff, and workers from the university, jointly held a commemorative meeting. In Wuhan, over 2,000 teacher representatives from 280 public and private universities, colleges, middle schools, and primary schools gathered at the auditoriums of Huazhong University in Wuchang, the National Institute of Physical Education, and the Wuhan First Boys' Middle School in Hankou, respectively. In Jinan, over 2,300 teachers from public and private universities, middle schools, and primary schools gathered to commemorate the occasion. Teachers in various places expressed at the commemorative meetings that they would work hard to study, integrate with the people, and strive for the cause of New Democratic education in the future.

### 上海南京等地隆重纪念教师节

1949-06-09
第3版()
专栏：

　　上海南京等地隆重纪念教师节
    【新华社北平八日电】上海、南京、武汉、济南的大中小学教师们都在“六、六”教师节这一天举行了盛大的纪念会。上海召开了包括各校教师代表一千五百余人的纪念会。市长陈毅、副市长韦悫等都在会上讲了话。会中并决议组织上海市中小学校教职员联合会。南京中央大学教授二百多人，与该校学生、职员、工友共同举行了纪念会。武汉各公私立大、专、中、小学校二百八十个单位的教师代表二千余人，分别在武昌华中大学、国立体专和汉口市立第一男中等校礼堂集会。济南市各公私立大中小学教师二千三百余人集会纪念。各地的教师们都在纪念会上表示，今后将努力学习，与人民结合，为新民主主义的教育事业努力。
