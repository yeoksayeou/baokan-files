Title: Takeover of Anyang Completed
Author:
Date: 1949-06-14
Page: 2

    【Taihang Telegram】The takeover work in Anyang City has been successfully completed. A total of 63 units of the puppet regime’s organs, as well as factories, banks, repair shops, and other places, have been taken over.
    【Another Telegram】The Anyang City Electric Light Company was repaired on the 27th of last month, and North and South Streets were brightly lit that evening.

### 安阳接管完毕

1949-06-14
第2版()
专栏：

　　安阳接管完毕
    【太行电】安阳市接管工作已顺利完成。共接管伪政权机关等六十三个单位，及工厂、银行、修械所等多处。
    【又电】安阳市电灯公司已于上月二十七日修复，当晚南北大街大放光明。
