Title: Nanjing Workers' Political School Opens

Author:
Date: 1949-06-22
Page: 1

    Nanjing Workers' Political School Opens
    [Broadcast by Nanjing People's Broadcasting Station] The Workers' Political School organized by the Nanjing Municipal Committee of the Chinese Communist Party and the second cadre training class for labor union work organized by the Nanjing Municipal Federation of Trade Unions jointly held an opening ceremony on the 20th. A total of 2,700 students from the two units participated in the ceremony. Mayor Liu Bocheng, Deputy Secretary-General of the Military Control Commission, Comrade Li Jingyi, Director of the Preparatory Committee of the Federation of Trade Unions, Comrade Gao Jun, Director of the Education Bureau, Comrade Xu Pingyu, and Director of the Preparatory Committee of the New Democratic Youth League in Nanjing, Wang Mingyuan, also attended the ceremony.

### 南京工人政治学校开学

1949-06-22
第1版()
专栏：

    南京工人政治学校开学
    【南京人民广播电台广播】中共南京市委会举办的工人政治学校和南京市总工会举办的第二届工运干部训练班，二十日联合举行开学典礼，参加典礼的两个单位学生共有二千七百人，刘伯承市长、军管会副秘书长李静宜同志、总工会筹委会主任高俊同志、教育局长徐平羽同志和新民主主义青年团南京筹委会主任王明远等都参加典礼。
