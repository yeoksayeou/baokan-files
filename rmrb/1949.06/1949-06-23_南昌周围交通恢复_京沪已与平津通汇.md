Title: Traffic Around Nanchang Restored; Beijing and Shanghai Now Connected to Tianjin and Beiping for Remittances
Author:
Date: 1949-06-23
Page: 1

    Traffic Around Nanchang Restored
    Beijing and Shanghai Now Connected to Tianjin and Beiping for Remittances
    [This Newspaper's Dispatch] According to Xinhua News Agency: Railway, highway, shipping, postal and telecommunications around Nanchang have gradually resumed. The eastern section of the Zhejiang-Jiangxi Railway has been opened to traffic as far as the tributary of the Fu River, and the western section has been opened to traffic to Zhangshu. The Nanchang-Jiujiang Railway has been opened to traffic to Tujiapu. The highways that have resumed traffic include the Nanchang-Wuchang Road from Nanchang to Wanjiubu, the Jiangxi-Guangdong Road from Nanchang to Xingan, and the Jiangxi-Fujian Road from Nanchang to Linchuan. In terms of shipping, Nanchang is now open to traffic with Jiujiang, Poyang, Duchang, Anqing and other places. The middle reaches of the Xin River, as a main shipping line in northeastern Jiangxi, also resumed navigation on the 15th. Postal service has been restored to cities such as Nanjing, Shanghai, Hangzhou, Hankou, Beiping, Tianjin, as well as Shandong, Shanxi, Henan, Shaanxi, Anhui, Northeast and northern Fujian. Telegrams, telephones, etc. are also open to traffic with various places.
    [Nanjing People's Broadcasting Station, 22nd Broadcast] In order to promote trade and invigorate finance, the Nanjing Branch of the People's Bank of China has now opened remittance business to six cities: Shanghai, Tianjin, Beiping, Xuzhou, Wuxi, and Tunxi.
    [Xinhua News Agency, Shanghai, 22nd Dispatch] In order to develop production, prosper the economy, and promote the exchange of materials in various liberated areas, the Shanghai Branch of the People's Bank of China has specially launched the business of Shanghai's remittance to major railway lines such as Beijing-Shanghai, Shanghai-Hangzhou, Tianjin-Pukou, and Beiping-Hankou, as well as cities along the lower reaches of the Yangtze River, starting today. The remittance locations include more than 50 places such as Nanjing, Hangzhou, Suzhou, Wuxi, Zhenjiang, Yixing, Beiping, Tianjin, Jinan, Xuzhou, Jiujiang, Wuhan, Kaifeng, Zhengzhou, Luoyang, Xuchang, and Luohe. Remittances are temporarily limited to mail transfers and bank drafts, with exchange rates ranging from a maximum of 60 yuan per thousand yuan to a minimum of 10 yuan.

### 南昌周围交通恢复  京沪已与平津通汇

1949-06-23
第1版()
专栏：

    南昌周围交通恢复
    京沪已与平津通汇
    【本报讯】据新华社电：南昌周围的铁路、公路、航运、邮电等交通已逐渐恢复。浙赣铁路的东段已通车至抚河支流之处，西段已通车到樟树。南（昌）浔（九江）铁路已通车到涂家埠。公路已恢复通车的有南（昌）武（昌）路的南昌至万九埠段，赣粤路的南昌至新淦段，赣闽路的南昌至临川段。航运方面，南昌与九江、鄱阳、都昌、安庆等地已经畅通。作为赣东北航运干线的信江中游，亦已于十五日复航。邮运已通至南京、上海、杭州、汉口、北平、天津等城市及山东、山西、河南、陕西、安徽、东北及福建北部，电报、电话等亦已与各地畅通。
    【南京人民广播电台廿二日广播】中国人民银行南京分行为了促进贸易，活泼金融，现在已开办和上海、天津、北平、徐州、无锡、屯溪六个城市汇款业务。
    【新华社上海二十二日电】中国人民银行上海分行为发展生产繁荣经济，促进解放区内各地物资交流，特自今日起开始举办上海对京沪、沪杭、津浦、平汉等各主要铁路干线及长江下游沿岸城市的汇兑。通汇地点计有南京、杭州、苏州、无锡、镇江、宜兴、北平、天津、济南、徐州、九江、武汉、开封、郑州、洛阳、许昌、漯河等五十余处。通汇暂以信汇、票汇为限，汇率每千元最高为六十元，最低十元。
