Title: *Pravda* Calls for Creation of New Technology to Increase Production and Accelerate the Transition to Communism
Author:
Date: 1949-06-25
Page: 3

Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, June 24th: TASS, Moscow—*Pravda* published an editorial calling for the study and application of the best production experiences. *Pravda* writes: The Soviet people are seeking new methods to shorten the timeframe stipulated by the Five-Year Plan and accelerate the Soviet state's transition from socialism to communism. Production innovators—outstanding Stakhanovites, foremen, and engineers—are boldly engaging in new creations in the technology and organization of production, developing new methods capable of high productivity and promoting technological progress. Their work serves as a model for all those engaged in industry and transportation. *Pravda* points out: The great progress of socialist competition is inextricably linked with the pioneering work of the participants and Stakhanovite creativity. In socialist industry, every valuable and useful plan of a certain enterprise is often immediately studied and applied by other factories and plants and other industrial sectors, and becomes a plan willingly adopted by millions of people. *Pravda* emphasizes: Promoting the best production experience is of great economic importance to the country. In order to complete the Five-Year Plan ahead of schedule, these experiences must be carefully collected, studied, and made available to all participants in the competition. This can be accomplished through methods such as technical discussions, studying and scientifically summarizing the experience of advanced enterprises in the mechanization of technology and production processes, exchanging experience, and so on. *Pravda* concludes: Arming the participants of socialist competition with progressive labor methods means further increasing labor productivity and further improving the quantity and quality of products.

### 真理报号召创造新技术  提高生产加速走向共产主义

1949-06-25
第3版()
专栏：

    真理报号召创造新技术
    提高生产加速走向共产主义
    【新华社北平二十四日电】塔斯社莫斯科讯：真理报发表社论，号召研究与应用最好的生产经验。真理报写道：苏维埃人民寻求新的方法来缩短五年计划所规定的期限，和加速苏维埃国家从社会主义到共产主义的转变。生产改进者——优秀的斯达哈诺夫运动者、领班和工程师们，在生产的技术与组织上大胆地从事于新的创造，发展着能够高度生产的新方法与促进技术的进步。他们的工作足为所有从事于工业与运输业的人们的模范。真理报指出：社会主义竞赛伟大的进展，是和参加竞赛者的先锋工作及斯达哈诺夫式的创造性不可分离地连系着的。在社会主义工业中，某一企业每一项有价值与有用的计划，常常马上就被别的制造厂与工厂及其他工业部门所研究与应用，而且成为千百万人民所乐于采用的计划。真理报着重指出：推广最好的生产经验，对于国家有着巨大的经济重要性。为了提早完成五年计划，这些经验必须细心地收集、研究，并使所有参加竞赛者能够加以利用。这一点可以通过如下方式来完成：例如技术讨论会，把先进企业在技术与生产过程机械化方面的经验加以研究与科学的总结，交换经验等等。真理报在结语里写道：用进步的劳动方法来武装社会主义竞赛的参加者，意味着劳动生产率的更加提高，产品数量和质量的进一步增进。
