Title: Huainan Coal Mine Third Power Plant Resumes Power Supply
Author:
Date: 1949-06-12
Page: 1

　　Huainan Coal Mine's Tianjia'an Power Plant No. 3's 2,000-kilowatt generator began supplying power on May 28. The machine was severely damaged in the hands of the Kuomintang. This time, through the joint efforts of workers and technicians, it took a month and a half, and after three failures, it was finally successfully repaired on the fourth attempt.

### 淮南煤矿第三发电厂恢复送电

1949-06-12
第1版()
专栏：

　　淮南煤矿第三发电厂恢复送电
    淮南煤矿田家庵电力三厂的二千千瓦发电机已于五月二十八日开始送电。该机器在国民党手中损坏颇重，此次经工人和技术人员通力合作，历时一个半月，中间经三次失败，终于第四次修装成功。
