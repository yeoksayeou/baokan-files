Title: Beiping History Museum Acquires Numerous Revolutionary Historical Materials
Author: Bai Sheng
Date: 1949-06-11
Page: 2

　　Beiping History Museum Acquires Numerous Revolutionary Historical Materials
    [This newspaper's report] To establish a revolutionary history museum, the Cultural Relics Department of the North China Higher Education Committee (originally the Cultural Relics Management Committee) entrusted the Beiping History Museum to collect revolutionary historical materials from all sides, and has now made great gains. In addition to transporting the gallows on which Comrade Li Dazhao was martyred, which was stored in the No. 2 Prison outside Deshengmen, to the History Museum, Mr. Hu Sidu donated a copper coin minted in 1933 when General Xu Xiangqian retreated to the Daba Mountains. Vice President Fan Wenlan of China University donated eight photographic negatives of revolutionary martyrs of the Literary Society during the Wuchang Uprising. Mr. Zhang Zhongrui donated a group photo of Comrade Li Dazhao and eight fellow martyrs. Mr. Ling Changyan donated 132 various revolutionary books and magazines, as well as many other valuable materials donated by various parties. It is reported that the museum has also purchased a large number of revolutionary cultural relics.
　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　(Bai Sheng)

### 北平历史博物馆征得不少革命史料
柏生
1949-06-11
第2版()
专栏：

　　北平历史博物馆征得不少革命史料
    【本报讯】为建立革命的历史博物馆，华北高等教育委员会文物处（原文管会文物部）委托北平历史博物馆向各方征集革命历史资料，现已有很大收获。除将德胜门外第二监狱存放的李大钊同志殉难的绞架运至历史博物馆外，又有胡思杜先生捐赠的一九三三年徐向前将军退守大巴山脉时所铸的铜钱一枚，华大范文澜副校长捐赠的武昌起义时期文学社殉难烈士照片底片八张，张仲锐先生捐赠的李大钊同志与八位同难者合影的照片一份，凌昌炎先生捐赠的各种有关革命图书杂志一百三十二册，以及各方捐献的许多资料，均甚珍贵。闻该馆并已购到大批革命文物。
　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　（柏生）
