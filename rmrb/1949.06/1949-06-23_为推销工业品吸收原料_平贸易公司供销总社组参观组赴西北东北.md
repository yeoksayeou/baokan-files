Title: To Promote Industrial Products and Absorb Raw Materials: Beijing Trade Company and Supply and Marketing Cooperative Form Visiting Groups to Northwest and Northeast China
Author: Cheng Jie
Date: 1949-06-23
Page: 2

    To Promote Industrial Products and Absorb Raw Materials
    Beijing Trade Company and Supply and Marketing Cooperative Form Visiting Groups to Northwest and Northeast China
    [Our Newspaper's News] In order to develop the exchange of materials between urban and rural areas, gain work experience, and understand the production, sales, transportation, and other conditions in the Northeast and Northwest regions, the Beijing Trade Company and the Beijing Cooperative General Agency have organized two visiting groups. The Northeast group, composed of five people including Fang Ming, and the Northwest group, composed of seven people including Zhao Guangyi, will depart in the next few days to work in Shanhaiguan, Shenyang, Harbin, Zhangjiakou, Xuanhua, Datong, and Fengzhen. The mission of these two groups is to promote the sale of industrial products from this city and absorb grain and industrial raw materials from the Northeast and Northwest regions (Cheng Jie).

### 为推销工业品吸收原料  平贸易公司供销总社组参观组赴西北东北
程捷
1949-06-23
第2版()
专栏：

    为推销工业品吸收原料
    平贸易公司供销总社组参观组赴西北东北
    【本报讯】北平市贸易公司和北平市合作总社为了开展城乡物资交流，吸取工作经验，了解东北、西北各地产、销、运等情况，特组织两个参观小组。东北组由方明等五人组成，西北组由赵光义等七人组成，日内即可出发，分赴山海关、沈阳、哈尔滨、张、宣、大同、丰镇等地工作。该两小组此行之任务，为推销本市工业品及吸收东北、西北各地粮食及工业原料（程捷）。
