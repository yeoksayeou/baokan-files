Title: Our Army Enters Yulin; Yulin Municipal Government Officially Established
Author:
Date: 1949-06-13
Page: 1
　　Our Army Enters Yulin
　　Yulin Municipal Government Officially Established
　　[Xinhua Broadcasting Station, Beiping, Broadcast] Xi'an, 11th: After the important town of Yulin in northern Shaanxi was peacefully liberated on the 1st of this month, the People's Liberation Army entered the city at 10:00 AM on the same day. On the 2nd, the Yulin Municipal People's Government was formally established by order of the Shaanxi-Gansu-Ningxia Border Region Government and is directly led by the Yulin Sub-district Commissioner's Office. The Yulin Daily was launched on the 5th; the Yulin Branch of the Xinhua News Agency also moved into the city to work on the same day.

### 我军进驻榆林　榆林市府正式成立

1949-06-13
第1版()
专栏：

　　我军进驻榆林
  　榆林市府正式成立
    【北平新华广播电台广播】西安十一日消息：陕北重镇榆林在本月一日宣告和平解放后，人民解放军在当天上午十点钟就进驻该城。二日，榆林市人民政府奉陕甘宁边区人民政府命令正式成立，由榆林分区专员公署直接领导。榆林报已经在五日创刊；新华社榆林分社也在同一天搬进城内工作。
