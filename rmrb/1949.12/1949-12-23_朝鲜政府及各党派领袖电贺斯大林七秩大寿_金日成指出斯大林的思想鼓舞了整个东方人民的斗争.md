Title: Korean Government and Party Leaders Send Congratulations to Stalin on His 70th Birthday; Kim Il-sung Points Out That Stalin's Thought Has Encouraged the Struggle of the Entire Eastern People
Author:
Date: 1949-12-23
Page: 2
Xinhua News Agency, Pyongyang, December 21st. Kim Il-sung, Chairman of the Cabinet of Ministers of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea, the Central Committee of the Democratic Front for the Unification of the Fatherland, the Central Committee of the Workers' Party, the Korean Democratic Party, the Young Friends Party, and Deputy Chairman of the Cabinet of Ministers Park Heon-young, all sent telegrams on the 20th to congratulate Marshal Stalin on his 70th birthday. Kim Il-sung's congratulatory telegram stated: "On the occasion of your 70th birthday, the Korean people extend their most sincere congratulations to you—the great father, teacher, and friend of the working people of the world. The ever-victorious thought of Lenin and Stalin has encouraged the just struggle for peace, democracy, freedom, and independence of hundreds of millions of people in the East, including the 30 million Korean people. Your name is the banner uniting progressive forces around the world against imperialism and reactionaries. May you live a long life for the freedom and happiness of the working people!" The congratulatory telegram from the Fatherland Unification Front stated: "With the friendly assistance of the great Soviet Union, the Korean people have achieved great results in economic and cultural construction, and at the same time, are vigorously carrying out the struggle for the unification of the fatherland." The congratulatory telegram from the Workers' Party stated: "Your name encourages us to actively carry out the struggle for the unification, independence, and freedom of the fatherland. Your name strengthens our belief in final victory. The doctrine of Lenin and Stalin is the only direction of our party in carrying out patriotic and internationalist education of the people."

### 朝鲜政府及各党派领袖电贺斯大林七秩大寿  金日成指出斯大林的思想鼓舞了整个东方人民的斗争

1949-12-23
第2版()
专栏：

　　朝鲜政府及各党派领袖电贺斯大林七秩大寿
    金日成指出斯大林的思想鼓舞了整个东方人民的斗争
    【新华社平壤二十一日电】朝鲜民主主义人民共和国部长会议主席金日成、祖国统一民主主义战线中央委员会、劳动党中央委员会、北朝鲜民主党、青友党、部长会议副主席朴宪永等，均于二十日电贺斯大林大元帅的七秩寿辰。金日成的贺电中说：“欣逢你的七十寿辰，朝鲜人民谨向你—全世界劳动人民的伟大父亲、导师和朋友，致以最真挚的祝贺。列宁、斯大林的百战百胜的思想，鼓舞了包括三千万朝鲜人民在内的东方数万万人民的和平、民主、自由与独立的正义斗争。你的名字是团结全世界进步力量来反对帝国主义和反动派的旗帜。为了劳动人民的自由幸福，祝你万寿无疆！”祖国统一阵线的贺电说：“朝鲜人民在伟大苏联的友谊援助下，在经济文化建设中取得了莫大的成果，同时有力地展开着争取祖国统一的斗争。”劳动党的贺电说：“你的名字，鼓舞我们积极进行为祖国统一独立和自由的斗争。你的名字使我们加强最后胜利的信念。列宁斯大林的学说，在对人民进行爱国主义和国际主义的教育中，是我党的唯一方向。”
