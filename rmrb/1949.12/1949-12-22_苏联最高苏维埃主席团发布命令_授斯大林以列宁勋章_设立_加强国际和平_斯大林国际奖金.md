Title: The Presidium of the Supreme Soviet of the USSR Issues Decree Awarding Stalin the Order of Lenin; Establishing the Stalin International Peace Prize "For Strengthening International Peace"
Author:
Date: 1949-12-22
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, December 21st. TASS Moscow Dispatch: The Presidium of the Supreme Soviet of the USSR issued the following decree awarding Comrade Stalin the Order of Lenin: On the occasion of Comrade Stalin's seventieth birthday, and in consideration of his special merits in strengthening and developing the Union of Soviet Socialist Republics, in building communism in our country, in organizing the defeat of the German fascist aggressors and Japanese imperialists, and in restoring and further developing the Soviet national economy in the post-war period, Comrade Joseph Vissarionovich Stalin is hereby awarded the Order of Lenin.
    Chairman of the Presidium of the Supreme Soviet of the USSR, Shvernik. Secretary of the Presidium of the Supreme Soviet of the USSR, Gorkin (signed).
    　　　　　　　December 20, 1949, in the Moscow Kremlin

Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, December 21st. TASS Moscow Dispatch: The Presidium of the Supreme Soviet of the USSR has issued a decree on the establishment of the Stalin International Peace Prize "For Strengthening International Peace." (1) Citizens of any country in the world, regardless of their political, religious, or racial affiliation, who have made outstanding contributions to the struggle against warmongers and the promotion of peace, may be awarded this prize. (2) Recipients of the Stalin International Peace Prize "For Strengthening International Peace" shall receive: (a) a certificate of honor for the Stalin International Prize; (b) a gold medal engraved with Stalin's portrait; (c) a prize of 100,000 rubles. (3) The Stalin International Peace Prize "For Strengthening International Peace" shall be awarded once a year, with five to ten recipients. The prize shall be awarded by a special committee of the Stalin International Prize, composed of representatives of democratic forces from various countries, appointed by the Presidium of the Supreme Soviet of the USSR. The date of the award ceremony shall be the birthday of Joseph Vissarionovich Stalin (December 21st) each year, and the first prize shall be awarded in 1950.
    　　　　　　　　　　　Chairman of the Presidium of the Supreme Soviet of the USSR, Shvernik
    　　　　　　　　　　　　Secretary of the Presidium of the Supreme Soviet of the USSR, Gorkin
    　　　　　　　December 20, 1949, in the Moscow Kremlin

### 苏联最高苏维埃主席团发布命令　授斯大林以列宁勋章  设立“加强国际和平”斯大林国际奖金

1949-12-22
第1版()
专栏：

　　苏联最高苏维埃主席团发布命令
    授斯大林以列宁勋章         
    设立“加强国际和平”斯大林国际奖金
    【新华社北京二十一日电】塔斯社莫斯科讯：苏联最高苏维埃主席团为授予斯大林同志以列宁勋章发布命令如下：值此斯大林同志七十寿辰之际，由于考虑到他在加强与发展苏维埃社会主义共和国联盟、在我国建设共产主义、在组织击溃德国法西斯侵略者和日本帝国主义者以及在战后时期恢复和进一步发展苏联国民经济中的特殊功勋，特授予约瑟夫·维萨里奥诺维奇·斯大林同志以列宁勋章。
    苏联最高苏维埃主席团主席什维尔尼克，苏联最高苏维埃主席团秘书高尔金（签字）。
    　　　　　　　一九四九年十二月二十日于莫斯科克里姆林宫
    【新华社北京二十一日电】塔斯社莫斯科讯：苏联最高苏维埃主席团发布关于设立“加强国际和平”斯大林国际奖金。全世界任何国家的公民，凡与战争贩子斗争及促进和平有卓越功绩者，不论其政治、宗教或种族为何，均可获得此项奖金。（二）凡获得“加强国际和平”斯大林国际奖金之人士，均将领到：（甲）斯大林国际奖金荣誉奖状；（乙）刻有斯大林肖像之金质勋章；（丙）十万卢布之奖金。（三）“加强国际和平”斯大林国际奖金每年颁发一次，名额为五名至十名。奖金苏联最高苏维埃主席团延聘世界各国民主力量的代表人物组成之斯大林国际奖金特别委员会颁发之。颁发奖金的日期，定为每年约瑟夫·维萨里奥诺维奇·斯大林的诞辰（十二月二十一日），并定于一九五○年颁发第一次奖金。
    　　　　　　　　　　　苏联最高苏维埃主席团主席什维尔尼克
    　　　　　　　　　　　　苏联最高苏维埃主席团秘书高尔金
    　　　　　　　一九四九年十二月二十日于莫斯科克里姆林宫
