Title: The Beijing Branch of the China Democratic National Construction Association Invites Xiao San to Give a Lecture
Author:
Date: 1949-12-22
Page: 2

　　The Beijing Branch of the China Democratic National Construction Association Invites Xiao San to Give a Lecture
    [This newspaper's dispatch] The 38th New Knowledge Forum, hosted by the Beijing Branch of the China Democratic National Construction Association, will be held at 7:00 PM on December 23rd (Friday) in the auditorium of the European and American Alumni Association on Nanheyan. Xiao San is specially invited to speak on "Stalin and the Chinese Revolution."

### 民建会京市分会请肖三讲演

1949-12-22
第2版()
专栏：

　　民建会京市分会请肖三讲演
    【本报讯】民主建国会北京市分会所主持的第三十八次新知识座谈会，订于十二月二十三日（星期五）下午七时在南河沿欧美同学会礼堂举行，特邀请肖三主讲“斯大林与中国革命”。
