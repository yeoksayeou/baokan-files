Title: Direct Freight Service Between Zhengzhou and Xi'an
Author:
Date: 1949-12-06
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Zhengzhou, 5th—The Longhai Railway line between Zhengzhou and Xi'an began passenger and freight operations on the 1st of this month. One pair each of ordinary passenger trains and mixed trains run daily between Zhengzhou and Xi'an. Direct freight services are available between Zhengzhou and Xi'an stations, while passengers and luggage can travel directly from Lianyungang to Xi'an.

### 郑州西安间　货运直达

1949-12-06
第4版()
专栏：

　　郑州西安间
  　货运直达
    【新华社郑州五日电】陇海铁路郑州与西安间已于本月一日起办理客货运营业。郑州和西安每日对开普通客车和混合列车各一对，郑州西安两站可办理直达货运，旅客和行李则可自连云港直达西安。
