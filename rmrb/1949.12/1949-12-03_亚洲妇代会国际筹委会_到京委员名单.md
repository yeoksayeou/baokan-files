Title: List of Committee Members of the International Preparatory Committee of the Asian Women's Conference Arriving in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1949-12-03
Page: 1

　　Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, December 2nd: The following members of the International Preparatory Committee of the Asian Women's Conference, which was formally established in Beijing on November 23rd, have arrived in Beijing: Simone Bertin (International Women's Federation), Tamara Marosova, Nina Yeremeyeva (Soviet Women's Anti-Fascist Committee), Kuriba Toktomanbetova (Kyrgyzstan), Cai Chang, Deng Yingchao, Li Dequan, Qu Mengjue, Zhang Qinqiu, Lu Cui (China), Kim Kwi-sun, Ahn Hyo-sang (Korea), Ho Thi Minh (Vietnam), Amrita Tavi (India), Maxine Farroqi (Iran), Huang Pinglian (Burma), Huang Nanjun (Philippines). Preparatory committee members from other Asian countries will arrive in Beijing and join the preparatory committee at any time thereafter.

### 亚洲妇代会国际筹委会  到京委员名单

1949-12-03
第1版()
专栏：

　　亚洲妇代会国际筹委会
    到京委员名单
    【新华社北京二日电】十一月二十三日在北京正式成立的亚洲妇女代表会议国际筹备委员会的委员已到北京的有：西门·贝特朗（国际妇联），塔玛拉·玛洛索娃、尼娜·耶列梅耶娃（苏联妇女反法西斯委员会），库里巴·托克托曼别托娃（吉尔吉斯），蔡畅、邓颖超、李德全、区梦觉、张琴秋、陆璀、（中国），金贵善、安孝相（朝鲜），胡德明（越南），阿米塔·台维（印度），玛辛·法洛琪（伊朗），黄平莲（缅甸），黄南君（菲律宾）。以后亚洲其他国家之筹备委员将随时到京，随时参加筹委会。
