Title: The Ministry of Posts and Telecommunications of the Central People's Government Convenes First National Postal Conference; Korean Minister of Foreign Communications Arrives in Beijing to Discuss Postal and Telegraph Communications

Author:

Date: 1949-12-10
Page: 1

　　Central People's Government Ministry of Posts and Telecommunications
  　Convenes First National Postal Conference
    Korean Minister of Foreign Communications Arrives in Beijing to Discuss Postal and Telegraph Communications
    [Beijing News] The first National Postal Conference convened by the Ministry of Posts and Telecommunications of the Central People's Government held a preliminary meeting yesterday morning, and the formal meeting is scheduled to open in Beijing today. This conference will discuss and resolve important issues such as the unified organizational structure of the People's Post, as well as the supply, finance, accounting, and operational systems. This is to lay the foundation for the People's postal service. The conference will also study personnel allocation, salary standards, and the establishment of collective contracts. Currently, sixty-one responsible cadres from the postal administration bureaus and major postal bureaus of the major administrative regions of North China, Northeast China, Northwest China, East China, Central China, and Southwest China have arrived in Beijing to attend the conference.
    [Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, 9th] The representative of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea, Minister of Foreign Communications of the Ministry of Posts and Communications, Cha Byung-Hyun, and the Chief of the Materials Management Department, Song Rye-Hyun, who came to our country to discuss postal and telegraph communications issues, arrived in Beijing at 7:15 this morning. Shen Guang, Director of the General Office of the Ministry of Posts and Telecommunications of our Central People's Government, and the directors of the Postal and Telecommunications Bureaus went to the station to welcome them. This afternoon, Zhu Xuefan, Minister of Posts and Telecommunications, and Wang Zheng, Vice Minister, hosted a banquet for the Korean representatives at the Beijing Hotel, accompanied by Yan Baohang, Deputy Director of the General Office of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs, and others.

### 中央人民政府邮电部　召开首次全国邮政会议  朝鲜国外通信部长来京商谈通邮通电

1949-12-10
第1版()
专栏：

　　中央人民政府邮电部
  　召开首次全国邮政会议
    朝鲜国外通信部长来京商谈通邮通电
    【北京讯】中央人民政府邮电部召集的第一次全国邮政会议已于昨日上午举行预备会议，正式会议定于今日在京开幕。这次会议将讨论和解决人民邮政的统一组织机构和供应、财务、会计、业务的制度等项重要问题。借以奠定人民邮政事业基础，并将研究人员编制、工薪标准以及订立集体合同等问题。现在华北、东北、西北、华东、华中、西南等各大行政区邮政管理总局、主要邮政管理局及邮政工会负责干部六十一人业已到京，准备出席会议。
    【新华社北京九日电】前来我国商谈通邮通电问题的朝鲜民主主义人民共和国代表、递信省国外通信部长车秉亨与物资管理处长宋礼贤，已于今日上午七时十五分抵达北京。我中央人民政府邮电部办公厅主任申光及邮政、电信两总局局长等前往车站欢迎。今日下午，邮电部部长朱学范、副部长王诤在北京饭店欢宴朝鲜代表，由外交部办公厅副主任阎宝航等作陪。
