Title: Sui Meng Daily Renamed Suiyuan Daily
Author:
Date: 1949-12-16
Page: 1

　　Xinhua News Agency, Suiyuan, 15th. In order to adapt to the new situation in Suiyuan Province, the "Sui Meng Daily," the organ of the Suiyuan Provincial Committee of the Chinese Communist Party, originally published in Datong, has been renamed "Suiyuan Daily" since December 1st, and has been changed from an every-other-day edition to a daily edition. The distribution scope has also been expanded to the entire province.

### 绥蒙日报改称绥远日报

1949-12-16
第1版()
专栏：

　　绥蒙日报改称绥远日报
    【新华社绥远十五日电】为了适应绥远省的新形势，原在大同出版的中共绥远省委会机关报“绥蒙日报”，自十二月一日起改名为“绥远日报”，并由隔日版改为每日版。发行范围也扩大到全省。
