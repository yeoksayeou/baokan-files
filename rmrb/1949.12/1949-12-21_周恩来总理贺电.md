　　Zhou Enlai's Congratulatory Telegram
    【Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, December 20th】Zhou Enlai, Premier of the State Administration Council (SAC) and Minister of Foreign Affairs of the Central People's Government of the People's Republic of China, sent a telegram on the 19th to congratulate Chairman Stalin on his 70th birthday. The text of the telegram is as follows: To Comrade Vyshinsky, Minister of Foreign Affairs of the Soviet Government, to be forwarded to Comrade Stalin, Chairman of the Council of Ministers of the Soviet Government:
    On the occasion of Your Excellency's seventieth birthday, I would like to express my sincere congratulations and wish Your Excellency good health and longevity.
    Premier of the State Administration Council and Minister of Foreign Affairs of the Central People's Government of thePeople's Republic of China, Zhou Enlai
    　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　December 19, 1949

### 周恩来总理贺电

1949-12-21
第1版()
专栏：

　　周恩来总理贺电
    【新华社北京二十日电】我中央人民政府政务院总理兼外交部部长周恩来十九日电贺斯大林主席七十大寿，电文如下：苏联政府外交部长维辛斯基阁下转致苏联政府部长会议主席斯大林阁下：
    欣逢阁下七十寿辰之际，谨致庆贺之忱，并祝阁下健康和长寿。
    　中华人民共和国中央人民政府政务院总理兼外交部部长　周恩来
    　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　一九四九年十二月十九日
