Title: Northeast Coal Mine Bureau, Trade Union Federation Telegraph Chairman Mao: Seven Major State-Owned Coal Mines in Northeast China Fulfill This Year's Task Ahead of Schedule
Author:
Date: 1949-12-15
Page: 3

Xinhua News Agency, Shenyang, December 14th: The seven major state-owned coal mines under the Coal Mine Bureau of the Industrial Department of the Northeast People's Government, namely Hegang, Jixi, Jiaohe, Xi'an, Fuxin, Tonghua, and Beipiao, all completed this year's production tasks ahead of schedule on the 27th of last month. The Coal Mine Bureau and the Northeast Coal Mine Trade Union Federation have sent a telegram to Chairman Mao to report on their production achievements. The telegram reads as follows: To Ministers Wang, Lü, and An of the Industrial Department of the Northeast People's Government, forwarded to Chairman Mao of the Central People's Government:

Under your leadership, all employees of the Coal Mine Bureau of the Industrial Department of the Northeast People's Government completed this year's planned output on November 27, 1949 (one month ahead of schedule). The production efficiency of all employees has also increased from 0.36 tons at the beginning of the year to 0.51 tons recently. It is expected that by the end of the year, the planned output will be exceeded by 12 percent. This is a special report.

Respectfully,
Northeast People's Government Industrial Department Coal Mine Bureau, Northeast Coal Mine Trade Union Federation
December 9th

### 东北煤矿管理局、总工会电告毛主席  东北七大国营煤矿提前完成今年任务

1949-12-15
第3版()
专栏：

　　东北煤矿管理局、总工会电告毛主席
    东北七大国营煤矿提前完成今年任务
    【新华社沈阳十四日电】东北人民政府工业部煤矿管理局所属鹤岗、鸡西、蛟河、西安、阜新、通化、北票七大国营煤矿，已提前于上月二十七日全部完成今年生产任务。煤矿管理局和东北煤矿总工会为此特电呈毛主席报告生产成绩。电文如下：东北人民政府工业部王、吕、安部长转呈中央人民政府毛主席：
    东北工业部煤矿管理局全体员工在你的领导下，已于一九四九年十一月二十七日（提前一个月）完成本年计划产量，全员生产能率亦从年初的零．三六吨到达最近的零．五一吨。预计到年底为止，可超过计划产量百分之十二，特此报告。
        此致
    敬礼！
    　　　　　　　东北人民政府工业部煤矿管理局、东北煤矿总工会
    　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　十二月九日
