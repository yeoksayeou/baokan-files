Title: Bulgarian Communist Party Central Committee Holds Plenary Session to Discuss Cominform Resolution; Plenary Session Resolution Believes it Will Give a Powerful Impetus to the Struggle of the People of the World
Author:
Date: 1949-12-05
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, December 3rd: Telegraph Agency Sofia, December 2nd: The Bulgarian Communist Party's organ, Worker's Cause newspaper, published a message stating that the Central Committee of the Bulgarian Communist Party held a plenary session on November 24th to discuss the resolutions of the recent Cominform meeting held in Hungary. The plenary session of the Bulgarian Communist Party Central Committee passed a resolution stating: The Cominform's resolutions will give a powerful impetus to the people of the world in their struggle to defend peace, consolidate the unity of the working class, and eliminate the Tito gang of spies and assassins.
　　Worker's Cause newspaper continued to publish resolutions passed by workers' gatherings across the country, all demanding that the Bulgarian traitorous spy Kostov and his accomplices be sentenced to death.

### 保共中央举行全体会议　讨论情报局决议  全会决议认为将使世界人民斗争获得强有力的推动力

1949-12-05
第4版()
专栏：

　　保共中央举行全体会议
  　讨论情报局决议
    全会决议认为将使世界人民斗争获得强有力的推动力
    【新华社北京三日电】电通社索非亚二日讯：保加利亚共产党机关报工人事业报刊载消息称，保加利亚共产党中央委员会于十一月二十四日举行全体会议，讨论在匈牙利举行的情报局最近一次会议的决议。保共中委会全会通过决议称：情报局的决议将使全世界人民在保卫和平、巩固工人阶级团结和消灭间谍与暗杀者的铁托匪帮的斗争中，获得一强有力的推动力。
    工人事业报继续刊登全国各地工人集会所通过的决议，纷纷要求对保叛国间谍科斯托夫及其同谋犯判处死刊。
