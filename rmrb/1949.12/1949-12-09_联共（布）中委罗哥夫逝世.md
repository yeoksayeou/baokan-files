Title: Rogov, Member of the Central Committee of the All-Union Communist Party (Bolsheviks), Passes Away
Author:
Date: 1949-12-09
Page: 4

　　联共（布）中委罗哥夫逝世
Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, December 6th: The Central Committee of the Communist Party of the Soviet Union (Bolsheviks) announces with deep sorrow that Lieutenant General Ivan Rogov passed away on December 5, 1949. Lieutenant General Rogov was a loyal son of the Bolshevik Party, a member of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of the Soviet Union (Bolsheviks), Deputy Commander for Political Affairs of the Baltic Military District, and a representative of the Supreme Soviet of the Soviet Union.

### 联共（布）中委罗哥夫逝世

1949-12-09
第4版()
专栏：

　　联共（布）中委罗哥夫逝世
    【新华社北京八日电】塔斯社莫斯科六日讯：苏联共产党（布）中央委员会怀着沉痛的心情宣布：伊凡·罗哥夫准上将于一九四九年十二月五日逝世。罗哥夫准上将是布尔什维克党忠实的儿子、苏联共产党（布）中央委员会委员、波罗的海军区政治事务副司令员和苏联最高苏维埃代表。
