Title: Government Administration Council Issues Circular to Local Governments: Strengthen Protection of Railway Communication Equipment, Strictly Arrest Criminals Damaging Wires Along the Railway

Author:
Date: 1949-12-09
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, December 8th – The Central People's Government Administration Council issued a circular to the governments of Beijing, Tianjin, Shandong, Pingyuan, Henan, Shanxi, Hebei, Jiangxi, Hunan, Zhejiang, Hubei, Shaanxi, Chahar, Suiyuan, Huaibei, and Southern Jiangsu provinces (regions) and municipalities, urging them to strengthen the protection of communication equipment along railways and strictly arrest criminals who damage railway wires. The circular pointed out: (1) Local governments should closely liaise with the public security and telecommunications departments of the local railway bureaus to determine the organization and methods for protection. (2) Local governments should conduct widespread propaganda and education on wire protection and anti-banditry among residents of towns and cities along the railway lines, and organize them accordingly. (3) Local governments should strictly investigate the source of copper materials for general copper workshops and factories; ban merchants and peddlers in the market who collect and sell old and broken copper and iron wires, in order to cut off the outlets for copper and iron wires. (4) Those caught committing the crimes should be strictly interrogated and dealt with according to the law, and not treated as general petty theft cases.

### 政务院通令各地政府　加强保护铁道通讯设备严缉破坏沿路电线匪犯

1949-12-09
第1版()
专栏：

　　政务院通令各地政府
  　加强保护铁道通讯设备严缉破坏沿路电线匪犯
    【新华社北京八日电】中央人民政府政务院通令北京、天津、山东、平原、河南、山西、河北、江西、湖南、浙江、湖北、陕西、察哈尔、绥远、淮北、苏南等省（区）、市政府，加强保护铁路沿线通讯设备，严缉破坏铁路电线的匪犯。通令指出：（一）各地政府应与该地铁路局的公安、电务两部门密切联系，洽定防护的组织和办法。（二）各地政府对铁路沿线城镇居民，应进行普遍的护线缉匪宣传教育和组织工作。（三）各地政府对一般铜器作坊工厂的铜料来源，应予严加调查；取缔市场上收售旧碎铜铁线的行商小贩，以断绝铜铁线的销路。（四）对于已逮捕之现行犯，应严予审讯法办，不得按一般穷盗案处理。
