　　Introduction to Political Economy Tutorial (61)
　　The Object, Task, and Characteristics of Political Economy Studies
    Wang Xuewen
    Section 4: The Revolutionary, Militant, and Practical Nature of Marxist-Leninist Political Economy
    Practicality is the Foundation or Source of All Other Characteristics of Marxist-Leninist Political Economy
    The reason why Marxist-Leninist political economy possesses scientific, historical, revolutionary, critical, and militant characteristics is precisely because it possesses practicality. Because it is practical, it is scientific, historical, class-based, international, partisan, revolutionary, critical, and combative. At the same time, the reason why it can be a unified, complete, and developing science stems from this fundamental characteristic of practicality. Socio-economic practice and the proletarian revolutionary movement are the most fundamental sources of Marxist-Leninist political economy. The scientific practicality of Marxist-Leninist political economy makes political economy scientific for the first time, enabling it to be established and grow in the struggle against bourgeois and all other reactionary and erroneous theories, and to possess full revolutionary, critical, and militant characteristics. Along with the development of socio-economic conditions, the strength of the proletariat, and the practice of the proletarian revolutionary movement, the theoretical content of Marxist-Leninist political economy itself is also enriched and developed day by day. This is because the emergence of this theory is completely in line with the practice of socio-economic development, suits the needs of the actual struggle of the international proletariat and the working class, and is completely consistent with the practical requirements of the proletariat. Therefore, Marxist-Leninist political economy is not only a pure scientific theory for the international proletariat and the working class and their political parties, but also a pointer for the revolutionary action and revolutionary construction of the proletariat and the working class and their political parties. Theoretical analysis points out the requirements and goals of the actual struggle of the proletariat and the working class. The practice of the proletarian and working class revolutionary struggle and revolutionary construction, in turn, enriches and develops the theory. The theory and practice of Marxist-Leninist political economy are thus closely and inseparably linked and combined. Therefore, we can also say that the practicality of Marxist-Leninist political economy is the foundation or source of all other characteristics of Marxist-Leninist political economy, and also the source of its invincible and all-conquering infinite power. Therefore, Lenin once said that Marxist doctrine is omnipotent, including Marxist-Leninist political economy, and it is omnipotent "because of its truth," that is, its practicality.
    This revolutionary, militant, and practical nature of Marxist-Leninist political economy is also a characteristic that bourgeois political economy, especially vulgar bourgeois political economy, does not have and cannot have.
    The Greatest Harm of Bourgeois Political Economy: Complete Separation of Books and Practice
    Bourgeois political economy, like all other sciences of the bourgeoisie, "one of the greatest evils that it has left us is the complete separation of books from practice," which is the "most abominable feature" of capitalist society and all other exploitative systems of society (Lenin: The Tasks of the Youth Leagues). Not only that, but the bourgeoisie, the feudal class, the slave owners, in short, all exploiting classes, except in the early stage of the establishment and development of this social system, had a certain degree of progressiveness and scientific nature due to the need to oppose and overthrow the old social system, once they reached the stage of high development and decline, they not only separated books from practice, but also, for their own narrow class interests, did not hesitate to conceal facts, obliterate facts, and even distort facts and fabricate facts. Therefore, it exposed the erroneousness, hypocrisy, deceptiveness, anti-scientific nature, and reactionary nature of their theoretical science.

### 政治经济学教程绪论（６１）
王学文
1949-12-29
第5版()
专栏：

　　政治经济学教程绪论（６１）
　  政治经济学的研究对象任务及其特点
    王学文
    第四节马列主义政治经济学的革命性、战斗性与实践性
    实践性是马列主义政治经济学其他一切特点的基础或根源
    马列主义政治经济学所以具有科学性，历史性，革命性，批判性，与战斗性，也就是由于它具有实践性。因为它是实践的，所以它是科学的，历史的，阶级的，国际的，党派的，革命的，批判的，战争的。同时，它所以能够在为一种统一的，完整的科学，并且有发展性，也就是根源于这一根本的实践性的特性。社会经济与无产阶级革命运动的实践，乃是马列主义政治经济学的最基本的源泉，马列主义政治经济学科学的实践性，使政治经济学第一次地成为科学的东西，并且使它能够在与资产阶级的及其他一切反动的错误理论的斗争中建立成长起来，而且有充分的革命性，批判性与战斗性，并伴随着社会经济的发展，无产阶级力量与无产阶级革命运动实践的发展，使马列主义政治经济学理论内容本身也日益充实与发展。这就是由于这个理论的产生是完全符合于社会经济发展的实践，适合着国际无产阶级和工人阶级实际斗争的需要，并与无产阶级实践的要求完全一致的。因此马列主义政治经济学不单单是国际无产阶级和工人阶级及其政党的一种单纯的科学理论，同时又是无产阶级和工人阶级及其政党革命行动革命建设的指针。理论的分析，指示出无产阶级和工人阶级实际斗争的要求与目标，无产阶级和工人阶级革命斗争及革命建设的实践，翻过来又充实着发展着理论。马列主义政治经济学的理论与实践便是这样密切不可分离的联系结合着的。所以，我们又可以说：马列主义政治经济学的实践性，乃是马列主义政治经济学其它一切特点的基础或根源，也是它不可战胜和战无不胜攻无不克的无穷力量的源泉。故列宁曾经说过，马克思主义学说是全能的，包括马列主义政治经济学在内，它所以全能，“就在于它的真实性”，即实践性。
    马列主义政治经济学的这种革命性，战斗性与实践性，也是资产阶级政治经济学，尤其是庸俗派资产阶级政治经济学所没有并且不可能具有的特点。
    资产阶级政治经济学最大的祸害：书本与实践完全隔离
    资产阶级政治经济学，正如资产阶级的其他一切科学一样，它“所遗留给我们的最大的祸害之一，就是书本与实践完全隔离”，乃是资本主义社会及其他一切剥削制度的社会“最可恶的特征”（列宁：共产主义青年团底任务），不仅如此；资产阶级、封建阶级、奴隶主，总之一切剥削阶级，除了这种社会制度成立发展的初期，由于反对推翻旧社会制度的需要，具有某种程度的进步性科学性以外，一到它们高度发展与没落衰亡的时期，他们不仅把书本和实践脱离，而且为了自己狭隘的阶级利益，不惜隐蔽事实，抹煞事实以至于歪曲事实捏造事实。因此暴露了他们理论科学的错误性，虚伪性，欺骗性，反科学性与反动性。
