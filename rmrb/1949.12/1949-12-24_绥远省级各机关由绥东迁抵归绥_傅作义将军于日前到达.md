Title: Suiyuan Provincial Organs Move from Suidong to Guisui; General Fu Zuoyi Arrives Several Days Ago
Author:
Date: 1949-12-24
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, Guisui, December 23rd: More than 1,200 cadres from the Suiyuan People's Government, the Chinese Communist Party Suiyuan Provincial Committee, all departments of the People's Liberation Army Suiyuan Military Region, the Chinese New Democratic Youth League Suiyuan Provincial Working Committee, the Suiyuan Provincial Federation of Trade Unions Preparatory Committee, the Suiyuan Provincial Democratic Women's Federation Preparatory Committee, and the Sino-Soviet Friendship Association Suiyuan Provincial Branch Preparatory Committee, originally stationed in the Suidong Liberated Area, led by Gao Kelin, Vice Chairman of the Suiyuan Military and Political Committee and Secretary of the Chinese Communist Party Suiyuan Provincial Committee, as well as Yao Zhe, Deputy Commander of the Suiyuan Military Region, and Yang Zhilin and Kui Bi, Vice Chairmen of the Suiyuan People's Government, arrived in Guisui City, the capital of Suiyuan Province, by special train from Fengzhen on December 17th. Dong Qiwu, Chairman of the Provincial Government originally stationed in Guisui City, and all members of the Suiyuan Military and Political Committee and Provincial Government, along with representatives from more than 60 units including government organs, troops, factories, schools, and street districts, totaling over 8,000 people, welcomed them at Guisui Railway Station. In his welcoming speech, Dong Qiwu pointed out: This welcome is fundamentally different from ordinary welcomes, because everyone has brought warmth and happiness to the people of Suiyuan. In the future, we must express our welcome with concrete actions of close unity, humble learning, hard work, and labor production. This was followed by a speech by Gao Kelin. In addition to extending sincere gratitude and greetings to the representatives from all walks of life who came to welcome them, he said: Today is the day that the peaceful liberation of Suiyuan enters a new stage. We must strive to realize Chairman Mao's instructions: unite as one, strive for progress, reform the old system, implement new policies, and struggle to build a new Suiyuan for the people.

Xinhua News Agency, Guisui, December 23rd: Fu Zuoyi, member of the Central People's Government and Minister of Water Resources, Chairman of the Suiyuan Military and Political Committee, and Commander of the Suiyuan Military Region, and Deng Baoshan, Chairman of the Gansu Provincial People's Government, arrived in Guisui by train from Beijing at 3:40 a.m. on December 22nd. Welcoming them at the station were Gao Kelin, Vice Chairman of the Military and Political Committee, Yao Zhe, Deputy Commander of the Suiyuan Military Region, Pei Zhouyu, Director of the Political Department of the Military Region, Su Qianyi, Deputy Secretary of the CCP Suiyuan Provincial Committee, Dong Qiwu, Chairman of the Suiyuan Provincial Government, Vice Chairmen Yang, Kui, and Sun, as well as directors and heads of departments of the Provincial Government and heads of provincial organs and organizations, totaling over a hundred people.

### 绥远省级各机关由绥东迁抵归绥　　傅作义将军于日前到达

1949-12-24
第1版()
专栏：

　　绥远省级各机关由绥东迁抵归绥
    傅作义将军于日前到达
    【新华社归绥二十三日电】原驻于绥东解放区的绥远人民政府、中国共产党绥远省委员会、人民解放军绥远军区各部、中国新民主主义青年团绥远省工作委员会、绥远省总工会筹备委员会、绥远省民主归女联合会筹备委员会、中苏友好协会绥远省分会筹备委员会等机关、团体干部一千二百余人，在绥远省军政委员会副主席、中国共产党绥远省委员会书记高克林及绥远军区副司令员姚哲、绥远人民政府副主席杨植林、奎璧等率领下，于十二月十七日由丰镇乘专车迁抵绥远省会归绥市。原驻归绥市的省府主席董其武及绥远军政委员会各委员、省政府各委员，机关、部队、工厂、学校、街区六十余单位代表八千余人在归绥车站欢迎。董其武在欢迎词中指出：此次欢迎，在本质上和平常的欢迎完全不同，因为大家给绥远人民带来了温暖与幸福。今后要以亲密团结、虚心学习、努力工作、劳动生产的具体行动来表示欢迎。继由高克林讲话。他除向前来欢迎的各界代表致诚恳的感谢和慰问外，并说：今天是绥远和平解放事业进入一个新阶段的日子。我们要努力实现毛主席的指示：团结一致，力求进步，改革旧制度，实行新政策，为建设人民的新绥远而奋斗。
    【新华社归绥二十三日电】中央人民政府委员兼水利部长、绥远军政委员会主席、绥远军区司令傅作义将军，甘肃省人民政府邓宝珊主席，于十二月二十二日早三时四十分，同车由京抵达归绥，到站欢迎的有军政委员会高克林副主席、绥远军区姚哲副司令员、军区政治部裴周玉主任、中共绥远省委苏谦益副书记、绥远省人民政府董主席、杨、奎、孙副主席及省府各厅、处长、省级机关团体负责人百余人。
