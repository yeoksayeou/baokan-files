Title: Our Army Liberates Jiange City; Song Xilian, Bandit, Captured in Western Sichuan
Author:
Date: 1949-12-22
Page: 1

　　Xinhua News Agency, Northwest Front, 21st: A unit of the People's Liberation Army advancing from the Northwest into Sichuan, on the 17th at 24:00, captured Jiange Pass in northern Sichuan in one fell swoop, capturing more than 300 enemy troops. On the 18th, they liberated Jiange County, south of Jiange Pass. Another unit of the Liberation Army made a light-equipped surprise attack on Bikou Town, bordering Gansu and Sichuan provinces, on the morning of the 15th, annihilating an enemy battalion after one hour of combat.
　　
　　Xinhua News Agency, Southwest Front, 21st: Song Xilian, the bandit chief of the Kuomintang Sichuan-Hunan-Hubei Pacification Headquarters, has been captured in the Emei area of western Sichuan. After a unit of the People's Liberation Army annihilated most of Song Xilian's bandit headquarters southwest of Emei on the 17th of this month, they continued to pursue southwest, capturing the bandit leader Song Xilian at Jinkouhe, 50 miles southwest of Emei, on the 19th, and seizing more than 30 vehicles.

### 我军解放剑阁城　川西俘获宋匪希濂

1949-12-22
第1版()
专栏：

　　我军解放剑阁城
    川西俘获宋匪希濂
    【新华社西北前线二十一日电】由西北向四川进军的人民解放军一部于十七日二十四时一举攻占川北的剑门关，俘敌三百余人，十八日又解放剑门关以南的剑阁县城。另部解放军于十五日上午轻装奔袭甘肃、四川两省交界的碧口镇，经一小时战斗，歼敌一个营。
    【新华社西南前线二十一日电】国民党川湘鄂绥署主任宋匪希濂，已在川西峨嵋地区被俘获。人民解放军某部于本月十七日在峨嵋西南歼灭宋匪希濂总部大部后，继即向西南穷追，十九日在峨嵋西南五十里之金口河将匪首宋希濂俘虏，并缴获汽车三十余辆。
