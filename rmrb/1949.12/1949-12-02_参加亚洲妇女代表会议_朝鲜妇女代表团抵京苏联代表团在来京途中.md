Title: Korean Women's Delegation Arrives in Beijing to Attend Asian Women's Representative Conference; Soviet Delegation En Route to Beijing
Author:
Date: 1949-12-02
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, December 1st—The Korean delegation to the Asian Women's Representative Conference, including Xu Zhenshu (Minister of Culture and Propaganda of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea) and Liu Yingjun (Chairman of the South Korean Democratic Women's League), a total of 32 people, arrived in our capital, Beijing, by train at 1:00 PM today. Also arriving was a Korean art troupe composed of 45 actors and artists. The delegation was warmly welcomed at the station by Deng Yingchao, Vice-Chairman of the All-China Democratic Women's Federation, Li Dequan, Yan Baohang representing Foreign Minister Zhou Enlai of the Central People's Government, Li Bozhao representing the All-China Federation of Literary and Art Circles, Xiao San, Ai Qing, and representatives from various people's organizations, totaling over 100 people. Deng Yingchao, Vice-Chairman of the All-China Democratic Women's Federation, delivered a welcome speech on behalf of the International Preparatory Committee of the Asian Women's Representative Conference and the National Women's Federation. Li Bozhao, representing the All-China Federation of Literary and Art Circles and the Beijing literary and art circles, also delivered a welcoming speech. Korean representatives Xu Zhenshu and Liu Yingjun gave replies. When the delegation passed through Shenyang yesterday, representatives from the Northeast Women's Federation, the Shenyang Municipal Women's Federation, the Northeast People's Government, the Shenyang Preparatory Committee of the Youth League, and the Korean Middle School, totaling over 100 people, went to the station to welcome them and held a welcoming ceremony. Guo Mingqiu and 14 other representatives from the Northeast attending the Asian Women's Representative Conference traveled to Beijing in the same train as the delegation.
Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, December 1st—As from Ashkhabad on November 30th, the Minister of Social Security of the Turkmen Republic reported: The Soviet women's delegation left Moscow for Beijing on November 29th to attend the Asian Women's Representative Conference. The composition of the delegation is as follows: Bafinovna, Vice-Chairman of the Soviet Women's Anti-Fascist Committee (head of the delegation); Aslanova, Deputy of the Supreme Soviet of the Soviet Union, educator of the Azerbaijan Republic; Tuta Nibisova; Professor Basanova, Director of the Physiology Department of the Alma-Ata Veterinary Research Institute; Ubaydulayeva, student of the Tashkent Agricultural Institute, Socialist Labor Hero; Toktoman Zhetova, a famous mass leader of the Kyrgyz Republic; Troitskaya, a well-known railway manager, Deputy Director of the Moscow Metro and member of the Soviet Women's Anti-Fascist Committee.

### 参加亚洲妇女代表会议　朝鲜妇女代表团抵京苏联代表团在来京途中

1949-12-02
第1版()
专栏：

　　参加亚洲妇女代表会议
   朝鲜妇女代表团抵京苏联代表团在来京途中
    【新华社北京一日电】出席亚洲妇女代表会议的朝鲜代表团许贞淑（朝鲜民主主义人民共和国文化宣传部部长）、刘英俊（南朝鲜民主妇女同盟委员长）等三十二人，已于今日下午一时乘车到达我国首都北京。同来的尚有由四十五个演员和艺术家组成的朝鲜文艺工作队。代表团在车站上曾受到热烈的欢迎，欢迎者有中华全国民主妇女联合会副主席邓颖超、李德全、中央人民政府外交部长周恩来代表阎宝航、中华全国文学艺术界联合会代表李伯钊、肖三、艾青及各人民团体代表一百余人。中华全国民主妇女联合会副主席邓颖超代表亚洲妇女代表会议国际筹委会全国妇联致欢迎词，中华全国文学艺术界联合会代表李伯钊代表全国文联及北京文艺界亦致词欢迎，朝鲜代表许贞淑、刘英俊致答词。代表团昨过沈时，东北妇联、沈市妇联、东北人民政府、青年团沈市筹委会、朝鲜中学等单位代表百余人赴车站欢迎，并举行欢迎会。东北出席亚洲妇女代表会议的代表郭明秋等十四人当与代表团同车来京。
    【新华社北京一日电】塔库曼共和国社会保险部长阿斯社莫斯科十一月三十日讯：苏联妇女代表团于十一月二十九日离莫斯科赴北京，参加亚洲妇女代表会议。代表团的组成如下：苏联妇女反法西斯委员会副主席巴菲诺娃（团长）；苏联最高苏维埃代表、阿塞拜疆共和国教育工作者阿斯拉诺娃；土塔尼比索娃；阿拉木图兽医研究所生理系主任巴萨诺娃教授；塔什干农业研究所学生、社会主义劳动英雄乌巴伊杜拉耶娃；吉尔吉斯共和国著名群众领袖托克托曼捷托娃；著名的铁路管理工作者、莫斯科地下铁道副局长及苏联妇女反法西斯委员会委员特罗伊茨卡娅。
