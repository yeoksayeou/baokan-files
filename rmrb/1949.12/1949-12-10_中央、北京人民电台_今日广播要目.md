Title: Central, Beijing People's Radio Today's Broadcast Highlights
Author:
Date: 1949-12-10
Page: 3

　　Central, Beijing People's Radio
  　Today's Broadcast Highlights
    [This newspaper reports] The Central People's Broadcasting Station this (10th) evening at 17:30, youth program: an article from the 27th issue of "Chinese Youth": In memory of Comrade Huang Cheng, the chairman of the Peiping Student Union during the "December 9th" Movement, who died in the Shangrao concentration camp after the Southern Anhui Incident. 18:00 First Arts and Entertainment Program: Ma Xiaorong sings the Henan Zhui Zi "Good Husband and Wife." 21:00 Second Arts and Entertainment Program: A music program celebrating the opening of the Asian Women's Representatives Conference.
    [Also reported] Beijing People's Radio 12:30 Staff Time: Luo Changpei speaks on "The Impressions of Education Workers on the Asian-Australian Trade Union Representatives Conference". 16:40 Women's Life Time: "Impressions After Seeing the Exhibition of the Life and Struggle of Asian Workers and Women". 19:15 Municipal Voice: ① A report from the Commercial Bureau "Regarding the Strengthening of Direct Retail Sales by the City's Grain, Coal and Iron, and Cotton Yarn and Cloth Companies to Guarantee the Livelihood of Citizens", ② "Several Hundred Medical Workers Successfully Complete the City's Epidemic Prevention Task".

### 中央、北京人民电台　今日广播要目

1949-12-10
第3版()
专栏：

　　中央、北京人民电台
  　今日广播要目
    【本报讯】中央人民广播电台今（十）晚十七点三十分青年节目：“中国青年”第二十七期上的一篇文章：追悼“一二九”时的北平学联主席，皖南事变后在上饶集中营中死难的黄诚同志。十八点第一次文艺节目：马小荣演唱河南坠子“好夫妻”。二十一点第二次文艺节目：庆祝亚洲妇女代表会议开幕的音乐节目。
    【又讯】北京人民电台十二点三十分职工时间：罗常培讲“教育工作者对于亚澳工会代表会议的感想”。十六点四十分妇女生活时间：“看了亚洲工人妇女生活斗争展览会以后的感想”。十九点十五分市政之声：①商业局“关于本市粮食、煤铁、花纱布三个分公司为了保障市民生活加强直接零售的报导”，②“几百个医务工作者胜利完成本市防疫的任务”。
