Title: Strengthen Sino-Soviet People's Solidarity: Soviet Women Present Precious Gifts; Presentation Ceremony Held Yesterday in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1949-12-16
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, 15th—To strengthen the solidarity between the Chinese and Soviet people, Soviet women presented China with precious exhibition items. The presentation ceremony was held at noon today in the capital. The head of the Soviet delegation to the Asian Women's Representative Conference, Bafinov, delivered a speech on behalf of Soviet women at the ceremony. She said that Soviet women were very happy about the victory of the Chinese people, and they presented China with some of the products personally made by them in their socialist labor to show their respect. Cai Chang, Chairman of the All-China Democratic Women's Federation, accepted the gift on behalf of the Chinese people and delivered a speech of thanks. She said that the Chinese people know that the Soviet Union has always been our most loyal friend for decades. The Chinese people can fully appreciate this great friendship and are learning from the Soviet Union with the greatest perseverance and determination. Cai Chang said that the gifts from the Soviet women will be transported to major cities across the country for exhibition, so that the people and women of the new China can have a deeper understanding of the Soviet Union and be encouraged to further strengthen the solidarity between the people of China and the Soviet Union, and strive to defend world peace. At the presentation ceremony, Soviet women representatives Harobova read a letter from the Soviet Women's Anti-Fascist Committee to the All-China Democratic Women's Federation, and Nikifilova read a letter from Soviet women working in the Red Army to Cai Chang, Chairman of the All-China Democratic Women's Federation. Both letters expressed the determination of Soviet women to strive for world peace and to protect the happiness of workers, and wished the Chinese people and women greater success in building the country. Xiao San, representative of the Sino-Soviet Friendship Association, also gave a speech, saying that the gifts from the Soviet women can not only tell Chinese women and all the people how to work, but also tell everyone how to achieve a happy life. Today, representatives from various countries attending the Asian Women's Representative Conference participated in the presentation ceremony. After the ceremony, the entire audience unanimously shouted: Long live the friendship between the people of China and the Soviet Union!

### 加强中苏人民团结　苏联妇女赠我珍贵礼品　昨在京举行赠予典礼

1949-12-16
第1版()
专栏：

　　加强中苏人民团结
  　苏联妇女赠我珍贵礼品
  　昨在京举行赠予典礼
    【新华社北京十五日电】苏联妇女以珍重的展览品赠送给中国，赠予典礼今天中午十二时在首都举行。出席亚洲妇女代表会议的苏联代表团团长巴菲诺娃在典礼上代表苏联妇女致词，她说苏联的妇女对于中国人民的胜利非常欢欣，她们将亲身参加社会主义劳动的一部分制造品赠送给中国，以表示敬意。中华全国民主妇女联合会主席蔡畅代表受礼，并致谢词。她说中国人民都知道，几十年来苏联一直是我们最忠实的朋友，中国人民完全能够体会这种伟大的友情，并正在以最大的毅力和恒心向苏联学习。蔡畅说苏联妇女赠送的礼品将要运到全国各大城市展览，让新中国的人民和妇女更深刻地认识苏联，并且得到鼓舞，以进一步加强中苏两国人民的团结，为保卫世界持久和平而奋斗。在赠予典礼上，苏联妇女代表哈罗波娃宣读了苏联妇女反法西斯委员会写给中华全国民主妇联的一封信，尼克菲洛娃宣读了苏联妇女在红军中工作者写给中华全国民主妇联主席蔡畅的一封信，两信一致表示苏联妇女为争取世界和平和保卫劳动者的幸福而斗争的决心，并祝中国人民和妇女在建设国家中获得更大的成功。中苏友好协会总会代表肖三也发表讲演，他说苏联妇女赠送的礼品，不仅可以告诉中国妇女和全体人民怎样劳动，而且可以告诉大家怎样达到幸福的生活。今天参加赠予典礼的有亚洲妇女代表会议的各国代表。当受礼完毕后，全场一致高呼中苏两国人民友好万岁！
