Title: Generals Wang Boxun and Zhang Tao Lead Uprising in Qianxi
Author:
Date: 1949-12-22
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, Southwest Front, December 21st: General Wang Boxun, former Deputy Commander of the Kuomintang 19th Army Corps, and General Zhang Tao, Commander of the 89th Army, recently contacted the People's Liberation Army stationed in Guizhou to request an uprising. Liu Bocheng and Deng Xiaoping, Commanders and Political Commissar of the Second Field Army of the People's Liberation Army, replied by telegram, expressing their welcome. At this moment, Generals Wang Boxun and Zhang Tao have led the original 328th and 343rd Divisions of the 89th Army in the Puan and Panxian areas of western Guizhou to formally announce their break from the Kuomintang bandit gang government and are obeying orders to move to the designated areas.

### 王伯勋张涛两将军率部起义黔西

1949-12-22
第1版()
专栏：

　　王伯勋张涛两将军率部起义黔西
    【新华社西南前线二十一日电】原国民党十九兵团副司令王伯勋将军及八十九军军长张涛将军日前曾向驻在贵州的人民解放军接洽要求起义，业经人民解放军第二野战军司令员刘伯承和政治委员邓小平两将军复电表示欢迎。刻王伯勋、张涛两将军已率原八十九军三二八师、三四三师在贵州西部之普安、盘县地区正式宣布脱离国民党匪帮政府，并遵令开到指定地区。
