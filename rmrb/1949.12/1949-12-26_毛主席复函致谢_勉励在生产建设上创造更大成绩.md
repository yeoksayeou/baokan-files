Author:
Date: 1949-12-26
Page: 1

Chairman Mao's Reply Expresses Gratitude
Encourages Greater Achievements in Production and Construction

[This newspaper reported] Chairman Mao, after receiving the letter of respect signed by more than 510,000 people from all walks of life in Lüda, replied to the people of Lüda on the first of this month to express his gratitude, and encouraged the people of Lüda to work harder in production and construction and create greater achievements. Kang Minzhuang, Deputy Minister of the Propaganda Department of the CPC Lüda District Committee, brought back the letter. The original text of Chairman Mao's reply is as follows:

Comrades of the Lüda District Committee, and through you to the people of Lüda: The signed letter and gifts brought by Comrade Kang Minzhuang have been received. Thank you for your and the people of Lüda! I hope you will work harder in production and construction and create greater achievements!

　　　　　　　　　Mao Zedong
　　　　　　　　　December 1, 1949

### 毛主席复函致谢　勉励在生产建设上创造更大成绩

1949-12-26
第1版()
专栏：

　　毛主席复函致谢
　  勉励在生产建设上创造更大成绩
    【本报讯】毛主席在接到旅大各界五十一万余人民签名致敬书以后，于本月一日复函旅大人民致谢，并勉励旅大人民在生产建设上更加努力，创造更大成绩。该信由中共旅大区党委宣传部副部长康敏庄带回。毛主席的复信原文如下：
    旅大区党委同志们，并转旅大区人民：由康敏庄同志带来的签名书及礼品，均已收到，谢谢你们和旅大的人民！希望你们在生产建设上更加努力，创造更大的成绩！
    　　　　　　　　　毛泽东
    　　一九四九年十二月一日
