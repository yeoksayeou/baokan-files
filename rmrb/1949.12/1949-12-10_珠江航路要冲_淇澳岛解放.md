Title: Key Point on the Pearl River Route: Qi'ao Island Liberated
Author:
Date: 1949-12-10
Page: 1

Pearl River Route Key Point

　　Qi'ao Island Liberated
　　【Xinhua News Agency, South China Front, 9th】A unit of the People's Liberation Army crossed the sea at dawn on the 5th and liberated Qi'ao Island, which belongs to Zhongshan County. The island is located in the Lingding Ocean, east of Zhongshan County, and controls a key point on the Pearl River route.

### 珠江航路要冲　淇澳岛解放

1949-12-10
第1版()
专栏：

　　珠江航路要冲
  　淇澳岛解放
    【新华社华南前线九日电】人民解放军一部于五日拂晓渡海解放中山县属之淇澳岛。该岛位于中山县东面之伶仃洋上，扼珠江航路要冲。
