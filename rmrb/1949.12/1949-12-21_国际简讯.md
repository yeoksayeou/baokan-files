International Briefs

　　▲Hoxha, Chairman of the Council of Ministers and Minister of Foreign Affairs of the People's Republic of Albania, received a telegram on the 10th from Dodinger, Minister of Foreign Affairs of the German Democratic Republic, stating that the Provisional Government of the German Democratic Republic agreed to establish diplomatic relations between the People's Republic of Albania and the German Democratic Republic.
    ▲Gross, the Ambassador of the German Democratic Republic to Czechoslovakia, presented his credentials to Czechoslovak President Gottwald on the 12th.
    ▲The World Federation of Trade Unions recently sent a strongly worded protest to Brazilian President Dutra, condemning the vicious attacks by Brazilian police on workers, and demanding permission to freely exercise trade union rights and to guarantee the freedom of trade union organizations and their leaders.
    ▲The Far Eastern Commission, dominated by the United States and its followers, elected U.S. representative Hamilton as its chairman on the 8th. Hamilton succeeded McCoy, who resigned on November 12th, as the chief U.S. representative to the Commission. The Far Eastern Commission originally consisted of eleven countries. After the Commission admitted Burma and Pakistan as members on November 17th, its membership increased to thirteen countries.
　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　(Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, 19th)

### 国际简讯

1949-12-21
第5版()
专栏：国际简讯

　　国际简讯
    ▲阿尔巴尼亚人民共和国部长会议主席兼外交部长霍查，于十日接到德意志民主共和国外交部长窦丁格尔的电报称，德意志民主共和国临时政府同意建立阿尔巴尼亚人民共和国和德意志民主共和国之间的外交关系。
    ▲德意志民主共和国驻捷大使格罗斯，于十二日向捷克斯洛伐克总统哥特瓦尔德呈递国书。
    ▲世界工会联合会最近将一件措词有力的抗议书，送达巴西总统杜特拉，谴责巴西警察对工人的恶毒袭击，并要求准许自由执行工会权利，及保障工会组织及其领袖们的自由。
    ▲美国及其追随者占据多数的十三国远东委员会，于八日选出美国代表韩密尔顿为该会主席。韩密尔顿系于十一月十二日继辞职的麦考伊出任美国驻该会首席代表。按远东委员会原为十一国组成，在该委员会于十一月十七日通过缅甸、巴基斯坦为会员国后，其会员国已增至十三国。
    　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　（新华社北京十九日电）
