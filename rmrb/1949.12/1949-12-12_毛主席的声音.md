Title: Chairman Mao's Voice
Author: Zhang Ming
Date: 1949-12-12
Page: 6

　　Chairman Mao's Voice
    Zhang Ming
    We are accustomed to hearing your voice
    Whenever
    The most difficult period of the revolution
    We then heard your voice
    Your voice
    As loud as a bell
    As firm as Mount Tai
    Your voice
    Pointed out the path for us
    Enabled us to see the bright future
    History has proven
    Every time you issue a voice
    It brought confidence to the people of the whole country
    Overcame the difficult situation
    And obtained happiness and light
    Today
    In the days when the new China begins comprehensive construction
    You again speak to all the Chinese people
    Issued your loud and firm voice:
    "There are difficulties, there are solutions, there is hope."
    "It is estimated that next year will be better than this year,
    And the economy may be fully recovered in three to five years."
    Your words full of confidence
    Brought us great strength
    Your voice
    Like a bridge
    We step on it
    Inevitably transition to the shore of victory
    Your voice
    Like a ladder
    We climb it
    Inevitably reach the peak of victory
    Your voice
    Is power
    Your voice
    Is the solution
    Your voice
    Is hope
    Your voice
    Is victory
    Chairman Mao, ah, Chairman Mao
    Let us always follow your voice
    Advance in victory
　　　　　　　　　　　　 (Changxindian, evening of December 7th)

### 毛主席的声音
张铭
1949-12-12
第6版()
专栏：

　　毛主席的声音
    张铭
    我们听惯了你的声音
    每当
    革命最艰难的时期
    我们便听到了你的声音
    你的声音
    象洪钟那样响亮
    象泰山那样坚定
    你的声音
    给我们指出了道路
    使我们看到了光明的前程
    历史证明了
    你每一次发出的声音
    都给全国人民带来了信心
    克服了危难的局面
    得到幸福、光明
    今天
    在新中国开始全面建设的日子
    你又向全中国人民
    发出了你那洪亮而坚定的声音：
    “有困难，有办法，有希望。”
    “估计明年要比今年好，
    三、五年经济可能完全恢复。”
    你那充满信心的话
    给我们带来了伟大的力量
    你的声音
    象一座桥梁
    我们踏着它
    必然的过渡到胜利的彼岸
    你的声音
    象一架梯子
    我们攀登着它
    必然的达到胜利的峰顶
    你的声音
    就是力量
    你的声音
    就是办法
    你的声音
    就是希望
    你的声音
    就是胜利
    毛主席啊毛主席
    让我们永远追随着你的声音
    在胜利中前进
    　　　　　　　　　　　　 （十二月七日晚长辛店）
