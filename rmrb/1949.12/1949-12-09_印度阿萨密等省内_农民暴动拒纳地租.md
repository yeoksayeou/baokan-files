Title: Peasant Uprising in Assam and Other Provinces of India Refuses to Pay Land Rent
Author:
Date: 1949-12-09
Page: 4
　　
    Xinhua News Agency, Delhi, December 3rd: According to TASS, peasant uprisings have occurred in Assam province and the Amritsar, Gadaspur, and Kandela districts of East Punjab province in India. Peasants are refusing to pay exploitative land rent to landlords, and they are resolutely resisting the police sent to suppress them.

### 印度阿萨密等省内　农民暴动拒纳地租

1949-12-09
第4版()
专栏：

　　印度阿萨密等省内
  　农民暴动拒纳地租
    【新华社北京六日电】据塔斯社德里三日讯：印度阿萨密省及东旁遮普省的阿姆利则，加达斯帕和堪德拉区都发生农民暴动。农民拒绝向地主缴纳奴役的地租，他们坚决抵抗派来镇压他们的警察。
