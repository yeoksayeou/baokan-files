Title: Central, Beijing People's Radio Today's Broadcast Highlights
Author:
Date: 1949-12-07
Page: 3

　　Central, Beijing People's Radio
  　Today's Broadcast Highlights
    [Our Newspaper] Central People's Broadcasting Station tonight (7th) at 17:30 Youth Time: Broadcasting "A Brief Discussion on the Production Leadership of Rural Youth" from the 27th issue of "China Youth". 18:30 Lecture on Common Sense of Natural Science: "Spring, Summer, Autumn, Winter". 18:00 First Literary Program: Jingyun Dagu "Labor Hero Zhang Zifu". 21:00 Second Literary Program, Introduction to Folk Music of Lüda.
    [Furthermore] Beijing People's Radio 12:30 Staff Time: A brief overview of the work of the Preparatory Committee of the Municipal Federation of Trade Unions in the second half of November. 16:15 Children's Time: Sun Jingxiu continues to tell the story of "Paul". 16:45 Youth Time: Hu Sheng's lecture record "On the Question of Why Study". 17:00 Music Program: The Eighth Middle School sings six new songs.

### 中央、北京人民电台　今日广播要目

1949-12-07
第3版()
专栏：

　　中央、北京人民电台
  　今日广播要目
    【本报讯】中央人民广播电台今（七）晚十七点三十分青年时间：播送“中国青年”二十七期刊载的“略谈农村青年的生产领导问题”。十八点三十分自然科学常识讲座：“春、夏、秋、冬”。十八点第一次文艺节目：京韵大鼓“劳动英雄张子富”。二十一点第二次文艺节目，旅大民间音乐介绍。
