Title: Asian Workers' and Women's Living Exhibition Closes; Over 100,000 People Visited; Seeing the Achievements of the Soviet Union and Other Countries Strengthens Confidence in Construction
Author:
Date: 1949-12-28
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, December 27th: The Asian Workers', Women's Lives and Struggles Exhibition held in the capital closed as scheduled on the 25th. Over the past twenty days, a total of 106,400 people visited, including 317 units in organized groups. The exhibition was well-received by the audience. Many visitors expressed that seeing the rapid economic and cultural construction of the Soviet Union and other people's democratic countries has further strengthened our confidence in the future construction of the new China. In particular, the display room of gifts presented by Soviet women to Chinese women was very popular among the audience. The passage to the display room was often so crowded that it was impossible to pass through. People admired the gifts from Soviet friends one by one and were reluctant to leave for a long time.

Korean Trade Union Delegation Sends Letter Thanking Us for Warm Reception

Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, December 27th: Choi Choon-duk, head of the Korean delegation to the Asian-Australian Trade Union Conference, who returned to Korea on the 18th of this month, sent a letter to Liu Ningyi, vice chairman of the All-China Federation of Trade Unions, expressing his gratitude for the warm reception of the Chinese working class during the conference and visit. The telegram is as follows: To Comrade Liu Ningyi, Vice Chairman of the All-China Federation of Trade Unions:

During the conference and our visit to major cities in China, we were warmly welcomed and cordially received by Chinese comrades. We express our deep gratitude for this internationalist fraternal friendship and wish the heroic Chinese working class, who are fighting for the construction of a new China, greater victories!

	Choi Choon-duk, Head of the Korean Delegation
	December 18, 1949

### 亚洲工人妇女生活展览会闭幕　参观群众共十万余人  看了苏联等国的成就增强建设信心

1949-12-28
第1版()
专栏：

　　亚洲工人妇女生活展览会闭幕
　  参观群众共十万余人
    看了苏联等国的成就增强建设信心
    【新华社北京二十七日电】在首都举行的亚洲工人、妇女生活与斗争展览会，已于二十五日如期闭幕。二十天来参观的群众共达十万六千四百余人，其中集体参观的共有三百一十七个单位。这次展览会很得观众好评。许多参观者表示：看到苏联及各人民民主国家经济、文化建设突飞猛进的情景，更增强了我们对新中国建设前途的信心。尤其是苏联妇女赠送中国妇女的礼品的陈列室，极得观众喜爱，通往该陈列室的通道时常挤得无法通过，人们逐件地欣赏着苏联友人赠送的礼品，久久不肯离去。
    朝鲜工会代表团函谢我热烈招待
    【新华社北京二十七日电】于本月十八日返抵朝鲜的参加亚澳工会代表会议的朝鲜代表团团长崔＠德，致函中华全国总工会副主席刘宁一，对会议和参观期间中国工人阶级的热情招待，表示感谢。电文如下：中华全国总工会副主席刘宁一同志：
    在大会与参观中国各大都市期间，我们受到中国同志们的热烈欢迎和亲切招待。对这种国际主义的兄弟般友谊，我们深表谢意，并祝福为建设新中国而奋斗的英勇的中国工人阶级争取更伟大的胜利！
  　　　　　　　　朝鲜代表团团长崔＠德
    一九四九年十二月十八日
