Xinhua Radio Tonight's Program

[This newspaper dispatch] Beijing Xinhua Broadcasting Station's fifth broadcast time will be moved to the first half hour, starting today (the 1st). At 17:30 and 18:45 this evening, "Indian Women's Struggle for Human Rights, Peace, and True Independence" will be broadcast in Mandarin and Japanese respectively. At 18:30, the popular science lecture will be: "Birth, Growth, Aging, Illness, and Death." At 18:00, the first art program: Beijing Drum Ballad "Heroic Daughter Wang Guiying." At 21:00, the second art program: the second half of the new Ping Opera "Tears of a Poor Girl."

[Another dispatch] On the second channel, at 12:30, during the workers' time, worker representatives Li Shumin and Zhao Zhiqi will talk about their gains and thoughts from attending the First Beijing Women's Congress. In the evening at 16:15, during children's time, the children of the Fourth District Central National Primary School will sing. At 17:00, new song practice, continuing to teach "Railway Workers' Song."

### 新华电台今晚要目

1949-12-01
第3版()
专栏：

　　新华电台今晚要目
    【本报讯】北京新华广播电台第五次播音时间，从今（一）天起提到前半点钟。今晚十七点三十分和十八点四十五分分别用国语和日语播送一“印度妇女为争取人权、和平及真正独立的斗争”。十八点三十分自然科学常识讲座：“生、长、衰、老、病、死”。十八点第一次文艺节目：京韵大鼓。“女儿英雄王桂英”，二十一点第二次文艺节目：新评戏“贫女泪”后半段。
    【又讯】第二台十二时三十分职工时间，工人代表李淑敏和赵质奇讲参加北京市第一次妇女大会的收获和感想。晚间十六时十五分儿童时间，由第四区中心国民小学小朋友们唱歌。十七时新歌练习，继续教唱“铁路工人歌”。
