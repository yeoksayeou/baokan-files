Title: Nanjing Ten Scientific Organizations Hold Joint Annual Meeting
Author:
Date: 1949-12-14
Page: 3
Xinhua News Agency, Nanjing, December 13th: Ten scientific organizations in Nanjing held a joint annual meeting on the 10th and 11th at the former Academia Sinica. Participants included the Nanjing branch of the China Society for the Study of Natural Sciences, the Nanjing Pharmaceutical Association, as well as the Nanjing branches of the Chinese Astronomical Society, the Chinese Meteorological Society, the Chinese Soil Science Society, the Chinese Zoological Society, the Chinese Chemical Society, the Chinese Geographical Society, the Chinese Geophysical Society, and the Chinese Botanical Society.

### 南京十个科学团体举行联合年会

1949-12-14
第3版()
专栏：

　　南京十个科学团体举行联合年会
    【新华社南京十三日电】南京十个科学团体在十、十一两日在原中央研究院举行联合年会，参加的有中华自然科学社南京分社、南京药学会、以及中国天文学会、中国气象学会、中国土壤学会、中国动物学会、中国化学会、中国地理学会、中国地球物理学会、中国植物学会等会的南京分会。
