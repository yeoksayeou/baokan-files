Title: Polish, Czechoslovak, Mongolian, Korean, and Austrian Delegations Arrive in Soviet Capital to Offer Birthday Greetings

Author:
Date: 1949-12-21
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, 20th. (TASS dispatch) Delegations from Poland, Czechoslovakia, Mongolia, Korea, Austria and other countries' working people, to offer congratulations on Marshal Stalin's 70th birthday, have arrived in Moscow successively. The Polish Republic Labor People's Delegation, headed by Juzwiak, a member of the Central Committee of the Polish United Workers' Party, arrived in Moscow on the 19th. The delegations from Czechoslovakia, Mongolia, and Korea also arrived on the same day. Among the members of the Korean delegation are Kim Tu-bong, Kim Tal-hyon, and Li Cheng-yop. The Austrian delegation arrived in Moscow on the 18th. The head of the delegation is Kolleni and Feernberg. Upon arriving at the station, all delegations were greeted by representatives of the Committee for Celebrating Stalin's 70th Birthday organized by the Presidium of the Supreme Soviet of the Soviet Union, representatives of the Moscow Labor People's Soviet, the Soviet Ministry of Foreign Affairs, representatives of the capital's public organizations, and the heads of the respective embassies or legations in Moscow.

### 波、捷、蒙、朝、奥祝寿代表团抵苏京

1949-12-21
第4版()
专栏：

　　波、捷、蒙、朝、奥祝寿代表团抵苏京
    【新华社北京二十日电】综合塔斯社消息：波、捷、蒙、朝、奥等国劳动人民祝贺斯大林大元帅七十寿辰的代表团，已陆续抵达莫斯科。以波兰统一工人党中委尤兹维亚克为首的波兰共和国劳动人民代表团，系十九日抵达莫斯科，捷、蒙、朝三国的代表团亦于同日到达。朝鲜代表团中有金枓奉、金达铉和李承烨。奥地利的代表团于十八日到达莫斯科。该代表团团长为柯伦尼与费恩柏。各代表团到达车站时，均受到苏联最高苏维埃主席团所组织的庆祝斯大林七十寿辰委员会的代表，莫斯科劳动人民代表苏维埃、苏联外交部与首都公共团体的代表以及各该国驻莫斯科大使馆或公使馆首长的欢迎。
