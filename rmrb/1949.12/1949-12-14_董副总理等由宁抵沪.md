Title: Vice Premier Dong and Others Arrive in Shanghai from Nanjing
Author:
Date: 1949-12-14
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Shanghai, December 13th: Dong Biwu, Vice Premier of the Central People's Government and head of the East China Working Group of the Government Administration Council's Reception Guidance Committee, along with Deputy Head Nan Hanchen and over twenty others, arrived in Shanghai by train from Nanjing at 3:30 AM on the 12th.  Rao Shushi, Chairman of the East China Military and Political Committee, Chen Yi, Mayor of Shanghai, and representatives from various circles including Zeng Shan, Pan Hannian, Sheng Pi Hua, Liu Xiao, Liu Changsheng, Pan Zhenya, and Hu Zi Ying, numbering over fifty people, went to the station to welcome them.  The main task of the group's arrival in Shanghai is to comprehensively guide and handle the reception work of the former Kuomintang government's central organs, personnel, archives, books, property, and materials in the East China region.

### 董副总理等由宁抵沪

1949-12-14
第1版()
专栏：

　　董副总理等由宁抵沪
    【新华社上海十三日电】中央人民政府政务院指导接收工作委员会华东区工作团团长董必武副总理、副团长南汉宸等一行二十余人于十二日晨三时半由宁乘车抵沪。华东军政委员会主席饶漱石、上海市陈毅市长及曾山、潘汉年、盛丕华、刘晓、刘长胜、潘震亚、胡子婴等各界代表五十余人赴车站欢迎。该团来沪的主要任务为统筹指导和处理华东地区前国民党政府中央各机关、人员、档案、图书、财产、物资等接收工作。
