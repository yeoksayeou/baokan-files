Title: US 400,000 Coal Miners Demand Wage Increase and Stage Strike
Author:
Date: 1949-12-06
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, New York Dispatch, December 5th: According to a report from the Electric News Agency in New York, 400,000 American coal miners went on strike again on the 1st. The American coal miners had previously initiated a strike on September 19th, demanding increased wages and welfare benefits. After 52 days of struggle, they temporarily resumed work on November 9th under the decision of United Mine Workers of America President Lewis. However, during the three weeks of resumption of work, the mine owners still refused to accept the workers' reasonable demands, forcing the workers to strike again.

### 美四十万煤矿工要求增资大罢工

1949-12-06
第4版()
专栏：

　　美四十万煤矿工要求增资大罢工
    【新华社北京五日电】据电通社纽约讯：美国四十万煤矿工人又于一日起实产行罢工。按美国煤矿工人为要求增加工资及福利金，曾于九月十九日开始罢工，坚持了五十二天的斗争，而于十一月九日在联合矿工工会主席路易斯的决定之下，暂时恢复工作。但在三周的复工期间，矿主们仍拒绝接受工人的合理要求，工人们不得不再行罢工。
