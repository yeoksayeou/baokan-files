Title: People from All Walks of Life in Wuhan, Hangzhou, Jinan, and Lanzhou Cheer for Chairman Mao's Visit to the Soviet Union, Deeply Believing That Sino-Soviet Friendship Will Be Further Consolidated
Author:
Date: 1949-12-26
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, December 25th: People from all walks of life in Wuhan, Hangzhou, Jinan, Lanzhou and other places are encouraged and excited by Chairman Mao's visit to the Soviet Union. They all believe that Chairman Mao's visit will have great importance in strengthening the friendship between the people of China and the Soviet Union and strengthening the international united front for peace and democracy. Zhao Min, director of the Wuhan Federation of Trade Unions, said: "The meeting between Chairman Mao and Marshal Stalin marks the further consolidation and development of the great friendship between the people of China and the Soviet Union, and marks the closer unity between the Chinese working class and the Soviet people." Huang Tie of the Propaganda Department of the Wuhan Democratic Women's Federation said: Chairman Mao's visit to the Soviet Union and his meeting with Marshal Stalin is a great encouragement to the liberation struggle of the people and women of China and Asia. The person in charge of the Wuhan Student Union said: "We should step up the study of the Soviet Union's nation-building experience to celebrate Chairman Mao's visit to the Soviet Union, a great happy event." Li Daigeng, deputy director of the Preparatory Committee of the Hangzhou Federation of Trade Unions, believes that Chairman Mao's visit to the Soviet Union "has increased infinite confidence and strength for building a new China and striving for lasting world peace and people's democracy." Zhou Lixing, director of the Preparatory Committee of the Democratic Youth Federation, and Yuan Weizi, a teacher from Hangzhou Normal School who attended the provincial normal school conference, also made speeches, unanimously expressing their celebration. Li Lin, director of the Shandong Federation of Trade Unions, said: This is the greatest happy event among our happy events. Shandong workers will organize more extensively and learn from the Soviet Union's nation-building example. Yu Zengren, director of the Fourth Factory of the Jinan Industrial Bureau, said that this time Chairman Mao's visit to the Soviet Union brings our workers' infinite respect to Marshal Stalin. Zhang Bochuan, vice president of Huadong University in Jinan, said: After we saw this news, all the teachers and students were very excited. With the people of the world and the great Soviet Union as our loyal friends, our future is bright and promising. The students of the school once beat gongs and drums to celebrate Chairman Mao's visit to the Soviet Union. Li Jianzhen, director of the Shandong Democratic Women's Federation, said: "The friendship and unity between China and the Soviet Union will become closer from now on." The Preparatory Committee of the Jinan Federation of Industry and Commerce, Miao Hainan, manager of Chentong Yarn Factory, and Yang Guangmin, a farmer in the Eighth District, also made speeches, congratulating Chairman Mao on his visit to the Soviet Union. All circles in Lanzhou believe that Chairman Mao's visit will strengthen the friendship between the two major countries of China and the Soviet Union. Zhou Fuzhi, Secretary-General of the Standing Committee of the People's Representative Conference of the city, believes that Chairman Mao's visit to the Soviet Union will further strengthen the world peace camp.

### 武汉杭州济南兰州各界人民  为毛主席访苏同声欢呼  深信中苏友谊将更加巩固

1949-12-26
第1版()
专栏：

　　武汉杭州济南兰州各界人民
    为毛主席访苏
    同声欢呼深信中苏友谊将更加巩固
    【新华社北京二十五日电】武汉、杭州、济南、兰州等地各界人民为毛主席访苏而鼓舞振奋，一致认为毛主席此行对于加强中苏两国人民友谊和加强国际和平民主统一战线将有极大重要性。武汉市总工会主任赵敏说：“毛主席和斯大林大元帅的会见，标志着中苏两国人民伟大友谊的更加巩固和发展，标志着中国工人阶级与苏联人民更紧密的团结。”武汉市民主妇女联合会宣传部黄铁说：毛主席到苏联和斯大林元帅会见，对于中国和亚洲的人民和妇女大众的解放斗争，是一个巨大的鼓舞。武汉市学生联合会负责人说：“我们应该加紧学习苏联的建国经验，来庆祝毛主席访苏这件大喜事。”杭州市总工会筹备会副主任李代耕认为毛主席的访苏“对于建设新中国和争取世界持久和平及人民民主增加了无限的信心和力量。”民主青联筹委会主任周力行及出席全省师范学校会议的杭州师范教师袁微子都发表谈话，一致表示欢庆。山东省总工会主任李林说：这是我们喜事中的大喜事，山东工人将更广泛地组织起来，学习苏联的建国榜样。济南工业局第四厂厂长于增仁表示，这次毛主席访苏，把我们工人无限的敬意带给斯大林大元帅。济南华东大学副校长张勃川说：我们看到这个消息以后，全体师生甚为兴奋。我们有全世界的人民和伟大的苏联作为我们的忠实朋友，我们的前途是远大而光明的。该校学生们曾经敲起锣鼓来庆祝毛主席访苏。山东民主妇联主任李坚贞说：“中苏两国友谊团结，从此将更加亲密。”济南市工商联合会筹备会及成通纱厂经理苗海南、八区农民杨广民均发表谈话，庆幸毛主席访苏。兰州市各界相信毛主席此行将加强中苏两大国的友谊。该市各界人民代表会议常委会秘书长周服之认为毛主席的访苏，将进一步加强世界和平阵线。
