Title: CPC Central Committee North China Bureau Holds Birthday Celebration; Propaganda Department Minister Zhang Panshi Delivers Report
Author:
Date: 1949-12-22
Page: 2

Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, December 21st: The CPC Central Committee North China Bureau held a meeting today to celebrate Stalin's 70th birthday. After Organization Department Minister Wang Congwu reported on the significance of the meeting, Propaganda Department Minister Zhang Panshi delivered a report. He explained, from history, especially at crucial moments in the Chinese revolution, the great help Stalin gave to the Chinese revolution. He earnestly told everyone: we must, as Chairman Mao instructed, celebrate him, support him, and learn from him, and also greatly promote him. He said that Chairman Mao's learning of Stalin's ideological methods is our model, and we must follow Chairman Mao in learning from Stalin.

### 中共中央华北局举行祝寿大会　宣传部部长张磐石作报告

1949-12-22
第2版()
专栏：

　　中共中央华北局举行祝寿大会
    宣传部部长张磐石作报告
    【新华社北京二十一日电】中共中央华北局于今日举行庆祝斯大林七十寿辰大会。组织部部长王从吾报告开会意义后，由宣传部部长张磐石作报告。他从历史上特别是中国革命的重要关头，说明了斯大林对于中国革命的伟大帮助。他谆谆告诉大家：要如毛主席所指示的要庆祝他、拥护他、学习他，还要大大地宣传他。他说，毛主席学习斯大林的思想方法是我们的典范，我们必须跟着毛主席向斯大林学习。
