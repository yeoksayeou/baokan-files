　　Congress Declaration
    The congress, amidst thunderous applause from all the representatives, adopted a telegram congratulating Stalin on his seventieth birthday.
    The declaration passed by the congress stated: The federation unites agricultural and forestry workers from all countries and their national organizations, regardless of race, nationality, creed, ideology, or political views, and regardless of whether their trade unions are affiliated with the World Federation of Trade Unions. The declaration listed the current tasks of the federation as follows: to unite the organizations of agricultural and forestry workers worldwide, to enhance the fraternal and regular cooperation between working people, to defend and develop their democratic rights, to actively participate in the struggle for peace, to support the cooperative movement, to implement land reform, to oppose exploitation, to oppose unemployment, to fight for the right to strike and shorter working days, and to oppose barbaric forced labor on farms, to strive for equal pay for equal work, to strive for social insurance, and so on. The declaration concluded by calling on the hundreds of millions of people working on the land in Europe, America, Asia, and Australia to unite in a single front under the banner of the World Federation of Trade Unions, and called on all agricultural workers worldwide to join the federation.

### 大会宣言

1949-12-23
第4版()
专栏：

　　大会宣言
    大会在全体代表风暴般的掌声中，通过了祝贺斯大林七十寿辰的电文。
    大会通过的宣言称：联合会团结各国的农业与林木业工人及其全国性的组织，不分种族、国别、信仰、思想与政见，也不论他们的工会是否隶属于世界工联。宣言列举联合会当前的任务如下：团结全世界农业与林木业工人的组织，增进劳动人民之间兄弟般的经常合作，保卫并发展他们的民主权利，积极参加争取和平的斗争，支持合作社运动，实行土地改革，反对剥削，反对失业，为罢工权利与缩短工作日而斗争，并反对农场上野蛮的强迫劳动，争取同工同酬，争取社会保险等等。宣言最后号召欧洲、美洲、亚洲和澳洲在土地上工作的亿万人民，在世界工联的旗帜下团结在一条阵线中，并号召全世界的农业工人都参加联合会。
