Title: The 13th Meeting of the Government Administration Council Approves Five Nomination Lists
Author:
Date: 1949-12-31
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, December 30th: The 13th Government Administration Council meeting of the Government Administration Council of the Central People's Government approved the following five nomination lists:

　　　　I. List of Directors-General of the Bureaus of the People's Supervision Commission of the Government Administration Council of the Central People's Government

　　Director-General of the First Bureau: Zhang Muyao (concurrent)
　　Director-General of the Second Bureau: Gan Cisen, currently an alternate member of the Central Committee of the Revolutionary Committee of the Chinese Kuomintang.
　　Director-General of the Third Bureau: Zhang Xiuyan (concurrent)

　　　　II. List of Personnel of the Ministry of Communications of the Central People's Government

　　Deputy Director of the General Office: Yang Yitang, currently a member of the Central Executive Committee of the Chinese Peasants' and Workers' Democratic Party.
　　Director of the Highway General Administration: Xu Binru, formerly Director of the Highway Bureau of the Northeast People's Government.
　　Deputy Directors: Cao Chengzong, formerly Director of the Highway General Administration of the Communications Department of the North China People's Government.
　　　　　　　　　Wang Yifan, currently a member of the Central Executive Committee of the Chinese Peasants' and Workers' Democratic Party.
　　　　　　　　　Feng Yujiu, formerly Deputy Director of the Highway General Administration of the Communications Department of the North China People's Government.
　　Director of the Navigation General Administration: Zhang Wen'ang (concurrent)
　　Deputy Directors: Yu Mei, formerly Deputy Minister of the Transportation Department and Director of the Navigation Bureau of the East China Liberated Area.
　　　　　　　　　Wang Jiyi, currently a member of the Central Executive Committee of the Chinese Peasants' and Workers' Democratic Party.
　　　　　　　　　Gao Yuan, formerly Chief of the Engineering Section of the Communications Department of the North China People's Government.

　　　　III. List of Personnel of the Beijing Municipal People's Government

　　Deputy Secretary-General: Li Gongxia, formerly Deputy Secretary-General of the Beijing Military Control Commission.
　　Director of the Financial and Economic Committee: Zhang Youyu (concurrent)
　　　　　　　　　　　　　Deputy Director: Cheng Hongyi (concurrent)
　　Deputy Director of the Civil Affairs Bureau: Ma Yuhuai, formerly Director of the Hui People's Work Committee of the Beijing Municipal People's Government.
　　Deputy Director of the Industry Bureau: Yu Diqing, formerly Deputy Director of the General Office and Chief of the Personnel Section of the Beijing Municipal People's Government.
　　Director of the Public Enemy Property Management Bureau: Xue Zizheng (concurrent)
　　　　　　　　　Deputy Director: Tong Zheng, formerly Deputy Director of the Public Enemy Property Management Bureau of the Beijing Municipal People's Government.
　　Director of the Foreign Affairs Office: Wang Yinpu, formerly Director of the Foreign Affairs Office of the Beijing Municipal People's Government.
　　　Deputy Director: Ma Zhenwu, formerly Deputy Director of the Foreign Affairs Office of the Beijing Municipal People's Government.
　　Director of the Suburban Work Committee: Zhou Fengming, formerly Deputy Director of the Civil Affairs Bureau of the Beijing Municipal People's Government.

　　　　IV. Supplementary List of Personnel of the Xinjiang Provincial People's Government

　　Deputy Director of the Civil Affairs Department: Gao Wentian, formerly Commissioner of the Third Sub-district of Jinxi Northwest.
　　Deputy Director of the Industry and Mining Department: Ke Maliding
　　Director of the Water Conservancy Department: Alimujiang (Uzbek), formerly Commissioner of the Tacheng Special District.
　　Deputy Director: Wang Heting, formerly Director of the Water Conservancy Bureau of the Kuomintang Xinjiang Province.
　　Deputy Director of the Education Department: Ma Wen
　　Director of the Health Department: Yahefuboke (Uyghur), formerly Manager of the Yili Official Pharmacy.
　　Deputy Manager of the Trade Company: Abulizi Yili (Uyghur)

　　　　V. List of Mayor and Deputy Mayors of the Dihua Municipal People's Government

　　Mayor: Qu Wu (concurrent)
　　Deputy Mayor: Hu Sigong, formerly Director of the Military-Civilian Transportation Department of the First Field Army of the People's Liberation Army.
