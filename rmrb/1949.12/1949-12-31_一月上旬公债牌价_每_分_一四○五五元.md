Title: Early January Government Bond Quotation: 14,055 Yuan per "Share"
Author:
Date: 1949-12-31
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, 30th -- The Head Office of the People's Bank of China has, based on the average prices of four commodities (rice, wheat flour, cloth, and coal) in Shanghai, Tianjin, Hankou, Guangzhou, Chongqing, and Xi'an, set the early January 1950 People's Victory Real Value Government Bond quotation at 14,055 yuan per "share" in Renminbi.

### 一月上旬公债牌价　每“分”一四○五五元

1949-12-31
第1版()
专栏：

　　一月上旬公债牌价
  　每“分”一四○五五元
    【新华社北京三十日电】中国人民银行总行已根据上海、天津、汉口、广州、重庆、西安六地的米、麦粉、布、煤四种实物平均价格，制定一九五○年一月上旬人民胜利折实公债每“分”的牌价为人民币一万四千零五十五元。
