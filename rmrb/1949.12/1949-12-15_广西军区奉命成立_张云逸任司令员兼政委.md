Title: Guangxi Military Region Established Under Command; Zhang Yunyi Appointed Commander and Political Commissar
Author:
Date: 1949-12-15
Page: 3

　　Guangxi Military Region Established Under Command
 　　Zhang Yunyi Appointed Commander and Political Commissar
    [Xinhua News Agency, Guilin, 14th] The General Headquarters of the Guangxi Military Region of the People's Liberation Army, under the order of the People's Revolutionary Military Commission, was formally established on the 9th. Zhang Yunyi was appointed Commander and Political Commissar, Huang Yongsheng as First Deputy Commander, Li Tianyou as Second Deputy Commander, Peng Mingzhi as Third Deputy Commander, Mo Wenhua as First Deputy Political Commissar, Wu Faxian as Second Deputy Political Commissar and Director of the Political Department, and Zeng Guohua as Chief of Staff, all of whom have taken office.

### 广西军区奉命成立　张云逸任司令员兼政委

1949-12-15
第3版()
专栏：

　　广西军区奉命成立
  　张云逸任司令员兼政委
    【新华社桂林十四日电】人民解放军广西军区司令部，已奉人民革命军事委员会命令，于九日正式成立，司令员兼政治委员张云逸，第一副司令黄永胜，第二副司令李天佑，第三副司令彭明治，第一副政委莫文骅，第二副政委兼政治部主任吴法宪，参谋长曾国华，均已就职。
