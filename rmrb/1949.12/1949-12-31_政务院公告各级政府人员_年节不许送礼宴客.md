Title: Government Administration Council Announces Government Personnel at All Levels Are Not Allowed to Give Gifts or Host Banquets During New Year's Holidays
Author:
Date: 1949-12-31
Page: 1

　　Government Administration Council Announcement: Government Personnel at All Levels
　　Are Not Allowed to Give Gifts or Host Banquets During New Year's Holidays
    [Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, 30th] The Government Administration Council today issued an announcement stipulating that government personnel at all levels are not allowed to host banquets, give or receive gifts, or pay New Year visits to each other during the New Year's Day and Spring Festival holidays, except for taking time off.

### 政务院公告各级政府人员　年节不许送礼宴客

1949-12-31
第1版()
专栏：

　　政务院公告各级政府人员
  　年节不许送礼宴客
    【新华社北京三十日电】政务院今日发布公告，规定各级人民政府工作人员，在元旦和春节，除放假外，一律不许宴额、收送礼物，并不许相互拜年。
