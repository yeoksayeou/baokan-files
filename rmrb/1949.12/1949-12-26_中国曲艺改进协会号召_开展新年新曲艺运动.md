Title: China Balladry Improvement Association Calls for Launching New Year New Balladry Movement
Author:
Date: 1949-12-26
Page: 3

The China Balladry Improvement Association Calls for Launching New Year New Balladry Movement

[This newspaper dispatch] The Preparatory Committee of the China Balladry Improvement Association has recently issued a notice, calling on balladry workers across the country to launch a fervent New Balladry Movement in the New Year of 1950. The notice stated: "In order to summarize the work of balladry reform over the past year, to develop the new balladry work of 1950, and to welcome the high tide of cultural construction, we call on balladry workers across the country to launch a fervent New Balladry Movement in the New Year of 1950, to review the achievements and progress of balladry reform work through performance exhibitions or competition forms, and at the same time, to lay a new foundation for the new year's reform work. We also hope that you can vigorously continue this movement, universally organize balladry workers, and launch sustained study and mutual criticism, so as to improve politically, ideologically, culturally, and professionally, and to implement the literary and artistic policy instructed by Chairman Mao."

### 中国曲艺改进协会号召　开展新年新曲艺运动

1949-12-26
第3版()
专栏：

　　中国曲艺改进协会号召
　  开展新年新曲艺运动
    【本报讯】中国曲艺改进协会筹备委员会，顷发出通知，号召全国各地曲艺工作者在一九五○年新年展开一个热烈的新曲艺运动。通知称：“我们为了总结一年来的曲艺改革工作，开展一九五○年的新曲艺工作，以迎接文化建设的高潮，号召全国各地曲艺工作者在一九五○年新年展开一个热烈的新曲艺运动，以演出展览形式，或以竞赛形式，来检阅曲艺改革工作的成果与进度，同时给新年度打定一个新的改革工作的基础。更希望你们能大力的继续这一运动，把曲艺工作者普遍的组织起来，展开持久的学习与互相的批评，以求政治思想上，文化上，业务上都能提高一步，来贯彻毛主席指示的文艺方针。”
