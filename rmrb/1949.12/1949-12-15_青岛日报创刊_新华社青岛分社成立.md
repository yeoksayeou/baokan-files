Title: Qingdao Daily Inaugurated; Xinhua News Agency Qingdao Branch Established
Author:
Date: 1949-12-15
Page: 3

　　Qingdao Daily Inaugurated
  　Xinhua News Agency Qingdao Branch Established
    [Xinhua News Agency, Qingdao, 14th] "Qingdao Daily," the organ of the Qingdao Municipal Committee of the Chinese Communist Party, was inaugurated on the 10th of this month. The Qingdao Branch of Xinhua News Agency was also established on the same day.

### 青岛日报创刊　新华社青岛分社成立

1949-12-15
第3版()
专栏：

　　青岛日报创刊
  　新华社青岛分社成立
    【新华社青岛十四日电】中共青岛市委会机关报“青岛日报”已于本月十日创刊。新华社青岛分社也在同一天成立。
