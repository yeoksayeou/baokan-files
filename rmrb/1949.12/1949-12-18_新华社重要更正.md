Title: Xinhua News Agency Important Correction
Author:
Date: 1949-12-18
Page: 2

　　Xinhua News Agency Important Correction
    In yesterday's broadcast of the "Regulations on the People's Victory Inflation-Adjusted Public Bonds for the First Period of 1950," Article 2, "to be issued starting on January 1st," should read "to be issued starting on January 5th." We hereby issue this correction.
