Title: Asian Women's Delegate Conference Adjourned Yesterday for One Day
Author:
Date: 1949-12-16
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, 15th. The Asian Women's Representatives Conference adjourned today. At noon, all the representatives attended the ceremony for the donation of the Soviet Women's Life and Work Exhibition items to China, and in the afternoon, they toured the Forbidden City.

### 亚洲妇代会议昨休会一日

1949-12-16
第4版()
专栏：

　　亚洲妇代会议昨休会一日
    【新华社北京十五日电】亚洲妇女代表会议今天休会，中午全体代表参加苏联妇女生活与工作展览品赠予中国典礼，下午游览故宫。
