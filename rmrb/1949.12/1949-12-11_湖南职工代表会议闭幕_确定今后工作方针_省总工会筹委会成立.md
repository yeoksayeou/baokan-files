Title: Hunan Workers' Representative Conference Concludes, Determining Future Work Guidelines; Preparatory Committee of the Provincial Federation of Trade Unions Established
Author:
Date: 1949-12-11
Page: 2

Xinhua News Agency, Changsha, December 9th: The Hunan Provincial Workers' Representative Conference, which opened on the 1st of this month and lasted for five days, concluded on the 5th. A total of 234 worker representatives from various regions attended the conference. Tang Xing, the representative of Cheng Qian, Director of the Hunan Military and Political Committee, Huang Kecheng, Secretary of the Hunan Provincial Committee of the Chinese Communist Party, and Wang Shoudao, Deputy Director of the Changsha Military Control Commission, all addressed the meeting. Song Xinhuai, Director of the Hunan Office of the Preparatory Committee of the Central China Federation of Trade Unions, also delivered a report on the current situation of the workers' movement in Hunan and its future tasks. The conference also elected Huang Kecheng, Gao Wenhua, Song Xinhuai and 38 others as members of the Preparatory Committee of the Hunan Provincial Federation of Trade Unions, formally establishing the Preparatory Committee of the Hunan Provincial Federation of Trade Unions. Regarding the tasks of the trade union work in Hunan Province in the future, the conference agreed to Song Xinhuai's report, taking the restoration and development of production as the goal, and focusing on the workers in state-owned enterprises, but also not relaxing the leadership of workers and shop assistants in private enterprises. The conference also stipulated that the main industrial and mining sectors throughout the province must convene a general membership meeting or a membership representative meeting before April next year to formally establish trade unions. In places with a concentration of workers, worker training classes should be held, and night schools, remedial classes and cultural and recreational activities should be organized. In addition, consumer cooperatives should be organized to help workers improve their lives.

### 湖南职工代表会议闭幕  确定今后工作方针　省总工会筹委会成立

1949-12-11
第2版()
专栏：

　　湖南职工代表会议闭幕
    确定今后工作方针
  　省总工会筹委会成立
    【新华社长沙九日电】本月一日开幕的湖南省职工代表会议，历时五日，已于五日闭幕。出席这次会议者，共有各地职工代表二百三十四人。湖南军政委员会主任程潜的代表唐星，中国共产党湖南省委员会书记黄克诚，长沙军管会副主任王首道等，都曾到会讲话。华中总工会筹委会湖南办事处主任宋新怀也在会上作了关于湖南目前工人运动情况和今后任务的报告。会议并选举黄克诚、高文华、宋新怀等四十一人为湖南省总工会筹备委员会委员，正式成立湖南省总工会筹备委员会。关于今后湖南省工会工作的任务，会议同意宋新怀的报告，以恢复和发展生产为目的，把工作重点放在公营企业工人上，但也不放松对于私营企业工人和店员工作的领导。会议又规定全省各地主要的工矿业部门，在明年四月以前，必须召开工会会员大会或会员代表会议，正式成立工会。工人集中的地方，应举办工人训练班，组织夜校、补习班和文化娱乐活动。此外，又应组织消费合作社，以帮助工人改善生活。
