Title: Greek Communist Party Central Committee Chairman of the Korean Cabinet Kim Il-sung Sends Congratulatory Telegram on Albanian National Day
Author:
Date: 1949-12-02
Page: 4

　　Greek Communist Party Central Committee Chairman of the Korean Cabinet Kim Il-sung
    Sends Congratulatory Telegram on Albanian National Day
    Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, December 1st: The Telegraph Agency reports from the Free Greece Radio Station: The Central Committee of the Greek Communist Party sent a letter to Enver Hoxha, General Secretary of the Albanian Labor Party, congratulating Albania on the fifth anniversary of its liberation and the establishment of a people's democratic regime. The letter stated: "The Greek people do not support the royalist fascists and the Tito clique's provocations and aggression plans against Albania. The Greek people consider the Albanian people to be their allies and are willing to maintain solid peace and friendship with them. The Greek people will never take up arms against them."
    Xinhua News Agency, Pyongyang, December 1st: Kim Il-sung, Chairman of the Cabinet of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea, sent a telegram to Enver Hoxha, Chairman of the Council of Ministers of the People's Republic of Albania, on November 29th, congratulating Albania on the fifth anniversary of its liberation. The telegram stated: "The Korean people, who are fighting for peace, independence, and democracy, are paying great attention to and sympathizing with the Albanian people, who have strengthened the forces of peace and democracy in the struggle against the instigators of a new war and international imperialism, and have achieved brilliant results on the construction front. We hope you will have even greater success."

### 希腊共产党中央委员会朝鲜部长会议主席金日成  电贺阿国国庆

1949-12-02
第4版()
专栏：

　　希腊共产党中央委员会朝鲜部长会议主席金日成
    电贺阿国国庆
    【新华社北京一日电】电通社引自由希腊电台报道：希腊共产党中央委员会致函阿尔巴尼亚工人党总书记霍查，祝贺阿尔巴尼亚解放及建立人民民主政权五周年。函称：“希腊人民并不支持保皇法西斯及铁托集团对阿尔巴尼亚的挑衅和侵略计划。希腊人民认为阿尔巴尼亚人民是自己的盟友，并愿与之保持稳固的和平和友谊，希腊人民决不拿起武器来反对他们。”
    【新华社平壤一日电】朝鲜民主主义人民共和国部长会议主席金日成十一月二十九日致电阿尔巴尼亚人民共和国部长会议席霍查，祝贺阿国解放五周年。电称：“正为和平、独立、民主而斗争的朝鲜人民，对在反对新战争挑拨者、国际帝国主义的斗争中加强了和平民主力量，又在建设战线上获得光辉成果的阿尔巴尼亚人民，寄以莫大的关心与同情。希望你们有更大的成功。”
