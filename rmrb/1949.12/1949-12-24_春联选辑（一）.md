Selected Spring Couplets (I)
　　Celebrate the New Year, do not forget production and national construction
　　Congratulate the New Year, especially conserve and support the army

　　Labor to build a family and drink the wine of liberation
　　Happiness fills the door, singing the song of liberation

　　National liberation, universal celebration
　　The army and the people are happy together, the earth returns to spring

　　Celebrate liberation, do not forget the Communist Party
　　Enjoy peace and thank the People's Liberation Army

　　Model household for supporting the army and the front
　　Hero's family of production and labor
