Title: Beijing Autumn Property Tax: Additional Collection Points Established
Author:
Date: 1949-12-05
Page: 3

Beijing News: To make it more convenient for citizens to pay the autumn property tax, the Beijing Municipal People's Government has added collection points, entrusting private banks, firms, and money shops to collect the tax. Citizens are not limited to the locations specified on the payment notice and can pay at any of the entrusted collection points. The addresses of the newly added collection points are as follows: Continental Bank (West Jiaomin Lane), New China Trust Bank (Langfang Toutiao), China Industrial Bank (West Jiaomin Lane), China Agricultural and Industrial Bank (West Jiaomin Lane), Juxingcheng Bank (Qianmenwai Avenue), Renfa Public Money Shop (Guanyin Temple, Qianmenwai), Yiju Money Shop (Damochang, Qianmenwai), Yongtai Public Money Shop (Dongzhushikou), Rongchanghou Money Shop (Langfang Ertiao), Qianxing Money Shop (Shijia Hutong), Jinhongyu Money Shop (Dashilan, Qianmenwai), Fusunde Money Shop (West Jiaomin Lane).

### 京市秋季房产税　增设收款处

1949-12-05
第3版()
专栏：

　　京市秋季房产税
  　增设收款处
    【北京讯】北京市人民政府为使市民缴纳秋季房产税方便起见，特增添收款处，委托私营银行、号及钱庄代收。市民可以不必受款通知单上地点的限制，随意到各委托收款处缴纳。新添收款处地址如下：大陆银行（西交民巷）、新华信托银行（廊房头条）、中国实业银行（西交民巷）、中国农工银行（西交民巷）、聚兴成银行（前门外大街）、仁发公钱庄（前门外观音寺）、义聚钱庄（前门外打磨厂）、永泰公钱庄（东珠市口）、荣长厚钱庄（廊房二条）、谦兴钱庄（施家胡同）、晋鸿裕钱庄（前门外大栅栏）、福顺德钱庄（西交民巷）。
