Title: Xinhua Radio Tonight's Program Highlights
Author:
Date: 1949-12-03
Page: 3

[This newspaper dispatch]
　　Xinhua News Agency, Beijing: Starting today (the 3rd), Beijing Xinhua Broadcasting Station will replace the natural science knowledge lecture with music every Saturday. Tonight (the 3rd) at 17:30, the youth program will broadcast "The Education 'December 9th' Gave Me" from the 27th issue of "China Youth". At 18:45, a full Mandarin broadcast of Maxine Farroki's English speech from yesterday. The first literary program at 18:00: Jingyun Dagu "Flying Over Luding Bridge". The second literary program at 21:00: the first half of the new Peking Opera "He Bo Takes a Wife".
　　[Another dispatch] Second station at 12:30, employee time: "The Great Spirit of the Working Class". 16:15, children's time: (1) Current Affairs Talk, (2) Singing by the children of Wofosi Street Elementary School. 16:45, women's time: "Talking about Childcare Work". 17:00, new song practice: teaching the new song "Forward, Sisters!"

### 新华电台今晚要目

1949-12-03
第3版()
专栏：

　　新华电台今晚要目
    【本报讯】北京新华广播电台从今（三）天起每星期六的自然科学常识讲座改播音乐。今（三）晚十七点三十分青年节目：播送“中国青年”第二十七期的“一二一给我的教育”。十八点四十五分用国语播送玛辛·法洛琪昨天用英语广播演讲的全文。十八点第一次文艺节目；京韶大鼓“飞夺泸定桥”。二十一点第二次文艺节目：新京剧“何伯娶妇”前半部。
    【又讯】第二台十二点半职工时间：“工人阶级的伟大精神”。十六点十五分儿童时间：（一）时事讲话，（二）卧佛寺街小学小朋友们唱歌。十六点四十五分妇女时间：谈谈儿童保育工作”。十七点新歌练习：教唱新歌“前进吧，姊妹们！”
