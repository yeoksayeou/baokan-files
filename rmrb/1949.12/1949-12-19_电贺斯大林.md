Title: Telegram of Congratulations to Stalin
Author:
Date: 1949-12-19
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, December 18th. The full text of the telegram from the China People's Relief Association congratulating Marshal Stalin on his seventieth birthday is as follows:

To Marshal Stalin, Moscow:

We congratulate you on your seventieth birthday for your great achievements in leading the people of the world towards lasting peace and people's democracy. We also wish you good health!

China People's Relief Association
December 18, 1949, in Beijing

### 电贺斯大林

1949-12-19

专栏：

　　电贺斯大林
    【新华社北京十八日电】中国人民救国会祝贺斯大林大元帅七十寿辰电全文如下：莫斯科斯大林大元帅：
    为了您领导全世界人民走向持久和平和人民民主的丰功伟绩，我们仅贺您的七十寿辰。并祝健康！
    中国人民救国会
