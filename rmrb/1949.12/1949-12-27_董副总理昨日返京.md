Title: Vice Premier Dong Returned to Beijing Yesterday
Author:
Date: 1949-12-27
Page: 1

　　Vice Premier Dong Returned to Beijing Yesterday
　　[Our Newspaper] Vice Premier of the Government Administration Council, Dong Biwu, led the Government Administration Council's Guidance and Acceptance Work Committee, East China Region Work Group, to Nanjing and Shanghai on the 8th of this month to carry out acceptance work. After more than half a month of consultations with relevant local authorities, all acceptance work has been arranged and group acceptances have begun. Vice Premier Dong took a train back to Beijing on the 16th for official business.
　　(Report from the Political and Legal Affairs Committee)

### 董副总理昨日返京

1949-12-27
第1版()
专栏：

　　董副总理昨日返京
    【本报讯】政务院董必武副总理于本月八日率领政务院指导接收工作委员会华东区工作团赴南京、上海进行接收工作，半月余来，经与有关地方当局洽商，各项接收工作，均已布置就绪，并已分组开始接收。董副总理因公于十六日乘车先行返京。
    （政法委会报导组）
