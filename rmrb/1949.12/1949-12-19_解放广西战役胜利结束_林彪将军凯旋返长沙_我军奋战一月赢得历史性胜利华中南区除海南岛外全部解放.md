Title: Victory in the Guangxi Campaign Concludes: General Lin Biao Returns Triumphantly to Changsha; Our Army's Month-Long Struggle Secures Historic Victory; Central South China Liberated Except for Hainan Island
Author:
Date: 1949-12-19
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Changsha, 18th: Lin Biao, Commander of the Fourth Field Army of the Chinese People's Liberation Army and Chairman of the Central South Military and Political Committee, along with Deputy Political Commissar Tan Zheng, Chief of Staff Xiao Ke, and others, returned triumphantly to Changsha from the South China front this morning. Those who went to the station to welcome them included Cheng Qian, Vice Chairman of the Central South Military and Political Committee; Xiao Jinguang, Commander of the Hunan Military Region; Huang Kecheng, Political Commissar; Jin Ming, Deputy Political Commissar; Chen Mingren, Chairman of the Hunan Provisional Provincial Government; Yuan Renyuan, Vice Chairman; Tang Shengzhi, member of the Central South Military and Political Committee; and more than 5,000 workers, students, women, and democrats from Changsha. In his welcoming speech, Vice Chairman Cheng Qian celebrated the great victory of completely annihilating the Bai Chongxi bandit army and the total liberation of South China, and welcomed General Lin Biao's triumphant return to lead the construction work of the Central South region's people. In his reply, Commander Lin pointed out: This victory was achieved under the leadership of Chairman Mao, with the support of the people of the whole country, and with the courageous and coordinated operations of the national liberation army. In the future, we should step up the elimination of bandits, stabilize social order, carry out social reforms, and vigorously develop economic, cultural, and national defense construction in all aspects. When Commander Lin and others walked out of the station, they were warmly welcomed by the masses along the way.

### 解放广西战役胜利结束　林彪将军凯旋返长沙   我军奋战一月赢得历史性胜利华中南区除海南岛外全部解放

1949-12-19
第1版()
专栏：

　　解放广西战役胜利结束
  　林彪将军凯旋返长沙
    我军奋战一月赢得历史性胜利华中南区除海南岛外全部解放
    【新华社长沙十八日电】中国人民解放军第四野战军司令员兼中南军政委员会主席林彪、副政委谭政、参谋长萧克等一行今晨由华南前线凯旋返抵长沙。前赴车站欢迎者有中南军政委员会副主席程潜、湖南军区司令员萧劲光、政治委员黄克诚、副政治委员金明、湖南临时省政府主席陈明仁、副主席袁任远、中南军政委员会委员唐生智及长沙市工人、学生、妇女及民主人士共五千余人。程潜副主席致欢迎词时，庆祝彻底歼灭白崇禧匪军和华南全部解放的伟大胜利并欢迎林彪将军凯旋回来领导中南区人民的建设工作。林司令员致答词时指出：这次战争胜利是在毛主席的领导下，全国人民的支援及全国解放军奋勇配合作战下取得的。今后应加紧消灭土匪，安定社会秩序，进行社会改革，大力展开经济、文化、国防各方面的建设工作。当林司令员等走出车站时，受到沿途群众的热烈欢迎。
