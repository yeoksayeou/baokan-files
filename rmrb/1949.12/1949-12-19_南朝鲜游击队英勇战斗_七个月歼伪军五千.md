Title: South Korean Guerrillas Fight Valiantly, Eliminating 5,000 Puppet Troops in Seven Months
Author:
Date: 1949-12-19
Page: 4

　　Xinhua News Agency, Pyongyang, 17th. According to the South Korean Laborer's Newspaper, the comprehensive battle results of the South Korean People's Guerrilla forces from April to October of this year are as follows: The total number of people mobilized (i.e., the total number of people participating in battles or attacks and the number of times) was 298,489, conducting 5,508 operations, attacking puppet county- and city-level police stations 28 times, attacking puppet district-level police branches 414 times, attacking puppet district offices 45 times, and attacking railway stations 29 times; eliminating 3,284 puppet soldiers and police, capturing 1,735 enemies, seizing a total of 3,153 rifles and machine guns, and 31,636 rounds of ammunition; destroying 3,155 utility poles and 42,307 meters of electrical wire.

### 南朝鲜游击队英勇战斗　七个月歼伪军五千

1949-12-19
第4版()
专栏：

　　南朝鲜游击队英勇战斗
  　七个月歼伪军五千
    【新华社平壤十七日电】据南朝鲜劳力者报发表，南朝鲜人民游击队从本年四月到十月的七个月综合战果如下：动员的总人数（按即参加战斗或袭击的人数与次数的总和）为二十九万八千四百八十九名，作战五千五百零八次，袭击伪县市级警察署二十八次，袭击伪区级警察支署四百一十四次，袭击伪区公所四十五次，袭击火车站二十九次；消灭伪军警三千二百八十四名，俘敌一千七百三十五名，缴获步枪、机枪共三千一百五十三枝，子弹三万一千六百三十六发；破坏电线杆三千一百五十五根，破坏电线四万二千三百零七米。
