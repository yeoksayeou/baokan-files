Title: The Whole Nation Vows to Wipe Out the Remnants of the Bandits, Firmly Supports Foreign Minister Zhou's Statement, Warns Imperialist Countries Not to Play With Fire and Burn Themselves

Author:
Date: 1949-12-03
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, December 2nd: People from all walks of life in Wuhan, Changsha, Henan, and other places warmly support the solemn statement made by our Foreign Minister Zhou Enlai regarding the fleeing of Kuomintang bandit remnants to foreign countries such as Vietnam. The Wuhan Municipal People's Second Representative Conference, currently in session, immediately issued a circular after learning of this statement, representing the 1.2 million people of Wuhan in expressing their support. The Preparatory Committee of the Huazhong and Wuhan Municipal Federation of Trade Unions also issued a circular stating: "The remaining bandit gangs are attempting to flee to foreign countries such as Vietnam in a last-ditch struggle. It is the unanimous belief of the 475 million people to completely eliminate these dregs who have cruelly ruled the Chinese people for more than 20 years, and this belief is unshakable. We firmly support Foreign Minister Zhou's solemn statement and vow to be its backing."

People from all walks of life in Changsha also issued statements, expressing their warm support. Cheng Qian, Director of the Hunan People's Military and Political Committee, said: "Now the Kuomintang reactionary bandit gangs have collapsed and scattered. If Vietnam and Burma, which are close to our southwestern border, allow them to sneak in and regain their breath, they can still cause harm. Minister Zhou's statement warning the neighboring countries bordering us is righteous and stern." Xiao Jingguang, Commander of the 12th Army Corps of the People's Liberation Army and Commander of the Hunan Military Region, said: "I represent all the commanders and fighters of the 12th Army Corps and the Hunan Military Region in firmly supporting Foreign Minister Zhou's statement. Even if the remnants of the Kuomintang reactionaries flee to the ends of the earth, we will completely wipe them out." Chen Mingren, Commander of the 21st Army Corps of the People's Liberation Army, said: "All the commanders and fighters of our 21st Army Corps firmly support Foreign Minister Zhou's statement. We will not stop advancing until all the remaining armed forces of the Kuomintang are eliminated." Zou Dexin, Deputy Director of the Preparatory Committee of the Changsha Municipal Women's Federation, Kong Anmin of the Changsha Municipal Youth Committee, democrat Kang De, and Zeng Guangshu, Chairman of the Preparatory Committee of the Student Federation, also issued statements expressing their warm support.

People from all walks of life in Kaifeng also responded to Foreign Minister Zhou's statement. Wu Zhipu, Chairman of the Hunan Provincial Government, said: "The bandit gangs' attempt to collude with imperialism and its collaborators, using Vietnam and other places as a place to harbor filth and hide dirt, is something that our people across the country cannot tolerate under any circumstances." The Henan Military Region of the People's Liberation Army, the Henan Provincial Working Committee, the Youth Federation, the Communist Youth League, the Women's Federation, and the Federation of Industry and Commerce also issued statements of support.

Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, December 2nd: People from all walks of life in Tianjin, Xi'an, Jiangxi, and other places warmly support the solemn statement issued by Foreign Minister Zhou Enlai on November 29 regarding the flight of Kuomintang bandit troops to Vietnam and other places. The person in charge of the Preparatory Committee of the Tianjin Municipal Federation of Trade Unions, on behalf of the city's 500,000 workers, expressed firm support for Foreign Minister Zhou's statement. He said: "The Vietnamese French imperialist colonial authorities and their puppet Bao Dai, or any other imperialist elements, if they dare to abet the evildoers and harbor fleeing bandits, must bear all possible consequences." Zuo Da, representative of the Tianjin Democratic Youth Federation and the Municipal Preparatory Committee of the Communist Youth League, Luo Yun, Director of the Democratic Women's Federation, Xiao Caiyu, Director of the Tianjin University Faculty and Staff Union, Wang Chunhai, model worker of the post office, Wang Liankai, labor model of the waterworks, Bi Mingqi, Director of the Municipal Chamber of Commerce Consolidation Committee, Guo Zuyuan, Deputy Chief Engineer of the Municipal Government Works Bureau, and Zi Yaohua, Manager of Shanghai Bank, also issued statements expressing their firm support for Foreign Minister Zhou's statement.

Zhao Boping, Chairman of the Xi'an Municipal People's Representative Conference, said: "No matter where the Kuomintang bandits flee, we will never allow these remnants to preserve their remaining strength in any place, attempting to make a comeback and harm the people." Zhu Zitong, Deputy Director of the Preparatory Committee of the Xi'an Municipal Federation of Trade Unions, said: "The workers of Xi'an suffered oppression and massacre by the Jiang Hu bandit gangs before liberation. We workers will never forget these bloody facts. Wherever they flee, we will have the right to intervene." Heads of organizations such as the Preparatory Committee of the Northwest Federation of Trade Unions, the Northwest Working Committee of the Chinese New Democratic Youth League, the Shaanxi-Gansu-Ningxia Border Region Cultural Circles Association, the Shaanxi-Gansu-Ningxia Border Region Women's Federation, the Xi'an Student Federation, the Preparatory Committee of the Xi'an Federation of Industry and Commerce, the Preparatory Committee of the Xi'an Drama Association, the Xi'an Branch of the Sino-Soviet Friendship Association, and the Preparatory Committee of the Xi'an Branch of the All-China Journalists Association also issued statements expressing their support and warning imperialist countries not to play with fire and burn themselves.

Chen Qihan, Commander of the Jiangxi Military Region, on behalf of all the commanders and fighters of the Jiangxi Military Region, expressed firm support for Foreign Minister Zhou Enlai's statement. The Preparatory Committee of the Jiangxi Provincial Federation of Trade Unions, the Jiangxi Working Committee of the Chinese New Democratic Youth League, and the Jiangxi Provincial Women's Working Committee also issued written statements, pointing out that any country's government that dares to harbor the remaining bandit troops of the Kuomintang is an act of hostility and provocation against the Chinese people, and expressing their determination to fully support Foreign Minister Zhou's statement.

### 全国人民誓必全歼残匪　坚决拥护周外长声明  警告帝国主义国家勿玩火自焚

1949-12-03
第1版()
专栏：

　　全国人民誓必全歼残匪
  　坚决拥护周外长声明
    警告帝国主义国家勿玩火自焚
    【新华社北京二日电】武汉、长沙、河南等地各界人民，热烈拥护我外交部长周恩来关于国民党残匪逃往国外越南等地一事发表的严正声明。正在举行的武汉市各界第二届代表会议获悉此一声明后，立即发表通电，代表武汉市一百二十万人民表示拥护。华中及武汉市总工会筹委会亦发表通电说：残余匪帮竟图逃往国外越南等地，以作临死前的挣扎。最后完全消灭这些残酷统治中国人民二十多年的败类，乃是四万万七千五百万人民一致的信念，这个信念是不可动摇的。我们坚决拥护周外长的严正声明，并誓为其后盾。
    长沙各界人士也纷纷发表谈话，表示热烈拥护。湖南人民军政委员会主任程潜说：“现在国民党反动匪帮已纷纷溃散，其接近我国西南国境的越南、缅甸若任其窜入喘息稍苏，仍可为祸害。周部长声明警告和我毗连的邻国实为义正辞严。”人民解放军第十二兵团司令员兼湖南军区司令员肖劲光说：“我代表十二兵团及湖南军区的全体指战员坚决拥护周外长的声明，即使国民党反动派残余的武装逃到天涯海角、我们也要把他们完全歼灭。”人民解放军第二十一兵团司令员陈明仁说：“我们二十一兵团全体指战员坚决拥护周外长的声明，直到国民党残余武装全部消灭为止，我们决不停止进军。”长沙市妇联筹委会副主任邹得心、长沙市青委孔安民、民主人士康德、学联筹委会主席曾光曙亦均发表谈话，热烈拥护。
    开封各界人士也纷纷响应周外长声明。湖南省府主席吴芝圃说：“匪帮企图勾结帝国主义及其合作者，以越南等地作为其藏垢纳污之所，是我全国人民无论如何不能容许的。”人民解放军河南军区、河南省工委、青联、青年团、妇联及工商界联合会等亦均发表声明拥护。
    【新华社北京二日电】天津、西安、江西等地各界人民热烈拥护外交部长周恩来十一月二十九日就国民党匪军逃往越南等地一事发表的严正声明。天津市总工会筹委会负责人代表全市五十万工人表示坚决拥护周外长的声明。他说：越南法帝国主义殖民当局及其傀儡保大，或其他任何帝国主义分子，如胆敢助纣为虐，窝藏逃匪，必须承担一切可能发生之后果。天津市民主青年联合会及青年团市筹委会代表左达、民主妇女联合会主任罗云、天津大学教职员联合会主任肖采瑜、邮局模范工人王春海、自来水厂劳动模范王连凯、市商会整理委员会主任毕鸣岐、市府工务局副总工程师过祖源、上海银行经理资耀华等均发表谈话，表示坚决拥护周外长声明。
    西安市各界人民代表会主席赵伯平说：不管国民党匪徒们逃至天涯海角，我们决不允许这些残匪在任何地方保存残余力量，企图卷土重来残害人民。西安市总工会筹委会副主任朱子彤说：西安工人在解放前曾受尽蒋胡匪帮的压迫和屠杀，我们工人永远不会忘记这些血的事实，他们逃到什么地方，我们都将有权过问。西北总工会筹委会、中国新民主主义青年团西北工作委员会、陕甘宁边区文化界协会、陕甘宁边区妇女联合会、西安市学生联合会、西安市工商联合会筹委会、西安市戏剧协会筹备会、中苏友好协会西安分会、中华全国新闻工作者协会筹备会西安分会等团体负责人，亦均发表声明表示拥护，并警告帝国主义国家勿玩火自焚。
    江西省军区司令员陈奇涵代表江西军区全体指战员表示坚决拥护周恩来外长的声明。江西省总工会筹委会，中国新民主主义青年团江西工作委员会和江西省妇女工作委员会也先后发表书面谈话，指出任何国家的政府敢于容留国民党的残余匪军，都是对中国人民的一种敌意和挑衅行为，并表示决以全力作周外长声明的后盾。
