Title: Asian Workers' and Women's Life and Struggle Exhibition

Author: 
Date: 1949-12-06
Page: 1

In order to exchange experiences of Asian workers and women's struggles, and to introduce the workers' and women's movements and living conditions in Asian countries, the Asian Workers' and Women's Life and Struggle Exhibition is being held.

I. Content: (1) Photographs: Photographs of the life and struggle of workers and women in the Soviet Union, China, Mongolia, North Korea, Vietnam, India, Indonesia, Iran and other countries. (2) Objects: Handicraft products and literary works of women from the Soviet Union and China, as well as progressive books and newspapers from Mongolia, Japan, Vietnam and other countries.

II. Exhibition Dates: From December 5, 1949 to December 25, daily from 9:00 am to 4:00 pm.

III. Exhibition Location: Zhongshan Park, Beijing. All sectors are welcome to visit. Factories, organizations, schools, troops and groups that organize collective visits of 20 or more people must first bring a letter of introduction to the Exhibition Office (located in the Hygiene Exhibition Hall of Zhongshan Park) from 9:00 am to 12:00 pm and from 2:00 pm to 4:00 pm daily to arrange a visiting schedule to avoid crowding, and free admission will be granted.

　　　　　　　　　　　　　Asian Workers' and Women's Life and Struggle Exhibition
　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　December 4

### 亚洲工人妇女生活与斗争展览会

1949-12-06
第1版()
专栏：

　　亚洲工人妇女生活与斗争展览会
    为交流亚洲工人妇女斗争经验和介绍亚洲各国工人与妇女运动及生活情况，特举办亚洲工人妇女生活与斗争展览会。
    一、内容：（一）照片：苏联、中国、蒙古、朝鲜、越南、印度、印尼、伊朗等国的工人、妇女的生活与斗争照片。（二）实物：苏联、中国妇女手工业产品、文艺作品以及蒙古、日本、越南等国的进步书报。
    二、展览日期：一九四九年十二月五日至十二月二十五日每日上午九时至下午四时。
    三、展览地点：北京中山公园欢迎各界前来参观，凡工厂、机关、学校、部队、团体，组织二十人以上集体前来参观者，须先持介绍信每日上午九时至十二时，下午二时至四时，到展览会办事处（在中山公园卫生陈列馆）接洽，以便排定参观日程避免拥挤，并予免费入场。
    　　　　　　　　　　　　　亚洲工人妇女生活与斗争展览会
    　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　十二月四日
