Title: Xinjiang Military Region Established; Peng Dehuai Appointed Commander and Political Commissar
Author:
Date: 1949-12-24
Page: 1

　　Xinhua News Agency, Dihua, 23rd: The Xinjiang Military Region of the People's Liberation Army was formally established on the 17th upon the order of the People's Revolutionary Military Commission. Peng Dehuai is the Commander and Political Commissar of the Military Region, Wang Zhen is the First Deputy Commander (concurrently), Tao Zhiyue is the Second Deputy Commander, Saifuddin is the Third Deputy Commander, Zhang Xiqin is the First Chief of Staff, Zeng Zhenwu is the Second Chief of Staff, Xu Liqing is the Director of the Political Department, Zeng Han and Yuan Shalofu are the Deputy Directors of the Political Department, Zhang Fengyi is the Chief of the Staff Office, and Gu Zhendong is the Deputy Chief.

### 新疆军区成立　彭德怀任司令员兼政委

1949-12-24
第1版()
专栏：

　　新疆军区成立
    彭德怀任司令员兼政委
    【新华社迪化二十三日电】人民解放军新疆省军区奉人民革命军事委员会命令于十七日正式成立，彭德怀为军区司令员兼政治委员，王震为第一副司令员（兼），陶峙岳为第二副司令员，赛福鼎为第三副司令员，张希钦为第一参谋长，曾震五为第二参谋长，徐立清为政治部主任，曾涵，员沙洛夫为政治部副主任，张风仪为参谋处长，谷振东为副处长。
