Title: Xinjiang Provincial People's Government Established; 50,000 People in Dihua March in Celebration
Author:
Date: 1949-12-26
Page: 1

　　Xinjiang Provincial People's Government Established
　  Dihua Five Thousand People March in Celebration
    Xinhua News Agency, Xi'an, December 25th: The Xinjiang People's Government was established on the 17th, and the first meeting of the Government Committee was held on the same day. The meeting was presided over by Burhan Shahidi, Chairman of the Provincial People's Government. The meeting unanimously adopted the current policy guidelines of the Xinjiang Provincial People's Government. Burhan said in his speech at the meeting: The establishment of the Xinjiang Provincial People's Government and the adoption of policy guidelines are long-awaited good news for the people of Xinjiang. This is an unprecedented people's victory in the history of Xinjiang. It is our greatest honor that the Central People's Government and the people of Xinjiang have entrusted us with this glorious task. We believe that in the implementation of the policy program, with the correct leadership of Chairman Mao of the Central People's Government, Chairman Peng Dehuai, Xi Zhongxun, and Vice Chairman Zhang Zhizhong of the Northwest Military and Political Committee, all members of the provincial government and the people of Xinjiang will be able to overcome all difficulties and successfully complete all tasks. Then Vice Chairman Saifudin spoke. He expressed that under the correct leadership of the Chinese Communist Party and the Central People's Government, the people of Xinjiang have full confidence in building a backward Xinjiang into an advanced Xinjiang.

### 新疆省人民政府成立　迪化五万人大游行庆祝

1949-12-26
第1版()
专栏：

　　新疆省人民政府成立
　  迪化五万人大游行庆祝
    【新华社西安二十五日电】新疆人民政府于十七日成立，并于当日举行了政府委员会第一次会议。会议由省人民政府主席鲍尔汉主持。会上一致通过了新疆省人民政府当前施政方针。鲍尔汉在会上致词称：新疆省人民政府的成立和通过的施政方针，为全疆人民期待已久的福音，这是新疆有史以来空前的人民胜利。中央人民政府及全疆人民把这个光荣的任务交给我们，是我们最大的荣幸。相信省府全体人员及全疆人民在执行施政纲领中，由于中央人民政府毛主席，西北军政委员会彭德怀主席、习仲勋及张治中副主席的正确领导，一定能克服一切困难，胜利完成全部任务。继由副主席赛福鼎讲话。他表示在中国共产党和中央人民政府的正确领导下，新疆人民有充分信心，把落后的新疆建设成先进的新疆。
