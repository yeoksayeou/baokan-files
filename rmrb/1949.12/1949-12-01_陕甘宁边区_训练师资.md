Title: Shaanxi-Gansu-Ningxia Border Region Trains Teachers
Author:
Date: 1949-12-01
Page: 3

　　Shaanxi-Gansu-Ningxia Border Region
    Training Teachers

    [Xi'an News] The Education Department of the Shaanxi-Gansu-Ningxia Border Region Government, considering the transformation of existing teachers as the key to transforming schools, has issued a notice. In addition to strengthening the learning of in-service teachers in various schools and establishing elementary school teacher training classes by district, it has decided to organize educational research classes. Middle schools and complete elementary schools in various localities will select in-service teachers to participate in the study, with the aim of enabling them to become the backbone for further transforming schools. The number of selected participants has been specified: 158 middle school teachers and 74 complete elementary school teachers, selected from Xi'an, Weinan, Sanyuan, Baoji, Binxian, Dali, Xianyang, and other districts. If conditions permit, Gansu, Ningxia, and Qinghai provinces will also select students to study there. The study duration of the class is tentatively set at six months, and it began on November 1st.

### 陕甘宁边区  训练师资

1949-12-01
第3版()
专栏：

　　陕甘宁边区
    训练师资
    【西安讯】陕甘宁边区政府教育厅以改造现有的师资为改造学校的关键，已发出通知，除加强各学校在职教员的学习及分区设法举办小学教师训练班外，决举办教育研究班，由各地中等学校、完全小学选送在职教员参加学习，以期能成为进一步改造学校的骨干分子。选送人数已有规定，计中学教员一百五十八人，完小教员七十四人，由西安市、渭南、三原、宝鸡、彬县、大荔，咸阳等分区选送；甘、宁、青三省如有条件时也尽可能选送学员前往学习。该班学习时间暂定半年，十一月一日已开始学习。
