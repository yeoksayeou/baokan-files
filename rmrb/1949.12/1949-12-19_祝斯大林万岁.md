Title: Long Live Stalin
Author: Shao Lizhi
Date: 1949-12-19
Page: 2

　　Long Live Stalin
    Shao Lizhi
    Many villains in the world curse Stalin,
    Baseless slanders say he is failing and ailing;
    Today we welcome his seventieth birthday,
    Knowing he maintains a vigorous spirit, anyway.
    War-mongers are frightened out of their wits,
    How can righteous people not be encouraged and delighted!
    Everywhere they shout Stalin's great name,
    Everywhere they praise Stalin's great achievements.
    He inherited Marx, Engels, and Lenin,
    He guarantees the victorious completion of socialism.
    He led the heroic and unparalleled Red Army,
    And won the war defeating fascism;
    He also leads the free and democratic camp,
    To win lasting peace for the whole world again.
    He is the savior of all the oppressed,
    He is the teacher of all who seek progress.
    He understands the characteristics of the Chinese revolution best,
    And constantly helps us with theory and material.
    He constantly cares about the Chinese people,
    And the Chinese people reciprocate with the greatest enthusiasm.
    Anti-Soviet slanders are not worth a penny,
    The cause of the liberation of all mankind is rising like the sun.
    Stalin's name will last as long as the universe,
    Stalin's achievements will be as new as the years.
    Learning from Stalin is the common aspiration of all,
    Long live Stalin is the unanimous voice of all.

### 祝斯大林万岁
邵力子
1949-12-19
第2版()
专栏：

　　祝斯大林万岁
    邵力子
    世间不少恶徒咒骂斯大林，
    无耽的谰言说人衰病交侵；
    今天迎接他的七十岁诞辰，
    都知道他保持矍铄的精神。
    战争贩子们吓得胆战心惊，
    正直的人怎能不鼓舞欢欣！
    到处高呼着斯大林的大名，
    到处颂扬着斯大林的伟勋。
    他继承马克思恩格斯列宁，
    他保证社会主义胜利完成。
    他领导了英勇无比的红军，
    已赢得打垮法西斯的战争；
    他又领导自由民主的阵营，
    要再赢得全世界持久和平。
    他是一切被压迫者的救星，
    他是一切求进步者的先生。
    他最了解中国革命的特征，
    理论与物质不断帮助我们。
    他对中国人民经常地关心，
    中国人民也报以最大热情。
    反苏的谰言早是不值一文，
    全人类解放事业如日方升。
    斯大林的名字与宇宙长存，
    斯大林的勋业与岁月俱新。
    向斯大林学习是万众一心，
    祝斯大林万岁是万口同声。
