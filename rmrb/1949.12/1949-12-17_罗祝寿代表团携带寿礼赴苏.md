Title: Romanian Delegation Takes Birthday Gifts to Soviet Union
Author:
Date: 1949-12-17
Page: 3

Xinhua News Agency, Bucharest, 16th: The Romanian delegation celebrating Stalin's birthday, led by Minister of Metallurgy and Chemical Industry Stoica, left for Moscow by train on the 13th with gifts from the Romanian working people. The Chairman of the Romanian Council of Ministers, Groza, Foreign Minister Pok, and other dignitaries, as well as the Soviet Ambassador to Romania, Kavtaradze, went to the station to see them off.

### 罗祝寿代表团携带寿礼赴苏

1949-12-17
第3版()
专栏：

  　罗祝寿代表团携带寿礼赴苏
    【新华社北京十六日电】布加勒斯讯：罗马尼亚庆祝斯大林寿辰的代表团由冶金与化学工业部长斯托依卡率领，于十三日携带罗马尼亚劳动人民的贺礼乘火车赴莫斯科。罗马尼亚部会议主席格罗查、外交部长波克及其他要员，苏联驻罗马尼亚大使卡夫塔拉兹均亲往车站送行。
