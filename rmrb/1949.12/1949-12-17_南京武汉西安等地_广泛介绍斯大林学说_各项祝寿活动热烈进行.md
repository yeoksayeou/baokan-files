Title: Nanjing, Wuhan, Xi'an and other places widely introduce Stalin's theories; birthday celebration activities are in full swing
Author:
Date: 1949-12-17
Page: 1

　　Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, 16th. Nanjing, Wuhan, Henan, Xi'an and other places are enthusiastically preparing to celebrate the 70th birthday of the teacher of the people of the world, Marshal Stalin. The Nanjing Branch of the Sino-Soviet Friendship Association held a meeting on the 15th and decided to hold a birthday celebration, and cooperated with the Municipal Federation of Trade Unions Preparatory Committee, the East China Military and Political University and other seven units to hold special lectures, publish special issues, hold exhibitions of Stalin's photos and works, introduce Stalin's life and his theory of guiding the Chinese revolution, and screen films related to Stalin's life and career. The signatures of more than 100,000 people in Nanjing initiated on the International Day for the Struggle for Peace will be bound into volumes and presented to Stalin as a congratulatory gift. The Sino-Soviet Friendship Association branch also called for the universal establishment of branches and sub-branches to educate members and consolidate and strengthen Sino-Soviet friendship.
　　The Wuhan Branch of the Sino-Soviet Friendship Association decided on the 14th to hold a large-scale lecture to introduce Stalin's life, theories and his contribution to the world revolution. Each branch will hold a discussion. On the 21st, the branch will hold a grand cocktail party and birthday party, inviting Soviet citizens in Wuhan to participate. In addition, the association will also hold an exhibition of photos of Soviet production and construction. The Henan Branch of the Sino-Soviet Friendship Association also compiled and printed a booklet on Stalin's life and distributed it to various places.
　　The Preparatory Committee of the Xi'an Branch of the Sino-Soviet Friendship Association, which already has 10,000 members, is scheduled to hold an inaugural meeting on Stalin's birthday on the 21st. The association will cooperate with Xinhua Bookstore to hold a large-scale photo and pictorial exhibition. Various organs and schools will organize reports on Stalin or the Soviet Union respectively.

### 南京武汉西安等地  广泛介绍斯大林学说　各项祝寿活动热烈进行

1949-12-17
第1版()
专栏：

　　南京武汉西安等地
    广泛介绍斯大林学说
  　各项祝寿活动热烈进行
    【新华社北京十六日电】南京、武汉、河南、西安各地热烈筹备庆祝全世界人民的导师斯大林大元帅的七十寿辰。中苏友好协会南京分会于十五日开会，决定举行祝寿大会，并协同市总工会筹委员，华东军政大学等七个单位举办专题讲演，出版专刊，举办斯大林照片及著作展览，介绍斯大林生平及其指导中国革命的理论，放映与斯大林生平事业有关的电影等。在国际和平斗争日发起的南京十余万人民的签名纸将订成册，献给斯大林作为贺礼。中苏友协分会并号召普遍成立支会、支分会，教育会员，巩固和加强中苏友谊。
    中苏友好协会武汉市分会十四日决定召开一个大规模的演讲会，介绍斯大林生平、学说及其对世界革命的贡献。各支会则将分别举行座谈会。二十一日，分会将举行盛大鸡尾酒会及祝寿晚会，邀请在汉苏侨参加。此外，该会并将举办苏联生产建设照片展览。中苏友好协会河南分会，并编印斯大林生平的小册子，散发各地。
    已拥有会员万人的中苏友好协会西安分会筹委会订于二十一日斯大林筹辰举行成立会，该会与新华书店合办大规模的照片画报展览。各机关学校则分别组织有关斯大林或苏联的报告。
