Title: Solemn Pledge to Exert Utmost Effort to Prevent New War: British Communist Party Congress Closes; Passes Executive Committee Report and Election Program
Author:
Date: 1949-12-07
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Prague, December 6th: London dispatch: The Communist Party of Great Britain held its 21st Congress from November 26th to 28th at St. George's Hall in Liverpool.

　　A total of 411 delegates attended the conference, coming from mines, factories, workshops, transportation stations, laboratories, shops, institutions and families across the country. Representatives from the Communist Parties of France, Spain, West Germany, the Netherlands, Belgium, Denmark, Northern Ireland, Czechoslovakia, Italy, and Greece attended the conference as observers.

　　Portraits of Stalin, Mao Zedong, and Pollitt hung around the presidium of the conference.

　　When telegrams of congratulation from the Communist Party of the Soviet Union, the Communist Party of China, the Communist Parties of the United States, Argentina, Finland, Sweden, India, Australia, Austria, the Albanian Labor Party, the German Socialist Unity Party, the Romanian Workers' Party, and the Canadian Labour-Progressive Party were read to the conference, the delegates unanimously rose and applauded thunderously.

　　The conference was opened by A. Horner, member of the Executive Committee of the Communist Party of Great Britain, who vehemently criticized right-wing trade union leaders for striving to split the international working-class movement.

　　General Secretary of the Communist Party of Great Britain, Pollitt, currently in the hospital, submitted a report to the conference, which was read by J. R. Campbell, a member of the Executive Committee. The report contrasted the peaceful progress of the Soviet Union and the New Democratic countries with the crisis in the capitalist countries. The report pointed out that the past year has been characterized by the rapid growth of unemployment and the increasing signs of acute crisis in the capitalist countries. Pollitt pointed out: The problem of the current crisis can only be solved by building a socialist society.

### 庄严保证尽全力阻止新战争　英共代表大会闭幕  通过执委会报告和竞选纲领

1949-12-07
第4版()
专栏：

　　庄严保证尽全力阻止新战争
  　英共代表大会闭幕
    通过执委会报告和竞选纲领
    【新华社布拉格六日电】伦敦航讯：英国共产党于十一月二十六日至二十八日在利物浦圣乔治大厅举行第二十一届代表大会。
    出席大会的代表共四百十一人，他们来自各地的矿坑、工厂、作坊、运输站、实验室、店铺、机关和家庭。列席大会的，有法兰西，西班牙、西德、荷兰、比利时、丹麦、北爱尔兰、捷克斯洛伐克、意大利和希腊的共产党代表。
    在大会主席团的周围，悬挂着斯大林、毛泽东和波立特的肖像。
    当向大会宣读苏联共产党、中国共产党、美国、阿根廷、芬兰、瑞典、印度、澳大利亚、奥地利的共产党，阿尔巴尼亚工人党、德国统一社会党、罗马尼亚工人党和加拿大劳工进步党的贺电时，代表们一致起立，掌声雷动。
    大会由英共执行委员会委员Ａ·霍尔纳致开幕词，他猛烈抨击右翼工会领袖力谋分裂国际工人阶级运动。
    现在医院中的英共总书记波立特向大会提出报告，由执行委员会委员Ｊ·Ｒ·坎培尔向大会宣读。这一报告把苏联和新民主主义国家的和平进展和资本主义国家的危机作了一个对照。报告指出，过去一年的特点，是资本主义国家中失业的迅速增长及尖锐危机迹象的愈来愈多。波立特指出：目前危机的问题，只能用建筑一个社会主义社会来解决。
