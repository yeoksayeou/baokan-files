Title: Commodity Unit Savings Exchange Rate Today

Author: 
Date: 1949-12-22
Page: 3

　　Commodity Unit Savings Exchange Rate Today
    The Beijing Branch Savings Department of the People's Bank of China announces that the commodity unit exchange rate for commodity unit savings today (December 22) is 3,282.73 yuan.
