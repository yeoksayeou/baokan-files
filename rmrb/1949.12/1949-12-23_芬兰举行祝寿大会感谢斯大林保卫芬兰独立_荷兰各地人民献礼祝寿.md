Title: Finland Holds Celebration to Thank Stalin for Defending Finnish Independence; People in Various Parts of the Netherlands Present Gifts to Celebrate His Birthday
Author:
Date: 1949-12-23
Page: 2

Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, December 22nd: TASS, Helsinki: The Finnish-Soviet Association held a celebration on the 20th to commemorate Stalin's 70th birthday. Attendees included Finnish President Paasikivi, Finnish People's Democratic League Chairman Kuusinen, Finnish Communist Party Chairman Aaltonen, representatives from mass organizations and worker groups, Finnish Prime Minister Fagerholm, Speaker of Parliament Kekkonen, Soviet Chargé d'Affaires to Finland Fedorov, and representatives from the diplomatic missions of people's democratic countries in Finland. Finnish-Soviet Association Secretary-General Kahanen delivered the opening address, stating, "Stalin is the consistent defender of our country's independence. The best way for us to express our gratitude to Stalin and the Soviet people is to fight for peace and our country's independence, and to fight against any attempt to once again make Finland a springboard for attacking the Soviet Union." The Finnish Communist Party and Finnish democratic groups also sent telegrams to Stalin to offer their congratulations.

Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, December 22nd: ADN, Amsterdam, December 21st: Parades celebrating Stalin's 70th birthday were held in many places in the Netherlands. In Amsterdam, nearly ten thousand working people participated in the celebration, which also bid farewell to the Soviet peace delegation that had come to the Netherlands at the invitation of the Dutch Peace Committee. At the same time, the Dutch people sent more than two hundred birthday gifts to Soviet Chargé d'Affaires to Amsterdam, Vitrov.

The Dutch Communist Party held a birthday celebration in Amsterdam. Dutch Communist Party General Secretary Groot delivered a speech to the assembly. The "Indonesian Association," a group of Indonesians residing in the Netherlands, sent a telegram to Stalin congratulating him and thanking him and the Soviet people for their support in the colonial peoples' struggle for freedom and independence, stating: "The victory of the Chinese people has proven to the colonial peoples that friendship with the Soviet Union is a solid foundation for defeating the forces of imperialism."

### 芬兰举行祝寿大会感谢斯大林保卫芬兰独立　荷兰各地人民献礼祝寿

1949-12-23
第2版()
专栏：

　　芬兰举行祝寿大会感谢斯大林保卫芬兰独立
    荷兰各地人民献礼祝寿
    【新华社北京二十二日电】塔斯社赫尔辛基讯：芬苏协会于二十日举行庆祝斯大林七十寿辰大会。出席者有芬兰总统巴锡基维，芬兰人民民主联盟主席库洛，芬兰共产党主席亚尔托宁，群众团体和工人团体的代表，芬兰总理法吉荷尔姆，议会主席吉科宁，苏联驻芬代办费多罗夫和人民民主国家驻芬兰外交使团的代表多人。芬苏协会秘书长卡汉宁致开幕词称：“斯大林是我国独立的始终如一的保卫者，我们对斯大林和苏维埃人民表示感激的最好办法，就是为和平与我国的独立而斗争，为反对任何再次使芬兰成为进攻苏联的跳板的图谋而斗争。”芬兰共产党、芬兰民主团体均致电斯大林祝贺。
    【新华社北京二十二日电】电通社阿姆斯特丹二十一日讯：荷尔许多地方都举行了庆祝斯大林七十寿辰的游行。在阿姆斯特丹，近万劳动人民参加了祝寿会，大会并欢送应荷兰和平委员会之邀前来荷兰的苏联和平代表团。同时，荷兰人民送了二百多件寿礼给苏联驻阿姆斯特丹代办维特洛夫。
    荷兰共产党在阿姆斯特丹举行祝寿大会。荷共总书记格鲁特向大会发表演说。侨居荷兰的印尼团体“印尼人协会”致电斯大林祝贺，感谢他和苏联人民给予殖民地人民争取自由与独立之斗争的支持，并称：“中国人民的胜利向殖民地人民证明了：对苏友好是击败帝国主义势力的坚实基础。”
