Title: Four Youth Organizations in Beijing

Author:

Date: 1949-12-08
Page: 3

　　Four Youth Organizations in Beijing
  　Convey Resolutions of All-Circles Representative Conference
    [Beijing News] Representatives from the Beijing Municipal Committee of the Communist Youth League, the Beijing Municipal Joint Association of Primary and Secondary School Teachers, the Preparatory Committee of the Beijing Municipal Democratic Youth Federation, and the Beijing Municipal Student Federation who attended the Second Beijing Municipal People's Representative Conference, in order to implement the conference's resolutions and go among the masses, made reports on the 3rd and 4th, divided by district, to teachers and students of middle schools throughout the city.

### 京市四青年团体

1949-12-08
第3版()
专栏：

　　京市四青年团体
  　传达各界代表会决议
    【北京讯】青年团北京市工委会，北京市中小学教联、北京市民主青联筹委会及北京市学联四团体出席北京市第二届各界人民代表会议的代表，为贯彻大会决议到群众中去，于三、四两日分区向全市中学教职员、学生作传达报告。
