Title: Korean Trade Union Representatives Present Flags to the Chinese Working Class
Author:
Date: 1949-12-03
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, December 2nd: Representatives of the North Korean Federation of Trade Unions, Choi Kyung-duk, and representatives of the South Korean National Council of Korean Labor Unions, Park Se-young, presented two red satin flags with exquisite embroidery to the Chinese working class at the All-China Federation of Trade Unions Auditorium at 2:00 PM today. Li Lisan, Vice Chairman of the All-China Federation of Trade Unions, accepted the flags on behalf of the Chinese working class. Amidst enthusiastic cheers and applause from the crowd, Choi Kyung-duk, Park Se-young, and Li Lisan each delivered brief but excited speeches. One side of the flag presented by the North Korean representative was embroidered with a grand production scene, while the other side was embroidered with a Korean inscription: "Respectfully presented to the heroic Chinese people and their vanguard, the Chinese working class, who, under the leadership of the Chinese Communist Party, have won the People's Republic of China!" One side of the flag presented by the South Korean representative was embroidered with a vivid and heroic scene of guerrilla warfare, while the other side was embroidered with the Korean inscription: "The Chinese working class, standing at the forefront of the heroic Chinese people and establishing the People's Republic of China, should have eternal glory!". Both flags were embroidered with the national flags of the People's Republic of China and the Democratic People's Republic of Korea, symbolizing the close unity between the peoples of the two countries.

### 朝鲜职工团体代表  向中国工人阶级献旗

1949-12-03
第1版()
专栏：

　　朝鲜职工团体代表
    向中国工人阶级献旗
    【新华社北京二日电】北朝鲜职业总同盟代表崔ｊǐｎｇ＠①德，和南朝鲜的朝鲜劳动组合全国评议会代表朴世荣，今日下午二时在全国总工会礼堂，以两面红色缎质刺绣精致的锦旗，献与中国工人阶级，当由中华全国总工会副主席李立三代表接受。崔ｊǐｎｇ＠①德、朴世荣和李立三在群众热烈的欢呼和掌声中，各作简短而兴奋的演说。北朝鲜代表所献旗帜，一面绣着伟大的生产画面，另一面绣着朝鲜文颂词：“谨向在中国共产党的领导下，争取了中华人民共和国的英雄的中国人民和他的先锋队中国工人阶级献以荣誉！”。南朝鲜代表所献旗帜，一面绣着生动的游击战争的英勇画面，另一面所绣的朝鲜文颂词为：“站在英勇的中国人民的前面，树立了中华人民共和国的中国工人阶级应有的永远的光荣！”。两面旗上，都绣有中华人民共和国和朝鲜民主主义人民共和国的国旗，象征着两国人民的亲密团结。
