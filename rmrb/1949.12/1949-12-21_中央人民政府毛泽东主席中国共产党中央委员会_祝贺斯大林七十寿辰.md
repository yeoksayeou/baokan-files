Title: Central People's Government Chairman Mao Zedong, Central Committee of the Communist Party of China Congratulates Stalin on his Seventieth Birthday
Author:
Date: 1949-12-21
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, 20th: Chairman Mao Zedong of our Central People's Government congratulated Chairman of the Council of Ministers of the Soviet Union, Stalin, on his seventieth birthday on the 19th. The congratulatory message is as follows: To Comrade Stalin, Chairman of the Council of Ministers of the Soviet Union:

    On the occasion of your seventieth birthday, please accept our respects, and we wish that the bulwark of world peace and democracy will grow stronger under your leadership.

    　　　　　　　　　　　　Chairman Mao Zedong of the Central People's Government of the People's Republic of China
    　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　December 19, 1949

Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, 20th: The Central Committee of the Communist Party of China yesterday sent a telegram to congratulate Comrade Stalin on his seventieth birthday. The text of the telegram is as follows: Dear Comrade Stalin and Comrades of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of the Soviet Union:

    We, the Chinese Communists, warmly congratulate Comrade Stalin on his glorious seventieth birthday. Comrade Stalin is the greatest Marxist after Lenin. Under the leadership of Comrade Stalin, the Soviet people have built the first socialist society in human history and are advancing towards the construction of a communist society. Under the leadership of Comrade Stalin, the Soviet people and the progressive people of the world have defeated Fascism and are fighting victoriously against the new common enemy, the imperialist aggressors. The awakened Chinese people, like all other awakened people of all countries, regard Stalin as their comrade and friend, and regard Stalin as the banner of unity and victory for the people of the world. Long live Stalin!

    　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　Central Committee of the Communist Party of China
    　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　December 19, 1949

### 中央人民政府毛泽东主席中国共产党中央委员会  祝贺斯大林七十寿辰

1949-12-21
第1版()
专栏：

　　中央人民政府毛泽东主席中国共产党中央委员会
    祝贺斯大林七十寿辰
    【新华社莫斯科二十日电】我中央人民政府毛泽东主席十九日祝贺苏联政府部长会议主席斯大林七十寿辰，祝词如下：苏联政府部长会议主席斯大林阁下：
    欣逢阁下七十寿辰，谨致敬意，并祝世界和平民主堡垒在阁下领导下日益强盛。
    　　　　　　　　　　　中华人民共和国中央人民政府主席毛泽东
    　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　一九四九年十二月十九日
    【新华社北京二十日电】中国共产党中央委员会昨日电贺斯大林同志七十寿辰，电文如下：亲爱的斯大林同志和苏联共产党中央委员会同志们：
    我们中国共产党人热诚地祝贺斯大林同志的光荣的七十岁生日。斯大林同志是列宁以后的最伟大的马克思主义者。在斯大林同志的领导之下，苏联人民建设了人类第一个社会主义社会，并正向着建设共产主义社会前进。在斯大林同志领导之下，苏联人民和全世界进步人类战胜了法西斯主义，并正在向新的共同敌人即帝国主义侵略分子作胜利的斗争。觉悟了的中国人民和所有其他各国觉悟了的人民一样，看斯大林是自己的同志和朋友，看斯大林是全世界人民团结和胜利的旗帜。斯大林万岁！
    　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　中国共产党中央委员会
    　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　一九四九年十二月十九日
