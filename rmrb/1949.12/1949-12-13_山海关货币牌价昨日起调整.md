Title: Shanhaiguan Currency Exchange Rate Adjusted Starting Yesterday
Author:
Date: 1949-12-13
Page: 2

Shanhaiguan Currency Exchange Rate
	Adjusted Starting Yesterday

	[Shanhaiguan News] The Shanhaiguan Joint Office of the People's Bank of China and the Northeast Bank has changed its exchange rate starting yesterday (the 12th) to one yuan of Renminbi for twenty yuan of Northeast currency.

### 山海关货币牌价昨日起调整

1949-12-13
第2版()
专栏：

　　山海关货币牌价
  　昨日起调整
    【山海关讯】中国人民银行、东北银行山海关联合办事处自昨（十二）日起牌价改为人民券一元兑东北券二十元。
