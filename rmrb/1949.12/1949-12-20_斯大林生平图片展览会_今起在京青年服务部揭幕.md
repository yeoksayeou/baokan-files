Title: Exhibition of Stalin's Life in Pictures Opens Today at Beijing Youth Service Department
Author:
Date: 1949-12-20
Page: 2

　　Stalin's Life in Pictures Exhibition
  　Opens Today at Beijing Youth Service Department
    [From Our Newspaper] To celebrate Marshal Stalin's seventieth birthday, the "Youth Service Department," jointly established by the Central Committee of the New Democratic Youth League and the Beijing Working Committee of the Youth League, will hold a photo exhibition at the department from today. The exhibition will introduce Marshal Stalin's life, daily life, and works. All are welcome to visit.

### 斯大林生平图片展览会　今起在京青年服务部揭幕

1949-12-20
第2版()
专栏：

　　斯大林生平图片展览会
  　今起在京青年服务部揭幕
    【本报讯】新民主主义青年团中央委员会与青年团北京市工作委员会共同设立之“青年服务部”，为庆祝斯大林大元帅七十寿辰，于即日起在该部举行斯大林大元帅生平、日常生活及著作介绍的图片展览会。欢迎各界前往参观。
