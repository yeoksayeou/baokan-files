Title: Guo Moruo's Speech: We Must Do a Good Job in the Chinese Revolution as the Best Gift for His Birthday

Author:
Date: 1949-12-22
Page: 2

Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, December 21st. Vice President of the Sino-Soviet Friendship Association, Guo Moruo, delivered a speech at the Sino-Soviet Friendship Association's general meeting celebrating the 70th birthday of Marshal Stalin, which is summarized as follows:

　　Today is Marshal Stalin's 70th birthday, a great celebration for all mankind. With Stalin, Marxism-Leninism has a reliable foundation and development, the political struggle of the proletariat, the revolutionary tasks of the proletarian vanguard, and the liberation cause of all mankind have correct leadership.

　　Stalin is the Marx and Lenin of today, Stalin is the great savior of all mankind. All mankind naturally wants to celebrate his longevity and eternal immortality!

　　To celebrate Stalin, we must learn from Stalin and support Stalin.

　　We must study Stalin's thought, study the Marxism-Leninism that Stalin has developed and promoted, grasp dialectical materialism and historical dialecticism, and flexibly apply it to solve all the problems we have in common with the world in cultural construction and economic construction.

　　We must support Stalin's cause, support Lenin and Stalin's party, support the Soviet Union, which has successfully built socialism, support the peaceful and democratic camp headed by the Soviet Union, work hard and wage an unyielding struggle against imperialism, expose the war conspiracies of the warmongers, strive for the liberation of oppressed nations and people, and strive for lasting peace in the world.

　　These tasks are very arduous. But in China, it is not difficult to accomplish these tasks today. Because we have Chairman Mao Zedong leading us.

　　Chairman Mao Zedong is Marshal Stalin's most outstanding student, most powerful comrade, and comrade-in-arms in China.

　　Stalin is most concerned about our Chinese revolution. The Chinese People's Revolution is an important part of the world revolution. If we do a good job in the Chinese revolution, that will be a very good contribution to the world revolution.

　　In fact, under the leadership of Chairman Mao, we have correctly integrated internationalism and patriotism.

　　We believe that this is the best gift we can give to celebrate Marshal Stalin's 70th birthday. We will continuously increase this gift to celebrate Marshal Stalin's 80th birthday, 90th birthday, 100th birthday, and even his eternal and immortal birthday.

　　Long live Marxism-Leninism!

　　Long live the success of the cause of the liberation of all mankind!

　　Long live, long live, long live the great savior of all mankind, Marshal Stalin!

### 郭沫若演说  我们要做好中国革命作为祝寿的最好礼物

1949-12-22
第2版()
专栏：中苏友协总会祝寿大会演词

　　郭沫若演说
    我们要做好中国革命作为祝寿的最好礼物
    【新华社北京二十一日电】中苏友好协会总会副会长郭沫若在中苏友好协会总会庆祝斯大林大元帅七十寿辰大会上发表演说，略称：
    今天是斯大林大元帅的七十寿辰，是全人类的一个大喜庆。全人类有了斯大林，马克思列宁主义便有了可靠的寄托和发扬，无产阶级的政治斗争、无产阶级先锋队的革命任务、全人类的解放事业，便有了正确的领导。
    斯大林是今天的马克思和列宁，斯大林是全人类的大救星。全人类自然要庆祝他万年长寿，永远不朽！
    我们庆祝斯大林，就要学习斯大林，拥护斯大林。
    我们要学习斯大林的思想，学习斯大林所发扬光大了的马克思列宁主义，掌握唯物辩证主义与历史辩证主义，灵活地运用它，来解决我们自己的和全世界所共通的一切文化建设和经济建设上的问题。
    我们要拥护斯大林的事业，拥护列宁和斯大林的党，拥护社会主义建设成功了的苏联，拥护以苏联为首的和平民主阵营，努力和帝国主义作不屈不挠的斗争，揭破战争贩子们的战争阴谋，争取被压迫民族和人民的解放，争取全世界的持久和平。
    这些任务是很艰巨的。但在我们中国，在今天要完成这些任务也并不困难。因为我们有了毛泽东主席在领导着我们。
    毛泽东主席便是斯大林大元帅在我们中国的最优秀的一位学生，最有力的一位同志和战友。
    斯大林是最关心我们中国的革命问题的。我们中国的人民革命是世界革命的重要的一环。我们如果把中国革命做好了，那就是对于世界革命的很好的贡献。
    事实上，我们在毛主席的领导之下，很正确地把国际主义和爱国主义打成了一片。
    我们相信，这是我们庆祝斯大林大元帅七十大寿的最好的礼物。我们将要把这项礼物不断地墙加，来庆祝斯大林大元帅的八十大寿、九十大寿、一百岁的大寿，乃至万年不朽的永远的大寿。
    马克思列宁主义胜利万岁！
    全人类解放事业成功万岁！
    全人类的救星斯大林大元帅万岁，万万岁！
