Title: Supporting Hometown Elders in Production and Overcoming Famine: A Certain North China Expeditionary Corps Launches Disaster Relief Donation Drive
Author:
Date: 1949-12-30
Page: 2

　　Supporting Hometown Elders in Production and Overcoming Famine
　　Hua Bei A Certain Expeditionary Corps
    Launches Disaster Relief Donation Drive
    [Our Newspaper's Report] The People's Liberation Army North China Field Army, which is marching to the Northwest, is warmly concerned for the elders of North China. The Logistics Department and directly subordinate units of a certain corps have donated over 13,000 catties of millet and over 390,000 yuan in Renminbi to relieve the disaster-stricken people. Currently, disaster relief donations of 370,000 yuan in Renminbi and a letter of sympathy have been sent to the "North China People's Liberation Army" newspaper office, entrusting the newspaper to forward them to relevant organs to relieve the disaster-stricken people. The commanders and fighters of the department expressed their great concern and sympathy for the disaster-stricken elders of North China in the letter, and stated: In order to repay the nurturing and support of the elders and brothers of North China over the past few years, in addition to resolutely implementing the orders of the Central People's Government and Chairman Mao and Commander-in-Chief Zhu, continuing to advance, eliminating the remnant bandits, and thoroughly liberating all of China, they have also launched a disaster relief donation campaign in the Logistics Political Department and directly subordinate units of the corps. Many units and individuals have responded enthusiastically. As of now, a total of 13,473 catties and twelve ounces of millet and 391,700 yuan in Renminbi have been donated.

### 支援故乡父老生产渡荒　华北某出征兵团  展开救灾捐款运动

1949-12-30
第2版()
专栏：

　　支援故乡父老生产渡荒
　  华北某出征兵团
    展开救灾捐款运动
    【本报讯】出征西北的人民解放军华北野战兵团热情开怀华北父老，某某兵团后勤部及直属各单位已捐献小米一万三千余斤，人民币三十九万余元救济灾胞。目前已有救灾捐款人民币三十七万元，及慰问信一封汇抵“华北解放军”报社，托该社代转有关机关，救济灾胞。该部指战员在信上表示对华北受灾父老的极大关怀与同情，并称：为了回答华北父老兄弟几年来的抚养和支援，他们除了坚决执行中央人民政府和毛主席、朱总司令的命令，继续进军，消灭残匪，彻底解放全中国外，并在兵团后勤政治部及直属各单位展开了捐献救灾运动，许多单位及个人纷纷响应，截至现在，共捐出小米一万三千四百七十三斤十二两，人民币三十九万一千七百元。
