Title: Communist Parties in Italy, Austria, Sweden, and Other Countries Hold Meetings, Present Gifts, and Cheer in Celebration; Danish Workers Present Hand-Woven Carpets

Author:

Date: 1949-12-23
Page: 2

Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, December 21st: Teletype from Rome, December 20th: Palmiro Togliatti, General Secretary of the Italian Communist Party, and Pietro Secchia, Deputy General Secretary, have led a delegation to Moscow to represent the Italian Communist Party in the celebrations for Stalin's 70th birthday.

Celebrations are scheduled to be held in Italian towns and villages on the 20th and 21st. The Italian Communist Party's newspaper, *Unità* (Unity), is scheduled to publish a birthday special issue on the 21st, featuring Togliatti's speech on Stalin. The Italian Communist Party's magazines, *Rinascita* (Rebirth) and *Vie Nuove* (New Life), will also publish special issues. The first volume of the Italian translation of Stalin's Complete Works will be published, and a booklet introducing Stalin's life and achievements has been distributed nationwide.

### 意奥瑞典等国共产党　开会献礼欢呼祝寿　丹麦工人敬献手织地毡

1949-12-23
第2版()
专栏：

　　意奥瑞典等国共产党
    开会献礼欢呼祝寿
    丹麦工人敬献手织地毡
    【新华社北京二十一日电】电通社罗马二十日讯：意大利共产党总书记托里亚蒂和副总书记谢嘉，已率领代表团启程赴莫斯科，代表意大利共产党参加斯大林七十寿辰的庆祝。
    意大利各城镇乡村均定于二十、二十一日举行庆祝会。意共机关报团结报定于二十一日出刊祝寿特刊，刊登托里亚蒂关于斯大林的演说。意共机关杂志“再生”和“新生活”，亦将出版特刊。意大利文的斯大林全集的第一卷将出版，一种介绍斯大林生平和成就的小册子，已行销全国。
