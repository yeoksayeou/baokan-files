Title: French Communist Party Central Committee Plenum Concludes; Adopts Important Report by Thorez; National Congress Scheduled for Next April
Author:
Date: 1949-12-26
Page: 4

　　Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, December 25th: Moscow Radio broadcast on the 14th: The plenary session of the Central Committee of the French Communist Party, held in Paris in mid-December, has concluded. French Communist Party General Secretary Thorez delivered an important report at the meeting. French Communist Party Central Committee member Joes Cornie also delivered a report on "The Struggle Against the Tito Murderers and Spy Group" at the meeting. The plenary session passed a resolution to hold the French Communist Party National Congress in Toulouse from April 2nd to 5th, 1950. The agenda of the National Congress is as follows:
　　1. Report of the Central Committee of the French Communist Party (Reporter: Thorez). 2. Report on the Situation of Farmers and the Party's Activities and Tasks (Reporter: Valgic Roshi). 3. Election of the Central Committee and the Central Financial Committee.
　　The Central Committee plenary session also passed general resolutions and resolutions on financial issues.

### 法共中央全会闭幕　通过多列士重要报名  全国代表大会定明年四月举行

1949-12-26
第4版()
专栏：

　　法共中央全会闭幕
　  通过多列士重要报名
    全国代表大会定明年四月举行
    【新华社北京二十五日电】莫斯科电台十四日广播：十二月中旬在巴黎举行的法国共产党中央委员会全会，已告闭幕。法共总书记多列士曾在会上发表重要报告。法共中央委员约尔士·康聂亦在会上发表关于“对铁托杀人犯及间谍集团斗争”的报告。全会通过关于一九五○年四月二日至五日在土鲁斯城举行法共全国代表大会的决议。全国代表大会的议程如下：
    一、法共中央委员会报告（报告人多列士）。二、关于农民情况与党的活动及任务的报告（报告人瓦尔吉克·罗希）。三、中央委员会及中央财务委员会的选举。
    本届中央全会并通过了一般性的决议以及关于财政问题的决议。
