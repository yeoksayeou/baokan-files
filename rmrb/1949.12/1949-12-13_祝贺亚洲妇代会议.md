Title: Congratulations to the Asian Women's Representative Conference
Author:
Date: 1949-12-13
Page: 4

　　French Fourteen Groups' Congratulatory Message
　　Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, December 12th: Fourteen democratic groups, including the French Women's Union, sent a letter to Chinese women to congratulate them on the convening of the Asian Women's Representative Conference in China. The full text of the letter is as follows: Dear Chinese women,
　　On behalf of the two million women members of the French Women's Union, I express my affectionate and warm congratulations on the heroic struggle waged by you and the entire Chinese people.
　　The reason why this unprecedented Asian Women's Representative Conference can be convened is entirely due to your victory. The success of this conference is of great significance in developing the liberation movement of Asian and African women to defend peace.
　　Dear Chinese friends, we are also honored that you have led your struggle with courage and unyielding strength to the final victory, liberating all of China from oppression and exploitation.
　　A vista of a happy and rational life has unfolded before you; we are sincerely delighted.
　　We would also like to thank you for the great help that the birth of the People's Democratic New China has brought to the peace camp.
　　Your example encourages us and makes us feel that our struggle for peace and democracy will never fail.
　　Your success will accelerate the final victory of the people of the world in their struggle for life, happiness and peace.
　　Long live the glorious Chinese women!
　　Long live the women of the world united for happiness and peace!
　　French Women's Union, French Communist Party, French General Confederation of Labor, War Widows Association, War Dead Families Association, French Young Women's Association, National Volunteer Guerrilla People's Power Federation, Single Socialist Party, Progressive Christian Union, Friends of Peace Society, France-Soviet Friendship Association, Prisoners of War Friendship Association, France-Spain Friendship Association, International Women's League.
    German Democratic Women's League Congratulatory Telegram
    Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, December 11th: The German Democratic Women's League sent a telegram to congratulate the opening of the Asian Women's Representative Conference. The telegram reads as follows:
    Asian Women's Representative Conference:
    We, the members of the International Women's Federation, the women of the German Democratic Women's League, pay tribute to the freedom-loving people of Asia, and especially to the Chinese women who are fighting for the happiness of mankind, and wish them even greater success. The German Democratic Republic and the German Democratic Women's League will fully support this heroic struggle for the freedom of the Asian people.
    We extend our highest congratulations to the conference.
    　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　German Democratic Women's League
    　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　Aessa Syumitte
    　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　December 8, 1949

    Albanian Women's Union Congratulatory Telegram
    Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, December 11th: The Albanian Women's Union sent a telegram to congratulate the opening of the Asian Women's Representative Conference. The telegram reads:
    Comrade Cai Chang:
    In the name of the Albanian Women's Union, we warmly pay our respects to the Asian Women's Representative Conference. This conference not only signifies a great success for the Asian women's movement and the Albanian women's movement, but also signifies the success of the world's forces for peace. The women of Albania will always extend the greatest sympathy to your struggle and efforts for rights and against imperialist colonialists. The women of Albania are wholeheartedly concerned with all your efforts to win freedom, win sovereignty, win national independence, and to protect the lives of your children, and are willing to stand with you. Because your struggle is just, and for the peace of the world, we congratulate you on your success!
    　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　Albanian Women's Union

    Northeast, Shanghai Women's Federation Congratulatory Telegram
    Xinhua News Agency, Shenyang, December 12th: The Northeast Democratic Women's Federation and the Preparatory Committee of the Shanghai Democratic Women's Federation have sent telegrams to congratulate the Asian Women's Representative Conference. The Northeast Democratic Women's Federation's congratulatory telegram reads: With great excitement and pleasure, we celebrate the opening of the Asian Women's Representative Conference in Beijing, the capital of the Chinese people. This is an unprecedented great unity of Asian women sisters, and also a great unity of Asian women and world democratic women. It will greatly strengthen the world's forces for peace and democracy and promote the development of the Eastern women's liberation movement. We, the two thousand women of Northeast China, extend our cordial respect and congratulations to all the representatives. We will take practical actions to implement all the resolutions of the conference. We sincerely hope that after the successful conclusion of the conference, the representatives can visit Northeast China, which will greatly help the women's movement in Northeast China. We warmly welcome and await you. The congratulatory telegram from the Preparatory Committee of the Shanghai Democratic Women's Federation said: The Asian Women's Representative Conference is a manifestation of the great unity of Asian women, and also a development and consolidation of the friendship and unity of democratic women around the world. Women have played a great role in the victory of the Chinese people's liberation struggle and in the liberation struggles of the people of Southeast Asian countries. After this meeting, the united strength of peace-loving and democratic women around the world will be further enhanced to jointly strive to safeguard lasting world peace and the welfare of women and children. We, on behalf of all the women of Shanghai, sincerely support the convening of the conference and offer our practical actions as a gift to the conference.

### 祝贺亚洲妇代会议

1949-12-13
第4版()
专栏：

    祝贺亚洲妇代会议
　　法国十四团体祝词
    【新华社北京十二日电】法国妇女联合会等十四个民主团体，为亚洲妇女代表会议在中国召开而致函中国妇女祝贺，该函全文如下：亲爱的中国妇女们：
    我代表法国妇女联合会的二百万妇女会员，对于你们和全中国的人民所进行的英勇的斗争，表示友爱而热烈的庆祝。
    这一个史无前例的亚洲妇女代表会议之所以能够召开，完全是因为有了你们的胜利。这会议的成功，在发展亚非两洲妇女保卫和平的解放运动方面说来，意义是极为广大的。
    亲爱的中国朋友，你们以勇敢和毫不让步的力量领导你们的斗争，一直达到最后胜利，从被压迫与被剥削下解放了全中国，这是我们也引为荣幸的。
    在你们面前已展开了一个幸福而合理的生活的远景；我们由衷地感到愉快。
    人民民主新中国的诞生，给和平阵营带来了一个极大的帮助，这一点也是我们要向你们表示感谢的。
    你们的榜样鼓励了我们，使我们感到我们所进行的和平民主的斗争绝不失败。
    你们的成功，加速了全世界人民为生活、幸福与和平而斗争的最后胜利。
    光荣的中国妇女万岁！
    为幸福与和平而团结的全世界妇女万岁！
    法国妇女联合会、法国共产党、法国总工会、战争寡妇会、作战死亡家属会、法国青年妇女会、全国义勇军游击队人民力量联合会、单一社会党、进步基督教联合会、和平之友社、法苏友好协会、战俘友好协会、法国，西班牙友好协会、国际妇女同盟会
    德国民主妇联贺电
    【新华社北京十一日电】德国民主妇女联合会电贺亚洲妇女代表会议开幕。电文如下：
    亚洲妇女代表会议：
    我们国际妇联的会员，德国民主妇联的妇女们，谨向爱好自由的亚洲人民致敬，特别向为人类幸福而斗争的中国妇女以预祝她们获致更大的成功。民主德国共和国和德国民主妇联，将以全力支持此一争取亚洲人民自由的英勇斗争。
    谨向大会致高度的贺忱。
    　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　德国民主妇女联合会
    　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　爱沙·叙密特
    　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　一九四九年十二月八日
    阿尔巴尼亚妇联贺电
    【新华社北京十一日电】阿尔巴尼亚妇女联合会电贺亚洲妇女代表会议开幕。电文称：
    蔡畅同志：
    我们以阿尔巴尼亚妇女联合会的名义，热烈地向亚洲妇女代表会议表示敬意。这一次的会议不仅表示亚洲妇女运动和阿尔巴尼亚妇女运动是一种大大的成功，而且表示了全世界和平力量的成功。阿尔巴尼亚的妇女，对于你们的为权利的、反帝国主义殖民分子的斗争与努力，永远寄与极大的同情。你们为了赢得自由，赢得主权，赢得民族独立，为了保障孩子们的生活而作的一切努力，阿尔巴尼亚的妇女是全心全意地关切你们，愿和你们站在一道。因为你们的斗争是公正的，是为全世界的和平的，我们祝贺你们成功！
    　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　阿尔巴尼亚妇女联合会
    东北、上海妇联贺电
    【新华社沈阳十二日电】东北民主妇女联合会与上海民主妇联筹委会顷分别电贺亚洲妇女代表会议东北民主妇联贺电称：我们以极大的兴奋和愉快的心情，庆祝亚洲妇女代表会议在中国人民首都北京开幕。这是亚洲妇女姐妹空前的大团结，也是亚洲妇女和世界民主妇女的大团结。她将大大的增强世界和平民主力量，推动东方妇女解放运动的发展，我们东北两千妇女特向诸位代表致以亲切的敬意和祝贺。我们要以实际行动执行大会的一切决议，我们竭诚希望在大会胜利闭幕之后，代表们能光临东北，这对东北妇女运动将有很大帮助。我们热烈欢迎与等待着。上海民主妇女联合会筹备委员会的贺电说：亚洲妇女代表会议是亚洲妇女大团结的表现，也是全世界民主妇女友谊团结的发展和巩固的表现。中国人民解放斗争的胜利与东南亚各国人民在解放斗争中，妇女都曾起了伟大的作用，经过这次会师，将更增强全世界和平民主妇女的团结力量，共同为保障世界的持久和平以及妇女儿童的福利而斗争。我们代表全上海市妇女竭诚拥护会议的召开，以实际行动来作为我们对会议的献礼。
