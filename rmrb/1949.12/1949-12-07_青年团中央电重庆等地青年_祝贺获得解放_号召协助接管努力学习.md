Title: Youth League Central Committee Sends Telegram to Youth in Chongqing and Other Places: Congratulating on Liberation, Calling on Them to Assist in Takeover and Strive to Study
Author:
Date: 1949-12-07
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, December 6th: The Central Committee of the China New Democratic Youth League sent a telegram to the youth of Chongqing and all newly liberated cities, congratulating them on their liberation. The telegram reads as follows: Youth of Chongqing and all newly liberated cities:

Congratulations on your liberation. Under the dark rule of the Kuomintang reactionaries, you bravely launched a series of struggles against the US-Chiang regime. Now, under the leadership of the People's Government, it is believed that you will actively assist in the takeover work, strive to study revolutionary theory and various knowledge, establish a revolutionary outlook on life of serving the people, and strive to build a new democratic New China.

　　　　　　　　　　　　　　Central Committee of the China New Democratic Youth League
　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　December 4th

### 青年团中央电重庆等地青年  祝贺获得解放　号召协助接管努力学习

1949-12-07
第1版()
专栏：

　　青年团中央电重庆等地青年
    祝贺获得解放
  　号召协助接管努力学习
    【新华社北京六日电】中国新民主主义青年团中央委员会电贺重庆市和一切新解放城市的青年获得解放，电文如下：重庆市和一切新解放城市的青年们：
    庆贺你们获得了解放。在国民党反动派的黑暗统治下，你们曾英勇地展开一连串的反对美蒋的斗争。现在在人民政府领导下，相信你们定能积极协助接管工作，努力学习革命理论和各种知识，树立为人民服务的革命人生观，为建设新民主主义的新中国而奋斗。
    　　　　　　　　　　　　　　中国新民主主义青年团中央委员会
    　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　十二月四日
