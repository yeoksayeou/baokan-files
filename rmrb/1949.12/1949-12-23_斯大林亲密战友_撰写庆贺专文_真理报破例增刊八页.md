Title: Stalin's Close Comrades Write Congratulatory Articles; Pravda Publishes Unprecedented Eight-Page Supplement
Author:
Date: 1949-12-23
Page: 1

　　Stalin's Close Comrades
    Write Congratulatory Articles
    Pravda Publishes Unprecedented Eight-Page Supplement
    Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, December 22nd: TASS, Moscow: On the 21st, Pravda published twelve pages, breaking the past convention of four pages. In addition to publishing the decree of the Presidium of the Supreme Soviet of the USSR and letters and telegrams of congratulations to Stalin, the newspaper also published special articles by Stalin's closest comrades on Stalin's life and work. These included Malenkov's "Comrade Stalin—Leader of Progressive Humanity"; Molotov's "Stalin and Stalin's Leadership"; Beria's "Great Inspirer and Organizer of the Victory of Communism"; Voroshilov's "Genius Leader of the Great Patriotic War"; Mikoyan's "Great Architect of Communism"; Kaganovich's "Stalin Leads Us to the Victory of Communism"; Bulganin's "Stalin and the Soviet Armed Forces"; Andreyev's "Stalin and the Collective Farm Peasants"; Khrushchev's "Stalin Friendship Between Nationalities—Our Invincible Guarantee"; Kosygin's "Our Victory is Due to the Great Stalin"; Shvernik's "Comrade Stalin—Successor to Lenin's Great Cause"; Shkiryatov's "The People Praise Comrade Stalin"; and Poskrebyshev's "Beloved Father and Great Mentor."
    On the 21st, Izvestia also published the following articles for Stalin's 70th birthday: "Comrade Stalin—Creator and Leader of the Multi-ethnic Soviet State" by Gorkin, Secretary of the Presidium of the Supreme Soviet of the USSR; "Stalin Cares for Soviet Citizens" by women's social worker Popova; and "The Pride and Joy of Our Lives" by writer Vsevolod Ivanov.
    Red Star published an article by Marshal Vasilevsky, Minister of the Armed Forces of the USSR, "Comrade Stalin—The Founder of the Armed Forces of the Soviet State."
    Newspapers continue to widely report on the results of the national mass socialist competition held to celebrate Comrade Stalin's 70th birthday. The foreign news column reported on the celebrations of Stalin's 70th birthday by the working people of the world.

### 斯大林亲密战友　撰写庆贺专文　真理报破例增刊八页

1949-12-23
第1版()
专栏：

　　斯大林亲密战友
    撰写庆贺专文
    真理报破例增刊八页
    【新华社北京二十二日电】塔斯社莫斯科讯：二十一日，真理报出了十二页，打破了过去四页的惯例。该报除了刊登苏联最高苏维埃主席团命令和致斯大林的祝寿函电外，并发表了斯大林最亲密的战友关于斯大林生活及工作的专文。内有马林科夫的“斯大林同志——进步人类的领袖”；莫洛托夫的“斯大林和斯大林的领导”；贝利亚的“共产主义胜利的伟大鼓舞者与组织者”；伏罗希洛夫的“伟大爱国战争的天才领袖”；米高扬的“共产主义的伟大建筑师”；卡冈诺维奇的“斯大林领导我们走向共产主义的胜利”；布尔加宁的“斯大林和苏联武装部队”；安得烈夫的“斯大林和集体农场农民”；赫鲁舍切夫的“各民族间的斯大林友谊——我国不可战胜的保证”；柯西金的“我们的胜利归功于伟大的斯大林”；什维尔尼克的“斯大林同志——列宁伟大事业的继承人”；施基利亚托夫的“人民颂扬斯大林同志”；和波斯克里彼舍夫的“挚爱的父亲和伟大的导师。”
    二十一日的消息报也为斯大林七十寿辰而刊登了以下的文章：苏联最高苏维埃主席团秘书高尔金的“斯大林同志——多民族苏维埃国家的缔造者和领袖”；妇女社会工作者波波娃的“斯大林关怀苏联公民”，和作家维西沃洛德·伊凡诺夫的“我们生活的骄傲和快乐”。
    红星报刊登了苏联武装部队部部长、苏联元帅华西列夫斯基的文章：“斯大林同志——苏维埃国家武装部队的创建者”。
    各报继续广泛反映为庆祝斯大林同志七十寿辰而举行的全国规模的群众性的社会主义竞赛的成果。外国消息栏则报道了全世界劳动人民庆祝斯大林七十寿辰的情形。
