Title: Inaugural Meeting of the International Transport Workers' Trade Union Department Concludes
Author:
Date: 1949-12-20
Page: 4

　　Xinhua News Agency, Bucharest, 19th -- The inaugural meeting of the International Trade Union Federation of Transport Workers, which opened here on the 10th, concluded on the 15th. Delegations from the Soviet Union, China, Poland, Romania, Czechoslovakia, Germany, France, Italy, and other countries attended the meeting. Apostol, an executive member of the World Federation of Trade Unions, and Rostovky, Secretary of the World Federation of Trade Unions, also attended to provide guidance. After listening to reports from the delegations of various countries and conducting discussions, the conference elected an executive committee with the French citizen Tronel as chairman and the Romanian citizen Moraru as general secretary.
    The conference adopted a letter to transport workers all over the world, calling on them to fight more resolutely for peace, freedom, democracy, and the solidarity of the international working class. The conference also sent a telegram to Stalin and the World Federation of Trade Unions, vowing to wage a relentless struggle against warmongers.

### 国际运输工人工会部成立大会闭幕

1949-12-20
第4版()
专栏：

　　国际运输工人工会部成立大会闭幕
    【新华社北京十九日电】布加勒斯特讯：国际运输工人工会联合会成立大会，于十日此间开幕，并已于十五日闭幕。出席大会的有苏、中、波、罗、捷、德、法、意等国的代表团。世界工会联合会执行委员阿波斯托尔、世界工联书记罗斯托夫斯基等，亦出席指导。大会于听取各国代表团报告及进行讨论后，选出以法国的特罗纳尔为主席和以罗马尼亚的莫拉鲁为总书记的执行委员会。
    大会通过告全世界运输工人书，号召全世界的运输工人，更加坚决地为和平、自由、民主与国际工人阶级的团结而斗争。大会并致电斯大林及世界工联，誓言与战争贩子进行无情的斗争。
