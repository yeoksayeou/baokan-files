Title: Debao Labor People's Delegation Arrives in Soviet Capital to Attend Birthday Celebration
Author:
Date: 1949-12-20
Page: 2

　　Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, 19th. TASS news agency reports: The Labor People's Delegation of the German Democratic Republic, headed by Ulbricht and Döttinger, arrived in Moscow on December 18 to participate in the celebrations for Comrade Stalin's 70th birthday. The Labor People's Delegation of the People's Republic of Bulgaria, headed by Chervenkov, Zhankov, and Nechev, also arrived in Moscow for the same purpose. When the delegations of the two countries arrived, representatives of the Committee established by the Presidium of the Supreme Soviet of the Soviet Union to celebrate Stalin's 70th birthday, representatives of the Soviet Ministry of Foreign Affairs, and the diplomatic envoys of the two countries in Moscow went to welcome them.

### 德保劳动人民代表团抵苏京参加祝寿大典

1949-12-20
第2版()
专栏：

　　德保劳动人民代表团抵苏京参加祝寿大典
    【新华社北京十九日电】综合塔斯社消息：以乌布利希和窦丁格尔为首的德意志民主共和国劳动人民代表团，于十二月十八日抵达莫斯科，以参加斯大林同志七十寿辰的庆祝大典。以契尔文科夫、詹科夫和奈捷夫三人为首的保加利亚人民共和国劳动人民代表团，也为着同一目的，到达莫斯科。当两国代表团先后到达时，苏联最高苏维埃主席团为庆祝斯大林七十寿辰而设立的委员会的代表、苏联外交部代表和该两国驻莫斯科的外交使节，均曾分别前往欢迎。
