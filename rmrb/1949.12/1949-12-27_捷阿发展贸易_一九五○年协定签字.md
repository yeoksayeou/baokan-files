Title: Czech-Albanian Trade Development: 1950 Agreement Signed
Author:
Date: 1949-12-27
Page: 4

　　Czech-Albanian Trade Development
　  1950 Agreement Signed
    [Xinhua News Agency, Tirana, 26th] TASS, Tirana: As a result of friendly negotiations between the People's Republic of Albania and the Republic of Czechoslovakia, the two countries signed a trade agreement for 1950 in Tirana on December 22. The agreement was signed by Czechoslovak Republic's Envoy Extraordinary and Minister Plenipotentiary to Albania, Orban, and Albanian Minister of Trade, Noçira, on behalf of their respective governments. The agreement stipulates that the trade volume between the two countries in 1950, both in terms of goods exchange and monetary value, should be higher than in 1949.

### 捷阿发展贸易　一九五○年协定签字

1949-12-27
第4版()
专栏：

　　捷阿发展贸易
　  一九五○年协定签字
    【新华社北京二十六日电】塔斯社地拉那讯：阿尔巴尼亚人民共和国及捷克斯洛伐克共和国经过友谊谈判结果，两国已于十二月二十二日在地拉签订一九五○年贸易协定。协定由捷克斯洛伐克共和国驻阿尔巴尼亚特命全权公使俄尔班及阿尔巴尼亚贸易部长诺齐拉代表两国政府签字。协定规定：一九五零年两国的贸易额，不论在货物交换方面及在货款方面应较一九四九年为高。
