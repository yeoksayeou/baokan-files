Title: A Tribute to Stalin
Author: Amita Devi
Date: 1949-12-21
Page: 2

　　A Tribute to Stalin
    Amita Devi, Indian Women's Representative, Asian Women's Congress
    Dear Comrade Stalin:
    We, the Indian representatives attending the Asian Women's Congress, extend our heartfelt congratulations to you. Your longevity is the longevity of the people of the world who are fighting for human rights. Dear Comrade, the greatest defender of peace and comrade-in-arms in the cause of liberation for oppressed peoples, we salute you.

### 向斯大林致敬
阿米塔·台维
1949-12-21
第2版()
专栏：

　　向斯大林致敬
    亚洲妇代会、印度妇女代表  阿米塔·台维亲爱的斯大林同志：
    我们出席亚洲妇女代表会议的印度代表们向你致衷心的祝贺。你的长寿也就是世界为人权斗争的人民的长寿。亲爱的同志，最伟大的和平保卫者，和被压迫民族解放事业的战友，我们向你致敬。
