Title: Unite Under the Great Banner of Stalin, Consolidate Sino-Soviet Friendship, and Defend World Peace! Various Chinese Parties and Organizations Send Congratulatory Telegrams on Stalin's Birthday
Author:
Date: 1949-12-20
Page: 2

Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, December 19th: The Enlarged Fourth Plenary Session of the China Democratic League has sent a telegram congratulating Marshal Stalin on his 70th birthday. The text of the telegram is as follows:

Dear Marshal Stalin: This year, December 21st is your 70th birthday. We, all members of the China Democratic League, extend our warmest and sincerest congratulations to you. Under your brilliant and wise leadership, the working people of the world are carrying out victorious struggles for their own freedom and happiness, for socialism and people's democracy. All peace-loving and democratic people in the world are uniting under your great banner, carrying out unyielding struggles against imperialist aggression. The Soviet working people, thanks to your brilliant leadership, have achieved victorious socialist construction and are further transitioning from socialism to the lofty and beautiful ideal of communism. This is also the great common goal of the working people of the world. We, the Chinese working people, have basically completed the revolutionary mission against US imperialism and its running dog, the Chiang Kai-shek bandit gang, under the great banners of you and Mao Zedong, and are beginning to build an independent, free, and prosperous New Democratic country. We firmly believe that the people of New China will always achieve brilliant victories in the great cause of striving for world peace and building New Democracy under the wise leadership of you and Mao Zedong. Your banner is the banner of the people's victory. We extend to you the most sublime revolutionary salute!

The Enlarged Fourth Plenary Session of the China Democratic League
December 19th

Central Committee of the Chinese Peasants' and Workers' Democratic Party

To Marshal Stalin, Moscow: You are the great teacher of the world revolution. All oppressed classes, oppressed nations, and peace-loving people are willing to unite under your banner to strive for the happiness of mankind. Your 70th birthday symbolizes the development of a new life for mankind. We send you our congratulations and wish you good health. The Central Committee of the Chinese Peasants' and Workers' Democratic Party respectfully sends greetings.

Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, December 19th: The Central Committee of the Chinese New Democratic Youth League sent a telegram congratulating Marshal Stalin on his 70th birthday. The text of the telegram is as follows: Respected Teacher - Comrade Stalin:

We, on behalf of all members of the New Democratic Youth League, warmly congratulate you on your 70th birthday. In the past long years of arduous struggle, we, the Chinese youth and the Chinese people, have received help, sympathy, and encouragement from you and the Soviet people under your leadership, which we will never forget. We respectfully wish you good health and wish the people and youth of China and the Soviet Union to be forever closely united.

The Central Committee of the Chinese New Democratic Youth League
December 18th

Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, December 19th: The National Seamen's Union Representative Conference today sent a telegram congratulating Stalin on his 70th birthday. The text of the telegram is as follows:

Respected Marshal Stalin:

We, the representatives of the National Seamen's Union, are meeting in Beijing. Rejoicing at your 70th birthday, we extend to you our highest respect and heartfelt congratulations.

Thank you and the socialist Soviet Union under your leadership for the sympathy and assistance you have given to the Chinese working class and people in their struggle for liberation. We, the Chinese seamen workers, vow to devote ourselves wholeheartedly to the restoration and development of New China's water transport industry, consolidate Sino-Soviet friendship, and resolutely defend world lasting peace under the leadership of your close comrade-in-arms and best student - the great leader of the Chinese people, Chairman Mao Zedong, as a gift to celebrate your birthday. We respectfully wish you

Long life
The Representative Conference of the All-China Seamen's Union
December 19th

### 团结在斯大林伟大旗帜下，巩固中苏友谊保卫世界和平！  我国各党派团体电贺斯大林寿辰

1949-12-20
第2版()
专栏：

　　团结在斯大林伟大旗帜下，巩固中苏友谊保卫世界和平！
    我国各党派团体电贺斯大林寿辰
　　民盟四中全会扩大会议
    【新华社北京十九日电】中国民主同盟四中全会扩大会议，顷致电祝贺斯大林大元帅七十寿辰，电文如下：
    亲爱的斯大林大元帅：今年十二月二十一日是您的七十寿辰，我们中国民主同盟全体盟员，特向您致热烈诚挚的贺忱。在您的天才英明领导之下，全世界劳动人民正在为自身的自由和幸福，为社会主义和人民民主主义进行着胜利的斗争。全世界爱好和平民主的人民，正团结在您的伟大旗帜之下，进行着反帝国主义反侵略战争的不屈不挠的斗争。苏维埃的劳动人民，由于您的天才领导，实现了胜利的社会主义建设，并且正在进一步由社会主义过渡到共产主义这个崇高美满的理想目标。这也就是全世界劳动人民共同的伟大目标。我们中国劳动人民，业已在您和毛泽东的伟大旗帜之下，基本上完成了反对美帝国主义及其走狗蒋介石匪帮的革命使命，并正在开始建设一个独立、自由、富强的新民主主义的国家。我们深信，新中国的人民将永远在您和毛泽东的英明领导之下，在争取世界和平和建设新民主主义的伟大事业中，不断地取得光辉的胜利。您的旗帜，就是人民胜利的旗帜。谨向您致最崇高的革命敬礼！
　　中国民主同盟四中全会扩大会议
　　十二月十九日
　　中国农工民主党中央委员会
    莫斯科斯大林大元帅：您是全世界革命的伟大导师，所有一切被压迫的阶级，被压迫的民族，和爱好和平的人民，都愿意团结在您的旗帜之下，为获得人类的幸福而奋斗；您的七十寿辰，正象征着人类的新生命的发展，我们谨电致贺，并祝健康。中国农工民主党中央委员会叩号。
　　新民主主义青年团中央委员会
    【新华社北京十九日电】中国新民主主义青年团中央委员会电贺斯大林大元帅七十寿辰，电文如下：敬爱的导师——斯大林同志：
    我们代表新民主主义青年团全体团员，热烈地祝贺您的七十寿辰。在过去漫长的艰苦斗争的岁月里，我们中国青年和中国人民，得到您和您所领导的苏联人民的帮助、同情和鼓舞，使我们永志不忘。我们敬祝您的身体康健，敬祝中苏两国人民和青年永远地亲密团结。
　　中国新民主主义青年团中央委员会
　　十二月十八日
　　全国海员工会代表会议
    【新华社北京十九日电】全国海员工会代表会议今日致电祝贺斯大林七十寿辰，电文如下：
    敬爱的斯大林大元帅：
    我们全国海员工会代表正在北京集会，欣逢你的七十大寿，我们谨向你致崇高的敬意与衷心的祝贺。
    感谢你和在你领导下的社会主义的苏联，在我们中国工人阶级与人民争取解放斗争中所给予的同情和援助，我们中国海员工人誓在你的亲密的战友与最好的学生——中国人民伟大领袖毛泽东主席的领导之下，全心全力为恢复与发展新中国的水上运输事业，巩固中苏友谊和坚决保卫世界持久和平，作为向你祝寿的献礼，敬祝你
    万寿无疆
    中华全国海员工会代表会议
　　十二月十九日
