Title: North Korean People Warmly Celebrate Birthday; Pyongyang Filled with Festive Atmosphere; Simultaneous Rallies Held Across Regions Last Night
Author:
Date: 1949-12-21
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Pyongyang, December 20th: The movement of the North Korean working people to celebrate the 70th birthday of the nation's savior, Marshal Stalin, has reached its climax. Under the auspices of the Fatherland Unification Democratic Front, rallies will be held simultaneously in all provinces, cities, and counties on the evening of the 20th. Thousands of congratulatory telegrams will be sent to the Kremlin. From the 15th, factories, institutions, schools, military units, rural villages, and fishing villages have been holding various lectures and discussions on Stalin, as well as exhibitions of Stalin's photos and works, and screenings of films about Stalin. The streets of Pyongyang are filled with a festive atmosphere, and the song "Ode to Stalin" can be heard everywhere. Stalin's works are selling unprecedentedly well in bookstores. The "Labor News," the organ of the Labor Party, published a special issue on the 19th, containing a lengthy paper jointly published by the Propaganda and Agitation Department of the Central Committee of the All-Union Communist Party (Bolsheviks) and the Marx-Engels-Lenin Institute, entitled "Stalin is the Great Leader and Teacher of the Communist Party and the Soviet People." Radio stations are constantly broadcasting speeches introducing Stalin's revolutionary activities. The exhibition organized by the Korean-Soviet Culture Association has attracted tens of thousands of men and women since its opening on the 17th. The birthday celebration meeting jointly organized by the Korean-Soviet Culture Association and the Soviet Society for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries, and the birthday celebration meeting organized by the Central Committee of the Fatherland Front, will be held on the 20th and 21st respectively at the Cultural Hall and the National Art Theater.

### 北朝鲜人民热烈祝寿　平壤充满节日气氛　各地昨晚同时举行大会

1949-12-21
第4版()
专栏：

　　北朝鲜人民热烈祝寿
    平壤充满节日气氛
    各地昨晚同时举行大会
    【新华社平壤二十日电】北朝鲜劳动人民祝贺民族救星——斯大林大元帅七十寿辰的运动达到最高潮，在祖国统一民主主义战线主持下，各道、市、郡将于二十日晚同时举行祝寿大会。千百通祝寿的电报，将拍到克里姆林宫去。工厂、机关、学校、部队、农村、渔村、从十五日起，即纷纷举行关于办斯大林的各种讲演会、座谈会，并举办斯大林照片、著作的展览会，放映有关斯大林的影片。平壤街头充满节日的气氛，到处听见“斯大林颂”的歌声。书店里的斯大林著作，空前畅销。劳动党机关报“劳动新闻”，十九日发行特刊，登载联共中央宣传鼓动部和马恩列研究院所联合发表的题为“斯大林是共产党和苏维埃人民的伟大领袖和导师”的长篇论文。广播电台不断播送介绍斯大林革命活动的演讲。朝苏文化协会主办的展览会，从十七日开幕以来，吸引着数以万计的男女观众。朝苏文化协会和苏联对外文化关系协会合办的祝寿大会，和祖国战线中央委员会主办的祝寿大会，于二十、二十一两日分别在文化馆和国立艺术剧场举行。
