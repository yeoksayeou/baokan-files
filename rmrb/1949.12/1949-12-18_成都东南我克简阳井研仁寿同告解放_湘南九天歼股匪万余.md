Title: Chengdu's Southeast, Jianyang, Jingyan, and Renshou are Liberated; In Southern Hunan, Over 10,000 Bandits are Eliminated in Nine Days
Author:
Date: 1949-12-18
Page: 2

Xinhua News Agency, Southwest Front, 17th: A certain unit of the People's Liberation Army on the Southwest Front liberated Jianyang County seat, 90 li southeast of Chengdu, on the 15th, annihilating one enemy battalion. Another PLA unit liberated Jingyan and Renshou county seats, south of Chengdu, on the 14th and 16th respectively.

Xinhua News Agency, Southwest Front, 16th: A certain unit of the People's Liberation Army annihilated one regiment of the 31st Division of the 27th Army southeast of Jingyan, south of Chengdu, on the 14th, killing, wounding, and capturing over 1,400 enemy soldiers including battalion commanders.

Xinhua News Agency, Southwest Front, 16th: All units under the Command of the People's Liberation Army for Suppressing Bandits in Southern Hunan, in coordination with the local forces of the Lingling and Chenzhou sub-districts, launched a campaign against the bandit gangs of Wang Chunhui, Xie Shengyi, and others entrenched in Linwu, Lanshan, and Jiahe in Southern Hunan. In the nine days of combat from the 1st to the 9th of this month, the bandits have been basically eliminated. The preliminary statistics of the battle results are as follows:

	(1) 10,559 bandits were eliminated, including the capture of the Southern Hunan Column Commander Dian Baomeng, Deputy Commander Yan Xiewei, Division Commander Xie Shengyi, Deputy Brigade Commander of the Traffic Police Second Brigade Zou Tianyuan, Major General Commander of the Traffic Police Fourteenth Detachment Li Zhimin, Major General Deputy Commander of the Eighteenth Detachment Wang Ruiwen, and 8,200 other officers and soldiers. 400 bandit officers and soldiers were killed or wounded, and 1,959 bandit soldiers surrendered.

	(2) The following were seized: 3 mortars, 4 sixty-mortars, 10 small cannons, 73 grenade launchers, 20 rocket launchers, 61 heavy machine guns, 112 light machine guns, 1,040 submachine guns, 1,069 carbines, 6,645 rifles and other military supplies.

### 成都东南我克简阳井研仁寿同告解放　湘南九天歼股匪万余

1949-12-18
第2版()
专栏：

　　成都东南我克简阳井研仁寿同告解放
  　湘南九天歼股匪万余
    【新华社西南前线十七日电】西南前线解放军某部十五日解放成都东南九十华里的简阳县城，歼敌一个营。另部解放军十四、十六两日解放成都以南的井研、仁寿两县城。
    【新华社西南前线十六日电】人民解放军某部，十四日在成都以南之井研东南歼敌二十七军三十一师一个团，毙伤俘敌营长以下一千四百余。
    【新华社西南前线十六日电】人民解放军湘南剿匪指挥部所属各部，配合零陵、郴州两分区地方部队，进剿盘距湘南临武、蓝山、嘉禾的股匪王春晖、谢声溢等部，自本月一日至九日的九天作战中，已将该匪基本消灭。此役战果初步统计如下：
    （一）歼匪一万零五百五十九名，其中俘匪湘南纵队司令典保梦、副司令晏协威、师长谢声溢、交警第二旅副旅长邹田元、交警十四总队少将总队长李治民、十八总队少将副总队长汪瑞文以下官兵八千二百名，毙伤匪官兵四百名，匪军投降者一千九百五十九名。
    （二）缴获迫击炮三门、六零炮四门、小炮十门、掷弹筒七十三个、火箭筒二十个、重机枪六十一挺、轻机枪一一二挺、冲锋枪一零四零支、卡宝枪一零六九支、长短枪六六四五支，其他军用物资一部。
