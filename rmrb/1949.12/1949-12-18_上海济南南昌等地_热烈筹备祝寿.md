Title: Shanghai, Jinan, Nanchang and other places enthusiastically prepare for birthday celebrations
Author:
Date: 1949-12-18
Page: 2
Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, December 17th: Shanghai, Jinan, Nanchang and other places are enthusiastically preparing to celebrate Marshal Stalin's 70th birthday. The preparatory committee of the Sino-Soviet Friendship Association Shanghai Branch is scheduled to hold a birthday celebration meeting on the morning of the 21st, and formally establish the "Sino-Soviet Friendship Association Shanghai Branch." The association has commissioned a beautifully crafted commemorative album and is widely soliciting inscriptions, paintings or signatures from people from all walks of life as a congratulatory gift to Marshal Stalin. In order to launch a signature campaign of 200,000 people to celebrate Stalin's birthday, the Jinan Branch of the Sino-Soviet Friendship Association held a gathering on the evening of the 16th to publicize the significance of the signature campaign. Peng Kang, Minister of the Propaganda Department of the Shandong Sub-bureau of the CPC Central Committee, Wang Zhe, Director of the Education Department of the Shandong Provincial Government, Xia Zhengnong, Minister of the Propaganda Department of the Jinan Municipal Committee of the CPC, and Zhang Bochuan, Vice President of East China University, reported on Stalin's life and his relationship with the Chinese revolution and the world revolution. The city's Federation of Trade Unions also called on trade unions in various factories to widely publicize the Soviet Union's assistance to our country's revolution and mobilize trade union members to participate in the Sino-Soviet Friendship Association. Representatives from all walks of life in Nanchang City jointly formed a birthday celebration preparatory committee on the 14th, and decided to designate the period from the 15th to the 21st as a publicity week to mobilize various agencies and schools to publicize Stalin's life and Soviet construction experience. On the 21st, cadres from various agencies and representatives from all walks of life will collectively celebrate his birthday.

### 上海济南南昌等地　热烈筹备祝寿

1949-12-18
第2版()
专栏：

　　上海济南南昌等地
  　热烈筹备祝寿
    【新华社北京十七日电】上海、济南、南昌等地热烈筹备庆祝斯大林大元帅七十寿辰。中苏友好协会上海分会筹备会定二十一日上午举行祝寿大会，并正式成立“中苏友好协会上海分会”该会已定制精美纪念册一本，广泛征求各界人士题辞，绘画或签名，作为献给斯大林大元帅的贺礼。中苏友好协会济南分会为了展开二十万人的签名祝寿运动，特于十六日晚举行集会，宣传签名运动的意义，由中共中央山东分局宣传部部长彭康、山东省政府教育厅厅长王哲、中共济南市委宣传部部长夏征农及华东大学副校长张勃川等报告有关斯大林的生平及其与中国革命、世界革命的关系。该市总工会并号召各工厂工会广泛宣传苏联对我国革命的帮助，发动工会会员参加中苏友好协会。南昌市各界代表已于十四日联合组成祝寿筹委会，决定自十五日至二十一日为宣传周，发动各机关、学校宣传斯大林的生平及苏联建设经验， 二十一日由各机关干部及各界代表集体祝寿。
