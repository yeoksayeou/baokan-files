    Important Correction
    In the twenty-fifth issue of this publication, we mistakenly used the photo of "Captain Maisky" for Dr. Rogojin, and the photo of "Dr. Rogojin" for Captain Maisky. We hereby correct the error and apologize to the individuals involved.
