Title: Khrushchev Appointed First Secretary of the Moscow Oblast Committee of the CPSU

Author:

Date: 1949-12-20
Page: 4

　　Khrushchev Appointed First Secretary of the Moscow Oblast Committee of the CPSU
    [Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, 19th] TASS, Moscow: The joint plenary session of the Moscow Oblast Committee and the Moscow City Committee of the Communist Party of the Soviet Union was held from the 13th to the 16th. The meeting, considering that the First Secretary of the Moscow Oblast and Moscow City Committees, Popov, had been transferred to a responsible position in urban development, passed a resolution to relieve Popov of his duties and unanimously elected Khrushchev, the former First Secretary of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of Ukraine, to succeed him as the First Secretary of the Moscow Oblast Committee. At the same time, at its recent plenary session, the Central Committee of the Communist Party of Ukraine also passed a resolution to relieve Khrushchev of his position as First Secretary of the Communist Party of Ukraine and his positions as a member of the Political Bureau and the Organization Bureau of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of Ukraine. The meeting unanimously elected the Second Secretary of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of Ukraine, Mirnikov, to succeed Khrushchev as the First Secretary of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of Ukraine, and elected Kirichenko to succeed him as the Second Secretary of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of Ukraine and as a member of the Political Bureau and the Organization Bureau.

### 赫鲁舍切夫任联共莫斯科州委第一书记

1949-12-20
第4版()
专栏：

　　赫鲁舍切夫任联共莫斯科州委第一书记
    【新华社北京十九日电】据塔斯社莫斯科讯：苏联共产党莫斯科州委员会与莫斯科市委员会，在十三日至十六日举行全体联席会议。会议考虑到莫斯科州与莫斯科市委员会第一书记波波夫已调任城市发展方面的负责工作，遂通过决议解除波波夫的职务，并一致选举原任乌克兰共产党中央委员会第一书记赫鲁舍切夫继任莫斯科州委会第一书记。同时，乌克兰共产党中央委员会在最近举行的全体会议上，亦已通过决议，解除了赫鲁舍切夫乌共第一书记，及其担任乌共中央委员会政治局与组织局委员的职务，会议并一致选举乌共中央第二书记米尔尼科夫继任乌共中央第一书记，选举基利琴科继任乌共中央第二书记及政治局与组织局委员。
