Title: Southern Shaanxi Liberates Zhashui and Zhen'an; Shangluo Region Entirely Liberated
Author:
Date: 1949-12-04
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, Xi'an, 3rd. A portion of the people's local armed forces of the Xianyang Region liberated the cities of Zhashui and Zhen'an on November 21st and 24th respectively. With this, the seven counties of the Shangluo Region – Luonan, Shangxian, Shangnan, Danfeng (newly established county, formerly Longjuzhai), Shanyang, Zhen'an, and Zhashui – are entirely liberated.

### 陕南解放柞水镇安　商洛分区全部解放

1949-12-04
第1版()
专栏：

　　陕南解放柞水镇安
  　商洛分区全部解放
    【新华社西安三日电】咸阳分区人民地方武装一部，于十一月二十一、二十四两日先后解放柞水、镇安两城。至此，商洛分区，洛南、商县、商南、丹凤（新设县、原为龙驹寨）、山阳、镇安、柞水等七县已告全部解放。
