Title: Shijiazhuang Cadres Study United Front Policy; Municipal Study Committee Makes Specific Arrangements
Author:
Date: 1949-12-06
Page: 3

　　Shijiazhuang City Cadres
  　Study United Front Policy
  　Municipal Study Committee Makes Specific Arrangements
    [From Our Newspaper] The Shijiazhuang Municipal Study Committee issued a notice a few days ago, arranging for all city cadres to study the United Front policy. The entire month of December is designated as the study period. The study requires each cadre to have a relatively comprehensive understanding of the United Front, to strengthen unity in practical work, and to achieve the goal of improving work. The designated study documents are: the first half of the "Communist" inaugural editorial, Chapter 4 of "On Coalition Government," Section 5 of "On New Democracy," Section 7 of "The Present Situation and Our Tasks," Chapter 2, Section 5 of "The Chinese Revolution and the Chinese Communist Party," the second half of "On the People's Democratic Dictatorship," Chairman Mao's speech at the Preparatory Committee of the CPPCC, and Sections 8 and 9 of "Carry the Revolution Through to the End."

### 石家庄市干部　学习统一战线政策　市学委作具体布置

1949-12-06
第3版()
专栏：

　　石家庄市干部
  　学习统一战线政策
  　市学委作具体布置
    【本报讯】石家庄市学委会于日前发出通知，布置全市干部学习统一战线政策。定十二月全月为学习时间。在学习中要求每个干部对统一战线都有比较全面的认识，在实际工作中能加强团结，达到提高工作的目的。指定学习的文件为：“共产党人”发刊词前半部、“论联合政府”第四章、“新民主主义论”第五节、“目前形势和我们的任务”第七节、“中国革命与中国共产党”第二章第五节、“论人民民主专政”后半部、毛主席在政协筹备会上的讲词及“将革命进行到底”第八、九段。
