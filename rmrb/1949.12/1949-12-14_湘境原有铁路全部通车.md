Title: All Original Railways in Hunan Province Reopened to Traffic
Author:
Date: 1949-12-14
Page: 2

Xinhua News Agency, Changsha, 13th. All the original railways within Hunan Province are now open to traffic. The Yuehan Railway has been opened from Wuchang to Dengjiatang Station on the border between Hunan and Guangdong provinces. The Zhuzhou-Pingxiang section of the Zhejiang-Jiangxi Railway has long been unimpeded. The section from Hengyang to Dong'an on the Xianggui Railway has also officially resumed passenger and freight transportation.

### 湘境原有铁路全部通车

1949-12-14
第2版()
专栏：

　　湘境原有铁路全部通车
    【新华社长沙十三电】湖南省境内原有铁路现已全部通车。粤汉铁路已由武昌通至湘粤两省交界处的邓家塘车站；浙赣铁路株州至萍乡一段早已畅通；湘桂铁路衡阳至东安间亦已正式恢复客货运输。
