Title: State Council Guiding Reception Work Committee; East China Work Group Arrives in Nanjing
Author:
Date: 1949-12-11
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Nanjing, 10th. The first group of personnel from the State Council Guiding Reception Work Committee's East China Work Group, led by Vice Premier of the State Council Dong Biwu, arrived in Nanjing on the morning of the 10th. Su Yu, Director of the Nanjing Military Control Commission, and Tang Liang, Deputy Director, personally welcomed them at Xiaguan Railway Station. The headquarters of the work group will be located in Nanjing, and its main task will be to统筹指导 (tǒng chóu zhǐ dǎo) comprehensively guide and handle the reception of all former Kuomintang government central agencies, personnel, archives, books, property, and materials in East China.

### 政务院指导接收工委会　华东区工作团抵南京

1949-12-11
第1版()
专栏：

　　政务院指导接收工委会
  　华东区工作团抵南京
    【新华社南京十日电】中央人民政府政务院指导接收工作委员会华东区工作团首批人员，由政务院董必武副总理率领，已于十日晨抵南京。本市军管会粟裕主任、唐亮副主任等均亲赴下关车站欢迎。该工作团团部决定设在南京，其主要任务为统筹指导和处理华东有关前国民党政府中央各机关、人员、档案、图书、财产、物资等接收事宜。
