Title: Chongqing Market Becomes Increasingly Active
Author:
Date: 1949-12-20
Page: 3

Xinhua News Agency, Chongqing, December 19th: Since liberation, the Chongqing market has become increasingly active due to the restoration of transportation, with a gradual increase in import and export goods. During the 11th and 12th, customs declarations for export of local products included: 11,340 jin of hog bristles, 332,480 jin of tung oil, 140,214 jin of rapeseed oil, as well as large quantities of pickled mustard tuber, raw lacquer, medicinal materials, rock candy, silk cloth, black fungus, drawing paper, kraft paper, sheepskin, white wax, coal, etc. The amount of goods shipped from other regions to Sichuan is also increasing.

### 重庆市场日趋活跃

1949-12-20
第3版()
专栏：

　　重庆市场日趋活跃
    【新华社重庆十九日电】解放以来的重庆市场由于交通的恢复而日趋活跃，出入口货物渐增。十一、十二两日报关出口的土产品计有：猪鬃一万一千三百四十斤，桐油三十三万二千四百八十斤，菜油十四万零二百十四斤及大批榨菜、生漆、药材、冰糖、绸布、黑木耳、道林纸、牛皮纸、羊皮、白腊、煤斤等。外埠运川货物亦在增加中。
