Title: Central Committee of the Chinese Communist Party Sends Condolences to Yang Hucheng's Eldest Son, Encouraging Him to Inherit General Yang's Cause and Thoroughly Eliminate Remaining Bandits
Author:
Date: 1949-12-23
Page: 2

Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, December 22nd: The Central Committee of the Communist Party of China sent a telegram of condolence to Yang Zhengmin, Commander of the Dali Military Sub-district and eldest son of General Yang Hucheng. The telegram reads as follows:

Comrade Yang Zhengmin:

We are shocked and deeply saddened to learn that General Yang Hucheng was secretly murdered by Kuomintang special agent bandits in a Chongqing prison on September 17th of this year. General Yang's wife, his second son Yang Youzheng, and Secretary Song Qiyun and his wife also tragically suffered at their poisonous hands. In 1936, General Yang Hucheng cooperated with the Chinese Communist Party to promote national unity against Japan, rendering meritorious service to the nation and its people. General Yang was therefore hated by the bandit Chiang Kai-shek, imprisoned by the Chiang bandits for as long as twelve years, and sacrificed himself for upholding a patriotic and democratic stance. This sacrifice is glorious. General Yang's heroic name will be forever commemorated by the people of the whole country. We hereby send our condolences and hope that you will restrain your grief and strive to inherit General Yang's patriotic cause and thoroughly eliminate the remnants of the reactionary bandit gangs.

　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　The Central Committee of the Communist Party of China
　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　December 16th

### 中共中央委员会电唁杨虎城将军长子　勉继承杨将军事业彻底消灭残匪

1949-12-23
第2版()
专栏：

　　中共中央委员会电唁杨虎城将军长子
    勉继承杨将军事业彻底消灭残匪
    【新华社北京二十二日电】中国共产党中央委员会电唁杨虎城将军长子大荔军分区司令员杨拯民。电文如下：杨拯民同志：
    惊悉扬虎城将军于本年九月十七日在重庆监狱被国民党特务匪徒秘密杀害，杨将军夫人和次公子杨幼拯、秘书宋绮云夫妇等也先后惨遭毒手，极为痛愤。杨虎城将军在一九三六年与中国共产党合作，推动全国一致抗日，有功于国家民族。杨将军由此而受到蒋匪介石的仇视，被蒋匪囚禁达十二年之久，并因坚持爱国民主立场而牺牲，这个牺牲是光荣的。杨将军的英名将为全国人民所永远纪念。谨电哀悼，并望勉节哀思，为继承杨将军的爱国事业，彻底消灭反动匪帮的残余而奋斗。
    　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　中国共产党中央委员会　
    　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　十二月十六日
