Title: Northern Sichuan Liberated: Luojiang and Bazhong
Author:
Date: 1949-12-25
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Southwest Front, 24th—The People's Liberation Army liberated Luojiang County, southwest of Mianyang in northern Sichuan, on the 22nd, capturing more than 300 enemy soldiers. Another unit of the People's Liberation Army liberated Bazhong County on the 21st.

### 川北解放罗江巴中　

1949-12-25
第1版()
专栏：

　　川北解放罗江巴中　
    【新华社西南前线二十四日电】人民解放军于二十二日解放川北绵阳西南的罗江县城，俘敌三百余人。解放军另部于二十一日解放巴中县城。
