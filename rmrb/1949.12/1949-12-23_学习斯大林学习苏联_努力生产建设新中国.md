Title: Learn from Stalin, Learn from the Soviet Union, Strive to Produce and Build a New China
Author:
Date: 1949-12-23
Page: 2

Learn from Stalin, Learn from the Soviet Union
	Strive to Produce and Build a New China
	On the afternoon of the 20th, the leading party, government, and military organs of the Southwest and Chongqing jointly held a banquet to celebrate the 70th birthday of Marshal Stalin. At the meeting, General Liu Bocheng, Chairman of the Southwest Military and Political Committee and Commander of the Second Field Army of the People's Liberation Army, first stood up on behalf of the party, government, and military organs of the Southwest, representing the 70 million people of the Southwest, hundreds of thousands of People's Liberation Army soldiers, and tens of thousands of Communist Party members, to propose a toast to Marshal Stalin. Afterwards, General Deng Xiaoping, First Secretary of the Southwest Bureau of the CPC Central Committee and Political Commissar of the Second Field Army of the People's Liberation Army, continued to speak. He emphasized: Stalin's health and longevity are in the interest of the people of the world and the people of China. For the benefit of the Chinese people and the people of the world, we respectfully wish Marshal Stalin health and longevity. Under the leadership of Political Commissar Deng, the entire audience stood up again and raised their glasses to warmly congratulate Stalin's health. The banquet ended at 6 o'clock.
	People of all ethnic groups and all walks of life in Lanzhou held a celebration meeting on the 21st. On the same day, the Lanzhou China-Soviet Friendship Association Preparatory Committee held a banquet and a grand evening party. During the celebration week from the 15th to the 21st, factories, government organs, and schools in Lanzhou launched a wave of learning from Stalin and the Soviet Union.
	In Shenyang, Northeast China, celebration meetings have been held for several days since the 18th. At the celebration meeting jointly convened by the Northeast Bureau of the CPC Central Committee, the Northeast People's Government, and the Northeast General Branch of the China-Soviet Friendship Association on the 21st, Gao Gang, Secretary of the Northeast Bureau, delivered a congratulatory speech. More than 1,000 staff members of the Northeast People's Government are actively studying "A Brief Biography of Stalin" and "Stalin and the Chinese Revolution." Middle schools throughout the city are widely studying "A Great and Simple Person" and "A Brief Biography of Stalin," and carrying out learning activities in the form of special reports, seminars, and story evenings. In addition, workers, students, and organizations in cities such as Jilin, Harbin, Jiamusi, Mudanjiang, Shuangcheng, Tangyuan, Hulan, and Boli also held celebration meetings, wrote congratulatory letters, and gave special reports. Various congratulatory activities have been launched in Qiqihar since the 15th. In addition to the celebration meetings held by various units, more than 8,000 people in Qiqihar signed their names to congratulate him, and more than 500 people wrote congratulatory letters. More than 10,000 people from all walks of life in Chengde gathered on the 21st to congratulate Marshal Stalin on his 70th birthday. More than 2,000 gifts, including congratulatory banners, birthday greetings, and collective signature letters, were submitted to the conference for forwarding. The activities of the people of Andong City celebrating Marshal Stalin's birthday began at the beginning of the month. Factories, schools, and government organs held meetings to discuss Stalin's life and mobilized to learn from the Soviet Union's construction experience.
	More than 1,500 representatives of the 50,000 employees of the Tianjin Railway Administration held a grand birthday celebration meeting on the 20th. A signature campaign to congratulate him on his birthday was launched at the meeting. A certain unit of the People's Liberation Army stationed in Tianjin also held a symposium on the evening of the 20th. At the meeting, all officers and soldiers unanimously expressed their determination to abide by Chairman Mao's "Instructions on the Army's Participation in Production and Construction in 1950" and celebrate with practical actions of hard work and production. On the 21st, more than 600 cadres from the Preparatory Committee of the Tianjin Federation of Trade Unions and its affiliated trade unions gathered to celebrate the birthday. In addition, factories such as China Textile Factory No. 2, North China Electric Power Company Factory No. 1, Public Transportation Management Office, Municipal Waterworks, and various government organs and schools all held grand meetings to celebrate Stalin's 70th birthday.
	Party, government, and military leading organs and people's organizations in Shandong Province and Jinan City have successively celebrated Marshal Stalin's 70th birthday. The Shandong Dazhong Daily published eight pages on the 21st, with five pages devoted to articles and photos about Stalin's life and thought, as well as congratulatory messages, letters, and congratulatory speeches from all walks of life. The Shandong Branch of the CPC Central Committee, the Shandong Provincial People's Government, the Shandong Military Region of the People's Liberation Army, the Jinan Municipal Committee of the CPC, the Jinan Municipal People's Government, the Jinan Branch of the China-Soviet Friendship Association, and various people's organizations all published congratulatory messages in the Dazhong Daily, unanimously congratulating Stalin on his 70th birthday.
	The Qingdao Branch of the China-Soviet Friendship Association convened a lecture meeting on the 18th, attended by more than 1,500 people. Hua Gang, vice president of the association, gave a speech on Stalin's thought. Employees of various factories conducted production competitions to express their congratulations. All workers of the China Textile Printing and Dyeing Factory celebrated with the practical action of ensuring the completion of the December production task one week ahead of schedule.
	Other cities across the country, such as: Baoding, Taiyuan, Xinxiang, Zhangjiakou, Gui Sui, Jinan, Qingdao, Hangzhou, Zhenjiang, Wuxi, Changzhou, Tai'an, Yangzhou, Nantong, Dongtai, Hefei, Wuhu, Kaifeng, Zhengzhou, Changsha, Hengyang, Nanchang, Fuzhou, and Xi'an, all held celebration meetings and other celebratory activities.

### 学习斯大林学习苏联　努力生产建设新中国

1949-12-23
第2版()
专栏：

　　学习斯大林学习苏联
    努力生产建设新中国
    西南及重庆市党政军各领导机关二十日下午四时半联合举行庆祝斯大林大元帅七十寿辰宴会。会上，西南军政委员会主席、人民解放军第二野战军司令员刘伯承将军首先起立代表西南党政军机关、代表西南七千万人民、数十万人民解放军、数万共产党员，为斯大林大元帅干杯致敬。其后，中共中央西南局第一书记、人民解放军第二野战军政治委员邓小平将军继起讲话。他着重指出：斯大林的健康和长寿，就是世界人民和中国人民的利益。我们为中国人民和世界人民的利益，恭祝斯大林大元帅的健康和长寿。在邓政委领导下，全场又一致起立举杯，热烈祝贺斯大林的健康。宴会至六时始散。
    兰州市各族各界人民于二十一日举行了庆祝大会。同日晚间，兰州中苏友协筹备会举行宴会及盛大晚会。在十五日到二十一日的庆祝周内，兰州各工厂、机关、学校都掀起了学习斯大林和苏联的热潮。
    东北沈阳市自十八日起，连日均有祝寿大会；在二十一日由中共中央东北局、东北人民政府及中苏友协东北总分会联合召开的祝寿大会上，东北局书记高岗发表了祝寿讲演。东北人民政府千余工作人员积极地在学习“斯大林传略”、“斯大林与中国革命”。全市各中学普遍进行学习“伟大而质朴的人物”一文及“斯大林传略”，并以专题报告、座谈会、故事晚会等形式展开学习活动。此外，吉林、哈尔滨、佳木斯、牡丹江、双城、汤源、呼兰、勃利等城市的工人、学生和机关、团体等亦均有庆祝会、写贺信、专题报告等活动。齐齐哈尔市各界自十五日起即展开了各种祝寿活动。除各单位分别举行庆祝会外，齐市尚有八千余人签名祝寿，五百多人写了祝贺信。承德市各界人民万余人于二十一日集会祝贺斯大林大元帅的七十寿辰，交由大会转送的礼物，有贺幛、祝寿信与集体签名信达两千余件。安东市人民庆祝斯大林大元帅的活动自月初就已开始，各工厂、学校、机关均开会座谈斯大林生平，并动员学习苏联建设经验。
    天津铁路管理局所属五万员工的代表一千五百余人已于廿日举行盛大的祝寿大会。会上发起签名祝寿运动。驻津人民解放军某部也于二十日晚间举行座谈会，会上全体指战员一致表示决遵照毛主席的“关于一九五○年军队参加生产建设的指示”，以努力劳动生产的实际行动来庆祝。二十一日津市总工会筹委会及所属各工会全体干部六百余人集会祝寿。此外，中纺二厂、华北电力公司一厂、公共汽车管理处、市自来水厂等工厂及各机关学校，都分别举行了庆祝斯大林七十寿辰的大会。
    山东省和济南市党政军领导机关和各人民团体纷纷庆贺斯大林大元帅七十寿辰。山东大众日报二十一日出八版，以五版地位刊载关于斯大林生平、思想的文章和照片及各界的贺电、贺信、祝词。中共中央山东分局、山东省人民政府、人民解放军山东军区、中共济南市委会、济南市人民政府、中苏友协济南分会和各人民团体都在大众日报上发表了祝词，一致祝贺斯大林的七十大寿。
    青岛中苏友协分会于十八日召集讲演会，到会一千五百余人，由该会副会长华岗演讲斯大林思想。各工厂的职工进行生产竞赛，表示祝寿之意。中纺印染厂全体工人以保证提前一周完成十二月份的生产任务的实际行动来庆祝。
    全国的其他城市，如：保定、太原、新乡、张家口、归绥、济南、青岛、杭州、镇江、无锡、常州、泰安、扬州、南通、东台、合肥、芜湖、开封、郑州、长沙、衡阳、南昌、福州、西安等都分别举行了庆祝会及其他庆祝活动。
