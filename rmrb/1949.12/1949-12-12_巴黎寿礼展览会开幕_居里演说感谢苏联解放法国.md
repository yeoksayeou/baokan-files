Title: Paris Gift Exhibition Opens: Joliot-Curie Speech Thanks Soviet Union for Liberating France
Author:
Date: 1949-12-12
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, November 11th: TASS, Paris: The exhibition of gifts for Stalin's seventieth birthday opened in Paris on December 6th in a festive atmosphere. At the entrance to the exhibition hall, huge red and tricolor flags read: "Long Live Stalin, the fighter for the cause of peace, freedom, and human happiness." Thousands of gifts were sent by working people from all over France. A huge crowd gathered at the exhibition building. The exhibition was inaugurated by the chairman of the exhibition committee, the famous French scientist Joliot-Curie. Soviet Ambassador to France, Bogomolov, attended the opening ceremony as an honored guest. Also participating in the opening ceremony were: Thorez, Duclos, Marty, Vaillant-Couturier, Fajon, Monmousseau, Mauvais, Paul Éluard, Guyot, Madame Bloch, General Petit, and many other representatives of the French public. The exhibition is scheduled to run from the 7th to the 15th. The content of the exhibition includes paintings, photographs, carpets, drawings, albums, etc., reflecting the French people's struggle for peace. In his speech to the audience, Joliot-Curie pointed out Stalin's and the Soviet people's greatest contributions to the struggle for peace and thanked Stalin and the Soviet people for defeating the German fascist barbarians and liberating France. Joliot-Curie's speech was greeted with stormy applause.

### 巴黎寿礼展览会开幕　居里演说感谢苏联解放法国

1949-12-12
第4版()
专栏：

　　巴黎寿礼展览会开幕
  　居里演说感谢苏联解放法国
    【新华社北京十一日电】塔斯社巴黎讯：斯大林七十寿辰的礼物展览会，于十二月六日在节日的气氛中在巴黎开幕。展览会大厅的入口处，巨大的红色与三色旗帜上写着：“为和平、自由与人类幸福的事业而斗争的战士斯大林万岁”。法国国内各地的劳动人民送来了上千份的礼物。巨大的人类拥聚在展览会大楼。展览会系由展览会委员会主席、著名的法国科学家约里奥——居里揭幕。苏联驻法大使鲍格莫洛夫作为荣誉来宾参加了开幕礼。参加展览会开幕礼的还有：多列士、杜克洛、马尔梯、佛拉商、法戎、蒙摩娑、摩维、保罗·伊瓦德、盖洛、勃洛克夫人、柏蒂将军及其他法国公众的许多代表。展览会时间规定自七日至十五日。展览的内容都是反映法国人民为和平斗争的绘画、照片、地毯、图画、画册等。约里奥——居里在向观众演说时，指出斯大林与苏联人民在争取和平的斗争中的最伟大的贡献，并感谢斯大林和苏联人民战胜德国法西斯野蛮人和解放法国。约里奥——居里的演说，博得了暴风雨般的掌声。
