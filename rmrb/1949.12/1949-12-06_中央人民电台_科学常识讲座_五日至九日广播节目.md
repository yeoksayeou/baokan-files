Title: Central People's Broadcasting Station: Science and Common Sense Lectures - Broadcast Schedule from the 5th to the 9th
Author:
Date: 1949-12-06
Page: 3

　　Central People's Broadcasting Station
  　Science and Common Sense Lectures
  　Broadcast Schedule from the 5th to the 9th
    Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, December 5th: The Central People's Broadcasting Station (formerly the Beijing Xinhua Broadcasting Station's first channel) will broadcast the following natural science common sense lectures from December 5th to the 9th:
    December 5th (Monday): Common Knowledge of the Calendar (1)
    December 6th (Tuesday): Common Knowledge of the Calendar (2) (Yang Tongtang, Physics Department, Fu Jen Catholic University)
    December 7th (Wednesday): Spring, Summer, Autumn, and Winter
    December 8th (Thursday): Day and Night (Yang Guangzhong, Beijing Institute of Atomic Energy)
    December 9th (Friday): Characteristics of China's Climate (Zhang Yan, Beijing Meteorological Observatory)
