Title: Central, Beijing People's Broadcasting Station Today's Broadcast Highlights
Author:
Date: 1949-12-06
Page: 3

　　Central, Beijing People's Broadcasting Station
  　Today's Broadcast Highlights
    [This newspaper reports] The Central People's Broadcasting Station tonight (6th) at 17:30, Worker's Time: "Introducing Zhao Guoyou, the model worker in Northeast China who created new production records." 18:30, Natural Science Knowledge Lecture: "Common Knowledge of Calendars." 18:40, broadcasting the full text of Kim Gui-soon's speech given in Korean yesterday, in English. 18:00, First Art and Literature Program: Qinshu "Blue Bridge Hate" second half. 21:00, Second Art and Literature Program: Soviet Music.
    [Another News Item] Beijing People's Broadcasting Station 12:30, Worker's Time: Wang Wenbin speaks on: Thoughts on attending the Asian-Australian Trade Union Representative Conference. 16:15, Children's Time: Singing by children from the Affiliated Primary School of North China Middle School. 16:30, Women's Time: Zhang Xiaomei speaks on "Welcoming the Asian Women's Representative Conference." 17:00, continuing to teach singing "Forward, Sisters!" 19:15, Voice of the City: (1) The Bureau of Hygiene speaks on "Terrible Winter Coal Gas Poisoning," (2) The Public Security Bureau issues a notice to citizens regarding the rectification of household registration in the city, (3) Slogans from the Public Security Bureau regarding the rectification of household registration in the city.

### 中央、北京人民电台　今日广播要目

1949-12-06
第3版()
专栏：

　　中央、北京人民电台
  　今日广播要目
    【本报讯】中央人民广播电台今（六）晚十七点三十分职工时间：“介绍东北工人创造生产新纪录的模范——赵国有”。十八点三十分自然科学常识讲座：“历法的常识”。十八点四十分，用英语播送金贵善昨天用朝鲜话广播演讲的全文。十八点第一次文艺节目：琴书“蓝桥恨”后半段。二十一点第二次文艺节目：苏联音乐。
    【又讯】北京人民电台十二点三十分职工时间王文斌讲：参加亚澳工会代表会议的感想。十六点十五分儿童时间由华北中学附属小学小朋友们唱歌。十六点三十分妇女时间张晓梅讲“迎接亚洲妇女代表会议”。十七点继续教唱“前进吧！姊妹们！”。十九点十五分市政之声：（１）卫生局讲“可怕的冬季煤气中毒”，（２）公安局为整理本市户口发布的告市民书，（３）公安局关于整理本市户口的宣传口号。
