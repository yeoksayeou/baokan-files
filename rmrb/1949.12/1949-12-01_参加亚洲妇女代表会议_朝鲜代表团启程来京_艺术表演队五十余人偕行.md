Title: North Korean Delegation Departs for Beijing to Attend Asian Women's Representative Conference; Art Performance Troupe of Over Fifty People Accompanies Them
Author:
Date: 1949-12-01
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Pyongyang, 30th—A North Korean delegation of thirty-seven people, led by the head of the delegation, Minister of Culture and Propaganda of the North Korean Central Government, Heo Jeong-suk, departed from Pyongyang by special train at 3 PM on the 29th for Beijing to attend the Asian Women's Representative Conference. An art performance troupe of fifty-five people accompanied them.

### 参加亚洲妇女代表会议　朝鲜代表团启程来京  艺术表演队五十余人偕行

1949-12-01
第1版()
专栏：

　　参加亚洲妇女代表会议
  　朝鲜代表团启程来京
    艺术表演队五十余人偕行
    【新华社平壤三十日电】参加亚洲妇女代表会议的朝鲜代表团一行三十七人，已于二十九日午后三时在团长、朝鲜中央政府文化宣传部部长许贞淑率领下，自平壤乘专车赴北京。艺术表演队五十五人偕行。
