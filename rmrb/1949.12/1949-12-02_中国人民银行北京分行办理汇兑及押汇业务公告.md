Announcement of the Beijing Branch of the People's Bank of China on Handling Remittance and Advance Exchange Business
(1949年月日)

To facilitate customers, our branch has decided that all its business department and the three offices in the East, West, and South City will handle three types of remittances: telegraphic transfer, mail transfer, and bill of exchange. The business department will also handle general inter-port advance exchange, and the Beijing Branch of the Bank of China will specialize in advance exchange business related to export goods.
