Title: Czechoslovak Government Announces Next Year's Production Plan
Author:
Date: 1949-12-26
Page: 4

　　Xinhua News Agency, Prague, December 25th: TASS reports: The Czechoslovak government has announced the plan for the second year of the Five-Year National Economic Plan - the plan for 1950. The broad masses of the working people of Czechoslovakia directly participated in the drafting of this plan. The plan stipulates that all sectors of the national economy of the Czechoslovak Republic should greatly increase production compared with 1949, with particular emphasis on the metallurgical, machine-building, and construction industries. In agriculture, the second year plan of the Five-Year Plan stipulates that the number of livestock and the production of feed should be increased. The plan also stipulates that construction should increase by 41 percent compared to 1949.

### 捷克政府发表明年生产计划

1949-12-26
第4版()
专栏：

　　捷克政府发表明年生产计划
    【新华社二十五日电】塔斯社布拉格讯：捷克斯洛代克政府发表了关于五年国民经济计划中第二年的计划——一九五○年的计划。捷克斯洛伐克广大劳动人民群众曾直接参与这一计划的草拟。该计划规定捷克斯洛伐克共和国国民经济各部门要较一九四九年大大增加生产，尤其侧重于冶金、机器制造和建筑工业上。在农业方面，五年计划的第二年计划规定，要增加牲口的头数和饲料的生产。计划也规定在建筑方面要比一九四九年增加百分之四十一。
