Title: Dimitrov Mausoleum Opens, Sofia People Pay Solemn Respects

Author:

Date: 1949-12-20
Page: 4

　　Xinhua News Agency, Sofia: The Dimitrov Mausoleum was opened here on the 11th. Residents of Sofia stood in long lines on "September 9th Square," paying their respects with heavy hearts to the remains of their beloved teacher and the outstanding leader of the international labor movement. After the mausoleum doors opened in the morning, cadres of the Sofia Party organization, members of the Presidium of the National Assembly, members of the Sofia City Council, and the best shock workers of the Bulgarian capital entered in order. The Soviet Ambassador to Bulgaria, Bodrov, and envoys from various countries also went to pay their respects. From morning to night, the working people of Bulgaria continuously flowed past the sarcophagus, with approximately 10,000 people visiting the tomb on the first day.

### 季米特洛夫陵墓开放索亚人民沉痛谒陵

1949-12-20
第4版()
专栏：

　　季米特洛夫陵墓开放索亚人民沉痛谒陵
    【新华社北京十九日电】塔斯社索非亚讯：季米特洛夫陵墓于十一日在此间开放。索非亚居民在“九九广场”上排成长长的行列，怀着沉痛的心情瞻仰他们爱戴的导师、国际劳工运动卓越领袖的遗容。上午陵门开启后，索非亚党组织干部，国民议会主席团委员，索非亚市议会的议员以及保加利亚首都最优秀的突击工人依次入内。苏驻保大使保德罗夫及各国使节亦往谒陵。保加利亚的劳动人民从早到晚不继地在石棺旁流动，第一日谒墓者约达一万人。
