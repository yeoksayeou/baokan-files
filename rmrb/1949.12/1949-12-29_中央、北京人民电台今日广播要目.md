Title: Central, Beijing People's Radio Today's Broadcast Schedule
Author:
Date: 1949-12-29
Page: 3

　　【This Newspaper News】The Central People's Broadcasting Station tonight (29th) at 17:30, Women's Time: "Eradicating the Prostitution System from the Root." 18:30, Natural Science Common Knowledge Lecture: "Alum." 18:00, First Arts and Entertainment Program: Xihe Drum "Sister-in-Law Ye Rowing a Boat Across the River." 21:00, Second Arts and Entertainment Program: Amateur National Music Research Society performs national music.
    【Another News】Beijing People's Radio at 12:30, Workers' Time: Construction Bureau Workers' Choir singing. 16:15, Children's Time: Children from the Second Affiliated Elementary School of the Normal University perform the opera "Scolding and Beating is Always Wrong." 16:45, Listener Services: (1) Answering the question of how many universities there are in the Northeast, (2) Publication Administration Bureau, Shenyang Compilation Bureau's announcement soliciting popular reading materials, (3) Six unclaimed telegrams from the Beijing Telecommunications Bureau, (4) The Central Academy of Drama's announcement of entrance examinations for transfer students in the general studies program, (5) An announcement seeking Wang Xianxiang. 17:00, New Song Practice: Teaching to sing "Celebrating the Year of Victory." 19:15, Voice of the Municipality: Chai Zemin speaks on "The Policies and Methods of Land Reform in the Suburbs of Beijing."

### 中央、北京人民电台今日广播要目

1949-12-29
第3版()
专栏：

　　中央、北京人民电台今日广播要目
    【本报讯】中央人民广播电台今（二十九）晚十七点三十分妇女时间：“从根铲除妓院制度”。十八点三十分自然科学常识讲座：“白矾”。十八点第一次文艺节目：西河大鼓“叶大嫂摇船渡江”。二十一点第二次文艺节目：业余国乐研究社演播国乐。
    【又讯】北京人民电台十二点三十分，职工时间：建设局工人歌咏队唱歌。十六点十五分儿童时间：师大第二附小小朋友们演唱歌剧“打骂获短都不对”。十六点四十五分听众服务：（一）回答关于东北有多少个大学的问题，（二）出版总署编渖局征集通俗读物资料的启事，（三）北京电信局六件招领的电报，（四）中央戏剧学院招考普通科插班生的启事，（五）寻找王显祥的一件启事。十七点新歌练习：教唱“庆祝胜利年”。十九点十五分市政之声：柴泽民讲“北京市郊区土地改革的政策和方法”。
