Title: All-China Federation of Literary and Art Circles Sends Telegram to Congratulate Stalin on his 70th Birthday
Author:
Date: 1949-12-19
Page: 2

Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, December 18th.

　　The All-China Federation of Literary and Art Circles Sends Telegram to Congratulate Stalin on his 70th Birthday
The All-China Federation of Literary and Art Circles sent a telegram to congratulate Marshal Stalin on his 70th birthday. The text of the telegram is as follows:

To Marshal Stalin:

We warmly congratulate you on your 70th birthday! Your health and longevity are the greatest happiness of the working people of the world. The people of the world are uniting around you, continuing to organize and expand their forces, and striving for lasting world peace and people's democracy. We believe that with your wise leadership, our cause will surely be victorious. We shout:

Long live Marshal Stalin, the great teacher of mankind!

All-China Federation of Literary and Art Circles
December 17, 1949

### 中华全国文联电贺斯大林七十大寿

1949-12-19
第2版()
专栏：

　　中华全国文联电贺斯大林七十大寿
    【新华社北京十八日电】中华全国文学艺术界联合会电贺斯大林大元帅七十寿辰，电文如下：斯大林大元帅：
    我们热烈地祝贺您的七十大寿！您的健康和长寿，是全世界劳动人民的最大幸福。全世界人民正团结在您的周围，继续组织和扩大力量，为争取世界持久和平与人民民主而奋斗。我们相信，有您英明的领导，我们的事业一定胜利。我们高呼：
    人类伟大的导师斯大林大元帅万岁！
    中华全国文学艺术界联合会
    一九四九年十二月十七日
