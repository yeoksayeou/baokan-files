Title: Second Session of Beijing Opera Troupe Training Class Holds Opening Ceremony
Author:
Date: 1949-12-03
Page: 3

　　Cultural Trends

　　▲ The second session of the Beijing Opera Troupe Training Class held its opening ceremony on the morning of the 1st. Over 1,400 people attended, including Shang Xiaoyun, Xun Huisheng, Tan Fuying, and Xiao Cuihua. Guests included Tian Han, Director of the Opera Improvement Bureau; Li Bozhao of the Beijing Municipal Committee of the Chinese Communist Party; Wang Yaping, Vice President of the Popular Literature and Art Creation Research Association; and Xue Enhou, person in charge of the Beijing Opera Research Institute. Tian Han spoke at the meeting, pointing out that the great unity of the opera world today is the best phenomenon since Beijing had Peking Opera and other operas. The more than 1,400 actors present have tens of millions of viewers. The drama of the capital of the People's Republic of China is valued by the whole world. Therefore, we must strengthen the performance of plays with educational significance in order to complete the great task. Li Bozhao also conveyed the spirit of the Second Session of the People's Representatives Conference of All Circles of Beijing. Li Chao, on behalf of Wu Yuzhang, President of North China University, and Vice Presidents Cheng Fangwu and Fan Wenlan, extended congratulations to all students.

### 北京戏曲界讲习班第二期行开学典礼

1949-12-03
第3版()
专栏：文化动态

　　北京戏曲界讲习班第二期行开学典礼
    ▲北京市戏曲界第二届讲习班一日上午举行开学典礼。到会的有尚小云、荀慧生、谭富英、筱翠花等一千四百余人，来宾有戏曲改进局局长田汉、中共北京市文委李伯钊、大众文艺创作研究会副会长王亚平、京剧研究院负责人薛恩厚等。田汉在会上讲话，指出今天戏曲界的大团结是北京自有京剧曲艺以来最好的现象，到会的一千四百多演员有着千万个观众，中华人民共和国首都的戏剧是全世界所重视的，因此，必须加强演出有教育意义的戏，才能完成伟大的任务。李伯钊并传达了北京市第二届各界人民代表会议的精神。李超代表华大校长吴玉章，副校长成仿吾、范文澜向全体学员致贺。
