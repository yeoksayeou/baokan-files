Title: Central People's Broadcasting Station Natural Science Common Knowledge Lecture Program from the 12th to the 16th

Author:
Date: 1949-12-13
Page: 3

Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, December 11th: The Central People's Broadcasting Station's Natural Science Common Knowledge Lecture program from December 12th to 16th is as follows:

	December 12th (Monday) Rainfall in Various Parts of China
	December 13th (Tuesday) Clouds, Fog, Rain, Snow, and Hail (Above by Zhang Yan of the Beijing Meteorological Observatory)
	December 14th (Wednesday) From Cement to China's Cement Industry
	December 15th (Thursday) Molybdenum and Chromium (Above by Gong Jiesheng of the Geology Department of Peking University)
	December 16th (Friday) Talking about Lead (Li Zhi of the Geology Department of Peking University)

### 中央人民广播电台自然科学常识讲座　从十二日到十六日节目

1949-12-13
第3版()
专栏：

　　中央人民广播电台
  　自然科学常识讲座
  　从十二日到十六日节目
    【新华社北京十一日电】中央人民广播电台自然科学常识讲座自十二月十二日到十六日的节目如下：
    十二日（星期一）中国各地下雨的情况
    十三日（星期二）云、雾、雨、雪和冰雹（以上北京气象台章淹作）
    十四日（星期三）从水泥谈到我国的水泥工业
    十五日（星期四）钼和铬（以上北京大学地质系巩杰生作）
    十六日（星期五）谈铅（北京大学地质系李质）
