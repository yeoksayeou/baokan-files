Title: Mongolian Exhibition Celebrating Longevity Opens; Premier Choibalsan Presides Over Preparations

Author:
Date: 1949-12-20
Page: 2

Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, 19th: TASS, Ulaanbaatar, 18th: The exhibition on the life and work of Stalin, organized by the Mongolian People's Republic Cultural Association for Soviet Union, has opened in Ulaanbaatar. Those attending the opening ceremony included all members of the government committee, headed by Marshal Choibalsan, Premier of the Mongolian People's Republic, who presided over the preparations for the celebration of Stalin's 70th birthday. Marshal Choibalsan delivered a speech stating: The Mongolian people, like celebrating their great national day, celebrate this memorable day of Comrade Stalin's 70th birthday together with the great Soviet people and the working people of all countries. Marshal Choibalsan emphasized that the Mongolian people have launched a high tide of the signature campaign to send letters to Comrade Stalin.

### 蒙古祝寿展览会开幕　总理乔巴山主持筹备事宜

1949-12-20
第2版()
专栏：

　　蒙古祝寿展览会开幕
  　总理乔巴山主持筹备事宜
    【新华社北京十九日电】塔斯社乌兰巴托尔十八日讯：蒙古人民共和国对苏文化协会所组织的关于斯大林生平与工作的展览会，已在乌兰巴托尔开幕。参加开幕典礼者，有以蒙古人民共和国总理乔巴山元帅为首的主持斯大林七十寿辰庆典之筹备事宜的政府委员会全体委员。乔巴山元帅发表演说称：蒙古人民象庆祝他们的伟大的国庆日一样，和伟大的苏联人民及各国的劳动人民一道，来庆祝斯大林同志七十寿辰这个可纪念的日子。乔巴山元帅强调指出：蒙古人民已掀起了致信斯大林同志的签名运动的高潮。
