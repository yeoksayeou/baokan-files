Title: Ninth Meeting of the Government Administration Council: Approved Organizational Regulations and Five Appointment Proposals
Author:
Date: 1949-12-03
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, 2nd -- The Central People's Government Administration Council held its ninth meeting today. The meeting approved the "General Organizational Rules of the Central People's Government Administration Council and its Subordinate Organs," which will be implemented after being submitted to the Central People's Government Committee for approval. The meeting also approved five appointment proposals, which will be submitted to the Central People's Government Committee for approval.

### 政务院第九次会议　通过组织通则及五项任命案

1949-12-03
第1版()
专栏：

　　政务院第九次会议
  　通过组织通则及五项任命案
    【新华社北京二日电】中央人民政务院今日举行第九次政务会议。会议通过了“中央人民政府政务院及其所属各机关组织通则”，俟提请中央人民政府委员会批准后施行。会议又通过了五项任命案，将提请中央人民政府委员会批准任命。
