Title: Eighty Cities Liberated, Guangxi Province Entirely Liberated
Author: 
Date: 1949-12-31
Page: 1

Eighty cities liberated. They are: Liping, Rongjiang, and Congjiang in Guizhou Province; Tongdao, Jingxian, Daoxian, Yongming, Jianghua, Dong'an in Hunan Province; Lingshan, Qinxian, Fangcheng, Hepu, Lianjiang, Haikang, and Xuwen in Guangdong Province. As well as Guilin, the capital of Guangxi Province, and Nanning, Liuzhou, Wuzhou, Sanjiang, Rongxian, Luocheng, Yishan, Si'en, Yibei, Hechi, Donglan, Wangang, Baise, Tianyang, Du'an, Xincheng, Liucheng, Longshan, Qianjiang, Binyang, Laibin, Wuxuan, Xiangxian, Xiuren, Guixian, Guiping, Pingnan, Tengxian, Mengshan, Luorong, Liujiang, Lipu, Yangshuo, Zhongshan, Hexian, Xindu, Zhaoping, Fuchuan, Jin County, Xing'an, Lingchuan, Longsheng, Yining, Ziyuan, Baishou, Rongxian, Beiliu, Bobai, Luchuan, Yulin, Xingye, Lingxi, Hengxian, Yongchun, Suilu, Sile, Shangsi, Mingjiang, Ningming, Pingxiang, Chongshan, and Longzhou. (Note: The entire territory of Guangxi has been liberated. Other county towns not liberated in this campaign are not included in the statistics.)
    
    (III) Seizures: A total of 1,258 artillery pieces of various types, including: 33 howitzers, 47 field guns, 97 mountain guns, 64 direct-fire guns, 11 anti-aircraft guns, 7 anti-aircraft guns, 23 infantry guns, 39 anti-tank guns, 44 rocket launchers, 20 cannon, 21 chemical mortars, 274 light and heavy mortars, and 578 60mm mortars. In addition: 303 grenade launchers, 52,625 guns of various types, including: 639 heavy machine guns, 3,622 light machine guns, 38 anti-aircraft machine guns, 1,386 submachine guns, 20 anti-tank rifles, 1,032 carbines, 390 automatic rifles, 43,055 rifles and muskets, 2,288 pistols, 133 rifle grenade launchers, 32 signal guns, 1,709 bayonets, 5,746,665 rounds of various bullets, 56,259 rounds of various artillery shells, 17,621 hand grenades, 936 rifle grenades, 1,516 vehicles, 4 armored vehicles, 2,149 mules and horses, 262 radio stations, 835 telephone trunk lines, 2,621 single units, 63 wireless telephones, 1,475 li of covered wire, 11 gunboats, 2 enemy aircraft shot down, and the seizure of many other warehouses, medical equipment, communication equipment, locomotives and carriages, and military factories and clothing factories, with materials piled up like mountains.

### 解放城市八十座　广西省全境解放

1949-12-31
第1版()
专栏：

    解放城市八十座　广西省全境解放
    （贰）解放城市八十座。计：贵州省之黎平、榕江、从江，湖南省之通道、靖县、道县、永明、江华、东安，广东省之灵山、钦县、防城、合浦、廉江、海康、徐闻。广西省省会桂林及南宁、柳州、梧州、三江、融县、罗城、宜山、思恩、宜北、河池、东兰、万冈、百色、田阳、都安、忻城、柳城、隆山、迁江、宾阳、来宾、武宣、象县、修仁、贵县、桂平、平南、藤县、蒙山、雒容、榴江、荔浦、阳朔、锺山、贺县、信都、昭平、富川、金县、兴安、灵川、龙胜、义宁、资源、百寿、容县、北流、博白、陆川、郁林、兴业、岭溪、横县、永淳、绥渌、思乐、上思、明江、宁明、凭祥、崇善、龙州。（按：广西全境均已获得解放，其余县城不在此次战役中解放者，未列入统计）
    （叁）缴获：各种炮一千二百五十八门计：榴弹炮三十三门，野炮四十七门，山炮九十七门，平射炮六十四门，机关炮十一门，高射炮七门，步兵炮二十三门，高射炮七门，步兵炮二十三门，战防炮三十九门，火箭炮四十四门，加农炮二十门，化学迫击炮二十一门，轻重迫击炮二七四门，六零炮五七八门。另缴：掷弹筒三百零三个，各种枪五万二千六百二十五枝计：重机枪六三九挺，轻机枪三六二二挺，高射机枪三十八挺，冲锋枪一三八六挺，战防枪二十枝，卡宾枪一零三二枝，自动步枪三九零枝，步马枪四三零五五枝，短枪二二八八枝，枪榴筒一三三枝，信号枪三十二枝，刺刀一七零九把，各种子弹五百七十四万六千六百六十五发，各种炮弹五万六千二百五十九发，手榴弹一万七千六百二十一颗，枪榴弹九百三十六枚，汽车一千五百十六辆，装甲车四辆，骡马二千一百四十九头，电台二百六十二部，电话总线八百三十五部，单机二千六百二十一部，无线电话六十三部，被复线一千四百七十五里，炮艇十一艘，击落匪机两架，缴获其他仓库、卫生器材、通讯器材、火车头与车皮、以及兵工厂、被服厂等多所、物资堆集如山。
