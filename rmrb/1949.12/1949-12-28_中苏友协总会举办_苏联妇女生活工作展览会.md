Title: Sino-Soviet Friendship Association Headquarters to Hold "Soviet Women's Life and Work Exhibition"
Author:
Date: 1949-12-28
Page: 3

　　The Sino-Soviet Friendship Association Headquarters to Hold "Soviet Women's Life and Work Exhibition"

　　[By Our Own Correspondent] The Asian Workers' and Women's Life and Struggle Exhibition, sponsored by the Asian Women's Representatives Conference, closed a few days ago. The Soviet Women's Life and Work pictures in the exhibition have been presented to the Sino-Soviet Friendship Association by the Soviet Women's Delegation. At the request of all circles, the Sino-Soviet Friendship Association Headquarters has decided to continue the exhibition at the original site (Children's Library, Zhongshan Park) from December 28th to January 4th. To avoid overcrowding, it is stipulated that organizations and groups wishing to visit in groups must contact and register in advance in order to arrange a tour schedule.

### 中苏友协总会举办　苏联妇女生活工作展览会

1949-12-28
第3版()
专栏：

　　中苏友协总会举办
　  苏联妇女生活工作展览会
    【本报讯】亚洲妇女代表会议主办之亚洲工人、妇女生活与斗争展览会业余日前闭幕。展览会中之苏联妇女生活与工作图片已由苏联妇女代表团赠予中苏友好协会总会。中苏友协总会应各界所请，定于二十八日至一月四日仍在原址（中山公园儿童图书馆）继续展览。为避免观众拥挤起见，该地规定各机关团体集体前往参观者须预先接洽登记，以便排定参观日程。
