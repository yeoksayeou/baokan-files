Title: Shanhaiguan Currency Exchange Rate Adjusted Starting Yesterday
Author:
Date: 1949-12-06
Page: 2

　　Shanhaiguan Currency Exchange Rate
　　Adjusted Starting Yesterday
　　[Shanhaiguan News] The Shanhaiguan Joint Office of the People's Bank of China and the Northeast Bank has changed the exchange rate, effective yesterday (the 5th), to 1 RMB equaling 22 Northeast Yuan.

### 山海关货币牌价　昨日起调整

1949-12-06
第2版()
专栏：

　　山海关货币牌价
  　昨日起调整
    【山海关讯】中国人民银行、东北银行山海关联合办事处自昨（五）日起牌价改为人民券一元兑东北券二十二元。
