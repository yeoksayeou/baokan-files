Delegations from Various Countries Present Gifts to Each Other
The Chinese Delegation Submits Birthday Gifts to the Soviet Delegation for Presentation to Stalin

At the meeting, the exchange of gifts between the delegations of various countries demonstrated the profound and sincere friendship between the people of all nations. To celebrate the 70th birthday of the great teacher of all mankind, Marshal Stalin, the Chinese delegation presented birthday gifts to the Soviet delegation for forwarding to Marshal Stalin. Delegations from Korea, France, China, and others jointly presented a large number of valuable gifts to Chairman Mao Zedong, Vice-Chairmen Zhu De, Liu Shaoqi, and Premier Zhou Enlai. In addition, delegations from Indonesia, Burma, and others presented flags to the conference, the Chinese delegation presented gifts to the representatives of various countries and guests, and the Soviet delegation presented gifts to the Chinese delegation.

After the gift presentation ceremony, Ms. Guguireva, Secretary-General of the International Democratic Women's Federation, delivered a closing speech to the conference. She pointed out that the Asian Women's Representative Conference had achieved great success, which would strengthen the struggle of women in Asia and around the world against imperialism and for national independence, people's democracy, and lasting world peace.

The meeting adopted a congratulatory telegram to Marshal Stalin, and also passed a telegram to Vice-Chairman of the Central People's Government and Honorary Chairman of the All-China Democratic Women's Federation, Soong Ching Ling, thanking her for her concern for the meeting.

This historically significant conference finally concluded with the singing of "The Internationale," announced by Chairman Bafunova.

### 各国代表团互相献礼　中国代表团将祝寿礼品交苏代表团转献斯大林

1949-12-17
第1版()
专栏：

　　各国代表团互相献礼
  　中国代表团将祝寿礼品交苏代表团转献斯大林
    会上各国代表团的互相献礼表现了各国人民深厚诚挚的友谊。中国代表团为庆祝全世界人类伟大导师斯大林大元帅的七十寿辰，将祝寿礼品献交苏联代表团转送斯大林大元帅。朝鲜、法国、中国等代表团联合将大批贵重礼品献给了毛泽东主席、朱德、刘少奇副主席和周恩来总理。此外，有印尼、缅甸等代表团向大会献旗，中国代表团向各国代表及来宾献礼，苏联代表团向中国代表团献礼。
    国际民主妇联总书记古久里夫人在献礼程序后向大会致闭幕词，她指出亚洲妇女代表会议获得了巨大的成功，这个成功，将加强整个亚洲以及全世界各国妇女为反对帝国主义，争取民族独立及人民民主及世界持久和平的斗争。
    会上通过了致斯大林大元帅的贺电，同时并通过致电我中央人民政府副主席、中华全国民主妇女联合会名誉主席宋庆龄，感激她对会议的关怀。
    此一具有历史意义的会议最后在“国际歌”声中由主席巴菲诺娃宣告闭幕。
