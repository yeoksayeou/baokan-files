Title: Southern Shaanxi Continues to Capture Mian County, Southern Gansu Liberates Xigu

Author: 
Date: 1949-12-11
Page: 1

　　[Xinhua News Agency, Southern Shaanxi Front, 10th] The People's Liberation Army captured Mian County on the 8th. Another unit of the Liberation Army liberated Xigu County in Southern Gansu on the 7th. The defending enemy fled south.

### 陕南续克沔县陇南解放西固

1949-12-11
第1版()
专栏：

　　陕南续克沔县陇南解放西固
    【新华社陕南前线十日电】人民解放军于八日进占沔县城，另部解放军于七日解放陇南之西固县城，守敌向南逃窜。
