　　International Briefs
    ▲United Press International, Tokyo: Draper, former U.S. Undersecretary of the Army and Wall Street banker, publicly announced in Tokyo on the 16th that his visit to Japan was to investigate whether "sufficient guarantees for foreign investment have been proposed." After recalling his bank, Dillon, Read & Co.'s "good experience in dealing with Japanese companies" before the war, Draper said: The time is ripe for foreign investment in Japan. His bank believes that Japan will once again become a good place to invest.
    　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　Xinhua News Agency, Beijing

### 国际简讯

1949-12-23
第4版()
专栏：国际简讯

　　国际简讯
    ▲据合众社东京讯：美前陆军部副部长、华尔街银行家德莱柏，十六日在东京公开宣布，他此次访日是为了调查对“外国投资的充分保障，是否已经建议”。德莱柏在回溯他的银行——狄隆雷德银行战前曾与日本公司的“良好相处经验”后说：外国对日本投资的时机，已经成熟。他的银行相信，日本将再度成为投资的好地方。
    　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　【新华社北京讯】
