Title: Delegates to the Asian Women's Representative Conference: Soviet Delegation Arrives in Beijing Yesterday; Syrian and Lebanese Representatives Arrive Together. Beijing Holds Grand Gathering Yesterday to Extend a Warm Welcome

Author:

Date: 1949-12-09
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, December 8th: Headed by Bafuinova, the Soviet delegation, along with Syrian representatives Salma Buni and Ameni Aleph Kasabu, Lebanese representative Victoria El Hailu, and more than ten others, arrived in Beijing at 10:30 this morning to attend the Asian Women's Representative Conference. Guests who will attend the conference as observers include Mrs. Eugene from West Africa and Ye Saier Rabesahara from Madagascar, who also arrived together. Delegates from various countries who have already arrived in Beijing, as well as Vice Chairpersons of the All-China Democratic Women's Federation Deng Yingchao, Li Dequan, and Xu Guangping, Vice Chairman of the All-China Federation of Trade Unions Liu Ningyi, representative of the All-China Federation of Literary and Art Circles Xiao San, Zhou Enlai's representative from the Ministry of Foreign Affairs of the Central People's Government Yan Baohang, representative of the Beijing Municipal People's Government Ma Zhenwu, and Chairperson of the Beijing Municipal Democratic Women's Federation Zhang Xiaomei, all went to welcome them. When the delegates from various countries arrived at the guesthouse accompanied by the welcomers, Mrs. Kuli and over a hundred Chinese women representatives stepped forward to shake hands, and a welcome meeting was immediately held. Deng Yingchao, Li Dequan, and Zhang Xiaomei successively delivered welcome speeches. Deng Yingchao said, "The path taken by Soviet women is the path we must take." Li Dequan, on behalf of the International Preparatory Committee of the Asian Women's Representative Conference, paid tribute to the delegates and said, "Soviet women are the closest friends of Asian women and women all over the world. The sympathy and assistance of Soviet women towards oppressed nations all over the world gives us unparalleled excitement and emotion. The women of Syria, Lebanon, West Africa, and Madagascar are our friends in the struggle for national independence, people's democracy, and lasting world peace. We warmly welcome your arrival." Zhang Xiaomei, on behalf of the one million women of Beijing, welcomed the sisters from various countries and expressed that the women of Beijing will do their best to help with the work of the Asian Women's Representative Conference. In their reply speeches, the delegates from various countries unanimously praised the victory of the Chinese people. Bafuinova, head of the Soviet delegation and Vice Chairman of the Soviet Women's Anti-Fascist Committee, said, "The victory of the Chinese people is the victory of human progress all over the world, the victory of the people all over the world in striving for lasting peace, and the victory of China has further strengthened the strength of the world democratic and peaceful camp headed by the Soviet Union." The representatives of Lebanon and Syria said that the victory of the Chinese people was the most serious blow to the imperialists. When the guests from Madagascar and West Africa spoke, they expressed their infinite respect for the Chinese people and their great leader Chairman Mao Zedong. They believe that the victory of China is a new inspiration to the oppressed people all over the world and strengthens their confidence in defeating imperialism.

### 参加亚洲妇女代表会议　苏代表团昨抵京　叙利亚黎巴嫩代表同来  京昨举行盛会热烈欢迎

1949-12-09
第1版()
专栏：

　　参加亚洲妇女代表会议
  　苏代表团昨抵京
  　叙利亚黎巴嫩代表同来
    京昨举行盛会热烈欢迎
    【新华社北京八日电】以巴菲诺娃为首的苏联代表团及叙利亚代表萨尔玛·布尼，阿梅涅·阿莱芙·喀萨布，黎巴嫩代表维多利亚·爱尔·海露等十余人已于今日上午十时半到达北京，参加亚洲妇女代表会议。将列席会议的来宾有西非洲的欧仁夫人及马达加斯加的叶赛尔·拉贝萨哈拉亦随同前来。已抵北京之各国代表及中华全国民主妇联副主席邓颖超、李德全、许广平、中华全国总工会副主席刘宁一，中华全国文学艺术界联合会代表萧三，中央人民政府外交部长周恩来代表阎宝航，北京市人民政府代表马振武，北京市民主妇联主席张晓梅等均往欢迎。各国代表由欢迎者陪同至招待所时，古久里夫人及中国妇女代表百余人趋前握手，并即举行欢迎会。邓颖超、李德全及张晓梅相继致欢迎词。邓颖超说：“苏联妇女所走的道路，就是我们所要走的道路。”李德全代表亚洲妇女代表会议国际筹备委员会向各代表致敬，并说：“苏联妇女是亚洲妇女以及全世界妇女的最亲密的朋友。苏联妇女对全世界被压迫民族的同情与援助，给予我们无上的兴奋与感动。叙利亚、黎巴嫩、西非洲及马达加斯加的妇女是我们在争取民族独立，人民民主与世界持久和平斗争中的朋友，我们热烈欢迎你们的到来。”张晓梅代表北京一百万妇女欢迎各国姊妹，并表示北京妇女一定以全力帮助亚洲妇女代表会议的工作。各国代表在致答词中，一致赞扬中国人民的胜利。苏联代表团长、苏联妇女反法西斯委员会副主席巴菲诺娃说：“中国人民的胜利，是全世界人类进步的胜利，是全世界人民争取持久和平的胜利，中国的胜利，更加强了以苏联为首的世界民主和平阵营的力量。”黎巴嫩及叙利亚的代表们说，中国人民的胜利对帝国主义者一个最严重的打击。马达加斯加和西非来宾讲话时，对于中国人民及其伟大领袖毛泽东主席，表示无限敬意。她们认为中国的胜利，是全世界被压迫人民的新的鼓舞，加强了他们战胜帝国主义的信心。
