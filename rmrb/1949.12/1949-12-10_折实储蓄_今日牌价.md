Title: Value-Guaranteed Savings Exchange Rate Today

Author: 
Date: 1949-12-10
Page: 2

　　Value-Guaranteed Savings
  　Today's Exchange Rate
    The People's Bank of China, Beijing Branch Savings Department, announced today (the 10th) that the standard unit price for value-guaranteed savings is 3,042.91 yuan.
