Title: Soviet Government Orders Display of National Flags for Celebration; Over Eight Hundred Higher Education Institutions Throughout the Soviet Union Enthusiastically Prepare to Celebrate Birthday; Athletes in Various Cities Organize Cross-Country Propaganda Runs and Skating Competitions

Author:

Date: 1949-12-21
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, 20th: TASS Moscow, 20th - To celebrate the 70th birthday of Chairman of the Council of Ministers of the USSR, Stalin, the Presidium of the Supreme Soviet of the USSR has instructed all Soviet central and local authorities, Soviet embassies, legations, consulates, and trade representatives abroad, to display the national flag of the Union of Soviet Socialist Republics on their respective buildings, at railway stations, docks and inland river stations, on ships of the Soviet navy and merchant fleet, and on residences, on December 21st.

Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, 19th: TASS Moscow - The Soviet Union's eight hundred and thirty-seven institutions of higher education are all preparing to celebrate the 70th birthday of Comrade Stalin, the great leader of the Soviet people. Universities and colleges in various regions will hold scientific conferences and gatherings to report on Stalin's incomparably great genius and the outstanding role he has played in the development of the Soviet Union. Students at the Yakutsk Pedagogical Institute in distant Yakutia have already held an academic discussion on the topic of "Joseph Stalin - The Great Scientific Leader". Students in Kiev, Alma-Ata, Frunze, Baku, Minsk, and Stalinabad are all preparing with great enthusiasm to celebrate the 70th birthday of their beloved leader. Azerbaijan University has announced a competition for the best scientific and literary works to celebrate Comrade Stalin.

Soviet youth have decided to hold thousands of exhibitions to congratulate their great teacher and friend. Student clubs will hold concerts and photo exhibitions about Stalin's life and deeds on this commemorative holiday.

Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, 20th: TASS Moscow - Soviet athletes, together with the entire Soviet people, are preparing to solemnly celebrate the glorious festival - the 70th birthday of the great Stalin. Athletes in Moscow, Leningrad, Tbilisi, Yerevan, Perm, Molotov, and other cities are organizing cross-country propaganda runs on motorcycles, skis, and bicycles, as well as skating, swimming, and sports competitions.

A competition week open to any skater began on the 15th at the "Red Banner" stadium in the Stalin district of Moscow. The winners of these competitions will receive prizes commemorating Stalin. The cross-country propaganda run for the best skiers is scheduled to begin on the 18th at the Stalin Culture and Recreation Park in Izmailovo. They will first go to the Uvarov district of the Moscow region (belonging to the Stalin district of Moscow). Participants will deliver speeches in villages along the way about the life and revolutionary deeds of the great leader of the working people of the world. Skiers in the Urals have organized a four-hundred-kilometer race. Participants include the best athletes - Stakhanovite workers from Molotov City who completed the Stalin Five-Year Plan long ago. They will visit Kungur, Chusovoy, Lysva, and other cities. This race will end on the 21st in the central square of Molotov City.

### 苏政府训令悬国旗志庆　全苏八百余所高等教育机关热烈筹备祝寿  各城市运动员组织越野宣传及溜冰等竞赛

1949-12-21
第4版()
专栏：

　　苏政府训令悬国旗志庆
    全苏八百余所高等教育机关热烈筹备祝寿
    各城市运动员组织越野宣传及溜冰等竞赛
    【新华社北京二十日电】塔斯社莫斯科二十日讯：为庆祝苏联部长会议主席斯大林七十大寿，苏联最高苏维埃主席团训令所有一切苏联中央与地方机关，苏联驻国外的大使馆、公使馆、领事馆及贸易代表，于十二月二十一日在他们各自的建筑物上，在火车站、码头与内河航站、在苏联的海军与商船队的船只上，并且在住宅上，都悬挂苏维埃社会主义共和国联盟的国旗。
    【新华社北京十九日电】塔斯社莫斯科讯：苏联的八百三十七所高等教育机关，都在筹备庆祝苏联人民伟大的领袖斯大林同志的七十寿辰。各地大学和学院，均将举行科学会议和集会，报告斯大林无比伟大的天才、以及在苏联发展串所起的杰出作用。遥远的雅库吉阿教育学院的学生，已举行了学术讨论会，讨论的题目是“约瑟夫·斯大林——伟大的科学领袖”。基辅、阿拉木图、伏龙芝、巴库、明斯克、斯大林阿巴德的学生们，都在以非常的热忱来筹备庆祝他们所爱戴的领袖的七十寿辰。阿塞拜疆大学已宣布举行最优秀的科学著作和最优秀的文学著作的比赛，以庆贺斯大林同志。
    苏联青年已决定举行千百个展览会，以祝贺他们的伟大导师和朋友。在这个纪念节日，学生俱乐部将举行音乐会以及关于斯大林生平事绩的图片展览会。
    【新华社北京二十日电】塔斯社莫斯科讯：苏联的运动员和苏联全国人民一道，正在准备隆重地庆祝光荣的节日——伟大的斯大林的七十寿辰。莫斯科、列宁格勒、第佛利斯、埃里温、培林、莫洛托夫及其他城市的运动员，正在组织机器脚踏车、滑雪、自行车的越野宣传赛和溜冰、游泳与运动的竞赛。
    任何溜冰者都能参加的竞赛周，已于十五日在莫斯科斯大林区的“红旗”运动场开始。在这些竞赛中的优胜者，将得到纪念斯大林的奖品。最优秀滑雪者的越野宣传赛定于十八日在伊兹马伊罗沃的斯大林文化与休息公园开始。他们将首途赴莫斯科州的乌瓦罗夫区（属莫斯科的斯大林区）。参加比赛者在沿途村庄中将发表关于全世界劳动人民的伟大领袖的生平及其革命事迹的演说。乌拉尔的滑雪者组织了四百分里的赛跑。参加者包括最好的运动员——早就完成了斯大林五年计划的莫洛托夫城的斯塔哈诺夫运动者。他们将访问龚古尔、朱梭沃伊、里斯区及其他城市。这个赛跑将于二十一日在莫洛托夫城的中央广场结束。
