Title: Postal and Telecommunications Services Resume Between Beijing, Shanghai, and Chongqing
Author:
Date: 1949-12-06
Page: 4

　　Between Beijing, Shanghai, and Chongqing
    Postal and Telecommunications Services Resume
    [Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, 3rd] On the day after Chongqing's liberation (the 1st), Beijing, Tianjin, Shanghai, Xi'an, and other places resumed radio communications with Chongqing. On the 1st, the Shanghai Telecommunications Bureau received many telegrams from Chongqing reporting "All is safe." Wireless telephone service between Shanghai and Chongqing was also re-established on the same day.
    Post offices in all liberated areas of the country have also resumed postal services with Chongqing and the liberated areas of Sichuan, Guizhou, and Guangxi.

### 京沪渝间  邮电恢复

1949-12-06
第4版()
专栏：

　　京沪渝间
    邮电恢复
    【新华社北京三日电】北京、天津、上海、西安等地在重庆解放后的第二天（一日）就与重庆恢复了无线电通报。上海电讯局一日接到由重庆发出的“报告平安”的电报很多。沪渝间无线电话，亦于同日取得连络。
    全国解放区各地邮局，亦已与重庆和川、黔、桂已解放各地恢复通邮。
