Title: Telegram of Respect to Chairman Mao

Author: 
Date: 1949-12-27
Page: 1

　　Telegram of Respect to Chairman Mao
    [Another News] The 25th Capital Area Annual Meeting of the Geological Society of China unanimously passed a telegram of respect to the great people's leader Chairman Mao, stating: "From now on, we will more resolutely follow you forever, accept revolutionary political education, strive to improve our professional skills, and serve the people wholeheartedly!"

### 电毛主席致敬

1949-12-27
第1版()
专栏：

　　电毛主席致敬
    【又讯】中国地质学会第二十五届首都区年会一致通过向伟大的人民领袖毛主席致敬的电文；内称：“今后我们要更坚决地永远跟着您走，接受革命的政治教育，努力提高业务水平，全心全意地为人民服务！”
