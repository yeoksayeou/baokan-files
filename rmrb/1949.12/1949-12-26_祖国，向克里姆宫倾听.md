Title: Motherland, Listen to the Kremlin
Author: Niu Han
Date: 1949-12-26
Page: 6

　　Motherland, Listen to the Kremlin
    Niu Han
    Chairman Mao,
    What are you thinking
    as you enter the Kremlin?
　  Thinking:
    The people of the motherland
    have words to say to Stalin.
    Thinking:
    The cities and villages of the motherland,
    parades of joy,
    are marching on, one after another.
　  Thinking:
    At this very moment,
    as you stand at Yaroslavsky Station,
    the Soviet people incessantly shout "Hurrah! Mao Zedong! Hurrah! Mao Zedong!..."
    In China, how many newly liberated cities
    have raised the red flag?
    Your people,
    your soldiers,
    are also cheering your name.
　  Chairman Mao,
    let me tell you,
    all our arms
    are reaching out towards the North Star!
    The land of the motherland
    is listening to Moscow,
    the people of the motherland
    are listening to Moscow,
    listening to you,
    how you enter the Kremlin,
    and how you talk so intimately with Stalin...
　  Stalin's outstretched hand towards the East
    is familiar to the Chinese people.
    That gesture,
    for thirty years,
    has guided us to advance bravely,
    pointing us to
    the direction of victory.
    Chairman Mao,
    the land of China,
    is now listening to the Kremlin...
　  We
    seem to hear
    the Kremlin bells echoing in the blue Moscow sky.
    You and Stalin
    are walking hand in hand,
    laughing,
    talking intimately,
    walking step by step across Red Square,
    standing
    atop Lenin's Mausoleum.
　  You gaze into the distance,
    the whole world
    is unfolded gloriously
    before you!
    　　December 20, 1949.

### 祖国，向克里姆宫倾听
牛汉
1949-12-26
第6版()
专栏：

　　祖国，向克里姆宫倾听
    牛汉
    毛主席呵
    你走进克里姆宫的时候
    想些什么？
　  想到：
    祖国的人民
    向斯大林要说的话
    想到：
    祖国底城市和乡村
    欢舞的行列
    正在一队跟着一队的行进
　  想到：
    就在这时
    你站在雅罗斯拉夫车站的时候
    苏联人民不息地呼喊着“乌拉！毛泽东！乌拉！毛泽东！……”
    在中国又有多少个新解放的城市
    插起了红旗
    你底人民
    你底战士
    也正在欢呼着你底名字
　  毛主席
    告诉你
    我们底手臂
    全都向北极星伸出来了！
    祖国底在地
    正向莫斯科倾听
    祖国底人民
    正在向莫斯科倾听
    倾听着你
    怎样走进克里姆宫
    又怎样和斯大林亲切的谈论着……
　  斯大林底向东方伸出的手臂
    中国底人民的熟悉的呵
    那手势
    卅年来
    一直指引着我们奋勇前进
    指给了我们
    胜利的方向
    毛主席呵
    中国底大地
    现在，正在向克里姆宫倾听……
　  我们
    仿佛听到
    克里姆宫底钟声在蓝色的莫斯科底天空回响着
    你和斯大林
    正手挽着手
    笑着
    亲热的谈论着
    一步一步地走过红场
    站到
    列宁底墓顶上
　  你们向着远方凝望着
    全世界
    在你们面前
    光彩地展开了！
    　　一九四九，一二，二○。
