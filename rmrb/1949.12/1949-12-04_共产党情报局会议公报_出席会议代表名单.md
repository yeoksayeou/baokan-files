Title: Communist Information Bureau Meeting Communiqué List of Delegates Attending the Meeting
Author:
Date: 1949-12-04
Page: 2

Xinhua News Agency, Prague, 3rd: The full text of the Communist Information Bureau meeting communiqué is as follows:

In the second half of November, the Information Bureau held a meeting in Hungary. The delegates attending the meeting were as follows:

　　Bulgarian Communist Party: Comrade V. Chervenkov, Comrade V. Poptomov; Romanian Workers' Party: Comrade G. Gheorghiu-Dej, Comrade J. Chisinevschi, Comrade A. Moghioroş;

　　Hungarian Working People's Party: Comrade M. Rákosi, Comrade A. Gerő, Comrade J. Révai, Comrade J. Kádár;

　　Polish United Workers' Party: Comrade J. Berman, Comrade A. Zawadzki;

　　Communist Party of the Soviet Union (Bolsheviks): Comrade M. Suslov, Comrade P. Yudin;

　　French Communist Party: Comrade J. Duclos, Comrade E. Fajon, Comrade G. Cogniot;

　　Czechoslovak Communist Party: Comrade R. Slánský, Comrade S. Bašťovanský, Comrade L. Kopřiva, Comrade B. Geminder;

　　Italian Communist Party: Comrade P. Togliatti, Comrade E. D'Onofrio, Comrade A. Scoccimarro.

　　The meeting heard the following reports: Comrade M. Suslov – "Defending Peace and the Struggle Against Warmongers"; Comrade P. Togliatti – "The Unity of the Working Class and the Tasks of the Communist and Workers' Parties"; Comrade G. Gheorghiu-Dej – "The Yugoslav Communist Party in the Hands of Murderers and Spies".

　　After exchanging opinions on these reports, the delegates reached complete agreement in their views and unanimously adopted the corresponding resolutions.

### 共产党情报局会议公报　出席会议代表名单

1949-12-04
第2版()
专栏：

　　共产党情报局会议公报
  　出席会议代表名单
    【新华社布拉格三日电】共产党情报局会议公报全文如下：
    在十一月下半月，情报局在匈牙利举行了一次会议，出席会议的代表如下：
    保加利亚共产党：Ｖ·契尔文科夫同志，Ｖ·波普托莫夫同志；罗马尼亚工人党：Ｇ·乔治乌——德治同志，Ｊ·契希纳乌斯奇同志，Ａ·莫格罗斯同志；
    匈牙利劳动人民党：Ｍ·拉科西同志，Ａ·格罗同志，Ｊ·里瓦伊同志，Ｊ·卡达尔同志；
    波兰统一工人党：Ｊ·柏尔曼同志，Ａ·萨瓦德斯基同志；
    苏联共产党（布）：Ｍ·苏斯洛夫同志，Ｐ·尤丁同志；
    法国共产党：Ｊ·杜克洛同志，Ｅ·法戎同志，Ｇ·郭尼奥同志；
    捷克斯洛伐克共产党：Ｒ·斯兰斯基同志，Ｓ·巴斯托凡斯基同志，Ｌ·科普里瓦同志，Ｂ·詹明德同志；
    意大利共产党：Ｐ·托里亚蒂同志，Ｅ·多诺弗利奥同志，Ａ·西卡里尼同志。
    会议听取了下列报告：Ｍ·苏斯洛夫同志——“保卫和平与对战争贩子的斗争”；Ｐ·托里亚蒂同志——“工人阶级的团结与共产党和工人党的任务”；Ｇ·乔治乌——德治同志——“在杀人犯与间谍掌握中的南斯拉夫共产党”。
    代表们就这些报告交换意见后，在见解上取得完全一致，并一致通过了相应的决议案。
