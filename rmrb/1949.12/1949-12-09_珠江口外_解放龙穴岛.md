Title: Liberating Longxue Island Outside the Pearl River Estuary
Author:
Date: 1949-12-09
Page: 1

　　Pearl River Estuary

  　Liberating Longxue Island
    [Xinhua News Agency, Guangzhou, 8th] A unit of the People's Liberation Army's Guangdong-Guangxi Column liberated Longxue Island outside the Pearl River Estuary at 5 PM on the 4th, the defending enemy fled in disarray. Longxue Island is located in the Pearl River Estuary, fifteen li southwest of Humen, and was a nest for remnant Kuomintang bandits' naval plunder.

### 珠江口外　解放龙穴岛

1949-12-09
第1版()
专栏：

　　珠江口外
  　解放龙穴岛
    【新华社广州八日电】人民解放军两广纵队一部四日下午五时解放珠江口外的龙穴岛，守敌逃窜。龙穴岛位于珠江口，在虎门西南十五里，为国民党残匪舰艇进行海上掠夺的巢穴。
