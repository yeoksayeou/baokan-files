Title: Norwegian Communist Party Central Committee and Swedish Communist Party Central Committee Congratulate Stalin on his Birthday - Your name is the source of strength for the Soviet people and all freedom-loving people in the world.
Author:
Date: 1949-12-25
Page: 4

　　Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, 23rd. (Teletype dispatch from Telepress, 22nd) The Central Committee of the Norwegian Communist Party, on behalf of the Norwegian Communist Party and all Norwegian men and women who live and fight for peace and socialism, sent a telegram congratulating Stalin on his birthday. The telegram stated: "In the difficult years of the war, when Fascism launched an offensive against all democracy, all progress, and civilization, your name was the source of strength for the Soviet people and all freedom-loving people in the world. The Norwegian people also drew inspiration and strength from you in actively resisting Nazi barbarism."

    The Central Committee of the Swedish Communist Party sent congratulations to Stalin, stating: "We salute you as a symbol of socialism and progress, a great fighter for the cause of peace, a representative of good neighborly policies towards our country, and a powerful defender of small countries."

### 挪共中央瑞共中央电贺斯大林寿辰——你的名字是苏联人民和全世界一切爱好自由人民的力量泉源。

1949-12-25
第4版()
专栏：

　　挪共中央瑞共中央电贺斯大林寿辰
    ——你的名字是苏联人民和全世界一切爱好自由人民的力量泉源。
    【新华社北京二十三日电】综合电通社二十二日消息：挪威共产党中央委员会代表挪威共产党和一切为和平和社会主义而生活和战斗的挪威男女电贺斯大林寿辰。贺电称：“在战争的艰苦岁月里，法西斯主义对一切民主、一切进步和文明发动进攻，你的名字是苏联人民和全世界一切爱好自由人民的力量泉源。挪威人民在积极抵抗纳粹野蛮主义中也从你那里吸取了鼓舞和力量”。
    瑞典共产党中央委员会向斯大林致贺称：“我们向你致敬，是向社会主义和进步的象征及和平事业的伟大战士致敬、是向对我国执行善邻政策的代表和小国的强有力的保卫者致敬。
