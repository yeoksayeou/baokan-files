Title: Beijing, Lushun-Dalian and Shandong and Other Places Prepare to Hold Celebration Parties, Launch Signature Campaign to Offer Birthday Wishes

Author:

Date: 1949-12-16
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, 15th—The Sino-Soviet Friendship Associations in Beijing, Lushun-Dalian, Shandong, and other places are actively preparing to celebrate the 70th birthday of the great teacher of mankind, Marshal Stalin. The Beijing branch of the Sino-Soviet Friendship Association has decided to launch a signature campaign to offer birthday wishes. Various branch associations under the association will hold various forms of celebration parties on the 20th or 21st. On the 21st, they will go to the Soviet Embassy to offer birthday wishes to Marshal Stalin. The branch also decided to screen films such as "The Battle of Stalingrad" and "The Vow" in factories and troops from the 15th to the 25th.

The Lushun-Dalian area established a preparatory committee for celebrating Marshal Stalin's 70th birthday on the 12th and decided to hold a grand celebration meeting and celebration party on the 21st. Each city and county will hold separate celebrations on the same day; all organs, groups, and schools above the district level will, in addition to separate celebrations, invite experts to report on Stalin's life. The Lushun-Dalian branch of the Sino-Soviet Friendship Association decided to hold a Stalin photo exhibition. The Lushun-Dalian Postal Administration plans to issue Stalin commemorative stamps.

All parts of Shandong are also actively preparing for birthday celebrations. The Jinan branch of the Sino-Soviet Friendship Association decided to launch a 200,000-person signature campaign to offer birthday wishes, and a birthday celebration party is scheduled for the 21st. Jinan People's Broadcasting Station will add programs on this day to introduce Stalin's life. The Qingdao branch of the Sino-Soviet Friendship Association has designated the period from the 18th to the 25th as a propaganda week, mobilizing all members to study Stalin's biography; each branch will hold seminars and prepare two artistic portraits of Stalin as birthday gifts. The Municipal Federation of Trade Unions called on the use of practical actions of production and frugality to celebrate Stalin's birthday.

Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, 15th—The capital's youth are welcoming Marshal Stalin's 70th birthday with extreme excitement. The Beijing Municipal Committee of the Communist Youth League, representing 13,000 League members in the city, wrote a letter to Marshal Stalin to offer birthday wishes and called on all League members and the broad masses of youth to study Chen Boda's essay "Stalin and the Chinese Revolution" together with people from all walks of life; actively participate in the signature birthday wishes, lectures, exhibitions, and visits to the Soviet Embassy organized by the Beijing branch of the Sino-Soviet Friendship Association, and other celebration activities. The China Youth Society and the Beijing Youth Society jointly organized the China Youth Lecture Series to hold a lecture on "Stalin's Stories." In addition to participating in activities centered on the branches of the Sino-Soviet Friendship Association, the youth of various factories, schools, and organs also hold various forms of small-scale celebrations according to the specific conditions of each unit. "Beijing Youth" and "Beijing Children" have both published special issues, publishing large photos of Stalin, Stalin's deeds and stories, etc.

### 北京、旅大和山东等地  筹备举行庆祝晚会　发起签名祝寿运动

1949-12-16
第1版()
专栏：

　　北京、旅大和山东等地
    筹备举行庆祝晚会
  　发起签名祝寿运动
    【新华社北京十五日电】北京，旅大和山东等地中苏友好协会纷纷准备庆祝伟大的人类导师斯大林大元帅的七十寿辰。中苏友好协会北京分会决定发起签名祝寿运动。该会所属各支会将于二十日或二十一日举行各种形式的庆祝晚会，并定二十一日分别前往苏联大使馆为斯大林大元帅祝寿。该分会并决定十五日至二十五日在工厂、部队中巡回放映“斯大林格勒保卫战”、“宣誓”等影片。
    旅大地区于十二日成立庆祝斯大林大元帅七十寿辰筹备委员会，并决定二十一日举行各界祝寿大会及祝寿晚会。各市、县将于同日分别举行庆祝会；各区级以上机关，团体及中等以上学校除分别庆祝外，并将邀请专人报告斯大林生平事迹。中苏友好协会旅大分会决定举办斯大林照片展览。旅大邮政总局拟发行斯大林纪念邮票。
    山东各地亦积极筹备祝寿。中苏友好协会济南分会决定发起二十万人签名祝寿运动；并定二十一日举行祝寿晚会。济南人民广播电台将于是日增加节目，介绍斯大林的生平。中苏友好协会青岛分会定十八日起至二十五日止为宣传周，发动全体会员学习斯大林传略；各支会则举行座谈会，并拟制斯大林美术肖像两幅，作为寿辰献礼。市总工会号召用生产节约的实际行动来庆祝斯大林的寿辰。
    【新华社北京十五日电】首都青年正以极端兴奋的心情，迎接斯大林大元帅七十寿辰。代表全市一万三千青年团员的青年团北京市工作委员会写信给斯大林大元帅祝寿，并号召全市团员和广大青年与各界人民在一起，学习陈伯达所著“斯大林与中国革命”的论文；积极参加中苏联友好协会北京分会组织的签名祝寿、演讲会、展览会及前往苏联大使馆祝寿等庆祝活动。中国青年社和北京青年社合办的中国青年讲演会举行“斯大林故事”的讲演。各工厂、学校、机关的青年，除以中苏友协支会为中心参加活动外，并根据各单位具体情况，分别举行各种形式的小规模庆祝。“北京青年”、“北京儿童”都出版特刊，刊载大幅斯大林照片、斯大林事迹及故事等。
