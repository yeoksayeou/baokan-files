Title: Sino-Korean Postal and Telecommunications Agreement Signed; Representatives of Both Countries Deliver Speeches Full of Friendly Spirit
Author:
Date: 1949-12-26
Page: 1
　　Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, December 25th: The negotiations between the People's Republic of China and the Democratic People's Republic of Korea on mutual postal and telecommunications services have concluded successfully. The Sino-Korean Postal and Telecommunications Agreement was formally signed today by representatives of both sides and will take effect upon approval by both governments. Signing on behalf of our country were Wang Zigang, Deputy Director of the Telecommunications Bureau of the Ministry of Posts and Telecommunications and Chief Representative of our country participating in the negotiations, and representatives Xu Chuanxian and Huang Ruzu; signing on behalf of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea were Cha Byeong-heon, Minister of the Foreign Communications Department of the Ministry of Posts and Telecommunications and Chief Representative of Korea, and representative Song Rye-hyeon. Prior to the signing, the chief representatives of China and Korea delivered speeches successively. Wang Zigang, the chief representative of our country, expressed his gratitude to the Korean representatives for their friendly cooperation in the negotiations. Cha Byeong-heon, the chief representative of Korea, expressed his gratitude to the Chinese side for their friendship and assistance and stated that he would conscientiously implement the agreement after returning to his country. Zhu Xuefan, Minister of Posts and Telecommunications of our country, also spoke at the conclusion of the signing ceremony, saying: This agreement was signed on the basis of equality and mutual benefit between the two great nations of China and Korea and in a spirit of friendship and harmony. It will be of great help in strengthening the friendly relations between China and Korea. Those who also attended this Sino-Korean Postal and Telecommunications Agreement signing ceremony included Chen Yun, Vice Premier of the Central People's Government and Director of the Financial and Economic Committee, Zhang Hanfu, Deputy Minister of Foreign Affairs of the Central People's Government, Qi Yanming, Acting Secretary-General of the State Council of the Central People's Government, Xue Muqiao, Secretary-General of the Financial and Economic Committee of the State Council, and Yan Baohang, Deputy Director of the General Office of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs.

### 中朝通邮通电协定签字  两国代表致词充满友好精神

1949-12-26
第1版()
专栏：

　　中朝通邮通电协定签字
  　两国代表致词充满友好精神
    【新华社北京二十五日电】中华人民共和国与朝鲜民主主义人民共和国互通邮电谈判已圆满结束，中朝通邮通电协定已于今日由双方代表正式签字，一俟双方政府批准即行生效。代表我国签字者为参加此次谈判的我国首席代表邮电部电讯总局副局长王子纲、代表徐传贤、黄如祖；代表朝鲜民主主义人民共和国签字者为朝鲜首席代表递信省国外通信部部长车秉亨和代表宋礼贤。在签字之前，中朝两国首席代表相继致词，我国首席代表王子纲对朝鲜代表在谈判中的友好合作表示谢意，朝鲜首席代表车秉亨对中国方面的友谊帮助表示感谢，并表示回国后将切实履行协定。我国邮电部朱学范部长亦于签字完毕时讲话，他说：这一协定是在中朝两大民族平等互利的基础上，在友好和谐的精神下签定的。它对于加强中朝两国的友好关系将大有帮助。参加这次中朝通邮通电协定签字典礼的尚有中华人民共和国中央人民政府政务院副总理兼财经委员会主任陈云、中央人民政府外交部副部长章汉夫、中央人民政府政务院代秘书长齐燕铭、政务院财经委员会秘书长薛幕桥、外交部办公厅副主任阎宝航等。
