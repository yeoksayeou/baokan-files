Title: Southwest of Fushun, Western Sichuan: An Entire Enemy Division Annihilated
Author:
Date: 1949-12-14
Page: 1

　　Xinhua News Agency, Southwest Front, 13th: A unit of the People's Liberation Army, advancing towards western Sichuan, annihilated the entire 364th Division of the 110th Army (reorganized from the Sichuan Independent Division) at Qiangliba, 50 li southwest of Fushun, on the 8th of this month, capturing over 2,200 men including Division Chief of Staff Chen Shuohong.

### 川西富顺西南　歼敌一个师

1949-12-14
第1版()
专栏：

　　川西富顺西南
  　歼敌一个师
    【新华社西南前线十三日电】向川西进击的人民解放军某部，本月八日在富顺西南五十里地区之枪历坝，歼敌一一零军之三六四师（四川独立师改编）全部，俘师参谋长陈硕宏以下二千二百余人。
