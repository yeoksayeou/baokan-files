Title: Chengdu Northeast Subjugates Deyang; Military Control Commissions Established in Yibin and Other Areas of Southern Sichuan
Author:
Date: 1949-12-27
Page: 1
　　Xinhua News Agency, Southwest Front, 26th: The People's Liberation Army of Northern Sichuan liberated Deyang County City, northeast of Chengdu, on the 23rd.
　　Xinhua News Agency, Chongqing, 26th: Military Control Commissions of Yibin, Neijiang, Luxian, Zigong and other areas in Southern Sichuan have been established upon the order of the Second Field Army of the Chinese People's Liberation Army. Chen Lin is the Director of the Yibin Military Control Commission; Chen Gang and Zhang Li are the Director and Deputy Director of the Neijiang Military Control Commission; Wang Qihai is the Director of the Luxian Military Control Commission; Wang ＠gang is the Director of the Zigong Municipal Military Control Commission.

### 成都东北克德阳　川南宜宾等地军管会成立

1949-12-27
第1版()
专栏：

　　成都东北克德阳
　  川南宜宾等地军管会成立
    【新华社西南前线二十六日电】川北人民解放军于二十三日解放成都东北的德阳县城。
    【新华社重庆二十六日电】川南宜宾、内江、泸县、自贡等地军事管制委员会，已奉中国人民解放军第二野战军命令成立。陈林为宜宾军事管制委员会主任，陈刚、张励为内江军事管制委员会正副主任，王其海为泸县军事管制委员会主任，王＠刚为自贡市军事管制委员会主任。
