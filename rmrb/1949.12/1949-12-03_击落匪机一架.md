Title: One Bandit Plane Shot Down
Author:
Date: 1949-12-03
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, South China Front, December 2nd. On the morning of the 28th of last month, a unit of the People's Liberation Army shot down a Kuomintang P-51 aircraft in the territory of Luoding County, southwest of Guangdong. The bandit Lieutenant pilot Yu Dunzhi was captured by farmers in Dexin Township, Maoming County, and has been escorted to a unit of the People's Liberation Army. The plane belonged to the 28th Squadron of the 3rd Group of the bandit air force, and its number was 2816.

### 击落匪机一架

1949-12-03
第1版()
专栏：

　　击落匪机一架
    【新华社华南前线二日电】上月二十八日晨人民解放军某部在粤西南罗定县境击落国民党Ｐ五一型飞机一架。匪中尉飞行员余敦质当被茂名县德新乡农民俘获，并已押送解放军某部。该机属匪空军第三大队第二十八中队，机号为二八一六号。
