Title: Asian Women's Conference Sends Respects to Mrs. Gordon and Dolores

Author:
Date: 1949-12-15
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, December 14th.
Asian Women's Conference Sends Respects to Mrs. Gordon
The Asian Women's Representative Conference today passed a message of respect to Mrs. Gordon, President of the Women's International Democratic Federation. The text of the telegram is as follows:
Dear President of the Women's International Democratic Federation:

The Asian Women's Representative Conference is very grateful for your congratulations and expresses its regret that you could not attend our conference.

The conference expresses its warm respect for your active participation in the struggle for the independence of oppressed nations and the protection of the rights of women and children, and for your active participation in the work of the world peace camp.

This conference can assure you that it will make a more decisive struggle to safeguard the bright future of women and children in freedom, democracy and peace!

Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, December 14th.
The Asian Women's Representative Conference today passed a message of respect to Dolores Ibárruri, Vice-President of the Women's International Democratic Federation. The text of the telegram is as follows:
Dear Dolores:

The Asian Women's Representative Conference has received your telegram congratulating the success of our conference and is very moved, and at the same time, expresses its deep gratitude to you.

We salute the women of Spain, who are persecuted by Franco but still stand strong and fight for world peace and democracy.

We make a solemn promise to the women of Spain that we will fight with a hundred times more effort to win a victory every day in order to overthrow the terrorist and bloody system of imperialism and fascism and build a happy, just and peaceful world.

　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　Asian Women's Representative Conference

### 亚洲妇女会议　电戈登夫人、多露丽斯致敬

1949-12-15
第4版()
专栏：

　　亚洲妇女会议
  　电戈登夫人、多露丽斯致敬
    【新华社北京十四日电】亚洲妇女代表会议今日通过向国际民主妇联主席戈登夫人的致敬电。电文如下：亲爱的国际民主妇联主席：
    亚洲妇女代表会议对于你的祝贺，十分感谢；对于你的不能来参加我们的会议，表示非常遗憾。
    你的积极参加被压迫民族的独立和妇女儿童权利保障的斗争，你的积极参加世界和平阵营的工作，大会都向你表示热烈的敬意。
    这一次的大会可以向你保证，关于保障妇女儿童在自由、民主与和平中的光明的前途，将作更有决定性的斗争！
    　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　亚洲妇女代表会议
  　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　十二月十四日
    【新华社北京十四日电】亚洲妇女代表会议今日通过向国际民主妇联副主席多露丽斯·依巴鲁里致敬电，电文如下：亲爱的多露丽斯：
    你的祝贺我们会议成功的电报，亚洲妇女代表会议已经收到，非常感动，同时，深深地向你表示谢意。
    西班牙的妇女为佛朗哥所残害，但仍然坚强地站立着为世界的和平与民主而斗争，我们向你们致敬。
    我们向西班牙妇女作一个庄严的诺言，我们一定要用百倍的努力来斗争，争取每天都能获得一种胜利以便打倒帝国主义和法西斯主义的恐怖流血制度，建设一个幸福、公正与和平的世界。
    　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　亚洲妇女代表会议
    　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　十二月十四日
