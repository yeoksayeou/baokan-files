Title: Value-Guaranteed Savings Today's Rate

Author: 
Date: 1949-12-09

　　Value-Guaranteed Savings
  　Today's Rate
    The Beijing Branch of the People's Bank of China, Savings Department, announces that the standard commodity unit rate for value-guaranteed savings today (9th) is 3,050.91 yuan.
