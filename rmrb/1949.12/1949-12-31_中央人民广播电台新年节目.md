Title: Central People's Broadcasting Station New Year Program
Author:
Date: 1949-12-31
Page: 3
Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, December 29th: The Central People's Broadcasting Station, in accordance with the Broadcasting Affairs Bureau's decision for New Year's Day holidays, will only broadcast important news and cultural programs on New Year's Day and January 2, 1950. The broadcast schedule for these two days is as follows:

    First broadcast time (8:30—9:30)
    8:30—8:45　Opening music, report on the day's program
    8:45—9:30　Cultural programs

    Second broadcast time (17:30—22:00)
    17:30—17:45　Opening music, report on the evening's program
    17:45—18:00　Japanese News
    18:00—19:00　Cultural programs
    19:00—19:15　Xiamen dialect news
    19:15—19:30　Chaozhou dialect news
    19:30—19:45　Cantonese news
    19:45—20:00　Records
    20:00—20:20　Joint broadcast of news from local People's Broadcasting Stations, third news broadcast.
    20:20—21:30　Cultural programs
    21:30—22:00　English news, closing music
