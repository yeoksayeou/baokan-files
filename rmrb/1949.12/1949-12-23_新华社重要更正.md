Title: Xinhua News Agency Important Correction
Author:
Date: 1949-12-23
Page: 2

　　Xinhua News Agency Important Correction
    In Article 7, Item (3) of the "General Rules for the Organization of the People's Government Committee of the Greater Administrative Region" published on the 19th of this month, "as well as national cultural enterprises that should be transferred to the central government for operation" should be changed to "as well as national state-owned cultural enterprises." This correction is hereby made.

### 新华社重要更正

1949-12-23
第2版()
专栏：

　　新华社重要更正
    本月十九日发表之“大行政区人民政府委员会组织通则”第七条第（三）项中“以及全国性的应划归中央经营的文化企业”，应改为“以及全国性的国营文化企业”，特此更正。
