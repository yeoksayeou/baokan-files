Title: The Central Committee of the Communist Youth League Direct Organs Committee Holds Oath-Taking and Fellowship Meeting for League Members
Author:
Date: 1949-12-29
Page: 1

　　Youth League Central Committee Directly Subordinate League Committee
　　Holds Oath-Taking and Fellowship Meeting for League Members
    [Beijing News] The Central Committee of the New Democratic Youth League Direct Organs Committee held an oath-taking and fellowship meeting for League members at 7:00 PM yesterday (the 28th) in the auditorium of the Government Information Administration. More than 1,600 League members from 47 units directly under the Central People's Government and the CPC Central Committee attended. The meeting opened with the national anthem. After the chairman delivered the opening speech, 353 new League members took the oath to join the League. Subsequently, Jiang Nanxiang, Deputy Secretary of the Youth League Central Committee, and Liu Huafeng of the CPC Central Committee Direct Organs Committee, delivered speeches, encouraging everyone to be proficient in their work, study diligently, be practical and realistic, and do their work well in their respective positions under the leadership of the Chinese Communist Party, and that everyone should be a good League member.

### 青年团中央直属团委　举行团员宣誓联欢大会

1949-12-29
第1版()
专栏：

　　青年团中央直属团委
　  举行团员宣誓联欢大会
    【北京讯】新民主主义青年团中央直属团委于昨（二十八）日下午七时在新闻总署大礼堂举行团员宣誓联欢大会，到会有中央人民政府及中共中央所属各机关四十七个单位一千六百多团员。大会在国歌声中开幕，主席致开幕词后，三百五十三位新团员宣誓入团。继由团中央副书记蒋南翔及中共中央直属机关党委刘华峰讲话，勉励大家在中国共产党的领导下，要精通业务，努力学习，实事求是，在自己的工作岗位上把工作做好，人人都要做个好团员。
