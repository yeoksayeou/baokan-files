Title: Guilin Military Control Commission Established; "Guangxi Daily" Inaugurated on the 3rd

Author: 
Date: 1949-12-07
Page: 1

　　Guilin Military Control Commission Established
  　"Guangxi Daily" Inaugurated on the 3rd
    [Xinhua News Agency, Guilin, 6th] The Guilin Military Control Commission was established on November 30th by order. Chen Manyuan is the director, and He Wei and Zhong Wei are the deputy directors.
    In addition, the Guangxi Branch of the People's Bank of China was established on the 2nd. The "Guangxi Daily," the organ newspaper of the Guangxi Provincial Committee of the Chinese Communist Party, was inaugurated on the 3rd of this month.

### 桂林军管会成立　“广西日报”已于三日创刊

1949-12-07
第1版()
专栏：

　　桂林军管会成立
  　“广西日报”已于三日创刊
    【新华社桂林六日电】桂林市军事管制委员会已奉命于十一月三十日成立，陈漫远为主任，何伟、钟伟为副主任。
    又中国人民银行广西分行已于二日成立。中共广西省委机关报“广西日报”已于本月三日创刊。
