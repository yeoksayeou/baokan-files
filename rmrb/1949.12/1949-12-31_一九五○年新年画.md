Date: 1949-12-31

    People's Pictorial

    New Year Pictures for 1950

    This year, art workers in various regions have created many New Year pictures. Due to time constraints, New Year pictures from some regions (such as Northeast China and Pingyuan Province) have not yet been collected, and the original manuscripts of New Year pictures from some regions, such as Beijing, are being printed and could not be reproduced for publication. The New Year pictures published this time only include some works from the Shanxi Artists Association, the Inner Mongolia Bookstore, the Shijiazhuang Dazhong Fine Arts Society, and the Shanghai Dazhong Fine Arts Society.
