Title: Value of Commodity Unit Savings Today

Author: 
Date: 1949-12-26
Page: 2

Commodity Unit Savings Value Today

The Beijing Branch Savings Department of the People's Bank of China announces that the standard commodity unit price for commodity unit savings today (December 26th) is 3,287.82 yuan.
