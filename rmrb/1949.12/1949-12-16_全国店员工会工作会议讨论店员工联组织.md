Title: National Shop Employees' Union Work Conference Discusses the Organization of Shop Employee Joint Associations
Author:
Date: 1949-12-16
Page: 1

　　National Shop Employees' Union Work Conference Discusses the Organization of Shop Employee Joint Associations
    [Beijing News] The National Shop Employees' Union Work Conference is continuing. Since the 12th, representatives from shop employees' unions in Tianjin, Zhangjiakou, Shanghai, Nanjing, Wuhan, Tangshan, and other places have reported on the work of shop employees' unions in their respective areas, and exchanged experiences and lessons learned in establishing collective contracts and organizing shop employees' unions. On the 14th, a member of the Shop Employees' Work Committee of the All-China Federation of Trade Unions reported on the organizational system, organizational scope, and trade union staffing of the China Shop Employees' Union Federation. On the 15th, Chang Jinglin, Director of the Shop Employees' Work Committee of the All-China Federation of Trade Unions, reported on the experience of signing collective contracts in private businesses. Representatives from various regions are currently continuing to discuss these reports.

### 全国店员工会工作会议讨论店员工联组织

1949-12-16
第1版()
专栏：

　　全国店员工会工作会议讨论店员工联组织
    【北京讯】全国店员工会工作会议正在继续进行。从十二日起，天津、张家口、上海、南京、武汉、唐山、等地的店员工会代表分别报告了各地店员工会工作的情况，交换了彼此订立集体合同、组织店员工会的经验教训。十四日全国总工会店员工作委员会委员报告了关于中国店员工会联合会的组织系统、组织范围和工会编制问题。十五日全国总工会店员工作委员会主任常景林报告了在私营商业中签订集体合同的经验。现各地代表正在继续讨论这几个报告。
