Title: Long Live Stalin! Celebrations Held in Paris by People from All Walks of Life

Author:

Date: 1949-12-19
Page: 2

Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, December 18th: TASS, Paris, December 17th: On the evening of December 16th, the France-USSR Friendship Association held a celebration in Paris at the Salle Pleyel for Stalin's 70th birthday, attended by people from all walks of life in Paris. The meeting was presided over by Frédéric Joliot-Curie. The hall was filled with a festive atmosphere. A large portrait of Stalin hung on the platform, beneath which was written: "Wishing Marshal Stalin – the architect of peace and the warrior for human happiness and prosperity – longevity and boundless life."

　　In his closing remarks, Curie emphasized Stalin's great historical contribution to defeating the common enemy of mankind – Hitlerite fascism. He said: "On the occasion of Stalin's 70th birthday, we respectfully offer our congratulations to Stalin. Today we are celebrating the birthday of a man who is infinitely loved by people all over the world."

　　All the speakers pointed out the great role Stalin played in building a new life and ensuring peace.

### 敬祝斯大林万寿无疆　巴黎各界举行祝寿大会

1949-12-19
第2版()
专栏：

　　敬祝斯大林万寿无疆
  　巴黎各界举行祝寿大会
    【新华社北京十八日电】塔斯社巴黎十七日讯：十二月十六日晚，法苏友好协会于巴黎的普雷耶尔大厅，举行巴黎各界人士庆祝斯大林七十寿辰大会。会议由约里奥·居里主持。大厅呈现节日的欢愉景象。讲台上悬有斯大林的巨幅肖像，下面写着：“祝斯大林大元帅——和平的建筑师和人类幸福与繁荣的战士——老当益壮，万寿无疆。”
    居里在闭幕词中强调斯大林在击败人类共同的敌人——希特勒法西斯主义中对历史的伟大贡献。他说：“值此斯大林七旬大寿之际，我们敬向斯大林祝贺。今天我们在庆祝一个为全世界的人类所无限敬爱的人的生日。”
    所有演讲的人都指出斯大林在建设新生活及保障和平中所起的巨大作用。
