Title: Hebei Supply and Marketing Cooperative General Agency and North China Coal and Iron Company Sign Contract to Purchase 400,000 Tons of Luan Coal on Credit to Supply the Needs of Farmers in Disaster Areas
Author: Wei Zhong
Date: 1949-12-16
Page: 4

　　Hebei Supply and Marketing Cooperative General Agency and North China Coal and Iron Company Sign Contract to Purchase 400,000 Tons of Luan Coal on Credit to Supply the Needs of Farmers in Disaster Areas

　　[Report from Tianjin by this newspaper] In order to solve the fuel difficulties of farmers in disaster areas of Hebei Province, save grass and manure, increase fertilizer, and lay a favorable foundation for next year's large-scale production campaign, the Hebei Provincial Supply and Marketing Cooperative General Agency signed a contract with the North China Coal and Iron Company in Tianjin on the 12th to purchase 400,000 tons of Kailuan coal on credit. The cooperative is responsible for transporting the coal. In addition to guaranteeing one train per day, the Ministry of Railways will transport 1,200 tons of coal to designated locations; the freight rate is reduced by approximately 25% compared to ordinary coal. The first batch of coal has been shipped since the 13th, supplying disaster-stricken farmers in five special districts including Cangxian, Tianjin, Baoding, Tongxian, and Tangshan.
　　　　　　　　(Wei Zhong)

### 河北供销总社华北煤铁公司订立合同  赊购滦煤四十万吨供应灾区农民需要
伟中
1949-12-16
第4版()
专栏：

　　河北供销总社华北煤铁公司订立合同
    赊购滦煤四十万吨供应灾区农民需要
    【本报天津专讯】为解决河北省灾区农民燃料困难，以节省草粪，增加肥料，给明年大生产运动打下有利基础，河北省供销合作总社于十二日与华北煤铁公司在津订立赊购开滦煤四十万吨合同。运煤由合作社负责，铁道部除保证每日拨给一列车，载煤一千二百吨运送指定地点外；运费较普通煤斤减低约百分之二十五。首批煤斤已于十三日起运，供给沧县、天津、保定、通县、唐山等五个专区受灾农民。
    　　　　　　　　（伟中）
