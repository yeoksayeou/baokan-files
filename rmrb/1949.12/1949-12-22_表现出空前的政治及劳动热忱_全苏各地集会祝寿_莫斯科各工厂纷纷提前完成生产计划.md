Title: Displaying Unprecedented Political and Labor Enthusiasm, Assemblies Throughout the Soviet Union Offer Birthday Greetings; Moscow Factories Complete Production Plans Ahead of Schedule
Author:
Date: 1949-12-22
Page: 2

　　Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, December 21st: TASS Moscow, December 20th: Soviet newspapers published numerous reports about the achievements of the nationwide socialist competition launched by the Soviet working people to celebrate the birthday of the leader, as well as the celebratory rallies and lecture evenings held throughout the Soviet Union to celebrate this glorious festival. The newspapers described the unprecedented political and labor enthusiasm of the working people of Moscow on the eve of the festival. In recent days, personnel from various manufacturing plants and factories in Moscow have participated in the Stakhanovite movement launched to celebrate the 70th birthday of the great leader of the Soviet people, using new labor achievements to celebrate this festival. Personnel at the Moscow Stalin Automobile Factory completed their additional obligations and their annual production plan on the 19th to celebrate the 70th birthday of the great leader of the Soviet people. In the "Zhdanov" district of Moscow alone, more than forty enterprises have completed their annual plans ahead of schedule. In the "October District" of Moscow, the competition among thirty-three enterprises to celebrate Stalin's birthday has resulted in the advance fulfillment of the five-year plans of these enterprises in terms of total output. On the 19th, many celebratory rallies were held by Party, Soviet, and public organizations in many districts of Moscow. Many seminars, lectures, and report evenings on Stalin's life were organized by various enterprises and institutions in Moscow, attracting tens of thousands of listeners. Newspapers also reported on the labor enthusiasm in Leningrad, Tula, Minsk, Kiev, Petrozavodsk, Baku, and other cities, as well as the celebratory rallies and lecture meetings held to celebrate this glorious festival. All newspapers reported on the arrival of foreign representatives to Moscow to participate in the celebration of Stalin's birthday.

### 表现出空前的政治及劳动热忱　全苏各地集会祝寿  莫斯科各工厂纷纷提前完成生产计划

1949-12-22
第2版()
专栏：

　　表现出空前的政治及劳动热忱
    全苏各地集会祝寿
    莫斯科各工厂纷纷提前完成生产计划
    【新华社北京二十一日电】塔斯社莫斯科二十日讯：苏联各报刊载许多消息，报道苏联劳动人民为祝寿而发起的全国性社会主义竞赛所获得的成就，以及苏联各地为庆祝这个光辉的节日而举行的庆祝大会与演讲晚会的情形。报纸描写了节日前夜莫斯科劳动人民洋溢着空前的政治及劳动热忱的情形。这几天，莫斯科各制造厂与工厂的人员，都参加了为祝寿而发动的斯塔哈诺夫运动，他们以新的劳动成绩庆祝这个节日。莫斯科斯大林汽车工厂人员已在十九日完成为庆祝苏联人民伟大领袖七十寿辰所担负的额外义务和他们的一年生产计划。仅在莫斯科“日丹诺夫”一区，便有四十家以上的企业提前完成了他们的一年计划。在莫斯科“十月区”，三十三家企业为了庆祝斯大林寿辰而竞赛的结果，已在总产量方面提前完成了各该企业的五年计划。十九日，莫斯科许多区的党、苏维埃及公共机关举行了许多庆祝大会。莫斯科各企业和机关布置了许多关于斯大林生平的座谈会、演讲和报告晚会，吸引了成千成万的听众。报纸也报道了列宁格勒、图拉、明斯克、基辅、彼得罗萨佛得斯克、巴库及其他城市的劳动热忱和为了庆祝这个光辉节日而举行的庆祝大会、演讲会的消息。各报都报道了前来莫斯科参加庆祝斯大林寿辰的外国代表到临的消息。
