Title: Various Departments of the Soviet Academy of Sciences Celebrate Stalin's Birthday; Prominent Scientists Publish Important Papers

Author:

Date: 1949-12-28
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, December 27th: TASS, Moscow—Departments of the Soviet Academy of Sciences held celebrations in Moscow on the 26th to mark Stalin's 70th birthday. The celebrations were attended by prominent Soviet scientists, professors and students from institutions of higher learning, and representatives of mass organizations in the capital. At the celebration of the Department of Geography, Academician Obruchev, a leading Soviet geologist, gave a report on the rapid progress of Soviet geology in the Stalin era. At the celebration of the Department of Technical Sciences, Academicians Skotchinsky and Vedyensky, and Professor Chernyshev reported on the achievements of Soviet scientists in mining, radio, machine manufacturing, and the fuel industry. At the celebration of the Department of History and Philosophy, Academician Mitin reported on "Stalin and Marxist-Leninist Science." Academician Alexandrov reported on "Stalin's Struggle for Party Spirit in Marxist-Leninist Philosophy." Professor Tolstov described the achievements of Soviet archaeology. At the celebration of the Department of Biology (the largest institution within the Soviet Academy of Sciences, overseeing more than thirty scientific research institutes), Academician Oparin emphasized the diversity of Soviet biological research, encompassing studies from protein molecules to higher nervous activity. Soviet scientists have made discoveries in all branches of biology, enriching theoretical science. Oparin pointed out that, like the achievements of Soviet science in all fields, the achievements of Soviet biology are due to the tireless care of the great Stalin, who guides science to serve the people.

### 苏联科学院所属各部　欢庆斯大林寿辰　卓越科学家纷纷发表重要论文

1949-12-28
第4版()
专栏：

　　苏联科学院所属各部
　  欢庆斯大林寿辰
　  卓越科学家纷纷发表重要论文
    苏联科学巨大成就归功于斯大林指导
    【新华社北京二十七日电】塔斯社莫斯科讯：苏联科学院所属各部门，于二十六日在莫斯科纷纷举行斯大林七十大寿庆祝会。出席庆祝会的，有卓越的苏联科学家、高等教育机关的教授和学生，以及首都各群众组织的代表。地理部的庆祝会上听取了苏联地质学权威、科学院院士奥赫鲁契夫关于苏联地质学在斯大林时代的突飞猛进的报告。在技术科学部的庆祝会上，科学院院士斯科琴斯基、维典斯基和车尼西夫教授报告了苏联科学家在采矿、无线电、机器制造和燃料工业上的成就。在历史与哲学部的庆祝会上，科学院院士米丁报告“斯大林与马列主义科学”。科学院院士阿历山大洛夫报告“斯大林为马列主义哲学中的党性的斗争”。托尔斯托夫教授叙述了苏联考古学的成就。在生物学部（苏联科学院中最大的机构，管辖着三十多个科学研究所）的庆祝会上，科学院院士奥巴林着重指出苏联生物学研究的多样性。这些研究，包罗了从蛋白质分子到高级神经活动的研究。在生物学各部门里，苏联科学家都有所发现，这些发现丰富了理论科学。奥巴林指出：正如苏联各部门科学一样，苏联生物学的成就应归功于伟大的斯大林的不倦关心，他指导科学去为人民服务。
