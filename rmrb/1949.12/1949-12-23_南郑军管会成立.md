Title: Nanzheng Military Control Commission Established
Author:
Date: 1949-12-23
Page: 2

Xinhua News Agency, Xi'an, 21st—The Nanzheng Municipal Military Control Commission was officially established on the 8th of this month. Chen Xianyu is the director of the Military Control Commission, and Zhang Tao and Liu Guosheng are the deputy directors.

　　Furthermore, the Nanzheng Municipal Garrison Command and Political Department were established on the 9th. Chen Xianyu is the commander, Bai Chengming is the political commissar, Zhang Tao is the deputy commander, Wu Wenbin is the deputy political commissar, and Li Zhizhi is the director of the Political Department.

### 南郑军管会成立

1949-12-23
第2版()
专栏：

　　南郑军管会成立
    【新华社西安二十一日电】南郑市军事管制委员会已于本月八日奉命宣告成立。陈先瑜为军管会主任，张涛、刘国声为副主任。
    又南郑市警备司令部、政治部奉命于九日成立。陈先瑜为司令员，白成铭为政治委员，张涛为副司令员，武文斌为副政治委员，李之实为政治部主任。
