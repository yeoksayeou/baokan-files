Title: Xinjiang Branch Established; New Qian Daily Inaugurated in Guiyang
Author:
Date: 1949-12-14
Page: 3

Xinhua News Agency

　　Xinhua News Agency Xinjiang Branch Established
　　New Qian Daily Inaugurated in Guiyang
　　[Xinhua News Agency, Dihua, 13th] The Xinhua News Agency Xinjiang Branch was officially established in Dihua on the 7th.
　　[Xinhua News Agency, Guiyang, 13th] The Guizhou Provincial Committee of the Chinese Communist Party's organ, "New Qian Daily," was inaugurated on November 28th. The newspaper published an editorial on the 2nd, calling on the people of the entire province to quickly stabilize social order, restore production, repair roads, and vigorously support the advance to liberate the entire Southwest.

### 新疆分社成立　新黔日报在贵阳创刊

1949-12-14
第3版()
专栏：

　　新华社新疆分社成立
  　新黔日报在贵阳创刊
    【新华社迪化十三日电】新华社新疆分社七日在迪化正式成立。
    【新华社贵阳十三日电】中共贵州省委机关报“新黔日报”已经在十一月二十八日创刊。该报二日发表社论，号召全省人民迅速安定社会秩序，恢复生产、修补公路，大力支援解放全西南的进军。
