Title: People's Revolutionary Military Commission Establishes Civil Aviation and Meteorological Bureaus; Zhong Chibing and Tu Changwang Appointed as Directors Respectively
Author:
Date: 1949-12-09
Page: 1

　　People's Revolutionary Military Commission
  　Establishes Civil Aviation and Meteorological Bureaus
  　Zhong Chibing and Tu Changwang Appointed as Directors Respectively
    [Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, 8th] Our Central People's Government's People's Revolutionary Military Commission has recently established the Civil Aviation Administration and the Meteorological Administration to manage civil aviation and national meteorological work respectively. The selection of the heads of the two bureaus has been approved by the Military Commission. Zhong Chibing will be the Director of the Civil Aviation Administration; Tu Changwang will be the Director of the Meteorological Administration, with Zhang Naizhao and Lu Wo as Deputy Directors, and Jiang Jintao as Director of the General Office.

### 人民革命军事委员会　设民航气象两局　钟赤兵涂长望分任局长

1949-12-09
第1版()
专栏：

　　人民革命军事委员会
  　设民航气象两局
  　钟赤兵涂长望分任局长
    【新华社北京八日电】我中央人民政府人民革命军事委员会近已设立民用航空局与气象局，分别管理民用航空事业与全国气象工作。两局负责人选业经军委委定，民用航空局由钟赤兵为局长；气象局由涂长望为局长，张乃召、卢鋈为副局长，蒋金涛为办公厅主任。
