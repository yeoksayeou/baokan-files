Title: Chen Jiageng and Others Arrive in Fuzhou
Author:
Date: 1949-12-06
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Fuzhou, 5th—Central People's Government Committee Member Chen Jiageng, and Central Overseas Chinese Affairs Committee Members Zhuang Mingli and Zhang Shuming, arrived in Fuzhou at 8:30 PM on the 3rd. Zhang Dingcheng, Chairman of the Fujian Provincial People's Government, Vice Chairman Ye Fei, Wei Guoqing, Director of the Fuzhou Military Control Commission, Deputy Mayor Xu Ya, and many of Committee Member Chen's friends went to welcome them. Committee Member Chen Jiageng departed from Beijing after the closing of the People's Political Consultative Conference, and arrived here after visiting Jinan, Xuzhou, Wuhan, and other places.

### 陈嘉庚等抵达福州

1949-12-06
第1版()
专栏：

　　陈嘉庚等抵达福州
    【新华社福州五日电】中央人民政府委员陈嘉庚，中央华侨事务委员会委员庄明理、张殊明，于三日晚八时半抵达福州。福建省人民政府主席张鼎丞、副主席叶飞，福州市军管会主任韦国清、副市长许亚等及陈委员友好多人，前往欢迎。陈嘉庚委员系于人民政协闭幕后自北京出发，先后参观济南、徐州、武汉等地后抵达此间。
