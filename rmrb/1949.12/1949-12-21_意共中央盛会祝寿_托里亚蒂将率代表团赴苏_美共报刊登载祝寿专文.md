Title: Italian Communist Party Central Committee Holds Grand Celebration for Birthday; Togliatti to Lead Delegation to Soviet Union; American Communist Press Publishes Special Articles Celebrating Birthday
Author:
Date: 1949-12-21
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, December 20th: TASS, Rome - The Central Committee of the Italian Communist Party held a grand celebration in Rome on the 18th to celebrate Stalin's 70th birthday. Participants included members of the Italian Communist Party Central Committee, members of the Italian Communist Party in the upper and lower houses of parliament, leaders of the Rome Provincial Committee of the Italian Communist Party, 3,000 communist activists from Rome Province, and representatives from democratic groups and democratic newspapers. The General Secretary of the Italian Communist Party, Togliatti, reported on Stalin's life and achievements, as well as his leading role in calling on the people to fight for peace and socialism. The conference unanimously adopted a telegram from the Central Committee of the Italian Communist Party to Comrade Stalin, and elected a delegation headed by the General Secretary of the Italian Communist Party, Togliatti, and Deputy General Secretary Scoccimarro to go to Moscow to pay respects and congratulate Stalin.
    The Italy-Soviet Union Association sent a letter congratulating Stalin on his birthday, stating: "Today, when the threat of war looms darker than before over our Italy, we look with reverence at the Soviet state and its leader, who has made great contributions to strengthening the cause of world peace."
    Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, December 20th: TASS, New York - American progressives are preparing to celebrate Marshal Stalin's 70th birthday. The National Council of the American-Soviet Friendship Association is scheduled to hold a special meeting on the 20th to hear reports on Stalin's role in the creation of the Soviet state and socialist construction, Stalin as the leader of the anti-fascist alliance in the war against Hitler's Germany, and the role of this great leader in the struggle to maintain world peace. On the 17th, the Daily Worker added a special twelve-page section dedicated to articles commemorating Stalin's 70th birthday. The special issue contains articles by William Z. Foster, Chairman of the National Committee of the Communist Party of the United States, the prominent American progressive leader Paul Robeson, the editor of the Daily Worker, Gates, and the editor of the black newspaper published in Harlem, Abner Berry, among others. The American Communist Party's organ, "Political Affairs" monthly, published the concluding remarks of Stalin's biography compiled by the Marx-Engels-Lenin Institute of the Soviet Union in an editorial titled "Stalin is Lenin Today." Another article commemorating Stalin's 70th birthday discussed the great significance of Stalin's activities for progressive humanity worldwide.

### 意共中央盛会祝寿　托里亚蒂将率代表团赴苏　美共报刊登载祝寿专文

1949-12-21
第4版()
专栏：

　　意共中央盛会祝寿
    托里亚蒂将率代表团赴苏
    美共报刊登载祝寿专文
    【新华社北京二十日电】塔斯社罗马消息：意大利共产党中央委员会于十八日在罗马举行盛大庆祝会，庆祝斯大林的七十寿辰。与会者有意共中央委员会的委员，意共上下院议员，意共罗马省省委会的领袖，三千名罗马省的共产党积极分子和民主团体以及民主报纸的代表。意共总书记托里亚蒂在会上报告斯大林的生平和业迹，以及他在号召人民为和平与社会主义而奋斗的事业中的领导作用。大会一致通过意共中央委员会向斯大林同志致贺的电文，并选出以意共总书记托里亚蒂和副总书记谢嘉为首的代表团赴莫斯科向斯大林致敬祝贺。
    意苏协会致函祝贺斯大林的寿辰称：“今天，当战争的威胁在我们意大利上空较前更为阴暗的时候，我们满怀敬意，仰望着苏维埃国家，及其对加强全世界和平事业有巨大贡献的领袖。”
    【新华社北京二十日电】塔斯社纽约讯：美国进步人士正在准备庆祝斯大林大元帅的七十寿辰。美苏友好协会全国委员会定于二十日举行特别会议，听取关于斯大林在苏维埃国家的缔造及社会主义建设中所起的作用、作为反希特勒德国战争中的反法西斯联盟领袖的斯大林，以及这位伟大的领袖在维护世界和平的斗争中所起的作用的报告。在十七日，工人日报增辟十二页特别，专载纪念斯大林七十寿辰的文章。特刊中载有美国共产党全国委员会主席福斯特、著名美国进步领袖罗伯逊、工人日报编辑盖兹和在哈莱姆出版的黑人报纸的编辑埃勃纳芮等人的文章。美共机关刊物“政治事务”月刊，在题为“斯大林是今天的列宁”的社论中发表苏联马恩列学院所编纂的斯大林传略的结束语。在另一篇纪念斯大林七十寿辰的文章中，谈到斯大林的活动对于全世界进步人类的伟大意义。
