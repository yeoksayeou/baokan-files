Title: Central People's Broadcasting Station Tonight Will Feature Zhu Xuefan Broadcasting

Author:
Date: 1949-12-30
Page: 3

　　Central People's Broadcasting Station Tonight Will Feature Zhu Xuefan Broadcasting
    [This Newspaper News] The Central People's Broadcasting Station tonight (30th) at 17:30 Employee Time: (1) Zhu Xuefan will broadcast: "Guarantee the Realization of the 1950 Financial Budget", (2) Recorded Broadcast: "Circular from the All-China Federation of Trade Unions Regarding the Non-Payment of Membership Fees by Railway Trade Unions at All Levels to Provincial and Municipal Local Trade Unions." 18:30 Natural Science Common Sense Lecture: "Fluorite". 18:00 First Literary Program: Beijing Rhyme Drum "Dong Cunrui Sacrifices Himself to Blow Up the Bunker". 21:00 Second Literary Program: Shanghai Opera: Rural Light Interlude "Wang Gui and Li Xiangxiang", Shandong Kuaishu: "Lu Lian Eliminates the Tyrant".

### 中央人民广播电台今晚由朱学范播讲

1949-12-30
第3版()
专栏：

　　中央人民广播电台今晚由朱学范播讲
    【本报讯】中央人民广播电台今（三十）晚十七点三十分职工时间：（一）朱学范播讲：“保证实现一九五零年财政概算”，（二）记录广播：“中华全国总工会关于各级铁路工会不向各省市地方工会缴纳会费的通知”。十八点三十分自然科学常识讲座：“萤石”。十八点第一次文艺节目：京韵大鼓“董存瑞舍身炸碉堡”。二十一点第二次文艺节目：沪剧：农村之光插曲“王贵与李香香”，山东快书：“鲁连除霸”。
