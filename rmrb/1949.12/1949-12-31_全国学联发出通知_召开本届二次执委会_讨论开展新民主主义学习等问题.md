Title: All-China Students' Federation Issues Notice: Convening the Second Executive Committee Meeting of the Current Session to Discuss Issues Such as Carrying Out New Democracy Study

Author:
Date: 1949-12-31
Page: 3

　　All-China Students' Federation Issues Notice
　  Convening the Second Executive Committee Meeting of the Current Session
    Discussing Issues Such as Carrying Out New Democracy Study

    Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, December 29th: The Presidium of the All-China Students' Federation issued a notice today, scheduling the second meeting of the 14th Executive Committee of the Federation to be held in Beijing on February 20th of next year. The meeting will mainly discuss the work report of the All-China Students' Federation since the 14th National Student Congress, as well as issues concerning the implementation of New Democracy study and the organization of the All-China Students' Federation and student unions. Units attending will include the six major administrative regions of Northeast, Northwest, North China, East China, Central South, and Southwest, as well as the eleven major cities of Beijing, Tianjin, Shanghai, Nanjing, Wuhan, Guangzhou, Shenyang, Chongqing, Chengdu, Kunming, and Xi'an, and the Inner Mongolia Autonomous Region. According to the regulations, each unit will send one executive committee member for every 25,000 students (an additional member will be added if the remainder exceeds 12,500), to be selected by the local student federations. Regions where student federations have not been established will entrust the local people's government education authorities or the local organizations of the Chinese New Democratic Youth League and student representatives or student unions of various schools to negotiate and select representatives. The notice also requires local student federations to prepare materials related to students' ideology and living conditions, as well as materials on the implementation of New Democracy study and statistical materials on the number of secondary schools, the number of students, and student unions and student federations, and send them to the All-China Students' Federation by the end of January next year. Executive committee members attending should register in Beijing before February 17, 1950.

### 全国学联发出通知　召开本届二次执委会  讨论开展新民主主义学习等问题

1949-12-31
第3版()
专栏：

　　全国学联发出通知
　  召开本届二次执委会
    讨论开展新民主主义学习等问题
    【新华社北京二十九日电】中华全国学生联合会主席团于今日发出通知，定于明年二月二十日在北京召开该会第十四届第二次执行委员会。会议将主要讨论十四届全国学生代表大会以来全国学联的工作报告及开展新民主主义学习和关于学联及学生会组织的一些问题。出席的单位为东北、西北、华北、华东、中南、西南等六大行政区，北京、天津、上海、南京、武汉、广州、沈阳、重庆、成都、昆明、西安等十一大城市及内蒙自治区。各单位出席人数按章程规定每二万五千学生选派执委一名（余额在一万二千五百人以上者增加一名），由各地学联自行选派。未成立学联的地区则委托当地人民政府教育机关或中国新民主主义青年团地方组织和各校学生或学生会协商选派。通知并要求各地学联应准备有关学生思想、生活状况以及开展新民主主义学习的材料和中等学校数目、学生人数、学生会、学联的统计材料，于明年一月底寄交全国学联。出席执委应于一九五○年二月十七日以前到北京报到。
