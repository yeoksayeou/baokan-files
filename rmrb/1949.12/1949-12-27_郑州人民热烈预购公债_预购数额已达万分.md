Title: Zhengzhou People Enthusiastically Pre-Purchase Public Bonds; Pre-Purchase Amount Reaches Ten Thousand Shares
Author:
Date: 1949-12-27
Page: 1

　　Zhengzhou People Enthusiastically Pre-Purchase Public Bonds
　　Pre-Purchase Amount Reaches Ten Thousand Shares
    [Report from this newspaper] According to Xinhua News Agency: In Zhengzhou City, Henan Province, the movement of people from all walks of life to pre-purchase public bonds has reached a fever pitch. As of the 18th, the pre-purchase amount had reached ten thousand shares. People from all industries unanimously guarantee that their actual purchases next year will exceed the pre-purchase quota.

### 郑州人民热烈预购公债　预购数额已达万分

1949-12-27
第1版()
专栏：

　　郑州人民热烈预购公债
　  预购数额已达万分
    【本报讯】据新华社电：河南省郑州市各阶层人民预购公债的运动已达到热潮，十八日止预购数额已达到一万分。各业人士一致保证在明年实际购买要超过预购定额。
