Title: Beijing Coal and Iron Branch Company Transports Large Quantities of Timber

Author:
Date: 1949-12-15
Page: 3

　　Beijing Coal and Iron Branch Company Transports Large Quantities of Timber
　　[Beijing News] The Beijing Coal and Iron Branch Company, in order to supply construction materials to the capital, has recently transported a large quantity of red and white pine timber from the Northeast. This timber is mostly four to five meters long and thirty centimeters in diameter, suitable for building houses. More than 2,000 cubic meters have been transported since the 10th of this month and sales have already begun. All sectors are welcome to visit the company to purchase.

### 京煤铁分公司运来大批木材

1949-12-15
第3版()
专栏：

　　京煤铁分公司运来大批木材
    【北京讯】北京煤铁分公司为供应首都建筑原料，近由东北运到大批红、白松木材。该项木材多系四、五米长，直径三十公分，适宜建筑房屋之用。自本月十日起已先后运到二千余立方米，现已开始出售，各界可赴该公司选购。
