Title: Central, Beijing People's Radio Today's Broadcast Schedule
Author:
Date: 1949-12-11
Page: 3

[This Newspaper] News: The Central People's Broadcasting Station tonight (11th) at 17:30, worker's time: Xiao Ming will speak on "Our workers must use the great achievements in production and construction to celebrate the success of the Asian-Australian Trade Union Representative Conference." At 18:30, Natural Science Knowledge Lecture: "China's Railway Engineer Zhan Tianyou." At 18:00, First Art Program: Yin Fulai sings the brand opera "Hundred Birds Paying Homage to the Phoenix." At 21:00, Second Art Program: The Combat Drama Troupe broadcasts the opera "Female Hero Liu Hulan."
[Another News] Beijing People's Radio at 12:30, worker's time: The No. 2 Clothing Factory Choir sings. At 13:00, Local Miscellaneous Music: Wooden Clapper Ballad "Three Sons-in-Law Celebrating Birthday." At 16:15, Children's Time: Sun Jingxiu tells the Soviet fairy tale "Anait." At 16:45, Listener Services: (1) Answer Li Yifeng's questions about several specialized terms in economic news, (2) The address of the newly established coal briquette retail store of the Beijing Coal and Iron Company Branch, (3) The All-China Federation of Trade Unions is soliciting Russian translators, (4) The Ministry of Health is recruiting typists and copyists. At 19:15, Introduction to the happy lives of Soviet peasant women and children.

### 中央、北京人民电台今日广播要目

1949-12-11
第3版()
专栏：

　　中央、北京人民电台今日广播要目
    【本报讯】中央人民广播电台今（十一）晚十七点三十分职工时间：肖明讲“我们工人要用生产建设上的伟大成就来庆祝亚澳工会代表会议的成功”。十八点三十分自然科学常识讲座：“中国的铁道工程师詹天佑”。十八点第一次文艺节目：尹福来演唱牌子戏“百鸟朝凤”。二十一点第二次文艺节目：战斗剧社演播歌剧“女英雄刘胡兰”。
    【又讯】北京人民电台十二点三十分职工时间：被服二厂歌咏队唱歌。十三点地方杂曲：木板大鼓“三女婿拜寿”。十六点十五分儿童时间：孙敬修讲苏联童话故事“阿娜依特”。十六点四十五分听众服务：（一）答李一峰问经济新闻里几个专门名词，（二）北京煤铁公司分公司新设立的煤球零卖店的地址，（三）全总征求俄文翻译启事，（四）卫生部招考打字员和缮写员启事。十九点十五分介绍苏联的农民妇女和儿童的幸福生活。
