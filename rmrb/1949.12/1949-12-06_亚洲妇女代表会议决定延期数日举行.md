Title: Asian Women's Representative Conference Decided to Postpone for Several Days
Author:
Date: 1949-12-06
Page: 1
　　
Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, December 5th: The International Preparatory Committee of the Asian Women's Representative Conference decided at its third meeting on December 5th that the Asian Women's Representative Conference would be postponed for several days because some of the representatives and guests attending the conference were still en route.

### 亚洲妇女代表会议决定延期数日举行

1949-12-06
第1版()
专栏：

　　亚洲妇女代表会议决定延期数日举行
    【新华社北京五日电】亚洲妇女代表会议国际筹备委员会十二月五日第三次会议议决因有一部分出席会议的代表及来宾尚在途中，故亚洲妇女代表会议延缓数日举行。
