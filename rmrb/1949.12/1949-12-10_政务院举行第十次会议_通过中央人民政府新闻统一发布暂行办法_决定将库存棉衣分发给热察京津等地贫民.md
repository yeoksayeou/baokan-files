Title: Government Administration Council Holds Tenth Meeting; Approves Provisional Measures for Unified News Release by the Central People's Government; Decides to Distribute Stockpiled Cotton Clothing to Poor People in Rehe, Chahar, Beijing, Tianjin and Other Areas
Author:
Date: 1949-12-10
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, December 9th. The Government Administration Council of the Central People's Government held its tenth plenary meeting at 3:00 PM today. Deputy Secretary-General Guo Chuntao attended and reported on the work of the Guidance Committee for Reception Work. The first group of personnel from the East China Region Working Group of the committee, led by Group Leader and Vice Premier Dong Biwu, departed for Nanjing on the 8th. The Government Administration Council additionally appointed Nan Hanchen as Deputy Group Leader of the group, who also set off at the same time. The meeting discussed the draft organizational rules for the working bodies of the major administrative regions and approved the provisional measures for the unified release of important news by the Central People's Government and its subordinate organs. The meeting approved the nomination to the Central People's Government Committee to appoint Guo Renzhi as Deputy Secretary-General of the People's Supervision Committee of the Government Administration Council. The meeting decided to distribute over 300,000 sets of old cotton clothes, originally belonging to the enemy and puppets, stockpiled in warehouses in the Beijing-Tianjin area, received by the Logistics Department of the North China Military Region, to the poor people in Rehe, Chahar, Suiyuan, Northwest Shanxi, and the cities of Beijing and Tianjin.

### 政务院举行第十次会议　通过中央人民政府新闻统一发布暂行办法  决定将库存棉衣分发给热察京津等地贫民

1949-12-10
第1版()
专栏：

　　政务院举行第十次会议
　  通过中央人民政府新闻统一发布暂行办法
    决定将库存棉衣分发给热察京津等地贫民
    【新华社北京九日电】中央人民政府政务院今天下午三时举行第十次政务会议。郭春涛副秘书长列席报告了指导接收工作委员会的工作。该委员会华东区工作团第一批人员，已于八日由团长董必武副总理率领前往南京。政务院增派南汉宸为该团副团长，亦同时启程。会议讨论了各大行政区工作机构的组织通则草案，并通过了关于统一发布中央人民政府及其所属各机关重要新闻的暂行办法。会议通过提请中央人民政府委员会任命郭任之为政务院人民监察委员会副秘书长。会议决定将华北军区后勤部接收之京津地区仓库中原存敌伪旧棉衣三十余万套，分发给热河、察哈尔、绥远、晋西北地区和京津两市的贫民。
