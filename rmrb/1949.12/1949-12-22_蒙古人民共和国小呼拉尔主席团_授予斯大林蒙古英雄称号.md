Title: Presidium of the Little Khural of the Mongolian People's Republic Awards Stalin the Title of Hero of Mongolia
Author:
Date: 1949-12-22
Page: 1

　　Xinhua News Agency, Ulan Bator, December 20th: The Presidium of the Little Khural of the Mongolian People's Republic issued an order that, on the occasion of the 70th birthday of the great leader of the working people, Stalin, he be awarded the title of Hero of the Mongolian People's Republic and the Order of the Golden Star of Batol, for his especially great contribution to winning and consolidating the freedom and national independence of the Mongolian people and to the economic and cultural development of the Mongolian People's Republic. Another order will name the Volandator Railway, the Fan Bator Meat Packing Factory, the National Central Library, and twelve border guard detachments after Stalin.
    Letters of congratulation to Stalin on his 70th birthday, signed by working people from all over the Mongolian People's Republic, are being sent daily to Ulan Bator from all over the country.

### 蒙古人民共和国小呼拉尔主席团　授予斯大林蒙古英雄称号

1949-12-22
第1版()
专栏：

　　蒙古人民共和国小呼拉尔主席团
    授予斯大林蒙古英雄称号
    【新华社北京二十一日电】据塔斯社乌兰巴托尔二十日讯：蒙古人民共和国小呼拉尔主席团发布命令，在劳动人民的伟大领袖斯大林七秩寿辰之际，特授以蒙古人民共和国英雄称号及巴托尔金星勋章。因为他在赢得与巩固蒙古人民的自由与国家独立和在蒙古人民共和国的经济与文化发展上，有特别伟大的贡献。另一命令则将伏兰达托尔铁路、凡·巴托尔肉类包装工厂、国家中央图书馆和十二个边防分遣队、冠以斯大林之名。
    蒙古人民共和国全国各地劳动人民签名祝贺斯大林七十寿辰的贺函，正每日从全国各地寄到乌兰巴托尔。
