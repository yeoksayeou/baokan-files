Title: Executive Committee Members of the World Federation of Trade Unions, Representatives of Asian and Australasian Trade Unions Conference, Arrive in Tianjin by Special Train from Beijing Yesterday
Author:
Date: 1949-12-04
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, December 3rd. The Executive Committee members of the World Federation of Trade Unions (WFTU) who came to Beijing to preside over the Asian and Australasian Trade Unions Conference, and the representatives of various countries attending the conference, left Beijing by special train at 3:30 this afternoon. Liu Shaoqi, Honorary Chairman of the All-China Federation of Trade Unions, Li Lisan and Zhu Xuefan, Vice-Chairmen of the All-China Federation of Trade Unions, Wu Yuzhang, Vice-President of the Sino-Soviet Friendship Association, Yan Baohang, representative of Minister of Foreign Affairs of the Central People's Government Zhou Enlai, Nie Rongzhen, Mayor of the Beijing Municipal People's Government, Deng Yingchao and Li Dequan, Vice-Chairwomen of the All-China Democratic Women's Federation, and more than 500 representatives of various people's organizations, went to the station to see them off. Louis Saillant, General Secretary of the WFTU, and the representatives of various countries embraced and shook hands with the well-wishers, expressing their gratitude for the hospitality extended to them by New China. Some of the representatives of various countries will visit Tianjin, Shanghai and other places, accompanied by Liu Ningyi, Vice-Chairman of the All-China Federation of Trade Unions.

Xinhua News Agency, Tianjin, December 3rd. Louis Saillant, General Secretary of the World Federation of Trade Unions, and the delegations from the Soviet Union, Mongolia, India, Indonesia, North Korea, Vietnam and other countries attending the Asian and Australasian Trade Unions Conference, accompanied by Liu Ningyi, Vice-Chairman of the All-China Federation of Trade Unions, arrived in Tianjin from Beijing at 6:10 this afternoon. Huang Jing, Mayor of Tianjin, Liu Xiufeng, Deputy Mayor, Huang Huoqing, Director of the Preparatory Committee of the Tianjin Federation of Trade Unions, Li Tianhuan, Political Commissar of the Corps of the People's Liberation Army, and more than 800 representatives from various agencies and workers in Tianjin went to the station to welcome them. After Saillant and others got off the train, the trade union representatives presented flowers to Saillant and others, and presented folk art works, commemorative albums, carpets and other gifts. At 6:45, Saillant and other members of the Executive Committee of the WFTU and the delegations from the Soviet Union and Iran boarded the train to the Northeast, while the delegations from Mongolia, North Korea, India, Indonesia, Thailand, Burma and other countries were accompanied by Mayor Huang Jing to tour the city. In the evening, the municipal government also hosted a banquet for the delegations.

### 世界工会联合会执行局委员亚澳工会代表会议各国代表  昨专车离京抵津

1949-12-04
第1版()
专栏：

　　世界工会联合会执行局委员亚澳工会代表会议各国代表
    昨专车离京抵津
    【新华社北京三日电】来京主持亚澳工会代表会议的世界工联执行局委员及参加会议的各国代表，已于今日下午三时半乘专车离开北京。中华全国总工会名誉主席刘少奇、中华全国总工会副主席李立三、朱学范，中苏友好协会副会长吴玉章，中央人民政府外交部周恩来部长代表阎宝航，北京市人民政府市长聂荣臻，中华全国民主妇女联合会副主席邓颖超、李德全等，以及各人民团体代表五百余人，赴车站欢送。世界工联总书记赛扬与各国代表依依不舍地和欢送者拥抱、握手，一致表示感谢新中国给予他们的招待。各国代表中的一部分将赴天津、上海等地参观，由中华全国总工会副主席刘宁一陪同前往。
    【新华社天津三日电】世界工会联合会总书记路易·赛扬等及出席亚澳工会代表会议的苏联、蒙古、印度、印尼、朝鲜、越南等国代表团一行，今日下午六时十分由中华全国总工会副主席刘宁一陪同自京抵津。天津市长黄敬、副市长刘秀峰、天津市总工会筹委会主任黄火青、解放军兵团政治委员李天焕及津市各机关、职工代表八百多人至车站欢迎。赛扬等下车后，即由工会代表向赛扬等献花，并赠送民间艺术作品、纪念册、地毯等礼物。六时四十五分，赛扬等世界工联执行局委员及苏联、伊朗代表团登车赴东北，蒙古、朝鲜、印度、印尼、泰国、缅甸等国代表团则由黄敬市长陪同周游全市。晚间市府并设宴招待各代表团。
