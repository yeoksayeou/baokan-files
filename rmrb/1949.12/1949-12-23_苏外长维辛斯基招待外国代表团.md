Title: Soviet Foreign Minister Vyshinsky Hosts Foreign Delegations
Author:
Date: 1949-12-23
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, December 22nd: TASS Moscow, December 22nd: Soviet Foreign Minister Vyshinsky hosted foreign delegations on December 21st in celebration of the 70th birthday of the Chairman of the Council of Ministers of the USSR. The heads and personnel of foreign delegations in Moscow attended the reception: Kim Tu-bong, Chairman of the Standing Committee of the Supreme People's Assembly of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea; Kim Tal-hyon, Vice-Chairman of the Supreme People's Assembly of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea; Koço Theodhosi, Vice-Chairman of the Council of Ministers of the People's Republic of Albania; Mátyás Rákosi, Vice-Chairman of the Council of Ministers of the People's Republic of Hungary; Yumjaagiin Tsedenbal and Damdin Sükhbaatar, Deputy Prime Ministers of the Mongolian People's Republic; Viliam Široký, Deputy Prime Minister of the Czechoslovak Republic; Walter Ulbricht, Deputy Prime Minister of the German Democratic Republic; Georg Dertinger, Foreign Minister of the German Democratic Republic; Georgi Ganev, Minister of Foreign Trade of the People's Republic of Bulgaria; Jenő Gergely, Under-Secretary of State of the Ministry of Foreign Trade of the People's Republic of Hungary; Yi Seung-yeop, Minister of Justice of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea; Hilary Minc, Minister of Foreign Trade of the Polish Republic; Grigore Preoteasa, Minister of Foreign and Commercial Trade of the Romanian People's Republic; Ludvík Svoboda, Minister of National Defence of the Czechoslovak Republic; Antonín Gregor, Minister of Foreign Trade of the Czechoslovak Republic; Elisabeth Wandel, Minister of Education of the German Democratic Republic; Luitpold Steidle, Minister of Labor and Health of the German Democratic Republic; Hans Loch, Minister of Finance of the German Democratic Republic; Uuno Takki, Minister of Trade and Industry of Finland; István Varga, Chairman of the National Planning Bureau of the People's Republic of Hungary; Yrjö Murto and Hertta Kuusinen, members of the Parliament of Finland; Franz Korinek, member of the Parliament of Austria, and members of foreign delegations who came to Moscow to celebrate Stalin's 70th birthday.
The Soviet side was represented by: V.V. Kuznetsov, Vice-Chairman of the Presidium of the Supreme Soviet of the USSR; Mikhail Menshikov, Minister of Foreign Trade of the USSR; Sergei Kaftanov, Minister of Higher Education of the USSR; Arseny Zverev, Minister of Finance of the USSR; Boris Beshchev, Minister of Railways of the USSR; Yefim Smirnov, Minister of Public Health of the USSR; Konstantin Gorshenin, Minister of Justice of the USSR; Marshal Govorov of the Soviet Union; Lieutenant General of the Air Force Zhevolonkov; Deputy Ministers of Foreign Affairs of the USSR Gromyko, Zorin, and Lavrentiev; Deputy Ministers of Foreign Trade of the USSR Kumykin, Borisov, Kuzmin, Loshakov, Yeremin, Serdechik; General of the Army Zakharov; Chairman of the All-Union Central Council of Trade Unions Kuznetsov; President of the Academy of Sciences of the USSR Vavilov; Lieutenant General Zhigarev, Artemyev, Academician Grekov; senior officials of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs, the Ministry of Foreign Trade, and the Ministry of Armed Forces of the USSR, scientists, writers, artists, actors, and representatives of the Soviet and foreign press.

### 苏外长维辛斯基招待外国代表团

1949-12-23
第1版()
专栏：

　　苏外长维辛斯基招待外国代表团
    【新华社北京二十二日电】塔斯社莫斯科二十二日电：苏联外交部长维辛斯基于十二月二十一日为庆祝苏联部长会议主席七十寿辰，特招待外国代表团。出席招待会的有莫斯科外国代表团的首长与人员：朝鲜民主主义人民共和国最高人民会议常任委员会委员长金枓奉、朝鲜民主主义人民共和国最高人民会议副议长金达玹、阿尔巴尼亚人民共和国部长会议副主席柯勒卡、匈牙利人民共和国部长会议副主席拉科西、蒙古人民共和国副总理泽登巴尔与梅达尔、捷克斯洛伐克共和国副总理西罗基、德意志民主共和国副总理乌布利希、德意志民主共和国外交部长窦丁格尔、保加利亚人民共和国对外贸易部长加涅夫、匈牙利人民共和国对外贸易部次长加伊朝鲜民主主义人民共和国司法部长李承烨、波兰共和国对外贸易部长基德、罗马尼亚人民共和国外交贸易部长巴利迪亚努、捷克斯洛伐克共和国国防部长斯沃波达、捷克斯洛伐克共和国对外贸易部长格里哥尔、德意志民主共和国教育部长汪戴尔、德意志民主共和国劳工与卫生部长斯泰格尔、德意志民主共和国财政部长洛赫、芬兰贸易与工业部长达基、匈牙利人民共和国计划局主席伐尔丹、芬兰国会议员贝西与库西宁夫人、奥地利议员柯伦尼及为庆贺斯大林七十寿辰来莫斯科的外国代表团团员。
    苏联方面出席的有：苏联最高苏维埃主席团副主席伏拉索夫，苏联对外贸易部部长孟希科夫，苏联高等教育部部长卡夫坦诺夫，苏联财政部长兹维列夫，苏联铁道部长别什切夫，苏联公共卫生部长斯米尔诺夫，苏联司法部长高尔谢宁，苏联元帅戈沃罗夫，空军中将热沃隆科夫，苏联外交部副部长葛罗米柯，佐林与拉夫伦捷夫，苏联对外贸易副部长库米金、鲍里邵夫、库兹明、洛萨科夫、叶列明、塞尔杰契克、陆军上将查哈罗夫，全苏工会中央理事会主席库兹涅佐夫，苏联科学院院长瓦维洛夫，陆军准上将日加列夫、阿尔捷米也夫、科学院院士格列科夫，苏联外交部对外贸易部与武装部队部高级官员、科学家、作家、艺术家、演员及苏联与外国新闻界代表。
