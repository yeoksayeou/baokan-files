Title: Statement by Foreign Minister Hoang of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam: Defend the Northern Border of Vietnam; Condemn the Kuomintang Bandits' Collusion with French Imperialists
Author:
Date: 1949-12-18
Page: 2

Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, December 17th: Vietnam News Agency, North Vietnam, December 16th: Vietnamese Foreign Minister Hoang Minh Giam recently issued a statement on defending the northern border region of Vietnam as follows: "As the Chinese war develops southward towards the northern border of Vietnam, there are now signs that Chinese Kuomintang troops, with the help of French imperialists, are attempting to flee into northern Vietnam. These two reactionary factions may take joint military action against Vietnam. Therefore, the Vietnamese Ministry of Foreign Affairs announces:
	1. The Government of Resistance of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam solemnly protests and condemns to the world the collusion between the Chinese Kuomintang clique and the French imperialists, whose purpose is to enable the defeated Kuomintang army to retreat safely into Vietnam.
	2. In accordance with international law, the Vietnamese army has been ordered to carry out its task of defending the nation's territory, independence, and the interests of the Vietnamese people."

### 越南民主共和国黄外长声明　保卫越南北部边境  斥责国民党匪帮与法帝狼狈为奸

1949-12-18
第2版()
专栏：

　　越南民主共和国黄外长声明
  　保卫越南北部边境
    斥责国民党匪帮与法帝狼狈为奸
    【新华社北京十七日电】越南通讯社北越十六日讯：越南外交部长黄明基顷发表关于保卫越南北部边境地区的声明如下：“随着中国战事的朝着越南北部边境向南发展，现在已有迹象表明：中国国民党部队在法国帝国主义的帮助之下企图逃入越南北部，这两帮反动分子可能对越南采取联合的军事行动。因此，越南外交部宣布：
    一、越南民主共和国抵抗政府向全世界着重抗议和斥责中国国民党集团与法国帝国主义者之间的狼狈为奸，他们的目的是要使失败的国民党军队能够安全退入越南。
    二、根据国际法，越南军队已奉令执行其保卫国家的领土、独立和越南人民的利益的任务”。
