Title: Grand Celebration in Tianjin for Stalin's Birthday; Sino-Soviet Friendship Association Branch Established
Author:
Date: 1949-12-21
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Tianjin, 20th—The Tianjin Sino-Soviet Friendship Association inaugural meeting and the celebration of Stalin's 70th birthday were jointly held today at the China Grand Theater. Attending the meeting were more than 2,000 representatives from Tianjin’s various agencies, organizations, military units, factories, schools, branches of the Sino-Soviet Friendship Association in various districts, and Soviet expatriates. Ding Ling, a representative of the Sino-Soviet Friendship Association headquarters, also attended. The meeting began at approximately 2:00 PM. Huang Jing, Chairman of the Preparatory Committee of the Tianjin Branch of the Sino-Soviet Friendship Association, delivered the opening speech. After briefly explaining the Soviet Union's great assistance to the Chinese revolution over the past thirty years, he especially emphasized that the great teacher of the world proletariat, Stalin, provided extremely practical and concrete assistance to the Chinese people. Because of him, we have today’s victory, tomorrow’s hope, and a bright future! We celebrate his 70th birthday not only to thank him for his valuable assistance but also to continue learning from him, learning his revolutionary experience and revolutionary theory. Following this, Ding Ling, the representative of the headquarters, delivered a speech, congratulating the establishment of the Tianjin Branch of the Sino-Soviet Friendship Association. The Acting Consul General of the Soviet Union in Tianjin and the representative of the Soviet Union’s Foreign Cultural Association in Tianjin, Gilford, said in his speech: The Soviet people have always held an eternal and sincere friendship for the great Chinese people, a noble friendship cultivated by Lenin and Stalin. Stalin is the best friend of the Chinese people! After speeches by Li Zhuchen, Zhang Guofan, and others, children from the Tianjin Nursery presented peaches of longevity to Marshal Stalin, and representatives of the Tianjin New Democratic Youth League members and Young Pioneers presented flowers and messages to Marshal Stalin, which were accepted by Gilford on behalf of Stalin. The meeting then approved the organizational charter of the Tianjin Branch of the Sino-Soviet Friendship Association, elected Huang Jing as the chairman, Huang Songling, Huang Huoqing, Li Zhuchen, and fourteen others as vice-chairmen, and elected 110 directors. The meeting concluded amidst cheers of "Long live Marshal Stalin!" and "Long live the friendship and cooperation between the Chinese and Soviet peoples!"

### 天津盛会祝寿　中苏友协分会成立

1949-12-21
第4版()
专栏：

　　天津盛会祝寿
    中苏友协分会成立
    【新华社天津二十日电】天津市中苏友好协会成立大会和庆祝斯大林七十寿辰大会，于今日在中国大戏院合并举行。参加大会的有津市各机关、团体、部队、工厂、学校、各区中苏友好协会支会代表和苏联侨民共两千余人。中苏友好协会总会代表丁玲也出席参加。大会在下午二时许开始。首由中苏友好协会天津分会筹委会主任委员黄敬致开幕词。他在扼要说明了苏联三十年来对中国革命的伟大援助后，特别提出：世界无产阶级伟大的导师斯大林对中国人民的援助是极其现实具体的。有了他，我们才有了今天的胜利、明天的希望以及美好的将来！我们庆祝他的七十寿辰，不仅要感谢他对我们的宝贵援助，而且要继续向他学习，学习他的革命经验和革命理论。继由总会代表丁玲致词，她对中苏友好协会天津分会的成立表示庆贺。苏联驻津代理总领事及苏联对外文化协会驻津代表吉尔福在讲话中说：苏联人民对伟大的中国人民始终抱有永恒真挚的友情，这种崇高的友情，是列宁、斯大林培养起来的。斯大林是中国人民最好的朋友！在李烛尘、张国藩等相继讲话后，由津市保育院儿童向斯大林大元帅献寿桃及津市新民主主义青年团团员、少年先锋队队员代表向斯大林大元帅献花、献词，当由吉尔福代表接受。大会继通过中苏友好协会天津分会组织章程，选出黄敬为正会长，黄松龄、黄火青、李烛尘等十四人为副会长，并选出理事一百一十人。至此大会即在“斯大林大元帅万寿无疆！”、“中苏人民友好合作万岁！”等欢呼声中结束。
