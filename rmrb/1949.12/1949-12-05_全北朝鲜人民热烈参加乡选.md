Title: The Entire North Korean People Enthusiastically Participate in Township Elections
Author:
Date: 1949-12-05
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Pyongyang, December 4th: According to a report in the Democratic Korea newspaper on the 2nd, the people of the entire North Korea enthusiastically participated in the elections for the North Korean Township People's Committees (note: Korean township governments combine legislative and executive functions, township councilors are also township government committee members) on November 25th. In all 10,317 townships in North Korea, 99.99 percent of registered voters participated in the voting, electing a total of 56,113 township councilors. The composition of the elected township councilors is as follows: 2,780 workers, 51,471 farmers, 1,428 staff members, 176 merchants, 72 capitalists and handicraftsmen, 33 religious believers, and 153 others, including 8,494 women.

### 全北朝鲜人民热烈参加乡选

1949-12-05
第4版()
专栏：

　　全北朝鲜人民热烈参加乡选
    【新华社平壤四日电】据民主朝鲜报二日报道：整个朝鲜北部的人民热烈地参加了十一月二十五日北鲜乡人民委员会（按：朝鲜乡政府是议行合一，乡议员即乡政府委员。）的选举。在整个朝鲜北部一万零三百十七个乡中，登记的选民参加投票者，占总数的百分之九十九点九九，共选出乡议员五万六千一百十三名。当选乡议员的成分如下：工人二千七百八十名，农民五万一千四百七十一名，职员一千四百二十八名，商人一百七十六名，资本家和手工业者七十二名，教徒三十三名，其他一百五十三名，其中妇女八千四百九十四名。
