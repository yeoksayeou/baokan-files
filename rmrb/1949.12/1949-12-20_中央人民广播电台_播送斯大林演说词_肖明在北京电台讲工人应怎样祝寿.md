Title: Central People's Broadcasting Station Broadcasts Stalin's Speech, Xiao Ming Speaks on Beijing Radio on How Workers Should Celebrate His Birthday
Author:
Date: 1949-12-20
Page: 2

Central People's Broadcasting Station
　Broadcasts Stalin's Speech
    Xiao Ming Speaks on Beijing Radio on How Workers Should Celebrate His Birthday
    [Reported by this newspaper] The Central People's Broadcasting Station tonight (20th) at 17:30 during the staff and workers' hour: will broadcast a section of Stalin's speech at the conference of economic workers on June 23, 1931, "New Situation and New Tasks of Economic Construction." At 18:30, Natural Science Common Knowledge Lecture: "Soviet Science." At 18:00, the first literary program: continuing to teach the song "Song of Stalin." At 21:00, the second literary program: broadcasting songs such as the China-Soviet Friendship Medley. At 18:45 and 19:45, Soviet songs will be broadcast.
    [Further News] Beijing People's Radio at 12:30 during the staff and workers' hour: Xiao Ming will speak on "How We Beijing Workers Should Celebrate Marshal Stalin's Birthday." At 16:15 during children's hour: speeches and songs by the children of Dongbajiao Elementary School in the Eighth District. Dong Yumin will read aloud a letter of congratulations dedicated to Marshal Stalin. At 16:45 during women's hour: Yang Zaoxin will speak on "Gains and Thoughts from Participating in the Asian Women's Conference." At 17:00, teach singing: "Ode to Stalin." At 19:15, Voice of the Municipality: (1) Common knowledge for citizens on preventing carbon monoxide poisoning in winter, (2) Notice from the Tax Bureau regarding the entrusted collection points for the first half of this year's profit-seeking enterprise income tax and summer business tax.

### 中央人民广播电台　播送斯大林演说词  肖明在北京电台讲工人应怎样祝寿

1949-12-20
第2版()
专栏：

　　中央人民广播电台
  　播送斯大林演说词
    肖明在北京电台讲工人应怎样祝寿
    【本报讯】中央人民广播电台今（二十）晚十七点三十分职工时间：播送斯大林大元帅一九三一年六月二十三日在经济工作人员会议上的演说词“新的环境和新的经济建设任务”中的一节。十八点三十分自然科学常识讲座：“苏联的科学”。十八点第一次文艺节目：继续教唱“斯大林之歌”。二十一点第二次文艺节目：播送中苏友谊小唱等歌曲。十八点四十五分和十九点四十五分播送苏联歌曲。
    【又讯】北京人民电台十二点三十分职工时间：肖明讲“我们北京工人应该怎样庆祝斯大林大元帅寿辰。十六点十五分儿童时间：八区东八角小学小朋友们讲话和唱歌。董玉敏朗读一封献给斯大林大元帅的祝寿信。十六点四十五分妇女时间：杨造新讲“参加亚洲妇代会的收获和感想”。十七点教唱：“斯大林颂”。十九点十五分市政之声：（１）市民冬季预防煤气中毒的常识，（２）税务局所委托的本年上半年营利事业所得税和夏季营业税税款代收处的通知。
