Title: Chairman Mao Zedong's Speech Receives Enthusiastic Welcome from the Audience
Author:
Date: 1949-12-23
Page: 1

　　Chairman Mao Zedong's Speech Receives Enthusiastic Welcome from the Audience
    The audience enthusiastically welcomed Chairman Mao Zedong of the Central People's Government of the People's Republic of China. Mao Zedong said: At this grand gathering, I would like to, on behalf of the Chinese people and the Chinese Communist Party, celebrate Comrade Stalin's 70th birthday, wish him health and longevity, wish our great ally the Soviet Union happiness and prosperity under the leadership of Comrade Stalin, and hail the unprecedented great unity of the world working class under the leadership of Comrade Stalin. Long live the leader of the world working class and the international communist movement—the great Stalin! Long live the Soviet Union, the fortress of world peace and democracy!
    The next speaker was Yuzvik, the representative of the working people of the Republic of Poland.

### 毛泽东主席致词受听众热烈欢迎

1949-12-23
第1版()
专栏：

　　毛泽东主席致词受听众热烈欢迎
    听众热烈欢迎中华人民共和国中央人民政府主席毛泽东。毛泽东说：在这个盛会上，我谨以中国人民和中国共产党的名义庆祝斯大林同志的七十大寿，祝福他的健康与长寿，祝福我们伟大的友邦苏联在斯大林同志领导下幸福与强盛，并欢呼世界工人阶级在斯大林同志领导下的空前大团结。全世界工人阶级和国际共产主义运动的领袖——伟大的斯大林万岁！世界和平与民主的堡垒苏联万岁！
    继之发言的是波兰共和国劳动人民的代表尤兹维克。
