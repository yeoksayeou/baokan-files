Title: Raising a Glass High
Author: Yan Chen
Date: 1949-12-21
Page: 8

　　Raising a Glass High
    Yan Chen
  　　　　　I
　
    Raising a glass high,
    Long live, long live, we all offer birthday wishes;
    Men, women, old and young, smiling,
    War peddlers tremble.
　
    Raising a glass high,
    Hurray, hurray, our hearts bloom;
    Stalin leads the way,
    The democratic ranks are overwhelming.
    　　　　II
    Thousands of fires, tens of thousands of fires,
    None as warm as Stalin's heart;
    Ice and snow melt into long flowing water,
    Barren branches bloom.
　
    Thousands of lamps, tens of thousands of lamps,
    None as bright as Stalin's heart;
    Shining so the bad guys have nowhere to escape,
    Shining so the suffering people have turned themselves over.
　
　　　　　　　III
　
    Thousands of stars in the sky,
    Not as many as the roads of the world;
    No matter how high the mountains or how far the road,
    Every road leads to Moscow.
　
    The sun in the sky is red and bright,
    Even redder than the hearts of ten thousand people;
    A magnet attracting iron is hard to separate,
    Everyone's heart is towards Stalin!

### 高高举起一杯酒
严辰
1949-12-21
第8版()
专栏：

　　高高举起一杯酒
    严辰
  　　　　　一
　
    高高举起一杯酒，
    万岁万岁齐祝寿；
    男女老少眯眯笑，
    战争贩子索索抖。
　
    高高举起酒一杯，
    呜啦呜啦心花开；
    斯大林前头来引路，
    民主队伍排山倒海。
    　　　　二
    千把火，万把火，
    那有斯大林心暖和；
    冰雪化成长流水，
    枯树枝头开花朵。
　
    千盏灯，万盏灯，
    那有斯大林心里明；
    照得坏蛋没处逃，
    照得受苦人翻了身。
　
　　　　　　　三
　
    天上星星千颗，
    比不上人间大路多；
    那怕山高路又远，
    条条通到莫斯科。
　
    天上太阳红喷喷，
    再红比不上万人心；
    磁石吸铁难分离，
    人人心向斯大林！
