Title: Guarantee the Correct Implementation of Tax Policies: Beijing Establishes a Unified Collection Review Committee; Vice Mayor Zhang Youyu Appointed Chairman

Author: 
Date: 1949-12-08
Page: 2

　　Guarantee the Correct Implementation of Tax Policies
　　Beijing Establishes a Unified Collection Review Committee
　　Vice Mayor Zhang Youyu Appointed Chairman

　　[Beijing News] Beijing has now uniformly commenced the collection of income tax on profit-making enterprises and the summer business tax for the first half of this year. To ensure the smooth completion of the collection work and the correct implementation of tax policies, the Municipal Government’s political affairs meeting on the 6th passed a resolution to establish a Unified Collection Review Committee for the two taxes, with Zhang Youyu, Cheng Hongyi, Mou Zexian, Zhao Zishang, Wang Feiran, Han Zhaoe, and Yan Jingqing as members, and Vice Mayor Zhang Youyu as Chairman.

### 保证正确贯彻税收政策　京成立统一征收复核委会  张友渔副市长任主任委员

1949-12-08
第2版()
专栏：

　　保证正确贯彻税收政策
  　京成立统一征收复核委会
    张友渔副市长任主任委员
    【北京讯】北京市本年上半年度营利事业所得税与夏季营业税刻已统一开始征收。为使征收工作顺利完成，正确贯彻税收政策，六日在市府政务会议上，通过成立两税统一征收复核委员会，由张友渔、程宏毅、牟泽衔、赵子尚、王斐然、韩兆鹗、严镜清等七人为委员，并由张友渔副市长任主任委员。
