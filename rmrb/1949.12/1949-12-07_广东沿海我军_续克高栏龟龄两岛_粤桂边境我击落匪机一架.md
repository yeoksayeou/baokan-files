Title: Our Army in Coastal Guangdong Continues to Capture Gaolan and Guiling Islands; Our Army Shoots Down Bandit Plane on Guangdong-Guangxi Border
Author:
Date: 1949-12-07
Page: 1

　　Guangdong Coastal Our Army
  　Continues to Capture Gaolan and Guiling Islands
  　Our Army Shoots Down Bandit Plane on Guangdong-Guangxi Border
    Xinhua News Agency, Guangzhou, 6th: Delayed news: The People's Liberation Army's Liangguang Column liberated Gaolan Island, which belongs to Zhongshan County, Guangdong, on the 21st of last month. The bandits guarding the island fled in disarray. Since entering the Pearl River Delta to fight, the Liangguang Column, with the support of the local people, has successively liberated strategic locations outside the Pearl River Estuary such as Sanzao Island, Dahengqin Island, Xiaohengqin Island, Dachan Island, and Qianshan Wanzi Island. In the operations to liberate the above-mentioned areas, a total of 561 officers and soldiers including Xu Da, a major general and senior staff officer of the 21st Bandit Corps, were captured, and 25 various artillery pieces, 75 light and heavy machine guns, and 757 rifles and carbines were seized.
    Xinhua News Agency, Guangzhou, 6th: The People's Liberation Army liberated Guiling Island on November 29th, which is located ten miles from the coast of Shanwei in Haifeng County. The island was a long-term lair for pirates and bandits, and the Japanese imperialists used it as one of their naval bases when they occupied our coastal areas. In the operation to liberate the island, a certain unit of the People's Liberation Army killed and wounded more than ten bandits, captured 153 bandits including the bandit leader Huang Zhong, and seized one 60mm mortar, four light and heavy machine guns, three carbines, more than 80 rifles, and other supplies.
    Xinhua News Agency, South China Front, 6th: On the 28th of last month, a certain unit of the People's Liberation Army on the Guangdong-Guangxi border shot down a Kuomintang bandit army bomber in Baishi Market, west of Xinyi.

### 广东沿海我军　续克高栏龟龄两岛　粤桂边境我击落匪机一架

1949-12-07
第1版()
专栏：

　　广东沿海我军
  　续克高栏龟龄两岛
  　粤桂边境我击落匪机一架
    【新华社广州六日电】迟到消息：人民解放军两广纵队于上月二十一日解放广东中山县属之高栏岛，守匪狼狈逃窜。两广纵队自进入珠江三角洲作战以来，在当地人民支援下先后解放珠江口外的三灶岛、大小横琴岛、大铲岛、前山湾仔岛等战略要地。在解放以上诸地的作战中，共俘匪二十一兵团部少将高级参谋徐达等以下官兵五百六十一名，缴获各种炮二十五门，轻重机枪七十五挺，长短枪七五七支。
    【新华社广州六日电】人民解放军于十一月二十九日解放海丰县距汕尾海岸十里的龟龄岛。该岛为海盗、土匪长期盘据的巢穴，日本帝国主义侵占我沿海地区时曾以此为海军基地之一。人民解放军某部于解放该岛作战中，计毙伤匪十余名，俘匪首黄忠以下一百五十三名，缴获六零炮一门，轻重机枪四挺，卡宾枪三支，步枪八十余支及其他物资一部。
    【新华社华南前线六日电】粤桂边境人民解放军某部于上月二十八日在信宜以西之白石墟击落国民党匪军轰炸机一架。
