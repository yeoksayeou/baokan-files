Title: Jinghan Railway, Anyang-Shijiazhuang Section: Xingtai-Handan Section Successfully Connected
Author:
Date: 1949-12-10
Page: 2

The Xingtai-Handan section of the Shijiazhuang-Anyang section of the Jinghan Railway was successfully connected at 4:40 PM on the 3rd of this month at kilometer marker 416 and 150 meters north of Handan (between Dadilian and Linmingguan) thanks to the active repairs undertaken by the 28th Line Brigade of the 4th Detachment of the Railway Corps. Since the 28th Line Brigade began its southward repairs from Xingtai on the 25th of last month, all commanders and fighters have displayed a high level of labor enthusiasm, creating a new record of repairing 5 kilometers and 330 meters in one day, and the quality is very good.

### 京汉铁路安阳石家庄间　刑邯段胜利接轨

1949-12-10
第2版()
专栏：

　　京汉铁路安阳石家庄间
  　刑邯段胜利接轨
    【本报讯】京汉路石（家庄）安（阳）段，在铁道兵团四支队二十八线路大队积极抢修下，刑台、邯郸段，已于本月三日下午四时四十分，在邯郸北四百十六公里一百五十公尺处（褡裢、临洺关间）胜利接轨。二十八线路大队自上月二十五日从刑台向南抢修时，全体指战员发挥了高度的劳动热情，创造了一天抢修五公里三百三十公尺的新纪录，而且质量很好。
