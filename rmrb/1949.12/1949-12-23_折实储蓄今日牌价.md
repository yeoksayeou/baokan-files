Title: Real Savings Today's Exchange Rate
Author:
Date: 1949-12-23
Page: 3

　　Real Savings Today's Exchange Rate
　　The Beijing Branch Savings Department of the People's Bank of China announces that the standard real unit exchange rate for real savings today (23rd) is 3,305.82 yuan.
