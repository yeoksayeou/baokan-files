Spring Couplets Collection (II)
　　Never forget the Communist Party's arduous struggle, the revolution has triumphed!
　　Thank Chairman Mao for his wise leadership, the people have stood up!
　　Welcoming Victory
　　Defending Peace
　　Sharing democratic blessings on New Year's Eve
　　Singing songs of freedom together in the New Year
　　Work hard to build a new China
　　Thoroughly transform the old style
　　Work diligently
　　Celebrate the New Year joyfully

### 春联选辑（二）

1949-12-27
第6版()
专栏：

　　春联选辑（二）
    不忘共产党艰苦斗争，革命胜利！
    感谢毛主席英明领导，人民翻身！
　  迎接胜利
    保卫和平
　  除夕共享民主福
    新年合唱自由歌
　  努力建设新中国
    彻底改造旧作风
    勤勤恳恳做事
    欢欢喜喜过年
