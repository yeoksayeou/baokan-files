　　"New China Women's Monthly" Issue No. 6 Published Today
　　[From Our Newspaper] The sixth issue of "New China Women" focuses on childcare work, introducing some excellent childcare work experiences, and is a valuable reference material for every childcare worker. It includes a reprint of the editorial "Glory Belongs to Motherhood" from the "Soviet Women" magazine and the guiding article "Several Ways to Improve Nursery Work" written by the Women and Children's Welfare Department of the All-China Democratic Women's Federation, as well as the valuable experience introduction from the training class students of the All-China Democratic Women's Federation in their nursery practice: "We Improved Ourselves During the Internship," etc. It is scheduled for publication today.

### “新中国妇女月刊”第六期今日出版

1949-12-20
第4版()
专栏：

　　“新中国妇女月刊”第六期今日出版
    【本报讯】“新中国妇女”第六期是以儿童保育工作为中心，介绍了一些很好的托儿工作经验，是每一个儿童保育工作者宝贵的参考资料。其中有转载“苏联妇女”杂志上“光荣属于母性”的社论及全国民主妇联妇女儿童福利部所写的带有指导性的“改进托儿所工作的几种方法”的文章，有全国民主妇联开办的训练班学员在托儿所实习中的珍贵经验介绍：“我们在实习中提高了自己”等。定于今日出版。
