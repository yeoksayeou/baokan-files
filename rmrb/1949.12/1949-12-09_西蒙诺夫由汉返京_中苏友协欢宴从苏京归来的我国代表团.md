Title: Simonov Returns to Beijing from Hankou; Sino-Soviet Friendship Association Hosts Banquet for Chinese Delegation Returning from Soviet Capital

Author:

Date: 1949-12-09
Page: 1

　　Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, 8th. Simonov, the deputy head of the Soviet cultural, artistic, and scientific workers delegation and famous writer, who was invited to visit our country two months ago, returned to Beijing from Hankou at 9:00 AM today. Liu Ningyi, Yan Baohang, Xiao San, Xu Guangping, Lai Yali, representatives of the Sino-Soviet Friendship Association, and Xie Liejin, the Soviet Consul General in Beijing, and other people, more than a hundred, went to the station to welcome him.
　　Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, 8th. The Secretariat of the Sino-Soviet Friendship Association held a banquet last night at 7:00 PM to welcome the Chinese delegation returning from Soviet capital after attending the celebration of the 32nd anniversary of the October Socialist Revolution in the Soviet Union. Ding Ling, the head of the delegation, Xu Zhizhen, Wu, the deputy heads, Sha Kefu, the secretary, Cao Yu, the deputy secretary-general, and members Ding Xielin, Li Fenglian, Xu Guangping, and Zhao Shuli all attended.

### 西蒙诺夫由汉返京　中苏友协欢宴从苏京归来的我国代表团

1949-12-09
第1版()
专栏：

　　西蒙诺夫由汉返京
  　中苏友协欢宴从苏京归来的我国代表团
    【新华社北京八日电】两月前应邀来我国访问的苏联文化艺术科学工作者代表团副团长、名作家西蒙诺夫于今日上午九时自汉口返抵北京。中苏友好协会总会代表刘宁一、阎宝航、萧三、许广平、赖亚力及苏联驻北京总领事谢列金等百余人曾赴车站欢迎。
    【新华社北京八日电】中苏友好协会总会干事会昨晚七时欢宴前往苏京参加苏联十月社会主义革命三十二周年纪念盛典归来的我国代表团。代表团团长丁玲，副团长许之桢、吴，秘书长沙可夫，副秘书长曹禺，团员丁燮林、李凤莲、许广平、赵树理等均参加。
