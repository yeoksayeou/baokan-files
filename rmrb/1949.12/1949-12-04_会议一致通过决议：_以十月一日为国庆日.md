Title: Meeting Unanimously Passes Resolution: October 1st to be National Day
Author: 
Date: 1949-12-04
Page: 1

　　Meeting Unanimously Passes Resolution:
    October 1st to be National Day
    The final item on the agenda was the adoption of a resolution on National Day. The meeting unanimously resolved that October 1st of each year shall be the National Day of the People's Republic of China.
    The following people attended yesterday's meeting: Mao Zedong, Zhu De, Liu Shaoqi, Li Jishen, Zhang Lan, Lin Boqu, He Xiangning, Wu Yuzhang, Peng Zhen, Bo Yibo, Nie Rongzhen, Zhou Enlai, Dong Biwu, Rao Shushi, Deng Zihui, Ulanhu, Xu Teli, Liu Geping, Ma Yinchu, Chen Yun, Ma Xulun, Guo Moruo, Shen Junru, Shen Yanbing, Chen Shutong, Situ Meitang, Li Xijiu, Huang Yanpei, Cai Tingkai, Peng Zemin, Fu Zuoyi, Li Zhuchen, Zhang Bojun, Zhang Xiruo, Chen Mingshu, Tan Pingshan, Zhang Nanxian, Liu Yazi, Zhang Dongsun, and 39 others. Invited to attend as observers were Li Fuchun, Vice Chairman of the Northeast People's Government, and members of the National Committee of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference in Beijing, members of the Government Administration Council, and heads of various commissions, ministries, departments, institutes, administrations, and banks under the Government Administration Council, members of the People's Revolutionary Military Commission, Vice President and members of the Supreme People's Academy, and Deputy Procurator-General and members of the Supreme People's Procuratorate, totaling more than 130 people. The meeting adjourned at 12:30 PM.

### 会议一致通过决议：  以十月一日为国庆日

1949-12-04
第1版()
专栏：

　　会议一致通过决议：
    以十月一日为国庆日
    会议最后的议程是通过关于国庆日的决议。会议一致决议以每年的十月一日为中华人民共和国的国庆日。
    出席昨天会议的为：毛泽东、朱德、刘少奇、李济深、张澜、林伯渠、何香凝、吴玉章、彭真、薄一波、聂荣臻、周恩来、董必武、饶漱石、邓子恢、乌兰夫、徐特立、刘格平、马寅初、陈云、马叙伦、郭沫若、沈钧儒、沈雁冰、陈叔通、司徒美堂、李锡九、黄炎培、蔡廷锴、彭泽民、傅作义、李烛尘、章伯钧、张奚若、陈铭枢、谭平山、张难先、柳亚子、张东荪等三十九人。被邀列席的，有东北人民政府副主席李富春，和在北京的中国人民政协全国委员会委员，政务委员及政务院所属各委、部、会、院、署、行的负责人，人民革命军事委员会委员，最高人民学院副院长和委员、以及最高人民检查署副检察长和委员等，共一百三十余人。会议至十二时三十分结束。
