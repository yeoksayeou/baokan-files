Title: World Peace Congress Standing Committee Reporting Bureau Meeting Passes Resolution Calling on National Parliaments to Urge the Declaration of Atomic Weapons as Illegal
Author:
Date: 1949-12-20
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, 19th: TASS, Paris: The Executive Bureau of the Standing Committee of the World Peace Congress held a meeting on the 16th under the chairmanship of Joliot-Curie. Those present included: Eugène Cotton, Vice-Chairman of the Executive Bureau of the Standing Committee of the World Peace Congress; Fadeyev, Soviet writer; Bernal, British physicist; Pietro Nenni, leader of the Italian Socialist Party; Jean Laffitte, Secretary-General of the Standing Committee of the World Peace Congress; Bozhilov, Secretary-General of the World Federation of Democratic Youth; and Lisette, member of the Republican Resistance Movement Alliance. The Executive Bureau discussed a telegram to the parliaments of all countries in the world, urging the national parliaments to demand their respective governments to: declare atomic weapons illegal, conduct international negotiations to stop the wars in Greece, Vietnam and Indonesia, and sign peace pacts among the major powers. The telegram also protested against the re-militarization of Western Germany.

### 世界和大常设委会报告局集会  通过致电世界各国国会敦促宣布原子武器非法

1949-12-20
第4版()
专栏：

　　世界和大常设委会报告局集会
    通过致电世界各国国会敦促宣布原子武器非法
    【新华社北京十九日电】塔斯社巴黎讯：世界拥护和平大会常设委员会执行局于十六日在约里奥——居里主持下举行会议。出席者有：世界拥护和平大会常设委员会执行局副主席尤金尼·戈登，苏联作家法捷耶夫，英物理学家贝尔纳，意大利社会党领袖彼得·南尼，世界拥护和平大会常设委员会总书记约翰·拉斐德，世界民主青年联盟总书记博若拉及共和抵抗运动联盟会员利塞特。执行局讨论了致世界各国国会通电，该电敦促各国国会向各该国政府要求：宣布原子武器非法，进行国际谈判，以制止在希腊、越南及印尼的战争，签订大国和平公约。通电并对德国西部重行军国主义化一事提出抗议。
