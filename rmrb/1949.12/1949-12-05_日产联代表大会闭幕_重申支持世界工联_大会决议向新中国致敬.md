Title: Japan Federation of Industrial Labor Unions (Sanbetsu) Congress Concludes: Reaffirms Support for World Federation of Trade Unions; Congress Resolution Pays Tribute to New China
Author:
Date: 1949-12-05
Page: 4
　　
　　Japan Federation of Industrial Labor Unions (Sanbetsu) Congress Concludes
  　Reaffirms Support for World Federation of Trade Unions
  　Congress Resolution Pays Tribute to New China
    【Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, 3rd】 Dentsu News Agency, Tokyo, 1st: The fifth congress of the Japan Federation of Industrial Labor Unions (Sanbetsu), which closed yesterday, passed a resolution by an overwhelming majority, reaffirming its support for the World Federation of Trade Unions and paying tribute to the People's Republic of China. The resolution pointed out that the establishment of the People's Republic of China is clear evidence of the growth of the power of the people of the world. Three hundred and twenty-eight delegates representing thirteen unions with 750,000 members attended the congress. A strongly worded resolution condemned the rumored plans to conclude a separate peace treaty without the participation of the Soviet Union and China. The resolution stated: "A separate peace treaty violates the interests of Japanese workers and endangers world peace." Metalworker leader Sukeshige Yoshida was elected as the new chairman of Sanbetsu.

### 日产联代表大会闭幕　重申支持世界工联　大会决议向新中国致敬

1949-12-05
第4版()
专栏：

　　日产联代表大会闭幕
  　重申支持世界工联
  　大会决议向新中国致敬
    【新华社北京三日电】电通社东京一日讯：昨日闭幕的日本全国产业劳动组合联合会（简称产联）第五届代表大会，以压倒多数通过决议，重申支持世界工联，并向中华人民共和国致敬。决议指出中华人民共和国的成立是世界人民力量增长的明证。出席大会的有代表着拥有七十五万会员的十三个工会的三百二十八名代表。一个措词强硬的决议，谴责传说中的缔结没有苏联与中国参加的单独和约的计划。该决议称：“单独和约违背日本工人的利益，并危害世界和平。”五金工人领袖吉田资治当选为产联的新主席。
