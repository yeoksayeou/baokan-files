Title: Mongolian Women's Exhibition Items Donated to the All-China Women's Federation
Author:
Date: 1949-12-17
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, 16th. Mongolian women have donated all the exhibits from one hall of the Asian Workers' and Women's Life and Struggle Exhibition to the All-China Democratic Women's Federation. The donation ceremony was held in the capital at 2:00 PM today. Dorongmajawa, the head of the Mongolian delegation to the Asian Women's Representatives Conference, delivered a speech on behalf of the Mongolian women. Li Dequan, Vice-Chairman of the All-China Democratic Women's Federation, accepted the gift on behalf of the Federation.

### 蒙古妇女展览品赠予我全国妇联

1949-12-17
第1版()
专栏：

　　蒙古妇女展览品赠予我全国妇联
    【新华社北京十六日电】蒙古妇女以参加亚洲工人妇女生活与斗争展览会一个室的全部展览品赠送给中华全国民主妇女联合会。赠送典礼今日下午二时在首都举行，出席亚洲妇女代表会的蒙古代表团团长多龙玛贾娃代表蒙古妇女致赠送词，中华全国民主妇女联合会副主席李德全代表受礼。
