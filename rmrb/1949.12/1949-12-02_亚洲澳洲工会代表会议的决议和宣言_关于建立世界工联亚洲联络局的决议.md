Title: Resolutions and Declarations of the Asian-Australian Trade Union Representatives Conference: Resolution on the Establishment of the World Federation of Trade Unions Asian Liaison Bureau
Author:
Date: 1949-12-02
Page: 2

Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, 1st: The full text of the "Resolution of the Asian-Australian Trade Union Representatives Conference on the Establishment of the World Federation of Trade Unions Asian Liaison Bureau" is as follows:

    In order to implement the resolution on the establishment of various liaison bureaus of the World Federation of Trade Unions (Item 5 of the Executive Bureau meeting agenda, Beijing, November 1949) and with reference to the resolution passed by the Asian-Australian Trade Union Conference (Beijing, November 1949), the Executive Bureau of the World Federation of Trade Unions hereby decides:

    I. To establish an Asian Liaison Bureau and, in accordance with the agreement reached with the All-China Federation of Trade Unions, to establish its headquarters in China (provisionally in Beijing).

    II. To stipulate that the number of members of the Asian Liaison Bureau shall be four, namely:
        One representative of the All-China Federation of Trade Unions.
        One representative of the All-India Trade Union Congress.
        One representative of the Soviet Trade Unions.
        One representative of the Australian Trade Unions.
        The candidate for the Australian Trade Union representative must be agreed upon by the Trade Union International (the industrial department of the World Federation of Trade Unions).
        The appointment of all members must be approved by the Executive Bureau of the World Federation of Trade Unions.

    III. The main responsible representative in the Liaison Bureau will be the member appointed by the All-China Federation of Trade Unions.

    IV. This decision shall be effective from January 1, 1950.

    V. All administrative and financial measures required to implement this decision will be handled by the Secretariat of the World Federation of Trade Unions, which shall submit a report on this work to the next Executive Bureau meeting.

    Beijing, December 1, 1949

### 亚洲澳洲工会代表会议的决议和宣言  关于建立世界工联亚洲联络局的决议

1949-12-02
第2版()
专栏：

　　亚洲澳洲工会代表会议的决议和宣言
    关于建立世界工联亚洲联络局的决议
    【新华社北京一日电】“亚洲澳洲工会代表会议关于建立世界工联亚洲联络局的决议”全文如下：
    为实践关于建立世界工联各联络局的决议（执行局会议日程第五项、北京、一九四九年十一月）并参照亚洲澳洲工会会议所通过的决议（北京、一九四九年十一月），世界工联执行局兹决定：
    一、建立一个亚洲的联络局，并按照和中华全国总工会取得的协议，将其总部设立于中国（暂设于北京）。
    二、规定亚洲联络局的委员数目为四人，即：中华全国总工会代表一人。
    全印度工会大会代表一人。
    苏联工会代表一人。
    澳大利亚工会代表一人。
    澳大利亚工会代表人选须商得工会国际（世界工联的产业部）的同意。
    全部委员的委派须经世界工联执行局的同意。
    三、联络局中主要负责代表将为中华全国总工会所委派的委员。
    四、此决定从一九五○年一月一日起为有效。
    五、执行此决定时所需一切管理与财政措施，将交由世界工联秘书处办理，秘书处须向下届执行局会议提出关于此项工作的报告。
    北京　一九四九年十二月一日
