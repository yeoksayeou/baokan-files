Title: North Korea Establishes Birthday Celebration Preparatory Committee; Publishes Large Quantities of Stalin's Works
Author:
Date: 1949-12-12
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Pyongyang, 11th. The Central Committee of the Korean Fatherland United Democratic Front held a Central Standing Committee meeting on the evening of the 8th. It was decided to establish a Preparatory Committee for the Celebration of Marshal Stalin's 70th Birthday. Premier Kim Il-sung will serve as Chairman, and 42 individuals including Kim Tu-bong, Pak Hon-yong, Kim Chaek, Hong Myong-hui, Choe Yong-gon, Kim Tal-hyon, and Kang Ryang-uk, leaders of various democratic parties and people's organizations, will serve as members, responsible for various preparatory matters.

The North Korean Workers' Party Publishing House, in accordance with the decision of the Party's Political Committee, has recently translated, published, or reprinted the following works by Stalin: Stalin's Collected Works, Volumes 1 to 5; Problems of Leninism, Volumes 1, 2, and 3; History of the Communist Party of the Soviet Union (Bolsheviks) Short Course; Stalin's Biography; On the Great Patriotic War of the Soviet Union; Anarchism or Socialism?; Marxism and the National Question; and a collection of Stalin's speeches, talks, and Q&As after World War II.

### 朝鲜成立祝寿筹备委员会　大量出版斯大林著作

1949-12-12
第4版()
专栏：

　　朝鲜成立祝寿筹备委员会
  　大量出版斯大林著作
    【新华社平壤十一日电】朝鲜祖国统一民主主义战线中央委员会，于八日晚举行中央常务委员会，决定成立庆祝斯大林大元帅七十寿辰筹备委员会，由部长会议主席金日成任委员长，各民主党派、人民团体负责人金枓奉、朴宪永、金策、洪命熹、崔镛健、金达铉、康良煜等四十二人任委员，负责各项筹务事宜。
    北朝鲜劳动党出版社最近已依照该党政治委员会的决定，翻译出版或翻印了斯大林的如下各项著作：斯大林全集第一册到第五册，列宁主义问题第一、二、三分册，联共党史简明教程，斯大林传略，论苏联伟大自卫战争，无政府主义还是社会主义，马克思主义和民族问题以及二次大战后斯大林的演说、谈话、问答集等。
