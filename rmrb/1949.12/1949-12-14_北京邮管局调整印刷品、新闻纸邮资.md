Title: Beijing Postal Administration Adjusts Postage for Printed Matter and Newspapers
Author:
Date: 1949-12-14
Page: 3

[Beijing News] The Beijing Postal Administration and its subordinate bureaus will adjust the postage for printed matter and newspaper categories from the 13th of this month as follows:

　　I. Printed Matter: Small packages of printed matter within 50 centimeters: 20 yuan within the district, 70 yuan outside the district, and 350 yuan internationally. Bulk printed matter, per 100 centimeters: 40 yuan within the district, 140 yuan outside the district, and 700 yuan internationally. Business flyers sent outside the district, in addition to the printed matter postage, will be charged an additional 300 yuan per 100 copies or fraction thereof for distribution fees; local delivery will only be charged the distribution fee.

　　II. General Newspapers: Per 50 centimeters: 20 yuan within the district, 60 yuan outside the district, and 300 yuan internationally. Registered Newspapers: Per 100 centimeters: 40 yuan within the district, 120 yuan outside the district. Total Package Newspapers: Per 500 centimeters: 200 yuan within the district, 650 yuan outside the district.

　　III. Samples: Per 100 centimeters: 140 yuan domestically, 700 yuan internationally.

### 北京邮管局调整印刷品、新闻纸邮资

1949-12-14
第3版()
专栏：

　　北京邮管局调整印刷品、新闻纸邮资
    【北京讯】北京邮政管理局及所辖各局十三日起调整印刷品类及新闻纸类邮资如下：
    一、印刷品类：小包印刷品五十公分以内本区二十元，外区七十元，国际三五○元。大批印刷品每重一百公分本区四十元，外区一四○元，国际七○○元。商务传单外区者除收取印刷品资费外，每百份或其零数另加分送费三○○元，本地投递者只收分送费。
    二、普遍新闻纸类：每重五十公分本区二十元，外区六十元，国际三○○元。立券新闻纸每重一百公分本区四十元，外区一二○元。总包新闻纸每重五百公分本区二百元，外区六五○元。
    三、货样类：每重一百公分国内一四○元，国际七○○元。
