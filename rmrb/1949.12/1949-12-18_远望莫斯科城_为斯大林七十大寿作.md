Title: Looking Afar at Moscow – Written for Stalin's Seventieth Birthday
Author: Tian Jian
Date: 1949-12-18
Page: 5

　　Looking Afar at Moscow
    — Written for Stalin's Seventieth Birthday
    Tian Jian
　　(I)
    Looking afar at Moscow,
    Where Stalin lives.
    We are looking at Stalin,
    Respectfully celebrating his seventieth birthday;
    Stalin is like an open door,
    He is also looking at us.
    Stalin is not just anyone,
    He is today's Lenin,
    Leading us to change heaven and earth,
    Opening the door to a new world.
    His hand is higher than the sky,
    His heart is broader than the earth.
    In millions of cities and towns in the world,
    Live Stalins,
    In Stalin's heart,
    Also live millions of working people.
    He is with us,
    Just like the moon and the stars,
    The sky is full of stars surrounding the moon,
    The moon shines on the sky full of stars.
    I look afar at Moscow,
    The blue sky above the city,
    Is Stalin,
    Brightness is Stalin!
　　(II)
    Above Moscow,
    Hangs an eternal light,
    Illuminating the roads of the world,
    Leading to the revolutionary family.
    Moscow and Beijing,
    The road is ten thousand miles of red light,
    Moscow and Beijing,
    Red horses galloping on the road,
    Thousands upon thousands of vows,
    Sent to Stalin;
    Thousands upon thousands of respects,
    Sent into Moscow. (December 1949)

### 远望莫斯科城——为斯大林七十大寿作
田间
1949-12-18
第5版()
专栏：

　　远望莫斯科城
    ——为斯大林七十大寿作
    田间
　　（一）
    远望莫斯科城，
    住的斯大林。
    咱们在望斯大林，
    敬贺他七十大寿；
    斯大林象开着门，
    他也在望咱们。
    斯大林不是别人，
    是今天的列宁，
    领导咱换天换地，
    打开新世界的门。
    他的手比天高，
    他的心胸比地阔。
    世界上千万个城镇，
    都住着斯大林，
    斯大林的心上，
    也住着千万劳动人。
    他和咱们在一起，
    正如月亮和星星，
    满天星星围住月亮，
    月亮照着满天星。
    我远望莫斯科城，
    城上碧蓝的青天，
    就是斯大林，
    光明就是斯大林！
　　（二）
    莫斯科城上，
    挂的长明灯，
    照的天下道路，
    通到革命大家庭。
    莫斯科和北京，
    路上是万里红光，
    莫斯科和北京，
    红马在路上飞奔，
    千千万万誓言，
    送给斯大林；
    千千万万敬意，
    送进莫斯科城。　（一九四九、一二、）
