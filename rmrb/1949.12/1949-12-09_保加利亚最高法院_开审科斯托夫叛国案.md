Title: Bulgarian Supreme Court Opens Trial of Kostov for Treason
Author:
Date: 1949-12-09
Page: 4
　　
　　Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, December 8th. TASS reports: The Bulgarian Supreme Court began the trial of Kostov and his accomplices for treason on the 7th in the Central Building of the People's Army in Sofia. The court is composed of the following individuals: Chief Justice Lochanov, Judges Nechev and Spasov, Associate Judges Koyeykov, Popov, Ignatov, and Mikhailov. The prosecutors are Dimchev and Chakov. Eleven lawyers are serving as defense counsel for the defendants. Sixty-four witnesses and twenty-nine experts have also been summoned to the court. Representatives from all sectors of Bulgarian society, as well as Bulgarian and foreign journalists, were present to observe the proceedings. On the morning of the 7th, the indictment from the Supreme People's Procuratorate of the People's Republic of Bulgaria was read in court.

### 保加利亚最高法院　开审科斯托夫叛国案

1949-12-09
第4版()
专栏：

　　保加利亚最高法院
  　开审科斯托夫叛国案
    【新华社北京八日电】据塔斯社讯：保加利亚最高法院于七日开始在索非亚人民军中央大厦审讯科斯托夫及其同谋的叛国案。法庭由下列诸人组成：首席推事洛查诺夫，法官奈切夫和斯巴索夫，陪审官科耶伊科夫、波波夫、伊格那托夫和米哈伊洛夫。起诉人为第姆切夫和查科夫。十一位律师担任被告的辩护人，六十四位证人和二十九位专家，亦已被传到案。保加利亚各界代表及保国与外国新闻记者均出席旁听。七日上午在庭上宣读了保加利亚人民共和国最高检察署的起诉书。
