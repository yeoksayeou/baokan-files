Title: Wald et al. Deported: Left Shenyang Yesterday
Author:
Date: 1949-12-08
Page: 1

　　Wald et al. Deported
  　Left Shenyang Yesterday
    Xinhua News Agency, Shenyang, 7th. Following the notice from the Shenyang Municipal People's Government Public Security Bureau ordering them to leave the country, the former U.S. Consulate in Shenyang's Wald and 19 others left Shenyang at 3:40 AM today and will exit China through Tianjin.

### 瓦尔德等驱逐出境　昨日离沈

1949-12-08
第1版()
专栏：

　　瓦尔德等驱逐出境
  　昨日离沈
    【新华社沈阳七日电】在我沈阳市人民政府公安机关限令离境的通知下，前美国驻沈阳领事馆瓦尔德等二十人，已于今日上午三时四十分离开沈阳，将经天津出中国国境。
