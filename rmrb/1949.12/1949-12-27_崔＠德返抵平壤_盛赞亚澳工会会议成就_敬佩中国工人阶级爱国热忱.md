Title: Choe Yong-gon Returns to Pyongyang; Praises Achievements of Asian-Australasian Trade Union Conference; Admires Patriotic Zeal of Chinese Working Class
Author:
Date: 1949-12-27
Page: 4
　　Choe Yong-gon Returns to Pyongyang
　　Praises Achievements of Asian-Australasian Trade Union Conference
　　Admires Patriotic Zeal of Chinese Working Class
    [Xinhua News Agency, Pyongyang, December 25th] Choe Yong-gon, head of the Korean delegation to the Asian-Australasian Trade Union Conference and Chairman of the Central Committee of the North Korean Federation of Trade Unions, made a statement on the 23rd after returning to Pyongyang, highly praising the great achievements of the conference. He said that this conference was a huge victory in the history of the world workers' movement. He pointed out that the conference especially praised the experience of the Chinese working class, under the leadership of the Chinese Communist Party, standing at the forefront of the 900 million people and achieving victory through bloody struggle. Speaking of their impressions after visiting Beijing, Tianjin, Shanghai, Nanjing, and other places after the meeting, he said: We saw the wounds that the Chiang Kai-shek bandit gang left on the Chinese working class, and deeply realized how bravely and resolutely the Chinese working class carried out the anti-imperialist, anti-feudal, and anti-bureaucratic-capitalist struggle under the reactionary policy of massacre. We express our heartfelt admiration for the iron-willed fighting spirit of the Chinese people, led by the Chinese working class, under the banner of the new People's Republic of China, overcoming various difficulties, and victoriously carrying out the construction of a new China with unparalleled patriotic zeal. Finally, he expressed his determination to transform the confidence and valuable experience given to him by this conference into the strength of the Korean working class in the struggle for the reunification and independence of the fatherland.

### 崔＠德返抵平壤　盛赞亚澳工会会议成就  敬佩中国工人阶级爱国热忱

1949-12-27
第4版()
专栏：

　　崔＠德返抵平壤
　  盛赞亚澳工会会议成就
    敬佩中国工人阶级爱国热忱
    【新华社平壤二十五日电】出席亚澳工人代表会议的朝鲜代表团团长北朝鲜职业总同盟中央委员会委员长崔＠德，在返抵平壤之后，于廿三日发表谈话，盛赞这次会议的伟大成就。他说，这次会议在世界工人运动史上是一个巨大的胜利。他指出，大会特别赞扬了中国工人阶级在中国共产党领导下，站在九万万人民的前头，用血的斗争所取得的胜利的经验。谈到他们会后参观北京、天津、上海、南京等地的观感时，他说：我们看到了蒋介石匪帮给中国工人阶级留下的创伤，深深体会到中国工人阶级在反动派屠杀政策下是怎样英勇、果敢地进行了反帝、反封建、反官僚资本的斗争。我们对于以中国工人阶级为首的中国人民，在崭新的中华人民共和国旗帜下，以无比高度的爱国热忱，克服各种困难，胜利地进行着建设新中国的钢铁般的斗争意志，表示衷心的敬佩。最后，他表示决心将这次大会所给他的自信心和宝贵经验，变成朝鲜工人阶级为争取祖国统一独立斗争的力量。
