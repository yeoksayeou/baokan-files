Title: Our Army in Southern Shaanxi Advances on Northern Sichuan, Liberating Hanzhong and Five Other Cities; Huixian and Chengxian in Southern Gansu Liberated in Succession; Six More Cities Captured in Sichuan, Annihilating 8,000 Enemy Troops
Author:
Date: 1949-12-10
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Southern Shaanxi Front, 9th: The important town of Hanzhong in Southern Shaanxi has been liberated. The People's Liberation Army advancing on Sichuan liberated the city on the evening of the 6th, and was welcomed by the local people who set off firecrackers and hung lanterns along the streets. The vanguard of the People's Liberation Army has approached the Sichuan border. In addition to Hanzhong, Xixiang (5th), Baocheng (6th), and Chenggu (7th) have also been liberated successively. Ningshan, east of Hanzhong, was also liberated on the 5th. Another unit of the People's Liberation Army liberated the two cities of Huixian and Chengxian in southern Gansu on the 4th.

Xinhua News Agency, Southwest Front, 9th: The People's Liberation Army, advancing northward from the Neijiang-Zigong line west of Chongqing, liberated Zizhong and Weiyuan on the 7th. Rongxian had been liberated the day before. In the above battles, the People's Liberation Army annihilated more than 1,000 enemies from the 21st Army, the 31st Division of the 27th Army, and other miscellaneous bandit troops. Zhou Zhizai, commander of the 150th Division of the 44th Army, deputy commander Xiao Dexuan, chief of staff Fang Renxi, and chief of the political work department Tong Lengliao, led the division's directly subordinate units and the two regimental headquarters of the 449th Regiment and the 450th Regiment, a total of more than 1,000 people, to surrender to our garrison in Longchang.

Xinhua News Agency, Southwest Front, 9th: The People's Liberation Army liberated the county towns of Yongchuan and Rongchang, west of Chongqing, on the 7th. Zhang Heng, commander of the 701st Regiment of the 234th Division of the enemy in Yongchuan, led six battalions, a total of more than 4,000 people, to surrender to our army. The People's Liberation Army also annihilated parts of the 367th Division and the 34th Division in the Jiang'an area southwest of Chongqing on the 5th and in the Naxi area on the 6th, capturing more than 2,600 enemy troops, including the commander of the 367th Division, Ai Xingheng. Another unit of the People's Liberation Army liberated the county town of Linshui, north of Chongqing, on the 6th.

### 陕南我军进逼川北　解放汉中等五城　甘肃南部徽县成县相继解放  川境再克六城消灭敌军八千

1949-12-10
第1版()
专栏：

　　陕南我军进逼川北
  　解放汉中等五城
  　甘肃南部徽县成县相继解放
    川境再克六城消灭敌军八千
    【新华社陕南前线九日电】陕南重镇汉中已告解放。向四川进军的人民解放军于六日傍晚解放该城，受到燃炮挂灯的当地人民夹道欢迎。解放军前锋已迫近四川边境，除汉中外，尚先后解放西乡（五日）、褒城（六日）、城固（七日）三城。汉中以东的宁陕亦已于五日解放。另部解放军亦于四日解放甘肃南部的徽县、成县两城。
    【新华社西南前线九日电】自重庆以西之内江、自贡一线北进之人民解放军，于七日解放资中、威远两城，在此之前一日已解放荣县。在以上战斗中，解放军歼敌二十一军、二十七军三十一师以及其他游杂匪军各一部共一千余人。敌四四军一五○师师长周之再、副师长肖德煊、参谋长方人熙、政工处长佟冷僚率该师直属队及四四九团、四五○团两个团部共一千余人向我在隆昌之驻军投诚。
    【新华社西南前线九日电】人民解放军于七日解放重庆以西之永川、荣昌两县城，永川之敌二三四师七○一团团长张衡率六个营共四千余人向我军投诚。解放军并于五日在重庆西南江安地区和于六日在纳溪地区先后歼敌三六七师及三四师各一部，俘敌三六七师师长艾猩珩以下二千六百余人。另部解放军于六日解放重庆以北之邻水县城。
