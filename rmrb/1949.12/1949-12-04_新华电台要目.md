　　Xinhua Radio Program Schedule
    [Our Newspaper] Beijing Xinhua Broadcasting Station will broadcast the following programs tonight (4th): 17:30, Workers' Program: "The Northeast Workers' Movement to Create New Production Records." 18:45, Broadcasting of Maxine Farrokh's speech from the 2nd in Japanese. 18:00, First Literary Program: Local Opera "Hou Zhaoyin Kills the Enemy and Saves Her Daughter." 20:30, Weekly Literary Program: Korean Music. 21:00, Second Literary Program: Second half of the New Peking Opera "The River God Takes a Bride."
    [Further News] The Second Station's 12:30, Workers' Time: "How the Fourth Training Class of the Telecommunications Bureau Was Organized." 13:00, Local Folk Music: Lianhualuo "Yang Fagui Smashes His Son." 16:15, Children's Time: "The Story of Pavel." 16:45, Listener Service: Doctor Zeng Zhaoyi's thoughts on attending the Beijing Women's Congress. 19:15, Introduction to the Soviet automotive, electrical, and textile industries.

### 新华电台要目

　　新华电台要目
    【本报讯】北京新华广播电台今（四）晚十七点三十分职工节目：“东北工人的创造生产新纪录运动”。十八点四十五分用日语播送玛辛·法洛琪二日广播演讲词。十八点第一次文艺节目：牌子戏“侯昭银杀敌救女记”。八点三十分星期文艺节目：朝鲜音乐。二十一点第二次文艺节目：新京剧“河伯娶妇”后半部。
