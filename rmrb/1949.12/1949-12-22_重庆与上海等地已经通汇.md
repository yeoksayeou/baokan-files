Title: Chongqing and Shanghai and other places have already opened remittances
Author:
Date: 1949-12-22
Page: 3

　　Xinhua News Agency, Chongqing, 20th. Chongqing has opened remittances with Shanghai, Hankou, Changsha, Changde, Xi'an, Hanzhong, Baoji, Yichang, Shashi and other places since the 16th.

### 重庆与上海等地已经通汇

1949-12-22
第3版()
专栏：

　　重庆与上海等地已经通汇
    【新华社重庆二十日电】重庆十六日起已与上海、汉口、长沙、常德、西安、汉中、宝鸡、宜昌、沙市等地通汇。
