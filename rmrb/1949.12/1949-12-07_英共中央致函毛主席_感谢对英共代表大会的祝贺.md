Title: CPGB Central Committee Sends Letter to Chairman Mao, Thanking Him for Congratulations on CPGB Congress
Author: Qiao Zhi·Masiwusi
Date: 1949-12-07
Page: 4

　　CPGB Central Committee Sends Letter to Chairman Mao
  　Thanking Him for Congratulations on CPGB Congress
    [Xinhua News Agency, Prague, December 6th] The Central Committee of the Communist Party of Great Britain sent a letter to Comrade Mao Zedong on November 30th, thanking him for his congratulations on the 21st Congress of the Communist Party of Great Britain. The original letter is as follows: Dear Comrade Mao Zedong,
    Our Party's 21st National Congress received your fraternal congratulatory telegram with joyful hearts.
    The Congress sends you our warmest congratulations, expresses admiration for the great struggle and victory of the Chinese people under the leadership of the great Communist Party of China, and assures you that it will continue to fight with renewed strength to achieve the British government's recognition of the People's Government of China and friendly trade and political relations between the two countries.
    The 21st National Congress of our Party especially sends our congratulations to you, Comrade Mao Zedong, the outstanding and beloved leader of the Chinese people.
    Fraternal greetings!
    　　　　　　　　　　　　Deputy Secretary George Matthews (signed)

### 英共中央致函毛主席　感谢对英共代表大会的祝贺
乔治·马休斯
1949-12-07
第4版()
专栏：

　　英共中央致函毛主席
  　感谢对英共代表大会的祝贺
    【新华社布拉格六日电】英国共产党中央委员会于十一月三十日致函毛泽东同志，感谢他对英国共产党第二十一届代表大会的祝贺。原函如下：亲爱的毛泽东同志：
    我党的第二十一届全国代表大会以欢庆的心情接到了你对我党的兄弟般的贺电。
    大会向你致送我们最热烈的祝贺，对中国人民在伟大中国共产党领导下所进行的巨大斗争和胜利表示钦佩，并且保证它将继续以新的力量进行斗争，以获致英国政府对中国人民政府的承认和两国间友好的贸易和政治关系。
    我党第二十一届全国代表大会特别向您，毛泽东同志，中国人民的杰出和受爱戴的领袖，致送我们的祝贺。
    致兄弟的敬礼！
    　　　　　　　　　　　　副书记乔治·马休斯（签字）
