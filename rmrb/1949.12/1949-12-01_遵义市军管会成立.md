Title: Zunyi Military Control Commission Established
Author:
Date: 1949-12-01
Page: 1

　　Xinhua News Agency, Southwest Front, 30th. The Zunyi Municipal Military Control Commission of Guizhou Province was officially established on the 26th upon the order of the Second Field Army Headquarters and Political Department of the People's Liberation Army. Chen Puran and Li Hecheng serve as the Chairman and Vice Chairman of the Military Control Commission, respectively. They have already taken office and begun their work.

### 遵义市军管会成立

1949-12-01
第1版()
专栏：

　　遵义市军管会成立
    【新华社西南前线三十日电】贵州遵义市军事管制委员会已于二十六日奉人民解放军第二野战军司令部、政治部命令宣告成立。陈朴如、李和程为军管会正副主任委员。已于即日到职工作。
