Title: Our Women's Delegation Hosts a Reception to Warmly Welcome Women Representatives from Various Countries; Delegations from Twelve Countries Attending the Asian Women's Conference Have Arrived in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1949-12-08
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, December 7th. The General Secretary of the Women's International Democratic Federation, Madame Eugénie Cotton, and the representatives from various countries attending the Asian Women's Conference were warmly received by the Chinese women representatives at a reception hosted by the Chinese delegation today. The reception was held at 4:00 PM. Lu Cui, Secretary-General of the International Preparatory Committee for the Asian Women's Conference, first introduced Deng Yingchao, head of the Chinese delegation, and vice-heads Yang Zhihua, Xu Guangping, Zhang Yun, and Honorary President He Xiangning and Vice-President Li Dequan of the All-China Democratic Women's Federation to the representatives from various countries. At the same time, General Secretary of the Women's International Democratic Federation, Madame Eugénie Cotton, was introduced to the Chinese representatives. Then, Yang Zhihua, the vice-head of the Chinese delegation, stood up and delivered a short welcome speech. She said: Thanks to the leadership of the Chinese Communist Party, the heroic fighting of the Chinese People's Liberation Army, and the help of the international democratic forces headed by the Soviet Union, the Chinese women representatives have the opportunity to welcome the sisters of the international anti-imperialist democratic camp to China for a conference. We feel extremely honored and happy. She said: From today onwards, the solidarity and friendship of women from all countries will be more consolidated, the anti-imperialist democratic camp will be stronger, and the mutual assistance of women from all countries will be further enhanced. Deng Yingchao, the head of the Chinese delegation, expressed condolences to the representatives and guests from various countries on behalf of the All-China Democratic Women's Federation and President Cai Chang. She pointed out that the convening of the Asian Women's Conference is an unprecedented event in the history of the Asian women's movement. Although the Asian women's liberation movement still faces many difficulties, the oppressed women of Asia will surely be liberated under the leadership of the Women's International Democratic Federation and the active efforts of women from all countries. The Chinese delegation also included female worker representative Zhou Jimei, army hero Chen Yan, and rural women worker Wang Yuelin, who spoke at the meeting. They respectively introduced the heroic deeds of Chinese women actively participating in the people's liberation struggle in factories, the army, and rural areas. These deeds were unanimously praised by the representatives and guests from various countries. At the meeting, the unity and friendship of women from all countries were also expressed through songs in various languages. After the Chinese delegation sang, the delegations from Korea, India, Mongolia, the Soviet Union, and others sang many wonderful songs. General Secretary of the Women's International Democratic Federation, Madame Eugénie Cotton, finally thanked the Chinese delegation on behalf of the representatives and guests from various countries. Then, the entire venue sang the majestic "Internationale" and "March of the Motherland," and the guests and hosts walked out of the venue in joyful singing.

Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, December 7th. Besides China, the following twelve delegations attending the Asian Women's Conference have arrived in Beijing: the Kirghiz Soviet Socialist Republic of the Soviet Union, the Mongolian People's Republic, the Democratic People's Republic of Korea, Vietnam, India, Indonesia, Burma, the Philippines, Malaya, Thailand, Iran, and Israel. The delegations from the other five Asian republics of the Soviet Union, Syria, Lebanon, and guests from Equatorial Africa are on their way to Beijing and will arrive soon.

### 我妇女代表团主持联欢会　热烈招待各国妇女代表  出席亚洲妇代会十二国代表团已到京

1949-12-08
第1版()
专栏：

　　我妇女代表团主持联欢会
  　热烈招待各国妇女代表
    出席亚洲妇代会十二国代表团已到京
    【新华社北京七日电】国际民主妇联总书记古久里夫人和亚洲妇女代表会议的各国代表，今日在中国代表团主持的联欢会上受到中国妇女代表的热烈招待。联欢会于下午四时举行，亚洲妇女代表会议国际筹委会秘书长陆璀首先将中国代表团团长邓颖超、副团长杨之华、许广平、章蕴和中华全国民主妇女联合会名誉主席何香凝、副主席李德全一一介绍给各国代表，同时将国际民主妇联总书记古久里夫人介绍给中国代表。接着，中国代表团副团长杨之华起立致简短欢迎词。她说：由于中国共产党的领导，由于中国人民解放军的英勇作战，由于以苏联为首的国际民主力量的帮助，使中国妇女代表能有机会来欢迎国际反帝国主义的民主阵营的姊妹们来中国开会，我们感到极大光荣和幸福。她说：从今天起，各国妇女的团结友谊将更巩固，反帝国主义的民主阵营将更加强，各国妇女的互助将更进一步。中国代表团团长邓颖超在讲话中国代表中华全国民主妇女联合会及蔡畅主席向各国代表和来宾表示慰问后，指出：亚洲妇女代表会议的召开，在亚洲妇女运动史上是空前的大事。目前亚洲妇女解放运动虽然还有许多困难，但在国际民主妇联领导及各国妇女积极努力下，亚洲被压迫的妇女定将得到解放。中国代表团在会上讲话的尚有女工代表周吉妹、部队英雄陈炎、农村妇女工作者王月林。她们分别介绍了中国妇女在工厂、部队、农村中积极参加人民解放斗争的英雄事迹，这些事迹得到各国代表和来宾一致的赞扬。在会上各国妇女的团结和友谊还通过各种语言的歌声表现出来。在中国代表团歌唱后，朝鲜代表团和印度、蒙古、苏联等代表都唱了许多精彩的歌曲。国际民主妇联总书记古久里夫人最后代表各国代表和来宾向中国代表团致谢。接着，全场唱起了雄壮的“国际歌”和“祖国进行曲”，宾主在愉快的歌声中走出会场。
    【新华社北京七日电】出席亚洲妇女代表会议的各国代表团，除中国外已有下列十二个到达了北京。计：苏联吉尔吉斯加盟共和国、蒙古人民共和国、朝鲜民主主义人民共和国、越南、印度、印度尼西亚、缅甸、菲律宾、马来亚、泰国、伊朗、以色列。苏联亚洲部分的其他五个加盟共和国和叙利亚、黎巴嫩代表团以及来自赤道非洲的来宾则正在来京途中，不日即可到达。
