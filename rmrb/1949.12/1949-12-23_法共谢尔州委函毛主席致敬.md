Title: French Communist Party Committee of Cher Sends Greetings to Chairman Mao
Author:
Date: 1949-12-23
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, December 22nd. The French Communist Party Committee of Cher sent a letter to Chairman Mao of our Central People's Government to express their respect. The full text of the letter is as follows:

　　To Chairman Mao Zedong of the People's Republic of China,

　　The Cher Committee of the French Communist Party, assembled on October 3rd, sends its warmest sympathies to Mao Zedong, the first Chairman of the People's Republic of China, on his inauguration.

　　We deeply regret that the French government – in contrast to the Soviet government – has not yet recognized the Chinese government, and we are now taking the necessary actions to obtain this recognition, which is a guarantee of Sino-French friendship.

　　Long live Mao Zedong!

　　Long live the People's Republic of China!

### 法共谢尔州委函毛主席致敬

1949-12-23
第1版()
专栏：

　　法共谢尔州委函毛主席致敬
    【新华社北京二十二日电】法国共产党谢尔州委员会致函我中央人民政府毛主席致敬。该函全文如下：
    向中华人民共和国主席毛泽东致敬
    法国共产党谢尔州委员会于十月三日集会，向中华人民共和国首任主席毛泽东，在其就职时，致最热烈的同情。
    我们对法国政府——与苏联政府相反——尚未承认中国政府，甚为遗憾，现正进行采取必要的行动，以获得此项承认，这是中法友谊的保证。
    毛泽东万岁！
    中华人民共和国万岁！
    　　　　　　　　　　　法国共产党谢尔州委员会（旧章）
    　　　　　　　　　　　　　　一九四九年十月五日于布尔日
