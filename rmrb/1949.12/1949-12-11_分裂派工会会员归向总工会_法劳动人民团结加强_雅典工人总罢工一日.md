Title: Splitter Trade Union Members Return to the General Confederation of Labor; Unity of French Laboring People Strengthened; General Strike of Workers in Athens for One Day
Author:
Date: 1949-12-11
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, December 10th. TASS, Paris, December 8th: The unity of the French working people is steadily growing. Following the twenty-four-hour general strike on November 25th, many members of the "Workers' Force" splinter trade union have left "Workers' Force" and joined the progressive General Confederation of Labor. These include the mayor of Hayange in the Moselle province, Anglaret, a well-known representative of the miners' union in Vargnies, Pas-de-Calais, and the secretary of the Montluçon postal workers' union, Golières. On December 6th, forty-seven tram workers in Lyon, including the secretary of the tram workers' union, jointly announced that they were leaving "Workers' Force" and joining the General Confederation of Labor.

Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, December 10th. According to a report by the Electric Communication Agency quoting a broadcast from Greek Radio: All workers in Athens and Piraeus are scheduled to hold a twenty-four-hour general strike on the 10th, demanding a 40 percent wage increase. Greek civil servants have also announced that they will strike on the 11th until their demands are met. Secondary school teachers throughout Greece have also gone on strike, demanding salary increases. Athens Radio has been suspended for four days due to a strike by its employees demanding wage increases.

### 分裂派工会会员归向总工会　法劳动人民团结加强  雅典工人总罢工一日

1949-12-11
第4版()
专栏：

　　分裂派工会会员归向总工会
  　法劳动人民团结加强
    雅典工人总罢工一日
    【新华社北京十日电】塔斯社巴黎八日讯：法国劳动人民的团结正日渐增强中。十一月二十五日举行二十四小时总罢工后，分裂派的“工人力量”工会的许多会员，都脱离“工人力量”而加入进步的总工会，其中有莫塞尔省的阿阳格市市长，加莱省瓦格尼斯地方矿工工会的著名代表社会党员昂格莱勒，蒙律松邮政工人工会书记高列埃兹等。十二月六日，里昂的四十七名电车工人连同电车工人工会书记在内一同宣称：他们退出“工人力量”而加入总工会。
    【新华社北京十日电】据电通社引自由希腊电台广播：雅典和比里犹斯的全体工人，定于十日举行二十四小时总罢工，要求增加工资百分之四十，希腊公务员也宣布他们将于十一日举行罢工，直到要求被满足时为止。全希腊的中等学校的教师，亦已罢教要求增加薪金。雅典电台因为职员要求增加工资而罢工，已停顿了四天。
