Title: Central, Beijing People's Radio Today's Broadcast Highlights
Author:
Date: 1949-12-27
Page: 3

　　Central, Beijing People's Radio Today's Broadcast Highlights
　　[This newspaper dispatch] The Central People's Broadcasting Station will present a lecture on common sense of natural science at 18:30 this evening (27th): "The Story of the Mountain." The first literary program at 18:00: Leting Drum "Train Driver Zhang Fulin." The second literary program at 21:00: The Asian Women's Congress Korean Delegation Art Performance Team presents "Korean Folk Songs."
　　[Another dispatch] Beijing People's Radio at 12:30, Workers' Time: Introducing the creative process of "Producing One Inch Longer." Children's Time at 16:15: Singing by children from Beichizi Primary School in the Sixth District. Women's Time at 16:45: "The Process of Workers and Families at the Fengtai Bridge Factory Unloading Bricks at Night." New Song Practice at 17:00: Teaching to sing "Celebrating the Year of Victory." Voice of the Municipality at 19:15: (1) Beijing Telecommunications Bureau explains some frequently occurring problems for telephone users, (2) The Suburban Work Committee's announcement regarding the registration of land used by troops, institutions, and schools in order to manage the city's suburban land, (3) The Suburban Work Committee is responsible for allocating land in the city's suburbs and resolving the issue of farmland for farmers whose land has been reclaimed, (4) The measures decided by the Public Security Bureau, Civil Affairs Bureau, and Health Bureau to clear snow in the city.

### 中央、北京人民电台今日广播要目

1949-12-27
第3版()
专栏：

　　中央、北京人民电台今日广播要目
    【本报讯】中央人民广播电台今（二十七）晚十八点三十分自然科学常识讲座：“山的故事”。十八点第一次文艺节目：乐亭大鼓“火车司机张福林”。二十一点第二次文艺节目：亚洲妇代会朝鲜代表团艺术演出队演播“朝鲜民歌”。
    【又讯】北京人民电台十二点三十分职工时间：介绍“生产长一寸”的创作经过。十六点十五分儿童时间：六区北池子小学小朋友唱歌。十六点四十五分妇女时间：“丰台桥梁厂职工和家属们夜间卸砖的经过”。十七点新歌练习：教唱“庆祝胜利年”。十九点十五分市政之声：（一）京电信局解释电话用户时常发生的一些问题，（二）郊区工作委会为管理本市郊区土地进行登记部队、机关、学校使用土地的通告，（三）郊区工作委会负责抽调本市郊区土地和解决被收回土地的农民耕地问题的通告，（四）公安局、民政局、卫生局决定的清除本市积雪的办法。
