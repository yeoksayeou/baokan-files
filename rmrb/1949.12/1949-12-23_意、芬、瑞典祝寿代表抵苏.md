Title: Italian, Finnish, Swedish Birthday Delegation Arrives in Soviet Union
Author:
Date: 1949-12-23
Page: 2

　　Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, 22nd. TASS dispatch: Italian Communist Party representatives Togliatti and Scoccimarro, Finnish Communist Party representatives Bessi and Kuusinen, and Swedish Communist Party representative Hagberg, all arrived in Moscow on the 20th to participate in the celebration of Comrade Stalin's 70th birthday.

### 意、芬、瑞典祝寿代表抵苏

1949-12-23
第2版()
专栏：

　　意、芬、瑞典祝寿代表抵苏
    【新华社北京二十二日电】塔斯社讯：意大利共产党代表托里亚蒂和谢嘉、芬兰共产党代表贝西和库西宁、瑞典共产党代表海格堡，都在二十日抵达莫斯科，参加庆祝斯大林同志七十寿辰的典礼。
