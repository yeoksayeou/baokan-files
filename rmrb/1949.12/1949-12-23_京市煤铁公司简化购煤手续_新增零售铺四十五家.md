Beijing Coal and Iron Company Simplifies Coal Purchasing Procedures
　　New Retail Outlets Added
[Beijing News] To facilitate coal usage for citizens during the winter, the Beijing Branch of the Coal and Iron Company has simplified coal purchasing procedures: Effective immediately, citizens can purchase coal from the company or its affiliated coal yards without presenting a household registration booklet or business license.

The company has also newly established 45 coal briquette retail outlets, bringing the total to 198 (the contracts with the Sansheng Gong Coal Shop in the Inner Fourth District and the Sun Ji Coal Shop in the Inner Third District were terminated due to illegal sale of wet coal briquettes; the Baoyun Shun and four other coal shops voluntarily withdrew due to their remote locations). Other local cooperatives can also contact the company directly to purchase.

### 京市煤铁公司简化购煤手续　新增零售铺四十五家

1949-12-23
第3版()
专栏：

　　京市煤铁公司简化购煤手续
    新增零售铺四十五家
    【北京讯】煤铁公司北京分公司为便于市民冬季用煤，特简化购煤手续：自即日起，市民不持户口单或营业照，即可到该公司或所辖煤厂去购煤。
