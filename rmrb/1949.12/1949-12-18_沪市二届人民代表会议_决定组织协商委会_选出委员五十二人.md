Title: Shanghai Municipal Second People's Representative Conference Decides to Organize Consultative Committee, Elects 52 Members
Author:
Date: 1949-12-18
Page: 2

Xinhua News Agency, Shanghai, 16th -- The Shanghai Municipal Second People's Representative Conference has decided to organize the Shanghai Municipal Consultative Committee as a local consultative body. After deliberation and multiple consultations by representatives from various units, 52 members of the Consultative Committee have been selected. The list is as follows (in order of the number of strokes in the surnames): Wang Zhihua, Wang Genzhong, Wang Yunsheng, Shen Baowen, Bao Dasan, Jiang Yong, Jiang Hengyuan, Ren Hong＠, Shen Han, Shen Tilan, Leng＠, Wu Youxun, Wu Yun Chu, Wu Xuezhou, Zhou Yutong, Jin Zhonghua, Hu JueWen, Yu Huancheng, Xu Yongzuo, Guo Dihuo, Chen Sisheng, Chen Shutong, Chen Wangdao, Chen Yi, Chen Jianchen, Chen Baichen, Zeng Shan, Xu Dixing, Sheng Pihua, Zhang Yuanji, Zhang＠Bo, Zhang Yaoxiang, Zhang Ben, Xiang Shuxiang, Tang Guifen, Shu Tong, Shu Xincheng, Zhao Zukang, Zhao Puchu, Rong Yiren, Liu Xiao, Liu Jingji, Liu Changsheng, Liu Hongsheng, Li Yu, Li Zhaohuan, Kui Yanfang, Pan Hannian, Xie Renbing, Han Wucheng, Han Xuezhang, Yan E'sheng.

### 沪市二届人民代表会议　决定组织协商委会　选出委员五十二人

1949-12-18
第2版()
专栏：

　　沪市二届人民代表会议
  　决定组织协商委会
  　选出委员五十二人
    【新华社上海十六日电】上海市第二届各界人民代表会议决定组织上海市协商委员会，作为地方性的协商机构。经过各单位代表的酝酿和多次协商，推出协商委员会五十二人，名单如下：（以姓氏笔划为序）：王志华、王艮仲、王芸生、申葆文、包达三、江庸、江恒源、任鸿＠、沈涵、沈体兰、冷＠、吴有训、吴蕴初、吴学周、周予同、金仲华、胡厥文、俞寰澄、徐永祚、郭棣活、陈巳生、陈叔通、陈望道、陈毅、陈建晨、陈白尘、曾山、许涤新、盛丕华、张元济、张＠伯、张耀祥、张本、项叔翔、汤桂芬、舒同、舒新城、赵祖康、赵朴初、荣毅仁、刘晓、刘靖基、刘长胜、刘鸿生、黎玉、黎照寰、篑延芳、潘汉年、谢仁冰、韩武成、韩学章、严谔声。
