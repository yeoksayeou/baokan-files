Title: Beijing Municipal Government Committee Holds First Meeting: Approves List of Municipal Government Secretary-General and Bureau Directors; Accepts All Resolutions of People's Representatives Conference
Author:
Date: 1949-12-10
Page: 1

【Beijing News】The Beijing Municipal People's Government Committee held its first meeting yesterday at 2:30 PM. Mayor Nie Rongzhen, Deputy Mayors Zhang Youyu and Wu Han, and all committee members attended. The meeting was presided over by Mayor Nie and approved the following items: (1) Nominated Committee Member Xue Zizheng as Secretary-General of the Municipal Government and submitted the nomination to the Government Administration Council of the Central People's Government for appointment. (2) Approved the list of Bureau Directors and submitted it to the Government Administration Council of the Central People's Government for appointment. (3) Accepted all resolutions of the Second Beijing People's Representatives Conference, to be implemented separately by each bureau according to plan.

### 京市府委员会首次会议  通过市府秘书长及各局长名单  接受各界代表会议全部决议案

1949-12-10
第1版()
专栏：

　　京市府委员会首次会议
    通过市府秘书长及各局长名单
    接受各界代表会议全部决议案
    【北京讯】北京市人民政府委员会于昨日下午二时半举行第一次会议，聂荣臻市长，张友渔、吴晗副市长及全体委员均出席。会议由聂市长主持，通过事项如下：（一）推选薛子正委员为市府秘书长，呈请中央人民政府政务院任命。（二）通过各局局长名单，呈请中央人民政府政务院任命。（三）接受本市第二届各界人民代表会议全部决议案，由各局分别计划实施。
