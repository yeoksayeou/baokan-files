Title: Czechoslovakia Appoints Ambassador to China: Dr. Weiskopf to Assume Position
Author:
Date: 1949-12-23
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, December 22nd: The President of the Czechoslovak Republic has appointed Mr. František Weiskopf as the first Ambassador Extraordinary and Plenipotentiary of the Czechoslovak Republic to the People's Republic of China. This appointment has been approved by our Central People's Government.

Dr. František Weiskopf was born in Prague in 1900. He has been engaged in journalism and writing for many years. He previously served as Counselor of the Czech Legation in Washington. From the beginning of 1949, he served as the Minister of the Czechoslovak Republic to Sweden, with the rank of Ambassador.

### 捷任命驻华大使  由魏伊斯柯普夫博士担任

1949-12-23
第1版()
专栏：

　　捷任命驻华大使
    由魏伊斯柯普夫博士担任
    【新华社北京二十二日电】捷克斯洛伐克共和国总统已任命法朗梯谢克·魏伊斯柯普夫先生为捷克斯洛伐克共和国驻中华人民共和国的首任特命全权大使。此项任命已征得我中央人民政府同意。
    法朗梯谢克·魏伊斯柯普夫博士于一九○○年生于布拉格。从事新闻及写作事业多年。曾担任捷克驻华盛顿公使衔参赞。一九四九年初起，任捷克斯洛代克共和国驻瑞典的大使衔公使。
