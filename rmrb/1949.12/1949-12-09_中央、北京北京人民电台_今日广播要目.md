　　Central, Beijing People's Radio Station
  　Today's Broadcast Highlights
    [Our Newspaper] The Central People's Broadcasting Station tonight (9th) at 17:30 Workers' Time: "The New Record Creation Movement of the Shenyang No. 3 Machine Factory." 18:30 Natural Science Knowledge Lecture: "The Characteristics of China's Climate." 18:00 First Literary Program: Cai Liangui sings the Xihe Dagu "Great Battle of Dayang Lake." 21:00 Second Literary Program: The Shenyang City Federation of Trade Unions Art Troupe performs the Little Yangko Opera "Reporting Good News."
    [Another News] Beijing People's Radio Station 12:30 Workers' Time: A worker family's story "Guan Qian." 16:15 Children's Time: Continuing the story of "Little Red Riding Hood." 16:30 Youth Time: Beijing Youth No. 37 editorial and an article: "Overcoming Difficulties in Victory," "Talking about the Contradiction between Work and Studies." 17:00 Music Program: The New China Women's Vocational School Choir sings new songs.
