Title: Beijing Xinhua, Sanlian and Other Bookstores Jointly Organize Mobile Supply Teams to Widely Sell Stalin's Works
Author:
Date: 1949-12-18
Page: 2

Beijing News: To celebrate the 70th birthday of the great revolutionary teacher Stalin, Beijing Xinhua, Sanlian and more than ten other bookstores are jointly offering a seven-day price reduction from the 15th to the 21st of this month. As of yesterday (17th), the Beijing branch of Xinhua Bookstore and its two sub-branches alone sold more than 50,000 books in three days, of which more than 30,000 were sold yesterday alone. Readers are eagerly buying Stalin's works and works about Stalin. Yesterday, Xinhua Bookstore and Sanlian Bookstore jointly organized a mobile supply team and dispatched a flower truck loaded with books to celebrate Stalin's 70th birthday. When the flower truck arrived outside various factories and schools, it was immediately surrounded by workers and students who scrambled to buy books. To facilitate people's purchase of books, the mobile supply team plans to sell books in Zhongshan Park, Taihe Hall, and on the streets today (18th).

### 京市新华三联等书店　联合组织流动供应队广泛出售斯大林著作

1949-12-18
第2版()
专栏：

　　京市新华三联等书店
  　联合组织流动供应队广泛出售斯大林著作
    【北京讯】京市新华、三联等十余书店为了庆祝伟大革命导师斯大林的七十寿辰，自本月十五日至二十一日特联合减价七天。截至昨（十七）日止，仅新华书店北京分店及两个支店三日来即售出书籍五万余册，其中三万多册是昨天一天售出的。读者争购的多为斯大林的著作及有关斯大林的著作。新华书店与三联书店于昨日联合组织流动供应队，出动庆祝斯大林七十寿辰的满载书籍的花卡车一辆。花车走到各工厂、学校门外时，即被工人、学生团团围住，争着购买。流动供应队为了便利人们购买书籍，预备今（十八）日至中山公园、太和殿和街头去售卖。
