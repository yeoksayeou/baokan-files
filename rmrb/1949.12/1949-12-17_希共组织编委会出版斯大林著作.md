Title: Greek Communist Party Organizing Committee Publishes Stalin's Works
Author:
Date: 1949-12-17
Page: 3

Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, December 16th: According to the Telegraph Agency, the Central Committee of the Greek Communist Party, at its meeting on December 11th, passed a special resolution on celebrating Stalin's 70th birthday. The meeting decided to organize mass study of Stalin's works, introduce his works in newspapers, and publish a Greek-translated Stalin biography prepared for his birthday.

The Central Committee has decided to translate and publish several volumes of works by Marx, Engels, Lenin, and Stalin as an important task for the Party to celebrate Stalin's birthday. A special editorial committee has been organized, composed of Politburo members and senior cadres of the Communist Party: Bassalides (member), Bazotas (member), Loussos (alternate member), Porphyrogenis, Zografos, Mavrommatis, Hatzivasiliou, and Karagiozis.

### 希共组织编委会出版斯大林著作

1949-12-17
第3版()
专栏：

　　希共组织编委会出版斯大林著作
    【新华社北京十六日电】据电通社讯：希腊共产党中央委员会于十二月十一日的会议上，通过关于庆祝斯大林七十寿辰的特别决议。会议决定组织对斯大林著作的群众性的学习，在报纸上介绍他的论著，并出版为他的寿辰而准备的希腊文译本的斯大林传。
    中央委员会已决定翻译及出版马克思、恩格斯、列宁和斯大林的若干卷著作，作为党庆祝斯大林寿辰的重要任务，并已组织一特别编辑委员会，由共产党的政治局委员及高级干部巴萨利德斯（委员）、巴佐塔斯（委员）、陆索斯（侯补委员）、坡非罗金尼斯、佐格拉伏斯、马夫罗马提斯、哈兹瓦西里翁和卡拉乔吉斯组成。
