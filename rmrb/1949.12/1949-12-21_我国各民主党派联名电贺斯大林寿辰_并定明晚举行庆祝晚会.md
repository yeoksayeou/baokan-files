Title: China's Democratic Parties Jointly Congratulate Stalin on His Birthday and Schedule a Celebration Evening for Tomorrow

Author:

Date: 1949-12-21
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, December 20th: China's various democratic parties have sent a joint telegram congratulating Marshal Stalin on his 70th birthday. The telegram reads as follows:

Respected Marshal Stalin:

On behalf of all the democratic parties of the People's Republic of China, we extend our warm and sincere congratulations to you on your 70th birthday. On this glorious, precious, and memorable day, the entire Chinese people and democratic parties are closely united, expanding their strength, and under the leadership of the Chinese Communist Party and Chairman Mao, are striving to expel American imperialism, eliminate feudal remnants, build a new democratic China, and fight for lasting world peace. We deeply understand that the victory of the Chinese people's liberation war today is inseparable from the assistance and encouragement you have given us, and the complete success of China's future revolutionary cause is also inseparable from your guidance and encouragement. We extend to you our highest respect and wish you health and longevity.

Revolutionary Committee of the Chinese Kuomintang, China Democratic League, China National Democratic Construction Association, Chinese Peasants' and Workers' Democratic Party, China Association for Promoting Democracy, China Zhi Gong Party, Jiusan Society, Taiwan Democratic Self-Government League

December 20, 1949

[Beijing News] The Revolutionary Committee of the Chinese Kuomintang, the China Democratic League, the Democratic National Construction Association, the Chinese Peasants' and Workers' Democratic Party, the China Association for Promoting Democracy, the China Zhi Gong Party, the Jiusan Society, and the Taiwan Democratic Self-Government League, held a forum at the Beijing Hotel the other day to celebrate Marshal Stalin's 70th birthday, and decided to jointly send a congratulatory telegram and gifts, and organize a joint celebration preparatory body. Since the 18th, more than 20 representatives from various democratic parties have been working at the address of the Democratic National Construction Association, and the following decisions have been made: 1. The preparatory committee is named "Preparatory Committee of Democratic Parties to Celebrate Stalin's 70th Birthday" and will concentrate its work at the Democratic National Construction Association; 2. The preparatory committee is divided into four groups: general affairs, decoration, reception, and entertainment, which will be responsible by personnel sent by the Democratic National Construction Association, the Revolutionary Committee of the Kuomintang, the Democratic League, and the Peasants' and Workers' Democratic Party respectively; 3. The celebration evening will be held at the Beijing Hotel at 7 pm on the 22nd. Invitations have been sent separately, in addition to the heads of the democratic parties, heads of government agencies and representatives of organizations, and international friends have also been invited. Important programs of the evening include the presentation of birthday gifts, congratulatory speeches, the presentation of birthday cakes, and various cultural and entertainment programs and a dance.

### 我国各民主党派联名电贺斯大林寿辰  并定明晚举行庆祝晚会

1949-12-21
第4版()
专栏：

　　我国各民主党派联名电贺斯大林寿辰
    并定明晚举行庆祝晚会
    【新华社北京二十日电】中国各民主党派顷电贺斯大林大元帅七十寿辰。电文如下：敬爱的斯大林大元帅：
    我们谨代表中华人民共和国各民主党派热烈地诚挚地向您祝贺七十大寿。在您这个光荣的宝贵的可纪念的生日，全中国人民和民主党派，正在紧密团结，扩大力量，在中国共产党和毛主席领导之下，为驱逐美帝、肃清封建残余势力，建设新民主主义的新中国和争取世界持久和平而奋斗。我们深切理解到今日中国人民解放战争的胜利，是和您所给予我们的援助和鼓励分不开的，中国将来革命事业的彻底成功，也是和您的指导和鼓励分不开的，我们谨向您致最崇高的敬礼，并祝您健康和长寿。
    中国国民党革命委员会、中国民主同盟、中国民主建国会、中国农工民主党、中国民主促进会、中国致公党、九三学社、台湾民主自治同盟
    　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　一九四九年十二月二十日
    【北京讯】中国国民党革命委员会、中国民主同盟、民主建国会、中国农工民主党、中国民主促进会、中国致公党、九三学社、台湾民主自治同盟等各民主党派，为庆祝斯大林大元帅七十寿辰，于日前在北京饭店举行座谈，决定联名致庆贺电及赠送礼物，组织联合庆祝筹备机构。自十八日起，各民主党派代表共二十余人在民主建国会会址开始工作，经决定：一、筹备会定名为“各民主党派庆祝斯大林七十寿辰筹备会”并在民主建国会集中办公；二、筹备会分总务、布置、招待、文娱等四组，由民主建国会、国民党革命委员会、民主同盟、农工民主党分别派员负责；三、庆祝晚会定二十二日下午七时在北京饭店举行。请柬已分别发出，除各民主党派负责人外，并请有政府机关首长及团体代表、国际友人。晚会的重要节目有送寿礼、致祝词、献寿糕、及各种文化娱乐节目和舞会。
