Title: Simonov and Gerasimov Depart Beijing for the Soviet Union, Taking Birthday Gifts for Stalin from Chinese Literary and Art Circles; Representatives from the Ministry of Foreign Affairs and Other Units See Them Off at the Station
Author: 
Date: 1949-12-12
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, 11th — The famous Soviet writer Konstantin Simonov and the famous director Sergei Gerasimov left Beijing by train on the evening of the 10th to return to the Soviet Union. Those who went to the station to see them off included over a hundred representatives from the Ministry of Foreign Affairs, the Ministry of Culture, the Sino-Soviet Friendship Association, the All-China Federation of Trade Unions, the All-China Democratic Women's Federation, the All-China Federation of Literary and Art Circles, and the Central Committee of the New Democratic Youth League of China. In the afternoon of the 10th, the All-China Federation of Literary and Art Circles held a farewell banquet. During the banquet, the Federation presented Simonov with a commemorative album of the national literary and art circles' celebration of Marshal Stalin's 70th birthday, requesting him to take it back to Moscow and give it to Marshal Stalin.

### 西蒙诺夫、格拉西莫夫离京返苏　带去我国文艺界献给斯大林的寿礼  外交部等单位代表赴站欢送

1949-12-12
第4版()
专栏：

　　西蒙诺夫、格拉西莫夫离京返苏
  　带去我国文艺界献给斯大林的寿礼
    外交部等单位代表赴站欢送
    【新华社北京十一日电】苏联名作家西蒙诺夫和名导演格拉西莫夫已于十日晚乘车离京返苏。到站欢送者有外交部、文化部、中苏友好协会总会、全国总工会、全国民主妇联、全国文联、中国新民主主义青年团中央委员会等单位代表百余人。十日下午全国文联曾设宴欢送，席间文联曾将全国文艺界庆祝斯大林大元帅七十寿辰的纪念册请西蒙诺夫带回莫斯科送给斯大林大元帅。
