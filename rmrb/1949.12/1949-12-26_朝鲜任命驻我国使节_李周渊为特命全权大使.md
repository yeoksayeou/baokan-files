Title: Korea Appoints Envoy to Our Country: Li Zhouyuan as Ambassador Extraordinary and Plenipotentiary
Author:
Date: 1949-12-26
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, December 25th: The Standing Committee of the Supreme People's Assembly of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea appointed Mr. Li Zhouyuan as Ambassador Extraordinary and Plenipotentiary of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea to the People's Republic of China on the 22nd. Our Central People's Government has already agreed to Mr. Li Zhouyuan's ambassadorship to China. Mr. Li Zhouyuan participated in the people's liberation movement domestically before the liberation of Korea in 1904, and was imprisoned for six years. After liberation, he served as the Minister of General Affairs of the North Korean People's Committee, then as the Chairman of the South Pyongan Province People's Committee, and later as the Chief Commercial Representative to the Soviet Union. He recently returned to Korea from the Soviet Union.

### 朝鲜任命驻我国使节  李周渊为特命全权大使

1949-12-26
第1版()
专栏：

　　朝鲜任命驻我国使节
    李周渊为特命全权大使
    【新华社北京二十五日电】朝鲜民主主义人民共和国最高人民会议常任委员会，二十二日任命李周渊先生为朝鲜民主主义人民共和国驻中华人民共和国特命全权大使。我中央人民政府对于李周渊先生的驻使中国业已同意。李周渊先生于一九○四年，朝鲜解放前在国内参加人民解放运动，入狱六年；解放以后，曾任北朝鲜人民委员会总务部部长，继任平安南道人民委员会委员长，以后任驻苏首席商务代表，最近由苏联返回朝鲜。
