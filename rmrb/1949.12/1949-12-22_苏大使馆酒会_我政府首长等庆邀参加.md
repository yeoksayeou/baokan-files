Title: Soviet Embassy Reception: Government Leaders and Others Invited to Attend Celebration

Author:

Date: 1949-12-22
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, December 21st: The Soviet Embassy in China held a grand cocktail reception at 3:30 this afternoon to celebrate the 70th birthday of Marshal Stalin. Those invited to attend included Zhu De, Liu Shaoqi, Li Jishen, and Zhang Lan, Vice Chairmen of the Central People's Government; Lin Boqu, Secretary-General of the Central People's Government; Zhou Enlai, Premier of the State Council of the Central People's Government; Chen Yun and Guo Moruo and Huang Yanpei, Vice Premiers; Shen Junru, President of the Supreme People's Court of the Central People's Government; heads of various departments of the Central People's Government; heads of various people's organizations and democratic parties; Nie Rongzhen, Mayor of Beijing; Zhang Youyu and Wu Han, Deputy Mayors; heads of various organs and people's organizations in Beijing; as well as Polish diplomatic personnel in China, Soviet experts, and overseas Chinese, totaling over a thousand people. Soviet Embassy Chargé d'Affaires S.V. Chervov and Counselor Tikhvinsky raised their glasses and shouted slogans such as "Long live Marshal Stalin!" and "Long live Chairman Mao!" Zhu De, Liu Shaoqi, Li Jishen, and Zhang Lan, Vice Chairmen of the Central People's Government, and Premier Zhou Enlai also raised their glasses and shouted, "Long live Marshal Stalin!" The reception ended at 5 o'clock.

### 苏大使馆酒会　我政府首长等庆邀参加

1949-12-22
第1版()
专栏：

　　苏大使馆酒会
    我政府首长等庆邀参加
    【新华社北京二十一日电】苏联驻我国大使馆今日下午三时半举行盛大鸡尾酒会庆祝斯大林大元帅七十寿辰。被邀请参加者计有中央人民政府副主席朱德、刘少奇、李济深、张澜中央人民政府秘书长林伯渠，中央人民政府政务院总理周恩来，副总理陈云、郭沫若、黄炎培，中央人民政府最高人民法院院长沈钧儒，中央人民政府各部门首长，各人民团体和民主党派负责人，北京市市长聂荣臻，副市长张友渔、吴晗，北京市各机关、人民团体负责人，以及波兰驻我国外交人员、苏联专家、侨民等共约千余人。苏联大使馆代办史白夫、参赞齐赫文斯基等举杯高呼：“斯大林大元帅万岁！”“毛主席万岁！”等口号。中央人民政府副主席朱德、刘少奇、李济深、张澜，和周恩来总理等亦举杯高呼：“斯大林大元帅万岁！”酒会至五时结束。
