Title: Wald, Stuk, and Others Have Been Expelled From Our Country

Author: 
Date: 1949-12-15
Page: 3

Xinhua News Agency, Tianjin, December 14th. Wald, Stuk, and other former foreign personnel of the U.S. Consulate in Shenyang, who were sentenced by the Shenyang Municipal People's Court to be expelled from China for directing espionage and assaulting Chinese workers, were escorted from Shenyang to Tianjin by personnel dispatched by the Shenyang Municipal People's Government Public Security Bureau on the 8th of this month. On the 12th, they were escorted by personnel from the Tianjin Municipal Public Security Bureau to the American ship "Lakeland Victory" at Dagukou. The convicts left Chinese territory at 12:10 PM today aboard the "Lakeland Victory".

### 瓦尔德、史笃克等已逐出我国国境

1949-12-15
第3版()
专栏：

　　瓦尔德、史笃克等已逐出我国国境
    【新华社天津十四日电】因指挥间谍和殴伤中国工人而被沈阳市人民法院判处驱逐出中国国境的前美国驻沈阳领事馆全体外籍人员瓦尔德、史笃克等，本月八日被沈阳市人民政府公安机关派员自沈押送到天津，十二日即由津市公安局派员押送到大沽口美轮“雷克兰胜利”号。该犯等已于今日十二时十分，乘“雷克兰胜利”号离开中国国境。
