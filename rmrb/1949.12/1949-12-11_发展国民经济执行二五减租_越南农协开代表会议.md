Title: Develop the National Economy, Implement the Two-Five Rent Reduction: Vietnam Farmers' Association Holds Representative Conference
Author:
Date: 1949-12-11
Page: 4

　　Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, December 10th: Vietnam News Agency, North Vietnam dispatch: The Vietnam Farmers' Association National Representative Conference was held from November 28th to December 4th. The main topics of discussion at the conference were: the development plan for the Vietnamese national economy, the implementation of the Vietnamese government's Two-Five Rent Reduction decree, and the handling of the land and property of Vietnamese traitors. Nearly 300 representatives from farmers' associations in various provinces of Vietnam attended the conference. Chen Dengke, Minister of Public Works, Wu Jin Yan, Deputy Minister of Agriculture, and several representatives from the People's United Front also participated in the conference. At the opening of the conference, letters from President Ho Chi Minh and Deputy Prime Minister Phan Van Dong were read first, calling on the representatives to assist in promoting the patriotic emulation movement.

### 发展国民经济执行二五减租　越南农协开代表会议

1949-12-11
第4版()
专栏：

　　发展国民经济执行二五减租
  　越南农协开代表会议
    【新华社北京十日电】越南通讯社北越讯：越南农民协会全国代表会议于十一月二十八日至十二月四日举行。会议讨论的主题是：越南国民经济的发展计划、越南政府规定二五减租法令的执行和越奸土地财产的处理办法。出席会议的有来自越南各省农民协会的代表近三百人。公共事业部部长陈登科、农业部副部长吴晋彦及人民统一战线代表数名也参加了会议。会议开幕时首先宣读胡志明主席和潘文登副总理的来信，来信号召各代表协助促进爱国竞赛运动。
