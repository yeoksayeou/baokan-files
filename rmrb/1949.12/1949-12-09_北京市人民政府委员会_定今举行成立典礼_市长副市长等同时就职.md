Title: Beijing Municipal People's Government Committee to Hold Inauguration Ceremony Today; Mayor, Deputy Mayors, and Others to Take Office Simultaneously
Author:
Date: 1949-12-09
Page: 1

　　Beijing Municipal People's Government Committee
  　To Hold Inauguration Ceremony Today
  　Mayor, Deputy Mayors, and Others to Take Office Simultaneously
    [Beijing News] Nie Rongzhen, elected by the Second Beijing People's Representative Conference as the Mayor of Beijing, along with Deputy Mayors Zhang Youyu, Wu Han, and members of the Beijing Municipal People's Government Committee Xue Zizheng, Mou Zexian, Luo Ruiqing, Cheng Hongyi, Wang Wenbin, Liang Sicheng, Yan Jingqing, Weng Dujian, Han Songshang, and Xu Chubo, after being nominated by the Government Administration Council and approved for appointment by the Central People's Government Committee, the Municipal Government received an order from the Government Administration Council on the 7th of this month, stating: Apart from the appointment letters being issued separately, it is hoped that the Municipal Government will immediately inform the aforementioned officials to assume their posts and duties. After receiving the order, Mayor Nie Rongzhen and others have decided to hold an inauguration ceremony at the Beijing Municipal People's Government at 9:00 AM today (the 9th). The establishment ceremony of the Beijing Municipal People's Government Committee will also be held simultaneously.

### 北京市人民政府委员会　定今举行成立典礼　市长副市长等同时就职

1949-12-09
第1版()
专栏：

　　北京市人民政府委员会
  　定今举行成立典礼
  　市长副市长等同时就职
    【北京讯】由北京市第二届各界人民代表会议选出的北京市市长聂荣臻，副市长张友渔、吴晗及北京市人民政府委员会委员薛子正、牟泽衔、罗瑞卿、程宏毅、王文斌、梁思成、严镜清、翁独健、韩诵裳、徐楚波等，经政务院提请中央人民政府委员会批准任命后，市府已于本月七日接奉政务院命令，内称：除任命通知书另行发给外，希市府即日转知上述各员到职视事。聂荣臻市长等奉令后，定于今（九）日上午九时在北京市人民政府举行就职典礼。北京市人民政府委员会成立典礼亦同时举行。
