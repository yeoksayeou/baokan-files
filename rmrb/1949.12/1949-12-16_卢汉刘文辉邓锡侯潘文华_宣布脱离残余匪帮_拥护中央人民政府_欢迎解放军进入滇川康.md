Title: Lu Han, Liu Wenhui, Deng Xihou, Pan Wenhua Announce Break from Remaining Bandit Gangs, Support the Central People's Government, Welcome the People's Liberation Army to Enter Yunnan, Sichuan, and Xikang

Author:

Date: 1949-12-16
Page: 1

　　Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, December 15th: Lu Han, former Chairman of the Yunnan Provincial Government of the Kuomintang, has revolted in Kunming on the 9th, announcing his break from the Kuomintang reactionary remnant bandit gangs, establishing the Yunnan People's Provisional Military and Political Committee, accepting the leadership of the Central People's Government, and abiding by the Eight Points of Attention of the Chinese People's Liberation Army announced by Chairman Mao and Commander-in-Chief Zhu on April 21st of this year, and the four appeals of Generals Liu Bocheng and Deng Xiaoping on November 21st, to maintain local order, protect public and private property, and await takeover. Lu Han telegraphed Liu Bocheng, Commander of the Second Field Army of the Chinese People's Liberation Army, and Political Commissar Deng Xiaoping on the 12th, requesting instructions and welcoming the People's Liberation Army to enter Yunnan as soon as possible to take over military and political affairs.
    Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, December 15th: Liu Wenhui, former Chairman of the Xikang Provincial Government of the Kuomintang, and Deng Xihou and Pan Wenhua, Deputy Commanders of the Southwest Military and Political Administration, jointly issued a telegram in Ya'an on the 9th of this month, announcing their uprising, breaking away from the Kuomintang reactionary remnant bandit gangs, and accepting the leadership of the Central People's Government. In addition to denouncing the Chiang Kai-shek bandit gang for sabotaging the resolutions of the Political Consultative Conference, launching a counter-revolutionary civil war, and promoting the bankruptcy of the national economy, the telegram expressed their sincere support for the Central People's Government, welcoming the Liberation Army to enter Sichuan and Xikang, thoroughly eliminating reactionary efforts, and ensuring the early safety of local order and the lives and property of the people in Sichuan and Xikang. The telegram also stated that in areas where the People's Liberation Army has not yet arrived, the original military and police in Sichuan and Xikang should jointly maintain public security; civil servants should adhere to their posts and serve as usual; and all schools, factories, and public and private equipment and buildings should be protected and await disposal by the People's Government.

### 卢汉刘文辉邓锡侯潘文华  宣布脱离残余匪帮  拥护中央人民政府  欢迎解放军进入滇川康

1949-12-16
第1版()
专栏：

　　卢汉刘文辉邓锡侯潘文华
    宣布脱离残余匪帮
    拥护中央人民政府
    欢迎解放军进入滇川康
    【新华社北京十五日电】前国民党云南省政府主席卢汉已于九日在昆明起义，宣布脱离国民党反动残余匪帮，成立云南人民临时军政委员会，接受中央人民政府领导，并遵照本年四月二十一日毛主席、朱总司令所宣布的人民解放军约法八章，和十一月二十一日刘伯承、邓小平二将军的四项号召，维持地方秩序，保护公私财产，听候接管。卢汉在十二日致电中国人民解放军第二野战军司令员刘伯承和政治委员邓小平请求指示，并欢迎解放军早日进入云南，接管军政。
    【新华社北京十五日电】前国民党西康省政府主席刘文辉、西南军政长官公署副长官邓锡侯、潘文华，于本月九日联名在雅安发表通电，宣布起义，脱离国民党反动残余匪帮，接受中央人民政府领导。通电中除痛斥蒋介石匪帮破坏政协决议，发动反革命内战，促成国民经济破产等种种罪行外，并表示竭诚拥护中央人民政府，欢迎解放大军进入川康，彻底扫除反动努力，使川康地方秩序与人民生命财产得以早庆安全。通电并称，在人民解放军尚未到达的地方，目前当使川康原有军警共同维持治安；文职人员谨守岗位，照常服务；并对各学校，各工厂暨公私器材及建筑物等一律加以保护，听候人民政府处理。
