Title: Central, Beijing People's Radio Tonight's Highlights
Author:
Date: 1949-12-08
Page: 3

　　Central, Beijing People's Radio
  　Tonight's Highlights
    [This newspaper reports] Central People's Broadcasting Station's special broadcast on the Asian Women's Conference tonight (8th) at 17:30: A speech by Mrs. Robertson, an American guest attending the Asian Women's Representative Conference. 18:30 Natural Science Common Sense Lecture: "Day and Night". 18:45 Broadcast in Japanese of the speech by Kim Gui-seon, Secretary-General of the Central Committee of the Democratic Women's League of North Korea and member of the Preparatory Committee for the Asian Women's Conference, broadcast on the station on December 5: "Korean Women Fight for National Reunification, Independence, and Lasting World Peace." 18:00 First Art and Literature Program: Broadcasting the Leting Drum "Examining the Witch". 21:00 Second Art and Literature Program, performed by the Amateur National Music Research Society: "National Music".
    [Another news item] Beijing People's Broadcasting Station today (7th) at 16:15 Children's Time: Telling the story of "Little Red Riding Hood's Escape"; 16:45 broadcast: (1) Introducing the "Exhibition of the Lives and Struggles of Asian Workers and Women," (2) The addresses of the newly established retail coal briquette shops of the Beijing Coal and Iron Company's branches in Inner District 1, Inner District 2, and Inner District 3, (3) Notice of telegrams claimed by the Beijing Telecommunications Bureau.
　　Xinhua News Agency Correction
    In the article "Partial Adjustment of the Central Committee of the Communist Youth League" on November 30 (published in this newspaper), after "Student Department and Secretariat," "Liaison Department" should be added under "Student Department." After "The Publishing Committee is headed by Li Geng and Yang Jun as Director and Deputy Director respectively," the sentence "A Youth Publishing House is established under it" should be added.

### 中央、北京人民电台　今晚要目

1949-12-08
第3版()
专栏：

　　中央、北京人民电台
  　今晚要目
    【本报讯】中央人民广播电台今（八）晚十七点三十分关于亚洲妇代会专题广播：由参加此次亚洲妇女代表会议的美国来宾罗伯逊夫人演讲。十八点三十分自然科学常识讲座：“昼夜”。十八点四十五分用日语播送北朝鲜民主妇女总同盟中委会秘书长、亚洲妇代会筹委金贵善十二月五号在该台广播演讲的讲词：“朝鲜妇女为祖国统一独立与世界持久和平而斗争”。十八点第一次文艺节目：播送乐亭大鼓“考神婆”。二十一点第二次文艺节目，由业余国乐研究社演播“国乐”。
    【又讯】北京市人民广播电台今（七）日十六时十五分儿童时间：讲“小红帽脱险记的故事”；十六时四十五分播送：（一）介绍“亚洲工人妇女生活和斗争展览会”，（二）北京煤铁公司分公司在内一、内二、内三三区新建立的零卖煤球铺的地址，（三）北京电信局招领电报的通知。
　　新华社补正
    十一月三十青年团中央机构部分调整一稿（载一日本报）中，“学生部和秘书处外”，“学生部”之下，应加“联络部”三字。“出版委员会由李庚和杨俊分任正副主任”之下，应加“下设青年出版社”一句。
