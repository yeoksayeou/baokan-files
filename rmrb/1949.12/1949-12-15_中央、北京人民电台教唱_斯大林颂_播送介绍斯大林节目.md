Title: Central, Beijing People's Radio Station Teach Singing "Song of Stalin", Broadcast Programs Introducing Stalin
Author:
Date: 1949-12-15
Page: 3

　　Central, Beijing People's Radio Station
  　Teach Singing "Song of Stalin"
  　Broadcast Programs Introducing Stalin
    [This newspaper's dispatch] The Central People's Broadcasting Station tonight (15th) at 17:30, Women's Time: (1) Stalin talks about women's labor issues, (2) Stalin's concern for the protection of motherhood and children's welfare. 18:30 Natural Science Knowledge Lecture: "Molybdenum and Chromium". 18:00 First Literary Program: Teach singing "Song of Stalin". 21:00 Second Literary Program: Broadcast women's songs.
    [Another dispatch] Beijing People's Radio Station 12:30 Staff Time: Housheng Match Factory Choir sings. 16:45 Listener Service: (1) Answers to several questions regarding the collection of real estate tax in this city. (2) Several unclaimed telegrams from the Beijing Telecommunications Bureau, and notices from the All-China Federation of Trade Unions seeking Russian translators, the Beijing General Hospital of the Logistics Department of the North China Military Region recruiting senior nurses, and the North China Coal Mine Management Bureau's cultural troupe recruiting members. 17:00 New Song Practice: Teach singing "Song of Stalin." 19:15 Voice of the Municipality: (1) Citizens' winter fire prevention knowledge. (2) "The Two Major Contributions of the Beijing Tram Company Repair Factory to the People's Tramway Undertaking since Liberation - The Successful Trial Production of Asbestos Brake Pads and Rectifier Fire Glass Rings".

### 中央、北京人民电台教唱“斯大林颂”　播送介绍斯大林节目

1949-12-15
第3版()
专栏：

　　中央、北京人民电台
  　教唱“斯大林颂”
  　播送介绍斯大林节目
    【本报讯】中央人民广播电台今（十五）晚十七点三十分妇女时间：（一）斯大林谈妇女劳动问题，（二）斯大林对于保护母性和儿童福利的关怀。十八点三十分自然科学常识讲座：“钼和铬”。十八点第一次文艺节目：教唱“斯大林颂”。二十一点第二次文艺节目：播送妇女歌曲。
    【又讯】北京人民电台十二点三十分职工时间：厚生火柴厂歌咏队唱歌，十六点四十五分听众服务：（一）关于征收本市房产税几项疑问的回答。（二）北京市电信局几件招领的电报，及全总征求俄文翻译、华北军区后勤部卫生部北京总院招聘高级护土、华北煤矿管理总局文工团招考团员的启事。十七点新歌练习：教唱“斯大林之歌”。十九点十五分市政之声：（一）市民冬季的防火常识。（二）“北京市电车公司修理厂解放后对人民电车事业的两大贡献——试制石绵质大闸盒和整流子火玻璃圈的成功”。
