Author:
Date: 1949-12-27
Page: 3

Shijiazhuang Egg Factory Holds Business Lectures

[This newspaper dispatch] The Shijiazhuang Yumin Industrial Company's Egg Factory, in order to help cadres become familiar with their work, master technology, and improve leadership methods, convened a cadre joint meeting on the 12th of this month and held a lecture on egg product inspection. Technicians were invited to speak on the egg product manufacturing process, finished product inspection methods, etc., and the technicians answered various technical questions raised by the cadres. After the meeting, some cadres reflected: "We should hold meetings like this more often in the future, so that we can improve our production management."

### 石家庄制蛋厂举办业务讲座

1949-12-27
第3版()
专栏：

　　石家庄制蛋厂举办业务讲座
    【本报讯】石家庄裕民实业公司制蛋厂为帮助干部熟习业务，掌握技术，改进领导方法，于本月十二日召开干部联席会，并举办蛋品检验讲座，请技师讲蛋品制造过程、成品检验方法等，并由技师解答干部提出的技术上的各种问题。会后有的干部反映：“这样的会以后多开几次，好改进我们的生产管理。”
