Title: The Suiyuan Branch of the People's Bank of China is Established; Begins to Redeem Counterfeit Silver Yuan Notes
Author:
Date: 1949-12-14
Page: 2

　　The Suiyuan Branch of the People's Bank of China is Established
　　Begins to Redeem Counterfeit Silver Yuan Notes
　　[Report from this newspaper] The Suiyuan Branch of the People's Bank of China was officially established on November 26th. The former Suiyuan Provincial Bank has ceased operations. It is now in the process of consolidating the currency circulating in the local market, announcing the Renminbi as the standard currency, prohibiting the circulation of silver yuan, and redeeming the silver yuan notes issued by the former Suiyuan Provincial Bank. This work has already been launched simultaneously in Gui Sui, Wuyuan, Baotou, Shanba and other places.

### 中国人民银行绥远分行成立　开始收兑伪银元券

1949-12-14
第2版()
专栏：

　　中国人民银行绥远分行成立
  　开始收兑伪银元券
    【本报讯】中国人民银行绥远分行于十一月二十六日正式成立。原绥远省银行已停止营业。现正对当地市场流通的货币加以整理，宣布以人民币为本位币，禁止银元流通，收兑前绥远省银行发行的银元券。此项工作已同时在归绥、五原、包头、陕坝等地展开。
