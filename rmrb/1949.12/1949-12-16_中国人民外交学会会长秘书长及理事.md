Title: President, Secretary-General, and Board Members of the Chinese People's Institute of Foreign Affairs
Author:
Date: 1949-12-16

Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, December 15th. The list of the President and Board Members of the Chinese People's Institute of Foreign Affairs is as follows:
Honorary President: Zhou Enlai
President: Zhang Xiruo
Vice-Presidents: Zhou Gengsheng, Hu Yuzhi, Qian Duansheng, Qiao Guanhua
Secretary-General: Wu Maosun
Board Members: Wang Bingnan, Wang Tieya, Shen Duanxian, He Sijing, Shao Lizi, Jin Zhonghua, Ke Bonian, Xu Yonghuan, Huan Xiang, Weng Dujian, Zhang Hanfu, Huang Qixiang, Fei Xiaotong, Fei Qing, Zhang Dongsun, Zhang Mingyang, Zhang Bo, Lu Cui, Yang Gang, Liao Chengzhi, Liu Ningyi, Fan Hong, Lai Yali, Qian Junrui, Ji Chaoding, Luo Longji, Gong Peng
