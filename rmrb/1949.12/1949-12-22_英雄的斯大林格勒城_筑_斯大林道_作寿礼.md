Title: Heroic Stalingrad Builds "Stalin Avenue" as Birthday Gift
Author:
Date: 1949-12-22
Page: 2

Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, December 21st: The Telegraph Agency of the Soviet Union (TASS), Moscow, December 20th: The heroic city of Stalingrad is currently constructing a major avenue, which will be named "Stalin Avenue," as a tribute to Stalin's birthday. Stalin Avenue will extend for dozens of kilometers, traversing the entire new urban area. Trees will be planted on both sides, and sculptures and fountains will be installed. The avenue will be brightly lit at night. Many work teams are working enthusiastically to complete the construction of Stalin Avenue in record time.

### 英雄的斯大林格勒城　筑“斯大林道”作寿礼

1949-12-22
第2版()
专栏：

　　英雄的斯大林格勒城
    筑“斯大林道”作寿礼
    【新华社北京二十一日电】电通社莫斯科二十日讯：英雄的斯大林格勒城，正在从事一条大街的建筑，这条大街将命名为“斯大林大道”，作为对斯大林寿辰的献礼。斯大林大道将延伸数十公里，跨越所有新建筑的市区。两旁将种植树木，并设置塑像、喷泉。晚上将灯光辉煌。许多工作队正在热情地工作着，俾能在打破纪录的时间内完成斯大林大道的建筑。
