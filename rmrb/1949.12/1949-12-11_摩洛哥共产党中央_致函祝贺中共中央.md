Title: Moroccan Communist Party Central Committee Sends Letter of Congratulations to the CCP Central Committee
Author:
Date: 1949-12-11
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Prague, December 10th: The secretaries of the Central Committee of the Moroccan Communist Party, Ali Yata, Adeslan Bourya, Mohammed Kaui, and Henri Lafer, sent a letter to the CCP Central Committee on October 15th, expressing their congratulations. The letter is as follows:

Dear Comrades,

The Central Committee of the Moroccan Communist Party warmly congratulates the establishment of the People's Republic of China, which marks the victory of the unity of progressive forces in the country under the brilliant leadership of the Communist Party and its Central Committee, and the visionary leadership of Chairman Mao Zedong. The establishment of the People's Republic of China represents a new strengthening of the democratic and peaceful camp and the end of the enslavement of China by Anglo-American imperialism. It opens up a century of freedom and progress for the Chinese people. The establishment of the People's Republic of China is a valuable education and encouragement to the people of dependent countries and colonies, especially to the Moroccan people, in developing their struggle for national liberation.

Long live the People's Republic of China!

On behalf of the Secretaries of the Central Committee of the Moroccan Communist Party,
Ali Yata Adeslan Bourya
Mohammed Kaui Henri Lafer

### 摩洛哥共产党中央　致函祝贺中共中央

1949-12-11
第4版()
专栏：

　　摩洛哥共产党中央
  　致函祝贺中共中央
    【新华社布拉格十日电】摩洛哥共产党中央委员会书记阿里·雅塔，阿代斯兰·蒲尔雅，麦罕默德·卡乌意和亨利·拉法，于十月十五日致函中共中央，表示祝贺，该函如下：亲爱的同志们：
    摩洛哥共产党中央委员会热烈祝贺中华人民共和国的成立，它标志着国内进步力量在光辉的共产党及其中央委员会和毛泽东主席的有远见的领导下团结的胜利。中华人民共和国的成立，表示民主和平阵营新的加强，是美英帝国主义奴役中国的终结，它给中国人民开辟了一个自由进步的世纪。中华人民共和国的成立，对于附属国和殖民地人民，特别是对摩洛哥人民在发展其为争取民族解放的斗争中，是一个宝贵的教育和鼓励。
    中华人民共和国万岁！
    代表摩洛哥共产党中央委员会书记
    阿里·雅塔　阿代斯兰·蒲尔雅
    麦罕默德·卡乌意　亨利·拉法
