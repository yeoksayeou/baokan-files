Title: Asian-Australasian Trade Union Representatives Arrive in Tianjin for Visit; Representatives from All Sectors Hold Grand Welcome Ceremony
Author:
Date: 1949-12-07
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Tianjin, December 6th: More than a thousand representatives from Tianjin's enterprise workers, government organs, organizations, and military units gathered at 11:00 AM on the 6th to welcome the visiting representatives from fraternal trade unions of Asian and Australasian countries. After Tianjin Municipal People's Government Mayor Huang Jing and Tianjin Federation of Trade Unions Preparatory Committee Director Huang Huoqing delivered welcoming speeches, the representatives attending the Asian-Australasian Trade Union Conference, including Korean representative Choi Kyung-deok, Mongolian representative Adli Bish, Indonesian representative Majono, Vietnamese representative Liu Defu, Indian representative Shelvankar, and Ceylonese representative Wickramasinghe, spoke successively. They unanimously stated that the victory of the Chinese people's revolution has given the working class of the oppressed nations of Asia and Australasia infinite encouragement. It has also strengthened the unity of the people of all countries to fight to the end against imperialist aggression and for national independence. The representatives' impassioned and excited words were met with warm applause from the representatives of various government organs and organizations present. At the welcoming ceremony, representatives from the Tianjin Federation of Trade Unions and various factories presented flowers, commemorative albums, commemorative badges, fabrics, and clay figurines to the national trade union representatives.

### 亚澳工会代表抵津参观　各界代表盛会欢迎

1949-12-07
第1版()
专栏：

　　亚澳工会代表抵津参观
  　各界代表盛会欢迎
    【新华社天津六日电】天津市各企业职工及机关、团体、部队代表等一千余人，六日上午十一时集会，欢迎来津参观的亚澳各国兄弟工会的代表们。津市人民政府黄敬市长，市总工会筹委会黄火青主任致欢迎词后，出席亚澳工会会议的朝鲜代表崔璟德、蒙古代表阿德利比什、印度尼西亚代表玛佐诺、越南代表刘德福、印度代表谢尔凡卡、锡兰代表魏克马沁等相继讲话，一致表示中国人民革命的胜利给亚澳被压迫国家的工人阶级以无限的鼓舞，同时也加强了各国人民的团结，一致为反对帝国主义的侵略与争取民族独立而斗争到底。代表们激昂兴奋的语句，与到会各机关团体代表们热烈的鼓掌相应。在欢迎会上，津总工会及各厂的代表们向国工会代表献花并赠送纪念册、纪念章、布料及泥人等。
