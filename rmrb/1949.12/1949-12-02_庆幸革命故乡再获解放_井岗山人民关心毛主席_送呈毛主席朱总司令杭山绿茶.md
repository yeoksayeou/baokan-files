Title: Celebrating the Re-Liberation of the Revolutionary Homeland: Jinggangshan People Care for Chairman Mao and Send Hangshan Green Tea to Chairman Mao and Commander-in-Chief Zhu
Author:
Date: 1949-12-02
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Nanchang, 1st: After a certain unit of the People's Liberation Army captured the bandit chief Xiao Jiabi, who was entrenched in Jinggangshan, in late September and liberated the revolutionary homeland of Jinggangshan area, the people of the area, rejoicing in their re-liberation, inquired with great concern about the health of Chairman Mao and Commander-in-Chief Zhu, and sent Hangshan green tea, a famous local product of Jinggangshan, to the Central China Military Region to be forwarded to Chairman Mao and Commander-in-Chief Zhu. Lin Biao, commander of the Central China Military Region, and Deng Zihui, political commissar, sent a special telegram to the People's Liberation Army in the area on the 6th of this month and forwarded it to the people of Jinggangshan, informing them that the Hangshan green tea had been forwarded to Chairman Mao and Commander-in-Chief Zhu, and extended their regards and encouragement.

### 庆幸革命故乡再获解放　井岗山人民关心毛主席  送呈毛主席朱总司令杭山绿茶

1949-12-02
第1版()
专栏：

　　庆幸革命故乡再获解放
  　井岗山人民关心毛主席
    送呈毛主席朱总司令杭山绿茶
    【新华社南昌一日电】人民解放军某部于九月底生擒盘踞井岗山的匪首肖家璧，解放革命故乡井岗山地区后，庆幸再获解放的该地人民非常关心地纷纷询问毛主席和朱总司令的健康，并以井岗山著名土产杭山绿茶送华中军区转呈毛主席和朱总司令。华中军区林彪司令员和邓子恢政委于本月六日特致电该地解放军并转井岗山人民，告以杭山绿茶已转呈毛主席朱总司令，并予以慰问和勉励。
