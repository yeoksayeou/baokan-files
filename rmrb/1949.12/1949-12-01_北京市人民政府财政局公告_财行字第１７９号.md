Announcement of the Beijing Municipal People's Government Finance Bureau, Cai Xing Zi No. 179

　　I. The autumn real estate tax for this year has already begun to be collected, and has been announced by the Municipal Government.

　　II. To facilitate taxpayers' payments, it has been decided to entrust various banks and post offices to collect the tax by district. The payment locations are hereby listed and announced for compliance.

　　Attached is a list of the names and addresses of the autumn real estate tax collection locations.

　　Director Zhao Zishang
