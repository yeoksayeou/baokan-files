Title: Central, Beijing People's Radio Today's Broadcast Schedule
Author:
Date: 1949-12-14
Page: 3

　　[This Newspaper] Central, Beijing People's Radio Today's Broadcast Schedule
    [This Newspaper] The Central People's Broadcasting Station will broadcast a lecture on common sense of natural science at 6:30 PM this evening (the 14th): "From Cement to China's Cement Industry." The first literary program at 6:00 PM: Xiang Yanqiu will perform the Beijing drum song "Wang Yinsheng Leads the Way to Take Beifeng." The second literary program at 9:00 PM: Broadcasting Soviet music.
    [Also News] Beijing People's Radio, 12:30 PM, Employee Time: Xu Ping will speak on "The Work of the Handicraft Workers' Union Working Committee in the Past Nine Months." 4:15 PM, Children's Time: Sun Jingxiu continues to tell the Soviet fairy tale "Anahit." 4:40 PM, Youth Time: The recorded speech of Li Yaqun at the Youth League Middle School Propaganda and Education Cadre Business Study Meeting, entitled "School Wall Newspapers and Communication Work." 5:00 PM, Music Program: Huiwen Middle School Choir sings new songs.

### 中央、北京人民电台今日广播要目

1949-12-14
第3版()
专栏：

　　中央、北京人民电台今日广播要目
    【本报讯】中央人民广播电台今（十四）晚十八点三十分自然科学常识讲座：“从水泥谈到我国的水泥工业”。十八点第一次文艺节目：由香艳秋演唱京韵大鼓“王银生带路取北峰”。二十一点第二次文艺节目：播送苏联音乐。
    【又讯】北京人民电台十二点三十分职工时间：许平讲“手工业工会工作委员会九个月来的工作情况”。十六点十五分儿童时间：孙敬修继续讲苏联童话“阿娜依特”的故事。十六点四十分青年时间：李亚群在青年团中学宣教干部业务学习会上的讲演记录，题目“学校壁报和通讯工作”。十七点音乐节目：汇文中学歌咏团演唱新歌曲。
