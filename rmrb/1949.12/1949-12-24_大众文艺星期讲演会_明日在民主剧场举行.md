Title: Mass Literature and Art Weekly Lecture to be Held at Democracy Theater Tomorrow
Author:
Date: 1949-12-24
Page: 3

　　Mass Literature and Art Weekly Lecture
    To be Held at Democracy Theater Tomorrow
    [Beijing News] The Mass Literature and Art Weekly Lecture, organized by the Beijing Mass Literature and Art Creation Research Association, will be held at the Democracy Theater this Sunday (the 25th) at 9:30 a.m. Miao Peishi will be invited to speak on: "How to Write Popular Literature and Art". Cao Baolu's single-string "Good Couple" will also perform to add to the fun.

### 大众文艺星期讲演会　明日在民主剧场举行

1949-12-24
第3版()
专栏：

　　大众文艺星期讲演会
    明日在民主剧场举行
    【北京讯】北京市大众文艺创作研究会举办的大众文艺星期讲演会，定于本星期日（二十五日）上午九时半在民主剧场举行，邀请苗培时讲：“通俗文艺如何写”。并有曹宝录单弦“好夫妻”助兴。
