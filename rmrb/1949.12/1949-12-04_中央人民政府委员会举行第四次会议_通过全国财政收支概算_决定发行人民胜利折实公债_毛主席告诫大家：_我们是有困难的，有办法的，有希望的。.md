Title: The Central People's Government Committee Holds its Fourth Meeting, Approves the National Budget of Revenue and Expenditure, and Decides to Issue the People's Victory Discounted Public Bonds. Chairman Mao Admonishes Everyone: We have difficulties, we have solutions, and we have hope.
Author:
Date: 1949-12-04
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, December 3rd. The Central People's Government Committee held its fourth meeting yesterday afternoon at 7:00 PM, unanimously passing the 1950 National Budget of Revenue and Expenditure and the decision on issuing the People's Victory Discounted Public Bonds. Other results of the meeting were: Passage of the organizational rules for People's Representative Conferences at the municipal, county, and provincial levels; Passage of the organizational rules for the Government Administration Council and its affiliated organs; Passage of twenty-six appointments; and Passage of the resolution on National Day.

The first item on the meeting's agenda was the issue of the 1950 National Budget of Revenue and Expenditure and the issuance of the People's Victory Discounted Public Bonds. Before discussing this issue, Vice Premier Chen Yun of the Government Administration Council gave a report on the issue of commodity prices and the issuance of public bonds, and Bo Yibo, Vice Chairman of the Financial and Economic Committee of the Government Administration Council and Minister of Finance, gave a report on the compilation of the 1950 National Budget of Revenue and Expenditure. Then, Li Fuchun, Vice Chairman of the Northeast People's Government, who attended the meeting as a non-voting delegate, gave a report on the Northeast Financial and Economic Plan for 1950, and Committee members Rao Shushi and Deng Zihui gave reports on the work situation in the East China Region and the Central South China Region, respectively.

### 中央人民政府委员会举行第四次会议  通过全国财政收支概算  决定发行人民胜利折实公债  毛主席告诫大家：  我们是有困难的，有办法的，有希望的。

1949-12-04
第1版()
专栏：

　　中央人民政府委员会举行第四次会议
    通过全国财政收支概算     
    决定发行人民胜利折实公债
    毛主席告诫大家：我们是有困难的，有办法的，有希望的。
    【新华社北京三日电】中央人民政府委员会昨天下午七时举行第四次会议，一致通过了一九五零年度全国财政收支概算和关于发行人民胜利折实公债的决定。会议的其他结果是：通过市的、县的和省的各界人民代表会议的组织通则；通过政务院及其所属各机关的组织通则；通过二十六项任命；通过关于国庆日的决议。
    会议的第一项议程是关于一九五零年度全国财政收支概算和发行人民胜利折实公债问题。在讨论这个问题以前，政务院阵云副总理就物价问题和发行公债问题，政务院财政经济委员会薄一波副主任兼财政部长就一九五零年度全国财政收支概算的编制分别作了报告。然后由列席会议的东北人民政府李富春副主席作了关于一九五零年东北财政经济计划的报告，饶漱石委员和邓子恢委员作了关于华东区工作情况和华中南区工作情况的报告。
