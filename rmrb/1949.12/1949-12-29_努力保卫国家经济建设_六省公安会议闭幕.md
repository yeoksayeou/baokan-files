Title: Strive to Defend National Economic Construction: Six-Province Public Security Conference Concludes
Author:
Date: 1949-12-29
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, December 28th:

　　Strive to Defend National Economic Construction
　  Six-Province Public Security Conference Concludes
    After days of group and plenary discussions, the Public Security Conference of Six Provinces in North China and Shandong concluded on the 26th. Yang Qiqing, Deputy Minister of Public Security of the Central People's Government, pointed out in his summary: The public security work in the North China region has been successful, and the tasks of the September conference last year have been basically completed. This is mainly reflected in: First, the destruction of secret agent organizations, the establishment of public security, and the resettlement of most of the large number of people returning to their hometowns after the liberation of Tianjin and Beijing. Second, the strengthening and establishment of public security organizations at all levels, the transfer of a large number of cadres to support the national liberation (according to incomplete statistics, more than 2,000 public security cadres above the district level were transferred externally), and the gradual replenishment of vacancies in organizations at all levels, the establishment of organizations in the liberated areas, and the restoration and establishment of organizations have reached more than 70 or 80 percent. Third, opposition to disorganization and lack of discipline, and the establishment of a system of requesting instructions and reporting. Minister Yang put forward the guidelines and tasks of public security work for the coming year, saying: The guidelines and tasks for next year should be to defend production and construction, consolidate the revolutionary order, maintain public security, and create better conditions for production and construction. After analyzing the activities of enemy agents, he emphasized: The armed enemy has been defeated, and secret agents dispatched by reactionary forces at home and abroad are carrying out activities in a more covert manner, especially sabotaging economic construction and taking assassination measures. He called on public security cadres to be highly vigilant and not to be paralyzed. Finally, Vice Chairman Zhu De of the Central People's Government came to speak, encouraging the cadres present to work hard and defend national economic construction. During the meeting, Peng Zhen, Deputy Director of the Political and Legal Affairs Committee of the State Council, and Bo Yibo, Political Commissar of the North China Military Region, also attended the meeting and gave detailed instructions on public security work under the new situation.

### 努力保卫国家经济建设　六省公安会议闭幕

1949-12-29
第1版()
专栏：

　　努力保卫国家经济建设
　  六省公安会议闭幕
    【新华社北京二十八日电】华北山东六省公安会议经连日小组与大会讨论后，已于二十六日闭会。中央人民政府公安部副部长杨奇清在总结时指出：华北区所来公安工作是有成绩的，基本上完成了去年九月会议的任务。主要表现在：一、摧毁了特务组织，确立了治安，安置了平津解放后大批还乡人员的大部。二、加强与建立各级公安组织，为支援全国解放而调出大批干部（据不完全统计，仅外调区以上公安干部两千余人），并逐渐补充各级组织的缺额，建立了解放区的组织，恢复与建立了组织已达百分之七、八十以上。三、反对无组织无纪律，建立了请示报告制度。杨制部长提出明年公安工作的方针与任务，他说：明年的方针与任务应是保卫生产建设，巩固革命秩序，维持治安，给生产建设创造更好的条件。他分析了敌特活动的情况后强调指出：武装敌人已被打倒，国内外反动派派遣的特务正以更荫蔽的方式进行活动，特别是破坏经济建设与采取暗杀手段。他号召公安干部必须高度警惕，不可麻痹。最后，中央人民政府朱德副主席亲临讲话，勉励到会干部努力工作，保卫国家经济建设。会议期间政务院政法委员会彭真副主任及华北军区薄一波政委均莅会讲话，对新形势下的公安工作作了详尽的指示。
