Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, December 4th:

　　Organizational Regulations for People's Representative Conferences at the Municipal, County, and Provincial Levels and for Organs of the Government Administration Council
    Organizational Regulations for People's Representative Conferences at the Municipal Level

    Article 1: The Municipal People's Representative Conference of All Circles shall be convened by the Municipal Military Control Commission (hereinafter referred to as the Military Control Commission) and the Municipal People's Government. Cities without a Military Control Commission shall be convened by the Municipal People's Government.

    Article 2: The number of representatives to the Municipal People's Representative Conference of All Circles shall be: approximately 200 to 500 representatives for cities with a population of 200,000 or more; approximately 100 to 200 representatives for cities with a population of 30,000 or more.

    Article 3: Qualifications of representatives to the Municipal People's Representative Conference of All Circles: All people who oppose imperialism, feudalism, and bureaucratic capitalism, endorse the Common Program, and are 18 years of age or older, regardless of ethnicity, class, gender, or belief, except for those suffering from mental illness or deprived of their civil rights, are eligible to be elected as representatives.

    Article 4: The election of representatives to the Municipal People's Representative Conference of All Circles: The participating units and the allocation of representative quotas for the Municipal People's Representative Conference of All Circles shall be jointly determined by the Military Control Commission, the Municipal People's Government, and the Consultative Committee of the previous Municipal People's Representative Conference of All Circles (hereinafter referred to as the Consultative Committee). Cities without a Consultative Committee shall be decided by the Military Control Commission and the Municipal People's Government.

    Representatives of the Military Control Commission and the Municipal People's Government shall be filled by the Director and Deputy Directors of the Military Control Commission, and the Mayor and Deputy Mayors of the Municipal People's Government. Representatives of the various democratic parties, people's organizations, agencies and troops stationed in the city shall be elected and sent by the respective parties, organizations, agencies, and troops. Representatives from other fields shall be invited by the Military Control Commission and the Municipal People's Government in consultation with the Consultative Committee; or invited by the Military Control Commission and the Municipal People's Government through consultation.

    Article 5: Term of office of representatives to the Municipal People's Representative Conference of All Circles: Before the Municipal People's Representative Conference of All Circles exercises the functions and powers of the Municipal People's Congress, representatives may be re-elected each time, but may be re-elected for consecutive terms; from the time it exercises the functions and powers of the Municipal People's Congress, the term of office of each People's Representative Conference shall be one year, and they may be re-elected for consecutive terms. Various democratic parties, people's organizations, agencies and troops may replace their representatives after consultation with the Consultative Committee; invited representatives may also be replaced after consultation with the Consultative Committee. In cities where a Consultative Committee has not been established, the Municipal People's Government shall decide on the replacement.

    Article 6: The functions and powers of the Municipal People's Representative Conference of All Circles: In the initial stage of military control, the People's Representative Conference of All Circles is a consultative body for the Military Control Commission and the Municipal People's Government to convey policies and connect with the masses. Its functions and powers are:

    One, to listen to reports from the Military Control Commission and the Municipal People's Government on policy guidelines, policies, plans, and work progress, and to discuss them, and offer criticisms and suggestions.

    Two, to reflect the opinions and demands of the people to the Military Control Commission and the Municipal People's Government, and to suggest matters related to municipal reforms.

    Three, to convey and explain the resolutions of the Municipal People's Representative Conference of All Circles to the people, and to assist the Municipal People's Government in mobilizing the people to implement them.

    Article 7: With the approval of the People's Government at the directly subordinate higher level, the Municipal People's Representative Conference of All Circles may exercise the following functions and powers of the People's Congress:

    One, to listen to and review the work report of the Municipal People's Government, and to decide on the municipal guidelines and policies.

    Two, to review and approve the municipal government's budget and final accounts.

    Three, to suggest and resolve matters related to municipal reforms.

    Four, to convey and explain the resolutions of the Municipal People's Representative Conference of All Circles to the people, and to assist the Municipal People's Government in mobilizing the people to implement them.

    Five, to elect the Mayor, Deputy Mayors, and committee members of the Municipal People's Government, and to form the Municipal People's Government Committee.

    Article 8: If the resolutions of the Municipal People's Government Representative Conference conflict with the policies and laws of the higher-level People's Government, the higher-level People's Government may abolish, amend, or suspend their implementation.

    Article 9: The Municipal People's Representative Conference of All Circles shall establish a presidium, composed of several chairpersons elected by the conference, responsible for presiding over the proceedings of the conference; a secretary-general and several deputy secretaries-general shall be appointed by the presidium and approved by the conference to assist the chairperson in handling the daily affairs of the conference.

    The Municipal People's Representative Conference of All Circles may establish a proposal review committee and other committees.

    Article 10: During the adjournment of the Municipal People's Representative Conference of All Circles, a Consultative Committee of the Municipal People's Representative Conference of All Circles shall be established, composed of one chairperson, several vice-chairpersons, and several committee members elected by the Municipal People's Representative Conference of All Circles; its functions and powers are:

    One, to assist the Municipal People's Government in implementing the resolutions of the Municipal People's Representative Conference of All Circles.

    Two, to consult and propose suggestions to the Municipal People's Government.

    Three, to assist the Municipal People's Government in mobilizing the people to support the front lines, establish revolutionary order, suppress counter-revolutionaries, and participate in construction work.

    Four, to be responsible for the preparatory work for the next People's Representative Conference of All Circles.

    Five, to be responsible for the democratic united front work in the city.

    Article 11: The Consultative Committee shall be convened and presided over by the chairperson. The Consultative Committee shall appoint one secretary-general and several deputy secretaries-general to assist the chairperson and vice-chairpersons in their work.

    The Consultative Committee may establish various committees as needed, absorbing members of the Consultative Committee and representatives of the Municipal People's Representative Conference of All Circles to participate in the work. Each committee shall be presided over by a director appointed by the Consultative Committee.

    Article 12: The Municipal People's Representative Conference of All Circles shall be convened once every three months, but may be convened earlier or later if proposed by the Municipal People's Government or one-third of the representatives of the People's Representative Conference of All Circles, or when the Consultative Committee deems it necessary.

    The Consultative Committee shall be convened once a month, and may also be convened earlier or later when necessary.

### 市、县、省各界人民代表会议及政务院各机关组织通则   市各界人民代表会议组织通则

1949-12-05
第3版()
专栏：

　　市、县、省各界人民代表会议及政务院各机关组织通则         
    市各界人民代表会议组织通则
    【新华社北京四日电】市各界人民代表会议组织通则一九四九年十二月二日中央人民政府委员会第四次会议通过
    第一条　市各界人民代表会议由市军事管制委员会（以下简称军管会）及市人民政府召开之。未设立军管会的市由市人民政府召开之。
    第二条　市各界人民代表会议的代表的代表名额：二十万人口以上的市，二百人至五百人左右；三万人口以上的市，一百人至二百人左右。
    第三条　市各界人民代表会议代表资格：凡反对帝国主义、封建主义、官僚资本主义，赞成共同纲领，年满十八岁之人民，除患精神病及褫夺公权者外，不分民族、阶级、性别、信仰，均得当选为代表。
    第四条　市各界人民代表会议代表的产生：市各界人民代表会议的参加单位及代表名额之分配，由军管会、市人民政府和上届市各界人民代表会议协商委员会（以下简称协商委员会）共同商定之。尚未成立协商委员会之市，则由军管会和市人民政府决定之。
    军管会、市人民政府的代表由军管会主任、副主任、市人民政府市长、副市长等充任之。各民主党派、各人民团体、驻在该市的各机关及部队的代表由各党派、各团体、各机关及部队自行选派之。其他方面的代表经军管会、市人民政府和协商委员会商定，由军管会、市人民政府邀请之；或由军管会、市人民政府商定邀请之。
    第五条　市各界人民代表会议代表的任期：市各界人民代表会议未代行市人民代表大会职权以前，可每次推选，但连选得连任；从代行市人民代表大会职权时起，每届人民代表会议的任期为一年，连选得连任。各民主党派、人民团体、机关及部队经与协商委员会商定，均得更换其代表；被邀请的代表，经协商委员会商定，亦得更换之。未成立协商委员会之市，由市人民政府商定更换之。
    第六条　市各界人民代表会议的职权：在军事管制初期，各界人民代表会议是军管会和市人民政府传达政策、联系群众的协议机关，其职权：
    一、听取军管会及市人民政府关于施政方针、政策、计划及工作情况之报告，并进行讨论，提出批评和建议。
    二、向军管会及市人民政府反映人民的意见和要求，建议有关市政兴革事宜。
    三、向人民传达并解释市各界人民代表会议的决议案，并协助市人民政府动员人民推行之。
    第七条　市各界人民代表会议经直属上级人民政府批准，可代行人民代表大会的如下的职权：
    一、听取与审查市人民政府的工作报告，决定市的施政方针和政策。
    二、审查与通过市人民政府的预决算。
    三、建议与决议有关市政兴革事宜。
    四、向人民传达并解释市各界人民代表会议的决议案，并协助市人民政府动员人民推行之。
    五、选举市人民政府市长、副市长、委员，组成市人民政府委员会。
    第八条　市各界人民政府代表会议的决议，有与上级人民政府的政策法令抵触时，上级人民政府得废除、修改或停止其执行。
    第九条　市各界人民代表会议设主席团，由大会选举主席若干人组成之，负责主持会议的进行；设秘书长一人，副秘书长若干人，由主席团提请大会通过之，协助主席处理会议日常事务。
    市各界人民代表会议得设提案审查委员会及其他委员会。
    第十条　市各界人民代表会议休会期间，设市各界人民代表会议协商委员会，由市各界人民代表会议选举主席一人，副主席若干人及委员若干人组成之；其职权：
    一、协助市人民政府实行市各界人民代表会议的决议。
    二、协商并提出对市人民政府的建议。
    三、协助市人民政府动员人民支援前线，建立革命秩序，镇压反革命并参加建设工作。
    四、负责进行下届各界人民代表会议的准备工作。
    五、负责进行本市民主统一战线工作。
    第十一条　协商委员会由主席负责召集并主持会议。协商委员会推定秘书长一人，副秘书长若干人，协助主席、副主席进行工作。
    协商委员会根据需要得设各种委员会，吸收协商委员会委员及市各界人民代表会议的代表参加工作，各委员会由协商委员会分别推定主任一人负责主持之。
    第十二条　市各界人民代表会议每三个月召开一次，但经市人民政府或各界人民代表会议三分之一代表之提议，或协商委员会认为需要时，得提前或延期召集之。
    协商委员会每月召集一次，必要时亦得提前或延期召集之。
