Title: Central, Beijing People's Radio Today's Broadcast Highlights
Author:
Date: 1949-12-13
Page: 3

[This Newspaper Reported] Central People's Broadcasting Station today (13th) 17:30 Staff Program: How were the two "Sino-Soviet Friendship" locomotives repaired? 18:30 Regular Lectures on Natural Science: Clouds, Fog, Rain, Snow, and Hail. 18:00 First Literary and Art Program: Leting Drum "Mother Ma Investigates the Spy". 21:00 Second Literary and Art Program: Opera "Wang Xiuluan".

[Another Dispatch] Beijing People's Radio 12:30 Staff Time: "We Improved Our Work and Saved Millet for the People". 16:15 Children's Time: Children from the Central National Elementary School in the Seventh District sing songs and introduce the school's "Good Student Movement". 16:45 Women's Time: "The situation of the representatives of various countries attending the Asian Women's Representative Conference meeting with sisters in this city." 17:00 New Song Practice: Continue teaching "Forward, Sisters!" 19:15 Voice of the City: (1) Regarding the use of urban telephones and matters that should be paid attention to, (2) Explanation by the Finance Bureau on several issues concerning the collection of real estate tax.

### 中央、北京人民电台今日广播要目

1949-12-13
第3版()
专栏：

　　中央、北京人民电台今日广播要目
    【本报讯】中央人民广播电台本日（十三）十七点三十分职工节目：两台“中苏友好号”机车是怎样修成的？十八点三十分自然科学常职讲座：云、雾、雨、雪、和冰雹。十八点第一次文艺节目：乐亭大鼓“马大娘探子”。二十一点第二次文艺节目；歌剧“王秀鸾”。
    【又讯】北京人民电台十二点三十分职工时间：“我们改进了工作，并且为人民节省了小米”。十六点十五分儿童时间：七区中心国民小学小朋友们唱歌，并介绍该校的“好学生运动”。十六点四十五分妇女时间：“出席亚洲妇女代表会议的各国代表和本市姐妹们见面的情形”。十七点新歌练习：继续教唱“前进吧！姊妹们！”十九点十五分市政之声：（一）关于市内电话的使用方法和应当注意的事项，（二）财政局“关于征收房产税的几个问题的解释”。
