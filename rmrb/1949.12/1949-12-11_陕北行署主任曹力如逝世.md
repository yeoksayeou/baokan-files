Title: Shaanbei Administrative Office Director Cao Liru Passes Away
Author:
Date: 1949-12-11
Page: 1

　　Shaanbei Administrative Office Director Cao Liru Passes Away
    [Xinhua News Agency, Xi'an, 10th] Cao Liru, Director of the Shaanbei Administrative Office, passed away at Guangren Hospital in this city at 5:00 PM on the 8th after suffering severe injuries when the car he was traveling in from Xi'an to Lintong on official business overturned near Baqiao. News of his death has been met with great sorrow by people from all walks of life in the Northwest Party, government, military, and civilian sectors, as well as by Cao's friends. A funeral committee has now been formed, consisting of twenty-one people including Xi Zhongxun, Wang Weizhou, Liu Jingfan, Jia Tuofu, Wang Ziyi, and Ma Xiwu. The Shaanxi-Gansu-Ningxia Border Region Government and the Northwest Bureau of the CPC Central Committee have each sent telegrams of condolence to Cao's family.

### 陕北行署主任曹力如逝世

1949-12-11
第1版()
专栏：

　　陕北行署主任曹力如逝世
    【新华社西安十日电】陕北行署主任曹力如八日因公由西安市乘汽车赴临潼，返抵坝桥附近时，因汽车颠覆，身受重伤，于当日下午五时在本市广仁医院逝世。西北党、政、军、民各界及曹氏生前好友闻讯极为痛悼。现已由习仲勋、王维舟、刘景范、贾拓夫、王子宜、马锡五等二十一人组成治丧委员会。陕甘宁边区政府及中共中央西北局已分别致电曹氏家属吊唁。
