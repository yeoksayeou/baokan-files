Title: Yuehan Railway Expected to Be Fully Repaired by End of Month; Wuchang-Chenxian and Guangzhou-Qujiang Sections Now Open to Traffic
Author:
Date: 1949-12-01
Page: 1
　　
　　Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, November 30th: The 715-kilometer section of the Yuehan Railway between Wuchang and Chenxian in Hunan Province has been repaired and is open to traffic. Formal passenger and freight operations will begin on December 1st. The 225-kilometer southern section of the line between Guangzhou and Qujiang also began passenger and freight operations on November 27th. Currently, only the Chenxian-Qujiang section of the Yuehan Railway remains to be repaired. It is expected that the entire line will be open to traffic by the end of December.

### 粤汉全线月底可修通　武昌郴县间广州曲江间现已通车

1949-12-01
第1版()
专栏：

　　粤汉全线月底可修通
  　武昌郴县间广州曲江间现已通车
    【新华社北京三十日电】粤汉铁路武昌至湖南郴县间七百十五公里已修复通车，十二月一日起正式办理客货运营业。该路南段广州至曲江间二百二十五公里亦已于十一月二十七日开始办理客货运营业。现粤汉全线仅郴县至曲江一段尚待修复。预计全线可于十二月底通车。
