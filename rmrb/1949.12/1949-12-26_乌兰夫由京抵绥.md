Title: Ulanhu Arrives in Suiyuan from Beijing
Author:
Date: 1949-12-26
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, Guisui, December 25th: Ulanhu, Chairman of the People's Government of the Inner Mongolia Autonomous Region, Vice Chairman of the Suiyuan Provincial Military and Political Committee, and Deputy Commander of the Suiyuan Military Region, arrived in Suiyuan from Beijing on the 23rd. Fu Zuoyi, Chairman of the Suiyuan Military and Political Committee, Gao Kelin, Vice Chairman, Dong Qiwu, Chairman of the Suiyuan Provincial People's Government, Deng Baoshan, Chairman of the Gansu Provincial People's Government, and more than eighty others went to the station to welcome him.

### 乌兰夫由京抵绥

1949-12-26
第1版()
专栏：

　　乌兰夫由京抵绥
    【新华社归绥二十五日电】内蒙古自治区人民政府主席兼绥远省军政委员会副主席、绥远军区副司令员乌兰夫，于二十三日由京抵绥。绥远军政委员会傅作义主席、高克林副主席、绥远省人民政府董其武主席、甘肃省人民政府邓宝珊主席等八十余人赴车站欢迎。
