Title: South Korean Farmers' Uprising Expands: Over 40,000 Join Guerrillas in November, Annihilating 2,700 Puppet Troops and Police
Author:
Date: 1949-12-30
Page: 4

　　Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, 28th: TASS, Pyongyang, 26th: The South Korean Labor News published the following results of the South Korean people's guerrilla war in November: In November, the guerrillas fought 1,260 battles in various regions of South Korea, mobilizing 77,922 people, killing 1,800 puppet troops and police, wounding 555, and capturing 413, seizing one grenade launcher, 637 rifles, and 13,850 boxes of ammunition.

　　In November, 42,900 farmers participated in large-scale uprisings, cooperating with the guerrilla war. In North and South Jeolla Provinces, there were 24 peasant uprisings, in North and South Gyeongsang Provinces, there were 22 peasant uprisings, and in North Chungcheong Province, there were 4 uprisings, liquidating over 1,400 manors of reactionary landlords and traitors. With the support and assistance of the guerrillas, the insurgent peasants carried out land reform and restored the People's Committees.

### 南朝鲜农民起义扩大  十一月中有四万余人参加游击队歼伪军警二千七百

1949-12-30
第4版()
专栏：

　　南朝鲜农民起义扩大
    十一月中有四万余人参加游击队歼伪军警二千七百
    【新华社北京二十八日电】塔斯社平壤二十六日讯：南朝鲜劳力者报发表南朝鲜人民游击战争十一月份战果如下：在十一月中，游击队在南朝鲜各地区作战一千二百六十次，动员人数七万七千九百二十二名，击毙伪军警一千八百人，伤五百五十五人，俘虏四百零十三人，缴获掷弹筒一个，步枪六百三十七人枝，弹药一万三千八百五十匣。
    十一月中，有四万二千九百名农民参加大规模起义，配合游击战争。在全罗南北两道农民起义二十四次，庆尚南北两道农民起义二十二次，忠清北道起义四次，清算反动地主和卖国贼的田庄一千四百余处。起义农民关在游击队的支持与帮助下，进行土地改革，恢复人民委员会。
