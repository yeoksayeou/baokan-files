Title: Bandit Planes Invade Nanjing: The Entire City is Outraged
Author:
Date: 1949-12-09
Page: 1
　　Bandit Planes Invade Nanjing
  　The Entire City is Outraged

    [Xinhua News Agency, Nanjing, 8th] Two American-made Kuomintang B-24 bombers invaded Nanjing City on the 6th at noon, dropping twelve bombs along the Yangtze River in Pukou, killing 25 residents and injuring 14. British national Harrison and his wife were also injured. The entire city is extremely indignant at the heinous crimes of the Kuomintang bandit gang.

  　Xinhua News Agency Correction
    In the November 26th dispatch "National Tax Conference Opens" (published on page one of this newspaper on November 27th), Hu Zishou, the Second Minister of the Finance Department of the Inner Mongolia Autonomous Government, was mistakenly identified as the Deputy Minister. Luo Dongming, Deputy Director of the Northwest District Taxation Bureau, was mistakenly identified as the Director of the Northwest Goods Tax Bureau.

### 匪机侵袭南京　全市人民愤慨万分

1949-12-09
第1版()
专栏：

　　匪机侵袭南京
  　全市人民愤慨万分
    【新华社南京八日电】六日中午美制国民党Ｂ２４型轰炸机两架侵袭南京市，在浦口沿江投弹十二枚，炸死居民二十五名，伤十四名。英侨哈瑞生及其妻亦负伤。全市人民对国民党匪帮此种穷凶极恶的罪行，愤慨万分。
  　新华社更正
    本社十一月二十六日电讯“全国税务会议开幕”稿中（载本报十一月二七日一版），内蒙人民自治政府财政部第二部长胡子寿误为副部长。西北区税务总局副局长罗东明误为西北货物税局局长。
