Title: Youth League Central Committee Publication Committee Establishes Youth Publishing House
Author:
Date: 1949-12-29
Page: 3

　　【Beijing News】In order to strengthen the leadership of the League's publishing endeavors, the Publishing Committee of the Central Committee of the Chinese New Democratic Youth League has reorganized the original China Youth Society Distribution Department and the Zhongqing Printing Factory to establish the Youth Publishing House. In addition to publishing periodicals such as "China Youth" and "China Children," it is also preparing to compile and print various reading materials for young people and children. The Beijing retail department of the publishing house will open on New Year's Day, 1950.

### 青年团中央出版委会办青年出版社

1949-12-29
第3版()
专栏：

　　青年团中央出版委会办青年出版社
    【北京讯】中国新民主主义青年团中央出版委员会为加强领导团的出版事业，将原有的中国青年社发行部和中青印刷厂改组，创办青年出版社，除出刊“中国青年”“中国儿童”等刊物外，并准备编印各种青年、儿童读物。该社北京门市部将于一九五○年元旦开幕。
