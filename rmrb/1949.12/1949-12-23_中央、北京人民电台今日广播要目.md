　　Central, Beijing People's Radio Today's Broadcast Schedule

　　[This newspaper dispatch] The Central People's Broadcasting Station's Workers' Time tonight (the 23rd) at 17:30: Liu Dachao's opening speech at the All-China Seamen's Representative Conference on December 18th. 18:30 Natural Science Knowledge Lecture: "How Does the Soviet Union Train Scientific Talents?" 18:00 First Literary Program: Lianhualuo "Close Relatives." 21:00 Second Literary Program: Songs such as "Pinghan Road Serenade."

　　[Another dispatch] Beijing People's Radio, 12:30 Workers' Time: (1) "The Learning Situation of Workers' Training Classes," (2) "The Re-election Process of the Tram Company's Trade Union." 16:15 Children's Time: Singing, storytelling, and nursery rhymes by the children of the Kindergarten of the Second Affiliated Elementary School of the Normal University. 16:40 Youth Time: "How the Broadcasting Group of the Literature Department of the Normal University Got Started." 17:00 Music Program: New songs sung by the Chongci Girls' Middle School Choir. Russian Language Lecture.

### 中央、北京人民电台今日广播要目

1949-12-23
第4版()
专栏：

　　中央、北京人民电台今日广播要目
    【本报讯】中央人民广播电台今（二十三）晚十七点三十分职工时间：十二月十八日中华全国海员代表会议上刘达潮的开幕词。十八点三十分自然科学常识讲座：“苏联怎样训练科学人材？”十八点第一次文艺节目：莲花落“亲骨肉”。二十一点第二次文艺节目“平汉路小唱”等歌曲。
    【又讯】北京人民电台十二点三十分职工时间：（一）“工人干训班学习情况”，（二）“电车公司工会改选经过”。十六点十五分儿童时间：师大第二附小幼稚园小朋友唱歌、讲故事，说童谣。十六点四十分青年时间：“师大文学院广播组是怎样搞起来的”。十七点音乐节目：崇慈女中歌咏团演唱新歌曲。俄文讲座。
