Title: Notice from the Beijing Municipality 1949 First Half of the Year Profit-Seeking Enterprise Income Tax, Summer Business Tax Collection Promotion Committee, Lin Ying Zi No. 1, December 3, 1949 (Not to be Distributed Separately)
Author:
Date: 1949-12-03
Page: 3

It is hereby notified that the tax scores for various industries for the first half of 1949 profit-seeking enterprise income tax and summer business tax have been reviewed and evaluated multiple times by this committee and related industries based on the business conditions and profits of each industry, and have been submitted to the Municipal Government for approval. The list of tax scores for various industrial and commercial sectors is hereby announced as follows. It is hoped that the industry evaluation committees will quickly and reasonably evaluate the scores of this industry to each household and submit the evaluation results to this committee so that they can be forwarded to the Tax Bureau for calculating the tax amount. This is hereby notified.

Chairman: Zhao Zishang

### 北京市一九四九年  上半年营利事业所得税、夏季营业税  征收推进委员会通知　临营字第一号一九四九年十二月三日（不另行文）

1949-12-03
第3版()
专栏：

　　北京市一九四九年
    上半年营利事业所得税、夏季营业税
    征收推进委员会通知
    临营字第一号一九四九年十二月三日（不另行文）
