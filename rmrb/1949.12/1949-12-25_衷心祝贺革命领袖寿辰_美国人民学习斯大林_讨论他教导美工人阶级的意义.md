Title: Heartfelt Congratulations on the Revolutionary Leader's Birthday; American People Study Stalin; Discussing the Significance of His Teachings for the American Working Class
Author:
Date: 1949-12-25
Page: 4

　　Xinhua News Agency dispatch: The American working class and progressives widely celebrate Stalin's 70th birthday. The National Committee of the Communist Party of the United States sent a telegram to Stalin on the 20th, expressing their most heartfelt congratulations and warmest respect. The staff of the Communist Party of New York State held a series of celebration meetings on the 21st to discuss the significance of Stalin's teachings and writings for the American working class. The American Slavic Congress sent a telegram to Stalin on the 21st, respectfully wishing him a long life.

　　To celebrate Stalin's 70th birthday, the American-Soviet Friendship Association held a symposium on Stalin on the evening of the 20th, to hear reports on the role Stalin played in the founding of the Soviet state and socialist construction, Stalin as the leader of the anti-fascist alliance in the war against Hitler's Germany, and the role this great leader played in the struggle to maintain world peace.

　　The New York Jefferson School of Social Science designated the week of the 18th to the 23rd as "Stalin Week" and held a series of special lectures.

　　The "Worker Magazine" published a "Special Issue Celebrating Stalin's 70th Birthday" on the 22nd, which included articles such as "An Assessment of Stalin's Place in History" by William Z. Foster, Chairman of the Communist Party of the United States.

### 衷心祝贺革命领袖寿辰　美国人民学习斯大林  讨论他教导美工人阶级的意义

1949-12-25
第4版()
专栏：

　　衷心祝贺革命领袖寿辰
    美国人民学习斯大林
    讨论他教导美工人阶级的意义
    【本报讯】据新华社电：美国工人阶级及进步人士广泛庆祝斯大林的七十大寿。美国共产党全国委员会于二十日电贺斯大林，对他表示最衷心的祝贺和最热烈的敬意。美国共产党纽约州的工作人员，二十一日举行一系列的庆祝会议，讨论斯大林的教导和著作对美国工人阶级的意义。美籍斯拉夫人大会于二十一日致电斯大林，谨祝斯大林万寿无疆。
    美苏友好协会为了庆祝斯大林七十寿辰，在二十日晚举行了一个关于斯大林的座谈会，听取关于斯大林在苏维埃国家的缔造及社会主义建设中所起的作用，作为反希特勒德国战争中的反法西斯联盟领袖的斯大林，以及这位伟大领袖在维护世界和平的斗争中所起的作用的报告。
    纽约杰弗逊社会科学院将十八日二十三日的一周定为“斯大林周”，举办了一系列的特别讲座。
    “工人杂志”于二十二日出版了“庆祝斯大林七十寿特刊”，其中载有美国共产党主席福斯特所作的“斯大林在历史上的地位的估计”等文。
