Title: National Democratic Women's Federation Hosts Cocktail Party to Bid Farewell to Women Representatives from Various Countries; Beijing to Hold Grand Rally Today to Celebrate the Success of the Asian Women's Representative Conference
Author:
Date: 1949-12-18
Page: 2

　　National Democratic Women's Federation Hosts Cocktail Party
　　Bids Farewell to Women Representatives from Various Countries
    Beijing to Hold Grand Rally Today to Celebrate the Success of the Asian Women's Representative Conference
    [Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, December 17th] The All-China Democratic Women's Federation held a cocktail party this evening to bid farewell to women representatives and guests from various Asian countries. Vice Chairmen of the Central People's Government Zhu De, Li Jishen, and Zhang Lan, Premier Zhou Enlai, Honorary Chairman of the All-China Democratic Women's Federation He Xiangning, Vice Chairmen of the All-China Federation of Trade Unions Li Lisan and Liu Ningyi, and Chairman of the All-China Democratic Youth Federation Liao Chengzhi and others attended as hosts. Representatives of the Asian-Australian Trade Union Conference, who had just returned from a visit to Shanghai, also attended. Today, representatives and guests from various countries also toured the Summer Palace and participated in Qinghua University's celebration of Stalin's 70th birthday and welcome to the Asian Women's Representative Conference. The Beijing Municipal People's Government held a cocktail party at the Summer Palace to fete the representatives.
    [Beijing News] The Beijing People's Celebration Rally for the Victorious Success of the Asian Women's Representative Conference is scheduled to be held solemnly at the Hall of Supreme Harmony at noon today (the 18th). More than 40,000 people, including women, workers, students, soldiers, staff, business people, and citizens, will participate in the rally. Mayor Nie Rongzhen will personally deliver a speech. Those invited to speak include Ms. Gucuri, Secretary-General of the Women's International Democratic Federation, and women representatives from the Soviet Union, Korea, India, and the United States. At the rally, the Municipal Women's Federation will present a flag and flowers to the Women's International Democratic Federation; working women from various districts will present gifts to representatives from various countries.

### 全国民主妇联举行鸡尾酒会　欢送各国妇女代表  京今举行庆祝亚洲妇代会成功大会

1949-12-18
第2版()
专栏：

　　全国民主妇联举行鸡尾酒会
  　欢送各国妇女代表
    京今举行庆祝亚洲妇代会成功大会
    【新华社北京十七日电】中华全国民主妇女联合会今晚举行鸡尾酒会，欢送亚洲各国妇女代表及各国来宾。中央人民政府朱德副主席、李济深副主席、张澜副主席，周恩来总理，中华全国民主妇联名誉主席何香凝，中华全国总工会副主席李立三、刘宁一及中华全国民主青年联合会主席廖承志等出席作陪。刚由上海参观归来之亚澳工会代表会议各代表亦出席参加。各国代表及来宾今日并游览颐和园，和参加清华大学庆祝斯大林七十大寿欢迎亚洲妇女代表大会。北京市人民政府曾在颐和园举行鸡尾酒会欢宴各代表。
    【北京讯】北京市各界人民庆祝亚洲妇女代表会议胜利成功大会定于今（十八）日正午时在太和殿隆重举行。参加大会的将有妇女、工人、学生、部队、职员、工商界、市民等四万余人。聂荣臻市长将亲临致辞。被邀讲话的有国际民主妇联总书记古久里夫人及苏联、朝鲜、印度、美国等国的妇女代表。会上市妇联将向国际民主妇联献旗、献花；各区劳动妇女向各国代表献礼。
