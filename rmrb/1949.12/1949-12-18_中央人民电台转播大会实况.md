Title: Central People's Broadcasting Station to Broadcast Live Coverage of the Conference
Author:
Date: 1949-12-18
Page: 2

　　Central People's Broadcasting Station to Broadcast Live Coverage of the Conference
    [This newspaper report] The Central People's Broadcasting Station and the Beijing People's Broadcasting Station will broadcast live coverage of the Beijing People's Celebration of the Successful Conclusion of the Asian Women's Representatives Conference starting at 11:30 AM today (18th). The scheduled noon program will be suspended.
    [Another news item] Central People's Broadcasting Station tonight (18th) at 5:30 PM Employee Time: The experience of the Beijing Federation of Trade Unions Preparatory Committee's Shop Employees Work Committee in concluding collective contracts. 6:00 PM First cultural program: Continue teaching to sing "Song of Stalin". 9:00 PM Second cultural program: Hao Shutang performs Northeast Drum "Offering the Red Flag". 8:30 PM Weekly Arts: Broadcast Soviet music.
    [Another news item] Beijing People's Broadcasting Station at 7:15 PM introduces the life of the world revolutionary leader Stalin.

### 中央人民电台转播大会实况

1949-12-18
第2版()
专栏：

　　中央人民电台转播大会实况
    【本报讯】中央人民广播电台及北京人民广播电台今（十八）日从十一点三十分起转播北京市各界人民庆祝亚洲妇女代表会议胜利成功大会的实况，原定中午的节目暂停。
    【又讯】中央人民广播电台今（十八）晚十七点三十分职工时间：北京市总工会筹委会店员工作委员会订立集体合同的经验。十八点第一次文艺节目：继续教唱“斯大林之歌”。二十一点第二次文艺节目：郝树棠演播东北大鼓“献红旗”。八点三十分星期文艺：播送苏联音乐。
    【又讯】北京人民广播电台十九点十五分介绍世界革命领袖斯大林的生平。
