Beijing Sino-Soviet Friendship Association's Birthday Celebration Conference
Telegram to Marshal Stalin
[Beijing News] The Beijing Sino-Soviet Friendship Association's conference celebrating Marshal Stalin's 70th birthday passed a telegram of congratulations to Marshal Stalin. The original telegram is as follows:

Respected Marshal Stalin:

Your 70th birthday is the most joyous festival for people all over the world. We, on behalf of all members of the Beijing Sino-Soviet Friendship Association, extend our most profound respect and warmest congratulations to you.

We, the Chinese people, deeply remember your unparalleled contributions to world peace and human liberation. With you, all oppressed people around the world have hope, confidence in victory, and the possibility of achieving it. We, the Chinese people, under your and Chairman Mao's wise leadership, have achieved a great victory. Therefore, we firmly believe that thanks to you and the existence of the glorious socialist country of the Soviet Union, the proletariat and working people of the whole world will surely achieve victory in the near future and win a beautiful and happy future.

The 170,000 members of the 200 branches of our Beijing Sino-Soviet Friendship Association are celebrating your 70th birthday with a glorious and joyful mood. Workers, students, and people from all walks of life have spontaneously launched a signature campaign to wish you a long life. Factories, troops, schools, government agencies, and people's organizations in Beijing are celebrating your birthday by developing and establishing organizations of the Sino-Soviet Friendship Association and universally learning your theories and the great national construction experience of the Soviet people under your leadership.

The shouts of "Long live Marshal Stalin!" and "May Marshal Stalin live ten thousand years!" resound in every corner of the capital of the People's Republic of China. This cry comes from the bottom of the hearts of the Chinese people, because your health and longevity are the greatest happiness of all mankind. We want to shout with all our hearts:

Long live the best friend of the Chinese people, the genius leader and mentor of all progressive humanity, Marshal Stalin! Long live! Long live! Long long live!

Long live the profound friendship between the Chinese and Soviet people!

Beijing Sino-Soviet Friendship Association's Conference Celebrating Marshal Stalin's 70th Birthday
December 20, 1949

### 京市中苏友协祝寿大会　致斯大林大元帅电

1949-12-21
第4版()
专栏：

　　京市中苏友协祝寿大会
    致斯大林大元帅电
    【北京讯】北京市中苏友好协会庆祝斯大林大元帅七十寿辰大会通过向斯大林大元帅致电庆贺，原电如下：敬爱的斯大林大元帅：
    您的七十大寿是全世界人民最欢欣的节日，我们谨代表北京市中苏友好协会全体会员向您致最崇高的敬礼和热烈的庆贺。
    我们中国人民深深记着您为世界和平和人类解放所贡献的无比的功勋，有了您全世界一切被压迫的人民便有了希望，便有了胜利的信心和可能，我们中国人民便是在您和毛主席的英明领导之下，获得了伟大的胜利，所以我们深信由于你和光辉的社会主义国家苏联的存在，全世界的无产阶级和劳动人民，在不久的将来，必将获得胜利，赢得一个美满幸福的将来。
    我们北京市中苏友好协会二百个支会的十七万会员，正以光荣愉快的心情在庆贺您的七十大寿，工人学生和各界人民不约而同的发起了签名祝寿运动。北京的各工厂、部队、学校、机关、人民团体纷纷以发展与建立中苏友好协会的组织，普遍展开学习您的理论，学习您所领导下的苏联人民伟大的建国经验，来庆贺您的寿辰。
    “斯大林大元帅万岁！”“斯大林大元帅万无寿疆！”的呼声，响澈中华人民共和国首都的每一个角落，这个呼声是中国人民从心底深处发出来的，因为您的健康和长寿是全人类的最大幸福，我们要尽情高呼：
    中国人民最好的朋友，一切进步人类的天才领袖与导师斯大林大元帅万岁！万岁！万万岁！
    中苏两国人民深厚的友谊万岁！
    北京市中苏友好协会庆祝斯大林大元帅七十寿辰大会
    　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　一九四九年十二月二十日
