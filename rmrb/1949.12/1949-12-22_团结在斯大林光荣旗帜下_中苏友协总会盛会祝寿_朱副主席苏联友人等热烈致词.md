Title: Unite Under the Glorious Banner of Stalin: Sino-Soviet Friendship Association Holds Grand Celebration for Birthday; Vice Chairman Zhu, Soviet Friends, and Others Deliver Enthusiastic Speeches
Author:
Date: 1949-12-22
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, December 21st:

	The Sino-Soviet Friendship Association held a grand gathering at 8:00 PM tonight to celebrate the 70th birthday of the Chinese people's dearest friend, the great leader and revolutionary teacher of the working people of the world, Marshal Stalin. Invited to attend this birthday celebration were the leaders of the Central People's Government, leading figures from various democratic parties and people's organizations, personnel from the Soviet Embassy, Soviet experts invited to assist in the construction of New China, and Polish diplomatic personnel in Beijing, totaling over one thousand people. The congress elected Liu Shaoqi, Zhu De, Li Jishen, Zhang Lan, Zhou Enlai, Shen Junru, Huang Yanpei, Guo Moruo, Chen Yun, Shi Baifu, Stogrev, Wu Yuzhang, Bo Yibo, Nie Rongzhen, Peng Zhen, Shao Lizi, Li Lisan, Cai Chang, Liao Chengzhi, Zhang Xiruo, Liang Xi, Shen Yanbing, Qian Junrui and other twenty-three people to form the presidium. Zhu De, Vice Chairman of the Central People's Government, Shi Baifu, Chargé d'Affaires of the Soviet Embassy in China, Li Jishen, Chairman of the Revolutionary Committee of the Chinese Kuomintang and Vice President of the Sino-Soviet Friendship Association, Soviet expert Stogrev, Shen Junru and Guo Moruo, Vice Presidents of the Sino-Soviet Friendship Association, delivered congratulatory speeches successively. Speakers unanimously praised Marshal Stalin’s monumental achievements in faithfully struggling for the interests of the Soviet people and the working people of the world, especially his and the Soviet people he led's great assistance to the Chinese people's revolutionary cause, and expressed the heartfelt gratitude of the Chinese people, the Soviet people, and the working people of the world to their great friend, leader, and teacher, and their warm celebration of his birthday. The congress adopted a congratulatory telegram to Marshal Stalin in the name of the congress. Then, the Young Artists Theater Children's Team, representing the Sino-Soviet Friendship Association and ten other units, presented birthday gifts to Marshal Stalin. The congress concluded with celebratory entertainment programs, including music, poetry recitations, and dances.

### 团结在斯大林光荣旗帜下　中苏友协总会盛会祝寿  朱副主席苏联友人等热烈致词

1949-12-22
第1版()
专栏：

　　团结在斯大林光荣旗帜下
    中苏友协总会盛会祝寿
    朱副主席苏联友人等热烈致词
    【新华社北京二十一日电】中苏友好协会总会今天晚上八点钟举行盛大的集会，庆祝中国人民的至友、全世界劳动人民伟大的领袖和革命导师斯大林大元帅的七十寿辰。应邀参加这个祝寿大会的有中央人民政府各首长、各民主党派和人民团体的领导人物、苏联大使馆人员、应邀帮助新中国的建设工作的苏联专家和在北京的波兰外交人员等一千余人。大会推举了刘少奇、朱德、李济深、张澜、周恩来、沈钧儒、黄炎培、郭沫若、陈云、史白夫、斯都格略夫、吴玉章、薄一波、聂荣臻、彭真、邵力子、李立三、蔡畅、廖承志、张奚若、梁希、沈雁冰、钱俊瑞等二十三人为主席团。中央人民政府朱德副主席，苏联驻华大使馆代办史白夫，中国国民党革命委员会主席、中苏友好协会总会副会长李济深，苏联专家斯都格略夫，中苏友好协会总会副会长沈钧儒、郭沫若等相继致祝词。讲演者一致称颂斯大林大元帅为苏联人民和全世界劳动人民的利益而忠贞奋斗的丰功伟绩，特别是他和他领导的苏联人民对于中国人民革命事业的伟大援助，并且表示了中国人民，苏联人民和全世界劳动人民对于自己伟大的朋友、领袖和导师的衷心感谢和对于他的生辰的热烈庆祝。大会通过了以大会名义致斯大林大元帅的贺电。接着由青年艺术剧院少年儿童队代表中苏友好协会总会等十个单位向斯大林大元帅呈献祝寿的礼物。大会最后进行祝寿游艺节目，内容有音乐、诗歌朗诵、舞蹈等。
