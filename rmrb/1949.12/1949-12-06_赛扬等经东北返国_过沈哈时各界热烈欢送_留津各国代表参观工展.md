Title: Saillant and Others Return Home via Northeast China; Enthusiastic Farewell from All Sectors in Shenyang and Harbin; Representatives of Various Countries in Tianjin Visit Industrial Exhibition
Author:
Date: 1949-12-06
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, Shenyang, December 5th: Louis Saillant, Secretary-General of the World Federation of Trade Unions, and representatives of Vice-Presidents Kuznetsov (USSR), Lery (France), Toledano (Mexico), Piña (Cuba), Abdullah (Africa), Subka (Czechoslovakia), Broeksz (Netherlands), as well as the Soviet delegation attending the Asian-Australian Trade Union Representatives Conference, passed through Shenyang yesterday afternoon at 2:30 PM. Those who went to the station to see them off included Gao Gang, Chairman of the Northeast People's Government, and Vice-Chairmen Lin Feng and Gao Chongmin, Zhang Weizhen, Chairman of the Northeast Federation of Trade Unions, Huang Oudong, Secretary of the Shenyang Municipal Committee of the Communist Party of China, Zhu Qiwen, Mayor of the Shenyang Municipal People's Government, Xu Zhi, Vice-Chairman of the Shenyang Municipal Federation of Trade Unions, and more than 3,000 people from various units such as the Shenyang Railway Bureau Trade Union, Northeast Workers' Political University, and China Medical University. Saillant and his party got off the train amidst swirling snowflakes and accepted the enthusiastic farewell. After Lin Feng, Vice-Chairman of the Northeast People's Government, delivered a farewell speech, Solovyov delivered a reply on behalf of the World Federation of Trade Unions Executive Bureau and the Soviet delegation. He said, "We believe that the resolutions of the Asian-Australian Trade Union Representatives Conference will write a new page in the liberation cause of colonial and semi-colonial countries. At the same time, these resolutions will certainly guide the struggles of these countries towards new victories. The World Federation of Trade Unions Executive Bureau feels extremely proud of the great victory of the Chinese people. We wish the Chinese people a life of victory forever." Subsequently, various units such as the Northeast Federation of Trade Unions, the Shenyang Federation of Trade Unions, and the Shenyang Municipal People's Government presented more than 200 gifts, including Mao Zedong's portrait reliefs, silver shields, and silver bottles, to Saillant, the Vice-Presidents of the World Federation of Trade Unions, and the Soviet delegation. The Lu Xun Academy of Arts also performed programs to bid farewell to the international friends. After the performance, Saillant and his party boarded the train and left Shenyang. Zhang Weizhen, Chairman of the Northeast Federation of Trade Unions, accompanied them to Manzhouli.

Xinhua News Agency, Harbin, December 5th: Louis Saillant, Secretary-General of the World Federation of Trade Unions Executive Bureau, who presided over the Asian-Australian Trade Union Representatives Conference, and the Soviet delegation, passed through Harbin at 8:30 AM on the 5th on their way home after successfully completing their tasks. Li Yanlu, Vice-Chairman of the Songjiang Provincial People's Government, Rao Bin, Mayor of Harbin, Zhang Xiuzhu, Chairman of the Harbin Federation of Trade Unions, and representatives from all sectors, about 400 people, went to the station to see them off. Soviet representative Solovyov got off the train and expressed his gratitude to the welcomers. The special train then left Harbin.

Xinhua News Agency, Tianjin, December 5th: The delegations from Korea, Mongolia, India, Indonesia, Vietnam, and the Philippines, who attended the Asian-Australian Trade Union Representatives Conference, visited the Tianjin Industrial Exhibition yesterday at 11:00 AM, accompanied by Liu Ningyi, Vice-Chairman of the All-China Federation of Trade Unions, Huang Huoqing, Director of the Tianjin Federation of Trade Unions Preparatory Committee, Vice-Director Qiu Jin, and representatives from Tianjin industry and commerce such as Song Beiqing and Zhu Jisheng. At 1:00 PM, the Tianjin Industrial Exhibition hosted a banquet for all the representatives. At the banquet, Du Xinbo, Chairman of the Tianjin Industrial Exhibition Working Committee, first delivered a speech, welcoming the representatives to visit Tianjin and paying tribute to them. Choi Kyung-duk, head of the Korean delegation, Sherfan Ka, head of the Indian delegation, and Liu Defu, head of the Vietnamese delegation, also spoke successively. In their speeches, they unanimously stated that they felt very honored to have the opportunity to visit the Tianjin Industrial Exhibition. They highly praised the great achievements of Tianjin's industrial production in a very short period after liberation.

### 赛扬等经东北返国　过沈哈时各界热烈欢送　留津各国代表参观工展

1949-12-06
第1版()
专栏：

　　赛扬等经东北返国
  　过沈哈时各界热烈欢送
  　留津各国代表参观工展
    【新华社沈阳五日电】世界工会联合会总书记路易·赛扬，副主席库兹涅佐夫代表索洛维耶夫（苏联）、勒里普（法）、托列达诺（墨西哥）、皮尼亚（古巴）、阿勃杜拉（非洲）、苏布卡（捷克）、勃罗克萨尔（荷兰），以及出席亚澳工会代表会议的苏联代表团等一行，于昨日下午二时三十分路过沈阳。前往车站欢送的有东北人民政府主席高岗和副主席林枫、高崇民，东北总工会主席张维桢，中共沈阳市委员会书记黄欧东，沈阳市人民政府市长朱其文，沈阳市部工会副主席徐志，以及沈阳市铁路局总工会、东北工人政治大学、中国医科大学等各单位三千余人。赛扬等一行在雪花飞舞中下车接受热情的欢送。在东北人民政府副主席林枫致欢送词后，索洛维耶夫代表世界工联执行局和苏联代表致答词。他说：“我们相信亚澳工会代表会议的决议，将给殖民地、半殖民地国家的解放事业写上新的一页。同时，这些决议也一定会指导这些国家的斗争走向新的胜利。世界工联执行局对于中国人民的伟大胜利感到莫大的骄傲。我们祝福中国人民永远过着胜利的生活。”接着是东北总工会、沈阳市总工会、沈阳市人民政府等各单位纷纷将毛主席像浮雕、银盾、银瓶等礼品二百余件，分别赠给赛扬、世界工联各副主席和苏联代表团。鲁迅文艺学院并表演节目，欢送国际友人。表演完毕，赛扬等一行即登车离沈。东北总工会主席张维桢随车陪送至满洲里。
    【新华社哈尔滨五日电】主持亚澳工会代表会议的世界工联执行局总书记路易·赛扬等及苏联代表团一行，在胜利完成任务的归国途中，于五日晨八时三十分过哈。松江省人民政府副主席李延禄、哈尔滨市长饶斌、哈尔滨总工会主席张修竹及各界代表约四百余人到车站欢送。苏联代表索洛维耶夫下车向欢送者致词表示感谢后，专车旋即离哈。
    【新华社天津五日电】出席亚澳工会代表会议的朝鲜、蒙古、印度、印尼、越南和菲律宾等国的代表团，昨日午前十一时，由中华全国总工会副主席刘宁一，天津总工会筹备委员会主任黄火青、副主任丘金、天津工商业等代表宋棐卿、朱继圣等陪同参观天津市工业展览会。下午一时，天津市工业展览会设宴招待全体代表。席上，先由天津市工业展览会工作委员会主任委员杜新波致词，欢迎代表们前来天津参观；并向代表们致敬。朝鲜代表团团长崔璟德、印度代表团团长谢尔凡卡、越南代表团团长刘德福等也先后讲话。他们在讲话中一致表示能够有机会参观天津的工业展览会，感到非常荣幸。他们对于天津市的工业生产在解放以后很短时期内就有很大的成绩，备加赞扬。
