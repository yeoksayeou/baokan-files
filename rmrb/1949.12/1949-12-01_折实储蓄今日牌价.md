Title: Current Price of Savings in Terms of Real Value
Author:
Date: 1949-12-01
Page: 2
	
	Current Price of Savings in Terms of Real Value
	The Savings Department of the Beijing Branch of the People's Bank of China announces that the standard real value unit price for savings in terms of real value for today (December 1st) is 3,020.63 yuan.
