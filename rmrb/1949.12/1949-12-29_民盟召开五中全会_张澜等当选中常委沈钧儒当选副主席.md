Title: Democratic League Holds Fifth Plenary Session; Zhang Lan and Others Elected to Standing Committee, Shen Junru Elected Vice Chairman
Author:
Date: 1949-12-29
Page: 1

　　Democratic League Holds Fifth Plenary Session
　　Zhang Lan and Others Elected to Standing Committee, Shen Junru Elected Vice Chairman
    Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, 28th — Following the conclusion of the enlarged Fourth Plenary Session of the China Democratic League, the Fifth Plenary Session was convened at 7:00 PM on the 27th. Chairman Zhang Lan, Central Committee members Shen Junru, Zhang Dongsun, Zhang Bojun, Huang Yanpei, Ma Xulun, Peng Zemin, Zhou Xinmin, Pan Guangdan, Zhou Jingwen, Hu Yuzhi, Xu Guangping, Guo Zeshen, Li Xiangfu, Li Wenyi, Zeng Zhaolun, Zhou Jianren, Wang Shiming, Sha Yankai, Ji Fang, Xin Zhichao, Liu (Wang) Liming, Huang Yaomian, Qian Jiaju, Sa Kongliao, Fei Zhendong, Luo Ziwei, and Ye Duyi, among others, totaling fifty-two people, attended. The大会推沈钧儒主席. After Chairman Shen Junru gave the opening address, Zhou Xinmin reported on the proceedings of the enlarged Fourth Plenary Session. The assembly unanimously approved the list of candidates for Vice Chairman, members of the Central Standing Committee, members of the Central Political Bureau, Secretary-General of the Central Political Bureau, and Directors and Deputy Directors of the various headquarters departments. As a result, Shen Junru was elected Vice Chairman, Zhang Lan, Shen Junru, Zhang Bojun, Zhang Dongsun, and seventeen others were elected to the Central Standing Committee, Zhang Lan, Shen Junru, and ten others were elected as members of the Central Political Bureau, and Zhang Bojun was elected Secretary-General of the Central Political Bureau. After the successful conclusion of the election, Shen Junru delivered a speech on behalf of all those elected. Ma Xulun, Xu Guangping, Zhou Jianren, Wang Shiming, Huang Qixiang, He Gonggan, Li Wenyi, Chen Minzhi, and others spoke in succession. The assembly finally concluded with resounding slogans.

### 民盟召开五中全会  张澜等当选中常委沈钧儒当选副主席

1949-12-29
第1版()
专栏：

　　民盟召开五中全会
    张澜等当选中常委沈钧儒当选副主席
    【新华社北京二十八日电】中国民主同盟四中全会扩大会议闭幕后，于二十七日下午七时召开五中全会。出席张澜主席，中央委员沈钧儒，章伯钧，张东荪，黄炎培，马叙伦，彭泽民，周新民，潘光旦，周鲸文，胡愈之，许广平，郭则沉，李相符，李文宜，曾昭抡，周建人，汪世铭，沙彦楷，季方，辛志超，刘（王）立明，黄药眠，千家驹，萨空了，费振东，罗子为，叶笃义等五十二人。大会推沈钧儒为主席。主席致开会词毕，继由周新民报告四中全会扩大会议经过情形。大会全体一致通过副主席、中央常务委员、中央政治局委员、中央政治局秘书长及总部各处会主任副主任候选人名单。结果沈钧儒当选副主席，张澜、沈钧儒、章伯钧、张东荪等一十七人当选中常委，张澜、沈钧儒等十人当选中央政治局委员，章伯钧当选中央政治局秘书长。选举圆满结束，即由沈钧儒代表全体当选人致词，马叙伦、许广平、周建人、汪世铭、黄琪翔、何公敢、李文宜、陈敏之等相继讲话，大会最后在高呼口号中宣告结束。
