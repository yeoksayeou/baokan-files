Title: Chahar Province Epidemic Area Lifts Blockade; Continues Sanitation and Rat Eradication Campaign
Author:
Date: 1949-12-11
Page: 3

　　Chahar Province Epidemic Area Lifts Blockade
  　Continues Sanitation and Rat Eradication Campaign
    [Our newspaper's Zhangjiakou dispatch] In recent months, no plague patients have been discovered in the epidemic areas of Inner Mongolia, northern Chahar, and Jijiabafangzi in Zhangjiakou. As of the 5th of this month, all blockades have been lifted, and normal activities have resumed. However, camels and furs exported from the epidemic area must still undergo strict inspection, and only those that are confirmed not to carry rat fleas are allowed to be traded. To prevent the recurrence of the plague next year, a certain number of epidemic prevention personnel will remain in the epidemic area to continue their work. The epidemic prevention committees at all levels in Chahar Province have been converted into permanent, fixed organizations, planning to take control of epidemic prevention work, and continue to carry out mass sanitation and rat and flea eradication campaigns.
    　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　(Guangming)

### 察省疫区解除封锁　继续进行清洁捕鼠运动
光明
1949-12-11
第3版()
专栏：

　　察省疫区解除封锁
  　继续进行清洁捕鼠运动
    【本报张家口专讯】内蒙、察北及张家口市姬家房子等疫区，近月来均未发现鼠疫患者。自本月五日起，一切封锁以解除，恢复正常活动。惟经疫区输出之骆驼、皮毛仍须经严格检查，确未带鼠蚤者，方准贩运。为防止来年鼠疫复发，疫区仍留有一定防疫人员继续工作，察省各级防疫委员会改为经常固定组织，计划掌握防疫工作，并继续开展群众性的清洁卫生与捕鼠灭蚤运动。
    　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　（光明）
