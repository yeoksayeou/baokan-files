Title: Ding County Special District Establishes Various People's Schools; 20,000 Peasants Begin Studying; Winter School Scale Exceeds Any Previous Year
Author:
Date: 1949-12-21
Page: 7

　　Ding County Special District Establishes Various People's Schools
　　Two Thousands Peasants Begin Studying
　　Winter School Scale Exceeds Any Previous Year
　　[Report from this newspaper] The winter school movement in Ding County Special District, Hebei Province, has been initially launched. According to statistics, several counties including Ding County, Xinle, Wuji, Anguo, Boye, Lixian, Xingtang, and Raoyang have established 725 various people's schools for youth, women, cadres, militia, adults, drama troupes, and production teams, with 22,727 people participating in the studies. The scale of the winter school movement exceeds any previous year. These people's schools were mostly established after the autumn harvest slack season and the conclusion of the people's school teacher training classes. Based on past experience, in order to ensure the continuation of these people's schools, the education sections of each county should strengthen leadership, not only paying attention to leadership at meetings but also conducting in-depth inspections and resolving problems in a timely manner.
　　[Another report] News from Cang County Special District: The winter school movement in Cang County Special District has also been universally launched. According to statistics from six counties including Suning, Jianguo, Hejian, Jiaohe, Renqiu, Qingxian, and Bozhen, 231 villages have established winter schools, with a total of 12,520 people participating in the studies.

### 定县专区建立各种民校　两万农民开始学习  冬学规模超过以往任何一年

1949-12-21
第7版()
专栏：

　　定县专区建立各种民校
    两万农民开始学习
    冬学规模超过以往任何一年
    【本报讯】河北定县专区冬学运动已经初步展开。据统计：定县、新乐、无极、安国、博野、蠡县、行唐、饶阳等几个县，已建立了青年、妇女、干部、民兵、壮年、剧团、生产组等各种民校七百二十五所，有二万二千七百二十七人参加了学习。冬学运动的规模超过了以往任何一年。这些民校多是在秋后农闲和民校教师训练班结束以后成立起来的。根据往年经验，为了使这些民校坚持下去，各县教育科应加强领导，不仅要注意会议领导，同时也该深入检查，及时解决问题。
    【又讯】沧县专区消息：沧县专区冬学运动亦已普遍展开，据肃宁、建国、河间、交河、任邱、青县、泊镇等六个县的统计，已建立冬学的有二百三十一个村，共一万二千五百二十人参加学习。
