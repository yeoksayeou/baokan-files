Title: Soviet Anti-Epidemic Team Leaves Harbin and Returns Home
Author:
Date: 1949-12-12
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Harbin, 11th. The Soviet anti-epidemic team of 39 people, led by team leader Maisky, who were invited to China and successfully completed the task of preventing and controlling plague in West Manchuria and North Chahar, left Harbin on the morning of the 9th to return home. Among those who went to the station to see them off were Harbin Mayor Rao Bin, Director of the Municipal Health Bureau Zhang Baishang, Soviet Deputy Consul in Harbin Koshelev, and more than 800 representatives from all walks of life.

### 苏联防疫队离哈返国

1949-12-12
第1版()
专栏：

　　苏联防疫队离哈返国
    【新华社哈尔滨十一日电】应邀来华在西满和察北胜利完成防治鼠疫任务的苏联防疫队一行三十九人，在队长马意斯基领导下，九日上午离哈尔滨回国。到车站欢送的有哈尔滨市长饶斌、市卫生局长张柏尚、苏驻哈总领事馆副领事阔谢辽夫和各界代表八百多人。
