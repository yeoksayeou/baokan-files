Announcement of the Beijing Branch of the People's Bank of China

Concerning the change of Qianmen Dajie, Di’anmen Dajie, and Xizhimen Dajie branches to sub-branches:

In order to facilitate customers and adapt to the development of business, this bank has changed the Qianmen Dajie, Di’anmen Dajie, and Xizhimen Dajie three sub-branches into sub-branches. All are scheduled to open for business on December 1, handling various deposits, loans, savings, remittances, and agency collection and payment services. This is hereby announced.

　　Qianmen Dajie Sub-branch: 114 Qianmen Dajie, Telephone 3-1380
　　Di’anmen Dajie Sub-branch: 159 Di’anmen Dajie, Telephone 4-0447
　　Xizhimen Dajie Sub-branch: 53 Xizhimen Dajie, Telephone 2-1390

　　Manager: Jia Xingwu
　　Deputy Manager: Yin Yukun
　　December 1, 1949

　　中国人民银行北京分行公告
    为公告前门大街，地安门大街，西直门大街改设办事处各节
