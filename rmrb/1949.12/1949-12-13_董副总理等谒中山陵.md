Title: Vice Premier Dong and Others Visit Sun Yat-sen Mausoleum
Author: 
Date: 1949-12-13
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Nanjing, December 12th:
　　Dong Biwu, Vice Premier of the Central People's Government's Government Administration Council and head of the East China Working Group of the Guidance Committee for Takeover Work, led more than 30 members of the group to visit the Sun Yat-sen Mausoleum on the 11th and presented a wreath to pay their respects.

### 董副总理等谒中山陵

1949-12-13
第1版()
专栏：

　　董副总理等谒中山陵
    【新华社南京十二日电】中央人民政府政务院指导接收工作委员会华东工作团团长董必武副总理，率领该团团员三十余人于十一日谒中山陵，并献花圈致敬。
