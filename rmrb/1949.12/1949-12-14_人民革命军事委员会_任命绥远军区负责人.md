Title: People's Revolutionary Military Commission Appoints Leaders of Suiyuan Military Region
Author:
Date: 1949-12-14
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, December 13th. The Central People's Government's People's Revolutionary Military Commission recently appointed the leading personnel of the Suiyuan Provincial Military Region. The list is as follows:

　　Commander: Fu Zuoyi
　　Political Commissar: Bo Yibo (concurrent)
　　Deputy Commanders: Ulanhu, Dong Qiwu, Yao Zhe, Sun Lanfeng
　　Deputy Political Commissars: Gao Kelin, Yang Yepeng, Wang Kejun
　　Director of the Political Department: Pei Zhouyu
　　Deputy Directors: Lei Yizhi, Yan Youwen
