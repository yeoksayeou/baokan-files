Title: Shanghai Jiubai Elementary School Teachers Join the Communist Youth League
Author:
Date: 1949-12-03
Page: 3

Shanghai News: Fourteen hundred members of the Shanghai Municipal Elementary School Teachers' Communist Youth League gathered at McTyeire School on the 27th for a social gathering, and at the same time held an initiation ceremony for nine hundred new League members. Dai Baitao, member of the Shanghai Municipal Committee of the Communist Youth League and Director of the Education Bureau, attended and spoke, representing the Shanghai Municipal Working Committee of the Communist Youth League in welcoming the nine hundred new League member teachers. He encouraged everyone to eliminate the feudal and comprador remnant thinking still existing in schools, to develop new democratic cultural and educational undertakings, and to cultivate a new generation of young people. At the same time, they should intensify their self-transformation, study Marxism-Leninism and Mao Zedong Thought to arm their minds, and become model people's teachers.

### 上海九百小学教员入团

1949-12-03
第3版()
专栏：

　　上海九百小学教员入团
    【上海讯】上海市小学教师青年团员一千四百人于二十七日齐集中西女中举行联欢大会，同时举行九百位新团员入团仪式。青年团上海市委员兼教育局局长戴百韬出席讲话，代表青年团上海市工委对九百教师新团员表示欢迎，并勉励大家在目前要肃清学校中尚存在着的封建、买办残余思想，开展新民主主义的文化教育事业，培养新的少年一代。同时要加紧改造自己，学习马列主义与毛泽东思想，用以武装自己的头脑，作一个模范人民教师。
