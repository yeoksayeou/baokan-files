Title: Asian-Australian Trade Union Conference Delegates Pass Through Nanjing from Shanghai on their Return North

Author:

Date: 1949-12-17
Page: 1

　　Xinhua News Agency, Nanjing, 16th—The trade union delegations from Korea, India, Ceylon, Vietnam, Indonesia, Thailand, and other countries, totaling thirty-four people, who are returning north from Shanghai, passed through Nanjing on the 15th. Representatives from eighty-three units of the Nanjing Municipal Party, government, military, and people's organizations, totaling more than 3,500 people, held a grand welcoming meeting at the People's Auditorium. Before the welcoming meeting, the delegation also visited Yuhuatai, Sun Yat-sen Mausoleum, and the former Presidential Palace. The delegation left Nanjing by train at approximately 12 o'clock that evening, heading north.

### 亚澳工会会议代表由沪北返过宁

1949-12-17
第1版()
专栏：

　　亚澳工会会议代表由沪北返过宁
    【新华社南京十六日电】离沪北返的朝鲜、印度、锡兰、越南、印尼、泰国等国工会代表团一行三十四人，十五日过宁，宁市党、政、军及各人民团体八十三个单位代表三千五百余人，曾于人民大会堂举行盛大欢迎会。在欢迎会前，该团并曾赴雨花台、中山陵及伪总统府等地游览。代表团于当晚十二时许乘车离宁北上。
