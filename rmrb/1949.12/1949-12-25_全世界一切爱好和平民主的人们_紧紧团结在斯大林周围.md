Title: All Peace-Loving and Democratic People of the World, Unite Closely Around Stalin

Author:
Date: 1949-12-25
Page: 1

　　All Peace-Loving and Democratic People of the World,
  　Unite Closely Around Stalin
    Cheers! To the Chinese people, to the Chinese Communist Party, to the leader of the Chinese people, Mao Zedong; to the people of the People's Democratic countries marching towards socialism, to all fraternal Communist Parties, to all laborers of the world, to the great unity of all democratic forces in the world!
    The toast to the Chinese people, the organizer and leader of the victory of the great Chinese people, the Chinese Communist Party, and to the representative of the People's Republic of China present, the leader of the Chinese people Mao Zedong, was met with prolonged applause.
    Subsequently, Shvernik proposed a toast to the Polish people, who, under the leadership of the Polish United Workers' Party, are confidently advancing along the road to socialism, to the Polish people's representatives present and the head of the delegation, Jóżwiak; to the Bulgarian people and their achievements in building a new life, to the Bulgarian Communist Party and its leader Chervenkov, and to the Bulgarian people's representatives present; to the Czechoslovak people, the Czechoslovak Communist Party leading its people along the road of building socialism, the Czechoslovak people's representatives present and the head of the delegation, Široký; to the Hungarian People's Republic, the Hungarian Workers' Party leading the Hungarian people in the struggle for socialism, the Hungarian delegation and its head, Rákosi.
    The attendees warmly hailed the representatives of the friendly People's Democratic countries, whose people have resolutely embarked on the road to socialism.
    Shvernik proposed a toast to the Romanian people, who are building socialism under the leadership of the Romanian Workers' Party, to the Romanian people's representatives present and the head of the delegation, Gheorghe Gheorghiu-Dej; to the Mongolian people, the Mongolian People's Revolutionary Party, the Mongolian people's delegation and its head, Tsedenbal; to the Korean people who are fighting for the establishment of a unified and independent Korea, the representatives of the Korean people, the Chairman of the Presidium of the Supreme People's Assembly of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea, Kim Tu-bong.
    These proposals were greeted with stormy applause.
    Next, he proposed a toast to the Italian Communist Party, the Italian working class and all laborers, the Italian delegation and the head of the delegation, Togliatti; to the Spanish Communist Party and all Spanish patriots fighting for a democratic Spain, the Spanish delegation and its head, Ibárruri; to the French Communist Party, the French working class, all French democrats who support the friendship between the French and Soviet peoples and oppose all and any warmongers, the French delegation and its head, Madel.
    All attendees warmly congratulated the representatives of the fraternal Communist Parties.
    Shvernik proposed a toast to the German Democratic Republic, the Socialist Unity Party of Germany, and all German patriots who are striving to establish a unified, democratic, and peace-loving Germany, the representatives of the German Democratic Republic present and the head of the delegation, Ulbricht.
    His words aroused prolonged applause.
    Then, Shvernik proposed a toast to the Finnish Communist Party, all Finnish democrats who are striving for Soviet-Finnish friendship and opposing all and any fascists and warmongers, the Finnish delegation who came to Moscow and its head, Pessi; to the Austrian Communist Party, all Austrian democrats who are striving for a democratic and independent Austria that is friendly to all freedom-loving people, the Austrian delegation who came to Moscow and its head, Koreny.
    Music and prolonged applause merged into one in the hall.
    Shvernik proposed a toast to the Albanian people, the most outstanding forces of the Albanian people united by the Albanian Workers' Party, to the Albanian Workers' Party, to the delegation from Albania who came to Moscow and the head of the delegation, Koleka.
    The entire audience warmly congratulated the Albanian delegation.
    Shvernik proposed a toast to the powerful Stalinist friendship and cooperation between the Soviet people and the people of the People's Democratic countries, to the unity of all democratic forces in the world in the struggle against the instigators of a new war and for world peace.
    Prolonged applause resounded throughout the hall.
    Shvernik said: Comrades! I propose a toast to all of you present at this government reception celebrating Comrade Stalin's seventieth birthday.
    These words aroused applause and cheers: "Wishing Comrade Stalin good health! Hurrah! Comrade Stalin! Hurrah!"
    The participants of the reception were all immersed in a storm of cheers paying tribute to Stalin. Applause expressing love and gratitude for the great leader and teacher of the Communist Party and the Soviet people resounded throughout the Georgievsky Hall and continued for a long time.
    Everyone who participated in the historic reception will forever remember the meeting with Stalin and the leaders of the Party and the government.
    A grand concert was also held during the reception.

### 全世界一切爱好和平民主的人们　紧紧团结在斯大林周围

1949-12-25
第1版()
专栏：

　　全世界一切爱好和平民主的人们
  　紧紧团结在斯大林周围       
    干杯！为中国人民，为中国共产党，为中国人民领袖毛泽东；为走向社会主义的各人民民主国家的人民，为各兄弟共产党，为全世界一切劳动者，为全世界一切民主力量的大团结！
    为中国人民、伟大中国人民的胜利的组织者和领导者中国共产党，为在场的中华人民共和国代表、中国人民领袖毛泽东的干杯，受到了经久不绝的鼓掌。
    随后，什维尔尼克建议干杯祝贺在波兰统一工人党领导下满怀信心地沿着社会主义道路前进的波兰人民，在场的波兰人民代表及代表团团长尤兹维雅克；祝贺保加利亚人民和他们在建设新生活中的成就，保加利亚共产党和它的领袖契尔文科夫及在场的保加利亚人民代表；祝贺捷克斯洛伐克人民，领导其人民沿着建设社会主义道路前进的捷克斯洛伐克共产党，在场的捷克斯洛伐克人民代表及代表团团长西罗基；祝贺匈牙利人民共和国，领导匈牙利人民为社会主义而斗争的匈牙利工人党，匈牙利代表团及其团长拉科西。
    与会者热烈地欢呼友好的人民民主国家的代表，他们的人民已坚定地开始走上了社会主义的道路。
    什维尔尼克建议干杯祝贺在罗马尼亚工人党领导下正在建设社会主义的罗马尼亚人民，在场的罗马尼亚人民代表及代表团团长乔治乌——德治；祝贺蒙古人民，蒙古人民革命党，蒙古人民的代表团及其团长泽登巴尔；祝贺正在为建立统一和独立的朝鲜而战斗的朝鲜人民，朝鲜人民的代表，朝鲜民主主义人民共和国最高人民议会主席团主席金枓奉。
    这些建议受到暴风雨般的掌声欢迎。
    接着，他建议干杯祝贺意大利共产党，意大利工人阶级和一切劳动者，意大利代表团及代表团团长托里亚蒂；祝贺西班牙共产党和一切为民主西班牙而战斗的西班牙爱国者，西班牙代表团及其团长伊巴露丽；祝贺法国共产党，法国工人阶级，一切支持法苏两国人民友谊反对一切和任何战争贩子的法国民主分子，法国代表团及其团长马戴尔。
    全体与会者均热烈地祝贺兄弟共产党的代表们。
    什维尔尼克建议干杯祝贺德意志民主共和国，德国统一社会党和一切为建立统一、民主及爱好和平的德国而奋斗的德国爱国者，在场的德意志民主共和国代表和代表团团长乌布利希。
    他的话引起了长久不息的掌声。
    继而，什维尔尼克建议干杯祝贺芬兰共产党，一切为争取苏芬友谊而奋斗并反对一切和任何法西斯主义者和战争贩子的芬兰民主分子，前来莫斯科的芬兰代表团及其团长贝西；祝贺奥地利共产党，为争取民主独立的并对一切爱好自由的人民友好的奥地利而奋斗的一切奥地利民主分子，前来莫斯科的奥地利代表团及其团长柯伦尼。
    乐声与经久不息的掌声在大厅中溶成一片。
    什维尔尼克建议为阿尔巴尼亚人民，为阿尔巴尼亚工人党所团结起来的阿国人民最优秀的力量，为阿尔巴尼亚工人党，为自阿尔巴尼亚前来莫斯科的代表团及代表团团长柯勒卡干杯。
    全场热烈祝贺阿尔巴尼亚代表团。
    什维尔尼克建议为苏联人民与人民民主国家人民间的强大的斯大林的友谊及合作，为全世界一切民主力量在反对新战争挑拨者及争取全世界和平的斗争中的团结干杯。
    长时间的掌声震响全场。
    什维尔尼克说：同志们！我建议为全体出席这次庆贺斯大林同志七十寿辰的政府招待会的在座各位干杯。
    这些话激起了鼓掌与欢呼：“敬祝斯大林同志健康！乌拉！斯大林同志！乌拉！”
    招待会的参加者一同交融在向斯大林致敬的暴风雨般的欢呼声里。表示对共产党及苏联人民伟大领袖与导师的热爱与感激的掌声，响澈乔治也夫斯基大厅，经久不绝。
    每一个参与历史性招待会的人，将永远牢记着与斯大林、与党和政府的领袖们会晤的情形。
    招待会期间并举行了盛大的音乐会。
