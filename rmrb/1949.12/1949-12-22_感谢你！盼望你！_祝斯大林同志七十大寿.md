Title: Thank You! We Await You! A Birthday Wish to Comrade Stalin on His Seventieth Birthday
Author: Li Xianyao
Date: 1949-12-22
Page: 6

　　Thank You! We Await You!
　　A Birthday Wish to Comrade Stalin on His Seventieth Birthday
　　Li Xianyao
　　December 21st,
　　Is the day of your birth,
　　Is the “Christmas” of the world’s people,
　　Comrade Stalin,
　　You brought the world a precious gift,
　　You gave the hungry and cold bread and cotton clothes,
　　You allowed those who fled to return to their homes,
　　You gave the ignorant culture,
　　You gave those who lost their freedom liberation!
　　In the Soviet Union,
　　In Eastern Europe,
　　In Outer Mongolia,
　　In North Korea,
　　In Northeast China,
　　There, the people
　　Call the most beautiful park Stalin Park,
　　Call the most neat boulevard Stalin Avenue,
　　Erect your magnificent bronze statue on the highest peak,
　　Even primary school students in the most remote villages
　　Know you, recognize you,
　　Know that Uncle Stalin is their benefactor!

　　Comrade Stalin!
　　It is your brilliant theory,
　　That illuminates the path of the Chinese people’s revolution!
　　Mao Zedong Thought,
　　Is also Stalin Thought on Chinese soil!
　　You analyzed very early and brilliantly:
　　“The characteristic of the Chinese revolution,
　　Is the armed people opposing the armed counter-revolution!”
　　Yes, the Chinese Workers’ and Peasants’ Red Army that crossed the snowy mountains and grasslands!
　　In the relentless bloody battles with reactionary mercenaries for more than twenty years,
　　Has grown into an invincible and ever-victorious People’s
　　Liberation Army!
　　You also clearly stated:
　　“The question of colonies and semi-colonies,
　　Is essentially the question of the peasants!”
　　Yes, in the places in China where land reform has been carried out,
　　Everywhere is filled with the songs of those who have stood up and prospered!
　　The peasants, who account for eighty percent of the population,
　　Have become the basic and decisive force in liberating China!

　　Comrade Stalin,
　　Your love is broad,
　　Your care embraces the earth like air,
　　You care about the black slaves trampled on by white people,
　　You care about the Indian peasants slaughtered by Nehru,
　　You care about the Greek sons and daughters executed on the gallows,
　　You care about the Indonesian guerrillas emerging from the oak forests,
　　You care about all the oppressed and enslaved people,
　　Because of your guidance,
　　Because of the powerful existence of the socialist Soviet Union led by you,
　　It has encouraged the thousands upon thousands of people still fighting hard
　　With a hundredfold courage!
　　With infinite hope!!

　　Comrade Stalin,
　　A French writer once told us:
　　You are a man who loves to laugh.
　　How could you not laugh?
　　The laws of history make you optimistic,
　　The power of the people makes you optimistic,
　　From your smile with a pipe in your mouth,
　　We see the brilliant tomorrow of the new world!
　　Tomorrow, is held in the hands of the working people of the world.

　　Comrade Stalin,
　　The people in happiness thank you,
　　The people in suffering await you,
　　The grace you give to the people is infinite
　　Your seventieth birthday,
　　Who can not be happy, who can not sing and dance?
　　Workers all over the world have launched a fiery birthday labor emulation,
　　Dedicate all their strength to the construction of the new world,
　　The train carrying gifts from people all over the world,
　　From all directions,
　　Advances towards the Kremlin!
　　The gifts you receive,
　　Will be richer than the granaries of Ukraine,
　　What you receive is the most precious gift in the world,
　　It is the hearts of the people of the world with infinite love and infinite loyalty to you!

　　Stalin, Stalin,
　　We call out as if calling our own relatives,
　　Listen, long live! Stalin!!
　　The cheers of Long Live Stalin are surging,
　　To cheer for you,
　　Is to cheer for the happy tomorrow of mankind!
　　Long live Stalin!
　　Comrades, cheer!
　　Long live Stalin!!
　　Dear Comrade Stalin,
　　Wish you eternal health like the Volga River flowing!
　　Wish you eternal youth like the Siberian forest!!

### 感谢你！盼望你！　祝斯大林同志七十大寿
黎先耀
1949-12-22
第6版()
专栏：

　　感谢你！盼望你！
    祝斯大林同志七十大寿
    黎先耀
    十二月二十一日，
    是你诞生的日子，
    是全世界人民的“圣诞节”，
    斯大林同志，
    你带给世界宝贵的礼物，
    你叫饥寒的有了麦包和棉衣，
    你叫逃亡的能重归自己的家乡，
    你叫愚昧的有了文化，
    你叫失掉自由的得到了解放！
    在苏联，
    要东南欧，
    在外蒙古，
    在北朝鲜，
    在中国的东北，
    在那里，人民
    将最美丽的公园叫做斯大林公园，
    将最整洁的林荫道叫做斯大林大道，
    在最高的山峰上竖起你雄伟的铜像，
    在最偏僻乡村的小学生
    都认识你，都知道你，
    知道斯大林伯伯是他们的恩人！
　
    斯大林同志！
    是你光辉的学说，
    照亮了中国人民革命前进的道路！
    毛泽东思想，
    也就是中国土地上的斯大林思想！
    你很早就天才地分析说：
    “中国革命的特点，
    就是武装的人民反对武装的反革命！”
    是的，爬过雪山草地的中国工农红军！
    在二十多年来不断和反动佣兵的血战中，
    已将自己壮大成一支攻无不克、战无不胜的人民
    解放军！
    你又明白地说过：
    “殖民地半殖民地的问题，
    实质上就是农民的问题！”
    是的，在中国经过土地改革的地方，
    处处洋溢着翻身发家的歌声！
    占百分之八十人口的农民，
    成了解放中国的基本的决定的力量！
　
    斯大林同志，
    你的爱是广阔的，
    你的关心象空气一样拥抱着地球，
    你关心着被白种人践踏的黑奴，
    你关心着被尼赫鲁屠杀的印度农民，
    你关心着处死在绞架上的希腊儿女，
    你关心着出没在橡树林中的印尼游击队，
    你关心着所有被压迫、被奴役的的人民，
    因为有你的指导，
    因为有你所领导的社会主义苏联的强大存在，
    鼓舞了千千万万仍在艰苦中战斗的人民
    勇气百倍！
    希望无穷！！
　
    斯大林同志，
    一位法国作家曾告诉我们：
    你是一个爱笑的人。
    怎么会不笑呢？
    历史的法则使你乐观，
    人民的力量使你乐观，
    从你衔着烟斗的微笑中，
    我们看到新世界璀灿的明天！
    明天，握在全世界劳动人民的手中。
　
    斯大林同志，
    在幸福中的人民感谢你，
    在苦难中的人民盼望你，
    你给予人民的恩惠无穷尽
    你的七十大寿，
    谁能不高兴，谁能不歌舞？
    全世界工人展开了如火的祝寿劳动竞赛，
    对新世界的建设献出全幅力量，
    装载全世界人民礼物的列车，
    从四面八方，
    向克里姆林宫前进！
    你所收到的礼物，
    一定会比乌克兰的谷仓更丰富，
    你所收到的是世界上最珍贵的礼物，
    是全世界人民对你无限敬爱无限忠诚的心！
　
    斯大林，斯大林，
    我们象呼唤自己的亲人一样，
    听，万岁！斯大林！！
    斯大林万岁的欢呼在翻腾，
    向你欢呼，
    就是向人类幸福的明天欢呼！
    斯大林万岁！
    同志们欢呼吧！
    斯大林万岁！！
    亲爱的斯大林同志，
    祝你如伏尔加河奔流一样永远健康！
    祝你如西伯利亚森林一样永远青春！！
