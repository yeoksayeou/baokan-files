Title: Solemn Ceremony Held in Sofia: Dimitrov Mausoleum Inaugurated; Chairman of the Bulgarian Council of Ministers Swears Loyalty Before the Mausoleum to Follow His Teachings and Cherish Friendship with the Soviet Union
Author:
Date: 1949-12-18
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, December 13th: TASS reports: The inauguration ceremony of the Dimitrov Mausoleum was held in Sofia on December 10th. The sarcophagus containing Dimitrov's remains was placed on a large black marble base in the mausoleum. At the head of the sarcophagus was a wreath of flowers with a wide red ribbon, inscribed: "To our dear friend and comrade, Georgi Mikhailovich Dimitrov. Stalin." Chairman of the Council of Ministers Kolarov, Secretary of the Central Committee of the Bulgarian Communist Party and Deputy Chairman of the Council of Ministers Chervenkov, members of the Political Bureau of the Central Committee of the Bulgarian Communist Party and the Standing Committee of the National Committee of the Fatherland Front, and Dimitrov's wife and children, successively walked past the sarcophagus. Also participating in the inauguration ceremony were Professor Zbarsky, Hero of Socialist Labor, and a group of Soviet scientists he led (they were the ones who embalmed Dimitrov's body). After the visit, a joint meeting of the Council of Ministers, the Central Committee of the Bulgarian Communist Party, and the Standing Committee of the National Committee of the Fatherland Front was held in the Council of Ministers Hall. Chairman of the Council of Ministers Kolarov stated: "At the moment of opening the Dimitrov Mausoleum to the people, we swear before the lifelike image of our deceased leader: 'We will be loyal and faithful to his instructions, and we will especially cherish friendship with the Soviet Union as we cherish the pupils of our eyes.'" Secretary of the Central Committee of the Bulgarian Communist Party and Deputy Chairman of the Council of Ministers Chervenkov read out the joint resolution of the Council of Ministers, the Central Committee of the Bulgarian Communist Party, and the National Committee of the Fatherland Front – expressing sincere gratitude to Comrade Stalin for helping the Bulgarian people preserve Dimitrov's remains, making them eternally immortal.

### 索非亚举行隆重典礼　季米特洛夫陵揭幕  保部长会议主席在陵前宣誓忠诚遵循遗训珍爱对苏友谊

1949-12-18
第4版()
专栏：

　　索非亚举行隆重典礼
  　季米特洛夫陵揭幕
    保部长会议主席在陵前宣誓忠诚遵循遗训珍爱对苏友谊
    【新华社北京十三日电】塔斯社讯：季米特洛夫陵揭幕典礼于十二月十日在索非亚举行。置有季米特洛夫遗体的石棺，放在陵寝中巨大的黑色大理石座上。石棺的头上有一个配着红色宽带的鲜花圈，带子上写着：“给亲爱的友人和同志格奥尔基·米哈伊洛维奇·季米特洛夫。斯大林。”部长会议主席科拉罗夫、保加利亚共产党中央委员会书记及部长会议副主席契尔文科夫、保加利亚共产党中央委员会政治局及祖国阵线全国委员会常务委员会委员、季米特洛夫的妻儿，相继走过石棺的前面。参加揭幕典礼者还有社会主义劳动英雄兹巴尔斯基教授和他所率领的一批苏联科学家（把季米特洛夫遗体用香料裹起来的就是他们）。谒陵后，部长会议、保加利共产党中央委员会和祖国阵线全国委员会常委会在部长会议厅中举行了联席会议。部长会议主席科拉罗夫称：在向人民开放季米特洛夫陵寝的时候，我们在故领袖的栩栩如生的遗像面前宣誓：“我们将矢忠矢诚于他的训示，我们尤其要珍爱对苏联的友谊象珍爱我们的眼珠一样”。保加利亚共产党中央委员会书记及部长会议副主席契尔科夫宣读部长会议、保加利亚共产党中央委员会和祖国阵线全国委员会的共同决议——向斯大林同志表示热诚的感谢，感谢他帮助保加利亚人民保存季米特洛夫的遗体，使之长存不朽。
