Title: The Establishment and Prosperity of Soviet People's Culture Are Linked to Stalin - Interview with the Minister of Higher Education of the Soviet Union
Author:
Date: 1949-12-28
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, December 27th: TASS reports: The Minister of Higher Education of the Soviet Union, Kaftanov, stated to a TASS reporter before the 70th birthday of the great people's leader Stalin: The Soviet people associate the entire history of the development of the world's first socialist country and the establishment and prosperity of the new culture of millions of working people, the culture of the entire people, with the name of Stalin. Stalin has always been an agitator, soul, and inspirer for the people of all nationalities in their arduous struggle to master knowledge, culture, and science. Under Stalin's leadership, the Soviet Union has carried out a true cultural revolution. Illiteracy accounted for three-quarters of the total population in old Russia, but now illiteracy is very rare in the Soviet Union.

### 苏联人民文化的建立和昌盛　和斯大林联系在一起   苏联高等教育部部长谈话

1949-12-28
第4版()
专栏：

　　苏联人民文化的建立和昌盛
　  和斯大林联系在一起
    苏联高等教育部部长谈话
    【新华社北京二十七日电】据塔斯社讯：苏联高等教育部部长卡夫坦诺夫在伟大人民领袖斯大林七十寿辰前，对塔斯社记者发表谈话称：苏联人民把世界上第一个社会主义国家发展的全部历史和数以百万计的劳动人民的新文化、全体人民的文化的建立与昌盛，和斯大林的名字联系在一起。斯大林一贯是各族人民为精通知识、文化与科学而进行顽强斗争的鼓动家、灵魂与启发者。在斯大林的领导下，苏联业已进行了真正的文化革命。旧俄文盲占全人口四分之三，而现在苏联文盲是非常稀少了。
