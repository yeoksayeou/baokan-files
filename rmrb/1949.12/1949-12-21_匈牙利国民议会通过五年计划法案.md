Title: Hungarian National Assembly Approves Five-Year Plan Bill
Author:
Date: 1949-12-21
Page: 5
　　
　　Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, 19th - TASS, Budapest: The National Assembly of the Hungarian People's Republic concluded its discussion on the bill regarding the Republic's First Five-Year Plan for National Economy on the 10th, and passed it with exceptionally warm unanimity.

### 匈牙利国民议会通过五年计划法案

1949-12-21
第5版()
专栏：

　　匈牙利国民议会通过五年计划法案
    【新华社北京十九日电】塔斯社布达佩斯讯：匈牙利人民共和国国民议会已于十日结束了关于共和国国民经济第一次五年计划法案的讨论，并且异常热烈地一致予以通过。
