Title: Summarizing the Experience and Lessons of Collective Contract Negotiation - National Shop Employees' Union Work Conference Opens - Preparing to Convene a National Congress to Organize Shop Employees Nationwide
Author:
Date: 1949-12-14
Page: 2

Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, 13th - The National Shop Employees' Union Work Conference, convened by the All-China Federation of Trade Unions, officially opened in Beijing yesterday (the 12th). A total of thirty-three representatives from fifteen major cities, including Beijing, Tianjin, Shanghai, Nanjing, Wuhan, Shenyang, Harbin, and Dalian, are attending this conference. The conference began with an opening speech by Chang Jinglin, Director of the Shop Employees' Work Committee of the All-China Federation of Trade Unions. He stated that this conference aims to accomplish the following three main tasks: (1) Summarize the experiences and lessons learned from negotiating collective contracts industry by industry in various regions; (2) Determine the organizational system, scope, and staffing of shop employees' unions; (3) Establish a national preparatory committee for the All-China Shop Employees' Union Federation to prepare for the convening of a National Shop Employees' Union Congress next year, with the goal of organizing shop employees nationwide in approximately one year. Following this, Zhang Chunjiu, Deputy Director of the Shop Employees' Work Committee of the All-China Federation of Trade Unions, reported on Beijing's experience and lessons learned in establishing normal labor-capital relations and organizing unions through negotiating collective contracts industry by industry. In addition, Wang Liuwen, Director of the Tianjin Shop Employees' Union, and Liu Xie, Deputy Director of the Zhangjiakou Shop Employees' Union, also reported on the situation of union work in Tianjin and Zhangjiakou, respectively, with a particular focus on the experience and lessons learned from negotiating collective contracts.

### 总结订立集体合同经验教训　全国店员工会工作会议开幕  将筹开全国代表大会组织全国店员

1949-12-14
第2版()
专栏：

　　总结订立集体合同经验教训
  　全国店员工会工作会议开幕
    将筹开全国代表大会组织全国店员
    【新华社北京十三电】中华全国总工会召开的全国店员工会工作会议，已于昨日（十二日）起在北京正式开幕。出席这次会议的，共有北京、天津、上海、南京、武汉、沈阳、哈尔滨、大连等十五个主要城市的代表三十三人。会议首由中华全国总工会店员工作委员会主任常景林致开幕词。他说，这次会议要完成以下三个主要任务：（一）总结各地通过一行一业订立集体合同的经验教训；（二）确定店员工会的组织系统、组织范围和工会编制；（三）建立中国店员工会联合会的全国筹备委员会，筹备明年召开全国店员工会代表大会，完成一年左右把全国店员组织起来的任务。继由中华全国总工会店员工作委员会副主任张春久报告北京市通过一行一业订立集体合同，以建立正常劳资关系和组织工会的经验教训。此外，天津店员工会主任王柳文，张家口店员工会副主任刘燮，也在会上分别报告天津、张家口两地工会工作中的情况，特别是关于订立集体合同的经验教训。
