Title: Preparing to Celebrate Stalin's Birthday: Mongolian People's Revolutionary Party Organizes Birthday Celebration Committee
Author:
Date: 1949-12-10
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency

　　Preparing to Celebrate Stalin's Birthday
  　Mongolian People's Revolutionary Party Organizes Birthday Celebration Committee
    [Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, December 9th] TASS, Ulaanbaatar, December 8th: The Central Committee of the Mongolian People's Republic Revolutionary Party passed a resolution on celebrating Stalin's seventieth birthday. The resolution stated: "The name of Comrade Stalin is the banner of the working people's struggle for the victory of the great ideas of Marxism-Leninism, for peace and democracy, and a symbol of the friendly cooperation, freedom, and happiness of all nationalities." The resolution continued: "Comrade Stalin's brilliant, great, and constant concern for our people and the consolidation and development of our country is the decisive factor in the great achievements our people have made since the revolution. On the occasion of Comrade Stalin's seventieth birthday, all our people, together with the Soviet people and the working people of the world, will demonstrate their infinite loyalty to the great Stalin – our brilliant friend, leader, and mentor." The committee preparing for the celebration of Stalin's seventieth birthday has now been formed in Mongolia, under the chairmanship of Marshal Choibalsan.

### 筹备庆祝斯大林大寿　蒙古人民革命党组织祝寿委员会
新华社
1949-12-10
第1版()
专栏：

　　筹备庆祝斯大林大寿
  　蒙古人民革命党组织祝寿委员会
    【新华社北京九日电】塔斯社乌兰巴托尔八日讯：蒙古人民共和国人民革命党中央委员会通过关于庆祝斯大林七十寿辰的决议。决议称：“斯大林同志的名字，是劳动人民为马列主义伟大思想的胜利、为和平与民主而斗争的旗帜，是各民族友好合作、自由与幸福的象征。”决议继称：“光辉的斯大林对我国人民及我国之巩固和发展的巨大而经常的关心，是我国人民在革命以来获得伟大成就的决定性因素。当此斯大林同志七十寿辰之际，我全体人民与苏联人民及全世界劳动人民一起，将表明他们对伟大斯大林——我们光辉的朋友、领袖和导师——的无限忠诚。”现在，蒙古人民筹备庆祝斯大林七十寿辰的委员会，已在乔巴山（前译却伊巴桑或却尔巴桑）元帅的主持下组成。
