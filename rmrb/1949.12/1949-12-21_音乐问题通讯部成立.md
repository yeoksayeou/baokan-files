Title: Music Problem Correspondence Department Established
Author:
Date: 1949-12-21
Page: 7

　　Music Problem Correspondence Department Established
    【Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, 19th】The All-China Music Workers Association and the National Conservatory of Music recently jointly established the Music Problem Correspondence Department, responsible for answering difficult questions encountered by music workers scattered throughout various regions. The correspondence department is located at 11 Jing Road, Fifth District, Tianjin. In addition to the responsible persons of the All-China Music Association and the National Conservatory of Music answering questions, 59 well-known domestic experts in music theory, composition, vocal music, and instrumental music have been hired as consultants.

### 音乐问题通讯部成立

1949-12-21
第7版()
专栏：

　　音乐问题通讯部成立
    【新华社北京十九日电】全国音乐工作者协会和国立音乐学院最近联合设立音乐问题通讯部，负责给散处各地的音乐工作者解答工作中所遇到的困难问题。该通讯部设在天津第五区十一经路；除由全国音协和国立音乐学院各负责人担任解答问题外，并聘定国内著名音乐理论、作曲、声乐、器乐各方面专家五十九人为顾问。
