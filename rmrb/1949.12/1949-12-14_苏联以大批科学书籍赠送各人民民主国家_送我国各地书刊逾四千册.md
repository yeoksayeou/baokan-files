Title: The Soviet Union Donates a Large Number of Scientific Books to Various People's Democratic Countries; Over 4,000 Volumes of Books and Periodicals Sent to Various Parts of China
Author:
Date: 1949-12-14
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, December 13th: TASS reports: The library of the Soviet Academy of Sciences has donated more than 20,000 books to scientific research institutions in various People's Democratic countries this year. These books are works by Soviet scientists on physics, mathematics, chemistry, geology, geography, biology, linguistics, Oriental studies, history, philosophy, economics, and law. The exchange of scientific literature between the Soviet Union and the People's Republic of China has also greatly increased. The number of Soviet books and periodicals sent to scientific research institutions in Beijing, Nanjing, Guangzhou, Shanghai, and other Chinese cities has exceeded 4,000. The library of the Soviet Academy of Sciences has established close ties with scientific research institutions in Bulgaria. The Soviet Union and Bulgaria have signed an agreement to increase the exchange of scientific literature between the two countries. The director of the Romanian Bucharest Bibliography Institute wrote to the library of the Soviet Academy of Sciences, saying: "Our socialist industry is developing rapidly, and the technical and experiential assistance given to us by the Soviet Union is invaluable."

At the request of the Astronomical Institute of the University of Warsaw, the library of the Soviet Academy of Sciences is sending the institute new works by Soviet physicists. The library of the Soviet Academy of Sciences has also received a letter from a group of students in Shanghai who want to study the achievements of Soviet astronomy. The library has sent them various works on Soviet astronomy.

### 苏联以大批科学书籍赠送各人民民主国家　送我国各地书刊逾四千册

1949-12-14
第1版()
专栏：

　　苏联以大批科学书籍
    赠送各人民民主国家
  　送我国各地书刊逾四千册
    【新华社北京十三日电】塔斯社讯：苏联科学院的图书馆今年曾以两万多册书籍送给各人民民主国家的科学研究机关。这些书籍是苏联科学家关于物理、数学、化学、地质、地理、生物、语言、东方语文学、历史、哲学、经济和法律的著作。苏联与中华人民共和国交换科学文献也大有增加。送给北京、南京、广州、上海和中国其他城市科学研究机关的苏联书籍和期刊已经超过了四千册。苏联科学院的图书馆已经和保加利亚的科学研究机关建立了紧密的联系。苏联和保加利亚已经签订了增加交换两国科学文献的协定。罗京布加勒斯特书目编纂所所长写信给苏联科学院图书馆说：“我们的社会主义工业正在迅速发展，苏联在技术上和经验上给予我们的援助是无可估计的”。
    按照华沙大学天文学研究所的请求，苏联科学院图书馆正以苏联物理学家的新著作送给该研究所。苏联科学院图书馆也收到了上海一群学生的来信，他们要学习苏联天文学的成就。图书馆已经把苏联天文学的各种著作送给他们。
