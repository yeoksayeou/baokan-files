Title: Director of the Social Education Department, Yu Qingtang, Passes Away
Author:
Date: 1949-12-07
Page: 1

　　Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, December 5th: Yu Qingtang, Director of the Social Education Department of the Ministry of Education of the Central People's Government and female educator, passed away this morning due to a cerebral hemorrhage. All staff members of the Ministry of Education and Director Yu's relatives and friends in Beijing were deeply saddened by the news. Vice Premiers of the Government Administration Council, Dong Biwu, Guo Moruo, and Huang Yanpei, Minister of Culture Shen Yanbing, and Vice Minister of Light Industry Yang Weiyu, all personally went to offer condolences. Colleagues from the Ministry of Education and Director Yu's relatives and friends are preparing for a memorial service. Yu Qingtang: Native of Taicang, Jiangsu, was 52 years old. She had been engaged in social education for many years and founded Jiangsu Provincial Education College. She served as the Director-General and Executive Director of the China Social Education Society, Professor at former Central, Soochow, and Shanghai universities, and Principal of the Workers' School and Workers' Children's School of the Shanghai Shenxin No. 2 Factory and No. 5 Factory. She returned to China this autumn and was elected as a representative to the People's Political Consultative Conference. After the establishment of the Central People's Government, she served as the Director of the Social Education Department of the Ministry of Education.
　　
　　Xinhua News Agency Correction
    In the list of twenty-seven appointments of the Central People's Government Committee (published in this newspaper on the 5th), Tang Liang "Vice Mayor of Nanjing Municipal People's Government" in the list of the East China Military and Political Commission was a mistake for "Deputy Director of the Nanjing Municipal Military Control Commission," Wang Shoudao "Vice Chairman of the Hunan Provisional Provincial Government" and Qiu Ao "Member of the Hunan Provisional Provincial Government" in the list of the Central South Military and Political Commission were both mistakes for "Member of the Hunan People's Military and Political Commission," and are hereby corrected.

### 社会教育司司长俞庆棠病逝

1949-12-07
第1版()
专栏：

　　社会教育司司长俞庆棠病逝
    【新华社北京五日电】中央人民政府教育部社会教育司司长，女教育家俞庆棠先生于今晨因脑出血逝世。教育部全体工作人员及俞司长在京亲友闻讯极为哀痛。政务院副总理董必武、郭沫若、黄炎培，文化部部长沈雁冰，轻工业部副部长杨卫玉等均亲往吊唁。教育部同人及俞司长亲友正筹备追悼事宜。俞庆棠先生：江苏太仓人，年五十二岁。曾从事社会教育多年，手创江苏省立教育学院。历任中国社会教育社总干事、常务理事，前中央、东吴、沪江等大学教授，上海申新二厂、五厂工人学校及工人子弟学校校长等职。今秋归国被选为人民政治协商会议代表，中央人民政府成立后任教育部社会教育司司长。
　　新华社更正
    中央人民政府委员会二十七项任命名单（刊五日本报）中，华东军政委员会名单内唐亮“南京市人民政府副市长”系“南京市军事管制委员会副主任”之误，中南军政委员会名单内王首道“湖南临时省政府副主席”、仇鳌“湖南临时省政府委员”均系“湖南人民军政委员会委员”之误，特此更正。
