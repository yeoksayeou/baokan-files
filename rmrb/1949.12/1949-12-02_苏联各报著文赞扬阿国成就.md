Title: Soviet Newspapers Praise Albania's Achievements
Author:
Date: 1949-12-02
Page: 4

　　Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, 30th: TASS reports: The 29th marks the fifth anniversary of Albania's liberation. The "Izvestia" published a commemorative article by Kudryavtsev stating: Albania knows that it is an equal member of the family of democratic peoples headed by powerful socialist countries and has made valuable contributions to the struggle for peace, democracy, and socialism. When the two-year plan began on January 1st of this year, Albania's industry had exceeded the pre-war level by 267%, agriculture by 165%, and livestock breeding by 190%. The timber industry, which plays a huge role in Albania's economic life, exceeded it by 750%. Albania's first three railway lines have been built, and the road network has also been improved and expanded. State-owned and cooperative trade are now successfully repelling private capital, which accounted for 16% of total trade in 1949. In the new Albanian countryside, the first dozens of collective farms have emerged. The two-year plan stipulates guaranteeing the victorious construction of the foundations of socialism in Albania. "Labor Daily" and "Komsomolskaya Pravda" also published commemorative articles on the fifth anniversary of Albania's liberation.

### 苏联各报著文赞扬阿国成就

1949-12-02
第4版()
专栏：

　　苏联各报著文赞扬阿国成就
    【新华社北京三十日电】塔斯社莫斯科讯：廿九日为阿尔巴尼亚解放五周年纪念日，消息报刊载库德利亚夫切夫所撰纪念专文称：阿尔巴尼亚知道，它是以强大的社会主义国家为首的、民主人民的大家庭中平等的一员、并且在争取和平，民主与社会主义的斗争中作了有价值的贡献。在今年一月一日两年计划开始时，阿尔巴尼亚的工业已超过战前水平百分之二百六十七，农业超过百分之一百六十五，牲畜饲养百分之一百九十，在阿尔巴尼亚经济生活中起巨大作用的木材工业超过了百分之七百五十。阿尔巴尼亚第一批三条铁路线业已建设起来，公路网也改进扩大了。国营与合作社贸易现正胜利的排斥在一九四九年占整个贸易百分之十六的私人资本。在新阿尔巴尼亚农村，涌现了第一批数十处集体农场。两年计划规定保证胜利地建设阿国社会主义的基础。劳动报与共产主义青年团真理报也刊载了阿国解放五周年的纪念的专文。
