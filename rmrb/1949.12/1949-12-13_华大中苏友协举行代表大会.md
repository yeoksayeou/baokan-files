Title: China University Sino-Soviet Friendship Association Holds Representative Conference
Author:
Date: 1949-12-13
Page: 3

　　China University Sino-Soviet Friendship Association Holds Representative Conference
    [Report from this newspaper] The first representative conference of the China University Sino-Soviet Friendship Association was held at the school headquarters starting on the 8th, lasting for two days. In addition to more than 80 representatives, guests included Soviet famous writer Konstantin Simonov, educator Filipov, Ding Xiu from the Sino-Soviet Friendship Association, and Xiao Song from the Beijing Sino-Soviet Friendship Association branch. On the morning of the first day of the conference, amidst warm applause, Wu Yuzhang, Fan Wenlan, Cheng Fangwu, Nie Zhen, Ding Xiu, Xiao Song, and others, thirteen in total, were elected as the presidium. After Chairman Nie Zhen delivered the opening speech, guests spoke successively, unanimously stating that the most important work of the Sino-Soviet Friendship Association is to conduct internationalism and patriotism education, develop Sino-Soviet friendship, and absorb the Soviet Union's advanced construction experience. On the morning of the second day of the conference, enthusiastic and serious discussions were held, proposals were passed, and Nie Zhen, Li Xin, and Ma Chengqi were elected as president and vice presidents. In the afternoon, Soviet famous writer Simonov and Filipov arrived, and thunderous applause immediately erupted in the venue. All the representatives excitedly and happily welcomed the Soviet friends. Then, four children from the nursery school represented North China University in presenting flowers and gifts to Simonov. In his speech, Simonov explained that the people of China and the Soviet Union should work for the patriotism of the Soviet Union and China. Whether Soviet writers or Chinese writers, they should contribute all their strength to the friendship between the people of the two countries. He said: "I have lived in China for two and a half months, trying to understand the new China and the Chinese People's Liberation Army, and I am preparing to introduce the great achievements of the Chinese Communist Party and the Chinese people to the Soviet Union. I hope everyone will help me complete this important task." Finally, he shouted in Chinese, "Long live Sino-Soviet friendship! Long live Comrade Stalin and Comrade Mao Zedong!" The conference successfully concluded at 4 pm, and grand evening parties were held at the Second Department auditorium and the school headquarters in the evening to add to the excitement!
    　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　(China University News Group)

### 华大中苏友协举行代表大会

1949-12-13
第3版()
专栏：

　　华大中苏友协举行代表大会
    【本报讯】华大中苏友好协会第一次代表大会于八日起在校部召开会议，历时二日，除到会代表八十余人外，来宾有苏联名作家西蒙诺夫、教育家菲力波夫、中苏友好总会丁秀、北京中苏友协分会肖松等。会议第一日上午在热烈掌声中通过吴玉章、范文澜、成仿吾、聂真、丁秀、肖松等十三人为主席团。由主席聂真致开幕词后，来宾相继讲话，一致说明中苏友协最重要的工作是进行国际主义和爱国主义的教育，发展中苏友谊和吸取苏联先进的建设经验。大会第二日上午，热烈、认真地进行讨论，通过提案，并选出聂真、李新、马诚齐为正副会长。下午苏联名作家西蒙诺夫及菲力波夫莅临，会场立刻传出雷动的掌声，全体代表，兴奋愉快地迎接苏联友人，接着由保育院四个小朋友代表华北大学向西蒙诺夫献花、献礼。西蒙诺夫在讲话中，说明中苏两国人民应为苏联和中国的爱国主义而工作，不论苏联作家或中国作家都应为中苏两国人民的友好，把自己所有的力量贡献出来。他说：“我在中国住了两个半月，尽量了解新的中国及中国人民解放军，准备将中国共产党及中国人民的伟大成就介绍给苏联，希望大家帮助我完成这个重大的任务。”最后他用中国话高呼中苏友好万岁，斯大林同志和毛泽东同志万岁。下午四时大会胜利闭幕，晚上分别在二部礼堂与校部举行盛大晚会助兴！
    　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　（华大通讯组）
