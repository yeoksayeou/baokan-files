Title: Japanese Communist Party Central Committee Congratulates Stalin; Akahata Publishes Important Birthday Essays
Author:
Date: 1949-12-23
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, December 22nd: According to a Tokyo dispatch from the Dentsu News Agency: The Central Committee of the Japanese Communist Party sent a telegram on the 21st to congratulate Stalin on his seventieth birthday, wishing him health and continued leadership of the forces of human liberation. On the same day, the JCP's official newspaper, Akahata, devoted almost its entire issue to publishing congratulatory essays and telegrams. JCP Central Committee member Yoshio Shiga published an important essay on the theory of Stalinism, and JCP Central Committee Political Bureau member Sanzo Nosaka published an article on his impressions and reminiscences of Stalin.

### 日共中央电贺斯大林　赤旗报刊载祝寿重要论文

1949-12-23
第1版()
专栏：

　　日共中央电贺斯大林
    赤旗报刊载祝寿重要论文
    【新华社北京二十二日电】据电通社东京讯：日本共产党中央委员会于二十一日电贺斯大林七十寿辰，祝他健康和继续领导人类解放的力量。同日，日共机关报亦旗报几乎以全部篇幅刊载祝贺的论文与贺电。日共中央委员志贺义雄发表了关于斯大林主义理论的重要论文，日共中央政治局委员野坂参三发表了关于斯大林的印象与回忆录的文章。
