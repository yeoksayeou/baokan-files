Title: Korean People Present Crafts and Ginseng as Birthday Gifts to Stalin
Author:
Date: 1949-12-19
Page: 2

Xinhua News Agency, Pyongyang, 18th: The working people of North Korea, in order to express their heartfelt gratitude to Stalin for liberating the Korean people from the shackles of Japanese imperialism and for helping the Korean people carry out democratic construction after the war, have specially presented crafts, artwork, and the world-renowned Korean specialty, "ginseng," to Stalin as birthday gifts. The gifts have been taken to Moscow by the Korean People's Delegation. The crafts are gold and silver stoves, fire tongs, etc., produced in Korea. The stove is engraved with the four characters "Long Live." The artwork consists of two precious ancient paintings. In addition, there are Korean translations of Stalin's brilliant works, such as "Problems of Leninism" and "Dialectical and Historical Materialism."

The Pyongyang Cultural Center has been holding celebrations every day throughout December, attracting many spectators to participate.

### 朝鲜人民以工艺品及人参　献给斯大林作寿礼

1949-12-19
第2版()
专栏：

　　朝鲜人民以工艺品及人参
  　献给斯大林作寿礼
    【新华社平壤十八日电】北朝鲜劳动人民为了表示他们对斯大林从日本帝国主义枷锁下解放朝鲜人民，并在战后帮助朝鲜人民进行民主建设的衷心感激，特以工艺品、美术品和闻名世界的朝鲜特产——“人参”，献给斯大林，作为祝寿的礼物。寿礼已由朝鲜人民代表团带到莫斯科。工艺品是用朝鲜出产的金银制成的火炉火筷等。火炉上刻有“万寿无疆”四字。美术品是两张名贵的古画。此外尚有斯大林的天才著作——列宁主义问题、辩证唯物主义与历史唯物主义等书的朝鲜文译本。
    平壤文化馆在十二月整月中，每天都有庆祝活动，吸引着许多观众前往参加。
