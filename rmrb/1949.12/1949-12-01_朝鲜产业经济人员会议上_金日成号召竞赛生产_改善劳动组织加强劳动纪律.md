Title: At the Korean Industrial Economic Personnel Conference, Kim Il-sung Calls for Production Competition, Improvement of Labor Organization, and Strengthening of Labor Discipline
Author:
Date: 1949-12-01
Page: 4

　　Xinhua News Agency, Pyongyang, 30th—Kim Il-sung, Chairman of the Cabinet of Ministers of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea, in his speech at the conference of activists in the industrial and economic sectors and trade unions, instructed the attendees on future work tasks, saying: All factories should implement a correct piece-rate wage system, improve workers' housing and living conditions to prevent the existing worker turnover phenomenon. At the same time, it is necessary to improve labor organization in factories, strengthen the "sole management system" in various departments, and strengthen labor discipline and production discipline. He pointed out that in order to ensure the technical workers and technicians needed by industry, it is necessary to continuously educate economic leaders to carry out work using scientific methods, promote the creativity of the masses, cultivate production talents with creative abilities, and practice strict economy. He called for a widespread production competition, actively promote trade union work, eliminate bureaucracy and irresponsible phenomena in work, conduct ideological education on protecting state property, and carry out criticism and self-criticism. He finally reminded the attendees to raise their vigilance, expose the hostile elements hidden in the northern half of the republic who attempt to sabotage or hinder production development by various means, and suppress these enemies of the people through the people's regime.

### 朝鲜产业经济人员会议上  金日成号召竞赛生产  改善劳动组织加强劳动纪律

1949-12-01
第4版()
专栏：

　　朝鲜产业经济人员会议上
    金日成号召竞赛生产
    改善劳动组织加强劳动纪律
    【新华社平壤三十日电】朝鲜民主主义人民共和国部长会议主席金日成，在产业经济部门和工会积极分子会议上的讲话中，向到会人员指示今后的工作任务说：各工厂应当实施正确的计件工资制，改善工人的住宅及生活条件，以防止现有工人的流动现象，同时必须改善工厂中的劳动组织，加强各部门的“唯一管理制”，和加强劳动纪律和生产纪律。他指出，要保证产业所需要的技术工人和技师，不断地教育经济指导人员用科学的方法来进行工作，发扬群众的创造性，培养有创造能力的生产人才，并厉行节约。他号召广泛展开生产竞赛，积极推动工会工作，并消灭工作中的官僚主义和不负责现象，进行保护国家财产的思想教育，展开批评与自我批评。他最后提醒到会人员提高警惕性，揭发暗藏在共和国北半部的、企图用各种手段来破坏或阻碍生产发展的敌对分子，并经过人民政权来镇压这些人民的敌人。
