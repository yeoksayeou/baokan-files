Title: Austrian People Love Stalin and Actively Launch Birthday Celebration Movement
Author:
Date: 1949-12-18
Page: 2

Xinhua News Agency, Vienna, December 16th: Austrian progressives are now actively preparing to celebrate Stalin's 70th birthday. Progressive newspapers publish congratulatory letters from the Austrian masses, full of love and respect for the great leader of progressive humanity. Recently, special gatherings have been held in many districts of Vienna, and the Baden Association for Cultural and Economic Relations with the Soviet Union has also organized a celebration. The democratic newspapers in Vienna pay great attention to this birthday. The "Austrian People's Opinion" newspaper published many articles about Stalin's life and his career.

### 奥人民热爱斯大林积极展开祝寿运动

1949-12-18
第2版()
专栏：

　　奥人民热爱斯大林积极展开祝寿运动
    【新华社北京十七日电】塔斯社维也纳十六日讯：奥国进步人士现正活跃地筹备庆祝斯大林七十寿辰。进步的报纸刊载奥国群众的贺信，充满对进步人类的伟大领袖的敬爱。近日来，维也纳许多区都举行了特别集会，巴登地方的对苏文化经济关系协会，也组织了庆祝会。维也纳民主报纸对此次寿辰非常注意。“奥地利人民舆论”报刊载许多关于斯大林的生平及其事业的文章。
