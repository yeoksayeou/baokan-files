Title: Beijing Business Community to Hold Symposium on How to Promote Victory Bonds

Author:

Date: 1949-12-26
Page: 2

　　Beijing Business Community to Hold Symposium on How to Promote Victory Bonds
    [Beijing News] In order to support the People's Victory Parity Bonds and strengthen publicity among industrialists and merchants in the city, the Preparatory Committee of the Beijing Federation of Industry and Commerce has decided to hold a symposium on "How to Promote the People's Victory Parity Bonds" starting on the 28th of this month. It will convene symposiums with its affiliated trade unions in turn, mobilizing industrialists and merchants throughout the city to actively subscribe in order to ensure the completion of this glorious task on schedule.
    [Another News] The Preparatory Committee of the Beijing Federation of Industry and Commerce held a new knowledge lecture at its headquarters at 9:00 am yesterday (the 25th). More than 200 members attended. Sun Xiaocun, Deputy Director of the Planning Bureau of the Financial and Economic Committee of the Central People's Government, spoke on "Why Issue People's Victory Parity Bonds".

### 京工商界将举办如何推行胜利公债座谈会

1949-12-26
第2版()
专栏：

　　京工商界将举办如何推行胜利公债座谈会
    【北京讯】北京市工商联合会筹备委员会为拥护人民胜利折实公债，加强对全市工商业者的宣传，决定自本月二十八日起举办“如何推行人民胜利折实公债”座谈会，轮流召集所属各同业工会座谈，发动全市工商业者踊跃认购，以保证按期完成这一光荣任务。
    【又讯】北京市工商联合会筹备委员会昨（二十五）日上午九时在该会举行新知识讲演会，到有会员二百余人，由中央人民政府政务院财经委员会计划局副局长孙晓村讲：“为什么要发行人民胜利折实公债”。
