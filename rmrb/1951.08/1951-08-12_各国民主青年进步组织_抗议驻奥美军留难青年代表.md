Title: Democratic Youth and Progressive Organizations from Various Countries Protest Against US Occupation Forces in Austria for Obstructing Youth Representatives

Author:
Date: 1951-08-12
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, 11th: Democratic youth and other progressive organizations from various countries have strongly protested against the US occupation forces in Austria for obstructing youth representatives from Britain, France, and other countries from attending the World Youth Festival in Berlin. Billinguer, Chairman of the World Youth and Students for Peace Festival Committee and Chairman of the World Federation of Democratic Youth, held a press conference in Berlin on the 8th to strongly protest against the barbaric behavior of the US occupation forces in Austria. The Secretariat of the World Federation of Democratic Youth sent a telegram to the detained youth representatives of various countries on the 7th, expressing warm greetings. The telegram said: "Freedom-loving youth from all countries are waiting for you in Berlin." A telegram of sympathy from the Free Austrian Youth League stated: "The democratic youth of Austria are deeply indignant at the atrocities of the American occupation authorities. We follow your courageous struggle with admiration, and we will do our utmost to ensure that you can successfully travel to Berlin. Austrian youth fighting to maintain peace will not allow Americans to engage in such shameless behavior on our territory." The French General Confederation of Labor, the Republican Youth Union of France, the French Women's League, and the French Young Women's League have all sent representatives to protest to the US Embassy in Paris on the 8th.

### 各国民主青年进步组织  抗议驻奥美军留难青年代表

1951-08-12
第4版()
专栏：

　　各国民主青年进步组织
    抗议驻奥美军留难青年代表
    【新华社十一日讯】各国民主青年和其他进步团体对奥境美国占领军留难到柏林参加世界青年联欢节的英、法等国青年代表一事提出强硬抗议。世界青年与学生和平联欢节委员会主席、世界民主青年联盟主席比林格尔，八日在柏林招待记者，对奥境美占领军的野蛮行为提出强硬抗议。世界民主青联书记处在七日致电被扣留的各国青年代表，表示热烈的慰问，电文说：“各国爱好自由的青年都在柏林等候你们。”自由奥地利青年联盟的慰问电说：“民主奥地利青年对于美国占领当局的暴行深为愤慨，我们以敬佩的心情关注着你们的勇敢的斗争，我们将尽最大力量使你们能顺利地前往柏林。为维护和平而斗争的奥地利青年，不允许美国人在我们领土上有这种无耻行为。”法国总工会、法兰西共和青年联盟、法兰西妇女联盟和法兰西女青年联盟都推派了代表，于八日向美国驻巴黎大使馆提出抗议。
