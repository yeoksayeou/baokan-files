Title: Celebrating the Founding Anniversary of the Chinese People's Liberation Army, Our Embassy in India and Switzerland Held Receptions
Author:
Date: 1951-08-05
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, New Delhi, August 3rd: To celebrate the 24th anniversary of the founding of the Chinese People's Liberation Army, Colonel Zhu Kaiyin, the military attaché of our embassy in India, held a reception on the evening of the 1st.

Among those attending the reception were ministers and senior officials from various Indian government departments, General Kariappa, Chief of Staff of the Indian Army and Commander-in-Chief of the Army, Admiral Pahlaj, Chief of Staff of the Indian Navy and Commander-in-Chief of the Navy, Major General Mukherjee, Deputy Chief of Staff of the Indian Air Force, Lieutenant General Thapar, Director General of the Indian Armed Forces Medical Services, Lieutenant General Shenajesh, Commander of the Western Zone Garrison, Major General Kanojgar, Commander of the Delhi Garrison, as well as three hundred people representing Indian social figures, cultural workers, trade unions, youth and women's organizations.

Diplomatic officials from the embassies and legations of the Soviet Union, Czechoslovakia, Burma, Pakistan, Afghanistan, Finland, Sweden and other countries, as well as members of the Indonesian Air Force delegation visiting India, also attended the reception.

After the reception, the film "The Whole Nation Celebrates" was screened, which was warmly applauded by those present.

Xinhua News Agency, Prague, August 3rd: Bern dispatch: Our legation in Switzerland held a reception on the evening of July 31st to celebrate the 24th anniversary of the founding of the Chinese People's Liberation Army. Among those invited to attend were Federal Council member, Swiss Minister of Posts, Telegraphs and Railways Escher, Federal Council Secretary-General Remgruuber, Swiss Army Chief of Staff Dubois, and heads of various Swiss government agencies, well-known figures from the Swiss literary world, diplomatic envoys from countries with diplomatic relations with China, and local overseas Chinese, totaling more than 130 people.

At the reception, a documentary of the celebration of the first anniversary of the founding of the People's Republic of China was screened, which was warmly welcomed by the guests.

### 庆祝中国人民解放军建军节  我驻印度瑞士使馆举行招待会

1951-08-05
第4版()
专栏：

　　庆祝中国人民解放军建军节
    我驻印度瑞士使馆举行招待会
    【新华社新德里三日电】为了庆祝中国人民解放军建军节二十四周年，我国驻印度大使馆陆军武官朱开印上校于一日晚举行招待会。
    参加招待会的有印度政府各部部长和高级官员，印度陆军参谋长兼陆军总司令卡里阿伯上将，印度海军参谋长兼海军总司令泊尔莱上将，印度空军副参谋长摩克吉少将，印度军医署署长塔巴尔中将，西区卫戍司令谢纳杰施中将，德里卫戍司令卡诺加尔少将，以及印度社会名流、文化工作者、工会和青年、妇女团体的代表等三百人。
    苏联、捷克斯洛伐克、缅甸、巴基斯坦、阿富汗、芬兰、瑞典等国大使馆和公使馆的外交官员以及印尼空军访印代表团人员，亦出席了招待会。
    会后放映电影《普天同庆》，获得到会者的热烈鼓掌。
    【新华社布拉格三日电】伯尔尼消息：我国驻瑞士公使馆于七月三十一日晚举行招待会，庆祝中国人民解放军建军二十四周年纪念。应邀赴会的有联邦会议委员、瑞士政府邮电与铁道部长艾斯旭、联邦会议秘书长雷姆格鲁伯、瑞士陆军参谋总长杜波依斯及瑞士政府各机关首长，瑞士文学界著名人士、与中国有外交关系的各国外交使节和当地华侨共一百三十余人。
    招待会上放映了中华人民共和国成立第一周年庆祝会的纪录片，受到来宾们热烈欢迎。
