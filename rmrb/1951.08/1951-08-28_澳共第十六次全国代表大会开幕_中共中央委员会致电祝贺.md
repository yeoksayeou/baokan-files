Title: Australian Communist Party's 16th National Congress Opens; Central Committee of the Chinese Communist Party Sends Congratulatory Message
Author:
Date: 1951-08-28
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Sydney, August 24th: According to TASS, the 16th National Congress of the Australian Communist Party opened in Sydney on August 23rd. After electing the Presidium of the congress, an Honorary Presidium was selected. Amidst the warm applause of delegates and guests, Comrade Stalin was elected to the Honorary Presidium. Others elected to the Honorary Presidium included Mao Zedong, Gottwald, Bierut, Rákosi, Pieck, Chervenkov, Gheorghiu-Dej, Hoxha, Thorez, Togliatti, Foster, Pollitt, etc. The congress received congratulatory telegrams from many fraternal Communist parties. The congress welcomed the congratulatory telegram from the Central Committee of the Communist Party of the Soviet Union (Bolsheviks) with extremely warm applause. It stated: "We send fraternal greetings to the 16th Congress of the Australian Communist Party and wish the Australian Communist Party success in the struggle for peace, democracy and the vital interests of the Australian people."
    Australian Communist Party Chairman Dixon, in his opening address, gave a brief review of the domestic and international situation since the closing of the 15th Congress three years ago. Australian Communist Party General Secretary Sharkey, amidst thunderous applause, delivered a report on the draft program of the Australian Communist Party.

Xinhua News Agency, August 27th: The Central Committee of the Communist Party of China sent a congratulatory telegram to the 16th National Congress of the Australian Communist Party. The full text is as follows: To Comrade Sharkey and all delegates of the 16th National Congress of the Australian Communist Party:
    The Central Committee of the Communist Party of China extends warm congratulations to the 16th National Congress of the Australian Communist Party. We sincerely wish the Australian Communist Party greater achievements in leading the Australian people in the struggle to defend world peace and strive for their own liberation, and we wish the working class and people of China and Australia unity in the cause of defending peace in the Pacific. Long live the Australian Communist Party, the defender of the interests of the Australian people!
                The Central Committee of the Communist Party of China

### 澳共第十六次全国代表大会开幕  中共中央委员会致电祝贺

1951-08-28
第4版()
专栏：

　　澳共第十六次全国代表大会开幕
    中共中央委员会致电祝贺
    【新华社二十七日讯】据塔斯社悉尼二十四日讯：澳大利亚共产党第十六届全国代表大会已于八月二十三日在悉尼开幕。大会选出主席团以后，接着选出了名誉主席团。大会在代表与来宾热烈的掌声中把斯大林同志选入名誉主席团。选入名誉主席团的还有：毛泽东、哥特瓦尔德、贝鲁特、拉科西、皮克、契尔文科夫、乔治乌—德治、霍查、多列士、托里亚蒂、福斯特、波立特等。大会接到许多兄弟共产党发来的贺电。大会以极其热烈的掌声欢迎苏联共产党（布）中央委员会的贺电。其中说：“我们向澳大利亚共产党第十六届代表大会致兄弟的敬礼，并祝澳大利亚共产党在争取和平、澳大利亚人民的民主与切身利益的斗争中获得成功。”
    澳大利亚共产党主席狄克逊在开幕词中对第十五届代表大会闭幕三年以来的国内外局势作了简短的回顾。澳大利亚共产党总书记夏基在雷动的掌声中作了关于澳大利亚共产党纲领草案的报告。
    【新华社二十七日讯】中国共产党中央委员会致澳洲共产党第十六次全国代表大会贺电，全文如下：夏基同志和澳洲共产党第十六次全国代表大会的全体代表同志：
    中国共产党中央委员会谨向澳洲共产党第十六次全国代表大会致热烈的庆贺。敬祝澳洲共产党在领导澳洲人民保卫世界和平和争取自身解放的斗争中获得更大的成就，并祝中、澳两国工人阶级和两国人民在保卫太平洋和平的事业上团结一致。澳洲人民利益的捍卫者——澳洲共产党万岁！
                中国共产党中央委员会
