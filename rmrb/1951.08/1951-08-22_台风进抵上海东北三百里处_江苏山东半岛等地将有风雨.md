Title: Typhoon Reaches 300 Li Northeast of Shanghai; Jiangsu, Shandong Peninsula, and Other Areas Will Experience Wind and Rain
Author:
Date: 1951-08-22
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, August 21st: The Central Meteorological Observatory issued a typhoon warning at 8:00 PM on August 21st: At 2:00 PM on August 21st, the center of the typhoon was located 300 li northeast of Shanghai (i.e., 32 degrees north latitude, 123 degrees east longitude), moving in a direction west-northwest at a speed of approximately 18 li per hour. The typhoon's intensity has not changed significantly. In the next 24 hours, Jiangsu, the Shandong Peninsula, the Bohai Sea, and the Liaodong Peninsula will experience wind and rain. The wind speed in the affected areas will be between 80-110 li per hour (i.e., force 5-6 winds).

### 台风进抵上海东北三百里处  江苏山东半岛等地将有风雨

1951-08-22
第1版()
专栏：

　　台风进抵上海东北三百里处
    江苏山东半岛等地将有风雨
    【新华社二十一日讯】中央气象台八月二十一日下午八时发出台风警报：二十一日下午二时台风中心的位置在上海东北三百华里（即北纬三十二度、东经一百二十三度的地方）向西北偏西的方向进行，每小时移动约十八华里。台风强度无大变化。未来二十四小时内，江苏、山东半岛、渤海、辽东半岛有风雨。受影响地域风速在每小时八十——一百一十华里之间（即风力五、六级）。
