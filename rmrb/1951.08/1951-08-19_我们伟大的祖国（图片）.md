Title: Our Great Motherland (Picture)
Author:
Date: 1951-08-19
Page: 1

    Our Great Motherland
    The signing of the agreement on the peaceful liberation of Tibet has closely united the Tibetan people with the big family of all nationalities in China. Therefore, this agreement has been warmly supported by the Tibetan people. This is the scene of lamas at Tagong Monastery in Kangding reading the full text of the agreement on the peaceful liberation of Tibet.

### 我们伟大的祖国（图片）

1951-08-19
第1版()
专栏：

    我们伟大的祖国
    和平解放西藏协议的签订，使得藏族人民紧密地团结在中华各民族的大家庭里，因此这个协议受到了藏族人民的热烈拥护。这是康定塔公寺喇嘛朗读和平解放西藏的协议全文的情形。
