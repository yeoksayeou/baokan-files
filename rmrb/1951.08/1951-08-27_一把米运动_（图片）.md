Title: "A Handful of Rice Movement" (Picture)
Author: Deng Shu, Gu Qun
Date: 1951-08-27
Page: 3

       "A Handful of Rice Movement" (Sketches of the Huai River Project, No. 4)
       By Deng Shu and Gu Qun
    The patriotic laborers have launched the "A Handful of Rice Movement" to resist America and oppose the United States' arming of Japan. They are donating a portion of their surplus grain and are determined to do a good job in the Huai River project to support the Chinese People's Volunteers.

### “一把米运动”（图片）
邓澍  顾群
1951-08-27
第3版()
专栏：

       “一把米运动”（图片）
     （治淮速写之四）
       邓澍  顾群作
    热爱祖国的民工们，掀起了抗美援朝、反对美国武装日本的“一把米运动”，他们把自己的一部分余粮捐献出来，并决心搞好治淮工作，支援中国人民志愿军。
