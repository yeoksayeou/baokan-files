Title: General Vo Nguyen Giap Sends Congratulations on Our Army Day
Author:
Date: 1951-08-01
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, 31st - General Vo Nguyen Giap, Commander-in-Chief of the Vietnamese People's Army, sent a telegram to Commander-in-Chief Zhu, congratulating the 24th anniversary of the founding of the Chinese People's Liberation Army. The telegram is as follows:

To General Zhu De, Commander-in-Chief of the Chinese People's Liberation Army:

On the occasion of the anniversary of the founding of the Chinese People's Liberation Army, I, on behalf of the Vietnamese Democratic Republic People's Army, extend my warmest greetings to you, the Chinese People's Liberation Army, and the Chinese People's Volunteers who are currently fighting heroically in Korea to resist America and aid Korea. I wish the Chinese People's Liberation Army, under the wise leadership of Chairman Mao and yourself, even greater success in defending China's independence and unity, building a new China, and safeguarding world peace.

　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　Vo Nguyen Giap

### 武元甲将军电贺我建军节

1951-08-01
第1版()
专栏：

　　武元甲将军电贺我建军节
    【新华社三十一日讯】越南人民军总司令武元甲将军致电朱总司令，祝贺中国人民解放军建军二十四周年。电文如下：中国人民解放军总司令朱德将军：
    值此中国人民解放军建军节之际，我谨代表越南民主共和国人民军向您和中国人民解放军，及目前正在朝鲜为抗美援朝而英勇作战的中国人民志愿军致以最热烈的敬礼。谨祝中国人民解放军，在毛主席和您的英明领导下，在保卫中国的独立统一、建设新中国和保卫世界和平的事业中，获得更多的成功。
                  武元甲
