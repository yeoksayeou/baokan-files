Title: Polish Defense Minister and Chief of Staff Congratulate Commander-in-Chief Zhu and Chief of General Staff Xu
Author:
Date: 1951-08-01
Page: 2

　　Xinhua News Agency, 31st. Polish Republic Minister of National Defense Marshal Rokossovsky and Polish Republic Army Chief of Staff General Korchits sent telegrams to Commander-in-Chief Zhu De and Chief of General Staff Xu Xiangqian of the Chinese People's Liberation Army, congratulating them on the "August 1st" Army Day of the Chinese People's Liberation Army. Marshal Rokossovsky's congratulatory telegram to Commander-in-Chief Zhu is as follows:
Comrade Zhu De, Commander-in-Chief of the Chinese People's Liberation Army:
　　On the occasion of the 24th anniversary of the founding of the Chinese People's Liberation Army, on behalf of the Polish army and myself personally, I would like to extend formal, fraternal greetings to you personally and, through you, to the armed forces of the People's Republic of China, and wish the Chinese People's Liberation Army further victories in strengthening its combat power and consolidating the friendly relations between our two armies. I extend my highest respect to you.
　　　　　　　　　　　　　　Marshal Rokossovsky, Minister of National Defense of Poland
　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　July 28, 1951

General Korchits' congratulatory telegram to Chief of General Staff Xu is as follows:
Comrade General Xu Xiangqian, Chief of General Staff of the Chinese People's Liberation Army:
　　On the occasion of the great festival of the Chinese People's Liberation Army, I would like to wish the armed forces of the People's Republic of China and you personally further victories in the cause of consolidating peace and opposing war instigators. The Polish people and their armed forces have been and will always be striving to maintain the close and indestructible friendship with the great Chinese people and army.
　　　　　　　　　　　　　　General Korchits, Chief of Staff of the Polish Army
　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　July 28, 1951

### 波兰国防部长和参谋总长电朱总司令和徐总参谋长祝贺

1951-08-01
第2版()
专栏：

　　波兰国防部长和参谋总长
    电朱总司令和徐总参谋长祝贺
    【新华社三十一日讯】波兰共和国国防部长罗科索夫斯基元帅和波兰共和国军队参谋总长考尔切茨上将，致电中国人民解放军总司令朱德和总参谋长徐向前，祝贺中国人民解放军“八一”建军节。罗科索夫斯基元帅致朱总司令的贺电如下：中国人民解放军总司令朱德同志：
    值此中国人民解放军建军二十四周年纪念之际，谨以波兰军队及我个人名义，向您个人及通过您向中华人民共和国武装力量致正式的、兄弟的敬礼，并祝中国人民解放军在壮大其战斗力量与巩固贵我两国军队间的友好关系中，获得进一步的胜利。谨向您致崇高的敬意。
                              波兰国防部长罗科索夫斯基元帅
                                       一九五一年七月二十八日
    考尔切茨上将致徐总参谋长的贺电如下：中国人民解放军总参谋长徐向前将军同志：
    值此中国人民解放军伟大的节日，特祝中华人民共和国人民武装力量及您个人在为巩固和平、反对战争挑拨者的事业中获得进一步的胜利。波兰人民及其武装力量过去和将来永远是为着维护与伟大的中国人民及军队的紧密及不可摧毁的友谊而努力。
                              波兰军队参谋总长考尔切茨上将
                                    一九五一年七月二十八日
