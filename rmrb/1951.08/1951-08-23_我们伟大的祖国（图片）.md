    Our Great Motherland
    Today is the second anniversary of the liberation of Ruijin, Jiangxi. Ruijin was the red capital during the Second Revolutionary Civil War. This is a house in Shazhouba, Ruijin, where Chairman Mao lived.

    我们伟大的祖国
