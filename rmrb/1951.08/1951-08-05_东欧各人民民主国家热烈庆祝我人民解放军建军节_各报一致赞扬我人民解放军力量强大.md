Title: Eastern European People's Democratic Countries Warmly Celebrate the Anniversary of the Founding of the Chinese People's Liberation Army; Newspapers Unanimously Praise the Powerful Strength of the Chinese People's Liberation Army
Author:
Date: 1951-08-05
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Prague, August 4th: Eastern European people's democratic countries are celebrating the 24th anniversary of the founding of the Chinese People's Liberation Army. Hungarian newspapers have all published articles commemorating the anniversary of the founding of the Chinese People's Liberation Army. The Hungarian Working People's Party organ, *Szabad Nép* (Free People), published an editorial on the 1st stating: "The establishment of the Chinese People's Liberation Army is a serious warning to all imperialists, and a fighting example for the people of all colonial countries struggling for freedom. This powerful force strengthens the confidence of the Hungarian people and other countries fighting for peace and freedom. This army is a strong ally of the Soviet army and a great defender of world peace."

The Romanian Workers' Party organ, *Scînteia* (Spark), published an article on the 1st entitled "The Twenty-Fourth Anniversary of the Glorious Chinese People's Army." The article said: "The victory won by the Chinese People's Liberation Army is one of the most serious blows to the imperialists, following the victory of the October Revolution and the victory of the Soviet army during the Second World War. The great strength of the new China and its strong army serve the cause of defending peace in Asia and the world. Progressive mankind throughout the world expresses its high admiration for this invincible force." *Apărarea Patriei* (Defense of the Fatherland), the organ of the Romanian Ministry of National Defense, also published an article introducing the history of the Chinese People's Liberation Army's struggle and wrote an article to celebrate it.

Czechoslovak newspapers widely commemorated the anniversary of the founding of the Chinese People's Liberation Army on the 1st. The exhibition of the Chinese People's Liberation Army held by the Czech Ministry of National Defense opened on the 1st. General Hruska, Director of the Political Department of the Czech Ministry of National Defense, said at the opening ceremony: "We Czechs and Slovaks thank the Soviet army and Comrade Stalin for liberating us. We also thank the Chinese People's Liberation Army and Chairman Mao Zedong and Commander-in-Chief Zhu De, because China's victory over American imperialists has greatly strengthened the world peace camp."

Bulgaria's *Rabotnichesko Delo* (Workers' Cause) and *Otechestven Front* (Fatherland Front) both published editorials and articles commemorating the anniversary of the founding of the Chinese People's Liberation Army. The Sofia Radio Station hosted a special program on the evening of the 1st introducing the Chinese People's Liberation Army.

Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, August 4th: To celebrate the 24th anniversary of the founding of the Chinese People's Liberation Army, the Chinese Embassy in the Soviet Union held a photo exhibition of the Chinese People's Liberation Army in Moscow on the evening of the 2nd, and showed the Chinese film "Steel Warrior," which recently won an award at the 6th International Film Festival, and the documentary film "Universal Celebration" commemorating the first anniversary of the founding of our country, to entertain foreign guests.

### 东欧各人民民主国家热烈庆祝我人民解放军建军节  各报一致赞扬我人民解放军力量强大

1951-08-05
第4版()
专栏：

　　东欧各人民民主国家
    热烈庆祝我人民解放军建军节
    各报一致赞扬我人民解放军力量强大
    【新华社布拉格四日电】东欧各人民民主国家庆祝中国人民解放军建军二十四周年。匈牙利各报纸都刊载了纪念中国人民解放军建军节的文章。匈牙利劳动人民党机关报《自由人民报》于一日发表社论说：“中国人民解放军的建立，是对一切帝国主义者的严重警告，对于为自由而斗争的各殖民地国家的人民，却是一个战斗的范例。这个强大的力量增强了匈牙利人民以及其他为和平和自由而斗争的各国人民的信心。这支军队是苏联军队的强大的盟友，是世界和平的伟大保卫者。”
    罗马尼亚工人党机关报《火花报》于一日发表题为《光荣的中国人民军队的二十四周年》一文。这篇文章说：“中国人民解放军所获得的胜利，是继十月革命胜利和第二次世界大战期间苏联军队的胜利之后，对帝国主义者的一个最严重的打击。新中国的巨大力量及其坚强的军队是服务于保卫亚洲及全世界和平的事业的。全世界进步人类对这支不可战胜的力量都表示崇高的敬仰。”罗马尼亚国防部机关报《军人之声》报也刊登了介绍中国人民解放军斗争历史的文章，并著文表示庆祝。
    捷克斯洛伐克报纸在一日广泛纪念了中国人民解放军建军节。捷国防部举办的中国人民解放军展览会已于一日开幕。捷国防部政治司司长赫鲁斯加将军在开幕仪式上说：“我们捷克人和斯洛伐克人感谢苏联军队和斯大林同志解放了我们。我们也感谢中国人民解放军和毛泽东主席及朱德总司令，因为中国对美帝国主义者的胜利，大大地加强了世界和平阵营。”
    保加利亚的《工人事业》报和《祖国阵线》报都发表了纪念我人民解放军建军节的社论和论文。索非亚广播电台在一日晚举办了介绍中国人民解放军的特别节目。
    【新华社莫斯科四日电】我国驻苏联大使馆为庆祝中国人民解放军建军二十四周年纪念，于二日晚间在莫斯科举行中国人民解放军照片展览，并放映最近在第六届国际电影赛会中获奖的我国影片“钢铁战士”，和我国开国一周年纪念的纪录影片《普天同庆》，以招待外宾。
