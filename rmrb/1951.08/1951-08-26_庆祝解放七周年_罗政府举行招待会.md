Title: Romanian Government Holds Reception to Celebrate the Seventh Anniversary of Liberation
Author: 
Date: 1951-08-26
Page: 4

　　Celebrating the Seventh Anniversary of Liberation
    Romanian Government Holds Reception
    Xinhua News Agency, Bucharest, August 25th: The Romanian Council of Ministers held a reception on the 23rd at the Ministry of Interior Building in Bucharest to celebrate the seventh anniversary of Romania's liberation. Present were Parhon, Chairman of the Presidium of the Romanian National Assembly, Groza, Chairman of the Council of Ministers, Gheorghe Gheorghiu-Dej, General Secretary of the Central Committee of the Romanian Workers' Party, members of the Central Committee of the Romanian Workers' Party, and government officials. The Soviet government delegation headed by Soviet Marshal Voroshilov and government delegations from other countries who came to participate in the celebration were also present. Also present were diplomatic envoys from various countries, foreign dignitaries, figures from the scientific, cultural and artistic circles, representatives of the armed forces, and advanced male and female workers. The reception took place in a joyous atmosphere.

### 庆祝解放七周年  罗政府举行招待会

1951-08-26
第4版()
专栏：

　　庆祝解放七周年
    罗政府举行招待会
    【新华社二十五日讯】罗马尼亚通讯社布加勒斯特讯：罗马尼亚部长会议为庆祝罗马尼亚解放七周年纪念日，于二十三日在布加勒斯特内务部大厦举行招待会。出席的有罗马尼亚国民议会主席团主席巴洪，部长会议主席格罗查，罗马尼亚工人党中央委员会总书记乔治乌—德治，罗马尼亚工人党中央委员会委员们以及政府官员们。以苏联元帅伏罗希洛夫为首的苏联政府代表团和前来参加庆典的其他国家政府代表团也都出席。与会者尚有各国外交使节，外国贵宾、科学文化艺术界人士、武装部队代表、先进的男女工人等。招待会在欢乐的气氛中进行。
