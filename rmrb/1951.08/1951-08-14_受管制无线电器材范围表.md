Author:
Date: 1951-08-14
Page: 

　　List of Controlled Radio Equipment
　　I. Complete radio transmitters, radiotelegraph receivers, and radios capable of receiving telegrams (including radiotelephone transmitters/receivers, regardless of power).
　　II. Transmitting vacuum tubes specifically for radio use and all vacuum tubes, new or old, with an output of two watts or more. For example: old types 3033, 43, 45, 6L6, 6V6, 807, 1625, etc. New types: 6VB6, 6AH5, 3A5, 2B6, 1H4G, 813, etc. Japanese types: 510B, 845, 814, etc.
　　III. Assembled rectifiers with a direct current output voltage of 250 volts or more.
　　IV. All types of capacitors with a withstand voltage of 600 volts or more.
　　V. All types of milliampere meters, antenna current meters, and frequency/wavelength meters.
　　VI. All types of keys for radio use.
　　VII. All types of generators with an output voltage of 150 volts or more.
　　VIII. Other accessories specifically for transmitters are also subject to control.
　　IX. Scope of equipment subject to registration: All rectifier vacuum tubes (excluding those inside assembled radios and those 12F or below). For example: 83, 80, 523, etc. All types of audio frequency amplifiers (i.e., speech amplifiers, including the sound parts of cinemas), recorders, and manufactured B batteries.

　　(Editor's note: The "Provisional Administrative Regulations for City Lodging Industry" etc. will be published successively.)

　　受管制无线电器材范围表
    一、整架无线电发射机、收报机及能收电报之收音机（包括收发话报机，电力不论大小）。
    二、专供无线电用之发射真空管及输出在二瓦特以上之一切新旧式真空管。例如：旧式三○三三  四三  四五  六Ｌ六  六Ｖ六  八○七
  一六二五等等。新式：六ＶＢ六  六ＡＨ五  三Ａ五  二Ｂ六  一Ｈ四Ｇ  八一三等等。日本式：五一○Ｂ  八四五  八一四等等。
    三、输出直流电压二百五十伏特以上之成件整流器。
    四、耐电压六百伏特以上之各型储电器。
    五、各型千分安培表天线流表及周率波长表。
    六、各型无线电用之电键。
    七、各型输出电压在一百五十伏特以上之发电机。
    八、发射机专用之其他附件也在管制之列。
    九、应进行登记器材范围：全部之整流用真空管
    （成件收音机内者及十二Ｆ以下者不受此限）
