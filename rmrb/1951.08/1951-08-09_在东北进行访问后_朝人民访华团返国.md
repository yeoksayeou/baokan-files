Title: Korean People's Delegation to China Returns Home After Visiting Northeast

Author:

Date: 1951-08-09
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Shenyang, 7th. The Korean People's Delegation and Art Troupe to China, after visiting Shenyang, Changchun, Harbin and other cities, returned to Korea via Andong on the 4th. The delegation arrived in Shenyang on July 25th from Tianjin. During their stay in Shenyang, they presented flags and gifts to the working people of the city, families of the Chinese People's Volunteers, Gao Gang, Chairman of the Northeast People's Government, Vice-Chairmen Li Fuchun and Lin Feng, and Shenyang Mayor Zhu Qiwen, etc. They also held forums with workers, women, youth, literary and art circles, and industrial and commercial circles in Shenyang to exchange experiences in the anti-American struggle of the Chinese and Korean people. On July 30th, the delegation arrived in Changchun from Shenyang and presented flags to the working people of Changchun. On August 1st, the delegation arrived in Harbin. In Harbin, they attended the welcoming meetings of representatives from all walks of life in Songjiang Province and Harbin City, as well as the welcoming meetings and forums of workers, youth, women, cultural, and industrial and commercial circles in Harbin. They visited the Northeast Martyrs Memorial Hall and visited families of the Chinese People's Volunteers. On the 4th, the delegation returned to Korea via Shenyang and Andong.

### 在东北进行访问后  朝人民访华团返国

1951-08-09
第1版()
专栏：

　　在东北进行访问后
　　朝人民访华团返国
    【新华社沈阳七日电】朝鲜人民访华代表团及艺术团访问沈阳、长春、哈尔滨等市以后，已于四日经安东返回朝鲜。该代表团于七月二十五日由天津到达沈阳市，在沈阳逗留期间，曾向市区劳动人民、志愿军家属、东北人民政府主席高岗、副主席李富春、林枫，以及沈阳市长朱其文等献旗献礼；并与沈阳市工人、妇女、青年、文艺界、工商界等人士分别举行座谈会，交流中朝人民抗美斗争的经验。七月三十日该代表团由沈阳抵达长春，向长春市劳动人民献旗。八月一日，该团到达哈尔滨。他们在哈尔滨市先后出席了松江省和哈尔滨市各界代表的欢迎大会及哈尔滨市职工、青年、妇女、文化、工商界的欢迎会和座谈会；参观了东北烈士纪念馆；访问了中国人民志愿军家属。四日，该代表团经沈阳转安东返回朝鲜。
