Title: Notice from the News Office of the Beijing Municipal People's Government
Author:
Date: 1951-08-18
Page: 2

Notice from the News Office of the Beijing Municipal People's Government

The draft of the "Instructions of the Beijing Municipal People's Government on the Democratic Election of District and Village People's Governments" issued by this office on the 15th of this month (see page 3 of the People's Daily on August 17, page 1 of the Guangming Daily on August 17, and page 2 of the Xinmin Daily on August 16) was an unfinalized draft and should be withdrawn. The full text will be published separately.
