Title: Our Great Motherland (Picture)
Author:
Date: 1951-08-08
Page: 1

    Our Great Motherland
    Good wheat seeds are an important condition for a bumper wheat harvest. This old farmer woman from Su County, northern Anhui, holding a bundle of plump wheat ears, tells her neighbors: "If it weren't for the wheat seeds the people's government lent me last year, how could I have such a good harvest!"

### 我们伟大的祖国（图片）

1951-08-08
第1版()
专栏：

    我们伟大的祖国
    优良的麦种是小麦丰收的重要条件。这位皖北宿县的老农妇，搂着一捆颗粒肥大的麦穗，告诉邻人们说：“没有去年人民政府贷给我的麦种，那有这样好的收成呀！”
