Title: Our Great Motherland (Picture)
Author:
Date: 1951-08-06
Page: 1

    Our Great Motherland
    Joining the People's Air Force to defend the skies of our great motherland and prevent invaders from attacking us from the air is the ideal of many young people in New China. This Young Pioneer is playing with a glider model, and he is determined to be a heroic fighter in the Chinese People's Air Force.

### 我们伟大的祖国（图片）

1951-08-06
第1版()
专栏：

    我们伟大的祖国
    参加人民空军，保卫我们伟大祖国的天空，不许侵略者从空中侵犯我们，这是许多新中国少年的理想。这个少年儿童队员正在玩着滑翔机模型，他决心要做中国人民空军的一个英雄战士。
