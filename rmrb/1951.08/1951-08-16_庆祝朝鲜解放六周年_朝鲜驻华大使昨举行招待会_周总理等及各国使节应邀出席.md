Title: Celebrating the Sixth Anniversary of the Liberation of Korea, the Korean Ambassador to China Held a Reception Yesterday. Premier Zhou and Other Ambassadors from Various Countries Were Invited to Attend
Author:
Date: 1951-08-16
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, August 15th—
　　To celebrate the sixth anniversary of the "August 15th" liberation of Korea, the Ambassador of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea to China, Li Zhouyuan, held a reception on the evening of the 15th.
　　Those invited to attend included Premier and Foreign Minister of the Central People's Government Zhou Enlai, Vice Premiers of the Government Administration Council Dong Biwu and Guo Moruo, Acting Chief of the General Staff of the Chinese People's Revolutionary Military Commission Nie Rongzhen, President of the Supreme People's Court Shen Junru, Vice Chairman of the National Committee of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference Chen Shutong, and Ma Yinchu, Ma Xulun, Shen Yanbing, Cai Tingkai, Peng Zemin, Zhang Bojun, Fu Zuoyi, Zhang Xiruo, Luo Longji, Li Shucheng, Liang Xi, Shi Liang, Zhang Hantian, Wu Xiuquan, Yang Qiqing, Yao Yilin, Yang Weiyu and other heads of various departments of the Central People's Government, responsible persons of various democratic parties, and Vice Mayor of the Beijing Municipal People's Government Zhang Youyu, totaling more than 300 people.
　　Diplomatic envoys to China who attended were: Romanian Ambassador Rudenko, Hungarian Ambassador Szafrankó, Chargé d'Affaires ad interim of the Soviet Embassy Fedlin, Chargé d'Affaires ad interim of the Mongolian Embassy Labudan, Chargé d'Affaires ad interim of the Diplomatic Mission of the German Democratic Republic Grutner, Chargé d'Affaires ad interim of the Polish Embassy Daoding, Chargé d'Affaires ad interim of the Czechoslovak Embassy Reif, Chargé d'Affaires ad interim of the Bulgarian Embassy Grigorov, First Secretary of the Vietnamese Delegation Zhou Liang, and embassy staff.
　　During the reception, the hosts and guests frequently toasted the health of Marshal Stalin, Chairman Mao Zedong, and General Kim Il-sung, and toasted the Korean People's Liberation War and the common victory of the Chinese and Korean people. The reception proceeded in a warm and friendly atmosphere. (Photo attached)

### 庆祝朝鲜解放六周年  朝鲜驻华大使昨举行招待会  周总理等及各国使节应邀出席

1951-08-16
第1版()
专栏：

　　庆祝朝鲜解放六周年
    朝鲜驻华大使昨举行招待会
    周总理等及各国使节应邀出席
    【新华社十五日讯】朝鲜民主主义人民共和国驻我国大使李周渊为庆祝“八·一五”朝鲜解放六周年，于十五日晚间举行招待会。
    应邀出席者有中央人民政府政务院总理兼外交部长周恩来，政务院副总理董必武、郭沫若，中国人民革命军事委员会代总参谋长聂荣臻，最高人民法院院长沈钧儒，中国人民政协全国委员会副主席陈叔通及马寅初、马叙伦、沈雁冰、蔡廷锴、彭泽民、章伯钧、傅作义、张奚若、罗隆基、李书城、梁希、史良、章汉天、伍修权、杨奇清、姚依林、杨卫玉等中央人民政府各部门首长、各民主党派负责人以及北京市人民政府副市长张友渔等共三百余人。
    驻我国外交使节出席者有：罗马尼亚大使鲁登科、匈牙利大使夏法朗柯、苏联大使馆临时代办费德林、蒙古大使馆临时代办拉布丹、德意志民主共和国外交使团临时代办葛鲁特纳、波兰大使馆临时代办道定、捷克斯洛伐克大使馆临时代办赖夫、保加利亚大使馆临时代办格利郭洛夫、越南代表团一等秘书周亮及使馆人员。
    在招待会进行中，宾主频频为斯大林大元帅、毛泽东主席、金日成将军的健康干杯，并为朝鲜人民解放战争，中朝人民的共同胜利干杯。招待会在热烈友好的气氛中进行。（附图片）
