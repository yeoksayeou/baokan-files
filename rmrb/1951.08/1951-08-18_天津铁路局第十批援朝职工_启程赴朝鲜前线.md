Title: The Tenth Batch of Tianjin Railway Bureau Employees Aiding Korea Depart for the Korean Front

Author:

Date: 1951-08-18
Page: 2

　　Tianjin Railway Bureau's Tenth Batch of Employees Aiding Korea
    Set Off for the Korean Front

    【Xinhua News Agency, Tianjin, 15th】 The Tianjin Railway Bureau's tenth batch of 482 volunteer employees aiding Korea departed for the Korean front on the 15th. The Tianjin Municipal Branch of the Resist-America, Aid-Korea Association and people from all walks of life held a grand send-off meeting of more than 2,400 people on the 14th.

### 天津铁路局第十批援朝职工  启程赴朝鲜前线

1951-08-18
第2版()
专栏：

　　天津铁路局第十批援朝职工
    启程赴朝鲜前线
    【新华社天津十五日电】天津铁路管理局第十批志愿援朝职工四百八十二名，于十五日出发前往朝鲜前线。天津市抗美援朝分会和各界人民曾于十四日举行了两千四百余人的盛大欢送会。
