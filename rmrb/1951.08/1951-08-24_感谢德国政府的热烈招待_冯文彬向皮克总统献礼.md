Title: To Thank the German Government for Their Warm Hospitality, Feng Wenbin Presents Gifts to President Pieck
Author:
Date: 1951-08-24
Page: 4

　　Thanks to the German Government for Their Warm Hospitality
    Feng Wenbin Presents Gifts to President Pieck
    [Xinhua News Agency, Berlin, 23rd] Feng Wenbin, head of the Chinese youth delegation participating in the World Youth and Students for Peace Festival, presented gifts to Wilhelm Pieck, President of the German Democratic Republic, and Prime Minister Otto Grotewohl on the afternoon of the 20th on behalf of the Chinese youth, expressing gratitude to the government of the German Democratic Republic and the German youth for their warm hospitality to the Chinese youth delegation during the festival. On the morning of the 21st, Feng Wenbin presented silk embroidered portraits of Marx, Engels, Lenin, and Stalin, as well as other gifts, to the Central Committee of the Socialist Unity Party of Germany and General Secretary Walter Ulbricht. Ji Pengfei, head of the Chinese diplomatic mission to Germany, attended both gift-giving ceremonies.
    After the closing of the festival, the Chinese youth delegation and performing arts troupe held a get-together with the Young Pioneers of Berlin and the People's Police of Berlin on the 20th. A group of nineteen youth representatives left Berlin on the 21st to visit various parts of the German Democratic Republic.

### 感谢德国政府的热烈招待  冯文彬向皮克总统献礼

1951-08-24
第4版()
专栏：

　　感谢德国政府的热烈招待
    冯文彬向皮克总统献礼
    【新华社柏林二十三日电】参加世界青年与学生和平联欢节的中国青年代表团团长冯文彬，于二十日下午代表中国青年向德意志民主共和国总统皮克及总理格罗提渥献礼，表示感谢德意志民主共和国政府和德国青年在联欢节期间对中国青年代表团的热烈招待。二十一日上午，冯文彬把丝质的马克思、恩格斯、列宁和斯大林绣像及其他礼物献赠德国统一社会党中央委员会及总书记乌布利希。中国驻德外交使团团长姬鹏飞曾参加这两次献礼仪式。
    中国青年代表团和文工团在联欢节闭幕后，曾于二十日与柏林的少年先锋队和柏林的人民警察举行联欢会。一部青年代表十九人已于二十一日离开柏林，到德意志民主共和国各地参观。
