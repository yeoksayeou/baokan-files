Title: World Student Summer Games Grandly Open in Berlin
Author:
Date: 1951-08-09
Page: 4

　　World Student Summer Games
    Grandly Open in Berlin

    [By Our Correspondent] According to Xinhua News Agency report: The 11th World Student Summer Games grandly opened on the 6th at the Walter Ulbricht Stadium in Berlin. This Games is being held concurrently with the World Youth and Student Festival for Peace. The various friendship competitions taking place from August 6th to 15th will demonstrate the world's youth's will to strive for international peace and friendship.

    When President Pieck and Deputy Prime Minister Ulbricht of the German Democratic Republic, as well as IUS President Grohman and members of the International Preparatory Committee for the 11th World Student Games, entered the stadium, they were warmly welcomed by German youth, foreign youth representatives, and Young Pioneers who filled the stadium. At 3:00 PM, to the strains of the World Democratic Youth Anthem, a team of 300 referees entered the stadium. Led by a giant banner of the International Union of Students, athletes from thirty-six countries marched past the podium in formation.

    International Union of Students President Grohman first extended congratulations to the athletes participating in the Games. He said: This year's World Student Summer Games is the largest and most significant of all international student sports competitions held in the past twenty-five years. The number of athletes participating in this Games is twice as many as in any previous Games.

    Deputy Prime Minister Ulbricht of the German Democratic Republic rose to speak amidst a storm of cheers. He wished that the 11th World Student Games would help strengthen the cause of world peace.

    Subsequently, the holder of the highest sports record in the German Democratic Republic, Frierst, took the oath on behalf of all the athletes, and the opening ceremony of the Games came to a close. Delegations from various countries left the stadium amidst cheers and applause.

    Finally, the student football teams of Hungary and Czechoslovakia competed. The Hungarian football team won 5-0.

    In the evening, Soviet youth artists performed spectacular programs at the Friedrichstadt-Palast.

### 世界学生夏季运动会在柏林隆重开幕

1951-08-09
第4版()
专栏：

　　世界学生夏季运动会
    在柏林隆重开幕
    【本报讯】据新华社讯：第十一届世界学生夏季运动会于六日在柏林的乌布利希运动场隆重开幕。这次运动会是与世界青年与学生和平联欢节同时举行的。从八月六日到十五日间进行的各种友谊比赛，将表明世界青年争取国际和平与友好的意志。
    德意志民主共和国总统皮克和副总理乌布利希，以及国际学联主席格罗曼和第十一届世界学生运动会国际筹备委员会委员进入运动场时，受到挤满运动场的德国青年、外国青年代表和少年先锋队的热烈欢迎。下午三点钟，随着世界民主青年进行曲的歌声，三百名裁判员组成的联队进入运动场。在巨幅国际学生联合会的横旗的引导下，三十六个国家的运动员列队走过主席台前。
    国际学生联合会主席格罗曼首先向参加运动会的选手们致贺词。他说：今年的世界学生夏季运动会是过去二十五年中所举行的一切国际学生体育竞赛中具有最大和最重要意义的一次。参加这一届运动会的运动员要比参加过去任何一届运动会的数目多一倍。
    德意志民主共和国副总理乌布利希在暴风雨般的欢呼声中起立讲话。他祝第十一届世界学生运动会将有助于加强世界和平事业。
    接着，德意志民主共和国最高运动纪录保持者弗里斯特代表全体运动员宣誓后，运动会开幕典礼乃告结束。各国代表团在欢呼声和掌声中离开运动场。
    最后，匈牙利和捷克斯洛伐克两国学生足球队举行比赛。匈牙利足球队以五比零获胜。
    晚上，苏联青年艺术家在弗利德里希斯塔德宫表演了精采节目。
