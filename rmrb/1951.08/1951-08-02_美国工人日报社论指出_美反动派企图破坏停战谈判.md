Title: U.S. Worker's Daily Editorial Points Out that American Reactionaries Attempt to Sabotage Armistice Negotiations
Author:
Date: 1951-08-02
Page: 4

　　U.S. Worker's Daily Editorial Points Out
　　American Reactionaries Attempt to Sabotage Armistice Negotiations
　　[Xinhua News Agency, 1st dispatch] According to a dispatch from Radiotelegraph Agency, New York, 31st: The New York *Worker's Daily* published an editorial on the 30th, commenting on the position adopted by the United States at the Kaesong conference. The paper warned that the Korean armistice is in danger. "Pentagon (U.S. Department of Defense) negotiators may cause the ceasefire talks to break down. Without a doubt, this is exactly what many powerful people in Washington want to see." The *Worker's Daily* editorial urged action, demanding a ceasefire and withdrawal to the 38th parallel, demanding the withdrawal of all non-Korean troops, and holding a peace conference with the People's Republic of China and the Soviet Union to conclude a five-power peace pact.

### 美国工人日报社论指出  美反动派企图破坏停战谈判

1951-08-02
第4版()
专栏：

　　美国工人日报社论指出
    美反动派企图破坏停战谈判
    【新华社一日讯】据电通社纽约三十一日电：纽约《工人日报》在三十日发表社论，评论美国在开城会议中所采取的立场。该报警告说，朝鲜休战在危险中。“五角大楼（美国国防部）的谈判人可能使停火会谈破裂。毫无疑问的，这正是华盛顿许多有权势的人们所愿意看到的事情。”《工人日报》社论促请采取行动，要求停火和撤退到三八线，要求撤退所有非朝鲜军队，并与中华人民共和国和苏联举行和平会议，以便缔结五大国和平公约。
