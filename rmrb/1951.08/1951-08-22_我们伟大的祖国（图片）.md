Title: Our Great Motherland (Picture)
Author:
Date: 1951-08-22
Page: 1

    Our Great Motherland
     Fourteen years ago today, the Chinese People's Liberation Army set off from the Shaanxi-Gansu-Ningxia Border Region to the front lines of the War of Resistance Against Japan. After the Lugouqiao Incident in 1937, the Kuomintang reactionaries' troops retreated southward one after another, but the Chinese People's Liberation Army, under the command of Chairman Mao and Commander-in-Chief Zhu, bravely advanced into the enemy's rear. This is a part of the people's cavalry that opened up the Jin-Cha-Ji Anti-Japanese Base Area in those years.

### 我们伟大的祖国（图片）

1951-08-22
第1版()
专栏：

    我们伟大的祖国
     十四年前的今天，中国人民解放军由陕甘宁边区出发开赴抗日前线。一九三七年芦沟桥事变发生后，国民党反动派的军队纷纷向南溃退，但是中国人民解放军，却在毛主席和朱总司令的指挥下，勇敢地向敌后挺进。这是当年开辟晋察冀抗日根据地的人民骑兵的一部分。
