Title: French Democratic Newspapers Comment: Welcoming the Soviet Union's Participation in the San Francisco Conference; Denouncing the U.S. Draft Treaty as a War Treaty
Author:
Date: 1951-08-25
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency, Paris, August 21st: Democratic newspapers in France, commenting on the San Francisco Peace Treaty Conference, welcomed the Soviet Union's decision to participate. The newspapers pointed out that the U.S. proposal is to sign "a war treaty," while the Soviet Union will propose a genuine peace policy.

    "L'Humanité" wrote: "The governments of certain countries have expressed satisfaction with the Soviet Union's participation in the conference. In fact, the Soviet Union's participation is the most reliable support for the vital interests of all Far Eastern countries." The newspaper pointed out that the U.S. proposal is a direct threat to the security of the various Asian nations.

    "La Nouvelle France" weekly, commenting on the U.S.'s belligerent aims, said: "A treaty that disregards the opinions of the countries concerned, a treaty that does not include China – the main victim of Japanese imperialist aggression – a treaty that deliberately undermines all previously signed Cairo Declarations, Yalta Agreements, and Potsdam Agreements, a treaty that refuses to grant reparations to the victims, a treaty that incorporates the Ryukyu Islands and the Ogasawara Islands into the United States without considering that Taiwan is Chinese territory, a treaty that stipulates the continued presence of U.S. occupation forces in Japan without restricting the rearmament of Japan; such a treaty is not a peace treaty, but a war treaty."

### 法国民主报纸著论  欢迎苏联参加旧金山会议  斥美国和约草案是战争条约

1951-08-25
第4版()
专栏：

　　法国民主报纸著论
    欢迎苏联参加旧金山会议
    斥美国和约草案是战争条约
    【新华社二十二日讯】电通社巴黎二十一日讯：法国民主报纸在评论旧金山对日和约会议时，对苏联决定参加此次会议表示欢迎。报纸指出：美国的建议是要签订“一个战争条约”，而苏联将提出一种真正的和平政策。
    《人道报》写道：“某些国家的政府已对苏联参加此次会议事表示满意。因为，实际上苏联参加会议即是远东各国切身利益最可靠的支持。”该报指出：美国的建议是对亚洲各个民族的安全的直接威胁。
    《新法兰西》周刊在评论美国的好战目的时说：“一个不考虑有关各国的意见的条约，一个不包括日本帝国主义侵略的主要受难者——中国——在内的条约，一个蓄意破坏所有以前签订的开罗宣言、雅尔塔协定、波茨坦协定的条约，一个拒绝给予受难者以赔偿的条约，一个把琉球群岛和小笠原群岛并入美国而不考虑台湾是中国领土的条约，一个规定美国占领军留驻日本而对重新武装日本不加限制的条约，这样一种条约不是和平条约，而是战争条约。”
