Our Great Motherland

The national model locomotive banner – the "Mao Zedong" locomotive – has traveled more than 250,000 kilometers without a single accident. In the patriotic production competition movement to resist U.S. aggression and aid Korea, it frequently saves fuel coal and over-axis pulls (i.e., carries more and runs faster), ensuring the completion of national transportation tasks. Comrade Li Yong, a nationally renowned labor model, was the driver chief of the train before being transferred to the position of Vice Chairman of the National Committee of the China Railway Trade Union. This is Comrade Li Yong driving the train, taking the lead in "over-axis" pulling.

### 我们伟大的祖国（图片）

1951-08-13
第1版()
专栏：

    我们伟大的祖国
    全国模范机车旗帜——“毛泽东号”机车，已经行驶二十五万多公里，没有发生一次事故。在抗美援朝爱国主义生产竞赛运动中，它经常节省燃煤，超轴牵引（即多拉快跑），保证了国家运输任务的完成。全国著名的劳动模范李永同志，在调任中国铁路工会全国委员会副主席以前是该车司机长。这是李永同志驾驶该车，带头“超轴”的情形。
