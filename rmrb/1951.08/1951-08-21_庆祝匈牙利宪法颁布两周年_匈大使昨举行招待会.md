Title: Hungarian Ambassador Holds Reception Yesterday to Celebrate the Second Anniversary of the Promulgation of the Hungarian Constitution
Author:
Date: 1951-08-21
Page: 1

　　Celebrating the Second Anniversary of the Promulgation of the Hungarian Constitution
    Hungarian Ambassador Holds Reception Yesterday
    [Xinhua News Agency, 20th] The Ambassador of the People's Republic of Hungary to China, Sándor Szirmai, held a reception at 7:00 PM on the 20th to celebrate the second anniversary of the promulgation of the Constitution of the People's Republic of Hungary.
    Those invited to attend included Shen Junru, President of the Supreme People's Court; Yu Xinqing, Director of the Bureau of Ceremonies of the Central People's Government; Wu Xiuquan, Deputy Minister of Foreign Affairs; Shi Liang, Shen Yanbing, Xu Deheng, Chen Jinkun, and Lei Renmin, heads of various departments of the Central People's Government; Zhang Youyu, Deputy Mayor of the Beijing Municipal People's Government; and responsible persons of various people's organizations, totaling more than one hundred people.
    The ambassadors of Korea, Romania, India, and Sweden, as well as the Minister of Switzerland, and officials from the embassies of the Soviet Union, Czechoslovakia, Poland, the German Democratic Republic, Mongolia, Bulgaria, Vietnam, Indonesia, and Pakistan were also invited to attend.
    The reception proceeded in a friendly atmosphere.

### 庆祝匈牙利宪法颁布两周年  匈大使昨举行招待会

1951-08-21
第1版()
专栏：

　　庆祝匈牙利宪法颁布两周年
    匈大使昨举行招待会
    【新华社二十日讯】匈牙利人民共和国驻华大使夏法朗柯，为庆祝匈牙利人民共和国宪法颁布两周年，特于二十日下午七时举行招待会。
    应邀出席的有最高人民法院院长沈钧儒，中央人民政府典礼局局长余心清，外交部副部长伍修权，中央人民政府各部门首长史良、沈雁冰、许德珩、陈瑾昆和雷任民及北京市人民政府副市长张友渔和各人民团体负责人等一百余人。
    朝鲜、罗马尼亚、印度和瑞典等国大使及瑞士公使，苏联、捷克斯洛伐克、波兰、德意志民主共和国、蒙古、保加利亚、越南、印尼、巴基斯坦等国使馆官员都应邀到会。
    招待会在友好气氛中进行。
