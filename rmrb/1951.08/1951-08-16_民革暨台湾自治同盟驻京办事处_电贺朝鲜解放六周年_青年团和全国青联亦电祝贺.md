Title: Revolutionary Committee of the Kuomintang and Taiwan Autonomous League Office in Beijing Congratulate the Sixth Anniversary of the Liberation of Korea; Communist Youth League and All-China Youth Federation Also Send Congratulatory Telegrams
Author:
Date: 1951-08-16
Page: 1

　　[Report from this newspaper] The Revolutionary Committee of the Chinese Kuomintang and the Beijing Office of the Taiwan Democratic Self-Government League have sent telegrams to congratulate the sixth anniversary of the liberation of Korea. The Central Committee of the China New Democratic Youth League and the All-China Youth Federation also sent telegrams to the Korean Democratic Youth League to express their congratulations.
    The telegram from the Revolutionary Committee of the Chinese Kuomintang to General Kim Il-sung stated: "At this moment when Chinese and Korean soldiers are jointly resisting the aggression of the American imperialists and have achieved a great victory, causing the aggressors to agree to ceasefire negotiations, we firmly support your fair and reasonable proposal to use the '38th parallel' as the military demarcation line and to expose and strike at the aggressors' conspiracies at any time."
    The telegram from the Beijing Office of the Taiwan Democratic Self-Government League to General Kim Il-sung and the entire Korean military and people stated: "Under your wise and correct leadership, we firmly believe that you will be able to stop the aggression of American imperialism and achieve the complete liberation of Korea!"
    The telegram from the Central Committee of the China New Democratic Youth League and the All-China Youth Federation to the Korean Democratic Youth League stated: "On the occasion of the sixth anniversary of the liberation of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea, the Chinese youth extend warm greetings to the heroic Korean youth and people and congratulate you on your great victory in the struggle against American aggressors for national independence. The unity and friendship between the youth of China and Korea has been increasingly consolidated in the joint struggle against our common enemy. Let us, together with all peace-loving and just youth in the world, continue to strengthen unity and work hand in hand to defend world and Asian peace."

### 民革暨台湾自治同盟驻京办事处  电贺朝鲜解放六周年  青年团和全国青联亦电祝贺

1951-08-16
第1版()
专栏：

　　民革暨台湾自治同盟驻京办事处
    电贺朝鲜解放六周年         
    青年团和全国青联亦电祝贺
    【本报讯】中国国民党革命委员会、台湾民主自治同盟驻京办事处分别致电祝贺朝鲜解放六周年。中国新民主主义青年团中央委员会和中华全国民主青年联合总会亦致电朝鲜民主青年同盟表示祝贺。
    中国国民党革命委员会致金日成将军的贺电称：“值此中朝战士共同抵抗美帝国主义者侵略，取得伟大胜利，而使侵略者不得不同意进行停火谈判之际，我们坚决拥护你们的以‘三八’线为军事分界线的公平合理的主张和对于侵略者的阴谋随时揭露与打击。”
    台湾民主自治同盟驻京办事处致金日成将军和全朝鲜军民的贺电称：“在您的英明正确领导下，深信必能制止美帝国主义的侵略，获得朝鲜的彻底解放！”
    中国新民主主义青年团中央委员会、中华全国民主青年联合总会致朝鲜民主青年同盟的贺电称：“值此朝鲜民主主义人民共和国解放六周年，中国青年谨向英勇的朝鲜青年与人民致以热烈的敬礼，并祝贺你们在抗击美国侵略者争取民族独立斗争中的伟大胜利。中、朝两国青年的团结与友谊，已在反对我们共同敌人的并肩作战中日益巩固，让我们与全世界一切爱好和平正义的青年继续加强团结，为保卫世界与亚洲和平而携手前进。”
