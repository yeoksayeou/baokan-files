Title: Typhoon Warning Lifted
Author:
Date: 1951-08-24
Page: 1

　　Typhoon Warning Lifted
    [Xinhua News Agency, 23rd] The Central Meteorological Observatory issued a typhoon cancellation warning at 8:00 PM on August 23rd: At 2:00 PM on August 23rd, the typhoon was moving northeast over the Korean Peninsula (i.e., 38 degrees north latitude, 128 degrees east longitude) at a speed of approximately 60 li per hour. The typhoon's intensity has greatly weakened. Except for the Yalu River area which will be slightly affected by rain, other areas of China are no longer affected by this typhoon.

### 台风警报已经解除

1951-08-24
第1版()
专栏：

　　台风警报已经解除
    【新华社二十三日讯】中央气象台八月二十三日下午八时发出台风解除警报：八月二十三日下午二时台风在朝鲜半岛（即北纬三十八度，东经一百二十八度）向东北方向进行，每小时移动约六十华里。台风强度已大为减弱。现除鸭绿江沿岸有雨略受影响外，我国其他地区已不再受此台风的影响。
