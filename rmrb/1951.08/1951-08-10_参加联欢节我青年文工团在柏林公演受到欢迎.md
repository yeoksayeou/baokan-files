Title: Our Youth Art Troupe Participating in the Festival Receives Warm Welcome in Berlin Performances
Author:
Date: 1951-08-10
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Berlin, 9th. Our Youth Art Troupe participating in the Third World Youth and Student Peace Festival gave its first performance at the Berlin Sports Palace on the evening of the 7th. The theater, which can accommodate more than 6,400 people, was completely full. The President of the German Democratic Republic, Wilhelm Pieck, Prime Minister Otto Grotewohl, Deputy Prime Minister Walter Ulbricht, and others attended the performance. The Yellow River Cantata, The East is Red chorus, piano and violin solos and solo vocals, as well as silk dances, stunt cycling, and the Peking opera "San Cha Kou" performed by the Art Troupe were very popular. Before the public performance, the Art Troupe performed twice at the Berlin Comedy Theater and Bebelplatz, and both received praise from the audience. The Art Troupe will perform fifteen or sixteen performances successively during the festival.

### 参加联欢节我青年文工团在柏林公演受到欢迎

1951-08-10
第4版()
专栏：

　　参加联欢节我青年文工团
    在柏林公演受到欢迎
    【新华社柏林九日电】参加第三届世界青年与学生和平联欢节的我国青年文工团，七日晚在柏林体育馆作第一次公演，可容六千四百多人的话剧场全部满座。德意志民主共和国总统皮克、总理格罗提渥、副总理乌布利希等都到场观剧。文工团演出的黄河大合唱、东方红合唱、钢琴和小提琴独奏及独唱，以及绸舞、车技和京剧三岔口等节目，很受欢迎。文工团在公演前曾在柏林喜剧院和倍倍尔广场表演两次，均获得观众的赞扬。文工团在联欢节期间将陆续演出十五六场。
