Title: High-Interest Loans Appear in Rural Areas of Jin County and Other Places; Local Governments Should Immediately Investigate and Handle Them
Author: Lin Decheng, Zhang Linquan
Date: 1951-08-22
Page: 6

(I) To the Editor:

　　High-interest loan sharks have appeared in rural areas around Beiniepan Village in Jin County, Hebei. For example, before planting cotton, if one borrows 100 jin of cotton, one must return 140 jin of cotton in the autumn (about three months later). During the cotton planting season, if one borrows 300 jin of bean cake (currently priced at 600 yuan per jin locally, worth a total of 180,000 yuan), one must return 100 jin of seed cotton when the new cotton comes on the market (currently priced at 3,200 yuan per jin for medium-grade seed cotton, worth a total of 320,000 yuan). I heard that there are more than 20 households in Beiniepan Village with such lending relationships. Although I am not yet clear on the details of this matter, I feel that the appearance of such high-interest loans is detrimental to rural production. I hope the local government can conduct investigation and research.

　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　Lin Decheng

(II) To the Editor:

　　Since the beginning of spring this year, high-interest loans have appeared in the border area between Tangyi and Qingping counties in Pingyuan Province, and Linqing county in Hebei Province. The common situation is that if one borrows one dou of corn in the spring (about 16,000 yuan per dou), one must return one dou of wheat by the Mid-Autumn Festival (about 31,000 yuan per dou). Or, if one borrows 100 jin of seed cotton in the spring (highest price 300,000 yuan), one must return one shi and five dou of wheat in the autumn (more than 310,000 yuan per shi). Such high-interest loans are very detrimental to rural production. I hope the government will pay attention to this matter.

　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　Zhang Linquan, Hougu Town, Fourth District, Tangyi County, Pingyuan Province

### 晋县等地农村出现高利贷  当地政府应即进行调查处理
林德成　张林泉
1951-08-22
第6版()
专栏：

　　晋县等地农村出现高利贷
    当地政府应即进行调查处理
  （一）编辑同志：
    河北晋县北捏盘村一带农村中，出现了高利贷者，例如在植棉前出借棉花一百斤，到秋后（约隔三个多月）就要还棉花一百四十斤。在植棉期间贷出豆饼三百斤（目前当地市价每斤六百元，共值十八万元），到新棉上市时要还籽棉一百斤（目前当地市价中级籽棉每斤三千二百元，共值三十二万元）。听说北捏盘村一共有二十多家有这样借贷关系的。这件事情的详细情况虽然我还弄不清楚，但是我觉得这种高利贷的出现是不利于农村生产的，希望当地政府能进行调查研究。
        林德成
  （二）编辑同志：
    今年入春以来，平原省堂邑、清平和河北省临清等县的交界地带，出现了高利贷。普通情况是春天借进玉米一斗（每斗约一万六千元），到中秋节要还小麦一斗（每斗约三万一千元）。或者是春天借进一百斤籽棉（最高价三十万元）秋后还一石五斗麦子（每石三十一万元以上）。这种高利贷，对农村生产很不利。希望政府注意此事。
          平原省堂邑县四区堠堌镇  张林泉
