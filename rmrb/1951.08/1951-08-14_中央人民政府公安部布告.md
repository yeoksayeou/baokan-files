Title: Proclamation of the Ministry of Public Security of the Central People's Government
Author:
Date: 1951-08-14
Page: 2

In order to prevent and restrict the conspiracies of enemy agents, bandits, and other illegal elements, and to protect the legitimate operations of legal businesses, so as to consolidate social order, the "Provisional Regulations for the Administration of Radio Equipment," "Provisional Regulations for the Administration of Urban Hotels," "Provisional Regulations for the Administration of Public Entertainment Venues," and "Provisional Regulations for the Administration of the Seal Carving Industry" are hereby formulated and were approved by the Political and Legal Committee of the State Council of the Central People's Government on June 30 of this year. They are now promulgated, and it is hoped that all regions will comply with and implement them.

This order. (No separate document will be issued)

### 中央人民政府公安部布告

1951-08-14
第2版()
专栏：

　　中央人民政府公安部布告
    为防止与限制敌特、盗匪及其他不法分子的阴谋活动，保护合法营业的正当经营，以巩固社会治安，特制定《无线电器材管理暂行条例》、《城市旅栈业暂行管理规则》、《公共娱乐场所暂行管理规则》、《印铸刻字业暂行管理规则》四个条例，并经中央人民政府政务院政治法律委员会于本年六月三十日批准。兹公布，希各地遵照执行。
    此令                                  （不另行文）
