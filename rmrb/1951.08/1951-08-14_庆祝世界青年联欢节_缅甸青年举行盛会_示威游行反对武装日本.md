Title: Celebrating the World Youth Festival, Burmese Youth Hold Grand Gathering, Demonstration Protests Against Arming Japan
Author:
Date: 1951-08-14
Page: 4

　　Celebrating the World Youth Festival
    Burmese Youth Hold Grand Gathering
    Demonstration Protests Against Arming Japan
    [Xinhua News Agency, 12th Dispatch from Yangon] A Burmese youth celebration of the Third World Democratic Youth and Student Peace Festival was held in Yangon on the 5th. Over 2,500 Burmese youth representatives participated in the conference. The Chairman of the conference, Ba Swe, pointed out in his speech that this conference demonstrated an unprecedented great unity of all sectors of Burmese youth. He criticized the Burmese government's refusal to issue national visas to the twenty-three Burmese youth representatives preparing to go to Berlin. He pointed out that opposing war is a major task for youth and students. He emphasized: "American imperialists have already invaded Burma, and we must drive them out!"
    Other youth representatives unanimously expressed their opposition to American imperialist aggression in their speeches and called on all Burmese youth and students to fight for the defense of world peace.
    The conference passed nine resolutions, including supporting all resolutions on defending world peace passed by the Berlin World Youth Festival, opposing the arming of Japan and West Germany, requiring that all countries participating in the anti-fascist war be invited when treaties of peace are signed with Germany and Japan, immediately stopping the Korean War, demanding that the United States immediately withdraw from Taiwan, and demanding the conclusion of a five-power peace pact.
    Youth of all ethnic groups participating in the conference also held a signature campaign against the arming of Japan and held a demonstration parade after the conference.
    The conference held a photo exhibition of the Chinese, Soviet, and Vietnamese youth and student movements and performed various recreational programs, which was very enthusiastic.

### 庆祝世界青年联欢节  缅甸青年举行盛会  示威游行反对武装日本

1951-08-14
第4版()
专栏：

　　庆祝世界青年联欢节
    缅甸青年举行盛会
    示威游行反对武装日本
    【新华社十二日讯】仰光消息：缅甸青年庆祝第三届世界民主青年和学生和平联欢节大会于五日在仰光举行。参加大会的有缅甸青年代表二千五百多人。大会主席八莫阵旺在演说中指出：这次大会表现了缅甸各界青年空前的大团结。他指责缅甸政府拒绝发出国签证给二十三名准备赴柏林的缅甸青年代表的措施。他指出反对战争是青年和学生的重大任务。他强调说：“美国帝国主义者已经侵入缅甸，我们要把他们赶出去！”
    其他青年代表们在发言中一致表示反对美帝国主义侵略，并号召全缅青年和学生为保卫世界和平而奋斗。
    大会通过了九项决议，其中包括拥护柏林世界青年联欢节大会通过的关于保卫世界和平的一切决议，反对武装日本与西德，要求对德、对日签订和约时应邀请一切参加反法西斯战争的国家参加，迅速停止朝鲜战争，要求美国迅速退出台湾，要求缔结五大国和平公约等。
    参加大会的各族青年并举行了反对武装日本签名运动，并在会后举行了示威游行。
    大会举办了中国、苏联和越南青年、学生运动的图片展览，并演出了各种游艺节目，情况甚为热烈。
