Title: Vietnamese People's Delegation Arrives in Tianjin
Author:
Date: 1951-08-12
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Tianjin, 10th. The Vietnamese People's Delegation, led by Head of Delegation Hoang Quoc Viet, arrived in Tianjin from Beijing this morning. More than 500 people, including Tianjin Deputy Mayor Zhou Shutao, Director of the Foreign Affairs Office of the Municipal People's Government Zhang Wenjin, and representatives from democratic parties and people's organizations in Tianjin, went to the station to welcome them. The welcoming party held a welcome meeting at the station. Deputy Mayor Zhou Shutao delivered a welcoming speech at the meeting, and Head of Delegation Hoang Quoc Viet delivered a reply.

That afternoon, the delegation visited the city of Tianjin, and at 7 p.m., the Tianjin Municipal People's Government hosted a banquet.

### 越人民访华代表团抵天津

1951-08-12
第4版()
专栏：

　　越人民访华代表团抵天津
    【新华社天津十日电】越南人民访华代表团在黄国越团长的率领下，于今天上午由北京抵达天津。前往车站欢迎的有天津市副市长周叔韬、市人民政府外事处处长章文晋及天津市各民主党派、人民团体代表等五百余人。赴站欢迎人员并在车站举行欢迎会。会上由周叔韬副市长致欢迎词，黄国越团长致答词。
    当日下午该团参观了天津市市容，晚七时由天津市人民政府设宴招待。
