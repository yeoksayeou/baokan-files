Title: Polish Foreign Minister Sends Note to U.S. Ambassador: Poland to Participate in San Francisco Conference
Author:
Date: 1951-08-21
Page: 4

　　Xinhua News Agency, Warsaw, August 19th: On August 16th, the Foreign Minister of the Polish Democratic Republic, Mr. Skrzeszewski, received the U.S. Ambassador, Mr. Flack, concerning the issue of the U.S. and British governments inviting Poland to attend the San Francisco conference on the Japanese Peace Treaty scheduled for September 4, 1951.

　　Foreign Minister Skrzeszewski handed the U.S. Ambassador a note stating that the Polish government would participate in the conference.

　　The Polish government has appointed the following representatives to attend the San Francisco conference: Deputy Foreign Minister Mr. Wierblowski (Head of Delegation), the Polish Democratic Republic's Ambassador to the U.S., Mr. Winiewicz, and Professor Lachs, Director of the Treaty and Legal Department of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs.

### 波外长照会美大使  波兰将参加旧金山会议

1951-08-21
第4版()
专栏：

　　波外长照会美大使
    波兰将参加旧金山会议
    【新华社二十日讯】塔斯社华沙十九日讯：八月十六日，波兰民主共和国外交部长斯克列杰夫斯基为美英两国政府邀请波兰参加一九五一年九月四日在旧金山召开的对日和约会议问题，接见美国大使佛拉克。
    斯克列杰夫斯基外长递给美国大使一个照会，声明波兰政府将参加会议。
    波兰政府已委派下列代表出席旧金山会议：副外长威尔勃洛夫斯基（代表团团长），波兰民主共和国驻美大使温尼维兹，外交部条约法律司司长莱克斯教授。
