Title: Enterprises Implementing Labor Insurance Nationwide Reach Over Two Thousand Four Hundred and Forty Units
Author:
Date: 1951-08-12
Page: 2

　　Across the country, the number of factories, mines, and enterprises approved to implement labor insurance has reached 2,445 as of the end of June, with a total of 2,178,400 employees. The labor insurance card registration work has also been basically completed.
                                                    (Xinhua News Agency)

### 全国实行劳动保险的企业已达两千四百四十余单位

1951-08-12
第2版()
专栏：

　　全国实行劳动保险的企业已达两千四百四十余单位
    截至六月底止，全国被批准实施劳动保险的厂、矿企业单位已达二千四百四十五个，职工人数为二百一十七万八千四百人，劳动保险的卡片登记工作也已基本完成。
                                                    （新华社）
