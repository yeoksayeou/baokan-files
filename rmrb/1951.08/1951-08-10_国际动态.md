　　International News

　　Don't Allow Farmland to Become Training Grounds
　　Seven thousand five hundred villagers in the Hanmerburg area of West Germany recently held a rally to protest the American soldiers' attempt to force them to move out of the area in order to build a tank training ground. A farmer said at the rally: "We farmers have been inseparable from our land for generations. No matter what the situation, we will not leave our hometown. If force is to be used against us, we will resist to the end."
　　The people of West Germany have answered the American war policy with a firm attitude.

　　Shameful Hooligan Behavior
　　The holding of the Berlin World Youth Festival has caused great consternation among the warmongers. Imperialist reactionaries have used all kinds of vile means, from slander to batons, in an attempt to prevent youth from various countries from meeting in Berlin. In Vienna, French and Austrian police even resorted to thuggish hooliganism, attacking representatives traveling by train to attend the festival, injuring twelve youth representatives.
　　The thuggish behavior will never achieve their vile purposes, but will only arouse greater anger and hatred among the people.

### 国际动态

1951-08-10
第4版()
专栏：国际动态

　　国际动态
    不准把耕地变成练兵场
    西德汉麦尔堡地区的乡村居民七千五百人，最近举行大会，抗议美国兵为了建立一个坦克练兵场，而要强迫他们迁出该区。一个农民在大会上说：“我们农民世世代代是跟我们的土地分不开的。不管在什么样的情形之下，我们都不离开我们的故乡。倘使要对我们采用武力，我们将抵抗到底。”
    西德人民已经用坚决的态度回答了美国的战争政策。
    可耻的流氓行为
    柏林世界青年联欢节的举行，引起了战争贩子们的极大惊恐。帝国主义反动派用了从诽谤到警棍的一切卑劣手段，妄图阻挠各国青年到柏林去会面。在维也纳，法国和奥地利的警察竟施展出打手的流氓行为，袭击乘坐火车前往出席联欢节的代表，因而使十二位青年代表受了伤。
    打手的行为决不能达到他们卑劣的目的，而只有激起人民更大的愤怒与仇恨。
