Title: US Congress Passes New "Defense Production" Act, Even More Favorable to War Profiteers Than the Original Law

Author:

Date: 1951-08-02
Page: 4

　　Xinhua News Agency, Washington, 31st (TASS dispatch): The US Congress has passed a new "Defense Production" Act, which is even more favorable to monopoly capitalists and war profiteers than the current law expiring on July 31.

　　The new law retains the wage freeze clause from the existing law, but greatly relaxes the price "control" clauses. In addition, the new law allows rent increases of up to 20 percent in areas where rent "control" is in effect. Note: The original "Defense Production" Act was passed after the outbreak of the Korean War, granting the President wartime economic dictatorial powers such as mobilizing manpower and materials, and implementing various economic controls.

### 美国会通过新“国防生产”法  较原订法律更利于战争投机商人

1951-08-02
第4版()
专栏：

　　美国会通过新“国防生产”法
    较原订法律更利于战争投机商人
    【新华社一日讯】塔斯社华盛顿三十一日讯：美国国会通过了一个新的“国防生产”法，它甚至比七月三十一日到期的现行法律还要更有利于垄断资本家与战争投机商。
    新法律一方面仍然保留现行法律中的冻结工资条款，另一方面却大大地放松了物价“管制”条款。此外，新法律还允许在实行房租“管制”地区的房租可以增加百分之二十。按：原有“国防生产”法是在朝鲜战争爆发后通过，它授予总统以如动员人力、物资和实施各种经济管制等各项战时经济独裁权力。
