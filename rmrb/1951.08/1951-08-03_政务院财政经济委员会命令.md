Order of the Financial and Economic Committee of the Government Administration Council

Based on the "Decision on Clearing Assets and Verifying Funds of State-Owned Enterprises" of this Committee, the "Interim Measures for Asset Clearing and Valuation of State-Owned Enterprises" and the "Interim Measures for Fund Verification of State-Owned Enterprises" are hereby formulated and promulgated for your compliance and execution! This order.

　　　　　　　Chairman Chen Yun
　　　　　　　　July 31st

### 政务院财政经济委员会命令

1951-08-03
第2版()
专栏：

　　政务院财政经济委员会命令
    兹根据本委《关于国营企业清理资产核定资金的决定》特制定《国营企业资产清理及估价暂行办法》与《国营企业资金核定暂行办法》，随令颁发，希即遵照执行！此令。
                主任  陈云
              七月三十一日
