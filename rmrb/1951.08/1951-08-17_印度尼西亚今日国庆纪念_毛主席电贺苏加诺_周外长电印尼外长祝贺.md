Title: Indonesia Celebrates National Day Today; Chairman Mao Sends Congratulatory Telegram to Sukarno; Foreign Minister Zhou Sends Congratulations to Indonesian Foreign Minister
Author:
Date: 1951-08-17
Page: 1
　　Xinhua News Agency, August 16th: August 17th marks the National Day of the Republic of Indonesia. Chairman Mao Zedong of the Central People's Government and Foreign Minister Zhou Enlai sent telegrams to President Sukarno and Foreign Minister Subardjo of the Republic of Indonesia, respectively, to express their congratulations. The telegrams are as follows:
　　　　Chairman Mao's Congratulatory Telegram to His Excellency President Sukarno of the Republic of Indonesia:
　　On the occasion of your country's National Day, on behalf of the government and people of the People's Republic of China, I extend my heartfelt congratulations to Your Excellency and the people of Indonesia, and wish the friendship between our two peoples will be further consolidated and developed.
　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　Chairman Mao Zedong of the Central People's Government of the People's Republic of China
　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　August 14, 1951, Beijing
　　　　Foreign Minister Zhou's Congratulatory Telegram to His Excellency Foreign Minister Subardjo of the Republic of Indonesia:
　　On the occasion of your country's National Day, I hereby extend my congratulations.
　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　Minister of Foreign Affairs Zhou Enlai
　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　August 14, 1951, Beijing

### 印度尼西亚今日国庆纪念  毛主席电贺苏加诺  周外长电印尼外长祝贺

1951-08-17
第1版()
专栏：

　　印度尼西亚今日国庆纪念
    毛主席电贺苏加诺
    周外长电印尼外长祝贺
    【新华社十六日讯】八月十七日为印度尼西亚共和国国庆纪念，我中央人民政府毛泽东主席与周恩来外交部长分别致电印度尼西亚共和国苏加诺总统与苏巴佐外交部长，表示祝贺。电文如下：
            毛主席贺电印度尼西亚共和国总统苏加诺先生：
    欣逢贵国国庆纪念，我代表中华人民共和国政府及人民谨向阁下及印度尼西亚人民致庆贺之忱，并祝贵我两国人民的友谊日益巩固与发展。
                           中华人民共和国中央人民政府主席毛泽东
                                     一九五一年八月十四日于北京
            周外长贺电印度尼西亚共和国外交部部长苏巴佐先生：
    欣逢贵国国庆纪念，特此致贺。
                                   中华人民共和国中央人民政府
                                               外交部部长周恩来
                                     一九五一年八月十四日于北京
