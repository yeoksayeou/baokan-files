Title: Under the Oppression of Heavy Taxes and Rising Prices, Suicide Cases Among Japanese People Surge
Author:
Date: 1951-08-27
Page: 4

　　Xinhua News Agency, August 22nd, TASS, August 21st: Due to unbearable heavy taxes and the steady rise in prices of daily necessities, due to unemployment and the continuous deterioration of living conditions, suicide cases in Japan have surged. Family suicides also frequently occur.
　　According to a survey by the Japanese National Police Agency, 21,702 people committed suicide last year. This shows that an average of sixty people committed suicide in Japan every day last year. In Tokyo alone, there are three suicide cases every day.

### 在重税盘剥及物价高涨压迫下  日本人民自杀事件激增

1951-08-27
第4版()
专栏：

　　在重税盘剥及物价高涨压迫下
    日本人民自杀事件激增
    【新华社二十二日讯】塔斯社二十一日讯：由于难以忍受的重税及日用必需品价格的步步上涨，由于失业以及生活条件的不断恶化，日本的自杀事件已经激增。全家自杀的事件也常常发生。
    根据日本国家警察本部的调查，去年有二万一千七百零二人自杀。这说明了日本去年平均每天有六十人自杀。仅在东京一地，每天就有自杀事件三起。
