Title: Government Administration Council Holds Meeting
Author:
Date: 1951-08-04
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, 3rd — The 96th Government Administration Council meeting of the Central People's Government was held on the afternoon of the 3rd. The meeting heard and approved the report on the suppression of counter-revolutionaries made by Luo Ruiqing, Minister of Public Security of the Central People's Government, and adopted the Government Administration Council's decision on the relationship of local level consultative committees. The meeting also passed supplementary regulations regarding the functions and powers of the standing committees of people's representative conferences at the county level, and the general rules for the organization of consultative committees of people's representative conferences at the provincial and municipal levels. The aforementioned supplementary regulations and organizational rules will be submitted to the Chairman of the Central People's Government for approval and promulgation. In addition, the meeting approved several appointment cases and submitted several appointment and removal cases to the Central People's Government Committee for approval.

### 政务院举行会议

1951-08-04
第1版()
专栏：

　　政务院举行会议
    【新华社三日讯】中央人民政府政务院第九十六次政务会议于三日下午举行。会议听取和批准了中央人民政府公安部罗瑞卿部长所作的关于镇压反革命的报告，通过了政务院关于地方各级协商委员会的关系的决定。会议并通过了关于县各界人民代表会议常务委员会职权的补充规定，省、市各界人民代表会议协商委员会组织通则。上述补充规定和组织通则将呈请中央人民政府主席批准后公布。此外，会议通过任命案及提请中央人民政府委员会批准任免案多起。
