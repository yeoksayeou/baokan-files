Title: Central Committee of the Workers' Party of Korea Issues Slogans Commemorating "August 15th," Calls on People to Enhance Vigilance and Fight Resolutely
Author:
Date: 1951-08-14
Page: 4
　
　　Xinhua News Agency, Pyongyang, August 13th: The Central Committee of the Workers' Party of Korea issued commemorative slogans on August 12th celebrating the sixth anniversary of the "August 15th" Liberation. The slogans begin by stating: "Long live the sixth anniversary of 'August 15th,' when Korea was liberated from Japanese imperialist colonial rule by the force of the great Soviet army! Long live the great Soviet Union, the liberator of the Korean people and the giver of sincere assistance in the Korean people's struggle for national reunification, independence, and democratic development! Long live the eternal fraternal friendship between the great Soviet people and the Korean people! Victory and glory to the heroic officers and soldiers of the People's Army and male and female guerrillas who resolutely defend the independence and freedom of the motherland in the great Fatherland Liberation War against the American aggressors and the Syngman Rhee bandits! Victory and glory to all the commanders and fighters of the Chinese People's Volunteers who volunteered to participate in the liberation war of our country to resist the common enemy of the Korean and Chinese peoples – the American imperialist aggressors! Long live the profound friendship and consolidated unity of the Korean and Chinese peoples, forged in blood in the struggle to eliminate the common enemy of the two peoples! Express the nationwide gratitude to the freedom-loving people of the Soviet Union, the People's Republic of China, Poland, Czechoslovakia, Hungary, Romania, Mongolia, Bulgaria, Albania, the German Democratic Republic, and other countries who have given great assistance in the great Fatherland Liberation War for the victory of the Korean people!"

　　The slogans continued: "Dear compatriots, brothers, and sisters: The unification, independence, freedom, democracy, and peace of the motherland can only be achieved through the firm unity and indomitable struggle of the people. Strengthen the iron-like unity and invincible strength in the cruel war! All the people of the Republic: The enemy is cunning and vicious, expose and smash any enemy conspiracies in a timely manner, and raise the vigilance of all the people!"

　　The slogans called on all officers and soldiers of the People's Army to fight resolutely, further strengthen the combat friendship with the Chinese People's Volunteers, and more bravely eliminate the American aggressors. The slogans called on workers and peasants to rapidly restore production, increase production, and support the front lines. The slogans called on women and youth to more bravely participate in the struggle to eliminate the enemy and support the front lines.

　　The slogans called: "Brave and respectable male and female guerrillas: Further expand and strengthen the guerrilla forces behind enemy lines, strengthen ties with the people, and establish liberated areas everywhere! All compatriots behind enemy lines: The American aggressors brought in by the Syngman Rhee bandits are humiliating and slaughtering your parents, brothers, and wives, and destroying cities and villages. You must raise your national hatred for the enemy and launch a struggle for revenge! Congratulations to the patriotic compatriots, brothers and sisters, who are bravely resisting the struggle against American imperialism and the Syngman Rhee bandits in the enemy-occupied areas. Victory and glory belong to you!"

　　The slogans concluded: "Glory to the wounded veterans who were wounded in the heroic struggle for the independence, freedom, honor, and happiness of the motherland and future generations. All the people must cherish and respect them and strengthen the work of providing them with care! The great achievements of the heroic Soviet officers and soldiers who sacrificed themselves for the liberation of our motherland and the freedom of the Korean people in the struggle against Japanese imperialists will be immortal! Glory to the heroic officers and soldiers of the People's Army, male and female guerrillas, and patriotic martyrs who sacrificed themselves in the heroic struggle to win the independence, freedom, and honor of the motherland! Glory to the heroic commanders and fighters of the Chinese People's Volunteers who sacrificed themselves in the liberation war to defend the People's Republic of China and defend our country from the bloody claws of American imperialism! Long live the iron-like international unity of the peace-loving people of the world, headed by the great Soviet people, who are fighting against instigators of a new war and for lasting peace and security for mankind! Long live the Workers' Party of Korea, the inspirer and organizer of the victory of the great Korean people's Fatherland Liberation War! Long live the Fatherland Unification Democratic Front! Long live the Democratic People's Republic of Korea! Long live the unified Korean people! Long live General Kim Il-sung, the respected leader of the Korean people, the Supreme Commander of the People's Army, and the Premier of the Republic's Cabinet! Long live Chairman Mao Zedong, the leader of the Chinese people, the founder of the People's Republic of China, and the sincere aid to our liberation war! Long live Marshal Stalin, the brilliant leader of the working people of the world, the liberator and savior of the Korean people, and the closest friend!"

### 朝鲜劳动党中央委员会  发布纪念“八、一五”口号  号召人民提高警惕性坚决战斗

1951-08-14
第4版()
专栏：

　　朝鲜劳动党中央委员会
    发布纪念“八、一五”口号 
    号召人民提高警惕性坚决战斗
    【新华社平壤十三日电】朝鲜劳动党中央委员会十二日发布庆祝“八、一五”解放六周年的纪念口号。口号首称：朝鲜由于伟大苏军的武力从日本帝国主义殖民地统治下获得解放的“八、一五”六周年万岁！朝鲜人民的解放者和在朝鲜人民争取祖国统一独立和民主发展的斗争中给予真诚援助的伟大的苏联万岁！伟大的苏联人民和朝鲜人民的永恒的兄弟的友谊万岁！胜利和光荣归于在反抗美国侵略者与李承晚匪帮的伟大的祖国解放战争中坚决保卫祖国独立自由的英雄的人民军官兵和男女游击队员！胜利和光荣归于为反抗朝中两国人民的共同仇敌——美帝国主义侵略者而志愿参加我祖国解放战争的中国人民志愿军全体指战员！在消灭朝中两国人民的共同仇敌的斗争中用鲜血结成的朝鲜人民和中国人民的深厚友谊与巩固团结万岁！向在伟大的祖国解放战争中为了朝鲜人民的胜利而给予巨大援助的苏联、中华人民共和国、波兰、捷克斯洛伐克、匈牙利、罗马尼亚、蒙古、保加利亚、阿尔巴尼亚、德意志民主共和国及其他各国爱好自由的人民表示全民的感谢！
    口号继称：同胞兄弟姊妹们：祖国的统一、独立、自由、民主、和平，是只有以人民的坚强团结和不屈的斗争才能取得的。加强在残酷的战争中的钢铁般的团结和不可争服的力量！共和国的全体人民：敌人是狡猾而凶恶的，及时揭露和粉碎敌人的任何阴谋诡计，提高全体人民的警惕性！
    口号号召人民军全体官兵坚决战斗，进一步加强与中国人民志愿军的战斗友谊，更加勇敢地消灭美国侵略者。口号号召工人、农民迅速恢复生产、增加生产、支援前线。口号号召妇女和青年更加勇敢地参加消灭敌人、支援前线的斗争。
    口号号召：勇敢可敬的男女游击队员们：进一步扩大和加强敌后游击队伍，加强与人民的联系，到处建立解放区！敌后的全体同胞们：李承晚匪帮引进来的美国侵略者凌辱和屠杀着你们的父母兄弟妻子，破坏了城市和农村，你们要提高对敌人的民族仇恨，展开复仇的斗争！向在敌占区英勇进行反抗美帝国主义和李承晚匪帮的斗争的爱国同胞兄弟姊妹祝贺，胜利和光荣属于你们！
    口号最后称：光荣归于在为祖国的独立、自由、荣誉和后代幸福的英勇斗争中负伤的荣誉军人，全体人民要爱护和尊敬他们，加强对他们的抚恤工作！在对日本帝国主义者的斗争中为了我们祖国的解放和朝鲜人民的自由而牺牲的英雄的苏军官兵的伟大功勋，永垂不朽！在争取祖国独立自由荣誉的英勇斗争中牺牲的英雄的人民军官兵、男女游击队员和爱国烈士们光荣不朽！在为保卫中华人民共和国和从美帝国主义血爪中保卫我国的解放战争中牺牲的英雄的中国人民志愿军指战员们光荣不朽！为反对新战争挑拨者、争取人类持久和平与安全而斗争的以伟大的苏联人民为首的全世界爱好和平人民钢铁般的国际团结万岁！伟大的朝鲜人民祖国解放战争的胜利鼓舞者和组织者——朝鲜劳动党万岁！祖国统一民主主义战线万岁！朝鲜民主主义人民共和国万岁！统一的朝鲜人民万岁！朝鲜人民敬爱的领袖、人民军最高司令官、共和国内阁首相金日成将军万岁！中国人民领袖、中华人民共和国的创建者、诚挚援助我们解放战争的毛泽东主席万岁！全世界劳动人民的英明领袖、朝鲜人民的解放救星和最亲密的朋友——伟大的斯大林大元帅万岁！
