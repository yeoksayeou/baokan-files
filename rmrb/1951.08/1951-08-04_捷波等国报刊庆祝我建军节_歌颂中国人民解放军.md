Title: Czechoslovakian, Polish and Other Countries' Newspapers Celebrate the Anniversary of the Founding of Our Army, Praising the Chinese People's Liberation Army
Author:
Date: 1951-08-04
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency, Prague, 3rd - Czechoslovakian and Polish newspapers widely introduce the Chinese People's Liberation Army. The Czechoslovakian People's Army organ, *People's Defense*, and the pictorial *Our Army* both published special issues commemorating the anniversary of the founding of the Chinese People's Liberation Army. *People's Defense* published General Xiao Hua's article, "The Chinese Communist Party and the Chinese People's Liberation Army," and a paper analyzing how the Liberation Army defeated its powerful enemies. The *Our Army* pictorial featured a large photograph of Commander-in-Chief Zhu De, a paper entitled "China Points Out the Road for the Southeast Asian Struggle Against Imperialism," and other articles introducing the history of the founding and the achievements of the Liberation Army.

*People's Defense* frequently publishes Korean dispatches describing the Chinese People's Volunteers. On the occasion of the 30th anniversary of the Chinese Communist Party, the newspaper published Commander-in-Chief Zhu De's article, "How the Chinese People Defeated Chiang Kai-shek's Reactionary Clique Armed by US Imperialism." From this month onward, it will also continuously publish Chairman Mao's "Strategic Problems in China's Revolutionary War." The *Our Army* pictorial frequently publishes a large number of photographs of the Chinese People's Volunteers' achievements in Korea, historical photographs of the growth of the Chinese People's Liberation Army, and photographs of Liberation Army generals, soldiers, and cultural troupes.

The monthly magazine *Military Education* and *Military Opinion*, intended for soldiers, and the weekly magazine *Party Life* often use the strategy and tactics of the Chinese People's Liberation Army as important teaching materials for the Czechoslovakian National Defense Forces. The Foreign Languages Press's publication *The Chinese People's Liberation Army* has been translated into Czech and will be distributed by the Czechoslovakian National Defense Forces Publishing Bureau in mid-September.

The Polish National Defense Forces' organ, *Freedom Fighter*, and other Polish newspapers have published articles commemorating the anniversary of the founding of the Chinese People's Liberation Army. Not long ago, *Freedom Fighter* published Commander-in-Chief Zhu De's article, "How the Chinese People Defeated Chiang Kai-shek's Reactionary Clique Armed by US Imperialism." The newspaper will soon publish Nie Rongzhen's "How the Chinese People Defeated the Japanese Fascist Aggressors" and Xiao Hua's "The Chinese Communist Party and the Chinese People's Liberation Army." These articles will be printed as pamphlets and distributed to Polish soldiers. The Polish National Defense Forces' pictorial weekly *Polish Soldier* frequently publishes photographs of the Chinese People's Liberation Army and the Chinese People's Volunteers. The publication recently translated and continuously published the *People's Daily*'s Korean dispatches: "Who Are the Most Beloved People?", "Days and Nights on the South Bank of the Han River," and "Glorious Blocking Action," and plans to print these dispatches into a special collection.

Xinhua News Agency, 3rd - According to the Telegraph Agency of Bulgaria, Sofia: Bulgarian newspapers warmly celebrated the 24th anniversary of the founding of the Chinese People's Liberation Army on the 1st. The Bulgarian Communist Party organ *Workers' Cause* stated: "The glorious history of the Chinese People's Liberation Army is the history of the Chinese people's armed struggle under the leadership of the Chinese Communist Party." The Bulgarian Fatherland Front organ *Fatherland Front* stated: "The Chinese People's Liberation Army is the main force of the Chinese revolution. It enables the Chinese people to resolutely advance along the path of democracy and socialism. Free China and its army are now preventing the American imperialists' plans to launch a new war, and are a guarantee that the people of Asian countries will achieve further victories in their struggle to completely shake off the shackles of imperialism."

Xinhua News Agency, Berlin, 3rd - In order to celebrate the anniversary of the founding of the Chinese People's Liberation Army, many newspapers in Berlin, the capital of the German Democratic Republic, published news and papers about the Chinese People's Liberation Army on August 1st.

*Neues Deutschland* published excerpts from Commander-in-Chief Zhu De's paper and, under the title "The People's Army, the Victorious Army," reported on the Chinese People's Liberation Army exhibition held in Berlin. The newspaper also published an article entitled "The People's Army in Battle, Learning, and Labor," which stated: "All progressive mankind sees in the Chinese People's Liberation Army an example of patriotism and dedication to freedom, democracy, peace, and friendship among all peoples." The article emphasized that this army is so beloved by the people "because it fights heroically, selflessly helps the people, and because it itself is part of the people."

*Daily Review* also published an article introducing "how this army of the common people has fought from 1927 until it achieved great victory." The newspaper pointed out: "Thanks to the efforts of the Chinese People's Liberation Army, China has become the most powerful country in Asia and stands in the camp of peace and democracy."

### 捷波等国报刊庆祝我建军节  歌颂中国人民解放军

1951-08-04
第4版()
专栏：

　　捷波等国报刊庆祝我建军节
    歌颂中国人民解放军
    【新华社布拉格三日电】捷克斯洛伐克和波兰报纸广泛介绍中国人民解放军。捷克斯洛伐克国防军机关报《人民防线报》和画报《我们的军队》都在中国人民解放军建军节出版了纪念专刊。人民防线报刊登了萧华将军的文章：《中国共产党与中国人民解放军》，和一篇分析解放军如何战胜了强大敌人的论文。《我们的军队》画刊登载了朱德总司令的巨幅照片，一篇题为《中国指出了东南亚反对帝国主义斗争的道路》的论文和其他介绍解放军建军史与战绩的文章。
    人民防线报经常登载描写中国人民志愿军的朝鲜通讯。在纪念中国共产党三十周年时，该报曾刊登了朱总司令的文章《中国人民怎样击败了美帝国主义武装的蒋介石反动派》，从本月起又将连续刊载毛主席的《中国革命战争的战略问题》。《我们的军队》画刊经常大量刊登关于中国人民志愿军在朝鲜的战绩的照片，中国人民解放军成长的历史性的照片，和解放军将领、战士、文工团活动的照片。
    专供士兵阅读的月刊《军事教育》和《军事意见》及周刊《党的生活》，经常以中国人民解放军的战略和战术作为捷国防军的重要教材。北京外文出版社的《中国人民解放军》已译成捷克文，将于九月中旬由捷国防军出版局发行。
    波兰国防军机关报《自由战士》报和其他波兰报纸，都登载了纪念中国人民解放军建军节的文章。不久以前，《自由战士》报登载了朱德总司令的文章《中国人民怎样击败了美帝国主义武装的蒋介石反动派》。该报即将发表聂荣臻的《中国人民怎样战胜了日本法西斯侵略者》和萧华的《中国共产党与中国人民解放军》。这些文章都将印成小册子，分发给波兰士兵。波兰国防军的《波兰战士》画报周刊经常登载关于中国人民解放军和中国人民志愿军的照片。该刊最近曾连续译载了人民日报的朝鲜通讯：《谁是最可爱的人》，《汉江南岸的日日夜夜》和《光辉的阻击战》等，并准备把这些通讯印成专集。
    【新华社三日讯】据电通社索非亚讯：保加利亚各报于一日热烈庆祝中国人民解放军建军第二十四周年。保加利亚共产党机关报《工人事业报》说：“中国人民解放军的光荣历史，就是中国人民在中国共产党领导下进行武装斗争的历史。”保加利亚祖国阵线机关报《祖国阵线报》说：“中国人民解放军是中国革命的主要力量，它使中国人民能够坚决地沿着民主和社会主义的道路前进。自由中国及其军队现在正阻止着美国帝国主义发动新战争的计划，并且是使亚洲各国人民在争取彻底摆脱帝国主义镣铐的斗争中得到进一步胜利的保证。”
    【新华社柏林三日电】为了庆祝中国人民解放军建军节，德意志民主共和国首都柏林许多报纸于八月一日都发表了关于中国人民解放军的消息和论文。
    《新德意志报》节要地刊载了朱德总司令的论文，并在《人民的军队，胜利的军队》这个标题下，报道了在柏林举行的中国人民解放军展览会的情况。该报还刊登了一篇题为《人民的军队在战斗、学习和劳动》的文章。其中说：“全体进步人类在中国人民解放军身上看到了爱国主义和献身于自由、民主、和平及各国人民间的友谊的范例。”该文着重指出这支军队之所以如此受到人民爱戴，是“因为它英雄地战斗，忘我地帮助人民，因为它本身就是人民的一部分”。
    《每日评论》也发表文章，介绍“这支老百姓的队伍怎样从一九二七年一直战斗到取得伟大的胜利”。该报指出：“由于中国人民解放军的努力，中国已成为亚洲最强大的国家而屹立在和平民主阵营中。”
