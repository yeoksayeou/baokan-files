Statistics on the Number of People Nationwide Signing in Support of Concluding a Peace Treaty and Voting Against the United States Arming Japan
　　Editor's note: The final results of the national signature campaign in support of concluding a peace treaty and the voting campaign against arming Japan are hereby announced by region.
