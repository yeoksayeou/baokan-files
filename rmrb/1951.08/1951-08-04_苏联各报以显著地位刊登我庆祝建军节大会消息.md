Title: Soviet Newspapers Prominently Publish News of My Army Day Celebration Meeting

Author:

Date: 1951-08-04
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, 3rd. Soviet newspapers Pravda, Izvestia, Trud, Red Star, Red Fleet and Moscow Pravda, all published prominently on the 2nd news of the Beijing commemoration meeting for the 24th anniversary of the founding of the Chinese People's Liberation Army and Commander-in-Chief Zhu De's speech at the meeting. Pravda also published a dispatch from its Beijing correspondent, pointing out that the Chinese people "showed great unity in the struggle for peace and national independence in commemorating the day of their armed forces."

### 苏联各报以显著地位刊登我庆祝建军节大会消息

1951-08-04
第4版()
专栏：

　　苏联各报以显著地位刊登我庆祝建军节大会消息
    【新华社莫斯科三日电】苏联真理报、消息报、劳动报、红星报、红海军报和莫斯科真理报，均于二日以显著地位刊登中国人民解放军建军二十四周年北京纪念大会的消息和朱德总司令在大会上的讲话。真理报并发表了该报驻北京记者的通讯，指出中国人民在“纪念自己武装力量的日子里，表现了为和平和民族独立而斗争的伟大的团结”。
