Title: Our Great Motherland (Picture)
Author:
Date: 1951-08-02
Page: 1

    Our Great Motherland
    Comrade Ji Bingqian, a representative of the Chinese People's Volunteers returning from Korea, and others went deep into the minority areas of Guizhou Province to report the heroic deeds of the Chinese People's Volunteers in the Korean War to the broad masses of ethnic minorities. Here, Miao women in Wuliqiao Village, Huangping County, eastern Guizhou, are dancing their favorite bench dance to welcome the representatives of the Volunteers.

### 我们伟大的祖国（图片）

1951-08-02
第1版()
专栏：

    我们伟大的祖国
    中国人民志愿军归国代表嵇炳前同志等深入到贵州省少数民族地区，把中国人民志愿军在朝鲜作战的英雄事迹报告给广大的少数民族的人民。在这里，贵州东部黄平县五里桥寨的苗族妇女，正跳着她们最喜爱的板凳舞，来欢迎志愿军的代表。
