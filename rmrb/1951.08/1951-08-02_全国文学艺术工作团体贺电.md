Title: Congratulatory Telegram from National Literary and Art Work Groups
Author:
Date: 1951-08-02
Page: 1

To Commander-in-Chief Zhu De and all commanders and fighters of the Chinese People's Liberation Army:

　　On the glorious occasion of the 24th anniversary of the founding of the Chinese People's Liberation Army, we, the literary and art workers of the whole country, extend our highest respect and warmest congratulations to you with infinite enthusiasm and gratitude!

　　Our Chinese People's Liberation Army, under the correct leadership of Chairman Mao and Commander-in-Chief Zhu, has overthrown the reactionary rule of feudalism and bureaucrat-capitalism through twenty-four years of arduous struggle, driven out the aggressive forces of imperialism, and completely eliminated the counter-revolutionary armed forces of Chiang Kai-shek, the chief culprit of the civil war. Only then has the Chinese revolution achieved a brilliant victory. You are the most outstanding sons and daughters of the Chinese people, you are the meritorious servants who created the new society, and you are the cornerstones for defending world peace. Now, American imperialism is still obstructing and undermining our construction and continuing to invade Korea and our territory of Taiwan, attempting to make a separate peace with Japan and rearm Japan. American imperialism is the mortal enemy of the Chinese people. As long as its aggressive policy is not eliminated, our struggle against resistance cannot be relaxed for a single day. We, the literary and art workers of the whole country, pledge to do our best to fulfill the three major calls of the General Association to Resist U.S. Aggression and Aid Korea, strive for the early arrival of the final victory in resisting U.S. Aggression and Aid Korea, and build our independent, happy, and prosperous New China!

　　Long live the Chinese People's Liberation Army!
　　Long live the Chinese Communist Party!
　　Long live Chairman Mao!
　　Long live Commander-in-Chief Zhu!
　　All-China Federation of Literary and Art Circles, All-China Association of Literary Workers, All-China Association of Fine Arts Workers, All-China Association of Drama Workers, All-China Association of Music Workers, All-China Association of Dance Workers, All-China Association of Film Workers, All-China Association of Ballad Singers, All-China Association of Traditional Opera Workers
　　August 1st

### 全国文学艺术工作团体贺电

1951-08-02
第1版()
专栏：

　　全国文学艺术工作团体贺电
    中国人民解放军朱总司令暨全体指战员同志们：
    欣逢中国人民解放军建军节二十四周年的光辉纪念日，我们全国文学艺术工作者以无限的热情和感激的心情，向你们致以最崇高的敬意和最热烈的祝贺！
    我们中国人民解放军，是在毛主席、朱总司令的正确领导之下，经过了二十四年的艰苦奋斗，推翻了封建主义与官僚资本主义的反动统治，驱逐了帝国主义的侵略势力，彻底消灭了内战祸首蒋介石的反革命武装力量，中国的革命才取得了辉煌的胜利。你们是中国人民最优秀最杰出的儿女，你们是创造新社会的功臣，你们是保卫世界和平的柱石。现在美帝国主义仍在阻挠和破坏我们的建设，并继续侵略朝鲜和我国领土台湾，妄想单独对日媾和，重新武装日本。美帝国主义是中国人民的死敌，它的侵略政策一天不消灭，我们反抗斗争就一天不能松懈。我们全国文学艺术工作者誓以一切力量来做好抗美援朝总会的三大号召，争取抗美援朝的最后胜利早日到来，建设我们独立、幸福、繁荣的新中国！
    中国人民解放军万岁！
    中国共产党万岁！
    毛主席万岁！
    朱总司令万岁！

  中华全国舞蹈工作者协会  中华全国电影工作者协会  中华全国曲艺工作者协会  中华全国戏曲工作者协会
