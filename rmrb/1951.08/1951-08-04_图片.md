Title: Pictures
Author:
Date: 1951-08-04
Page: 4

    I. Women in Tehran, the capital of Iran, hold a demonstration parade, their flags reading: "We don't want the same fate as Korea!"
    II. Vast numbers of workers and students in Tehran, despite the Iranian government's suppression and persecution, hold grand parades and demonstrations, opposing the Anglo-Iranian Oil Company plunderers and demanding the nationalization of the oil industry.

### 图片

1951-08-04
第4版()
专栏：

    一、伊朗首都德黑兰妇女示威游行，她们的旗子上写着：“我们不要和朝鲜一样的命运！”
    二、德黑兰广大的工人与学生，不顾伊朗政府的镇压与迫害，举行盛大游行示威，反对英伊石油公司掠夺者，要求石油工业国有化。
