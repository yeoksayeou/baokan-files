Title: Typhoon Reaches 400 Li Northeast of Shanghai; Coastal Areas North of the Yangtze River Estuary Experience Wind and Rain
Author:
Date: 1951-08-23
Page: 1

　　Typhoon Reaches 400 Li Northeast of Shanghai
    Coastal Areas North of the Yangtze River Estuary Experience Wind and Rain
    [Xinhua News Agency, August 22nd] The Central Meteorological Observatory issued a typhoon warning at 8:00 PM on August 22nd:
    At 2:00 PM on the 22nd, the center of the typhoon was at sea 420 li northeast of Shanghai (33 degrees North latitude, 123 degrees East longitude), moving northward at a speed of 15 li per hour. In the next 24 hours, there will be wind and rain from the Yangtze River Estuary north to the Liaodong Peninsula and Korea. The wind force will be greater in the Shandong Peninsula, with wind speeds up to 75 li per hour (force 6 winds).

### 台风进抵上海东北四百余里处  长江口以北沿海有风雨

1951-08-23
第1版()
专栏：

　　台风进抵上海东北四百余里处
    长江口以北沿海有风雨
    【新华社二十二日讯】中央气象台八月二十二日下午八时发出台风警报：
    二十二日下午二时，台风中心在上海东北四百二十华里的海上（北纬三十三度，东经一百二十三度）向北方进行，每小时移动十五华里。未来二十四小时中，自长江口以北至辽东半岛以及朝鲜仍将有风雨，山东半岛一带风力较大，风速每小时可到七十五华里（六级风）。
