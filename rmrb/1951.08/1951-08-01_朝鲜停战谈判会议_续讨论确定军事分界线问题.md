Title: Korean Armistice Talks Continue to Discuss the Demarcation Line Issue
Author:
Date: 1951-08-01
Page: 2
Xinhua News Agency, Pyongyang, July 31st: This reporter learned from the Korean front headquarters that the fifteenth meeting of the Korean armistice talks was held at 10:00 AM (Korean time) on July 31st, continuing to discuss the issue of determining a military demarcation line to establish a demilitarized zone. At 11:32 AM, the United Nations Command delegation suggested adjournment. The sixteenth meeting is scheduled for 10:00 AM on August 1st.

### 朝鲜停战谈判会议  续讨论确定军事分界线问题

1951-08-01
第2版()
专栏：

　　朝鲜停战谈判会议
    续讨论确定军事分界线问题
    【新华社平壤三十一日电】本社记者自朝鲜前线总部获悉：朝鲜停战谈判会议第十五次会议，在七月三十一日上午十时（朝鲜时间）举行，继续讨论确定双方军事分界线以建立非军事地区问题，十一时三十二分联合国军代表团建议休会。第十六次会议定于八月一日上午十时举行。
