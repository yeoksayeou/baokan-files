    Our Great Motherland
    After the Luo Yongjin team of the state-owned Fengfeng Coal Mine created a new record of 254.4 tons of coal mined in eight hours, the mine's workers asked Luo Yongjin to introduce his experience in creating the new record. Here, Luo Yongjin (first person on the right) is introducing his production experience to his fellow workers.

### 我们伟大的祖国（图片）

1951-08-28
第1版()
专栏：

    我们伟大的祖国
    国营峰峰煤矿的罗永金组，在创造了八小时内采煤二百五十四点四吨的最新纪录后，该矿工人纷纷要求罗永金介绍创造新纪录的经验。在这里，罗永金（右第一人）正在向工友们介绍生产经验。
