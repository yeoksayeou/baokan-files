    Our Great Motherland
    Using photo exhibitions to carry out Resist America, Aid Korea propaganda is an experience worth promoting. This is a photo exhibition held by the News Photography Bureau of the News Administration at the Beijing Railway Station. Travelers from all over the country can gain new knowledge and receive patriotic education here.

### 我们伟大的祖国（图片）

1951-08-09
第1版()
专栏：

    我们伟大的祖国    
    利用照片展览来进行抗美援朝宣传，是一个值得推广的经验。这是新闻总署新闻摄影局在北京车站举办的照片展览，每天来往各地的旅客，都能在这里得到新的知识，受到爱国主义的教育。
