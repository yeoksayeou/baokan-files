Title: Workers Deliver Water Gates (Sketches from Harnessing the Huai River, Part 3)
Author: Deng Shu, Gu Qun
Date: 1951-08-06
Page: 3

    Workers Deliver Water Gates (Sketches from Harnessing the Huai River, Part 3)
    By Deng Shu and Gu Qun
    In order to prevent the peasant brothers on both banks of the Huai River from being threatened by floods again, the workers in factories in various places enthusiastically delivered the rush-produced machine gates to various construction sites, supplying the needs of the Huai River harnessing project.

### 工人送水闸　（治淮速写之三）（图片）
邓澍  顾群
1951-08-06
第3版()
专栏：

    工人送水闸　（治淮速写之三）
    邓澍  顾群  作
    为了使淮河两岸的农民兄弟不再受水灾的威胁，各地工厂的工人们积极热情地把赶制出来的机器闸门，送往各个施工地点，供应了治淮工程上的需要。
