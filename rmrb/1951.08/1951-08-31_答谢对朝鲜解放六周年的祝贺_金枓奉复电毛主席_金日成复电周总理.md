Title: Replying to Congratulations on the Sixth Anniversary of the Liberation of Korea: Kim Tu-bong Sends a Reply Telegram to Chairman Mao, Kim Il-sung Sends a Reply Telegram to Premier Zhou

Author: 
Date: 1951-08-31
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Pyongyang, August 30th: Kim Tu-bong, Chairman of the Standing Committee of the Supreme People's Assembly of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea, and Premier Kim Il-sung of the Cabinet, sent telegrams to Chairman Mao Zedong of the Central People's Government of China and Premier Zhou Enlai of the State Council on the 22nd, respectively, to thank Chairman Mao and Premier Zhou for the congratulatory telegrams they sent on the eve of the sixth anniversary of the "August 15th" Liberation of Korea. The texts of the reply telegrams are as follows:

(1) Chairman Kim Tu-bong's reply telegram to His Excellency Mao Zedong, Chairman of the Central People's Government of the People's Republic of China:

　　I extend my heartfelt thanks for the warm congratulations Your Excellency gave on the sixth anniversary of the great Soviet armed forces liberating Korea from the shackles of Japanese imperialism. I wish Your Excellency even greater achievements in the cause of the prosperity and development of the People's Republic of China.

　　Chairman of the Standing Committee of the Supreme People's Assembly of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea
　　Kim Tu-bong
　　August 22, 1951

(2) Premier Kim Il-sung's reply telegram to His Excellency Zhou Enlai, Premier of the State Council of the Central People's Government of the People's Republic of China:

　　I extend my utmost gratitude for the warm congratulations Your Excellency gave on the sixth anniversary of the heroic Soviet army liberating Korea. I wish Your Excellency greater achievements in future endeavors.

　　Premier of the Cabinet of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea
　　Kim Il-sung
　　August 22, 1951

### 答谢对朝鲜解放六周年的祝贺  金枓奉复电毛主席  金日成复电周总理

1951-08-31
第1版()
专栏：

　　答谢对朝鲜解放六周年的祝贺
    金枓奉复电毛主席
    金日成复电周总理
    【新华社平壤三十日电】朝鲜民主主义人民共和国最高人民会议常任委员会金枓奉委员长与内阁首相金日成，于二十二日分别致电我中央人民政府毛泽东主席与政务院周恩来总理，答谢毛主席和周总理在“八、一五”朝鲜解放六周年纪念日前夕拍给他们的贺电。复电电文如下：
  （一）金枓奉委员长复电中华人民共和国中央人民政府主席毛泽东阁下：
    对于阁下在伟大的苏联武力使朝鲜从日本帝国主义羁绊中获得解放的六周年纪念日所给予的热烈祝贺，谨致衷心的感谢。祝阁下在为中华人民共和国的富强发展的事业中有更巨大的成就。
          朝鲜民主主义人民共和国最高人民会议常任委员会委员长
            金枓奉
                一九五一年八月二十二日
  （二）金日成首相复电中华人民共和国中央人民政府政务院总理周恩来阁下：
    对于阁下在英雄的苏联军队使朝鲜获得解放的六周年纪念日所给予的热烈祝贺，谨致无上的感谢。祝阁下在今后的事业中有更大的成就。
        朝鲜民主主义人民共和国内阁首相金日成
              一九五一年八月二十二日
