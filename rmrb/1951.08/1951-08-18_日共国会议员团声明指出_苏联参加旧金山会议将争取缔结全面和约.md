Title: Japanese Communist Party Diet Members' Statement Points Out that the Soviet Union's Participation in the San Francisco Conference Will Strive to Conclude a Comprehensive Peace Treaty
Author:
Date: 1951-08-18
Page: 2
　　
　　Xinhua News Agency, 17th Dispatch: Tokyo News: The Japanese Communist Party Diet Members' group issued a statement on the 15th regarding the Soviet government's participation in the San Francisco Conference, stating: The Soviet Union's purpose in participating in the conference is to bring the peace treaty issue back on the right track, prevent the revival of Japanese militarism, and safeguard peace. The Soviet Union's participation in the conference will not only not hinder a comprehensive peace treaty, but will also strive for a peace treaty with the participation of all countries.

### 日共国会议员团声明指出  苏联参加旧金山会议将争取缔结全面和约

1951-08-18
第2版()
专栏：

　　日共国会议员团声明指出
    苏联参加旧金山会议将争取缔结全面和约
    【新华社十七日讯】东京消息：日本共产党国会议员团于十五日就苏联政府参加旧金山会议事，发表声明称：苏联参加会议的目的是希望把和约问题拉回正路，防止日本军国主义复活和保卫和平。苏联参加会议不但不会阻碍全面和约，而且将争取一个所有国家参加的和约。
