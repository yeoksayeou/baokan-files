Title: Southern Old Revolutionary Base Visiting Delegation Departs from Hankou to Visit Various Locations
Author:
Date: 1951-08-04
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Hankou, 3rd. The Southern Old Revolutionary Base Visiting Delegation of the Central People's Government, consisting of over ninety people and led by Head of Delegation Xie Juezai and Deputy Heads of Delegation Zhu Xuefan and Fu Qiutao, arrived in Hankou on July 29th. The one hundred and ninety-nine representatives from all circles of the people of East China participating in the visiting delegation, led by Deputy Head of Delegation Li Buxin, also arrived in Hankou on the afternoon of the same day. After several days of preparation, the Hunan-Hubei-Jiangxi sub-delegation led by Deputy Head Fu Qiutao, the Hunan-Jiangxi border sub-delegation led by Deputy Head Zhu Xuefan, and the personnel participating in the Guangdong sub-delegation led by Deputy Director of the Overseas Chinese Affairs Commission of the Central People's Government, Li Tiemin, departed on the 2nd. Other sub-delegations and the main delegation will also set off in the next few days to visit various old revolutionary base areas.

### 南方老根据地访问团  由汉口分赴各地访问

1951-08-04
第1版()
专栏：

　　南方老根据地访问团
    由汉口分赴各地访问
    【新华社汉口三日电】中央人民政府南方老根据地访问团一行九十余人，在团长谢觉哉、副团长朱学范、傅秋涛率领下，已于七月二十九日到达汉口。参加访问团的华东各界人民代表一九九人，在副团长李步新率领下，也于当天下午到达汉口。经过连日的准备，傅秋涛副团长率领湘鄂赣分团，朱学范副团长率领湘赣边分团和中央人民政府华侨事务委员会李铁民副主任率领参加广东分团的人员，已于二日分别出发。其他分团和总团也将于日内启程分赴各老革命根据地进行访问。
