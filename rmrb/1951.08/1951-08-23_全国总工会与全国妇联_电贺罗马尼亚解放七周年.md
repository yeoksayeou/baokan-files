Title: All-China Federation of Trade Unions and All-China Women's Federation Send Congratulatory Messages on the Seventh Anniversary of the Liberation of Romania
Author:
Date: 1951-08-23
Page: 4

　　All-China Federation of Trade Unions and All-China Women's Federation
    Send Congratulatory Messages on the Seventh Anniversary of the Liberation of Romania
    [Xinhua News Agency, 22nd] August 23rd is the seventh anniversary of the liberation of Romania. The All-China Federation of Trade Unions sent a telegram to the Romanian General Confederation of Labor to congratulate them. The telegram reads as follows: To the Romanian General Confederation of Labor:
    On the occasion of the seventh anniversary of the liberation of Romania, I extend warm congratulations to the Romanian working people on behalf of all the Chinese working people, and wish you greater achievements in the great cause of implementing the Five-Year Economic Plan and defending world peace.
            All-China Federation of Trade Unions
    [Report from this newspaper] According to Xinhua News Agency: August 23rd is the seventh anniversary of the liberation of Romania. Cai Chang, Chairman of the All-China Democratic Women's Federation, sent a telegram to Mrs. Constanța Crăciun, Chairman of the Romanian Democratic Women's Union, to offer congratulations. The telegram reads as follows:
    On the occasion of the seventh anniversary of the liberation of Romania and the overthrow of fascist tyranny, I extend warm congratulations to you and all the sisters in Romania, and wish you greater success in the struggle to build a new Romania and defend world peace.

### 全国总工会与全国妇联  电贺罗马尼亚解放七周年

1951-08-23
第4版()
专栏：

　　全国总工会与全国妇联
    电贺罗马尼亚解放七周年
    【新华社二十二日讯】八月二十三日是罗马尼亚解放七周年纪念日，中华全国总工会特电罗马尼亚总工会祝贺，电文如下：罗马尼亚总工会：
    值此罗马尼亚解放七周年纪念日，谨代表全中国劳动人民向罗马尼亚劳动人民致以热烈的祝贺，并祝你们在实行五年经济计划与保卫世界和平的伟大事业中获得更大的成就。
            中华全国总工会
    【本报讯】据新华社讯：八月二十三日为罗马尼亚解放七周年纪念日，中华全国民主妇女联合会主席蔡畅特电罗马尼亚民主妇女联合会主席康斯汤达·克拉西恩夫人致贺。电文如下：
    兹值罗马尼亚解放及推翻法西斯专制第七周年纪念日，我谨向你及罗马尼亚的全体姊妹们致热烈的祝贺，祝你们在建设新罗马尼亚与保卫世界和平的斗争中获得更大的成功。
