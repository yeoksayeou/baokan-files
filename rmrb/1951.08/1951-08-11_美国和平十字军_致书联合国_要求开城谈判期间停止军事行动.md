Title: "American Peace Crusaders" Send Letter to the United Nations Demanding Cessation of Military Action During Kaesong Negotiations
Author:
Date: 1951-08-11
Page: 4

　　Xinhua News Agency, Tenth Dispatch from New York: On August 7th, a delegation from the "American Peace Crusaders" visited the United Nations headquarters in New York and presented a petition to Mr. Cohen, an assistant to Mr. Lie. The delegation was composed of ten prominent progressive figures, including Paul Robeson, Professor Morrison, Reverend Upham, and Mr. Glasgow, Chairman of the American Youth for Peace Committee. The petition requested a cessation of hostilities in Korea during the Kaesong armistice negotiations. The petition pointed out that the American people long to end the Korean War, and the American soldiers fighting there welcome suggestions to end the killing in Korea.

　　The petition pointed out: A ceasefire and armistice in Korea is the first step towards establishing peace in the Far East. It can open the way for major powers to hold the broadest possible negotiations to reach agreements on major issues on which they disagree. In order to establish peace in Korea and the Far East, the United Nations should reassume its role as an objective mediator and no longer stand on the side of any one country or any one group of countries to become a belligerent.

### “美国和平十字军”致书联合国  要求开城谈判期间停止军事行动

1951-08-11
第4版()
专栏：

　　“美国和平十字军”致书联合国
    要求开城谈判期间停止军事行动
    【新华社十日讯】塔斯社纽约九日讯：八月七日，“美国和平十字军”代表团访问纽约联合国总部，向赖伊的助手柯亨提出请愿书。代表团由十位著名的进步人士组成，其中有保罗·罗伯逊，摩里逊教授，乌法斯牧师，美国青年和平委员会主席格拉斯哥等。请愿书要求在开城停战谈判期间在朝鲜停止敌对行动。请愿书指出：美国人民渴望停止朝鲜战争，在那里作战的美国士兵欢迎结束在朝鲜的杀戮的建议。
    请愿书指出：在朝鲜停火和停战，是建立远东和平的第一步。它可以打开道路，让各大国举行最广泛的谈判，在他们意见分歧的主要问题上求得协议，为了建立朝鲜及远东的和平，联合国应该重新负起客观的调停者的职责，不要再站在任何一国或任何一个国家集团的一方成为交战方面。
