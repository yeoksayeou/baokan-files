Title: World Youth Festival Preparatory Committee Announces Festival Program
Author:
Date: 1951-08-04
Page: 4

　　Xinhua News Agency, Berlin, August 2nd: The Preparatory Committee for the Third World Youth and Student Peace Festival held a meeting on the 1st and selected the committee responsible for presiding over the upcoming festival. World Federation of Democratic Youth President Billinguer reported on the preparations for the festival. He announced that youth representatives from all European countries, all Asian countries, thirty-eight colonial countries, and seventeen Latin American countries would participate in the festival. He emphasized that the work of preparing for the festival in various countries has greatly promoted the peace movement.

　　Next, International Union of Students President Grossman reported on the festival's program: The festival will open on the afternoon of the 5th at the Ulrich Stadium. The 11th World University Summer Games will be held starting on the 6th. The International Arts Program Competition will be held starting on the 7th. The 9th will be Young Women's Day. The 12th will be German Youth Peace Fighter Day. The 17th will be World Students' Day. A grand gathering will be held on the evening of the 19th, with all foreign delegations and 500,000 German youth participating. The festival will then close. During the various programs, many concerts and recreational competitions will be held.

### 世界青年联欢节筹委会宣布联欢节节目

1951-08-04
第4版()
专栏：

　　世界青年联欢节筹委会宣布联欢节节目
    【新华社三日讯】据塔斯社柏林二日讯：第三届世界青年与学生和平联欢节筹备委员会于一日举行会议，选出了负责主持即将开幕的联欢节的委员会。世界民主青年联盟主席比林格尔报告了联欢节的筹备工作。他宣布所有的欧洲国家，所有的亚洲国家，三十八个殖民地国家和十七个拉丁美洲国家都有青年代表参加联欢节。他着重指出，各国筹备联欢节的工作大大地推动了和平运动。
    接着，国际学联主席格罗曼报告联欢节的节目：联欢节将于五日下午在乌布利希运动场开幕。六日起举行第十一届世界大学生夏季运动会。七日起举行国际文艺节目竞赛。九日是女青年日。十二日是德国青年和平战士日。十七日是世界学生日。十九日晚将举行盛大集会，所有外国代表团及五十万德国青年将参加大会。联欢节至此闭幕。在各项节目进行时，将举行许多次音乐会和游艺比赛。
