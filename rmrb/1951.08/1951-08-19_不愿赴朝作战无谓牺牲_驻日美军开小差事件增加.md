Title: Increased Desertion Among US Troops Stationed in Japan Due to Unwillingness to Fight and Sacrifice Needlessly in Korea

Author:

Date: 1951-08-19
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, August 18th, Tokyo dispatch: TASS reported on the 17th that desertions from the US Seventh Army stationed in Japan by soldiers unwilling to fight on the Korean front lines have greatly increased recently. Therefore, the head of the US military police in Osaka recently formally requested the Japanese police authorities of the city to cooperate with the US occupation authorities in jointly apprehending deserters. An Osaka police officer said: Desertions from the US Seventh Army stationed in the city occur every day. Sometimes, soldiers deserting from Korea also appear in the city.

### 不愿赴朝作战无谓牺牲  驻日美军开小差事件增加

1951-08-19
第1版()
专栏：

　　不愿赴朝作战无谓牺牲
    驻日美军开小差事件增加
    【新华社十八日讯】塔斯社十七日电，东京消息：驻日美军第七兵团不愿到朝鲜前线打仗而开小差的事件，近来大大增加。因此，驻大阪的美国军事警察首脑最近正式要求该市的日本警察当局与美国占领当局合作，共同缉捕逃兵。大阪某警官说：驻该市的美军第七兵团每天都有开小差的。有时还有从朝鲜开小差的兵士在该市出现。
