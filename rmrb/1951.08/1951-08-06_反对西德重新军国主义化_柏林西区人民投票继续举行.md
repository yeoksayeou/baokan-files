Title: West Berlin Residents Continue to Vote Against West German Remilitarization
Author:
Date: 1951-08-06
Page: 4

　　Against West German Remilitarization
    West Berlin Residents Continue to Vote
    【Xinhua News Agency, 2nd Dispatch】According to the German News Agency in Berlin: The results of the referendum against West German remilitarization held among residents of the Tiefensee, Spandau, and Charlottenburg districts of West Berlin from July 20th to 30th showed that 88 percent of the residents who participated in the vote opposed remilitarization and advocated the conclusion of a peace treaty with Germany in 1951.

### 反对西德重新军国主义化  柏林西区人民投票继续举行

1951-08-06
第4版()
专栏：

　　反对西德重新军国主义化
    柏林西区人民投票继续举行
    【新华社二日讯】据德意志通讯社柏林消息：七月二十日至三十日在西柏林的蒂潘、史盘刀与沙洛敦堡等区的居民中间举行反对西德重新军国主义化人民投票的结果，参加投票的居民中有百分之八十八表示反对重新军国主义化，主张在一九五一年内缔结对德和约。
