Title: Head of Our Mission to Germany Holds Reception Celebrating Army Day and Opens PLA Photo Exhibition
Author:
Date: 1951-08-03
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Berlin, August 2nd—General Ji Pengfei, Ambassador-level Head of the Diplomatic Mission of the People's Republic of China to the German Democratic Republic, held a reception at the Youth Palace here on July 31 to celebrate the anniversary of the founding of the Chinese People's Liberation Army. Invited to the reception were Johannes Dieckmann, President of the People's Chamber of the German Democratic Republic; Prime Minister Otto Grotewohl; Deputy Prime Minister Nuschke; Foreign Minister Dellinger; other heads of government of the German Democratic Republic; and social figures, totaling 200 people.

Also invited were Pushkin, Head of the Soviet Diplomatic Mission; personnel from the diplomatic missions of various People's Democracies; and General Chuikov, Chairman of the Soviet Control Commission.

At the reception, Head of Mission Ji Pengfei delivered a speech explaining the history of the founding of the Chinese People's Liberation Army and the great tasks it now undertakes in defending the national defense of the motherland and world peace. Prime Minister Grotewohl and General Chuikov raised their glasses at the meeting to wish good health to Chairman Mao and Commander-in-Chief Zhu, the founders of the powerful Chinese People's Army. The participants also raised their glasses to wish good health to President Pieck and Marshal Stalin.

The reception proceeded in a friendly atmosphere, and the documentary film "Celebrating the Whole Country," commemorating the first anniversary of the founding of the People's Republic of China, as well as newsreels from the Korean front, were shown.

Starting July 31, the Chinese Diplomatic Mission is holding a photo exhibition of the Chinese People's Liberation Army at the Soviet Cultural Center to introduce the achievements of the Chinese People's Liberation Army.

### 我驻德使团团长举行酒会  庆祝解放军建军节  并举办我解放军图片展览会

1951-08-03
第4版()
专栏：

　　我驻德使团团长举行酒会
    庆祝解放军建军节
    并举办我解放军图片展览会
    【新华社柏林二日电】中华人民共和国驻德意志民主共和国外交使团大使衔团长姬鹏飞将军，于七月三十一日在此间青年宫举行招待会，庆祝中国人民解放军建军节。应邀到会的有德意志民主共和国人民议会议长狄克曼、总理格罗提渥、副总理努舍克、外交部长窦丁格尔暨其他德意志民主共和国政府首长、以及社会名流等共二百人。
    苏联外交使团团长普式金、各人民民主国家外交使团人员、苏联管制委员会主席崔可夫将军也应邀到会。
    姬鹏飞团长在招待会上致词，说明中国人民解放军的建军历史和现在所负的保卫祖国国防及世界和平的伟大任务。格罗提渥总理和崔可夫将军在会上举杯祝强大中国人民的军队的创建者毛主席和朱总司令健康。与会者并举杯祝皮克总统和斯大林大元帅健康。
    招待会一直在友好气氛中进行，会上并放映了中国开国一周年纪念的记录影片《普天同庆》以及朝鲜前线的新闻片。
    七月三十一日起，中国外交使团假苏联文化馆举办中国人民解放军图片展览会，介绍中国人民解放军的事迹。
