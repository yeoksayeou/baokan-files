Title: British "Worker's Daily" Reporter Reports from Kaesong: The U.S. Fabricates News to Deceive the People of the World and Denies Responsibility for Destroying the Neutral Zone Agreement
Author: Alan Winnington
Date: 1951-08-24
Page: 1

　　British "Worker's Daily" Reporter Reports from Kaesong:
    The U.S. Fabricates News to Deceive the People of the World
    Denies Responsibility for Destroying the Neutral Zone Agreement
    【Xinhua News Agency, 23rd】 Alan Winnington, a reporter for the British "Worker's Daily" in London, reported from Kaesong on the 22nd: The U.S. denial of responsibility for Sunday's shooting incident has caused great dissatisfaction here. No one here believes, not even the journalists in the American military camps, Ridgeway's despicable attempts to deny responsibility. But censorship forces them to send out fabricated news, or not to send out any news at all. This situation highlights the special circumstances that have arisen since the U.S. proposed the establishment of a subcommittee. One hundred percent of the news media representatives are Americans. Reuters now only sends an American citizen, Kaplan, to represent this sole British news agency. An iron curtain absolutely seals off all information. In this way, the Americans can arbitrarily create any crazy propaganda, fabricating that the troops attacking the neutral zone are "guerrillas from some unknown side."

### 英工人日报记者自开城报道  美方捏造消息欺骗世界人民  抵赖破坏中立区协议的责任
阿兰·魏宁顿
1951-08-24
第1版()
专栏：

　　英工人日报记者自开城报道
    美方捏造消息欺骗世界人民
    抵赖破坏中立区协议的责任
    【新华社二十三日讯】英国伦敦工人日报记者阿兰·魏宁顿二十二日自开城报道称：美方否认星期日射击事件的责任，此间对于美国这种态度甚为不满。此间没有人相信，甚至美国军营里的新闻记者都不相信李奇微抵赖责任的种种可鄙的企图。可是新闻检查使他们不得不把虚构的消息发出去，或者什么新闻稿都不发出去。这种情形着重说明了从美国提议成立小组委员会以来所发生的特殊情况。新闻界代表百分之百是美国人。路透社现在只派了一个美国公民卡普兰代表这个唯一的英国新闻社。铁幕把一切消息绝对地封锁起来。这样一来，美国人就可以随意制造出任何疯狂的宣传，捏造说：袭击中立区的军队是“不知那一方面的游击队”。
