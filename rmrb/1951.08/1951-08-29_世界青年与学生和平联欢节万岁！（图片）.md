Title: Long Live the World Youth and Students for Peace Festival! (Image)
Author:
Date: 1951-08-29
Page: 5

来信摘要 (Lai Xin Zhai Yao) means "Letters to the Editor"
Summary of Letters:

    Long Live the World Youth and Students for Peace Festival!
    Delegation of the Mongolian People's Republic participating in the festival.
    Delegation of Romania participating in the festival.
    Delegation of Albania participating in the festival.
    Delegation of Canada participating in the festival.
    Delegation of India participating in the festival.
    Delegation of Bulgaria participating in the festival.
    Delegation of Czechoslovakia participating in the festival.
    This is the delegation of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea in the stands.
    Column of German youth athletes participating in the German Youth Peace Fighters Day demonstration at the festival.
    Delegation of Poland participating in the festival.
    Delegation of France participating in the festival.
    Chinese student athletes led by the Five-Star Red Flag.
    As the Chinese youth delegation team, following the Five-Star Red Flag, appeared at the venue, the stands erupted with cheers of "Long Live Mao Zedong!"
    This is the Soviet youth delegation participating in the World Youth and Students for Peace Festival. When they appeared at the venue, tens of thousands of people cheered "Long Live Stalin!"
    The Third World Youth and Students for Peace Festival opened on August 5th at the Ulbricht Square in Berlin. This is the scene on August 12th when Wilhelm Pieck (right), President of the German Democratic Republic, and Enrico Berlinguer (left), Chairman of the World Federation of Democratic Youth, reviewed the two million German youth holding the German Youth Peace Fighters Day demonstration parade on the reviewing stand.
    After the conclusion of the Third World Youth and Students for Peace Festival on August 5th, German athletes, in accordance with the solemn rhythm of the "Song of Stalin," formed the word "Peace" in English, Russian, and German on the green grass. At the same time, in front of a wide sandy slope near the stadium, a colorful portrait of Stalin was set off.

### 世界青年与学生和平联欢节万岁！（图片）

1951-08-29
第5版()
专栏：来信摘要

    世界青年与学生和平联欢节万岁！
    参加联欢节的蒙古人民共和国代表团。
    参加联欢节的罗马尼亚代表团。
    参加联欢节的阿尔巴尼亚代表团。
    参加联欢节的加拿大代表团。
    参加联欢节的印度代表团。
    参加联欢节的保加利亚代表团。
    参加联欢节的捷克斯洛伐克代表团。
    这是在看台上的朝鲜民主主义人民共和国代表团。
    参加联欢节德国青年和平战士日示威的德国青年运动员纵队。
    参加联欢节的波兰代表团。
    参加联欢节的法国代表团。
    以五星红旗为前导的中国学生运动员。
    这是随着五星红旗后面的中国青年代表团队伍，在会场出现时，看台上传来了一片“毛泽东万岁！”的欢呼声。
    这是参加世界青年与学生和平联欢节的苏联青年代表团。在会场出现时，成千上万的人欢呼着“斯大林万岁！”。
    第三届世界青年与学生和平联欢节于八月五日在柏林的乌布利希广场上开幕。这是八月十二日德意志民主共和国总统皮克（右）和世界民主青年联盟主席比林格尔（左）在检阅台上检阅两百万德国青年举行德国青年和平战士日示威游行的情形。
    德国运动员于八月五日第三届世界青年与学生和平联欢节大会结束后，按照着庄严的“斯大林颂”歌声的节拍，绿色的草地上出现了英、俄、德三种文字的“和平”字样。同时，在运动场近旁一片宽阔的沙坡前面，衬托着一幅彩色的斯大林的画像。
