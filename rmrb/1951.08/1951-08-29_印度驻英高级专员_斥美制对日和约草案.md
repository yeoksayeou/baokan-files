Title: Indian High Commissioner to the UK Condemns US-Drafted Peace Treaty with Japan
Author:
Date: 1951-08-29
Page: 1

　　Xinhua News Agency, August 28th Dispatch from London via Electrone Telegraph Agency: The Indian High Commissioner to the UK, Menon, explained at a press conference on the 27th the reasons for India's refusal to attend the San Francisco Peace Conference with Japan. He stated that India strongly opposes Japan "becoming an armed camp of another country." Menon condemned the US-drafted peace treaty with Japan and flatly said: "Taiwan is a part of Chinese territory."

　　Menon opposed Japan rearming under American control. Referring to the matter of the United States building bases in Japan, Menon said that India opposes the United States taking measures to "acquire bases in Japan at a time when Japan cannot oppose it." He said that the Indian government opposes any trusteeship over any part of Japanese territory and opposes Japan providing any bases "under coercion."

### 印度驻英高级专员  斥美制对日和约草案

1951-08-29
第1版()
专栏：

　　印度驻英高级专员
    斥美制对日和约草案
    【新华社二十八日讯】电通社伦敦讯：印度驻英高级专员梅农二十七日在记者招待会上解释印度拒绝参加旧金山对日和约会议的事情时说：印度强烈反对日本“成为另一国的武装军营”。梅农斥责美制对日和约草案，并断然说：“台湾是中国领土的一部分。”
    梅农反对日本在美国控制之下进行重新武装。梅农谈到美国在日本建筑基地的事情时说：印度反对美国“在日本不能反对的时候取得基地”的措施。他说：印度政府反对对日本任何一部分领土施行托管制，反对日本“在受强迫的情况下”提供任何基地。
