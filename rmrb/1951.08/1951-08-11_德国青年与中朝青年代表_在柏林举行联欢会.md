Title: German Youth and Chinese and Korean Youth Representatives Hold a Joint Celebration in Berlin
Author:
Date: 1951-08-11
Page: 4

　　German Youth and Chinese and Korean Youth Representatives
    Hold a Joint Celebration in Berlin
    [Report from this newspaper] According to Xinhua News Agency: German youth and Chinese and Korean youth representatives held a joint celebration at the headquarters of the Free German Youth League on the 8th. This was the first joint celebration between German youth and foreign youth since the opening of the World Youth and Students for Peace Festival.
    After speeches by Honecker, Feng Wenbin, and Jin Suozhen, the youth representatives of the three countries exchanged gifts at the meeting. German youth presented machine models and telescopes to the Chinese and Korean youth delegations. The Korean youth delegation presented flags to the German and Chinese youth representatives, and the Chinese youth representatives presented a flag of the Chinese New Democratic Youth League to the German youth. When accepting the flag of the Chinese Youth League, Honecker said: "Let this victorious flag bring the victory of the Chinese people to the German people!"
    The atmosphere of the celebration was extremely warm, and finally, the cultural troupes of the three countries performed wonderful programs.

### 德国青年与中朝青年代表  在柏林举行联欢会

1951-08-11
第4版()
专栏：

　　德国青年与中朝青年代表
    在柏林举行联欢会
    【本报讯】据新华社讯：德国青年和中国、朝鲜青年代表八日在自由德国青年联盟总部举行联欢会，这是世界青年与学生和平联欢节开幕以来德国青年和外国青年的首次联欢会。
    在昂纳克、冯文彬与金琐镇三人致词后，三国青年代表在会上交换了礼物。德国青年以机器模型和望远镜送给中国和朝鲜青年代表团。朝鲜青年代表团向德国和中国青年代表赠旗，中国青年代表以中国新民主主义青年团团旗一面送给德国青年。昂纳克在接受中国青年团的团旗时说：“让这面胜利的旗帜把中国人民的胜利带给德国人民吧！”
    联欢会的气氛极为热烈，最后三国青年文工团表演了精彩节目。
