Title: New York "Youth Peace Committee" Demands End to Korean War
Author:
Date: 1951-08-26
Page: 4

　　Xinhua News Agency, Washington, August 23rd, TASS dispatch: A delegation from the New York State "Youth Peace Committee" visited New York State legislators, demanding an "immediate end to the slaughter in Korea." This peace committee, representing many youth committees, clubs, and independent organizations, pointed out: "There is no reason to lose a single life while negotiations are ongoing." The committee also requested that negotiations continue uninterrupted until an agreement is reached. "We also believe that this settlement of the Korean War must be the beginning of universal peace talks between major powers to guarantee lasting peace for our generation and the future of American and world youth."

### 纽约“青年和平委员会”要求停止朝鲜战争

1951-08-26
第4版()
专栏：

　　纽约“青年和平委员会”要求停止朝鲜战争
    【新华社二十三日讯】塔斯社华盛顿二十一日讯：纽约州“青年和平委员会”代表团访问纽约州的议员们，要求“立即停止在朝鲜的屠杀”。这个代表许多青年委员会、俱乐部和独立组织的和平委员会指出：“在谈判继续进行的时候，没有理由要损失一条性命。”委员会并要求谈判应不间断地进行，直到获致协议为止。“我们还认为，朝鲜战争的这种和解，必须是大国间为保证我们这一代的持久和平以及美国和全世界青年的前途而举行的普遍和平谈判的开端。”
