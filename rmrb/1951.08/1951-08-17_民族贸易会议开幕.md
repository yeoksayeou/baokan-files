Title: National Trade Conference Opens
Author:
Date: 1951-08-17
Page: 1
　　
　　National Trade Conference Opens
    [Report from this newspaper] The National Ethnic Trade Conference convened by the Ministry of Trade of the Central People's Government officially opened today (the 17th). A total of more than 140 representatives from various ethnic groups, including Mongolians, Hui, Tibetans, Uyghurs, Miao, Yi, Zhuang, Yao, Li, Dong, and Han, attended. These include representatives from local governments, trade administration departments from various regions, representatives from public and private businesses in various regions, representatives from various industries, and farmer representatives. The main objectives of the conference are to: determine the guidelines and policies for trade in minority areas; resolve existing problems in ethnic trade work; and exchange experiences in ethnic trade work in various regions. This conference will further invigorate trade in minority areas in the future.

### 民族贸易会议开幕

1951-08-17
第1版()
专栏：

　　民族贸易会议开幕
    【本报讯】中央人民政府贸易部召开之全国民族贸易会议于今日（十七日）正式开幕。计到各区蒙、回、藏、维吾尔、苗、彝、僮、徭、黎、侗、汉等族代表共一百四十余人。包括地方政府代表、各地贸易行政部门代表、各区公私商业代表、各行业代表、农民代表等。会议的主要目的是：确定少数民族地区贸易的方针政策；解决少数民族贸易工作中存在的问题，及交流各地区少数民族贸易工作的经验。这个会议将使今后少数民族地区贸易更加活跃。
