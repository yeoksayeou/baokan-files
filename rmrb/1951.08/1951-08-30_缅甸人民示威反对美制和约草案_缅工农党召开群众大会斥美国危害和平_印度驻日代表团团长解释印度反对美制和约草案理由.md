Title: Burmese People Demonstrate Against the U.S.-Drafted Peace Treaty; Burmese Labor-Farmer Party Holds Mass Meeting to Condemn U.S. for Endangering Peace; Head of Indian Delegation to Japan Explains Reasons for India's Opposition to U.S.-Drafted Peace Treaty

Author:
Date: 1951-08-30
Page: 4

　　Xinhua News Agency, August 29th: According to a Reuter report from London on the 28th, citing the Times: Burmese workers and farmers held a demonstration in Rangoon on the 25th against the U.S.-drafted peace treaty with Japan. During the demonstration, the masses shouted slogans against U.S. imperialism.
    The mass meeting held by the Burmese Labor-Farmer Party condemned the draft treaty for "endangering world peace and harming Asian prosperity."
    Xinhua News Agency, August 29th: Tokyo dispatch: The head of the Indian delegation to Japan, K. K. Chettur, held a press conference on the 27th to explain the reasons for India's refusal to participate in the San Francisco Conference. Chettur said that India opposed the U.S. draft peace treaty with Japan because it restricted Japan's sovereignty. He said that under the current plan, Japan's acceptance of the continued occupation by U.S. troops would be forced "humiliation." He pointed out: "It is impossible to return sovereignty to a country and at the same time restrict its sovereignty."
    At the meeting, a "fierce debate" took place between Chettur and the NBC reporter, Cadamborn. The American reporter tried his best to defend the U.S.'s aggressive policy, and said to Chettur: "You may be right in theory. But we are at war, and Japan is an important base from which we must wage this war." Chettur retorted: "You can't first say that you have returned sovereignty to Japan, and then say: 'Sign here'."
    Chettur also said that the Indian government opposed the draft treaty because it did not make a decision on the Taiwan issue. He pointed out that international agreements stipulate that Taiwan should be returned to China, and the Kurile Islands and southern Sakhalin should be returned to the Soviet Union.

### 缅甸人民示威反对美制和约草案  缅工农党召开群众大会斥美国危害和平  印度驻日代表团团长解释印度反对美制和约草案理由

1951-08-30
第4版()
专栏：

　　缅甸人民示威反对美制和约草案
    缅工农党召开群众大会斥美国危害和平
    印度驻日代表团团长解释印度反对美制和约草案理由
    【新华社二十九日讯】据电通社伦敦二十八日电引泰晤士报消息：缅甸工人和农民二十五日在仰光举行反对美制对日和约草案的示威游行。在示威游行时，群众高呼反对美帝国主义的口号。
    缅甸工农党主持召开的群众大会谴责和约草案“危害世界和平，损害亚洲的繁荣”。
    【新华社二十九日讯】东京消息：印度驻日本代表团团长祁图尔在二十七日举行记者招待会，解释印度拒绝参加旧金山会议的理由。祁图尔说：印度所以反对美国对日和约草案，是因为这个草案限制了日本的主权。他说：按照目前的计划，日本接受美国军队的继续占领，将是被迫“忍辱含垢”。他指出：“不可能一方面把主权交还一个国家，同时又限制它的主权。”
    会上，祁图尔与美国全国广播公司记者卡丹波恩之间，发生了“激烈的争辩”。这个美国记者竭力为美国的侵略政策强辩，并对祁图尔说：“你在理论上也许是对的。但是我们正在作战，而日本是一个我们必须从那里进行这个战争的重要基地。”祁图尔反驳他说：“你不能先说你已把主权归还日本，然后又说：‘在这里签字’。”
    祁图尔又说：印度政府之所以反对和约草案，也是由于这个草案没有对台湾问题作出决定。他指出：国际协定规定台湾归还中国，千岛群岛和库页岛南部交还苏联。
