Title: Indian People Celebrate the Anniversary of the Founding of the Chinese People's Liberation Army; Exhibition Held in New Delhi to Introduce the PLA
Author:
Date: 1951-08-04
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency, New Delhi, 3rd. An exhibition commemorating the anniversary of the founding of the Chinese People's Liberation Army was held at Freemasons' Hall in New Delhi from August 1st to 5th. The exhibition depicts the history of the growth and victory of the Chinese People's Liberation Army. The people of New Delhi visited in droves and unanimously praised the great achievements of the People's Liberation Army.
　　
In Calcutta, the India-China Friendship Association of Calcutta held a grand public gathering to celebrate the 24th anniversary of the Chinese People's Liberation Army. The conference was chaired by Tarasankar Bandopadhyay, one of the most famous modern novelists in Bengali. Speakers at the meeting included Indian Communist Party leader Gopalan, renowned Bengali stage actor Bhattacharya, labor leader Ramji Ray Singh, Professor Chattopadhyaya, Professor Bhattacharjee, Professor Mukherjee, and Secretary-General of the India-China Friendship Association and professor of Chinese history, Chakraborty. The speakers praised the glorious history of the Chinese People's Liberation Army and explained the great significance of its victory.

### 印度人民庆祝我建军节  新德里举行展览会介绍解放军

1951-08-04
第4版()
专栏：

　　印度人民庆祝我建军节
    新德里举行展览会介绍解放军
    【新华社新德里三日电】纪念中国人民解放军建军节的展览会，从八月一日至五日在新德里的共济堂举行。展览会描写中国人民解放军的成长与胜利的历史。新德里的人民纷纷前往参观，并一致赞扬人民解放军的伟大成就。
    在加尔各答，加尔各答印中友好协会举行一个盛大的公众集会，庆祝中国人民解放军二十四周年纪念。大会由最著名的孟加拉语现代小说家之一泰拉先卡·班那吉担任主席。在会上演说的人有印度共产党领袖高普兰，孟加拉著名的舞台演员柏塔米吉，工人领袖拉姆齐雷特·辛格，卡托巴达雅教授，巴塔查班教授，慕寇吉教授与印中友好协会秘书长、中国历史教授查克拉弗蒂等。演说者称颂中国人民解放军的光荣历史，并解释其胜利的重大意义。
