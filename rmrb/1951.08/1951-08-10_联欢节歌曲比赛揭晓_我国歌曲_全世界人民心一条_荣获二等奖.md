Title: Song Competition of the Youth Festival Announced: China's Song "People of the World Are of One Heart" Wins Second Prize
Author:
Date: 1951-08-10
Page: 4

　　Song Competition of the Youth Festival Announced
    China's Song "People of the World Are of One Heart" Wins Second Prize
    [By Our Correspondent] According to Xinhua News Agency: The results of the song competition of the Third World Youth and Student Peace Festival have been announced. Sixty-nine songs composed by young musicians from various countries participated in the competition. As a result of the competition, eight songs won prizes, and another thirteen songs received honorary certificates.
    The judging panel unanimously decided to award the first prize to the "March of the Soviet Youth," composed by Soviet composer D.D. Shostakovich, with lyrics by M. Matusovsky.
    The second prize winners are: "Song of the Festival," composed by Novikov, with lyrics by Kharitonov (Soviet Union); "People of the World Are of One Heart," composed by Qu Xixian, with lyrics by Zhao Si (China); "Friendship, Unity, and Peace," composed by Atlier, with lyrics by Keller (Germany); "Gathering of Twenty-Year-Old Youth," composed by Wenner, with lyrics by Bashi (France).
    The third prize winners are: "Youth, Use Your Strength," composed by Robin, with lyrics by Horne; "Song of Youth," composed by Dunayevsky, with lyrics by Matusovsky; "Roses Blooming in August," composed by Friedrich, with lyrics by Muller.

### 联欢节歌曲比赛揭晓  我国歌曲“全世界人民心一条”荣获二等奖

1951-08-10
第4版()
专栏：

　　联欢节歌曲比赛揭晓
    我国歌曲“全世界人民心一条”荣获二等奖
    【本报讯】据新华社讯：第三届世界青年与学生和平联欢节歌曲竞赛已经揭晓，参加竞赛的有各国青年音乐家创作的六十九个歌曲，比赛结果有八个歌曲得奖，另有十三个歌曲获得荣誉奖状。
    评判团一致决定把一等奖授给苏联作曲家杜立科夫作曲、多尔玛托夫斯基作词的“苏联青年进行曲”。
    荣获二等奖的是：“联欢节之歌”：作曲者诺维科夫，作词者哈里托诺夫（苏联）；“全世界人民心一条”：作曲者瞿希贤，作词者招司（中国）；“友谊、团结与和平”：作曲者阿茨利尔，作词者凯勒（德国）；“二十岁青年的聚会”：作曲者温纳，作词者巴席（法国）。
    荣获三等奖的是：“青年们，拿出你们的力量”作曲者罗滨，作词者霍恩；“青年之歌”：作曲者杜纳耶夫斯基，作词者马杜索夫斯基；“八月盛开的玫瑰”：作曲者弗利德里希，作词者穆勒。
