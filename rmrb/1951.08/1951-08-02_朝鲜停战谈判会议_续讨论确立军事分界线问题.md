Title: Korean Armistice Talks: Continued Discussion on Establishing a Military Demarcation Line
Author:
Date: 1951-08-02
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Pyongyang, August 1st: This reporter learned from the headquarters on the Korean front that the sixteenth meeting of the Korean armistice talks continued at 10:00 AM (Korean time) on August 1st to discuss the establishment of a military demarcation line between the two sides in order to establish a demilitarized zone. At 12:25 PM, the United Nations Command delegation suggested a recess. The seventeenth meeting is scheduled to be held at 10:00 AM on August 2nd.

### 朝鲜停战谈判会议  续讨论确立军事分界线问题

1951-08-02
第1版()
专栏：

　　朝鲜停战谈判会议
    续讨论确立军事分界线问题
    【新华社平壤一日电】本社记者自朝鲜前线总部获悉：朝鲜停战谈判会议第十六次会议，在八月一日上午十时（朝鲜时间），继续讨论确立双方军事分界线，以建立非军事地区的问题，中午十二时二十五分联合国军代表团建议体会。第十七次会议定于八月二日上午十时举行。
