Title: All Circles in Beijing Welcome the Visiting Vietnamese People's Delegation

Author:
Date: 1951-08-04
Page: 1

　　All Circles in Beijing Welcome the Visiting Vietnamese People's Delegation
　　On the evening of the 2nd, people from all walks of life in Beijing held a gathering at the Zhongshan Park Concert Hall to welcome the Vietnamese People's Delegation to China. The meeting was presided over by the Beijing Municipal People's Representative Conference Consultative Committee. About 5,000 people attended, including Peng Zhen, Chairman of the Beijing Municipal People's Representative Conference Consultative Committee, Vice Chairman Liu Ren, and committee members. Zhang Xiruo first delivered a welcome speech on behalf of the people from all walks of life in Beijing. Huang Quoc Viet, head of the Vietnamese People's Delegation, then presented a flag on behalf of the Vietnam National United Front to the Beijing Municipal People's Representative Conference Consultative Committee, which was received by Peng Zhen, Chairman of the Beijing Municipal People's Consultative Committee. Head of Delegation Huang Quoc Viet then reported to the assembly on the situation of the Vietnamese people's national liberation struggle. Peking opera performances were staged after the meeting.

### 北京各界欢迎越南人民访华团

1951-08-04
第1版()
专栏：

　　北京各界欢迎越南人民访华团
    二日晚，北京市各界人民在中山公园音乐堂集会欢迎越南人民访华代表团。大会由北京市各界人民代表会议协商委员会主持，出席大会的有北京市各界人民代表会议协商委员会主席彭真、副主席刘仁及委员等约五千人。张奚若首先代表北京市各界人民致欢迎词。越南人民访华代表团团长黄国越继代表越南国民联合战线向北京市各界人民代表会议协商委员会献旗，由北京市各界人民协商委员会主席彭真受旗。黄国越团长随即向大会报告了越南人民民族解放斗争的情形。会后演出京剧节目。
