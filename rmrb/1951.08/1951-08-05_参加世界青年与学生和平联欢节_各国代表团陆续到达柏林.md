Title: Delegations from Various Countries Arrive in Berlin to Participate in the World Youth and Students for Peace Festival
Author:
Date: 1951-08-05
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, August 3rd:

　　Peace-loving youth representatives from all over the world are gathering in Berlin to participate in the World Youth and Students for Peace Festival. According to TASS Berlin: Delegations from China, North Korea, Vietnam, Mongolia, Albania, Norway, Iraq, Lebanon, Costa Rica, Spain, the Netherlands, the United States, and Finland successively arrived in Berlin on July 31st and August 1st, and were warmly welcomed by German youth. Youth representatives from Britain, France, Belgium, Austria, Iceland, Denmark, West Africa, Australia, and New Zealand are en route to Berlin. In addition, delegations from India, Indonesia, Cuba, South Africa, Ireland, Bulgaria, and Czechoslovakia have already arrived in Berlin.

　　Iranian youth representatives, who overcame the obstruction of the Iranian government to reach Berlin, told TASS reporters that despite the government authorities' arrests and refusal to issue passports, Iranian youth, with the help of the working people, finally formed a delegation and sent it to Berlin. The exhibits we brought to Berlin, overcoming all difficulties, will reflect the Iranian youth's struggle for peace and national independence.

　　A portion of the Italian youth representatives has overcome difficulties to reach Berlin. The broad democratic youth within Italy are continuing to angrily protest the Italian government's obstruction of Italian youth representatives from participating in the festival by refusing to issue passports. The Rome Youth Delegation has lodged a protest with the Speaker of the House of Representatives, and youth representatives from cities such as Florence and Naples have lodged similar protests with the mayors of these cities. Youth workers in many industrial centers in northern Italy have held protest strikes. In response to the government's illegal measures, the youth of Perugia have organized a "Signature Week for the Peace Pact Declaration."

Xinhua News Agency, Berlin, August 4th:

　　Feng Wenbin, head of the Chinese Youth Delegation to the World Youth and Students for Peace Festival, and Wu Han, deputy head, arrived in Berlin by the same plane as Mikhailov, head of the Soviet delegation, on the morning of the 3rd. Bilinger, Chairman of the Festival Preparatory Committee, Onacker, Chairman of the Free German Youth League, and youth representatives from various countries went to the airport to welcome them. Pushkin, head of the Soviet diplomatic mission, and Ji Pengfei, head of the Chinese diplomatic mission, also went to the airport to welcome them.

### 参加世界青年与学生和平联欢节  各国代表团陆续到达柏林

1951-08-05
第4版()
专栏：

　　参加世界青年与学生和平联欢节
    各国代表团陆续到达柏林
    【新华社三日讯】爱好和平的青年代表正陆续由全世界各地汇集柏林，来参加世界青年与学生和平联欢节。据塔斯社柏林讯：中国、朝鲜、越南、蒙古、阿尔巴尼亚、挪威、伊拉克、黎巴嫩、哥斯达黎加、西班牙、荷兰、美国和芬兰等国代表团已于七月三十一日和八月一日相继到达柏林，受到德国青年的热烈欢迎。英国、法国、比利时，奥地利、冰岛、丹麦、西非、澳大利亚和新西兰的青年代表正在赴柏林途中。此外，业已先期到达柏林的有印度、印尼、古巴、南非、爱尔兰、保加利亚和捷克斯洛伐克的代表团。
    冲破伊朗政府的阻挠到达柏林的伊朗青年代表对塔斯社记者发表谈话说：尽管政府当局实行逮捕并拒发出国护照，伊朗青年在劳动人民的帮助下，却终于组成了代表团，并把代表团派到柏林来了。我们克服一切困难带到柏林来的展览品，将反映出伊朗青年的争取和平与民族独立的斗争。
    意大利青年代表一部分已冲破困难到达柏林。意大利国内广大的民主青年正继续愤怒地抗议意大利政府以拒发护照阻挠意大利青年代表参加联欢节。罗马青年代表团已向下院议长提出了抗议，佛罗伦萨和那不勒斯等城市的青年代表已向这些城市的市长提出同样的抗议。意大利北部许多工业中心的青年工人举行了抗议罢工。柏鲁基亚的青年为了回答政府的非法措施，已组织了“为和平公约宣言征集签名周”。
    【新华社柏林四日电】出席世界青年与学生和平联欢节的中国青年代表团团长冯文彬、副团长吴晗，于三日上午与苏联代表团团长米哈依洛夫同机飞抵柏林。前往机场欢迎的有联欢节筹备委员会主席比林格尔，自由德国青年联盟主席昂纳克，以及来自各国的青年代表们。苏联外交使团团长普式金和中国外交使团团长姬鹏飞也到机场欢迎。
