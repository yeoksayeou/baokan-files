Author:
Date: 1951-08-27

    Our Great Motherland
    The long-distance telephone service of New China is developing rapidly. Last year, 195 percent of the planned installation projects were completed. Three-channel carrier equipment has been installed on the main trunk lines throughout the country, so that at any given moment, three pairs of people can talk to each other simultaneously on these trunk lines without interference. This is the situation at the long-distance exchange of the Wuhan Telecommunications Bureau.

### 我们伟大的祖国（图片）

1951-08-27
第1版()
专栏：

    我们伟大的祖国
    新中国的长途电话，目前正在飞跃发展。去年一年中已完成了装机工程计划的百分之一百九十五，在全国主要干线上，都装置了三路载波机，使得每时每刻，在这些干线上，都可以有三对人同时互相通话，不受干扰。这是武汉电信局长途台工作的情形。
