Title: Government Administration Council Holds Meeting Yesterday
Author:
Date: 1951-08-18
Page: 2

　　Xinhua News Agency, 17th dispatch: The 98th Government Administration Council meeting of the Central People's Government was held yesterday afternoon. The meeting listened to and approved the report made by Liu Lantao, Minister of the North China Affairs Department of the Central People's Government, on agricultural production and disaster relief in North China. The meeting also approved several personnel appointment and removal cases and cases to be submitted to the Central People's Government Committee for approval of appointments.

### 政务院昨举行会议

1951-08-18
第2版()
专栏：

　　政务院昨举行会议
    【新华社十七日讯】中央人民政府政务院第九十八次政务会议于十七日下午举行。会议听取和批准了中央人民政府华北事务部刘澜涛部长所作的关于华北农业生产和抗灾情况的报告。会议并通过任免案及提请中央人民政府委员会批准任命案多起。
