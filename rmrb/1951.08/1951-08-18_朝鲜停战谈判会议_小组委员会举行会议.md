Title: Korean Armistice Negotiation Meeting - Subcommittee Holds Meeting
Author:
Date: 1951-08-18
Page: 2

　　Korean Armistice Negotiation Meeting
    Subcommittee Holds Meeting
    [Xinhua News Agency, Pyongyang, 17th] This reporter learned from the headquarters on the Korean front that the first meeting of the Joint Subcommittee of the Korean Armistice Negotiation Conference was held at 10:00 AM (Korean time) on August 17th. The representatives of the Korean People's Army and the Chinese People's Volunteer Army attending the Joint Subcommittee were General Li Xiangchao and General Xie Fang, and the representatives of the United Nations Command delegation were Major General Hodge and Rear Admiral Burke. According to the agreement of both delegations, the first meeting of the subcommittee initially discussed ways to resolve the current stalemate in the conference. The first meeting of the subcommittee ended at 3:20 PM on August 17th. Its second meeting is scheduled to be held at 10:00 AM on August 18th.

### 朝鲜停战谈判会议  小组委员会举行会议

1951-08-18
第2版()
专栏：

　　朝鲜停战谈判会议
    小组委员会举行会议
    【新华社平壤十七日电】本社记者自朝鲜前线总部获悉：朝鲜停战谈判会议联合小组委员会第一次会议在八月十七日上午十时（朝鲜时间）举行。朝鲜人民军与中国人民志愿军代表团出席联合小组委员会的代表为李相朝将军与解方将军，联合国军代表团的代表为霍治陆军少将与勃克海军少将。根据双方代表团的协议，小组委员会第一次会议初步地讨论了解决目前会议僵局的办法。小组委员会第一次会议在八月十七日下午三时二十分结束。其第二次会议定于八月十八日上午十时举行。
