Title: Sixth Anniversary of the Soviet Army's Liberation of Korea: Premier Kim Il-sung Cables Marshal Stalin to Express Respect; Marshal Stalin Cables Premier Kim Il-sung to Offer Congratulations
Author:
Date: 1951-08-18
Page: 2

Xinhua News Agency, Pyongyang, 17th: On the occasion of the sixth anniversary of the liberation of Korea, Premier Kim Il-sung sent a telegram to Marshal Stalin to express his respect and gratitude. Marshal Stalin sent a telegram to Premier Kim Il-sung to offer his congratulations.

Telegram of Respect and Gratitude to Marshal Stalin:

Marshal Stalin:

On the occasion of the sixth anniversary of our country's liberation from Japanese rule thanks to the heroic Soviet army, I, on behalf of the government of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea, all the Korean people, and myself, extend our deepest respect and infinite gratitude to Your Excellency and, through you, to all the Soviet people and their armed forces—the Soviet army, our nation's liberator.

Thanks to the continued and selfless assistance of the Soviet people under your leadership, the Korean people have had the opportunity to establish their own independent national state.

The democratic construction in the northern half of the Republic has spurred the creativity of our people and enabled the cause of democratic construction to achieve unprecedented development. Our people's desire for national unification and independence and their confidence in the people's democratic system have created the conditions for our people to achieve historic results in the struggle against the armed intervention of the United States and its satellite nations.

The Korean people are filled with the firm determination to persist in the just struggle until the American aggressors abandon their predatory plan to conquer Korea.

The Korean people, welcoming this national holiday, sincerely wish Your Excellency and all the Soviet people new achievements in their great creative labor and in the struggle for the peace and security of all nations, and wish Your Excellency a long life.

Kim Il-sung
Premier of the Cabinet of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea
Pyongyang, August 15, 1951

Marshal Stalin's Congratulatory Telegram to Premier Kim Il-sung:

To Comrade Kim Il-sung, Premier of the Cabinet of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea:

On the occasion of the national holiday of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea, I extend my sincere congratulations to Comrade Premier and wish the heroic Korean people success in their struggle for independence.

Stalin, August 15

### 苏军解放朝鲜六周年纪念日  金日成首相电斯大林大元帅致敬  斯大林大元帅致电金日成首相祝贺

1951-08-18
第2版()
专栏：

　　苏军解放朝鲜六周年纪念日
    金日成首相电斯大林大元帅致敬
    斯大林大元帅致电金日成首相祝贺
    【新华社平壤十七日电】朝鲜解放六周年纪念日金日成首相致电斯大林大元帅致敬致谢。斯大林大元帅致电金日成首相祝贺。
            向斯大林大元帅致敬致谢电：斯大林大元帅：
    当此由于英雄的苏军武力使我国从日寇统治下获得解放的第六周年之际，我谨以朝鲜民主主义人民共和国政府和全朝鲜人民及我个人的名义，向阁下并通过阁下向全体苏联人民及其武装力量——我民族的解放者苏联军队，致以深深的敬意和无限的感谢。
    由于阁下所领导的苏联人民继续不断的大公无私的援助，使得朝鲜人民有了得以建立自己民族的独立国家的可能。
    共和国北半部的民主建设促使我们人民发挥创造力，并使民主建设事业获得未之前见的发展。我们人民对于民族统一独立的渴望和对于人民民主制度的信心，创造了我们人民在和美国及其仆从国家武装干涉的斗争中能以取得历史性的各种成果的条件。
    朝鲜人民充满了坚持正义斗争直到美国侵略者放弃其征服朝鲜的强盗计划时为止的坚强决心。
    迎接民族节日的朝鲜人民，谨祝阁下和全体苏联人民在伟大的创造性劳动和为各族人民和平安全的斗争中获得新的成就，并祝阁下万寿无疆。
                      朝鲜民主主义人民共和国内阁首相金日成
                                一九五一年八月十五日平壤市
   斯大林大元帅致金日成首相祝贺电：朝鲜民主主义人民共和国内阁首相金日成同志：
    欣逢朝鲜民主主义人民共和国的民族节日，我谨向首相同志致以衷心的贺忱，并祝英雄的朝鲜人民在争取独立的斗争中获得成就。
                                          斯大林  八月十五日
