Title: USSR Announces Second Quarter Production Report: Total Industrial Output Increases by 16 Percent Compared to the Same Period Last Year
Author:
Date: 1951-08-10
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, 6th: The Central Statistical Bureau of the Council of Ministers of the USSR issued a public communiqué on the results of the implementation of the national economic development plan for the second quarter of 1951 on the 4th. The contents are as follows:

(1) Industry: The total industrial output plan for the second quarter was fulfilled by 104 percent overall. The total industrial output in the second quarter increased by 16 percent compared to the second quarter of last year. The percentages of completion of the industrial output plan for the major industrial sectors are as follows: Ministry of Ferrous Metallurgy 105, Ministry of Non-Ferrous Metallurgy 106, Ministry of Coal Industry 100.3, Ministry of Petroleum Industry 105, Ministry of Chemical Industry 105, Ministry of Electrical Industry 103, Ministry of Heavy Machine Building 100.8, Ministry of Automobile and Tractor Industry 100.7, Ministry of Machine Tool Manufacturing 99.6, Ministry of Machine and Instrument Manufacturing 100, Ministry of Transport Machine Building 100.5, Ministry of Agricultural Machine Building 104, Ministry of Light Industry of the USSR 103, Ministry of Food Industry of the USSR 107.

Labor productivity of industrial workers in the second quarter increased by 10 percent compared to the second quarter of last year. The goal of reducing industrial production costs in the second quarter has been exceeded.

(2) Agriculture: State farms, collective farms, and machine tractor stations have overfulfilled the sowing plan for spring crops. The total sown area this year has increased by 6 million hectares compared to last year, of which the wheat sown area has increased by 4 million hectares, cotton has increased by nearly 400,000 hectares, and the land area for pasture and feed has also greatly increased.

(3) Trade: The total volume of trade of state and cooperative organizations in the second quarter of this year, calculated at comparable prices, increased by 14 percent compared to the second quarter of last year. Sales of meat increased by 17 percent, cotton fabrics by 22 percent, silk fabrics by 20 percent, gramophones by 46 percent, watches by 24 percent, and cameras by 65 percent. Sales of agricultural products in the collective farm markets in the second quarter also greatly increased compared to the second quarter of last year.

### 苏联发表第二季生产公报  工业总产量较去年同期增百分之十六

1951-08-10
第4版()
专栏：

　　苏联发表第二季生产公报
    工业总产量较去年同期增百分之十六
    【新华社六日讯】据塔斯社莫斯科讯：苏联部长会议中央统计局于四日发表一九五一年第二季发展国民经济计划执行结果的公报，内容如下：
    （一）工业方面：第二季工业总产量计划整个说来完成了百分之一百零四，第二季工业总产量比去年第二季增加了百分之十六。各主要工业部门完成工业产量计划的百分比如下：黑色金属冶炼部一○五，有色金属冶炼部一○六，煤业部一○○·三，石油工业部一○五，化学工业部一○五，电气工业部一○三，重型机器制造部一○○·八，汽车与拖拉机工业部一○○·七，母机制造部九九·六，机器与器械制造部一○○，运输机器制造部一○○·五，农业机器制造部一○四，苏联轻工业部一○三，苏联食品工业部一○七。
    第二季产业工人劳动生产率比去年第二季提高了百分之十。第二季减低工业生产成本的目标已经超过。
    （二）农业方面：国家农场、集体农场和机器拖拉机站已超额完成了春季作物的播种计划。今年播种的总面积比去年增加了六百万公顷，其中小麦播种面积增加了四百万公顷，棉花差不多增加了四十万公顷，牧草、饲料的土地面积也大为增加。
    （三）贸易方面：今年第二季国家和合作社贸易额以比较价格计算，比去年第二季增加了百分之十四。肉类销售量增加了百分之十七，棉织品百分之二十二，丝织品百分之二十，留声机百分之四十六，表百分之二十四，照像机百分之六十五。第二季集体农场市场的农产品销售量也比去年第二季大为增加。
