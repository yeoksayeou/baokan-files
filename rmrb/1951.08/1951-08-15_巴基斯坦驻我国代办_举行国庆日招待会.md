Title: Pakistani Chargé d'Affaires in China Holds National Day Reception
Author: 
Date: 1951-08-15
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, August 14th: Ahmed Ali, Chargé d'Affaires of Pakistan in China, held a reception this evening to celebrate Pakistan's Independence Day, August 14th.

Zhou Enlai, Premier of the State Council and Minister of Foreign Affairs of the Central People's Government, was invited to attend. Also invited were Zhang Hanfu, Vice Minister of Foreign Affairs of the Central People's Government, Ma Xulun, Minister of Education, Zhou Yang, Vice Minister of Culture, and others.

Diplomatic envoys or officials from the Soviet Union, Czechoslovakia, Romania, India, Hungary, Burma, Bulgaria, Sweden, Indonesia, Denmark, Switzerland and other countries to China, as well as representatives from the governments of the Netherlands, the United Kingdom, and Norway who are in China for negotiations, were also invited to attend.

The reception took place in a friendly atmosphere.

### 巴基斯坦驻我国代办  举行国庆日招待会

1951-08-15
第1版()
专栏：

　　巴基斯坦驻我国代办
    举行国庆日招待会
    【新华社十四日讯】八月十四日是巴基斯坦独立纪念日，巴基斯坦驻华代办阿默德艾礼于今日晚间举行招待会庆祝。
    中央人民政府政务院总理兼外交部长周恩来应邀出席。应邀出席者尚有我中央人民政府外交部副部长章汉夫、教育部长马叙伦、文化部副部长周扬等人。
    苏联、捷克斯洛伐克、罗马尼亚、印度、匈牙利、缅甸、保加利亚、瑞典、印度尼西亚、丹麦、瑞士等国驻华使节或外交官员以及荷兰、英国、挪威三国政府的谈判代表，亦应邀与会。
    招待会在友好的气氛中进行。
