Title: Our Great Motherland (Picture)
Author:
Date: 1951-08-01
Page: 1

    Our Great Motherland
    Today is the glorious and great 24th Anniversary of "August 1st". In 1927, the first domestic revolutionary war in China failed due to the betrayal of the Kuomintang. A revolutionary army stationed in Nanchang, under the leadership of Comrades Zhou Enlai, He Long, Ye Ting, etc., launched an armed uprising on August 1st. This is Nanchang Park, the site where the "August 1st" Uprising troops fought against the reactionary Kuomintang army, and it has now been renamed "August 1st" Park.

### 我们伟大的祖国（图片）

1951-08-01
第1版()
专栏：

    我们伟大的祖国
    今天是光荣伟大的“八一”二十四周年纪念日。一九二七年，中国第一次国内革命战争因为国民党的背叛而失败，驻在南昌的一支革命军队，在周恩来、贺龙、叶挺等同志领导下，在八月一日举行武装起义。这是“八一”起义部队与国民党反动军队作战的地点南昌公园，现已改名为“八一”公园。
