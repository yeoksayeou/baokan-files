Title: The People of the Whole Country Send Telegrams of Congratulations on the 30th Anniversary of the Party

Author: 
Date: 1951-08-03
Page: 1

On the occasion of the 30th anniversary of the Party, people from all over the country, including individuals and organizations, have sent telegrams or letters to the Central Committee of the Communist Party of China and Chairman Mao, expressing their warm congratulations. The list of these organizations and individuals, in addition to those already published, is published successively as follows:

　　All teachers of the Fourth Primary School in Li'ou Township, Kaiping County. Industry and commerce circles of Licunxu, Kaiping County. All workers of the Licunxu Branch of the Guangdong Provincial China Store Employees' Union. Propaganda and Education Committee members and farmers of the Nan'ao Village Farmers' Association, Yudu County, Jiangxi. Farmers from Weibian and Tuwei Villages, Shanglao Township, Quyang District, Jiangxi Province. All staff and workers of the Yudu Grain Store of the China Grain Company. All faculty, staff, and students of Chengdu Women's Vocational School. The second branch of the Chengdu Women's Middle School Communist Youth League General Branch. The second group of Senior Three ○ (One) of Chengdu Women's Middle School. The first session of the first People's Representatives Conference of all circles in the Fifth District of Chengdu City. Representatives of Wangjia Township, Chongqing County. All farmers of Dongyi Village, Sixth Branch of the Farmers' Association of Huaiyuan Township, Chongqing County. Xiaogiao Work Group of the Yingshan Work Team of the Land Reform Work Group in Northern Sichuan District. The People's Government, Village Farmers' Association, and comrades of the Work Group of Yongning Township, Fushun District, Santai County. All workers of the Santai Nanfeng Temple Salt Workers' Preparatory Branch. All faculty and staff of Santai Xinde Elementary School. All staff and workers of the Santai County People's Rice Mill. The Stone Workers' Branch of the Seventh District of Langzhong, all faculty and students of Wencheng Township Complete Primary School, all youths of the Shuangbai Village Communist Youth League, the Women's Committee of Boshu Township, the Communist Youth League Branch, all the people, children, and all members of the Education Association. Farmers of Democratic Village and Donglin Village in Boshu Township, Langzhong County, cadres of Yangsi Village, Women's Federation, members of the Farmers' Association, all members of the village, the Farmers' Self-Defense Force, the entire government of Qingdian Village, all cadres and masses of Tutang Village in Wencheng Township, and all cadres and masses of Lingcheng Village.

### 全国人民电贺党的三十周年

1951-08-03
第1版()
专栏：

　　全国人民电贺党的三十周年
    在党的三十周年纪念日，全国各地人民，包括个人和团体，纷纷打电报或写信给中国共产党中央委员会和毛主席，表示热烈的祝贺。这些团体和个人的名单，除已发表者外，陆续发表如下：
    开平县里讴乡第四小学全体教师。开平县里村圩工商界。广东省中国店员工会里村圩全体工人。江西雩都县南坳村农会宣教委员、农民。江西省曲洋区上老乡圩边、土围村农民。中国粮食公司雩都粮店全体职工。成都女子职业学校全体师生员工。成都女中团总支第二分支一小组。成都女中高三○（一）第二小组。成都市第五区首届首次各界人民代表会议。崇庆县王家乡代表。崇庆县怀远乡农民协会第六分会东义村全体农民。川北区土地改革工作团营山工作队小桥工作组。三台县富顺区永宁乡人民政府、乡村农民协会、工作团同志。三台县南峰寺盐工筹备分会全体工人。三台新德完小全体教职员。三台县人民机米厂全体职工。阆中第七区石工分会、文成乡完全小学全体师生、双柏村青年团全体青年、博树乡妇女委员会、青年团支部、全体民众、儿童、教联会全体。阆中县博树乡民主村、东林村农民、杨泗村干部、妇女联合会、农会会员、全村会员、农民自卫队、清傎村人民政府全体、文成乡土堂村全体干部、灵城村全体干部和群众。
