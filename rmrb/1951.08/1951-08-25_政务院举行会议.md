Title: Government Administration Council Holds Meeting
Author:
Date: 1951-08-25
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, August 24th—The 99th meeting of the Government Administration Council of the Central People's Government was held on the afternoon of the 24th. The meeting heard and approved the report of the Overseas Chinese Affairs Commission of the Central People's Government on the First Expanded Overseas Chinese Affairs Conference. After listening to the report by Zhang Bojun, Minister of the Ministry of Communications of the Central People's Government, on the construction of the Tanggu New Port, the meeting approved the decision to establish the Tanggu Port Construction Committee of the Ministry of Communications of the Central People's Government. In addition, the meeting also approved a number of appointments and removals.

### 政务院举行会议

1951-08-25
第1版()
专栏：

　　政务院举行会议
    【新华社二十四日讯】中央人民政府政务院第九十九次政务会议于二十四日下午举行。会议听取和批准了中央人民政府华侨事务委员会关于第一次侨务扩大会议的报告。会议在听取中央人民政府交通部章伯钧部长所作的关于修建塘沽新港的报告后，通过了关于成立中央人民政府交通部塘沽建港委员会的决定。此外，会议并通过任免案多起。
