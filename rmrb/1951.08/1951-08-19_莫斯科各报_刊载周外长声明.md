Title: Moscow Newspapers Publish Foreign Minister Zhou's Statement
Author:
Date: 1951-08-19
Page: 4

　　Moscow Newspapers
    Publish Foreign Minister Zhou's Statement
    [Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, August 18th] Moscow newspapers all prominently published on August 17th the full text of the statement by Zhou Enlai, Minister of Foreign Affairs of the Central People's Government of the People's Republic of China, regarding the US-British draft peace treaty with Japan and the San Francisco Conference.

### 莫斯科各报  刊载周外长声明

1951-08-19
第4版()
专栏：

　　莫斯科各报
    刊载周外长声明
    【新华社莫斯科十八日电】莫斯科各报都在十七日以显著地位，全文刊载了中华人民共和国中央人民政府外交部周恩来部长发表的关于美英对日和约草案及旧金山会议的声明。
