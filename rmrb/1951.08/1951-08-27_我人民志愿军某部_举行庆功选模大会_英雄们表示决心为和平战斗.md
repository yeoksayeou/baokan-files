Title: A Unit of the Chinese People's Volunteer Army Holds Celebration and Model Selection Meeting; Heroes Express Determination to Fight for Peace
Author:
Date: 1951-08-27
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, Korean Front, 25th—A certain unit of the Chinese People's Volunteer Army held a celebration of merit and model selection meeting for the entire army in early July. At the meeting, heroes, models, and meritorious personnel reported their brilliant achievements and summarized and exchanged combat experiences. They unanimously expressed their determination to fight to the end to defend the motherland, defend the Far East, and defend world peace. The heroes, models, and meritorious personnel who participated in the meeting said in a letter to the China Peace Committee Against American Aggression and Aiding Korea: "For eight months, under the wise leadership of General Kim Il-sung and the correct leadership of higher-level leaders, and with the full support and enthusiastic encouragement of the Chinese and Korean people, we and our brother units have achieved the great victory of the Five Campaigns, which is also a victory for the world's peace and democracy camp." The letter said: "If the American aggressors dare to continue their aggression, we will, on the basis of the victory of the Five Campaigns, unite with our brother units and the Korean people, continue to carry forward the heroic and arduous fighting style, and fight to the end to completely defeat the American aggressors and defend world peace!" The meeting also wrote letters to Kim Tu-bong, Chairman of the Presidium of the Supreme People's Assembly of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea, and General Kim Il-sung, Supreme Commander of the Korean People's Army, to express their respects. The heroes and models expressed their determination in the letters to strive for a just peace in Korea.

### 我人民志愿军某部  举行庆功选模大会  英雄们表示决心为和平战斗

1951-08-27
第1版()
专栏：

　　我人民志愿军某部
    举行庆功选模大会
    英雄们表示决心为和平战斗
    【新华社朝鲜前线二十五日电】我国人民志愿军某部，在七月上旬举行了全军庆功劳选模范大会。会上，英雄、模范、功臣们报告了自己的辉煌战绩，总结了和互相交流了战斗经验。他们一致表示要为保卫祖国、保卫远东和全世界的和平而奋斗到底。参加大会的英雄模范功臣们在给祖国人民抗美援朝总会的信上说：“八个月以来，在金日成将军的英明指挥和上级首长的正确领导下，在中朝人民的全力支援和热情鼓舞下，我们和兄弟部队一道取得了五次战役的伟大胜利，这也是全世界和平民主阵营的胜利。”信中说：“如果美国侵略者敢于继续进犯，我们定将在五次战役胜利的基础上，与兄弟部队一道，团结朝鲜人民，再接再厉地发挥英勇艰苦的战斗作风，为彻底打败美国侵略者，保卫世界和平而战斗到底！”大会并写信给朝鲜民主主义人民共和国最高人民会议常任委员会委员长金枓奉和朝鲜人民军最高司令官金日成将军表示致敬。英雄模范们在信中表示了为争取朝鲜的公正的和平而斗争的决心。
