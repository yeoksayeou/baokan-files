Author:
Date: 1951-08-04

    Our Great Motherland
    The children of miners, who used to roam the streets in the old society, are now receiving education. This is a scene of students at the First Miners' Children's Primary School of the State-owned Fengfeng Coal Mine in Hebei Province listening attentively to their teacher's lecture.

### 我们伟大的祖国（图片）

1951-08-04
第1版()
专栏：

    我们伟大的祖国
    矿工们的子女，在旧社会是流浪街头的，现在受到教育了。这是河北省国营峰峰煤矿第一矿工人子弟小学校的学生们专心地在听教师讲课。
