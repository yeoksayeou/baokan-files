Title: Vietnamese People's Delegation Arrives in Shenyang
Author:
Date: 1951-08-16
Page: 1

　　Xinhua News Agency, Shenyang, 15th. The Vietnamese People's Delegation, led by Chairman Hoàng Quốc Việt, arrived in Shenyang on the afternoon of the 14th from Tianjin. Those who went to the station to welcome them included: Gao Chongmin, Vice Chairman of the Northeast People's Government, Zhang Weizhen, Chairman of the Northeast Federation of Trade Unions, Li Youwen, Secretary-General of the Northeast People's Government, Wang Guangwei, Secretary-General of the United Front Work Department of the Northeast Bureau of the CPC Central Committee, Zhu Qiwen, Mayor of Shenyang, and representatives from various democratic parties and people's organizations, totaling more than 600 people.
　　Mayor Zhu Qiwen delivered a welcoming speech at a brief welcoming ceremony held at the station, and Chairman Hoàng Quốc Việt delivered a reply.
　　During their visit to Shenyang, the delegation plans to attend a welcome rally for cadres in the city and will hold discussions with workers, youth, troops, and health workers in Shenyang.

### 越南人民访华代表团抵沈阳

1951-08-16
第1版()
专栏：

　　越南人民访华代表团抵沈阳
    【新华社沈阳十五日电】越南人民访华代表团在团长黄国越率领下，于十四日下午由天津抵达沈阳。前往车站欢迎的有：东北人民政府副主席高崇民、东北总工会主席张维桢、东北人民政府秘书长栗又文、中共中央东北局统一战线工作部秘书长王光伟、沈阳市长朱其文及各民主党派、各人民团体代表等共六百余人。
    朱其文市长在车站举行的简短欢迎会上致欢迎词，黄国越团长致答词。
    代表团在沈阳访问期间，拟参加该市干部欢迎大会，并将与沈阳市工人、青年、部队、卫生工作者举行座谈会。
