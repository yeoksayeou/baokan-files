Title: China Holds Joint Party with Youth Delegations from Various Countries; Representatives from Various Countries Pay Respects to Mao Zedong, the Great Teacher of Chinese Youth

Author:
Date: 1951-08-19
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency, Berlin, August 18th: The Chinese Youth Delegation continued to hold joint parties with youth representatives from various countries participating in the World Youth and Students Peace Festival on the 16th and 17th.

　　More than 500 youth representatives from Poland, Czechoslovakia, Hungary, Romania, Bulgaria, and Albania attended the joint party held on the 16th. Feng Wenbin, head of the Chinese Youth Delegation, gave the opening speech, emphasizing the fraternal relationship and friendship between Chinese youth and the youth of Eastern European people's democratic countries. When Feng Wenbin introduced the Chinese youth combat heroes who came from the Korean battlefield to participate in the Berlin Festival, warm cheers and applause rang out throughout the venue. Youth representatives from various fraternal countries delivered speeches one after another, praising the Chinese people's great contribution to the cause of world peace. At the meeting, youth representatives from various countries exchanged gifts and performed wonderful artistic programs.

　　On the 17th, the Chinese Youth Delegation held a joint party with 700 German youth. In his speech, delegation leader Feng Wenbin thanked the German youth for their great efforts in preparing for the festival and expressed condolences to the German youth who were injured in West Berlin when they were attacked by American-hired West Berlin police during a rally for peace. Representative Kushi of the Central Committee of the Free German Youth League then spoke, saying: "When German youth were attacked by American police during a rally for peace in West Berlin, they thought of Chinese youth. Chinese youth and the Chinese people have set many brilliant examples for us in their long struggle against imperialism and domestic reactionaries. The Chinese people's revolutionary movement and victory have inspired German youth and the German people to fight for national unity and independence." The joint party lasted for four hours, during which representatives exchanged gifts and performed entertainment programs.

　　Xinhua News Agency, Berlin, August 16th: The Chinese Youth Delegation held a joint party with youth representatives from Latin America and Africa at the Berlin Electrical Equipment Factory Building on the afternoon of the 15th. More than 300 youth representatives from 26 countries, including Argentina, Brazil, Chile, Mexico, the West Indies, Nigeria, Tunisia, Algeria, and the Gold Coast, attended.

　　Feng Wenbin, head of the Chinese Youth Delegation, greeted the youth friends from Latin America and Africa on behalf of the Chinese youth at the joint party. He said: "The experience of the Chinese youth's struggle proves that if the youth unite with all the people, victory will surely be theirs. Unity is strength." At this moment, thunderous applause rang out throughout the venue.

　　A youth representative from West Africa said: "We West African people regard Mao Zedong as a beacon in our struggle for national independence. We will never forget that the Chinese people achieved their final victory after a long struggle. Therefore, we must persevere in our struggle until the imperialists are eliminated from the earth." Other representatives also delivered speeches, expressing their deep admiration for the glorious Chinese youth and Mao Zedong, the great teacher of Chinese youth.

　　Youth representatives performed many national programs at the joint party, while Chinese youth performed wonderful acrobatic programs. The joint party proceeded in a friendly atmosphere for four hours, and the representatives finally left happily with gifts such as books, commemorative badges, and silk embroideries of Chairman Mao.

### 我国与各国青年代表团举行联欢会  各国代表向中国青年的伟大导师毛泽东致敬

1951-08-19
第4版()
专栏：

　　我国与各国青年代表团举行联欢会
    各国代表向中国青年的伟大导师毛泽东致敬
    【新华社柏林十八日电】中国青年代表团于十六日和十七日继续和参加世界青年与学生和平联欢节的各国青年代表举行联欢会。
    在十六日举行的联欢会上，出席的有波兰、捷克斯洛伐克、匈牙利、罗马尼亚、保加利亚和阿尔巴尼亚的青年代表五百多人。中国青年代表团团长冯文彬致开会词，强调指出了中国青年与东欧各人民民主国家青年之间的兄弟般的关系和友谊。当冯文彬介绍由朝鲜战场前来参加柏林联欢节的中国青年战斗英雄时，全场响起了热烈的欢呼声和掌声。各兄弟国家的青年代表相继发表了演说，盛赞中国人民对世界和平事业的伟大贡献。会上各国青年代表交换了礼物，并表演了精彩的文艺节目。
    十七日，我国青年代表团和七百名德国青年举行了联欢。冯文彬团长在致词中感谢德国青年在筹备联欢节期间的巨大努力，并对那些在西柏林争取和平集会中受到美国雇佣的西柏林警察袭击受伤的德国青年表示慰问。自由德国青年联盟中央委员会代表库希接着讲话，他说：“当德国青年在西柏林争取和平集会中受到美国警察袭击的时候，他们就想到了中国青年。中国青年和中国人民在反对帝国主义和国内反动派的长期斗争中，已为我们树立了许多光辉的榜样。中国人民的革命运动和胜利，鼓舞了德国青年和德国人民为争取国家统一和独立而斗争。”联欢会历时四小时，会上代表们交换了礼品并表演了文娱节目。
    【新华社柏林十六日电】中国青年代表团于十五日下午在柏林电工器材厂大厦和拉丁美洲及非洲青年代表举行联欢会。出席的有阿根廷、巴西、智利、墨西哥、西印度群岛、尼日利亚、突尼斯、阿尔及利亚、黄金海岸等二十六个国家的青年代表三百多人。
    中国青年代表团团长冯文彬在联欢会上代表中国青年向拉丁美洲和非洲的青年朋友们致意。他说：“中国青年斗争的经验证明：如果青年们和全体人民团结在一起，胜利就一定是他们的。团结就是力量。”这时，全场响起了雷动的掌声。
    西非的青年代表说：“我们西非人民把毛泽东认为是我们争取民族独立斗争中的明灯。我们永不会忘记：中国人民是在经过长期的斗争后，才获得了最后的胜利。所以我们必须坚持不渝地斗争，一直到地球上清除了帝国主义者为止。”其他的代表也发表了演说，他们都对光荣的中国青年和中国青年的伟大导师毛泽东深表敬仰。
    青年代表们在联欢会上表演了许多民族节目，中国青年则表演了精彩的杂技节目。联欢会在友好的气氛中进行了四小时，代表们最后拿着书籍、纪念章和毛主席的丝质绣像等礼物欢然离去。
