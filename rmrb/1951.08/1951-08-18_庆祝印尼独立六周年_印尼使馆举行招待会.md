Title: Indonesian Embassy Holds Reception to Celebrate the Sixth Anniversary of Indonesian Independence
Author:
Date: 1951-08-18
Page: 2

　　Indonesian Embassy Holds Reception to Celebrate the Sixth Anniversary of Indonesian Independence
    [Xinhua News Agency, 17th] August 17th is the sixth anniversary of the independence of the Republic of Indonesia. Mr. Isma'il Thajeb, Chargé d'affaires of the Indonesian Embassy in China, held a reception this evening to celebrate.
    Zhou Enlai, Premier and Minister of Foreign Affairs of the Central People's Government, was invited to attend. Those also invited included Yu Xinqing, Director of the Bureau of Ceremonies of the Central People's Government, Shen Yanbing, Minister of Culture, Ma Xulun, Minister of Education, Zhang Hanfu, Vice Minister of Foreign Affairs, Wang Shao'ao, Vice Minister of Finance, Lei Renmin, Vice Minister of Trade, Zeng Zhaolun, Vice Minister of Education, Sa Kongliao, Deputy Director of the News Administration, and Zhang Youyu, Deputy Mayor of the Beijing Municipal People's Government, and more than one hundred others.
    Diplomatic officials from the embassies of the Soviet Union, Czechoslovakia, Romania, India, Poland, Hungary, Burma, Bulgaria, Vietnam, Sweden, Denmark, Switzerland, and Pakistan in China, as well as the government negotiation representatives of the United Kingdom and Norway, were also invited to attend.
    The reception proceeded in a friendly atmosphere.

### 庆祝印尼独立六周年  印尼使馆举行招待会

1951-08-18
第2版()
专栏：

　　庆祝印尼独立六周年
    印尼使馆举行招待会
    【新华社十七日讯】八月十七日是印度尼西亚共和国独立第六周年纪念日，印尼驻华大使馆代办伊沙玛蒂于今日晚间举行招待会庆祝。
    中央人民政府政务院总理兼外交部长周恩来应邀出席。应邀出席者还有中央人民政府典礼局局长余心清、文化部部长沈雁冰、教育部部长马叙伦、外交部副部长章汉夫、财政部副部长王绍鏊、贸易部副部长雷任民、教育部副部长曾昭抡、新闻总署副署长萨空了等以及北京市人民政府副市长张友渔等百余人。
    苏联、捷克斯洛伐克、罗马尼亚、印度、波兰、匈牙利、缅甸、保加利亚、越南、瑞典、丹麦、瑞士、巴基斯坦等国驻华使馆的外交官员以及英国、挪威两国政府的谈判代表，亦应邀与会。
    招待会在友好的气氛中进行。
