Title: Hungarian Minister of Defense Sends Congratulations on PLA Founding Day
Author:
Date: 1951-08-02
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, 1st.

　　Hungarian Minister of Defense
    Sends Congratulations on PLA Founding Day
    [Xinhua News Agency, August 1st] Lieutenant General Farkas Mihály, the Hungarian Minister of Defense, sent a telegram to Commander-in-Chief Zhu, congratulating the 24th anniversary of the founding of the Chinese People's Liberation Army. The telegram reads as follows: Comrade Zhu De, Commander-in-Chief of the Chinese People's Liberation Army, Beijing:
    On the occasion of the 24th anniversary of the Chinese People's Liberation Army, on behalf of all fighters, commanders, and generals of the Hungarian People's Army, I extend warm congratulations and best wishes to you and all members of the fraternal armed forces of the People's Republic of China. I wish the Chinese People's Liberation Army future prosperity and new achievements in the cause of your country's people and in consolidating the peace camp headed by the great Stalin.
            Lieutenant General Farkas Mihály, Hungarian Minister of Defense
            Budapest, July 31, 1951

### 匈牙利国防部长  电贺我解放军建军节

1951-08-02
第1版()
专栏：

　　匈牙利国防部长
    电贺我解放军建军节
    【新华社一日讯】匈牙利国防部长法尔卡斯·米哈依中将，致电朱总司令，祝贺中国人民解放军建军二十四周年。电文如下：北京中国人民解放军总司令朱德同志：
    值此中国人民解放军二十四周年之际，谨代表匈牙利人民军所有战斗员、指挥员和将军们，向您及兄弟的中华人民共和国武装力量的全体的成员们，致以热烈的祝贺与最好的愿望。谨祝中国人民解放军将来的壮盛，并在为你们祖国人民的事业上和巩固以伟大的斯大林为首的和平阵营方面有新的成就。
            匈牙利国防部长  法尔卡斯·米哈依中将
            一九五一年七月三十一日，布达佩斯
