Author:
Date: 1951-08-31
Page: 1

    Our Great Motherland
    The successful completion of the Runheji Sluice Gate on the Huai River has brought a bright future to the vast number of farmers on both sides of the Huai River. This is a group of emancipated peasants from northern Anhui who have traveled from forty or fifty miles away to visit the Runheji Sluice Gate. A staff member is explaining the function of the sluice gate to them.

### 我们伟大的祖国（图片）

1951-08-31
第1版()
专栏：

    我们伟大的祖国
    淮河润河集分水闸的胜利完工，给淮河两岸的广大农民带来了美好生活的远景。这是一群皖北的翻身农民，他们从四、五十里以外赶来参观润河集分水闸，一位工作人员正在为他们讲解水闸的功用。
