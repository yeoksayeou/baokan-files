Our Great Motherland
Papaya is a fruit tree planted by farmers in Guangdong. In Guangdong, China, the value of papaya planted per mu is approximately equal to that of three mu of rice. Here, professors and students from the College of Agriculture of Lingnan University in Guangzhou are studying methods to improve papaya varieties.

### 我们伟大的祖国（图片）

1951-08-20
第1版()
专栏：

　　我们伟大的祖国
    木瓜是广东农民所种的一种果树，在我国广东种植每亩木瓜的价值约等于三亩水稻。在这里，广州岭南大学农学院的教授和同学正在研究改良木瓜品种的方法。
