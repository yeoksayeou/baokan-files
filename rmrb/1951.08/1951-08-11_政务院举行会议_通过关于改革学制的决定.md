Title: Government Administration Council Holds Meeting, Approves Decision on Reforming the Education System
Author:
Date: 1951-08-11
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, 10th. The 97th meeting of the Government Administration Council of the Central People's Government was held on the afternoon of the 10th. After hearing the explanation of Minister of Education Ma Xulun of the Central People's Government Ministry of Education, the meeting approved the decision on reforming the education system. The meeting also listened to and approved the "Summary of Work from January 1950 to June 1951 and Key Points of the Plan for the Second Half of 1951" report made by Director Hu Qiaomu of the Central People's Government News Administration. In addition, the meeting also passed several appointment and removal cases and submitted several appointment and removal cases for approval by the Central People's Government Committee.

### 政务院举行会议  通过关于改革学制的决定

1951-08-11
第1版()
专栏：

　　政务院举行会议
    通过关于改革学制的决定
    【新华社十日讯】中央人民政府政务院第九十七次政务会议于十日下午举行。会议在听取中央人民政府教育部马叙伦部长的说明后，通过了关于改革学制的决定。会议并听取和批准了中央人民政府新闻总署胡乔木署长所作的该署一九五零年一月至一九五一年六月工作总结及一九五一年下半年计划要点的报告。此外，会议并通过任免案及提请中央人民政府委员会批准任免案多起。
