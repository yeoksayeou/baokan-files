Title: Romanian Minister of Armed Forces Sends Congratulations on Army Day; Albanian Women's Federation Chairwoman Sends Congratulatory Telegram
Author: 
Date: 1951-08-05
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency, August 3rd -- Romanian Minister of Armed Forces, General Bodnaras, telegraphed Commander-in-Chief Zhu De to congratulate the 24th anniversary of the founding of the Chinese People's Liberation Army. The telegram reads as follows: To General Zhu De, Commander-in-Chief of the People's Liberation Army of the People's Republic of China:

    On the occasion of the 24th anniversary of the founding of the heroic Chinese People's Liberation Army, I extend my warm congratulations to you on behalf of all officers and soldiers of the Armed Forces of the Romanian People's Republic and myself.
      General Bodnaras
            Minister of the Armed Forces of Romania
        August 1, Bucharest

Xinhua News Agency, August 3rd -- Nesihimezhe Hoca, Chairwoman of the Albanian Women's Federation, telegraphed the All-China Democratic Women's Federation to congratulate the Chinese People's Liberation Army on Army Day. The telegram reads as follows:

    On the joyous occasion of the Chinese People's Liberation Army's Army Day, I extend to you warm greetings. And pay tribute to all the glorious Chinese women who are resolutely striving for the prosperity of the motherland. I wish you new victories in the struggle to defend the motherland, defend peace, and democracy.
            Nesihimezhe Hoca
        Chairwoman of the Albanian Women's Federation
        August 1

### 罗马尼亚武装部队部部长  电贺我八一建军节  阿尔巴尼亚妇联主席来电致贺

1951-08-05
第4版()
专栏：

　　罗马尼亚武装部队部部长
    电贺我八一建军节
    阿尔巴尼亚妇联主席来电致贺
    【新华社三日讯】罗马尼亚武装部队部部长波德纳拉斯上将电朱总司令祝贺中国人民解放军建军二十四周年。电文如下：中华人民共和国人民解放军总司令朱德将军：
    值此英勇的中国人民解放军建立二十四周年之际，谨以罗马尼亚人民共和国武装部队全体官兵及我个人的名义向您致以热烈的祝贺。
      罗马尼亚武装部队部部长
            波德纳拉斯上将
        八月一日于布加勒斯特
    【新华社三日讯】阿尔巴尼亚妇女联合会主席奈希米哲·霍查致电中华全国民主妇联，祝贺中国人民解放军建军节。电文如下：
    欣值中国人民解放军建军节，谨向你们致热烈的敬礼。并向全体为祖国的繁荣而坚决奋斗的、光荣的中国妇女致敬。谨祝你们在保卫祖国、保卫和平及民主的斗争中得到新的胜利。
            阿尔巴尼亚妇女联合会主席
        奈希米哲·霍查
        八月一日
