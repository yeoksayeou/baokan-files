Title: Vo Nguyen Giap Issues Order to Celebrate the August Revolution and National Day of Vietnam
Author:
Date: 1951-08-23
Page: 4

　　Xinhua News Agency, North Vietnam, August 20th: General Vo Nguyen Giap, Commander-in-Chief of the Vietnamese People's Army, issued an order to all commanders and fighters of the Vietnamese People's Army, People's Army local forces, militia, and guerrillas in celebration of the anniversary of the August Revolution and the sixth anniversary of the National Day of Vietnam (August 19th and September 2nd). General Vo Nguyen Giap ordered the entire army to carry forward the spirit of the August Revolution, unite with all the people, and make the People's Army a true army of the people.

　　General Vo Nguyen Giap ordered the regular troops of the People's Army to work hard to improve their technical, cultural, and ideological levels, strengthen their combat power, and annihilate more enemies; and ordered the local forces and militia of the People's Army, who are fighting in the enemy's rear and defending their villages, to cooperate closely with the people.

　　General Vo Nguyen Giap ordered the entire army to actively participate in all propaganda, organization, and mobilization work aimed at consolidating and defending the people's regime, and to actively participate in the struggle to defend world peace and democracy.

　　General Vo Nguyen Giap's order concluded: The Indochinese Communist Party and the Viet Minh Front have enabled the August Revolution to achieve victory; the Government of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam, the Vietnam Workers' Party, and the Viet Lien Front will surely enable our resistance war to achieve victory.

### 庆祝越南八月革命和国庆  武元甲发布命令

1951-08-23
第4版()
专栏：

　　庆祝越南八月革命和国庆
    武元甲发布命令
    【新华社二十一日讯】据越南通讯社北越二十日电：越南人民军总司令武元甲将军为庆祝越南八月革命及越南国庆六周年纪念（八月十九日和九月二日），向越南人民军、人民军地方部队、民兵与游击队的全体指挥员和战斗员发布命令。武元甲将军命令全军，发扬八月革命的精神，与全体人民团结一致，使人民军成为真正的人民军队。
    武元甲将军命令人民军正规部队，努力提高技术、文化和思想水平，增强战斗力量，更多地歼灭敌人；命令在敌人后方作战，保卫自己的村庄的人民军地方部队与民兵，密切地与人民合作。
    武元甲将军命令全军积极参加一切目的在于巩固和保卫人民政权的宣传、组织和动员工作，并积极参加保卫世界和平与民主的斗争。
    武元甲将军的命令最后指出：东洋共产党及越盟阵线已使八月革命获得胜利；越南民主共和国政府、越南劳动党及越联战线，一定能使我们的抗战获得胜利。
