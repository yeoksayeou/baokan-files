Title: Chinese Youth Delegation Arrives in Berlin to Participate in the World Youth Peace Festival
Author:
Date: 1951-08-03
Page: 4

　　Participating in the World Youth Peace Festival
    Our Youth Delegation Arrives in Berlin
    [Xinhua News Agency, Berlin, 2nd] The Chinese Youth Delegation attending the Third World Youth and Students Peace Festival, led by Deputy Head of Delegation Rong Gaotang, arrived in Berlin on the afternoon of July 31. Those who welcomed them at the station included Erich Honecker, Chairman of the Free German Youth League, Terni, Secretary-General of the World Federation of Democratic Youth, Grohman, Chairman of the International Union of Students, and youth delegations from the Soviet Union, North Korea, Vietnam, and India. A welcoming rally of more than 3,000 people was held at the station. Terni and Honecker delivered welcoming speeches on behalf of the Festival Preparatory Committee and the youth of all Germany and Berlin, respectively. They both emphasized the unity and friendship of the world's youth in the struggle to defend peace. Rong Gaotang, Deputy Head of the Chinese Youth Delegation, said in reply: Chinese youth are taking practical action to participate in the movement to resist U.S. aggression and aid Korea to defend world peace. The Chinese Youth Delegation has come to participate in the festival to strengthen the unity and friendship of peace-loving youth around the world in the struggle to defend peace. Rong Gaotang finally wished the festival success.
    The Chinese Youth Delegation received a warm welcome from German youth at the various stations along the way to Berlin.

### 参加世界青年和平联欢节  我青年代表团抵柏林

1951-08-03
第4版()
专栏：

　　参加世界青年和平联欢节
    我青年代表团抵柏林
    【新华社柏林二日电】出席第三届世界青年与学生和平联欢节的中国青年代表团，在副团长荣高棠率领下，已于七月三十一日下午抵达柏林。到车站欢迎的有自由德国青年联盟主席昂纳克、世界民主青年联盟总书记特尼、国际学生联合会主席格罗曼等人，以及苏联、朝鲜、越南和印度等国的青年代表团。在车站举行了三千多人的欢迎大会。特尼和昂纳克二人分别代表联欢节筹备委员会和全德国及柏林青年致欢迎词，他们都强调世界青年在保卫和平斗争中的团结与友谊。中国青年代表团副团长荣高棠致答词说：中国青年正在以参加抗美援朝运动的实际行动来保卫世界和平。中国青年代表团前来参加联欢节，是为了加强世界爱好和平的青年在保卫和平斗争中的团结和友谊。荣高棠最后并预祝联欢节成功。
    中国青年代表团在到柏林的沿路各车站时，都受到了德国青年的热烈欢迎。
