Economic Life Trends

    A Concrete Manifestation of Love for the Motherland

    Workers in the automobile group of the Longyan Iron Mine in Xuanhua, Chahar, repaired a large truck with a damaged engine using old parts, saving the state 15.57 million yuan. This is a concrete manifestation of their love for the motherland. They said, "The more money we save for the country, the more we strengthen the power to strike against the American aggressors!" When they repaired this truck at the end of June, the higher authorities awarded them 520 catties of millet; they donated half of it to buy airplanes and used the other half to make new work clothes.

    People's Bank of China Opens Inter-Customs Remittance Services

    The People's Bank of China has been handling inter-customs remittance business since July 15. Remittance locations outside the customs area include: Shenyang, Dalian, Harbin, Mudanjiang, Jilin, Changchun, Andong, Tonghua, Xiong Yuecheng, Wafangdian, Pulandian, Yingkou, Jinzhou, Siping, Qiqihar, Anshan, Fushun, Benxi, Chengde, etc.; Remittance locations inside the customs area include: Beijing, Tianjin, Baoding, Tangshan, Xingji, Anguo, Gaoyang, Shijiazhuang, Shanghai, Taiyuan, Changzhi, Yuci, Yuncheng, Linfen, Xinxiang, Anyang, Liaocheng, Changsha, Xiangtan, Hengyang, Changde, Nanjing, Hankou, Yinchuan, Xining, Dihua, Lanzhou, etc. This is a measure to facilitate the exchange of goods between inside and outside the customs area.

### 经济生活动态

1951-08-08
第2版()
专栏：经济生活动态

　　经济生活动态
    热爱祖国的具体表现
    察哈尔宣化龙烟铁矿汽车组工友，利用旧零件，修复了一辆因引擎损坏的大卡车，给国家节省了一千五百五十七万元。这是他们热爱祖国的具体表现。他们说：“我们多给国家省一文钱，就多增强打击美国侵略者的力量！”当他们在六月底修复这辆卡车时，上级曾奖给他们五百二十斤小米；他们拿出其中的一半捐献飞机；另一半做了新的工作服。
    中国人民银行开放关内外押汇
    中国人民银行已自七月十五日起办理关内外押汇业务，关外押汇地点有：沈阳、大连、哈尔滨、牡丹江、吉林、长春、安东、通化、熊岳城、瓦房店、普兰店、营口、锦州、四平、齐齐哈尔、鞍山、抚顺、本溪、承德等地；关内押汇地点有：北京、天津、保定、唐山、兴济、安国、高阳、石家庄、上海、太原、长治、榆次、运城、临汾、新乡、安阳、聊城、长沙、湘潭、衡阳、常德、南京、汉口、银川、西宁、迪化、兰州等地。这是便利关内外物资交流的一项措施。
