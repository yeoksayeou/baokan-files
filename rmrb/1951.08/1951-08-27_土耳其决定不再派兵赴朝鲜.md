Title: Turkey Decides to Stop Sending Troops to Korea
Author: 
Date: 1951-08-27
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Sofia, August 22nd, via Telegraph Agency:

　　The Turkish "Victory News" reported that the Turkish government has decided not to send any more troops to Korea. The newspaper pointed out that the Turkish government has instructed its permanent representative to the United Nations, Mr. Sabé, to notify the United Nations Secretariat of this decision.

### 土耳其决定不再派兵赴朝鲜

1951-08-27
第4版()
专栏：

　　土耳其决定不再派兵赴朝鲜
    【新华社讯】据电通社索非亚二十二日讯：土耳其“胜利报”报道，土耳其政府已决定不再增派军队赴朝鲜。该报指出，土耳其政府已指令该国驻联合国常任代表沙贝将这一决定通知联合国秘书处。
