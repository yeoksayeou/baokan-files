Title: Burmese Students Hold Congress; Congress Resolves to Oppose US-Made Japanese Peace Treaty
Author:
Date: 1951-08-23
Page: 4

　　Xinhua News Agency, Rangoon, 20th: The All-Burma Student Congress was held in Rangoon from the 15th to the 17th. More than one hundred student representatives from seventeen counties and eighteen cities in Burma participated in the meeting. The meeting decided to establish the All-Burma Student Union and elected the central executive committee of the union.

　　The congress passed resolutions opposing the US-made Japanese Peace Treaty, opposing the United States turning Burma into a military base, and opposing American cultural aggression in Burma. The congress supported the proposal for a five-power peace pact and supported all calls related to defending world peace made at the World Youth and Student Festival for Peace held in Berlin.

### 缅学生举行代表大会  大会决议反对美制对日和约

1951-08-23
第4版()
专栏：

　　缅学生举行代表大会
    大会决议反对美制对日和约
    【新华社二十日讯】仰光讯：全缅学生代表大会于十五日至十七日在仰光举行。参加会议的有缅甸十七个县、十八个市的学生代表共百余人。会议决定成立全缅学生联合总会，并选举了总会的中央执委会。
    大会通过反对美制对日和约、反对美国变缅甸为军事基地及在缅甸进行文化侵略的决议。大会支持五大国缔结和平公约的建议，支持在柏林举行的世界青年与学生和平联欢节中所提出的有关保卫世界和平的一切号召。
