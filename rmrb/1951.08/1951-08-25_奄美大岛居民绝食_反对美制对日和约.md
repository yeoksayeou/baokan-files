Title: Amami Ōshima Residents on Hunger Strike to Protest U.S.-Drafted Peace Treaty with Japan
Author:
Date: 1951-08-25
Page: 4

　　Xinhua News Agency dispatch from London, August 22nd, according to the Telecommunications Agency: The British "Daily Worker" states: Two hundred and twenty residents of Amami Ōshima, south of Japan, have held three all-day hunger strikes in the past six weeks to express their opposition to the U.S.-drafted peace treaty with Japan.
　　Amami Ōshima is located south of 29 degrees north latitude. According to the draft peace treaty, the island will remain a U.S. war base.

### 奄美大岛居民绝食  反对美制对日和约

1951-08-25
第4版()
专栏：

　　奄美大岛居民绝食  反对美制对日和约
    【新华社讯】据电通社伦敦二十二日讯：英国“工人日报”称：日本南面奄美大岛的居民二百二十人，在过去六个星期中曾经进行了三次全天绝食，表示反对美制对日和约草案。
    奄美大岛在北纬二十九度以南，按照和约草案的规定，该岛将依然是美国的战争基地。
