Title: The People's Liberation Army Celebrates its Twenty-Fourth Anniversary: Grand Celebration Held in the Capital
Author:
Date: 1951-08-01
Page: 2

Xinhua News Agency, August 31st: A grand celebration was held at 7:30 this evening in commemoration of the 24th anniversary of the founding of the Chinese People's Liberation Army. The attendees included: Zhu De, Vice Chairman of the Central People's Government, Vice Chairman of the People's Revolutionary Military Commission, and Commander-in-Chief of the Chinese People's Liberation Army; Liu Shaoqi, Vice Chairman of the Central People's Government and Vice Chairman of the People's Revolutionary Military Commission; Zhou Enlai, Vice Chairman of the People's Revolutionary Military Commission and Premier of the State Council; Li Jishen and Zhang Lan, Vice Chairmen of the Central People's Government; Guo Moruo, Chen Shutong, and Shen Junru, Vice Chairmen of the National Committee of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference; Nie Rongzhen, Acting Chief of the General Staff of the People's Revolutionary Military Commission; Xiao Hua, Deputy Director of the General Political Department of the People's Revolutionary Military Commission; responsible persons of various democratic parties; Standing Committee members of the National Committee of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference; members of the Central People's Government Committee; heads of various academies, commissions, committees, ministries, departments, and banks of the Central People's Government; heads of various departments of the People's Revolutionary Military Commission of the Central People's Government; senior military officers of the Army, Navy, and Air Force of the Chinese People's Liberation Army; representatives of combat heroes of the Chinese People's Liberation Army; representatives of returning Chinese People's Volunteers, totaling over 800 people. The entire delegation of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam visiting China was also invited to attend.
    The conference began with the military band playing the national anthem of the People's Republic of China and the march of the Chinese People's Liberation Army. Commander-in-Chief Zhu De of the Chinese People's Liberation Army first delivered a speech (the full text of which is on the first page of today's newspaper).
    Following this, Vice Chairman Guo Moruo of the National Committee of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference spoke. Guo Moruo pointed out: The great victory of the Chinese People's Revolution is inseparable from the arduous struggle and heroic combat of the Chinese People's Liberation Army led by Chairman Mao and the Chinese Communist Party. Guo Moruo called on: In order to consolidate the victory of our people and to defend the independence and security of our motherland, our people must continue to strengthen the People's Liberation Army, continue to strengthen the production and donation movement, the youth joining the army campaign, and the work of providing preferential treatment.
    After the speeches, representatives of the people from all walks of life in Beijing, representatives of the Beijing Young Pioneers, representatives of the nursery of the directly subordinate organs of the People's Revolutionary Military Commission of the Central People's Government, the summer capital visiting groups of the Young Pioneers from the Northeast and Northwest regions, the delegation of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam visiting China, and representatives of the workers of the Sixth Automobile Assembly Plant of the Chinese People's Liberation Army, successively presented flags, flowers, and gifts to Commander-in-Chief Zhu De. Shu Sheyu, a representative of the people of Beijing, Hoang Quoc Viet, head of the delegation of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam visiting China, and a representative of the workers of the Sixth Automobile Assembly Plant of the Chinese People's Liberation Army, all read out enthusiastic eulogies. The entire venue erupted in prolonged applause during the presentation of flags, flowers, and gifts, and the reading of eulogies by the representatives. After the celebration, the Beijing People's Art Theatre performed the opera "The Long March."

### 人民解放军建军二十四周年  首都隆重举行庆祝大会

1951-08-01
第2版()
专栏：

　　人民解放军建军二十四周年
    首都隆重举行庆祝大会
    【新华社三十一日讯】中国人民解放军建军二十四周年纪念大会今日下午七时半举行。出席大会的有：中央人民政府副主席、人民革命军事委员会副主席、中国人民解放军总司令朱德，中央人民政府副主席、人民革命军事委员会副主席刘少奇，人民革命军事委员会副主席、政务院总理周恩来，中央人民政府副主席李济深、张澜，中国人民政协全国委员会副主席郭沫若、陈叔通、沈钧儒，人民革命军事委员会代总参谋长聂荣臻，人民革命军事委员会总政治部副主任萧华，各民主党派负责人，中国人民政协全国委员会常务委员，中央人民政府委员会委员，中央人民政府各院、委、会、部、署、行首长，中央人民政府人民革命军事委员会各部首长，中国人民解放军陆海空军高级军官，中国人民解放军战斗英雄代表、中国人民志愿军归国代表，共八百余人。越南民主共和国访华代表团全体代表亦应邀出席。
    大会在军乐队高奏中华人民共和国国歌与中国人民解放军进行曲声中开始。中国人民解放军朱德总司令首先讲话（全文见本报今日第一版）。
    继由中国人民政治协商会议全国委员会郭沫若副主席讲话。郭沫若指出：中国人民革命的伟大胜利是与毛主席和中国共产党所领导的中国人民解放军的艰苦奋斗、英勇作战分不开的。郭沫若号召：为了巩固我国人民的胜利，为了保卫我们祖国的独立与安全，我国人民一定要继续加强人民解放军，继续加强增产捐献运动、青年参军工作和优抚工作。
    讲话完毕后，北京市各界人民代表、北京市少年儿童队代表、中央人民政府人民革命军事委员会直属机关保育院、东北区暨西北区少年儿童队夏季首都旅行团代表、越南民主共和国访华代表团、中国人民解放军第六汽车制配厂职工代表，先后向朱德总司令献旗、献花和献礼。北京市人民代表舒舍予、越南民主共和国访华代表团团长黄国越、中国人民解放军第六汽车制配厂职工代表，都宣读了热烈的颂词。献旗、献花、献礼和各代表宣读颂词时，全场掌声经久不息。庆祝大会结束后，由北京人民艺术剧院演出歌剧“长征”。
