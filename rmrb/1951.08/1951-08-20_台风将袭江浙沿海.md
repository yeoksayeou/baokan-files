Title: Typhoon to Strike Coastal Jiangsu and Zhejiang
Author:
Date: 1951-08-20
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, 19th: The Central Meteorological Observatory issued a typhoon warning at 7:00 AM today: At 2:00 AM on the 19th, the typhoon was located 150 nautical miles north of Okinawa (i.e., 28 degrees north latitude, 128.5 degrees east longitude), moving in a northwest-west direction at a speed of eight nautical miles per hour. The typhoon center's intensity is 940 millibars. In the next 24 hours, it will strike the coasts of Jiangsu and Zhejiang in our country. After the next 28 hours, it will strike the Yellow Sea and South Korea.

### 台风将袭江浙沿海

1951-08-20
第1版()
专栏：

　　台风将袭江浙沿海
    【新华社十九日讯】今日上午七时，中央气象台发出台风警报：十九日晨二时，台风位置在冲绳岛北一五○海里（即北纬二八度，东经一二八点五度）向西北偏西方向进行，每小时速度八海里。台风中心强度为九四○毫。未来二十四小时内，将袭击我国江苏、浙江海岸。未来二十八小时以后，将袭击黄海和南朝鲜。
