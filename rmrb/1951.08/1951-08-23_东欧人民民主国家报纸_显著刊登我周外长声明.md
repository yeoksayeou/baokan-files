Title: East European People's Democratic Countries' Newspapers Prominently Publish Our Foreign Minister Zhou's Statement
Author:
Date: 1951-08-23
Page: 4

　　Xinhua News Agency, Prague, 22nd. Major newspapers in the capitals of the European People's Democratic countries have prominently published the statement of Chinese Foreign Minister Zhou Enlai regarding the US-UK draft peace treaty with Japan.

　　The Czech newspaper "Red Right" on the 16th, the Hungarian "Free People's Daily" on the same day, the Romanian "Spark" on the 17th, and the Polish "People's Tribune" on the 18th all published the full text of the statement.

### 东欧人民民主国家报纸  显著刊登我周外长声明

1951-08-23
第4版()
专栏：

　　东欧人民民主国家报纸
    显著刊登我周外长声明
    【新华社布拉格二十二日电】欧洲各人民民主国家首都的主要报纸，都以显著地位登载中国外交部长周恩来关于美英对日和约草案的声明。
    十六日的捷克《红色权利报》、同日的匈牙利《自由人民报》、十七日的罗马尼亚《火花报》、十八日的波兰《人民论坛报》，都登载了声明全文。
