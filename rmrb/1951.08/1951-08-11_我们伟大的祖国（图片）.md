    Our Great Motherland
    Dongting Lake is a famous fish-producing area in our country. After liberation, the fishermen on the lake established fishermen's associations, fishery cooperatives, and production mutual aid groups, which greatly improved their production enthusiasm. This is the fisherman Qin Baoqing catching a large carp weighing twenty-seven catties with a fishing hook.

### 我们伟大的祖国（图片）

1951-08-11
第1版()
专栏：

    我们伟大的祖国
    洞庭湖是我国著名的产鱼区。湖边的渔民在解放后成立了渔民会，建立了渔业合作社和生产互助小组，大大地提高了他们的生产情绪。这是渔民秦保清用钓钩捉到了一条重达二十七斤的大鲤鱼。
