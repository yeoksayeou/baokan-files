Title: Swiss Minister to China Holds National Day Reception Yesterday
Author:
Date: 1951-08-02
Page: 4
　　
　　Xinhua News Agency, August 1st. The Swiss Confederation's National Day is August 1st. The Swiss Minister to China, Mr. Renzo Lardelli, held a reception this evening to celebrate.
　　Premier of the State Council and Minister of Foreign Affairs of the Central People's Government, Zhou Enlai, was invited to attend. Also invited were Vice Minister of Foreign Affairs Zhang Hanfu, Minister of Education Ma Xulun, Minister of Culture Shen Yanbing, Minister of Justice Shi Liang, Director of the General Administration of Customs Kong Yuan, Vice Minister of Trade Lei Renmin, Vice Mayor of Beijing Zhang Youyu, and over a hundred heads of various departments.
　　Diplomatic envoys to China from the Soviet Union, Czechoslovakia, Romania, India, Poland, Sweden, Hungary, Burma, Bulgaria, Indonesia, Pakistan and other countries were also invited to attend.
　　The reception was held in a friendly atmosphere.

### 瑞士驻华公使昨举行国庆招待会

1951-08-02
第4版()
专栏：

　　瑞士驻华公使
    昨举行国庆招待会
    【新华社一日讯】八月一日是瑞士联邦国庆日。瑞士驻华公使任佐立特于今晚举行招待会志庆。
    中央人民政府政务院总理兼外交部长周恩来应邀出席。此外应邀出席者，尚有外交部章汉夫副部长、教育部马叙伦部长、文化部沈雁冰部长、司法部史良部长、海关总署孔原署长、贸易部雷任民副部长、北京市张友渔副市长及各机关首长等百余人。
    苏联、捷克斯洛伐克、罗马尼亚、印度、波兰、瑞典、匈牙利、缅甸、保加利亚、印度尼西亚、巴基斯坦等国驻华外交使节亦应邀出席。
    招待会在友好气氛中进行。
