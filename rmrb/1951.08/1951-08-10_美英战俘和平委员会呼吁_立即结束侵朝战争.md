Title: US-British POW Peace Committee Calls for Immediate End to War of Aggression Against Korea
Author:
Date: 1951-08-10
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Pyongyang, 9th - The US-British POW Peace Committee, established on June 30th, has issued a declaration to the Chairman of the World Peace Council, the President of the United Nations General Assembly, and the President of the United Nations Security Council. The declaration states: We express our anger at the despicable policies implemented by our national ruling groups against the freedom-loving people of Korea, and hope for an immediate peaceful resolution to the Korean issue.

The declaration opposes continuing to prolong and expand this war, and hopes to end it as soon as possible.

The declaration believes that the Korean War was launched by the Syngman Rhee clique under the direction of US imperialism.

The declaration accuses the US military of inhumane bombing of Korean towns and villages, resulting in the tragic death of over one million innocent people. In addition, soldiers of the United Nations forces and the Syngman Rhee puppet army have also suffered huge casualties.

The declaration emphatically states: We want to expose this situation to the people of the world and urge them to work to stop this barbaric and futile war of aggression being waged by the United States in Korea.

The declaration is signed by Eugene, Chairman of the US-British POW Peace Committee, Cox, Vice Chairman, Carter, Secretary, and six committee members.

### 美英战俘和平委员会呼吁  立即结束侵朝战争

1951-08-10
第4版()
专栏：

　　美英战俘和平委员会呼吁
    立即结束侵朝战争
    【新华社平壤九日电】六月三十日成立的美英战俘和平委员会，向世界和平理事会主席、联合国大会主席及联合国安全理事会主席发出宣言。宣言内称：我们对于我们国家的统治集团对爱好自由的朝鲜人民执行的卑鄙政策表示愤怒，希望立即和平解决朝鲜问题。
    宣言反对继续拖延与扩大这个战争，希望尽早结束这个战争。
    宣言认为朝鲜战争是李承晚集团在美帝国主义指使下发动的。
    宣言指责朝鲜的城镇和乡村遭受美军惨无人道的轰炸，结果有一百万以上的无辜人民惨遭杀害。此外，联合国军队和李承晚傀儡军队的士兵也遭受巨大的伤亡。
    宣言力称：我们要向全世界人民揭露这种情形，促使他们努力制止美国在朝鲜进行的这场野蛮而无益的侵略战争。
    宣言由美英战俘和平委员会主席牛金特、副主席柯克斯、秘书卡特尔及委员六人署名。
