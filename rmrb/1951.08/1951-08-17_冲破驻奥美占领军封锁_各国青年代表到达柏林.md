Title: Breaking Through the Blockade of the American Occupation Forces in Austria, Youth Representatives from Various Countries Arrive in Berlin
Author:
Date: 1951-08-17
Page: 4

　　Xinhua News Agency, Berlin, August 14th: The last group of one thousand youth representatives from Britain, France, Italy, and the United States, who were detained in Innsbruck by the American occupation forces in Austria, have broken through the blockade. The first group has already arrived in Berlin, and the other representatives will arrive on the 14th. The International Committee of the World Youth and Student Festival for Peace announced this news on the 13th, and youth from various countries responded with prolonged and enthusiastic cheers. The International Committee of the Festival sent a telegram to the youth of Austria, expressing heartfelt thanks for their support to the youth representatives from Britain, France, and other countries. The Free Austrian Youth League also issued a statement thanking the Austrian people for their effective assistance to the youth representatives from various countries, enabling them to pass through Vienna to Berlin, thereby causing the attempt of American imperialism to obstruct the youth representatives from various countries from participating in the Peace Festival to fail.

### 冲破驻奥美占领军封锁  各国青年代表到达柏林

1951-08-17
第4版()
专栏：

　　冲破驻奥美占领军封锁  各国青年代表到达柏林
    【新华社十五日讯】塔斯社柏林十四日讯：被奥地利美国占领军扣留在音斯布鲁克的最后一批英国、法国、意大利及美国的青年代表一千人，已冲破封锁，其中第一批已经到达柏林，其他代表也将在十四日到达。世界青年与学生和平联欢节国际委员会在十三日宣布了这个消息，各国青年报以长久的热烈欢呼。联欢节国际委员会特致电奥地利青年，对他们给英、法等国青年代表的支援表示衷心的感谢。自由奥地利青年联盟也发表声明，感谢奥地利人民给予各国青年代表的有效帮助，使他们能经过维也纳到柏林去，从而使美帝国主义阻挠各国青年代表参加和平联欢节的企图归于失败。
