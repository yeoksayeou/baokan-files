Title: During the Korean Armistice Negotiations, US Aircraft Continued to Invade Our Airspace 39 Times in Half a Month
Author:
Date: 1951-08-08
Page: 1

　　During the Korean Armistice Negotiations
    US Aircraft Continued to Invade Our Airspace
    39 Times in Half a Month
    [Xinhua News Agency, 7th dispatch] During the Korean armistice negotiations, US aircraft invading Korea continued to invade our airspace. According to statistics, from July 17th to August 3rd, US aircraft invaded our airspace a total of 39 times, with 80 aircraft involved. The invaded areas included the airspace over Antung City and the counties of Ji'an and Kuandian on the Sino-Korean border, and extended deep into the counties of Fengcheng, Huanren, and Linjiang, as well as the northwestern suburbs of Benxi City, 25 kilometers south of Shenyang City, and 20 kilometers south of Fushun City.
    On July 21st alone, 20 US aircraft invaded our airspace. Among them, eight US "F"-94 jet fighters flew from Kuandian towards Shenyang and then turned towards Liaoyang and Fengcheng, seven of which were shot down by our air force.

### 朝鲜停战谈判期间  美机继续侵入我领空  半个月内达三十九次

1951-08-08
第1版()
专栏：

　　朝鲜停战谈判期间
    美机继续侵入我领空
    半个月内达三十九次
    【新华社七日讯】侵略朝鲜的美国飞机，在朝鲜停战谈判期间，继续不断侵入我国领空。据统计：从上月十七日到本月三日止，美国飞机侵入我国领空共达三十九次，侵入的飞机达八十架，侵入地区除中朝边境安东市及辑安、宽甸等县境上空外，复深入至凤城、桓仁、临江等县及本溪市西北郊区、沈阳市南二十五公里处、抚顺市南二十公里处上空。
    在上月二十一日一天中，侵入我国领空的美国飞机达二十架。其中有八架美国“Ｆ”九四式喷气战斗机，经宽甸飞向沈阳后又折向辽阳、凤城方向飞去，被我空军击落七架。
