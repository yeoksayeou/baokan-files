Title: People in Various Regions of the Korean Rear Continue to Donate to Support the Front Lines
Author:
Date: 1951-08-27
Page: 4

　　Xinhua News Agency, Pyongyang, August 25th: People in the rear areas of Korea are continuing to launch a movement to donate to a weapons fund to support the front lines. According to the Central Committee of the Korean Fatherland Defense Support Association: In the one hundred days from early May to August 10th, people from all walks of life across the country have donated more than 1,628,161,600 won (Korean currency) in cash and more than 55,000 pieces of various valuables. Among them, the people of North Pyongan Province alone donated more than 414 million won, the people of South Pyongan Province donated more than 274 million won, and the people of Hwanghae Province donated more than 14 million catties of grain.

### 朝鲜后方各地人民继续捐献支援前线

1951-08-27
第4版()
专栏：

　　朝鲜后方各地人民继续捐献支援前线
    【新华社平壤二十五日电】朝鲜后方人民正继续展开捐献武器基金的运动以支援前线。据朝鲜祖国保卫后援会中央委员会发表：从五月初到八月十日的一百天中，朝鲜全国各地各界人民已捐献了现金十六亿二千八百十六万一千六百余元（朝币），各种贵重物品五万五千余件，其中仅平安北道人民即捐献了四亿一千四百余万元，平安南道人民捐献了二亿七千四百余万元，黄海道人民捐献了粮食一千四百万斤以上。
