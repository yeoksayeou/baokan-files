Title: Delegations from Various Countries Participating in the Youth Festival Hold Various Artistic Performances; Performance by Chinese Youth Cultural Troupe Achieves Great Success
Author:
Date: 1951-08-12
Page: 4
(Xinhua News Agency, Berlin, August 10th) According to TASS, the World Festival of Youth and Students for Peace is proceeding enthusiastically. On the 7th and 8th, many art performances, sports competitions, and collective tours continued to be held in Berlin. An exhibition introducing the lives and struggles of young people from various countries has also opened.

　　On the evening of the 6th, Soviet youth artists held their first concert. The concert hall, which could only accommodate three thousand people, was packed with four thousand spectators. Wilhelm Pieck, President of the German Democratic Republic, Giuseppe Casadio, President of the World Federation of Democratic Youth, Jiří Pelikán, President of the International Union of Students, and heads of youth delegations from various countries attended the concert. Every program performed by the Soviet youth artists was greeted with stormy applause. The performances by the Leningrad Youth Workers' Choir and the Ural Folk Song Choir were particularly successful.

　　The first public performance by the Chinese Youth Cultural Troupe was a great success, and the Chinese dance programs left a deep impression on the audience. Youth artists from Korea, Poland, Czechoslovakia, Hungary, Bulgaria, Mongolia, Albania, and France performed in various theaters and open-air stages on the 7th. German youth drama groups and choirs, as well as Young Pioneer song and dance troupes, performed in various squares in Berlin until late at night every evening.

　　The opening of the exhibition "Soviet Youth in the Struggle for Peace" was one of the major events of the 7th. Countless exhibits showed the peaceful labor, study, and achievements of Soviet youth. The exhibition of the People's Republic of China aroused great interest among the festival delegates and the citizens of Berlin. The exhibits here illustrated China's long cultural and artistic history and introduced the life of the free New China. In addition, the exhibitions of the World Federation of Democratic Youth and the International Union of Students, as well as the exhibitions of Korea, Vietnam, Poland, and Czechoslovakia, also opened on the 7th.

　　Youth representatives from various countries continue to arrive in Berlin. On the 6th, 334 representatives from Switzerland, more than 100 representatives each from Italy and France, and representatives from Colombia, Egypt, and Iran arrived in Berlin. On the 7th, more than 100 of the 2,000 representatives from various countries detained by the American occupying forces in Austria broke through the obstruction and arrived in Berlin. Raymonde Dien, a young French female peace fighter, also arrived in Berlin on this day. Their arrival brought new inspiration to the youth representatives of various countries.

### 参加青年联欢节各国代表团  举行各种艺术表演  我青年文工团演出获巨大成功

1951-08-12
第4版()
专栏：

　　参加青年联欢节各国代表团
    举行各种艺术表演
    我青年文工团演出获巨大成功
    【新华社十日讯】据塔斯社柏林讯：世界青年与学生和平联欢节正在热烈进行中。在七日和八日两天，柏林继续举行了许多次艺术表演、运动比赛和集体游览。介绍各国青年的生活与斗争的展览会也已开幕。
    在六日晚间，苏联青年艺术家举行了首次音乐会。只能容纳三千人的音乐厅挤满了四千观众。德意志民主共和国总统皮克、世界民主青年联盟主席比林格尔、国际学联主席格罗曼、各国青年代表团团长都参加了音乐会。苏联青年艺术家的每一个节目都博得了暴风雨般的喝采声。列宁格勒青年工人合唱团和乌拉尔民歌合唱团的表演特别成功。
    中国青年文工团的第一次公演获得巨大成功，中国的舞蹈节目在观众中留下了深刻的印象。朝鲜、波兰、捷克斯洛伐克、匈牙利、保加利亚、蒙古、阿尔巴尼亚和法国的青年艺术家，七日分别在各剧院和露天舞台表演。德国青年的戏剧小组及合唱团，以及少年先锋队歌舞团，每晚都在柏林的各广场上表演到深夜。
    “在争取和平斗争中的苏联青年”展览会的开幕，是七日的重大事件之一。无数展览品表现出苏联青年的和平劳动、学习和成就。中华人民共和国展览会引起联欢节代表和柏林市民的巨大兴趣。这里的展览品说明了中国悠久文化和艺术历史，并介绍了自由的新中国的生活。此外，世界民主青联和国际学联的展览会，以及朝鲜、越南、波兰和捷克斯洛伐克的展览会也都在七日开幕。
    来自各国的青年代表正继续到达柏林。在六日，瑞士代表三百三十四人、意大利和法国代表各一百多人，以及来自哥伦比亚、埃及和伊朗的代表到达了柏林。在七日，被奥境美国占领军拘留的各国代表二千人中，有一百多人冲破阻挠到达了柏林，法国女青年和平战士雷蒙达·第安也在这一天到了柏林。他们的到达，给各国青年代表带来了新的鼓舞。
