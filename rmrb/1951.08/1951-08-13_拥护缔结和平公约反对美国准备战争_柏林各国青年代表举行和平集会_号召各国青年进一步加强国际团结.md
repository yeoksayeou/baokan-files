Title: Support the Conclusion of a Peace Pact, Oppose American War Preparations: Berlin Youth Representatives from Various Countries Hold Peace Rally, Calling on Youth from All Countries to Further Strengthen International Solidarity
Author:
Date: 1951-08-13
Page: 4
　　Xinhua News Agency, Berlin, 12th: Youth representatives from various countries participating in the World Youth and Student Festival have continuously held various peace rallies and gatherings to exchange experiences in the peace struggle of youth in various countries and to strengthen their solidarity in the peace struggle. According to TASS Berlin on the 11th: Youth representatives from ninety countries held a meeting on the 10th in the People's Chamber of the German Democratic Republic. Those attending the meeting were all youths who had made outstanding achievements in the signature campaign for the Peace Pact Declaration. Wilhelm Pieck, President of the German Democratic Republic, was invited to the meeting and received a warm welcome. Giuseppe Pierazzini, President of the World Federation of Democratic Youth, delivered the opening speech, emphasizing the great significance of the signature campaign for the Peace Pact Declaration. Pierazzini warmly supported the peace proposals put forward in the reply from Nikolai Shvernik, Chairman of the Presidium of the Supreme Soviet of the Soviet Union, to US President Truman. He pointed out that this step taken by the Soviet Union fully demonstrated its desire for peace. Subsequently, representatives from various countries exchanged experiences in collecting signatures and unanimously passed a telegram. The telegram pointed out: The conclusion of a Peace Pact by the five major powers is a decisive way to eliminate the existing international tension, and it will guarantee lasting peace and a happy future for the people of the world. The telegram also assured that young peace fighters from all countries will continue to collect more signatures for the Peace Pact Declaration.
　　Youth metalworkers from various countries participating in the festival held a meeting at the Bergmann-Borsig factory in Berlin on the same day. After listening to speeches from representatives from various countries, the meeting unanimously adopted a letter to the youth of the world, calling on youth from all countries to further strengthen international solidarity and fight for peace and against new war preparations. Prior to this, young miners and young transport workers participating in the festival held meetings on the 8th respectively. The young miners called on the vast number of young miners in various countries not to produce a ton of coal or a ton of ore for the imperialists' military purposes. The young transport workers pledged to do everything possible to stop American military transport and oppose the new world war being prepared by the American imperialists.
　　The festival fully demonstrated the respect and love of progressive youth from various countries for the Soviet Union. Delegations from various countries expressed their hope to meet with the Soviet delegation to convey the respect of progressive youth from their countries. The Soviet youth delegation held a grand meeting on the 10th to entertain the Iranian youth delegation. Iranian representatives Noursi and Jalali, in their speeches at the meeting, both emphasized that the Soviet Union has always encouraged Iranian youth to fight for the happiness of their motherland. Iranian youth are watching the Soviet youth's struggle to defend peace. That evening, Soviet youth held a gathering with Canadian youth in an atmosphere of close friendship.
　　On the 10th, representatives from various countries attending the festival attended a reception hosted by President Pieck and Prime Minister Grotewohl.
　　Various art performances of the festival are continuing in major squares in Berlin.

### 拥护缔结和平公约反对美国准备战争  柏林各国青年代表举行和平集会  号召各国青年进一步加强国际团结

1951-08-13
第4版()
专栏：

　　拥护缔结和平公约反对美国准备战争
    柏林各国青年代表举行和平集会
    号召各国青年进一步加强国际团结
    【新华社十二日讯】参加世界青年与学生联欢节的各国青年代表连续举行了各种和平集会和联欢会，以交流各国青年保卫和平斗争的经验，并加强他们在和平斗争中的团结。据塔斯社柏林十一日讯：九十个国家的青年代表于十日在德意志民主共和国人民议会大厦举行了会议。出席会议的都是在和平公约宣言签名运动中有卓越成绩的青年。德意志民主共和国总统皮克应邀到会，受到了热烈的欢迎。世界民主青年联盟主席比林格尔致开幕词时，着重指出和平公约宣言签名运动的伟大意义。比林格尔热烈拥护苏联最高苏维埃主席团主席什维尔尼克致美国总统杜鲁门的复信中提出的和平建议。他指出：苏联所采取的这一步骤充分表明了它对和平的愿望。接着，各国代表交换了征集签名的经验，并一致通过了一项通电。电文指出：五大国缔结和平公约是消除现存的国际紧张局势的决定性的办法，它将保证持久和平和全世界人民幸福的将来。电文并保证各国青年和平战士将继续为和平公约宣言征集更多的签名。
    参加联欢节的各国青年五金工人于同日在柏林的拜尔格曼·包尔齐格工厂举行了会议。会议在听取各国代表的发言后，一致通过了告全世界青年书，号召各国青年进一步加强国际团结，为争取和平、反对新战争准备而斗争。在这以前，参加联欢节的青年矿工和青年运输工人在八日分别举行了会议。青年矿工们号召各国的广大的青年矿工不为帝国主义的军事目的生产一吨煤和一吨矿砂。青年运输工人们保证要尽一切力量阻止美国的军事运输，反对美帝国主义者准备的新世界大战。
    联欢节充分显示了各国进步青年对苏联的敬仰与热爱，各国代表团纷纷表示希望会晤苏联代表团，向他们转达本国进步青年的敬意。苏联青年代表团在十日举行了盛会，招待伊朗青年代表团。伊朗代表诺尔西和贾拉利在会上发言时，都强调指出苏联一直鼓舞伊朗青年为祖国的幸福而奋斗。伊朗青年都注视着苏联青年保卫和平的斗争。当晚，苏联青年在亲密友好的气氛中与加拿大青年举行了联欢。
    出席联欢节的各国代表们在十日出席了皮克总统和格罗提渥总理主持的招待会。
    联欢节的各项艺术表演正继续在柏林各大广场上进行着。
