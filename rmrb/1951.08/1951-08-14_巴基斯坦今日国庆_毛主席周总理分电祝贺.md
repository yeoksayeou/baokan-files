Title: Pakistan Celebrates National Day Today: Chairman Mao and Premier Zhou Send Congratulatory Telegrams
Author:
Date: 1951-08-14
Page: 1

　　Pakistan Celebrates National Day Today
    Chairman Mao and Premier Zhou Send Congratulatory Telegrams
    [Xinhua News Agency, August 14th] August 14th is Pakistan's National Day. Chairman Mao Zedong of the Central People's Government of China and Premier Zhou Enlai of the Government Administration Council have sent telegrams to Pakistan's Governor-General Nazimuddin and Prime Minister Ali Khan respectively to offer congratulations. The texts of the two telegrams are as follows:
    (1) Chairman Mao's Congratulatory Telegram: To His Excellency Nazimuddin, Governor-General of Pakistan:
    On the occasion of your country's National Day, I, on behalf of the government and people of the People's Republic of China, extend my congratulations to Your Excellency and the people of Pakistan, and wish the friendship between the Chinese and Pakistani peoples grow stronger day by day.
    Mao Zedong, Chairman of the Central People's Government of the People's Republic of China, August 14, 1951, in Beijing
    (2) Premier Zhou's Congratulatory Telegram: To His Excellency Ali Khan, Prime Minister of Pakistan:
    On the joyous occasion of your country's National Day, I hereby extend my congratulations and wish the people of Pakistan happiness and progress.
    Zhou Enlai, Premier of the Government Administration Council of the People's Republic of China, August 14, 1951,
                                                in Beijing

### 巴基斯坦今日国庆  毛主席周总理分电祝贺

1951-08-14
第1版()
专栏：

　　巴基斯坦今日国庆
    毛主席周总理分电祝贺
    【新华社十四日讯】八月十四日为巴基斯坦国庆日。我中央人民政府毛泽东主席和政务院周恩来总理分别致电巴基斯坦总督纳齐穆丁和总理阿里汗祝贺。两电电文如下：
    （一）毛主席贺电：巴基斯坦总督纳齐穆丁先生：
    值此贵国国庆纪念之际，我代表中华人民共和国政府及人民谨向阁下及巴基斯坦人民致庆贺之忱，并祝中巴两国人民的友谊与日俱增。
    中华人民共和国中央人民政府主席毛泽东  一九五一年八月十四日于北京
    （二）周总理贺电：巴基斯坦总理阿里汗先生：
    欣逢贵国国庆，特此庆贺，并祝巴基斯坦人民幸福和进步。
    中华人民共和国中央人民政府政务院总理周恩来  一九五一年八月十四日
                                                于北京
