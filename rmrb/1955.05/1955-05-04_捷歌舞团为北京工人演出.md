Title: Czechoslovak Song and Dance Ensemble Performs for Beijing Workers
Author:
Date: 1955-05-04
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, May 2nd. The Czechoslovak State Song and Dance Ensemble performed a brilliant program for Beijing workers, who are celebrating the "May Day" International Labor Day holiday, at the open-air theater of the Soviet Exhibition Center in Beijing on the afternoon of the 2nd.
　　More than 3,000 people watched the performance of the song and dance ensemble, and many of them were activists in amateur cultural activities of workers.
　　The program performed by the song and dance ensemble was warmly welcomed by the audience.

### 捷歌舞团为北京工人演出

1955-05-04
第1版()
专栏：

　　捷歌舞团为北京工人演出
    新华社二日讯　捷克斯洛伐克国家歌舞团二日下午在北京苏联展览馆露天剧场为正在欢度“五一”国际劳动节假日的北京工人演出了精采节目。
    观看歌舞团演出的有三千多人，他们当中有不少是工人业余文艺活动的积极分子。
    歌舞团演出的节目，受到观众的热烈欢迎。
