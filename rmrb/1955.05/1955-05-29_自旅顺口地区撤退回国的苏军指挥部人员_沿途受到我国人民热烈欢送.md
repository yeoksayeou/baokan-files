Title: Soviet Army Headquarters Personnel Withdraw From Lushunkou Area and Return Home, Warmly Welcomed by the Chinese People Along the Way
Author:
Date: 1955-05-29
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, May 28th: Soviet Army headquarters personnel withdrawing from the Lushunkou area and returning home were warmly welcomed by the local people when they passed through Shenyang, Changchun, Harbin and other places.

　　When the Soviet Army headquarters personnel passed through Shenyang on the morning of the 27th, more than 2,500 people welcomed them. Among those who came to Shenyang Railway Station to see them off were Wang Zheng, Second Secretary of the Liaoning Provincial Committee of the Communist Party of China, Li Huang, Deputy Secretary, Du Zheheng, Governor of Liaoning Province, Li Tao, Deputy Director of the Preparatory Committee of the Liaoning Provincial Sino-Soviet Friendship Association, Jiao Ruoyu, Secretary of the Shenyang Municipal Committee of the Communist Party of China, Liu Baotian, Mayor of Shenyang, Gan Weihan, Deputy Political Commissar of the Shenyang Military Region of the Chinese People's Liberation Army, Tang Zian, Deputy Chief of Staff, and representatives from all walks of life. Soviet Consul General in Shenyang, Wei Lixiajin, Deputy Consuls Vasiliev, Vasilenko, Basmanov, and Jiang Xizhen, Director of the Shenyang Liaison Office of the Korean Embassy in China, also came to the station to see them off. When the train entered the station, people greeted them with enthusiastic cheers and applause. Young Pioneers presented bouquets and red scarves to the personnel of the Soviet Army headquarters. Afterwards, the welcomers held a grand farewell ceremony on the platform. Liu Baotian, Mayor of Shenyang, and Song Fenglin, a model worker in Shenyang, delivered farewell speeches successively. Then General Shvetsov of the Soviet Army headquarters delivered a farewell speech. The welcomers presented souvenirs, commemorative albums, and photos to the Soviet generals and officers as gifts.

　　At 1:00 PM on the 27th, when the train carrying the personnel of the Soviet Army headquarters passed through Changchun, Wu De, Secretary of the Jilin Provincial Committee of the Communist Party of China, Li Youwen, Governor of Jilin Province, He Jian, Commander of the Jilin Military Region of the Chinese People's Liberation Army, Fu Zhensheng, President of the Jilin Provincial Sino-Soviet Friendship Association, and more than 1,200 representatives from all walks of life in Jilin Province and Changchun went to welcome them. Li Youwen, Governor of Jilin Province, delivered a farewell speech at the station. General Shvetsov delivered a farewell speech. Then, the welcomers presented flowers to the senior Soviet generals and headquarters personnel, and gave exquisite children's toys to the family members and children of the Soviet Army. General Shvetsov and others walked into the crowd of welcomers to shake hands and say goodbye.

　　When the train passed through Harbin on the evening of the 27th, Ouyang Qin, Secretary of the Heilongjiang Provincial Committee of the Communist Party of China, Han Guang, Governor of Heilongjiang Province, Lin Xiaoxia, Deputy Secretary of the Harbin Municipal Committee of the Communist Party of China, Lü Qien, Mayor of Harbin, and more than 1,000 people from all walks of life welcomed the personnel of the Soviet Army headquarters at the station. Soviet Consul General in Harbin, Baranov, also came to the station to see them off. At the farewell meeting held at the station, Han Guang, Governor of Heilongjiang Province, and General Shvetsov of the Soviet Army headquarters delivered welcome and farewell speeches respectively. The Soviet Army headquarters personnel stayed in Harbin for several hours and toured the city. Some officers and soldiers and the families of officers held a get-together with students from universities and colleges in Harbin. Before leaving, the Heilongjiang Provincial Committee of the Communist Party of China and the Heilongjiang Provincial People's Committee held a reception to bid farewell to the senior generals and officers of the Soviet Army headquarters.

### 自旅顺口地区撤退回国的苏军指挥部人员  沿途受到我国人民热烈欢送

1955-05-29
第4版()
专栏：

　　自旅顺口地区撤退回国的苏军指挥部人员
　  沿途受到我国人民热烈欢送
    新华社二十八日讯　自旅顺口地区撤退回国的苏军指挥部人员，路过沈阳、长春、哈尔滨等地时，受到当地人民的热烈欢送。
    当苏军指挥部人员在二十七日上午路过沈阳时，有二千五百多人欢送他们。到沈阳车站欢送的有中共辽宁省委员会第二书记王铮、副书记李荒，辽宁省省长杜者蘅，辽宁省中苏友好协会筹备委员会副主任李涛，中共沈阳市委员会书记焦若愚，沈阳市市长刘宝田，中国人民解放军沈阳军区副政治委员甘渭汉，副参谋长唐子安，以及各界人民代表。苏联驻沈阳总领事魏立夏金，副领事华西列夫、瓦西连柯、巴斯曼诺夫和朝鲜驻华大使馆沈阳联络所所长姜锡贞也到车站欢送。当列车进站时，人们以热烈的欢呼和鼓掌迎接了他们。少年先锋队队员们把花束和红领巾献给苏军指挥部人员。接着，欢送的人们在站台上举行了隆重的欢送仪式。沈阳市市长刘宝田和沈阳市劳动模范宋凤林先后致欢送词。接着苏军指挥部什维佐夫将军致告别词。欢送者向苏军将领和军官们赠送了纪念章、纪念册和像片等礼物。
    二十七日午后一时，载着苏军指挥部人员的列车路过长春时，中共吉林省委员会书记吴德、吉林省省长栗又文、中国人民解放军吉林军区司令员贺健、吉林省中苏友好协会会长富振声，以及吉林省和长春市各界人民代表一千二百多人前往欢送。吉林省省长栗又文在车站致欢送词。什维佐夫将军致告别词。接着，欢送的人们向苏军高级将领和指挥部人员献花，给苏军家属儿童们送了精巧的儿童玩具。什维佐夫将军等走向欢送的人群中与欢送者一一握手告别。
    当列车在二十七日晚上经过哈尔滨时，在车站上欢送苏军指挥部人员的有中共黑龙江省委员会书记欧阳钦、黑龙江省省长韩光、中共哈尔滨市委员会副书记林肖硖、哈尔滨市市长吕其恩及各界人民一千多人。苏联驻哈尔滨总领事巴拉诺夫也到车站欢送。在车站上举行的欢送会上，黑龙江省省长韩光和苏军指挥部什维佐夫将军分别致欢送词和告别词。苏军指挥部人员在哈尔滨逗留了几个小时，游览了市容，一部分军官和士兵及军官的家属同哈尔滨市大专学校学生举行了联欢活动。临别前，中共黑龙江省委员会和黑龙江省人民委员会举行酒会欢送苏军指挥部高级将领和军官们。
