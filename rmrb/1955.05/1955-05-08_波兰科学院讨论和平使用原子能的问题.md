Title: Polish Academy of Sciences Discusses Peaceful Use of Atomic Energy
Author:
Date: 1955-05-08
Page: 4

　　Xinhua News Agency, Warsaw, May 7th: According to TASS, the Presidium of the Polish Academy of Sciences held a meeting in Warsaw to discuss research work on the peaceful use of atomic energy.

　　The meeting heard reports from representatives of the Polish delegation about their visit to the Soviet Union, which outlined the plans and scope of Soviet assistance to Polish scientists studying atomic energy.

　　Speakers at the meeting emphasized the great significance of the agreement on Soviet assistance to Poland in the peaceful use of atomic energy, according to which Poland will receive from the Soviet Union in 1956 an atomic pile with a heat output of 2,000 kilowatts, the latest type of cyclotron and auxiliary equipment, etc.

　　The meeting adopted a resolution stating that Polish scientists will do everything possible to devote themselves fully to the peaceful use of atomic energy.

### 波兰科学院讨论和平使用原子能的问题

1955-05-08
第4版()
专栏：

　　波兰科学院讨论和平使用原子能的问题
    新华社七日讯　据塔斯社华沙六日讯：波兰科学院主席团在华沙举行会议，讨论和平使用原子能方面的研究工作。
    会议听取了波兰代表团代表们关于访问苏联的报告，这些报告谈到代表团在访苏期间曾制定和确定了苏联对研究原子能的波兰科学家们的帮助的计划和范围。
    在会上发言的人都着重指出了关于苏联帮助波兰和平使用原子能的协定的巨大意义，根据协定，波兰在一九五六年将从苏联获得放热能为二千瓩的原子堆、最新式的回旋加速器和辅助器材等。
    会议通过了一项决议，表示波兰科学家将尽一切力量着手全面地利用原子能为和平目的服务。
