    Our Great Motherland
    In order to transform the natural environment of the windy and sandy areas in western Northeast China, the state will build a shelterbelt stretching over 1,000 kilometers and 300 kilometers wide from Heilongjiang Province to the Liaodong Peninsula within ten to fifteen years. This is the scene of the Kaitong Machine Afforestation Experimental Station in Jilin Province using machines for afforestation.

### 我们伟大的祖国（图片）

1955-05-14
第1版()
专栏：

    我们伟大的祖国
    为了改造东北西部多风沙地带的自然环境，国家将在十年到十五年内从黑龙江省到辽东半岛建造一条长达一千多公里，宽三百公里的防护林带。这是吉林省开通机器造林试验站正在用机器造林的情形。
