Title: Situ Meitang Passes Away
Author:
Date: 1955-05-09
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, 8th. Mr. Situ Meitang, member of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress of the People's Republic of China, member of the 2rd National Committee of the People's Political Consultative Conference of China, and member of the Overseas Chinese Affairs Committee of the People's Republic of China, passed away in Beijing at 9:10 PM on May 8, 1955, due to cerebral hemorrhage, despite medical treatment.

Mr. Situ Meitang was from Kaiping County, Guangdong Province, and was 89 years old.

Xinhua News Agency, 8th. The funeral committee for Mr. Situ Meitang was established on the afternoon of May 8th. The list is as follows: Lin Boqu, Li Jishen, Shen Junru, Guo Moruo, Huang Yanpei, Peng Zhen, Chen Shutong, Li Weihan, Xi Zhongxun, He Xiangning, Chen Jiageng, Cai Tingkai, Peng Zemin, Shao Lizi, Luo Longji, Zhang Naiqi, Xing Xiping, Liao Chengzhi, Zhang Su, Yu Yifu, Chen Qiyou, Chen Qiyuan, Jiang Guangnai, Tao Zhu, Zhu Guang, Yan Xichun, Huang Dingchen, Guan Wensen, Wu Juetian, Fang Fang, Li Tiemin, Zhuang Xiquan, Lian Guan, Wang Yuting, Tang Mingzhao, Situ Huimin, Situ Qiao.

### 司徒美堂逝世

1955-05-09
第1版()
专栏：

　　司徒美堂逝世
    新华社八日讯　中华人民共和国全国人民代表大会常务委员会委员、中国人民政治协商会议第二届全国委员会委员、中华人民共和国华侨事务委员会委员司徒美堂先生，患脑溢血症，经医生诊治无效，于一九五五年五月八日下午九时十分在北京逝世。
    司徒美堂先生，广东省开平县人，享年八十九岁。
