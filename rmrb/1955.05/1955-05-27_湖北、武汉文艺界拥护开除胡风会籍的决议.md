Title: Hubei and Wuhan Literary and Art Circles Support the Decision to Expel Hu Feng from Membership

Author:
Date: 1955-05-27
Page: 1

　　Hubei and Wuhan Literary and Art Circles Support the Decision to Expel Hu Feng from Membership
    This newspaper's Wuhan dispatch, May 26th: The Hubei Provincial Federation of Literary and Art Workers, the Wuhan Municipal Federation of Literary and Art Workers, and the Wuhan Branch of the Chinese Writers Association, telegraphed the Presidium of the China Federation of Literary and Art Circles and the Presidium of the Chinese Writers Association on May 26th, expressing their complete support for the resolution of the joint expanded meeting of the Presidium of the China Federation of Literary and Art Circles and the Presidium of the Chinese Writers Association on May 25th, expelling Hu Feng from membership, removing him from all positions in literary and art work, and requesting that, according to the Constitution, his qualifications as a deputy to the National People's Congress be revoked, and that he be severely punished according to the law. The telegram stated: "The struggle here is unfolding more broadly and deeply. We are determined to fight to the end to smash the bunker of the Hu Feng anti-Party clique."

### 湖北、武汉文艺界拥护开除胡风会籍的决议

1955-05-27
第1版()
专栏：

　　湖北、武汉文艺界拥护开除胡风会籍的决议
    本报武汉二十六日电　湖北省文学艺术工作者联合会、武汉市文学艺术工作者联合会、中国作家协会武汉分会，在五月二十六日打电报给中国文学艺术界联合会主席团和中国作家协会主席团，表示完全拥护五月二十五日中国文学艺术界联合会主席团和中国作家协会主席团联席扩大会议的决议，开除胡风会籍、撤销其在文艺工作中的一切职务，并要求根据宪法，撤销其全国人民代表大会代表的资格，依法严惩。电报中说：“此间斗争，正更广泛深入地展开，我们坚决为粉碎胡风反党集团的地堡而战斗到底。”
