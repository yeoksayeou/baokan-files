Title: Trade Union Delegations from Various Countries Arrive in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1955-05-01
Page: 4

　　Xinhua News Agency, 30th. At the invitation of the All-China Federation of Trade Unions and various industrial trade unions, the Indian trade union delegation and representatives, headed by Mao Moglai; the Burmese trade union delegation, headed by Cao Teli Ramatula; the Pakistani trade union delegation, headed by Cao Teli Ramatula; Hamada Sadasaku, the deputy head of the Japanese Workers' Delegation to China, and the second and third groups of representatives; the Tunisian trade union delegation, headed by Sadawi; four Indonesian trade union representatives; Four New Zealand trade union representatives, and one Iranian trade union representative arrived in Beijing by train and plane in batches on the 29th and 30th. When the trade union representatives from various countries arrived, they were warmly welcomed by the heads of the All-China Federation of Trade Unions and various industrial trade unions.
　　The Indian trade union delegation and trade union representatives are: the All-India Trade Union Congress delegation, headed by Basu; the Indian Labor Association delegation, headed by Baswan Sinha; the All-India United Trade Union Congress delegation, headed by Bhattacharya; the All-India Railwaymen's Federation delegation, headed by Chakravarti; the All-India Seafarers' Federation delegation, headed by Ya. S. Ali; the Indian Seafarers' Federation delegation, headed by Silang; the All-India Bank Employees Association delegation, headed by Kakar; the All-India Education Association delegation, headed by Shaharabod; Sunderam, a representative of the All-India Petroleum Workers' Union; Beranyap, a representative of the All-India Insurance Employees' Association; and Pinto, a representative of the All-India Port and Dock Workers' Federation.
　　"Polish Daily" Editor-in-Chief Krasicki Arrives in Beijing
　　Xinhua News Agency, 29th. According to the 1955 implementation plan of the Sino-Polish Cultural Cooperation Agreement, Fei Krasicki, editor-in-chief of the "Polish Daily" in Krakow Province, Poland, who was invited to China to attend the "May Day" celebrations and visit, arrived in Beijing by plane on the 29th.
　　Swiss Cultural Delegation Arrives in Beijing
　　Xinhua News Agency, 29th. At the invitation of the Chinese People's Association for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries, the Swiss cultural delegation headed by Dr. Kramer arrived in Beijing on April 29th. The person in charge of the Chinese People's Association for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries welcomed them at the airport.
　　Japanese Peace Advocate Ryosuke Miyazaki and Others Arrive in Beijing
　　According to Xinhua News Agency, Ryosuke Miyazaki, chairman of the Tokyo Peace Conference of Japan, and Seimin Miyazaki, a member of the Japan Peace Protection Committee, who were invited by the Chinese People's Committee for World Peace to attend the "May Day" celebrations and visit China, arrived in Beijing by plane on the afternoon of the 30th.
　　Representatives from Syria, Jordan, and Lebanon Arrive in Beijing
　　Xinhua News Agency, 30th. A group of six representatives from Syria, Jordan, and Lebanon, who were invited to China by the Chinese People's Committee for World Peace after attending the Asian countries' conference, arrived in Beijing by train on the morning of the 30th. They are: Syrian representative, Syrian parliament member Abdul Karim Chakhor, Syrian General Federation of Trade Unions Chairman Subhi A. K. Katibe, Syrian Progressive General Federation of Trade Unions Chairman Yusuf M. Imadi, Syrian Workers' Congress Chairman Ibrahim Bakli, Lebanese representative, journalist Sambul A. Magidallani, Jordanian representative, literary magazine editor Abdul Rahman Khalifa.
　　Zhu Xuefan, Wu Maosun, and other heads of organizations such as the Chinese People's Committee for World Peace and the All-China Federation of Trade Unions welcomed them at the station.

### 各国工会代表团陆续到京

1955-05-01
第4版()
专栏：

　  各国工会代表团陆续到京
    新华社三十日讯　应中华全国总工会和各产业工会的邀请，来我国参加“五一”节观礼和访问的印度各工会代表团和代表，以冒貌格礼为首的缅甸工会代表团，以曹特利·拉玛图拉为首的巴基斯坦工会代表团，日本工人访华代表团副团长滨田定作和第二、三批代表，以萨达威为首的突尼斯工会代表团，印度尼西亚工会代表四人，新西兰工会代表四人和伊朗工会代表一人，在二十九日和三十日分批乘火车和飞机到达北京。各国工会代表到达时，受到中华全国总工会和各产业工会负责人的热烈欢迎。
    印度各工会代表团和工会代表有：以巴苏为首的全印总工会代表团，以巴斯万·辛哈为首的印度劳工协会代表团，以巴塔查利亚为首的印度统一工会大会代表团，以查克拉瓦迪为首的全印铁路员工联合会代表团，以雅·斯·阿里为首的全印海员联合会代表团，以西兰格为首的印度海员联合会代表团，以卡卡尔为首的全印银行雇员协会代表团，以沙哈拉波德为首的全印教育协会代表团，全印石油工人联合会代表森德拉姆，全印保险雇员协会代表柏拉恩耶普和全印港口码头工人联合会代表品托。
　  “波兰日报”主编克拉西茨基到京
    新华社二十九日讯　根据中波文化合作协定一九五五年执行计划，应邀来我国参加“五一”节观礼和参观访问的波兰克拉科夫省“波兰日报”主编菲·克拉西茨基已在二十九日乘飞机抵京。
　  瑞士文化代表团到京
    新华社二十九日讯　应中国人民对外文化协会邀请，以克拉默博士为首的瑞士文化代表团五人在四月二十九日到达北京。到机场欢迎的有中国人民对外文化协会负责人。
    日本和平人士宫崎龙介等到京
    据新华社讯　应中国人民保卫世界和平委员会邀请来我国参加“五一”节观礼和参观访问的日本东京都和平会议主席宫崎龙介和日本拥护和平委员会委员宫崎世民等，在三十日下午乘飞机到达北京。
　  叙利亚、约旦、黎巴嫩代表到京
    新华社三十日讯　在出席亚洲国家会议后应中国人民保卫世界和平委员会邀请来我国的叙利亚、约旦和黎巴嫩代表一行六人，在三十日上午乘火车到达北京。他们是：叙利亚代表、叙利亚议会议员阿卜杜勒·卡里姆·查霍尔、叙利亚总工会主席苏布希·阿·克·卡蒂贝、叙利亚进步总工会主席优素福·麦·伊马迪、叙利亚工人大会主席易卜拉欣·巴克利，黎巴嫩代表、新闻记者桑布尔·阿·马吉达朗尼，约旦代表、文学杂志编辑阿卜杜勒·拉曼·哈利法。
    到车站欢迎的有：中国人民保卫世界和平委员会和中华全国总工会等团体负责人朱学范、吴茂荪等。
