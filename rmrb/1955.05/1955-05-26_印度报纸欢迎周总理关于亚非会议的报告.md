Title: Indian Newspapers Welcome Premier Zhou's Report on the Asian-African Conference
Author:
Date: 1955-05-26
Page: 4
　　
Xinhua News Agency, New Delhi, May 23rd. The "Indian Light" weekly newspaper, in its "International Stage" column on the 22nd, expressed a warm welcome to Premier Zhou Enlai's report to the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress of China on the Asian-African Conference.

　　The newspaper said, "Zhou Enlai's summary of the Taiwan issue is concise and correct." The article pointed out that Premier Zhou Enlai's clear explanation of China's foreign policy stands in stark contrast to the ambiguity of the U.S. State Department's statement.

　　The article pointed out, "It is the United States that is using force in the Taiwan area." The article said, "The presence of U.S. armed forces near the Chinese mainland is not only a threat to the security of the People's Republic of China, but also a clear and existing aggression," and this aggression "is a permanent threat to world peace."

　　The article opposes the proposal to place Taiwan under UN trusteeship. The article said that a country's national rights cannot be usurped by the United Nations or any other group of countries.

　　The Urdu newspaper "New Century" commented on the 22nd on the U.S.'s stubborn attitude on the issue of holding negotiations to ease tensions in the Taiwan area, writing that the very fact that the U.S. is putting forward unreasonable conditions "proves that the U.S. wants to continue to occupy Taiwan no matter what, and does not approve of easing tensions in the Far East, because this would hinder the realization of its plan to launch a war in Asia."

### 印度报纸欢迎周总理关于亚非会议的报告

1955-05-26
第4版()
专栏：

　　印度报纸欢迎周总理关于亚非会议的报告
    新华社新德里二十三日电　“印度之光”周报二十二日在它的专栏“国际舞台”中表示热烈欢迎周恩来总理在中国全国人民代表大会常务委员会上所作的关于亚非会议的报告。
    报纸说，“周恩来关于台湾问题的总结是扼要而正确的”。文章指出，周恩来总理的报告对中国外交政策的清楚的说明，同美国国务院的声明的含糊其词恰好成为鲜明的对照。
    文章指出，“在台湾地区使用武力的是美国”。文章说，“美国武装部队在中国大陆附近的存在，不仅是对中华人民共和国安全的威胁，而且是明显的、已经存在的侵略”，这种侵略“对世界和平是永久的威胁”。
    文章反对把台湾交给联合国托管的主张。文章说，一个国家的民族权利不能由联合国或任何其他国家集团来篡夺。
    乌尔都文“新世纪报”二十二日评论美国在就缓和台湾地区紧张局势举行谈判的问题上的顽固态度时写道：美国提出无理条件的事实本身，就“证明了美国想无论如何要继续占领台湾，并且不赞成缓和远东紧张局势，因为这样就会妨碍它在亚洲发动战争的计划的实现。”
