Title: Sholokhov Awarded the Order of Lenin
Author:
Date: 1955-05-25
Page: 4

　　Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, 24th: TASS reports: The Presidium of the Supreme Soviet of the Soviet Union awarded the writer M. Sholokhov the Order of Lenin on the 23rd, in recognition of his outstanding achievements in literature and art and to celebrate his fiftieth birthday.
    Sholokhov enjoys a great reputation in the Soviet Union and abroad. According to statistics from the Central Library of the Soviet Union, from 1925 to the present, Sholokhov's works have been published in 421 editions in 55 languages, totaling 20,773,000 copies. The novel "And Quiet Flows the Don" has been published in 7,755,000 copies in 18 languages. "Virgin Soil Upturned" has been published in 45 languages, with 132 reprints.

### 萧洛霍夫荣获列宁勋章

1955-05-25
第4版()
专栏：

　　萧洛霍夫荣获列宁勋章
    新华社二十四日讯　据塔斯社莫斯科讯：苏联最高苏维埃主席团二十三日授予作家米·萧洛霍夫列宁勋章，以奖励他在文艺方面的杰出功绩和庆贺他的五十寿辰。
    萧洛霍夫在苏联和国外享有盛名。根据苏联中央书库的统计，从一九二五年起到现在，萧洛霍夫的作品已用五十五种语言出版了四百二十一版，计二千零七十七万三千册。长篇小说“静静的顿河”以十八种语言出版了七百七十五万五千册。“被开垦的处女地”以四十五种语言出版，再版达一百三十二次。
