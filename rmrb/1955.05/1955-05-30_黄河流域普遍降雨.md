Title: Widespread Rainfall in the Yellow River Basin
Author:
Date: 1955-05-30
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, 28th dispatch: The area north of the Huai River and the Yellow River Basin, which had experienced little rainfall since the beginning of spring, has recently had widespread rainfall. On the morning of the 25th, rainfall first began in the Hetao area of the Inner Mongolia Autonomous Region and the northern part of Rehe Province. On the 26th, Qinghai, Shanxi, Hebei, Shandong, and the Huai River basin successively experienced rainfall. By 2:00 PM on the 27th, the rainfall in northern Hebei Province, northeastern Shanxi Province, southern Inner Mongolia Autonomous Region, eastern Qinghai Province, southern Shandong Province, northern Jiangsu Province, and northern Henan Province had all reached over 25 millimeters. The rainfall in Weixian County and the Zhangjiakou area of Hebei Province exceeded 50 millimeters. The southern part of Shandong Province and the Tai'an area also approached 50 millimeters. Today, with the exception of the eastern part of the Shandong Peninsula and other areas, the other regions have cleared up.

### 黄河流域普遍降雨

1955-05-30
第1版()
专栏：

　  黄河流域普遍降雨
    新华社二十八日讯　入春以来雨量稀少的淮河以北和黄河流域一带，近日普遍降雨。二十五日晨，内蒙古自治区的河套地区、热河省北部首先开始降雨。二十六日，青海、山西、河北、山东和淮河流域相继降雨。至二十七日下午二时止，河北省北部、山西省东北部、内蒙古自治区南部、青海省东部、山东省南部、江苏省北部和河南省北部雨量都达到二十五公厘以上。河北省蔚县和张家口一带雨量超过了五十公厘。山东省南部和泰安专区也接近五十公厘。今日，除山东半岛东部等地外，其他地区都已转晴。
