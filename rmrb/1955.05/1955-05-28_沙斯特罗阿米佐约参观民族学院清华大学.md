Title: Sastroamidjojo Visits the Nationalities Institute and Tsinghua University
Author:
Date: 1955-05-28
Page: 1

　　Sastroamidjojo Visits the Nationalities Institute and Tsinghua University

(Our newspaper's dispatch) Indonesian Republic Prime Minister Ali Sastroamidjojo and his wife, along with other accompanying personnel, accompanied by our country's ambassador to Indonesia, Huang Zhen, and his wife, and the Director of the Protocol Department of our country's Ministry of Foreign Affairs, Ke Hua, visited the Central Nationalities Institute and Tsinghua University in the western suburbs of Beijing on the afternoon of the 27th.

　　At 3:15 PM, Prime Minister Sastroamidjojo first arrived at the Central Nationalities Institute. Seven students wearing different ethnic costumes greeted him at the school gate on behalf of the students of all 48 ethnic groups in the college. The Vice Presidents of the Central Nationalities Institute, Su Keqin and Xiong Shouqi, accompanied Prime Minister Sastroamidjojo to visit the scripture hall for students who believe in Buddhism, the prayer hall for students who believe in Islam, and the classrooms, library, canteen, and the ethnic minority cultural relics exhibition room. In the cultural relics exhibition room, Prime Minister Sastroamidjojo and his wife showed great interest in the clothing and utensils of various ethnic groups in our country on display. Before leaving, Prime Minister Sastroamidjojo expressed his hope that the Central Nationalities Institute would achieve further development and greater achievements.

　　At 4:30 PM, Prime Minister Sastroamidjojo visited Tsinghua University. The students of Tsinghua University lined up in the rain to welcome the distinguished guests. Accompanied by President Jiang Nanxiang and Provost Qian Weichang, Prime Minister Sastroamidjojo visited the Hydraulic Engineering Laboratory, the metalworking practice factory of the Department of Mechanical Engineering, and the hydraulic hub, a hydroelectric power generation test equipment made according to the Yellow River, which can generate seven kilowatts of electricity. Finally, he took a car around the campus for a visit. When Prime Minister Sastroamidjojo left after the visit, the students cheered and waved goodbye to the distinguished guests.

### 沙斯特罗阿米佐约参观民族学院清华大学

1955-05-28
第1版()
专栏：

　　沙斯特罗阿米佐约参观民族学院清华大学
    本报讯　印度尼西亚共和国总理阿里·沙斯特罗阿米佐约和夫人及其他随行人员，由我国驻印度尼西亚大使黄镇和夫人、我国外交部礼宾司司长柯华陪同，在二十七日下午到北京西郊参观中央民族学院和清华大学。
    三时十五分，沙斯特罗阿米佐约总理首先到达中央民族学院。穿着不同民族服装的七位学生，代表全学院四十八个民族的学生出校门迎接。中央民族学院副院长苏克勤、熊寿祺陪同沙斯特罗阿米佐约总理参观了供信仰佛教的学生用的经堂、供信仰伊斯兰教学生用的礼拜堂和教室、图书馆、食堂以及少数民族文物陈列室。在文物陈列室内，沙斯特罗阿米佐约总理和夫人对陈列的我国各民族的服装和用具发生很大兴趣。临别时，沙斯特罗阿米佐约总理表示希望中央民族学院获得更进一步的发展和更大的成就。
    四时三十分，沙斯特罗阿米佐约总理到清华大学参观。清华大学的学生们，冒雨列队欢迎贵宾。在蒋南翔校长和钱伟长教务长陪同下，沙斯特罗阿米佐约总理参观了水利馆，机械制造系的金工实习工厂和按黄河制作的可以发电七瓩的水力发电试验设备——水力枢纽，最后并乘汽车绕行校园内各处参观。沙斯特罗阿米佐约总理参观完毕后离开时，学生们欢呼挥手和贵宾们道别。
