Title: Celebrating the Tenth Anniversary of the Liberation of Czechoslovakia, Prague Holds Grand Military Parade and Mass Parade; Široký and Čepička Host Grand Reception
Author:
Date: 1955-05-11
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency dispatch, Prague—A grand military parade and mass parade were held in Prague, the capital of the Czechoslovak Republic, on May 9th to celebrate the tenth anniversary of the liberation of Czechoslovakia by the Soviet army.

At 9:00 AM, Czechoslovak Communist Party and state leaders Zápotocký, Široký, Novotný, Fierlinger, and others, along with the Soviet government delegation led by First Deputy Chairman of the Council of Ministers of the USSR Kaganovich, the government delegation of the People's Republic of China headed by Vice Premier of the State Council Ulanhu, and government delegations from various People's Democratic countries, ascended the reviewing stand, at which point the entire venue erupted in enthusiastic applause.

President Zápotocký delivered a celebratory speech. Subsequently, First Deputy Prime Minister and Minister of National Defense Čepička inspected the troops lined up on the square.

The march-past began. Students from Czechoslovak military academies and schools, as well as troops from various branches of service, marched past the square in succession. More than two hundred jet fighters and other types of aircraft soared over the square.

After the military parade, the mass festival parade began. The mass parade continued until 1:00 PM. Afterwards, various mass recreational activities unfolded in the streets and squares of Prague.

Xinhua News Agency dispatch, Prague, May 10th—Czechoslovak Republic Prime Minister Široký and First Deputy Prime Minister and Minister of National Defense Čepička held a grand reception at the "Prague Palace" in Prague on the evening of May 9th to celebrate the tenth anniversary of the liberation of Czechoslovakia by the Soviet army.

Attendees at the reception included Czechoslovak Republic President Zápotocký, Chairman of the National Assembly Fierlinger, First Secretary of the Central Committee of the Czechoslovak Communist Party Novotný, heads of various departments of the Party and government, generals and model soldiers of the Czechoslovak army, as well as prominent figures in the cultural and scientific fields and advanced workers.

The Soviet government delegation headed by First Deputy Chairman of the Council of Ministers of the USSR Kaganovich, the government delegation of the People's Republic of China headed by Vice Premier of the State Council Ulanhu, and government delegations from various People's Democratic countries, as well as diplomatic envoys from various countries to Czechoslovakia, were invited to attend the reception.

The reception proceeded in a warm and friendly atmosphere.

### 庆祝捷克斯洛伐克解放十周年　布拉格举行盛大阅兵和群众游行　西罗基和契皮茨卡举行盛大招待会

1955-05-11
第4版()
专栏：

　　庆祝捷克斯洛伐克解放十周年
　　布拉格举行盛大阅兵和群众游行
　　西罗基和契皮茨卡举行盛大招待会
    据新华社讯　五月九日，捷克斯洛伐克共和国首都布拉格举行了盛大的阅兵和群众游行，庆祝苏联军队解放捷克斯洛伐克十周年。
    上午九时，捷克斯洛伐克共产党和国家的领导人萨波托斯基、西罗基、诺沃提尼、费林格等，以苏联部长会议第一副主席卡冈诺维奇为首的苏联政府代表团，以国务院副总理乌兰夫为首的中华人民共和国政府代表团和各人民民主国家的政府代表团登上了检阅台，这时，全场热烈鼓掌。
    萨波托斯基总统发表了庆祝节日的演说。接着，共和国第一副总理兼国防部长契皮茨卡检阅排列在广场上的部队。
    分列式开始。捷克斯洛伐克军事学院和学校的学员、各种兵种部队相继走过广场。二百多架喷气式飞机和其他类型飞机掠过广场上空。
    阅兵结束后，群众的节日游行开始。群众游行一直持续到下午一时。随后，布拉格各街道和广场上又展开了各种群众游艺活动。
    新华社布拉格十日电　捷克斯洛伐克共和国总理西罗基、第一副总理兼国防部长契皮茨卡，九日晚在布拉格的“布拉格宫”举行盛大招待会，庆祝苏军解放捷克斯洛伐克十周年。
    出席招待会的有捷克斯洛伐克共和国总统萨波托斯基，国民议会主席费林格，捷克斯洛伐克共产党中央委员会第一书记诺沃提尼，党和政府各部门的负责人，捷克斯洛伐克军队将领和模范战士，以及文化科学界著名人士和先进工作者等。
    以苏联部长会议第一副主席卡冈诺维奇为首的苏联政府代表团、以国务院副总理乌兰夫为首的中华人民共和国政府代表团和各人民民主国家政府代表团，以及各国驻捷外交使节应邀出席了招待会。
    招待会在热烈友好的气氛中进行。
