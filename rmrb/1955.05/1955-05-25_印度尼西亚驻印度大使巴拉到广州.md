Title: Indonesian Ambassador to India, Bala, Arrives in Guangzhou
Author:
Date: 1955-05-25
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Guangzhou, May 24th: Bala, the Indonesian Ambassador to India, who is accompanying the Prime Minister of the Republic of Indonesia, Ali Sastroamidjojo, on a friendly visit to our country, arrived in Guangzhou at 7:04 PM on the 24th via Hong Kong by train. Cao Ruoming, Director of the Foreign Affairs Office of the Guangdong Provincial People's Committee, went to the station to greet him.

### 印度尼西亚驻印度大使巴拉到广州

1955-05-25
第1版()
专栏：

　　印度尼西亚驻印度大使巴拉到广州
    新华社广州二十四日电　随同印度尼西亚共和国总理阿里·沙斯特罗阿米佐约来我国作友好访问的印度尼西亚共和国驻印度大使巴拉在二十四日下午七时四分经由香港乘火车先期到达广州。广东省人民委员会外事处处长曹若茗前往车站迎接。
