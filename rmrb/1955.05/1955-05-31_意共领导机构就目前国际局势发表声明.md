Title: Italian Communist Party Leadership Issues Statement on Current International Situation
Author:
Date: 1955-05-31
Page: 4

　　Xinhua News Agency, Rome, May 29th: The leadership of the Italian Communist Party held a meeting on the 26th and issued a statement on the international situation and the new situation created by the conclusion of the Austrian State Treaty.

　　The statement pointed out that an increasing number of countries in Central Europe wish to remain outside of military blocs, which is a significant contribution to easing international tensions. The statement pointed out that changes in the international situation require a reconsideration of Italy's foreign policy.

　　The statement continued that, while there is once again a great possibility for Italy to demonstrate peaceful initiative, the Italian authorities openly express their intention to strengthen Italy's ties with the North Atlantic Alliance. By allowing foreign military bases to be established on Italian soil for the use of occupying forces that will be supplied with atomic weapons, Italy will openly become a springboard for certain foreign countries to prepare for atomic war.

　　The statement said that Italy should take action to gradually ease international tensions by establishing a collective security system and cooperation among all European countries (regardless of their social systems). Italy should reject any foreign interference in its economic and political life, so that it can freely develop economic and cultural exchanges with all countries, reduce the burden of military expenditures, and get rid of any form of military occupation.

### 意共领导机构就目前国际局势发表声明

1955-05-31
第4版()
专栏：

　　意共领导机构就目前国际局势发表声明
    新华社三十日讯　据塔斯社罗马二十九日讯：意大利共产党领导机构二十六日举行会议并且就国际局势和由于缔结奥地利国家条约而形成的新局面发表了声明。
    声明指出，欧洲中部希望保持在军事集团之外的国家正在增多，这对缓和国际紧张局势是一个重大贡献。声明指出，国际局势的改变要求重新考虑意大利的外交政策。
    声明接着指出，当现在又一次有极大的可能使意大利表现和平的主动性的时候，意大利的执政者却公开表示还打算加强意大利同北大西洋联盟的政策的联系。由于准许外国在意大利土地上建立军事基地，供那些将得到原子武器供应的占领军使用，因此意大利将公开变成某些外国准备进行原子战争的跳板。
    声明说，意大利应当行动起来，通过建立欧洲一切国家（不问其社会制度如何）的集体安全体系和合作的办法来逐步缓和国际紧张局势。意大利应当拒绝外国对它的经济和政治生活进行任何干涉，以便能够同一切国家自由地发展经济和文化交流，减轻军事费用的负担和摆脱任何形式的军事占领。
