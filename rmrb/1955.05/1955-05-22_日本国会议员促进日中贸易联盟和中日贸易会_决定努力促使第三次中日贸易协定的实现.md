Title: Japanese Diet Members' League for the Promotion of Japan-China Trade and the Japan-China Trade Association Decide to Strive for the Realization of the Third Japan-China Trade Agreement
Author:
Date: 1955-05-22
Page: 4

　　Japanese Diet Members' League for the Promotion of Japan-China Trade and the Japan-China Trade Association
　　Decide to Strive for the Realization of the Third Japan-China Trade Agreement
    Xinhua News Agency, Tokyo, 21st - The Japanese Diet Members' League for the Promotion of Japan-China Trade held a board meeting in Tokyo on the 20th to discuss the work plan for this year. The meeting decided to focus its main efforts on promoting the realization of the Third Japan-China Trade Agreement.

    The Diet Members' League decided to work to get the Diet to pass a resolution requiring the Japanese government, on the basis of Prime Minister Ichiro Hatoyama's guarantee, to support the Japan-China Trade Agreement. The meeting also decided to request the Japanese government to conclude an official trade agreement with China and to relax "embargo" restrictions on China.

    The meeting also decided on holding a meeting with Chinese representatives to prepare for holding commodity exhibitions in Japan and China respectively, and to establish a research group including the Minister of Finance and the Bank of Japan in order to conclude a payment agreement with China.

    Xinhua News Agency, Tokyo, 19th - The Japan-China Trade Association held a special congress in Tokyo on the 18th, at which five resolutions were made regarding the implementation of the recently signed Third Japan-China Trade Agreement.

    The resolutions requested: 1. The Japanese government should immediately prepare to conclude an official trade agreement with China; 2. Negotiations should be started with China to conclude an agreement for direct settlement of accounts by the national banks of China and Japan; 3. Official trade representative offices should be dispatched to China and Japan as soon as possible; 4. The embargo should be relaxed so that the import and export targets stipulated in the Third Japan-China Trade Agreement can be achieved; 5. The holding of commodity exhibitions in Japan and China should be realized as scheduled.

    These resolutions will be negotiated with the Japanese government by the Japanese Diet Members' League for the Promotion of Japan-China Trade.

### 日本国会议员促进日中贸易联盟和中日贸易会　决定努力促使第三次中日贸易协定的实现

1955-05-22
第4版()
专栏：

　　日本国会议员促进日中贸易联盟和中日贸易会
　　决定努力促使第三次中日贸易协定的实现
    新华社二十一日讯　东京消息：日本国会议员促进日中贸易联盟二十日在东京举行理事会会议，讨论今年度的工作方针。会议决定把主要力量放在促使第三次中日贸易协定实现方面。
    议员联盟决定努力使国会通过决议，要求日本政府在首相鸠山一郎的保证的基础上，支持中日贸易协定。会议还决定要求日本政府和中国缔结官方的贸易协定，并放宽对中国的“禁运”限制。
    会议也决定了关于同中国代表举行会议来准备在中日两国互相举办商品展览会的事情，以及成立包括藏相和日本银行在内的研究小组，以便同中国缔结支付协定。
    新华社十九日讯　东京消息：中日贸易会十八日在东京举行特别大会，会上作出了有关实施最近签订的第三次中日贸易协定的五项决议。
    决议要求：一、日本政府立即准备和中国缔结一个官方的贸易协定；二、开始和中国谈判缔结由中日两国的国家银行直接结账的协定；三、迅速使中日两国互派官方的贸易代表机构；四、放宽禁运，以使第三次中日贸易协定所规定的进出口目标能够达到；五、如期实现中日两国互相举办商品展览会。
    这些决议的事项将由日本国会议员促进日中贸易联盟向日本政府进行交涉。
