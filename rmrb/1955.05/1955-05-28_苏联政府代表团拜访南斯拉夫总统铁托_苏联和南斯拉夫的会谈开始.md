Title: Soviet Government Delegation Visits Yugoslav President Tito; Soviet-Yugoslav Talks Begin
Author:
Date: 1955-05-28
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Belgrade, May 26th: TASS reports: The Soviet government delegation, headed by N.S. Khrushchev, Member of the Presidium of the Supreme Soviet of the USSR and First Secretary of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of the Soviet Union, visited Josip Broz Tito, President of the Federal People's Republic of Yugoslavia, on May 26th after arriving in Belgrade.

Xinhua News Agency, Belgrade, May 27th: TASS reports: Soviet-Yugoslav negotiations began at 10:40 AM Central European Time on May 27th at the Guards' House in Belgrade. The Soviet side participating in the negotiations included: N.S. Khrushchev, Member of the Presidium of the Supreme Soviet of the USSR and First Secretary of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of the Soviet Union (Head of Delegation), N.A. Bulganin, Chairman of the Council of Ministers of the USSR, A.I. Mikoyan, First Deputy Chairman of the Council of Ministers of the USSR, D.T. Shepilov, Chairman of the Foreign Affairs Committee of the Soviet Supreme Soviet's Nationalities Council and Member of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of the Soviet Union and Editor-in-Chief of "Pravda", A.A. Gromyko, First Deputy Minister of Foreign Affairs of the USSR, and P.N. Kumykin, Deputy Minister of Foreign Trade of the USSR.

The Yugoslav side participating in the negotiations included: Josip Broz Tito, President of the Federal People's Republic of Yugoslavia, E. Kardelj, A. Ranković and S. Vukmanović, Vice Presidents of the Federal Executive Council, K. Popović, Secretary of State for Foreign Affairs, M. Todorović, Member of the Federal Executive Council, and V. Mićunović, Deputy Secretary of State for Foreign Affairs.

### 苏联政府代表团拜访南斯拉夫总统铁托  苏联和南斯拉夫的会谈开始

1955-05-28
第4版()
专栏：

　　苏联政府代表团拜访南斯拉夫总统铁托
　  苏联和南斯拉夫的会谈开始
    新华社二十七日讯　塔斯社贝尔格莱德二十六日讯：以苏联最高苏维埃主席团委员、苏联共产党中央委员会第一书记尼·谢·赫鲁晓夫为首的苏联政府代表团，在五月二十六日到达贝尔格莱德后，当日拜访了南斯拉夫联邦人民共和国总统约瑟夫·布罗兹·铁托。
    新华社二十七日讯　塔斯社贝尔格莱德二十七日讯：苏联和南斯拉夫的谈判于五月二十七日中欧时间上午十时四十分在贝尔格莱德近卫军大厦开始。苏联方面参加谈判的有：苏联最高苏维埃主席团委员、苏联共产党中央委员会第一书记尼·谢·赫鲁晓夫（代表团团长）、苏联部长会议主席尼·亚·布尔加宁、苏联部长会议第一副主席阿·伊·米高扬、苏联最高苏维埃民族院外交委员会主席、苏联共产党中央委员会委员、“真理报”总编辑德·特·谢皮洛夫、苏联外交部第一副部长安·安·葛罗米柯和苏联对外贸易部副部长帕·尼·库米金。
    南斯拉夫方面参加谈判的有：南斯拉夫联邦人民共和国总统约瑟夫·布罗兹·铁托、联邦执行委员会副主席爱·卡德尔、亚·兰科维奇和斯·伏克曼诺维奇、外交秘书长科·波波维奇、联邦执行委员会委员米·托多罗维奇、外交副秘书长维·米丘诺维奇。
