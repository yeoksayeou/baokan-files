Title: Expansion of Hainan Douzong Irrigation Project Completed
Author:
Date: 1955-05-07
Page: 1

　　Hainan Douzong Irrigation Project Expansion Completed
Xinhua News Agency: The expansion of the largest Douzong Irrigation Project in the Hainan Li and Miao Autonomous Region, Guangdong Province, has been completed and water has been released for irrigation. The expansion project in the Douzong irrigation area this spring includes the renovation of dams and the opening of new canals. The irrigated area has been expanded by 5,800 mu compared with the original, totaling more than 35,000 mu. Among them, 3,800 mu of single-season rice fields can be converted into double-season or triple-season rice fields.

### 海南都总灌溉工程扩建完工

1955-05-07
第1版()
专栏：

　  海南都总灌溉工程扩建完工
    广东省海南黎族苗族自治区最大的都总灌溉工程扩建完工，并已放水灌田。今年春季都总灌溉区的扩建工程包括整修水坝和新开渠道两部分，灌溉面积比原来扩大五千八百亩，共有三万五千多亩。其中有三千八百亩单季稻田可改为双季或三季稻田。　（新华社）
