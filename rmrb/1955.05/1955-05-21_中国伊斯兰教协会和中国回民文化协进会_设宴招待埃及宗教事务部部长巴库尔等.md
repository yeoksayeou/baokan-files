Title: China Islamic Association and China Hui Nationality Culture Advancement Association Host Banquet for Egyptian Minister of Religious Affairs Bakur and Others
Author: 
Date: 1955-05-21
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, May 20th

　　The China Islamic Association and the China Hui Nationality Culture Advancement Association held a banquet on the evening of the 20th to welcome Egyptian Minister of Religious Affairs Bakur and Cairo University Constitutional Law Professor Kamel.

　　More than 40 people attended the banquet, including Vice Chairmen of the China Islamic Association Ma Yu-huai and Da Puren, Secretary-General Zhang Jie and members, and Vice Chairmen of the China Hui Nationality Culture Advancement Association Bai Shouyi and Ma Songting, Secretary-General Gan Chunlei and members.

　　Yang Jingren, Deputy Director of the Nationalities Affairs Commission of the People's Republic of China, also attended the banquet.

　　At the banquet, Vice Chairman Da Puren and Minister Bakur spoke successively, wishing for the continuous development of friendship between the peoples of China and Egypt and wishing the Muslim brothers of the two countries would contribute to the cause of defending human peace.

　　The banquet proceeded in a friendly atmosphere.

　　After the banquet, the China Islamic Association and the China Hui Nationality Culture Advancement Association presented gifts to Minister Bakur and Professor Kamel, as well as to Egyptian Muslims. The gifts included the Quran and a film of "The Life of Muslims in Beijing." Finally, a film depicting the life of Muslims in Beijing was shown.

### 中国伊斯兰教协会和中国回民文化协进会　设宴招待埃及宗教事务部部长巴库尔等

1955-05-21
第1版()
专栏：

　　中国伊斯兰教协会和中国回民文化协进会
　　设宴招待埃及宗教事务部部长巴库尔等
    新华社二十日讯　中国伊斯兰教协会和中国回民文化协进会，二十日晚举行宴会，欢迎埃及宗教事务部长巴库尔和开罗大学宪法教授卡迈尔。
    出席宴会的有中国伊斯兰教协会副主任马玉槐、达浦生，秘书长张杰和委员，中国回民文化协进会副主任白寿彝、马松亭，秘书长甘春雷和委员等四十多人。
    中华人民共和国民族事务委员会副主任杨静仁也出席了宴会。
    宴会上，达浦生副主任和巴库尔部长先后讲话，祝中埃两国人民的友谊日益发展，祝两国的穆斯林兄弟在保卫人类和平的事业中作出贡献。
    宴会在友好的空气中进行。
      宴会后，中国伊斯兰教协会和中国回民文化协进会向巴库尔部长和卡迈尔教授以及埃及伊斯兰教徒赠送礼品。礼品中有古兰经和“北京穆斯林生活”电影胶片等。最后，放映了描写北京穆斯林生活的电影。
