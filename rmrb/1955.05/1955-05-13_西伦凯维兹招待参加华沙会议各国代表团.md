Title: Cyrankiewicz Hosts Reception for Delegations to Warsaw Conference
Author:
Date: 1955-05-13
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, Warsaw, May 12th: According to a report from the Polish Press Agency, the Chairman of the Polish Council of Ministers, Cyrankiewicz, held a reception on the 11th to host the delegations to the Warsaw Conference of European countries for the assurance of European peace and security.

　　Those attending the reception included: the head of the delegation of the Union of Soviet Socialist Republics, Chairman of the USSR Council of Ministers, Bulganin, delegation members First Deputy Chairman of the USSR Council of Ministers and Minister of Foreign Affairs Molotov, Soviet Defense Minister and Marshal of the Soviet Union Zhukov, Marshal of the Soviet Union Konev, Chairman of the Council of Ministers of the Russian Federation Puzanov, Chairman of the Council of Ministers of the Ukrainian Republic Kalchenko, Chairman of the Council of Ministers of the Byelorussian Republic Matulov, Chairman of the Council of Ministers of the Latvian Republic Lacis, Chairman of the Council of Ministers of the Lithuanian Republic Gedvilas, Chairman of the Council of Ministers of the Estonian Republic Müürisepp, Deputy Minister of Foreign Affairs of the USSR Zorin, and Soviet Ambassador to Poland Ponomarenko; the head of the delegation of the Czechoslovak Republic, Prime Minister of the Czechoslovak Republic Široký, delegation members First Deputy Prime Minister and Minister of National Defense General Čepička and Minister of Foreign Affairs David; the head of the delegation of the German Democratic Republic, Prime Minister of the German Democratic Republic Grotewohl, delegation members Deputy Prime Minister Ulbricht, Deputy Prime Minister and Minister of the Interior Stoph, Deputy Prime Minister Nuschke, Deputy Prime Minister Loch, Deputy Prime Minister and Minister of Foreign Affairs Bolz, Minister of Agriculture and Forestry Reichelt, Deputy Minister of the Interior Hoffmann, and Ambassador of the German Democratic Republic to Poland Herrmann; the head of the delegation of the Hungarian People's Republic, Chairman of the Council of Ministers of the Hungarian People's Republic Hegedüs, delegation members Minister of Foreign Affairs Boldoczki, Minister of National Defense General Bata, First Deputy Minister of Foreign Affairs Sziklai, and Ambassador of the Hungarian People's Republic to Poland Drahos; the head of the delegation of the Romanian People's Republic, Chairman of the Council of Ministers of the Romanian People's Republic Gheorghe Gheorghiu-Dej, delegation members Vice-Chairman of the Council of Ministers and Minister of Armed Forces General Bodnăraș, Minister of Foreign Affairs Buji, Chief of the General Staff Lieutenant General Tutoveanu, First Deputy Minister of Foreign Affairs Preoteasa, and Ambassador of the Romanian People's Republic to Poland Iănus; the head of the delegation of the People's Republic of Bulgaria, Chairman of the Council of Ministers of the People's Republic of Bulgaria Chervenkov, delegation members Minister of Foreign Affairs Neichev and Minister of National Defense General Panchevski; the head of the delegation of the People's Republic of Albania, Chairman of the Council of Ministers of the People's Republic of Albania Shehu, delegation members First Deputy Chairman of the Council of Ministers and Minister of National Defense Balluku, and Minister of Foreign Affairs Shtulla; the observer of the People's Republic of China to the Warsaw Conference, Vice Premier of the State Council and Minister of National Defense of the People's Republic of China Peng Dehuai, as well as the advisors, experts and staff of various delegations.

　　The Polish side attending the reception included First Secretary of the Central Committee of the Polish United Workers' Party Beirut, Chairman of the Council of State Zawadzki, First Deputy Chairman of the Council of Ministers Nowak, Deputy Chairman of the Council of Ministers and Minister of National Defense Rokossovsky, Secretaries of the Central Committee of the Polish United Workers' Party Ochab and Mazur, Deputy Chairmen of the Council of Ministers Berman and Jozwiak-Witold, Ministers Radkiewicz and Zambrowski, as well as representatives of the Polish army, leaders of various political parties and social organizations, and figures from the scientific, cultural, and journalistic circles.

　　Also present at the reception were diplomatic envoys and military attachés from various countries stationed in Poland.

　　The reception proceeded in a very warm and friendly atmosphere.

### 西伦凯维兹招待参加华沙会议各国代表团

1955-05-13
第1版()
专栏：

　　西伦凯维兹招待参加华沙会议各国代表团
    新华社华沙十二日电　据波兰通讯社报道：波兰部长会议主席西伦凯维兹十一日举行招待会，招待参加欧洲国家保障欧洲和平和安全的华沙会议的各国代表团。
    出席招待会的有：苏维埃社会主义共和国联盟代表团团长、苏联部长会议主席布尔加宁，代表团团员苏联部长会议第一副主席兼外交部长莫洛托夫，苏联国防部长、苏联元帅朱可夫，苏联元帅科涅夫，俄罗斯联邦部长会议主席普扎诺夫，乌克兰共和国部长会议主席卡尔钦科，白俄罗斯共和国部长会议主席马楚罗夫，拉脱维亚共和国部长会议主席拉齐斯，立陶宛共和国部长会议主席盖德维拉斯，爱沙尼亚共和国部长会议主席缪里谢普，苏联外交部副部长佐林和苏联驻波兰大使波诺马连科；捷克斯洛伐克共和国代表团团长、捷克斯洛伐克共和国总理西罗基，代表团团员第一副总理兼国防部长契皮茨卡大将和外交部长戴维；德意志民主共和国代表团团长、德意志民主共和国总理格罗提渥，代表团团员副总理乌布利希，副总理兼内务部长斯多夫，副总理努舍克，副总理洛赫，副总理兼外交部长博尔茨，农林部长赖希尔特，内务部副部长霍夫曼，德意志民主共和国驻波兰大使赫曼；匈牙利人民共和国代表团团长、匈牙利人民共和国部长会议主席赫格居斯，代表团团员外交部长波尔多奇基，国防部长巴塔上将，外交部第一副部长西克和匈牙利人民共和国驻波兰大使德拉霍什；罗马尼亚人民共和国代表团团长、罗马尼亚人民共和国部长会议主席乔治乌—德治，代表团团员部长会议副主席兼武装部队部部长波德纳拉希大将，外交部长布吉奇，总参谋长图托维亚诺中将，外交部第一副部长普利奥特亚萨和罗马尼亚人民共和国驻波兰大使扬尼斯库；保加利亚人民共和国代表团团长、保加利亚人民共和国部长会议主席契尔文科夫，代表团团员外交部长涅伊切夫和国防部长潘切夫斯基大将；阿尔巴尼亚人民共和国代表团团长、阿尔巴尼亚人民共和国部长会议主席谢胡，代表团团员部长会议第一副主席兼国防部长巴卢库和外交部长什图拉；中华人民共和国出席华沙会议的观察员、中华人民共和国国务院副总理兼国防部长彭德怀以及各代表团的顾问，专家和工作人员。
    波兰方面出席招待会的有波兰统一工人党中央委员会第一书记贝鲁特，国务委员会主席萨瓦茨基，部长会议第一副主席诺瓦克，部长会议副主席兼国防部长罗科索夫斯基，波兰统一工人党中央委员会书记奥哈布和马茹尔，部长会议副主席贝尔曼和约兹维亚克—韦托德，部长拉德凯维奇和萨姆布罗夫斯基等人以及波兰军队代表，各政党和社会团体的负责人和科学、文化、新闻界人士。
    出席招待会的还有各国驻波兰的外交使节和武官。
    招待会在非常热烈和友好的气氛中进行。
