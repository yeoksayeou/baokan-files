Title: Vice Premier Chen Yi Receives and Hosts Egyptian Minister of Religious Affairs and Others
Author:
Date: 1955-05-22
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, May 21st. Vice Premier of the State Council of the People's Republic of China, Chen Yi, received the Egyptian Minister of Religious Affairs, Bakur, and the Cairo University Professor of Constitutional Law, Kamel, at 6:00 PM on May 21st.

Present at the reception were He Chengxiang, Director of the Religious Affairs Bureau of the State Council of the People's Republic of China, and Xie Li, Deputy Director of the Department of West European and African Affairs of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs.

Xinhua News Agency, May 21st. Vice Premier of the State Council of the People's Republic of China, Chen Yi, hosted a banquet on the evening of May 21st in honor of the Egyptian Minister of Religious Affairs, Bakur, and the Cairo University Professor of Constitutional Law, Kamel.

Present as hosts were Ye Jizhuang, Minister of Foreign Trade of the People's Republic of China, Zhang Hanfu, Vice Minister of Foreign Affairs, Qian Junrui, Acting Minister of Culture, He Chengxiang, Director of the Religious Affairs Bureau of the State Council, Xie Li, Deputy Director of the Department of West European and African Affairs of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs, and Wang Zhuoru, Deputy Director of the Protocol Department.

Also present as host was Da Puchen, Vice Chairman of the China Islamic Association.

The banquet proceeded in a friendly atmosphere.

### 陈毅副总理接见并招待埃及宗教事务部长等

1955-05-22
第1版()
专栏：

　　陈毅副总理接见并招待埃及宗教事务部长等
    新华社二十一日讯　二十一日下午六时，中华人民共和国国务院副总理陈毅接见了埃及宗教事务部长巴库尔和开罗大学宪法教授卡迈尔。
    接见时在座的有中华人民共和国国务院宗教事务局局长何成湘，外交部西欧非洲司副司长谢黎。
    新华社二十一日讯　中华人民共和国国务院副总理陈毅在二十一日晚举行宴会，招待埃及宗教事务部长巴库尔和开罗大学宪法教授卡迈尔。
    出席作陪的有中华人民共和国对外贸易部部长叶季壮，外交部副部长章汉夫，文化部代理部长钱俊瑞，国务院宗教事务局局长何成湘，外交部西欧非洲司副司长谢黎、礼宾司副司长王倬如。
    出席作陪的还有中国伊斯兰教协会副主任达浦生。
    宴会在友好的空气中进行。
