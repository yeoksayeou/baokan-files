Title: Sino-Soviet Governmental Delegations Continue Talks; Soviet Governmental Delegation Attends President Tito's Welcome Banquet
Author:
Date: 1955-05-30
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency, Belgrade, May 28th, TASS dispatch: On the 28th, the government delegation of the Union of Soviet Socialist Republics headed by N.S. Khrushchev continued talks with the government delegation of the Federal People's Republic of Yugoslavia headed by Josip Broz Tito.

During the talks, both sides exchanged views on the future relations between the Federal People's Republic of Yugoslavia and the Union of Soviet Socialist Republics.

On May 29th, the Soviet delegation will make a special visit to the Brijuni Islands at the invitation of the government of the Federal People's Republic of Yugoslavia.

Xinhua News Agency, Belgrade, May 28th, TASS dispatch: On May 28th, the Soviet government delegation, headed by Khrushchev—member of the Presidium of the Supreme Soviet of the Union of Soviet Socialist Republics, and First Secretary of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of the Soviet Union—attended the welcome banquet hosted by President Tito of the Federal People's Republic of Yugoslavia.

The Soviet government delegation visited the Boris Kidrič Institute in Vinča, near Belgrade.

In the evening, the Soviet government delegation attended a reception held by President Tito at the White Palace.

### 苏南两国政府代表团的会谈继续进行　苏联政府代表团出席铁托总统的欢迎宴会

1955-05-30
第4版()
专栏：

　　苏南两国政府代表团的会谈继续进行
　　苏联政府代表团出席铁托总统的欢迎宴会
    新华社二十九日讯　塔斯社贝尔格莱德二十八日讯：二十八日，以尼·谢·赫鲁晓夫为首的苏维埃社会主义共和国联盟政府代表团同以约·布·铁托为首的南斯拉夫联邦人民共和国政府代表团继续会谈。
    会谈时，双方就南斯拉夫联邦人民共和国同苏维埃社会主义共和国联盟的今后关系问题互相交换了意见。
    五月二十九日，苏联代表团将应南斯拉夫联邦人民共和国政府的邀请到布莱俄尼岛作一次特别访问。
    新华社二十九日讯　塔斯社贝尔格莱德二十八日讯：五月二十八日，以苏维埃社会主义共和国联盟最高苏维埃主席团委员、苏联共产党中央委员会第一书记赫鲁晓夫为首的苏联政府代表团出席了南斯拉夫联邦人民共和国总统铁托欢迎他们的宴会。
    苏联政府代表团参观了贝尔格莱德附近的在文奇的鲍里斯·基德里奇学院。
    晚上，苏联政府代表团出席了铁托总统在白宫举行的招待会。
