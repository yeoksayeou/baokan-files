    Our Great Motherland
    Many outstanding employees from factories and mines across our country are studying at worker-peasant rapid middle schools. They are not only improving their political and cultural understanding, but also further acquiring technical knowledge. Students at the Huadong Coal Mine Workers' Rapid Middle School, located near Mount Tai in Shandong Province, are conducting physics experiments in the laboratory.

### 我们伟大的祖国（图片）

1955-05-17
第1版()
专栏：

    我们伟大的祖国
    我国各地厂矿的许多优秀职工在工农速成中学学习。他们不仅提高了政治和文化水平，还进一步获得了技术知识。设在山东省泰山附近的华东煤矿工人速成中学的学生，正在实验室中进行物理试验。
