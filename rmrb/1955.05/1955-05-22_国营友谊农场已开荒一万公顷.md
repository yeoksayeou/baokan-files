Title: State-owned Friendship Farm Reclaims 10,000 Hectares of Wasteland
Author:
Date: 1955-05-22
Page: 1

　　Xinhua News Agency, Harbin, 21st: As of May 20th, the State-owned Friendship Farm has reclaimed 10,000 hectares of wasteland in nineteen days and nights, exceeding 45% of this year's planned reclamation area. This reclamation speed is much faster than planned. The Seventh Production Team of the Fourth Branch Farm has completed over 57% of this year's planned reclamation area, and the quality basically meets the requirements.
    Currently, all employees of the State-owned Friendship Farm are full of confidence in striving to complete the 21,999-hectare wasteland reclamation plan ahead of schedule to welcome the birthday of the Communist Party of China – July 1st.

### 国营友谊农场已开荒一万公顷

1955-05-22
第1版()
专栏：

　　国营友谊农场已开荒一万公顷
    新华社哈尔滨二十一日电　截至五月二十日为止，国营友谊农场已在十九个昼夜中开垦荒地一万公顷，为今年计划开荒面积百分之四十五强。这个开荒速度比计划所要求的快了许多。第四分场第七生产队已完成今年计划开荒面积百分之五十七强，质量基本上合乎要求。
    现在，国营友谊农场全体职工正信心百倍地争取提前完成两万一千九百九十九公顷的开荒计划，以迎接中国共产党的生日——七月一日。
