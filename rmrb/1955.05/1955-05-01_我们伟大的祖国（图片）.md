    Our Great Motherland
    Workers at the large rolling mill of Anshan Iron and Steel Company are striving to complete the annual task of increasing the production of heavy steel rails by 9,500 tons. A large quantity of heavy steel rails produced by this mill this year has supplied the needs of new railway construction projects in various regions. This is a corner of the large rolling mill.

### 我们伟大的祖国（图片）

1955-05-01
第1版()
专栏：

    我们伟大的祖国
    鞍山钢铁公司所属大型轧钢厂的职工正在为完成全年增产九千五百吨重型钢轨而努力。这个厂今年生产的大批重型钢轨，供应了各地铁路新建工程的需要。这是大型轧钢厂的一角。
