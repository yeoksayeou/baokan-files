Title: Indian Parliamentary Delegation Returns to Moscow After Visiting Various Parts of the Soviet Union; Voroshilov, Molotov, and Zhukov Receive the Delegation
Author:
Date: 1955-05-30
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, May 29th: According to TASS Moscow, the Chairman of the Presidium of the Supreme Soviet of the Soviet Union, Voroshilov, the First Deputy Chairman of the Council of Ministers of the Soviet Union and Minister of Foreign Affairs of the Soviet Union, Molotov, and the Minister of Defense of the Soviet Union, Zhukov, received the Indian Parliamentary Delegation headed by Mr. Krishnamurthy Rao, Deputy Chairman of the Indian Council of States, in the Kremlin on the 28th. Also present at the reception were Begov, Secretary of the Presidium of the Supreme Soviet of the Soviet Union, and Gorkin, Deputy Secretary of the Presidium of the Supreme Soviet of the Soviet Union. They had a conversation lasting more than an hour in a warm and friendly atmosphere.

	Voroshilov first extended a warm welcome to the delegation on behalf of the Supreme Soviet of the Soviet Union and asked them to share their impressions of their visit to the Soviet Union.

	Mr. Rao expressed his gratitude for the warm reception the delegation received during their stay in the Soviet Union. He said: We met people everywhere who talked about peace. We also hope for peace, and we came here as friends without any prejudice. From what we have seen, it is clear that the Soviet people want to live in peace and in friendship with the people of other countries. Delegation member Lal expressed his belief that the Soviet government's initiative in reducing armaments, banning nuclear weapons, and concluding the Austrian State Treaty would have a positive impact on the international situation. Mr. Rao finally pointed out that the delegation's visit will further strengthen the friendly relations between the people of India and the Soviet Union and will help improve the international situation. He asked that his warm congratulations and best wishes be conveyed to Bulganin and Khrushchev on his own behalf and on behalf of the delegation.

	Molotov, referring to the speeches made by the Indian representatives during the conversation, pointed out the importance of the friendly relations between the Soviet Union and India for safeguarding world peace and stressed the need to further develop and consolidate this friendship. He said, we have no doubt that on the main issue of peace, nothing can separate the Soviet people and the Indian people. He expressed the hope that India and the Soviet Union would follow a common path of peace to maintain peace.

	Zhukov said that the Soviet people, who made huge sacrifices in the last war, will never allow war to break out again. The Soviet people, recognizing their own strength and not fearing any threats, believe that peace is the only way to solve all international problems.

	Finally, Voroshilov, on behalf of the Supreme Soviet of the Soviet Union and the Soviet government, expressed his gratitude for the friendly words of the delegation members when they spoke of the Soviet people. He pointed out that the Soviet people who have visited India have all talked about many interesting things about India and have sincerely praised the great and industrious Indian people. Undoubtedly, as a result of this mutual contact, the relations between our two countries will develop into a true and deep friendship. Voroshilov wished the delegation members a safe journey home and asked them to convey sincere greetings to Indian President Prasad, Prime Minister Nehru, the Indian Parliament, and the great Indian people.

Xinhua News Agency, Moscow: According to TASS, the Indian Parliamentary Delegation, led by Krishnamurthy Rao, Deputy Chairman of the Indian Council of States, which was visiting the Soviet Union at the invitation of the Supreme Soviet of the Soviet Union, returned to Moscow on the 27th after visiting various parts of the Soviet Union.

	After arriving in Moscow on May 7, the delegation visited Moscow for three days and left Moscow for other parts of the Soviet Union on May 10. They visited Stalingrad, Akmolinsk, Alma-Ata, Stalinabad, Tbilisi, Sochi, Kiev, Leningrad, and other places.

### 印度国会代表团在苏联各地参观访问后回到莫斯科　伏罗希洛夫、莫洛托夫、朱可夫接见代表团

1955-05-30
第4版()
专栏：

　　印度国会代表团在苏联各地参观访问后回到莫斯科
  　伏罗希洛夫、莫洛托夫、朱可夫接见代表团
    新华社二十九日讯　据塔斯社莫斯科讯：苏联最高苏维埃主席团主席伏罗希洛夫，苏联部长会议第一副主席、苏联外交部长莫洛托夫和苏联国防部长朱可夫，二十八日在克里姆林宫接见了以印度联邦院副议长克里什纳穆蒂·拉奥先生为首的印度国会代表团。接见时在座的还有苏联最高苏维埃主席团秘书别哥夫，苏联最高苏维埃主席团副秘书戈尔金。他们在热烈、友好的气氛下进行了一个多小时的交谈。
    伏罗希洛夫首先代表苏联最高苏维埃热烈欢迎代表团，并请他们谈谈访苏观感。
    拉奥先生对代表团在苏联逗留期间受到的热情接待表示感谢。他说：我们在任何地方都遇到谈论和平的人。我们也希望和平，我们是作为没有任何偏见的朋友到这里来的。从我们所看到的事情中可以清楚地看出，苏联人民希望在和平中、在同其他各国人民的友谊中生活。代表团团员拉尔表示相信，苏联政府在裁减军备、禁止核子武器和缔结奥地利国家条约方面的主动行动将对国际局势产生积极的影响。拉奥先生最后指出，代表团的访问将进一步加强印苏两国人民之间的友好关系，并且有助于改善国际局势。他要求以他自己的名义并且代表代表团向布尔加宁和赫鲁晓夫致印度人民的热烈祝贺和最好的祝愿。
    莫洛托夫就印度代表们在交谈中的发言指出苏联和印度之间的友好关系对保卫世界和平的重要意义，并指出了进一步发展和巩固这种友谊的必要性。他说，我们毫不怀疑，在和平这个主要问题上，没有什么能使苏联人民和印度人民分开。他表示，希望印度和苏联遵循一个共同的和平道路来维护和平。
    朱可夫说，在上次大战中作了巨大牺牲的苏联人民决不允许战争再起。认识到自己的力量而且不害怕任何威胁的苏联人民，认为和平是解决一切国际问题的唯一办法。
    最后，伏罗希洛夫代表苏联最高苏维埃和苏联政府对代表团的团员们谈到苏联人民时的友好言辞表示感谢。他指出，访问过印度的苏联人都曾谈到关于印度的许多有趣的事情，并且都由衷地称赞伟大的、勤劳的印度人民。无疑的，由于这种相互接触的结果，我们两国间的关系将发展成为真实的、深厚的友谊。伏罗希洛夫祝代表团人员归国途中一路平安。并且要求他们代为向印度总统普拉沙德先生、总理尼赫鲁先生、印度国会和伟大的印度人民致诚挚的问候。
    据新华社讯　据塔斯社消息：应苏联最高苏维埃的邀请到苏联访问的印度国会代表团，由印度国会联邦院副议长克里什纳穆蒂·拉奥率领，在苏联各地参观访问后，已于二十七日回到莫斯科。
      代表团自五月七日抵达莫斯科后，在莫斯科进行了三天的参观访问，于五月十日离开莫斯科到苏联其他地区。他们先后访问了斯大林格勒、阿克摩林斯克、阿拉木图、斯大林纳巴德、第比利斯、索契、基辅、列宁格勒等地。
