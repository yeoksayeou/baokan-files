Title: People from All Walks of Life in Beijing Hold a Meeting to Welcome Prime Minister Sastroamidjojo
Author: 
Date: 1955-05-29
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, May 28th—People from all walks of life in Beijing held a grand rally at Zhongshan Park on the afternoon of the 28th to welcome the Prime Minister of the Republic of Indonesia, Ali Sastroamidjojo, and his wife, as well as other personnel accompanying him on his visit to China. The welcome rally was presided over by Peng Zhen, the Mayor of Beijing.
　　More than 16,000 people participated in this grand welcome meeting.
　　The venue was set up inside the Zhongshan Park Music Hall. The Indonesian and Chinese national flags were hung on the canopy of the rostrum. On the front of the entrance to the venue and on the wall opposite the rostrum, huge slogans written in Chinese and Indonesian, "Long Live Peace" and "Long Live Chinese-Indonesian Friendship," were hung respectively. The venue was filled and crowded with more than 4,000 male and female workers, students, staff, and citizens from factories, schools, organizations, groups, and streets. The rest of Zhongshan Park was also decorated for the festival, filled with a warm, joyful, and friendly atmosphere, with hundreds of thousands of people gathered there listening to the broadcast of the welcome meeting and participating in the park activities.
　　At 7:00 PM, Prime Minister Ali Sastroamidjojo and his wife arrived at the South Gate of Zhongshan Park by car with Premier Zhou Enlai. Accompanied by welcome music, they were greeted by Peng Zhen, the Mayor of Beijing, and other Beijing officials as they entered the venue. At this moment, a storm of applause and cheers erupted in the welcoming crowd lining the streets.
　　At 7:14 PM, Prime Minister Ali Sastroamidjojo and his wife, accompanied by Premier Zhou Enlai, Vice Premier Chen Yi, and Mayor Peng Zhen, ascended the rostrum. The entire audience stood up, and sustained applause erupted once again. At this moment, Prime Minister Ali Sastroamidjojo and his wife accepted bouquets of flowers presented by children from the capital.
　　Also ascending the rostrum with Prime Minister Ali Sastroamidjojo were Mr. Mononutu, Mr. Sukardjo Wirjopranoto and his wife, Mr. Bala, Mr. Subandrio, Mr. Djuanda, and Mr. Hasuno Reksoamojo.
　　Also on the rostrum were Xi Zhongxun, Secretary-General of the State Council of the People's Republic of China; Zhang Youyu, Wu Han, Xue Zizheng, Feng Jiping, Jia Tingsan, and Le Songsheng, Deputy Mayors of Beijing; Liu Ren, Chairman of the Beijing Committee of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference; Jiang Guangnai, Yu Xinqing, and Ling Qijun, Vice Chairmen; Zhang Xiruo, Chairman of the Beijing Branch of the Chinese People's Committee for World Peace; and other heads of people's organizations in Beijing.
　　At the beginning of the welcome meeting, the band played the national anthems of the Republic of Indonesia and China.
　　Mayor Peng Zhen first delivered a speech (full text to be published separately). He extended a warm welcome to Prime Minister Ali Sastroamidjojo on behalf of all the citizens of Beijing and, through him, extended his heartfelt respect to the citizens of Jakarta and all the people of Indonesia.
　　Prime Minister Ali Sastroamidjojo then delivered a reply (full text to be published separately). When he finished speaking, the audience applauded warmly.
　　Amidst music, Mayor Peng Zhen presented a banner and gifts to Prime Minister Ali Sastroamidjojo on behalf of the people of Beijing.
　　At 7:45 PM, the welcome meeting concluded amidst warm applause. Afterward, Prime Minister Ali Sastroamidjojo, accompanied by Premier Zhou Enlai, Vice Premier Chen Yi, and Mayor Peng Zhen, participated in the park activities.

### 北京市各界人民举行大会　欢迎沙斯特罗阿米佐约总理

1955-05-29
第1版()
专栏：

　　北京市各界人民举行大会
　　欢迎沙斯特罗阿米佐约总理
    新华社二十八日讯　北京市各界人民二十八日下午在中山公园隆重举行欢迎大会，欢迎印度尼西亚共和国总理阿里·沙斯特罗阿米佐约和夫人以及其他随同来华访问的人员。欢迎大会由北京市市长彭真主持。
    有一万六千多人参加了这个盛大的欢迎会。
    会场设在中山公园音乐堂内。主席台的天幕上悬挂着印度尼西亚和中国国旗。在会场入口处正面和主席台对面的墙壁上分别挂着用中文、印度尼西亚文写的“和平万岁”、“中国印度尼西亚友好万岁”的巨幅标语。会场里坐满和站满了来自工厂、学校、机关、团体和街道的男女工人、学生、工作人员和市民共四千多人。在中山公园其他各处也都披上节日盛装，到处洋溢着热烈、欢乐和友谊的气氛，成千上万的人们聚集在那里静听欢迎大会的广播和参加游园。
    下午七时，阿里·沙斯特罗阿米佐约总理和夫人同周恩来总理一起乘车到达中山公园的南门后，在欢迎曲声中由北京市市长彭真和北京市其他负责人迎接他们步入会场。这时，在夹道欢迎的群众行列中，响起了暴风雨般的掌声和欢呼声。
    七时十四分，阿里·沙斯特罗阿米佐约总理和夫人由周恩来总理、陈毅副总理和彭真市长陪同登上主席台。全场起立，再一次响起了经久不息的掌声。这时，阿里·沙斯特罗阿米佐约总理和夫人接受了首都儿童献给的鲜花。
    随同阿里·沙斯特罗阿米佐约总理登上主席台的还有莫诺努图先生、苏卡佐·维约普拉诺托先生和夫人、巴拉先生、苏班德里奥先生、朱安达先生和哈苏诺·雷克梭阿莫佐先生。
    在主席台上的还有中华人民共和国国务院秘书长习仲勋，北京市副市长张友渔、吴晗、薛子正、冯基平、贾庭三、乐松生，中国人民政治协商会议北京市委员会主席刘仁，副主席蒋光鼐、余心清、凌其峻，中国人民保卫世界和平委员会北京市分会主席张奚若和北京市其他各人民团体负责人。
    欢迎大会开始时，乐队奏印度尼西亚共和国和中国国歌。
    彭真市长首先致词（全文另发）。他代表北京市全体市民向阿里·沙斯特罗阿米佐约总理表示热烈的欢迎，并通过他向雅加达市民和印度尼西亚全体人民表示衷心的敬意。
    阿里·沙斯特罗阿米佐约总理接着致答词（全文另发）。在他讲话结束时，全场热烈鼓掌。
    在乐声中，彭真市长代表北京市各界人民向阿里·沙斯特罗阿米佐约总理献旗并赠送礼品。
    七时四十五分，欢迎大会在热烈的掌声中结束。接着，阿里·沙斯特罗阿米佐约总理由周恩来总理、陈毅副总理和彭真市长陪同参加游园。
