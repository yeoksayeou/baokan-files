Title: World Peace Council Secretariat Issues Communiqué Pointing Out That the Swift Conclusion of an Austrian State Treaty Will Contribute to the Cause of Peace in Europe
Author:
Date: 1955-05-01
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Vienna, 30th Dispatch from TASS: The Secretariat of the World Peace Council issued a communiqué on the issue of concluding an Austrian State Treaty on the 28th. The communiqué pointed out that the swift conclusion of an Austrian State Treaty will once again prove the correctness of the principle of consultation.

The communiqué said, "Today, we welcome the decision of the Republic of Austria not to participate in any military alliances and not to allow the establishment of military bases on its territory. This decision is a significant contribution to the cause of peace in Europe."

The communiqué said that the conclusion of the Austrian State Treaty will be a new victory in the struggle of the people of all countries for peace.

### 世界和平理事会书记处发表公报指出　迅速缔结对奥和约将有助于欧洲和平事业

1955-05-01
第4版()
专栏：

　　世界和平理事会书记处发表公报指出
　　迅速缔结对奥和约将有助于欧洲和平事业
    新华社三十日讯　据塔斯社维也纳讯：世界和平理事会书记处二十八日发表关于缔结对奥和约问题的公报。公报指出，对奥和约的迅速缔结，将又一次证明协商原则的正确性。
    公报说，我们今天欢迎奥地利共和国关于不参加任何军事联盟和不容许在自己的领土上建立军事基地的决定。这个决定是对欧洲和平事业的重大贡献。
    公报说，对奥和约的缔结，将是各国人民争取和平斗争的一个新胜利。
