Title: Four New Oil Wells in Xinjiang Transferred to Production
Author:
Date: 1955-05-19
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency

　　Drilling workers in the Dushanzi mining area of the Xinjiang Petroleum Company recently handed over four newly drilled oil wells to the oil extraction department. One of the new wells, after oil testing and installation of oil production equipment, has been officially put into production.

　　In addition to adding some new oil wells this year, the Dushanzi mining area also plans to drill several deep exploratory wells to provide resources for large-scale oilfield exploitation in the future. (Xinhua News Agency)

### 新疆四口新油井移交生产

1955-05-19
第1版()
专栏：

　　新疆四口新油井移交生产
    新疆石油公司独山子矿区的钻井工人，最近把钻成的四口新油井移交给采油部门。其中一口新井经过试油和安装出油设备后，已经正式投入生产。
    独山子矿区今年除增加一些新的油井以外，还计划钻凿几口深探井，为今后大量开采油田提供资源。　（新华社）
