Title: Premier Zhou Enlai and Others Visit Czechoslovak Exhibition
Author:
Date: 1955-05-25
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, May 24th: Premier of the State Council of the People's Republic of China, Zhou Enlai, and heads of various ministries and commissions visited the Exhibition of Achievements of Ten Years of Socialist Construction in Czechoslovakia on the 24th.

Czechoslovak Ambassador to China, Mr. Gregor, and the Director of the Exhibition of Achievements of Ten Years of Socialist Construction in Czechoslovakia, Mr. Pok, accompanied them on the visit. Premier Zhou Enlai and others visited all parts of the exhibition.

At 5 p.m. on the same afternoon, diplomatic envoys and officials from various countries stationed in China also visited the exhibition. After the visit, they watched a Czechoslovak film.

### 周恩来总理等参观捷克斯洛伐克展览会

1955-05-25
第1版()
专栏：

　　周恩来总理等参观捷克斯洛伐克展览会
    新华社二十四日讯　中华人民共和国国务院总理周恩来和各部、各委员会负责人在二十四日参观了捷克斯洛伐克十年社会主义建设成就展览会。
    捷克斯洛伐克驻我国大使格里哥尔和捷克斯洛伐克十年社会主义建设成就展览会主任波克陪同参观。周恩来总理等参观了展览会各个部分。
    同日下午五时，各国驻华使节及外交官员也参观了展览会。参观后，并观看了捷克斯洛伐克电影。
