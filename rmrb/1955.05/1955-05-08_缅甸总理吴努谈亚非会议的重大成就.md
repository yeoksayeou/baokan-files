Title: Burmese Prime Minister U Nu Discusses the Significant Achievements of the Asian-African Conference
Author:
Date: 1955-05-08
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Rangoon, May 7th: The Rangoon "New Light of Burma" published a report on the 3rd stating that Burmese Prime Minister U Nu, in a report on the Asian-African Conference at a regular cabinet meeting on the 2nd, said that the Asian-African Conference symbolized the unity of the countries of Asia and Africa, and the conference would become a glorious page in history. He said that the Asian-African Conference also promoted friendship and economic cooperation among countries and opened up the path of mutual trade.

### 缅甸总理吴努谈亚非会议的重大成就

1955-05-08
第4版()
专栏：

　　缅甸总理吴努谈亚非会议的重大成就
    新华社七日讯　仰光消息：缅甸“新仰光报”三日刊载消息说，缅甸联邦总理吴努二日在内阁例会上作关于亚非会议的报告时说，亚非会议象征着亚非两大洲国家的团结，会议将成为历史上光荣的一页。他说，亚非会议还促进了各国间的友好和经济合作，开辟了相互贸易的道路。
