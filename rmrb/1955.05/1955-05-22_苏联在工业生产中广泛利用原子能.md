Title: The Soviet Union Widely Utilizes Atomic Energy in Industrial Production
Author:
Date: 1955-05-22
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, 21st. Correspondent of TASS, Moscow: Yemelyanov, Corresponding Academician of the Soviet Academy of Sciences, published an article in "Izvestia" on the 20th, discussing the achievements of the Soviet Union in the peaceful utilization of atomic energy in industry.

He said that the Soviet Union is manufacturing a large number of various radioactive isotopes, which are used to inspect industrial production processes, promote production automation, and conduct extremely fine chemical analysis.

The Soviet Union is now using radioactive isotopes to inspect the thickness of various workpieces, welds, and various metal coatings. For example, radioactive isotopes have been used to inspect welds in the laying of the gas pipeline from Dasava to Kiev and in many other large projects. When inspecting the thickness of workpieces, the inspection can be carried out during the manufacturing process of the workpiece. This prevents the production linkage machine from stopping due to measurement and prevents defective products.

Using radioactive isotopes to control and adjust technical operations can create comprehensive production automation. For example, radioactive isotopes can be used to automate the adjustment work of rolling mills and automatically adjust the size of the gap between the rolls, so that the flat steel or steel plate rolled out by the rolling mill has a high degree of precise thickness. In order to develop the automatic operation of the production process, the Soviet Union has now used radioactive isotopes to manufacture completely new automatic technical inspection instruments. With this instrument, the device or part of the device can be seen through, so that the movement of liquids, granules, and gases inside the device, as well as changes in pressure and density, can be continuously monitored.

In organic chemistry and inorganic chemistry, radioactive isotopes can be used to study various chemical processes that could not be studied by other methods before. "Tracer" atoms have now been widely used to study the complex chemical technology process of manufacturing rubber. In the petroleum industry, radioactive polonium can be used to determine the oil content of oil layers. Studies have shown that when processing petroleum, irradiating petroleum with a strong ray flow of gamma rays from radioactive cobalt can obtain new petroleum products.

The use of radioactive isotopes is of great use for studying heat-resistant alloys. For example, if radioactive iron is placed in ordinary iron, you can see how the radioactive iron atoms move, how they change, and where they are. This provides a very wide range of possibilities for creating new alloys and new compounds.

### 苏联在工业生产中广泛利用原子能

1955-05-22
第4版()
专栏：

　　苏联在工业生产中广泛利用原子能
    新华社二十一日讯　据塔斯社莫斯科讯：苏联科学院通讯院士叶麦利雅诺夫二十日在“消息报”上发表了一篇文章，论述苏联在工业方面和平利用原子能的成就。
    他说，苏联正在制造大量的各种放射性同位素，用来检查工业生产过程，促进生产自动化，并进行极细微的化学分析。
    苏联现在正利用放射性同位素来检查各种不同厚度的制件、焊缝和各种金属涂料的厚度。例如，在敷设从达萨瓦到基辅的煤气管以及在进行其他许多大工程中就已利用放射性同位素来检查焊缝。在检查制件的厚度时，可以在制件的制造过程中进行检查，这就可以不因为测量而使生产联动机停顿下来，并可以防止出废品。
    利用放射性同位素来控制和调整技术操作过程可以造成全盘的生产自动化。例如，利用放射性同位素可以使轧钢机的调整工作自动化，可以自动调整轧辊之间的间隙的大小，使得轧钢机轧出的扁钢或钢板具有高度精确的厚度。为了发展生产过程的自动操纵，苏联现在已利用放射性同位素制造成完全新型的自动技术检查仪器。利用这种仪器可以透视装置或装置的一部分，从而可以不断地监视装置内部液体、散粒体、煤气的运动以及压力和密度的变化等。
    在有机化学和无机化学方面可以利用放射性同位素来研究各种以前用其他方法无法研究的化学过程。现在已利用“示踪”原子广泛研究制造橡皮的复杂的化学技术过程。在石油工业方面，利用放射性钋可以测定油层的含油量。研究证明：在对石油进行加工时，用放射性钴的丙种射线的很强的射线流来辐射石油，可以获得新的石油产品。
    利用放射性同位素对于研究耐热合金有极大的作用。例如，把放射性的铁放在普通铁中，就可以看到放射性的铁原子怎样运动，怎样变化以及它是在什么地方。这样就为创造新的合金、新的化合物提供了极广泛的可能性。
