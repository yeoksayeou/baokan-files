Title: British General Election Results Announced: Conservative Party Wins Majority of Seats
Author:
Date: 1955-05-29
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, London, May 28th—The results of the British parliamentary election have been announced. The Conservative Party has won a majority of seats in Parliament and will therefore continue to govern for the next five years.

Based on the election results from 629 of the 630 constituencies, the Conservative Party won 344 seats, the Labour Party won 277 seats, the Liberal Party 6 seats, and other parties 2 seats.

Although the British Communist Party candidates did not win any seats in the election, the number of votes they received under extremely difficult conditions increased by nearly 50 percent compared to the last election.

### 英国大选结果揭晓  保守党获得多数议席

1955-05-29
第4版()
专栏：

　　英国大选结果揭晓
　  保守党获得多数议席
    新华社二十八日讯　伦敦消息：英国议会选举结果已经揭晓。保守党获得议会的多数席位，因此将在未来五年中继续执政。
    根据六百三十个选区中六百二十九个选区的选举结果，保守党获得三百四十四席，工党获得二百七十七席，自由党六席，其他党派两席。
    英国共产党候选人在选举中虽然没有得到议席，但是他们在极端困难的条件下所获得的票数比上次大选增加了近百分之五十。
