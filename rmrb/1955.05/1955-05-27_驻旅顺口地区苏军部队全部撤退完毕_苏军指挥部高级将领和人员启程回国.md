Title: Soviet Troops Stationed in the Lüshunkou Area Completely Withdraw; Senior Soviet Military Commanders and Personnel Depart for Home

Author: 
Date: 1955-05-27
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Lüda, May 26th. The senior commanders and all personnel of the Soviet military headquarters stationed in the Lüshunkou area departed from Lüshun at 4:00 PM on May 26th to return home.

By this point, all Soviet troops stationed in the Lüshunkou area have completely withdrawn from the Lüshunkou area.

Gan Siqi, representative of the Chinese People's Liberation Army, accompanied the Soviet military headquarters personnel to our national border. Yu Guying, Deputy Mayor of Lüda City, also accompanied them to the border.

Song Li, Second Secretary of the CCP Lüda Municipal Committee and Mayor of Lüda City, Hu Zhonghai, Deputy Secretary of the CCP Lüda Municipal Committee, Qiao Chuanjue, President of the Lüda Sino-Soviet Friendship Association, and representatives of the Chinese People's Liberation Army stationed in the Lüshunkou area, among others, accompanied the Soviet military headquarters personnel from Lüshun to Shihe Railway Station on the border of Lüda City. Belyanov, the Soviet Consul General in Dalian, also accompanied the Soviet military headquarters personnel to Shihe Railway Station.

### 驻旅顺口地区苏军部队全部撤退完毕  苏军指挥部高级将领和人员启程回国

1955-05-27
第1版()
专栏：

　　驻旅顺口地区苏军部队全部撤退完毕
　  苏军指挥部高级将领和人员启程回国
    新华社旅大二十六日电　驻旅顺口地区苏军指挥部的高级将领和指挥部全体人员，已在五月二十六日下午四时从旅顺启程回国。
    至此，驻旅顺口地区苏军部队已全部从旅顺口地区撤退完毕。
    中国人民解放军代表甘泗淇陪送苏军指挥部人员到我国国境。陪送到国境的还有旅大市副市长于谷莺。
    中共旅大市委员会第二书记、旅大市市长宋黎，中共旅大市委员会副书记胡忠海，旅大市中苏友好协会会长乔传珏，以及中国人民解放军驻旅顺口地区部队代表等，由旅顺陪送苏军指挥部人员到旅大市边界石河车站。陪送苏军指挥部人员到石河车站的还有苏联驻大连总领事别良诺夫。
