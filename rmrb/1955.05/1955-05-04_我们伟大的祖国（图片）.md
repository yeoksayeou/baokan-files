Author:
Date: 1955-05-04
Page: 1

    Our Great Motherland
　　The First Youth Production Group of the Third Work Section of the Machine Assembly Workshop of the Yangfeng Tool Factory has completed or overfulfilled the state production plan every month for the past four years. This year, the workers of this group completed the first quarter's production plan fourteen days ahead of schedule, and proposed to reduce working hours by 9 percent in the second quarter and strive to eliminate defective products.

### 我们伟大的祖国（图片）

1955-05-04
第1版()
专栏：

    我们伟大的祖国
　　阳风动工具厂机装车间三工段第一青年生产小组，四年来都逐月完成或超额完成了国家生产计划。今年这个小组的工人又提前十四天完成了第一季度的生产计划，并提出在第二季度内要压缩工时百分之九，争取消灭废品。
