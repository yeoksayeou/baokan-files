Title: Soviet Government Delegation Arrives in Warsaw
Author:
Date: 1955-05-12
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Warsaw, 11th Dispatch from TASS: The Soviet delegation attending the second meeting of the European countries on guaranteeing European peace and security, led by the head of the delegation, Chairman of the Council of Ministers of the Soviet Union, N.A. Bulganin, arrived in Warsaw by plane on the 10th. The members of the delegation include: First Deputy Chairman of the Council of Ministers of the Soviet Union and Minister of Foreign Affairs of the Soviet Union, V.M. Molotov, Minister of National Defence of the Soviet Union, G.K. Zhukov (already arrived in Warsaw from Berlin), Marshal of the Soviet Union I.S. Konev, Chairman of the Council of Ministers of the Russian Federation A.M. Puzanov, Chairman of the Council of Ministers of the Ukrainian Republic N.T. Kalchenko, Chairman of the Council of Ministers of the Byelorussian Republic K.T. Mazurov, Chairman of the Council of Ministers of the Latvian Republic V.T. Latsis, Chairman of the Council of Ministers of the Lithuanian Republic M.A. Gedvilas, Chairman of the Council of Ministers of the Estonian Republic A.A. Müürisepp, Deputy Minister of Foreign Affairs of the Soviet Union V.A. Zorin, and Soviet Ambassador to Poland P.K. Ponomarenko.
　　
The delegation was greeted at the Central Airport in Warsaw by: First Secretary of the Central Committee of the Polish United Workers' Party, B. Bierut, Chairman of the State Council of the Polish People's Republic, A. Zawadzki, Chairman of the Council of Ministers, J. Cyrankiewicz, Deputy Chairman of the Council of Ministers and Minister of National Defence, Marshal of Poland K. Rokossowski, and Polish Minister of Foreign Affairs S. Skrzeszewski, etc.
　　
Also greeting the delegation were the heads and members of the delegations from various countries who had arrived in Warsaw earlier to attend the meeting, diplomatic envoys of several countries stationed in Poland, and representatives of the Polish and foreign press.
At the airport, the national flags of the Soviet Union and Poland were flying, and a guard of honor was lined up.

Bulganin made a statement at the airport, saying: We have come here as the Soviet delegation participating in the European countries' meeting on guaranteeing European peace and security, to work together with the representatives of the participating countries to accomplish an important task aimed at further strengthening the security of our countries.

Although the struggle of the people of all countries to ease international tensions has achieved some success, the situation in some parts of the world has recently been causing serious concern.

The peace-loving countries of Europe can no longer remain indifferent to such a situation. The current situation requires us to work together and take new measures to strengthen our defense capabilities. The Soviet government hopes that these tasks will be completed at the upcoming meeting, and hopes that the work of the participating countries will make a significant contribution to strengthening world peace and the security of all countries.

### 苏联政府代表团到达华沙

1955-05-12
第1版()
专栏：

　　苏联政府代表团到达华沙
    新华社十一日讯　据塔斯社华沙十日讯：参加欧洲国家保障欧洲和平和安全会议第二次会议的苏联代表团，在代表团团长、苏联部长会议主席尼·亚·布尔加宁率领下，于十日乘飞机到达华沙。代表团团员有：苏联部长会议第一副主席兼苏联外交部长维·米·莫洛托夫，苏联国防部部长格·康·朱可夫（已由柏林抵华沙），苏联元帅伊·斯·科涅夫，俄罗斯联邦部长会议主席亚·麦·普扎诺夫，乌克兰共和国部长会议主席尼·特·卡尔钦科，白俄罗斯共和国部长会议主席克·特·马楚罗夫，拉脱维亚共和国部长会议主席维·杰·拉齐斯，立陶宛共和国部长会议主席麦·亚·盖德维拉斯，爱沙尼亚共和国部长会议主席阿·亚·缪里谢普，苏联外交部副部长瓦·亚·佐林，苏联驻波兰大使潘·康·波诺马连科。
    在华沙中央机场迎接代表团的有：波兰统一工人党中央委员会第一书记波·贝鲁特，波兰人民共和国国务委员会主席亚·萨瓦茨基，部长会议主席约·西伦凯维兹，部长会议副主席兼国防部长、波兰元帅康·罗科索夫斯基，波兰外交部长斯·斯克热歇夫斯基等。
    迎接代表团的还有先期到华沙参加会议的各国代表团的团长和团员，若干国家驻波兰外交使节，以及波兰和外国报界代表。机场上
          悬挂着苏联和波兰的国旗，排列着仪仗队。
    布尔加宁在机场发表声明说：我们以苏联参加欧洲国家保障欧洲和平和安全会议的代表团的资格来到这里，是要同与会国家的代表们一道，完成一件目的在于进一步加强我们国家的安全的重大工作。
    虽然各国人民争取缓和国际紧张局势的斗争已获得了某些成功，但是世界某些地区的局势最近一直在引起严重的忧虑。
    欧洲各爱好和平的国家不能继续对这样一种局势漠不关心了。目前的局势要求我们各国共同努力，采取新的措施来加强我们的防御能力。苏联政府希望，在即将举行的会议上将完成这些任务，并且希望与会各国的工作将对加强世界和平和各国安全作出重大贡献。
