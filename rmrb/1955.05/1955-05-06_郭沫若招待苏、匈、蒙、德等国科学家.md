Title: Guo Moruo Hosts Scientists from the Soviet Union, Hungary, Mongolia, and Germany

Author:
Date: 1955-05-06
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, May 5th: Guo Moruo, President of the Chinese Academy of Sciences, hosted a banquet on the 5th to welcome the visiting delegation from the Soviet Academy of Sciences and scientists from Hungary, Mongolia, and the German Democratic Republic.

Attending the banquet were: Academician Bardin, head of the Soviet Academy of Sciences delegation, and all members of the delegation; Academician Ligeti, Vice President of the Hungarian Academy of Sciences; Nachagdaldorj, Vice Chairman of the Mongolian Scientific Committee; and Academician Gerisch and Professor Elkes, renowned scientists from the German Democratic Republic.

Also present were: Chen Boda, Zhang Jiafu, Zhu Kezhen, Wu Youxun, Vice Presidents of the Chinese Academy of Sciences, heads of various departments, the Secretariat, and various bureaus, and more than 30 relevant scientists.

Also present were: Kovda and Rafikov, advisors to the Chinese Academy of Sciences.

At the meeting, Guo Moruo, President of the Chinese Academy of Sciences, delivered a speech expressing gratitude for the friendly visits and selfless assistance from scientists from the Soviet Union and various People's Democratic countries. The hosts and guests repeatedly raised their glasses, toasting to our scientific cooperation and to world peace. The banquet proceeded in a friendly and warm atmosphere throughout.

On May 5th, Bardin, head of the Soviet Academy of Sciences delegation, and delegation members Tananayev and Voronin continued to give academic reports to Chinese scientists on "Problems in the Soviet Iron and Steel Metallurgical Industry," "Physicochemical Analysis in Analytical Chemistry," and "Conditioned Reflexes – A Universal Adaptation Phenomenon in the Animal Kingdom." Delegation member Kostenko discussed relevant issues with engineers from the Electrical Industry Administration Bureau of the First Ministry of Machine Building of the People's Republic of China. Belousov visited the Petroleum Administration Bureau of the Ministry of Fuel Industry. Delegation member Mishushkin visited the Germplasm Preservation Committee of the Chinese Academy of Sciences.

### 郭沫若招待苏、匈、蒙、德等国科学家

1955-05-06
第1版()
专栏：

　　郭沫若招待苏、匈、蒙、德等国科学家
    新华社五日讯　中国科学院院长郭沫若五日设宴招待来我国访问的苏联科学院代表团和匈牙利、蒙古、德意志民主共和国的科学家。
    出席宴会的有：苏联科学院代表团团长巴尔金院士和全体团员，匈牙利科学院副院长利格蒂院士，蒙古科学委员会副主席纳查格达尔日，德意志民主共和国著名科学家格尔希院士和艾尔克斯教授。
    出席作陪的有：中国科学院副院长陈伯达、张稼夫、竺可桢、吴有训和各学部、秘书处、各厅局的负责人和有关科学家等三十多人。
    出席作陪的还有：中国科学院顾问科夫达、拉菲科夫。
    会上，中国科学院院长郭沫若致词感谢苏联和各人民民主国家科学家的友好访问和无私帮助。宾主们一再举杯，为我们的科学合作，为世界和平而干杯。宴会始终在友好热烈的空气中进行。
    五月五日苏联科学院代表团团长巴尔金、团员塔纳纳耶夫和沃罗宁继续向中国科学家作了“苏联钢铁冶金工业中的问题”、“分析化学中的物理化学分析”和“条件反射——动物界普遍的适应现象”等学术报告。团员科斯琴科和中华人民共和国第一机械工业部电器工业管理局的工程师讨论了有关问题。别洛乌索夫访问了燃料工业部石油管理总局。团员米舒斯金访问了中国科学院菌种保藏委员会。
