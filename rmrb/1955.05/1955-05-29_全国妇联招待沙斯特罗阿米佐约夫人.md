Title: All-China Women's Federation Hosts Mrs. Sastroamidjojo
Author:
Date: 1955-05-29
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, May 28th. The All-China Democratic Women's Federation held a tea party at 11:00 a.m. on the 28th to host the wife of Indonesian Prime Minister Ali Sastroamidjojo, who is visiting China, and the wife of Mr. Sukardjo Wirjopranoto, Director of the Asian-Pacific Department of the Indonesian Ministry of Foreign Affairs, who accompanied Prime Minister Ali Sastroamidjojo on his visit. Accompanied them at the tea party was the wife of Huang Zhen, China's Ambassador to the Republic of Indonesia.

Attending the tea party on the host side were Zhang Yun, Vice President of the All-China Democratic Women's Federation, Luo Qiong and Cao Mengjun, Secretaries of the Secretariat of the All-China Democratic Women's Federation, Kang Keqing, Secretary-General of the Chinese People's National Committee for the Protection of Children, and women representatives from all walks of life, including Luo Shuzhang, Sun Wenshu, Han Youtong, Gong Pusheng, Xie Bingxin, Deng Yuzhi, Pu Jiexiu, and more than 30 others.

At the tea party, the guests and hosts had a cordial conversation on issues related to women and children in China and Indonesia. The tea party was conducted in a pleasant and friendly atmosphere throughout.

### 全国妇联招待沙斯特罗阿米佐约夫人

1955-05-29
第1版()
专栏：

　　全国妇联招待沙斯特罗阿米佐约夫人
    新华社二十八日讯　中华全国民主妇女联合会在二十八日上午十一时举行茶会，招待前来我国访问的印度尼西亚共和国总理阿里·沙斯特罗阿米佐约的夫人，和随同阿里·沙斯特罗阿米佐约总理前来访问的印度尼西亚外交部亚洲—太平洋司司长苏卡佐·维约普拉诺托的夫人。陪同她们参加茶会的有我国驻印度尼西亚共和国大使黄镇的夫人。
    主人方面参加茶会的，有中华全国民主妇女联合会副主席章蕴，中华全国民主妇女联合会书记处书记罗琼、曹孟君，中国人民保卫儿童全国委员会秘书长康克清，以及各界妇女代表罗叔章、孙文淑、韩幽桐、龚普生、谢冰心、邓裕志、浦洁修等三十多人。
    在茶会上，宾主双方就中国和印度尼西亚两国有关妇女、儿童方面的问题进行了亲切的交谈，茶会始终在愉快友好的空气中进行。
