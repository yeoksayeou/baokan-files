Title: Premier Zhou Enlai Hosts Indian Chief Delegate to the United Nations, Menon
Author:
Date: 1955-05-20
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, 19th. Premier Zhou Enlai of the People's Republic of China held a reception in Beijing on the evening of the 19th to entertain Indian Chief Delegate to the United Nations, Menon, who is currently visiting our country.

Indian Ambassador to China, Raghunath Krishnan, and other foreign envoys to China were invited to attend the reception.

Vice Premier Chen Yi, Secretary-General Xi Zhongxun, Vice Foreign Ministers Zhang Wentian, Zhang Hanfu, and Ji Pengfei, Beijing Mayor Peng Zhen, responsible persons of various ministries and commissions of the People's Republic of China, members of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress, and Vice Mayors of Beijing were present.

Also present were Liao Chengzhi, Vice Chairman of the Chinese People's Committee for World Peace, Ding Xilin, President of the Sino-Indian Friendship Association, Vice President Chen Hansheng and council members, Chu Tunan, President of the Chinese People's Association for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries, Zhang Xiruo, President of the Chinese People's Institute of Foreign Affairs, as well as responsible persons from various democratic parties and other people's organizations, and figures from the capital's scientific, educational, literary, and journalistic circles.

The reception proceeded in a warm and friendly atmosphere.

### 周总理招待印度驻联合国首席代表梅农

1955-05-20
第1版()
专栏：

　　周总理招待印度驻联合国首席代表梅农
    新华社十九日讯　中华人民共和国国务院总理周恩来十九日晚在北京举行酒会，招待正在我国访问的印度驻联合国首席代表梅农。
    印度驻华大使赖嘉文和其他各国驻华使节应邀出席了酒会。
    出席作陪的有国务院副总理陈毅，秘书长习仲勋，外交部副部长张闻天、章汉夫、姬鹏飞，北京市市长彭真，以及中华人民共和国各部、各委员会负责人，全国人民代表大会常务委员会委员，北京市副市长。
    出席作陪的还有中国人民保卫世界和平委员会副主席廖承志，中印友好协会会长丁西林、副会长陈翰笙和理事，中国人民对外文化协会会长楚图南，中国人民外交学会会长张奚若以及各民主党派和其他各人民团体的负责人，首都科学、教育、文艺、新闻界人士。
    酒会在热烈友好的空气中进行。
