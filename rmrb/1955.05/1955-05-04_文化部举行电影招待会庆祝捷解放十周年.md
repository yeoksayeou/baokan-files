Title: Ministry of Culture Holds Film Reception to Celebrate the Tenth Anniversary of the Liberation of Czechoslovakia
Author:
Date: 1955-05-04
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, May 3rd. To celebrate the tenth anniversary of the liberation of Czechoslovakia on May 9th, the Ministry of Culture of the People's Republic of China held a film reception at the Capital Cinema on the evening of May 3rd. The Czechoslovak film "Dawn Illuminates Us" was screened at the reception.
    Czechoslovak Ambassador to China, Mr. Gregor, and embassy staff, as well as diplomatic envoys and officials from other countries stationed in China, were invited to attend the reception.
    The attendees at the reception included: Zheng Zhenduo, Vice Minister of Culture of the People's Republic of China; Li Zheren, Vice Minister of Foreign Trade; Zeng Zhaolun, Vice Minister of Higher Education; Wei Que, Vice Minister of Education; Hong Shen, Director of the Bureau of Foreign Cultural Relations; and heads of various people's organizations.
    Before the film screening, Zheng Zhenduo and Gregor both delivered speeches, congratulating the tenth anniversary of the liberation of Czechoslovakia, and wishing further development and consolidation of the friendship and cultural exchange between the people of China and Czechoslovakia.

### 文化部举行电影招待会庆祝捷解放十周年

1955-05-04
第1版()
专栏：

　　文化部举行电影招待会庆祝捷解放十周年
    新华社三日讯　五月九日是捷克斯洛伐克解放十周年，中华人民共和国文化部三日晚在首都电影院举行电影招待会，表示庆祝。会上放映了捷克斯洛伐克影片“曙光照耀着我们”。
    捷克斯洛伐克驻我国大使格里哥尔和使馆人员，以及其他各国驻华使节和外交官员都应邀出席了招待会。
    出席招待会的有：中华人民共和国文化部副部长郑振铎，对外贸易部副部长李哲人，高等教育部副部长曾昭抡，教育部副部长韦悫，对外文化联络局局长洪深，以及各人民团体负责人。
    影片放映前，郑振铎和格里哥尔都讲了话，庆贺捷克斯洛伐克解放十周年，并祝中捷两国人民的友谊和文化交流的进一步发展和巩固。
