Title: Warsaw Conference of European Countries Continues; Heads of Delegations Address the Meeting
Author:
Date: 1955-05-13
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Warsaw, 12th - TASS, Warsaw, 11th: The following is the communiqué of the Warsaw Conference of European countries for Ensuring Peace and Security in Europe, May 11, 1955:

    The Conference of European Countries for Ensuring Peace and Security in Europe opened at 10:00 a.m. on May 11, 1955, in the Polish Council of Ministers Building on Krakowskie Przedmieście Street, Warsaw.

    Delegations from eight countries attended the conference: the People's Republic of Albania, the People's Republic of Bulgaria, the People's Republic of Hungary, the German Democratic Republic, the People's Republic of Poland, the People's Republic of Romania, the Union of Soviet Socialist Republics, and the Czechoslovak Republic. An observer from the People's Republic of China also participated in the conference.

    The morning session was chaired by the head of the Polish delegation, Chairman of the Council of Ministers of the People's Republic of Poland, Józef Cyrankiewicz, who delivered a welcoming speech at the opening of the conference.

    The conference adopted the agenda and then began discussions. The head of the Soviet delegation, Chairman of the Council of Ministers of the USSR, Nikolai A. Bulganin; the head of the Czechoslovak delegation, Prime Minister of the Czechoslovak Republic, Viliam Široký; and the head of the Polish delegation, Chairman of the Council of Ministers of the People's Republic of Poland, Józef Cyrankiewicz, spoke at the meeting.

    The afternoon session was chaired by the head of the Romanian delegation, Chairman of the Council of Ministers of the Romanian People's Republic, Gheorghe Gheorghiu-Dej.

    The head of the delegation of the German Democratic Republic, Prime Minister of the German Democratic Republic, Otto Grotewohl, and the head of the delegation of the Hungarian People's Republic, Chairman of the Council of Ministers of the Hungarian People's Republic, András Hegedüs, spoke at the meeting.

    The next meeting will be held on May 12.

Xinhua News Agency, Warsaw, 12th - According to the Polish Press Agency, Warsaw, 12th: The following is the communiqué of the Warsaw Conference of European countries for Ensuring Peace and Security in Europe:

    The Warsaw Conference of European countries for Ensuring Peace and Security in Europe continued its discussions on May 12.

    The morning session was chaired by the head of the delegation of the Hungarian People's Republic, András Hegedüs.

    The head of the delegation of the People's Republic of Albania, Mehmet Shehu, and the head of the delegation of the People's Republic of Bulgaria, Vulko Chervenkov, made speeches at the meeting.

    The discussions are continuing.

### 欧洲国家华沙会议继续举行　各国代表团团长在会上讲话

1955-05-13
第1版()
专栏：

　　欧洲国家华沙会议继续举行
　　各国代表团团长在会上讲话
    新华社十二日讯　塔斯社华沙十一日讯：欧洲国家保障欧洲和平和安全的华沙会议一九五五年五月十一日会议公报：
    欧洲国家保障欧洲和平和安全会议于一九五五年五月十一日十时在华沙克拉科夫斯基—普热德密西大街的波兰部长会议大厦开幕。
    出席会议的有阿尔巴尼亚人民共和国、保加利亚人民共和国、匈牙利人民共和国、德意志民主共和国、波兰人民共和国、罗马尼亚人民共和国、苏维埃社会主义共和国联盟和捷克斯洛伐克共和国八国的代表团。中华人民共和国的一位观察员也参加了会议。
    主持上午会议的波兰代表团团长、波兰人民共和国部长会议主席约·西伦凯维兹在会议开幕时致欢迎词。
    会议通过了议程，接着开始讨论。苏联代表团团长、苏联部长会议主席尼·亚·布尔加宁，捷克斯洛伐克代表团团长、捷克斯洛伐克共和国总理威·西罗基，波兰代表团团长、波兰人民共和国部长会议主席约·西伦凯维兹在会上讲了话。
    下午会议由罗马尼亚代表团团长、罗马尼亚人民共和国部长会议主席乔治乌—德治主持。
    德意志民主共和国代表团团长、德意志民主共和国总理奥·格罗提渥和匈牙利代表团团长、匈牙利人民共和国部长会议主席安·赫格居斯在会上讲了话。
    下次会议将在五月十二日举行。
    新华社十二日讯　据波兰通讯社华沙十二日讯：欧洲国家保障欧洲和平和安全会议的华沙会议公报：
    欧洲国家保障欧洲和平和安全的华沙会议于五月十二日继续进行讨论。
    上午会议由匈牙利人民共和国代表团团长安·赫格居斯主持。
    阿尔巴尼亚人民共和国代表团团长穆·谢胡和保加利亚人民共和国代表团团长伏·契尔文科夫在会上作了发言。
    讨论在继续进行。
