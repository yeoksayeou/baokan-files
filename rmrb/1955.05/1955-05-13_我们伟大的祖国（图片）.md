    Our Great Motherland
    Heze County's Peony Township in Shandong Province is one of China's famous peony production areas. Peony flowers have been cultivated here for more than 400 years since the Ming Dynasty. Young Pioneers admire the blooming white jade peonies in the garden of the Wang Lizhuang Second Mutual Aid Group in Peony Township.

### 我们伟大的祖国（图片）

1955-05-13
第1版()
专栏：

    我们伟大的祖国
    山东省菏泽县牡丹乡是我国著名的牡丹花产地之一。这里培植牡丹花，从明代至今已有四百多年。少年先锋队员们在牡丹乡王黎庄第二互助组的花园里，观赏盛开的白玉牡丹。
