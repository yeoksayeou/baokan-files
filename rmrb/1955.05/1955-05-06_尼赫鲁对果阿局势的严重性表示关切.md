Title: Nehru Expresses Concern over Seriousness of Goa Situation
Author:
Date: 1955-05-06
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, New Delhi, May 5th. A news bulletin released by the Indian Information Bureau states that Prime Minister Nehru, speaking in the People's Chamber of Parliament on the 4th, expressed concern over the recent developments in the Goa situation, which indicate that the Goa crisis is deepening.

Nehru spoke in detail about the cruel suppression measures taken by the Portuguese authorities against patriots in Goa. He said that the Portuguese authorities are increasingly relying on the military to deal with the people of Goa. They are also doing their utmost to prohibit the disclosure of any news about the truth of Goa. He said, "This situation shows the seriousness of the Goa situation, showing that the Portuguese authorities are thoroughly suppressing even normal civil liberties and implementing a reign of terror over the people."

### 尼赫鲁对果阿局势的严重性表示关切

1955-05-06
第4版()
专栏：

　　尼赫鲁对果阿局势的严重性表示关切
    新华社新德里五日讯　据印度新闻局发布的新闻公报说，尼赫鲁总理四日在国会人民院发表谈话，对最近果阿局势的发展表示关切，这种发展表明果阿危机正在加深。
    尼赫鲁详细谈到葡萄牙当局对果阿的爱国者所采取的残酷的镇压措施。他说，葡萄牙当局越来越依靠军队来对付果阿人民。他们并且竭力禁止透露任何关于果阿真象的消息。他说，“这种情况表明果阿局势的严重性，表明葡萄牙当局对甚至是正常的公民自由加以彻底镇压，对人民实行恐怖统治。”
