Title: Premier Zhou Enlai Holds Grand Banquet to Welcome Sastroamidjojo
Author: 
Date: 1955-05-28
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, May 27th—Premier Zhou Enlai of the State Council of the People's Republic of China held a grand banquet on the evening of the 27th to welcome the Prime Minister of the Republic of Indonesia, Ali Sastroamidjojo, and his wife, who are visiting China at the invitation of our government.

Accompanying Prime Minister Ali Sastroamidjojo on his visit to China were the Indonesian Ambassador to China, Mononutu; the Director-General of the Asia-Pacific Department of the Indonesian Ministry of Foreign Affairs, Sukardjo Wirjopranoto, and his wife; the Indonesian Ambassador to India, Balar; the Indonesian Ambassador to the Soviet Union, Subandrio; the Director of the Indonesian National Planning Bureau, Djuanda; and a senior official of the Foreign Economic Relations Department of the Indonesian Ministry of Economy, Hasuno Reksoamojo, who were also invited to the banquet.

Personnel from the Indonesian Embassy in China were also invited to attend the banquet.

The Chinese side present at the banquet included: Vice Chairman of the People's Republic of China Zhu De, and Chairman of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress Liu Shaoqi.

Also present at the banquet were: Vice Premiers of the State Council Deng Zihui, He Long, Chen Yi, Ulanhu, Li Fuchun, and Li Xiannian; Secretary-General Xi Zhongxun; Directors of various offices Luo Ruiqing, Lin Feng, Jia Tuofu, Wang Shoudao, and Deputy Directors of various offices; Ministers Xie Juezai, Qian Ying, Zhang Naqi, Ye Jizhuang, Zhang Linzhi, Chen Yu, Jiang Guangnai, Teng Daiyuan, Zhu Xuefan, Liao Luyan, Yang Xiufeng, and Li Dequan; Vice Ministers of Foreign Affairs Zhang Wentian, Zhang Hanfu, and Ji Pengfei, as well as Vice Ministers of various ministries and Deputy Directors of various committees; heads of agencies directly under the State Council; Mayor of Beijing Peng Zhen, and Ambassador of the People's Republic of China to Indonesia Huang Zhen.

Also present at the banquet were: Vice Chairmen of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress Li Jishen, Shen Junru, Guo Moruo, and Huang Yanpei; members Zhou Jianren, Shao Lizi, Hu Qiaomu, Zhang Bangying, Zhang Su, Huang Kecheng, Huang Shaohong, Xiong Kewu, and Cai Tingkai.

Also present at the banquet were: President of the Supreme People's Court Dong Biwu, Procurator-General of the Supreme People's Procuratorate Zhang Dingcheng, Vice Chairman of the National Defense Council Fu Zuoyi, members Wang Hongkun, Wang Bingzhang, Wang Xinting, Wang Zhen, Hong Xuezhi, Xu Guangda, Zhang Zongxun, Chen Shiju, Chen Zaidao, Chen Geng, Peng Shaohui, Yang Chengwu, Liu Yalou, Zheng Dongguo, Lu Han, and Su Zhenhua; Chief of General Staff of the Chinese People's Liberation Army Su Yu, Deputy Director of the General Political Department Xiao Hua, and senior generals.

Also present at the banquet were: Vice Chairman of the National Committee of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference He Xiangning; Standing Committee members Wang Congwu, An Ziwen, Wu Gaizhi, Li Liuru, Sun Qimeng, Kang Sheng, Zhang Jichun, and Qian Duansheng; Vice Chairman of the China Association for Promoting Democracy Wang Shaoao; Secretary-General of the Central Committee of the Chinese Peasants' and Workers' Democratic Party Huang Qixiang; Chairman of the China Zhi Gong Party Chen Qiyou; Secretary-General of the Central Committee of the Jiusan Society Tu Changwang; Vice Chairman of the Taiwan Democratic Self-Government League Li Chunqing; and Secretary of the Secretariat of the Central Committee of the Chinese New Democratic Youth League Liu Daosheng.

Also present at the banquet were: Vice Chairman of the Chinese People's Committee for World Peace Liao Chengzhi; President of the Chinese People's Association for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries Chu Tunan; President of the Chinese People's Institute of Foreign Affairs Zhang Xiruo; Chairman of the All-China Federation of Trade Unions Lai Ruoyu; Vice Chairman of the All-China Democratic Women's Federation Zhang Yun; Vice Chairman of the All-China Youth Federation Wu Han; Chairman of the All-China Students' Federation Tian Demin; Vice Chairman of the China Federation of Literary and Art Circles Zhou Yang; Deputy Director of the All-China Federation of Industry and Commerce Meng Yongqian; Deputy Directors of the China Islamic Association Yang Jingren, Ma Yuhuai, and Da PUsheng; Deputy Directors of the Chinese Hui People's Cultural Advancement Association Bai Shouyi and Ma Songting; Acting President of the Buddhist Association of China Xiraojiacuo; Standing Committee member of the Three-Self Patriotic Movement Committee of the Protestant Churches of China Wang Zizhong, and other heads of people's organizations; Director of the Dalai Lama's Office in Beijing Dunwang·Jianzan Zhaba; Director of the Panchen Erdeni's Office in Beijing Sunge Badun; Director of the Inner Mongolia Autonomous Region's Office in Beijing Guan Bao, and other representatives of ethnic minorities.

Also present at the banquet were: figures from the scientific community Wu Youxun, Pan Zinian, Qian Sanqiang, Zeng Zhaolun, Ding Xilin, Hou Debang, Mao Yisheng, and Chen Fengtong; figures from the educational community Jiang Nanxiang, Jiang Longji, Zou Lufeng, He Xilin, and Fu Zhongsun; figures from the literary and art circles Zheng Zhenduo, Lao She, Tian Han, Ouyang Yuqian, Hong Shen, Yang Hansheng, and Jiang Feng; figures from the press Deng Tuo, Zhu Muzhi, Wang Yunsheng, Chang Zhiqing, and Fan Jin, as well as other social figures Weng Wenhao, Wei Lihuang, etc.

The heads of diplomatic missions and diplomatic officials from various countries in China who attended the banquet were: Ambassador of Burma U Hla Maung, Ambassador of Poland Kirylok, Ambassador of India R.K. Nehru, Ambassador of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam Hoang Van Hoan, Ambassador of Sweden Wistrand, Ambassador of Romania Cotescu, Ambassador of Mongolia Ochirbat, Ambassador of the Soviet Union Yudin, Ambassador of Bulgaria Dimov, Ambassador of Albania Nase, Ambassador of Hungary Siklós, Ambassador of Finland Sundström, Ambassador of Czechoslovakia Gregor, Ambassador of Pakistan Ahmed, Minister of Denmark গ্রেডে Jensen, Minister of Switzerland Bernouilli, Chargé d'Affaires of the Netherlands Sman, Chargé d'Affaires ad interim of Norway Klimoden, Chargé d'Affaires ad interim of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea Kim Gui Nam, Chargé d'Affaires ad interim of the German Democratic Republic Grutner, Chargé d'Affaires ad interim of Yugoslavia Milicevic, and Counselor of the British Representative Office Addis.

More than seven hundred people attended the banquet.

Before the banquet began, Premier Zhou Enlai introduced Prime Minister Ali Sastroamidjojo to the leading staff members of our country, heads of various departments, heads of various democratic parties, heads of people's organizations, figures from all walks of life, and heads of diplomatic missions of various countries in China.

At the banquet, Premier Zhou Enlai delivered a speech first, and at the end of his speech, he raised his glass to wish President Sukarno of the Republic of Indonesia good health.

Prime Minister Ali Sastroamidjojo then delivered a speech, and at the end of his speech, he raised his glass to wish Chairman Mao Zedong of the People's Republic of China good health.

The banquet proceeded in a warm and friendly atmosphere. (Attached pictures)

### 周恩来总理举行盛大宴会　欢迎沙斯特罗阿米佐约

1955-05-28
第1版()
专栏：

　　周恩来总理举行盛大宴会
　　欢迎沙斯特罗阿米佐约
    新华社二十七日讯　中华人民共和国国务院总理周恩来在二十七日晚举行盛大宴会，欢迎应我国政府邀请来我国访问的印度尼西亚共和国总理阿里·沙斯特罗阿米佐约和他的夫人。
    随同阿里·沙斯特罗阿米佐约总理访问我国的印度尼西亚驻华大使莫诺努图，印度尼西亚外交部亚洲—太平洋司司长苏卡佐·维约普拉诺托和夫人，印度尼西亚驻印度大使巴拉，印度尼西亚驻苏联大使苏班德里奥，印度尼西亚国家计划局局长朱安达，印度尼西亚经济部对外经济关系司高级官员哈苏诺·雷克梭阿莫佐等也应邀出席了宴会。
