Title: Vice Premier Chen Yi Receives Hungarian Academy of Sciences Vice President Ligeti
Author:
Date: 1955-05-14
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, 12th: Chen Yi, Vice Premier of the State Council of the People's Republic of China, received Lajos Ligeti, Vice President of the Hungarian Academy of Sciences, at 11:00 AM on the 12th, who is visiting China according to the 1955 implementation plan of the Sino-Hungarian Cultural Cooperation Agreement.

Present at the reception were Wu Youxun, Vice President of the Chinese Academy of Sciences, Hong Shen, Director of the Bureau of Foreign Cultural Relations, Luo Changpei, Director of the Institute of Linguistics of the Chinese Academy of Sciences, and Liu Jingyu, Deputy Director of the Department of Soviet and Eastern European Affairs of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs.

Also present at the reception was Hungarian Ambassador to China, Szkladán.

### 陈毅副总理接见匈科学院副院长利格蒂

1955-05-14
第1版()
专栏：

　　陈毅副总理接见匈科学院副院长利格蒂
    新华社十二日讯　中华人民共和国国务院副总理陈毅十二日上午十一时接见了根据中匈文化合作协定一九五五年执行计划来我国访问的匈牙利科学院副院长拉约希·利格蒂。
    接见时在座的，有中国科学院副院长吴有训、对外文化联络局局长洪深、中国科学院语言研究所所长罗常培、外交部苏联东欧司副司长刘靖宇。
    接见时在座的还有匈牙利人民共和国驻华大使斯克拉丹。
