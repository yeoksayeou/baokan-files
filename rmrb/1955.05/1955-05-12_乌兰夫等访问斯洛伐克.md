Title: Ulanhu and Others Visit Slovakia
Author:
Date: 1955-05-12
Page: 1

　　Xinhua News Agency, Prague, May 11th: The Chinese government delegation headed by Vice Premier of the State Council Ulanhu left Prague for Slovakia on the 11th for a visit. Accompanied were the Deputy Minister of Foreign Affairs of Czechoslovakia, Ladislav Šimovič.
    Xinhua News Agency, Prague, May 11th: The government delegation of the People's Republic of China, headed by Vice Premier Ulanhu, visited the village of Vinařice on the 10th, which has a deep friendship with Zhangguozhuang in Fengtai District, Beijing, accompanied by Václav Pašek, Secretary of the Secretariat of the Central Committee of the Czechoslovak Communist Party, Minister of Agriculture M. Šmída, and Deputy Minister of Foreign Affairs L. Šimovič. The village chief and the director of the unified agricultural cooperative addressed the welcoming meeting, expressing a warm welcome to the delegation. Vice Premier Ulanhu delivered a speech wishing further development of the deep friendship between China and Czechoslovakia, and wishing Vinařice Village and the cooperative greater achievements in advancing towards socialism.
    Finally, the hosts and guests exchanged gifts. The delegation also visited the unified agricultural cooperative in the village.
    Before the 10th, the Chinese government delegation also visited the Slapy Dam and aircraft factories, etc.

### 乌兰夫等访问斯洛伐克

1955-05-12
第1版()
专栏：

　　乌兰夫等访问斯洛伐克
    新华社布拉格十一日电　以国务院副总理乌兰夫为首的我国政府代表团十一日离布拉格前往斯洛伐克进行访问。陪同前往的有捷外交部副部长拉吉斯拉夫·西莫维茨。
    新华社布拉格十一日电　以乌兰夫副总理为首的中华人民共和国政府代表团，十日在捷克斯洛伐克共产党中央委员会书记处书记瓦·帕歇克、农业部长马·斯米达和外交部副部长拉·西莫维茨的陪同下，访问了同北京市丰台区张郭庄有着深厚友谊的维纳尔日采村。这个村的村长和统一农业合作社主任在欢迎会上致词，对代表团表示热烈欢迎。乌兰夫副总理致词祝中捷两国的深厚友谊进一步发展，祝维纳尔日采村及合作社在向社会主义前进中获得更大的成就。
    最后，宾主双方互赠礼物。代表团还参观了这个村的统一农业合作社。
    中国政府代表团在十日以前还参观了斯拉皮水闸和飞机工厂等。
