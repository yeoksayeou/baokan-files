Romania and Hungary Discuss Strengthening Economic Cooperation

Government delegations of the Romanian People's Republic and the Hungarian People's Republic recently held a meeting in Bucharest to discuss issues related to strengthening economic cooperation between the two countries.

The meeting reviewed the implementation of existing economic agreements between the two countries and suggestions for expanding existing economic cooperation. The scope of review was quite broad, including chemical, electric power, and steel industries, machine industry, light industry, timber and wood processing industries, and agriculture.

The meeting also determined the date for talks to conclude a long-term trade agreement, with a term from 1956 to 1960.

### 罗匈两国讨论加强经济合作问题

1955-05-20
第4版()
专栏：

　  罗匈两国讨论加强经济合作问题
    罗马尼亚人民共和国和匈牙利人民共和国的政府代表团，最近在布加勒斯特举行了会议，讨论有关加强两国间经济合作的问题。
    会议审议了两国间现有的经济协定的执行情况和扩大现有的经济合作的建议等。审议问题的范围相当广泛，其中包括化学、电力和钢铁工业，机器工业，轻工业，木材和木材加工工业以及农业等。
    会议还确定了缔结一个长期贸易协定的会谈日期，这个贸易协定的期限是从一九五六年到一九六零年。
