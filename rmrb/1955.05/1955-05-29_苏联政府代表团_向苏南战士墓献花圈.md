Title: Soviet Government Delegation Lays Wreaths at the Cemetery of South-Central China Warriors
Author:
Date: 1955-05-29
Page: 4
　
　　Xinhua News Agency, Belgrade, 27th (TASS dispatch): The Soviet government delegation laid a wreath at the Tomb of the Unknown Soldier on Mount Avala near Belgrade. The wreath read: "Presented by the Soviet Government Delegation to the soldiers of the Yugoslav army who sacrificed in the struggle for the independence of the motherland."
　　On the same day, the Soviet government delegation went to the cemetery of Yugoslav and Soviet soldiers who died in the battle to liberate Belgrade and laid wreaths. The wreaths read: "Presented by the Soviet Government Delegation to the heroic Soviet soldiers who sacrificed in the common struggle to liberate the fraternal people of Yugoslavia from the fascist aggressors."
　　"Presented by the Soviet Government Delegation to the heroic soldiers of the Yugoslav People's Army who sacrificed in the struggle to liberate the motherland from the fascist aggressors."

### 苏联政府代表团  向苏南战士墓献花圈

1955-05-29
第4版()
专栏：

　　苏联政府代表团
　  向苏南战士墓献花圈
    新华社二十八日讯　据塔斯社贝尔格莱德二十七日讯：苏联政府代表团向贝尔格莱德附近阿瓦拉山上的无名战士墓献花圈。花圈上写着：“苏联政府代表团献给在为祖国的独立而进行的斗争中牺牲的南斯拉夫军队的战士。”
    同一天，苏联政府代表团前往在解放贝尔格莱德战斗中牺牲的南斯拉夫和苏联战士墓献花圈。花圈上写着：“苏联政府代表团献给在为了把南斯拉夫兄弟人民从法西斯侵略者手中解放出来的共同斗争中牺牲的英勇的苏军战士”。
      “苏联政府代表团献给在为了把祖国从法西斯侵略者手中解放出来的斗争中牺牲的英勇的南斯拉夫人民军战士。”
