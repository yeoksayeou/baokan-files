Title: Diamond Mine Discovered in Yuan River Valley
Author:
Date: 1955-05-31
Page: 1

Geological prospecting teams conducting geological surveys in Qianyang, Yuanling, and other areas of the Yuan River basin in Hunan Province have discovered diamond mines coexisting with placer gold in the river valley. The exploration team has now developed an exploration design based on the survey data, and exploration has begun in two areas.

Diamonds are mainly used to manufacture cutting, wire-drawing, and drilling tools, cutting extra-hard metals and drawing tungsten filaments for light bulbs. Some drill bits in drilling machines are also inlaid with diamonds. Diamond powder and diamond grinding wheels can be used to grind shafts and glass parts on precision instruments.
(Xinhua News Agency)

### 沅水流域河谷发现金刚石矿

1955-05-31
第1版()
专栏：

　  沅水流域河谷发现金刚石矿
    在湖南省沅水流域黔阳、沅陵等地进行地质普查的地质勘探队，在河谷中发现了与砂金共生的金刚石矿。现在这个勘探队已根据普查资料作出勘探设计，有两个地区已开始勘探。
    金刚石主要用来制造切削、抽丝和钻眼的工具，切削特硬的金属和抽制电灯泡的钨丝。钻探机的有些钻头也镶有金刚石。金刚粉、金刚石石砂轮可用来磨制精密仪器上的轴和玻璃零件。
                                    （新华社）
