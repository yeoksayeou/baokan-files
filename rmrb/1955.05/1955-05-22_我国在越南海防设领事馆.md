Title: China Establishes Consulate in Haiphong, Vietnam
Author:
Date: 1955-05-22
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, 21st. With the agreement of the Government of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam, the Chinese government has decided to establish a consulate in Haiphong and has appointed Dai Lu as Consul in Haiphong and Li Fangping as Vice-Consul in the Consulate in Haiphong.

### 我国在越南海防设领事馆

1955-05-22
第1版()
专栏：

　　我国在越南海防设领事馆
    新华社二十一日讯　我国政府经征得越南民主共和国政府同意后，已决定在海防设立领事馆，并已任命戴路为驻海防领事，李方平为驻海防领事馆副领事。
