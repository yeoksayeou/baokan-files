Title: Prime Minister U Nu to Visit Yugoslavia
Author:
Date: 1955-05-24
Page: 4

　　Xinhua News Agency, Prague, May 23rd: According to a Belgrade dispatch from the Yugoslav News Agency: At the invitation of Yugoslav President Tito, Burmese Prime Minister U Nu will visit Yugoslavia from June 6th to 16th before visiting the United States.

### 吴努总理将访问南斯拉夫

1955-05-24
第4版()
专栏：

　　吴努总理将访问南斯拉夫
    新华社布拉格二十三日电　据南斯拉夫通讯社贝尔格莱德消息：缅甸总理吴努应南斯拉夫总统铁托邀请，将在访问美国以前，从六月六日到十六日在南斯拉夫进行访问。
