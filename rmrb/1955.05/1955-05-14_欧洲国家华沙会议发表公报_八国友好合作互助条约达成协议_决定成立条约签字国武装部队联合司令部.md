Title: Warsaw Conference of European Countries Issues Communiqué; Treaty of Friendship, Cooperation, and Mutual Assistance Among Eight Nations Concluded; Decision Made to Establish Joint Command of Armed Forces of Treaty Signatory States
Author:
Date: 1955-05-14
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, Warsaw, May 13th. – The Warsaw Conference of European countries for ensuring peace and security in Europe held a meeting on May 12th.
The meeting was chaired by the head of the Hungarian delegation, Hegedüs, Chairman of the Council of Ministers of the Hungarian People's Republic.
The following spoke at the meeting: Shehu, head of the Albanian delegation and Chairman of the Council of Ministers of the Albanian People's Republic; Gheorghe Gheorghiu-Dej, head of the Romanian delegation and Chairman of the Council of Ministers of the Romanian People's Republic; Chervenkov, head of the Bulgarian delegation and Chairman of the Council of Ministers of the Bulgarian People's Republic.
Peng Dehuai, observer from the People's Republic of China and Vice Premier and Minister of National Defense of the State Council of the People's Republic of China, addressed the meeting.
A closed session was also held on May 12th, at which General A. I. Antonov reported on various issues related to the formation of joint armed forces.
In the afternoon, the various committees established by the conference held meetings.
The next meeting will be held on May 13th.
Xinhua News Agency, Warsaw, May 13th. – Communiqué of the May 13th meeting of the Warsaw Conference of European countries for ensuring peace and security in Europe:
The fourth meeting of the Warsaw Conference of European countries for ensuring peace and security in Europe was held on May 13th under the chairmanship of N. A. Bulganin, Chairman of the Council of Ministers of the Soviet Union and head of the Soviet delegation.
The meeting discussed, article by article, the clauses of the Treaty of Friendship, Cooperation, and Mutual Assistance between the People's Republic of Albania, the People's Republic of Bulgaria, the Hungarian People's Republic, the German Democratic Republic, the Polish People's Republic, the Romanian People's Republic, the Union of Soviet Socialist Republics, and the Czechoslovak Republic.
The delegations of the countries attending the meeting announced that they had reached agreement on the provisions of the aforementioned treaty.
The meeting decided that the treaty would be signed at 10:00 a.m. on May 14th in the building of the State Council of the Polish People's Republic.
The meeting decided to establish a joint command of the armed forces of the treaty signatory states.

### 欧洲国家华沙会议发表公报　八国友好合作互助条约达成协议　决定成立条约签字国武装部队联合司令部

1955-05-14
第1版()
专栏：

　　欧洲国家华沙会议发表公报
　　八国友好合作互助条约达成协议
　　决定成立条约签字国武装部队联合司令部
    新华社华沙十三日　电欧洲国家保障欧洲和平和安全的华沙会议十二日公报：
    欧洲国家保障欧洲和平和安全的华沙会议在五月十二日举行了会议。
    会议由匈牙利代表团团长、匈牙利人民共和国部长会议主席赫格居斯主持。
    在会上发言的有：阿尔巴尼亚代表团团长、阿尔巴尼亚人民共和国部长会议主席谢胡，罗马尼亚代表团团长、罗马尼亚人民共和国部长会议主席乔治乌—德治，保加利亚代表团团长、保加利亚人民共和国部长会议主席契尔文科夫。
    中华人民共和国观察员、中华人民共和国国务院副总理兼国防部长彭德怀在会上发了言。
    五月十二日并举行了一次秘密会议，由阿·伊·安东诺夫大将就有关组成联合武装部队的各项问题作了报告。
    下午，会议成立的各委员会举行了会议。
    下次会议将在五月十三日举行。
    新华社华沙十三日电　欧洲国家保障欧洲和平和安全的华沙会议五月十三日会议公报：
    欧洲国家保障欧洲和平和安全的华沙会议第四次会议五月十三日在苏联部长会议主席、苏联代表团团长尼·亚·布尔加宁的主持下举行。
    会议逐条讨论了阿尔巴尼亚人民共和国、保加利亚人民共和国、匈牙利人民共和国、德意志民主共和国、波兰人民共和国、罗马尼亚人民共和国、苏维埃社会主义共和国联盟和捷克斯洛伐克共和国之间的友好、合作和互助条约的条款。
    出席会议的各国代表团宣布它们对上述条约的条文达成协议。
    会议决定，条约将在五月十四日上午十时在波兰人民共和国国务委员会大厦签字。
    会议决定成立条约签字国的武装部队的联合司令部。
