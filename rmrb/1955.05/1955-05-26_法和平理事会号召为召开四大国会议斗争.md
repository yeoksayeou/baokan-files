Title: French Peace Council Calls for Struggle to Convene a Four-Power Conference

Author:
Date: 1955-05-26
Page: 4

　　Xinhua News Agency, Paris, May 24th: According to TASS, the French National Peace Council issued a statement calling on French patriots to strengthen the struggle for the convening of a Four-Power Conference.

　　The statement also said, "The interests of France require the establishment of a unified and democratic Germany that does not militarily threaten any of its neighbors, and that this unified Germany should join a pan-European collective security system."

　　The statement called on the French people to demand that the French Parliament and government take measures in line with national and peace interests.

### 法和平理事会号召为召开四大国会议斗争

1955-05-26
第4版()
专栏：

　　法和平理事会号召为召开四大国会议斗争
    新华社二十五日讯　据塔斯社巴黎二十四日讯：法国全国和平理事会发表声明，号召法国爱国者为召开四大国会议而加强斗争。
    声明还说，“法国的利益要求建立一个在军事上不威胁任何邻国的、统一的和民主的德国，并且要使这个统一的德国加入全欧集体安全体系。”
    声明号召法国人民要求法国议会和政府采取符合国家利益和和平利益的措施。
