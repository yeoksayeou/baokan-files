Title: National Conference of Model Workers in Highway Construction Concludes

Author:
Date: 1955-05-12
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, 11th: The first National Conference of Model Workers in Highway Construction concluded on the 11th.

　　The conference lasted nine days. Delegates extensively exchanged and studied advanced experiences and working methods in highway construction, automobile transportation, automobile repair, and highway maintenance.

　　The conference selected and awarded prizes to model highway workers. Qi Shuchun, leader of the survey work on the Kangzang Highway; Lu Chao, a driver who safely drove 130,000 kilometers on the Kangzang Plateau; Chen Rongcai, a driver from Zhejiang Province who safely drove 230,000 kilometers and 200,000 kilometers without major repairs; Tan Fuju, who created the "pavement assembly line operation method"; Wang Zhenxiang, a model who mobilized and organized the masses to maintain highways; Ma Mingshan, a demobilized soldier who worked hard to learn technology; Luo Hongtao, a driver who performed meritorious service many times in supporting the War to Resist U.S. Aggression and Aid Korea, and eight others received first-class medals issued by the Ministry of Communications of the People's Republic of China. Li Huiying, a female Hani squad leader who was a model road builder, and Toiybaerdi, a Kazak model road builder, and fifty-three others received second-class medals issued by the Ministry of Communications. In addition, eleven units received collective awards.

　　Wang Hongcai and Xiang Shugui, heroes of the Chinese People's Liberation Army who participated in highway construction, Li Wenyuan, the hero who crossed the Nujiang River during road construction, and twelve other soldiers received first-class or second-class medals issued by the Ministry of Communications. The "Zhang Fulin Class," named in memory of the martyr Zhang Fulin who sacrificed himself in the arduous project of conquering Mount Que'er on the Kangzang Highway, also received a collective first-class award.

　　Vice Minister of Communications Li Yunchang made a summary of the meeting. Gan Siqi, Deputy Director of the General Political Department of the Chinese People's Liberation Army, spoke at the closing ceremony. During the meeting, Lai Ruoyu, Chairman of the All-China Federation of Trade Unions, made a report.

　　The conference passed a message of salute to Chairman Mao, the Chinese People's Liberation Army, and the Chinese People's Volunteers.

### 全国公路劳动模范代表大会闭幕

1955-05-12
第1版()
专栏：

　　全国公路劳动模范代表大会闭幕
    新华社十一日讯　全国公路第一次劳动模范代表大会已经在十一日闭幕。
    大会一共进行了九天。代表们曾经广泛地交流和研究了公路建设、汽车运输、汽车修理和公路养护方面的先进经验和先进工作法。
    大会评出了公路劳动模范，并进行授奖。康藏公路勘测工作的领导人齐树椿、在康藏高原安全行驶十三万公里的汽车驾驶员路超、安全行驶二十三万公里和行驶二十万公里未大修的浙江省汽车驾驶员陈荣材、创造“配路面流水作业法”的谭福巨、发动和组织群众养护公路的模范王振祥、努力学习技术的转业军人马明山、在支援抗美援朝战争中多次立功的汽车驾驶员罗洪涛等十一人，获得了中华人民共和国交通部颁发的一等奖章。筑路模范哈尼族女分队长李惠英、筑路模范哈萨克族托依保尔地等五十五人，获得了交通部颁发的二等奖章。另外，有十一个单位获得了集体奖。
    参加修筑公路的中国人民解放军功臣王洪才、相树桂，在修路中强渡怒江的英雄李文炎和其他十二名战士，获得了交通部颁发的一等奖章或二等奖章。为了纪念在征服康藏公路上雀儿山艰险工程中牺牲的烈士张福林而命名的“张福林班”，也获得了集体一等奖。
    会议由交通部副部长李运昌作总结。中国人民解放军总政治部副主任甘泗淇在闭幕会上讲话。在会议期间，中华全国总工会主席赖若愚曾作了报告。
    大会通过了向毛主席、中国人民解放军、中国人民志愿军的致敬电。
