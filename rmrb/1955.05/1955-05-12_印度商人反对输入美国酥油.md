Title: Indian Merchants Oppose the Import of American Ghee
Author:
Date: 1955-05-12
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency Report: According to a report in the "Hindustan Standard" on the 8th, Indian ghee merchants recently submitted a memorandum to the Indian Minister of Food and Agriculture, A. P. Jain, requesting the government not to import American ghee (butter products).

The memorandum stated that the import of American ghee would not be beneficial to the Indian ghee industry, nor would it be beneficial to the health of the Indian people.

### 印度商人反对输入美国酥油

1955-05-12
第4版()
专栏：

　　印度商人反对输入美国酥油
    据新华社讯　据“印度斯坦旗报”八日报道，印度酥油商人最近向印度粮食和农业部长阿·普·贾殷提出了一项备忘录，要求政府不要输入美国的酥油（牛油产品）。
    备忘录说，美国酥油的输入将不会有利于印度的酥油业，也不会有利于印度人民的健康。
