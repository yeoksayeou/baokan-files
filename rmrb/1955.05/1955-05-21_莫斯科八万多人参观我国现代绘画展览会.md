Title: Over 80,000 People in Moscow Visit Exhibition of Modern Chinese Painting
Author:
Date: 1955-05-21
Page: 4

　　Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, May 19th. The Exhibition of Modern Chinese Painting of the People's Republic of China, recently held at the Central Soviet Army House in Moscow, has concluded. More than 80,000 Soviet soldiers, workers, collective farmers, and staff members visited the exhibition.

　　The exhibition featured more than 230 pieces of modern Chinese art. These works reflected the development of China's industrialization, agricultural transformation, and cultural education and health undertakings. The exhibition also displayed many works depicting the Chinese people's struggle for peace.

### 莫斯科八万多人参观我国现代绘画展览会

1955-05-21
第4版()
专栏：

　　莫斯科八万多人参观我国现代绘画展览会
    新华社莫斯科十九日电　最近在莫斯科中央苏军之家举办的中华人民共和国现代绘画展览会已经结束。参观展览会的苏军战士、工人、集体农民和职员等共有八万多人。
    这次展览会共展出了二百三十多件中国现代美术作品。这些作品反映出中国工业化、农业改造以及文化教育、卫生事业等方面的发展情况。展览会还展出了许多表现我国人民为和平而斗争的作品。
