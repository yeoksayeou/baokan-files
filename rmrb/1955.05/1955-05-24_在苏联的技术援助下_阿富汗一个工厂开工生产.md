Title: Afghan Factory Starts Production with Soviet Technical Assistance
Author:
Date: 1955-05-24
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Kabul, 21st (TASS dispatch) – A factory in Kabul producing asphalt and concrete mixtures began production on the 19th. The factory's equipment was shipped from the Soviet Union and assembled under the guidance of Soviet experts, according to the Soviet Union's plan of technical assistance to Afghanistan.

　　"Pamir Newspaper" pointed out that "the construction of this huge and very useful factory is an astonishing undertaking," which will promote the country's progress because it will advance the improvement projects of streets and highways in Kabul and other cities. The newspaper said that the factory will become a base for training technicians nationwide.

### 在苏联的技术援助下　阿富汗一个工厂开工生产

1955-05-24
第4版()
专栏：

　　在苏联的技术援助下
  　阿富汗一个工厂开工生产
    新华社二十二日讯　塔斯社喀布尔二十一日讯：喀布尔的一个柏油和混凝土混合工厂在十九日开始生产。这个工厂的装备是按照苏联对阿富汗的技术援助的计划，从苏联运来并在苏联专家的指导下装配起来的。
    “帕米尔报”指出，“建筑这个巨大的、非常有用的工厂是一个惊人的事业”，这个工厂将促进国家的进步，因为它将使喀布尔和其他城市的街道和公路的改善工程向前推进一步。这个报纸说，这个工厂将成为训练全国技术人员的基地。
