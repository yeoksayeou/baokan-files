Title: Xikang Daliangshan Power Plant Under Construction
Author:
Date: 1955-05-05
Page: 1

The first power plant in Daliangshan is under construction. The power plant is located in Zhaojue, the capital of the Liangshan Yi Autonomous Prefecture in Xikang Province. The factory building was completed at the end of March, and the generator and other necessary equipment have been transported there. Currently, surveying lines and installing machines are underway. It is expected to start generating electricity on July 1st. The people of all ethnic groups in Zhaojue have expressed infinite excitement and concern about the construction of this power plant, and many people often visit the construction site to see the progress of the project. (Our correspondent stationed in Ya'an)

### 西康大凉山发电厂正在修建

1955-05-05
第1版()
专栏：

　  西康大凉山发电厂正在修建
    大凉山里第一个发电厂正进行修建。发电厂设在西康省凉山彝族自治州首府昭觉。厂房已经在三月底修好，发电机及其他应有设备都已经运到。目前正进行测量线路，安装机器等项工作，预计七月一日可开始发电。昭觉的各族人民对这个发电厂的修建表示无限的兴奋和关怀，许多人常到工地探望工程进展情况。　（本报驻雅安记者）
