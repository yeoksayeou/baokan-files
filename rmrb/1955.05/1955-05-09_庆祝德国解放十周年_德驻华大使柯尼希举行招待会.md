Title: German Ambassador to China, Koenig, Holds Reception Celebrating the Tenth Anniversary of Germany's Liberation
Author:
Date: 1955-05-09
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, May 8th. German Democratic Republic's Ambassador Extraordinary and Plenipotentiary to China, Mr. Koenig, held a reception on the evening of the 8th to celebrate the tenth anniversary of Germany's liberation.

Zhu De, Vice Chairman of the People's Republic of China, was invited to attend the reception.

Also invited were: Chen Yun and Chen Yi, Vice Premiers of the State Council; Shen Junru, Vice Chairman of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress; Zhang Wentian, Vice Minister of Foreign Affairs; Jiang Guangnai, Minister of Textile Industry; Jia Tuofu, Minister of Light Industry; Zhang Xiruo, Minister of Education; Vice Ministers of various ministries; and Wu Han, Vice Mayor of Beijing.

Also invited were leaders of various people's organizations and figures from the capital's scientific and artistic circles.

Diplomatic envoys and officials from various countries stationed in China were also invited to the reception.

At the reception, Ambassador Koenig and Vice Premier Chen Yun spoke successively, wishing that the fraternal and friendly cooperative relations between China and Germany would continue to develop and consolidate in the struggle to defend peace and in the great cause of socialist construction.

The reception proceeded in a warm and friendly atmosphere.

### 庆祝德国解放十周年　德驻华大使柯尼希举行招待会

1955-05-09
第4版()
专栏：

    庆祝德国解放十周年
　　德驻华大使柯尼希举行招待会
    新华社八日讯　德意志民主共和国驻我国特命全权大使柯尼希，八日晚举行招待会，庆祝德国解放十周年。
    中华人民共和国副主席朱德，应邀出席了招待会。
    应邀出席的还有：国务院副总理陈云、陈毅，全国人民代表大会常务委员会副委员长沈钧儒，外交部副部长张闻天，纺织工业部部长蒋光鼐，轻工业部部长贾拓夫，教育部部长张奚若，各部副部长和北京市副市长吴晗。
    应邀出席的还有各人民团体的负责人和首都科学界、文艺界人士。
    各国驻华使节和外交官员也应邀出席了招待会。
    招待会上，柯尼希大使和陈云副总理先后讲话，祝在保卫和平的斗争中，在社会主义建设的伟大事业中，中德两国兄弟般的友好合作关系，得到不断的发展和巩固。
    招待会在热烈友好的空气中进行。
