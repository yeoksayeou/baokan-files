Title: Polish Parliament Approves Treaty of Friendship, Cooperation, and Mutual Assistance of Eight Countries
Author:
Date: 1955-05-21
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Warsaw, May 20th -- According to a report by the Polish News Agency, the Parliament of the Polish People's Republic held its seventh session on the 19th and approved the Treaty of Friendship, Cooperation, and Mutual Assistance of the Eight European Countries signed in Warsaw.

At the meeting, Chairman of the Council of Ministers, Cyrankiewicz, delivered a report on behalf of the government regarding the approval of the treaty. Cyrankiewicz said that the Warsaw Treaty was concluded among friendly countries advancing along the road of socialist construction because they were threatened by the remilitarization of West Germany. This treaty provides us with full guarantees that any attempt to invade our countries will be met with a united and resolute rebuff. The Warsaw Treaty embodies the rock-solid will of friendly countries to cooperate in consolidating each other's security and to jointly defend sovereignty and independence.

Cyrankiewicz then pointed out the close connection between the Warsaw Conference and the struggle to strive for peace and ease international tensions. Finally, on behalf of the government, he requested the parliament to approve the Treaty of Friendship, Cooperation, and Mutual Assistance of the Eight Countries.

After discussion, the meeting unanimously adopted a resolution approving the Treaty of Friendship, Cooperation, and Mutual Assistance.

The meeting then passed a resolution on changing the composition of the State Council and approved the State Council's resolution on changing the composition of the Council of Ministers of the Polish People's Republic.

### 波兰议会批准八国友好合作互助条约

1955-05-21
第4版()
专栏：

　　波兰议会批准八国友好合作互助条约
    新华社华沙二十日电　据波兰通讯社报道：波兰人民共和国议会十九日举行第七次会议，批准了在华沙签订的欧洲八国友好合作互助条约。
    会上，部长会议主席西伦凯维兹代表政府就批准条约问题做了报告。西伦凯维兹说，沿着社会主义建设的道路前进的友好国家之间缔结了华沙条约，因为它们受到西德重新军国主义化的威胁。这一条约使我们有了充分的保障，侵略我们国家的任何企图都会立刻遭到联合的和坚决的反击。华沙条约体现出为巩固彼此的安全而进行合作的友好国家共同保卫主权和独立的坚如磐石的意志。
    西伦凯维兹接着指出了华沙会议同争取和平和缓和国际紧张局势的斗争的密切联系。最后，他代表政府请求议会批准八国友好合作互助条约。
    会议经过讨论后一致通过决议批准了友好合作互助条约。
    会议接着通过了关于更动国务委员会组成人员的决议，批准了国务委员会关于更动波兰人民共和国部长会议组成人员的决议。
