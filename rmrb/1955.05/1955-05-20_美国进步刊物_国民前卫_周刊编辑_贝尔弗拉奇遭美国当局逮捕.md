Title: U.S. Authorities Arrest Belfrage, Editor of U.S. Progressive Publication "National Guardian" Weekly

Author:
Date: 1955-05-20
Page: 4

　　Xinhua News Agency, May 18th: New York dispatch: James Belfrage, editor of the American progressive publication "National Guardian" weekly, was arrested at his New York residence on the 13th by agents of the U.S. Federal Bureau of Investigation. U.S. authorities are plotting to deport Belfrage.
　　Belfrage has been subjected to discriminatory treatment in federal prison, and as of the 15th, he was on a hunger strike to protest this treatment.
　　A spokesperson for the "National Guardian" weekly said that they will launch a struggle against the deportation of Belfrage.

### 美国进步刊物“国民前卫”周刊编辑  贝尔弗拉奇遭美国当局逮捕

1955-05-20
第4版()
专栏：

　　美国进步刊物“国民前卫”周刊编辑
　  贝尔弗拉奇遭美国当局逮捕
    新华社十八日讯　纽约消息：美国进步刊物“国民前卫”周刊编辑贝尔弗拉奇十三日在纽约寓所被美国联邦调查局的特务逮捕。美国当局正在策划把贝尔弗拉奇放逐出境。
    贝尔弗拉奇在联邦监狱里受到了歧视待遇，到十五日，他一直在绝食抗议这种待遇。
    “国民前卫”周刊发言人说，他们将开展反对放逐贝尔弗拉奇的斗争。
