Title: Indonesian Cabinet Holds Meeting to Discuss Investigation Report on the "Kashmir Princess"
Author:
Date: 1955-05-27
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Jakarta, May 26th – The Indonesian Cabinet held a meeting on the 25th, presided over by Deputy Prime Minister Zainal Arifin, to discuss the crash of the Indian “Kashmir Princess” aircraft. A communiqué issued by the meeting stated that the meeting heard and discussed the preliminary report by Indonesian Minister of Transportation, Gani, regarding the crash of the “Kashmir Princess” aircraft near the Natuna Islands on April 11, 1955. The communiqué said: “The report of the committee established by the Minister of Transportation to investigate this unfortunate incident will be further studied by the government.”

### 印度尼西亚内阁举行会议　讨论关于“克什米尔公主号”的调查报告

1955-05-27
第4版()
专栏：

　　印度尼西亚内阁举行会议
  　讨论关于“克什米尔公主号”的调查报告
    新华社雅加达二十六日电　印度尼西亚内阁在二十五日举行的由副总理蔡纳·阿里芬主持的会议上，讨论了印度“克什米尔公主号”飞机坠毁问题。会议发表的公报说，会议听取了并讨论了印度尼西亚交通部长干尼关于“克什米尔公主号”飞机一九五五年四月十一日在纳土纳群岛附近海面坠毁事件的初步报告。公报说：“交通部长设立的调查这一不幸事件的委员会的报告将由政府加以更深入的研究。”
