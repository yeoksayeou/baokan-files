Title: Chinese Government Delegation Arrives in Prague
Author:
Date: 1955-05-07
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Prague, 6th. The government delegation of the People's Republic of China, led by Ulanhu, Vice Premier of the State Council, arrived in Prague by plane on the afternoon of May 5th at the invitation of the Government of the Czechoslovak Republic to attend the celebrations for the 10th anniversary of the liberation of Czechoslovakia.

Those who welcomed the delegation at the airport included Czechoslovak Deputy Premiers Kopecký and Jankovcová, Secretary of the Central Committee of the Czechoslovak Communist Party, Pasek, Minister of Foreign Affairs, David, and Deputy Minister of National Defense and Chief of General Staff, General Kratochvíl, and many others.

Also at the airport to welcome the delegation were Cao Ying, the Chinese Ambassador to Czechoslovakia, and embassy staff, as well as diplomatic envoys from the Soviet Union and various People's Democratic countries stationed in Czechoslovakia.

After Vice Premier Ulanhu and others disembarked, they reviewed the guard of honor accompanied by General Kratochvíl.

### 我国政府代表团到达布拉格

1955-05-07
第1版()
专栏：

　　我国政府代表团到达布拉格
    新华社布拉格六日电　应捷克斯洛伐克共和国政府的邀请参加捷克斯洛伐克解放十周年庆祝典礼的中华人民共和国政府代表团，由国务院副总理乌兰夫率领，在五月五日下午乘飞机到达布拉格。
    到机场欢迎的有捷克斯洛伐克副总理柯别茨基和扬科夫卓娃、捷克斯洛伐克共产党中央委员会书记处书记帕歇克、外交部长戴维、国防部副部长兼总参谋长克拉托赫维尔上将等多人。
    到机场欢迎的还有我国驻捷克斯洛伐克大使曹瑛和大使馆人员，以及苏联和各人民民主国家驻捷克斯洛伐克外交使节。
  乌兰夫副总理等下机后，在克拉托赫维尔上将陪同下，检阅了仪仗队。
