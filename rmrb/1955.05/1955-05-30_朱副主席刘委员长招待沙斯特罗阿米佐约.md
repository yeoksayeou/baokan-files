Title: Vice Chairman Zhu and Chairman Liu Host Sastroamidjojo

Author: 
Date: 1955-05-30
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, 29th. Indonesian Republic Prime Minister Ali Sastroamidjojo and his wife attended a tea party on the afternoon of the 29th hosted by Vice Chairman Zhu De and his wife, and Chairman of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress Liu Shaoqi and his wife.

Also invited to the tea party were Indonesian Ambassador to China Mononutu, Director of the Asia-Pacific Department of the Indonesian Ministry of Foreign Affairs Sukardjo Wirjopranoto and his wife, Indonesian Ambassador to India Bala, Indonesian Ambassador to the Soviet Union Subandrio, and Director of the Indonesian National Planning Bureau Djuanda, who were accompanying Prime Minister Ali Sastroamidjojo on his visit to China.

Present were Vice Chairmen of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress Li Jishen, Shen Junru, and Huang Yanpei.

Chinese Ambassador to Indonesia Huang Zhen and his wife were also present.

This newspaper reports that on the morning of the 29th, Indonesian Republic Prime Minister Sastroamidjojo and his wife, along with accompanying personnel, visited the state-owned Beijing No. 2 Cotton Textile Factory and the state-owned Beijing No. 1 Cotton Textile Factory in the eastern suburbs of Beijing, accompanied by Chinese Ambassador to Indonesia Huang Zhen and his wife, and Director of the Protocol Department of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs, Ke Hua.

At the state-owned Beijing No. 2 Cotton Textile Factory, Prime Minister Sastroamidjojo visited the entire production process of all seven workshops and inquired about some production issues. This new type of cotton textile factory only began construction in March of last year and started trial production in April of this year. It has 100,000 spindles and 2,400 looms. All the mechanical equipment in the factory is manufactured by various mechanical factories in China, including the latest machines produced in 1954, such as single-process cotton cleaners, single-process three-draft roving frames, and high-speed winders. After understanding these circumstances, Prime Minister Sastroamidjojo, on behalf of his wife and entourage, expressed his appreciation for such a large factory.

Next, Prime Minister Sastroamidjojo visited the state-owned Beijing No. 1 Cotton Textile Factory, which has 50,000 spindles. Prime Minister Sastroamidjojo entered each workshop, and the workers who were in production warmly applauded to welcome the distinguished guests. In the finishing workshop, Prime Minister Sastroamidjojo watched the workers operate the packaging machines and asked Director Yang Huijie about the factory's monthly output. Before leaving, Prime Minister Sastroamidjojo expressed his delight at seeing the factory led by this female director. (Picture attached)

### 朱副主席刘委员长招待沙斯特罗阿米佐约

1955-05-30
第1版()
专栏：

　　朱副主席刘委员长招待沙斯特罗阿米佐约
    新华社二十九日讯　印度尼西亚共和国总理阿里·沙斯特罗阿米佐约和夫人，在二十九日下午应邀出席了中华人民共和国副主席朱德和夫人、全国人民代表大会常务委员会委员长刘少奇和夫人的茶会。
    随同阿里·沙斯特罗阿米佐约总理访问我国的印度尼西亚驻华大使莫诺努图，印度尼西亚外交部亚洲—太平洋司司长苏卡佐·维约普拉诺托和夫人，印度尼西亚驻印度大使巴拉，印度尼西亚驻苏联大使苏班德里奥和印度尼西亚国家计划局局长朱安达也应邀出席了茶会。
    在座的有全国人民代表大会常务委员会副委员长李济深、沈钧儒和黄炎培。
    我国驻印度尼西亚大使黄镇和夫人也在座。
    本报讯　二十九日上午，印度尼西亚共和国总理沙斯特罗阿米佐约和夫人及随行人员，在我国驻印度尼西亚大使黄镇和夫人、外交部礼宾司司长柯华陪同下，到北京东郊的国营北京第二棉纺织厂和国营北京第一棉纺织厂参观。
    沙斯特罗阿米佐约总理在国营北京第二棉纺织厂参观了全厂七个车间的全部生产过程，并询问了关于生产上的一些问题。这座新型的棉纺织厂是在去年三月才开始建筑的，今年四月已开始试车生产。它拥有十万纱锭和二千四百台布机。全厂的机械设备全部是中国各机械工厂所制造，其中包括一九五四年生产的最新机器单程式清花机、单程三道粗纱机和高速络筒机等。沙斯特罗阿米佐约总理在了解了这些情况后，代表他的夫人和随员们对这样一个大的工厂表示赞赏。
    接着，沙斯特罗阿米佐约总理又参观了拥有五万纱锭的国营北京第一棉纺织厂。沙斯特罗阿米佐约总理走进每一个车间，正在生产的工人们都热烈鼓掌欢迎贵宾。在整理车间，沙斯特罗阿米佐约总理观看了工人操作包装机的情况，并向杨惠洁厂长询问全厂一个月的产量。临别时，沙斯特罗阿米佐约总理表示很高兴看到这位女厂长领导下的工厂。（附图片）
