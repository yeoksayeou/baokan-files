Title: Reorganization of the USSR State Planning Committee
Author:
Date: 1955-05-27
Page: 4
　　Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, 26th - TASS reports: In order to improve the planning work of the Soviet Union's national economy, the Presidium of the Supreme Soviet of the USSR issued an order on the 25th to reorganize the State Planning Committee of the Council of Ministers of the USSR. The State Planning Committee is reorganized into:

　　1. The State Committee for Long-Term National Economic Planning of the Council of Ministers of the USSR (USSR State Planning Committee).

　　2. The State Economic Committee for Annual National Economic Planning of the Council of Ministers of the USSR (USSR State Economic Committee).

　　The Presidium of the Supreme Soviet of the USSR appointed N.K. Baibakov as Chairman of the State Committee for Long-Term National Economic Planning of the Council of Ministers of the USSR, relieving him of his post as Minister of the Petroleum Industry of the USSR; appointed M.Z. Saburov, First Deputy Chairman of the Council of Ministers of the USSR, as Chairman of the State Economic Committee for Annual National Economic Planning of the Council of Ministers of the USSR.

### 苏联国家计划委员会改组

1955-05-27
第4版()
专栏：

　  苏联国家计划委员会改组
    新华社二十六日讯　塔斯社莫斯科讯：为改进苏联国家国民经济计划的工作，苏联最高苏维埃主席团二十五日下令改组苏联部长会议国家计划委员会，把国家计划委员会改组为：
    一、苏联部长会议国民经济长期计划国家委员会（苏联国家计划委员会）。
    二、苏联部长会议国民经济年度计划国家经济委员会（苏联国家经济委员会）。
    苏联最高苏维埃主席团任命尼·康·巴伊巴科夫为苏联部长会议国民经济长期计划国家委员会主席，解除他的苏联石油工业部长职务；任命苏联部长会议第一副主席马·扎·萨布罗夫为苏联部长会议国民经济年度计划国家经济委员会主席。
