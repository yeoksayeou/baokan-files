Title: Chairman Mao and Vice Chairman Zhu Meet with Menon Separately; Premier Zhou and Vice Premier Chen Yi Host a Farewell Banquet for Menon
Author:
Date: 1955-05-21
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, 20th. Chairman Mao of the People's Republic of China met with Mr. Menon, the chief representative of India to the United Nations, at six o'clock in the afternoon of the 20th at Qin Zheng Dian, Zhongnanhai, Beijing.

	Present at the meeting were Zhou Enlai, Premier of the State Council, and Chen Yi, Vice Premier.

	Prior to this, Zhu De, Vice Chairman of the People's Republic of China, also met with Menon on the morning of the 18th.

	Xinhua News Agency, 20th. Zhou Enlai, Premier of the State Council of the People's Republic of China, and Chen Yi, Vice Premier, hosted a banquet in Beijing on the evening of the 20th to bid farewell to Menon, the chief representative of India to the United Nations, who is about to leave Beijing and return to his country.

	The Indian Ambassador to China, Mr. R.K. Nehru, and Mr. Kopardekar, Counselor of the Indian Embassy in China, were invited to attend the banquet.

	Also present were Zhang Wentian and Zhang Hanfu, Vice Ministers of Foreign Affairs, Qiao Guanhua, Assistant Minister, and Chen Jiakang, Assistant Minister and Director of the First Asian Department.

	Also present were Ding Xilin, President of the Sino-Indian Friendship Association, Zhang Xiruo, President of the Chinese People's Institute of Foreign Affairs, and people from all walks of life including Huang Yanpei, Zhang Zhizhong, Fu Zuoyi, Luo Longji, Cai Tingkai, Huang Shaohong, Lu Han, Zheng Dongguo, Weng Wenhao, and Wei Lihuang.

	The banquet proceeded in a friendly atmosphere.

### 毛主席和朱副主席分别接见梅农　周总理和陈毅副总理设宴为梅农饯行

1955-05-21
第1版()
专栏：

　　毛主席和朱副主席分别接见梅农
  　周总理和陈毅副总理设宴为梅农饯行
    新华社二十日讯　中华人民共和国主席毛泽东二十日下午六时在北京中南海勤政殿接见了印度驻联合国首席代表梅农。
    接见时，在座的有国务院总理周恩来、副总理陈毅。
    在此以前，中华人民共和国副主席朱德也曾于十八日上午接见了梅农。
    新华社二十日讯　中华人民共和国国务院总理周恩来和副总理陈毅二十日晚在北京设宴为即将离京返国的印度驻联合国首席代表梅农饯行。
    印度驻我国大使赖嘉文和印度驻我国大使馆参赞高伯登应邀出席了宴会。
    出席作陪的有外交部副部长张闻天、章汉夫，部长助理乔冠华，部长助理兼第一亚洲司司长陈家康。
    出席作陪的还有中印友好协会会长丁西林，中国人民外交学会会长张奚若，以及社会各界人士黄炎培、张治中、傅作义、罗隆基、蔡廷锴、黄绍竑、卢汉、郑洞国、翁文灏、卫立煌。
    宴会在友好的空气中进行。
