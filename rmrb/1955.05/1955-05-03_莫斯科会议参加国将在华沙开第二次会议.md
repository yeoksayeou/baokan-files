Title: Moscow Conference Participating Nations to Hold Second Meeting in Warsaw
Author:
Date: 1955-05-03
Page: 1
　　Xinhua News Agency, May 2nd: According to the decision of the Moscow Conference of European countries on ensuring peace and security in Europe, relevant consultations have recently been conducted among the governments of the Soviet Union, the Polish People's Republic, the Czechoslovak Republic, the German Democratic Republic, the Hungarian People's Republic, the Romanian People’s Republic, the Bulgarian People's Republic, and the Albanian People's Republic. These consultations primarily concerned the conclusion of a treaty of friendship, cooperation, and mutual assistance between the eight participating countries, and secondarily concerned the organization of a joint command of the treaty participating countries.

　　Now, due to the ratification of the Paris Agreements, the aforementioned countries have agreed to convene a second meeting in Warsaw on May 11th to discuss the issues arising from the decisions of the Moscow Conference.The Government of the People's Republic of China will also participate in the Warsaw Conference and will send its observers to the meeting.

### 莫斯科会议参加国将在华沙开第二次会议

1955-05-03
第1版()
专栏：

　　莫斯科会议参加国将在华沙开第二次会议
　　新华社二日讯　根据欧洲国家保障欧洲和平和安全的莫斯科会议的决定，最近时期在苏联、波兰人民共和国、捷克斯洛伐克共和国、德意志民主共和国、匈牙利人民共和国、罗马尼亚人民共和国、保加利亚人民共和国和阿尔巴尼亚人民共和国的政府之间，进行了相应的磋商。这些磋商首先涉及在八个会议参加国之间缔结友好、合作和互助条约的问题，其次涉及组织条约参加国的联合司令部的问题。
　　现在由于巴黎协定的批准，上述国家已议妥于五月十一日在华沙召开第二次会议，以讨论由莫斯科会议的决定所产生的问题。中华人民共和国政府也将参加华沙会议，它将派遣自己的观察员到会。
