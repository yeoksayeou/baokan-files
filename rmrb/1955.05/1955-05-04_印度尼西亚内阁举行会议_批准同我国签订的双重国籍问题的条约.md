Title: Indonesian Cabinet Meeting Approves Treaty with China on Dual Nationality Issue
Author: 
Date: 1955-05-04
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Jakarta, May 3rd: According to a report by Antara News Agency on the 3rd, the Indonesian Cabinet held a meeting on the evening of the 2nd. The meeting heard and approved Prime Minister Sastroamidjojo's report on the Asian-African Conference. The Cabinet meeting also approved the treaty between the People's Republic of China and the Republic of Indonesia on the issue of dual nationality, as well as the treaty of friendship between Indonesia and Afghanistan, which were signed during the Asian-African Conference. These treaties will soon be submitted to Parliament.

### 印度尼西亚内阁举行会议　批准同我国签订的双重国籍问题的条约

1955-05-04
第1版()
专栏：

　　印度尼西亚内阁举行会议
　　批准同我国签订的双重国籍问题的条约
　　新华社雅加达三日电　据安塔拉通讯社三日报道，印度尼西亚内阁在二日晚举行会议。会议听取并批准了沙斯特罗阿米佐约总理提出的关于亚非会议的报告。内阁会议还批准了在亚非会议期间签订的中华人民共和国和印度尼西亚共和国关于双重国籍问题的条约以及印度尼西亚和阿富汗的友好条约。这些条约不久即将提交议会。
