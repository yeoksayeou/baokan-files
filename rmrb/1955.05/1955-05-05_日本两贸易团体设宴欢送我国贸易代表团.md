Title: Japanese Trade Groups Host Banquet to Bid Farewell to Chinese Trade Delegation
Author:
Date: 1955-05-05
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, Tokyo, 4th - The Japan International Trade Promotion Association and the Japan Diet Members' League for the Promotion of Japan-China Trade held a banquet at Hannya-en in Tokyo on the afternoon of the 4th to bid farewell to the Chinese Trade Delegation visiting Japan.

Japanese attendees at the banquet included Mr. Shozo Murata, President of the Japan International Trade Promotion Association, Mr. Masanosuke Ikeda, Representative Director of the Japan Diet Members' League for the Promotion of Japan-China Trade, and Japanese negotiators Mr. Keita Hoashi, Mr. Koichi Uda, Mr. Masao Tajima, Mr. Ginzo Nagashima, Mr. Goro Sudo, and others, as well as business figures from shipbuilding, shipping, and textile machinery industries, including Mr. Tatsuo Kaminaka, Mr. Hiroshi Nitta, Mr. Seizo Miki, Mr. Den Kawakatsu, Mr. Kiichi Kodama, and others. Also present were Mr. Kanzo Uchiyama, Vice President of the Japan-China Friendship Association, Mr. Kanjima Majima, Secretary-General of the National Council for the Restoration of Japan-China and Japan-Soviet Diplomatic Relations, Mr. Tadao Kudo, Director of Foreign Affairs of the Japanese Red Cross Society, Mr. Kiyoto Ito, representative of the General Council of Trade Unions of Japan, Mr. Mingqiu Kang, a representative of the overseas Chinese community, and many others.

Mr. Shozo Murata said in his speech at the banquet: The Chinese Trade Delegation's visit to Japan for observation and discussions will not only help promote friendship and economic exchange between China and Japan but will also play a significant role in enhancing understanding between the peoples and business communities of the two countries. Mr. Masanosuke Ikeda also delivered a farewell speech at the banquet.

In his speech, delegation leader Lei Renmin expressed his gratitude for the cooperation extended by the Japanese side since the delegation's arrival in Japan. Lei Renmin said: I believe that with the joint efforts of all of you and those who are enthusiastic about China-Japan trade, the difficulties in implementing the China-Japan trade agreement signed this time can be overcome. Our common desire is to see the agreement signed this time fully realized.

During the banquet, the two Japanese trade groups negotiating the agreement presented souvenirs to the Chinese Trade Delegation visiting Japan.

### 日本两贸易团体设宴欢送我国贸易代表团

1955-05-05
第1版()
专栏：

　　日本两贸易团体设宴欢送我国贸易代表团
    新华社东京四日电　日本国际贸易促进协会、日本国会议员促进日中贸易联盟四日下午在东京般若苑举行宴会，欢送中国访问日本贸易代表团。
    日本方面出席宴会的有日本国际贸易促进协会会长村田省藏、日本国会议员促进日中贸易联盟代表理事池田正之辅和日本方面的谈判代表帆足计、宇田耕一、田岛正雄、长岛银藏、须藤五郎等人，以及造船、海运、纺织机械等企业界人士上中龙男、似田博、三木正三、川胜传、儿玉喜一等人，日中友好协会副会长内山完造，恢复日中、日苏邦交国民会议事务总长马岛涧，日本红十字会外事部长工藤忠夫，日本工会总评议会代表伊藤清远，华侨代表康鸣球等多人。
    村田省藏在宴会上致词说：中国贸易代表团这次在日本进行参观访问，不仅有助于促进中日两国的友好和经济交流，而且对增进两国人民和工商界之间的了解，将起很大的作用。池田正之辅也在宴会上致欢送词。
    雷任民团长在致词中对代表团来到日本后日本方面给予的合作表示感谢。雷任民说：我相信这次签订的中日贸易协定，在各位先生和热心于中日贸易人士共同努力下，困难是可以克服的。我们的共同愿望是使这次签订的协议圆满实现。
    席间，日本谈判贸易两团体向中国访日贸易代表团赠送了纪念品。
