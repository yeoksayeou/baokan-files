Title: Czechoslovak President Approves Eight-Nation Treaty of Friendship, Cooperation and Mutual Assistance
Author: 
Date: 1955-05-28
Page: 4
　　
    Xinhua News Agency, Prague, May 27th: According to the Czechoslovak News Agency, Czechoslovak Republic President Zapotocky approved on May 26th the Eight-Nation Treaty of Friendship, Cooperation and Mutual Assistance signed in Warsaw on May 14th.

### 捷总统批准八国友好合作互助条约

1955-05-28
第4版()
专栏：

　  捷总统批准八国友好合作互助条约
    新华社布拉格二十七日电　据捷克斯洛伐克通讯社报道：捷克斯洛伐克共和国总统萨波托斯基二十六日批准了五月十四日在华沙签订的八国友好合作互助条约。
