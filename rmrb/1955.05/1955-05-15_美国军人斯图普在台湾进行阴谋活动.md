Title: U.S. Military Man Stroup Engages in Conspiracy Activities in Taiwan
Author:
Date: 1955-05-15
Page: 4

　　Xinhua News Agency, 12th. U.S. Pacific Fleet Commander Stroup left Taiwan on the 11th after holding three days of secret talks with the Chiang Kai-shek traitorous clique. Stroup announced before his departure that he had discussed "mutually related issues" and military aid with the Chiang Kai-shek traitorous clique, and also discussed the issues of Kinmen and Matsu. He revealed that U.S. military aid to Taiwan "has been increasing due to increased needs," and will "continue to increase" in accordance with the U.S.-Chiang treaty. The U.S. Military Assistance Advisory Group will "take care of" the supply issues to the coastal islands occupied by Chiang's bandit troops.
　　Stroup inspected the jet aircraft base in Taiwan on the 10th. It is reported that the U.S.-Chiang talks discussed the establishment of a permanent U.S. air force base system in Taiwan and the stationing of more U.S. air force units and anti-aircraft artillery units.

### 美国军人斯图普在台湾进行阴谋活动

1955-05-15
第4版()
专栏：

　　美国军人斯图普在台湾进行阴谋活动
    新华社十二日讯　美国太平洋舰队司令斯图普同蒋介石卖国集团举行三天的秘密会谈以后，在十一日离开台湾。斯图普在临行时宣布，他曾和蒋介石卖国集团会谈“共同有关问题”和军事援助等问题，并且谈到有关金门和马祖问题。他透露，美国对台湾的军事援助“一直由于需要的增加而增加”，并且按照美蒋条约“还将继续增加”。美国军事援助顾问团将会“照顾”对蒋贼军盘踞下的沿海岛屿的供应问题。
    斯图普十日视察过台湾的喷气飞机基地。有消息说，在这次美蒋会谈中谈到了在台湾建立永久性的美国空军基地系统和驻扎更多的美国空军部队和高射炮部队的问题。
