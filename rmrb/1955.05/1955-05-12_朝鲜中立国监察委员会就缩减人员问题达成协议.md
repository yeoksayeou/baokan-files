Title: Neutral Nations Supervisory Commission on Korea Reaches Agreement on Reducing Personnel
Author:
Date: 1955-05-12
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Pyongyang, May 10th: According to a report from the Korean Central News Agency in Kaesong on the 8th, the Neutral Nations Supervisory Commission has reached a consensus agreement on specific measures concerning the reduction of commission personnel. Based on this, the Neutral Nations Supervisory Commission sent a letter to both the Korean-Chinese side and the U.S. side of the Military Armistice Commission on the 3rd, suggesting that the Military Armistice Commission agree to the changes regarding the Neutral Nations Inspection Teams.

According to the provisions of the Korean Armistice Agreement, any revised measures regarding the Neutral Nations Inspection Teams must be approved by both the Korean-Chinese side and the U.S. side of the Military Armistice Commission.

### 朝鲜中立国监察委员会就缩减人员问题达成协议

1955-05-12
第4版()
专栏：

　　朝鲜中立国监察委员会就缩减人员问题达成协议
    新华社平壤十日电　据朝鲜中央通讯社开城八日讯：据军事停战委员会朝中方面的消息，中立国监察委员会已就关于缩减委员会人员问题的具体措施达成了一致协议。在这个基础上，中立国监察委员会三日向军事停战委员会双方发出一封信，建议军事停战委员会同意中立国视察小组方面的变更。
    根据朝鲜停战协定的规定，任何关于中立国视察小组的修正的措施都必须得到军事停战委员会朝中方面和美方的同意。
