Title: Pollitt Delivers Campaign Speech at London Campaign Rally
Author:
Date: 1955-05-20
Page: 4
　
Xinhua News Agency, May 15th, TASS London, May 14th: British Communist Party General Secretary Harry Pollitt delivered a speech at a campaign rally in Southwark, London. He said, "The policy of arming Germany, the hydrogen bomb strategy, and the aggressive North Atlantic Treaty – these are policies of war, not policies of peace."

Pollitt said that the Communist Party is putting forward the following demands: do not produce hydrogen bombs, but conclude an international agreement on banning atomic weapons, hydrogen weapons, and reducing all armaments; do not arm West Germany, but create a European collective security treaty and establish a unified, democratic Germany; grant the People's Republic of China its seat in the United Nations; return Taiwan to the People's Republic of China, and withdraw American troops from Britain.

### 波立特在伦敦竞选大会上发表竞选演说

1955-05-20
第4版()
专栏：

　　波立特在伦敦竞选大会上发表竞选演说
    新华社十五日讯　塔斯社伦敦十四日讯：英国共产党总书记哈里·波立特在伦敦沙特沃克的竞选大会上发表演说。他说：“武装德国的政策、氢弹战略和侵略性的北大西洋条约——这些都是战争政策，而不是和平政策。”
    波立特说，共产党提出下列各项要求：不要生产氢弹，而要缔结关于禁止原子武器、氢武器和裁减一切军备的国际协定；不要武装西德，而要欧洲集体安全公约和建立统一的民主德国；给予中华人民共和国在联合国中的席位；把台湾归还中华人民共和国以及美军自英国撤退。
