Title: Vice Premier Peng Dehuai Visits the Construction and Activities of the Polish People's Army; Vice Premier Ulanhu Returns to Prague After Visiting Slovakia
Author:
Date: 1955-05-20
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, Warsaw, May 19th: Peng Dehuai, Vice Premier and Minister of National Defense of the People's Republic of China, who is visiting Poland at the invitation of the Polish government, has been visiting Poland for several days.

On the 16th, Vice Premier Peng Dehuai, accompanied by Polish Deputy Chairman of the Council of Ministers and Minister of National Defense Marshal Rokossovsky, visited a large-scale field exercise of the mechanized troops of the Polish People's Army. The commanders and fighters of the troops participating in the field exercise warmly welcomed Vice Premier Peng Dehuai.

On the 17th, Vice Premier Peng Dehuai, accompanied by the Chief of General Staff of the Polish People's Army, Bozhivovsky, visited the Military Engineering Academy of the Polish People's Army and the air force units equipped with jet aircraft manufactured in Poland. The air force units performed a formation flight display to welcome Vice Premier Peng Dehuai.

During the visit, Vice Premier Peng Dehuai was deeply impressed by the excellent equipment of the Polish People's Army, the perfection of teaching facilities, the thoughtful combat training plan, the strict organization, the skilled actions, the high learning enthusiasm of the commanders and fighters, and the close relationship between them.

On the evening of the 17th, Marshal Rokossovsky hosted a banquet in honor of Vice Premier Peng Dehuai. Wan Yi, Xiao Xiangrong, Chen Chu, Lei Yingfu, Wang Bingnan, Chinese Ambassador to Poland, and Lin Fang, Military Attaché, were invited to the banquet. Bozhivovsky and other senior Polish generals attended the banquet.

Xinhua News Agency, Prague, May 18th: The government delegation of the People's Republic of China, headed by Vice Premier of the State Council Ulanhu, returned to Prague on the 17th after visiting Slovakia for several days.

During the delegation's stay in Slovakia, accompanied by Jelen, member of the Political Bureau of the Central Committee of the Slovak Communist Party and Deputy Chairman of the Slovak Administrative Committee, they visited the city of Bratislava, the capital of Slovakia, as well as an aluminum factory, an art gallery, the Orava Dam, and a sanatorium in the Petra Mountain area. Accompanied by Pashek, Secretary of the Secretariat of the Central Committee of the Czechoslovak Communist Party, and Leitmeier, Minister of Metallurgy and Mining Industry, the delegation visited Ostrava, the coal and steel capital of Czechoslovakia, and visited the new and old plants of the Gottwald Iron and Steel Factory. The delegation also visited the historical sites of Brno.

### 彭德怀副总理参观波兰人民军建设和活动　乌兰夫副总理访问斯洛伐克后回布拉格

1955-05-20
第1版()
专栏：

　　彭德怀副总理参观波兰人民军建设和活动
　　乌兰夫副总理访问斯洛伐克后回布拉格
    新华社华沙十九日电　应波兰政府的邀请在波兰访问的中华人民共和国国务院副总理兼国防部长彭德怀，连日在波兰参观访问。
    十六日彭德怀副总理在波兰部长会议副主席兼国防部长罗科索夫斯基元帅的陪同下，参观了波兰人民军机械化部队规模巨大的野营演习。参加野营演习的部队的指战员热烈欢迎彭德怀副总理。
    十七日彭德怀副总理在波兰人民军总参谋长博齐沃夫斯基的陪同下参观了波兰人民军军事工程学院和用波兰自己制造的喷气式飞机装备起来的空军部队。空军部队为欢迎彭德怀副总理作了编队飞行表演。
    在参观中，彭德怀副总理对波兰人民军装备的优良、教学设备的完善、战斗训练计划的周到、组织的严密、动作的熟练、指战员的高度学习热情和他们之间关系的亲密等有很深刻的印象。
    十七日晚，罗科索夫斯基元帅设宴招待彭德怀副总理。应邀赴宴的还有万毅、萧向荣、陈楚、雷英夫和中国驻波兰大使王炳南、武官林放等。博齐沃夫斯基和其他波兰高级将领都出席了宴会。
    新华社布拉格十八日电　以国务院副总理乌兰夫为首的中华人民共和国政府代表团，在斯洛伐克进行了几天访问后，于十七日回到布拉格。
    代表团在斯洛伐克期间，曾由斯洛伐克共产党中央委员会政治局委员、斯洛伐克行政委员会副主席耶伦陪同，参观了斯洛伐克首府布拉迪斯拉发的市容和制铝工厂、油画馆以及奥拉瓦水闸和培特拉山区的疗养院等。代表团由捷克斯洛伐克共产党中央委员会书记处书记帕歇克和冶金工业与采矿部长雷特马耶尔陪同，到捷克斯洛伐克的煤钢之都俄斯特拉发访问，参观了哥特瓦尔德钢铁工厂的新旧两厂。代表团还参观了布尔诺的名胜古迹。
