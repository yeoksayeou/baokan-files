Title: Qishuyan Locomotive Factory Pays Attention to Utilizing Waste Materials
Author:
Date: 1955-05-20
Page: 2

The Qishuyan Locomotive and Rolling Stock Repair Factory of the Ministry of Railways utilized 174 tons of scrap steel in April, an increase of 40 percent compared to March, saving the state approximately 87,000 yuan. All workshops in the factory paid attention to utilizing waste materials in April. The Materials Utilization Workshop specifically organized waste material utilization teams and materials personnel from various workshops to discuss and research, and set utilization targets. Material staff from the forging and freight car workshops frequently visited the Materials Utilization Workshop to understand what waste materials could be utilized. The Materials Utilization Workshop strengthened its internal organization and inspection work, providing usable or processed materials to various workshops in a timely manner. Foreman Zhou Rongxiang paid attention to utilizing waste materials for production whenever he accepted new tasks. The group he belonged to exceeded the planned target for waste material utilization by 14 tons in April, saving the state more than 7,000 yuan. (Our correspondent in Nanjing)

### 戚墅堰机车厂注意利用废料

1955-05-20
第2版()
专栏：

　  戚墅堰机车厂注意利用废料
    铁道部戚墅堰机车车辆修理工厂在四月份利用废钢铁一百七十四吨，比三月份增加百分之四十，约为国家节省八万七千多元。全厂各车间四月份都注意进行了利用废料的工作。利材车间曾专门组织各车间利用废料小组和材料人员讨论研究，订出了利用废料的指标。锻冶、货车等车间的材料员经常到利材车间去了解可以利用的废料。利材车间内部加强了整理和检查工作，把可用或经过加工的材料比较及时地供给各车间使用。工长周荣祥每当接受新任务以后，都注意利用废料生产。他所在的小组四月份利用的废料超过计划指标十四吨，为国家节约七千多元。　（本报驻南京记者）
