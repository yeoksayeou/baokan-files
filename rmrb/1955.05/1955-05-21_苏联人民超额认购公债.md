Title: Soviet People Oversubscribe to Government Bonds
Author:
Date: 1955-05-21
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, May 20th: According to TASS, Moscow, May 19th: The subscription amount for the 1955 National Economic Development Bonds of the Soviet Union has exceeded the issuance amount (originally planned for 32 billion rubles). As of the 18th, the subscription amount has reached 34,276,973,000 rubles.

The Soviet Ministry of Finance, under the instructions of the Soviet Council of Ministers, has specifically ordered that the sale of government bonds be terminated uniformly in all regions starting from May 20th.

### 苏联人民超额认购公债

1955-05-21
第4版()
专栏：

　　苏联人民超额认购公债
    新华社二十日讯　据塔斯社莫斯科十九日讯：一九五五年发展苏联国民经济国家公债的认购数额已超过发行额（原定为三百二十亿卢布）。截至十八日为止，认购数额已达三百四十二亿七千六百九十七万三千卢布。
    苏联财政部根据苏联部长会议的指示，特下令自五月二十日起各地一律结束销售公债的工作。
