Title: European Countries Conference to Guarantee European Peace and Security Opens in Warsaw
Author:
Date: 1955-05-12
Page: 1
　
Xinhua News Agency, Warsaw, May 11th (TASS dispatch): The Conference of European Countries to Guarantee European Peace and Security opened at 10:00 a.m. on May 11th in the building of the Council of Ministers of the Polish People's Republic in Warsaw. This conference will discuss the issues arising from the decisions of the Moscow European Countries Conference held from November 29th to December 2nd, 1954.

　　Attending the conference were delegations from the eight signatory countries of the Moscow Declaration of December 2nd, 1954: the Soviet Union, the Polish People's Republic, the Czechoslovak Republic, the German Democratic Republic, the Hungarian People's Republic, the Romanian People’s Republic, the Bulgarian People's Republic, and the Albanian People's Republic. The members of the aforementioned delegations included heads of government, foreign ministers, defense ministers, and other national activists of each country.

　　Representatives of the People's Republic of China also participated in the conference as observers.

　　Polish and other countries' journalists attended the opening ceremony of the conference.

　　The head of the delegation of the Polish People's Republic, J. Cyrankiewicz, served as the chairman of the first meeting and delivered the opening speech.

　　Next, the head of the Soviet delegation, N.A. Bulganin, spoke.

　　After the speech by the head of the Soviet delegation, Bulganin, the head of the delegation of the Czechoslovak Republic, Široký, and the head of the delegation of the Polish People's Republic, Cyrankiewicz, spoke.

### 欧洲国家保障欧洲和平和安全会议在华沙开幕

1955-05-12
第1版()
专栏：

　　欧洲国家保障欧洲和平和安全会议在华沙开幕
    新华社十一日讯　塔斯社华沙十一日电：欧洲国家保障欧洲和平和安全会议于十一日上午十时在华沙波兰人民共和国部长会议大厦开幕。这次会议将讨论一九五四年十一月二十九日到十二月二日举行的莫斯科欧洲国家会议的决定所产生的问题。
    出席会议的有一九五四年十二月二日莫斯科宣言的签字国——苏联、波兰人民共和国、捷克斯洛伐克共和国、德意志民主共和国、匈牙利人民共和国、罗马尼亚人民共和国、保加利亚人民共和国和阿尔巴尼亚人民共和国八国的代表团。上述各国代表团的成员包括各国政府首脑、外交部长、国防部长及其他国家活动家。
    中华人民共和国的代表也以观察员的身份参加会议。
    波兰和其他国家的记者参加了会议开幕式。
    波兰人民共和国代表团团长约·西伦凯维兹担任第一次会议主席并致开幕词。
    接着，苏联代表团团长尼·亚·布尔加宁讲了话。
    在苏联代表团团长布尔加宁讲话以后，捷克斯洛伐克共和国代表团团长西罗基和波兰人民共和国代表团团长西伦凯维兹讲了话。
