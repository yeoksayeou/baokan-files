Title: Egyptian Prime Minister Nasser Says Egypt Supports the Principle of Countries Determining Their Own Destiny
Author:
Date: 1955-05-19
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Saipan, 17th – According to a report from Antara News Agency of Indonesia on the 16th: Egyptian Prime Minister Nasser, in a speech to the masses in Cairo after attending the Asian-African Conference, said: "I went to Bandung to announce that Egypt has gained complete independence, and to announce its support for the proposition that all countries in the world gain independence, and cooperate hand in hand without any country becoming a tool of a great power or a political battleground." Speaking of Egypt's foreign policy, Prime Minister Nasser said: "Our foreign policy is independent, and its purpose is to support the principle of countries determining their own destiny and safeguarding human rights."

### 埃及总理纳赛尔说　埃及支持各国决定自己的命运的原则

1955-05-19
第4版()
专栏：

　　埃及总理纳赛尔说
　　埃及支持各国决定自己的命运的原则
    新华社十七日讯　据印度尼西亚安塔拉通讯社塞班十六日消息报道：埃及总理纳赛尔在参加亚非会议以后在开罗向群众发表演说道：“我是到万隆去宣布埃及已经获得完全的独立的，并宣布它赞成这样的主张：全世界所有国家都获得独立，而在没有任何一个国家成为大国的工具，或是一块政治敌对的地盘的情况下携手合作。”在谈到埃及的外交政策时，纳赛尔总理说：“我们的外交政策是独立的，其目的在于支持由各国来决定它自己的命运和维护人权的原则。”
