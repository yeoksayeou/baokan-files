Title: Guo Moruo and Others Return to Beijing
Author: 
Date: 1955-05-05
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, 4th. Guo Moruo, head of the Chinese delegation attending the "Asian Countries Conference," and others, returned to Beijing by plane on the afternoon of the 4th. Those who welcomed them at the airport included Chen Shutong, Vice-Chairman of the Chinese People's Committee for World Peace, and representatives of the Soviet Academy of Sciences delegation currently visiting our country.
　　The remaining members of the delegation have returned home earlier.

### 郭沫若等回京

1955-05-05
第1版()
专栏：

　　郭沫若等回京
　　据新华社四日讯　出席“亚洲国家会议”的中国代表团团长郭沫若等已在四日下午乘飞机返回北京。到机场欢迎的有：中国人民保卫世界和平委员会副主席陈叔通等和正在我国访问的苏联科学院代表团代表。
　　代表团其余团员都已先期返国。
