Title: Albania, Bulgaria, Hungary, Germany, Poland, Romania, Soviet Union, Czechoslovakia Sign Eight-Nation Treaty of Friendship, Cooperation and Mutual Assistance
Author:
Date: 1955-05-15
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, Warsaw, 14th. The Treaty of Friendship, Cooperation and Mutual Assistance between the People's Republic of Albania, the People's Republic of Bulgaria, the People's Republic of Hungary, the German Democratic Republic, the People's Republic of Poland, the People's Republic of Romania, the Union of Soviet Socialist Republics, and the Czechoslovak Republic was signed on May 14th in the Polish People's Republic Council of State Building in Warsaw. The signing took place in a solemn atmosphere.

　　Signing the treaty were: Chairman of the Council of Ministers of the People's Republic of Albania, M. Shehu; Chairman of the Council of Ministers of the People's Republic of Bulgaria, V. Chervenkov; Chairman of the Council of Ministers of the People's Republic of Hungary, A. Hegedüs; Prime Minister of the German Democratic Republic, O. Grotewohl; Chairman of the Council of Ministers of the People's Republic of Poland, J. Cyrankiewicz; Chairman of the Council of Ministers of the People's Republic of Romania, G. Gheorghiu-Dej; Chairman of the Council of Ministers of the Union of Soviet Socialist Republics, N. A. Bulganin; and Prime Minister of the Czechoslovak Republic, V. Široký.

### 阿、保、匈、德、波、罗、苏、捷　八国友好合作互助条约签字

1955-05-15
第1版()
专栏：

　　阿、保、匈、德、波、罗、苏、捷
　　八国友好合作互助条约签字
    新华社华沙十四日电　阿尔巴尼亚人民共和国、保加利亚人民共和国、匈牙利人民共和国、德意志民主共和国、波兰人民共和国、罗马尼亚人民共和国、苏维埃社会主义共和国联盟和捷克斯洛伐克共和国之间的友好合作互助条约，于五月十四日在华沙波兰人民共和国国务委员会大厦内签字，签字在隆重的气氛中进行。
    在条约上签字的是：阿尔巴尼亚人民共和国部长会议主席穆·谢胡，保加利亚人民共和国部长会议主席伏·契尔文科夫，匈牙利人民共和国部长会议主席安·赫格居斯，德意志民主共和国总理奥·格罗提渥，波兰人民共和国部长会议主席约·西伦凯维兹，罗马尼亚人民共和国部长会议主席格·乔治乌—德治，苏维埃社会主义共和国联盟部长会议主席尼·亚·布尔加宁，捷克斯洛伐克共和国总理威·西罗基。
