Title: Joint Communiqué of the Soviet and Yugoslav Government Delegations
Author: 
Date: 1955-05-29
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency, Belgrade, May 27th, TASS dispatch: The joint communiqué of the Soviet Government Delegation and the Yugoslav Government Delegation is as follows:

　　The government delegation of the Union of Soviet Socialist Republics and the government delegation of the Federal People’s Republic of Yugoslavia held talks on May 27, 1955.
    The Yugoslav side attending the talks included the following members of the delegation: Josip Broz Tito (Head of Delegation), E. Kardelj, A. Ranković, S. Vukmanović-Tempo, K. Popović, M. Todorović, V. Mićunović, and D. Vidić, Ambassador of the Federal People’s Republic of Yugoslavia to the Soviet Union.
    The Soviet side attending the talks included the following members of the delegation: N.S. Khrushchev (Head of Delegation), N.A. Bulganin, A.I. Mikoyan, D.T. Shepilov, A.A. Gromyko, P.N. Kumykin, and V.A. Valkov, Soviet Ambassador to Yugoslavia.
    During the talks, opinions were exchanged on the international situation and the relations between the Federal People’s Republic of Yugoslavia and the Union of Soviet Socialist Republics.
    The next talks will be held on May 28, 1955.

### 苏南两国政府代表团发表会谈公报

1955-05-29
第4版()
专栏：

　　苏南两国政府代表团发表会谈公报
    新华社二十八日讯　塔斯社贝尔格莱德二十七日讯：苏联政府代表团和南斯拉夫政府代表团会谈公报如下：
    苏维埃社会主义共和国联盟政府代表团和南斯拉夫联邦人民共和国政府代表团于一九五五年五月二十七日进行了会谈。
    南斯拉夫方面出席会谈的有代表团人员：约瑟夫·布罗兹·铁托（代表团团长）、爱·卡德尔、亚·兰科维奇、斯·伏克曼诺维奇—泰波、科·波波维奇、米·托多罗维奇和维·米丘诺维奇和南斯拉夫联邦人民共和国驻苏大使德·维迪兹。
    苏联方面出席会谈的有代表团人员：尼·谢·赫鲁晓夫（代表团团长）、尼·亚·布尔加宁、阿·伊·米高扬、德·特·谢皮洛夫、安·安·葛罗米柯和帕·尼·库米金和苏联驻南斯拉夫大使瓦·阿·瓦尔科夫。
    在会谈中，就国际局势和南斯拉夫联邦人民共和国和苏维埃社会主义共和国联盟之间的关系问题交换了意见。
    下次会谈将于一九五五年五月二十八日举行。
