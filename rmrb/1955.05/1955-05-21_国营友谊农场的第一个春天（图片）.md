The First Spring of the State-Owned Friendship Farm

　　In early April, the State-Owned Friendship Farm sowed 2,000 hectares of spring wheat in a timely manner, of which more than 85 percent of the land adopted the Soviet advanced cross-sowing method.

　　Soviet experts V. A. Akhalomov and F. I. Kropyvchin went deep into the field to directly guide the cultivation. They are discussing the adjustment of the sowing amount on the sowing machine with Yang Xianbin, an agricultural technician from the 11th production team.

　　Workers at the State-Owned Friendship Farm are hanging five-bottom plows behind tractors, preparing for cultivation.

　　A large number of high-quality wheat seeds transported from various places have strongly supported the production of the State-Owned Friendship Farm.

　　The Fushun Artificial Petroleum Plant supplied the State-Owned Friendship Farm with a large amount of diesel fuel needed for tractors.

　　Xinhua News Agency reporter Tan Zhiqiang

### 国营友谊农场的第一个春天（图片）
谭志强
1955-05-21
第2版()
专栏：

　　国营友谊农场的第一个春天
    国营友谊农场在四月初适时地播种了二千公顷春小麦，其中百分之八十五以上的土地采用了苏联先进的交?播种法。
    苏联专家瓦·阿·阿哈洛莫也夫和夫·依·克罗必琴深入现场直接指导耕作，他们正同第十一生产队的农业技术员杨贤彬研究调整播种机上的播种量问题。
    国营友谊农场的工人们正在拖拉机后面挂五铧犁，准备耕作。
    从各地运到的大批优良麦种，有力地支援了国营友谊农场的生产。
    抚顺人造石油厂供给了国营友谊农场大量拖拉机所需的柴油。
                  新华社记者　谭志强摄
