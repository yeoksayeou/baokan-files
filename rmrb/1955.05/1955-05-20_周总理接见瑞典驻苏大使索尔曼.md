Title: Premier Zhou Enlai Receives Swedish Ambassador to the Soviet Union, Sohlman
Author:
Date: 1955-05-20
Page: 1

　　Xinhua News Agency, 19th. Premier Zhou Enlai of the State Council of the People's Republic of China received Swedish Ambassador to the Soviet Union, Mr. Sohlman, who is currently visiting Beijing, on the afternoon of the 19th.
  Present at the meeting was Huang Hua, Director of the Department of West European and African Affairs of our Ministry of Foreign Affairs.
  Also present was Swedish Ambassador to China, Mr. Wistrand.

### 周总理接见瑞典驻苏大使索尔曼

1955-05-20
第1版()
专栏：

　　周总理接见瑞典驻苏大使索尔曼
    新华社十九日讯　中华人民共和国国务院总理周恩来于十九日下午接见了现在北京访问的瑞典驻苏联大使索尔曼。
  在座的有我外交部西欧非洲司司长黄华。
  在座的还有瑞典驻华大使魏斯特朗。
