Title: Nasser Discusses Achievements of the Asian-African Conference
Author:
Date: 1955-05-04
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Cairo, May 3rd. TASS Cairo, May 2nd: Egyptian Prime Minister Nasser said in a statement in Cairo on May 2nd that the representatives of the Asian and African countries participating in the Bandung Conference "held a meeting for the first time without the participation of the countries that previously ruled them. Their gathering was to declare that they are no longer willing to live under foreign rule."

Nasser also said that although the conference participants had differences in ideology and political issues, they reached a consensus that peace must be maintained and war prevented, atomic weapons must be banned, and atomic energy must be used for the benefit of mankind.

### 纳赛尔谈亚非会议的成就

1955-05-04
第4版()
专栏：

　　纳赛尔谈亚非会议的成就
    新华社三日讯　塔斯社开罗五月二日讯：埃及总理纳赛尔五月二日在开罗发表谈话说，参加万隆会议的亚非国家代表“第一次在没有先前统治他们的国家的参加下举行会议。他们的集会是为了宣布：他们再也不愿意在外国统治下生活了”。
    纳赛尔还说，会议参加者尽管在思想意识和政治问题上存在着分歧，但是他们达成了一致协议，认为必须维护和平和防止战争，必须禁止原子武器并且利用原子能造福人类。
