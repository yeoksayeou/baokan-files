Title: Vice Chairman Zhu and Premier Zhou Receive Representatives of the Soviet Army in the Lushunkou Area
Author:
Date: 1955-05-23
Page: 1

　　Xinhua News Agency, May 22nd: At the invitation of the Ministry of National Defense of the People's Republic of China, a delegation of the Soviet Army stationed in the Lushunkou area, headed by General Shvetsov, arrived in Beijing on May 20th for a tour and visit.

　　Vice Chairman of the People's Republic of China Zhu De and Premier of the State Council Zhou Enlai received General Shvetsov, General Vasiagin, General Semenov, General Turantayev, General Voyevodin, General Peniazhko, General Kudryavtsev, Colonel Vasheko, and Colonel Belousov, nine representatives of the Soviet Army stationed in the Lushunkou area, at 6:00 PM on May 22nd.

　　Present at the reception were: Academician Yudin, the Soviet Ambassador to the People's Republic of China, and Colonel Fumin, the acting military attache, and others.

　　Also present at the reception were: Vice Premiers of the State Council He Long and Chen Yi, Vice Chairman of the National Defense Council Ye Jianying, Vice Ministers of National Defense Tan Zheng, Xiao Ke, and Li Da, Chief of the General Staff of the Chinese People's Liberation Army Su Yu, Deputy Chief of the General Staff Chen Geng, Deputy Director of the General Political Department Xiao Hua, Air Force Commander Liu Yalou, Deputy Commander of the Navy Wang Hongkun, Deputy Commander of the Artillery Qiu Chuangcheng, Commander of the Shenyang Military Region Deng Hua, and Commander of the Beijing-Tianjin Garrison Yang Chengwu, and others.

　　After the reception, a reception banquet and soiree were held.

### 朱副主席周总理接见驻旅顺口地区苏军代表

1955-05-23
第1版()
专栏：

　　朱副主席周总理接见驻旅顺口地区苏军代表
    新华社二十二日讯　驻旅顺口地区苏军部队代表什维佐夫将军等九人应中华人民共和国国防部邀请，在五月二十日来北京参观访问。
    中华人民共和国副主席朱德、国务院总理周恩来在二十二日下午六时接见了驻旅顺口地区苏军部队代表什维佐夫将军、瓦西亚金将军、谢苗诺夫将军、图兰塔耶夫将军、沃耶沃金将军、佩尼昂日科将军、库德里雅夫采夫将军、瓦谢科上校、别洛乌索夫上校等九人。
    接见时在座的有：苏联驻中华人民共和国大使尤金院士和代理武官富敏科上校等。
    接见时在座的还有：中华人民共和国国务院副总理贺龙、陈毅，国防委员会副主席叶剑英，国防部副部长谭政、萧克、李达，中国人民解放军总参谋长粟裕、副总参谋长陈赓，总政治部副主任萧华，空军司令员刘亚楼，海军副司令员王宏坤，炮兵副司令员邱创成，沈阳军区司令员邓华，京津卫戍司令员杨成武等。
    接见后，举行了招待宴会和晚会。
