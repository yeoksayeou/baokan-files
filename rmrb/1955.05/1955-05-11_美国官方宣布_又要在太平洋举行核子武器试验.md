Title: US Officially Announces Another Nuclear Weapons Test in the Pacific
Author:
Date: 1955-05-11
Page: 4

　　US Officially Announces
　　Another Nuclear Weapons Test in the Pacific

Xinhua News Agency, Washington, 10th. The U.S. Department of Defense and the Atomic Energy Commission jointly announced on the 9th that the United States will soon conduct an underwater nuclear weapons test in the Eastern Pacific. Last year, the U.S. government tested hydrogen bombs in the Marshall Islands region of the Pacific Ocean, harming Japanese fishermen fishing on the high seas, which was condemned by Japan and widespread public opinion around the world.

### 美国官方宣布　又要在太平洋举行核子武器试验

1955-05-11
第4版()
专栏：

　　美国官方宣布
　　又要在太平洋举行核子武器试验
    新华社十日讯　华盛顿消息：美国国防部和原子能委员会九日联合宣布，美国不久将在东太平洋举行一次海底核子武器试验。美国政府去年在太平洋的马绍尔群岛地区试验氢弹，伤害了在公海上捕鱼的日本渔民，曾经受到日本和世界广泛的舆论的谴责。
