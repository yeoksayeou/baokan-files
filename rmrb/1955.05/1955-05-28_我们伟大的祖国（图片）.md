    Our Great Motherland
    Beijing Municipality is actively developing the organization of the Young Pioneers. Now there are more than 150,000 Young Pioneers. The new members of the Young Pioneers of the First Central Primary School in the Dongsi District have begun to wear bright red scarves.

### 我们伟大的祖国（图片）

1955-05-28

专栏：

    我们伟大的祖国
    北京市积极发展少年先锋队的组织，现在少年先锋队员已有十五万多人。东四区第一中心小学少年先锋队的新队员们，开始戴上了鲜艳的红领巾。
