Title: The People of Lüda Warmly See Off the Withdrawal of Soviet Troops from Lushunkou (Photos)
Author:
Date: 1955-05-09
Page: 2

Lüda People Warmly See Off the Withdrawal of Soviet Troops from Lushunkou

    People from all walks of life in Lushun City held a meeting to warmly see off the Soviet troops stationed in the Lushunkou area who were about to withdraw to their country.
                    Xinhua News Agency Reporter Zou Jiandong (photograph)
    Women in Lüda City held a farewell party to see off the families of Soviet military officers stationed in the Lushunkou area. This is Jin Lingzhi (second from the left in the front row), a labor model from Lüda City, asking Soviet women to sign souvenirs exchanged as mementos. Xinhua News Agency Reporter Zou Jiandong (photograph)
    Grandma Yu Xiaolan, 62 years old, from Lushun City, gives gifts to Soviet soldiers.
                    Xinhua News Agency Reporter Zou Jiandong (photograph)
    Doctors from the Lushun City Hospital give gifts to Soviet military doctors.
                    Xinhua News Agency Reporter Zou Jiandong (photograph)
    Young Pioneers give their most beloved red scarves to Soviet uncles.
                    Xinhua News Agency draft, Ye Ping (photograph)
