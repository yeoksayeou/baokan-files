Title: New Zealand MP Warren Freer Returns Home
Author:
Date: 1955-05-08
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, May 5th. Warren Wilfred Freer, a member of the New Zealand Parliament and his wife, who were invited by the Chinese People's Institute of Foreign Affairs to visit our country, visited Shenyang, Anshan, Shanghai and other places before and after attending our country's "May 1st" celebrations. They left Beijing on the 5th via Guangzhou to return home.

### 新西兰议员华伦·弗里尔返国

1955-05-08
第4版()
专栏：

　　新西兰议员华伦·弗里尔返国
    新华社五日讯　应中国人民外交学会邀请来我国访问的新西兰议会议员华伦·维尔菲雷德·弗里尔和夫人，在参加我国“五一”节观礼前后，到沈阳、鞍山、上海等地参观访问，五日离北京取道广州返国。
