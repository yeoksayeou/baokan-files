Title: Commander Wang Dinglie of a certain unit of the People's Air Force Receives He Weiqin
Author:
Date: 1955-05-25
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Guangzhou, May 24th: Wang Dinglie, commander of a certain unit of the Chinese People's Liberation Army Air Force, received He Weiqin, former Kuomintang Air Force Lieutenant, Second-Class Intelligence Officer, who had revolted and flown back, on the morning of the 22nd.

Wang Dinglie, on behalf of the People's Air Force, expressed a warm welcome to He Weiqin's patriotic act of revolting and flying back to the motherland's mainland. He also explained the policies and reward measures promulgated by Peng Dehuai, Minister of National Defense of the People's Republic of China, for Kuomintang officers and soldiers who revolt and surrender, and introduced the rapid development of the People's Air Force and the leap forward in the motherland's construction. Wang Dinglie said that the people of the motherland welcome the Kuomintang officers and soldiers who are still in Taiwan to learn from He Weiqin's example of abandoning the darkness and turning to the light, and to return to the mainland of the motherland.

He Weiqin expressed his gratitude for the warm welcome and enthusiastic reception from the Chinese People's Liberation Army and the local people. He said, "The people of the motherland treat me so well, I will definitely work hard for the people in the future."

### 人民空军某部指挥员王定烈接见何伟钦

1955-05-25
第1版()
专栏：

　　人民空军某部指挥员王定烈接见何伟钦
    新华社广州二十四日电　中国人民解放军空军某部指挥员王定烈，二十二日上午接见了驾机起义的前蒋军空军中尉二级情报官何伟钦。
    王定烈代表人民空军，对何伟钦起义飞回祖国大陆的爱国行动表示热烈欢迎，并向何伟钦阐述了中华人民共和国国防部长彭德怀颁布的对蒋军起义、投诚人员的政策及奖励办法，介绍了人民空军日益强大和祖国建设突飞猛进的情况。王定烈说，祖国人民欢迎还留在台湾的蒋军官兵学习何伟钦弃暗投明的榜样，回到祖国大陆来。
    何伟钦对中国人民解放军和当地人民的热烈欢迎和热情接待表示感激。他说：“祖国人民待我这样好，我今后一定好好地为人民做事。”
