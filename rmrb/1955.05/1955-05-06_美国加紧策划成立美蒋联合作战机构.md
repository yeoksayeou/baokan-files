Title: US Intensifies Plans to Establish US-Chiang Joint Operations Agency
Author: 
Date: 1955-05-06
Page: 4

　　Xinhua News Agency, 4th. The United States is further planning to establish a joint operations agency with the Chiang Kai-shek traitorous clique. According to International News Service, in order to finalize the establishment of a US-Chiang joint military action center, Hopwood, Chief of Staff of the United States Pacific Fleet, recently visited Taiwan. The joint military action center will merge the Taiwan Liaison Center established not long ago. The report stated that the new joint military agency is an expansion of the Liaison Center, which is in charge of coordinating the Chiang bandit navy and the US Seventh Fleet, including the US Air Force providing cover for the Chiang bandit navy operating in the Taiwan Strait.

### 美国加紧策划成立美蒋联合作战机构

1955-05-06
第4版()
专栏：

　　美国加紧策划成立美蒋联合作战机构
    新华社四日讯　美国正在进一步策划和蒋介石卖国集团成立联合作战机构。据国际新闻社消息，为了最后成立美蒋联合军事行动中心，美国太平洋舰队司令参谋长霍普伍德日前访问了台湾。这个联合军事行动中心将合并不久以前成立的台湾联络中心。消息说，新的联合军事机构是联络中心的扩大物，联络中心掌管调配蒋贼海军和美国第七舰队、包括掩护在台湾海峡活动的蒋贼海军的美国空军的事宜。
