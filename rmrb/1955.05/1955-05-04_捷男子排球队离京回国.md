Title: Czechoslovakian Men's Volleyball Team Leaves Beijing for Home

Author: 
Date: 1955-05-04
Page: 4

　　Xinhua News Agency Report: The seventeen-member Czechoslovak People's Army Central House Men's Volleyball Team, who were invited to visit our country, left Beijing by plane on the morning of the 3rd to return home.
    Those who went to the airport to see them off included the heads of units such as the State Physical Culture and Sports Commission of the People's Republic of China, the Cultural Department of the General Political Department of the Chinese People's Liberation Army, and the Beijing Physical Culture and Sports Commission, as well as more than 50 of our country's volleyball players.

### 捷男子排球队离京回国

1955-05-04
第4版()
专栏：

　　捷男子排球队离京回国
　　据新华社讯　应邀来我国访问的捷克斯洛伐克军队中央之家男子排球队全体人员一行十七人已在三日晨乘飞机离北京回国。
    到机场欢送的有中华人民共和国体育运动委员会、中国人民解放军总政治部文化部和北京市体育运动委员会等单位的负责人，以及我国的排球运动员等共五十多人。
