Title: Many Indian Newspapers Comment on Premier Zhou's Report on the Asian-African Conference
Author: 
Date: 1955-05-22
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, New Delhi, May 21st. Indian newspapers continue to comment on Premier Zhou Enlai's stance on the Taiwan issue in his report on the Asian-African Conference to the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress of China.

　　The "New Century" editorial published on the 20th pointed out that the part of Premier Zhou Enlai's report on the Taiwan issue is "an important step in easing the tension in the Far East." The editorial said, "The only reason for the tension in this region is American military intervention."

　　The editorial then refuted the unreasonable conditions put forward by the United States to obstruct negotiations, saying that the United States put forward these conditions intending to continue the occupation of Taiwan by the United States and its Kuomintang puppets. The editorial pointed out, "Now, the rulers of the United States must face reality and take the path of negotiation instead of threats and blackmail."

　　The "National Herald" editorial on the 19th said that the purpose of negotiations to ease the tension in the Taiwan region can only be the withdrawal of the U.S. Seventh Fleet from the Taiwan Strait and the end of U.S. interference in China. In criticizing some people's argument of putting Taiwan under UN trusteeship, the editorial said, "UN trusteeship means US trusteeship and separates Taiwan from China."

　　The "Hindu" editorial published on the 19th said that the United States' so-called "defense" argument in the Pacific is a factor complicating the situation in the region. "It is definitely inappropriate for the United States to regard Taiwan and the Penghu Islands as bases serving American interests." The editorial said that India recognizes that Taiwan belongs to the legitimate government of all of China.

　　The "Amrita Bazar Patrika" editorial on the 19th believes that the United States' attitude towards the Taiwan issue "is to want China to give up its demands on Taiwan." The editorial said that this is not only a violation of past treaties, but also ignores the fact that Taiwan is a part of China.

### 印度许多报纸　评周总理关于亚非会议报告

1955-05-22
第4版()
专栏：

　　印度许多报纸
  　评周总理关于亚非会议报告
    新华社新德里二十一日电　印度报纸继续评论周恩来总理在中国全国人民代表大会常务委员会会议上关于亚非会议的报告中就台湾问题所表明的立场。
      “新世纪报”二十日发表的社论指出周恩来总理报告中关于台湾问题的部分是“缓和远东紧张局势的一个重要步骤”。社论说，“造成这个地区的紧张局势的唯一原因就是美国的军事干涉。”
    社论接着驳斥美国为了阻挠谈判而提出的无理条件说，美国提出这些条件是打算由美国和它的国民党傀儡继续占领台湾。社论指出，“现在，美国的统治者必须面对现实，并且采取谈判的道路而不是威胁和讹诈的道路。”
      “国民先驱报”十九日的社论说，缓和台湾地区紧张局势的谈判的目的只能是美国第七舰队撤出台湾海峡，并结束美国对中国的干涉。在抨击有些人提出的由联合国托管台湾的论调时，社论说，
  “联合国托管就是意味着美国托管，并且使台湾脱离中国。”
    “印度教徒报”十九日发表社论说，美国在太平洋的所谓“防御”的论调是使这个区域形势复杂起来的因素。“美国把台湾和澎湖列岛看作是为美国利益服务的基地，这肯定地是不恰当的。”社论说，印度承认台湾是属于全中国的合法政府的。
      “甘露市场报”十九日的社论认为美国对台湾问题的态度“是想要中国放弃对台湾的要求”。社论说，这不仅仅是违反了过去的条约，而且也是无视台湾是中国的一部分这个事实。
