Title: Indonesian Ambassador to the Soviet Union, Subandrio, Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1955-05-27
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, May 26th:

　　Indonesian Ambassador to the Soviet Union, Subandrio, who is accompanying Indonesian Prime Minister Ali Sastroamidjojo on his visit to China, arrived in Beijing by plane from the Soviet Union on the morning of May 26th.

　　Assistant Minister of Foreign Affairs Chen Jiakang and Director of the Protocol Department Ke Hua were at the airport to greet him.

　　Also at the airport to greet him were officials from the Indonesian Embassy in China and the Soviet Embassy in China.

### 印度尼西亚驻苏大使　苏班德里奥到北京

1955-05-27
第1版()
专栏：

　　印度尼西亚驻苏大使
  　苏班德里奥到北京
    新华社二十六日讯　随同印度尼西亚共和国总理阿里·沙斯特罗阿米佐约访问我国的印度尼西亚驻苏联大使苏班德里奥，在二十六日上午自苏联乘飞机到达北京。
    到机场迎接的有我国外交部部长助理陈家康、礼宾司司长柯华。
    到机场迎接的还有印度尼西亚驻我国大使馆和苏联驻我国大使馆官员。
