Title: The Hotan Region of Xinjiang Develops Silk Production
Author:
Date: 1955-05-15
Page: 2

The Hotan Region of Xinjiang Province plans to promote more than 48,500 boxes of silkworm eggs this year, an increase of 20,500 boxes compared to 1954.

The Hotan Region is located south of the Tianshan Mountains, with a warm climate suitable for silkworm rearing. Local Uyghur farmers also have a tradition of silkworm rearing. In order to create favorable conditions for a bumper harvest of cocoons, in the winter of 1954, Moyu, Hotan, and Lop County established short-term sericulture training classes. In early March this year, the Hotan Sericulture Experiment Station also sent cadres to Lop, Pishan, Moyu, and other counties, focusing on agricultural producers' cooperatives, to impart silkworm rearing and mulberry planting techniques.
　　　　　　　　　　　　　(Our correspondent in Urumqi)

### 新疆和阗专区发展蚕丝生产

1955-05-15
第2版()
专栏：

　　新疆和阗专区发展蚕丝生产
    新疆省和阗专区今年计划推广蚕种四万八千五百多盒，比一九五四年增加两万零五百盒。
    和阗专区在天山以南，气候温暖，适于养蚕。当地维吾尔族农民也有养蚕习惯。为了给蚕茧丰收创造有利条件，一九五四年冬季，墨玉、和阗、洛浦三县开办了蚕桑短期训练班。今年三月初，和阗蚕桑试验站还派出干部，分别到洛浦、皮山、墨玉等县，以农业生产合作社为重点，传授养蚕、栽桑的技术。
                    （本报驻乌鲁木齐记者）
