Title: French Medical Delegation Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1955-05-26
Page: 4

　　Xinhua News Agency, May 25th: The French medical delegation, invited by the Chinese Medical Association to visit China, arrived in Beijing at 4:00 PM on May 24th. The delegation consists of Mr. René Digon, President of the French Surgical Society, cardiac surgeon, and professor of surgery at the Toulouse School of Medicine; Mrs. Digon; and Mr. Gaili Haima, a gynecological expert and professor of gynecology at the Toulouse School of Medicine.

　　Fang Shishan, Vice Chairman of the Chinese Medical Association, Cui Yitian, Director, and members of the medical community in the capital were at the airport to welcome them.

### 法国医学代表团到京

1955-05-26
第4版()
专栏：

　  法国医学代表团到京
    新华社二十五日讯　应中华医学会邀请前来我国访问的法国医学代表团——法国外科学会会长、心脏外科专家、督卢兹医学院外科教授瑞·迪根，迪根夫人及妇科专家、督卢兹医学院妇科教授盖利海玛，一行三人在五月二十四日下午四时到达北京。
    到机场欢迎的有中华医学会副理事长方石珊，理事崔义田以及首都医药界人士。
