Title: Soviet Army in Lushunkou Transfers Cultural, Health, and Other Facilities to Our Army Without Compensation

Author:
Date: 1955-05-23
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Lushun-Dalian, May 22nd: The Soviet Army stationed in the Lushunkou area has transferred all cultural, health, welfare, and other facilities to the Chinese People's Liberation Army troops stationed in the Lushunkou area without compensation.

　　Many Chinese military doctors are now working in the twelve military hospitals with complete medical equipment that have been taken over. In the four huge "Officers' Homes" and dozens of clubs, many officers and soldiers of the Chinese People's Liberation Army engage in various recreational activities during their evening rest periods and on Sundays. The bathhouses that have been taken over have begun to regularly serve Chinese soldiers and officers. These bathhouses have complete modern equipment. The Soviet Army also transferred six nurseries, ten primary and secondary schools that can accommodate thousands of people, a cultural and recreational equipment repair factory, the Lushun Military History Museum, and a large-scale newspaper printing factory to the Chinese troops.

　　During the handover process, Soviet soldiers enthusiastically taught Chinese soldiers the technology or management methods for mastering this equipment.

### 旅顺口苏军文化卫生等设备无偿移交我军

1955-05-23
第1版()
专栏：

　　旅顺口苏军文化卫生等设备无偿移交我军
    新华社旅大二十二日电　驻旅顺口地区苏军把所有的文化、卫生、福利等设备全部无偿地移交给了中国人民解放军驻旅顺口地区部队。
    现在已有许多中国军医陆续在接收过来的具有完整医疗设备的十二所军医医院里工作。在四所规模巨大的“军官之家”和数十处俱乐部里，在每天的黄昏休息的时候和星期日，都有许多中国人民解放军官兵在那里进行各种娱乐活动。接收过来的澡塘已开始定期招待中国战士和军官。这些澡塘，都有完整的近代化设备。苏军移交给中国部队的还有六处托儿所、可容纳数千人的十所中小学校、一个文娱器材修理厂、旅顺军事历史博物馆和规模庞大的报社印刷厂等。
    在交接的过程中苏军战士们热情地教会中国战士掌握这些设备的技术或管理方法。
