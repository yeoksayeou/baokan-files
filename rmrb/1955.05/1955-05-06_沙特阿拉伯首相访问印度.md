Title: Saudi Arabian Prime Minister Visits India
Author:
Date: 1955-05-06
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency Dispatch: Prince Faisal Al Saud, Prime Minister and Foreign Minister of Saudi Arabia, arrived in New Delhi on the 2nd for a visit. Indian Prime Minister Nehru greeted him at the airport. Prince Faisal Al Saud will be in India for a three-day visit.

### 沙特阿拉伯首相访问印度

1955-05-06
第4版()
专栏：

　  沙特阿拉伯首相访问印度
    据新华社讯　沙特阿拉伯首相兼外交大臣费萨尔·沙特太子在二日抵达新德里访问。印度总理尼赫鲁到飞机场迎接。费萨尔·沙特太子将在印度作为期三天的访问。
