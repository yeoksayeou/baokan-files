Title: Lao Kingdom Army Continues to Attack Pathet Lao Forces
Author:
Date: 1955-05-06
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Hanoi, May 5th: According to the Vietnam News Agency on the 4th, Vientiane Radio has been constantly clamoring in the past few days that the Lao side has dispatched "reinforcements" and will "attack" the Kingdom's army in Muong Peng and Hui Tou. According to information obtained from the Lao side, in reality, the Kingdom's army has been constantly attacking the Pathet Lao fighting forces.

　　On the afternoon of April 23, the Kingdom's army launched an attack on the Pathet Lao fighting forces on the road from Muong Peng to Na Nong.

　　On April 27, a platoon of the Kingdom's army attacked a Lao unit stationed in Peng No.

　　The next day, two platoons of the Kingdom's army launched another attack on the Pathet Lao forces in Peng Nei.

### 老挝王国军队仍在进攻寮国部队

1955-05-06
第4版()
专栏：

　　老挝王国军队仍在进攻寮国部队
    新华社河内五日电　据越南通讯社四日讯，过去几天，万象电台不断地叫嚷寮国方面已经派遣“援军”并将“攻击”在芒奔和会头的王国军队。据从寮国方面获得的消息，实际上是王国军队在不断进攻寮国战斗部队。
    四月二十三日下午，王国军队在从芒奔通到那农的公路上向寮国战斗部队发动了进攻。
    四月二十七日，王国军队一个排进攻了驻在彭诺的一支寮国部队。
    第二天，王国军队两个排又在彭内向寮国部队进行了另一次攻击。
