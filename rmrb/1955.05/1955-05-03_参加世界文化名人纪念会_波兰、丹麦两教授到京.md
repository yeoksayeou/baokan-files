Title: Polish and Danish Professors Arrive in Beijing to Attend World Cultural Figures Commemoration Meeting
Author:
Date: 1955-05-03
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, May 2nd: Polish representative Kazimierz Budzyk, professor of Warsaw University and literary historian, arrived in Beijing by plane on May 2nd, having been invited to China to attend the World Cultural Figures Commemoration Meeting to be held in Beijing in early May. In addition to participating in the commemoration meeting in Beijing, Kazimierz Budzyk will also visit other cities.

Xinhua News Agency: At the invitation of the Chinese People's Committee for World Peace and the Chinese People's Association for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries, Danish literature professor and Hans Christian Andersen expert Svend Prange Møller Kristensen and his wife arrived in Beijing by plane on the afternoon of April 28th to participate in activities commemorating world cultural figures in China.

Representatives from the Chinese People's Committee for World Peace, the Chinese People's Association for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries, and the Chinese Writers Association welcomed them at the airport.

### 参加世界文化名人纪念会　波兰、丹麦两教授到京

1955-05-03
第1版()
专栏：

　　参加世界文化名人纪念会
　　波兰、丹麦两教授到京
    新华社二日讯　应邀前来我国参加五月初在京举行的世界文化名人纪念会的波兰代表克·布泽克在五月二日乘飞机到达北京。克·布泽克是华沙大学教授、文学历史家，除在京参加纪念大会外，并将到其他城市参观访问。
　　据新华社讯　应中国人民保卫世界和平委员会和中国人民对外文化协会的邀请，来我国参加纪念世界文化名人活动的丹麦文学教授、研究安徒生的专家斯温普·穆尔·克里斯坦逊和他的夫人在四月二十八日下午乘飞机到达北京。
　　到机场欢迎的有中国人民保卫世界和平委员会、中国人民对外文化协会和中国作家协会的代表。
