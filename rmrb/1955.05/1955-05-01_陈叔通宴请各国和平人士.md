Title: Chen Shutong Hosts a Banquet for Peace Advocates from Various Countries
Author:
Date: 1955-05-01
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, 30th April. Chen Shutong, Vice-Chairman of the Chinese People's Committee for World Peace, hosted a banquet on the evening of the 30th to welcome peace advocates from Denmark, Japan, Jordan, Syria, and other countries who have recently visited China.

Invited guests included: Danish Professor Christenson and his wife; the Japanese delegation to China headed by Kinichi Yoshioka; Ryosuke Miyazaki, Chairman of the Tokyo Peace Conference; Semail Miyazaki, a member of the Japan Peace Supporters Committee; Khalifa, editor of the Jordanian literary magazine; and Zahor, a member of the Syrian Parliament.

Peace advocates from Japan, New Zealand, and other countries in Beijing were also invited to attend the banquet.

Also present were: Xu Deheng, Xu Baoju, and Luo Longji, Standing Committee members of the Chinese People's Committee for World Peace, as well as representatives from various people's organizations, including Wu Hongbin and Liao Mengxing.

The banquet proceeded in a warm and friendly atmosphere.

### 陈叔通宴请各国和平人士

1955-05-01
第1版()
专栏：

　　陈叔通宴请各国和平人士
    新华社三十日讯　中国人民保卫世界和平委员会副主席陈叔通在三十日晚设宴欢迎最近来我国访问的丹麦、日本、约旦、叙利亚等国和平人士。
    应邀出席的有：丹麦教授克里斯坦逊和他的夫人，以吉冈金市为首的日本访华代表团，日本东京都和平会议主席宫崎龙介，日本拥护和平委员会委员宫崎世民，约旦文学杂志编辑哈利法，叙利亚议会议员查霍尔等。
    在京的日本、新西兰等国和平人士也应邀出席了宴会。
    出席作陪的有：中国人民保卫世界和平委员会常务委员许德珩、许宝驹、罗隆基，以及各人民团体代表吴鸿宾、廖梦醒等。
    宴会在热烈友好空气中进行。
