Title: Indonesian Newspapers Praise Prime Minister Sastroamidjojo's Visit to China; All-China Journalists Association Hosts Banquet to Welcome Indonesian Journalists
Author:
Date: 1955-05-28
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency, Jakarta, 26th. Jakarta newspapers on the 26th praised Prime Minister Ali Sastroamidjojo's visit to China.

　　The Indonesian "People's Daily" said that a policy of peace and good neighborliness is very important for Indonesia, and Prime Minister Ali Sastroamidjojo's visit to China is a good start. "This will give the Indonesian people and government greater impetus to take far-reaching steps for the benefit of Indonesia and the world."

　　The newspaper pointed out, "Indonesia increasingly feels the need to establish normal and smooth economic relations with all other countries, especially with the People's Republic of China, because the People's Republic of China implements mutually beneficial trade policies and has no economic crisis."

　　"Dongxing Daily" said that this visit "is a step forward in implementing a policy of international peace and cooperation that is beneficial to both countries. This is in line with the Five Principles of Peaceful Coexistence."

　　"Indonesia News" said that Prime Minister Sastroamidjojo's visit is a manifestation of the spirit of cooperation and peaceful coexistence among Asian and African countries from the Asian-African Conference.

　　When talking about the Taiwan issue, "Indonesia News" pointed out that from the perspective of the Indonesian government represented by Prime Minister Sastroamidjojo, Taiwan is clearly the territory of the People's Republic of China and must be returned to the People's Republic of China.

　　The newspaper hoped that Prime Minister Sastroamidjojo's visit would further strengthen the economic relations between the two countries.

　　Xinhua News Agency, 27th. Deng Tuo, President of the All-China Journalists Association, held a reception in Beijing on the 27th to welcome the Indonesian journalists who accompanied Indonesian Prime Minister Ali Sastroamidjojo on his visit to China. They are: Asa Bafaji of "Social Envoy Newspaper", Sunayo of Antara News Agency, Suleiman of "Indonesian Torch Newspaper", Kuluweite of "Dongxing Daily" and Da Xin Suladi of "Indonesia News". More than 50 people from Beijing press circles and foreign journalists attended the reception. Indonesian press officer to China Isnan and Gong Peng, director of the Information Department of the Chinese Ministry of Foreign Affairs, were also invited to attend the reception. ### 印度尼西亚报纸  赞扬沙斯特罗阿米佐约总理访问中国  我新闻工作者联谊会设宴欢迎印度尼西亚记者

1955-05-28
第4版()
专栏：

　　印度尼西亚报纸
　  赞扬沙斯特罗阿米佐约总理访问中国
    我新闻工作者联谊会设宴欢迎印度尼西亚记者
    据新华社雅加达二十六日电　雅加达各报二十六日对阿里·沙斯特罗阿米佐约总理前往中国访问表示赞扬。
    印度尼西亚“人民日报”说，和平和睦邻政策对于印度尼西亚来说是十分重要的，阿里·沙斯特罗阿米佐约总理的访问中国是一个良好的开端。“这将给予印度尼西亚人民和政府更大的动力来为了印度尼西亚和全世界的利益而采取远大的步骤”。
    这家报纸指出，“印度尼西亚日益感到有必要同所有其他国家，特别是同中华人民共和国建立正常的、顺利的经济关系，因为中华人民共和国执行着互利的贸易政策，而且没有经济危机”。
    “东星报”说，这次访问“是在执行对两国都有利的国际和平和合作的政策的道路上前进了一步。这是符合和平共处的五项原则的。”
    “印度尼西亚新闻”说，沙斯特罗阿米佐约总理的这次访问是贯彻亚非会议的亚非国家之间进行合作和和平共处的精神。
    “印度尼西亚新闻”在谈到台湾问题时指出，从以沙斯特罗阿米佐约总理为代表的印度尼西亚政府的观点来看，台湾很明显是中华人民共和国的领土，必须交还给中华人民共和国。
    这家报纸希望沙斯特罗阿米佐约总理的这次访问会使两国之间的经济关系更加密切。
    新华社二十七日讯中国新闻工作者联谊会会长邓拓二十七日在北京举行酒会，欢迎随印度尼西亚阿里·沙斯特罗阿米佐约总理访问中国的印度尼西亚记者，他们是：“社会使者报”的阿萨·巴法吉，安塔拉通讯社的苏纳约，“印度尼西亚火炬报”的苏莱曼，“东星报”的克鲁韦特和“印度尼西亚新闻”的达新·苏拉地。出席酒会的有北京新闻界人士和外国记者五十多人。印度尼西亚驻我国使馆新闻专员依斯纳尼和我国外交部情报司司长龚澎也应邀出席了酒会。（附图片）
    右：沙斯特罗阿米佐约总理和夫人五月二十七日参观了中央民族学院，观看所收藏的经文。　新华社记者　刘东鳌摄
    下：沙斯特罗阿米佐约总理五月二十七日参观清华大学，受到清华大学师生的热烈欢迎。　新华社记者　吴化学摄
