Title: Parliaments of the People's Republic of Bulgaria and the People's Republic of Albania Simultaneously Approve the Eight-Nation Treaty of Friendship, Cooperation and Mutual Assistance
Author:
Date: 1955-05-30
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Prague, May 29th -- Sofia dispatch: The National Assembly of the People's Republic of Bulgaria held a special meeting on May 28th to discuss the ratification of the Eight-Nation Treaty of Friendship, Cooperation and Mutual Assistance signed in Warsaw on May 14th.

　　Vulko Chervenkov, Chairman of the Council of Ministers of the People's Republic of Bulgaria and head of the government delegation of the People's Republic of Bulgaria to the Warsaw Conference, delivered a report on the Warsaw Conference of European countries. He emphasized that the Warsaw Treaty fully conforms to the vital interests of the Bulgarian people and is a reliable guarantee of Bulgaria's national independence and national sovereignty. He suggested that the National Assembly ratify the Warsaw Treaty.

　　S. Ganovski, Academician of the Bulgarian Academy of Sciences and member of parliament, reported to the meeting that the Foreign Affairs Committee of the National Assembly had carefully examined the treaty and decided to recommend that the National Assembly ratify it.

　　Subsequently, the National Assembly discussed Chervenkov's report. All speakers welcomed the signing of the Warsaw Treaty, considering it another contribution to strengthening world peace, and warmly supported the treaty.

　　The National Assembly unanimously approved the Treaty of Friendship, Cooperation and Mutual Assistance of the eight countries participating in the Warsaw Conference.

　　Xinhua News Agency, Prague, May 29th -- According to the Albanian News Agency, Tirana: The People's Assembly of the People's Republic of Albania held a meeting on the afternoon of the 28th to discuss the ratification of the Eight-Nation Treaty of Friendship, Cooperation and Mutual Assistance signed in Warsaw on May 14th. Enver Hoxha, Mehmet Shehu, Haxhi Lleshi and other leaders of the Albanian Labor Party and the state attended the meeting.

　　Mehmet Shehu, Chairman of the Council of Ministers and head of the government delegation of the People's Republic of Albania to the Warsaw Conference, delivered a report on the Warsaw Conference at the meeting. He said that the government delegation was convinced that the Warsaw Treaty embodies the will of the entire Albanian people. For the Albanian people, the Warsaw Treaty has a particularly important historical significance: For the first time in history, our people have participated in such a strong system of defense and international security, which guarantees the independence of our people, greatly strengthens our country's international position, and frustrates all the plans of the bellicose imperialists and their servants to deal with our country. This treaty is a very powerful tool for safeguarding international peace and security, and it will play a huge role in the favorable development of the international situation. It not only conforms to the vital interests of the peoples of the contracting parties, but also to the vital interests of the peoples of all countries in the world, because its supreme goal is to safeguard the peace cherished by all peoples. On behalf of the Council of Ministers, Shehu proposed that the People's Assembly ratify the Eight-Nation Treaty of Friendship, Cooperation and Mutual Assistance.

　　Subsequently, F. Paçrami, Chairman of the Foreign Affairs Committee of the People's Assembly, spoke. He said that all members of the Foreign Affairs Committee unanimously agreed with the Warsaw Treaty and recommended that the People's Assembly ratify the treaty. Enver Hoxha, First Secretary of the Central Committee of the Albanian Labor Party, and other members of parliament spoke in the discussion.

　　After discussion, the People's Assembly unanimously approved the Eight-Nation Treaty of Friendship, Cooperation and Mutual Assistance signed in Warsaw.

　　Xinhua News Agency, May 29th -- According to TASS, Warsaw, May 28th: Karel Vojáček, Ambassador of the Czechoslovak Republic to the People's Republic of Poland, deposited with the government of the People's Republic of Poland on the 27th the instruments of ratification of the Eight-Nation Treaty of Friendship, Cooperation and Mutual Assistance signed at the Warsaw Conference.

　　Polish Minister of Foreign Affairs Skrzeszewski and Vojáček signed the protocol on the deposit of the instruments of ratification.

### 保加利亚人民共和国和阿尔巴尼亚人民共和国的议会  同时批准八国友好合作互助条约

1955-05-30
第4版()
专栏：

    保加利亚人民共和国和阿尔巴尼亚人民共和国的议会
　  同时批准八国友好合作互助条约
    新华社布拉格二十九日电　索非亚消息：保加利亚人民共和国国民议会五月二十八日举行特别会议，讨论批准五月十四日在华沙签订的八国友好合作互助条约的问题。
    保加利亚部长会议主席、出席华沙会议的保加利亚人民共和国政府代表团团长伏·契尔文科夫作了关于欧洲国家华沙会议的报告。他强调说，华沙条约完全符合保加利亚人民的切身利益，并且是保加利亚的民族独立和国家主权的一个可靠保障。他建议国民议会批准华沙条约。
    保加利亚科学院院士、议员萨·加诺夫斯基向会议报告：国民议会外交委员会详细审议了这个条约，决定建议国民议会予以批准。
    接着，国民议会对契尔文科夫的报告进行了讨论。所有发言者都欢迎华沙条约的签订，认为这是对加强世界和平的又一个贡献，并且热烈支持这个条约。
    国民议会一致批准了参加华沙会议的八国的友好合作互助条约。
    新华社布拉格二十九日电　据阿尔巴尼亚通讯社地拉那讯：阿尔巴尼亚人民共和国人民议会二十八日下午举行会议，讨论批准五月十四日在华沙签订的八国友好合作互助条约的问题。阿尔巴尼亚劳动党和国家的领导人恩·霍查、穆·谢胡、哈·列希等都出席了会议。
    部长会议主席、出席华沙会议的阿尔巴尼亚人民共和国政府代表团团长谢胡在会上作了关于华沙会议的报告。他说，政府代表团深信：华沙条约体现了阿尔巴尼亚全体人民的意志。对于阿尔巴尼亚人民说来，华沙条约具有特别重要的历史意义：我国人民在历史上第一次参加了这样强有力的防御和国际安全的体系，这种体系保障我国人民的独立、大大加强我们国家的国际地位、使好战的帝国主义者和他们的仆从们的一切对付我国的计划不能得逞。这个条约是保障国际和平和安全的一个非常强有力的工具，它在国际局势的有利发展中将起巨大的作用。它不仅符合缔约国人民的切身利益，而且也符合全世界所有国家人民的切身利益，因为它的最高目的是保障所有人民都珍视的和平。谢胡代表部长会议建议人民议会批准八国友好合作互助条约。
    接着，人民议会外交委员会主席法·帕奇拉米发言。他说，外交委员会全体委员一致赞同华沙条约并建议人民议会批准这个条约。在讨论中发言的有阿尔巴尼亚劳动党中央委员会第一书记霍查和其他议员。
    人民议会经过讨论后一致批准了在华沙签订的八国友好合作互助条约。
    新华社二十九日讯　据塔斯社华沙二十八日讯：捷克斯洛伐克共和国驻波兰人民共和国大使卡·沃雅泽克，二十七日向波兰人民共和国政府交存了在华沙会议上签订的八国友好合作互助条约的批准书。
    波兰人民共和国外交部长斯克热歇夫斯基和沃雅泽克在交存批准书的议定书上签了字。
