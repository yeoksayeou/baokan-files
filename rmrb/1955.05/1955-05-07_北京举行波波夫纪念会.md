Title: Beijing Holds Popov Commemoration Meeting
Author:
Date: 1955-05-07
Page: 1
　　Beijing Holds Popov Commemoration Meeting
    Xinhua News Agency, 6th dispatch: The commemoration meeting for the 60th anniversary of the invention of radio by the great Russian scholar and genius radio inventor Alexander Stepanovich Popov was solemnly held in Beijing on the evening of the 6th. Those attending the commemoration meeting included: leaders and representatives from the Chinese Academy of Sciences, the Sino-Soviet Friendship Association, the Ministry of Posts and Telecommunications of the People's Republic of China, the Broadcasting Affairs Bureau, the Chinese People's Association for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries, the China Association for Science and Technology, the China Association for Scientific and Technological Popularization, and the Film Bureau, as well as professors and students from the Radio Department of Tsinghua University and more than 1,000 radio enthusiasts from all walks of life in the capital. Foreign guests included: Soviet Embassy Counselor Malitsev and Soviet radio experts assisting in our country's construction.
    Zhu Xuefan, Minister of the Ministry of Posts and Telecommunications of the People's Republic of China, Wu Youxun, Vice President of the Chinese Academy of Sciences, Wen Jize, Deputy Director of the Broadcasting Affairs Bureau, and Soviet expert Chernenko spoke at the meeting.
    After the meeting, the film "The Genius Inventor - Popov" was shown.

### 北京举行波波夫纪念会

1955-05-07
第1版()
专栏：

　　北京举行波波夫纪念会
    据新华社六日讯　俄罗斯伟大的学者、天才的无线电发明家亚·斯·波波夫发明无线电六十周年纪念会，六日晚在北京隆重举行。参加纪念大会的有：中国科学院、中苏友好协会总会、中华人民共和国邮电部、广播事业局、中国人民对外文化协会、中华全国自然科学专门学会联合会、中华全国科学技术普及协会、电影局的负责人和代表，清华大学无线电系的教授和学生以及首都各界的无线电爱好者共一千多人。外宾有：苏联大使馆参赞马立则和帮助我国建设事业的苏联无线电专家。
    中华人民共和国邮电部部长朱学范、中国科学院副院长吴有训、广播事业局副局长温济泽、苏联专家切尔年柯都在会上讲了话。
    会后演出了电影：“天才发明家——波波夫”。
