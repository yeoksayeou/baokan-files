Title: Four Foreign Ministers of the Soviet Union, the United States, Britain and France Meet and Issue Communiqué: Announce General Agreement on the Issue of a Four-Power Summit Conference
Author:
Date: 1955-05-16
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency, Vienna, May 15th: TASS, Vienna, May 15th: On the evening of the 14th, Soviet Foreign Minister Molotov, US Secretary of State Dulles, British Foreign Secretary Macmillan, and French Foreign Minister Pinay held talks. After the talks, the foreign ministers decided to issue the following communiqué:

　　The Foreign Ministers of the Soviet Union, the United States, Britain, and France, in their talks, reached a general agreement on the purposes and methods of a meeting of the heads of government of the Soviet Union, the United States, Britain, and France. The details of this matter are still under study.

### 苏美英法四外长会谈并发表公报　宣布就四国首脑会议问题达成一般协议

1955-05-16
第4版()
专栏：

　　苏美英法四外长会谈并发表公报
　　宣布就四国首脑会议问题达成一般协议
    新华社十五日讯  塔斯社维也纳十五日讯：十四日晚间，苏联外交部长莫洛托夫、美国国务卿杜勒斯、英国外交大臣麦克米伦、法国外交部长比内进行了会谈，在会谈之后，外长们决定发表下列公报：
    苏、美、英、法四国外交部长在会谈时对苏、美、英、法四国政府首脑会议的目的和方法问题达成了一般协议。关于这个问题的细节还在继续研究中。
