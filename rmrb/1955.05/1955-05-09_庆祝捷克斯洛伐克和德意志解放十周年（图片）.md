Title: Celebrating the Tenth Anniversary of the Liberation of Czechoslovakia and Germany (Pictures)
Author:
Date: 1955-05-09
Page: 5

　　Celebrating the Tenth Anniversary of the Liberation of Czechoslovakia and Germany
　　Beautiful Prague - the capital of the Czechoslovak Republic.
　　Czechoslovakia's large-scale Slapy hydroelectric power station.
　　The multi-bucket excavator manufactured by the Lenin Factory in Pilsen, Czechoslovakia, can replace the daily labor of thousands of people per hour.
　　Czechoslovakia's high degree of agricultural mechanization has improved agricultural productivity and reduced heavy physical labor.
　　The living standards of the working people of Czechoslovakia are improving day by day. This is the newly built workers' housing in Gottwaldov.
　　The working people of Czechoslovakia are spending their holidays in scenic resorts.
　　Stalin Avenue, a newly built street in Berlin, the capital of the German Democratic Republic.
　　Exterior view of the "Stalin" steel plant in Stalin City, German Democratic Republic.
　　The State-owned Sachsen Television Factory in Radeberg, German Democratic Republic, produces exquisite televisions. This is a technician conducting a final inspection of the finished product.
　　The Carl Zeiss optical instrument factory in the German Democratic Republic produces a large number of cameras.
　　The German Democratic Republic uses radio to serve agricultural production. This is a tractor team leader reporting to the tractor station by radiotelephone.
　　An apprentice club in the German Democratic Republic.

第5版()
专栏：

　　庆祝捷克斯洛伐克和德意志解放十周年
    美丽的布拉格——捷克斯洛伐克共和国的首都。
　　捷克斯洛伐克规模宏大的斯拉普水力发电站。

　　捷克斯洛伐克劳动人民的生活水平日益提高。这是哥特瓦尔德夫城新建的工人住宅。
　　捷克斯洛伐克劳动人民正在风景幽美的风景区度假日。
　　德意志民主共和国的首都柏林新建的斯大林大街。
　　德意志民主共和国斯大林城“斯大林”钢铁厂的外景。
　　德意志民主共和国拉德堡的国营沙克森电视机制造厂，生产精致的电视机。这是技师对成品进行最后检查。
　　德意志民主共和国蔡司光学仪器工厂生产着大量的照像机。
　　德意志民主共和国利用无线电为农业生产服务。这是拖拉机队长正在用无线电话向拖拉机站报告工作。
　　德意志民主共和国的一个学徒工俱乐部。
