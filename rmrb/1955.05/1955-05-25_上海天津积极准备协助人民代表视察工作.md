Title: Shanghai and Tianjin Actively Prepare to Assist People's Representatives in Inspection Work
Author:
Date: 1955-05-25
Page: 1
　
　　Xinhua News Agency, Shanghai, May 24th: The Shanghai Municipal People's Committee held an enlarged meeting on May 23rd to discuss how to actively assist deputies to the National People's Congress in their inspection work in Shanghai.
　　The meeting decided that the Shanghai Municipal People's Committee and relevant departments should, according to the inspection requests of the deputies to the National People's Congress, assist them in making specific arrangements; all organs, organizations, factories, schools, hospitals and other units in the city should truthfully reflect the situation and provide the representatives with all conveniences in their work when the People's Representatives arrive for inspection. The meeting also decided to convene a temporary meeting of the First Shanghai Municipal People's Congress to discuss the specific steps for the Shanghai Municipal People's Congress deputies to coordinate with the National People's Congress deputies in inspecting the work of Shanghai.
　　The deputies to the National People's Congress currently in Shanghai also participated in the meeting and jointly studied the inspection work.
　　Xinhua News Agency, Tianjin, May 22nd: According to the decision of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress, the Tianjin Municipal Office for Deputies to the National People's Congress was formally established on the 21st. According to the notice from the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress, the Tianjin deputies to the National People's Congress have decided to focus on inspecting the city's industry, grain, culture, education, health, and capital construction in the near future. The Representative Office has begun to contact relevant departments to prepare for the representatives' inspection work.

### 上海天津积极准备协助人民代表视察工作

1955-05-25
第1版()
专栏：

　　上海天津积极准备协助人民代表视察工作
    新华社上海二十四日电　上海市人民委员会在五月二十三日举行扩大会议，讨论如何积极协助全国人民代表大会代表来上海视察的问题。
    会议决定，上海市人民委员会和各有关部门应按照全国人民代表大会代表的视察要求，协助他们进行具体安排；全市各机关、团体、工厂、学校、医院等单位，在人民代表到达视察时，应实事求是地反映情况，给予代表们以工作上的一切便利。会议还决定召开上海市第一届人民代表大会临时会议，讨论上海市人民代表大会代表协同全国人民代表大会代表对上海市工作进行视察的具体步骤。
    现在在上海的全国人民代表大会代表也参加了这次会议，共同就视察工作问题进行了研究。
    新华社天津二十二日电　根据全国人民代表大会常务委员会的决定，天津市全国人民代表大会代表办事处在二十一日正式成立。天津市的全国人民代表大会代表根据全国人民代表大会常务委员会的通知，确定在最近有重点地视察本市的工业、粮食，文教卫生和基本建设等方面的情况。代表办事处已开始和有关部门联系，为代表们的视察工作作准备。
