Title: Soviet Union and People's Democratic Countries' Government Delegations Arrive in Berlin to Attend the Tenth Anniversary Celebration of the Liberation of Germany
Author:
Date: 1955-05-08
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Berlin, 7th (TASS dispatch from Berlin, 6th) The Soviet government delegation invited to attend the tenth anniversary celebration of the liberation of Germany arrived in Berlin on the 6th.

The delegation, headed by M.G. Pervukhin, Member of the Presidium of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of the Soviet Union and First Deputy Chairman of the Council of Ministers of the Soviet Union, included G.K. Zhukov, Minister of Defense of the Soviet Union and Marshal of the Soviet Union, N.M. Pegov, Secretary of the Presidium of the Supreme Soviet of the Soviet Union, and G.M. Pushkin, Soviet Plenipotentiary Ambassador to the German Democratic Republic, who was already in Berlin.

The Soviet government delegation was welcomed at Berlin's Schönefeld Airport by O. Grotewohl, Prime Minister of the German Democratic Republic, W. Ulbricht, First Secretary of the Central Committee of the Socialist Unity Party of Germany and Deputy Prime Minister of the German Democratic Republic, H. Matern, Acting Chairman of the People's Chamber of the German Democratic Republic, Dr. L. Bolz, Deputy Prime Minister and Foreign Minister of the German Democratic Republic, K. Schirdewan and F. Oelssner, Members of the Political Bureau of the Central Committee of the Socialist Unity Party of Germany, M. Reimann, First Secretary of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of Germany, as well as other government officials and prominent representatives of various parties and mass organizations of the German Democratic Republic.

Also present to welcome the delegation were Pushkin, Soviet Plenipotentiary Ambassador to the German Democratic Republic, senior officials of the Soviet Embassy and the Soviet High Commissioner's Office in Germany, representatives of the Soviet Forces in Germany, and personnel from the government delegations of the People's Democracies who had already arrived in Berlin.

Members of foreign diplomatic missions were also present at the airport to welcome the delegation.

Pervukhin, head of the Soviet government delegation, and Grotewohl, Prime Minister of the German Democratic Republic, delivered speeches at the airport one after the other.

In his speech, Pervukhin first extended his congratulations to the German people. He then said: We have come here to celebrate with you and the entire German people the tenth anniversary of Germany's liberation from Hitler's tyranny. The Soviet people, like all peace-loving people, are following with deep sympathy the struggle of the patriotic forces of Germany against the revival of German militarism, against the Paris Agreements, and for the unification of Germany on a peaceful and democratic basis. Allow me to express my belief that the German people will achieve complete victory in this struggle and will achieve the peaceful and democratic development of Germany.

In his welcoming speech, Grotewohl said: We believe that your presence here to participate in the May 8th celebrations will help further strengthen the close friendly relations between the Soviet and German peoples. We welcome Comrade Marshal Zhukov, who is now standing on the land where he commanded the victorious battles for the liberation of the German people ten years ago.

Xinhua News Agency, Berlin, 7th (TASS dispatch from Berlin, 6th) President Pieck of the German Democratic Republic received on the 6th the Soviet government delegation headed by Pervukhin, Member of the Presidium of the Central Committeee of the Communist Party of the Soviet Union and First Deputy Chairman of the Council of Ministers of the Soviet Union, who had come to attend the tenth anniversary celebration of the liberation of Germany.

Present at the meeting were Grotewohl, Prime Minister of the German Democratic Republic, and Ulbricht, Deputy Prime Minister of the German Democratic Republic and First Secretary of the Central Committee of the Socialist Unity Party of Germany.

Xinhua News Agency, Berlin, 7th (TASS dispatch from Berlin) The government delegation of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea headed by Hong Myung-hee, Deputy Premier of the Cabinet and President of the Academy of Sciences of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea, the government delegation of the People's Republic of Albania headed by Bilbil Klosi, Minister of Justice of the People's Republic of Albania, and the government delegation of the Mongolian People's Republic headed by B. Damdin, Member of the Political Bureau of the Central Committee of the Mongolian People's Revolutionary Party, arrived in Berlin on the 5th, having been invited to attend the tenth anniversary celebration of the liberation of Germany.

The government delegation of the Polish People's Republic headed by S. Wapot, Vice-Chairman of the Council of Ministers, the government delegation of the Czechoslovak Republic headed by Vice-Premier Václav Škoda, the government delegation of the Hungarian People's Republic headed by József Mekis, First Vice-Chairman of the Council of Ministers, the government delegation of the Romanian People's Republic headed by Constantin Pârvulescu, Chairman of the National Assembly, and the government delegation of the Bulgarian People's Republic headed by Ivan Mikhailov, Vice-Chairman of the Council of Ministers, arrived in Berlin on the 6th.

### 应邀参加德国解放十周年庆祝典礼  苏联和各人民民主国家政府代表团到柏林

1955-05-08
第4版()
专栏：

　　应邀参加德国解放十周年庆祝典礼
　  苏联和各人民民主国家政府代表团到柏林
    新华社七日讯　据塔斯社柏林六日讯：应邀参加德国解放十周年庆典的苏联政府代表团六日抵达柏林。
    以苏联共产党中央委员会主席团委员、苏联部长会议第一副主席米·格·别尔乌辛为首的代表团中包括：苏联国防部长、苏联元帅格·康·朱可夫和苏联最高苏维埃主席团秘书尼·米·别哥夫以及已经在柏林的苏联驻德意志民主共和国特命全权大使格·马·普希金。
    在柏林什涅菲尔德机场欢迎苏联政府代表团的有德意志民主共和国总理奥·格罗提渥，德国统一社会党中央委员会第一书记、德意志民主共和国副总理瓦·乌布利希，德意志民主共和国人民议院代理主席赫·马特恩，德意志民主共和国副总理兼外交部长洛·博尔茨博士，德国统一社会党中央委员会政治局委员卡·希德万和弗·厄斯纳，德国共产党中央委员会第一书记马·雷曼，以及德意志民主共和国政府其他官员和各党派及群众团体的著名代表们。
    欢迎代表团的还有苏联驻德意志民主共和国特命全权大使普希金，苏联大使馆和苏联驻德国高级专员公署高级官员，苏联驻德国部队司令部代表，以及已经到柏林的各人民民主国家政府代表团的人员。
    到机场欢迎的还有各国外交使团的人员。
    苏联政府代表团团长别尔乌辛和德意志民主共和国总理格罗提渥在机场先后致辞。
    别尔乌辛在他的讲话中首先向德国人民表示祝贺。他接着说：我们到这里来，是为同你们和全体德国人民一起庆祝德国从希特勒暴政下获得解放的十周年纪念的。苏联人民和所有爱好和平的人民一样，以深切的同情注视着德国的爱国力量为反对德国军国主义的复活、反对巴黎协定、争取在和平和民主的基础上统一德国而进行的斗争。请允许我表示，我相信，德国人民将在这个斗争中获得完全的胜利，将获得德国的和平和民主的发展。
    格罗提渥在他的欢迎词中说：我们相信，你们到这里来参加五月八日的庆祝将有助于苏德两国人民的密切的友好关系的进一步加强。我们欢迎朱可夫元帅同志，他现在正站在他在十年前指挥解放德国人民的胜利的战斗的土地上。
    新华社七日讯　据塔斯社柏林六日讯：德意志民主共和国总统皮克六日接见了前来参加德国解放十周年庆典的以苏联共产党中央委员会主席团委员、苏联部长会议第一副主席别尔乌辛为首的苏联政府代表团。
    接见时在座的有德意志民主共和国总理格罗提渥，德意志民主共和国副总理、德国统一社会党中央委员会第一书记乌布利希。
    新华社七日讯　据塔斯社柏林讯：应邀参加德国解放十周年庆典的以朝鲜民主主义人民共和国内阁副首相、科学院院长洪命熹为首的朝鲜民主主义人民共和国政府代表团，以阿尔巴尼亚人民共和国司法部长比尔比尔·克洛西为首的阿尔巴尼亚人民共和国政府代表团、以蒙古人民革命党中央委员会政治局委员布·达姆丁为首的蒙古人民共和国政府代表团，已在五日到达柏林。
    以部长会议副主席斯·瓦波特为首的波兰人民共和国政府代表团，以副总理瓦茨拉夫·什科达为首的捷克斯洛伐克共和国政府代表团，以部长会议第一副主席约瑟夫·麦基什为首的匈牙利人民共和国政府代表团，以国民议会主席康斯坦丁·帕伏列斯库为首的罗马尼亚人民共和国政府代表团和以部长会议副主席伊凡·米哈伊洛夫为首的保加利亚人民共和国政府代表团，已在六日抵达柏林。
