Title: A Mechanized Statistics Factory Begins Operation
Author:
Date: 1955-05-21
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency

　  The newly built mechanized statistics factory of the Beijing Railway Administration has begun using machines for statistical work. The statistical staff in the factory do not use abacuses or crank calculators, but rather operate a series of machines. These mechanical devices were all shipped from the Czechoslovak Republic. Using these machines can accurately and precisely compile original data from the massive, heavy, and complex railway transportation work. This is more efficient and of better quality than using abacuses and crank calculators, and it can also reduce the labor intensity of the staff.

### 一座机械化统计工厂开始工作

1955-05-21
第1版()
专栏：

　  一座机械化统计工厂开始工作
    北京铁路管理局新建的机械化统计工厂开始使用机器进行统计工作。工厂里的统计工作人员不是打算盘或是摇计算机，而是操纵着一系列的机械。这些机械设备都是从捷克斯洛伐克共和国运来的。使用这些机械可以正确地、精密地统计大宗、繁重、复杂的铁路运输工作中的原始资料，比起打算盘、摇计算机的效率高、质量好，而且能减轻工作人员的劳动强度。　（新华社）
