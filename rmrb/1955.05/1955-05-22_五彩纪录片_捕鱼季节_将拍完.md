Title: Five-Color Documentary "Fishing Season" Nears Completion
Author: 
Date: 1955-05-22
Page: 2

The Central Newsreel and Documentary Film Studio is filming the five-color documentary "Fishing Season" in the port of Yantai. The main content of this film is to introduce the fishing operations of the crew members of the state-owned aquatic products company in the beautiful and rich ocean of the motherland – the Yantai fishing grounds, as well as the situation after the fishermen embarked on the road of mutual assistance and cooperation, with gradual improvements in production and living conditions. Footage of ship repair and net mending, sea scenery, send-offs before voyages, catching yellow croaker, the lives of the crew members, and the rescue of fishing boats by the aquatic products company's fishing vessels during storms have already been captured. The filming work is expected to be completed before the end of May. (Our correspondent in Jinan)

### 五彩纪录片“捕鱼季节”将拍完

1955-05-22
第2版()
专栏：

　　五彩纪录片“捕鱼季节”将拍完
    中央新闻纪录电影制片厂正在烟台海港摄制五彩纪录片“捕鱼季节”。这个影片的主要内容，是介绍国营水产公司船员们在祖国美丽富饶的海洋——烟台渔场进行捕捞作业的情景，以及渔民们走上了互助合作的道路后，生产逐步提高，生活随着改善的情形。现在已经摄取了修船补网、海上风光、欢送出海、捕黄花鱼、船员们的生活和水产公司的渔轮在风暴中抢救渔船等素材，五月底以前即可完成拍摄工作。　（本报驻济南记者）
