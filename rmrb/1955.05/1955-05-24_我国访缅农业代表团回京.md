Title: Chinese Agricultural Delegation to Burma Returns to Beijing
Author:
Date: 1955-05-24
Page: 1

　　Xinhua News Agency, 23rd. The Chinese agricultural delegation to Burma, led by head of delegation Liu Ruilong, recently returned to Beijing after visiting Burma at the invitation of the Burmese government. This delegation went to Burma in February of this year.

### 我国访缅农业代表团回京

1955-05-24
第1版()
专栏：

　　我国访缅农业代表团回京
    新华社二十三日讯　应缅甸政府邀请访问缅甸的我国农业访问团，在团长刘瑞龙率领下最近返回北京。这个代表团是今年二月去缅甸的。
