Our Great Motherland
Children of China's ethnic minorities in the big family of the motherland are living a happy new life. Young Pioneers from the No. 1 Primary School directly under the Inner Mongolia Autonomous Region are spending their team day in the suburbs of Hohhot. They are dancing the "Prairie Herdsman Dance" on the grassland by the Dahei River.

### 我们伟大的祖国（图片）

1955-05-30
第1版()
专栏：

    我们伟大的祖国
    在祖国大家庭里的我国少数民族的儿童，过着幸福的新生活。内蒙古自治区直属第一小学的队员们在呼和浩特郊区过队日，他们在大黑河畔的草地上跳“草原牧民舞”。
