Title: British Right-Wing Union Boss Deakin Dies
Author:
Date: 1955-05-05
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, London, 4th: British right-wing union leader Deakin died in Leicester on the 1st. Deakin was 64 years old and was known for his anti-communism and undermining of the workers' movement.

### 英国右翼工会头子狄金病死

1955-05-05
第4版()
专栏：

　　英国右翼工会头子狄金病死
    新华社四日讯　伦敦消息：英国右翼工会领袖狄金一日在雷斯特病死。狄金今年六十四岁，以反共和破坏工人运动著称。
