Title: Sino-Indian Friendship Association Holds Third Anniversary Celebration

Author: 
Date: 1955-05-18
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, 16th. The Sino-Indian Friendship Association held a celebration in Beijing on the 16th to celebrate the third anniversary of the founding of the Sino-Indian Friendship Association.

The Indian Ambassador to China, Mr. R.K. Nehru, and members of the embassy were invited to attend the celebration.

Also invited to attend the celebration were Mr. Menon, the Chief Representative of India to the United Nations, who is currently visiting China, and Mr. Raghu Vira, a famous Indian linguist.

The following attended the celebration: Chen Yi, Vice Premier of the State Council of the People's Republic of China, and Chen Jiakang, Assistant Minister of Foreign Affairs.

Also attending the celebration were: Ding Xilin, President of the Sino-Indian Friendship Association, Chen Hansheng, Vice President, and members of the Council; Chu Tunan, President of the Chinese People's Association for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries, Zhao Yimin, Yang Hansheng, and Hong Shen, Vice Presidents; Zhou Gengsheng, Hu Yuzhi, and Qian Duansheng, Vice Presidents of the Chinese People's Institute of Foreign Affairs; and heads of other people's organizations, representatives of various Chinese delegations who had visited India, and people from the capital's scientific, educational, cultural, journalistic, and religious circles.

In the center of the rostrum hung portraits of Chairman Mao Zedong and President Prasad, flanked by the national flags of China and India. The venue also displayed photos of Indian Prime Minister Nehru's visit to China, as well as some gifts from the Indian government and people to the various Chinese delegations that visited India.

The celebration was presided over by Chen Hansheng.

Ding Xilin and R.K. Nehru spoke successively at the meeting, discussing the development of friendly relations between China and India in the past few years, and congratulating the further development of Sino-Indian friendly relations.

After the meeting, the Chinese cultural delegation, which recently returned from a visit to India, performed Chinese and Indian songs and dances.

### 中印友好协会举行成立三周年庆祝会

1955-05-18
第1版()
专栏：

　　中印友好协会举行成立三周年庆祝会
    新华社十六日讯　中印友好协会十六日晚在北京举行庆祝会，庆祝中印友好协会成立三周年。
    印度驻华大使赖嘉文和使馆人员应邀出席了庆祝会。
    应邀出席庆祝会的还有：正在我国访问的印度驻联合国首席代表梅农和印度著名语言学家拉古·维拉等。
    出席庆祝会的有：中华人民共和国国务院副总理陈毅，外交部部长助理陈家康。
    出席庆祝会的还有：中印友好协会会长丁西林、副会长陈翰笙和理事，中国人民对外文化协会会长楚图南、副会长赵毅敏、阳翰笙、洪深，中国人民外交学会副会长周鲠生、胡愈之、钱端升，以及其他各人民团体的负责人，曾先后赴印度访问的我国各个代表团代表，首都科学、教育、文艺、新闻、宗教界人士。
    主席台正中挂着毛泽东主席和普拉沙德总统的画像，两旁悬着中印两国国旗。会场里还陈设着印度总理尼赫鲁在中国访问的照片，以及印度政府和人民赠送给访问印度的我国各个代表团的部分礼品。
    庆祝会由陈翰笙主持。
    丁西林和赖嘉文相继在会上讲话，他们谈到了中印两国友好关系在过去几年来的发展，并祝贺中印友好关系获得进一步的发展。
    会后，最近访问印度归来的中国文化代表团表演了中、印歌舞。
