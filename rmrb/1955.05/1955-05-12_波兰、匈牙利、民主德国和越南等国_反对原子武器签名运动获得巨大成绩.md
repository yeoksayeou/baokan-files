Title: Poland, Hungary, East Germany, and Vietnam Achieve Great Success in Anti-Atomic Weapon Signature Campaign

Author:

Date: 1955-05-12
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Warsaw, 11th. Polish newspapers on the 10th published a communique from the Polish National Front All-Poland Committee regarding the signature campaign against the use of atomic weapons.

The communique stated that 19,993,000 people across Poland had signed the World Peace Council's "Appeal to the People of the World." During the campaign, the Polish working people held tens of thousands of mass rallies, expressing their firm will to uphold peace and oppose war.

Xinhua News Agency, Prague, 5th. According to a Hungarian News Agency report on the 3rd: Hungary's signature campaign against the preparation for atomic warfare has ended. A total of 7,146,480 people in various regions signed the World Peace Council's "Appeal to the People of the World."

Xinhua News Agency, Berlin, 7th. According to the preparatory committee of the German Peace Committee for the "Appeal to the People of the World" signature campaign's announcement on the 5th, 7 million people throughout the German Democratic Republic have signed the "Appeal to the People of the World" against the preparation for atomic warfare. During the signature campaign, thousands of signature collection teams were organized in urban and rural areas. In the Halle district alone, there were 571 teams in 560 towns and villages.

The signature campaign is still ongoing.

Xinhua News Agency, Hanoi, 11th. The signature campaign against atomic warfare continues to unfold in various regions of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam. As of May 7th, more than 3,380,000 people had signed the World Peace Council's "Appeal to the People of the World."

### 波兰、匈牙利、民主德国和越南等国　反对原子武器签名运动获得巨大成绩

1955-05-12
第4版()
专栏：

　　波兰、匈牙利、民主德国和越南等国
　　反对原子武器签名运动获得巨大成绩
    新华社华沙十一日电　波兰报纸十日刊载了波兰全国阵线全波委员会关于反对使用原子武器签名运动的公报。
    公报说，波兰全国各地已经有一千九百九十九万三千人在世界和平理事会“告全世界人民书”上签了名。在运动展开过程中，波兰劳动人民举行了上万次群众集会，表示了他们拥护和平和反对战争的坚定意志。
    新华社布拉格五日电　据匈牙利通讯社三日报道：匈牙利反对准备原子战争的签名运动已经结束，各地在世界和平理事会“告全世界人民书”上签名的共有七百一十四万六千四百八十人。
    新华社柏林七日电　据德国和平委员会关于
  “告全世界人民书”签名运动的筹备委员会五日宣布，德意志民主共和国全境已经有七百万人在反对准备原子战争的“告全世界人民书”上签了名。在签名运动中，各地城乡组织了成千的征集签名工作小组。仅哈雷专区五百六十个乡镇中，就有五百七十一个小组。
    签名运动还在继续进行。
    新华社河内十一日电　越南民主共和国各地继续展开反对原子战争的签名运动。截至五月七日为止，已有三百三十八万多人在世界和平理事会“告全世界人民书”上签了名。
