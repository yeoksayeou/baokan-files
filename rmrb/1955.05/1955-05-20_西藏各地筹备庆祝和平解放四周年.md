Title: Tibet Prepares to Celebrate the Fourth Anniversary of Peaceful Liberation
Author:
Date: 1955-05-20
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Lhasa, 18th. Preparations are underway across Tibet to celebrate the fourth anniversary of the peaceful liberation of Tibet. The Tibetan local government is preparing to hold a celebration symposium on the anniversary of the peaceful liberation of Tibet. Starting from the 17th, Lhasa wired radio station began broadcasting in Tibetan to the citizens the full text of the State Council's Seventh Plenary Session's decision on various issues in Tibet, the report on Tibetan work by Zhang Jingwu, representative of the People's Republic of China in Tibet, and the speeches of the Dalai Lama and Panchen Erdeni at the meeting. Several thousand copies of a supplement to the Tibetan "News Briefs" containing the above-mentioned documents have been published and are being prepared for distribution to people of all ethnic Tibetan communities before the 23rd. The Political Department of the Tibet Military Region of the Chinese People's Liberation Army is rushing to produce enlarged pictures of Tibetan cultural relics and historical sites, which will be exhibited during the festival. Preparations for celebrations are also underway in Shigatse, Gyantse, Yatung, Heihe, Ali, and other major towns in the Qamdo area.

### 西藏各地筹备庆祝和平解放四周年

1955-05-20
第1版()
专栏：

　　西藏各地筹备庆祝和平解放四周年
    新华社拉萨十八日电　西藏各地筹备庆祝西藏和平解放四周年。西藏地方政府准备在西藏和平解放四周年纪念日举行庆祝座谈会。拉萨有线广播电台从十七日起就用藏语向市民广播国务院第七次全体会议关于西藏各项问题的决定、中华人民共和国政府驻西藏代表张经武关于西藏工作的报告和达赖喇嘛、班禅额尔德尼在这次会议上的讲话等全文。藏文“新闻简讯”出版了几千份上述文件的增刊，准备在二十三日前发给各界藏族人民。中国人民解放军西藏军区政治部正在赶制有关西藏文物古迹的放大图片，准备在节日展出。日喀则、江孜、亚东、黑河、阿里等地以及昌都地区的各主要城镇，也正在进行筹备庆祝活动。
