Title: Soviet Locust Control Team Arrives in Xinjiang
Author:
Date: 1955-05-05
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, Urumqi, 4th. The deputy head of the Soviet Locust Control Team, Olenin, and the majority of its personnel, who were invited by our government to Xinjiang Province to assist in aerial locust control, arrived in Urumqi on April 21st and 29th, respectively. Eight locust control aircraft arrived on the 29th.

### 苏联治蝗团到达新疆

1955-05-05
第1版()
专栏：

　　苏联治蝗团到达新疆
　　新华社乌鲁木齐四日电　应我国政府聘请前来新疆省帮助进行飞机治蝗的苏联治蝗团副团长奥列宁和大部分人员，已分别在四月二十一日和二十九日到达乌鲁木齐。八架治蝗飞机已在二十九日到达。
