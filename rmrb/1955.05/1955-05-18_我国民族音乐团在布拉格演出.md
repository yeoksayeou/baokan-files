Title: China National Music Ensemble Performs in Prague
Author:
Date: 1955-05-18
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, Prague, May 17th: The China National Music Ensemble, participating in the "Prague Spring" International Music Festival, gave a concert in the Smetana Hall in Prague on the 15th.

    Those attending the concert included Czechoslovak Deputy Prime Minister Václav Kopecký, musicians from various countries participating in the "Prague Spring," and citizens of Prague.

    The Chinese folk music and songs performed by the China National Music Ensemble aroused great interest among the audience and were warmly welcomed.

    The music ensemble performed in Ostrava, a famous coal and steel city in Czechoslovakia, on May 13th.

### 我国民族音乐团在布拉格演出

1955-05-18
第1版()
专栏：

　　我国民族音乐团在布拉格演出
    据新华社布拉格十七日电　参加“布拉格之春”国际音乐节的中国民族音乐团，十五日在布拉格斯麦塔那大厅举行了演奏会。
    出席演奏会的有捷克斯洛伐克副总理瓦茨拉夫·柯别茨基，参加“布拉格之春”的各国音乐家以及布拉格市民。
      中国民族音乐团演唱的中国各民族乐曲和歌曲，引起了听众的巨大兴趣，受到了热烈的欢迎。
    音乐团五月十三日曾在捷克斯洛伐克著名的煤钢城市俄斯特拉发演出。
