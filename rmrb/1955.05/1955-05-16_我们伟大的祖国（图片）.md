Title: Our Great Motherland (Picture)
Author:
Date: 1955-05-16
Page: 1

    Our Great Motherland
    The herdsmen of the Zhalute Banner in the Zherim League of the Inner Mongolia Autonomous Region have basically settled down. The children of the members of the new pastoral agricultural production cooperatives have also entered the Nutuke (district) schools. This is the scene of the students' spring outing on holidays.

### 我们伟大的祖国（图片）

1955-05-16
第1版()
专栏：

    我们伟大的祖国
    内蒙古自治区哲里木盟扎鲁特旗的牧民已基本定居下来。新牧农牧业生产合作社社员们的孩子也都进了努图克（区）学校。这是学生们在假日春游的情形。
