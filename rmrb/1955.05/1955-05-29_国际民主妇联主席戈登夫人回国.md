Title: President of the International Democratic Women's Federation, Mrs. Gordon, Returns Home

Author: 
Date: 1955-05-29
Page: 4

　　Xinhua News Agency, 28th. At the invitation of the All-China Democratic Women's Federation, Mrs. Eugénie Cotton, President of the International Democratic Women's Federation and Vice-President of the World Peace Council, and French women Mrs. Balantyne, Mrs. Alexandre, and Mrs. Wildemeer left Beijing by plane on the 28th to return to their country.
　　Those who went to the airport to see them off included Cai Chang, President of the All-China Democratic Women's Federation, Vice Presidents Li Dequan and Zhang Yun, Kang Keqing, Secretary-General of the China National Committee for the Protection of Children, Liu Guanyi, Secretary-General of the China Peace Committee, Qian Duansheng, Vice President of the Chinese People's Institute of Foreign Affairs, and many other women from all walks of life in the capital. Mrs. Gordon and others visited Shanghai, Hangzhou, Guangzhou, Wuhan and other places. On the eve of her departure from Beijing, Cai Chang, President of the All-China Democratic Women's Federation, hosted a farewell banquet.

### 国际民主妇联主席戈登夫人回国

1955-05-29
第4版()
专栏：

　　国际民主妇联主席戈登夫人回国
    新华社二十八日讯　应中华全国民主妇女联合会邀请，前来我国访问的国际民主妇女联合会主席、世界和平理事会副主席欧仁妮·戈登夫人和法国妇女巴朗蒂尼夫人、亚历山大夫人、维尔德美夫人在二十八日乘飞机离京返国。
    前往机场欢送的有，中华全国民主妇女联合会主席蔡畅，副主席李德全、章蕴，中国人民保卫儿童全国委员会秘书长康克清，中国人民保卫世界和平委员会秘书长刘贯一，中国人民外交学会副会长钱端升以及首都各界妇女等多人。戈登夫人等曾赴上海、杭州、广州、武汉等地参观。在离京前夕，中华全国民主妇女联合会主席蔡畅曾设宴欢送。
