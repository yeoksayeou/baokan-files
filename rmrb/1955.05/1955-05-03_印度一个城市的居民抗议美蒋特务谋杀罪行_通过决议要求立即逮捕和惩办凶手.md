Title: Residents of an Indian City Protest the Crimes of Murder by American-Chiang Kai-shek Special Agents; Pass Resolution Demanding Immediate Arrest and Punishment of the Murderers
Author:
Date: 1955-05-03
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, New Delhi, May 2nd. The Telugu-language newspaper "Great Andhra" reported on April 24th: Residents of Narasipatnam town in Visakhapatnam district passed a resolution at a recent meeting condemning the crimes of American-Chiang Kai-shek special agents in murdering members of the Chinese delegation to the Asian-African Conference and Indian pilots. The resolution demanded the immediate arrest and punishment of the murderers.

### 印度一个城市的居民抗议美蒋特务谋杀罪行　通过决议要求立即逮捕和惩办凶手

1955-05-03
第4版()
专栏：

　　印度一个城市的居民抗议美蒋特务谋杀罪行
　　通过决议要求立即逮捕和惩办凶手
    新华社新德里二日电　泰鲁固文日报“大安得拉报”四月二十四日报道：维萨卡帕特南县的纳西帕特南城居民在最近的一次会议上通过了一项决议，谴责美蒋特务谋杀出席亚非会议的中国代表团人员和印度飞行员的罪行。决议要求立即逮捕和惩办凶手。
