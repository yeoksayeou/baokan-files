Title: Trade Union Representatives from the Soviet Union and Other Countries Leave Beijing for Home
Author:
Date: 1955-05-26
Page: 4
　
Xinhua News Agency, May 25th: The Soviet trade union delegation headed by Korobova, which was invited to China to participate in the "May Day" celebrations and visit, left Beijing by plane on the 25th to return home. Zhu Xuefan, Vice Chairman of the All-China Federation of Trade Unions, and Secretaries of the Secretariat, Li Zaiwen and Dong Xin, were among those who saw them off at the airport.

Prior to this, the Bulgarian trade union delegation, some members of the Czechoslovak trade union delegation, the Sudanese trade union representative, and the head of the French trade union delegation, Monmousseau, had successively left Beijing to return home.

### 苏联等国工会代表离京回国

1955-05-26
第4版()
专栏：

　  苏联等国工会代表离京回国
    新华社二十五日讯　应邀来我国参加“五一”节观礼和访问的以柯罗鲍娃为首的苏联工会代表团在二十五日乘飞机离北京返国。到机场欢送的，有中华全国总工会副主席朱学范，书记处书记栗再温、董昕等。
    在此以前，保加利亚工会代表团、捷克斯洛伐克工会代表团部分团员、苏丹工会代表和法国工会代表团团长蒙慕梭已陆续离北京返国。
