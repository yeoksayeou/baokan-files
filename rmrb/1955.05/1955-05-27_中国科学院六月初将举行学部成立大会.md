Title: Chinese Academy of Sciences to Hold Inaugural Meeting of Academic Divisions in Early June
Author:
Date: 1955-05-27
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, 26th: The Chinese Academy of Sciences will hold an inaugural meeting of its academic divisions in Beijing in early June, formally establishing the four academic divisions of the Chinese Academy of Sciences: the Division of Physics, Mathematics, and Chemistry; the Division of Biology and Earth Sciences; the Division of Technological Sciences; and the Division of Social Sciences.

Outstanding scientists from various disciplines of scientific research institutions, universities, and industrial sectors across the country will come to Beijing to participate in this conference.

The delegation of the Soviet Academy of Sciences, the delegation of the Polish Academy of Sciences, and scientists from other countries currently visiting China, as well as Soviet experts assisting China in its construction, will also be invited to participate.

The conference will hear and discuss the report on the work of the Chinese Academy of Sciences by Guo Moruo, President of the Chinese Academy of Sciences, and the reports on the work of each academic division by the directors of the four academic divisions. It will also discuss the Outline of the Five-Year Plan of the Chinese Academy of Sciences, discuss and adopt the Outline of the 1955 Work Plan for each academic division, and elect the standing committees of each academic division. Scientists will also present academic papers at the conference.

The establishment of the four academic divisions of the Chinese Academy of Sciences will play a significant role in strengthening the academic leadership of national scientific research work and strengthening the unity of the national scientific community.

### 中国科学院六月初将举行学部成立大会

1955-05-27
第1版()
专栏：

　　中国科学院六月初将举行学部成立大会
    新华社二十六日讯　中国科学院将在六月初在北京举行学部成立大会，正式成立中国科学院的四个学部——物理学数学化学部、生物学地学部、技术科学部和社会科学部。
    全国各地科学研究机关、高等学校和产业部门的各个学科的优秀科学家都将来京参加这次大会。
    正在我国访问的苏联科学院代表团、波兰科学院代表团和其他国家的科学家，以及帮助我国建设的苏联专家等也将应邀参加。
    大会将听取并讨论中国科学院院长郭沫若的关于中国科学院工作的报告以及四个学部主任的关于各学部的工作报告，讨论中国科学院的五年计划纲要，讨论并通过各学部一九五五年工作计划纲要，选举各学部的常务委员会。科学家还将在大会上宣读学术论文。
    中国科学院四个学部的成立，对加强全国科学研究工作的学术领导以及加强全国科学界的团结，将起重大的作用。
