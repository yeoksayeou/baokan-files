Title: Chinese Buddhist Delegation to Burma Returns to Beijing
Author:
Date: 1955-05-10
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, May 8th. The Chinese Buddhist Delegation to Burma, led by head of the delegation Xi Rao Jia Cuo, returned to Beijing by plane on the afternoon of the 8th after a month of friendly visits in Burma.

### 中国佛教访缅代表团回北京

1955-05-10
第1版()
专栏：

　　中国佛教访缅代表团回北京
    新华社八日讯　中国佛教访缅代表团在缅甸经过一个月的友好访问后，八日下午在团长喜饶嘉错率领下乘飞机回到北京。
