Title: Czechoslovakia's Achievements in the Peaceful Use of Atomic Energy
Author:
Date: 1955-05-24
Page: 4

Czechoslovakia continues to achieve successes in the peaceful use of atomic energy.

	In the construction industry, scientific workers from the Concrete Construction Department of the Brno Institute of Architecture have recently used artificial radioactive isotopes to inspect the quality of reinforced concrete projects. They place radioactive cobalt in a lead device and use it to photograph the part of the project to be inspected. They then use the photographs to understand the internal conditions of the reinforced concrete construction in detail and accurately, thereby determining the load-bearing capacity and strength of the project.

	In the medical field, Czechoslovak doctors have begun to use radioactive sodium and saline injected into human blood to detect blood circulation from the surface of the human body, which is of great help to doctors in diagnosing diseases. For example, it can help doctors more accurately determine whether a limb injury requires amputation.

	In addition, Czechoslovak scientists have also used radioactive isotopes in food preservation and plant cultivation research. After two months of experiments, they have been able to use radioactive cobalt to put potatoes in a "dormant state" to prevent them from sprouting, so that storing potatoes can keep their nutrition and taste unchanged. At the same time, they can also use radioactive phosphorus to determine the speed and amount of nutrients transported in plants.
                              (According to Xinhua News Agency)

### 捷克斯洛伐克和平使用原子能的成就

1955-05-24
第4版()
专栏：

    捷克斯洛伐克和平使用原子能的成就
    捷克斯洛伐克在和平使用原子能方面继续获得成就。
    在建筑业方面，布尔诺的建筑学院混凝土建筑系的科学工作者，最近已经利用人造放射性同位素检查加固钢筋混凝土工程的质量。他们把放射性钴放置在一种铅质装置中，利用它对所要检查的工程部分进行摄影，然后根据照片来详细和精确地了解加固钢筋混凝土建筑工程的内部情况，从而确定工程的负荷能力和强固性。
    在医疗方面，捷克斯洛伐克医生已经开始利用放射性钠和食盐水注入人体血液中的办法来从人体表面觉察血液的循环情况，这对医生诊断疾病有很大帮助。例如，它可以帮助医生更准确地确定人体四肢受伤部分是否需要截断。
    此外，在保存食物和研究植物培植方面，捷克斯洛伐克的科学工作者也利用了放射性同位素。经过两个月的试验，他们已经能把放射性钴用来使马铃薯处于“睡眠状态”，不发芽，这样储存马铃薯可使它的营养和滋味不变。同时他们还能够利用放射性磷确定营养物在植物中的输送速度和所需分量。
                              （据新华社讯）
