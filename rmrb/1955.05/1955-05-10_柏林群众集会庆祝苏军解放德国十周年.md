Title: Berlin Mass Rally Celebrates Tenth Anniversary of Soviet Army's Liberation of Germany
Author:
Date: 1955-05-10
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Berlin, May 9th: Two hundred thousand Berlin laborers held a mass rally in Berlin's Marx-Engels Square on the 8th to celebrate the tenth anniversary of the Soviet Army's liberation of Germany.

　　German and Soviet national flags and red flags fluttered around Marx-Engels Square and on the streets leading to the square. Huge banners read: "Defend the German Democratic Republic," "Defend Peace," and "Strive to Restore National Unity on a Democratic Basis."

　　The conference began at 11 a.m. On the rostrum were Wilhelm Pieck, President of the German Democratic Republic, the leaders of the German Democratic Republic government headed by Prime Minister Otto Grotewohl, Walter Ulbricht, First Secretary of the Socialist Unity Party of Germany, and members of the Central Politburo. The Soviet government delegation headed by Mikhail Pervukhin, the Chinese government delegation headed by Peng Dehuai, and government delegations from various People's Democratic countries were also on the rostrum. The broad masses warmly welcomed Soviet Marshal Georgy Zhukov, who led the Soviet army to liberate Berlin ten years ago.

　　Ulbricht spoke at the meeting. He emphasized that the German Democratic Republic has become a strong bulwark for defending peace in Europe and striving for German unification. He said that the German people warmly support the upcoming Warsaw Conference.

　　The heads of the Soviet, Polish, and Czechoslovak government delegations also spoke at the meeting.

### 柏林群众集会庆祝苏军解放德国十周年

1955-05-10
第4版()
专栏：

　　柏林群众集会庆祝苏军解放德国十周年
    新华社柏林九日电　二十万柏林劳动人民八日在柏林马克思—恩格斯广场举行群众大会，庆祝苏联军队解放德国十周年。
    在马克思—恩格斯广场四周和通向广场的各条大街上飘扬着德国和苏联的国旗和红旗。巨幅的标语牌上写着：“保卫德意志民主共和国”、“保卫和平”、“争取在民主的基础上恢复国家的统一”等口号。
    上午十一时大会开始。在主席台上的有德意志民主共和国总统皮克、以格罗提渥总理为首的德意志民主共和国政府负责人、德国统一社会党第一书记乌布利希和中央政治局委员等。以别尔乌辛为首的苏联政府代表团，以彭德怀为首的中华人民共和国政府代表团以及各人民民主国家的政府代表团也在主席台上。广大群众对于十年前率领苏军解放柏林的苏联元帅朱可夫的在场表示热烈欢迎。
    乌布利希在会上讲话。他着重指出：德意志民主共和国已经成为捍卫欧洲和平和争取德国统一的坚强堡垒。他表示，德国人民热烈拥护即将召开的华沙会议。
    在会上讲话的还有苏联、波兰、捷克斯洛伐克政府代表团的团长。
