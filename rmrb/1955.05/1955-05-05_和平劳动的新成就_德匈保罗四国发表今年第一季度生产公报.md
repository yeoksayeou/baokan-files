Title: New Achievements in Peaceful Labor – Production Communiqués Released by Germany, Hungary, Bulgaria, and Romania for the First Quarter of This Year
Author:
Date: 1955-05-05
Page: 4
　　New Achievements in Peaceful Labor
　　——Production Communiqués Released by Germany, Hungary, Bulgaria, and Romania for the First Quarter of This Year
　　Democratic Germany

　　The German Democratic Republic has released a communiqué on the execution of the national economic plan for the first quarter of 1955.
　　The communiqué stated that the overall industrial production plan was 99.3 percent completed in the first quarter of this year. Compared with the first quarter of last year, total industrial output increased by 3 percent. In the first quarter of this year, heavy industry completed 99.7 percent of its plan, machine manufacturing completed 97.8 percent, light industry completed 99.5 percent, and the food industry completed 100.4 percent of its plan. The communiqué pointed out that in the first quarter of this year, the productivity of state-owned industries increased by 7 percent compared to the first quarter of last year, and average wages increased by 5 percent.
　　The communiqué said that in agriculture, the number of agricultural machines has increased. For example, the number of potato planters has doubled compared to the same quarter last year, and the number of tractors has increased by 19 percent.
　　The communiqué stated that in the first quarter of this year, the volume of domestic trade increased by 14 percent compared to the first quarter of last year, and the volume of foreign trade increased by 8 percent.
　　Hungary
　　The Hungarian Central Statistical Office released a communiqué on the execution of the national economic plan for the first quarter of 1955.
　　The communiqué stated that industry completed 104.6 percent of the first quarter plan. Industrial output increased by 11.4 percent compared to the same period last year. In the first quarter, many engineering units were put into production, including the Soroksár iron smelter, the steel rolling workshop of the Lenin Metallurgical Combine, and the lime and dolomite calcination workshop of the Stalin Metallurgical Combine, among others.
　　In the first quarter of this year, labor productivity in industry increased by 8 percent compared to the same period last year, and the cost of products decreased.
　　In the first quarter, 15,000 people across the country joined agricultural production cooperatives. Rural areas received 1,100 tractors, 1,200 tractor-drawn plows, and many other new agricultural machines.
　　In the first quarter, the volume of commodity circulation increased by 6.2 percent.
　　Bulgaria
　　The Central Statistical Bureau of the Council of Ministers of the People's Republic of Bulgaria released a communiqué on the execution of the national economic development plan for the first quarter of 1955.
　　The communiqué stated that the total industrial output plan for the first quarter of this year was completed by 106 percent. Thanks to the creative labor of the broad masses of workers and staff, the cost of state-owned industrial products decreased by more than 3 percent, and the labor productivity plan of state-owned industries was completed by 104.1 percent.
　　The transportation plan for the first quarter was also completed. Among them, the railway freight volume plan was completed by 100.3 percent.
　　In agriculture, as of March 31, the spring sowing area had expanded by two times compared to the same period last year. In the first quarter, agriculture received 431 tractors (each calculated at 15 horsepower), 336 grain harvesting combines, and some other agricultural machines.
　　The retail trade plan for the first quarter of this year was completed by 104.6 percent.
　　Romania
　　The Central Statistical Bureau of the Council of Ministers of the Romanian People's Republic recently released a communiqué on the execution of the national economic plan for the first quarter of 1955.
　　The communiqué stated that the total output plan of socialist industry for the first quarter of this year was completed by 106.2 percent. During this period, many metallurgical, machine-manufacturing, electricity, electrical equipment, petroleum, chemical, timber, building materials industries, as well as light industry and food industry enterprises, completed their five-year plans nearly nine months ahead of schedule. More than sixty other industrial enterprises are currently working on their 1956 tasks. In the first quarter, several new production units and equipment were put into production.
　　In the first quarter of this year, agriculture received 22 percent more chemical fertilizers and 36 percent more seeds than in the same period last year. By the end of the first quarter, the number of collective farms and mutual aid teams in the country reached 5,250. Compared with the first quarter of last year, the number of various livestock in collective farms and state farms increased.
　　The sales of various foods and daily necessities by state-owned businesses in the first quarter all increased compared to the same period in 1954. (According to Xinhua News Agency)

### 和平劳动的新成就——德匈保罗四国发表今年第一季度生产公报

1955-05-05
第4版()
专栏：

    和平劳动的新成就
　　——德匈保罗四国发表今年第一季度生产公报
    民主德国
    德意志民主共和国公布了一九五五年第一季度国民经济计划执行情况的公报。
    公报说：工业生产总计划在今年第一季度完成了百分之九十九点三。与去年第一季度相比，工业总产量增加了百分之三。今年第一季度，重工业完成了计划的百分之九十九点七，机器制造业完成了百分之九十七点八，轻工业完成了九十九点五，食品工业完成了计划的百分之一百点四。公报指出：今年第一季度国营工业的生产率比去年第一季度增加了百分之七，平均工资增加了百分之五。
    公报说：在农业方面，农业机器的数目已经增加。例如，马铃薯种植机的数目比去年同一季度增加了两倍，拖拉机的数目增加了百分之十九。
    公报说：今年第一季度国内贸易额比去年第一季度增加了百分之十四，对外贸易额增加了百分之八。
    匈牙利
    匈牙利中央统计局发表了关于一九五五年第一季国民经济计划执行情况的公报。
    公报说，工业完成了第一季计划的百分之一百零四点六。工业产量比去年同期增加了百分之十一点四。在第一季内开始投入生产的有许多工程单位，其中有索罗克萨炼铁厂、列宁冶金联合工厂的轧钢车间、斯大林冶金联合企业的石灰和白云石焙烧车间等等。
    今年第一季，工业中的劳动生产率比去年同期提高了百分之八，产品的成本降低了。
    在第一季中，全国有一万五千人加入了农业生产合作社。农村得到了一千一百台拖拉机、一千二百部拖拉机牵引犁和许多其他新式农业机器。
    在第一季中，商品流转额增加了百分之六点二。
    保加利亚
    保加利亚人民共和国部长会议中央统计局发表了一九五五年第一季国民经济发展计划执行情况的公报。
    公报说，今年第一季的工业总产量计划完成了百分之一百零六。由于广大职工的创造性劳动，国营工业产品的成本降低了百分之三以上，国营工业的劳动生产率计划完成了百分之一百零四点一。
    第一季的运输计划也完成了。其中铁路货运量计划完成了百分之一百零点三。
    在农业方面，截至三月三十一日为止，春季的播种面积比去年同期扩大了两倍。第一季中农业获得了四百三十一部拖拉机（每部以十五匹马力计算）、三百三十六部谷物收割联合机和其他一些农业机器。
    今年第一季的零售贸易计划完成了百分之一百零四点六。
    罗马尼亚
    罗马尼亚人民共和国部长会议中央统计局最近发表了一九五五年第一季国民经济计划执行情况的公报。
    公报说，今年第一季社会主义工业的总产量计划完成了百分之一百零六点二。在这期间，有许多冶金、机器制造、电力、电气设备、石油、化学、木材、建筑材料工业以及轻工业和食品工业企业，差不多提前九个月完成了五年计划。有六十多个其他工业企业目前正在作一九五六年的工作。在第一季内有若干新的生产单位和设备投入生产。
    今年第一季，农业比去年同期多获得了百分之二十二的化学肥料和百分之三十六的种子。到第一季末，全国的集体农庄和共耕社的数目达到了五千二百五十个。和去年第一季相比，集体农庄和国营农场的各类牲畜增加了。
    第一季国营商业销售的各类食品和日用品都比一九五四年同期增加了。　（据新华社讯）
