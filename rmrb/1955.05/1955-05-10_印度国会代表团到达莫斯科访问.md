Title: Indian Parliamentary Delegation Arrives in Moscow for Visit
Author:
Date: 1955-05-10
Page: 4

　　Indian Parliamentary Delegation Arrives in Moscow for Visit
    Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, 8th. According to TASS, an Indian Parliamentary delegation led by the Deputy Speaker of the Council of States of the Indian Parliament, Krishnamoorthy Rao, arrived in Moscow on the 7th at the invitation of the Supreme Soviet of the USSR.
    Those who welcomed them at the airport included Tarasov, Vice-President of the Presidium of the Supreme Soviet of the USSR and Chairman of the Presidium of the Supreme Soviet of the Russian Federation; Lebedev, Vice-Chairman of the Soviet of the Union of the Supreme Soviet of the USSR; Shepilov, Chairman of the Foreign Affairs Committee of the Soviet of Nationalities; Savronov, Chairman of the Budget Committee of the Soviet of Nationalities; Gorkin, Deputy Secretary of the Presidium of the Supreme Soviet of the USSR; V. Kuznetsov, First Deputy Minister of Foreign Affairs of the USSR; and Bobrovnikov, Vice-Chairman of the Moscow City Soviet, among others.
    K. Menon, the Indian Ambassador to the Soviet Union, also welcomed them at the airport.
    Tarasov delivered a welcoming speech on behalf of the Supreme Soviet of the USSR and expressed his firm belief that the delegates could see the sincere sympathy of the Soviet people for all the peoples of India everywhere in the Soviet Union.
    In his reply, Krishnamoorthy Rao said that the presence of members of the Indian Parliament of the Republic in the Soviet Union, dedicated to consolidating the cause of peace, would promote the friendship between the Indian and Soviet peoples and would contribute to the cause of peace.

### 印度国会代表团到达莫斯科访问

1955-05-10
第4版()
专栏：

　　印度国会代表团到达莫斯科访问
    新华社八日讯　据塔斯社莫斯科讯：由印度国会联邦院副议长克里什纳穆蒂·拉奥率领的印度国会代表团，应苏联最高苏维埃的邀请，在七日到达莫斯科。
    到机场欢迎的有苏联最高苏维埃主席团副主席、俄罗斯联邦最高苏维埃主席团主席塔拉索夫，苏联最高苏维埃联盟院副主席列别杰夫、民族院外交委员会主席谢皮洛夫、民族院预算委员会主席萨夫罗诺夫，苏联最高苏维埃主席团副秘书戈尔金，苏联外交部第一副部长瓦·库兹涅佐夫、莫斯科市苏维埃副主席波布罗夫尼科夫等。
    印度驻苏大使克·梅农也到机场欢迎。
    塔拉索夫代表苏联最高苏维埃致欢迎词，并表示深信代表们在苏联任何地方都可以看到苏联人民真诚地同情印度各族人民。
    克里什纳穆蒂·拉奥在答词中说，印度共和国国会议员来到致力于巩固和平事业的苏联，将会促进印度和苏联人民的友谊，并将有助于和平事业。
