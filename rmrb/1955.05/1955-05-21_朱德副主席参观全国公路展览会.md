Title: Vice Chairman Zhu De Visits National Highway Exhibition
Author:
Date: 1955-05-21
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, May 20th. Vice Chairman Zhu De of the People's Republic of China visited the National Highway Exhibition on the afternoon of May 19th.
　　Vice Chairman Zhu De gained a detailed understanding of highway work, gave many important instructions, and expressed his appreciation for the achievements and experiences gained in highway transportation over the past few years.

### 朱德副主席参观全国公路展览会

1955-05-21
第1版()
专栏：

　　朱德副主席参观全国公路展览会
    新华社二十日讯　中华人民共和国朱德副主席五月十九日下午参观了全国公路展览会。
    朱德副主席对公路工作作了详细的了解，给予很多重要的指示，对公路交通几年来所取得的成就和经验表示赞许。
