Title: Telegram from the Central Committee of the Communist Party of China

Author:

Date: 1955-05-20
Page: 1

To Comrade Ho Chi Minh:

　　On the occasion of your sixty-fifth birthday, the Central Committee of the Communist Party of China extends warm, fraternal congratulations to you, the organizer and leader of the Vietnam Workers' Party, and the outstanding leader of the Vietnamese people.

　　We believe that under the leadership of the Central Committee of the Labor Party headed by you, the Vietnamese people will achieve new victories in realizing the independence, peace, unity, and democracy of their motherland. We wish you good health and longevity.

　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　The Central Committee of the Communist Party of China
　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　 May 19, 1955

### 中国共产党中央委员会电

1955-05-20
第1版()
专栏：

　　中国共产党中央委员会电胡志明同志：
    当您六十五岁寿辰之际，中国共产党中央委员会谨向您，越南劳动党的组织者与领导者，越南人民的杰出领袖致热烈的、兄弟般的祝贺。
    我们相信，越南人民将在以您为首的劳动党中央委员会领导之下，为实现祖国的独立、和平、统一与民主而获得新的胜利。祝您健康长寿。
                                  中国共产党中央委员会
　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　      一九五五年五月十九日
