Title: Yugoslav Embassy Chargé d'Affaires Milicevic Arrives in Beijing Yesterday
Author:
Date: 1955-05-21
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, May 20th -- The Chargé d'Affaires of the Embassy of the Federal People's Republic of Yugoslavia to the People's Republic of China, Milicevic, arrived in Beijing by car this afternoon. Cui Lie, Deputy Section Chief of the Protocol Department of our Ministry of Foreign Affairs, was among those who went to the station to greet him; in addition, there were diplomatic personnel from various countries' embassies in China.

### 南斯拉夫大使馆临时代办  米里切维奇昨日到达北京

1955-05-21
第1版()
专栏：

　　南斯拉夫大使馆临时代办
    米里切维奇昨日到达北京
    新华社二十日讯　南斯拉夫联邦人民共和国驻中华人民共和国大使馆临时代办米里切维奇于今日下午乘车抵京。前往车站迎接者有我外交部礼宾司副科长崔烈；此外，还有各国驻华使馆外交人员。
