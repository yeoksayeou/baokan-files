Title: Korean Education Delegation Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1955-05-14
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, May 13th. The Korean education delegation of fifteen members, invited to China according to the 1955 Sino-Korean Cultural Exchange Implementation Plan, arrived in Beijing on the 13th, led by Li Leyan, Deputy Minister of Education of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea and head of the delegation. Huang Songling, Deputy Minister of Higher Education, and Wei Que, Deputy Minister of Education, and others went to the station to welcome them.
    Kim Gui-nam, Charge d'Affaires ad interim of the Embassy of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea in China, and embassy staff also went to the station to welcome them.

### 朝鲜教育考察团到京

1955-05-14
第1版()
专栏：

　  朝鲜教育考察团到京
    新华社十三日讯　根据中朝一九五五年文化交流执行计划应邀来我国的朝鲜教育考察团一行十五人，在团长、朝鲜民主主义人民共和国教育省副相李乐彦率领下，十三日到达北京。前往车站欢迎者有高等教育部黄松龄副部长，教育部韦悫副部长等人。
    到车站欢迎者还有朝鲜民主主义人民共和国驻华大使馆临时代办金贵南以及使馆人员。
