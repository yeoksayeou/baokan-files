Title: Former Indian Ambassador to China, Pannikar, said at a Taiwan issue discussion in New Delhi that the British "Two Chinas" argument is untenable.
Author:
Date: 1955-05-04
Page: 4
　　Xinhua News Agency, New Delhi, 3rd May: The Indian Council of World Affairs held a discussion on the Taiwan issue in New Delhi on the evening of May 1st. The discussion was presided over by Pannikar, former Indian Ambassador to China. Participants included A. Appadorai, Secretary-General of the Indian Council of World Affairs, who recently returned from Bandung, Indian Members of Parliament, university professors, prominent social figures, and university students.
　　Dr. Rao, a professor at Delhi University, said at the meeting that the Five Principles of Peaceful Coexistence put forward by Premier Zhou Enlai and Prime Minister Nehru have undoubtedly proved China's peaceful intentions.
　　In his concluding remarks, Pannikar said that the Chinese people are completely unanimous in the view that Taiwan belongs to China. He said: Even the Americans cannot oppose this view.
　　Pannikar said that therefore, the British "Two Chinas" argument is unreasonable and untenable. He said that although Chiang Kai-shek claims to represent China, the undeniable fact is that everyone recognizes that the Chinese government on the mainland is the real Chinese government. He said: "There are simply not two Chinas."
　　Pannikar pointed out that if the United States had not intervened, China would have liberated Taiwan long ago.

### 前印度驻中国大使潘尼迦在新德里的台湾问题讨论会上说　英国方面“两个中国”的说法是站不住脚的

1955-05-04
第4版()
专栏：

　　前印度驻中国大使潘尼迦在新德里的台湾问题讨论会上说
　　英国方面“两个中国”的说法是站不住脚的
    新华社新德里三日电　印度世界事务委员会五月一日晚在新德里举行了一次台湾问题讨论会。讨论会由前印度驻中国大使潘尼迦主持。参加讨论会的有最近从万隆回国的印度世界事务委员会秘书长阿巴多拉伊和印度国会议员、大学教授、社会名流和大学生等。
    德里大学教授拉奥博士在会上发言说，周恩来总理和尼赫鲁总理所提出的和平共处五项原则已毫无疑问地证明了中国的和平意图。
    潘尼迦在作总结发言时说，中国人民完全一致地认为台湾是属于中国的。他说：甚至美国人也无法反对这个意见。
    潘尼迦说，因此，英国方面的所谓“两个中国”的说法是不合理的和站不住脚的。他说，虽然蒋介石声称代表中国，但是，无可否认的事实是，人人都承认在大陆上的中国政府才是真正的中国政府。他说：“根本就没有两个中国。”
    潘尼迦指出，要是美国没有进行干涉，中国早就解放台湾了。
