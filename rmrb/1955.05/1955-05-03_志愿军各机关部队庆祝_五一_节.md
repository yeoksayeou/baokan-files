Title: Volunteer Army Organs and Troops Celebrate May Day

Author:

Date: 1955-05-03
Page: 2

The organs and troops of the Chinese People's Volunteer Army warmly celebrated International Labor Day on May 1st.

	On the morning of the holiday, commanders and fighters of the Volunteer Army's leading organs and various units gathered around radios or loudspeakers to listen to the live broadcast of the grand meeting celebrating International Labor Day in the motherland's capital. The leading organs of the Volunteer Army's logistics units and troops stationed on the West Coast held separate celebrations, where responsible cadres delivered reports on the motherland's construction achievements and the international situation.

	On this festive day, many Volunteer Army units also sent representatives to congratulate Korean workers. Many units' commanders and fighters held celebrations together with local Korean workers, farmers, and Korean People's Army soldiers, and some also held friendly ball game competitions. Many units also dispatched film projection teams to Korean factories and rural areas to screen movies for the Korean working people.

	Chinese aid workers who are helping the Korean people with construction in Pyongyang participated in the Pyongyang people from all walks of life celebrating International Labor Day on May 1st rally and parade. (Xinhua News Agency)

### 志愿军各机关部队庆祝“五一”节

1955-05-03
第2版()
专栏：

　　志愿军各机关部队庆祝“五一”节
    中国人民志愿军各机关、部队热烈庆祝“五一”国际劳动节。
    节日的上午，志愿军领导机关和各部队的指挥员、战斗员都围在收音机或扩音器旁，收听了祖国首都庆祝“五一”国际劳动节大会的实况播音。志愿军后勤部队和驻西海岸部队的领导机关分别举行了庆祝会，由负责干部在会上作了关于祖国建设成就和国际形势的报告。
    在节日这一天，志愿军许多部队还派出代表向朝鲜工人们祝贺。许多部队的指战员和当地朝鲜工人、农民及人民军战士一起举行了庆祝会，有的还举行了球类友谊比赛。许多部队还派出电影放映队到朝鲜的工厂和农村，为朝鲜劳动人民放映电影。
    正在平壤市帮助朝鲜人民进行建设的中国援朝工人参加了平壤市各界人民庆祝“五一”国际劳动节大会和游行。（新华社）
