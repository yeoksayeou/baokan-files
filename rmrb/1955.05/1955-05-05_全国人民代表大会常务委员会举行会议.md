Title: Standing Committee of the National People's Congress Holds Meeting
Author:
Date: 1955-05-05
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, 4th. The Standing Committee of the National People's Congress held its fourteenth meeting on May 4th.

　　The meeting heard a report by Jia Tuofu, Director of the Fourth Office of the State Council, on the situation and problems in national light industrial production.

### 全国人民代表大会常务委员会举行会议

1955-05-05
第1版()
专栏：

　　全国人民代表大会常务委员会举行会议
　　新华社四日讯　全国人民代表大会常务委员会在五月四日举行第十四次会议。
　　会议听了国务院第四办公室主任贾拓夫关于全国轻工业生产方面情况和问题的报告。
