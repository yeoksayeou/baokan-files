Title: Beijing Celebrates the Tenth Anniversary of the Liberation of Czechoslovakia with a Grand Meeting Yesterday
Author:
Date: 1955-05-08
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, 7th dispatch: People from all walks of life in the capital held a grand meeting last night to celebrate the tenth anniversary of the liberation of Czechoslovakia. The celebration was hosted by the Ministry of Culture of the People's Republic of China.

Zhu De, Vice Chairman of the People's Republic of China, attended the celebration.

Also attending the celebration were: Deng Zihui and Li Xiannian, Vice Premiers of the State Council; Xi Zhongxun, Secretary-General of the State Council; Wang Shoudao, Director of the Sixth Office of the State Council; Shen Yanbing, Minister of Culture; Ji Pengfei, Vice Minister of Foreign Affairs; as well as ministers and vice ministers of various ministries and vice chairmen of various commissions.

Also attending the celebration were: Li Jishen, Peng Zhen, and Chen Shutong, Vice Chairmen of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress; Feng Jiping, Deputy Mayor of Beijing; as well as heads of various people's organizations and representatives from the capital's scientific, cultural, artistic, and journalistic circles, totaling over 600 people.

The Ambassador of the Czechoslovak Republic to China, Gregor, and embassy officials were invited to attend the meeting.

Also attending the celebration were diplomatic envoys and officials from various countries stationed in China.

The celebration was presided over by Shen Yanbing, Minister of Culture.

As the meeting began, the orchestras played the national anthems of Czechoslovakia and China.

Wang Shoudao, Director of the Sixth Office of the State Council of the People's Republic of China, delivered a speech first at the meeting (see page two of today's newspaper), warmly congratulating the tenth anniversary of the liberation of Czechoslovakia.

The Ambassador of Czechoslovakia to China, Gregor, then delivered a speech (see pages two and three of today's newspaper). He described in detail the great achievements made by the Czechoslovak people in restoring and developing the national economy and improving their material and cultural living standards, and spoke of the increasingly developing and consolidating friendship between the people of Czechoslovakia and China.

During the speeches by Wang Shoudao and Gregor, warm applause repeatedly resounded throughout the hall.

At the celebration, the Czechoslovak State Song and Dance Ensemble performed wonderful song and dance programs.

### 庆祝捷克斯洛伐克解放十周年　北京各界昨日举行庆祝大会

1955-05-08
第1版()
专栏：

　　庆祝捷克斯洛伐克解放十周年
　　北京各界昨日举行庆祝大会
    据新华社七日讯　首都各界七日晚举行庆祝大会，隆重庆祝捷克斯洛伐克解放十周年。庆祝大会由中华人民共和国文化部举办。
    中华人民共和国副主席朱德出席了庆祝大会。
    出席庆祝大会的还有：国务院副总理邓子恢、李先念，秘书长习仲勋，国务院第六办公室主任王首道，文化部长沈雁冰，外交部副部长姬鹏飞，以及各部部长、副部长，各委员会副主任。
    出席庆祝大会的还有：全国人民代表大会常务委员会副委员长李济深、彭真、陈叔通，北京市副市长冯基平，以及各人民团体负责人，首都科学、文化、艺术、新闻界人士等六百多人。
    捷克斯洛伐克共和国驻我国大使格里哥尔和使馆官员应邀出席了大会。
    出席庆祝大会的还有各国驻华使节和外交官员。
    庆祝大会由文化部长沈雁冰主持。
    大会开始，乐队奏捷、中两国国歌。
    中华人民共和国国务院第六办公室主任王首道在会上首先讲话（见今天本报第二版），热烈地祝贺捷克斯洛伐克解放十周年。
    捷克斯洛伐克驻我国大使格里哥尔接着讲话
  （见今天本报第二、三版）。他详尽地叙述了捷克斯洛伐克人民在恢复和发展国民经济及提高物质和文化生活水平各方面取得的巨大成就，谈到了捷中两国人民日益发展和巩固的友谊。
    王首道和格里哥尔讲话时，全场一再响起了热烈的掌声。
    在庆祝大会上，捷克斯洛伐克国家歌舞团表演了精采的歌舞节目。
