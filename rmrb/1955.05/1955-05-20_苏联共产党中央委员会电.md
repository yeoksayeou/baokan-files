Title: Telegram from the Central Committee of the Communist Party of the Soviet Union
Author:
Date: 1955-05-20
Page: 1

To Comrade Ho Chi Minh:
    On the occasion of your sixty-fifth birthday, the Central Committee of the Communist Party of the Soviet Union extends to you—the organizer and leader of the Vietnam Workers' Party, and the tireless fighter for the Vietnamese people's liberation from the shackles of imperialism—warm, fraternal greetings and wishes for good health.
    We wish you further success in the struggle to restore and develop the national economy, to improve the living standards of the working people, and to achieve a unified, independent, and democratic Vietnam.
                                    The Central Committee of the Communist Party of the Soviet Union

### 苏联共产党中央委员会电

1955-05-20
第1版()
专栏：

　　苏联共产党中央委员会电亲爱的胡志明同志：
    在您六十五岁寿辰之际，苏联共产党中央委员会谨向您——越南劳动党的组织者和领导者、为争取越南人民摆脱帝国主义枷锁而奋斗的不倦战士——致以热烈的、兄弟的敬意，并祝您健康。
    我们祝您在争取恢复和发展国民经济，在争取提高劳动人民生活水平，在争取统一的、独立的和民主的越南的斗争中取得进一步的成就。
                                    苏联共产党中央委员会
