Title: President Pieck Holds Reception to Celebrate Tenth Anniversary of German Liberation
Author:
Date: 1955-05-11
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency, Berlin, 10th - According to a report by the German News Agency: The President of the German Democratic Republic, Wilhelm Pieck, held a reception on the evening of the 8th to celebrate the tenth anniversary of the liberation of Germany.
    Present at the reception were the Soviet government delegation, the government delegation of the People's Republic of China, government delegations from various People's Democratic countries, and delegations from communist and workers' parties from capitalist countries.
    Also present at the reception were the Prime Minister of the German Democratic Republic, Otto Grotewohl, and Deputy Prime Minister Walter Ulbricht, members of the Central Committee of the Socialist Unity Party of Germany, representatives of the People's Chamber and the Chamber of States, representatives of various democratic parties and mass organizations, as well as officers of the People's Police, labor heroes, and prominent figures from all walks of life.
    At the reception, President Pieck and others frequently raised their glasses to toast the lasting friendship between Germany and the Soviet Union, and to the prosperity of the great Chinese people and the people of other friendly countries.

### 皮克总统举行招待会庆祝德国解放十周年

1955-05-11
第4版()
专栏：

　　皮克总统举行招待会庆祝德国解放十周年
    新华社柏林十日电　据德意志通讯社报道：德意志民主共和国总统皮克八日晚举行招待会，庆祝德国解放十周年。
    出席招待会的有苏联政府代表团、中华人民共和国政府代表团、各人民民主国家的政府代表团以及来自资本主义国家的共产党和工人党的代表团。
    出席招待会的还有德意志民主共和国总理格罗提渥和副总理乌布利希等，德国统一社会党中央委员会的委员，人民议院和参议院的代表，各民主党派和群众团体的代表，以及人民警察的官员、劳动英雄和各界著名人士。
    招待会上，皮克总统等频频举杯祝贺德苏两国之间的持久友谊，祝贺伟大的中国人民和其他友好国家的人民的繁荣。
