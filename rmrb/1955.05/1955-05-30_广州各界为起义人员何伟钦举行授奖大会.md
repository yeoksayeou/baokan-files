Title: Guangzhou Circles Hold Award Ceremony for Insurgent He Weiqin
Author:
Date: 1955-05-30
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Guangzhou, May 29th: The Chinese People's Liberation Army troops stationed in Guangzhou and people from all walks of life in Guangzhou, more than 1,200 people, held an award ceremony for He Weiqin, an air force officer who revolted from the Chiang Kai-shek army, at the Lingnan Cultural Relics Palace on the morning of the 29th.

A leader of a certain unit of the Chinese People's Liberation Army announced at the award ceremony: In accordance with the policy and reward measures for officers and men of the Chiang Kai-shek army who revolt and surrender, promulgated by Peng Dehuai, Minister of National Defense of the People's Republic of China, on New Year's Day this year, Lieutenant He Weiqin, a former Chiang Kai-shek army air force second-class intelligence officer who piloted an aircraft in revolt, is awarded a bonus of 5,000 yuan. Then, amidst applause, he presented the bonus to He Weiqin.

Chen Hao, a representative of a certain air force unit of the Chinese People's Liberation Army, and Guo Qiaoran, Deputy Mayor of Guangzhou, expressed warm welcome in their speeches to He Weiqin's patriotic act of piloting an aircraft in revolt and flying back to the mainland of the motherland. Deputy Mayor Guo Qiaoran said: He Weiqin's patriotic and righteous action once again pointed out a bright path for the officers and men of the Chiang Kai-shek army who are still in Taiwan and are suffering under the rule and enslavement of the Chiang bandits, we also welcome all officers and men of the Chiang Kai-shek army to abandon the darkness and come back to the mainland of the motherland.

After accepting the bonus, He Weiqin said: "I am extremely grateful to the Ministry of National Defense of the People's Republic of China for the reward given to my revolt. I am very grateful to the people of the motherland for their leniency and care for me, and I must work hard for the people in the future and contribute all my strength to build the motherland."

### 广州各界为起义人员何伟钦举行授奖大会

1955-05-30
第1版()
专栏：

　　广州各界为起义人员何伟钦举行授奖大会
    新华社广州二十九日电　中国人民解放军驻广州市部队和广州市各界人民一千二百多人，二十九日上午在岭南文物宫为蒋军起义空军人员何伟钦举行授奖大会。
    中国人民解放军某部首长在授奖大会上宣布：遵照中华人民共和国国防部长彭德怀今年元旦颁布的对蒋军起义、投诚人员的政策及奖励办法，授予驾机起义的前蒋军空军中尉二级情报官何伟钦以五千元奖金。接着，他在掌声中向何伟钦授予奖金。
    中国人民解放军某空军部队代表陈浩、广州市副市长郭翘然在致词中对何伟钦驾机起义飞回祖国大陆的爱国行动表示热烈欢迎。郭翘然副市长说：何伟钦这一爱国的正义行动，再一次给目前还留在台湾受着蒋贼统治奴役的蒋军官兵指出了一条弃暗投明的光明大道，我们同样欢迎一切蒋军官兵弃暗投明回到祖国大陆来。
    何伟钦接受奖金后说：“我对中华人民共和国国防部对我的起义行动给予的奖励表示万分感激。我非常感谢祖国人民对我的宽大与关怀，今后一定要好好为人民做事，把所有的力量贡献出来建设祖国。”
