Title: Egyptian Undersecretary of Commerce and Industry Talks About Sino-Egyptian Economic Cooperation
Author:
Date: 1955-05-06
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency, Cairo, May 5th: According to a TASS report from Cairo on the 4th, the Egyptian Undersecretary of Commerce and Industry, Nuseir, who participated in the Asian-African Conference, emphasized in an interview with the "Republic Newspaper" the great significance of the meeting between the Egyptian delegation and the personnel of the People's Republic of China in Bandung on the issue of strengthening economic cooperation between the two countries. Nuseir pointed out that the establishment of broad trade relations between Egypt and the People's Republic of China is beneficial to Egypt. He said that China can purchase large quantities of Egyptian cotton, and at the same time, Egypt needs goods that China has.

### 埃及工商部副部长谈中埃经济合作问题

1955-05-06
第4版()
专栏：

　　埃及工商部副部长谈中埃经济合作问题
    新华社五日讯　据塔斯社开罗四日讯：参加亚非会议的埃及工商部副部长努赛尔在向“共和国报”记者发表谈话时，着重指出了埃及代表团和中华人民共和国代表团人员在万隆就加强两国经济合作问题举行会谈的重大意义。努赛尔指出，埃及和中华人民共和国之间建立广泛的贸易关系是对埃及有利的。他说，中国可以购买埃及的大量棉花，同时在中国也有埃及所需要的商品。
