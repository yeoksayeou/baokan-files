    Our Great Motherland
    In order to meet the needs of production, the Yumen Oil Mine will continue to expand its various facilities this year. This Soviet-made automatic pumping unit has been transported to the Yumen Oil Mine. This pumping unit can extract oil from underground rock formations thousands of meters deep.

### 我们伟大的祖国（图片）

1955-05-06
第1版()
专栏：

    我们伟大的祖国
    玉门油矿为了适应生产的需要，今年继续扩建各项设备。这台苏联制造的自动抽油机已运到了玉门油矿，用这种抽油机可从几千公尺深的地下岩层中抽出石油来。
