Title: Guo Moruo Hosts Madame Eugénie Cotton, President of the Women's International Democratic Federation
Author:
Date: 1955-05-25
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, May 24th: Guo Moruo, Chairman of the China Peace Committee, hosted a banquet on the evening of the 24th to welcome Madame Eugénie Cotton, President of the Women's International Democratic Federation and Vice-President of the World Peace Council, Madame Wildebrand from France, Madame Alexandre, and Madame Barantini, who are visiting China.

Li Dequan and Zhang Yun, Vice-Chairwomen of the All-China Democratic Women's Federation, Qian Sanqiang, a member of the World Peace Council, and Liu Guanyi, Secretary-General of the China Peace Committee, were also present.

The banquet took place in a friendly atmosphere.

### 郭沫若招待国际民主妇联主席戈登夫人

1955-05-25
第1版()
专栏：

　　郭沫若招待国际民主妇联主席戈登夫人
    新华社二十四日讯　中国人民保卫世界和平委员会主席郭沫若在二十四日晚设宴招待前来我国访问的国际民主妇女联合会主席、世界和平理事会副主席欧仁妮·戈登夫人、法国妇女维尔德美夫人、亚历山大夫人和巴朗蒂尼夫人。
    出席作陪的有中华全国民主妇女联合会副主席李德全、章蕴，世界和平理事会理事钱三强，中国人民保卫世界和平委员会秘书长刘贯一等。
    宴会在友好的空气中进行。
