Title: Fifth All-Soviet Peace Congress Concludes; Delegation to World Peace Congress Elected; Mandate Approved
Author:
Date: 1955-05-15
Page: 4
　
Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, 13th dispatch from TASS: The Fifth All-Soviet Peace Congress concluded in Moscow on the 12th after three days. The meeting discussed the report "The Current International Situation and the Struggle of the Peoples for Peace" made by Korneichuk, a member of the Presidium of the Soviet Peace Defense Committee.

During the discussion of Korneichuk's report, all speakers enthusiastically supported the peaceful diplomatic policy of the Soviet government, the Soviet government's proposals on reducing armaments, prohibiting atomic weapons, and eliminating the threat of a new war, and unanimously expressed the determination of the Soviet people to consolidate the power of the Soviet state and to strengthen the struggle to defend peace and friendship among peoples of all countries.

During the meeting, a delegation from the World Peace Council and participants in the World Federation of Trade Unions Executive Committee meeting being held in Moscow were invited to attend the meeting. They also spoke at the meeting.

Finally, the meeting elected the Soviet Peace Defense Committee, elected a delegation of thirty-seven members to attend the Helsinki World Peace Congress, and unanimously adopted a mandate for the delegation.

Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, 14th dispatch from TASS: The Soviet Peace Defense Committee, elected by the Fifth All-Soviet Peace Congress, held its first plenary session on the 13th.

The meeting discussed organizational issues. The writer N. Tikhonov was elected Chairman of the Soviet Peace Defense Committee, the writer I. Ehrenburg and the Director of the Tuberculosis Research Institute Z. Lebedeva were elected Vice-Chairmen, and the journalist M. Kotov was elected Executive Secretary.

The plenary session also elected other members of the Presidium: train driver Blazhenov, Professor Grushchenko, writer Korneichuk, President of the Soviet Academy of Sciences Nesmeyanov, Chairman of the Soviet Chamber of Commerce Nesterov, Chairman of the Soviet Women's Anti-Fascist Committee Popova, Chairman of the Soviet Youth Anti-Fascist Committee Romanovsky, and writers Surkov and Fadeev.

### 第五届全苏保卫和平大会闭幕　选出出席世界和平大会的代表团并通过委托书

1955-05-15
第4版()
专栏：

　　第五届全苏保卫和平大会闭幕
　　选出出席世界和平大会的代表团并通过委托书
    新华社十三日讯　据塔斯社莫斯科讯：第五届全苏保卫和平大会十二日在莫斯科闭幕。会议历时三天。会议讨论了苏联保卫和平委员会主席团委员考涅楚克在会上所作的“当前的国际局势和各国人民保卫和平的斗争”的报告。
    在讨论考涅楚克的报告时，所有发言的人都热烈支持苏联政府的和平外交政策，支持苏联政府关于裁减军备、禁止原子武器和消除新战争威胁的建议，并且一致表示了苏联人民在巩固苏维埃国家的威力，在加强保卫和平和各国人民之间的友谊的斗争方面的决心。
    会议进行期间，世界和平理事会代表团、正在莫斯科举行的世界工会联合会执行局会议的参加者应邀参加了会议。他们并在会上讲了话。
    会议最后选举了苏联保卫和平委员会，选出了由三十七人组成的出席赫尔辛基世界和平大会的代表团，并且一致通过了给代表团的委托书。
    新华社十四日讯　据塔斯社莫斯科讯：由第五届全苏保卫和平大会选出的苏联保卫和平委员会，在十三日举行了第一次全体会议。
    会议讨论了组织问题。作家尼·吉洪诺夫当选为苏联保卫和平委员会主席，作家伊·爱伦堡和肺病研究所所长齐·列别杰娃当选为副主席，新闻工作者米·科托夫当选为执行书记。
    全体会议还选出了主席团的其他委员：火车司机布拉仁诺夫、教授格鲁希钦科、作家考涅楚克、苏联科学院院长涅斯米扬诺夫、苏联商会主席涅斯捷罗夫、苏联妇女反法西斯委员会主席波波娃、苏联青年反法西斯委员会主席罗马诺夫斯基、作家苏尔科夫和法捷耶夫。
