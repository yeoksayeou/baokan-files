Title: Polish Technical and Scientific Delegation Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1955-05-15
Page: 1
　  Xinhua News Agency, 14th. The Polish delegation of twelve, led by the head of the delegation, Deputy Chairman of the State Economic Planning Commission of the Polish People's Republic, M. Lesz, arrived in Beijing by plane on the 14th to participate in the second session of the Sino-Polish Joint Committee on Technical and Technical Scientific Cooperation, which is about to open in Beijing.
    Wang Shoudao, Chairman of the Chinese Group of the Committee and Director of the Sixth Office of the State Council of the People's Republic of China, and Polish Ambassador to China, Kirylok, were at the airport to welcome them.

### 波兰技术科学代表团到京

1955-05-15
第1版()
专栏：

　  波兰技术科学代表团到京
    新华社十四日讯　前来参加即将在北京开幕的中波技术与技术科学合作联合委员会第二届会议的波兰代表团一行十二人，在团长、波兰人民共和国国家经济计划委员会副主席米·莱什率领下，在十四日乘飞机到达北京。
    前往机场欢迎的有委员会中国组主席、中华人民共和国国务院第六办公室主任王首道和波兰驻我国大使基里洛克等。
