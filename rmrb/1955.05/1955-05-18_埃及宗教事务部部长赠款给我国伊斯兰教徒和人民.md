Title: Egyptian Minister of Religious Affairs Donates to Chinese Muslims and People
Author:
Date: 1955-05-18
Page: 1

　　Xinhua News Agency, 17th. Ahmed Hassan Al-Baquri, the Egyptian Minister of Religious Affairs, who is currently visiting China, presented a donation of 1,000 English pounds on behalf of the Egyptian Ministry of Religious Affairs to poor Muslim believers in China when he met with Premier Zhou Enlai of the State Council of China on the afternoon of the 16th; another donation of 1,000 English pounds was given to other poor people. Premier Zhou expressed his gratitude and promised to hand over the above-mentioned donations to the China Islamic Association and the China People's Relief Administration for handling respectively.

### 埃及宗教事务部部长赠款给我国伊斯兰教徒和人民

1955-05-18
第1版()
专栏：

　　埃及宗教事务部部长赠款给我国伊斯兰教徒和人民
    新华社十七日讯　现在正在我国访问的埃及政府宗教事务部部长艾哈迈德·哈萨尼·巴库尔在十六日下午拜会我国务院总理周恩来时，曾以埃及政府宗教事务部的名义把一千英镑捐款赠给中国伊斯兰教贫穷的信徒；另以一千英镑捐款赠给其他的贫穷人民。周总理表示感谢，并允将上述捐款分别交给中国伊斯兰教协会及中国人民救济总会负责处理。
