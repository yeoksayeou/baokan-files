Title: Beijing Holds Meeting to Celebrate the Tenth Anniversary of Germany's Liberation
Author:
Date: 1955-05-07
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, 6th Dispatch:  The capital held a celebration meeting on the evening of the 6th to grandly celebrate the tenth anniversary of Germany's liberation. The celebration meeting was organized by the Ministry of Culture of the People's Republic of China.

Liu Shaoqi, Chairman of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress of the People's Republic of China, attended the celebration meeting.

Also attending the celebration meeting were: Vice Premiers of the State Council Chen Yun and Deng Xiaoping, Vice Minister of Foreign Affairs Zhang Wentian, Director of the Second Office of the State Council Lin Feng, Minister of Culture Shen Yanbing, as well as deputy ministers of various ministries and deputy directors of various committees.

Also attending the celebration meeting were: Vice Chairmen of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress Lin Boqu, Guo Moruo, Huang Yanpei, Peng Zhen, Vice Mayor of Beijing Wu Han, as well as heads of various people's organizations, and more than 600 people from the capital's scientific, cultural, artistic, and journalistic circles.

König, Ambassador of the German Democratic Republic to China, and embassy officials were invited to attend the meeting.

Also attending the celebration meeting were heads of diplomatic missions and diplomatic officials from various countries stationed in China.

The celebration meeting was presided over by Minister of Culture Shen Yanbing.

At the beginning of the meeting, the band played the national anthems of Germany and China.

Lin Feng, Director of the Second Office of the State Council of the People's Republic of China, spoke first at the meeting (see page two of today's newspaper), congratulating the German people and the government of the German Democratic Republic on the tenth anniversary of Germany's liberation.

Ambassador König then spoke (see pages two and three of today's newspaper). He spoke about the significant achievements of the working people of the German Democratic Republic in restoring and developing various sectors of the national economy and culture since liberation, and about the current tasks of the German people's struggle.

The speeches by Lin Feng and König repeatedly drew enthusiastic applause from the audience.

At the celebration meeting, German musicians and the Central Song and Dance Troupe of China performed wonderful music and dance programs.

### 北京举行德国解放十周年庆祝会

1955-05-07
第1版()
专栏：

　　北京举行德国解放十周年庆祝会
    据新华社六日讯　首都各界六日晚举行庆祝大会，隆重庆祝德国解放十周年。庆祝大会由中华人民共和国文化部举办。
    中华人民共和国全国人民代表大会常务委员会委员长刘少奇出席了庆祝大会。
    出席庆祝大会的还有：国务院副总理陈云、邓小平，外交部副部长张闻天，国务院第二办公室主任林枫，文化部长沈雁冰，以及各部副部长，各委员会副主任。
    出席庆祝大会的还有：全国人民代表大会常务委员会副委员长林伯渠、郭沫若、黄炎培、彭真，北京市副市长吴晗，以及各人民团体负责人，首都科学、文化、艺术、新闻界人士等六百多人。
    德意志民主共和国驻我国大使柯尼希和使馆官员应邀出席了大会。
    出席庆祝大会的还有各国驻华使节和外交官员。
    庆祝大会由文化部长沈雁冰主持。
    大会开始，乐队奏德、中两国国歌。
    中华人民共和国国务院第二办公室主任林枫在会上首先讲话（见今天本报第二版），向德国人民和德意志民主共和国政府祝贺德国解放十周年。
    柯尼希大使接着讲话（见今天本报第二、三版）。他谈到了解放以来，德意志民主共和国劳动人民在恢复和发展国民经济各部门和文化方面的重大成就，谈到了目前德国人民的斗争任务。
    林枫和柯尼希的讲话一再博得全场热烈的掌声。
    在庆祝大会上，最后由德国音乐家和我中央歌舞团表演了精采的音乐歌舞节目。
