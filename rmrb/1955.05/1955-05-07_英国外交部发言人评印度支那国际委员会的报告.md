Title: British Foreign Office Spokesman Comments on the Report of the International Commission for Indochina
Author:
Date: 1955-05-07
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, London, May 5th: According to a report from the British Central News Agency, the British Parliament received on the 3rd a white paper containing reports from the three international commissions overseeing and monitoring the implementation of the Geneva Accords in Vietnam, Laos, and Cambodia.

    The British Foreign Office spokesman said regarding this matter: "We are satisfied with these reports, which indicate that the International Commissions have completed valuable and important work under conditions of great political and material difficulty."

### 英国外交部发言人评印度支那国际委员会的报告

1955-05-07
第4版()
专栏：

　　英国外交部发言人评印度支那国际委员会的报告
    新华社五日讯　据塔斯社伦敦四日讯：英国中央新闻社消息，英国议会已在三日收到监督和监察越南、老挝和柬埔寨执行日内瓦协定情况的三个国际委员会的报告的白皮书。
    英国外交部发言人就这件事说道：“我们对这些报告感到满意，它们表示了国际委员会在巨大的政治和物质困难的条件下完成了有价值的和重要的工作。”
