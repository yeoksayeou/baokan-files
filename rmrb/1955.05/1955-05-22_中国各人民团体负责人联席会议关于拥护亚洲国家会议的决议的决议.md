Title: Resolution of the Joint Meeting of Heads of Chinese People's Organizations on Supporting the Asian Countries Conference
Author:
Date: 1955-05-22
Page: 1

　　The Joint Meeting of Heads of Chinese People's Organizations listened with satisfaction to the report by Guo Moruo, head of the Chinese delegation to the Asian Countries Conference, on the proceedings and achievements of the conference.

　　The Joint Meeting of Heads of Chinese People's Organizations warmly celebrated the success of the Asian Countries Conference. The meeting unanimously agreed that the resolutions of the Asian Countries Conference are a major call to defend peace in Asia at the present time. These resolutions will greatly promote and encourage the struggle of the people of Asian countries to win and safeguard peace, independence, and freedom, and to oppose colonialism, eliminate the threat of new wars, and implement the Five Principles of Peaceful Coexistence. They will play a significant role in easing tensions in the Far East and strengthening friendly cooperation among the people of Asian countries. These resolutions reflect the common aspirations and demands of the people of Asian countries, and also demonstrate their firm will to defend peace and oppose aggression.

　　We believe that through the continuous efforts and cooperation of the people of our Asian countries in the future, the common cause of the people of our Asian countries to jointly defend peace and strengthen friendly cooperation will surely achieve greater success and development. The Joint Meeting of Heads of Chinese People's Organizations fully endorses and supports all the resolutions adopted by the Asian Countries Conference, and is willing to continue to unite with the people of Asian countries, overcome difficulties and obstacles, and work together to realize the resolutions of the Asian Countries Conference, to consolidate and ensure peace in Asia and the world, to achieve peaceful coexistence among countries, and to promote international cooperation.

### 中国各人民团体负责人联席会议关于拥护亚洲国家会议的决议的决议

1955-05-22
第1版()
专栏：

　　中国各人民团体负责人联席会议关于拥护亚洲国家会议的决议的决议
    中国各人民团体负责人联席会议满意地听取了出席亚洲国家会议中国代表团团长郭沫若关于会议的经过和成就的传达报告。
    中国各人民团体负责人联席会议热烈庆祝亚洲国家会议的成功。会议一致认为，亚洲国家会议的决议是当前保卫亚洲和平的重大号召。这些决议将大大地推动和鼓舞亚洲各国人民争取和维护和平、独立、自由和反对殖民主义、消除新战争威胁推行和平共处五项原则的斗争，对缓和远东紧张局势和加强亚洲各国人民之间的友好合作将会发生重大作用。这些决议反映了亚洲各国人民的共同愿望和要求，也表现了他们保卫和平和反对侵略的坚决意志。
    我们相信，今后通过我们亚洲各国人民不断的努力和合作，我们亚洲各国人民共同保卫和平和加强友好合作的共同事业一定会得到更大的成就和发展。中国各人民团体负责人联席会议完全拥护和支持亚洲国家会议所通过的全部决议，并愿继续同亚洲各国人民团结一起，克服困难和障碍，为实现亚洲国家会议的决议，为巩固和确保亚洲及世界和平，为实现各国和平共处和促进国际合作而共同努力。
