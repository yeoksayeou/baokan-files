Title: Guo Moruo Hosts a Banquet for Japanese Visiting Delegations and Japanese Peace Activists
Author:
Date: 1955-05-11
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, May 10th: Guo Moruo, Chairman of the China Peace Committee, hosted a banquet on the 10th for the Japanese delegation led by Kin-ichi Yoshioka and Yoshio Kobayashi, who visited China after attending the Asian Countries Conference, the Japanese Agricultural Delegation led by Toyoaki Ogawa and Matsuo Tagaya, and Japanese peace activists Tatsunosuke Miyazaki and Seya Miyazaki.

　　Present at the banquet were Liao Chengzhi, Vice Chairman of the China Peace Committee; Xu Baoju, Standing Committee Member; Liu Guanyi, Secretary-General; and Yan Guhang, Deputy Director of the All-China Federation of Supply and Marketing Cooperatives, among others.

　　Also present at the banquet were Togo Kameda, Deputy Secretary-General of the Asian and Pacific Regions Peace Liaison Committee; Rewi Alley, Committee Member; and Japanese peace activist Wan'emon Nakamura, who is in Beijing.

　　During the banquet, the hosts and guests toasted the growing friendship between the people of the two countries and the development of the Asian peace cause.

### 郭沫若欢宴日本访华团体和日本和平人士

1955-05-11
第1版()
专栏：

　　郭沫若欢宴日本访华团体和日本和平人士
    新华社十日讯　中国人民保卫世界和平委员会主席郭沫若在十日欢宴来我国访问的以吉冈金市、小林义雄为首的出席亚洲国家会议后来我国访问的日本访华代表团，以小川丰明、多贺谷松雄为首的日本农业代表团和日本和平人士宫崎龙介、宫崎世民等。
    出席宴会的有中国人民保卫世界和平委员会副主席廖承志，常务委员许宝驹，秘书长刘贯一和中华全国供销合作总社副主任阎顾行等多人。
    出席宴会的还有亚洲及太平洋区域和平联络委员会副秘书长龟田东伍、委员艾黎和在京的日本和平人士中村玩右卫门等。
    席间宾主双方互为两国人民日益增长的友谊和亚洲和平事业的发展而干杯。
