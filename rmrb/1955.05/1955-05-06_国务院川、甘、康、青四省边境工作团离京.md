Title: State Council's Work Group for the Border Regions of Sichuan, Gansu, Xikang, and Qinghai Provinces Leaves Beijing
Author:
Date: 1955-05-06
Page: 1

　　Xinhua News Agency, May 5th: The State Council's Work Group for the Border Regions of Sichuan, Gansu, Xikang, and Qinghai Provinces, a group of fifty-three people led by Deputy Heads Zhang Chengwu and Zhang Guoquan, departed from Beijing on May 5th for Aba Tibetan Autonomous Prefecture in Sichuan Province.

　　The State Council's Work Group for the Border Regions of Sichuan, Gansu, Xikang, and Qinghai Provinces will cooperate with local Party and government organs in Tibetan-populated areas such as the Tibetan Autonomous Prefecture of Sichuan Province, the Gannan Tibetan Autonomous Prefecture of Gansu Province, the Tibetan Autonomous Prefecture of Xikang Province, and the Guoluo Tibetan Autonomous Prefecture of Qinghai Province to publicize ethnic policies, strengthen ethnic work in the border areas of the four provinces, and further promote unity among and within the various ethnic groups in the border areas of the four provinces.

　　The work group also includes a medical team and a film projection team.

### 国务院川、甘、康、青四省边境工作团离京

1955-05-06
第1版()
专栏：

　　国务院川、甘、康、青四省边境工作团离京
    新华社五日讯　国务院川、甘、康、青四省边境工作团一行五十三人，由副团长张承武、张国权率领，本月五日从北京出发，前往四川省藏族自治区阿坝。
    国务院川、甘、康、青四省边境工作团将在藏族人民聚居的四川省藏族自治区、甘肃省甘南藏族自治区、西康省藏族自治州、青海省果洛藏族自治区等地区，配合当地党、政机关宣传民族政策，加强四省边境地区的民族工作，和进一步促进四省边境各民族间和民族内部的团结。
    工作团还设有医疗队和电影放映队。
