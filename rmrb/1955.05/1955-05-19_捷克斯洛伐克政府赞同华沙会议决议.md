Title: Czechoslovak Government Endorses Warsaw Conference Resolutions
Author:
Date: 1955-05-19
Page: 4

　　Xinhua News Agency, Prague, 18th. The Czechoslovak News Agency reports that the Government of the Czechoslovak Republic held a meeting on the 17th to discuss the report by the Prime Minister of the Republic, Široký, on the Warsaw Conference, and unanimously endorsed the Treaty of Friendship, Cooperation and Mutual Assistance signed in Warsaw and the resolution on the establishment of a Joint Command of the Armed Forces.
    The Government of the Republic also decided to submit the treaty to the National Assembly for ratification.

### 捷克斯洛伐克政府赞同华沙会议决议

1955-05-19
第4版()
专栏：

　　捷克斯洛伐克政府赞同华沙会议决议
    新华社布拉格十八日电　据捷克斯洛伐克通讯社报道，捷克斯洛伐克共和国政府十七日举行会议，讨论共和国总理西罗基关于华沙会议的报告，并且一致赞同在华沙签订的友好合作互助条约和关于成立武装部队联合司令部的决议。
    共和国政府并决定把这个条约提交国民议会批准。
