Title: People from All Walks of Life in Lüda City See Off Soviet Army Headquarters Personnel Returning Home
Author:
Date: 1955-05-27
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, Lüda, May 26th: Yesterday afternoon, more than 20,000 representatives from all walks of life in Lüda City warmly saw off the personnel of the Soviet Army Headquarters stationed in the Lushunkou area as they withdrew to return to their country.

　　The farewell rally was held in the square in front of Dalian Railway Station.

　　Colorful flags were planted all around the venue. Huge portraits of Chairman Mao Zedong and Chairman Bulganin were hung on the rostrum.

　　When the senior generals and officers of the Soviet Army Headquarters appeared on the rostrum, warm applause and cheers rang out in the venue.

　　The farewell rally began with the playing of the national anthems of China and the Soviet Union. Song Li, Second Secretary of the CPC Lüda Municipal Committee and Mayor of Lüda City, delivered a farewell speech at the meeting. On behalf of the CPC Lüda Municipal Committee, the Lüda Municipal People's Committee, and the people of the city, he expressed his heartfelt gratitude and high respect to all comrades of the Soviet Army Headquarters headed by General Shvetsov, and to the great Soviet people and the heroic Soviet army. He said: The people of Lüda City will always cherish you and will transform the feelings of cherishing the Soviet army into a huge force to work hard to build our motherland and vigilantly defend peace. Finally, he wished all comrades of the Soviet Army Headquarters a safe journey home and good health; and wished the heroic Soviet army greater achievements in the struggle to defend peace and defend the just cause of mankind.

　　Next to speak was General Deng Hua, Acting Chairman of the Chinese Military Delegation to the Sino-Soviet Joint Military Commission. He said: As you are about to completely withdraw from the Lushunkou area today, we bid farewell to you with infinite gratitude and reluctance. On behalf of the Chinese Military Delegation to the Sino-Soviet Joint Military Commission and all the commanders and fighters of the Army, Navy, and Air Force of the Chinese People's Liberation Army stationed in the Lüda area, I would like to express my high respect and heartfelt gratitude to all the Soviet generals, officers, and soldiers headed by General Shvetsov. The development and consolidation of the sincere friendship between the people of China and the Soviet Union and the fighting friendship between the two armies is a force that no enemy can defeat. Let us join hands and advance victoriously in the common cause of defending the Far East and world peace.

　　General Shvetsov of the Soviet Army Headquarters delivered a farewell speech. He said: We are leaving your beautiful country with gratitude. The brilliant image of the Soviet people's faithful friend—the great Chinese people—will always remain in our hearts. He said: Please allow us to solemnly assure you and the entire Chinese people that the Soviet people and their powerful armed forces will always be the faithful and long-tested friends of the great Chinese people and their heroic army. Please take our heartfelt gratitude as the most sincere and friendly expression of the Soviet people and their army to the 600 million Chinese people, to the heroic Chinese army, to the glorious Chinese Communist Party and the Government of the People's Republic of China, and to the wise and long-tested leader Chairman Mao Zedong. Finally, he wished the Chinese people new and greater achievements in socialist construction, in vigilantly defending the fruits of victory, and in the struggle for peace and security in the Far East and the world. After the speech, dozens of Young Pioneers and soldiers of the Army, Navy, and Air Force of the Chinese People's Liberation Army stepped onto the rostrum to present flowers to the Soviet generals and officers.

　　The farewell rally ended with the music of "Moscow-Beijing". At this time, the Soviet generals and officers waved goodbye to the crowd. The venue resounded with stormy cheers of "Thank you, Soviet Army!" and "Long live the great friendship between China and the Soviet Union!"

　　General Shvetsov and others reviewed the honor guard on the station platform. Before boarding the train, General Shvetsov and others once again shook hands and said goodbye to the people who came to see them off.

### 旅大市各界人民欢送苏军指挥部人员回国

1955-05-27
第1版()
专栏：

　　旅大市各界人民欢送苏军指挥部人员回国
    新华社旅大二十六日电　二十六日下午，旅大市各界人民代表两万多人热烈欢送驻旅顺口地区苏军指挥部人员撤退回国。
    欢送大会会场设在大连车站前的广场上。
    会场四周插满了彩旗。主席台上悬挂着中苏两国国旗和毛泽东主席、布尔加宁主席的巨幅画像。
    当苏军指挥部的高级将领和军官们出现在主席台上时，会场上响起热烈的掌声和欢呼声。
    欢送大会开始，奏中苏两国国歌。中共旅大市委员会第二书记、旅大市市长宋黎在会上致欢送词。他代表中共旅大市委员会、旅大市人民委员会和全市人民向以什维佐夫将军为首的苏军指挥部全体同志，向伟大的苏联人民和英勇的苏军致衷心的感激和崇高的敬意。他说：旅大市人民将永远怀念着你们，将把怀念苏军的心情化为巨大的力量，来辛勤地建设我们的祖国，警惕地保卫和平。他最后祝苏军指挥部全体同志回国途中一路平安，身体健康；祝英勇的苏联军队在保卫和平、保卫人类正义事业的斗争中获得更辉煌的成就。
    接着讲话的是中苏联合军事委员会中国军事代表团代理主席邓华将军。他说：在你们今天就要全部撤离旅顺口地区的时候，我们怀着无限感激和留恋的心情来欢送你们，我谨代表中苏联合军事委员会中国军事代表团和驻旅大地区的中国人民解放军陆、海、空军全体指战员，对以什维佐夫将军为首的苏军将军、军官、士兵全体同志，致以崇高的敬意和衷心的感谢。中苏两国人民之间的诚挚友谊和两国军队之间的战斗友谊的发展和巩固，是任何敌人所不能战胜的力量。让我们在保卫远东及世界和平的共同事业中，携手并肩胜利前进。
    苏军指挥部什维佐夫将军致告别词。他说：我们怀着感激的心情离开你们美好的国家。苏联人民的忠实朋友——伟大中国人民的光辉形象将永远留在我们的心中。他说：请让我们郑重地向你们和全中国人民保证，苏联人民及其强大的武装力量，永远是伟大的中国人民及其英雄军队的忠实的久经考验的朋友。请把我们的衷心感谢做为苏联人民及其军队对六万万中国人民，对英勇的中国军队，对光荣的中国共产党和中华人民共和国政府，对英明的久经考验的领袖毛泽东主席的最真诚友好的表现。他最后祝中国人民在社会主义建设中，在警惕地保卫胜利果实中，在争取远东和世界和平与安全的斗争中，获得新的更大的成就。讲话毕，数十名少年先锋队员和中国人民解放军的陆、海、空军战士走上主席台向苏军将领和军官们献花。
    欢送大会在“莫斯科—北京”乐声中结束。这时，苏军将领和军官们向欢送的人群频频招手告别。会场上响起了“感谢苏军！”、“中苏两国伟大的友谊万岁！”的暴风雨般的欢呼声。
    什维佐夫将军等在车站站台上检阅了仪仗队。登车以前，什维佐夫将军等再一次和欢送的人们握手告别。
