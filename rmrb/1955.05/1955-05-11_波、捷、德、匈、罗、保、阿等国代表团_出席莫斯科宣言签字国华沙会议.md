Title: Delegations from Poland, Czechoslovakia, East Germany, Hungary, Romania, Bulgaria, Albania, and other countries attend the Warsaw Conference of Signatories to the Moscow Declaration
Author:
Date: 1955-05-11
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Warsaw, 10th May: The Polish Press Agency reported on the 9th that the Government of the People's Republic of Poland has appointed the government delegation to attend the Warsaw Conference of signatories to the Moscow Declaration of December 2, 1954. The head of the government delegation is Chairman of the Council of Ministers Cyrankiewicz. The members of the delegation are Deputy Chairman of the Council of Ministers and Minister of National Defense, Marshal of Poland Rokossovsky, Minister of Foreign Affairs Skrzeszewski, and Deputy Minister of Foreign Affairs Naszkowski.

Xinhua News Agency, Prague, 10th May: The Czechoslovak News Agency reported that the Czechoslovak government delegation left Prague by train for Warsaw on the 10th to attend the Warsaw Conference of signatories to the Moscow Declaration of December 2, 1954. The head of the delegation is Premier of the Republic Široký, and the members of the delegation include First Deputy Premier and Minister of National Defense Čepička and Minister of Foreign Affairs David.

Xinhua News Agency, 10th May: TASS Berlin, 10th May: The government delegation of the German Democratic Republic to attend the Warsaw Conference of signatories to the Moscow Declaration of December 2, 1954, left Berlin for Warsaw on the 9th.

The delegation is led by Prime Minister of the German Democratic Republic O. Grotewohl. Members of the delegation include: Deputy Prime Ministers W. Ulbricht, W. Stoph, L. Bolz, O. Nuschke, H. Rau, Minister of Agriculture and Forestry H. Reichelt, Deputy Minister of the Interior H. Hoffmann, and Ambassador of the German Democratic Republic to Poland S. Hermann.

Xinhua News Agency, 10th May: TASS Budapest, 10th May: The government delegation of the Hungarian People's Republic left Budapest for Warsaw on the 9th to participate in the Warsaw Conference of signatories to the Moscow Declaration of December 2, 1954.

The head of the delegation is Chairman of the Council of Ministers of the Hungarian People's Republic A. Hegedüs, and the members of the delegation include Minister of Foreign Affairs J. Boldoczki, Minister of National Defense General I. Bata, and First Deputy Minister of Foreign Affairs A. Szik.

Xinhua News Agency, 10th May: TASS Bucharest, 9th May: The Council of Ministers of the Romanian People's Republic has appointed the government delegation of the Romanian People's Republic to attend the Warsaw Conference of signatories to the Moscow Declaration of December 2, 1954. The head of the delegation is Chairman of the Council of Ministers Gheorghe Gheorghiu-Dej, and the members of the delegation include Deputy Chairman of the Council of Ministers and Minister of Armed Forces Bodnăraș, Minister of Foreign Affairs Bujiçi, Chief of the General Staff I. Tutoveanu, First Deputy Minister of Foreign Affairs Ploteasa, and Romanian Ambassador to Poland Iannski.

Xinhua News Agency: The government delegation of the People's Republic of Bulgaria, led by Chairman of the Council of Ministers Chervenkov, arrived in Warsaw by train on the 9th and will attend the Warsaw Conference of signatories to the Moscow Declaration of December 2, 1954. Members of the delegation include Minister of Foreign Affairs Neichev and Minister of National Defense General Panchevski.

Chervenkov made a statement at the station.

Xinhua News Agency: The government delegation of the People's Republic of Albania, led by Chairman of the Council of Ministers Shehu, arrived in Warsaw by plane on the 9th to attend the Warsaw Conference of signatories to the Moscow Declaration of December 2, 1954. Members of the delegation include First Deputy Chairman of the Council of Ministers and Minister of National Defense Baluku and Minister of Foreign Affairs Shtulla.

Shehu made a statement at the airport, pointing out the great significance of the upcoming Warsaw Conference for ensuring European peace and security.

### 波、捷、德、匈、罗、保、阿等国代表团　出席莫斯科宣言签字国华沙会议

1955-05-11
第4版()
专栏：

    波、捷、德、匈、罗、保、阿等国代表团
　　出席莫斯科宣言签字国华沙会议
    新华社华沙十日电　据波兰通讯社九日报道：波兰人民共和国政府已经任命了出席一九五四年十二月二日莫斯科宣言签字国华沙会议的政府代表团。政府代表团团长是部长会议主席西伦凯维兹，团员是部长会议副主席兼国防部长、波兰元帅罗科索夫斯基，外交部长斯克热歇夫斯基和外交部副部长纳斯科夫斯基。
    新华社布拉格十日电　据捷克斯洛伐克通讯社报道：捷克斯洛伐克政府代表团十日由布拉格乘火车前往华沙，出席一九五四年十二月二日莫斯科会议宣言签字国华沙会议。代表团团长是共和国总理西罗基，代表团团员包括第一副总理兼国防部长契皮茨卡和外交部长戴维。
    新华社十日讯　据塔斯社柏林十日讯：德意志民主共和国出席一九五四年十二月二日莫斯科宣言签字国华沙会议的政府代表团，在九日离开柏林前往华沙。
    代表团由德意志民主共和国总理奥·格罗提渥率领。代表团团员包括：副总理瓦·乌布利希、威·斯多夫、洛·博尔茨、奥·努舍克、汉·劳，农林部长汉·赖希尔特，内务部副部长海·霍夫曼和德意志民主共和国驻波兰大使斯·赫曼。
    新华社十日讯　据塔斯社布达佩斯十日讯：匈牙利人民共和国政府代表团在九日离开布达佩斯前往华沙，参加一九五四年十二月二日莫斯科宣言签字国华沙会议。
    代表团团长是匈牙利人民共和国部长会议主席安·赫格居斯，代表团团员包括外交部长雅·波尔多奇基、国防部长伊·巴塔上将、外交部第一副部长安·西克。
    新华社十日讯　据塔斯社布加勒斯特九日讯：罗马尼亚人民共和国部长会议已经任命罗马尼亚人民共和国出席一九五四年十二月二日莫斯科宣言签字国华沙会议的政府代表团。代表团团长是部长会议主席乔治乌—德治，代表团团员包括部长会议副主席兼武装部队部部长波德纳拉希、外交部长布吉奇、总参谋长扬·图托维亚诺、外交部第一副部长普利奥特亚萨和罗马尼亚驻波兰大使扬尼斯库。
    据新华社讯　保加利亚人民共和国政府代表团在部长会议主席契尔文科夫的率领下，九日乘火车到达华沙，即将出席一九五四年十二月二日莫斯科宣言签字国华沙会议。代表团团员包括外交部长涅伊切夫和国防部长潘切夫斯基大将。
    契尔文科夫在车站发表了谈话。
    据新华社讯　阿尔巴尼亚人民共和国政府代表团在部长会议主席谢胡率领下，九日乘飞机到达华沙，出席一九五四年十二月二日莫斯科宣言签字国华沙会议。代表团团员包括部长会议第一副主席兼国防部长巴卢库和外交部长什图拉。
    谢胡在机场上发表了谈话，指出了即将召开的华沙会议对于保障欧洲和平和安全的巨大意义。
