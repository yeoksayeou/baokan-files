Title: Vietnamese Council of Ministers Passes Resolution on Restoring the National Economy
Author:
Date: 1955-05-18
Page: 4
　　
　　Xinhua News Agency, Hanoi, May 15th: Vietnam News Agency reported on the 15th that the Council of Ministers of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam recently held a meeting under the chairmanship of President Ho Chi Minh.
    The Council of Ministers approved a number of policies aimed at restoring normal life and activities in all aspects of the newly liberated port of Haiphong and coastal areas.
    The meeting decided to promote the work of restoring normal relations between northern and southern Vietnam.
    The meeting also passed an eight-point policy aimed at encouraging agricultural production in areas where land reform has been completed, and approved a production plan for autumn crops.
    The meeting passed a resolution to strengthen the Ministry of Industry and Commerce in order to develop state-owned and private industrial and commercial enterprises.

### 越南部长会议通过恢复国民经济决议

1955-05-18
第4版()
专栏：

　　越南部长会议通过恢复国民经济决议
    新华社河内十五日电　越南通讯社十五日报道，越南民主共和国部长会议最近在胡志明主席的主持下举行了会议。
    部长会议批准了若干目的在于恢复新近解放的海防港和沿海地区的正常生活和各方面活动的政策。
    会议决定推进恢复越南北部和南部之间的正常关系的工作。
    会议还通过了目的在于鼓励已经完成土地改革地区的农业生产的八点政策，批准了秋季作物的生产计划。
    会议通过一项加强工商部的决议，以便发展国营和私营的工商企业。
