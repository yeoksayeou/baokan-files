Title: Inner Mongolia Autonomous Region Elects Members of the People's Committee
Author:
Date: 1955-05-06
Page: 2

Xinhua News Agency Report:

　　The Second Session of the First People's Congress of the Inner Mongolia Autonomous Region was held in Hohhot from April 25th to 30th.

　　The meeting listened to the "Work Report of the People's Government of the Inner Mongolia Autonomous Region in 1954" by Ulanhu, Chairman of the People's Government of the Inner Mongolia Autonomous Region, and the "Report on the Draft National Economic Plan of the Inner Mongolia Autonomous Region for 1955" by Yang Zhilin, Vice Chairman of the People's Government of the Autonomous Region. In his report, Ulanhu pointed out that after the Central Government decided to abolish the Suiyuan Province and put its original areas under the unified leadership of the Inner Mongolia Autonomous Region in 1954, the people of all ethnic groups in the autonomous region became more united, and the economic, cultural and other construction undertakings of the people of all ethnic groups have made more significant progress. Farmers organized in the autonomous region account for 62.16 percent of the total number of farmers, and organized herdsmen account for about 30 percent of the herdsmen in pastoral areas. Ulanhu also examined the shortcomings existing in various tasks. He proposed to launch an anti-bureaucracy struggle in agencies above the banner and county levels in connection with the current specific tasks, and pointed out that the main purpose of this struggle is to solve the problems of adjusting and streamlining institutions, strengthening the responsibility system, deeply examining the problems in the work, overcoming the unreasonable phenomenon of having too many people doing too little work or too few people doing too much work, and the bureaucratic style of generalized leadership.

　　The delegates carefully discussed these two reports. They unanimously agreed with the two reports and put forward many criticisms and suggestions on the work of the government.

　　The meeting finally adopted the draft organic regulations of the people's congresses and people's committees at all levels in the autonomous region, and elected the members of the People's Committee of the Inner Mongolia Autonomous Region: Chairman Ulanhu (Mongolian), Vice Chairmen Su Qianyi, Yang Zhilin, Kui Bi (Mongolian), Hafunga (Mongolian), Wang Zaitian (Mongolian), Sun Lanfeng, Wang Yilun, Members Wang Wenda, Wang Haishan (Mongolian), Wang Duo, Jiyatay (Mongolian), Keligeng (Mongolian), Li Shijie, Song Zhending, Naqin Shuanghe'er (Mongolian), Ruan Muhan, Tong Shufan (Manchu), Zhou Beifeng, Hu Zhaoheng, Hu'erqin Bilige (Mongolian), Wuliji Aoxi'er (Mongolian), Wulitu (Mongolian), Ulan (female, Mongolian), Gaobuzebo (Mongolian), Gao Zengpei, Liang Yiming, Chen Bingqian, Bilig Batu'er (Mongolian), Duguerzhabu (Mongolian), Zhang Lifan, Zhang Shuliang, Zhang Qin, Zhang Zhuoqing (Hui), Boyan Mandu (Mongolian), Oqir Hoyaqtu (Mongolian), Qasabatar (Mongolian), Yang Yepeng, Zhao Zhanshan, Zhao Yunshi, Zhao Cheng (Mongolian), Rong Xiang (Mongolian), Liu Jingping, Yan Jinli, Quan Xingyuan. The meeting also elected Temuer Bagan (Mongolian) as the President of the Higher People's Court of the Inner Mongolia Autonomous Region.

### 内蒙古自治区选出人民委员会组成人员

1955-05-06
第2版()
专栏：

　　内蒙古自治区选出人民委员会组成人员
    内蒙古自治区第一届人民代表大会第二次会议四月二十五日到三十日在呼和浩特市举行。
    会议听取了内蒙古自治区人民政府主席乌兰夫关于自治区人民政府一九五四年的工作报告，和自治区人民政府副主席杨植霖关于一九五五年度内蒙古自治区国民经济计划草案的报告，乌兰夫在报告中指出：一九五四年，中央决定撤消了绥远省建制，将其原辖地区划归内蒙古自治区统一领导后，自治区各族人民更加团结，各族人民经济、文化等各项建设事业有了更显著的发展。自治区组织起来的农民已占总农户的百分之六十二点一六，组织起来的牧民约占牧区牧户的百分之三十左右。乌兰夫并检查了各项工作中所存在的缺点。他提议结合当前各项具体工作，在旗、县以上各机关中，展开一次反官僚主义的斗争，并指出，这一斗争主要解决调整和精简机构，加强责任制度，深入检查工作中的问题，克服人多事少、事多人少不合理的现象和领导一般化的官僚主义作风。
    代表们认真讨论了这两个报告，他们一致同意这两个报告，并对政府工作提出了许多批评和建议。
    会议最后通过了自治区各级人民代表大会和各级人民委员会组织条例草案，并选举了内蒙古自治区人民委员会的组成人员：主席乌兰夫（蒙族），副主席苏谦益、杨植霖、奎璧（蒙族）、哈丰阿
  （蒙族）、王再天（蒙族）、孙兰峰、王逸伦，委员王文达、王海山（蒙族）、王铎、吉雅泰（蒙族）克力更（蒙族）、李世杰、宋振鼎、那钦双和尔（蒙族）阮慕韩、佟树藩（满族）、周北峰、胡昭衡、胡尔钦毕力格（蒙族）、乌力吉敖喜尔（蒙族）、乌力图（蒙族）、乌兰（女，蒙族）、高布泽博（蒙族）、高增培、梁一鸣、陈炳谦、毕力格巴图尔（蒙族）、都古尔扎布（蒙族）、张立范、张淑良、张钦、张濯清（回族）、博彦满都（蒙族）、鄂齐尔呼雅克图（蒙族）、喀萨巴塔尔（蒙族）、杨叶澎、赵展山、赵云驶、赵诚（蒙族）、荣祥（蒙族）、刘景平、燕金利、权星垣。会议还选举了特木尔巴根（蒙族）为内蒙古自治区高级人民法院院长。　（据新华社讯）
