Title: Burmese Prime Minister U Nu Points Out That the Asian-African Conference Achieved Great Victory
Author:
Date: 1955-05-03
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Rangoon, 1st dispatch: Burmese Prime Minister U Nu said at a press conference held on April 29 that although the countries participating in the Asian-African Conference had different political systems and religious beliefs, they held frank and cordial talks on common issues. This is commendable. He said that the joint communiqué adopted by the conference and the increased mutual understanding among some countries through the conference proved that the conference achieved a great victory.

U Nu said that the success of this conference should be attributed to those who were sincere and patiently sought reconciliation. He said that Premier Zhou Enlai contributed greatly to the success of the conference.

### 缅甸总理吴努指出亚非会议获得巨大胜利

1955-05-03
第4版()
专栏：

　　缅甸总理吴努指出亚非会议获得巨大胜利
    新华社一日讯　仰光消息：缅甸联邦总理吴努在四月二十九日举行的记者招待会上说，参加亚非会议的国家虽然政治制度和宗教信仰各有不同，但是对共同的问题都作了坦率和亲切的会谈。这是难能可贵的。他说，会议通过的联合公报和有些国家通过这次会议而增进了相互的了解，证明这次会议获得了巨大的胜利。
    吴努说，这个会议获得胜利应该归功于那些态度诚恳、耐心谋取和解的人。他说，周恩来总理对会议的成功贡献了很大的力量。
