    Our Great Motherland
    In recent years, China's meteorological work has greatly developed. The Central Meteorological Observatory frequently issues medium-term weather forecasts, which play a significant role in local production and the prevention of natural disasters. A radiosonde observer at the Central Meteorological Observatory is releasing a large balloon into the high altitude to conduct a radiosonde observation.

### 我们伟大的祖国（图片）

1955-05-18
第1版()
专栏：

    我们伟大的祖国
    我国的气象事业，近年来有了很大的发展。中央气象台经常发布中期天气预报，对各地生产和防止自然灾害起着不少作用。中央气象台的探空观测员正把大汽球放到高空中去，进行探空观测。
