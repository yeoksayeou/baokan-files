Title: After the Signing of the US-Cambodia Military "Aid" Agreement, American Military Personnel are Already Active in Cambodia
Author:
Date: 1955-05-25
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Hanoi, May 24th: According to a Vietnam News Agency report on the 23rd, following the recent signing of a military "aid" agreement with the Royal Government of Cambodia, the United States will dispatch a military assistance advisory group to be stationed in Cambodia. Currently, an advance team of American military officers has already arrived in Cambodia and begun operations.

The report also stated that the railway between Phnom Penh and Bangkok has been restored to facilitate the transfer of American weapons from Thailand into Cambodia.

### 美柬军事“援助”协定签字后  美国军人已在柬埔寨活动

1955-05-25
第4版()
专栏：

　　美柬军事“援助”协定签字后
　  美国军人已在柬埔寨活动
    新华社河内二十四日电　据越南通讯社二十三日的消息说，根据最近和柬埔寨王国政府签订的军事“援助”协定，美国将派遣一个军事援助顾问团驻在柬埔寨。目前，一个美国军官的先遣小组已经到达柬埔寨，并开始展开活动。
    消息还说，金边和曼谷之间的铁路已经恢复通车，以便于把美国武器从泰国转运入柬埔寨。
