Title: Egyptian Prime Minister Nasser Discusses Egypt's Pursuit of an Independent Foreign Policy
Author:
Date: 1955-05-21
Page: 4
　
Xinhua News Agency, Cairo, May 20th: Egyptian Prime Minister Nasser delivered a speech at the Cairo Officers' Club on the 19th. He announced that Egypt would establish a National Assembly in January of the following year.

Nasser stated, when discussing Egypt's foreign policy, that Egypt is now pursuing an independent foreign policy. Egypt will fight against imperialism. "Egypt will not tolerate conspiracies carried out in the name of democracy. In Lebanon, Syria, Jordan, and Iraq, I see foreign pawns engaging in conspiratorial activities for foreign interests." Nasser said, "We will not succumb to any form of foreign pressure."

### 埃及总理纳赛尔谈埃及执行独立的外交政策

1955-05-21
第4版()
专栏：

　　埃及总理纳赛尔谈埃及执行独立的外交政策
    新华社二十日讯　开罗消息：埃及总理纳赛尔十九日在开罗军官俱乐部发表演说。他宣布，埃及将在明年一月成立国民议会。
    纳赛尔在谈到埃及外交政策时说，埃及现在执行独立的外交政策。埃及将同帝国主义作斗争。“埃及将不能容忍在民主的名义下所进行的阴谋。在黎巴嫩、叙利亚、约旦和伊拉克，我看到外国的爪牙正在为外国的利益进行阴谋活动。”纳赛尔说，“我们不向任何形式的外国压力屈服”。
