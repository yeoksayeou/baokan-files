Title: Sawadzki Hosts Reception for Delegations

Author: 
Date: 1955-05-15
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Warsaw, May 14th: According to a report by the Polish Press Agency, the Chairman of the State Council of the Polish People's Republic, A. Sawadzki, held a reception on the 13th to entertain the delegations participating in the Warsaw Conference of European countries on safeguarding peace and security in Europe.

    Those attending the reception included: the Soviet delegation headed by Chairman of the Council of Ministers Bulganin, the delegation of the Czechoslovak Republic headed by Prime Minister Široký, the delegation of the German Democratic Republic headed by Prime Minister Grotewohl, the delegation of the Hungarian People's Republic headed by Chairman of the Council of Ministers Hegedüs, the delegation of the Romanian People's Republic headed by Chairman of the Council of Ministers Gheorghe Gheorghiu-Dej, the delegation of the Bulgarian People's Republic headed by Chairman of the Council of Ministers Chervenkov, the delegation of the Albanian People's Republic headed by Chairman of the Council of Ministers Shehu, and the observer of the People's Republic of China attending the Warsaw Conference, Peng Dehuai, Vice Premier and Minister of National Defense of the State Council of the People's Republic of China.

    The Polish side attending the reception included First Secretary of the Central Committee of the Polish United Workers' Party Beirut, head of the delegation of the Polish People's Republic to the Warsaw Conference and Chairman of the Council of Ministers Cyrankiewicz, First Vice Chairman of the Council of Ministers Nowak, Vice Chairman of the Council of Ministers and Minister of National Defense Rokossovsky, Secretaries of the Central Committee of the Polish United Workers' Party Ochab and Mazur, Vice Chairmen of the Council of Ministers Berman and Jóźwiak-Witold, Ministers Radkiewicz and Zambrowski, and others.

    Also attending the reception were the diplomatic envoys to Poland from the participating countries of the Warsaw Conference.

    The reception took place in a very warm and friendly atmosphere.

### 萨瓦茨基招待各国代表团

1955-05-15
第1版()
专栏：

　　萨瓦茨基招待各国代表团
    新华社华沙十四日电　据波兰通讯社报道：波兰人民共和国国务委员会主席亚·萨瓦茨基在十三日举行招待会，招待参加欧洲国家保障欧洲和平和安全的华沙会议的各国代表团。
    出席招待会的有：以部长会议主席布尔加宁为首的苏联代表团，以总理西罗基为首的捷克斯洛伐克共和国代表团，以总理格罗提渥为首的德意志民主共和国代表团，以部长会议主席赫格居斯为首的匈牙利人民共和国代表团，以部长会议主席乔治乌—德治为首的罗马尼亚人民共和国代表团，以部长会议主席契尔文科夫为首的保加利亚人民共和国代表团，以部长会议主席谢胡为首的阿尔巴尼亚人民共和国代表团，中华人民共和国出席华沙会议的观察员、中华人民共和国国务院副总理兼国防部长彭德怀。
    波兰方面出席招待会的有波兰统一工人党中央委员会第一书记贝鲁特，波兰人民共和国参加华沙会议代表团团长、部长会议主席西伦凯维兹，部长会议第一副主席诺瓦克，部长会议副主席兼国防部长罗科索夫斯基，波兰统一工人党中央委员会书记奥哈布和马茹尔，部长会议副主席贝尔曼和约兹维亚克—韦托德，部长拉德凯维奇和萨姆布罗夫斯基等人。
    出席招待会的还有华沙会议的参加国驻波兰的外交使节。
    招待会在非常热烈和友好的气氛中进行。
