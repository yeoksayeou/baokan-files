Title: Presidium of the Supreme Soviet of the USSR Awards the Order of the Red Banner of Labor to Moscow University
Author:
Date: 1955-05-08
Page: 4

　　Presidium of the Supreme Soviet of the USSR
　　Awards the Order of the Red Banner of Labor to Moscow University
    Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, 7th - TASS, Moscow, 6th: The Presidium of the Supreme Soviet of the USSR has awarded the Order of the Red Banner of Labor to the "Lomonosov" Moscow State University, which has already been awarded the Order of Lenin, in celebration of its bicentennial anniversary, and in recognition of its role in promoting the development of science and culture and training skilled personnel.

### 苏联最高苏维埃主席团　授予莫斯科大学以劳动红旗勋章

1955-05-08
第4版()
专栏：

　　苏联最高苏维埃主席团
　　授予莫斯科大学以劳动红旗勋章
    新华社七日讯　据塔斯社莫斯科六日讯：苏联最高苏维埃主席团把劳动红旗勋章授予曾经荣获列宁勋章的国立“洛蒙诺索夫”莫斯科大学，以庆祝它成立二百周年，表扬它在促进科学和文化的发展以及训练熟练干部方面所起的作用。
