    A modern wood processing plant has been completed and put into production in Xi'an. The entire production process of this plant is mechanized. In the future, this plant will mass-produce various building materials and prefabricated doors and windows for basic construction projects in the Northwest region.

　　Bottom photo: The control room of the drying workshop. Xinhua News Agency reporter Yao Zongyi photographed.
