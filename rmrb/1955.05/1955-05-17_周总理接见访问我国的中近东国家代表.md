Title: Premier Zhou Enlai Receives Representatives from Middle Eastern and Near Eastern Countries Visiting China
Author:
Date: 1955-05-17
Page: 4

　　Xinhua News Agency, May 16th: Premier of the State Council of the People's Republic of China, Zhou Enlai, received representatives from Middle Eastern and Near Eastern countries visiting China after attending the Asian Countries Conference at 7:30 PM on May 16th. The representatives included: Abdul Karim Zahour, Member of the Syrian Parliament; Subhi A.K. Katibi, Chairman of the Syrian Trade Union Federation; Yusuf M. Imadi, Chairman of the Syrian Progressive Trade Union Federation; Ibrahim Bakri, Chairman of the Syrian Workers' Congress; Samboul A. Magidalan, Lebanese Journalist; and Abdul Rahman Khalifa, Editor of the Jordanian Literary Magazine.

　　Present at the reception were Guo Moruo, Chairman of the Chinese People's Committee for World Peace; Liao Chengzhi, Vice Chairman; Liu Guanyi, Secretary-General; and Lai Ruoyu, Chairman of the All-China Federation of Trade Unions.

### 周总理接见访问我国的中近东国家代表

1955-05-17
第4版()
专栏：

　　周总理接见访问我国的中近东国家代表
    新华社十六日讯　中华人民共和国国务院总理周恩来于五月十六日下午七时三十分接见了参加亚洲国家会议后来我国访问的中近东国家代表，叙利亚议会议员阿卜杜勒·卡里姆·查霍尔，叙利亚工会联合会主席苏布希·阿·克·卡蒂贝，叙利亚进步工会联合会主席优素福·麦·伊马迪，叙利亚工人大会主席易卜拉欣·巴克利，黎巴嫩新闻记者桑布尔·阿·马吉达朗尼，约旦文学杂志编辑阿卜杜勒·拉曼·哈利法。
    接见时在座的有中国人民保卫世界和平委员会主席郭沫若、副主席廖承志、秘书长刘贯一，中华全国总工会主席赖若愚。
