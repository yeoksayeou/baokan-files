Title: Holiday Activities of Beijing Youth (Photos)
Author:
Date: 1955-05-10
Page: 3

The youth of a certain unit of the Chinese People's Liberation Army Navy held a get-together with the youth of Beijing in Beihai Park. They introduced heroic deeds of fighting on the East China Sea front at the meeting.
　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　Our reporter Gao Liang photographed

Youths of various ethnic groups and youths of the People's Liberation Army sign autographs for each other as souvenirs.
　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　Our reporter Na Yi photographed

The Literary and Art Troupe of the Political Department of the Chinese People's Liberation Army Navy performed many wonderful programs in Beihai Park, which were warmly welcomed by the workers and student youth of Beijing.
　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　Our reporter Gao Liang photographed

### 首都青年的假日活动（图片）

1955-05-10
第3版()
专栏：

　　首都青年的假日活动
    中国人民解放军海军某部的青年们在北海公园同首都青年举行联欢。他们在会上介绍了东海前线作战的英雄事迹。
                                本报记者　高粮摄
　　各民族青年同解放军青年互相签名留念。
　                              本报记者　纳一摄
    中国人民解放军海军政治部文艺工作团在北海公园演出了许多精彩节目，受到首都工人、学生青年们的热烈欢迎。
　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　本报记者　高粮摄
