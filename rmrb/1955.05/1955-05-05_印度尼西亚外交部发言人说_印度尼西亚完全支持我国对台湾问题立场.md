Title: Indonesian Foreign Ministry Spokesman Says Indonesia Fully Supports Our Country's Position on the Taiwan Issue
Author:
Date: 1955-05-05
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Jakarta, 4th. The Indonesian newspaper "Kompas," quoting the words of the Indonesian Foreign Ministry spokesman, emphasized that Indonesia fully supports the People's Republic of China on the Taiwan issue—an inseparable part of Chinese territory—just as Zhou Enlai made it particularly clear at the Bandung Conference that the People's Republic of China supports Indonesia on the West Irian issue.

### 印度尼西亚外交部发言人说　印度尼西亚完全支持我国对台湾问题立场

1955-05-05
第4版()
专栏：

　　印度尼西亚外交部发言人说
　　印度尼西亚完全支持我国对台湾问题立场
　　新华社雅加达四日电　印度尼西亚“指南针报”在援引印度尼西亚外交部发言人的话时强调说，印度尼西亚在台湾——中国领土的不可分割的部分——问题上完全支持中华人民共和国，正如周恩来在万隆会议上特别清楚地说明中华人民共和国在西伊里安问题上支持印度尼西亚一样。
