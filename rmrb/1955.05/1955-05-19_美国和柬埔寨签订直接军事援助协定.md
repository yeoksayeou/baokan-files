Title: US and Cambodia Sign Direct Military Aid Agreement
Author:
Date: 1955-05-19
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Washington, May 17th: The US State Department issued a statement on the 16th, announcing that the United States and Cambodia signed a direct military aid agreement in Phnom Penh on the 16th. According to this agreement, the United States will send a military aid advisory group to be stationed in Phnom Penh.

The military agreement between the United States and Cambodia is a new measure by the United States to undermine the Geneva armistice agreement and control Cambodia.

### 美国和柬埔寨签订直接军事援助协定

1955-05-19
第1版()
专栏：

　　美国和柬埔寨签订直接军事援助协定
    新华社十七日讯　华盛顿消息：美国国务院在十六日发表声明，宣布美国同柬埔寨十六日在金边签订了一项直接军事援助协定。根据这一协定，美国将派遣一个军事援助顾问团驻在金边。
    美国和柬埔寨签订这个军事协定，是美国力图破坏日内瓦停战协定和控制柬埔寨的新措施。
