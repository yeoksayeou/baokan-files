Title: British Communist Party General Secretary Harry Pollitt Departs Beijing for Home

Author:
Date: 1955-05-03
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, 2nd. British Communist Party General Secretary Harry Pollitt left Beijing by plane at 10 a.m. on the 2nd to return to Britain. Those who saw him off at the airport included Liu Shaoqi, member of the Political Bureau of the CPC Central Committee and Secretary of the Secretariat of the CPC Central Committee; Deng Xiaoping, member of the Political Bureau of the CPC Central Committee and Secretary-General of the CPC Central Committee; Wang Jiaxiang, member of the CPC Central Committee; Yang Shangkun, Director of the General Office of the CPC Central Committee; and Zhao Yimin, Li Chuli, and Li Qixin, responsible staff members of the CPC Central Committee organs.

Robert Stewart, Chairman of the Supervisory Committee of the British Communist Party, who came to China for a visit with Harry Pollitt and is still continuing his visit in China, also saw him off at the airport.

### 英国共产党总书记哈里·波立特离京回国

1955-05-03
第1版()
专栏：

　　英国共产党总书记哈里·波立特离京回国
    新华社二日讯　英国共产党总书记哈里·波立特在二日上午十时乘飞机离开北京返归英国。到飞机场送行的有中共中央政治局委员、中央书记处书记刘少奇，中共中央政治局委员、中共中央秘书长邓小平，中共中央委员王稼祥，中共中央办公厅主任杨尚昆，中共中央机关负责工作人员赵毅敏、李初梨、李启新等人。
    到飞机场送行的还有同哈里·波立特一道来中国参观、现尚在中国继续参观的英共中央监察委员会主席罗伯特·斯图尔特。
