Title: Prime Minister Sastroamidjojo Arrives in Guangzhou; Over 10,000 People from All Circles in Guangdong Province and Guangzhou City Welcome Him

Author: 
Date: 1955-05-26
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Jakarta, May 25th: Indonesian Republic Prime Minister Ali Sastroamidjojo and his wife, along with other personnel accompanying Prime Minister Ali Sastroamidjojo on his visit to China, left Jakarta by plane at 1:00 AM on May 25th at the invitation of the Government of the People's Republic of China, embarking on their journey to Beijing.

Chinese Ambassador to Indonesia Huang Zhen and his wife accompanied Prime Minister Sastroamidjojo on the same flight to Beijing.

Those seeing them off at the airport included: Indonesian cabinet ministers and other high-ranking officials, diplomatic envoys from various countries to Indonesia, and personnel from the Chinese Embassy and the Chinese Consulate General in Jakarta.

When answering questions from reporters at the airport, Prime Minister Ali Sastroamidjojo said that his visit to China at the invitation of the Government of the People's Republic of China is in line with the spirit of the Asian-African Conference regarding peace and good neighborliness, and he hopes that all relevant parties will implement the resolutions of the Asian-African Conference.

Xinhua News Agency, Guangzhou, May 25th: Prime Minister of the Republic of Indonesia, Ali Sastroamidjojo, and his wife, along with other personnel accompanying Prime Minister Ali Sastroamidjojo on his visit to China at the invitation of our government, arrived in Guangzhou by special plane at 12:30 PM on the 25th.

Chinese Ambassador to the Republic of Indonesia, Huang Zhen, and his wife accompanied Prime Minister Ali Sastroamidjojo on the same flight to Guangzhou.

Dong Yueqian, Director of the Special Office of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs, came from Beijing to Guangzhou to greet them. Indonesian Ambassador to India, Bala, who arrived in Guangzhou earlier on the 24th, also welcomed them at the airport.

Those welcoming them at the airport included: Guangdong Province Governor Tao Zhu, Vice Governors Gu Dacun, Feng Baiju, He Ximing, Wen Minsheng, Chen Rutang, and Qiu Zhe; Cao Ruoming, Director of the Foreign Affairs Office of the Guangdong Provincial People's Committee, Guangzhou Mayor Zhu Guang, Vice Mayors Zhong Ming, Huang Changshui, Chen Zhifang, Xue Yan, Li Minxin, and Guo Qiaoran, Deputy Commander of the Guangzhou Military Region of the Chinese People's Liberation Army Wen Niansheng, Deputy Political Commissar Liu Xingyuan, as well as senior generals of the Navy and Air Force, and others.

Also welcoming them at the airport were responsible persons of the Guangdong and Guangzhou Municipal Committees of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference, responsible persons of the Guangdong and Guangzhou branches of the Chinese People's Committee for the Defense of World Peace, responsible persons of people's organizations in Guangdong and Guangzhou, as well as staff members of state organs, representatives from the scientific, educational, literary, artistic, journalistic, and religious circles, and people from all walks of life.

Polish People's Republic Consul General in Guangzhou, Piszczek, and Soviet Consul in Guangzhou, Koliushchy, also welcomed them at the airport.

More than 10,000 people welcomed them inside and outside the airport.

The flags of the Republic of Indonesia and the People's Republic of China were hung at the airport.

The plane landed in heavy rain. Prime Minister Ali Sastroamidjojo alighted from the plane in the rain and shook hands cordially with Governor Tao Zhu and other welcoming personnel. The band played the national anthems of the Republic of Indonesia and the People's Republic of China.

Prime Minister Ali Sastroamidjojo reviewed the guard of honor at the airport, accepted flowers presented by children from Guangzhou, and issued a written statement to the press. At this time, the welcoming people applauded warmly and shouted welcoming slogans. Prime Minister Ali Sastroamidjojo walked to the front of the welcoming crowd and waved frequently to the welcoming masses to express his greetings. After leaving the airport, Prime Minister Ali Sastroamidjojo was accompanied by Guangdong Province Governor Tao Zhu in an open-top car to the Yingbin Hotel to rest, and was warmly welcomed by the people cheering along the way.

### 沙斯特罗阿米佐约总理到广州　广东省和广州市各界一万多人前往欢迎

1955-05-26
第1版()
专栏：

　　沙斯特罗阿米佐约总理到广州
　　广东省和广州市各界一万多人前往欢迎
    新华社雅加达二十五日电　应中华人民共和国政府的邀请访问中国的印度尼西亚共和国总理阿里·沙斯特罗阿米佐约和夫人以及其他随同阿里·沙斯特罗阿米佐约总理来华访问的人员，在五月二十五日晨一时乘飞机离开雅加达，启程前往北京。
    中国驻印度尼西亚大使黄镇和夫人陪同沙斯特罗阿米佐约总理同机前往北京。
    到机场送行的有：印度尼西亚内阁的部长们和其他高级官员，各国驻印度尼西亚外交使节以及中国大使馆和中国驻雅加达总领事馆人员等。
    阿里·沙斯特罗阿米佐约总理在机场回答记者们提出的问题时说，他应中华人民共和国政府的邀请访问中国，是符合亚非会议关于和平和睦邻的精神的，他希望有关各方都将实施亚非会议的决议。
    新华社广州二十五日电　应我国政府邀请前来我国访问的印度尼西亚共和国总理阿里·沙斯特罗阿米佐约和夫人及其他随同阿里·沙斯特罗阿米佐约总理来华访问的人员在二十五日十二时三十分乘专机到达广州。
    我国驻印度尼西亚共和国大使黄镇和夫人陪同阿里·沙斯特罗阿米佐约总理同机到达广州。
    我国外交部特派办公厅主任董越千从北京前来广州迎接。二十四日先行到达广州的印度尼西亚共和国驻印度大使巴拉也到机场迎接。
    到机场欢迎的，有广东省省长陶铸，副省长古大存、冯白驹、贺希明、文敏生、陈汝棠、丘哲；广东省人民委员会外事处处长曹若茗，广州市市长朱光，副市长钟明、黄长水、陈志方、薛焰、李民欣、郭翘然，中国人民解放军广州军区副司令员文年生、副政治委员刘兴元以及海、空军高级将领等。
    到机场欢迎的，还有中国人民政治协商会议广东省、广州市委员会负责人，中国人民保卫世界和平委员会广东和广州分会负责人，广东省和广州市人民团体的负责人，以及国家机关工作人员、科学、教育、文学、艺术、新闻、宗教界的代表和各界人民群众。
    波兰人民共和国驻广州总领事彼什契克和苏联驻广州领事柯六奇也到机场欢迎。
    在机场内外欢迎的共有一万多人。
    机场上悬挂着印度尼西亚共和国和中华人民共和国国旗。
    飞机在大雨中降落。阿里·沙斯特罗阿米佐约总理冒雨下机后，和陶铸省长及其他欢迎人员亲切握手。乐队高奏印度尼西亚共和国和中华人民共和国国歌。
    阿里·沙斯特罗阿米佐约总理在机场上检阅了仪仗队，接受了广州市儿童的献花，并向报界发表了书面谈话。这时，欢迎的人们热烈鼓掌和高呼欢迎的口号。阿里·沙斯特罗阿米佐约总理走到欢迎群众行列的前面，向欢迎群众频频招手致意。阿里·沙斯特罗阿米佐约总理步出机场后，即由广东省省长陶铸陪同乘敞篷汽车到迎宾馆休息，沿途受到夹道欢呼的人们的热烈欢迎。
