Title: Peng Dehuai, Vice Premier, Arrives in Warsaw and Delivers Speech at Airport; Bolesław Bierut, Józef Cyrankiewicz, and Others Meet with Peng Dehuai
Author:
Date: 1955-05-12
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Warsaw, May 11th: Peng Dehuai, Vice Premier of the State Council and Minister of National Defense of the People's Republic of China, and observer to the Warsaw Conference of European Countries for Ensuring Peace and Security in Europe, arrived in Warsaw by plane from Berlin on May 10th.

Welcoming him at the airport were Nowak, First Deputy Chairman of the Council of Ministers of Poland, Rokossovsky, Deputy Chairman of the Council of Ministers and Minister of National Defense, Skrzeszewski, Minister of Foreign Affairs, and other Polish government officials.

Also present at the airport to welcome him were the diplomatic envoys of the Soviet Union and various People's Democratic countries to Poland, as well as Wan Yi, Xiao Xiangrong, Chen Chu, Lei Yingfu, advisors who had arrived in Warsaw earlier, and Wang Bingnan, advisor and Chinese Ambassador to Poland.

The national flags of China and Poland were hung at the airport. After disembarking, Vice Premier Peng Dehuai, accompanied by Nowak and Rokossovsky, inspected the guard of honor and delivered a speech at the airport.

In his speech, Peng Dehuai first extended his sincere respect on behalf of the government and people of the People's Republic of China to the people of Warsaw, who had bravely fought against fascism and are gloriously rebuilding their capital. Peng Dehuai then said that now, the imperialist aggressive blocs are trying every possible means to implement the Paris Agreement and revive West German militarism, and the people of Europe and the world are facing the threat of a new war. The Chinese people firmly believe that the Warsaw Conference, convened based on the resolutions of the Moscow Conference of European Countries for Ensuring Peace and Security in Europe and through consultations among participating countries, is of particularly great significance for maintaining peace and security in Europe and the world. The Warsaw Conference will further strengthen the unity and cooperation of the countries in the peace, democracy, and socialist camp headed by the Soviet Union, and this unity and cooperation is a powerful and invincible force for maintaining peace in Europe and the world.

Xinhua News Agency, Warsaw, May 11th: Bolesław Bierut, First Secretary of the Central Committee of the Polish United Workers' Party, received Peng Dehuai, member of the Political Bureau of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of China, Vice Premier of the State Council of the People's Republic of China, and observer to the second meeting of the European Conference of European Countries for Ensuring Peace and Security, on the afternoon of the 10th. Also received were advisors Wan Yi, Xiao Xiangrong, Chen Chu, and Lei Yingfu. Wang Bingnan, Chinese Ambassador to Poland, accompanied them.

Józef Cyrankiewicz, Chairman of the Council of Ministers of the Polish People's Republic, and Rokossovsky, Deputy Chairman of the Council of Ministers and Minister of National Defense, received Peng Dehuai and Wan Yi, Xiao Xiangrong, Chen Chu, and Lei Yingfu on the afternoon of the 10th. Wang Bingnan, Chinese Ambassador to Poland, accompanied them.

Aleksander Zawadzki, Chairman of the Council of State of the Polish People's Republic, also received Peng Dehuai and Wan Yi and others on the same afternoon.

### 彭德怀副总理到达华沙并在机场发表谈话　贝鲁特、西伦凯维兹等接见彭德怀

1955-05-12
第1版()
专栏：

　　彭德怀副总理到达华沙并在机场发表谈话
　　贝鲁特、西伦凯维兹等接见彭德怀
    新华社华沙十一日电　中华人民共和国出席欧洲国家保障欧洲和平和安全的华沙会议的观察员、中华人民共和国国务院副总理兼国防部长彭德怀，五月十日自柏林乘飞机抵达华沙。
    到机场欢迎的有波兰部长会议第一副主席诺瓦克、部长会议副主席兼国防部长罗科索夫斯基、外交部长斯克热歇夫斯基和波兰政府的其他官员。
    到机场欢迎的还有苏联和各人民民主国家驻波兰的外交使节，先期到达华沙的我国观察员的顾问万毅、萧向荣、陈楚、雷英夫及顾问、我国驻波兰大使王炳南等。
    机场上悬挂着中、波两国国旗。彭德怀副总理下机后，在诺瓦克和罗科索夫斯基的陪同下，检阅了仪仗队，并在机场上发表了谈话。
    彭德怀在谈话中首先代表中华人民共和国政府和人民向曾经英勇地进行反法西斯斗争并正在辉煌地重建着自己首都的华沙人民致以衷心的敬意。彭德怀接着说，现在，帝国主义侵略集团正在千方百计地实现巴黎协定和复活西德军国主义，欧洲和世界人民面临着新战争的威胁。中国人民深信：根据欧洲国家保障欧洲和平和安全的莫斯科会议的决议，以及根据参加国之间的磋商而召开的这次华沙会议，对于维护欧洲和世界的和平安全有特别重大的意义。华沙会议将进一步加强以苏联为首的和平、民主和社会主义阵营国家的团结合作，这种团结合作是维护欧洲和世界和平的强大而不可战胜的力量。
    据新华社华沙十一日电　波兰统一工人党中央委员会第一书记贝鲁特，在十日下午接见了我国出席欧洲国家保障欧洲和平和安全会议第二次会议的观察员、中国共产党中央委员会政治局委员、中华人民共和国国务院副总理兼国防部长彭德怀。同时被接见的还有顾问万毅、萧向荣、陈楚、雷英夫。我国驻波兰大使王炳南陪同进见。
    波兰人民共和国部长会议主席西伦凯维兹和部长会议副主席兼国防部长罗科索夫斯基，在十日下午接见了彭德怀和万毅、萧向荣、陈楚、雷英夫。我国驻波兰大使王炳南陪同进见。
    波兰人民共和国国务委员会主席萨瓦茨基也在同日下午接见了彭德怀和万毅等人。
