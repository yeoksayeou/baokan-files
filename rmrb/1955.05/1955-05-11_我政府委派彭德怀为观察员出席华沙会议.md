Title: My Government Appoints Peng Dehuai as Observer to Attend Warsaw Conference
Author:
Date: 1955-05-11
Page: 1

　　Xinhua News Agency, 10th. The Government of the People's Republic of China has appointed Peng Dehuai, Vice Premier of the State Council and Minister of National Defense of the People's Republic of China, as its representative, along with advisors Wan Yi, Wang Bingnan, Xiao Xiangrong, Chen Chu, Lei Yingfu, etc., to attend the second meeting of the European Conference on Guaranteeing Peace and Security in Europe, to be held in Warsaw on May 11, as observers.

### 我政府委派彭德怀为观察员出席华沙会议

1955-05-11
第1版()
专栏：

　　我政府委派彭德怀为观察员出席华沙会议
    新华社十日讯　中华人民共和国政府已委派中华人民共和国国务院副总理兼国防部长彭德怀为代表，偕顾问万毅、王炳南、萧向荣、陈楚、雷英夫等，以观察员身份出席五月十一日在华沙召开的欧洲国家保障欧洲和平和安全会议的第二次会议。
