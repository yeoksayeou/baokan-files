Title: Another Group of Overseas Chinese Refugees Unjustly Expelled by Thailand Returns to China
Author: 
Date: 1955-05-10
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Guangzhou, 9th - Another group of 125 overseas Chinese refugees and 65 of their family members, unjustly expelled by the Thai government, arrived in Shantou on the evening of the 8th. They were welcomed and received by the local overseas Chinese affairs authorities.

### 被泰国无理驱逐的又一批难侨回国

1955-05-10
第1版()
专栏：

　  被泰国无理驱逐的又一批难侨回国
    新华社广州九日电　被泰国政府无理驱逐出境的又一批我国难侨一百二十五人及其家属六十五人在八日晚抵达汕头，他们受到当地侨务机关的欢迎和接待。
