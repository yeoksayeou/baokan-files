Title: Southeast Asian Aggressor Bloc Deploys Spies for Sabotage

Author: 
Date: 1955-05-06
Page: 4

　　Xinhua News Agency, May 4th, Bangkok dispatch: Before the Manila Treaty countries' military representatives conference held in the Philippines had even concluded, these countries' intelligence personnel held a secret meeting in Bangkok, Thailand on the 3rd concerning so-called "anti-subversion activities." The purpose of the meeting was to formulate measures to strengthen terrorist activities in order to counter the struggle of Asian countries' people for independence, peace and democracy.

　　Representatives from the United States, Britain, France, Australia, Pakistan, Thailand, and the Philippines participated in the meeting. New Zealand did not send a representative to the meeting.

### 东南亚侵略集团布置间谍破坏活动

1955-05-06
第4版()
专栏：

　　东南亚侵略集团布置间谍破坏活动
    新华社四日讯　曼谷消息：在菲律宾举行的马尼拉条约国家军事代表会议还没有结束，这些国家的情报人员三日又在泰国的曼谷举行所谓“反颠覆活动”秘密会议。会议的目的是制定加强恐怖活动的办法，来对付亚洲各国人民争取独立、和平和民主的斗争。
    参加会议的有美国、英国、法国、澳大利亚、巴基斯坦、泰国、菲律宾等国的代表。新西兰没有派代表参加会议。
