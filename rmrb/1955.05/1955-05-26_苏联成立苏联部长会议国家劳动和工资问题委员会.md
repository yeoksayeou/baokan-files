Title: The Soviet Union Establishes the State Committee on Labor and Wages under the Council of Ministers

Author:

Date: 1955-05-26
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, May 24th, TASS: In order to strengthen state supervision over the work of various ministries and administrations, and to improve their work in labor and wages, the Presidium of the Supreme Soviet of the Soviet Union ordered on the 24th to establish the State Committee on Labor and Wages under the Council of Ministers of the Soviet Union.

The Presidium of the Supreme Soviet of the Soviet Union appointed Lazar Moiseyevich Kaganovich, First Deputy Chairman of the Council of Ministers of the Soviet Union, as Chairman of the State Committee on Labor and Wages under the Council of Ministers of the Soviet Union.

### 苏联成立苏联部长会议国家劳动和工资问题委员会

1955-05-26
第4版()
专栏：

　　苏联成立苏联部长会议国家劳动和工资问题委员会
    新华社二十五日讯　塔斯社莫斯科二十四日讯：为了加强国家对各部和各主管机关工作的监督以及改进它们在劳动和工资方面的工作，苏联最高苏维埃主席团二十四日命令成立苏联部长会议国家劳动和工资问题委员会。
    苏联最高苏维埃主席团任命苏联部长会议第一副主席拉扎尔·莫伊塞耶维奇·卡冈诺维奇为苏联部长会议国家劳动和工资问题委员会主席。
