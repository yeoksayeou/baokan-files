Title: Ambassador Liu Xiao Presents Gifts to Moscow University on Behalf of Peking University
Author: 
Date: 1955-05-06
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, May 5th: Ambassador Liu Xiao of the People's Republic of China visited Moscow State University in the Soviet Union on the 4th, on behalf of all faculty and students of Peking University in our country, to present gifts to Moscow State University and congratulate it on its 200th anniversary.

    Moscow University held a gift presentation ceremony. Those attending the ceremony included Petrovsky, President of Moscow State University, Mikiforov, Director of the Foreign Affairs Department, professors from Moscow State University, Xu Tiemin, Counselor of the Chinese Embassy in the Soviet Union, and Wu Zhihua, Chairman of the Chinese Students Association at Moscow University.

    After Ambassador Liu Xiao presented the gifts to President Petrovsky, he delivered a speech congratulating all faculty and students of Moscow University. President Petrovsky then spoke, expressing his gratitude to all faculty and students of Peking University and Ambassador Liu Xiao.

    The gifts presented by Peking University this time included a large embroidery of the Moscow University campus scenery, a pair of large vases painted with portraits of Lomonosov, a set of the Twenty-Four Histories of China in eight hundred and twenty volumes, and a set of the Sibu Congkan first series in two thousand one hundred volumes.

    After the gift presentation, Ambassador Liu Xiao, accompanied by President Petrovsky, visited the new buildings of Moscow University.

### 刘晓大使代表北京大学向莫斯科大学送礼

1955-05-06
第4版()
专栏：

　　刘晓大使代表北京大学向莫斯科大学送礼
    新华社莫斯科五日电　中华人民共和国驻苏联大使刘晓四日前往苏联国立莫斯科大学，代表我国北京大学全体师生向莫斯科大学赠送礼品，祝贺莫斯科大学成立二百周年。
    莫斯科大学举行了赠礼仪式。参加赠礼仪式的有莫斯科大学校长彼得罗夫斯基、外国部主任米基弗罗夫、莫斯科大学的教授们、我国驻苏联大使馆参赞许铁民和莫斯科大学中国学生会主席吴治华等。
    刘晓大使把礼品交给彼得罗夫斯基校长后，向莫斯科大学全体师生致词祝贺。接着，彼德罗夫斯基校长讲话，他对北京大学全体师生和刘晓大使表示感谢。
    北京大学这次赠送的礼品包括一大幅莫斯科大学校景的刺绣、一对绘有洛蒙诺索夫像的大花瓶、一部八百二十册的中国二十四史、一部两千一百册的四部丛刊初编。
    赠礼以后，刘晓大使由彼得罗夫斯基校长陪同，参观了莫斯科大学新校舍的大楼。
