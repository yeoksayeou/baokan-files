Title: First Oil Exploration Well Drilled in Minhe Basin
Author:
Date: 1955-05-14
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency

　　The first oil exploration well to be drilled in the Minhe Basin, Qinghai Province, this year has started. Since drilling began on May 8th, the well has completed more than 30 percent of its monthly progress plan within three days.

　　Leading Party and government officials of Qinghai Province and representatives of various ethnic groups visited the drilling site on May 11th. After visiting the towering drilling rig and observing the workers' skilled operations, they were all very excited and expressed their intention to organize the masses of people of all ethnic groups to support oil exploration and extraction work in all aspects.

### 民和盆地第一口石油探井开钻

1955-05-14
第1版()
专栏：

　　民和盆地第一口石油探井开钻
    青海省民和盆地今年要钻凿的第一口石油探井开钻了。这口井从五月八日开钻以来，三天内就完成了月进计划的百分之三十以上。
    青海省党政负责人和各民族的代表在五月十一日参观了这个钻井工地。他们在参观了高大的钻井架和工人们熟练的操作以后，都很兴奋，并表示要组织各民族人民群众从各方面来支援石油勘探和采油工作。　（新华社）
