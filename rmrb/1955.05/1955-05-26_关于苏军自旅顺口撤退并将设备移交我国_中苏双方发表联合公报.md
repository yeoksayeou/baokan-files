Title: Joint Communiqué Issued by China and the Soviet Union on the Withdrawal of Soviet Troops from Port Arthur and the Transfer of Equipment to China
Author:
Date: 1955-05-26
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, May 25th—Joint Communiqué between China and the Soviet Union on the Withdrawal of Soviet Troops from the Jointly Used Chinese Naval Base of Port Arthur and the Transfer of Equipment in the Area to the People's Republic of China Without Compensation:

　　In accordance with the Sino-Soviet Agreement on Port Arthur of February 14, 1950, and the Sino-Soviet Joint Communiqué of October 12, 1954, regarding the withdrawal of Soviet troops from the jointly used Chinese naval base of Port Arthur and the transfer of the base to the complete control of the People's Republic of China, the Government of the Union of Soviet Socialist Republics has withdrawn its troops from the jointly used Chinese naval base of Port Arthur and has transferred the equipment in the area to the Government of the People's Republic of China without compensation. The Government of the People's Republic of China has fully taken over the Port Arthur naval base and the equipment in the area.

　　The Sino-Soviet Joint Military Commission for Port Arthur, established in accordance with the agreement of February 14, 1950, signed the final protocol on May 24, 1955, regarding the withdrawal of Soviet troops from the jointly used Chinese naval base of Port Arthur and the transfer of the equipment in the area to the People's Republic of China.

　　The work of the Sino-Soviet Joint Military Commission in implementing various measures related to the withdrawal of Soviet troops and the transfer of the equipment in the Port Arthur naval base area to the Government of the People's Republic of China was carried out in an atmosphere of complete mutual understanding and friendly cooperation.

### 关于苏军自旅顺口撤退并将设备移交我国　中苏双方发表联合公报

1955-05-26
第1版()
专栏：

　　关于苏军自旅顺口撤退并将设备移交我国
　　中苏双方发表联合公报
    新华社二十五日讯　中苏关于苏联军队自共同使用的中国旅顺口海军根据地撤退并将该地区的设备无偿地移交中华人民共和国的联合公报：
    根据一九五○年二月十四日中苏关于旅顺口的协定，并根据一九五四年十月十二日中苏关于苏联军队自共同使用的中国旅顺口海军根据地撤退并将该根据地交由中华人民共和国完全支配的联合公报，苏维埃社会主义共和国联盟政府已将它的军队自共同使用的中国旅顺口海军根据地撤退，并已将该地区的设备无偿地移交中华人民共和国政府，中华人民共和国政府已完全接管旅顺口海军根据地和该地区的设备。
    根据一九五○年二月十四日的协定组成的旅顺口中苏联合军事委员会，已于一九五五年五月二十四日，就苏联军队自共同使用的中国旅顺口海军根据地撤退并将该地区的设备移交中华人民共和国事宜，签署了最后议定书。
    中苏联合军事委员会实现有关苏联军队撤退并将旅顺口海军根据地地区的设备移交中华人民共和国政府的各种措施的工作，是在彼此完全谅解和友好合作的空气中进行的。
