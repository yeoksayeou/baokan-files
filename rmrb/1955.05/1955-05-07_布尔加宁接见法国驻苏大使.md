Title: Bulganin Receives French Ambassador to the Soviet Union
Author:
Date: 1955-05-07
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, May 5th. TASS, Moscow, May 5th: The Chairman of the Council of Ministers of the Soviet Union, N.A. Bulganin, received the French Ambassador Extraordinary and Plenipotentiary to the Soviet Union, Louis Joxe, on the 5th.

### 布尔加宁接见法国驻苏大使

1955-05-07
第4版()
专栏：

　　布尔加宁接见法国驻苏大使
    新华社六日讯　塔斯社莫斯科五日讯：苏联部长会议主席尼·亚·布尔加宁五日接见了法国驻苏联特命全权大使路易·约克斯。
