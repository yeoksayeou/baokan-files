Title: USSR Issues 32 Billion Ruble Public Bonds to Absorb Residents' Funds and Further Develop the National Economy
Author:
Date: 1955-05-14
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, May 12th (TASS dispatch): In order to absorb residents' funds for the further development of the Soviet national economy, the USSR Council of Ministers has announced the issuance of 32 billion rubles in 1955 National Bonds for the Development of the Soviet National Economy, to be repaid over twenty years.

The bonds will only be issued to residents. Bond certificates and lottery winnings will be exempt from all state and local taxes.

The issuance period for the new bonds will run from November 1, 1955, to November 1, 1975. The face values will be in denominations of 500 rubles, 200 rubles, 100 rubles, 50 rubles, 25 rubles, and 10 rubles.

All interest earned on the bonds will be paid in the form of lottery prizes. The total prize money will be calculated at an average annual interest rate of 2 percent over the twenty-year period. During the twenty-year period of the bond issuance, 25 percent of the bonds will be drawn for prizes, and the remaining 75 percent will be redeemed at face value. The prizes are set so that each 100-ruble bond can win prizes ranging from 200 to 5,000 rubles (including the face value of the bond).

During the twenty-year period of the bond issuance, there will be forty draws, twice a year. Bonds that do not win prizes will be redeemed in installments at face value, starting from November 1, 1960, during the final fifteen years of the bond issuance period.

### 吸收居民的资金进一步发展国民经济　苏联发行公债三百二十亿卢布

1955-05-14
第4版()
专栏：

　　吸收居民的资金进一步发展国民经济
　　苏联发行公债三百二十亿卢布
    新华社十二日讯　据塔斯社莫斯科十一日讯：为了吸收居民的资金用于进一步发展苏联国民经济，苏联部长会议公布发行一九五五年发展苏联国民经济国家公债三百二十亿卢布，分二十年偿还。
    公债只在居民中发行，公债券和中签奖金免征国家和地方的各种赋税。
    新公债的发行期间从一九五五年十一月一日起到一九七五年十一月一日止，票面价值分为五百卢布、二百卢布、一百卢布、五十卢布、二十五卢布和十卢布等种。
    公债券的全部利息所得用中签奖金的形式付出。全部中签奖金按照二十年内每年平均利率百分之二的比例计算。在公债发行的二十年期间，将有百分之二十五的公债券抽签，其余的百分之七十五的公债按照票面价值还本。中签奖金的规定是，每一百卢布的公债券可以得到二百到五千卢布的奖金
  （包括公债券的票面本金在内）。
    在公债发行的二十年期间内，将抽签四十次，每年抽签两次。凡没有得奖的公债券，从一九六○年十一月一日起，即公债发行期的最后十五年中，依照公债券的票面价值分期还本。
