Title: Indonesian Consulate General in Hong Kong Statement Refutes Western News Agency Rumors About Crashed Indian Plane
Author:
Date: 1955-05-16
Page: 4

　　Indonesian Consulate General in Hong Kong Statement
　  Refutes Western News Agency Rumors About Crashed Indian Plane
    Xinhua News Agency, May 15th, Hong Kong News: The Indonesian Consulate General in Hong Kong issued a statement on the afternoon of the 14th refuting rumors. The statement quoted Iman Wang, Chairman of the Indonesian Investigation Committee investigating the sabotage crash of the Indian plane "Kashmir Princess," as saying that he never said that the "Kashmir Princess" plane crash was "a normal accident." Prior to this, some Hong Kong newspapers published news spread by Western news agencies. These messages said that Iman Wang had made the above remarks in Manila.
    Iman Wang arrived in Hong Kong by plane on the afternoon of the 13th. He will conduct investigations in Hong Kong regarding the "Kashmir Princess" plane crash. Several Indonesian and Indian officials participating in the investigation arrived in Hong Kong at the same time as Iman Wang.

### 印度尼西亚驻香港总领事馆声明  驳斥西方通讯社有关失事印度飞机的谣言

1955-05-16
第4版()
专栏：

　　印度尼西亚驻香港总领事馆声明
　  驳斥西方通讯社有关失事印度飞机的谣言
    新华社十五日讯  香港消息：印度尼西亚驻香港总领事馆十四日下午发表声明辟谣。声明引述调查印度飞机“克什米尔公主号”被破坏失事事件的印度尼西亚调查委员会主席伊马汪的话说，他从未说过“克什米尔公主号”飞机失事的事件是“一个通常的事故”。在此以前，某些香港报纸刊载了西方通讯社散播的消息。这些消息说，伊马汪在马尼拉时曾经发表过上述谈话。
    伊马汪是在十三日下午乘飞机到达香港的。他将在香港就“克什米尔公主号”飞机失事事件，进行调查。和伊马汪同时到达香港的还有参加调查工作的印度尼西亚和印度的官员数人。
