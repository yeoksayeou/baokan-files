Title: A Batch of Displaced Overseas Chinese Unjustifiably Expelled by Thailand Returns Home
Author:
Date: 1955-05-07
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Guangzhou, May 5th: A group of 168 displaced overseas Chinese and 21 of their family members, who were unjustifiably expelled by the Thai government, arrived in Shantou on the Haihou vessel on the evening of April 30th. They received proper reception and care from the Overseas Chinese Affairs Bureau of the Eastern Guangdong Administrative Office.

### 被泰国无理驱逐的难侨一批回国

1955-05-07
第1版()
专栏：

　　被泰国无理驱逐的难侨一批回国
    新华社广州五日电　一批被泰国政府无理驱逐出境的归国难侨一百六十八人及其家属二十一人在四月三十日晚乘海后轮抵达汕头。他们受到粤东行署华侨事务局的妥善招待和照顾。
