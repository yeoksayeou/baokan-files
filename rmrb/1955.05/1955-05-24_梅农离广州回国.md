Title: Menon Leaves Guangzhou for Return to His Country

Author: 
Date: 1955-05-24
Page: 1

　　Xinhua News Agency, Guangzhou, May 22nd. Indian Chief Representative to the United Nations, Menon, arrived in Guangzhou at 4:45 PM on the 21st by plane from Beijing. He left Guangzhou by train at 7:40 AM on the 22nd, heading back to his country via Hong Kong. Cao Ruoming, Director of the Foreign Affairs Office of the Guangdong Provincial People's Committee, met him at the airport and train station.

### 梅农离广州回国

1955-05-24
第1版()
专栏：

　　梅农离广州回国
    新华社广州二十二日电　印度驻联合国首席代表梅农在二十一日下午四时四十五分由北京乘飞机到达广州，二十二日早晨七时四十分乘火车离广州取道香港回国。广东省人民委员会外事处处长曹若茗曾到机场和火车站迎送。
