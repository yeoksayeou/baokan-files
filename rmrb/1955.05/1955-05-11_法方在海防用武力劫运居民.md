Title: French Forces Use Violence to Seize and Transport Residents in Haiphong
Author:
Date: 1955-05-11
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency dispatch: On May 4th, French Federal Forces deployed 500 troops along the access roads around the Haiphong Glass Factory, cutting off traffic in an attempt to transport many civilians detained there to the South.

On the evening of that day, relatives and friends of those detained, as well as neighboring residents, went there to stop the illegal actions of the other side. The number of people gradually increased from 500 to 2,000, sometimes reaching 3,000. The struggle continued for a full day and night on the 5th.

On the morning of the 6th, French Federal Forces used violence, seriously injuring several civilians. Residents of Haiphong have filed a complaint with the International Commission inspection team, requesting intervention.

### 法方在海防用武力劫运居民

1955-05-11
第4版()
专栏：

　　法方在海防用武力劫运居民
    据新华社讯　五月四日，法兰西联邦部队把五百名军队布置在海防玻璃厂周围的通道上，截断交通，企图把被拘禁在那里的许多平民运到南部去。
    当天晚上，被拘者的亲友和邻近居民去那里制止对方的非法行为，人数从五百人逐渐增加到二千人，有时达到三千人。斗争在五日继续坚持了一昼夜。
    六日上午，法兰西联邦部队使用了暴力，重伤若干平民。海防居民已经向国际委员会视察小组提出控诉，要求加以干涉。
