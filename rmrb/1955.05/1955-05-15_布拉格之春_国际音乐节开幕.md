Title: "Prague Spring" International Music Festival Opens
Author:
Date: 1955-05-15
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Prague, May 14th: The tenth "Prague Spring" International Music Festival opened in Prague on the 12th. Czechoslovak Republic President Zápotocký, National Assembly Chairman Fierlinger, Deputy Prime Minister Kopecký, and diplomatic envoys from many countries attended the opening ceremony.

Musicians from the Soviet Union, the People's Republic of China, other People's Democratic countries, as well as more than a dozen countries including Britain, France, Italy, and West Germany are participating in this international music festival.

### “布拉格之春”国际音乐节开幕

1955-05-15
第4版()
专栏：

　　“布拉格之春”国际音乐节开幕
    新华社布拉格十四日电　第十届“布拉格之春”国际音乐节十二日在布拉格开幕。捷克斯洛伐克共和国总统萨波托斯基、国民议会主席费林格、副总理柯别茨基和许多国家的外交使节参加了开幕典礼。
    参加这届国际音乐节的有苏联、中华人民共和国和其他人民民主国家以及英、法、意、西德等十几个国家的音乐家。
