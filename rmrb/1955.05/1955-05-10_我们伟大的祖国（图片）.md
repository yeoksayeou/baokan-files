Author:
Date: 1955-05-10

    Our Great Motherland
　　The track-laying and bridge-building project of the Baoji-Chengdu Railway has reached the border of Sichuan and is about to enter Shaanxi. On the newly laid tracks, trains loaded with road-building materials are passing through the mountains towards the construction site.

### 我们伟大的祖国（图片）

1955-05-10
第1版()
专栏：

    我们伟大的祖国
　　宝（鸡）成（都）铁路的铺轨架桥工程已到达四川边境，即将进入陕西。在新铺成的轨道上，满载筑路材料的列车，正通过山区驶向施工现场。
