Title: Mongolian People's Revolutionary Party Central Committee Holds Plenary Session
Author:
Date: 1955-05-21
Page: 4

　　Xinhua News Agency, Ulan Bator, May 20th: According to TASS, a plenary session of the Central Committee of the Mongolian People's Revolutionary Party was recently held in Ulan Bator. The meeting heard a report by Damba, First Secretary of the Central Committee of the Mongolian People's Revolutionary Party, on the work of procuring livestock feed and the tasks of party organizations at all levels. This year, the country will procure 1,387,000 tons of hay and plant more than 1,500 hectares of maize.

　　The plenary session criticized the shortcomings in the work of the Ministry of Animal Husbandry of the Mongolian People's Republic and the State Planning Commission. Tsedenbal, Prime Minister of the Mongolian People's Republic, made a lengthy speech at the meeting.

### 蒙古人民革命党中央委员会举行全体会议

1955-05-21
第4版()
专栏：

　　蒙古人民革命党中央委员会举行全体会议
    新华社二十日讯　据塔斯社乌兰巴托十九日讯：蒙古人民革命党中央委员会全体会议最近在乌兰巴托举行。会议听取了蒙古人民革命党中央委员会第一书记达姆巴的报告——关于牲畜饲料的采办工作和党各级组织的任务。今年，全国要采办一百三十八万七千吨干草，将播种一千五百多公顷玉蜀黍。
    全体会议对蒙古人民共和国畜牧部和国家计划委员会工作的缺点提出批评。蒙古人民共和国总理泽登巴尔在会上作了长篇讲演。
