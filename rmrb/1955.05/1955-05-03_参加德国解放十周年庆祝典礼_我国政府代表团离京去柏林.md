Title: Our Government Delegation Leaves Beijing for Berlin to Attend the Tenth Anniversary Celebration of Germany's Liberation
Author:
Date: 1955-05-03
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, May 2nd. At the invitation of the government of the German Democratic Republic, the government delegation of the People's Republic of China, led by Peng Dehuai, Vice Premier of the State Council, left Beijing by plane for Berlin on May 2nd to attend the tenth anniversary celebration of Germany's liberation.

Those who saw them off at the airport were: Chang Lifu, Deputy Secretary-General of the State Council, Ji Pengfei, Vice Minister of Foreign Affairs, He Wei, Assistant Minister, and Wang Jinchuan, Commissioner of the Protocol Department, among others. Also seeing them off at the airport were Mr. Koenig, Ambassador of the German Democratic Republic to China, and embassy staff.

The list of the government delegation of the People's Republic of China is as follows:

Head of Delegation: Peng Dehuai, Vice Premier of the State Council

Members: Sha Qianli, Minister of Local Industry

　　　　 Lai Jifa, Vice Minister of Heavy Industry

　　　　 Zeng Yongquan, Ambassador Extraordinary and Plenipotentiary of the People's Republic of China to the German Democratic Republic (already in Berlin)

### 参加德国解放十周年庆祝典礼　我国政府代表团离京去柏林

1955-05-03
第1版()
专栏：

　　参加德国解放十周年庆祝典礼
　　我国政府代表团离京去柏林
    新华社二日讯　应德意志民主共和国政府的邀请前往参加德国解放十周年庆祝典礼的中华人民共和国政府代表团，由国务院副总理彭德怀率领，在五月二日乘飞机离京去柏林。
    到机场送行的有：国务院副秘书长常黎夫，外交部副部长姬鹏飞，部长助理何伟，礼宾司专员王锦川等。到机场送行的还有德意志民主共和国驻华大使柯尼希和使馆人员。
　　中华人民共和国政府代表团名单如下：
　　团长　彭德怀国务院副总理
　　团员　沙千里地方工业部部长
　　　　　赖际发　重工业部副部长
　　　　　曾涌泉　中华人民共和国驻德意志民主共和国特命全权大使（已在柏林）
