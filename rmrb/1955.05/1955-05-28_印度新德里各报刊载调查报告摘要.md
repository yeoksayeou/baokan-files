Title: Abstracts of Investigation Reports Published in Various Newspapers in New Delhi, India
Author:
Date: 1955-05-28
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, New Delhi, May 27th. All newspapers in New Delhi published abstracts of the Indonesian Investigation Committee's report confirming the sabotage of the Indian aircraft "Kashmir Princess" on the 27th, with large headlines and prominent placement.

　　The "Times of India" headline was: "'Kashmir Princess' Was Sabotaged," "Mechanisms of Time Bombs Found in the Wreckage."

　　The "Statesman" headline was: "Time Bomb Was the Cause of the Crash of the Air India International Plane."

### 印度新德里各报刊载调查报告摘要

1955-05-28
第1版()
专栏：

　  印度新德里各报刊载调查报告摘要
    新华社新德里二十七日电　新德里所有报纸二十七日都以大字标题和显著地位刊载了证实印度飞机“克什米尔公主号”遭到破坏的印度尼西亚调查委员会报告摘要。
    “印度时报”的标题是：“‘克什米尔公主号’是被破坏的”、“在残骸中发现定时炸弹的机械”。
    “政治家报”的标题是：“定时炸弹是使印度国际航空公司飞机坠毁的原因”。
