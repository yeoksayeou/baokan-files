Title: Afghan Foreign Minister and Indonesian Minister of Information Discuss the Achievements of the Asian-African Conference and Cooperation Among Asian-African Countries
Author:
Date: 1955-05-05
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, April 4th, Kabul: The Afghan newspaper "Islah" published Afghan Foreign Minister Mohammed Naim Khan's answers to questions posed by capital newspaper reporters regarding the results of the Bandung Conference. Mohammed Naim Khan pointed out that the Bandung Conference was "a historic event" for the people of Asia and Africa, and although the participants in the conference had different views on many issues, they were able to unanimously pass resolutions that were in line with the interests of peace. Naim Khan emphasized that Afghanistan "hopes to have good relations with all peace-loving people in Asia and Africa and the people of other countries." Therefore, the Afghan delegation at the conference advocated strengthening economic and cultural ties between Asian and African countries and expressed its willingness to support proposals aimed at strengthening these ties.
    Xinhua News Agency, Jakarta, April 4th: Indonesian Minister of Information Dubin stated to reporters from the Indonesian News Agency on the 1st that the countries participating in the Asian-African Conference can use the Joint Communiqué as a basis for cooperation.
    Dubin said that the Joint Statement between the Prime Ministers of China and Indonesia is an example of the realization of the conference's final communiqué. He said, "This joint statement is in line with the principles of the final communiqué."

### 阿富汗外交大臣和印度尼西亚情报部长　谈亚非会议的成就和亚非国家的合作

1955-05-05
第4版()
专栏：

　　阿富汗外交大臣和印度尼西亚情报部长
　　谈亚非会议的成就和亚非国家的合作
    新华社四日讯　塔斯社喀布尔四日讯：阿富汗“革新报”刊载了阿富汗外交大臣穆罕默德·纳伊姆汗对首都报纸记者提出的关于万隆会议结果的问题的答复。穆罕默德·纳伊姆汗指出，万隆会议对亚非各国人民来说是“一个历史性事件”，虽然会议的参加者在许多问题上有不同的观点，但他们却能够一致通过了符合和平利益的决议。纳伊姆汗着重指出，阿富汗“希望同亚非各国所有爱好和平的人民和其他国家的人民有良好的关系”，因此，阿富汗代表团在会议上主张加强亚非各国之间在经济和文化上的联系，并且表示愿意支持旨在加强这种关系的建议。
    新华社雅加达四日电　印度尼西亚情报部长杜斌一日对印度尼西亚新闻社记者发表谈话说，参加亚非会议的国家可以用联合公报作为合作的基础。
    杜斌说，中国和印度尼西亚两国总理的联合声明，是实现会议最后公报的一个例证。他说，“这个联合声明符合最后公报的原则。”
