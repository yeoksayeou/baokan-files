Title: International Conference on Peaceful Uses of Atomic Energy to be Held in Geneva in August; Skobeltsyn Appointed Vice-President of the International Conference
Author:
Date: 1955-05-23
Page: 4

　　Xinhua News Agency, New York, 22nd. TASS dispatch: United Nations Secretary-General Hammarskjöld announced on the 20th the appointment of six people as vice-presidents of the International Conference on the Peaceful Uses of Atomic Energy. Soviet Academician Skobeltsyn is also one of the vice-presidents.
　　The conference will be held in Geneva from August 8 to 20, 1955.
　　The United Nations Secretary-General announced that the governments of fifty-three countries have accepted the invitation to participate in this conference.

### 和平利用原子能国际会议八月间在日内瓦举行　斯科贝尔琴被任命为国际会议的副主席

1955-05-23
第4版()
专栏：

　　和平利用原子能国际会议八月间在日内瓦举行
　　斯科贝尔琴被任命为国际会议的副主席
    新华社二十二日讯　塔斯社纽约讯：联合国秘书长哈马舍尔德二十日宣布任命六个人为和平利用原子能国际会议的副主席。苏联科学院院士斯科贝尔琴也是副主席之一。
    会议将在一九五五年八月八日到二十日在日内瓦举行。
    联合国秘书长宣布，五十三个国家的政府已经接受参加这次会议的邀请。
