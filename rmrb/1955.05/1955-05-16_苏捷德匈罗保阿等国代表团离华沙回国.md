Title: Soviet, Czechoslovak, Hungarian, Romanian, Bulgarian, and Albanian Delegations Leave Warsaw for Home
Author:
Date: 1955-05-16
Page: 4

　　Soviet, Czechoslovak, Hungarian, Romanian, Bulgarian, and Albanian Delegations Leave Warsaw for Home

　　Xinhua News Agency, Warsaw, May 15th: The government delegations of the Czechoslovak Republic, the German Democratic Republic, the Hungarian People's Republic, the Romanian People’s Republic, the Bulgarian People’s Republic, and the Albanian People’s Republic, which participated in the Warsaw Conference of European Countries on Ensuring European Peace and Security, left Warsaw on the 14th to return to their countries.

　　Officials of the Polish People's Republic government, headed by Chairman of the Council of Ministers Józef Cyrankiewicz, and diplomatic envoys of various countries to Poland saw off the delegations.

　　Xinhua News Agency, May 15th: TASS, Warsaw, May 15th: The Soviet delegation, headed by Chairman of the Council of Ministers of the USSR, N.A. Bulganin, which attended the Warsaw Conference of European Countries on Ensuring European Peace and Security, left Warsaw on the 15th to return to Moscow.

### 苏捷德匈罗保阿等国代表团离华沙回国

1955-05-16
第4版()
专栏：

　　苏捷德匈罗保阿等国代表团离华沙回国
　　新华社华沙十五日电  参加欧洲国家保障欧洲和平和安全华沙会议的捷克斯洛伐克共和国、德意志民主共和国、匈牙利人民共和国、罗马尼亚人民共和国、保加利亚人民共和国和阿尔巴尼亚人民共和国的政府代表团，在十四日离开华沙回国。
    欢送各代表团的有：以部长会议主席西伦凯维兹为首的波兰人民共和国政府的官员和各国驻波兰的外交使节。
    新华社十五日讯  塔斯社华沙十五日电：以苏联部长会议主席尼·亚·布尔加宁为首的、出席欧洲国家保障欧洲和平和安全的华沙会议的苏联代表团十五日离开华沙返莫斯科。
