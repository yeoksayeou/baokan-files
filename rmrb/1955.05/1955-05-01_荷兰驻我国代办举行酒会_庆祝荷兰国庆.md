Title: Dutch Chargé d'Affaires in China Holds Reception to Celebrate Dutch National Day
Author:
Date: 1955-05-01
Page: 1
　　
　　Xinhua News Agency, April 30th: The Dutch Chargé d'Affaires in China, Mr. S.E. van Voorst tot Voorst, held a reception on the afternoon of April 30th to celebrate Dutch National Day.

　　Among those invited to the reception were Huang Yueqian, Director of the General Office of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs of the People's Republic of China, Ke Hua, Director of the Protocol Department, and Zhang Yue, Deputy Director of the Department of Western European and African Affairs.

　　Diplomatic envoys and officials from various countries stationed in China were also invited to attend.

### 荷兰驻我国代办举行酒会　庆祝荷兰国庆

1955-05-01
第1版()
专栏：

　　荷兰驻我国代办举行酒会
  　庆祝荷兰国庆
    新华社三十日讯　荷兰驻华代办司曼在四月三十日下午举行酒会庆祝荷兰国庆日。
    应邀参加酒会的有中华人民共和国外交部办公厅主任黄越千、礼宾司司长柯华、西欧非洲司副司长张越等。
    应邀参加者还有各国驻华使节和外交官员。
