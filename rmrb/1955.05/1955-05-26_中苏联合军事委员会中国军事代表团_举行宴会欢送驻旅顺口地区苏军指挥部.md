Title: Chinese Military Delegation of the Sino-Soviet Joint Military Commission Holds Banquet to See Off the Soviet Army Command in the Lushunkou Area
Author:
Date: 1955-05-26
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Lüda, May 25th: The Chinese military delegation of the Sino-Soviet Joint Military Commission held a banquet in Lushunkou on the afternoon of the 25th to see off the Soviet Army Command in the Lushunkou area, which is about to withdraw to its country.

Invited to the banquet were Generals Shvetsov, Semyonov, Turandaev, Kudryavtsev, and others from the Soviet Army Command, as well as all officers of the Command and their wives.

Soviet Consul General to Dalian, Belyanov, was also invited to the banquet.

On the Chinese side, those present at the banquet included Deng Hua, acting chairman of the Chinese military delegation of the Sino-Soviet Joint Military Commission, members Liu Yalou, Zeng Shaoshan, Gan Siqi, deputy director of the General Political Department of the Chinese People's Liberation Army, senior commanders of the Chinese People's Liberation Army stationed in the Lushunkou area, Guo Shushen, secretary of the CPC Lüda Municipal Committee, Song Li, mayor of Lüda, and Qiao Chuanjue, president of the Lüda Sino-Soviet Friendship Association.

At the banquet, General Deng Hua delivered a farewell speech, wishing all comrades of the Soviet Army Command good health and a pleasant journey.

General Shvetsov of the Soviet Army Command, Soviet Consul General to Dalian Belyanov, Gan Siqi, deputy director of the General Political Department of the Chinese People's Liberation Army, Zeng Shaoshan, member of the Chinese military delegation of the Sino-Soviet Joint Military Commission, Guo Shushen, secretary of the CPC Lüda Municipal Committee, and Song Li, mayor of Lüda, also spoke at the banquet successively.

The banquet proceeded in a friendly atmosphere.

### 中苏联合军事委员会中国军事代表团　举行宴会欢送驻旅顺口地区苏军指挥部

1955-05-26
第1版()
专栏：

　　中苏联合军事委员会中国军事代表团
  　举行宴会欢送驻旅顺口地区苏军指挥部
    新华社旅大二十五日电　中苏联合军事委员会中国军事代表团二十五日下午在旅顺口举行宴会，欢送即将撤退回国的驻旅顺口地区苏军指挥部。
    应邀出席宴会的有苏军指挥部什维佐夫、谢苗诺夫、图兰大也夫、库德良夫采夫等将军，以及指挥部全体军官和军官们的夫人。
    苏联驻大连总领事别良诺夫也应邀出席了宴会。
    中国方面出席宴会的有中苏联合军事委员会中国军事代表团代理主席邓华、委员刘亚楼、曾绍山，中国人民解放军总政治部副主任甘泗淇，中国人民解放军驻旅顺口地区部队指挥部的高级指挥员，中共旅大市委员会书记郭述申，旅大市市长宋黎，旅大市中苏友好协会会长乔传珏等。
    宴会上，邓华将军致欢送词，他祝苏军指挥部全体同志身体健康，旅途愉快。
    苏军指挥部什维佐夫将军及苏联驻大连总领事别良诺夫、中国人民解放军总政治部副主任甘泗淇、中苏联合军事委员会中国军事代表团委员曾绍山、中共旅大市委员会书记郭述申、旅大市市长宋黎等都在宴会上相继讲话。
    宴会在友好的空气中进行。
