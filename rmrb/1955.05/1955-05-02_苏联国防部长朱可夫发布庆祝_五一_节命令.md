Title: Soviet Defense Minister Zhukov Issues Order Celebrating May Day

Author: 
Date: 1955-05-02
Page: 2

　　Soviet Defense Minister Zhukov Issues Order Celebrating "May Day"
　　Soviet Defense Minister Zhukov issued the following order to celebrate International Labor Day "May Day": Comrades soldiers and sergeants! Comrades officers and generals!
　　Today, the Soviet people, together with their armed forces, celebrate the festival of unity of working people all over the world, the festival of fraternal friendship of workers in all countries - "May Day."
　　I congratulate all personnel of the Soviet Army and Navy on "May Day." I wish all soldiers, sergeants, officers and generals new achievements in combat and political training, in mastering modern technology and weapons, and in further improving the combat readiness of the Soviet Union's armed forces. To celebrate "May Day", I order: On May 1st, in our motherland's capital - Moscow, in the capitals of the various union republics, and in the hero cities of Leningrad, Stalingrad, Sevastopol and Odessa, twenty salvoes of artillery will be fired in each city. Long live our motherland - the Union of Soviet Socialist Republics! Long live the great Soviet people and their heroic armed forces! Long live our beloved Soviet government! Long live the inspirer and organizer of all our victories - the Communist Party of the Soviet Union!
　　(Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, April 30th, based on TASS report)

### 苏联国防部长朱可夫发布庆祝“五一”节命令

1955-05-02
第2版()
专栏：

　　苏联国防部长朱可夫发布庆祝“五一”节命令
    苏联国防部长朱可夫发布庆祝“五一”国际劳动节命令如下：士兵和士官同志们！军官和将军同志们！
    今天，苏联人民同他们的武装部队一道庆祝全世界劳动人民团结的节日，一切国家工人兄弟友爱的节日——“五一”节。
    我向苏联陆军和海军的全体人员祝贺“五一”节。祝全体士兵、士官、军官和将军们在战斗和政治训练上、在掌握现代化技术和武器上、在进一步提高苏维埃国家武装部队的战斗准备上获得新的成就。为庆祝“五一”节，我命令：五月一日，在我们祖国的首都——莫斯科，在各加盟共和国首都，在英雄城列宁格勒、斯大林格勒、塞瓦斯托波尔和敖德萨各鸣礼炮二十响。我们的祖国——苏维埃社会主义共和国联盟万岁！伟大的苏联人民和他们的英勇的武装部队万岁！我们亲爱的苏联政府万岁！我们一切胜利的鼓舞者和组织者——苏联共产党万岁！
      （新华社据塔斯社莫斯科四月三十日讯）
