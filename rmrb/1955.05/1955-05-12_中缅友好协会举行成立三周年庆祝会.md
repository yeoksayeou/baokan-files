Title: Sino-Burmese Friendship Association Holds Third Anniversary Celebration

Author: 
Date: 1955-05-12
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, 11th. The Sino-Burmese Friendship Association held a celebration in Beijing on the evening of the 11th to celebrate the third anniversary of the establishment of the Sino-Burmese Friendship Association.

    U Ohn Maung, Chargé d'Affaires ad interim of the Embassy of the Union of Burma in our country, and embassy officials were invited to attend the celebration.

    The following attended the celebration: Zheng Zhenduo, President of the Sino-Burmese Friendship Association, Qian Weichang, Vice President, and directors, Chu Tunan, President of the Chinese People's Association for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries, Yang Hansheng, Vice President, Zhou Gengsheng and Chen Hansheng, Vice Presidents of the Chinese People's Institute of Foreign Affairs, and heads and representatives of other people's organizations, representatives of various Chinese delegations that had visited Burma, and people from the capital's science, education, art, news, and religious circles.

    A portrait of Chairman Mao Zedong and President Ba U hung on the front of the rostrum, with the national flags of China and Burma hanging on both sides. The venue also displayed photos of Burmese Federal Prime Minister U Nu's activities during his visit to China, as well as some gifts from the Burmese government and people to the various Chinese delegations visiting Burma.

    The celebration was presided over by Qian Weichang. At the meeting, Zheng Zhenduo and U Ohn Maung both spoke, congratulating the further development of friendly relations between China and Burma.

    After the meeting, the Chinese cultural delegation, which had recently returned from a visit to Burma, performed Chinese and Burmese songs and dances, as well as Peking Opera and other programs.

### 中缅友好协会举行成立三周年庆祝会

1955-05-12
第1版()
专栏：

　　中缅友好协会举行成立三周年庆祝会
    新华社十一日讯　中缅友好协会十一日晚在北京举行庆祝会，庆祝中缅友好协会成立三周年。
    缅甸联邦驻我国大使馆临时代办吴茂茂铎和使馆官员应邀出席了庆祝会。
    出席庆祝会的有：中缅友好协会会长郑振铎、副会长钱伟长和理事，中国人民对外文化协会会长楚图南、副会长阳翰笙，中国人民外交学会副会长周鲠生、陈翰笙，以及其他各人民团体负责人和代表，曾先后赴缅甸访问的我国各个代表团代表，首都科学、教育、文艺、新闻、宗教界人士。
    主席台正面挂着毛泽东主席和巴宇总统的像，两旁悬着中缅两国国旗。会场里还陈设着缅甸联邦总理吴努在中国访问活动的照片，以及缅甸政府和人民赠送给访问缅甸的我国各个代表团的部分礼品。
    庆祝会由钱伟长主持。会上，郑振铎和吴茂茂铎都讲了话，祝贺中缅两国友好关系获得进一步的发展。
    会后，由最近访问缅甸归来的中国文化代表团演出了中、缅歌舞和京剧等节目。
