Title: US Far East Air Force to Hold Atomic Bomb Attack Exercises
Author:
Date: 1955-05-12
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Tokyo, 9th. The US Far East Air Force Headquarters announced on the 7th that it would conduct a series of aerial attack exercises in May, including atomic bomb attack exercises. The exercises will be commanded separately by the commanders of the US Far East Air Force bases stationed in Japan, South Korea, Okinawa, Guam, Hawaii, and the Philippines.

### 美国远东空军将举行原子弹攻击演习

1955-05-12
第4版()
专栏：

　　美国远东空军将举行原子弹攻击演习
      新华社九日讯　东京消息：美国远东空军司令部七日宣布在五月里要进行一系列的空中攻击演习，包括原子弹攻击演习。演习将由驻在日本、南朝鲜、冲绳、关岛、夏威夷和菲律宾的美国远东各空军基地司令分别指挥。
