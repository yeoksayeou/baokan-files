Title: Deng Xiaoping and Wang Jiaxiang Receive Yugoslav Trade Union Delegation
Author:
Date: 1955-05-28
Page: 4

　  Deng Xiaoping and Wang Jiaxiang Receive Yugoslav Trade Union Delegation
    Xinhua News Agency, May 27th—Deng Xiaoping, member of the Political Bureau of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of China and Secretary-General of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of China, and Wang Jiaxiang, member of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of China, at the request of the Yugoslav Trade Union Delegation visiting our country, received Stamenkovic, head of the Yugoslav Trade Union Delegation, member of the Presidium of the Central Council of the Confederation of Trade Unions of Yugoslavia, and member of the Central Committee of the League of Communists of Yugoslavia, Begovic, a member of the delegation, director of the Yugoslav "Borba" newspaper, member of the Central Committee of the League of Communists of Yugoslavia, and Jegrotija, a member of the delegation and representative of the Yugoslav Zagreb Chemical Workers' Union, on May 26th and 27th and held lengthy talks; exchanged opinions on a number of major issues, and expressed their respective positions and viewpoints.

### 邓小平和王稼祥接见南斯拉夫工会代表团

1955-05-28
第4版()
专栏：

　  邓小平和王稼祥接见南斯拉夫工会代表团
    新华社二十七日讯　中共中央政治局委员、中共中央秘书长邓小平，中共中央委员王稼祥，应被邀来我国访问的南斯拉夫工会代表团的要求，在五月二十六日、二十七日接见了南斯拉夫工会代表团团长、南斯拉夫工会联合会中央理事会主席团委员、南斯拉夫共产主义者联盟中央委员斯塔门科维奇，代表团团员、南斯拉夫“战斗报”社社长、南斯拉夫共产主义者联盟中央委员贝戈维奇和代表团团员、南斯拉夫萨格勒布化学工人工会代表叶哥罗提亚，并进行了长时间的谈话；对若干重大问题交换了意见，并表达了各自的立场和观点。
