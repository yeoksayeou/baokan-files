Title: Trade Union Representatives from Burma and Japan Arrive in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1955-05-26
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, May 24th. The Chairman of the All Burma Trade Union Congress, Tekin Lun, and the delegation of the General Council of Trade Unions of Japan, headed by Kitagawa Yoshito, arrived in Beijing by plane on the afternoon of the 24th at the invitation of the All-China Federation of Trade Unions for a visit to China.

Zhang Weizhen and Dong Xin, Secretaries of the Secretariat of the All-China Federation of Trade Unions, and heads of various industrial trade unions, were at the airport to welcome them.

### 缅甸和日本的工会代表到京

1955-05-26
第4版()
专栏：

　  缅甸和日本的工会代表到京
    新华社二十四日讯　应中华全国总工会邀请来我国参观访问的全缅工会大会主席德钦伦，以北川义行为首的日本工会总评议会代表团在二十四日下午乘飞机到达北京。
    到机场欢迎的，有中华全国总工会书记处书记张维桢、董昕和各产业工会负责人。
