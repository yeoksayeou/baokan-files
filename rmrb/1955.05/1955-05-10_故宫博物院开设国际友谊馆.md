Title: Palace Museum Opens International Friendship Hall
Author:
Date: 1955-05-10
Page: 2
 
　  Palace Museum Opens International Friendship Hall
    The International Friendship Hall of the Palace Museum opened in Beijing on May 6.

    On display here are more than 2,300 gifts presented to China by 40 countries in Asia, Africa, Australia, Europe, and the Americas over the past five years. These include precious gifts from the Soviet Union and government delegations of various people's democratic countries to the Chinese government and Chairman Mao; gifts from countries such as India, Burma, Indonesia, Pakistan, and Japan; and souvenirs of political significance presented by people from other countries. The gifts include paintings, sculptures, ceramics, lacquerware, gold and silver utensils, textiles, embroidery, musical instruments, and national costumes. Each gift embodies the close friendship between the people of various countries and the people of China.
                                    (Xinhua News Agency)

### 故宫博物院开设国际友谊馆

1955-05-10
第2版()
专栏：

　  故宫博物院开设国际友谊馆
    故宫博物院国际友谊馆五月六日在北京开幕。
    在这里陈列着亚洲、非洲、澳洲、欧洲、美洲共四十个国家五年来赠给我国的礼品，共两千三百多件。其中有苏联和各人民民主国家政府代表团赠给我国政府和毛主席的珍贵礼物；有来自印度、缅甸、印度尼西亚、巴基斯坦和日本等国的礼品；也有其他国家人民赠送的富有政治意义的纪念品。礼品中包括绘画、雕塑、陶瓷品、漆器、金银器皿、纺织品、刺绣、乐器和民族服装等。每件礼品都蕴涵着各国人民和我国人民之间的亲密友谊。
                                    （新华社）
