Title: Lao Fighting Units Accuse the Lao Kingdom of Another Armed Attack
Author:
Date: 1955-05-22
Page: 4

　　Lao Fighting Units
　　Accuse the Lao Kingdom of Another Armed Attack
　　Xinhua News Agency, Hanoi, 21st -- According to Vietnam News Agency: According to the "Liberation News", the organ of the Lao Free National United Front, armed groups commanded by the Lao Kingdom government continued to carry out sabotage operations in Baka, Sang Nua province, a gathering area of the Lao Fighting Units, and attacked the Lao Fighting Units stationed nearby on the evening of May 17th. The Lao Fighting Units were forced to fight back in self-defense.
　　"Liberation News" said that the commander of the local Lao Fighting Units has filed a complaint with the International Commission and requested an immediate investigation into the incident.

### 寮国战斗部队　控诉老挝王国又一次武装进攻

1955-05-22
第4版()
专栏：

　　寮国战斗部队
　　控诉老挝王国又一次武装进攻
    新华社河内二十一日电　据越南通讯社讯：据寮国自由民族统一战线机关报“解放报”报道：老挝王国政府方面指挥的武装集团继续在寮国战斗部队集结区桑怒省的巴卡进行破坏行动，并在五月十七日的晚上进攻驻在附近的寮国战斗部队。寮国战斗部队为了自卫，被迫进行回击。
    “解放报”说，当地的寮国战斗部队的司令官已向国际委员会提出控诉，并且要求立刻对这个事件进行调查。
