Title: Radio Stations Established in Gansu Minority Areas
Author:
Date: 1955-05-05
Page: 1

Gansu Minority Areas Establish Radio Stations
    Eighty-one radio stations and five wired broadcasting stations have been established in minority areas of Gansu Province. Four hundred and fifty radios are about to be transported to minority areas soon.
    Radio stations in various locations publish broadcast bulletins, promptly disseminating news, scientific knowledge lectures, weather forecasts, and other information they receive to rural areas and grasslands. This year, the "Broadcast Bulletin" of the Aksai Kazakh Autonomous Region's radio station promptly transmitted the news of the national economic construction bond issuance to the grasslands, and the herdsmen actively responded to this national call. After seeing the broadcast bulletin, all the cadres of the Aksai Working Committee of the Chinese Communist Party subscribed to 2,218 yuan worth of bonds within three days. (Xinhua News Agency)

### 甘肃少数民族地区设收音站

1955-05-05
第1版()
专栏：

　  甘肃少数民族地区设收音站
    甘肃省少数民族地区已经建立了八十一个收音站和五个有线广播站。最近即将有四百五十部收音机运到少数民族地区去。
    各地收音站都出版广播快报，及时把收听到的消息、科学知识讲话、天气预告等传播到农村和草原上。阿克塞哈萨克族自治区收音站的“广播快报”，今年及时把国家发行经济建设公债的消息传到草原上，牧民们积极响应了这一国家的号召。中共阿克塞工作委员会的全体干部看到广播快报后，三天内认购了两千二百一十八元。　（新华社）
