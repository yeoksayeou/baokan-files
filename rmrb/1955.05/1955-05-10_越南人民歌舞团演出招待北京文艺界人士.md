Title: Vietnamese People's Song and Dance Troupe Performance Hosts Beijing Literary and Art Circles

Author:
Date: 1955-05-10
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, 8th: The Vietnamese Democratic Republic People's Song and Dance Troupe performed in Beijing on the evening of the 8th, hosting members of the capital's literary and art circles.

　　Before the performance, Zhou Weizhi, Deputy Director of the Arts Administration Bureau of the Ministry of Culture of the People's Republic of China, delivered a welcoming speech on behalf of the capital's literary and art circles. Liu Zhonglu, head of the Vietnamese Democratic Republic People's Song and Dance Troupe, delivered a reply.

　　The Vietnamese Democratic Republic People's Song and Dance Troupe performed eight song and dance programs with rich national characteristics, filled with optimism and expressing the national character of the people's brave advance. Among them was the bamboo forest dance adapted from the Mang people's dance celebrating a rich hunting harvest, which depicted the scene of the military and civilians celebrating together after defeating the enemy; the Dawn Butterfly Dance, which depicted the life of Dai girls playing with flowers and butterflies in the fields after the restoration of peace; the Production Joy Dance, which depicted the joy of farmers producing on their own land under the leadership of the Vietnamese Labor Party and President Ho Chi Minh; the Artillery Pulling Song, which was created during the Dien Bien Phu battle, profoundly depicting the invincible will of the Vietnamese people; in addition, there were also beautiful folk song solos and guzheng solos. Their performances were warmly welcomed by the audience.

　　Chinese literary and art groups also participated in the performance.

　　Those who attended the performance included: Zheng Zhenduo and Zhang Zhixiang, Vice Ministers of the Ministry of Culture of the People's Republic of China, Dong Yueqian, Director of the General Affairs Office of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs, Chu Tunan, President of the Chinese People's Association for Cultural Exchange with Foreign Countries, Yang Hansheng and Zhao Yimin, Vice Presidents, and literary and art figures such as Tian Han, Ouyang Yuqian, and Chen Yi.

　　Zhou Liang, Charge d'Affaires ad interim of the Embassy of the Vietnamese Democratic Republic in China, also attended the performance.

### 越南人民歌舞团演出招待北京文艺界人士

1955-05-10
第1版()
专栏：

　　越南人民歌舞团演出招待北京文艺界人士
    新华社八日讯　越南民主共和国人民歌舞团八日晚在北京演出，招待首都文艺界人士。
    演出前，中华人民共和国文化部艺术事业管理局副局长周巍峙代表首都文艺界致欢迎词。越南民主共和国人民歌舞团团长刘仲卢致答词。
    越南民主共和国人民歌舞团演出了八个富有民族特色的歌舞节目，它充满了乐观情绪和表现了人民勇敢前进的民族性格。其中有芒族人民庆祝狩猎丰获的舞蹈改编成的竹丛舞，它表现了战胜敌人以后军民联欢的情形；有表现恢复和平后的傣族少女在田间玩花扑蝶的生活的黎明蝴蝶舞；有表现农民在越南劳动党和胡志明主席领导下，在自己土地上生产的生产欢乐舞；有在奠边府战斗中产生的拉炮歌，它深刻地描写出越南人民不可战胜的意志；此外，还有优美的民歌独唱和古筝独奏。他们的演出受到观众热烈欢迎。
    我国文艺团体也参加了演出。
    出席观看的有：中华人民共和国文化部副部长郑振铎、张致祥，外交部办公厅主任董越千，中国人民对外文化协会会长楚图南、副会长阳翰笙、赵毅敏以及文艺界人士田汉、欧阳予倩、陈沂等。
    出席观看的还有越南民主共和国驻我国大使馆临时代办周亮等。
