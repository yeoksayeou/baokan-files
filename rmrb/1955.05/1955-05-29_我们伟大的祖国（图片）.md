Our Great Motherland
    In order to meet the needs of forest protection and afforestation, the Inner Mongolia Forestry Science Research Institute has been established. The staff of the research institute is actively participating in the afforestation work in eastern Inner Mongolia. The protective forests that have grown up in this area are like a green barrier.

### 我们伟大的祖国（图片）

1955-05-29
第1版()
专栏：

    我们伟大的祖国
    为了适应护林和造林的需要，内蒙古成立了林业科学研究所。研究所的工作人员正积极参加内蒙古东部的造林工作。这一带已经成长起来的防护林，像一道绿色的屏障。
