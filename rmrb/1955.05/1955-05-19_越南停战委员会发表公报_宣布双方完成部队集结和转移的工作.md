Title: The Vietnam Ceasefire Commission Issues Communiqué Announcing Completion of Troop Concentration and Transfer Work by Both Sides
Author:
Date: 1955-05-19
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Hanoi, May 18th: Vietnam News Agency reported on the 18th that the Central Joint Ceasefire Commission in Vietnam issued a joint communiqué on May 17th.

The communiqué stated that both sides had completed the work of concentrating military forces and transferring them to temporary garrison areas.

The communiqué stated: "Both sides are determined to continue to bear their responsibility for thoroughly fulfilling the provisions of the Geneva Accords and the final declaration of the Geneva Conference, and reiterate their willingness to respect and thoroughly implement the terms and provisions of this agreement in order to consolidate peace and achieve the unification of Vietnam through general elections."

The communiqué was signed by General Van Tien Dung, Chief Representative of the Delegation of the General Headquarters of the Vietnam People's Army, and General Debreuil, Chief Representative of the Delegation of the General Headquarters of the French Union Forces.

### 越南停战委员会发表公报　宣布双方完成部队集结和转移的工作

1955-05-19
第4版()
专栏：

　　越南停战委员会发表公报
　　宣布双方完成部队集结和转移的工作
    新华社河内十八日电　越南通讯社十八日讯：越南中央联合停战委员会五月十七日发表一项联合公报。
    公报说，双方已经完成了军事部队集结和移交临时驻扎地段的工作。
    公报说：“双方决心继续担负它们彻底履行日内瓦协定和日内瓦会议最后宣言中的规定的责任，并再一次声明，它们愿意尊重和彻底执行这个协定的条款和规定，以巩固和平和通过普选实现越南的统一。”
    公报由越南人民军总司令部代表团首席代表文进勇将军和法兰西联邦部队总司令部代表团首席代表德布勒比松将军签字。
