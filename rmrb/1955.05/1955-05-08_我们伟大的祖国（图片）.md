Our Great Motherland
Han Chinese workers at the Xinjiang "July 1st" Cotton Textile Factory enthusiastically help minority workers learn production techniques. This is a picture of Wang Chuanfen (middle), a Han Chinese female worker and model worker of the first grade in the fine yarn workshop, explaining the things to pay attention to when looking at the yarn spindles to a new Uygur worker.

### 我们伟大的祖国（图片）

1955-05-08
第1版()
专栏：

    我们伟大的祖国
    新疆“七一”棉纺织厂汉族工人热情帮助少数民族工人学习生产技术。这是细纱车间甲等劳动模范、汉族女工王传芬（中）正向新参加生产的维吾尔族工人讲解看纱锭时应注意的事项。
