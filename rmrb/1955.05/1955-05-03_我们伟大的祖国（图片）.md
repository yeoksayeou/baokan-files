    Our Great Motherland
　　Workers in factories and mines across the country are working with heroic and selfless labor to complete the national economic construction plan. The smelting workshop of the Shanghai State-owned Asia Steel Plant has strictly implemented the suggestions of Soviet experts, which has continuously improved the record of electric furnace life.

### 我们伟大的祖国（图片）

1955-05-03
第1版()
专栏：

    我们伟大的祖国
　　全国各地厂矿工人正以英勇忘我的劳动，为完成国家经济建设计划而努力。上海国营亚细亚钢铁厂冶炼车间严格执行了苏联专家建议，使得电炉炉龄的纪录不断提高。
