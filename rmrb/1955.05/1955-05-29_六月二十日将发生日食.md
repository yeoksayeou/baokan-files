Title: Solar Eclipse Will Occur on June 20th
Author:
Date: 1955-05-29
Page: 2

　  A solar eclipse will occur on June 20th
  A total solar eclipse will occur on June 20th this year. Most areas within China can see a partial eclipse between 10:00 AM and 1:00 PM.

    The path of totality mainly lies over the Indian and Pacific Oceans. On land, total eclipse can be seen in Ceylon, Burma, Thailand, Cambodia, Laos, Vietnam, the Philippines, and the Xisha Islands of China. The place where the total eclipse can be seen for the longest time is about 300 kilometers southeast of the Zhongsha Islands of China, lasting for seven minutes and eight seconds (the average total eclipse lasts about three minutes). This total solar eclipse is the longest total eclipse in one thousand two hundred and fifty years. There will be no total solar eclipse lasting longer than this one until July 6, 2168 AD.

    For this solar eclipse, except for the northern parts of Xinjiang, Inner Mongolia, and the three northeastern provinces, the entire country of China can see a partial eclipse. Hainan Island can see 90% of the partial eclipse. The further north you go, the smaller the partial eclipse you will see. South of the Yangtze River, approximately more than half of the eclipse can be seen. (Xinhua News Agency)

### 六月二十日将发生日食

1955-05-29
第2版()
专栏：

　  六月二十日将发生日食
  今年六月二十日将要发生日全食，我国境内绝大部分地区在十时到十三时之间都可以先后看到偏食。
    全食带主要在印度洋和太平洋的海面上，陆地上可以看到全食的地方有锡兰、缅甸、泰国、柬埔寨、老挝、越南、菲律宾和我国的西沙群岛。看到全食时间最长的地方是在我国中沙群岛东南约三百公里的海面上，长达七分零八秒（普通全食时间长约三分钟）。这次日全食是一千二百五十年以来时间最长的一次全食，以后直到公元二千一百六十八年七月六日以前，再没有比这次全食时间更长的日全食。
    这次日食，我国除新疆、内蒙和东北三地的北部外，全国都可以看到偏食。海南岛可以看到百分之九十的偏食，越往北去看到的偏食越小，长江以南大约可以看到一半以上的偏食。（新华社）
