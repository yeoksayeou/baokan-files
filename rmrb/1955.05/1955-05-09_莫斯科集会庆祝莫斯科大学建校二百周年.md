Title: Moscow Rally Celebrates the Bicentennial of Moscow University

Author:

Date: 1955-05-09
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency dispatch: A rally celebrating the bicentennial of Moscow University was held on May 7th in the square in front of the main buildings of the new campus of the "Lomonosov" Moscow State University on Lenin Hills. Several thousand people attended the rally, including faculty and students of Moscow University, representatives of the Moscow public, representatives of various universities in the Soviet Union, and foreign guests who came to attend the anniversary celebration.

    Amidst thunderous applause, Pospelov, Secretary of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of the Soviet Union, read the congratulatory message from the Central Committee of the CPSU and the Council of Ministers of the Soviet Union.

    The congratulatory message stated that since its establishment, Moscow University has always played an important role in training scientific and educational personnel and in promoting Russian and world science. In the Soviet era, the university has cultivated a new generation of talented experts and scientists who are working effectively in all sectors of the Soviet national economy, science, and culture. Currently, the National Moscow University and all higher education institutions in the Soviet Union have the mission of training active builders of communist society.

    Next, Academician Petrovsky, President of Moscow University, spoke. Yelyutin, Minister of Higher Education of the Soviet Union, and Afanasenko, Secretary of the Moscow City Committee of the Communist Party of the Soviet Union, delivered congratulatory speeches at the meeting.

    The meeting adopted a letter of salute to the Central Committee of the CPSU and the Council of Ministers of the Soviet Union in a very warm atmosphere.

    Xinhua News Agency dispatch: A grand celebration was held at the Bolshoi Theatre in Moscow on the 7th to celebrate the bicentennial of Moscow University.

    The conference was attended by teachers and students of Moscow University, representatives of various universities, scientific institutions and social organizations, production innovators and cultural workers. Guests from various People's Democracies and Sweden, Norway, the United States, France, Finland, Austria, and the United Kingdom, as well as diplomatic envoys from some countries to the Soviet Union, also attended the conference.

    Leaders of the Soviet Communist Party and the government, Bulganin, Voroshilov, Malenkov, Mikoyan, Molotov, Khrushchev, Ponomarenko, Secretary of the Central Committee of the CPSU Pospelov, and Suslov, entered the presidium amidst thunderous applause.

    The celebration was presided over by Yelyutin, Minister of Higher Education of the Soviet Union.

    Academician Petrovsky, President of Moscow University, gave a report on the bicentennial of Moscow University.

    Furtseva, Secretary of the Moscow City Committee of the Communist Party of the Soviet Union, Nesmeyanov, President of the Soviet Academy of Sciences, Solovyov, Vice Chairman of the All-Union Central Council of Trade Unions, and Shelepin, Secretary of the Central Committee of the Communist Youth League of the Soviet Union, successively delivered congratulatory speeches at the meeting.

    The congratulatory message from the Central Committee and the Council of Ministers of the Soviet Union and the letter of salute from all faculty and students of Moscow University to the Party and the government were also read out at the meeting.

    A grand artistic performance was held after the meeting.

### 莫斯科集会庆祝莫斯科大学建校二百周年

1955-05-09
第4版()
专栏：

　　莫斯科集会庆祝莫斯科大学建校二百周年
    据新华社讯　五月七日，在苏联国立“洛蒙诺索夫”莫斯科大学列宁山上新校舍主要建筑物前面的广场上，举行了莫斯科大学成立二百周年庆祝大会。参加大会的有莫斯科大学的教职员、学生、莫斯科公众的代表、苏联各高等学校的代表和前来参加校庆的外国来宾共数千人。
    苏联共产党中央委员会书记波斯伯洛夫在暴风雨般的掌声中宣读了苏共中央和苏联部长会议的贺辞。
    贺辞说，莫斯科大学自从成立以来，在训练科学和教育人员和推进俄国和世界科学方面始终起着重要的作用。在苏维埃年代。这所大学培养了新的一代有才能的专家和科学家，他们正在苏联的国民经济、科学和文化所有各个部门中卓有成效地进行工作。目前，国立莫斯科大学和苏联所有的高等学校负有训练共产主义社会的积极建设者的使命。
    接着，莫斯科大学校长彼得罗夫斯基院士讲话。苏联高等教育部长叶留金和苏联共产党莫斯科市委员会书记阿凡纳森科在会上致了贺词。
    会议在非常热烈的气氛中通过了给苏共中央和苏联部长会议的致敬电。
    据新华社讯　莫斯科苏联大剧院七日举行了盛大的庆祝会：庆祝莫斯科大学成立二百周年。
    参加大会的有莫斯科大学师生、各高等学校、科学机关和社会团体的代表、生产革新者和文化工作者。各人民民主国家和瑞典、挪威、美国、法国、芬兰、奥地利、英国的来宾以及一些国家驻苏使节也参加了大会。
    苏联共产党和政府的领导人布尔加宁、伏罗希洛夫、马林科夫、米高扬、莫洛托夫、赫鲁晓夫、波诺马连科、苏共中央书记波斯伯洛夫、苏斯洛夫进入主席团席位时，全场报以暴风雨般的掌声。
    庆祝会由苏联高等教育部部长叶留金主持。
    莫斯科大学校长彼得罗夫斯基院士作了关于莫斯科大学二百周年的报告。
    苏联共产党莫斯科市委员会书记福尔采娃、苏联科学院院长涅斯米扬诺夫、全苏工会中央理事会副主席索洛维耶夫和苏联列宁共产主义青年团中央委员会书记谢列平相继在会上致贺词。
    会上还宣读了苏共中央和部长会议的贺辞以及莫斯科大学全体师生给党和政府的致敬电。
    会后举行了盛大的艺术表演。
