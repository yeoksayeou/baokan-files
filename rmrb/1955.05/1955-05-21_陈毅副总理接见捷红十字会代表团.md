Title: Vice Premier Chen Yi Receives Czechoslovak Red Cross Delegation
Author:
Date: 1955-05-21
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, May 20th – Vice Premier Chen Yi of the State Council of the People's Republic of China received Eduard Tuma, head of the Czechoslovak Red Cross delegation, and other members of the delegation who were invited to visit our country, on the morning of the 20th.

Present at the reception were Li Dequan, President of the Red Cross Society of China, and Gregor, the Czechoslovak Ambassador to China.

### 陈毅副总理接见捷红十字会代表团

1955-05-21
第1版()
专栏：

　　陈毅副总理接见捷红十字会代表团
    新华社二十日讯　中华人民共和国国务院副总理陈毅在二十日上午接见了应邀来我国访问的捷克斯洛伐克红十字会代表团团长爱德华·图马和代表团其他人员。
    接见时在座的有中国红十字会总会会长李德全和捷克斯洛伐克驻我国大使格里哥尔。
