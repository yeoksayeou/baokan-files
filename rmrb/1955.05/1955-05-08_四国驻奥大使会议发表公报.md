Title: Four-Power Ambassadors' Meeting in Austria Issues Communiqué
Author:
Date: 1955-05-08
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency, Vienna, May 7th: TASS Vienna reported on the 6th that the ambassadors of the Soviet Union, the United States, the United Kingdom, and France in Vienna, with the participation of Austrian representatives, held a meeting and have made significant progress in drafting the Austrian peace treaty in the past week.
    The meeting will soon conclude its work.
    The next meeting will be held on Monday, May 9th.

### 四国驻奥大使会议发表公报

1955-05-08
第4版()
专栏：

　　四国驻奥大使会议发表公报
    新华社七日讯　据塔斯社维也纳六日讯：苏、美、英、法驻维也纳大使会议发表了下列公报：在过去一个星期中，苏联、美国、联合王国和法国大使在奥地利代表的参加下举行的会议在草拟对奥和约方面获得了很大的进展。
    会议不久即将结束它的工作。
    下次会议将在星期一，即五月九日举行。
