Title: Bulgarian Volleyball Team Departs Beijing for Home

Author: 
Date: 1955-05-08
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, 7th. The entire contingent of thirty-one members of the Bulgarian national men's and women's volleyball mixed team, who were invited to visit our country, have departed Beijing by plane on the mornings of the 5th and 7th respectively to return home.

Responsible persons from units such as the Physical Culture and Sports Commission of the People's Republic of China, the Cultural Department of the General Political Department of the Chinese People's Liberation Army, the Military Sports Department of the Central Committee of the Chinese New Democratic Youth League, and the Beijing Physical Culture and Sports Commission, as well as our country's volleyball athletes, went to the airport to see them off.

Personnel from the Bulgarian Embassy in our country also went to the airport to see them off.

During their stay in Beijing, the Bulgarian national men's and women's volleyball mixed team, in addition to conducting several friendly matches with our country's volleyball team, also gave reports to our country's volleyball athletes and sports workers, and visited the Central Institute of Physical Education and toured Beijing's scenic spots and historical sites.

On the evening of the 4th, Cai Shufan, Vice-Chairman of the Physical Culture and Sports Commission of our country, hosted a banquet to bid farewell to the Bulgarian national men's and women's volleyball mixed team.

### 保加利亚排球队离京回国

1955-05-08
第4版()
专栏：

　　保加利亚排球队离京回国
    新华社七日讯　应邀来我国访问的保加利亚国家男、女排球混合队全体人员三十一人已分别在五日和七日晨乘飞机离北京回国。
    中华人民共和国体育运动委员会、中国人民解放军总政治部文化部、中国新民主主义青年团中央委员会军事体育部和北京市体育运动委员会等单位的负责人，以及我国的排球运动员等曾到机场欢送。
    保加利亚驻我国大使馆人员也曾到机场欢送。
    保加利亚国家男、女排球混合队在北京期间，除和我国排球队进行了多次友谊比赛外，还为我国排球运动员和体育工作者做了报告，并参观了中央体育学院和游览了北京的名胜古迹。
    四日晚，我国体育运动委员会副主任蔡树藩曾设宴欢送保加利亚国家男、女排球混合队。
