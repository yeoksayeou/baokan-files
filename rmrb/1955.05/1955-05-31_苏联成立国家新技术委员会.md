Title: Soviet Union Establishes State Committee for New Technology
Author:
Date: 1955-05-31
Page: 4

    Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, May 29th (TASS dispatch): In order to better organize the adoption of advanced science, technology, and operational methods in the national economy, and to strengthen scientific and technological promotion, the Presidium of the Supreme Soviet of the Soviet Union ordered the establishment of the State Committee for New Technology of the Council of Ministers of the Soviet Union (Soviet Union State Technology Committee) on the 28th.
    The Presidium of the Supreme Soviet of the Soviet Union appointed V.A. Malyshev, Deputy Chairman of the Council of Ministers of the Soviet Union, as Chairman of the State Committee for New Technology of the Council of Ministers of the Soviet Union.

### 苏联成立国家新技术委员会

1955-05-31
第4版()
专栏：

    苏联成立国家新技术委员会
    新华社三十日讯　塔斯社莫斯科二十九日电：为了更好地组织在国民经济中采用先进的科学、技术和操作法，为了加强科学技术宣传，苏联最高苏维埃主席团二十八日命令成立苏联部长会议国家新技术委员会（苏联国家技术委员会）。
    苏联最高苏维埃主席团任命苏联部长会议副主席维·亚·马利歇夫为苏联部长会议国家新技术委员会主席。
