Title: Chinese Trade Delegation to Japan Returns Home
Author:
Date: 1955-05-17
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency, Harbin, 14th. The Chinese Trade Delegation to Japan, led by head of delegation Lei Renmin, returned to Harbin by train on the morning of the 14th. The delegation left Yokohama Port in Japan on the 6th by ship, transited through Vladivostok, and then returned to China by train.

### 我国访日贸易代表团回国

1955-05-17
第4版()
专栏：

　　我国访日贸易代表团回国
    新华社哈尔滨十四日电　中国访日贸易代表团在团长雷任民率领下，已在十四日晨乘火车回到哈尔滨。代表团在六日由日本横滨港搭船离开日本转道海参威改乘火车回国。
