Title: Czechoslovak Government Committee for Research and Peaceful Use of Atomic Energy Holds Meeting, Takes Important Measures on Utilizing Soviet Assistance
Author:
Date: 1955-05-03
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency, Prague, May 2nd - According to a report by the Czechoslovak News Agency, the Czechoslovak Government Committee for Research and Peaceful Use of Atomic Energy recently held a meeting, listened to a report from the Czechoslovak delegation that went to Moscow to participate in talks on the Soviet Union providing scientific, technical, and production assistance to Czechoslovakia in the peaceful use of atomic energy, and took important measures on utilizing Soviet assistance.

### 捷研究及和平利用原子能政府委员会举行会议　就利用苏联帮助问题采取重要措施

1955-05-03
第4版()
专栏：

　　捷研究及和平利用原子能政府委员会举行会议
　　就利用苏联帮助问题采取重要措施
　　新华社布拉格二日电　据捷克斯洛伐克通讯社报道，捷克斯洛伐克关于研究及和平利用原子能的政府委员会最近举行了会议，听取捷克斯洛伐克派往莫斯科参加苏联在和平使用原子能方面对捷克斯洛伐克提供科学技术和生产帮助的会谈的代表团的报告，政府委员会并就利用苏联帮助问题采取了重要措施。
