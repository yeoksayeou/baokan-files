Title: China National Music Ensemble Arrives in Prague
Author:
Date: 1955-05-11
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Prague, 9th: The 1955 "Prague Spring" International Music Festival will open on May 12th. The Chinese National Music Ensemble, consisting of twenty-eight members led by Li Yuanqing, Director and Deputy Director of the Institute of National Music of the Central Conservatory of Music, arrived in Prague on the evening of the 7th.

After participating in the "Prague Spring" International Music Festival, the Chinese National Music Ensemble will also perform in some cities in Czechoslovakia.

### 我国民族音乐团到布拉格

1955-05-11
第4版()
专栏：

　　我国民族音乐团到布拉格
    新华社布拉格九日电　一九五五年“布拉格之春”国际音乐节将在五月十二日开幕。参加音乐节的中国民族音乐团一行二十八人在团长、中央音乐学院民族音乐研究所副所长李元庆率领下在七日晚到达布拉格。
    中国民族音乐团在参加“布拉格之春”国际音乐节以后还将到捷克斯洛伐克一些城市演出。
