Title: Moscow Celebrates Victory Day Over Fascist Germany
Author:
Date: 1955-05-10
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, 9th. TASS reports from Moscow: Moscow’s Soviet, Communist Party organizations and social groups, along with representatives of the Soviet Army and Navy, held a celebration rally at the Soviet Grand Theatre on the 8th to celebrate Victory Day over Fascist Germany. The rally was attended by generals of the Soviet Army and Navy, innovators in industrial and agricultural production, figures from the science and culture sectors, and representatives from various social organizations. Soviet soldiers who met Allied forces on the Elbe River also attended the rally.

Also participating in the celebration were foreign diplomatic envoys to the Soviet Union, as well as a group of participants in the French Resistance movement, members of the National Assembly, and senators.

At 6:00 PM, leaders of the Soviet Communist Party and the government, Bulganin, Voroshilov, Malenkov, Mikoyan, Molotov, Saburov, Khrushchev, Ponomarenko, Central Committee Secretaries of the Soviet Communist Party Pospelov and Suslov, took their seats on the presidium. Attendees gave them enthusiastic applause.

Chairman of the Soviet Council of Ministers, Bulganin, gave the opening remarks. At his suggestion, all attendees stood and observed a moment of silence for Soviet soldiers and Soviet people who sacrificed their lives in the Great Patriotic War.

Bulganin announced the commencement of the celebration rally. The band played the Soviet national anthem.

Soviet Marshal I.S. Konev delivered a report on the tenth anniversary of the Soviet Union's victory in the Great Patriotic War against Fascism.

Subsequently, Secretary of the Moscow City Committee of the Soviet Communist Party, Kapitonov, foreman of the "Hammer and Sickle" steel factory, Turlanov, Academician of the Soviet Academy of Sciences, Oparin, and textile worker Kotova delivered speeches. Representing the working people of Moscow, the Soviet trade unions, the Soviet intellectuals, and the youth, they warmly congratulated the Soviet soldiers on the tenth anniversary of their victory over Fascist Germany.

Kapitonov said that the Soviet Union's historic victory in the Great Patriotic War was a victory for the Soviet socialist system and state system, and for our heroic armed forces. The Soviet people, who are engaged in peaceful and constructive labor, are vigilantly watching the conspiracies of the enemies of peace. The powerful Soviet armed forces, equipped with the latest weapons and combat technology, are defending peace and the security of the motherland and have the strength to safeguard the honor and independence of the Soviet state.

A grand artistic performance was held after the meeting.

### 莫斯科庆祝对法西斯德国胜利日

1955-05-10
第4版()
专栏：

    莫斯科庆祝对法西斯德国胜利日
    新华社九日讯　据塔斯社莫斯科讯：莫斯科的苏维埃、共产党组织和社会团体会同苏联陆海军代表，于八日在苏联大剧院举行庆祝大会，庆祝对法西斯德国胜利日。参加庆祝大会的有苏联陆海军将领、工农业生产革新者、科学和文化界人士以及各社会团体的代表。在易北河上同盟军会师的苏军军人也出席了大会。
    参加庆祝大会的还有各国驻苏联的外交使节，以及来自法国的一批抵抗运动的参加者、国民议会议员和参议员等。
    下午六时，苏联共产党和政府的领导人布尔加宁、伏罗希洛夫、马林科夫、米高扬、莫洛托夫、萨布罗夫、赫鲁晓夫、波诺马连科，苏联共产党中央委员会书记波斯伯洛夫和苏斯洛夫走上主席台就座。这时，到会者向他们热烈鼓掌。
    苏联部长会议主席布尔加宁在会上致开会词。全体到会者在他的建议下起立，为在伟大卫国战争中牺牲的苏联军人和苏联人民默哀。
    布尔加宁宣布庆祝大会开始。乐队奏起了苏联国歌。
    苏联元帅伊·斯·科涅夫在会上作了关于苏联反对法西斯主义的伟大卫国战争胜利十周年的报告。
    接着，苏联共产党莫斯科市委员会书记卡皮托诺夫、“镰刀与锤子”钢铁工厂工长图尔塔诺夫、苏联科学院院士奥巴林、纺织女工科托娃先后在会上致词。他们分别代表莫斯科劳动人民、苏联工会、苏联知识分子和青年，向苏联军人热烈祝贺战胜法西斯德国十周年。
    卡皮托诺夫说，苏联在伟大卫国战争中的历史性胜利，是苏维埃社会制度和国家制度以及我们英勇的武装部队的胜利。正在从事和平的建设性劳动的苏联人民警惕地注视着和平的敌人的阴谋。以最新式的武器和战斗技术装备起来的、有力量维护苏维埃国家的荣誉和独立的强大的苏联武装力量，正在捍卫着和平和祖国的安全。
    会后举行了盛大的文艺演出。
