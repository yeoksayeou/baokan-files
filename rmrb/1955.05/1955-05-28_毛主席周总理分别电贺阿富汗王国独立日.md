Title: Chairman Mao and Premier Zhou Send Congratulatory Telegrams on Afghanistan's Independence Day
Author:
Date: 1955-05-28
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, 27th. Chairman Mao Zedong of the People's Republic of China sent a congratulatory telegram to King Mohammed Zahir Shah of the Kingdom of Afghanistan. The full text is as follows:
His Majesty King Mohammed Zahir Shah of the Kingdom of Afghanistan: On the occasion of the anniversary of the independence of the Kingdom of Afghanistan, I extend, on behalf of the Chinese people and in my own name, warm congratulations to the Afghan people and to you, and wish the Kingdom of Afghanistan prosperity and the Afghan people happiness.
Chairman Mao Zedong of the People's Republic of China
May 26, 1955

Xinhua News Agency, 27th. Premier Zhou Enlai of the State Council of the People's Republic of China sent a congratulatory telegram to Sardar Mohammed Daoud Khan, Prime Minister of the Kingdom of Afghanistan. The full text is as follows:
Your Excellency Sardar Mohammed Daoud Khan, Prime Minister of the Kingdom of Afghanistan: On the occasion of the anniversary of the independence of the Kingdom of Afghanistan, I extend, on behalf of the Government of the People's Republic of China, the Chinese people, and in my own name, sincere congratulations to Your Excellency, and through Your Excellency, to the Government and people of the Kingdom of Afghanistan. I also wish that the friendly relations between China and Afghanistan will be increasingly enhanced in the cause of safeguarding peace in Asia and the world.
Premier of the State Council of the People's Republic of China Zhou Enlai
May 27, 1955

### 毛主席周总理分别电贺阿富汗王国独立日

1955-05-28
第1版()
专栏：

　　毛主席周总理分别电贺阿富汗王国独立日
　  新华社二十七日讯　中华人民共和国主席毛泽东给阿富汗王国国王穆罕默德·查希尔·沙阿的贺电，全文如下：阿富汗王国国王穆罕默德·查希尔·沙阿陛下：值此阿富汗王国独立纪念日之际，我谨代表中国人民，并以我个人的名义向阿富汗人民和您致热烈的祝贺，并祝阿富汗王国繁荣，阿富汗人民幸福。中华人民共和国主席毛泽东
                    一九五五年五月二十六日新华社二十七日讯　中华人民共和国国务院总理周恩来给阿富汗王国首相萨达尔·穆罕默德·达乌德汗的贺电，全文如下：阿富汗王国首相萨达尔·穆罕默德·达乌德汗阁下：值此阿富汗王国独立纪念日之际，我谨代表中华人民共和国政府，中国人民并以我个人的名义向阁下，并通过阁下向阿富汗王国政府和阿富汗人民表示热诚的祝贺，并祝中阿两国间的友好关系在维护亚洲和世界和平的事业中日益增进。中华人民共和国国务院总理　周恩来
                    一九五五年五月二十七日
