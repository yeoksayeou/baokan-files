Title: Mr. Situ Meitang's Body Was Placed in a Casket Yesterday
Author: 
Date: 1955-05-10
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, 9th. Mr. Situ Meitang's coffin was moved to the Sun Yat-sen Hall in Zhongshan Park on the evening of the 8th, and the body was placed in the casket on the morning of the 9th.

Those who personally oversaw the placing of the body in the casket included: Zhou Enlai, Premier of the State Council and Chairman of the Second National Committee of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference; Chen Yun, Deng Xiaoping, and Chen Yi, Vice Premiers of the State Council; Dong Biwu, Vice Chairman of the Second National Committee of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference, and some members of the Standing Committee; Lin Boqu, Li Jishen, Shen Junru, Guo Moruo, Huang Yanpei, Peng Zhen, Li Weihan, and Chen Shutong, Vice Chairmen of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress, as well as some members; Xi Zhongxun, Secretary-General of the State Council; He Xiangning, Director of the Overseas Chinese Affairs Commission; Liao Chengzhi, Fang Fang, and Zhuang Xiquan, Deputy Directors, and some members; and Chen Qiyou, Chairman of the Central Committee of the China Zhi Gong Party, and some members. Friends of Mr. Situ Meitang were also present to oversee the placing of the body in the casket.

When the body was placed in the casket, Mr. Situ Meitang's wife, sons, and grandchildren were present to keep vigil.

### 司徒美堂先生遗体昨日入殓

1955-05-10
第1版()
专栏：

　　司徒美堂先生遗体昨日入殓
    新华社九日讯　司徒美堂先生灵柩在八日晚移到中山公园中山堂，九日上午入殓。
    亲视入殓的有：国务院总理、中国人民政治协商会议第二届全国委员会主席周恩来，国务院副总理陈云、邓小平、陈毅，中国人民政治协商会议第二届全国委员会副主席董必武和部分常务委员，全国人民代表大会常务委员会副委员长林伯渠、李济深、沈钧儒、郭沫若、黄炎培、彭真、李维汉、陈叔通以及部分委员，国务院秘书长习仲勋，华侨事务委员会主任何香凝，副主任廖承志、方方、庄希泉和部分委员，中国致公党中央委员会主席陈其尤以及部分委员。亲视入殓的还有司徒美堂先生生前亲友。
    入殓时，司徒美堂先生的夫人及其儿、孙在旁守灵。
