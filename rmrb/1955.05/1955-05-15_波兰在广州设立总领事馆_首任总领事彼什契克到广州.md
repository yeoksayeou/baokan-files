Title: Poland Establishes Consulate General in Guangzhou, First Consul General Piszczek Arrives in Guangzhou
Author:
Date: 1955-05-15
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Guangzhou, May 11th - With the agreement of the Chinese government, the government of the Polish People's Republic has established a Consulate General of the Polish People's Republic in Guangzhou. The first Consul General, Piszczek, has arrived in Guangzhou.
　　
Piszczek paid courtesy calls to Tao Zhu, Governor of Guangdong Province, and Zhu Guang, Mayor of Guangzhou, on the 10th.

### 波兰在广州设立总领事馆  首任总领事彼什契克到广州

1955-05-15
第1版()
专栏：

　  波兰在广州设立总领事馆
    首任总领事彼什契克到广州
    新华社广州十一日电　波兰人民共和国政府征得我国政府同意后，在广州设立波兰人民共和国驻广州总领事馆，首任总领事彼什契克已到达广州。
    彼什契克在十日先后拜会了广东省省长陶铸和广州市市长朱光。
