Title: Indonesian Newspapers Publish Commentaries Welcoming the Joint Statement of the Chinese and Indonesian Prime Ministers
Author:
Date: 1955-05-03
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency, Jakarta, 29th - Indonesian newspapers welcomed the joint statement issued in Jakarta on the 28th by the Prime Ministers of China and Indonesia.

    "Indonesia Raya" published an editorial on the 29th, titled "Friendly Gestures," stating that the joint statement by the Prime Ministers of China and Indonesia proves that the desires of Indonesia and China for international cooperation and peace must and certainly can be realized. The editorial stated that the joint statement "is full of deep and sincere friendship." "This friendship will guarantee that we all wholeheartedly realize our wishes."

    "Harian Rakjat" published a commentary on the 29th, stating that the joint statement by the Prime Ministers of China and Indonesia laid the foundation for the principles guiding relations between the two countries. These principles include peace, cooperation, and peaceful coexistence based on mutual respect.

### 印度尼西亚报纸发表评论　欢迎中国和印度尼西亚总理的联合声明

1955-05-03
第4版()
专栏：

　　印度尼西亚报纸发表评论
　　欢迎中国和印度尼西亚总理的联合声明
    新华社雅加达二十九日电　印度尼西亚报纸欢迎中国和印度尼西亚两国总理二十八日在雅加达发表的联合声明。
    “印度尼西亚新闻”二十九日以“友好的表示”为题发表社论说，中国和印度尼西亚两国总理的联合声明，证明印度尼西亚和中国要求国际合作与和平的愿望必须而且一定可以实现。社论说，联合声明中“充满了深切的和真诚的友谊。”“这种友谊将保证我们大家全心全意地实现我们的愿望。”
    “东星报”二十九日发表评论说，中国和印度尼西亚两国总理联合声明奠定了指导两国关系的原则的基础。这些原则包括和平、合作以及在互相尊重的基础上和睦共处。
