Title: Trade Union Organizations from Mongolia and Other Countries Sent Cables to Chinese Trade Unions to Congratulate "May Day"; Lai Ruoyu Telegraphs Chairmen of Japanese "Sohyo" and "Sanbetsu" to Extend Congratulations
Author:
Date: 1955-05-03
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency dispatch: The Central Council of Trade Unions of the Mongolian People's Republic, the General Council of Trade Unions of Japan, and Vittorio, the Secretary-General of the Italian General Confederation of Labour, have respectively sent telegrams to the All-China Federation of Trade Unions, congratulating them on "May Day."

Xinhua News Agency, 2nd: Lai Ruoyu, Chairman of the All-China Federation of Trade Unions, on the 1st, separately telegraphed Fujita Tohtaro, Chairman of the General Council of Trade Unions of Japan, and Yoshida Shigeharu, Chairman of the All-Japan Congress of Industrial Unions, to congratulate them on International Labor Day.

### 蒙古等国工会组织致电我国工会祝贺“五一”　赖若愚分电日本“总评”和“产别”主席致贺

1955-05-03
第4版()
专栏：

　　蒙古等国工会组织致电我国工会祝贺“五一”
　　赖若愚分电日本“总评”和“产别”主席致贺
    据新华社讯　蒙古人民共和国工会中央理事会、日本工会总评议会、意大利总工会总书记维多里奥分别致电中华全国总工会，祝贺“五一”节。
    新华社二日讯　中华全国总工会主席赖若愚，一日分别致电日本工会总评议会主席藤田藤太郎和全日本产业别工会会议主席吉田资治，祝贺“五一”国际劳动节。
