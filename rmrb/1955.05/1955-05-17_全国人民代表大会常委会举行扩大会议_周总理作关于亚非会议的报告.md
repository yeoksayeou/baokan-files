Title: Standing Committee of the National People's Congress Holds Enlarged Meeting: Premier Zhou Reports on the Asian-African Conference
Author:
Date: 1955-05-17
Page: 1

　　Standing Committee of the National People's Congress Holds Enlarged Meeting
　　Premier Zhou Reports on the Asian-African Conference
    Xinhua News Agency, 16th - The Standing Committee of the National People's Congress held its 15th enlarged meeting on May 13th.
    Zhou Enlai, Chief Representative of the Chinese Delegation to the Asian-African Conference and Premier of the State Council, delivered a report on the Asian-African Conference at the meeting. Chen Yi, Ye Jizhuang, representatives of the delegation, and Liao Chengzhi, advisor to the delegation, made supplementary reports at the meeting.
    The meeting conducted a heated discussion of these reports. Speakers unanimously expressed their congratulations on the achievements of the Asian-African Conference and believed that the achievements of the Asian-African Conference were of historical significance and a victory of the forces of peace and democracy over the forces of war. The situation of the Asian-African Conference is a microcosm of the world's struggle, and its success and victory are victories of global significance. The meeting unanimously expressed satisfaction with the work of the Chinese delegation to the Asian-African Conference and agreed with Premier Zhou Enlai's report.

### 全国人民代表大会常委会举行扩大会议　周总理作关于亚非会议的报告

1955-05-17
第1版()
专栏：

　　全国人民代表大会常委会举行扩大会议
　　周总理作关于亚非会议的报告
    新华社十六日讯　全国人民代表大会常务委员会在五月十三日举行第十五次会议扩大会议。
    我国出席亚非会议代表团首席代表、国务院总理周恩来在会上作了关于亚非会议的报告，代表团代表陈毅、叶季壮和代表团顾问廖承志在会上分别作了补充报告。
    会议对这些报告热烈地进行了讨论。发言者一致表示庆贺亚非会议的成就，并认为亚非会议所获得的成就，是具有历史意义的，是和平民主力量对于战争势力的胜利。亚非会议的形势是世界斗争形势的缩影，它的成功和胜利是世界意义的胜利。会议对我国出席亚非会议代表团的工作一致认为满意，并同意周恩来总理的报告。
