Title: Egyptian Minister of Religious Affairs Bakur, Cairo University Constitutional Law Professor Kamel Hold Talks with Heads of China's Trade and Cultural Departments
Author:
Date: 1955-05-31
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, 30th: Egyptian Minister of Religious Affairs Bakur and Cairo University Constitutional Law Professor Kamel held talks with Ye Jizhuang, Minister of Foreign Trade of the People's Republic of China, on the morning of the 30th. The hour-long meeting involved an exchange of views on increasing trade between China and Egypt.

On the evening of the 28th and the morning of the 29th, Bakur and Kamel also held talks with the heads of the Ministry of Culture, the Ministry of Higher Education, the Chinese Academy of Sciences, and the China Islamic Association, making detailed studies on the development of cultural exchanges between China and Egypt. Many issues related to cultural exchange raised during the talks reached a consensus.

Chinese representatives participating in the talks included Qian Junrui, Acting Minister of Culture, Ding Xilin, Vice Minister of Culture, Huang Songling, Vice Minister of Higher Education, Huang Hua, Director of the Department of West European and African Affairs of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs, Pan Zinian, representative of the Chinese Academy of Sciences, Bao Erhan, Chairman of the China Islamic Association, and Da Puren, Vice Chairman of the China Islamic Association.

Both meetings took place in a friendly atmosphere.

### 埃及宗教事务部长巴库尔、开罗大学宪法教授卡迈尔  同我国贸易和文化等部门负责人举行会谈

1955-05-31
第1版()
专栏：

　　埃及宗教事务部长巴库尔、开罗大学宪法教授卡迈尔
　  同我国贸易和文化等部门负责人举行会谈
    新华社三十日讯　三十日上午，埃及宗教事务部长巴库尔、开罗大学宪法教授卡迈尔和中华人民共和国对外贸易部部长叶季壮举行会谈，历时一小时，双方就增进中埃两国之间的贸易问题交换了意见。
    二十八日晚间和二十九日上午，巴库尔和卡迈尔曾和文化部、高等教育部、中国科学院和中国伊斯兰教协会的负责人举行了会谈，就发展中埃两国间的文化交流问题作了详尽的研究。会谈中双方提出的许多有关文化交流的问题都取得了一致的意见。
    中国方面参加会谈的，有文化部代理部长钱俊瑞、副部长丁西林，高等教育部副部长黄松龄，外交部西欧非洲司司长黄华，中国科学院代表潘梓年，中国伊斯兰教协会主任鲍尔汉、副主任达浦生等。
    两次会谈都是在友好的空气中进行。
