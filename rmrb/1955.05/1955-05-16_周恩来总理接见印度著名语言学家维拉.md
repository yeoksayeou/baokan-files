Title: Premier Zhou Enlai Receives Prominent Indian Linguist Vira
Author:
Date: 1955-05-16
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, May 15th. Premier Zhou Enlai of the State Council of the People's Republic of China received Raghu Vira, member of the Council of States of the Indian Parliament, President of the International Academy of Indian Culture, and prominent Indian linguist, at 11:00 a.m. on May 15th.

Present at the reception were Guo Moruo, President of the Chinese Academy of Sciences, Luo Changpei, Director of the Institute of Linguistics, Zheng Zhenduo, Vice Minister of the Ministry of Culture, Chen Jiakang, Assistant Minister of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs, Hong Shen, Director of the Bureau of Foreign Cultural Relations, Chen Zhongjing, Deputy Director, writer Xie Bingxin, and Professor of Oriental Languages ​​Ji Xianlin.

Also present at the reception were Indian Ambassador to China, R.K. Nehru, and Raghu Vira's daughter, Sudarshana Devi.

### 周恩来总理接见印度著名语言学家维拉

1955-05-16
第1版()
专栏：

　　周恩来总理接见印度著名语言学家维拉
    新华社十五日讯  中华人民共和国国务院周恩来总理于十五日上午十一时接见了印度国会联邦院议员、印度文化国际学院院长、印度著名语言学家拉古·维拉。
    接见时在座的有中国科学院院长郭沫若，语言研究所所长罗常培，文化部副部长郑振铎，外交部部长助理陈家康，对外文化联络局局长洪深、副局长陈忠经，作家谢冰心和东方语文学教授季羡林。
    接见时在座的还有印度驻华大使赖嘉文，拉古·维拉的女儿苏达尔·夏娜·德维。
