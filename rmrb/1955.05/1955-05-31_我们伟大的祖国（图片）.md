Our Great Motherland
　　In order to ensure the irrigation of farmland, Jiangsu Province has comprehensively inspected and repaired more than 4,000 water pumps this year. After being repaired, the water pumps in Changshu County are undergoing a collective test run.

### 我们伟大的祖国（图片）

1955-05-31

专栏：

    我们伟大的祖国
    为了保证农田的灌溉，江苏省今年对四千多部抽水机普遍进行了检查和修理。常熟县的抽水机经过检修后，正进行集体试车。
