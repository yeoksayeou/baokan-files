Title: President Ho Chi Minh Receives People's Representatives
Author:
Date: 1955-05-25
Page: 4

　　Xinhua News Agency, Hanoi, May 23rd: President of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam, Ho Chi Minh, received more than 30 representatives from Hong Gai, Quang Yen, Haiphong, Southern Vietnam, and the Thai-Meo Autonomous Region on the morning of the 23rd. He had cordial conversations with the representatives.

　　The workers' representative from the newly liberated city of Haiphong reported to President Ho Chi Minh about the increase in production and the practice of thrift among Haiphong workers after liberation.

### 胡志明主席接见人民代表

1955-05-25
第4版()
专栏：

　　胡志明主席接见人民代表
    新华社河内二十三日电　越南民主共和国主席胡志明在二十三日上午接见了来自鸿基、广安、海防、南越和泰族—苗族自治区的代表三十多人。他同代表们作了亲切的谈话。
    新解放的海防市工人代表向胡志明主席报告了海防工人在解放后增加生产、厉行节约的情况。
