Title: Chinese Government Delegation Arrives in Berlin
Author:
Date: 1955-05-08
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, Berlin, 7th. The People's Republic of China government delegation, headed by Vice Premier Peng Dehuai, which was invited to attend the tenth anniversary of Germany's liberation, arrived in Berlin by plane on the 6th.

　　At the airport to welcome them were Deputy Prime Minister and Minister of Foreign Affairs of the German Democratic Republic, Bolz, Secretary of the Central Committee of the German Socialist Unity Party, Oelsner, and many other high-ranking government officials.

　　China's ambassador to Germany, Zeng Yongquan, and diplomatic personnel from the embassy were also at the airport to welcome them.

　　Representatives from the embassies of the Soviet Union and other People's Democratic countries in Germany were also at the airport to welcome them.

　　After the delegation disembarked, the band played the national anthems of China and Germany. Then, Vice Premier Peng Dehuai and others inspected the honor guard at the airport.

### 我国政府代表团到达柏林

1955-05-08
第1版()
专栏：

　　我国政府代表团到达柏林
    新华社柏林七日电　以彭德怀副总理为首的应邀参加德国解放十周年庆祝典礼的中华人民共和国政府代表团，六日乘飞机抵达柏林。
    在飞机场欢迎的有德意志民主共和国副总理兼外交部长博尔茨，德国统一社会党中央委员会书记厄斯纳及其他高级政府官员多人。
    我国驻德大使曾涌泉及大使馆外交人员也到机场欢迎。
    到机场欢迎的，还有苏联及人民民主国家驻德使馆代表。
    代表团下飞机后，乐队奏起中德两国国歌，接着，彭德怀副总理等在机场检阅了仪仗队。
