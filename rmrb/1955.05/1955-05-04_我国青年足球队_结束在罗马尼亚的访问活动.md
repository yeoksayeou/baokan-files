Title: Our Youth Football Team Concludes Visit to Romania

Author:

Date: 1955-05-04
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency dispatch: Our Youth Football Team, which was invited to visit Romania, concluded its visit on April 30th and departed for Hungary.

　　After arriving in Romania on April 19th, our Youth Football Team played three friendly matches with Romanian football teams in Bucharest, Constanta, and Arad, with a total audience of approximately 100,000 people. The match between the Youth Football Team and the Romanian 1954 National Champion Team - the Red Flag Football Team of Arad, ended in a 1-1 tie.

### 我国青年足球队　结束在罗马尼亚的访问活动

1955-05-04
第4版()
专栏：

　　我国青年足球队
　　结束在罗马尼亚的访问活动
    据新华社讯　应邀到罗马尼亚访问的我国青年足球队，在四月三十日结束了在罗马尼亚的访问活动，转赴匈牙利访问。
    我国青年足球队从四月十九日到达罗马尼亚后，曾先后在布加勒斯特、康斯坦塔和阿拉德三市同罗马尼亚的足球队作了三次友谊比赛，观众共约有十万人。青年足球队同罗马尼亚一九五四年全国冠军队——阿拉德市红旗足球队的比赛，以一比一打成平局。
