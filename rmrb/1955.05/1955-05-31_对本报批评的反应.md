Title: Responses to Criticisms from This Newspaper
Author:
Date: 1955-05-31
Page: 2

　　Responses to Criticisms from This Newspaper
    On March 14th, this newspaper published an article entitled "Curbing the Destruction of Forest Resources," criticizing the serious phenomenon of unreasonable logging in Northeast China's forest industry. The Ministry of Forestry has discussed this issue and studied it at the conference of directors of state-owned forest area forest industry bureaus held in April, and put forward specific measures to strengthen rational logging and the management of state-owned forests.
    The Ministry of Forestry is also preparing to organize inspection teams to specifically help grassroots units implement the decisions of the conference and overcome unreasonable logging practices.
                  ×　×　×
    On March 28th, this newspaper criticized the waste in the construction of the New Beijing Hotel. The State Council Government Offices Administration Bureau, which is responsible for the construction of the new building of the Beijing Hotel, has sent a letter of self-criticism.

### 对本报批评的反应

1955-05-31
第2版()
专栏：

　　对本报批评的反应
    三月十四日本报发表了“制止破坏森林资源的行为”一文，批评东北森林工业采伐不合理的严重现象。林业部对此问题已作了讨论，并在四月份召开的国有林区森林工业局长会议上作了研究，提出了加强合理采伐和国有林经营管理的具体办法。
    林业部还准备组织检查组具体帮助基层单位贯彻会议决议，克服不合理的采伐现象。
                  ×　×　×
    三月二十八日本报对新北京饭店建筑中的浪费提出了批评。承担北京饭店新楼建筑任务的国务院机关事务管理局已来信检讨。
