Author:
Date: 1955-05-22

    Our Great Motherland
    Six processing plants serving the basic construction of Anshan Iron and Steel are installing machinery and will soon be put into production. After these plants are put into production, they will increase the production capacity of various materials required for basic construction and accelerate the progress of basic construction projects. This precast concrete factory has been put into production, and a large number of precast slabs and precast beams are being produced here.

### 我们伟大的祖国（图片）

1955-05-22
第1版()
专栏：

    我们伟大的祖国
    为鞍钢基本建设服务的六个加工厂正在安装机械，即将全部投入生产。这批工厂投入生产后，将提高基本建设需用的各种器材的生产能力，加快基本建设工程的进度。这座混凝土预制品工厂已投入生产，这里正生产着大量的预制板和预制梁。
