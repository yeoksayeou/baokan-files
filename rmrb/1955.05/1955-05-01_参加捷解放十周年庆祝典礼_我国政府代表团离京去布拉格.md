Title: Chinese Government Delegation Leaves Beijing for Prague to Attend the 10th Anniversary Celebration of the Liberation of Czechoslovakia
Author:
Date: 1955-05-01
Page: 1

　　Attending the Czechoslovak Liberation Tenth Anniversary Celebration
　　Chinese Government Delegation Leaves Beijing for Prague
    Xinhua News Agency, 30th. At the invitation of the Government of the Czechoslovak Republic, the Government Delegation of the People's Republic of China, led by Ulanhu, Vice Premier of the State Council, left Beijing for Prague by plane on April 30th to attend the celebrations of the tenth anniversary of the liberation of Czechoslovakia.
    Among those seeing them off at the airport were Chang Lifu, Deputy Secretary-General of the State Council, He Wei, Assistant Minister of Foreign Affairs, and Wang Tuo, Commissioner of the Protocol Department.
    Also at the airport to see them off were the Ambassador of the Czechoslovak Republic to China, Grigor, and embassy staff.
    The list of the Government Delegation of the People's Republic of China is as follows:
    Head of Delegation: Ulanhu, Vice Premier of the State Council
    Members: Shao Lizi, Member of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress
	 Wang Lin, Vice Minister of the Ministry of Fuel Industry
	 Cao Ying, Ambassador Extraordinary and Plenipotentiary of the People's Republic of China to the Czechoslovak Republic (already in Prague)

### 参加捷解放十周年庆祝典礼　我国政府代表团离京去布拉格

1955-05-01
第1版()
专栏：

　　参加捷解放十周年庆祝典礼
　　我国政府代表团离京去布拉格
    新华社三十日讯　应捷克斯洛伐克共和国政府的邀请前往参加捷克斯洛伐克解放十周年庆祝典礼的中华人民共和国政府代表团，由国务院副总理乌兰夫率领，在四月三十日乘飞机离京去布拉格。
    到机场送行的有：国务院副秘书长常黎夫，外交部部长助理何伟，礼宾司专员王拓等。
    到机场送行的还有捷克斯洛伐克共和国驻华大使格里哥尔和使馆人员，
    中华人民共和国政府代表团名单如下：
    团长　乌兰夫　国务院副总理
    团员　邵力子　全国人民代表大会常务委
          员会委员
          王　林　燃料工业部副部长
          曹　瑛　中华人民共和国驻捷克斯
                洛伐克共和国特命全权大使
                （已在布拉格）
