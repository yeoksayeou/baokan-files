Title: Draft Military and Political Agreement Between Egypt, Syria, and Saudi Arabia Completed

Author:

Date: 1955-05-21
Page: 4

　　Xinhua News Agency, Cairo, May 19th: According to newspaper reports, Egyptian Minister of National Guidance, Salah Salem, said on the 18th that the government had completed the final draft of the full text of the military and political agreement to be signed by Egypt, Syria, and Saudi Arabia.

　　Salah Salem emphasized that this agreement "is to protect Arab countries from foreign influence, which is increasingly strengthening in Iraq due to the signing of the Turkish-Iraqi pact."

### 埃及、叙利亚和沙特阿拉伯军事政治协定草案草拟完成

1955-05-21
第4版()
专栏：

　　埃及、叙利亚和沙特阿拉伯军事政治协定草案草拟完成
    新华社二十日讯　塔斯社开罗十九日讯：据报纸消息，埃及国家指导部长赛拉·萨勒姆十八日说，政府已经完成了埃及、叙利亚和沙特阿拉伯三国准备签订的军事政治协定全文最后草案的草拟工作。
    赛拉·萨勒姆着重指出，这个协定“是为了保护阿拉伯国家免受外国的影响，由于土耳其—伊拉克条约的签订，这种影响在伊拉克正日益加强。”
