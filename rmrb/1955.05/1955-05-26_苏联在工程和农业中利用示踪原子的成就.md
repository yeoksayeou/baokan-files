Title: Achievements of the Soviet Union in Utilizing Tracer Atoms in Engineering and Agriculture
Author:
Date: 1955-05-26
Page: 4
　　
　　Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, May 25th (TASS): Sokolov, Chief Engineer of the Central Laboratory for Physical Methods of Studying Materials in the Soviet Union, recently published an article in "Moscow Truth" describing the use of tracer atoms in engineering. He pointed out that research using tracer atoms makes it possible to "understand" the internal structure of matter. This principle is the basis for designing many types of labor-saving instruments and devices.
    The article stated that for a long time, in hydraulic engineering, it has been difficult to control the work of suction dredgers used to impound water-repellent weirs and dredge reservoirs and riverbeds. Now, a device called a gamma-ray soil measuring instrument has been designed, which can accurately measure the percentage of mud in the slurry. Using this instrument, the captain of the suction dredger can adjust the operation of his machine, increasing the machine's productivity by twenty-five percent. The industrial sector is already manufacturing this instrument for suction dredgers working on hydroelectric power station construction sites.
    This new instrument can also be used in coal mines, gold mines, and ore dressing equipment in the mining industry.
    Sokolov said that another special instrument using gamma rays can be used to determine the product quality of reinforced concrete factories. Using this instrument, it is possible to discover voids and other defects in concrete and reinforced concrete.
    Xinhua News Agency, Vladivostok, May 25th (TASS): The Far Eastern Branch of the Soviet Academy of Sciences, in studying the biological characteristics of soybeans, the main industrial raw material crop in the Soviet Primorsky region, used the tracer atom method and discovered the reason for soybean shattering when increasing the seed sowing rate. Scientifically speaking, this is a new discovery, because until now, this situation could not be explained due to the lack of direct research methods. The materials obtained are of great significance for determining the seed sowing rate and soybean sowing methods.

### 苏联在工程和农业中利用示踪原子的成就

1955-05-26
第4版()
专栏：

　　苏联在工程和农业中利用示踪原子的成就
　　新华社二十五日讯　据塔斯社莫斯科讯：苏联中央物理方法研究物质实验室总工程师索科洛夫，最近在“莫斯科真理报”上发表了一篇文章，叙述在工程中使用示踪原子的问题。他指出，使用示踪原子的研究使得“了解”物质的内部结构成为可能。这一原理是设计许多种减轻劳动的仪器和装置的依据。
    文章说，长久以来，在水力技术工程中，控制用来冲积拒水堰和疏浚水库和河床的吸泥机的工作，一直是很困难的。现在已经设计了一种叫做丙种射线泥土测量器，可以精确地测量泥浆中含泥的百分比。使用这种仪器，吸泥机的机长就能够调节他的机器的工作，可使机器的生产率提高百分之二十五。工业部门已经在为在水电站建筑工地工作的吸泥机制造这种仪器。
    这种新的仪器也能用在煤矿、金矿和采矿业的选矿设备上。
    索科洛夫说，利用丙种射线的另外一种特制的仪器可以用来测定钢筋混凝土工厂的产品质量。使用这种仪器能够发现混凝土和钢筋混凝土的空隙以及其他缺陷。
    新华社二十五日讯　据塔斯社海参崴讯：苏联科学院远东分院在研究苏联滨海地区的主要工业原料作物——大豆的生物特性时运用了示踪原子法，发现了在提高种子播种定额的情况下大豆脱粒的原因。在科学上说，这是一个新的发现，因为直到现在，由于缺乏直接的研究方法，这种情况无法加以说明。已经得到的材料对规定种子的播种定额和大豆的播种方法有很大作用。
