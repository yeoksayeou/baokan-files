Title: Soviet Union Uses Radioactive Isotopes to Treat Skin Diseases
Author:
Date: 1955-05-20
Page: 4

The hospitals of the Belorussian Soviet Socialist Republic have widely used radioactive isotopes to treat various skin diseases. According to a report in the "Medical Worker's Newspaper," the Belorussian Institute of Skin and Venereal Diseases has achieved good results using radioactive isotopes to treat psoriasis. The use of radioactive substances to treat cutaneous tuberculosis, eczema, cancerous diseases, etc., has also been effective.

The Institute has designed many different forms of devices that use radioactive substances to irradiate the affected parts of the skin for effective treatment. Sometimes, the method of administering isotopes internally to patients can also be used to treat skin diseases. At the same time, special devices manufactured by hospitals and scientific research institutions can also be used by doctors to observe the effects of radioactive substances on tissue cells when administered internally, thereby improving the therapeutic methods using radioactive substances.

### 苏联用放射性同位素治疗皮肤病

1955-05-20
第4版()
专栏：

　  苏联用放射性同位素治疗皮肤病
    白俄罗斯共和国的医院已广泛使用放射性同位素治疗各种皮肤病。据“医药工作者报”报道，白俄罗斯皮肤和性病研究所利用放射性同位素治疗鳞屑症已获得很好的效果。用放射性物质治疗皮肤性结核、湿疹、癌性病等也都起了有效的作用。
    研究所设计了许多不同形式的使用放射性物质的装置，用以照射皮肤的患病部分，进行有效的治疗。有时还可采用使病人内服同位素的方法，治疗皮肤病。同时，医院和科学研究机构制造的特制装置还可以供医生观察利用内服方法时放射性物质对组织细胞所产生的影响，从而改进利用放射性物质的治疗方法。
