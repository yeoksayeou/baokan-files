Title: Vice Premier Chen Yun Hosts Czechoslovak Government Delegation
Author:
Date: 1955-05-01
Page: 1

　　Xinhua News Agency, 30th. Vice Premier of the State Council of the People's Republic of China, Chen Yun, held a banquet on the evening of the 30th to host the Czechoslovak Republic Government Delegation.
　　Invited to the banquet were: the members of the Czechoslovak Republic Government Delegation, headed by the Vice Premier of the Czechoslovak Republic, Jankovcova; the Czechoslovak Ambassador Extraordinary and Plenipotentiary to China, Gregor, and embassy staff; and the Director of the Exhibition of Ten Years of Socialist Construction Achievements of Czechoslovakia, Pok, etc.
　　Attending the banquet were: Vice Premier of the State Council of the People's Republic of China, Li Xiannian; Vice Minister of Foreign Affairs, Zhang Wentian; Minister of Heavy Industry, Wang Heshou; Minister of Health, Li Dequan; Vice Ministers of Foreign Trade, Li Zheren and Fan Ziwen; Vice Minister of the First Ministry of Machine Building, Wang Daohan; Vice Minister of Culture, Zhang Zhixiang; Assistant Minister of Foreign Affairs, He Wei; Director of the Protocol Department, Ke Hua; and Chairman of the China Council for the Promotion of International Trade, Nan Hanchen, etc.
　　At the banquet, Vice Premier Chen Yun and Delegation Leader Jankovcova both gave speeches.
　　The banquet proceeded in a warm and friendly atmosphere.

### 陈云副总理招待捷政府代表团

1955-05-01
第1版()
专栏：

　　陈云副总理招待捷政府代表团
    新华社三十日讯　中华人民共和国国务院副总理陈云三十日晚间举行宴会，招待捷克斯洛伐克共和国政府代表团。
    应邀出席宴会的有：以捷克斯洛伐克共和国副总理扬科夫卓娃为首的捷克斯洛伐克共和国政府代表团人员，捷克斯洛伐克驻我国特命全权大使格里哥尔和使馆人员，捷克斯洛伐克十年社会主义建设成就展览会主任波克等。
    出席宴会的有：中华人民共和国国务院副总理李先念，外交部副部长张闻天，重工业部部长王鹤寿，卫生部部长李德全，对外贸易部副部长李哲人、范子文，第一机械工业部副部长汪道涵，文化部副部长张致祥，外交部部长助理何伟、礼宾司司长柯华，以及中国国际贸易促进委员会主席南汉宸等。
    宴会上，陈云副总理和扬科夫卓娃团长都讲了话。
    宴会在热烈友好的空气中进行。
