Title: Lüda Municipal Leading Organs and Sino-Soviet Friendship Association Host Banquet to See Off Soviet Troops
Author:
Date: 1955-05-15
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Lüda, May 14th: The CPC Lüda Municipal Committee, the Lüda Municipal People's Committee, and the Lüda Sino-Soviet Friendship Association hosted a banquet at 7:00 PM on May 14th to see off senior generals and officers of the Soviet Army Command stationed in the Lüshunkou area.

Invited to the banquet were senior generals and officers of the Soviet Army Command stationed in the Lüshunkou area, Soviet Consul General to Dalian Belyanov, and Soviet experts.

Present at the banquet were Guo Shushen, Secretary of the CPC Lüda Municipal Committee; Song Li, Mayor of Lüda; Qiao Chuanjue, President of the Lüda Sino-Soviet Friendship Association; senior commanders of the Chinese People's Liberation Army stationed in the Lüshunkou area; and leading cadres of the Party and government organs and people's organizations of Lüda.

More than two hundred people attended the banquet.

Guo Shushen, Secretary of the CPC Lüda Municipal Committee, spoke at the banquet. He first expressed his heartfelt gratitude and high respect to the Soviet Army stationed in the Lüshunkou area. He said: Now that you have successfully completed the glorious task entrusted to you by the Soviet government and the Soviet people, you are about to leave our country. We believe that our common ideals and common cause will forever unite us. Guo Shushen concluded by wishing the Soviet Army and the Soviet people brilliant new achievements in the cause of building a communist society and safeguarding human justice.

The representative of the Soviet Army Command in the Lüshunkou area also spoke at the banquet. He said: Today's banquet is another full manifestation of the sincere and pure friendship between the Chinese and Soviet people. For nearly ten years, the Soviet troops in China have felt the friendly relations shown by the Chinese people to the Soviet people at all times. He concluded by wishing the Chinese people great new achievements in the cause of building socialism, and wishing the commanders and fighters of the Chinese People's Liberation Army brilliant achievements in the task of defending the Lüshunkou naval base.

During the banquet, the hosts and guests repeatedly toasted the leaders of the Chinese and Soviet people and the great friendship between the Chinese and Soviet people.

At the banquet, the CPC Lüda Municipal Committee, the Lüda Municipal People's Committee, and the Lüda Sino-Soviet Friendship Association presented souvenirs to the senior generals and officers of the Soviet Army Command stationed in the Lüshunkou area.

### 旅大市领导机关和中苏友协设宴欢送苏军

1955-05-15
第1版()
专栏：

　　旅大市领导机关和中苏友协设宴欢送苏军
    新华社旅大十四日电　中共旅大市委员会、旅大市人民委员会和旅大市中苏友好协会，在十四日下午七时设宴欢送驻旅顺口地区苏军指挥部的高级将领和军官。
    应邀参加宴会的有驻旅顺口地区苏军指挥部的高级将领、军官和苏联驻大连总领事别良诺夫、苏联专家等。
    出席宴会的有中共旅大市委员会书记郭述申、旅大市市长宋黎、旅大市中苏友好协会会长乔传珏、中国人民解放军驻旅顺口地区的高级指挥员，以及旅大市党、政机关和人民团体的负责干部。
    出席宴会的共有二百多人。
    中共旅大市委员会书记郭述申在会上讲话。他首先向驻旅顺口地区苏军表示衷心感谢和崇高的敬意。他说：现在你们已胜利地完成了苏联政府和苏联人民所交给你们的光荣任务，就要离开我们的国家了，我们相信我们共同的思想和共同的事业将永远把我们团结在一起。郭述申最后祝苏军和苏联人民在建设共产主义社会和维护人类正义的事业中获得光辉的新成就。
    旅顺口地区苏军指挥部代表也在会上讲话。他说：今天的宴会是中苏两国人民之间真诚的纯洁的友谊的又一充分表现，苏军部队在中国将近十年的时间里，时时刻刻都感觉到中国人民对苏联人民所表现的友好关系。他最后祝中国人民在建设社会主义事业中获得巨大的新成就，并祝中国人民解放军的指挥员和战斗员在保卫旅顺口海军根据地的任务中获得辉煌成就。
    席间宾主不断地为中苏两国人民的领袖和中苏两国人民伟大的友谊干杯。
    在宴会上，中共旅大市委员会、旅大市人民委员会和旅大市中苏友好协会向驻旅顺口地区苏军指挥部的高级将领和军官赠送了纪念礼品。
