Title: Central Committee of the Communist Party of China Sends Congratulations on the 35th Anniversary of the Founding of the Communist Party of Indonesia
Author:
Date: 1955-05-21
Page: 1

　　The Central Committee of the Communist Party of China Sends Congratulations on the 35th Anniversary of the Founding of the Communist Party of Indonesia

　　This newspaper reports that the Central Committee of the Communist Party of China sent a telegram to the Central Committee of the Communist Party of Indonesia on the 19th, congratulating them on the 35th anniversary of the founding of the Communist Party of Indonesia. The telegram is as follows:
To the Central Committee of the Communist Party of Indonesia:
    On the occasion of the 35th anniversary of the founding of the Communist Party of Indonesia, the Central Committee of the Communist Party of China extends warm congratulations to the Communist Party of Indonesia. For thirty-five years, the Communist Party of Indonesia has persisted in a long and heroic struggle against foreign colonial rule and for the cause of national independence of the Indonesian people. We sincerely wish the Communist Party of Indonesia greater achievements in the struggle to safeguard national independence, oppose colonialism, defend peace, and defend democracy.
                                  The Central Committee of the Communist Party of China
                                  May 19, 1955

### 中国共产党中央委员会电贺印度尼西亚共产党成立三十五周年

1955-05-21
第1版()
专栏：

　　中国共产党中央委员会电贺印度尼西亚共产党成立三十五周年
    本报讯　中国共产党中央委员会十九日致电印度尼西亚共产党中央委员会，祝贺印度尼西亚共产党成立三十五周年。电文如下：印度尼西亚共产党中央委员会：
    值此印度尼西亚共产党成立三十五周年之际，中国共产党中央委员会谨向印度尼西亚共产党致以热烈的祝贺。三十五年来，印度尼西亚共产党为了反对外国的殖民统治，为了印度尼西亚人民的民族独立事业，坚持了长期的英勇的斗争。我们谨祝印度尼西亚共产党在为维护民族独立、反对殖民主义、保卫和平、保卫民主的斗争中获得更大的成就。
                                  中国共产党中央委员会
                                  一九五五年五月十九日
