Title: Lebanese Prime Minister Announces Lebanon Will Not Join the Turkish-Iraqi Pact
Author:
Date: 1955-05-22
Page: 4
　
　　Xinhua News Agency, Beirut, May 20th, TASS reported: Lebanon's "Telegraph" reported that Lebanese Prime Minister Sami al-Solh officially announced that Lebanon will not join the Turkish-Iraqi military pact. This statement by the Lebanese government leader was welcomed by representatives of various political parties and groups.

### 黎巴嫩总理宣布黎巴嫩不参加土伊条约

1955-05-22
第4版()
专栏：

　　黎巴嫩总理宣布黎巴嫩不参加土伊条约
    新华社二十一日讯　塔斯社贝鲁特二十日讯：黎巴嫩“电讯报”报道：黎巴嫩总理萨米·索勒哈正式宣布：黎巴嫩将不参加土耳其—伊拉克军事条约。黎巴嫩政府领袖的这项声明受到了各政党和团体代表的欢迎。
