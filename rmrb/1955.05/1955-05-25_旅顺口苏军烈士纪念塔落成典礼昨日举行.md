Title: Dedication Ceremony of the Soviet Army Martyrs Memorial Tower in Lüshunkou Held Yesterday
Author:
Date: 1955-05-25
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Lüda, May 24th. The dedication ceremony of the Soviet Army Martyrs Memorial Tower, built by the Soviet Army stationed in the Lüshunkou area, was held on the afternoon of the 24th.

　　The Soviet Army Martyrs Memorial Tower was built in the center of the Soviet Army Martyrs Cemetery in Lüshun.

　　Shvetsov, Chairman of the Soviet Military Delegation of the Sino-Soviet Joint Military Commission, Deputy Chairman Turandaev, and member Kudryavtsev; Deng Hua, Acting Chairman of the Chinese Military Delegation, and members Liu Yalou and Zeng Shaoshan, attended the dedication ceremony.

　　Belyanov, representative of the command of the Soviet Army stationed in the Lüshunkou area and Soviet Consul General in Dalian, also attended the dedication ceremony.

　　Also attending the ceremony were Gan Siqi, Deputy Director of the General Political Department of the Chinese People's Liberation Army, representatives of the Chinese People's Liberation Army stationed in the Lüshunkou area, Guo Shushen, Secretary of the CPC Lüda Municipal Committee, Hu Zhonghai, Deputy Secretary, Song Li, Mayor of Lüda City, and Qiao Chuanjue, President of the Lüda Sino-Soviet Friendship Association. A total of more than 500 people attended the ceremony.

　　After the dedication ceremony began, General Shvetsov spoke first. He said that according to the decision of the Soviet government, the Soviet Army Martyrs Memorial Tower, to commemorate the Soviet Army martyrs who heroically sacrificed themselves far away from their motherland for the great common interests of the Chinese and Soviet people, has now been completed. The blood of the Soviet people was not shed in vain in the Great Patriotic War. Because of the victory over fascism and the victory of the Chinese revolution, the flags of freedom and peace have been raised over many countries in Europe and Asia. Let the Soviet Army Martyrs Memorial Tower, which is being inaugurated today, become a permanent symbol of the Soviet soldiers' loyal fulfillment of their great internationalist duties and a symbol of the unbreakable friendship between the Chinese and Soviet people.

　　General Liu Yalou then spoke. First, on behalf of the Chinese Military Delegation of the Sino-Soviet Joint Military Commission and all commanders and fighters of the Chinese People's Liberation Army stationed in the Lüshunkou area, he expressed his deep condolences to the Soviet Army martyrs and his cordial greetings to the families of the martyrs. He said that ten years ago, the Soviet army helped us defeat the Japanese aggressors and liberate the people of our Northeast region. For the past ten years, the Soviet army has helped us guard the Lüshunkou naval base, which has played a huge role in defending peace in the Far East and our socialist construction. The Chinese people will never forget the great achievements of the Soviet army. We must always learn from the great internationalist spirit of the Soviet army.

　　Soviet Consul General in Dalian Belyanov and Hu Zhonghai, Deputy Secretary of the CPC Lüda Municipal Committee, also spoke at the meeting.

　　After the speeches, representatives of the Soviet Army and representatives from all walks of life in Lüda laid wreaths at the Soviet Army Martyrs Memorial Tower.

　　The meeting ended after the national anthems of the Soviet Union and China were played.

### 旅顺口苏军烈士纪念塔落成典礼昨日举行

1955-05-25
第1版()
专栏：

　　旅顺口苏军烈士纪念塔落成典礼昨日举行
    新华社旅大二十四日电　驻旅顺口地区苏军修建的苏军烈士纪念塔在二十四日下午举行落成典礼。
    苏军烈士纪念塔修建在旅顺苏军烈士墓地的中央。
    中苏联合军事委员会的苏联军事代表团主席什维佐夫，副主席图兰大也夫，委员库德良夫采夫；中国军事代表团代理主席邓华，委员刘亚楼、曾绍山等参加了落成典礼。
    驻旅顺口地区苏军指挥部代表、苏联驻大连总领事别良诺夫也参加了落成典礼。
    参加典礼的还有中国人民解放军总政治部副主任甘泗淇，中国人民解放军驻旅顺口地区部队代表、中共旅大市委员会书记郭述申，副书记胡忠海，旅大市市长宋黎，旅大市中苏友好协会会长乔传珏。参加典礼的共有五百多人。
    落成典礼开始后，什维佐夫将军首先讲话。他说，根据苏联政府的决定，为纪念远离祖国为中苏两国人民伟大的共同利益而英勇牺牲的苏军烈士纪念塔，现在已经建成了。在伟大的卫国战争中，苏联人民的鲜血是没有白流的。由于战胜了法西斯，由于中国革命的胜利，在欧亚两洲许多国家的上空都悬起了自由与和平的旗帜。让今天落成的苏军烈士纪念塔成为苏联军人忠诚地完成自己伟大的国际主义天职的永久标志，成为中苏两国人民间牢不可破的友谊的象征。
    刘亚楼将军接着讲话。他首先代表中苏联合军事委员会中国军事代表团和中国人民解放军驻旅顺口地区部队全体指战员，向苏军烈士们致深切的悼念，向烈士们的家属致以亲切的慰问。他说，十年前，苏联军队帮助我们战胜了日本侵略者，解放了我们东北地区的人民。十年以来，苏联军队帮助我们守卫了旅顺口海军根据地，为保卫远东和平和我国社会主义建设事业起了巨大的作用。苏联军队的这种丰功伟迹，中国人民将永志不忘。我们要永远学习苏联军队的伟大的国际主义精神。
    在会上讲话的还有苏联驻大连总领事别良诺夫和中共旅大市委员会副书记胡忠海。
    讲话毕，苏军代表和旅大市各界人民代表向苏军烈士纪念塔敬献花圈。
    大会在奏苏中两国国歌后结束。
