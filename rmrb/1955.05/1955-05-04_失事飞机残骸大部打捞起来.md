Title: Most of the Wreckage of the Crashed Plane Salvaged
Author:
Date: 1955-05-04
Page: 4

　　Xinhua News Agency, New Delhi, May 3rd. According to a report by the Indian Press Trust on the 2nd, most of the wreckage of the Air India International's "Kashmir Princess" plane, which crashed due to sabotage by American-Chiang Kai-shek agents, has been salvaged. The entire salvage operation is expected to be completed within a few days.
    The message stated that the body of one of the victims salvaged has been identified as Indian senior pilot Captain D. K. Jatar.
    After the news of Jatar's death was confirmed, Yue Daiheng, Consul of the People's Republic of China in Mumbai, and accompanying Consul Ma Longde visited Jatar's family to express their condolences and sympathy.

### 失事飞机残骸大部打捞起来

1955-05-04
第4版()
专栏：

　　失事飞机残骸大部打捞起来
    新华社新德里三日电　据印度报业托辣斯二日报道，因遭美蒋特务破坏而失事的印度国际航空公司的“克什米尔公主号”飞机残骸的大部分已经打捞起来。打捞飞机残骸的全部工作几天内即可完成。
    消息说，打捞起来的一名遇难者尸体已经辨明是印度高级驾驶员德·克·贾塔尔上尉。
    中华人民共和国驻孟买领事岳岱衡和随习领事马隆德在贾塔尔遇难的消息得到证实后，曾访问贾塔尔的家属，表示吊唁和慰问。
