Title: Czechoslovak Red Cross Delegation Leaves Beijing for Home

Author: 
Date: 1955-05-22
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, May 21st -- The Czechoslovak Red Cross delegation, led by Eduard Tuma, Chairman of the Czechoslovak Red Cross, which came to China at the invitation of the Chinese Red Cross to visit and attend the "May Day" International Labor Day celebrations, left Beijing for home on the 21st.

Those who went to the airport to see them off included Li Dequan, President of the Chinese Red Cross Society, Cui Yitian, Vice Minister of Public Health, and responsible cadres of the Chinese Red Cross Society, the Chinese Medical Association, and the Chinese Nurses Association.

Grigor, Ambassador of the Czechoslovak Republic to China, also went to the airport to see them off.

Before leaving Beijing, the Czechoslovak Red Cross delegation visited Wuhan, Guangzhou, Hangzhou, and Shanghai.

### 捷红十字会代表团离京回国

1955-05-22
第1版()
专栏：

　　捷红十字会代表团离京回国
    新华社二十一日讯　应中国红十字会邀请前来我国访问、参加“五一”国际劳动节观礼的捷克斯洛伐克红十字会代表团，在团长捷克斯洛伐克红十字会主席爱德华·图马率领下，于二十一日离京返国。
    前往机场欢送的有中国红十字会总会会长李德全，卫生部副部长崔义田，中国红十字会、中华医学会和中华护士学会负责干部等多人。
    前往机场欢送的还有捷克斯洛伐克共和国驻我国大使格里哥尔。
    捷克斯洛伐克红十字会代表团离京前曾赴武汉、广州、杭州和上海等地访问。
