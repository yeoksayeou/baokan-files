Title: British Parliament Dissolved, Campaign Begins
Author:
Date: 1955-05-09
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, London dispatch from TASS: The British Parliament's two houses read the Queen's speech on the end of the current parliament on the 6th. This speech, written for the Queen by the government, summarized the government's work in the current parliament.

After the dissolution of Parliament, a three-week campaign officially began, followed by parliamentary elections in Great Britain and Northern Ireland on May 26th. The House of Commons will elect six hundred and thirty members; the House of Lords will not hold elections. The Conservative and Labour parties have already nominated their candidates in each constituency. The Communist Party has nominated sixteen candidates, and the Liberal Party has nominated approximately one hundred candidates. There are approximately thirty-five million voters.

### 英国议会解散，竞选开始

1955-05-09
第4版()
专栏：

　　英国议会解散，竞选开始
    新华社七日讯　塔斯社伦敦讯：英国议会两院在六日宣读了女王就本届议会的结束发表的演说。这篇演说是政府替女王写成的，概述了政府在本届议会的工作。
    在议会解散以后，就正式展开为期三周的竞选运动，然后在五月二十六日在大不列颠和北爱尔兰举行议会选举。下院将选出六百三十个议员，上院不进行选举。保守党和工党在各选区已经提出了自己的候选人。共产党提出了十六个候选人，自由党大约提出了一百个候选人。选民约有三千五百万人。
