Title: Yugoslav Chargé d'Affaires Milicevic calls on Foreign Minister Zhou Enlai to present his credentials
Author:
Date: 1955-05-24
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, 23rd. Yugoslav Federal People's Republic's Chargé d'Affaires to the People's Republic of China, Milicevic, called on the Minister of Foreign Affairs of the People's Republic of China, Zhou Enlai, on the afternoon of the 23rd to present his credentials. Present at the meeting were: Ke Hua, Director of the Protocol Department of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs, and Liu Jingyu, Deputy Director of the Department of Soviet and Eastern European Affairs.

### 南斯拉夫临时代办米里切维奇　拜会周恩来外长递交介绍书

1955-05-24
第1版()
专栏：

　　南斯拉夫临时代办米里切维奇
  　拜会周恩来外长递交介绍书
    新华社二十三日讯　南斯拉夫联邦人民共和国驻中华人民共和国大使馆临时代办米里切维奇在二十三日下午拜会中华人民共和国外交部长周恩来，递交其介绍书。在座的有：外交部礼宾司司长柯华，苏联东欧司副司长刘靖宇。
