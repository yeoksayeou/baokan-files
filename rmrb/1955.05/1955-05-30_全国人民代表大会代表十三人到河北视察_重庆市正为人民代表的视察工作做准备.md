Title: Thirteen National People's Congress Deputies Inspect Hebei, Chongqing Prepares for NPC Deputies' Inspection

Author:

Date: 1955-05-30
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Baoding, May 27th: Thirteen National People's Congress deputies who came to Hebei Province for inspection have arrived in Baoding. On the morning of the 26th, the deputies held a symposium with the Governor and Deputy Governors of Hebei Province, as well as the heads of relevant provincial departments. Lin Tie, Governor of Hebei Province, and Yan Dakai, Deputy Governor, introduced the situation of grain unified purchase and sales, agricultural production mutual aid and cooperation, and social security work in Hebei Province. The deputies are preparing to go to Tangshan, Shijiazhuang, Zhangjiakou and other regions for inspection.

Some other deputies who are going to Hebei Province for inspection have already gone directly to the inspection areas from their places of residence.

Xinhua News Agency, Chongqing, May 29th: The Chongqing Municipal National People's Congress Deputies Office was officially established on the 28th. The office is now preparing for the inspection work of the National People's Congress deputies.

According to the notice of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress, the National People's Congress deputies in Chongqing have decided to focus on inspecting the city's unified grain sales, urban construction, and public security work in June. The inspectors are divided into two groups, one of which will also go to the neighboring special zone to inspect rural grain purchase work.

### 全国人民代表大会代表十三人到河北视察  重庆市正为人民代表的视察工作做准备

1955-05-30
第1版()
专栏：

　　全国人民代表大会代表十三人到河北视察
　  重庆市正为人民代表的视察工作做准备
    新华社保定二十七日电　到河北省视察工作的全国人民代表大会代表，有十三人到达保定。二十六日上午，代表们同河北省省长、副省长及省级各有关部门负责人举行了座谈会，河北省省长林铁和副省长阎达开在会上介绍了河北省的粮食统购统销、农业生产互助合作和社会治安等工作情况。代表们准备分赴唐山、石家庄、张家口等地区视察。
    还有一些赴河北省视察工作的代表们，已从本人驻地直接赴视察地区视察。
    新华社重庆二十九日电　重庆市全国人民代表大会代表办事处在二十八日正式成立。办事处现正为全国人民代表大会代表的视察工作做准备。
    重庆市的全国人民代表大会代表根据全国人民代表大会常务委员会的通知，确定在六月份内有重点地视察本市的粮食统销、城市建设和公安工作等方面的情况。视察的人共分两组，其中一组还将到邻近的专区去视察农村粮食统购工作。
