    Textile workers are working hard to produce more affordable and quality textiles. This is Wang Meigong (second from the left), a national model worker of the textile industry and assistant in the roving workshop of Jinan Renfeng Textile and Dyeing Factory, guiding workers to learn a new knotting method.
