Title: Hungarian Embassy in China Holds Press Conference to Introduce the Upcoming Hungarian Textile Testing Instrument Exhibition in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1955-05-20
Page: 1
　　
　　Hungarian Embassy in China Holds Press Conference
　　Introducing the Upcoming Hungarian Textile Testing Instrument Exhibition in Beijing

    Xinhua News Agency, 19th. The Hungarian Embassy in China held a press conference on the afternoon of the 19th for the Hungarian Textile Testing Instrument Exhibition which is about to open in Beijing.

    At the conference, the head of the Hungarian Textile Testing Instrument Exhibition Delegation, and Director of the Hungarian Textile Research Institute, Mr. Foldes, gave a brief introduction on the preparation process of the exhibition in Beijing, the situation of the exhibits, and the manufacturing and research work of Hungarian textile instruments.

    This exhibition is located in the Long Corridor of the Temple of Heaven in Beijing. It is divided into seven laboratories with two hundred and twenty kinds of exhibits, all of which are new-style machines and instruments related to the textile industry.

    Mr. Foldes and others led the reporters to visit each laboratory of the exhibition.

### 匈牙利驻我国大使馆举行记者招待会　介绍即将在北京开幕的匈牙利纺织试验仪器展览会

1955-05-20
第1版()
专栏：

　　匈牙利驻我国大使馆举行记者招待会
　　介绍即将在北京开幕的匈牙利纺织试验仪器展览会
    新华社十九日讯　匈牙利驻我国大使馆在十九日下午为即将在京开幕的匈牙利纺织试验仪器展览会举行记者招待会。
    会上，匈牙利纺织试验仪器展览会代表团团长、匈牙利纺织研究院院长弗尔德士对展览会在京筹备经过、展品情况、匈牙利纺织仪器的制造和研究工作状况都作了扼要介绍。
    这个展览会设在北京天坛长廊，共分七个实验室，有展品二百二十种，都是和纺织工业有关的新式的机械和仪器等。
    弗尔德士等引导记者们参观了展览会各个实验室。
