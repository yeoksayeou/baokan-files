Title: German Democratic Republic Council of Ministers Holds Meeting Approving the Treaty of Friendship, Cooperation and Mutual Assistance Signed in Warsaw
Author:
Date: 1955-05-19
Page: 4

　　German Democratic Republic Council of Ministers Holds Meeting
　　Approving the Treaty of Friendship, Cooperation and Mutual Assistance Signed in Warsaw
    Xinhua News Agency, Berlin, 18th. (TASS dispatch from Berlin, 17th) According to a press communiqué from the Prime Minister's Office of the German Democratic Republic, the Council of Ministers of the German Democratic Republic held a meeting on the 16th. Prime Minister Grotewohl gave a report at the meeting on the course and results of the Warsaw Conference. He analyzed in detail the content of the Treaty of Friendship, Cooperation and Mutual Assistance concluded in Warsaw, and pointed out the great significance of this treaty for peace, for further strengthening the friendship and cooperation between peace-loving peoples of all countries, and for guaranteeing the security of these countries.
    The Council of Ministers unanimously agreed to approve the negotiations and statements made by the delegation of the Government of the German Democratic Republic at the Warsaw Conference, to approve the German Democratic Republic's participation in the Treaty of Friendship, Cooperation and Mutual Assistance signed on May 14, 1955, and decided to submit this treaty to the People's Chamber of the German Democratic Republic with a recommendation for its ratification.

### 民主德国部长会议举行会议　批准在华沙签订的友好合作互助条约

1955-05-19
第4版()
专栏：

　　民主德国部长会议举行会议
　　批准在华沙签订的友好合作互助条约
    新华社十八日讯　据塔斯社柏林十七日讯：据德意志民主共和国总理新闻处公报，德意志民主共和国部长会议十六日举行会议。格罗提渥总理在会上作了关于华沙会议的经过和成果的报告。他详细分析了在华沙缔结的友好合作互助条约的内容，并指出这个条约对和平、对进一步加强爱好和平的各国人民之间的友谊和合作以及对保障这些国家的安全的重大意义。
    部长会议一致同意，批准德意志民主共和国政府代表团在华沙会议上的谈判和声明，批准德意志民主共和国参加一九五五年五月十四日签订的友好合作互助条约，并且决定把这个条约提交德意志民主共和国人民议院，建议加以批准。
