    Our Great Motherland
    Farmers in Xixia Village, Jingxi Township, Minhou County, Fujian Province, are actively taking the path of collectivization. The Xixia Agricultural Production Cooperative often receives help from workers in nearby factories, improving production techniques. The workers are installing water pumps for this cooperative to combat drought and provide irrigation.

### 我们伟大的祖国（图片）

1955-05-27
第1版()
专栏：

    我们伟大的祖国
    福建省闽侯县荆溪乡溪下村农民积极走合作化道路。溪下农业生产合作社经常得到附近工厂工人的帮助，改进了生产技术。工人们正为这个社安装抽水机，以便进行抗旱和灌溉。
