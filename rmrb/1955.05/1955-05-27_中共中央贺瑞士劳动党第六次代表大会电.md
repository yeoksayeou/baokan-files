Title: CPC Central Committee Sends Congratulations to the Sixth Congress of the Swiss Party of Labor

Author:

Date: 1955-05-27
Page: 1

CPC Central Committee's Telegram of Congratulations to the Sixth Congress of the Swiss Party of Labor

To the Central Committee of the Swiss Party of Labor and to all comrades of the Sixth Congress of the Swiss Party of Labor:

    The Central Committee of the Communist Party of China extends its heartfelt congratulations to the Sixth Congress of the Swiss Party of Labor. Congratulations on your new achievements in leading the Swiss working class and uniting all democratic forces in the struggle to defend European and world peace and to protect the interests of the Swiss working people. We wish the congress success.

                                 The Central Committee of the Communist Party of China
　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　 May 26, 1955

### 中共中央贺瑞士劳动党第六次代表大会电

1955-05-27
第1版()
专栏：

　　中共中央贺瑞士劳动党第六次代表大会电瑞士劳动党中央委员会并转瑞士劳动党第六次代表大会全体同志们：
    中国共产党中央委员会谨向瑞士劳动党第六次代表大会致以衷心的祝贺。祝贺你们在领导瑞士工人阶级并团结一切民主力量，为保卫欧洲及世界和平、为保护瑞士劳动人民的利益的斗争中获得新的成就。祝贺大会获得成功。
                                 中国共产党中央委员会
　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　 一九五五年五月二十六日
