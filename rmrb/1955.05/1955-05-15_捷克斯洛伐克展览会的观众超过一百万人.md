Title: Czechoslovakian Exhibition Exceeds One Million Visitors
Author:
Date: 1955-05-15
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, 12th. By the 11th, the number of visitors to the Exhibition of Achievements in Ten Years of Socialist Construction in Czechoslovakia, held in the capital, had exceeded one million.

　　After the leaders of the Chinese Communist Party, headed by Mao Zedong, visited the exhibition, Vice Premier of the State Council Chen Yun and more than 2,100 responsible persons, engineers, and technicians from relevant departments of the Chinese government made a full-day visit on the 7th. Czechoslovak experts guided the tour and answered various questions.

　　A total of 399 units, including many visiting units, factories and mines that use Czechoslovak machines, and scientific and cultural organizations, raised many specialized questions to the experts at the exhibition. To this end, the experts held seminars and reports, and went to some units to provide specific guidance or answer questions by letter. By the 8th, the problems of 243 units had been resolved. Many factories and mining enterprises using Czechoslovak machines learned valuable experience and mastered the performance of Czechoslovak machines. During this period, the Czechoslovak experts at the exhibition also went to Tianjin, Harbin, and other places to exchange experiences and provide specific guidance according to the requirements of relevant units. Starting on the 10th, the Beijing Railway Administration organized more than 60 people from seven units in Zhengzhou, Harbin, relevant departments in Beijing, and other places to invite Czechoslovak experts to give a series of ten-day special reports.

　　To meet the needs of the vast number of visitors, the exhibition will be extended for ten days and will close on May 25th. Professional visits will be organized from the 21st to the 25th.

### 捷克斯洛伐克展览会的观众超过一百万人

1955-05-15
第1版()
专栏：

　　捷克斯洛伐克展览会的观众超过一百万人
    新华社十二日讯　到十一日止，参观在首都举办的捷克斯洛伐克十年社会主义建设成就展览会的观众已超过一百万人。
    在以毛泽东为首的中国共产党领导者参观了展览会之后，国务院副总理陈云和我国政府各有关部门负责人、工程师和技术人员共二千一百多人在七日作了整天的参观，参观时，由捷克斯洛伐克专家引导并解答各项问题。
    很多参观单位和使用捷克斯洛伐克机器的厂矿企业以及科学文化团体共三百九十九个单位向展览会的专家们提出了许多专门性的问题。专家们为此曾分别举行座谈会、报告会，并到有些单位去作具体指导或者用书信答复，至八日止，已有二百四十三个单位的问题得到解决。许多使用捷克斯洛伐克机器的厂矿企业由此学得了宝贵的经验，掌握了捷克斯洛伐克机器的性能。在这一期间，展览会的捷克斯洛伐克专家们还根据各有关单位的要求，前往天津和哈尔滨等地交流经验和进行具体指导。北京铁路管理局从十日起组织郑州、哈尔滨等地和北京市有关部门共七个单位的六十多人，邀请捷克斯洛伐克专家作连续十天的专题报告。
    为满足广大观众的要求，展览会将延期十天到五月二十五日闭幕。自二十一日到二十五日将组织专业性的参观。
