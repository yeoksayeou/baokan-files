Title: Prime Minister Sastroamidjojo Leaves Beijing to Visit Northeast China
Author:
Date: 1955-05-31
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, May 30th. Indonesian Prime Minister Ali Sastroamidjojo and his wife left Beijing by plane at 8:00 a.m. on the 30th to visit various places in Northeast China.
Accompanying Prime Minister Ali Sastroamidjojo on his visit to Northeast China were: Indonesian Ambassador to China Mononutu, Director of the Asia-Pacific Department of the Indonesian Ministry of Foreign Affairs Sukardjo Wirjopranoto and his wife, Indonesian Ambassador to India Bala, Indonesian Ambassador to the Soviet Union Subandrio, Director of the Indonesian National Planning Bureau Djuanda, and senior official of the Foreign Economic Relations Department of the Indonesian Ministry of Economy Hasuno Reksoamojo, among others.
Huang Zhen, Ambassador of the People's Republic of China to Indonesia, and his wife accompanied them.
Those who saw them off at the airport included Zhang Hanfu, Vice Minister of Foreign Affairs of the People's Republic of China, Chen Jiakang, Assistant Minister, Dong Yueqian, Director of the General Office, Wang Zhuoru, Deputy Director of the Protocol Department, and officials from the Indonesian Embassy in China, among others.

### 沙斯特罗阿米佐约总理离北京去东北参观

1955-05-31
第1版()
专栏：

　　沙斯特罗阿米佐约总理离北京去东北参观
    新华社三十日讯　印度尼西亚共和国总理阿里·沙斯特罗阿米佐约和夫人在三十日上午八时乘飞机离开北京去东北各地参观。
    随同阿里·沙斯特罗阿米佐约总理前往东北参观的有：印度尼西亚驻华大使莫诺努图，印度尼西亚外交部亚洲—太平洋司司长苏卡佐·维约普拉诺托和夫人，印度尼西亚驻印度大使巴拉，印度尼西亚驻苏联大使苏班德里奥，印度尼西亚国家计划局局长朱安达，印度尼西亚经济部对外经济关系司高级官员哈苏诺·雷克梭阿莫佐等人。
    中华人民共和国驻印度尼西亚大使黄镇和夫人、外交部礼宾司司长柯华陪同前往。
    到机场欢送的，有中华人民共和国外交部副部长章汉夫、部长助理陈家康、办公厅主任董越千、礼宾司副司长王倬如和印度尼西亚驻华大使馆官员等多人。
