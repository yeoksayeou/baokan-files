Title: Ngo Dinh Diem Clique's Relations with French Military Authorities Deteriorate
Author:
Date: 1955-05-12
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Hanoi, 8th - According to Saigon sources, the U.S.-backed Ngo Dinh Diem clique's relations with the French military authorities are deteriorating daily.

    On the evening of the 6th, the Ngo Dinh Diem authorities accused the French military of using ambulances to help the Binh Xuyen faction transfer weapons from Saigon to the surrounding countryside. The French High Commissioner to Indochina and Commander-in-Chief of the French Expeditionary Forces in Indochina, General Ely, immediately issued a public statement on the 7th, accusing the Ngo Dinh Diem authorities of distorting facts and making false accusations.

    Prior to this, on the 4th, General Ely had issued a statement accusing "a strong and constant propaganda campaign of inciting hatred towards France."

### 吴庭艳集团同法军当局关系恶化

1955-05-12
第4版()
专栏：

    吴庭艳集团同法军当局关系恶化
    新华社河内八日电　据西贡消息，美国所支持的吴庭艳集团同法军当局的关系日益恶化。
    六日晚，吴庭艳当局指责法军以救护车帮助平川派把武器从西贡转运到西贡周围的乡村。法国驻印度支那高级专员兼印度支那法国远征军总司令埃利在七日立即发表公报，指责吴庭艳当局歪曲事实和诬告。
    在这以前，埃利在四日曾经发表声明，指责“一个强烈的、经常进行的宣传运动一直在煽动对法国的仇恨”。
