Title: Premier Zhou Enlai Receives Belgian Youth Delegation
Author:
Date: 1955-05-23
Page: 1

　　Xinhua News Agency, May 22nd: Premier Zhou Enlai of the State Council of the People's Republic of China received the Belgian Youth Delegation on the afternoon of May 22nd, who came to China for a visit at the invitation of the All-China Federation of Democratic Youth. The delegation includes the leaders of the Belgian People's Youth League, the Children's League, the Socialist Youth Organization, the Catholic Youth Organization, and the Christian Youth Organization.

　　Present at the reception were Liao Chengzhi, Chairman of the All-China Federation of Democratic Youth, Vice-Chairmen Wu Han and Qu Tangliang, Shi Ruzhang, Executive Secretary of the National Association of the Young Women's Christian Association of China, and Reverend Zhao Fusan, Vice-Dean of Studies of Yenching Union Theological Seminary.

### 周恩来总理接见比利时青年代表团

1955-05-23
第1版()
专栏：

　　周恩来总理接见比利时青年代表团
    新华社二十二日讯　中华人民共和国国务院总理周恩来在二十二日下午接见了应中华全国民主青年联合会邀请来我国访问的比利时青年代表团。代表团包括比利时人民青年联盟、少年联盟、社会党青年组织、天主教青年组织和基督教青年组织的负责人。
    接见时在座的有中华全国民主青年联合会主席廖承志，副主席吴晗、区棠亮，中华基督教女青年会全国协会干事施如璋，燕京协和神学院副教务长赵复三牧师等。
