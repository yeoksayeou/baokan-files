Title: Hatoyama Again Talks About Sino-Japanese Trade Issues
Author:
Date: 1955-05-16
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Tokyo, May 15th: Japanese Prime Minister Ichiro Hatoyama, in response to questioning in the House of Representatives Foreign Affairs Committee on the 14th, said that Japan and China "could consider exchanging consuls."

Regarding the issue of the Bank of Japan and the People's Bank of China concluding a direct settlement payment agreement, Hatoyama said he was willing to find ways to cooperate. He also said that the Japanese government may conclude an official trade agreement with the Chinese government in the future.

### 鸠山又谈中日贸易问题

1955-05-16
第4版()
专栏：

　　鸠山又谈中日贸易问题
    新华社十五日讯  东京消息：日本首相鸠山一郎十四日在众议院外务委员会上答复质询时说：日本和中国“互派领事是可以考虑的”。
    关于日本银行和中国人民银行缔结直接结账的支付协定的问题，鸠山说，他愿设法进行合作。他又说：日本政府将来可能同中国政府缔结官方的贸易协定。
