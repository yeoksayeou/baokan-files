Title: Standing Committee of the National People's Congress Holds Meeting; Resolution to Convene the Second Session of the National People's Congress

Author:

Date: 1955-05-19
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, 18th. The Standing Committee of the National People's Congress held its 16th meeting on May 18th.

    The meeting passed a resolution to convene the Second Session of the First National People's Congress in July. The meeting also decided to notify the deputies of the National People's Congress to take time before June 20th to conduct inspections in the areas of their original electoral units, their places of origin, or other areas.

    The meeting also decided on other matters.

### 全国人民代表大会常务委员会举行会议　决议召集全国人民代表大会第二次会议

1955-05-19
第1版()
专栏：

　　全国人民代表大会常务委员会举行会议
　　决议召集全国人民代表大会第二次会议
    新华社十八日讯　全国人民代表大会常务委员会在五月十八日举行第十六次会议。
    会议通过决议，准备在七月间召集第一届全国人民代表大会第二次会议。会议并决定通知全国人民代表大会代表，在六月二十日以前，抽出时间到原选举单位的地区、原籍或其他地区进行视察。
    会议还决定了其他事项。
