Title: Indian Government Considering Report on Plane Crash

Author:

Date: 1955-05-29
Page: 1

　　Indian Government Considering Report on Plane Crash

Xinhua News Agency, New Delhi, May 28th: The United Press of India reported from Calcutta on May 28th that the Deputy Minister of External Affairs of India, Syed Mahmud, said in Calcutta on May 28th that the Indian government is "considering" the report on the crash of Air India International's "Kashmir Princess" aircraft.

He said that the Indian government has consulted with the Hong Kong authorities and will consult with Indonesia, Burma, and China before deciding on the next steps.

### 印度政府正考虑关于飞机失事事件的报告

1955-05-29
第1版()
专栏：

　　印度政府正考虑关于飞机失事事件的报告
    新华社新德里二十八日电　印度统一通讯社二十八日发自加尔各答的消息说，印度外交部副部长赛义德·马茂德二十八日在加尔各答说，印度政府“正在考虑”关于印度国际航空公司的“克什米尔公主号”飞机失事事件的报告。
    他说，印度政府已经和香港当局进行了磋商，并将同印度尼西亚、缅甸和中国进行磋商，然后再决定下一步骤。
