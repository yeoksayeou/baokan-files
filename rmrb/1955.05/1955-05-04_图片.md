Title: Photo
Author: Gao Liang
Date: 1955-05-04
Page: 2

    The "Liberate Taiwan Exhibition" opened on May 2nd at the Beijing Working People's Cultural Palace. The picture shows visitors viewing the second part of the exhibition's exhibits. On the left of the picture is a map originally published in the American magazine "Collier's." This map illustrates that American imperialism is using Taiwan as a springboard for aggression against our country. 本报记者
    Our reporter Gao Liang took the picture.
