Title: Vice Premier Peng Dehuai Visits the Soviet Union at Invitation
Author:
Date: 1955-05-27
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, May 25th (TASS): After attending the Warsaw Conference of European nations to guarantee European peace and security as an observer, Peng Dehuai, Vice Premier and Minister of National Defense of the People's Republic of China, has arrived in Moscow from Warsaw.

At the invitation of the Soviet government, Peng Dehuai will stay in the Soviet Union for a few days as a guest of the Soviet government.

On the 23rd, Peng Dehuai called on Khrushchev, First Secretary of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of the Soviet Union, and Zhukov, Minister of Defense of the Soviet Union, and held talks with them.

Peng Dehuai visited places of interest in Moscow.

Peng Dehuai and his entourage have left for Leningrad for a few days of visits.

### 彭德怀副总理应邀在苏联进行访问

1955-05-27
第1版()
专栏：

　　彭德怀副总理应邀在苏联进行访问
    新华社二十六日讯　塔斯社莫斯科二十五日电：中华人民共和国国务院副总理兼国防部长彭德怀以观察员身份参加了欧洲国家保障欧洲和平和安全的华沙会议之后，已由华沙来到了莫斯科。
    彭德怀应苏联政府的邀请，作为苏联政府的贵宾在苏联逗留几天。
    二十三日，彭德怀拜访了苏联共产党中央委员会第一书记赫鲁晓夫和苏联国防部长朱可夫，并且同他们进行了交谈。
    彭德怀参观了莫斯科的名胜古迹。
    彭德怀和他的随员已前往列宁格勒作几天的访问。
