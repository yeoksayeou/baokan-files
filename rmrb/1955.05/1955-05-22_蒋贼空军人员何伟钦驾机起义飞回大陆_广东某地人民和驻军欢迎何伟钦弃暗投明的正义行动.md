Title: Chiang Kai-shek Air Force Personnel He Weiqin Pilots Aircraft in Uprising, Flies Back to Mainland; People and Garrison in a Certain Area of Guangdong Welcome He Weiqin's Just Action of Abandoning the Darkness and Seeking the Light
Author:
Date: 1955-05-22
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Guangzhou, 20th: He Weiqin, a member of the Chiang Kai-shek Air Force in Taiwan, piloted an F-47 fighter-bomber to revolt and flew back to the motherland mainland at 4:15 PM on May 18th. After he landed in a certain area of Guangdong Province, he was warmly welcomed and received by the People's Liberation Army troops stationed there and the masses of the people.

He Weiqin is from Changshu City, Jiangsu Province. He was originally a Second Lieutenant Intelligence Officer of the Seventh Squadron of the Third Brigade of the Chiang Kai-shek Air Force. His aircraft number is 233, and his military number is 2029.

Xinhua News Agency, Guangzhou, 21st: More than 2,000 people, including people and garrison troops in a certain area of Guangdong Province, gathered on the 19th to welcome He Weiqin, the Chiang Kai-shek Air Force personnel who revolted by flying an aircraft.

The political commissar of a certain unit of the Chinese People's Liberation Army delivered a speech at the meeting, expressing his welcome to He Weiqin's just action of abandoning the darkness and seeking the light.

He Weiqin said at the welcome meeting: "In the past, I was determined to break away from the reactionary army of the Chiang Kai-shek traitorous clique, but I suffered from a lack of opportunity. Yesterday, I was finally able to fly back to the motherland mainland that I had been longing for day and night, and I was welcomed and warmly received by the people of the motherland. I feel very excited. In the future, I am willing to contribute all my strength to the construction of our motherland."

### 蒋贼空军人员何伟钦驾机起义飞回大陆　广东某地人民和驻军欢迎何伟钦弃暗投明的正义行动

1955-05-22
第1版()
专栏：

　　蒋贼空军人员何伟钦驾机起义飞回大陆
　　广东某地人民和驻军欢迎何伟钦弃暗投明的正义行动
    新华社广州二十日电　台湾蒋贼空军人员何伟钦驾驶F—四七型战斗轰炸机一架，在五月十八日下午四时十五分起义飞返祖国大陆。当他驾机在广东省某地着陆后，受到驻在当地的人民解放军部队和人民群众的热烈欢迎和接待。
    何伟钦家居江苏省常熟市，原系蒋军空军三大队七中队中尉二级情报官，机号是233，军号是2029。
    新华社广州二十一日电　广东省某地人民和驻军共二千多人，十九日集会欢迎驾机起义的蒋军空军人员何伟钦。
    中国人民解放军某部政治委员在会上致词，他对何伟钦弃暗投明的正义行动表示欢迎。
    何伟钦在欢迎会上讲话说：“过去我曾决心脱离蒋介石卖国集团的反动军队，只是苦于没有机会。昨天我终于能够飞回我所日夜想望的祖国大陆，并且受到祖国人民的欢迎和热情招待，感到非常兴奋。今后，我愿为建设我们祖国贡献出我所有的力量。”
