Title: Farmers in Northern Vietnam Begin Summer Harvest
Author:
Date: 1955-05-24
Page: 4

Farmers in the Northern Vietnam region have begun the summer harvest. According to a "People's Daily" report on the 23rd, farmers in the northern regions, including the Viet Bac, Third, Fourth, Left Bank, and Northwest Interzones, planted over 2 million Viet Mu (one Viet Mu equals five Chinese Mu) of summer rice. The planted area of miscellaneous grain crops in the Third, Fourth, Left Bank, and Viet Bac Interzones alone amounts to more than 389,700 Viet Mu.

To combat drought, farmers in all regions except the Northwest Interzone have also dug nearly 400 kilometers of canals, built thousands of large and small water gates, and dug tens of thousands of wells.

### 越南北部地区农民开始夏收

1955-05-24
第4版()
专栏：

    越南北部地区农民开始夏收
    越南北部地区农民开始夏收。据“人民报”二十三日报道，北部地区包括越北、第三、第四、左岸和西北等联区的农民，种了二百万越亩（一越亩等于五华亩）以上的夏稻，西北联区所播种的面积还没有统计在内。杂粮作物的耕种面积，在第三、第四、左岸和越北联区就有三十八万九千七百多越亩。
    为了抗旱，除西北联区以外的各地区的农民还挖了近四百公里的水渠，修建了上千个大小水闸，挖了数万口水井。
