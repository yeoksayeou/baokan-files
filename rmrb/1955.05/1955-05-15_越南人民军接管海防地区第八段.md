Title: Vietnam People's Army Takes Over Section Eight of Haiphong Area
Author:
Date: 1955-05-15
Page: 4

　　Xinhua News Agency, Hanoi, May 14th: The Vietnam People's Army continued to take over Section Eight of the Haiphong area on the 14th - a portion of Kien Thuy district located south of Haiphong. The Vietnam People's Army marched into this area at six o'clock in the morning, taking over the lighthouse and some French military strongholds within the district. In this way, except for Do Son, the mainland of the Haiphong area has been completely liberated.

### 越南人民军接管海防地区第八段

1955-05-15
第4版()
专栏：

　　越南人民军接管海防地区第八段
    新华社河内十四日电　越南人民军在十四日继续接管了海防地区第八段——位于海防以南的建瑞县一部分地区。越南人民军在清晨六时向这个地区进军，接管了区内的灯塔和一些法军据点。这样，除涂山外，海防地区的大陆已完全解放。
