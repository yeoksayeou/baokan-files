Title: U Nu to Visit Israel and Other Countries
Author:
Date: 1955-05-30
Page: 4

　　Xinhua News Agency, New Delhi, May 29th: Burmese Prime Minister U Nu arrived in Calcutta from Rangoon on the 28th and has already left for Israel on the same day for a visit. After visiting Israel, Prime Minister U Nu will also visit many countries, including Yugoslavia, the United Kingdom, the United States, and Japan.

### 吴努前往以色列等国访问

1955-05-30
第4版()
专栏：

　  吴努前往以色列等国访问
    新华社新德里二十九日电　缅甸总理吴努二十八日自仰光到达加尔各答，并已在当天转往以色列进行访问。在访问以色列后，吴努总理还将访问南斯拉夫、英国、美国和日本等许多国家。
