Title: Indian Chief Representative to the UN, Menon, Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1955-05-12
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, 11th: V. K. Krishna Menon, Indian Chief Representative to the United Nations and member of the Council of States, arrived in Beijing by plane on the afternoon of the 11th. Dong Yueqian, Director of the General Office of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs, Zhang Wenjin, Director of the Second Asian Department, and Wang Zhuoru, Deputy Director of the Protocol Department, were at the airport to welcome him. The Indian Ambassador to China, R.K. Nehru, was also at the airport to welcome him.

Also at the airport to welcome him were the Swedish Ambassador to China, Wistrand, the British Chargé d'Affaires in China, William, and Zhou Liang, Chargé d'Affaires ad interim of the Embassy of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam in China.

### 印度驻联合国首席代表梅农到达北京

1955-05-12
第1版()
专栏：

　　印度驻联合国首席代表梅农到达北京
    新华社十一日讯　印度驻联合国首席代表、联邦院议员维·克·克里希纳·梅农于十一日下午搭机抵达北京。到机场欢迎的有我外交部办公厅主任董越千、第二亚洲司司长章文晋和礼宾司副司长王倬如。印度驻华大使赖嘉文也到机场欢迎。
    到机场欢迎的还有瑞典驻华大使魏斯特朗、英国驻华代办杜维廉和越南民主共和国驻华大使馆临时代办周亮。
