Title: All-Union Conference of Industrial Workers Opens

Author:

Date: 1955-05-18
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency dispatch: The All-Union Conference of Industrial Workers, convened by the Communist Party of the Soviet Union and the Council of Ministers of the Soviet Union, opened on the 16th in the Moscow Kremlin. Participating in the conference were leading personnel from various enterprises, planning and design institutions, scientific research institutions, production innovators, and representatives of social organizations. Representatives from many People's Democratic countries also attended.

The leaders of the Communist Party of the Soviet Union and the government, including Bulganin, Voroshilov, Kaganovich, Malenkov, Mikoyan, Pervukhin, Saburov, Khrushchev, Kirichenko, Pospelov, and Suslov, attended the conference.

Chairman of the Council of Ministers of the Soviet Union, Bulganin, delivered the opening speech.

The participants launched discussions on the adoption of new scientific and technological achievements and advanced experiences to further develop Soviet industry. Those who spoke at the meeting on the 16th included Soviet Minister of the Coal Industry A. Zademidko, Vice-Chairman of the Council of Ministers of the Ukrainian Republic I. Senin, as well as leaders and advanced producers from local industrial and mining enterprises, totaling twenty people. In their speeches, they talked about the achievements of Soviet industrial development and the importance of further adopting advanced experience and new scientific and technological achievements in industry.

The conference is continuing.

### 全苏工业工作人员会议开幕

1955-05-18
第4版()
专栏：

    全苏工业工作人员会议开幕
    据新华社讯　苏联共产党和苏联部长会议召开的全苏工业工作人员会议，十六日在莫斯科克里姆林宫开幕。参加会议的有各企业、各计划和设计机关、科学研究机关的领导人员、生产革新者和社会团体的代表。到会的还有许多人民民主国家的代表。
    苏联共产党和政府的领导人布尔加宁、伏罗希洛夫、卡冈诺维奇、马林科夫、米高扬、别尔乌辛、萨布罗夫、赫鲁晓夫、基里钦科、波斯伯洛夫和苏斯洛夫出席了会议。
    苏联部长会议主席布尔加宁致开幕辞。
    与会者就采用科学和技术新成就和先进经验以进一步发展苏联工业的问题展开讨论。先后在十六日会上发言的有：苏联采煤工业部部长亚·扎杰米德科、乌克兰共和国部长会议副主席伊·谢宁以及各地工矿企业的领导人和先进生产者等二十人。他们在发言中谈到了苏联工业发展的成就，谈到了在工业中进一步采用先进经验以及科学和技术新成就的重要意义。
    会议在继续进行。
