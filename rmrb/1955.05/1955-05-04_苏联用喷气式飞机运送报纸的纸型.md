Title: The Soviet Union Uses Jet Aircraft to Transport Newspaper Stereotypes
Author:
Date: 1955-05-04
Page: 4

　　Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, 3rd: TASS, Moscow reports: On May 1st, a Soviet-made "IL-20" jet mail plane took off from Moscow and flew over 3,000 kilometers, delivering stereotypes of the day's Moscow central newspapers to the Siberian region.
    After taking off, the plane landed at Sverdlovsk airport in a very short time. After refueling, it continued to fly to Novosibirsk.
    From then on, residents of Sverdlovsk and Novosibirsk will be able to read Moscow newspapers of the day very early.

### 苏联用喷气式飞机运送报纸的纸型

1955-05-04
第4版()
专栏：

　　苏联用喷气式飞机运送报纸的纸型
    新华社三日讯　据塔斯社莫斯科讯：五月一日，一架苏联制造的“伊尔—二○”喷气式邮件机从莫斯科起飞，飞越了三千公里的航程，把当天的莫斯科各中央报纸的纸型送到了西伯利亚地区。
    飞机起飞以后只经过一段很短的时间就在斯维德洛夫斯克的机场着落，添油以后又继续飞往新西伯利亚城。
    从此以后，斯维德洛夫斯克和新西伯利亚城的居民就能够很早地读到当天莫斯科的报纸了。
