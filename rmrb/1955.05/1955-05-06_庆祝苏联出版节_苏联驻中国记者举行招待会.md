Title: Soviet Journalists in China Hold Reception to Celebrate Soviet Publication Festival
Author:
Date: 1955-05-06
Page: 1

　　Celebrating the Soviet Publication Festival
　  Soviet Journalists in China Hold Reception
    Xinhua News Agency, May 5th: Soviet journalists from "Pravda", TASS, and "Komsomolskaya Pravda" stationed in China held a reception in Beijing on the 5th to celebrate the Soviet Publication Festival.

    Those invited to attend included the heads and representatives from the Propaganda Department of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of China, the Compilation and Translation Bureau of the Works of Marx, Engels, Lenin and Stalin of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of China, People's Daily, Xinhua News Agency, the Broadcasting Administration Bureau, Guangming Daily, Workers' Daily, China Youth Daily, Beijing Daily, the Sino-Soviet Friendship Association, and the Information Department of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs, totaling more than eighty people.

    Journalists from various countries stationed in Beijing were also invited to attend.

### 庆祝苏联出版节  苏联驻中国记者举行招待会

1955-05-06
第1版()
专栏：

　　庆祝苏联出版节
　  苏联驻中国记者举行招待会
    新华社五日讯　苏联“真理报”、塔斯社、“共青团真理报”驻中国记者五日在北京举行招待会，庆祝苏联出版节。
    应邀出席的有：中共中央宣传部、中共中央马克思、恩格斯、列宁、斯大林著作编译局、人民日报、新华通讯社、广播事业局、光明日报、工人日报、中国青年报、北京日报、中苏友好协会总会和外交部情报司等单位的负责人和代表共八十多人。
    应邀出席的还有各国驻北京记者。
