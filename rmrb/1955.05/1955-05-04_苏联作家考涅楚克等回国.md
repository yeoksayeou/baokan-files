Title: Soviet Writers Korneychuk and Others Return Home
Author:
Date: 1955-05-04
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, 3rd. Soviet writers Korneychuk and Vasilevskaia, who attended the Asian Countries Conference and then visited our country, returned home by plane on the morning of the 3rd.

Chen Shutong, Vice Chairman of the Chinese People's Committee for World Peace, and Academician Yudin, Soviet Ambassador to China, and others went to the airport to see them off.

During their stay in our country, Korneychuk and Vasilevskaia visited Tianjin, Nanjing, Shanghai, Hangzhou, Guangzhou, Kunming, Chongqing and other places, and had extensive contact with workers, peasants, intellectuals and others in our country to learn about the lives of our people.

### 苏联作家考涅楚克等回国

1955-05-04
第4版()
专栏：

　　苏联作家考涅楚克等回国
    新华社三日讯　出席亚洲国家会议后来我国访问的苏联作家考涅楚克和华西列夫斯卡娅在三日上午乘飞机回国。
    中国人民保卫世界和平委员会副主席陈叔通和苏联驻华大使尤金院士等到机场欢送。
    考涅楚克和华西列夫斯卡娅在我国期间，先后到天津、南京、上海、杭州、广州、昆明、重庆等地访问，同我国工人、农民、知识分子等进行了广泛的接触，了解我国人民的生活。
