Our Great Motherland
The Yunnan Workers' Sanatorium on the west bank of Dianchi Lake in Kunming, Yunnan Province, has a beautiful environment, complete facilities, and an attached lakeside park. More than one hundred workers of various ethnic groups are recuperating here.

### 我们伟大的祖国（图片）

1955-05-25
第1版()
专栏：

    我们伟大的祖国
    云南省昆明市滇池西岸的云南工人疗养院，环境优美，设备完善，还附设有湖滨公园。一百多个各民族工人正在这里疗养。
