Title: All-China Journalists Association Expresses Gratitude for Condolences Received for Deceased Journalists

Author:

Date: 1955-05-08
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, 7th. The All-China Journalists Association today issued the following statement:

　　After the Chinese journalists, including Shen Jiantu, who were going to Bandung to cover the Asian-African Conference, died as martyrs because the plane they were on was sabotaged by American-Chiang Kai-shek special agents, the All-China Journalists Association received condolence letters and telegrams from journalists and people from all walks of life across the country, as well as journalists, news organizations, and people from all walks of life from various countries. We would like to express our heartfelt gratitude. All Chinese journalists will inherit the will of the martyrs, further strengthen our ranks, and fight together with honest journalists around the world for the complete victory of the just cause of peace in Asia and the world.

### 中国新闻工作者联谊会　答谢各方对我遇难记者的吊唁

1955-05-08
第1版()
专栏：

　　中国新闻工作者联谊会
　　答谢各方对我遇难记者的吊唁
    新华社七日讯　中国新闻工作者联谊会今日发表声明如下：
    前往万隆采访亚非会议新闻的中国记者沈建图等同志，因所乘飞机被美蒋特务破坏而殉难以后，中国新闻工作者联谊会收到国内各地新闻工作者、各界人士和各国新闻工作者、新闻团体及各界人士的吊唁函电，我们谨表示衷心的感谢。中国全体新闻工作者将继承烈士遗志，更加加强自己的队伍，同世界正直的新闻工作者一道，为争取亚洲及世界和平正义事业的彻底胜利而斗争。
