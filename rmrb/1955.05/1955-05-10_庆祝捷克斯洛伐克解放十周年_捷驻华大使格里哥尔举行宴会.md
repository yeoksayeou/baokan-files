Title: Czechoslovak Ambassador to China Grigel Holds Banquet to Celebrate the Tenth Anniversary of Czechoslovakia's Liberation
Author:
Date: 1955-05-10
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, 9th: Czechoslovak Ambassador to China, Grigel, held a grand banquet in Beijing on the evening of the 9th to celebrate the tenth anniversary of the glorious Soviet army's liberation of Czechoslovakia.

The attendees included: Zhou Enlai, Premier of the State Council of the People's Republic of China; Vice Premiers Chen Yi and Li Xiannian; Li Jishen and Chen Shutong, Vice Chairmen of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress; Ji Pengfei, Vice Minister of Foreign Affairs; and Feng Jiping, Deputy Mayor of Beijing.

Also present were: Directors and Deputy Directors of the State Council's Offices, Ministers and Vice Ministers of various ministries, Directors and Deputy Directors of various commissions, members of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress, senior generals of the Chinese People's Liberation Army, members of the Standing Committee of the National Committee of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference, heads of various people's organizations, figures from the capital's science, education, literature and art, and journalism circles, combat heroes, and model workers.

Heads of diplomatic missions and diplomatic officials from various countries in China were also invited to attend the banquet.

More than 600 people attended the banquet.

At the meeting, Ambassador Grigel and Premier Zhou Enlai both spoke.

The banquet proceeded in a warm and friendly atmosphere.

After the meeting, the Czechoslovak State Song and Dance Ensemble performed brilliant programs.

### 庆祝捷克斯洛伐克解放十周年　捷驻华大使格里哥尔举行宴会

1955-05-10
第1版()
专栏：

　　庆祝捷克斯洛伐克解放十周年
　　捷驻华大使格里哥尔举行宴会
    新华社九日讯　捷克斯洛伐克共和国驻我国大使格里哥尔九日晚在北京举行盛大宴会，庆祝光荣的苏联军队解放捷克斯洛伐克十周年。
    出席宴会的有：中华人民共和国国务院总理周恩来，副总理陈毅、李先念，全国人民代表大会常务委员会副委员长李济深、陈叔通，外交部副部长姬鹏飞，北京市副市长冯基平。
    出席宴会的还有：国务院各办公室主任、副主任，各部部长、副部长，各委员会主任、副主任，全国人民代表大会常务委员会委员，中国人民解放军高级将领，中国人民政治协商会议全国委员会常务委员，各人民团体负责人，首都科学、教育、文艺、新闻界人士，战斗英雄、劳动模范等。
    各国驻华使节和外交官员也应邀出席了宴会。
    出席宴会的共有六百多人。
    会上，格里哥尔大使和周恩来总理都讲了话。
    宴会在热烈友好的空气中进行。
    会后，捷克斯洛伐克国家歌舞团演出了精采节目。
