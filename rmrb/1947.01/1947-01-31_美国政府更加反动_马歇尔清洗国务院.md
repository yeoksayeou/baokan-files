Title: The U.S. Government is Increasingly Reactionary; Marshall Purges the State Department
Author:
Date: 1947-01-31
Page: 4

    Xinhua News Agency, Yan'an, 28th: According to the Associated Press, Washington: Since taking office as Secretary of State, Marshall is now carrying out a "reorganization" of the State Department, with changes to personnel at all levels. The State Department announced on the 18th that a secret screening committee had, for some time, been purging 3,000 employees who had transferred to the State Department from the Strategic Services Unit, the Wartime Information Office, and the Foreign Economic Administration. Furthermore, the U.S. Department of the Army also issued a directive to all Army departments on the 25th, intensifying the purging of officers with "destructive tendencies" from the Army. The directive grants commanders broad powers in handling this matter.
    Xinhua News Agency, Yan'an, 28th: Washington dispatch: The U.S. State Department announced that Marshall will attend the Four Foreign Ministers Conference to be held in Moscow on March 10th (Marshall plans to implement the plan of former Secretary of State Byrnes to attend the Moscow conference). The Associated Press expects Vandenberg and Connally to accompany Marshall. Connally stated on the 25th that he would attend the Moscow conference, reportedly at the request of Truman and Marshall.

### 美国政府更加反动  马歇尔清洗国务院

1947-01-31
第4版()
专栏：

    美国政府更加反动
    马歇尔清洗国务院
    【新华社延安二十八日电】据美联社华盛顿讯：马歇尔于接任国务卿后现正进行“改组”国务院工作，各级人员将有所更迭。国务院曾于十八日宣布，秘密清洗委员会若干时间以来，已清洗了自战略勤务处，战时情报处，及国外经济处等部门转至国务院任职之职员三千名。又美陆军部亦于二十五日向所有陆军部门发出指令，加紧自陆军中清洗“具有破坏倾向”之军官，指令授予各指挥官处理此事时，有广泛权力。
    【新华社延安二十八日电】华盛顿讯：美国务院宣布马歇尔将出席三月十日在莫斯科举行之四外长会议（马氏拟执行前国务卿贝纳斯所拟参加莫斯科会议之计划）。美联社预料临登堡与康纳利将与马歇尔前往。康纳利已于二十五日表示将参加莫斯科会议，据称此为应杜鲁门及马歇尔之请。
