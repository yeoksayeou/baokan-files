Title: Indian Ministry of External Affairs' Director of the East Asia Division Holds Reception to Welcome Chinese Students Studying in India
Author: 
Date: 1955-08-01
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, New Delhi, 31st—T.N. Kaul, Director of the East Asia Division of the Indian Ministry of External Affairs, held a reception on the 30th to welcome ten Chinese students sent by the Chinese government to study in India.

Attendees at the reception included officials from the Ministry of External Affairs and the Department of Education, Panikkar, Chairman of the National Executive Committee of the India-China Friendship Association, former Indian Ambassador to China, prominent educators from Delhi, and officials from the Chinese Embassy in India.

The Chinese students who have come to study in India arrived in New Delhi on July 29th.

### 印外交部东方司司长举行招待会  欢迎到印度学习的我国学生

1955-08-01
第4版()
专栏：

    印外交部东方司司长举行招待会
    欢迎到印度学习的我国学生
    新华社新德里三十一日电　印度政府外交部东方司司长特·纳·高尔三十日举行招待会，欢迎中国政府派来印度学习的十名中国学生。
    出席招待会的有外交部和教育部的官员，印中友好协会全国执行委员会主席、前印度驻华大使潘尼迦，德里著名的教育家和中国驻印度大使馆官员。
    前来印度学习的中国学生是在七月二十九日到达新德里的。
