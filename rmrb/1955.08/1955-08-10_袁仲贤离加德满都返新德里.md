Title: Yuan Zhongxian Departs Kathmandu for New Delhi
Author: 
Date: 1955-08-10
Page: 1

　　Xinhua News Agency, New Delhi, August 8th: Kathmandu dispatch: The Ambassador of the People's Republic of China to the Kingdom of Nepal, Yuan Zhongxian, and his party left Kathmandu by plane on the 7th to return to New Delhi. Representatives of the King of Nepal and government officials were at the airport to see them off.
　　Ambassador Yuan Zhongxian reviewed the guard of honor at the airport.
　　Nineteen gun salute were fired as the plane took off.

### 袁仲贤离加德满都返新德里

1955-08-10
第1版()
专栏：

    袁仲贤离加德满都返新德里
    新华社新德里八日电　加德满都消息：中华人民共和国驻尼泊尔王国大使袁仲贤一行在七日乘飞机离开加德满都返新德里。到机场欢送的有尼泊尔国王的代表和政府官员。
    袁仲贤大使在机场检阅了仪仗队。
    当飞机起飞时曾鸣礼炮十九响。
