    Our Great Motherland
    In addition to assisting the school in completing its teaching plan, the Communist Youth League organization of Hailar No. 1 Middle School in Inner Mongolia also leads students in cultural, recreational, and sports activities. The choir of the arts group goes to the suburbs on holidays to sing the Mongolian folk song "My Ox."

### 我们伟大的祖国（图片）

1955-08-15
第1版()
专栏：

    我们伟大的祖国
    内蒙古海拉尔第一中学青年团组织除协助学校完成教学计划外，还领导同学进行文化娱乐和体育活动。文艺小组的合唱队在假日到郊外演唱蒙古民歌“我的牛”。
