Title: Shenyang People Support the Indian People's Struggle to Recover Goa
Author:
Date: 1955-08-30
Page: 1

　　Xinhua News Agency, Shenyang, August 29th: People from all walks of life in Shenyang held a rally on the evening of the 29th to support the Indian people's struggle to recover Goa. More than 1,500 people, including workers, women, youth, cadres of government organizations, people from the cultural and art circles, education circles, science circles, business circles, and religious circles, participated in the meeting.

　　Zhu Weiren, Chairman of the Shenyang Branch of the Chinese People's Committee for World Peace, in his speech, first expressed, on behalf of the people of Shenyang, great indignation and protest against the atrocities of the Portuguese colonialists, expressed sympathy and support to the Indian people who are bravely fighting to defend the integrity of their national territorial sovereignty, and extended deep condolences and sincere sympathy to the victimized Indian patriots and their families. He said: The people of Shenyang suffered fourteen years of colonial rule under Japanese imperialism in the past and tasted the cruel oppression and exploitation of foreign colonialists. Therefore, we deeply sympathize with the current situation of the people of Goa. We fully understand the feelings of the people of Goa who want liberation and to return to the embrace of their motherland. We are willing to support the Indian people's heroic struggle to recover Goa with our greatest strength.

　　Representatives from the Shenyang Federation of Trade Unions, the Shenyang Democratic Women's Federation, the Shenyang Democratic Youth Federation, the Shenyang Federation of Literary and Art Workers, and the Shenyang Federation of Industry and Commerce also spoke at the meeting. In their speeches, they unanimously protested against the atrocities of the Portuguese colonialists in massacring peaceful Indian people and expressed their firm support for the Indian people's struggle to recover Goa.

### 沈阳人民支持印度人民收复果阿的斗争

1955-08-30
第1版()
专栏：

　　沈阳人民支持印度人民收复果阿的斗争
    新华社沈阳二十九日电　沈阳各界人民在二十九日晚举行集会，支持印度人民收复果阿的斗争。参加大会的有工人、妇女、青年、机关干部和文化艺术界、教育界、科学界、工商界、宗教界人士一千五百多人。
    中国人民保卫世界和平委员会沈阳市分会主席朱维仁在讲话中，首先代表沈阳市人民，对葡萄牙殖民主义者的暴行表示极大的愤慨和抗议，向为了保卫祖国领土主权的完整而英勇斗争的印度人民表示同情和支援，并向受害的印度爱国者们及他们的家属致以沉痛的悼念和深切的慰问。他说：沈阳人民过去遭受日本帝国主义十四年的殖民统治，饱尝了外国殖民主义者的残酷压迫和剥削，因此，我们深深同情果阿人民今天的处境，我们完全了解果阿人民要求解放、要求回到祖国怀抱的心情，我们愿以最大力量支持印度人民收复果阿的英勇斗争。
    在会上讲话的还有沈阳市工会联合会、沈阳市民主妇女联合会、沈阳市民主青年联合会、沈阳市文学艺术工作者联合会和沈阳市工商业联合会的代表。他们在讲话中，一致抗议葡萄牙殖民主义者残杀和平的印度人民的暴行，并表示坚决支持印度人民收复果阿的斗争。
