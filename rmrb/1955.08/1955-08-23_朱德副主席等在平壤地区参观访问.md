Title: Vice Chairman Zhu De and Others Visit and Tour the Pyongyang Area
Author:
Date: 1955-08-23
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency, Pyongyang, August 22nd: Vice Chairman Zhu De and the entire delegation of the People's Republic of China, accompanied by Vice Premier of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea, Choi Chang-ik, and Minister of Social Sciences of the Central Committee of the Korean Workers' Party, Ha Ang-cheon, and others, continued their visits and tours in the Pyongyang area on the 21st and 22nd.

On the 21st, the entire delegation visited the Korean Central Broadcasting Station and the Korean National Central History Museum. They viewed Korean historical relics in the museum with great interest. Subsequently, the delegation toured the scenic spots and historical sites in the Mudanbong Park area, including Ulmiltae, Pubyokru, Chonggummum, and Chongnyubyok.

On the morning of the 22nd, the delegation went to the Lipo-ri village in Chunghwa County, South Pyongan Province, to visit and interviewed some local farmers. Afterward, the delegation visited the state-owned agricultural machinery service station in Chilbul-ri, East Pyongyang, and received a warm welcome from the tractor drivers. In the afternoon of the same day, the delegation took a boat trip to enjoy the beautiful scenery on both banks of the lower reaches of the Taedong River.

### 朱德副主席等在平壤地区参观访问

1955-08-23
第4版()
专栏：

　　朱德副主席等在平壤地区参观访问
    新华社平壤二十二日电　朱德副主席率领的中华人民共和国代表团全体人员二十一、二十二两日由朝鲜民主主义人民共和国内阁副首相崔昌益和朝鲜劳动党中央委员会社会科学部长河仰天等陪同，继续在平壤地区参观和访问。
    二十一日，代表团全体参观了朝鲜中央广播电台和朝鲜国立中央历史博物馆。他们在博物馆很有兴趣地观览了朝鲜历代文物。随后，代表团游览了牡丹峰公园区的乙密台、浮碧楼、转锦门、清流壁等名胜古迹。
    二十二日上午，代表团前往平安南道中和郡力浦里农村参观，访问了当地的一些农户。随后，代表团又参观了东平壤七佛里的国营农业机械代耕站，受到拖拉机手们的热烈欢迎。这一天下午，代表团乘船欣赏了大同江下游两岸的秀丽景色。
