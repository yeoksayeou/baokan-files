Title: Rallies Held Across Romania Demanding a Ban on Atomic Weapons
Author:
Date: 1955-08-08
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Bucharest, July 7th. TASS reports: The Romanian News Agency reports that the Romanian Peace Defense Committee held a mass rally in Bucharest on August 5th to commemorate the International Day of Struggle for Arms Reduction and a Complete Ban on Atomic Weapons, and to support the World Conference Against Atomic and Hydrogen Bombs held in Hiroshima.

Academician Constantinescu-Iaşi, Vice-Chairman of the Peace Defense Committee, spoke at the meeting. He pointed out that if humanity uses atomic energy for peaceful purposes, it will obtain enormous benefits. Other speakers emphasized that the Romanian people are working together to strive for peace and cooperation between all countries, and to strive for the declaration of atomic weapons and weapons of mass destruction as illegal.

Mass rallies and conferences commemorating the International Day of Struggle for Arms Reduction and a Complete Ban on Atomic Weapons were also held in cities such as Krosz, Craiova, Târgu Mureș, and Timisoara.

### 罗马尼亚各地举行集会  要求禁止原子武器

1955-08-08
第4版()
专栏：

    罗马尼亚各地举行集会
    要求禁止原子武器
    新华社七日讯　据塔斯社布加勒斯特讯：罗马尼亚通讯社消息，罗马尼亚保卫和平委员会八月五日在布加勒斯特举行群众大会，纪念要求裁减军备和完全禁止原子武器的国际斗争日，并且支持在广岛举行的禁止原子弹和氢弹世界大会。
    保卫和平委员会副主席康斯坦丁尼斯库—雅希院士在会上发言。他指出，人类把原子能用于和平目的，就会获得巨大的好处。其他的发言者着重指出，罗马尼亚人民同心协力争取和平和各国人民之间的合作，争取宣布原子武器和大规模毁灭性武器为非法。
    在克罗什、克拉约发、塔古尔谟勒斯、底密索拉等地也举行了纪念要求裁减军备和完全禁止原子武器的国际斗争日的群众集会和大会。
