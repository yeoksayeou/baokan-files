Title: Albanian People's Army Song and Dance Ensemble Passes Through Mongolia En Route to Beijing
Author:
Date: 1955-08-13
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Ulaanbaatar, 12th. The Albanian People's Army Song and Dance Ensemble, invited to visit China, and led by Lieutenant Colonel Mekur Çela, left Ulaanbaatar by train for Beijing at 6:30 PM on the 11th, after completing its tour of the Mongolian People's Republic.

Those seeing them off at the station included the Chief of Staff of the Mongolian People's Army, Lkhagvasüren; the Assistant Minister of the Mongolian Ministry of Foreign Affairs and Director of the Bureau of Foreign Cultural Relations, Tserendorj; the Director of the Protocol Department of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs, Luvsanchultem; and the Vice-Chairman of the Arts Committee, Burvee, among others. The Chinese Ambassador to Mongolia, He Ying, also saw them off at the station.

### 阿尔巴尼亚人民军歌舞团过蒙古来北京

1955-08-13
第1版()
专栏：

    阿尔巴尼亚人民军歌舞团过蒙古来北京
    新华社十二日讯　乌兰巴托消息：应邀到中国访问的阿尔巴尼亚人民军歌舞团在领队梅库尔·柴拉中校率领下，在蒙古人民共和国进行了访问演出后，已于十一日下午六时三十分乘火车离开乌兰巴托前往北京。
    到车站送行的有蒙古人民军参谋长拉哈瓦苏伦、蒙古外交部部长助理兼对外文化联络局局长策林道尔吉、外交部交际司长鲁布桑乔尔坦、艺术委员会副主席布尔维等。中国驻蒙古大使何英也到车站送行。
