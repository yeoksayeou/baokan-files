Title: Indonesian Arts and Crafts and Photo Exhibition Opens

Author:
Date: 1955-08-17
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, August 16th: The "Indonesian Arts and Crafts and Photo Exhibition" organized by the China-Indonesia Friendship Association opened in Beijing on the afternoon of the 16th.

    The hall in front of the exhibition venue was filled with Chinese and foreign guests attending the opening ceremony.

    Hu Yuzhi, Vice President of the China-Indonesia Friendship Association, delivered a speech at the opening ceremony, congratulating the Indonesian people on the 10th anniversary of Indonesian independence.

    The Indonesian Ambassador to China, Mononutu, delivered a speech thanking the China-Indonesia Friendship Association for organizing this exhibition. He pointed out that the foundation of friendly relations between China and Indonesia lies in the common historical experiences of the two countries, and that the friendly relations between the two countries are aimed at lasting world peace. He expressed his belief that the friendly relations between China and Indonesia are not only a blessing for the two countries, but also contribute to the peace and security of Asia and the world.

    After the ceremony, the guests visited the exhibition.

    Finally, the guests watched the documentary films "Premier Zhou Enlai's Visit to Indonesia", "Indonesian Prime Minister Ali Sastroamidjojo's Visit to Beijing" and the documentary about the Indonesian independence struggle "Unquenchable Flame".

### 印度尼西亚工艺美术及图片展览会开幕

1955-08-17
第1版()
专栏：

    印度尼西亚工艺美术及图片展览会开幕
    据新华社十六日讯　中国印度尼西亚友好协会举办的“印度尼西亚工艺美术及图片展览会”，十六日下午在北京举行开幕仪式。
    在展览会会场前面的大厅中，坐满了参加开幕仪式的中外来宾。
    中国印度尼西亚友好协会副会长胡愈之在开幕仪式上致词，向印度尼西亚人民祝贺印度尼西亚独立十周年。
    印度尼西亚驻华大使莫诺努图致词感谢中国印度尼西亚友好协会举办这个展览会，他指出：中国和印度尼西亚友好关系的基础是两国有着共同的历史遭遇，而且两国之间的这种友好关系是以世界持久和平为目的的。他表示相信中国和印度尼西亚的友好关系不仅是两国间的幸福，而且有助于亚洲和世界的和平与安全。
    在仪式结束后，来宾们参观了展览会。
    最后，来宾们观看了纪录影片“周恩来总理访问印度尼西亚”、“印度尼西亚总理阿里·沙斯特罗阿米佐约到北京”和关于印度尼西亚独立斗争的纪录片“不灭的火焰”。
