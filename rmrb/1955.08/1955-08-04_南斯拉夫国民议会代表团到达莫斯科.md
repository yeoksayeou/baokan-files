Title: Yugoslav National Assembly Delegation Arrives in Moscow
Author:
Date: 1955-08-04
Page: 4

    Xinhua News Agency dispatch, Moscow—A delegation from the National Assembly of the Federal People's Republic of Yugoslavia arrived in Moscow on the 2nd at the invitation of the Supreme Soviet of the Soviet Union. The head of the delegation is V. Bakarić, President of the Parliament of the People's Republic of Croatia.
    The crowd gathered at the airport warmly welcomed the representatives of the Yugoslav people. N.M. Volkov, Chairman of the Soviet of the Union of the Supreme Soviet of the Soviet Union, delivered a welcoming speech at the airport, saying: Your visit to the Soviet Union is a joyful event for all Soviet people, because it marks the restoration of the extremely friendly ties that existed between our two peoples during the war and in the early years after the war. The arrival of the delegation will enable our Yugoslav comrades to learn more carefully about our country's experience in socialist construction, establish friendly relations with Soviet men and women, and understand how deeply and sincerely the Soviet people sympathize with the fraternal Yugoslav people.
    Bakarić gave a reply speech, expressing his gratitude for the sincere and warm welcome given by the Soviet comrades. He said that every meeting we have with your people makes us realize the correctness of what our Comrade Tito said. He said that the hearts of Soviet men and women are filled with friendship towards our people, and there is no reason why our two peoples cannot cooperate in the future in a spirit of friendship and move towards safeguarding peace and seeking a better future for mankind.

### 南斯拉夫国民议会代表团到达莫斯科

1955-08-04
第4版()
专栏：

    南斯拉夫国民议会代表团到达莫斯科
    据新华社讯　南斯拉夫联邦人民共和国国民议会代表团应苏联最高苏维埃的邀请于二日抵达莫斯科。代表团团长是克罗地亚人民共和国议会议长弗·巴卡里奇。
    聚集在机场上的人群热烈欢迎南斯拉夫人民的代表。苏联最高苏维埃联盟院主席沃尔科夫在机场上致欢迎词说：你们来苏联访问对全体苏联人民来说是一件欢欣的事情，因为这标志着我们两国人民在战时和在战后头几年中所存在的极其友好的联系的恢复。代表团的到达将使我们南斯拉夫的同志们能够更仔细地了解我国社会主义建设的经验，同苏联男女建立友好联系，并了解苏联人民是多么深厚地、衷心地同情兄弟般的南斯拉夫人民。
    巴卡里奇致答词，对苏联同志所给予的诚恳和热情的欢迎表示感谢。他说，我们同你们的人民的每一个聚会都使我们体会到我们的铁托同志所讲的话的正确性。他说，苏联男女的心充满了对我国人民的友好，没有任何理由说我们两国人民不能够在将来本着友好的精神进行合作，并走向维护和平和为人类谋求美好的将来的道路。
