Title: Indian Cultural Delegation Arrives in Guangzhou After Visiting Hangzhou
Author:
Date: 1955-08-05
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency:

    The Indian Cultural Delegation, after nearly two months of visits and performances in China, arrived in Guangzhou by special train from Hangzhou at 6:00 PM on the 4th. Colored flags and huge banners welcoming the Indian Cultural Delegation were hung on the platform of the Guangzhou Railway Station. The station inside and outside was filled with welcoming crowds.
    More than 500 representatives from cultural and artistic circles were at the station to welcome the Indian Cultural Delegation. Zhu Guang, Mayor of Guangzhou, Guo Qiaoran, Deputy Mayor, Cao Ruoming, Director of the Foreign Affairs Office of the Guangdong Provincial People's Committee, Xiao Junying, Director of the Guangdong Provincial Cultural Bureau, Ding Bo, Director of the Guangzhou Municipal Cultural Bureau, and responsible persons from provincial and municipal cultural and artistic circles also welcomed the delegation at the station.
    Xia Yan, Vice Minister of the Ministry of Culture of the People's Republic of China, also welcomed the delegation at the station.
    Xinhua News Agency: The Indian Cultural Delegation performed and toured in Hangzhou.
    On the evening of July 31, more than 1,900 spectators in Hangzhou enjoyed the performance of the Indian Cultural Delegation. The audience in Hangzhou was greatly interested in the wonderful programs performed by the outstanding Indian dancers and musicians, and each performance was met with cheers and enthusiastic applause from the audience.
    On the morning of July 31, members of the Indian Cultural Delegation toured the West Lake. At about 10:00 AM, Chandha and other leaders of the delegation paid a visit to Li Fengping, Vice Governor of Zhejiang Province, Wu Xian, Mayor of Hangzhou, and Gu Chunlin, Deputy Mayor.
    On August 1, the Indian Cultural Delegation visited the Huanglong Cave and "Yuquan," both scenic summer resorts, and then traveled by car to the magnificent and ancient Lingyin Temple to view the stone Buddhas, meticulously carved by Chinese laborers more than a thousand years ago, on the Feilai Peak Mountain, and toured the "Tongtian Cave." In the afternoon, Chandha and his wife, along with other leaders of the delegation, visited the East China Branch of the Central Academy of Fine Arts and the Zhejiang Provincial Museum on the banks of the West Lake. In the evening, Wu Xian, Mayor of Hangzhou, held a reception on the lawn of a garden by the West Lake in Hangzhou to welcome the distinguished guests of the Indian Cultural Delegation.
    On August 2, after visiting the silk weaving factory and the production of silk umbrellas, the Indian Cultural Delegation left Hangzhou by car in the afternoon.

### 印度文化代表团访问杭州后到达广州

1955-08-05
第1版()
专栏：

    印度文化代表团访问杭州后到达广州
    据新华社讯　在我国访问和演出近两个月的印度文化代表团四日下午六时由杭州乘专车到达广州。广州车站月台上挂着彩旗和欢迎印度文化代表团的巨幅标语。车站内外排满了欢迎的人群。
    到车站欢迎印度文化代表团的有文化艺术界代表共五百多人。广州市市长朱光、副市长郭翘然，广东省人民委员会外事处处长曹若茗，广东省文化局局长萧隽英，广州市文化局局长丁波，以及省市文化艺术界负责人等也到车站欢迎。
    中华人民共和国文化部副部长夏衍也到车站欢迎。
    据新华社讯　印度文化代表团在杭州演出和游览。
    七月三十一日晚，杭州市一千九百多观众欣赏了印度文化代表团的演出。杭州市的观众们对印度杰出的舞蹈家和音乐家们所表演的精采节目，感到极大的兴趣，每当一个节目表演后，都引起全场欢呼和热烈的掌声。
    七月三十一日上午，印度文化代表团团员游览了西湖。上午十时许，钱达和代表团的其他领导人员拜会了浙江省副省长李丰平、杭州市市长吴宪、副市长顾春林。
    八月一日，印度文化代表团游览了避暑佳地黄龙洞和“玉泉”，然后乘车到了雄伟古老的灵隐寺，观看了飞来峰山上一千多年以前中国劳动人民精心雕刻的石佛群，游览了“通天洞”。下午钱达及他的夫人和代表团的其他领导人员参观访问了西湖边上的中央美术学院华东分院和浙江省博物馆。晚上，杭州市市长吴宪在杭州西湖边花园草坪上举行了酒会，欢宴印度文化代表团的贵宾们。
    八月二日，印度文化代表团在参观了丝绸织厂和绸伞生产情况后，于下午乘车离开杭州。
