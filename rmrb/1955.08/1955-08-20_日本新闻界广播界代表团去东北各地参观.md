Title: Japanese Press and Broadcasting Delegation Visits Various Places in Northeast China
Author:
Date: 1955-08-20
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, August 19th – The Japanese press and broadcasting delegation, after being received by Premier Zhou Enlai, flew from Beijing to Northeast China on the morning of the 19th to visit various places.

During its stay in Beijing, the delegation visited the heads of various government departments, democratic parties, and people's organizations.

On August 2nd, the delegation visited Guo Moruo, Chairman of the All-China Federation of Literary and Art Circles. Guo Moruo provided detailed answers to the many questions raised by the delegation members regarding cultural and artistic matters.

On the 5th, the delegation visited Fu Zuoyi, Minister of Water Resources. Fu Zuoyi introduced to the guests the brilliant achievements made by New China in water conservancy construction and answered many questions raised by the delegation.

The delegation visited Li Dequan and Liao Chengzhi at the headquarters of the Chinese Red Cross Society. During the visit, they focused on the issue of Japanese residents in China. The delegation also visited Zhang Xiruo, President of the Chinese People's Institute of Foreign Affairs, and Vice President Qiao Guanhua, and many of their questions were answered satisfactorily.

On the morning of the 6th, the heads of the democratic parties in China, including Zhang Bojun, Shao Lizi, Mei Gongbin, Li Zhuchen, Sun Qimeng, Zhou Jianren, Lin Handa, and Sa Kongliao, met with the delegation members and answered the questions raised by the delegation.

Delegation members visited Lei Renmin, Vice Chairman of the China Council for the Promotion of International Trade, to learn about the situation and problems of Sino-Japanese trade; visited Xu Dixin, Deputy Director of the Eighth Office of the State Council, and personnel from the State Planning Commission, discussing China's First Five-Year Plan, modern industry, and the socialist transformation of private industry and commerce. The delegation also visited Tan Zheng, Deputy Minister of National Defense, to discuss issues such as the conscription law. In addition, the delegation visited the Ministry of Higher Education, the All-China Federation of Trade Unions, the All-China Democratic Women's Federation, and the All-China Sports Federation.

The delegation held a forum with journalists in the capital, visited the People's Daily, Xinhua News Agency, and the Central Broadcasting Bureau, and held separate discussions with the heads of the above units. The delegation also talked with well-known Chinese writers Lao She, Zhao Shuli, and Liu Baiyu about literary creation and attended a plenary session of the Second Meeting of the First National People's Congress.

During its stay in Beijing, the delegation also visited the state-owned Beijing No. 2 Cotton Textile Factory, the Xishan Agricultural Production Cooperative in Xishan Township, Peking University, the Central Institute for Nationalities, the Asian Students Sanatorium, and the former residence and prison of Lu Xun. They also toured famous historical sites and scenic spots such as the Forbidden City, the Temple of Heaven, Beihai Park, the Summer Palace, the Temple of Azure Clouds, and the Badaling section of the Great Wall.

On the 12th, the delegation visited the state-owned Tianjin Paper Mill, the jointly owned Renli Industrial Co., Ltd., and the Workers' Cultural Palace in Tianjin.

### 日本新闻界广播界代表团去东北各地参观

1955-08-20
第1版()
专栏：

    日本新闻界广播界代表团去东北各地参观
    新华社十九日讯　日本新闻界广播界访华代表团，在周恩来总理接见之后，于十九日早晨乘飞机由北京去东北各地访问参观。
    代表团在北京期间，曾分别访问了政府各部门、各民主党派和各人民团体的负责人。
    八月二日，代表团访问了中国文学艺术界联合会主席郭沫若。郭沫若对代表团人员所提出的许多关于文化艺术方面的问题，都作了详尽的答复。
    五日，代表团访问了水利部部长傅作义，傅作义向客人们介绍了新中国在水利建设事业中所取得的光辉成就，并答复了代表团提出的许多问题。
    代表团到中国红十字会总会访问了李德全和廖承志，在访问时，着重谈到了在华日侨问题。代表团还访问了中国人民外交学会会长张奚若和副会长乔冠华，他们所提出的许多问题也都得到了圆满的解答。
    六日上午，中国各民主党派的负责人章伯钧、邵力子、梅龚彬、李烛尘、孙起孟、周建人、林汉达、萨空了等，接见了代表团人员，并解答了代表团所提出的问题。
    代表团人员访问了中国国际贸易促进委员会副主席雷任民，了解了中日贸易的情况和问题；访问了国务院第八办公室副主任许涤新和国家计划委员会人员，谈到中国第一个五年建设计划、现代工业和私营工商业的社会主义改造等问题。代表团还访问了国防部副部长谭政，谈到兵役法等问题。此外代表团还访问了高等教育部、中华全国总工会、中华全国民主妇女联合会和中华全国体育总会等单位。
    代表团和首都新闻界人士举行了座谈会，分别参观了人民日报社、新华通讯社、广播事业局等，并分别和上述各单位的负责人举行了座谈。代表团还和中国著名作家老舍、赵树理、刘白羽等座谈了文艺创作方面的问题，旁听了第一届全国人民代表大会第二次会议全体会议。
    在北京期间，代表团还参观了国营北京第二棉纺织厂、西山乡西山农业生产合作社、北京大学、中央民族学院、亚洲学生疗养院、鲁迅故居和监狱等，游览了故宫、天坛、北海、颐和园、碧云寺和八达岭等名胜古迹和风景区。
    十二日，代表团曾到天津参观了国营天津造纸厂、公私合营仁立实业股份有限公司和工人文化宫。
