Title: Japanese Parliamentary Delegation to Visit the Soviet Union
Author:
Date: 1955-08-25
Page: 4
　　
　　Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, August 24th: TASS reports that the Chairman of the Soviet of the Union of the Supreme Soviet of the USSR, Volkov, and the Chairman of the Soviet of Nationalities, Lacis, sent a telegram to the Speaker of the House of Representatives of the Japanese Diet, Masutani Hideji, and the President of the House of Councillors, Kawai Yahachi, inviting a delegation from the Japanese Diet to visit the Soviet Union. The Speakers of both Houses of the Japanese Diet recently replied, stating that both Houses of the Japanese Diet have gratefully accepted the invitation to visit the Soviet Union and have selected a delegation of the Japanese Diet to visit the Soviet Union. The Japanese Parliamentary Delegation will arrive in the Soviet Union at the end of August.

### 日本国会代表团将访问苏联

1955-08-25
第4版()
专栏：

　  日本国会代表团将访问苏联
    新华社二十四日讯　据塔斯社莫斯科讯：苏联最高苏维埃联盟院主席沃尔科夫和民族院主席拉齐斯曾致电日本国会众议院议长益谷秀次和参议院议长河井弥八，邀请日本国会派代表团访问苏联。日本国会两院议长最近复电说，日本国会两院已经感激地接受了访问苏联的邀请，并且选出了访问苏联的日本国会代表团。日本国会代表团将于八月底到达苏联。
