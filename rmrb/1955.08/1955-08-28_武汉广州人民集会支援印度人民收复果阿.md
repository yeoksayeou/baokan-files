Title: Wuhan and Guangzhou People Rally to Support Indian People in Recovering Goa
Author:
Date: 1955-08-28
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, Wuhan, August 27th: People from all walks of life in Wuhan held a mass rally on the 27th to support the Indian people's struggle to recover Goa.

　　Zeng Dun, Vice Chairman of the Wuhan Branch of the Chinese People's Committee for World Peace, first expressed, on behalf of the people of Wuhan, their respect to the Indian people who are fighting heroically to defend their territorial sovereignty, condolences and sympathy to the patriotic Indian martyrs who gloriously sacrificed themselves in the struggle and their families, and great indignation and protest against the atrocities of the Portuguese colonialists in massacring peaceful Indian people. Zeng Dun condemned the atrocities of the Portuguese colonial authorities in killing Indian patriots. He pointed out that this barbaric act violated the principle of respecting the sovereignty and territorial integrity of all countries as stipulated in the Asian-African Conference, and violated all humanitarian spirit and international standards of conduct. The Indian people's struggle to recover Goa will surely be victorious.

　　Zhao Tie夫, Chairman of the Wuhan Federation of Trade Unions, Li Dongqing, Deputy Director of the Wuhan Democratic Women's Federation, Dai Jinsheng, Vice Chairman of the Wuhan Democratic Youth Federation, Li Rui, Vice Chairman of the Wuhan Federation of Literary and Art Workers, and Hua Yuqing, Deputy Director of the Wuhan Federation of Industry and Commerce, also spoke at the meeting. They unanimously protested the tyrannical behavior of the Portuguese colonialists and expressed their support for the Indian people's struggle to recover Goa.

　　More than 1,000 workers, women, youth, cultural workers and industrialists and businessmen attended today's meeting.
    
　　Xinhua News Agency, Guangzhou, August 27th: More than 1,000 people from all walks of life in Guangzhou held a rally at the Trade Union Building on the afternoon of the 27th to support the Indian people's struggle to recover Goa.

　　Participants included workers, government cadres, suburban farmers, youth, teachers, women, medical personnel, religious figures, scientists, cultural figures, and people from industry and commerce.

　　The meeting was presided over by Guo Qiaoran, Vice Chairman of the Guangzhou Branch of the Chinese People's Committee for World Peace. He said that the Indian people's recovery of Goa is a completely patriotic and just action, and the barbaric and brutal means adopted by the Portuguese colonialists are a blatant provocation to the Asian people. After denouncing the Portuguese colonialists' brutal rule over the Indian people of Goa for hundreds of years, he emphasized that the Indian people's recovery of Goa is completely justified and well-founded.

　　Representatives from trade unions, cultural circles, youth, women, and industry and commerce also spoke at the meeting. They unanimously protested the barbaric behavior of the Portuguese occupiers against the Indian people. They all said: Goa is an inseparable part of Indian territory. It is the sacred duty of the Indian people to recover Goa.

### 武汉广州人民集会支援印度人民收复果阿

1955-08-28
第1版()
专栏：

　　武汉广州人民集会支援印度人民收复果阿
    新华社武汉二十七日电　武汉市各界人民在二十七日举行群众集会，支援印度人民收复果阿的斗争。
    中国人民保卫世界和平委员会武汉分会副主席曾惇在讲话中，首先代表武汉各界人民向为了保卫自己领土主权而英勇斗争的印度人民致敬，向在斗争中光荣牺牲的印度爱国者们及其家属致以哀悼和慰问，并对葡萄牙殖民主义者残杀和平的印度人民的暴行表示极大的愤慨和抗议。曾惇谴责了葡萄牙殖民当局残杀印度爱国者的暴行。他指出这一野蛮的暴行，违反了亚非会议所规定的尊重一切国家的主权和领土完整的原则，并且违反了一切人道精神和国际行为的标准。印度人民收复果阿的斗争，一定会获得胜利。
    武汉市工会联合会主席赵铁夫、武汉市民主妇女联合会副主任李冬青、武汉市民主青年联合会副主席戴今生、武汉市文学艺术工作者联合会副主席李蕤、武汉市工商业联合会副主任委员华煜卿都在会上讲了话，他们一致抗议葡萄牙殖民主义者的横暴行为，并表示支援印度人民收复果阿的斗争。
    今天到会的共有工人、妇女、青年、文化工作者和工商业者一千多人。
    新华社广州二十七日电　广州市各界人民一千多人二十七日下午在工会大厦举行集会，支援印度人民收复果阿的斗争。
    参加集会的有工人、机关干部、郊区农民、青年、教师、妇女、医务界、宗教界、科学界、文化界和工商界等人士。
    会议由中国人民保卫世界和平委员会广州分会副主席郭翘然主持。他说，印度人民要收复果阿完全是爱国的正义的行动，葡萄牙殖民主义者所采取的野蛮残暴的手段是对亚洲人民的狂妄挑衅。他斥责葡萄牙殖民主义者几百年来对果阿印度人民的残酷统治后着重指出，印度人民收复果阿是完全合乎正义和具有充分理由的。
    会上发言的还有工会、文化、青年、妇女、工商界的代表，他们一致抗议葡萄牙占领者对印度人民的野蛮行为。他们都说到：果阿是印度领土的一个不可分割的一部分。印度人民要收复果阿，是印度人民神圣的责任。
