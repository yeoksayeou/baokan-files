Title: Congratulations on the Tenth Anniversary of Indonesia's Independence - Chairman Mao Zedong's Telegram to President Sukarno
Author:
Date: 1955-08-17
Page: 1

    Congratulations on the Tenth Anniversary of Indonesia's Independence
    Chairman Mao Zedong's Telegram to President Sukarno
    To His Excellency Sukarno, President of the Republic of Indonesia:
    On the occasion of the tenth anniversary of the independence of the Republic of Indonesia, I extend warm congratulations to Your Excellency and the people of your country. I wish Indonesia prosperity and its people thriving, and wish Your Excellency good health.
                Mao Zedong, Chairman of the People's Republic of China
                Beijing, August 15, 1955

### 祝贺印度尼西亚独立十周年  毛泽东主席致苏加诺总统电

1955-08-17
第1版()
专栏：

    祝贺印度尼西亚独立十周年
    毛泽东主席致苏加诺总统电印度尼西亚共和国总统苏加诺先生阁下：
    值此印度尼西亚共和国独立十周年纪念之际，谨向阁下和贵国人民致热烈的祝贺。祝印度尼西亚国家繁荣、人民兴旺，并祝阁下身体健康。
                中华人民共和国主席  毛泽东
                一九五五年八月十五日于北京
