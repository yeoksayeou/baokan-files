Title: Opening of the Second National Congress of the Chinese Coal Miners' Union
Author:
Date: 1955-08-23
Page: 2

The Second National Congress of the Chinese Coal Miners' Union opened in Beijing on August 22nd.

There were 241 delegates attending the congress. Among them were model workers, advanced producers, engineering and technical personnel, enterprise management personnel, and trade union workers, as well as ethnic minority miners.

Comrade Chen Gengfu delivered the opening speech on behalf of the presidium of the congress. He pointed out that the main task of this congress is to mobilize all coal mine workers to strive for the successful completion and overfulfillment of the First Five-Year Plan for the coal industry. The congress will discuss and approve the work report of the Chinese Coal Miners' Union for the past four years and determine future tasks; discuss and approve the financial work report of the First National Committee of the Chinese Coal Miners' Union and the report of the Audit Committee on the audit of expenses; and elect the Second National Committee and the Audit Committee of the Chinese Coal Miners' Union.

Lai Ruoyu, Chairman of the All-China Federation of Trade Unions, delivered a congratulatory speech to the congress on behalf of the All-China Federation of Trade Unions (full text to be published separately). All the representatives responded to his congratulations with warm applause.

Xu Daben, Vice Minister of the Ministry of Coal Industry of the People's Republic of China, Ju Kangjie, representative of the Third Ministry of Machine Building of the People's Republic of China, Liu Kun, representative of the Central Committee of the Chinese New Democratic Youth League, and Zhou Xifeng, Vice Chairman of the National Committee of the First Machine Building Workers' Union of China, also spoke at the meeting to offer their congratulations.

The congress received a congratulatory telegram from Carlos Pozo, Secretary of the Miners' Trade Unions International, one of the industrial departments of the World Federation of Trade Unions. The Chairman of the Central Committee of the Soviet Coal Miners' Union, the Chairman of the Hungarian Coal Miners' Union, the Chairman of the Central Committee of the Romanian Miners' Union, and the Chairman of the Scottish Miners' Union all sent congratulatory letters or telegrams. The congress also received congratulatory letters from many units in our country.

### 中国煤矿工会第二届全国代表大会开幕

1955-08-23
第2版()
专栏：

　　中国煤矿工会第二届全国代表大会开幕
    中国煤矿工会第二届全国代表大会于八月二十二日在北京开幕。
    出席代表大会的有二百四十一位代表。他们中间有劳动模范、先进生产者、工程技术人员、企业管理人员和工会工作者，还有少数民族矿工。
    陈耕夫同志代表大会主席团致开幕词。他指出本次大会的主要任务是：动员全体煤矿职工为胜利完成和超额完成煤炭工业的第一个五年计划而奋斗。大会将讨论和批准中国煤矿工会四年来的工作报告并确定今后的任务；讨论和批准中国煤矿工会第一届全国委员会财务工作报告和经费审查委员会关于经费审查的报告；选举中国煤矿工会第二届全国委员会和经费审查委员会。
    中华全国总工会主席赖若愚同志代表中华全国总工会向大会致祝词（全文另发）。全体代表对他的祝贺报以热烈的掌声。
    在会上发言向大会致贺的还有中华人民共和国煤炭工业部副部长徐达本、中华人民共和国第三机械工业部代表鞠抗捷、中国新民主主义青年团中央委员会代表刘昆以及中国第一机械工会全国委员会副主席周西风。
    代表大会收到世界工会联合会产业部之一的矿工工会国际书记卡洛斯·波左的贺电。苏联煤矿工会中央委员会主席、匈牙利煤矿工会主席、罗马尼亚矿工工会中央委员会主席以及苏格兰矿区工会主席都寄来了贺信或拍来了贺电。大会还收到我国的许多单位的贺信。
