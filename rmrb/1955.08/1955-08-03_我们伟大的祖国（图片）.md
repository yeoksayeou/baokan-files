    Our Great Motherland
    Mount Lu is located in the area between Poyang Lake and the Yangtze River in northern Jiangxi Province. It is a famous summer resort in our country. On the mountain range of Hanpokou, the main scenic area of Mount Lu, tourists can overlook the beautiful scenery of Poyang Lake.

### 我们伟大的祖国（图片）

1955-08-03
第1版()
专栏：

    我们伟大的祖国
    庐山在江西省北部鄱阳湖和长江中间，是我国著名的避暑胜地。在庐山主要风景区含鄱口的山岭上，游人可以远眺鄱阳湖的美丽景色。
