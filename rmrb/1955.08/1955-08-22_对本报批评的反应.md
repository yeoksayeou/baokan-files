Author:
Date: 1955-08-22
Page: 2

　　Reactions to Criticism in This Newspaper
    On May 19th, this newspaper published an article entitled "Oppose the 'Losing Money Plan'," criticizing the Shenyang Municipal Bureau of Commerce for erroneously approving the money-losing plan formulated by the Municipal Food Company. Now, the Shenyang Municipal Bureau of Commerce has sent a letter of self-criticism and proposed preliminary improvement measures.
    On July 4th, this newspaper published an article entitled "'Guaranteeing Progress' Cannot Be Used as an Excuse to Resist Thrift," criticizing Comrade Wang Xinmin, Deputy Director of the Fourth Machine Industry Bureau of the First Ministry of Machine Building, for not actively practicing thrift when reviewing the architectural design of a factory in Shanghai, using "cannot delay progress" as an excuse. Now, Comrade Wang Xinmin has sent a letter of self-criticism.

### 对本报批评的反应

1955-08-22
第2版()
专栏：对本报批评的反应

　　对本报批评的反应
    五月十九日本报发表了“反对‘赔钱计划’”一文，批评沈阳市商业局错误地批准市食品公司所订的赔钱的计划。现在，沈阳市商业局已来信检讨，并提出了初步的改进办法。
    七月四日本报发表了“‘保证进度’不能作为抗拒节约的借口”一文，批评第一机械工业部第四机器工业管理局副局长王新民同志在审查上海一个工厂的建筑设计时，借口“不能耽误进度”，不积极节约。现在，王新民同志已来信检讨。
