Title: Chinese Youth Delegation Visits Iceland
Author: 
Date: 1955-08-28
Page: 3
　
    The Chinese Youth Delegation, led by head of delegation Lu Zhao, arrived in Reykjavik on the evening of the 25th at the invitation of the Iceland-China Committee to visit Iceland. Those who welcomed them at the airport included: Einar Gunnar Einarsson, Chairman of the Iceland-China Committee, the Chairman of the Icelandic Youth International Committee, the Chairman of the Icelandic Socialist Youth League, the Secretary of the Iceland-China Cultural Association, and more than forty others.
    That evening, the Iceland-China Committee held a banquet for the Chinese Youth Delegation. (Xinhua News Agency)

### 中国青年代表团访问冰岛

1955-08-28
第3版()
专栏：

　  中国青年代表团访问冰岛
    应冰岛中国委员会邀请访问冰岛的中国青年代表团在鲁钊团长率领下，已经在二十五日晚到达雷克雅未克。到机场欢迎的有：冰岛中国委员会主席埃纳尔·古纳尔·埃纳尔逊、冰岛青年国际委员会主席、冰岛社会主义青年联盟主席、冰中文化协会秘书等四十多人。
    当晚，冰岛中国委员会为中国青年代表团举行了宴会。（新华社）
