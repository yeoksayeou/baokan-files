Title: Letter to the Youth of All Countries from the 5th World Youth Festival
Author:
Date: 1955-08-17
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Warsaw, 15th. The 5th World Youth Festival issued a letter to the youth of all countries at the closing ceremony on the 14th. The full text is as follows:

    We, thirty thousand young men and women from one hundred and fourteen countries, have traveled over mountains and seas to come here—Warsaw—to participate in the 5th World Youth and Student Festival for Peace and Friendship—a traditional gathering of the younger generation, a review of culture and sports, and a meeting place for young people who hope to know each other and strengthen understanding.
    In this hopeful summer, after the Four-Power Geneva Conference, our gathering proves that despite differences in belief, race, and language, we can cooperate, and such cooperation is necessary to guarantee our right to live a real life in peace, and to guarantee our rights to work, education, happiness, and friendship.
    Nothing can stop us from always being true friends and ensuring that the slogan written on the festival banner—“Peace and Friendship”—becomes the common property of all young people in every one of our countries.
    Friends, young men and women, the youth force of the whole world,
    Please listen to our appeal!
    Let us unite more closely for the same purpose and in the same activities.
    Let us take action to make the ideals of the festival exist and triumph!
    Just as we have done here, let us strengthen our mutual understanding and relations, our friendship, and the friendship and cooperation between the people of all countries everywhere. Let us further strengthen the activities that are more necessary than ever before to safeguard peace. Let us work together to ease international tensions, reduce armaments, and ban atomic weapons and all weapons of mass destruction.
    Let us unite to defend our youth, our hopes, and strive for progress and a bright future for all mankind.

### 第五届世界青年联欢节告各国青年书

1955-08-17
第4版()
专栏：

    第五届世界青年联欢节告各国青年书
    新华社华沙十五日电　第五届世界青年联欢节在十四日闭幕式上发表告各国青年书，全文如下：
    我们一百一十四个国家的三万个青年男女翻山越海来到这里——华沙，来参加第五届世界青年与学生和平友谊联欢节——青年一代传统的盛会，文化、体育的检阅和希望彼此认识和加强了解的青年人的聚会场所。
    在这个充满希望的夏季，在四大国日内瓦会议以后，我们的集会证明：尽管有信仰、种族和语言的不同，我们是可以合作的，而且为了保障我们在和平中过真正生活的权利，保障我们工作、受教育、得到幸福和友谊的权利，这种合作是必要的。
    任何东西都不能阻挡我们始终是真正的朋友并且保证写在联欢节旗帜上的口号——“和平与友谊”在我们每一个国家中成为一切青年人的共同财产。
    朋友们，男女青年们，全世界的青年力量，
    请听我们的呼吁！
    让我们为了同样的目的，在同样的活动中更紧密地团结在一起。
    让我们采取行动来使联欢节的理想存在下去并且获得胜利！
    正如我们在这里所做的一样，让我们在各个地方加强我们的互相了解和关系、我们的友谊以及各国人民之间的友谊和合作。让我们再度加强比过去任何时候都更为必要的活动，来保障和平。让我们共同努力来缓和国际紧张局势，裁减军备，禁止原子武器和一切大规模毁灭性武器。
    让我们团结起来保卫我们的青春、我们的希望，争取进步和全人类的光辉的明天。
