Title: Liu Ningyi and Others Visit Japanese Trade Unions and Express Sympathy to Atomic Bomb Victims
Author:
Date: 1955-08-19
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Tokyo, 17th. Liu Ningyi, head of the Chinese delegation attending the World Conference for the Prohibition of Atomic and Hydrogen Bombs and Vice Chairman of the All-China Federation of Trade Unions, along with Sofia Vasilkovska, the Polish trade union representative, and William Golan, the Australian trade union representative, visited the General Council of Trade Unions of Japan (Sohyo) on the afternoon of the 16th at their invitation.

They were welcomed by Sohyo Chairman Fujida Tohtaro, Vice Chairmen Ota Kaoru, Imamura Akira, Shioji Nobuo, Secretary-General Iwai Akira, and Gao Ye Shi and all other permanent members of the standing committee. Fujida Tohtaro delivered a welcoming speech saying: "Thanks to the foreign representatives who have come to participate in the World Conference for the Prohibition of Atomic and Hydrogen Bombs, the connection between Japanese workers and foreign workers has been strengthened. The Japanese working class is determined to fight for the complete elimination of war."

Liu Ningyi said: "We have brought the friendship of the Chinese workers to the Japanese workers. We believe that our visit to Japan will further strengthen the solidarity and friendship between Chinese and Japanese workers. The solidarity between Chinese and Japanese workers is the foundation for enhancing the friendship between the two peoples." He emphasized: "We should further strengthen our solidarity and overcome all difficulties to enhance the friendship between the two peoples and fight for peace in Asia."

The hosts and guests had a friendly and cordial conversation.

In the evening, Liu Ningyi and other foreign trade union representatives also attended a welcome banquet hosted by the General Council of Trade Unions of Japan. More than 170 people attended the banquet, including cadres from various trade union organizations and Nozomi Katsu from the left-wing Socialist Party, Kato Kanji from the right-wing Socialist Party, and Suzuki Ichizo from the Communist Party.

On the afternoon of the 16th, Liu Ningyi and other foreign trade union representatives also paid friendly visits to the headquarters of various trade union organizations such as the Japanese Federation of Trade Unions, the National Federation of Industrial Unions, the All-Japan Federation of Industrial Unions, and the All-Japan Council of Trade Unions.

Xinhua News Agency, Tokyo, 18th. Liu Ningyi, head of the Chinese delegation attending the World Conference for the Prohibition of Atomic and Hydrogen Bombs and Vice Chairman of the All-China Federation of Trade Unions, along with Sofia Vasilkovska, Secretary of the Central Council of Polish Trade Unions, William Golan, representative of the Australian Council of Trade Unions, and Hai Frank, Vice Chairman of the Free German Trade Union Federation, visited the headquarters of national trade union organizations from various industrial sectors affiliated with the General Council of Trade Unions of Japan on the afternoon of the 17th, including the Japan Teachers' Union, the Japan Coal Miners' Union, the Synthetic Chemical Industry Workers' Union Federation, the Japan Iron and Steel Industry Workers' Union Federation, and the National Railway Workers' Union. They were warmly welcomed by all the trade union cadres at the headquarters of each trade union.

That evening, Liu Ningyi and other foreign trade union representatives also attended a welcome banquet hosted by the National Railway Workers' Union. After the banquet, they were invited by the General Council of Trade Unions of Japan to watch Kabuki.

Cheng Fangwu and Xie Bingxin, members of the Chinese delegation, were invited to attend a welcome banquet hosted by the Tokyo Metropolitan Assembly on the afternoon of the same day. More than 30 people attended the banquet, including Tokyo Metropolitan Assembly Vice Chairman Nakazawa Shigeru and Tokyo Metropolitan Assembly member Kitajima Yoshihiko.

Xinhua News Agency, Tokyo, 17th. The Chinese delegation and other delegations from various countries attending the World Conference for the Prohibition of Atomic and Hydrogen Bombs received approximately 40 representatives of atomic bomb victims from Hiroshima and Nagasaki, including Professor Moritaki Ichiro of Hiroshima University, at the hotel on the morning of the 15th.

The representatives of the victims, with atomic bomb scars remaining on their faces or arms, tearfully recounted the suffering they had endured to the representatives of various countries. They expressed their heartfelt gratitude for the letters of sympathy and consolation money brought to them by the Chinese delegation, and for the resolution passed at this World Conference for the Prohibition of Atomic and Hydrogen Bombs to establish an international relief fund committee. They expressed their hope to the representatives of various countries that the people of the world would stand with them and work to prohibit atomic weapons and strive for peace.

The representatives of various countries kindly comforted the victims of the atomic bombs.

### 刘宁一等访问日本工会并慰问原子弹受害者

1955-08-19
第4版()
专栏：

    刘宁一等访问日本工会并慰问原子弹受害者
    新华社十七日讯　东京消息：出席禁止原子弹和氢弹世界大会的中国代表团团长、中华全国总工会副主席刘宁一，十六日下午同波兰工会代表索非亚·瓦西尔科夫斯卡、澳大利亚工会代表威廉·戈兰一起，应邀访问了日本工会总评议会。
    他们受到了总评主席藤田藤太郎，副主席太田薰、今村彰、盐谷信雄，事务局长岩井章和高野实等常任干事会全体常任干事的欢迎。藤田藤太郎致欢迎词说：这次由于外国代表前来参加禁止原子弹和氢弹世界大会，我们日本工人和外国工人加强了联系。日本工人阶级决心为彻底消灭战争而奋斗。
    刘宁一说：我们带来了中国工人对日本工人的友谊。我们相信，我们的来日本访问将更进一步加强中日两国工人的团结和友谊。中日两国工人的团结是增进两国人民友谊的基础。他强调说：我们应该更进一步加强团结，克服一切困难来增进两国人民的友谊，为亚洲和平而奋斗。
    宾主在友好亲切的空气中进行了交谈。
    晚间，刘宁一和其他外国工会代表还出席了日本工会总评议会主办的欢迎宴会。参加宴会的有各工会组织的干部和左派社会党的野沟胜、右派社会党的加藤勘十、共产党的铃木市藏等一百七十多人。
    十六日下午，刘宁一和其他外国工会代表还曾到日本工会总同盟、全国产业别工会联合会、全日本产业别工会会议和全日本工会会议等各工会组织的总部作了友好的访问。
    新华社十八日电　东京消息：出席禁止原子弹和氢弹世界大会的中国代表团团长、中华全国总工会副主席刘宁一，十七日下午同波兰工会中央理事会书记索非亚·瓦西尔科夫斯卡、澳大利亚工会理事会代表威廉·戈兰、自由德国工会联合会副主席海·弗兰克一起访问了日本教职员工会、日本煤矿工会、合成化学产业工会联合会、日本钢铁产业工会联合会、国营铁道工会等参加日本工会总评议会的各产业部门的全国性工会组织的总部。他们在各工会总部受到了全体工会干部的热烈欢迎。
    当天晚上，刘宁一和其他外国工会代表还出席了国营铁道工会主办的欢迎宴会。会后他们还应日本工会总评议会的邀请观赏了歌舞伎。
    中国代表团团员成仿吾、谢冰心等当天下午应邀出席了东京都议会主办的欢迎宴会。参加宴会的有东京都议会副议长中泽茂、东京都议会议员北岛义彦等三十多人。
    新华社十七日讯　东京消息：出席禁止原子弹和氢弹世界大会的中国代表团和其他各国代表团，十五日上午在旅馆接见了来自广岛和长崎的广岛大学教授森泷市郎等原子弹受害者代表约四十人。
    在脸上或胳膊上残留着原子弹伤痕的受害者代表们含着眼泪向各国代表控诉了他们所遭受的痛苦。他们对中国代表团带来给他们的慰问信、慰问金和在这次禁止原子弹和氢弹世界大会上通过设立国际救济基金委员会的决议，表示了衷心的感谢。他们向各国代表表示希望全世界人民同他们站在一道，为禁止原子武器、争取和平而努力。
    各国代表们亲切地安慰了原子弹的受害者。
