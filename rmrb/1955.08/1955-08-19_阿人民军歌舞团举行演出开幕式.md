Title: Albanian People's Army Song and Dance Troupe Holds Opening Performance

Author: 
Date: 1955-08-19
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, August 18th. The Albanian People's Army Song and Dance Troupe held its first song and dance performance at the Tianqiao Theater in Beijing on the evening of August 18th. A grand opening ceremony was held before the performance.

Those who attended the opening ceremony and watched the song and dance performance tonight included Peng Dehuai, Vice Premier of the State Council and Minister of National Defense, Yang Xiufeng, Minister of Higher Education, Zhang Hanfu, Vice Minister of Foreign Affairs, Xia Yan, Vice Minister of Culture, Zhang Zongxun and Chen Geng, Deputy Chiefs of the General Staff of the Chinese People's Liberation Army, Fu Zhong and Gan Siqi, Deputy Directors of the General Political Department, Xiao Xiangrong, Director of the General Office of the Ministry of National Defense, senior generals of the Chinese People's Liberation Army, Wang Kunlun, Deputy Mayor of Beijing, Chu Tunan, President of the Chinese People's Association for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries, members of the literary and art circles in the capital and literary and art workers of the Chinese People's Liberation Army, as well as Nasse, the Albanian Ambassador to China, the Albanian cultural delegation led by Fadil Paçrami, and diplomatic envoys and officials from various countries in China.

At the beginning of the opening ceremony, Chen Yi, Director of the Cultural Department of the General Political Department of the Chinese People's Liberation Army, Xia Yan, Vice Minister of the Ministry of Culture of the People's Republic of China, Lü Ji, Chairman of the Chinese Musicians Association, Chen Qitong, Head of the Art Troupe of the General Political Department of the Chinese People's Liberation Army, and others went onto the stage with flowers, representing the Chinese literary and art circles and the art workers of the armed forces to welcome the Albanian people's artistic envoys to perform in China.

After the band played the national anthems of Albania and China, Chen Yi delivered a welcome speech, saying: The Chinese people love the fraternal Albanian people and love the art of the Albanian people. This time, the Albanian People's Army Song and Dance Troupe has brought the beautiful songs and dances of the Albanian people and their deep friendship from Tirana to China, which will give new encouragement to the Chinese people and the Chinese People's Liberation Army. After the speech, Chen Yi and others presented flowers to Lieutenant Colonel Mekur Çela, the leader and head of the song and dance troupe, to pay their respects. At this time, the warm applause lasted for a long time.

Lieutenant Colonel Mekur Çela delivered a reply speech on behalf of the song and dance troupe, saying: Our Albanian People's Army Song and Dance Troupe is greatly honored to come to the People's Republic of China as the first Albanian art group to perform. The visit and performance of the song and dance troupe in China will further consolidate and develop the friendship between the people and armies of Albania and China.

The song and dance performance then began. The Albanian People's Army Song and Dance Troupe performed twenty wonderful song and dance programs at the evening party, which were warmly welcomed by the audience.

### 阿人民军歌舞团举行演出开幕式

1955-08-19
第1版()
专栏：

    阿人民军歌舞团举行演出开幕式
    新华社十八日讯　阿尔巴尼亚人民军歌舞团十八日晚间在北京天桥剧场举行首次歌舞表演会。在演出前隆重举行了演出开幕式。
    今晚出席演出开幕式和观看歌舞表演的，有国务院副总理兼国防部长彭德怀，高等教育部部长杨秀峰，外交部副部长章汉夫，文化部副部长夏衍，中国人民解放军副总参谋长张宗逊、陈赓，总政治部副主任傅锺、甘泗淇，国防部办公厅主任萧向荣，及中国人民解放军高级将领，北京市副市长王崑崙，中国人民对外文化协会会长楚图南，首都文艺界人士和中国人民解放军文艺工作者，以及阿尔巴尼亚驻华大使纳赛，以法迪尔·帕奇拉米为首的阿尔巴尼亚文化代表团，各国驻华使节和外交官员。
    开幕式开始，中国人民解放军总政治部文化部部长陈沂、中华人民共和国文化部副部长夏衍、中国音乐家协会主席吕骥、中国人民解放军总政治部文工团团长陈其通等携着鲜花一同登上舞台，代表中国文艺界和部队文艺工作者欢迎阿尔巴尼亚人民的艺术使者在中国演出。
    乐队奏阿中两国国歌后，陈沂致欢迎词说：中国人民热爱兄弟的阿尔巴尼亚人民，热爱阿尔巴尼亚人民的艺术。这次阿尔巴尼亚人民军歌舞团把阿尔巴尼亚人民优美的歌舞和他们的深厚友谊一道，从地拉那带到了中国，这将给中国人民和中国人民解放军新的鼓舞。讲话后，陈沂等向歌舞团领队兼团长梅库尔·柴拉中校等献花致敬。这时，全场热烈的掌声历久不息。
    梅库尔·柴拉中校代表歌舞团致答词，他说：我们阿尔巴尼亚人民军歌舞团作为第一个阿尔巴尼亚艺术团体，来到中华人民共和国进行访问演出，感到莫大的荣幸。歌舞团在中国进行访问和演出，将进一步巩固和发展阿中两国人民及其军队之间的友谊。
    歌舞表演接着开始。阿尔巴尼亚人民军歌舞团在晚会上表演了二十个精采的歌舞节目，受到观众的热烈欢迎。
