Title: Portuguese Authorities Suppress Patriotic Goan People
Author:
Date: 1955-08-23
Page: 4

　　Portuguese Authorities Suppress Patriotic Goan People
    Xinhua News Agency, New Delhi, August 22nd: According to a report by the Indian Press Trust on the 21st, Portuguese police opened fire on nationalist demonstrators in Ecosi, Bardez district, North Goa, on August 15th, killing two Goan nationalists. According to another report from the Indian Information Service on the same day: A special military tribunal established by the Portuguese authorities in Goa sentenced four Goan youths to long-term imprisonment on the 8th for establishing a "nationalist group seeking the merger of Goa with India." Among these four people, two were officials of the local government, who were sentenced to eight and six years in prison respectively; the other two medical students were sentenced to three years in prison.

### 葡当局镇压爱国的果阿人民

1955-08-23
第4版()
专栏：

　　葡当局镇压爱国的果阿人民
    新华社新德里二十二日电　据印度报业托辣斯二十一日报道，葡萄牙警察曾在八月十五日在果阿北部的巴德兹区的埃可希向举行示威的民族主义者开枪，打死了两名果阿民族主义者。另据印度新闻处同一天的消息：果阿葡萄牙当局设立的特别军事法庭曾在八日将四名建立“民族主义者小组谋求果阿与印度合并”的果阿青年判处长期徒刑。这四个人中，有两个人是当地政府的官员，他们分别被判处八年和六年的徒刑；另外两名医科学生被判处三年徒刑。
