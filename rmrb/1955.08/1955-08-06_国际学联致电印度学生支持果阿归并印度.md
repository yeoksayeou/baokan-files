Title: International Student Union Calls Indian Students to Support Goa's Merger with India
Author: 
Date: 1955-08-06
Page: 4

    Xinhua News Agency, New Delhi, August 4th. The Secretariat of the International Student Union sent a telegram to Indian students expressing support for the movement to strive for Goa's merger with India.
    The telegram reiterated that the International Student Union has always given all possible help to students fighting against colonialism and for independence. The telegram guaranteed full support to Indian students and people from all walks of life in India in their struggle to liberate Goa.

### 国际学联致电印度学生支持果阿归并印度

1955-08-06
第4版()
专栏：

    国际学联致电印度学生支持果阿归并印度
    新华社新德里四日电　国际学生联合会书记处打了一个电报给印度学生，表示支持争取果阿归并印度的运动。
    电报重申国际学生联合会一向对为反对殖民主义和争取独立而进行斗争的学生给予一切可能的帮助。电报保证对印度学生同印度各阶层人民一道为争取果阿的解放而进行的斗争给予充分的支持。
