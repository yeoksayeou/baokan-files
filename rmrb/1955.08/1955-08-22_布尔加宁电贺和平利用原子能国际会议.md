Title: Bulganin Sends Congratulations on the International Conference on the Peaceful Uses of Atomic Energy
Author:
Date: 1955-08-22
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, August 20th: TASS reported that the Chairman of the Council of Ministers of the Soviet Union, Bulganin, telegraphed Mr. Baba, Chairman of the International Scientific and Technical Conference on the Peaceful Uses of Atomic Energy, on the 19th, saying: "The upcoming International Conference on the Peaceful Uses of Atomic Energy is a major step on the road to establishing international cooperation in this important field of the peaceful uses of atomic energy. The exchange of opinions and information at the conference has made a valuable contribution to the further development of the science and technology of the peaceful uses of atomic energy. This conference will undoubtedly contribute to the cause of peace and will contribute to further easing international tensions."
    Bulganin also said: "The Soviet government congratulates all participants in the conference on their achievements, and hopes that this international cooperation in the field of the peaceful uses of atomic energy, which has started so smoothly at this conference, will be continued by regular meetings of scientists from various countries."

### 布尔加宁电贺和平利用原子能国际会议

1955-08-22
第1版()
专栏：

　　布尔加宁电贺和平利用原子能国际会议
    新华社二十一日讯　据塔斯社莫斯科二十日讯：苏联部长会议主席布尔加宁十九日打电报给和平利用原子能国际科学技术会议主席巴巴先生说：“即将结束的和平利用原子能国际会议，是在和平利用原子能这一个重要领域建立国际合作的道路上的一个重大步骤。在会议上交换意见和情报，对和平利用原子能的科学和技术的进一步发展作出了宝贵的贡献。这一个会议无疑将有助于和平事业，并且将促成进一步缓和国际紧张局势。”
    布尔加宁还说：“苏联政府向会议的所有参加者祝贺他们取得的成就，并且希望这个会议这样顺利地开始的在和平利用原子能领域内的国际合作将由各国科学家定期召开类似的会议而继续下去。”
