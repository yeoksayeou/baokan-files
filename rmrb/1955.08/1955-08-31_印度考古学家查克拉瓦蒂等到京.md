Title: Indian Archaeologist Chakravarti Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1955-08-31
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, August 29th: Indian archaeologist Professor Chakravarti and his wife, along with Deb Burman, Dean of the Art Institute of the International University of India, and one other person, arrived in Beijing by plane on the afternoon of the 28th at the invitation of the Ministry of Culture of the People's Republic of China.

### 印度考古学家查克拉瓦蒂等到京

1955-08-31
第4版()
专栏：

　  印度考古学家查克拉瓦蒂等到京
    新华社二十九日讯　应中华人民共和国文化部邀请前来中国访问的印度考古学家查克拉瓦蒂教授和他的夫人，印度国际大学艺术院院长德布·巴曼等三人，在二十八日下午乘飞机到达北京。
