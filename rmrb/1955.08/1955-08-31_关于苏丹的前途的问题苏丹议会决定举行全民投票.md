Title: On the Question of Sudan's Future, Sudanese Parliament Decides to Hold Referendum
Author:
Date: 1955-08-31
Page: 4

　　Xinhua News Agency, Cairo, August 30th, (via TASS): Omdurman Radio in Sudan broadcast that the Sudanese Parliament unanimously passed a resolution to hold a referendum on the question of Sudan's future. The referendum will replace the election of a constituent assembly. According to the Anglo-Egyptian Agreement on the Sudan issue of February 12, 1953, the Sudanese Parliament was entrusted to decide whether Sudan would merge with Egypt in some form or become an independent state. Now, the Sudanese Parliament has resolved that the question of Sudan's self-determination will be decided by a referendum. The Sudanese Parliament has requested Britain and Egypt to approve this decision; this decision of the Sudanese Parliament is a modification of the provisions regarding Sudan's self-determination in the aforementioned Anglo-Egyptian Agreement.

### 关于苏丹的前途的问题苏丹议会决定举行全民投票

1955-08-31
第4版()
专栏：

    关于苏丹的前途的问题苏丹议会决定举行全民投票
    新华社三十日讯　塔斯社开罗三十日讯：苏丹恩图曼电台广播，苏丹议会一致通过了一项关于就苏丹的前途的问题举行一次全民投票的决议。全民投票将代替制宪议会的选举。根据一九五三年二月十二日的英埃关于苏丹问题的协定，苏丹议会是曾经受委托在苏丹将以某种形式同埃及合并抑或成为一个独立国家的问题作出决定的。现在，苏丹议会决议，关于苏丹自决的问题将交由全民投票决定。苏丹议会要求英国和埃及批准这个决定；苏丹议会的这项决定是对上述英埃协定中关于苏丹自决问题的规定作了修改的。
