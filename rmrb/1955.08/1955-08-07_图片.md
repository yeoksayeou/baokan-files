Date: 1955-08-07

    The Heilongjiang Province Wasteland Survey Team has reached the Muling River basin and Luobei area in two routes. They will complete the wasteland survey of 1.2 million hectares this year. The soil survey team in Luobei area, which is mostly composed of graduates from universities and secondary agricultural schools this year, is conducting surveys on the wasteland.　Xinhua News Agency reporter Tan Zhiqiang photo
