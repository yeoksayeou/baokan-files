Title: Chinese Government Delegation to Sofia to Attend the Plovdiv International Fair
Author:
Date: 1955-08-25
Page: 1

　　To Attend the Plovdiv International Fair
　  Chinese Government Delegation to Sofia
    Xinhua News Agency, August 23rd. The Chinese government delegation, invited to participate in the 16th International Fair in Plovdiv, Bulgaria, left Beijing by plane on August 23rd, led by Zhong Fuxiang, head of the delegation and Deputy Minister of Posts and Telecommunications of the People's Republic of China, and traveled to Sofia via Moscow.
    During its stay in Bulgaria, the delegation will also discuss and sign a postal and telecommunications agreement between China and Bulgaria.

### 参加普罗夫迪夫国际博览会  我国政府代表团去索非亚

1955-08-25
第1版()
专栏：

　　参加普罗夫迪夫国际博览会
　  我国政府代表团去索非亚
    新华社二十三日讯　应邀参加保加利亚在普罗夫迪夫城举行的第十六届国际博览会的中国政府代表团，在团长、中华人民共和国邮电部副部长钟夫翔率领下，二十三日乘飞机离开北京，取道莫斯科前往索非亚。
    代表团在保加利亚期间，还要商谈并签订中保两国的邮电协定。
