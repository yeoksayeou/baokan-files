Title: Our Great Motherland (Picture)
Author:
Date: 1955-08-08
Page: 1

    Our Great Motherland
    The Ministry of Agriculture dispatched three airplanes and allocated 900 tons of "666" insecticide powder to help farmers in the autumn locust-affected areas of Hebei Province control locusts. Each airplane can eliminate locust nymphs on 30,000 mu of land per day.

### 我们伟大的祖国（图片）

1955-08-08
第1版()
专栏：

    我们伟大的祖国
    农业部派了三架飞机，拨给了九百吨“六六六”治蝗药粉，为河北省发现秋蝗地区的农民治蝗。每架飞机每天可以消灭三万亩土地上的蝗蝻。
