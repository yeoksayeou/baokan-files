Title: People of Zaragoza, Spain, Hold Demonstration Against Franco
Author:
Date: 1955-08-12
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, 9th. TASS reports: "L'Humanité" quoted news from the Spanish Communist Party's underground newspaper "Worker's World" as saying: Recently, the people of Zaragoza, Spain, held a large demonstration against Franco. More than four thousand Spaniards, angered by police brutality, shouted slogans against Franco. During the demonstration, Franco's police clashed with the workers.

### 西班牙萨拉哥萨城人民举行反对佛朗哥的示威

1955-08-12
第4版()
专栏：

    西班牙萨拉哥萨城人民举行反对佛朗哥的示威
    新华社十日讯　据塔斯社巴黎九日讯：“人道报”据西班牙共产党地下报纸“工人世界”的消息报道说：最近西班牙萨拉哥萨城人民举行了一次反对佛朗哥的大示威。为警察暴行所激动的四千多个西班牙人高呼反对佛朗哥的口号。在示威游行的时候，佛朗哥的警察和劳动者发生了冲突。
