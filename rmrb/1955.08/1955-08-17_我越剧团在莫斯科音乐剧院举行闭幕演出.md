Title: Our Yue Opera Troupe Holds Closing Performance at the Moscow Musical Theater
Author: Li He
Date: 1955-08-17
Page: 4
Our Yue Opera Troupe Holds Closing Performance at the Moscow Musical Theater

    (By our correspondent in Moscow, 16th) The Chinese Yue Opera Troupe gave its closing performance at the Moscow Musical Theater on the 15th, performing "The Butterfly Lovers." Voroshilov, Kaganovich, Malenkov, Mikoyan, Pervukhin, Suslov, and Pospelov attended the performance. Liu Xiao, our ambassador to the Soviet Union, also accompanied them. At the end of the performance, the audience applauded the Yue Opera Troupe enthusiastically.
    The Yue Opera Troupe will travel to Leningrad on the 17th to continue its performances.
                                  (Li He)

### 我越剧团在莫斯科音乐剧院举行闭幕演出
李何
1955-08-17
第4版()
专栏：

    我越剧团在莫斯科音乐剧院举行闭幕演出
    本报莫斯科十六日电　中国越剧团十五日在莫斯科音乐剧院举行闭幕演出，演出了“梁山伯与祝英台”。伏罗希洛夫、卡冈诺维奇、马林科夫、米高扬、别尔乌辛、苏斯洛夫、伯斯别洛夫出席观剧。我驻苏联大使刘晓也陪同观剧。剧终时，观众向越剧团热烈喝采。
    越剧团将于十七日赴列宁格勒继续演出。
                                  （李何）
