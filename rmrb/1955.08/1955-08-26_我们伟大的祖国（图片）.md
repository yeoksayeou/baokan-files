Author:
Date: 1955-08-26
Page: 1

    Our Great Motherland
    After the promulgation of the "Provisional Measures for Rationed Grain Supply in Towns and Cities," residents of Beijing actively support this measure. Wu Laotaitai and Wu Laoxiansheng, residents of Xiangluying Fourth Alley in the Qianmen District, stated that they will be even more economical in their grain consumption in the future. The two of them are carefully calculating and formulating their household grain consumption plan for September.

### 我们伟大的祖国（图片）

1955-08-26
第1版()
专栏：

    我们伟大的祖国
    市镇粮食定量供应暂行办法公布后，北京市居民积极拥护这一措施。前门区香炉营四条居民吴老太太和吴老先生，表示今后更要节约用粮，他们两人在精打细算地制定九月份的家庭用粮计划。
