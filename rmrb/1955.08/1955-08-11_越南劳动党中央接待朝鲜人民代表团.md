Title: The Central Committee of the Vietnam Workers' Party Receives the Korean People's Delegation
Author:
Date: 1955-08-11
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Hanoi, 10th - According to Vietnam News Agency: The Central Committee of the Vietnam Workers' Party received the Korean People's Delegation headed by Baek Nam-un on the afternoon of the 9th. Trường Chinh, General Secretary of the Vietnam Workers' Party, and Lê Văn Lương, Nguyễn Chí Thanh, Hoàng Văn Hoan, Phạm Hùng, members of the Central Committee of the Vietnam Workers' Party, held cordial talks with the members of the Korean People's Delegation.

    The Korean People's Delegation then visited the National Committee of the Vietnam National United Front.

    Baek Nam-un, on behalf of the Korean People's Delegation and the Korean Art Troupe, presented many gifts to the Central Committee of the Vietnam Workers' Party and the National Committee of the Vietnam National United Front.

### 越南劳动党中央接待朝鲜人民代表团

1955-08-11
第4版()
专栏：

    越南劳动党中央接待朝鲜人民代表团
    新华社河内十日讯　据越南通讯社讯：越南劳动党中央委员会九日下午接待了以白南云为首的朝鲜人民代表团。越南劳动党总书记长征和越南劳动党中央委员会委员黎文良、阮志清、黄文欢、范雄等同朝鲜人民代表团团员进行了亲切的谈话。
    朝鲜人民代表团然后访问了越南国民联合战线全国委员会。
    白南云代表朝鲜人民代表团和朝鲜艺术团向越南劳动党中央委员会和越南国民联合战线全国委员会赠送了许多礼品。
