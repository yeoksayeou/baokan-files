Title: Exhibition on Chairman Ho Chi Minh and the Government Delegation of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam's Visit to the Soviet Union, China, and Mongolia Opens in Hanoi
Author:
Date: 1955-08-18
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Hanoi, 17th. The exhibition on Chairman Ho Chi Minh and the Government Delegation of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam's visit to the Soviet Union, China, and Mongolia opened in Hanoi today. The opening ceremony was presided over by Hoang Minh Giam, Minister of Propaganda of the Government of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam. Le Dinh Tham, Chairman of the Vietnam Committee for the Defense of World Peace, government ministers and deputy ministers, and representatives of the Hanoi Municipal Administrative Committee attended the opening ceremony. Xie Shuangqiu, Chargé d'affaires ad interim of the Chinese Embassy in Vietnam, Sokolov, Chargé d'affaires ad interim of the Soviet Embassy, and diplomatic officials from other countries also attended the opening ceremony.

Hoang Minh Giam spoke at the opening ceremony. He said that every Vietnamese person deeply feels that the visit of Chairman Ho Chi Minh and the government delegation of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam and the joint communiqués between Vietnam and the Soviet Union and Vietnam and China have increased their strength in the struggle for peace, unification, independence, and democracy. He said that this exhibition will bring the enthusiasm of the people of the fraternal countries to the Vietnamese people, and also allow us to see some aspects of the great construction undertakings and happy lives of the people of the Soviet Union, China, and Mongolia.

Afterwards, the guests viewed the exhibited pictures and the gifts given to Chairman Ho Chi Minh and the delegation by the governments and people of the Soviet Union, China, and Mongolia.

### 胡志明主席和越南民主共和国政府代表团  访问苏联、中国、蒙古展览会在河内开幕

1955-08-18
第4版()
专栏：

    胡志明主席和越南民主共和国政府代表团
    访问苏联、中国、蒙古展览会在河内开幕
    新华社河内十七日电　胡志明主席和越南民主共和国政府代表团访问苏联、中国、蒙古展览会十七日在河内市开幕。开幕式由越南民主共和国政府宣传部部长黄明鉴主持。越南保卫世界和平委员会主席黎廷探、政府各部长、副部长和河内市行政委员会代表都参加了开幕式。参加开幕式的还有中国驻越南大使馆临时代办谢爽秋、苏联大使馆临时代办索科洛夫和其他国家的外交官员。
    黄明鉴在开幕式上讲了话。他说，每个越南人民都深深感到胡志明主席和越南民主共和国政府代表团的访问以及越苏、越中的联合公报给自己为和平、统一、独立、民主而作的斗争增加了力量。他说，这次展览将给越南人民带来各兄弟国家人民的热情，同时也使我们看到苏、中、蒙人民的伟大的建设事业和幸福生活的一些面貌。
    接着，来宾们参观了展出的图片和苏、中、蒙政府和人民送给胡志明主席和代表团的礼品。
