Title: Chinese Student Delegation Arrives in Jakarta
Author:
Date: 1955-08-21
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Jakarta. At the invitation of the Indonesian National Student Union to attend the Indonesian Independence Day celebrations, the five-member Chinese student delegation, led by Qian Dawei, Deputy Secretary-General of the All-China Students' Federation, arrived in Jakarta by plane on the afternoon of the 18th. They were welcomed at the airport by Agustin, Chairman of the Indonesian National Student Union.

### 我国学生代表团到达雅加达

1955-08-21
第4版()
专栏：

　　我国学生代表团到达雅加达
    据新华社雅加达电　应印度尼西亚全国大学生联合会邀请参加印度尼西亚独立纪念日庆祝典礼的中国学生代表团一行五人，在中华全国学生联合会副秘书长钱大卫率领下，十八日下午乘飞机到达雅加达。他们在机场受到印度尼西亚全国大学生联合会主席阿古斯丁的欢迎。
