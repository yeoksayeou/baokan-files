Title: National People's Congress Representative Zhu Zaoguan Passes Away
Author:
Date: 1955-08-21
Page: 1

　　Xinhua News Agency, 20th. National People's Congress Representative, member of the National People's Congress Nationalities Committee, and Deputy Director of the General Office of the Ministry of National Defense, Zhu Zaoguan, passed away in Beijing at 12:00 on August 20, 1955, due to heart disease.

　　The Zhu Zaoguan Funeral Committee is composed of Huang Kecheng, Gan Siqi, Zhang Aiping, Xu Liqing, Zhang Su, Liu Geping, Xiao Xiangrong, Wang Feng, Shen Bocun, Liu Qiren, and Wu Tao. A memorial service will be held on August 22, and a public memorial ceremony will be held at 9:00 a.m. on August 23.

### 全国人民代表大会代表朱早观逝世

1955-08-21
第1版()
专栏：

　　全国人民代表大会代表朱早观逝世
    新华社二十日讯　全国人民代表大会代表、全国人民代表大会民族委员会委员、国防部办公厅副主任朱早观，因患心脏病，在一九五五年八月二十日十二时在北京逝世。
    朱早观治丧委员会由黄克诚、甘泗淇、张爱萍、徐立清、张苏、刘格平、萧向荣、汪锋、申伯纯、刘其人、吴涛组成。订于八月二十二日开吊，八月二十三日上午九时公祭。
