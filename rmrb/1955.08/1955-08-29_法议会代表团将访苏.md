Title: French Parliamentary Delegation to Visit Soviet Union
Author:
Date: 1955-08-29
Page: 4

　　Xinhua News Agency, August 27th. According to a TASS report on August 25th: The French Parliament has accepted the invitation of the Supreme Soviet of the Soviet Union and decided to send a delegation to visit the Soviet Union between September 10th and 30th this year.
　　In the reply letters from the Speaker of the National Assembly of France, Schneiter, and the Speaker of the Senate of the Republic, Monnerville, to the Chairman of the Soviet of the Union of the Supreme Soviet of the Soviet Union, Volkov, and the Chairman of the Soviet of Nationalities, Lacis, it was stated that, in order to show the French Parliament's importance to this first meeting, the Speaker of the National Assembly, with the consent of the Speaker of the Senate, will personally lead this delegation.

### 法议会代表团将访苏

1955-08-29
第4版()
专栏：

　  法议会代表团将访苏
    新华社二十七日讯　据塔斯社二十五日报道：法国议会已经接受苏联最高苏维埃的邀请，决定派遣代表团在今年九月十日至三十日期间访问苏联。
    苏联最高苏维埃联盟院主席沃尔科夫和民族院主席拉齐斯收到的法国国民议会议长施奈特和共和国参议院议长蒙纳维的复信中说，为了表示法国议会对这个第一次会见的重视，国民议会议长将在取得参议院议长同意下，自己率领这个代表团。
