Title: Japanese Ms. Natsu Kawasaki and Others Arrive in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1955-08-04
Page: 1
    Japanese delegation to the World Mother's Congress, led by Ms. Natsu Kawasaki, the head of the delegation, arrived in Beijing by plane on the afternoon of the 2nd. They are visiting China at the invitation of the All-China Democratic Women's Federation. The second group of eight representatives and one attendant were greeted at the airport by Luo Qiong and Cao Mengjun, secretaries of the All-China Democratic Women's Federation, and Yang Yunyu, secretary of the International Democratic Women's Federation, among others.

    Another dispatch: At the invitation of the All-China Democratic Women's Federation to visit China, the Japanese delegation to the World Mother's Congress, including five representatives, had already arrived in Beijing by plane on July 27, led by Vice-Chairman Katsuo Hattori, in order to participate in the World Congress against Atomic and Hydrogen Bombs held in Hiroshima on August 6, and left Beijing for home on the 30th.

### 日本河崎夏女士等到京

1955-08-04
第1版()
专栏：

    日本河崎夏女士等到京
    本报讯　出席世界母亲大会后应中华全国民主妇女联合会邀请来中国参观访问的日本代表团第二批代表八人、随员一人，由团长河崎夏女士率领，在二日下午乘飞机到达北京。前往机场欢迎的有中华全国民主妇女联合会书记处书记罗琼、曹孟君，国际民主妇联书记杨蕴玉等多人。
    又讯　应中华全国民主妇女联合会邀请前来我国参观访问的出席世界母亲大会的日本代表团，其中有五位代表因要参加八月六日在广岛举行的禁止原子弹和氢弹世界大会，已在副团长服部胜尾率领下，于七月二十七日乘飞机先行到达北京，三十日离京返国。
