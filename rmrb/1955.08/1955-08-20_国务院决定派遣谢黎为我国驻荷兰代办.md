Title: The State Council Decides to Appoint Xie Li as Chargé d'affaires to the Netherlands

Author:
Date: 1955-08-20
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, August 19th—The State Council of the People's Republic of China has decided to appoint Xie Li as Chargé d'affaires of the People's Republic of China to the Netherlands.
