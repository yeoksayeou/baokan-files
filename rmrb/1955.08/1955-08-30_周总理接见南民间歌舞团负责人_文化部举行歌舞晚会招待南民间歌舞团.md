Title: Premier Zhou Receives Leaders of Yugoslav Folk Song and Dance Troupe; Ministry of Culture Holds Song and Dance Soiree to Welcome the Yugoslav Folk Song and Dance Troupe
Author:
Date: 1955-08-30
Page: 1
　
Xinhua News Agency, August 29th—Premier Zhou Enlai of the People's Republic of China received Olga Skovran, the head of the Yugoslav "Kolo" Folk Song and Dance Troupe, on the afternoon of August 29th.

Also present at the reception were: Žorko Milanović, leader of the dance troupe's orchestra; Dobrivoje Putnik, ballet director and orchestral leader; Lazar Jakošić, manager of the dance troupe; Stepan Duzević and Stanko Milanović, members of the dance troupe's committee; and Yelena Vujošević, Miodrag Despotović, and Miodrag Vilković, performers of the dance troupe.

Also present at the reception were Chen Yi, Vice Premier of the State Council; Xi Zhongxun, Secretary-General; Qian Junrui, Acting Minister of Culture; Xia Yan, Vice Minister; He Wei, Assistant Minister of Foreign Affairs; Chen Zhongjing, Acting Director of the Bureau of Foreign Cultural Relations; and Zhou Weizhi, Deputy Director of the Arts Administration Bureau of the Ministry of Culture.

Yugoslav Ambassador to China, Popović, was also present at the reception.

Xinhua News Agency, August 29th—The Ministry of Culture of the People's Republic of China held a song and dance soiree on the evening of August 29th to welcome all members of the Yugoslav "Kolo" Folk Song and Dance Troupe, led by Skovran. Yugoslav Ambassador to China, Popović, also attended the soiree.

At the soiree, the Song and Dance Troupe of the Chinese People's Liberation Army and the Central Experimental Opera House performed many wonderful music, dance, and song programs, which were warmly welcomed.

Accompanying the guests were Qian Junrui, Acting Minister of Culture, and Vice Ministers Xia Yan and Chen Kehan. (Attached image)

Top image: Premier Zhou Enlai receives Olga Skovran, head of the Yugoslav "Kolo" Folk Song and Dance Troupe, and others. From left to right: Zhou Enlai, Skovran, Popović.

### 周总理接见南民间歌舞团负责人　文化部举行歌舞晚会招待南民间歌舞团

1955-08-30
第1版()
专栏：

　　周总理接见南民间歌舞团负责人
　　文化部举行歌舞晚会招待南民间歌舞团
    新华社二十九日讯　中华人民共和国国务院总理周恩来，在二十九日下午接见了南斯拉夫“科罗”民间歌舞团团长奥尔加·斯科夫兰。
    受接见的还有：歌舞团乐队领导人若尔科·米兰诺维奇，巴蕾舞导演和管弦乐队领导人多布里沃埃·普特尼克，歌舞团经理拉查尔·雅科什奇，歌舞团委员会委员斯捷潘·杜泽维奇和斯坦科·米兰诺维奇，歌舞团演员叶莲娜·伍伊奥歇维奇、米奥德拉尔·杰斯波托维奇和米奥德拉尔·维尔科维奇。
    接见时在座的，有国务院副总理陈毅、秘书长习仲勋，文化部代理部长钱俊瑞、副部长夏衍，外交部部长助理何伟，对外文化联络局代理局长陈忠经和文化部艺术事业管理局副局长周巍峙。
    接见时，南斯拉夫驻华大使波波维奇也在座。
    新华社二十九日讯　中华人民共和国文化部在二十九日晚举行歌舞晚会，招待由斯科夫兰率领的南斯拉夫“科罗”民间歌舞团全体人员。南斯拉夫驻华大使波波维奇也出席了晚会。
    晚会上，中国人民解放军歌舞团和中央实验歌剧院演出了许多精采的音乐、舞蹈和歌唱节目，受到热烈欢迎。
    陪同客人们观剧的，有文化部代理部长钱俊瑞、副部长夏衍、陈克寒。（附图片）
