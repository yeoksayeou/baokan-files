Title: The Central Committee of the Korean Fatherland United Democratic Front and the Korean National Peace Committee Send a Telegram to the People of South Korea to Congratulate Them on the "August 15th" Anniversary
Author:
Date: 1955-08-15
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency, Pyongyang, August 13th: The Korean Central News Agency reports that the Central Committee of the Korean Fatherland United Democratic Front and the Korean National Peace Committee sent a telegram to the people of South Korea to congratulate them on the tenth anniversary of the "August 15th" Liberation.

    The telegram stated: We warmly support your patriotic struggle over the past decade to strive for the peaceful reunification and freedom of the fatherland, to oppose the division of territory, national division, and the colonial enslavement policies of foreign aggressors and the traitorous Syngman Rhee.

    The Central Committee of the Korean Fatherland United Democratic Front and the Korean National Peace Committee wish the people of South Korea even more brilliant achievements in further expanding and strengthening the ranks of the struggle to save the nation by opposing war and imperialism, and in the struggle to closely unite around the government of the Republic to strive for the peaceful reunification and independence of the fatherland.

### 朝鲜祖国统一战线中央和朝鲜拥护和平全国民族委员会  致电南朝鲜人民祝贺“八一五”

1955-08-15
第4版()
专栏：

    朝鲜祖国统一战线中央和朝鲜拥护和平全国民族委员会
    致电南朝鲜人民祝贺“八一五”
    新华社平壤十三日电  朝鲜中央通讯社讯：朝鲜祖国统一民主主义战线中央委员会和朝鲜拥护和平全国民族委员会拍电报给南朝鲜人民祝贺“八一五”解放十周年。
    贺电说：我们热烈支持你们在过去十年中为争取祖国的和平统一和自由、反对领土分割、国家分裂以及外国侵略者的殖民奴役政策和卖国贼李承晚而进行的爱国斗争。
    朝鲜祖国统一民主主义战线中央委员会和朝鲜拥护和平全国民族委员会祝南朝鲜人民在进一步扩大和加强反对战争、反对帝国主义者的救国斗争的队伍方面，在紧密团结在共和国政府周围争取祖国和平统一和独立的斗争方面，取得更加辉煌的成就。
