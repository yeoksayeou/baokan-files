Title: International Trade Promotion Committee Decides to Strengthen Activities
Author:
Date: 1955-08-01
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency, Vienna, 30th August - The International Trade Promotion Committee issued a statement saying: The Standing Committee of the International Trade Promotion Committee held a meeting in Vienna from July 25th to 27th.

The statement said: The Standing Committee considered the new prospects opened up by the relaxation of international tensions for the expansion of world trade. In this regard, the Standing Committee paid particular attention to the issue of East-West trade and noted the growing interest of economic and business figures throughout Europe in broadly discussing these issues.

In view of the new opportunities to promote international trade relations - a result of the improvement in the international situation, the Standing Committee decided to strengthen the activities of the International Trade Promotion Committee.

The Standing Committee has established a prize to solicit papers on the following topic: "International Trade from the Perspective of Promoting Peaceful Coexistence and Raising Living Standards."

### 国际贸易促进委员会决定加强活动

1955-08-01
第4版()
专栏：

    国际贸易促进委员会决定加强活动
    新华社三十一日讯　据塔斯社维也纳三十日讯：国际贸易促进委员会发表声明说：国际贸易促进委员会常务委员会七月二十五日到二十七日在维也纳举行了会议。
    声明说：常务委员会考虑了国际紧张局势的缓和为扩大世界贸易所打开的新的前景。在这方面，常务委员会特别考虑了东西方贸易问题，并且注意到欧洲各地经济和实业界人士对于广泛讨论这些问题所感到的日益增长的兴趣。
    鉴于促进国际贸易关系方面的新机会——这是国际局势改善的结果，常务委员会决定加强国际贸易促进委员会的活动。
    常务委员会已经设立一份奖金，征求关于下一题目的论文：“从促进和平共处和提高生活水平方面来看国际贸易。”
