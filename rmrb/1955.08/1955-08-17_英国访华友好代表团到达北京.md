Title: British Goodwill Delegation Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1955-08-17
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, August 16th. The British Goodwill Delegation, invited by the Chinese People's Committee for World Peace, arrived in Beijing by plane on the afternoon of the 16th, led by head of the delegation, Maurice Orbach, and deputy head Kathleen Lonsdale. Liu Guan-yi, Secretary-General of the Chinese People's Committee for World Peace, and many others went to the airport to welcome them. After disembarking, the guests accepted flowers presented by the welcomers.

### 英国访华友好代表团到达北京

1955-08-17
第1版()
专栏：

    英国访华友好代表团到达北京
    新华社十六日讯　应中国人民保卫世界和平委员会邀请的英国访华友好代表团在团长莫里斯·奥巴奇和副团长凯思林·朗斯—戴尔率领下，于十六日下午乘飞机到达北京。前往机场欢迎的有中国人民保卫世界和平委员会秘书长刘贯一等多人。客人们下机后接受了欢迎人们的献花。
