Title: Order of the State Council of the People's Republic of China
Author:
Date: 1955-08-25
Page: 2

The "Provisional Measures for the Unified Purchase and Sale of Grain in Rural Areas" was adopted at the 17th Plenary Meeting of the State Council on August 5, 1955. It is hereby promulgated, and it is hoped that it will be conscientiously implemented.

 	Premier Zhou Enlai
 	August 25, 1955

### 中华人民共和国国务院命令

1955-08-25
第2版()
专栏：

    中华人民共和国国务院命令
    农村粮食统购统销暂行办法，已于一九五五年八月五日经国务院全体会议第十七次会议通过，现予发布，希认真贯彻执行。
                                      总理　周恩来
                                    一九五五年八月二十五日
