Title: Indian Volunteers Continue to Enter Goa
Author:
Date: 1955-08-21
Page: 4
　
　　Xinhua News Agency, New Delhi, August 20th: According to the decision of the Indian parties' Support Goa Liberation Committee, seventy of the 1,500 Indian volunteers gathered on the Indian-Goan border entered Goa on the 19th to continue the peaceful resistance movement.
    Strikes, demonstrations, and rallies continued to be held across India on the 18th to protest the Portuguese authorities' atrocities. Madras held a strike. Shops and schools were closed, and more than 100,000 workers left factories. Students held demonstrations, chanting slogans against Portugal.
    Strikes were also held in Guwahati, Karnal, Cuttack, and Moradabad.

### 印度一批志愿人员继续进入果阿

1955-08-21
第4版()
专栏：

　　印度一批志愿人员继续进入果阿
    新华社新德里二十日电　按照印度各党派支援解放果阿委员会的决定，聚集在印度和果阿边境的一千五百名印度志愿人员中有七十人在十九日进入了果阿，继续进行和平抵抗运动。
    印度各地在十八日继续举行了罢工、示威游行和集会，抗议葡萄牙当局的暴行。马德拉斯举行了罢市。商店和学校都关了门，十多万工人离开了工厂。学生们举行了示威游行，他们高呼反对葡萄牙的口号。
    高哈蒂、卡纳尔、克塔克和谟拉达巴德等地也举行了罢市。
