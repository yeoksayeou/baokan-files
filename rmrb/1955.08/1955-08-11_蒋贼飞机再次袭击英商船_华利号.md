Title: Chiang Kai-shek Bandit Aircraft Again Attacks British Merchant Ship "Wah Lee"
Author: 
Date: 1955-08-11
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, August 6th. The British merchant ship "Wah Lee" was attacked again by Chiang Kai-shek bandit aircraft near Fuzhou on the 3rd.

	That afternoon, two Chiang Kai-shek bandit aircraft bombed and strafed the British merchant ship, causing damage to the ship and injuring two crew members.

	The British Consul in Tamsui, Herman, lodged a protest with the Chiang Kai-shek bandit authorities in Taiwan on the morning of the 5th.

	The "Wah Lee" had previously been attacked by four Chiang Kai-shek bandit aircraft on July 9th.

### 蒋贼飞机再次袭击英商船“华利号”

1955-08-11
第1版()
专栏：

    蒋贼飞机再次袭击英商船“华利号”
    新华社六日讯　英国商船“华利号”三日在福州附近再一次遭到蒋贼飞机的袭击。
    这天下午，两架蒋贼飞机对这艘英国商船投弹和扫射，船只遭到损伤，还有两名船员受伤。
    驻淡水的英国领事赫尔曼五日上午向台湾蒋贼当局提出了抗议。
    “华利号”曾经在七月九日遭到过四架蒋贼飞机的袭击。
