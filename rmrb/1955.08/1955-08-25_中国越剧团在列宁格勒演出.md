Title: Chinese Yue Opera Troupe Performs in Leningrad
Author:
Date: 1955-08-25
Page: 3

　　Chinese Yue Opera Troupe Performs in Leningrad
　　The Chinese Yue Opera Troupe performed the Yue Opera "Romance of the West Chamber" on August 20th in the hall of the Rimsky-Korsakov Conservatory in Leningrad. This was the first performance of the Chinese Yue Opera Troupe after arriving in Leningrad.
　　The performance was a great success.
　　(Xinhua News Agency, Leningrad, based on TASS report) (Attached picture)
　　The Chinese Yue Opera Troupe, led by Xu Guangping, arrived in Moscow on August 6, 1955. Photo by V. Savostyanov, TASS (Xinhua News Agency)

### 中国越剧团在列宁格勒演出

1955-08-25
第3版()
专栏：

　　中国越剧团在列宁格勒演出
    中国越剧团于八月二十日在列宁格勒的林斯基—科尔萨科夫音乐院的大厅里演出越剧“西厢记”。这是中国越剧团到达列宁格勒后的首次演出。
    演出获得了很大的成功。
          （新华社据塔斯社列宁格勒讯）（附图片）
　　由许广平率领的中国越剧团在一九五五年八月六日抵达莫斯科。维·萨沃斯扬诺夫摄塔斯社稿（新华社发）
