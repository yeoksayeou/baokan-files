Title: Our Country's Cultural Delegation Received a Warm Welcome in Medan and Other Places; Head and Deputy Head of the Delegation Bid Farewell to President Sukarno

Author:

Date: 1955-08-13
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency: During its week in Medan, the Chinese Cultural Delegation held four public performances, causing a sensation in this largest city in northern Sumatra. Approximately 60,000 people attended the performances, accounting for more than 10% of Medan's population.

The delegation's performances were unanimously praised by the audience and more than a dozen newspapers in Medan. Amin, the Governor of North Sumatra Province, said, "The performance of the Chinese Cultural Delegation is perfect." He said that the performance, makeup, and costumes were all very harmonious and pleasing to the eye.

Xinhua News Agency: The Chinese Cultural Delegation visited and performed in Bukittinggi, the capital of Central Sumatra Province, on the 9th. This was the last city the delegation visited and performed in during its tour of Indonesia. Ruslan Muljohardjo, the Governor of Central Sumatra Province, watched the performance and held a reception for the Cultural Delegation after the performance.

In his speech at the reception, Muljohardjo said that the Chinese artists' acting and music were perfect, and the success of the performance was beyond words. He also said that the visit of the Chinese Cultural Delegation undoubtedly promoted mutual understanding and friendship between the people of the two countries. He also expressed hope that the Cultural Delegation would come to Indonesia again in the future.

In his reply speech, Zheng Zhenduo, the head of the Chinese Cultural Delegation, expressed his gratitude to the government and people of Central Sumatra Province for their warm hospitality.

At the reception, the hosts solemnly treated the Chinese Cultural Delegation with traditional hospitality for distinguished guests. Two young men led ten girls wearing brightly colored national costumes to the members of the Cultural Delegation. The two young men held trays filled with betel nuts covered with colorful ribbons. They invited the members of the delegation to touch the ribbons covering the betel nuts with their hands.

The Cultural Delegation left Bukittinggi in two groups by plane on the 10th and 11th and returned to Jakarta, the capital of Indonesia.

Xinhua News Agency, Jakarta, 12th: Zheng Zhenduo, head of the Chinese Cultural Delegation, and Zhou Erfu, deputy head of the delegation, went to the Bogor Presidential Palace on the morning of the 11th to bid farewell to Indonesian President Sukarno. Huang Zhen, the Chinese Ambassador to Indonesia, and Zhong Qingfa, the Counselor, also accompanied them.

### 我国文化代表团在棉兰等地演出受到欢迎  代表团正副团长向苏加诺总统辞行

1955-08-13
第4版()
专栏：

    我国文化代表团在棉兰等地演出受到欢迎
    代表团正副团长向苏加诺总统辞行
    据新华社讯　中国文化代表团在棉兰的一星期期间，举行了四次公开演出，轰动了这个苏门答腊北部最大的城市。前往观看演出的约有六万人，占棉兰人口的百分之十以上。
    代表团的演出受到观众和棉兰十几家报纸的一致赞扬。北苏门答腊省省长阿明说：“中国文化代表团的演出是完美的。”他说，表演、化装和服装都配合得非常和谐，并且是非常悦目的。
    据新华社讯　中国文化代表团九日在中苏门答腊省省会武吉丁宜访问和演出。这是代表团在印度尼西亚进行访问和演出的最后一个城市。中苏门答腊省省长鲁斯兰·穆利约哈佐观看了演出，并且在演出结束后举行招待会招待文化代表团。
    穆利约哈佐在招待会上致词说，中国艺术家的演技和音乐是十全十美的，演出的成功不能用言语来形容。他又说，中国文化代表团的访问无疑地促进了两国人民相互之间的谅解和友谊。他并且希望文化代表团将来再到印度尼西亚。
    中国文化代表团团长郑振铎在致答词时对中苏门答腊省政府和人民的热情招待表示感谢。
    在招待会上，主人以招待贵宾的传统礼节隆重地招待中国文化代表团。两个青年男子带着十个穿着光彩夺目的民族服装的姑娘走向文化代表团团员们的面前。这两个青年男子手里捧着满盛着槟榔上盖彩带的盘子。他们请团员们用手按一下盖着槟榔的彩带。
    文化代表团于十日和十一日分两批乘飞机离开了武吉丁宜回到印度尼西亚首都雅加达。
    新华社雅加达十二日电　中国文化代表团团长郑振铎和副团长周而复十一日上午到茂物总统行邸向印度尼西亚总统苏加诺辞行。中国驻印度尼西亚大使黄镇和参赞锺庆发也陪同前往。
