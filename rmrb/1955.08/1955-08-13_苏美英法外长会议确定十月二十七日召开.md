Title: Four-Power Foreign Ministers' Meeting Set for October 27th
Author:
Date: 1955-08-13
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, August 11th: After consultations through diplomatic channels, the governments of the French Republic, the United Kingdom, the Union of Soviet Socialist Republics, and the United States of America have unanimously agreed that the meeting of the four Foreign Ministers, as stipulated in the instructions approved by the heads of government on July 23rd, will be held in Geneva on Thursday, October 27th, 1955. They have also unanimously agreed that the Foreign Ministers' meeting will utilize the services of the joint secretariat of the four powers.

### 苏美英法外长会议确定十月二十七日召开

1955-08-13
第4版()
专栏：

    苏美英法外长会议确定十月二十七日召开
    新华社十二日讯　塔斯社莫斯科十一日讯：法兰西共和国、联合王国、苏维埃社会主义共和国联盟和美利坚合众国四国政府在通过外交途径进行磋商以后，一致同意：政府首脑们七月二十三日通过的指示所规定的四大国外长会议将在一九五五年十月二十七日（星期四）在日内瓦召开。他们还一致同意：外长会议将利用四大国联合秘书处的服务。
