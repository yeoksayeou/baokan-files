Title: Guangdong Xinhui Palm-leaf Fans (Picture)
Author: Liang Feng
Date: 1955-08-11
Page: 2

    Guangdong Xinhui Palm-leaf Fans
    Palm-leaf fans are a specialty of Xinhui County, Guangdong Province, with an annual output of approximately 50 million units, sold domestically and in Southeast Asia, the Americas, and Africa. The local state-owned palm-leaf fan factory is further expanding production to meet domestic and international needs.

    广东新会葵扇
