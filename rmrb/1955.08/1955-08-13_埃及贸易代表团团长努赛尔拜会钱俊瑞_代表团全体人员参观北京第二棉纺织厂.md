Title: Egyptian Trade Delegation Head Nuseir Visits Qian Junrui; Delegation Visits Beijing No. 2 Cotton Textile Factory

Author:
Date: 1955-08-13
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, August 12th: The head of the Egyptian Republic Government Trade Delegation, Egyptian Minister of Industry and Commerce Mohammed Abu Nuseir, paid a visit to Qian Junrui, Acting Minister of Culture of the People's Republic of China, at 4:00 PM on August 12th.

Present at the meeting were Ding Xilin, Vice Minister of Culture; Huang Songling, Vice Minister of Higher Education; Zhang Jiafu, Vice President of the Chinese Academy of Sciences; Huang Hua, Director of the Department of West European and African Affairs of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs; and Zhang Jie, Secretary-General of the China Islamic Association.

Xinhua News Agency, August 12th: All members of the Egyptian Republic Government Trade Delegation today visited the state-owned Beijing No. 2 Cotton Textile Factory, which has nearly 100,000 spindles.

The head of the delegation, Egyptian Minister of Industry and Commerce Mohammed Abu Nuseir, highly praised this large factory, which can produce 191 bales of cotton yarn and 6,000 bolts of cotton cloth every day. He expressed his hope that in the near future, "the textile factory can use Egyptian cotton to weave better cloth to supply the Chinese people."

Ahmed Najib, a member of the delegation and director of the Egyptian Cotton Commission, said that he had visited many cotton textile factories in many countries, but he had never seen such good production order as in this textile factory. He expressed great admiration for the fact that all the factory's machinery and equipment were made in China.

The guests arrived at the factory at 2:00 PM. Accompanied by their hosts, they carefully visited each production process of the factory. In the opening and cleaning workshop, the guests carefully studied the fibers of Chinese cotton; in the manufacturing workshop, they carefully admired the newly woven plain cloth. The various cotton textile machines made in China and installed in each spacious workshop aroused great interest among the guests.

Before the visit, Song Ting, the director of the textile factory's preparatory office, introduced the factory's situation to the guests.

### 埃及贸易代表团团长努赛尔拜会钱俊瑞  代表团全体人员参观北京第二棉纺织厂

1955-08-13
第1版()
专栏：

    埃及贸易代表团团长努赛尔拜会钱俊瑞
    代表团全体人员参观北京第二棉纺织厂
    新华社十二日讯　埃及共和国政府贸易代表团团长、埃及工商部部长穆罕默德·阿卜·努赛尔，在十二日下午四时拜会了中华人民共和国文化部代理部长钱俊瑞。
    拜会时在座的，有文化部副部长丁西林，高等教育部副部长黄松龄，中国科学院副院长张稼夫，外交部西欧非洲司司长黄华和中国伊斯兰教协会秘书长张杰等。
    新华社十二日讯　埃及共和国政府贸易代表团全体人员，今天参观了拥有近十万纱锭的国营北京第二棉纺织厂。
    代表团团长、埃及工商部长穆罕默德·阿卜·努赛尔对这个每天能生产一百九十一件棉纱、六千匹棉布的巨大工厂，备加称赞，他希望在不久以后，“纺织厂能用埃及的棉花织出更好的布匹供应中国人民”。
    代表团团员、埃及棉花委员会主任艾哈迈德·纳吉布说，他曾参观过许多国家的许多棉纺织厂，但他从来没有看到像这个纺织厂这样好的生产秩序。他对工厂的全部机械设备都是中国自己制造的，表示非常敬佩。
    客人们是在下午二时到达工厂的。他们在主人陪同下，仔细地参观了工厂的各个生产工序。在清花车间，客人们仔细地研究了中国棉花的纤维；在制造车间仔细地欣赏了新织好的平布。各个宽敞的车间里安装的中国自己制造的各棉纺织机械，更引起了客人们的很大兴趣。
    在参观以前，纺织厂筹建处处长宋汀曾向客人们介绍了这个工厂的情况。
