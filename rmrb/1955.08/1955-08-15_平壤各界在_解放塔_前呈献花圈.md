Title: Pyongyang Circles Present Wreaths at the "Liberation Tower"
Author:
Date: 1955-08-15
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, Pyongyang, August 14th: Circles in Pyongyang presented wreaths at the "Liberation Tower" on Moranbong Hill this morning to commemorate the great Soviet army's liberation of Korea.

	At 10 a.m., amidst solemn military music, Park Chang-ok, Kim Il, Choi Chang-ik, Park Kum-chul, Park Young-bin, and others, representing Marshal Kim Il-sung, the Korean Workers' Party, and the government of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea, first presented a wreath.

	Then, the delegation of the People's Republic of China, invited to Korea to participate in the celebration of the 10th anniversary of the liberation of Korea, presented a wreath, represented by delegation members Teng Daiyuan, Ji Pengfei, and Pan Zili.

	Also presenting wreaths were the Soviet, Chinese, and other countries' ambassadors and diplomatic officials stationed in Korea.

	Afterward, representatives of various democratic parties and social organizations in Korea, as well as the working people of Pyongyang, presented wreaths at the "Liberation Tower."

### 平壤各界在“解放塔”前呈献花圈

1955-08-15
第1版()
专栏：

    平壤各界在“解放塔”前呈献花圈
    新华社平壤十四日电  平壤各界十四日上午在牡丹峰上纪念伟大苏军解放朝鲜的“解放塔”前呈献花圈。
    上午十时，在庄严的军乐声中，首先由朴昌玉、金一、崔昌益、朴金哲、朴永彬等代表金日成元帅、朝鲜劳动党和朝鲜民主主义人民共和国政府呈献花圈。
    接着，应邀来朝鲜参加朝鲜解放十周年庆祝典礼的中华人民共和国代表团由团员滕代远、姬鹏飞、潘自力代表呈献花圈。
    呈献花圈的还有苏联、中国和各国驻朝使节和外交官员。
    然后，朝鲜各民主政党和社会团体以及平壤市劳动人民的代表在“解放塔”前呈献了花圈。
