Title: People from All Walks of Life in Lüda Celebrate the Tenth Anniversary of the Soviet Army's Liberation of Lüda
Author:
Date: 1955-08-24
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, Lüda, August 23rd. More than 1,500 people from all walks of life in Lüda City held a celebration meeting for the tenth anniversary of the "August 22nd" Soviet Army's liberation of Lüda at the Lüda People's Cultural Club on the evening of the 21st. Party and government leaders of Lüda City attended the meeting. Soviet Consul General to Dalian, Belyanov, and some Soviet experts in Lüda were also invited to attend the celebration.

　　Song Li, Second Secretary of the CPC Lüda Municipal Committee and Mayor of Lüda City, spoke at the meeting. He first expressed, on behalf of the people of Lüda, heartfelt gratitude to the liberators and sincere helpers of the people of Lüda—the great Soviet Union and its armed forces. After outlining the situation of the Soviet army guarding Lüda and helping the people of Lüda in their construction over the past ten years, Song Li said: When celebrating the tenth anniversary of the Soviet army's liberation of Lüda, we cannot help but be filled with boundless respect and remembrance for the Soviet army. In the future, we firmly believe that under the leadership of the Chinese Communist Party, we will continue to promote and consolidate this precious Sino-Soviet friendship, and better exert the infinite power generated by this friendship in safeguarding peace and our socialist construction.

　　Representatives of the Chinese People's Liberation Army stationed in Lüda and Soviet Consul General to Dalian, Belyanov, also spoke at the celebration meeting.

　　On the eve of this celebration meeting, more than 50,000 workers, students, government employees, and residents of Lüda City held a garden party in Labor Park to celebrate the tenth anniversary of the Soviet Army's liberation of Lüda. Many amateur troupes performed songs, dances, dramas, and other programs on open-air stages and by the lotus pond in the park.

### 旅大市各界人民庆祝苏军解放旅大十周年

1955-08-24
第1版()
专栏：

　　旅大市各界人民庆祝苏军解放旅大十周年
    新华社旅大二十三日电　旅大市各界人民一千五百多人，二十一日晚在旅大人民文化俱乐部里举行了庆祝“八二二”苏军解放旅大十周年大会。旅大市的党、政负责人都参加了大会。苏联驻大连总领事别良诺夫和在旅大的部分苏联专家也应邀出席了这个庆祝会。
    中共旅大市委员会第二书记、旅大市市长宋黎在会上讲话。他首先代表旅大人民向旅大人民的解放者和真诚的援助者——伟大的苏联及其武装部队致以衷心的感谢。宋黎在概述了十年来苏军守卫旅大和帮助旅大人民建设的情况后说：庆祝苏军解放旅大十周年的时候，不能不激起我们对苏军的无限敬爱和怀念。今后，我们坚信在中国共产党的领导下，一定会把这种珍贵的中苏友谊继续发扬和巩固下去，把这种友谊所产生的无穷的力量更好地发挥在保卫和平和我们的社会主义建设事业上。
    在庆祝会上讲话的还有中国人民解放军驻旅大部队代表和苏联驻大连总领事别良诺夫。
    在这个庆祝会的前夕，旅大市的工人、学生、机关工作人员和居民等五万多人，在劳动公园里举行了庆祝苏军解放旅大十周年的游园晚会。许多业余剧团在公园里的露天讲台上和荷花池畔演出了歌舞戏剧等节目。
