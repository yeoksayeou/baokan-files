Title: US Military Conducts Large-Scale Combat Exercises on Okinawa

Author: 
Date: 1955-08-18
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Naha, August 17th: U.S. forces stationed in Okinawa conducted the largest amphibious combat exercise since the war on Okinawa Island on the 16th. The exercise involved U.S. Army, Navy, and Air Force units.

According to the Associated Press, the Chiang Kai-shek traitorous clique also sent four jet fighters to participate in the exercise.

### 美军在冲绳岛进行大规模作战演习

1955-08-18
第4版()
专栏：

    美军在冲绳岛进行大规模作战演习
    新华社十七日讯　那霸消息：美国驻在冲绳岛的陆、海、空军部队，十六日在冲绳岛进行了战后规模最大的一次两栖作战演习。
    据美联社报道，蒋介石卖国集团也派了四架喷气式战斗机参加了这次演习。
