Title: PLA General Political Department and the Ministry of Culture of China Host a Banquet for the Albanian People's Army Song and Dance Troupe
Author:
Date: 1955-08-15
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, August 14th. The General Political Department of the Chinese People's Liberation Army and the Ministry of Culture of the People's Republic of China jointly held a banquet on the evening of the 14th to welcome the Albanian People's Army Song and Dance Troupe, led by Lieutenant Colonel Mekur Çela.

Nesti Nase, the Ambassador of the People's Republic of Albania to China, and the Albanian cultural delegation headed by Fadil Paçrami were invited to attend the banquet.

Xiao Hua, Deputy Director of the General Political Department of the Chinese People's Liberation Army, delivered a speech welcoming the distinguished guests. Xiao Hua said: "The Chinese people and their army are watching every victory of the People's Republic of Albania in socialist construction with joy and pleasure, and regard the victory of the Albanian people as the victory of their own cause. This victory has brought encouragement to the fraternal Chinese people and the Chinese People's Liberation Army. Let us join hands with the Soviet Union, other fraternal countries, and peace-loving people all over the world to advance together."

Qian Junrui, Acting Minister of the Ministry of Culture of the People's Republic of China, expressed warm welcome and respect to the representatives and cultural envoys of the heroic Albanian people and their army in his speech, and raised a glass to wish the Albanian People's Army Song and Dance Troupe great achievements in their visit to our country, good health to all comrades of the song and dance troupe, and greater success to the People's Republic of Albania in its socialist construction.

Mekur Çela, leader and head of the Albanian People's Army Song and Dance Troupe, said in his reply that he was honored to have the opportunity to meet representatives of the Chinese People's Liberation Army and Chinese literary and art circles, and thanked them for their warm welcome.

During the banquet, Xiao Hua, Fadil Paçrami, Mei Lanfang, Nesti Nase, Qian Junrui, Lin Feng, Gaqo Avrazi and others successively proposed toasts to the friendship between the Chinese and Albanian people and their armies, to the development of cultural exchanges between China and Albania and the health of literary and art workers of the two countries, to the victory of socialist construction in China and Albania, and to the health of the leaders of the Chinese and Albanian people, Mao Zedong and Enver Hoxha. When Fadil Paçrami and Lin Feng proposed a toast to the health of the leaders of the two peoples, the audience stood up and applauded and cheered enthusiastically for a long time.

More than 200 people attended the banquet tonight, including Lin Feng, Director of the Second Office of the State Council, Xiao Ke, Deputy Minister of National Defense, Chen Geng, Deputy Chief of General Staff of the Chinese People's Liberation Army, Xiao Xiangrong, Director of the General Office of the Ministry of National Defense, Liu Zhijian, Director of the Propaganda Department of the General Political Department of the Chinese People's Liberation Army, Chen Yi, Minister of Culture, Yang Xiufeng, Minister of Higher Education, Xia Yan, Deputy Minister of Culture, He Wei, Assistant Minister of Foreign Affairs, Chen Zhongjing, Acting Director of the Bureau of Foreign Cultural Relations, Wang Kunlun and Feng Jiping, Deputy Mayors of Beijing, as well as people from the capital's literary and art and news circles.

### 解放军总政治部和我国文化部  欢宴阿尔巴尼亚人民军歌舞团

1955-08-15
第1版()
专栏：

    解放军总政治部和我国文化部
    欢宴阿尔巴尼亚人民军歌舞团
    新华社十四日讯  中国人民解放军总政治部和中华人民共和国文化部十四日晚联合举行宴会，欢迎由梅库尔·柴拉中校率领的阿尔巴尼亚人民军歌舞团。
    阿尔巴尼亚人民共和国驻华大使纳赛，以法迪尔·帕奇拉米为首的阿尔巴尼亚文化代表团应邀出席了宴会。
    中国人民解放军总政治部副主任萧华致词欢迎贵宾们。萧华说：“中国人民及其军队怀着喜悦和愉快的心情，注视着阿尔巴尼亚人民共和国在社会主义建设中的每一个胜利，并把阿尔巴尼亚人民的胜利看成是自己切身事业的胜利。这个胜利给兄弟的中国人民和中国人民解放军带来了鼓舞。让我们同苏联、同其他兄弟国家以及全世界爱好和平的人民一起携手前进。
    中华人民共和国文化部代理部长钱俊瑞在致词中向英雄的阿尔巴尼亚人民及其军队的代表和文化使者表示热烈的欢迎和敬意，并举杯祝阿尔巴尼亚人民军歌舞团在访问我国的工作中获得巨大的成就，祝歌舞团全体同志健康，祝阿尔巴尼亚人民共和国在建设社会主义的事业中获得更大的成功。
    阿尔巴尼亚人民军歌舞团领队兼团长梅库尔·柴拉致答词时，表示对他们能有机会会见中国人民解放军的代表和中国文艺界人士感到荣幸，并感谢对他们的热烈欢迎。
    在宴会进行中，萧华、法迪尔·帕奇拉米、梅兰芳、纳赛、钱俊瑞、林枫、加乔·阿夫拉齐等相继提议为中、阿两国人民及其军队的友谊，为中、阿两国文化交流的发展及两国文艺工作者的健康，为中、阿两国社会主义建设的胜利，为中、阿两国人民领袖毛泽东和恩维尔·霍查的健康干杯。当法迪尔·帕奇拉米和林枫提议为两国人民领袖的健康干杯时，全场起立，长久地热烈鼓掌欢呼。
    出席今晚宴会的，有国务院第二办公室主任林枫，国防部副部长萧克，中国人民解放军副总参谋长陈赓，国防部办公厅主任萧向荣，中国人民解放军总政治部宣传部部长刘志坚、文化部部长陈沂，高等教育部部长杨秀峰，文化部副部长夏衍，外交部部长助理何伟，对外文化联络局代理局长陈忠经，北京市副市长王昆仑、冯基平，以及首都文艺、新闻界人士等二百多人。
