Title: Chinese Art Troupe Arrives in Hanoi to a Warm Welcome
Author:
Date: 1955-08-30
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Hanoi, August 28th: The Chinese Art Troupe, invited to visit the Democratic Republic of Vietnam, led by Ding Xilin, head of the troupe and Deputy Minister of the Ministry of Culture of the People's Republic of China, arrived in Hanoi at 5:00 PM on August 28th.

Those who greeted the troupe at the station included To Huu, Deputy Minister of Propaganda, Nguyen Khanh Toan, Deputy Minister of Education, Nguyen Thanh Ha, Director of the Department of Socialist and People's Democratic Countries of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs, and Vu Dinh Vinh, Director of the Protocol Department, as well as Thi San, President of the Vietnam-China Friendship Association, and leading figures and representatives from various mass organizations, totaling 2,000 people.

Xie Shuangqiu, Chargé d'Affaires ad interim of the Embassy of the People's Republic of China in the Democratic Republic of Vietnam, also welcomed the troupe at the station.

More than 10,000 Hanoi citizens had automatically gathered on the streets near the station to wait for the welcome two or three hours before the Chinese Art Troupe arrived in Hanoi.

When all the members of the Chinese Art Troupe stepped off the train, the welcomers waved flowers and flags and cheered enthusiastically. Young Pioneers rushed forward to present flowers to the members of the Chinese Art Troupe.

At the welcoming ceremony held at Hanoi Station, To Huu, Deputy Minister of Propaganda of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam, delivered a welcoming speech, saying that the Chinese Art Troupe's visit to Vietnam demonstrates the Chinese people and the Chinese government's heartfelt concern for the Vietnamese people's struggle to consolidate peace and achieve national reunification, as well as their peaceful construction endeavors. This visit will further consolidate the firm and close friendship between the Vietnamese and Chinese people. To Huu also expressed joy and gratitude that the Chinese Art Troupe would celebrate Vietnam's National Day with the Vietnamese people.

Ding Xilin, head of the Chinese Art Troupe, said in his reply that the Chinese Art Troupe's visit to Vietnam was to express the Chinese people's respect for the victories achieved by the Vietnamese people in their heroic struggle over the past decade, and to extend fraternal congratulations to the Vietnamese people on the achievements they have made in peaceful construction. Ding Xilin, on behalf of the Chinese Art Troupe, expressed his gratitude to the Vietnamese government and people for their grand and enthusiastic welcome.

Subsequently, members of the Central People's Art Troupe of Vietnam and the People's Army Art Troupe of Vietnam, as well as representatives of mass organizations, presented flowers to all members of the Chinese Art Troupe, and warmly embraced and shook hands with each other. At this moment, cheers rang out at the station, and hosts and guests sang "Unity is Strength" together in Vietnamese and Chinese.

When the members of the Chinese Art Troupe left the station for their residence, the welcoming crowds enthusiastically escorted them to their vehicles, and the thousands upon thousands of people gathered near the station kept cheering the members of the Chinese Art Troupe.

### 我国艺术团到达河内受到热烈欢迎

1955-08-30
第4版()
专栏：

　　我国艺术团到达河内受到热烈欢迎
    新华社河内二十八日电　应邀访问越南民主共和国的中国艺术团在团长、中华人民共和国文化部副部长丁西林率领下，已经在二十八日下午五时抵达河内。
    到车站迎接的有越南民主共和国政府宣传部副部长素友，教育部副部长阮庆全，外交部社会主义和人民民主国家司司长阮清河和礼宾司司长武廷莹，越中友好协会会长诗山，以及各群众团体的负责人和各界代表二千人。
    中华人民共和国驻越南民主共和国大使馆临时代办谢爽秋也到车站欢迎。
    有一万多河内市民在中国艺术团到达河内的两三小时以前，就从各处自动聚集到车站附近的街道上等候欢迎。
    当中国艺术团全体团员走下列车时，欢迎者挥着鲜花、旗帜，热烈的欢呼。少年儿童队队员们涌上来把鲜花献给中国艺术团人员。
    在河内车站举行的欢迎仪式上，越南民主共和国宣传部副部长素友致欢迎词说，中国艺术团这次来越南的访问，显示中国人民和中国政府对于越南人民为巩固和平、实现祖国统一的斗争以及和平建设事业的衷心关怀。这次访问将使越中两国人民牢固密密的友谊更加巩固。素友并且对中国艺术团将和越南人民共同欢度越南国庆节表示欢欣和感谢。
    中国艺术团团长丁西林致答词说，中国艺术团这次访问越南是为了表达中国人民对于越南人民十年来英勇斗争所取得的胜利的敬意，对于越南人民在和平建设上所取得的成就致以兄弟的祝贺。丁西林代表中国艺术团对越南政府和人民的盛大而热情的欢迎表示感谢。
    随后，越南中央人民文工团和越南人民军文工团的团员们以及群众团体的代表们向中国艺术团全体人员献花，互相热烈地拥抱和握手。这时车站上响起了一片欢呼声，主人和客人一起用越南话和中国话高唱“团结就是力量”。
    当中国艺术团人员离开车站前往住所时，欢迎的群众一直热情地把他们送到车上，聚集在车站附近的成千成万群众不住地向中国艺术团人员发出欢呼。
