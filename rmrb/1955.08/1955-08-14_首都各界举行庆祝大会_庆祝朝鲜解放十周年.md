Title: Capital Holds Grand Celebration Rally Celebrating the Tenth Anniversary of the Liberation of Korea
Author:
Date: 1955-08-14
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, August 13th. People from all walks of life in the capital held a grand celebration rally tonight to celebrate the tenth anniversary of the liberation of Korea.

The celebration rally was organized by the Ministry of Culture of the People's Republic of China. Among those present at today's celebration rally were Premier of the State Council Zhou Enlai, Vice Premiers Peng Dehuai and Chen Yi, Vice Chairman of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress Shen Junru, Director of the Second Office of the State Council Lin Feng, Acting Minister of Culture Qian Junrui, Minister of Foreign Trade Ye Jizhuang, Minister of Communications Zhang Bojun, Vice Minister of Foreign Affairs Zhang Hanfu, Deputy Chiefs of General Staff of the Chinese People's Liberation Army Zhang Zongxun, Li Kenong, and Chen Geng, Deputy Director of the General Political Department Xiao Hua, Deputy Mayor of Beijing Zhang Youyu, as well as ministers and vice ministers of various ministries, deputy directors of various committees, heads of various people's organizations, and people from the capital's scientific, educational, literary, journalistic circles, and others, totaling more than 600 people.

Choi Il, Ambassador of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea to China, embassy officials, as well as diplomatic envoys and diplomatic officials from various countries to China were invited to attend the celebration rally.

The celebration rally was presided over by Qian Junrui.

At the beginning of the rally, the band played the national anthems of Korea and China. Lin Feng spoke amidst warm applause (see page 3 of today's newspaper).

Choi Il, Ambassador of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea to China, then spoke at the meeting (see page 3 of today's newspaper), and warm applause repeatedly resounded throughout the venue.

After the celebration rally, the song and dance troupe of the Chinese People's Liberation Army performed wonderful song and dance programs.

### 首都各界举行庆祝大会  庆祝朝鲜解放十周年

1955-08-14
第1版()
专栏：

    首都各界举行庆祝大会
    庆祝朝鲜解放十周年
    据新华社十三日讯　首都各界十三日晚举行庆祝大会，隆重庆祝朝鲜解放十周年。
    庆祝大会由中华人民共和国文化部举办，出席今天庆祝大会的，有国务院总理周恩来，副总理彭德怀、陈毅，全国人民代表大会常务委员会副委员长沈钧儒，国务院第二办公室主任林枫，文化部代理部长钱俊瑞，对外贸易部部长叶季壮，交通部部长章伯钧，外交部副部长章汉夫，中国人民解放军副总参谋长张宗逊、李克农、陈赓，总政治部副主任萧华，北京市副市长张友渔，以及各部部长、副部长，各委员会副主任，各人民团体负责人，首都科学、教育、文艺、新闻界人士等六百多人。
    朝鲜民主主义人民共和国驻华大使崔一和使馆官员，以及各国驻华使节和外交官员应邀出席了庆祝大会。
    庆祝大会由钱俊瑞主持。
    大会开始，乐队奏朝、中两国国歌。林枫在热烈的掌声中讲话（见今天本报第三版）。
    朝鲜民主主义人民共和国驻华大使崔一接着在会上讲话（见今天本报第三版），全场一再响起了热烈的掌声。
    在庆祝大会后，由中国人民解放军歌舞团表演了精彩的歌舞节目。
