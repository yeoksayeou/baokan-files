Title: Telegram from the Central Committee of the Communist Party of China

Author: 
Date: 1955-08-15
Page: 1

Telegram from the Central Committee of the Communist Party of China to the Central Committee of the Korean Workers' Party:

    On the occasion of the tenth anniversary of the liberation of Korea, the Central Committee of the Communist Party of China extends warm, fraternal congratulations to the Korean Workers' Party and the Korean people.
    Over the past ten years, the heroic Korean people, under the leadership of the Korean Workers' Party, have achieved great successes in the struggle for the peaceful reunification and independence of their motherland, especially in the cause of opposing American aggression and defending peace in Asia and the world, as well as in the post-war recovery and construction work. The Chinese people are infinitely delighted by this and extend fraternal concern and support.
    The Central Committee of the Communist Party of China sincerely wishes the Korean people, under the leadership of the Korean Workers' Party, even greater achievements in the struggle to resolve the issue of Korean reunification through peaceful consultation and to build a peaceful, unified, independent, and democratic country.
      The Central Committee of the Communist Party of China
    August 14, 1955

### 中国共产党中央委员会电

1955-08-15
第1版()
专栏：

    中国共产党中央委员会电朝鲜劳动党中央委员会：
    值此朝鲜解放十周年之际，中国共产党中央委员会谨向朝鲜劳动党和朝鲜人民致以热烈的、兄弟般的祝贺。
    十年来，英雄的朝鲜人民在朝鲜劳动党的领导下，在为争取祖国的和平、统一、独立的斗争中，特别是在反对美国的侵略、捍卫亚洲和世界的和平事业中，以及在战后的恢复与建设工作中，都取得了巨大的成就；中国人民对此感到无限的欣快，并寄以兄弟般的关怀和支持。
    中国共产党中央委员会谨祝朝鲜人民在朝鲜劳动党的领导下，在为争取以和平协商的方式解决朝鲜统一问题，为建立一个和平、统一、独立、民主的国家而进行的斗争中取得更大的成就。
      中国共产党中央委员会
    一九五五年八月十四日
