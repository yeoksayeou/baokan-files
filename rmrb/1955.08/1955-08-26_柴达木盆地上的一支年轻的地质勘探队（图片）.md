A Young Geological Exploration Team in the Qaidam Basin

A batch of young geological exploration personnel are searching for rich oil deposits for the motherland on the vast Gobi Desert of the Qaidam Basin. In just two short months this year, geological personnel from the Qinghai Provincial Petroleum Exploration Team alone have discovered more than a dozen large oil-bearing structures.

The geological exploration personnel have received enthusiastic support from the local Mongolian, Hui, Uyghur, Tibetan, and other ethnic groups in their work. The geological exploration personnel have expressed that they will actively complete the glorious tasks assigned to them by the state while ensuring quality. (Attached pictures)

Geological detail surveyors traverse mountains to understand the complete picture of oil-bearing structures.
　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　Xinhua News Agency Reporter Zhao Huaiqing Photo

The members of the geological detail surveying team are measuring structural points. Connecting the determined structural points on the drawing will create a complete map of the oil-bearing structure, allowing drilling personnel to determine deep well locations based on this map.　　　　　　TextViewStyle TextView TextView TextView TextView TextView Xinhua News Agency Reporter Zhao Huaiqing Photo

At dusk, the members of the geological exploration team gather around the radio to listen to news from Beijing.
　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　Xinhua News Agency Reporter Zhao Huaiqing Photo

Living in the Gobi Desert, water is very precious. The drinking water for the geological exploration personnel is transported from dozens of kilometers away by camel teams from local ethnic groups supporting the exploration work.

### 柴达木盆地上的一支年轻的地质勘探队（图片）

1955-08-26
第2版()
专栏：

　　柴达木盆地上的一支年轻的地质勘探队
　　一批批年轻的地质勘探人员，正在柴达木盆地的辽阔的戈壁滩上，为祖国寻找丰富的石油矿藏。在今年短短的两个月中，仅青海省石油勘探队的地质人员就发现了十几个面积很大的储油构造。
　　地质勘探人员在工作中得到了当地的蒙、回、维吾尔、藏等族人民的热情支援。地质勘探人员纷纷表示：要在保证质量的条件下，积极完成国家交给自己的光荣任务。（附图片）
　　地质细测人员翻山越岭了解储油构造的全貌。
　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　新华社记者　赵淮青摄
    地质细测队的队员们正在测量构造点。把确定了的许多构造点在图纸上联结起来，就能绘成一幅储油构造的全图，钻探人员便可以根据此图来确定深钻井位。　　　　　　　　　新华社记者　赵淮青摄
    黄昏时候，地质勘探队的队员们围着收音机收听来自北京的消息。　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　新华社记者　赵淮青摄
    生活在戈壁滩上，水是非常宝贵的。地质勘探人员的饮水，是由当地各族人民支援勘探工作的骆驼队从数十公里以外运来的。
　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　新华社记者　赵淮青摄
