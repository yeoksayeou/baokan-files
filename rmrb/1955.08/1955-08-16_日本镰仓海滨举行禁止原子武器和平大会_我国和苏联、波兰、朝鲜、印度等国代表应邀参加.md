Title: Peace Conference Against Atomic Weapons Held on the Beach in Kamakura, Japan; Representatives from China, the Soviet Union, Poland, North Korea, India and Other Countries Invited to Attend
Author:
Date: 1955-08-16
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency, Tokyo, 15th: A peace conference against atomic and hydrogen bombs, attended by 30,000 people, was held on the beach in Kamakura, Kanagawa Prefecture on the morning of August 14th.

Among those attending the conference were: Heiro Yoshitaro, representative of the Japanese Preparatory Committee for the World Conference Against Atomic and Hydrogen Bombs; Hongo Shin, representative of the Japanese Peace Committee; Ozawa Masamoto, representative of the Japan-China Friendship Association; Kurahara Korehito, representative of the Japanese Communist Party; Ogata Shoji, representative of the Japan-Soviet Friendship Association; Takano Minoru, representative of the General Council of Trade Unions of Japan; Yagi Hideo, chairman of the Kanagawa Prefectural Council of Trade Unions; the Deputy Mayor of Kamakura, representatives from all sectors and organizations, citizens of Kamakura, overseas Chinese, and Korean residents in Japan.

Representatives from China, the Soviet Union, Poland, North Korea, India, France, Italy and the World Peace Council, who were attending the World Conference Against Atomic and Hydrogen Bombs, were also invited to attend the peace conference.

The conference opened with the song "No Atomic Bombings." Representatives from various countries spoke at the conference. They unanimously emphasized the importance of the World Conference Against Atomic and Hydrogen Bombs, and praised the Japanese people's enthusiasm and determination in striving for peace. Liu Ningyi, head of the Chinese delegation, said in his speech: The number of people around the world demanding the prohibition of atomic and hydrogen bombs is increasing, and their strength is growing. Those who use atomic bombs for blackmail are becoming increasingly unpopular and isolated. Let us unite closely and move forward towards the bright road of peace! His speech was met with thunderous applause from the audience.

The conference concluded by listening to and unanimously approving the conclusions reached at the Hiroshima meeting of the World Conference Against Atomic and Hydrogen Bombs and the conference declaration.

After the conference, participants and foreign guests held a get-together on the beach with group dances and chorus singing.

The Chinese delegation and representatives from various countries also attended a welcome banquet hosted by the Kamakura City Welcome Committee on the afternoon of the 14th and visited scenic spots in Kamakura City.

The Chinese delegation also went to Kugenuma Beach in Fujisawa City to lay flowers at the Nie Er memorial.

### 日本镰仓海滨举行禁止原子武器和平大会  我国和苏联、波兰、朝鲜、印度等国代表应邀参加

1955-08-16
第4版()
专栏：

    日本镰仓海滨举行禁止原子武器和平大会
    我国和苏联、波兰、朝鲜、印度等国代表应邀参加
    新华社十五日讯　东京消息：八月十四日上午在神奈川县镰仓海滨举行了共有三万人参加的禁止原子弹和氢弹和平大会。
    参加大会的有禁止原子弹和氢弹世界大会日本筹备会代表平野义太郎、日本拥护和平委员会代表本乡新、日中友好协会代表小泽正元、日本共产党代表藏原惟人、日苏亲善协会代表尾形昭二、日本工会总评议会代表高野实、神奈川地方工会评议会主席八木秀夫、镰仓市副市长等各界各团体代表和镰仓市民以及华侨、朝鲜侨民等。
    中国、苏联、波兰、朝鲜、印度、法国、意大利等国和世界和平理事会出席禁止原子弹和氢弹世界大会的代表也应邀参加了和平大会。
    大会在“不准原子弹轰炸”的歌声里开幕。各国代表都在大会上讲话。他们一致强调禁止原子弹和氢弹世界大会的重要性，并且称赞日本人民争取和平的热情和决心。中国代表团团长刘宁一在讲话中说：全世界要求禁止原子弹和氢弹的人们越来越多，力量越来越大了，用原子弹进行讹诈的人越来越不得人心，越来越孤立了。让我们紧密地团结起来，向着和平的光明大道前进吧！他的讲话博得全场雷动的掌声。
    大会最后听取并且一致同意了禁止原子弹和氢弹世界大会在广岛会议上所得到的结论和大会宣言。
    大会结束后，与会的人同外国来宾一起在海滨举行了集体跳舞和合唱等联欢活动。
    中国代表团和各国代表十四日下午还出席了镰仓市欢迎委员会主办的欢迎宴会并且参观了镰仓市的名胜。
    中国代表团还到藤泽市鹄沼海滨向聂耳纪念碑献花。
