Title: The Situation and Achievements of the Soviet Academy of Sciences in Studying Nuclear Problems
Author: 
Date: 1955-08-31
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, August 30th: "People's Daily" published a full page on August 29th introducing the situation and achievements of the Soviet Academy of Sciences' Institute of Nuclear Problems in studying nuclear problems.

　　In an article, author Trifonov gave a popular account of the development of people's views on the structure of matter from ancient China to the present, and then introduced the world's largest charged particle accelerator installed in the Institute of Nuclear Problems—the synchrocyclotron. Scientists use this equipment to comprehensively study various atomic nuclei, understand the internal conditions of atomic nuclei, study various "basic" particles, study the interaction between these particles and with complex atomic nuclei, and artificially obtain particles that could only be found in cosmic rays that are shot from outer space to the earth.

　　The synchrocyclotron is a complex construction project. Before building it, all arrangements were made in advance to protect staff from the effects of various rays that are harmful to health. The main hall where the synchrocyclotron is located resembles a huge workshop in a modern industrial enterprise. The scale of the equipment installed here is astonishingly large. For example, an electromagnet weighs 7,000 tons, and the circumference of the magnet is six meters.

　　Trifonov then gave a detailed introduction to the structure and working principle of the synchrocyclotron of the Soviet Academy of Sciences' Institute of Nuclear Problems. He pointed out that this synchrocyclotron utilizes the so-called automatic phase stabilization principle proposed by Veksler, Corresponding Academician of the Soviet Academy of Sciences, in 1944. It can accelerate protons to an energy of 680 million electron volts (usually the maximum energy of protons in a cyclotron cannot exceed 20 million electron volts). Using this accelerator, it is possible to obtain strong beams of Л mesons with positive and negative charges with energies up to 400 million electron volts and neutron beams with energies up to 600 million electron volts. It can also be used to obtain deuterium nuclei with an energy of 420 million electron volts and A particles (helium nuclei) with an energy of 840 million electron volts.

　　When the particles increase to their maximum speed, they enter a device in the chamber, from which they can be released to the outside. A target is installed here, and charged particles are shot at the target at a huge speed. As a result, neutrons, mesons, and gamma rays are emitted from the target. After the target is bombarded, its atomic nucleus is destroyed and becomes the atomic nucleus of another element. Using this principle, one chemical element can be transformed into another. For example, gold and platinum can be obtained from bismuth. Due to these types of experiments carried out in accelerators, many new elements that have never existed in nature continue to appear in Mendeleev's periodic table.

　　The particles are emitted from the accelerator through special holes in the heavy steel plate above the concrete barrier, becoming thin beams of particle rays entering the laboratory behind the barrier. Duty engineers use remote control to manage the synchrocyclotron.

　　The synchrocyclotron is a powerful tool for understanding the material world.

　　Based on the objective laws of natural development, under the guidance of the dialectical materialism method, Soviet physicists are increasingly deeply aware of the mysteries of the atom.

　　Soviet scientists working at the synchrocyclotron of the Soviet Academy of Sciences' Institute of Nuclear Problems have made significant contributions to the research of nuclear energy problems.

　　New and important materials have been obtained here regarding the characteristics of proton smashing protons, and the situation of neutron smashing neutron with huge energy was studied for the first time. Combining these research results with the experimental data of American physicists can obtain new materials about the radius of protons. Originally, a proton is a complex unit. There is a very solid core in its center. The size of the core is two-thirds smaller than the previously thought radius of the proton. The institute also obtained new materials about the interaction between neutrons and protons, and accumulated a large amount of experimental materials for studying the interaction between Л mesons and intranuclear particles and nucleons. The phenomenon of the nucleus being split by Л mesons was discovered for the first time. All of this is of utmost importance for establishing a rigorous theory of the atomic nucleus.

　　The research work of Soviet scientists is being further developed. The Soviet Union is building a more efficient synchrocyclotron, which is expected to obtain an energy of 10 billion electron volts.

　　The author finally wrote that the research work of Soviet physicists is by no means a purely observational work. Their mission is to make the powerful forces of nature serve mankind and to promote the happy future of the people of all countries with their labor.

### 苏联科学院研究核子问题的情况和成就

1955-08-31
第4版()
专栏：

　　苏联科学院研究核子问题的情况和成就
    新华社三十日讯　据塔斯社莫斯科讯：“真理报”八月二十九日以整版篇幅介绍苏联科学院核子问题研究所研究核子问题的情况和成就。
    作者特里丰诺夫在一篇文章中通俗地叙述了从古代中国到现在人们对物质构造的观点的发展，随后介绍了核子问题研究所里装置的一部全世界最大的带电质点加速器——同步回旋加速器。科学家利用这种装备，全面地研究各种原子核，了解原子核的内部情况和研究各种“基本”质点，研究这些质点彼此之间以及同复杂的原子核的互相作用，人工地获得过去只有在从宇宙空间射到地球上来的宇宙线中才能发现的质点。
    同步回旋加速器是一种复杂的建造工程。在建造它以前，就预先作好一切安排，以保护工作人员免受各种有害健康的射线的影响。同步回旋加速器所在的主要大厅好似近代工业企业中的巨大车间。这里所装置的设备的规模巨大得惊人。例如，一个电磁铁就重七千吨，磁铁的圆周直径为六公尺。
    特里丰诺夫接着详细介绍了苏联科学院核子问题研究所的同步回旋加速器的构造和它的工作原理。他指出，这部同步回旋加速器利用了苏联科学院通讯院士维克斯勒在一九四四年提出的所谓自动定相的原理。它可以把质子加速到具有六亿八千万电子伏特的能量（通常质子在回旋加速器中最大能量不能超过二千万电子伏特）。利用这个加速器可以得到具有达四亿电子伏特能量的强烈的带有正电和负电的Л介子束和具有达六亿电子伏特能量的中子束。利用它还可以得到具有四亿二千万电子伏特能量的氘核和具有八亿四千万电子伏特的A质点（氦核）。
    质点在增加到最大速度的时候，便进入小室内的一个装置中，经由这里可以释放到外面去。这里安装有一个靶子，带电荷的质点以巨大的速度向这个靶子射击。结果便从靶子放射出中子、介子和丙种射线。靶子受到射击之后，它的原子核就被破坏，而变成了另一种原素的原子核。利用这个原理可以把一种化学原素变成另一种原素。例如，可以从铋得出金和铂。由于在加速器中进行的这类实验，在门德雷叶夫元素表中还不断地出现许多在自然界中不曾有过的新元素。
    质点从加速器经由混凝土障壁上面的重钢板的专门孔洞射出，成为一束束细细的质点射线进入障壁后面的实验室。值班工程师利用远距离操纵来管理同步回旋加速器。
    同步回旋加速器是认识物质世界的一个强有力的工具。
    苏联物理学家依据自然发展的客观法则，在辩证唯物主义的方法的指导之下，越来越深刻地认识到原子的奥秘。
    在苏联科学院核子问题研究所同步回旋加速器工作的苏联科学家对核子能问题的研究作出了重大的贡献。
    这里已经获得关于质子击碎质子的特性的新的重要材料，并第一次研究巨大能量的中子击碎中子的情况。这些研究结果和美国物理学家的实验数据结合在一起，就可以获得关于质子半径的新材料。原来，质子是一个复杂的单位，在它的中心有一个非常坚固的核心，核心的大小比以前所认为的质子半径的大小还要小三分之二。研究所还获得了关于中子和质子相互作用的新材料，积累了有关研究Л 介子同核内质点和核子相互作用的大批实验材料。第一次发现核子被Л介子分裂的现象。所有这一切对创立严密的原子核理论具有极其重要的价值。
    苏联科学家的研究工作正在获得进一步的开展。苏联正在建设一个效能更大的同步回旋加速器，预计这个加速器可获得一百亿电子伏特的能量。
    作者最后写道，苏联物理学家的研究工作决不是一种纯粹观察的工作。他们的使命是使强大的自然力量为人类服务，并且用自己的劳动来促进各国人民的幸福的未来。
