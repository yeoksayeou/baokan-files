Title: Sino-American Ambassadors Hold Eighth Meeting
Author: 
Date: 1955-08-19
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, Geneva, August 18th: The Sino-American ambassadorial talks held their eighth meeting at 10:00 AM local time on the 18th. The meeting adjourned at 12:20 PM. The next meeting will be held on August 20th (Saturday) at 10:00 AM. At the meeting on the 18th, the two ambassadors continued to discuss the first item on the agenda – the repatriation of civilians by both sides.

### 中美两国大使举行第八次会谈

1955-08-19
第1版()
专栏：

    中美两国大使举行第八次会谈
    新华社日内瓦十八日电  中美两国大使级会谈在十八日本地时间上午十时举行第八次会议。会议在十二时二十分休会。下次会议将在八月二十日（星期六）上午十时举行。在十八日会议上，双方大使继续讨论了第一项议程——双方平民回国问题。
