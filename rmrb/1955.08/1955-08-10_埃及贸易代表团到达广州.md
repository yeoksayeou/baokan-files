Title: Egyptian Trade Delegation Arrives in Guangzhou
Author:
Date: 1955-08-10
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, Guangzhou, August 9th. The Egyptian trade delegation, led by Egyptian Minister of Industry and Commerce Muhammad Abu Nuseir, arrived in Guangzhou from Shenzhen on the evening of the 9th, totaling ten people. Sun Chuanlu, Director of the Guangdong Provincial Foreign Trade Bureau, and Cao Ruoming, Director of the Foreign Affairs Office of the Guangdong Provincial People's Committee, and others went to the station to welcome them.

### 埃及贸易代表团到达广州

1955-08-10
第1版()
专栏：

    埃及贸易代表团到达广州
    新华社广州九日电　由埃及工商部长穆罕默德·阿卜·努赛尔率领的埃及贸易代表团共十人在九日晚上从深圳到达广州。前往车站欢迎的有广东省对外贸易局局长孙传禄、广东省人民委员会外事处处长曹若茗等。
