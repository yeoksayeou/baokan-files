Title: State Council Appoints Personnel to State Organs and Universities

Author: 
Date: 1955-08-15
Page: 2

Xinhua News Agency

    The State Council of the People's Republic of China appointed a number of personnel to state organs and universities on August 5. The list is as follows:

    Cheng Tan as Vice Minister of the Ministry of Supervision;

    Xu Daben as Vice Minister of the Ministry of Coal Industry, Liu Xiangsan and Zhong Ziyun as Assistant Ministers;

    Wang Lin as Vice Minister of the Ministry of Electric Power Industry, Cheng Mingsheng as Assistant Minister;

    Li Fanyi, Li Renjun, and Zhou Wenlong as Vice Ministers of the Ministry of Petroleum Industry, Kang Shi'en, Liu Fang, and Huang Kai as Assistant Ministers;

    Zeng Shufan as Vice President of Sichuan Institute of Finance and Economics.

### 国务院任命一批国家机关和高等学校工作人员

1955-08-15
第2版()
专栏：

    国务院任命一批国家机关和高等学校工作人员
    中华人民共和国国务院在八月五日任命一批国家机关工作人员和高等学校工作人员，名单如下：
    程坦为监察部副部长；
    徐达本为煤炭工业部副部长，刘向三、钟子云为部长助理；
    王林为电力工业部副部长，程明升为部长助理；
    李范一、李人俊、周文龙为石油工业部副部长，康世恩、刘放、黄凯为部长助理；
    曾庶凡为四川财经学院副院长。（新华社）
