Title: Chinese Delegation Presents Gifts to the Korean Government
Author: 
Date: 1955-08-15
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Pyongyang, August 14th. The Delegation of the People's Republic of China, headed by Vice Chairman Zhu De, presented gifts to the government of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea on the morning of the 14th. The gifts included a set of lacquer "antiquities display" hanging screens from Fujian Province, a Chinese specialty, and a pair of cloisonné vases.

### 我国代表团向朝鲜政府赠送礼物

1955-08-15
第1版()
专栏：

    我国代表团向朝鲜政府赠送礼物
    新华社平壤十四日电  以朱德副主席为首的中华人民共和国代表团在十四日上午向朝鲜民主主义人民共和国政府赠送礼物，礼物有我国特产福建漆博古挂屏一堂和景泰蓝花瓶一对。
