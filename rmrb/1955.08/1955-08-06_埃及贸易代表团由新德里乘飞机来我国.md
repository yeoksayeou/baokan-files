Title: Egyptian Trade Delegation Arrives in China by Plane from New Delhi
Author:
Date: 1955-08-06
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, New Delhi, August 5th: The Egyptian trade delegation, led by Egyptian Minister of Industry and Commerce Muhammad Abu Nuseir, left for China by plane on the 5th after a two-day stay in New Delhi.

Fang Jianxin, Chargé d'Affaires ad interim of the Embassy of the People's Republic of China in India, hosted a dinner on the 4th to entertain the Egyptian trade delegation. Amin Mufta, Chargé d'Affaires ad interim of the Egyptian Embassy in India, also attended the banquet.

### 埃及贸易代表团由新德里乘飞机来我国

1955-08-06
第1版()
专栏：

    埃及贸易代表团由新德里乘飞机来我国
    新华社新德里五日电　由埃及工商部长穆罕默德·阿卜·努赛尔率领的埃及贸易代表团在新德里停留两天后，五日乘飞机前往中国。
    中华人民共和国驻印度大使馆临时代办方建新在四日设晚宴招待埃及贸易代表团。埃及驻印度大使馆临时代办阿明·穆夫塔也出席了宴会。
