Title: Our Country's Exhibition Delegation Head Arrives in Damascus to Participate in the Damascus International Fair
Author:
Date: 1955-08-28
Page: 3

Xinhua News Agency:

　　Jiang Ming, head of the China Council for the Promotion of International Trade exhibition delegation and Vice Minister of the Ministry of Foreign Trade, arrived in Damascus, the capital of Syria, by plane on the 25th to participate in the Damascus International Fair. The director of the Damascus International Fair and others welcomed him at the airport.
    The Damascus International Fair is scheduled to open on September 1st and will last for one month. Twenty-one countries are participating. The scale of the People's Republic of China pavilion at this year's fair is larger than last year, covering an area of 2,900 square meters, and will exhibit more than 2,000 exhibits.

### 参加大马士革国际博览会　我国展览团团长抵大马士革

1955-08-28
第3版()
专栏：

　　参加大马士革国际博览会
  　我国展览团团长抵大马士革
    中国国际贸易促进委员会参加大马士革国际博览会展览团团长、中国对外贸易部副部长江明在二十五日乘飞机到达叙利亚首都大马士革。到机场欢迎的有大马士革国际博览会主任等。
    大马士革国际博览会定在九月一日开幕，举行一个月。参加的有二十一个国家。参加今年博览会的中华人民共和国馆的规模比去年的更大，占地二千九百平方公尺，将展出两千多件展览品。
                                    （新华社）
