Title: Picture
Author: Liu Peiteng
Date: 1955-08-24
Page: 2

    The first 3,700-ton large-scale Class-A oil barge built by Dalian Shipyard in our country was completed on July 31st and delivered to the transportation unit for use. The picture shows the completed oil barge docked in the port of Dalian Shipyard.
        Photo by Liu Peiteng (Xinhua News Agency manuscript)
