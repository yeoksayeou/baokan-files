Title: Czechoslovak Premier Široký Receives Head and Deputy Head of Chinese Art Troupe
Author:
Date: 1955-08-30
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Prague, August 29th: Czechoslovak Premier Široký received Zhang Zhixiang, head of the Chinese Art Troupe, and Zhao Feng, deputy head, on the morning of August 29th. Cao Ying, Chinese Ambassador to Czechoslovakia, accompanied them.

　　A cordial conversation lasting one hour took place during the meeting.

　　On the same day, Czechoslovak Minister of Culture Štol also received the head and deputy head of the Chinese Art Troupe.

### 捷总理西罗基接见我国艺术团正副团长

1955-08-30
第4版()
专栏：

　　捷总理西罗基接见我国艺术团正副团长
    新华社布拉格二十九日电　捷克斯洛伐克共和国总理西罗基二十九日上午接见了中国艺术团团长张致祥和副团长赵沨。中国驻捷大使曹瑛陪同前往。
    接见时进行了一小时的亲切会谈。
    同日，捷克斯洛伐克文化部长斯托尔也接见了中国艺术团的正副团长。
