Title: Ye Jizhuang Hosts Banquet to Celebrate Signing of Sino-Egyptian Trade Agreement; Egyptian Trade Delegation and Responsible Persons from Relevant Chinese Departments Hold Economic Symposium

Author:
Date: 1955-08-23
Page: 1

　　Xinhua News Agency, August 22nd: Minister of Foreign Trade of the People's Republic of China, Ye Jizhuang, hosted a banquet on the evening of August 22nd to celebrate the signing of the trade agreement between the People's Republic of China and the Republic of Egypt, and the protocol regarding the first agreement year of the Sino-Egyptian trade agreement.

　　Head of the Egyptian Republic Government Trade Delegation, Minister of Industry and Commerce of Egypt, Muhammad Abu Nuseir, all members of the delegation, and advisors were invited to attend the banquet.

　　On the Chinese side, the attendees included: Vice Minister of Foreign Trade Lei Renmin, Vice Minister of Foreign Affairs Zhang Hanfu, Assistant Minister Chen Jiakang, and responsible persons from various bureaus and professional corporations under the Ministry of Foreign Trade.

　　After the banquet, the Chinese Acrobatic Troupe performed wonderful programs.

　　Xinhua News Agency, August 22nd: On the morning of August 22nd, Head of the Egyptian Republic Government Trade Delegation, Minister of Industry and Commerce of Egypt, Muhammad Abu Nuseir, delegation member Mustafa Kamel Ibrahim, and advisor Ahmed Fouad, held an economic symposium with responsible persons from relevant Chinese departments.

　　The symposium was presided over by Vice Minister of Foreign Trade Lei Renmin. Participants included: Deputy Director of the State Planning Commission, Han Zheyuan, Deputy Secretary-General of the Rural Work Department of the CPC Central Committee, Wang Qian, Deputy Director of the Agricultural Economic Planning Bureau of the Ministry of Agriculture, Liu Qian, and others.

　　The guests were particularly interested in the work of the State Planning Commission. Delegation advisor Ahmed Fouad, who is a member of the Egyptian National Production Development Committee, inquired with concern about the organizational structure of the Chinese State Planning Commission, the formulation of production plans, and how they are implemented. Han Zheyuan provided detailed answers to all questions.

　　At the symposium, Wang Qian and Liu Qian answered questions raised by the guests regarding agriculture.

### 叶季壮举行宴会庆祝中埃贸易协定签字　埃及贸易代表团和我国有关方面负责人举行经济座谈会

1955-08-23
第1版()
专栏：

　　叶季壮举行宴会庆祝中埃贸易协定签字
　　埃及贸易代表团和我国有关方面负责人举行经济座谈会
    新华社二十二日讯　中华人民共和国对外贸易部部长叶季壮，在二十二日晚举行宴会，庆祝中华人民共和国和埃及共和国贸易协定及关于中、埃两国贸易协定第一个协定年度议定书的签字。
    埃及共和国政府贸易代表团团长、埃及工商部部长穆罕默德·阿卜·努赛尔和代表团全体团员和顾问，都应邀出席了宴会。
    中国方面出席宴会的有：对外贸易部副部长雷任民，外交部副部长章汉夫、部长助理陈家康，以及对外贸易部各局和专业公司的负责人。
    宴会以后，由中国杂技团表演了精采节目。
    新华社二十二日讯　二十二日上午，埃及共和国政府贸易代表团团长、埃及工商部部长穆罕默德·阿卜·努赛尔和代表团团员穆斯塔法·卡迈尔·易卜拉欣、顾问艾哈迈德·福阿德，同中国有关部门的负责人举行了经济座谈会。
    座谈会由对外贸易部副部长雷任民主持。参加座谈会的有：国家计划委员会副主任韩哲一，中共中央农村工作部副秘书长王谦，农业部农业经济计划局副局长刘潜等。
    客人们对国家计划委员会的工作特别感到兴趣。代表团顾问艾哈迈德·福阿德是埃及国家生产发展委员会的委员，他关心地问到了中国国家计划委员会的组织机构、生产计划的制定和怎样贯彻执行等许多问题，都由韩哲一作了详尽答复。
    在座谈会上，王谦和刘潜答复了客人们提出的关于农业方面的问题。
