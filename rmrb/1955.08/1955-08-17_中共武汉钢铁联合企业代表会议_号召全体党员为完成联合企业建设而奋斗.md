Title: CCP Wuhan Iron and Steel United Enterprise Representative Meeting Calls on All Party Members to Strive for the Completion of the United Enterprise Construction
Author: 
Date: 1955-08-17
Page: 2

    CCP Wuhan Iron and Steel United Enterprise Representative Meeting
    Calls on All Party Members to Strive for the Completion of the United Enterprise Construction
    The Chinese Communist Party organization of the Wuhan Iron and Steel United Enterprise held its first Party Representative Meeting from August 9th to 13th. The meeting called on Party members of the United Enterprise to unite all employees, give full play to their enthusiasm and creativity, overcome all difficulties, and strive to complete the construction of the Wuhan Iron and Steel United Enterprise, one of the key construction projects of the country's first Five-Year Plan. The meeting discussed and approved the report on striving to build the Wuhan Iron and Steel United Enterprise well, the report on strengthening the Party's leadership in the enterprise and strengthening political and ideological work, and the report on the plant construction plan, and elected the Supervisory Committee of the Chinese Communist Party of the Wuhan Iron and Steel United Enterprise.
    The delegates to the meeting discussed and approved the Wuhan Iron and Steel United Enterprise's savings plan for this year. The representatives pointed out in their speeches that the Wuhan Iron and Steel United Enterprise is a key national construction project, and it is necessary to carry out anti-waste work among all Party members and all employees, establish a long-term, regular, and comprehensive concept of thrift, and systematically establish fundamental systems and methods for rational use of national investment in all aspects, and conduct irreconcilable struggles against any individualistic or departmentalist ideas that lack a national concept or construction concept.
    The meeting also pointed out that in order to ensure the completion of the enterprise construction tasks, it is also necessary to raise vigilance, overcome erroneous ideas such as "only taking care of business and not politics," eliminate all counter-revolutionary elements, and transform the team building the enterprise into a pure and strong team. (Xinhua News Agency)

### 中共武汉钢铁联合企业代表会议  号召全体党员为完成联合企业建设而奋斗

1955-08-17
第2版()
专栏：

    中共武汉钢铁联合企业代表会议
    号召全体党员为完成联合企业建设而奋斗
    武汉钢铁联合企业的中国共产党组织，八月九日到十三日召开了第一次党的代表会议。会议号召联合企业的党员团结全体职工，充分发挥积极性和创造性，战胜一切困难，为完成国家第一个五年计划的建设重点之一——武汉钢铁联合企业的建设而奋斗。会议讨论和通过了关于为建设好武汉钢铁联合企业而奋斗的报告、关于加强党在企业中的领导作用和加强政治思想工作的报告以及关于建厂计划的报告，并选出了武汉钢铁联合企业的中国共产党监察委员会。
    到会代表讨论和通过了武汉钢铁联合企业今年的节约计划。代表们在发言中指出：武汉钢铁联合企业是国家建设重点，必须在全体党员和全体职工中开展反浪费的工作，树立长期、经常、全面节约的思想，从各方面有系统地建立根本性的合理使用国家投资的制度和办法，对任何缺乏国家观念、建设观念的个人主义、本位主义思想，进行不调和的斗争。
    会议还指出：为了保证完成企业建设任务，还必须提高警惕性，克服“只管业务、不管政治”等错误思想，肃清一切反革命分子，把建设企业的队伍变成一支纯洁的、坚强的队伍。（新华社）
