Title: Indian Dr. Pal Talks About the Achievements of the Soviet Union in the Peaceful Use of Atomic Energy
Author:
Date: 1955-08-11
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, New Delhi, August 9th. Dr. Pal of the Indian Medical College, who was invited to the Soviet Union to participate in the Soviet Academy of Sciences' discussion on the peaceful use of atomic energy, told the press on the 7th that the Soviet Union "applies atomic energy in physiology and biology in a wider range than any other country in the world." Pal said that, in addition, "Russians are conducting newer and more sophisticated experiments in the use of radioactive isotopes to diagnose diseases."

Pal said that in agriculture, Soviet scientists are using atomic energy as an insecticide and to improve grains. Atomic energy is also being used to improve food storage.

### 印度帕尔博士  谈苏联和平利用原子能的成就

1955-08-11
第4版()
专栏：

    印度帕尔博士
    谈苏联和平利用原子能的成就
    新华社新德里九日电　曾应邀到苏联参加苏联科学院讨论和平利用原子能的会议的印度医学院的帕尔博士七日对报界谈论苏联和平利用原子能的成就说，苏联“在生理学和生物学上应用原子能的范围比世界上其他任何国家要广泛”。帕尔说，此外，“在利用放射性同位素来诊断疾病方面俄罗斯人正在作更新的和更为完善的实验”。
    帕尔说，苏联科学家在农业方面正在利用原子能作为杀虫药和改善谷物。原子能也正被用来改善食物的储藏。
