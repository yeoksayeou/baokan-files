Title: Romanian Ambassador Chivu Stoica Holds Reception Celebrating the 11th Anniversary of Romania's Liberation
Author:
Date: 1955-08-24
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, August 23rd - Romanian Ambassador to China, Chivu Stoica, held a grand reception on the evening of the 23rd to celebrate the 11th anniversary of Romania's liberation.

At 8 p.m., Chivu Stoica, accompanied by Premier of the State Council of the People's Republic of China Zhou Enlai, Vice-Chairman of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress Soong Ching Ling, Vice-Chairman of the National Defense Council Cheng Qian, Long Yun, Vice-Chairman of the National Committee of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference Zhang Bojun, Vice Minister of Foreign Affairs Zhang Hanfu, and Vice Minister of National Defense Huang Kecheng, entered the hall, and the band played a melodious welcome song. The reception then began.

Ambassador Chivu Stoica and Premier Zhou Enlai spoke at the reception successively.

More than seven hundred people attended the reception tonight. Among them were ministers and vice-ministers of various ministries of the People's Republic of China, vice-chairmen of various commissions, members of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress, senior generals of the Chinese People's Liberation Army, the Vice-Mayor of Beijing, heads of various people's organizations, Chinese scientists, heads of various universities and famous hospitals in Beijing, people from literary and art circles and journalistic circles, heads of foreign diplomatic missions and diplomatic officials stationed in China, as well as all members of the Romanian Central Cooperative Union delegation, the Albanian cultural delegation, and the leaders of the Albanian People's Army Song and Dance Troupe.

After the reception, the Song and Dance Troupe of the Chinese People's Liberation Army, which had visited Romania, gave a brilliant performance, including many Romanian songs and dances.

### 庆祝罗马尼亚解放十一周年　罗马尼亚大使郭佐文举行招待会

1955-08-24
第1版()
专栏：

　　庆祝罗马尼亚解放十一周年
　　罗马尼亚大使郭佐文举行招待会
    据新华社二十三日讯　罗马尼亚驻华大使郭佐文在二十三日晚举行盛大招待会，庆祝罗马尼亚解放十一周年。
    晚上八时，郭佐文陪同中华人民共和国国务院总理周恩来，全国人民代表大会常务委员会副委员长宋庆龄，国防委员会副主席程潜、龙云，中国人民政治协商会议全国委员会副主席章伯钧，外交部副部长章汉夫，国防部副部长黄克诚等进入大厅，乐队奏起了悠扬悦耳的欢迎曲。招待会接着开始。
    郭佐文大使和周恩来总理先后在会上讲话。
    出席今晚招待会的，共有七百多人。他们中有中华人民共和国各部部长、副部长，各委员会副主任，全国人民代表大会常务委员会委员，中国人民解放军高级将领，北京市副市长，各人民团体负责人，中国的科学家，北京市各高等学校、各著名医院负责人，文艺界、新闻界人士，各国驻华使节和外交官员，以及罗马尼亚中央合作总社代表团全体人员、阿尔巴尼亚文化代表团和阿尔巴尼亚人民军歌舞团负责人等。
    招待会后，曾经访问过罗马尼亚的中国人民解放军歌舞团作了精采表演，其中有许多是罗马尼亚的歌舞节目。
