Title: Chinese People's Committee for World Peace Cables the All-India Peace Council in Support of the Indian People's Action to Demand Goa's Return to the Motherland
Author:
Date: 1955-08-22
Page: 1

　　Xinhua News Agency, August 21st: The Chinese People's Committee for World Peace cabled the All-India Peace Council on August 21st in support of the Indian people's just struggle to liberate Goa. The text of the telegram is as follows: All-India Peace Council:
　　The Chinese people express their utmost indignation and protest against the atrocities committed by the Portuguese colonial military police in Goa on August 15th, in which they brutally killed and wounded more than two hundred unarmed Indian citizens, and express deep condolences and sympathy to the victimized Indian patriots and their families.
　　Goa is an indivisible part of Indian territory, and the Indian people's just struggle to demand Goa's return to the motherland has won the sympathy and support of the people of Asia, Africa, and the world who love peace and uphold justice. We, the six hundred million Chinese people who love peace, fully sympathize with and support the Indian people's just struggle to liberate Goa, and firmly believe that you will win the final victory.
　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　Chinese People's Committee for World Peace
　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　August 21, 1955

### 中国人民保卫世界和平委员会致电全印和平理事会　支持印度人民要求果阿重归祖国的行动

1955-08-22
第1版()
专栏：

　　中国人民保卫世界和平委员会致电全印和平理事会
　　支持印度人民要求果阿重归祖国的行动
    新华社二十一日讯　中国人民保卫世界和平委员会二十一日打电报给全印和平理事会，支持印度人民解放果阿的正义斗争。电文如下：全印和平理事会：
    中国人民对于果阿葡萄牙殖民军警于八月十五日残酷地杀死和杀伤二百多名手无寸铁的印度公民的暴行表示极大的愤慨和抗议，并且对受害的印度爱国者和他们的家属表示深切的哀悼和慰问。
    果阿是印度领土不可分割的一部分，印度人民要求果阿重归祖国的正义斗争是得到亚洲、非洲以及全世界爱好和平和主持正义的人民的同情和支持的。我们爱好和平的六亿中国人民，完全同情和支持印度人民解放果阿的正义斗争，并且深信你们必将赢得最后胜利。
                中国人民保卫世界和平委员会
                      一九五五年八月二十一日
