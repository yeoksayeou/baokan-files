Title: China's Yue Opera Troupe Arrives in Minsk, Capital of Belarus
Author:
Date: 1955-08-03
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency dispatch:

    China's Yue Opera Troupe Arrives in Minsk, Capital of Belarus

    According to Xinhua News Agency, the Chinese Yue Opera Troupe arrived in Minsk, the capital of Belarus, on August 1st after its tour performance in Berlin.
    At the Minsk train station, the Chinese Yue Opera Troupe actors were warmly welcomed by people from all walks of life in Belarus and Minsk.
    The Minister of Culture of the Belarusian Republic, Kiselev, delivered a welcoming speech, and Xu Guangping, head of the Chinese Yue Opera Troupe, expressed deep gratitude on behalf of the Yue Opera Troupe actors for the warm welcome.
    After performing in Minsk, the Chinese Yue Opera Troupe will also go to Moscow, Leningrad, Sverdlovsk, and Novosibirsk for a month and a half of tour performances. The performing programs are "The West Chamber" and "The Butterfly Lovers."

### 我国越剧团到达白俄罗斯首都明斯克

1955-08-03
第4版()
专栏：

    我国越剧团到达白俄罗斯首都明斯克
    据新华社讯　中国越剧团在柏林巡回演出后，在八月一日到达白俄罗斯首都明斯克。
    在明斯克车站上，中国越剧团演员受到了白俄罗斯和明斯克各界人士的热烈欢迎。
    白俄罗斯共和国文化部长基谢廖夫致欢迎词，中国越剧团团长许广平代表越剧团演员们对热情的欢迎表示深切的感谢。
    中国越剧团在明斯克演出后，还将到莫斯科、列宁格勒、斯维德洛夫斯克、新西伯利亚城作为期一个半月的巡回演出。演出的节目是“西厢记”和“梁山伯与祝英台”。
