Title: Latin American Countries' Conference for the Defense of Democratic Freedoms Opens
Author:
Date: 1955-08-14
Page: 4

　　Xinhua News Agency, Montevideo, August 12th: According to the Chilean newspaper "El Siglo," the Latin American Countries' Conference for the Defense of Democratic Freedoms opened in Santiago, Chile, on August 12th. All Latin American countries have sent representatives to participate in this conference. Many organizations, social figures, and politicians from Brazil, Mexico, Bolivia, Uruguay, Argentina, and other countries have expressed their support for this conference.
　　"El Siglo" reported that the main tasks of this four-day conference are: to strive for the release of individuals imprisoned in Latin American countries for participating in student and trade union movements, or for political and religious reasons; and to launch a movement in these countries, involving residents from all walks of life, to defend democratic laws, freedom of speech and publication, freedom of religion and culture, and to defend political and trade union rights.

### 拉丁美洲国家保卫民主自由会议开幕

1955-08-14
第4版()
专栏：

    拉丁美洲国家保卫民主自由会议开幕
    新华社十三日讯　据塔斯社蒙得维的亚十二日讯：智利“世纪报”报道，拉丁美洲国家保卫民主自由会议八月十二日在智利圣地亚哥开幕。所有拉丁美洲国家都派有代表参加这个会议。巴西、墨西哥、玻利维亚、乌拉圭、阿根廷以及其他国家的许多组织、社会人士和政界人士都表示支持这个会议。
    “世纪报”报道，这个为期四天的会议的主要任务是：争取从拉丁美洲各国的监狱中释放由于参加学生和工会运动、由于政治和宗教原因而被囚禁的人士，在这些国家里发起一个有各阶层居民参加的保卫民主法律、保卫言论和出版自由、保卫宗教自由和文化以及保卫政治权利和工会权利的运动。
