Title: Premier Zhou Enlai Receives and Hosts Egyptian Trade Delegation
Author:
Date: 1955-08-22
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, August 21st. Premier Zhou Enlai of the State Council of the People's Republic of China received Mohamed Abul Nur, head of the Egyptian government trade delegation and Minister of Industry and Commerce of Egypt, along with all members and advisors of the delegation, at 6:00 PM on August 21st.

Present at the reception were Ye Jizhuang, Minister of Foreign Trade, Lei Renmin, Vice Minister, and Huang Hua, Director of the Department of West European and African Affairs of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs.

Xinhua News Agency, August 21st. Premier Zhou Enlai of the State Council of the People's Republic of China held a banquet on the evening of August 21st to host all members of the Egyptian government trade delegation headed by Mohamed Abul Nur.

Those present at the banquet included Xi Zhongxun, Secretary-General of the State Council, Ye Jizhuang, Minister of Foreign Trade, Lei Renmin, Vice Minister, Zhang Hanfu, Vice Minister of Foreign Affairs, Zhang Xi, Vice Chairman of the State Planning Commission, Qian Zhiguang, Vice Minister of Textile Industry, Liu Zhiming, Vice Minister of Culture, Huang Songling, Vice Minister of Higher Education, Chu Tunan, President of the Chinese People's Association for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries, Yang Hansheng, Vice President, Yang Jingren, Vice Chairman of the China Islamic Association, Bai Shouyi, Vice Chairman of the Chinese Hui People's Cultural Advancement Association, Wang Kunlun, Deputy Mayor of Beijing, and representatives from religious circles in the capital.

Before the banquet began, Premier Zhou Enlai introduced the members of the delegation and the heads of various departments attending the banquet, as well as the religious figures from the capital, shaking hands with each one.

Premier Zhou Enlai and others also took a group photo with the members of the delegation.

Xinhua News Agency, Shenyang, August 20th. The Egyptian government trade delegation left Shenyang by plane on the morning of the 20th to return to Beijing.

After arriving in Shenyang on the 18th, the delegation visited the Northeast Industrial Exhibition Hall, the Shenyang Pneumatic Tools Factory, and the No. 1 Machine Tool Plant. After visiting the Northeast Industrial Exhibition Hall, the head of the delegation, Mohamed Abul Nur, excitedly said, "Before coming to the Northeast, I really didn't expect the industry in the Northeast to be so developed, but this is only a part of China's industry." Zhang Chen, the female director of the Pneumatic Tools Factory, introduced the factory's development to the guests. The entire delegation then visited various workshops and praised the factory for its excellent production and organization. When the guests visited the No. 1 Machine Tool Plant, they saw workers engaged in production in bright, clean, and orderly workshops. After the visit, the head of the delegation said to the deputy director, Liu Jingyu, "I have visited factories in many countries that have been around for decades; your newly rebuilt factory is comparable to them."

Before leaving Shenyang, the guests also attended a reception hosted by Chen Hexuan, Deputy Mayor of Shenyang, and watched a Chinese film. (Attached picture)

### 周恩来总理接见并招待埃及贸易代表团

1955-08-22
第1版()
专栏：

　　周恩来总理接见并招待埃及贸易代表团
    新华社二十一日讯　中华人民共和国国务院总理周恩来，在二十一日下午六时接见了埃及共和国政府贸易代表团团长、埃及工商部部长穆罕默德·阿卜·努赛尔和代表团全体团员及顾问。
    接见时在座的，有对外贸易部部长叶季壮、副部长雷任民，外交部西欧非洲司司长黄华。
    新华社二十一日讯　中华人民共和国国务院总理周恩来在二十一日晚举行宴会，招待以穆罕默德·阿卜·努赛尔为首的埃及共和国政府贸易代表团全体人员。
    出席作陪的有，国务院秘书长习仲勋，对外贸易部部长叶季壮、副部长雷任民，外交部副部长章汉夫，国家计划委员会副主任张玺，纺织工业部副部长钱之光，文化部副部长刘芝明，高等教育部副部长黄松龄，中国人民对外文化协会会长楚图南、副会长阳翰笙，中国伊斯兰教协会副主任杨静仁，中国回民文化协进会副主任白寿彝，北京市副市长王昆仑，以及首都宗教界人士等。
    在宴会开始前，周恩来总理介绍代表团人员和参加宴会的各部门负责人及首都宗教界人士一一握手。
    周恩来总理等并和代表团人员合影留念。
    新华社沈阳二十日电　埃及共和国政府贸易代表团在二十日上午乘飞机离开沈阳回北京。
    代表团十八日到达沈阳后，先后参观了东北工业陈列馆、沈阳风动工具厂和第一机床厂。代表团团长穆罕默德·阿卜·努赛尔在参观了东北工业陈列馆后兴奋地表示：“未来东北之前，真没有想到东北的工业这样发达，但是，这仅是中国工业的一部分。”风动工具厂的女厂长张忱向客人们介绍了工厂发展的情况后，代表团全体人员参观了各个车间，都称赞这个工厂在生产和组织上都很好。客人们参观第一机床厂时，看到工人们在阳光充足、清洁整齐的车间里进行生产。参观后，代表团团长向副厂长刘敬余说：“我参观过许多国家有几十年历史的工厂；你们这个新改建的工厂足以和它们相比美。”
    客人们离开沈阳以前，还出席了沈阳市副市长陈鹤轩的招待宴会，并观看了中国电影。（附图片）
