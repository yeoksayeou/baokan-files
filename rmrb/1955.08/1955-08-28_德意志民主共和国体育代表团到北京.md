Title: German Democratic Republic Sports Delegation Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1955-08-28
Page: 1

　　Xinhua News Agency, August 27th: The sports delegation of the German Democratic Republic, consisting of twenty-two members, arrived in Beijing by plane on the 26th and 27th upon invitation to visit our country. The delegation is headed by Manfred Ewald, Chairman of the State Committee for Physical Culture and Sports of the German Democratic Republic, with Peter Döbler as the deputy head.

　　Rong Gaotang, Deputy Director of the Physical Culture and Sports Commission of the People's Republic of China, leading members of the Beijing Municipal Sports Commission, and Chinese gymnasts and others welcomed them at the airport.

　　Also present at the airport to welcome them were personnel from the Embassy of the German Democratic Republic in China.

　　The sports delegation of the German Democratic Republic includes a gymnastics team consisting of twelve male and female athletes.

### 德意志民主共和国体育代表团到北京

1955-08-28
第1版()
专栏：

　　德意志民主共和国体育代表团到北京
    新华社二十七日讯　应邀来我国访问的德意志民主共和国体育代表团一行二十二人，已在二十六日和二十七日分别乘飞机到达北京。代表团由德意志民主共和国国家体育运动委员会主席曼·埃瓦德任团长，由彼·多伯尔丁任副团长。
    中华人民共和国体育运动委员会副主任荣高棠，北京市体育运动委员会负责人和中国体操运动员等多人曾到机场欢迎。
    到机场欢迎的还有德意志民主共和国驻华使馆人员。
    德意志民主共和国体育代表团包括一个由十二名男、女运动员组成的体操队。
