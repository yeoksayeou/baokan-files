Title: Arab Countries Peace Delegates Leave Xinjiang for Shanghai
Author:
Date: 1955-08-07
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, Urumqi: The forty peace delegates from Arab countries concluded their visit to Xinjiang and left Urumqi by plane on the morning of the 3rd. Bao Erhan, Chairman of the Xinjiang Provincial People's Government and Director of the China Islamic Association, Ma Zhongcai and A. Saide, Deputy Mayors of Urumqi, Nusreti, Chairman of the Urumqi Branch of the Chinese People's Committee for World Peace, and over 120 people from religious and business circles went to the airport to see them off.
    Muhammad Ismail Hewand, the delegate from Syria, spoke on behalf of the peace delegates from various countries at the airport. He said: Now we bid farewell to you with infinite reluctance. Although the time we spent together was short, our hearts are filled with love for you. We have seen the many factories and large oases built in what were once barren and desert areas. These achievements of yours on the peaceful labor front have left us with an unforgettable impression. We will tell our people about these achievements and your support for us, and we will also tell them how you, people of different languages and beliefs, are working together for a common goal. Friends, we cannot repay your deep friendship, but we will continue to fight for peace together with you and the people of the world.
    The day before the delegates left Urumqi, they attended a symposium hosted by Bao Erhan, Chairman of the Xinjiang Provincial People's Government. At the meeting, delegates from Syria and Jordan described their feelings and impressions during the visit. They praised the spirit of unity and friendship among the people of all ethnic groups in Xinjiang in jointly building the motherland and defending world peace, and expressed their gratitude for the warm hospitality.
    During their stay in Urumqi, the peace delegates from Arab countries celebrated the Corban Festival with Chinese Muslims. The peace delegates also visited the Xinjiang October Automobile Repair Factory, the Xinjiang July 1 Cotton Textile Factory, and the Urumqi Power Plant, all of which are state-owned local enterprises. On August 1st, the anniversary of the founding of the Chinese People's Liberation Army, the peace delegates visited the agricultural production and construction units of the Xinjiang Military Region of the Chinese People's Liberation Army in the Manas Reclamation Area, about 140 to 150 kilometers from Urumqi.
    Xinhua News Agency, Shanghai, 4th: The forty peace delegates from Syria, Iraq, Lebanon, Jordan, and Sudan who were invited to visit our country arrived in Shanghai by special plane on the 4th from Xinjiang. Zhu Ziqi, Deputy Director of the Liaison Department of the Chinese People's Committee for World Peace, and Jia Kulin, Vice Chairman of the Xinjiang Branch, accompanied them.
    Among those who welcomed them at the airport were Zhong Min, Vice Chairman of the Shanghai Branch of the Chinese People's Committee for World Peace, Islamic imams, and people from all walks of life. Yang Shida, a representative of our country to the Helsinki World Peace Conference, also came to the airport to welcome them.
    After getting off the plane, the delegates shook hands cordially with the welcomers and accepted flowers from the Young Pioneers.
    At the airport, Zhong Min, on behalf of the people of Shanghai, extended a warm welcome to the peace delegates for their visit to Shanghai. He said: The people of Shanghai express their heartfelt respect for your unremitting efforts for world peace. Nuhad Gadri, the Syrian delegate, thanked the people of Shanghai for their warm welcome on behalf of the peace delegates from various countries. He said that we will tell our people that the Chinese people are working happily and united in the struggle for peace; we will also tell our people that there is freedom of religious belief and equality of nationalities in China. Finally, Nuhad Gadri shouted: "Long live the friendship between the people of Arab countries and the Chinese people!" "Long live world peace!"

### 阿拉伯国家和平代表离新疆到上海

1955-08-07
第1版()
专栏：

    阿拉伯国家和平代表离新疆到上海
    据新华社乌鲁木齐讯　阿拉伯国家的和平代表四十人已结束在新疆的访问，在三日清晨乘飞机离开乌鲁木齐市。新疆省人民政府主席、中国伊斯兰教协会主任鲍尔汉，乌鲁木齐市副市长马仲才、阿·赛德、中国人民保卫世界和平委员会乌鲁木齐市分会主席努斯热提，以及宗教界、工商界等人士一百二十多人前往机场欢送。
    叙利亚的代表穆罕默德·伊斯梅尔·赫旺德在机场上代表各国和平代表讲话，他说：现在我们怀着无限惜别之情向你们告别了。我们虽然相聚的时间很短，但内心里充满了对你们的热爱。我们看到了你们在原来是荒凉和沙漠的地方，建筑起的许多工厂和大片的绿洲。你们这些在和平劳动战线上的成就，给我们留下了永远不会忘记的印象。我们要把这些成就和你们对我们的支持告诉给我们的人民，我们还要告诉他们，你们这些有着不同语言、不同信仰的各民族人民是如何团结一致地为一个共同的目标而进行工作。朋友们，我们无法报答你们的深情厚谊，但我们要同你们一起、同世界人民一起继续为和平而进行斗争。
    代表们离开乌鲁木齐的前天，曾参加了新疆省人民政府主席鲍尔汉主持的座谈会。会上，叙利亚、约旦的代表都叙述了在参观访问中的感想和印象，他们对新疆各族人民团结友爱共同建设祖国和保卫世界和平的精神表示赞扬，并感谢对他们的热情招待。
    阿拉伯国家的和平代表们在乌鲁木齐市期间，曾同中国的穆斯林们一起欢度古尔邦节。和平代表们还参观了地方国营新疆十月汽车修配厂、国营新疆七一棉纺织厂和乌鲁木齐发电厂等工厂。在八月一日中国人民解放军建军节的当天，和平代表们到距乌鲁木齐约一百四、五十公里的玛纳斯垦区，访问了中国人民解放军新疆军区的农业生产建设部队。
    新华社上海四日电　应邀来我国访问的叙利亚、伊拉克、黎巴嫩、约旦、苏丹五个阿拉伯国家的和平代表一行四十人，四日由新疆乘专机到达上海。陪同前来的有中国人民保卫世界和平委员会联络部副部长朱子奇和新疆省分会副主席贾库林。
    到机场欢迎的，有中国人民保卫世界和平委员会上海市分会副主席钟民、伊斯兰教教长和各界人士等。我国出席赫尔辛基世界和平大会代表杨士达也到机场欢迎。
    代表们下飞机后，和欢迎者亲切握手，并接受了少年先锋队队员的献花。
    在机场上，钟民代表上海市人民对和平代表来上海访问表示热烈欢迎。他说：上海人民对你们为世界和平的不懈努力，表示由衷的敬意。叙利亚代表努哈德·加德里代表各国和平代表对上海人民的热情欢迎表示感谢。他说，我们回去要告诉我们的人民，中国人民正愉快劳动，团结一致为和平事业斗争；我们还要告诉我们的人民，在中国宗教信仰是自由的，民族是平等的。最后努哈德·加德里并高呼：“阿拉伯各国人民和中国人民的友谊万岁！”“世界和平万岁！”
