Title: Premier Kim Il-sung Attends Korean National Games
Author:
Date: 1955-08-18
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Pyongyang, August 17th: Kim Il-sung, Premier of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea, along with Kim Tu-bong, Park Jung-ai, and other leaders of the Korean Workers' Party and the government, watched the Korean National Games held at the Moranbong Stadium in Pyongyang on the morning of the 16th.

A. B. Aristov, head of the Soviet government delegation, Zhu De, head of the People's Republic of China delegation, and all members of the Soviet and Chinese delegations, as well as Yang Yong, commander of the Chinese People's Volunteers, were also invited to watch the Games.

Athletes from all over Korea performed wonderful programs on the morning of the 16th.

At 8:00 PM on the same day, Park Jung-ai, Vice-Chairman of the Central Committee of the Korean Workers' Party, and Vice Premiers of the Cabinet Park Chang-ok, Choi Chang-ik, Hong Myung-hee, Jung Il-ryong, Park Ui-wan, and other party and government leaders accompanied the Soviet government delegation headed by Aristov and the People's Republic of China delegation headed by Zhu De to watch the Korean national drama "The Tale of Shim Chong" at the Moranbong Theater.

Zhu De, head of the People's Republic of China delegation, and all members of the delegation also visited the Korean Fatherland Liberation War Memorial Hall on the afternoon of the 16th. Ha Yang-cheon, Minister of Social Sciences of the Central Committee of the Korean Workers' Party, accompanied the delegation on the visit.

### 金日成首相观看朝鲜全国体育大会

1955-08-18
第4版()
专栏：

    金日成首相观看朝鲜全国体育大会
    新华社平壤十七日电　朝鲜民主主义人民共和国内阁首相金日成和金枓奉、朴正爱等朝鲜劳动党和政府的领导人，十六日上午观看了在平壤牡丹峰运动场举行的朝鲜全国体育大会。
    苏联政府代表团团长阿·布·阿里斯托夫、中华人民共和国代表团团长朱德和苏、中两国代表团的全体团员以及中国人民志愿军司令员杨勇都应邀观看了体育大会。
    来自朝鲜各地的运动员在十六日上午表演了精彩的节目。
    同日晚八时，朝鲜劳动党中央委员会副委员长朴正爱，内阁副首相朴昌玉、崔昌益、洪命熹、郑一龙、朴义琓等党和政府的领导人陪同以阿里斯托夫为首的苏联政府代表团和以朱德为首的中华人民共和国代表团等在牡丹峰剧场观看了朝鲜民族戏剧“沈清传”。
    中华人民共和国代表团团长朱德和全体团员在十六日下午还参观了朝鲜祖国解放战争纪念馆。陪同代表团参观的有朝鲜劳动党中央委员会社会科学部长河仰天。
