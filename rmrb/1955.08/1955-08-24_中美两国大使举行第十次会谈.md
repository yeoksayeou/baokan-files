Title: Sino-U.S. Ambassadorial Talks Held for the Tenth Time
Author:
Date: 1955-08-24
Page: 1

　　Xinhua News Agency, Geneva, August 23rd – The Sino-U.S. ambassadorial talks held their tenth meeting at the Palais des Nations at 10:00 AM on the 23rd. The meeting adjourned at 10:30 AM. The next meeting will be held on August 25th (Thursday) at 10:00 AM.

　　At the meeting on the 23rd, the ambassadors of both sides continued to discuss the first item on the agenda – the repatriation of civilians from both sides.

### 中美两国大使举行第十次会谈

1955-08-24
第1版()
专栏：

　　中美两国大使举行第十次会谈
    新华社日内瓦二十三日电　中美两国大使级会谈在二十三日上午十时在国联大厦举行第十次会议。会议在十时三十分休会，下次会议将在八月二十五日（星期四）上午十时举行。
    在二十三日会议上，双方大使继续讨论了第一项议程——双方平民回国问题。
