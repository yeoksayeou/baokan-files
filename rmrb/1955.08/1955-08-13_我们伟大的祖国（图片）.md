    Our Great Motherland
    In Qingpu County, Jiangsu Province, farmers are actively eliminating rice borers in order to ensure a good rice harvest and prevent borer damage. Young members of the Cangyuan Agricultural Production Cooperative are using gasoline lamps to catch rice moths at night.

专栏：

    我们伟大的祖国
    江苏省青浦县农民为了确保水稻丰收和防止螟害，积极除治螟虫。仓园农业生产合作社青年社员们在夜晚用汽灯捕捉螟蛾。
