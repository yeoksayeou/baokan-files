Title: Chief Commissioner Zheng Guolu of the Korean-Chinese Side Hands Over Declarations of the Korean and Chinese Sides to the Neutral Nations Supervisory Commission
Author: 
Date: 1955-08-14
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency, Pyongyang, 13th: The Korean Central News Agency, Kaesong, reports: Major General Zheng Guolu, Chief Commissioner of the Korean-Chinese side of the Military Armistice Commission, authorized by the Government of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea, delivered a copy of the statement issued by the Ministry of Foreign Affairs of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea regarding the provocative acts of the Syngman Rhee clique to the members of the four countries of the Neutral Nations Supervisory Commission on the 11th.

On the 12th, Major General Zheng Guolu, authorized by the Government of the People's Republic of China, delivered a copy of the statement of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs of the People's Republic of China regarding the same issue to the members of the four countries of the Neutral Nations Supervisory Commission.

### 朝中方面首席委员郑国禄  把朝中两国声明交给中立国监察委员会

1955-08-14
第4版()
专栏：

    朝中方面首席委员郑国禄
    把朝中两国声明交给中立国监察委员会
    新华社平壤十三日电　朝鲜中央通讯社开城讯：军事停战委员会朝中方面首席委员郑国禄少将由朝鲜民主主义人民共和国政府授权在十一日把朝鲜民主主义人民共和国外务省就李承晚集团的挑衅行为发表的声明一份交给中立国监察委员会的四个国家的委员。
    在十二日，郑国禄少将由中华人民共和国政府授权把中华人民共和国外交部关于同一问题的声明一份交给中立国监察委员会的四个国家的委员。
