Title: Nepalese King Hosts Banquet for Chinese Government Delegation
Author:
Date: 1955-08-04
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, New Delhi, August 3rd: King Mahendra Bir Bikram Shah Deva of Nepal hosted a banquet at the Royal Palace on the 1st to celebrate the establishment of diplomatic relations between the People's Republic of China and the Kingdom of Nepal, in honor of the Chinese government delegation.

    Before the start of the banquet, the King and Queen received Yuan Zhongxian, head of the Chinese government delegation, and the delegation members, and exchanged greetings.

    Those attending the banquet included Sardar Gunjiman Singh (Chief Advisor to the King of Nepal), head of the Kingdom of Nepal government delegation participating in the Sino-Nepalese diplomatic relations negotiations, and delegation members Sowarag Jung Thapa (Minister of Foreign Affairs), Sardar Bhim Bahadur Pande (Minister of Planning and Development), Kaiser Bahadur Khatri Chhetri (Member of the Public Service Commission), and Bharat Raj Pandalay (Vice Minister of Foreign Affairs).

    Also present were: Prince Himalaya Bir Bikram Shah Dev, General Kiran Shamsher Jung Bahadur Rana, Commander-in-Chief of the Nepalese Army, General Anand Shamsher Jung Bahadur Rana, Deputy Chief Advisor to the King, Bogendra Raj Guru, Advisor to the King, Parendra Bikram Shah, Advisor to the King, Anurudha Prasad Singh, Advisor to the King, Secretary-General Sardar Hansa Man Singh, and Lieutenant General Yoga Bikram Rana, Military Secretary to the King.

    On the King's orders, General Kiran Shamsher Jung Bahadur Rana, Commander-in-Chief of the Army, proposed a toast to the health of Chairman Mao Zedong of the People's Republic of China. Yuan Zhongxian, head of the Chinese government delegation, also proposed a toast to the health of the King of Nepal and the prosperity of Nepal.

    The banquet proceeded in a warm and friendly atmosphere.

### 尼泊尔国王设宴招待我国政府代表团

1955-08-04
第1版()
专栏：

    尼泊尔国王设宴招待我国政府代表团
    新华社新德里三日电　尼泊尔国王马亨德拉·比尔·比克拉姆·沙阿·德瓦为了庆祝中华人民共和国和尼泊尔王国之间建立外交关系，一日在王宫中设宴招待中华人民共和国政府代表团。
    在宴会开始前，国王和王后接见了中国政府代表团团长袁仲贤和团员，并且互相致意。
    出席宴会的有参加中尼两国建立外交关系谈判的尼泊尔王国政府代表团团长萨达尔·甘吉曼·辛格（尼泊尔国王首席顾问），团员索瓦格·詹格·塔帕（外交大臣）、萨达尔·比姆·巴哈杜尔·潘德（计划和开发大臣）、凯塞·巴哈杜尔·喀特里·契特里（公务委员会委员）、巴拉特·拉吉·潘达礼（副外交大臣）。
    出席宴会的还有：希马拉雅·比尔·比克拉姆·沙阿·德夫亲王，尼泊尔陆军总司令基兰·沙姆谢尔·忠格·巴哈杜尔·拉纳将军，国王副首席顾问阿南德·沙姆谢尔·忠格·巴哈杜尔·拉纳将军，国王顾问博甘德拉·拉志·古鲁，国王顾问普伦德拉·比克拉姆·沙阿，国王顾问阿努鲁达·普拉萨德·辛格，秘书长萨达尔·汉萨·曼·辛格，国王军事秘书约加·比克拉姆·拉纳中将。
    根据国王的命令，陆军总司令基兰·沙姆谢尔·忠格·巴哈杜尔·拉纳将军建议为中华人民共和国主席毛泽东的健康干杯。中国政府代表团团长袁仲贤也建议为尼泊尔国王的健康和尼泊尔的繁荣干杯。
    宴会在热烈友好的空气中进行。
