Title: Such "Approval"
Author: Zhongyang Meishu Xueyuan Manhua Zu Jiti Chuangzuo
Date: 1955-08-04
Page: 2

Every Collective Mess Hall Unit Must Save Food

    Such "Approval"
    Collectively Created by the Cartoon Group of the Central Academy of Fine Arts
    Some unit leaders believe that the work of verifying grain supply is a "small matter" and find it troublesome, thus adopting a bureaucratic attitude. This cartoon, exhibited at the Beijing Food Saving Exhibition, satirizes this phenomenon. (The painting published here has been slightly modified by this newspaper.)

专栏：每个集体伙食单位都要节约粮食

    这样的“批准”
