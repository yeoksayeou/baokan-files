Title: Ten Indian Members of Parliament Decide to Enter Goa to Participate in the Struggle; Goa Congress Peaceful Resistance Council Calls on People to Conduct Non-Cooperation Movement
Author:
Date: 1955-08-25
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, New Delhi, August 24th: Delhi newspapers reported that ten Indian Members of Parliament decided on the 22nd to participate in the peaceful resistance movement to liberate Goa in the second week of September. It is expected that some members of the Provincial Legislative Assembly will also join them. The time and place of their entry into Goa will be decided after consultation with the various parties' support Goa liberation committee.

Xinhua News Agency, New Delhi, August 24th: According to the Indian Press Trust, the Goa Congress Peaceful Resistance Council held a meeting on the 22nd and 23rd in Karwar, an Indian port not far south of Goa. The meeting passed a resolution calling on all Goans to use all possible actions within the scope of the non-cooperation movement to paralyze the Portuguese government in Goa.

### 印度十名国会议员决定进入果阿参加斗争　果阿国大党和平抵抗理事会号召人民进行不合作运动

1955-08-25
第4版()
专栏：

　　印度十名国会议员决定进入果阿参加斗争
　　果阿国大党和平抵抗理事会号召人民进行不合作运动
    新华社新德里二十四日电　德里报纸报道，十名印度国会议员二十二日决定在九月份第二个星期参加争取解放果阿的和平抵抗运动。预料一些省议会议员也将同他们一起参加。他们进入果阿的时间和地点将在与各党派支援解放果阿委员会磋商后决定。
    新华社新德里二十四日电　据印度报业托辣斯报道，果阿国大党和平抵抗理事会二十二日和二十三日在果阿以南不远的印度港口卡瓦尔举行了会议。会议通过决议，号召全体果阿人在不合作运动的范围内用一切可能的行动来使果阿的葡萄牙政府陷于瘫痪。
