Title: Indonesian Communist Party Candidates Determine Election Platform; Indonesian Trade Union Confederation and Nationalist Party Leaders Oppose Attempts to Postpone General Election
Author:
Date: 1955-08-30
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Jakarta, August 29th: According to a report in the Jakarta "People's Daily" on August 29th, the Central Committee of the Indonesian Communist Party held a meeting on the 28th with non-party figures who were included on the Indonesian Communist Party's list of candidates for the general election. The meeting agreed to adopt the "National United Government Program" adopted at the Third Plenary Session of the Central Committee of the Indonesian Communist Party as its election platform.

Among the non-party figures who attended the meeting were labor leaders, peasant leaders, women leaders, members of parliament, former cabinet ministers, scholars, poets, and painters.

The newspaper reported that similar meetings between the Indonesian Communist Party and non-party figures on the Indonesian Communist Party's list of general election candidates will be held in the provincial capitals.

Xinhua News Agency, Jakarta, August 29th: The Indonesian "People's Daily" published a statement on August 29th from the Central Secretariat of the Indonesian Trade Union Confederation. The statement said that the Indonesian general election is facing the danger of being sabotaged. The statement called on all Indonesian workers to provide all assistance to ensure that the general election is held as scheduled.

The statement said that the Indonesian Trade Union Confederation guarantees to give full support to implementing the five tasks put forward by President Sukarno in his Independence Day speech: strengthening national unity, suppressing elements disrupting public order, redoubling efforts in construction, recovering West Irian, and not postponing the general election. The Indonesian Trade Union Confederation opposes any attempts to postpone the general election.

According to an Antara News Agency report on August 28th, the First Deputy Chairman of the Indonesian Nationalist Party, Mangunsarkoro, warned people in a mass meeting in Bandung to be vigilant against attempts by foreign capital to bribe certain political parties and politicians in order to influence the election results. The Second Deputy Chairman of the Indonesian Nationalist Party, Wilopo, said at the meeting that the Nationalist Party will insist on holding the general election on the scheduled date. He said that this is the will of the people, and no one can disobey the will of the people.

### 印度尼西亚共产党候选人确定竞选政纲　印度尼西亚总工会和国民党领袖反对推迟普选的企图

1955-08-30
第4版()
专栏：

　　印度尼西亚共产党候选人确定竞选政纲
　　印度尼西亚总工会和国民党领袖反对推迟普选的企图
    新华社雅加达二十九日电　据雅加达“人民日报”二十九日报道，印度尼西亚共产党中央委员会同被列入印度尼西亚共产党的普选候选人名单的无党派人士在二十八日举行了会议。会议同意以印度尼西亚共产党中央委员会第三次全体会议上通过的“民族联合政府纲领”作为竞选政纲。
    参加这次会议的无党派人士中有工人领袖、农民领袖、妇女领袖、国会议员、前内阁部长、学者、诗人和画家等。
    这家报纸报道，印度尼西亚共产党和被列入印度尼西亚共产党的普选候选人名单的无党派人士的类似会议将在各省省会举行。
    新华社雅加达二十九日电　印度尼西亚“人民日报”二十九日刊载了印度尼西亚总工会中央书记处的一项声明。声明说，印度尼西亚的普选已面临着遭受破坏的危险。声明号召印度尼西亚全体工人给予一切援助，使普选得以如期举行。
    声明说，印度尼西亚总工会保证给予充分支持，以执行苏加诺总统在独立日发表的演说中所提出的加强全国团结、镇压扰乱治安的分子、加倍努力建设、收回西伊里安、不推延普选等五项任务。印度尼西亚总工会反对任何推迟普选的企图。
    另据安塔拉通讯社二十八日报道，印度尼西亚国民党第一副主席曼贡萨科罗在万隆一个群众大会上发表演说，警告人们警惕外国资本对某些政党和政客行贿以图影响选举结果的企图。印度尼西亚国民党第二副主席韦洛坡在会上说，国民党将坚持在预定日期进行普选。他说，这是人民的意愿，任何人都不能违抗人民的意愿。
