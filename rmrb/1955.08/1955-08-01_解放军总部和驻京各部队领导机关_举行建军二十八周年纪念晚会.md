Title: PLA Headquarters and Leading Organs of Troops Stationed in Beijing Hold Anniversary Celebration Soirée for the 28th Anniversary of the Founding of the Army
Author:
Date: 1955-08-01
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, July 31st. The headquarters of the Chinese People's Liberation Army and the leading organs of the various services and arms stationed in Beijing held a celebration soirée in the capital at 7:30 pm on July 31st to commemorate the 28th anniversary of the founding of the Chinese People's Liberation Army on "August 1st".  Those attending the celebration soirée included Peng Dehuai, Vice Premier of the State Council and concurrently Minister of National Defense of the People's Republic of China, the heads of various departments of the headquarters of the Chinese People's Liberation Army, the heads of various services and arms, senior cadres of troops stationed in Beijing, and responsible persons from the Beijing Municipal Committee of the Chinese Communist Party, the Beijing Municipal People's Committee, and various people's organizations in Beijing, totaling 800 people. Wonderful song and dance performances were staged at the celebration soirée.

### 解放军总部和驻京各部队领导机关  举行建军二十八周年纪念晚会

1955-08-01
第1版()
专栏：

    解放军总部和驻京各部队领导机关
    举行建军二十八周年纪念晚会
    新华社三十一日讯　中国人民解放军总部和驻京各军种、兵种领导机关三十一日下午七时三十分在首都举行中国人民解放军“八一”建军二十八周年纪念晚会。出席纪念晚会的有中华人民共和国国务院副总理兼国防部长彭德怀，中国人民解放军总部各部门、各军种、兵种首长和驻京部队的高级干部以及中共北京市委员会、北京市人民委员会、北京市各人民团体负责人等共八百人。纪念晚会上演出了精彩的歌舞节目。
