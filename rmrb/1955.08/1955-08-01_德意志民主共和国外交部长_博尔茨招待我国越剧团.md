Title: Minister of Foreign Affairs of the German Democratic Republic, Bolz, Hosts Chinese Yue Opera Troupe
Author:
Date: 1955-08-01
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Berlin, July 30th. The Minister of Foreign Affairs of the German Democratic Republic, Lothar Bolz, and his wife held a tea party on the afternoon of the 28th to host all members of the Chinese Yue Opera Troupe. Among those present at the tea party were the Premier of the German Democratic Republic, Otto Grotewohl, and his wife, and Wang Daohan, Secretary of the Central Committee of the Socialist Unity Party of Germany. Wang Yutian, Chargé d'Affaires ad interim of the Chinese Embassy in the German Democratic Republic, and Counselor Xu Ming also attended the tea party.

At the tea party, Grotewohl spoke highly of the Chinese Yue Opera Troupe's performances. Bolz and Xu Guangping, head of the Chinese Yue Opera Troupe, also delivered speeches, believing that the visit of the Chinese Yue Opera Troupe would help strengthen the friendship between the people of Germany and China.

### 德意志民主共和国外交部长  博尔茨招待我国越剧团

1955-08-01
第4版()
专栏：

    德意志民主共和国外交部长
    博尔茨招待我国越剧团
    新华社柏林三十日电　德意志民主共和国外交部长洛塔·博尔茨夫妇二十八日下午举行茶会，招待中国越剧团全体团员。出席茶会的，有德意志民主共和国总理格罗提渥夫妇、德国统一社会党中央委员会书记汪戴尔等人。中国驻德意志民主共和国大使馆临时代办王雨田和参赞徐明也出席了茶会。
    在茶会上，格罗提渥对中国越剧团的演出评价甚高。博尔茨和中国越剧团团长许广平也先后致词，认为中国越剧团的访问有助于加强德、中两国人民的友谊。
