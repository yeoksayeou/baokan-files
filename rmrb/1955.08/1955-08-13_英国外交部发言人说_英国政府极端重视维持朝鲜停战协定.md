Title: British Foreign Office Spokesman Says British Government Attaches Extreme Importance to Maintaining Korean Armistice Agreement
Author:
Date: 1955-08-13
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, London, August 12th – According to a report from an Agence France-Presse correspondent in London, a British Foreign Office spokesman said on the 11th, commenting on the statement from the Chinese Foreign Ministry, that the British government "attaches extreme importance" to maintaining the Korean Armistice Agreement.

The spokesman also said that Britain "fully recognizes the view that the United Nations Command is responsible for the safety of the Neutral Nations Supervisory Commission."

### 英国外交部发言人说  英国政府极端重视维持朝鲜停战协定

1955-08-13
第4版()
专栏：

    英国外交部发言人说
    英国政府极端重视维持朝鲜停战协定
    新华社十二日讯　据法新社驻伦敦记者报道，英国外交部发言人十一日在评论中国外交部的声明时说，英国政府“极端重视”维持朝鲜停战协定。
    发言人又说，英国“完全承认这种看法：联合国军总司令部对于中立国监察委员会的安全负有责任”。
