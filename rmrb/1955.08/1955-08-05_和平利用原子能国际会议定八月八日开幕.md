Title: International Conference on Peaceful Uses of Atomic Energy Scheduled to Open on August 8

Author: 
Date: 1955-08-05
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Geneva, August 3rd: The preparatory work for the International Conference on the Peaceful Uses of Atomic Energy, scheduled to open on August 8, is nearing completion. It is expected that sixty-six countries will participate in the conference. The various halls in the Palais des Nations for plenary sessions and departmental work have been prepared. The work of arranging the exhibition showing the achievements of various countries in the peaceful use of nuclear energy is almost complete. The Soviet exhibition hall occupies one of the largest halls in the exhibition, and will display exhibits illustrating the application of nuclear energy in the industrial sector, the biological sector, and the application of radioactive isotopes in medical and chemical research work.

### 和平利用原子能国际会议定八月八日开幕

1955-08-05
第4版()
专栏：

    和平利用原子能国际会议定八月八日开幕
    新华社四日讯　据塔斯社日内瓦三日讯：定于八月八日开幕的和平利用原子能国际会议的筹备工作已经快要结束。预料将有六十六个国家参加会议。国联大厦内供举行全体会议和进行各部门工作的各个大厅已经准备妥当。表明各国在把核子能用于和平目的方面的成就的展览会的布置工作差不多已经完成。苏联展览馆占展览会中最大的大厅之一，其中将陈列说明苏联在工业部门、生物学部门应用核子能，以及在医学、化学研究工作中应用放射性同位素的展品。
