Title: French Foreign Ministry Issues Communiqué Regarding Ngô Đình Diệm Government Statement
Author:
Date: 1955-08-12
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency, August 11th, from a United Press report from Paris: Following the Ngô Đình Diệm government's statement on the issue of Vietnamese general elections on the 10th, the French Foreign Ministry issued a communiqué regarding this on the same day.

The French Foreign Ministry said: "Authoritative French figures express some understanding of the difficulties that Prime Minister Ngô Đình Diệm must face," but the communiqué recalled that French Prime Minister Faure and other cabinet members have repeatedly stated that "nothing should be done to hinder the implementation of the Geneva Accords."

### 法国外交部就吴庭艳政府声明发表公报

1955-08-12
第4版()
专栏：

    法国外交部就吴庭艳政府声明发表公报
    新华社十一日讯　据合众社从巴黎报道：在吴庭艳政府十日就越南普选问题发表声明之后，法国外交部在当天对此发表了一个公报。
    法国外交部说：“权威的法国人士对吴庭艳总理所必须面临的困难表示有某种谅解”，但是公报追述说：法国总理富尔和内阁的其他阁员曾一再说明，“不应该做任何事情来阻碍日内瓦协定的履行”。
