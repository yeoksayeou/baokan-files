Title: Grand Gathering Held in Pyongyang by All Circles to Celebrate the Tenth Anniversary of Korea's Liberation
Author: 
Date: 1955-08-16
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, Pyongyang, August 15th: A grand gathering was held by all circles in Pyongyang at the Moranbong Theater on the evening of the 14th to solemnly celebrate the tenth anniversary of Korea's liberation on "August 15th."

More than 700 people participated in the conference, including personnel from the Korean Workers' Party and state organs, representatives of various democratic parties and social organizations, senior generals of the People's Army, Heroes of the Republic, Labor Heroes, and representatives of the working people of Pyongyang.

Invited to attend the conference were diplomatic envoys and diplomatic officials from the Soviet Union, China, and other countries stationed in Korea, the Chinese People's Volunteer Army delegation, the Soviet cultural delegation and art troupe currently visiting Korea, and Japanese peace representatives attending the Helsinki World Peace Congress.

At exactly 6 o'clock, when the leaders of the Korean Workers' Party and government, headed by Premier Kim Il-sung, along with the Soviet government delegation headed by Aristov, the People's Republic of China delegation headed by Zhu De, and Yang Yong, Commander of the Chinese People's Volunteer Army, appeared on the rostrum, all attendees rose in unison, and sustained applause resounded throughout the venue.

The conference was presided over by Kim Tu-bong, Chairman of the Standing Committee of the Supreme People's Assembly of Korea.

Premier Kim Il-sung delivered an important report at the conference. Kim Il-sung's report was met with continuous and enthusiastic applause from all attendees.

Next, Aristov, the head of the Soviet government delegation, spoke. On behalf of the Communist Party of the Soviet Union, the Soviet government, and all the Soviet people, he extended heartfelt and brotherly congratulations to the Korean people. He highly praised the contribution of the Korean people, with the assistance of the Chinese People's Volunteer Army, in defeating the aggressors and winning the Korean armistice through their own initiative, to the cause of peace. Aristov expressed his belief that the Korean people would surely achieve brilliant victories in peaceful construction, just as they had achieved victories in the war.

Zhu De, head of the delegation of the People's Republic of China, then spoke.

Diplomatic envoys of various people's democratic countries stationed in Korea also spoke at the conference.

### 庆祝朝鲜解放十周年  平壤各界举行盛大集会

1955-08-16
第1版()
专栏：

    庆祝朝鲜解放十周年
    平壤各界举行盛大集会
    据新华社平壤十五日讯  平壤各界十四日晚在牡丹峰剧场举行盛大集会，隆重庆祝“八一五”朝鲜解放十周年。
    参加大会的有朝鲜劳动党和国家机关人员，各民主政党和社会团体的代表，人民军高级将领和共和国英雄，劳动英雄，平壤市劳动人民代表等共七百多人。
    应邀出席大会的有驻朝鲜的苏联、中国和其他国家外交使节和外交官员，中国人民志愿军代表团，正在访问朝鲜的苏联文化代表团和艺术团以及出席赫尔辛基世界和平大会的日本和平代表等。
    六时正，当以金日成首相为首的朝鲜劳动党和政府领导人同以阿里斯托夫为首的苏联政府代表团和以朱德为首的中华人民共和国代表团以及中国人民志愿军司令员杨勇等在主席台上出现时，全体到会者一致起立，全场响起了经久不息的掌声。
    大会由朝鲜最高人民会议常任委员会委员长金枓奉主持。
    金日成首相在大会上做了重要报告。金日成的报告受到全体到会者不断的热烈鼓掌。
    接着，苏联政府代表团团长阿里斯托夫讲话。他以苏联共产党、苏联政府和全体苏联人民的名义向朝鲜人民致衷心的兄弟般的祝贺。他盛赞朝鲜人民在中国人民志愿军协助下打败侵略者并且采取主动而赢得的朝鲜停战对和平事业的贡献。阿里斯托夫表示相信，朝鲜人民一定能够像在战争中取得胜利一样，在和平建设中也取得光辉胜利。
    中华人民共和国代表团团长朱德继起讲话。
    在大会上讲话的还有各人民民主国家驻朝鲜的外交使节。
