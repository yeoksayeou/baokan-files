Title: Pakistani Prime Minister Announces New Cabinet List
Author:
Date: 1955-08-14
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, New Delhi, August 13th: Karachi News: Pakistan's new Prime Minister, Chaudhri Muhammad Ali, announced the list of the new cabinet on the 12th. In the new cabinet, he serves as Prime Minister and Minister of Finance, and temporarily concurrently manages foreign affairs, defense, economy, and federal relations. The Minister of the Interior is United Front leader Fazlul Huq, the Minister of Commerce and Industry is the incumbent Habib Ibrahim Rahimtoola, and the Minister of Information and Broadcasting is the Muslim League's Ali Muhammad Rashidi.

Of his total of eleven cabinet members, the Muslim League occupies five positions, the United Front five, and non-party one.

### 巴基斯坦总理宣布新阁名单

1955-08-14
第4版()
专栏：

    巴基斯坦总理宣布新阁名单
    新华社新德里十三日讯　喀喇蚩消息：巴基斯坦新任总理乔德里·穆罕默德·阿里十二日宣布了新内阁的名单。在新内阁中，他担任总理兼财政部长，并且暂时兼管外交、国防、经济和联邦关系事务，内政部长是联合阵线领袖法兹鲁尔·胡克，工商部长是原任的哈比布·易卜拉欣·拉欣图拉，宣传广播部长是回教联盟的阿里·穆罕默德·拉希迪。
    在他全部十一名阁员中，回教联盟占五名，联合阵线五名，无党派一名。
