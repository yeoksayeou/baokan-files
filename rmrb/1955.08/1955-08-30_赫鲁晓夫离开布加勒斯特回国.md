Title: Khrushchev Leaves Bucharest for Home

Author:
Date: 1955-08-30
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency dispatch: After participating in the celebrations for the 11th anniversary of the liberation of Romania, the First Secretary of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of the Soviet Union, Khrushchev, left Bucharest on the 26th.

Tens of thousands of working people in Bucharest gathered in the square in front of the station to bid farewell to Comrade Khrushchev.

Present at the station to see off Khrushchev were: Gheorghe Gheorghiu-Dej, Chairman of the Council of Ministers of the Romanian People's Republic; Groza, Chairman of the Presidium of the Grand National Assembly; Apostol, First Secretary of the Central Committee of the Romanian Workers' Party; and other leaders of the Romanian Workers' Party and the government.

### 赫鲁晓夫离开布加勒斯特回国

1955-08-30
第4版()
专栏：

　　赫鲁晓夫离开布加勒斯特回国
    据新华社讯　苏联共产党中央委员会第一书记赫鲁晓夫在参加了罗马尼亚解放十一周年庆祝典礼后，于二十六日离开布加勒斯特。
    布加勒斯特成千的劳动人民聚集在车站前面的广场上欢送赫鲁晓夫同志。
    在车站上欢送赫鲁晓夫的有：罗马尼亚人民共和国部长会议主席乔治乌—德治，国民议会主席团主席格罗查，罗马尼亚工人党中央委员会第一书记阿波斯托尔，以及罗马尼亚工人党和政府的其他领导人。
