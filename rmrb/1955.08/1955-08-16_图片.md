Author: Qian Sijie
Date: 1955-08-16
Page: 4

    The delegation of the People's Republic of China, led by Vice Chairman Zhu De, which was invited to the Democratic People's Republic of Korea to attend the celebration of the tenth anniversary of the liberation of Korea, arrived in Pyongyang on August 13. The picture shows Vice Chairman Zhu De and Premier Kim Il-sung, Marshal of the Korean Cabinet, and Chairman Kim Tu-bong of the Standing Committee of the Supreme People's Assembly of Korea, who went to the station to welcome them, at the station. From left to right: Kim Tu-bong, Zhu De, Kim Il-sung, Teng Daiyuan.

                    Xinhua News Agency Reporter Qian Sijie photo
