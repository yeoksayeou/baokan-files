Title: Government Meeting of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam Cables Our Government, Expressing Satisfaction with the Results of the Sino-Vietnamese Talks
Author:
Date: 1955-08-02
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, 1st.

    Government Meeting of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam Cables Our Government, Expressing Satisfaction with the Results of the Sino-Vietnamese Talks
    Xinhua News Agency, August 1st - The government meeting of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam cabled the government of the People's Republic of China on July 26th, expressing satisfaction with the results of the Sino-Vietnamese talks. The cable reads as follows:
    The government meeting of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam listened to the work report of the delegation of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam government led by President Ho Chi Minh on the satisfactory results obtained during the visit to the fraternal countries of the Soviet Union and China.
    The Vietnamese government hereby expresses its heartfelt gratitude to the great Chinese government and people for the warm welcome extended to our delegation.
    The Vietnamese government meeting expresses its extreme satisfaction with the complete agreement of views between the Chinese and Vietnamese governments on various international political issues and on various methods for consolidating peace in Indochina and realizing the unification of Vietnam.
    The government of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam expresses its sincere gratitude to the Chinese government and people for the selfless and friendly assistance given to the restoration and development of our economic and cultural undertakings, and we will rationally use this assistance in the cause of increasing industrial and agricultural production, restoring and developing commerce, improving the people's living standards, and healing the wounds of war.
    The government and people of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam believe that the consistency of views between fraternal countries and the economic and cultural assistance given to us is a new proof of the profound friendship of the Chinese government and people towards the Vietnamese government and people, which is a great encouragement to the just struggle of the Vietnamese people and a major contribution to the consolidation of peace in Southeast Asia and the world.
    Long live the People's Republic of China!
    Long live the consolidation and development of the eternal friendship between Vietnam and China!

### 越南民主共和国政府会议致电我国政府  对中越两国会谈的成果表示满意

1955-08-02
第1版()
专栏：

    越南民主共和国政府会议致电我国政府
    对中越两国会谈的成果表示满意
    新华社一日讯　越南民主共和国政府会议在七月二十六日致电中华人民共和国政府，对中越会谈的成果表示满意。电文如下：
    越南民主共和国政府会议听取了由胡志明主席率领的越南民主共和国政府代表团关于访问苏、中两兄弟国家期间获得美满结果的工作报告。
    对于伟大的中国政府和人民对我们代表团的热忱欢迎，越南政府谨表示衷心的感谢。
    越南政府会议对中、越两国政府在各项国际政治问题上以及巩固印度支那的和平和实现越南统一的各项方法上的观点的完全一致，表示极为满意。
    越南民主共和国政府对中国政府和人民给予为恢复和发展我国经济和文化事业的无私的友谊援助表示挚诚的感谢，我们将在增加工农业生产，恢复和发展商业，提高人民生活水平和医治战争创伤的事业中，合理地使用此项援助。
    越南民主共和国政府和人民认为，兄弟国家之间的观点一致以及给予我们的经济文化方面的援助，是中国政府和人民对越南政府和人民深厚友谊的新的证明，这对越南人民的正义斗争是一个巨大的鼓舞，对东南亚及世界和平的巩固是一个重大的贡献。
    中华人民共和国万岁！
    越中两国永恒友谊的巩固与发展万岁！
