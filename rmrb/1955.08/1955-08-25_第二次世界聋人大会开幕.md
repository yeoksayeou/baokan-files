Title: Second World Congress of the Deaf Opens

Author: 
Date: 1955-08-25
Page: 4

　  Xinhua News Agency, Prague, August 24th: According to a Zagreb dispatch from the Yugoslav News Agency: The Second World Congress of the Deaf opened in Zagreb, Yugoslavia on the 23rd.

　  Nearly 1,500 delegates from thirty-two countries are attending the congress. The conference will discuss the settlement of deaf people and the tasks of the World Federation of the Deaf. Seventy reports will be presented at the meeting.

### 第二次世界聋人大会开幕

1955-08-25
第4版()
专栏：

　  第二次世界聋人大会开幕
    新华社布拉格二十四日电　据南斯拉夫通讯社札格雷布消息：第二次世界聋人大会二十三日在南斯拉夫札格雷布开幕。
    出席这次大会的，有三十二个国家的将近一千五百个代表。大会将讨论聋人安顿的情况和世界聋人联合会的任务。有七十个报告将在会上宣读。
