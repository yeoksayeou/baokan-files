Title: Premier Zhou Enlai's Telegram to Prime Minister Harahap
Author:
Date: 1955-08-17
Page: 1

To His Excellency Mr. Burhanuddin Harahap, Prime Minister of the Republic of Indonesia:

On the occasion of the tenth anniversary of the independence of the Republic of Indonesia, I, on behalf of the Government of the People's Republic of China and in my own name, extend warm congratulations to the Government of the Republic of Indonesia and to Your Excellency, and wish that the friendly cooperative relations already established between China and Indonesia will continue to develop.

Zhou Enlai
Premier of the State Council of the People's Republic of China
August 15, 1955, in Beijing

### 周恩来总理致哈拉哈普总理电

1955-08-17
第1版()
专栏：

    周恩来总理致哈拉哈普总理电印度尼西亚共和国总理伯汉努丁·哈拉哈普先生阁下：
    值此印度尼西亚共和国独立十周年纪念之际，谨以中华人民共和国政府和我个人的名义，向印度尼西亚共和国政府和阁下表示热烈的祝贺，并祝中国和印度尼西亚之间已经建立起来的友好合作关系继续得到发展。
                  中华人民共和国国务院总理  周恩来
                  一九五五年八月十五日于北京
