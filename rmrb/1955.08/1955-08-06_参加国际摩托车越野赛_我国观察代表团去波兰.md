Title: China Sends Observer Delegation to Poland for International Motorcycle Motocross

Author:

Date: 1955-08-06
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, August 4th:

To attend the 13th International Datel Motorcycle Motocross, held in Zakopane from August 7th to 9th, the Central National Defense Sports Club has sent an observer delegation at the joint invitation of the Polish People's Republic League of Friends of Soldiers and the Polish People's Republic Automobile and Motorcycle Association.

The six-member observer delegation, led by the head of the delegation, Qiu Feng, left Beijing by plane on the morning of the 4th for the Polish People's Republic.

### 参加国际摩托车越野赛  我国观察代表团去波兰

1955-08-06
第1版()
专栏：

    参加国际摩托车越野赛
    我国观察代表团去波兰
    新华社四日讯　应波兰人民共和国战士之友同盟和波兰人民共和国汽车、摩托总会的共同邀请，中央国防体育俱乐部派出观察代表团参加八月七日到九日在扎考班诺举行的第十三届国际达特尔摩托车越野赛。
    观察代表团六人已在团长秋峰率领下，四日上午乘飞机离北京去波兰人民共和国。
