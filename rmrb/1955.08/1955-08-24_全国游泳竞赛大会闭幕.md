Title: National Swimming Competition Concludes
Author: 
Date: 1955-08-24
Page: 2

The 1955 National Swimming Competition concluded on the 23rd. Over the past four days, two athletes and one relay team broke three national records; and seven athletes and one relay team broke the highest records from the 1954 National Swimming Competition.

　　On the 23rd, the final seven of the eighteen final events were held. In the women's 200-meter breaststroke, women's 100-meter butterfly, and men's 200-meter breaststroke finals, five athletes broke the highest records from the 1954 National Swimming Competition. These five athletes were Cai Meiling from Fujian, Huang Ziyan from Guangdong; Huang Lianhua and Pan Jialing from Shanghai; and Qi Lieyun from Guangdong.

　　On the morning of the 23rd, the women's springboard diving preliminary and final rounds were held with thirteen athletes participating. Liang Xiuying from Guangdong was the champion of the 1954 National Women's Springboard Diving. On the 23rd, she performed various complex and graceful movements while diving from a 3-meter springboard, earning the highest score and retaining her title as national champion. The second place in women's diving was won by Liang Xiulan from Guangdong. Wen Yijing from Guangdong took third place.

　　The five water polo teams participating in this competition had already played eight matches. Two more matches were held on the 23rd. The match between Sichuan and Liaoning ended with a score of five to one, with Sichuan winning. The match between Guangdong and Shanghai was the final match and also the most exciting. Both teams had won all three of their matches over the past three days. Guangdong's forwards were fast with the ball and shot fiercely, resulting in a 6-2 victory and the water polo championship for this competition. Shanghai won second place.

　　The closing ceremony was held at 5:00 PM. Shanghai Deputy Mayor Jin Zhonghua delivered the closing address and presented prizes to the winners of the swimming, diving, and water polo competitions. Huang Lianhua, Qi Lieyun, and the Shanghai women's 4x100 meter freestyle relay team also received medals for breaking national records.
(Xinhua News Agency)

### 全国游泳竞赛大会闭幕

1955-08-24
第2版()
专栏：

　　全国游泳竞赛大会闭幕
    一九五五年全国游泳竞赛大会已在二十三日闭幕。四天来，共有两个运动员、一个接力队打破了三项全国最高纪录；并有七个运动员、一个接力队打破了一九五四年全国游泳竞赛大会的最高纪录。
    二十三日，大会举行了十八项决赛中的最后七项决赛。在女子二百公尺蛙泳、女子一百公尺蝶泳和男子二百公尺蛙泳决赛中，共有五名运动员打破了一九五四年全国游泳竞赛大会的最高纪录。这五名运动员是福建队的蔡美玲、广东队的黄紫颜；上海队的黄莲华、潘家玲；广东队的戚烈云。
    二十三日上午举行了有十三名运动员参加的女子跳板跳水预、决赛。广东队的梁秀英是一九五四年全国女子跳板跳水的冠军。二十三日她在三公尺高的跳板上跳水时，表演了各种复杂而优美的动作，获得最高分数，保持了全国冠军的称号。女子跳水第二名由广东队的梁秀兰获得。广东队的温一静获得第三名。
    参加这次大会的五个水球队已进行过八场比赛，二十三日又进行了两场比赛。四川队对辽宁队的比赛以五比一结束，四川队获胜。广东队对上海队的比赛是最后一场比赛，也是最精采的一场比赛。两队三天来都是三战三胜。广东队前锋带球快、射门猛烈，结果以六比二获胜，取得了这次大会的水球冠军。上海队获得亚军。
    下午五时，大会举行了闭幕式。上海市副市长金仲华致闭幕词，并发给游泳、跳水和水球比赛的优胜者奖品。黄莲华、戚烈云和上海女子四百公尺自由泳接力队还获得了打破全国最高纪录的奖章。
                                    （新华社）
