Our Great Motherland
Sichuan Province has over 50 million mu of medium-season rice, and the yield per unit area can exceed that of previous years. The rice in the famous rice-producing area of the province, the Dujiangyan Irrigation Area, generally matures faster and has more uniform heading this year due to the implementation of shallow-water irrigation. Members of the Huihe Agricultural Production Cooperative in Qingping Township, Wenjiang County, are inspecting the length of rice ears in the paddy fields.

### 我们伟大的祖国（图片）

1955-08-23
第1版()
专栏：

    我们伟大的祖国
    四川省五千多万亩中熟稻，单位面积产量可以超过往年。全省著名水稻产区——都江堰灌溉区的水稻，由于实行了浅水灌溉，今年成熟期一般较快，抽穗也较整齐。温江县清平乡惠和农业生产合作社的社员在稻田里检查稻穗的长度。
