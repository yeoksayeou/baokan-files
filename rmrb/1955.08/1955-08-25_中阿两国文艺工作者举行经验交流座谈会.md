Title: Sino-Albanian Artists Hold Experience Exchange Symposium
Author:
Date: 1955-08-25
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, August 24th: Albanian People's Army Song and Dance Troupe artists and Chinese artists held a symposium on the afternoon of the 24th to exchange experiences in music and dance.

　　Ouyang Yuqian, member of the Presidium of the China Federation of Literary and Art Circles and vice chairman of the Chinese Dramatists Association, delivered a speech at the beginning of the symposium. He welcomed the Albanian artists on behalf of the Chinese literary and art circles, and expressed that Chinese artists were very happy to have this opportunity to learn from the Albanian artists. Ouyang Yuqian said that we have had the opportunity to appreciate the excellent performances of the Albanian People's Army Song and Dance Troupe. Every melody and every movement in Albanian music, song, and dance fills us with joy from the depths of our hearts. Through the artistic performances of the song and dance troupe, the hearts of our two peoples have been united.

　　The symposium then split into two groups, music and dance. In the music group, hosted by Ma Sicong, vice chairman of the Chinese Musicians Association, Gaco Avrazi, the artistic director of the song and dance troupe, introduced the characteristics of Albanian music in terms of melody and rhythm, the work of compiling and collecting Albanian folk songs, and the situation of musical creation. In the dance group, hosted by Ouyang Yuqian, vice chairman of the Chinese Dramatists Association, and Hu Guogang, vice chairman of the Chinese Dance Art Research Association, Chinese and Albanian dance workers exchanged information about collecting and compiling folk dances and the organization and development of Chinese dance groups.

　　Next, the Chinese and Albanian artists gathered together again to enjoy demonstration performances of music and dance from both countries. After Chang Xiangyu, a famous豫剧 (Yu Opera) actress, sang a section from "Torturing Hong," Albanian singers sang four representative Albanian folk songs, and Major Gaco Avrazi also participated in the chorus of two folk songs. Chinese national musicians Guan Pinghu and Zha Fuxi performed the qin and xiao duet "Wild Geese Descending on the Sandbank," and Li Tingsong performed the pipa solo "Marquis of Hanshou." Chinese and Albanian artists also successively performed many Chinese and Albanian dances and songs, and each program was warmly welcomed.

　　At the symposium, the Chinese Musicians Association and the Chinese Dance Art Research Association presented the Albanian People's Army Song and Dance Troupe with gifts such as Chinese music records and scores.

### 中阿两国文艺工作者举行经验交流座谈会

1955-08-25
第1版()
专栏：

　　中阿两国文艺工作者举行经验交流座谈会
    新华社二十四日讯　阿尔巴尼亚人民军歌舞团的艺术家们同中国的文艺工作者在二十四日下午举行座谈会，互相交流在音乐和舞蹈艺术方面的经验。
    中国文学艺术界联合会主席团委员、中国戏剧家协会副主席欧阳予倩在座谈会开始时致词，他代表中国文艺界欢迎阿尔巴尼亚艺术家，并且表示中国的文艺工作者很高兴得到这个向阿尔巴尼亚艺术家学习的机会。欧阳予倩说，我们已经有机会欣赏到阿尔巴尼亚人民军歌舞团的优秀的表演。阿尔巴尼亚音乐歌舞中的每一个旋律、每一个动作，都使我们从心的深处产生喜悦。通过歌舞团的艺术表演，已经把我们两国人民的心结合在一起了。
    座谈会接着分成音乐和舞蹈两个小组进行。在中国音乐家协会副主席马思聪主持的音乐组，歌舞团的艺术指导加乔·阿夫拉齐介绍了阿尔巴尼亚音乐在曲调和节拍上的特点，阿尔巴尼亚整理搜集民歌的工作和音乐创作情况。在中国戏剧家协会副主席欧阳予倩和中国舞蹈艺术研究会副主席胡果刚主持的舞蹈小组，阿中两国的舞蹈工作者交谈了搜集整理民间舞蹈和中国舞蹈团体的组织和发展情况。
    接着，中阿两国的艺术家又聚集在一起，欣赏了两国音乐舞蹈的示范性表演。在豫剧名演员常香玉演唱了“拷红”中的一段之后，阿尔巴尼亚的歌唱家们演唱了四支有代表性的阿尔巴尼亚民歌，加乔·阿夫拉齐少校也参加了两支民歌的合唱。中国国乐家管平湖、查阜西表演了琴箫合奏的“平沙落雁”、李廷松表演了琵琶独奏“汉寿亭侯”。中阿两国的艺术家们还相继表演了许多中国和阿尔巴尼亚的舞蹈和歌曲，每一个节目都受到热烈欢迎。
    座谈会上，中国音乐家协会和中国舞蹈艺术研究会向阿尔巴尼亚人民军歌舞团赠送了中国音乐唱片、乐谱等礼品。
