    Our Great Motherland
    In Wuligou, west of the Beiwuding River in Mizhi County, Suide Prefecture, Shaanxi Province, a "Weaving Girl Irrigation Canal" has been built, which can irrigate 8,000 mu of farmland. This is the scene at the water inlet gate of the Weaving Girl Irrigation Canal.
