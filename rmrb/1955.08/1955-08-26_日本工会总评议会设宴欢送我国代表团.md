Title: Japan General Council of Trade Unions Hosts Banquet to See Off Chinese Delegation

Author: 
Date: 1955-08-26
Page: 4

　　Xinhua News Agency, Tokyo, 25th: The Japan General Council of Trade Unions held a banquet on the evening of the 24th to see off the Chinese delegation attending the World Conference Against Atomic and Hydrogen Bombs and to welcome trade union delegations from the Soviet Union, Germany, Indonesia and other countries.

　　Those attending the banquet included Fujita Tohtaro, Chairman of the Japan General Council of Trade Unions, Vice-Chairman Akira Imamura, Secretary-General Akira Iwai, and Minoru Takano and other permanent directors, as well as representatives of the 39 member unions participating in the Japan General Council of Trade Unions, and Shigeharu Yoshida, Chairman of the All-Japan Federation of Industrial Trade Unions, and more than eighty other people.

### 日本工会总评议会设宴欢送我国代表团

1955-08-26
第4版()
专栏：

　　日本工会总评议会设宴欢送我国代表团
    新华社二十五日讯　东京消息：日本工会总评议会二十四日晚举行宴会，欢送出席禁止原子弹和氢弹世界大会的中国代表团和欢迎苏联、德国、印度尼西亚等国工会代表团。
    参加宴会的有日本工会总评议会主席藤田藤太郎、副主席今村彰、事务局长岩井章和高野实等常任干事以及参加日本工会总评议会的三十九个会员工会的代表和全日本产业别工会会议主席吉田资治等八十多人。
