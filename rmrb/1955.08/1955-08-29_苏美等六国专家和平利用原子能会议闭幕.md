Title: Six-Nation Experts from USSR, US, et al. Conclude Meeting on Peaceful Use of Atomic Energy
Author:
Date: 1955-08-29
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Geneva, August 28th: A secret meeting of experts from the Soviet Union, the United States, the United Kingdom, France, Canada, and Czechoslovakia on the peaceful use of atomic energy concluded in Geneva on the 27th.

The meeting on the 27th was held under the chairmanship of Academician Skobeltsyn. The following communiqué was issued after the meeting:

"Representatives from Canada, Czechoslovakia, France, the Soviet Union, the United Kingdom, and the United States held a secret meeting in Geneva from August 22 to 27 to discuss issues related to ensuring the peaceful use of atomic energy and measures to prevent the use of fissile materials for other purposes.

The meeting discussed certain technical aspects of these issues.

Technical experts discussed many preliminary proposals put forward by several countries. As a result of the discussions, the experts exchanged beneficial technical opinions. These issues will now be submitted to the governments of the countries concerned for consideration."

### 苏美等六国专家和平利用原子能会议闭幕

1955-08-29
第4版()
专栏：

　　苏美等六国专家和平利用原子能会议闭幕
    新华社二十八日讯　据塔斯社日内瓦讯：苏联、美国、英国、法国、加拿大和捷克斯洛伐克六国专家举行的和平利用原子能问题专家秘密会议二十七日在日内瓦闭幕。
    二十七日会议是在斯科贝尔琴院士主持下进行的。会后发表了下列公报：
    “加拿大、捷克斯洛伐克、法国、苏联、联合王国和美国的代表从八月二十二日至二十七日在日内瓦举行了秘密会议，讨论保证和平利用原子能的问题和防止把分裂物质用于其他目的的措施。
    会议讨论了这些问题的某些技术方面。
    技术专家讨论了若干国家提出的许多初步建议，讨论的结果，使专家们交换了有益的技术意见。现在这些问题将提交给有关国家的政府考虑。”
