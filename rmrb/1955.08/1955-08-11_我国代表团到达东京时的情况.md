Title: The Situation When Our Delegation Arrived in Tokyo
Author:
Date: 1955-08-11
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Tokyo, 10th: The Chinese delegation, led by head of delegation Liu Ningyi, arrived in Tokyo by plane on the afternoon of the 9th, invited to attend the World Congress for the Prohibition of Atomic and Hydrogen Bombs.

More than 300 representatives from various political parties and organizations came to the airport to welcome them, including Matsumoto Jiichiro, member of the House of Councillors of the Japanese Leftist Socialist Party, Tanaka Toshio and Tanaka Orinosuke, members of the House of Representatives of the Leftist Socialist Party, Suzuki Ichizo, alternate member of the Central Committee of the Japanese Communist Party, Shioji Nobuo, vice chairman of the General Council of Trade Unions of Japan, Takano Minoru, standing director, Yoshida Sukeyuki, chairman of the All Japan Industrial Union Conference, Yamamoto Kumaichi, secretary-general of the Japan International Trade Promotion Association, Suzuki Kazuo, managing director of the Sino-Japanese Trade Association, Nakayama Riri, director of the International Bureau of the All Japan Buddhist Association, Ito Takeo, chairman of the Japan-China Friendship Association, Nanba Hideo, vice chairman of the Japan National Relief Association, Tanda Tokusaburo, standing member of the Japan Peace Protection Committee, Kuraishi Takeshiro, professor at Tokyo University, Akamatsu Toshiko, painter, and Kang Mingqiu, chairman of the Tokyo Overseas Chinese Association.

Shioji Nobuo delivered a welcome speech at the airport on behalf of the Japanese Preparatory Committee for the World Congress for the Prohibition of Atomic and Hydrogen Bombs. He said: We are finally able to welcome the arrival of the Chinese delegation today. This is a victory for the people, and this victory can be said to be the greatest gift for the World Congress for the Prohibition of Atomic and Hydrogen Bombs.

Liu Ningyi said in his speech: Thanks to the efforts of many friends in Japan, we are finally able to arrive in Tokyo today. This is a bridge built by the friendship between the Chinese and Japanese people, allowing us to cross the sea, cross the mountains, overcome various difficulties, and meet our Japanese brothers and sisters as happily and safely as family members. This makes us feel exceptionally excited.

Liu Ningyi said: The Chinese people have always advocated peace and advocated the prohibition of atomic and hydrogen weapons and other weapons of mass destruction. We fully support holding this conference in Japan. The Chinese people are willing to work together with the Japanese people and the people of all countries in the world to oppose these weapons, oppose wars of aggression, and defend peace in Asia and the world.

Liu Ningyi concluded by saying, I would like to express my respect on behalf of the Chinese people to the Japanese people who are fighting against atomic and hydrogen weapons, and extend my cordial condolences to the Japanese atomic bomb victims. At the Third Beijing Municipal Women's Congress

### 我国代表团到达东京时的情况

1955-08-11
第1版()
专栏：

    我国代表团到达东京时的情况
    新华社十日讯　东京消息：应邀出席禁止原子弹和氢弹世界大会的中国代表团在团长刘宁一率领下，九日下午乘飞机到达东京。
    到机场欢迎的有日本左派社会党参议员松本治一郎，左派社会党众议员田中稔男、田中织之进，日本共产党候补中央委员铃木市藏，日本工会总评议会副主席盐谷信雄、常任干事高野实，全日本产业别工会会议主席吉田资治，日本国际贸易促进协会事务总长山本熊一，中日贸易会常务理事铃木一雄，全日本佛教会国际局长中山理理，日中友好协会理事长伊藤武雄，日本国民救援会副会长难波英夫，日本拥护和平委员会常务委员淡德三郎，东京大学教授仓石武四郎，画家赤松俊子，东京华侨总会会长康鸣球等各政党、团体代表共三百多人。
    盐谷信雄在机场代表禁止原子弹和氢弹世界大会日本筹备会致欢迎词。他说：我们今天终于能够迎接中国代表团的到达，这是人民的胜利，这个胜利对于禁止原子弹和氢弹世界大会可说是一个最大的礼物。
    刘宁一致词说：由于日本许许多多朋友的努力，我们今天终于能够到达了东京，这是中日两国人民的友谊，架起了一座桥梁，使我们能够越过大海，跨过高山，克服各种困难，愉快安然地和日本的兄弟姊妹们有如家人一样地相见了。这使我们分外的兴奋。
    刘宁一说：中国人民一贯主张和平，主张禁止原子武器和氢武器以及其他大规模毁灭性武器。我们完全支持在日本举行这个大会。中国人民愿同日本人民和世界各国人民一道，为反对这些武器，反对侵略战争，保卫亚洲与世界和平而共同努力。
    刘宁一最后说，我谨代表中国人民，向正在为反对原子武器和氢武器而奋斗的日本人民致敬，向日本原子弹受害者致亲切的慰问。在北京市第三届妇女代表大会上
