Title: British Foreign Office Spokesperson Points Out: Both Sides of Vietnam Should Begin Negotiations in Accordance with the Geneva Accords
Author:
Date: 1955-08-11
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, London, August 10th: A British Foreign Office spokesperson said on the 9th that the British Foreign Office was disturbed by the communique issued by the South Vietnamese Foreign Ministry on the evening of the 8th. The South Vietnamese Foreign Ministry's communique implied that the United Kingdom, France, and the United States expressed sympathy for South Vietnam's failure to begin negotiations with the Democratic Republic of Vietnam from July 20th in accordance with the Geneva Accords.

The British Foreign Office spokesperson said that the British Ambassador to Saigon, Stephenson, had been instructed to seek clarification of the South Vietnamese Foreign Ministry's communique. He said that the United Kingdom still believes that negotiations should begin in accordance with the provisions of the Geneva Armistice Agreement.

### 英国外交部发言人指出  越南双方应当按照日内瓦协定开始进行协商

1955-08-11
第4版()
专栏：

    英国外交部发言人指出
    越南双方应当按照日内瓦协定开始进行协商
    新华社十日讯　伦敦消息：英国外交部发言人九日说，英国外交部对南越外交部在八日晚间发表的公报感到不安。南越外交部的公报暗示，英、法、美三国对于南越没有按照日内瓦协定从七月二十日开始同越南民主共和国进行协商一事是表示同情的。
    英国外交部发言人说，英国驻西贡大使斯蒂芬逊已经奉命设法得到对南越外交部公报的澄清。他说，英国仍然认为，应当按照日内瓦停战协定的规定开始进行协商。
