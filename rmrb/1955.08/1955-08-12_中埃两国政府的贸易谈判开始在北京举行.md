Title: Sino-Egyptian Government Trade Negotiations Begin in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1955-08-12
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, 11th August. Trade negotiations between the governments of the People's Republic of China and the Republic of Egypt began in Beijing on the morning of the 11th.

    At the first plenary meeting of the two negotiating teams held this morning, the representatives of the Chinese and Egyptian governments reached an agreement on issues such as the organization and procedure of the trade negotiations. After the plenary meeting, the negotiating teams divided into four committees—Trade Agreement, Chinese Exports, Egyptian Exports, and Economic Cooperation—to begin specific discussions.

    The Egyptian side participating in the negotiations included the head of the Egyptian government trade delegation, Egyptian Minister of Industry and Commerce Muhammad Abu Nuseir, and all members and advisors of the delegation.

    The Chinese side participating in the negotiations included Minister of Foreign Trade Ye Ji-zhuang, Vice Minister Lei Renmin, and heads of relevant bureaus and specialized companies of the Ministry of Foreign Trade.

### 中埃两国政府的贸易谈判开始在北京举行

1955-08-12
第1版()
专栏：

    中埃两国政府的贸易谈判开始在北京举行
    新华社十一日讯　中华人民共和国政府和埃及共和国政府的贸易谈判，十一日上午在北京开始举行。
    在今天上午举行的第一次双方谈判人员全体会议上，中埃两国政府的代表就贸易谈判的组织和程序等问题达成了协议。在全体会议以后，双方谈判人员就分为贸易协定、中国出口、埃及出口、经济合作四个委员会开始进行具体商谈。
    埃及方面参加谈判的，有埃及共和国政府贸易代表团团长、埃及工商部长穆罕默德·阿卜·努赛尔和代表团全体团员及顾问。
    中国方面参加谈判的，有对外贸易部部长叶季壮、副部长雷任民以及对外贸易部各有关局和各专业公司的负责人。
