Title: Indonesian Political Groups Continue to Struggle Over Cabinet Formation

Author:
Date: 1955-08-08
Page: 4

　　TASS, Jakarta, August 5th: Newspapers report that the struggle among Indonesian political groups over the formation of a new cabinet continues. Harahap, leader of the Masyumi Party, who was tasked with forming the cabinet, held consultations with leaders of various political parties on the 4th and 5th regarding the selection of personnel for the new cabinet. Newspaper reports indicate that the Masyumi Party intends to occupy the most important positions in the new cabinet.

### 印度尼西亚各政治集团  在组阁问题上继续进行斗争

1955-08-08
第4版()
专栏：

    印度尼西亚各政治集团
    在组阁问题上继续进行斗争
    新华社六日讯　塔斯社雅加达五日讯：报纸报道，印度尼西亚各政治集团在组织新内阁问题上的斗争在继续中。受命组阁的马斯友美党领袖哈拉哈普四、五两日曾就新内阁的人选问题同各政党领袖会商。报纸消息提到，马斯友美党打算占有新内阁中的最重要的职位。
