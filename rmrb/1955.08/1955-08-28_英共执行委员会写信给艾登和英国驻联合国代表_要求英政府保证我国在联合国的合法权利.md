Title: British Communist Party Executive Committee Writes to Eden and the British Representative to the United Nations Demanding the British Government Guarantee Our Country's Legitimate Rights in the United Nations
Author:
Date: 1955-08-28
Page: 4

　　Xinhua News Agency, Berlin, August 27th: The London "Daily Worker" reported on the 26th that the Executive Committee of the British Communist Party has requested the British government to take active steps to guarantee China's legitimate representation in the United Nations. This request was made in a letter it sent to British Prime Minister Eden and the British representative to the United Nations, Dixon, on the 25th.
　　Harry Pollitt, General Secretary of the British Communist Party, speaking on behalf of the Executive Committee of the British Communist Party, said that this request is "shared by the majority of organizations in the labor movement and the majority of people in this country."
　　The letter points out that the steps already taken to ease international tensions make the issue of restoring China's legitimate status in the United Nations even more pressing.
　　The letter accuses the British government of agreeing to sponsor a proposal that amounts to continuing to exclude China from the United Nations by postponing the discussion of China's representation. The letter states: "We believe that if the British government takes steps to urge an immediate decision on this issue at the next (United Nations) General Assembly, it will receive the support of the vast majority of United Nations member states."
　　The letter concludes: "Therefore, we earnestly hope that the British delegation attending the next United Nations General Assembly will take all possible steps to ensure that a decision is made on the issue of China's representation in the United Nations."

### 英共执行委员会写信给艾登和英国驻联合国代表　要求英政府保证我国在联合国的合法权利

1955-08-28
第4版()
专栏：

　　英共执行委员会写信给艾登和英国驻联合国代表
　　要求英政府保证我国在联合国的合法权利
    新华社柏林二十七日电　伦敦“工人日报”二十六日报道：英国共产党执行委员会要求英国政府采取积极步骤来保证中国在联合国中的合法代表权。这个要求是在它二十五日给英国首相艾登和英国驻联合国代表狄克逊的一封信中提出的。
    英国共产党总书记波立特代表英国共产党执行委员会说，这个要求是“劳工运动的大多数组织和这个国家的大多数人所共有的”。
    这封信指出，已经采取的缓和国际紧张局势的步骤，使得恢复中国在联合国中的合法地位的问题成为更加迫切了。
    这封信里指责英国政府同意发起等于继续把中国排斥在联合国之外的延期讨论中国代表权问题的提案。这封信说：“我们相信，如果英国政府采取步骤，在下届（联合国）大会上力促立即就这个问题作出决定，那末，它会得到绝大多数联合国会员国的支持。”
    这封信最后说：“因此，我们热烈希望出席下届联合国大会的英国代表团会采取一切可能的步骤，使得就中国在联合国中的代表权问题作出决定。”
