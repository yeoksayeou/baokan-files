Title: Chinese Construction Engineering Delegation Arrives in Moscow
Author:
Date: 1955-08-28
Page: 3

　  Our Construction Engineering Delegation Arrives in Moscow
    A Chinese Construction Engineering Delegation, led by Liu Xiufeng, Minister of Construction Engineering of the People's Republic of China, and Wang Shitai, Deputy Director of the State Construction Commission, consisting of twenty-five members, arrived in Moscow on the 25th to conduct a two-month study of the Soviet Union's construction engineering industry.
      Those who went to the airport to welcome them included: Skorobayev, Vice Chairman of the State Construction Committee of the Soviet Council of Ministers, Degai, Minister of Construction of the Soviet Union, and Raize, Minister of Construction of Metallurgical and Chemical Industry Enterprises of the Soviet Union, and others.
                                    (Xinhua News Agency)

### 我建筑工程考察团到莫斯科

1955-08-28
第3版()
专栏：

　  我建筑工程考察团到莫斯科
    由中华人民共和国建筑工程部长刘秀峰和国家建设委员会副主任王世泰率领的中国建筑工程考察团一行二十五人在二十五日到达莫斯科，准备对苏联的建筑工程事业作两个月的考察。
      前往机场欢迎的有：苏联部长会议国家建设委员会副主席斯科罗鲍夫、苏联建造部长德加伊、苏联冶金工业和化学工业企业建造部长拉伊泽等。
                                    （新华社）
