    Our Great Motherland
    Shanghai's largest children's park, Helen Park, is divided into three sections: an infant activity area, a children's activity area, and a maze obstacle activity area. It also has various game equipment suitable for children of different ages. The most popular among children is this spiral slide.

### 我们伟大的祖国（图片）

1955-08-21
第1版()
专栏：

    我们伟大的祖国
    上海市最大的儿童公园——海伦公园，设有幼儿活动区、儿童活动区、迷阵障碍活动区三部分，并有适合不同年龄的儿童需要的各种游戏设备。最受儿童欢迎的要算这座螺旋滑梯了。
