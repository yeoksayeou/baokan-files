Title: Chinese Yue Opera Troupe Arrives in Moscow
Author:
Date: 1955-08-10
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency, Moscow – The Chinese Yue Opera Troupe arrived in Moscow on the afternoon of the 6th from Minsk.

The Yue Opera Troupe stayed in Minsk for five days, performing "Liang Shanbo and Zhu Yingtai" for the laborers of Belarus. All the performances were warmly welcomed by the audience. On the 5th, the Yue Opera actors also held a friendly meeting with cultural workers in Minsk and performed programs for each other. Before leaving, the Yue Opera Troupe attended a reception hosted by the Minister of Culture of the Belorussian Soviet Socialist Republic, Kiselev.

### 我国越剧团到达莫斯科

1955-08-10
第4版()
专栏：

    我国越剧团到达莫斯科
    据新华社莫斯科电　中国越剧团于六日下午由明斯克到达莫斯科。
    越剧团在明斯克逗留了五天，为白俄罗斯的劳动者演出“梁山伯与祝英台”。所有的演出都受到了观众的热烈欢迎。五日，越剧演员们还同明斯克的文化工作者举行了友谊的会见，并且相互表演了节目。临行前，越剧团人员曾出席了白俄罗斯共和国文化部长基谢廖夫的招待会。
