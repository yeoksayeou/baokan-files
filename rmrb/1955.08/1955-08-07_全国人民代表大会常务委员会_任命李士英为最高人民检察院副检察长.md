Title: The Standing Committee of the National People's Congress Appoints Li Shiying as Deputy Procurator-General of the Supreme People's Procuratorate
Author:
Date: 1955-08-07
Page: 1

    Xinhua News Agency, August 6th—The 20th meeting of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress of the People's Republic of China passed the following list of appointments on August 6th:
    Deputy Procurator-General of the Supreme People's Procuratorate: Li Shiying

### 全国人民代表大会常务委员会  任命李士英为最高人民检察院副检察长

1955-08-07
第1版()
专栏：

    全国人民代表大会常务委员会
    任命李士英为最高人民检察院副检察长
    新华社六日讯　中华人民共和国全国人民代表大会常务委员会八月六日第二十次会议通过任命名单如下：
    最高人民检察院副检察长　李士英
