Title: Albanian People's Army Song and Dance Troupe Passes Through Erenhot En Route to Beijing
Author:
Date: 1955-08-14
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Erenhot, August 13th: The Albanian People's Army Song and Dance Troupe, consisting of 137 members led by Lieutenant Colonel Mekur Çela, arrived in Erenhot, China, at 7:00 AM today at the invitation of the Chinese government for a performance tour.

Greeting the Albanian People's Army Song and Dance Troupe in Erenhot from Beijing were Tian Han, representative of the Ministry of Culture of the People's Republic of China, Li Wei, representative of the Ministry of National Defense, and Bilo, First Secretary of the Albanian People's Republic Embassy in China.

Upon disembarking from the train at Erenhot Station, the members of the Albanian People's Army Song and Dance Troupe were warmly welcomed. They accepted bouquets from a flower-offering team of over a hundred people amidst enthusiastic applause.

A brief welcoming ceremony was held at the station, where Tian Han delivered a welcome speech on behalf of the Ministry of National Defense and the Ministry of Culture of the People's Republic of China. Lieutenant Colonel Mekur Çela, the leader of the Albanian People's Army Song and Dance Troupe, delivered a reply.

After a short rest in Erenhot, the Albanian People's Army Song and Dance Troupe left Erenhot for Beijing by special train in the afternoon of the 13th.

### 阿尔巴尼亚人民军歌舞团过二连来北京

1955-08-14
第1版()
专栏：

    阿尔巴尼亚人民军歌舞团过二连来北京
    新华社二连十三日电　应邀来中国访问演出的阿尔巴尼亚人民军歌舞团一行一百三十七人在领队梅库尔·柴拉中校率领下，今天上午七时到达中国国境二连。
    由北京到二连来欢迎阿尔巴尼亚人民军歌舞团的有中华人民共和国文化部代表田汉，国防部代表李伟，以及阿尔巴尼亚人民共和国驻华大使馆一等秘书比洛等。
    阿尔巴尼亚人民军歌舞团人员在二连车站下车后，受到欢迎者的热烈欢迎。他们在热烈的掌声中接受了百余人的献花队伍的献花。
    在车站上举行了简短的欢迎会，田汉代表中华人民共和国国防部和文化部致欢迎词，阿尔巴尼亚人民军歌舞团领队梅库尔·柴拉中校致答词。
    阿尔巴尼亚人民军歌舞团在二连稍事休息后，已在十三日下午乘专车离开二连前往北京。
