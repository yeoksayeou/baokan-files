Title: Director of the Bureau of Foreign Cultural Relations, Hong Shen, Passes Away
Author:
Date: 1955-08-30
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, August 29th: Hong Shen, Director of the Bureau of Foreign Cultural Relations, Vice President of the Chinese People's Association for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries, Vice Chairman of the Chinese Dramatists Association, Deputy to the First National People's Congress, Member of the Second National Committee of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference, and Member of the Second National Committee of the China Federation of Literary and Art Circles, passed away in Beijing at 5:13 PM on August 29, 1955, due to lung cancer despite medical treatment.

Comrade Hong Shen was a native of Changzhou, Jiangsu Province, and was 60 years old.

In addition: The funeral committee for Comrade Hong Shen was established on the 29th, composed of the following forty-two individuals: Guo Moruo, Xi Zhongxun, Shen Yanshui, Zhou Yang, Xing Xiping, Qian Junrui, Ding Xilin, Xia Yan, Zhang Hanfu, Fan Changjiang, Chu Tunan, Zhao Yimin, Yang Hansheng, Zhang Su, Yu Xinqing, Chen Zhongjing, Wu Huazhi, Luo Longji, Huang Qixiang, Huang Shaohong, Zhang Zhirang, Wu Han, Tian Han, Ouyang Yuqian, Mei Lanfang, Zhou Xinfang, Lao She, Cao Yu, Zhang Geng, Ma Yanxiang, Zhang Guangnian, Chen Baichen, Song Zhi's, Wu Xue, Yuan Muzhi, Cai Chusheng, Yu Ling, Ying Yunwei, Zheng Junli, Zhao Dan, Zhang Mingyang, and Li Chao.

### 对外文化联络局局长洪深逝世

1955-08-30
第1版()
专栏：

　　对外文化联络局局长洪深逝世
    新华社二十九日讯　对外文化联络局局长、中国人民对外文化协会副会长、中国戏剧家协会副主席、第一届全国人民代表大会代表、中国人民政治协商会议第二届全国委员会委员、中国文学艺术界联合会第二届全国委员会委员洪深，患肺癌症经医生诊治无效，于一九五五年八月二十九日下午五时十三分在北京逝世。
    洪深同志，江苏省常州市人，享年六十岁。
    又讯：洪深同志治丧委员会已于二十九日成立，由下列四十二人组成：郭沫若、习仲勋、沈雁水、周扬、邢西萍、钱俊瑞、丁西林、夏衍、章汉夫、范长江、楚图南、赵毅敏、阳翰笙、张苏、余心清、陈忠经、吴化之、罗隆基、黄琪翔、黄绍竑、张志让、吴晗、田汉、欧阳予倩、梅兰芳、周信芳、老舍、曹禺、张庚、马彦祥、张光年、陈白尘、宋之的、吴雪、袁牧之、蔡楚生、于伶、应云卫、郑君里、赵丹、张明养、李超。
