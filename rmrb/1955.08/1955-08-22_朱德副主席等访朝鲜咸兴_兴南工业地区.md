Title: Vice Chairman Zhu De and Others Visit Hamhung-Hungnam Industrial Area in Korea
Author:
Date: 1955-08-22
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency, Pyongyang, August 21st: Vice Chairman Zhu De and the delegation of the People's Republic of China he led, accompanied by Vice Premier of the Korean Democratic People's Republic, Choi Chang-ik, visited the Hamhung-Hungnam industrial area in South Hamgyong Province on the east coast of Korea for four days from the 17th to the 20th. The delegation returned to Pyongyang on the evening of the 20th. Those who welcomed them at Pyongyang Station included Vice Premiers of the Korean Democratic People's Republic, Park Chang-ok and Kim Il, Foreign Minister Nam Il, Minister of Transportation Kim Hoe-il, Director of the Propaganda and Agitation Department of the Central Committee of the Korean Workers' Party, Park Yong-bin, and Director of the Department of Social Sciences, Ha Yang-cheon, and others.

When the delegation arrived in Hamhung, they were warmly welcomed by Li Wei-min, Chairman of the People's Committee of South Hamgyong Province, Li Taek-keun, Vice Chairman of the South Hamgyong Province Committee of the Korean Workers' Party, and more than 4,000 working people of Hamhung City. In the Hamhung-Hungnam area, Vice Chairman Zhu De and the members of the delegation visited the Bongung Chemical Factory and the Hungnam Fertilizer Factory successively, talked cordially with the factory负责人, workers, and technicians, and asked them about the situation of recovery and construction, production, and workers' lives. Vice Chairman Zhu De and the members of the delegation also visited the Sohojin fishing port in the suburbs of Hamhung City in the company of Vice Premier Choi Chang-ik, talked with the fishermen, and held discussions with the chairmen of several agricultural production cooperatives in Hamju County. The delegation also toured the Panlong Mountain Park, a scenic spot in Hamhung City.

Vice Chairman Zhu De and the members of the delegation were warmly welcomed by Korean workers, farmers, and fishermen everywhere. Han Hu-bang, Chairman of the First Agricultural Production Cooperative of Heungbo-ri, Hamju County, and Li Sung-nam, model worker and diver of the Sohojin Aquatic Products Office, presented the delegation with vegetables, fruits, straw mats, sea cucumbers, abalones, scallops, etc., which they produced themselves, expressing their warm welcome and profound friendship to the Chinese delegation.

### 朱德副主席等访朝鲜咸兴——兴南工业地区

1955-08-22
第4版()
专栏：

　　朱德副主席等访朝鲜咸兴——兴南工业地区
    新华社平壤二十一日电　朱德副主席和他所率领的中华人民共和国代表团在朝鲜民主主义人民共和国内阁副首相崔昌益等陪同下，在从十七日到二十日的四天中，访问了朝鲜东海岸咸镜南道的咸兴—兴南工业地区。代表团已在二十日晚回到平壤。前往平壤车站欢迎的有朝鲜民主主义人民共和国内阁副首相朴昌玉、金一，外务相南日，交通相金会一，朝鲜劳动党中央委员会宣传鼓动部长朴永彬、社会科学部长河仰天等。
    代表团在到达咸兴时，曾受到咸镜南道人民委员会委员长李维民、朝鲜劳动党咸镜南道委员会副委员长李泽根和咸兴市劳动人民四千多人的热烈欢迎。在咸兴—兴南地区，朱德副主席和代表团团员们先后参观了本宫化学工厂和兴南肥料工厂，同工厂负责人、工人、技术人员亲切地谈话，向他们询问了有关恢复建设、生产和工人生活的情况。朱德副主席和代表团团员们还在崔昌益副首相等陪同下到咸兴市郊区的西湖津渔港参观，和渔民们谈话，并且同咸州郡几个农业生产合作社社长们举行了座谈。代表团并且游览了咸兴市的名胜盘龙山公园。
    朱德副主席和代表团团员们到处受到朝鲜工人、农民和渔民的热烈欢迎。咸州郡兴保里第一农业生产合作社社长韩后方援和西湖津水产事务所模范工人、潜水手李成男分别向代表团赠送了他们自己生产的蔬菜、水果、草席和海参、鲍鱼、甘贝等，表示他们对中国代表团的热烈欢迎和深厚友谊。
