Title: Vice Chairman Zhu De and the Chinese Delegation Leave Pyongyang
Author:
Date: 1955-08-24
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Pyongyang, August 23rd. The delegation of the People's Republic of China, led by Vice Chairman Zhu De, left Pyongyang by special train on the afternoon of the 23rd, after attending the celebration of the 10th anniversary of the liberation of Korea at the invitation of the government of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea.

Marshal Kim Il-sung, Premier of the Cabinet of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea, Kim Tu-bong, Chairman of the Standing Committee of the Supreme People's Assembly, and other leaders of the Korean Workers' Party and the government went to the station to see them off.

When Vice Chairman Zhu De and the members of the delegation arrived at the station, the military band played the national anthems of China and Korea. Then, accompanied by Marshal Kim Il-sung, Vice Chairman Zhu De reviewed the guard of honor lined up at the station. After the review, children from Pyongyang presented flowers to the Chinese delegation.

Vice Chairman Zhu De delivered a speech at the station (full text to be released separately). Then, the members of the Chinese delegation waved goodbye to the people who lined up at the station to see them off, and the people who saw them off waved flowers and enthusiastically cheered the delegation.

Then, Vice Chairman Zhu De and the members of the delegation shook hands with Marshal Kim Il-sung and others to say goodbye. As the special train left the station amid warm applause, Vice Chairman Zhu De continued to wave to the well-wishers.

Those who saw them off at the station today included: Deputy Premiers of the Cabinet of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea, Park Chang-ok, Kim Il, Choi Chang-ik, Hong Myung-hui, Park Ui-wan, Minister of Foreign Affairs Nam Il and cabinet ministers, Chairman of the Supreme People's Assembly Li Young and heads of democratic parties and social organizations, senior generals of the Korean People's Army, Chairman of the Pyongyang People's Committee, Jung Yeon-pyo, and more than 4,000 working people of Pyongyang.

Personnel from the Chinese Embassy in North Korea, and Soviet and other foreign envoys to North Korea also went to the station to see them off.

Accompanying Vice Chairman Zhu De in leaving Pyongyang were Choi Yong-kun, Deputy Premier of the Cabinet of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea, Ha Yang-cheon, Minister of Social Sciences of the Central Committee of the Korean Workers' Party, Li Bong-xi, Counselor of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs, and Choi In, Director of the Second Department of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs.

### 朱德副主席和我国代表团离开平壤

1955-08-24
第4版()
专栏：

　　朱德副主席和我国代表团离开平壤
    新华社平壤二十三日电　应朝鲜民主主义人民共和国政府邀请参加朝鲜解放十周年庆祝典礼的中华人民共和国代表团在团长朱德副主席率领下，于二十三日下午乘专车离开平壤。
    朝鲜民主主义人民共和国内阁首相金日成元帅、最高人民会议常任委员会委员长金枓奉等朝鲜劳动党和政府的领导人都到车站欢送。
    朱德副主席和代表团团员们到达车站后，军乐队奏起了中朝两国国歌。接着，在金日成元帅的陪同下，朱德副主席检阅了排列在车站上的仪仗队。检阅毕，平壤市儿童向中国代表团献了花。
    朱德副主席在车站上发表了谈话（全文另发），然后，中国代表团团员向在车站上列队欢送的人们招手道别，欢送的人们也高擎鲜花向代表团热烈地欢呼。
    接着，朱德副主席和代表团团员们同金日成元帅等亲切地握手道别。当专车在热烈掌声中驶离车站时，朱德副主席还向欢送者挥手不已。
    今天到车站欢送的还有：朝鲜民主主义人民共和国内阁副首相朴昌玉、金一、崔昌益、洪命熹、朴义琓，外务相南日和内阁各相，最高人民会议议长李英和各民主党派、社会团体负责人，朝鲜人民军高级将领，平壤市人民委员会委员长郑然彪和平壤市劳动人民四千多人。
    中国驻朝鲜大使馆人员、苏联和各国驻朝使节也都到车站欢送。
    陪送朱德副主席离开平壤的有朝鲜民主主义人民共和国内阁副首相崔庸健，朝鲜劳动党中央委员会社会科学部长河仰天，外务省参事李凤溪、外务省第二部部长崔仁等。
