    Our Great Motherland
    The Shanghai Factory of the Yaohua Glass Company, a joint state-private enterprise, successfully trial-produced tempered glass. Tempered glass has a wide range of uses and is a necessary material for manufacturing automobiles, trains, etc. Workers pull the heated glass from the electric furnace into the wind grid for cooling.

### 我们伟大的祖国（图片）

1955-08-07
第1版()
专栏：

    我们伟大的祖国
    公私合营耀华玻璃公司上海工厂试制钢化玻璃成功。钢化玻璃用途很广，是制造汽车、火车等必需的材料。工人把加热后的玻璃从电炉内拉进风栅里进行冷却。
