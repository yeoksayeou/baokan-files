Title: The Broad Masses of the Vietnamese People Demand a Consultative Conference
Author:
Date: 1955-08-18
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Hanoi, 17th - According to the Vietnam News Agency on the 17th: As of August 10th, over 650,000 people in North Vietnam had signed over 6,500 letters expressing their support for the statement and note of the Government of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam regarding the holding of a consultative conference. These letters have been sent to President Ho Chi Minh, the Government of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam, the Central Committee of the Vietnam Workers' Party, and the National Committee of the Vietnam National United Front.

In South Vietnam, the struggle of the people to achieve a consultative conference between the representatives of the responsible authorities of the two regions is also developing. On August 9th, the people of Sa Đéc held a demonstration expressing their desire for national reunification. On August 1st, residents of Chieu village near Saigon held a demonstration demanding that the Southern authorities agree to hold a consultative conference.

In the Saigon-Cholon area and the provinces of Bình Định, Phan Rang, and Bình Thuận, leaflets have been distributed clarifying the people's determination to strive for national reunification and oppose the arbitrary measures of the Southern authorities.

In Quảng Nam province, Bình Định province, and Khánh Hòa province, people in some areas spontaneously gathered to discuss the issue of free general elections and demanded that the South Vietnamese authorities hold consultations with the Government of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam.

### 越南广大人民要求举行协商会议

1955-08-18
第4版()
专栏：

    越南广大人民要求举行协商会议
    新华社河内十七日电　据越南通讯社十七日讯：截至八月十日，越南北方已经有六十五万多人在六千五百多份信件上签名，表示支持越南民主共和国政府关于举行协商会议的声明和照会。这些信件已经送给胡志明主席、越南民主共和国政府、越南劳动党中央委员会和越南国民联合战线全国委员会。
    在越南南方，人民争取举行两个地区负责当局代表之间的协商会议的斗争也在发展。八月九日，沙沥人民举行了一次示威，表示他们要求国家统一的愿望。八月一日，西贡市附近昭村的居民举行示威，要求南方当局同意举行协商会议。
    在西贡、堤岸地区以及平定、藩朗、平顺等省都有人散发传单，阐明人民争取国家统一和反对南方当局专横措施的决心。
    在广南省、平定省和庆和省，有些地方的人民自发地聚集在一起讨论自由普选问题，并且要求南越当局同越南民主共和国政府进行协商。
