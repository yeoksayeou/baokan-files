Title: Chairman Bulganin Holds Grand Reception to Host Diplomatic Envoys from Various Countries to the Soviet Union
Author:
Date: 1955-08-09
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, August 8th: TASS reports from Moscow: Soviet Minister Council Chairman Bulganin held a grand reception on the 7th to host diplomatic envoys from various countries to the Soviet Union.

	Guests began to arrive at 2:00 PM at a country villa located in one of the scenic areas of the Moscow region. Diplomats' cars traveled one after another along the road through birch forests and meadows. Cars carrying Soviet politicians and public figures also drove on the road.

	Welcoming the guests in the magnificent old garden were: Bulganin, Kaganovich, Malenkov, Mikoyan, Molotov, Pervukhin, Suslov, Khrushchev, Soviet Communist Party Central Committee Secretaries Aristov, Belyayev, Pospelov, and Shepilov. Communist Party and Soviet state leaders warmly welcomed the diplomatic envoys of various countries to the Soviet Union, their wives, and children. The sound of an orchestra came from afar.

	The ambassadors to the Soviet Union from various countries who attended were: U Ohn from the Union of Burma, Walters from Belgium, Nguyen Luong Bang from the Democratic Republic of Vietnam, Stefano from Italy, K.P.S. Menon from India, Bravo from Argentina, Bohlen from the United States, Ajil Bishi from Mongolia, Prifti from Albania, Vidić from Yugoslavia, Díaz from Mexico, Watkins from Canada, Subandrio from Indonesia, Avidar from Israel, and Li Xiangchao from the Democratic People's Republic of Korea. The envoys from various countries who attended were: Sreshthaputra from Thailand, Thorsteinsson from Iceland, Hani from Syria, Najjar from Lebanon, and Moch from Denmark. Also participating in the reception were the chargé d'affaires from the embassies of Iran, Pakistan, Egypt, Turkey, Afghanistan, the People's Republic of China, the German Democratic Republic, Romania, the United Kingdom, the Netherlands, Czechoslovakia, Sweden, Finland, Norway, France, Hungary, Austria, Poland, Greece, Bulgaria, Luxembourg, Ethiopia, and Switzerland to the Soviet Union.

	Bulganin warmly welcomed Bakaric, head of the delegation of the National Assembly of the Federal People's Republic of Yugoslavia, and P.S. Lokanathan, Executive Secretary of the United Nations Economic Commission for Asia and the Far East.

	Also attending the reception were: Soviet Minister Council Deputy Chairmen Lobanov and Tevosyan, Russian Federation Supreme Soviet Presidium Chairman Tarasov, Russian Federation Minister Council Chairman Puzanov, Soviet government ministers Benediktov, Yelyutin, Zhukov, and Zverev, Soviet Marshals Biryuzov and Konev, Soviet Minister Council State Committee for Long-Term National Economic Planning Chairman Baibakov, Soviet Supreme Soviet Presidium Secretary Pegov, Central Bureau of Economic Relations Director Koval, TASS Director Pargunov, "Trud" Chief Editor Burkov, deputy ministers of some Soviet ministries, Moscow Oblast Soviet Executive Committee Chairman Volkov, Moscow City Soviet Executive Committee Chairman Yasnov, and responsible staff members from the Soviet Minister Council, the Ministry of Foreign Affairs, and mass organizations.

	Soviet and foreign journalists also attended the reception.

	The hosts and guests immediately began a warm conversation. Khrushchev invited all those attending the reception to tour the garden. Foreign and Soviet guests strolled along the tree-lined paths, admiring the mirror-like pond. There were hammocks and comfortable wicker chairs in the woods, as well as boats and motorboats by the pool, and various fishing tackle for enthusiasts to use. Mikoyan and the U.S. Ambassador's family shared a small boat, followed by several other boats rowed by other guests. Waves of happy laughter were heard. After touring the garden, the guests began to take their seats, and the banquet was set up on the shore of the pool.

	At the end of the banquet, Bulganin, on behalf of the Soviet government, thanked all those who attended for being able to attend this reception. He said that it was a very pleasant thing to spend a day in such a gathering. Bulganin said that today's gathering reflected the spirit of friendship established in Geneva, which made such gatherings possible. Bulganin expressed his hope that such gatherings could be held more often in the future. He raised his glass to congratulate the strengthening of friendship between the representatives of the various countries gathered here.

	Burmese Ambassador U Ohn, in his capacity as the head of the diplomatic corps in the Soviet Union, delivered a reply. He expressed his gratitude to the Soviet government for its concern for the diplomatic envoys of various countries. He wished for achievements in strengthening friendship, developing mutual understanding based on the spirit of the Geneva Conference, and expanding economic and cultural ties between all countries in the East, West, South, and North.

	At the table, everyone continued the friendly conversation that had begun during the walk. Many of the guests were "football fans" and occasionally ran to the television set under the shade of the fir trees to watch the British and Soviet football players playing at the "Dynamo" stadium.

	An artistic performance was held at the reception, with the participation of many famous Soviet actors.

	The reception was conducted in an unrestrained and particularly friendly atmosphere.

### 招待各国驻苏联的外交使节  布尔加宁主席举行盛大招待会

1955-08-09
第4版()
专栏：

    招待各国驻苏联的外交使节
    布尔加宁主席举行盛大招待会
    新华社八日讯　据塔斯社莫斯科讯：苏联部长会议主席布尔加宁七日举行盛大招待会，招待各国驻苏联的外交使节。
    客人们在下午二时开始到达位于莫斯科州的风景区之一的一个乡村别墅。外交官的汽车沿着穿过桦树林和草地的公路络绎不绝地前进。在公路上，也驶行着载着苏联政治家和公众领袖的汽车。
    在壮丽的古花园里欢迎客人的有：布尔加宁、卡冈诺维奇、马林科夫、米高扬、莫洛托夫、别尔乌辛、苏斯洛夫、赫鲁晓夫，苏联共产党中央委员会书记阿里斯托夫、别利雅耶夫、波斯伯洛夫和谢皮洛夫。共产党和苏维埃国家的领导人热烈地欢迎各国驻苏联的外交使节、他们的夫人和子女。从远处传来了管弦乐队的乐声。
    到这儿作客的各国驻苏联大使有：缅甸联邦的吴旺，比利时的华特斯，越南民主共和国的阮良朋，意大利的斯蒂芬诺，印度的克·普·西·梅农，阿根廷的布拉沃，美国的波伦，蒙古的阿吉尔毕希，阿尔巴尼亚的普里夫蒂，南斯拉夫的维迪兹，墨西哥的迪亚斯，加拿大的华金斯，印度尼西亚的苏班德里奥，以色列的阿维达，朝鲜民主主义人民共和国的李相朝。到会各国公使有：泰国的斯勒什塔普特拉，冰岛的索尔斯坦逊，叙利亚的哈尼，黎巴嫩的纳贾尔，丹麦的莫希。参加招待会的还有伊朗、巴基斯坦、埃及、土耳其、阿富汗、中华人民共和国、德意志民主共和国、罗马尼亚、英国、荷兰、捷克斯洛伐克、瑞典、芬兰、挪威、法国、匈牙利、奥地利、波兰、希腊、保加利亚、卢森堡、埃塞俄比亚和瑞士等国驻苏联使馆的临时代办。
    布尔加宁热烈地欢迎南斯拉夫联邦人民共和国国民议会代表团团长巴卡里奇和联合国亚洲及远东经济委员会执行秘书普洛坎纳桑。
    参加招待会的还有：苏联部长会议副主席洛巴诺夫和捷沃西安，俄罗斯联邦最高苏维埃主席团主席塔拉索夫，俄罗斯联邦部长会议主席普扎诺夫，苏联政府各部部长别涅吉克托夫、叶留金、朱可夫和兹维列夫，苏联元帅比留佐夫和科涅夫，苏联部长会议国民经济长期计划国家委员会主席巴伊巴科夫，苏联最高苏维埃主席团秘书别哥夫，中央经济关系局局长科瓦尔，塔斯社社长帕尔古诺夫，“劳动报”总编辑布尔科夫，苏联一些部的副部长，莫斯科州苏维埃执行委员会主席沃尔科夫，莫斯科市苏维埃执行委员会主席雅斯诺夫，以及苏联部长会议、外交部和群众团体的负责工作人员。
    出席招待会的还有苏联和外国记者。
    宾主即刻开始了热烈的交谈。赫鲁晓夫邀请全体出席招待会的人游园。外国和苏联来宾沿着林荫小道漫步，欣赏着平静如镜的池水。树林中有吊床和舒适的藤椅，池边有小船和汽艇，也有各种钓鱼用具可供爱好者使用。米高扬和美国大使一家人共乘一个小艇，随他们之后，其他来宾们又划出了几只小船。高兴的谈笑声一阵阵地传来。来宾们在游园之后即开始入座，酒宴设在水池岸边。
    宴会完毕时，布尔加宁代表苏联政府感谢所有到会者能够出席这次招待会。他说，在这样一伙人中度过一天，是十分愉快的事。布尔加宁说，今天的聚会反映了在日内瓦所建立的友好精神，这种精神使得这样的聚会成为可能。布尔加宁表示希望今后能多多举行这样的聚会。他举杯祝贺在这里聚会的各国代表之间友谊的加强。
    缅甸大使吴旺以各国驻苏外交团团长的资格致答辞。他表示感谢苏联政府对各国外交使节的关注。他预祝在加强友谊，根据日内瓦会议精神发展互相谅解，扩大东、西、南、北一切国家之间的经济联系和文化联系这些方面获得成就。
    在席上，大家继续进行在散步时开始的友好的交谈。来宾中有不少“足球迷”，他们不时地跑到枞树浓荫下的电视机旁，去看英国和苏联足球运动员正在“狄纳莫”运动场上比赛的情况。
    招待会上举行了艺术表演，由许多苏联著名演员参加演出。
    招待会在毫不拘束和特别友好的气氛中进行。
