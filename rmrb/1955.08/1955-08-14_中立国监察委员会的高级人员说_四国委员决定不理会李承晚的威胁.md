Title: Senior Official of Neutral Nations Supervisory Commission Says: Four-Nation Committee Decides to Ignore Syngman Rhee's Threats
Author:
Date: 1955-08-14
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, August 13th: According to a report from Seoul by the United Press, a senior official of the Neutral Nations Supervisory Commission (NNSC) said on the 12th that the four-nation committee of the commission had decided to ignore the South Korean government's "ultimatum" demanding that they leave South Korea before 24:00 on August 13th.

    He said, "We have decided not to leave." He also said that all four commissioners had agreed to this.

    He declared, "Our staying or leaving" should be "decided by the governments of Switzerland, Sweden, Czechoslovakia, and Poland, or the signatories of the armistice agreement."

Xinhua News Agency, August 13th: According to an Associated Press reporter in Panmunjom, a Swiss member of the NNSC said on the 12th that the NNSC "is not prepared to concede to any outside demands that it leave Korea."

    He said that the Swiss delegation of this four-nation body specifically believes that it must remain on the commission until the signatories of the armistice agreement relieve them of their duties.

### 中立国监察委员会的高级人员说  四国委员决定不理会李承晚的威胁

1955-08-14
第1版()
专栏：

    中立国监察委员会的高级人员说
    四国委员决定不理会李承晚的威胁
    新华社十三日讯　据合众社从汉城报道，中立国监察委员会的一位高级人员十二日说，委员会的四国委员已经决定不理会南朝鲜政府要他们在八月十三日二十四时以前离开南朝鲜的“最后通牒”。
    他说，“我们决定不走”。他又说，所有四位委员都已经同意这样做。
    他宣称，“我们的去留”要由“瑞士、瑞典、捷克斯洛伐克和波兰各国政府或停战协定的签字国来决定”。
    新华社十三日讯　据美联社驻板门店记者报道，中立国监察委员会的一位瑞士人员十二日说，中立国监察委员会“不准备向任何要它离开朝鲜的外界要求让步。”
    他说，这个四国机构的瑞士代表团特别认为，它必须留在委员会，直到停战协定的签字国解除他们的职务时为止。
