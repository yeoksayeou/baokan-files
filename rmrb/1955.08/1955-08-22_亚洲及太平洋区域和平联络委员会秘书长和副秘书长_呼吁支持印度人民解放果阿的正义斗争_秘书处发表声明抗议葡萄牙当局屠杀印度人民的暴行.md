Title: Secretary-General and Deputy Secretary-General of the Asian and Pacific Regions Peace Liaison Committee Call for Support for the Just Struggle of the Indian People to Liberate Goa; Secretariat Issues Statement Protesting the Portuguese Authorities' Massacre of the Indian People

Author:

Date: 1955-08-22
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, August 21st. The Secretary-General of the Asian and Pacific Regions Peace Liaison Committee, Liu Ningyi, and Deputy Secretaries-General Kameta Togo and Wantuleli sent telegrams on August 21st to the peace committees of countries in the Asian and Pacific regions, hoping that they would consider taking action to support the just struggle of the Indian people to demand the return of Goa to India.

The telegram said: "Enclosed is a telegram from Mr. Romesh Chandra of the All-India Peace Council, dated August 15, which reads as follows:

'On August 15, the Portuguese armed forces in Goa unjustifiably killed more than thirty Indian citizens and injured many more. We request that the peace committees and peace-loving people of all countries express sympathy and support for the liberation of Goa and condemn the atrocities of Portugal. Prominent figures and organizations in various countries must protest to the Portuguese consulates and embassies in their respective countries and express support for India's demand that Portugal immediately end its rule in Goa.'

The brutal acts of the Portuguese colonialists in Goa cannot but arouse the indignation of peace-loving people. We hope that you will consider taking action to protest the Portuguese colonial authorities' massacre of the Indian people and support the just struggle of the Indian people to demand the return of Goa to India."

The above telegrams have been sent to the peace committees of the following countries: Australia, Burma, Cambodia, Canada, Ceylon, Chile, Colombia, Cyprus, Ecuador, Indonesia, Israel, Japan, Democratic People's Republic of Korea, Laos, Lebanon, Mexico, Mongolia, Nepal, New Zealand, Syria, the United States, the Soviet Union, and the Democratic Republic of Vietnam.

Xinhua News Agency, August 21st. The Secretariat of the Asian and Pacific Regions Peace Liaison Committee issued the following statement on August 21st regarding the Portuguese authorities' massacre of peaceful Indian people:

The Portuguese colonialists, in violation of all humanitarian spirit and standards of international conduct, used force in Goa on August 15 to kill and injure more than two hundred unarmed, peaceful Indian people. Such brutal means of suppression cannot but arouse great anger and solemn protest from the people of the Asian and Pacific regions, and the peace-loving, independent, and free people of the world.

The just demand of the Indian people to liberate Goa, safeguard peace, and ensure national sovereignty and territorial integrity, is not only supported by the entire Indian people but also receives the sympathy and support of peace-loving people all over the world, especially in the Asian and Pacific regions. We firmly believe that with the support of peace-loving, independent, and free people around the world, the Indian people's just struggle to liberate Goa will surely be victorious.

### 亚洲及太平洋区域和平联络委员会秘书长和副秘书长　呼吁支持印度人民解放果阿的正义斗争　秘书处发表声明抗议葡萄牙当局屠杀印度人民的暴行

1955-08-22
第1版()
专栏：

　　亚洲及太平洋区域和平联络委员会秘书长和副秘书长
　　呼吁支持印度人民解放果阿的正义斗争
　　秘书处发表声明抗议葡萄牙当局屠杀印度人民的暴行
    新华社二十一日讯　亚洲及太平洋区域和平联络委员会秘书长刘宁一，副秘书长龟田东伍和万徒勒里在二十一日打电报给亚洲和太平洋区域各国的和平委员会，希望它们考虑采取行动，支持印度人民要求果阿重归印度的正义斗争。
    电报说：“兹转上全印和平理事会罗米希·钱德拉先生八月十五日来电一通，电文如下：
   ‘八月十五日在果阿的葡萄牙武装部队无理地打死了三十多个印度公民，并有很多人受伤。我们要求各国和平委员会和爱好和平的人士对解放果阿表示同情和支持，并且谴责葡萄牙的暴行。各国著名人士和组织必须向在各该国的葡萄牙领事馆和使馆提出抗议，并且对印度要求葡萄牙立即结束在果阿的统治表示支持。’
    葡萄牙殖民主义者在果阿的这种凶暴举动，不能不引起爱好和平的人们的愤慨。希望你们考虑采取行动，抗议葡萄牙殖民当局屠杀印度人民的暴行，支持印度人民要求果阿重归印度的正义斗争。”
    上项的电报已发给下述各国的和平委员会：澳大利亚、缅甸、柬埔寨、加拿大、锡兰、智利、哥伦比亚、塞浦路斯、厄瓜多尔、印度尼西亚、以色列、日本、朝鲜民主主义人民共和国、老挝、黎巴嫩、墨西哥、蒙古、尼泊尔、新西兰、叙利亚、美国、苏联和越南民主共和国。
    新华社二十一日讯　亚洲及太平洋区域和平联络委员会秘书处在二十一日就葡萄牙当局屠杀和平的印度人民发表声明如下：
    葡萄牙殖民主义者违反一切人道的精神和国际行为的标准，于八月十五日在果阿使用武力杀死和杀伤二百多名赤手空拳的和平的印度人民，这种残暴的镇压手段，不能不引起亚洲和太平洋区域以及全世界爱好和平、独立、自由的人民的极大愤怒和严正抗议。
    印度人民解放果阿这一维护和平、保障国家主权和领土完整的正义要求，不仅得到全印度人民的支持，而且也得到全世界特别是亚洲及太平洋区域爱好和平的人民的同情和支持。我们坚信，在全世界爱好和平、独立、自由的人民的支持下，印度人民要求解放果阿的正义斗争是一定会得到胜利的。
