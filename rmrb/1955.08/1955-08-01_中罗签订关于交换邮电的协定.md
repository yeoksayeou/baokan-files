Title: China and Romania Sign Agreement on Exchange of Postal and Telecommunications Services
Author:
Date: 1955-08-01
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, Prague, July 31st: According to the Romanian News Agency, Bucharest: The agreement between the Romanian People's Republic and the People's Republic of China on the exchange of postal and telecommunications services was signed on July 30th at the Ministry of Posts and Telecommunications of the Romanian People's Republic. Dumitru Simulescu, Minister of Posts and Telecommunications of Romania, signed on behalf of the Romanian People's Republic, and Ke Bonian, Ambassador of the People's Republic of China to Romania, signed on behalf of the People's Republic of China.

### 中罗签订关于交换邮电的协定

1955-08-01
第1版()
专栏：

    中罗签订关于交换邮电的协定
    新华社布拉格三十一日电　据罗马尼亚通讯社布加勒斯特讯：罗马尼亚人民共和国和中华人民共和国关于交换邮电的协定于七月三十日在罗马尼亚人民共和国邮电部签订。代表罗马尼亚人民共和国签字的是罗马尼亚邮电部长杜米特鲁·西穆莱斯库，代表中华人民共和国签字的是中华人民共和国驻罗马尼亚大使柯柏年。
