    Our Great Motherland
    The National Juvenile Science and Technology and Craft Works Exhibition will open in Beijing tomorrow. This exhibition will play a positive role in further developing science and technology and craft activities for children and young people. This is a scene of children visiting a parachute tower model during the pre-exhibition.

### 我们伟大的祖国（图片）

1955-08-09
第1版()
专栏：

    我们伟大的祖国
    全国少年儿童科学技术和工艺作品展览会将于明日在北京开幕。这个展览会将对进一步开展少年儿童的科学技术和工艺活动起积极的作用。这是小朋友们在预展时参观跳伞塔模型的情形。
