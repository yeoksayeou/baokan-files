Title: Head of Chinese Art Troupe Zhang Zhixiang Discusses Cultural Exchange with Belgian Minister of Education
Author:
Date: 1955-08-04
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency, Prague, August 3rd: Brussels dispatch: Zhang Zhixiang, head of the Chinese Art Troupe, paid a visit to Belgian Minister of Education Collard and Director of the Ministry of Education's Office Coupers on the 2nd. Collard and Coupers expressed their hope for the two countries to exchange reproductions of fine arts and hold exhibitions. The conversation between the two sides was very harmonious, and they agreed to have direct contact in the future to discuss the development of cultural exchange.

### 我国艺术团团长  张致祥同比利时教育大臣谈文化交流问题

1955-08-04
第4版()
专栏：

    我国艺术团团长
    张致祥同比利时教育大臣谈文化交流问题
    新华社布拉格三日电　布鲁塞尔消息：中国艺术团团长张致祥在二日拜访了比利时教育大臣科拉德和教育部办公厅主任库柏斯。科拉德和库柏斯表示希望两国交换造型艺术复制品和举行展览会。双方交谈甚为融洽，并且同意就今后发展文化交流问题进行直接接触。
