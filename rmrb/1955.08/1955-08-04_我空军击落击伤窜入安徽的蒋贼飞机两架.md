Title: Our Air Force Shot Down and Damaged Two Chiang Kai-shek Bandit Planes that Intruded into Anhui

Author:

Date: 1955-08-04
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Hefei, 3rd. The Chinese People's Liberation Army Air Force shot down and damaged one American-made Chiang Kai-shek bandit F-86 jet each.

At 11:50 AM on the 3rd, two American-made Chiang Kai-shek bandit F-86 jet planes intruded into the airspace over Qingyang County, Anhui Province. The Chinese People's Liberation Army Air Force immediately took off to intercept them, using accurate artillery fire to shoot down one and damage another. The damaged Chiang Kai-shek bandit plane fled in a panic towards the ocean airspace in the southeast direction.

### 我空军击落击伤窜入安徽的蒋贼飞机两架

1955-08-04
第1版()
专栏：

    我空军击落击伤窜入安徽的蒋贼飞机两架
    新华社合肥三日电　中国人民解放军空军飞机击落击伤美造蒋贼F—86型喷气飞机各一架。
    三日十一时五十分，美造蒋贼F—86型喷气飞机两架窜入安徽省青阳县境上空，中国人民解放军空军飞机当即起飞迎击，以准确的炮火将其击落一架、击伤一架。被击伤的这架蒋贼飞机狼狈地向东南方向海洋上空逃去。
