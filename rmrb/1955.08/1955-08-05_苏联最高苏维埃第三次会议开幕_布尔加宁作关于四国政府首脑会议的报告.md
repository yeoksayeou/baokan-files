Title: Third Session of the Supreme Soviet of the USSR Opens; Bulganin Delivers Report on the Four-Power Summit Conference
Author:
Date: 1955-08-05
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Moscow dispatch from TASS, August 4th: The third session of the Fourth Supreme Soviet of the USSR opened at 3:00 PM on August 4th in the Grand Kremlin Palace in Moscow.

Deputies of the Supreme Soviet of the USSR, representing the various nationalities of the multinational Soviet Union, attended the meeting.

Many guests were present in the meeting hall, including advanced workers from Soviet enterprises, cultural workers, scientific workers, Soviet military personnel, and representatives from all walks of life in the capital.

In special seats were diplomatic envoys from various countries stationed in Moscow, as well as Soviet and foreign journalists. A delegation of Yugoslav journalists currently visiting the Soviet Union also attended the meeting.

At 3:00 PM, Chairman of the Council of the Union, Volkov, and Chairman of the Council of Nationalities, Lacis, as well as the Vice-Chairmen of both houses, took their seats on the presidium.

The leaders of the Communist Party of the Soviet Union and the Soviet government entered the hall, at which point the deputies and guests welcomed them with stormy and prolonged applause.

Chairman of the Council of the Union, Volkov, who presided over the meeting, announced the opening of the joint session of the Council of the Union and the Council of Nationalities. He announced that a delegation from the National Assembly of the Federal People's Republic of Yugoslavia, headed by Bakarić, President of the Parliament of the People's Republic of Croatia, was attending the meeting. On behalf of the deputies of the Supreme Soviet of the USSR, Volkov extended a heartfelt welcome to the distinguished guests.

Following this, Chairman of the Council of Ministers of the USSR, Bulganin, delivered a report on the Soviet government delegation's participation in the Geneva Four-Power Summit Conference.

### 苏联最高苏维埃第三次会议开幕  布尔加宁作关于四国政府首脑会议的报告

1955-08-05
第4版()
专栏：

    苏联最高苏维埃第三次会议开幕
    布尔加宁作关于四国政府首脑会议的报告
    新华社四日讯　塔斯社莫斯科讯：八月四日下午三时，第四届苏联最高苏维埃第三次会议在莫斯科大克里姆林宫开幕。
    苏联人民的代表——代表多民族的苏联的各族人民的苏联最高苏维埃代表们出席了会议。
    会议厅中坐有许多来宾，他们是苏联企业的先进工作者、文化工作者、科学工作者、苏军军人、首都各界代表。
    在专席是各国驻莫斯科的外交使节，苏联和外国记者。正在苏联访问的南斯拉夫新闻工作者代表团也参加了会议。
    下午三时，联盟院主席沃尔科夫和民族院主席拉齐斯以及两院的副主席都到主席团席位上就座。
    苏联共产党和苏联政府的领导人进入会场，这时代表们和来宾们以暴风雨般的、经久不息的鼓掌欢迎他们。
    担任会议主席的联盟院主席沃尔科夫宣布联盟院和民族院联席会议开幕。他宣布说，正在苏联访问的、以克罗地亚人民共和国议会议长巴卡里奇为首的南斯拉夫联邦人民共和国国民议会代表团参加了会议。沃尔科夫代表苏联最高苏维埃代表向贵宾们表示衷心的欢迎。
    接着由苏联部长会议主席布尔加宁做出席日内瓦四国政府首脑会议的苏联政府代表团的报告。
