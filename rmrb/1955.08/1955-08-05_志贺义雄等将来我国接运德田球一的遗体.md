Title: Shiga Yoshio and Others to Come to Our Country to Transport the Remains of Tokuda Kyuichi
Author:
Date: 1955-08-05
Page: 1

    Xinhua News Agency, 4th. According to Kyodo News Agency, the Central Committee of the Japanese Communist Party decided on the 1st to send Central Committee member Shiga Yoshio, Tokuda Tatsu, the wife of the deceased General Secretary Tokuda Kyuichi, and Tokuda Masatsugu, the younger brother, to China to transport the remains of Comrade Tokuda Kyuichi.

### 志贺义雄等将来我国接运德田球一的遗体

1955-08-05
第1版()
专栏：

    志贺义雄等将来我国接运德田球一的遗体
    新华社四日讯　据共同社报道，日本共产党中央委员会在一日决定派中央委员志贺义雄和已故总书记德田球一的夫人德田辰和弟弟德田正次三人前往中国，接运德田球一同志的遗体。
