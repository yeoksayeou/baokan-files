Title: Our Art Troupe Performs Again in Belgium
Author:
Date: 1955-08-03
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency, Prague, August 2nd: Brussels dispatch: To meet the demands of Belgian audiences, the Chinese Art Troupe performed for the second time in Belgium from July 29th to 31st, after concluding its visit to the Netherlands.

The Chinese Art Troupe performed a total of twelve shows in Brussels, Antwerp, and Ostend during its two visits, with audiences totaling more than 14,000 people.

Belgian newspapers unanimously praised the performance of the Chinese Art Troupe. "Red Flag" said: The Chinese Art Troupe excellently introduced the history and life of the Chinese people to the West, and "people are quickly attracted by the remarkable popular character of this performance." "Last Hour" said: "The Chinese Art Troupe has opened a door for us to a truly new world."

"People's Daily" expressed the hope that Western audiences would have more opportunities to see such performances. It said that this "will shorten the distance between us and the great China."
Xinhua News Agency, Prague, August 1st: Brussels dispatch: The Mayor of Antwerp, Belgium, Mr. Craeybeckx, and the Mayor of Ostend, Mr. Van Graefschepe, separately received the head of the Chinese Art Troupe, Zhang Zhixiang, and the main members of the art troupe during the troupe's performances in these two cities. During the meeting, both sides expressed their willingness to develop the friendship and cultural ties between the Belgian and Chinese people, and exchanged gifts.

On July 31st, Zhang Zhixiang held a reception in Ostend. Many artists and art critics from Ostend attended the reception.

### 我国艺术团再次在比利时演出

1955-08-03
第4版()
专栏：

    我国艺术团再次在比利时演出
    新华社布拉格二日电　布鲁塞尔消息：中国艺术团为满足比利时观众的要求，在结束了在荷兰的访问演出之后，在七月二十九日至三十一日第二次到比利时演出。
    中国艺术团前后两次共在布鲁塞尔、安特卫普和奥斯顿德演出了十二场，观众达一万四千余人。
    比利时报纸一致赞扬中国艺术团的演出。“红旗报”说：中国艺术团把中国人民的历史和生活卓越地介绍给西方，“人们很快地为这种演出的显著的人民性所吸引”。“最后时刻报”说：“中国艺术团向我们打开了走向真正新的世界去的大门”。
  “人民报”表示希望西方观众能够有更多的机会观看到这样的演出。它说：这就“会缩短我们同伟大的中国之间的距离。”
    新华社布拉格一日电　布鲁塞尔消息：比利时安特卫普市长克莱·贝克斯和奥斯顿德市长范格拉贝克在中国艺术团在这两个城市演出期间分别接见了中国艺术团团长张致祥和艺术团的主要团员。接见时，双方表示愿意发展比、中两国人民的友谊和文化联系，并且互赠礼物。
      七月三十一日，张致祥在奥斯顿德举行了招待会。奥斯顿德许多美术家和艺术批评家都出席了招待会。
