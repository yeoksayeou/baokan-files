Title: Premier Zhou Enlai Receives Albanian Cultural Delegation and People's Army Song and Dance Troupe
Author:
Date: 1955-08-19
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, August 18th: Premier Zhou Enlai of the State Council of the People's Republic of China received Fadil Paçrami, head of the Albanian Cultural Delegation, and members Gjon Rrahmani, Hasan Duma, Aleks Çaçi, and Nexhmedin Çaçi on the afternoon of August 18th. He also received Lieutenant Colonel Mekur Çela, leader and head of the Albanian People's Army Song and Dance Troupe, artistic director Gjafer Avrazi, and members Stavri Rafael and Hysen Gudi.

Present at the reception were: Peng Dehuai, Vice Premier of the State Council and Minister of National Defense; Yang Xiufeng, Minister of Higher Education; Zhang Hanfu, Vice Minister of Foreign Affairs; Xia Yan, Vice Minister of Culture; Su Yu, Chief of the General Staff of the Chinese People's Liberation Army; Chen Geng, Deputy Chief of the General Staff; Fu Zhong, Deputy Director of the General Political Department; Xiao Xiangrong, Director of the General Office of the Ministry of National Defense; Chen Yi, Director of the Cultural Department of the General Political Department of the Chinese People's Liberation Army; Mei Yi, Director of the Broadcasting Affairs Bureau; and Chen Zhongjing, Acting Director of the Bureau of Foreign Cultural Relations.

Albanian Ambassador to China, Nesti Nase, was also present at the reception. (Attached picture)

　　　Premier Zhou Enlai receives the leaders of the Albanian Cultural Delegation and the People's Army Song and Dance Troupe. From left to right: Albanian Ambassador to China Nesti Nase, Lieutenant Colonel Mekur Çela, leader and head of the People's Army Song and Dance Troupe, Fadil Paçrami, head of the Cultural Delegation, and Premier Zhou Enlai. ### 周总理接见阿文化代表团和人民军歌舞团

1955-08-19
第1版()
专栏：

    周总理接见阿文化代表团和人民军歌舞团
    新华社十八日讯  中华人民共和国国务院总理周恩来，在十八日下午接见了阿尔巴尼亚文化代表团团长法迪尔·帕奇拉米，团员吉米特尔·拉玛尼、哈桑·杜玛、阿列克斯·恰奇和聂泽梅丁·查伊米，阿尔巴尼亚人民军歌舞团领队兼团长梅库尔·柴拉中校，艺术指导加乔·阿夫拉齐，团员斯塔夫里·拉斐尔和希声·古迪。
    接见时在座的有：国务院副总理兼国防部长彭德怀，高等教育部部长杨秀峰，外交部副部长章汉夫，文化部副部长夏衍，中国人民解放军总参谋长粟裕、副总参谋长陈赓，总政治部副主任傅锺，国防部办公厅主任萧向荣，中国人民解放军总政治部文化部部长陈沂，广播事业局局长梅益，对外文化联络局代理局长陈忠经。
    接见时，阿尔巴尼亚驻华大使纳赛也在座。（附图片）
    周恩来总理接见阿尔巴尼亚文化代表团和人民军歌舞团负责人。自左至右：阿尔巴尼亚驻我国大使纳赛、人民军歌舞团领队兼团长梅库尔·柴拉中校、文化代表团团长法迪尔·帕奇拉米、周恩来总理。新华社记者  齐观山摄
