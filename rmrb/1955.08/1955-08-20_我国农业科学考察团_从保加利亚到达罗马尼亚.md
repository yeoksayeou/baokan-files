Title: Chinese Agricultural Science Delegation Arrives in Romania from Bulgaria
Author:
Date: 1955-08-20
Page: 4

    Xinhua News Agency, Prague, August 15th: The Chinese Agricultural Science Delegation, led by Sun Xiaocun, President of Beijing Agricultural University, arrived in Bucharest on August 15th by train, after a visit to Bulgaria for over a month at the invitation of the Bulgarian government.
    During its stay in Bulgaria, the delegation visited agricultural science research institutes, experimental stations, state farms, and agricultural production cooperatives in Sofia, Plovdiv, and Stalin City. They also exchanged experiences with Bulgarian scientists. On August 12th, Vulko Chervenkov, First Deputy Chairman of the Bulgarian Council of Ministers, received Sun Xiaocun, the head of the delegation, and other members.

### 我国农业科学考察团  从保加利亚到达罗马尼亚

1955-08-20
第4版()
专栏：

    我国农业科学考察团
    从保加利亚到达罗马尼亚
    据新华社布拉格十五日电　由北京农业大学校长孙晓村率领的中国农业科学考察团，应邀到保加利亚访问一个多月后，于八月十四日乘车离开索非亚，并于十五日到达布加勒斯特。
    代表团在保加利亚期间，曾先后访问了索非亚、普罗夫迪夫和斯大林城等地的农业科学研究所、试验站、国营农场和农业生产合作社，并同保加利亚科学工作者交流了经验。八月十二日，保加利亚部长会议第一副主席于哥夫接见了代表团团长孙晓村和其他团员。
