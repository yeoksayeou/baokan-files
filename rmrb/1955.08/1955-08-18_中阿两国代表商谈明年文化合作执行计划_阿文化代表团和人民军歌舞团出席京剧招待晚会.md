Title: China and Albania Discuss Next Year's Cultural Cooperation Implementation Plan; Albanian Cultural Delegation and People's Army Song and Dance Troupe Attend Peking Opera Reception
Author:
Date: 1955-08-18
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, 17th: Representatives from the People's Republic of China and the People's Republic of Albania held talks on the 1956 implementation plan for the Sino-Albanian Cultural Cooperation Agreement on the 16th.

Representing the Chinese side in the negotiations were Qian Junrui (Chief Representative), Acting Minister of the Ministry of Culture of the People's Republic of China, Chen Zhongjing, Acting Director of the Bureau of Foreign Cultural Liaison, and Wu Qing, Specialist of the Soviet and Eastern European Department of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs; representing the Albanian side were the head of the Albanian Cultural Delegation and Chairman of the Committee for Foreign Cultural Liaison of the People's Republic of Albania, Pacrami (Chief Representative), Director of the Information Department of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs, Lamani, and President of the Higher Normal School, Duma.

Xia Yan, Vice Minister of the Ministry of Culture of the People's Republic of China, and Nasse, Ambassador of the People's Republic of Albania to China, also attended the meeting.

The talks proceeded in a friendly and harmonious atmosphere and have been fully agreed upon. The draft plan will be formally signed in Beijing after being approved by the two governments.

Xinhua News Agency, 17th: The General Political Department of the Chinese People's Liberation Army and the Ministry of Culture of the People's Republic of China held a Peking Opera soiree on the evening of the 17th to entertain all members of the Albanian Cultural Delegation and the Albanian People's Army Song and Dance Troupe.

Albanian Ambassador to China Nasse also attended the Peking Opera soiree.

At the soiree, famous Chinese performing artist Mei Lanfang and others performed "The Drunken Concubine," and the Chinese Opera School and the China Peking Opera Theatre performed "Jinshan Temple" and "Meeting the Empress" respectively. When the performance ended, Mei Lanfang and all the performers accepted flowers from the guests.

The Song and Dance Troupe of the Chinese People's Liberation Army performed more than a dozen wonderful programs of music, dance, and the dance drama "Luo Shengjiao" for the Albanian guests on the evening of the 15th. The Albanian song "Ode to the Party and Leader – Hoxha" sung by the song and dance troupe was re-sung once at the request of the guests' applause.

### 中阿两国代表商谈明年文化合作执行计划  阿文化代表团和人民军歌舞团出席京剧招待晚会

1955-08-18
第1版()
专栏：

    中阿两国代表商谈明年文化合作执行计划
    阿文化代表团和人民军歌舞团出席京剧招待晚会
    新华社十七日讯　中华人民共和国和阿尔巴尼亚人民共和国的代表，在十六日就中阿文化合作协定一九五六年执行计划举行了会谈。
    代表中国方面参加谈判的是中华人民共和国文化部代理部长钱俊瑞（首席代表），对外文化联络局代理局长陈忠经，外交部苏联东欧司专员吴青；代表阿尔巴尼亚方面参加谈判的是阿尔巴尼亚文化代表团团长、阿尔巴尼亚人民共和国对外文化联络委员会主席帕奇拉米（首席代表），外交部新闻司司长拉玛尼，高等师范学校校长杜玛。
    中华人民共和国文化部副部长夏衍和阿尔巴尼亚人民共和国驻华大使纳赛也参加了这次会谈。
    会谈在友好融洽的气氛中进行，并已全部达成协议。计划草案经双方政府批准后即将在北京正式签订。
    新华社十七日讯　中国人民解放军总政治部和中华人民共和国文化部，在十七日晚举行京剧晚会，招待阿尔巴尼亚文化代表团和阿尔巴尼亚人民军歌舞团全体人员。
    阿尔巴尼亚驻华大使纳赛也出席了京剧晚会。
    晚会上，中国著名表演艺术家梅兰芳等演出了“贵妃醉酒”，中国戏曲学校和中国京剧院分别演了“金山寺”和“遇皇后”。当演出结束时，梅兰芳及全体参加演出者接受了客人们的献花。
    中国人民解放军歌舞团曾在十五日晚为阿尔巴尼亚客人们演出了音乐、舞蹈和舞剧“罗盛教”等十多个精采节目。歌舞团演唱的阿尔巴尼亚歌曲“歌颂党和领袖——霍查”，在客人们的鼓掌要求下，重唱了一次。
