Title: PLA and PVA Units Celebrate "August 1st" Army Day; People in Various Regions Conduct Activities to Support the Army and Give Preferential Treatment to Families of Servicemen Before and After "August 1st"
Author:
Date: 1955-08-03
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency Report: The People's Liberation Army (PLA) stationed in various locations, the leadership organs of the Chinese People's Volunteer Army (PVA), and various units held various commemorative activities to warmly celebrate the 28th anniversary of the "August 1st" Army Day. Before and after "August 1st," the masses in various regions extensively launched various activities to support the army and give preferential treatment to families of servicemen.

    On August 1st, the PLA stationed on the front lines of the southeast coast presented a festive scene everywhere. In each company's club and position of various armed services, wall newspapers and special issues celebrating the "August 1st" Festival were published. In the "Hall of Honor Exhibition" held by a certain fighter jet unit of the Air Force, many combat charts, photos, heroic deeds, and awards and banners were displayed. Some units held forums for new and old soldiers, and officers and veteran soldiers told new soldiers stories of battles. Many units also held military sports competitions and cultural evenings. On this day, the troops of the Tibet Military Region held a celebration rally in the People's Square in front of the Potala Palace in Lhasa; the troops of the Xinjiang Military Region also gathered at the "August 1st" Square in Urumqi to celebrate Army Day.
    On the evening of August 1st, the leadership organs of the Chinese People's Volunteer Army held a commemorative meeting. Yang Yong, commander of the Chinese People's Volunteer Army, called on the entire army to maintain and carry forward the fine traditions of the Chinese People's Army, strengthen the combat friendship with the heroic Korean People's Army, continue to be vigilant, maintain the Korean armistice, and strive for the realization of the peaceful reunification of Korea. Li Yong-ho, head of the Korean People's Army delegation and Deputy Minister of the Korean National Defense Ministry, said: The Korean people and soldiers of the People's Army feel infinitely happy and proud to have such heroic friends—the fraternal Chinese people and the Chinese People's Liberation Army. After the meeting, the Korean People's Army Concert Troupe gave a wonderful performance. The various units of the Volunteer Army also held commemorative meetings on the same day. The people of Beijing carried out many activities to support the army and give preferential treatment to families of servicemen to celebrate the "August 1st" Army Day. The heads of the Xisi and Xuanwu District People's Committees respectively visited the hospitals in their respective areas to express their regards to the wounded and sick soldiers of the Chinese People's Liberation Army and the Chinese People's Volunteer Army who were recuperating. In many cinemas and squares, tens of thousands of families of martyrs, families of servicemen, revolutionary disabled soldiers, and demobilized and transferred soldiers watched movies for free.
    Before the festival, the people of various districts in Shanghai also carried out activities such as military-civilian get-togethers, visits to families of revolutionary martyrs, families of revolutionary soldiers, and demobilized construction soldiers. Students from Fudan University also organized a visiting team to visit the troops stationed in Shanghai. The Shanghai Peking Opera Theater, the Shanghai Orchestra, and four other units organized four performing teams to go to various military hospitals to perform and express their regards to the wounded and sick soldiers of the Chinese People's Liberation Army and the Chinese People's Volunteer Army who were recuperating in Shanghai.
    Before and after "August 1st," relevant units in Tianjin visited the troops stationed in Tianjin and the convalescents, and also formed inspection teams to inspect the placement of demobilized construction soldiers in various units in the city.
    People from all walks of life in Shenyang formed three visiting groups, led by Chen Xianzhou, Deputy Mayor of Shenyang, on August 1st, and led performing teams to visit wounded and sick soldiers in various hospitals of the troops stationed in Shenyang.
    On the eve of "August 1st," the Jiangxi Provincial People's Committee allocated 800,000 yuan in relief funds to help the families of martyrs, families of servicemen, and veteran Red Army soldiers in 33 counties, including Ruijin, Xingguo, and Yiyang, and Jinggangshan, the base areas during the Second Revolutionary Civil War, to build houses. During the "August 1st" Festival, the Guangxi Provincial People's Committee sent four film teams to the border areas to express their regards to the border troops.

### 解放军和志愿军各部队纪念“八一”建军节  各地人民在“八一”前后进行拥军优属活动

1955-08-03
第1版()
专栏：

    解放军和志愿军各部队纪念“八一”建军节
    各地人民在“八一”前后进行拥军优属活动
    据新华社讯　中国人民解放军驻各地部队、中国人民志愿军领导机关和各部队举行各种纪念活动，热烈庆祝“八一”建军二十八周年。“八一”前后，各地群众广泛展开了各种拥军优属活动。
    八月一日，在东南沿海前线的解放军的驻地上，到处呈现着节日的景象。各兵种部队的每个连队的俱乐部和阵地上，都出版了庆祝“八一”节的墙报专刊。在空军某歼击机部队举办的“荣誉展览室”里，陈列着很多战斗图表、照片、英雄事迹和奖品、奖旗等。有的部队举行了新、老战士座谈会，军官和老战士向新战士们讲述了战斗故事。许多部队还举行了军事体育比赛和文娱晚会。这一天，西藏军区部队在拉萨布达拉宫前的人民广场举行庆祝大会；新疆军区部队也在乌鲁木齐的“八一”广场集会庆祝建军节。
    八月一日晚上，中国人民志愿军领导机关举行了纪念大会。中国人民志愿军司令员杨勇号召全军要保持和发扬中国人民军队的优良传统，增强和英雄的朝鲜人民军之间的战斗友谊，继续提高警惕，维护朝鲜的停战，并为争取朝鲜和平统一的实现而努力。朝鲜人民军代表团团长、朝鲜民族保卫省副相李永镐说：朝鲜人民和人民军将士们有了这样英雄的朋友——兄弟般的中国人民和中国人民解放军，觉得无限幸福和骄傲。会后，朝鲜人民军协奏团作了精采的演出。志愿军各部队也在同一天分别举行了纪念会。北京市人民为庆祝“八一”建军节，进行了很多拥军优属活动。西四和宣武区人民委员会的负责人，分别到所在地的医院慰问了正在休养的中国人民解放军和中国人民志愿军伤病员，在很多电影院和广场，数以万计的烈属、军属、革命残废军人和复员转业军人，免费观看了电影。
    上海市各区人民还在节日前先后开展了军民联欢、慰问革命烈士家属、革命军人家属和复员建设军人等活动。复旦大学学生还组织了一个慰问队慰问了上海的驻军。上海京剧院、上海乐团等六个单位组织了四个演出队分赴各部队医院演出，慰问在沪休养的中国人民解放军和中国人民志愿军伤病员。
    天津市有关单位在“八一”前后向驻津部队和休养员进行了慰问，并且组成检查组对市内各单位安置复员建设军人的情况进行了检查。
    沈阳市各界人民组成三个慰问团，在八月一日由沈阳市副市长陈先舟等率领，并带领演出队分赴驻沈阳部队各医院慰问伤病员。
    江西省人民委员会在“八一”前夕拨出了八十万元救济款帮助第二次国内革命战争时期的根据地瑞金、兴国、弋阳等三十三个县和井冈山上的烈属、军属和红军老战士们修建房屋。广西省人民委员会在“八一”节时派了四个电影队到边防地区去慰问边防部队。
