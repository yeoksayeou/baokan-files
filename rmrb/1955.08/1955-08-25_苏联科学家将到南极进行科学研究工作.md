Title: Soviet Scientists to Conduct Scientific Research in Antarctica

Author: 
Date: 1955-08-25
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, August 24th: TASS reports: A comprehensive scientific expedition organized by the Soviet Academy of Sciences is preparing to conduct geophysical research in the Antarctic region. This is part of the Soviet Union's participation in the Third International Geophysical Year.

According to the International Geophysical Year plan, the main scientific work will be carried out simultaneously by scientists from many countries around the world from July 1, 1957, to the end of 1958. Because Antarctica is very far from the Soviet Union and natural conditions are very harsh, organizing scientific research in Antarctica will encounter many difficulties. Therefore, the Soviet expedition will go to Antarctica in November this year to conduct preliminary exploration and prepare scientific bases.

The expedition is led by Somyev, a well-known Soviet polar researcher. The expedition will set sail on the "Ob," a 12,600-ton exploration ship capable of navigating through ice, and an auxiliary ship. The expedition is also equipped with an air force consisting of several transport planes and helicopters, and will carry tractors, tracked vehicles, and various state-of-the-art scientific instruments.

It is expected that the expedition will establish three scientific stations on the Knox Coast in eastern Antarctica and in the inland central region. The research work of these three scientific stations will be coordinated with marine observations and geophysical observations carried out by specialized ships on the Antarctic sea.

The expedition will conduct comprehensive scientific research on meteorology, geomagnetism, polar aurora, ionosphere, solar radiation, cosmic rays, glaciology, and other aspects. Meteorologists will specifically study the impact of the Antarctic continent, covered by ice, on the overall airflow on Earth to improve long-term forecasting methods. Scientists will also compile materials on the natural geography of Antarctica, the Antarctic ice sheet, geological characteristics, the history of the Antarctic region, and the biogeographic characteristics of Antarctica.

The Soviet expedition will cooperate with expeditions from the United States and other countries that have established scientific stations in Antarctica during its work.

The plan for Antarctic exploration will be finalized and approved at a special committee meeting on hosting the International Geophysical Year to be held in Brussels in September.

### 苏联科学家将到南极进行科学研究工作

1955-08-25
第4版()
专栏：

　　苏联科学家将到南极进行科学研究工作
    新华社二十四日讯　据塔斯社报道：苏联科学院组织的一个综合性科学考察队正在准备往南极地区进行地球物理学研究工作。这是苏联参加第三次国际地球物理学年度活动的一部分。
    根据国际地球物理学年度的计划，主要的科学工作将由世界许多国家的科学家在一九五七年七月一日起至一九五八年年底这一时期同时进行。由于南极距离苏联非常辽远，自然条件非常恶劣，组织南极的科学研究工作会遇到许多困难，所以苏联的考察队将在今年十一月就前往南极，以便进行初步的探查和准备科学基地。
    考察队以苏联著名的极地研究家索美夫为队长。考察队将乘坐一艘可以在冰块中航行的、排水量一万二千六百吨的“鄂毕号”探险船和一艘辅助船出海。考察队还配备有由几架运输机和直升飞机组成的航空队，并且将携带拖拉机、履带牵引汽车和各种最新式的科学仪器。
    预计考察队将在南极洲东部的诺克斯岸上和内陆中心地区分别建立三个科学站。这三个科学站的研究工作将同专门船只在南极海面上的海洋观测和地球物理观测配合进行。
    考察队将对气象学、地磁学、极地光、电离层、太阳辐射、宇宙线、冰河学等方面的问题进行综合的科学研究。气象学家将特别研究覆盖组冰层的南极大陆对地球上总的气流的影响，以便改善长期预测的方法。科学家们还要编写南极的自然地理、南极的冰层、地质的特性、南极区的历史、南极的生物地理特性等材料。
    苏联考察队在进行工作时，将同在南极洲建立有科学站的美国和其他国家的考察队合作。
    关于在南极考察的计划，将在九月间在布鲁塞尔举行的关于举办国际地球物理学年度的专门委员会会议上取得最后的同意和批准。新华社更正二十三日新闻稿“郑国禄抗议美方军用飞机侵入朝中方面上空进行敌对活动”稿（载二十三日本报第四版），第五段“非军事区北缘以北二百三十公尺”中“二百三十公尺”应为“二千三百公尺”。二十四日新闻稿“果阿——印度领土不可分割的一部分”稿（载二十四日本报第四版）第二段中，“面积共三千三百平方公里”，应为“面积共三千九百八十三平方公里”。
