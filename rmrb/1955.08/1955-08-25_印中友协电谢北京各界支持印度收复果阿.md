Title: Sino-Indian Friendship Association Sends Telegram Thanking Beijing for Supporting India's Recovery of Goa
Author:
Date: 1955-08-25
Page: 1

　　Xinhua News Agency, August 24th: Ding Xilin, President of the Sino-Indian Friendship Association, received a reply telegram on the 24th from Sundarlal, Chairman of the Indian-Chinese Friendship Association, Gyan Chand, Chairman of the National Council of the Indian-Chinese Friendship Association, and Panikkar, Chairman of the National Executive Committee of the Indian-Chinese Friendship Association. The reply telegram stated that the telegram sent by the people of all circles in Beijing supporting the Indian people's recovery of Goa to the Sino-Indian Friendship Association has been received. "The Indian-Chinese Friendship Association and the Indian people express our deep gratitude to you and your association, as well as to the people of all circles in Beijing, for the sympathy and support you have shown for India's efforts to liberate Goa."
    The telegram concluded by saying: India is deeply confident of success.

### 印中友协电谢北京各界支持印度收复果阿

1955-08-25
第1版()
专栏：

　　印中友协电谢北京各界支持印度收复果阿
    新华社二十四日讯　中印友好协会会长丁西林二十四日收到印度印中友好协会主席森德拉尔、印中友好协会全国理事会主席吉安·昌德和印中友好协会全国执行委员会主席潘尼迦的复电。复电说，北京各界人民支援印度人民收复果阿大会致印中友协的电报收到了。“印中友好协会和印度人民对你和贵会以及北京市各界人民对印度为解放果阿而作的努力所表示的同情和支持，表示我们深深的感激” 。
    电报最后说：印度深信必能成功。
