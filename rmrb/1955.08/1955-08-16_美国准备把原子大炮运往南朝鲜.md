Title: U.S. Prepares to Ship Atomic Cannons to South Korea
Author:
Date: 1955-08-16
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, 15th dispatch: London - The "Sunday Times" published a dispatch from Tokyo by Richard Hughes on the 14th, stating that the Chairman of the U.S. Joint Chiefs of Staff intends to transfer six atomic cannons to South Korea. These atomic cannons have recently been shipped to Okinawa.

The report said that this action would necessitate issuing a statement that the United Nations considers the provision in the Korean Armistice Agreement prohibiting the introduction of new weapons and troops to be invalid. The report revealed, however, that even among the sixteen countries that participated in the Korean War, "many countries strongly oppose abolishing this agreement."

### 美国准备把原子大炮运往南朝鲜

1955-08-16
第4版()
专栏：

    美国准备把原子大炮运往南朝鲜
    新华社十五日讯  伦敦消息：“星期日泰晤士报”十四日刊载理查德·休斯从东京发出的一篇电讯报道，美国参谋长联席会议主席打算把六门原子大炮调往南朝鲜。这些原子大炮最近已经运到冲绳。
    消息说，这个行动将使发表下列声明成为必要，即：联合国认为朝鲜停战协定中禁止运入新武器和军队的规定是无效的。消息透露，但是，即使在参加过侵朝战争的十六个国家中，也“有许多国家强烈反对废除这个协定”。
