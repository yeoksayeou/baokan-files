    Our Great Motherland
    Khotan carpets, produced in Xinjiang Province, are famous throughout the country. The People's Government has established a carpet factory in Khotan County to carry out planned production, and has provided guidance and assistance to Uyghur farmers engaged in sideline carpet weaving. Workers at the Khotan Carpet Factory are measuring this newly woven large carpet.

### 我们伟大的祖国（图片）

1955-08-12
第1版()
专栏：

    我们伟大的祖国
    新疆省出产的和阗毯，闻名全国。人民政府在和阗县建立了一座毯子厂，进行有计划的生产，并对从事织毯副业生产的维吾尔族农民进行了指导和扶助。和阗毯子厂的工人正在量这幅新织好的大地毯。
