    Our Great Motherland
    In the Eighth District, Second Township of Urumqi County, Xinjiang Province, over 90% of the more than 200 households are Kazakh herdsmen. Since 1952, the number of sheep and goats here has increased by more than 8,500. The herdsmen have also organized three year-round mutual aid teams and eight temporary mutual aid teams. Members of the mutual aid teams are feeding lambs.

### 我们伟大的祖国（图片）

1955-08-27
第1版()
专栏：

    我们伟大的祖国
    新疆省乌鲁木齐县第八区第二乡的二百多户居民，有百分之九十以上是哈萨克族牧民。从一九五二年以来，这里绵羊和山羊增加了八千五百多只。牧民们并组织了三个常年互助组和八个临时互助组。互助组的组员正在喂羊羔。
