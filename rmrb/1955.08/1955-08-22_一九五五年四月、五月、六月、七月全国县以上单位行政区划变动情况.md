Title: Changes in Administrative Divisions Above County Level Nationwide in April, May, June, and July 1955
Author:
Date: 1955-08-22
Page: 2

Xinhua News Agency

Changes in Administrative Divisions Above County Level Nationwide in April, May, June, and July 1955

    Hebei Province: The Ongniud Banner Mongolian Autonomous Region, equivalent to a county level, was changed to Ongniud Mongolian Autonomous Banner.
    Qinghai Province: The Yushu Tibetan Autonomous Region, equivalent to a prefecture level, was changed to Yushu Tibetan Autonomous Prefecture; the Haibei Tibetan Autonomous Region was changed to Haibei Tibetan Autonomous Prefecture; the Hainan Tibetan Autonomous Region was changed to Hainan Tibetan Autonomous Prefecture; the Haixi Mongolian-Tibetan-Kazak Autonomous Region was changed to Haixi Mongolian-Tibetan-Kazak Autonomous Prefecture; the Huangnan Tibetan Autonomous Region was changed to Huangnan Tibetan Autonomous Prefecture; the Guoluo Tibetan Autonomous Region was changed to Guoluo Tibetan Autonomous Prefecture. The Huzu County Tu Autonomous Region, equivalent to a county level, was changed to Huzu Tu Autonomous County; the Hualong County Hui Autonomous Region was changed to Hualong Hui Autonomous County; the Xunhua County Salar Autonomous Region was changed to Xunhua Salar Autonomous County; the Henan Mongolian Autonomous Region was changed to Henan Mongolian Autonomous County; the Yuanyi County Hui Autonomous Region was changed to Yuanyi Hui Autonomous County; the Guoluo Tibetan Autonomous Prefecture successively established Jiuzhi and Banma counties between March and April 1955.
    Xinjiang Province: The Bayingol Mongolian Autonomous Region, equivalent to a prefecture level, was changed to Bayingol Mongolian Autonomous Prefecture; the Bortala Mongolian Autonomous Region was changed to Bortala Mongolian Autonomous Prefecture; the Kizilsu Kirghiz Autonomous Region was changed to Kizilsu Kirghiz Autonomous Prefecture; the Changji Hui Autonomous Region was changed to Changji Hui Autonomous Prefecture. The Yanqi Hui Autonomous Region, equivalent to a county level, was changed to Yanqi Hui Autonomous County; the Chabuchaer Xibe Autonomous Region was changed to Chabuchaer Xibe Autonomous County; the Mulei Kazak Autonomous Region was changed to Mulei Kazak Autonomous County; the Tashkurgan Tajik Autonomous Region was changed to Tashkurgan Tajik Autonomous County; the Hoboksar Mongolian Autonomous Region was changed to Hoboksar Mongolian Autonomous County; the Barkol Kazak Autonomous Region was changed to Barkol Kazak Autonomous County.
    Anhui Province: Hudong County was renamed Zongyang County.
    Hunan Province: The Xiangxi Miao Autonomous Region, equivalent to a prefecture level, was changed to Xiangxi Miao Autonomous Prefecture.
    Guangdong Province: The Qinzhou Special District of Guangxi Province (including Qin County, Hepu, Lingshan, Fangcheng, and Pubei counties and Beihai City) was placed under the leadership of Guangdong Province from July 1, 1955. The Liannan Yao Autonomous Region, equivalent to a county level, was changed to Liannan Yao Autonomous County.
    Yunnan Province: HeKou City was abolished, and the original jurisdiction was changed to HeKou County. Malipo City was abolished, and the original jurisdiction was changed to Malipo County. Lancang County was abolished, and the original jurisdiction (i.e., the Ximeng mountainous area) was temporarily entrusted to the Lancang Lahu Autonomous Region, equivalent to a county level, for leadership.
    Xikang Province: The Xikang Province Tibetan Autonomous Region, equivalent to a prefecture level, was changed to Xikang Province Tibetan Autonomous Prefecture. (Xinhua News Agency)

### 一九五五年四月、五月、六月、七月全国县以上单位行政区划变动情况

1955-08-22
第2版()
专栏：

　　一九五五年四月、五月、六月、七月全国县以上单位行政区划变动情况
    热河省：相当县级的翁牛特旗蒙古族自治区改为翁牛特蒙古族自治旗。
    青海省：相当专区级的玉树藏族自治区改为玉树藏族自治州；海北藏族自治区改为海北藏族自治州；海南藏族自治区改为海南藏族自治州；海西蒙藏哈萨克族自治区改为海西蒙藏哈萨克族自治州；黄南藏族自治区改为黄南藏族自治州；果洛藏族自治区改为果洛藏族自治州。相当县级的互助县土族自治区改为互助土族自治县；化隆县回族自治区改为化隆回族自治县；循化县撒拉族自治区改为循化撒拉族自治县；河南蒙族自治区改为河南蒙族自治县；亹源县回族自治区改为亹源回族自治县；果洛藏族自治州于一九五五年三、四两月间先后成立久治、班玛两县。
    新疆省：相当专区级的巴音郭楞蒙古族自治区改为巴音郭楞蒙古族自治州；博尔塔拉蒙古族自治区改为博尔塔拉蒙古族自治州；克孜勒苏柯尔克孜族自治区改为克孜勒苏柯尔克孜族自治州；昌吉回族自治区改为昌吉回族自治州。相当县级的焉耆回族自治区改为焉耆回族自治县；察布查尔锡伯族自治区改为察布查尔锡伯族自治县；木垒哈萨克族自治区改为木垒哈萨克族自治县；塔什库尔干塔吉克族自治区改为塔什库尔干塔吉克族自治县；和布克赛尔蒙古族自治区改为和布克赛尔蒙古族自治县；巴里坤哈萨克族自治区改为巴里坤哈萨克族自治县。
    安徽省：湖东县改名为枞阳县。
    湖南省：相当专区级的湘西苗族自治区改为湘西苗族自治州。
    广东省：广西省的钦州专区（包括钦县、合浦、灵山、防城、浦北五县和北海市）自一九五五年七月一日起划归广东省领导。相当县级的连南瑶族自治区改为连南瑶族自治县。
    云南省：撤销河口市，将原辖区改设为河口县。撒销麻栗坡市，将原辖区改设为麻栗坡县。撤销澜沧县，将原辖区（即西盟山区）暂委托相当县级的澜沧拉祜族自治区领导。
    西康省：相当专区级的西康省藏族自治区改为西康省藏族自治州。　（新华社）
