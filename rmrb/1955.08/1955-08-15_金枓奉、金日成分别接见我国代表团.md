Title: Kim Tu-bong and Kim Il-sung Meet with Chinese Delegation Separately
Author:
Date: 1955-08-15
Page: 1

    Xinhua News Agency, Pyongyang, August 14th: Kim Tu-bong, Chairman of the Standing Committee of the Supreme People's Assembly of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea, met with Vice Chairman Zhu De and members of the People's Republic of China delegation Li Jishen, Teng Daiyuan, Ji Pengfei, and Pan Zili at 12:00 noon on August 14th.
    Present at the meeting were: Kim Ung-ki and Li Guk-ro, Vice-Chairmen of the Standing Committee of the Supreme People's Assembly, Kang Ryang-uk, Secretary-General, Ha Ang-cheon, Director of the Social Science Department of the Central Committee of the Korean Workers' Party, and Li Sin-pal, Vice Minister of Foreign Affairs.
    Xinhua News Agency, Pyongyang, August 14th: Marshal Kim Il-sung, Premier of the Cabinet of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea, met with Zhu De, head of the People's Republic of China delegation, and delegation members Li Jishen, Teng Daiyuan, Ji Pengfei, and Pan Zili at 8:30 p.m. on August 13th.
    Present at the meeting were: Park Chang-ok, Kim Il, Choi Chang-ik, and Jung Il-ryong, Vice Premiers of the Korean Cabinet, Nam Il, Minister of Foreign Affairs, Ha Ang-cheon, Director of the Social Science Department of the Central Committee of the Korean Workers' Party, and Li Sin-pal, Vice Minister of Foreign Affairs.
    Xinhua News Agency, Pyongyang, August 14th: According to the Korean Central News Agency, Marshal Kim Il-sung, Premier of the Cabinet of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea, met with foreign diplomatic representatives stationed in Korea on August 13th to congratulate him on the 10th anniversary of the liberation of Korea. Minister of Foreign Affairs Nam Il was present at the meeting.
    On the same day, foreign diplomatic representatives stationed in Korea paid visits to Kim Tu-bong, Chairman of the Standing Committee of the Supreme People's Assembly of Korea, and Minister of Foreign Affairs Nam Il, to extend their congratulations.

### 金枓奉、金日成分别接见我国代表团

1955-08-15
第1版()
专栏：

    金枓奉、金日成分别接见我国代表团
    新华社平壤十四日电  朝鲜民主主义人民共和国最高人民会议常任委员会委员长金枓奉在十四日中午十二时接见朱德副主席和他率领的中华人民共和国代表团团员李济深、滕代远、姬鹏飞、潘自力。
    接见时在座的有：最高人民会议常任委员会副委员长金应基、李克鲁，书记长康良煜，朝鲜劳动党中央委员会社会科学部长河仰天，外务省副相李新八。
    新华社平壤十四日电  朝鲜民主主义人民共和国内阁首相金日成元帅在十三日晚八时三十分接见中华人民共和国代表团团长朱德和代表团团员李济深、滕代远、姬鹏飞、潘自力。
    接见时在座的有：朝鲜内阁副首相朴昌玉、金一、崔昌益、郑一龙，外务相南日，朝鲜劳动党中央委员会社会科学部长河仰天，外务省副相李新八。
    新华社平壤十四日电  据朝鲜中央通讯社讯：朝鲜民主主义人民共和国内阁首相金日成元帅在八月十三日接见前来向他祝贺朝鲜解放十周年纪念的各国驻朝鲜外交代表。接见时在座的有外务相南日。
    同日，各国驻朝鲜外交代表拜会了朝鲜最高人民会议常任委员会委员长金枓奉和外务相南日，向他们致贺。
