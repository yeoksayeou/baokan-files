Title: First Drawing Ceremony for the 1954 National Bonds Held in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1955-08-11
Page: 2

The first drawing ceremony for the repayment of principal for the 1954 National Economic Construction Bonds was held in Beijing on the afternoon of August 10th. Representatives from central government agencies, the All-China Federation of Trade Unions, the General Political Department of the Chinese People's Liberation Army, the All-China Federation of Industry and Commerce, farmers and street residents of Beijing, as well as the Federations of Industry and Commerce of the eight major cities of Beijing, Tianjin, Shanghai, Shenyang, Wuhan, Guangzhou, Chongqing, and Xi'an were all delegated to participate.

The drawing was conducted by Zhou Daidan, representative of the All-China Federation of Trade Unions, and Huang Jieran, representative of the All-China Federation of Industry and Commerce. Ge Zhida, representative of the Fifth Office of the State Council, and Pan Zhenya, representative of the Ministry of Supervision, supervised the drawing.

Bei Zhongxuan, Assistant Minister of Finance and Chairman of the meeting, addressed the assembly, stating that according to the regulations of the 1954 National Economic Construction Bonds, interest on these bonds would be paid once a year on September 30th, starting from 1955. The principal would be repaid in eight installments over eight years, i.e., a drawing for repayment of principal would be held once a year on September 30th, starting from 1955. The first and second drawings would each repay 5% of the total amount, the third and fourth drawings would each repay 10% of the total amount, the fifth and sixth drawings would each repay 15% of the total amount, the seventh drawing would repay 20% of the total amount, and the eighth drawing would repay 20% of the total amount. To ensure that bondholders in various regions have sufficient time to redeem their principal and interest starting from September 30th, the annual drawing date has been set in advance for August 10th. Bei Zhongxuan also said that the issuance of the 1954 National Economic Construction Bonds received enthusiastic support from the people of the whole country. The actual sales amount of this bond exceeded the original issuance amount.

Li Zaiwen, representative of the All-China Federation of Trade Unions, Luo Yijing, representative of the General Political Department of the Chinese People's Liberation Army, Wang Shilong, farmer representative from Beijing, and Huang Changshui, Ling Qijun, Xiang Shuxiang, and Wu Jinhang, representatives from the industrial and commercial sectors, all spoke at the meeting, expressing their satisfaction with the issuance of the 1954 National Economic Construction Bonds and the timely drawing for repayment of principal and interest.

(Xinhua News Agency)

### 一九五四年公债首次还本抽签大会在京举行

1955-08-11
第2版()
专栏：

    一九五四年公债首次还本抽签大会在京举行
    一九五四年国家经济建设公债第一次还本抽签大会八月十日下午在北京举行。中央一级机关、中华全国总工会、中国人民解放军总政治部、中华全国工商业联合会、北京市农民和街道居民以及北京、天津、上海、沈阳、武汉、广州、重庆、西安八大城市工商业联合会，都推派有代表参加。
    大会由中华全国总工会代表周待旦、中华全国工商业联合会代表黄玠然执行抽签，由国务院第五办公室代表葛致达、监察部代表潘震亚监督抽签。
    大会主席、财政部部长助理贝仲选向大会致词说，按照一九五四年国家经济建设公债条例规定，本公债自一九五五年起，每年九月三十日付息一次；本金分八年作八次偿还，即自一九五五年起每年九月三十日抽签还本一次，第一、二次各抽还总额的百分之五，第三、四次各抽还总额的百分之十，第五、六次各抽还总额的百分之十五，第七次抽还总额的百分之二十，第八次还总额的百分之二十。为了照顾各地持有中签债券者有充分时间能在九月三十日起兑付本息，特把每年的抽签时间定在八月十日提前举行。贝仲选还说，一九五四年国家经济建设公债发行时，得到了全国人民的热烈支持。这期公债的实际推销额超过了原定发行额。
    中华全国总工会代表栗再温、中国人民解放军总政治部代表罗亦经、北京市农民代表王世龙和工商界代表黄长水、凌其峻、项叔翔、吴晋航等都在会上讲了话，对一九五四年国家经济建设公债的发行情况和按时抽签还本付息表示满意。
                                     （新华社）
