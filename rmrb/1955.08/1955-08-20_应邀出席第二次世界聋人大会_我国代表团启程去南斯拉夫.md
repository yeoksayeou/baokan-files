Title: Invited to Attend the Second World Congress of the Deaf, Our Country's Delegation Departs for Yugoslavia
Author: 
Date: 1955-08-20
Page: 4

    Xinhua News Agency, August 19th: At the invitation of the World Federation of the Deaf, the Preparatory Committee of the China Welfare Association for the Deaf and Mute has sent a delegation to attend the Second World Congress of the Deaf to be held in Zagreb, Yugoslavia.
    The delegation, led by head Lin Shixiao and deputy head Wu Yansheng, departed from Beijing by plane on the 17th.

### 应邀出席第二次世界聋人大会  我国代表团启程去南斯拉夫

1955-08-20
第4版()
专栏：

    应邀出席第二次世界聋人大会
    我国代表团启程去南斯拉夫
    新华社十九日讯　中国聋哑人福利会筹备委员会应世界聋哑人联合会的邀请，派了一个代表团去参加即将在南斯拉夫扎格雷布召开的第二次世界聋人大会。
    代表团由团长林士笑、副团长吴燕生率领，在十七日乘飞机自北京启程。
