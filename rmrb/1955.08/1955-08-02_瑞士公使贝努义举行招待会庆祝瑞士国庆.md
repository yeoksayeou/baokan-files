Title: Swiss Envoy Benoît Holds Reception to Celebrate Swiss National Day
Author:
Date: 1955-08-02
Page: 1
    Xinhua News Agency, August 1st. Swiss Envoy Extraordinary and Plenipotentiary to China, Mr. Benoît, held a reception on the afternoon of the first to celebrate Swiss National Day.

    Among those invited to attend the reception were Premier of the State Council of the People's Republic of China Zhou Enlai, Vice Premier Chen Yi, Vice Minister of Foreign Affairs Zhang Hanfu, Vice Minister of Foreign Trade Lei Renmin, Acting Minister of Culture Qian Junrui, Vice Minister of Culture Ding Xilin, Chief of General Staff of the Chinese People's Liberation Army Su Yu, Vice Mayor of Beijing Wu Han, and others.

    Heads of diplomatic missions and diplomatic officials of various countries in China were also invited to attend the reception.

    At the reception, Envoy Benoît first gave a speech thanking everyone for attending the reception celebrating Swiss National Day. He proposed a toast to the health of Chairman Mao Zedong of the People's Republic of China and to the happiness of the entire Chinese people.

    Premier Zhou Enlai then gave a speech. He said that today is the National Day of the Swiss Confederation. On behalf of the Government of the People's Republic of China and the Chinese people, I extend warm congratulations. Premier Zhou Enlai suggested a toast to the prosperity and happiness of the peace-loving Swiss people, to the further development of friendship between China and Switzerland, to lasting and consolidated world peace, and to the health of Mr. Petitpierre, President of the Swiss Confederation and Minister of Political Affairs.

    Further Dispatch: Premier of the State Council of the People's Republic of China, Zhou Enlai, sent a telegram to the President of the Swiss Confederation, Petitpierre, on July 31st, congratulating him on the Swiss National Day.

    Hanoi Holds Ceremony for the Inauguration of Joint China-Vietnam Railway Transportation
    Xinhua News Agency, Hanoi, August 1st. At 6:00 AM on the first, a ceremony was held at the Hanoi Railway Station in the capital of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam to inaugurate joint railway transportation between Vietnam and China.

    Attending the ceremony were Minister of Transport and Public Works of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam Tran Dang Khoa, Director of the General Railway Administration Nguyen Huu Mai, Chairman of the Hanoi Municipal Administrative Committee Tran Duy Hung, and others.

    Also attending was Xie Shuangqiu, Chargé d'Affaires ad interim of the Embassy of the People's Republic of China in the Democratic Republic of Vietnam.

    Minister of Transport and Public Works of Vietnam, Tran Dang Khoa, said in his speech that the railway joint transportation agreement signed by the governments of Vietnam and China in May 1955 has now been realized. The joint railway transportation between Vietnam and China connects Vietnam with fraternal countries and will further expand and develop economic and cultural exchanges between Vietnam and fraternal countries.

    Chargé d'Affaires ad interim of the Chinese Embassy, Xie Shuangqiu, delivered a speech congratulating the inauguration of joint railway transportation between China and Vietnam.

    At 6:40, after Tran Dang Khoa cut the ribbon, a decorated train with the national flags of Vietnam and China hanging on it slowly departed from Hanoi Station amid the music played by the band.

### 瑞士公使贝努义举行招待会庆祝瑞士国庆

1955-08-02
第1版()
专栏：

    瑞士公使贝努义举行招待会庆祝瑞士国庆
    新华社一日讯　瑞士驻我国特命全权公使贝努义在一日下午举行招待会，庆祝瑞士联邦国庆日。
    应邀出席招待会的有中华人民共和国国务院总理周恩来、副总理陈毅，外交部副部长章汉夫，对外贸易部副部长雷任民，文化部代理部长钱俊瑞、副部长丁西林，中国人民解放军总参谋长粟裕，北京市副市长吴晗等。
    各国驻华使节和外交官员也应邀出席了招待会。
    招待会上，贝努义公使首先致词感谢大家光临庆祝瑞士联邦国庆日的招待会。他提议为中华人民共和国主席毛泽东的健康，为全中国人民的幸福干杯。
    周恩来总理接着致词。他说，今天是瑞士联邦的国庆纪念日。我代表中华人民共和国政府和中国人民致热烈的祝贺。周恩来总理建议为爱好和平的瑞士人民的繁荣和幸福，为中瑞两国友谊的进一步发展，为持久的和巩固的世界和平，为瑞士联邦主席兼政治部部长彼蒂彼爱阁下的健康干杯。
    又讯：中华人民共和国国务院总理周恩来在七月三十一日曾致电瑞士联邦主席彼蒂彼爱，祝贺瑞士联邦国庆纪念日。河内举行越中两国铁路联运通车典礼
    新华社河内一日电　一日晨六时，在越南民主共和国首都河内车站，举行了越、中两国间铁路联运通车典礼。
    出席典礼的有越南民主共和国交通公政部部长陈登科、铁路总局局长阮友梅、河内市行政委员会主席陈维兴等。
    出席的还有中华人民共和国驻越南民主共和国大使馆临时代办谢爽秋。
    越南交通公政部部长陈登科讲话说，一九五五年五月越中两国政府签订的铁路联运协定，今天已经实现了。越中两国间铁路的联运，使越南和各兄弟国家联结在一起，将愈加扩大和发展越南和各兄弟国家间的经济和文化交流。
    中国大使馆临时代办谢爽秋致词，祝贺中越两国间铁路联运通车。
    六时四十分，陈登科剪彩后，一列悬挂越、中两国国旗的彩车，在乐队奏起的音乐声中徐徐驶离河内车站。
