Title: Our Delegation Visits Atomic Bomb Victims in Hiroshima and Conducts Friendly Exchanges with Various Circles in Tokyo and Other Places
Author:
Date: 1955-08-21
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Hiroshima, August 20th: The entire Chinese delegation attending the World Conference Against Atomic and Hydrogen Bombs, along with Hai Frank, Vice Chairman of the Free German Trade Union Federation, arrived in Hiroshima from Tokyo on the afternoon of the 19th.

They immediately went to the Peace Memorial Park and laid flowers at the memorial tower for the atomic bomb victims. Subsequently, they visited the Japanese Red Cross Hospital to express their sympathy to the atomic disease patients and held a symposium with more than twenty atomic bomb survivors. The survivors recounted the tragic circumstances of the atomic bombing at the symposium and expressed their ardent desire for peace. Liu Ningyi, head of the Chinese delegation, stated at the symposium that the Chinese people are willing to stand together with all peace-loving people in the world to resolutely oppose the use, manufacture, and stockpiling of atomic weapons.

On the same evening, the Chinese delegation attended a welcome rally held at the Hiroshima Public Hall. Attending the rally were Binai Nobuzo, Chairman of the Hiroshima Preparatory Committee for the World Conference Against Atomic and Hydrogen Bombs, Moritaki Ichiro, Secretary-General, Takano Minoru, Permanent Secretary of the General Council of Trade Unions of Japan, Ohara Toru, Chairman of the Hiroshima Prefectural Trade Union Council, Sasaki Sen, Deputy Mayor of Hiroshima, and representatives from all walks of life, as well as approximately 1,300 Hiroshima citizens.

Xinhua News Agency, Tokyo, August 19th: Liu Ningyi, head of the Chinese delegation attending the World Conference Against Atomic and Hydrogen Bombs and Vice Chairman of the All-China Federation of Trade Unions, visited the headquarters of the Japan National Metalworkers' Union, the All-Japan Postal Workers' Union, and the All-Japan Telecommunication Workers' Union on the afternoon of the 18th, along with trade union representatives from Poland, East Germany, Australia, and other countries. They then traveled from Tokyo to Yokohama to visit the headquarters of the Kanagawa Regional Council of Trade Unions and attended a welcome dinner party hosted by it.

Cheng Fangwu, a member of the Chinese delegation, along with representatives from the Soviet Union and Poland, attended a welcome reception hosted by the Science Council of Japan on the afternoon of the 18th and exchanged views with Japanese scholars on promoting academic exchanges and friendship.

On the morning of the 18th, Cheng Fangwu also attended a welcome reception hosted by the Japan Federation of Ministry of Education Workers' Unions. At the meeting, Cheng Fangwu introduced the lives and activities of Chinese educators, as well as the educational policies and systems of New China.

Xie Bingxin, a member of the Chinese delegation, along with Sutova, a member of the Soviet delegation, attended a welcome reception hosted by the Japan Federation of Women's Organizations on the afternoon of the 18th.

Xinhua News Agency, Tokyo, August 18th: Zhao Puchu, a member of the Chinese delegation attending the World Conference Against Atomic and Hydrogen Bombs and Vice President of the Buddhist Association of China, attended a welcome reception hosted by the All-Japan Buddhist Association on the afternoon of the 17th.

Attending the welcome reception were approximately one hundred prominent figures from the Japanese Buddhist community, including Shiio Benkyo, Vice Chairman of the All-Japan Buddhist Association, Tomomatsu Entei, Secretary-General, Takakai Ryusen, Abbot of the Soto Sect, Kurimoto Toshimichi, Executive Director of the Tokyo Buddhist Association, Shigemaga Sen, "Rotational Abbot" of Asakusa Honganji Temple, Otani Eijun, Chairman of the Executive Committee for the Memorial Service for Chinese Prisoners of War, and Nakanoukyo Atsushi, representative of the Japan-China Buddhist Exchange Conference.

The welcome reception began with a solemn Buddhist ceremony. Zhao Puchu read a letter from the Buddhist Association of China to the All-Japan Buddhist Association at the meeting. The letter wished the Japanese people and all the people of the world a peaceful and happy life, forever free from the threat of war. The letter stated, "Chinese Buddhists are willing to work together with Japanese Buddhists, the Japanese people, and all the people of the world to jointly strive for the cause of human peace."

Zhao Puchu then presented a statue of Guanyin Bodhisattva, sent by the Buddhist Association of China, to Shiio Benkyo, Vice Chairman of the All-Japan Buddhist Association.

Tomomatsu Entei, Secretary-General of the All-Japan Buddhist Association, gave a thank-you speech, saying: We are willing to enhance the friendship and connection between the Chinese and Japanese Buddhist communities under the symbol of this Guanyin Bodhisattva statue.

A banquet followed. The guests exchanged views on Sino-Japanese Buddhist exchanges in a friendly and cordial atmosphere.

### 我国代表团到广岛慰问原子弹被害者　并在东京等地和各界进行友好交谈

1955-08-21
第4版()
专栏：

　　我国代表团到广岛慰问原子弹被害者
　　并在东京等地和各界进行友好交谈
    新华社二十日讯　广岛消息：出席禁止原子弹和氢弹世界大会的中国代表团全体人员，十九日下午同自由德国工会联合会副主席海·弗兰克一起，从东京到达广岛。
    他们随即到和平纪念公园，在原子弹牺牲者纪念塔前献花。接着，他们又到日本红十字会病院慰问了原子病患者，并且同二十多个原子弹受害者举行了座谈会。受害者们在座谈会上叙述了他们遭受原子弹轰炸的悲惨情况，并且表示了维护和平的热望。中国代表团团长刘宁一在座谈会上表示，中国人民愿与世界上一切爱好和平的人们站在一起，坚决反对使用、制造和储藏原子武器。
    当天晚上，中国代表团出席了在广岛公会堂举行的欢迎大会。参加大会的有禁止原子弹和氢弹世界大会广岛筹备会会长滨井信三、秘书长森泷市郎，日本工会总评议会常任干事高野实，广岛县工会会议主席大原亨，广岛市副市长佐佐木铣等各界代表以及广岛市民约一千三百多人。
    新华社十九日讯　东京消息：出席禁止原子弹和氢弹世界大会的中国代表团团长、中华全国总工会副主席刘宁一，十八日下午同波兰、民主德国、澳大利亚等国工会代表一起，到日本全国金属工会、全递信从业员工会和全国电气通信从业员工会的总部作了友好的访问。然后，他们从东京到横滨去访问了神奈川地方工会评议会总部并且出席了它主办的欢迎晚餐会。
    中国代表团团员成仿吾和苏联、波兰的代表，十八日下午出席了日本学术会议主办的欢迎会，同日本的学者们就促进学术交流和友好的问题进行了交谈。
    十八日上午，成仿吾还曾出席日本文部省职员工会主办的欢迎会。成仿吾在会上介绍了中国教育工作者的生活和活动情况以及新中国的教育方针和教育制度。
    中国代表团团员谢冰心，十八日下午同苏联代表团团员苏托娃一起，出席了全日本妇女团体联合会主办的欢迎会。
    新华社十八日讯　东京消息：出席禁止原子弹和氢弹世界大会的中国代表团团员、中国佛教协会副会长赵朴初，十七日下午出席了全日本佛教会主办的欢迎会。
    参加欢迎会的有全日本佛教会副会长椎尾辩匡、秘书长友松圆谛，曹洞宗管长正主教高阶珑仙，东京佛教团常务理事栗本俊道，浅草本愿寺“轮番”重永潜，中国俘虏殉难者追悼执行委员会委员长大谷莹润，日中佛教交流恳谈会代表中能教笃等日本佛教界著名人士约一百人。
    欢迎会首先举行了隆重的佛教仪式。赵朴初在会上宣读了中国佛教协会给全日本佛教会的一封信。这封信祝愿日本人民以及全世界人民获得和平幸福的生活，永远脱离战争的威胁。信中说，“中国佛教徒愿与日本佛教徒、日本人民以及全世界人民一道，为人类和平事业共同努力”。
    赵朴初接着将中国佛教协会送的观音菩萨圣像一尊交给全日本佛教会副会长椎尾辩匡。
    全日本佛教会秘书长友松圆谛致谢词说：我们愿在这个观音菩萨圣像的象征之下，增进中日佛教界的友谊和联系。
    接着举行了宴会。宾主在友好亲切的空气中，就中日佛教交流问题进行了交谈。
