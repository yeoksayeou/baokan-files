Title: Yugoslav Parliamentary Delegation to Visit the Soviet Union
Author:
Date: 1955-08-01
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, July 27th. According to TASS, in order to further enhance the friendly relations between the Soviet Union and Yugoslavia, the Chairman of the Soviet of the Union of the Supreme Soviet of the USSR, V.V. Kuznetsov, and the Chairman of the Soviet of Nationalities, J.E. Kalnberzins, on behalf of the Supreme Soviet of the USSR, sent a telegram to the Chairman of the Federal People's Assembly of the Federal People's Republic of Yugoslavia, Pijade, inviting Yugoslavia to send a parliamentary delegation to visit the Soviet Union.

In his reply, Pijade said that the Federal People's Assembly of the Federal People's Republic of Yugoslavia, at a joint session of the two houses, had appointed a delegation to visit the Soviet Union. The delegation will arrive in the Soviet Union on August 1st.

### 南斯拉夫议会代表团将访苏

1955-08-01
第4版()
专栏：

    南斯拉夫议会代表团将访苏
    新华社二十八日讯　据塔斯社莫斯科二十七日讯：为了进一步增进苏联和南斯拉夫之间的友好关系，苏联最高苏维埃联盟院主席沃尔科夫和民族院主席拉齐斯曾代表苏联最高苏维埃致电南斯拉夫联邦人民共和国联邦人民议会主席皮雅杰，邀请南斯拉夫派议会代表团访问苏联。
    皮雅杰在复信中说，南斯拉夫联邦人民共和国联邦人民议会在两院联席会议上任命了一个访问苏联的代表团。代表团将在八月一日到达苏联。
