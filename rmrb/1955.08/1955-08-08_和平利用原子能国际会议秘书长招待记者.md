Title: Secretary-General of the International Conference on the Peaceful Uses of Atomic Energy Holds Reception for Journalists
Author: 
Date: 1955-08-08
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, Geneva: Professor Whitman (USA), Secretary-General of the International Conference on the Peaceful Uses of Atomic Energy, held a reception for journalists on the 6th at the Palais des Nations in Geneva, according to TASS.

Whitman said that the conference was convened in accordance with the resolution of the Ninth Session of the United Nations General Assembly. The United Nations Advisory Committee, with representatives from the United States, the Soviet Union, the United Kingdom, France, Canada, and India, prepared and organized the conference, which will open on August 8th. All the work of this committee was carried out in a spirit of cooperation and received continuous and strong support from all countries participating in the conference.

He said that the basic proposition of this conference is: "Atomic energy serves mankind."

Professor Vavilov (Soviet Union), Assistant Secretary-General of the conference, said that the scope of participation in the conference exceeded the expectations of all those involved in the preparatory meetings. There will be 1,260 delegates and 800 observers participating in the conference. Scientists from various countries will present more than 1,500 works as scientific materials for the conference. During the conference, exhibitions will be held inside and outside the Palais des Nations where the conference is being held. In addition to bringing exhibits, many delegations also brought scientific films. For example, the Soviet delegation will show a film of the Soviet atomic energy power station.

### 和平利用原子能国际会议秘书长招待记者

1955-08-08
第1版()
专栏：

    和平利用原子能国际会议秘书长招待记者
    新华社七日讯　据塔斯社日内瓦讯：和平利用原子能国际会议秘书长惠特曼教授（美国）六日在日内瓦国联大厦举行记者招待会。
    惠特曼说，会议是根据联合国大会第九届会议的决议召开的。有美国、苏联、英国、法国、加拿大和印度等国代表参加的联合国咨询委员会，为将在八月八日开幕的会议进行了筹备和组织工作。这个委员会的全部工作是在合作的精神中进行的，并且受到了参加会议各国不断的大力支持。
    他说，这次会议的基本主张是：“原子能为人类服务”。
    会议助理秘书长瓦维洛夫教授（苏联）说，参加这次会议的人的范围的广泛，超过了所有筹备会议的人的预料。参加会议的将有一千二百六十名代表和八百名观察员。各国的科学家将提出一千五百多件著作作为会议的科学材料。在会议期间，在举行会议的国联大厦内和在大厦以外的地方都将举行展览会。许多代表团除了带来展览品以外，还带来了科学影片。譬如，苏联代表团就将放映苏联原子能发电站的影片。
