Title: Representatives from China, Soviet Union, Poland, India and Four Other Nations Attend Nagasaki Peace Congress
Author:
Date: 1955-08-12
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, Nagasaki, August 11th: The Chinese delegation attending the World Congress Against Atomic and Hydrogen Bombs arrived in Nagasaki, which was bombed by the second atomic bomb, from Tokyo on the afternoon of the 10th and attended the Nagasaki Atomic Bombing Tenth Anniversary Peace Congress.

Attending the conference were representatives from the Soviet Union, Poland, India, Chile, North Korea, Australia and six other countries, as well as Japan-China Friendship Association President Matsumoto Jiichiro, Left-wing Socialist Party Councilor Yoshida Hosei, Nagasaki City Mayor Tagawa Tsutomu, Nagasaki Prefecture Council of Trade Unions Chairman Kikuchi, and representatives of various groups and citizens of Nagasaki, totaling more than 3,000 people.

Liu Ningyi, head of the Chinese delegation, and representatives from various countries spoke at the meeting. These speeches aroused thunderous applause from the audience.

The congress adopted resolutions such as "Do not manufacture or use atomic and hydrogen bombs," "No war, all issues should be resolved through consultation," and "Do not bring atomic cannons to Japan."

The congress closed with the entire audience singing "Don't Bomb with Atomic Bombs."

### 中苏波印等七国代表出席长崎和平大会

1955-08-12
第1版()
专栏：

    中苏波印等七国代表出席长崎和平大会
    新华社十一日讯　长崎消息：出席禁止原子弹和氢弹世界大会的中国代表团十日下午从东京到达遭受第二颗原子弹轰炸的长崎，出席了长崎遭受原子弹轰炸十周年纪念和平大会。
    出席大会的还有苏联、波兰、印度、智利、朝鲜、澳大利亚等六个国家的代表和日中友好协会会长松本治一郎、左派社会党参议员吉田法晴、长崎市市长田川务、以及长崎县工会评议会主席菊池等各团体代表和长崎市民共三千多人。
    中国代表团团长刘宁一和各国代表在大会上讲话。这些讲话都引起了全场如雷的掌声。
    大会通过了“不要制造或使用原子弹和氢弹”、“不要战争，一切问题都用协商来解决”、“不要把原子炮搬到日本来”等决议。
    大会在全场合唱“不准原子弹轰炸”的歌声里闭幕。在西藏的全国人民代表大会代表发表谈话
