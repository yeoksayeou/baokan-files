Title: Chinese Delegation Returns to Beijing from North Korea; Vice Chairman Zhu De Cables Premier Kim Il-sung from Sinuiju to Express Gratitude
Author:
Date: 1955-08-26
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, August 25th -- The delegation of the People's Republic of China, invited by the government of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea to attend the celebration of the 10th anniversary of the Great Soviet Army's liberation of Korea on "August 15th," returned to Beijing this afternoon.

Xinhua News Agency, Sinuiju, August 24th -- The delegation of the People's Republic of China, led by Vice Chairman Zhu De, was invited by the government of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea to attend the celebration of the 10th anniversary of the liberation of Korea and to visit various parts of Korea. It returned to China by special train from Sinuiju at 7:30 PM on the 24th.

Greeting the delegation at Sinuiju Station were Pak Chong-ae, Vice Chairman of the Central Committee of the Workers' Party of Korea; Han Jon-chong, Chairman of the People's Committee of North Pyongan Province, So Chun-sik, Chairman of the North Pyongan Provincial Committee of the Workers' Party of Korea, and officials of Sinuiju, as well as more than 3,000 working people of Sinuiju.

Marshal Choe Yong-gon, Vice Premier of the Cabinet of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea, Ha Ang-cheon, Minister of Social Sciences of the Central Committee of the Workers' Party of Korea, and Pan Zili, Chinese Ambassador to Korea, who accompanied Vice Chairman Zhu De to the Korean border, also came to the station to see them off.

At the station, Vice Chairman Zhu De, accompanied by Vice Chairman Pak Chong-ae and Marshal Choe Yong-gon, reviewed the guard of honor. After the review, Vice Chairman Zhu De and members of the delegation received flowers from the children of Sinuiju. At this moment, stormy applause and cheers resounded at the station.

Vice Chairman Zhu De and the members of the delegation repeatedly shook hands cordially with the well-wishers and waved to the cheering crowds in thanks.

Then, the special train slowly left the station amidst the enthusiastic cheers of the masses, crossing the Yalu River under the setting sun.

On their way from Pyongyang to Sinuiju, Vice Chairman Zhu De and the members of the delegation visited the Kusong Textile Factory and the Supung Hydroelectric Power Station, where they were warmly welcomed by the Korean workers.

Xinhua News Agency, Sinuiju, August 24th -- Vice Chairman Zhu De, head of the delegation of the People's Republic of China, who was invited to attend the celebration of the 10th anniversary of the liberation of Korea, sent a telegram to Premier Kim Il-sung to express his gratitude when he left the Korean border on the evening of the 24th. The text of the telegram is as follows:

To Comrade Kim Il-sung, Premier of the Cabinet of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea, Pyongyang:

As we are about to leave the magnificent land of Korea, please allow me, on behalf of the delegation of the People's Republic of China and myself, to once again express our heartfelt gratitude for the friendly care and warm hospitality extended to us by your government, the Korean people, and yourself!

I wish the Korean people even greater victories in their cause of striving for peaceful reunification and restoring and developing the national economy!

　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　Zhu De
　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　August 24, 1955, in Sinuiju

### 我国代表团从朝鲜回到北京　朱德副主席离新义州时电金日成首相致谢

1955-08-26
第1版()
专栏：

　　我国代表团从朝鲜回到北京
　　朱德副主席离新义州时电金日成首相致谢
    新华社二十五日讯　应朝鲜民主主义人民共和国政府邀请，赴朝鲜参加“八·一五”伟大苏联军队解放朝鲜十周年庆祝典礼的中华人民共和国代表团，今日下午已回到北京。
    新华社新义州二十四日电　由朱德副主席率领的中华人民共和国代表团应朝鲜民主主义人民共和国政府邀请参加朝鲜解放十周年庆祝典礼并且到朝鲜各地参观访问以后，已于二十四日下午七时半乘专车自新义州返国。
    在新义州车站上欢送的有朝鲜劳动党中央委员会副委员长朴正爱、朝鲜平安北道人民委员会委员长韩典锺，朝鲜劳动党平安北道委员会委员长徐春植和新义州市负责人以及新义州劳动人民三千多人。
    陪送朱德副主席到朝鲜国境的朝鲜民主主义人民共和国内阁副首相崔庸健次帅、朝鲜劳动党中央委员会社会科学部长河仰天和中国驻朝大使潘自力也到车站欢送。
    在车站上，朱德副主席由朴正爱副委员长，崔庸健次帅陪同，检阅了仪仗队。检阅毕，朱德副主席和代表团团员接受了新义州儿童们的献花。这时，车站上响起了暴风雨般的掌声和欢呼声。
    朱德副主席和代表团团员们同欢送者一再亲切地握手道别，并且向列队欢呼着的人群招手答谢。
    接着，专车在群众狂热的欢呼声中，徐徐驶离车站，渡过夕阳照耀下的鸭绿江。
    朱德副主席和代表团团员在从平壤到新义州途中，曾参观了龟城纺织工厂和水丰水力发电站，受到朝鲜职工们的热烈迎送。
    新华社新义州二十四日电　应邀参加朝鲜解放十周年庆祝典礼的中华人民共和国代表团团长朱德副主席在二十四日晚离开朝鲜国境时，致电金日成首相表示感谢。电文如下：平壤朝鲜民主主义人民共和国内阁首相金日成同志：
    当我们即将离开壮丽的朝鲜国土之际，请允许我以中华人民共和国代表团和我本人的名义对于贵国政府、朝鲜人民和您本人给予我们的友好关怀和热诚款待，再一次表示衷心的感谢！
    祝朝鲜人民在争取和平统一和恢复发展国民经济的事业中获得更大的胜利！
　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　朱德
　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　一九五五年八月二十四日于新义州
