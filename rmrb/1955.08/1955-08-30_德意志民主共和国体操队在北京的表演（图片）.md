　　Gymnastics Team of the German Democratic Republic Performing in Beijing
    The Gymnastics Team of the German Democratic Republic gave its first performance in Beijing at the Beihai Stadium on the evening of August 28th. The picture shows Chinese and German gymnasts at the performance.

    Vera Ewoldt, sports athlete of the German Democratic Republic and champion of the balance beam in the German Democratic Republic, performs on the balance beam.
