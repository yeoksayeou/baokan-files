Title: Li Zuoxi Creates Agricultural Compound Machine
Author:
Date: 1955-08-08
Page: 1

Li Zuoxi, a technician from a certain unit of the Production and Construction Corps of the Xinjiang Military Region's Shihezi Reclamation Area, recently created a compound machine through comprehensive design based on the structure and advantages of cultivators, fertilizer applicators, ditching machines, and rollers. This compound machine can complete four field operations—cultivation, fertilization, ditching, and rolling—in one process. Working ten hours a day with two people and one horse, it can cultivate more than 40 mu of land, increasing work efficiency by six to seven times compared to the original method. Using this compound machine in large-scale cotton field management can save a large amount of manpower and animal power. During the design and trial production process, he received a great deal of guidance from Soviet agricultural machinery experts and agricultural science experts. The Shihezi Repair Shop Technical Research Group of this unit also provided a lot of specific assistance.
	(Our newspaper's collective correspondent, Xinjiang Daily Editorial Department)

### 李作西创造农业复式作业机

1955-08-08
第1版()
专栏：

    李作西创造农业复式作业机
    新疆军区生产部队石河子垦区某部技工李作西在最近根据中耕器、追肥器、开沟器、镇压器的构造和优点，进行综合设计，创造了一种复式作业机。这种复式作业机可以把中耕、追肥、开沟、镇压等四项田间作业用一道手续做完。每天只要两个人一匹马工作十小时，就可耕作四十多亩地，比原来的工作效率提高了六、七倍。在大面积棉田管理工作中，使用这种复式作业机可以节省出大批人力、畜力。在设计和试制过程中，曾得到苏联农业机械专家、农业科学专家很多的指导。这支部队的石河子修配厂技术研究小组也给予了许多具体帮助。
          （本报集体通讯员新疆日报编辑部）
