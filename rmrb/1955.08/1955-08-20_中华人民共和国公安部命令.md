Order of the Ministry of Public Security of the People's Republic of China

The Urban Traffic Regulations have been submitted to and approved by the State Council and are hereby promulgated. It is decided that they will be implemented from October 1955. From the date of implementation, the "Provisional Regulations on Urban Land Traffic Management" promulgated by the Ministry of Public Security of the Central People's Government in 1951 shall be repealed.

This order.

Minister Luo Ruiqing
August 19, 1955

### 中华人民共和国公安部命令

1955-08-20
第2版()
专栏：

    中华人民共和国公安部命令
    城市交通规则业经报请国务院批准，特令公布，并决定自一九五五年十月份起施行。自施行之日起，中央人民政府公安部一九五一年公布的“城市陆上交通管理暂行规则”即行废止。
    此令
                     部长　罗瑞卿
                     一九五五年八月十九日
