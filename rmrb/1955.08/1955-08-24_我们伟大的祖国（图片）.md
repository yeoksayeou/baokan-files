Title: Our Great Motherland (Picture)
Author:
Date: 1955-08-24
Page: 1

    Our Great Motherland
    In the summer of this year, Guilin City held a Young Pioneers summer camp. Two hundred and twenty-nine Young Pioneers members from Han, Hui, Zhuang, Dong, Yao and other ethnic groups participated in the summer camp activities. Members of the sketching group are depicting the scenery of Guilin on Diecai Mountain.

### 我们伟大的祖国（图片）

1955-08-24
第1版()
专栏：

    我们伟大的祖国
    桂林市在今年夏天举办了少年先锋队夏令营，参加夏令营生活的有汉、回、僮、侗、瑶等民族的二百二十九名少年先锋队员。写生小组的组员正在叠彩山上描绘桂林的风光。
