Title: British Foreign Office Spokesman Reiterates: British Government Attaches Extreme Importance to Maintaining the Korean Armistice Agreement
Author:
Date: 1955-08-14
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, London, August 13th: According to British Information Services, a British Foreign Office spokesman, in response to a reporter's question regarding recent events in Korea on the 12th, reiterated the earlier statement that the British government attaches extreme importance to maintaining the Armistice Agreement. The British government also acknowledges the view that the United Nations Command bears responsibility for the safety of the Neutral Nations Supervisory Commission.

Reuters also reports that the British Foreign Office spokesman also stated that Britain has "taken full note" of the proposal made by China and the Democratic People's Republic of Korea regarding holding a Far East conference to consider the Korean situation.

### 英国外交部发言人重申  英国政府极端重视维持朝鲜停战协定

1955-08-14
第4版()
专栏：

    英国外交部发言人重申
    英国政府极端重视维持朝鲜停战协定
    新华社十三日讯　伦敦消息：据英国新闻处消息，英国外交部发言人十二日在答复记者关于朝鲜最近的事件的问题时，重申了早先的声明，即：英国政府极端重视维持停战协定。英国政府也承认这种看法：联合国军总司令部对中立国监察委员会的安全负有责任。
    另据路透社报道，英国外交部发言人还说，英国已“充分注意到”中国和朝鲜民主主义人民共和国提出的关于举行远东会议来考虑朝鲜局势的建议。
