Title: Inaugural National Congress of the First National Machinery Industry Union of China Opens

Author: 
Date: 1955-08-17
Page: 2

    The inaugural National Congress of the First National Machinery Industry Union of China was held in Beijing on August 15th. The main tasks of this congress are to summarize the work of the First National Machinery Industry Union of China from 1950 to the present, to discuss and determine the working principles and tasks of the First National Machinery Industry Union of China during the First Five-Year Plan, and to formally establish the national leading body of the First National Machinery Industry Union of China.
    There were 211 delegates attending this congress, and 54 delegates attending as observers. They represent the staff and workers of state-owned, locally state-owned, jointly state-private owned, and private-owned machinery industries in more than 20 provinces, municipalities, and the Inner Mongolia Autonomous Region across the country. Among the delegates are model workers, advanced producers, engineering and technical personnel, enterprise management personnel, and trade union workers. Representatives from the First Ministry of Machine Building and the Third Ministry of Machine Building also attended.
    After Ma Peixun, Deputy Director of the Preparatory Committee of the First National Machinery Industry Union of China, delivered the opening speech, the congratulatory message from the Central Committee of the Communist Party of China was read.
    Lai Ruoyu, Chairman of the All-China Federation of Trade Unions, Li Yu, Vice Minister of the First Ministry of Machine Building, Fan Xipeng, representative of the Central Committee of the Chinese New Democratic Youth League, Zhu Shunyu, Deputy to the National People's Congress and Shanghai Model Worker, and Tang Liyan, Deputy to the National People's Congress and Dalian-Lushun Model Worker, all spoke at the opening ceremony.
    The Preparatory Committee of the Chinese Heavy Industry Union, the Preparatory Committee of the Chinese Agricultural and Water Conservancy Union, and other units also sent representatives to congratulate the congress. The congress also received congratulatory letters from the Ministry of Labor and other units. (Xinhua News Agency)

### 中国第一机械工会首次全国代表大会开幕

1955-08-17
第2版()
专栏：

    中国第一机械工会首次全国代表大会开幕
    中国第一机械工会第一次全国代表大会八月十五日在北京举行。这次大会的主要任务是总结一九五零年到现在的中国第一机械工会的工作，讨论和确定中国第一机械工会在第一个五年计划期间的工作方针任务，以及正式成立中国第一机械工会的全国领导机关。
    出席这次大会的代表有二百一十一人，列席的代表有五十四人。他们代表着全国二十多个省、市和内蒙古自治区的国营、地方国营、公私合营和私营的机械工业的职工。代表中有劳动模范、先进生产者、工程技术人员、企业管理人员和工会工作者。第一机械工业部和第三机械工业部也有代表出席。
    大会由中国第一机械工会筹备委员会副主任马佩勋致开幕词后，宣读了中国共产党中央委员会的祝词。
    中华全国总工会主席赖若愚，第一机械工业部副部长黎玉，中国新民主主义青年团中央委员会代表范希彭，全国人民代表大会代表、上海市劳动模范朱顺余，全国人民代表大会代表、旅大市劳动模范唐立言都在开幕式上讲了话。
    中国重工业工会、中国农业水利工会筹备委员会等单位也都派代表向大会祝贺。大会还收到劳动部等单位的贺信。（新华社）
