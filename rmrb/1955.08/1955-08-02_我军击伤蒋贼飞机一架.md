Title: Our Army Shoots Down a Chiang Kai-shek Bandit Plane
Author:
Date: 1955-08-02
Page: 1
    Xinhua News Agency, Guangzhou, 30th. At 12:34 on the 28th, four Chiang Kai-shek bandit F-47 aircraft intruded into the airspace over Shantou City, Guangdong Province. The People's Liberation Army's anti-aircraft artillery units immediately opened fire, hitting and damaging one of them.

### 我军击伤蒋贼飞机一架

1955-08-02
第1版()
专栏：

    我军击伤蒋贼飞机一架
    新华社广州三十日电　二十八日十二时三十四分，蒋贼F—47型飞机四架窜扰广东省汕头市上空，中国人民解放军高射炮部队当即开火射击，击伤其中的一架。
