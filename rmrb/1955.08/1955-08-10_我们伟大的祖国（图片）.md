Author:
Date: 1955-08-10

    Our Great Motherland
    The new Shijiazhuang Thermal Power Plant is under intense construction and is expected to generate partial power next year, gradually supplying industrial electricity to Shijiazhuang City. The thermal energy of the thermal power plant can also be used by textile, printing and dyeing factories to reduce the boiler equipment of these factories. This is a bulldozer leveling the roadbed in front of the main plant building.

### 我们伟大的祖国（图片）

1955-08-10
第1版()
专栏：

    我们伟大的祖国
    新建石家庄热雷站正在紧张施工，预计明年就可以局部发电，逐步供应石家庄市的工业用电。热电站的热能还可供纺织、印染等工厂使用，以减少这些工厂的锅炉设备。这是推土机正在主厂房前面铲平路基的情形。
