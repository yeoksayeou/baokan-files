Title: China and the Soviet Union Sign Agreement on Crop Quarantine and Prevention and Control of Plant Diseases and Insect Pests
Author:
Date: 1955-08-17
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, August 16th – The governments of the People's Republic of China and the Union of Soviet Socialist Republics, in order to strengthen the plant protection work of the two countries and develop the agricultural production of the two countries, have signed an agreement in Beijing on August 16th regarding crop quarantine and the prevention and control of plant diseases and insect pests after friendly negotiations.

Yang Xiandong, Vice Minister of Agriculture of the People's Republic of China, signed the agreement on behalf of the Chinese side, and V.P. Migunov, Acting Commercial Representative of the Soviet Union in China, signed the agreement on behalf of the Union of Soviet Socialist Republics.

The Chinese side participating in the signing ceremony included: He Wei, Assistant Minister of Foreign Affairs; Liao Tiren, Deputy Director of the Commodity Inspection Bureau of the Ministry of Foreign Trade; Xi Fengzhou, Deputy Director of the General Office of the Ministry of Agriculture, and others.

The Soviet side participating in the signing ceremony included: Romain, Chargé d'Affaires ad interim of the Soviet Embassy in China; Sorokin, Acting Director of the Protocol Department; Sherbina, Deputy Commercial Representative of the Soviet Union in China, and expert Orlov, and others.

In the evening, Yang Xiandong held a banquet for this purpose.

### 中苏签订农作物检疫和防治病虫害的协定

1955-08-17
第1版()
专栏：

    中苏签订农作物检疫和防治病虫害的协定
    新华社十六日讯　中华人民共和国政府和苏维埃社会主义共和国联盟政府为了加强两国植物保护事业，发展两国农业生产，双方经过友好的谈判，已于八月十六日在北京签订了中苏关于农作物检疫和防治病虫害的协定。
    代表中国方面在协定上签字的，是中华人民共和国农业部副部长杨显东，代表苏维埃社会主义共和国联盟在协定上签字的，是苏联驻华商务代表处代办瓦·彼·米古诺夫。
    中国方面参加签字仪式的有：外交部部长助理何伟、对外贸易部商品检验总局副局长廖体仁、农业部办公厅副主任席凤洲等。
    苏联方面参加签字仪式的有：苏联驻华大使馆临时代办罗迈进、交际处代理处长索罗金，苏联驻华商务代表处副代办谢尔比那和专家奥尔洛夫等。
    晚上，杨显东为此举行了宴会。
