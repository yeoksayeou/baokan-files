Title: Fourteen Asian-African Nations Request UN General Assembly Discussion on West Irian Issue
Author:
Date: 1955-08-13
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, New York, August 11th, TASS: A United Press International correspondent stationed at the UN Secretariat reported that the Asian-African group of fourteen countries at the United Nations jointly sent a letter and a memorandum to the UN Secretary-General, requesting that the West Irian (West New Guinea) issue be included in the agenda of the UN General Assembly to be held this autumn.

The issue of New Guinea under Dutch rule was included in the agenda of last year's UN General Assembly. However, the resolution calling for the organization of negotiations between the Netherlands and Indonesia did not receive the necessary two-thirds majority in the plenary session.

The memorandum to Hammarskjöld stated that, according to law and the constitution, West Irian is permanently part of the former Netherlands East Indies, and therefore, according to the Hague Round Table Conference Agreement of 1949 transferring the East Indies to Indonesia, it should become part of the Republic of Indonesia.

The countries that signed the memorandum expressed the hope that this year's UN General Assembly will adopt constructive decisions.

### 亚非十四国要求联大讨论西伊里安问题

1955-08-13
第4版()
专栏：

    亚非十四国要求联大讨论西伊里安问题
    新华社十二日讯　塔斯社纽约十一日讯：合众社驻联合国秘书处记者报道，联合国亚非集团十四个国家联名致联合国秘书长一封信和一件备忘录，要求把西伊里安（西新几内亚）问题列入今年秋天举行的联合国大会的议程。
    关于荷兰统治下的新几内亚的问题曾列入去年的联合国大会议程，但是，大会号召组织荷兰和印度尼西亚谈判的决议没有得到全体会议三分之二的必要多数通过。
    在给哈马舍尔德的备忘录中说，根据法律和宪法，西伊里安是永远属于前荷属东印度群岛的一部分，因此根据把东印度群岛移交给印度尼西亚的一九四九年海牙圆桌会议协定，它应该成为印度尼西亚共和国的一部分。
    在备忘录上签字的国家表示希望今年的联合国大会将通过建设性的决定。
