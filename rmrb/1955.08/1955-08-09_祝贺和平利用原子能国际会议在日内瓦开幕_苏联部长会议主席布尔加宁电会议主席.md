Title: Congratulations on the Opening of the International Conference on the Peaceful Uses of Atomic Energy: Bulganin, Chairman of the Council of Ministers of the Soviet Union, Sends a Telegram to the Chairman of the Conference
Author:
Date: 1955-08-09
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, August 8th: According to TASS, the Chairman of the Council of Ministers of the Soviet Union, Bulganin, sent a telegram to Baba, Chairman of the International Scientific and Technical Conference on the Peaceful Uses of Atomic Energy, on the 7th, congratulating him on the opening of the conference. Bulganin said in the telegram: "The Soviet Union attaches great importance to developing broad international cooperation in using the great scientific discoveries of our time not for the purposes of war and destruction, but for constructive purposes, for the benefit of mankind, to improve the welfare of all nations and raise their living standards. Therefore, the Soviet Union welcomes the international scientific and technical conference to exchange knowledge and experience in the peaceful uses of atomic energy.
    The Soviet government hopes that the conference will take a big step forward in developing international scientific cooperation in the peaceful uses of atomic energy, and wishes the conference every success in achieving this noble task."

### 祝贺和平利用原子能国际会议在日内瓦开幕  苏联部长会议主席布尔加宁电会议主席

1955-08-09
第1版()
专栏：

    祝贺和平利用原子能国际会议在日内瓦开幕
    苏联部长会议主席布尔加宁电会议主席
    新华社八日讯　据塔斯社莫斯科讯：苏联部长会议主席布尔加宁七日致电和平利用原子能国际科学技术会议主席巴巴，祝贺会议开幕。布尔加宁在电报中说：“苏联十分重视在使我们时代的伟大科学发现不用于战争和破坏目的而用于建设性目的，用于造福人类、增进各国人民的福利和提高他们的生活水平方面，发展广泛的国际合作。因此苏联欢迎交流和平利用原子能方面的知识和经验的国际科学技术会议。
    苏联政府希望会议在发展和平利用原子能的国际科学合作方面向前迈进一大步，并且祝会议在实现这一崇高任务中获得一切成就。”
