Title: Uruguayan Communist Youth Hold National Congress
Author:
Date: 1955-08-30
Page: 4

    Xinhua News Agency, Montevideo, August 29th (TASS): The Uruguayan Communist Youth held a national congress in Montevideo from August 24th to 27th. The congress decided to reinstate the Uruguayan Communist Youth League, which will dedicate itself to uniting Uruguayan youth in the struggle for peace and the rights of the younger generation. The congress also unanimously decided that the Uruguayan Communist Youth League would join the World Federation of Democratic Youth.

### 乌拉圭共产主义青年举行全国代表会议

1955-08-30
第4版()
专栏：

    乌拉圭共产主义青年举行全国代表会议
    新华社二十九日讯　据塔斯社蒙得维的亚讯：乌拉圭共产主义青年从八月二十四日到二十七日在蒙得维的亚举行了全国代表会议。会议决定恢复乌拉圭共产主义青年团，青年团将致力于团结乌拉圭青年为和平和青年一代的权利而奋斗。会议并且一致决定乌拉圭共产主义青年团参加世界民主青年联盟。
