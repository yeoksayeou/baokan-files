Title: Our Consul-General in Geneva Hands Over the Foreign Ministry Spokesperson's Statement to the Japanese Consul-General
Author:
Date: 1955-08-19
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, Geneva, August 18th. Shen Ping, Consul-General of the People's Republic of China in Geneva, met with Keiichi Tazuke, the Japanese Consul-General in Geneva, at the Chinese Consulate-General on the afternoon of the 17th. During the meeting, Consul-General Shen Ping handed over a copy of the statement by the spokesperson of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs of the People's Republic of China regarding the issue of the Japanese government's so-called withdrawal of Japanese nationals remaining on the Chinese mainland, to the Japanese Consul-General.

### 我驻日内瓦总领事将外交部发言人声明交给日本总领事

1955-08-19
第1版()
专栏：

    我驻日内瓦总领事将外交部发言人声明交给日本总领事
    新华社日内瓦十八日电　中华人民共和国驻日内瓦总领事沈平十七日下午在中国总领事馆会见了日本驻日内瓦总领事田付景一。在会见时，沈平总领事把中华人民共和国外交部发言人就日本政府提出所谓撤退留在中国大陆的日本人问题发表的声明的抄件交给了日本总领事。
