Title: The Czechoslovak Government Issues a Statement on the Outcome of the Geneva Four-Power Conference; The Czechoslovak Republic Decides to Participate in the Austrian State Treaty
Author:
Date: 1955-08-15
Page: 4
    Xinhua News Agency, Prague, August 13th: The Czechoslovak News Agency reports that the Czechoslovak government held a plenary session on August 10th to hear Prime Minister Široký's report on the Geneva Four-Power Heads of Government Conference and adopted a statement.

    The plenary session excitedly pointed out that the Geneva Conference confirmed that the path of negotiation is the only correct way to solve international problems.

    The statement pointed out that the Geneva Conference was a significant milestone on the road to easing world tensions and an important contribution to the cause of peace.

    The statement fully supports the Soviet proposal on European security and emphasizes that this proposal is in line with the interests and needs of Czechoslovakia and all European countries.

    The Czechoslovak government fully agrees with the Soviet government's view on the reunification of Germany. The Czechoslovak government believes that under the current conditions, the joint efforts of the Four Powers and the efforts of the German people are the only realistic way to unify Germany. The Czechoslovak government fully supports the efforts of the German people to establish a unified, democratic, peace-loving, and independent German nation, and believes that in solving the German question, the interests and will of the people of the German Democratic Republic must be respected. While further strengthening and developing friendly cooperation with the German Democratic Republic, the Czechoslovak government is also willing to "discuss with the government of the Federal Republic of Germany the establishment of diplomatic relations and the further expansion and normalization of trade and cultural relations."

    The statement emphasized that the Soviet proposal on disarmament expresses the wishes and demands of the people of all countries in the world.

    The statement said that the Czechoslovak government will do everything possible to help implement the results of the Geneva Conference. The statement also said that the Czechoslovak government will sincerely strive for the full development of friendly and mutual cooperation with the Federal People's Republic of Yugoslavia.

    Xinhua News Agency, Prague, August 14th: According to the Czechoslovak News Agency, the government of the Czechoslovak Republic discussed the attitude towards the Austrian State Treaty on the 10th.

    Deputy Minister of Foreign Affairs, G. Sekaninová-Čakrtová, said in a report on this issue that the Austrian State Treaty, which restores Austria as an autonomous, independent, and democratic country, is an extremely important step towards peaceful coexistence in Europe. The Czechoslovak government is convinced that the two peace-loving neighbors now have all the favorable preconditions and conditions to develop and consolidate friendly ties in all aspects based on mutual understanding and cooperation.

    The government of the Czechoslovak Republic has decided to participate in the Austrian State Treaty. This treaty will be submitted to the National Assembly for approval and submitted to the President of the Republic for ratification.

### 捷政府就日内瓦四国会议结果发表声明  捷克斯洛伐克共和国决定参加奥地利国家条约

1955-08-15
第4版()
专栏：

    捷政府就日内瓦四国会议结果发表声明
    捷克斯洛伐克共和国决定参加奥地利国家条约
    新华社布拉格十三日电  捷克斯洛伐克通讯社报道：捷克斯洛伐克政府，八月十日举行全体会议，听取了西罗基总理关于日内瓦四国政府首脑会议的报告，并且通过了一项声明。
    全体会议兴奋地指出，日内瓦会议证实谈判的道路是解决国际问题唯一正确的途径。
    声明指出，日内瓦会议是缓和世界紧张局势的道路上的一座意义重大的里程碑，是对和平事业的重要贡献。
    声明完全支持苏联关于欧洲安全的建议，并且强调指出，这个建议是符合捷克斯洛伐克和所有欧洲国家的利益和需要的。
    捷克斯洛伐克政府完全同意苏联政府对于统一德国的见解。捷克斯洛伐克政府认为，在目前的条件下，四大国的共同努力以及德国人民的努力是统一德国唯一现实的道路。捷克斯洛伐克政府完全支持德国人民为建立一个统一的、民主的、爱好和平和独立的德意志国家所作的努力，并且相信，在解决德国问题时，必须尊重德意志民主共和国人民的利益和意志。捷克斯洛伐克政府在进一步加强和发展同德意志民主共和国的友好合作的同时还愿意“同德意志联邦共和国政府讨论建立外交关系和使贸易和文化关系进一步扩大和正常化的问题”。
    声明强调指出，苏联关于裁减军备的建议表达了世界上所有国家的人民的愿望和要求。
    声明说，捷克斯洛伐克政府将尽一切可能来帮助贯彻日内瓦会议的结果。声明又说，捷克斯洛伐克政府将真诚地争取全面发展同南斯拉夫联邦人民共和国的友好和相互合作。
    新华社布拉格十四日电  据捷克斯洛伐克通讯社报道，捷克斯洛伐克共和国政府在十日讨论了对奥地利国家条约的态度问题。
    外交部副部长格·塞卡尼诺娃—查克尔托娃在关于这个问题的报告中说，恢复奥地利为自主、独立和民主的国家的奥地利国家条约是向欧洲和平共处迈进的极其重要的一步。捷克斯洛伐克政府确信，现在两个爱好和平的邻国完全具有各种有利的前提和条件来在相互谅解和合作的基础上发展和巩固各方面的友好联系。
    捷克斯洛伐克共和国政府决定参加奥地利国家条约。这个条约将提交国民议会同意并且提请共和国总统批准。
