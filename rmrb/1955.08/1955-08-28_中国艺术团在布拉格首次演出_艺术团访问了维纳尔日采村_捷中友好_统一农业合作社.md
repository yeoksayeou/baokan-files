Title: Chinese Art Troupe's First Performance in Prague; Art Troupe Visits "Czechoslovak-Chinese Friendship" United Agricultural Cooperative in Vinarice Village
Author:
Date: 1955-08-28
Page: 3

The Chinese Art Troupe, invited to Czechoslovakia for a performance tour, held its first performance at the National Theatre in Prague on the evening of August 24th after a two-week rest in Czechoslovakia.

Present at the performance were Czechoslovak Premier Široký, Chairman of the National Assembly Fierlinger, Secretary of the Central Committee of the Czechoslovak Communist Party Hendrych, Minister of Culture of the Republic Štol, Minister of Education Kahuda, heads of Czechoslovak mass organizations, and figures from the cultural and artistic circles.

Also present were diplomatic envoys from various countries to Czechoslovakia.

Chinese Ambassador to Czechoslovakia Cao Ying and his wife also attended the performance.

Before the performance, Deputy Minister of Culture of Czechoslovakia Ludvík delivered a speech welcoming the representatives of the great Chinese people and the envoys of outstanding art, and congratulated the Chinese Art Troupe on its success in its performance tour in Western countries, and wished further success in future performances.

Head of the Troupe Zhang Zhixiang delivered a reply. On behalf of the art troupe, he expressed his most sincere thanks to the Czechoslovak government and people for their brotherly warm care and reception to the Chinese Art Troupe, and pointed out that the frequent cultural exchanges between China and Czechoslovakia in recent years are constantly enhancing the close friendship between the two countries.

Subsequently, the art troupe performed six Chinese classical plays including "San Cha Kou," "Farewell My Concubine," "Havoc in Heaven," "Broken Bridge," "Autumn River," and "Yandang Mountain." Each performance was warmly welcomed.

"San Cha Kou" and "Havoc in Heaven" were curtain-called as many as seven times, and were re-performed for a section at the repeated requests of the audience.

At the end of the performance, Zhang Zhixiang and Zhao Feng led all the actors in repeated curtain calls. The audience responded with prolonged thunderous applause. Representatives from the Czechoslovak Ministry of Culture presented the art troupe with a flower basket from Minister of Culture Štol. Members of the Czechoslovak Youth League went on stage to present flowers to the Chinese actors. The Chinese actors threw bouquets of flowers to the audience. The audience flocked to the front of the stage, applauding and cheering enthusiastically. The friendly feelings between the Chinese and Czechoslovak people made the entire theater boil over. (Xinhua News Agency)

Members of the Chinese Art Troupe, invited to Czechoslovakia for a performance tour, led by Deputy Head Zhao Feng, visited the "Czechoslovak-Chinese Friendship" United Agricultural Cooperative in Vinarice Village on the 23rd.

The members of the art troupe were warmly received by the cooperative members and presented with flowers. The hosts warmly told the guests about the cooperative's production situation and the growing friendship with Zhang Guozhuang Township in Beijing. Cooperative Chairman Hermann pointed to the wheat being harvested and said, "Last year, our cooperative's wheat yield was 2.4 tons per hectare, and this year it is expected to be about 3 to 3.3 tons per hectare." He also talked about the positive impact of the friendship with Zhang Guozhuang Township on their progress and achievements.

In the evening, members of the art troupe held a dinner with the cooperative's leading personnel and representatives of the local garrison. The chairman of the cooperative presented the art troupe with a record player and a set of Dvořák's Slavonic Dances records, and the representatives of the garrison presented a wooden model tank to the art troupe as a souvenir.

Subsequently, the art troupe performed programs such as "San Cha Kou," a pas de deux, and a zither solo for the farmers of Vinarice Village, workers from nearby factories, and the garrison, which were warmly welcomed by the audience. (Xinhua News Agency)

### 中国艺术团在布拉格首次演出　艺术团访问了维纳尔日采村“捷中友好”统一农业合作社

1955-08-28
第3版()
专栏：

　　中国艺术团在布拉格首次演出
　　艺术团访问了维纳尔日采村“捷中友好”统一农业合作社
    应邀到捷克斯洛伐克作访问演出的中国艺术团，在捷克斯洛伐克休息了半个月以后，八月二十四日晚间在布拉格民族剧院举行首次演出。
    出席观看这次演出的有捷克斯洛伐克共和国总理西罗基、国民议会主席费林格、捷克斯洛伐克共产党中央委员会书记亨德里赫、共和国文化部长斯托尔、教育部长卡胡达、捷克斯洛伐克群众团体负责人和文化艺术界人士等。
    出席观看的还有各国驻捷外交使节。
    中国驻捷大使曹瑛偕夫人也出席观剧。
    演出前，捷文化部副部长卢德维克致词欢迎伟大中国人民的代表和卓越艺术的使者，并且祝贺中国艺术团在西方国家访问演出中所获得的成功，和希望未来演出的进一步成功。
    张致祥团长致答词。他代表艺术团对捷克斯洛伐克政府和人民对中国艺术团的兄弟般的热情关怀和接待表示最衷心的感谢，并且指出几年来中捷文化的频繁交流正在不断增进着两国间的亲密友谊。
    随后艺术团演出了“三岔口”、“霸王别姬”、
  “闹天宫”、“断桥”、“秋江”和“雁荡山”等六个中国古典剧目。每一个剧目都受到热烈的欢迎。
  “三岔口”和“闹天宫”谢幕达七次之多，最后在观众的一再要求下都重演了一段。
    表演结束时，张致祥、赵沨率领全体演员再三谢幕。台下响起了经久的雷动般的掌声。捷文化部代表把文化部长斯托尔送给艺术团的一个花篮献给艺术团。捷克斯洛伐克青年联盟盟员走上舞台向中国演员献花。中国演员就把一束束的鲜花投给观众。观众们涌向台前，热烈鼓掌欢呼。中捷人民的友谊之情使整个剧院都沸腾起来。（新华社）
    应邀到捷克斯洛伐克作访问演出的中国艺术团团员们，二十三日由副团长赵沨率领，访问了维纳尔日采村的“捷中友好”统一农业合作社。
      艺术团团员们受到社员们热情的接待和献花。主人们热情地向客人们讲述着合作社的生产情况以及同北京张郭庄乡日益深厚的友谊。合作社主席赫尔曼指着正在收割着的麦子说：“去年我们社的小麦产量每公顷是二十四公担，今年每公顷约可产三十到三十三公担。”他并且谈到同张郭庄乡的友谊对于他们的进步和成绩的良好影响。
    晚间，艺术团团员们同合作社负责人员和当地驻军代表等举行了聚餐。合作社主席把一架电唱机和德沃夏克的斯拉夫舞曲唱片一套、驻军代表把一辆木制小型坦克赠给艺术团留作纪念。
    随后，艺术团为维纳尔日采村农民、附近工厂的工人和驻军演出了“三岔口”、双人舞和古筝独奏等节目，受到了观众的热烈欢迎。（新华社）
