Our Great Motherland

The Shanghai Xuhui District Committee of the Communist Youth League organized a summer activity most loved by children—the "Children's Factory." Five hundred primary school students voluntarily signed up to work as small workers in this factory. In the doll workshop, the young workers make beautiful toys for the younger children in kindergartens.

### 我们伟大的祖国（图片）

1955-08-05
第1版()
专栏：

    我们伟大的祖国
    青年团上海市徐汇区委员会主办了儿童们最喜爱的一种暑期活动——“儿童工厂”。五百多小学生自动报名到这个工厂作小工人。在布娃娃车间，小工人替幼儿园的小弟弟和小妹妹做美丽的玩偶。
