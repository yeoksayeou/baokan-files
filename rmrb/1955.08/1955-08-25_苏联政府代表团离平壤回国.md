Title: Soviet Government Delegation Leaves Pyongyang for Home

Author: 
Date: 1955-08-25
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Pyongyang, August 23rd (TASS dispatch): The Soviet government delegation headed by Aristov, Secretary of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of the Soviet Union, left Pyongyang on the 22nd to return home after participating in the celebrations of the 10th anniversary of the liberation of Korea.

Those who went to the station to see them off included Kim Il-sung, Kim Tu-bong, Park Jung-ai, Choi Yong-kun, Park Chang-ok, Choi Chang-ik, Hong Myung-hee, Park Ui-wan, Lee Young, Nam Il and other leaders of the Korean Workers' Party and the government, representatives of public organizations, members of the Chinese People's Republic delegation, staff of the Soviet Embassy in Korea, and envoys from various countries stationed in Pyongyang.

An honor guard was lined up at the station. After the Soviet national anthem and the national anthem of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea were played, Aristov inspected the honor guard.

Then, Aristov delivered a speech. He expressed his deep gratitude for the warm reception and welcome the Soviet government delegation received in Korea. On behalf of the Soviet people, he wished the Korean people an early realization of their greatest aspiration - the reunification of Korea and the establishment of a unified and democratic state, as well as the earliest possible recovery and development of economy, science, and culture, and the improvement of the material living standards of the working people. He emphasized that the Korean people could be assured that the Soviet people would always be the best friends of the Korean people, and the Soviet people would continue to give the Korean people selfless brotherly assistance.

Aristov's speech aroused stormy applause and cheers. Thousands of people who saw them off at the station shouted: "Long live the friendship between Korea and the Soviet Union."

Park Jung-ai, Vice-Chairman of the Central Committee of the Korean Workers' Party, and Lee Dong-geon, Vice-Minister of Foreign Affairs, accompanied the Soviet government delegation to the Korean border.

### 苏联政府代表团离平壤回国

1955-08-25
第4版()
专栏：

　　苏联政府代表团离平壤回国
    新华社二十四日讯　据塔斯社平壤二十三日讯：以苏联共产党中央委员会书记阿里斯托夫为首的苏联政府代表团在参加朝鲜解放十周年庆祝典礼后已于二十二日离平壤回国。
    到车站欢送的有金日成、金枓奉、朴正爱、崔庸健、朴昌玉、崔昌益、洪命熹、朴义琓、李英、南日等朝鲜劳动党和政府的领导人，各公众团体代表，中华人民共和国代表团团员，苏联驻朝鲜大使馆工作人员和各国驻平壤使节。
    车站上排列了仪仗队。在奏完苏联国歌和朝鲜民主主义人民共和国国歌以后，阿里斯托夫检阅了仪仗队。
    接着，阿里斯托夫发表了谈话。他对苏联政府代表团在朝鲜所受到的热情的招待和欢迎表示深深的感谢。他代表苏联人民祝朝鲜人民早日实现他们的最大的愿望——重新统一朝鲜，并且建立一个统一的、民主的国家，同时尽可能早地恢复和发展经济、科学和文化，并且提高劳动人民的物质水准。他强调说，朝鲜人民可以确信，苏联人民将永远是朝鲜人民的最好的朋友，苏联人民将继续给朝鲜人民以无私的兄弟般的援助。
      阿里斯托夫的谈话引起了暴风雨般的掌声和欢呼声。在车站上欢送的成千人们高呼：“朝鲜—苏联友谊万岁”。
    陪送苏联政府代表团到朝鲜边境的有：朝鲜劳动党中央委员会副委员长朴正爱和外务省副相李东建。
