Title: Pyongyang and Hanoi Hold Rallies Against the Use of Atomic Weapons
Author:
Date: 1955-08-07
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Pyongyang, 6th - According to the Korean Central News Agency: Pyongyang held a mass rally against atomic and hydrogen weapons on the evening of the 5th.

Li Gye-ru, a member of the Korean National Committee for the Defense of Peace, delivered a report at the meeting. He analyzed the two opposing attitudes toward the use of atomic energy, and expressed support for the Soviet Union's attitude on this issue and its unremitting efforts.

He emphasized that the people demand that the atomic war of destruction that will bring terrible disasters to mankind should no longer occur on Earth. He said that the Korean people are fully determined to actively participate in the worldwide struggle against atomic and hydrogen weapons.

Subsequently, workers, missionaries, teachers, scientists, and students spoke one after another. They emphasized that atomic energy must serve the prosperity and happiness of mankind. They congratulated the Hiroshima Conference on its success.

The conference passed a resolution and a congratulatory telegram to the Hiroshima Conference amidst stormy applause. The conference also passed a letter protesting the Japanese government's refusal to allow the Korean representatives to attend the Hiroshima Conference to enter Japan.

Xinhua News Agency, Hanoi, 6th - The Vietnam Committee for the Defense of World Peace held a rally in Hanoi on the evening of the 5th against the use of atomic weapons, to support the call of the World Peace Conference for the prohibition of atomic weapons and the reduction of armaments.

Attending the meeting were Ton Duc Thang, Chairman of the National Committee of the Vietnam National United Front, Le Dinh Tham, Chairman of the Vietnam Committee for the Defense of World Peace, and representatives of various political parties, organizations, and people from all walks of life in Vietnam, totaling 500 people.

At the meeting, Le Dinh Tham, on behalf of the Vietnamese peace delegation, reported on the process of participating in the Helsinki World Peace Conference. Dr. Pham Khac Quang, a member of the Vietnamese peace delegation, reported on the discussion at the World Peace Conference regarding the reduction of armaments and the opposition to the use of atomic weapons.

Nguyen Xuan Tram, a representative of the Vietnam Committee for the Signature Campaign Against Atomic Weapons, gave a special report on "Continuing the Struggle Against Atomic Weapons."

The conference finally passed a resolution, unanimously and warmly supporting the World Peace Conference's call for the reduction of armaments and the prohibition of the use of atomic weapons, and responding to the World Peace Council's call to make August 6th of this year the International Day of Struggle for the reduction of armaments and the destruction of atomic weapons. The resolution also protested the United States' interference in Indochina, and demanded that the South Vietnamese authorities and the government of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam hold consultations to discuss free general elections and the unification of the motherland.

### 平壤和河内分别集会反对使用原子武器

1955-08-07
第4版()
专栏：

    平壤和河内分别集会反对使用原子武器
    新华社平壤六日电　据朝鲜中央通讯社讯：平壤市在五日晚举行反对原子武器和氢武器群众大会。
    朝鲜拥护和平全国民族委员会委员李克鲁在会上作报告。他分析了在利用原子能问题上的两种相反态度，并且对于苏联在这个问题上的态度和它所作的不懈努力表示支持。
    他强调指出，人民都要求使将给人类带来可怕的灾害的原子战争破坏不再在地球上发生。他说，朝鲜人民有充分的决心来积极参加世界性的反对原子武器和氢武器的斗争。
    接着，工人、传教士、教员、科学家和学生相继发言。他们强调指出原子能必须为人类的繁荣和幸福服务。他们祝广岛会议成功。
    大会在暴风雨般的掌声中通过了决议和致广岛会议的贺电。大会也通过了一封抗议日本政府拒绝出席广岛会议的朝鲜代表进入日本的信。
    新华社河内六日电　越南保卫世界和平委员会五日晚在河内举行反对使用原子武器大会，来支持世界和平大会关于要求禁止原子武器和裁减军备的号召。
    出席大会的有越南国民联合战线全国委员会主席孙德胜，越南保卫世界和平委员会主席黎庭探，以及越南各政党、团体和各阶层人民的代表共五百人。
    会上，黎庭探代表越南和平代表团报告了参加赫尔辛基世界和平大会的经过。越南和平代表团团员范克广博士报告了世界和平大会讨论要求裁减军备、反对使用原子武器问题的情况。
    越南反对原子武器签名运动委员会代表阮春针作了关于“继续为反对原子武器而斗争”的专题报告。
    大会最后通过了决议，一致热烈拥护世界和平大会关于裁减军备和禁止使用原子武器的呼吁，响应世界和平理事会关于把今年八月六日作为要求裁减军备和销毁原子武器的国际斗争日的号召。决议还抗议美国干涉印度支那，要求南越当局和越南民主共和国政府举行协商会议，讨论自由普选和统一祖国的问题。
