Title: Nepalese Foreign Minister Hosts Chinese Ambassador Yuan Zhongxian; Yuan Zhongxian Hosts Nepalese King Mahendra
Author:
Date: 1955-08-07
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, New Delhi, 6th – The Nepalese Foreign Minister, Sohrag Jang Thapa, held a banquet at the Lion Palace on the 3rd, under the orders of the King, to entertain Yuan Zhongxian, the Ambassador of the People's Republic of China to Nepal.

Those attending the banquet included King Mahendra, Prince Himalaya, the King’s Chief Advisor Ganji Man Singh, Chief Justice Hari Prasad Pradhan, Commander-in-Chief of the Army General Kiran Shamsher, as well as other senior civil and military officials.

Also present at the banquet were former Prime Minister Koirala, former ministers Regmi and Tanka Prasad Acharya, and prominent social figures.

Indian Ambassador Sahay, members of the Indian Military Delegation, and the First Secretary of the British Embassy also attended the banquet.

At the banquet, more than two hundred guests celebrated the establishment of diplomatic relations between China and Nepal. The King and Ambassador Yuan Zhongxian exchanged congratulations.

Xinhua News Agency, New Delhi, 6th – Yuan Zhongxian, the Ambassador of the People's Republic of China to Nepal, held a banquet in Kathmandu on the 4th to entertain King Mahendra of Nepal.

Invited to the banquet were Prince Himalaya, the King’s Chief Advisor Ganji Man Singh, Commander-in-Chief of the Army Kiran Shamsher, Foreign Minister Sohrag Jang Thapa, members of the Nepalese government delegation who participated in the establishment of diplomatic relations between China and Nepal, and other officials. Indian Ambassador to Nepal, Sahay, also attended the banquet.

At the banquet, Ambassador Yuan Zhongxian proposed a toast to the King of Nepal. Commander-in-Chief of the Army Kiran Shamsher, on behalf of the King, proposed a toast to Chairman Mao Zedong. During the toasts, the military band played the Nepalese national anthem and the Chinese national anthem.

Prior to this, Ambassador Yuan Zhongxian also held a reception to entertain Nepalese government officials and prominent figures from all walks of life. The Indian Ambassador, members of the Indian Military Delegation, and officials from the British Embassy also attended the reception.

### 尼泊尔外交大臣招待我国大使袁仲贤  袁仲贤招待尼泊尔国王马亨德拉

1955-08-07
第1版()
专栏：

    尼泊尔外交大臣招待我国大使袁仲贤
    袁仲贤招待尼泊尔国王马亨德拉
    新华社新德里六日电　尼泊尔外交大臣索瓦格·詹格·塔帕三日奉国王之命在狮宫举行宴会，招待中华人民共和国驻尼泊尔大使袁仲贤。
    出席宴会的有国王马亨德拉，希马拉雅亲王，国王首席顾问甘吉曼·辛格，首席法官哈里·普拉萨德·普拉丹，陆军总司令基兰·沙姆谢尔将军以及其他高级文武官员。
    出席宴会的还有前首相柯伊拉腊、前大臣雷格米和坦卡·普拉萨德·阿查雅以及社会名流。
    印度大使萨海、印度军事代表团人员和英国大使馆一等秘书也出席了宴会。
    在宴会上，宾主二百多人共同庆贺中尼两国建立外交关系。国王和袁仲贤大使并互致祝贺。
    新华社新德里六日电　中华人民共和国驻尼泊尔大使袁仲贤四日在加德满都举行宴会，招待尼泊尔国王马亨德拉。
    应邀出席宴会的还有希马拉雅亲王、国王首席顾问甘吉曼·辛格、陆军总司令基兰·沙姆谢尔、外交大臣索瓦格·詹格·塔帕以及参加中尼两国建立外交关系谈判的尼泊尔政府代表团人员和其他官员。印度驻尼泊尔大使萨海也出席了宴会。
    在宴会上，袁仲贤大使建议为尼泊尔国王干杯。陆军总司令基兰·沙姆谢尔奉国王之命建议为毛泽东主席干杯。干杯时，军乐队奏起了尼泊尔国歌和中国国歌。
    在此以前，袁仲贤大使还举行了招待会，招待尼泊尔政府官员和社会各界著名人士。印度大使、印度军事代表团人员及英国大使馆官员也出席了招待会。
