Title: Czechoslovak Government Decides to Reduce Army by 34,000 Before End of Year
Author:
Date: 1955-08-26
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency, Prague, August 25th: The Czechoslovak News Agency reported that the Government of the Czechoslovak Republic, at its government meeting on August 24th, passed the following resolution:

　　The Government of the Czechoslovak Republic, in view of the fact that the results of the Geneva Conference of the Heads of Government of the Four Powers and recent developments have promoted and are promoting a clear improvement in the international situation, and taking into account the need to further ease international tensions and build trust between countries, has decided to reduce the size of the Czechoslovak army by 34,000 troops before December 28, 1955.

### 捷政府决定今年年底前裁军三万四千人

1955-08-26
第4版()
专栏：

　　捷政府决定今年年底前裁军三万四千人
    新华社布拉格二十五日电　据捷克斯洛伐克通讯社报道，捷克斯洛伐克共和国政府在八月二十四日的政府会议上通过了下列决定：
    捷克斯洛伐克共和国政府，鉴于四国政府首脑日内瓦会议的结果以及最近的事态发展已经促进了并且正在促进国际局势的明确改善这一事实，并考虑到进一步缓和国际紧张局势和建立国与国之间的信任的需要，决定在一九五五年十二月二十八日以前把捷克斯洛伐克军队的兵力裁减三万四千人。
