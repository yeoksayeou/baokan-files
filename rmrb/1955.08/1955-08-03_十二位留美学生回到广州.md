Title: Twelve Students Returning from the U.S. Arrive in Guangzhou
Author:
Date: 1955-08-03
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Guangzhou, August 2nd – Twelve students returning from the United States, including Shao Shibin and Xie Jialin, arrived in Guangzhou by train from Shenzhen at 6:30 PM on the afternoon of the 2nd. The Guangzhou Municipal Reception Committee for Returned Students from Abroad sent personnel to Shenzhen to receive them and provided them with proper care.

### 十二位留美学生回到广州

1955-08-03
第1版()
专栏：

    十二位留美学生回到广州
    新华社广州二日电　由美国回国学生，邵士斌、谢家麟等十二人，在二日下午六时三十分自深圳乘火车抵达广州。广州市回国留学生接待委员会曾派员前往深圳接待，并给予妥善的照顾。
