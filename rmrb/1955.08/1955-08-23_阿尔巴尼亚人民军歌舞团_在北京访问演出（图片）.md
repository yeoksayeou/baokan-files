Title: Albanian People's Army Song and Dance Ensemble Visits and Performs in Beijing (Pictures)
Author:
Date: 1955-08-23
Page: 5

The Albanian People's Army Song and Dance Ensemble held a series of performances in Beijing. The many beautiful and moving artistic programs performed by the ensemble were repeatedly warmly welcomed by the audience. The successful performances of these song and dance programs have enabled the Chinese people to further understand the glorious revolutionary traditions and revolutionary optimism of the Albanian people, and further enhanced the deep friendship between the people of China and Albania.

The photos on this page, unless otherwise credited, are taken by Wang Zhiyuan of this newspaper.

	Chorus: March of the First Division
	Composer: Dora Leka
	Conductor: Gajo Avrazi
	The First Division of the Albanian People's Army waged a fierce struggle against the fascist invaders and occupies a very important place in the glorious history of the liberation struggle. Below is the final section of this march:
	"Charge! Charge! Charge into
	the raging flames of war!
	Everywhere, the flag of freedom
	is rising,
	Bringing light to the motherland,
	Opening the way for the heroic
	people."

	South Albanian Suite
	This is a suite of folk dances from southern Albania. First, young girls enter with gentle steps, dancing lyrically; then, young men also enter, and the dance movements gradually become healthy and powerful.
	In this dance, we can see the beauty of the costumes, the diversity of the music, and the coordination of the movements. (Photo by Wu Fuqiang)

	Mogitka Tribe Suite
	In the past, the feudal class of the Mogitka tribe cruelly oppressed the people, but the people always yearned for freedom, loved their motherland, and always showed strong hatred for the oppressors. This dance expresses the masses' brave and unyielding struggle against the feudal class to maintain their marriage customs. (Photo by Qi Guanshan, Xinhua News Agency reporter)

	Female Solo: Spinning Cotton (Chinese folk song)
	Soloist: Maria Kraja, actress of the Albanian National Conservatory of Music
	Piano accompaniment: Ljora Çoka
	Male Solo: Selection from the opera "Othello"
	Soloist: Johan Adanas, winner of the international competition at the World Youth Festival
	Piano accompaniment: Ljora Çoka

	Duet: I truly fell in love with you (Albanian folk song)
	Performers: Maria Kraja, actress of the Albanian National Conservatory of Music, and Avni Mula, winner of the international competition at the World Youth Festival

	Tirana Suite
	This dance is composed of a combination of lyrical and beautiful Tirana dances, expressing the joyful and happy mood of young people when they get married. The costumes in the dance show the artistic talent of the Albanian people, and the rich treasures of Albanian folk art creation can be seen from these dances. This is a close-up scene from the dance.

	A scene of the Albanian People's Army Song and Dance Ensemble performing at the Tianqiao Theater.

### 阿尔巴尼亚人民军歌舞团　在北京访问演出（图片）

1955-08-23
第5版()
专栏：

    阿尔巴尼亚人民军歌舞团
　　在北京访问演出
    阿尔巴尼亚人民军歌舞团在北京举行了访问演出。歌舞团表演的许多优美动人的艺术节目，都一再地受到观众的热烈欢迎。这些歌舞节目的成功演出，使中国人民进一步认识了阿尔巴尼亚人民光荣的革命传统和革命的乐观主义精神，进一步增强了中、阿两国人民的深厚友谊。
                     本版照片除署名的以外都是本报王志渊照的
    合唱　第一师进行曲
    作曲：多拉·列卡
    指挥：加乔·阿夫拉齐
    阿尔巴尼亚人民军第一师同法西斯侵略者进行了残酷的斗争，在解放斗争的光荣史上占有很重要的地位。下面就是这一进行曲的最后一段：
    “冲击！冲击！冲向弥
      漫的战火！
    到处，自由的旗帜在升
      起，
    给祖国带来光明，
    给英雄的人民开辟道
      路。”
    南阿尔巴尼亚组舞
    这是一组阿尔巴尼亚南部的民间舞蹈。首先是少女们以柔美的步伐跳着抒情的舞蹈出场；而后，青年小伙子们也出场了，舞蹈的动作就逐渐显得健康而有力。
    在这一舞蹈中，我们可以看到服装的优美，音乐的多样性和动作的协调一致。　（伍福强摄）
    莫吉特卡族组舞
    过去，莫吉特卡族的封建阶级对人民的压迫是很残酷的，但是，人民永远渴望自由，热爱祖国，对压迫者总是表现着强烈的仇恨。在这个舞蹈中，表现出人民群众为维护自己的婚姻习惯，勇敢地向封建阶级进行了刚毅不屈的斗争。
  （新华社记者齐观山摄）
    女声独唱　纺棉花（中国民歌）
    独唱者：阿尔巴尼亚国立音乐院演员玛丽亚·克拉亚　钢琴伴奏：廖拉·乔卡男声独唱　歌剧“奥赛罗”选曲独唱者：世界青年联欢节国际竞赛获奖者约翰·阿达纳斯　钢琴伴奏：廖拉·乔卡
    二重唱　我真心地爱上了你（阿尔巴尼亚民歌）
    演唱者：阿尔巴尼亚国立音乐院演员玛丽亚·克拉亚和世界青年联欢节国际竞赛获奖者阿夫尼·穆利亚
    地拉那组舞
    这个舞蹈是用一些抒情而美丽的地拉那舞组合而成的，表现了青年们结婚时欢乐而愉快的心情。舞蹈中的服装显示了阿尔巴尼亚人民的艺术才能，从这些舞蹈中可以看到阿尔巴尼亚民间艺术创作的丰富宝藏。这是舞蹈中的一个特写场面。
    阿尔巴尼亚人民军歌舞团在天桥剧场演出时的场面。
