Title: Egyptian Trade Delegation Head Nuseir Holds Reception

Author:
Date: 1955-08-24
Page: 1

　　Xinhua News Agency, August 23rd: The head of the Egyptian Republic Government Trade Delegation, Egyptian Minister of Industry and Commerce, Mohamed Abu Nuseir, held a banquet in Beijing at noon on the 23rd to entertain the Chinese representatives participating in the Sino-Egyptian trade negotiations.

　　Invited to attend were the Chinese representatives participating in the Sino-Egyptian trade negotiations: Minister of Foreign Trade Ye Jizhuang, Vice Minister Lei Renmin, and the heads of relevant bureaus and specialized companies of the Ministry of Foreign Trade.

　　Vice Minister of Foreign Affairs Zhang Hanfu, and Director of the Department of West European and African Affairs of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs, Huang Hua, were also invited to attend the banquet.

　　All members and advisors of the Egyptian Republic Government Trade Delegation attended the banquet.

　　In the evening, the delegation's advisors, Ahmed Fouad and Ismail Shafer, representatives of the Misr Bank and Misr Cotton Export Company of Egypt, held a banquet to entertain people from Chinese economic, trade, and religious circles.

### 埃及贸易代表团团长努赛尔举行招待宴会

1955-08-24
第1版()
专栏：

　　埃及贸易代表团团长努赛尔举行招待宴会
    新华社二十三日讯　埃及共和国政府贸易代表团团长、埃及工商部部长穆罕默德·阿卜·努赛尔，二十三日中午在北京举行宴会招待参加中埃两国贸易谈判的中国方面的代表。
    应邀出席的有中国参加这次中埃两国贸易谈判的代表：对外贸易部部长叶季壮、副部长雷任民，以及对外贸易部各有关局和专业公司的负责人。
    外交部副部长章汉夫、西欧非洲司司长黄华也应邀出席了宴会。
    出席宴会的有埃及共和国政府贸易代表团全体团员和顾问。
    晚间，代表团顾问、埃及密斯尔银行及密斯尔棉花出口公司的代表艾哈迈德·福阿德和伊斯梅尔·沙费尔举行了宴会，招待中国经济贸易界和宗教界人士等。
