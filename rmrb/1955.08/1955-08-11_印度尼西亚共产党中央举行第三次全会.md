Title: Third Plenary Session of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of Indonesia Held
Author:
Date: 1955-08-11
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency, Jakarta, August 10th: The Indonesian "People's Daily" published a communique issued by the Secretariat of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of Indonesia on the 9th.

The communique stated that the Central Committee of the Communist Party of Indonesia, at its Third Plenary Session on August 7th, discussed the resolution of the First National Congress of the Communist Party of Indonesia on the preparations for the general election and the resolution passed by the Political Bureau of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of Indonesia on June 24th regarding the demand to establish a national united government through general elections.

The Central Committee also adopted General Secretary Aidit's report "Striving for the Victory of the National Front in the General Election" at this plenary session.

In his report, Aidit emphasized the importance of striving for the victory of the national front in the general election and demanded the prevention of the establishment of a reactionary cabinet to replace the Ali-Arifin cabinet, which resigned on July 24th. Aidit also discussed the issue of carrying out criticism within the party and conducting communist education for party members.

At this plenary session, the Central Committee also accepted three new Central Committee members elected by the First National Congress of the Communist Party of Indonesia: Zhu Yiduo, S. Utayo, and Ruslan Kamarudin.

### 印度尼西亚共产党中央举行第三次全会

1955-08-11
第4版()
专栏：

    印度尼西亚共产党中央举行第三次全会
    新华社雅加达十日电　印度尼西亚“人民日报”九日刊载了印度尼西亚共产党中央委员会书记处发表的公报。
    公报说，印度尼西亚共产党中央委员会在八月七日的第三次全体会议上讨论了印度尼西亚共产党第一次全国代表会议关于普选的准备工作的决议和印度尼西亚共产党中央委员会政治局在六月二十四日通过的关于要求通过普选建立民族联合政府的决议。
    中央委员会在这次全体会议上还通过了总书记艾地的“为争取普选中民族阵线的胜利而努力”的报告。
    艾地在他的报告中强调指出争取民族阵线在普选中的胜利的重要性，并要求防止成立一个反动内阁来接替在七月二十四日辞职的阿里—阿里芬内阁。艾地并且论述了在党内开展批评和对党员进行共产主义教育问题。
    中央委员会在这次全体会议上还接纳了由印度尼西亚共产党第一次全国代表会议选出的三名新中央委员：朱义多，斯·乌塔约和鲁斯兰·卡马鲁丁。
