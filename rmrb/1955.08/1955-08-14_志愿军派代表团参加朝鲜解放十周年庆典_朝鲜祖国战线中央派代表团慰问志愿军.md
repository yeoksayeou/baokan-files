Title: Volunteer Army Sends Delegation to Participate in the 10th Anniversary of Korean Liberation Celebration; Central Committee of the Korean Fatherland Front Sends Delegation to Console the Volunteer Army
Author:
Date: 1955-08-14
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Pyongyang, August 13th: Upon the invitation of the Government of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea to participate in the "August 15th" 10th Anniversary Celebration of Korean Liberation, the Chinese People's Volunteer Army delegation of more than 200 people, led by General Yang Yong, Commander of the Chinese People's Volunteer Army, departed for Pyongyang on the 13th.

The Volunteer Army delegation includes commanders, political work cadres, meritorious heroes, model workers, and literary and sports workers from the troops.

Xinhua News Agency, Pyongyang, August 13th: On the eve of the "August 15th" 10th Anniversary of Korean Liberation, the Central Committee of the Democratic Front for the Reunification of the Fatherland of Korea dispatched a delegation headed by Minister of Commerce Yun Gong-heum to the leading organs of the Chinese People's Volunteer Army to express their condolences.

The leading organs of the Volunteer Army held a welcoming meeting for the delegation on the evening of the 12th. Present at the meeting were Yun Gong-heum and all members of the delegation, Yang Yong, Commander of the Chinese People's Volunteer Army, Wang Yunrui, Deputy Chief of Staff, Zhang Nansheng, Deputy Director of the Political Department, and more than 600 cadres and soldiers from the Volunteer Army's directly subordinate organs and troops.

Yun Gong-heum first delivered a message of condolence at the meeting. He said that while celebrating the anniversary of the great Soviet army's liberation of Korea, the Korean people also extend their heartfelt gratitude and high respect to the heroic Chinese People's Volunteer Army, which aided the just Fatherland Liberation War of Korea with blood and steadfastly defended the peace frontline after the war. He said that the collective heroism and high spirit of sacrifice displayed by the Chinese People's Volunteer Army in the Korean Liberation War are a model of internationalism. They will always be cherished and respected by the Korean people and will be passed down to future generations.

Subsequently, Yun Gong-heum, on behalf of the Central Committee of the Democratic Front for the Reunification of the Fatherland of Korea, presented the Chinese People's Volunteer Army with a banner, as well as a large number of consolation items such as pencils, commemorative albums, cigarettes, and toothbrushes.

Zhang Nansheng, Deputy Director of the Political Department of the Chinese People's Volunteer Army, delivered a reply at the meeting. He first expressed congratulations and gratitude to the heroic Korean people on behalf of all commanders and fighters of the Chinese People's Volunteer Army. He said that the Chinese People's Volunteer Army will continue to maintain a high degree of vigilance, and together with the Korean People's Army, safeguard the peace of Korea and the security of China, resolutely uphold the implementation of the Korean Armistice Agreement, strive for a peaceful settlement of the Korean issue, consolidate and develop the militant friendship between the people of China and Korea, and fight for the defense of peace in Asia and the world.

After the meeting, the Volunteer Army Political Department's Song and Dance Troupe and Peking Opera Troupe gave performances for the delegation.

### 志愿军派代表团参加朝鲜解放十周年庆典  朝鲜祖国战线中央派代表团慰问志愿军

1955-08-14
第1版()
专栏：

    志愿军派代表团参加朝鲜解放十周年庆典
    朝鲜祖国战线中央派代表团慰问志愿军
    新华社平壤十三日电　应朝鲜民主主义人民共和国政府邀请，参加“八·一五”朝鲜解放十周年庆祝典礼的中国人民志愿军代表团二百多人，在中国人民志愿军司令员杨勇将军率领下，于十三日动身赴平壤。
    志愿军代表团中有指挥员、政治工作干部、功臣、模范、部队文艺工作者和体育工作者。
    新华社平壤十三日电　“八·一五”朝鲜解放十周年纪念日前夕，朝鲜祖国统一民主主义战线中央委员会派出了以商业相尹公钦为首的代表团，到中国人民志愿军领导机关进行慰问。
    志愿军领导机关在十二日晚间举行了欢迎代表团的大会。出席大会的有尹公钦和代表团全体团员，中国人民志愿军司令员杨勇、副参谋长王蕴瑞、政治部副主任张南生和志愿军直属机关、部队的干部和战士共六百多人。
    尹公钦首先在会上致慰问词。他说，朝鲜人民在欢庆伟大苏联军队解放朝鲜的纪念节日的时候，同样，要对以鲜血援助了朝鲜正义的祖国解放战争、在战后又坚强地保卫着和平防线的英雄的中国人民志愿军致以衷心的感谢和崇高的敬意，他说，中国人民志愿军在朝鲜解放战争中所发挥的集体英雄主义和高度的牺牲精神，是国际主义的榜样，他们将永远受到朝鲜人民的爱护和尊敬，并将被传颂给子孙万代。
    接着，尹公钦代表朝鲜祖国统一民主主义战线中央委员会向中国人民志愿军赠送了锦旗，以及铅笔、纪念册、纸烟、牙刷等大批慰问品。
    中国人民志愿军政治部副主任张南生在会上致答词。他首先代表中国人民志愿军全体指战员向英雄的朝鲜人民表示祝贺和感谢。他说，中国人民志愿军将继续以高度的警惕性，同朝鲜人民军并肩捍卫朝鲜的和平与中国的安全，坚决维护朝鲜停战协定的实施，为争取朝鲜问题和平解决、为巩固和发展朝中两国人民的战斗友谊、为保卫亚洲和世界和平而奋斗。
    大会结束后，志愿军政治部文工团和京剧团为代表团作了表演。
