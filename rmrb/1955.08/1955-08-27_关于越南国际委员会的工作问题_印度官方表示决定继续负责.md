Title: On the Work of the International Commission for Vietnam: Indian Official States Decision to Continue Responsibility
Author:
Date: 1955-08-27
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, New Delhi, August 26th: Delhi newspapers reported on the 26th that an Indian official spokesperson said: "The Indian government has at no time considered withdrawing its responsibility as chairman of the International Supervisory and Control Commission for Vietnam." The spokesperson said that, despite difficulties in holding preliminary consultations for the Vietnamese elections next year, India will continue its efforts "to change the attitude of South Vietnam."

### 关于越南国际委员会的工作问题　印度官方表示决定继续负责

1955-08-27
第4版()
专栏：

　　关于越南国际委员会的工作问题
　　印度官方表示决定继续负责
    新华社新德里二十六日电　德里报纸二十六日报道，印度官方发言人说：“印度政府在任何时候都没有考虑撤消它作为越南国际监督和监察委员会主席的责任的问题。”发言人说，尽管在为明年的越南选举举行预备性的协商方面有困难，但是印度将继续进行它的努力“来使南越改变态度”。
