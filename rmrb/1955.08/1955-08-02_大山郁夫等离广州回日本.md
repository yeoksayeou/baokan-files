Title: Oyama Ikuo and Others Leave Guangzhou for Japan
Author:
Date: 1955-08-02
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, Guangzhou, 1st. Ikuo Oyama and two other Japanese peace activists, invited to China by the Chinese People's Committee for World Peace, and the first group of nine members of the Japanese peace delegation headed by Okinoshin Tanaka and Jintaro Taketani, arrived in Guangzhou on July 30th and 31st, respectively, and left Guangzhou for their home country on the morning of the 1st.

### 大山郁夫等离广州回日本

1955-08-02
第1版()
专栏：

    大山郁夫等离广州回日本
    新华社广州一日电　应中国人民保卫世界和平委员会邀请来中国访问的日本和平人士大山郁夫等三人，和以田中织之进、武谷甚太郎两人为首的日本和平代表团首批九人，分别在七月三十日、三十一日到达广州，于一日上午离广州回国。
