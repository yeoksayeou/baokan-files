Title: China and the United States Ambassadors Hold Fourth Meeting and Issue Communiqué
Author:
Date: 1955-08-10
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, Geneva, 9th—The ambassadors of the People's Republic of China and the United States of America held their fourth meeting at 10:00 AM (local time) on the 8th at the Palais des Nations. The meeting adjourned at 12:30 PM. The next meeting is scheduled for Wednesday (the 10th). A mutually agreed communiqué was released after the meeting as follows: The two ambassadors held a meeting today and continued discussions on the first item on the agenda. The two sides agreed that they will hold another meeting at 10:00 AM on August 10th.
    In order to correct possible misunderstandings about the current talks, the two sides agreed to publish the following account of the discussions to date:
    Apart from Mr. Wang (Bingnan)'s announcement of the release of eleven airmen, the negotiations to date have been limited to the issue of the repatriation of civilians from both sides.
    The two ambassadors have exchanged views on all aspects of this issue. The two ambassadors agreed that, in order to seriously seek to resolve the issues they are authorized to discuss, no public statements should be made about the proceedings of each meeting without mutual consent or prior notification by one party to the other.

### 中美两国大使举行第四次会谈并发表公报

1955-08-10
第1版()
专栏：

    中美两国大使举行第四次会谈并发表公报
    新华社日内瓦九日电　中华人民共和国和美利坚合众国两国大使会谈八日上午十时（当地时间）在国联大厦举行第四次会议。会议在十二点三十分休会。下次会议定于星期三（十日）举行。会后发表了一个双方同意的公报如下：两国大使今天举行会议，继续讨论议程上的第一个项目。双方商定他们将在八月十日上午十时再举行会议。
    为了纠正对目前的会谈可能存在的误解，双方商定发表到今天为止的讨论情况如下：
    除了王（炳南）先生宣布释放十一名空军人员的第一个声明外，到今天为止，谈判始终限于双方平民各回本国的问题。
    两国大使已经就这个问题的各个方面交换了意见。两国大使商定，为了认真地设法解决他们受权讨论的问题起见，非经相互同意或事先由一方通知另一方，不得对每次会议的情况发表任何公开声明。
