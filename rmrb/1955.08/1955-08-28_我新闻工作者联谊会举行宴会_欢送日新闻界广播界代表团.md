Title: All-China Journalists Association Holds Banquet to See Off Japanese Press and Broadcasting Delegation
Author:
Date: 1955-08-28
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, August 27th. The All-China Journalists Association held a banquet on the evening of the 27th to bid farewell to the Japanese press and broadcasting delegation, which is about to leave Beijing after their visit.

	Wu Lengxi, Vice President of the All-China Journalists Association, delivered a speech at the banquet. He expressed hope that the delegation, upon returning to Japan, would introduce the true situation of peaceful construction in New China to the Japanese people and convey the Chinese people's desire to promote the normalization of relations between China and Japan, especially to strengthen friendly cooperation between the two peoples.

	He said that extensive contact and communication between the press and broadcasting circles of China and Japan are of great help in deepening mutual understanding and friendly cooperation. He expressed hope that such contact and communication could be further strengthened.

	Subsequently, Minoru Yokota, head of the Japanese press and broadcasting delegation, spoke and expressed his gratitude to the All-China Journalists Association for their warm hospitality. He said that before coming to China, they were prepared to listen and see as much of the facts as possible in order to deepen their understanding of New China, and now they have achieved this goal. He said that the delegation learned a lot in China. He believes that China is a newly reborn country.

	Yokota Minoru also said that he also felt that the normalization of relations between China and Japan should be promoted, and the delegation would "definitely take up the pen and work hard for it" after returning home. He also hoped that the contact, friendly cooperation and mutual exchanges between the press and broadcasting circles of China and Japan would be further developed.

	At the banquet, the All-China Journalists Association also presented gifts to the delegation.

	Among those present at the banquet tonight were Wang Yunsheng, Vice President of the All-China Journalists Association; Xu Huang, Deputy Director of the Information Department of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs; Hu Jiwei, Deputy Editor-in-Chief of the People's Daily; Wen Jize, Deputy Director of the Broadcasting Administration; Liu Zunqi, Editor-in-Chief of People's China; Chen Hansheng, Editor of China Reconstructs; and other heads of newspapers, periodicals, news agencies and radio stations in Beijing. ### 我新闻工作者联谊会举行宴会　欢送日新闻界广播界代表团

1955-08-28
第1版()
专栏：

　　我新闻工作者联谊会举行宴会
　　欢送日新闻界广播界代表团
    新华社二十七日讯　中国新闻工作者联谊会在二十七日晚举行宴会，欢送即将离开北京的日本新闻界广播界访华代表团。
    中国新闻工作者联谊会副会长吴冷西在会上致词。他希望代表团回国后向日本人民介绍新中国和平建设的真实情况，并把中国人民希望促进中日两国关系正常化，特别是希望加强两国人民间的友好合作的愿望转达给日本人民。
    他说，中日两国新闻界广播界间的广泛联系和接触，对加深彼此的了解和友好合作都有很大帮助。他希望这种联系和接触能继续加强。
    接着，日本新闻界广播界访华代表团团长横田实讲话，对中国新闻工作者联谊会的热情招待表示谢意。他说，我们来中国前就准备尽可能多听听、多看看事实，以加深对新中国的认识，现在我们这个目的是达到了。他说，代表团在中国学到很多东西。他认为中国是一个重新诞生的新国家。
    横田实又说，他同样感到应促进中日两国关系正常化，代表团回国后“一定拿起笔杆为它努力”。他还希望中日两国新闻界广播界间的联系、友好合作和相互往来得到进一步发展。
    会上，中国新闻工作者联谊会还向代表团赠送了礼品。
    出席今晚宴会的，有中国新闻工作者联谊会副会长王芸生，外交部新闻司副司长徐晃，人民日报副总编辑胡绩伟，广播事业局副局长温济泽，人民中国总编辑刘尊棋；中国建设主编陈翰笙以及北京其他各报刊和通讯社、广播电台的负责人。（附图片）
