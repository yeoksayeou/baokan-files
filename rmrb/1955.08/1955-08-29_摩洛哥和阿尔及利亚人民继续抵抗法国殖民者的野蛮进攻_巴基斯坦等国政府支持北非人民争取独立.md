Title: The People of Morocco and Algeria Continue to Resist the Savage Attacks of French Colonialists; Governments of Pakistan and Other Countries Support the North African People's Struggle for Independence
Author:
Date: 1955-08-29
Page: 4

　　Xinhua News Agency, Casablanca, August 28th: French colonial troops in Morocco continued their military suppression in the Atlas Mountains on the 27th. According to a United Press report, French jet fighters continuously flew low over the mountains to search, and thousands of French troops equipped with tanks and field guns participated in the military operation to encircle and massacre the Berber tribes. French colonial troops burned the camps of the herders in vast areas to the ground. According to the incomplete figures revealed so far, French troops have killed more than seven hundred Berbers in recent days.
　　Moroccan patriots continue to stubbornly resist the savage attacks of the French army. Despite the threats from Franchi, the French military governor of Casablanca who commands the attacking troops, to "face severe punishment" if they do not lay down their arms, thousands of Berber tribal warriors continue to hide in the deep mountains and fight back against their enemies with old rifles.
　　In Algeria, the armed resistance movement of the local people is still unfolding. On the 27th, Algerian armed people fought with the French colonial army in Ain Mlila and Canrobert, between Constantine and Batna. Due to the activity of the people's armed forces, the French colonial authorities announced on the 27th that the important Algerian port of Philippeville had entered a state of "alert."
　　Xinhua News Agency, New Delhi, August 28th: Karachi dispatch: Pakistani Prime Minister Chaudhri Muhammad Ali told a delegation of Pakistanis on the 27th who demanded that the Pakistani government take action on the Moroccan and Algerian issues that the sympathy of the Pakistani government and people is entirely on the side of Morocco and Algeria. He said, "We hope to see the people of Morocco get an opportunity as soon as possible. We have supported their cause in the past and will continue to support their cause in the future." He called on France to "allow the people of Morocco and Algeria to realize their national aspirations."
　　Chaudhri Muhammad Ali said that the Pakistani representative to the United Nations has been instructed to ensure that the issues of Morocco and Algeria are included on the agenda of the United Nations General Assembly.
　　Xinhua News Agency, Jakarta, August 27th: The Indonesian Ministry of Foreign Affairs announced on the 27th that Indonesian Foreign Minister Anak Agung Gde Agung had summoned Raymond Priorestroguernier, the Chargé d'Affaires ad interim of the French Embassy in Indonesia, two days ago and told him that Indonesia was closely monitoring the recent developments and increasingly tense situation in Morocco and Algeria.
　　Anak Agung Gde Agung also told him that Indonesia has always supported any movement of people striving for independence.

### 摩洛哥和阿尔及利亚人民继续抵抗法国殖民者的野蛮进攻　巴基斯坦等国政府支持北非人民争取独立

1955-08-29
第4版()
专栏：

　　摩洛哥和阿尔及利亚人民继续抵抗法国殖民者的野蛮进攻
　　巴基斯坦等国政府支持北非人民争取独立
    新华社二十八日讯　卡萨布兰卡消息：摩洛哥的法国殖民军队在亚特拉斯山区展开的军事镇压行动二十七日仍在继续中。据合众社报道，法军喷气式飞机在山区上空不断低飞盘旋进行搜索，配备有坦克和野炮的数千名法国军队参加了包围和屠杀柏伯尔部落人民的军事行动。法国殖民军队把广大地区中的牧民的营房烧得精光。根据目前透露出来的远不完全的数字，法军在最近几天中已经杀死了七百多个柏伯尔人。
    摩洛哥爱国者继续顽强地抵抗法军的野蛮进攻。尽管指挥进攻部队的法国驻卡萨布兰卡军事总督弗朗希发出威胁，恫吓他们如果不放下武器就要“面临严惩”，但是，好几千个柏伯尔部落的战士继续隐蔽在深山中，用陈旧的来福枪来还击他们的敌人。
    在阿尔及利亚，当地人民的武装抵抗运动仍在展开，二十七日，在君士坦丁和巴特那之间的艾因姆利拉以及坎雷伯两地，阿尔及利亚的武装人民和法国殖民军发生战斗。由于人民武装的活跃，法国殖民当局二十七日已经宣布阿尔及利亚重要港口菲利普维尔进入“警戒状态”。
    新华社新德里二十八日电　喀喇蚩消息：巴基斯坦总理乔德里·穆罕默德·阿里二十七日对要求巴基斯坦政府对摩洛哥和阿尔及利亚问题采取行动的巴基斯坦人代表团说，巴基斯坦政府和人民的同情完全在摩洛哥和阿尔及利亚方面。他说，“我们希望看到摩洛哥人民尽早得到机会。我们过去支持他们的事业，将来将继续支持他们的事业。”他呼吁法国“让摩洛哥和阿尔及利亚人民实现他们的民族愿望”。
    乔德里·穆罕默德·阿里说，已经指示巴基斯坦驻联合国代表一定要使摩洛哥和阿尔及利亚问题列入联合国大会议程。
    新华社雅加达二十七日电　印度尼西亚外交部二十七日宣布，印度尼西亚外交部长阿纳克·阿贡·格德·阿贡两天前曾召见法国驻印度尼西亚大使馆临时代办雷蒙·普里翁斯特罗格拜尼埃，并且告诉他说，印度尼西亚正密切注视摩洛哥和阿尔及利亚的最近发展和日益紧张的局势。
    阿纳克·阿贡·格德·阿贡还告诉他说，印度尼西亚一贯支持人民争取独立的任何运动。
