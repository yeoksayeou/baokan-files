Title: Premier Zhou Cables Chief Advisor to the King of Nepal to Congratulate on the Establishment of Diplomatic Relations Between China and Nepal
Author:
Date: 1955-08-03
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, August 2nd. Premier of the State Council of the People's Republic of China, Zhou Enlai, sent a cable to Sardar Gunjiman Singh, Chief Advisor to the King of Nepal, to congratulate him on the establishment of normal diplomatic relations between the People's Republic of China and the Kingdom of Nepal. The cable reads as follows:
Your Excellency Sardar Gunjiman Singh, Chief Advisor to the King of Nepal, Kathmandu:

	On the occasion of the establishment of normal diplomatic relations between the People's Republic of China and the Kingdom of Nepal, I would like to extend my warm congratulations to your government, people, and Your Excellency on behalf of the government and people of the People's Republic of China and in my own name. May the friendly relations between China and Nepal have new developments on the basis of the Five Principles of Peaceful Coexistence.

		Zhou Enlai, Premier of the State Council of the People's Republic of China
		August 2, 1955, Beijing

### 周总理电尼泊尔国王首席顾问贺中尼建交

1955-08-03
第1版()
专栏：

    周总理电尼泊尔国王首席顾问贺中尼建交
    新华社二日讯　中华人民共和国国务院总理周恩来致电尼泊尔国王首席顾问萨达尔·甘吉曼·辛格，祝贺中华人民共和国同尼泊尔王国建立正常外交关系。电文如下：加德满都尼泊尔国王首席顾问萨达尔·甘吉曼·辛格先生阁下：
    值此中华人民共和国同尼泊尔王国建立正常外交关系之际，我谨代表中华人民共和国政府和人民并以我个人名义向贵国政府、人民和阁下致热烈的祝贺。愿中尼两国的友好关系在和平共处五项原则的基础上有新的发展。
        中华人民共和国国务院总理　周恩来	一九五五年八月二日于北京
