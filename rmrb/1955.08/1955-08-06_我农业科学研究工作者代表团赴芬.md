Title: Chinese Agricultural Science Research Workers Delegation to Finland
Author: 
Date: 1955-08-06
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, 3rd. A Chinese agricultural science research workers delegation of five people, led by Ma Shi'an, vice president of Beijing Agricultural University, left Beijing by plane on the 3rd for Helsinki, having been invited to participate in the Sixth Meeting of the International Committee of Agricultural Tillage Science Research Workers and the Ninth Conference of International Agricultural Economists.

### 我农业科学研究工作者代表团赴芬

1955-08-06
第1版()
专栏：

    我农业科学研究工作者代表团赴芬
    新华社三日讯　应邀参加国际农业耕作科学研究工作者委员会第六次会议和国际农业经济学家第九届会议的中国农业科学研究工作者代表团一行五人，由北京农业大学副校长马适安率领，在三日由北京乘飞机前往赫尔辛基。
