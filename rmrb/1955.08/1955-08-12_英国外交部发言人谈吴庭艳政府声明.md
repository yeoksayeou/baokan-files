Title: British Foreign Office Spokesman Comments on Ngo Dinh Diem Government Statement
Author:
Date: 1955-08-12
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency, London, August 11th: A British Foreign Office spokesman said on the 10th, when asked about the South Vietnamese government's statement on the issue of general elections: "At first glance, this statement does not seem to offer any further contribution to the implementation of the Geneva Accords." The spokesman also said that the statement was "not very encouraging."

### 英国外交部发言人谈吴庭艳政府声明

1955-08-12
第4版()
专栏：

    英国外交部发言人谈吴庭艳政府声明
    新华社十一日讯　伦敦消息：英国外交部发言人十日在被问到关于南越政府就普选问题发表的声明时说：“初看起来，这个声明似乎对于日内瓦协定的履行没有提供任何进一步的贡献。”这个发言人还说：这个声明是“不很令人鼓舞的”。
