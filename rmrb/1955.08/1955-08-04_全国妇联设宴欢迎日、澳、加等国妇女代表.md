Title: All-China Women's Federation Hosts Banquet to Welcome Women Representatives from Japan, Australia, Canada, and Other Countries
Author:
Date: 1955-08-04
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, 3rd. The All-China Democratic Women's Federation held a banquet on the evening of the 3rd to welcome representatives from Japan, Australia, Canada, Chile, Bolivia, Colombia and other countries who are visiting China to attend the World Mothers' Congress.

The hosts at tonight's banquet included Li Dequan and Zhang Yun, Vice-Chairpersons of the All-China Democratic Women's Federation; Kang Keqing, Secretary-General of the Chinese People's National Committee for the Protection of Children; Zhang Jiexun, Secretary-General of the Beijing Democratic Women's Federation, as well as representatives from our country who returned from attending the World Mothers' Congress and women representatives from all walks of life in the capital.

At the banquet, Li Dequan delivered a welcoming speech to the guests.

In an atmosphere full of friendship, the guests and hosts constantly toasted the great achievements of the World Mothers' Congress, the safety and happiness of children, the new victories of the world peace cause, and the friendship between women and mothers of all countries.

### 全国妇联设宴欢迎日、澳、加等国妇女代表

1955-08-04
第1版()
专栏：

    全国妇联设宴欢迎日、澳、加等国妇女代表
    新华社三日讯　中华全国民主妇女联合会三日晚举行宴会，欢迎来我国访问的出席世界母亲大会的日本、澳大利亚、加拿大、智利、玻利维亚、哥伦比亚等国的代表。
    今晚宴会的主人有中华全国民主妇女联合会副主席李德全、章蕴，中国人民保卫儿童全国委员会秘书长康克清，北京市民主妇女联合会秘书长张洁珣，还有我国出席世界母亲大会归来的代表和首都各界妇女代表。
    在宴会上，李德全向客人致词表示欢迎。
    在充满友谊的空气中，宾主们不断为庆贺世界母亲大会的伟大成就，为孩子们的安全和幸福，为世界和平事业的新的胜利，为各国妇女和母亲之间的友谊而干杯。
