Title: Chinese Art Troupe Arrives in Prague for Performance Tour
Author:
Date: 1955-08-09
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency, Prague, 7th: The Chinese Art Troupe, led by its head Zhang Zhixiang, arrived in Prague by plane on the morning of August 7th. Among those welcoming the troupe at the airport were Ludvík, the Deputy Minister of Culture of Czechoslovakia, representatives from the Ministry of Foreign Affairs, and Cao Ying, the Chinese Ambassador to Czechoslovakia.

The art troupe is scheduled to begin performing on August 24th, but Prague residents have already started purchasing tickets enthusiastically.

Over the past few weeks, "Red Rights," "Free Speech," "Literary Gazette," "Agricultural News," "Prague Evening News," the Czechoslovak News Agency, and Prague radio stations have continuously published articles introducing the Chinese Art Troupe and the achievements it attained during its performances in France, Belgium, and the Netherlands. They have also introduced Peking Opera to readers and listeners. A report by the Czechoslovak News Agency pointed out that the Chinese Art Troupe's performances in Prague "will be a major event in the art world."

### 我国艺术团到达布拉格作访问演出

1955-08-09
第4版()
专栏：

    我国艺术团到达布拉格作访问演出
    新华社布拉格七日电　中国艺术团在团长张致祥的率领下在八月七日晨乘飞机抵达布拉格。到机场欢迎的有捷克斯洛伐克文化部副部长卢德维克、外交部代表及中国驻捷克斯洛伐克大使曹瑛等。
    艺术团定于八月二十四日开始演出，但布拉格居民已开始热烈地购票了。
    在过去几星期中，“红色权利报”、“自由谈报”、“文学报”、“农业报”、“布拉格晚报”、捷克斯洛伐克通讯社以及布拉格的广播电台，不断发表文章介绍中国艺术团和它在法国比利时荷兰演出时所获得的成就，并且向读者和听众介绍中国京剧。捷克斯洛伐克通讯社的报道指出，中国艺术团在布拉格的演出“将成为艺术界的一桩大事”。
