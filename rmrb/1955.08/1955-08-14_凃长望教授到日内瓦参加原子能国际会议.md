Title: Professor Tu Changwang Arrives in Geneva to Attend International Conference on Atomic Energy
Author:
Date: 1955-08-14
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency, Geneva, 13th - Professor Tu Changwang, Honorary Secretary and member of the Standing Committee of the World Federation of Scientific Workers, and Chinese meteorologist, arrived in Geneva by plane on the 11th. He is attending the International Conference on the Peaceful Uses of Atomic Energy here as an observer for the World Federation of Scientific Workers.

### 凃长望教授到日内瓦参加原子能国际会议

1955-08-14
第4版()
专栏：

    凃长望教授到日内瓦参加原子能国际会议
    新华社日内瓦十三日电　世界科学工作者协会名誉秘书和常务委员会委员、中国气象学家凃长望教授十一日乘飞机到达日内瓦。他以世界科学工作者协会观察员的身份，来参加正在这里举行的和平利用原子能国际会议。
