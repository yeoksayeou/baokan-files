Title: British Delegation to China Visits Various Places
Author:
Date: 1955-08-25
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, August 23rd—The British goodwill delegation to China, invited by Guo Moruo, President of the Chinese Academy of Sciences, to visit China, left Beijing on the 23rd in two groups to visit the Northeast and East China after a seven-day visit in Beijing.
    In addition to touring and visiting Beijing, the delegation met with Ji Chaoding, Secretary-General of the China Council for the Promotion of International Trade, Jin Zhifu, Chairman of the National Committee of the Coal Miners' Union, Deng Chenxi, Deputy Director of the All-China Federation of Supply and Marketing Cooperatives, Li Dequan, Vice-Chairman of the National Women's Federation, and Wu Yifang from religious circles, as well as many other well-known people from all walks of life, and had friendly conversations with each other.

### 英国访华代表团去各地访问

1955-08-25
第1版()
专栏：

　　英国访华代表团去各地访问
    据新华社二十三日讯　应中国科学院院长郭沫若邀请前来我国参观访问的英国访华友好代表团在北京进行了七天的参观访问后，二十三日分两批分别去东北和华东各地进行参观访问。
    代表团在北京游览和参观外，分别会见了我国国际贸易促进委员会秘书长冀朝鼎、煤矿工会全国委员会主席金直夫、全国供销合作总社副主任邓辰西，以及全国民主妇女联合会副主席李德全和宗教界吴贻芳等人，还和其他许多的各界知名人士见了面，彼此进行了友好的交谈。
