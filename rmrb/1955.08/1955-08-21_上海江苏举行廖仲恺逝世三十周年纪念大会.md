Title: Shanghai and Jiangsu Hold Commemorative Meetings on the 30th Anniversary of Liao Zhongkai's Death
Author:
Date: 1955-08-21
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, Shanghai, August 20th. The Shanghai Municipal Committee of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference (CPPCC) held a commemorative meeting on the morning of August 20th to mark the 30th anniversary of Mr. Liao Zhongkai's death. Participants included members of the Shanghai Municipal Committee of the CPPCC, members of the National Committee of the CPPCC in Shanghai, responsible persons from the Shanghai Municipal Committee of the Chinese Communist Party and local organizations of various democratic parties in Shanghai, and responsible persons from various people's organizations in Shanghai.

　　Liu Shuzhou, a member of the Shanghai Municipal Committee of the Chinese Communist Party, spoke at the meeting. He said: As we have now achieved revolutionary victory and are engaged in the great cause of socialist construction, we must remember the lessons of the assassination of Mr. Liao Zhongkai thirty years ago, and learn from the exposure of the Pan Hannian counter-revolutionary case and the criminal activities of the Hu Feng counter-revolutionary clique. We must heighten our vigilance, eliminate all counter-revolutionary elements, and defend socialist construction.

　　Zhao Zukang, Deputy Chairman of the Shanghai Municipal Committee of the Revolutionary Committee of the Chinese Kuomintang, recalled in his speech Mr. Liao Zhongkai's lifelong struggle for China's independence, freedom, and prosperity. He said: Today, as we commemorate the 30th anniversary of Mr. Liao Zhongkai's death, we must unite under the leadership of the Chinese Communist Party and Chairman Mao, sharpen our political acumen, learn to identify counter-revolutionary elements, and resolutely, thoroughly, cleanly, and completely eliminate all counter-revolutionary elements.

　　Xinhua News Agency, Nanjing, August 20th. On the morning of August 20th, people from all walks of life in Jiangsu Province and Nanjing City held a meeting to commemorate the 30th anniversary of Mr. Liao Zhongkai's death. Participants in the meeting included Hui Yuyu, Deputy Secretary of the Jiangsu Provincial Committee of the CPC and Governor of Jiangsu Province; Leng Yu and Ji Fang, Deputy Governors of Jiangsu Province; Ren Chonggao, convener of the preparatory committee of the Jiangsu Provincial Branch of the Revolutionary Committee of the Chinese Kuomintang; Peng Chong, First Deputy Secretary of the Nanjing Municipal Committee of the CPC and Mayor of Nanjing; responsible persons from local organizations of various democratic parties and people's organizations; and representatives from all walks of life, totaling more than eight hundred people.

　　Hui Yuyu and Ren Chonggao spoke at the meeting, introducing Mr. Liao Zhongkai's life and his deeds in the struggle for the Chinese democratic revolution. Their speeches also addressed the important role Mr. Liao Zhongkai played in promoting cooperation between the Kuomintang and the Communist Party, as well as Mr. Liao Zhongkai's great spirit of resolutely struggling to promote Dr. Sun Yat-sen's Three Great Policies of alliance with Russia, cooperation with the Communist Party, and assistance to peasants and workers.

　　After the meeting, members of the presidium of the conference laid flowers at Mr. Liao Zhongkai's tomb to express their condolences.

### 上海江苏举行廖仲恺逝世三十周年纪念大会

1955-08-21
第1版()
专栏：

　　上海江苏举行廖仲恺逝世三十周年纪念大会
    新华社上海二十日电　中国人民政治协商会议上海市委员会二十日上午举行廖仲恺先生逝世三十周年纪念大会。参加纪念大会的有中国人民政治协商会议上海市委员会委员、中国人民政治协商会议全国委员会在上海的委员、中国共产党上海市委员会和各民主党派上海市地方组织、上海市各人民团体负责人等。
    中国共产党上海市委员会委员刘述周在会上讲话。他说：当我们今天已经取得革命胜利、正在进行伟大的社会主义建设的时候，必须记取三十年前廖仲恺先生被刺事件的教训，并从揭发潘汉年反革命事件和胡风反革命集团的罪恶活动中吸取教训，提高警惕，肃清一切反革命分子，保卫社会主义建设。
    中国国民党革命委员会上海市委员会副主任委员赵祖康在讲话中追述了廖仲恺先生一生为争取中国的独立、自由、富强而奋斗的事迹。他说：我们今天纪念廖仲恺先生逝世三十周年，要在中国共产党和毛主席领导下团结起来，提高政治嗅觉，学会辨别反革命分子的本领，坚决、彻底、干净、全部地肃清一切反革命分子。
    新华社南京二十日电　二十日上午，江苏省和南京市各界人民开会纪念廖仲恺先生逝世三十周年。参加大会的有中共江苏省委员会副书记、江苏省省长惠浴宇，江苏省副省长冷遹、季方，中国国民党革命委员会江苏省分部筹备委员会召集人任崇高，中共南京市委员会第一副书记、南京市市长彭冲，各民主党派地方组织、各人民团体负责人及各界代表八百多人。
    惠浴宇和任崇高在会上讲话，介绍了廖仲恺先生的生平和他为中国民主革命斗争的事迹。他们的讲话还讲了廖仲恺先生在促进国共合作中所起的重要作用，以及廖仲恺先生坚决为推行孙中山先生的联俄、联共、扶助工农三大政策而斗争的伟大精神。
    会后，大会主席团人员前往廖仲恺先生墓地献花悼念。
