Title: Japanese People Enthusiastically Prepare for the World Conference Against Atomic and Hydrogen Bombs
Author:
Date: 1955-08-04
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Tokyo, 2nd. News: To welcome the upcoming World Conference Against Atomic and Hydrogen Bombs to be held in Hiroshima and other places, the public in various parts of Japan have successively elected representatives to attend the conference, and are enthusiastically raising funds and carrying out various preparatory activities for the conference.

　　Hiroshima City, the first city to be bombed by an American atomic bomb, is now actively engaged in a fundraising campaign. Many mass organizations have distributed pamphlets to citizens publicizing the significance of the World Conference. Citizens from all walks of life, including housewives and patients in hospitals, are enthusiastically donating money to support the convening of the conference. In Kure City, southwest of Hiroshima City, the mayor, councilors, and heads of economic organizations, trade unions, and women's groups have participated in fundraising activities. At the same time, they and the citizens are also preparing to hold a citizens' rally on August 5 to demand the prohibition of atomic weapons. On the morning of August 6, the tenth anniversary of the American atomic bombing of Hiroshima, all factories, temples, etc. in the city will sound their whistles or ring their bells together, and all citizens will silently commemorate the victims of the American atomic bombing.

　　In Nagasaki (the second city to be bombed by an American atomic bomb), many mass organizations have jointly formed a committee to prepare for the tenth anniversary of the atomic bombing, to select representatives to attend the World Conference, hold memorial services for the atomic bomb victims, and hold an exhibition of atomic bomb disasters. At the same time, they are also planning to build a peace statue more than 20 meters high to express their firm will to demand peace.

　　University professors, principals, councilors, and heads of mass organizations in Wakayama Prefecture recently issued a joint statement expressing their support for the convening of the World Conference Against Atomic and Hydrogen Bombs. They have decided to launch a prefecture-wide campaign to raise funds for the World Conference and select representatives, and to hold a prefecture-wide peace festival and a memorial service for the atomic bomb victims. In Nagano, Akita, Mie, Aomori, Miyagi and other prefectures, preparations for the World Conference are also being carried out enthusiastically. In addition, the people of Tokyo, Osaka, and Kanagawa Prefecture are actively preparing peace gatherings with rich cultural and entertainment programs to welcome and entertain foreign guests who come to Japan to participate in the World Conference.

### 日本人民热烈筹备禁止原子弹和氢弹世界大会

1955-08-04
第4版()
专栏：

    日本人民热烈筹备禁止原子弹和氢弹世界大会
    新华社二日讯　东京消息：为迎接即将在广岛等地举行的禁止原子弹和氢弹世界大会，日本全国各地公众陆续选出参加大会的代表，并且热烈地为大会募集基金和进行各项筹备活动。
    第一个遭受美国原子弹轰炸的城市——广岛市，现在正在积极地展开募捐运动。很多群众团体向市民发出了宣传世界大会意义的小册子，各阶层市民，包括家庭妇女和病院里的病人，都踊跃地捐款，支持大会的召开。在广岛市西南部的吴市，市长、议员和经济团体、工会、妇女团体的负责人等都参加了募捐活动。同时，他们和市民一起还在筹备于八月五日举行要求禁止原子武器的市民大会，在八月六日——美国用原子弹轰炸广岛十周年纪念日的早晨，全市工厂、寺庙等将一齐鸣笛或鸣钟，全体市民将一齐默念美国原子弹轰炸下的牺牲者。
    在长崎（遭受美国原子弹轰炸的第二个城市），许多群众团体已经共同组成了筹备纪念遭受原子弹轰炸十周年的委员会，来选派参加世界大会的代表、举行原子弹牺牲者的追悼会和举行原子弹灾害的展览会。同时，他们还正在筹建一个高二十多公尺的和平像，来表示他们要求和平的坚决意志。
    和歌山县的大学教授、校长、议员和群众团体的负责人们，最近曾发表了联合声明，表示支持召开禁止原子弹和氢弹世界大会。他们决定展开全县性的为世界大会筹集基金和选派代表的运动，并举行全县性的和平节和原子弹牺牲者的追悼会。在长野、秋田、三重、青森、宫城等县，世界大会的筹备活动也在热烈地进行中。另外，东京、大阪和神奈川县的人民，正在积极筹备包括有丰富的文娱节目的和平集会，来欢迎和招待前来日本参加世界大会的外宾。
