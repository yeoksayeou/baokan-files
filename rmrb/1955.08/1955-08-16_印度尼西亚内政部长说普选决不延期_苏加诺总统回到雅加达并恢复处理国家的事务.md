Title: Indonesian Interior Minister Says General Elections Will Not Be Postponed; President Sukarno Returns to Jakarta and Resumes Handling State Affairs
Author:
Date: 1955-08-16
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency, Jakarta, August 15th: Indonesian Interior Minister Sunarjo stated on the 12th that the new cabinet will not postpone the general elections. Indonesia's general elections are scheduled for September 29th. He said that the technical preparations have reached a very advanced stage, so the elections can be held within the stipulated period.

According to the "Indonesia News" report on the 15th, Mrs. Pujo Bonoto, Chairman of the Jakarta Committee of the Indonesian General Election Commission, issued a statement regarding rumors that the general election would be postponed, saying that there is no reason to postpone the general election. She said that most members of the central and local election committees belong to the parties participating in the current cabinet. Postponing the election would waste the millions of rupiahs already spent on preparatory work.

"Indonesia News" also published an editorial on the 15th, requesting the government to create favorable conditions so that the general election can be conducted in a democratic atmosphere, through democratic means without political pressure or an atmosphere of terror.

Jakarta's "People's Daily" reported on the 15th that Hartoyo, First Deputy Secretary of the Indonesian Farmers' Front, representing the farmers, expressed opposition to postponing the date of the general election.

Xinhua News Agency, Jakarta, August 15th: According to the Indonesian News Agency, Indonesian President Sukarno resumed handling state affairs on the 15th. President Sukarno had been on vacation preparing his speech for Indonesian Independence Day (August 17th) after returning from a pilgrimage to Mecca on August 5th. President Sukarno returned to Jakarta from his Bogor villa on the afternoon of the 15th.

### 印度尼西亚内政部长说普选决不延期  苏加诺总统回到雅加达并恢复处理国家的事务

1955-08-16
第4版()
专栏：

    印度尼西亚内政部长说普选决不延期
    苏加诺总统回到雅加达并恢复处理国家的事务
    新华社雅加达十五日电  印度尼西亚内政部长苏纳里约在十二日发表声明说，新内阁决不会推迟普选。印度尼西亚的普选规定在九月二十九日举行。他说，技术方面的准备工作已进行到了很高的阶段，所以选举可以在规定的时期内举行。
    据“印度尼西亚新闻”十五日报道，印度尼西亚普选委员会雅加达委员会主席普佐邦托罗夫人就传闻普选将推迟一事发表声明说，没有理由推迟普选。她说，中央和地方选举委员会的大多数委员都是属于参加目前内阁的那些党派的。推迟选举就浪费掉了已经花在准备工作上的几百万盾。
    “印度尼西亚新闻”十五日还发表社论，要求政府创造有利条件，以便普选能够在民主的气氛中，通过没有政治压力没有恐怖气氛的民主方式进行。
    雅加达“人民日报”十五日报道，印度尼西亚农民阵线第一副书记哈托约代表农民表示反对推迟普选的日期。
    新华社雅加达十五日电　据印度尼西亚新闻社报道，印度尼西亚总统苏加诺在十五日恢复处理国家的事务。苏加诺总统在八月五日从麦加朝圣回国后，一直在休假准备他在印度尼西亚独立纪念日（八月十七日）的演说。苏加诺总统已经在十五日下午从他的茂物别墅回到雅加达。
