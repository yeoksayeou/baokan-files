Title: Cambodian People's Party Announces Domestic and Foreign Policies
Author:
Date: 1955-08-20
Page: 4

    Xinhua News Agency, Hanoi, August 19th (via Vietnam News Agency, August 18th): The Cambodian People's Party's organ, "The Nation," elucidated on August 2nd the political line of the People's Party in its current struggle to consolidate peace, achieve independence, and realize democracy in Cambodia. The newspaper reiterated that former Khmer resistance fighters are always prepared to sincerely unite with all patriotic social strata in order to struggle for the correct and thorough implementation of the Cambodian armistice agreement.
    "The Nation" stated: The domestic policies of the People's Party are:
    To demand guarantees of democratic freedoms in Cambodia, so that all Cambodian citizens can enjoy freedom of speech, freedom of assembly, freedom of election and to be elected;
    To demand respect for freedom of belief and protection of religion, and to oppose those who distort or abuse religion in order to deceive and oppress the people;
    To demand reductions in land rent and interest rates, increased production, improved farming techniques, assistance to farmers in production to improve their living standards, and reductions in high taxes levied on farmers;
    To demand employment insurance for workers and strict enforcement of labor laws;
    To demand genuine equality between men and women.
    Regarding foreign policy, the People's Party announced that Cambodia needs to develop friendly relations with neighboring countries based on the principles of mutual respect for territorial integrity and sovereignty, mutual non-aggression, mutual non-interference in internal affairs, equality and mutual benefit, and peaceful coexistence; and resolutely opposes any imperialist interference and conspiracies to drag Cambodia into military alliance blocs.
    The People's Party believes that only by implementing the above policies can Cambodia become an independent country and achieve genuine democracy.

### 柬埔寨人民派宣布国内和外交政策

1955-08-20
第4版()
专栏：

    柬埔寨人民派宣布国内和外交政策
    新华社河内十九日电　据越南通讯社十八日讯：柬埔寨人民派机关报“民族报”在八月二日阐释了人民派目前为在柬埔寨巩固和平、完成独立和实现民主的斗争中的政治路线。这家报纸重申：前高棉抗战人员始终准备真诚地同一切爱国的社会阶层联合，以便为正确和彻底履行柬埔寨停战协定而斗争。
    “民族报”说：人民派的国内政策是：
    要求保证柬埔寨的民主自由，以使柬埔寨全体公民可以享受言论自由、集会自由、选举和被选举的自由；
    要求尊重信仰自由和保护宗教，反对为了欺骗和压迫人民而曲解宗教或滥用宗教的那些人；
    要求减少地租和利息，增加生产，改进耕作技术，在生产方面帮助农民以便提高他们的生活水平，降低对农民征课的高额赋税；
    要求给予工人以就业保险和严格执行劳工法；
    要求男女之间的真正平等。
    关于外交政策，人民派宣布，柬埔寨需要根据互相尊重领土完整和主权、互不侵犯、互不干涉内政、平等互利、和平共处的原则发展同邻国的友好关系；坚决反对帝国主义的任何干涉，反对把柬埔寨拉入军事联盟集团的阴谋。
    人民派认为，唯有执行上述政策，柬埔寨才有可能成为一个独立的国家和达到真正的民主。
