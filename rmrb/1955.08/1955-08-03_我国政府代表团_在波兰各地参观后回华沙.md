Title: Chinese Government Delegation Returns to Warsaw After Visiting Various Places in Poland
Author:
Date: 1955-08-03
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency, Warsaw, August 1st. The People's Republic of China government delegation, headed by Vice Premier of the State Council He Long, returned to Warsaw on the afternoon of July 30th after a week-long visit to various parts of Poland.
    The delegation first visited the International Fair in Poznań, then visited the Lenin Metallurgical Combine in Nowa Huta near Kraków and the former site of the Nazi concentration camp in Auschwitz. On the way to Zakopane, a famous resort city in Poland, the delegation visited Bolonin, where Lenin lived before his death. In addition, the delegation also visited a glider factory and a glider show in Bielsko; in the Silesian industrial zone, they visited a fully mechanized coal mine and a mining machinery manufacturing factory; and on the Baltic coast, they visited the Gdynia seaport and the shipbuilding industry in Gdańsk.

### 我国政府代表团  在波兰各地参观后回华沙

1955-08-03
第4版()
专栏：

    我国政府代表团
    在波兰各地参观后回华沙
    新华社华沙一日电　以国务院副总理贺龙为首的中华人民共和国政府代表团在波兰各地进行了为期一周的参观访问后，于七月三十日下午返回华沙。
    代表团首先到波兹南参观了国际博览会，接着到克拉科夫附近的诺瓦胡塔参观了列宁冶金联合企业和奥斯威辛的纳粹集中营的故址。代表团在到波兰休养名城萨科巴尼途中，访问了列宁生前住过的地方波罗尼尼。此外，代表团还在俾尔斯克参观了滑翔机工厂和滑翔机表演；在西里西亚工业区参观了全部机械化采煤的煤矿和矿山机械制造工厂；在波罗的海沿岸参观了琴尼亚海港和革但斯克的造船工业。
