Title: A Mass Meeting Held in Sao Paulo, Brazil, Attended by Leaders from All Walks of Life, Demanding the Government Establish Diplomatic and Trade Relations with the Soviet Union, China, and Other Countries
Author:
Date: 1955-08-05
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency, Montevideo, August 4th: According to TASS, a mass meeting was held in Sao Paulo, Brazil, on the 1st, attended by members of parliament, business representatives, and leaders of labor unions and student movements, demanding that the Brazilian government establish diplomatic and trade relations with the Soviet Union, the People's Republic of China, and various people's democracies. Brazilian newspapers reported that this demand has received support from representatives of the business community and the public in the state of Sao Paulo.

Seventeen members of the Sao Paulo State Legislative Assembly also introduced a motion in the assembly demanding the resignation of Brazilian Finance Minister Whitaker because he opposed the principle of "re-establishing direct trade relations with all countries in the world."

### 巴西圣保罗举行有各界领袖参加的群众大会  要求政府同苏中等国建立外交和贸易关系

1955-08-05
第4版()
专栏：

    巴西圣保罗举行有各界领袖参加的群众大会
    要求政府同苏中等国建立外交和贸易关系
    新华社四日讯　据塔斯社蒙德维的亚讯：巴西圣保罗在一日举行了有议员、企业界人士、工会和学生运动领袖参加的群众大会，要求巴西政府同苏联、中华人民共和国和各人民民主国家建立外交和贸易关系。巴西报纸报道，这种要求得到了圣保罗州的企业界代表和公众的支持。
    圣保罗州立法议会的十七名议员在议会里也提出了一项动议，要求巴西财政部长惠塔克辞职，因为他反对“同世界上一切国家重新建立直接贸易关系”的原则。
