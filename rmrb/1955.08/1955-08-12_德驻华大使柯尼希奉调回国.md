Title: GDR Ambassador König Recalled Home

Author:

Date: 1955-08-12
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, August 11th: The Ambassador Extraordinary and Plenipotentiary of the German Democratic Republic to China, Mr. König, left Beijing on the 11th to return home.

Ke Hua, Director of the Protocol Department of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs of the People's Republic of China, went to the airport to see him off.

Diplomatic envoys and staff from various countries' embassies in China, as well as all members of the Embassy of the German Democratic Republic in China, also went to see him off.

Mr. König has been appointed Ambassador Extraordinary and Plenipotentiary of the German Democratic Republic to the Union of Soviet Socialist Republics and concurrently Minister Extraordinary and Plenipotentiary to the Mongolian People's Republic.

### 德驻华大使柯尼希奉调回国

1955-08-12
第1版()
专栏：

    德驻华大使柯尼希奉调回国
    新华社十一日讯　德意志民主共和国驻华特命全权大使柯尼希在十一日离开北京回国。
    前往机场送行的有中华人民共和国外交部礼宾司司长柯华。
    各国驻华使节、使馆人员和德意志民主共和国驻华大使馆全体馆员也前往送行。
    柯尼希已被任命为德意志民主共和国驻苏维埃社会主义共和国联盟特命全权大使兼驻蒙古人民共和国特命全权公使。
