Title: Head of Chinese Exhibition Delegation Holds Reception in Jakarta
Author:
Date: 1955-08-22
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Jakarta, August 20th: Ma Yimin, head of the Chinese Exhibition Delegation participating in the Jakarta International Fair, held a reception at the Chinese Pavilion on the evening of the 19th to celebrate the opening of the Chinese Pavilion.

More than 2,200 people were invited to the reception, including Indonesian Cabinet Minister Sunarijo, Deputy Minister Asraroedin and other senior officials, members of parliament, Chairman of the International Fair Leihard, former Foreign Minister Sunaryo, and other prominent figures.

Diplomatic envoys and commercial representatives from the Soviet Union, India, and other countries also attended the reception.

In his speech at the reception, Ma Yimin said that the holding of the Jakarta International Fair would help promote economic cooperation and friendship between Asian and African countries, especially between China and Indonesia. Ma Yimin said: "Both China and Indonesia are rich, and there are many products that can be exchanged with each other. If trade between the two countries develops on the basis of equality and mutual benefit, there will be broad prospects."

Indonesian Deputy Minister of Transportation Asraroedin said in his speech that the exhibition illustrated the great achievements of China's economic construction in recent years. He expressed his hope that the friendly relations between China and Indonesia would develop day by day.

The Chairman of the Fair also spoke.

The guests visited the Chinese Exhibition Hall, and they were very interested in the performances of high-speed cutting lathes and textile machines operated by Chinese workers.

### 我国展览团团长在雅加达举行招待会

1955-08-22
第4版()
专栏：

　　我国展览团团长在雅加达举行招待会
    新华社雅加达二十日电　参加雅加达国际博览会的中国展览团团长马一民十九日晚在中国馆举行招待会，庆祝中国馆开幕。
    应邀出席招待会的有二千二百多人，其中有印度尼西亚内阁部长苏纳里约、副部长阿斯拉鲁丁和其他高级官员，国会议员，国际博览会主席莱哈德，前外交部长苏纳约及其他著名人士。
    苏联、印度等国的外交使节和商务代表也出席了招待会。
    马一民在招待会上讲话时说，雅加达国际博览会的举行，将有助于促进亚非国家之间、特别是中国和印度尼西亚之间的经济合作和友谊。马一民说：“中国和印度尼西亚都是富饶的，并且有很多产品可以互相交换。两国之间的贸易如果在平等互利的基础上发展，将有广阔的前途。”
    印度尼西亚交通部副部长阿斯拉鲁丁在讲话中说，展览会说明了近几年中国经济建设的巨大成就。他表示希望中国和印度尼西亚的友好关系日益发展。
    博览会主席也讲了话。
    来宾们参观了中国展览馆，他们对于由中国工人开动的高速切削车床和纺织机器的表演感到很大的兴趣。
