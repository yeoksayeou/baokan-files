Title: Chinese Ambassador to Czechoslovakia Holds Reception for Performance by Chinese Art Troupe; President Zápotocký and Others Attend Performance

Author:
Date: 1955-08-31
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Prague, August 30th - Chinese Ambassador to the Czechoslovak Republic, Cao Ying, and his wife held a reception for the performance of the Chinese Art Troupe at the National Theater in Prague on the evening of the 29th.

Among those present at the performance were Czechoslovak Republic President Zápotocký, Vice-Premiers Kopecký and Škodá, Foreign Minister David, Minister of Culture Štol, President of the Academy of Sciences Nýdl, leaders of various mass organizations, and prominent figures from the cultural and artistic circles.

Also present at the performance were the ambassadors of Romania, Bulgaria, North Korea, Hungary, Albania, and the United Kingdom; the ministers of Afghanistan, Mexico, Finland, Switzerland, and Brazil; the chargés d'affaires and diplomatic officials of the Soviet Union, India, the German Democratic Republic, the Democratic Republic of Vietnam, Yugoslavia, Israel, Italy, Norway, the Netherlands, Denmark, and Sweden.

The eight programs performed by the Art Troupe, including "Yandang Mountain," "Mu Kezhai," and "Red Silk Dance," were warmly welcomed.

During the intermission, President Zápotocký received the head of the Art Troupe, Zhang Zhixiang, the deputy head, Zhao Feng, and the main performers. Also present at the meeting were Kopecký, David, Štol, and Ambassador Cao Ying and his wife.

### 我驻捷大使举行我国艺术团演出招待会　萨波托斯基总统等人出席观看招待演出

1955-08-31
第4版()
专栏：

　　我驻捷大使举行我国艺术团演出招待会
　　萨波托斯基总统等人出席观看招待演出
    新华社布拉格三十日电　中国驻捷克斯洛伐克共和国大使曹瑛和夫人二十九日晚在布拉格民族剧院举行中国艺术团演出招待会。
    出席观看招待演出的有捷克斯洛伐克共和国总统萨波托斯基，副总理柯别茨基和什科达，外交部长戴维，文化部长斯托尔，科学院长尼耶德利和各群众团体负责人以及文化艺术界著名人士等。
    出席观看招待演出的还有罗马尼亚、保加利亚、朝鲜、匈牙利、阿尔巴尼亚和英国的大使，阿富汗、墨西哥、芬兰、瑞士和巴西的公使，苏联、印度、德意志民主共和国、越南民主共和国、南斯拉夫、以色列、意大利、挪威、荷兰、丹麦和瑞典的临时代办及外交官员。
    艺术团演出的“雁荡山”、“穆柯寨”和“红绸舞”等八个节目受到热烈的欢迎。
    萨波托斯基总统在演出休息时接见了艺术团团长张致祥、副团长赵沨和主要演员。接见时在座的有柯别茨基、戴维、斯托尔和曹瑛大使和夫人。
