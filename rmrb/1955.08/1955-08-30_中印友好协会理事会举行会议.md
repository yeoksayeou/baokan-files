Title: Council Meeting of the Sino-Indian Friendship Association Held
Author:
Date: 1955-08-30
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, August 29th: The Council of the Sino-Indian Friendship Association held a meeting in Beijing on the 22nd. The meeting listened to and discussed the report on the association's work given by the association's president, Ding Xilin, and passed a resolution on holding a rally to support the Indian people's recovery of Goa. The meeting also decided to strengthen the association's work in the second half of this year, holding a memorial and exhibition for the 1,500th anniversary of the Ajanta murals of India, and an Indian crafts exhibition.

The Council approved the All-China Federation of Trade Unions, the All-China Democratic Women's Federation, the All-China Youth Federation, the China Federation of Literary and Art Circles, the All-China Federation of Natural Science Societies, the Buddhist Association of China, the Islamic Association of China, the Red Cross Society of China, the All-China Sports Federation, and the China Political Science and Law Association as group members of the Sino-Indian Friendship Association.

The Council also added fourteen people from various circles of Chinese society as directors of the association, and added Wu Hua-zhi as the association's General Secretary. The fourteen new directors added by the Council are Zhu Qiwen, Du Qian, Wang Jiqi, Wang Huzhen, Li Shaochun, Wu Huazhi, Wu Quanheng, Zhou Weizhi, Yuan Shuipai, Zhang Shuihua, Zhang Weizhen, Zhao Puchu, Xie Bingxin, and Saifudin.

### 中印友好协会理事会举行会议

1955-08-30
第1版()
专栏：

　　中印友好协会理事会举行会议
    新华社二十九日讯　中印友好协会理事会二十二日在北京举行会议。会议听了和讨论了这个协会会长丁西林关于会务的报告，通过了关于举行支援印度人民收复果阿大会的决议。会议并决定加强协会今年下半年的工作，举办印度阿旃陀壁画一千五百年纪念会及展览和印度工艺展览。
    理事会通过中华全国总工会、中华全国民主妇女联合会、中华全国民主青年联合会、中国文学艺术界联合会、中华全国自然科学专门学会联合会、中国佛教协会、中国伊斯兰教协会、中国红十字会总会、中华全国体育总会和中国政治法律学会等十个人民团体为中印友好协会的团体会员。
    理事会并且增选中国社会各界人士十四人为协会的理事，增选吴化之为协会的总干事。理事会增选的十四位新理事是朱其文、杜前、汪季琦、汪胡桢、李少春、吴化之、吴全衡、周巍峙、袁水拍、张水华、张维桢、赵朴初、谢冰心、赛福鼎。
