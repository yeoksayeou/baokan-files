Title: Indonesian President Sukarno Returns to Jakarta
Author:
Date: 1955-08-06
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Jakarta, August 5th. Indonesian President Sukarno returned to Jakarta on the 5th after making a pilgrimage to Mecca. Among those welcoming him at the airport were Sastroamidjojo, the recently resigned Prime Minister of the Cabinet, and ministers, Sadono, Speaker of the Parliament, Sudiro, Mayor of Jakarta, Burhanuddin Harahap, the newly appointed formateur, and senior officers of the Army, Navy, and Air Force, as well as foreign diplomatic envoys.

Thousands of people gathered outside the airport. When President Sukarno left the airport, the crowd cheered him.

President Sukarno gave a brief speech at the airport. He said that he needed rest and needed time to prepare for his speech on Indonesian Independence Day (August 17), so he would be on vacation until August 15. He said that during this period, he would entrust all state affairs to Vice President Hatta.

### 印度尼西亚总统苏加诺回到雅加达

1955-08-06
第4版()
专栏：

    印度尼西亚总统苏加诺回到雅加达
    新华社雅加达五日讯　印度尼西亚总统苏加诺在麦加朝圣后，五日回到雅加达。在机场欢迎的有最近辞职的内阁总理沙斯特罗阿米佐约和部长们，国会议长沙多诺，雅加达市长苏迪罗，新受命组阁的伯汉努丁·哈拉哈普，陆、海、空军高级军官以及各国外交使节等。
    机场外面聚集着数以千计的群众。当苏加诺总统离开机场时，群众向他欢呼。
    苏加诺总统在机场发表了简短的谈话。他说，他需要休息，并且需要时间来准备他在印度尼西亚独立纪念日（八月十七日）的演说，因而他将要休假到八月十五日为止。他说，在这一段时期内，他将把国家的全部事务交由副总统哈达处理。
