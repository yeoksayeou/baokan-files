Title: Indian Government Welcomes Establishment of Diplomatic Relations Between China and Nepal
Author:
Date: 1955-08-18
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, New Delhi, August 17th: According to information from the Indian Information Service, Prime Minister Nehru stated in the People's Assembly of the Indian Parliament on the 16th that the Indian government welcomes the establishment of diplomatic relations between China and Nepal, as well as the announcement by China and Nepal that the Five Principles of Peaceful Coexistence will guide relations between the two countries.

### 印度政府欢迎中尼建交

1955-08-18
第4版()
专栏：

    印度政府欢迎中尼建交
    新华社新德里十七日电　据印度新闻处消息：尼赫鲁总理十六日在印度国会人民院说，印度政府欢迎中国和尼泊尔建立外交关系，以及中尼两国宣布以五项原则指导两国之间的关系。
