Title: Chiang Kai-shek Bandit Planes Intruded into Border Areas of Fujian and Zhejiang
Author:
Date: 1955-08-20
Page: 1

　　Xinhua News Agency, 19th. A U.S.-made Chiang Kai-shek bandit B-25 aircraft intruded into the airspace over the border areas of Fujian and Zhejiang provinces at 0:50 a.m. on the 18th, under cover of night, to conduct reconnaissance and harassment. After our planes took off, the Chiang Kai-shek bandit planes immediately fled in a southeasterly direction.

### 蒋贼飞机窜扰福建浙江边沿地区

1955-08-20
第1版()
专栏：

    蒋贼飞机窜扰福建浙江边沿地区
    新华社十九日讯　美制蒋贼B—25型飞机一架，在十八日零时五十分乘深夜窜入我国福建、浙江省边沿地区上空，进行侦察骚扰。我机起飞后，蒋贼飞机即向东南方向逃去。
