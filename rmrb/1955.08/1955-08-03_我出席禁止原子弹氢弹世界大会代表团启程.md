Title: Delegation to Attend World Conference Against Atomic and Hydrogen Bombs Departs

Author:

Date: 1955-08-03
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, 2nd. The Chinese delegation to the World Conference Against Atomic and Hydrogen Bombs, which will be held in Hiroshima and Tokyo, Japan, on August 6th, led by head of delegation Liu Ningyi, left Beijing by plane on the 2nd. The Chinese delegation was invited by the preparatory committee of the Japan World Conference Against Atomic and Hydrogen Bombs, the Japan National Relief Association, the General Council of Trade Unions of Japan, the Japan Federation of Women's Organizations, and other groups to attend the conference. In addition to head of delegation Liu Ningyi, the delegation also includes members Cheng Fangwu, Zhao Puchu, Xie Bingxin, Chen Tiqiang, secretary Zheng Senyu, translators Sun Shengquan, and An Shuqu.

Those who came to the airport to see them off included: Liu Guan, Secretary-General of the Chinese People's Committee for World Peace; Wu Yunfu, Secretary-General of the Chinese People's Relief Association; Hu Lansheng, Vice President and Secretary-General of the Red Cross Society of China; Juzan, Standing Director of the Buddhist Association of China; and Cao Mengjun, Secretary of the Secretariat and Deputy Secretary-General of the All-China Democratic Women's Federation.

Japanese peace activists in Beijing, Kameda Tongo and Nakamura Ganemon, also came to the airport to see them off.

### 我出席禁止原子弹氢弹世界大会代表团启程

1955-08-03
第1版()
专栏：

    我出席禁止原子弹氢弹世界大会代表团启程
    新华社二日讯　出席八月六日在日本广岛和东京举行的禁止原子弹、氢弹世界大会的中国代表团由团长刘宁一率领已于二日乘飞机离京。中国代表团是应日本禁止原子弹、氢弹世界大会筹备委员会、日本国民救援会、日本工会总评议会、全日本妇女团体联合会等团体的邀请前往参加大会的。代表团除团长刘宁一外，还有团员成仿吾、赵朴初、谢冰心、陈体强，秘书郑森禹、翻译孙盛泉、安淑渠等。
    到机场欢送的有：中国人民保卫世界和平委员会秘书长刘贯一、中国人民救济总会秘书长伍云甫、中国红十字总会副会长兼秘书长胡兰生、中国佛教协会常务理事巨赞、中华全国民主妇女联合会书记处书记兼副秘书长曹孟君等。
    在京的日本和平人士龟田东伍、中村玩右卫门也到机场送行。
