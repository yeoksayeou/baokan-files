Title: Premier U Nu Says Portugal Has No Right to Remain in India
Author:
Date: 1955-08-22
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, New Delhi, August 21st: According to the Indian Press Trust, Rangoon newspapers reported on the 20th that Burmese Premier U Nu told reporters that the Portuguese have no right to remain in India. He emphasized, "I don't want the importance of the Goa issue to be underestimated. It is a spark that can ignite a big fire."

U Nu believes that Portugal's insistence on staying in India long after Britain and France withdrew is a surprising phenomenon in politics. He pointed out, "All Asians cannot tolerate colonialism, whether it is Portuguese colonialism or the colonialism of other countries."

### 吴努总理说葡萄牙没有权利留在印度

1955-08-22
第1版()
专栏：

　　吴努总理说葡萄牙没有权利留在印度
    新华社新德里二十一日电　据印度报业托辣斯消息，仰光报纸二十日报道，缅甸总理吴努对记者发表谈话说，葡萄牙人没有权利留在印度。他强调说，“我不希望果阿问题的重要性被过低估计。这是一种能够引起大火的火星。”
    吴努认为，葡萄牙甚至在英国和法国撤出印度后很久的时候还坚持留在印度一事，是政治上的一个惊人现象。他指出，“凡是亚洲人都不能容忍殖民主义，不管它是葡萄牙的殖民主义还是其他国家的殖民主义。”
