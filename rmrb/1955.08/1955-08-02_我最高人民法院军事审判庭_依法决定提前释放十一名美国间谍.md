Title: Military Tribunal of the Supreme People's Court Decides to Release Eleven American Spies Early According to Law
Author:
Date: 1955-08-02
Page: 1

    Xinhua News Agency, 1st dispatch: The American spies John Knox Arnold, William Heulbaumer, Eugene John Wiedi, Wallace Lamar Brown, Elmer Fred Llewellyn, John Woodrow Booker, Howard William Brown, Steve Edward Shiba, Harry Martin Pengemin, Daniel Chris Schmidt, and John Valk Thompson, were previously sentenced to imprisonment by the Military Tribunal of the Supreme People's Court of our country for entering China's borders by plane to conduct espionage activities, endangering our country's national security. These eleven criminals have been able to abide by discipline and have shown good performance during their imprisonment, and the executive organ has requested the Military Tribunal of the Supreme People's Court to grant them leniency. The Military Tribunal of the Supreme People's Court has, on July 31, decided according to law to release the eleven criminals early, and has notified the executive organ to complete the release procedures immediately and send them out of our country's borders.

### 我最高人民法院军事审判庭  依法决定提前释放十一名美国间谍

1955-08-02
第1版()
专栏：

    我最高人民法院军事审判庭
    依法决定提前释放十一名美国间谍
    新华社一日讯　美国间谍约翰·诺克斯·阿诺德、威廉·赫尔·包莫、尤金·约翰·维地、华莱斯·拉马尔·布朗、埃尔默·弗雷德·刘维林、约翰·伍德罗·布克、霍华德·威廉·布朗、斯蒂夫·爱德华·齐巴、哈里·马丁·彭介民、丹尼尔·柯里斯·施密特、约翰·瓦尔克·汤普逊十一人因乘用飞机偷入中国国境，进行间谍活动，危害我国国家安全，前经我国最高人民法院军事审判庭分别判处徒刑。这十一名罪犯在服刑期间尚能遵守纪律，并有良好表现，已经执行机关报请最高人民法院军事审判庭予以宽大处理。最高人民法院军事审判庭于七月三十一日已经依法决定将该十一名罪犯提前释放，并通知执行机关即日办理释放手续，送出我国国境。
