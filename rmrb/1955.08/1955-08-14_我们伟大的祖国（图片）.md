    Our Great Motherland
    In the past two years, eight coke ovens of Anshan Iron and Steel Works have been put into production. The coke produced here not only fully meets the needs of iron smelting, but also can supply many domestic smelters and machinery manufacturers. This newly put into production No. 13 coke oven is being discharged.

### 我们伟大的祖国（图片）

1955-08-14
第1版()
专栏：

    我们伟大的祖国
    两年多来，鞍钢已有八座炼焦炉投入生产。这里生产的焦炭除了充分满足炼铁的需要外，还有一部分可以供应国内许多冶炼厂和机械制造厂。这座新投入生产的第十三号炼焦炉正在出焦。
