Title: Mongolian People's Revolutionary Army Song and Dance Troupe Arrives in Taiyuan for Visit
Author:
Date: 1955-08-13
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, Taiyuan, August 9th. The Mongolian People's Revolutionary Army Song and Dance Troupe, led by Major General Dunruibu·Damdingh, arrived in Taiyuan by special train on the morning of the 8th.

Those who welcomed the troupe at the station included Zeng Baotang, Deputy Commander of the Shanxi Military Region of the Chinese People's Liberation Army, Zheng Lin, Deputy Governor of Shanxi Province, and Liu Shuxia, Deputy Mayor of Taiyuan.

All members of the song and dance troupe accepted flowers from the welcomers and walked out of the station hand-in-hand with the young people who presented the flowers.

A welcoming ceremony was held in the station square. Wang Lisheng, Director of the Political Department of the Shanxi Military Region of the Chinese People's Liberation Army, and Jiang Ping, Director of the Shanxi Provincial Cultural Bureau, delivered welcoming speeches respectively. Colonel Qindi, Deputy Head of the Song and Dance Troupe, delivered a reply.

When the song and dance troupe traveled to their residence by car, the welcomers gathered in front of the car and shook hands cordially with the troupe members. Citizens along the way cheered and waved to them.

In the evening, Major General Damdingh and all members of the troupe attended a welcome banquet hosted by Wang Zifeng, Commander of the Shanxi Military Region of the Chinese People's Liberation Army, Zheng Lin, Deputy Governor of Shanxi Province, and Yue Weifan, Mayor of Taiyuan.

### 蒙古人民革命军歌舞团到达太原访问

1955-08-13
第1版()
专栏：

    蒙古人民革命军歌舞团到达太原访问
    新华社太原九日电　由敦儒布·达姆丁呼少将率领的蒙古人民革命军歌舞团，于八日上午乘专车到达太原。
    到车站欢迎的有中国人民解放军山西军区副司令员曾保堂、山西省副省长郑林、太原市副市长刘舒侠等。
    歌舞团全体团员接受了欢迎者的献花，并和献花的青年们挽手并肩地走出了车站。
    在车站广场上举行了欢迎仪式。中国人民解放军山西军区政治部主任王黎生和山西省文化局局长江萍分别致欢迎词，歌舞团副团长钦迪上校致答词。
    当歌舞团乘汽车前往寓所时，欢迎的人齐集车前和歌舞团团员亲切地握手，沿途市民纷纷向他们欢呼招手。
    当晚，达姆丁呼少将和全体团员出席了中国人民解放军山西军区司令员王紫峰、山西省副省长郑林、太原市市长岳维藩的欢迎宴会。
