Title: Yugoslavia and Bulgaria, Hungary Exchange Films
Author:
Date: 1955-08-01
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Prague, July 31st: According to a Belgrade dispatch from the Yugoslav News Agency: Representatives of the film distribution organizations of Yugoslavia and Bulgaria have signed an agreement in Belgrade for the exchange of films. According to this agreement, Yugoslavia will export ten films to Bulgaria; Bulgaria will export four films to Yugoslavia.
    Not long ago, Yugoslavia and Hungary also signed a similar agreement for the exchange of films.

### 南斯拉夫和保匈两国交换影片

1955-08-01
第4版()
专栏：

    南斯拉夫和保匈两国交换影片
    新华社布拉格三十一日讯　据南斯拉夫通讯社贝尔格莱德消息：南斯拉夫和保加利亚的影片发行机构的代表在贝尔格莱德签订了一项交换影片的协定。根据这一协定，南斯拉夫将向保加利亚输出十部影片；保加利亚将向南斯拉夫输出四部。
    不久以前，南斯拉夫和匈牙利也签订了一项类似的交换影片的协定。
