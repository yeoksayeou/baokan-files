Title: US Ambassador to Thailand Preevey Dies in Car Crash
Author:
Date: 1955-08-15
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Bangkok, August 13th: US Ambassador to Thailand John E. Preevey died on August 12th from injuries sustained in a head-on collision between his car and a truck on a highway near Hua Hin, Prachuap Khiri Khan Province, Thailand. Prior to his current position, Preevey served as the US Ambassador to Guatemala, where he directed subversive activities to overthrow the Guatemalan government.

### 美驻泰大使普里弗伊撞车身死

1955-08-15
第4版()
专栏：

    美驻泰大使普里弗伊撞车身死
    新华社十三日讯  曼谷消息：美国驻泰国大使约翰·埃·普里弗伊十二日在泰国巴蜀府华欣附近公路上驾驶汽车时同一辆卡车对面相撞，受伤身死。普里弗伊在任现职以前，曾任美国驻危地马拉大使，当时他曾指挥过推翻危地马拉政府的颠覆活动。
