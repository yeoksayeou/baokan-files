Title: Soviet Newspapers Publish Statements from China and North Korea
Author:
Date: 1955-08-14
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, August 13th. “Pravda,” “Izvestia,” and “Red Star” all published on the 13th two statements from the Ministry of Foreign Affairs of the People's Republic of China and the Ministry of Foreign Affairs of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea regarding the South Korean authorities' attempts to undermine the Korean Armistice Agreement through threatening actions.

### 苏联报纸刊载中朝两国声明

1955-08-14
第1版()
专栏：

    苏联报纸刊载中朝两国声明
    新华社莫斯科十三日电　“真理报”、“消息报”、“红星报”十三日都刊载了中华人民共和国外交部和朝鲜民主主义人民共和国外务省关于南朝鲜当局企图以威胁行动破坏朝鲜停战协定的两个声明。
