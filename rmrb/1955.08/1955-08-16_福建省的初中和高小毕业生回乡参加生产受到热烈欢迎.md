Title: Middle School and Elementary School Graduates in Fujian Province Warmly Welcomed to Return Home and Participate in Production
Author:
Date: 1955-08-16
Page: 2
Xinhua News Agency

    Middle School and Elementary School Graduates in Fujian Province Warmly Welcomed to Return Home and Participate in Production

    Middle school and elementary school graduates in Fujian Province who returned to their hometowns to participate in labor and production this summer were warmly welcomed by the peasants in various regions.

    When these graduates returned to their hometowns, local governments, Party and Communist Youth League organizations, and the broad masses of peasants held gatherings to welcome them back to participate in production, and helped them solve problems related to production and self-study. Of the middle school and elementary school graduates who returned to Minlang Town in Minhou County, sixteen have been hired as accountants, cashiers, and newspaper readers for agricultural production cooperatives and mutual aid teams, fourteen have been hired as teachers and childcare workers in kindergartens, and thirty-one have formed self-study groups with the help of Party and Communist Youth League branches.

    After middle school graduate Dai Shengtang returned to Shuidong Township in Guangze County, many farmers competed to invite him to serve as the accountant for the agricultural production cooperative. After elementary school graduate Xiao Fangmu and two others returned to their hometown in Youxi County, they were immediately hired as accountants for agricultural production cooperatives and credit cooperatives, and as teachers in people's schools. (Xinhua News Agency)

### 福建省的初中和高小毕业生回乡参加生产受到热烈欢迎

1955-08-16
第2版()
专栏：

    福建省的初中和高小毕业生回乡参加生产受到热烈欢迎
    福建省今年暑期回乡参加劳动生产的初中和高小毕业生，受到各地农民的热烈欢迎。
    当这些毕业生回到家乡时，当地政府、党团组织和广大农民都集会欢迎他们回乡参加生产，帮助他们解决生产和自学问题。回到闽侯县闽琅镇的初中和高小毕业生已有十六人被聘请为农业生产合作社和互助组的会计、出纳和读报员，有十四人被聘请为幼儿班的教师和保育员，有三十一人在党团支部帮助下成立了自学小组。
    初中毕业生戴声堂回到光泽县水东乡后，许多农民争先来约请他担任农业生产合作社的会计。尤溪县高小毕业生萧芳沐等三人回到家乡后，也立刻被聘为农业生产合作社、信用合作社的会计和民校教师。（新华社）
