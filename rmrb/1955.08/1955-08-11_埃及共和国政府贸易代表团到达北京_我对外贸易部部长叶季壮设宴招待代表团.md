Title: Egyptian Government Trade Delegation Arrives in Beijing; Minister of Foreign Trade Ye Jiuzhuang Hosts Banquet for Delegation
Author:
Date: 1955-08-11
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, August 10th—An Egyptian government trade delegation of ten people, led by Egyptian Minister of Commerce and Industry Muhammad Abu Nusseir, arrived in Beijing by plane from Guangzhou on the afternoon of the 10th. This is the first Egyptian government trade delegation to visit China.

When the delegation arrived, Ye Jiuzhuang, Minister of Foreign Trade of the People's Republic of China, Vice Minister Lei Renmin, Huang Hua, Director of the Department of West European and African Affairs of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs, heads of relevant bureaus and departments of the Ministry of Foreign Trade, and various specialized companies, as well as Indian Ambassador to China, Raghunath Krishna Ram Rau, were at the airport to welcome them.

The list of the Egyptian government trade delegation is as follows:
Head of Delegation: Muhammad Abu Nusseir
Members:　Ahmed Naguib
　　　　Muhammad Madhat Fahr
　　　　Mustafa Kamel Ibrahim
　　　　Munir Himaya
Advisors:　Ahmed Fouad
　　　　Ismail Shafei
　　　　Muhammad Abdul Moneim Said
　　　　Choukri Selim Tadaugi
　　　　Ahmed Yusuf Guindi

Xinhua News Agency, August 10th—Ye Jiuzhuang, Minister of Foreign Trade of the People's Republic of China, hosted a banquet on the evening of the 10th to welcome the Egyptian government trade delegation.

The entire Egyptian government trade delegation, headed by Egyptian Minister of Commerce and Industry Muhammad Abu Nusseir, attended the banquet.

Among those present were Lei Renmin, Vice Minister of Foreign Trade, Zhang Hanfu, Vice Minister of Foreign Affairs, Chen Jiakang, Assistant Minister, Huang Hua, Director of the Department of West European and African Affairs, Qiao Peixin, Acting Director of the Foreign Operations Bureau of the People's Bank of China, and Chen Ming, Sun Chun, Zhao Chongde, Zhou Huamin, Yu Guang, Ni Weiting, Sun Liji, and Huang Guoguang, heads of relevant bureaus and specialized companies of the Ministry of Foreign Trade.

Ye Jiuzhuang delivered a speech at the banquet. On behalf of the Chinese government, he extended a warm welcome to the Egyptian government trade delegation, which was invited to Beijing to discuss trade agreements between China and Egypt and other issues related to the development of trade between the two countries. He said that the visit of the Egyptian government trade delegation to China was the result of an agreement reached at the Asian-African Conference and a concrete manifestation of the implementation of the decisions on economic cooperation made at the Asian-African Conference by China and Egypt. Ye Jiuzhuang continued, saying that he believed that this discussion would open up broad prospects for the development of trade between China and Egypt, thereby contributing to the economic development of both countries. He wished the trade negotiations between China and Egypt success.

Muhammad Abu Nusseir, head of the Egyptian government trade delegation, spoke at the banquet. He thanked the Chinese government for its warm hospitality to the delegation. He said that China and Egypt had cultural and economic ties long ago. China had the Great Wall very early on, and Egypt had the pyramids very early on, which showed the great strength of the people of China and Egypt. The further strengthening of the economic and cultural relations between these two countries with long histories will be of great benefit to the people of both countries. He wished success to the economic cooperation between China and Egypt.

Before the banquet, Muhammad Abu Nusseir, head of the Egyptian government trade delegation and Egyptian Minister of Commerce and Industry, led the entire delegation to pay a visit to Ye Jiuzhuang, Minister of Foreign Trade of the People's Republic of China.

### 埃及共和国政府贸易代表团到达北京  我对外贸易部部长叶季壮设宴招待代表团

1955-08-11
第1版()
专栏：

    埃及共和国政府贸易代表团到达北京
    我对外贸易部部长叶季壮设宴招待代表团
    新华社十日讯　由埃及工商部长穆罕默德·阿卜·努赛尔率领的埃及共和国政府贸易代表团一行十人，在十日下午由广州乘飞机到达北京。这是埃及政府第一个来中国访问的贸易代表团。
    当代表团到达时，中华人民共和国对外贸易部部长叶季壮、副部长雷任民，外交部西欧非洲司司长黄华以及对外贸易部有关局、处和各专业公司的负责人以及印度驻华大使赖嘉文都到机场欢迎。
    埃及共和国政府贸易代表团名单如下：团长穆罕默德·阿卜·努赛尔团员　艾哈迈德·纳吉布
      穆罕默德·麦德哈特·法尔
      穆斯塔法·卡迈尔·易卜拉欣
      穆尼尔·希马雅顾问　艾哈迈德·福阿德
      伊斯梅尔·沙费尔
      穆罕默德·阿卜杜勒·莫宁·赛义德
      乔克里·赛利姆·陶杜吉
      艾哈迈德·优素福·古英迪
    新华社十日讯　中华人民共和国对外贸易部部长叶季壮在十日晚设宴招待埃及共和国政府贸易代表团。
    以埃及工商部长穆罕默德·阿卜·努赛尔为首的埃及共和国政府贸易代表团全体人员出席了宴会。
    出席作陪的有对外贸易部副部长雷任民，外交部副部长章汉夫、部长助理陈家康、西欧非洲司司长黄华，中国人民银行国外业务局代局长乔培新，对外贸易部有关局和各专业公司的负责人陈明、孙纯、赵重德、周化民、余光、倪蔚庭、孙立基、黄国光等。
    叶季壮在宴会上致词。他代表中国政府对应邀前来北京商谈中埃两国间的贸易协定以及其他有关发展中埃两国贸易问题的埃及共和国政府贸易代表团，表示热烈欢迎。他说，埃及共和国政府贸易代表团访问中国，是亚非会议时双方协议的结果，也是中埃两国执行亚非会议有关经济合作决定的具体表现。叶季壮接着说，他相信这次商谈将会为中埃两国贸易的发展开辟广阔的前途，从而有助于两国经济的发展。他预祝中埃两国贸易谈判的成功。
    埃及共和国政府贸易代表团团长穆罕默德·阿卜·努赛尔在宴会上讲了话。他感谢中国政府对代表团的盛情招待。他说，中国和埃及两个国家远在很久以前就有了文化和经济上的联系，中国很早就有了万里长城，埃及很早就有了金字塔，这说明了中埃两国人民的伟大力量。这两个历史悠久的国家经济和文化关系的进一步加强，将会对两国人民有很大利益。他预祝中埃两国经济合作的成功。
    在宴会以前，埃及共和国政府贸易代表团团长、埃及工商部长穆罕默德·阿卜·努赛尔率领代表团全体人员，拜会了中华人民共和国对外贸易部部长叶季壮。（附图片）
