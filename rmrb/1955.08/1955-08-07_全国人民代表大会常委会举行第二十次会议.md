Title: NPC Standing Committee Holds Twentieth Meeting
Author:
Date: 1955-08-07
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, 6th Dispatch The Standing Committee of the National People's Congress held its twentieth meeting on August 6th.

　　After discussion, the meeting approved the "Decision on the Inspection Work of Deputies to the National People's Congress and Deputies to the People's Congresses of Provinces, Autonomous Regions, and Municipalities Directly Under the Central Government" and the "Regulations on Overseas Chinese Applying for the Use of State-Owned Barren Mountains and Wasteland" proposed by the State Council.

　　The meeting also approved several appointments and removals.

### 全国人民代表大会常委会举行第二十次会议

1955-08-07
第1版()
专栏：

    全国人民代表大会常委会举行第二十次会议
    新华社六日讯　全国人民代表大会常务委员会在八月六日举行第二十次会议。
    会议经过讨论，通过了“关于全国人民代表大会代表和省自治区直辖市人民代表大会代表视察工作的决定”和国务院提出的“华侨申请使用国有的荒山荒地条例”。
    会议还通过和批准任命事项多起。
