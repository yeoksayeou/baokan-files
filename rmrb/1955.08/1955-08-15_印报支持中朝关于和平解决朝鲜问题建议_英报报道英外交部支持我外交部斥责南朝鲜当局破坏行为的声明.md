Title: Indian Newspapers Support the Sino-Korean Proposal for Peaceful Resolution of the Korean Question; British Newspapers Report the British Foreign Office's Support for the Chinese Foreign Ministry's Statement Condemning the South Korean Authorities' Sabotage
Author:
Date: 1955-08-15
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, New Delhi, August 14th: Indian newspapers have expressed concern about the Korean situation caused by the provocative actions of the Lee Seung-man group, and generally support the proposal put forward by the governments of China and the Democratic People's Republic of Korea to convene a Far Eastern conference with broad participation of Asian countries to seek a peaceful resolution of the Korean question.

　　The Hindi "Hindustan Daily" published an editorial on the 13th saying: "The necessity of holding such a conference cannot be denied." The newspaper said: "Only through such a conference can a peaceful resolution of the Korean question be expected."

　　The Bengali daily "Yugantar" on the 12th refuted the South Korean authorities' accusation that the Neutral Nations Supervisory Commission was engaged in espionage activities and assisting the Democratic People's Republic of Korea. The newspaper said: "This illusory suspicion is just an excuse" to force the dissolution of the Neutral Nations Supervisory Commission and undermine the Korean Armistice Agreement.

　　The Telugu daily "Andhra Patrika" said on the 12th: "The attitude of the United States is the main reason for Lee Seung-man's sabotage."

　　Xinhua News Agency, August 14th: TASS, London, August 14th: The diplomatic correspondent of "The Times" wrote, when discussing the South Korean situation: "The United Nations Command's assurance that the Neutral Nations Supervisory Commission will be protected is welcomed in London."

　　The reporter emphasized: "Britain believes that the commission is doing useful work and should temporarily preserve its current strength."

　　The reporter pointed out: "London has no objection to what the Ministry of Foreign Affairs of the People's Republic of China said in its statement of August 11 regarding the provocative actions of the South Korean authorities. When China condemned actions that undermine the armistice agreement and believed that the United Nations Command should be responsible for taking effective measures, it received heartfelt support from the Foreign Office this time."

### 印报支持中朝关于和平解决朝鲜问题建议  英报报道英外交部支持我外交部斥责南朝鲜当局破坏行为的声明

1955-08-15
第4版()
专栏：

    印报支持中朝关于和平解决朝鲜问题建议
  英报报道英外交部支持我外交部斥责南朝鲜当局破坏行为的声明
    新华社新德里十四日电  印度报纸对李承晚集团的挑衅行动引起的朝鲜局势表示关切，一般都支持中国和朝鲜民主主义人民共和国政府提出的召开有亚洲国家广泛参加的远东会议来谋求和平解决朝鲜问题的建议。
    印地文“印度斯坦日报”十三日发表社论说：“举行这种会议的必要是不能否认的。”报纸说：“唯有通过这种会议才能期望朝鲜问题的和平解决。”
    十二日的孟加拉文日报“划时代报”驳斥了南朝鲜当局诬蔑中立国监察委员会进行间谍活动和协助朝鲜民主主义人民共和国的说法。报纸说：“这种幻想的猜疑只是一种借口”，目的是要强迫解散中立国监察委员会和破坏朝鲜停战协定。
    泰鲁固文日报“安得拉报”十二日说：“美国的态度是李承晚进行捣乱的主要原因”。
    新华社十四日讯  塔斯社伦敦十四日讯：“泰晤士报”外交记者在论述南朝鲜局势时写道：“联合国军总司令部保证中立国监察委员会将受到保护，这在伦敦受到欢迎”。
    记者着重说：“英国认为，委员会正在做有益的工作，应该暂时保存它目前的力量”。
    记者指出：“对于中华人民共和国外交部八月十一日发表的关于南朝鲜当局挑衅行为的声明中所说的话，伦敦方面没有什么反对的意见。当中国斥责破坏停战协定的行动并且认为联合国军总司令部应负责采取有效措施的时候，它这一次得到了外交部衷心的支持。”
