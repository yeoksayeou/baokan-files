Title: Telegram of Congratulations from the Asian and Pacific Regions Peace Liaison Committee
Author:
Date: 1955-08-06
Page: 1

    Telegram of Congratulations from the Asian and Pacific Regions Peace Liaison Committee to the World Conference Against Atomic and Hydrogen Bombs:
    On the occasion of the opening of the World Conference Against Atomic and Hydrogen Bombs in Hiroshima, Japan, the Asian and Pacific Regions Peace Liaison Committee extends its heartfelt congratulations to the conference. The people of the Asian and Pacific regions and the world resolutely oppose atomic war and atomic weapons. The powerful movement against atomic weapons carried out by the Japanese people, who have suffered severely from atomic and hydrogen bombs three times, has received the deep sympathy and support of the people of Asian countries. The convening of this conference marks the firm will of the people of Asia and the world to oppose atomic war and atomic weapons. We wish the conference complete success.
	Asian and Pacific Regions Peace Liaison Committee
	August 5, 1955

### 亚洲及太平洋区域和平联络委员会的贺电

1955-08-06
第1版()
专栏：

    亚洲及太平洋区域和平联络委员会的贺电禁止原子弹、氢弹世界大会：
    兹值禁止原子弹、氢弹世界大会在日本广岛开幕之际，亚洲及太平洋区域和平联络委员会谨向大会表示衷心的祝贺。亚洲及太平洋区域以及全世界的人民都坚决反对原子战争和原子武器。曾三次遭受原子弹、氢弹严重灾害的日本人民所进行的反对原子武器的强大运动，是得到亚洲各国人民深切的同情和支持的。这次大会的召开，标志着亚洲和全世界人民反对原子战争和原子武器的坚强意志。我们谨祝大会获得圆满成就。	亚洲及太平洋区域和平联络委员会	一九五五年八月五日
