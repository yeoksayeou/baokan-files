Title: State Council Holds Sixteenth Plenary Meeting
Author:
Date: 1955-08-04
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, 3rd August—The Sixteenth Plenary Meeting of the State Council of the People's Republic of China was held on August 2nd. Thirty-four responsible persons from People's Committees of provinces, autonomous regions, municipalities directly under the central government attended the meeting in Beijing. The meeting focused on soliciting the opinions of responsible persons from People's Committees of provinces, autonomous regions, and municipalities directly under the central government on the issues of wage standards for state organ personnel, streamlining state organs, and reducing investment and expenses for basic construction.
    The meeting finally passed the regulations on unified leadership of slaughterhouses and on-site hygiene and veterinary work.

### 国务院举行第十六次全体会议

1955-08-04
第1版()
专栏：

    国务院举行第十六次全体会议
    新华社三日讯　中华人民共和国国务院全体会议第十六次会议在八月二日举行。各省、自治区、直辖市人民委员会在京负责人三十四人参加了这次会议。会议着重就国家机关工作人员工资问题、国家机关整编工作问题，和基本建设投资和费用的削减问题听取了各省、自治区、直辖市人民委员会负责人的意见。
    会议最后通过了关于统一领导屠宰场及场内卫生和兽医工作的规定。
