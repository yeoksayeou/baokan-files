Title: Jakarta Citizens Hold Large-Scale Torchlight Parade to Celebrate the Convocation of the All-Indonesia People's Congress
Author:
Date: 1955-08-17
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency, Jakarta, August 16th: More than 10,000 citizens of the Indonesian capital, Jakarta, held a large-scale torchlight parade on the evening of the 15th to celebrate the convocation of the All-Indonesia People's Congress.

　　Participants in the parade included representatives of the All-Indonesia People's Congress, workers, farmers, and people from all walks of life. The parade was led by Arudji Kartawinata, Chairman of the All-Indonesia People's Congress.

　　The torchlight parade队伍 (parade contingent) held high red banners and chanted "Indonesia Independence Song," marching from the "Declaration Building" to the "Independence Palace," and presented the "torch of revolution" to President Sukarno.

　　People in the parade shouted slogans such as "Liberate West Irian," "Dutch get out," "Eliminate the Darul Islam movement," and "Abolish the Round Table Conference Agreement" on the streets. Many citizens standing on both sides of the street spontaneously joined the parade.

　　President Sukarno delivered a brief speech in front of the "Independence Palace." He said that the Indonesian revolution is a revolution of all the Indonesian people, and its success depends on all the Indonesian people. As long as our goal is not achieved, the torch of revolution will never be extinguished.

### 庆祝全印度尼西亚人民代表大会的召开  雅加达市民万余人举行大规模的火炬游行

1955-08-17
第4版()
专栏：

    庆祝全印度尼西亚人民代表大会的召开
    雅加达市民万余人举行大规模的火炬游行
    新华社雅加达十六日电　印度尼西亚首都市民一万多人十五日晚间举行大规模的火炬游行，庆祝全印度尼西亚人民代表大会的召开。
    参加游行的有全印度尼西亚人民代表大会的代表、工人、农民和其他各界人士。游行队伍由全印度尼西亚人民代表大会主席阿鲁齐·卡塔韦纳塔率领。
    火炬游行队伍高举着红色的横幅标语，唱着“印度尼西亚独立歌”，从“宣言大厦”走到“独立宫”，并且把“革命的火炬”献给苏加诺总统。
    游行的人在街上高呼“解放西伊里安”、“荷兰人滚回去”、“消灭回教国运动”和“废除圆桌会议协定”等口号。许多站在街道两旁的市民自发地参加了游行队伍。
    苏加诺总统在“独立宫”前面发表了简短的谈话。他说，印度尼西亚的革命是全体印度尼西亚人民的革命，它的成功依靠全体印度尼西亚人民。只要我们的目标没有达到，革命的火炬就决不会熄灭。
