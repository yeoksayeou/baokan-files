Title: Premier Kim Il-sung Holds Banquet to Celebrate the Tenth Anniversary of Korea's Liberation
Author:
Date: 1955-08-17
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Pyongyang, August 16th: Premier Kim Il-sung of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea held a grand banquet in Pyongyang on the evening of the 15th to celebrate the tenth anniversary of the "August 15th" liberation of Korea.

Attending the banquet were leaders of the Korean Workers' Party and government, heads of various democratic parties and social organizations, generals of the People's Army, and figures from the scientific, cultural, and artistic circles.

The Soviet government delegation headed by A.B. Aristov and the People's Republic of China delegation headed by Zhu De were invited to attend the banquet. General Yang Yong, commander of the Chinese People's Volunteer Army, was also invited to attend.

Diplomatic envoys of various brotherly countries to Korea and congratulatory delegations participating in the "August 15th" celebration ceremony also attended the banquet.

At the beginning of the banquet, Premier Kim Il-sung delivered a speech. He said that when greeting this national holiday, all the Korean people deeply thanked the great Soviet Union, which liberated Korea and continued to provide the Korean people with tremendous assistance and support in the ten years after liberation. He then said that the friendship with a long history between the Korean and Chinese people had been further consolidated in the war against the common enemy. After the war, the Chinese people continued to provide the Korean people with material and spiritual assistance. Today, the Korean people express their utmost respect and gratitude to the fraternal Chinese people. Next, he extended a warm welcome to the Soviet government delegation and the People's Republic of China delegation who were invited to Pyongyang to participate in the "August 15th" celebration ceremony. Finally, he expressed his gratitude to the brotherly countries that provided various assistance to the Korean people.

The head of the Soviet government delegation, A.B. Aristov, raised a glass at the banquet and proposed a toast to the heroic Korean people, to the Korean Workers' Party and the Republic's government, to the Central Committee of the Workers' Party headed by Comrade Kim Il-sung, and to the immortal friendship between the Korean and Soviet people.

The head of the People's Republic of China delegation, Zhu De, delivered a speech at the banquet. He first extended warm congratulations to Premier Kim Il-sung and all the attendees on behalf of the People's Republic of China delegation. He then said: "The demand for the peaceful reunification of the motherland is the strongest desire of the Korean people, and this just desire will be fully realized with the broad support of the peace-loving forces in the world, headed by the Soviet Union." He emphasized: "The Chinese people and the Korean people will always stand together. In your future struggles, you can believe that the Chinese people will continue to give you their full support and assistance, as in the past." Then, he raised his glass and proposed a toast to the unbreakable friendship between the Chinese and Korean people and to the health of Comrade Kim Il-sung.

During the banquet, the hosts and guests repeatedly raised their glasses, wishing the Korean people brilliant victories in the cause of rebuilding the motherland and peacefully reunifying the motherland, and wishing the internationalist friendship and unity among the people of the socialist camp to be increasingly consolidated.

### 金日成首相举行宴会庆祝朝鲜解放十周年

1955-08-17
第4版()
专栏：

    金日成首相举行宴会庆祝朝鲜解放十周年
    新华社平壤十六日电　朝鲜民主主义人民共和国内阁金日成首相十五日晚在平壤举行盛大宴会，庆祝“八一五”朝鲜解放十周年。
    出席宴会的有朝鲜劳动党和政府的领导人，各民主政党、社会团体负责人，人民军将领，科学、文化艺术界人士等。
    以阿·布·阿里斯托夫为首的苏联政府代表团和以朱德为首的中华人民共和国代表团应邀出席了宴会。中国人民志愿军司令员杨勇将军也应邀出席了宴会。
    各兄弟国家驻朝使节和参加“八一五”庆祝典礼的祝贺团体也出席了宴会。
    宴会开始时，金日成首相首先致词。他说，全体朝鲜人民在迎接这个民族节日的时候，深深地感谢解放朝鲜并且在解放后的十年间继续不断地给予朝鲜人民以巨大的援助和支持的伟大的苏联。他接着说，朝鲜人民和中国人民之间的有悠久历史的友谊在反抗共同敌人的战争中更加巩固了。在战后，中国人民又继续给予朝鲜人民以物质和精神的援助。今天，我们朝鲜人民向兄弟般的中国人民表示最大的敬意和感谢。接着，他对应邀来平壤参加“八一五”庆祝典礼的苏联政府代表团和中华人民共和国代表团表示热烈的欢迎。最后，他对给予朝鲜人民以各种援助的各兄弟国家表示感谢。
    苏联政府代表团团长阿·布·阿里斯托夫在宴会上举杯提议为了英雄的朝鲜人民、为了朝鲜劳动党和共和国政府、为了以金日成同志为首的劳动党中央委员会、为了朝苏两国人民的不朽的友谊而干杯。
    中华人民共和国代表团团长朱德在宴会上致词。他首先以中华人民共和国代表团的名义向金日成首相和全体与会者致以热烈的祝贺，他接着说：“要求祖国和平统一是朝鲜人民最强烈的愿望，这种正义的愿望在以苏联为首的全世界爱好和平力量的广泛支持下必将得到完全实现。”他强调说：“中国人民和朝鲜人民将永远站在一起，在你们今后的斗争中，你们可以相信，中国人民将像过去的年代一样，继续给予你们以全力的支持和援助。”然后，他举杯提议为朝中两国人民牢不可破的友谊、为金日成首相同志的健康干杯。
    席间宾主一再举杯，祝朝鲜人民在重建祖国和和平统一祖国的事业中取得光辉的胜利，祝社会主义阵营各国人民间的国际主义友谊团结日益巩固。
