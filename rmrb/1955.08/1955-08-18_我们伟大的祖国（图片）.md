Our Great Motherland
The surveying work for the Lanzhou-Xinjiang Railway, west of Qingshui, has reached the Yumen Oilfield area. After all the surveying work is completed, technical design can begin. Surveyors are conducting route surveying on the Gobi Desert near the Yumen Oilfield.

### 我们伟大的祖国（图片）

1955-08-18
第1版()
专栏：

    我们伟大的祖国
    兰新铁路清水以西的定线测量工作已进行到玉门油矿区。全部勘测工作完成后，就可进行技术设计。勘测人员正在玉门油矿附近的戈壁滩上进行定线测量。
