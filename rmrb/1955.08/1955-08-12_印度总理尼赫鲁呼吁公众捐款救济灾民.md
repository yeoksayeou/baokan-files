Title: Indian Prime Minister Nehru Calls on Public to Donate to Relief for Disaster Victims
Author:
Date: 1955-08-12
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency, New Delhi, August 11th: Prime Minister Nehru, after inspecting the flood-stricken areas of Uttar Pradesh, issued a statement on the 9th upon his return to New Delhi, calling on the public to donate to the Prime Minister's National Relief Fund, so as to carry out relief work in the flood-stricken areas of Uttar Pradesh, Bihar, West Bengal, and Assam, as well as the drought-stricken areas of western India.

Nehru, in talking about his impressions of the disaster caused by the floods, said, "Thousands of villages have been completely washed away by the floods, and even more villages have been affected. Vast areas of crops have been damaged." He said that it is very clear that a large amount of relief in the form of food, clothing, shelter, and medical treatment must be provided immediately.

### 印度总理尼赫鲁呼吁公众捐款救济灾民

1955-08-12
第4版()
专栏：

    印度总理尼赫鲁呼吁公众捐款救济灾民
    新华社新德里十一日讯　尼赫鲁总理视察北方省水灾地区回到新德里以后在九日发表的声明中，呼吁公众为总理全国救济基金捐款，以便能够在北方省、比哈尔省、西孟加拉省和阿萨姆省的水灾地区以及印度西部的旱灾地区进行救济工作。
    尼赫鲁在谈到他对洪水造成的灾害的印象时说，“数以千计的村庄完全被洪水冲掉了，更多的村庄受到了洪水的影响。广大地区的庄稼受到了损害。”他说，非常明显，必须立即提供大量的食物、衣服、住所和医药治疗等方面的救济。
