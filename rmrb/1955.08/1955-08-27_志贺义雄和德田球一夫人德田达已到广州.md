Title: Shiga Yoshio and Mrs. Tokuda Kyuichi, Tokuda Tatsuko, Arrive in Guangzhou
Author:
Date: 1955-08-27
Page: 1

　　Xinhua News Agency, Guangzhou, August 26th. Shiga Yoshio, member of the Central Committee of the Japanese Communist Party, and Mrs. Tokuda Kyuichi, Tokuda Tatsuko, who came to China to transport the remains of the late Secretary-General of the Japanese Communist Party, Tokuda Kyuichi, arrived in Guangzhou on the afternoon of the 26th.
    Li Chuli, a responsible staff member of the Central Committee of the Chinese Communist Party, arrived in Shenzhen from Beijing on the 25th to greet them.
    When Shiga Yoshio and Tokuda Tatsuko arrived in Guangzhou by train from Shenzhen, Tao Zhu, Secretary of the Guangdong Provincial Committee of the Chinese Communist Party, Zhao Ziyang and Lin Liming, Deputy Secretaries, Wang De, Secretary of the Guangzhou Municipal Committee of the Chinese Communist Party, and Zhu Guang, Deputy Secretary, also went to the station to greet them.

### 志贺义雄和德田球一夫人德田达已到广州

1955-08-27
第1版()
专栏：

　　志贺义雄和德田球一夫人德田达已到广州
　　新华社广州二十六日电　来中国接运日本共产党已故总书记德田球一遗体的日本共产党中央委员志贺义雄和德田球一夫人德田达，在二十六日下午到达广州。
    中共中央负责工作人员李初梨在二十五日先期由北京到广州前往深圳迎接。
    志贺义雄和德田达由深圳乘火车到达广州时，中国共产党广东省委员会书记陶铸、副书记赵紫阳、林李明，中共广州市委员会书记王德、副书记朱光等也到车站迎接。
