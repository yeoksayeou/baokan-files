Title: China and Egypt Sign Agreement on Egypt Importing Steel from China; Sign Contract on China Importing Cotton from Egypt
Author:
Date: 1955-08-24
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, August 23rd: After friendly discussions in Beijing, Mr. Mohamed Abdul Moneim Said, consultant to the Trade Delegation of the Government of the Republic of Egypt, representing Egyptian steel importers and relevant authorities, and the China National Metals and Minerals Import Corporation, signed on the 23rd, in accordance with the Sino-Egyptian Trade Agreement and the Protocol of the First Agreement Year between the two countries, the first agreement for Egypt to import 60,000 tons of steel from China.

Mr. Mohamed Abdul Moneim Said, consultant to the Trade Delegation of the Government of the Republic of Egypt, signed on behalf of the Egyptian side, and Mr. Zhou Huamin, manager of the China National Metals and Minerals Import Corporation, signed on behalf of the Chinese side.

Xinhua News Agency, August 23rd: After discussions in Beijing, the China National Import and Export Corporation and the Egyptian Cotton Committee and Misr Cotton Export Company, signed on the 23rd, in accordance with the Sino-Egyptian Trade Agreement and the Protocol of the First Agreement Year between the two countries, the first contract for China to import 45,000 bales (15,000 tons) of cotton from Egypt.

Mr. Ni Weiting, deputy manager of the China National Import and Export Corporation, signed on behalf of the Chinese side. Mr. Ahmed Najib, director of the Egyptian Cotton Committee, and Mr. Ismail Shafei, general manager of Misr Cotton Export Company, signed on behalf of the Egyptian side.

### 中埃签订埃及自中国进口钢材的协议书　并签订中国自埃及进口棉花的合同

1955-08-24
第1版()
专栏：

　　中埃签订埃及自中国进口钢材的协议书
　　并签订中国自埃及进口棉花的合同
    新华社二十三日讯　埃及共和国政府贸易代表团顾问穆罕默德·阿卜杜勒·莫宁·赛义德代表埃及钢铁进口商及有关当局同中国五金进口公司，在北京经过友好的商谈以后，在二十三日根据两国所签订的中埃贸易协定和第一个协定年度议定书，签订了第一个埃及自中国进口钢材六万吨的协议书。
    代表埃及方面签字的是埃及共和国政府贸易代表团顾问穆罕默德·阿卜杜勒·莫宁·赛义德，代表中国方面签字的是中国五金进口公司经理周化民。
    新华社二十三日讯　中国进出口公司同埃及共和国棉花委员会和密斯尔棉花出口公司，经过在北京商谈以后，在二十三日根据两国所签订的中埃两国贸易协定和第一个协定年度的议定书，签订了第一个中国自埃及进口棉花四万五千包（一万五千吨）的合同。
    代表中国方面签字的是中国进出口公司副经理倪蔚庭。代表埃及方面签字的是埃及棉花委员会主任艾哈默德·纳吉布和密斯尔棉花出口公司总经理伊斯梅尔·沙费尔。
