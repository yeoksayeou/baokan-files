Title: World Federation of Democratic Youth Council Holds Meeting in Warsaw, Calls on Youth of All Countries to Strive for Peace and Promote Friendship
Author: 
Date: 1955-08-20
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency, Warsaw, August 19th: The Council of the World Federation of Democratic Youth held a meeting in Warsaw from the 16th to the 18th. Three hundred and seventy delegates from ninety-three countries participated in the meeting.

　　The meeting heard a report by Bruno Bernini, President of the World Federation of Democratic Youth, on the achievements of the Fifth World Youth Festival and the future tasks of the World Federation of Democratic Youth, and held a warm discussion. In their speeches, the delegates of various countries emphasized that the Geneva Four-Power Summit has created good prospects for further easing international tensions. Therefore, the youth of all countries should redouble their efforts to strive for the establishment of lasting peace and friendship between the people of all countries.

　　After the discussion, the meeting adopted a resolution on accepting youth organizations from many countries to join the World Federation of Democratic Youth, and elected a new Executive Committee of the World Federation of Democratic Youth consisting of forty-five people. Bruno Bernini was re-elected as President of the World Federation of Democratic Youth, and Jacques Denis was re-elected as Secretary-General of the World Federation of Democratic Youth.

　　The meeting then adopted a resolution on "The Achievements of the Fifth World Youth Festival and the Future Tasks of the World Federation of Democratic Youth." The resolution pointed out that the success of this festival clearly shows that the World Federation of Democratic Youth is capable of more successfully completing the great tasks it faces in the struggle for the interests of youth. The Council called on young men and women of all countries and their organizations to spread the achievements of this festival around the world and to promote the ideals of peace and friendship.

　　The Council welcomed the many recent victories of the world's peace forces, and pointed out that further efforts must be made to consolidate peace. The Council recommends that young men and women of all countries, together with the people of the world, take various measures to strengthen their activities and strive for world peace and the promotion of friendship and mutual understanding among the people of all countries.

　　The resolution said that, considering the important role played by young women and their special needs, the Council decided to convene a representative conference of young women's organizations in 1956.

　　The resolution pointed out that vigorously carrying out cultural exchanges and sports activities between countries and internationally is of great significance for strengthening the friendship and understanding of young people of all countries. In this regard, the Council put forward many specific measures to be taken by youth organizations of various countries.

　　The resolution emphasized the World Federation of Democratic Youth's fervent desire to promote world youth solidarity, and hopes to cooperate with other youth forces on the basis of mutual benefit, mutual respect, and mutual assistance, and to take joint action with them to solve the problems facing world youth.

　　The resolution concluded by saying that this year marks the tenth anniversary of the founding of the World Federation of Democratic Youth. The Council is convinced that the World Federation of Democratic Youth will strengthen and expand its ranks and know how to better accomplish its tasks.

　　Finally, the Council meeting of the World Federation of Democratic Youth also adopted a resolution on holding the Sixth World Youth and Student Peace and Friendship Festival in Moscow in 1957.

### 世界民主青联理事会在华沙举行会议  号召各国青年争取和平促进友谊

1955-08-20
第4版()
专栏：

    世界民主青联理事会在华沙举行会议
    号召各国青年争取和平促进友谊
    新华社华沙十九日电　世界民主青年联盟理事会从十六日到十八日在华沙举行会议。参加这次会议的有九十三个国家的三百七十名代表。
    会议听取了世界民主青年联盟主席布伦诺·柏尼尼关于第五届世界青年联欢节的成就和世界民主青年联盟在今后的任务的报告，并且展开了热烈的讨论。各国代表们在发言中强调指出：日内瓦四国政府首脑会议为进一步缓和国际紧张局势创造了良好的前景，因此，各国青年应该加倍努力，为争取建立持久和平和各国人民之间的友谊而斗争。
    会议在讨论结束后通过了关于接受许多国家的青年组织参加世界民主青年联盟的决定，选出了由四十五人组成的世界民主青年联盟理事会新的执行委员会。布伦诺·柏尼尼重新当选为世界民主青年联盟主席，雅克·德尼重新当选为世界民主青年联盟总书记。
    会议接着通过了关于“第五届世界青年联欢节的成就和世界民主青年联盟今后的任务”的决议。决议指出，本届联欢节的成功清楚地说明，世界民主青年联盟是有能力更成功地去完成在为青年的利益而斗争的工作中所面临的伟大任务的。理事会号召各国青年男女和他们的组织在世界各地传播这届联欢节的成就，宣扬和平和友谊的理想。
    理事会欢迎世界和平力量最近取得的许多胜利，同时指出必须作进一步努力来巩固和平。理事会建议各国青年男女和世界人民一道，采取各种方式，加强他们的活动，为争取世界和平、促进各国人民之间的友谊和相互了解而斗争。
    决议说，由于考虑到青年妇女所起的重要作用和她们的特殊需要，理事会决定在一九五六年召开青年妇女组织的代表会议。
    决议指出，大力开展各国和国际间的文化交流和体育活动，对于加强各国青年的友谊和了解具有重要意义。在这方面，理事会向各国青年组织提出了建议采取的许多具体措施。
    决议强调说明世界民主青年联盟促进世界青年团结的热烈愿望，并且希望在互利、互相尊重、互相帮助的基础上同其他青年力量合作，同它们采取联合行动来解决世界青年面临的问题。
    决议最后说，今年是世界民主青年联盟成立十周年纪念。理事会深信，世界民主青年联盟将会加强和扩大自己的队伍，并且懂得怎样才能更好地完成它的任务。
    世界民主青年联盟理事会会议最后还通过了关于一九五七年在莫斯科举行第六届世界青年与学生和平友谊联欢节的决议。
