Title: Mirza Assumes Office as Acting Governor-General of Pakistan
Author:
Date: 1955-08-10
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, New Delhi, August 8th: Karachi dispatch: Pakistani Interior Minister Iskander Mirza assumed office as Acting Governor-General of Pakistan on the 7th. Prior to this, the Pakistani government announced on the 6th that the Governor-General of Pakistan, Ghulam Muhammad, had been granted two months' leave due to illness. Queen Elizabeth II of England has approved the appointment of Mirza as Acting Governor-General of Pakistan.

### 米尔扎就任巴代理总督

1955-08-10
第4版()
专栏：

    米尔扎就任巴代理总督
    新华社新德里八日电　喀喇蚩消息：巴基斯坦内政部长伊斯坎德尔·米尔扎已在七日就任巴基斯坦代理总督。在这以前，据巴基斯坦政府六日宣布，巴基斯坦总督吴拉姆·穆罕默德因病获准休假两个月。英国女王伊丽莎白二世已经批准任命米尔扎为巴基斯坦代理总督。
