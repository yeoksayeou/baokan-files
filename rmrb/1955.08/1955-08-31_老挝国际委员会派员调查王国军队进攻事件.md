Title: Lao International Commission Sends Personnel to Investigate Kingdom Army Attack Incident
Author:
Date: 1955-08-31
Page: 4

　　Xinhua News Agency, Hanoi, August 30th: Lao news: At the request of the General Headquarters of the Lao Fighting Forces, the Lao International Commission has dispatched a group to the areas of Na Xing, Ban Chang, Gu Hai, Sha Lei, Na Sa Mong, etc., on August 22nd. They are preparing to investigate incidents such as the Lao Kingdom government army attacking the Lao Fighting Forces in these areas.

### 老挝国际委员会派员调查王国军队进攻事件

1955-08-31
第4版()
专栏：

　　老挝国际委员会派员调查王国军队进攻事件
    新华社河内三十日电　老挝消息：根据寮国战斗部队总司令部的要求，老挝国际委员会已派遣一个小组在八月二十二日动身到那馨、班敞、古海、沙雷、那萨蒙等地区去。他们准备就老挝王国政府军队在这些地区进攻寮国战斗部队等事件进行调查。
