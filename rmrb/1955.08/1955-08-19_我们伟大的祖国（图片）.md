    Our Great Motherland
    Guizhou Province has expanded the area for planting flue-cured tobacco this year by 30 percent compared to last year. The He Ping Agricultural Production Cooperative in Kaili Town, Lushan Miao Autonomous County, has achieved a bumper harvest of flue-cured tobacco.

### 我们伟大的祖国（图片）

1955-08-19
第1版()
专栏：

    我们伟大的祖国
    贵州省今年种植烤烟的面积比去年扩大了百分之三十。炉山苗族自治县凯里镇和平农业生产合作社种植的烤烟获得丰收。
