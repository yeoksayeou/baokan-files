Title: Youth Representatives from Sixteen Countries Including Argentina Arrive in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1955-08-31
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, August 30th. Forty-eight youth representatives from sixteen countries arrived in Beijing at 6:20 PM on the international train on August 30th.

These youth representatives are visiting China at the invitation of the Central Committee of the Chinese New Democratic Youth League and the All-China Federation of Democratic Youth after participating in the Fifth World Youth Festival. They are from the following countries: Argentina, Brazil, Chile, Cuba, Spain, United States, Iraq, Kuwait, Madagascar, Morocco, Mexico, Paraguay, Portugal, Union of South Africa, Uruguay, Venezuela.

When the youth representatives from various countries arrived at the Beijing Railway Station by train, warm applause and cheers rang out at the station. More than one hundred young people in Beijing rushed forward to offer them flowers and warmly embraced, shook hands, and kissed them. Then, the youth representatives from various countries joined hands with the Chinese youth and sang "The March of the World Democratic Youth" in different languages while walking, and shouted "Peace! Long Live!"

Present at the station today to welcome them were the responsible persons and representatives of the Central Committee of the Chinese New Democratic Youth League and the All-China Federation of Democratic Youth.

### 阿根廷等十六国青年代表到达北京

1955-08-31
第4版()
专栏：

　　阿根廷等十六国青年代表到达北京
    新华社三十日讯　来自十六个国家的四十八位青年代表，在三十日下午六时二十分乘国际列车到达北京。
    这些青年代表是在参加第五届世界青年联欢节以后，应中国新民主主义青年团中央委员会和中华全国民主青年联合会的邀请来中国访问的。他们是来自下列各国：阿根廷、巴西、智利、古巴、西班牙、美国、伊拉克、科威特、马达加斯加、摩洛哥、墨西哥、巴拉圭、葡萄牙、南非联邦、乌拉圭、委内瑞拉。
    当各国青年代表们乘车到达北京车站时，车站上响起了热烈的掌声和欢呼声。北京一百多位青年拥上前去，向他们献花，并和他们热情地拥抱、握手和接吻。然后，各国青年代表们又和中国青年们一起手挽着手，一边走一边用不同的语言，同声歌唱着“世界民主青年进行曲”，高呼着“和平！万岁！”
    今天到车站迎接的，有中国新民主主义青年团中央委员会和中华全国民主青年联合会的负责人和代表。
