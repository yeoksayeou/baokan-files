Title: Sino-American Ambassadors Hold Fifth Meeting
Author:
Date: 1955-08-12
Page: 1

    Xinhua News Agency, Geneva, August 11th: The Sino-American ambassadorial talks held their fifth meeting here at the Palace of Nations this morning at 10:00 AM (local time). The meeting adjourned at 11:30 AM and will reconvene on August 13th at 10:00 AM.

    At the August 11th meeting, the two ambassadors continued to exchange views on the first item on the agenda - the return of civilians to their respective countries.

### 中美两国大使举行第五次会谈

1955-08-12
第1版()
专栏：

    中美两国大使举行第五次会谈
    新华社日内瓦十一日电　中美两国大使级会谈十一日上午十时（当地时间）在这里的国联大厦举行第五次会议。会议在十一时三十分休会，至八月十三日上午十时再开。
    在十一日会议上，两国大使继续就会谈的第一项议程——双方平民回国问题——交换了意见。
