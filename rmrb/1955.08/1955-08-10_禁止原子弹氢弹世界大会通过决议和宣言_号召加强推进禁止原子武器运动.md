Title: World Conference Against Atomic and Hydrogen Bombs Passes Resolution and Declaration, Calling for Strengthening and Promoting the Movement to Ban Atomic Weapons
Author:
Date: 1955-08-10
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, Hiroshima, 9th. The World Conference Against Atomic and Hydrogen Bombs held a plenary meeting on the 8th. This was the final meeting of the three-day Hiroshima conference, which began on the 6th. The conference passed a resolution and declaration, calling on the people of the world to strengthen and promote the movement to ban atomic weapons.

    The meeting on the 8th was chaired by eight executive chairmen, including Nino Friere, head of the World Peace Council delegation and secretary of the World Peace Council. Approximately two thousand representatives from all over Japan and foreign representatives attended the meeting.

    Yasui Kaoru, Secretary of the National Council for the Signature Campaign Against Atomic and Hydrogen Bombs in Japan, announced at the meeting that the number of people throughout Japan who had signed the "Appeal to the People of the World" against atomic weapons and preparations for atomic war had increased to more than 32,160,000 by 10:00 PM on August 7th. The number of signatures worldwide has reached 660 million. He also requested all those attending the conference to urge politicians and opinion leaders at home and abroad to vigorously promote the movement to ban atomic and hydrogen bombs. He also suggested establishing a permanent body to promote this movement.

    Sofia Vasilkovska, a member of the Parliament of the Polish People's Republic, and Kidwai, a member of the Indian delegation and a member of the Indian Parliament, spoke at the meeting. They unanimously emphasized that preventing the tragedy of Hiroshima from recurring is the call of the people of the world, and the people of the world must unite for peace.

    The meeting decided to send a telegram to the International Conference on the Peaceful Uses of Atomic Energy, which opened in Geneva, urging the representatives attending the conference to work together to ban atomic and hydrogen bombs and save mankind from the terror of atomic weapons.

    The meeting adopted a resolution and declaration in warm applause. The resolution included: demanding an international conference with participation of the Soviet Union, China, the United States, the United Kingdom, France and India to ease international tensions; opposing the transport of atomic weapons into Japan, and demanding the withdrawal of the US Atomic Bomb Casualty Commission stationed in Hiroshima from Japan; launching a campaign to raise a relief fund for atomic bomb victims and their families; demanding the enactment of a law providing assistance to atomic disease patients.

    The conference declaration called on the people of the world to further vigorously promote the movement to ban atomic and hydrogen bombs. The declaration said that our movement must continue until mankind achieves true peace.

    Finally, all the representatives sang "The Song Against the Use of Atomic Bombs," and the Hiroshima conference of the meeting closed.

    The conference will formally conclude all its activities after participating in the Peace Festival to be held in Tokyo on August 15th. Before this, the representatives will investigate the atomic bomb damage in Hiroshima and Nagasaki.

### 禁止原子弹氢弹世界大会通过决议和宣言  号召加强推进禁止原子武器运动

1955-08-10
第1版()
专栏：

    禁止原子弹氢弹世界大会通过决议和宣言
    号召加强推进禁止原子武器运动
    新华社九日讯　广岛消息：禁止原子弹和氢弹世界大会在八日举行全体会议。这是从六日开始的为期三天的大会广岛会议的最后一次会议。会议通过了决议和宣言，号召世界人民加强推进禁止原子武器的运动。
    八日的会议由世界和平理事会代表团团长、世界和平理事会书记尼诺·弗利雅雷西等八人担任执行主席。参加会议的有来自日本各地的代表和外国代表约两千人。
    日本禁止原子弹和氢弹签名运动全国协议会事务局长安井郁在会上宣布，日本全国在“告全世界人民书”上签名反对原子武器和准备原子战争的人数，到八月七日下午十时为止，又增加到三千二百一十六万多人。全世界的签名人数已经达到六亿六千万人。他并且要求所有参加这次大会的人们，推动国内外的政治家和舆论界，强有力地推进要求禁止原子弹和氢弹的运动。他还建议设立推行这个运动的常设机构。
    波兰人民共和国议会议员索非亚·瓦西尔科夫斯卡和印度代表团团员、印度国会议员吉德瓦尼等在会上讲话。他们一致强调说：不许广岛悲剧重演是全世界人民的呼声，全世界人民为了和平必须团结起来。
    会议决定致电在日内瓦开幕的和平利用原子能国际会议，吁请参加这个会议的代表们共同努力来禁止原子弹和氢弹，使人类免受原子武器的恐怖。
      会议在热烈的掌声中通过了决议和宣言。决议的内容是：要求举行有苏联、中国、美国、英国、法国和印度参加的国际会议来缓和国际紧张局势；反对把原子武器运进日本，并且要求美国设在广岛的原子弹灾害委员会从日本撤走；发起一个为原子弹受害者和他们的家属募集救济基金的运动；要求颁布一项规定给予原子病患者以援助的法律。
    大会宣言号召全世界人民进一步强有力地推进禁止原子弹和氢弹运动。宣言说，我们的运动必须开展下去，直到人类获得真正的和平为止。
    最后，全体代表合唱“不准使用原子弹之歌”，大会的广岛会议至此闭幕。
    大会将在参加八月十五日在东京举行的和平联欢节后，正式结束它的全部活动。在这以前，代表们将考察广岛和长崎两地遭受原子弹灾害的情况。
