Title: Indonesian Ambassador Holds Celebration Banquet on the Eve of the Tenth Anniversary of Indonesian Independence
Author:
Date: 1955-08-17
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, August 16th—On the eve of the tenth anniversary of the Republic of Indonesia's National Day, August 16th, the Indonesian Ambassador to China, Mononutu, held a celebration banquet.

Invited to attend the reception were: Premier of the State Council Zhou Enlai, Vice Premier Peng Dehuai, Minister of Foreign Trade Ye Jizhuang, Minister of Labor Ma Wenrui, Minister of Higher Education Yang Xiufeng, Vice Minister of Foreign Affairs Zhang Hanfu, Deputy Chief of General Staff of the Chinese People's Liberation Army Zhang Zongxun, Vice President of the China-Indonesia Friendship Association Hu Yuzhi, and others.

At the banquet, Indonesian Ambassador Mononutu and Premier Zhou Enlai spoke successively. They both talked about the development of friendly relations between China and Indonesia in recent years and expressed the hope that this friendly relationship will continue to develop and be consolidated in the future.

During the banquet, the hosts and guests repeatedly toasted the friendship between the People's Republic of China and the Republic of Indonesia, the peace of Asia and the world, and the prosperity and happiness of the two peoples.

### 在印度尼西亚独立十周年前夕  印度尼西亚大使举行庆祝宴会

1955-08-17
第1版()
专栏：

    在印度尼西亚独立十周年前夕
    印度尼西亚大使举行庆祝宴会
    新华社十六日讯　印度尼西亚驻华大使莫诺努图，在印度尼西亚共和国国庆十周年的前夕——八月十六日晚，举行庆祝宴会。
    应邀出席招待会的有：国务院总理周恩来、副总理彭德怀，对外贸易部部长叶季壮，劳动部部长马文瑞，高等教育部部长杨秀峰，外交部副部长章汉夫，中国人民解放军副总参谋长张宗逊，中国印度尼西亚友好协会副会长胡愈之等。
    宴会上，印度尼西亚大使莫诺努图和周恩来总理先后讲话。他们都谈到了近年来中国、印度尼西亚两国间友好关系的发展情况，并希望这种友好关系在今后能继续得到发展和巩固。
    席间，宾主一再为中华人民共和国和印度尼西亚共和国的友谊，为亚洲和世界的和平，为两国人民的繁荣和幸福而干杯。
