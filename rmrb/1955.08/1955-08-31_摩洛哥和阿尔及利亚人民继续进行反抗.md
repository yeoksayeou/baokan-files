Title: Moroccan and Algerian People Continue to Resist
Author:
Date: 1955-08-31
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency, Paris, August 30th: Throughout Morocco and Algeria, local patriotic people continue to resist the French colonial military police who are suppressing them.
　　An Agence France-Presse reporter reported that airplanes have been continuously transporting soldiers and military supplies to Algeria and Morocco in the past three days. In Morocco, French troops continued their military suppression operations in the Khenifra region on the 29th. Meanwhile, according to Paris radio, conflicts occurred again in Rabat on the 29th. Fires broke out in Fez and Meknes. Local people attacked the French colonizers' train from Casablanca to Rabat and cut off the telephone lines in Marrakesh. Near Constantine in Algeria, French colonial troops had armed conflicts with local patriots.

### 摩洛哥和阿尔及利亚人民继续进行反抗

1955-08-31
第4版()
专栏：

　　摩洛哥和阿尔及利亚人民继续进行反抗
    新华社三十日讯　巴黎消息：在摩洛哥和阿尔及利亚各地，当地爱国人民继续反抗镇压他们的法国殖民军警。
    法新社记者报道，过去三天中不断有飞机向阿尔及利亚和摩洛哥运送士兵和军用物资。在摩洛哥，法国军队二十九日继续在肯尼弗拉地区进行军事镇压行动。同时，据巴黎电台广播，二十九日在拉巴特又发生了冲突事件。在非斯和麦克尼斯发生了火灾。当地人民袭击了从卡萨布兰卡到拉巴特的法国殖民者的火车，并且切断了马拉喀什的电话线。在阿尔及利亚的君士坦丁附近，法国殖民军队同当地爱国者发生了武装冲突。
