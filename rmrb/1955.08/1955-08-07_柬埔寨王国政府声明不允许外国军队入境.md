Title: Kingdom of Cambodia Government Statement: Foreign Troops Not Permitted to Enter

Author:

Date: 1955-08-07
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Hanoi, 5th. Phnom Penh dispatch: The Royal Government of Cambodia issued a statement on the 4th, saying that Cambodia will handle its own defense and will not allow foreign troops to enter Cambodian territory for this purpose.

Reuters reported that the Cambodian government's statement was announced after Thai Prime Minister Pibul Songgram recently made a statement saying that Thailand would send troops to Laos within the framework of the so-called Manila Treaty Organization.

### 柬埔寨王国政府声明不允许外国军队入境

1955-08-07
第4版()
专栏：

    柬埔寨王国政府声明不允许外国军队入境
    新华社河内五日电　金边消息：柬埔寨王国政府四日发表声明说，柬埔寨将自己处理它的防务，而不允许外国军队为这个目的进入柬埔寨的领土。
    路透社说，柬埔寨王国政府的声明是在泰国总理銮披汶最近发表声明后宣布的。銮披汶的声明说，泰国将在所谓马尼拉条约组织的范围内派遣军队到老挝去。
