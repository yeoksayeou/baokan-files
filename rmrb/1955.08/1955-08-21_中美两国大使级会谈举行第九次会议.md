Title: Ninth Meeting of Sino-US Ambassadorial Talks Held

Author:
Date: 1955-08-21
Page: 1

　　Xinhua News Agency, Geneva, 20th — The ninth meeting of the Sino-US ambassadorial talks was held at 10:00 am local time on the 20th. The meeting adjourned at 11:30 am. The next meeting will be held at 10:00 am on August 23rd (next Tuesday). At the meeting on the 20th, the two ambassadors continued to discuss the first item on the agenda – the return of civilians to their respective countries.

### 中美两国大使级会谈举行第九次会议

1955-08-21
第1版()
专栏：

　　中美两国大使级会谈举行第九次会议
　　新华社日内瓦二十日电　中美两国大使级会谈在二十日当地时间上午十时举行第九次会议。会议在十一时三十分休会，下次会议将在八月二十三日（下星期二）上午十时举行。在二十日会议上，双方大使继续讨论了第一项议程——双方平民回国问题。
