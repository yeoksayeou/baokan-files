Title: Vice Premier He Long Returns to Beijing
Author:
Date: 1955-08-09
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, August 8th. Vice Premier He Long, head of the government delegation of the People's Republic of China, who was invited to Warsaw to attend the closing ceremony of the 10th anniversary celebration of the founding of People's Poland, returned to Beijing on the 8th.

Those who welcomed him at the airport included Zeng Yifan, Deputy Secretary-General of the State Council, and He Wei, Assistant Minister of Foreign Affairs.

A representative from the Polish Embassy in China also greeted him at the airport.

### 贺龙副总理回京

1955-08-09
第1版()
专栏：

    贺龙副总理回京
    新华社八日讯　应邀到华沙参加人民波兰成立十周年纪念年闭幕典礼的中华人民共和国政府代表团团长贺龙副总理在八日回到北京。
    到机场欢迎的，有国务院副秘书长曾一凡、外交部部长助理何伟。
    波兰驻华大使馆的代表也到机场迎接。
