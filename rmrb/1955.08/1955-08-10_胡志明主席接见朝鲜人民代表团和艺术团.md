Title: Chairman Ho Chi Minh Receives Delegation and Art Troupe from North Korea
Author:
Date: 1955-08-10
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency, Hanoi, August 9th: Ho Chi Minh, President of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam, received the North Korean People's Delegation at the Presidential Palace at 6:15 PM on August 8th. The delegation included Chairman Baek Nam-un, Vice-Chairmen Heo Seong-taek, Jang Ping-san, and Jang Ha-il, as well as the head of the Korean Art Troupe, Cho Hak-rae, and the deputy head, Choe Chang-eun.

Present at the reception were Pham Van Dong, Vice-Premier of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam; Vo Nguyen Giap, Minister of National Defense and Commander-in-Chief of the Vietnamese People's Army; Ton Duc Thang, Chairman of the Standing Committee of the National Assembly of Vietnam and Chairman of the National Committee of the Vietnam Lien Viet Front; Truong Chinh, General Secretary of the Vietnam Workers' Party; and Hoang Quoc Viet, Vice-Chairman of the National Committee of the Vietnam Lien Viet Front and member of the Politburo of the Vietnam Workers' Party.

Also present was Seo Cheol, Ambassador of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea to the Democratic Republic of Vietnam.

During the reception, Baek Nam-un, the head of the Korean People's Delegation, presented gifts from Premier Kim Il-sung and the Korean people to President Ho Chi Minh and the government of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam.

At 6:30 PM, President Ho Chi Minh also received all members of the Korean People's Delegation and the Korean Art Troupe.

At 7:00 PM, the Korean Art Troupe performed songs and dances for President Ho Chi Minh, heads of various departments of the Vietnamese government, members of the Standing Committee of the National Assembly of Vietnam, and members of the National Committee of the Vietnam Lien Viet Front.

After the performance, the government of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam, the National Assembly of Vietnam, and the National Committee of the Vietnam Lien Viet Front jointly held a reception for all members of the Korean People's Delegation and the Korean Art Troupe.

Diplomatic envoys from various countries to the Democratic Republic of Vietnam also watched the performance and attended the reception.

### 胡志明主席接见朝鲜人民代表团和艺术团

1955-08-10
第4版()
专栏：

    胡志明主席接见朝鲜人民代表团和艺术团
    新华社河内九日电　越南民主共和国主席胡志明八日晚六时十五分在主席府接见了朝鲜人民代表团团长白南云，副团长许成泽、张平山、张河一，以及朝鲜艺术团团长赵鹤来、副团长崔昌恩。
    接见时在座的有越南民主共和国副总理范文同，国防部部长兼越南人民军总司令武元甲，越南国民大会常务委员会主席、越南国民联合战线全国委员会主席孙德胜，越南劳动党总书记长征，越南国民联合战线全国委员会副主席、越南劳动党政治局委员黄国越等人。
    接见时在座的还有朝鲜民主主义人民共和国驻越南民主共和国大使徐哲。
    在胡志明主席接见时，朝鲜人民代表团团长白南云代表金日成首相和朝鲜人民向胡志明主席和越南民主共和国政府赠礼。
    六时三十分，胡志明主席又接见了朝鲜人民代表团和朝鲜艺术团的全体团员。
    七时正，朝鲜艺术团为胡志明主席和越南政府各部门负责人，越南国民大会常务委员会和越南国民联合战线全国委员会的委员们演出了歌舞节目。
    表演结束后，越南民主共和国政府、越南国民大会和越南国民联合战线全国委员会联合举行酒会招待朝鲜人民代表团和朝鲜艺术团的全体人员。
    各国驻越南民主共和国的外交使节也观看了表演和出席了酒会。
