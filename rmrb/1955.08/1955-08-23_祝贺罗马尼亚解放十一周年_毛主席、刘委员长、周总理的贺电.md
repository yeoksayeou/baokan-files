Author:
Date: 1955-08-23
Page: 1

Congratulations on the 11th Anniversary of the Liberation of Romania
Congratulatory Messages from Chairman Mao, Chairman Liu, and Premier Zhou

To Comrade P. Groza, Chairman of the Presidium of the National Assembly of the Romanian People's Republic:
To Comrade G. Gheorghiu-Dej, Chairman of the Council of Ministers of the Romanian People's Republic:
To Comrade S. Burgei, Minister of Foreign Affairs of the Romanian People's Republic:

On the occasion of the 11th anniversary of the liberation of Romania, we, on behalf of the government and people of the People's Republic of China, extend our heartfelt and warm congratulations to you, and through you, to the government and people of the Romanian People's Republic.

The government and people of the People's Republic of China are watching with great excitement the tremendous achievements made by the Romanian People's Republic in the cause of socialist construction under the leadership of the Romanian Workers' Party over the past eleven years. This achievement has strengthened the power of the peace, democracy, and socialist camp headed by the Soviet Union. We wish the fraternal Romanian people new victories in consolidating and prospering their motherland and in safeguarding peace in Europe and the world. We wish the unbreakable friendship between China and Romania further consolidation and development.

　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　Mao Zedong, Chairman of the People's Republic of China
　　　Liu Shaoqi, Chairman of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress of the People's Republic of China
　　　　　　　　　Zhou Enlai, Premier and Minister of Foreign Affairs of the State Council of the People's Republic of China
　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　August 21, 1955

### 祝贺罗马尼亚解放十一周年  毛主席、刘委员长、周总理的贺电

1955-08-23
第1版()
专栏：

　　祝贺罗马尼亚解放十一周年
　　毛主席、刘委员长、周总理的贺电罗马尼亚人民共和国国民议会主席团主席彼·格罗查同志：罗马尼亚人民共和国部长会议主席格·乔治乌—德治同志：罗马尼亚人民共和国外交部长西·布吉奇同志：
    值此罗马尼亚解放十一周年之际，我们谨代表中华人民共和国政府和人民向你们，并通过你们向罗马尼亚人民共和国政府和人民致衷心的热烈的祝贺。
    中华人民共和国政府和人民怀着万分兴奋的心情注视着罗马尼亚人民共和国十一年来在罗马尼亚工人党的领导下在社会主义的建设事业中所取得的巨大成就。这个成就加强了以苏联为首的和平、民主、社会主义阵营的力量。祝兄弟的罗马尼亚人民在巩固和繁荣自己的祖国以及保卫欧洲和世界的和平的事业中获得新的胜利。祝中罗两国牢不可破的友谊进一步巩固和发展。
　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　中华人民共和国主席　毛泽东
　　　中华人民共和国全国人民代表大会常务委员会委员长　刘少奇
　　　　　　　　　中华人民共和国国务院总理兼外交部长　周恩来
　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　一九五五年八月二十一日
