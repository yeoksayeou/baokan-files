Title: Egyptian Prime Minister Nasser Accepts Invitation to Visit the Soviet Union
Author:
Date: 1955-08-12
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Cairo, August 10th, TASS: The Egyptian government issued a communique stating that the Soviet government has invited Egyptian Prime Minister Gamal Abdel Nasser to visit the Soviet Union at his convenience. Prime Minister Nasser has accepted the invitation and plans to visit the Soviet Union in the spring of 1956.

### 埃及总理纳赛尔接受访问苏联的邀请

1955-08-12
第4版()
专栏：

    埃及总理纳赛尔接受访问苏联的邀请
    新华社十一日讯　塔斯社开罗十日讯：埃及政府发表公报说，苏联政府邀请埃及总理加麦尔·阿卜杜勒·纳赛尔在他方便的时候去访问苏联。纳赛尔总理已经接受了这个邀请，并且打算在一九五六年春天去苏联访问。
