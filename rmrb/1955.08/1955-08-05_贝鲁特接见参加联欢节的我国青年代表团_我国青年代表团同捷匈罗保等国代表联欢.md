Title: Beirut Receives Chinese Youth Delegation Participating in the Festival; Chinese Youth Delegation Holds Get-Together with Representatives from Czechoslovakia, Hungary, Romania, Bulgaria, and Other Countries
Author:
Date: 1955-08-05
Page: 4

    Xinhua News Agency, Warsaw, August 4th: The First Secretary of the Central Committee of the Polish United Workers' Party, Bolesław Bierut, received the Chinese Youth Delegation participating in the Fifth World Youth Festival on August 2nd.
    Representatives of the Chinese Youth Delegation presented some gifts to Bierut, including an embroidery depicting the scenery of Warsaw and a huge porcelain vase painted with a portrait of the Polish poet Adam Mickiewicz.
    Bierut warmly thanked the Chinese delegation, expressing his gratitude for these beautiful gifts that symbolize the deep friendship between the Chinese and Polish people.
    According to Xinhua News Agency: The Chinese Youth Delegation participating in the World Youth Festival held a get-together on August 2nd with youth representatives from Czechoslovakia, Hungary, Romania, Bulgaria, Albania, Vietnam, Korea, Mongolia, Germany, and Austria.
    The get-together with youth representatives from Czechoslovakia, Hungary, Romania, Bulgaria, Albania, Vietnam, Korea, and Mongolia was held on the morning of August 2nd. This get-together was a vivid demonstration of the fraternal friendship and solidarity among the youth of the peaceful and democratic camp. The get-together began with cultural performances by youth from various countries. Young singers sang in each other's languages. The Chinese youth's performance of "Following Rakosi" in Hungarian and a Romanian folk song were warmly welcomed. An Albanian youth singing "Ode to Mao Zedong, Leader of the Chinese People" in Chinese aroused cheers from the audience.
    The get-together proceeded in an extremely warm atmosphere, and finally concluded with the chorus of "March of the World's Democratic Youth."
    The get-togethers with the German and Austrian youth delegations were held separately on the afternoon of August 2nd. The head of the German delegation and First Secretary of the Free German Youth League, Namokel, delivered a speech at the beginning of the get-together. During the get-together, Chinese youth and youth from various parts of Germany held conversations and exchanged souvenirs.
    The Austrian youth showed the excitement of newly liberated and independent people during their get-together with the Chinese youth. The head of the Austrian youth delegation emphasized that Austrian youth demanded strengthened friendship and solidarity with youth from all countries and praised China's contribution to the cause of defending peace. Qu Tangliang, deputy head of the Chinese Youth Delegation, said in his speech that Chinese youth understood the feelings of Austrian youth in gaining freedom and independence, and were willing to establish close friendship with them. His words were warmly welcomed, and the Austrian youth cheered "Mao Zedong!" "Mao Zedong!" for as long as ten minutes.

### 贝鲁特接见参加联欢节的我国青年代表团  我国青年代表团同捷匈罗保等国代表联欢

1955-08-05
第4版()
专栏：

    贝鲁特接见参加联欢节的我国青年代表团
    我国青年代表团同捷匈罗保等国代表联欢
    新华社华沙四日电　波兰统一工人党中央委员会第一书记贝鲁特在八月二日接见参加第五届世界青年联欢节的中国青年代表团。
    中国青年代表团的代表们把一些礼品赠送给贝鲁特，其中包括一幅绣着华沙风景的刺绣和一个画着波兰诗人密茨凯维奇画像的巨大瓷瓶。
    贝鲁特热烈地向中国代表团致谢，感谢这些象征着中国和波兰人民之间深厚的友谊的美丽的礼品。
    据新华社讯　参加世界青年联欢节的中国青年代表团二日同捷克斯洛伐克、匈牙利、罗马尼亚、保加利亚、阿尔巴尼亚、越南、朝鲜、蒙古、德国和奥地利的青年代表举行了联欢。
    同捷克斯洛伐克、匈牙利、罗马尼亚、保加利亚、阿尔巴尼亚、越南、朝鲜和蒙古青年代表的联欢在二日上午举行。这次联欢是和平民主阵营国家的青年之间兄弟般的友谊和团结的生动表现。联欢以各国青年表演文艺节目开始。青年歌手们互相用兄弟国家的语言歌唱。中国青年用匈牙利文唱的“跟着拉科西走”和用罗马尼亚文唱的民歌受到了热烈的欢迎。阿尔巴尼亚青年用中文唱的“歌颂中国人民领袖毛泽东”的歌曲引起了全场的欢呼。
    联欢始终在极为热烈的气氛中进行，最后在合唱“世界民主青年进行曲”的歌声中结束。
    同德国和奥地利青年代表团的联欢是在二日下午分别举行的。德国代表团团长、自由德国青年联盟第一书记纳莫克尔在联欢开始时致词。在联欢中，中国青年和来自德国各地的青年进行交谈，互相交换纪念品。
    奥地利青年在和中国青年联欢时表现了新近获得自由独立的人民的兴奋心情。奥地利青年代表团团长强调奥地利青年要求和各国青年加强友好团结，并且盛赞中国在保卫和平事业中的贡献。中国青年代表团副团长区棠亮致词说，中国青年了解奥地利青年获得自由独立的心情，并且愿意和他们建立亲密的友谊。他的话受到热烈的欢迎，奥地利青年欢呼“毛泽东！”，“毛泽东！”达十分钟之久。
