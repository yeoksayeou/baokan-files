Title: I Attended the Executive Committee Meeting of the Inter-Parliamentary Union Delegation
Author:
Date: 1955-08-07
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, 6th. The Executive Committee of the People's Delegation of the People's Republic of China to the Inter-Parliamentary Union held its first meeting on the afternoon of the 6th.

	The meeting approved Qu Wu, Gong Pusheng, and Wu Maosun as deputy secretaries-general of the Executive Committee, and established various committees.

	At the meeting, Li Yimang and Hu Yuzhi were unanimously elected as members of the Council of the Inter-Parliamentary Union for our country, and a delegation was formed to represent our country at the 44th General Assembly of the Inter-Parliamentary Union.

### 我参加各国议会联盟代表团执委会举行会议

1955-08-07
第1版()
专栏：

    我参加各国议会联盟代表团执委会举行会议
    新华社六日讯　中华人民共和国参加各国议会联盟的人民代表团执行委员会在六日下午举行第一次会议。
    会议通过以屈武、龚普生、吴茂荪为执行委员会副秘书长，并设立了各种委员会。
    会上，一致选出李一氓、胡愈之为我国参加各国议会联盟理事会理事，并组成了我国出席各国议会联盟第四十四届大会代表团。
