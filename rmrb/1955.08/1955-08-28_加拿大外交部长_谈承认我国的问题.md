Title: Canadian Foreign Minister Discusses the Question of Recognizing Our Country
Author:
Date: 1955-08-28
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, August 27th: According to a report by the United Press, Canadian Foreign Minister Lester B. Pearson stated on August 25th at the Vancouver Canadian Women's Club that Canada "will soon re-examine" the question of diplomatically recognizing the government of the People's Republic of China. Pearson said that Canada's recognition of the government of the People's Republic of China would be a step "in line with our own national interests."

### 加拿大外交部长　谈承认我国的问题

1955-08-28
第4版()
专栏：

　　加拿大外交部长
　　谈承认我国的问题
    新华社二十七日讯　据合众社报道，加拿大外交部长皮尔逊八月二十五日在温哥华加拿大妇女俱乐部说，加拿大“不久就要重新研究”在外交上承认中华人民共和国政府的问题。皮尔逊说，加拿大政府承认中华人民共和国政府将是一个“符合我们自己国家的利益”的步骤。
