Title: Sino-U.S. Ambassadorial Talks Hold Third Meeting, Continued Exchange of Views on the Issue of Civilians Returning to Their Countries
Author:
Date: 1955-08-05
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Geneva, August 4th: The third meeting of the ambassador-level talks between the People's Republic of China and the United States of America was held here at the Palais des Nations at 10:00 AM (local time) this morning. The meeting adjourned at 11:45 AM. The next meeting will continue next Monday (the 8th) morning.

At the meeting on the 4th, the Chinese and American ambassadors continued to exchange views on the first item on the agenda of the talks, namely the issue of civilians returning to their respective countries.

### 中美两国大使级会谈举行第三次会议  就双方平民回国问题继续交换了意见

1955-08-05
第1版()
专栏：

    中美两国大使级会谈举行第三次会议
    就双方平民回国问题继续交换了意见
    新华社日内瓦四日电　中华人民共和国与美利坚合众国两国大使级会谈四日上午十时（本地时间）在此间国联大厦举行第三次会议。会议在十一时四十五分休会。下次会议将在下星期一（八日）上午续开。
    在四日会议上，中美两国大使就会谈的第一项议程，即双方平民回国问题继续交换了意见。
