Title: Korean People's Delegation to Vietnam Arrives in Hanoi
Author:
Date: 1955-08-09
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency, Hanoi, August 7th - The Korean People's Delegation to Vietnam and the Korean Art Troupe arrived in Hanoi at 4:30 PM on August 7th.

Those who welcomed them at the station included Hoang Quoc Viet, Vice Chairman of the National United Front of Vietnam and member of the Political Bureau of the Vietnam Workers' Party; Ton Quang Phiet, Vice Chairman of the National United Front of Vietnam and Vice Chairman of the Standing Committee of the National Assembly of Vietnam; Le Dinh Tham, Chairman of the Vietnam Committee for the Defense of World Peace; Hoang Minh Giam, Minister of Propaganda of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam; as well as representatives from various democratic parties, people's organizations, and the Vietnamese government and army, totaling 3,000 people.

Also welcoming them at the station was Xu Zhe, Ambassador of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea to Vietnam.

A warm welcoming ceremony was held at the station.

Hoang Quoc Viet delivered a welcoming speech, saying that the Korean people and the Vietnamese people are one family. The people of Korea and Vietnam have come to know and care for each other even more in the arduous and glorious war of resistance against imperialist aggression. The visit of the Korean People's Delegation and Art Troupe to Vietnam will further strengthen the friendship between the two countries and will contribute to world peace.

The head of the Korean People's Delegation, Bai Nanyun, Minister of Education of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea, expressed gratitude for the welcome from the Vietnamese people. Bai Nanyun said that the people of Korea and Vietnam have forged an unbreakable friendship in the struggle against foreign imperialist aggression. The Korean people sincerely congratulate the heroic Vietnamese people on their glorious victory in restoring the national economy and unifying the country. This visit of the Korean People's Delegation and Art Troupe will strengthen the friendship and cultural exchange between Korea and Vietnam.

When the Korean People's Delegation and the Korean Art Troupe left the station to go to the guest house, they were warmly cheered by tens of thousands of Hanoi citizens gathered along the streets near the station.

The Korean People's Delegation, headed by Bai Nanyun, consisted of a total of fourteen people; the Korean Art Troupe consisted of sixty people, led by Colonel Zhao Helai.

### 朝鲜人民访越代表团到达河内

1955-08-09
第4版()
专栏：

    朝鲜人民访越代表团到达河内
    新华社河内七日电　朝鲜人民访问越南代表团和朝鲜艺术团在八月七日下午四时半到达河内。
    到车站欢迎的有越南国民联合战线全国委员会副主席、越南劳动党政治局委员黄国越，越南国民联合战线全国委员会副主席、越南国民大会常务委员会副主席孙光阀，越南保卫世界和平委员会主席黎廷探，越南民主共和国宣传部部长黄明鉴，以及越南各民主党派、各人民团体以及越南政府和军队的代表等共三千人。
    在车站上欢迎的还有朝鲜民主主义人民共和国驻越南大使徐哲。
    在车站上举行了热烈的欢迎仪式。
    黄国越致欢迎词说，朝鲜人民和越南人民是一家人，朝、越两国人民在反对帝国主义侵略的艰苦、光荣的抗战中更加互相了解，互相关怀。朝鲜人民代表团和艺术团的访问越南将进一步密切两国间的友谊并将有助于世界的和平。
    朝鲜人民代表团团长、朝鲜民主主义人民共和国教育相白南云对越南人民的欢迎表示感谢。白南云说，朝越两国人民在反对外国帝国主义侵略的斗争中结成了牢不可破的友谊，朝鲜人民衷心祝贺英雄的越南人民一定会取得恢复战后国民经济和统一国家的光荣的胜利。这次朝鲜人民代表团和艺术团的访问将加强朝越两国间的友谊和文化交流。
    当朝鲜人民代表团和朝鲜艺术团离开车站前往招待所时，受到拥集在车站附近街道两旁的成千成万河内市民的热烈欢呼。
    以白南云为首的朝鲜人民代表团一行共十四人；朝鲜艺术团共六十人，由赵鹤来上校率领。
