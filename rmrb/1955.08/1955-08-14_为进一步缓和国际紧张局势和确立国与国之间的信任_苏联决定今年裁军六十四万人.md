Title: To Further Ease International Tensions and Establish Trust Between Nations, the Soviet Union Decides to Disarm 640,000 People This Year
Author:
Date: 1955-08-14
Page: 4

    To Further Ease International Tensions and Establish Trust Between Nations
    The Soviet Union Decides to Disarm 640,000 People This Year
    Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, August 13th, TASS dispatch: TASS communique: Recent developments, particularly the results of the Geneva conference of the four-power heads of government, have demonstrated some easing of international tensions.
    In order to further ease international tensions and establish trust between nations, the Soviet government has decided to reduce the number of Soviet armed forces by 640,000 people before December 15, 1955. Demobilized soldiers and sailors will be employed in enterprises, state farms, and collective farms in their places of residence.

### 为进一步缓和国际紧张局势和确立国与国之间的信任  苏联决定今年裁军六十四万人

1955-08-14
第4版()
专栏：

    为进一步缓和国际紧张局势和确立国与国之间的信任
    苏联决定今年裁军六十四万人
    新华社十三日讯　塔斯社莫斯科十二日电：塔斯社公报：最近时期的一些情况，特别是四国政府首脑日内瓦会议的结果证明，国际紧张局势有了一些缓和。
    苏联政府为了进一步缓和国际紧张局势和确立国与国之间的信任，已决定在一九五五年十二月十五日以前把苏联武装部队的人数减少六十四万人。陆、海军的复员军人将在他们居住的地方到企业、国营农场和集体农庄中就业。
