Title: Chairmen of the Two Houses of the Supreme Soviet of the USSR Receive Delegation from the Yugoslav National Assembly
Author:
Date: 1955-08-05
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, August 4th – TASS reports: The Chairman of the Soviet of the Union of the Supreme Soviet of the USSR, V.V. Volkov, and the Chairman of the Soviet of Nationalities, J.J. Latsis, received the delegation of the National Assembly of the Federal People's Republic of Yugoslavia, headed by the President of the Parliament of the People's Republic of Croatia, V. Bakarić, in the Kremlin on the 2nd, and had a cordial conversation with the members of the delegation.

    Following this, the members of the delegation visited the Kremlin. People who were touring there warmly welcomed the Yugoslav guests.

    On the 3rd, the delegation paid their respects at the Lenin-Stalin Mausoleum and laid a wreath.

### 苏联最高苏维埃两院主席  接见南国民议会代表团

1955-08-05
第4版()
专栏：

    苏联最高苏维埃两院主席
    接见南国民议会代表团
    新华社四日讯　据塔斯社莫斯科讯：苏联最高苏维埃联盟院主席沃尔科夫和民族院主席拉齐斯于二日在克里姆林宫接见了以克罗地亚人民共和国议会议长弗·巴卡里奇为首的南斯拉夫联邦人民共和国国民议会代表团，并和代表团人员进行了亲切的交谈。
    接着，代表团人员参观了克里姆林宫。正在这里游览的人热烈欢迎南斯拉夫客人。
    三日，代表团拜谒了列宁—斯大林陵墓，并敬献了花圈。
