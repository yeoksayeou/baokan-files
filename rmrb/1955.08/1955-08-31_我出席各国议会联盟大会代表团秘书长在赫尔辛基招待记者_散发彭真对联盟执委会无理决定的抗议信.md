Title: Secretary-General of the Chinese Delegation to the Inter-Parliamentary Union Conference Receives Journalists in Helsinki; Distributes Peng Zhen's Letter of Protest Against the Union's Executive Committee's Unreasonable Decision
Author:
Date: 1955-08-31
Page: 1

　　Xinhua News Agency, Helsinki, 30th – Li Yimang, Secretary-General of the Delegation of the People's Republic of China to the 44th Inter-Parliamentary Union Conference, held a press conference in Helsinki at 7:00 PM on the 29th. More than 30 reporters from newspapers and news agencies in Finland, the Soviet Union, the United Kingdom, the United States, France, and other countries attended the reception.

　　At the meeting, Li Yimang distributed a letter of protest from Peng Zhen, Chairman of the People's Delegation of the People's Republic of China to the Inter-Parliamentary Union, to Lord Stansgate, Chairman of the Union Council, and Lennart Heljas, Chairman of the 44th Union Conference, regarding the Executive Committee of the Inter-Parliamentary Union's delay in China's participation in the Union.

### 我出席各国议会联盟大会代表团秘书长在赫尔辛基招待记者　散发彭真对联盟执委会无理决定的抗议信

1955-08-31
第1版()
专栏：

　　我出席各国议会联盟大会代表团秘书长在赫尔辛基招待记者
　　散发彭真对联盟执委会无理决定的抗议信
    新华社赫尔辛基三十日电　中华人民共和国出席各国议会联盟第四十四届大会代表团秘书长李一氓二十九日下午七时在赫尔辛基举行记者招待会，出席招待会的有芬兰、苏联、英国、美国、法国等国家的报纸和通讯社记者三十多人。
    会上，李一氓散发了中华人民共和国参加各国议会联盟的人民代表团主席彭真就各国议会联盟执行委员会拖延中国参加联盟一事，给联盟理事会主席斯坦斯盖特勋爵和联盟第四十四届大会主席伦纳特·希尔加斯的抗议信。
