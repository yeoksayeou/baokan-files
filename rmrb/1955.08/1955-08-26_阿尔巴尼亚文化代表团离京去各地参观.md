Title: Albanian Cultural Delegation Leaves Beijing for Visits to Various Locations
Author: 
Date: 1955-08-26
Page: 1
　　Xinhua News Agency, August 25th. After conducting discussions in Beijing with the Chinese government regarding the 1956 implementation plan for the Sino-Albanian Cultural Cooperation Agreement and the Sino-Albanian Broadcasting Cooperation Agreement, and reaching agreements, the Albanian Cultural Delegation departed Beijing by plane on the morning of the 25th to visit various locations in China.
　　During their stay in Beijing, the Albanian Cultural Delegation had extensive contact with cultural circles in the capital and engaged in professional visits. The delegation visited the Ministry of Culture, the Ministry of Education, the Chinese Writers Association, and the Chinese Artists Association, and also held discussions. Qian Junrui, Acting Minister of Culture, discussed the development of cultural work in China and Albania with the delegation, and Dong Chuncai, Vice Minister of Education, discussed issues such as normal education and general education with Hasan Duma, a member of the delegation. Fadil Paçrami, head of the delegation, reported on the achievements of Albanian cultural construction over the past decade to literary and art workers in Beijing, and exchanged experiences in newspaper work between China and Albania with Deng Tuo, editor-in-chief of the People's Daily.
　　The delegation also visited the Museum of Revolutionary History, the National Exhibition of Natural Resources, the National Exhibition of Scientific, Technological and Craft Works of Children and Young People, the Literary Training Institute, the Xu Beihong Memorial Hall, and Rong Bao Zhai, as well as some universities and factories. They also toured scenic spots and historical sites such as the Working People's Cultural Palace, the Forbidden City, Beihai Park, the Temple of Heaven, the Summer Palace, and the Great Wall.

### 阿尔巴尼亚文化代表团离京去各地参观

1955-08-26
第1版()
专栏：

　　阿尔巴尼亚文化代表团离京去各地参观
    新华社二十五日讯　阿尔巴尼亚文化代表团在北京同中国政府就中阿文化合作协定一九五六年执行计划、中阿广播合作协定进行商谈并达成协议后，在二十五日晨乘飞机离北京到中国各地参观访问。
    阿尔巴尼亚文化代表团在北京期间，曾同首都文化界进行广泛的接触，并进行了专业性的参观活动。代表团曾访问了文化部、教育部、中国作家协会和美术家协会，还举行了座谈。文化部代理部长钱俊瑞同代表团交谈了中阿两国文化工作的发展情况，教育部副部长董纯才同代表团团员哈桑·杜玛座谈了师范教育和普通教育等问题。代表团团长法迪尔·帕奇拉米曾向北京市文艺工作者报告了阿尔巴尼亚十年来文化建设的成就，和人民日报总编辑邓拓交流了中阿两国报刊工作的经验。
    代表团还参观了革命历史博物馆、祖国自然资源展览、全国少年儿童科学技术和工艺作品展览会，文学讲习所、徐悲鸿纪念馆、荣宝斋，以及一些高等学校和工厂，还游览了劳动人民文化宫、故宫、北海、天坛、颐和园、长城等名胜古迹。
