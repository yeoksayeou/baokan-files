Title: Chinese Art Troupe Leaves Beijing for Friendly Visit to Vietnam
Author:
Date: 1955-08-24
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, August 23rd: A 170-member Chinese art troupe, led by Ding Xilin, head of the troupe and Vice Minister of Culture, and Wu Baihua, deputy head of the troupe, left Beijing by train for Hanoi on the 23rd. This is the first art troupe sent by China to Vietnam. The art troupe is visiting at the invitation of the government of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam to pay a friendly visit and participate in the upcoming Vietnamese August Revolution and the tenth anniversary of the National Day.
    The art troupe includes a song and dance team and a Peking Opera team. It is scheduled to visit various parts of Vietnam for two and a half months.

### 我国艺术团离北京去越南进行友好访问

1955-08-24
第1版()
专栏：

　　我国艺术团离北京去越南进行友好访问
    新华社二十三日讯　一个由一百七十人组成的中国艺术团，在团长、文化部副部长丁西林、副团长吴白桦率领下，二十三日乘火车前往河内。这是中国派往越南去的第一个艺术团，这个艺术团应越南民主共和国政府邀请前往进行友好访问并参加即将到来的越南八月革命及国庆十周年纪念。
    艺术团包括一个歌舞队和一个京剧队。预定在越南各地作两个半月的访问。
