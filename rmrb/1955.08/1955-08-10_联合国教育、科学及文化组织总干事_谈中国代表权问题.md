Title: UNESCO Director-General Discusses China's Representation Issue
Author: 
Date: 1955-08-10
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, New Delhi, 9th: According to the "Hindustan Standard," the Director-General of UNESCO, Luther Evans, said at a press conference in Madras on the 7th that, legally speaking, UNESCO could accept the People's Republic of China as a member state to replace the Chiang Kai-shek government. He also said that the issue of China's representation should be decided by the United Nations itself.

### 联合国教育、科学及文化组织总干事  谈中国代表权问题

1955-08-10
第4版()
专栏：

    联合国教育、科学及文化组织总干事
    谈中国代表权问题
    新华社新德里九日电　据“印度斯坦旗报”报道，联合国教育、科学及文化组织总干事路德·伊凡斯七日在马德拉斯举行的记者招待会上说：在法律上说，联合国教育、科学及文化组织可以接纳中华人民共和国代替蒋介石政府为会员国。他又说：不过，关于中国的代表权问题应当由联合国本身决定。
