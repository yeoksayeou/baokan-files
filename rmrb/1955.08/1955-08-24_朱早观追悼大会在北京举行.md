Title: Zhu Zaoguan Memorial Service Held in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1955-08-24
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, August 23rd: A memorial service for Zhu Zaoguan, Representative of the National People's Congress, member of the National People's Congress Ethnic Affairs Committee, and Deputy Director of the General Office of the Ministry of National Defense, was held in Beijing at 9:00 a.m. on the 23rd.

Huang Kecheng, Deputy Minister of National Defense, presided over the memorial service. Li Weihan, Su Yu, Gan Siqi, Song Renqiong, Zhang Aiping, Zhang Su, Liu Geping, Xiao Xiangrong, and Shen Bocun attended as pallbearers.

Representatives from the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress, the National Defense Commission, the Ethnic Affairs Committee, the National Committee of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference, various democratic parties, people's organizations, and representatives from the People's Liberation Army units stationed in Beijing attended the memorial service.

Zhu Zaoguan's remains were moved to the Babaoshan Revolutionary Cemetery for burial on the morning of the same day.

### 朱早观追悼大会在北京举行

1955-08-24
第1版()
专栏：

　　朱早观追悼大会在北京举行
    新华社二十三日讯　全国人民代表大会代表、全国人民代表大会民族委员会委员、国防部办公厅副主任朱早观追悼大会二十三日上午九时在北京举行。
    追悼大会由国防部副部长黄克诚主祭。李维汉、粟裕、甘泗淇、宋任穷、张爱萍、张苏、刘格平、萧向荣、申伯纯陪祭。
    参加追悼大会的有全国人民代表大会常务委员会、国防委员会、民族委员会、中国人民政治协商会议全国委员会、各民主党派各人民团体的代表、人民解放军驻京机关部队代表等。
    朱早观遗体在当日上午移八宝山人民公墓安葬。
