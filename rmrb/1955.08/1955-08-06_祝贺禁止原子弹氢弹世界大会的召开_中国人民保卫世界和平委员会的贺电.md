Title: Congratulatory Telegram from the Chinese People's Committee for World Peace on the Convening of the World Conference Against Atomic and Hydrogen Bombs
Author:
Date: 1955-08-06
Page: 1

    Congratulatory Telegram from the Chinese People's Committee for World Peace on the Convening of the World Conference Against Atomic and Hydrogen Bombs
    To the World Conference Against Atomic and Hydrogen Bombs:
    The Chinese People's Committee for World Peace extends its warm congratulations to the World Conference Against Atomic and Hydrogen Bombs.
    The conference will surely help the development of the current powerful movement of people of all countries in the world demanding disarmament and opposing atomic weapons, thereby contributing to the struggle for easing international tensions and ensuring lasting world peace.
    Atomic weapons must be prohibited and destroyed, and atomic energy must be used for peaceful purposes. This is the unanimous and firm demand and the wish that must be realized by the people of the world. We, the Chinese people, are willing to work with the Japanese people and all peace-loving people to promote the realization of the prohibition of atomic weapons and disarmament.

		Chinese People's Committee for World Peace
		August 5, 1955

### 祝贺禁止原子弹氢弹世界大会的召开  中国人民保卫世界和平委员会的贺电

1955-08-06
第1版()
专栏：

    祝贺禁止原子弹氢弹世界大会的召开
    中国人民保卫世界和平委员会的贺电禁止原子弹、氢弹世界大会：
    中国人民保卫世界和平委员会谨向禁止原子弹、氢弹世界大会致以热烈祝贺。
    大会必将有助于当前世界各国人民要求裁减军备和反对原子武器强大运动的发展，从而对争取缓和国际紧张局势和确保世界持久和平的斗争作出贡献。
    原子武器必须禁止和销毁，原子能必须用于和平目的，这是全世界人民一致坚决的要求和必须实现的愿望。我们中国人民愿与日本人民以及所有一切爱好和平的人民，共同致力促使禁止原子武器和裁减军备的实现。		中国人民保卫世界和平委员会		一九五五年八月五日
