Title: Tianjin Circles Hold Rally to Support Indian People in Recovering Goa
Author:
Date: 1955-08-26
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Tianjin, August 25th: A rally was held today in Tianjin by people from all walks of life to support the Indian people in recovering Goa. The conference unanimously resolved: The people of Tianjin fully support the Indian people's struggle to recover Goa.

More than 1,000 people, including workers, women, students, teachers, literary and art workers, scientific workers, medical workers, government cadres, industrialists, business people, and religious figures, attended the conference.

The conference was opened by Fang Ji, Vice Chairman of the Tianjin Branch of the Chinese People's Committee for World Peace. Then, Gu Xiaobo, Chairman of the Tianjin Federation of Trade Unions, spoke on behalf of the city's workers, saying: When we hear of the brutal massacres of Indian patriots by the Portuguese colonialists, we cannot help but feel immense indignation. We cannot allow the colonialists to run rampant in Asia, we protest the atrocities of the Portuguese colonialists, and we support the just struggle of the Indian people to recover Goa.

Yu Aifeng, Vice Chairman of the Tianjin Democratic Women's Federation, expressed condolences and sympathy at the meeting on behalf of the women of Tianjin to the victimized Indian patriots and their families, and expressed support for the struggle of Indian women and the Indian people against the Portuguese colonialists.

Wu Daren, Vice Chairman of the Tianjin Democratic Youth Federation, said: The youth of Tianjin have personally suffered from the oppression of the colonialists, and we deeply sympathize with the Indian people in their struggle to recover their territory – Goa. We firmly support the Indian youth and the Indian people in carrying out this struggle to the final victory.

Li Juye, Vice Chairman of the Tianjin Federation of Literary and Art Workers, on behalf of the city's literary and art workers, expressed full support for the just struggle of the Indian people, who have a long history and culture, to recover Goa.

Bi Mingqi, Deputy Director of the Tianjin Federation of Industry and Commerce, said: The Indian people's struggle is just, and this struggle will surely achieve final victory.

### 天津各界举行支援印度人民收复果阿大会

1955-08-26
第1版()
专栏：

　　天津各界举行支援印度人民收复果阿大会
    新华社天津二十五日电　天津市各界人民二十五日举行支援印度人民收复果阿大会。大会一致决议：天津市人民完全支援印度人民收复果阿的斗争。
    参加大会的有工人、妇女、学生、教师、文艺工作者、科学工作者、医务工作者、机关干部、工商界和宗教界人士一千多人。
    大会由中国人民保卫世界和平委员会天津市分会副主席方纪致开会词。接着，天津市工会联合会主席谷小波代表全市工人讲话，他说：当我们听到了葡萄牙殖民主义者对印度爱国人民进行残酷的屠杀时，不能不引起无比的愤慨。我们不能听任殖民主义者在亚洲横行霸道，我们抗议葡萄牙殖民主义者的暴行，我们支持印度人民为收复果阿所进行的正义斗争。
    天津市民主妇女联合会副主席俞霭峰在会上代表天津市妇女对受害的印度爱国者及其家属致以悼念和慰问，并表示支持印度妇女和印度人民对葡萄牙殖民主义者所进行的斗争。
    天津市民主青年联合会副主席吴大任说：天津青年曾亲身遭受过殖民主义者的压迫，我们对印度人民为收复自己的领土——果阿所进行的斗争是深切同情的。我们坚决支援印度青年和印度人民把这一斗争进行到最后胜利。
    天津市文学艺术工作者联合会副主席李霁野代表全市文艺工作者，对于有着悠久的文化的印度人民收复果阿的正义斗争表示完全支持。
    天津市工商业联合会副主任委员毕鸣岐说：印度人民的斗争是正义的，这一斗争一定会取得最后胜利。
