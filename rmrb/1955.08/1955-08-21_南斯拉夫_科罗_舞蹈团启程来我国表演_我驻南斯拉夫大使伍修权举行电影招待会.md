Title: Yugoslav "Kolo" Dance Troupe Departs for Performance in China; Our Ambassador to Yugoslavia, Wu Xiuquan, Holds Film Reception
Author:
Date: 1955-08-21
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, August 20th. The "Kolo" Folk Dance Troupe of the Federal People's Republic of Yugoslavia, at the invitation of the Ministry of Culture of the People's Republic of China, left Moscow by train on the evening of the 18th after concluding its tour in the Soviet Union, heading to China for performances.
    Xinhua News Agency, Prague, August 19th. According to a report from the Yugoslav News Agency: Wu Xiuquan, Ambassador of the People's Republic of China to the Federal People's Republic of Yugoslavia, held a reception in Belgrade on the evening of the 17th, screening the Chinese color film "The Butterfly Lovers."  Among those attending the film reception were Simic, Vice President of the Yugoslav National Assembly; Popovic, Member of the Federal Executive Council; Stepanovic, Secretary-General of the Interior; Bulkich, Deputy Secretary-General of Foreign Affairs; prominent Yugoslav cultural figures; and diplomatic personnel from various countries stationed in Yugoslavia.
    "The Butterfly Lovers" will soon be publicly screened in cinemas in Belgrade and other Yugoslav cities, marking the first new Chinese film to be shown in Yugoslavia. The Belgrade-published "Borba" published an article on the 17th introducing the film.

### 南斯拉夫“科罗”舞蹈团启程来我国表演　我驻南斯拉夫大使伍修权举行电影招待会

1955-08-21
第4版()
专栏：

　　南斯拉夫“科罗”舞蹈团启程来我国表演
　　我驻南斯拉夫大使伍修权举行电影招待会
    新华社莫斯科二十日电　南斯拉夫联邦人民共和国“科罗”民间舞蹈团应中华人民共和国文化部的邀请，在结束在苏联的巡回演出以后，十八日晚乘火车离莫斯科前往中国表演。
    新华社布拉格十九日电　据南斯拉夫通讯社消息：中华人民共和国驻南斯拉夫联邦人民共和国大使伍修权，十七日晚在贝尔格莱德举行招待会，放映中国彩色影片“梁山伯与祝英台”。参加电影招待会的有南斯拉夫国民议会副议长西米茨、联邦执行委员会委员波波维奇、内务秘书长斯捷潘诺维奇、外交副秘书长布尔基奇、南斯拉夫著名文化工作者和各国驻南斯拉夫的外交人员等。
    “梁山伯与祝英台”不久将在贝尔格莱德和其他南斯拉夫城市的电影院公开放映，这是在南斯拉夫放映的第一部新中国影片。贝尔格莱德出版的“战斗报”十七日刊载文章，对这部影片作了介绍。
