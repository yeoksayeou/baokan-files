Title: Announcement of the Ministry of Finance of the People's Republic of China Regarding the First Principal Repayment Lottery for the 1954 National Economic Construction Bonds
Author:
Date: 1955-08-11
Page: 2

Ministry of Finance of the People's Republic of China Announcement Regarding the First Principal Repayment Lottery for the 1954 National Economic Construction Bonds

The first principal repayment lottery for the 1954 National Economic Construction Bonds was held on August 10th in Beijing, supervised by representatives from various sectors. The five winning numbers are hereby announced as follows: "11", "37", "48", "61", "82".

Regardless of whether they are 10,000 yuan bonds, 20,000 yuan bonds, 50,000 yuan bonds, 100,000 yuan bonds, or 500,000 yuan bonds, any bonds with the last two digits of the bond number matching one of the above winning numbers are the bonds drawn for the first principal repayment. The People's Bank of China has been entrusted to start repaying the principal and paying interest on these winning bonds from September 30th of this year, based on the legal ratio converted to the new currency. Interest for one year will also be paid for the non-winning bonds. Bondholders are requested to follow the procedures for principal repayment and interest payment of this public bond and present the bonds to the People's Bank of China to process the payment.

This is hereby announced.

Minister Li Xiannian
August 10, 1955

### 中华人民共和国财政部关于一九五四年国家经济建设公债第一次还本抽签的公告

1955-08-11
第2版()
专栏：

    中华人民共和国财政部关于一九五四年国家经济建设公债第一次还本抽签的公告
    一九五四年国家经济建设公债第一次还本抽签业于八月十日在北京市由各界代表监督举行。兹将中签号码伍个公布如下：“壹壹（11）”“叁柒（37）”“肆捌（48）”“陆壹（61）”“捌贰（82）”
    不论是壹万元券、贰万元券、伍万元券、拾万元券、伍拾万元券，其债券号码末尾两个数字，同于以上中签号码之一的，即为第一次中签还本的债券。此项中签的债券，已委托中国人民银行自本年九月三十日起开始凭券按法定比价折合新币还本付息，未中签债券亦付一年利息，希持券人按照本公债还本付息办法，凭券向人民银行办理领款手续。
    特此公告
                 部长　李先念
                 一九五五年八月十日
