Title: Striving to Fulfill the First Five-Year Plan Ahead of Schedule: The People of the Capital Resolve to Contribute All Their Strength

Author:
Date: 1955-08-01
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, August 31st: Many factories, schools, and suburban farms and agricultural producers' cooperatives in Beijing listened to the live broadcast of the closing of the Second Session of the First National People's Congress on the afternoon of the 30th. People unanimously celebrated the successful conclusion of the meeting.

    The workers of the Shijingshan Iron and Steel Plant held a celebration meeting at 8 p.m. on the 30th. At the meeting, Gao Runzhi, Deputy Director and Engineer of the Ironmaking Department, and Dai Honglin, a model worker of the Coking Department, spoke on behalf of the workers, expressing their determination to work harder and strive to overfulfill the state production plan. The workers of the Qinghe Woolen Mill in Beijing, who had already overfulfilled the state plan for July, held a literary and artistic celebration evening yesterday. Miao Kun, a Beijing model worker and worker at the Qinghe Woolen Mill, said at the meeting: We must strive to learn from advanced experiences, practice strict economy, overcome difficulties, and strive to complete the first Five-Year Plan ahead of schedule.

    All workers and staff members of the vegetable team of the Beijing State-owned Western Suburb Farm, which won the honorary title of Beijing Model Farm, were very excited when they heard the resolution on the first Five-Year Plan for the Development of the National Economy being passed on the radio. Li Jianping, the deputy captain of the vegetable team, said: This year's more than 570 mu of summer vegetables and 260 greenhouses have all overfulfilled their tasks, and we have the confidence to complete the first Five-Year Plan. Zhao Biao, the farm director, said: In order to develop the new farm, our farm has the glorious task of training a complete set of staff for the new farm, and we have the confidence to complete it. The news reached the Yongqing Vegetable Production Cooperative. Zhai Ming, a 68-year-old cooperative member, said to Wang Kangshi, a 50-year-old female cooperative member: Our lives are getting better and better, we must strive to increase production to complete the first Five-Year Plan.

    More than 2,000 graduating students from Renmin University of China, who are waiting for the motherland to assign them work, also listened to the live recording of the meeting passing the first Five-Year Plan. Bai Yinqi, a "Three Good" student from a worker's background and a graduating student from the Mechanical Class of the Department of Industrial Economics, said: The formulation of the Five-Year Plan is our happiness and pride. I must wholeheartedly dedicate all my strength to the construction of the motherland.

### 争取提前完成第一个五年计划  首都人民决心贡献全部力量

1955-08-01
第1版()
专栏：

    争取提前完成第一个五年计划
    首都人民决心贡献全部力量
    据新华社三十一日讯　北京市很多工厂、学校和郊区的农场、农业生产合作社在三十日下午，都收听了第一届全国人民代表大会第二次会议闭幕的实况广播，人们一致庆贺会议胜利闭幕。
    石景山钢铁厂职工在三十日下午八时举行庆祝会。在会上，炼铁部副主任兼工程师高润芝、炼焦部劳动模范戴洪林代表职工讲话，他们表示要更加努力生产，争取超额完成国家生产计划。已超额完成七月份国家计划的北京市清河制呢厂职工在昨天举行了文艺庆祝晚会。北京市劳动模范、清河制呢厂工人苗昆在会上说：我们要努力学习先进经验，厉行节约，克服困难，争取提前完成第一个五年计划。
    荣获北京市模范农场光荣称号的北京市国营西郊农场蔬菜队全体工人和工作人员，从收音机中听到通过关于发展国民经济的第一个五年计划的决议时，非常兴奋。蔬菜队副队长李建平说：今年的五百七十多亩和二百六十间温室夏季蔬菜都超额完成了任务，我们有信心完成第一个五年计划。场长赵彪说：为了发展新农场，我场负有培养一个新农场全套工作人员的光荣任务，我们有信心来完成它。消息传到了永青蔬菜生产合作社。六十八岁老社员翟明对五十岁的女社员王康氏说：咱们的生活越过越有味道了，一定要努力增产，来完成第一个五年计划。
    正在等候祖国分配的中国人民大学二千多名应届毕业生也收听了会议通过第一个五年计划的实况录音广播。工人出身的“三好”学生、工业经济系机械班应届毕业生白银奇说：五年计划的制定是我们的幸福和骄傲，我一定要全心全意为祖国建设献出自己的一切力量。
