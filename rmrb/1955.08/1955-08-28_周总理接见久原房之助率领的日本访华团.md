Title: Premier Zhou Enlai Receives the Japanese Delegation Led by Kuhara Fusanosuke
Author:
Date: 1955-08-28
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, August 27th: Premier Zhou Enlai of the People's Republic of China, on August 27th, received the Japanese delegation from the National Council for the Restoration of Japan-China and Japan-Soviet Diplomatic Relations, led by Kuhara Fusanosuke, Chairman of the Council, along with Madeshima Masaru, Secretary-General, Obata Tadashi, Director-General of the Kansai Branch, and Nishikawa Teiichi, Standing Director of the Oki Ube Coal Mining Company, who accompanied them.

Present at the meeting were Zhang Xiruo, President of the Chinese People's Institute of Foreign Affairs, Zhou Gengsheng and Qiao Guanhua, Vice Presidents, Li Dequan, President of the Red Cross Society of China, Liao Chengzhi, Advisor to the Red Cross Society of China, and Lei Renmin, Vice Chairman of the China Council for the Promotion of International Trade.

### 周总理接见久原房之助率领的日本访华团

1955-08-28
第1版()
专栏：

　　周总理接见久原房之助率领的日本访华团
    新华社二十七日讯　中华人民共和国国务院总理周恩来，在八月二十七日接见了日本恢复日中、日苏邦交国民会议会长久原房之助，事务总长马岛僴，关西分会理事长小畑忠良及与他们同来的冲宇部煤矿公司常务董事西川贞一等四人所组成的日本恢复日中、日苏邦交国民会议访华团。
    在座的有中国人民外交学会会长张奚若，副会长周鲠生、乔冠华，中国红十字会会长李德全，中国红十字会顾问廖承志，中国国际贸易促进委员会副主席雷任民等。
