Title: Huaibei Salt Field Sets Lower Half-Year Savings Plan
Author:
Date: 1955-08-12
Page: 2

    Huaibei Salt Field Sets Lower Half-Year Savings Plan
    Having completed the state's annual production target as early as the beginning of June, the workers of Huaibei Salt Field, in the increase production movement to strive for an additional 5.8 million dan of salt this year, have also formulated a plan to save more than 770,000 yuan in the second half of the year.
      This savings plan was determined after full discussions among the workers, exposing wasteful phenomena, and finding ways to save money from production management, disposal of stagnant materials, acceleration of capital turnover, elimination of substandard salt, and utilization of waste materials. At present, all the workers in the field are full of confidence and are carrying out labor competitions. (Xinhua News Agency)

### 淮北盐场订出下半年节约计划

1955-08-12
第2版()
专栏：

    淮北盐场订出下半年节约计划
    早在六月上旬完成了国家全年生产任务的淮北盐场职工，在争取今年多生产五百八十万担食盐的增产运动中，又订出了下半年节约七十七万多元的计划。
      这个节约计划是经过职工充分讨论，揭发了浪费现象，从生产管理、处理呆滞物资、加速资金周转、淘汰废品盐、利用废物等方面找出节约办法以后确定的。目前，全场职工正满怀信心地进行劳动竞赛。　（新华社）
