Title: Soviet Government Delegation Arrives in Pyongyang
Author:
Date: 1955-08-15
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, Pyongyang, August 14th: The Soviet government delegation, led by A.B. Aristov, Secretary of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of the Soviet Union, arrived in Pyongyang by special train on the afternoon of the 14th at the invitation of the Korean government to attend the celebrations of the tenth anniversary of the liberation of Korea.

    Korean Premier Marshal Kim Il-sung welcomed the Soviet government delegation at Pyongyang Station. Among those present at the station to welcome the delegation were Kim Tu-bong, Chairman of the Standing Committee of the Supreme People's Assembly, Park Jung-ai, Vice Chairman of the Central Committee of the Korean Workers' Party, Vice Premiers of the Cabinet Choi Yong-kun, Park Chang-ok, Kim Il, Choi Chang-ik, Hong Myung-hee, Chung Il-yong, Park Ui-wan and Cabinet ministers, Li Young, Speaker of the Supreme People's Assembly, and leaders of various democratic parties and social organizations, and senior generals of the Korean People's Army.

    Zhu De, head of the Chinese People's Republic delegation, and members Teng Daiyuan, Ji Pengfei, and Pan Zili also went to the station to welcome the delegation. Also present at the station to welcome the delegation were diplomatic envoys from various countries stationed in Korea.

    After Aristov alighted from the train, he reviewed the honor guard accompanied by Marshal Kim Il-sung. He then addressed more than 20,000 people welcoming the Soviet government delegation in the square in front of the station. In his speech, he congratulated the Korean people on their new achievements in the cause of rebuilding their motherland and striving for the peaceful reunification of their country on behalf of the Soviet people. The delegation left the station by car amidst warm applause and cheers from the welcoming crowd.

### 苏联政府代表团到平壤

1955-08-15
第1版()
专栏：

    苏联政府代表团到平壤
    新华社平壤十四日电  应邀参加朝鲜解放十周年庆祝典礼的苏联政府代表团在团长、苏联共产党中央委员会书记阿·布·阿里斯托夫率领下，十四日下午乘专车到达平壤。
    朝鲜内阁首相金日成元帅到平壤车站欢迎苏联政府代表团。到车站欢迎的还有最高人民会议常任委员会委员长金枓奉，朝鲜劳动党中央委员会副委员长朴正爱，内阁副首相崔庸健、朴昌玉、金一、崔昌益、洪命熹、郑一龙、朴义琓和内阁各相，最高人民会议议长李英和各民主政党、社会团体负责人，朝鲜人民军高级将领等。
    中华人民共和国代表团团长朱德和团员滕代远、姬鹏飞、潘自力也到车站欢迎。到车站欢迎的还有各国驻朝外交使节。
    阿里斯托夫下车后，由金日成元帅陪同检阅了仪仗队，接着在车站前广场上向欢迎苏联政府代表团的两万多人讲话。他在讲话中代表苏联人民祝贺朝鲜人民在重建祖国和争取祖国和平统一的事业中取得新的成就。代表团在欢迎人群的热烈掌声和欢呼声中乘汽车离开车站。
