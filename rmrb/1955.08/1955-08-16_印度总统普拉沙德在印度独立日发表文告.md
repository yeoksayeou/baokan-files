Title: Indian President Prasad Issues Message on Indian Independence Day
Author:
Date: 1955-08-16
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, New Delhi, August 15th: Indian President Dr. Rajendra Prasad issued a message on August 15th commemorating Indian Independence Day. The message called on the Indian people to "dedicate themselves in thought and action to the ideals of peace and mutual tolerance."

He praised India's efforts in domestic construction work and its assistance to "world peace forces."

In his message, the President mentioned Prime Minister Nehru's recent visit to the Soviet Union and the People's Democratic countries. He said that as a result, "the Five Principles have the hope of becoming a symbol of peace and mutual respect between nations."

### 印度总统普拉沙德在印度独立日发表文告

1955-08-16
第4版()
专栏：

    印度总统普拉沙德在印度独立日发表文告
    新华社新德里十五日电　印度总统拉金德拉·普拉沙德博士在八月十五日发表纪念印度独立日的文告。文告中号召印度人民“在思想上和行动上致力于和平和互相容忍的理想”。
    他赞美印度在国内建设工作上所作的努力以及它对“世界和平力量”所给的帮助。
    总统在文告中谈到尼赫鲁总理最近对苏联和人民民主国家的访问。他说，结果“五项原则有希望成为和平和各国间互相尊敬的象征”。
