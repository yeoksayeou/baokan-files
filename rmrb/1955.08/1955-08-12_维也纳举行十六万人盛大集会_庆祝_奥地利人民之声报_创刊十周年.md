Title: Vienna Holds Grand Rally of 160,000 People Celebrating the 10th Anniversary of "Austrian People's Voice"

Author:

Date: 1955-08-12
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Vienna, August 10th: According to a TASS report from Vienna on August 9th: On August 7th, more than 160,000 working people from Vienna and all over Austria held a rally in Vienna's Prater Park to celebrate the 10th anniversary of the Austrian Communist Party's organ, "Austrian People's Voice."

    Communist Party Chairman Koplenig and the newspaper's editor-in-chief, Juk-Schilling, spoke at the meeting. They described the newspaper's efforts in promoting the signing of the Austrian State Treaty, the establishment of an independent, democratic and neutral Austria, striving for mutual understanding and peace among the peoples of all countries, and fighting for the legitimate demands of the working people.

    Representatives of fraternal Communist Parties and workers' newspapers from the German Democratic Republic, Czechoslovakia, Poland, and other countries also spoke at the meeting.

    After the meeting, a grand concert was held, and fireworks were set off.

### 维也纳举行十六万人盛大集会  庆祝“奥地利人民之声报”创刊十周年

1955-08-12
第4版()
专栏：

    维也纳举行十六万人盛大集会
    庆祝“奥地利人民之声报”创刊十周年
    新华社十日讯　据塔斯社维也纳九日讯：八月七日，维也纳和来自奥地利各地的十六万以上的劳动人民在维也纳的普拉特公园举行了集会，庆祝奥地利共产党机关报“奥地利人民之声报”创刊十周年。
    共产党主席柯普勒尼希和这个报纸的总编辑朱克—席林在会上讲了话。他们叙述了这个报纸在促使奥地利国家条约签订，成立一个独立、民主和中立的奥地利，争取各国人民之间的互相了解、和平，以及争取满足劳动人民的正当要求方面所作的努力。
    德意志民主共和国、捷克斯洛伐克、波兰和其他国家的兄弟共产党和工人报纸的代表们也在大会上讲了话。
    会后，举行了盛大的音乐会，并且放了焰火。
