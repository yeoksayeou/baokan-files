Title: American Spy Released Early by Us Goes to Hong Kong
Author:
Date: 1955-08-05
Page: 1

    Xinhua News Agency, Guangzhou, 4th - The eleven American spies, including John Knox Arnold, who were ordered to be released early by the Military Tribunal of the Supreme People's Court of our country on July 31st, arrived in Shenzhen from Guangzhou by train at 11:55 a.m. on the 4th. After completing departure formalities in Shenzhen, they left the Shenzhen border at 12:35 p.m. and entered Hong Kong.

### 我提前释放的美国间谍去香港

1955-08-05
第1版()
专栏：

    我提前释放的美国间谍去香港
    新华社广州四日电　我国最高人民法院军事审判庭在七月三十一日决定依法提前释放的美国间谍约翰·诺克斯·阿诺德等十一人，四日上午十一时五十五分乘火车自广州抵达深圳。他们在深圳办理了离境手续，在十二时三十五分离开深圳边境进入香港。
