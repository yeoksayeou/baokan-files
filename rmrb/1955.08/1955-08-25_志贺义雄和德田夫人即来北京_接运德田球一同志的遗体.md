Title: Shiga Yoshio and Mrs. Tokuda to Arrive in Beijing Soon to Escort Comrade Tokuda Kyuichi's Remains

Author: 
Date: 1955-08-25
Page: 1

　　Shiga Yoshio and Mrs. Tokuda to Arrive in Beijing Soon
　　To Escort Comrade Tokuda Kyuichi's Remains
    Xinhua News Agency, August 24th: Tokyo dispatch: The Japanese Communist Party issued a written statement on August 24th announcing that Shiga Yoshio, a member of the Central Committee of the Japanese Communist Party, and Tokuda Tatsu, the widow of the late General Secretary Tokuda Kyuichi, are preparing to leave Tokyo by plane on the evening of August 25th to go to Beijing to escort the remains of Comrade Tokuda Kyuichi.
    The Japanese Communist Party plans to hold a funeral for Comrade Tokuda Kyuichi on October 14th. Shiga Yoshio and Tokuda Tatsu will return to Tokyo before this date.

### 志贺义雄和德田夫人即来北京　接运德田球一同志的遗体

1955-08-25
第1版()
专栏：

　　志贺义雄和德田夫人即来北京
　　接运德田球一同志的遗体
    新华社二十四日讯　东京消息：日本共产党二十四日发表书面声明宣布，日本共产党中央委员志贺义雄和已故总书记德田球一夫人德田辰准备在八月二十五日晚间乘飞机离东京，前往北京接运德田球一同志的遗体。
    日本共产党预定在十月十四日举行德田球一同志的葬礼。志贺义雄和德田辰将在这个日期以前返回东京。
