Title: Egyptian Trade Delegation Visits New Delhi En Route to China
Author:
Date: 1955-08-05
Page: 1

    Xinhua News Agency, New Delhi, August 4th: The Egyptian trade delegation, led by Egyptian Minister of Industry and Commerce Muhammad Abu Nuseir, arrived in New Delhi on the 3rd while en route to China. The delegation left Cairo on the 1st.
    While passing through Mumbai, Nuseir stated that Egypt eagerly desires to implement the recommendations of the Asian-African Conference regarding economic cooperation among Asian and African nations. He said that the Egyptian delegation will make every effort to find ways to expand trade between Egypt and China. The trip to China will help Egypt understand the full range of possibilities for doing business in the Far East.

### 埃及贸易代表团来我国访问途中到新德里

1955-08-05
第1版()
专栏：

    埃及贸易代表团来我国访问途中到新德里
    新华社新德里四日电　由埃及工商部长穆罕默德·阿卜·努赛尔率领的埃及贸易代表团在前往中国途中在三日到达新德里。代表团是在一日离开开罗的。
    努赛尔在经过孟买时，曾发表谈话说，埃及切望实行亚非会议关于进行亚非各国间的经济合作的建议。他说，埃及代表团将作出一切努力，寻找扩大埃及和中国贸易的途径。中国之行将有助于使埃及了解在远东做生意的可能性的全部情况。
