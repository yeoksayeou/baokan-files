Title: Indonesian Vice President Hatta Appoints Masjumi Party Leader to Form Cabinet
Author:
Date: 1955-08-05
Page: 4

    Xinhua News Agency, Jakarta, August 4th. Indonesian Vice President Hatta appointed Burhanuddin Harahap, leader of the Masjumi Party's parliamentary faction, on the evening of the 3rd to form a new cabinet. Vice President Hatta gave him one week to form the new cabinet.
    According to the Indonesian News Agency, Burhanuddin Harahap convened a meeting of senior Masjumi Party leaders on the morning of the 4th to discuss the formation of the new cabinet.

### 印度尼西亚副总统哈达  任命马斯友美党领袖组阁

1955-08-05
第4版()
专栏：

    印度尼西亚副总统哈达
    任命马斯友美党领袖组阁
    新华社雅加达四日电　印度尼西亚副总统哈达在三日晚间任命马斯友美党国会党团领袖伯汉努丁·哈拉哈普组织新内阁。哈达副总统给他一星期的时间组成新内阁。
    据印度尼西亚新闻社报道，伯汉努丁·哈拉哈普在四日上午召集了马斯友美党高级领袖举行会议，讨论组织新内阁问题。
