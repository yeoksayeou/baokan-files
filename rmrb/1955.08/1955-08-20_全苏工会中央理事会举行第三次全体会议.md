Title: AUCCTU Holds Third Plenary Session
Author:
Date: 1955-08-20
Page: 4

    Xinhua News Agency, August 10th: The Third Plenary Session of the All-Union Central Council of Trade Unions (AUCCTU) recently convened in Moscow.
    The session had two items on its agenda: 1. Discussion of trade union work based on the resolutions of the July Plenary Session of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of the Soviet Union "Regarding Tasks in the Further Upsurge of Industry, Technological Progress, and Improvement of Production Organization"; 2. Organizational matters.
    AUCCTU Vice-Chairman Solovyov delivered a report on the first item.
    Thirty-three people spoke on AUCCTU Vice-Chairman Solovyov's report. In their speeches, they unanimously pointed out that the resolutions of the July Plenary Session of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of the Soviet Union had inspired a new upsurge in the political enthusiasm and labor activism of the working people.
    The meeting unanimously adopted a resolution fully endorsing the resolutions of the July Plenary Session of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of the Soviet Union and committing to resolutely implement them in their work.
    During the discussion of the second item on the agenda, organizational matters, the meeting, in view of the fact that AUCCTU Secretary Goroshkin had been assigned to another position, elected Prokhorov to replace him.

### 全苏工会中央理事会举行第三次全体会议

1955-08-20
第4版()
专栏：

    全苏工会中央理事会举行第三次全体会议
    据新华社十日讯　全苏工会中央理事会第三次全体会议最近在莫斯科举行。
    会议的议程共两项：一、根据苏联共产党中央委员会七月全体会议决议“关于在工业的进一步高涨、技术的进步和生产组织的改进方面的任务”讨论工会工作；二、组织问题。
    全苏工会中央理事会副主席索洛维耶夫就第一项问题作了报告。
    有三十三人就全苏工会中央理事会副主席索洛维耶夫的报告作了发言。他们在发言中一致指出，苏联共产党中央委员会七月全体会议的决议，启发了劳动人民的政治热情和劳动积极性的新的高涨。
    会议一致通过决议，完全赞同苏联共产党中央委员会七月全体会议的决议，并且要坚决地把它贯彻到工作里去。
    在讨论第二项议程组织问题时，会议鉴于全苏工会中央理事会书记戈罗什金另有任用，选出普罗霍罗夫来代替他的职位。
