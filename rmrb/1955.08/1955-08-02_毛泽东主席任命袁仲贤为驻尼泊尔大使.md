Title: Chairman Mao Appoints Yuan Zhongxian as Ambassador to Nepal
Author:
Date: 1955-08-02
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, 1st. Chairman Mao of the People's Republic of China has appointed Yuan Zhongxian as the Ambassador Extraordinary and Plenipotentiary of the People's Republic of China to the Kingdom of Nepal.

### 毛泽东主席任命袁仲贤为驻尼泊尔大使

1955-08-02
第1版()
专栏：

    毛泽东主席任命袁仲贤为驻尼泊尔大使
    新华社一日讯　中华人民共和国主席毛泽东任命袁仲贤为中华人民共和国驻尼泊尔王国特命全权大使。
