Title: Syrian President Opposes Joining Military Blocs
Author:
Date: 1955-08-29
Page: 4

　　Xinhua News Agency, August 28th, TASS dispatch from Damascus, August 27th: The new President of the Syrian Republic, Shukri al-Kuwatli, told reporters: "I am not one of those who favor joining a military treaty, nor do I think it is necessary to establish ties with Western countries."

### 叙总统反对参加军事集团

1955-08-29
第4版()
专栏：

　　叙总统反对参加军事集团
    新华社二十八日讯　塔斯社二十七日报道，大马士革消息：新任叙利亚共和国总统舒克里·库瓦特利对记者说：“我不是属于那种赞成参加某个军事条约的人，我也不认为必须同西方国家建立联系。”
