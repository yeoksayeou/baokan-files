    The Agricultural Production Cooperative of Heping Village, Xichang County, Xikang Province, has expanded from its original eight member households to more than two hundred households in the past three years. The cooperative has also built a new pumping station to ensure the irrigation of 800 mu of corn, more than 300 mu of cotton, and more than 300 mu of rice. Yang Changhe (New China News Agency draft)
