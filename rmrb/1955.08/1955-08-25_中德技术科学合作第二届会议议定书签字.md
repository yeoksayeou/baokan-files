Title: Protocol Signed for the Second Meeting of the Sino-German Technical and Scientific Cooperation

Author:

Date: 1955-08-25
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Berlin, August 24th: The following press release was issued on the morning of August 23rd following the second meeting of the Sino-German Standing Committee for Technical and Scientific Cooperation:

    The protocol for the second meeting of the Technical and Scientific Cooperation between the People's Republic of China and the German Democratic Republic was signed in Berlin on August 20, 1955.

    The meeting demonstrated the sincere friendship between the two countries and their efforts to deepen and develop cooperation.

    The German Democratic Republic will provide the People's Republic of China with technical information concerning metallurgy, heavy machinery manufacturing, telecommunications, electrical engineering, and the textile industry, and will enable more Chinese technicians to have the opportunity to study in factories in various industrial sectors.

    The People's Republic of China will supply the German Democratic Republic with technical information, particularly concerning the textile, paper-making, food processing, and agricultural industries.

    The meeting also decided to send a German expert delegation to the People's Republic of China to study the characteristics of various materials and products used under subtropical climatic conditions.

    Xinhua News Agency, Berlin, August 22nd: The Sino-German Standing Committee for Technical and Scientific Cooperation held its second meeting in Berlin from August 1st to 20th, and the protocol for the second meeting was signed on the evening of the 20th. The protocol includes technical cooperation projects such as the exchange of technical information and the exchange of experts.

    Cao Xiangren, the head of the Chinese group of the Sino-German Standing Committee for Technical and Scientific Cooperation, and Vice Minister of the First Ministry of Machine Building of the People's Republic of China, signed the protocol on behalf of the Chinese side. Goschütz, the head of the German group and First Deputy Minister of Heavy Industry of the German Democratic Republic, signed on behalf of the German side.

### 中德技术科学合作第二届会议议定书签字

1955-08-25
第1版()
专栏：

　　中德技术科学合作第二届会议议定书签字
    新华社柏林二十四日电　中德技术与技术科学合作常任委员会第二届会议后，双方在二十三日上午发表公报如下：
    中华人民共和国和德意志民主共和国技术科学合作第二届会议议定书于一九五五年八月二十日在柏林签字。
    会议证明了两国间的真诚友谊和加深和发展两国间合作的努力。
    德意志民主共和国将交付中华人民共和国有关冶金、重型机械制造、电讯、电工和纺织工业方面的技术资料，并且使较多的中国技术人员获得在工业部门各工厂中学习的机会。
    中华人民共和国将供应德意志民主共和国技术资料，特别是有关纺织、造纸、食品工业和农业方面的技术资料。
    会议也决定了派遣德国专家代表团至中华人民共和国研究各种材料和制品在亚热带气候条件使用下的特点。
    新华社柏林二十二日电　中德技术与技术科学合作常任委员会在八月一日到二十日在柏林举行了第二届会议，二十日晚间签订了第二届会议议定书。议定书包括交换技术资料，互派专家等技术合作项目。
    代表中国方面在议定书上签字的是中德技术与技术科学合作常任委员会中国组组长、中华人民共和国第一机械工业部副部长曹祥仁，代表德国方面签字的是德国组组长、德意志民主共和国重工业部第一副部长戈舒埃茨。
