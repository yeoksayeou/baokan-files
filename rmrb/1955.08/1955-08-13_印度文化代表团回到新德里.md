Title: Indian Cultural Delegation Returns to New Delhi
Author:
Date: 1955-08-13
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, New Delhi, 12th. The Indian cultural delegation led by Mr. Chand, Deputy Minister of the Indian Ministry of Foreign Affairs, returned to New Delhi on the evening of the 11th after a two-month visit to China.

Gore, Director of the Eastern Division of the Indian Ministry of Foreign Affairs, Indian government officials, and representatives of the Sino-Indian Friendship Association were at the station to welcome them.

Yuan Zhongxian, the Chinese Ambassador to India, and personnel from the embassy were also at the station to welcome the delegation.

Chand, the head of the Indian cultural delegation, told reporters at the airport when the delegation passed through Calcutta that the delegation received extremely cordial and warm hospitality everywhere during its visit to China. The Chinese people greatly appreciated the performances of the Indian artists. Chinese newspapers published many articles praising Indian music and dance.

Chand said that eight Chinese dancers who were interested in Indian dance were sent to accompany the delegation on its visit. They learned Indian dance very well, so well that they were able to participate in some of the programs. Similarly, some members of the Indian delegation also learned Chinese music and played Chinese tunes with their instruments.

The delegation traveled 13,000 miles in China and visited remote places like Urumqi and Lushun.

### 印度文化代表团回到新德里

1955-08-13
第1版()
专栏：

    印度文化代表团回到新德里
    新华社新德里十二日电　印度外交部副部长钱达率领的印度文化代表团在中国进行了两个月的访问以后，在十一日晚间回到新德里。
    到车站欢迎的有印度外交部东方司司长高尔、印度政府官员和印中友好协会代表。
    中国驻印度大使袁仲贤和大使馆人员也到车站上欢迎代表团。
    印度文化代表团团长钱达在代表团经过加尔各答时在机场对记者发表谈话说，代表团在中国访问期间，到处都受到极其殷勤和热情的招待。中国人民非常欣赏印度艺术家的表演。中国的报纸发表了好些赞扬印度音乐和舞蹈的文章。
    钱达说，八名对印度舞蹈感兴趣的中国舞蹈工作者被派遣随同代表团进行访问。他们学习印度舞蹈学得非常好，以至于能参加某些节目的演出。同样，印度代表团的一些团员也学习了中国音乐，并且用他们的乐器演奏了中国曲调。
    代表团在中国旅行了一万三千英里，访问了像乌鲁木齐和旅顺这样的遥远的地方。
