Title: Egyptian Trade Delegation Returns Home After Visiting Shanghai and Hangzhou
Author:
Date: 1955-08-30
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Guangzhou, August 29th: The Egyptian government trade delegation of ten people, led by the head of the delegation, the Egyptian Minister of Industry and Commerce, Muhammad Abu Nuseir, arrived in Guangzhou from Shanghai on the afternoon of the 28th and left Guangzhou for Hong Kong on the morning of the 29th to return home.

According to Xinhua News Agency, from the 24th to the 26th, the Egyptian government trade delegation visited the Shanghai Industrial Production Achievement Exhibition Hall and the China Textile Machinery Factory in Shanghai, and also visited silk, oil, papermaking, and enamel factories. They also went to silk, department store, and native produce companies to select silk and various daily necessities produced in China. The representatives also visited samples at the Shanghai branches of export companies for sundries, native produce, tea, and grain and oil products. Some representatives also signed contracts with these companies. During their stay in Shanghai, the representatives attended a luncheon hosted by the Shanghai Foreign Trade Bureau, watched Peking Opera and Yue Opera, and toured the city.

The Egyptian government trade delegation arrived in Hangzhou from Shanghai on the 26th for visits and sightseeing. The delegation visited the state-owned Zhejiang Linen Textile Factory and the Hangzhou Du Jinsheng Silk Weaving Factory in Hangzhou, and visited scenic spots and historical sites such as West Lake, Lingyin Temple, Yuquan, and Yue Temple. They left Hangzhou on the evening of the 27th.

### 埃及贸易代表团访问上海杭州后回国

1955-08-30
第1版()
专栏：

　　埃及贸易代表团访问上海杭州后回国
    新华社广州二十九日电　埃及共和国政府贸易代表团一行十人，由团长、埃及工商部部长穆罕默德·阿卜·努赛尔率领，在二十八日下午从上海到达广州，二十九日上午离广州经香港返国。
    据新华社讯　二十四日至二十六日，埃及共和国政府贸易代表团，在上海参观了上海市工业生产成绩展览馆、中国纺织机械厂，并分别参观了丝绸、油脂、造纸、搪瓷等工厂，到丝绸、百货、土产公司选购了我国出产的丝绸和各种日用品。代表们还到杂品、土产、茶业、粮油等出口公司在上海的分公司里参观了样品，有些代表并和这些公司签订了合同。在上海期间，代表们出席了上海市对外贸易局的便宴，还观看了京剧、越剧和游览了市区。
    埃及共和国政府贸易代表团在二十六日从上海到达杭州参观、游览。代表团在杭州参观了国营浙江麻纺织厂和杭州都锦生丝织厂，游览了西湖、灵隐寺、玉泉、岳庙等名胜古迹，并在二十七日晚离开杭州。
