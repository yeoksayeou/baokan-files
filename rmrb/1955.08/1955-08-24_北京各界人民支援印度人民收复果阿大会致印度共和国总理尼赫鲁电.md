Title: Telegram from the People of All Circles in Beijing Supporting the Indian People's Recovery of Goa to Indian Prime Minister Nehru
Author:
Date: 1955-08-24
Page: 1

Telegram from the People of All Circles in Beijing Supporting the Indian People's Recovery of Goa to Mr. Nehru, Prime Minister of the Republic of India:
    On August 23, people from all circles in Beijing held a meeting to support the Indian people's struggle to recover Goa. The meeting paid high tribute to the Indian people who are bravely fighting to defend national territorial sovereignty and the government of the Republic of India that is leading this just struggle; and expressed deep condolences and sympathy to the patriotic Indian people who gloriously sacrificed in the struggle and their families. The meeting also protested with great indignation against the atrocities of the Portuguese colonialists in massacring peace-loving Indian people.
    The meeting expressed its firm belief that the Indian people and the government of the Republic of India's struggle to recover Goa is just, and it has received the sympathy and support of the Chinese people and the people of Asia and the world, and it will surely be victorious!
                    The People of All Circles in Beijing Supporting the Indian People's Recovery of Goa Conference, August 23, 1955

### 北京各界人民支援印度人民收复果阿大会致印度共和国总理尼赫鲁电

1955-08-24
第1版()
专栏：

　　北京各界人民支援印度人民收复果阿大会致印度共和国总理尼赫鲁电印度共和国总理尼赫鲁先生：
    八月二十三日北京各界人民举行大会，支援印度人民收复果阿的斗争。大会向为了保卫国家领土主权而英勇斗争的印度人民和领导这一正义斗争的印度共和国政府致以崇高的敬意；向在斗争中光荣牺牲的印度爱国者们及其家属表示深切的哀悼和慰问。大会并以极大的愤慨抗议葡萄牙殖民主义者屠杀爱好和平的印度人民的暴行。
    大会表示坚决相信：印度人民和印度共和国政府要求收复果阿的斗争是正义的，它受到中国人民和亚洲以及全世界人民的同情与支援，它将一定获得胜利！
                    中国北京各界人民支援印度人民收复果阿大会一九五五年八月二十三日
