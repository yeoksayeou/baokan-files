Title: Chinese Art Troupe's Performance in the Netherlands Receives Warm Welcome
Author:
Date: 1955-08-01
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency, Prague, July 31st: Over 11,000 Dutch audience members watched the eight performances given by the Chinese Art Troupe in Amsterdam, Rotterdam, and The Hague from July 19th to 28th. Although it is not the theater season, the audience for the Chinese Art Troupe was very enthusiastic. They expressed a rare and warm welcome to the performances.

Major Dutch newspapers published news and reviews about the performances of the Chinese Art Troupe. The Dutch newspaper "De Waarheid" wrote: "The beauty, talent, and enthusiasm that the Chinese Art Troupe brings to Europe and our country are inspired by a great goal: the friendship between the people of all countries." "Nieuwe Rotterdamsche Courant" wrote: "The Chinese Art Troupe has completed its cultural mission, and its achievement is very significant."

Many newspapers also introduced some of the performances with admiring tones. "De Waarheid" said that "Autumn River" is a very vivid and interesting pastoral poem; "Red Silk Dance" fully expresses the happy life of the Chinese people. Many newspapers also praised the captivating nature of "San Cha Kou," "Havoc in Heaven," and "Yandang Mountain." "Het Volk" wrote: The perfect skills of the Chinese Art Troupe's performance constitute an "extremely wonderful, poetic, and imaginative world—a world composed of ancient civilization and vitality."

The overseas Chinese in the Netherlands were very excited to watch the performances of the motherland's cultural delegation and presented silk banners to the art troupe. They expressed great joy in the motherland's growing prosperity and happy life.

On the 28th, the Mayor of The Hague, Mr. Schokking, received Zhang Zhixiang, the head of the Chinese Art Troupe, and the main actors of the art troupe. Zhang Zhixiang presented gifts to Schokking.

Xinhua News Agency, Prague, July 29th: The Hague News: The Chinese Art Troupe, which is currently on a performance tour in the Netherlands, held a reception at the Wittebrug Hotel in The Hague on the 27th.

More than 300 people attended the reception, including Mr. de Hoogh, Director of Cultural Relations of the Dutch Ministry of Education, representatives of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs, and prominent figures from the cultural and artistic circles.

The diplomatic envoys of Chile, Poland, Czechoslovakia, Argentina, Japan, Romania, Uruguay, Finland, Indonesia, Hungary, Israel, and Norway to the Netherlands also attended the reception.

At the reception, the guests unanimously praised the great success of the Chinese Art Troupe's performances. Many pointed out that these performances have made a beneficial contribution to further strengthening the understanding and cultural relations between the Dutch and Chinese people.

### 我国艺术团在荷兰演出受到热烈欢迎

1955-08-01
第4版()
专栏：

    我国艺术团在荷兰演出受到热烈欢迎
    新华社布拉格三十一日电　一万一千名以上的荷兰观众观看了中国艺术团从七月十九日到二十八日先后在阿姆斯特丹、鹿特丹和海牙举行的八次演出。虽然现在不是戏剧季节，但是中国艺术团的观众仍然十分踊跃。他们对演出表示了稀有的热烈欢迎。
    荷兰的主要报纸都发表了关于中国艺术团的演出的消息和评论。荷兰“真理报”写道：“中国艺术团给欧洲和我国所带来的美丽、天才和热忱是为一个伟大的目标所鼓舞的，这就是：各国人民间的友谊。”“卖士河报信者报”写道：“中国艺术团已经完成了它的文化使命，它的成就是非常巨大的。”
    许多报纸还以赞美的语调介绍了某些演出的节目。“真理报”说，“秋江”是一首非常生动、有趣的田园诗；“红绸舞”充分表达了中国人民幸福的生活。许多报纸也盛赞“三岔口”、“闹天宫”和“雁荡山”的引人入胜。“人民报”写道：中国艺术团的表演的完善技巧构成了一个“极其美妙的、富有诗意的、充满想像的世界——由古老文明和生命力所组成的世界”。
    在荷兰的华侨非常兴奋地观看了祖国文化使团的这次演出，并且向艺术团赠送锦旗。他们对祖国日益增长的繁荣和幸福生活表示了莫大的喜悦。
    二十八日，海牙大市长肖金接见了中国艺术团团长张致祥和艺术团的主要演员。张致祥向肖金赠送了礼物。
    新华社布拉格二十九日电　海牙消息：现在荷兰作访问演出的中国艺术团二十七日在海牙维德普勒旅馆举行了招待会。
    出席招待会的有三百多人，其中有荷兰教育部文化关系局局长德霍、外交部代表以及文化界和艺术界的著名人士。
    智利、波兰、捷克斯洛伐克、阿根廷、日本、罗马尼亚、乌拉圭、芬兰、印度尼西亚、匈牙利、以色列和挪威驻荷兰的外交使节也出席了招待会。
    在招待会上，来宾们一致盛赞中国艺术团演出的巨大成功。有许多人指出，这些演出已经对进一步加强荷兰人民和中国人民的了解和文化关系作出了有益的贡献。
