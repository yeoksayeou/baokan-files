Title: Xinjiang Ethnic Groups Visiting Delegation Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1955-08-31
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, August 30th: The Xinjiang Province Ethnic Groups Visiting Delegation, consisting of one hundred and twenty people, led by the head of the delegation, Maitiming (Uyghur ethnicity), has arrived in Beijing. The members of this visiting delegation include ten ethnic groups: Uyghur, Kazakh, Hui, Han, Mongol, Xibe, Kirgiz, Tajik, Tatar, and Daur.

### 新疆各族参观团到京

1955-08-31
第1版()
专栏：

　　新疆各族参观团到京
    据新华社三十日讯　新疆省各民族参观团一行一百二十人，在团长麦提明（维吾尔族）率领下，已经到达北京。这个参观团的成员包括维吾尔、哈萨克、回、汉、蒙、锡伯、柯尔克孜、塔吉克、塔塔尔、达呼尔十个民族成分。
