Title: Korean Workers' Party Central Committee Issues "August 15th" Commemorative Slogans
Author:
Date: 1955-08-10
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Pyongyang, August 9th. The Central Committee of the Korean Workers' Party issued the commemorative slogans for the tenth anniversary of the liberation of Korea on August 8th.

In its slogans, the Central Committee of the Korean Workers' Party extended warm congratulations and gratitude to the people of the Soviet Union, China, and other People's Democratic countries for their enormous assistance to the Korean people.

The slogans called on all the people of the Republic to further consolidate the people's power, which has guaranteed the people's freedom and happiness for ten years, to further consolidate the unity of the patriotic democratic forces based on the worker-peasant alliance and the United Democratic Fatherland Front, to unite around the Korean Workers' Party and the government of the Republic like a rock, and to advance towards new victories; the slogans called on all the people of the Republic to struggle more resolutely against the policies of the U.S. imperialism and the Syngman Rhee clique to divide the Korean territory and nation and provoke a new war; the slogans called on all the people of the Republic to complete and overfulfill the Three-Year Economic Plan ahead of schedule, in order to strengthen the democratic base, improve the people's livelihood, and promote the peaceful reunification of the motherland.

### 朝鲜劳动党中央发布“八一五”纪念口号

1955-08-10
第4版()
专栏：

    朝鲜劳动党中央发布“八一五”纪念口号
    新华社平壤九日电　朝鲜劳动党中央委员会在八月八日发布了“八一五”朝鲜解放十周年的纪念口号。
    朝鲜劳动党中央委员会在口号中对给予朝鲜人民巨大援助的苏联、中国和其他人民民主国家的人民致以热烈的祝贺和感谢。
    口号号召共和国全体人民进一步巩固十年来保障了人民自由幸福的人民政权，进一步巩固以工农联盟为基础的爱国民主力量和祖国统一民主主义战线的团结，像磐石一样地团结在朝鲜劳动党和共和国政府的周围，向着新的胜利前进；号召共和国全体人民为反对美帝国主义和李承晚匪帮割裂朝鲜国土和民族、挑拨新战争的政策而更加坚决地斗争；号召共和国全体人民提前完成和超额完成三年经济计划，以加强民主基地，改善人民生活，促进祖国的和平统一。
