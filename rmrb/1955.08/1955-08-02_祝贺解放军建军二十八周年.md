Title: Congratulations on the 28th Anniversary of the Founding of the People's Liberation Army
Author:
Date: 1955-08-02
Page: 1

Congratulations on the 28th Anniversary of the Founding of the People's Liberation Army
Comrade Ho Chi Minh, President of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam, cables Comrade Mao Zedong, Chairman of the People's Republic of China:

    On the occasion of the 28th anniversary of the founding of the Chinese People's Liberation Army, I, on behalf of the government of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam, the Vietnamese people, and in my own name, extend to you my cordial congratulations. I wish the Chinese People's Liberation Army even greater new achievements in the cause of consolidating its strength, defending the motherland, and safeguarding world peace.
        Ho Chi Minh
    August 1, 1955

Comrade Pham Van Dong, Vice Premier and Minister of Foreign Affairs of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam, cables Comrade Zhou Enlai, Premier and Minister of Foreign Affairs of the State Council of the People's Republic of China:

    On the occasion of the 28th anniversary of the founding of the heroic Chinese People's Liberation Army, please accept my warm congratulations.
        Pham Van Dong
    August 1, 1955

Balku, Minister of National Defence of Albania, cables Comrade Peng Dehuai, Minister of National Defence of the People's Republic of China:

    On the occasion of the glorious founding anniversary of the Chinese People's Liberation Army, I, on behalf of all the commanders and fighters of the Albanian People's Army and myself, extend to you, Minister, and through you to the Chinese People's Liberation Army, my heartfelt and warm congratulations.
    I wish the Chinese People's Liberation Army even greater achievements in strengthening its combat capabilities in the cause of defending the socialist peaceful construction of the Chinese people and in the cause of defending peace in the Far East and the world.
            Lieutenant General Bekir Balku, Minister of National Defence of the People's Republic of Albania

### 祝贺解放军建军二十八周年

1955-08-02
第1版()
专栏：

    祝贺解放军建军二十八周年
    越南民主共和国主席胡志明电中华人民共和国主席毛泽东同志：
    值此中国人民解放军建军二十八周年之际，我谨代表越南民主共和国政府、越南人民并以我个人名义向您致以亲切的祝贺。谨祝中国人民解放军在巩固自己的力量、保卫祖国和维护世界和平的事业中获得更多新的成就。
        胡志明
    一九五五年八月一日
    越南民主共和国副总理兼外长范文同电中华人民共和国国务院总理兼外交部长周恩来同志：
    值此英勇的中国人民解放军建军二十八周年之际，请您接受我热烈的祝贺。
        范文同
    一九五五年八月一日
    阿尔巴尼亚国防部长巴卢库电中华人民共和国国防部长彭德怀同志：
    值此光荣的中国人民解放军建军节之际，谨代表阿尔巴尼亚人民军全体指挥员、战斗员以及我本人向您，部长同志，并通过您向中国人民解放军致衷心的、热烈的祝贺。
    谨祝中国人民解放军在为保卫中国人民的社会主义和平建设以及为保卫远东和世界和平的事业而进一步加强自己的战斗力的事业中取得更大的成就。
            阿尔巴尼亚人民共和国国防部长  贝基尔·巴卢库中将
