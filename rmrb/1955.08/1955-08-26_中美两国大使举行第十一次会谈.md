Title: Sino-U.S. Ambassadors Hold Eleventh Meeting
Author:
Date: 1955-08-26
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, Geneva, August 25th. The Sino-U.S. Ambassadorial talks held their eleventh meeting at the Palais des Nations in Geneva at 10:00 a.m. on the 25th.

The meeting adjourned at 12:05 p.m. The next meeting will be held at 10:00 a.m. on August 27th.

At the meeting on the 25th, the two ambassadors continued to discuss the first item on the agenda—the repatriation of civilians of both sides.

### 中美两国大使举行第十一次会谈

1955-08-26
第1版()
专栏：

　　中美两国大使举行第十一次会谈
　　新华社日内瓦二十五日电　中美两国大使级会谈在二十五日上午十时在日内瓦国联大厦举行第十一次会议。
　　会议在十二时零五分休会，下次会议将在八月二十七日上午十时举行。
　　在二十五日会议上，双方大使继续讨论了第一项议程——双方平民回国问题。
