    Our Great Motherland
    At the foot of Changbai Mountain on the southeastern border of Jilin Province, there are abundant coking coal seams. After several years of hard work, the exploration team has surveyed the coal fields in this area, providing accurate data for the mining departments. This is the exterior view of the drilling rig standing on the mountain.

### 我们伟大的祖国（图片）

1955-08-16
第1版()
专栏：

    我们伟大的祖国
    在吉林省东南边境的长白山麓，蕴藏着丰富的炼焦煤层。勘探队员经过了几年的辛勤劳动，已探清了这一带的煤田，给开采部门提供了精确的资料。这是矗立在山上的钻机外貌。
