Title: Norwegian Prime Minister Accepts Invitation for Regular Visits to the Soviet Union
Author:
Date: 1955-08-25
Page: 4
　
　  Norwegian Prime Minister Accepts Invitation for Regular Visits to the Soviet Union
    Xinhua News Agency, Oslo, August 24th: TASS, Oslo, reported that Norwegian newspapers published an official announcement on the 22nd, stating that Norwegian Prime Minister Einar Gerhardsen has accepted the invitation from the Soviet government to him and his wife, and decided to visit the Soviet Union around November after the municipal elections in Norway. The exact date of departure will be decided later.
    The announcement said: In response to the Soviet invitation, Prime Minister Gerhardsen said that Norway believes that there are no major political issues between Norway and the Soviet Union that need to be discussed during this visit, but he believes that such an opportunity to meet and talk with Soviet leaders will promote mutual understanding between the two countries.

### 挪威首相接受邀请定期访苏

1955-08-25
第4版()
专栏：

　  挪威首相接受邀请定期访苏
    新华社二十四日讯　塔斯社奥斯陆讯：挪威各报二十二日发表官方公报，宣布挪威首相埃纳尔·基哈德森已经接受苏联政府对他和他的夫人提出的邀请，决定在挪威各地市选举行以后大约十一月间访问苏联。动身的确实日期以后再决定。
    公报说：基哈德森首相在回答苏联的邀请时说，挪威方面认为，挪威与苏联两国之间并不存在什么重要的政治问题需要在这次访问期间加以讨论，但是，他认为有这样的一个机会同苏联领导人员会见和交谈将促进两国之间的相互谅解。
