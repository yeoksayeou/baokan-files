Title: Romanian Central Cooperative Union Delegation Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1955-08-04
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, 2nd. A delegation from the Romanian Central Cooperative Union, headed by Fe. Constantine, First Vice Chairman of the Romanian Central Cooperative Union, arrived in Beijing on August 1st, according to the 1955 execution plan of the Sino-Romanian Cultural Cooperation Agreement. The delegation will visit Chinese supply and marketing cooperatives and handicraft production cooperatives in Beijing and other places, introduce the situation of Romanian cooperatives to Chinese cooperatives, and exchange experiences with each other.

### 罗中央合作总社代表团到京

1955-08-04
第1版()
专栏：

    罗中央合作总社代表团到京
    新华社二日讯　根据中罗文化合作协定一九五五年执行计划来我国访问的以罗马尼亚中央合作总社第一副主席费·康士坦丁为首的代表团一行四人，已在八月一日到达北京。代表团将在北京和各地参观中国供销合作社和手工业生产合作社，并向中国合作社介绍罗马尼亚合作社情况，相互交流经验。
