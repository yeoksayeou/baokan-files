Title: International Conference on Peaceful Uses of Atomic Energy: Subcommittees Continue Meetings
Author:
Date: 1955-08-21
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency, Geneva, August 19th: The International Conference on the Peaceful Uses of Atomic Energy continues to hold subcommittee meetings.

　　On the 18th, the Radioactive Isotopes Subcommittee began its work. This subcommittee will discuss issues related to the production of radioactive isotopes and their utilization in science, technology, agriculture, medicine, and other sectors.

　　Some of the reports presented at the subcommittee meeting on the 18th addressed common problems faced by various countries in the utilization and production of radioactive isotopes, particularly those related to the measurement of radioactive isotope radiation. The Czechoslovakian representative, Mr. Shmana, presented a report on the significant achievements Czechoslovakia has made in utilizing radioactive isotopes in many sectors such as medicine, biology, and industry. Representatives from Australia, the United States, the United Kingdom, and other countries also spoke at the meeting about their countries' achievements in various aspects of science and technology utilizing radioactive isotopes.

　　The Physics and Reactor Subcommittee meetings have been discussing scientific and technical issues related to nuclear energy reactors for several days. The discussions have shown that although scientists from the Soviet Union, the United States, the United Kingdom, and other countries have been working in isolation from each other for many years, they have been conducting research and experiments in almost the same direction. This has strengthened the desire of scientists from various countries for extensive international cooperation.

### 和平利用原子能国际会议各小组继续开会

1955-08-21
第4版()
专栏：

　　和平利用原子能国际会议各小组继续开会
    新华社十九日讯　据塔斯社日内瓦讯：和平利用原子能国际会议继续举行各小组的会议。
    十八日，放射性同位素小组开始工作。这个小组要讨论一些有关生产放射性同位素和在科学、技术、农业和医学等部门利用放射性同位素的问题。
    十八日小组会议上所作的一些报告谈到了各国在利用和生产放射性同位素上的一些共同问题，特别是同放射性同位素射线的测定有关的问题。捷克斯洛伐克代表什马纳作了关于捷克斯洛伐克在医学、生物学、工业等许多部门中利用放射性同位素获得重大成就的报告。澳大利亚、美国、英国和其他国家的代表也在会上谈到这些国家在科学技术各方面利用放射性同位素的成绩。
    物理学和反应堆小组会议连日来讨论了同核子能反应堆有关的一些科学技术问题。讨论情况表明，虽然苏联、美国、英国等国科学家过去多年来一直是在互相隔绝的情况下进行工作的，但是他们差不多都是朝着同一方向进行研究和实验的，这样的讨论因此更加加强了各国科学家要求进行广泛的国际合作的愿望。
