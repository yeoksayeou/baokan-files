Our Great Motherland
The Youth League organization of the Yumen Oil Mine established a "Children's Paradise" to allow the elementary school children of the mine's employees to enjoy their summer vacation. They organized beneficial collective activities for the children. This is a picture of the children participating in various activities in the mine's park.

### 我们伟大的祖国（图片）

1955-08-17
第1版()
专栏：

    我们伟大的祖国
    玉门油矿青年团组织为了使矿区职工子弟小学的少年儿童愉快地度过暑假，举办了“少年儿童乐园”，组织少年进行有益的集体活动。这是少年儿童们在矿区公园里进行各种活动的情形。
