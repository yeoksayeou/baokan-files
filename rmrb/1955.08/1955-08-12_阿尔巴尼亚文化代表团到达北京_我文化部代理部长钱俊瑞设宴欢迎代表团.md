Title: Albanian Cultural Delegation Arrives in Beijing, Acting Minister of Culture Qian Junrui Hosts Banquet for the Delegation
Author:
Date: 1955-08-12
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, August 11th:

    Albanian Cultural Delegation Arrives in Beijing
    Acting Minister of Culture Qian Junrui Hosts Banquet for the Delegation
    Xinhua News Agency, August 11th. The Albanian Cultural Delegation, consisting of five members, arrived in Beijing by plane on the 11th. The head of the delegation is Fadil Paçrami, member of the Central Committee of the Albanian Labor Party and Chairman of the Albanian Committee for Foreign Cultural Relations. The four members are Gjimiter Ramadani, Director of the Information Department of the Albanian Ministry of Foreign Affairs, Hasan Duma, Principal of the Higher Normal School, writer Aleks Çaçi, and painter Nexhmedin Zajmi. The Albanian Cultural Delegation will negotiate and sign the 1956 executive plan for the Sino-Albanian Cultural Cooperation Agreement and the Sino-Albanian Radio Cooperation Agreement with the Chinese government, and will visit Beijing and other places.
    Those who went to the airport to welcome them included Liu Zhiming, Vice Minister of the Ministry of Culture of the People's Republic of China, Chen Zhongjing, Acting Director of the Bureau of Foreign Cultural Relations, Chen Yi, Director of the Cultural Department of the General Political Department of the Chinese People's Liberation Army, representatives of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs, the Ministry of Higher Education, and the Ministry of Education, as well as Albanian Ambassador to China, Nesti Nase, and others.
    Xinhua News Agency, August 11th, Ulaanbaatar dispatch: The Albanian Cultural Delegation, consisting of five members, which was invited to visit China, led by Fadil Paçrami, Chairman of the Albanian Committee for Foreign Cultural Relations, left Ulaanbaatar by plane on the morning of the 11th for Beijing after a friendly visit to the Mongolian People's Republic.
    Those who saw them off at the airport included Luvsan, Deputy Prime Minister of the Mongolian People's Republic, Zhamtsarano, Minister of Education, Tserendorj, Director of the Foreign Cultural Relations Bureau, Choiramt, Chairman of the Arts Committee, Luvsanchultem, Director of the Protocol Department of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs, and others. He Ying, the Chinese Ambassador to Mongolia, also accompanied the embassy staff to the airport to see them off.
    Xinhua News Agency, August 11th. Qian Junrui, Acting Minister of Culture of the People's Republic of China, held a banquet on the evening of the 11th to welcome the Albanian Cultural Delegation headed by Fadil Paçrami.
    Albanian Ambassador to China, Nesti Nase, was also invited to attend the banquet.
    Those present at the banquet included Xi Zhongxun, Secretary-General of the State Council, Xia Yan and Liu Zhiming, Vice Ministers of the Ministry of Culture, He Wei, Assistant Minister of Foreign Affairs, Chu Tunan, President of the Chinese People's Foreign Cultural Association, Chen Zhongjing, Acting Director of the Bureau of Foreign Cultural Relations, heads of cultural and educational departments of the Chinese government, and cultural figures Huang Songling, Dong Chuncai, Rong Gaotang, Chen Yi, Wen Jize, Chen Baichen, Ge Baoquan, and Hua Junwu.
    Qian Junrui, on behalf of the Ministry of Culture of the People's Republic of China and the Chinese cultural and artistic circles, extended cordial fraternal greetings and respect to all comrades of the Albanian Cultural Delegation who came from afar. Qian Junrui said: Although China and Albania are thousands of miles apart, the distant geographical distance cannot block like-minded friends. In his speech, Qian Junrui finally expressed his lofty respect to the Albanian people and all Albanian cultural workers who are building their socialist motherland, and wished the cultural cooperation between China and Albania continuous development.
    Fadil Paçrami then delivered a speech. On behalf of all Albanian literary and art workers and all the Albanian people, he extended warm greetings to the Chinese people and all Chinese literary and art workers. He said that the Albanian people are the most loyal and reliable friends of the Chinese people, because the friendship between the people of China and Albania is built on the common aspirations of the peaceful, democratic, and socialist camp headed by the Soviet Union. ### 阿尔巴尼亚文化代表团到达北京  我文化部代理部长钱俊瑞设宴欢迎代表团

1955-08-12
第1版()
专栏：

    阿尔巴尼亚文化代表团到达北京
    我文化部代理部长钱俊瑞设宴欢迎代表团
    新华社十一日讯　阿尔巴尼亚文化代表团一行五人，在十一日乘飞机到达北京。代表团团长是阿尔巴尼亚劳动党中央委员、阿尔巴尼亚对外文化联络委员会主席法迪尔·帕奇拉米，团员四人是阿尔巴尼亚外交部新闻司司长吉米特尔·拉玛尼、高等师范学校校长哈桑·杜玛、作家阿列克斯·恰奇和画家聂泽梅丁·查伊米。阿尔巴尼亚文化代表团将同中国政府商谈并签订中阿文化合作协定一九五六年执行计划、中阿广播合作协定，并将在北京和各地参观访问。
    前往机场欢迎的，有中华人民共和国文化部副部长刘芝明，对外文化联络局代理局长陈忠经，中国人民解放军总政治部文化部部长陈沂，外交部、高等教育部和教育部的代表，以及阿尔巴尼亚驻华大使纳赛等人。
    新华社十一日讯　乌兰巴托消息：应邀到中国访问的阿尔巴尼亚文化代表团一行五人，在团长阿尔巴尼亚对外文化联络委员会主席法迪尔·帕奇拉米率领下，在蒙古人民共和国进行了友好访问后，已于十一日晨乘飞机离开乌兰巴托去北京。
    到机场送行的有蒙古人民共和国副总理鲁布桑、教育部长扎木苏荣、对外文化联络局局长策林道尔吉、艺术委员会主席乔热姆茨、外交部交际司长鲁布桑乔尔坦等。中国驻蒙古大使何英也偕同使馆人员到机场送行。
    新华社十一日讯　中华人民共和国文化部代理部长钱俊瑞在十一日晚举行宴会，欢迎以法迪尔·帕奇拉米为首的阿尔巴尼亚文化代表团。
    阿尔巴尼亚驻华大使纳赛也应邀出席了宴会。
    出席作陪的有国务院秘书长习仲勋，文化部副部长夏衍、刘芝明，外交部部长助理何伟，中国人民对外文化协会会长楚图南，对外文化联络局代理局长陈忠经，中国政府文教部门的负责人和文化界人士黄松龄、董纯才、荣高棠、陈沂、温济泽、陈白尘、戈宝权、华君武等人。
    钱俊瑞代表中华人民共和国文化部和中国文化艺术界向远道来中国的阿尔巴尼亚文化代表团全体同志致以亲切的兄弟的问候和敬意，钱俊瑞说：虽然中阿两国相距万里，但遥远的地域是阻隔不了心意相投的朋友的。钱俊瑞在致词中最后表示向建设自己的社会主义祖国的阿尔巴尼亚人民和阿尔巴尼亚全体文化工作者致以崇高的敬意，并祝中阿两国的文化合作不断发展。
    法迪尔·帕奇拉米接着致词。他代表阿尔巴尼亚全体文艺工作者和阿尔巴尼亚全体人民，向中国人民和中国全体文艺工作者致以热烈的敬礼。他说，阿尔巴尼亚人民是中国人民最忠诚的可靠的朋友，因为中阿两国人民之间的友谊是建筑在以苏联为首的和平民主和社会主义阵营的共同愿望之上的。（附图片）
