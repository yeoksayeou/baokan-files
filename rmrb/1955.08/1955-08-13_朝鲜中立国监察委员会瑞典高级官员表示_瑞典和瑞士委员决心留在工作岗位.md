Title: North Korean Neutral Nations Supervisory Commission: Senior Swedish Official Says Swedish and Swiss Members Determined to Remain in Post

Author:

Date: 1955-08-13
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Seoul, August 12th: According to an Associated Press report from Seoul, a senior Swedish official said on the 11th that the Swiss and Swedish members of the Neutral Nations Supervisory Commission (NNSC) would not leave South Korea under pressure.

He said the South Korean government's campaign against the Neutral Nations Supervisory Commission "only strengthens our determination to remain in our posts."

The NNSC personnel also said that Sweden does not intend to take unilateral action that would lead to the dissolution of the NNSC.

### 朝鲜中立国监察委员会瑞典高级官员表示  瑞典和瑞士委员决心留在工作岗位

1955-08-13
第4版()
专栏：

    朝鲜中立国监察委员会瑞典高级官员表示
    瑞典和瑞士委员决心留在工作岗位
    新华社十二日讯　据美联社自汉城报道，一个高级瑞典官员十一日说，中立国监察委员会的瑞士委员和瑞典委员不会在压力下离开南朝鲜。
    他说，南朝鲜政府反对中立国监察委员会的运动的继续，“只有加强我们留在工作岗位上的决心”。
    这个中立国监察委员会人员又说，瑞典不打算采取足以促使中立国监察委员会解散的片面行动。
