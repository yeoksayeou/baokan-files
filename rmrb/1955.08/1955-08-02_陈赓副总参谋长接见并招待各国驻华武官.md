Title: Deputy Chief of General Staff Chen Geng Receives and Hosts Military Attachés from Various Countries

Author:
Date: 1955-08-02
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, 1st August. Deputy Chief of the General Staff of the Chinese People's Liberation Army, Chen Geng, received military attachés from various countries in China and held a reception on the afternoon of the 1st to celebrate the 28th anniversary of the founding of the army.

At 4:00 PM, General Chen Geng received Brigadier General Malik, the Indian Military Attaché, Colonel Monath, the Polish Military Attaché, Colonel Durey, the Czechoslovak Military Attaché, Colonel Fominko, the acting Soviet Military Attaché, Lieutenant Colonel Shalin, the Indonesian Military Attaché, Lieutenant Colonel Maung Maung, the Burmese Military Attaché, and Major Chigek, the Deputy Czechoslovak Military Attaché.

Following this, a reception was held to entertain the military attachés of various countries in China.

Also present were Zhang Zongxun, Deputy Chief of the General Staff of the Chinese People's Liberation Army, Fu Zhong and Gan Siqi, Deputy Directors of the General Political Department, Xiao Xiangrong, Director of the General Office of the Ministry of National Defense of the People's Republic of China, Lei Yingfu, Director of the Operations Department of the General Staff of the Chinese People's Liberation Army, and Jiang Keding, Acting Director of the Foreign Liaison Department of the Ministry of National Defense of the People's Republic of China.

### 陈赓副总参谋长接见并招待各国驻华武官

1955-08-02
第1版()
专栏：

    陈赓副总参谋长接见并招待各国驻华武官
    新华社一日讯　中国人民解放军副总参谋长陈赓为庆祝建军二十八周年，在一日下午接见各国驻华武官，并举行酒会招待。
    下午四时，陈赓将军接见了印度武官马立克准将，波兰武官莫纳特上校，捷克斯洛伐克武官杜瑞上校，苏联代理武官富敏科上校，印度尼西亚武官沙林中校，缅甸武官茂茂中校，捷克斯洛伐克副武官契格克少校。
    接着，举行了酒会招待各国驻华武官。
    出席作陪的，有中国人民解放军副总参谋长张宗逊，总政治部副主任傅锺、甘泗淇，中华人民共和国国防部办公厅主任萧向荣，中国人民解放军总参谋部作战处长雷英夫，中华人民共和国国防部对外联络处代理处长蒋克定。
