Title: Joint Statement by the Governments of Iran and Saudi Arabia
Author:
Date: 1955-08-19
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency, Tehran, 18th. King Aziz Saud of Saudi Arabia has concluded his eight-day visit to Iran and returned home on the 17th.
    The Iranian government and the Saudi Arabian Embassy in Iran issued a joint statement on the talks between the two kings, stating that the policies of the two countries "will be based on the United Nations Charter and the resolutions adopted by the Bandung Conference." The statement also revealed that the two countries agreed to upgrade their legations to embassies.

### 伊朗和沙特阿拉伯两国政府发表联合声明

1955-08-19
第4版()
专栏：

    伊朗和沙特阿拉伯两国政府发表联合声明
    新华社十八日讯　德黑兰消息：沙特阿拉伯国王阿齐兹·沙特已经结束了他在伊朗的为时八天的访问，并且在十七日回国。
    伊朗政府和沙特阿拉伯驻伊朗公使馆就两国国王的会谈发表联合声明说，两个国家的政策“将以联合国宪章和万隆会议通过的决议为基础”。声明还透露，两国同意把它们的公使馆升格为大使馆。
