Title: Ministry of Culture Holds Film Reception to Celebrate the Tenth Anniversary of the Liberation of Korea
Author:
Date: 1955-08-13
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, August 12th: The Ministry of Culture of the People's Republic of China held a film reception tonight to celebrate the upcoming tenth anniversary of the Soviet Army's liberation of Korea. The Korean film "Beautiful Song" was screened to entertain people from all walks of life in the capital.

　　Before the film screening, Wang Lanxi, Director of the Film Bureau of the Ministry of Culture of the People's Republic of China, said in his speech that cultural exchanges between China and Korea have greatly developed in recent years. Among them, the exchange of film art is particularly beneficial to all Chinese film workers. Chinese film workers have translated and produced many Korean films for screening in China, which have been loved by countless audiences. Chinese films are also welcomed by the Korean people in Korea. The friendship forged with blood by the people of China and Korea in the heroic struggle against the invaders in the past few years has been reflected in the film creation of the two countries. In order to consolidate and develop this friendship, Chinese film workers are still making further efforts.

　　Subsequently, Kim Kwi-nam, Counselor of the Embassy of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea in China, expressed his gratitude for the film reception. He said that the close cooperation between China and Korea in science, culture and art has strengthened the friendship and unity between the two peoples and encouraged the socialist construction of the two peoples.

　　The Korean film "Beautiful Song" screened tonight recorded outstanding Korean artistic works such as music, dance, and opera. These outstanding works reflect the brilliant development achievements of the national art of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea after "August 15th", expressing the Korean people's resolute struggle for the freedom and independence of their motherland, and the tenacious and rich combat emotions of the tenacious construction for the prosperity of the motherland.

　　Tonight, those who attended the film reception included Qian Junrui, Acting Minister of the Ministry of Culture, and other vice ministers Li Zheren, Wu Shengxiu, Zhou Jianren, Ye Shengtao, Chen Jiakang, Assistant Minister of Foreign Affairs, Wang Lanxi, Director of the Film Bureau of the Ministry of Culture, heads of various people's organizations, people from all walks of life, combat heroes, labor models and staff of institutions, as well as Choi Il, Ambassador of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea to China, and embassy staff.

### 文化部举行电影招待会庆祝朝鲜解放十周年

1955-08-13
第1版()
专栏：

    文化部举行电影招待会庆祝朝鲜解放十周年
    新华社十二日讯　中华人民共和国文化部为庆祝即将届临的苏军解放朝鲜十周年，在今晚举行电影招待会，放映朝鲜影片“美丽的歌”，招待首都各界人士。
    影片放映前，中华人民共和国文化部电影事业管理局局长王阑西致词说，几年来，中朝两国文化交流方面获得很大的发展。其中电影艺术上的交流，对于中国全体电影工作者是特别有益的。中国电影工作者译制了许多朝鲜影片在中国放映，获得无数观众的喜爱。中国的影片也在朝鲜受到朝鲜人民的欢迎。中朝两国人民在过去几年间抵抗侵略者的英勇斗争中用鲜血凝成的友谊，已在两国的电影创作中反映了出来。为了巩固和发展这种友谊，中国电影工作者还在作更进一步的努力。
    接着，朝鲜民主主义人民共和国驻华大使馆参赞金贵南致词，对电影招待会表示感谢。他说，朝中两国科学文化艺术的密切合作，加强了两国人民的友谊团结，鼓舞了两国人民的社会主义建设。
    今晚放映的朝鲜影片“美丽的歌”，纪录了朝鲜优秀的音乐、舞蹈和歌剧等艺术作品，这些优秀作品反映了“八·一五”以后，朝鲜民主主义人民共和国的民族艺术的灿烂发展的成果，表达了朝鲜人民为祖国自由独立而坚决战斗、为祖国富强而顽强建设的坚韧而丰富的战斗感情。
    今晚，出席电影招待会的有文化部代理部长钱俊瑞以及其他各部副部长李哲人、吴生秀、周建人、叶圣陶，外交部部长助理陈家康，文化部电影事业管理局局长王阑西，各人民团体负责人，各界人士，战斗英雄，劳动模范和机关工作人员以及朝鲜民主主义人民共和国驻华大使崔一和使馆人员等。
