Title: President of the British Peace Committee, Mr. Pritt, and his wife arrive in Beijing
Author: 
Date: 1955-08-03
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, 2nd. Mr. Pritt, President of the British Peace Committee, recipient of the Stalin International Peace Prize "For Strengthening Peace Among Nations," and President of the International Association of Democratic Lawyers, arrived in Beijing by plane on the 2nd, at the invitation of the China Peace Committee and the China Political Science and Law Association.

Those who went to the airport to welcome them included Shen Junru, Zhang Zhiran, and Qian Duansheng, Vice Presidents of the China Political Science and Law Association; Luo Longji, Standing Committee Member of the China Peace Committee; Liu Guanyi, Secretary-General; and Cao Mengjun, Deputy Secretary-General of the All-China Democratic Women's Federation.

### 英国和平委员会会长普里特和夫人到北京

1955-08-03
第1版()
专栏：

    英国和平委员会会长普里特和夫人到北京
    新华社二日讯　应中国人民保卫世界和平委员会和中国政治法律学会的邀请，来我国访问的英国和平委员会会长、“加强国际和平”斯大林国际奖金得奖人、国际民主法律工作者协会主席普里特和他的夫人在二日乘飞机抵达北京。
    前往机场欢迎的，有中国政治法律学会副主席沈钧儒、张志让、钱端升，中国人民保卫世界和平委员会常务委员罗隆基、秘书长刘贯一，中华全国民主妇女联合会副秘书长曹孟君。
