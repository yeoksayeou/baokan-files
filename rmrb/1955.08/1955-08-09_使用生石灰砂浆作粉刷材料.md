Title: Using Quicklime Mortar as a Plastering Material
Author: Li Shengting
Date: 1955-08-09
Page: 2
Advanced Experiences and Advanced Producers

    Using quicklime mortar instead of lime putty as a plastering material can greatly reduce construction costs. The First Civil Engineering Company of the Northwest Construction Engineering Bureau trialed this method, resulting in savings of one-half of the quicklime and twenty percent of the sand. According to preliminary statistics, in a building area of 10,000 square meters of classroom buildings, the ceiling plastering alone can save 21 tons of quicklime. At 25 yuan per ton of quicklime, this amounts to a saving of 525 yuan. After the Fifth Engineering Company of the Beijing Construction Engineering Bureau used quicklime mortar as a plastering material, it can save the state about 90,000 yuan annually.

    Using quicklime mortar as a plastering material not only saves money but also ensures quality. The Beijing Packaging Company used quicklime mortar for plastering walls, and it does not fall off or crack. The First Civil Engineering Company of the Northwest Construction Engineering Bureau found that using quicklime mortar for plastering walls can shorten the setting time by one-third. Moreover, since quicklime releases a large amount of heat energy when dissolved, it can provide frost protection. In winter construction, it can accelerate the progress of the project and shorten the construction period.

    When using quicklime mortar, attention should be paid to the relationship between mortar and temperature and air humidity, and the mixing ratio should be strictly controlled. At the same time, the lime must be thoroughly sieved and must not contain any broken pieces or particles to prevent blisters and cracks from appearing on the wall surface.

### 使用生石灰砂浆作粉刷材料
李圣庭
1955-08-09
第2版()
专栏：先进经验和先进生产者

    使用生石灰砂浆作粉刷材料
    李圣庭
    使用生石灰砂浆代替石灰膏粉作粉刷材料，可以大大降低工程造价。西北建筑工程局第一土建工程公司试用了这一方法，结果节约了生石灰二分之一，砂子百分之二十。据初步统计，在一万平方公尺教室大楼的建筑面积中，只天棚抹灰一项就可节约二十一吨生石灰，一吨生石灰以二十五元计算，就可节约五百二十五元。北京市建筑工程局第五工程公司使用生石灰砂浆作粉刷材料后，每年能为国家节约九万元左右。
    使用生石灰砂浆作粉刷材料不但可以节约，而且还能保证质量。北京市包装公司用生石灰砂浆抹墙，不掉不裂。西北建筑工程局第一土建工程公司使用生石灰抹墙，凝结时间可缩短三分之一。又由于生石灰溶解时能放出大量的热能，可以起到防冻的作用，在冬季施工时可加速工程进度、缩短工期。
    使用生石灰砂浆应注意砂浆同温度、空气湿度的关系，严格掌握配合比例。同时，石灰必须筛净，不得掺有碎块和颗粒，免使墙面发生凸泡和崩裂。
