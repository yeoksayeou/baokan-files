Title: US Military Personnel Plan to Build More Airports in South Vietnam
Author:
Date: 1955-08-22
Page: 4

　　Xinhua News Agency, Hanoi, 21st -- "Voice of Vietnam" radio broadcast on the 20th that after the South Vietnamese authorities newly built a military airport in An Phu area, Chau Doc province, some American military personnel in South Vietnam went to Can Tho, Vinh Long, Ha Tien, Rach Gia, Soc Trang and other places in mid-August for inspection. Now, they are studying the establishment of military airports in these places.

### 美国军事人员计划在南越增建机场

1955-08-22
第4版()
专栏：

　　美国军事人员计划在南越增建机场
    新华社河内二十一日电　据“越南之声”电台二十日广播：继南越当局在朱笃省安富地区新建一个军用飞机场之后，在南越的一些美国军事人员又在八月中旬到芹苴、永隆、河仙、迪石、朔庄等地进行视察。现在，他们正在研究在这些地方建立军用飞机场。
