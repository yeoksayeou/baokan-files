Title: China and Egypt Sign Contract on Egyptian Cotton Sales to China
Author:
Date: 1955-08-11
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, 10th. After negotiations between representatives of the China National Import and Export Corporation and the Egyptian Government Cotton Commission, a contract was recently signed in Cairo regarding the sale of 40,000 bales of Egyptian cotton (a total of more than 13,000 tons) to the People's Republic of China. In addition, the China National Import and Export Corporation purchased another 6,000 bales of cotton (a total of 2,000 tons) on the Egyptian market.

### 中埃签订关于埃及售给中国棉花的合同

1955-08-11
第1版()
专栏：

    中埃签订关于埃及售给中国棉花的合同
    新华社十日讯　中国进出口公司代表和埃及政府棉花委员会经过谈判以后，最近在开罗签订了关于埃及售给中华人民共和国棉花四万包（共一万三千多吨）的合同。另外，中国进出口公司又在埃及市场上购买了棉花六千包（共二千吨）。
