Title: Manager of Italian Comert Company Concludes Transaction with Chinese Trade Organization
Author:
Date: 1955-08-23
Page: 1

　　Manager of Italian Comert Company
　  Concludes Transaction with Chinese Trade Organization
    Xinhua News Agency, 22nd. Gentili, the manager of the Italian Comert Company, who arrived in Beijing on July 21st, held discussions with Chinese trade organizations and concluded transactions. He left Beijing by plane on August 20th via Moscow to return to his country.

### 意大利科默特公司经理  同我国贸易机构作成交易

1955-08-23
第1版()
专栏：

　　意大利科默特公司经理
　  同我国贸易机构作成交易
    新华社二十二日讯　七月二十一日到北京的意大利科默特公司经理根蒂利同中国贸易机构进行了商谈，并作成了交易。他在八月二十日乘飞机离京经莫斯科回国。
