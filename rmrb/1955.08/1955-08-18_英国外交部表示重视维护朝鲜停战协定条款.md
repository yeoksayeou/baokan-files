Title: British Foreign Office Expresses Importance of Maintaining Korean Armistice Agreement Terms
Author:
Date: 1955-08-18
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, London, August 17th (via Reuters) - A British Foreign Office spokesperson commented on U.S. Secretary of State Dulles' remarks at a press conference in Washington regarding the Neutral Nations Supervisory Commission in Korea, stating that the British view is: "Even if the armistice terms are considered to have certain shortcomings, Britain attaches great importance to maintaining the armistice terms."

The spokesperson added that the British Foreign Office is unaware of any specific measures taken by the sixteen United Nations allied countries that fought in Korea aimed at disbanding the Neutral Nations Supervisory Commission.

### 英国外交部表示重视维护朝鲜停战协定条款

1955-08-18
第4版()
专栏：

    英国外交部表示重视维护朝鲜停战协定条款
    新华社十七日讯　据路透社伦敦消息：英国外交部发言人十六日晚就美国国务卿杜勒斯在华盛顿记者招待会上关于朝鲜中立国监察委员会的谈话表示意见说，英国的看法是：“即使认为停战条款有某些缺点，英国还是极其重视维护停战条款”。
    发言人又说，英国外交部不知道在朝鲜作战的十六个联合国盟国采取目的在解散中立国监察委员会的任何具体措施的事情。
