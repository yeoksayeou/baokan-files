Title: All-China Women's Federation Hosts Farewell Banquet for Representatives from Chile and Other Countries

Author:

Date: 1955-08-22
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, August 20th: The representatives from Chile, Bolivia, Colombia, and other countries who attended the World Mothers' Congress and were invited to visit China, left Beijing by plane on the morning of the 20th to return to their respective countries. The representatives from Canada and Australia who remained in Beijing will also return to their countries in succession in the near future.

On the evening of the 19th, Deng Yingchao and Li Dequan, vice-chairpersons of the All-China Democratic Women's Federation, held a farewell banquet for the representatives. At the banquet, Deng Yingchao raised a glass to congratulate women and mothers of all countries on achieving greater success in implementing the resolutions of the Lausanne World Mothers' Congress and striving for peace and promoting friendship, and wished the representatives a safe journey home. Deng Yingchao also warmly welcomed the representatives of all countries to visit China again and welcomed more foreign friends to visit China. The entire banquet was filled with a friendly atmosphere. After the banquet, the hosts and guests embraced repeatedly with reluctant farewell feelings.

During their stay in China, the representatives visited Shanghai, Dalian, Shenyang, Anshan, Harbin and other places. They visited factories, rural areas, nurseries, children's palaces, and workers' cultural palaces in various places, and had extensive contact with women from all walks of life. Many foreign guests expressed surprise and admiration at the scale and speed of China's construction. Mrs. Lía Rafae, head of the Chilean delegation, said that she had a very deep impression of the improvement of the social status of Chinese women and was very interested in the welfare measures for women and children. Representatives from Chile, Bolivia, and Colombia all said that after returning home, they would tell the people of their countries what they had seen and heard in China, and strive to expand the peace movement and enhance the understanding and friendship of the people and women of their countries towards New China.

In addition: During the visit to China by the representatives of various countries attending the World Mothers' Congress, Deng Yingchao, Vice-Chairman of the All-China Democratic Women's Federation, met separately with representatives from Vietnam, Canada, and Australia, and introduced the situation and experience of China's work on women and children.

### 我全国民主妇联设宴欢送智利等国代表

1955-08-22
第4版()
专栏：

　　我全国民主妇联设宴欢送智利等国代表
    新华社二十日讯　应邀来中国访问的出席世界母亲大会的智利、玻利维亚、哥伦比亚等国代表在二十日晨乘飞机离开北京分别返国。留在北京的加拿大和澳大利亚代表们也将在最近陆续返国。
    十九日晚，中华全国民主妇女联合会副主席邓颖超和李德全举行欢送宴会，为代表们饯行。宴会上，邓颖超举杯祝贺各国妇女和母亲们在贯彻洛桑世界母亲大会决议，争取和平、增进友谊的事业中获得更大的成就，并祝代表们在回国途中一路平安。邓颖超还热诚地欢迎各国代表再来中国访问并欢迎更多的外国朋友来访问中国。整个宴会充满着友好的气氛。宴会结束后，宾主们怀着依依不舍的惜别心情一再拥抱。
    代表们在中国期间曾到上海、大连、沈阳、鞍山、哈尔滨等地参观访问。他们访问了各地的工厂、农村、托儿所、少年宫、工人文化宫，并和各界妇女广泛地接触。许多外宾对中国建设规模之大、速度之快，表示惊奇和敬佩。智利代表团团长利亚·拉法耶夫人表示对中国妇女社会地位的提高有了极为深刻的印象，对妇女儿童福利措施很感兴趣。智利、玻利维亚、哥伦比亚的代表都表示回国后，要把她们在中国的所见所闻，转告给国内人民，并为扩大和平运动、增进国内人民和妇女对新中国的了解和友谊而努力。
    又讯：出席世界母亲大会的各国代表在访问中国期间，中华全国民主妇女联合会副主席邓颖超曾分别接见了越南、加拿大和澳大利亚代表，介绍了中国妇女儿童工作方面的情况和经验。
