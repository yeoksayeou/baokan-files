Title: Two Villages in Goa Declare Independence from Portuguese Rule
Author:
Date: 1955-08-06
Page: 4

    Xinhua News Agency, New Delhi, August 5th: According to Indian newspapers, the two villages of Maem and Siolim in Goa declared their independence from Portuguese colonial rule on the 2nd. Residents of the two villages held parades and raised the Indian flag.

### 果阿两个村庄宣布脱离葡萄牙统治

1955-08-06
第4版()
专栏：

    果阿两个村庄宣布脱离葡萄牙统治
    新华社新德里五日电　据印度报纸消息，果阿的麦伊姆和苏拉两个村庄在二日宣布脱离葡萄牙的殖民统治。这两个村庄的居民举行了游行，并且升起了印度国旗。
