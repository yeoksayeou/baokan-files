Title: Li Dequan Reports on the World Mothers' Congress
Author:
Date: 1955-08-11
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, 10th August. Li Dequan, head of the Chinese delegation to the World Mothers' Congress, today delivered a report on the situation and achievements of the World Mothers' Congress at the Third Beijing Municipal Women's Congress.

At today's meeting, after listening to Li Dequan's report, the representatives unanimously passed the "Resolution on Endorsing the Lausanne World Mothers' Congress Declaration and Various Documents," calling on all women and mothers in Beijing, together with the people of the whole country, to fully support the Chinese government's peaceful diplomatic policies, strive to complete the first Five-Year Plan for the development of the national economy, strive for an international peaceful environment, strengthen the friendship between women and mothers of all countries, and contribute all their strength to the realization of the resolutions of the World Mothers' Congress.

The Third Beijing Municipal Women's Congress has been held in Beijing since the 8th. The meeting is still in progress.

### 李德全作关于世界母亲大会的报告

1955-08-11
第1版()
专栏：

    李德全作关于世界母亲大会的报告
    新华社十日讯　中国出席世界母亲大会代表团团长李德全今天在北京市第三届妇女代表大会上作了关于世界母亲大会的情况和成就的报告。
    在今天的会议上，代表们听了李德全报告后，一致通过了“关于拥护洛桑世界母亲大会宣言及各项文件的决议”，号召北京全市的妇女和母亲和全国人民一起，全力支持中国政府的和平外交政策，努力完成祖国发展国民经济的第一个五年计划，争取国际和平环境，加强各国妇女和母亲间的友谊，为实现世界母亲大会的决议而贡献出一切力量。
    北京市第三届妇女代表大会是从八日起在北京市举行的。会议还在继续进行中。
