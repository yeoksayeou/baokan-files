Title: Sino-American Ambassadors Hold Seventh Meeting
Author:
Date: 1955-08-17
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, Geneva, 16th -- The Sino-American ambassadorial talks held their seventh meeting at 10:00 AM local time on the 16th. The meeting adjourned at 12:20 PM. The next meeting will be held at 10:00 AM on August 18th.

　　At the meeting on the 16th, the two ambassadors continued to discuss the first item on the agenda - the repatriation of civilians by both sides.

### 中美两国大使举行第七次会谈

1955-08-17
第1版()
专栏：

    中美两国大使举行第七次会谈
    新华社日内瓦十六日电　中美两国大使级会谈在十六日本地时间上午十时举行第七次会议。会议在十二时二十分休会。下次会议将在八月十八日上午十时举行。
    在十六日会议上，双方大使继续讨论了第一项议程——双方平民回国问题。
