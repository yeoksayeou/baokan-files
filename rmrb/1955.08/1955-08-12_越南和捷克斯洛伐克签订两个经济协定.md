Title: Vietnam and Czechoslovakia Sign Two Economic Agreements
Author:
Date: 1955-08-12
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency Report:
    Vietnam and Czechoslovakia signed two economic agreements in Hanoi on the afternoon of the 10th.
    The first agreement concerns the exchange of goods and payments between the two countries in 1955. According to this agreement, the Czechoslovak Republic will mainly supply diesel engines, water pumps and other machinery, as well as textiles and chemicals; the Democratic Republic of Vietnam will supply oil crops, timber, rattan, tea, coffee and spices.
    The second agreement is an economic aid agreement aimed at assisting the Democratic Republic of Vietnam in its economic recovery. According to this agreement, the Czechoslovak Republic will supply machinery and goods, provide scientific and technical experts, and provide Vietnamese students with the possibility of conducting technical studies in Czechoslovak schools.
    Negotiations on the above two agreements were conducted in an atmosphere of friendly cooperation.

### 越南和捷克斯洛伐克签订两个经济协定

1955-08-12
第4版()
专栏：

    越南和捷克斯洛伐克签订两个经济协定
    据新华社讯　越南民主共和国和捷克斯洛伐克共和国十日下午在河内签订了两个经济协定。
    第一个协定是关于一九五五年两国之间的换货和支付的协定。根据这一协定，捷克斯洛伐克共和国主要地将供给柴油发动机、抽水机和其他机器，以及纺织品和化学品；越南民主共和国将供给油料作物、木材、藤、茶、咖啡和香料。
    第二个协定是一个目的在帮助越南民主共和国进行经济恢复的经济援助协定。根据这一协定，捷克斯洛伐克共和国将供给机器和货物，提供科学和技术专家，并且为越南学生提供在捷克斯洛伐克等学校进行技术研究的可能性。
    上述两个协定的谈判是在友好合作的气氛中进行的。
