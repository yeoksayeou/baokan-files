Title: Photo

Author: 
Date: 1955-08-08
Page: 4

    The ambassadorial talks between the People's Republic of China and the United States of America began on August 1st at the Palace of Nations in Geneva. The photo shows the representatives from both countries attending the talks before they formally began. The third person from the left is Wang Bingnan, our ambassador to Poland, and the fourth person is U. Alexis Johnson, the U.S. ambassador to Czechoslovakia.
                                Xinhua News Agency稿
