Title: US Assistant Secretary of State Merchant Arrives in Paris to Discuss Preparations for Four Foreign Ministers Meeting with France
Author:
Date: 1955-08-31
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Paris, August 30th: According to TASS, US Assistant Secretary of State for European Affairs Merchant arrived in Paris on August 29th. According to a broadcast by Paris Radio, Merchant said that he would discuss the preparations for the Four Foreign Ministers Meeting, scheduled to be held in Geneva on October 27th, with French official representatives.

Merchant will go to Bonn on August 30th, and then to London. Parisian newspapers say that Merchant's trip is intended to prepare for the Western Three Foreign Ministers Meeting to be held before the October Four Foreign Ministers Meeting.

### 美国助理国务卿麦钱特到巴黎  同法国商谈四外长会议准备问题

1955-08-31
第4版()
专栏：

　　美国助理国务卿麦钱特到巴黎
　  同法国商谈四外长会议准备问题
    新华社三十日讯　据塔斯社巴黎讯：美国负责欧洲事务的助理国务卿麦钱特八月二十九日到巴黎。据巴黎电台广播，麦钱特说，他将和法国官方代表商谈订于十月二十七日在日内瓦举行的四国外长会议的准备问题。
    麦钱特在八月三十日要去波恩，接着还要去伦敦。巴黎报纸说，麦钱特此行的目的是要为在十月四国外长会议前夕举行的西方三国外长会议做好准备。
