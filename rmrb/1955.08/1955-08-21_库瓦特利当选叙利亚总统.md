Title: Kuwatli Elected President of Syria
Author:
Date: 1955-08-21
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Damascus, August 20th: The Syrian Parliament elected Shukri al-Kuwatli as President of Syria on the 18th with 91 votes to 41.

In his first speech after being elected, Shukri al-Kuwatli stated that he would stand on the position of supra-partisan interests and asked the parliament to cooperate with him to realize the tasks facing Syria.

After Shukri al-Kuwatli was elected, Foreign Minister and Acting Minister of Defense Khaled al-Azem, who had been nominated to participate in the presidential election, has applied to resign.

### 库瓦特利当选叙利亚总统

1955-08-21
第4版()
专栏：

　　库瓦特利当选叙利亚总统
    新华社二十日讯　大马士革消息：叙利亚议会十八日以九十一票对四十一票选举了舒克里·库瓦特利为叙利亚总统。
    舒克里·库瓦特利在当选后第一次发表的演说中表示他将站在超党派利益的立场上，并且要求议会同他合作以实现叙利亚所面临的任务。
    在舒克里·库瓦特利当选后，曾经被提名参加总统竞选的外交部长兼代理国防部长哈勒德·阿泽姆已经申请辞职。
