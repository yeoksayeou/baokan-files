Title: Released Crew Members of Soviet Tanker "Tuapse" Arrive in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1955-08-01
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, July 31st: The twenty-nine released crew members of the Soviet tanker "Tuapse," having passed through Guangzhou and Wuhan, arrived in Beijing on July 31st. They will soon return to their country from Beijing.

### 苏联油船“图阿普斯号”获释船员到京

1955-08-01
第4版()
专栏：

    苏联油船“图阿普斯号”获释船员到京
    新华社三十一日讯　苏联油船“图阿普斯号”获释的二十九个船员，经过广州、武汉已在七月三十一日到达北京。他们即将由北京返国。
