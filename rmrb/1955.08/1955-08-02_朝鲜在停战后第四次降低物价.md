Title: Korea Reduces Prices for the Fourth Time After the Armistice
Author:
Date: 1955-08-02
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency, Pyongyang, August 1st: According to the Korean Central News Agency, the Cabinet of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea issued a decision to reduce state retail prices for daily necessities, effective August 1st. This is the fourth price reduction in the Democratic People's Republic of Korea since the armistice. This price reduction will benefit residents of North Korea by more than 3.6 billion won (Korean currency) annually.

The decision stipulates that the state retail prices of 335 kinds of daily industrial products will be reduced this time. Among them, the price of cotton fabrics will be reduced by 11 to 31 percent, the price of underwear will be reduced by 6 to 40 percent, the price of rubber shoes will be reduced by 12.5 to 44 percent, the price of cigarettes will be reduced by 16.7 to 30 percent, the price of metal products will be reduced by 11 to 46 percent, the price of household medicines will be reduced by 10 to 77 percent, and the price of salt will be reduced by 25 percent.

The decision states: Thanks to the selfless labor of all workers and working people, the people's economic recovery and development plan is being implemented effectively. In particular, labor productivity in the industrial sector has been significantly improved, and production costs have been continuously reduced. Under these circumstances, the Cabinet of the Republic has decided to reduce prices in order to further improve the living standards of the people.

### 朝鲜在停战后第四次降低物价

1955-08-02
第4版()
专栏：

    朝鲜在停战后第四次降低物价
    新华社平壤一日电　据朝鲜中央通讯社讯：朝鲜民主主义人民共和国内阁发布决定，从八月一日起降低人民生活必需品的国家零售价格。这是朝鲜民主主义人民共和国在停战后第四次降低物价。这次降低物价将使朝鲜北部居民一年受益三十六亿圆（朝鲜币）以上。
    决定中规定，这次共降低三百三十五种日用工业产品的国家零售价格，其中棉织品价格降低百分之十一到百分之三十一，内衣价格降低百分之六到百分之四十，胶鞋价格降低百分之十二点五到百分之四十四，香烟价格降低百分之十六点七到百分之三十，金属制品价格降低百分之十一到百分之四十六，家用医药品价格降低百分之十到百分之七十七，盐价格降低百分之二十五。
    决定中说：由于全体工人和劳动人民的忘我劳动，人民经济恢复发展计划在有成效地实行着。特别是在工业部门中劳动生产率得到显著提高，生产成本不断降低。在这种情况下，共和国内阁为了进一步提高人民的生活水平，决定降低物价。
