Title: President Sukarno and the King of Saudi Arabia Issue Joint Statement
Author:
Date: 1955-08-05
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Jeddah, August 4th: Indonesian President Sukarno, who recently made a pilgrimage to Mecca, left Jeddah, Saudi Arabia, on the 3rd to return home. He is scheduled to return to Jakarta on August 5th.

President Sukarno and Saudi Arabian King Saud issued a joint statement on the evening of the 2nd, stating that during President Sukarno's official visit to King Saud in Jeddah, they held talks. The joint statement said that during the talks, they addressed many important issues and paid particular attention to the issue of implementing cooperation and mutual assistance between the two countries in practical and feasible ways based on the resolutions of the Asian-African Conference.

### 苏加诺总统和沙特国王发表联合声明

1955-08-05
第4版()
专栏：

    苏加诺总统和沙特国王发表联合声明
    新华社四日讯　吉达消息：最近到麦加朝圣的印度尼西亚总统苏加诺，已在三日离开沙特阿拉伯的吉达回国。他预定在八月五日回到雅加达。
    苏加诺总统和沙特阿拉伯国王沙特在二日晚间发表联合声明说，在苏加诺总统在吉达正式访问国王沙特期间，他们举行了会谈。联合声明说，他们在会谈中，曾涉及许多重要问题，并且特别注意到根据亚非会议的决议两国通过切实可行的方式来实行合作互助的办法问题。
