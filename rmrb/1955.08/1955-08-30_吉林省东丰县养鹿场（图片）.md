　　Jilin Province Dongfeng County Deer Farm
　　The state-owned Dongfeng County Deer Farm in Jilin Province now raises more than 1,900 deer. Every year, it harvests a large amount of antler velvet and also produces many by-products such as deerskin, deer tendons, deer tails, and deer placenta cream.

　　Antler velvet is a specialty of China and a very precious medicinal material. It has great economic value, and the products are sold both domestically and abroad. This year, the Dongfeng County Deer Farm plans to produce more than 3,270 taels of antler velvet, which has already been harvested. Photo by Li Keming (Manuscript from Jilin Daily)

### 吉林省东丰县养鹿场（图片）
李可明
1955-08-30
第2版()
专栏：

　　吉林省东丰县养鹿场
　　吉林省东丰县地方国营养鹿场现在养了一千九百多只鹿，每年收获大量的鹿茸，还生产许多鹿皮、鹿筋、鹿尾、鹿胎膏等副产品。
　　鹿茸是我国的特产，也是一种非常名贵的药材，有很大的经济价值，产品销售国内外。今年，东丰县鹿场计划生产鹿茸三千二百七十多两，现在已经收割了。李可明摄（吉林日报社稿）
