Title: Film Week Held for National Youth Activists

Author:

Date: 1955-08-16
Page: 2

The Ministry of Culture of the People's Republic of China and the Central Committee of the Chinese New Democratic Youth League have decided to jointly host the "National Youth Socialist Construction Activists Conference Film Week" from September 1st to 15th.

The Film Week will be held in Beijing, Tianjin, Shanghai, Shenyang, Harbin, Changchun, Wuhan, Guangzhou, Chongqing, Kunming, Xi'an, Urumqi, Anshan, Fushun, Dalian, Taiyuan, Lanzhou, Hohhot, Qingdao, and Nanjing. The main films screened in the first twelve cities will be "Son of the Partisan" or "For Fourteen Lives." The latter eight cities will mainly screen "Girl of the Guerrilla" or "Hasan and Gamila."

The purpose of holding this Film Week is to welcome and cooperate with the convening of the National Youth Socialist Construction Activists Conference, to conduct an education for the vast number of young people in loving the motherland, building the motherland, and defending the motherland, so as to mobilize the vast number of young people to strive to realize the first five-year plan.

(Xinhua News Agency)

### 为全国青年积极分子举办电影周

1955-08-16
第2版()
专栏：

    为全国青年积极分子举办电影周
    中华人民共和国文化部和中国新民主主义青年团中央委员会决定在九月一日到十五日，联合主办“全国青年社会主义建设积极分子大会电影周”。
    电影周将在北京、天津、上海、沈阳、哈尔滨、长春、武汉、广州、重庆、昆明、西安、乌鲁木齐、鞍山、抚顺、旅大、太原、兰州、呼和浩特、青岛、南京举行。前十二个城市放映的主要影片是“游击队员之子”或“为了十四条生命”。后八个城市主要放映“游击队的姑娘”或“哈森与加米拉”。
    这次举办电影周是为了迎接和配合全国青年社会主义建设积极分子大会的召开，对广大青年进行一次热爱祖国、建设祖国和保卫祖国的教育，从而动员广大青年为实现第一个五年计划而奋斗。
                                  （新华社）
