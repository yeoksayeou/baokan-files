Title: Vietnamese People Warmly Welcome the Anniversary of the August Revolution and National Day

Author:
Date: 1955-08-19
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Hanoi, August 18th: According to the Vietnam News Agency, the people of North Vietnam are warmly welcoming the anniversary of the Vietnamese August Revolution (August 19th) and the Vietnamese National Day (September 2nd).

In the past few weeks, people in various places have gathered to study the achievements of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam in the past ten years. In the ten days up to August 15th, more than 55,800 people in the suburbs of Hanoi participated in the study.

In the past few days, workers, civil servants, soldiers, farmers, and people from other strata in North Vietnam have been reviewing the results of the competition campaigns they have launched to welcome the Revolution Day and Independence Day.

The workers of the An Phu Power Plant in Hanoi have proposed 177 initiatives, thereby saving nearly 63 million Vietnamese dong in raw materials and 6,700 work hours.

Workers at a phosphate factory in Nghệ An province decided to increase production by 15 percent in August. In the first three days of this month alone, the plant's hourly output of phosphate increased by 149 kilograms.

Farmers' drought resistance efforts have also achieved good results.

### 越南人民热烈迎接八月革命纪念日和国庆节

1955-08-19
第4版()
专栏：

    越南人民热烈迎接八月革命纪念日和国庆节
    新华社河内十八日电　据越南通讯社讯：越南北方的人民热烈地迎接越南八月革命纪念日（八月十九日）和越南国庆节（九月二日）。
    过去几星期中，各地人民都聚会学习过去十年中越南民主共和国所获得的成就。在截至八月十五日为止的十天中，河内郊区就有五万五千八百多人参加了学习。
    在过去几天中，北越的工人、公务人员、军人、农民和其他阶层的人民都在检阅他们为迎接革命节和独立日而举行的竞赛运动的成绩。
    河内安埠发电厂的工人们已经提出了一百七十七项创议，从而节约了将近六千三百万越币的原料和节省了六千七百个工时。
    义安省一个磷酸盐工厂的工人决定在八月份把产量提高百分之十五。仅在这个月的头三天中，这个工厂全厂每小时的磷酸盐产量就增加了一百四十九公斤。
    农民的抗旱运动也取得了良好的成绩。
