Title: Lebanese Foreign Minister Tells Reporters in Egypt: Lebanon Will Never Participate in Military Blocs
Author:
Date: 1955-08-30
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Cairo, August 28th (TASS): Lebanese Foreign Minister Hamid Frangieh arrived in Egypt for an official visit. Egyptian newspapers believe this visit is of great significance and express their belief that Frangieh's talks with the Egyptian government will help consolidate cooperation between Arab countries opposed to joining military blocs.

According to "Egyptian Gazette," Frangieh emphasized in a statement to Egyptian newspaper reporters that Lebanon is firmly opposed to participating in military blocs. He said, "We have completely rejected the proposals to join the Turkish-Iraqi pact."

### 黎巴嫩外长在埃及向记者表示　黎巴嫩决不参加军事集团

1955-08-30
第4版()
专栏：

　　黎巴嫩外长在埃及向记者表示
　　黎巴嫩决不参加军事集团
    新华社二十九日讯　塔斯社开罗二十八日讯：黎巴嫩外交部长哈米德·弗朗基到达埃及作正式访问。埃及报纸认为这次访问的意义重大，并且表示相信，弗朗基同埃及政府的谈判将有助于巩固反对参加军事集团的阿拉伯国家之间的合作。
    据“埃及日报”报道，弗朗基在向埃及报纸记者发表谈话时着重指出，黎巴嫩坚决不参加军事集团。他说，“我们完全拒绝了关于参加土耳其—伊拉克协定的建议”。
