Title: State Council Appoints a Batch of State and Higher Education Staff

Author: 
Date: 1955-08-02
Page: 2

Xinhua News Agency
    The State Council appointed a batch of state and higher education staff on July 18. The list is as follows:
    Tan Shengbin as Assistant Minister of the Ministry of Supervision;
    Zhang Cuizhong as Vice President of the Academy of Educational Administration;
    Ling Sha (female) as President of Beijing Normal College;
    Li Jianxun as Vice President of Tianjin Normal College;
    Sun Zongrong as President of Hefei Mining Institute, Zhu Xianmin and Cheng Zili as Vice Presidents;
    Guo Qingfan as Vice President of Jiangxi Normal College;
    Chen Weishi as President of South China Normal College, Xu Jiyuan as Vice President;
    Zhang Yongqing as President of Southwest Normal College, Xie Lihui and Wang Zhupi as Vice Presidents.
                                (Xinhua News Agency)

### 国务院任命一批国家工作人员和高等学校工作人员

1955-08-02
第2版()
专栏：

    国务院任命一批国家工作人员和高等学校工作人员
    国务院七月十八日任命了一批国家工作人员和高等学校工作人员，名单如下：
    谭生彬为监察部部长助理；
    张萃中为教育行政学院副院长；
    凌莎（女）为北京师范学院院长；
    李建勋为天津师范学院副院长；
    孙宗溶为合肥矿业学院院长，朱献民、程自立为副院长；
    郭庆棻为江西师范学院副院长；
    陈唯实为华南师范学院院长，徐霁远为副院长；
    张永青为西南师范学院院长，谢立惠、王逐萍为副院长。
                                （新华社）
