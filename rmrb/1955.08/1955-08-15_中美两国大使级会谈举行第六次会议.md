Title: Sino-American Ambassadorial Talks Hold Sixth Meeting
Author: 
Date: 1955-08-15
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, Geneva, August 14th: The Sino-American ambassadorial talks held their sixth meeting on the 13th at 10:00 AM local time. The meeting adjourned at 11:45 AM. The next meeting is scheduled for 10:00 AM on August 16th.

    At the meeting on the 13th, the two ambassadors continued to discuss the first item on the agenda - the return of civilians to their respective countries.

### 中美两国大使级会谈举行第六次会议

1955-08-15
第1版()
专栏：

    中美两国大使级会谈举行第六次会议
    新华社日内瓦十四日电  中美两国大使级会谈在十三日本地时间上午十时举行第六次会议，会议在十一时四十五分休会。下次会谈订于八月十六日上午十时举行。
    在十三日会议上，双方大使继续讨论了第一项议程——双方平民回国问题。
