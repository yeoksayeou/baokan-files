Title: Yugoslav "Kolo" Folk Dance Troupe Passes Through Manzhouli on its Way to Beijing
Author:
Date: 1955-08-27
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Manzhouli, August 26th. The Yugoslav "Kolo" Folk Dance Troupe, led by its director Skovran, arrived in Manzhouli at 6:10 PM today on an international train. Those who welcomed the troupe at the station included Ma Yanxiang, the representative of the Ministry of Culture who came from Beijing to Manzhouli to greet the dance troupe; Zhao Yuting, the Deputy Mayor of Manzhouli City; Zeng Yuanhui, the Director of the Foreign Affairs Office of the People's Committee of the Inner Mongolia Autonomous Region, and others. After accepting flowers, Director Skovran and all members of the troupe attended a luncheon hosted by the Deputy Mayor of Manzhouli. The dance troupe boarded the train at 8:40 PM and departed Manzhouli for Beijing.

### 南斯拉夫“科罗”民间歌舞团过满洲里来京

1955-08-27
第1版()
专栏：

　　南斯拉夫“科罗”民间歌舞团过满洲里来京
    新华社满洲里二十六日电　南斯拉夫“科罗”民间歌舞团在团长斯科夫兰率领下，二十六日下午六时十分乘国际列车到达满洲里。到车站欢迎的有从北京来满洲里迎接歌舞团的文化部代表马彦祥，满洲里市副市长赵玉亭，内蒙古自治区人民委员会外事处长曾远辉等。斯科夫兰团长接受献花以后，同全体团员出席了满洲里副市长的便宴。歌舞团在八时四十分登车离满洲里去北京。
