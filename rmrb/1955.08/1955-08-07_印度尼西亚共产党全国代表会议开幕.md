Title: Indonesian Communist Party National Congress Opens
Author:
Date: 1955-08-07
Page: 4
　
　　Xinhua News Agency, Jakarta, 6th dispatch from TASS: The Indonesian Communist Party National Congress has already opened on the 5th. According to the Secretariat of the Central Committee of the Indonesian Communist Party, the convening of the representative conference is because the general election is about to be held, and it is necessary to prepare for this matter. The representative conference will discuss the resolution of the Political Bureau of the Central Committee of the Indonesian Communist Party on June 24 this year, which pointed out that the general election should promote the formation of a national coalition government.

### 印度尼西亚共产党全国代表会议开幕

1955-08-07
第4版()
专栏：

    印度尼西亚共产党全国代表会议开幕
    新华社六日讯　塔斯社雅加达讯：印度尼西亚共产党全国代表会议已经在五日开幕。据印度尼西亚共产党中央委员会书记处宣布，召开代表会议是因为普选就要举行了，需要对这件事进行准备。代表会议将要讨论印度尼西亚共产党中央委员会政治局今年六月二十四日的决议，这个决议曾指出，普选应当促使民族联合政府的组成。
