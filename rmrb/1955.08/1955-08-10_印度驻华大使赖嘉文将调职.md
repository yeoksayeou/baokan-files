Title: Indian Ambassador to China, R.K. Nehru, to be Transferred
Author: 
Date: 1955-08-10
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, New Delhi, August 9th: According to a news item from the Indian Information Service on August 8th: The current Indian Ambassador to the People's Republic of China, R.K. Nehru, will be transferred to become the Indian Ambassador to Argentina and concurrently the Indian Minister to Chile.

### 印度驻华大使赖嘉文将调职

1955-08-10
第1版()
专栏：

    印度驻华大使赖嘉文将调职
    新华社新德里九日讯　据印度新闻处八日消息：现任印度驻中华人民共和国大使赖嘉文将调任印度驻阿根廷大使兼印度驻智利公使。
