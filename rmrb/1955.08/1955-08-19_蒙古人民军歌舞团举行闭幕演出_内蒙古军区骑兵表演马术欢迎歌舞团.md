Title: Mongolian People's Army Song and Dance Ensemble Holds Closing Performance; Inner Mongolia Military Region Cavalry Performs Equestrian Skills to Welcome Song and Dance Ensemble
Author:
Date: 1955-08-19
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, Hohhot, August 18th: The Mongolian People's Army Song and Dance Ensemble, which was invited to visit and perform in various parts of China for a month and a half, held a grand closing ceremony and its last performance at the Ulanqab Theater (Red Theater) in Hohhot on the evening of the 18th.

Attending the closing ceremony and watching the performance were Li Zhaobing, Deputy Director of the Cultural Department of the General Political Department of the Chinese People's Liberation Army, who came to Hohhot from Beijing, and Ai Linqingsu Nuomu, Second Secretary of the Mongolian Embassy in China, as well as Kong Fei and Liu Huaxiang, Deputy Commanders of the Inner Mongolia Military Region of the Chinese People's Liberation Army, Ting Mao, Deputy Political Commissar, Yang Zhilin and Ha Feng'a, Vice Chairmen of the Inner Mongolia Autonomous Region, Zhang Shuliang, Director of the Cultural Bureau, Ruan Muhhan, Mayor of Hohhot, and 1,300 representatives from Hohhot organs, troops, and literary and art workers.

At the beginning of the closing ceremony, the band played the national anthems of China and Mongolia. Li Zhaobing, Deputy Director of the Cultural Department of the General Political Department of the Chinese People's Liberation Army, spoke at the meeting. First, on behalf of the Government of the People's Republic of China, the Chinese people, and the Chinese People's Liberation Army, he expressed his heartfelt gratitude to the Mongolian People's Army Song and Dance Ensemble for its visit and performance in China. He believed that the Mongolian People's Army Song and Dance Ensemble's visit and performance in China had made a great contribution to further strengthening the friendship between the people and armies of China and Mongolia. Finally, he hoped that the song and dance ensemble would take back the respect and friendship of the Chinese government, people, and army towards the Mongolian people and army.

Major General Damdin Huu, the head of the song and dance ensemble, then spoke. He expressed his sincere gratitude for the warm help received from responsible comrades, literary and art workers, and working people in various places during the song and dance ensemble's visit and performance in China.

Then, Kong Fei presented a banner to the song and dance ensemble on behalf of the Inner Mongolia Military Region of the Chinese People's Liberation Army, and the audience stood up and applauded warmly.

After the closing ceremony, the Mongolian People's Army Song and Dance Ensemble performed twelve song and dance programs. The songs and dances performed tonight, such as "Army Waist Drum," "The Sun That Never Sets Rises on the Prairie," and "Shearing Wool," were newly rehearsed programs during the song and dance ensemble's visit to China. Their wonderful performances received enthusiastic applause and cheers from the audience. The actors took bows repeatedly, and many programs were re-performed again and again. Finally, more than 100 members of the Inner Mongolia Song and Dance Theater and the Inner Mongolia Military Region Cultural Troupe went on stage to present flowers to the Mongolian artists, and the audience applauded and cheered enthusiastically for a long time.

Xinhua News Agency, Hohhot, August 18th: The cavalry unit of the Inner Mongolia Military Region of the Chinese People's Liberation Army held an equestrian performance in Hohhot on the afternoon of the 18th to welcome the Mongolian People's Army Song and Dance Ensemble.

Thirty-six cavalry soldiers performed eight programs, including horsemanship, horseback obstacle crossing, polo, and light riding. Major Tserendorj, artistic director of the Mongolian People's Army Song and Dance Ensemble, said after watching the performance: "After I return to my country, I will compose lyrics for the equestrian performance with superb skills that I saw today and convey them to the Mongolian people."

Accompanying the Mongolian People's Army Song and Dance Ensemble to watch the equestrian performance were: Li Zhaobing, Deputy Director of the Cultural Department of the General Political Department of the Chinese People's Liberation Army, Kong Fei, Deputy Commander of the Inner Mongolia Military Region, and Ha Feng'a, Vice Chairman of the Inner Mongolia Autonomous Region. More than 32,000 people of all ethnic groups in Hohhot also gathered in the square. Ai Linqingsu Nuomu, Second Secretary of the Embassy of the People's Republic of Mongolia in China, also watched the equestrian performance.

### 蒙古人民军歌舞团举行闭幕演出  内蒙古军区骑兵表演马术欢迎歌舞团

1955-08-19
第1版()
专栏：

    蒙古人民军歌舞团举行闭幕演出
    内蒙古军区骑兵表演马术欢迎歌舞团
    新华社呼和浩特十八日电　应邀到中国各地进行了一个半月访问和演出的蒙古人民军歌舞团，十八日晚在呼和浩特的乌兰恰特（红色剧院）举行盛大的闭幕式和最后一次演出。
    出席闭幕式和观看演出的有，从北京前来呼和浩特的中国人民解放军总政治部文化部副部长李兆炳和蒙古驻华大使馆二等秘书艾林清苏诺木，还有中国人民解放军内蒙古军区副司令员孔飞、刘华香，副政治委员廷楙，内蒙古自治区副主席杨植霖、哈丰阿，文化局局长张淑良，呼和浩特市市长阮慕韩和呼和浩特机关部队代表、文艺工作者一千三百人。
    闭幕式开始，乐队高奏中蒙两国国歌。中国人民解放军总政治部文化部副部长李兆炳在会上讲话。他首先代表中华人民共和国政府、中国人民和中国人民解放军，向蒙古人民军歌舞团在中国的访问和演出表示衷心的感谢。他认为蒙古人民军歌舞团，在中国的访问演出，为进一步加强中蒙两国人民及其军队的友谊作了巨大贡献。他最后希望歌舞团把中国政府、人民和军队对蒙古人民及其军队的敬意和友谊带回去。
    歌舞团团长达姆丁呼少将接着讲话。他对歌舞团在中国访问演出期间，受到各地负责同志、文艺工作者以及劳动人民的热情帮助，表示衷心的感谢。
    接着，孔飞代表中国人民解放军内蒙古军区向歌舞团献旗，全场起立热烈鼓掌。
    闭幕式举行后，蒙古人民军歌舞团演出了十二个歌舞节目。今晚演出的“陆军腰鼓”、“草原上升起了不落的太阳”和“剪羊毛”等歌舞，是歌舞团在我国访问期间新排练的节目。他们的精采表演获得观众热烈的掌声和欢呼，演员们再三谢幕，有许多节目一再重演。最后，内蒙古歌舞剧团团员和内蒙古军区文工队队员一百多人，走上舞台向蒙古艺术家们献花，全场热烈的鼓掌和欢呼声经久不息。
    据新华社呼和浩特十八日电　中国人民解放军内蒙古军区骑兵部队，十八日下午在呼和浩特市举行马术表演，欢迎蒙古人民军歌舞团。
    三十六位骑兵战士表演了乘马斩劈、乘马超越障碍、马球和轻乘表演等八个节目。蒙古人民军歌舞团艺术指导策仍道尔吉少校看了表演后说：“我回国后，将把今天看到的具有高超的技艺的马术表演编一支歌词，传达给蒙古人民。”
    陪同蒙古人民军歌舞团观看马术表演的有：中国人民解放军总政治部文化部副部长李兆炳、内蒙古军区副司令员孔飞、内蒙古自治区副主席哈丰阿。广场上还聚集着呼和浩特市三万二千多名各族人民。蒙古人民共和国驻华大使馆二等秘书艾林清苏诺木也观看了这次马术表演。
