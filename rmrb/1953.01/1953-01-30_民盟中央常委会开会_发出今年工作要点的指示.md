Title: The Central Standing Committee of the China Democratic League Holds a Meeting, Issues Instructions on This Year's Work Priorities

Author:
Date: 1953-01-30
Page: 1

    The Central Standing Committee of the China Democratic League Holds a Meeting
    Issues Instructions on This Year's Work Priorities

    [News] The China Democratic League held a meeting of its Central Standing Committee on January 25th. The meeting decided to issue "Instructions on the Key Points of Work for 1953 by the Central Standing Committee of the China Democratic League" to local organizations at all levels throughout the country. The key points of the China Democratic League's work for 1953 are to focus on cooperating in the completion of the three major tasks of the country in 1953 as the central political task, and stipulate the work guidelines of facing culture and education, continuing self-reform, and developing the organization and adjusting the institutions at all levels with emphasis. The work priorities also propose that an enlarged meeting of the Seventh Plenary Session of the Central Committee of the China Democratic League will be held in March and April this year to decide on the current specific guidelines and tasks.

### 民盟中央常委会开会  发出今年工作要点的指示

1953-01-30
第1版()
专栏：

    民盟中央常委会开会
    发出今年工作要点的指示
    【本报讯】中国民主同盟于一月二十五日举行中央常务委员会会议。会议决定向全国各级地方组织发出“中国民主同盟中央常务委员会关于一九五三年工作要点的指示”。中国民主同盟一九五三年工作要点中，确定以配合完成国家一九五三年三大任务为中心政治任务，并规定面向文教、继续进行自我改造以及有重点地发展组织和调整各级机构等项工作方针。工作要点中还提出将在今年三、四月间召开中国民主同盟中央委员会第七次全体会议扩大会议，以决定当前的具体方针和任务。
