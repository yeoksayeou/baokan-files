Title: Envoys from People's Democratic Countries to the Soviet Union, Representatives of Communist and Workers' Parties Visit Lenin's Mausoleum on the 29th Anniversary of Lenin's Death
Author:
Date: 1953-01-24
Page: 4

(Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, 23rd) According to TASS, Moscow: On the 21st, the 29th anniversary of Lenin's death, the diplomatic envoys to the Soviet Union from eleven countries, including the People's Republic of China, the Democratic People's Republic of Korea, the German Democratic Republic, Poland, Czechoslovakia, Hungary, Romania, Bulgaria, Albania, Mongolia, and the Democratic Republic of Vietnam, paid their respects at Lenin's Mausoleum and laid wreaths. On the same day, representatives of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of China, the Central Committee of the Korean Workers' Party, the Central Committee of the Socialist Unity Party of Germany, the Central Committee of the Polish United Workers' Party, the Central Committee of the Romanian Workers' Party, and the Central Committee of the Mongolian People's Revolutionary Party also laid wreaths.

### 人民民主国家驻苏使节、共产党和工人党代表  在列宁逝世二十九周年纪念日谒列宁陵墓

1953-01-24
第4版()
专栏：

    人民民主国家驻苏使节、共产党和工人党代表
    在列宁逝世二十九周年纪念日谒列宁陵墓
    【新华社二十三日讯】据塔斯社莫斯科讯：在二十一日——列宁逝世二十九周年纪念日，中华人民共和国、朝鲜民主主义人民共和国、德意志民主共和国、波兰、捷克斯洛伐克、匈牙利、罗马尼亚、保加利亚、阿尔巴尼亚、蒙古、越南民主共和国等十一国驻苏外交使节敬谒列宁陵墓，并献花圈。同日，中国共产党中央委员会、朝鲜劳动党中央委员会、德国统一社会党中央委员会、波兰统一工人党中央委员会、罗马尼亚工人党中央委员会和蒙古人民革命党中央委员会的代表也去献了花圈。
