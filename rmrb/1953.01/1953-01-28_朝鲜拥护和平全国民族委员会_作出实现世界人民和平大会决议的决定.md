Title: The Korean National Committee for the Defense of Peace Decides to Implement the Resolutions of the World Peace Congress
Author:
Date: 1953-01-28
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Pyongyang, January 27th: The Korean delegation to the World Peace Congress returned to Korea on the 20th. On the 23rd, the Korean National Committee for the Defense of Peace held a meeting to hear the delegation's report and decided to convey the resolutions of the World Peace Congress to the Korean people and implement these resolutions. Han Seol-ya, the head of the Korean delegation, made a statement after returning to the country, praising the great achievements of the World Peace Congress. Han Seol-ya said: The representatives who participated in the World Peace Congress, representing the peaceful will of the people of all countries in the world, unanimously condemned the aggressive atrocities of the so-called "United Nations" army in Korea and demanded the immediate cessation of the war of aggression against Korea. This conference clearly showed that peace-loving people all over the world stand on the side of the Korean people. In every corner of the world, there are true friends, sympathizers and supporters of the Korean people. Han Seol-ya pointed out that the strength of the peace camp is incomparably strong and invincible, and the Korean people standing on the side of the peace camp will surely win.

### 朝鲜拥护和平全国民族委员会  作出实现世界人民和平大会决议的决定

1953-01-28
第4版()
专栏：

    朝鲜拥护和平全国民族委员会
    作出实现世界人民和平大会决议的决定
    【新华社平壤二十七日电】参加世界人民和平大会的朝鲜代表团已在二十日回到朝鲜。二十三日，朝鲜拥护和平全国民族委员会举行会议，听取代表团的报告，并作出了向朝鲜人民传达世界人民和平大会决议并实现这些决议的决定。朝鲜代表团团长韩雪野在回国之后发表谈话，赞扬世界人民和平大会的巨大成就。韩雪野说：参加世界人民和平大会的代表全世界各国人民和平意志的代表们，一致斥责所谓“联合国”军在朝鲜的侵略暴行，并要求立即停止侵朝战争。这次大会很清楚地显示出全世界爱好和平的人民都站在朝鲜人民这一边。在全世界的每一个角落里都有朝鲜人民的真正朋友、同情者和支持者。韩雪野指出：和平阵营的力量是强大无比和不可战胜的，站在和平阵营方面的朝鲜人民必将取得胜利。
