Title: Our Country's Cultural and Artistic Exhibition Hall Warmly Welcomed in Hyderabad, India
Author:
Date: 1953-01-09
Page: 4

    Our Country's Cultural and Artistic Exhibition Hall
    Warmly Welcomed in Hyderabad, India
    (Xinhua News Agency, New Delhi, 8th) At the invitation of the All-India Industrial Exhibition in Hyderabad State, the "Chinese Cultural and Artistic Exhibition Hall" organized by our embassy in India opened at this exhibition on New Year's Day this year. The exhibits included more than 180 pictures of "New China in Peaceful Construction" and famous embroideries, brocades, silks, porcelains, lacquerwares, ivory carvings, New Year pictures, and art works.
    With the assistance of the India-China Friendship Association of Hyderabad, the Chinese Pavilion has become one of the largest, most beautifully decorated, and most attractive pavilions in the exhibition. On the first day of the Chinese Pavilion's opening, there were more than 4,000 visitors, including senior officials of the Hyderabad State government, cultural and art workers, and people from all walks of life. They were very interested in the peaceful construction of New China and praised the Chinese handicrafts on display. After visiting the Chinese Pavilion, visitors left their impressions in the opinion book. One visitor wrote: China's excellent culture and art have left an unforgettable impression on the Indian people. The achievements of New China in construction over the past three years have further encouraged the Indian people. Let us unite and strive to win and defend peace!

### 我国文化艺术展览馆  在印海得拉巴受到热烈欢迎

1953-01-09
第4版()
专栏：

    我国文化艺术展览馆
    在印海得拉巴受到热烈欢迎
    【新华社新德里八日电】我国驻印度大使馆应海得拉巴邦全印工业展览会的邀请在这个展览会内举办的“中国文化艺术展览馆”，在今年元旦开幕。展览品包括“和平建设中的新中国”图片一百八十多幅和著名的刺绣、织锦、丝绸、瓷器、漆器、象牙雕刻、年画和美术作品等。
    中国馆在海得拉巴印中友好协会协助筹备下，成为展览会中规模宏大、布置美丽，最能吸引观众的一个展览馆。参观中国馆的第一天就有四千多人，其中包括海得拉巴邦政府高级官员、文化艺术工作者及各阶层人民。他们对新中国的和平建设事业感到很大兴趣。对展出的中国工艺品都很赞扬。观众参观中国馆后纷纷在意见簿上留下他们的感想。一位参观者写道：中国的优秀文化艺术给印度人民留下了难忘的印象。新中国三年来在建设上的成就，更鼓舞了印度人民。让我们团结起来为争取和保卫和平而奋斗罢！
