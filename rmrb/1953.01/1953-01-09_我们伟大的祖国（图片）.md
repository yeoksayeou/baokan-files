    Our Great Motherland
    Harbin Institute of Technology, based on the Soviet experience, has adopted a teaching system of production practice. This is the mechanical manufacturing engineering specialty group of the Department of Mechanical Engineering. Postgraduate students are doing a demonstration practice of high-speed cutting method on a planer.
