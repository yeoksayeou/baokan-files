Title: Northeast Administrative Committee Formally Established
Author: 
Date: 1953-01-25
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, Shenyang, January 24th: The Northeast Administrative Committee was formally established on the 23rd. The first plenary session of the committee was held on the same day. Lin Feng, Vice Chairman of the Northeast Administrative Committee, gave a detailed explanation at the meeting regarding the tasks and working methods of the Northeast Administrative Committee, as well as the work for the entire year of 1953 and the first quarter. He pointed out that the task of the Administrative Committee is to represent the Central People's Government in leading, supervising, and inspecting all work departments of local governments and central government agencies directly under its jurisdiction in the area, to ensure the implementation of the central government's policies and the realization of national plans. The method to accomplish this task is to regularly organize forces to conduct in-depth inspections of various tasks in a planned, organized, and led manner, to discover and solve problems in a timely manner, and to report to the central government in a timely manner. After explaining the work tasks for the entire year of 1953 and the first quarter, Lin Feng emphasized that the Northeast is located on the frontline of national defense and is also an important region for the country's first Five-Year Plan construction, with heavy tasks and great responsibilities. Therefore, cadres at all levels must study diligently, lead the people of the entire region, and conscientiously do a good job in the three major central tasks this year. Li Zhuoran and Wang Jinxiang, Vice Chairmen of the Northeast Administrative Committee, and members Che Xiangchen, Chen Xianzhu, He Jinnian, Han Guang, and Jin Shuliang, spoke successively at the meeting. They unanimously expressed their determination to contribute to the completion of various major tasks.

### 东北行政委员会正式成立

1953-01-25
第1版()
专栏：

    东北行政委员会正式成立
    【新华社沈阳二十四日电】东北行政委员会二十三日正式成立。同日举行第一次全体委员会。东北行政委员会副主席林枫在会上对东北行政委员会的任务、工作方法以及一九五三年全年和第一季度的工作作了详细说明。他指出，行政委员会的任务是代表中央人民政府对地方政府一切工作部门和中央在当地的直属机构进行领导、督促和检查，保证中央各项政策的执行和国家计划的实现。完成这一任务的方法，是要经常组织力量，有计划、有组织、有领导地对各项工作进行深入检查，及时发现问题，解决问题，并及时报告中央。林枫在说明一九五三年全年和第一季度的工作任务后，着重指出，东北处在国防前线，同时又是国家第一个五年计划建设的重要地区，任务繁重，责任重大。因此，各级干部必须努力学习，领导全区人民，兢兢业业做好今年的三大中心工作。东北行政委员会副主席李卓然、汪金祥，委员车向忱、陈先舟、贺晋年、韩光、靳树梁等在会上相继发言，他们一致表示要为完成各项重大任务而贡献力量。
