Title: The Czechoslovakian "Red Right Newspaper" on China's Implementation of the Five-Year Plan: Points out that this is a Major Event in the Entire International Life

Author: 
Date: 1953-01-14
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Prague, January 13th: The Czechoslovakian "Red Right Newspaper" published an editorial on the 12th, stating that China's commencement of its first Five-Year Plan is not only a major event for the Chinese people in terms of cultural and economic development, but also a major event in the entire international life and the strengthening of the camp of peace and democracy.

The editorial summarized the amazing achievements made by the Chinese working people in the three years after liberation and praised the "enthusiastic builders of New China who are constantly supported by the world's first socialist country—the Soviet Union." The editorial pointed out the assistance given by the Soviet Union to China in the economic, trade, scientific, and cultural fields and emphasized that "the unbreakable friendship and alliance between China and the Soviet Union has become a powerful force that greatly helps the People's China develop its economy and culture. This friendship and alliance is also a solid fortress of peace throughout the world." The editorial pointed out that "the implementation of the first Five-Year Plan proves that the Chinese people are not afraid of the threats of imperialism, and proves the great strength and love of peace of the Chinese people."

### 捷“红色权利报”论我国开始执行五年计划  指出这是整个国际生活中的一个重大事件

1953-01-14
第4版()
专栏：

    捷“红色权利报”论我国开始执行五年计划
    指出这是整个国际生活中的一个重大事件
    【新华社布拉格十三日电】捷克斯洛伐克“红色权利报”十二日发表专论说，中国开始执行第一个五年计划不仅是中国人民在文化和经济发展方面的重大事件，而且是整个国际生活和加强和平民主阵营方面的一个重大事件。
    专论综述了中国劳动人民在解放后三年中所取得的惊人成就，并赞扬“不断得到世界上第一个社会主义国家——苏联支持的热情洋溢的新中国建设者”。专论指出了苏联在经济、贸易、科学和文化各方面所给予中国的援助，并且强调说：“中苏两国牢不可破的友谊和同盟，已成为一种大力帮助人民中国发展它的经济和文化的强大力量。这种友谊和同盟又是全世界坚固的和平堡垒。”专论指出，“第一个五年计划的实施，证明中国人民毫不害怕帝国主义的威胁，证明中国人民的巨大力量和对和平的热爱。”
