Title: People in Northeast China, East China, and Wuhan and other places launch activities to support the military and give preferential treatment to families of revolutionary soldiers and martyrs.
Author:
Date: 1953-01-05
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, 4th: People in Northeast China, East China, Wuhan and other places have launched New Year activities to support the army and give preferential treatment to families of revolutionary soldiers and martyrs. The Northeast Branch of the China Anti-U.S. and Aid Korea Association organized a New Year's comfort mission composed of people from all walks of life in Northeast China, and set off in three routes to visit the wounded and sick of the Chinese People's Volunteers recuperating in Northeast China. The delegation carried a large number of books, periodicals, and letters of comfort from people from all walks of life in Northeast China to the wounded and sick of the Chinese People's Volunteers. The delegation included five performing arts troupes and nine film projection teams, which prepared many wonderful programs and films for New Year's comfort performances for the wounded and sick. The comfort delegations organized by the East China Branch of the China Anti-U.S. and Aid Korea Association and the Shanghai Branch of the China Anti-U.S. and Aid Korea Association paid New Year's visits to the wounded and sick of the Chinese People's Liberation Army and the Chinese People's Volunteers recuperating in Shanghai. After reading out the New Year's letter of comfort to the wounded and sick from the East China Administrative Committee and the Shanghai Municipal People's Government in the hospital, representatives of the delegation visited the wards separately. The East China Branch of the China Anti-U.S. and Aid Korea Association and the Shanghai Branch of the China Anti-U.S. and Aid Korea Association also wrote letters to all commanders and fighters of the Chinese People's Volunteers fighting on the Korean front to congratulate them on the New Year. The East China Administrative Committee and the Shanghai Municipal People's Government also wrote letters to express their condolences to revolutionary disabled soldiers, families of revolutionary martyrs, and families of revolutionary soldiers. Residents in various districts and alleys in Shanghai also spontaneously organized New Year's visit teams to express their condolences to the families of martyrs and military personnel during the New Year. On the eve of the New Year, people from all walks of life in Wuhan presented their beloved souvenirs engraved with Chairman Mao's portrait and peace doves, New Year cards, bookmarks and other gifts to the Volunteers. Many people wrote letters to the Volunteers, reporting on their progress in work and study over the past year and their determination to continue to support the Volunteers. People from all walks of life in Wuhan also formed delegations during the New Year to express their condolences to revolutionary disabled soldiers and the wounded and sick.

### 东北华东及武汉市等地人民  展开拥军优属活动

1953-01-05
第1版()
专栏：

    东北华东及武汉市等地人民
    展开拥军优属活动
    【新华社四日讯】东北区、华东区、武汉市等地人民展开新年拥军优属活动。东北抗美援朝总分会在新年前夕组织了东北各界人民慰问团，分三路出发慰问在东北区休养的中国人民志愿军伤病员。慰问团携带了东北各界人民献给中国人民志愿军伤病员的大批书刊和慰问信。慰问团里有五个文艺演出队和九个电影放映队，准备了许多精彩的节目和影片为伤病员作新年慰问演出。华东抗美援朝总分会和上海市抗美援朝分会组织的慰问团，在新年慰问了在上海休养的中国人民解放军和中国人民志愿军的伤病员。慰问团代表在医院里向伤病员宣读了华东行政委员会、上海市人民政府新年致伤病员的慰问信之后，分别深入病房进行慰问。华东抗美援朝总分会和上海市抗美援朝分会还写信给战斗在朝鲜前线的中国人民志愿军全体指挥员、战斗员祝贺新年。华东行政委员会、上海市人民政府并写信向革命残废军人、革命烈士家属和革命军人家属表示慰问。上海市各区里弄居民在新年也自动组织拜年队慰问烈属和军属。武汉市各界人民在新年前夕纷纷把自己心爱的镌有毛主席像和和平鸽的纪念章、贺年片、书签等礼物送给志愿军。很多人写信给志愿军，报告自己一年来在工作上、学习上的进步和继续支援志愿军的决心。武汉市各界人民并在新年组成代表团，向革命残废军人和伤病员进行慰问。
