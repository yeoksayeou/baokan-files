Author:
Date: 1953-01-04

Protecting the country's forest resources is of great importance to basic construction. This is the forest area airport just before the mountains are closed by heavy snow. People are clearing snow to allow the forestry aircraft to make the last winter patrol flight over the great forest.

### 我们伟大的祖国(图片）

1953-01-04
第1版()
专栏：

    我们伟大的祖国
    保护国家的森林资源，对于基本建设有重要作用。这是在大雪即将封山以前的林区机场。人们正在打扫积雪，好让护林飞机在大森林上空作冬季的最后一次巡护飞行。
