Title: Northwest Administrative Committee Formally Established
Author:
Date: 1953-01-29
Page: 1

    Northwest Administrative Committee Formally Established
    [Xinhua News Agency, Xi'an, 28th] The Northwest Administrative Committee was formally established on January 27th. The original Northwest Military and Political Committee was simultaneously announced as dissolved. On the same day, the Northwest Administrative Committee held its first administrative meeting. The meeting discussed issues such as a portion of the organizational structure of the Northwest Administrative Committee and the allocation of cadres.

### 西北行政委员会正式成立

1953-01-29
第1版()
专栏：

    西北行政委员会正式成立
    【新华社西安二十八日电】西北行政委员会在一月二十七日正式成立。原西北军政委员会同时宣布撤销。同日，西北行政委员会举行第一次行政会议。会议讨论了西北行政委员会一部分组织机构及干部调配等问题。
