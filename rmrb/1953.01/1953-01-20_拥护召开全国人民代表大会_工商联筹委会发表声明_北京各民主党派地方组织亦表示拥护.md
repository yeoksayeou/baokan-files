Title: Support the Convening of the National People's Congress: Preparatory Committee of the All-China Federation of Industry and Commerce Issues Statement; Local Organizations of Democratic Parties in Beijing Also Express Support
Author:
Date: 1953-01-20
Page: 1

The Preparatory Committee of the All-China Federation of Industry and Commerce Issues Statement
Local Organizations of Democratic Parties in Beijing Also Express Support
【This newspaper's report】The Preparatory Committee of the All-China Federation of Industry and Commerce issued a statement on the 19th expressing enthusiastic support for the convening of the National and local People's Congresses at all levels. The full text of the statement is as follows:

The 20th meeting of the Central People's Government Committee passed a resolution to implement universal suffrage in 1953 and convene National and local People's Congresses at all levels. We, on behalf of the national industrialists and businessmen, express our warm support. This resolution is of historical significance. It marks that our great motherland is about to enter a new historical stage. At the same time, this will further promote and consolidate the superiority of our country's people's democratic regime. Now that large-scale national construction is beginning, our national industrialists and businessmen will shoulder important responsibilities in the future process of building the motherland. While cheering and supporting this great decision of the Central People's Government, we must mobilize all industrialists and businessmen to study and publicize this resolution, and under the leadership of the Central People's Government, ensure that all preparations are made for the convening of the National and local People's Congresses at all levels, and strive to further consolidate the people's democratic dictatorship, continue to strengthen the victory of resisting U.S. aggression and aiding Korea, and building the motherland.

Xinhua News Agency, 18th: The local organizations of the democratic parties in Beijing jointly wrote a letter to Chairman Mao, supporting the convening of the National and local People's Congresses at all levels. The letter stated: "This marks that our great motherland is about to enter a new historical period. The people of the whole country will further give play to the initiative and creativity of being masters of their own affairs, so that we can achieve greater victories in large-scale national construction and continue to strengthen the struggle to resist U.S. aggression and aid Korea, thereby laying a solid foundation for national industrialization. It will also give a brilliant example to the people of all countries in the world who are striving for democracy, freedom, and national independence. All members of the democratic parties in Beijing are unanimously rejoicing and encouraging this correct, wise, and timely great decision, and guarantee to contribute all their strength together with the people of the whole country to complete this glorious political task."

### 拥护召开全国人民代表大会  工商联筹委会发表声明  北京各民主党派地方组织亦表示拥护

1953-01-20
第1版()
专栏：

    拥护召开全国人民代表大会
    工商联筹委会发表声明
    北京各民主党派地方组织亦表示拥护
    【本报讯】中华全国工商业联合会筹备委员会于十九日发表关于热烈拥护召开全国及地方各级人民代表大会的声明。声明全文如下：
    中央人民政府委员会第二十次会议，通过了在一九五三年实行普选，召开全国及地方各级人民代表大会的决议，我们谨代表全国工商业者表示热烈的拥护。这个决议，是富有历史意义的。它标志着我们伟大的祖国即将进入一个新的历史阶段。同时这将更加发扬与巩固我国人民民主政权的优越性。现在，国家大规模建设正在开始，我们全国工商业者在今后建设祖国的进程中更将担负起重要的责任。我们在欢呼与拥护中央人民政府这一伟大的决定下，一定动员全体工商业者学习和宣传这一决议，在中央人民政府领导下，保证作好召开全国及地方各级人民代表大会的一切准备工作，为进一步巩固人民民主专政，继续加强抗美援朝和建设祖国的胜利而努力。
    【新华社十八日讯】北京市各民主党派地方组织联名写信给毛主席，拥护召开全国和地方各级人民代表大会。信内说：“这标志着我们伟大祖国即将进入一个新的历史时期。全国人民将更进一步发挥主人翁的积极性和创造性，使我们在大规模国家建设和继续加强抗美援朝的斗争中取得更伟大的胜利，从而为国家工业化奠定坚强的基础。并将给予全世界争取民主自由和争取民族独立的各国人民一个光辉的范例。我们北京市全体民主党派成员，一致为这个正确的、英明的、及时的伟大决策而欢欣鼓舞，并保证和全国人民一道为完成这一光荣的政治任务而贡献出一切力量。”
