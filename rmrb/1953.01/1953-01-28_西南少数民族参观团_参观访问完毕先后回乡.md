Title: Southwest Minority Nationalities Visiting Delegation Concludes Visit and Returns Home
Author:
Date: 1953-01-28
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, Chongqing, January 27th: The Southwest Minority Nationalities Visiting Delegation has victoriously concluded its visit throughout the country. The representatives, led by the head of the delegation, Songmou (Tibetan nationality), departed from Chongqing on November 10th of last year, and visited Beijing, Nanjing, Hangzhou, Shanghai, Tianjin, and other places. They also participated in the New Year's Day group worship held by the Central People's Government in Beijing. During the visit, the representatives personally experienced the greatness and warmth of the motherland. They expressed that they would do a good job after returning, and build their hometowns as beautiful as the interior. The representatives already returned to Southwest via the Yangtze River and Yuehan Railway from Hankou in the middle of January. The representatives from Xikang and Sichuan provinces recently passed through Chongqing on their way home.

### 西南少数民族参观团  参观访问完毕先后回乡

1953-01-28
第1版()
专栏：

    西南少数民族参观团
    参观访问完毕先后回乡
    【新华社重庆二十七日电】西南少数民族参观团已胜利结束在全国各地的参观访问。代表们已在一月中旬先后由汉口分别取道长江和粤汉铁路返回西南各地。西康、四川两省的代表已于最近路过重庆回乡。该团于去年十一月十日在团长松谋（藏族）率领下由重庆出发，先后参观了北京、南京、杭州、上海、天津等地。在北京时并参加了中央人民政府所举行的元旦团拜。代表们在参观访问中，亲身体验到祖国的伟大和温暖，他们表示回去后要做好工作，把自己的家乡建设得和内地一样美好。
