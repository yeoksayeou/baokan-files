Title: Exchange of Instruments of Ratification for Sino-Mongolian Economic and Cultural Cooperation Agreement; Ceremony for the Exchange of Instruments of Ratification Held in Ulaanbaatar, Capital of Mongolia
Author:
Date: 1953-01-03
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Ulaanbaatar, 2nd: The ceremony for the exchange of instruments of ratification for the Sino-Mongolian Economic and Cultural Cooperation Agreement was held in Ulaanbaatar, capital of the Mongolian People's Republic, on December 29, 1952. The Chinese side participating in the ceremony for the exchange of instruments of ratification included: Ji Yatai, Ambassador of China to the Mongolian People's Republic, and embassy staff. The Mongolian side included: Ramsuren, Deputy Prime Minister and Minister of Foreign Affairs of the Mongolian People's Republic; Lubsan, Deputy Prime Minister and Minister of Trade; Jamtsarano, Deputy Minister of Education; Purbuzal, Secretary-General of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs; Sanji Mituob, Director of the Department of Oriental Affairs; and Ochirbatu, Director of the Department of Protocol.

During the signing ceremony, Ramsuren and Ji Yatai delivered speeches successively. Finally, toasts were raised to the health of Chairman Mao Zedong, the health of Prime Minister Tsedenbal, and the fraternal friendship between the people of China and Mongolia.

### 我与蒙古互换经济及文化合作协定批准书  互换批准书的仪式已在蒙古首都乌兰巴托举行

1953-01-03
第1版()
专栏：

    我与蒙古互换经济及文化合作协定批准书
    互换批准书的仪式已在蒙古首都乌兰巴托举行
    【新华社二日讯】乌兰巴托消息：中蒙经济及文化合作协定互换批准书的仪式于一九五二年十二月二十九日在蒙古人民共和国首都乌兰巴托举行。参加互换批准书仪式的，中国方面有：中国驻蒙古人民共和国大使吉雅泰以及使馆人员；蒙古方面有：蒙古人民共和国副总理兼外交部长拉姆苏伦、副总理兼贸易部长鲁布桑、教育部副部长札木苏荣、外交部总秘书长普尔布扎勒、东方司司长桑几米托布、交际司司长奥齐尔巴图。
    在签字仪式中，拉姆苏伦和吉雅泰相继讲话；最后，并举杯为毛泽东主席的健康、泽登巴尔总理的健康、中蒙两国人民兄弟般的友谊而干杯。
