Title: The Brazilian Government Persecutes Representatives Attending the World People's Peace Congress
Author:
Date: 1953-01-15
Page: 4

International Briefs

    The Brazilian Government Persecutes Representatives Attending the World People's Peace Congress
    According to the Brazilian newspaper "People's Daily," the Brazilian police, at the behest of the United States, arrested two Brazilian representatives to the World People's Peace Congress, Olímpio Bondezan and Emílio Piriça, at Galeão Airport. Lackeys always imitate their masters.

### 巴西政府迫害出席世界人民和平大会代表

1953-01-15
第4版()
专栏：国际简讯

    巴西政府迫害出席世界人民和平大会代表
    据巴西的“人民报”报道，巴西警察奉美国之命，在加列奥机场逮捕巴西两个出席世界人民和平大会的代表：奥利姆皮奥·邦戴萨与爱米利奥·皮里沙。奴才总是效法自己的主子的。
