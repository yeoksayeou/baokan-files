Title: Chinese Delegation to World Peace Congress Returns to Beijing; Australian and Burmese Delegations Arrive in China for Visits
Author:
Date: 1953-01-18
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, 17th: Guo Moruo, Vice-Chairman of the Chinese delegation to the World Peace Congress, Chairman of the China Peace Committee, and Chinese delegates Meng Yongqian, Qian Sanqiang, Dong Yueqian and his party of four, returned to Beijing by plane on the afternoon of the 17th. They were welcomed at the airport by Peng Zhen and Chen Shutong, Vice-Chairmen of the China Peace Committee, Liu Guanyi, Secretary-General, Li Siguang, Zhang Hanfu, Luo Longji, Xu Baoju, Yu Xinqing, Zhang Jiafu, Shakov, Jin Cheng, Standing Committee members of the China Peace Committee, and representatives from various people's organizations and circles, and more than 60 Chinese delegates to the World Peace Congress who had arrived in Beijing earlier.
    Xinhua News Agency, 17th: A group of forty-four Chinese delegates and staff members attending the World Peace Congress, led by Liu Guanyi and Wu Maosun, Deputy Secretaries-General of the delegation, returned to Beijing by train on the morning of the 17th. Chinese delegates Ma Yinchu, Zhang Bojun, Cai Tingkai, Liao Chengzhi, Wu Yaozong, and Chen Shutong, among others, eighteen in total, had returned to Beijing by plane in the previous days.
    Arriving on the same train were: An Australian delegation of fourteen led by Alan Douglas Brand, and a Burmese delegation of fourteen led by Deqin Chi Mao, who came to China for visits after attending the World Peace Congress.
    Also arriving on the same train were several representatives from Vietnam, Cambodia, and Laos, who were returning to their countries via China after attending the World Peace Congress.
    Welcoming them at the station were Chen Shutong, Vice-Chairman of the China Peace Committee, Cui Yueli, Deputy Secretary-General, Zhang Xiuzhu, Standing Committee member of the China Peace Committee and Deputy Director of the Culture and Education Department of the All-China Federation of Trade Unions, Jin Ziming, Deputy Director of the International Liaison Department, Cao Mengjun, Deputy Secretary-General of the All-China Democratic Women's Federation, Shakov, Secretary-General of the All-China Federation of Literary and Art Circles, as well as representatives from various circles and Chinese delegates to the World Peace Congress who had returned to Beijing earlier, totaling over two hundred people.

### 我国出席世界人民和平大会代表团  郭沫若副团长等返抵北京  澳大利亚和缅甸代表团前来我国参观

1953-01-18
第1版()
专栏：

    我国出席世界人民和平大会代表团
    郭沫若副团长等返抵北京
    澳大利亚和缅甸代表团前来我国参观
    【新华社十七日讯】　出席世界人民和平大会的中国代表团副团长、中国人民保卫世界和平委员会主席郭沫若和中国代表孟用潜、钱三强、董越千等一行四人，在十七日下午乘飞机回到北京。到机场欢迎的有：中国人民保卫世界和平委员会副主席彭真、陈叔通，秘书长刘贯一，中国人民保卫世界和平委员会常务委员、各人民团体和各界代表李四光、章汉夫、罗隆基、许宝驹、余心清、张稼夫、沙可夫、金城以及先行到达北京的出席世界人民和平大会的中国代表等六十多人。
    【新华社十七日讯】　出席世界人民和平大会的部分中国代表和工作人员等一行四十四人，在代表团副秘书长刘贯一、吴茂荪率领下，在十七日上午乘火车回到北京。中国代表马寅初、章伯钧、蔡廷锴、廖承志、吴耀宗、陈叔通等十八人已在日前陆续乘飞机回到北京。
    同车到达的有出席世界人民和平大会后前来我国参观访问的：以阿兰·道格拉斯·布兰德为首的澳大利亚代表团一行十四人，以德钦漆貌为首的缅甸代表团一行十四人。
    同车到达的还有出席世界人民和平大会后途经我国返国的越南、高棉、寮国等国的代表多人。
    前往车站欢迎的有：中国人民保卫世界和平委员会副主席陈叔通，副秘书长崔月犁和中国人民保卫世界和平委员会常务委员、中华全国总工会文教部副部长张修竹、国际联络部副部长金子明，中华全国民主妇女联合会副秘书长曹孟君，中华全国文学艺术界联合会秘书长沙可夫，以及各界代表和先行回到北京的出席世界人民和平大会的中国代表共二百余人。
