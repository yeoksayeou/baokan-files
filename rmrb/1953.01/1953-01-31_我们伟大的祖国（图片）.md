    Our Great Motherland
    In order to completely eradicate the Yellow River's water disasters and develop the Yellow River basin's resources, the Yellow River Water Conservancy Commission and the Ministry of Fuel Industry organized the Yellow River Source Exploration Team to thoroughly investigate the Yellow River's source in the Qinghai Province grassland area. They discovered some past errors in the geographical situation of this area. This is the scene of the exploration team members measuring the main peak of the Labze North-South Pass, 4,900 meters above sea level.

### 我们伟大的祖国（图片）

1953-01-31
第1版()
专栏：

    我们伟大的祖国
    为了彻底根绝黄河水患和开发黄河流域的资源，黄河水利委员会和中央燃料工业部组织的黄河河源勘查队，深入青海省草原地带查勘了黄河河源的情况，发现了过去人们对这一带地理形势上的若干错误。这是勘查队员在测量拔海四千九百公尺的拉不泽南北山口主峰的情形。
