Title: Sino-Burmese Friendship Association and Burmese-Chinese Friendship Association Exchange Cables to Celebrate Burmese-Chinese Friendship Week
Author:
Date: 1953-01-31
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency, Rangoon, January 30th: On the occasion of Burmese-Chinese Friendship Week, the Burmese-Chinese Friendship Association held a press conference in Rangoon. U Htin Pe Myint, Vice Chairman of the Burmese-Chinese Friendship Association, read out a telegram from Zheng Zhenduo, President of the Sino-Burmese Friendship Association, congratulating Burmese-Chinese Friendship Week. The telegram said: "This Burmese-Chinese Friendship Week will undoubtedly greatly enhance the friendship and mutual understanding between the people of China and Burma, and will contribute to the peace movement in Asia and the Pacific region." U Htin Pe Myint also made a statement to the press. He pointed out that in order to promote and consolidate the friendship between the people of China and Burma, it is necessary to strengthen cultural and economic exchanges between the two countries.

Rangoon musicians held a symposium during Burmese-Chinese Friendship Week. The Burmese musicians present unanimously pointed out that the music of New China serves the people and is completely different from the pornographic music of capitalist countries. They believe that the Burmese music community should attach importance to the study of Chinese music and strengthen music exchanges between China and Burma.

Xinhua News Agency, Rangoon, January 30th: To celebrate Burmese-Chinese Friendship Week, Dr. U Htin Pe, Chairman of the Burmese-Chinese Friendship Association, wrote a letter to Zheng Zhenduo, President of the Sino-Burmese Friendship Association in Beijing. In his letter, Dr. U Htin Pe first expressed the deep friendship of the Burmese people towards the Chinese people. He then wrote: This is the first time we have held Burmese-Chinese Friendship Week in this way. Our purpose, as indicated by the name of our association, is for the sincere friendship between the people of Burma and China. A year of experience has told us that cultural exchanges and friendship between our two countries are strengthening day by day; this is because our purpose is just and sincere, and our path is correct.

Dr. U Htin Pe concluded: Let us be determined to make the greatest efforts for the cultural and economic exchanges between our two countries, and cheer long live the friendly future of the Burmese and Chinese people!

### 中缅友协和缅中友协互电庆祝缅中友好周

1953-01-31
第4版()
专栏：

    中缅友协和缅中友协互电庆祝缅中友好周
    【新华社三十日讯】仰光消息：在缅中友好周举行之际，缅中友好协会在仰光举行记者招待会。缅中友好协会副主席吴登佩密在会上宣读了中缅友好协会会长郑振铎祝贺缅中友好周的电报。贺电说：“这个缅中友好周无疑地将会大大地增进中缅两国人民之间的友谊与互相了解，并将有助于亚洲及太平洋区域的和平运动。”吴登佩密并向报界发表谈话。他指出：为了促进和巩固中缅两国人民的友谊，需要加强两国间的文化和经济的交流。
    仰光音乐界人士在缅中友好周举行了座谈会。到会的缅甸音乐家一致指出：新中国的音乐是为人民服务的，和资本主义国家的色情音乐截然不同。他们认为，缅甸音乐界应该重视对中国音乐的研究和加强中缅两国间音乐的交流。
    【新华社三十日讯】仰光消息：为庆祝缅中友好周的举行，缅中友好协会主席宇信波博士写信给北京中缅友好协会会长郑振铎。宇信波博士在信中首先表示了缅甸人民对中国人民的深厚友谊。他接着写道：我们这样举行缅中友好周还是第一次。我们的目的正如我们协会的名字所指的，是为了缅中两国人民的诚挚友谊。一年的经验告诉了我们：我们两国间的文化交流和友谊正在一天一天地加强；这是由于我们的目的是正义的、诚挚的，我们的道路是正确的。
     宇信波博士最后写道：让我们决心为我们两国文化交流和经济交流尽最大的努力，为缅中人民的友好前途欢呼万岁！
