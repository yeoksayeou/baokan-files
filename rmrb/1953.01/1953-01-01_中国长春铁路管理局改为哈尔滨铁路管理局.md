Title: China Changchun Railway Bureau Reorganized as Harbin Railway Bureau
Author:
Date: 1953-01-01
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Harbin, December 31st: After the transfer of the China Changchun Railway, the Ministry of Railways of the Central People's Government reorganized the original China Changchun Railway Bureau into the "Harbin Railway Bureau" at 18:00 on December 31, 1952, directly under the leadership of the Ministry of Railways. The jurisdiction of the Harbin Railway Bureau includes the original jurisdiction of the China Changchun Railway, as well as the jurisdiction of the original Andong Railway Sub-bureau directly under the Ministry of Railways of the Central People's Government, and individual stations adjusted from neighboring bureaus. The main lines are: Manzhouli to Harbin line, Harbin to Suifenhe line, Harbin to Dalian line, Shenyang to Andong line, Xiachengzi to Lishuzhen line, Harbin to Wuchang line, and Hunhe to Fushun line. The branch lines are: Taolaizhao to Yushu line, Dashiqiao to Yingkou line, Jinzhou to Chengzituan line, Nanguanling to Ganjingzi line, Zhou Shuizi to Lushunkou line, Dafangshen to Liushutun line, Yantai to Yantai Mine line, Liaoyang to Benxi line, Benxi to Tianshifu line, Fenghuangcheng to Kuandian line, and Guanshui to Saimaji line. The Harbin Railway Bureau has seven sub-bureaus: Manzhouli, Harbin, Mudanjiang, Changchun, Shenyang, Dalian, and Andong. Lu Ping is the director of the Harbin Railway Bureau, and Guo Lu, Zhuang Lin, Liu Yingcai, and Wang Guangwen are the deputy directors.

### 中国长春铁路管理局改为哈尔滨铁路管理局

1953-01-01
第1版()
专栏：

    中国长春铁路管理局改为哈尔滨铁路管理局
    【新华社哈尔滨三十一日电】中长铁路移交后，我中央人民政府铁道部已于一九五二年十二月三十一日十八时把原中国长春铁路管理局改为“哈尔滨铁路管理局”，由铁道部直接领导。哈尔滨铁路管理局的管辖范围，除原中长路的管辖范围外，还包括原由中央人民政府铁道部直接领导的安东铁路分局的管辖范围以及由邻局调整过来的个别车站等。其干线为：满洲里至哈尔滨线、哈尔滨至绥芬河线、哈尔滨至大连线、沈阳至安东线、下城子至梨树镇线、哈尔滨至五常线、浑河至抚顺线。其支线为：陶赖昭至榆树线、大石桥至营口线、金州至城子疃线、南关岭至甘井子线、周水子至旅顺口线、大房身至柳树屯线、烟台至烟台矿线、辽阳至本溪线、本溪至田师付线、凤凰城至宽甸线、灌水至赛马集线。哈尔滨铁路管理局下设满洲里、哈尔滨、牡丹江、长春、沈阳、大连、安东等七个分局。哈尔滨铁路管理局由陆平任局长，由郭鲁、庄林、刘英才、王光文任副局长。
