On January 5th, the award ceremony for the Stalin International Peace Prize for "Strengthening International Peace" was held in the Sverdlov Hall of the Kremlin, Moscow. Saifuddin Kitchlew, Chairman of the All-India Peace Council, received the "Strengthening International Peace" award. This is Academician Skobeltsyn, Chairman of the Stalin International Peace Prize for "Strengthening International Peace" Committee and member of the Soviet Academy of Sciences, awarding Dr. Kitchlew the medal and certificate of the Stalin International Peace Prize for "Strengthening International Peace." (Xinhua News Agency draft, TASS radioteletype)
