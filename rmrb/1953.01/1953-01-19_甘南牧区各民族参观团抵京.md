Title: Gansu Southern Pasture Area Ethnic Groups Visiting Delegation Arrives in Beijing
Author: 
Date: 1953-01-19
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, January 17th—The Gansu Southern Pasture Area Ethnic Groups Visiting Delegation, a group of fifty-eight people, led by the head of the delegation, Daji (Tibetan), and deputy heads Yang Shenghua (Tibetan) and Yang Shiheng (Hui), arrived in Beijing on the 16th. Among those who went to the station to welcome them were Gan Chunlei and A'aisha, deputy directors of the General Office of the Nationalities Affairs Commission of the Central People's Government, Lian Huizhu, deputy director of the Panchen's Beijing Office, and more than 30 cadres from the Nationalities Affairs Commission.
    The Gansu Southern Pasture Area Ethnic Groups Visiting Delegation includes representatives of the Tibetan, Hui, and Han ethnic groups from Xiahe County, Lintan County, Zhuoni Autonomous Region, and Xigu County Wuping Autonomous Region in southern Gansu.

### 甘南牧区各民族参观团抵京

1953-01-19
第1版()
专栏：

    甘南牧区各民族参观团抵京
    【新华社十七日讯】甘肃南部牧区各民族参观团一行五十八人，在团长达吉（藏族），副团长杨生华（藏族）、杨世珩（回族）率领下，于十六日到达北京。前往车站欢迎的有中央人民政府民族事务委员会办公厅副主任甘春雷、阿艾沙，班禅驻京办事处副处长联慧珠及民族事务委员会干部等三十多人。
    甘肃南部牧区各民族参观团包括甘肃南部的夏河县、临潭县、卓尼自治区和西固县武坪自治区的藏、回、汉三个民族的代表。
