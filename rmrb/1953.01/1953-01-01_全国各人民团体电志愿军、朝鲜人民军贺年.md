Title: National People's Organizations Send New Year's Greetings to the Volunteer Army and the Korean People's Army
Author:
Date: 1953-01-01
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency Report: National people's organizations sent telegrams to the Chinese People's Volunteer Army on the 30th, congratulating them on the New Year of 1953. The organizations sending congratulatory telegrams to the Chinese People's Volunteer Army were: All-China Federation of Trade Unions, All-China Democratic Women's Federation, Chinese People's National Committee for the Defense of Children, All-China Federation of Democratic Youth, All-China Federation of Literary and Art Circles, All- China Students’ Federation, All-China Federation of Specialized Societies of Natural Sciences, All-China Association for Science and Technology Popularization, Sino-Soviet Friendship Association, and China People's Relief Association. The congratulatory telegrams unanimously stated that they would continue to strengthen the work of resisting U.S. aggression and aiding Korea, and strive to defend peace in the Far East and the world.

Xinhua News Agency Report: National people's organizations sent telegrams to General Kim Il-sung and the Korean People's Army, congratulating them on the New Year of 1953. The organizations sending congratulatory telegrams to General Kim Il-sung and the Korean People's Army were: All-China Federation of Trade Unions, All-China Democratic Women's Federation, Chinese People's National Committee for the Defense of Children , All-China Federation of Democratic Youth, All-China Federation of Literary and Art Circles, All- China Students’ Federation,All-China Federation of Specialized Societies of Natural Sciences, All-China Association for Science and Technology Popularization, Sino-Soviet Friendship Association, and China People's Relief Association. The congratulatory telegrams unanimously congratulated them on the victories they achieved in 1952, and wished them even more brilliant victories in 1953.

### 全国各人民团体电志愿军、朝鲜人民军贺年

1953-01-01
第4版()
专栏：

    全国各人民团体电志愿军、朝鲜人民军贺年
    据新华社讯：全国各人民团体三十日致电中国人民志愿军，祝贺一九五三年新年。向中国人民志愿军致贺电的有：中华全国总工会、中华全国民主妇女联合会、中国人民保卫儿童全国委员会、中华全国民主青年联合总会、中华全国文学艺术界联合会、中华全国学生联合会、中华全国自然科学专门学会联合会、中华全国科学技术普及协会、中苏友好协会总会和中国人民救济总会。贺电一致表示：将继续加强抗美援朝工作，为保卫远东和世界和平而斗争。
    据新华社讯：全国各人民团体致电金日成将军暨朝鲜人民军，祝贺一九五三年新年。向金日成将军暨朝鲜人民军致贺电的有：中华全国总工会、中华全国民主妇女联合会、中国人民保卫儿童全国委员会、中华全国民主青年联合总会、中华全国文学艺术界联合会、中华全国学生联合会、中华全国自然科学专门学会联合会、中华全国科学技术普及协会、中苏友好协会总会和中国人民救济总会。贺电一致祝贺他们在一九五二年获得的胜利，并预祝他们在一九五三年获得更辉煌的胜利。
