Title: Our Great Motherland (Picture)
Author:
Date: 1953-01-01
Page: 1

    Our Great Motherland
    Our country's large-scale construction has begun. In order to industrialize the country, we must develop heavy industries such as machine manufacturing. This is a heavy machinery workshop in our country's newly built Jingwei Textile Machinery Factory. Workers are installing machines manufactured in the Soviet Union, preparing to be fully operational this year.

### 我们伟大的祖国(图片）

1953-01-01
第1版()
专栏：

    我们伟大的祖国
    我国大规模建设开始了。为了使国家工业化，我们必须发展机器制造等重工业。这是我国新建的经纬纺织机械制造厂的一个重型机械工场。工人们正在安装苏联制造的机器，准备在今年全部开工。
