Title: Chinese Red Cross Replies to Japanese Red Cross and Other Three Organizations, Agreeing to the Japanese Delegation Led by Shimazu Tadatsugu and Six Others to Come for Consultation on the Repatriation of Japanese Nationals
Author: 
Date: 1953-01-08
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, 7th: Regarding the issue of assisting Japanese nationals in China to return to their country, since the Chinese Red Cross telegraphed the Japanese Red Cross, the Japanese Peace Liaison Committee, and the Japan-China Friendship Association on December 22, 1952, requesting them to conduct consultations and jointly form a delegation to come to Beijing, the Chinese Red Cross received a telegram from the aforementioned three organizations on December 26, 1952, proposing a list of representatives. The Chinese Red Cross telegraphed the aforementioned three organizations on January 6, stating: The Chinese Red Cross agrees to the Japanese delegation being composed of Mr. Shimazu Tadatsugu, Mr. Kudo Tadao, Mr. Uchiyama Kanzo, Mr. Kashima Toshio, Mr. Hirano Yoshitaro, Mr. Hatanaka Masaharu, and Mr. Takarayoshi Tomishichi. This association regrets that it is unable to receive other personnel. Regarding the scope of this discussion between the two sides, it should be handled in accordance with the relevant statements made by the relevant departments of the Government of the People's Republic of China on December 1, 1952, that is, to negotiate and resolve the formalities for incoming ships and various specific issues regarding the repatriation of Japanese nationals. The Chinese Red Cross also agreed that the Japanese delegation could enter the country through Guangzhou after January 15.

### 中国红十字会电复日本红十字会等三团体  同意日方岛津忠承等七人组代表团前来协商日侨归国问题

1953-01-08
第1版()
专栏：

    中国红十字会电复日本红十字会等三团体
    同意日方岛津忠承等七人组代表团前来协商日侨归国问题
    【新华社七日讯】关于协助在中国的日本侨民归国问题，自一九五二年十二月二十二日中国红十字会电复日本红十字会、日本和平联络委员会、日中友好协会三团体，由他们进行协商，共同组成代表团来北京后，中国红十字会曾收到上述三团体于一九五二年十二月二十六日的来电，提出了代表名单。中国红十字会已于一月六日电复上述三团体称：中国红十字会同意日方代表团由岛津忠承、工籐忠夫、内山完造、加岛敏雄、平野义太郎、畑中政春、高良富七先生组成，其他人员本会碍难接待。关于此次双方讨论范围，应根据中华人民共和国政府有关方面在一九五二年十二月一日的谈话办理，即协商解决来船手续以及日侨回国的各项具体问题。中国红十字会并同意日方代表团自一月十五日以后即可由广州入境。
