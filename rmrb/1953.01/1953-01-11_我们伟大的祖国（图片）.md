    Our Great Motherland
    Longkou vermicelli from Shandong Province is a famous local product of our country. Its main production area is in the Zhaoyuan County area of Shandong Province. This is a scene of farmers in Wenjia Village, District 1, Zhaoyuan County, drying vermicelli.
