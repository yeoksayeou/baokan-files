Title: Government Administration Council Holds 166th Meeting Decides to Establish Marriage Law Implementation Movement Committee
Author:
Date: 1953-01-10
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, January 9th—The Government Administration Council of the Central People's Government held its 166th plenary meeting on the afternoon of the 9th. The meeting listened to a report by Lai Chuanzhu, Deputy Director of the General Cadre Management Department of the People's Revolutionary Military Commission, on the Regulations (Draft) of the People's Republic of China for Awarding the "August 1st" and "Honor" Medals, and adopted the "Regulations of the People's Republic of China for Awarding the August 1st Medal" and the "Regulations of the People's Republic of China for Awarding the Honor Medal." These two sets of regulations will be implemented after being submitted to the Central People's Government Committee for approval. The meeting listened to a report by Hu Jingyun, Deputy Governor of the People's Bank of China, on the People's Bank of China's report on the issue of settling pre-liberation deposits, and approved the "Measures for Paying Unsettled Deposits of Banking and Money Industries Before Liberation" and the "Measures for Registering Deposits of Banking and Money Industries Before Liberation." The meeting approved the "Decision on the Merger of Customs and Foreign Trade Administration Organs," which will be implemented after being submitted to the Central People's Government Committee for approval. The meeting decided to establish the Central Marriage Law Implementation Movement Committee. In addition, the meeting approved various appointment and removal cases and cases to be submitted to the Central People's Government Committee for approval of appointments and removals.

### 政务院举行第一六六次会议  决定成立贯彻婚姻法运动委员会

1953-01-10
第1版()
专栏：

    政务院举行第一六六次会议
    决定成立贯彻婚姻法运动委员会
    【新华社九日讯】中央人民政府政务院第一百六十六次政务会议在九日下午举行。会议听取了人民革命军事委员会总干部管理部赖传珠副部长所作的关于中华人民共和国授予“八一”、“荣誉”奖章条例（草案）的说明，通过了“中华人民共和国授予八一奖章条例”及“中华人民共和国授予荣誉奖章条例”。这两个条例，将于报请中央人民政府委员会批准后施行。会议听取了中国人民银行胡景澐副行长所作的中国人民银行关于清偿解放前存款问题的报告，批准了“解放前银钱业未清偿存款给付办法”及“解放前银钱业存款登记办法”。会议通过了“关于海关与对外贸易管理机关实行合并的决定”，此一决定将于报请中央人民政府委员会批准后施行。会议决定成立中央贯彻婚姻法运动委员会。此外，会议通过了各项任免案及提请中央人民政府委员会批准任免案。
