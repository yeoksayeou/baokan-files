Title: Peace Organizations and People from All Walks of Life in Poland, Hungary, Czechoslovakia, France, and Other Countries Enthusiastically Support the Resolutions of the World People's Peace Congress
Author:
Date: 1953-01-09
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency, January 8th: According to TASS, after the delegations from Poland and Hungary attending the World People's Peace Congress returned to their countries, they are conveying the resolutions of the congress to the people and calling on them to intensify their struggle to implement these resolutions.

    A mass rally was held in Warsaw, the capital of Poland, on the 3rd, attended by 4,000 people. The Polish delegation to the Vienna Congress, led by Iwaszkiewicz, Chairman of the Polish National Peace Committee, representatives from Argentina, Bolivia, Chile, Cuba, Paraguay, Venezuela, and North Africa who participated in the congress and are currently visiting Poland, and Anna Seghers, a famous German female writer and winner of the Stalin International Prize for "Strengthening International Peace," all participated in the rally. Iwaszkiewicz gave a report at the meeting, stating: The representatives attending the Vienna Congress reached a consensus, and the resolutions of the congress have been enthusiastically supported by all peace-loving people in the world. Our people are full of boundless confidence in their own strength and the great strength of the world peace camp. In accordance with the resolutions of the congress, they will fight for peace more unyieldingly. The congress unanimously passed a resolution expressing that the working people of Warsaw and all of Poland wholeheartedly support the resolutions of the World People's Peace Congress.

    On December 30th, a mass rally was held in Budapest, the capital of Hungary. Academician Andics, a member of the World Peace Council and Chairman of the Hungarian National Peace Council, reported on the great achievements of the World People's Peace Congress at the meeting. He said: This congress shows that more and more people are now condemning the U.S. aggressive policy. The people of all countries do not want to be cannon fodder and tools in the hands of war-mongers. We, as the people of a free country, must do our best to strengthen the cause of peace. Many Hungarian representatives who participated in the Vienna Congress then spoke, expressing the unanimous desire of the Hungarian people amidst enthusiastic cheers from the masses: to work more resolutely for world peace and to strive to realize the resolutions of the World People's Peace Congress.

    Furthermore, according to Mukarovsky, a member of the Czechoslovak Committee for the Defense of Peace, the Second Czechoslovak Congress for the Defense of Peace is scheduled to be held on January 17th and 18th. The tasks outlined in the documents of the World People's Peace Congress will be discussed at the meeting. Mukarovsky pointed out that the Czechoslovak people should contribute to the cause of peace by completing the final year plan of the First Czechoslovak Five-Year Plan ahead of schedule.

    Xinhua News Agency, January 4th: TASS, Paris, December 31, 1952: "Liberation" published a communiqué from the meeting of the Executive Committee of the French National Peace Council, which discussed the implementation of the decisions adopted by the Vienna World People's Peace Congress.

    The communiqué said: Many French political and trade union leaders, as well as figures from the literary, scientific, and artistic circles, although not belonging to the peace movement, participated in the Vienna Congress and agreed with its decisions. The Executive Committee of the National Peace Council is deeply gratified by this situation. The National Peace Council hopes to cooperate with people from all walks of life to strive to put our country on the path of negotiated solutions to disputes and to conclude a Five-Power Peace Pact as soon as possible.

    "Liberation" reported: Many local farmers' peace committees have now been established, including Issy-les-Moulineaux, a suburb of Paris, and Le Vieilleville (Marais Department). A Farmers' Peace Provincial Committee has been established in the Pas-de-Calais department.

### 波匈捷法等国和平组织及各阶层人民  热烈拥护世界人民和平大会决议

1953-01-09
第4版()
专栏：

    波匈捷法等国和平组织及各阶层人民
    热烈拥护世界人民和平大会决议
   【新华社八日讯】据塔斯社讯：波兰和匈牙利出席世界人民和平大会的代表团回国后，正在向各国人民传达大会的决议，并号召人民为贯彻这些决议而加紧斗争。
    波兰首都华沙在三日举行了群众大会，出席大会的有四千人，以波兰全国和平委员会主席伊瓦希凯维奇为首的波兰出席维也纳大会的代表们，现正在波兰访问的阿根廷、玻利维亚、智利、古巴、巴拉圭、委内瑞拉和北非的参加大会的代表们和“加强国际和平”斯大林国际奖金得奖人、德国著名女作家安娜·西格斯都参加了大会。伊瓦希凯维奇在会上作了报告，他说：参加维也纳大会的代表们取得了一致的意见，大会的决议已得到世界上所有爱好和平的人民的热烈拥护。我国人民对自己的力量和全世界和平阵营的伟大的力量充满着无比的信心。他们根据大会的决议，将更加不屈不挠地为和平而斗争。大会最后一致通过了一项决议。表示华沙和全波兰的劳动人民全心拥护世界人民和平大会的决议。
    十二月三十日在匈牙利首都布达佩斯举行了一个群众大会，世界和平理事会理事、匈牙利全国和平理事会主席安迪奇院士在会上报告了世界人民和平大会的巨大成就。他说：这次大会表明现在谴责美国侵略政策的人们越来越多了。世界各国人民都不愿当炮灰和战争制造者手中的工具。我们是一个自由国家的人民，必须尽力加强和平事业。许多参加维也纳大会的匈牙利代表接着发言，他们在群众热烈欢呼声中，表示了匈牙利人民一致的愿望：要更加坚决地为世界和平而工作，要为实现世界人民和平大会的决议而努力。
    又据捷克斯洛伐克保卫和平委员会委员穆卡罗夫斯基宣布：第二届捷克斯洛伐克保卫和平大会定于一月十七日和十八日举行，会上将讨论世界人民和平大会各项文件所指出的任务。穆卡罗夫斯基指出，捷克斯洛伐克人民应当以提前完成第一个捷克斯洛伐克五年计划的最后一年计划，作为对和平事业的贡献。 【新华社四日讯】塔斯社巴黎一九五二年十二月三十一日讯：“解放报”刊载了法国全国和平理事会执行委员会会议的公报，这次会议讨论了关于实行维也纳世界人民和平大会所通过的各项决定的问题。
    公报说：许多法国政界和工会领袖以及文学、科学和艺术界人士虽不属于和平运动但都参加了维也纳的大会并赞同大会的各项决定，全国和平理事会执行委员会对于这种情况，深感满意。全国和平理事会希望与各界人士合作，借以努力使我国走上以协商的解决纠纷的道路，并早日缔结五大国和平公约。
    “解放报”报道：现在有许多地方已经成立了农民和平委员会，其中包括伊西·勒·摩里努，巴黎郊区，列维埃尔拉区（沼原省）等地。在加来海峡省已设立了一个农民和平省委员会。
