Title: Chinese Society for Microbiology Holds Representative Conference
Author: 
Date: 1953-01-26
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, January 25th: The Chinese Society for Microbiology, a member society of the All-China Federation of Natural Science Societies, held its first National Member Representative Conference in Beijing from December 18th to 20th, 1952. Representatives of microbiology workers from all over the country attended the conference. Several microbiologists who participated in the anti-bacterial warfare work on the Korean front were also invited to attend the conference.

Fu Lianzhang, Vice Minister of the Ministry of Health of the Central People's Government, and Zhu Kezhen, member of the All-China Federation of Natural Science Societies and Vice President of the Chinese Academy of Sciences, gave clear instructions on the future direction and tasks at the conference. Then, the conference listened to the work report of Tang Feifan, Chairman of the Chinese Society for Microbiology, and Professor Wei Xi's report on the bacteriological warfare conducted by the US imperialist aggressors in Korea and Northeast China, discussed many important issues in microbiology and the future direction, tasks, and work plans, and criticized the past research methods. Papers were also read at the conference, and the articles of association were adopted, and new directors were elected. Finally, the conference passed a message of respect to Chairman Mao of the Central People's Government, a message of respect to the Chinese People's Volunteers, and an open letter to microbiology workers around the world.

### 中国微生物学会开代表大会

1953-01-26
第1版()
专栏：

    中国微生物学会开代表大会
    【新华社二十五日讯】中华全国自然科学专门学会联合会会员学会中国微生物学会于一九五二年十二月十八日到二十日在北京召开了第一次全国会员代表大会。参加这次大会的有全国各地的微生物工作者的代表。大会还邀请了几位在朝鲜前线参加反细菌战工作的微生物学家出席大会。
    中央人民政府卫生部副部长傅连暲，中华全国自然科学专门学会联合会委员、中国科学院副院长竺可桢在大会上对今后方针与任务作了明确的指示；然后大会听取了中国微生物学会汤飞凡理事长的会务报告和魏曦教授关于美帝国主义侵略者在朝鲜及我国东北进行细菌战的报告，讨论了许多微生物学方面的重要问题和今后的方针任务及工作计划，批判了过去的研究工作方法。大会上还宣读了论文，通过了会章，选举了新理事。大会最后通过了向中央人民政府毛主席致敬电、向中国人民志愿军致敬电和致全世界微生物学工作者的公开信。
