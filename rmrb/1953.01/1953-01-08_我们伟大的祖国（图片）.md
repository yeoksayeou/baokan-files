    The newly built Chongqing Laboring People's Cultural Palace has a children's park, exhibition hall, library, games room, concert hall, Sino-Soviet cultural room, football field, skating rink and zoo. This is a panoramic view of the skating rink. Young people are playing skating games here.

### 我们伟大的祖国（图片）

专栏：

    我们伟大的祖国
    新建的重庆市劳动人民文化宫，设有儿童乐园、展览室、图书馆、游艺室、音乐厅、中苏文化室、足球场、溜冰场和动物院。这是溜冰场全景。青年们正在这里作溜冰游戏。
