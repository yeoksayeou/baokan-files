Title: The Third Redemption Lottery for the First Phase of People's Victory Indexed Bonds Will Be Held the Day After Tomorrow

Author: 
Date: 1953-01-30
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, January 29th. The third redemption and interest payment lottery ceremony for the first phase of People's Victory Indexed Bonds has been decided to be held publicly at the Chang'an Grand Theater in Beijing on February 1st. The winning numbers will be announced immediately after the lottery, so that bondholders across the country can redeem the principal and accrued interest of the winning bonds from their local People's Bank of China starting on March 31st.

### 第一期人民胜利折实公债第三次还本抽签后天举行

1953-01-30
第1版()
专栏：

    第一期人民胜利折实公债第三次还本抽签后天举行
    【新华社二十九日讯】第一期人民胜利折实公债第三次还本付息抽签仪式，已决定二月一日在北京长安大戏院公开举行。抽签后即公布中签号码，以便全国各地持有债券的人在三月三十一日起向当地中国人民银行兑取中签债券本款与到期利息。
