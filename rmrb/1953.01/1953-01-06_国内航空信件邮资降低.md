Title: Domestic Airmail Postage Reduced
Author:
Date: 1953-01-06
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, January 5th: The Ministry of Posts and Telecommunications of the Central People's Government has decided to reduce domestic airmail postage rates starting from January 5th. The airmail postage for a domestic airmail letter weighing ten grams will be reduced from 1,500 yuan to 800 yuan, making the total cost, including regular postage, 1,600 yuan.

### 国内航空信件邮资降低

1953-01-06
第1版()
专栏：

    国内航空信件邮资降低
    【新华社五日讯】中央人民政府邮电部决定从一月五日起降低国内航空信件的航空资费。每封重十公分的国内航空信的航空资费由过去的一千五百元减为八百元，连一般邮资在内共一千六百元。
