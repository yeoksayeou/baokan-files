    Our Great Motherland
    The promotion of new-style farm tools will be done even better this year than last year. The Zhangjiakou Farm Tool Factory has manufactured a large number of new-style walking plows. This batch of walking plows is being selected and will soon be distributed to the countryside.

### 我们伟大的祖国（图片）

1953-01-26
第1版()
专栏：

    我们伟大的祖国
    新式农具的推广工作，今年将比去年做得更好。张家口农具制造厂制造了大批新式的步犁。这一批步犁正经过挑选，就要分发到农村去了。
