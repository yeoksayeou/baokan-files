Title: Siroky Visits Korean Children in Czechoslovakia
Author:
Date: 1953-01-07
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency, Prague, January 5th: The "Red Right Newspaper" reported that Czechoslovak Deputy Prime Minister and Minister of Foreign Affairs Viliam Široký, accompanied by Korean Ambassador to Czechoslovakia Kim Ung-gi, visited the Korean children residing in the children's home in Spatz-Hustka on New Year's Eve in 1952. They watched the Korean children perform Korean and Czechoslovak songs and dances and other recreational programs. The Korean children then received gifts from the Czechoslovak working people. Korean Ambassador Kim Ung-gi expressed his gratitude to the Czechoslovak government and people for their kind care of the Korean children. Široký congratulated the Korean people on the victory of their just cause before leaving.

### 西罗基访问在捷朝鲜儿童

1953-01-07
第4版()
专栏：

    西罗基访问在捷朝鲜儿童
    【新华社布拉格五日电】“红色权利报”报道：捷克斯洛伐克副总理兼外交部部长威廉·西罗基由朝鲜驻捷克斯洛伐克公使金应基陪同，在一九五二年除夕访问了住在斯巴茨·胡斯特卡的儿童之家里面的朝鲜儿童。他们参观了朝鲜儿童所表演的朝鲜和捷克斯洛伐克歌舞等游艺节目。朝鲜儿童随后接受了捷克斯洛伐克劳动人民送给他们的礼物。朝鲜公使金应基对捷克斯洛伐克政府和人民对朝鲜儿童的亲切照顾，表示感谢。西罗基在临别时祝贺朝鲜人民的正义事业取得胜利。
