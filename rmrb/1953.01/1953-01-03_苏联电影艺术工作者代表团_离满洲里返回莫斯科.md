Title: Soviet Film Artists Delegation Leaves Manchouli for Moscow
Author:
Date: 1953-01-03
Page: 4

    Soviet Film Artists Delegation
    Leaves Manchouli for Moscow

    [Xinhua News Agency, Manchouli Dispatch] The Soviet Film Artists Delegation, which came to our country to participate in the "Sino-Soviet Friendship Month" and the "Soviet Film Exhibition Month," left our country from Manchouli by train on December 23 to return to Moscow.

    Fedorov and the delegation members had been filled with feelings of reluctance since they left Beijing by train on the 20th. On the 22nd, the delegation members and the staff who accompanied them in various places to participate in the "Sino-Soviet Friendship Month" activities held a farewell banquet on the train. Chekov, Smirnova, and others were tearful when they delivered their speeches.

    On the 23rd, before the Soviet Film Artists Delegation left Manchouli, Manchouli Mayor Su Lin held a farewell banquet. At the station, the delegation members and the staff who accompanied them, along with those seeing them off, warmly embraced and bid farewell, with tears welling up in everyone's eyes. Fedorov said, "Don't cry, everyone, we will meet again," but he himself could not help but shed tears. Cherkasov, Chekov, and Ladinina got on the train, then got off again to embrace the people seeing them off, fully expressing their deep friendship with the Chinese people.

### 苏联电影艺术工作者代表团  离满洲里返回莫斯科

1953-01-03
第4版()
专栏：

    苏联电影艺术工作者代表团
    离满洲里返回莫斯科
    【新华社讯】满洲里讯：来我国参加“中苏友好月”和“苏联影片展览月”活动的苏联电影艺术工作者代表团在十二月二十三日从满洲里乘车离开我国返回莫斯科。
    费道罗夫和团员们自二十日乘车离开北京后，满怀着惜别之情。二十二日，团员们和陪同他们在各地参加“中苏友好月”活动的工作人员在车上举行了告别宴会。契尔柯夫、斯米尔诺娃等在致词时，都热泪涔涔。
    二十三日，苏联电影艺术工作者代表团离开满洲里前，满洲里市市长苏林曾设宴欢送。在车站上，团员们和陪同他们的工作人员和欢送者热烈拥抱告别，大家的眼泪都夺眶而出。费道罗夫说：“大家不要流泪，我们还要再见的”，但他自己也情不自禁地流下泪来。契尔卡索夫、契尔柯夫、拉迪尼娜上车后，再度下车，和欢送的人们拥抱，充分表露了他们对中国人民的深厚的友谊。
