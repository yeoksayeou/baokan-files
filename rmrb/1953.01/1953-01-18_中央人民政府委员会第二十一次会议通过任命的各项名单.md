I. Supplementary List of Vice Ministers of the Ministry of Foreign Trade of the Central People's Government
    Vice Ministers:
    Li Zheren: Currently a member of the North China Administrative Committee of the Central People's Government's Government Administration Council and Director of the Trade Bureau.
    Kong Yuan: Currently Director of the General Administration of Customs of the Central People's Government.

II. Supplementary List of Vice Ministers of the Ministry of Commerce of the Central People's Government
    Vice Ministers:
    Wang Lei: Currently Minister of the Trade Department of the Southwest Military and Administrative Committee.
    Wu Xuezhi: Currently Minister of the Trade Department of the East China Military and Administrative Committee.

III. Supplementary List of Vice Ministers of the Ministry of Railways of the Central People's Government
    Vice Ministers:
    Zhao Jianmin: Currently Minister of the Transportation Department of the Southwest Military and Administrative Committee.

IV. Supplementary List of Vice Presidents of the Chinese Academy of Sciences
    Vice President:
    Zhang Jiafu: Currently Deputy Director of the Cultural and Educational Committee of the Northwest Military and Administrative Committee.
