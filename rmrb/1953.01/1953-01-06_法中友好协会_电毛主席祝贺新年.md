Title: France-China Friendship Association Sends Telegram to Chairman Mao to Congratulate the New Year
Author:
Date: 1953-01-06
Page: 1

    Xinhua News Agency, 5th. The Chairman of the France-China Friendship Association, De Lessart, sent a telegram to Chairman Mao Zedong of the Central People's Government of China to congratulate the New Year. The telegram said: "The France-China Friendship Association extends warm congratulations to you and the Chinese people for 1953."

### 法中友好协会  电毛主席祝贺新年

1953-01-06
第1版()
专栏：

    法中友好协会
    电毛主席祝贺新年
    【新华社五日讯】法中友好协会主席德莱士致电我中央人民政府毛泽东主席祝贺新年。电文说：“法中友好协会谨向您及中国人民热烈地祝贺一九五三年。”
