Title: Five Burmese Peace Representatives Leave Beijing for Home

Author:

Date: 1953-01-29
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, January 28th: Five Burmese peace representatives, U Tin, Tekin Kyaing Maung, U Yin, U Chan Ba, and U Ba La Aung, who attended the World People's Peace Congress and were invited by the Chinese People's Committee for World Peace to visit China, left Beijing by train for home on the 28th. Liu Guan-yi, Secretary-General of the Chinese People's Committee for World Peace, and Zheng Zhenduo, President of the Sino-Burmese Friendship Association, and others were at the station to see them off.

### 缅甸和平代表五人离北京回国

1953-01-29
第1版()
专栏：

    缅甸和平代表五人离北京回国
    【新华社二十八日讯】出席世界人民和平大会后应中国人民保卫世界和平委员会邀请来中国参观的缅甸部分和平代表吴登、德钦庆貌、吴迎、吴昌波、吴巴拉昂五人，二十八日乘火车离北京回国。到车站送行的有：中国人民保卫世界和平委员会秘书长刘贯一和中缅友好协会会长郑振铎等人。
