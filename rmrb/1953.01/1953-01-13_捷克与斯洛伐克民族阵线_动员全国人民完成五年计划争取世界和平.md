Title: Czech and Slovak National Front Mobilizes the Entire Nation to Complete the Five-Year Plan and Strive for World Peace
Author:
Date: 1953-01-13
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Prague: According to TASS, the Presidium of the Central Action Committee of the Czech and Slovak National Front held an enlarged meeting in Prague on the 9th. The theme of the meeting was to mobilize the Czechoslovak people to complete the Five-Year Plan victoriously and strive for world peace with all their strength. Attendees included members of the Czechoslovak government headed by Czechoslovak Prime Minister Antonín Zápotocký, members of the Slovak Commissioner's Office, members of the Czechoslovak National Assembly and the Slovak National Council (formerly translated as the National Assembly), chairmen of the People's Committees of various states, representatives of various political parties, advanced workers in industry, transportation, and agriculture, and cultural workers.

Prime Minister Zápotocký delivered a lengthy report on domestic and foreign policy at the meeting. He described the significant achievements the country has made so far in developing the economy and improving the people's living standards, as well as new tasks in this regard. Zápotocký spoke of the growing friendship and unbreakable alliance between Czechoslovakia and the great Soviet Union, as well as the eagerness and determination of the working people of Czechoslovakia to accelerate socialist construction in order to safeguard world peace. The entire venue was filled with storms of prolonged applause from time to time.

Following this, a heated discussion was held on Zápotocký's report. Speakers unanimously agreed with the National Front's current tasks in ensuring the completion of the Five-Year Plan this year as proposed by Zápotocký. The speakers all spoke of the growing political and labor enthusiasm shown by the Czechoslovak people in all aspects of socialist construction.

Note: The first Five-Year Plan implemented by Czechoslovakia starting in 1949 had its targets increased in early 1951. According to this expanded Five-Year Plan, by the end of 1953, the total industrial output would increase by 98 percent compared to 1948, while the original plan stipulated an increase of 57 percent; the total agricultural output would increase by 53 percent compared to 1948, while the original plan stipulated an increase of 37 percent. According to the report by Klement Gottwald, Chairman of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of Czechoslovakia and President of the Czechoslovak Republic, at the National Congress of the Communist Party of Czechoslovakia on December 16, 1952, the total industrial output of Czechoslovakia had increased by 82 percent in the first four years of the Five-Year Plan compared to 1948.

### 捷克与斯洛伐克民族阵线  动员全国人民完成五年计划争取世界和平

1953-01-13
第4版()
专栏：

    捷克与斯洛伐克民族阵线
    动员全国人民完成五年计划争取世界和平
    【新华社十一日讯】据塔斯社布拉格讯：捷克与斯洛伐克民族阵线中央行动委员会主席团九日在布拉格举行扩大会议。会议的主题是动员捷克斯洛伐克人民以全部力量胜利地完成五年计划和争取世界和平。出席会议的有以捷克斯洛伐克共和国总理安托宁·萨波托斯基为首的捷克斯洛伐克政府委员以及斯洛伐克专员公署的委员，捷克斯洛伐克国民议会的议员和斯洛伐克民族议会（前译民族会议）的议员，各州人民委员会的主席，各政党代表，工业、运输业和农业的先进工作者和文化工作者。
    萨波托斯基总理在会上作了关于内政和外交政策的长篇报告。他叙述了到现在为止国家在发展经济和提高人民生活水平方面取得的重大成就，以及这方面的新任务。萨波托斯基谈到捷克斯洛伐克和伟大的苏联两国间不断增长的友谊和牢不可破的同盟以及捷克斯洛伐克的劳动人民加速进行社会主义建设以维护世界和平的热望和决心。全场时时响起暴风雨般经久不息的掌声。
    接着会议就萨波托斯基的报告进行了热烈的讨论。发言的人一致表示同意萨波托斯基所提出的民族阵线在保证今年完成五年计划方面的当前任务。发言的人都谈到了捷克斯洛伐克人民在社会主义建设各方面表现的日益增长的政治热情和劳动热情。
    按：捷克斯洛伐克从一九四九年起开始实行的第一个五年计划，到一九五一年初，曾经追加任务。根据这个扩大了的五年计划，到一九五三年底，工业总产量将比一九四八年增加百分之九十八，而原计划规定增加百分之五十七；农业总产量将比一九四八年增加百分之五十三，而原计划规定增加百分之三十七。根据捷克斯洛伐克共产党中央委员会主席和捷克斯洛伐克共和国总统克利门特·哥特瓦尔德一九五二年十二月十六日在捷共全国代表会议上的报告，在五年计划的前四年中，捷全部工业总产量已比一九四八年增加了百分之八十二。
