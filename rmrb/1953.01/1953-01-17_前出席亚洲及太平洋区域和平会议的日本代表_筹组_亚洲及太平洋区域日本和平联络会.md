Title: Former Japanese Representatives to the Asian and Pacific Regional Peace Conference Organize "Japan Peace Liaison Council for the Asian and Pacific Region"
Author: 
Date: 1953-01-17
Page: 4

    [News from this newspaper] According to a report in the Japanese "Akahata" on December 24, 1952: The representatives who were previously selected to attend the Asian and Pacific Regional Peace Conference have recently initiated the organization of the "Japan Peace Liaison Council for the Asian and Pacific Region." Since they established the preparatory committee and started their work, people from all walks of life have enthusiastically requested to join as members. Currently, the "Japan Peace Liaison Council for the Asian and Pacific Region" has 84 members, including Jiichiro Matsumoto (former Vice-President of the House of Councillors), Shigetoku Amano (Chairman of the Japan Council of Public Corporation and National Enterprise Workers' Unions), Akira Kazami (Member of Parliament), Kitaro Shimizu (Professor), Ichiko Kamichika (President of the Women's Times), Heishi Toyama (Former Liberal Party Councilor), Kumao Terada (Professor), Wenfang Gan (Overseas Chinese Federation), Gaeseong Ho (Democratic United Front of Korean Residents in Japan), Masako Higa (President of the Kansai Regional Women's Federation), and Yoshio Nakano (Professor), among other peace activists from all walks of life. The secretariat of the preparatory committee stated: "Whether individual or organization, we welcome participation." The aims of the Japan Peace Liaison Council are: 1. To maintain close contact with peace movements in Asian and Pacific countries through the Asian and Pacific Regional Peace Liaison Committee in Beijing; 2. To regularly exchange peace envoys with peace movements in the Asian and Pacific region, as well as within Japan; 3. To fight against the Yoshida government's policy of restricting foreign travel and strive for freedom to travel abroad in order to achieve the above objectives; 4. To exchange international and domestic news related to the peace movement; 5. To dedicate efforts to the work of a unified front for the peace movement.

### 前出席亚洲及太平洋区域和平会议的日本代表  筹组“亚洲及太平洋区域日本和平联络会”

1953-01-17
第4版()
专栏：

    前出席亚洲及太平洋区域和平会议的日本代表
    筹组“亚洲及太平洋区域日本和平联络会”
    【本报讯】据一九五二年十二月二十四日日本“赤旗报”的消息说：前被推选为出席亚洲及太平洋区域和平会议的代表们，最近发起组织“亚洲及太平洋区域日本和平联络会”。自从他们成立了筹备会展开工作以来，各方面要求加入为会员的人极为踊跃。现在“亚洲及太平洋区域日本和平联络会”的会员已达八十四人，其中包括松本治一郎（前参议院副议长）、天野重德（日本官公厅工会协议会议长）、风见章（议员）、清水几太郎（教授）、神近市子（妇女时报社长）、远山丙市（自由党前参议员）、寺田熊雄（教授）、甘文芳（华侨总会）、高成浩（旅日朝鲜侨民民主统一战线）、比嘉正子（关西地区妇女联合会会长）、中野好夫（教授）等各阶层的和平运动人士。筹备会的秘书处说：“不论个人或团体，我们都欢迎参加”。日本和平联络会的宗旨是：一、与北京的亚洲及太平洋区域和平联络委员会的亚洲及太平洋地区各国的和平运动团体进行密切的连络工作；二、定期与亚洲及太平洋地区以及日本国内的和平运动团体互派和平使节；三、为了达到上述的目的而与吉田政府限制出国的政策作斗争，争取出国自由；四、交换国际的以及国内的有关和平运动的消息；五、致力于和平运动统一战线的工作。
