Title: Government Administration Council Holds 167th Meeting
Author:
Date: 1953-01-24
Page: 1

    Xinhua News Agency, January 23rd: The 167th session of the Central People's Government Administration Council was held on the afternoon of the 16th. It heard and approved the report by Vice Minister of Health, He Cheng, of the Central People's Government Ministry of Health, on the Hygiene Administration Conference and the Second National Hygiene Conference.

### 政务院举行第一六七次会议

1953-01-24
第1版()
专栏：

    政务院举行第一六七次会议
    【新华社二十三日讯】中央人民政府政务院第一百六十七次政务会议在十六日下午举行，听取和批准了中央人民政府卫生部贺诚副部长所作的关于卫生行政会议与第二届全国卫生会议的报告。
