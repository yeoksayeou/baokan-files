Title: China-Mongolia Postal and Telecommunications Agreements Signed in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1953-01-17
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, 16th—To facilitate communication and cultural exchange between the people of China and the Mongolian People's Republic, representatives of the Ministry of Posts and Telecommunications of the Central People's Government of China and the Ministry of Posts and Telecommunications of the Mongolian People's Republic, in accordance with the spirit of the Economic and Cultural Cooperation Agreement signed in Beijing on October 4th last year between the Central People's Government of China and the Great People's Khural of the Mongolian People's Republic, held a meeting on postal and telecommunications matters between the two countries. The meeting was conducted in an extremely friendly and harmonious atmosphere, and on January 16th, the China-Mongolia Postal and Telecommunications Agreements were formally signed in Beijing. The signatories to the agreements were Zhu Xuefan, Minister of the Ministry of Posts and Telecommunications of the Central People's Government of China, and Jalkhasaikhan, Ambassador Extraordinary and Plenipotentiary of the Mongolian People's Republic to China.

Attending the signing ceremony on the Chinese side were Zhang Hanfu, Deputy Minister of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs of the Central People's Government, Chen Jiakang, Director of the Asian Department, Shen Ping, Deputy Director of the Protocol Department, Wang Zigang, Vice Minister of the Ministry of Posts and Telecommunications, Su Younong, Director of the General Postal Bureau, Cao Danhui, Director of the General Bureau of Long-Distance Telecommunications, and others. On the Mongolian side was Nima, First Secretary of the Embassy of the Mongolian People's Republic in China, and others.

Minister Zhu Xuefan and Ambassador Jalkhasaikhan both delivered speeches after the signing, unanimously congratulating the signing of these two agreements, and pointed out that the signing of these two agreements shortly after the Economic and Cultural Cooperation Agreement between China and Mongolia was approved by the two governments is of great significance.

After the signing ceremony, Minister Zhu Xuefan hosted a banquet in honor of Ambassador Jalkhasaikhan and all members of the embassy.

### 中蒙邮政及电信两个协定在京签订

1953-01-17
第1版()
专栏：

    中蒙邮政及电信两个协定在京签订
    【新华社十六日讯】为便利我国与蒙古人民共和国两国人民间的通信联络和文化交流，我中央人民政府邮电部与蒙古人民共和国邮电部代表，根据我中央人民政府与蒙古人民共和国大人民呼拉尔于去年十月四日在北京签订的经济及文化合作协定的精神，就两国间邮电通信事项举行会议。会议在极其友好与融洽的气氛中进行，并于一月十六日在北京正式签订中蒙邮政及电信两个协定。在协定上签字的，中国方面为中央人民政府邮电部部长朱学范，蒙古方面为蒙古人民共和国驻中国特命全权大使贾尔卡赛汗。
    参加签字仪式的，中国方面有中央人民政府外交部副部长章汉夫、亚洲司司长陈家康、交际处副处长沈平，邮电部副部长王子纲、邮政总局局长苏幼农、长途电信总局局长曹丹辉等；蒙古方面有蒙古人民共和国驻中国大使馆一等秘书尼玛等。
    朱学范部长和贾尔卡赛汗大使都在签字后致词，对这两个协定的签订，一致表示庆贺，并指出这两个协定在中蒙经济及文化合作协定经两国政府批准后不久即行签订，具有重要的意义。
    在签字仪式后，朱学范部长设席欢宴贾尔卡赛汗大使和大使馆全体人员。
