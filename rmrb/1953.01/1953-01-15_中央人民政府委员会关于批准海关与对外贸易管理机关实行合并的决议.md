    Resolution of the Central People's Government Committee on Approving the Merger of Customs and Foreign Trade Management Agencies
    (Passed at the 21st Meeting of the Central People's Government Committee on January 14, 1953)
    The 21st meeting of the Central People's Government Committee resolved: To approve the decision of the Central People's Government Administration Council on the merger of customs and foreign trade management agencies.
