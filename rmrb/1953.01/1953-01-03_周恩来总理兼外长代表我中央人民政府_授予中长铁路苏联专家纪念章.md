Title: Premier and Foreign Minister Zhou Enlai, on Behalf of the Central People's Government, Awards Commemorative Medals to Soviet Experts of the China Changchun Railway
Author:
Date: 1953-01-03
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, 2nd. To thank and commemorate the more than 1,300 Soviet experts of the former China Changchun Railway for their work achievements during the period of Sino-Soviet co-management of the China Changchun Railway and their great help to the Chinese people's railway construction cause, the Central People's Government specially awarded them the "China Changchun Railway Commemorative Medal." The awarding ceremony was held at 6:00 p.m. on January 1, 1953, at the Harbin Railway Culture Center, where Premier and Foreign Minister Zhou Enlai of the State Council of the Central People's Government presented the medals on behalf of the Central People's Government.

Those who attended the awarding ceremony included Minister of Railways Teng Daiyuan of the Central People's Government, Vice Minister of Foreign Affairs Wu Xiuquan, Soviet Ambassador to China P. Yudin, Vice Chairman of the Northeast People's Government Gao Chongmin, Chairman of the National Committee of the Chinese Railway Trade Union Li Jiebo, as well as directors and supervisors of the board of directors and board of supervisors of the former China Changchun Railway Company, responsible cadres of the Harbin Railway Administration, representatives of railway workers in the Harbin area, and other guests, totaling nearly a thousand people.

After a brief speech by Premier and Foreign Minister Zhou Enlai, he personally awarded commemorative medals to approximately two hundred Soviet experts, including M.S. Erogov, N.A. Grunichev, and N.G. Vasilyev, and shook hands with each of them. The commemorative medals for the remaining Soviet experts who were unable to attend the ceremony will be forwarded by M.S. Erogov.

When receiving the commemorative medal, Erogov delivered a speech of thanks on behalf of all Soviet experts of the former China Changchun Railway. Ye Niujin, former director of the Second Branch of the China Changchun Railway, and Poljakov, former director of the Finance Department of the China Changchun Railway, also delivered brief speeches of thanks when receiving the commemorative medals. After all the Soviet experts attending the ceremony received their awards, Ambassador P. Yudin delivered a speech of thanks on behalf of the Soviet government and the awarded Soviet experts.

Minister of Railways Teng Daiyuan of the Central People's Government and Yu Guangsheng, Chairman of the Board of Directors of the former China Changchun Railway Company, delivered congratulatory messages to the awarded Soviet experts successively.

After the awarding ceremony, Premier and Foreign Minister Zhou Enlai also took a group photo with the awarded Soviet experts as a souvenir.

### 周恩来总理兼外长代表我中央人民政府  授予中长铁路苏联专家纪念章

1953-01-03
第1版()
专栏：

    周恩来总理兼外长代表我中央人民政府
    授予中长铁路苏联专家纪念章
    【新华社二日讯】中央人民政府为了感谢和纪念原中国长春铁路一千三百余名苏联专家在中苏共管中国长春铁路期间的工作成绩以及对中国人民铁路建设事业的巨大帮助，特授予他们以“中国长春铁路纪念章”，并于一九五三年一月一日下午六时在哈尔滨铁路文化馆举行授章典礼，由中央人民政府政务院周恩来总理兼外交部部长代表中央人民政府授章。
    出席授章典礼的有中央人民政府铁道部部长滕代远、外交部副部长伍修权、苏联驻华大使潘友新、东北人民政府副主席高崇民、中国铁路工会全国委员会主席李颉伯以及原中国长春铁路公司理事会和监事会的理事和监事、哈尔滨铁路管理局负责干部和哈尔滨地区铁路职工代表以及其他来宾，约近千人。
    周恩来总理兼外交部部长简短致词后，即亲自向姆·斯·叶洛果夫、恩·阿·格鲁尼切夫、恩·格·华西里耶夫等约二百名苏联专家授予纪念章，并一一与之握手。给其余未能出席典礼的苏联专家的纪念章，将由姆·斯·叶洛果夫转授。
    叶洛果夫于领受纪念章时，代表原中国长春铁路全体苏联专家致谢词。原中国长春铁路第二分局局长叶牛金和原中国长春铁路财务处处长波里雅科夫在领受纪念章时，也都致了简短谢词。全体出席典礼的苏联专家受奖后，潘友新大使代表苏联政府及受章的苏联专家致谢词。
    中央人民政府铁道部部长滕代远和原中国长春铁路公司理事会主席余光生，先后向受章的苏联专家致贺词。
    授章典礼礼成后，周恩来总理兼外交部部长并与受章的苏联专家合影，留作纪念。
