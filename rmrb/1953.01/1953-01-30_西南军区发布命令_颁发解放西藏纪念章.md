Title: Southwest Military Region Issues Order: Awarding of the Liberation of Tibet Medal
Author:
Date: 1953-01-30
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Chongqing, January 29th: The Southwest Military Region of the Chinese People's Liberation Army has issued an order for the awarding of the Liberation of Tibet Medal. The order states: "Under the wise leadership of Chairman Mao and the Central People's Government, all commanders, fighters, logistics personnel, engineering and technical personnel, and local personnel accompanying the army into Tibet in the Southwest Military Region of the Chinese People's Liberation Army have correctly implemented the central government's ethnic policies and have demonstrated the spirit of fearing no hardship and fighting heroically. They have overcome various natural difficulties in the Tibet region and have successfully completed the glorious and great task of peacefully liberating Tibet. In order to commemorate this historically significant victory, with the approval of the People's Revolutionary Military Commission of the Central People's Government, the Liberation of Tibet Medal is hereby awarded."

The theme of the Liberation of Tibet Medal is "Planting the Flag of Mao Zedong on the Summit of Mount Everest." The upper center of the medal's design features a bright red flag, under which is a radiant red five-pointed star. Below the five-pointed star is a map of the People's Republic of China. The flagpole is planted in the middle of the Tibetan plateau mountain range shown on the map. Golden wheat ears on both sides of the medal's edge hang down and connect with each other in the center of the lower end. At the bottom of the design, the six characters "Liberation of Tibet Commemoration" are written in both Han and Tibetan languages.

### 西南军区发布命令  颁发解放西藏纪念章

1953-01-30
第1版()
专栏：

    西南军区发布命令
    颁发解放西藏纪念章
    【新华社重庆二十九日电】中国人民解放军西南军区发布颁发解放西藏纪念章的命令。命令说：西南军区全体指挥员、战斗员、后勤工作人员、工程技术人员和随军进入西藏的地方工作人员，在毛主席和中央人民政府的英明领导下，正确执行了中央的民族政策和高度发扬了我军不怕艰苦、英勇奋斗的精神，战胜了西藏地区自然界各种困难，顺利地完成和平解放西藏的光荣伟大的任务。为了纪念这一具有历史意义的胜利，经呈准中央人民政府人民革命军事委员会，颁发解放西藏纪念章。
    解放西藏纪念章以“把毛泽东的旗帜插在珠穆朗玛山峰”为主题。纪念章图面正中上端为一面鲜艳的红旗，旗下为放射着光芒的红色五角星。五角星之下为中华人民共和国全图。红旗旗杆插在图中显出的西藏高原山脉中间。纪念章两侧边缘金黄色的麦穗垂向下端中央互相衔接。图面下方用汉藏两种文字写着：“解放西藏纪念”六字。
