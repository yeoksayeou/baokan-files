Title: North Korea Reforms Administrative System
Author:
Date: 1953-01-03
Page: 4

    Xinhua News Agency, Pyongyang: The Standing Committee of the Supreme People's Assembly of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea, in order to reform the administrative system in the northern half of Korea and bring higher-level leadership closer to lower levels, passed a decree on December 22, 1952, to abolish the "myeon" (township) level in the "do" (province), "si" (city), "gun" (county), "myeon," and "ri" (village) administrative system. At the same time, in order to rapidly implement state policies, it also passed a decree on adjusting administrative regions, deciding to reduce the size of the former "gun," expand the "ri," and designate the center of the "gun" as "eup" (town); and decided to designate the "ri" of factories, mines, and fishing villages (limited to those with more than 400 adults, of whom 65% or more rely on wage income as their basic source of livelihood) as workers' districts.

### 朝鲜改革行政体制

1953-01-03
第4版()
专栏：

    朝鲜改革行政体制
    【新华社平壤电】朝鲜民主主义人民共和国最高人民会议常任委员会，为了改革朝鲜北半部地区的行政体制，使上级领导接近下级，在一九五二年十二月二十二日通过了关于在“道”、“市”、“郡”、“面”、“里”行政体制中取消“面”的政令。同时，为了迅速地执行国家政策，又通过调整政区的政令，决定缩小从前的“郡”，扩大“里”，将“郡”的中心称为“邑”；并决定将工厂、矿山、渔村的“里”
    （限于居有四百名以上的成人，而其中百分之六十五以上是以工资收入为基本生活来源者），定为劳动者区。
