Title: China and Romania Sign This Year's Agreement on Exchange of Goods and Payments
Author:
Date: 1953-01-20
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, January 19th—After friendly and harmonious negotiations, the Ministry of Foreign Trade of the Central People's Government of the People's Republic of China and the Economic Delegation of the Government of the People's Republic of Romania signed the China-Romania Agreement on the Exchange of Goods and Payments for 1953 in Beijing on January 19, 1953. The trade volume stipulated in this agreement is more than 200% higher than in 1952, fully demonstrating the great development of trade between China and Romania.
    Lei Renmin, Acting Minister of the Ministry of Foreign Trade of the Central People's Government of the People's Republic of China, signed the agreement on behalf of China. Stanciu, Head of the Economic Delegation of the Government of the People's Republic of Romania, signed the agreement on behalf of Romania.
    The following people attended the signing ceremony: From the Chinese side were Vice Minister of Foreign Affairs Wu Xiuquan, Vice Minister of Foreign Trade Xu Xuehan, Director of the Soviet Union and Eastern Europe Department of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs Xu Yixin, Director of the Import Bureau of the Ministry of Foreign Trade Zhang Huadong, Deputy Director of the Export Bureau Fu Shenglin, Deputy Director of the Second Bureau Yi Qintang, Director of the Protocol Department Yang Mian, and Deputy Director of the Protocol Department of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs Shen Ping, among others.
    From the Romanian side were Romanian Ambassador to China Guo Zuowen, Deputy Head of the Economic Delegation of the Government of the People's Republic of Romania Anghel, Counselor of the Romanian Embassy in China Szilard, Economic Counselor Andon, First Secretary Mentianu, and all members of the Economic Delegation.

### 中罗签订今年交换货物及付款协定

1953-01-20
第1版()
专栏：

    中罗签订今年交换货物及付款协定
    【新华社十九日讯】中华人民共和国中央人民政府对外贸易部和罗马尼亚人民共和国政府经济代表团之间，经过友好与融洽的谈判，于一九五三年一月十九日在北京签订了中罗一九五三年交换货物及付款协定。这个协定所规定的贸易额比一九五二年增加了百分之二百以上，充分显示了中罗两国间贸易的很大发展。
    代表中国方面签订这个协定的是中华人民共和国中央人民政府对外贸易部代理部长雷任民；代表罗马尼亚方面签订这个协定的是罗马尼亚人民共和国政府经济代表团团长斯坦裘。
    参加签字典礼的：中国方面有外交部副部长伍修权，对外贸易部副部长徐雪寒，外交部苏联东欧司司长徐以新，对外贸易部进口局局长张化东、出口局副局长傅生麟、第二局副局长依钦堂、交际处处长杨勉，外交部交际处副处长沈平等人。
    罗马尼亚方面参加典礼的有：罗马尼亚驻华大使郭佐文、罗马尼亚人民共和国政府经济代表团副团长安格尔、罗马尼亚驻华大使馆参赞席拉德、经济参赞安定、一等秘书孟天努及经济代表团全体团员。
