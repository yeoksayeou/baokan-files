Title: Qinghai Guoluo District, Gannan Pastoral Area Visiting Group Leaves Beijing to Visit Eastern Inner Mongolia Pastoral Area
Author:
Date: 1953-01-25
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, January 24th—The Qinghai Guoluo District Tibetan Visiting Group and the Southern Gansu Pastoral Area Ethnic Groups Visiting Group, totaling 111 people, left Beijing on the 24th to visit the Hulunbuir League and other pastoral areas in eastern Inner Mongolia Autonomous Region. Those who came to the station to see them off included Liu Geping, Deputy Chairman of the Nationalities Affairs Commission of the Central People's Government, Gan Chunlei, Deputy Director of the General Office, Yan Xinmin, Director of the Counsellors' Office, Lian Huizhu, Deputy Director of the Panchen Lama's Beijing Office, and representatives of minority students from the Central Committee of the Chinese New Democratic Youth League Central Youth League School, totaling more than 50 people.

### 青海果洛区、甘南牧区参观团  离京去内蒙古东部牧区参观

1953-01-25
第1版()
专栏：

    青海果洛区、甘南牧区参观团
    离京去内蒙古东部牧区参观
    【新华社二十四日讯】青海果洛区藏族参观团和甘肃南部牧区各民族参观团共一百一十一人，于二十四日离北京前往内蒙古自治区东部牧区呼纳盟等地参观。到车站欢送的有中央人民政府民族事务委员会副主任委员刘格平、办公厅副主任甘春雷、参事室主任严信民、班禅驻京办事处副处长联慧珠和中国新民主主义青年团中央团校少数民族班学员代表共五十多人。
