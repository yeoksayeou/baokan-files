Title: China and Ceylon Governments Issue Communiqué on the Approval of Trade Agreements
Author:
Date: 1953-01-04
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, 3rd.

    China and Ceylon Governments Issue
    Communiqué on the Approval of Trade Agreements

    [Xinhua News Agency, January 3rd] The communiqué of the Governments of the People's Republic of China and Ceylon on the approval of trade agreements between the two countries. The text is as follows:

    The Central People's Government of the People's Republic of China and the Government of Ceylon have approved the following agreements recently negotiated in Beijing by the representatives of the two governments: (1) the General Trade Agreement signed in Beijing on October 4, 1952; (2) the Five-Year Trade Agreement on the Sale and Purchase of Rubber and Rice signed in Beijing on December 18, 1952, and the two contracts for Rubber and Rice for the year 1953.

    According to the understanding between the two governments, the above agreements shall enter into force between the two governments as of January 1, 1953.

### 中锡两国政府发布  关于批准两国贸易协定的公报

1953-01-04
第1版()
专栏：

    中锡两国政府发布
    关于批准两国贸易协定的公报
    【新华社三日讯】中锡两国政府关于批准两国贸易协定的公报。文如下：
    中华人民共和国中央人民政府和锡兰政府已批准两国政府的代表最近在北京商谈的下列协定：（一）一九五二年十月四日于北京签订的一般贸易协定；（二）一九五二年十二月十八日于北京签订的关于买卖橡胶大米五年贸易协定和一九五三年度关于橡胶大米的两个合同。
    根据两国政府之间的谅解，以上各协定将自一九五三年一月一日起在两国政府之间生效。
