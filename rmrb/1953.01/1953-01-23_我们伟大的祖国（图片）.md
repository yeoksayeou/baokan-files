    Our Great Motherland
    The People's Government is extremely concerned about returned overseas Chinese. The Wanqingsha Overseas Chinese Collective Farm in Dongguan County, Guangdong Province, is specifically established to resettle compatriots returning from Southeast Asia. After a bumper harvest on the farm, people happily dance a collective dance to celebrate their happy life.

### 我们伟大的祖国（图片）

1953-01-23
第1版()
专栏：

    我们伟大的祖国
    人民政府对于归国的华侨，极为关心。广东省东莞县万顷沙归国华侨集体农场，是专为安置从南洋一带回国的侨胞而设立的。在农场丰收之后，人们快乐地跳着集体舞，庆祝幸福的生活。
