Title: The Government Administration Council Holds its 165th Meeting
Author: 
Date: 1953-01-09
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, January 8th—The 165th session of the Central People's Government Administration Council was held on the afternoon of the 2nd. After listening to the explanation given by Li Lisan, Minister of the Central People's Government Labor Department, the meeting approved the "Decision on Several Revisions to the Labor Insurance Regulations of the People's Republic of China" and the revised “Labor Insurance Regulations of the People's Republic of China.” The meeting also approved various appointment and removal cases and submitted appointment and removal cases for approval by the Central People's Government Committee.

### 政务院举行第一六五次会议

1953-01-09
第1版()
专栏：

    政务院举行第一六五次会议
    【新华社八日讯】中央人民政府政务院第一百六十五次政务会议在二日下午举行。会议在听取了中央人民政府劳动部李立三部长所作的说明后，通过了关于中华人民共和国劳动保险条例若干修改的决定及修正后的中华人民共和国劳动保险条例。会议并通过了各项任免案及提请中央人民政府委员会批准任免案。
