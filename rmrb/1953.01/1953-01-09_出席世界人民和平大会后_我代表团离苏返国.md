Title: After Attending the World People's Peace Congress, Our Delegation Leaves the Soviet Union to Return Home
Author:
Date: 1953-01-09
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, 8th. TASS, Moscow, 8th: After attending the World People's Peace Congress, delegations from various countries, invited by the Soviet Society for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries to visit the Soviet Union, departed from Moscow on the 7th en route to Beijing. Among these delegations were those from China, Korea, Vietnam, Burma, Australia, and other countries in the Pacific region.

The delegation from the Mongolian People's Republic has already returned home.

During their stay in the Soviet Union, the delegations visited Moscow and Leningrad, and toured the peaceful labor and achievements of the Soviet people.

At the railway station to see them off were Soviet scientists, cultural and artistic workers, representatives of the Moscow public, members of the Soviet Peace Committee, leading cadres of the Society for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries, and officials from many embassies in Moscow.

### 出席世界人民和平大会后  我代表团离苏返国

1953-01-09
第4版()
专栏：

    出席世界人民和平大会后
    我代表团离苏返国
    【新华社八日讯】塔斯社莫斯科八日讯：出席世界人民和平大会后应苏联对外文化协会邀请抵苏联参观的各国代表团，已在七日离莫斯科启程赴北京。这些代表团中有中国、朝鲜、越南、缅甸、澳大利亚和太平洋区域其他国家的代表团。
    蒙古人民共和国代表团已经返国。
    各国代表团在苏联逗留期间，访问了莫斯科和列宁格勒，参观了苏联人民的和平劳动和成就。
    在火车站欢送他们的有苏联科学、文化及艺术工作者，莫斯科的公众代表、苏联拥护和平委员会委员、对外文化协会的负责干部以及许多国家驻莫斯科大使馆的官员。
