"Factory construction" is an important method to accelerate capital construction. The Harbin No. 5 Engineering Affiliated Mechanized Wood Factory of the Central Heavy Industry Ministry Construction Engineering Company produces a large number of semi-finished wood products and unified-specification doors, windows, and stairs for various construction sites, accelerating the progress of the projects. This is a scene of the factory's manufacturing workers assembling doors and windows.

### 我们伟大的祖国（图片）

1953-01-10
第1版()
专栏：

    我们伟大的祖国
    “工厂化施工”是加速基本建设的重要方法。中央重工业部建设工程公司哈尔滨第五工程附属机械化木工厂，生产大量的木材半成品和统一规格的门、窗、楼梯等，供给各工地，加速了工程的进度。这是该厂制配工人正在装配门、窗的情形。
