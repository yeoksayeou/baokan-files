Title: Establishment of the Central South Administrative Committee
Author:
Date: 1953-01-22
Page: 1

    Xinhua News Agency, Wuhan, January 21st: According to the appointment of the 21st meeting of the Central People's Government Committee, Lin Biao, Chairman of the Central South Administrative Committee, issued an order on the 21st, announcing the official establishment of the Central South Administrative Committee. The new seal will be put into use, and official business will commence. The original Central South Military and Political Committee was simultaneously abolished by order.

### 中南行政委员会成立

1953-01-22
第1版()
专栏：

    中南行政委员会成立
    【新华社武汉二十一日电】根据中央人民政府委员会第二十一次会议任命，中南行政委员会主席林彪在二十一日发布命令，宣布中南行政委员会正式成立，并启用新印信，开始办公。原中南军政委员会同时奉令撤销。
