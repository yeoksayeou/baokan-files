Title: State Council Financial and Economic Committee Cables Reply to All Staff and Workers of Anshan Iron and Steel Company, Congratulating Them on Completing the 1952 Production Increase Plan for Major Products
Author:
Date: 1953-01-11
Page: 1

    State Council Financial and Economic Committee Cables Reply to All Staff and Workers of Anshan Iron and Steel Company
    Congratulating Them on Completing the 1952 Production Increase Plan for Major Products
    [This newspaper reports] After receiving the good news that the state-owned Anshan Iron and Steel Company had completed its 1952 production increase plan for major products ahead of schedule, the Financial and Economic Committee of the Central People's Government's State Council sent a reply cable to all staff and workers of the state-owned Anshan Iron and Steel Company on January 5th to congratulate them. The full text of the congratulatory telegram is as follows: To Comrade Ma Bin, General Manager of the State-owned Anshan Iron and Steel Company, Deputy General Managers Hua Ming and Wang Yuqing, and to all staff and worker comrades:
    We have received your good news of December 29, 1952. Under the assistance of Soviet experts, you have displayed a high degree of creativity and labor enthusiasm, and completed the 1952 production increase plan for major products ahead of schedule. We extend our congratulations to you. We hope that you will further implement the suggestions of Soviet experts, conscientiously promote important advanced experiences such as the "full air, fast material, smooth operation method" and the "rapid steelmaking method", and strive to complete the 1953 plan.
    Financial and Economic Committee of the Central People's Government's State Council
    January 5, 1953

### 政务院财政经济委员会电复鞍钢全体职工  祝贺完成一九五二年主要产品的增产计划

1953-01-11
第1版()
专栏：

    政务院财政经济委员会电复鞍钢全体职工
    祝贺完成一九五二年主要产品的增产计划
    【本报讯】中央人民政府政务院财政经济委员会，收到国营鞍山钢铁公司提前完成一九五二年主要产品增产计划的捷报后，特于一月五日复电祝贺国营鞍山钢铁公司全体职工。贺电全文如下：国营鞍山钢铁公司马宾总经理，华明、王玉清副总经理，并转全体职工同志：
    收到了你们一九五二年十二月二十九日的捷报。你们在苏联专家的帮助下，发挥了高度的创造性和劳动热情，提前完成了一九五二年主要产品的增产计划，特向你们祝贺。并希望你们更进一步贯彻执行苏联专家的建议，认真推广“全风快料顺行法”、“快速炼钢法”等重要先进经验，为完成一九五三年计划而努力。
    中央人民政府政务院财政经济委员会
    一九五三年一月五日
