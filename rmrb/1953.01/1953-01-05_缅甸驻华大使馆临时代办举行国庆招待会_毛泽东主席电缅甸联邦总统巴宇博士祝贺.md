Title: Burmese Chargé d'Affaires in China Holds National Day Reception; Chairman Mao Zedong Sends Congratulatory Telegram to President Ba U of the Union of Burma
Author:
Date: 1953-01-05
Page: 1

    Xinhua News Agency, 4th. January—The fifth National Day of the Union of Burma was on January 4th. The Chargé d'Affaires ad interim of the Union of Burma Embassy in China, U Myint Toe, held a reception in Beijing.

    Those invited to attend were: Vice Premier of the Central People's Government Administration Council Dong Biwu, Minister of Agriculture of the Central People's Government Li Shucheng, Vice Minister of Foreign Affairs Zhang Hanfu, Vice Minister of Culture Ding Xilin, President of the Sino-Burmese Friendship Association Zheng Zhenduo, Vice Mayor of Beijing Wu Han, and over two hundred others.

    Also invited to attend were the heads of diplomatic missions and diplomats from various countries in China.

    The reception proceeded in a friendly atmosphere.

    In addition: Chairman Mao Zedong of our Central People's Government sent a telegram to President Ba U of the Union of Burma on January 2nd, congratulating him on Burma's National Day.

### 缅甸驻华大使馆临时代办举行国庆招待会  毛泽东主席电缅甸联邦总统巴宇博士祝贺

1953-01-05
第1版()
专栏：

    缅甸驻华大使馆临时代办举行国庆招待会
    毛泽东主席电缅甸联邦总统巴宇博士祝贺
    【新华社四日讯】一月四日是缅甸联邦第五届国庆。缅甸联邦驻华大使馆临时代办吴蜜栋特在北京举行招待会。
    应邀到会的有：中央人民政府政务院副总理董必武，中央人民政府农业部部长李书城，外交部副部长章汉夫，文化部副部长丁西林，中缅友好协会会长郑振铎，北京市人民政府副市长吴晗等二百余人。
    应邀到会的还有各国驻华使节和外交官。
    招待会在友好的气氛中进行。
    又讯：我中央人民政府毛泽东主席于一月二日致电缅甸联邦总统巴宇博士，祝贺缅甸国庆。
