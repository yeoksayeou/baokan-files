Title: The Central Ministry of Internal Affairs Announces 1953 Standards for Preferential Treatment
Author:
Date: 1953-01-25
Page: 2

    To guarantee and appropriately improve the lives of the families of revolutionary martyrs, the families of revolutionary soldiers, and disabled revolutionary soldiers, the Ministry of Internal Affairs of the Central People's Government has appropriately adjusted the various standards for preferential treatment and pensions, and formulated the "1953 Standards for Various Preferential Treatment and Pensions." This standard has been approved by the Government Administration Council for implementation in 1953 and has been announced by the Ministry of Internal Affairs. The full text of the standard is as follows:

     1953 Standards for Various Preferential Treatment and Pensions (Unit: 10,000 Yuan) (Attached Chart)

### 中央内务部公布一九五三年优抚标准

1953-01-25
第2版()
专栏：

    中央内务部公布一九五三年优抚标准
    为了保证和适当地改善革命烈士家属、革命军人家属和革命残废军人的生活，中央人民政府内务部将各项优待和抚恤标准予以适当调整，制订了“一九五三年各项优待抚恤标准”。该标准业经政务院批准在一九五三年内执行，并已由内务部公布。该标准全文如下：
     一九五三年各项优待抚恤标准  （以万元为单位）（附图表）
