Title: Finnish Trade Delegation Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1953-01-27
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, January 26th

    Finnish Trade Delegation Arrives in Beijing
    [Xinhua News Agency, January 26th] The Finnish Republic trade delegation, including representative Hukkanen, experts Kuren and Brandt, arrived in Beijing at 4 p.m. on the 26th.
    Those who greeted them at the airport were: Liu Xiwen, Deputy Director of the Third Bureau of the Ministry of Foreign Trade of the Central People's Government, Section Chief Sun Liji, and Section Chief Zhou Liecheng of the Reception Department.
    Also greeting them at the airport were: Finnish Envoy to China and Head of the Trade Delegation, Mr. Knorring, and Commercial Secretary of the Finnish Legation in China, Mr. Martila.

### 芬兰贸易代表团抵京

1953-01-27
第1版()
专栏：

    芬兰贸易代表团抵京
    【新华社二十六日讯】芬兰共和国贸易代表团代表希卡能、专家柯伦及勃兰特等一行三人于二十六日下午四时到达北京。
    到机场迎接的有：中央人民政府对外贸易部第三局副局长刘希文，科长孙立基，交际处科长周烈承。
    到机场迎接的还有：芬兰驻华公使兼贸易代表团团长克诺林，及芬兰驻华公使馆商务秘书马狄拉等。
