Title: "Chinese New Year Pictures" Exhibition Held in Poland
Author: 
Date: 1953-01-03
Page: 4

    Xinhua News Agency, Warsaw, 2nd. According to a report from the Polish News Agency in Warsaw: The "Chinese New Year Pictures" exhibition organized by the Polish Foreign Cultural Committee opened in Warsaw on December 30, 1952. The opening ceremony was attended by Mr. S. Skrzeszewski, Minister of Foreign Affairs of the Polish People's Republic, Mr. Jan Wende, Secretary-General of the Polish Foreign Cultural Committee, and representatives from cultural circles in Warsaw. Also present at the opening ceremony were Mr. Zeng Yongquan, Ambassador of the People's Republic of China to Poland, and other members of the Embassy. Mr. Gulikiewicz, a famous Polish painter and member of the Polish cultural delegation who had just returned to Warsaw from China, delivered the opening speech. He said that this exhibition would greatly promote the further dissemination of Chinese culture among the Polish people and further enhance cooperation and friendly relations between the people of China and Poland.
    The exhibition displays seventy-six reproduced colorful New Year pictures by modern Chinese artists. Many of the paintings praise the Chinese Communist Party, praise the Chinese people's leader Chairman Mao Zedong, show the eternal friendship between the Chinese and Soviet people, and introduce the struggle to defend peace.

### 波兰举办“中国新年画”展览会

1953-01-03
第4版()
专栏：

    波兰举办“中国新年画”展览会
    【新华社二日讯】据波兰通讯社华沙消息：波兰对外文化委员会举办的“中国新年画”展览会在一九五二年十二月三十日在华沙揭幕。参加揭幕式的有波兰人民共和国外交部部长斯·斯克热歇夫斯基，波兰对外文化委员会秘书长杨·温德和华沙文化界的代表们。参加揭幕式的还有中华人民共和国驻波兰大使曾涌泉和大使馆其他人员。刚从中国返回华沙的波兰文化代表团团员之一、波兰名画家古力谢维兹致开幕词，他说，这个展览会对于在波兰人民中进一步宣传中国的文化，对于进一步增强中波两国人民之间的合作和友好关系，都有很大的促进作用。
    展览会中陈列着七十六幅复制的中国现代艺术家所作的五彩年画。其中许多画幅的题材是歌颂中国共产党，歌颂中国人民领袖毛泽东主席，显示中苏人民的永恒友谊和介绍保卫和平的斗争的。
