    Our Great Motherland
    The Zhangjiakou Mining Machinery Factory has successfully manufactured Soviet-style surface loaders. This loader can accelerate the loading of rocks in mines and greatly reduce the labor intensity of loading workers. This is a scene of the factory workers configuring the surface loader.

### 我们伟大的祖国（图片）

1953-01-20
第1版()
专栏：

    我们伟大的祖国
    张家口矿山机械厂制造苏联式的地面装车机已经获得成功。这种装车机可以加速矿山石块的装车工作，大大地减轻装车工人的劳动强度。这是该厂工人正在配制地面装车机的情形。
