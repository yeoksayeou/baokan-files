Title: Exhibition on US Military's Crimes of Germ Warfare to Open in Berlin Next Week
Author: 
Date: 1953-01-15
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Berlin, January 14th: The Chinese delegation, headed by Bai Xiqing, responsible for exhibiting the evidence of the US military's crimes of germ warfare, arrived in Berlin on January 10th from Vienna. Greeting them at the station were: Ernst Goldenbaum, member of the Presidium of the National Front of Democratic Germany, as well as representatives from the National Front of Democratic Germany, the Aid Korea Committee of the National Committee of the National Front of Democratic Germany, the German Peace Committee, and representatives of the government of the German Democratic Republic. Personnel from the diplomatic mission of the People's Republic of China in Germany also welcomed them at the station. This exhibition was held in Vienna from December 15th to 19th, 1952, during the World People's Peace Congress. Peace delegates from various countries, as well as people from Vienna and the Austrian occupation zones, visited the exhibition and saw various powerful pieces of evidence of the US imperialists' crimes of waging germ warfare in Korea and Northeast China. Many visitors wrote their opinions in various languages in the opinion book, expressing their indignation at this cruel crime, their strong protest against the US government, and their determination to intensify the struggle for peace and to protect mankind from the threat of artificial infectious diseases.
	The Berlin exhibition is scheduled to open next week in the famous "Berolina" House on Alexanderplatz in the center of Berlin.

### 美军进行细菌战罪行展览会  下周在柏林揭幕

1953-01-15
第4版()
专栏：

    美军进行细菌战罪行展览会
    下周在柏林揭幕
    【新华社柏林十四日电】由白希清率领的负责展出美军进行细菌战罪行展览会的我国代表团于一月十日自维也纳抵达柏林。到车站欢迎他们的有：民主德国全国阵线主席团委员恩内斯特·戈登堡姆以及民主德国全国阵线、民主德国全国阵线全国委员会援朝委员会、德国和平委员会的人士和德意志民主共和国政府的代表。中华人民共和国驻德外交使团人员也到车站欢迎他们。这个展览会在世界人民和平大会举行会议期间曾于一九五二年十二月十五日到十九日在维也纳举行。来自各国的和平代表们以及维也纳和奥地利各占领区的人民都曾前往参观，看到了美帝国主义者在朝鲜及中国东北发动细菌战的罪行的各种有力的证据。许多参观的人用各种文字在意见簿上写下了他们的意见，他们对这种残忍的罪行表示愤恨，对美国政府表示强烈的抗议，而且他们表示决心要为争取和平与保卫人类免受人为的传染病的威胁而加紧斗争。
    柏林的展览会定于下星期在柏林市中心亚历山大广场上著名的“柏罗林纳”大厦揭幕。
