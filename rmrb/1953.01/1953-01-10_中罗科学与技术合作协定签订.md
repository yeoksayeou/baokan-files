Title: Sino-Romanian Scientific and Technological Cooperation Agreement Signed
Author:
Date: 1953-01-10
Page: 1

    Sino-Romanian Scientific and Technological Cooperation Agreement Signed
    [Xinhua News Agency, 9th] The Scientific and Technological Cooperation Agreement between the People's Republic of China and the Romanian People's Republic was signed in Beijing on the 9th. Representing the Chinese side in signing was Chen Yu, Minister of Fuel Industry of the Central People's Government of the People's Republic of China, and representing the Romanian side was Stangeu, Chairman of the National Supply Committee of the Romanian People's Republic. Attending the signing ceremony on the Chinese side were: Jia Tuofu, Deputy Director of the Financial and Economic Committee of the Government Administration Council; Wu Xiuquan, Deputy Minister of Foreign Affairs; Qian Zhiguang, Deputy Minister of Textile Industry; Xu Yixin, Director of the Soviet and Eastern European Affairs Department of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs; Wen Ning, Deputy Director; Dong Xibai, Chief Secretary of the Treaty Committee; Wang Zhuoru, Director of the Protocol Department; and Li Zhuozhi, our negotiating representative. On the Romanian side were: Guo Zuowen, Romanian Ambassador to China; Angel, Deputy Head of the Romanian Government Economic Delegation; negotiating representatives Tragmir and Anding; and Silad, Counselor of the Romanian Embassy in China, and Mentiannu, First Secretary.

### 中罗科学与技术合作协定签订

1953-01-10
第1版()
专栏：

    中罗科学与技术合作协定签订
    【新华社九日讯】中华人民共和国和罗马尼亚人民共和国科学与技术合作协定已于九日在北京签订。代表中国方面签字的为中华人民共和国中央人民政府燃料工业部部长陈郁，代表罗马尼亚方面签字的为罗马尼亚人民共和国国家供应委员会主席斯坦裘。参加签字典礼的，我方有：政务院财政经济委员会副主任贾拓夫，外交部副部长伍修权，纺织工业部副部长钱之光，外交部苏联东欧司司长徐以新、副司长温宁，条约委员会主任秘书董希白，交际处处长王倬如及我方谈判代表李琢之等。罗方有：罗马尼亚驻华大使郭佐文，罗马尼亚政府经济代表团副团长安格尔，谈判代表特拉格米尔和安定及罗马尼亚驻华大使馆参赞席拉德和一等秘书孟天努。
