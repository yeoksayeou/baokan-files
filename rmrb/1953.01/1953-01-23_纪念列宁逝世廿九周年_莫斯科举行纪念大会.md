Title: Moscow Holds Commemorative Meeting on the 29th Anniversary of Lenin's Death
Author:
Date: 1953-01-23
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, January 22nd: According to TASS, the Soviet people solemnly commemorate the 29th anniversary of the death of Lenin, the great leader and teacher of the world's working people. On the evening of the 21st, members of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of the Soviet Union, members of the Moscow Oblast and Moscow City Committees, members of the Presidium of the Supreme Soviet of the Soviet Union and the Presidium of the Supreme Soviet of the Russian Soviet Federative Socialist Republic, ministers of various ministries of the Soviet government, representatives of the Soviets of Working People's Deputies of Moscow Oblast and Moscow City, members of the Central Committee of the All-Union Leninist Young Communist League, members of the Moscow Oblast and Moscow City Committees, and members of the Presidium of the All-Union Central Council of Trade Unions, together with representatives of other organizations, people's groups, and the Soviet army of the Communist Party of the Soviet Union, held a commemorative meeting at the Bolshoi Theatre in Moscow. Celebrities from the scientific, cultural, and artistic circles, as well as male and female workers from Moscow, attended the meeting. Foreign delegations visiting Moscow also attended the meeting.

	At the back of the rostrum hung a huge portrait of Lenin, surrounded by flowers. On both sides were crimson flags, marked with the years: 1924-1953. Above the rostrum hung a horizontal banner, which read: "Under the banner of Lenin, under the leadership of Stalin, advance to the victory of communism!"

	The meeting was opened by N.M. Shvernik, Chairman of the Presidium of the Supreme Soviet of the Soviet Union. He proposed that all present observe a moment of silence to commemorate Lenin, the founder of the Soviet state. After the silent tribute, N.A. Mikhailov (formerly translated as En. A. Mikhailov), Secretary of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of the Soviet Union, delivered a long report on the 29th anniversary of Lenin's death. Every time Mikhailov mentioned the name of Comrade Stalin, the great successor to Lenin's cause, the audience burst into prolonged applause. In his conclusion, Mikhailov hailed the Communist Party of the Soviet Union and Comrade Stalin, the great successor to Lenin's cause, the wise leader and teacher. His words won sustained and enthusiastic applause. The meeting closed with the singing of the Internationale.

Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, January 22nd: According to TASS, in the past few days, the people of Moscow have carried out various activities to commemorate the 29th anniversary of Lenin's death. On the 19th, workers in the Proletarsky district held a grand commemoration meeting in the Palace of Culture of the Stalin Automobile Factory. Workers in the Frunze, Molotov, and other districts also held commemoration meetings one after another. Lectures and symposiums are being held everywhere, and tens of thousands of people have visited exhibitions introducing Lenin's life. A very large number of people visited the Lenin Museum, the Lenin Mausoleum, and Lenin's former residence. People came from all corners of the Soviet state and from all corners of the world to Gorki village near Moscow to visit the place where Lenin died. Every exhibit preserved in the memorial hall of the village is a valuable relic of Lenin's life, revolutionary activities, and his great cooperation with Stalin. There are many photos, documents, pictures, and Lenin's personal belongings. The museum also preserves visitors' guestbooks, inscribed in Russian, Ukrainian, Georgian, Chinese, and English with words of love and respect for the leader of the world's working people. Among them is a touching inscription by the outstanding Chinese peace fighter Guo Moruo: "The great man's breath will forever linger in the atmosphere, and every blade of grass and tree silently expresses praise."

### 纪念列宁逝世廿九周年  莫斯科举行纪念大会

1953-01-23
第1版()
专栏：

    纪念列宁逝世廿九周年
    莫斯科举行纪念大会
    【新华社二十二日讯】据塔斯社莫斯科讯：苏联人民隆重纪念世界劳动人民的伟大领袖和导师列宁逝世二十九周年。二十一日晚，苏联共产党中央委员会委员、莫斯科州和莫斯科市委员会委员，苏联最高苏维埃主席团和俄罗斯苏维埃联邦社会主义共和国最高苏维埃主席团委员，苏联政府各部部长，莫斯科州和莫斯科市劳动人民代表苏维埃的代表，苏联列宁共产主义青年团中央委员会委员，莫斯科州和莫斯科市委员会委员，全苏工会中央理事会主席团委员，会同苏联共产党的其他组织、人民团体和苏联军队的代表在莫斯科大剧院举行纪念大会。参加会议的有科学、文化、艺术各界名人和莫斯科男女劳动者。参加会议的还有正在访问莫斯科的外国代表团。
    主席台的深处悬挂着巨幅列宁像，像的四周围着鲜花，两边是深红的旗帜，还标着年代：一九二四—一九五三。主席台上面挂着一幅横旗，上面写着：“在列宁的旗帜下，在斯大林的领导下，向共产主义的胜利前进！”
    大会由苏联最高苏维埃主席团主席尼·什维尔尼克主持开幕。他提议全场默哀追念苏维埃国家的缔造者列宁。全场肃立默哀后，苏联共产党中央委员会书记纳·阿·米哈伊洛夫（前译恩·阿·米哈依洛夫）作了关于列宁逝世二十九周年的长篇报告。每逢米哈伊洛夫提到列宁事业的伟大继承者斯大林同志的名字时，全场都响起长时间的掌声。米哈伊洛夫在结语中欢呼苏联共产党万岁，欢呼列宁事业的伟大继承人、英明的领袖和导师斯大林万岁。他的话博得了经久不息的掌声。大会在国际歌声中闭幕。
    【新华社二十二日讯】据塔斯社莫斯科讯：这几天来，莫斯科人民进行了各种纪念列宁逝世二十九周年的活动。无产者区的劳动者十九日在斯大林汽车工厂的文化宫举行了隆重的纪念会。伏龙芝区、莫洛托夫区及其他区的劳动者也先后举行了纪念会。到处都在举行演讲会、座谈会，成千成万的人参观了介绍列宁生平的展览会。到列宁博物馆、列宁陵墓、列宁故居去参观的人非常多。人们从苏维埃国家的各个角落，从全世界各个角落来到莫斯科附近的高尔克村，参观列宁逝世的地方。保存在该村纪念馆中的每一件陈列品，都是列宁的生活、革命活动以及他和斯大林的伟大合作的宝贵遗迹，那里有许多照片、文件、画片和列宁的个人所有物。馆中还保存有参观者的留言簿，上面有俄文、乌克兰文、格鲁吉亚文、中文、英文写的对全世界劳动人民领袖表示爱戴的词句。其中有中国杰出的和平战士郭沫若感人的题词：“伟人的呼息永远在大气中留传，一草一木都在沉默中表示着咏赞。”
