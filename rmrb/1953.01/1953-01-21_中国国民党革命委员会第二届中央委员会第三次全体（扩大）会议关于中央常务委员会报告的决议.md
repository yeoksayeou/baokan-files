Title: Resolution of the Third Plenary (Enlarged) Session of the Second Central Committee of the Revolutionary Committee of the Chinese Kuomintang on the Report of the Standing Committee of the Central Committee
Author: 
Date: 1953-01-21
Page: 1

    The Third Plenary (Enlarged) Session of the Second Central Committee heard the report of the Standing Committee of the Central Committee, conducted in-depth discussions in groups, and put forward several amendments and supplementary opinions. The大会 considers that the report correctly summarizes the Party's work over the past two years, points out the main achievements and shortcomings, and puts forward the current political tasks and directions of efforts. Therefore, the大会 fully agrees with this report.

    The大会 decided to publish this report to the entire Party as a guiding document for the Party. It calls on all Party members to conduct in-depth study. And it authorizes the Standing Committee of the Central Committee to instruct Party organizations at all levels, based on the spirit and content of the report, to formulate implementation plans in light of the actual work, implement them, and strive to complete the Party's political tasks!

### 中国国民党革命委员会第二届中央委员会第三次全体（扩大）会议关于中央常务委员会报告的决议

1953-01-21
第1版()
专栏：

    中国国民党革命委员会第二届中央委员会第三次全体（扩大）会议关于中央常务委员会报告的决议
    中央委员会第三次全体（扩大）会议听取了中央常务委员会的报告，分组进行了深入的讨论，提出了若干修正和补充的意见。大会认为报告正确地总结了两年来党的工作，指出了主要的成绩和缺点，提出了当前的政治任务和努力方向，因此，大会完全同意这一报告。
    大会决定将这一报告公布全党，作为党的指导文件。号召全体党员进行深入地学习。并授权中央常务委员会根据报告的精神和内容，指示各级地方组织，结合工作实际，制定实施计划，贯彻执行，为完成党的政治任务而奋斗！
