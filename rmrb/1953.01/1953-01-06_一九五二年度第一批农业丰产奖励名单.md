    First List of Agricultural Bumper Harvest Awards for 1952
    I. Bumper Harvest Counties and Model Workers
    Zhaoyuan County, Heilongjiang Province, in 1952, the total harvested area of the county was 1,405,515 mu, with an average grain yield of 229.1 jin per mu, an increase of 92.9 percent compared to 1951 and an increase of 47 percent compared to the highest historical year (1950). The honorary title of "Bumper Harvest County" is hereby awarded, along with a patriotic bumper harvest banner and a bonus of 100 million yuan.
    Ren Guodong, Secretary of the Zhaoyuan County Committee of the Chinese Communist Party, during the 1952 patriotic bumper harvest movement, went deep into the masses and the production process, carefully studying the main issues in local yield increases. He summarized and promoted the advanced cultivation methods of "wide sowing, equal spacing, thinning, and seedling protection," achieving significant results in large-area bumper harvests. The honorary title of "Patriotic Bumper Harvest Model Worker" is hereby awarded, along with a patriotic bumper harvest gold star medal.

第2版()
专栏：

    一九五二年度第一批农业丰产奖励名单
    壹、丰产县和模范工作者
    黑龙江省肇源县，一九五二年全县收获面积一、四○五、五一五市亩，每亩平均产粮二二九·一市斤，较一九五一年增产百分之九十二点九，较历史上最高年（一九五○年）每亩产量增产百分之四十七。特授予“丰产县”的光荣称号，奖给爱国丰产奖旗一面。奖金一亿元。
    中国共产党黑龙江省肇源县委员会书记任国栋，在一九五二年爱国丰产运动中，深入群众，深入生产过程，认真研究了当地增产中的主要问题。总结与推广了“宽播、等距、间苗、保苗”的先进耕作方法，获得了大面积丰产的显著成绩，特授予“爱国丰产模范工作者”的光荣称号，并奖给爱国丰产金星奖章一枚。
