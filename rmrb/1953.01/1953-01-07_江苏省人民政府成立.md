Title: Jiangsu Provincial People's Government Established
Author:
Date: 1953-01-07
Page: 1

    Jiangsu Provincial People's Government Established
    (Xinhua News Agency) The Jiangsu Provincial People's Government was officially announced to be established in Nanjing on January 1st, and held its first government committee meeting. Attending the meeting were Tan Zhenlin, Chairman of the Jiangsu Provincial People's Government, Vice Chairmen Ke Qingshi, Guan Wenwei, Leng Yu, and members of the Provincial People's Government.
    At the meeting, Tan Zhenlin, Chairman of the Jiangsu Provincial People's Government, put forward six central tasks for the people of Jiangsu to carry out this year, based on the current three major tasks of the people of the whole country, which were unanimously approved by the meeting. The meeting also discussed the draft of the "Provisional Organizational Regulations of the Provincial People's Government" and the draft resolution on adjusting the divisions of special districts, cities, and counties.
    The Jiangsu Provincial People's Government is located in Nanjing. The province covers an area of 100,000 square kilometers, with a population of about 40 million. The province has jurisdiction over eight special districts, six province-administered cities, six special district-administered cities, and seventy-one counties. In addition, there is also an administrative office and a salt area.

### 江苏省人民政府成立

1953-01-07
第1版()
专栏：

    江苏省人民政府成立
    据新华社讯：江苏省人民政府一月一日在南京正式宣布成立，并举行了首次政府委员会会议。出席会议的有江苏省人民政府主席谭震林，副主席柯庆施、管文蔚、冷遹及省人民政府委员等。
    江苏省人民政府主席谭震林在会议上根据全国人民当前的三大任务，提出了江苏人民今年进行的六项中心工作，获得了会议的一致通过。会议还讨论了省人民政府试行组织条例草案和关于调整专区及市、县区划的决议草案等。
    江苏省人民政府设于南京。全省面积十万平方公里，人口四千万左右。全省辖有八个专区、六个省属市、六个专区辖市和七十一个县。另外还有一个行政办事处和一个盐区。
