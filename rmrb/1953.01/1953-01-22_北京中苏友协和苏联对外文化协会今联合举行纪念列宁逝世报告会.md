Title: Beijing Sino-Soviet Friendship Association and Soviet Society for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries Jointly Hold Commemorative Report Meeting on Lenin's Death

Author:

Date: 1953-01-22
Page: 3

The Beijing Sino-Soviet Friendship Association and the Soviet Society for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries will jointly hold a report meeting at the Soviet Society for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries, No. 19 Nanheyan Street, at 7:00 p.m. today (the 22nd) to commemorate the 29th anniversary of Lenin's death. If any branches of the Beijing Sino-Soviet Friendship Association at all levels wish to attend the lecture, they may obtain admission tickets by presenting a letter of introduction from their branch to the Beijing Sino-Soviet Friendship Association at No. 2 Zhengyi Road, Dongjiaomin Lane.

### 北京中苏友协和苏联对外文化协会今联合举行纪念列宁逝世报告会

1953-01-22
第3版()
专栏：

    北京中苏友协和苏联对外文化协会今联合举行纪念列宁逝世报告会
    北京市中苏友好协会与苏联对外文化协会定于今日（二十二日）晚七时在南河沿十九号苏联对外文化协会联合举行纪念列宁逝世二十九周年报告会。北京市中苏友协各级支会如有前往听讲的，可持支会介绍信到东交民巷正义路二号北京市中苏友协索取入场券。
