Title: Shanghai Railway Administration Hangzhou Branch Employees Organize Transportation Team to Work in Korea

Author: 
Date: 1953-01-06
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Hangzhou, January 5th:
In response to the call of the Chinese People's Committee to Resist America and Aid Korea to continue strengthening the work of resisting America and aiding Korea, 109 employees of the Shanghai Railway Administration Hangzhou Branch formed a transportation team and left Hangzhou on the 2nd to go to the Korean front to undertake transportation work. Before their departure, more than 600 railway workers held a farewell meeting at Hangzhou Railway Station. At the meeting, the heads of the Hangzhou Regional Working Committee of the Chinese Railway Trade Union and the Hangzhou Railway Working Committee of the Chinese New Democratic Youth League unanimously encouraged the employees going to work in Korea to strive for meritorious service at the front. They also called on all employees to welcome them with practical actions to continue strengthening the work of resisting America and aiding Korea and launching patriotic labor competitions. Employees of the Zhakou Locomotive Depot guaranteed at the meeting to complete 40 to 50 percent of the first quarter's tasks in January. The conductor representatives also guaranteed to have no accidents during driving and to take good care of the families of the comrades working in Korea.

### 上海铁路管理局杭州分局职工  组织运输队赴朝鲜工作

1953-01-06
第1版()
专栏：

    上海铁路管理局杭州分局职工
    组织运输队赴朝鲜工作
    【新华社杭州五日电】为响应中国人民抗美援朝总会关于继续加强抗美援朝工作的号召，上海铁路管理局杭州分局职工一百零九人组成运输队，于二日离杭前往朝鲜前线担任运输工作。临行前，六百多铁路职工在杭州车站举行了欢送大会。会上中国铁路工会杭州分区工作委员会和中国新民主主义青年团杭州铁路工作委员会负责人一致勉励入朝工作的职工们要争取在前线立功，并号召全体职工以继续加强抗美援朝工作、开展爱国主义劳动竞赛的实际行动来欢送他们。闸口机务段职工在会上保证要在一月份完成第一季度任务的百分之四十到五十。乘务员代表也保证在行车中不出事故，并好好照顾入朝工作同志们的家属。
