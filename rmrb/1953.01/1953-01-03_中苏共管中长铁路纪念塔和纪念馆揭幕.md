Title: Inauguration of the Sino-Soviet Joint Administration of the Chinese Changchun Railway Memorial Tower and Memorial Hall
Author:
Date: 1953-01-03
Page: 1

(Xinhua News Agency, 2nd) The Sino-Soviet Joint Administration of the Chinese Changchun Railway Memorial Tower and Memorial Hall were inaugurated and opened in Harbin on January 1st at 2:00 PM and 3:00 PM, respectively.

Attending the unveiling ceremony of the memorial tower were Teng Daiyuan, representative of the Central People's Government and Minister of Railways; Pan Youxin, Soviet Ambassador to China; Dobashin, Soviet Consul General in Harbin; Li Jiebo, Chairman of the National Committee of the Chinese Railway Trade Union; Yu Guangsheng, Chairman of the Council of the former Chinese Changchun Railway Company; Vice Chairman Yelogov; Chairman of the Supervisory Board Vasilyev; Vice Chairman Li Mingzhe; Grunichev, Director of the Administration Bureau; Lu Ping, Director of the Harbin Railway Administration Bureau; and Deputy Directors Guo Lu, Zhuang Lin, Liu Yingcai, and Wang Guangwen. Also attending the unveiling ceremony were Soviet experts from the former Chinese Changchun Railway, as well as railway workers from the Harbin area and guests from various sectors, totaling over 2,000 people.

Minister Teng Daiyuan, representative of the Central People's Government, delivered a speech after cutting the ribbon for the unveiling of the memorial tower, saying: This memorial tower illustrates the extremely brilliant achievements of the Sino-Soviet joint administration of the Chinese Changchun Railway over the past three years, symbolizes the eternal friendship and cooperation between the great Chinese and Soviet peoples that is constantly consolidated and developed, and also expresses the Chinese people's eternal remembrance of the Soviet people's fraternal friendship and assistance. It is hoped that all employees of the Harbin Railway Administration Bureau will strive to carry forward and consolidate the existing achievements of the Chinese Changchun Railway in the future. Soviet Consul General in Harbin, Dobashin, then delivered a speech. He said: The Sino-Soviet Joint Administration of the Chinese Changchun Railway Memorial Tower, built on the proposal of the Government of the People's Republic of China, demonstrates the profound friendship between the railway workers of China and the Soviet Union, and also demonstrates the unbreakable great friendship between the Chinese and Soviet people. He wished all employees of the Harbin Railway Administration Bureau continued success in their work in the future, and wished them greater achievements in the economic and cultural construction of their motherland. Finally, He Jijun, Second Chairman of the Harbin District Committee of the Chinese Railway Trade Union, and Guo Lu, Deputy Director of the Harbin Railway Administration Bureau, respectively read out congratulatory telegrams from the Harbin railway workers to Comrade Stalin and Comrade Mao Zedong.

The Sino-Soviet Joint Administration of the Chinese Changchun Railway Memorial Tower was inscribed by Chairman Mao himself. The memorial tower is located in the square in front of the main entrance of the Harbin Railway Administration Bureau, with a height of 14 meters and a base area of 110 square meters. On the front of the memorial tower, there are bronze bust statues of Chairman Mao and Comrade Stalin above, and bronze statues of two Chinese and Soviet railway workers shaking hands below. On the back of the memorial tower, the bronze national emblems of China and the Soviet Union are hung on the upper left and upper right sides, respectively, and a bronze train model is cast below.

The opening of the Sino-Soviet Joint Administration of the Chinese Changchun Railway Memorial Hall was also marked by a ribbon-cutting ceremony by Minister Teng Daiyuan. After the ribbon-cutting and opening, Minister Teng Daiyuan, Chairman Li Jiebo, and Ambassador Pan Youxin immediately toured the memorial hall.

### 中苏共管中长铁路纪念塔和纪念馆揭幕

1953-01-03
第1版()
专栏：

    中苏共管中长铁路纪念塔和纪念馆揭幕
    【新华社二日讯】中苏共同管理中国长春铁路纪念塔和纪念馆先后于一月一日下午二时和三时在哈尔滨揭幕和开馆。
    参加纪念塔揭幕典礼的有中央人民政府代表、铁道部部长滕代远，苏联驻华大使潘友新，苏联驻哈尔滨总领事多巴申，中国铁路工会全国委员会主席李颉伯，原中国长春铁路公司理事会主席余光生、副主席叶洛果夫、监事会主席华西里耶夫、副主席李明哲、管理局局长格鲁尼切夫，哈尔滨铁路管理局局长陆平、副局长郭鲁、庄林、刘英才、王光文。参加纪念塔揭幕典礼的还有原中国长春铁路苏联专家以及哈尔滨地区铁路职工及各方面来宾，约二千余人。
    中央人民政府代表滕代远部长在为纪念塔揭幕剪彩后致词说：这座纪念塔，说明了三年来中苏共管中国长春铁路的极其辉煌的成就，象征着伟大的中苏两国人民永恒的友好合作不断巩固与发展，还表示着中国人民对苏联人民兄弟般的友谊援助永志不忘。希望哈尔滨铁路管理局所属全体职工今后努力发扬与巩固中国长春铁路已有的成果。继由苏联驻哈尔滨总领事多巴申致词。他说：由中华人民共和国政府提议修建的中苏共同管理中国长春铁路纪念塔，表现着中苏两国铁路职工的深厚友谊，也表现着中苏两国人民牢不可破的伟大友谊。预祝哈尔滨铁路管理局所属全体职工今后在自己的工作中继续胜利前进，并祝他们在自己祖国经济和文化建设上获得更大的成就。最后，中国铁路工会哈尔滨区委员会第二主席何计君和哈尔滨铁路管理局副局长郭鲁分别向大会宣读了哈尔滨铁路职工给斯大林同志和毛泽东同志的致敬电。
    中苏共同管理中国长春铁路纪念塔是由毛主席亲笔题名的。纪念塔位于哈尔滨铁路管理局正门前广场上，塔高十四公尺，底面一百一十平方公尺。纪念塔的正面上方有毛主席和斯大林同志的浮雕半身铜像，下方有两位互相握着手的中苏铁路职工的铜像。纪念塔的背面上左和上右方分别悬有铜质的中苏两国国徽，下方有铜铸的火车模型。
    中苏共同管理中国长春铁路纪念馆的开馆也是由滕代远部长剪彩。剪彩开馆后，滕代远部长、李颉伯主席和潘友新大使等当即进行了参观。
