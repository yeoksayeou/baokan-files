Title: Congratulating the Third Anniversary of the Establishment of Diplomatic Relations Between China and Vietnam, President Ho Chi Minh Sends a Telegram to Chairman Mao Zedong; Foreign Minister Huang Mingjian Also Sends a Telegram to Foreign Minister Zhou Enlai to Express Congratulations
Author:
Date: 1953-01-20
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, January 19th: President Ho Chi Minh of the Government of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam sent a telegram to Chairman Mao Zedong on the 18th of this month to congratulate the third anniversary of the establishment of diplomatic relations between Vietnam and China. The full text of the congratulatory telegram is as follows:
To Chairman Mao Zedong of the Central People's Government of the People's Republic of China:
	On the occasion of the third anniversary of the establishment of diplomatic relations between the People's Republic of China and the Democratic Republic of Vietnam, I would like to extend my heartfelt congratulations and respect to you, the Chinese government, and the great Chinese people on behalf of the Vietnamese people and government, and in my own name.
	I wish the Chinese government and people victory under your leadership in the great construction work that has begun with the first five-year plan, and in the cause of resisting American aggression and aiding Korea, and defending peace in Asia and the world.
	I am confident that with the sympathy and support of the Chinese people, the Vietnamese people will surely achieve victory in the struggle against imperialist aggression and for the restoration of freedom and independence, and will contribute to the cause of defending peace in Southeast Asia and the world.
	I wish you, Mr. Chairman, a long life!
	Long live the friendship between the Chinese and Vietnamese people! President Ho Chi Minh of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam

In addition: Vietnamese Foreign Minister Huang Mingjian sent a telegram to our Foreign Minister Zhou Enlai on the same day to express congratulations. The congratulatory telegram briefly stated: I firmly believe that the increasingly consolidated friendship between Vietnam and China will further strengthen the Vietnamese people's struggle against French imperialists and American interventionists for the restoration of national independence, and will contribute to the cause of defending peace in Southeast Asia and the world.

### 祝贺中越建交三周年  胡志明主席致电毛泽东主席  黄明鉴外长亦电周恩来外长表示祝贺

1953-01-20
第1版()
专栏：

    祝贺中越建交三周年
    胡志明主席致电毛泽东主席
    黄明鉴外长亦电周恩来外长表示祝贺
    【新华社十九日讯】越南民主共和国政府胡志明主席于本月十八日致电毛泽东主席祝贺越中建交三周年，贺电全文如下：中华人民共和国中央人民政府毛泽东主席：
    值此中华人民共和国和越南民主共和国建交三周年纪念之际，我谨代表越南人民和政府，并以我本人的名义向您、中国政府和伟大的中国人民致衷心的祝贺和敬意。
    谨祝中国政府和人民在您的领导下，在随着第一个五年计划而开始的伟大建设工作中、在抗美援朝和保卫亚洲及世界和平的事业中取得胜利。
    我确信，在中国人民的同情和支持下，越南人民一定能够取得反对帝国主义侵略、争取恢复自由和独立的斗争的胜利，并将对保卫东南亚和世界和平的事业有所贡献。
    我谨祝您，主席先生，万寿无疆！
    中国人民和越南人民的友谊万岁！越南民主共和国主席胡志明
    又讯：越南外交部长黄明鉴于同日致电我国周恩来外长表示祝贺，贺电略称：我坚信，越南和中国之间的日益巩固的友谊，将会进一步加强越南人民反对法国帝国主义者和美国干涉者、争取恢复民族独立的斗争，并对保卫东南亚及世界和平的事业有所贡献。
