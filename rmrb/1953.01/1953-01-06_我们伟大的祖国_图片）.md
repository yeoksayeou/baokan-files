    Our Great Motherland
    The entire project of China's newly built Taiyuan Heavy Machinery Plant was designed by Chinese engineers and technicians with the help and guidance of Soviet experts using advanced socialist technical experience. This is the large shear truck, a steel rolling machine being installed in the plant's cold working shop.

### 我们伟大的祖国(图片）

1953-01-06
第1版()
专栏：

    我们伟大的祖国
    我国新建的太原重型机器厂的全部工程，是在苏联专家以社会主义先进技术经验帮助与指导下，由中国工程师和技术人员设计的。这是该厂冷作工场正在安装的轧钢机器——大剪刀车。
