Title: Ulaanbaatar Grand Gathering Commemorates Marshal Choibalsan
Author:
Date: 1953-01-29
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Ulaanbaatar, 28th: According to TASS, the Mongolian people commemorated the first anniversary of the death of their leader, Marshal Choibalsan. On January 26th, Mongolian workers, herdsmen, and intellectuals continuously visited Choibalsan's tomb and offered many wreaths.

    Soviet Ambassador to the People's Republic of Mongolia, I.A. Ivanov, Ambassador of the People's Republic of China, Ji Yatai, and Ambassador of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea, Kim Ryong-chen, all presented wreaths.

    On the evening of January 26th, the Presidium of the Great People's Hural of the Mongolian People's Republic, the Central Committee of the Mongolian People's Revolutionary Party, the Ulaanbaatar City Hural, the Ulaanbaatar City Committee of the Mongolian People's Revolutionary Party, and representatives from party, government, people's organizations, the Mongolian Army, and working people of the entire city held a grand gathering in Ulaanbaatar to commemorate the first anniversary of Marshal Choibalsan's death. Damba, Secretary of the Central Committee of the Mongolian People's Revolutionary Party, delivered a report on "Adhering to Marshal Choibalsan's Legacy." The meeting passed a telegram to the leader of the world's working people, Joseph Vissarionovich Stalin, amidst warm applause.

### 乌兰巴托隆重集会  纪念乔巴山元帅

1953-01-29
第4版()
专栏：

    乌兰巴托隆重集会
    纪念乔巴山元帅
    【新华社二十八日讯】据塔斯社乌兰巴托讯：蒙古人民纪念他们的领袖乔巴山元帅逝世一周年。一月二十六日，蒙古工人、牧民和知识分子，源源不断地谒乔巴山墓，并献了许多花圈。
    苏联驻蒙古人民共和国大使伊凡尼科夫、中华人民共和国大使吉雅泰和朝鲜民主主义人民共和国大使金龙镇都献了花圈。
    一月二十六日晚上，蒙古人民共和国大人民呼拉尔主席团、蒙古人民革命党中央委员会、乌兰巴托市呼拉尔、蒙古人民革命党乌兰巴托市委员会以及党、政府、人民团体、蒙古陆军和全市劳动人民的代表等在乌兰巴托举行了隆重的集会，纪念乔巴山元帅逝世一周年。蒙古人民革命党中央委员会书记达姆巴作了关于
    “恪守乔巴山元帅的遗训”的报告。会议在热烈掌声中通过了致世界劳动人民领袖约·维·斯大林的电报。
