Title: Central Committee of the Mongolian People's Revolutionary Party Decides to Commemorate the Anniversary of Choibalsan's Death
Author:
Date: 1953-01-09
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Ulan Bator, January 7th: The Central Committee of the Mongolian People's Revolutionary Party passed a resolution regarding the holding of commemorations for the first anniversary of the death of Mongolian People's Leader Marshal Choibalsan (January 26th). Reports and symposiums on the life and deeds of Marshal Choibalsan will be held in enterprises, institutions, schools, and military units in various sums (counties) and baghs (districts). Commemorative meetings will be held in Ulan Bator and the central areas of various districts. The Marshal Choibalsan Mausoleum and Museum in Ulan Bator are scheduled to open on January 26th.

### 蒙古人民革命党中央决定纪念乔巴山逝世周年

1953-01-09
第4版()
专栏：

    蒙古人民革命党中央决定纪念乔巴山逝世周年
    【新华社八日讯】据塔斯社乌兰巴托七日讯：蒙古人民革命党中央委员会通过关于举行蒙古人民领袖乔巴山元帅逝世一周年（一月二十六日）纪念的决议。各苏木（县）和巴戈（区）的企业部门、机关、学校、部队将举行关于乔巴山元帅生平事迹的报告和座谈会。乌兰巴托与各区中心将举行纪念会。乌兰巴托的乔巴山元帅陵墓和博物馆定于一月二十六日开放。
