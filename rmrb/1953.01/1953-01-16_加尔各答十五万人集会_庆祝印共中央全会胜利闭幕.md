Title: Calcutta Rally of 150,000 People Celebrates the Successful Conclusion of the Enlarged Plenary Session of the CPC Central Committee
Author:
Date: 1953-01-16
Page: 4

    Xinhua News Agency, New Delhi, January 15th: A rally of 150,000 people was held in Calcutta on the 11th to celebrate the successful conclusion of the enlarged plenary session of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of India. Representatives of workers, peasants, intellectuals and patriotic bourgeois elements participated in the rally. The General Secretary of the Communist Party of India, Ajoy Ghosh, delivered a speech at the meeting and was warmly welcomed by the masses.

### 加尔各答十五万人集会  庆祝印共中央全会胜利闭幕

1953-01-16
第4版()
专栏：

    加尔各答十五万人集会
    庆祝印共中央全会胜利闭幕
    【新华社新德里十五日电】加尔各答十五万人在十一日举行大会，庆祝印度共产党中央委员会扩大全体会议胜利闭幕。参加大会的有工人、农民、知识分子和资产阶级爱国人士的代表等。印度共产党总书记高士在会上发表演说，受到群众的热烈欢迎。
