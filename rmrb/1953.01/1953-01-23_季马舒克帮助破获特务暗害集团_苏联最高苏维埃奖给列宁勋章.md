Title: Timashuk Helped Uncover a Traitorous Assassination Group; Soviet Supreme Soviet Awards Order of Lenin

Author:
Date: 1953-01-23
Page: 4

(Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, January 22nd) TASS Moscow, January 21st: The Presidium of the Supreme Soviet of the USSR issued an order: The Order of Lenin is awarded to Dr. Lydia Timashuk, because Timashuk helped the Soviet government uncover the case of the doctor murderers.

### 季马舒克帮助破获特务暗害集团  苏联最高苏维埃奖给列宁勋章

1953-01-23
第4版()
专栏：

    季马舒克帮助破获特务暗害集团
    苏联最高苏维埃奖给列宁勋章
    【新华社二十二日讯】塔斯社莫斯科二十一日讯：苏联最高苏维埃主席团颁布命令：把列宁勋章奖给丽佳·季马舒克医生，因为季马舒克帮助苏联政府破获了医生杀人犯案件。
