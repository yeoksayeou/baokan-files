Title: Our Great Motherland (Picture)
Author:
Date: 1953-01-12
Page: 1

    Our Great Motherland
    On New Year's Day in 1953, the Chinese People's Volunteers conducted several outstanding battles on the Korean front. This is an anti-aircraft artillery position of the Volunteers. The soldiers are using anti-aircraft artillery donated by the people of the motherland to shoot down American planes.

### 我们伟大的祖国（图片）

1953-01-12
第1版()
专栏：

    我们伟大的祖国
    在一九五三年的元旦，中国人民志愿军在朝鲜前线进行了好几次出色的战斗。这是志愿军的高射炮阵地。战士们正用祖国人民捐献的高射炮打中了美国的飞机。
