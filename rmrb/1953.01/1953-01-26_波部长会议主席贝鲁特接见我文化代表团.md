Title: Chairman Beirut of the Polish Council of Ministers Receives Chinese Cultural Delegation
Author:
Date: 1953-01-26
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, Warsaw, January 25th:

    Chairman Beirut of the Polish People's Republic Council of Ministers received the Chinese Government Cultural Delegation headed by Shen Yanbing, Minister of Culture of the Central People's Government of China, at the Belweder Palace in Warsaw on the 23rd. Zeng Yongquan, China's Ambassador to Poland, accompanied the delegation. Present at the reception were Polish Minister of Foreign Affairs Skrzeszewski, Minister of Higher Education Łapacki, and Secretary-General of the Polish Committee for Cultural Cooperation with Foreign Countries, Wende.
    The Chinese Government Cultural Delegation is visiting Poland for a short period to participate in the formulation of the 1953 Sino-Polish Cultural Cooperation Plan, in accordance with the provisions of the Sino-Polish Cultural Cooperation Agreement. After arriving in Warsaw on January 4, the delegation left Warsaw on the 7th and 8th to visit the famous Polish seaport of Gdańsk, where they inspected local construction projects, the Youth Palace of Culture, the Institute of Science and Technology, and other cultural institutions, and spoke with Polish sailors in Gdańsk. After returning to Warsaw from Gdańsk, the delegation laid a wreath on the 9th at the tomb of Soviet soldiers who died in the war to liberate Poland. The delegation also visited the area of Kraków, one of Poland's major cities, and the Silesia region. In the Kraków area, they visited Jagiellonian University, Poland's oldest university, other cultural institutions, and "Nowa Huta," the Polish steel center under construction.

### 波部长会议主席贝鲁特接见我文化代表团

1953-01-26
第1版()
专栏：

    波部长会议主席贝鲁特接见我文化代表团
    【新华社华沙二十五日电】波兰人民共和国部长会议主席贝鲁特二十三日在华沙贝尔魏德宫接见以我国中央人民政府文化部部长沈雁冰为首的中华人民共和国政府文化代表团。陪同代表团前往的有我国驻波兰大使曾涌泉。接见时在座的有波兰外交部部长斯克热歇夫斯基，高等教育部部长拉帕茨基和波兰对外文化合作委员会秘书长温德。
    我政府文化代表团是根据中波文化合作协定的规定，赴波参加制订一九五三年中波文化合作计划，并作短期访问的。代表团在一月四日抵达华沙后，曾于七、八两日离华沙赴波兰著名海港革但斯克，参观当地的建设工程、青年文化宫、科学与技术学院及其他文化机关，并曾和革但斯克的波兰水手谈话。代表团从革但斯克返抵华沙后，曾在九日向苏军解放波兰战争中阵亡战士的陵墓献了花圈。代表团并曾先后赴波兰大城市之一克拉科夫一带和西里西亚地区访问，他们在克拉科夫一带参观了波兰历史最久的大学——雅格隆尼亚大学、其他文化机关和正在建设的波兰钢铁中心——“诺伐·胡塔”城。
