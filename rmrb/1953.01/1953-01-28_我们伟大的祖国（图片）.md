    Our Great Motherland
    The Shanghai Electric Machinery Plant has developed into a new type of factory manufacturing giant generators, transformers, and motors. This is the newly built rotating electrical machinery workshop. The crane is hoisting the newly manufactured first 300 kW large DC generator.

### 我们伟大的祖国（图片）

1953-01-28
第1版()
专栏：

    我们伟大的祖国
    上海电机厂已发展成为一个制造巨型发电机、变压器和马达的新型工厂。这是新建的旋转电机车间。行车正在吊运新制成的第一部三百瓩大型直流发电机。
