Title: "Exhibition on "Korea Defends Peace" Held in Prague"
Author: 
Date: 1953-01-13
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Prague, January 12th: The "Korea Defends Peace" exhibition opened in Prague on the 8th. Among those attending the opening ceremony were Czechoslovak Deputy Prime Minister Josef Kysely and other prominent Czechoslovak figures. Also attending were: Chinese Ambassador Tan Xilin, Korean Minister Kim Ung-gi, as well as diplomats from the Soviet Union and various People's Democracies.

The exhibition displays various photographs showing the happy life of the Korean people with the assistance of their liberator, the Soviet Union, the situation of the United States launching the war of aggression against Korea, the atrocities of the US military, and the crimes of the US aggressors using bacteriological weapons.

Other photographs illustrate the great victories achieved by the Korean People's Army and the Chinese People's Volunteers.

One section is dedicated to exhibiting various Czechoslovak publications praising the Korean people in combat, as well as photographs illustrating the aid of people from various countries to the Korean people.

Czechoslovak Deputy Minister of Propaganda and Culture Brival, in his opening speech, pointed out that this exhibition not only demonstrates the heroic deeds of the Korean people and the Chinese People's Volunteers, but also proves the unparalleled superiority of the forces of peace over the warmongers and the great unity of millions of peace-loving people around the world.

At the opening ceremony, Korean children in Czechoslovakia performed Korean songs and dances.

### 布拉格举行“朝鲜保卫和平”展览会

1953-01-13
第4版()
专栏：

    布拉格举行“朝鲜保卫和平”展览会
    【新华社布拉格十二日电】“朝鲜保卫和平”展览会于八日在布拉格开幕。参加开幕典礼的有捷克斯洛伐克副总理约瑟夫·基斯里和其他捷克斯洛伐克著名人士。参加的还有：中国大使谭希林、朝鲜公使金应基以及苏联和各人民民主国家的外交官。
    展览会陈列各种照片，显示出朝鲜人民在他们的解放者苏联的援助下所获得的幸福生活、美国发动侵朝战争的情形、美军的暴行以及美国侵略者使用细菌武器的罪行等。
    另外有些照片说明朝鲜人民军和中国人民志愿军取得的伟大胜利。
    有一个部分专门展览各种歌颂战斗中的朝鲜人民的捷克斯洛伐克出版物以及说明各国人民援助朝鲜人民的照片。
    捷克斯洛伐克宣传与文化部副部长布里瓦尔在开幕词中指出，这次展览会不仅表现出朝鲜人民和中国人民志愿军的英勇事迹，并且证明和平力量对战争贩子的无比优势以及全世界千百万爱好和平人民的大团结。
    开幕典礼上，在捷克斯洛伐克的朝鲜儿童表演了朝鲜歌曲和舞蹈。
