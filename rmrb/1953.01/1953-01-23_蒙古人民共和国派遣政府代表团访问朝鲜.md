Title: Mongolian People's Republic Sends Government Delegation to Visit Korea
Author:
Date: 1953-01-23
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Pyongyang, January 22nd: To express the Mongolian people's support for the heroic Korean people resisting American aggressors, the Government of the Mongolian People's Republic recently dispatched a Mongolian government delegation headed by Comrade Suranzhab, Vice Premier of the Republic and member of the Political Bureau of the Mongolian People's Revolutionary Party, to visit Korea. The delegation includes: Mongolian Ambassador to Korea, Ravdan; Deputy Minister of the Interior of Mongolia and member of the Mongolian Aid to Korea Committee, Jamiran; and Secretary-General of the Mongolian Aid to Korea Committee, Rihamusulun.

The delegation arrived in Korea on January 4th, accompanied by Kim Yong-jin, the Korean Ambassador to Mongolia. Entrusted by the Great People's Hural of Mongolia, the delegation, during its visit to Pyongyang, awarded 288 medals and awards of the Mongolian People's Republic to leaders of the Korean government, the Workers' Party, and the People's Army, as well as to Korean heroes and model figures who have made contributions during the war. The delegation also brought a large amount of aid materials donated by the Mongolian people, including 1,000 tons of grain, 700 tons of meat, 45,000 pieces of fur, and more than 20,000 pieces of clothing.

Premier Kim Il-sung of the Korean Cabinet received the delegation on the 5th. During the meeting, Suranzhab, on behalf of the Presidium of the People's Hural of Mongolia, awarded General Kim Il-sung the Sukhe Bator Order, Mongolia's highest honor. On the 6th and 7th, the delegation held consecutive award ceremonies in Pyongyang. Kim Doo-bong, Park Heon-young, Park Jung-ai, Heo Ga-i, Lee Seung-yeop, Park Il-woo, Park Chang-ok, Choi Yong-kun, and Nam Il were awarded the Sukhe Bator Order; Hong Myung-hee, Choi Chang-ik, Jung Il-ryong, Hong Gi-ju and thirty-five others were awarded the Order of the Red Banner of Labor; Republic double hero Fang Hushan and fifty-eight others were awarded the Order of the Red Banner of Combat; Republic double hero Kim Ki-woo and thirty-nine others were awarded the Order of Combat Merit; Hyun Hoon, Han Seol-ya, and Yoon Ok-ki, the mother who sent eight sons to the front lines, and thirty-nine others were awarded the Order of the Polar Star; and another one hundred people were awarded the Military Service Medal and the Merit Medal.

The Mongolian government delegation visited residential areas, cultural institutions, and transportation agencies in Pyongyang and other places that had been frantically destroyed by the US air force, and participated in welcoming meetings and symposiums of local residents and agency staff. They expressed their cordial condolences to the heroic Korean people, and conveyed the Mongolian people's concern and enthusiasm for assistance. The delegation was warmly welcomed by the Korean government and people from all walks of life.

The delegation returned home on the 15th.

### 蒙古人民共和国派遣政府代表团访问朝鲜

1953-01-23
第4版()
专栏：

    蒙古人民共和国派遣政府代表团访问朝鲜
    【新华社平壤二十二日电】蒙古人民共和国政府为表达蒙古人民对英勇抗击美国侵略者的朝鲜人民的支援，最近特派遣以共和国副总理、蒙古人民革命党政治局委员苏兰扎布为首的蒙古政府代表团访问朝鲜。代表团包括：蒙古驻朝鲜大使拉夫旦，蒙古内务部副部长、蒙古援助朝鲜委员会委员加米然和蒙古援助朝鲜委员会秘书长日哈木苏伦。
    代表团在一月四日由朝鲜驻蒙古大使金龙镇陪同抵达朝鲜。代表团受蒙古大人民呼拉尔的委托，在访问平壤期间，以蒙古人民共和国的各种勋章和奖章二百八十八枚，授予朝鲜政府、劳动党、人民军的领导人和在战争期间建立功勋的朝鲜英雄模范人物。代表团并带来了蒙古人民赠送的大量援助物资，计粮食一千吨、肉类七百吨、皮毛四万五千张、衣物二万多件。
    朝鲜内阁金日成首相在五日接见代表团。接见时苏兰扎布代表蒙古人民呼拉尔主席团以蒙古的最高勋章苏赫巴托尔勋章授予金日成将军。六日和七日，代表团在平壤市连续举行授勋典礼。金枓奉、朴宪永、朴正爱、许嘉谊、李承烨、朴一禹、朴昌玉、崔庸健和南日荣获苏赫巴托尔勋章；洪命熹、崔昌益、郑一龙、洪箕畴等三十九人获得劳动红旗勋章；共和国双重英雄方虎山等五十九人获得战斗红旗勋章；共和国双重英雄金基禹等四十人获得战斗功勋勋章；玄勋、韩雪野和送八个儿子上前线的母亲尹玉琦等四十人获得北极星勋章；另有一百人获得军功奖章和功劳奖章。
    蒙古政府代表团曾访问了受到美国空军疯狂破坏的平壤市和其他地方的居民区和文化、交通机关，参加了当地居民和机关工作人员的欢迎会和座谈会。他们向英勇的朝鲜人民表示了亲切的慰问，并转达了蒙古人民对于他们的关怀和援助热情。代表团受到了朝鲜政府和各界人民的热烈欢迎。
    代表团已在十五日返国。
