Title: North China Administrative Council of the Government Administration Council Establishes Warehouse Adjustment Committee
Author:
Date: 1953-01-11
Page: 2

    North China Administrative Council of the Government Administration Council
    Establishes Warehouse Adjustment Committee
    The North China Administrative Council of the Government Administration Council of the Central People's Government has established a Warehouse Adjustment Committee, with Liu Xiufeng as the chairman and Han Zheyi and Zhou Wenlong as the vice-chairmen. The main tasks of the Warehouse Adjustment Committee are: to ascertain the number and volume (warehouse, freight yard, foundation area) of existing warehouses in various systems in North China and their usage, and to understand the quantity and types of materials stored in warehouses in various locations, in order to reform the operation and management of warehouses, and to rationally and effectively utilize the existing warehouses and materials in various locations in North China to meet the needs of national economic construction. (Xinhua News Agency)

### 政务院华北行政委员会  成立仓库调整委员会

1953-01-11
第2版()
专栏：

    政务院华北行政委员会
    成立仓库调整委员会
    中央人民政府政务院华北行政委员会成立仓库调整委员会，由刘秀峰任主任委员，韩哲一、周文龙任副主任委员。仓库调整委员会的主要任务是：查清华北各地各系统现有仓库的数量和容积（库房、货场、地基面积）及其使用情况，了解各地仓库所存物资的数量和种类，以便改革仓库的经营管理工作，合理而有效地利用华北各地现有的仓库和物资，适应国家经济建设的需要。　（新华社）
