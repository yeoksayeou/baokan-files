Title: Polish Artists' Performances in Moscow Meet with Great Success; Stalin, Molotov, Malenkov, and Others Attend the Performance

Author:

Date: 1953-01-14
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, 13th: According to TASS, artists from the Polish People's Republic held a grand performance at the Soviet Grand Theatre on the 12th. Singers, symphony orchestra, choir, and ballet dancers from the Polish National Stanisław Moniuszko Opera (Poznań) and the Polish State Mazowsze Song and Dance Ensemble participated in the performance.

The performance was attended by Soviet government leaders: J.V. Stalin, V.M. Molotov, G.M. Malenkov, L.P. Beria, K.Y. Voroshilov, N.S. Khrushchev, and others.

The performance was a great success. The audience warmly welcomed the Polish artists and applauded their performances continuously. Particularly outstanding were singers Domenecki, Klenowski, Furtin, and Kavetska; pianist Czerny-Stefańska and violinist Wilkomirska. The Poznań Opera orchestra gave an excellent performance under the direction of conductor Burdja. The performance of the Polish State Mazowsze Song and Dance Ensemble also received stormy applause.

### 波兰艺术家在莫斯科表演获得极大成功  斯大林莫洛托夫马林科夫等出席演出会

1953-01-14
第4版()
专栏：

    波兰艺术家在莫斯科表演获得极大成功
    斯大林莫洛托夫马林科夫等出席演出会
    【新华社十三日讯】据塔斯社莫斯科讯：波兰人民共和国艺术家十二日在苏联大剧院举行盛大的演出会。波兰国立斯坦尼斯拉夫·莫纽希科歌剧院（波兹南）和波兰国家玛素希歌舞文工团的歌唱家、交响乐队、合唱队和芭蕾舞家参加了演出。
    出席演出会的有苏联政府首长：约·维·斯大林，维·米·莫洛托夫，格·马·马林科夫，拉·巴·贝利亚，凯·叶·伏罗希洛夫，尼·塞·赫鲁晓夫等。
    演出会获得极大的成功。听众热烈地欢迎波兰艺术家并对他们的表演报以经久不息的掌声。特别出色的是歌唱家多麦内茨基，克莱诺夫斯基，弗尔廷，卡维茨卡；钢琴家切尔尼—斯特凡斯卡和小提琴家维尔科密斯卡。波兹南歌剧院交响乐队在队长伯尔迪亚的指挥下作了出色的演奏。波兰国家玛素希歌舞文工团的演出也获得了暴风雨般的掌声。
