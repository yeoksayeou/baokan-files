Title: Soong Ching-ling Leaves Moscow to Return to China
Author: 
Date: 1953-01-16
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, January 15th: TASS Moscow, January 15th: Soong Ching-ling, Vice-President of the Sino-Soviet Friendship Association, left Moscow on January 14th to return to Beijing after staying in the Soviet Union for three weeks.

    During her stay in Moscow, Soong Ching-ling visited Lenin's Mausoleum and laid a wreath, toured Moscow's scenic spots, and visited cultural and medical institutions and many museums.

    Those who saw her off at the Yaroslavl Railway Station included N.S. Tikhonov, Chairman of the Soviet Peace Committee; Denisov, Chairman of the Soviet Society for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries; Popova, Chairman of the Soviet Women's Anti-Fascist Committee; members of the Soviet Peace Committee; senior officials from the Soviet Ministry of Foreign Affairs and the Soviet Society for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries; Guo Moruo, President of the Chinese Academy of Sciences; and Zhang Wentian, Ambassador Extraordinary and Plenipotentiary of the People's Republic of China to the Soviet Union.

### 宋庆龄离莫斯科返国

1953-01-16
第1版()
专栏：

    宋庆龄离莫斯科返国
    【新华社十五日讯】塔斯社莫斯科十五日讯：中苏友好协会总会副会长宋庆龄在苏联逗留三周后，于一月十四日离莫斯科返北京。
    宋庆龄在莫斯科逗留期间，晋谒了列宁陵墓并献花圈，游览了莫斯科名胜，参观了文化、医疗机关和许多博物馆。
    在雅罗斯拉夫尔火车站送行的有苏联拥护和平委员会主席吉洪诺夫、苏联对外文化协会委员会主席杰尼索夫、苏联妇女反法西斯委员会主席波波娃、苏联拥护和平委员会委员、苏联外交部和苏联对外文化协会的高级人员、中国科学院院长郭沫若和中华人民共和国驻苏联特命全权大使张闻天。
