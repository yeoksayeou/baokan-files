Title: People of Korea, Mongolia, Hungary and Albania Commemorate the 29th Anniversary of Lenin's Death
Author:
Date: 1953-01-24
Page: 4

    Xinhua News Agency, January 23rd: According to TASS, the people of Korea, Mongolia, Hungary, and Albania commemorated the 29th anniversary of the death of Lenin, the leader and teacher of the working people of the world.
    In Pyongyang, the capital of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea, a solemn memorial meeting was held on the 21st. Park Chang-ok, Secretary of the Central Committee of the Korean Workers' Party, delivered a report on Lenin's life and work at the meeting. On the 21st, Pyongyang newspapers published portraits of Lenin and articles commemorating him. Pyongyang Radio broadcast poems by Soviet and Korean poets praising Lenin. In the streets and squares of Pyongyang, groups of people gathered to view photographs of Lenin's life and revolutionary career, which were exhibited to commemorate his death.
    In Ulaanbaatar, the capital of the Mongolian People's Republic, a grand memorial meeting was held on the 21st. Leaders of the Mongolian People's Revolutionary Party and the Mongolian government, headed by Yumjaagiin Tsedenbal, General Secretary of the Central Committee of the Mongolian People's Revolutionary Party and Prime Minister of the People's Republic, as well as diplomatic representatives from the Soviet Union, the People's Republic of China, and the Democratic People's Republic of Korea, attended the meeting. Zedib, Deputy Chairman of the Presidium of the Great People's Hural of the Mongolian People's Republic, delivered a report on the 29th anniversary of Lenin's death.
    In Budapest, the capital of the Hungarian People's Republic, a memorial meeting was held on the 21st. Mátyás Rákosi, General Secretary of the Hungarian Working People's Party and Chairman of the Council of Ministers, István Dobi, Chairman of the Presidential Council of the Hungarian People's Republic, and Ye. Kiselev, the Soviet Ambassador to Hungary, attended the meeting. Antal Apró, a member of the Political Bureau of the Central Committee of the Hungarian Working People's Party, delivered a report at the meeting, explaining the epoch-making significance of Lenin's work and doctrine.
    In Tirana, the capital of Albania, the people held a memorial meeting at the People's Theater on the 21st. Enver Hoxha, General Secretary of the Central Committee of the Albanian Labor Party and Chairman of the Council of Ministers, other party and government leaders, and representatives of people's organizations attended the meeting. F. Pakrami, a member of the Central Committee of the Albanian Labor Party, delivered a report on Lenin's life and work at the meeting. Finally, a film about Lenin was shown. Memorial meetings were also held in other parts of the country.

### 朝蒙匈阿人民纪念列宁逝世二十九周年

1953-01-24
第4版()
专栏：

    朝蒙匈阿人民纪念列宁逝世二十九周年
    【新华社二十三日讯】据塔斯社消息：朝鲜、蒙古、匈牙利和阿尔巴尼亚的人民纪念全世界劳动人民的领袖和导师列宁逝世二十九周年。
    在朝鲜民主主义人民共和国的首都平壤，二十一日举行了庄严肃穆的纪念会。朝鲜劳动党中央委员会书记朴昌玉在会上作了关于列宁生平和工作的报告。平壤各报二十一日都刊载了列宁像和纪念列宁的文章。平壤电台广播了苏联诗人和朝鲜诗人歌颂列宁的诗篇。在平壤的街道和广场上，成群的人围着观看为纪念列宁逝世而展出的关于列宁生平和革命事业的照片。
    在蒙古人民共和国的首都乌兰巴托，二十一日举行了隆重的纪念会，到会的有以蒙古人民革命党中央委员会总书记、人民共和国总理尤穆佳·泽登巴尔为首的蒙古人民革命党和蒙古政府的领袖们，以及苏联、中华人民共和国和朝鲜民主主义人民共和国的外交代表。蒙古人民共和国大人民呼拉尔主席团副主席泽狄布在会上作了关于列宁逝世二十九周年的报告。
    在匈牙利人民共和国首都布达佩斯，二十一日举行了纪念会，匈牙利劳动人民党总书记和部长会议主席马·拉科西、匈牙利人民共和国主席团主席伊·道比和苏联驻匈牙利大使叶·基塞列夫都参加了纪念会。匈牙利劳动人民党中央委员会政治局委员安·赫格在会上作了报告，说明列宁的工作和学说的划时代意义。
    阿尔巴尼亚首都地拉那的人民二十一日在人民剧院举行了纪念会。到会的有阿尔巴尼亚劳动党中央委员会总书记和部长会议主席恩·霍查、其他党、政领袖和人民团体代表。阿尔巴尼亚劳动党中央委员会委员法·帕克拉米在会上作了关于列宁的生平和工作的报告。最后，还放映了关于列宁的影片。全国其他各地也都举行了纪念会。
