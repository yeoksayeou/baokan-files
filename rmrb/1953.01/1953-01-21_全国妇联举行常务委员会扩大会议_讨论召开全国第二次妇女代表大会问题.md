Title: All-China Women's Federation Holds Standing Committee Enlarged Meeting to Discuss Issues Concerning the Convening of the Second National Women's Congress

Author:
Date: 1953-01-21
Page: 1

    Xinhua News Agency, 19th: The All-China Democratic Women's Federation held its 37th Standing Committee Enlarged Meeting on January 15th. Those present and attending the meeting included Standing Committee members, Executive Committee members and Alternate Executive Committee members in Beijing, and other cadres, totaling over 70 people. The meeting approved the Outline of the Work Plan of the All-China Democratic Women's Federation for the first half of 1953, and also approved the "Notice" on issues such as the agenda, delegate quota allocation, and election methods for the Second National Women's Congress of China, which is planned to be held in Beijing on April 15th this year.
    The meeting decided that the main agenda of the Second National Women's Congress of China would be: (1) to summarize the women's movement in the past three years and determine the future direction and tasks; (2) to revise the Constitution of the All-China Democratic Women's Federation; (3) to elect the Executive Committee of the Second All-China Democratic Women's Federation; (4) to elect representatives of our country to attend the World Women's Congress. The quota of delegates for the Second National Women's Congress of China was decided to be 925 people. Among the delegates, there should be representative female figures from all ethnic groups, all classes, all democratic parties, all religions, all occupations, as well as family members of revolutionary martyrs, family members of revolutionary soldiers, and family members of revolutionary workers, including women workers, female models in industrial and agricultural production, and other female model figures.
    Finally, the meeting listened to Standing Committee member Luo Qiong convey the resolutions of the Executive Council of the Women's International Democratic Federation.

### 全国妇联举行常务委员会扩大会议  讨论召开全国第二次妇女代表大会问题

1953-01-21
第1版()
专栏：

    全国妇联举行常务委员会扩大会议
    讨论召开全国第二次妇女代表大会问题
    【新华社十九日讯】中华全国民主妇女联合会于一月十五日举行第三十七次常务委员会扩大会议，出席和列席会议的有常务委员、在京的执行委员和候补执行委员及其他干部共七十余人。会议通过了中华全国民主妇女联合会一九五三年度上半年的工作计划纲要，并通过了关于拟在今年四月十五日在北京召开中国第二次全国妇女代表大会的议程、代表名额分配和选举办法等问题的通知。
    会议决定中国第二次全国妇女代表大会主要议程是：（一）总结三年来妇女运动并确定今后的方针任务；（二）修改中华全国民主妇女联合会组织章程；（三）选举第二届中华全国民主妇女联合会执行委员；（四）选举我国出席世界妇女代表大会代表。中国第二次全国妇女代表大会代表名额决定为九百二十五人。代表中要有各民族、各阶层、各民主党派、各宗教、各种职业及革命烈士家属、革命军人家属、革命工作人员家属等妇女代表人物，包括妇女工作者、工农业生产中的妇女模范以及其他妇女模范人物。
    会议最后听取了常务委员罗琼传达国际民主妇联理事会的决议。
