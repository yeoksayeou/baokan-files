Title: Czechoslovak Peace Conference Concludes

Author:
Date: 1953-01-21
Page: 4

    Xinhua News Agency, Prague Dispatch from TASS: The Second Czechoslovak Peace Conference, which opened in Prague on the 17th, closed on the 18th. At the afternoon session on the 17th, Li Yimang, a representative of the Chinese Peace Movement attending the conference, delivered a congratulatory speech. His speech was repeatedly interrupted by stormy applause and shouts of "Long live China!" and "Long live Mao Zedong!" The meeting on the 18th discussed the reports by Anežka Hodinová-Spurná, Chairman of the Czechoslovak Committee for the Defense of Peace, and Mr. Baučík. Forty representatives spoke in the discussion. They expressed the unanimous determination of the Czechoslovak people to work for the consolidation of peace. On behalf of the millions of people of Czechoslovakia, they expressed their support for the resolutions of the World People's Peace Congress and expressed their unwavering commitment to working for the implementation of these resolutions. The conference elected a new Czechoslovak Committee for the Defense of Peace: Anežka Hodinová-Spurná was elected Chairman of the Committee, Gusta Fučíková was elected Vice-Chairman, and Václav Laštovička was elected Secretary. The conference also adopted a letter to the entire people of the Czechoslovak Republic. The conference closed with the participants shouting "Long live Stalin, long live Gottwald, long live peace!"

### 捷保卫和平大会闭幕

1953-01-21
第4版()
专栏：

    捷保卫和平大会闭幕
    据新华社讯：据塔斯社布拉格讯：十七日上午在布拉格开幕的捷克斯洛伐克第二届保卫和平大会已在十八日闭幕。在十七日下午的会议上，列席大会的中国和平运动的代表李一氓曾致贺词，他的发言一再为暴风雨般的掌声和“中国万岁！”“毛泽东万岁！”的欢呼声所打断。十八日的会议讨论了捷克斯洛伐克保卫和平委员会主席安涅日卡·荷第诺娃—斯普尔纳的报告和鲍契克的报告。在讨论中发言的有四十位代表。他们表示了捷克斯洛伐克人民为巩固和平而努力的一致决心。他们代表捷克斯洛伐克的千百万人民表示赞成世界人民和平大会的决议，并且表示要坚定不移地为实现这些决议而努力。大会选出了新的捷克斯洛伐克保卫和平委员会：安涅日卡·荷第诺娃—斯普尔纳当选为委员会主席，古丝妲·伏契科娃当选为副主席，瓦茨拉夫·洛伦茨当选为书记。大会还通过了告捷克斯洛伐克共和国全国人民书。大会在与会者为斯大林、为哥特瓦尔德、为和平而欢呼万岁声中闭幕。
