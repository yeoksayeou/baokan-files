Title: U.S. "Collier's" Magazine Admits: U.S. Occupies Ryukyu as an Aggression Base
Author:
Date: 1953-01-08
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency: Reports from American magazines and news agencies expose the U.S. government's intention to indefinitely occupy the Ryukyu Islands and use them as a war base for aggression in the Far East.

The reactionary American magazine "Collier's," in a report on Okinawa (the largest island in the Ryukyu Islands) published on October 11th of last year, stated: "If you draw a semicircle with a radius of 2,300 miles with Okinawa as the center on a map of Asia, the greater value of the island becomes obvious. The entire area within the semicircle can be bombed by B-29 aircraft carrying atomic bombs taking off from Okinawa. This arc includes all of Southeast Asia, China, Manchuria, and Soviet Siberia from Lake Baikal to the southern tip of the Kamchatka Peninsula... From the third day after the outbreak of the Korean War to the present, B-29 bombers based on Okinawa have dropped a total of 100,000 tons of bombs on the enemy."

The report continued: Another reason that gives Okinawa great value "is that the Japanese peace treaty [referring to the Treaty of San Francisco] gives us the right to stay in Okinawa until we voluntarily request United Nations trusteeship or mandate – in other words, an indefinite stay. We do not have this guarantee for any base in Japan or the Philippines."

U.S. officials do not shy away from the fact that the military bases in the Ryukyu Islands are aimed at China and the Soviet Union. According to a United Press dispatch, U.S. "military officials" stationed in the Ryukyu Islands told a United Press reporter that the U.S. "wants to turn Okinawa into a powerful fortress off the coast of China." The aforementioned "Collier's" magazine article also nakedly admitted: "If we face a showdown with communism, our most valuable base in Asia will be Okinawa." These words fully expose the aggressive purpose of the U.S. government in stipulating in the San Francisco "Peace Treaty" that the Ryukyu Islands be placed under U.S. trusteeship.

### 美国“柯里尔”杂志供认  美国霸占琉球作侵略基地

1953-01-08
第4版()
专栏：

    美国“柯里尔”杂志供认
    美国霸占琉球作侵略基地
    据新华社讯：美国的杂志和通讯社的报道，暴露了美国政府无限期地霸占琉球群岛并将它作为侵略远东的战争基地的用心。
    美国反动刊物“柯里尔”杂志在去年十月十一日发表的一篇关于冲绳岛（琉球群岛中的最大岛屿）的报道中说：“如果你在亚洲地图上，以冲绳岛为圆心，画一个二千三百哩长半径的半圆，那末该岛更大的价值就很明显了。半圆内整个地区都可以被由冲绳起飞的载着原子弹的B二十九型机予以轰炸。这个圆弧里包含着东南亚全部、中国、满洲以及从贝加尔湖到堪察加半岛南端的苏联的西伯利亚……自从朝鲜战争爆发后的第三天到现在为止，以冲绳岛为基地的B二十九型轰炸机总共已向敌人投了十万吨炸弹。”
    这篇报道接着说：使冲绳岛具有重大价值的“另外一个原因，就是日本和约（指旧金山和约）让我们有权利留驻冲绳岛，直到我们自愿要求由联合国委任统治或托管的时候为止——换句话说，就是无限期的留驻。我们在日本或菲律宾的任何基地都没有这种保证。”
    美国官方并不讳言琉球群岛的军事基地是针对着中国和苏联的。据合众社消息，驻在琉球群岛的美国“军方官员”曾向合众社记者表示：美国“要把冲绳岛变成中国海岸外的一座强大的堡垒。”就是前述的“柯里尔”杂志的那篇通讯中，也曾赤裸裸地承认：“如果和共产主义摊牌，我们在亚洲最宝贵的基地将是冲绳岛。”这些话充分暴露了美国政府在旧金山“和约”中规定把琉球群岛交由美国托管的侵略目的。
