Title: The Central Committee of the Communist Youth League Holds its Fourth Plenary Session and Passes a Resolution to Convene the Second National Congress

Author:

Date: 1953-01-18
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, January 17th: The Fourth Plenary Session of the Central Committee of the China New Democratic Youth League was held in Beijing on January 5th. Thirty-one members and alternate members of the Central Committee of the Youth League attended the meeting, and twenty-four people including the Director of the Youth Department of the General Political Department of the People's Revolutionary Military Commission, the Deputy Secretaries of the Youth League Committees of major administrative regions, and responsible cadres from various departments of the Central Committee of the Youth League attended as non-voting delegates.

This meeting discussed and adopted the resolution on several issues concerning the convening of the Second National Congress of the Youth League. The full text of the resolution is as follows:

Resolution of the Fourth Plenary Session of the Central Committee of the China New Democratic Youth League on Several Issues Concerning the Convening of the Second National Congress of the Youth League

I. The Second National Congress will be held in Beijing on June 20th.

II. The draft work report of the Central Committee of the League to the Second National Congress and the draft revision of the League Constitution are entrusted to the Secretariat of the Central Committee of the League to be drafted by May 20th and distributed to the League organizations at all levels for discussion.

III. The Second National Congress will have a total of 810 delegates, allocated according to the proportion of League members, while also considering that each region and sector can have an appropriate number of delegates attending the Congress. The specific allocation is as follows:

	Beijing Municipality: 12 delegates	Hebei Province: 47 delegates
	Shanxi Province: 28 delegates	Tianjin Municipality: 9 delegates
	Inner Mongolia Autonomous Region: 10 delegates	Suiyuan Province: 9 delegates
	Liaodong Province: 11 delegates	Liaoxi Province: 10 delegates
	Jilin Province: 13 delegates	Songjiang Province: 13 delegates
	Rehe Province: 7 delegates	Heilongjiang Province: 12 delegates
	Shenyang Municipality: 10 delegates	Luda Municipality: 9 delegates
	Fushun Municipality: 4 delegates	Anshan Municipality: 5 delegates
	Benxi Municipality: 3 delegates	Shanghai Municipality: 20 delegates
	Shandong Province: 54 delegates	Jiangsu Province: 41 delegates
	Anhui Province: 28 delegates	Zhejiang Province: 21 delegates
	Fujian Province: 18 delegates	Henan Province: 39 delegates
	Hubei Province: 21 delegates	Hunan Province: 20 delegates
	Jiangxi Province: 16 delegates	Guangdong Province: 21 delegates
	Guangxi Province: 17 delegates	Wuhan Municipality: 7 delegates
	Guangzhou Municipality: 6 delegates	Shaanxi Province: 20 delegates
	Gansu Province: 18 delegates	Ningxia Province: 5 delegates
	Xinjiang Province: 17 delegates	Qinghai Province: 5 delegates
	Xi'an Municipality: 6 delegates	Sichuan Province: 35 delegates
	Yunnan Province: 21 delegates	Guizhou Province: 17 delegates
	Xikang Province: 8 delegates	Chongqing Municipality: 9 delegates
	Tibet: 3 delegates	Taiwan: 3 delegates
	Central Directly Subordinate Units: 5 delegates	East China Directly Subordinate Units: 2 delegates
	North China, Northeast, Northwest, Southwest, and Central South Directly Subordinate Units: 1 delegate each; People's Liberation Army, Chinese People's Volunteer Army, and Public Security Forces: 90 delegates.

IV. Delegates are generally elected by the Provincial (Municipal) League Congress. Under special circumstances, they may be elected by the Provincial (Municipal) Delegate Conference with the approval of the Central Committee of the League. The delegates from the army are to be produced by the army's political organs in accordance with specific circumstances and approved by the Central Committee of the League. All delegates attending the Congress shall be elected and reported to the Central Committee of the League before May 20th.

### 青年团中央举行第四次全体会议  通过召开第二次全国代表大会的决定

1953-01-18
第1版()
专栏：

    青年团中央举行第四次全体会议
    通过召开第二次全国代表大会的决定
    【新华社十七日讯】　中国新民主主义青年团中央委员会第四次全体会议于一月五日在北京举行。出席会议的有青年团中央委员和候补中央委员共三十一人，列席会议的有人民革命军事委员会总政治部青年部部长、各大行政区团委副书记、团中央机关各部负责干部等二十四人。
    这次会议讨论和通过了关于召开青年团第二次全国代表大会几个问题的决定。决定全文如下：
    中国新民主主义青年团中央委员会第四次全体会议
    关于召开青年团第二次全国代表大会几个问题的决定
    一、第二次全国代表大会订于六月二十日在北京召开。
    二、团的中央委员会向第二次代表大会的工作报告草案和团章修改草案，责成团中央书记处于五月二十日前拟就，并发给各级团的组织讨论。
    三、第二次代表大会代表名额共八百一十人，按照团员数量比例，并照顾各个地区、方面均能有适当数量的代表出席大会，具体分配如下：
