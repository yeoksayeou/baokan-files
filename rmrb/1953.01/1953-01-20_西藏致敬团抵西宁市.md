Title: Tibetan Delegation Arrives in Xining City
Author:
Date: 1953-01-20
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, Xining, January 19th:

    The Tibetan Delegation, led by Head of Delegation, Liu Xia·Tudeng Tabar, arrived in Xining City, Qinghai Province, by car from Lanzhou on the evening of the 18th, and was warmly welcomed by people from all ethnic groups in Qinghai.

    Those who went to the station to welcome them included Zhang Zhongliang, Chairman of the Qinghai Provincial People's Government, Zhang Guosheng, Secretary-General, Ma Letian (Hui nationality), Deputy Secretary-General, Zhou Renshan, Chairman of the Provincial Ethnic Affairs Commission, Li Shumao, Deputy Commander of the Qinghai Military Region of the Chinese People's Liberation Army, Zhong Shengyi, Deputy Political Commissar, Yang Xiyao, Member of the Qinghai Provincial People's Government, Xia Ricang, Living Buddha of Longwu Temple in Tongren County, Qinghai, Songbu (Tu nationality), Deputy Director of the People's Supervision Commission of the Qinghai Provincial People's Government, Xue Keming, Director of the Provincial Public Security Department, Zhao Jinfeng, Director of the Department of Agriculture and Forestry, Wang Zhiyun, Director of the Department of Culture and Education, Sang Ruojiacuo (Tibetan), Deputy Director, Xue Hongfu, Director of the Department of Commerce, Song Zhishu, Deputy Director of the Panchen Lama's Office in Xining, Maichengzhang, Imam of the Xining Great Mosque, and representatives of seven ethnic groups including Han, Tibetan, Mongolian, Hui, Tu, Salar, and Kazakh, and representatives of five monasteries including Tar Monastery, Zhuanjing Monastery, Hongjue Monastery, Jinta Monastery, and Dafo Monastery, totaling more than 500 people.

    After the Tibetan Delegation got off the train, Head of Delegation Liu Xia·Tudeng Tabar presented a Hada to Zhang Zhongliang, Chairman of the Qinghai Provincial People's Government.

    Representatives of the Qinghai Provincial Children's Team presented flowers to Liu Xia·Tudeng Tabar. At the same time, Tibetan students from Qinghai Provincial Ethnic College, Qinghai Normal School, and Health School, and representatives of lamas from five monasteries including Tar Monastery and Zhuanjing Monastery presented Hadas to Liu Xia·Tudeng Tabar.

### 西藏致敬团抵西宁市

1953-01-20
第1版()
专栏：

    西藏致敬团抵西宁市
    【新华社西宁十九日电】西藏致敬团一行在团长柳霞·土登塔巴率领下，于十八日晚间由兰州乘汽车到达青海省西宁市，受到青海各族各界人民热烈欢迎。
    前往车站欢迎的有青海省人民政府主席张仲良、秘书长张国声、副秘书长马乐天（回族）、省民族事务委员会主任委员周仁山、中国人民解放军青海军区副司令员李书茂、副政治委员钟生溢、青海省人民政府委员杨希尧、青海同仁县隆务寺活佛夏日仓、青海省人民政府人民监察委员会副主任松布（土族）、省人民政府公安厅厅长薛克明、农林厅厅长赵锦峰、文教厅厅长王志匀、副厅长桑若嘉错（藏）、商业厅厅长薛宏福、班禅驻西宁办事处副处长宋之枢、西宁清真大寺教长买成章和汉、藏、蒙、回、土、撒拉、哈萨克等七个民族的代表及塔尔寺、转经寺、宏觉寺、金塔寺、大佛寺等五个寺院的代表共五百多人。
    西藏致敬团下车后，团长柳霞·土登塔巴向青海省人民政府主席张仲良敬献哈达。
    青海省少年儿童队代表向柳霞·土登塔巴献花。同时，青海省民族公学、青海师范、卫生学校等校藏族学生及塔尔寺、转经寺等五个寺院的喇嘛代表向柳霞·土登塔巴献哈达。
