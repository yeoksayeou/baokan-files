Title: Indonesian and Burmese Peace Representatives Arrive in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1953-01-24
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, January 23rd—Indonesian representatives Widjojo (Member of Parliament), Moeditto (Member of Parliament), Sunardi (Member of Parliament), Soegri Krisnanto (Editor-in-Chief of Antara News Agency) and Burmese representative Ko Htun Thin (Lawyer), a delegation of five, arrived in Beijing by plane on January 23rd at the invitation of the China Peace Committee after attending the Vienna World People's Peace Congress.

Also arriving on the same plane were Liu Ningyi, Secretary-General of the Chinese Delegation to the World People's Peace Congress, Deputy Secretary-General Li Zaiwen, and representatives Qu Tangliang and Shi Ruzhang.

Welcoming them at the airport were Liu Guan-yi, Secretary-General of the China Peace Committee, Lai Ruoyu, Secretary-General of the All-China Federation of Trade Unions, and other Chinese representatives who had already returned to Beijing from the World People's Peace Congress.

### 印尼、缅甸和平代表抵京

1953-01-24
第1版()
专栏：

    印尼、缅甸和平代表抵京
     【新华社二十三日讯】出席维也纳世界人民和平大会后应中国人民保卫世界和平委员会邀请来我国访问的印度尼西亚代表威多约（国会议员）、莫迪多（国会议员）、苏纳第（国会议员）、苏格立许努（安塔拉通讯社总编辑）和缅甸代表哥吞丁（律师）一行五人，二十三日乘飞机抵达北京。
    同机到达的有中国出席世界人民和平大会代表团秘书长刘宁一、副秘书长栗再温、代表区棠亮、施如璋四人。
    到机场欢迎的有中国人民保卫世界和平委员会秘书长刘贯一、中华全国总工会秘书长赖若愚和已回到北京的出席世界人民和平大会的中国代表等人。
