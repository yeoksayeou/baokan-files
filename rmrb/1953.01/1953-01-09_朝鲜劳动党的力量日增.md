Title: The Strength of the Korean Workers' Party Increases Daily

Author:
Date: 1953-01-09
Page: 4

    The Strength of the Korean Workers' Party Increases Daily
    Large Numbers of Advanced Workers, Farmers, and Soldiers Join the Party
    [Xinhua News Agency, Pyongyang, 7th] Tass, Pyongyang, 6th: The "Rodong Sinmun," the organ of the Central Committee of the Korean Workers' Party, stated that in the past year, the Korean Workers' Party, due to improvements in organizational work and closer ties with the masses, has greatly expanded its ranks and increased its strength. Currently, the Korean Workers' Party has over one million members, who are vanguard fighters for the glory, freedom, and independence of Korea. During the Fatherland Liberation War, nearly 500,000 people joined the Party, which clearly demonstrates the leading role of the Workers' Party and its growing prestige. On the eve of the New Year, thousands of advanced workers, farmers, and soldiers of the Korean People's Army filled out applications to join the Party, expressing their hope to fight for a free and independent Korea in the ranks of the Party.

### 朝鲜劳动党的力量日增

1953-01-09
第4版()
专栏：

    朝鲜劳动党的力量日增
    大批先进工人、农民和战士参加了党
    【新华社七日讯】塔斯社平壤六日讯：朝鲜劳动党中央委员会的机关报“劳动新闻”说：朝鲜劳动党在过去一年中，由于改进了组织工作和对群众有了更密切的联系，它的队伍大大扩充，并且增加了力量。目前，朝鲜劳动党拥有一百多万党员，他们是为朝鲜的光荣、自由和独立而战的先锋战士。在卫国解放战争期间，差不多有五十万人参加了党，这清楚地证明了劳动党的领导作用和它的威信日益提高。在新年前夕，有千百位先进工人、农民和朝鲜人民军的战士填写入党申请书，表示他们希望在党的队伍中为自由和独立的朝鲜而斗争。
