Title: Exhibition of U.S. Government's Germ Warfare Crimes Held in Vienna (Photos)
Author: Yuan Ling
Date: 1953-01-12
Page: 4

Exhibition of U.S. Government's Germ Warfare Crimes Held in Vienna

    The exhibition on the U.S. government's germ warfare crimes, co-sponsored by the Chinese People's Committee for World Peace and the Austrian Peace Council, opened in Vienna on December 15 and closed on the 19th. Representatives from various countries attending the World People's Peace Congress and many citizens of Vienna visited the exhibition. They all paid close attention to the evidence displayed at the exhibition of the U.S. military's germ warfare in Korea and Northeast China, and unanimously expressed their indignation at the heinous crimes of the U.S. military.

    Top right: Professor Madel, a French member of the International Scientific Commission investigating the facts of germ warfare in Korea and China, explaining the situation of the Ishii-style germ bombs to visitors.

    Bottom right: Peace representatives from various countries visiting the exhibition of U.S. government's germ warfare crimes.

    Top left: Peace representatives listening attentively to the recorded confessions of U.S. prisoners of war regarding the U.S. government's germ warfare.

    Middle left: Italian representatives want to take the informational pamphlets about the U.S. government's germ warfare crimes back to their country. They said, "We want to publicize this scientifically irrefutable fact to the people."

    Bottom left: Peace representatives writing in the autograph book after visiting the exhibition: "We express our indignation at the crimes of imperialism."
                        (Xinhua News Agency Reporter Yuan Ling photographed)

### 在维也纳举行的美国政府细菌战罪行展览会（图片）
袁苓
1953-01-12
第4版()
专栏：

    在维也纳举行的美国政府细菌战罪行展览会
    由中国人民保卫世界和平委员会和奥地利和平理事会主办的美国政府细菌战罪行展览会于十二月十五日在维也纳揭幕，至十九日闭幕。参加世界人民和平大会的各国代表和维也纳的许多市民都前往参观。他们都以极大注意参观了会上陈列的美军在朝鲜和我国东北进行细菌战的罪证，并对美军这种滔天罪行一致表示愤慨。
    右上：调查在朝鲜和中国的细菌战事实国际科学委员会委员法国马戴尔教授向参观者解释石井式细菌炸弹的情形。
    右下：各国和平代表参观美国政府细菌战罪行展览会。
    左上：和平代表们专注地静听美军战俘关于美国政府进行细菌战的供词录音。
    左中：意大利代表们要把美国政府进行细菌战罪行说明书带回国去。他们说：“我们要向人民宣传这一科学的铁的事实”。
    左下：和平代表们在参观展览会后在签名簿上写道：“我们表示对帝国主义罪行的愤慨”。
                        （新华社记者袁　苓摄）
