Title: Comrade Liu Shaoqi, Head of the CPC Central Committee Delegation to the CPSU Congress, Returned to Beijing Yesterday. Comrades Zhou Enlai and Zhu De and Others Welcomed Him at the Airport

Author: 
Date: 1953-01-12
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, 11th. Liu Shaoqi, head of the Central Committee Delegation of the Communist Party of China to the 19th Congress of the Communist Party of the Soviet Union, returned to Beijing yesterday afternoon, the 11th, after flying from Moscow on the 7th.

Those who went to the airport to welcome him were: members of the Political Bureau of the CPC Central Committee Zhou Enlai, Zhu De, Chen Yun, Gao Gang, Lin Boqu, Dong Biwu, Peng Zhen; members of the Central Committee Chen Yi, Deng Xiaoping, Zeng Shan, Ye Jianying, Nie Rongzhen, Wu Yuzhang, Teng Daiyuan, Bo Yibo, Liao Chengzhi, Chen Boda, Huang Kecheng; alternate members of the Central Committee Deng Yingchao, Ulanhu, Luo Ruiqing, Zhang Zongxun, Chen Geng, Xi Zhongxun, Liu Lantao; and heads of various ministries and commissions under the CPC Central Committee, various departments under the Military Commission, and people's organizations Li Kenong, Li Weihan, An Ziwen, Hu Qiaomu, Li Chuli, Zhou Shidi, Chen Xilian, Li Tao, Liu Daosheng, Wu Faxian, Zhang Hanfu, Lai Ruoyu, Hu Yaobang, etc. (Attached picture)

Liu Shaoqi (right) meets with Zhou Enlai (left) and Zhu De (center) at the airport. Xinhua News Agency reporter Qi Guanshan.

### 中共中央参加联共代表大会代表团团长  刘少奇同志昨返抵北京  周恩来朱德同志等前往机场欢迎

1953-01-12
第1版()
专栏：

    中共中央参加联共代表大会代表团团长
    刘少奇同志昨返抵北京
    周恩来朱德同志等前往机场欢迎
    【新华社十一日讯】参加苏联共产党第十九次代表大会的中国共产党中央委员会代表团团长刘少奇于七日飞离莫斯科后，已在十一日下午返抵北京。
    前往机场欢迎的，有：中共中央政治局委员周恩来、朱德、陈云、高岗、林伯渠、董必武、彭真、中央委员陈毅、邓小平、曾山、叶剑英、聂荣臻、吴玉章、滕代远、薄一波、廖承志、陈伯达、黄克诚，候补中央委员邓颖超、乌兰夫、罗瑞卿、张宗逊、陈赓、习仲勋、刘澜涛、以及中共中央所属各部委、军委所属各部和人民团体的负责人李克农、李维汉、安子文、胡乔木、李初梨、周士第、陈锡联、李涛、刘道生、吴法宪、章汉夫、赖若愚、胡耀邦等。（附图片）
    刘少奇同志（右）与周恩来同志（左）、朱德同志（中）在机场会晤。新华社记者　齐观山摄
