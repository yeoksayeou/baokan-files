Title: Qinghai Golog Tibetan Delegation Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1953-01-08
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, January 7th: A 53-member Tibetan delegation from the Golog region of Qinghai Province, led by Head Zha Xi Wang Xu and Deputy Heads Kang Kemin, Dan Zhen Jian Mucuo, and Yi Xin Wang Jie, arrived in Beijing on the afternoon of the 6th. Those who went to the station to welcome them included Liu Geping and Liu Chun, Deputy Directors of the Nationalities Affairs Commission of the Central People's Government; Gan Chunlei, Deputy Director of the General Office of the Nationalities Affairs Commission; Luo Wang, Acting Director of the Panchen Lama's Office in Beijing; Lian Huizhu, Deputy Director; cadres of the Nationalities Affairs Commission, students of the Central Institute for Nationalities, and lamas from various temples in Beijing, totaling more than 60 people.

After the head and deputy heads of the Tibetan delegation from the Golog region of Qinghai Province got off the train, they presented Hada scarves to Liu Geping and Liu Chun, Deputy Directors of the Nationalities Affairs Commission of the Central People's Government.

### 青海果洛区藏族参观团抵京

1953-01-08
第1版()
专栏：

    青海果洛区藏族参观团抵京
    【新华社七日讯】青海省果洛区藏族参观团一行五十三人，在团长扎喜旺徐、副团长康克明、旦贞尖木错、一心王杰率领下，于六日下午到达北京。前往车站欢迎的有中央人民政府民族事务委员会副主任委员刘格平、刘春，民族事务委员会办公厅副主任甘春雷，班禅驻京办事处代理处长罗旺、副处长连慧珠，和民族事务委员会干部、中央民族学院学员，以及北京市各寺院喇嘛等共六十余人。
    青海省果洛区藏族参观团团长、副团长下车后，即向中央人民政府民族事务委员会副主任委员刘格平、刘春敬献哈达。
