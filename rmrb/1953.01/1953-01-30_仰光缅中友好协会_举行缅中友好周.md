Title: Rangoon Sino-Burmese Friendship Association Holds Sino-Burmese Friendship Week
Author:
Date: 1953-01-30
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Rangoon, January 29th: To further strengthen the friendship between the peoples of Burma and China, the Rangoon Sino-Burmese Friendship Association is holding Sino-Burmese Friendship Week, starting January 25th.

The opening ceremony of Sino-Burmese Friendship Week was held in Rangoon on January 25th. Dr. U Htin Aung, Chairman of the Sino-Burmese Friendship Association, called in his opening speech for efforts to promote Sino-Burmese cultural exchange and enhance the friendship between the two peoples.

Thakin Kodaw Hmaing, Chairman of the Burmese Peace Committee, spoke at the meeting. He said: China is a good neighbor of Burma, and the peoples of China and Burma have a close friendship and relationship in history. This relationship was once destroyed by imperialism and colonial rulers. This Sino-Burmese Friendship Week is to strengthen the already restored friendship between the peoples of Burma and China. He said: I hope Burmese friends will visit China more often so that they can personally see China's progress and see that the Chinese people love Chairman Mao Zedong.

Daw Khin Lay, Chairman of the Burmese Women's Association, and Daw Khin Khit, member of the Burmese Peace Committee, also spoke at the meeting. They all emphasized that the development of friendship between Burma and China is in line with the demands of the Burmese people, and this friendship has a great effect on world peace.

After the opening ceremony, an exhibition of cultural relics and photographs of New China and a New China film evening were held. The exhibits included construction pictures of New China, ancient and modern Chinese works of art, folk paper-cuts and paintings, etc. The evening featured films such as "A Visit to China by the Burmese Cultural Delegation," "We Must Harness the Huai River," and "Happy Xinjiang," with an audience of 4,000 people. The exhibits and films of New China were warmly welcomed by the Burmese people.

During the Sino-Burmese Friendship Week, the Sino-Burmese Friendship Association also held symposiums on various aspects of New China's construction, with special reports on education, art, literature, women, and children in New China.

### 仰光缅中友好协会  举行缅中友好周

1953-01-30
第4版()
专栏：

    仰光缅中友好协会
    举行缅中友好周
    【新华社二十九日讯】仰光消息：为进一步加强缅中两国人民的友谊，仰光缅中友好协会在一月二十五日起举行缅中友好周。
    缅中友好周的开幕典礼二十五日在仰光举行，缅中友好协会主席宇信波博士在开幕词中号召努力促进中缅文化交流和增强两国人民的友谊。
    缅甸保卫世界和平委员会主席德钦哥都迈在会上讲话。他说：中国是缅甸的善邻，中缅两国人民在历史上有密切的友谊和关系，这一关系曾经被帝国主义和殖民地统治者破坏，这次缅中友好周是为了加强已经恢复的缅中人民的友谊。他说：希望缅甸友人多到中国参观，这样可以亲自看到中国的进步，看到中国人民热爱毛泽东主席。
    接着在会上讲话的有缅甸妇女协会主席都庆拉和缅甸保卫世界和平委员会委员都庆漆。她们都强调说：发展缅中两国友谊符合缅甸人民的要求，这种友谊对世界和平有很大作用。
    开幕典礼结束后，接着举行了新中国文物图片展览会和新中国电影晚会。展览品中有新中国的建设图片、中国古代和近代的艺术品、民间剪纸和绘画等。晚会放映了“缅甸文化代表团访问中国记”、“一定要把淮河修好”和“欢乐的新疆”等影片，观众达四千人。新中国的展览品和影片受到缅甸人民热烈的欢迎。
    在缅中友好周中，缅中友好协会还举办关于新中国各方面建设的座谈会，座谈会上有关于新中国教育、艺术、文学、妇女、儿童等专题报告。
