Title: President of the Soviet Academy of Sciences on the Great Tasks of Soviet Science This Year: The Goal of Soviet Science is to Stand at the Forefront of World Science
Author:
Date: 1953-01-07
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, December 31, 1952 (TASS): Academician Nesmeyanov, President of the Soviet Academy of Sciences, published an article in "Izvestia" entitled "Soviet Science in 1953."

Nesmeyanov wrote: The 19th Congress of the Communist Party of the Soviet Union set a great goal for Soviet science—to stand at the forefront of world science. The main task of Soviet science in 1953 (the third year of the Fifth Stalin Five-Year Plan) is determined by the resolutions of the Party Congress. It is the responsibility of Soviet scientists to solve many fundamental problems in various knowledge sectors in order to promote the construction of the material and technical base of communism.

The assistance of scientists to the great construction projects will greatly increase in 1953. The work teams dispatched by the Soviet Academy of Sciences to the Volga River, Dnieper River, Don River, and Amu Darya River will also greatly expand their work. Because the plan stipulates the comprehensive utilization of the power resources of the Angara River and the construction of hydropower stations on the Angara River, geologists, hydrologists, and hydraulic engineers are facing major tasks.

In the future, special attention will be paid to scientific issues related to the development of already utilized hydropower stations and new irrigation and water supply systems. New scientific centers and experimental stations will be established in many places to conduct regular research work. Extensive research will be conducted to promote major industrial sectors such as metallurgy, petroleum, coal, and machine manufacturing. Geological exploration teams will explore new coal mines, oil fields, and other mineral deposits; extensive research will also be conducted on the theory of machine manufacturing and the scientific principles of production process automation. Physicists, mathematicians, and chemists are facing very important tasks. Experts in nuclear physics, radiophysics, electronics, and optics are continuing important research work, which has a decisive effect on future technology.

More extensive research will be conducted in areas such as geochemistry and rare element chemistry to further promote their use in industry.

1953 will open up broad prospects for the further development of people's literature. Nesmeyanov said: The main guiding principle of research work in this area is based on the fundamental tasks of the present era, which is the gradual transition of Soviet society from socialism to communism and the establishment of a communist worldview among the Soviet people.

In biology, special attention will be paid to the important theoretical issues of summarizing the experiments of several research institutes. Among these issues are the developmental laws of non-cellular and cellular protoplasm, heredity and its variability, the study of proteins and metabolism, the regeneration of tissues and organs, and so on. The latest precision methods will be used to study many new physical phenomena, such as high-frequency ultra-short electromagnetic waves, ultrasonic waves, and ultra-strong sound stimulation.

Nesmeyanov finally emphasized: Advanced Soviet science, with its various discoveries and research work, helps the people gain deeper knowledge and use the country's endless natural wealth more perfectly, and brings them closer and closer to their single-minded goal, the construction of communism.

### 苏联科学院院长论今年苏联科学的伟大任务  苏联科学的目标是站上世界科学第一位

1953-01-07
第4版()
专栏：

    苏联科学院院长论今年苏联科学的伟大任务
    苏联科学的目标是站上世界科学第一位
    【新华社讯】塔斯社莫斯科一九五二年十二月三十一日电：苏联科学院院长涅斯米扬诺夫院士在“消息报”上发表一文，题为“一九五三年的苏联科学”。
    涅斯米扬诺夫写道：苏联共产党第十九次代表大会为苏联科学规定了一个伟大的目标——站上世界科学的第一位。苏联科学在一九五三年（第五个斯大林五年计划的第三年）的主要任务是由党代表大会的决议来决定的。为了促进共产主义物质与技术基础的建设而来解决各种知识部门的许多基本问题乃是苏联科学家的责任。
    科学家对伟大建设工程的协助在一九五三年将要大大增加起来。苏联科学院派到伏尔加河、第聂伯河、顿河和阿姆河去的工作队也要大大扩充它们的工作。因为计划中规定要全面利用安加拉河的动力资源并在安加拉河畔建筑水电站，所以地质学家、水文学家和水利工程师们都面临着重大的任务。
    今后将特别注意有关于发展已运用的水电站和新的灌溉及供水系统的科学问题。许多地方将建立新的科学中心和实验站，以便进行经常的研究工作。为了促进冶金、石油、煤炭与机器制造等主要工业部门，将要进行广泛的研究工作。地质学考察队将勘察新的煤矿、油矿和其他矿物的矿藏；在机器制造的理论及生产过程自动化的科学原则方面也将进行广泛的研究。物理学家、数学家和化学家们都面临着很重要的任务。原子核物理学、放射性物理学、电子学和光学的专家们都在继续进行重要的研究工作，这些工作对于未来的技术具有决定的作用。
    在地球化学和稀有元素化学等方面都将进行更广大的研究工作，以便在工业上更加推广它们的用途。
    一九五三年将为人民文学的进一步发展开拓广大的前途。涅斯米扬诺夫说：这一方面研究工作的主要方针是从现时代基本的任务出发的，这个基本的任务就是使苏维埃社会从社会主义逐渐过渡到共产主义以及在苏联人民中间树立共产主义的人生观。
    生物学方面将特别注意把若干研究所的实验加以归纳的重要的理论问题。这些问题之中有非细胞形和细胞形有生物质的发展规律，遗传及其可变性，蛋白质及新陈代谢作用的研究，组织与器官的再生等等。今后将采用最新式的精密方法来研究许多新的物理现象，如高频率超短电磁波、超音波和超强声音刺戟等。
    涅斯米扬诺夫最后强调指出：先进的苏联科学凭着它的各种发现与研究工作来帮助人民获得较深的知识以及更完善地使用他们国内无尽的天然财富，并且使他们一心向往的目的，即共产主义的建设越来越近。
