Title: Indian Ambassador N. Raghavan Holds National Day Reception
Author:
Date: 1953-01-27
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, January 26th: Indian Ambassador to China, N. Raghavan, held a reception in Beijing on January 26th to celebrate the third anniversary of the founding of the Republic of India.

Those invited to attend included: Guo Moruo, Vice Premier of the Government Administration Council of the Central People's Government; Zhang Naiqi, Minister of Food of the Central People's Government; Zhang Hanfu, Vice Minister of Foreign Affairs; Lei Renmin, Vice Minister of Foreign Trade; Ding Xilin, President of the Sino-Indian Friendship Association; University Presidents Wu Yuzhang and Ma Yinchu; Wu Han, Deputy Mayor of the Beijing Municipal People's Government, and other individuals, totaling two hundred people.

Also invited to attend were diplomatic envoys and diplomats from various countries stationed in China.

In other news: Chairman Mao Zedong of our Central People's Government and Premier Zhou Enlai of the Government Administration Council sent telegrams to President Prasad and Prime Minister Nehru of the Republic of India on the 24th, respectively, to congratulate them on Indian National Day.

### 印度大使赖嘉文举行国庆招待会

1953-01-27
第1版()
专栏：

    印度大使赖嘉文举行国庆招待会
    【新华社二十六日讯】印度共和国驻华大使赖嘉文，为庆祝印度共和国成立三周年，二十六日在北京举行招待会。
    应邀参加的有：中央人民政府政务院副总理郭沫若，中央人民政府粮食部部长章乃器，外交部副部长章汉夫，对外贸易部副部长雷任民，中印友好协会会长丁西林，大学校长吴玉章、马寅初，北京市人民政府副市长吴晗等二百人。
    应邀参加的还有各国驻华使节和外交官。
    又讯：我中央人民政府毛泽东主席和政务院周恩来总理在二十四日分别打电报给印度共和国普拉沙德总统和尼赫鲁总理，祝贺印度国庆。
