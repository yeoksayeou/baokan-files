Title: Sino-Polish Cultural Cooperation Agreement's Implementation Plan for This Year Signed
Author: 
Date: 1953-01-29
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, 28th: The Sino-Polish Cultural Cooperation Joint Committee convened a meeting in Warsaw in January according to the stipulations of the Sino-Polish Cultural Cooperation Agreement. The meeting was presided over by the Chairman of the Joint Committee, Mr. Rapacki, Minister of Higher Education of the Polish People's Republic. The Chinese delegation headed by Mr. Shen Yanbing, Minister of Culture of the Central People's Government of the People's Republic of China, and the Polish delegation headed by Mr. Winde, Secretary-General of the Committee for Foreign Culture of the Polish People's Republic, participated in the meeting. The meeting proceeded in an atmosphere of deep friendship and mutual understanding, and complete agreement was reached on the 1953 implementation plan of the Sino-Polish Cultural Cooperation Agreement. This implementation plan will expand and strengthen cultural cooperation between the two countries. The plan specifically stipulates that the two countries will send delegations of cultural, scientific and artistic workers to each other, exchange students and postgraduates, and exchange cultural troupes. The plan also stipulates further cooperation between the two countries in the fields of news, film, broadcasting and sports. Both sides also agreed to expand the exchange of academic materials between the Academies of Sciences and scientific research institutions of the two countries. The implementation plan was signed on January 26 by Mr. Rapacki, Chairman of the Joint Committee, Mr. Shen Yanbing, Head of the Chinese Delegation, and Mr. Winde, Head of the Polish Delegation. After the signing, each signatory delivered a speech in succession. The signing ceremony was held in the Hall of the Council of Ministers of Poland, and was attended by Mr. Sirenkiewicz, Vice Chairman of the Council of Ministers of the Polish People's Republic, Mr. Skrzeszewski, Minister of Foreign Affairs, Mr. Jarosinski, Minister of Education, Mr. Mijar, Director of the Office of the Council of Ministers, Mr. Naszkowski, Deputy Minister of Foreign Affairs, and Mr. Wilczek, Deputy Minister of Culture and Art. Also attending the signing ceremony were Mr. Zeng Yongquan, Ambassador of the People's Republic of China to Poland, Mr. Yang Qiliang, Counselor, and all members of both delegations.

### 中波文化合作协定今年的执行计划签字

1953-01-29
第1版()
专栏：

    中波文化合作协定今年的执行计划签字
    【新华社二十八日讯】中波文化合作联合委员会根据中波文化合作协定的规定，于一月间在华沙召开会议。会议由联合委员会主席、波兰人民共和国高等教育部部长拉帕茨基主持，参加会议的有：以中华人民共和国中央人民政府文化部部长沈雁冰为首的中国代表团和以波兰人民共和国对外文化委员会秘书长温德为首的波兰代表团。会议在深厚的友谊和相互谅解的气氛中进行，对于中波文化合作协定一九五三年执行计划取得了完全的协议。此项执行计划将扩大并加强中波两国间的文化合作，计划中特别规定两国互派文化、科学与艺术工作者代表团，互换留学生和研究生，以及互派文工团。计划并规定两国在新闻、电影、广播及体育方面的进一步合作，双方并协议扩大交换两国科学院及科学研究机构的学术资料。执行计划在一月二十六日由联合委员会主席拉帕茨基、中国代表团团长沈雁冰和波兰代表团团长温德签署，签字后各签署人并相继致词。签字仪式在波兰部长会议大厅内举行，参加的有：波兰人民共和国部长会议副主席西伦凯维兹、外交部部长斯克热歇夫斯基、教育部部长雅罗辛斯基、部长会议办公厅主任米雅尔、外交部副部长纳斯科夫斯基、文化艺术部副部长维尔切克。参加签字仪式的还有中华人民共和国驻波兰大使曾涌泉、参赞杨琪良以及双方代表团全体团员。
