Title: The Broad Masses of the Burmese People Love Chairman Mao's Works
Author:
Date: 1953-01-31
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Rangoon, January 30th: The Burmese people love Chairman Mao Zedong's works and various publications from New China. According to incomplete statistics, since 1949, Burmese writers have translated 24 kinds of Chinese books.

Chairman Mao's works that have been translated into Burmese include "On New Democracy," "On the People's Democratic Dictatorship," "Talks at the Yan'an Forum on Literature and Art," "On Coalition Government," "On Practice," and "On Contradiction." These translations have been warmly welcomed by the broad masses of the Burmese people after their publication. In addition, Liu Shaoqi's "On the Party," "How to Be a Good Communist," and Zhou Enlai's "Struggle for the Consolidation and Development of the People's Victory," have also been translated into Burmese.

The Burmese translations also include important laws and decrees of New China such as the Trade Union Law and the Land Reform Law, as well as other Chinese books.

Burmese readers also love Chinese literary works, especially the works of the Chinese revolutionary writer Lu Xun. Lu Xun's works that have been translated into Burmese include "My Old Home" and "The True Story of Ah Q."

### 缅甸广大人民  热爱毛主席著作

1953-01-31
第4版()
专栏：

    缅甸广大人民
    热爱毛主席著作
    【新华社三十日讯】仰光消息：缅甸人民热爱毛泽东主席的著作和新中国的各种出版物。据不完全的统计，从一九四九年到现在，缅甸作家翻译了中国书籍二十四种。
    毛主席的著作被译成缅甸文的已经有“新民主主义论”、“论人民民主专政”、“在延安文艺座谈会上的讲话”、“论联合政府”、“实践论”和“矛盾论”。这些译本出版后，受到了缅甸广大人民的热烈欢迎。此外，刘少奇的“论党”、“论共产党员的修养”和周恩来的“为巩固和发展人民的胜利而奋斗”等，也都被译成缅甸文。
    缅甸文译本中还有工会法和土地改革法等新中国的重要法令，以及其他中国书籍。
    缅甸读者也爱好中国的文艺作品，特别是中国革命作家鲁迅的著作。已译成缅文的鲁迅著作有“故乡”和“阿Q正传”等。
