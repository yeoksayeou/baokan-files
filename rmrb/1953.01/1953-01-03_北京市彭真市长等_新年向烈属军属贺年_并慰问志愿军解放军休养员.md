Title: Beijing Mayor Peng Zhen and Others Extend New Year Greetings to Families of Martyrs and Military Personnel, and Visit Volunteer Army and People's Liberation Army Recuperating Soldiers
Author:
Date: 1953-01-03
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, January 2nd: Beijing Municipal People's Government Mayor Peng Zhen sent New Year cards to the families of revolutionary martyrs and revolutionary military personnel on New Year's Day, extending his greetings to them. Deputy Mayors Zhang Youyu, Wu Han, Secretary-General Xue Zizheng, Beijing Branch of the Resist America, Aid Korea Association Chairman Zhang Xiruo, Vice Chairman Zeng Zhaolun, Secretary-General Li Leguang, and more than 60 people including heads of various bureaus and offices of the Municipal People's Government, various democratic parties, and various people's organizations, visited the residences of recuperating soldiers of the Chinese People's Volunteer Army and the Chinese People's Liberation Army on New Year's Day afternoon and the morning of the 2nd, bringing a large number of gifts for comfort and support. The arrival of the visiting group gave the recuperating soldiers great encouragement. They unanimously expressed that they would recuperate with peace of mind, strive to regain their health as soon as possible, and return to the forefront of defending peace.

During the New Year holidays, people from all walks of life in Beijing responded to the call of the Beijing Branch of the Resist America, Aid Korea Association and automatically launched a campaign to support the army and give preferential treatment to families of military personnel. On New Year's Day, residents of various streets organized New Year's visiting teams to generally visit the families of revolutionary martyrs and revolutionary military personnel to extend New Year greetings to them. Workers, students, government cadres, and people from other classes rushed to make many exquisite New Year cards before the holidays and wrote many enthusiastic letters of condolence, sending them to the front lines in Korea to extend New Year greetings and condolences to the most beloved people of the motherland – the commanders and fighters of the Chinese People's Volunteer Army.

### 北京市彭真市长等  新年向烈属军属贺年  并慰问志愿军解放军休养员

1953-01-03
第1版()
专栏：

    北京市彭真市长等
    新年向烈属军属贺年
    并慰问志愿军解放军休养员
    【新华社二日讯】北京市人民政府彭真市长元旦给革命烈士家属和革命军人家属致送贺年片，向他们致意；副市长张友渔、吴晗、秘书长薛子正，北京市抗美援朝分会主席张奚若、副主席曾昭抡、秘书长李乐光以及市人民政府各局、处，各民主党派，各人民团体负责人等六十多人，在元旦下午和二日上午携带大批慰问品，分别到中国人民志愿军和中国人民解放军休养员住地进行慰问。慰问团的来到给予休养员们以莫大的鼓舞，他们一致表示要安心疗养，争取早日恢复健康，重新回到保卫和平的最前线。
    在年节里，北京市各界人民纷纷响应北京市抗美援朝分会的号召，自动展开拥军优属工作。各街道居民元旦组织拜年队，普遍访问革命烈士家属和革命军人家属，给他们拜年。工人、学生、机关干部以及其他阶层人民，年节前就赶制了许多精致的贺年片、同时书写许多充满着热情的慰问信，送往朝鲜前线去，向祖国人民最可爱的人——中国人民志愿军的指挥员、战斗员们贺年和慰问。
