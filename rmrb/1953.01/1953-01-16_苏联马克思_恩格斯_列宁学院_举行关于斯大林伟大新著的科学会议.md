Title: Soviet Marx-Engels-Lenin Institute Holds Scientific Conference on Stalin's Great New Work
Author:
Date: 1953-01-16
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, 15th: According to TASS, the Marx-Engels-Lenin Institute under the Central Committee of the Communist Party of the Soviet Union began a scientific conference on the 13th regarding Stalin's brilliant work "Economic Problems of Socialism in the USSR." Researchers from the Marx-Engels-Lenin Institute, the Lenin Central Museum, and the Party History Institute (a branch of the Marx-Engels-Lenin Institute) affiliated with the Moscow City Committee of the Communist Party of the Soviet Union are participating in the conference.

The opening ceremony was presided over by Professor G.D. Obichkin, Director of the Marx-Engels-Lenin Institute. He said: Comrade Stalin's classic work not only marks the highest stage in the development of Marxist-Leninist political economy, but also the highest stage in the development of the entire Marxism-Leninism, because in addition to political and economic issues, it also expounds on the basic issues of philosophy and scientific communism. This work arms the Party and the Soviet people with knowledge of the objective laws of social development, points out the possibility of using these laws to benefit society, and points out and illuminates the path for our country to build communism. Comrade Stalin's brilliant work has world-wide historical significance. "Economic Problems of Socialism in the USSR" is now being thoroughly studied not only in our country, but also abroad. Studying this work and the documents of the 19th Congress of the Communist Party of the Soviet Union will greatly improve the level of the entire ideological work of our party.

At the meeting, P.N. Pospelov, Corresponding Academician of the Soviet Academy of Sciences, gave a report on "The Historical Significance of the 19th Congress of the Communist Party of the Soviet Union and Comrade Stalin's Classic Work 'Economic Problems of Socialism in the USSR'."

The first meeting also heard two other reports: A.V. Tushunov, Deputy Director of the Marx-Engels-Lenin Institute, on "Comrade Stalin on the Basic Economic Laws of Modern Capitalism and Socialism," and Dr. of History N.I. Shatagin on "Comrade Stalin on the Three Basic Prerequisites for the Transition from Socialism to Communism."

The meeting will continue.

### 苏联马克思—恩格斯—列宁学院  举行关于斯大林伟大新著的科学会议

1953-01-16
第4版()
专栏：

    苏联马克思—恩格斯—列宁学院
    举行关于斯大林伟大新著的科学会议
    【新华社十五日讯】据塔斯社莫斯科讯：苏联共产党中央委员会所属马克思—恩格斯—列宁学院十三日开始举行关于斯大林的天才著作“苏联社会主义经济问题”的科学会议。参加会议的有马克思—恩格斯—列宁学院、列宁中央博物馆和苏联共产党莫斯科市委员会所属党史研究所（马克思—恩格斯—列宁学院的一个分部）的研究工作者。
    会议由马克思—恩格斯—列宁学院院长格·德·奥比奇金教授主持开幕。他说：斯大林同志的这一经典著作不仅标志着马克思列宁主义政治经济学发展中的最高阶段而且标志着整个马克思列宁主义发展中的最高阶段，因为它除了政治经济学问题外，还阐扬了哲学上和科学的共产主义上的基本问题。这部著作用关于社会发展的客观法则的知识武装了党和苏联人民，指出了利用这些法则为社会谋福利的可能性，指出并照亮了我国建设共产主义的道路。斯大林同志的光辉著作具有世界范围的历史意义。“苏联社会主义经济问题”现在不仅在我国，而且在外国被透彻地研读着。研究这部著作和苏联共产党第十九次代表大会的文件将大大提高我们党的整个思想工作的水平。
    会上，苏联科学院通讯院士彼·恩·波斯伯洛夫作了关于“苏联共产党第十九次代表大会和斯大林同志的经典著作‘苏联社会主义经济问题’的历史意义”的报告。
    第一次会议还听取了另外两个报告：马克思—恩格斯—列宁学院副院长阿·维·图舒诺夫的“斯大林同志论现代资本主义和社会主义的基本经济法则”和历史学博士纳·伊·夏塔金的“斯大林同志论从社会主义过渡到共产主义的三个基本先决条件”。
    会议将继续举行。
