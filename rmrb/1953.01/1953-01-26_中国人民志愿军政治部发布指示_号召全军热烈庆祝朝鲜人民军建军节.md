Title: Chinese People's Volunteer Army Political Department Issues Directive Calling on the Entire Army to Warmly Celebrate the Korean People's Army Founding Day
Author:
Date: 1953-01-26
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Korean Front, January 24th: The Political Department of the Chinese People's Volunteer Army recently issued a directive to all units of the Volunteer Army on welcoming and celebrating the 5th anniversary of the founding of the Korean People's Army. The directive stated that February 8th is the 5th anniversary of the founding of the Korean People's Army. To celebrate this festival, all units of the Volunteer Army should widely publicize the glorious and great struggle deeds of the Korean People's Army since its establishment, especially publicizing the major victories of the Korean People's Army in the war against America and its great contributions to safeguarding world peace; calling on all officers and soldiers to learn from the heroic fighting and arduous spirit of the Korean People's Army, learn the military technology and combat experience of the People's Army, so as to further enhance the unity of the Chinese and Korean people's armies, fight side by side more closely, and prepare to crush any military offensive by the enemy at any time. The Political Department of the Volunteer Army notified all units in the directive that they should hold celebration or discussion meetings according to the situation of the troops; the newspapers of each unit can publish celebratory articles, publish pictures related to the Korean War, and show Korean films such as "Return to the Front" and "Young Guerrilla" in the troops in turn. Each unit should also appoint delegations or representatives to attend the celebration meetings or commemorative meetings held by local Korean government agencies, the people, or Korean People's Army units to express warm congratulations.

### 中国人民志愿军政治部发布指示  号召全军热烈庆祝朝鲜人民军建军节

1953-01-26
第1版()
专栏：

    中国人民志愿军政治部发布指示
    号召全军热烈庆祝朝鲜人民军建军节
    【新华社朝鲜前线二十四日电】中国人民志愿军政治部最近向志愿军各部队发布了迎接与庆祝朝鲜人民军第五届建军节的指示。指示中说，二月八日，是朝鲜人民军建军五周年纪念日。为庆祝这个节日，志愿军各部队应广泛宣传朝鲜人民军建军以来光荣伟大的斗争事迹，尤其是宣传朝鲜人民军在抗美战争中的重大胜利及对保卫世界和平事业的伟大贡献；号召全军干部、战士学习朝鲜人民军英勇杀敌、坚苦卓绝的战斗精神，学习人民军的军事技术和战斗经验，以进一步增强中朝人民军队的团结，更紧密地并肩作战，准备随时粉碎敌人的任何军事进攻。志愿军政治部在指示中通知各部队，应根据部队情况分别召开纪念会或座谈会进行庆祝；各部队报纸可发表庆祝文章，刊登有关朝鲜战争的图片，并在部队中轮回放映“重返前线”、“少年游击队”等朝鲜影片。各部队还应指派代表团或代表应邀参加当地朝鲜政府机关、人民群众或朝鲜人民军部队所召开的庆祝会或纪念会，表示热烈祝贺。
