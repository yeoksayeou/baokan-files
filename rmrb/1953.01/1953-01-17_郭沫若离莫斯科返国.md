Title: Guo Moruo Departs Moscow for Return to China
Author:
Date: 1953-01-17
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, 16th: Guo Moruo, Chairman of the Chinese People's Committee for World Peace and President of the Chinese Academy of Sciences, left Moscow by plane on the 15th to return to China. During his stay in Moscow, Guo Moruo visited factories, theaters, and concert halls.

At Vnukovo Airport, Guo Moruo was seen off by Nikolai Tikhonov, Chairman of the Soviet Peace Committee; Denisov, Chairman of the Soviet Society for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries; members of the Soviet Peace Committee; senior officials from the Soviet Ministry of Foreign Affairs and the Soviet Society for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries; and Zhang Wentian, Ambassador Extraordinary and Plenipotentiary of the People's Republic of China to the Soviet Union, and officials from the Chinese Embassy.

### 郭沫若离莫斯科返国

1953-01-17
第1版()
专栏：

    郭沫若离莫斯科返国
    【新华社十六日讯】塔斯社莫斯科十六日讯：中国人民保卫世界和平委员会主席、中国科学院院长郭沫若，于十五日离莫斯科乘飞机返国。郭沫若在莫斯科期间曾参观了工厂、剧院和音乐厅。
    在伏努科夫机场欢送郭沫若的有：苏联拥护和平委员会主席吉洪诺夫、苏联对外文化协会委员会主席杰尼索夫、苏联拥护和平委员会的委员们、苏联外交部和苏联对外文化协会的高级人员们和中华人民共和国驻苏联特命全权大使张闻天和中国大使馆的官员们。
