Title: China Establishes Consulate-General in Bombay, India
Author:
Date: 1953-01-09
Page: 1

    China Establishes Consulate-General in Bombay, India
    [Xinhua News Agency, New Delhi, 8th] The Consulate-General of the People's Republic of China in Bombay, India, was established on January 7th. Consul-General Yao Nian arrived in Bombay on the 3rd to take office.

### 我国在印度孟买设总领事馆

1953-01-09
第1版()
专栏：

    我国在印度孟买设总领事馆
    【新华社新德里八日电】中华人民共和国驻印度孟买总领事馆在一月七日设立。总领事姚念已于三日到达孟买就任。
