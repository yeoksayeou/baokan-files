Title: World Federation of Trade Unions and Other Organizations Send New Year Greetings to ACFTU

Author: 
Date: 1953-01-03
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, January 2nd. The All-China Federation of Trade Unions (ACFTU) has continued to receive telegrams from Louis Saillant, Secretary-General of the World Federation of Trade Unions; Toledano, President of the Latin American Labor Confederation; Vittorio, Secretary-General of the Italian General Confederation of Labor; and the French General Confederation of Labor, congratulating the Chinese working class on achieving even greater successes in the struggle for a better future and world peace in 1953.

### 世界工联等团体  致电我总工会贺年

1953-01-03
第4版()
专栏：

    世界工联等团体
    致电我总工会贺年
    【新华社二日讯】中华全国总工会继续收到世界工会联合会总书记路易·赛扬、拉丁美洲劳工联盟主席托列达诺、意大利总工会总书记维多里奥和法国总工会的来电，祝贺中国工人阶级一九五三年在争取更美好的将来和世界和平的斗争中获得更伟大的成就。‘
