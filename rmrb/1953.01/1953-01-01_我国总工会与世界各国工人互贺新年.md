Title: All-China Federation of Trade Unions Exchanges New Year Greetings with Workers Around the World
Author:
Date: 1953-01-01
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency dispatch: On the eve of the New Year of 1953, the All-China Federation of Trade Unions sent a telegram to the World Federation of Trade Unions, extending warm New Year greetings on behalf of the Chinese working class to the World Federation of Trade Unions – the leader of the world trade union movement.

The All-China Federation of Trade Unions sent a telegram to the All-Union Central Council of Trade Unions on the 30th, saying: Under the leadership of the Communist Party of the Soviet Union headed by the great Comrade Stalin, the Soviet working class is victoriously implementing the Fifth Five-Year Plan, making brilliant contributions to creating the most beautiful future for mankind, and inspiring working people all over the world to fight for their freedom, happiness, and lasting world peace.

The All-China Federation of Trade Unions sent a telegram to the General Federation of Trade Unions of Korea, saying: Chinese workers are willing to work with you to increase production, practice strict economy, and overfulfill production tasks to support the Korean front and strive for the victorious conclusion of the Korean War.

The All-China Federation of Trade Unions sent telegrams to the Central Council of Trade Unions of Mongolia, the Central Council of Trade Unions of Poland, the Central Council of Trade Unions of Czechoslovakia, the Central Council of Trade Unions of Hungary, the Central Council of Trade Unions of Bulgaria, the General Confederation of Labor of Romania, and the Central Council of Trade Unions of Albania, congratulating them on the New Year and wishing them brilliant victories in the struggle for socialist construction and the defense of world peace in 1953.

In telegrams to the General Council of Trade Unions of Japan, the All-Japan Congress of Industrial Unions, the Japan Liaison Bureau of the World Federation of Trade Unions, and the Japan Joint Wage Struggle Liaison Council, the All-China Federation of Trade Unions said: On behalf of the entire Chinese working class, we extend fraternal warm congratulations and the highest and deepest respect to the Japanese working class, who are enduring severe suffering and waging a resolute and heroic struggle under the cruel rule of the U.S. and Japanese reactionaries. The Chinese working class is willing to unite more closely with the Japanese working class in the great year of 1953 and fully support the struggle of the Japanese working class!

The All-China Federation of Trade Unions sent New Year's congratulatory telegrams to the Central Committee of the Free German Trade Union Federation, the All-India Trade Union Congress, the Indian Labor Association, the Indian United Trade Union Congress, the Pakistan Federation of Labor, the All Burma Trade Union Congress, the Indonesian Central Trade Union Organization, the Ceylon Federation of Labor, the Tunisian Workers' Union, the Algerian Trade Union Liaison Committee, the Latin American Labor Confederation, the General Confederation of Labor of France, the Italian General Confederation of Labor, and workers in the Union of South Africa, Britain, and the United States, wishing them greater achievements in 1953 in leading the working classes of their respective countries to strengthen unity, strive to improve their lives, defend trade union rights, and defend peace and freedom.

The All-China Federation of Trade Unions received New Year's telegrams from the All-Union Central Council of Trade Unions, the Central Council of Trade Unions of Mongolia, the Central Council of Trade Unions of Czechoslovakia, the General Secretary of the Central Council of Trade Unions of Hungary István Kristóf, the Chairman of the General Confederation of Labor of Romania Moraru, the Chairman of the Central Council of Trade Unions of Albania Biro Pirishti, and the Executive Committee of the Free German Trade Union Federation. These congratulatory telegrams wished the Chinese people greater achievements in economic construction and the struggle to defend world peace in 1953.

### 我国总工会与世界各国工人互贺新年

1953-01-01
第4版()
专栏：

    我国总工会与世界各国工人互贺新年
    据新华社讯：在一九五三年新年前夕，中华全国总工会致电世界工会联合会，代表中国工人阶级向世界工会联合会——世界工会运动的领导者致热烈的新年祝贺。
    中华全国总工会三十日致电全苏工会中央理事会说：苏联工人阶级正在以伟大的斯大林同志为首的苏联共产党领导下胜利地实现着第五个五年计划，为创立人类最美好的未来作了光辉的贡献，鼓舞了全世界劳动人民为自己的自由幸福和世界的持久和平而斗争。
    中华全国总工会致电朝鲜职业总同盟说：中国工人愿与你们一道，增加生产，厉行节约，超额完成生产任务，来支援朝鲜前线，为实现胜利地结束朝鲜战争而奋斗。
    中华全国总工会曾致电蒙古工会中央理事会、波兰工会中央理事会、捷克斯洛伐克工会中央理事会、匈牙利工会中央理事会、保加利亚总工会中央理事会、罗马尼亚总工会和阿尔巴尼亚总工会中央理事会，祝贺新年，并祝他们一九五三年在社会主义建设和保卫世界和平的斗争中获得辉煌的胜利。
    中华全国总工会在给日本工会总评议会、全日本产业别工会会议、世界工联日本联络事务局、日本工资共同斗争联络会的电文说：我们谨代表全中国工人阶级向因美、日反动派残酷统治，处于严重苦难境地而进行着坚决英勇斗争的日本工人阶级，致以兄弟般的热烈的祝贺与崇高的深厚敬意。中国工人阶级愿与日本工人阶级在伟大的一九五三年中，更亲密地团结在一起，并以全力支持日本工人阶级的斗争！
    中华全国总工会曾分别向自由德国工会联合会中央委员会、全印总工会、印度劳工协会、印度统一工会大会、巴基斯坦总工会、全缅工会大会、印尼总工会、锡兰工会联合会、突尼斯工人工会、阿尔及利亚工会联络委员会、拉丁美洲劳工联盟、法国总工会、意大利总工会以及南非联邦、英国和美国的工人，发出了新年贺电。祝他们一九五三年在领导各国工人阶级加强团结统一，争取改善生活及保卫工会权利、保卫和平与自由的斗争中取得更大的成就。
    我中华全国总工会收到了全苏工会中央理事会、蒙古工会中央理事会、捷克斯洛伐克工会中央理事会、匈牙利工会中央理事会总书记伊斯特万·克里斯托夫、罗马尼亚总工会主席莫拉鲁、阿尔巴尼亚总工会中央理事会主席比罗·皮里斯特利和自由德国工会联合会执行委员会的贺年电。这些贺电祝我国人民一九五三年在经济建设和保卫世界和平的斗争中获得更伟大的成就。
