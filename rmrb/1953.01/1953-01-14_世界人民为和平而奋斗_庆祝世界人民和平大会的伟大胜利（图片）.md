The People of the World Fight for Peace
Celebrating the Great Victory of the World People's Peace Congress

    This is the exterior of the Vienna Concert Hall, the capital of Austria. The World People's Peace Congress, a great event in human history, was held here from December 12 to December 20, 1952. (Photo by Yuan Ling, Xinhua News Agency)

    Joliot-Curie, Chairman of the World People's Peace Congress and Chairman of the World Peace Council, delivers the opening speech. (TASS Draft)

    The solemn scene of the Presidium of the World People's Peace Congress. (Photo by Yuan Ling, Xinhua News Agency)

    A portion of the delegates from the Chinese delegation attending the World People's Peace Congress (Song Qingling, head of the Chinese delegation, is in the center). (Photo by Yuan Ling, Xinhua News Agency)

    Delegates from the Soviet delegation attending the World People's Peace Congress. (Photo by Yuan Ling, Xinhua News Agency)

    When the Chinese delegation attending the World People's Peace Congress arrived in Vienna on December 11, 1952, they were warmly welcomed by members of the World Peace Council, peace delegates from various countries, and the people of Vienna. (Photo by Yuan Ling, Xinhua News Agency)

    Delegates from the Indian delegation attending the World People's Peace Congress. (TASS Draft)

    Some of the delegates from various African delegations attending the World People's Peace Congress together. (TASS Draft)

    On December 20, 1952, at the closing session of the World People's Peace Congress, after the resolution was passed, all the delegates stood up and applauded to celebrate the successful conclusion of the Congress. (TASS Draft)
The Chinese delegation attending the World People's Peace Congress presents gifts to the Austrian delegation to thank them for their contribution to the Congress and their warm hospitality to the Chinese delegation. (Photo by Yuan Ling, Xinhua News Agency)

    The Chinese delegation joins in a gathering with delegations from Korea, Vietnam, Mongolia, Burma, Indonesia, Japan, Malaya, the Philippines, Pakistan, Thailand, and Laos. Guo Moruo, deputy head of the Chinese delegation, delivers a welcome speech to the delegates from various countries. (Photo by Yuan Ling, Xinhua News Agency)

### 世界人民为和平而奋斗  庆祝世界人民和平大会的伟大胜利（图片）

1953-01-14
第5版()
专栏：

    世界人民为和平而奋斗
    庆祝世界人民和平大会的伟大胜利
    这是奥地利首都维也纳音乐厅的外景。一九五二年十二月十二日至十二月二十日，人类历史上具有伟大意义的世界人民和平大会就在这里举行。（新华社记者袁苓摄）
    世界人民和平大会主席、世界和平理事会主席约里奥·居里致开幕词。　　　（塔斯社稿）
    世界人民和平大会主席台的庄严景象。（新华社记者袁苓摄）
    出席世界人民和平大会的我国代表团的一部分代表（中为我国代表团宋庆龄团长）。　　（新华社记者袁苓摄）
    出席世界人民和平大会的苏联代表团代表。（新华社记者袁苓摄）
    出席世界人民和平大会的我国代表团于一九五二年十二月十一日抵达维也纳时，受到世界和平理事会理事、各国和平代表和维也纳人民的热烈欢迎。　（新华社记者袁苓摄）
    出席世界人民和平大会的印度代表团代表。　（塔斯社稿）
    出席世界人民和平大会的非洲各国代表团的部分代表们在一起。（塔斯社稿）
    一九五二年十二月二十日在世界人民和平大会闭幕会上，大会通过决议后，全体代表们起立鼓掌庆贺大会的胜利成功。　　（塔斯社稿）我国出席世界人民和平大会代表团向奥地利代表团献礼，感谢他们对大会的贡献和对我国代表团的热情款待。（新华社记者袁苓摄）
    我国代表团与朝鲜、越南、蒙古、缅甸、印度尼西亚、日本、马来亚、菲律宾、巴基斯坦、泰国、寮国等代表团联欢。我国代表团副团长郭沫若向各国代表致欢迎词。　　（新华社记者袁苓摄）
