Title: People of All Ethnic Groups in Xinjiang Support the Convening of the National People's Congress; Capital Scientific Workers Determined to Redouble Their Efforts to Welcome the Convening of the Congress
Author:
Date: 1953-01-19
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, Dihua, 18th. People from all ethnic groups and all walks of life in Xinjiang Province warmly support the convening of the National People's Congress and local people's congresses at all levels. Bao Erhan, Chairman of the Xinjiang Provincial People's Government and Director of the Preparatory Committee of the China Islamic Association, said: "The convening of the National People's Congress and local people's congresses at all levels is the hope of the Chinese people for decades. It will make the big family of the People's Republic of China more prosperous, the friendship between people of all ethnic groups stronger, and the victory won by the Chinese people through long-term arduous struggle more consolidated. I would like to represent the 5.3 million people of 13 ethnic groups in Xinjiang in warmly and sincerely supporting this wise decision." Saifuding, Vice Chairman of the Xinjiang Provincial People's Government and Chairman of the Xinjiang People's Democratic League, said: "In the past three years, under the wise leadership of the Chinese Communist Party and Chairman Mao, people of all ethnic groups have achieved great victories on all fronts of the people's revolutionary cause and have begun to enter a planned, large-scale national economic construction. On the basis of this victory, it is necessary and timely to convene the National People's Congress and local people's congresses at all levels. People of all ethnic minorities in Xinjiang Province must work hard to do a good job in the general election and to realize this great system of people's democratic power." Laxi Da, Deputy Director of the Xinjiang Provincial Democratic Women's Federation, said: "Under the leadership of Chairman Mao and the Communist Party, our motherland has made rapid progress in the past three years. People of all ethnic groups in Xinjiang, especially women of all ethnic groups, have felt the warmth of the motherland's big family since liberation. I wholeheartedly support the resolution to convene the National People's Congress and local people's congresses at all levels, which will further encourage women of all ethnic groups in Xinjiang to actively participate in the motherland's construction undertakings."
    Xinhua News Agency, 18th. Scientific workers in the capital held a forum on the 18th, unanimously supporting the convening of the National People's Congress and local people's congresses at all level. The All China Federation of Natural Science Societies and the All China Association for Science and Technology Popularization also issued declarations on the same day, supporting the convening of the National People's Congress.
    The forum was presided over by Li Siguang, Chairman of the All China Federation of Natural Science Societies, and Liang Xi, Chairman of the All China Association for Science and Technology Popularization. More than 30 people attended, including standing committee members of the two associations and heads of various specialized societies. At the meeting, Li Siguang and Liang Xi gave detailed explanations of the great significance of convening the National People's Congress and local people's congresses at all levels. The participants unanimously agreed in their speeches that the convening of the National People's Congress and local people's congresses at all levels will further promote the enthusiasm of the people across the country, enabling the country's construction work to proceed smoothly and achieve great victories. Hou Debang, Vice Chairman of the All China Federation of Natural Science Societies and the Chinese Institute of Chemical Engineers, said: We have only been building our country for more than three years. Due to the accelerated implementation of various reforms and construction work, we are now in a position to convene the National People's Congress and local people's congresses at all levels. This is a very great achievement, and we have enough confidence to complete this task. Fang Shishan of the Chinese Medical Association said: The decision of the Central People's Government to convene the National People's Congress and local people's congresses at all levels is wise, which will further exert the power of the people across the country. Shi Zhiren of the Chinese Mechanical Engineering Society proposed to conduct publicity in science journals to mobilize scientific workers across the country to participate in the preparations for the convening of the People's Congress. Wang Junkui of the Preparatory Committee of the Chinese Aeronautical Engineering Society proposed that scientific workers study the documents on democratic nation-building in order to strengthen their understanding of the convening of the National People's Congress and local people's congresses at all levels, and do a good job in the general election. Their proposals were warmly echoed by the participants. Zhu Kezhen, Ding Xilin, Mao Yisheng, Yuan Hanqing, Cheng Shaojiong, Liang Sicheng and others all spoke enthusiastically at the meeting. They unanimously stated that scientific workers should participate in the country's construction work with redoubled efforts to welcome the convening of the National People's Congress.

### 新疆各族人士拥护召开全国人民代表大会  首都科学工作者决以加倍的努力来迎接大会的召开

1953-01-19
第1版()
专栏：

    新疆各族人士拥护召开全国人民代表大会
    首都科学工作者决以加倍的努力来迎接大会的召开
    【新华社迪化十八日电】新疆省各族各界人士热烈拥护召开全国和地方各级人民代表大会。新疆省人民政府主席、中国伊斯兰教协会筹备委员会主任鲍尔汉说：“召开全国和地方各级人民代表大会是中国人民数十年来的希望。它将使中华人民共和国大家庭更加繁荣，各民族人民之间的友谊更为加强，中国人民经过长期艰苦斗争所获得的胜利更加巩固。我谨代表新疆十三个民族五百三十万人民热烈地、衷心地拥护这一英明的决定。”新疆省人民政府副主席、新疆人民民主同盟主席赛福鼎说：“三年来，各族人民在中国共产党和毛主席的英明领导下，人民革命事业的各个战线上都获得了伟大的胜利，并开始进入有计划的大规模的国家经济建设。在这个胜利基础上，召开全国人民代表大会及地方各级人民代表大会，是必需的、适时的。新疆省各少数民族人民一定要为做好普选工作，为实现这一伟大的人民民主政权制度而努力。”新疆省民主妇女联合会副主任拉西达说：“我们祖国三年来在毛主席、共产党领导下有着飞跃的进步，新疆的各族人民，尤其是各族妇女，在解放以来特别感到祖国大家庭的温暖。我衷心拥护召开全国及地方各级人民代表大会的决议，它将更加鼓舞着新疆各族妇女积极参加祖国各项建设事业。”
    【新华社十八日讯】在首都的科学工作者十八日举行座谈会，一致拥护召开全国和地方各级人民代表大会。中华全国自然科学专门学会联合会和中华全国科学技术普及协会并于同日发表宣言，拥护召开全国人民代表大会。
    座谈会由中华全国自然科学专门学会联合会主席李四光和中华全国科学技术普及协会主席梁希主持，出席的有两会常务委员和各专门学会负责人共三十多人。会上，李四光和梁希对召开全国人民代表大会和地方各级人民代表大会的伟大意义作了详细说明。与会者在发言中都一致认为全国和地方各级人民代表大会的召开，将进一步发挥全国人民的积极性，使国家的建设工作顺利进行并取得伟大的胜利。中华全国自然科学专门学会联合会副主席、中国化学工程学会侯德榜说：我们建国才三年多，由于加速进行各项改革和建设工作，现在已经具备了召开全国和地方各级人民代表大会的条件，这是非常伟大的成就，我们有足够的信心来完成这一任务。中国医学会方石珊说：中央人民政府召开全国和地方各级人民代表大会的决定是英明的，这将进
    一步发挥全国人民的力量。中国机械工程学会石志仁提议在科学界出版的刊物上进行宣传，以便动员全国科学工作者参加召开人民代表大会的准备工作。中国航空工程学会筹委会王俊奎提议科学工作者学习民主建政文件，以便加强对召开全国和地方各级人民代表大会的认识，做好普选工作。他们的提议，都受到了与会者的热烈响应。竺可桢、丁西林、茅以升、袁翰青、程绍迥、梁思成等都在会上热烈发言，他们一致表示：科学工作者要在参加国家建设工作中，以加倍的努力来迎接全国人民代表大会的召开。
