The Ministry of Agriculture of the Central People's Government Issues an Order to Award Agricultural Patriotic Model Laborers for High Yield in 1952, and Announces the First Batch of Agricultural High Yield Award List for 1952

The Ministry of Agriculture of the Central People's Government issued an order on December 30, 1952, to award patriotic model laborers for high yield in the agricultural patriotic high-yield movement in 1952, and announced the first batch of agricultural high-yield award list for 1952. The full text of the order is as follows:

Order of the Ministry of Agriculture of the Central People's Government

In 1952, the patriotic high-yield movement on China's agricultural production front achieved greater success. High-yield units emerged everywhere, the area of high yield expanded significantly, and examples of high yield in entire counties appeared. Many of the patriotic high-yield models created new records higher than those of 1951; at the same time, outstanding model workers also emerged among the cadres engaged in agricultural work. The first batch of agricultural high-yield award list for 1952 is hereby announced, and awards will be given in accordance with the "Interim Measures for Agricultural High-Yield Awards in 1952." Those with outstanding achievements will be awarded the Patriotic High-Yield Gold Star Medal.

All patriotic high-yield models and model workers must continue to advance on the basis of the high yield in 1952; all farmers and agricultural workers and cadres should learn from their high-yield experiences and advanced leadership experiences, and strive for greater agricultural high-yield achievements in 1953 to welcome large-scale economic construction!

This order

Minister Li Shucheng, December 30, 1952

### 中央人民政府农业部颁发命令奖励一九五二年农业爱国丰产模范  并公布一九五二年度第一批农业丰产奖励名单
李书城
1953-01-06
第2版()
专栏：

    中央人民政府农业部颁发命令奖励一九五二年农业爱国丰产模范
    并公布一九五二年度第一批农业丰产奖励名单
    中央人民政府农业部在一九五二年十二月三十日颁发命令，奖励一九五二年农业爱国丰产运动中的爱国丰产模范，并公布一九五二年度第一批农业丰产奖励名单。命令全文如下：
    中央人民政府农业部命令
    一九五二年我国农业生产战线上的爱国丰产运动获得了更大的成绩。丰产单位到处涌现，丰产面积显著扩大，并出现了全县丰产的范例。其中不少爱国丰产模范创造了较一九五一年更高的新纪录；同时，从事农业工作的干部中，也出现了优秀的模范工作者。兹特将一九五二年度第一批农业丰产奖励名单予以公布，并根据“一九五二年农业丰产奖励暂行办法”给予奖励，其中成绩卓越者，特授予爱国丰产金星奖章。
    所有爱国丰产模范和模范工作者，要在一九五二年丰产的基础上继续前进；全体农民和农业工作干部，应学习他们的丰产经验和先进领导经验，为迎接大规模经济建设，争取一九五三年更大的农业丰产成绩而奋斗！
    此令
    部长  李书城  一九五二年十二月三十日
