In addition to all the members of the Sino-Soviet Joint Committee, those who attended the celebration of the transfer ceremony of the Chinese Changchun Railway included Zhou Enlai, Premier of the State Administration Council and Minister of Foreign Affairs of the Central People's Government of China, Pan Youxin, Ambassador Extraordinary and Plenipotentiary of the Soviet Union to China, Teng Daiyuan, Minister of Railways of the Central People's Government of China, Wu Xiuquan, Deputy Minister of Foreign Affairs, and Gao Chongmin, Vice Chairman of the Northeast People's Government. This is a picture of Premier Zhou Enlai and others on the presidium of the celebration meeting.

On December 31, 1952, the Soviet government transferred all rights to the Chinese Changchun Railway, jointly managed by China and the Soviet Union, and all property belonging to the railway to the Chinese government free of charge. A grand celebration meeting for the transfer ceremony of the Chinese Changchun Railway was held at the Harbin Railway Cultural Center on the same day.

Premier Zhou taking a group photo with the Soviet experts who received commemorative medals.

At the celebration meeting for the transfer ceremony of the Chinese Changchun Railway, all Chinese and Soviet members of the Sino-Soviet Joint Committee signed the final protocol and inventory of property. This is a picture of our representative Yu Guangsheng (right) and Soviet representative Yelogorov (left) signing the documents.

In order to thank the Soviet experts for their great help to the cause of railway construction for the Chinese people, Zhou Enlai, Premier of the State Administration Council and Minister of Foreign Affairs of the Central People's Government of China, presented the Soviet experts with the "Chinese Changchun Railway Commemorative Medal" on behalf of the Central People's Government. This is a picture of Premier Zhou presenting the commemorative medal to Soviet expert Yelogorov at the award ceremony.

Minister Teng Daiyuan, on behalf of the Ministry of Railways, the Political Department of the Ministry of Railways, and the National Committee of the Railway Trade Union of China, awarded the National Railway Winning Rotating Red Flag to the Chinese Changchun Railway Administration, to be permanently preserved in the Memorial Hall of the Sino-Soviet Joint Management of the Chinese Changchun Railway.

Zhou Enlai, Premier of the State Administration Council and Minister of Foreign Affairs of the Central People's Government of China, speaking at the celebration meeting.

Pan Youxin, Ambassador Extraordinary and Plenipotentiary of the Soviet Union to China, speaking at the celebration meeting.

Yu Guangsheng, our representative, reading the Chinese version of the final protocol of the Sino-Soviet Joint Committee at the celebration meeting.

Yelogorov, the leader of the Soviet members, reading the Russian version of the final protocol of the Sino-Soviet Joint Committee at the celebration meeting.

The Memorial Tower and Memorial Hall of the Sino-Soviet Joint Management of the Chinese Changchun Railway opened in Harbin on the afternoon of January 1. This is a picture of Minister Teng Daiyuan, representative of the Central People's Government, cutting the ribbon.

Teng Daiyuan, Minister of Railways of the Central People's Government of China, speaking at the celebration meeting.

Grunichev, Director of the Chinese Changchun Railway Administration, announced the order to all employees of the Chinese Changchun Railway to terminate the joint Sino-Soviet management of the Chinese Changchun Railway with immediate effect.

Yang Maolin, an advanced worker of the Chinese Changchun Railway, speaking at the celebration meeting.

The Chinese Changchun Railway Administration was renamed the Harbin Railway Administration, and Director Lu Ping delivered a speech upon taking office.

This is the unveiling ceremony of the Memorial Tower of the Sino-Soviet Joint Management of the Chinese Changchun Railway.

### 伟大人民的伟大友谊（图片）

1953-01-07
第5版()
专栏：

    伟大人民的伟大友谊
    出席中长铁路移交仪式庆祝大会的除中苏联合委员会全体委员外，还有我中央人民政府政务院总理兼外交部部长周恩来、苏联驻我国特命全权大使潘友新、我中央人民政府铁道部部长滕代远、外交部副部长伍修权和东北人民政府副主席高崇民等。这是周恩来总理等在庆祝大会主席台上的情形。
    一九五二年十二月三十一日，苏联政府将中苏共同管理中国长春铁路的一切权利以及属于该路的全部财产无偿地移交给我国政府。当天在哈尔滨铁路文化馆举行了盛大的中长铁路移交仪式庆祝大会。
    周总理与领受纪念章的苏联专家们合影。
    在中长铁路移交仪式庆祝大会上，由中苏联合委员会中苏双方全体委员在最后议定书和财产清单上签字。这是我方委员代表余光生（右）、苏方委员领导人叶洛果夫（左）正在签字时的情形。
    为了感谢苏联专家对中国人民铁路建设事业的巨大帮助，我中央人民政府政务院总理兼外交部部长周恩来代表中央人民政府授予苏联专家们以“中国长春铁路纪念章”。这是在授章典礼上周总理将纪念章授予苏联专家叶洛果夫时的情形。
    滕代远部长代表我中央人民政府铁道部、铁道部政治部和中国铁路工会全国委员会，将全国铁路优胜循环红旗授与中国长春铁路管理局，永远保存于中苏共同管理中国长春铁路纪念馆。
    我中央人民政府政务院总理兼外交部部长周恩来在庆祝大会上讲话。
    苏联驻我国特命全权大使潘友新在庆祝大会上讲话。
    我方委员代表余光生在庆祝大会上宣读中苏联合委员会的中文最后议定书。
    苏方委员领导人叶洛果夫在庆祝大会上宣读中苏联合委员会的俄文最后议定书。
    中苏共同管理中国长春铁路纪念塔和纪念馆于一月一月下午在哈尔滨开馆。这是我中央人民政府代表滕代远部长在剪彩时的情形。
    我中央人民政府铁道部部长滕代远在庆祝大会上讲话。
    中国长春铁路管理局局长格鲁尼切夫宣读给中长铁路全体职工自即时起停止中长铁路中苏共管的命令。
    中国长春铁路先进工作者杨茂林在庆祝大会上讲话。
    中国长春铁路管理局改名为哈尔滨铁路管理局，陆平局长就职致词。
    这是中苏共同管理中长铁路纪念塔的揭幕式。
    这是中苏共同管理中长铁路纪念馆的外景。
