Title: Japanese Peace Representative Kōichi Saiyonji Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1953-01-28
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, January 27th:

    Japanese representative to the World People's Peace Congress, Kōichi Saiyonji (Councilor), arrived in Beijing by plane on the 27th for a visit. Liu Guanqi, Secretary-General of the China Peace Committee, and Tang Mingzhao, Deputy Director of the Liaison Department, were among those who welcomed him at the airport.

### 日本和平代表西园寺公一抵京

1953-01-28
第1版()
专栏：

    日本和平代表西园寺公一抵京
    【新华社二十七日讯】出席世界人民和平大会的日本代表西园寺公一（参议员），二十七日乘飞机抵北京访问。到机场欢迎的有中国人民保卫世界和平委员会秘书长刘贯一、联络部副部长唐明照等人。
