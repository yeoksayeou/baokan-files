Title: Central-South Henan Minority Nationalities Visiting Group Extends Greetings to Minority Nationalities in Various Parts of Henan

Author:

Date: 1953-01-19
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Kaifeng, January 15th: The Central-South Henan Minority Nationalities Visiting Group, composed of over 100 cadres from nine units, including the Ethnic Affairs Commission of the Central-South Military and Administrative Committee, the United Front Work Department of the CCP Central-South Bureau, and the Central Government's Ethnic Affairs Commission Central-South Ethnic Work Inspection Group, arrived in Kaifeng on the 14th from Wuhan. They will extend greetings to the Hui and other minority nationalities in various parts of Henan Province, publicize ethnic policies, and convey Chairman Mao's and the Central People's Government's concern for the people of all minority nationalities. Wu Zhipu, Chairman of the Henan Provincial People's Government, Ren Zhiming, Vice Chairman of the Henan Provincial People's Representative Conference Consultative Committee, Liu Hongwen, Deputy Minister of the United Front Work Department of the CCP Henan Provincial Committee, Ma Yunwu, member of the Preparatory Committee of the China Islamic Association and representative of the Hui people of Kaifeng, and representatives of various minority nationalities and organizations, totaling more than 2,000 people, went to the station to welcome them. A grand get-together of fraternal nationalities in Henan was held that evening.

The visiting group is headed by Zhang Zhiyi, Chairman of the Central-South Ethnic Affairs Commission and Minister of the United Front Work Department of the CCP Central-South Bureau, with Chen Jingshe (Hui), Xiong Shouqi, Vice Chairmen of the Central-South Ethnic Affairs Commission, Li Minghao, Deputy Minister of the Ministry of Civil Affairs of the Central-South Military and Administrative Committee, and Ma Jie (Hui), head of the Central Ethnic Affairs Commission Central-South Ethnic Work Inspection Group, as deputy heads. The visiting group has a cultural troupe, a film team, and a photo exhibition team, carrying films, pictures, and various books on ethnic policies, and has prepared magic lanterns, portraits of leaders, banners, commemorative badges, and other gifts in large quantities, which will be distributed to the minority nationalities in Henan.

### 中南河南少数民族访问团  慰问河南各地少数民族人民

1953-01-19
第1版()
专栏：

    中南河南少数民族访问团
    慰问河南各地少数民族人民
    【新华社开封十五日电】由中南军政委员会民族事务委员会、中共中央中南局统一战线工作部和中央人民政府民族事务委员会中南民族工作视察组等九个单位的一百多名干部组成的中南河南少数民族访问团，已于十四日自武汉抵达开封，慰问河南省各地的回族及各少数民族人民，宣传民族政策，传达毛主席和中央人民政府对各少数民族人民的关怀。前往车站欢迎的有河南省人民政府主席吴芝圃，省各界人民代表会议协商委员会副主席任芝铭，中共河南省委员会统一战线工作部副部长刘鸿文，中国伊斯兰教协会筹备委员会委员、开封市回民代表马运五及各少数民族、各机关团体的代表共二千多人。当晚，并举行了盛大的河南各兄弟民族联欢会。
    访问团由中南民族事务委员会主任委员、中共中央中南局统一战线工作部部长张执一任团长，中南民族事务委员会副主任委员陈经畬（回族）、熊寿祺，中南军政委员会民政部副部长李明灏，中央民族事务委员会中南民族工作视察组组长马杰（回族）任副团长。访问团备有文工团、电影队及图片展览小组等，携带了影片、图片和有关民族政策的书籍多种，并准备了幻灯机、领袖像、锦旗、纪念章等大批礼物，将分赠河南少数民族人民。
