Title: The Third Plenary Session of the Central Committee of the Revolutionary Committee of the Chinese Kuomintang Sends a Telegram to Chairman Mao Endorsing the Implementation of the People's Congress System
Author:
Date: 1953-01-17
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, January 16th: The Third Plenary (Enlarged) Session of the Second Central Committee of the Revolutionary Committee of the Chinese Kuomintang sent a telegram to Chairman Mao on the 16th, warmly endorsing the convocation of the National People's Congress and local people's congresses at all levels. The text of the telegram is as follows: Respected Chairman Mao: The Twentieth Meeting of the Central People's Government Committee resolved to convene the National People's Congress and local people's congresses at all levels. This is a great victory for the people of the whole country after a long struggle under your wise leadership. The implementation of the Constitution, which you personally led the drafting of, will be the beginning of a new and tremendous development in the political life of our people. All of our comrades are unanimously rejoiced and express sincere support for this great resolution.

　　Our meeting is conducting a heated discussion on how to greet the general election, implement the People's Congress system, and further consolidate the great political task of the people's democratic dictatorship. We assure you: Under your wise leadership and that of the Chinese Communist Party, we will mobilize all party members to conduct extensive propaganda to the masses, actively participate in various huge preparatory work, and strive to implement this great political task!

   The Third Plenary (Enlarged) Session of the Second Central Committee of the Revolutionary Committee of the Chinese Kuomintang
    January 16, 1953

### 中国国民党革命委员会中央委员会第三次全体会议  致电毛主席拥护实行人民代表大会制度

1953-01-17
第1版()
专栏：

    中国国民党革命委员会中央委员会第三次全体会议
    致电毛主席拥护实行人民代表大会制度
    【新华社十六日讯】中国国民党革命委员会第二届中央委员会第三次全体（扩大）会议十六日致电毛主席，热诚拥护召开全国人民代表大会及地方各级人民代表大会。电文如下：敬爱的毛主席： 中央人民政府委员会第二十次会议决议召开全国人民代表大会及地方各级人民代表大会。这是全国人民在您英明领导下久经奋斗才得到的伟大胜利。您亲自领导起草的宪法的实施，将是我们全国人民政治生活中一个新的巨大发展的开端。我们全体同志一致为这一伟大的决议欢欣鼓舞，并表示真诚的拥护。
    我们的会议对于如何迎接普选、实行人民代表大会制度、进一步巩固人民民主专政的伟大政治任务，正在进行热烈的讨论。我们向您保证：要在您和中国共产党的英明领导下，动员全体党员向人民群众进行广泛的宣传，积极地参加各项巨大的准备工作，为贯彻这一伟大的政治任务而奋斗！
   中国国民党革命委员会第二届中央委员会第三次全体（扩大）会议
    一九五三年一月十六日
