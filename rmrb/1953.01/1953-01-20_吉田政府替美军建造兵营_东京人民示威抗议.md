Title: Yoshida Government Builds Barracks for US Military; Tokyo People Demonstrate in Protest
Author:
Date: 1953-01-20
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Tokyo, 19th. Approximately four hundred Japanese residents demonstrated at the Japanese Foreign Ministry on the 17th to protest the Yoshida government's plan to build barracks for the stationed US military in Musashino City, which belongs to Tokyo.

The demonstrators held flags and placards, and they sent representatives, including the mayor of Musashino City, to negotiate with the Foreign Ministry. They opposed the relocation of the US troops stationed in the Tokyo urban area to Musashino and protested the US military's behavior at nearby military bases that corrupts social morals.

### 吉田政府替美军建造兵营  东京人民示威抗议

1953-01-20
第4版()
专栏：

    吉田政府替美军建造兵营
    东京人民示威抗议
    【新华社十九日讯】东京消息：约有四百名日本居民十七日在日本外务省举行示威，抗议吉田政府在属于东京的武藏野市内为驻日美军修建兵营的计划。
    参加示威的群众手里都举着旗子和标语牌，他们推出包括武藏野市长在内的代表向外务省进行交涉。他们反对把留驻在东京市区的美国军队移驻到武藏野，抗议美军在附近的军事基地败坏社会道德的行为。
