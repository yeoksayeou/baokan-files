Title: Soviet Embassy in France Awards Stalin Prize to Stie

Author:
Date: 1953-01-28
Page: 4

    Soviet Embassy in France
    Awards Stalin Prize to Stie
    [Xinhua News Agency Dispatch] TASS, Paris, January 25th: On January 24th, the Soviet Embassy in France held an award ceremony to present the certificate and medal of the Stalin Prize to French progressive writer André Stil. Stil's novel "The First Blow" earned him this prize.
    Soviet Ambassador to France, Pavlov, on behalf of the Soviet government, presented the certificate and medal to the writer and congratulated him.

### 苏联驻法国大使馆  授予斯梯以斯大林奖金

1953-01-28
第4版()
专栏：

    苏联驻法国大使馆
    授予斯梯以斯大林奖金
    【新华社讯】塔斯社巴黎二十五日电：一月二十四日，苏联驻法国大使馆举行了授奖仪式，以斯大林奖金获得者的奖状和奖章授予法国进步作家安德烈·斯梯。斯梯的小说“第一次打击”使他荣获了这个奖金。
    苏联驻法国大使巴夫洛夫代表苏联政府，以奖状和奖章授予这个作家，并且向他道贺。
