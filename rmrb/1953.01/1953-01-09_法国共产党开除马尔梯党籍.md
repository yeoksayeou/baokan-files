Title: French Communist Party Expels Marty from Party Membership
Author:
Date: 1953-01-09
Page: 4
    Xinhua News Agency, January 7th: TASS, Paris, January 5th: "L'Humanité" published a notice from the Seine Provincial Committee of the French Communist Party expelling André Marty from party membership. The notice is as follows: "Regarding the decision to expel from party membership: The Seine Provincial Committee convened on January 3, 1953, under the chairmanship of Madeleine Vincent, Secretary of the Provincial Committee, and, in accordance with Article 35 of the Party Constitution, reviewed the resolution unanimously passed by the Masson train station primary organization to expel André Marty from membership in the French Communist Party. The Provincial Committee reviewed the resolution passed by the Paris 13th District Party Committee on December 30, 1952, in which the District Committee unanimously agreed with the resolution of the Masson train station primary organization. After hearing the report of Comrade Bosse, a member of the Provincial Committee, the Seine Provincial Committee agreed to and approved these resolutions, and decided: to expel André Marty from membership in the French Communist Party."

### 法国共产党开除马尔梯党籍

1953-01-09
第4版()
专栏：

    法国共产党开除马尔梯党籍
    【新华社七日讯】塔斯社巴黎五日讯：“人道报”刊载了法国共产党塞纳省委员会开除安德烈·马尔梯党籍的通告，通告如下：“关于开除党籍的决议：塞纳省委员会于一九五三年一月三日在省委员会书记马德连·文生主持下开会，按照党章第三十五条审查马森火车站基层组织一致通过的关于开除安德烈·马尔梯法国共产党党籍的决议。省委员会审阅了巴黎第十三区党委员会在一九五二年十二月三十日通过的决议，区委员会一致同意马森火车站基层组织的决议。塞纳省委员会听取了省委员会委员波赛同志的报告后，同意并批准了这些决议，同时决定：开除安德烈·马尔梯法国共产党党籍。”
