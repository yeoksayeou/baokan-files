Title: Standing Committee of the CPPCC National Committee Holds Meeting; Decides to Convene the Fourth Session of the CPPCC National Committee Within the Month
Author:
Date: 1953-01-13
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, January 12th: The 44th enlarged meeting of the Standing Committee of the National Committee of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference was held on the 12th. The meeting decided to convene the Fourth Session of the First National Committee of the CPPCC within this month, and registration is scheduled to take place before January 27th.

The following attended and were present at the meeting: Vice-Chairmen of the National Committee Zhou Enlai, Li Jishen, Chen Shutong; Standing Committee members Li Weihan, Wang Kunlun, Jiang Guangnai, Huang Yanpei, Zhang Xiruo, Yang Xiufeng, Ulanhu, Deng Yingchao, Liang Xi, Chen Jiageng, Shao Lizi; Committee members in Beijing Chen Shaoxian, Yu Xinqing, Mei Gongbin, Luo Longji, Zhou Xinmin, Chu Tunan, Zeng Zhaolun, Sha Qianli, Fu Dingyi, Xu Guangping, Jiang Nanxiang, Lan Gongwu, Zhang Youyu, He Jifeng, Yi Lirong, Chen Shaomin, Shen Zijiu, Liao Chengzhi, Wu Han, Li Fanyi, Zhou Yang, Zheng Zhenduo, Li Siguang, Hou Debang, Ye Shengtao, Lin Liru, Pan Zhenya, Huan Xiang, Li Chengan, Zhu Zaoguan, Zhuang Mingli, Fei Zhendong, Ma Jian, Tao Menghe, Qian Changzhao, Li Shucheng, Ning Wu, Chen Jinkun, Chen Qiyuan, Zhang Shizhao, Huang Shaohong, Fu Zuoyi, Mei Lanfang; Members of the Central People's Government Committee Wu Yuzhang, Xu Teli, Liu Geping, Gao Chongmin, Peng Zemin, Chen Mingshu, Zhang Nanxian, Liu Yazi; Government Affairs Council members of the Government Administration Council Xie Juezai, Luo Ruiqing, Zeng Shan, Teng Daiyuan, as well as relevant staff members of the National Committee, the Central People's Government, and leaders of people's organizations, totaling more than 80 people.

### 政协全国委员会常委会举行会议  决定月内召开政协全国委员会第四次会议

1953-01-13
第1版()
专栏：

    政协全国委员会常委会举行会议
    决定月内召开政协全国委员会第四次会议
    【新华社十二日讯】中国人民政治协商会议全国委员会常务委员会第四十四次会议在十二日扩大举行。会议决定在本月份内召开人民政协第一届全国委员会第四次会议，并定于一月二十七日前报到。
    出席和列席会议的有全国委员会副主席周恩来、李济深、陈叔通，常务委员李维汉、王崑崙、蒋光鼐、黄炎培、章乃器、张奚若、杨秀峰、乌兰夫、邓颖超、梁希、陈嘉庚、邵力子，在京委员陈劭先、余心清、梅龚彬、罗隆基、周新民、楚图南、曾昭抡、沙千里、符定一、许广平、蒋南翔、蓝公武、张友渔、何基沣、易礼容、陈少敏、沈兹九、廖承志、吴晗、李范一、周扬、郑振铎、李四光、侯德榜、叶圣陶、林砺儒、潘震亚、宦乡、李承干、朱早观、庄明理、费振东、马坚、陶孟和、钱昌照、李书城、寗武、陈瑾昆、陈其瑗、章士钊、黄绍竑、傅作义、梅兰芳，中央人民政府委员会委员吴玉章、徐特立、刘格平、高崇民、彭泽民、陈铭枢、张难先、柳亚子，政务院政务委员谢觉哉、罗瑞卿、曾山、滕代远，以及全国委员会、中央人民政府的有关工作人员和人民团体的负责人等共八十余人。
