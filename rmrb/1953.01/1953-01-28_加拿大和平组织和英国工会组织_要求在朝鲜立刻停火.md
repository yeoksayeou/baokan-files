Title: Canadian Peace Organizations and British Trade Union Organizations Demand Immediate Ceasefire in Korea
Author:
Date: 1953-01-28
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, January 26th: According to TASS, Ottawa, January 25th: The National Council of the Canadian Peace Congress, which recently held a meeting in Ottawa, has issued an appeal to the Canadian government, requesting the Canadian government to try to bring about an immediate ceasefire in Korea.

The council emphasized that the continuation of the Korean War has endangered world peace and may lead to the expansion of the war. The council called on all Canadians to unite and demand an immediate ceasefire, and to support this appeal to the government.

The resolution of the council emphasized that no differences are impossible to resolve through peaceful negotiations. The resolution urged Canadians to study the resolutions of the Vienna World People's Peace Congress, and especially drew people's attention to the World People's Peace Congress's proposal to conclude a Five-Power Peace Pact and achieve a ceasefire in Korea.

Xinhua News Agency, January 26th: TASS, London, January 24th: According to the "Worker's Daily," the South Govan (Glasgow) branch of the Amalgamated Engineering Union has submitted a request to the union's executive committee, stating that in addition to India's proposal on the Korean issue at the United Nations, the Soviet Union's proposal on the Korean issue should also be sent to all union branches. The Glasgow branch of the Transport and General Workers' Union also expressed its demand for an immediate ceasefire in Korea.

Workers in a department of the Ford factory in Dagenham, Essex, signed a peace postcard demanding a ceasefire, the repatriation of all prisoners of war, and the withdrawal of British troops from Korea.

### 加拿大和平组织和英国工会组织  要求在朝鲜立刻停火

1953-01-28
第4版()
专栏：

    加拿大和平组织和英国工会组织
    要求在朝鲜立刻停火
    【新华社二十六日讯】据塔斯社渥太华二十五日讯：最近在渥太华举行会议的加拿大和平大会全国理事会向加拿大政府发出呼吁，要求加拿大政府设法促使在朝鲜立即实现停火。
    理事会强调指出：朝鲜战争的继续已危及世界和平，并可能引起战争的扩大。该会号召所有加拿大人一致起来要求立即停火，并支持向政府发出的这个呼吁。
    理事会的决议中强调指出：一切分歧，没有不可能用和平谈判解决的。决议敦促加拿大人研究维也纳世界人民和平大会的决议，并特别提请人们注意世界人民和平大会要求缔结五大国和平公约及实现朝鲜停火的建议。
    【新华社二十六日讯】塔斯社伦敦二十四日讯：据“工人日报”报道：混合机器工会南加凡（格拉斯哥）支会向工会执行委员会提出要求说，除了印度在联合国所提的关于朝鲜问题的提案以外，还应该把苏联所提出的关于朝鲜问题的提案也发给工会各支会。运输与普通工人工会格拉斯哥支会也表示要求在朝鲜立刻停火。
    达金汉姆·埃赛克斯地方的福特工厂一个部门的工人在一张和平明信片上签了名，要求停火、遣返所有战俘、并将英国军队撤出朝鲜。
