Title: Soong Ching Ling, Head of the Chinese Delegation to the World People's Peace Congress, Returns to Beijing; The China Peace Committee Hosts a Banquet for Peace Representatives from Argentina and Other Countries

Author: 
Date: 1953-01-25
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, January 24th: Soong Ching Ling, head of the Chinese delegation to the World People's Peace Congress, returned to Beijing by train on the morning of the 24th. Those who welcomed her at the station included: Guo Moruo, Chairman of the China Peace Committee; Peng Zhen, Vice Chairman; Liu Guan Yi, Secretary-General; Nie Zhen, Vice Chairman of the North China Branch of the China Peace Committee; Zhang Xiruo, Chairman of the Beijing Branch; and Wu Han, Vice Chairman.

Also welcoming her at the station were: Zhou Enlai and Chen Shutong, Vice Chairmen of the National Committee of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference; Lin Boqu, Xu Bing, Qi Yanming, Shao Lizi, Liu Yazi, Chen Shaoxian, Zhang Bojun, Li Zhuchen, Luo Shuzhang, Mo Yichang, Ma Xulun, Peng Zemin, Zhu Xuefan, Lai Ruoyu, Xu Guangping, Zeng Xianzhi, Sha Qianli, Wu Yunfu, Gu Jinxin, Cui Yueli, Hu Zhongchi, Yu Zhenying, Tang Mingzhao, and other representatives of democratic parties and people's organizations, as well as Chinese representatives to the World People's Peace Congress who had returned to Beijing earlier, including Wang Xier, Shi Liang, Zhu Bochen, Xiang Jinquan, Lü Hongbin, Wu Maosun, Shao Yu, Jin Zhonghua, Ma Yinchu, Ni Feijun, Chen Wengui, Chang Xiangyu, Xu Jingzhang, Mei Lanfang, Huang Jiasi, He Luting, Dong Yueqian, Dapusheng, Yang Lanhua, Cai Tingkai, Pan Changyou, Luo Longji, and Luo Qiong.

Xinhua News Agency, January 24th: The China Peace Committee held a banquet on the evening of the 24th to welcome peace representatives from various countries who came to our country for a visit after attending the World People's Peace Congress.

Invited to attend were: the Argentine delegation headed by Ernesto Giudici, the Australian delegation headed by Alan Douglas Brand, the Burmese delegation headed by Deqin Qimao, and Indonesian representatives Widoyo, Modido, Sunati, and Sugri Shunu.

Guo Moruo, Chairman of the China Peace Committee, Chen Shutong, Vice Chairman, Liu Guan Yi, Secretary-General, and Standing Committee members Ma Yinchu, Zhang Xiruo, and Luo Longji attended the reception. Representatives of people's organizations Zhu Xuefan, Cao Mengjun, Qian Sanqiang, Zhou Yang, and Ding Zan, as well as many Chinese representatives to the World People's Peace Congress, were also present.

Chairman Guo Moruo delivered a speech at the banquet, warmly welcoming the peace representatives from various countries and expressing that the Chinese people are willing to work together with the people of all countries to strive for the realization of the resolutions of the World People's Peace Congress. Peace representatives from Argentina, Australia, Burma, and Indonesia also delivered speeches at the meeting, thanking the Chinese people for their warm hospitality and frequently raising their glasses to toast "Long Live Peace!"

The banquet proceeded in a warm and friendly atmosphere.

### 出席世界人民和平大会中国代表团  宋庆龄团长回到北京  我保卫世界和平委员会欢宴阿根廷等国和平代表

1953-01-25
第1版()
专栏：

    出席世界人民和平大会中国代表团
    宋庆龄团长回到北京
    我保卫世界和平委员会欢宴阿根廷等国和平代表
    【新华社二十四日讯】出席世界人民和平大会中国代表团团长宋庆龄在二十四日上午乘火车回到北京。到车站欢迎的有：中国人民保卫世界和平委员会主席郭沫若，副主席彭真，秘书长刘贯一，中国人民保卫世界和平委员会华北总分会副主席聂真，北京市分会主席张奚若，副主席吴晗。
    到车站欢迎的还有：中国人民政治协商会议全国委员会副主席周恩来、陈叔通，各民主党派和各人民团体的代表林伯渠、徐冰、齐燕铭、邵力子、柳亚子、陈劭先、章伯钧、李烛尘、罗叔章、莫艺昌、马叙伦、彭泽民、朱学范、赖若愚、许广平、曾宪植、沙千里、伍云甫、顾锦心、崔月犁、胡仲持、于振瀛、唐明照，以及先行回到北京的出席世界人民和平大会的中国代表王洗耳、史良、朱伯琛、向金全、吕鸿宾、吴茂荪、邵宇、金仲华、马寅初、倪斐君、陈文贵、常香玉、许敬章、梅兰芳、黄家驷、贺绿汀、董越千、达浦生、杨兰花、蔡廷锴、潘长有、罗隆基、罗琼等。
    【新华社二十四日讯】中国人民保卫世界和平委员会在二十四日晚间举行宴会，欢迎出席世界人民和平大会后前来我国参观访问的各国和平代表。
    应邀出席的有：以恩内斯托·朱迪契为首的阿根廷代表团，以阿兰·道格拉斯·布兰德为首的澳大利亚代表团，以德钦漆貌为首的缅甸代表团，印度尼西亚代表威多约、莫迪多、苏纳第和苏格立许努。
    中国人民保卫世界和平委员会主席郭沫若、副主席陈叔通，秘书长刘贯一，常务委员马寅初、张奚若、罗隆基等出席招待。出席作陪的有各人民团体代表朱学范、曹孟君、钱三强、周扬、丁瓒和出席世界人民和平大会的中国代表多人。
    郭沫若主席在席间致词，热烈欢迎各国和平代表，并表示中国人民愿与各国人民共同努力，争取实现世界人民和平大会的决议。阿根廷、澳大利亚、缅甸和印尼等国和平代表也在会上致词，感谢中国人民的热烈招待，并频频举杯祝和平万岁。
    宴会在热烈友好的空气中进行。
