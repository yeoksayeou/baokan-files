Title: Various Places in China Commemorate the 29th Anniversary of Lenin's Death
Author:
Date: 1953-01-23
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency Dispatch: To commemorate the 29th anniversary of Lenin's death, report meetings and commemorative gatherings were held in various places in China. The Beijing Sino-Soviet Friendship Association and the Soviet Society for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries jointly held a report meeting at 7:00 PM on the 22nd. Approximately 600 representatives from various levels of the Sino-Soviet Friendship Association in Beijing and directly affiliated organizations of the Central People's Government participated in this report meeting. Soviet expert Kokalev gave a special report entitled "Commemorating the 29th Anniversary of Lenin's Death." The Soviet film "Lenin in October" was screened after the meeting. The Shanghai Sino-Soviet Friendship Association held a report meeting on the 20th, where Secretary-General Yao Qin reported on Lenin's life and deeds, and Lenin's great contributions to the revolutionary cause of the Soviet people, the Chinese people, and the people of the world. On the same day, the Shanghai Sino-Soviet Friendship Association exhibited pictures related to Lenin's life, work, and other aspects in the Maoming South Road exhibition corridor. Various branches and sub-branches of the Shanghai Sino-Soviet Friendship Association also conducted various commemorative activities. Major newspapers in Shanghai published pictorials and articles about Lenin's life on the 20th and 21st respectively. The Changsha Sino-Soviet Friendship Association held a meeting on the 21st to commemorate the 29th anniversary of Lenin's death, attended by more than 1,500 members. At the meeting, Tu Xichou, director of the Department of Economics at Hunan University, gave a report on Soviet economic construction. The Soviet film "The Unforgettable Year 1919" was screened after the meeting.

### 我国各地纪念列宁逝世廿九周年

1953-01-23
第1版()
专栏：

    我国各地纪念列宁逝世廿九周年
    据新华社讯：为了纪念列宁逝世二十九周年，我国各地分别举行报告会和纪念会。北京市中苏友好协会和苏联对外文化协会在二十二日晚七时联合举行报告会。参加这一报告会的有北京市及中央人民政府直属机关的中苏友好协会的各级组织代表约六百余人。苏联专家科卡列夫以“纪念列宁逝世二十九周年”为题，作了专题报告。会后放映了苏联影片：“列宁在十月”。上海市中苏友好协会在二十日举行报告会，由总干事姚溱报告列宁的生平事迹和列宁对苏联人民、中国人民和世界人民革命事业的伟大贡献。同日，上海市中苏友好协会在茂名南路展览走廊展出有关列宁的生活、工作等图片。上海市中苏友好协会所属各分会和支会，也进行了各种纪念活动。上海各大报分别在二十日、二十一日出版了有关列宁生平的画刊和文章。长沙市中苏友好协会在二十一日集会纪念列宁逝世二十九周年，到会员一千五百多人。会上，湖南大学经济系主任凃西畴作了有关苏联经济建设的报告。会后放映苏联影片“难忘的一九一九年”。
