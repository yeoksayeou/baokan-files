Title: East China Administrative Committee Undergoes Reorganization

Author:
Date: 1953-01-28
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Shanghai, 27th—The East China Administrative Committee was formally established on December 2nd of last year. Currently, a series of reorganization works are underway. In addition to the four committees already established—Political and Legal, Financial and Economic, Cultural and Educational, People's Supervision—and the General Office, the East China Administrative Committee will establish institutions such as the Ethnic Affairs Committee, the Sports Committee, and the Committee for Eliminating Illiteracy. The various departments formerly under the East China Military and Political Committee have been reorganized into separate bureaus and offices according to their professional nature, and are under the leadership of the relevant departments of the Central People's Government or the East China Administrative Committee. The entire reorganization work will be completed by the end of February.

### 华东行政委员会进行整编工作

1953-01-28
第1版()
专栏：

    华东行政委员会进行整编工作
    【新华社上海二十七日电】华东行政委员会已在去年十二月二日正式成立。目前正在进行一系列的整编工作。华东行政委员会除仍设有政治法律、财政经济、文化教育、人民监察四个委员会和办公厅外，将成立民族事务委员会、体育运动委员会和扫除文盲工作委员会等机构。原华东军政委员会所属各部按照专业性质，已分别确定改组为单独性的局、处，分属中央人民政府各主管部门或华东行政委员会领导。全部整编工作将在二月底完成。
