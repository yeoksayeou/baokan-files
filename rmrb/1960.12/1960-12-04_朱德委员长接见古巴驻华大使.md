Title: Chairman Zhu De Receives the Cuban Ambassador to China
Author:
Date: 1960-12-04
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, December 3rd: Zhu De, Chairman of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress, received Oscar Pino Santos, the first Ambassador Extraordinary and Plenipotentiary of the Republic of Cuba to China, this afternoon. Present at the reception was Lin Ping, Deputy Director of the Department of American and Australian Affairs of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs. (Attached picture)
Chairman Zhu De receives Oscar Pino Santos, the first Ambassador Extraordinary and Plenipotentiary of the Republic of Cuba to China. (Xinhua News Agency reporter Zhang Bin photo)

### 朱德委员长接见古巴驻华大使

1960-12-04
第1版()
专栏：

    朱德委员长接见古巴驻华大使
    新华社3日讯　全国人民代表大会常务委员会委员长朱德今日下午接见了古巴共和国首任驻华特命全权大使奥斯卡·皮诺·桑托斯。接见时在座的有外交部美澳司副司长林平。（附图片）
