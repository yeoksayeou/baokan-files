Title: Prince Sihanouk and Other Cambodian Guests Visit Military Museum and Agricultural Exhibition
Author:
Date: 1960-12-17
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency, December 16th. Cambodian Head of State Prince Norodom Sihanouk, accompanied by Premier of the State Council Zhou Enlai and Vice Premiers Chen Yi and Ulanhu, visited the Chinese People's Revolution Military Museum and the National Agricultural Exhibition Center today.

Accompanying Prince Sihanouk in visiting the Military Museum and the Agricultural Exhibition Center were: Vice-Chairman of the Cambodian Throne Council, Penn Nouth, Prime Minister Pho Proeung, First Deputy Prime Minister Nhiek Tioulong, and other Cambodian guests.

Premier Zhou Enlai and Vice Premier Marshal Chen Yi accompanied Prince Sihanouk and other distinguished guests on a visit to the Military Museum in the evening. When the distinguished guests arrived at the entrance of the museum, they were warmly welcomed by Grand General Luo Ruiqing, Vice Premier of the State Council and Chief of General Staff of the Chinese People's Liberation Army, Air Force General Liu Yalou, Vice Minister of National Defense, and many officers and soldiers. The distinguished guests carefully viewed various cultural relics, pictures, weapons, and other exhibits.

Prince Sihanouk and his wife, along with other distinguished guests, visited the National Agricultural Exhibition Center in the morning, accompanied by Vice Premier Ulanhu. Minister of Agriculture Liao Luyan, Vice Minister of Agriculture and Director of the National Agricultural Exhibition Center, Wei Zhenwu, and Deputy Director Wu Wenhua warmly welcomed the distinguished guests at the gate, and more than 400 staff members of the exhibition center lined the entrance to welcome them. After visiting the comprehensive pavilion, water conservancy pavilion, crop pavilion, and special products pavilion, Prince Sihanouk enthusiastically wrote an inscription as a souvenir. His inscription read: "Everything we have seen in this magnificent agricultural exhibition of the People's Republic of China has amazed us. The achievements made by the people and government of New China in improving agricultural technology, diversified management, farmland irrigation, handicrafts, agricultural product processing industry, and people's communes are very impressive and even miraculous. The People's Republic of China is striding towards the top of the world with giant steps. We express our infinite admiration for this. Long live the friendship between our two countries!"

Also accompanying Prince Sihanouk on the visit were Wang Youping, Chinese Ambassador to Cambodia, and his wife, and Cambodian Ambassador to China, Leng Ngeth.

This afternoon, General Nhou Hue, Chief of Staff of the Cambodian Royal Air Force and Technical Advisor to the Head of State, visited the Military Museum, accompanied by Lieutenant General Xiao Xiangrong, Director of the General Office of the Ministry of National Defense.

Prince Sihanouk's daughter, Princess Boutsady, and Princes Naradipo and Kemanourak, visited the Beijing Zoo this afternoon. (Attached photo)

### 西哈努克亲王等柬埔寨贵宾  参观军事博物馆农业展览馆

1960-12-17
第4版()
专栏：

    西哈努克亲王等柬埔寨贵宾  参观军事博物馆农业展览馆
    新华社16日讯　柬埔寨国家元首诺罗敦·西哈努克亲王今天先后由国务院总理周恩来和副总理陈毅、乌兰夫陪同，参观了中国人民革命军事博物馆和全国农业展览馆。
    同西哈努克亲王一起参观军事博物馆和农业展览馆的还有：柬埔寨王位最高委员会副主席宾努，首相福·波伦，第一副首相?·刁隆和其他柬埔寨贵宾。
    周恩来总理和副总理陈毅元帅陪同西哈努克亲王及其他贵宾们在傍晚参观了军事博物馆。贵宾们到达博物馆的门前时，受到国务院副总理、中国人民解放军总参谋长罗瑞卿大将，国防部副部长刘亚楼空军上将，和许多官兵们的热烈欢迎。贵宾们在这里仔细地观看了各种文物、图片、武器等展品。
    西哈努克亲王及夫人和其他贵宾们，在上午由乌兰夫副总理陪同参观了全国农业展览馆，农业部部长廖鲁言，农业部副部长、全国农业展览馆总馆长魏震五和副总馆长吴文华在大门口热情地迎接贵宾，展览馆的四百多名工作人员在门口夹道欢迎。西哈努克亲王在这里参观了综合馆、水利馆、农作物馆和特产馆以后，热情地题词留念。他的题词是：“我们在这座富丽堂皇的中华人民共和国农业展览馆中所见到的一切，使我们惊羡不已。新中国的人民和政府在提高农作技术、多种经营、农田水利、手工业、农产品加工工业、人民公社等方面已经取得的成就十分令人感动，甚至可以说是奇妙无比。中华人民共和国正在以巨人般的步伐迈向世界顶峰。我们对此表示无限敬佩。我们两国之间的友谊万岁！”
    陪同西哈努克亲王参观的还有中国驻柬埔寨大使王幼平和夫人，柬埔寨驻中国大使兰·?特。
    在今天下午，柬埔寨皇家空军参谋长、国家元首技术顾问努呼将军曾由国防部办公厅主任萧向荣中将陪同，参观了军事博物馆。
    西哈努克亲王的女儿伦赛公主和王子纳拉迪波、克马努拉克今天下午游览了北京动物园。（附图片）
