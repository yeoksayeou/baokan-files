Title: Korean Writer Li Genrong Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1960-12-19
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency, December 18th. Korean writer Li Genrong arrived in Beijing by train this afternoon for a visit to our country under the 1960 executive plan of the Sino-Korean Cultural Cooperation Agreement. Writers including Li Yingru were at the station to welcome him.

### 朝鲜作家李根荣到京

1960-12-19
第4版()
专栏：

    朝鲜作家李根荣到京
    新华社18日讯　根据中朝文化合作协定1960年执行计划来我国访问的朝鲜作家李根荣，今天下午乘火车到达北京。到车站欢迎的有作家李英儒等人。
