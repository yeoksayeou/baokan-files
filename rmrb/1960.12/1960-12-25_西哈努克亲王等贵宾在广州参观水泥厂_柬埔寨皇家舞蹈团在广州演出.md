Title: Prince Sihanouk and Other Distinguished Guests Visit Guangzhou Cement Plant; Cambodian Royal Dance Troupe Performs in Guangzhou
Author:
Date: 1960-12-25
Page: 4
    Xinhua News Agency, Guangzhou, December 24th: Prince Norodom Sihanouk, Head of State of Cambodia, and other distinguished Cambodian guests, visited the Guangzhou Cement Plant this afternoon and were warmly welcomed by the workers.

    Vice Premier Chen Yi, Governor Chen Yu, and Mayor Zeng Sheng accompanied the distinguished guests on their visit.

    When the distinguished guests arrived at the factory gate, the workers lined up to welcome them. Among the welcoming crowd were factory leaders and Cambodian interns from the factory, who extended cordial greetings to Prince Sihanouk and the other distinguished Cambodian guests. As the distinguished guests entered the factory, they saw welcome slogans posted everywhere, on the tall chimneys, in the workshops, and by the machines. Many workshop entrances were crowded with workers welcoming the guests, enthusiastically applauding and waving to them. Prince Sihanouk clasped his hands together to express his gratitude for the sincere friendship of the Chinese workers.

    The Guangzhou Cement Plant is currently responsible for training technicians and skilled workers for cement plants in the Kingdom of Cambodia. During the visit, Prince Sihanouk said to the factory director: "Your training of technicians for our country is a noble endeavor. We express our sincere gratitude to the Chinese government, and thank China for helping us build cement plants." Governor Chen Yu instructed the factory leaders: "For the sake of the friendship between the people of China and Cambodia, we must do our best to ensure the training work is well done."

    The distinguished guests visited the factory's various large kilns. Prince Sihanouk praised the factory's production technology and wished the factory even greater achievements.

    Xinhua News Agency, Guangzhou, December 24th: The Cambodian Royal Dance Troupe gave a performance in Guangzhou tonight. The artists deeply captivated the Guangzhou audience with their dance art rich in national characteristics.

    Vice Governor of Guangdong Province, Lin Qiangyun, Vice Mayors of Guangzhou, Jiao Linyi and Lin Xi, heads of the Cultural Bureaus of Guangdong Province and Guangzhou City, and people from all walks of life attended the performance. Actors from the Chinese Chao Opera Troupe, who had recently visited Cambodia and learned dance art from this dance troupe, watched the Cambodian artists perform again tonight.

    Some of the distinguished guests who accompanied Prince Sihanouk on his visit, as well as Cambodian interns in Guangzhou, also attended the performance. (Attached photo)

    Prince Sihanouk, accompanied by Vice Premier Chen Yi and Governor Chen Yu, is warmly welcomed by the people of Guangzhou as he walks out of the airport.　　　Xinhua News Agency Reporter Liu Qingrui (Fax Photo)

### 西哈努克亲王等贵宾在广州参观水泥厂  柬埔寨皇家舞蹈团在广州演出

1960-12-25
第4版()
专栏：

    西哈努克亲王等贵宾在广州参观水泥厂
    柬埔寨皇家舞蹈团在广州演出
    新华社广州24日电　柬埔寨国家元首西哈努克亲王和柬埔寨其他贵宾，今天下午在广州参观了广州水泥厂，受到职工们的热烈欢迎。
    陈毅副总理、陈郁省长和曾生市长陪同贵宾们参观。
    贵宾们到达工厂门口时，职工们列队欢迎。在欢迎的队伍中，有工厂的负责人和这个厂的柬埔寨实习生，他们向西哈努克亲王及柬埔寨其他贵宾致亲切的问候。当贵宾们进入厂内的时候，看到在高大的烟囱上，在车间里，在机器旁，到处都张贴着欢迎的标语。许多车间的门口聚集着欢迎贵宾的工人，他们热烈地鼓掌和向贵宾们招手致意。西哈努克亲王双手合十感谢中国工人真诚的友谊。
    广州水泥厂现在担负着帮助柬埔寨王国培训水泥厂技术人员和技术工人的任务。西哈努克亲王在参观的时候，对厂长说：“你们给我国培养技术人员是崇高的事情，我们向中国政府表示真诚的感谢，并且感谢中国帮助我们建设水泥厂。”陈郁省长嘱咐这个厂的负责人说：“为了中柬两国人民的友谊，一定要尽力把培训工作作好。”
    贵宾们参观了这个厂的各个大窑，西哈努克亲王对工厂的生产技术表示赞扬，并且预祝这个工厂获得更好的成就。
    据新华社广州24日电　柬埔寨皇家舞蹈团今晚在广州举行访问演出。艺术家们以富有民族色彩的舞蹈艺术，深深地吸引了广州的观众。
    广东省副省长林锵云，广州市副市长焦林义、林西，广东省和广州市文化局负责人以及各方面的人士，出席观看了演出。不久前到柬埔寨访问演出时曾向这个舞蹈团学习过舞蹈艺术的中国潮剧团的演员们，今晚再一次观看了柬埔寨艺术家们的表演。
    随西哈努克亲王前来访问的部分贵宾和在广州的柬埔寨实习生，也出席观看了演出。（附图片）
    西哈努克亲王由陈毅副总理和陈郁省长陪同步出机场时，受到广州人民的热烈欢迎。　　　新华社记者　刘庆瑞摄（传真照片）
