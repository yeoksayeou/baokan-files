Title: Delegations to the Five-Nation Fisheries Conference Return Home and Visit Various Locations
Author: 
Date: 1960-12-03
Page: 4
    Xinhua News Agency dispatch: The head of the Vietnamese delegation, Lê Duy Trinh, and the member of the Soviet delegation, Viginsky, who participated in the Fifth Plenary Session of the Western Pacific Fisheries Research Commission, returned to their countries by train on November 30. Jin Cheng, Vice Minister of Aquatic Products, and others saw them off at the station. Members of the other national delegations attending the meeting traveled to Shanghai, Hangzhou, Tianjin, Dalian, and other places on the same day for visits.

### 五国渔业会议代表团分别回国和到各地访问

1960-12-03
第4版()
专栏：

    五国渔业会议代表团分别回国和到各地访问
    新华社讯　参加太平洋西部渔业研究委员会第五次全体会议的越南代表团团长黎维贞、苏联代表团团员维金斯基，11月30日乘火车回国。水产部副部长金城等到车站送行。出席这次会议的其他各国代表团成员，同天分别前往上海、杭州和天津、大连等地参观访问。
