Title: Czechoslovakia and Cyprus Establish Diplomatic Relations
Author: 
Date: 1960-12-27
Page: 5

    Xinhua News Agency, Prague -- According to a report by the Czechoslovak News Agency: The government of the Czechoslovak Socialist Republic and the government of the Republic of Cyprus have decided to establish diplomatic relations at the level of ministers, and to send envoys to each other.

### 捷克斯洛伐克和塞浦路斯建交

1960-12-27
第5版()
专栏：

    捷克斯洛伐克和塞浦路斯建交
    新华社布拉格电　据捷克斯洛伐克通讯社报道：捷克斯洛伐克社会主义共和国政府和塞浦路斯共和国政府决定，两国建立公使级的外交关系，并互派使节。
