Title: Burmese Embassy Military Attaché Colonel Kyaw Saw Mon Hosts Reception to Bid Farewell to Chinese Military Delegation Visiting Burma
Author:
Date: 1960-12-20
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, 19th. Burmese Embassy Military Attaché Colonel Kyaw Saw Mon hosted a reception on the evening of the 16th to bid farewell to the Chinese Military Delegation within the People’s Republic of China Goodwill Delegation to the Union of Burma, which is about to pay a friendly visit to Burma. General Zhang Aiping, head of the Chinese Military Delegation, and members of the delegation in Beijing attended the reception. Senior generals of the Chinese People's Liberation Army and representatives of troops stationed in Beijing were also invited to the reception. Military attachés from various countries' embassies in China were also invited.

In his toast at the reception, Colonel Kyaw Saw Mon warmly welcomed the Chinese Military Delegation to visit Burma. He said that not long ago, when the Burmese Military Goodwill Delegation accompanied Prime Minister U Nu and General Ne Win on a visit to China, they were warmly received, which enhanced the friendship between the people and armies of the two countries. We are very pleased to have the opportunity to receive the Chinese Military Delegation in Burma. We believe that this visit of the Chinese Military Delegation to Burma will further enhance the understanding and friendship between the people and armies of the two countries.

In his toast, Zhang Aiping said that the people of China and Burma have always been friendly. The friendship between the armies of China and Burma has been further enhanced with the recent visit of the Burmese Military Goodwill Delegation to our country. The friendly relations between our two peoples and armies can be called "Pauk-Phaw" relations. This time we are visiting Burma with the deep friendship of the Chinese people and army towards the Burmese people and army. This friendly visit will further develop the friendship between the people and armies of China and Burma.

The reception took place in a cordial and friendly atmosphere, with the guests frequently raising their glasses to wish for the continuous development of the friendship between the people and armies of China and Burma.

### 缅甸使馆武官景沙门上校举行酒会  为我军事代表团访缅饯行

1960-12-20
第5版()
专栏：

    缅甸使馆武官景沙门上校举行酒会
    为我军事代表团访缅饯行
    新华社19日讯　缅甸驻华大使馆武官景沙门上校曾在16日晚举行酒会，为即将前往缅甸进行友好访问的中华人民共和国访问缅甸联邦友好代表团中的中国军事代表团饯行。中国军事代表团团长张爱萍上将和在京的代表团团员出席了酒会。应邀出席酒会的还有中国人民解放军高级将领和驻京部队的代表。各国驻华使馆武官也应邀出席了酒会。
    景沙门上校在酒会上祝酒时，对中国军事代表团前往缅甸访问表示热烈欢迎。他说，不久以前，当缅甸军事友好代表团随同吴努总理和奈温将军前来中国访问时，受到了热情的接待，增进了两国人民和军队之间的友谊。我们很高兴有机会在缅甸接待中国军事代表团。我们相信，这次中国军事代表团到缅甸访问，将进一步增强两国人民和军队之间的了解和友谊。
    张爱萍上将在祝酒时说，中缅两国人民一向是友好的。中缅两国军队之间的友谊，随着不久前缅甸军事友好代表团来我国访问，又有了进一步增强。我们两国人民和军队之间的友好关系称得起是“胞波”的关系。这次我们到缅甸访问，是带着中国人民和军队对缅甸人民和军队的深厚友谊前去访问的，这种友好的访问，将更加发展中缅两国人民和军队之间的友谊。
    酒会在亲切友好的气氛中进行，宾主频频举杯祝中缅两国人民和军队之间的友谊不断发展。
