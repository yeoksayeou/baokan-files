Title: Chinese Chargé d'Affaires ad interim to Cuba, Huang Wenyou, Presents Letter of Credence to Cuban Acting Foreign Minister
Author:
Date: 1960-12-03
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Havana, November 30th. Huang Wenyou, Chargé d'Affaires ad interim of the Chinese Embassy in Cuba, visited Cuban Acting Foreign Minister Carlos Olivares this afternoon and presented him with his letter of credence.

Subsequently, the host and guest had a cordial conversation.

### 中国驻古巴大使馆临时代办黄文友  向古巴代理外长递交委任书

1960-12-03
第1版()
专栏：

    中国驻古巴大使馆临时代办黄文友
    向古巴代理外长递交委任书
    新华社哈瓦那11月30日电　中华人民共和国驻古巴大使馆临时代办黄文友今天下午访问了古巴代理外交部长卡洛斯·奥利瓦雷斯，并且向他递交了委任书。
    随后，宾主之间进行了亲切的会谈。
