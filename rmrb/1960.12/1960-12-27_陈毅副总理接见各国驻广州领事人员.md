Title: Vice Premier Chen Yi Receives Consular Personnel from Various Countries Stationed in Guangzhou
Author:
Date: 1960-12-27
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Guangzhou, December 26th: Vice Premier of the State Council and Minister of Foreign Affairs Chen Yi received Kopecky, the Consul of Czechoslovakia in Guangzhou; Wygałkowski, the Consul of Poland in Guangzhou; and Trần Văn Thông and Hoàng Thanh Mai, Vice Consuls of the Consulate General of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam in Guangzhou, in Guangzhou this afternoon and held cordial and friendly conversations with them.

Present at the reception were Zeng Sheng, Mayor of Guangzhou, and Wang Jinchuan, Director of the Foreign Affairs Office of the Guangdong Provincial People's Committee, among others.

### 陈毅副总理接见各国驻广州领事人员

1960-12-27
第4版()
专栏：

    陈毅副总理接见各国驻广州领事人员
    新华社广州26日电　国务院副总理兼外交部长陈毅，今天下午在广州接见了捷克斯洛伐克驻广州领事考贝克，波兰驻广州领事维高茨基，越南民主共和国驻广州总领事馆副领事陈文通、黄青梅，并且同他们进行了亲切友好的交谈。
    接见时在座的有广州市市长曾生，广东省人民委员会外事处处长王锦川等。
