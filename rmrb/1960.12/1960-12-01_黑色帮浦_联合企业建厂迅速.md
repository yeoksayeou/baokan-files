Title: "Black Pump" United Enterprise Building Rapidly
Author:
Date: 1960-12-01
Page: 5
German Democratic Republic Production News Compilation:

    "Black Pump" United Enterprise Building Rapidly
    The construction of the "Black Pump" United Enterprise in the Cottbus district of the German Democratic Republic is progressing very rapidly. This largest lignite united enterprise in the world now occupies an area of approximately twenty-five square kilometers.
    This enterprise will be fully completed in 1964, at which time it will have three power stations, four briquette factories, three coking plants and a pressure gas plant, among which, the production capacity of the pressure gas plant will rank first in the world.

### “黑色帮浦”联合企业建厂迅速

1960-12-01
第5版()
专栏：德意志民主共和国生产消息集纳

    “黑色帮浦”联合企业建厂迅速
    德意志民主共和国科特布斯专区的“黑色帮浦”联合企业的建厂工作进展很迅速，这个世界上最大的烟煤联合企业现已占地二十五平方公里左右。
    这个企业将于1964年全部建成，那时它将拥有三座发电站、四座煤砖厂、三座焦碳厂和一座压力煤气工厂，其中，压力煤气工厂的生产能力将居世界第一位。
