Title: Iraqi Engineers Delegation Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1960-12-18
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, December 17th: An Iraqi engineers delegation of ten people, led by the head of the delegation, radio traffic engineer Ha. Wa. Abdul, arrived in Beijing by plane today to visit China in accordance with the Sino-Iraqi Cultural Cooperation Agreement.

Those who welcomed them at the airport included Hou Debang, Vice Chairman of the China Association for Science and Technology; Wang Shuntong and Zhang Zhenqiu, Secretaries of the Secretariat of the Association for Science and Technology; and Wang Shuzhuang, Vice Chairman of the Beijing Association for Science and Technology.

Also at the airport to greet them was Fadil, the Iraqi Ambassador to China.

### 伊拉克工程师代表团到京

1960-12-18
第4版()
专栏：

    伊拉克工程师代表团到京
    新华社17日讯　执行中伊文化合作协定来我国访问的伊拉克工程师代表团一行十人，在团长、无线电交通工程师哈·瓦·阿卜杜勒率领下，今天乘飞机到达北京。
    到机场欢迎的，有中华人民共和国科学技术协会副主席侯德榜，科协书记处书记王顺桐、张震球，北京市科协副主席王书庄等。
    到机场迎接的，还有伊拉克驻华大使法迪尔。
