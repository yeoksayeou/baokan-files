Title: 《Soviet Russia》 Publishes Article Pointing Out that the United States is the Main Accomplice in France's War of Aggression Against Algeria
Author:
Date: 1960-12-28
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, 21st (TASS): *Soviet Russia* published an article pointing out that the United States plays a major accomplice role in the dirty colonial war waged by France against Algeria.

The article said that American and French military concerns have a stake in the colonial war in Algeria, and they reap huge profits by providing weapons. But the main motivation for Washington's support of France is far from America's desire to strengthen France's position in North Africa. On the contrary, Washington believes that the deeper France is mired in this doomed war, the more likely the United States will be to replace France in North Africa.

When discussing the issue of granting independence to Algeria, the author pointed out that not only the French ruling clique, but also the United States, is unwilling to let Algeria become independent. It is not surprising that the United States voted against granting Algeria independence in the United Nations.

### 《苏维埃俄罗斯报》发表文章指出  美国是法国侵阿战争主要帮凶

1960-12-28
第6版()
专栏：

    《苏维埃俄罗斯报》发表文章指出
    美国是法国侵阿战争主要帮凶
    据新华社讯　据塔斯社莫斯科21日讯：《苏维埃俄罗斯报》刊载一篇文章，指出美国在法国对阿尔及利亚进行的肮脏的殖民主义战争中起着主要的帮凶作用。
    文章说，美国和法国军事康采恩在阿尔及利亚的殖民主义战争中有着利害关系，它们通过提供武器获取巨额利润。但是促使华盛顿支持法国的主要动机，远不是美国想要加强法国在北非的地位。相反，华盛顿认为，法国在这场注定要失败的战争中陷得越深，美国就越有可能取代法国在北非的地位。
    作者在谈到给予阿尔及利亚以独立的问题时指出，不仅法国统治集团，而且美国也不愿意让阿尔及利亚独立。美国在联合国投票反对给予阿尔及利亚以独立，这是不奇怪的。
