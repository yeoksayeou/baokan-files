Title: Southeast Asia Treaty Aggression Group Plots Military Intervention in Laos
Author:
Date: 1960-12-23
Page: 5
Southeast Asia Treaty Aggression Group
Plots Military Intervention in Laos

　　Xinhua News Agency, 21st dispatch from Bangkok: According to Thai newspapers, after the Southeast Asia Treaty Organization, controlled by the United States, held three consecutive "emergency meetings" in Bangkok from the 12th to the 15th, it held another meeting in Bangkok on the 19th to further deploy armed intervention in Laos.

　　On the same day, the secretary-general of this military aggression group, Pote Sarasin, said in response to a reporter's question about whether the Southeast Asia Treaty Organization was prepared to send troops to intervene in Laos, "The Southeast Asia Treaty Organization is constantly on alert." Sarasin met with Thai Prime Minister Sarit Thanarat on the 18th to discuss the Laotian situation and the issue of requiring all member states of the Southeast Asia Treaty Organization to send troops to intervene in Laos.

### 东南亚条约侵略集团  策划出兵干涉老挝

1960-12-23
第5版()
专栏：

    东南亚条约侵略集团
    策划出兵干涉老挝
    据新华社21日讯　曼谷消息：据泰国报纸报道，由美国控制的东南亚条约组织在12日到15日期间接连在曼谷召开三次“紧急会议”之后，19日再次在曼谷举行会议，进一步部署对老挝的武装干涉。
    同一天，这个军事侵略集团的秘书长沙拉信，在答复一个记者提出的东南亚条约组织是否准备派兵干涉老挝的问题时说，“东南亚条约组织经常在戒备中”。沙拉信在18日曾会见泰国总理乃沙立，就老挝局势问题作了会谈，讨论了要求东南亚条约组织所有的成员国都派兵干涉老挝的问题。
