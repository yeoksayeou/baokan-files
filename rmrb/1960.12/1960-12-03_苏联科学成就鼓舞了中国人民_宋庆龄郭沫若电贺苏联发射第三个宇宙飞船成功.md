Title: Soviet Scientific Achievements Inspire the Chinese People; Soong Ching Ling and Guo Moruo Send Telegrams Congratulating the Soviet Union on the Successful Launch of its Third Spaceship

Author:

Date: 1960-12-03
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, December 2nd - The President of the Sino-Soviet Friendship Association, Soong Ching Ling, sent a telegram to Popova, Chairman of the Union of Soviet Societies for Friendship and Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries, and Andreyev, Chairman of the Council of the Soviet-Chinese Friendship Association, congratulating the Soviet Union on the successful launch of its third satellite spaceship. The telegram reads as follows:

Comrade Popova, Chairman of the Presidium of the Union of Soviet Societies for Friendship and Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries:
Comrade Andreyev, Chairman of the Council of the Soviet-Chinese Friendship Association:

We are delighted to hear that the Soviet Union successfully launched its third spaceship into Earth satellite orbit on December 1st according to its scientific exploration work plan. On behalf of all members of the Sino-Soviet Friendship Association, I would like to extend my heartfelt congratulations to you, and through you, to the great Soviet people, outstanding Soviet workers, designers, and scientists.

The Soviet Union has continuously achieved tremendous and astonishing successes in the sacred cause of mankind's conquest of outer space, in the most important fields of modern science and technology, leaving the United States far behind. These achievements give tremendous encouragement to the Chinese people who are building socialism, as well as to all progressive mankind.

I wish the great Soviet people even more brilliant and greater achievements in the future.

　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　President of the Sino-Soviet Friendship Association　Soong Ching Ling
　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　December 2, 1960

Xinhua News Agency, December 2nd - Guo Moruo, President of the Chinese Academy of Sciences, sent a telegram to Nesmeyanov, President of the Soviet Academy of Sciences, congratulating the Soviet Union on the successful launch of its third satellite spaceship once again. The telegram reads as follows:

President Nesmeyanov of the Soviet Academy of Sciences:

We are delighted to hear that the Soviet Union has once again successfully launched its third satellite spaceship on December 1st. This will add new and rich data for the study of outer space and the realization of human spaceflight. On behalf of the Chinese scientific workers, I would like to extend my warm congratulations to you and, through you, to all Soviet scientific workers. I wish you even greater achievements.

　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　President of the Chinese Academy of Sciences　Guo Moruo
　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　December 2, 1960

### 苏联科学成就鼓舞了中国人民  宋庆龄郭沫若电贺苏联发射第三个宇宙飞船成功

1960-12-03
第5版()
专栏：

    苏联科学成就鼓舞了中国人民
    宋庆龄郭沫若电贺苏联发射第三个宇宙飞船成功
    新华社2日讯　中苏友好协会总会会长宋庆龄致电苏联对外友好和文化协会联合会主席波波娃、苏中友好协会理事会主席安德烈耶夫，祝贺苏联成功地发射了第三个卫星式宇宙飞船。电文如下：苏联对外友好和文化协会联合会主席团主席波
  波娃同志：苏中友好协会理事会主席安德烈耶夫同志：
    欣闻苏联按照科学考察工作计划于12月1日成功地把第三个宇宙飞船发射到地球卫星轨道，谨代表中苏友好协会全体会员，向您们，并通过您们，向伟大的苏联人民、杰出的苏联工人、设计师、科学家们，表示衷心的祝贺。
    苏联在人类征服宇宙空间的神圣事业中，在现代科学技术最重要的领域内，连续获得巨大的惊人的成就，把美国远远地抛在后面。这些成就，给予正在建设社会主义的中国人民，以及全体进步人类以极大的鼓舞。
    谨祝伟大的苏联人民今后取得更加光辉更加巨大的成就。
          中苏友好协会总会会长　宋庆龄
                          1960年12月2日
    新华社2日讯　中国科学院院长郭沫若致电苏联科学院院长涅斯米扬诺夫，祝贺苏联再一次成功地发射第三个卫星式宇宙飞船。电文如下：苏联科学院涅斯米扬诺夫院长：
  欣闻苏联于12月1日再一次成功地发射了第三个卫星式宇宙飞船。这将为宇宙空间的研究和实现人类宇宙飞行，增添新的丰富的资料。谨代表中国科学工作者向您并通过您向苏联全体科学工作者致以热烈的祝贺。预祝您们获得更大的成就。
                中国科学院院长　郭沫若
                          1960年12月2日
