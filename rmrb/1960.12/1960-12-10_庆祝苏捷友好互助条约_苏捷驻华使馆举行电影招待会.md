Title: Celebrating the Sino-Soviet Treaty of Friendship, Alliance and Mutual Assistance: Soviet and Czechoslovak Embassies in China Hold Film Reception
Author: 
Date: 1960-12-10
Page: 4
    Xinhua News Agency, December 9th. The Soviet and Czechoslovak Embassies in China jointly held a film reception tonight to celebrate the 17th anniversary of the signing of the Treaty of Friendship, Mutual Assistance and Post-War Cooperation between the Union of Soviet Socialist Republics and the Czechoslovak Republic.

    The Sino-Soviet Treaty of Friendship, Alliance and Mutual Assistance was signed on December 12, 1943. Tonight's film reception was held at the Soviet Embassy in China. Before the screening of the film, Soviet Chargé d'Affaires ad interim to China, Sudarikov, and Czechoslovak Ambassador to China, Cédik, delivered speeches at the reception.

    Among those present at tonight's film reception were Vice Ministers of Foreign Affairs Zhang Hanfu, Luo Guibo, and Zeng Yongquan, Director of the Consular Department of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs, Qin Lizhen, and Deputy Director of the Department of Soviet and European Affairs, Yu Zhan. Heads of diplomatic missions of various countries in China were also invited to attend the film reception.

### 庆祝苏捷友好互助条约  苏捷驻华使馆举行电影招待会

1960-12-10
第4版()
专栏：

    庆祝苏捷友好互助条约
    苏捷驻华使馆举行电影招待会
    新华社9日讯　苏联驻华大使馆和捷克斯洛伐克社会主义共和国驻华大使馆今晚联合举行电影招待会，庆祝苏维埃社会主义共和国联盟和捷克斯洛伐克共和国友好互助与战后合作条约签订十七周年。
    苏捷友好互助与战后合作条约是在1943年12月12日签订的。今晚的电影招待会在苏联驻华大使馆举行。在放映电影以前，苏联驻华大使馆临时代办苏达利柯夫和捷克斯洛伐克社会主义共和国驻华大使赛迪维先后在会上讲了话。
    出席今晚电影招待会的有外交部副部长章汉夫、罗贵波、曾涌泉，外交部领事司司长秦力真，苏欧司副司长余湛。各国驻华使节也应邀出席了电影招待会。
