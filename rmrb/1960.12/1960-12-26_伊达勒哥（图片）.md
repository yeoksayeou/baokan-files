    Hidalgo (Woodcut)
  　Mexico　Andréa Gómez
    Hidalgo was a national hero in Mexican history. He was originally an ordinary village priest. On September 16, 1810, he launched and led the independence movement against Spanish colonial rule in Dolores, but was later defeated and killed. Since then, this day has become Mexico's Independence Day.

### 伊达勒哥（图片）
安德列阿·戈麦士
1960-12-26
第8版()
专栏：

    伊达勒哥（木刻）
  　墨西哥　安德列阿·戈麦士
    伊达勒哥是墨西哥历史上的民族英雄，他原是一个普通的乡村教士。1810年9月16日他在多洛累斯发动和领导了反对西班牙殖民统治的独立运动，后失败被杀。以后这一天就成为墨西哥的独立纪念日。
