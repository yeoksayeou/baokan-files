Title: Vice Chairman Dong Biwu Receives the Mayor of Havana, Cuba, and Simultaneously Receives Cuban Guests and Cuban Women's Delegation Visiting Together
Author:
Date: 1960-12-31
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, December 30th. Vice Chairman of the People's Republic of China, Dong Biwu, this afternoon received the Mayor of Havana, Cuba, José Llanusa, and Cuban guests visiting with him, as well as the Cuban Women's Delegation headed by Elena Hill, and held cordial and friendly talks with them.

The Cuban guests accompanying José Llanusa were: Major Raúl Menéndez Tomasovich, Director of the Cuban National Traffic Police Bureau; Captain Anriki González of the Cuban Rebel Army; Captain and Staff Officer Esiriel Leiba of the Cuban Rebel Army; and Jorge García Bangor, Deputy Mayor of Havana.

Present at the reception were: Vice Premiers of the State Council Li Xiannian and Xi Zhongxun, Vice Chairman of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress Lin Feng, Vice Minister of Foreign Affairs Zhang Hanfu, President of the Chinese-Latin American Friendship Association Chu Tunan, Vice Mayor of Beijing Feng Jiping, Vice President of the All-China Women's Federation Xu Guangping, and others.

Cuban Ambassador to China Oscar Pino Santos and his wife were also present. (Attached picture)

The left picture shows Vice Chairman Dong with the Cuban distinguished guests. The fifth person from the right in the front row is Mayor José Llanusa, the third person is Delegation Leader Elena Hill, and the sixth person from the left is Ambassador Pino Santos.

Xinhua News Agency reporter Li Jilu photographed.

### 董必武副主席接见古巴哈瓦那市长  同时接见同来访问的古巴外宾和古巴妇女代表团

1960-12-31
第1版()
专栏：

    董必武副主席接见古巴哈瓦那市长
    同时接见同来访问的古巴外宾和古巴妇女代表团
    新华社30日讯　中华人民共和国副主席董必武今天下午接见了古巴哈瓦那市长何塞·利亚努萨和同他一起来访的古巴外宾，以及以埃伦娜·希尔为首的古巴妇女代表团，并且同他们进行了亲切友好的谈话。
  和何塞·利亚努萨同来的古巴外宾是：古巴全国交通警察局局长劳尔·梅嫩德斯·托马索维奇少校，古巴起义军大尉安利吉·冈萨雷斯，古巴起义军大尉兼参谋艾西烈尔·列依巴，哈瓦那副市长豪尔赫·卡尔西亚·班戈。
    接见时在座的有：国务院副总理李先念、习仲勋，全国人民代表大会常务委员会副委员长林枫，外交部副部长章汉夫，中国拉丁美洲友好协会会长楚图南，北京市副市长冯基平，全国妇联副主席许广平等。
    古巴驻中国大使奥斯卡·皮诺·桑托斯和夫人也在座。（附图片）
    左图为董副主席同古巴贵宾合影。前排右起第五人是何塞·利亚努萨市长，第三人是埃伦娜·希尔团长，左起第六人是皮诺·桑托斯大使。
    新华社记者　李基禄摄
