Title: Many Christmas Accidents in the United States, Over Six Hundred Fatalities
Author:
Date: 1960-12-30
Page: 6
　
　　Xinhua News Agency, Chicago, 29th: According to the Associated Press, a total of 647 people died in various accidents in the United States during the three days of Christmas. Among them, 488 died in traffic accidents, averaging one death every ten minutes; 77 died in fires, and 82 died in other accidents.

### 美国圣诞事故多  六百余人丧生命

1960-12-30
第6版()
专栏：

    美国圣诞事故多
    六百余人丧生命
    新华社29日讯　芝加哥消息：据美联社报道，在圣诞节三天内，美国因各种事故共死亡六百四十七人。其中死于交通事故的有四百八十八人，平均每十分钟死一个人；死于火灾的七十七人，死于其他各事故的八十二人。
