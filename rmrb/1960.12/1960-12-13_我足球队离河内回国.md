Title: Our Football Team Departs Hanoi for Home
Author:
Date: 1960-12-13
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, Hanoi, December 12th: The Chinese football team, a delegation of twenty-five members, left Hanoi by train this afternoon to return home. They came here on November 26th to participate in the friendly football matches among China, North Korea, Mongolia, and Vietnam. Wang Biwang and Wu Lun, members of the Standing Committee of the Vietnam Central Sports Commission, as well as Vietnamese athletes, went to the station to see them off.

Wang Tao, Counselor of the Chinese Embassy in Vietnam, and Ai Wen, Cultural Counselor, also went to the station to see them off.

### 我足球队离河内回国

1960-12-13
第5版()
专栏：

    我足球队离河内回国
    新华社河内12日电　中国足球队一行二十五人，今天下午乘火车离开河内回国。他们是在11月26日来这里参加中、朝、蒙、越四国足球友谊赛的。前往车站送行的有，越南中央体育运动委员会常务委员王壁旺、吴伦，以及越南运动员。
    中国驻越南大使馆参赞汪滔、文化参赞艾汶也到车站送行。
