Title: Nehru Writes to the Two Chairmen of the Geneva Conference Suggesting a Meeting of the International Commission for Laos
Author:
Date: 1960-12-19
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, London, December 18th: The British government has received a letter from Indian Prime Minister Nehru requesting the convening of a meeting of the International Supervisory and Control Commission for Laos.

According to the Indian News Agency from New Delhi, Nehru stated at a press conference in New Delhi on the 15th that India has suggested that the two chairmen of the 1954 Geneva Conference—the Soviet Foreign Minister and the British Foreign Secretary—reconvene the International Supervisory and Control Commission for Laos. Nehru said, "The situation in Laos is terrible," and he has brought "the situation in Laos to the attention" of the two chairmen and suggested that they consider "whether the International Commission might be helpful if it were to work."

India is the chairman of the International Supervisory and Control Commission for Laos. On July 19, 1958, the commission, at the insistence of the Canadian commissioner and with the approval of the Indian commissioner, passed a resolution by a majority of two votes to one (Poland) to indefinitely suspend the commission's activities, but it could reconvene through normal procedures.

### 尼赫鲁致函日内瓦会议两位主席  建议召开老挝国际委员会会议

1960-12-19
第5版()
专栏：

    尼赫鲁致函日内瓦会议两位主席
    建议召开老挝国际委员会会议
    新华社18日讯　伦敦消息：英国政府已经收到印度总理尼赫鲁要求召集老挝国际监察和监督委员会开会的信件。
    据印度新闻处自新德里报道，尼赫鲁15日曾在新德里举行的记者招待会上表示，印度已经建议1954年日内瓦会议的两位主席——苏联外交部长和英国外交大臣重新召开老挝国际监察和监督委员会会议。尼赫鲁说，“老挝情况是糟糕的”，他已经提请两位主席“注意老挝情况”，并且建议他们考虑“国际委员会如果进行工作，也许是会有帮助的。”
    印度是老挝国际监察和监督委员会的主席。1958年7月19日，委员会在加拿大委员的坚持主张和印度委员的赞同下，以二票对一票（波兰）的多数通过决议，决定委员会无限期地停止活动，但可根据正常的程序重新复会。
