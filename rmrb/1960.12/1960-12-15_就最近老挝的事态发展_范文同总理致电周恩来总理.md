Title: Premier Phạm Văn Đồng Sends Telegram to Premier Zhou Enlai Regarding Recent Developments in Laos
Author: 
Date: 1960-12-15
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, December 14th: Premier Phạm Văn Đồng of the Government of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam sent a telegram to Premier Zhou Enlai of the State Council of the People's Republic of China on December 11 regarding the situation in Laos, appealing for urgent and effective action to stop the intervention of U.S. imperialism in Laos. The telegram is as follows:

Comrade Zhou Enlai, Premier of the State Council of the People's Republic of China:

I am deeply disturbed by the recent developments in Laos. These events expose the crude intervention in Laos by the United States, Thailand, South Vietnamese elements, and Chiang Kai-shek elements, who are militarily assisting the Phoumi Nosavan rebel group against the legitimate government of Prince Souvanna Phouma. On behalf of the government of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam—a signatory to the Geneva Agreements on Laos—I send you this urgent appeal, respectfully requesting that you consider taking all urgent and effective action to stop foreign interference, particularly American interference, to effectively support the legitimate government of Prince Souvanna Phouma, to ensure that the Geneva Agreements are respected, and to safeguard the security and peace of Indochina and Southeast Asia.

              Phạm Văn Đồng
              Premier of the Government of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam
                        Hanoi, December 11, 1960

### 就最近老挝的事态发展  范文同总理致电周恩来总理

1960-12-15
第1版()
专栏：

    就最近老挝的事态发展
    范文同总理致电周恩来总理
    新华社14日讯　越南民主共和国政府总理范文同12月11日就老挝局势致电中华人民共和国国务院总理周恩来，呼吁采取紧急和有效的行动制止美帝国主义对老挝的干涉，电文如下：中华人民共和国国务院总理周恩来同志：
    最近老挝的事态发展使我深感不安，这一事件揭露了美国、泰国、南越分子和蒋介石分子对老挝的粗暴干涉，在军事上帮助富米·诺萨万叛乱集团反对梭发那·富马亲王的合法政府。我代表越南民主共和国——关于老挝的日内瓦协定的签字国——的政府，向你发出紧急呼吁，谨请考虑采取一切紧急和有效的行动，以便制止外来干涉，特别是美国的干涉，有效地支持梭发那·富马亲王的合法政府，保证日内瓦协定得到尊重，维护印度支那和东南亚的安全与和平。
              越南民主共和国政府总理　范文同
                        1960年12月11日于河内
