Title: Vietnamese Railway Delegation Returns Home
Author:
Date: 1960-12-08
Page: 3

Xinhua News Agency, 7th December: The Vietnamese Ministry of Transport and Post’s Railway Delegation, led by Nguyen Cao Tham, concluded its two-month investigation in our country and returned home by train today. Xu Jianji, Deputy Director of the International Joint Transport Bureau of the Ministry of Railways, and Li Yuelin and Wang Mixuan, Deputy Directors of the Beijing Railway Bureau, were at the station to see them off.

During their stay in our country, the delegation conducted investigations in Beijing, Tianjin, Qinhuangdao and other places on railway transportation, freight transport, and passenger services. Yesterday, Lu Zhengcao and Liu Jianzhang, Vice Ministers of the Ministry of Railways, met with all members of the delegation and had friendly and cordial talks with them.

### 越南铁路考察团回国

1960-12-08
第3版()
专栏：

    越南铁路考察团回国
    新华社7日讯　以阮高参为首的越南交通邮电部铁路考察团，结束了在我国为期两个月的考察，今天乘火车回国。到车站欢送的有铁道部国际联运局副局长徐建基和北京铁路局副局长李岳林、王觅轩等。
    考察团在我国期间，先后在北京、天津、秦皇岛等地就铁路运输、货运和旅客服务等工作进行了考察。昨天，铁道部副部长吕正操、刘建章曾接见考察团全体人员，并且同他们进行了友好亲切的谈话。
