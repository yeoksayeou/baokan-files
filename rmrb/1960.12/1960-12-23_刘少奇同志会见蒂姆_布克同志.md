Title: Comrade Liu Shaoqi Meets with Comrade Tim Buck
Author:
Date: 1960-12-23
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, December 22nd. Comrade Liu Shaoqi, Vice Chairman of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of China, met and hosted a banquet this evening for Comrade Tim Buck, General Secretary of the Communist Party of Canada, and his wife. A cordial conversation took place during the meeting.

Comrades Liu Ningyi and Wu Xiuquan, members of the CPC Central Committee, attended the meeting and banquet. (Photo attached)

Vice Chairman of the CPC Central Committee Liu Shaoqi and General Secretary of the Communist Party of Canada Tim Buck shake hands warmly. Xinhua News Agency reporter Meng Qingbiao.

### 刘少奇同志会见蒂姆·布克同志

1960-12-23
第1版()
专栏：

    刘少奇同志会见蒂姆·布克同志
    新华社22日讯中国共产党中央委员会副主席刘少奇同志，今晚会见并设宴招待加拿大共产党总书记蒂姆·布克同志和夫人。会见时进行了亲切的谈话。
    参加会见和宴会的，有中共中央委员刘宁一、伍修权等同志。（附图片）
    中共中央副主席刘少奇和加拿大共产党总书记蒂姆·布克亲切握手  新华社记者  孟庆彪摄
