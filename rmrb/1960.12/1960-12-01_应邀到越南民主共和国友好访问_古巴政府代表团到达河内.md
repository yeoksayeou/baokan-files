Title: Cuban Government Delegation Arrives in Hanoi for Friendly Visit to the Democratic Republic of Vietnam at Invitation
Author:
Date: 1960-12-01
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, Hanoi, November 30th: The Cuban Revolutionary Government Delegation, led by Hector Rodriguez Llompart, Administrative Vice Minister of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs of the Cuban Revolutionary Government, arrived in Hanoi by plane this afternoon for a friendly visit to Vietnam at the invitation of the Government of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam. The delegation was warmly welcomed by people from all walks of life in Hanoi.

The delegation members included Major Eddie Suñol Ricart of the Cuban Army, and Lavin Mandunado, a senior cadre of the Cuban Foreign Trade Bank.

Welcoming the delegation at the airport were: Nguyen Khanh, Minister of the Premier's Office of Vietnam; Phan Anh, Minister of Foreign Trade; Hoang Van Tien, Deputy Minister of Foreign Affairs; Le Viet Luong, Director of the State Bank; Nguyen Duc Quy, Deputy Minister of Culture; and Tran Van Lai, Vice Chairman of the Hanoi Municipal Administrative Committee.

Diplomatic envoys from various countries to Vietnam also went to the airport to welcome the delegation. The national anthems of Cuba and Vietnam were played loudly at the airport. Delegation leader Hector Rodriguez Llompart, accompanied by Nguyen Khanh, reviewed the guard of honor.

### 应邀到越南民主共和国友好访问  古巴政府代表团到达河内

1960-12-01
第5版()
专栏：

    应邀到越南民主共和国友好访问
    古巴政府代表团到达河内
    据新华社河内30日电　应越南民主共和国政府邀请前来越南进行友好访问的古巴革命政府代表团，今天下午在代表团团长、古巴革命政府外交部行政副部长艾克托·罗德里格斯·略姆帕尔特率领下乘飞机到达河内，受到河内各界人民的热烈欢迎。
    代表团团员有：古巴军队少校埃迪·苏纽尔·里卡尔特，古巴外贸银行高级干部拉温·曼敦纳多。
    到飞机场欢迎代表团的有：越南总理府部长阮康，对外贸易部长潘英，外交部副部长黄文进，银行总行长黎曰亮，文化部副部长阮德葵，河内市行政委员会副主席陈文来。
    各国驻越南外交使节也到机场欢迎。机场上高奏古越两国国歌，代表团团长艾克托·罗德里格斯·略姆帕尔特由阮康陪同检阅了仪仗队。
