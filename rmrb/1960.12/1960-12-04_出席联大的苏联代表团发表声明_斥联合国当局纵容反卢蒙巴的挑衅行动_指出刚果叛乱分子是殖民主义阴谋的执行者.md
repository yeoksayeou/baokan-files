Title: Soviet Delegation to the UN Issues Statement Condemning UN Authorities' Connivance with Anti-Lumumba Provocations, Pointing out that the Congolese Rebels are Executors of Colonialist Conspiracies
Author:
Date: 1960-12-04
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, December 3rd, New York Dispatch: The Soviet delegation attending the 15th session of the United Nations General Assembly issued a statement on the evening of December 2nd regarding the illegal arrest of Congolese Republic Prime Minister Lumumba.

The statement pointed out that the anti-Lumumba provocations were successful as a result of the connivance and de facto collusion of the United Nations Command in the Congo.

The statement said that the recent developments in the Congo are only part of the crimes committed by the Belgian aggressors and their North Atlantic group allies, crimes which have been aided by Congolese who are willing to serve foreign masters and act as loyal executors of the colonialists' despicable conspiracies.

The Soviet delegation emphasized that the United Nations Secretary-General and the United Nations Command in the Congo bear direct responsibility for the lives and safety of members of the Congolese government. The Soviet delegation also requested the United Nations Secretary-General to provide full information on the situation of the Prime Minister of the Congolese Republic and the steps taken by the United Nations Command to ensure his safety.

### 出席联大的苏联代表团发表声明  斥联合国当局纵容反卢蒙巴的挑衅行动  指出刚果叛乱分子是殖民主义阴谋的执行者

1960-12-04
第6版()
专栏：

    出席联大的苏联代表团发表声明
    斥联合国当局纵容反卢蒙巴的挑衅行动
    指出刚果叛乱分子是殖民主义阴谋的执行者
    新华社3日讯　据塔斯社纽约讯：出席联合国大会第十五届会议的苏联代表团2日晚上就刚果共和国总理卢蒙巴被非法逮捕事发表声明。
    声明指出，反卢蒙巴的挑衅行动所以得逞，是由于在刚果的联合国司令部的纵容和实际上共谋的结果。
    声明说，最近刚果事态的发展，只是比利时侵略者和他们的北大西洋集团盟国所犯罪行的一部分，他们的这些罪行得到了那些甘愿为外国主子服务和充当殖民主义者卑鄙阴谋的忠实执行者角色的刚果人的帮助。
    苏联代表团强调指出，联合国秘书长和在刚果的联合国司令部，对刚果政府成员的生命和安全负有直接责任。苏联代表团并要求联合国秘书长就刚果共和国总理的情况以及联合国司令部在保障他的安全方面采取的步骤提供充分的情报。
