Title: Snowfall Again in Northern China

Author: 
Date: 1960-12-05
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, December 4th. Snow has fallen again in the northern part of North China, which is very beneficial for the overwintering of winter wheat.

On the afternoon of the 3rd, it began to snow in the Hetao area of ​​Inner Mongolia; from the night of the 3rd to the early morning of the 4th, snow also fell successively in northern Shanxi and northern and central Hebei. The amount of snow was generally around one millimeter, and some places were less than one millimeter. By noon on the 4th, the snow had basically stopped.

### 华北北部再次下雪

1960-12-05
第4版()
专栏：

    华北北部再次下雪
    新华社4日讯　华北北部地区再次下雪，对冬小麦越冬很有好处。
    3日下午，内蒙古河套一带开始下雪；3日夜到4日清晨，山西北部和河北北部、中部地区也相继下了雪。这场雪雪量一般在一毫米左右，有些地方不足一毫米。到4日中午前后，雪已基本停止。
