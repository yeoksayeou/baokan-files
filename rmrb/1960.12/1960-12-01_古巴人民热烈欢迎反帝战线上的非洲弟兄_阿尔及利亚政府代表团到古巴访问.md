Title: The Cuban People Warmly Welcome African Brothers on the Anti-Imperialist Front: Algerian Government Delegation Visits Cuba
Author:
Date: 1960-12-01
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, Havana Dispatch: The Algerian delegation headed by Ben Youssef Ben Khedda has been warmly welcomed by the Cuban government and people. The delegation arrived in Havana on the 26th for a visit.

On the 28th, Cuban President Dorticos received the delegation. Also present at the meeting were Cuban Acting Foreign Minister Carlos Olivares and Manuel Yepes, Director of the Protocol Department of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs.

The Cuban Association for Friendship with All Peoples held a reception on the evening of the 28th to welcome the Algerian delegation. Among those present at the reception were Minister of Revolutionary Armed Forces Raúl Castro, as well as other Cuban officials and public leaders. Huang Wenyou, Chargé d'Affaires ad interim of the Chinese Embassy in Cuba, Zeng Tao, Director of the Havana Branch of the New China News Agency, and Zou Si'er, Chinese Trade Representative, also attended the reception.

The Cuban Workers' Confederation has called on all its affiliated trade unions to hold a "Day of Solidarity with Algeria" on Tuesday.

Ben Youssef Ben Khedda said upon arriving in Havana on the 26th that "the heroism and fighting spirit of the heroic Cuban people are admired by the people of all countries in the world." He said, "Cuba and Algeria are on the same front: against imperialism. We firmly believe in certain victory, because history and the people of the whole world have declared that imperialism and colonialism are approaching death. The people of all countries want freedom, independence and complete sovereignty. No force in the world can stop them." When talking to a Xinhua News Agency reporter about his impressions of the delegation's visit to Latin America, Ben Youssef Ben Khedda said that the Latin American people are full of profound anti-imperialist spirit.

### 古巴人民热烈欢迎反帝战线上的非洲弟兄  阿尔及利亚政府代表团到古巴访问

1960-12-01
第6版()
专栏：

    古巴人民热烈欢迎反帝战线上的非洲弟兄
    阿尔及利亚政府代表团到古巴访问
    据新华社哈瓦那电　以本·优素福·本赫达为首的阿尔及利亚代表团受到古巴政府和人民的热烈欢迎。这个代表团是26日到达哈瓦那访问的。
    28日，古巴总统多尔蒂科斯接见了代表团。接见时在座的有古巴代理外交部长卡洛斯·奥利瓦雷斯和外交部礼宾司司长曼努埃尔·耶佩斯。
    古巴和各国人民友好协会28日晚举行招待会，欢迎阿尔及利亚代表团。出席招待会的有革命武装部部长劳尔·卡斯特罗以及其他古巴官员和公众领袖。中国驻古巴大使馆临时代办黄文友、新华通讯社哈瓦那分社社长曾涛和中国贸易代表邹斯颐也出席了招待会。
    古巴工人联合会号召全体所属工会在星期二举行声援阿尔及利亚日。
    本·优素福·本赫达26日到达哈瓦那时在机场说，“英勇的古巴人民的英雄主义和战斗精神受到世界各国人民的敬佩”。他说，“古巴和阿尔及利亚的战线是一样的：反对帝国主义。我们深信一定胜利，因为历史和全世界人民宣判帝国主义和殖民主义已接近死亡。各国人民要自由、独立和完整的主权。世界上没有任何力量能阻挡他们。”本·优素福·本赫达在向新华社记者谈代表团访问拉丁美洲的印象时说，拉丁美洲人民充满了深刻的反帝精神。
