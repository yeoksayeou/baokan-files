Title: Important Factors Promoting Economic and Cultural Upsurge: Talents Grow and Expand in the Soviet Central Asian Republics

Author:

Date: 1960-12-30
Page: 5

An Important Factor Promoting Economic and Cultural Upsurge

Talents Grow and Expand in the Soviet Central Asian Republics

    (Reported by this newspaper) According to Soviet newspapers, after the October Revolution, under the brilliant light of Lenin's national policy, construction talents in the Soviet Central Asian republics grew and expanded rapidly, playing a significant role in promoting the economic and cultural development of various nationalities. Comparing with 1926 (the year when the Soviet economy recovered to pre-revolutionary levels), the total number of people participating in the national economy nationwide has increased sixfold, while the Central Asian republics and Kazakhstan have increased tenfold.

    The number of technical workers, scientific and cultural cadres of various nationalities has grown even faster. The following charts show the specific growth:

### 促使经济和文化高涨的重要因素  苏联中亚各共和国建设人才成长壮大

1960-12-30
第5版()
专栏：

    促使经济和文化高涨的重要因素
    苏联中亚各共和国建设人才成长壮大
    本报讯　据苏联报纸报道：十月革命后，在列宁民族政策的光辉照耀下，苏联中亚细亚各加盟共和国的建设人才飞速地成长和壮大起来，对于促进各民族的经济和文化发展起了重大的作用。如以1926年作比较（1926年是苏联经济恢复到革命前水平的一年），目前全苏参加国民经济的人力增加了六倍，而中亚细亚各共和国及哈萨克斯坦则增长了十倍。
