Title: Khrushchev Replies to Abbas, Expressing that the Soviet People Sympathize with the Heroic Algerian People
Author:
Date: 1960-12-19
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, 18th. Soviet Chairman of the Council of Ministers Khrushchev, in replying to a telegram from Abbas, Premier of the Provisional Government of the Algerian Republic, said, "No matter how rampant the colonialists are, they cannot maintain the shameful colonial system that exists in Algeria and other regions where the red sun of national independence has not yet risen."

Khrushchev said that the tragic events in Algeria aroused the righteous indignation and extreme anger of all the Soviet people, and the sympathy of the Soviet people is entirely on the side of the heroic Algerian people who are striving to realize their national aspirations.

In his reply, Khrushchev emphasized that the French ruling clique cannot escape the serious responsibility for the crimes committed against the Algerian people once again.

Abbas sent a telegram to Khrushchev on December 12. In this telegram, he pointed out that the situation was extremely serious as French colonialists and French occupation forces were carrying out massacres in Algerian cities. He called for an immediate halt to this crime of exterminating the Algerian people.

### 赫鲁晓夫复电阿巴斯表示  苏联人民同情英雄的阿尔及利亚人民

1960-12-19
第6版()
专栏：

    赫鲁晓夫复电阿巴斯表示
    苏联人民同情英雄的阿尔及利亚人民
    新华社18日讯　据塔斯社莫斯科18日讯：苏联部长会议主席赫鲁晓夫在答复阿尔及利亚共和国临时政府总理阿巴斯的来电时说，“不管殖民主义者怎样猖獗，他们都无法保持住他们在阿尔及利亚以及其它民族独立的红日还没有升起的地区所存在的可耻的殖民主义制度。”
    赫鲁晓夫说，在阿尔及利亚发生的悲惨事件，激起了全体苏联人民的正义愤慨和万分的愤怒，苏联人民的同情完全在争取实现本民族愿望的英雄的阿尔及利亚人民一边。
    赫鲁晓夫在复电中着重指出，法国统治集团是无法逃脱对阿尔及利亚人民又一次犯下的罪行所承担的严重责任的。
    阿巴斯是在12月12日致电赫鲁晓夫的。他在这个电报中指出，法国殖民主义者和法国占领军在阿尔及利亚城市里进行集体屠杀，局势异常严重。他呼吁立即制止这种灭绝阿尔及利亚人民的罪行。
