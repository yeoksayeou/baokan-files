Title: Send-off

Author: Ji Peng

Date: 1960-12-01

Page: 8

　　Send-off
　　Ji Peng
　　The train travels station after station,
　　Those boarding fill the carriages to the brim,
　　Inside the train, voices and laughter, overflowing with joy,
　　Outside, colorful flags flutter, cheers unceasing.
　　Cadres going to the countryside find fellow passengers from the communes,
　　Chatting about digging canals, accumulating fertilizer, and reclaiming land for increased production;
　　Family members collectively read the newspaper editorials,
　　The Party's words seep into hearts like spring rain.
　　Young men and women's hearts are ablaze,
　　On guarantee letters they write magnificent vows:
　　To go to the commune, barren mountains, grasslands, remote wildernesses,
　　To be glorious commune members.
　　Every station has people departing on long journeys,
　　A great army setting out, with the force of a raging torrent,
　　Majestic, thousands upon thousands of troops,
　　Going to conquer nature, seizing the "treasure of treasures"!
　　What a magnificent song of departure,
　　I wonder how many towns, docks, and stations it has resounded through,
　　This is a song of battle, a song of victory,
　　Next year it will transform into news of a bountiful harvest frequently reported to Beijing.

### 壮行
纪鹏
1960-12-01
第8版()
专栏：

    壮行
    纪鹏
    车行一站又一站，
    上车的人把车厢装得满满，
    车上人声笑语，喜气洋溢，
    车下彩旗飘飘，欢声不断。
    下乡干部找到社员旅客，
    畅谈挖渠、积肥、拓荒增产；
    家属们在集体阅读报上社论，
    党的话像春雨渗入心田。
    小伙子和姑娘们心血沸腾，
    保证书上写下宏伟的志愿：
    到公社，荒山，草原，僻野，
    做个光荣的公社社员。
    每个车站都有远行的人，
    大军出征，势若狂澜，
    浩浩荡荡，千军万马，
    去战胜自然，夺取“宝中宝”！
    好一支壮美的出征歌呵，
    不知响遍多少城镇、码头、车站，
    这是支战斗的歌，胜利的歌，
    明年化为丰收捷报向北京频传。
