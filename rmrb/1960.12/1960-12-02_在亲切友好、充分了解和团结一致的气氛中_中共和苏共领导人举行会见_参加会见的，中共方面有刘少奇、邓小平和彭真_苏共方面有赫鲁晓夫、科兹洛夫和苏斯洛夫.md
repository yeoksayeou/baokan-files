Title: The Leaders of the Chinese and Soviet Communist Parties Hold Talks in a Cordial, Friendly, Fully Understanding, and United Atmosphere.
Author: 
Date: 1960-12-02
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, December 1st. The leaders of the Chinese Communist Party and the Soviet Communist Party held talks at the Kremlin in Moscow on November 30th. The Chinese Communist Party was represented by Liu Shaoqi, Vice Chairman of the Central Committee of the Chinese Communist Party; Deng Xiaoping, General Secretary and member of the Political Bureau of the Central Committee of the Chinese Communist Party; and Peng Zhen, member of the Political Bureau and Secretary of the Secretariat of the Central Committee of the Chinese Communist Party. The Soviet Communist Party was represented by Nikita Khrushchev, First Secretary of the Central Committee of the Soviet Communist Party, Frol Romanovich Kozlov, member of the Presidium and Secretary of the Secretariat of the Central Committee of the Soviet Communist Party, and Mikhail Suslov. The meeting took place in a cordial, friendly, fully understanding, and united atmosphere.

专栏：

    在亲切友好、充分了解和团结一致的气氛中
    中共和苏共领导人举行会见
    参加会见的，中共方面有刘少奇、邓小平和彭真
    苏共方面有赫鲁晓夫、科兹洛夫和苏斯洛夫
    新华社莫斯科1日电　中国共产党和苏联共产党的领导人11月30日在莫斯科克里姆林宫举行了会见。参加会见的，中国共产党方面有：中国共产党中央委员会副主席刘少奇，中国共产党中央委员会总书记、政治局委员邓小平，中国共产党中央政治局委员、书记处书记彭真。苏联共产党方面有：苏联共产党中央委员会第一书记赫鲁晓夫，苏联共产党中央委员会主席团委员、书记处书记科兹洛夫和苏斯洛夫。会见是在亲切友好、充分了解和团结一致的气氛中进行的。
