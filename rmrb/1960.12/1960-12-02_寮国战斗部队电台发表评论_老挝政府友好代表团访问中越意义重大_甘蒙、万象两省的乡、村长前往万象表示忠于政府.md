Title: Lao Fighting Forces Radio Station Publishes Commentary: Lao Government Goodwill Delegation's Visit to China and Vietnam of Great Significance; Village Chiefs from Khammouane and Vientiane Provinces Go to Vientiane to Express Loyalty to the Government
Author:
Date: 1960-12-02
Page: 6
Xinhua News Agency, Hanoi, December 1st: The Lao Fighting Forces Radio Station today published a commentary saying that the Lao people warmly welcome the goodwill delegation headed by Prime Minister Phouma's visit to the People's Republic of China and the Democratic Republic of Vietnam, and wish the delegation success in developing and consolidating the unbreakable friendly relations between the Lao people and China and Vietnam.
The commentary said that the Lao Royal Government's decision to send a goodwill delegation led by Prime Minister Phouma to visit China and the Democratic Republic of Vietnam is of great significance, proving that the Phouma government has embarked on a path of genuine peaceful neutrality, satisfying the Lao people's desire for Laos to establish good neighborly relations with all countries, especially the People's Republic of China and the Democratic Republic of Vietnam, which share a common border with Laos.
The commentary said that the People's Republic of China and the Democratic Republic of Vietnam have a long history of economic and cultural relations with Laos, and they both participated in the signing of the 1954 Geneva Agreements. China and Vietnam have consistently upheld the Five Principles of Peaceful Coexistence and supported Laos' path of peace and neutrality.
The radio station said that the Royal Government's decision to send a goodwill delegation has made the friendly and cooperative relations between the three countries closer, provided conditions for Laos to consolidate its national independence, strive for peace and neutrality, and build an independent economy, and contributed to defending peace in Southeast Asia and the world.
Xinhua News Agency, Hanoi, November 30th: According to a broadcast by Vientiane Radio today, more than 30 village chiefs from Khammouane Province and Vientiane Province arrived in Vientiane City on November 26th and met with Deputy Minister of Interior Boun Oum and Minister of Information Quinim Pholsena on the 28th and 29th. These village chiefs expressed their loyalty to the legitimate government headed by Prince Phouma.

### 寮国战斗部队电台发表评论  老挝政府友好代表团访问中越意义重大  甘蒙、万象两省的乡、村长前往万象表示忠于政府

1960-12-02
第6版()
专栏：

    寮国战斗部队电台发表评论
    老挝政府友好代表团访问中越意义重大
    甘蒙、万象两省的乡、村长前往万象表示忠于政府
    新华社河内1日电　寮国战斗部队电台今天发表评论说，老挝人民热烈欢迎以富马首相为首的友好代表团访问中华人民共和国和越南民主共和国，并祝代表团在发展和巩固老挝人民同中、越两国的牢不可破的友好关系方面取得成就。
    评论说，老挝王国政府决定派遣由富马首相率领的友好代表团到中国和越南民主共和国进行访问具有十分重大的意义，它证明富马政府走上了真正和平中立的道路，满足了老挝人民要求老挝同所有国家，特别是同老挝具有共同边界的中华人民共和国和越南民主共和国建立睦邻关系的愿望。
    评论说，中华人民共和国和越南民主共和国同老挝有着悠久的经济和文化关系，它们都曾经参加签订1954年日内瓦协议，中越两国一贯维护和平共处五项原则，并支持老挝的和平中立路线。
    这家电台说，王国政府派遣友好代表团的决定，使得三国人民的友好互助关系更加密切，为老挝巩固民族独立、争取和平中立、建设独立经济提供了条件，并为保卫东南亚和世界和平作出贡献。
    新华社河内30日电　据万象电台今天广播，老挝甘蒙省和万象省的三十多个乡长和村长11月26日到达万象市，并在28、29两日会见了内务副大臣文恩和宣传大臣贵宁·奔舍那。这些乡长和村长表示忠诚于以富马亲王为首的合法政府。
