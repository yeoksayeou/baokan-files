Title: Ghana Trades Union Congress Delegation Returns Home
Author:
Date: 1960-12-03
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, December 2nd. The Ghana Trades Union Congress delegation of five people, headed by Osafo-Ayifah, General Secretary of the Ghana Agricultural Workers' Union, left Beijing by plane today to return to their country.

### 加纳工会大会代表团回国

1960-12-03
第4版()
专栏：

    加纳工会大会代表团回国
    据新华社2日讯　以加纳农业工会总书记奥萨弗—艾法为首的加纳工会大会代表团一行五人，今天乘飞机离开北京回国。
