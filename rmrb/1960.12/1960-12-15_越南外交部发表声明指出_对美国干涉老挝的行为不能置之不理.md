Title: Vietnamese Foreign Ministry Issues Statement: Cannot Ignore U.S. Interference in Laos
Author:
Date: 1960-12-15
Page: 6
Xinhua News Agency, Hanoi, December 14th: The Vietnam News Agency reported that the Ministry of Foreign Affairs of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam issued a statement this morning, condemning the U.S. imperialists and their lackeys for helping the Lao traitorous Fumi Nosavan-Wen Weng Na Champasak group attack Vientiane, thereby directly interfering in Laos' internal affairs. The statement said that the Government of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam believes that the blatant aggression of the U.S. imperialists, Thailand, and other lackey countries of the United States against Laos, and the expansion of the civil war in Laos, are a destruction of peace in Indochina, a flagrant violation of the Geneva Agreements on Laos, and a threat to the security of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam.

The statement revealed that the troops of the Fumi Nosavan traitorous group, the running dogs of U.S. imperialism in Laos, launched an attack on Vientiane, the capital of the Kingdom of Laos, on the afternoon of December 13th. In this attack, the Fumi Nosavan group received active assistance from the Thai authorities, who helped the Fumi Nosavan group move their troops through Thai territory. In particular, a unit of the Fumi Nosavan group, together with a Thai army unit, crossed the Mekong River from Thailand on the other side of the Mekong River and entered the capital of Laos. Several South Vietnamese troops also participated in yesterday's attack on Vientiane.

The statement said that the Government of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam condemns the U.S. imperialists and Thailand for their acts of intervention and aggression against Laos, and demands that they stop such acts.

The statement emphasized that the Government of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam, in order to safeguard peace and the Geneva Agreements, and in view of its responsibility to defend the security of its territory, certainly cannot ignore this dangerous behavior. The U.S. imperialists, Thailand, and other lackey countries of the United States that have invaded Laos must bear full responsibility for the consequences of their actions. The statement finally called on the countries participating in the 1954 Geneva Conference, Asian and African countries, and all other peace-loving countries in the world to take urgent and effective measures to stop the aggressive activities of the reactionary forces headed by the U.S. imperialists in Laos.

### 越南外交部发表声明指出  对美国干涉老挝的行为不能置之不理

1960-12-15
第6版()
专栏：

    越南外交部发表声明指出
    对美国干涉老挝的行为不能置之不理
    新华社河内14日电　据越南通讯社报道，越南民主共和国外交部今天上午发表声明，谴责美帝国主义者及其仆从帮助老挝富米·诺萨万—文翁·纳占巴塞叛国集团进攻万象，从而对老挝的内政进行直接的干涉。声明说，越南民主共和国政府认为，美帝国主义者、泰国和美国的其他仆从国家明目张胆地侵略老挝和扩大老挝的内战，是对印度支那和平的破坏，是对关于老挝的日内瓦协议的公然的违反，是对越南民主共和国安全的威胁。
    声明揭露说，美帝国主义者在老挝的走狗富米·诺萨万叛国集团的军队在12月13日下午，对老挝王国的首都万象发动了进攻。在这次进攻中，富米·诺萨万集团得到了泰国当局的积极帮助。后者曾帮助富米·诺萨万集团通过泰国的领土调动他们的军队。特别是，富米·诺萨万集团的一支部队同一支泰国军队一起从湄公河彼岸泰国境内渡过湄公河，并进入老挝的首都。若干南越的军队也参加了昨天对万象的进攻。
    声明说，越南民主共和国政府谴责美帝国主义者和泰国对老挝的干涉和侵略行为，并且要求它们停止这种行为。
    声明强调说，越南民主共和国政府为了维护和平和日内瓦协议，并鉴于它有责任保卫它的领土的安全，肯定不能对这种危险的行为置之不理。美帝国主义者、泰国和侵犯老挝的美国的其他仆从国家，必须对它们的行为的后果负全部责任。声明最后要求参加1954年日内瓦会议的国家、亚非国家以及全世界其他一切爱好和平的国家采取紧急的和有效的措施，来制止以美帝国主义者为首的反动势力在老挝的侵略活动。
