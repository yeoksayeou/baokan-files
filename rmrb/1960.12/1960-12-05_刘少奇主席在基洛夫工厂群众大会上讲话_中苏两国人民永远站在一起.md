Title: Chairman Liu Shaoqi Speaks at Mass Meeting at the Kirov Factory: The Peoples of China and the Soviet Union Will Forever Stand Together
Author:
Date: 1960-12-05
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency, Leningrad, December 4th: The following is the full text of Chairman Liu Shaoqi's speech at the mass meeting at the Kirov Factory in Leningrad on December 3rd:

    Dear Comrades of the Kirov Factory, Dear Comrade Brezhnev, Dear Comrade Kozlov, Dear Factory Director, Dear Secretary of the Factory Party Committee:

    Today, our Chinese Party and Government Delegation is very pleased to visit your factory. I sincerely thank you for your warm welcome.

    Your factory has a long and glorious revolutionary tradition. The great Lenin carried out many revolutionary activities in this factory and delivered many world-famous speeches here. Your factory has cultivated many outstanding revolutionary activists and cadres for building socialism and communism. In China's long revolutionary struggle, there have also emerged some enterprises, mines, rural areas, troops, and organizations like your factory. In factories, mines, rural areas, and troops like the Kirov Factory, excellent revolutionary traditions and work styles have been established, namely, the style of combining theory with practice, the style of leaders closely linking with the masses, and the style of criticism and self-criticism. A large number of cadres cultivated by your factory have gone to work all over the country, spreading this excellent style to all places and carrying it forward. Therefore, doing a good job in a factory, a rural area, or a troop is not only of national significance but also of world significance. I hope that you will continue to carry forward the excellent revolutionary tradition and work style of the Leninist Kirov Factory.

    Your factory produces very important machines and equipment, and your labor is constantly increasing the power of the socialist camp. Your factory has supplied very good equipment to China, which has helped our socialist construction, and our technical cadres have also interned in your factory to learn technology. On behalf of the Chinese people, the Chinese Communist Party, and the Chinese government, I express my heartfelt gratitude to you and, through you, to all the Soviet people, the Soviet Communist Party, and the Soviet government.

    The Chinese people will forever stand with the Soviet people. We firmly believe, the Chinese people firmly believe, that the great Soviet Union and the Soviet people will forever stand with us. In our common struggle, the Chinese people are the most reliable and closest brothers and friends of the Soviet people.

    Long live the unbreakable friendship between the great people of China and the Soviet Union!

    Long live the great Soviet people!

    Long live the great Soviet Communist Party!

    Long live the Central Committee of the Soviet Communist Party headed by Comrade Khrushchev!

    Long live world peace!

### 刘少奇主席在基洛夫工厂群众大会上讲话  中苏两国人民永远站在一起

1960-12-05
第4版()
专栏：

    刘少奇主席在基洛夫工厂群众大会上讲话
    中苏两国人民永远站在一起
    新华社列宁格勒4日电　刘少奇主席12月3日在列宁格勒基洛夫工厂的群众大会上的讲话，全文如下：亲爱的基洛夫工厂的职工同志们，亲爱的勃列日涅夫同志，亲爱的科兹洛夫同志，亲爱的厂长同志，亲爱的工厂党委书记同志：
    今天，我们中国党政代表团十分高兴地参观了你们的工厂。我衷心地感谢你们的热情的欢迎。
    你们的工厂有悠久的光荣的革命传统。伟大的列宁当年在这个工厂里进行了许多革命活动，伟大的列宁在此发表过多次世界著名的演说。你们的工厂培养出了许多出色的革命活动家和建设社会主义和共产主义的干部。在中国的长期革命斗争中也产生了一些像你们工厂这样的企业、矿山、农村、部队和机关。在像基洛夫工厂这样的工厂、矿山、农村和部队里，树立了优秀的革命传统和工作作风，即理论和实际相结合的作风、领导密切联系群众的作风和批评与自我批评的作风。你们工厂培养出的大批干部到全国各地去工作，把这种优秀的作风传播到各地，并加以发扬光大。因此，一个工厂、一个农村、一个部队的工作作好了，不仅有全国的意义，而且有世界的意义。希望你们不断发扬列宁式的基洛夫工厂的优良革命传统和优秀的工作作风。
    你们的工厂生产十分重要的机器和设备，你们的劳动每时每刻都在增加着社会主义阵营的威力。你们的工厂供应了很好的设备给中国，帮助了我们的社会主义建设事业，同时我们的技术干部也在你们的工厂进行过实习，学习了技术。我代表中国人民、中国共产党和中国政府向你们、并且通过你们向全体苏联人民、苏联共产党和苏联政府表示衷心的感谢。
    中国人民将永远同苏联人民站在一起。我们深信，中国人民深信，伟大的苏联和苏联人民将永远和我们站在一起。在我们的共同斗争中，中国人民是苏联人民的最可靠和最亲密的兄弟和朋友。
    中苏两国伟大人民的牢不可破的友谊万岁！
    伟大的苏联人民万岁！
    伟大的苏联共产党万岁！
    以赫鲁晓夫同志为首的苏联共产党中央委员会万岁！
    世界和平万岁！
