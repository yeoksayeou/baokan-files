Title: Prince Sihanouk Arrives in Guangzhou; Premier Zhou Enlai Bids Farewell to Distinguished Guests in Hangzhou; Vice Premier Chen Yi Accompanies Them to Guangzhou for a Visit

Author: 
Date: 1960-12-25
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Hangzhou, December 24th: Cambodian Head of State Prince Norodom Sihanouk and his wife, along with other distinguished Cambodian guests, concluded their visit in Hangzhou. This morning, accompanied by Vice Premier Chen Yi and his wife, they departed by special plane for a visit to Guangzhou.

Premier Zhou Enlai warmly saw off the distinguished guests at the airport. With reluctance, he wished Prince Sihanouk and his wife, as well as the other distinguished Cambodian guests, a safe journey.

Governor of Zhejiang Province Zhou Jianren and his wife, Vice Governor of Zhejiang Province and Mayor of Hangzhou Wu Xian and his wife, Vice Chairman of the Zhejiang Provincial Committee of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference Yu Ji-yi, Commander of the People's Liberation Army stationed in Zhejiang Province Lieutenant General Qian Jun, responsible persons from various sectors of Zhejiang Province and Hangzhou City, directors of the Sino-Cambodian Friendship Association in Hangzhou, and Cambodian interns in Hangzhou all went to the airport to warmly see off Prince Sihanouk and his wife, as well as the other distinguished Cambodian guests. A group of children presented flowers to the distinguished guests.

Accompanying Prince Sihanouk on his departure from Hangzhou were: Vice Chairman of the Cambodian Throne Council Binnu, Prime Minister Pho Proeung, First Vice Prime Minister Nhiek Tioulong, Prince and Princess of Sihanouk, some senior Cambodian officials, and artists from the Cambodian Royal Ballet. Cambodian Ambassador to China, Leng Ngeth, also accompanied them to Guangzhou.

Accompanying the distinguished guests to Guangzhou were also Chinese Ambassador to Cambodia Wang Youping and his wife, and Director of the Protocol Department of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs Yu Peiwen.

Members of the Cambodian economic delegation who accompanied Prince Sihanouk on his visit to Hangzhou departed by train on the evening of the 23rd to visit Shanghai.

Xinhua News Agency, Guangzhou, December 24th: Cambodian Head of State Prince Norodom Sihanouk and his wife and other distinguished guests, carrying the fraternal sentiments of the Cambodian people, arrived in the warm southern city of Guangzhou for a visit. Vice Premier Chen Yi and his wife accompanied the distinguished guests on a special plane that arrived in Guangzhou this afternoon. The distinguished guests were warmly welcomed at the airport by Governor of Guangdong Province Chen Yu and his wife, Mayor of Guangzhou Zeng Sheng, and thousands of people from all walks of life.

Accompanying Prince Sihanouk on his visit to Guangzhou were Vice Chairman of the Cambodian Throne Council Binnu, Prime Minister Pho Proeung, First Vice Prime Minister Nhiek Tioulong, Prince and Princess of Sihanouk, senior Cambodian officials accompanying the visit to China, and artists from the Royal Ballet. Cambodian Ambassador to China, Leng Ngeth, also arrived in Guangzhou.

The people of Guangzhou welcomed the distinguished Cambodian guests from the friendly nation with great enthusiasm. Today, the Guangzhou airport was adorned with the national flags of China and Cambodia, and huge red slogans were hung on buildings, expressing a warm welcome to the Cambodian Head of State and all the distinguished Cambodian guests. Many people who came to welcome them held flowers, raised the national flags of China and Cambodia and portraits of Prince Sihanouk, and arrived at the airport early to await the distinguished guests.

At 1:30 pm, the special plane, decorated with the national flags of China and Cambodia, landed at the airport. The military band played welcoming music, and the whole place erupted in thunderous applause. The sound of welcoming gongs and drums and slogans formed a chorus. After Prince Sihanouk and his wife disembarked from the plane, the Prince folded his hands in greeting to the welcoming crowd. Governor Chen Yu and his wife, Mayor Zeng Sheng, Commander of the PLA Guangzhou Military Region Lieutenant General Wen Yucheng, and others stepped forward to shake hands cordially with Prince Sihanouk and his wife and other distinguished guests. A group of young women rushed forward to present flowers to the distinguished guests.

Prince Sihanouk and his wife and other distinguished guests walked past the welcoming crowd, and people enthusiastically cheered slogans. A group of costumed young men and women danced happily, some dancing with lions, others playing orchestral music. A moving and warm atmosphere of jubilation filled the airport. Prince Sihanouk and other distinguished guests circled the field, met with the welcoming crowd, and waved frequently in greeting.

Also present at the airport to welcome the distinguished guests today were: Vice Governor of Guangdong Province Lin Qiangyun, Vice Chairman of the Guangdong Provincial Committee of the CPPCC Zhang Boquan, responsible persons from various departments, local organizations of democratic parties, and people's organizations in Guangdong Province and Guangzhou City, as well as directors of the Sino-Cambodian Friendship Association in Guangzhou. Wang Kunlun, head of the Chinese Chaozhou Opera Troupe, and all members of the troupe, who had recently returned from a visit to Cambodia, also came to the airport to welcome them. Also present at the airport to welcome the distinguished guests were consuls and vice-consuls of various countries stationed in Guangzhou. Cambodian interns in Guangzhou also came to the airport to welcome them. Chinese Ambassador to Cambodia Wang Youping and his wife, and Director of the Protocol Department of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs Yu Peiwen, accompanied the distinguished guests to Guangzhou today.

### 西哈努克亲王到达广州  周总理在杭州欢送贵宾陈毅副总理陪同前往广州参观

1960-12-25
第1版()
专栏：

    西哈努克亲王到达广州
    周总理在杭州欢送贵宾陈毅副总理陪同前往广州参观
    新华社杭州24日电　柬埔寨国家元首诺罗敦·西哈努克亲王和夫人以及柬埔寨其他贵宾，结束了在杭州的参观访问，今天上午由陈毅副总理和夫人陪同，乘专机前往广州参观访问。
    周恩来总理到机场热烈欢送贵宾，他怀着惜别的心情祝亲王殿下和夫人以及柬埔寨其他贵宾们一路平安。
    浙江省省长周建人和夫人，浙江省副省长、杭州市市长吴宪和夫人，政协浙江省委员会副主席余纪一，人民解放军驻浙江省部队首长钱钧中将，浙江省、杭州市各方面负责人，中柬友好协会在杭州的理事，以及在杭州的柬埔寨实习生，都到机场热烈欢送西哈努克亲王和夫人以及柬埔寨其他贵宾。一群少年儿童向贵宾们献了花。
    随同西哈努克亲王离开杭州的有：柬埔寨王位最高委员会副主席宾努，首相福·波伦，第一副首相?·刁龙，西哈努克亲王的公主、王子，柬埔寨的一些高级官员以及柬埔寨皇家舞蹈团的艺术家们。柬埔寨驻华大使兰·?特也随同前往广州。
    陪同贵宾前往广州的，还有我国驻柬埔寨大使王幼平和夫人，外交部礼宾司司长俞沛文。
    随同西哈努克亲王来杭州参观访问的柬埔寨经济代表团成员，在23日晚上乘火车去上海参观访问。
    新华社广州24日电　柬埔寨国家元首诺罗敦·西哈努克亲王和夫人等一行贵宾，带着柬埔寨人民兄弟般的情谊，来到我国南方温暖的城市广州进行访问。陈毅副总理和夫人今天陪同贵宾乘专机于下午到达广州。贵宾们在机场受到广东省省长陈郁和夫人，广州市市长曾生，以及各界人民数千人的热烈欢迎。
    随同西哈努克亲王到广州访问的，有柬埔寨王位最高委员会副主席宾努，首相福·波伦，第一副首相?·刁龙，西哈努克亲王的公主、王子，随行访华的柬埔寨高级官员，以及皇家舞蹈团的艺术家们。柬埔寨驻我国大使兰·?特也随同到达广州。
    广州人民以极大的热情欢迎来自亲戚之邦的柬埔寨贵宾。今天，广州机场上飘扬着中柬两国国旗，建筑物上高悬着巨大的红色标语，向柬埔寨国家元首及全体柬埔寨贵宾表示热烈的欢迎。许多前来欢迎的群众拿着鲜花，举着中柬两国的国旗和西哈努克亲王的肖像，很早就来到机场迎候贵宾。
    下午一点三十分，插着中柬两国国旗的专机在机场上降落，军乐队奏起欢迎乐曲，全场顿时响起暴风雨般的掌声，欢迎的锣鼓声和口号声响成一片。西哈努克亲王和夫人走下飞机后，亲王双手合十向欢迎的群众致意。这时，陈郁省长和夫人，曾生市长，中国人民解放军驻广州部队首长温玉成中将等迎上前去，同西哈努克亲王和夫人以及其他贵宾们亲切握手。一群女青年涌上前去向贵宾们献花。
    西哈努克亲王和夫人等贵宾从欢迎的人群前面走过，人们热烈地欢呼口号。一群化装的男女青年欢乐地跳起舞来，有的舞着狮子，有的奏起管弦乐。机场上出现一片动人的热烈欢腾的气氛。西哈努克亲王和其他贵宾绕场一周，同欢迎的群众见面，并且频频招手致意。
    今天到机场欢迎贵宾的还有：广东省副省长林锵云，广东省政协副主席张?村，广东省和广州市各机关、各民主党派地方组织、各人民团体的负责人，以及中柬友好协会在广州的理事。不久前访问柬埔寨回国的中国潮剧团团长王昆仑和全体团员也到机场欢迎。到机场欢迎贵宾的，还有各国驻广州的领事、副领事。在广州的柬埔寨实习生也到机场欢迎。中国驻柬埔寨大使王幼平和夫人、外交部礼宾司司长俞沛文，今天陪同贵宾到达广州。
