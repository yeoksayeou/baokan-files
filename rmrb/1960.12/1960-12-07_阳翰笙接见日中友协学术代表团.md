Title: Yang Hansheng Receives Japanese-China Friendship Association Academic Delegation
Author:
Date: 1960-12-07
Page: 2

Xinhua News Agency, 6th December: Yang Hansheng, Vice President of the Chinese People's Association for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries, received and hosted a banquet this afternoon for the Japanese-China Friendship Association Academic Delegation headed by Yoichi Fukushima. Present at the reception and accompanying the banquet were Ding Xilin, Vice President of the Association for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries, Zhou Erfu, Secretary-General, and Lin Lin, Deputy Secretary-General.

Li Dequan, President of the Red Cross Society of China, also received the delegation this afternoon.

### 阳翰笙接见日中友协学术代表团

1960-12-07
第2版()
专栏：

    阳翰笙接见日中友协学术代表团
    新华社6日讯　中国人民对外文化协会副会长阳翰笙，今天下午接见并设宴招待以福岛要一为首的日中友好协会学术代表团。接见时在座和出席宴会作陪的有对外文化协会副会长丁西林，秘书长周而复、副秘书长林林等。
    中国红十字会会长李德全今天下午也接见了这个代表团。
