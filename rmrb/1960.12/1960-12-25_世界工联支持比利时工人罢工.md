Title: World Federation of Trade Unions Supports Belgian Workers' Strike
Author:
Date: 1960-12-25
Page: 5

    Xinhua News Agency, Prague, December 23 – The World Federation of Trade Unions issued a communique today expressing its support for the strike struggle of Belgian workers.

    The communique pointed out that the World Federation of Trade Unions is watching with concern the significant struggle being waged by the Belgian working class and its trade unions against the control of monopoly capital and against their government's actions that harm the interests of the working people.

    The communique stated that the Belgian workers' indomitable and heroic large-scale actions to satisfy their economic and social demands will greatly help workers in all countries participating in the European Economic Community to strengthen their struggle against colluding monopoly capital.

### 世界工联支持比利时工人罢工

1960-12-25
第5版()
专栏：

    世界工联支持比利时工人罢工
    新华社布拉格23日电　世界工会联合会今天发表一项公报，表示支持比利时工人的罢工斗争。
    公报指出，世界工会联合会关切地注视着比利时工人阶级及其工会正在进行的重大的斗争，他们进行的这种斗争是为了反对垄断资本的控制，反对他们的政府损害劳动人民的利益。
    公报说，比利时工人为了使他们的经济和社会要求得到满足而采取的不屈不挠和英勇的大规模行动，将大大有助于所有参加欧洲经济集团的国家的工人加强他们反对相互勾结在一起的垄断资本的斗争。
