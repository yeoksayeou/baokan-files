Title: Soviet Foreign Ministry Note to the U.S. Government Protesting U.S. Naval Vessels' Provocations Against Soviet Merchant Ships
Author:
Date: 1960-12-22
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, December 21st: The Soviet government demands that the U.S. government severely punish the criminals responsible for the provocative actions of the U.S. Navy destroyer No. 817. This destroyer, on November 4th, in the Mediterranean Sea, simulated an attack for an hour on the Soviet freighter "Faleshti," a motor vessel carrying cargo from Marseille to Odessa.

The Soviet Foreign Ministry delivered a protest note to the U.S. Embassy in Moscow on December 20th, making the aforementioned demand in the note.

The note stated that the actions of the U.S. destroyer against the Soviet merchant ship "Faleshti" were nothing but a provocative act undermining the principle of freedom of navigation on the high seas and trampling on the basic norms of international law.

The note pointed out that the Soviet government expects the U.S. government to take measures to ensure that such actions against Soviet merchant ships do not occur again in the future.

### 苏联外交部照会美国政府  抗议美舰对苏联商船挑衅

1960-12-22
第5版()
专栏：

    苏联外交部照会美国政府
    抗议美舰对苏联商船挑衅
    新华社21日讯　据塔斯社莫斯科21日讯：苏联政府要求美国政府严厉惩办美国海军第八一七号驱击舰的挑衅行动的罪犯。这艘驱击舰11月4日在地中海上摸拟性地进攻苏联的一艘从马赛开往敖德萨的《法列什蒂》号载货内燃机船达一小时之久。
    苏联外交部12月20日将一份抗议照会交给了美国驻莫斯科大使馆，照会中提出了上述要求。
    照会中说，美国驱击舰对苏联商船《法列什蒂》号的行动不是别的，而是破坏公海航行自由原则和蹂躏国际法基本准则的挑衅行动。
    照会指出，苏联政府期待美国政府将采取措施，使对苏联商船的这种行动今后不再发生。
