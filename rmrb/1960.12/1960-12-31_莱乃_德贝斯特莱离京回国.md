Title: René Depestre Leaves Beijing to Return Home
Author:
Date: 1960-12-31
Page: 4

    Xinhua News Agency, December 29th. Haitian poet René Depestre, who visited our country at the invitation of the Chinese Latin American Friendship Association, left Beijing by plane today to return home.
    Zhou Erfu, vice president of the Chinese Latin American Friendship Association, hosted a farewell banquet for the guest on the 28th.

### 莱乃·德贝斯特莱离京回国

1960-12-31
第4版()
专栏：

    莱乃·德贝斯特莱离京回国
    新华社29日讯　应中国拉丁美洲友好协会邀请，来我国访问的海地诗人莱乃·德贝斯特莱今日乘飞机离开北京回国。
    中国拉丁美洲友好协会副会长周而复曾于28日设宴为客人饯行。
