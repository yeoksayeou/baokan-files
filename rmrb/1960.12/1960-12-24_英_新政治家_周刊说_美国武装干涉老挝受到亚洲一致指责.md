Title: British "New Statesman" Weekly Says: US Armed Intervention in Laos Met With Unanimous Asian Condemnation
Author:
Date: 1960-12-24
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, London, December 22nd: The December 24th issue of the "New Statesman" weekly, published in advance, carried an article pointing out that the civil war in Laos was instigated by the United States.

The article stated that the current situation in Laos "unsettlingly looks like Dulles' 'brink of war' in Indochina in 1954... Behind these events, the Cold War has entered a more dangerous stage."

The article pointed out that the United States "has spent 250 million US dollars in Laos in the past five years and personally instigated the recent coup." The article said, "All communist and neutral countries in Asia are accusing the United States of military intervention in Laos, attempting to use the country as a bridge to China."

The article also advocated the restoration of the International Commission for Supervision and Control in Laos.

### 英“新政治家”周刊说  美国武装干涉老挝受到亚洲一致指责

1960-12-24
第6版()
专栏：

    英“新政治家”周刊说
    美国武装干涉老挝受到亚洲一致指责
    新华社伦敦22日电　提前出版的12月24日一期的《新政治家》周刊发表一篇文章，指出老挝的内战是美国策动的。
    文章说，老挝目前的局势“令人不安地看来像是处于1954年印度支那的杜勒斯的‘战争边缘’……在这些事件后面，冷战已进入了一个更危险的阶段。”
    文章指出，美国“在过去五年中在老挝花了两亿五千万美元，而且亲自策动了最近的政变”。文章说，“亚洲的所有共产党和中立国家都在指责美国对老挝进行军事干涉，企图利用这个国家作为通向中国的桥梁。”
    文章还主张恢复老挝国际监察和监督委员会。
