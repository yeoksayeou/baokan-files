Title: Chinese Women's Delegation Returns to Beijing
Author:
Date: 1960-12-17
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency, December 16th: The Chinese Women's Delegation, led by its head Yang Yunyu, returned to Beijing by plane today after attending the enlarged meeting of the Executive Committee of the Women's International Democratic Federation in Poland.

### 中国妇女代表团回京

1960-12-17
第4版()
专栏：

    中国妇女代表团回京
    新华社16日讯　赴波兰出席国际民主妇联理事会扩大会议的中国妇女代表团，在团长杨蕴玉率领下今天乘飞机回到北京。
