Title: Hungarian Table Tennis Team Holds Last Match of Visit to China
Author:
Date: 1960-12-15
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, December 14th: The Hungarian Budapest men's and women's table tennis team held their last friendly match of their visit to China in Beijing tonight. In the ten singles, doubles, and mixed doubles matches against the Chinese youth men's and women's table tennis team, they won eight matches.

### 匈乒乓球队举行访华最后一次比赛

1960-12-15
第4版()
专栏：

    匈乒乓球队举行访华最后一次比赛
    据新华社14日讯　匈牙利布达佩斯男女乒乓球队今晚在北京举行访华最后一次友谊比赛。他们在同中国青年男女乒乓球队进行的十场单打、双打和混合双打中，获得了八场胜利。
