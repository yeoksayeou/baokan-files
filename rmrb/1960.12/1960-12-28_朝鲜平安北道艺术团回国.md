Title: Korean North Pyongan Province Art Troupe Returns Home
Author:
Date: 1960-12-28
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Shenyang, December 25th. The 72-member North Pyongan Province Art Troupe of Korea, having been invited to Liaoning Province for a performance tour, concluded its tour and departed from Andong on the evening of the 24th to return home. Duan Yongjie, Secretary of the Andong Municipal Committee of the Chinese Communist Party and Mayor of Andong, along with other party and government leaders and over 400 people from all walks of life, gathered on the banks of the Yalu River to bid them farewell.

After arriving in Liaoning Province on the 7th, the art troupe performed 18 shows in Shenyang, Fushun, Anshan, Lushun-Dalian, Andong, and other places, with an audience of over 23,000 people.

On the evening of the 9th, when the art troupe held its first performance in Shenyang, Huang Huoqing, First Secretary of the Liaoning Provincial Committee of the Chinese Communist Party, Secretary Zhou Huan, Vice Governor of Liaoning Province Che Xiangchen, and Deputy Mayor of Shenyang Song Guang, among others, attended the performance and went on stage to offer congratulations at the end of the show.

### 朝鲜平安北道艺术团回国

1960-12-28
第4版()
专栏：

    朝鲜平安北道艺术团回国
    据新华社沈阳25日电　应邀到辽宁省进行访问演出的朝鲜平安北道艺术团一行七十二人，结束了访问演出活动后在24日晚离开安东回国。中共安东市委书记、安东市市长段永杰等党政领导人和各界群众四百多人到鸭绿江畔欢送。
    艺术团7日到达辽宁省后，曾先后在沈阳、抚顺、鞍山、旅大、安东等地演出了十八场，观众达两万三千多人。
    9日晚，艺术团在沈阳举行首次演出时，中共辽宁省委第一书记黄火青、书记周桓，辽宁省副省长车向忱，沈阳市副市长宋光等曾出席观看，并在演出结束时登台祝贺。
