Title: Chinese Architecture Photo Exhibition Opens in Warsaw
Author:
Date: 1960-12-21
Page: 5
Xinhua News Agency, Warsaw, 19th - A Chinese architecture photo exhibition opened in Warsaw today. The exhibition is sponsored by the Polish Architects Association and the Polish-Chinese Friendship Association.

Attending the opening ceremony were Olszewski, Minister of Economic Affairs of the Central Committee of the Polish United Workers' Party, Pietrusiewicz, Chairman of the Polish Urban Construction Planning Committee, and many other prominent figures in Polish architectural circles.

Wang Bingnan, Chinese Ambassador to Poland, also attended the opening ceremony.

Chairman Pietrusiewicz delivered a speech at the ribbon-cutting ceremony. He said that Chinese architecture serves the people, and after liberation, the genius of the Chinese people in architecture has been fully developed.

### 中国建筑图片展览会在华沙开幕

1960-12-21
第5版()
专栏：

    中国建筑图片展览会在华沙开幕
    新华社华沙19日电　中国建筑图片展览会今天在华沙开幕。这个展览会是由波兰建筑师学会和波中友好协会主办的。
    出席开幕式的有波兰统一工人党中央经济部长奥尔谢夫斯基、波兰城市建筑规划委员会主席彼特鲁西维奇，以及波兰建筑界著名人士等多人。
    中国驻波兰大使王炳南也出席了开幕式。
    彼特鲁西维奇主席在剪彩时发表了讲话。他说，中国的建筑是为人民服务的，解放后，中国人民在建筑方面的天才得到了充分的发挥。
