Title: Exchange of Notes on Implementation Measures for the Treaty on the Question of Dual Nationality Between China and Indonesia
Author: 
Date: 1960-12-22
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Jakarta, December 21st: Li Jusheng, Counselor of the Chinese Embassy in Indonesia, representing the People's Republic of China, and Suska, Director of the Asian and Pacific Affairs Department of the Indonesian Ministry of Foreign Affairs, representing Indonesia, today exchanged notes on the implementation measures of the Treaty on the Question of Dual Nationality.

The text of the implementation measures of the Treaty on the Question of Dual Nationality will be published simultaneously in Beijing and Jakarta on December 24.

### 我国和印度尼西亚  交换双重国籍问题条约实施办法换文

1960-12-22
第1版()
专栏：

    我国和印度尼西亚
    交换双重国籍问题条约实施办法换文
    新华社雅加达21日电　中国驻印度尼西亚大使馆参赞李菊生代表中华人民共和国，印度尼西亚外交部亚洲和太平洋司司长苏斯卡代表印度尼西亚今天交换了关于双重国籍问题的条约的实施办法的换文。
    关于双重国籍问题的条约的实施办法的文本将于12月24日在北京和雅加达同时发表。
