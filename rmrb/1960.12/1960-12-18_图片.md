Title: Photo
Author: Lü Xiangyou
Date: 1960-12-18
Page: 4

    Cambodian Head of State Prince Norodom Sihanouk and other distinguished Cambodian guests, accompanied by Vice Chairman Dong Biwu, Premier Zhou Enlai, and Vice Premier Chen Yi, watched the ballet "Le Corsaire" on December 17. The picture shows the Cambodian guests posing for a photo with the performers after the performance. (Our reporter Lü Xiangyou)
