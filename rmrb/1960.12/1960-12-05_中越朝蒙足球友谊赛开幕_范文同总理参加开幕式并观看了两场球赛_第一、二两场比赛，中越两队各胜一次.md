Title: Sino-Vietnamese-North Korean-Mongolian Football Friendship Tournament Opens; Premier Pham Van Dong Attends Opening Ceremony and Watches Two Matches; Chinese and Vietnamese Teams Each Win Once in the First and Second Games
Author:
Date: 1960-12-05
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, Hanoi, December 3rd: The Vietnam, China, North Korea, and Mongolia Four-Nation Football Friendship Tournament solemnly opened in Hanoi tonight. The Vietnamese team and the Mongolian team played the first game of this friendship tournament tonight. The Vietnamese team won with a score of 3-1.

Tonight, the Hang Day Stadium in Hanoi was unprecedentedly lively. One hundred and twenty floodlights illuminated the stadium, and the national flags of Vietnam, China, North Korea, and Mongolia and colorful flags fluttered around the stands. More than 25,000 spectators watched this first international football match held in North Vietnam.

Those attending tonight's opening ceremony included: Premier Pham Van Dong of the Government of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam, Deputy Premiers Pham Hung and Le Thanh Nghi, as well as heads of various party and government departments.

The delegation of the Cuban Revolutionary Government headed by Llumpart and diplomatic envoys from various countries to Vietnam were also invited to attend the opening ceremony.

The opening ceremony began at 7:30 tonight. After the national anthems of the four countries were played, Nguyen Van Quan, Deputy Director of the Vietnam Central Sports Commission and Director of the Organizing Committee of the Four-Nation Friendship Tournament, delivered the opening speech. On behalf of the Vietnam Central Sports Commission, he extended a warm welcome to the football teams of China, North Korea, and Mongolia, and wished the athletes of the four countries further achievements in consolidating and enhancing friendship and learning from each other.

This friendship tournament is the third one, following those held in Beijing in 1956 and in Pyongyang in 1959. The Mongolian team is participating for the first time this year. This competition adopts a round-robin system.

Xinhua News Agency, Hanoi, December 4th: In the Vietnam, China, North Korea, and Mongolia Four-Nation Football Friendship Tournament held in Hanoi this afternoon, the Chinese football team defeated the North Korean football team with a score of 1-0.

This was the second game of the Four-Nation Football Friendship Tournament. Vietnamese party and government leaders Truong Chinh, Pham Van Dong, Pham Hung, Vo Nguyen Giap, and diplomatic envoys from various countries to Vietnam attended and watched the game.

This intensely contested match attracted approximately 30,000 enthusiastic spectators, who frequently responded to the excellent performances of the players from both countries with warm applause and cheers.

### 中越朝蒙足球友谊赛开幕  范文同总理参加开幕式并观看了两场球赛  第一、二两场比赛，中越两队各胜一次

1960-12-05
第5版()
专栏：

    中越朝蒙足球友谊赛开幕
    范文同总理参加开幕式并观看了两场球赛
    第一、二两场比赛，中越两队各胜一次
    新华社河内3日电　越、中、朝、蒙四国足球友谊赛今天晚上在河内隆重开幕。越南队和蒙古队在今晚进行了这次友谊赛的第一场比赛。越南队以三比一获胜。
    今晚河内行底运动场内盛况空前。一百二十盏聚光灯把运动场照得通明，看台四周飘扬着越、中、朝、蒙四国国旗和五彩缤纷的彩旗。二万五千多名观众观看了这个第一次在越南北方举行的国际足球赛。
    参加今晚开幕式的有：越南民主共和国政府总理范文同，副总理范雄、黎清毅以及党政各部门负责人等。
    以略姆帕尔特为首的古巴革命政府代表团和各国驻越南外交使节也应邀参加了开幕式。
    开幕式是在今晚七时半开始的，在高奏四国国歌后，越南中央体育运动委员会副主任、四国友谊赛组织委员会主任阮文管致开幕词，他代表越南中央体育运动委员会对中国、朝鲜、蒙古三国足球队表示热烈欢迎，并祝四国运动员在巩固和增进友谊以及相互学习方面获得进一步的成就。
    这次友谊赛是继1956年在北京和1959年在平壤所举行的第三次友谊赛。蒙古队今年第一次参加。这次比赛采取循环制。
    据新华社河内4日电　中国足球队今天下午在河内举行的越、中、朝、蒙四国足球友谊赛中，以一比○胜朝鲜足球队。
    这是四国足球友谊赛的第二场比赛。越南党和政府领导人长征、范文同、范雄、武元甲以及各国驻越南的外交使节出席观看了这场球赛。
    这场争夺激烈的球赛，吸引了约三万名热情的观众，他们对两国队员的精彩表演，不时报以热烈掌声和欢呼声。
