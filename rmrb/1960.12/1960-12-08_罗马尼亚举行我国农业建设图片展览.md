Title: China's Agricultural Construction Photo Exhibition Held in Romania
Author:
Date: 1960-12-08
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, Bucharest, December 6th. An exhibition of photographs of China's agricultural construction, sponsored by the Romanian Association for Foreign Cultural Relations, opened here on the 6th. Buican Alexandru, Vice-Chairman of the Romanian Association for Foreign Cultural Relations, and others attended the opening ceremony. Xu Jianguo, Chinese Ambassador to Romania, also attended the opening ceremony. The exhibition displays many pictures showing the superiority of the Chinese rural people's communes and the support of all walks of life for agriculture.

### 罗马尼亚举行我国农业建设图片展览

1960-12-08
第6版()
专栏：

    罗马尼亚举行我国农业建设图片展览
    新华社布加勒斯特6日电　罗马尼亚对外文化联络协会主办的中国农业建设图片展览会6日在这里开幕。罗马尼亚对外文化联络协会副主席布伊康·亚历山德鲁等人参加了开幕式。中国驻罗马尼亚大使许建国也出席了开幕式。展览会展出了许多幅显示中国农村人民公社优越性和各行各业支援农业的图片。
