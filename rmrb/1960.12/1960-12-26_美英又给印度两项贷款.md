Title: US and Britain Provide India with Two More Loans
Author: 
Date: 1960-12-26
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, December 25th: According to a report by the Indian Press Trust on the 23rd, the Indian government, with the consent of the US government, will receive a loan of 50 million US dollars (equivalent to 240 million rupees) from the US Export-Import Bank for its Third Five-Year Plan. This is the second loan the Indian government has received from the US Export-Import Bank. The first loan of 150 million US dollars was obtained in 1958.

The news agency also reported that on the 23rd, the Indian government received a loan of 66.7 million rupees (equivalent to 5 million pounds) from Britain for its Second Five-Year Plan.

The report said that to date, India has received loans totaling over 1.07 billion rupees from Britain for its Second Five-Year Plan.

### 美英又给印度两项贷款

1960-12-26
第6版()
专栏：

    美英又给印度两项贷款
    新华社25日讯　据印度报业托辣斯23日报道，印度政府经美国政府同意，将从美国进出口银行得到五千万美元（相当于二亿四千万卢比）的贷款，供第三个五年计划之用。这是印度政府从美国进出口银行得到的第二笔贷款。第一笔贷款一亿五千万美元是1958年获得的。
    这家通讯社还报道，印度政府23日从英国得到六千六百七十万卢比（合五百万英镑）的贷款供第二个五年计划之用。
    报道说，到现在为止，印度从英国得到的用于第二个五年计划的贷款共十亿七千万卢比以上。
