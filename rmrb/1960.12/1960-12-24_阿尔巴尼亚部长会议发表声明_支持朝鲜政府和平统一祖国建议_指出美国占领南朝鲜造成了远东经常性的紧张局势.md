Title: Albanian Council of Ministers Issues Statement Supporting North Korean Government's Proposal for Peaceful Reunification of the Fatherland; Points out that US Occupation of South Korea Causes Frequent Tensions in the Far East
Author:
Date: 1960-12-24
Page: 5
Xinhua News Agency, Tirana, December 22nd: The Albanian Council of Ministers issued a statement on December 22nd, fully supporting the proposal of the Government of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea regarding the peaceful reunification of the fatherland; and expressing respect and full support for the peaceful policies pursued and the unremitting efforts made by the Government of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea in striving to ease the international situation and strengthen peace and cooperation among the countries of the Far East.

The statement said that the Government of the People's Republic of Albania particularly supports the various new proposals contained in the memorandum of the Government of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea of November 10, 1960, as well as the various proposals put forward by the last Supreme People's Assembly of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea. The Albanian government is convinced that these plans point out the only path to the peaceful reunification of Korea, and the implementation of these plans will greatly benefit the cause of international peace and security.

The statement pointed out that the occupation of South Korea by the United States military is the fundamental obstacle to the peaceful reunification of Korea and causes frequent tensions in the Far East.

The statement said that in order to end this serious and dangerous situation, and for the peaceful reunification of the fatherland, the Government of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea has taken many steps and has always carried out unremitting actions. The statement said that it must also be mentioned that the Chinese and Korean sides took the initiative to propose the withdrawal of the Chinese People's Volunteers, which is an extremely significant step on the road to achieving the peaceful reunification of Korea and striving to consolidate peace.

The statement said that the Government of the People's Republic of Albania fully agrees with the opinion of the Government of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea, that the peaceful reunification of Korea is only a matter for the Korean people themselves and should be carried out by the Korean people themselves without any external interference.

The statement emphasized that the People's Republic of Albania resolutely condemns the occupation of South Korea by US imperialism, and strongly supports the firm demands of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea for the immediate and complete withdrawal of US aggressor troops from South Korea, and for an end to US interference in South Korea under the guise of the United Nations or in any other form.

The statement stated that the People's Republic of Albania has consistently supported, and will continue to support, the sacred struggle of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea to peacefully reunify the fatherland, expose and reject the provocations and aggression of US imperialism, and defend peace in the Far East.

### 阿尔巴尼亚部长会议发表声明  支持朝鲜政府和平统一祖国建议  指出美国占领南朝鲜造成了远东经常性的紧张局势

1960-12-24
第5版()
专栏：

    阿尔巴尼亚部长会议发表声明
    支持朝鲜政府和平统一祖国建议
    指出美国占领南朝鲜造成了远东经常性的紧张局势
    新华社地拉那22日电　阿尔巴尼亚部长会议22日发表了一项声明，完全支持朝鲜民主主义人民共和国政府关于和平统一祖国的建议；并对朝鲜民主主义人民共和国政府为争取缓和国际局势和加强远东各国间的和平与合作所奉行的和平政策和作出的不懈努力表示敬意和完全支持。
    声明说，阿尔巴尼亚人民共和国政府特别支持朝鲜民主主义人民共和国政府的1960年11月10日的备忘录所包含的各项新建议，以及上次朝鲜民主主义人民共和国最高人民会议所提出的各项建议。阿尔巴尼亚政府确信，这些方案指出通向和平统一朝鲜的唯一途径，实行这些方案必将大大有利于国际和平和安全的事业。
    声明指出美国军队在南朝鲜的占领，是和平统一朝鲜的根本障碍，并且造成远东的经常性的紧张局势。
    声明说，为了结束这种严重和危险的局势，为了和平统一祖国，朝鲜民主主义人民共和国政府已经采取了很多步骤，并始终展开百折不挠的行动。声明说，还必须提到中朝方面主动提出撤出中国人民志愿军，这是在争取实现朝鲜和平统一和争取巩固和平的道路上的极其有意义的一步。
    声明说，阿尔巴尼亚人民共和国政府完全赞同朝鲜民主主义人民共和国政府的意见，即：和平统一朝鲜的事只是朝鲜人民自己的事情，应在没有任何外力干涉的情况下由朝鲜人民自己实行。
    声明强调说，阿尔巴尼亚人民共和国坚决谴责美帝国主义对南朝鲜的占领，并且强有力地支持朝鲜民主主义人民共和国关于美国侵略军队立即完全撤出南朝鲜，关于结束美国用联合国的幌子或其它任何形式对南朝鲜进行干涉等坚决要求。
    声明表示，阿尔巴尼亚人民共和国过去和将来都始终如一地支持朝鲜民主主义人民共和国所进行的争取祖国和平统一，揭露和拒绝美帝国主义的挑衅和侵略，以及保卫远东和平的神圣斗争。
