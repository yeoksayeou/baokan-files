Title: Academic Delegation of the Japan-China Friendship Association Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1960-12-01
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, December 30th. The academic delegation of the Japan-China Friendship Association, invited by the Chinese Academy of Sciences to visit and lecture in China, arrived in Beijing by train today. The delegation of eleven members was led by Fukushima Yoichi, head of the delegation, member of the Science Council of Japan, and director of the Japan-China Friendship Association. Those who welcomed them at the station included Zhang Youyu, Deputy Director of the Department of Philosophy and Social Sciences of the Chinese Academy of Sciences, historian Hou Wailu, zoologist Liu Qiaofei, and Pan Chun, Acting Director of the Bureau of Foreign Affairs of the Chinese Academy of Sciences. Japanese peace activist Kinkazu Saionji, who is currently in Beijing, also came to the station to welcome them.

### 日中友协学术代表团到北京

1960-12-01
第4版()
专栏：

    日中友协学术代表团到北京
    新华社30日讯　应中国科学院邀请前来我国访问讲学的日中友协学术代表团一行十一人，在团长、日本学术会议会员、日中友协理事福岛要一率领下，今天乘火车到达北京。到车站欢迎的有：中国科学院哲学社会科学部副主任张友渔、历史学家侯外庐、动物学家刘矫非、中国科学院对外联络局代局长潘纯等人。在北京的日本和平人士西园寺公一也到车站欢迎。
