Title: Chinese Delegations Visiting Myanmar Tour and Visit Yangon; Brigadier General Aung Gyi Hosts Banquet for Chinese Film Delegation
Author:
Date: 1960-12-31
Page: 5
Xinhua News Agency, Yangon, December 29th: Some members of the Chinese Cultural and Art Delegation, led by Zhang Zhixiang, Deputy Director of the Commission for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries, visited Bago, a city about 52 miles from Yangon, this morning. Yesterday afternoon, delegation leader Zhang Zhixiang, deputy leaders Senior Colonel Li Wei, Peng Hua, and key members of the Chinese Cultural and Art Delegation paid visits to Burmese Foreign Minister Sao Khun Kyi and Yangon Municipal Commissioner U Kyaw Min.

The Chinese Yunnan Provincial Delegation, led by Liu Minghui, Vice Governor of Yunnan Province, laid a wreath at the mausoleum of Burmese national hero General Aung San this morning. They also visited the Syriam Oil Refinery across the Yangon River from Yangon City this morning. The refinery manager hosted a luncheon for the delegation in the afternoon. The Chinese Buddhist Delegation, led by Master Xiraojiacuo, President of the Buddhist Association of China, visited the Shwedagon Pagoda this morning and the White Pagoda and Botataung Pagoda this afternoon.

The Chinese Film Delegation, led by Chen Bo, Director of the "August First" Film Studio, paid a visit to Brigadier General Aung Gyi, Deputy Chief of Staff of the Burmese Defense Forces, this morning. In the afternoon, they visited the Myawma Film Studio.

The Chinese Sports Delegation, led by Senior Col. Lu Ting, Chief of the Propaganda Department of the General Political Department of the Chinese People's Liberation Army, enjoyed a pleasant boat ride on the Yangon River this morning.

The Chinese Journalists Delegation, led by Kang Maozhao, Deputy Director of the Information Department of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs, laid a wreath at the Aung San Mausoleum this morning and visited the Shwedagon Pagoda and the World Peace Pagoda.

Later, the delegation visited U Ba Gyi, Secretary of the Burmese Ministry of Information.

U Ba Gyi hosted a banquet in the evening to entertain the Chinese Journalists Delegation. U Ba Gyi and Kang Maozhao both spoke at the banquet.

Xinhua News Agency, Yangon, December 30th: Brigadier General Aung Gyi, Deputy Chief of Staff of the Burmese Defense Forces and Chief Representative of the Burmese side of the Sino-Burmese Joint Boundary Committee, held a banquet here last night to welcome the Chinese Film Delegation, a friendly visiting delegation to Myanmar, headed by Chen Bo. Leaders of the Chinese Cultural and Art Delegation and the Sports Delegation were also invited to the banquet. Also attending the banquet were Brigadier General Ziggeler (Air Force), Lieutenant Colonel Lun and Lieutenant Colonel Win and other senior military officers, as well as well-known figures from the Burmese cultural and artistic circles and the film industry. Senior Colonel Jiang Keding, Military Attaché of the Chinese Embassy in Myanmar, was also invited to attend the banquet.

Brigadier General Aung Gyi spoke at the beginning of the banquet. He warmly welcomed the Chinese Film Delegation's visit to Myanmar. He said that the long-standing border issue between China and Myanmar has been completely resolved, and the edifice of friendship has been built. In the work of building and consolidating this edifice of friendship, cultural and artistic workers of both China and Myanmar have made their contributions. He proposed a toast to the building and consolidation of the Sino-Burmese friendship edifice, to the further development of the friendship between the two countries, and to the flourishing of art in both countries. He enthusiastically shouted in Chinese "Long live Sino-Burmese friendship!"

Chen Bo, head of the Chinese Film Delegation, first expressed his heartfelt gratitude for the genuine friendship of the "Pauk-Phaw" of Myanmar, which is like relatives. He said that this year is the Sino-Burmese Friendship Year. The coming year of 1961 will begin with a new upsurge in Sino-Burmese friendship. The friendship between the people of our two countries is as majestic and solid as the Kunlun Mountains and the Arakan Mountains, and as long and continuous as the Yangtze River and the Irrawaddy River. Delegation leader Chen Bo also praised General Aung Gyi for making outstanding contributions to enhancing understanding and friendship between the people of China and Myanmar.

Zhang Zhixiang, head of the Chinese Cultural and Art Delegation, also raised his glass at the meeting and invited everyone to drink to the consolidation of the newly built Sino-Burmese friendship edifice and to the eternal friendship between the two countries!

### 我国访缅各代表团在仰光参观访问  昂季准将欢宴我国电影代表团

1960-12-31
第5版()
专栏：

    我国访缅各代表团在仰光参观访问
    昂季准将欢宴我国电影代表团
    据新华社仰光29日电　由对外文化联络委员会副主任张致祥率领的中国文化艺术代表团的部分团员，今天上午访问了离仰光大约五十二英里的勃固市。团长张致祥和副团长李伟大校、彭华以及中国文化艺术代表团的主要团员昨天下午曾拜访了缅甸外交部长藻昆卓和仰光市政府专员吴觉敏。
    由云南省副省长刘明辉率领的中国云南省代表团今天上午向缅甸民族英雄昂山将军陵墓献了花圈。他们在上午还参观了位于仰光河畔仰光市对面的丝利奄炼油厂，炼油厂经理在下午举行午宴招待代表团。由中国佛教协会会长喜饶嘉错大师率领的中国佛教代表团上午参观了瑞光大金塔，下午参观了白塔和博塔唐塔。
    由“八一”电影制片厂厂长陈播率领的中国电影代表团今天上午拜访了缅甸国防军副总参谋长昂季准将。在下午，他们参观了谬马电影摄影棚。
    由中国人民解放军总政治部宣传部处长鲁挺上校率领的中国体育代表团今天上午在仰光河上作了一次愉快的泛舟。
    由外交部新闻司副司长康矛召率领的中国新闻工作者代表团今天上午向昂山陵墓献花圈，参观了瑞光大金塔和世界和平塔。
    晚些时候，代表团拜访了缅甸宣传部秘书吴巴觉。
    吴巴觉晚间举行宴会招待中国新闻工作者代表团。吴巴觉和康矛召在宴会上都讲了话。
    据新华社仰光30日电　缅甸国防军副总参谋长、中缅边界联合委员会缅方首席代表昂季准将，昨晚在这里举行宴会，欢迎以陈播团长为首的中国访缅友好代表团的电影代表团。应邀出席宴会的还有中国文化艺术代表团和体育代表团的领导人。出席宴会的还有知格勒准将
  （空军），伦中校和温中校等高级军官以及缅甸文化艺术界和电影界著名人士。我国驻缅甸大使馆武官蒋克定大校等也应邀出席了宴会。
    昂季准将在宴会开始时讲了话。他对中国电影代表团的来缅访问表示热烈欢迎。他说，中缅两国长期存在的边界问题已获得完满的解决，友好大厦已经建成。在建设和巩固这座友好大厦的工作中，中缅两国的文化艺术工作者曾尽了一分力量。他提议为中缅友好大厦的建成和巩固，为两国友谊的进一步发展，为两国艺术工作的百花齐放而干杯。他并热情地用中国话高呼
  “中缅友好万岁！”
    中国电影代表团团长陈播在讲话中，首先对于缅甸“胞波”的这种亲戚般的真挚友谊，表示衷心的感谢。他说，今年是中缅友好年。就要到来的1961年，又将以中缅友谊的新高涨而开始，我们两国人民的友谊像昆仑山和亚拉干山一样巍峨坚实，像长江和伊洛瓦底江一样长流不息。陈播团长还赞扬昂季将军为增进中缅两国人民之间的了解和友谊作出了卓越的贡献。
    中国文化艺术代表团团长张致祥也在会上举杯邀大家为巩固新建成的中缅友好大厦和为两国永远友好而干杯！
