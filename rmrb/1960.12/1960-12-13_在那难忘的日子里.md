Title: In Those Unforgettable Days
Author: Wei Chuantong
Date: 1960-12-13
Page: 8

    In Those Unforgettable Days
    Wei Chuantong
    The Korean People's Army Concert Troupe was invited to visit and perform in our country for over forty days. They not only brought beautiful and moving art to the broad masses of people and officers and soldiers of the People's Liberation Army, but also brought the deep friendship of the Korean people and their army. I felt deeply when I accompanied them to visit and perform in various places, and I wrote this poem to give to my comrades-in-arms.
    
    I
    Time and again gazing at your image,
    Time and again listening to your songs;
    Each time I see, my remembrance deepens,
    Each time I hear, my friendship increases!
    Your heroic image,
    Your moving songs;
    Left to our broad masses of people,
    Left to our broad masses of officers and soldiers.
    
    II
    In those unforgettable days,
    We met our most beloved Chairman Mao.
    He said: As long as we unite,
    All difficulties can be overcome.
    Things are different now,
    Unlike the guerrilla war period.
    We have the experience of piercing through paper tigers,
    And we will surely move from victory to victory!
    
    III
    On the Fujian front line,
    We stand on the same position,
    Using our common language,
    Aimed at our common enemy.
    Brothers and sisters infinitely rejoice,
    Solemn vows from the bottom of our hearts;
    For peaceful labor,
    We maintain high vigilance!
    
    IV
    We jointly meet with the ten sisters of the militia,
    Embrace "Little Eighth Route Army" He Danian;
    The fruits and melons of the people's commune on the front line,
    We enjoy its sweetness together.
    Your "Orchard Harvest,"
    I sincerely wish:
    Socialism gets better every day,
    Imperialism rots every day.
    
    V
    On the banks of the Egret River,
    On the shores of West Lake;
    We, together with the Young Pioneers,
    Had a moving meeting.
    Their pure hearts,
    Engaged in sweet revelry with us.
    They saw the heroic Korean uncles and aunts,
    So happy they were about to jump to the top of the high mountains.
    
    VI
    At Red Flower Hill,
    At Rain Flower Terrace;
    At the Martyrs' Cemetery in Shenyang,
    We mourned together.
    In order to defeat the common enemy,
    We cherish the legacy of the martyrs.
    The flower of friendship formed by fresh blood,
    Blooms in the hearts of the Chinese and Korean people!
    
    VII
    From north to south,
    And from south back to north;
    You crossed the Yellow River,
    You crossed the Yangtze River.
    How many people cheered enthusiastically,
    How many former volunteers looked forward:
    Say hello to Apa Ji and Ama Ni!
    Wish Premier Kim Il-sung good health!
    
    VIII
    Goodbye,
    "Warriors in the Dense Forest"!
    Goodbye,
    Brilliant flowers of the three thousand miles of rivers and mountains!
    Goodbye,
    Our closest comrades-in-arms!
      December 4, 1960 in Shenyang

### 在那难忘的日子里
魏传统
1960-12-13
第8版()
专栏：

    在那难忘的日子里
    魏传统
    朝鲜人民军协奏团应邀来我国访问演出，历时四十余日。他们不仅给我国广大人民和解放军官兵带来了优美动人的艺术，而且也带来了朝鲜人民及其军队的深情友谊。在我陪同他们去各地访问演出时感受很深，特赋诗以赠战友。
    一
    一次又一次注视你们的形象，
    一次又一次倾听你们的歌声；
    每看一次，加深怀念，
    每听一次，倍增友情！
    你们英雄的形象，
    你们动人的歌声；
    留给我们广大人民，
    留给我们广大官兵。
    二
    在那难忘的日子里，
    见到我们最敬爱的毛主席。
    他说：只要我们团结起来，
    一切困难都可以克服的。
    现在不同了，
    不像游击战争时期。
    我们有戳穿纸老虎的经验，
    就一定能从胜利走向胜利！
    三
    在福建前线，
    我们站在同一个阵地，
    使用我们共同的语言，
    对准我们共同的仇敌。
    兄弟姊妹无限欢喜，
    庄严的誓言发自心底；
    为了和平劳动，
    我们保持高度警惕！
    四
    我们一同会见民兵十姊妹，
    拥抱“小八路”何大年；
    前线人民公社的瓜果，
    我们一同享受它的香甜。
    你们的“果园丰收”，
    我衷心祝愿：
    社会主义天天好，
    帝国主义天天烂。
    五
    在鹭江之滨，
    在西子湖畔；
    我们一同与红领巾，
    作了动人的会见。
    他们纯洁的心灵，
    和我们进行甜蜜的联欢。
    她们见到英雄的朝鲜叔叔阿姨，
    乐得快要蹦上高山之巅。
    六
    在红花岗，
    在雨花台；
    在沈阳的烈士陵园，
    我们一同举哀。
    为了战胜共同的敌人，
    我们珍视先烈的遗爱。
    鲜血凝成的友谊之花，
    在中朝人民心中盛开！
    七
    从北方走向南方，
    又从南方转向北方；
    你们跨过黄河，
    你们渡过长江。
    多少人热情激动地欢呼，
    多少前志愿军官兵瞩望：
    向阿爸基、阿妈妮问好！
    祝金日成首相健康！
    八
    再见吧，
    “密林中的勇士”！
    再见吧，
    三千里江山灿烂的花朵！
    再见吧，
    我们最亲密的战友！
      1960年12月4日于沈阳
