Title: Japan's Fumihiko Takaragi Arrives in Beijing
Author: 
Date: 1960-12-31
Page: 4

    Xinhua News Agency, December 30th. At the invitation of Li Jinghan, Chairman of the National Committee of the China Postal and Telecommunications Workers' Union, Fumihiko Takaragi, Chairman of the Central Executive Committee of the Japan Postal Workers' Union, arrived in Beijing by plane today for a visit to our country.
    Li Jinghan, Chairman of the National Committee of the China Postal and Telecommunications Workers' Union, and Chen Yu, Director of the International Liaison Department of the All-China Federation of Trade Unions, were at the airport to greet him.

### 日本宝树文彦到京

1960-12-31
第4版()
专栏：

    日本宝树文彦到京
    新华社30日讯　应中国邮电工会全国委员会主席李景韩邀请，来我国访问的日本全国邮政工会中央执行委员长宝树文彦，今天乘飞机到达北京。
    到机场迎接的，有中国邮电工会全国委员会主席李景韩，中华全国总工会国际联络部部长陈宇。
