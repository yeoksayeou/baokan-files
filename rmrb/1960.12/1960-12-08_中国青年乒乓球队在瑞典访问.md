Title: Chinese Youth Table Tennis Team Visits Sweden
Author:
Date: 1960-12-08
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, Borås, 5th. The Chinese Youth Table Tennis Team held its first team match against the Swedish National Team in Borås, Sweden, on the 4th. The Chinese Youth Table Tennis Team won with a score of five to two.

On the 5th, the Chinese Youth Table Tennis Team went to Mariestad, Sweden, to continue its friendly matches in Sweden.

### 中国青年乒乓球队在瑞典访问

1960-12-08
第6版()
专栏：

    中国青年乒乓球队在瑞典访问
    新华社5日讯　博拉斯消息：中国青年乒乓球队4日在瑞典的博拉斯城与瑞典国家队举行了第一次团体赛，中国青年乒乓球队以五比二获胜。
    5日，中国青年乒乓球队前往瑞典的马里什塔德城继续进行在瑞典的友谊比赛。
