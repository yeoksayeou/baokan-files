Title: Cambodian Ambassador to Mongolia Presents Credentials to Sambuu
Author:
Date: 1960-12-14
Page: 5
Xinhua News Agency, Ulaanbaatar, December 12th: The Ambassador of the Kingdom of Cambodia to Mongolia, Khim Tit, presented his credentials to the Chairman of the Presidium of the Great People's Hural of Mongolia, Sambuu, this morning. During the presentation ceremony, Ambassador Khim Tit delivered a eulogy, and Chairman Sambuu delivered a reply.
    Present at the presentation ceremony was the Minister of Foreign Affairs of Mongolia, Shagdar Surun.

### 柬埔寨驻蒙大使向桑布呈递国书

1960-12-14
第5版()
专栏：

    柬埔寨驻蒙大使向桑布呈递国书
    新华社乌兰巴托12日电　柬埔寨王国驻蒙古大使钦迪，今天上午向蒙古大人民呼拉尔主席团主席桑布呈递了国书。在呈递国书的仪式上，钦迪大使致了颂词，桑布主席致了答词。
    呈递国书时在场的有蒙古外交部长沙格达尔苏伦。
