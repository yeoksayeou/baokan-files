Title: China's Economic Construction Achievement Exhibition Sends Some Agricultural Exhibits to Iraq
Author:
Date: 1960-12-10
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, Baghdad, December 7th: The China Economic Construction Achievement Exhibition, recently held here, today donated the complete set of improved simple agricultural tools and various crop samples exhibited at the exhibition to the relevant departments of the Iraqi government.

Attending the donation ceremony were Abdul Hadi Ismail, Director of the Research Bureau of the Ministry of Agriculture, Abdul Jabbar Uwaz, Director of the Agricultural Machinery Department of the Land Reform Department, Hafiz Tokmashi, Director of the Trade Bureau of the Ministry of Trade, and other officials. Li Xinnong, Commercial Counselor of the Chinese Embassy, also attended the ceremony.

Zhang Ziquan, the curator of the exhibition, said at the ceremony that the China Economic Construction Achievement Exhibition had achieved great success. More than 150,000 people from all walks of life in Iraq visited the exhibition and praised it highly. The exhibition made a great contribution to promoting friendship and mutual understanding between the two peoples. He said that in order to thank the sincere friendship of Iraqi friends, he, on behalf of the exhibition, donated the improved simple agricultural tools and various crop samples that aroused widespread interest among the Iraqi audience to the Iraqi Ministry of Agriculture and relevant departments. He hoped that this would be a good start for the exchange of agricultural technical experience between China and Iraq and would help promote friendly cooperation between the two countries.

Abdul Hadi Ismail thanked the Chinese government for donating these gifts on behalf of the Iraqi government. He said he hoped this would be the beginning of technical and scientific cooperation between the two countries.

### 我国经济建设成就展览会将部分农业展品送给伊拉克

1960-12-10
第6版()
专栏：

    我国经济建设成就展览会将部分农业展品送给伊拉克
    新华社巴格达7日电　不久前在这里举行的中国经济建设成就展览会今天把展览会中展出的整套改良简易农具和各种作物的样品赠送给伊拉克政府的各有关部门。
    参加在这里举行的赠送仪式的有农业部研究局局长阿卜杜勒·哈迪·伊斯梅尔，土改部农业机器司司长阿卜杜勒·贾巴尔·乌瓦兹，贸易部贸易局局长哈菲兹·托克马希和其它官员。中国大使馆商务参赞李新农也出席了这个仪式。
    展览馆馆长张子泉在仪式上讲话说，中国经济建设成就展览会获得了巨大的成功。有十五万以上的伊拉克各界人士参观了展览会，并对展览会备极赞扬。展览会对促进两国人民之间的友谊和互相了解作出了巨大的贡献。他说，为了感谢伊拉克朋友们的诚挚友谊，他以展览会的名义，把在伊拉克观众中间引起广泛兴趣的改良简易农具和各种作物的样品赠送给伊拉克农业部和各有关部门。他希望这将成为中国和伊拉克交流农业技术经验的良好开端，并有助于促进两国的友好合作。
    阿卜杜勒·哈迪·伊斯梅尔代表伊拉克政府感谢中国政府赠送这些礼物。他说，他希望这将成为两国间技术和科学合作的开端。
