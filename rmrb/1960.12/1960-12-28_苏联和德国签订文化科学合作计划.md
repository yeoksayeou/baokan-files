Title: Soviet Union and Germany Sign Cultural and Scientific Cooperation Plan
Author:
Date: 1960-12-28
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, Berlin News: The 1961 Soviet Union and German Democratic Republic Cultural and Scientific Cooperation Plan was signed in Berlin on the 23rd.

The plan stipulates close cooperation and extensive exchanges between the two countries in the fields of science, higher education, national education, healthcare, art, radio, television, newspapers, and sports.

The Soviet Union and the German Democratic Republic will send actors and musical groups to visit each other.

The plan also stipulates that the two countries will expand contacts between scientists and scientific research institutions, and experts from the two countries will conduct mutual visits, etc.

### 苏联和德国签订文化科学合作计划

1960-12-28
第5版()
专栏：

    苏联和德国签订文化科学合作计划
    新华社讯　据塔斯社柏林讯：1961年苏联和德意志民主共和国文化和科学合作计划23日在柏林签订。
    计划规定，两国在科学、高等教育、国民教育、保健、艺术、电台、电视、报刊和体育运动方面紧密的合作和广泛的交往。
    苏联和德意志民主共和国将互派演员和音乐团体进行访问。
    计划还规定，两国将扩大科学家和科学研究机关的联系，两国专家进行互相访问等。
