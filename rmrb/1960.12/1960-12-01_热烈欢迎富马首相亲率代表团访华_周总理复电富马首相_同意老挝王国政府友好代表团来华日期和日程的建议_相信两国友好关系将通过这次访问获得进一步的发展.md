Title: Warmly Welcome Premier Phouma's Visit to China at the Head of a Delegation. Premier Zhou Sends Reply to Premier Phouma, Agreeing to the Date and Schedule Proposed for the Visit of the Royal Lao Government Friendship Delegation. Believes that Friendly Relations Between the Two Countries Will Be Further Developed Through This Visit.
Author:
Date: 1960-12-01
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, 30th - Premier Souvanna Phouma of the Royal Lao Government sent a telegram to Premier Zhou Enlai on the 29th. The text is as follows: To His Excellency Premier Zhou Enlai of the People's Republic of China, Beijing:

	I have the honor to reply to your telegram of November 26, and hereby inform Your Excellency that the Royal Government's friendship delegation to China is preparing to depart from Vientiane on December 10 and arrive in Guangzhou via Hanoi on the same day. The delegation will visit Beijing from December 12 to 16 and leave Beijing on the 16th for Guangzhou via Nanjing. The Royal Government would be most grateful if the Government of the People's Republic of China could arrange for the delegation's travel from Guangzhou to Beijing to Nanjing to Guangzhou. The delegation will consist of eleven members, namely: His Highness Prince Souvanna Phouma, Prime Minister and Minister of Foreign Affairs; His Excellency Inpeng Souryalath, Minister of Finance; His Excellency Khamsouk Keola, Minister of Health; three members of parliament, whose names will be notified later; Lien Bouranavongkham, Secretary-General of the Cabinet; Lieutenant Colonel Soukan Vilaysane, Director of the National Archives; Dipatay Vilayhong, Private Secretary to the Prime Minister; an aide-de-camp to the Prime Minister and a photographer. I would be most grateful if you could inform me whether the suggested dates are suitable for the Government of the People's Republic of China. The exact timing of the trip will be notified separately.

	Please accept, Your Excellency, the assurances of my highest consideration.

			Souvanna Phouma
			Prime Minister of the Royal Lao Government
			Vientiane, November 29, 1960

Xinhua News Agency, 30th - Premier Zhou Enlai of the State Council of the People's Republic of China sent a telegram today to Premier Phouma of the Royal Lao Government, warmly welcoming Premier Phouma's personal visit to China at the head of the Royal Lao Government Friendship Delegation. The text is as follows: To His Highness Prince Souvanna Phouma, Prime Minister of the Royal Lao Government, Vientiane:

	I have the honor to receive Your Highness's telegram of November 29. The Chinese Government agrees to the suggestions made by Your Highness in the telegram regarding the date and schedule of the Royal Lao Government Friendship Delegation's visit to China. The Chinese Government and the Chinese people warmly welcome Your Highness's personal visit at the head of the Royal Lao Government Friendship Delegation, and believe that, through Your Highness's visit, the friendly relations between our two countries on the basis of the Five Principles of Peaceful Coexistence will be further developed.

	The Chinese Government suggests that our two governments maintain contact and make specific arrangements regarding the visit of the Royal Lao Government Friendship Delegation to China through our respective embassies in the Kingdom of Cambodia.

	Please accept, Your Highness, the assurances of my highest consideration.

							Zhou Enlai
							Premier of the State Council of the People's Republic of China
							Beijing, November 30, 1960

### 热烈欢迎富马首相亲率代表团访华  周总理复电富马首相  同意老挝王国政府友好代表团来华日期和日程的建议  相信两国友好关系将通过这次访问获得进一步的发展

1960-12-01
第1版()
专栏：

    热烈欢迎富马首相亲率代表团访华
    周总理复电富马首相
    同意老挝王国政府友好代表团来华日期和日程的建议
    相信两国友好关系将通过这次访问获得进一步的发展
    新华社30日讯　老挝王国政府首相梭发那·富马亲王在29日打电报给周恩来总理，电文如下：北京中华人民共和国周恩来总理阁下：
    我荣幸地答复您11月26日来电，特通知阁下，王国政府访华友好代表团准备于12月10日自万象启程，当日经河内抵达广州。代表团将在12月12日至16日访问北京，16日离北京经南京前往广州。如蒙中华人民共和国政府为代表团安排广州—北京—南京—广州的旅行，王国政府将不胜感激。代表团将由十一人组成，即：首相兼外交大臣梭发那·富马亲王殿下，财政大臣因彭·苏里亚泰阁下，卫生大臣坎苏·高拉阁下，另有三名议员，其姓名随后另行通知，内阁秘书长连·布拉冯汶坎，国家档案局局长苏坎·维莱山上校，首相私人秘书狄帕泰·维莱洪，首相副官及摄影记者各一名。如蒙您通知我所建议的时间对中华人民共和国政府是否适宜，我将十分感激。至于旅程的确切时间将另行通知。
    阁下，请您接受我最崇高的敬意。
            老挝王国政府首相
            梭发那·富马亲王
            1960年11月29日于万象
    新华社30日讯　中华人民共和国国务院总理周恩来今天打电报给老挝王国政府富马首相，热烈欢迎富马首相亲自率领老挝王国政府友好代表团访问中国，电文如下：万象老挝王国政府首相梭发那·富马亲王殿下：
    我荣幸收到殿下11月29日的来电。中国政府同意殿下在来电中所提关于老挝王国政府友好代表团来华日期和日程的建议。中国政府和中国人民热烈地欢迎殿下亲自率领老挝王国政府友好代表团的光临，并且相信，通过殿下的来访，贵我两国在和平共处五项原则基础上的友好关系将获得进一步的发展。
    中国政府建议，贵我两国政府通过双方驻柬埔寨王国大使馆就老挝王国政府友好代表团访华事保持联系，并且作出具体的安排。
    殿下，谨向你致以最崇高的敬意。
                          中华人民共和国国务院总理
                                                      周恩来
                                      1960年11月30日于北京
