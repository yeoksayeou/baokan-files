Title: Czechoslovakia and Germany Sign Payment Agreement
Author: 
Date: 1960-12-21
Page: 5

    Xinhua News Agency, Prague, Dec. 15th -- The Czechoslovak Socialist Republic and the German Democratic Republic signed a payment agreement here today for the years 1961-1965.
    Vice Minister of Foreign Trade Baura signed on behalf of the Czechoslovak side, and Vice Minister of Finance Roth signed on behalf of the German Democratic Republic.

### 捷克斯洛伐克德国签订支付协定

1960-12-21
第5版()
专栏：

    捷克斯洛伐克德国签订支付协定
    新华社布拉格电　捷克斯洛伐克社会主义共和国和德意志民主共和国15日在此间签订了1961—1965年支付协定。
    代表捷克斯洛伐克方面签字的是对外贸易部副部长鲍拉，代表德意志民主共和国方面签字的是财政部副部长罗特。
