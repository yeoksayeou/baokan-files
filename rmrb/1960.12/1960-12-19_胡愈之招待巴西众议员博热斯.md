Title: Hu Yuzhi Hosts Brazilian Congressman Borges
Author:
Date: 1960-12-19
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, December 18th. Brazilian Congressman and newly elected Governor of Goiás, Mauro Borges Teixeira, and his wife, arrived in Beijing on the 16th for a visit to China at the invitation of the Chinese People's Institute of Foreign Affairs.

On the evening of the 17th, Hu Yuzhi, Vice President of the Chinese People's Institute of Foreign Affairs, hosted a banquet for the guests. In his speech at the banquet, Hu Yuzhi expressed his hope that the people of China and Brazil would develop more exchanges to promote the further development of friendship between the two peoples.

In his speech at the banquet, Borges said: "After I return to Brazil, I will introduce more of the achievements of the Chinese people's work, and I will do my best, together with Brazilian friends who are friendly to China, to promote the establishment of trade and diplomatic relations between Brazil and China as soon as possible."

Among those present at the banquet were: Ji Chaoding, Vice President of the Chinese-Latin American Friendship Association; Wu Han, Deputy Mayor of Beijing; Shao Zonghan, head of the Chinese journalists delegation that had visited Brazil; and Wang Disan, Deputy Director of the Department of American and Australian Affairs of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs.

### 胡愈之招待巴西众议员博热斯

1960-12-19
第4版()
专栏：

    胡愈之招待巴西众议员博热斯
    新华社18日讯　巴西众议员、新当选的果亚斯州州长摩洛·博热斯·蒂希拉及夫人，应中国人民外交学会邀请来我国访问，已经在16日到达北京。
    17日晚上，中国人民外交学会副会长胡愈之设宴招待客人。胡愈之在宴会上致词，表示希望中国和巴西两国人民发展更多的往来，以促进中巴两国人民友谊的进一步发展。
    博热斯在宴会上致词说：“我回巴西后，将更多地介绍中国人民的工作成就，尽我最大的努力，同巴西的对中国友好的朋友们一起，促使巴西尽快地和中国建立贸易和外交关系。”
    出席这次宴会的有：中国拉丁美洲友好协会副会长冀朝鼎，北京市副市长吴晗，曾经访问过巴西的中国新闻工作者代表团负责人邵宗汉，外交部美澳司副司长王第三等。
