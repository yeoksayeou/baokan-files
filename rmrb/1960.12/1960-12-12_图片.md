    The Si Yi Production Team of the Keqiao Commune in Shaoxing County, Zhejiang Province, has been expanding its area of triple cropping year by year. This year, more than 60% of the land is implementing the new triple cropping system of wheat or rapeseed followed by double-cropped rice. This is the commune members sowing the first crop – wheat.

　　　　　　　　　　　　　　
　　　　　　　　　　　　Xinhua News Agency reporter Wu Yuanliu photo
