Title: Vientiane Radio Condemns Foreign Interventionists for Supporting Rebel Groups; Phoumi Nosavan Group Rebels Acquire Foreign Tanks and Motorboats

Author:
Date: 1960-12-05
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, Hanoi, December 4th. Vientiane Radio on the evening of the 3rd condemned foreign interventionists for directly supporting the Phoumi Nosavan group rebels with tanks and other military equipment, including artillery and military motorboats.

The radio said that according to a rebel defector, in the battle south of North Cardin, the Phoumi rebel group used 120cm mortars and 105cm artillery to attack the Mekong River bank within Laos. At the same time, artillery within Thailand also attacked the Lao Royal Government forces.

The defector also revealed that the Phoumi Nosavan rebel group also possesses a considerable number of M-24 tanks and other up-to-date equipment.

Vientiane Radio pointed out that before the coup on August 9th this year, Phoumi Nosavan's army only had M-24 tanks in Vientiane; there were none in other places. The above-mentioned defector's confession is sufficient proof that foreign interventionists have directly aided the rebels with tanks and other military equipment, including artillery and military motorboats.

### 万象电台谴责外国干涉者支持叛乱集团  富米·诺萨万集团叛军获得外国坦克和汽艇等装备

1960-12-05
第5版()
专栏：

    万象电台谴责外国干涉者支持叛乱集团
    富米·诺萨万集团叛军获得外国坦克和汽艇等装备
    新华社河内4日电　万象电台3日晚谴责外国干涉者直接以坦克和其他军事装备包括大炮和军用汽艇支持富米·诺萨万集团叛军。
    电台说，据一个开小差的叛军供称，在北卡定南面的战斗中，富米叛乱集团用一二○厘米的迫击炮和一○五厘米的大炮向老挝境内的湄公河沿岸袭击，同时，在泰国境内的大炮也向老挝王国政府军攻击。
    这个逃兵还透露说，富米·诺萨万叛乱集团还拥有相当数量的M·24型坦克以及其它最新的装备。
    万象电台指出，在今年8月9日政变以前，富米·诺萨万的军队只在万象一地拥有M ·24型坦克，其它地方是没有的。上述逃兵的供词，足以证明外国干涉者已经直接以坦克以及其它军事装备包括大炮和军用汽艇援助叛军。
