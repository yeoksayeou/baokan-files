    Number of Unemployed in Several Major Capitalist Countries in 1960
    United States: 4,031,000 (November)
    United Kingdom: 365,063 (December 12)
    France: 103,200 (August)
    West Germany: 136,355 (November)
    Italy: 1,324,000 (September)

    Consumer Price Index in 1960
    United States: 127.9 (November)
    United Kingdom: 121 (August)
    France: 135 (August)
    West Germany: 114 (September)
    Italy: 116 (August)
  
  (Note) In the consumer price index,
  the United States uses the average index of 1947-1949 as 100, and the other four countries use the index of 1953 as 100.
