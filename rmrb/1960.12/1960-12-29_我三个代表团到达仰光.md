Title: Three Chinese Delegations Arrive in Rangoon
Author:
Date: 1960-12-29
Page: 5
Xinhua News Agency, Rangoon, December 28th: The Yunnan Provincial Delegation of the Chinese Friendship Delegation to Burma, led by Liu Minghui, Deputy Governor of Yunnan Province, arrived here by plane from Kunming this morning.

　　Officials who went to the airport to welcome the delegation included U Htun Aye, Minister of Education and Health of the Shan State Government of Burma, and U Aung Thaung, Minister of Forestry and Agriculture of the Kachin State Government. Lei Yang and Sun Shengwei, Counselors of the Chinese Embassy in Burma, and other embassy officials also went to the airport to welcome them.

　　The Yunnan Provincial Delegation, as part of the Friendship Delegation of the People's Republic of China to Burma led by Premier Zhou Enlai, is visiting at the invitation of the Burmese government to participate in the 13th Anniversary Celebration of the Independence of the Union of Burma and the ceremony for the exchange of instruments of ratification of the Sino-Burmese Boundary Treaty.

　　Xinhua News Agency, Rangoon, December 28th: The Buddhist Delegation of the Chinese Friendship Delegation to Burma, led by Master Xirao Gyatso, President of the Buddhist Association of China, and Vice Presidents Zhao Puchu and Elder Songliu Agamuni, arrived here by plane this morning.

　　When Master Xirao Gyatso and other distinguished Chinese guests stepped out of the cabin, U Cho Khun Kyi, Speaker of the Burmese Chamber of Nationalities, U Ba Saw, Minister of Religious Affairs, U Than Shwe, Secretary of the Ministry of Religious Affairs to Parliament, and U Ba Sheng, Secretary of the Ministry of Religious Affairs, stepped forward to greet them with palms together. More than 400 representatives of the Burmese Buddhist community and famous Buddhist scholars gathered at the airport, waving religious banners and flowers to warmly welcome the Chinese Buddhist Delegation.

　　Lei Yang and Sun Shengwei, Counselors of the Chinese Embassy in Burma, other diplomatic officials of the embassy, and representatives of overseas Chinese in Rangoon also went to the airport to welcome them.

　　The Chinese Buddhist Delegation is part of the Friendship Delegation of the People's Republic of China to Burma led by Premier Zhou Enlai. The delegation will participate in the 13th Anniversary Celebration of the Independence of the Union of Burma and the ceremony for the exchange of instruments of ratification of the Sino-Burmese Boundary Treaty, and will visit various parts of Burma.

　　Xinhua News Agency, Rangoon, December 28th: The Chinese Journalists Delegation of the Chinese Friendship delegation to Burma, led by Kang Maozhao, Deputy Director of the Information Department of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs, also arrived here by plane this morning. The delegation is part of the Friendship Delegation of the People's Republic of China to Burma led by Premier Zhou Enlai. It will participate in the celebration of the 13th Anniversary of the Independence of the Union of Burma and the ceremony for the exchange of instruments of ratification of the Sino-Burmese Boundary Treaty, and will pay friendly visits to various parts of Burma.

　　About thirty people, including U Kyaw Soe, Director of the Burmese Information Bureau, U Khin, Deputy Director of the Burmese Information Bureau and Head of the Burmese News Delegation to China, U Aung Than, Chairman of the Burmese Journalists Association, U Tin Maung Gyi, Vice Chairman, and representatives of the Burmese press, were at the airport to welcome the delegation.

　　Xiao Ming, Cultural Counselor of the Chinese Embassy in Burma, and other diplomatic officials of the embassy were also present to welcome them.

### 我三个代表团到达仰光

1960-12-29
第5版()
专栏：

    我三个代表团到达仰光
    新华社仰光28日电　中国访缅友好代表团的云南省代表团在云南省副省长刘明辉率领下今天上午从昆明乘飞机到达这里。
    前往机场欢迎代表团的有缅甸掸邦政府教育和卫生部部长苏吞埃，克钦邦政府林业和农业部部长吴昂陶等官员。中国驻缅甸大使馆参赞雷扬、孙盛渭以及大使馆其他官员也到机场欢迎。
    云南省代表团是作为周恩来总理率领的中华人民共和国访问缅甸联邦友好代表团的一部分，应缅甸政府的邀请，来参加缅甸联邦独立十三周年庆典和互换中缅边界条约批准书的仪式的。
    新华社仰光28日电　中国访缅友好代表团的佛教代表团在团长、中国佛教协会会长喜饶嘉错大师和副团长、中国佛教协会副会长赵朴初居士和中国佛教协会副会长松榴·阿戛木尼亚长老率领下，今天上午乘飞机到达这里。
    当喜饶嘉错大师等中国贵宾走出机舱时，缅甸民族院议长藻昆基、宗教事务部部长吴巴素、宗教事务部驻议会秘书吴丹盛和宗教事务部秘书吴巴盛都迎上前去合十致敬。聚集在机场上的缅甸四百多位佛教界代表和著名的佛教学者挥动着宗教幡旗和鲜花热烈欢迎中国佛教代表团。
    中国驻缅甸大使馆参赞雷扬、孙盛渭和大使馆其他外交官员以及仰光华侨代表也到机场欢迎。
    中国佛教代表团是周恩来总理率领的中华人民共和国访问缅甸联邦友好代表团的一部分。代表团将参加缅甸联邦独立十三周年庆典和互换中缅边界条约批准书仪式，并去缅甸各地参观访问。
    新华社仰光28日电　中国访缅友好代表团的中国新闻工作者代表团，今天上午在外交部新闻司副司长康矛召率领下，乘飞机到达这里。代表团是周恩来总理率领的中华人民共和国访问缅甸联邦友好代表团的一部分。它将参加缅甸联邦独立十三周年的庆典和参加互换中缅边界条约批准书的仪式，并将到缅甸各地进行友好访问。
    到机场欢迎代表团的有缅甸新闻局局长吴觉梭、缅甸新闻局副局长、缅甸访华新闻代表团团长吴钦、缅甸新闻工作者协会主席吴昂丹、副主席吴丁貌季和缅甸新闻界代表等约三十人。
    中国驻缅甸大使馆文化参赞萧明和大使馆的其他外交官员也到场欢迎。
