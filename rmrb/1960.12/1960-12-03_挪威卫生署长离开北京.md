Title: Norwegian Health Director Leaves Beijing
Author:
Date: 1960-12-03
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, December 2nd: The Norwegian Health Director, Evang, and his wife left Beijing by train today. They will visit various parts of China before returning home.

Minister of Health Li Dequan hosted a banquet at noon today to bid farewell to Evang and his wife.

The Norwegian Ambassador to China, Tang Si, was invited to attend the banquet.

### 挪威卫生署长离开北京

1960-12-03
第4版()
专栏：

    挪威卫生署长离开北京
    新华社2日讯　挪威卫生署长埃万和他的夫人今天乘火车离开北京，他们将在我国各地参观访问以后回国。
    卫生部部长李德全今天中午设宴为埃万和他的夫人送行。
    挪威驻我国大使唐斯应邀出席了宴会。
