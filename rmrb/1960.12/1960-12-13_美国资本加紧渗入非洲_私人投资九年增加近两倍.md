Title: US Capital Intensifies Infiltration into Africa: Private Investment Nearly Triples in Nine Years
Author:
Date: 1960-12-13
Page: 6

    US Capital Intensifies Infiltration into Africa
    Private Investment Nearly Triples in Nine Years
    (Reported by this newspaper) A report released by the US Department of Commerce on the 11th stated that the total amount of US private investment in Africa reached $830 million by December 1959, an increase of nearly three times compared to $300 million in 1950. The sales of directly invested enterprises had already reached $1.2 billion in 1957, exceeding the total investment. These enterprises earned an annual profit of $128 million.

### 美国资本加紧渗入非洲  私人投资九年增加近两倍

1960-12-13
第6版()
专栏：

    美国资本加紧渗入非洲
    私人投资九年增加近两倍
    本报讯　美国商务部11日发表的一份报告说，美国在非洲的私人投资总额到1959年12月已达八亿三千万美元，比1950年的三亿美元增加近两倍，而直接投资的企业的销售额在1957年就已经达到十二亿美元，超过了投资总额。这些企业每年所赚的利润为一亿二千八百万美元。
