Title: Vice Premier Chen Yi Hosts Banquet for Cuban Ambassador to China
Author:
Date: 1960-12-04
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, December 3rd – Vice Premier and Foreign Minister Chen Yi and his wife hosted a luncheon today to welcome the first Ambassador Extraordinary and Plenipotentiary of the Republic of Cuba to China, Óscar Pino Santos, and his wife.  Those attending the banquet included Vice Foreign Minister Geng Biao and his wife, Deputy Director of the Protocol Department Ge Buhai, and Deputy Director of the Department of American and Australian Affairs Lin Ping. American progressive writer Anna Louise Strong was also invited to the banquet. (Picture attached)

### 陈毅副总理欢宴古巴驻华大使

1960-12-04
第1版()
专栏：

    陈毅副总理欢宴古巴驻华大使
    新华社3日讯　陈毅副总理兼外交部长和夫人今日设午宴欢迎古巴共和国首任驻华特命全权大使奥斯卡·皮诺·桑托斯和夫人。出席宴会的有外交部副部长耿飚和夫人，礼宾司副司长葛步海，美澳司副司长林平。美国进步作家安娜·路易斯·斯特朗也应邀出席了宴会。（附图片）
    国务院副总理兼外交部长陈毅和古巴大使在宴会上。美国进步作家安娜·路易斯·斯特朗女士（右）也应邀出席了宴会。　新华社记者　刘庆瑞摄
