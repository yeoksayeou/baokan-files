Title: Vietnamese Economic and Trade Delegation Visits Beijing
Author:
Date: 1960-12-08
Page: 2

Xinhua News Agency, December 7th: The distinguished guests of the Vietnamese Democratic Republic government's economic and trade delegation, led by Vice Premier Nguyen Duy Trinh, have been visiting Beijing in recent days.

From December 5th to 7th, Vice Premier Nguyen Duy Trinh and others, accompanied by responsible persons from relevant government departments and Beijing Municipality, visited the Great Hall of the People, Xuanwu Steel Plant, Guanghua Lumber Factory, National Agricultural Exhibition Hall, Erlong Road People's Commune, Beijing Oil Pressing Factory, Nanhua Oil Factory, etc. The guests were warmly welcomed by the hosts everywhere they went.

### 越南经济贸易代表团在京参观

1960-12-08
第2版()
专栏：

    越南经济贸易代表团在京参观
    新华社7日讯　以阮维桢副总理为首的越南民主共和国政府经济、贸易代表团的贵宾，连日来在北京进行了参观和访问。
    从5日到7日，阮维桢副总理等分别由政府有关部门和北京市的负责人陪同，参观和访问了人民大会堂、宣武钢铁厂、光华木材厂、全国农业展览馆、二龙路人民公社、北京市榨油厂、南花油厂等。贵宾们每到一处，都受到主人们的热烈欢迎。
