Title: Poland—Scientist Returns Home

Author: 
Date: 1960-12-28
Page: 4

    Xinhua News Agency, December 25th.  Bo. Sakherzewski, a scientist from the Polish Institute of International Affairs who came to China for a visit under the 1960-1961 executive plan of the Sino-Polish Cultural Cooperation Agreement, left Beijing by plane today to return to his country.
    Meng Yongqian, Director of the Chinese Institute of International Relations, hosted a farewell banquet for the guest on the 24th.

### 波兰—科学工作者回国

1960-12-28
第4版()
专栏：

    波兰—科学工作者回国
    新华社25日讯　根据中波文化合作协定1960—1961年执行计划来我国访问的波兰国际问题研究所科学工作者博·萨克热夫斯基，今天乘飞机离开北京回国。
    中国国际关系研究所所长孟用潜24日曾设宴欢送客人。
