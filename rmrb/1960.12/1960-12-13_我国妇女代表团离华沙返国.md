Title: Chinese Women's Delegation Leaves Warsaw for Home
Author:
Date: 1960-12-13
Page: 5
Xinhua News Agency, Warsaw, December 10th. The Chinese women's delegation attending the Enlarged Council Meeting of the Women's International Democratic Federation left Warsaw by train today to return home.

Among those who went to the station to see them off were: Sava Davetska, Secretary-General of the Polish Women's League, and Ashkanaezi, Secretary of the Secretariat of the Women's League. Wang Bingnan, the Chinese Ambassador to Poland, and his wife also went to see them off.

### 我国妇女代表团离华沙返国

1960-12-13
第5版()
专栏：

    我国妇女代表团离华沙返国
    新华社华沙10日电　参加国际民主妇女联合会理事会扩大会议的中国妇女代表团，今天乘火车离华沙返国。
    前往车站送行的有：波兰妇女联盟总书记萨瓦戴茨卡、妇女联盟书记处书记阿什坎纳日等人。中国驻波兰大使王炳南和夫人也前往送行。
