Title: Industrial Production in Poland Exceeds Expectations; Heavy Industry Completes 102.2% of Monthly Plan
Author:
Date: 1960-12-16
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, Warsaw, December 15th: According to reports in the local press, Poland achieved good results on the production front in November. Heavy industry completed 102.2% of its monthly plan, with metallurgy and machine manufacturing being the most outstanding sectors.

	Steelworkers produced 27,500 tons of steel, 20,000 tons of steel products, and 5,000 tons of coke above the plan for the month. In the first eleven months of this year, they produced a total of 4,104,000 tons of pig iron, 5,686,000 tons of steel, and 3,862,000 tons of steel products.

	The coal mining industry completed its monthly plan by November 29th. By the end of November, coal miners had over-fulfilled their plan by nearly 500,000 tons of coal, the first time in several years.

	The chemical industry also achieved great results, with the value of products produced above the plan in November reaching approximately 35 million zlotys.

	The building materials industry completed 101.7% of its monthly plan, an increase of 15.5% over the same period last year. Cement production in November increased by 4,700 tons over the original plan.

### 波上月工业生产成绩良好  重工业完成了月计划的102.2％

1960-12-16
第5版()
专栏：

    波上月工业生产成绩良好
    重工业完成了月计划的102.2％
    新华社华沙15日电　据此间报刊报道，波兰11月份生产战线上的成绩良好。重工业完成了月计划的102.2％，其中以冶金、机器制造业等部门的成绩最为突出。
    钢铁工人这个月超计划多生产了二万七千五百吨钢、二万吨钢材和五千吨焦炭。他们在今年头十一个月中，共生产了四百一十万四千吨生铁，五百六十八万六千吨钢和三百八十六万二千吨钢材。
    采煤工业在11月29日就完成了月度计划。到11月底，采煤工人超计划多开采了将近五十万吨煤，这是数年以来的第一次。
    化学工业也取得了很大的成绩，11月份超计划生产的产品价值约达三千五百万兹罗提。
    建筑材料工业完成了月计划的101.7％，比去年同期增加了15.5％。11月份水泥产量比原计划增加了四千七百吨。
