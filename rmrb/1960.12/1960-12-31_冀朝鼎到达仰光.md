Title: Ji Chaoding Arrives in Rangoon
Author:
Date: 1960-12-31
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, Rangoon, December 30th: Ji Chaoding, Vice Chairman of the China Council for the Promotion of International Trade and head of the exhibition group for the Industrial and Agricultural Exhibition of the People's Republic of China, arrived in Rangoon by plane yesterday evening.

Welcoming him at the airport were U Hla Gyaw, Parliamentary Secretary of the Ministry of Trade Development and Civil Supplies of Burma, as well as other Burmese government officials. Li Yimang, the Chinese Ambassador to Burma, and Peng Jinbo, the Commercial Counselor, also welcomed him at the airport. The China Industrial and Agricultural Exhibition is scheduled to open on January 3 next year.

### 冀朝鼎到达仰光

1960-12-31
第5版()
专栏：

    冀朝鼎到达仰光
    新华社仰光30日电　中国国际贸易促进委员会副主席、中华人民共和国工业、农业展览会展览团团长冀朝鼎昨天晚上乘飞机到达仰光。
    到机场欢迎的有缅甸贸易发展和民用物资供应部驻议会秘书吴拉觉以及缅甸政府的其他官员。中国驻缅甸大使李一氓、商务参赞彭金波也到机场欢迎。中国工业、农业展览会预定在明年1月3日开幕。
