Title: Vo Nguyen Giap Calls the Chairman of the International Commission for Supervision and Control to Protest the United States Creating Tension in South Vietnam
Author:
Date: 1960-12-24
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, Hanoi, Vo Nguyen Giap, General Commander of the Vietnamese People's Army, telegraphed Gopala Menon, Chairman of the International Commission for Supervision and Control in Vietnam, on the 17th, protesting the United States' dispatch of approximately twenty warships of the Seventh Fleet to the waters of Toulon, South Vietnam, and the deployment of seaplanes carrying numerous American officers to land at the Toulon military base, actions intended to create tension in Indochina.

The telegram stated that, as is well known, American imperialism is actively interfering in South Vietnam; the South Vietnamese authorities have already concentrated forces at the provisional military demarcation line, dispatching armed forces to invade buffer zones and create tension in the demilitarized zone. American imperialism and its lackeys in the Southeast Asian military aggression bloc are also carrying out armed aggression against Laos, undermining peace in Indochina and Southeast Asia.

The telegram emphasized that, under the aforementioned circumstances, the US Seventh Fleet's movement towards South Vietnam and the deployment of aircraft into Toulon are extremely dangerous actions. This blatant action is an integral part of the US's intensified efforts to create tension in Vietnam, and it is related to the US's intensified interference in Laos.

The telegram pointed out that the aforementioned actions seriously violate the Geneva Accords and are directly threatening the security of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam and the entire Indochina.

The telegram sternly protested the aforementioned aggressive conspiracies and actions of American imperialism and demanded that the International Commission for Supervision and Control in Vietnam resolutely oppose and promptly stop American imperialism's actions of interference and aggression against Vietnam.

### 武元甲致电国际监察和监督委员会主席  抗议美国在南越制造紧张局势

1960-12-24
第5版()
专栏：

    武元甲致电国际监察和监督委员会主席
    抗议美国在南越制造紧张局势
    新华社河内电　越南人民军总司令武元甲大将曾于17日致电越南国际监察和监督委员会主席戈帕拉·梅农，抗议美国把第七舰队的约二十艘军舰开到南越土伦海域，并派遣水上飞机，运载多名美国军官在土伦军事基地登陆，以制造印度支那紧张局势的行动。
    电报说，如众周知，美帝国主义正在积极干涉南越；南越当局已经在临时军事分界线集结兵力，派遣武装力量侵入缓冲地区，并在非军事区制造紧张局势。美帝国主义及其东南亚军事侵略集团中的仆从，还正在武装侵略老挝，破坏印度支那和东南亚的和平。
    电报强调指出，在上述局势下，美国第七舰队向南越进发，并派遣飞机进入土伦，是十分危险的行动。这一明目张胆的行动是美国加紧制造越南紧张局势的组成部分，同时它与美国加紧干涉老挝的行为有关系。
    电报指出，上述行动严重地违反了日内瓦协议，并正在直接地威胁越南民主共和国和整个印度支那的安全。
    电报严厉抗议美帝国主义上述的侵略阴谋和行动，并要求越南国际监察和监督委员会，坚决反对和及时制止美帝国主义干涉和侵略越南的行动。
