Title: Irkutsk Oblast Hosts Banquet for Chairman Liu; Chairman Liu Visits Irkutsk Hydroelectric Station and Tours Lake Baikal
Author:
Date: 1960-12-10
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Irkutsk, December 9th: The Irkutsk Oblast Committee of the Communist Party of the Soviet Union and the Irkutsk Oblast Soviet Executive Committee held a banquet today to host Liu Shaoqi, Vice Chairman of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of China, Chairman of the People's Republic of China, and head of the Chinese Party and Government Delegation.

Attending the banquet were Li Jingquan, Lu Dingyi, Yang Shangkun, Liu Ningyi, and Liu Xiao, members of the Chinese Party and Government Delegation.

Brezhnev, Member of the Presidium of the CPSU Central Committee and Chairman of the Presidium of the Supreme Soviet of the Soviet Union, attended the banquet.

Also attending the banquet were: Yelyutin, Minister of Higher and Secondary Specialized Education of the Soviet Union; Tugarinov, Member of the Collegium of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs; Molochkov, Chief of the Protocol Department of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs; and Chervonenko, Soviet Ambassador to China.

Also attending the banquet were: Sedyakin, First Secretary of the Irkutsk Oblast Committee of the CPSU; Grishchenko, Chairman of the Irkutsk Oblast Soviet Executive Committee; Sheshukov, First Secretary of the Irkutsk City Committee of the CPSU; Tomenko, Chairman of the Irkutsk City Soviet Executive Committee, and others.

Sedyakin, First Secretary of the Irkutsk Oblast Committee of the CPSU, spoke at the banquet. He extended a warm welcome to Chairman Liu Shaoqi and the members of the Chinese Party and Government Delegation.

Chairman Liu Shaoqi and Chairman Brezhnev also spoke at the banquet.

Their speeches were warmly applauded.

At the banquet, the hosts and guests frequently raised their glasses to toast the growing development and strengthening of Sino-Soviet friendship. The banquet was held in a warm and friendly atmosphere.

Xinhua News Agency, Irkutsk, December 9th: Liu Shaoqi, Vice Chairman of the Central Committee of the CPC, Chairman of the People's Republic of China, and head of the Chinese Party and Government Delegation, visited the Irkutsk Hydroelectric Station at 10:00 this morning.

Accompanying Chairman Liu Shaoqi on the visit were Brezhnev, Member of the Presidium of the CPSU Central Committee and Chairman of the Presidium of the Supreme Soviet of the Soviet Union, Sedyakin, First Secretary of the Irkutsk Oblast Committee of the Communist Party of the Soviet Union, and others.

Accompanying Chairman Liu Shaoqi on the visit were Li Jingquan, Lu Dingyi, Yang Shangkun, and Liu Ningyi, members of the Chinese Party and Government Delegation.

The national flags of China and the Soviet Union and welcoming slogans were hung on the building and dam of the hydroelectric station. The chief engineer of the power station gave Chairman Liu Shaoqi a detailed introduction to the construction and operation of the hydroelectric station.

Chairman Liu Shaoqi and others visited the building and generator units of the hydroelectric station.

The staff of the hydroelectric station and the masses along the way warmly welcomed Chairman Liu Shaoqi and the members of the Chinese Party and Government Delegation.

Xinhua News Agency, Irkutsk, December 9th: Liu Shaoqi, Vice Chairman of the Central Committee of the CPC, Chairman of the People's Republic of China, and head of the Chinese Party and Government Delegation, toured the famous Lake Baikal, 70 kilometers from Irkutsk, this afternoon, accompanied by Brezhnev, Member of the Presidium of the CPSU Central Committee and Chairman of the Presidium of the Supreme Soviet of the Soviet Union.

Also accompanying Chairman Liu Shaoqi on the tour were: Yelyutin, Minister of Higher and Secondary Specialized Education of the Soviet Union; Tugarinov, Member of the Collegium of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs; Molochkov, Chief of the Protocol Department of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs; Chervonenko, Soviet Ambassador to China; and Sedyakin, First Secretary of the Irkutsk Oblast Committee of the CPSU.

Accompanying Chairman Liu Shaoqi on the tour were Li Jingquan, Lu Dingyi, Yang Shangkun, Liu Ningyi, and Liu Xiao, members of the Chinese Party and Government Delegation.

### 伊尔库次克州设宴招待刘主席  刘主席参观伊尔库次克水电站并游览贝加尔湖

1960-12-10
第1版()
专栏：

    伊尔库次克州设宴招待刘主席
    刘主席参观伊尔库次克水电站并游览贝加尔湖
    新华社伊尔库次克9日电　苏联共产党伊尔库次克州委员会和伊尔库次克州苏维埃执行委员会今天举行宴会，招待中国共产党中央委员会副主席、中华人民共和国主席、中国党政代表团团长刘少奇。
    参加宴会的有中国党政代表团团员李井泉、陆定一、杨尚昆、刘宁一和刘晓。
    苏共中央主席团委员、苏联最高苏维埃主席团主席勃列日涅夫参加了宴会。
    参加宴会的还有：苏联高等和中等专业教育部部长叶留金、外交部部务委员图加林诺夫、外交部礼宾司司长莫洛奇科夫、苏联驻中国大使契尔沃年科。
    参加宴会的还有苏共伊尔库次克州委员会第一书记谢季宁，伊尔库次克州苏维埃执行委员会主席格里琴科，苏共伊尔库次克市委员会第一书记谢舒科夫，伊尔库次克市苏维埃执行委员会主席托缅科等人。
    苏共伊尔库次克州委员会第一书记谢季宁在宴会上讲了话。他对刘少奇主席和中国党政代表团团员们表示了热烈的欢迎。
    刘少奇主席和勃列日涅夫主席也在宴会上讲了话。
    他们的讲话受到热烈的鼓掌欢迎。
    宴会上，宾主们频频举杯祝贺中苏友谊的日益发展和日益加强。宴会是在热烈友好的气氛中进行的。
    新华社伊尔库次克9日电　中共中央副主席、中华人民共和国主席、中国党政代表团团长刘少奇，今天上午十时前往参观伊尔库次克水电站。
    陪同刘少奇主席前往参观的有苏共中央主席团委员、苏联最高苏维埃主席团主席勃列日涅夫、苏联共产党伊尔库次克州委员会第一书记谢季宁等。
    随同刘少奇主席参观的有中国党政代表团团员李井泉、陆定一、杨尚昆、刘宁一。
    在水电站的大楼和水坝上悬挂着中苏两国国旗和欢迎的标语。电站的总工程师向刘少奇主席详细地介绍了水电站的修建和工作情况。
    刘少奇主席等参观了水电站的大楼和发电机组。
    水电站的工作人员和沿途的群众都热烈欢迎刘少奇主席和中国党政代表团团员。
    新华社伊尔库次克9日电　中共中央副主席、中华人民共和国主席、中国党政代表团团长刘少奇，今天下午在苏共中央委员会主席团委员、苏联最高苏维埃主席团主席勃列日涅夫的陪同下，游览了离伊尔库次克七十公里的著名的贝加尔湖。
    陪同刘少奇主席游览的还有：苏联高等和中等专业教育部长叶留金，外交部部务委员图加林诺夫，外交部礼宾司司长莫洛奇科夫，苏联驻中国大使契尔沃年科和苏共伊尔库次克州委会第一书记谢季宁。
    随同刘少奇主席游览的有中国党政代表团团员李井泉、陆定一、杨尚昆、刘宁一、刘晓。
