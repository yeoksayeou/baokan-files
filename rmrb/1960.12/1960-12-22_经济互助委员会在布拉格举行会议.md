Title: Council for Mutual Economic Assistance Holds Meeting in Prague
Author:
Date: 1960-12-22
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, Prague, December 19th: According to TASS, the fourth meeting of the Standing Committee on Economic, Scientific, and Technical Cooperation in Light and Food Industries of the Council for Mutual Economic Assistance (CMEA), composed of the Soviet Union and Eastern European socialist countries, was held in Prague from December 8th to 12th.

    The meeting approved the committee's work plan for 1961 and discussed issues related to the development of meat, oil extraction, sugar refining, and canning industries production up to 1965, improving the processing of textile raw materials, and further expanding the variety of cloth exchange. Corresponding resolutions were passed on the aforementioned issues.

    The committee also discussed the organizational work for the long-term plan of mutual cooperation in developing light and food industries among CMEA member states up to 1980, stipulating that the expansion of production should ensure a rich supply of food, cloth, knitwear, and footwear products based on further technological and process development.

### 经济互助委员会在布拉格举行会议

1960-12-22
第5版()
专栏：

    经济互助委员会在布拉格举行会议
    新华社21日讯　据塔斯社布拉格19日讯：苏联和东欧社会主义国家的经济互助委员会轻工业和食品工业经济、科学和技术合作常设委员会第四次会议，于12月8日至12日在布拉格举行。
    会议通过了委员会1961年工作计划，并且讨论了到1965年为止的发展肉类、榨油、制糖和罐头工业生产的问题，改善纺织原料的加工问题和进一步扩大交流布匹的品种问题，并就上述问题通过了相应的决议。
    委员会还讨论了经济互助委员会成员国到1980年这一时期中互相配合发展轻工业和食品工业的远景计划的组织工作问题，规定以扩大生产来保证在技术和工艺进一步发展的基础上，得到丰富的食品、布匹、针织品和鞋类产品。
