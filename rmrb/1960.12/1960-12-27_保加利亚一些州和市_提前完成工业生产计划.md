Title: Bulgarian States and Cities Ahead of Schedule in Completing Industrial Production Plans
Author: 
Date: 1960-12-27
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, Sofia, 26th - To date, the industrial workers of Sofia City and seven states, including Kyustendil, Gabrovo, Stara Zagora, Sliven, Varna, Plovdiv and Silistra, in Bulgaria, have completed this year's industrial production plans eight to twenty-three days ahead of schedule, respectively.

    Sofia's total industrial output value accounts for approximately one-quarter of the country's total industrial output value. The city's industrial sector employees completed this year's industrial production plan on December 20th. They have also proposed to guarantee that products exceeding the plan will reach 350 million leva (Bulgarian currency) by the end of this year.

    In Ruse, in the north, the country's largest "Dimitrov" agricultural machinery manufacturing plant completed its production plan for this year on December 12th.

    All employees of the Bulgarian Industrial Committee's Electricity Bureau power plants completed this year's plan ten days ahead of schedule, producing 4.345 billion kilowatt-hours of electricity for the country. Employees of the construction and installation departments also completed this year's construction and installation plan twelve days ahead of schedule.

### 保加利亚一些州和市  提前完成工业生产计划

1960-12-27
第5版()
专栏：

    保加利亚一些州和市
    提前完成工业生产计划
    据新华社索非亚26日电　到目前为止，保加利亚已有索非亚市和库斯顿第尔、加布罗沃、斯塔拉扎哥拉、斯利文、瓦尔纳、普罗夫迪夫和西利斯特拉等七个州的工业企业职工分别提前八天到二十三天完成了今年工业生产计划。
    索非亚市的工业总产值约占全国工业总产值的四分之一，全市工业部门职工于12月20日完成今年工业生产计划。他们还提出保证，到今年年底使超计划生产的产品达到三亿五千万列弗（保币名）。
    在北部的鲁西市，全国最大的“季米特洛夫”农业机器制造厂，在12月12日完成了今年生产计划。
    保加利亚工业委员会电力局所属发电站全体职工提前十天完成了今年度计划，为国家生产了四十三亿四千五百万度电力。建筑和安装部门的职工们也提前十二天完成了今年建筑和安装计划。
