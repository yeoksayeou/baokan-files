Title: Liao Chengzhi Hosts Banquet for Japanese Guests
Author:
Date: 1960-12-10
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, December 9th: Liao Chengzhi, Chairman of the Chinese Committee for Afro-Asian Solidarity and Vice-Chairman of the Chinese People's Committee for World Peace, hosted a banquet tonight for all members of the Japanese-China Friendship Association Academic Delegation and the Japanese Gunma Prefecture Peace Delegation, who are currently visiting our country.

Present at the banquet were Ding Xilin, Wu Han, Zhao Puchu, Ou Tangliang, Wu Maosun, Zhao Anbo, Chen Yu, Zheng Senyu, Zhu Ziqi, Pan Chun, Sun Pinghua, Yang Chunsong and other figures from various circles.

Japanese peace activist in Beijing, Kinkazu Saionji, and his wife were also invited to attend the banquet.

### 廖承志欢宴日本贵宾

1960-12-10
第4版()
专栏：

    廖承志欢宴日本贵宾
    新华社9日讯　中国亚非团结委员会主席、中国人民保卫世界和平委员会副主席廖承志，今晚设宴招待正在我国访问的日本中国友好协会学术代表团和日本群马县和平代表团全体成员。
    出席宴会作陪的有丁西林、吴晗、赵朴初、区棠亮、吴茂荪、赵安博、陈宇、郑森禹、朱子奇、潘纯、孙平化、杨春松等各界人士。
    在北京的日本和平人士西园寺公一和他的夫人也应邀出席了宴会。
