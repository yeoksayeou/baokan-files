Title: Premier Zhou Enlai Holds Talks with Prince Sihanouk

Author:
Date: 1960-12-17
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, 16th December: Premier of the State Council Zhou Enlai held talks with the Head of State of Cambodia, Prince Norodom Sihanouk this afternoon.

The talks were conducted in a friendly atmosphere.

Participating in the talks on the Chinese side were: Vice Premiers of the State Council Chen Yi and Li Xiannian, Minister of Foreign Trade Ye Jizhuang, Vice Minister Li Qiang, Deputy Director of the State Planning Commission Gu Zhuoxin, Vice Minister of Foreign Affairs Ji Pengfei, and Chinese Ambassador to Cambodia Wang Youping, among others.

Participating in the talks on the Cambodian side were: Vice-Chairman of the Cambodian Supreme Council of the Throne Penn Nouth, Prime Minister Pho Proeung, First Deputy Prime Minister and Minister of Planning Nhiek Tioulong, Director-General of the Planning Department Song Yun Xi, Deputy Secretary-General of the Supreme Council of the Throne, Phoung Mach Gan, and Cambodian Ambassador to China Leng Nget. (Attached image)

Below: Premier Zhou Enlai and Prince Sihanouk in talks. From the left: Li Xiannian, Chen Yi, Zhou Enlai, Norodom Sihanouk, Penn Nouth, Pho Proeung, Nhiek Tioulong.
Xinhua News Agency reporter Liu Qingrui photographed.
