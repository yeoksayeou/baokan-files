Title: Lu Xun Monument Completed in Sendai, Japan
Author:
Date: 1960-12-07
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, Tokyo, December 5th: According to a report in today's *Asahi Shimbun*, the Lu Xun monument built in Aoba Castle, Sendai, Japan, was completed on the 4th. The monument is five meters high and weighs ten tons. It is engraved with a portrait of Lu Xun and an inscription by Guo Moruo.

　　The monument was built by the Lu Xun Monument Establishment Executive Committee. The chairman of this committee is Dr. Kumagai Taizō, a famous Japanese scholar and former president of Tohoku University. The predecessor of Tohoku University was Sendai Medical College, where Lu Xun studied when he was young.

　　According to reports, the committee plans to hold an unveiling ceremony for the monument in the spring of next year.

### 日本仙台市建成鲁迅纪念碑

1960-12-07
第6版()
专栏：

    日本仙台市建成鲁迅纪念碑
    新华社5日讯　东京消息：据《朝日新闻》今天报道，建立在日本仙台市青叶城的鲁迅纪念碑已在4日建成。纪念碑高五米，重十吨，上面雕有鲁迅遗像，并刻有郭沫若题的碑文。
    这座纪念碑是由鲁迅纪念碑建立实行委员会筹建的。这个委员会的委员长是日本著名学者、前东北大学校长熊谷岱藏博士。东北大学的前身是仙台医学专门学校，鲁迅年轻时，曾在那里读过书。
    据报道，这个委员会打算在明年春季举行纪念碑的揭幕典礼。
