Title: At the Invitation of the CPC Central Committee, General Secretary of the Communist Party of Canada, Tim Buck, Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1960-12-14
Page: 1

    Xinhua News Agency, December 13th: Tim Buck, General Secretary of the Communist Party of Canada, and his wife arrived in Beijing by plane this afternoon from Moscow at the invitation of the CPC Central Committee for a visit to our country.

    Welcoming them at the airport were Peng Zhen, member of the Political Bureau of the CPC Central Committee; Wu Xiuquan, member of the Central Committee; Zhang Xiangshan and other responsible staff members of the CPC Central Committee organs. (Attached picture)

    Left picture: Tim Buck, General Secretary of the Communist Party of Canada, shakes hands warmly with Peng Zhen, member of the Political Bureau of the CPC Central Committee, who came to welcome him at Beijing Airport. (Photo by Li Changyong, Xinhua News Agency)

### 应中共中央邀请来我国访问  加共总书记蒂姆·布克到京

1960-12-14
第1版()
专栏：

    应中共中央邀请来我国访问
    加共总书记蒂姆·布克到京
    新华社13日讯　应中共中央邀请来我国访问的加拿大共产党总书记蒂姆·布克和夫人，今天下午乘飞机从莫斯科到达北京。
    到机场欢迎的有中共中央政治局委员彭真，中央委员伍修权，中共中央机关负责工作人员张香山等。（附图片）
    左图：加拿大共产党总书记蒂姆·布克在北京机场和前去欢迎的中共中央政治局委员彭真亲切握手　　　　　　　　　　　新华社记者　李长永摄
