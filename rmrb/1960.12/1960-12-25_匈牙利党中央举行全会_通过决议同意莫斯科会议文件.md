Title: Hungarian Party Central Committee Holds Plenary Session, Adopts Resolution Agreeing to Moscow Conference Documents
Author:
Date: 1960-12-25
Page: 6
Xinhua News Agency, Budapest, December 23rd: According to a report in the "People's Freedom" on the 22nd, the Central Committee of the Hungarian Socialist Workers' Party held a plenary session on December 21st.  János Kádár, First Secretary of the Central Committee of the Hungarian Socialist Workers' Party, delivered a report at the meeting on the delegation of the Socialist Workers' Party's participation in the conference of representatives of Communist and Workers' Parties held in Moscow in November.  The meeting adopted a resolution expressing its complete agreement with the statement and the Appeal to the Peoples of the World adopted at the Moscow conference.

The resolution said that the statement, after a comprehensive, profound, and constructive discussion by the representatives of eighty-one fraternal parties and its unanimous adoption, demonstrates the consolidation of ideological unity within the world communist movement and the determination to wage a completely consistent struggle against imperialism and for peace, for the freedom, democracy, and socialism of all peoples.  The resolution called on all organizations of the Socialist Workers' Party and all party members to earnestly study the statement and the Appeal to the Peoples of the World, to apply them to the work of the party, and to do everything possible to make the broad working class understand this statement and the Appeal to the Peoples of the World.

### 匈牙利党中央举行全会  通过决议同意莫斯科会议文件

1960-12-25
第6版()
专栏：

    匈牙利党中央举行全会
    通过决议同意莫斯科会议文件
    新华社布达佩斯23日电　据《人民自由报》22日报道：匈牙利社会主义工人党中央委员会在12月21日举行全体会议。匈牙利社会主义工人党中央委员会第一书记卡达尔在会上作了关于社会主义工人党代表团参加11月在莫斯科举行的各国共产党和工人党代表会议的报告。会议通过了决议，表示完全同意莫斯科会议所通过的声明和告世界人民书。
    决议说，声明在经过八十一个兄弟党的代表的全面、深刻的建设性的讨论之后，获得一致通过，这表明世界共产主义运动思想上的团结的巩固和完全一致地进行反对帝国主义和争取和平，争取各国人民的自由、民主、社会主义的斗争的决心。决议号召社会主义工人党的一切组织和所有党员认真学习声明和告世界人民书，把它们运用到党的工作中去，并尽一切可能使广大的劳动者阶层了解这个声明和告世界人民书。
