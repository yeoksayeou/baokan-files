Title: US Unemployment Surges: Preliminary Figures for November Exceed 4.2 Million
Author:
Date: 1960-12-02
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, New York, December 1st—According to a report by the *Wall Street Journal*'s Washington correspondent, preliminary statistics indicate that the number of unemployed in the United States exceeded 4.2 million in November, possibly reaching 4.5 million. This is the highest November unemployment figure in the last fifteen years. In October, the number of unemployed in the United States was 3,579,000.

Weekly reports from the Department of Labor show that only 1,962,000 of the unemployed are receiving unemployment benefits.

### 美国失业人数激增  据初步统计11月份已超过420万

1960-12-02
第6版()
专栏：

    美国失业人数激增
    据初步统计11月份已超过420万
    新华社1日讯　纽约消息：据《华尔街日报》驻华盛顿记者报道说，根据初步统计材料，11月份美国失业人数超过了四百二十万人，可能达到四百五十万人。这是最近十五年中的11月份失业人数的最高数字。10月份美国失业人数为三百五十七万九千人。
    从劳工部的每周报告中可以看出，失业人数中只有一百九十六万二千人领到失业救济金。
