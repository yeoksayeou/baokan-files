Title: My Afro-Asian Solidarity Committee and the China-Africa Friendship Association Send Telegrams to Congratulate China and Somalia on the Establishment of Diplomatic Relations
Author:
Date: 1960-12-18
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, December 17th – The Chinese Afro-Asian Solidarity Committee today sent a telegram to His Excellency Abdi Rashid Ali Shermarke, Prime Minister of the Somali Republic, congratulating China and Somalia on the establishment of diplomatic relations.

The telegram said: "On the occasion of the establishment of diplomatic relations between China and Somalia, the Chinese Afro-Asian Solidarity Committee extends its heartfelt congratulations and respect to Your Excellency, and through Your Excellency, to the Government of the Somali Republic and all the people of Somalia."

The telegram said: "The Chinese people have always sympathized with and supported the just struggle of the Somali people against imperialism, colonialism, and for independence and freedom. We firmly believe that the establishment of diplomatic relations between China and Somalia will further promote the friendship between our two peoples and help strengthen Afro-Asian solidarity and consolidate the noble cause of world peace. We wish the Somali Republic prosperity and strength, and its people happiness."

The Chinese Afro-Asian Solidarity Committee wished in the telegram that the friendship between the Chinese and Somali people will grow and be consolidated day by day.

Xinhua News Agency, December 17th – The Chinese Afro-Asian Solidarity Committee today sent a telegram to Mr. Mohamed Haji Hussein, Chairman of the Greater Somalia League, congratulating China and Somalia on the establishment of diplomatic relations.

The telegram said, "The establishment of diplomatic relations between China and Somalia is the result of the long-term efforts of the Chinese and Somali people, and another victory for the Afro-Asian people in strengthening unity and opposing imperialism. We firmly believe that the friendly cooperation and mutual support between the Chinese and Somali people in the common cause of opposing imperialism and defending peace will be further strengthened and consolidated with the establishment of diplomatic relations between the two countries."

The telegram wished the hardworking and brave Somali people continuous new achievements in the noble cause of opposing imperialist aggression, consolidating national independence, and developing the national economy.

Xinhua News Agency, December 17th – Liu Changsheng, President of the Chinese-African People's Friendship Association, today sent a telegram to His Excellency Abdi Rashid Ali Shermarke, Prime Minister of the Somali Republic, congratulating China and Somalia on the establishment of diplomatic relations.

The telegram said, "On the occasion of the decision of the governments of China and Somalia to establish diplomatic relations, I would like to extend my warmest congratulations to Your Excellency and the people of the Somali Republic on behalf of the Chinese-African People's Friendship Association. I wish the precious friendship established between the peoples of China and Somalia in the struggle against imperialism and colonialism and in defense of world peace will be continuously consolidated and developed!"

### 我亚非团结委员会和中非友协致电  祝贺中国和索马里建立外交关系

1960-12-18
第6版()
专栏：

    我亚非团结委员会和中非友协致电
    祝贺中国和索马里建立外交关系
    新华社17日讯　中国亚非团结委员会今天致电索马里共和国总理阿卜迪·拉希德·阿里·舍马克阁下，祝贺中索两国建立外交关系。
    电报说：“值此中国和索马里两国建立外交关系之际，中国亚非团结委员会谨向阁下并通过阁下向索马里共和国政府及全体索马里人民表示衷心的祝贺和敬意。”
    电报说：“中国人民一贯同情和支持索马里人民反对帝国主义、殖民主义和争取独立自由的正义斗争。我们深信，中索两国外交关系的建立，将进一步促进我们两国人民的友谊，并有助于加强亚非团结和巩固世界和平的崇高事业。祝索马里共和国繁荣昌盛，人民幸福。”
    中国亚非团结委员会在电报中祝中索两国人民的友谊日益发展和巩固。
    新华社17日讯　中国亚非团结委员会今天打电报给大索马里联盟主席穆罕默德·哈吉·侯赛因先生，祝贺中国和索马里两国建立外交关系。
    电报说，“中国和索马里两国建立外交关系，是中索人民长期努力的结果，是亚非人民加强团结、反对帝国主义斗争的又一次胜利。我们深信，中索两国人民在反对帝国主义和保卫和平的共同事业中友好合作和互相支持，将随着两国建立外交关系进一步加强和巩固。”
    电报祝勤劳勇敢的索马里人民在反对帝国主义侵略、巩固民族独立和发展民族经济的崇高事业中，不断取得新的成就。
    新华社17日讯　中国非洲人民友好协会会长刘长胜今天致电索马里共和国总理阿卜迪·拉希德·阿里·舍马克阁下，祝贺中索两国建立外交关系。
    电报说，“值此中国索马里两国政府决定建立外交关系的时候，我谨代表中国非洲人民友好协会向阁下和索马里共和国人民表示最热烈的祝贺。祝中、索两国人民在反对帝国主义和殖民主义、保卫世界和平的斗争中所建立起来的珍贵友谊不断巩固和发展！”
