Title: United Arab Republic Egyptian Region Peace Committee Issues Statement: US Aggression Policy Threatens Peace
Author:
Date: 1960-12-08
Page: 7

Xinhua News Agency, Cairo, December 5th: The Peace Committee of the Egyptian Region of the United Arab Republic recently issued a statement here, condemning the imperialism headed by the United States for establishing military bases and military alliances around the world, threatening world peace, and undermining the national independence movements in Asia, Africa, and Latin America.

The statement was issued in response to the call by the Secretariat of the Asian-African People's Solidarity Organization to launch a bi-weekly struggle against military bases and aggressive military treaties.

The statement said that after World War II, the United States plotted the conspiracy of establishing aggressive military alliances, established the North Atlantic Treaty Organization, the Southeast Asia Treaty Organization, and the Baghdad Treaty Organization, forming a network of aggressive military alliances.

The statement said that the United States sent its fleet to the Mediterranean, the Taiwan Strait, and the Caribbean Sea, thereby threatening peace and national movements in these regions.

The statement pointed out that in Algeria, imperialism is using NATO weapons to massacre the Algerian people. In the Congo, thanks to the help of the United Nations, the military bases there have been used to sow discord within the Congolese national movement and hinder its development.

The statement concluded by saying that the struggle of the people of Asia, Africa, and Latin America against aggressive military bases and military alliances is their struggle for independence and world peace.

### 阿联埃及地区和平委员会发表声明  美国侵略政策威胁和平

1960-12-08
第7版()
专栏：

    阿联埃及地区和平委员会发表声明
    美国侵略政策威胁和平
    新华社开罗5日电　阿联埃及地区和平委员会最近在这里发表声明，谴责以美国为首的帝国主义在世界各地建立军事基地和军事联盟，威胁世界和平，破坏亚洲、非洲和拉丁美洲的民族独立运动。
    这个声明是为了响应亚非人民团结组织书记处关于开展反对军事基地和侵略性军事条约的双周斗争的号召而发表的。
    声明说，第二次世界大战以后，美国策划建立侵略性军事联盟的阴谋，建立了北大西洋公约组织、东南亚条约组织和巴格达条约组织，构成一个侵略性军事联盟网。
    声明说，美国把它的舰队派遣到地中海、台湾海峡和加勒比海，以此来威胁这些地区的和平和民族运动。
    声明指出，在阿尔及利亚，帝国主义正在以北大西洋公约组织的武器和屠杀阿尔及利亚人民。在刚果，由于联合国的帮助，那里的军事基地已被用来在刚果民族运动内部散播不和，阻挠它的发展。
    声明最后说，亚洲、非洲和拉丁美洲人民反对侵略性军事基地和军事联盟的斗争就是他们为争取独立和争取世界和平的斗争。
