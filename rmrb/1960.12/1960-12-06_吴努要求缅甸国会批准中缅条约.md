Title: U Nu requests Burmese Parliament to approve Sino-Burmese treaty
Author:
Date: 1960-12-06
Page: 6
Xinhua News Agency, Rangoon, December 5th. Burmese Prime Minister U Nu today proposed a resolution at the opening session of the Burmese Chamber of Deputies requesting approval of the Sino-Burmese Boundary Treaty signed by the Chinese and Burmese governments in Beijing on October 1st.
	Prime Minister U Nu delivered a lengthy speech when presenting the resolution, requesting Parliament to agree to ratify the treaty.

### 吴努要求缅甸国会批准中缅条约

1960-12-06
第6版()
专栏：

    吴努要求缅甸国会批准中缅条约
    新华社仰光5日电　缅甸总理吴努今天在缅甸国会代表院的开幕会议上提出了一项关于要求批准中国和缅甸政府于10月1日在北京签订的中缅边界条约的决议。　吴努总理在提出这项决议时，发表了要求国会同意批准这项条约的长篇讲话。
