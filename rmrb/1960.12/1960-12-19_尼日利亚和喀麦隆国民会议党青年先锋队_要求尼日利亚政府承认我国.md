Title: Nigerian and Cameroonian National Council Youth Vanguard Demands Nigerian Government Recognize Our Country
Author:
Date: 1960-12-19
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, Accra, December 16th: Lagos dispatch: The Nigerian and Cameroonian National Council Youth Vanguard, at its inaugural congress which concluded on the 11th, passed a resolution demanding the immediate release of Patrice Lumumba, the Prime Minister of the legitimate government of the Congo. The resolution also demanded that the Nigerian Federal Government recognize the People's Republic of China and establish diplomatic relations with it.

The fifteen-point resolution passed by the congress also demanded that the Nigerian Federal Government recognize the Algerian Provisional Government headed by Abbas Ferhat. The Nigerian and Cameroonian National Council Youth Vanguard is a newly established organization after the merger of the Zikist National Vanguard and the Nigerian and Cameroonian National Council Youth Association.

### 尼日利亚和喀麦隆国民会议党青年先锋队  要求尼日利亚政府承认我国

1960-12-19
第5版()
专栏：

    尼日利亚和喀麦隆国民会议党青年先锋队
    要求尼日利亚政府承认我国
    新华社阿克拉16日电　拉各斯消息：尼日利亚和喀麦隆国民会议党青年先锋队在11日闭幕的成立大会上通过决议，要求立即释放刚果合法政府总理卢蒙巴。决议还要求尼日利亚联邦政府承认中华人民共和国并同它建立外交关系。
    大会通过的十五点决议还要求尼日利亚联邦政府承认以阿巴斯·费尔哈特为首的阿尔及利亚临时政府。尼日利亚和喀麦隆国民会议党青年先锋队是在齐克国民先锋与尼日利亚和喀麦隆国民会议党青年协会合并后新成立的组织。
