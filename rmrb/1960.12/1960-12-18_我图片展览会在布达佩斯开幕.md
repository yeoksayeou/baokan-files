Title: Chinese Photo Exhibition Opens in Budapest
Author:
Date: 1960-12-18
Page: 6
Xinhua News Agency, Budapest. A photo exhibition entitled "China Completes the Second Year of its Five-Year Plan for National Economy" opened on the 9th at the Budapest Art Museum.
    This exhibition was sponsored by the Hungarian Bureau for Foreign Cultural Relations and the Budapest Art Museum.
    Among those attending the opening ceremony were Hungarian Deputy Minister of Foreign Affairs Sarka, Secretary-General of the Bureau of Cultural Relations Pataki János, people from all walks of life, and Chinese Ambassador to Hungary Hao Deqing.

### 我图片展览会在布达佩斯开幕

1960-12-18
第6版()
专栏：

    我图片展览会在布达佩斯开幕
    新华社布达佩斯电　“中华人民共和国两年完成国民经济五年计划”的图片展览会9日在布达佩斯艺术馆开幕。
    这次展览会是由匈牙利对外文化联络局和布达佩斯艺术馆主办的。
    参加开幕式的有匈牙利外交部副部长萨尔卡，文联局秘书长帕塔基·亚诺什，社会各界人士和中国驻匈牙利大使郝德青等。
