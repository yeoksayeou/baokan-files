Title: Guangdong Acrobatic Troupe Returns to Guangzhou from Macau
Author:
Date: 1960-12-22
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency, Guangzhou, 21st. The Guangdong Acrobatic Troupe returned to Guangzhou on the 19th after performing in Macau for over ten days. The Guangdong Acrobatic Troupe arrived in Macau on the 3rd and performed fourteen shows in total, with an audience of over 17,000 people. Their performances were warmly welcomed by compatriots in Macau.

### 广东曲艺杂技团由澳门回广州

1960-12-22
第4版()
专栏：

    广东曲艺杂技团由澳门回广州
    新华社广州21日电　广东曲艺杂技团在澳门演出十多天后，已经在19日回到广州。广东曲艺杂技团在3日到澳门后，一共演出了十四场，观众达一万七千多人次。他们的演出受到澳门同胞的热烈欢迎。
