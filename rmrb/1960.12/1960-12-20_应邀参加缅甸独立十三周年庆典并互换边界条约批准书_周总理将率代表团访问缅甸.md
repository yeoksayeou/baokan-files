Title: Premier Zhou to Lead Delegation to Visit Burma to Attend 13th Anniversary Celebration of Burmese Independence and Exchange Instruments of Ratification of Boundary Treaty

Author:

Date: 1960-12-20
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, 19th - At the invitation of Burmese Prime Minister U Nu, Premier of the State Council of the People's Republic of China, Zhou Enlai, will lead a goodwill delegation of the People's Republic of China to the Union of Burma to participate in the celebrations of the 13th anniversary of the independence of the Union of Burma, exchange instruments of ratification of the Sino-Burmese Boundary Treaty, and conduct a friendly visit. State leaders accompanying him on the friendly visit to Burma also include Vice Premier of the State Council and Minister of Foreign Affairs Chen Yi, and Vice Premier of the State Council and Chief of General Staff of the Chinese People's Liberation Army Luo Ruiqing.

Also travelling to Burma for a friendly visit are Minister of Light Industry Li Zhuchen, Vice Minister of Foreign Affairs Geng Biao, Vice Minister of Foreign Trade Lei Renmin, Deputy Director of the Commission for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries Zhang Zhixiang, Deputy Chief of General Staff of the Chinese People's Liberation Army Zhang Aiping, Deputy Director of the Sports Commission Cai Tingkai, Vice Minister of Textile Industry Rong Yiren, Vice Governor of Yunnan Province Liu Minghui, Director of the Premier's Office of the State Council Tong Xiaopeng, Chief Representative of the Chinese side of the Sino-Burmese Joint Boundary Committee Yao Zhongming, President of the Buddhist Association of China Xirao Gyatso, and others.

The goodwill delegation to Burma includes the Chinese government delegation headed by Premier Zhou Enlai, as well as the Chinese military delegation, the Chinese cultural and art delegation, the Chinese side delegation of the Sino-Burmese Joint Boundary Committee, the Yunnan Provincial delegation of China, the Chinese Buddhist delegation, the Chinese film delegation, the Chinese journalists delegation, and the China sports delegation, with a total of more than 400 members. The cultural and art delegation also includes a performance team composed of outstanding actors in Peking Opera, dance, music, acrobatics, etc., who will conduct visits and performances in Burma; the sports delegation consisting of football players and Chinese martial arts athletes will conduct friendly matches and performances in Burma.

### 应邀参加缅甸独立十三周年庆典并互换边界条约批准书  周总理将率代表团访问缅甸

1960-12-20
第1版()
专栏：

    应邀参加缅甸独立十三周年庆典并互换边界条约批准书
    周总理将率代表团访问缅甸
    新华社19日讯　应缅甸联邦总理吴努的邀请，中华人民共和国国务院总理周恩来将率领中华人民共和国访问缅甸联邦友好代表团前往缅甸，参加缅甸联邦独立十三周年的庆祝典礼并在缅甸互换中缅边界条约批准书、进行友好访问。前往缅甸进行友好访问的国家领导人还有国务院副总理兼外交部长陈毅和国务院副总理兼中国人民解放军总参谋长罗瑞卿。
    前往缅甸进行友好访问的还有轻工业部部长李烛尘、外交部副部长耿飚、对外贸易部副部长雷任民、对外文化联络委员会副主任张致祥、中国人民解放军副总参谋长张爱萍、体育运动委员会副主任蔡廷锴、纺织工业部副部长荣毅仁、云南省副省长刘明辉、国务院总理办公室主任童小鹏、中缅边界联合委员会中方首席代表姚仲明、中国佛教协会会长喜饶嘉错等。
    访缅友好代表团包括以周恩来总理为首的中国政府代表团，以及中国军事代表团、中国文化艺术代表团、中缅边界联合委员会中方代表团、中国云南省代表团、中国佛教代表团、中国电影代表团、中国新闻工作者代表团和中国体育代表团，全体成员四百多人。在文化艺术代表团中还包括由京剧、舞蹈、音乐、杂技等方面的优秀演员组成的演出队，将在缅甸进行访问演出；由足球运动员和中国武术运动员组成的体育代表团将在缅甸进行友谊比赛和表演。
