Title: Cuban Government Delegation Leaves Hanoi After Concluding Friendly Visit to Vietnam
Author:
Date: 1960-12-05
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, Hanoi, December 4th: The delegation of the Revolutionary Government of the Republic of Cuba, led by Hector Rodriguez Llompart, Administrative Vice Minister of the Cuban Ministry of Foreign Affairs, left Hanoi by plane this morning after concluding its visit to the Democratic Republic of Vietnam.

Those who came to the airport to see them off included Nguyen Khang, Minister of the Premier's Office of the Vietnamese Government, Phan Anh, Minister of Foreign Trade, Le Viet Luong, Governor of the State Bank, Hoang Van Tien, Deputy Minister of Foreign Affairs, and diplomatic envoys of various countries to Vietnam. Llompart, accompanied by Nguyen Khang, reviewed the guard of honor.

### 结束在越南的友好访问  古巴政府代表团离开河内

1960-12-05
第5版()
专栏：

    结束在越南的友好访问
    古巴政府代表团离开河内
    据新华社河内4日电　由古巴外交部行政副部长埃克托·罗德里格斯·略姆帕尔特率领的古巴共和国革命政府代表团，结束了对越南民主共和国的访问后，今天早上乘飞机离开河内。
    到机场欢送的有越南政府总理府部长阮康、对外贸易部部长潘英、国家银行行长黎曰亮、外交部副部长黄文进，以及各国驻越南外交使节等。略姆帕尔特在阮康的陪同下检阅了仪仗队。
