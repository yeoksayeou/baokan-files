Title: China-Cambodia Friendship Association and All-China Women's Federation Hold Reception to Welcome Prince Sihanouk and His Wife; Chairman Liu Shaoqi, Vice Chairman Dong Biwu, Chairman Zhu De, and Premier Zhou Enlai Attend Reception
Author:
Date: 1960-12-20
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, December 19th: The China-Cambodia Friendship Association and the All-China Women's Federation of the People's Republic of China held a reception this evening in the Hunan Hall of the Great Hall of the People to warmly welcome Cambodian Head of State Prince Norodom Sihanouk and his wife, and to celebrate the establishment of the China-Cambodia Friendship Association.

Chairman Liu Shaoqi, Vice Chairman Dong Biwu of the People's Republic of China, Chairman Zhu De of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress, and Premier Zhou Enlai of the State Council attended the reception.

Prince Penn Nouth, Vice-Chairman of the High Council of the Throne of Cambodia, Prime Minister Pho Proeung, and First Deputy Prime Minister Nhiek Tioulong, who accompanied Prince Sihanouk on his visit, were invited to attend the reception.

Prince Sihanouk's princesses, princes, and other Cambodian dignitaries, as well as Cambodian Ambassador to China Leng Ngeth, were also invited to attend the reception.

Qi Yanming, President of the China-Cambodia Friendship Association, toasted at the reception. He first extended a warm welcome to Prince Sihanouk and his wife, as well as other Cambodian dignitaries.

Qi Yanming said that the visit of His Royal Highness Prince Sihanouk to our country, the signing of the China-Cambodia Treaty of Friendship and Non-Aggression, and the establishment of the China-Cambodia Friendship Association are three major joyous events in the recent relations between China and Cambodia. We celebrate with unparalleled joy the significant development of the friendly relations between China and Cambodia, which marks a new historical stage in the traditional friendship between our two peoples.

Qi Yanming said that the friendly and cooperative relations between China and Cambodia, under the guidance of the Five Principles of Peaceful Coexistence and the spirit of the Bandung Conference, have been continuously consolidated and developed, becoming a good example of peaceful coexistence between countries with different social systems. This not only conforms to the interests of the peoples of China and Cambodia, but also helps to safeguard peace in Asia and the world.

Xu Guangping, Vice-President of the All-China Women's Federation, on behalf of the All-China Women's Federation and all Chinese women, extended a warm welcome to Prince Sihanouk and his wife, as well as the Cambodian dignitaries, and expressed the utmost respect for their contributions to strengthening the friendly relations between China and Cambodia. She said that in 1958, a Chinese women's delegation headed by the wife of Vice Premier Chen Yi was invited to visit Cambodia and received a warm reception. She expressed deep gratitude for this.

Prince Sihanouk spoke at the reception. He expressed his gratitude for the warm welcome from the China-Cambodia Friendship Association and the All-China Women's Federation.

Prince Sihanouk said that today is a day of historical significance, marking the development of fraternal friendship between Cambodia and China. He said that the establishment of the China-Cambodia Friendship Association demonstrates the deep friendship of the Chinese people towards the Cambodian people.

He pointed out that the China-Cambodia Friendship Association, like a torch, symbolizes the continuous development of the friendship between the Cambodian and Chinese people. It will continue to shine, illuminating the continuous development of our two countries in the political, economic, cultural, and social fields. He said that after returning to Cambodia, we will also establish the Cambodia-China Friendship Association to develop the friendship between the two peoples and benefit the peoples of Cambodia and China.

Prince Sihanouk said: Tomorrow we will leave you, but we will always work with you to create a peaceful and happy life.

Prince Sihanouk invited the China-Cambodia Friendship Association to send a delegation to visit Cambodia. He said that your delegation will see the Cambodian people's love for China and their admiration for China's achievements, as well as the great impact of People's China on Cambodia.

Also attending the reception were: Vice-Chairmen of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress Guo Moruo, Huang Yanpei, Peng Zhen, Panchen Erdeni Qoigyijabu, Vice-Premiers of the State Council Chen Yi, Ulanhu, Vice-Chairmen of the National Committee of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference Li Siguang, Burhan, Vice-Chairmen of the National Defense Council Zhang Zhizhong, Fu Zuoyi, President of the Supreme People's Court Xie Juezai, Procurator-General of the Supreme People's Procuratorate Zhang Dingcheng, Vice-Ministers of Foreign Affairs Zhang Hanfu, Ji Pengfei, Chinese Ambassador to Cambodia Wang Youping, Vice-Presidents of the China-Cambodia Friendship Association Wang Kunlun, Zhang Qian, Zhang Tianmin, Vice-Presidents of the All-China Women's Federation Yang Zhihua, Liu Qingyang, and people from all walks of life in the capital. ### 中柬友协和全国妇联举行酒会  欢迎西哈努克亲王和夫人  刘少奇主席、董必武副主席、朱德委员长、周恩来总理出席酒会

1960-12-20
第5版()
专栏：

    中柬友协和全国妇联举行酒会
    欢迎西哈努克亲王和夫人
    刘少奇主席、董必武副主席、朱德委员长、周恩来总理出席酒会
    新华社19日讯　中国柬埔寨友好协会和中华人民共和国全国妇女联合会，今天傍晚在人民大会堂湖南馆举行酒会，热烈欢迎柬埔寨国家元首诺罗敦·西哈努克亲王和夫人，庆祝中国柬埔寨友好协会成立。
    中华人民共和国主席刘少奇、副主席董必武，全国人民代表大会常务委员会委员长朱德，国务院总理周恩来出席了酒会。
    随同西哈努克亲王来访的柬埔寨王位最高委员会副主席宾努，首相福·波伦，第一副首相?·刁隆应邀出席了酒会。
    西哈努克亲王的公主、王子以及其他柬埔寨的贵宾，柬埔寨驻中国大使兰·?特，也应邀出席了酒会。
    中柬友好协会会长齐燕铭在酒会上祝酒。他首先对西哈努克亲王和夫人以及其他柬埔寨贵宾表示热烈欢迎。
    齐燕铭说，西哈努克亲王殿下前来我国访问，中柬友好和互不侵犯条约的签订和中柬友好协会成立，是最近中柬两国关系上的三件大喜事。我们以无比愉快的心情，庆祝中柬友好关系的重大的发展，它标志着我们两国人民的传统友谊进入了一个新的历史阶段。
    齐燕铭说，中柬两国的友好合作关系在和平共处五项原则和万隆会议精神的指导下，不断获得巩固和发展，成为不同社会制度国家和平共处的良好榜样，这不仅符合中柬两国人民的利益，而且有助于保卫亚洲和世界的和平。
    全国妇联副主席许广平代表全国妇联和全中国妇女，向西哈努克亲王和夫人以及柬埔寨贵宾们表示热烈的欢迎，并对他们在加强中柬两国友好关系方面所作的贡献，表示最大的敬意。她说，1958年，以陈毅副总理夫人为首的中国妇女代表团应邀访问了柬埔寨，受到了热情的款待。对此，她表示深切的感谢。
    西哈努克亲王在酒会上讲话。他对中柬友协和全国妇联盛情的热烈欢迎表示感谢。
    西哈努克亲王说，今天是一个具有历史意义的日子，它标志着柬中两国之间兄弟般的友谊的发展。他说，中柬友好协会的成立，表明了中国人民对柬埔寨人民的深厚友谊。
    他指出，中柬友好协会像火炬一样象征着柬中两国人民的友谊不断发展，它将不断发光，照耀着我们两国的政治、经济、文化和社会各方面的不断发展。他说，我们回国后也将成立柬中友好协会，发展两国人民的友谊，为柬中两国人民造福。
    西哈努克亲王说：明天我们就要离开你们了，但是我们将永远和你们一起创造和平幸福的生活。
    西哈努克亲王邀请中柬友好协会派代表团到柬埔寨访问。他说，你们的代表团将看到柬埔寨人民对中国的热爱和对中国成就的钦佩，以及人民中国在柬埔寨所产生的巨大影响。
    出席酒会的还有：全国人民代表大会常务委员会副委员长郭沫若、黄炎培、彭真、班禅额尔德尼·却吉坚赞，国务院副总理陈毅、乌兰夫，政协全国委员会副主席李四光、包尔汉，国防委员会副主席张治中、傅作义，最高人民法院院长谢觉哉，最高人民检察院检察长张鼎丞，外交部副部长章汉夫、姬鹏飞，中国驻柬埔寨大使王幼平，中柬友协副会长王昆仑、张茜、张天民，全国妇联副主席杨之华、刘清扬以及首都各界人士。（附图片）
