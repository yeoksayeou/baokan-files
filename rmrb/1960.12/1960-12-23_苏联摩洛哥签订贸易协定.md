Title: Soviet Union and Morocco Sign Trade Agreement
Author:
Date: 1960-12-23
Page: 6
Xinhua News Agency, Rabat - The Soviet Union and Morocco signed a trade agreement here on the evening of the 14th for the years 1960-1961. The agreement stipulates a trade volume of approximately 3.5 billion Moroccan francs for each party. This represents a significant increase compared to the trade agreement signed between the two countries in 1959.

According to this agreement, the Soviet Union will export petroleum products, machine tools, equipment, vehicles, automobiles, textiles, and timber to Morocco. It will import cork, citrus fruits, wool, canned sardines, and sheepskin from Morocco.

### 苏联摩洛哥签订贸易协定

1960-12-23
第6版()
专栏：

    苏联摩洛哥签订贸易协定
    新华社拉巴特电苏联和摩洛哥14日晚在这里签订了1960—1961年度贸易协定。协定规定每方贸易额为三十五亿左右摩洛哥法郎。同1959年两国签订的贸易协定相比有很大的增长。
    根据这项协定，苏联将向摩洛哥输出石油产品、工作母机、装备、车辆、汽车、纺织品以及木材等。并从摩洛哥输入软木、桔类、羊毛、沙丁鱼罐头以及羊皮等。
