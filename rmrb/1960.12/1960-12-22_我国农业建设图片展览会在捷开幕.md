Title: Chinese Agricultural Construction Photo Exhibition Opens in Czechoslovakia
Author:
Date: 1960-12-22
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, Prague, December 20th: The "People's Republic of China Agricultural Construction Photo Exhibition," jointly sponsored by the Czechoslovak Ministry of Education and Culture and the Prague National Museum, opened in Prague today.

At the opening ceremony, the First Deputy Minister of the Czechoslovak Ministry of Education and Culture, Mr. Krzystek, and the Cultural Counselor of the Chinese Embassy in Czechoslovakia, Mr. Lu Yaowu, delivered speeches respectively.

The photos on display reflect the achievements of China's agricultural production and the scene of the Great Leap Forward in the Chinese countryside, as well as the commune members' efforts in boosting production, developing water conservancy on a large scale, implementing the "Eight-Point Charter for Agriculture," engaging in agricultural scientific research, and the support from all walks of life for agriculture.

This photo exhibition is held in accordance with the 1960 implementation plan of the Sino-Czech Cultural Cooperation Agreement.

### 我国农业建设图片展览会在捷开幕

1960-12-22
第5版()
专栏：

    我国农业建设图片展览会在捷开幕
    据新华社布拉格20日电　由捷克斯洛伐克教育和文化部和布拉格民族博物馆主办的中华人民共和国农业建设图片展览会，今天在布拉格开幕。
    在开幕仪式上，捷克斯洛伐克教育和文化部第一副部长克日斯特克和中国驻捷克斯洛伐克大使馆文化参赞卢耀武分别讲了话。
    这次展出的图片反映了中国农业生产的成就和中国农村大跃进的景象，以及人民公社社员们大闹生产、大兴水利、贯彻农业“八字宪法”、从事农业科学研究和各行各业对农业的支援等方面的情况。
    这次图片展览是按照中捷文化合作协定1960年执行计划举办的。
