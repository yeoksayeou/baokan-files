Title: Lao Rebel Troops Repulsed by People's Armed Forces
Author:
Date: 1960-12-26
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, Hanoi, 25th. The Laos Fighting Forces Radio broadcasted on the evening of the 24th that the Vientiane Garrison Headquarters of the Lao legal government issued a communique on the 24th, stating that when the ten battalions of the Boun Oum-Phoumi Nosavan rebel troops and the American, Thai, and South Vietnamese troops carried out a “mopping-up” operation from Nakha along Highway 13 to a certain location on the 21st, they were repulsed by the people’s armed forces and government troops. Dozens of rebels surrendered with their weapons on the 24th. They were warmly received by the local people and government troops.

The communique said that in the counterattack, the people along the route also coordinated with the fighting. They often used the trees on both sides of the road to set up obstacles on the road, damaging the roads and bridges, making it impossible for the rebels to advance.

### 老挝叛军受到人民武装阻击

1960-12-26
第6版()
专栏：

    老挝叛军受到人民武装阻击
    新华社河内25日电　据寮国战斗部队电台24日晚上广播：老挝合法政府万象卫戍司令部24日发表公报说，当文翁·纳占巴塞—富米·诺萨万叛军和美国、泰国、南越军队等十个营21日从那卡沿着十三号公路向某地进行“扫荡”时，受到人民武装力量和政府军的阻击。叛军中有几十人于24日持械投诚。他们受到当地人民和政府军队的热诚接待。
    公报说，在阻击战中，沿途各地人民也配合战斗，他们经常利用公路两旁的树木在公路上设置障碍物，破坏公路和桥梁，使叛军不能前进。
