Title: Laotian Royal Delegation Postpones Visit to China
Author:
Date: 1960-12-12
Page: 4

    Xinhua News Agency, 11th — On December 9th, Prince Souvanna Phouma, Prime Minister of the Royal Government of Laos, telegraphed Premier Zhou Enlai, informing him that due to the domestic situation, the Laotian friendly delegation, originally scheduled to visit our country on December 10th for a friendly visit, could not visit as scheduled and suggested that the visit be postponed.

### 老挝王国代表团延期访华

1960-12-12
第4版()
专栏：

    老挝王国代表团延期访华
    新华社11日讯　12月9日老挝王国政府首相梭发那·富马亲王致电周恩来总理，告知原订于12月10日到我国进行友好访问的老挝友好代表团，由于国内的局势，不能如期来访，建议将这一访问延期进行。
