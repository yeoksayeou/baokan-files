Title: Vice Premier Luo Ruiqing and Others Receive Guests from Eight Latin American Countries
Author:
Date: 1960-12-27
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, December 26th: Vice Premier of the State Council Luo Ruiqing, Xi Zhongxun, Vice Chairman of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress Lin Feng, and Vice Chairman of the National Committee of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference Bao Erhan, this afternoon received guests from eight Latin American countries who are currently visiting China and held cordial and friendly conversations with them.

The foreign guests received were delegations and representatives from Cuba, Brazil, Haiti, Bolivia, Panama, Dominican Republic, Argentina, and Colombia, representing women, youth, and the arts.

Present at the reception were Liao Chengzhi, Chu Tunan, Zhang Hanfu, Ding Xilin, Yao Zhen, Dong Chuncai, Zhou Erfu, Xu Guangping, and Zhang Chao.

Cuban Ambassador to China, Óscar Pino Santos, was also present. (Attached picture)

### 罗瑞卿副总理等接见拉丁美洲八国外宾

1960-12-27
第4版()
专栏：

    罗瑞卿副总理等接见拉丁美洲八国外宾
    新华社26日讯　国务院副总理罗瑞卿、习仲勋，全国人民代表大会常务委员会副委员长林枫，政协全国委员会副主席包尔汉，今天下午接见了正在我国访问的拉丁美洲八个国家的外宾，并且同他们进行了亲切友好的谈话。
    被接见的外宾是来自古巴、巴西、海地、玻利维亚、巴拿马、多米尼加、阿根廷、哥伦比亚等国家的妇女、青年、文艺方面的代表团和代表。
    接见时在座的，有廖承志、楚图南、章汉夫、丁西林、姚溱、董纯才、周而复、许广平和张超等。
    古巴驻中国大使奥斯卡·皮诺·桑托斯也在座。（附图片）
