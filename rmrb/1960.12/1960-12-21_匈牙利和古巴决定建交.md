Title: Hungary and Cuba Decide to Establish Diplomatic Relations
Author:
Date: 1960-12-21
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, Budapest, December 20th. During the visit of the Cuban Revolutionary Government delegation led by Guevara to Hungary, friendly talks were held with representatives of the Hungarian government on the 18th, after which it was decided that the two countries would establish diplomatic relations at the ambassadorial level.

The protocol on the establishment of diplomatic relations was signed by Foreign Minister Sik on the Hungarian side and by Guevara, the head of the Cuban Revolutionary Government delegation, on the Cuban side.

### 匈牙利和古巴决定建交

1960-12-21
第5版()
专栏：

    匈牙利和古巴决定建交
    据新华社布达佩斯20日电　以格瓦拉为首的古巴革命政府代表团在访问匈牙利期间，于18日同匈牙利政府代表进行亲切友好的会谈后，决定两国建立大使级的外交关系。
    在建立外交关系的议定书上签字的匈牙利方面的代表是外交部长西克，古巴方面是古巴革命政府代表团团长格瓦拉。
