Title: Scientists from Various Countries Gather in Moscow to Discuss Disarmament and International Security; Chinese Scientists Expose the Erroneous Stance of Certain American Scientists
Author:
Date: 1960-12-25
Page: 6

    Discussing Disarmament and International Security
    Scientists from Various Countries Gather in Moscow
    Chinese Scientists Expose the Erroneous Stance of Certain American Scientists
    (Dispatch from this newspaper) A meeting of Eastern and Western scientists to discuss disarmament and international security was held in Moscow from November 27 to December 5.
    This meeting was first held in Pugwash, Canada, hence the name "Pugwash" International Scientists' Conference. This was the sixth session.
    Seventy-six scientists from fifteen countries, including the Soviet Union, China, Poland, Czechoslovakia, Hungary, Bulgaria, the German Democratic Republic, as well as the United States, Britain, France, Canada, the Netherlands, Australia, Austria, and West Germany, participated in the conference.
    The Chinese scientists who participated in the meeting were: Zhou Peiyuan, Yu Guangyuan, Zhang Wei, Feng Binfu.
    At this conference, scientists from various countries exchanged views on opposing nuclear war, banning nuclear weapons, ceasing nuclear testing, achieving disarmament, safeguarding world peace, and the responsibilities of scientists.
    During the conference, Soviet scientists elaborated on the Soviet Union's position on the issue of disarmament, which was supported by the vast majority of scientists. However, the speeches of some American scientists were consistent with the US government's rhetoric hindering disarmament. Scientists from the Soviet Union and other socialist countries engaged in sharp debates with these American scientists. In their speeches, Chinese scientists exposed the erroneous stance of certain American scientists, and also exposed their echoing of the US government's plot to provoke Sino-Soviet relations and create "two Chinas." At the meeting, Chinese scientists also clarified the Chinese government's and the Chinese people's consistent policy of cherishing peace.
    The conference finally issued a statement.

### 讨论裁军和国际安全问题  各国科学家在莫斯科集会  我国科学家揭露美国某些科学家的错误立场

1960-12-25
第6版()
专栏：

    讨论裁军和国际安全问题
    各国科学家在莫斯科集会
    我国科学家揭露美国某些科学家的错误立场
    本报讯　东西方科学家会见讨论裁军和国际安全问题的会议于11月27日至12月5日在莫斯科举行。
    这个会议第一次是在加拿大的帕格沃希举行的，所以命名为“帕格沃希”国际科学家会议。这次是第六届会议。
    参加这次会议的有来自苏联、中国、波兰、捷克斯洛伐克、匈牙利、保加利亚、德意志民主共和国以及美国、英国、法国、加拿大、荷兰、澳大利亚、奥地利、西德等十五个国家的七十六位科学家。
    参加会议的中国科学家是：周培源、于光远、张维、冯宾符。
    在这次会议上，各国科学家就反对核战争、禁止核武器、停止核试验、实现裁军、保卫世界和平以及科学家的责任问题交换了意见。
    在会议进程中，苏联科学家阐明了苏联在裁军问题上的主张，得到绝大多数科学家的支持。而某些美国科学家的发言却与美国政府阻挠裁军的言论相一致。苏联和其他各社会主义国家的科学家同这些美国科学家展开了尖锐的争论。中国科学家在发言中揭露了美国某些科学家的错误立场，并且揭露了他们附和美国政府挑拨中苏关系和制造
  “两个中国”阴谋的言论。中国科学家在会上还阐明了中国政府和中国人民一贯的爱好和平的政策。
    会议最后发表了声明。
