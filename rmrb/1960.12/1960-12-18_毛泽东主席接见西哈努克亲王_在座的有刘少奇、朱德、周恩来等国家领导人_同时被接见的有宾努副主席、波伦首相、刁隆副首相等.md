Title: Chairman Mao Zedong Receives Prince Sihanouk. Present were national leaders such as Liu Shaoqi, Zhu De, and Zhou Enlai. Also received were Vice Chairman Penn Nouth, Prime Minister Pho Proeung, and Deputy Prime Minister Nhiek Tioulong.
Author:
Date: 1960-12-18
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, December 17th: Chairman Mao Zedong received Cambodian Head of State Prince Norodom Sihanouk and his wife at the Qin Zheng Dian Hall in Zhongnanhai this afternoon. Chairman Mao Zedong had a cordial and friendly conversation with Prince Sihanouk.

Present at the reception were Chairman Liu Shaoqi, Chairman Zhu De, Premier Zhou Enlai and his wife.

Also received were Cambodian Royal Supreme Council Vice-Chairman Penn Nouth, Prime Minister Pho Proeung, and First Deputy Prime Minister Nhiek Tioulong, who accompanied Prince Sihanouk on his visit to our country. Also received were Prince Sihanouk's princess and prince, as well as some senior Cambodian officials.

Also present at the reception from the Chinese side were Vice-Chairmen of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress Guo Moruo, Huang Yanpei, and Peng Zhen, Vice Premier of the State Council Chen Yi and his wife, Ulanhu, and Li Xiannian, Minister of Foreign Trade Ye Jizhuang, Vice Minister of Foreign Affairs Ji Pengfei, and our country's Ambassador to Cambodia Wang Youping and his wife.

Cambodian Ambassador to China Leng Ngeth and his wife were also present at the reception.

After the reception, Chairman Mao Zedong hosted a banquet to entertain Prince Sihanouk and other distinguished Cambodian guests. (Attached picture)
        Left picture: Chairman Mao and Prince Sihanouk, his wife, princess, and prince pose for a photo.
                                                Xinhua News Agency reporter Hou Bo

    毛泽东主席接见西哈努克亲王
    在座的有刘少奇、朱德、周恩来等国家领导人
    同时被接见的有宾努副主席、波伦首相、刁隆副首相等
    新华社17日讯　毛泽东主席今天下午在中南海勤政殿接见柬埔寨国家元首诺罗敦·西哈努克亲王和夫人。毛泽东主席同西哈努克亲王进行了亲切友好的谈话。
    接见时在座的，有刘少奇主席、朱德委员长、周恩来总理和夫人。

    接见时我国方面在座的，还有全国人民代表大会常务委员会副委员长郭沫若、黄炎培、彭真，国务院副总理陈毅和夫人、乌兰夫、李先念，对外贸易部部长叶季壮，外交部副部长姬鹏飞，我国驻柬埔寨大使王幼平和夫人。
