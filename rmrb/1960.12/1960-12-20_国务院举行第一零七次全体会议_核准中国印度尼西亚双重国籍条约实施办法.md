Title: The State Council Held Its 107th Plenary Meeting Approving the Implementation Measures for the Sino-Indonesian Dual Nationality Treaty
Author:
Date: 1960-12-20
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, December 19th - The State Council held its 107th plenary meeting today, approving the "Implementation Measures for the Treaty between the Government of the People's Republic of China and the Government of the Republic of Indonesia on the Question of Dual Nationality." The meeting also approved matters concerning the administrative division changes of counties in Hebei Province, and passed a list of appointments and removals.

### 国务院举行第一零七次全体会议  核准中国印度尼西亚双重国籍条约实施办法

1960-12-20
第5版()
专栏：

    国务院举行第一零七次全体会议
    核准中国印度尼西亚双重国籍条约实施办法
    新华社19日讯　国务院今天举行第一零七次全体会议，核准了“中华人民共和国政府和印度尼西亚共和国政府关于双重国籍问题的条约的实施办法”。会议还通过了河北省县的行政区划变更事项。通过了任免名单。
