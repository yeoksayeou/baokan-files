Title: Vietnamese Musicians Delegation Visits Shanghai
Author:
Date: 1960-12-06
Page: 4
    Xinhua News Agency, December 5th: The Vietnamese musicians delegation, headed by Nguyen Xuan Khuat, Vice Chairman of the Vietnam Union of Literary and Art Associations, concluded its twenty-day visit to Beijing and departed for Shanghai on the 4th, accompanied by Li Yuanqing, Deputy Director of the Chinese Music Research Institute, before returning to their country.
    On the 3rd, Zhou Weizhi, Director of the Bureau of Art Administration of the Ministry of Culture, and Lü Ji, Chairman of the Chinese Musicians Association, hosted a farewell banquet for the guests.

### 越南音乐工作者代表团去上海访问

1960-12-06
第4版()
专栏：

    越南音乐工作者代表团去上海访问
    新华社5日讯　以越南文联副主席阮春阔为首的越南音乐工作者代表团，结束了在北京为期二十天的访问，4日由中国音乐研究所副所长李元庆陪同去上海访问，然后回国。
    三日，文化部艺术事业管理局局长周巍峙和中国音乐家协会主席吕骥曾设宴欢送客人。
