Title: Mongolia Holds State Banquet to Welcome Sihanouk.
Zedenbal Wishes Cambodian People Achievements in First Five-Year Plan.
Sihanouk Says Cambodian-Mongolian Friendship Will Develop on the Basis of Mutual Understanding.
Author:
Date: 1960-12-13
Page: 5
Xinhua News Agency, Ulaanbaatar, December 11th: The Presidium of the Great People's Khural and the Council of Ministers of Mongolia held a state banquet this evening to welcome Prince Sihanouk's visit to Mongolia.

Other Cambodian guests who came to visit with Prince Sihanouk also attended today's banquet.

Mongolian state leaders Sambuu, Zedenbal, Tsende, Balzhinnyam, Jagvaral, Maidar, and Foreign Minister Shagdarssuren all attended the banquet.

Chinese Ambassador to Mongolia Xie Fusheng and other foreign diplomatic envoys to Mongolia also attended today's banquet.

At the banquet, Zedenbal and Prince Sihanouk both spoke.

In his speech, Zedenbal said that the Mongolian people sincerely sympathize with and support the efforts of His Royal Highness Prince Sihanouk to ease international tensions, consolidate peace, and develop friendly cooperation among the peoples of all countries. Zedenbal expressed his belief that the hardworking and intelligent Cambodian people will surely be able to successfully realize the first five-year plan, and wished the Cambodian people success in this endeavor.

In his speech, Prince Sihanouk said that we are satisfied to feel that the Mongolian People's Republic is making sincere and confident struggles for peaceful coexistence and for the comprehensive development of its own country. Like you, Cambodia also adheres to this principle, so although the national systems of our two countries are different, we are close to you, and the friendship between our two countries will develop on the basis of mutual understanding. He expressed his firm belief that Cambodia and Mongolia will establish close relations.

Prince Sihanouk paid a visit to Sambuu, Chairman of the Presidium of the Great People's Khural of Mongolia, and Zedenbal, Chairman of the Council of Ministers, this afternoon.

Today, Sambuu and Zedenbal paid a return visit to Prince Sihanouk.

This morning, Prince Sihanouk, accompanied by Sambuu and Zedenbal, visited the Children's Palace. Mongolian children warmly welcomed the distinguished guests and performed programs for them.

In the afternoon, Prince Sihanouk, accompanied by Sambuu, visited the shoe factory and the woolen textile factory, and watched a sports performance at the gymnasium.

### 蒙古举行国宴欢迎西哈努克  泽登巴尔祝柬埔寨人民在第一个五年计划中取得成就  西哈努克说柬蒙友谊将在互相谅解的基础上向前发展

1960-12-13
第5版()
专栏：

    蒙古举行国宴欢迎西哈努克
    泽登巴尔祝柬埔寨人民在第一个五年计划中取得成就
    西哈努克说柬蒙友谊将在互相谅解的基础上向前发展
    据新华社乌兰巴托11日电　蒙古大人民呼拉尔主席团和部长会议今天晚上为西哈努克亲王访问蒙古举行国宴。
    随西哈努克亲王前来访问的其他柬埔寨客人也出席了今天的宴会。
    蒙古国家领导人桑布、泽登巴尔、曾德、巴尔吉尼雅姆、扎格瓦拉尔、迈达尔以及外交部长沙格达尔苏伦等都出席了宴会。
    中国驻蒙古大使谢甫生和其他各国驻蒙古外交使节也出席了今天的宴会。
    在宴会上，泽登巴尔和西哈努克亲王都讲了话。
    泽登巴尔在讲话中说，蒙古人民衷心地同情和支持西哈努克亲王殿下的为缓和国际紧张局势、巩固和平和发展各国人民友好合作的努力。泽登巴尔表示相信，勤劳智慧的柬埔寨人民一定能够胜利实现第一个五年计划，并祝柬埔寨人民在这个事业中取得成就。
    西哈努克亲王在讲话中说，我们满意地感到，蒙古人民共和国正在为争取和平共处和为自己国家的全面发展而作着真诚的充满信心的斗争。同你们一样，柬埔寨也奉行这个原则，因此我们两国的国家制度尽管不同，我们同你们是亲近的，我们两国之间的友谊将在互相谅解的基础上向前发展。他表示确信，柬蒙两国将建立起亲密关系。
    西哈努克亲王今天中午拜会了蒙古大人民呼拉尔主席团主席桑布和部长会议主席泽登巴尔。
    今天，桑布和泽登巴尔回拜了西哈努克亲王。
    今天上午，西哈努克亲王在桑布和泽登巴尔陪同下参观了少年宫，蒙古儿童们热烈地接待了贵宾，为贵宾们演出了节目。
    下午，西哈努克亲王在桑布的陪同下参观了皮鞋厂、毛纺织厂，并在体育馆观看了体育表演。
