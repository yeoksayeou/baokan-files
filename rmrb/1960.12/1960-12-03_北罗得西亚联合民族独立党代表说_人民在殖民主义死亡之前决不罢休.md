Title: Representative of the United National Independence Party of Northern Rhodesia Says People Will Never Give Up Until Colonialism Dies

Author:

Date: 1960-12-03
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, Cairo. Kamanaga, National Vice-Secretary of the United National Independence Party of Northern Rhodesia and representative of the party in Cairo, said here on November 29, "We, the people of Northern Rhodesia, have long been determined to drive away the imperialist beasts. We will never give up until we see the death of imperialism and colonialism."

Kamanaga made the remarks when meeting with a Xinhua News Agency reporter on the eve of "Imperialism Get Out of Africa Day" to be held on December 1. He also said, "The African people of Northern Rhodesia, together with millions of people in other countries, are fighting against imperialism and colonialism, and reaffirm that they will unswervingly carry the noble struggle to the end." Kamanaga pointed out that this year alone, sixteen African countries have declared independence, and even more countries are struggling for independence.

### 北罗得西亚联合民族独立党代表说  人民在殖民主义死亡之前决不罢休

1960-12-03
第6版()
专栏：

    北罗得西亚联合民族独立党代表说
    人民在殖民主义死亡之前决不罢休
    据新华社开罗电　北罗得西亚联合民族独立党全国副书记兼这个党驻开罗代表卡曼加11月29日在这里说，“我们北罗得西亚人民早已决定赶走帝国主义兽类。在看到帝国主义和殖民主义死亡之前，我们决不罢休。”
    卡曼加是将在12月1日举行的“帝国主义滚出非洲日”的前夕会见新华社记者时说这番话的。他还说，“北罗得西亚非洲人民正同其他国家千百万人民一起，同帝国主义和殖民主义进行斗争，并重申他们要不屈不挠地把崇高的斗争进行到底。”卡曼加指出，仅仅今年一年，非洲就有十六个国家宣告独立，同时还有更多的国家正在为争取独立而进行斗争。
