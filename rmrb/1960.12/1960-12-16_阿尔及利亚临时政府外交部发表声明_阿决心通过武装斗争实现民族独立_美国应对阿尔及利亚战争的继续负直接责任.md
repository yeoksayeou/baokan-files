Title: Algerian Provisional Government Foreign Ministry Issues Statement: Algeria Determined to Achieve National Independence Through Armed Struggle; The United States Bears Direct Responsibility for the Continuation of the Algerian War
Author: 
Date: 1960-12-16
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, Cairo, December 14th. The Algerian Provisional Government Foreign Ministry issued a statement at a press conference held here tonight regarding the large-scale killing of Algerian people by French colonial troops, saying that the heroic Algerian people refuse to compromise with French imperialism, will never abandon their goals, and are determined to achieve their independence through armed struggle. The statement said, "The recent bloodshed in Algeria demonstrates the true factors constituting the Algerian struggle and proves that any solution imposed by France is impossible to achieve."

The statement continued that De Gaulle's conceived "self-determination" means undermining the Algerian revolution by dividing Algeria and preserving French colonial rule over Algeria in order to continue to maintain the illegal interests of French colonialists in Algeria.

The statement pointed out, "However, the Algerian people have not been, and will never be, deceived by these conspiratorial means. The spontaneous demonstrations held by our people against De Gaulle during his trip to Algeria clearly demonstrate our people's political consciousness and their irresistible revolutionary strength. These spontaneous demonstrations are a strong and genuine echo of the continuous struggle waged by the Algerian National Liberation Army against the imperialists."

At the press conference, when a reporter asked, "What responsibility should the United States and the NATO countries bear for the recent Algerian disputes?" The Deputy Secretary-General of the Algerian Provisional Government Foreign Ministry, Mabrouk Belehoucine, said, "We have talked about this issue repeatedly, but it is worth saying again that the United States and the NATO countries bear direct responsibility for the continuation of the Algerian war. In fact, the weapons that France uses for combat all come from American factories."

### 阿尔及利亚临时政府外交部发表声明  阿决心通过武装斗争实现民族独立  美国应对阿尔及利亚战争的继续负直接责任

1960-12-16
第6版()
专栏：

    阿尔及利亚临时政府外交部发表声明
    阿决心通过武装斗争实现民族独立
    美国应对阿尔及利亚战争的继续负直接责任
    新华社开罗14日电　阿尔及利亚临时政府外交部今晚在这里举行的记者招待会上就法国殖民主义军队大批枪杀阿尔及利亚人民发表声明说，英勇的阿尔及利亚人民拒绝同法国帝国主义者妥协，他们决不背弃自己的目标，决心通过武装斗争来实现自己的独立。声明说，“阿尔及利亚最近发生的流血事件表明了构成阿尔及利亚斗争的真正因素，并证明，法国强加的任何解决办法都是不可能实现的。”
    声明接着说，戴高乐设想的“自决权”意味着用分裂阿尔及利亚的办法来破坏阿尔及利亚革命，保存法国殖民主义对阿尔及利亚的政治统治以便继续维护法国殖民主义者在阿尔及利亚的非法利益。
    声明指出，“但是，阿尔及利亚人民过去不会，将来也决不会为这些阴谋手段所欺骗。最近我国人民在戴高乐在阿尔及利亚旅行期间举行的反对他的自发的示威，清楚地表明了我国人民的政治觉悟和他们不可遏阻的革命力量，这些自发的示威是对阿尔及利亚民族解放军对帝国主义者进行的持续斗争的强烈的，真正反响。”
    在记者招待会上有记者问：“美国和北大西洋公约组织国家对最近的阿尔及利亚纠纷应负什么责任？”阿尔及利亚临时政府外交部副秘书长马卜鲁克·贝勒侯赛因说，“我们屡次谈过这个问题，但是不妨再说一遍，美国和北大西洋公约组织国家应对阿尔及利亚战争的继续进行负直接的责任。事实上，法国用来作战的武器都是从美国工厂来的。”
