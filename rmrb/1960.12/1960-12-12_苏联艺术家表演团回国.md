Title: Soviet Artists Performance Troupe Returns Home
Author:
Date: 1960-12-12
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, 11th - The Soviet Artists Performance Troupe, which came to China under the 1960 implementation plan of the Sino-Soviet Cultural Cooperation Agreement, concluded its forty-day visit and performance in China and, filled with the deep friendship of the Chinese people, left Beijing by plane this morning to return home.

Those who went to the airport to see them off included Qu Wu, Vice Chairman of the Commission for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries, Lu Ming, Deputy Director, Zhu Ming, Director of the Liaison Department of the Ministry of Culture, Bian Cheng, Deputy Secretary-General of the Sino-Soviet Friendship Association, Ding Bo, Manager of the China Performing Arts Agency, Huang Hui, Deputy Manager, Lu Jing, Deputy Secretary-General of the Chinese Dancers Association, Chen Zhifu, Deputy Head of the Central National Song and Dance Troupe who recently returned from a visit to the Soviet Union, and more than 40 musicians, singers, and dancers from the capital.

The Soviet Artists Performance Troupe gave twenty-five performances in Beijing, Shijiazhuang, Changsha, Wuhan, Guangzhou, Shanghai and other places in China, and thirteen special performances for workers, members of rural people's communes, students, and literary and art workers, with a total audience of more than 75,000 people. Their performances were warmly welcomed by the Chinese audience.

### 苏联艺术家表演团回国

1960-12-12
第5版()
专栏：

    苏联艺术家表演团回国
    新华社11日讯　根据中苏文化合作协定1960年执行计划来我国的苏联艺术家表演团，结束了在我国为期四十天的访问演出以后，满载着中国人民的深厚情谊，在今天早晨乘飞机离开北京回国。
    到机场欢送的有对外文化联络委员会副主任屈武、副司长鲁明，文化部联络司司长朱明，中苏友好协会总会副秘书长卞诚，中国演出经理公司经理丁波、副经理黄回，中国舞蹈工作者协会副秘书长陆静，最近访苏归来的中央民族歌舞团副团长陈之夫，以及首都音乐、歌唱、舞蹈演员等四十多人。
    苏联艺术家表演团在我国北京、石家庄、长沙、武汉、广州、上海等地演出了二十五场，并为各地工人、农村人民公社社员、学生、文艺工作者等作了十三场专场演出，观众共达七万五千多人。他们的演出，受到我国观众的热烈欢迎。
