Title: Two Members of the Republic of Mali Technical Delegation Return Home

Author:
Date: 1960-12-25
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Guangzhou, December 24th – Silla Fode and Diarra Tidiani, members of the Republic of Mali Technical Delegation who were invited by the Ministry of Light Industry to visit our country, departed from Guangzhou today to return to their country after visiting Beijing, Nanjing, Shanghai, Wuhan, Guangzhou and other places in our country.

### 马里共和国技术代表团二人回国

1960-12-25
第4版()
专栏：

    马里共和国技术代表团二人回国
    新华社广州24日电　应轻工业部邀请前来我国访问的马里共和国技术代表团成员西拉·福迪和迪亚拉·蒂迪亚尼，在我国北京、南京、上海、武汉、广州等地参观访问后，今天离开广州回国。
