Title: Cuban Student Delegation Returns Home
Author:
Date: 1960-12-05
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, December 4th: The Cuban student delegation, led by Lieutenant Colonel Ángel Quevedo, which came to China at the invitation of the All-China Students' Federation, left Beijing by plane this morning to return home.

Those who saw them off at the airport included Hu Qili, Chairman of the All-China Students' Federation, and Qian Dawei, Deputy Director of the International Liaison Department of the All-China Youth Federation.

### 古巴学生代表团回国

1960-12-05
第4版()
专栏：

    古巴学生代表团回国
    新华社4日讯　应中华全国学生联合会邀请来我国访问的以安赫尔·盖维多少校为首的古巴学生代表团，今天上午乘飞机离开北京回国。
    到机场欢送的有中华全国学生联合会主席胡启立，中华全国青年联合会国际联络部副部长钱大卫等。
