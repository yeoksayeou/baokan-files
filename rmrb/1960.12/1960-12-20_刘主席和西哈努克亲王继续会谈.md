Title: Chairman Liu and Prince Sihanouk Continue Talks
Author:
Date: 1960-12-20
Page: 1

    Xinhua News Agency, December 19th: Chairman of the People's Republic of China, Liu Shaoqi, and Cambodian Head of State, Prince Norodom Sihanouk, continued their talks this afternoon.
    The talks proceeded in a friendly atmosphere.
    Vice Chairman Dong Biwu, Chairman Zhu De, and Premier Zhou Enlai participated in the talks. The Chinese side also included Vice Chairmen of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress Guo Moruo, Huang Yanpei, Peng Zhen, and Lin Feng, Vice Premiers of the State Council Chen Yi, Ulanhu, and Li Xiannian, Minister of Foreign Trade Ye Jizhuang, Vice Minister Li Qiang, Vice Minister of Foreign Affairs Ji Pengfei, and Chinese Ambassador to Cambodia Wang Youping, among others.
    The Cambodian side included Vice Chairman of the Cambodian Supreme Council of the Throne, Penn Nouth, Prime Minister Pho Proeung, First Deputy Prime Minister Nhiek Tioulong, Chief of Staff of the Royal Air Force and Technical Advisor to the Head of State, Nhiek Nou, Cambodian Ambassador to China, Leng Ngeth, and other senior Cambodian officials.

### 刘主席和西哈努克亲王继续会谈

1960-12-20
第1版()
专栏：

    刘主席和西哈努克亲王继续会谈
    新华社19日讯　中华人民共和国主席刘少奇和柬埔寨国家元首诺罗敦·西哈努克亲王，今天下午继续举行会谈。
