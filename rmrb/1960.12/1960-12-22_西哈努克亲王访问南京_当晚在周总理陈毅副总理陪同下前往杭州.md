Title: Prince Sihanouk Visits Nanjing; Travels to Hangzhou in the Evening Accompanied by Premier Zhou and Vice Premier Chen Yi
Author:
Date: 1960-12-22
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Nanjing, December 21st: Cambodian Head of State Prince Norodom Sihanouk and his wife, along with other distinguished Cambodian guests, arrived in Nanjing this morning, accompanied by Premier Zhou Enlai, Vice Premier Chen Yi and their wives.

The distinguished guests were warmly welcomed at the station by Jiangsu Province Governor Hui Yuyu and his wife, Nanjing Mayor Chen Yang, and thousands of people from all walks of life.

Accompanying Prince Sihanouk on his visit to Nanjing were Penn Nouth, Vice Chairman of the Cambodian Supreme Council of the Throne, Prime Minister Pho Proeung, and First Deputy Prime Minister Nhiek Tioulong. Also arriving in Nanjing with the Prince were Prince Sihanouk's princesses and princes, high-ranking Cambodian officials accompanying the visit to China, members of the Cambodian economic delegation, and artists from the Royal Dance Troupe.

Cambodian Ambassador to China Leng Ngeth also arrived in Nanjing.

Today, the weather in Nanjing was clear, and Xiaguan Station was decorated like a festival. Huge banners welcoming the distinguished guests were hung at the station, and the national flags of China and Cambodia fluttered in the wind. Thousands of people gathered on the platform early in the morning with immense excitement, awaiting the first visit of the Cambodian Head of State to Nanjing.

At 9:40 a.m., the special train carrying the distinguished guests pulled into the station amidst applause and cheers from the welcoming crowd. People lit firecrackers, beat gongs and drums, and waved colorful flags and flowers, enthusiastically shouting: "Welcome Prince Sihanouk!" "Salute to the Cambodian people!" The entire station was filled with a moving scene of close unity between the Chinese and Cambodian people.

As Prince Sihanouk and his wife, along with other distinguished Cambodian guests, stepped out of the train carriage accompanied by Premier Zhou Enlai, Vice Premier Chen Yi and their wives, Jiangsu Province Governor Hui Yuyu and his wife, Vice Chairman of the Jiangsu Provincial Committee of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference Peng Chong, Commander of the Nanjing Military Region of the People's Liberation Army Lieutenant General Zhang Caiqian, Nanjing Mayor Chen Yang, and others stepped forward to warmly shake hands and greet the distinguished guests one by one. A group of young women presented flowers to Prince Sihanouk and all the distinguished guests. People cheered enthusiastically, expressing deep respect and warm welcome to Prince Sihanouk, who has made significant contributions to enhancing the friendship between the Chinese and Cambodian people. Prince Sihanouk and the distinguished guests waved frequently to greet the welcoming crowd.

Also at the station to welcome the distinguished guests today were the heads of the Jiangsu Province and Nanjing City local organizations of various democratic parties, the heads of various government agencies and people's organizations, and the directors of the China-Cambodia Friendship Association in Nanjing.

Also accompanying the distinguished guests to Nanjing today were Chinese Ambassador to Cambodia Wang Youping and his wife, and Director of the Protocol Department of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs Yu Peiwen.

This newspaper reports, according to Xinhua News Agency, Nanjing, December 21st: Jiangsu Province Governor Hui Yuyu hosted a warm banquet this evening in honor of Cambodian Head of State Prince Norodom Sihanouk and his wife, as well as other distinguished Cambodian guests. Premier Zhou Enlai, Vice Premier Chen Yi and his wife, Chinese Ambassador to Cambodia Wang Youping and his wife, and others accompanied all the distinguished Cambodian guests to the banquet.

Prince Sihanouk and his wife, along with other distinguished Cambodian guests, left Nanjing by special train in the evening, accompanied by Premier Zhou Enlai, Vice Premier Chen Yi and his wife, and others, to visit Hangzhou.

This newspaper reports, according to Xinhua News Agency, Nanjing, December 21st: Prince Sihanouk and other distinguished guests, accompanied by Premier Zhou Enlai, Vice Premier Chen Yi and his wife, Jiangsu Province Governor Hui Yuyu, and Nanjing Mayor Chen Yang, among others, paid their respects at the Sun Yat-sen Mausoleum, visited the Ming Xiaoling Mausoleum and Xuanwu Lake, and other scenic spots and historical sites this morning. In the afternoon, they visited the Wulaocun branch of the Baixia People's Commune and the Radio Industry School in Nanjing.

### 西哈努克亲王访问南京  当晚在周总理陈毅副总理陪同下前往杭州

1960-12-22
第1版()
专栏：

    西哈努克亲王访问南京
    当晚在周总理陈毅副总理陪同下前往杭州
    新华社南京21日电　柬埔寨国家元首诺罗敦·西哈努克亲王和夫人及柬埔寨的其他贵宾，在周恩来总理、陈毅副总理和夫人的陪同下，今天上午抵达南京。
    贵宾们在车站上受到江苏省省长惠浴宇和夫人，南京市市长陈扬及各界人民数千人的热烈欢迎。
    随同西哈努克亲王一起到南京参观访问的，有柬埔寨王位最高委员会副主席宾努，首相福·波伦和第一副首相?·刁龙。随同亲王到达南京的，还有西哈努克亲王的公主、王子、随行访华的柬埔寨高级官员、柬埔寨经济代表团成员，以及皇家舞蹈团的艺术家们。
    柬埔寨驻我国大使兰·?特也随同到达南京。
    今天南京天气晴朗，下关车站打扮得如同节日一般。车站上悬挂着欢迎贵宾的巨幅标语，中柬两国国旗迎风飘扬。数千群众怀着无比的兴奋心情，一早就聚集在月台上，迎候柬埔寨国家元首第一次光临南京。
    上午九时四十分，贵宾们乘坐的专车在欢迎群众的掌声和欢呼声中驶进车站。人们燃起鞭炮，敲起锣鼓，挥舞着彩旗和鲜花，热情地高呼：“欢迎西哈努克亲王！”“向柬埔寨人民致敬！”整个车站出现了中柬两国人民亲密团结的动人情景。
    当西哈努克亲王和夫人及柬埔寨的其他贵宾们由周恩来总理、陈毅副总理和夫人陪同步出车厢时，江苏省省长惠浴宇和夫人，政协江苏省委员会副主席彭冲，人民解放军南京部队首长张才千中将，南京市市长陈扬等迎上前去，和贵宾们一一热情握手问好。一群女青年向西哈努克亲王及全体贵宾献花。人们热烈欢呼，向为增进中柬人民友谊作出了重大贡献的西哈努克亲王，表示深切的敬意和热忱的欢迎。西哈努克亲王和贵宾们频频招手，向欢迎的群众致意。
    今天到车站欢迎贵宾的，还有各民主党派江苏省、南京市地方组织负责人，各机关、各人民团体负责人，以及中柬友好协会在南京的理事。
    今天陪同贵宾们一同到达南京的，还有我国驻柬埔寨大使王幼平和夫人，外交部礼宾司司长俞沛文。
    本报讯　据新华社南京21日电：江苏省省长惠浴宇今晚热烈欢宴柬埔寨国家元首诺罗敦·西哈努克亲王和夫人以及柬埔寨其他贵宾。周恩来总理、陈毅副总理和夫人、我国驻柬埔寨大使王幼平和夫人等陪同柬埔寨全体贵宾出席宴会。
    西哈努克亲王和夫人以及柬埔寨其他贵宾已在当晚乘专车离开南京，在周恩来总理、陈毅副总理和夫人等人的陪同下，前往杭州参观访问。
    本报讯　据新华社南京21日电：西哈努克亲王等一行贵宾，在周总理、陈毅副总理和夫人、江苏省省长惠浴宇和南京市市长陈扬等陪同下，今天上午晋谒了中山陵，游览了明孝陵和玄武湖等名胜古迹，下午访问了南京市白下人民公社五老村分社和无线电工业学校。
