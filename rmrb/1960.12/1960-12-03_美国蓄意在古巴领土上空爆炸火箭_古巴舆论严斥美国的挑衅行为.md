Title: The US Deliberately Explodes Rocket over Cuban Territory; Cuban Public Opinion Sternly Condemns US Provocations
Author:
Date: 1960-12-03
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, Havana, December 1st. Newspapers here today reported the incident of the United States deliberately detonating a rocket over Cuban territory on November 30th, and strongly condemned this new and serious provocation against Cuba by the US imperialists.

According to reports, at 2:55 PM on November 30th, residents of the Holguin region of Oriente Province in Cuba heard a huge explosion, which extended to areas such as San Andres, Aguas Claras, Peronal, and Mayari. After the explosion, thick smoke appeared in the sky.

Following the explosion, Cuban police and rebel army personnel collected fragments of US rockets and devices from the rockets in these areas. Among the rocket debris that has been handed over to the rebel army forces, there are rocket fragments marked with words proving they are US rockets, engines, and a forty-pound spherical device.

The United Press International, an American news agency, reported yesterday from Cape Canaveral, Florida, the rocket launch base in the United States, confirming that the two-stage rocket that exploded over Cuba was "deliberately destroyed a few minutes after launch." The news agency also quoted "authoritative sources" as saying that the wreckage of the rocket "may have landed at least partially in Cuba."

The Cuban newspaper *Revolución* condemned this new provocative act by the United States, saying, "This is an expression of American provocation and its contempt for the lives and safety of the people." It said, "The Cuban people angrily strike back against this new aggression by imperialism, which will neither frighten them nor cause them fear." *Hoy* pointed out that "this latest provocation by US imperialism shows that Washington's official hysteria against Cuba and Latin America has reached a point of foolishness and desperation."

### 美国蓄意在古巴领土上空爆炸火箭  古巴舆论严斥美国的挑衅行为

1960-12-03
第6版()
专栏：

    美国蓄意在古巴领土上空爆炸火箭
    古巴舆论严斥美国的挑衅行为
    新华社哈瓦那1日电　此间报纸今天报道了美国在11月30日蓄意使一枚火箭在古巴领土上空爆炸的事件，并且强烈谴责美帝国主义者对古巴的这一新的严重挑衅。
    据报道，11月30日下午两点五十五分，古巴奥连特省奥尔金地区的居民听到巨大的爆炸声，爆炸声扩及圣安德烈斯、阿瓜斯克拉拉斯、佩罗纳耳和马亚里等地。在发生这次爆炸之后，天空中出现了浓密的烟雾。
  发生这次爆炸事件之后，古巴警察和起义军人员在这些地区收集到了美国火箭碎片和火箭上的装置。在这些已经被送交起义军部队的火箭残骸中，包括标有证明是美国火箭的字样的火箭碎片、发动机和一个四十磅重的球形装置。
    美国通讯社合众国际社昨天发自美国佛罗里达州火箭发射基地卡纳维拉尔角的电讯证实，这支在古巴上空爆炸的两级火箭是在“发射几分钟后故意被炸毁的”。这家通讯社还援引“权威人士”的话说，这支火箭的残骸“可能至少有一部分落到了古巴。”
    古巴《革命报》谴责美国这一新的挑衅行动说，“这是美国的挑衅和它蔑视人民的生命和安全的表现”。它说，“古巴人民愤怒地反击帝国主义的这一新的侵略，这一侵略既不会使他们害怕，也不会使他们感到恐惧”。《今日报》指出，“美帝国主义最近的这次挑衅表明，华盛顿官方反对古巴和拉丁美洲的歇斯底里达到了愚蠢和绝望的程度。”
