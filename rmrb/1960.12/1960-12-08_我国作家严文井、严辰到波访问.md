Title: Chinese Writers Yan Wenjing and Yan Chen Visit Poland
Author:
Date: 1960-12-08
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, Warsaw, December 5th. Chinese writers Yan Wenjing and Yan Chen arrived in Warsaw by plane on the 5th. They are visiting Poland in accordance with this year's Sino-Polish cultural cooperation agreement and are expected to visit here for a month.

### 我国作家严文井、严辰到波访问

1960-12-08
第6版()
专栏：

    我国作家严文井、严辰到波访问
    新华社华沙5日电　中国作家严文井、严辰5日乘飞机到达华沙。他们是按照今年中波文化合作协定来波兰访问的，预计他们将在这里参观访问一个月。
