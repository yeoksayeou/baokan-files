Title: My Ambassador to Cuba Shen Jian Arrives in Havana
Author:
Date: 1960-12-25
Page: 5
Xinhua News Agency, Havana, December 23rd: Shen Jian, the first Ambassador Extraordinary and Plenipotentiary of the People's Republic of China to the Republic of Cuba, his wife, and some embassy staff members arrived in Havana this afternoon.

	Warmly welcoming them at the airport were Jiménez, Director of the National Institute of Agrarian Reform of Cuba; Manuel Yepe, Director of the Protocol Department of the Cuban Ministry of Foreign Affairs, and other Cuban officials; Ramón Calcines, member of the National Executive Bureau of the Cuban People's Socialist Party; Giraldo Mazola Collazo, Director of the Cuban Institute for Friendship with the Peoples; Bartolomé Alvárez Ríos, President of the Cuba-China Friendship Association; Marta Frayde, President of the Cuban Movement for the Defense of Peace and Sovereignty of the Peoples; and prominent Cuban figures who had visited China.

	Also present at the airport to welcome them were Huang Wenyou, Chargé d'Affaires ad interim of the Chinese Embassy in Cuba, and other members of the Chinese Embassy; Zeng Tao, Director of the Havana Branch of the Xinhua News Agency; Zou Siyi, Chinese trade representative; Lü Gezi, chairman of the New Democratic League, and representatives of other overseas Chinese organizations.

	Diplomatic envoys and diplomatic officials from the Soviet Union and other socialist countries stationed in Cuba were also present at the airport to welcome them.

	A team of Cuban militiamen holding the national flags of Cuba and China welcomed the Chinese ambassador and his entourage at the airport. Representatives of the Cuba-China Friendship Association and representatives of overseas Chinese presented flowers to the Chinese ambassador and his wife.

### 我驻古巴大使申健到哈瓦那

1960-12-25
第5版()
专栏：

    我驻古巴大使申健到哈瓦那
    新华社哈瓦那23日电　中华人民共和国驻古巴共和国首任特命全权大使申健和夫人及一部分使馆人员今天下午抵达哈瓦那。
    在机场上热烈欢迎他们的有古巴土地改革全国委员会主任希门尼斯、古巴外交部礼宾司司长曼努埃尔·耶佩斯和其他古巴官员，古巴人民社会党全国执行局委员拉蒙·卡尔西内斯、古巴和各国人民友好委员会主任希拉耳多·马索拉·科利亚索、古巴—中国友好协会主席巴耳多梅罗·阿耳瓦雷斯·里奥斯，古巴保卫和平和各国人民主权运动主席玛塔·弗赖德以及访问过中国的古巴著名人士。
    到机场欢迎的还有中国驻古巴临时代办黄文友及中国大使馆的其他人员、新华社哈瓦那分社社长曾涛、中国贸易代表邹斯颐、新民主同盟主席吕戈子及其他华侨团体的代表。
    到机场欢迎的还有苏联及其他社会主义国家驻古巴的外交使节和外交官员。
    一队手执古巴、中国两国国旗的古巴民兵在机场上欢迎中国大使和他的随行人员。古巴—中国友好协会的代表和华侨代表向中国大使和夫人献花。
