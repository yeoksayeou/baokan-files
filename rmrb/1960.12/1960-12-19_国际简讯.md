	International Briefs

	Italian Journalists on National Strike
	Rome News: Italian journalists nationwide began a two-day strike on the 16th, demanding higher wages. Due to this strike, newspapers and magazines across Italy could not be published for two days. Staff from the Italian "ANSA News Agency" and radio stations also participated in the strike.

	Ceylon Radio Stops Broadcasting BBC News
	Colombo News: According to the evening newspaper "Ceylon Observer," Ceylon Radio has decided to stop broadcasting BBC news programs. The newspaper stated that the new Director of Ceylon Radio, Ronnie de Mel, said that broadcasting BBC news was a remnant of the colonial past, and no independent radio station broadcasts news from other stations.

	Touré Visits Sierra Leone
	Conakry News: Guinean President Sékou Touré left on the morning of the 15th for a four-day official visit to Sierra Leone. He was accompanied by the Speaker of the Guinean National Assembly and Political Secretary of the Democratic Party of Guinea, Diallo Saifoulaye.

	Another Explosion at Jordanian Royal Palace
	Damascus News: According to newspapers here, another violent explosion occurred at the Royal Palace in Amman, the capital of Jordan, on the morning of the 10th. The entire city felt the tremors of the explosion. At that time, all roads leading to the Royal Palace were blocked for three hours. Many people were arrested afterward.

	Most Residents of Pakistani Capital Suffer from Colds
	Karachi News: According to the "Dawn" newspaper, this winter is severely cold in Rawalpindi, the capital of Pakistan, and 60% to 70% of the residents suffer from colds. It is said that the dry climate is one of the main causes of the colds.

### 国际简讯

1960-12-19
第6版()
专栏：国际简讯

    国际简讯
    意大利全国新闻记者罢工
    罗马讯　意大利全国的新闻记者从十六日开始罢工两天，要求增加工资。由于这次罢工，意大利全国两天都不能出版报纸和杂志。参加这次罢工的还有意大利“安莎通讯社”和电台的工作人员。
    锡兰电台停播英国广播公司新闻
    科伦坡讯　据晚报“锡兰观察家报”报道，锡兰广播电台决定停止转播英国广播公司的新闻节目。报纸说，锡兰广播电台新任台长龙尼·德梅尔表示，转播英国广播公司新闻是过去的殖民地的残余，任何独立的广播电台都不转播其他电台的新闻。
    杜尔去塞拉勒窝内访问
    科纳克里讯　几内亚总统塞古·杜尔十五日上午动身前往塞拉勒窝内进行四天正式访问。陪同他前往的有几内亚国民议会议长、几内亚民主党政治书记迪阿洛·塞福拉耶。
    约旦王宫又发生爆炸
    大马士革讯　据这里的报纸报道，约旦首都安曼的王宫十日上午又发生猛烈的爆炸。全市受到爆炸的震动。当时，通向王宫的所有马路被封锁了三个小时。事后有许多人被捕。
    巴基斯坦首都大部分居民患感冒
    卡拉奇讯　据“黎明报”报道，巴基斯坦首都拉瓦尔品第今年冬天严寒，百分之六十到百分之七十的居民患感冒。据说气候干燥是感冒的主要原因之一。
