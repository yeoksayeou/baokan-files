    Li Guoying, a female driver from the Second Automobile Team of the Fuyang Special District in Anhui Province, has diligently studied driving techniques with the cultivation and assistance of the Party, and has achieved good results on the transportation front. From August of last year to the present, she has continuously exceeded 10,000 ton-kilometers per vehicle-ton-month for sixteen months, and has saved 19,000 liters of gasoline. She has been repeatedly recognized as an advanced producer at the provincial, special district, and county levels. She is introducing her driving experience to everyone.

　Xinhua News Agency reporter Zhang Jiazhu
