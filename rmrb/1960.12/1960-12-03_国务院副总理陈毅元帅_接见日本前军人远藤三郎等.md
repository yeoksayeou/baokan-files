Title: Vice Premier of the State Council, Marshal Chen Yi, Receives Former Japanese Military Officer Saburo Endo, et al.
Author:
Date: 1960-12-03
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, December 2nd. Vice Premier of the State Council, Marshal Chen Yi, received Saburo Endo, a former Japanese military officer currently visiting China, and his secretary, Sueo Tokuchi, this afternoon and had a friendly conversation with them.

Present at the meeting was Zhang Xiruo, President of the Chinese People's Institute of Foreign Affairs, and others.

Also present at the meeting was Kinkazu Saionji, a Japanese peace activist in Beijing. (Attached picture)

December 2nd, Vice Premier of the State Council, Marshal Chen Yi, received former Japanese military officer Saburo Endo. The picture shows the scene of the meeting.
　　　Xinhua News Agency reporter Li Jilu

### 国务院副总理陈毅元帅  接见日本前军人远藤三郎等

1960-12-03
第4版()
专栏：

    国务院副总理陈毅元帅
    接见日本前军人远藤三郎等
    新华社2日讯　国务院副总理陈毅元帅今天下午接见了正在我国访问的日本前军人远藤三郎和他的秘书德地末夫，同他们进行了友好的谈话。
    接见时在座的，有中国人民外交学会会长张奚若等。
    接见时，在北京的日本和平人士西园寺公一也在座。（附图片）
    12月2日，国务院副总理陈毅元帅接见了日本前军人远藤三郎。图为接见时情形。
          新华社记者　李基禄摄
