Title: Bourhan Receives Delegation from the Somali Progressive Youth League
Author:
Date: 1960-12-26
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency, December 25th. Bourhan, Vice President of the Chinese-African People's Friendship Association, received the delegation from the Somali Progressive Youth League, headed by Abukar Haji Mou, Chairman of the Somali Progressive Youth League, who are currently visiting China, this morning. Wu Qing, Secretary-General of the Chinese-African People's Friendship Association, was present at the reception.

### 包尔汉接见索马里进步青年联盟代表团

1960-12-26
第4版()
专栏：

    包尔汉接见索马里进步青年联盟代表团
    新华社25日讯　中国非洲人民友好协会副会长包尔汉今天上午接见了正在我国访问的以索马里进步青年联盟主席阿布卡尔·哈吉·茅为首的索马里进步青年联盟代表团。接见时在座的有中非友协总干事吴青等。
