Title: Myanmar Maymyo Garrison Commander Hosts Banquet for the Chinese Delegation of the Joint China-Myanmar Boundary Committee
Author:
Date: 1960-12-23
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, Rangoon, December 22nd: According to a news bulletin released last night by the Myanmar News Bureau, Colonel Khon Nong, the Myanmar Maymyo Garrison Commander, held a grand banquet and Myanmar musical performance on the evening of the 20th to entertain the Chinese delegation of the Joint China-Myanmar Boundary Committee, headed by Yao Zhongming.

The Chinese delegation of twenty members arrived in Maymyo earlier on the 20th to participate in the fifth meeting of the Joint China-Myanmar Boundary Committee held in Maymyo.

The news bulletin stated that the Chinese delegation of the Joint China-Myanmar Boundary Committee, headed by Yao Zhongming, was warmly welcomed by more than 10,000 people from all walks of life upon arriving in Maymyo. Among those welcoming the Chinese delegation were Colonel Khon Nong, the local garrison commander, Lieutenant Colonel Su Weng, other senior military and government officials, and leaders of various parties and people's organizations.

The welcoming crowds shouted slogans such as "Long live China-Myanmar friendship" and "Long live the Five Principles of Peaceful Coexistence."

That afternoon, the distinguished Chinese guests, accompanied by their Myanmar hosts, visited the famous "Weiko" waterfall in Maymyo and had lunch there.

### 缅甸眉苗驻军首长设宴  招待中缅边界联合委员会我方代表团

1960-12-23
第6版()
专栏：

    缅甸眉苗驻军首长设宴
    招待中缅边界联合委员会我方代表团
    新华社仰光22日电据缅甸新闻局昨晚发表的新闻公报说，缅甸眉苗驻军首长孔农上校20日晚举行盛大宴会和缅甸音乐会，招待以姚仲明为首的中缅边界联合委员会中方代表团。
    中方代表团一行二十人是在20日早些时候到达眉苗的，他们将参加在眉苗举行的中缅边界联合委员会第五次会议。
    新闻公报说，以姚仲明为首的中缅边界联合委员会中方代表团在到达眉苗时，曾经受到当地一万多各界居民的热烈欢迎。欢迎中方代表团的还有当地驻军首长孔农上校、苏翁中校、其他高级军政官员和各党派、各人民团体的领袖。
    夹道欢迎的群众高呼：“中缅友好万岁”，“和平共处五项原则万岁”等口号。
    当天下午，中国的贵宾们在缅甸主人陪同下，参观了眉苗著名的“韦科”瀑布，并在当地进午餐。
