Title: Marshal Lin Biao Sends Telegram to General Vo Nguyen Giap Congratulating the Vietnamese People's Army on its 16th Anniversary

Author:
Date: 1960-12-22
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, December 21st. December 22nd is the anniversary of the founding of the Vietnamese People's Army. Marshal Lin Biao, Vice Premier of the State Council and Minister of National Defense, sent a telegram on the 20th to General Vo Nguyen Giap, Vice Premier and Minister of National Defense of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam, congratulating the Vietnamese People's Army on its 16th anniversary. The text of the telegram is as follows:

To Comrade General Vo Nguyen Giap, Vice Premier and Minister of National Defense of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam,

Respected Comrade General,

On the occasion of the glorious festival of the 16th anniversary of the founding of the Vietnamese People's Army, I, on behalf of all commanders and fighters of the Chinese People's Liberation Army and in my own name, extend to you and all officers and men of the Vietnamese People's Army the warmest and fraternal congratulations.

Your closest comrades-in-arms—the Chinese People's Liberation Army—are delighted and encouraged by the great achievements of the Vietnamese People's Army in the struggle for the peaceful reunification of the motherland, the defense of socialist construction, and the defense of peace in Asia and the world.

At present, due to the open support and instigation of the Lao rebel group by US imperialism, a large-scale civil war has broken out in Laos. The Chinese government and the Chinese people sternly condemn US imperialism and its lackey, the Thai reactionary authorities, for their rampant armed intervention in Laos, creating civil war in Laos, and threatening peace in Southeast Asia. We fully support the solemn position of the Government of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam on the Laotian question, and will resolutely work with the Government of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam and the Vietnamese people to make the greatest efforts to uphold the Geneva Accords and stop US imperialism's interference and aggression against Laos. The Chinese People's Liberation Army will stand closely with the Vietnamese People's Army, firmly support the just position of our two governments, redouble our vigilance, and carry out a common struggle to defend the security of our respective countries and safeguard peace in Asia and the world.

Lin Biao
Vice Premier of the State Council and Minister of National Defense of the People's Republic of China
Marshal of the People's Republic of China
December 20, 1960, in Beijing

### 林彪元帅致电武元甲大将  祝贺越南人民军建军十六周年

1960-12-22
第1版()
专栏：

    林彪元帅致电武元甲大将
    祝贺越南人民军建军十六周年
    新华社21日讯　12月22日为越南人民军建军节。国务院副总理兼国防部长林彪元帅20日致电越南民主共和国副总理兼国防部长武元甲大将，祝贺越南人民军建军十六周年。电文如下：越南民主共和国副总理兼国防部长武元甲大将同志敬爱的大将同志：
    欣逢越南人民军建军十六周年的光荣节日，我谨代表中国人民解放军全体指战员，并以我个人名义，向您和越南人民军全体官兵致以最热烈的、兄弟般的祝贺。
    你们最亲密的战友——中国人民解放军，为越南人民军在争取祖国和平统一，保卫社会主义建设，捍卫亚洲和世界和平的斗争中，所取得的巨大成就感到欢欣鼓舞。
    目前，由于美帝国主义对老挝叛乱集团的公然支持和策动，老挝已经爆发大规模的内战。中国政府和中国人民严厉谴责美帝国主义和它的仆从泰国反动当局猖狂地武装干涉老挝，制造老挝内战，威胁东南亚和平的罪恶行为；完全支持越南民主共和国政府关于老挝问题的严正立场；并将坚决地同越南民主共和国政府和越南人民一道，尽最大的努力，来维护日内瓦协议和制止美帝国主义对老挝的干涉和侵略。中国人民解放军将紧密地同越南人民军站在一起，坚决支持我们两国政府的正义立场，百倍提高警惕，为保卫我们各自祖国的安全，维护亚洲和世界的和平而进行共同的斗争。
    中华人民共和国国务院副总理兼国防部长
    中华人民共和国元帅  林彪
    1960年12月20日于北京
