Title: South Korean Political Parties and Organizations Form Council, Advocate Economic and Cultural Exchange and Personnel Exchanges Between North and South Korea
Author:
Date: 1960-12-30
Page: 6

　　Xinhua News Agency, Pyongyang, December 28th: According to a report by the Korean Central News Agency: Sixty-seven representatives of South Korean political parties, social organizations, and democratic figures gathered in Seoul on the 27th to form the "Central Council for National Autonomous Unification," advocating economic and cultural exchanges, personnel exchanges, and exchange of letters between North and South Korea.
　　The report said that Kim Chang-sook was elected as the chairman of the council and Chang Jian-sang as the vice-chairman on the same day.

### 南朝鲜各政党团体组成协议会  主张南北朝鲜间实行经济文化交流和人员往来

1960-12-30
第6版()
专栏：

    南朝鲜各政党团体组成协议会
    主张南北朝鲜间实行经济文化交流和人员往来
    据新华社平壤28日电　据朝鲜中央通讯社消息：南朝鲜各政党、社会团体代表和民主人士六十七人27日在汉城集会，组成“民族自主统一中央协议会”，主张南北朝鲜间实行经济文化交流、人员来往和交换信件。
    消息说，这一天选出金昌淑为协议会委员长、张建相为副委员长。
