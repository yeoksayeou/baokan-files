Wang Xingqi, the head of the youth experimental farm of Fuzhou Commune in Fuxian County, Liaoning Province, under the leadership of the Party and relying on the masses, reformed the old farming system and achieved high grain yields for five consecutive years. With the help of veteran farmers, Wang Xingqi created the successful experience of harvesting both rice and wheat in one year, breaking the convention in Liaoning Province of not planting two crops a year. The picture shows Wang Xingqi (left) and veteran farmers observing the growth of winter wheat in the field where both rice and wheat were harvested. Xinhua News Agency稿
