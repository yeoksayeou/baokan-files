Title: Our Ambassador to North Korea Holds a Soiree to Celebrate the Victorious Return of the Korean People's Army Ensemble from Their Visit to China; Guests and Hosts Emphasize That the Friendship Between the Chinese and Korean Peoples Cannot Be Destroyed by Any Force

Author:

Date: 1960-12-08
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, Pyongyang, December 6th: Chinese Ambassador to North Korea, Qiao Xiaoguang, held a soiree tonight to celebrate the return of the Korean People's Army Ensemble, led by Major General Zhang Ruihuan, after their friendly visit and performance in China. All members of the ensemble attended the event.

Xu Xixin, Director of the Third Bureau of the North Korean Ministry of Foreign Affairs, and others were invited to attend the soiree.

In his speech, Ambassador Qiao Xiaoguang first extended his heartfelt congratulations and sincere thanks to the Korean People's Army Ensemble for their hard work and success during their visit to China. He said that the ensemble's performances in China fully expressed the deep friendship of the Korean people and army towards the Chinese people and army, and greatly deepened this friendship between the two peoples and armies. Ambassador Qiao Xiaoguang stated that the friendship between the Chinese and Korean peoples is a traditional friendship, a fighting friendship, an evergreen friendship, and a friendship that cannot be destroyed by any force.

Major General Zhang Ruihuan then spoke. He said that the Korean People's Army Ensemble had friendly performances in eleven cities, including Beijing, the capital of China, and received warm welcome and meticulous care from the Chinese people and officers and soldiers of the Chinese People's Liberation Army. They spent moving and exciting days. He said that the meeting and performance before the Chinese Communist Party and government leaders, headed by the great leader of the Chinese people, Chairman Mao Zedong, was particularly unforgettable for the Korean People's Army Ensemble.

Major General Zhang Ruihuan said that the friendship between the peoples and armies of North Korea and China has a long history and a deep-rooted foundation. Our friendship was forged with blood in the long-term common struggle against imperialism, is firmly united in the big family of the socialist camp headed by the Soviet Union, and is built on the foundation of Marxism-Leninism and proletarian internationalism. This friendship cannot be destroyed by any imperialism and is eternal.

Major General Zhang Ruihuan said that the Korean People's Army Ensemble saw in China that the 650 million Chinese people, under the wise leadership of the Chinese Communist Party and Chairman Mao Zedong, are holding high the three red banners of the General Line, the Great Leap Forward, and the People's Commune, and have made great achievements in socialist construction. At the same time, they saw that the Chinese People's Liberation Army has grown stronger and become a powerful armed force with modern army, navy, and air force, and it is working hard to liberate Taiwan, and it is highly vigilant in defending the great achievements of the socialist motherland.

The soiree was filled with a happy and friendly atmosphere.

### 为朝鲜人民军协奏团访华胜利返国  我国驻朝鲜大使举行联欢晚会  宾主一致强调中朝人民友谊是任何力量破坏不了的

1960-12-08
第6版()
专栏：

    为朝鲜人民军协奏团访华胜利返国
    我国驻朝鲜大使举行联欢晚会
    宾主一致强调中朝人民友谊是任何力量破坏不了的
    据新华社平壤6日电　中国驻朝鲜大使乔晓光今晚举行联欢晚会，同在中国作友好访问演出后归来的、由张瑞焕少将率领的朝鲜人民军协奏团全体人员进行联欢。
    朝鲜外务省第三局局长许锡信等应邀出席了晚会。
    乔晓光大使在致词中首先对朝鲜人民军协奏团在中国访问期间的辛勤演出和获得的成功，表示衷心的祝贺和真挚的感谢。他说，朝鲜人民军协奏团在中国的演出，充分表达了朝鲜人民和军队对中国人民和军队的深情厚谊，并且大大加深了中朝两国人民和军队之间的这种深情厚谊。乔晓光大使说，中朝两国人民的友谊，是传统的友谊、战斗的友谊、万古长青的友谊，是任何力量也破坏不了的友谊。
    张瑞焕少将接着讲了话。他说，朝鲜人民军协奏团在中国首都北京等十一个城市进行了友好的演出，受到了全中国人民和中国人民解放军官兵们的热烈欢迎和无微不至的关怀，度过了令人感动和兴奋鼓舞的日子。他说，以中国人民伟大领袖毛泽东主席为首的中国共产党和政府领导人的接见和在各位领导人面前的演出，使朝鲜人民军协奏团特别难忘。
    张瑞焕少将说，朝中两国人民和军队之间的友谊，有着悠久的历史和根深蒂固的基础。我们的友谊，是在反对帝国主义的长期共同斗争中用鲜血凝成的，是在以苏联为首的社会主义阵营的大家庭里牢固地团结起来的，是建立在马克思列宁主义和无产阶级国际主义的基础上的。这种友谊，是任何帝国主义也绝对破坏不了的，是永恒的。
    张瑞焕少将说，朝鲜人民军协奏团在中国看到，六亿五千万中国人民在中国共产党和毛泽东主席的英明领导下，高举着总路线、大跃进、人民公社三面红旗，在社会主义建设中取得了巨大的成就。同时看到，中国人民解放军更加壮大了，它已成为具有现代化陆海空军的强有力的武装力量，它正在为解放台湾而努力，它高度警惕地保卫着社会主义祖国的巨大成就。
    晚会充满了欢乐和友好的气氛。
