Title: To enable compatriots on Jinmen and other islands and Chiang's army to celebrate the New Year, our army on the Fujian Front ceases shelling for two days
Author:
Date: 1960-12-31
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, Fujian Front, 30th - A spokesperson for the Headquarters of the Chinese People's Liberation Army on the Fujian Front announced: In order to allow compatriots and Chiang's army officers and soldiers on islands such as Greater Jinmen, Lesser Jinmen, Greater Dadan, and Lesser Dadan to quietly and happily celebrate the New Year, the artillery units of the People's Liberation Army on the Fujian Front have been ordered to cease shelling from December 31, 1960, to January 1, 1961, to show concern.

### 为使金门等岛同胞和蒋军欢度新年  福建前线我军停止炮击两天

1960-12-31
第1版()
专栏：

    为使金门等岛同胞和蒋军欢度新年
    福建前线我军停止炮击两天
    新华社福建前线30日电　中国人民解放军福建前线司令部发言人宣称：为了使大金门、小金门、大担、二担等岛屿上的同胞和蒋军官兵，能够安静愉快地欢度新年，福建前线人民解放军炮兵部队奉命在1960年12月31日至1961年1月1日停止炮击，以示关怀。
