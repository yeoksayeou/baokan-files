Title: Sino-British Friendship Association Hosts Chinese Medical Delegation
Author:
Date: 1960-12-16
Page: 5
Xinhua News Agency, London, December 13th. The Sino-British Friendship Association held a reception here on the evening of the 13th for the Chinese Medical Association medical delegation visiting Britain.

    Among those present at the reception were Derek Bryan, Vice-Chairman of the Sino-British Friendship Association, and Jack Dribbon, Secretary-General of the Association. Also present at the reception were Dr. Angus McPherson, Director of Neuropathology at the British Medical Research Council, prominent doctors, medical researchers, and other individuals.

    Qin Jialin, China's Chargé d'Affaires ad interim, also attended the reception.

    Zhang Xijun, a member of the Chinese delegation, gave a brief report at the meeting. He spoke about the achievements China has made in public health work and the integration of traditional Chinese and Western medicine.

    Qin Jialin, Chargé d'Affaires ad interim of the Chinese Embassy in the United Kingdom, held a reception on the evening of the 12th for the Chinese Medical Association medical delegation headed by Xue Gongchuo, which is currently visiting London.

    Among those present at the reception were Dr. Goldber, Chief Medical Officer of the British Ministry of Health, Dr. Joseph Needham, President of the Sino-British Friendship Association, and more than seventy well-known doctors, medical experts, professors, scientists, and members of parliament.

### 英中友协招待我医学代表团

1960-12-16
第5版()
专栏：

    英中友协招待我医学代表团
    据新华社伦敦13日电　英中友好协会13日晚在这里为访问英国的中华医学会医学代表团举行招待会。
    出席招待会的有英中友好协会副主席德里克·布莱恩和协会秘书长杰克·德里本。出席招待会的还有英国医学研究委员会神经病理学主任安格斯·麦克弗森博士，著名医生，医学研究工作者和其他人士。
    中国临时代办秦加林也出席了招待会。
    中国代表团团员张锡钧在会上作了简短的报告。他谈到了中国公共卫生工作和中西医结合所取得的成就。
    中国驻英国代办处临时代办秦加林在12日晚为正在伦敦访问的以薛公绰为首的中华医学会医学代表团举行招待会。
    出席招待会的有英国卫生部主任医官戈德伯博士、英中友好协会会长李约瑟博士和七十多名著名医生、医学专家、教授、科学家及议员。
