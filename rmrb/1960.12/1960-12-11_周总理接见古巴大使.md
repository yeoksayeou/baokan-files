Title: Premier Zhou Receives the Cuban Ambassador
Author:
Date: 1960-12-11
Page: 2

Xinhua News Agency, December 10th: Premier Zhou Enlai received the first Cuban Ambassador to China, Oscar Pino Santos, and his wife this afternoon and had a cordial conversation with them.

Present at the meeting were Shen Jian, Chinese Ambassador to Cuba, and Lin Ping, Deputy Director of the Department of American and Australian Affairs of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs. (Attached picture)

December 10th, Premier Zhou Enlai received the Cuban Ambassador to China, Oscar Pino Santos, and his wife. Xinhua News Agency reporter Du Xiuxian.

### 周总理接见古巴大使

1960-12-11
第2版()
专栏：

    周总理接见古巴大使
    新华社10日讯　周恩来总理今天下午接见了古巴首任驻中国特命全权大使奥斯卡·皮诺·桑托斯和他的夫人，并且同他们进行了亲切的谈话。
    接见时在座的，有中国驻古巴大使申健，外交部美澳司副司长林平。（附图片）
    十二月十日，周恩来总理接见了古巴驻中国大使奥斯卡·皮诺·桑托斯和他的夫人。　　　新华社记者　杜修贤摄
