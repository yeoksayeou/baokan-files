Title: Abbas Prime Minister Gives Televised Talk in New York Reaffirming Impossibility of Peace Negotiations with France; Algerian People's Struggle Receives Support from Socialist Camp
Author:
Date: 1960-12-12
Page: 6

    Xinhua News Agency, 10th December. New York dispatch: Prime Minister Abbas of the Algerian Provisional Government reiterated in a televised talk to the American Columbia Broadcasting Corporation on the 9th that he no longer believes it is possible to negotiate peace with the French government.
    Answering questions about Algeria's relations with the socialist camp countries and the Western countries, Abbas pointed out, "On one hand, there is the West, giving all possible assistance to French imperialism to fight against us, and on the other hand, there is the socialist camp, helping the Algerian people to fight for independence." He said Algeria has already made its choice from here.
    On the same day, Algerian Provisional Government Minister of Information Yazid also spoke to reporters at the U.S. National Press Club in Washington, expressing opposition to De Gaulle's so-called "referendum" on the Algerian question, scheduled for January 8th next year, and declaring that he does not recognize the legality of this vote. He criticized the United States for pretending to be anti-colonialist, but in reality supporting France against the Algerian people. He pointed out the insidious and cunning intention of the United States' approach, saying that the United States "displays anti-colonialist sentiments one day a week, while effectively supporting French colonialism for the other six days." He said that the United States hopes to benefit from such a policy.

### 阿巴斯总理在纽约发表电视谈话  重申不可能同法国谈判和平  阿尔及利亚人民的斗争得到社会主义阵营支持

1960-12-12
第6版()
专栏：

    阿巴斯总理在纽约发表电视谈话
    重申不可能同法国谈判和平
    阿尔及利亚人民的斗争得到社会主义阵营支持
    新华社10日讯　纽约消息：阿尔及利亚临时政府总理阿巴斯9日在对美国哥伦比亚广播公司发表电视谈话时重申，他不再认为有可能同法国政府谈判和平。
    在回答有关阿尔及利亚同社会主义阵营国家和西方国家的关系的问题时，阿巴斯指出，“一方面是给予法帝国主义以一切可能的援助以对我们作战的西方，另一方面是帮助阿尔及利亚人民争取独立的社会主义阵营”。他说，阿尔及利亚已经从这里作出选择。
    同一天，阿尔及利亚临时政府宣传部长亚齐德也在华盛顿美国全国新闻俱乐部对记者们发表谈话，表示反对戴高乐准备在明年1月8日就阿尔及利亚问题举行的所谓“公民投票”，宣布不承认这个投票的合法性。他批评美国假装反殖民主义，但是在实际上却支持法国反对阿尔及利亚人民。他指出，美国这种作法的阴险和狡猾的用意说，美国“一个星期中有一天表现出反殖民主义情绪，而在另外六天却有效地支持法殖民主义”。他说，美国希望从这样一种政策中得到好处。
