Title: More than 20,000 Miners in Lorraine, France Strike to Protest Government's Plan to Close Mines
Author:
Date: 1960-12-05
Page: 6

    Xinhua News Agency, Paris dispatch from TASS: The French government's plan to close a large number of mines to ease the serious crisis in the mining sector has aroused strong protests from miners.

    According to a report in "L'Humanité Hebdomadaire," more than 25,000 miners in various mines in the Lorraine mining area held a short strike on November 26 to protest the French government's plan, which threatens the survival of millions of miners and their families.

### 抗议政府打算关闭矿井  法国洛林二万多矿工罢工

1960-12-05
第6版()
专栏：

    抗议政府打算关闭矿井
    法国洛林二万多矿工罢工
    据新华社讯　据塔斯社巴黎讯：法国政府打算大量关闭矿井以缓和采矿部门的严重危机的作法，激起了矿工的强烈抗议。
    据《人道报星期刊》报道，洛林矿区各矿井的两万五千多名矿工11月26日举行短时间的罢工，抗议法国政府这种威胁千千万万矿工和他们的家庭的生存的计划。
