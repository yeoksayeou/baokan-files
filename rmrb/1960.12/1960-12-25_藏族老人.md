Tibetan Old Woman
Hu Changshan, Chen Hui (woodcut), Jiang Cheng (poem)

	Each wrinkle, engraved with years of bitterness;
	Each strand of white hair, condensed from the tears of many Langsheng [serfs].
	The heart of the Tibetan old mother, once grieved for her children,
	And was weary for her fate.
	Holding the land certificate, her heart flies towards the East;
	Flying over the snow-capped mountains, singing: "Long live Chairman Mao!"
	The heart of the Tibetan old mother, now laughs for liberation,
	And is drunk with happiness.

### 藏族老人
胡长山  陈惠  江城
1960-12-25
第8版()
专栏：

    藏族老人
    胡长山  陈惠木刻  江城配诗一道道皱纹，镌刻了多少辛酸的年岁；一绺绺白发，凝聚了多少朗生的眼泪。西藏老妈妈的心，曾经为子女哀伤，为命运憔悴。手拿土地证，心儿向着东方飞；飞过大雪山，欢唱：“毛主席万岁！”西藏老妈妈的心，如今为着翻身笑，为着幸福醉。（附图片）
