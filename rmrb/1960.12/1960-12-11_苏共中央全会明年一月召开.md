Title: CPSU Central Committee Plenary Session to be Held Next January
Author:
Date: 1960-12-11
Page: 5
Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, December 9th: TASS reported from Moscow on the 9th that the Central Committee of the Communist Party of the Soviet Union has decided to postpone the CPSU Central Committee Plenary Session to January 1961.

### 苏共中央全会明年一月召开

1960-12-11
第5版()
专栏：

    苏共中央全会明年一月召开
    新华社10日讯　塔斯社莫斯科9日讯：苏联共产党中央委员会决定，苏共中央全会改在1961年1月召开。
