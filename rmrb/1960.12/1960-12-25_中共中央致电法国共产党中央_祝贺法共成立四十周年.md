Title: CPC Central Committee Sends Telegram to French Communist Party Central Committee to Congratulate on the 40th Anniversary of the Founding of the French Communist Party
Author:
Date: 1960-12-25
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, December 24th. The 40th anniversary of the founding of the French Communist Party is on December 29th. The Central Committee of the Chinese Communist Party sent a telegram of congratulations on the 22nd. The full text of the congratulatory telegram is as follows: To the Central Committee of the French Communist Party:
    On the occasion of the 40th anniversary of the founding of the French Communist Party, the Central Committee of the Chinese Communist party, on behalf of all members of the Chinese Communist Party and the Chinese people, extends brotherly congratulations to the glorious and heroic French Communist Party, the French working class, and the French people.
    The Chinese Communists and the Chinese people have always followed with great concern your struggles against monopoly capital, against personal autocracy, for democracy and social progress, in support of national liberation movements, against West German militarism, to defend world peace and to strive for peaceful coexistence. We wish you further victories and achievements in your struggles.
    Long live the French Communist Party and the French working class!
    Long live the friendship between the Chinese and French people!
    Long live the solidarity of the international communist movement!
    Long live Marxism-Leninism—the ever-victorious banner!
          The Central Committee of the Chinese Communist Party, December 22, 1960

### 中共中央致电法国共产党中央  祝贺法共成立四十周年

1960-12-25
第1版()
专栏：

    中共中央致电法国共产党中央
    祝贺法共成立四十周年
    新华社24日讯　12月29日是法国共产党成立四十周年纪念日。中国共产党中央委员会在22日去电祝贺，贺电全文如下：法国共产党中央委员会：
    值此法国共产党成立四十周年之际，中国共产党中央委员会谨代表中国共产党全体党员和中国人民，向光荣的英勇的法国共产党、法国工人阶级和法国人民，致以兄弟般的祝贺。
    中国共产党人和中国人民，向来抱着十分关切的心情，注视着你们所进行的反对垄断资本、反对个人专权、争取民主和社会进步、支持民族解放运动、反对西德军国主义、保卫世界和平和争取和平共处的斗争。祝你们在斗争中取得进一步的胜利和成就。
    法国共产党和法国工人阶级万岁！
    中法两国人民的友谊万岁！
    国际共产主义运动的团结万岁！
    马克思列宁主义——战无不胜的旗帜万岁！
          中国共产党中央委员会　1960年12月22日
