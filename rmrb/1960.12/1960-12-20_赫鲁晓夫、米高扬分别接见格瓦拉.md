Title: Khrushchev and Mikoyan Meet with Guevara Separately
Author:
Date: 1960-12-20
Page: 7

Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, 19th: TASS reports: Soviet Premier Khrushchev and First Deputy Premier Mikoyan today separately received Cuban government economic delegation head Guevara in the Kremlin. Guevara returned to Moscow after visiting Germany and Hungary.

Guevara also met with Soviet Minister of Foreign Trade Patolichev today. The two sides discussed issues concerning the development of trade between the Soviet Union and Cuba.

### 赫鲁晓夫、米高扬分别接见格瓦拉

1960-12-20
第7版()
专栏：

    赫鲁晓夫、米高扬分别接见格瓦拉
    据新华社19日讯　据塔斯社莫斯科讯：苏联部长会议主席赫鲁晓夫、苏联部长会议第一副主席米高扬，今天分别在克里姆林宫接见了古巴政府经济代表团团长格瓦拉。格瓦拉是在访问德国和匈牙利后回到莫斯科的。
    格瓦拉今天还同苏联对外贸易部部长帕托利切夫举行了会晤，双方讨论了关于发展苏联、古巴之间的贸易的问题。
