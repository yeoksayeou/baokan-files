Title: Song of the Land
Author: Nicolás Guillén
Date: 1960-12-29
Page: 8

    Song of the Land
    [Cuba] Nicolás Guillén
    Forward! Forward! The horn of victory has resounded through the clouds. The land, together with the flowing water and the wind, is ours. The hands that sow are also ours. Ours! No force in the world can destroy our unity, nor can it change the direction of our advance. Either victory or death, either death or victory; our people will not die, but will live victoriously. We are on the land, sowing love and peace, cultivating and irrigating our flowers—the flowers of hope. Either victory or death, either death or victory; our people will not die, but will live victoriously. Live on! [Translated by Xiao San and Juan]
      [Editor's Note] "Song of the Land" has been set to music and has become the anthem of the Cuban Land Reform Committee. This poem is translated from the original lyrics.

### 土地之歌
尼古拉斯·纪廉
1960-12-29
第8版()
专栏：

    土地之歌
    〔古巴〕尼古拉斯·纪廉不停地前进！不停地前进！胜利的号角已响彻云霄。土地连同流水和风都是我们的。播种的手也是我们的。我们的！世界上任何力量都不能破坏我们的团
  结，也不能改变我们前进的方向。不是胜利，就是死亡，不是死亡，就是胜利；我们的人民不会死亡，而将胜利地活下去。我们在土地上，播种爱情与和平培植和灌溉我们的鲜花——希望的花朵。不是胜利，就是死亡，不是死亡，就是胜利；我们的人民不会死亡，而将胜利地活下去。活下去！〔萧三、胡安译〕
      〔编者附记〕《土地之歌》已谱成曲子，成了古巴土改委员会的会歌，这首诗是根据歌词原文译出的。
