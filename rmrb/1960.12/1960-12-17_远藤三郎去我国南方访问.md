Title: Saburo Endo Visits Southern China

Author: 
Date: 1960-12-17
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, 16th. Saburo Endo, a former Japanese soldier who was invited by the Chinese People's Institute of Foreign Affairs to visit China, concluded his visit to Northeast China and Beijing and left Beijing by train today to continue his visit to southern China, after which he will return home via Guangzhou.

Wu Maosun, Secretary-General of the Chinese People's Institute of Foreign Affairs, and Xiao Xiangqian, Xie Nanguang, and Wang Xiaoyun were at the station to see him off.

Japanese peace activist Kinkazu Saionji, who is in Beijing, also came to see him off.

### 远藤三郎去我国南方访问

1960-12-17
第4版()
专栏：

    远藤三郎去我国南方访问
    新华社16日讯　应中国人民外交学会邀请来我国访问的日本前军人远藤三郎，结束了在我国东北和北京的访问，今天乘火车离开北京去我国南方继续参观，然后将取道广州回国。
    到车站送行的有中国人民外交学会秘书长吴茂荪和萧向前、谢南光、王晓云等。
    在京的日本和平人士西园寺公一也前往送行。
