Title: King of Nepal Forms Council of Ministers
Author:
Date: 1960-12-28
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, Kathmandu, December 27th: King Mahendra of Nepal appointed a nine-member Council of Ministers headed by himself on the 26th to replace the government of the Kingdom of Nepal headed by Koirala, which was dissolved on the 15th.

It is reported that five members of the Council of Ministers were appointed as ministers, and the remaining four as assistant ministers.

Dr. Tulsi Giri holds the post of Minister of Palace Affairs, and also serves concurrently as Minister of Foreign Affairs, Defense, Transportation, Power, and Irrigation.

### 尼泊尔国王组成大臣会议

1960-12-28
第6版()
专栏：

    尼泊尔国王组成大臣会议
    新华社27日讯　加德满都消息：尼泊尔国王马亨德拉在26日任命了一个由他主持的包括九名成员的大臣会议，以接替在15日被解散的以柯伊拉腊为首的尼泊尔王国政府。
    据报道，大臣会议的五名成员被任命为大臣，其余四人为助理大臣。
    担任宫廷事务大臣职务的是图尔西·吉里博士，他还兼任外交、国防、交通运输、动力和灌溉大臣的职务。
