Title: Southeast Asia Treaty Aggressor Group Plots Intervention in Laos
Author:
Date: 1960-12-07
Page: 6

    Southeast Asia Treaty Aggressor Group Plots Intervention in Laos
    Xinhua News Agency, 6th Dispatch from Manila: According to a report in the *Manila Times* on the 5th, the Southeast Asia Treaty Organization's military advisors' meeting held in Bangkok last month plotted a "military" intervention in the internal affairs of Laos.

    The news said that Philippine Foreign Minister Serrano, in a report received on the 1st from the Philippine military representatives attending the meeting, confirmed that the meeting had discussed the situation in Laos "thoroughly" in order to consider the "appropriate action" to be taken by the Southeast Asia Treaty Organization when it was "urgently needed."

    The newspaper admitted that the military and economic "countermeasures" decided at a series of meetings in Bangkok were mainly to prepare for intervention in Laos.

### 东南亚条约侵略集团策划干涉老挝

1960-12-07
第6版()
专栏：

    东南亚条约侵略集团策划干涉老挝
    新华社6日讯　马尼拉消息：据《马尼拉时报》5日报道，上月在曼谷举行的东南亚条约组织军事顾问会议曾经策划对老挝内政进行“军事性质的”干涉。
    消息说，菲律宾外交部长塞兰诺在1日收到的出席这次会议的菲律宾军事代表所提出的报告证实，会议“彻底地”讨论了老挝的形势，以便考虑东南亚条约组织在“迫切需要时”采取“适当行动”。
    这家报纸承认，在曼谷举行的一系列会议上所决定的军事和经济“反措施”主要都是为了准备干涉老挝。
