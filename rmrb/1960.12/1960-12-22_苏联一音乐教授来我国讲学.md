Title: Soviet Music Professor Arrives in China to Lecture
Author:
Date: 1960-12-22
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, 21st - Sergei Sergeyevich Skrebkov, a professor at the Moscow Conservatory of Music in the Soviet Union, arrived in Beijing by plane today to lecture in China according to the 1960 executive plan of the Sino-Soviet Cultural Cooperation Agreement.

### 苏联一音乐教授来我国讲学

1960-12-22
第4版()
专栏：

    苏联一音乐教授来我国讲学
    新华社21日讯　根据中苏文化合作协定1960年执行计划来我国讲学的苏联莫斯科音乐学院教授谢尔盖·谢尔盖维奇·斯克列勃柯夫，今天乘飞机到达北京。
