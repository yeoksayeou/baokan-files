Title: Chinese National Education Workers Delegation to the Soviet Union
Author:
Date: 1960-12-13
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, 11th – At the invitation of the Soviet Ministry of Education, our delegation of national education workers to the Soviet Union, a group of three, led by Cui Zhongyuan, Director of the General Office of the Ministry of Education, flew to Moscow today.

Those who saw them off at the airport included Xu Fangting, Director of the General Education Department of the Ministry of Education, and Liu Ziyu, Deputy Director of the General Office. S.V. Sudarikov, Chargé d'Affaires ad interim of the Soviet Embassy in China, and Ablova, Second Secretary, also saw them off at the airport.

### 我国民教育工作者代表团赴苏

1960-12-13
第4版()
专栏：

    我国民教育工作者代表团赴苏
    新华社11日讯　应苏联教育部邀请，我赴苏访问的国民教育工作者代表团一行三人，在教育部办公厅主任崔仲远率领下，今天乘飞机去莫斯科。
    到机场欢送的有教育部普通教育司司长徐方庭、办公厅副主任刘子余等。苏联驻华大使馆临时代办苏达利柯夫和二等秘书阿布洛娃也到机场欢送。
