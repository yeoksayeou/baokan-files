Title: Soviet-Hungarian Trade Volume to Increase by 25% Next Year
Author:
Date: 1960-12-22
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, 21st - According to TASS: The Soviet and Hungarian government representatives signed a trade protocol for 1961 in Moscow on the 20th.

The protocol stipulates that the trade volume between the two countries will increase by 25% next year compared to this year.

The Soviet Union will provide Hungary with various machines, equipment, iron ore, petroleum, cotton and other commodities. Hungary will supply the Soviet Union with equipment for food and chemical industrial enterprises, ships, light industrial products, etc.

### 苏匈明年贸易额将增加25％

1960-12-22
第5版()
专栏：

    苏匈明年贸易额将增加25％
    新华社21日讯　据塔斯社莫斯科讯：苏联同匈牙利两国政府代表20日在莫斯科签订了1961年换货议定书。
    议定书规定，明年两国贸易额将比今年增长25％。
    苏联将提供匈牙利各种机器、设备、铁矿石、石油、棉花等商品。匈牙利将向苏联供应食品和化学工业企业的设备、船只、轻工业品等。
