Title: Former Cambodian Prime Minister Hoàng Ẩn Returns Home
Author:
Date: 1960-12-06
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Guangzhou, December 5th: Hoàng Ẩn, former Prime Minister of Cambodia, who was invited by the Chinese People's Institute of Foreign Affairs to visit China, left Guangzhou for home today after visiting Beijing, Wuhan and other places. Li Guangxiang, Vice Mayor of Guangzhou, and Yi Meihou, Chairman of the Guangdong Provincial Federation of Returned Overseas Chinese, and others went to the station to see him off.

### 柬埔寨前首相黄意回国

1960-12-06
第4版()
专栏：

    柬埔寨前首相黄意回国
  　新华社广州5日电　应中国人民外交学会邀请来我国访问的柬埔寨前首相黄意，在北京、武汉等地参观访问后，今天离开广州回国。广州市副市长李广祥、广东省归国华侨联合会主席蚁美厚等前往车站欢送。
