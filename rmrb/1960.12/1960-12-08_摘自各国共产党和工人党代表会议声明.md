Title: Statement from the Meeting of Representatives of Communist and Workers' Parties of Various Countries

Author:
Date: 1960-12-08
Page: 7

    The course of international events in recent years has provided much new evidence that U.S. imperialism is the main bastion of world reaction, the international gendarme, and the enemy of the people of the world.
  　——Excerpt from the statement of the meeting of representatives of Communist and Workers' parties of various countries
