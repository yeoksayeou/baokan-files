Title: China-Latin America Friendship Association Holds Reception to Celebrate the Second Anniversary of the Cuban Revolution
Wishing the newborn Cuba victory after victory
Vice Chairman Dong Biwu and Vice Premier Li Xiannian attended the reception
President Chu Tunan and Cuban Ambassador jointly wished for the continuous development of the militant friendship between the Chinese and Cuban people
Author:
Date: 1960-12-31
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, December 30th. The China-Latin America Friendship Association held a reception in the Great Hall of the People tonight to warmly celebrate the second anniversary of the victory of the Cuban Revolution, and to congratulate the just struggle of the heroic Cuban people and the national democratic movements of Latin American countries for continuously achieving greater victories in 1961.

Dong Biwu, Vice Chairman of the People's Republic of China, Li Xiannian and Xi Zhongxun, Vice Premiers of the State Council, and Lin Feng, Vice Chairman of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress, attended the reception.

Currently, the people of the capital are holding a series of friendly activities to commemorate the 150th anniversary of the struggle for national independence in Latin American countries and to celebrate the second anniversary of the victory of the Cuban Revolution. Tonight, people from all walks of life in the capital gathered again with excitement, to congratulate the newborn Republic of Cuba for forever shining with youthful brilliance, marching from victory to victory, and to congratulate the continuous development of the militant friendship between the Chinese and Cuban peoples.

Invited to attend the reception were Cuban Ambassador to China, Óscar Pino Santos and his wife, José Llanusa, Mayor of Havana, the capital of Cuba, who is currently visiting Beijing, Major Raúl Menéndez Tomasovich, Director of the Cuban National Traffic Police Bureau, Captain Anriji González of the Cuban Rebel Army, Captain and Staff Officer Aisi Lieer Lieiba of the Cuban Rebel Army, and Jorge García Bango, Deputy Mayor of Havana. The Cuban women's delegation led by Elena Gil and friends from various Latin American countries in Beijing also attended the reception.

Chu Tunan spoke at the reception, extending warm congratulations to the heroic Cuban people and the Cuban revolutionary government, and praising the Cuban people, with their glorious traditions, for standing on the front line of the struggle against U.S. imperialism today. He said that the revolutionary victory of the Cuban people has set a glorious example for the struggle of people all over the world against imperialism and colonialism.

He said that under the leadership of the revolutionary government headed by Prime Minister Castro, the Cuban people have made great achievements in various fields. He pointed out that any aggression and interference by U.S. imperialism against Cuba will continue to meet with shameful defeat.

Chu Tunan said that China and Cuba will always encourage and support each other in the struggle against our common enemy, U.S. imperialism. In the new year, the friendship between the Chinese and Cuban peoples will be further developed.

In his speech at the reception, Ambassador Óscar Pino Santos thanked the China-Latin America Friendship Association on behalf of the Cuban people and the people of Latin American countries for organizing a series of activities to commemorate the 150th anniversary of the struggle for national independence in Latin American countries and to celebrate the second anniversary of the victory of the Cuban Revolution. The ambassador said that such activities by the Chinese people are a form of support for us.

The ambassador recounted the struggle of the people of Latin American countries for national independence, pointing out that the people of Latin American countries are today fighting against the most vicious enemy of the people of the world - imperialism.

After speaking about the significance of the Cuban Revolution, Ambassador Pino Santos condemned the imperialist attempt to suppress the Cuban Revolution. The ambassador concluded by wishing for the continuous strengthening of the unity and mutual assistance of the people of all countries in the struggle against imperialism, especially the continuous strengthening of the unity and mutual assistance between the Chinese and Latin American peoples.

Also present at the reception were Zhang Hanfu, Vice Minister of Foreign Affairs, and Qian Junrui, Lu Xuzhang, Qu Wu, Luo Jun, Cui Yitian, and Huang Zhong, heads of other departments of state organs, Hu Yuzhi, Dong Chuncai, and Zhou Erfu, vice presidents of the China-Latin America Friendship Association, Feng Jiping and Wu Han, vice mayors of Beijing, and Yang Hansheng, Kang Yonghe, Xiao San, Xu Guangping, Lao She, Mei Lanfang, and other people from all walks of life in the capital, as well as people who have visited Latin America.

### 中拉友协举行招待会庆祝古巴革命胜利二周年  祝新生的古巴从胜利走向胜利  董必武副主席和李先念副总理等出席了招待会  楚图南会长和古巴大使齐祝中古人民的战斗友谊不断发展

1960-12-31
第1版()
专栏：

    中拉友协举行招待会庆祝古巴革命胜利二周年
    祝新生的古巴从胜利走向胜利
    董必武副主席和李先念副总理等出席了招待会
    楚图南会长和古巴大使齐祝中古人民的战斗友谊不断发展
    新华社30日讯　中国拉丁美洲友好协会今晚在人民大会堂举行招待会，热烈庆祝古巴革命胜利两周年，祝贺英雄的古巴人民和拉丁美洲各国人民进行的民族民主运动的正义斗争，在1961年里不断地获得更大的胜利。
    中华人民共和国副主席董必武，国务院副总理李先念、习仲勋，全国人民代表大会常务委员会副委员长林枫，出席了招待会。
    目前，首都人民正举行一系列友好活动，纪念拉丁美洲各国争取民族独立一百五十周年和庆祝古巴革命胜利二周年。今晚首都各界人士又怀着兴奋的心情聚会，祝贺新生的古巴共和国永远闪烁青春光辉，从胜利走向胜利，祝贺中古两国人民的战斗友谊不断发展。
    应邀出席招待会的，有古巴驻中国大使奥斯卡·皮诺·桑托斯和夫人，正在北京访问的古巴首都哈瓦那市市长何塞·利亚努萨，古巴全国交通警察局局长劳尔·梅嫩德斯·托马索维奇少校，古巴起义军大尉安利吉·冈萨雷斯，古巴起义军大尉兼参谋艾西烈尔·列依巴，哈瓦那市副市长豪尔赫·卡尔西亚·班戈。以埃伦娜·希尔为首的古巴妇女代表团和正在北京的拉丁美洲各国的朋友，也出席了招待会。
    楚图南在招待会上讲话，他向英雄的古巴人民和古巴革命政府致以热烈的祝贺，并且赞扬具有光荣传统的古巴人民今天站在反对美帝国主义斗争的最前线。他说，古巴人民的革命胜利，为全世界人民反对帝国主义、殖民主义的斗争，树立了光辉的榜样。
    他说，古巴人民在以卡斯特罗总理为首的革命政府的领导下，在各方面取得了巨大成就。他指出，美帝国主义对古巴进行的任何侵略和干涉，都将继续遭到可耻的失败。
    楚图南说，中古两国在反对共同敌人美帝国主义的斗争中，永远是互相鼓舞，互相支持的。在新的一年里，中古两国人民的友谊还会获得进一步的发展。
    奥斯卡·皮诺·桑托斯大使在招待会上的讲话中，代表古巴人民和拉丁美洲各国人民感谢中国拉丁美洲友好协会组织一系列活动，来纪念拉丁美洲各国争取民族独立一百五十周年和庆祝古巴革命胜利两周年。大使说，中国人民的这种活动就是对我们的一种支持。
    大使讲述了拉丁美洲各国人民争取民族独立的斗争，指出拉丁美洲各国人民今天正在向全世界人民最凶恶的敌人——帝国主义进行着斗争。
    皮诺·桑托斯大使在讲了古巴革命的意义以后，他谴责了帝国主义企图镇压古巴革命。大使最后祝世界各国人民在反对帝国主义斗争中的团结和相互援助不断加强，特别是中国和拉丁美洲各国人民之间的团结和相互援助的不断加强。
    出席招待会的还有：外交部副部长章汉夫和国家机关其他各部门负责人钱俊瑞、卢绪章、屈武、罗俊、崔义田、黄中，中国拉丁美洲友好协会副会长胡愈之、董纯才、周而复，北京市副市长冯基平、吴晗，首都各界人士阳翰笙、康永和、萧三、许广平、老舍、梅兰芳，以及访问过拉丁美洲的人士等。
