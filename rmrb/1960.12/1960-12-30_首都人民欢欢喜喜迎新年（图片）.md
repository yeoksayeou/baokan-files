Title: The People of the Capital Happily Welcome the New Year (Photos)
Author:
Date: 1960-12-30
Page: 4

The Yu Xiuwu family of the Fusui Jing People's Commune are tidying up potted flowers and cleaning up, welcoming the New Year with cleanliness.　Xinhua News Agency Reporter Gu Dehua photo

In the Wangfujing Gift Shop, people are selecting New Year's cards, preparing to give them to relatives and friends to congratulate them on the New Year. Xinhua News Agency Reporter Gu Dehua photo

Workers at the Beijing Children's Toy Factory are rushing to produce a batch of toys for children. Xinhua News Agency Reporter Wang Wenxi photo

The amateur "East Wind Advances" song and dance troupe orchestra of the Beijing Crane Factory is rehearsing dance music, preparing to perform at the New Year's Eve party. Liu Hongling photo (Xinhua News Agency draft)
