Title: Thank you for the kindness of the Soviet people and Soviet leaders. Chairman Liu Hosts a Grand Banquet for Soviet Leaders. Chairman Liu Shaoqi says that our friendship and unity have been further consolidated and developed. Chairman Brezhnev says that the Soviet people will protect Soviet-Chinese friendship like the apple of their eye.
Author:
Date: 1960-12-08
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, December 7th. Liu Shaoqi, Vice Chairman of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of China, Chairman of the People's Republic of China, and head of the Chinese Party and Government Delegation, held a grand banquet today at the Chinese Embassy in the Lenin Hills to entertain the leaders of the Soviet Communist Party and government.

    Chinese comrades attending as guests included: Li Jingquan, Lu Dingyi, Yang Shangkun, Liu Ningyi, and Liu Xiao, members of the Chinese Party and Government Delegation.

    Soviet comrades attending the banquet included: Soviet Communist Party and government leaders Aristov, Brezhnev, Ignatov, Kozlov, Kosygin, Mikoyan, Mukhitdinov, Suslov, Shvernik, Pospelov, Vice Chairman of the Presidium of the Supreme Soviet of the Soviet Union Organov, Chairman of the Union Council of the Supreme Soviet of the Soviet Union Lobanov, Secretary of the Presidium of the Supreme Soviet of the Soviet Union Georgadze, Deputy Chairman of the Council of Ministers of the Soviet Union Novikov and Ustinov, members of the Presidium of the Supreme Soviet of the Soviet Union Voroshilov and Budyonny, as well as ministers of the Soviet government, senior generals of the Soviet army, and prominent figures from society.

    Also attending today's banquet were diplomatic envoys from many countries to the Soviet Union.

    Chairman Liu Shaoqi spoke first at the banquet. He said that the Chinese Party and Government Delegation, at the invitation of the Soviet Communist Party and the Soviet government, participated in the celebration of the 43rd anniversary of the Great October Socialist Revolution and conducted a friendly visit. We have been warmly welcomed and grandly received by the broad masses of the Soviet people and Party and government leaders in Moscow and other places, which has made us deeply appreciate the profound and sincere fraternal friendship of the Soviet people towards the Chinese people. Please allow me to express my heartfelt thanks to the Soviet people and Soviet leaders for their kindness.

    Chairman Liu Shaoqi said that we came this time with the desire to strengthen Sino-Soviet friendship. We are pleased that our friendship and unity have been further consolidated and developed. This great friendship of ours is eternal and unbreakable.

    He said that we are pleased to see that the great Soviet people have achieved brilliant achievements in the comprehensive development of communism. The construction undertakings of all countries in our socialist camp have also made great progress. We, together with the people of all countries in the world, have made unremitting efforts to safeguard world peace. I believe that as long as all peace-loving countries and peoples in the world unite and work together, the cause of defending world peace will surely be victorious.

    Next, Brezhnev, member of the Presidium of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of the Soviet Union and Chairman of the Presidium of the Supreme Soviet of the Soviet Union, spoke.

    Chairman Brezhnev said that the Soviet people will continue to protect the eternal and unbreakable friendship between the Soviet Union and China, and protect the close unity of all socialist countries, as they would protect the apple of their eye.

    Brezhnev said that the achievements of the peoples of the Soviet Union, the People's Republic of China, and all other socialist countries vividly demonstrate the indisputable superiority of the socialist system. He said, "Now, the world socialist system is becoming the decisive factor in the development of the entire human society."

    Brezhnev said that the Moscow meeting of the Communist and Workers' Parties was an "outstanding event in the international communist movement." It marks a new historical milestone in the development of the international communist movement.

    He emphasized that we are deeply convinced that our just cause will surely be victorious. The fraternal friendship and unity of the peoples of the socialist camp and the ever-increasing power and rock-solid unity of the world communist movement and the workers' movement are a great guarantee of victory.

    Brezhnev said that the visit of the Chinese Party and Government Delegation to the Soviet Union will help to further strengthen the friendship and unity between the Soviet Union and China.

    During the banquet, the hosts and guests frequently toasted to the friendship between the peoples of China and the Soviet Union. The banquet was conducted in an atmosphere of warmth, cordiality, and brotherly friendship.

专栏：

    感谢苏联人民和苏联领导同志的盛情
    刘主席盛宴招待苏联领导人
    刘少奇主席说，我们的友谊和团结得到了进一步的巩固和发展勃
    列日涅夫主席说，苏联人民将像保护眼珠一样保护苏中友谊
    新华社莫斯科7日电　中国共产党中央委员会副主席、中华人民共和国主席、中国党政代表团团长刘少奇，今天在列宁山我国驻苏联大使馆大楼举行盛大宴会，招待苏联共产党和政府的领导人。
    中国方面出席作陪的有：中国党政代表团团员李井泉、陆定一、杨尚昆、刘宁一、刘晓。
    苏联方面出席宴会的有：苏联共产党和政府的领导人阿里斯托夫、勃列日涅夫、伊格纳托夫、科兹洛夫、柯西金、米高扬、穆希金诺夫、苏斯洛夫、什维尔尼克、波斯别洛夫，苏联最高苏维埃主席团副主席奥尔加诺夫，苏联最高苏维埃联盟院主席洛巴诺夫，苏联最高苏维埃主席团秘书格奥尔加泽，苏联部长会议副主席诺维科夫和乌斯季诺夫，苏联最高苏维埃主席团委员伏罗希洛夫和布琼尼，以及苏联政府的部长们、苏军高级将领和社会著名人士。
    出席今天宴会的还有许多国家驻苏联的外交使节。
    刘少奇主席在宴会上首先讲话。他说，中国党政代表团应苏联共产党和苏联政府的邀请，参加伟大十月社会主义革命四十三周年的庆祝典礼，并且进行友好访问。我们在莫斯科和其他各地，受到广大苏联人民和党政领导同志的热烈欢迎和隆重接待，使我们深深体会到苏联人民对中国人民深厚的、诚挚的兄弟情谊。请允许我对苏联人民和苏联领导同志的盛情，表示衷心的感谢。
    刘少奇主席说，我们这次怀着加强中苏友谊的愿望而来。使我们高兴的是，我们的友谊和团结得到了进一步的巩固和发展。我们这种伟大的友谊是永恒的，牢不可破的。
    他说，我们高兴地看到，伟大的苏联人民在全面展开共产主义的建设中，获得了辉煌的成就。我们社会主义阵营各国的建设事业也都获得很大的发展。我们和世界各国人民一起，为维护世界和平进行了不懈的努力。我相信：只要全世界爱好和平的国家和人民团结起来，共同奋斗，保卫世界和平的事业是一定会获得胜利的。
    接着，苏联共产党中央委员会主席团委员、苏联最高苏维埃主席团主席勃列日涅夫讲了话。
    勃列日涅夫主席说，苏联人民今后仍将像保护眼珠一样来保护苏联和中国的永恒的、牢不可破的友谊，保护各社会主义国家的紧密团结。
    勃列日涅夫说，苏联、中华人民共和国和所有其他社会主义国家的人民所取得的成就，鲜明地表明了社会主义制度所具有的无可争辩的优越性。他说：“现在，世界社会主义体系正在成为整个人类社会发展的决定性因素。”
    勃列日涅夫说，各国共产党和工人党莫斯科代表会议是“国际共产主义运动中卓越的事件”。它标志着国际共产主义运动发展中的新的历史里程碑。
    他强调说，我们深信，我们的正义事业一定获胜。社会主义阵营各国人民的兄弟友谊和团结以及世界共产主义运动和工人运动日益加强的威力和磐石般的团结，是取得胜利的伟大保证。
    勃列日涅夫说，中国党政代表团的访问苏联，有助于进一步加强苏中两国的友谊和团结。
    席间，宾主频频为中苏两国人民的友谊干杯。宴会是在热烈、亲切和兄弟般友谊的气氛中进行的。
