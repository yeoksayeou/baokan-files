    On November 19th, personnel from the U.S. Embassy recklessly drove a car in the Congo, running over and killing a Congolese cyclist on the roadside. The Congolese people, having reached the limit of their tolerance, severely beat these murderers. The picture shows the destroyed American car. (Facsimile photo) Xinhua News Agency.
