Title: The CPSU Central Committee, the Supreme Soviet, and the USSR Council of Ministers Host a Banquet for Chairman Liu and the Chinese Party and Government Delegation; Soviet Party and Government Leaders, Heads of Various Departments, and Prominent Figures from All Walks of Life Attend the Reception

Author: 
Date: 1960-12-08
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, 7th December. The Central Committee of the Communist Party of the Soviet Union, the Presidium of the Supreme Soviet of the USSR, and the Council of Ministers of the USSR, held a grand state banquet at the Kremlin at 6 p.m. (Moscow time) today for Liu Shaoqi, Vice-Chairman of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of China, President of the People's Republic of China, and head of the Chinese Party and Government Delegation, and the Chinese Party-Government Delegation's visit to the Soviet Union.

Soviet Party and government leaders attended the reception.

Li Jingquan, Lu Dingyi, Yang Shangkun, Liu Ningyi, and Liu Xiao, members of the Chinese Party and Government Delegation, also attended the reception.

Also attending the reception were heads of various departments of the Soviet government, generals, heads of mass organizations, and prominent figures from all walks of life.

Also attending the reception were diplomatic envoys of many countries stationed in the Soviet Union, as well as Soviet and foreign journalists.

When Chairman Liu Shaoqi and Soviet party and government leaders appeared in the banquet hall, those present applauded warmly, and the national anthems of China and the Soviet Union were played.

The reception proceeded in a warm and friendly atmosphere.

### 苏共中央、最高苏维埃和苏联部长会议  宴请刘主席和我党政代表团  苏联党政领导人、各部门负责人和各界著名人士等出席招待会

1960-12-08
第1版()
专栏：

    苏共中央、最高苏维埃和苏联部长会议
    宴请刘主席和我党政代表团
    苏联党政领导人、各部门负责人和各界著名人士等出席招待会
    新华社莫斯科7日电　苏联共产党中央委员会、苏联最高苏维埃主席团、苏联部长会议，今天下午六时（莫斯科时间）在克里姆林宫为中国共产党中央委员会副主席、中华人民共和国主席、中国党政代表团团长刘少奇和中国党政代表团访问苏联举行盛大国宴。
    苏联党政领导人出席了招待会。
    中国党政代表团团员李井泉、陆定一、杨尚昆、刘宁一、刘晓也出席了招待会。
    出席招待会的还有苏联政府各部门的负责人、将军们、群众团体的负责人和各界著名人士。
    出席招待会的还有许多国家驻苏联的外交使节和苏联及外国的新闻记者们。
    当刘少奇主席和苏联党政领导同志们出现在宴会大厅的时候，到会者热烈鼓掌，高奏中苏两国国歌。
    招待会在热烈友好的气氛中进行。
