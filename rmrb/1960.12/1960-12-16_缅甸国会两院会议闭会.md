Title: Burmese Parliament Concludes Joint Session
Author: 
Date: 1960-12-16
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, Rangoon, December 15th: Burmese President U Win Maung issued an order yesterday announcing the end of the session of the Burmese Chamber of Deputies held in December.

　　The Burmese Chamber of Deputies opened its session on December 5th. During the session, it approved the Sino-Burmese Boundary Treaty signed by the Chinese and Burmese governments in Beijing on October 1st.

　　The session also passed several draft amendments, including the government-proposed 1960 Penal Code (Amendment) Bill.

　　The Burmese Chamber of Nationalities, which opened on the same day as the Chamber of Deputies, also adjourned on December 13th after ratifying the Sino-Burmese Boundary Treaty.

### 缅甸国会两院会议闭会

1960-12-16
第5版()
专栏：

    缅甸国会两院会议闭会
    新华社仰光15日电　缅甸总统吴温貌昨天颁布一项命令，宣布缅甸国会代表院在12月举行的会议结束。
    缅甸代表院这次会议是在12月5日开幕的。会议期间批准了中国政府和缅甸政府10月1日在北京签订的中缅边界条约。
    会议还通过了几项修正草案，其中包括政府提出的1960年刑法（修正）案。
    与代表院同一天开幕的缅甸民族院会议在批准了中缅边界条约后，也已于12月13日闭会。
