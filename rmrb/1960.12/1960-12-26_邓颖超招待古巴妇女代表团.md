Title: Deng Yingchao Hosts Cuban Women's Delegation
Author:
Date: 1960-12-26
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, December 25th: Deng Yingchao, Vice-President of the All-China Women's Federation, hosted a banquet tonight to welcome the Cuban Women's Delegation led by Elena Hill. Among those present at the banquet were Liu Qingyang, Vice-President of the All-China Women's Federation, members of the Presidium Li Zhen, Cao Mengjun, Zeng Xianzhi, and members of the Secretariat. The wife of Cuban Ambassador to China, Oscar Pino Santos, was also invited to attend the banquet.

Before the banquet, Deng Yingchao met with all members of the Cuban Women's Delegation and had a cordial conversation with them. During the meeting, gifts were exchanged between the hosts and guests.

### 邓颖超招待古巴妇女代表团

1960-12-26
第4版()
专栏：

    邓颖超招待古巴妇女代表团
    新华社25日讯　中华人民共和国全国妇女联合会副主席邓颖超今晚设宴欢迎由埃伦娜·希尔率领的古巴妇女代表团。出席宴会作陪的，有全国妇联副主席刘清扬，主席团委员李贞、曹孟君、曾宪植和书记处的成员等。古巴驻中国大使奥斯卡·皮诺·桑托斯的夫人也应邀出席了宴会。
    宴会前，邓颖超接见了古巴妇女代表团全体成员，并且和她们进行了亲切的谈话。在接见的时候，宾主相互赠送了礼品。
