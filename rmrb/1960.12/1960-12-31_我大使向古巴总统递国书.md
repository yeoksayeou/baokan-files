Title: Our Ambassador Presents Credentials to Cuban President
Author:
Date: 1960-12-31
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Havana, December 29th: Our Ambassador to Cuba, Shen Jian, presented his credentials to Cuban President Dorticos at the Presidential Palace here yesterday.

Attending the credentials presentation ceremony were Cuban Foreign Minister Raúl Roa García, Presidential Secretary Luis Buch Rodríguez, and Cuban Foreign Ministry's Director of Protocol, Manuel Yepe.

Accompanying the Ambassador at the credentials presentation ceremony were Counselor of the Chinese Embassy, Huang Wenyou, and Fang Xiao.

At the credentials presentation ceremony, Ambassador Shen Jian first delivered a eulogy. Cuban President Dorticos then delivered a reply.

After the credentials presentation ceremony, the Cuban President and the Chinese Ambassador had a cordial and friendly conversation.

### 我大使向古巴总统递国书

1960-12-31
第1版()
专栏：

    我大使向古巴总统递国书
    据新华社哈瓦那29日电　中华人民共和国驻古巴大使申健昨天在这里的总统府向古巴总统多尔蒂科斯递交国书。
    出席递交国书仪式的有古巴外交部长劳尔·罗亚·加西亚、总统秘书路易斯·布奇·罗德里格斯、古巴外交部礼宾司司长曼努埃尔·耶佩斯。
    陪同大使参加递交国书仪式的有中国大使馆参赞黄文友和方晓。
    在递交国书的仪式上，申健大使首先致颂辞。古巴总统多尔蒂科斯接着致答辞。
    在递交国书的仪式结束以后，古巴总统和中国大使进行了亲切友好的交谈。
