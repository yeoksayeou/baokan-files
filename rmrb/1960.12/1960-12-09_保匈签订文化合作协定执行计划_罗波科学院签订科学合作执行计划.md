Title: Bulgaria and Hungary Sign Cultural Cooperation Agreement Implementation Plan, Romania and Poland Academies of Sciences Sign Scientific Cooperation Implementation Plan
Author:
Date: 1960-12-09
Page: 5

    In order to further strengthen cultural and scientific cooperation, exchange advanced experiences, Bulgaria and Hungary signed the 1961 and 1962 cultural cooperation agreement implementation plan on November 28 in Sofia.
    The plan stipulates that cooperation between the two countries in science, education, literature, art, film, and television should be greatly developed.
    The plan stipulates that in the next two years, the two countries will study issues related to pedagogy and the organization of production education in higher and secondary education, and organize exchanges of experience between scientists, museum workers, teachers, artists, musicians, physicians, etc.
    On December 5, the Romanian and Polish Academies of Sciences signed the 1961-1965 scientific cooperation agreement and the 1961 scientific cooperation implementation plan.
    According to the agreement, the two Academies of Sciences will carry out joint scientific research, help each other in scientific materials and scientific instruments, and exchange scientific publications.

### 保匈签订文化合作协定执行计划  罗波科学院签订科学合作执行计划

1960-12-09
第5版()
专栏：

    保匈签订文化合作协定执行计划
    罗波科学院签订科学合作执行计划
    本报讯　为了进一步加强相互间的文化科学合作和交流相互间的先进经验，保加利亚和匈牙利两国1961和1962年文化合作协定执行计划11月28日在索非亚签订。
    计划规定，要大大发展两国在科学、教育、文学、艺术、电影和电视方面的合作。
    计划规定，在今后两年中，两国互相研究高等和中等教育方面教学法和组织生产教育的一些问题，并且组织两国科学家、博物馆工作者、教师、艺术家、音乐家、医师等交流经验。
    罗马尼亚和波兰两国科学院在12月5日签订了1961—1965年科学合作协定和1961年科学合作执行计划。
    根据协定，两国科学院将进行共同的科学研究工作，在科学资料和科学仪器方面互相帮助和交换科学刊物。
