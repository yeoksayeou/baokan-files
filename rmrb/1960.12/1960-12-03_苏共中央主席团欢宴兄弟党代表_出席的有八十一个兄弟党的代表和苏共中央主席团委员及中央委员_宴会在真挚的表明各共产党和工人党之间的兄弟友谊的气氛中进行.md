Title: The CPSU Central Committee Presidium Hosts a Banquet for Representatives of Fraternal Parties
Author:
Date: 1960-12-03
Page: 1

    The Representatives of Eighty-One Fraternal Parties and Members of the CPSU Central Committee Presidium and Central Committee Members Attended
    The Banquet Took Place in an Atmosphere of Sincere Expression of Brotherly Friendship Between the Communist and Workers' Parties

    Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, December 2nd (TASS): The Presidium of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of the Soviet Union held a banquet in the Kremlin today to entertain the participants of the Communist and Workers' Parties meeting. Present at the banquet were the heads and members of the delegations of eighty-one Communist and Workers' Parties, members of the CPSU Central Committee Presidium and members of the CPSU Central Committee. The banquet took place in an atmosphere of sincere brotherly friendship, demonstrating the relations between the Communist and Workers' Parties.

专栏：

    苏共中央主席团欢宴兄弟党代表
    出席的有八十一个兄弟党的代表和苏共中央主席团委员及中央委员
    宴会在真挚的表明各共产党和工人党之间的兄弟友谊的气氛中进行
    新华社2日讯　塔斯社莫斯科2日电：苏联共产党中央委员会主席团今天在克里姆林宫举行宴会，招待共产党和工人党会议的参加者。出席宴会的有八十一个共产党和工人党的代表团的团长和团员们、苏共中央主席团委员和苏共中央委员们。宴会是在真挚的、表明各共产党和工人党之间的关系的兄弟友谊的气氛中进行的。
