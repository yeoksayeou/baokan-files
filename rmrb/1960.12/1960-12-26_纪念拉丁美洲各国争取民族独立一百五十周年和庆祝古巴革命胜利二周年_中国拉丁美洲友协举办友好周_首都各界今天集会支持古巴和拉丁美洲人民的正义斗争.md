Title: Commemorating the 150th Anniversary of Latin American Countries' Struggle for National Independence and Celebrating the Second Anniversary of the Cuban Revolution's Victory: China-Latin America Friendship Association Holds Friendship Week; People from All Walks of Life in the Capital Gather Today to Support the Just Struggle of the Cuban and Latin American People
Author:
Date: 1960-12-26
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, December 25th: The China-Latin America Friendship Association will hold "Friendship Week to Commemorate the 150th Anniversary of Latin American Countries' Struggle for National Independence and Celebrate the Second Anniversary of the Cuban Revolution's Victory." A series of celebratory activities will be launched from December 26th this year to January 1st, 1961.

    People from all walks of life in the capital will hold a gathering on the afternoon of the 26th to express the Chinese people's profound friendship with the people of Latin American countries, and to support the heroic Cuban people and the people of Latin American countries in their just struggle against US imperialist aggression, for the attainment and defense of national independence, and for the preservation of world peace.

    During Friendship Week, the China-Latin America Friendship Association will also hold report meetings, musical evenings, and film evenings to introduce to the people of the capital the glorious tradition of the peoples of Latin American countries in opposing imperialism and colonialism and striving for national independence, as well as the situation in which they are continuing to carry out their struggles victoriously. In addition, people from all aspects of the capital will also hold get-togethers with people from some Latin American countries who are visiting our country.

### 纪念拉丁美洲各国争取民族独立一百五十周年和庆祝古巴革命胜利二周年  中国拉丁美洲友协举办友好周  首都各界今天集会支持古巴和拉丁美洲人民的正义斗争

1960-12-26
第1版()
专栏：

    纪念拉丁美洲各国争取民族独立一百五十周年和庆祝古巴革命胜利二周年
    中国拉丁美洲友协举办友好周
    首都各界今天集会支持古巴和拉丁美洲人民的正义斗争
    新华社25日讯　中国拉丁美洲友好协会将举办“纪念拉丁美洲各国争取民族独立一百五十周年和庆祝古巴革命胜利二周年友好周”，从今年12月26日到1961年1月1日期间，将展开一系列的庆祝活动。
    首都各界人民将在26日下午举行集会，表达中国人民对拉丁美洲各国人民的深厚友谊，并且支持英雄古巴人民和拉丁美洲各国人民，为反对美帝国主义侵略、争取和维护民族独立、保卫世界和平所进行的正义斗争。
    在友好周期间，中国拉丁美洲友好协会还将举办报告会、音乐晚会和电影晚会，向首都人民介绍拉丁美洲各国人民反对帝国主义和殖民主义、争取民族独立的光荣斗争传统，以及他们正在继续胜利地进行斗争的情况。此外，首都各方面人士还将同正在我国访问的拉丁美洲一些国家的人士进行联欢。
