Title: Mayor of Havana Visits Beijing
Author:
Date: 1960-12-31
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency, December 30th. Havana Mayor José Llanusa and other distinguished Cuban guests, accompanied by Zhao Pengfei, Deputy Mayor of Beijing, toured Beijing today and visited the Great Hall of the People, the Chinese People's Revolutionary Military Museum, the National Industrial and Transportation Exhibition, the National Agricultural Exhibition Hall, the Beijing Workers' Stadium, and the Beijing Railway Station.

    At the Great Hall of the People, the distinguished guests visited the auditorium and banquet hall with great interest, and carefully viewed the Hunan Pavilion's Xiang embroidery screen and the Sichuan Pavilion's large-scale colored paintings of the Red Army crossing the Luding Bridge. During their visit to the Beijing Workers' Stadium, Mayor José Llanusa inquired in detail about the various sports activities of the people of Beijing and expressed great interest in the widespread development of mass sports activities.

### 哈瓦那市市长在京参观

1960-12-31
第4版()
专栏：

    哈瓦那市市长在京参观
    新华社30日讯　哈瓦那市市长何塞·利亚努萨等古巴贵宾，今天在北京市副市长赵鹏飞等陪同下游览了北京市容，并参观了人民大会堂、中国人民革命军事博物馆、全国工业交通展览会、全国农业展览馆、北京工人体育场和北京车站。
    在人民大会堂，贵宾们以极大的兴趣参观了大礼堂、宴会厅等，并且仔细地观赏了湖南馆的湘绣屏风和四川馆的红军强过泸定桥等巨幅彩画，在北京工人体育场参观时，何塞·利亚努萨市长详细地了解了北京人民的各项体育运动情况，他对广泛开展群众性的体育活动表示很感兴趣。
