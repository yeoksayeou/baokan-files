Title: Chairman Liu Receives Cuban Ambassador Pino Santos
Author:
Date: 1960-12-25
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, December 24th: Liu Shaoqi, Chairman of the People's Republic of China, received Oscar Pino Santos, the first Ambassador Extraordinary and Plenipotentiary of the Republic of Cuba to China, this afternoon and held cordial and friendly talks with him. Present at the reception were Zhang Hanfu, Vice Minister of Foreign Affairs; Lin Ping, Deputy Director of the Department of American and Australian Affairs; and Ge Buhai, Deputy Director of the Protocol Department. (Attached picture)
    Right picture: Chairman Liu Shaoqi shakes hands cordially with Oscar Pino Santos, the first Cuban Ambassador to China.
                Xinhua News Agency Reporter Meng Qingbiao

### 刘主席接见古巴大使皮诺·桑托斯

1960-12-25
第1版()
专栏：

    刘主席接见古巴大使皮诺·桑托斯
    新华社24日讯　中华人民共和国主席刘少奇，今天下午接见了古巴共和国首任驻中国特命全权大使奥斯卡·皮诺·桑托斯，并且同他进行了亲切友好的谈话。接见时在座的，有外交部副部长章汉夫、美澳司副司长林平，礼宾司副司长葛步海。（附图片）
    右图：刘少奇主席和古巴首任驻华大使奥斯卡·皮诺·桑托斯亲切握手。
                新华社记者　孟庆彪摄
