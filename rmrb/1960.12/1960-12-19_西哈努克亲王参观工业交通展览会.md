Title: Prince Sihanouk Visits Industrial and Transportation Exhibition
Author:
Date: 1960-12-19
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, 18th: Cambodian Head of State Prince Sihanouk and other Cambodian dignitaries, accompanied by Vice Premier Ulanhu, visited the National Industrial and Transportation Exhibition this afternoon.

　　When Prince Sihanouk arrived at the exhibition hall by car, more than 500 staff members gathered at both sides of the gate to warmly applaud and welcome him. Ji Chaoding, the general curator of the exhibition, and deputy general curators Yong Longgui, Wang Jiabao, and Wang Bin, who were waiting for the Cambodian guests in front of the door, guided Prince Sihanouk and other guests to carefully view the exhibits in the Machinery Hall, Light Industry Hall, Textile Industry Hall, Atomic Energy Hall, Metallurgical Industry Hall, and various Chinese-made cars and agricultural machinery on the West Square. Warm applause resounded when the distinguished guests entered each exhibition hall. Some staff members standing next to the machine exhibits specially performed demonstrations for Prince Sihanouk. After the visit, Prince Sihanouk expressed warm praise for the exhibits, saying, "You are already able to manufacture the machines needed by your country. Your machines are very precise, and your technology has reached the level of advanced countries. I express my admiration and congratulations for your achievements." The person in charge of the exhibition expressed deep gratitude for Prince Sihanouk's encouragement and praise.

　　This morning, Vice-Chairman of the Cambodian Throne Supreme Council, Penn Nouth, and Prime Minister Phou Proeung, accompanied by Wang Youping, the Chinese Ambassador to Cambodia, visited the Palace Museum.

### 西哈努克亲王参观工业交通展览会

1960-12-19
第1版()
专栏：

    西哈努克亲王参观工业交通展览会
    新华社18日讯　柬埔寨国家元首西哈努克亲王和柬埔寨其他贵宾由乌兰夫副总理陪同，今天下午参观了全国工业交通展览会。
    西哈努克亲王乘车到达展览会会馆的时候，有五百多名工作人员齐集在大门口两边热烈鼓掌欢迎。在门前迎接柬埔寨贵宾的展览会总馆长冀朝鼎，副总馆长勇龙桂、汪家宝、王彬等引导西哈努克亲王和其他贵宾们，仔细参观了机械馆、轻工业馆、纺织工业馆、原子能馆、冶金工业馆和西广场上的各种中国产的汽车及农业机械等展品。贵宾们走进每一个展览厅的时候，都响起了热烈的掌声。一些站在机器展品旁边的工作人员，专门为西哈努克亲王做了操作表演。西哈努克亲王在参观结束以后，对展览会的展品表示热烈的赞扬，他说，“你们已经能够制造自己国家所需要的机器了，你们的机器是很精密的，你们的技术已经达到先进国家的水平，我对你们的成就表示钦佩和祝贺。”展览会的负责人对西哈努克亲王的鼓励和赞扬表示深切的感谢。
    今天上午，柬埔寨王位最高委员会副主席宾努和首相福·波伦由我国驻柬埔寨大使王幼平陪同，参观了故宫博物院。
