Title: Defying World Public Opinion, France Conducts Another Atomic Explosion Test
Author:
Date: 1960-12-28
Page: 6

    Defying World Public Opinion
    France Conducts Another Atomic Explosion Test
    Xinhua News Agency, Paris, December 27th. According to an announcement by the French government, France conducted its third atomic explosion test in the Sahara atomic test site this morning. France's first and second atomic explosions were carried out on February 13th and April 1st this year, respectively. These two tests were universally condemned by African and world public opinion. Many African countries demanded a ban on nuclear weapons testing in Africa.

### 不顾世界舆论反对  法国又作原子爆炸试验

1960-12-28
第6版()
专栏：

    不顾世界舆论反对
    法国又作原子爆炸试验
    新华社巴黎27日电　据法国政府宣布，今天上午法国在撒哈拉的原子试验场进行了第三次原子爆炸试验。法国的第一、二两次原子爆炸是在今年2月13日和4月1日分别进行的。这两次试验遭到了非洲和世界舆论的普遍谴责。许多非洲国家要求禁止在非洲进行核武器试验。
