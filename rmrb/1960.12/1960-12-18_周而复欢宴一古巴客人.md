Title: Zhou Erfu Hosts Banquet for Cuban Guest

Author:

Date: 1960-12-18
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, December 17th. Zhou Erfu, Vice President of the Chinese-Latin American Friendship Association, hosted a banquet today in honor of Penido Sanchez Diago, the organizational secretary of the Cuban Mayari Province Trade Union. Among those attending the banquet were Ren Yinglun, Executive Director of the Chinese-Latin American Friendship Association, and individuals who had previously visited Cuba. The Cuban Ambassador to China, Oscar Pino Santos, also attended the banquet.

Penido Sanchez Diago arrived in Beijing by plane on the 16th at the invitation of the Chinese-Latin American Friendship Association.

### 周而复欢宴一古巴客人

1960-12-18
第4版()
专栏：

    周而复欢宴一古巴客人
    新华社17日讯　中国拉丁美洲友好协会副会长周而复今天设宴欢迎古巴马圭省总工会组织书记佩尼多·桑契斯·迪亚哥。参加宴会的，有中国拉丁美洲友好协会常务理事任映仑和曾经访问过古巴的人士等。古巴驻华大使奥斯卡·皮诺·桑托斯也出席了宴会。
    佩尼多·桑契斯·迪亚哥应中国拉丁美洲友好协会邀请，在16日乘飞机到达北京。
