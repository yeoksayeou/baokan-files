Title: Shanghai Yue Opera Troupe Arrives in Hong Kong
Author:
Date: 1960-12-22
Page: 4

    Xinhua News Agency, Hong Kong, December 21st: The Shanghai Yue Opera Troupe, led by its head Bai Yan and deputy head Yuan Xuefen, arrived in Hong Kong on the 20th and will soon perform at the Puqing and Gaosheng Theaters in Hong Kong.
    When the Shanghai Yue Opera Troupe arrived in Hong Kong by train on the afternoon of the 20th, they were warmly welcomed by over a hundred well-known figures from all walks of life in Hong Kong, including He Xian, Gao Zhuoxiong, Chen Yaocai, Wu Dizhou, Chen Pishi, Fei Yimin, Tong Chuxi, and Xia Meng. Liang Weilin, director of the Hong Kong branch of the Xinhua News Agency, also greeted them at the station.
    In the evening, He Xian, chairman of the Puqing and Gaosheng Theaters in Hong Kong, hosted a banquet to entertain all members of the Shanghai Yue Opera Troupe. More than 400 people from all walks of life in Hong Kong attended.

### 上海越剧团到达香港

1960-12-22
第4版()
专栏：

    上海越剧团到达香港
    据新华社香港21日电　上海越剧团在团长白彦、副团长袁雪芬率领下，已于20日到达香港，即将在香港的普庆、高升两戏院演出。
    上海越剧团20日下午乘火车到达香港时，受到香港各界知名人士何贤、高卓雄、陈耀材、吴荻舟、陈丕士、费彝民、僮楚希、夏梦等一百多人的热烈欢迎。新华通讯社香港分社社长梁威林也到车站迎接。
    当晚，香港普庆、高升两戏院董事长何贤设宴招待上海越剧团全体团员。香港各界人士四百多人出席参加。
