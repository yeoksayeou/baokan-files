Title: Chinese Parachuting and Aeromodelling Athletes Break Three World Records
Author:
Date: 1960-12-13
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency, December 12th: Chinese parachuting and aeromodelling athletes broke one parachuting world record and two aeromodelling world records in Beijing in November.

On November 17th, during a parachuting competition held in Liangxiang, a suburb of Beijing, female athletes He Jianhua, Zhang Minlan, and Mei Yan broke the world record in the women's daytime 1,500-meter team precision parachute jump with an average distance of 4.645 meters from the bullseye, surpassing the previous record of 5.72 meters set by Soviet athletes on May 7, 1959. On that day, the individual distances of the three women from the bullseye were as follows: He Jianhua, 21 years old, achieved the best result with 3.24 meters; Zhang Minlan, 21 years old, was second with 5.20 meters; and Mei Yan, 22 years old, was 5.495 meters from the bullseye.

The two aeromodelling world records were also broken in Liangxiang. During an aeromodelling record attempt on November 4th, Zhang Fusheng, 20 years old, flew a piston engine helicopter model aircraft from Liangxiang and landed in Zangtun Commune, Jinghai County, near Tianjin, achieving a straight-line flight distance of 137.5 kilometers, breaking the world record of 91.5 kilometers set by Sun Jingqiao, a student at the Beijing Institute of Aeronautics, in May of this year.

In another aeromodelling record attempt on November 11th, Huang Yongliang and Liu Jiajun's radio-controlled piston engine model aircraft reached a flight altitude of 2,080 meters, breaking the world record of 1,260 meters set by Chinese aeromodelling athletes Wang Yongxi and Zhao Jiazhen at the First National Games last year.

National record holders Zhong Guohua and Wang Chongming also broke their own national record of 48 minutes and 50 seconds for the airtime of radio-controlled piston engine model aircraft, which they had set at the First National Games last year, with a time of 1 hour and 32.8 seconds in Liangxiang on November 17th.

### 我国跳伞和航空模型选手  打破三项世界纪录

1960-12-13
第4版()
专栏：

    我国跳伞和航空模型选手
    打破三项世界纪录
    新华社12日讯　我国的跳伞和航空模型选手，11月份在北京又打破了一项跳伞世界纪录和两项航空模型世界纪录。
    11月17日在北京郊区良乡举行的一次跳伞对抗赛中，女运动员赫建华、张敏兰、梅严在女子日间一千五百米集体定点跳伞中，以平均距靶心四米六四五的成绩，打破了苏联运动员在1959年5月7日创造的这个项目平均距靶心五米七十二的世界纪录。这天，她们三人个人与靶心的距离以二十一岁的赫建华最好，是三米二十四；二十一岁的张敏兰其次，是五米二十；二十二岁的梅严距靶心五米四九五。
    两项航空模型世界纪录也是在良乡被打破的。在11月4日举行的一次航空模型创纪录测验中，二十岁的张涪生的活塞式发动机直升模型飞机由良乡起飞后，当天降落在天津附近的静海县臧屯公社，直线飞行距离达到了一百三十七点五公里，打破了北京航空学院学生孙景桥在今年5月间创造的九十一点五公里的世界纪录。
    在11月11日举行的另一次航空模型创纪录测验中，黄永良、刘嘉军的无线电操纵活塞式发动机模型飞机飞行高度达到了两千零八十米，打破了我国航空模型选手王永熙、赵嘉桢在去年第一届全国运动会上创造的这个项目一千二百六十米的世界纪录。
    全国纪录保持者钟国华和王崇明，11月17日在良乡也以一小时三十二秒八的成绩，打破了他们两人在去年第一届全国运动会上创造的无线电操纵活塞式发动机模型飞机留空时间四十八分五十秒的全国纪录。
