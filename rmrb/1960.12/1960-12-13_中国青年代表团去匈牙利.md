Title: Chinese Youth Delegation to Hungary
Author:
Date: 1960-12-13
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, December 12th: At the invitation of the Hungarian Communist Youth League, a three-member Chinese youth delegation, led by Lu Zhao, member of the Standing Committee of the Central Committee of the Communist Youth League, left Beijing today for Budapest to attend the Second Congress of the Hungarian Communist Youth League.

### 中国青年代表团去匈牙利

1960-12-13
第4版()
专栏：

    中国青年代表团去匈牙利
    新华社12日讯　应匈牙利共产主义青年团邀请，中国青年代表团一行三人，在团长、共青团中央常委鲁钊率领下，今天离开北京前往布达佩斯参加匈牙利共青团第二次代表大会。
