Title: Nehru Agrees to Resume Activities of the International Commission for Laos
Author:
Date: 1960-12-21
Page: 6
　　
　　Xinhua News Agency, Hanoi, December 19th: It was reported here today that the Indian Acting Consul General in Hanoi, Anji, notified the Ministry of Foreign Affairs of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam on December 16th that Prime Minister Nehru had carefully studied Prime Minister Pham Van Dong's letter to Indian Prime Minister Nehru on December 11, 1960, regarding the Laotian situation. On December 14th, he formally contacted the two chairmen of the Geneva Conference, reminding them of the dangerous situation in Laos and suggesting the resumption of the activities of the International Commission for Laos in order to create favorable conditions for easing the tension in Laos and restoring peace in Laos.
　　The Ministry of Foreign Affairs of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam notified the Government of the Republic of India on December 17th that the Government of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam expressed great concern about the current situation in Laos, and the Government of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam suggested reconvening the conference of participating countries of the 1954 Geneva Conference and resuming the activities of the International Commission for Laos.

### 尼赫鲁赞同恢复老挝国际委员会活动

1960-12-21
第6版()
专栏：

    尼赫鲁赞同恢复老挝国际委员会活动
    新华社河内19日电　此间今天报道，印度驻河内代理总领事安吉12月16日通知越南民主共和国外交部，关于范文同总理在1960年12月11日就老挝局势写给印度总理尼赫鲁的信，尼赫鲁总理已经仔细研究，并在12月14日同日内瓦会议两位主席正式联系，提醒两位主席注意老挝危险的局势，并建议恢复老挝国际委员会的活动，以便为缓和老挝紧张局势和为恢复老挝的和平创造顺利条件。
    越南民主共和国外交部在12月17日通知印度共和国政府，越南民主共和国政府对老挝当前局势表示非常担忧，越南民主共和国政府建议重新召开1954年日内瓦会议参加国会议和恢复老挝国际委员会的活动。
