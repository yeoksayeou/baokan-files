Title: Guangdong Province Second People's Congress Holds Third Session
Author:
Date: 1960-12-08
Page: 3

Xinhua News Agency, Guangzhou, Guangdong Province, December 3rd: The Third Session of the Second People's Congress of Guangdong Province was held in Guangzhou from November 28th to December 3rd.

Chen Yu, Governor of Guangdong Province, delivered a report on the work of the Provincial People's Committee at the meeting. Ji Jinzhang, Deputy Director of the Provincial Finance and Trade Committee and concurrently Director of the Provincial Department of Finance, delivered a report on the 1959 Guangdong Province final accounts and the 1960 Guangdong Province draft budget. The meeting, after careful discussion, adopted the above-mentioned reports and passed corresponding resolutions.

Zhu Guang, the former Vice Governor of Guangdong Province and concurrently Mayor of Guangzhou, could not continue in his current position due to work transfer. The meeting elected Zeng Sheng as Vice Governor of Guangdong Province.

The First Session of the Fourth People's Congress of Guangzhou City was also held from November 29th to December 3rd. The meeting elected Zeng Sheng as Mayor of Guangzhou, and Jiao Linyi, Liang Xiang, Li Langru, Sun Leyi, Zhang Ruiquan, Du Zhenxiang, Zhong Ming, Li Guangxiang, Yang Yi, Luo Peiyuan, and Lin Xi as Deputy Mayors.

### 广东省第二届人代会  举行第三次会议

1960-12-08
第3版()
专栏：

    广东省第二届人代会
    举行第三次会议
    新华社广州电　广东省第二届人民代表大会第三次会议11月28日到12月3日在广州举行。
    广东省省长陈郁在会上作了省人民委员会工作报告，省财贸委员会副主任兼财政厅厅长纪锦章作了关于1959年广东省决算和1960年广东省预算草案的报告。会议经过认真讨论通过了上述报告，并且通过了相应的决议。
    原任广东省副省长兼广州市市长朱光，因工作调动不能继续担任现职，会议补选曾生为广东省副省长。
    广州市第四届人民代表大会第一次会议也在11月29日到12月3日举行。会议选举曾生为广州市市长，焦林义、梁湘、李朗如、孙乐宜、张瑞权、杜祯祥、钟明、李广祥、杨毅、罗培元、林西为副市长。
