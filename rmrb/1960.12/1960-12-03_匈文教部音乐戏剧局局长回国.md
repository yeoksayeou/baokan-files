Title: Head of the Hungarian Ministry of Culture and Education's Music and Drama Bureau Returns Home
Author:
Date: 1960-12-03
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, December 2nd: Mr. Malyuk, Head of the Music and Drama Bureau of the Hungarian Ministry of Culture and Education, concluded his visit to our country and left Beijing by plane today to return home. Zhou Weizhi, Director of the Art Bureau of the Ministry of Culture, and others saw him off at the airport. Xia Yan, Vice Minister of Culture, hosted a banquet on the 1st to bid farewell to the guest.

### 匈文教部音乐戏剧局局长回国

1960-12-03
第4版()
专栏：

    匈文教部音乐戏剧局局长回国
    新华社2日讯　匈牙利文化教育部音乐戏剧局局长麦鲁克结束了在我国的访问，今天乘飞机离开北京回国。文化部艺术局局长周巍峙等到机场送行。文化部副部长夏衍1日曾设宴欢送客人。
