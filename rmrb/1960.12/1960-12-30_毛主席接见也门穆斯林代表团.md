Title: Chairman Mao Meets with the Yemeni Muslim Delegation
Author:
Date: 1960-12-30
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, December 29th: Chairman Mao Zedong received the Yemeni Muslim delegation headed by Said Ahmed ben Muhammad Zabarah this evening and had a friendly conversation with them. Also present at the reception were Burhan, Vice Chairman of the National Committee of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference and Chairman of the China Islamic Association, and He Ying, Director of the West Asia and Africa Department of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs. (Attached picture)
　　Chairman Mao and the Yemeni Muslim delegation in a group photo. The third person from the left is Said Ahmed ben Muhammad Zabarah. Photo by Hou Bo, Xinhua News Agency reporter.

### 毛主席接见也门穆斯林代表团

1960-12-30
第1版()
专栏：

    毛主席接见也门穆斯林代表团
    新华社29日讯　毛泽东主席今晚接见了以萨义德·艾哈默德·本·穆罕默德·扎巴拉为首的也门穆斯林代表团，并且同他们进行了友好的谈话。接见时在座的，有政协全国委员会副主席、中国伊斯兰教协会主任包尔汉，外交部西亚非洲司司长何英等。（附图片）
    毛主席和也门穆斯林代表团合影。左起第三人是萨义德·艾哈默德·本·穆罕默德·扎巴拉　新华社记者　侯　波摄
