Title: Ecuadorian Student Representatives Return Home
Author:
Date: 1960-12-13
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, December 12th: Ecuadorian student representatives Jorge Galarza Sanchez and two others, having concluded their visit to our country, departed Beijing today to return home.

### 厄瓜多尔学生代表回国

1960-12-13
第4版()
专栏：

    厄瓜多尔学生代表回国
    新华社12日讯　厄瓜多尔学生代表豪尔赫·加拉尔萨·桑切斯等三人结束了在我国的访问，今天离开北京回国。
