Title: CPSU Central Committee to Hold Plenary Session on January 10th Next Year
Author:
Date: 1960-12-26
Page: 5

    CPSU Central Committee to Hold Plenary Session on January 10th Next Year
    Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, December 25th: TASS, Moscow, December 25th: The Central Committee of the Communist Party of the Soviet Union has decided to convene a plenary session of the CPSU Central Committee on January 10, 1961.

### 苏共中央定明年一月十日召开全会

1960-12-26
第5版()
专栏：

    苏共中央定明年一月十日召开全会
    新华社25日讯　塔斯社莫斯科25日讯：苏联共产党中央委员会决定于1961年1月10日召开苏共中央全会。
