Title: Russian Basketball Teams Play in Shenyang and Changchun
Author:
Date: 1960-12-02
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency Report:

    Russian basketball teams from the Soviet Union played friendly matches against the Liaoning basketball teams in Shenyang on the evening of November 28th. The visiting men's team won 90-73, while the visiting women's team lost 48-53.
    
    Xinhua News Agency, Changchun, December 1st: The Russian basketball teams from the Soviet Union achieved victories in both friendly matches held in Changchun on the evening of November 30th. The men's team won 86-58, and the women's team won 69-56.

### 俄罗斯篮球队在沈阳长春比赛

1960-12-02
第4版()
专栏：

    俄罗斯篮球队在沈阳长春比赛
    据新华社讯　苏联俄罗斯联邦男女篮球队11月28日晚在沈阳同辽宁男女篮球队进行了两场友谊比赛。客队男队以九十比七十三胜，客队女队以四十八比五十三败。
    据新华社长春1日电　苏联俄罗斯联邦男女篮球队11月30日晚在长春进行的两场友谊比赛中双双获胜，男子队以八十六比五十八胜，女子队以六十九比五十六胜。
