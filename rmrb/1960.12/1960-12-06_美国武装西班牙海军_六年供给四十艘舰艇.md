Title: The United States Arms the Spanish Navy: Forty Ships Supplied Over Six Years
Author:
Date: 1960-12-06
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, Boston, 4th: According to the U.S. newspaper *The Christian Science Monitor*, the United States has given or lent forty various military ships to Franco's Spain since 1954.

The newspaper pointed out that the United States is also arming the Spanish Navy with modern equipment, supplying it with various cannons, communications equipment, and radar, and has drafted a special plan stipulating $42.2 million to改造 twenty-nine Spanish ships. Fourteen of these have already been rebuilt.

### 美国武装西班牙海军  六年供给四十艘舰艇

1960-12-06
第6版()
专栏：

    美国武装西班牙海军
    六年供给四十艘舰艇
    新华社4日讯　波士顿消息：据美国《基督教科学箴言报》报道，自从1954年以来，美国已给予或者借给佛朗哥西班牙各种军用舰艇四十艘。
    这家报纸指出，美国还正在用现代化装备武装西班牙海军，供给它各种大炮、通讯设备和雷达等，并且拟定了一项专门计划，规定用四千二百万美元来改造二十九艘西班牙舰艇。其中十四艘已改造完毕。
