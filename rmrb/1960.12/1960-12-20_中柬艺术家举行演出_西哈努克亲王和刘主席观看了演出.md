Title: Chinese and Cambodian Artists Hold Performance; Prince Sihanouk and Chairman Liu Watched the Performance
Author:
Date: 1960-12-20
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, December 19th: This afternoon, after the people of the capital held a rally to welcome Cambodian Head of State Prince Norodom Sihanouk and celebrate the signing of the Sino-Cambodian Treaty of Friendship and Mutual Non-Aggression, Chinese and Cambodian artists held a grand artistic performance at the Great Hall of the People.

Prince Norodom Sihanouk and Chairman Liu Shaoqi watched the performance by Chinese and Cambodian artists together.

Also watching the performance were Prince Sihanouk's wife, princesses, and princes, Cambodian Supreme Privy Council Vice-Chairman Penn Nouth, Prime Minister Pho Proeung, First Deputy Prime Minister Nhiek Tioulong, and other distinguished guests.

Vice-Chairman Dong Biwu, Chairman Zhu De, Premier Zhou Enlai, Vice-Premier Chen Yi, Mayor Peng Zhen, and other leaders also watched the performance together with the distinguished guests.

The wonderful programs performed by Chinese and Cambodian artists warmly praised the profound friendship between the peoples of China and Cambodia. Chinese artists performed the chorus "Welcome, Prince Sihanouk" and the song "Phnom Penh - Royal Capital," composed by Prince Sihanouk. Artists from the Cambodian Royal Ballet performed excerpts from the Cambodian "Ramayana" ballet and the "Cambodia-China Friendship Dance." In the "Cambodia-China Friendship Dance," each actor held the national flags of China and Cambodia, dancing and cheering for the friendship between the two peoples. At this moment, the audience erupted in thunderous applause, and people were completely immersed in the joy of friendship.

After the performance, Prince Sihanouk and other distinguished Cambodian guests, together with state leaders such as Liu Shaoqi, Dong Biwu, Zhu De, Zhou Enlai, Peng Zhen, and Chen Yi, walked onto the stage amidst music and a storm of applause, warmly shaking hands with the Chinese and Cambodian performers.

### 中柬艺术家举行演出  西哈努克亲王和刘主席观看了演出

1960-12-20
第5版()
专栏：

    中柬艺术家举行演出
    西哈努克亲王和刘主席观看了演出
    新华社19日讯　今天下午，在首都各界人民举行欢迎柬埔寨国家元首诺罗敦·西哈努克亲王、庆祝中柬友好和互不侵犯条约签订大会以后，中国和柬埔寨两国的艺术家在人民大会堂举行了盛大的文艺演出。
    诺罗敦·西哈努克亲王和刘少奇主席一起观看了中柬两国艺术家的表演。
    观看演出的，还有西哈努克亲王的夫人、公主和王子，柬埔寨王位最高委员会副主席宾努，首相福·波伦，第一副首相?·刁隆等贵宾。
    董必武副主席、朱德委员长、周恩来总理、陈毅副总理、彭真市长等领导人，也同贵宾们一起观看演出。
    中柬两国艺术家演出的精彩节目，热烈歌颂了中柬两国人民的深厚友谊。中国艺术家演出了《欢迎您，西哈努克亲王》大合唱和西哈努克亲王所创作的《金边——王都》歌曲。柬埔寨皇家舞蹈团的艺术家表演了柬埔寨《拉玛亚纳》舞剧中的几个片断和《柬中友谊舞》。在《柬中友谊舞》中，每个演员手执中柬两国国旗，一面起舞，一面欢呼两国人民的友谊。这时，全场掌声雷动，人们完全沉浸在友谊的欢乐之中。
    演出结束以后，西哈努克亲王和柬埔寨其他贵宾们，同刘少奇、董必武、朱德、周恩来、彭真、陈毅等国家领导人，在乐曲声和暴风雨般的掌声中一起走上舞台，同中柬两国的演员热烈地握手。
