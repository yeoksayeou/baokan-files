International Briefs

    Hungary and Soviet Union Sign Scientific Cooperation Plan
    Budapest dispatch: The Hungarian Academy of Sciences and the Soviet Academy of Medical Sciences signed a scientific cooperation plan for 1961-1962 in Budapest.
    The plan stipulates that both parties will exchange scientific personnel, mutually invite each other to participate in various congresses and scientific conferences, exchange scientific publications, etc. In the two years, Hungarian and Soviet medical institutions will conduct joint research on approximately twenty scientific topics.
  Czechoslovakia and Soviet Union Conclude Cultural and Scientific Cooperation Plan
    Prague dispatch: Czechoslovakia and the Soviet Union signed the 1961 Czechoslovak-Soviet cultural and scientific cooperation plan on the 23rd.
    According to the plan, Czechoslovakia and the Soviet Union will develop and strengthen cooperation in science, education and health on a larger scale.

### 国际简讯

1960-12-25
第6版()
专栏：国际简讯

    国际简讯
    匈苏签署科学合作计划
    布达佩斯讯　匈牙利科学院和苏联医学科学院在布达佩斯签署了1961—1962年的科学合作计划。
    计划规定双方交换科学工作人员、互相邀请参加各种代表大会和科学会议、交换科学刊物等等。在两年中，匈牙利和苏联的医学机关大约将就二十个科学题目进行共同研究。
  捷苏签订文化科学合作计划
    布拉格讯　捷克斯洛伐克和苏联23日签署了捷苏1961年文化和科学合作计划。
    根据计划，捷克斯洛伐克和苏联之间将在更大的范围内发展和加强科学、教育和卫生方面的合作。
