Title: Soviet Union and North Korea Sign Technical Assistance Agreement
Author:
Date: 1960-12-28
Page: 5

    Xinhua News Agency, Moscow News: The Soviet Union and the Democratic People's Republic of Korea signed an agreement in Moscow on the 24th regarding Soviet technical assistance to Korea in the construction and expansion of a series of industrial enterprises and power station projects from 1961 to 1967, as well as a goods exchange agreement between the Soviet Union and Korea from 1961 to 1965.

    The projects that Korea will newly build and expand include steel industrial enterprises and petroleum processing industrial enterprises.

    In the next five years, the trade volume between the two countries will increase by 80%.

    The Soviet Union will provide Korea with machinery and equipment, cotton, petroleum products, and many other commodities needed for the development of the national economy. The Soviet Union will receive non-ferrous metals, cement, steel, and other commodities from Korea.

    These two documents expanding economic and trade ties were signed respectively by S.A. Skachkov, Chairman of the State Committee for Foreign Economic Relations of the Soviet Council of Ministers, and M.R. Borisov, Deputy Minister of Foreign Trade of the Soviet Union, with Yi Chu-yon, Vice Premier of the Cabinet of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea and Minister of Trade.

### 苏联和朝鲜签订技术援助协定

1960-12-28
第5版()
专栏：

    苏联和朝鲜签订技术援助协定
    新华社讯　据塔斯社莫斯科讯：苏联和朝鲜民主主义人民共和国于24日在莫斯科签订了1961—1967年在新建和扩建一系列工业企业和电站工程中，苏联给予朝鲜技术援助的协定，以及1961—1965年苏联和朝鲜的换货协定。
    朝鲜将要新建和扩建的项目，有钢铁工业企业和石油加工工业企业。
    在今后的五年中，两国之间的贸易额将增加80％。
    苏联将向朝鲜提供机器和设备、棉花、石油制品以及发展国民经济所需要的许多其他商品。苏联将从朝鲜得到有色金属、水泥、钢和其他商品。
    这两个扩大经济和贸易联系的文件，分别由苏联部长会议国家对外经济关系委员会主席斯卡奇科夫和苏联对外贸易部副部长波里索夫同朝鲜内阁副首相兼朝鲜贸易相李周渊签署的。
