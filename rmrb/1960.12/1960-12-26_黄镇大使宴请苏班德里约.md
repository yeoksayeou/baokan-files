Title: Ambassador Huang Zhen Hosts a Banquet for Subandrio
Author:
Date: 1960-12-26
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, Jakarta, December 23rd: Chinese Ambassador to Indonesia Huang Zhen and his wife hosted a banquet tonight for Indonesian Foreign Minister Subandrio and his wife. This banquet was held after the signing of the implementation measures for the treaty between the People's Republic of China and the Republic of Indonesia on the issue of dual nationality.

Also present at the banquet were Minister of Justice Sahardjo, Minister of Internal Affairs Ipik Gandamana and his wife, and Secretary-General of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs Suwito and his wife. The banquet was held in a friendly atmosphere. The hosts and guests frequently toasted to the further consolidation and development of the friendly relations between the two countries.

### 黄镇大使宴请苏班德里约

1960-12-26
第5版()
专栏：

    黄镇大使宴请苏班德里约
    新华社雅加达23日电　中国驻印度尼西亚大使黄镇及其夫人今晚设宴招待印度尼西亚外长苏班德里约及其夫人。这次宴会是在中华人民共和国和印度尼西亚共和国签订关于双重国籍问题的条约的实施办法以后举行的。
    出席宴会的还有司法部长沙哈尔佐、内政部长伊皮克·甘达曼纳及其夫人，外交部秘书长苏维托及其夫人。宴会是在友好的气氛中举行的。宾主双方频频为两国的友好关系的进一步巩固和发展干杯。
