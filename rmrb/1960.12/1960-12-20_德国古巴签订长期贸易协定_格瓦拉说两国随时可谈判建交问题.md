Title: Germany, Cuba Sign Long-Term Trade Agreement; Guevara Says Two Countries Can Negotiate on Establishing Diplomatic Relations at Any Time
Author:
Date: 1960-12-20
Page: 7

    The German Democratic Republic and the Cuban Republic signed a long-term trade agreement for 1961-1965, a trade protocol for 1961, a payment agreement, and a scientific and technical cooperation agreement in Berlin on the 17th.

    This was announced by E. Kerber, Deputy Minister of Foreign Trade and Intra-German Trade of the German Democratic Republic, at a press conference on the 17th.

    According to the agreement and protocol, in 1961, the German Democratic Republic will supply Cuba with seventeen complete sets of industrial equipment and other industrial products. Cuba will supply the German Democratic Republic with goods such as sugar, tobacco, coffee, fruit, and chromium. The total trade volume between the two sides in 1961 will reach 45 million pesos.

    When asked about the possibility of establishing diplomatic relations between the German Democratic Republic and Cuba at a press conference, Major Guevara, head of the Cuban economic delegation, said that both the German Democratic Republic and Cuba are sovereign and independent countries, and they can conduct negotiations at any time on the basis of mutual benefit.

    Xinhua News Agency, December 19th, Moscow dispatch: TASS Moscow dispatch: Guevara, head of the Cuban government economic delegation, returned to Moscow on the 18th after visiting the German Democratic Republic and Hungary.

### 德国古巴签订长期贸易协定  格瓦拉说两国随时可谈判建交问题

1960-12-20
第7版()
专栏：

    德国古巴签订长期贸易协定
    格瓦拉说两国随时可谈判建交问题
    据新华社柏林17日电　德意志民主共和国和古巴共和国之间的1961—1965年长期贸易协定、1961年度贸易议定书、支付协定和科学技术合作协定，17日在柏林签字。
    这是德意志民主共和国对外和东西德贸易部副部长埃·克尔伯17日在一次记者招待会上宣布的。
    根据协定和议定书规定，1961年德意志民主共和国将供给古巴十七部成套工业设备和其他工业品。古巴将供给德意志民主共和国糖、烟草、咖啡、水果和铬等货物。1961年双方的贸易总额将达四千五百万比索。
    古巴经济代表团团长格瓦拉少校在记者招待会上回答关于德意志民主共和国和古巴之间建立外交关系的可能性时说，德意志民主共和国和古巴都是主权独立国家，它们可以随时在互利的基础上进行谈判。
    据新华社19日讯　塔斯社莫斯科讯：古巴政府经济代表团团长格瓦拉在访问了德意志民主共和国和匈牙利之后18日回到了莫斯科。
