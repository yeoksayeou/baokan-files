Title: Yasui Iku Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1960-12-30
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, December 29th: Yasui Iku, director of the Japan Council Against Atomic and Hydrogen Bombs, arrived in Beijing by plane this morning at the invitation of the Chinese People's Committee for World Peace. Bao Erhan, Vice Chairman of the Chinese People's Committee for World Peace, committee member Zhao Anbo, Deputy Secretary-General Wang Mingyuan and others went to the airport to greet him. Japanese peace activist Kinkazu Saionji, who is in Beijing, also went to the airport to greet him.

### 安井郁到达北京

1960-12-30
第4版()
专栏：

    安井郁到达北京
    新华社29日讯　应中国人民保卫世界和平委员会邀请前来我国访问的日本禁止原子弹氢弹协议会理事长安井郁于今天上午乘飞机到达北京。前往机场迎接的，有中国人民保卫世界和平委员会副主席包尔汉、委员赵安博、副秘书长王明远等人。在京的日本和平人士西园寺公一也到机场迎接。
