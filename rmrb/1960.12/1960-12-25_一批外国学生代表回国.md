Title: A Batch of Foreign Student Representatives Return Home
Author:
Date: 1960-12-25
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, December 23rd: The Bolivian student representative Rolando Camacho, the Salvadoran student representative Aristides Augusto Larin, and the Brazilian student representative Niomar Veigas de Carvalho Oliveira, who were invited by the All-China Students' Federation to visit our country, have concluded their visits to China and departed from Beijing for home on the 17th, 22nd, and 23rd, respectively.

### 一批外国学生代表回国

1960-12-25
第4版()
专栏：

    一批外国学生代表回国
    新华社23日讯　应中华全国学生联合会邀请前来我国访问的玻利维亚学生代表罗郎多·卡马乔，萨尔瓦多学生代表阿里斯迪德斯·阿古斯多·拉林和巴西学生代表尼奥马尔·维艾加斯·得·卡尔瓦罗·奥利维罗，结束了在我国的访问，已分别于17、22和23日离京回国。
