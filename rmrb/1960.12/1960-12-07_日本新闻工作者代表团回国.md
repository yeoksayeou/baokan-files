Title: Japanese Journalists Delegation Returns Home
Author:
Date: 1960-12-07
Page: 2

    Xinhua News Agency, Guangzhou, December 6th: The Japanese journalists delegation, consisting of eleven members led by head Kobayashi Yuichi and deputy head Tsuda Tatsuo, left Guangzhou this morning to return to their country.

### 日本新闻工作者代表团回国

1960-12-07
第2版()
专栏：

    日本新闻工作者代表团回国
    据新华社广州6日电　日本新闻工作者代表团一行十一人，在团长小林雄一、副团长津田达夫率领下，今天上午离开广州回国。
