Title: Chairman Liu Shaoqi Watches Song and Dance Performance in Minsk
Author:
Date: 1960-12-07
Page: 2

Xinhua News Agency, Minsk, December 5th: Liu Shaoqi, Vice Chairman of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of China, Chairman of the People's Republic of China, and Head of the Chinese Party and Government Delegation, watched a wonderful performance at the Minsk Opera and Ballet Theater tonight.

Accompanying Chairman Liu Shaoqi were Leonid Brezhnev, member of the Presidium of the CPSU Central Committee and Chairman of the Presidium of the USSR Supreme Soviet; Kirill Mazurov, alternate member of the Presidium of the CPSU Central Committee and First Secretary of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of Belarus; Vasily Kozlov, Chairman of the Presidium of the Supreme Soviet of the Belarusian Soviet Socialist Republic; Tikhon Kiselev, Chairman of the Council of Ministers of the Belarusian Soviet Socialist Republic; Stepan Chervonenko, Soviet Ambassador to China, and others.

Also accompanying Chairman Liu Shaoqi were members of the Chinese Party and Government Delegation Li Jingquan, Lu Dingyi, Yang Shangkun, Liu Ningyi, Liu Xiao and others.

The Chinese, Soviet and Belarusian flags were hung in the theater.

Chairman Liu Shaoqi and others were warmly welcomed with enthusiastic applause from the audience. The band played the national anthems of China, the Soviet Union and Belarus.

After the performance, the Chinese guests presented the performers with flower baskets.

### 刘少奇主席在明斯克观看歌舞

1960-12-07
第2版()
专栏：

    刘少奇主席在明斯克观看歌舞
    新华社明斯克5日电　中国共产党中央委员会副主席、中华人民共和国主席、中国党政代表团团长刘少奇，今天晚上在明斯克歌舞剧院观看了精彩的演出。
    陪同刘少奇主席前往观看的有苏共中央主席团委员、苏联最高苏维埃主席团主席勃列日涅夫，苏共中央主席团候补委员、白俄罗斯共和国共产党中央委员会第一书记马祖罗夫，白俄罗斯共和国最高苏维埃主席团主席瓦·伊·科兹洛夫，白俄罗斯共和国部长会议主席基谢廖夫、苏联驻中国大使契尔沃年科等人。
    随同刘少奇主席前往的还有中国党政代表团团员李井泉、陆定一、杨尚昆、刘宁一、刘晓等人。
    剧院悬挂着中国、苏联和白俄罗斯共和国的国旗。
    刘少奇主席等受到观众的热烈鼓掌欢迎。乐队高奏中国、苏联和白俄罗斯共和国国歌。
    演出结束后，中国贵宾向演员赠送了花篮。
