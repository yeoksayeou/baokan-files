Title: Capital to Hold Grand Rally Today Welcoming Prince Sihanouk and Celebrating the Signing of the Sino-Cambodian Treaty of Friendship and Non-Aggression
Author:
Date: 1960-12-19
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, 18th. People from all walks of life in the capital will hold a grand rally on the 19th to warmly welcome Cambodian Head of State, His Royal Highness Prince Norodom Sihanouk's visit to our country and to celebrate the signing of the Sino-Cambodian Treaty of Friendship and Non-Aggression. The Central People's Broadcasting Station will broadcast the live proceedings of the rally on its first program frequency, starting at 3:50 PM on the 19th.

### 首都今天举行盛会欢迎西哈努克亲王  同时庆祝中柬签订友好和互不侵犯条约

1960-12-19
第1版()
专栏：

    首都今天举行盛会欢迎西哈努克亲王
    同时庆祝中柬签订友好和互不侵犯条约
    新华社18日讯　首都各界人民将在19日举行盛大集会，热烈欢迎柬埔寨国家元首诺罗敦·西哈努克亲王殿下访问我国和庆祝中柬签订友好和互不侵犯条约。中央人民广播电台将从19日下午三时五十分开始，用第一套节目的频率转播大会的实况。
