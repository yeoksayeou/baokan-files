Title: Liu Baiyu Heads to Ceylon
Author:
Date: 1960-12-30
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, 29th. Liu Baiyu, head of the Chinese Writers Delegation attending the Standing Committee meeting of the Asian-African Writers Conference to be held in Colombo in early January 1961, Vice Chairman of the Chinese Writers Association, and Vice Chairman of the Chinese Liaison Committee of the Asian-African Writers Conference, left Beijing by plane today for Colombo. Yang Shuo, a member of the Chinese Writers Delegation, has already arrived in Ceylon. Another member, Han Beiping, will travel there later.

### 刘白羽前往锡兰

1960-12-30
第4版()
专栏：

    刘白羽前往锡兰
    新华社29日讯　出席将于1961年1月初在科伦坡召开的亚非作家会议常设委员会会议的中国作家代表团团长、中国作家协会副主席、亚非作家会议中国联络委员会副主席刘白羽，今天乘飞机离开北京，前往科伦坡。中国作家代表团团员杨朔已先期到达锡兰。另一个团员韩北屏将在以后前往。
