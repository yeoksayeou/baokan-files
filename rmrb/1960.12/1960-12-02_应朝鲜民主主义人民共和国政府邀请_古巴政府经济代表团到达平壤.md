Title: At the Invitation of the Government of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea, Cuban Government Economic Delegation Arrives in Pyongyang
Author:
Date: 1960-12-02
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, Pyongyang, December 1st. At the invitation of the Government of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea, the Cuban Government Economic Delegation, led by Major Ernesto "Che" Guevara, President of the National Bank of Cuba, arrived in Pyongyang by special plane this afternoon. They were warmly welcomed by Korean Deputy Premier Jung Il-ryong and other party and government leaders, as well as 150,000 people from Pyongyang. The welcoming crowds held DPRK and Cuban flags and bouquets, enthusiastically shouting, "Yes to Cuba, no to the Yankees!"

When the special plane carrying Major Guevara and other members of the delegation landed at the snow-covered Pyongyang Airport, the more than three thousand people welcoming them at the airport cheered "Hurrah!" The slogans held by the crowd read: "Yankee Imperialists Get Out of Cuba!" "Yankee Imperialists Get Out of South Korea!" "Long Live Premier Castro!" "Long Live Marshal Kim Il-sung!" etc.

After Major Guevara and delegation member Major Mora, General Manager of the Cuban Foreign Trade Bank, and others disembarked, they warmly shook hands with Deputy Premier Jung Il-ryong and others and received flowers presented by children from Pyongyang. Also present at the airport today to welcome the distinguished Cuban guests were Korean Deputy Premiers Kim Kwang-hyup, Lee Joo-yeon, Vice-Chairman of the Central Committee of the Korean Workers' Party, Park Kum-chul, Minister of Finance Han Sang-doo, President of the Supreme Court Kim Ik-sun, Minister of Foreign Affairs Park Sung-chul, Chairman of the Central Committee of the North Korean Democratic Party Kang Ryang-uk, and Chairman of the Central Committee of the Chondoist Chongu Party Park Shin-duk, and others. Diplomatic envoys from various countries to the Democratic People's Republic of Korea also went to the airport to welcome them.

Deputy Premier Jung Il-ryong and Major Guevara delivered welcome and response speeches at the airport, respectively. Deputy Premier Jung Il-ryong extended a warm welcome to the delegation's arrival. He said that the friendly relations between the people of Korea and Cuba are being strengthened and developed through the common struggle against American imperialism. Both Korea and Cuba stand on the forefront against American imperialism, and the Korean people are as happy about every victory achieved by the Cuban people as if it were their own.

In his reply, Major Guevara warmly praised the victory of the Korean people in defeating American imperialism in the three-year Korean War. He said that we will visit and learn from the achievements and experiences of the respected Korean people in the struggle against American imperialism like a student and bring it to our government and people.

Then, Major Guevara, accompanied by Deputy Premier Jung Il-ryong, rode in an open-top car through the central streets of Pyongyang to his residence. The streets were lined with welcoming crowds holding Korean and Cuban flags and bouquets, who enthusiastically cheered the distinguished guests and shouted the slogan "Yes to Cuba, no to the Yankees."

### 应朝鲜民主主义人民共和国政府邀请  古巴政府经济代表团到达平壤

1960-12-02
第5版()
专栏：

    应朝鲜民主主义人民共和国政府邀请
    古巴政府经济代表团到达平壤
    新华社平壤1日电　应朝鲜民主主义人民共和国政府的邀请，前来这里进行访问的古巴政府经济代表团，在古巴国家银行行长埃尔内斯托·切·格瓦拉少校的率领下，今天下午乘专机到达平壤，受到了朝鲜内阁副首相郑一龙等党政领导人和平壤市十五万名群众的热烈欢迎。欢迎群众举着朝古两国国旗和花束，热情高呼：“要古巴，不要美国佬！”
    格瓦拉少校等乘坐的专机在覆盖着白雪的平壤机场上着陆时，机场上的三千多名欢迎群众一起发出“万岁”的欢呼声。群众拿着的标语上写着：“美帝国主义从古巴滚出去！”“美帝国主义从南朝鲜滚出去！”“卡斯特罗总理万岁！”“金日成元帅万岁！”等。
    格瓦拉少校和代表团团员古巴外贸银行总经理莫拉少校等走下飞机后，同郑一龙副首相等热烈握手，并接受了平壤少年儿童们的献花。今天到机场欢迎古巴贵宾的还有朝鲜内阁副首相金光侠、李周渊、朝鲜劳动党中央委员会副委员长朴金喆、财政相韩相斗、最高裁判所所长金翊善、外务相朴成哲、北朝鲜民主党中央委员会委员长康良煜、天道教青友党中央委员会委员长朴信德等。各国驻朝鲜民主主义人民共和国外交使节也到机场欢迎。
    郑一龙副首相和格瓦拉少校先后在机场上致欢迎词和答词。郑一龙副首相对代表团的到来表示热烈的欢迎。他说，朝鲜和古巴两国人民的友好关系，通过反对美帝国主义的共同斗争，日益加强和发展。朝古两国都站在反对美帝国主义的最前哨，朝鲜人民对古巴人民取得的每一个胜利都当作自己的胜利一样而感到高兴。
    格瓦拉少校致答词时热情地赞扬了朝鲜人民在三年的朝鲜战争中打败了美帝国主义的胜利。他说，我们将像一个学生一样参观和学习尊敬的朝鲜人民在反对美帝国主义的斗争中取得的成就和经验，并把它带给我们的政府和人民。
    然后，格瓦拉少校由郑一龙副首相陪同，乘敞篷汽车，经过平壤市区的中心街道前往寓所，沿途大街两旁排满了手执着朝古两国国旗和花束的欢迎群众，他们向贵宾们热烈欢呼，并高呼“要古巴不要美国佬”的口号。
