Title: Czechoslovakia Loans to the Syrian Region of the United Arab Republic
Author:
Date: 1960-12-23
Page: 6

    Xinhua News Agency, Damascus Electric: The Czechoslovak government has provided a loan of one million pounds to the Syrian region of the United Arab Republic. This loan will be used by the Ministry of Municipal and Rural Affairs of the Syrian region to import machinery and equipment needed for construction projects from Czechoslovakia.
    Tuma Awadallah, Minister of Municipal and Rural Affairs of the Syrian region of the United Arab Republic, and a representative of the Czechoslovak Ministry of Foreign Trade signed the loan agreement here on the 14th.

### 捷克斯洛伐克贷款给阿联叙利亚地区

1960-12-23
第6版()
专栏：

    捷克斯洛伐克贷款给阿联叙利亚地区
    据新华社大马士革电捷克斯洛伐克政府已经向阿联叙利亚地区提供一百万英镑的贷款，供叙利亚地区城乡事务部用来从捷克斯洛伐克进口建设工程所需要的机器和设备。
    阿联叙利亚地区城乡事务部长图马·阿瓦达拉和捷克斯洛伐克对外贸易部代表14日在这里签订了这项贷款协定。
