Title: Prince Sihanouk and Entourage Guest List
Author: 
Date: 1960-12-16
Page: 2

Xinhua News Agency, 15th. The list of distinguished guests in the entourage of Cambodian Head of State Prince Norodom Sihanouk is as follows:

    His Royal Highness Prince Norodom Sihanouk, Head of State of Cambodia
    Princess Soriya Lumsey, wife of Sihanouk and his daughter
    His Excellency Penn Nouth, Vice-Chairman of the Supreme Council of the Throne
    His Excellency Phou Proeung, Prime Minister
    His Excellency Nhiek Tioulong, First Deputy Prime Minister and Minister of Planning
    General Nhiek Tioulong, Chief of Staff of the Royal Cambodian Air Force, Technical Advisor to the Head of State
    Mrs. Nhiek Tioulong
    Mr. Song Yun Xi, Director General of the Planning Department
    Prince Norodom Kantol, Member of the National Assembly
    Mr. Keo Sokan, Member of the National Assembly
    Mr. Hong Kimny, Member of the National Assembly
    Mrs. Hong Kimny
