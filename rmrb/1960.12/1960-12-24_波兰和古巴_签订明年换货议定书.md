Title: Poland and Cuba Sign Goods Exchange Protocol for Next Year
Author:
Date: 1960-12-24
Page: 5

    Poland and Cuba
    Sign Goods Exchange Protocol for Next Year
    Xinhua News Agency, Warsaw, December 23rd: According to today's report in the Polish "People's Tribune," Poland and Cuba signed a goods exchange protocol in Warsaw on the 22nd for 1961. Both sides also decided to extend the trade and payment agreement signed in Havana this March to the end of March 1965.
    The goods exchange protocol stipulates that the trade volume between the two sides in 1961 will be $22.2 million each.

### 波兰和古巴  签订明年换货议定书

1960-12-24
第5版()
专栏：

    波兰和古巴
    签订明年换货议定书
    据新华社华沙23日电　据波兰《人民论坛报》今天报道：波兰和古巴22日在华沙签订了1961年换货议定书。双方并决定将今年3月在哈瓦那签订的贸易和支付协定延长到1965年3月底。
    换货议定书规定，1961年双方的贸易额将各为二千二百万美元。
