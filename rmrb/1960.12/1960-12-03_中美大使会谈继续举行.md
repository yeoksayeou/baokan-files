Title: Sino-US Ambassadorial Talks Continue

Author:
Date: 1960-12-03
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, Warsaw, December 1st. The 102nd meeting of the Sino-US ambassadorial talks was held in Warsaw at 2 p.m. on December 1st.

    The meeting lasted one hour and fifty-five minutes. After the meeting, both sides announced that the next meeting would be held on February 2nd of next year.

### 中美大使会谈继续举行

1960-12-03
第5版()
专栏：

    中美大使会谈继续举行
    新华社华沙1日电　中美大使级会谈第一百零二次会议1日下午二时在华沙举行。
    这次会议历时一小时五十五分钟。会后双方宣布，下次会谈将在明年2月2日举行。
