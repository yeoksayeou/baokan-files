Title: Exhibition of Works by Hungarian Painter Gulácz Opens in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1960-12-28
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency, December 27th. The "Exhibition of Works by Hungarian Painter Gulácz," jointly sponsored by the Commission for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries and the Chinese Artists Association, opened this afternoon at the Water Pavilion in Zhongshan Park, Beijing. This exhibition is being held in accordance with the 1960 implementation plan of the Sino-Hungarian Cultural Cooperation Agreement. Qu Wu, Vice-Chairman of the Commission for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries and President of the Sino-Hungarian Friendship Association, Ye Qianyu, Vice-Chairman of the Chinese Artists Association, and well-known figures from the capital's literary and art circles attended the opening ceremony. The Chargé d'Affaires ad interim of the Hungarian Embassy in China, Szegti, and cultural officials from the embassies of socialist countries in China were also invited to attend the opening ceremony. Painter Gulácz is one of the most talented representatives of contemporary Hungarian artists, and this exhibition features thirty oil, watercolor, and tempera paintings depicting natural landscapes, rural life, and figures.

### 匈画家顾鲁茨作品展览会在京开幕

1960-12-28
第4版()
专栏：

    匈画家顾鲁茨作品展览会在京开幕
    新华社27日讯　对外文化联络委员会和中国美术家协会联合举办的“匈牙利画家顾鲁茨作品展览会”，今天下午在首都中山公园水榭开幕。这个展览会是根据中匈文化合作协定1960年执行计划举办的。对外文化联络委员会副主任、中匈友好协会会长屈武，中国美术家协会副主席叶浅予，以及首都文艺界著名人士参加了开幕式。匈牙利驻中国大使馆临时代办西格蒂和社会主义国家驻华使馆的文化官员也应邀参加了开幕式。画家顾鲁茨是匈牙利当代艺术家中最有才能的代表之一，这次展出了他描绘大自然风景、农村生活和人物的三十幅油水胶颜料画。
