Title: Zanzibar's "People's Daily" Calls on East African Countries to Prevent the Infiltration of the US Dollar
Author:
Date: 1960-12-13
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, Zanzibar News: The Zanzibar Nationalist Party's newspaper, "People's Daily," published an editorial on the 7th, stating that due to the dollar crisis, the US government insists that all countries accepting its "aid" must import goods from the US at prices higher than the international market, attempting to stop the outflow of US dollars. This claim exposes the truth about US economic "aid."

The newspaper warned the people of East African countries to be wary of the infiltration of the US dollar. It said that US "aid" only benefits large US corporations.

### 桑给巴尔《人民报》要求  东非各国防止美元渗入

1960-12-13
第6版()
专栏：

    桑给巴尔《人民报》要求
    东非各国防止美元渗入
    新华社讯　桑给巴尔消息：桑给巴尔民族主义党机关报《人民报》7日发表社论说，由于美元危机，美国政府坚持所有接受它的“援助”的国家都必须以高于国际市场的价格从美国进口货物，妄图制止美元外流，这个主张暴露了美国经济“援助”的真相。
    这家报纸警告东非各国人民要提防美元的渗入。它说，美“援”只有利于美国大企业。
