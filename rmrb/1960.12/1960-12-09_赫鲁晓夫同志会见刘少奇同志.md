Title: Comrade Khrushchev Meets with Comrade Liu Shaoqi
Author: 
Date: 1960-12-09
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, December 8th: TASS Moscow, December 8th: Nikita Khrushchev, First Secretary of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of the Soviet Union and Chairman of the Council of Ministers of the Soviet Union, met on December 7th with Liu Shaoqi, head of the Chinese Party and Government Delegation, Chairman of the People's Republic of China, and Vice Chairman of the Central Committee of the Chinese Communist Party, who is currently visiting the Soviet Union. The meeting took place in a warm and friendly atmosphere.

### 赫鲁晓夫同志会见刘少奇同志

1960-12-09
第1版()
专栏：

    赫鲁晓夫同志会见刘少奇同志
    新华社8日讯　塔斯社莫斯科8日讯：苏联共产党中央委员会第一书记、苏联部长会议主席尼基塔·赫鲁晓夫在12月7日同正在苏联访问的中国党政代表团团长、中华人民共和国主席、中国共产党中央委员会副主席刘少奇举行了会见。这次会见是在热烈而友好的气氛中举行的。
