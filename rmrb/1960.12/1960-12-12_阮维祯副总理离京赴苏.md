Title: Nguyễn Duy Trinh Departs Beijing for the Soviet Union
Author:
Date: 1960-12-12
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, December 11th: Nguyễn Duy Trinh, Deputy Prime Minister and head of the economic and trade delegation of the Government of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam, left Beijing by plane this morning for a visit to the Soviet Union. Deputy Prime Minister Nguyễn Duy Trinh will later return to Beijing to continue his visit to China.

Those who saw him off at the airport today included Bo Yibo, Vice Premier of the State Council; Ye Jizhuang, Minister of Foreign Trade; Li Qiang, Vice Minister of Foreign Trade; Ji Pengfei, Vice Minister of Foreign Affairs, and others.

Also at the airport to see him off today were Tran Tu Binh, Vietnamese Ambassador to China; Sudarikov, Chargé d'Affaires ad interim of the Soviet Embassy in China; Li Ban and Ngo Minh Loan, deputy heads of the Vietnamese government economic and trade delegation in Beijing, and other members of the delegation.

### 阮维祯副总理离京赴苏

1960-12-12
第1版()
专栏：

    阮维祯副总理离京赴苏
    新华社11日讯　越南民主共和国政府经济、贸易代表团团长阮维祯副总理，今天上午乘飞机离开北京前往苏联进行访问。阮维祯副总理日后还将到北京继续在中国进行访问。
    今天到机场欢送的有，国务院副总理薄一波，对外贸易部部长叶季壮、副部长李强，外交部副部长姬鹏飞等。
    越南驻中国大使陈子平，苏联驻中国大使馆临时代办苏达利柯夫，在北京的越南政府经济、贸易代表团副团长李班、吴明鸾和团员，今天也到机场送行。
