Title: Our Youth Delegation Leaves Pyongyang for Home

Author: 
Date: 1960-12-01
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, Pyongyang, November 30th. The Chinese Youth Delegation, led by Li Qitao, Secretary of the Central Committee of the Chinese Communist Youth League, left Pyongyang by train this afternoon to return home after a friendly visit to Korea lasting more than twenty days.

Today, Gao Shangjun, Vice Chairman of the Central Committee of the Korean Democratic Youth League, Han Yishu, Chairman of the Pyongyang Municipal Committee of the Democratic Youth League, Liu Xianglun, Counselor of the Chinese Embassy in Korea, and others saw the delegation off at the station. More than 200 young students from Pyongyang enthusiastically sang "The Song of General Kim Il-sung" and "The East is Red" on the station platform, and shouted slogans such as "Long live the friendship between Korea and China" to bid farewell to the delegation. During their visit to Korea, the delegation visited factories, agricultural cooperatives, construction sites, and schools in Pyongyang, Kaesong, Wonsan, Hamhung, and other places, and was warmly welcomed by the Korean youth everywhere.

Wu Hyun-joo, Chairman of the Central Committee of the Korean Democratic Youth League, hosted a farewell banquet for the Chinese Youth Delegation on the evening of the 29th.

### 我青年代表团离平壤回国

1960-12-01
第5版()
专栏：

    我青年代表团离平壤回国
    新华社平壤30日电　由中国共产主义青年团中央书记处书记李琦涛率领的中国青年代表团，在朝鲜进行了二十多天的友好访问后，今天下午乘火车离平壤回国。
    今天，到车站送行的有朝鲜民主青年同盟中央委员会副委员长高尚俊，民主青年同盟平壤市委员会委员长韩益洙，中国驻朝鲜大使馆参赞刘祥纶等。平壤市的二百多名青年学生也在车站月台上热情地唱着“金日成将军之歌”和“东方红”，并高呼“朝中友谊万岁”的口号来欢送代表团。代表团在访朝期间，先后访问了平壤、开城、元山、咸兴等地的工厂、农业社、建设工地和学校，到处受到了朝鲜青年们的热烈欢迎。
    朝鲜民主青年同盟中央委员会委员长吴铉周在29日晚曾设宴欢送中国青年代表团。
