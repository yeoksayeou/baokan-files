Title: Leaders of North Korean, Hungarian and Polish Delegations Return Home After Attending October Revolution Celebration in the Soviet Union
Author:
Date: 1960-12-04
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, December 3rd: According to TASS, the party and government delegations from North Korea, Hungary, Bulgaria, and Romania, who were invited to Moscow to attend the 43rd anniversary of the Great October Socialist Revolution, have left Moscow and returned to their countries.

	The North Korean Party and Government delegation headed by Kim Il, member of the Standing Committee of the Central Committee of the Workers' Party of Korea and First Deputy Prime Minister of the North Korean Cabinet, and the Hungarian Party and government delegation headed by János Kadar, First Secretary of the Central Committee of the Hungarian Socialist Workers' Party, returned home on the 2nd.

	The Bulgarian Party and Government delegation headed by Todor Zhivkov, First Secretary of the Central Committee of the Bulgarian Communist Party, and the Romanian Party and Government delegation headed by Gheorghe Gheorghiu-Dej, First Secretary of the Central Committee of the Romanian Workers' Party, returned home on the 3rd.

### 在苏联参加十月革命节庆典后  朝匈保罗代表团领导人回国

1960-12-04
第5版()
专栏：

    在苏联参加十月革命节庆典后
    朝匈保罗代表团领导人回国
    新华社3日讯　据塔斯社莫斯科讯：应邀到莫斯科参加十月社会主义革命四十三周年庆典的朝鲜、匈牙利、保加利亚、罗马尼亚四国党政代表团，已经离开莫斯科回国。
    以朝鲜劳动党中央委员会常务委员会委员、朝鲜内阁第一副首相金一为首的朝鲜党政代表团和以匈牙利社会主义工人党中央委员会第一书记卡达尔为首的匈牙利党政代表团是在2日回国的。
    以保加利亚共产党中央委员会第一书记日夫科夫为首的保加利亚党政代表团和以罗马尼亚工人党中央委员会第一书记乔治—乌德治为首的罗马尼亚党政代表团是在3日回国的。
