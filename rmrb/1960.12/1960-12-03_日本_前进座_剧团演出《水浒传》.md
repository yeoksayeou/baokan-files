Title: Japanese "Zenshinza" Troupe Performs "Water Margin"
Author:
Date: 1960-12-03
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, Tokyo: The Japanese progressive theater troupe "Zenshinza" has begun an eighteen-day run of "Water Margin" at the prestigious Tokyo Shinbashi Enbujo Theater. "Zenshinza" utilizes many techniques from Chinese Peking Opera in its performance of this play.

On November 30th, "Zenshinza" invited many news reporters and photographers to view a preview of "Water Margin." The troupe's director, Masakiyo Miyakawa, told reporters that it is very important for Japanese and Chinese dramatists to strengthen cooperation in the arts. The "Zenshinza" troupe stayed in China for about a month at the invitation of the Chinese People's Association for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries.

### 日本“前进座”剧团演出《水浒传》

1960-12-03
第5版()
专栏：

    日本“前进座”剧团演出《水浒传》
    据新华社讯　东京消息：日本进步剧团“前进座”开始在日本第一流剧场东京新桥演舞场演出《水浒传》十八天。“前进座”在演出这个戏剧时大部分运用了中国京剧的手法。
    “前进座”11月30日邀请了许多新闻记者和摄影记者来观看《水浒传》的预演。剧团的导演宫川雅清对新闻记者说，日本和中国的戏剧家在艺术方面加强合作是十分重要的。“前进座”剧团应中国人民对外文化协会的邀请在中国逗留了一个月左右。
