Title: Another Batch of Chinese Rice Shipped to Conakry
Author:
Date: 1960-12-28
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, Conakry, December 26th: Another 5,000 tons of Chinese rice recently arrived in Conakry, the capital of Guinea, by sea. Together with the first batch of 5,000 tons of Chinese rice that arrived in Conakry in August of this year, the total amounts to 10,000 tons.

These 10,000 tons of rice are a gift from the government of the People's Republic of China to the government of the Republic of Guinea.

### 我国又一批大米运到科纳克里

1960-12-28
第5版()
专栏：

    我国又一批大米运到科纳克里
    新华社科纳克里26日电　又一批中国大米五千吨最近由海路运到几内亚首都科纳克里，加上今年8月第一批运到科纳克里的五千吨中国大米，共一万吨。
    这一万吨大米是中华人民共和国政府赠送给几内亚共和国政府的。
