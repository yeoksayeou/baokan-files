Title: Soviet Union Airlifts Fuel to Vientiane to Aid Laos
Author:
Date: 1960-12-06
Page: 6
Xinhua News Agency, Hanoi, December 5th. According to a report by the Vietnam News Agency today, the Soviet Union's fuel aid to Laos began to be regularly transported to Laos via the Hanoi-Vientiane route on the 4th.

　　This airlift is being carried out in accordance with the agreement between the Soviet and Laotian governments and with the consent of the government of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam.

　　According to TASS, the first Soviet aircraft carrying out the airlift arrived in Vientiane on December 3. The Soviet airlift personnel were warmly welcomed by the Laotian government and people.

### 苏联援助老挝的燃料空运万象

1960-12-06
第6版()
专栏：

    苏联援助老挝的燃料空运万象
    新华社河内5日电　据越南通讯社今天报道，苏联援助老挝的燃料已在4日开始经由河内—万象航线定期运往老挝。
    这次的空运是根据苏联和老挝政府之间的协定并取得越南民主共和国政府的同意进行的。
    据塔斯社报道，进行空运的第一架苏联飞机在12月3日到达万象。苏联的空运人员受到老挝政府和人民的热烈欢迎。
