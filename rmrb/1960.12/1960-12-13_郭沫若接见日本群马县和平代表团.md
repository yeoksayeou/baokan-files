Title: Guo Moruo Receives Japanese Gunma Prefecture Peace Delegation
Author:
Date: 1960-12-13
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, December 12th. Guo Moruo, Chairman of the China Peace Committee, received the entire Japanese Gunma Prefecture Peace Delegation headed by Ishiguro Inkichi this morning and had friendly talks with them.

Present at the reception were Zhao Puchu, Standing Committee member of the China Peace Committee, and Wu Maosun and Wang Mingyuan, Deputy Secretaries-General.

Japanese peace figure Kinkazu Saionji, who is in Beijing, was also present.

### 郭沫若接见日本群马县和平代表团

1960-12-13
第4版()
专栏：

    郭沫若接见日本群马县和平代表团
    新华社12日讯　中国人民保卫世界和平委员会主席郭沫若今天上午接见了以石黑寅龟为首的日本群马县和平代表团全体成员，并且同他们进行了友好的谈话。
    接见时在座的，有中国人民保卫世界和平委员会常务委员赵朴初，副秘书长吴茂荪、王明远等。
    在北京的日本和平人士西园寺公一也在座。
