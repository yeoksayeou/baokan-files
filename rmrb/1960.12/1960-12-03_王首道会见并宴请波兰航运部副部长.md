Title: Wang Shoudao Meets and Fetes Polish Deputy Minister of Shipping

Author: 
Date: 1960-12-03
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, December 2nd. At the invitation of the Ministry of Communications, the Polish Deputy Minister of Shipping, Wiesław Wiśniewski, arrived in Beijing by plane today for a visit to China.

In the evening, Minister of Communications Wang Shoudao met with the guest. Present at the meeting were Vice Minister of Communications Yu Mei, Director of the Ocean Shipping Bureau of the Ministry of Communications Feng Yujiu, and others. Counselor Furato of the Polish Embassy in China was also present at the meeting. After the meeting, Minister Wang Shoudao hosted a welcome banquet for the guest.

### 王首道会见并宴请波兰航运部副部长

1960-12-03
第4版()
专栏：

    王首道会见并宴请波兰航运部副部长
    新华社2日讯　应交通部邀请来我国访问的波兰航运部副部长维斯?夫斯基今天乘飞机到达北京。
    晚上，交通部部长王首道会见了客人，会见时在座的有交通部副部长于眉、交通部远洋运输局局长冯于九等。会见时，波兰驻中国大使馆参赞傅拉托也在座。会见后，王首道部长为客人举行了欢迎宴会。
