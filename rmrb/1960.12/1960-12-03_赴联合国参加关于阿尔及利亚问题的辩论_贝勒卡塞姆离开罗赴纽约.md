Title: Belkacem Leaves Cairo for New York to Attend UN Debate on Algerian Question
Author:
Date: 1960-12-03
Page: 6
Xinhua News Agency, Cairo, December 1st:　Deputy Prime Minister and Foreign Minister of the Provisional Government of the Algerian Republic, Krim Belkacem, left here this morning for New York to attend the United Nations debate on the Algerian question. Before his departure, he said, "The Algerian question is part of the overall struggle to eliminate imperialism in the world and is a major issue facing the United Nations."
    Krim Belkacem said, "The task of the Algerian delegation in attending this meeting is to safeguard the right of the Algerian people to independence and to expose the French government's conspiracy to create suspicion about the goals of our struggle and attempt to impose a new type of imperialist rule in Algeria." He also said, "The goals of the Algerian revolution will surely be realized, and the Algerian people are determined to achieve their independence through armed struggle."

### 赴联合国参加关于阿尔及利亚问题的辩论  贝勒卡塞姆离开罗赴纽约

1960-12-03
第6版()
专栏：

    赴联合国参加关于阿尔及利亚问题的辩论
    贝勒卡塞姆离开罗赴纽约
    新华社开罗1日电　阿尔及利亚共和国临时政府副总理兼外交部长克里姆·贝勒卡塞姆今天上午离开这里前往纽约参加联合国关于阿尔及利亚问题的辩论。他在临行前发表谈话说，“阿尔及利亚问题是在世界上消灭帝国主义的全面斗争的一部分，是摆在联合国面前的主要问题。”
    克里姆·贝勒卡塞姆说，“阿尔及利亚代表团出席这次会议的任务是维护阿尔及利亚人民的独立权利，并将揭露法国政府目的在于对我们斗争的目标制造猜疑、企图在阿尔及利亚强行新型的帝国主义统治的阴谋。”他还说，“阿尔及利亚革命的目的必将实现，阿尔及利亚人民决心通过武装斗争来实现他们的独立。”
