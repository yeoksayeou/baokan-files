Title: Chinese Medical Workers Leave Pakistan for Home

Author: 
Date: 1960-12-08
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, Karachi, December 6th: Wei Mingzhong, Vice President of Xi'an Medical College, and Wu Decheng, attending physician of Beijing Union Medical College Hospital, left here this morning to return home after completing their visit to Pakistan.

They came here on November 19th at the invitation of the Pakistan Medical Association to attend the Sixth All-Pakistan Medical Conference. During their stay here, they visited Karachi and Lahore, the capital of West Pakistan. At the Sixth All-Pakistan Medical Conference, Dr. Wu Decheng read a scientific paper on urethral tuberculosis and kidney surgery. The doctors attending the conference listened to the paper with great interest and warmly applauded the achievements of Chinese medical workers in medical scientific research. A professor at Dow Medical College in Karachi spoke at the meeting, praising the paper warmly. Many Pakistani doctors and medical students visited them at the hotel and asked them about the development of medical services in China.

### 我医学工作者离巴基斯坦回国

1960-12-08
第6版()
专栏：

    我医学工作者离巴基斯坦回国
    据新华社卡拉奇6日电　中国西安医学院副院长魏明中和北京协和医院主治医师吴德诚在结束对巴基斯坦的访问后，今天上午离开这里回国。
    他们是在11月19日应巴基斯坦医学协会的邀请前来参加第六届全巴基斯坦医学会议的。他们在这里逗留期间，曾访问了卡拉奇和西巴基斯坦首府拉合尔。在第六届全巴基斯坦医学会议上，吴德诚医师宣读了一篇关于尿道结核症和肾脏手术的科学论文，参加会议的医生们以极大的兴趣听取了这篇论文，并热烈鼓掌祝贺中国医务工作者在医学科学研究工作方面的成就。卡拉奇杜伍医学院的一位教授在会上讲话时，热烈赞扬了这篇论文。许多巴基斯坦医生和医学院学生到旅馆里去访问，并向他们了解中国医疗事业发展情况。
