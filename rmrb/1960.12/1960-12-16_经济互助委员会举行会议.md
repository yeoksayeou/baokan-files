Title: Council for Mutual Economic Assistance Holds Meeting
Author:
Date: 1960-12-16
Page: 5

    Xinhua News Agency, December 15th: According to a report from the Romanian News Agency, the Council for Mutual Economic Assistance, composed of the Soviet Union and other European socialist countries, held a meeting of directors of scientific research and design institutes of the coal industry in Bucharest from December 7th to 11th. This meeting was organized by the Standing Committee on Coal Issues of the Council for Mutual Economic Assistance.

    Representatives from Bulgaria, Hungary, the German Democratic Republic, Poland, Romania, the Soviet Union, and Czechoslovakia participated in the meeting.

    The meeting discussed the work of the scientific research and design institutes of the coal industry of the member countries of the Council for Mutual Economic Assistance in 1960 and formulated a draft plan for cooperation in 1961.

### 经济互助委员会举行会议

1960-12-16
第5版()
专栏：

    经济互助委员会举行会议
    新华社15日讯　据罗马尼亚通讯社报道，由苏联和欧洲其他社会主义国家组成的经济互助委员会于12月7日到11日在布加勒斯特举行了煤炭工业科学研究和设计研究所所长会议。这次会议是由经济互助委员会煤炭问题常设委员会组织的。
    参加会议的有保加利亚、匈牙利、德意志民主共和国、波兰、罗马尼亚、苏联和捷克斯洛伐克等国的代表。
    会议讨论了经济互助委员会成员国煤炭工业科学研究和设计研究所1960年的工作情况，并制定了1961年合作计划草案。
