Title: Somali Youth Delegation Arrives in Beijing
Author: 
Date: 1960-12-06
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, 5th. A three-member Somali youth delegation headed by Abukar Haji Mou, chairman of the Somali Progressive Youth League, arrived in Beijing by train this morning for a visit to China at the invitation of the All-China Youth Federation. Greeting them at the station was Qian Dawei, Deputy Director of the International Liaison Department of the All-China Youth Federation, and others.

### 索马里青年代表团到京

1960-12-06
第4版()
专栏：

    索马里青年代表团到京
    新华社5日讯　以索马里进步青年联盟主席阿布卡尔·哈吉·茅为首的索马里青年代表团一行三人，应中华全国青年联合会邀请来我国访问，在今天上午乘火车到达北京。到车站欢迎的有中华全国青年联合会国际联络部副部长钱大卫等。
