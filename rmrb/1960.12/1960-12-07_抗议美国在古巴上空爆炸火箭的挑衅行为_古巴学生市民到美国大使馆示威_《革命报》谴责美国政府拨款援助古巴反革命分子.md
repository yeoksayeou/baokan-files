Title: Protesting the Provocative Act of the U.S. Exploding a Rocket Over Cuba; Cuban Students and Citizens Demonstrate at the U.S. Embassy; "Revolution" Condemns the U.S. Government's Appropriation to Aid Cuban Counter-Revolutionaries
Author:
Date: 1960-12-07
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, Havana, December 5th – The incident of the United States deliberately exploding a rocket over Cuba continues to be condemned by the Cuban people.

Hundreds of students, cooperative members, and ordinary residents held a protest demonstration in front of the U.S. Embassy on the 4th. Demonstrators shouted: "Cuba yes, Yankees no!" "Yankees get out!"

The U.S. rocket exploded last week in the Holguín region of Oriente Province, its debris killing a cow belonging to the National Institute of Agrarian Reform.

Demonstrators held a "symbolic burial" for the cow in front of the U.S. Embassy.

On the same day, students also organized similar demonstrations in Santa Clara and other parts of Cuba.

According to newspaper reports, the United States has given the Cuban government a note, shamelessly demanding the return of the wreckage of the rocket that fell in Cuba to the United States. This incident has further aroused the great anger of the Cuban people. The Federation of University Students has issued a protest statement regarding this matter.

Xinhua News Agency, Havana, December 4th – Cuba's "Revolution" newspaper condemned the Eisenhower government's open assistance to the aggressive acts of Cuban counter-revolutionaries in the United States in an editorial on the 3rd.

The editorial condemned Eisenhower's decision to allocate one million dollars to aid Cuban counter-revolutionaries in Miami, Florida, in the United States. The editorial pointed out that this decision by the U.S. government is clearly an indisputable act of aggression against Cuba. The U.S. government has turned Florida into a base for mercenaries and secret agents maintained by the funds of the U.S. Treasury. No government has degenerated to such a degree as the United States, and no government has shown such moral depravity and recklessness.

"The World" newspaper, in condemning the imperialist robber logic, said that while the United States is accusing Cuba of promoting revolutions against dictatorial regimes in the Americas, it is brutally sending aircraft carriers to the Caribbean and openly allocating funds to help aggression against neighboring countries. This exposes its aggressive true face before the whole world.

### 抗议美国在古巴上空爆炸火箭的挑衅行为  古巴学生市民到美国大使馆示威  《革命报》谴责美国政府拨款援助古巴反革命分子

1960-12-07
第6版()
专栏：

    抗议美国在古巴上空爆炸火箭的挑衅行为
    古巴学生市民到美国大使馆示威
    《革命报》谴责美国政府拨款援助古巴反革命分子
    新华社哈瓦那5日电　美国使火箭故意在古巴爆炸一事继续遭到古巴人民的谴责。
    成百的学生、合作社社员和普通居民4日在美国大使馆前面举行了抗议示威。示威者高呼：“要古巴不要美国佬！”“美国佬滚出去！”
    美国火箭是上星期在奥连特省奥尔金地区爆炸的，它的碎片打死了土地改革全国委员会的一头奶牛。
    示威者在美国大使馆前为这头奶牛举行了“象征性的安葬”。
    同一天，在圣克拉拉和古巴的其他地方，学生们也组织了类似的示威游行。
    据报纸报道，美国已经交给古巴政府一个照会，无耻地要求把坠落在古巴的火箭的残片交还给美国。这件事进一步激起了古巴人民的极大愤怒。大学生联合会已经就这件事发表一个抗议声明。
    新华社哈瓦那4日电　古巴《革命报》3日在社论中谴责艾森豪威尔政府公开帮助在美国的古巴反革命分子的侵略行为。
    社论谴责了艾森豪威尔关于拨款一百万美元来援助在美国佛罗里达州迈阿密城的古巴反革命分子的决定。社论指出，美国政府的这个决定显然是对古巴的无可争辩的侵略。美国政府把佛罗里达变成了以美国国库的基金来维持的雇佣兵和特务的基地。任何政府都没有像美国那样堕落到这般地步，任何政府都没有表现出来这样的道德败坏和肆无忌惮。
    《世界报》在谴责帝国主义的强盗逻辑时说，美国在指责古巴促进反对美洲独裁政权的革命的同时，却粗暴地派遣航空母舰到加勒比海，并且公开拨款来帮助对邻国的侵略。这就在全世界面前暴露了它的侵略的真面目。
