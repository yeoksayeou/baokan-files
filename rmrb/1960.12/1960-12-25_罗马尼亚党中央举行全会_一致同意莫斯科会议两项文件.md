Title: Romanian Party Central Committee Holds Plenary Session, Unanimously Approves Two Documents from Moscow Meeting
Author:
Date: 1960-12-25
Page: 6
    Xinhua News Agency, Bucharest, December 23rd. According to Romanian newspapers, the Central Committee of the Romanian Workers' Party held a plenary session on the 19th and 20th.

    Gheorghe Gheorghiu-Dej, First Secretary of the Central Committee of the Romanian Workers' Party, delivered a report at the meeting on the Meeting of Representatives of Communist and Workers' Parties held in Moscow. The plenary session unanimously approved the Statement and the Appeal to the Peoples of the World of the Moscow Meeting. The Central Plenary Session also heard a briefing by Minister of Finance Aurel Vijoli on the draft state budget for 1961, and a report by Nicolae Ceaușescu, member of the Political Bureau of the Central Committee of the Workers' Party, on some suggestions made by the Central Political Bureau for improving the national administrative divisions. The Central Plenary Session decided to submit the draft state budget for 1961 and the suggestions for improving the national administrative divisions to the session of the Romanian Grand National Assembly for discussion.

### 罗马尼亚党中央举行全会  一致同意莫斯科会议两项文件

1960-12-25
第6版()
专栏：

    罗马尼亚党中央举行全会
    一致同意莫斯科会议两项文件
    新华社布加勒斯特23日电　据罗马尼亚报纸报道：罗马尼亚工人党中央委员会19日和20日举行了全体会议。
    罗马尼亚工人党中央委员会第一书记乔治乌—德治在会上作了关于在莫斯科举行的各国共产党和工人党代表会议的报告。全会一致同意莫斯科会议的声明和告世界人民书。中央全会还听取了财政部长奥雷尔·维若利关于1961年国家预算草案的说明和工人党中央政治局委员尼古拉·齐奥塞斯库关于中央政治局就改进国家行政区划的一些建议的报告。中央全会决定把1961年国家预算草案和关于改进国家行政区划的建议将提交罗马尼亚大国民议会会议讨论。
