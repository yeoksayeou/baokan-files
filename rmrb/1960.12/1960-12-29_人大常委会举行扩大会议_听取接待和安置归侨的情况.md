Title: NPC Standing Committee Holds Enlarged Meeting to Hear Report on Reception and Resettlement of Returned Overseas Chinese
Author:
Date: 1960-12-29
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, December 28th: The Standing Committee of the Second National People's Congress held its 34th meeting in enlarged session today to hear a report by Fang Fang, Vice Chairman of the Overseas Chinese Affairs Commission, on the work of receiving and resettling returned overseas Chinese. The meeting held discussions and expressed agreement with the report made by Fang Fang.
	Members of the Standing Committee of the National Committee of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference attended the meeting as observers.

### 人大常委会举行扩大会议  听取接待和安置归侨的情况

1960-12-29
第1版()
专栏：

    人大常委会举行扩大会议
    听取接待和安置归侨的情况
    新华社28日讯　第二届全国人民代表大会常务委员会今天举行第三十四次会议扩大会议，听取华侨事务委员会副主任方方作关于接待和安置归国华侨工作情况的报告。会议进行了讨论，表示同意方方所作的报告。
    政协全国委员会常务委员列席了这次会议。
