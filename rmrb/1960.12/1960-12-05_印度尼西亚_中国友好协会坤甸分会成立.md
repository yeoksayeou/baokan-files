Title: Establishment of the Indonesian-Chinese Friendship Association's Pontianak Branch

Author: 
Date: 1960-12-05
Page: 5

    Xinhua News Agency, Jakarta, December 3rd: According to an Antara News Agency report today, the West Kalimantan Province Pontianak branch of the Indonesian-Chinese Friendship Association has been declared established. Major Bambang Purnomo is the president of the branch.
    There are now branches of the Indonesian-Chinese Friendship Association in fifty regions of Indonesia, including Jakarta, Surabaya, Medan, Bandung, and other major cities, as well as Bali and other islands.

### 印度尼西亚——中国友好协会坤甸分会成立

1960-12-05
第5版()
专栏：

    印度尼西亚——中国友好协会坤甸分会成立
    新华社雅加达3日电　据安塔拉通讯社今天报道，印度尼西亚—中国友好协会西加里曼丹省坤甸分会已宣告成立。班邦·普诺莫少校任该分会的会长。
    现在在印度尼西亚五十个地区有印度尼西亚—中国友好协会的分会，这些地区包括雅加达、泗水、棉兰、万隆和其他大城市以及巴厘和其他岛屿。
