Title: Nyasaland Trade Union Congress General Secretary Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1960-12-23
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, December 22nd:
    Chisiza, General Secretary of the Nyasaland Trade Union Congress, arrived in Beijing by plane today. He is visiting China at the invitation of the All-China Federation of Trade Unions.

    Kang Yonghe, Secretary of the Secretariat of the All-China Federation of Trade Unions, welcomed the guest at the airport.

### 尼亚萨兰工会大会总书记到京

1960-12-23
第4版()
专栏：

    尼亚萨兰工会大会总书记到京
    新华社22日讯　尼亚萨兰工会大会总书记契西萨今天乘飞机到达北京。他是应中华全国总工会邀请来我国访问的。
    中华全国总工会书记处书记康永和到机场欢迎客人。
