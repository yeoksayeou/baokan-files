Title: Prince Sihanouk Arrives in Hangzhou; Zhejiang Governor Holds Banquet to Warmly Welcome Cambodian Guests
Author:
Date: 1960-12-23
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, Hangzhou, December 22nd: Cambodian Head of State Prince Norodom Sihanouk and his wife, along with other distinguished Cambodian guests, arrived in Hangzhou this morning by special train, accompanied by Premier Zhou Enlai and Vice Premier Chen Yi and his wife. They were warmly welcomed by thousands of people.

Governor Zhou Jianren of Zhejiang Province and his wife, Vice Governor of Zhejiang Province and Mayor of Hangzhou Wu Xian and his wife, as well as responsible persons from various sectors, greeted the distinguished guests at the station.

Among the distinguished Cambodian guests accompanying Prince Sihanouk on his visit to Hangzhou were: Vice Chairman of the Cambodian Royal Supreme Council, Penn Nouth; Prime Minister Pho Proeung; First Deputy Prime Minister Nhiek Tioulong; Prince and Princesses of Prince Sihanouk; high-ranking Cambodian officials accompanying the visit; members of the Cambodian economic delegation; and artists from the Royal Dance Troupe.

Cambodian Ambassador to China, Leng Ngeth, also accompanied the delegation to Hangzhou.

At 9:00 a.m., when the special train carrying the distinguished guests arrived at Hangzhou Station, cheers erupted from the welcoming crowd. People beat gongs and drums, waved Chinese and Cambodian flags, and shouted slogans to pay tribute to the fraternal Cambodian people and to Cambodian Head of State Prince Sihanouk. In the warm and festive atmosphere, Governor Zhou Jianren and his wife, Vice Governor Wu Xian and his wife, and other leaders of Zhejiang Province and Hangzhou Municipality greeted the distinguished guests at the train car door. A group of children rushed forward to present flowers to the guests. As the distinguished guests emerged from Hangzhou Station, a group of gorgeously dressed young women showered them with colored confetti.

The distinguished guests traveled by car through the tranquil Bai Causeway to the guesthouse. A large crowd of welcomers gathered on the lawn in front of the guesthouse. They set off firecrackers, played musical instruments, and danced with flower rings, bouquets, colored flags, and lions, warmly welcoming the guests. Prince Sihanouk and his wife, along with other distinguished Cambodian guests, appeared on the balcony of the guesthouse, accompanied by Premier Zhou Enlai, Vice Premier Chen Yi, and the leaders of Zhejiang Province and Hangzhou Municipality. The welcoming people surged forward, shouting enthusiastic cheers.

Also present at the station to welcome the distinguished guests today were: Vice Chairman of the Zhejiang Provincial CPPCC, Yu Ji-yi; Commander of the PLA troops stationed in Zhejiang, Lieutenant General Qian Jun; responsible persons from various departments and people's organizations in Zhejiang Province and Hangzhou Municipality; members of the China-Cambodia Friendship Association in Hangzhou; and Cambodian interns in Hangzhou.

Chinese Ambassador to Cambodia, Wang Youping and his wife, and Director of the Protocol Department of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs, Yu Peiwen, accompanied the distinguished guests to Hangzhou today.

This newspaper reports, according to Xinhua News Agency, Hangzhou, December 22nd: Prince Sihanouk and his wife, and other distinguished guests, boated on West Lake in Hangzhou this morning, touring scenic spots. This afternoon, they visited the Du Jinsheng Silk Weaving Factory. They were warmly welcomed everywhere they went.

Premier Zhou Enlai, Vice Premier Chen Yi and his wife, Governor Zhou Jianren of Zhejiang Province and his wife, Mayor Wu Xian of Hangzhou and his wife, and others accompanied the distinguished guests on their tour.

This newspaper reports, according to Xinhua News Agency, Hangzhou, December 22nd: Governor Zhou Jianren of Zhejiang Province held a banquet this evening to warmly welcome Cambodian Head of State Norodom Sihanouk and his wife, along with other distinguished Cambodian guests.

Prince Sihanouk and his wife, Vice Chairman of the Cambodian Royal Supreme Council Penn Nouth, Prime Minister Pho Proeung, First Deputy Prime Minister Nhiek Tioulong, Cambodian Ambassador to China Leng Ngeth, and all other distinguished guests, attended the banquet, accompanied by Premier Zhou Enlai, Vice Premier Chen Yi and his wife, Chinese Ambassador to Cambodia Wang Youping and his wife, and others.

After the banquet, Prince Sihanouk and his wife, along with other distinguished Cambodian guests, watched Yue opera, dance, and the Peking opera "Taking Xie Hu," performed by the famous Peking opera actor Gai Jiaotian. (Photo attached)

Prince Sihanouk and his wife, along with other distinguished Cambodian guests, arrived in Nanjing on December 21st, accompanied by Premier Zhou Enlai and Vice Premier Chen Yi and his wife. ### 西哈努克亲王到达杭州  浙江省长举行宴会热烈欢迎柬埔寨贵宾

1960-12-23
第1版()
专栏：

    西哈努克亲王到达杭州
    浙江省长举行宴会热烈欢迎柬埔寨贵宾
    新华社杭州22日电柬埔寨国家元首诺罗敦·西哈努克亲王和夫人及柬埔寨其他贵宾，在周恩来总理、陈毅副总理和夫人陪同下，今天上午乘专车到达杭州，受到数千群众的热烈欢迎。
    浙江省省长周建人和夫人，浙江省副省长、杭州市市长吴宪和夫人以及各方面的负责人都到车站欢迎贵宾。
    随同西哈努克亲王一起到杭州参观访问的柬埔寨贵宾有：柬埔寨王位最高委员会副主席宾努，首相福·波伦，第一副首相涅·刁龙，西哈努克亲王的公主、王子，随同访华的柬埔寨高级官员，柬埔寨经济代表团成员，以及皇家舞蹈团的艺术家们。
    柬埔寨驻我国大使兰·涅特也陪同到达杭州。
    上午九时，贵宾乘坐的专车到达杭州车站时，欢迎的人群中响起了一片欢呼声。人们敲锣打鼓，挥动着中柬两国国旗，高呼口号，向兄弟般的柬埔寨人民致敬，向柬埔寨国家元首西哈努克亲王致敬。在热烈欢腾的气氛中，周建人省长和夫人，吴宪副省长和夫人，以及浙江省和杭州市其他领导人，到车厢门前迎接贵宾，一群少年儿童涌上前去向贵宾们献花。当贵宾们走出杭州车站时，一群盛装的女青年，把彩纸屑撒到贵宾们的身上。
    贵宾乘车穿过幽静的白堤前往宾馆。在宾馆门前的草坪上聚集了众多的欢迎群众。他们燃放鞭炮，吹奏乐器，舞着花环、花束、彩旗和狮子，热情地迎接贵宾。西哈努克亲王和夫人以及柬埔寨其他贵宾，在周恩来总理、陈毅副总理及浙江省、杭州市负责人的陪同下出现在宾馆的阳台上，欢迎的人们涌上前去，发出了热烈的欢呼声。
    今天到车站欢迎贵宾的还有：浙江省政协副主席余纪一，人民解放军驻浙江部队首长钱钧中将，浙江省、杭州市各机关、各人民团体负责人，中柬友好协会在杭州的理事，以及在杭州的柬埔寨实习生。
    我国驻柬埔寨大使王幼平和夫人，外交部礼宾司司长俞沛文，今天陪同贵宾一起到达杭州。
    本报讯据新华社杭州22日电：西哈努克亲王和夫人等贵宾今天上午在杭州泛舟西子湖，游览了名胜，下午参观了都锦生丝织厂。他们到处都受到了人们的热烈欢迎。
    周恩来总理、陈毅副总理和夫人、浙江省省长周建人和夫人、杭州市市长吴宪和夫人等陪同贵宾们参观游览。
    本报讯据新华社杭州22日电：浙江省省长周建人今晚举行宴会，热烈欢迎柬埔寨国家元首诺罗敦·西哈努克亲王和夫人以及柬埔寨其他贵宾。
    西哈努克亲王和夫人、柬埔寨王位最高委员会副主席宾努、首相福·波伦、第一副首相涅·刁龙、柬埔寨驻华大使兰·涅特等全体贵宾，由周恩来总理、陈毅副总理和夫人、我国驻柬埔寨大使王幼平和夫人等陪同出席了宴会。
    宴会结束以后，西哈努克亲王和夫人以及柬埔寨其他贵宾观看了越剧、舞蹈和著名京剧演员盖叫天演出的京剧“拿谢虎”。（附图片）
