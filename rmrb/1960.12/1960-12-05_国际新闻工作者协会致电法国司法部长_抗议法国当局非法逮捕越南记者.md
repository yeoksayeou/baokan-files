Title: International Organization of Journalists Calls French Minister of Justice Protesting the Illegal Arrest of Vietnamese Journalist by French Authorities
Author:
Date: 1960-12-05
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, Prague. The International Organization of Journalists sent a telegram to the French Minister of Justice, protesting the French authorities' arrest of Trần Thanh Xuân, head of the Vietnam News Agency's Paris bureau.

The telegram stated that this arrest is an attack on freedom of the press and human rights.

The telegram stated that the International Organization of Journalists demands that the French authorities release Trần Thanh Xuân and respect the rights of journalists.

### 国际新闻工作者协会致电法国司法部长  抗议法国当局非法逮捕越南记者

1960-12-05
第5版()
专栏：

    国际新闻工作者协会致电法国司法部长
    抗议法国当局非法逮捕越南记者
    新华社布拉格电　国际新闻工作者协会打电报给法国司法部长，抗议法国当局逮捕越南通讯社驻巴黎分社社长陈青春。
    电报说，这次逮捕是对新闻自由和人权的进攻。
    电报说，国际新闻工作者协会要求法国当局释放陈青春，并尊重新闻记者的权利。
