Title: China and Somalia Establish Diplomatic Relations; Premier Zhou and Foreign Minister Chen Congratulate Premier Sharmarke and Foreign Minister Issa Respectively
Author:
Date: 1960-12-17
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, December 16th – Premier Zhou Enlai and Foreign Minister Chen Yi of the People’s Republic of China today sent telegrams to Premier Sharmarke and Foreign Minister Issa of the Somali Republic, warmly congratulating them on the establishment of diplomatic relations and the exchange of ambassadors between China and Somalia.

Premier Zhou Enlai wrote to Premier Sharmarke on November 28, on behalf of the Chinese government, suggesting that China and Somalia establish diplomatic relations and exchange ambassadors. Premier Sharmarke replied to Premier Zhou Enlai on December 14 on behalf of the Somali government, agreeing to the Chinese government's proposal.

The full texts of the exchange of letters are as follows:

December 16, 1960, Telegram from Premier Zhou Enlai to Premier Sharmarke
To His Excellency Abdi Rashid Ali Sharmarke, Premier of the Somali Republic, Mogadishu, Somalia:

Your Excellency's telegram of December 14, 1960, has been received. I am very pleased to learn that the Government of the Somali Republic has agreed to the proposal I made on behalf of my government in my letter to Your Excellency on November 28, 1960, regarding the willingness of the People's Republic of China to establish diplomatic relations and exchange ambassadors with the Somali Republic. On the occasion of the establishment of diplomatic relations between China and Somalia, I would like to express, on behalf of the Chinese people and the Chinese government, and in my own name, my warm congratulations to Your Excellency, the people of your country, and the government of your country. I wish the friendship between the Chinese and Somali peoples and the friendly relations between the two countries further development. I wish the Somali government and the Somali people new achievements in the cause of safeguarding national independence and building their own country. I extend to Your Excellency my highest respect.

Zhou Enlai, Premier of the State Council of the People's Republic of China
Beijing, December 16, 1960

December 16, 1960, Telegram from Foreign Minister Chen Yi to Foreign Minister Issa
To His Excellency Abdullahi Issa, Foreign Minister of the Somali Republic, Mogadishu, Somalia:

On the occasion of the decision by the People's Republic of China and the Somali Republic to establish diplomatic relations, I would like to extend my warm congratulations to Your Excellency on behalf of the Government of the People's Republic of China. I wish the friendship between the Chinese and Somali peoples growing development and consolidation.

Chen Yi, Minister of Foreign Affairs of the People's Republic of China
Beijing, December 16, 1960

November 28, 1960, Letter from Premier Zhou Enlai to Premier Sharmarke
To His Excellency Premier Abdi Rashid Ali Sharmarke:

At the time of the independence of the Somali Republic, the Government and people of the People's Republic of China extended warm congratulations to your government and your people, and expressed the hope to further develop friendly relations between China and Somalia. I would like to take the opportunity of Your Excellency's visit to Cairo to instruct our Ambassador to the United Arab Republic, Chen Jiakang, to exchange views with Your Excellency on the development of relations between China and Somalia, including the establishment of diplomatic relations between China and Somalia. If Your Excellency and the Somali Government have the same desire, the Chinese Government is willing to establish diplomatic relations with the Somali Republic and exchange ambassadors as soon as possible. I eagerly await Your Excellency's reply. I wish your country prosperity and your people happiness.

Zhou Enlai, Premier of the State Council of the People's Republic of China
Beijing, November 28, 1960

December 14, 1960, Telegram from Premier Sharmarke to Premier Zhou Enlai
To His Excellency Premier Zhou Enlai of the State Council of the People's Republic of China, Beijing:

In reply to your letter of November 28, 1960, delivered to me in Cairo by the Ambassador of the People's Republic of China to the United Arab Republic, I am pleased to inform Your Excellency that the Council of Ministers of the Government of the Somali Republic has decided: (1) to legally recognize the State of the People's Republic of China; (2) to establish diplomatic relations between Somalia and China and to exchange diplomatic missions at the ambassadorial level as soon as possible. I am confident that the establishment of diplomatic relations between our two countries will help strengthen the friendship and mutual understanding between the Chinese and Somali peoples. I would also like to wish your country prosperity and your people happiness on behalf of the Government and people of the Somali Republic. Please accept, Your Excellency, my highest respect.

Abdi Rashid Ali Sharmarke, Premier of the Government of the Somali Republic
Mogadishu, December 14, 1960

### 中国索马里建立外交关系  周总理、陈外长分别向舍马克总理、伊萨外长祝贺

1960-12-17
第1版()
专栏：

    中国索马里建立外交关系
    周总理、陈外长分别向舍马克总理、伊萨外长祝贺
    新华社16日讯　中华人民共和国国务院总理周恩来和外交部长陈毅今天分别打电报给索马里共和国总理舍马克和外交部长伊萨，热烈祝贺中、索两国建立外交关系和互派大使。
    周恩来总理曾在11月28日致函舍马克总理，代表中国政府建议中、索两国建立外交关系和互派大使级外交代表。舍马克总理于12月14日代表索马里政府复电周恩来总理，同意中国政府的建议。
    来往函电的全文如下：
    1960年12月16日周恩来总理致舍马克总理的电报索马里　摩加迪沙索马里共和国总理阿卜迪·拉希德·阿里·舍马克阁下：
    阁下1960年12月14日来电收悉。我非常高兴地知道索马里共和国政府已经同意我代表我国政府在1960年11月28日致阁下函中关于中华人民共和国愿同索马里共和国建立外交关系和互派大使的建议。值此中索两国建立外交关系的时候，我谨代表中国人民和中国政府并且以我个人的名义，向阁下、贵国人民和贵国政府表示热烈的祝贺。祝中索两国人民的友谊和两国的友好关系获得进一步的发展。祝索马里政府和索马里人民在维护民族独立和建设自己的国家的事业中取得新的成就。顺向阁下致以最崇高的敬意。
        中华人民共和国国务院总理　周恩来
                      1960年12月16日于北京
    1960年12月16日陈毅外长致伊萨外长的电报索马里　摩加迪沙索马里共和国外交部长阿卜杜拉希·伊萨阁下：
    值此中华人民共和国和索马里共和国决定建立外交关系之际，我谨代表中华人民共和国政府向阁下表示热烈的祝贺。祝中索两国人民的友谊日益发展和巩固。
          中华人民共和国外交部长　陈毅
                      1960年12月16日于北京
    1960年11月28日周恩来总理给舍马克总理的信件阿卜迪·拉希德·阿里·舍马克总理阁下：
    在索马里共和国独立的时候，中华人民共和国政府和中国人民向贵国政府和贵国人民表示了热烈的祝贺，并且表达了希望进一步发展中索两国友好关系的愿望。我愿意趁阁下访问开罗的时候，指示我国驻阿拉伯联合共和国大使陈家康，同阁下就发展中索两国关系问题，包括中索两国建立外交关系的问题，交换意见。如果阁下和索马里政府具有同样的愿望，中国政府愿意迅速地同索马里共和国建立外交关系并互派大使级外交代表。我殷切地等待阁下的回音。顺祝贵国国家繁荣，人民幸福。
      中华人民共和国国务院总理　周恩来　1960年11月28日于北京
    1960年12月14日舍马克总理致周恩来总理的电报北京中华人民共和国国务院总理周恩来阁下：
    为答复你由中华人民共和国驻阿拉伯联合共和国大使在开罗递交给我的1960年11月28日的来信，我愉快地通知阁下：索马里共和国政府部长会议已经决定：（1）在法律上承认中华人民共和国国家；（2）在索马里和中国之间建立外交关系，并且早日互派大使级的外交使团。我确信，我们两国之间外交关系的建立将有助于加强中国人民和索马里人民之间的友谊和相互了解。我还愿意代表索马里共和国政府和人民祝贵国繁荣，人民幸福。阁下，请接受我最崇高的敬意。
        索马里共和国政府总理　阿卜迪·拉希德·阿里·舍马克
                                  1960年12月14日于摩加迪沙
