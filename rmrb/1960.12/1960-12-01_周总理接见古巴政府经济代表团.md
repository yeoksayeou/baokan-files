Title: Premier Zhou Enlai Receives the Cuban Government Economic Delegation
Author:
Date: 1960-12-01
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, 30th - Premier Zhou Enlai received Major Ernesto "Che" Guevara, President of the National Bank of Cuba, and the distinguished guests of the Cuban Revolutionary Government Economic Delegation he led this evening, and had a cordial and friendly conversation with them.

Present at the reception were Vice Premiers of the State Council Chen Yi and Li Xiannian, and responsible persons from relevant departments including Ye Jizhuang, Geng Biao, Li Qiang, Lu Xuzhang, and Shen Jian.

Also present at the reception was the Cuban Ambassador to China, Oscar Pino Santos.

### 周总理接见古巴政府经济代表团

1960-12-01
第1版()
专栏：

    周总理接见古巴政府经济代表团
    新华社30日讯　周恩来总理今晚接见了古巴国家银行行长埃·切·格瓦拉少校和由他率领的古巴革命政府经济代表团的贵宾，同他们进行了亲切、友好的谈话。
    接见时在座的，有国务院副总理陈毅、李先念，和有关方面的负责人叶季壮、耿飚、李强、卢绪章、申健等。
    接见时，古巴驻中国大使奥斯卡·皮诺·桑托斯也在座。
