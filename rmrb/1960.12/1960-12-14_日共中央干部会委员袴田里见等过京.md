Title: Japanese Communist Party Central Committee Member Hakamada Satomi and Others Pass Through Beijing
Author:
Date: 1960-12-14
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, December 13th: Hakamada Satomi, member of the Central Committee Secretariat of the Japanese Communist Party, and Nishizawa Tomio, deputy director of the Central Committee of the Japanese Communist Party, who were invited to Moscow to attend the 43rd anniversary celebrations of the October Socialist Revolution, arrived in Beijing by plane from Moscow this afternoon, passing through our country on their way home. After a brief rest at the airport, Comrade Hakamada Satomi and others left Beijing by plane for Guangzhou in the evening. Peng Zhen, member of the Political Bureau of the CPC Central Committee, Liao Chengzhi, member of the Central Committee, Wu Xiuquan, and Zhang Xiangshan and other responsible staff members of the organs of the CPC Central Committee greeted and saw them off at the airport. (Attached picture)

### 日共中央干部会委员袴田里见等过京

1960-12-14
第1版()
专栏：

    日共中央干部会委员袴田里见等过京
    新华社13日讯　应邀去莫斯科参加十月社会主义革命四十三周年庆祝典礼的日本共产党中央委员会干部会委员袴田里见和日共中央副部长西泽富夫，今天下午乘飞机从莫斯科到达北京，取道我国回国。袴田里见等同志在机场略事休息后，已于当晚乘飞机离开北京赴广州。到机场迎接和送行的有中共中央政治局委员彭真，中央委员廖承志、伍修权，中共中央机关负责工作人员张香山等。（附图片）
    右图：日本共产党中央委员会干部会委员袴田里见在北京机场和前去迎接的中共中央政治局委员彭真亲切交谈
                新华社记者　李长永摄
