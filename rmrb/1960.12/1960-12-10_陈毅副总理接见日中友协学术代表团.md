Title: Vice Premier Chen Yi Receives the Academic Delegation of the Japan-China Friendship Association
Author:
Date: 1960-12-10
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, December 9th: Vice Premier Chen Yi received all members of the Academic Delegation of the Japan-China Friendship Association, headed by Yoichi Fukushima, this morning and had a friendly conversation with them.

Present at the reception were Du Runsheng, Deputy Secretary-General of the Chinese Academy of Sciences, Pan Chun, Acting Director of the Bureau of Foreign Liaison, and Yang Chunsong, Director of the Chinese People's Association for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries.

Japanese peace activist Kinkazu Saionji, who is in Beijing, was also present. (Attached picture)

### 陈毅副总理接见日中友协学术代表团

1960-12-10
第4版()
专栏：

    陈毅副总理接见日中友协学术代表团
    新华社9日讯　陈毅副总理今天上午接见了以福岛要一为首的日中友协学术代表团全体成员，并且同他们进行了友好的谈话。
    接见时在座的，有中国科学院副秘书长杜润生、对外联络局代局长潘纯和中国人民对外文化协会理事杨春松等。
    在北京的日本和平人士西园寺公一也在座。（附图片）
