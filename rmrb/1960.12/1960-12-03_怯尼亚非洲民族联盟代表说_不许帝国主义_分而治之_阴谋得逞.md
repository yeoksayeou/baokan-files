Title: Kenyan African National Union Representative Says: Do Not Allow Imperialist "Divide and Rule" Plots to Succeed
Author:
Date: 1960-12-03
Page: 6
Xinhua News Agency, Cairo. Vera Ambiso, the representative of the Kenyan African National Union in Cairo, condemned the imperialist plot to split the Kenyan national struggle at a press conference here on November 29. He said that imperialists are trying to pursue the old trick of "divide and rule," but this trick will not succeed because the doomsday of the imperialists is coming soon.

Ambiso refuted the imperialist claim that Kenya lacks technical personnel. He said that the United States' request to send Kenyan students to the United States to study is "for political considerations and Cold War purposes."

Ambiso strongly condemned the crimes of imperialists and their running dogs in murdering African nationalists. He said that the French "Red Hand" gang (which murdered the chairman of the Cameroon People's Union, Moumié) is a tool of the North Atlantic Treaty Organization. We will never be intimidated by such actions. We will continue to fight until Kenya achieves freedom and independence.

He also said that Kenyan national movement leader Kenyatta must be released immediately, otherwise the British imperialist government will have to bear the consequences caused by its attitude.

### 怯尼亚非洲民族联盟代表说  不许帝国主义“分而治之”阴谋得逞

1960-12-03
第6版()
专栏：

    怯尼亚非洲民族联盟代表说
    不许帝国主义“分而治之”阴谋得逞
    新华社开罗电　怯尼亚非洲民族联盟驻开罗代表韦腊·安比索11月29日在这里的记者招待会上谴责帝国主义分裂怯尼亚民族斗争的阴谋，他说，帝国主义者企图奉行“分而治之”的老手法，但是这种手法是不能得逞的，因为帝国主义者的末日快来到了。
    安比索驳斥帝国主义者所谓怯尼亚缺乏技术人员的说法。他说，美国要求派遣怯尼亚学生前往美国学习，只是“为了政治上的考虑和冷战的目的”。
    安比索强烈谴责帝国主义分子和他们的走狗谋害非洲民族主义者的罪行。他说，（杀害喀麦隆人民联盟主席穆米埃的）法国“红手”匪帮是北大西洋公约组织的工具。我们绝不会被这种行动所吓倒，我们将继续进行斗争，直到怯尼亚取得自由和独立为止。
    他还说，必须立即释放怯尼亚民族运动领袖肯雅塔，否则英帝国主义政府将不得不承担由于它的态度所引起的后果。
