Title: Prince Sihanouk to Visit China in Mid-December at the Invitation of Chairman Liu and Premier Zhou
Author:
Date: 1960-12-10
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, 9th: At the invitation of Liu Shaoqi, Chairman of the People's Republic of China, and Zhou Enlai, Premier of the State Council, His Royal Highness Prince Norodom Sihanouk, Head of State of Cambodia, will pay a state visit to our country in mid-December.

At the invitation of Zhou Enlai, Premier of the State Council of the People's Republic of China, and Vice Premier Chen Yi, His Excellency Prince Penn Nouth, Vice-Chairman of the Supreme Council of the Throne of Cambodia, His Excellency Pho Proeung, Prime Minister of the Royal Government of Cambodia, and First Deputy Prime Minister Nhiek Tioulong will accompany Prince Sihanouk on the visit.

Accompanying Prince Sihanouk on his visit will also be his wife and his daughter, Princess Soriya Roeungsey, some senior Cambodian officials, an economic delegation, and some artists from the Royal Ballet Troupe.

Prince Sihanouk and his party will arrive in Beijing around December 15 after visiting Czechoslovakia, the Soviet Union, and Mongolia.

### 应刘主席和周总理的邀请  西哈努克亲王中旬访华

1960-12-10
第1版()
专栏：

    应刘主席和周总理的邀请
    西哈努克亲王中旬访华
    新华社9日讯　应中华人民共和国主席刘少奇和国务院总理周恩来的邀请，柬埔寨国家元首诺罗敦·西哈努克亲王殿下将于12月中旬前来我国进行国事访问。
    应中华人民共和国国务院总理周恩来和副总理陈毅的邀请，柬埔寨王位最高委员会副主席宾努亲王阁下、柬埔寨王国政府首相福·波伦阁下、第一副首相涅·刁隆阁下将陪同西哈努克亲王前来访问。
    随同西哈努克亲王来访的还有他的夫人和他的女儿索丽娅·伦赛公主、柬埔寨的一些高级官员和一个经济代表团以及皇家芭蕾舞团的部分艺术家。
    西哈努克亲王等一行将在访问捷克斯洛伐克、苏联和蒙古以后，于12月15日左右抵达北京。
