Title: Turkish Students Demonstrate in Support of the Algerian People; UAR Minister of State Condemns New Crimes of French Imperialism
Author:
Date: 1960-12-15
Page: 6

    Xinhua News Agency, December 13th, Istanbul: Approximately three thousand university students held a demonstration in Istanbul, Turkey's largest city, on December 12th. They showed their support for the heroic Algerian people's struggle for national independence, while strongly protesting the recent atrocities committed by French imperialism in massacring Algerian patriots.
    Waving the flags of the Algerian National Liberation Front, the demonstrating students marched to the city center square, where they expressed their condolences for the Muslim students who sacrificed themselves in the Algerian war of national independence.
    They then paraded through the bustling streets of Istanbul to demonstrate in front of the French Consulate. They posted slogans such as "Give Peace to Algeria!" and "French Go Home!" on the walls of the consulate, while placing the flag of the Algerian National Liberation Front in front of the French Consulate.
    Xinhua News Agency, Cairo, December 13th: United Arab Republic Minister of State, Hatem, issued a statement here today saying that the UAR condemns the new crimes of French imperialism in massacring the Algerian people.
    The statement said: "The UAR strongly condemns the brutal attacks on the citizens of Algiers and Oran, which have resulted in the sacrifice and injury of hundreds of unarmed civilians."
    The statement pointed out that France's large-scale massacre of the Algerian people is the most appalling crime of human rights violations.

### 土耳其学生示威支持阿尔及利亚人民  阿联国务部长谴责法帝国主义新罪行

1960-12-15
第6版()
专栏：

    土耳其学生示威支持阿尔及利亚人民
    阿联国务部长谴责法帝国主义新罪行
    新华社13日讯　伊斯坦布尔消息：约有三千名大学生12日在土耳其的最大城市伊斯坦布尔举行示威游行，支持英勇的阿尔及利亚人民争取民族独立的斗争，同时强烈抗议法帝国主义最近屠杀阿尔及利亚爱国者的暴行。
    示威的大学生们高举着阿尔及利亚民族解放阵线的旗帜，列队前往市中心广场，在那里表示悼念在阿尔及利亚民族独立战争中牺牲的穆斯林学生。
    他们随后通过伊斯坦布尔的闹市，游行到法国领事馆前示威。他们把写着“给阿尔及利亚以和平！”“法国佬，滚回家去！”这样一些口号的标语贴在领事馆墙上，同时把阿尔及利亚民族解放阵线的旗帜放在法国领事馆门前。
    新华社开罗13日电　阿拉伯联合共和国国务部长哈特姆今天在这里发表声明说，阿联谴责法帝国主义屠杀阿尔及利亚人民的新罪行。
    声明说：“阿联强烈地谴责对阿尔及尔和奥兰市民的残暴的进攻，这种进攻使数以百计手无寸铁的市民牺牲和受伤。”
    声明指出，法国对阿尔及利亚人民进行大规模的屠杀是侵犯人权的最骇人听闻的罪行。
