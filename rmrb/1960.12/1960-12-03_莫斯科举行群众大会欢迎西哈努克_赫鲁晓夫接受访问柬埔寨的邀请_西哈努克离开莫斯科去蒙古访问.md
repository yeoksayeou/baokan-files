Title: Moscow Holds Mass Rally to Welcome Sihanouk; Khrushchev Accepts Invitation to Visit Cambodia; Sihanouk Leaves Moscow for Visit to Mongolia
Author:
Date: 1960-12-03
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, December 1st. A Soviet-Cambodian friendship mass rally was held today in the Grand Kremlin Palace to welcome the Cambodian guests headed by Cambodian Head of State Prince Sihanouk.

Among those attending the rally were Khrushchev and other leaders of the Soviet government. The Kremlin Hall was filled with workers, scientists, cultural workers, and students. Foreign envoys were also among the participants. Bobrovnikov, Chairman of the Executive Committee of the Moscow Soviet, presided over the rally.

Brezhnev, Chairman of the Presidium of the Supreme Soviet of the Soviet Union, welcomed Prince Sihanouk and his entourage on behalf of the Presidium of the Supreme Soviet of the Soviet Union and the Soviet government.

Prince Sihanouk delivered a speech to the attendees. He pointed out that colonialism should be eliminated from the earth forever. He also pointed out that the People's Republic of China must be restored to its seat in the United Nations because it is the sole representative of the Chinese people. He also believed that the Mongolian People's Republic should be admitted as a member of the United Nations.

Speakers at the mass rally emphasized that the Soviet people deeply sympathized with Cambodia's struggle for national rejuvenation and expressed their desire to further strengthen the friendly relations between the two peoples.

On the same day, the Presidium of the Supreme Soviet of the Soviet Union and the Soviet government held a reception to entertain Prince Sihanouk. Khrushchev and Sihanouk both spoke at the reception.

Khrushchev said in his speech that he accepted the invitation to visit Cambodia.

On the morning of November 30th, the ambassadors to the Soviet Union from the countries participating in the Bandung Conference hosted a banquet to welcome Cambodian Head of State Prince Sihanouk.

Brezhnev, Mukhitdinov, Furtseva, and others from the Soviet side attended the banquet.

Prince Sihanouk said in his speech at the banquet that a new spirit of solidarity was born in Bandung among the long-oppressed peoples, and it strengthened their confidence in their own destiny.

He also said, "We will never allow anyone to threaten us, to threaten our neutrality, or to allow anyone to target our policies as a threat. We oppose any sabotage and any foreign interference."

Liu Xiao, the Chinese Ambassador to the Soviet Union, also spoke at the banquet. He expressed a warm welcome to Prince Sihanouk's visit to the Soviet Union.

On the 28th, Prince Sihanouk visited Khrushchev, Chairman of the Council of Ministers of the Soviet Union, and Brezhnev, Chairman of the Presidium of the Supreme Soviet of the Soviet Union, in the Kremlin.

On the 29th, the Soviet government and the Presidium of the Supreme Soviet of the Soviet Union held a banquet in the Kremlin to welcome Prince Sihanouk.

Prince Sihanouk and his entourage paid their respects at the Lenin-Stalin Mausoleum on the 30th and laid wreaths. On the same day, Prince Sihanouk visited the Exhibition of Achievements of the National Economy of the Soviet Union.

Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, December 2nd. Prince Sihanouk, Head of State of Cambodia, who visited the Soviet Union at the invitation of the Presidium of the Supreme Soviet of the Soviet Union and the Soviet government, left Moscow by train on the evening of the 1st to visit the Mongolian People's Republic. Sihanouk will also visit Novosibirsk and Irkutsk on his way to the Mongolian People's Republic.

### 莫斯科举行群众大会欢迎西哈努克  赫鲁晓夫接受访问柬埔寨的邀请  西哈努克离开莫斯科去蒙古访问

1960-12-03
第5版()
专栏：

    莫斯科举行群众大会欢迎西哈努克
    赫鲁晓夫接受访问柬埔寨的邀请
    西哈努克离开莫斯科去蒙古访问
    新华社莫斯科1日电　今天在大克里姆林宫举行了苏联—柬埔寨友好群众大会，欢迎以柬埔寨国家元首西哈努克亲王为首的柬埔寨客人。
    出席大会的有赫鲁晓夫和苏联政府其他领导人。克里姆林宫大厅中坐满工人、科学家、文化工作者和学生。与会者当中还有外国使节。莫斯科市苏维埃执行委员会主席波布罗夫尼科夫主持大会。
    苏联最高苏维埃主席团主席勃列日涅夫代表苏联最高苏维埃主席团和苏联政府欢迎西哈努克亲王及其随行人员。
    西哈努克亲王向到会者发表了演说。他指出，应当把殖民主义从地球上永远消灭掉。他还指出，必须恢复中华人民共和国在联合国的席位，因为它是中国人民的唯一代表。他还认为，应当接纳蒙古人民共和国为联合国会员国。
    在群众大会上讲话的人都着重指出，苏联人非常同情柬埔寨为争取民族复兴而进行的斗争，并且表示要进一步加强两国人民之间的友好关系。
    同一天，苏联最高苏维埃主席团和苏联政府举行了招待会，招待西哈努克亲王。赫鲁晓夫和西哈努克都在会上讲了话。
    赫鲁晓夫在会上讲话时表示接受访问柬埔寨的邀请。
    在11月30日晨，万隆会议参加国驻苏联的大使们曾设宴欢迎柬埔寨国家元首西哈努克亲王。
    苏联方面出席宴会的有勃列日涅夫、穆希金诺夫、福尔采娃等。
    西哈努克亲王在宴会上讲话说，在万隆诞生了受长期压迫的人民之间的新的团结精神，并增强了他们对自己命运的信心。
    他还说，我们任何时候都不允许有人对我们施加威胁，威胁我国的中立，不允许有人把我国的政策作为威胁的对象。我们反对任何破坏活动，反对任何外国干涉。
    中国驻苏联大使刘晓也在宴会上讲了话。他对西哈努克亲王访问苏联表示热烈欢迎。
    28日，西哈努克亲王前往克里姆林宫访问了苏联部长会议主席赫鲁晓夫和苏联最高苏维埃主席团主席勃列日涅夫。
    29日，苏联政府和苏联最高苏维埃主席团在克里姆林宫举行宴会，欢迎西哈努克亲王。
    西哈努克亲王及随行人员30日拜谒了列宁—斯大林陵墓，并敬献了花圈。同一天，西哈努克亲王参观了苏联国民经济成就展览会。
    据新华社莫斯科2日电　应苏联最高苏维埃主席团和苏联政府的邀请访问苏联的柬埔寨国家元首西哈努克亲王，1日晚间乘火车离开莫斯科，前往蒙古人民共和国访问。西哈努克在前往蒙古人民共和国途中还将访问新西伯利亚和伊尔库次克。
