Title: Five U.S. Warships Invade Our Territorial Waters; We Issue the 130th Serious Warning
Author:
Date: 1960-12-25
Page: 1

　　Xinhua News Agency, December 24th: From 19:33 on December 23rd to 3:22 on December 24th, five U.S. warships intruded into the sea area south of Shanwei, Guangdong Province. Regarding this military provocation by the U.S. warships, the spokesperson of our Ministry of Foreign Affairs was ordered to issue the 130th serious warning.

### 美国军舰五艘侵入我领海  我提出第130次严重警告

1960-12-25
第1版()
专栏：

    美国军舰五艘侵入我领海
    我提出第130次严重警告
　　新华社24日讯　12月23日十九时三十三分至24日三时二十二分，美国军舰五艘侵入我广东省汕尾以南地区海域。对于美国军舰的这种军事挑衅，我外交部发言人奉命提出第一百三十次严重警告。
