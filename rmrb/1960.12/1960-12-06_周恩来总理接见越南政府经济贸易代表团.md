Title: Premier Zhou Enlai Receives Vietnamese Government Economic and Trade Delegation
Author:
Date: 1960-12-06
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, December 5th. Premier Zhou Enlai received the distinguished guests of the Vietnamese Government Economic and Trade Delegation, headed by Nguyen Duy Trinh, Vice Premier of Democratic Republic of Vietnam, this evening and had a cordial conversation with them. Present at the reception were Vice Premier of the State Council Bo Yibo, and responsible persons from relevant departments Ye Jizhuang, Ji Pengfei, Fang Yi, Li Qiang, etc. Vietnamese Ambassador to China Tran Tu Binh was also present at the reception.

### 周恩来总理接见越南政府经济贸易代表团

1960-12-06
第1版()
专栏：

    周恩来总理接见越南政府经济贸易代表团
    新华社5日讯　周恩来总理今晚接见了以越南民主共和国政府副总理阮维桢为首的越南政府经济、贸易代表团的贵宾，同他们进行了亲切的谈话。接见时在座的，有国务院副总理薄一波，和有关方面的负责人叶季壮、姬鹏飞、方毅、李强等。接见时，越南驻中国大使陈子平也在座。
