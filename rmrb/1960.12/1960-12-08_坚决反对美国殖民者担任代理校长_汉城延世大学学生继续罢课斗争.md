Title: Resolutely Oppose American Colonialists Serving as Acting Principal: Seoul's Yonsei University Students Continue Strike Struggle
Author:
Date: 1960-12-08
Page: 7

Xinhua News Agency, Pyongyang, December 3rd: Seoul dispatch: Yonsei University students in Seoul, who have launched a struggle to expel the American colonialists Underwood and Sauer, held a conference on the 1st, resolutely refusing to attend classes and persisting in their struggle against the reactionary authorities.

According to South Korean Tonghwa News Agency, Yonsei University students, at the conference held on this day, condemned the puppet authorities' fascist suppression of arresting students. They said: "Our classmates are still being detained, we cannot attend classes!". They strongly demanded that the puppet authorities immediately release the detained classmates.

### 坚决反对美国殖民者担任代理校长  汉城延世大学学生继续罢课斗争

1960-12-08
第7版()
专栏：

    坚决反对美国殖民者担任代理校长
    汉城延世大学学生继续罢课斗争
    据新华社平壤3日电　汉城消息：为驱逐美国殖民者恩德乌德、萨乌尔而展开斗争的汉城延世大学的学生，在1日举行大会，坚决拒绝上课，坚持同反动当局进行斗争。
    据南朝鲜同和通讯社报道，延世大学的学生在这一天举行大会时，谴责傀儡当局逮捕学生的法西斯镇压行为。他们说：“同学们还在被扣押，我们不能上课！”。他们强硬地要求傀儡当局立即释放被扣押的同学。
