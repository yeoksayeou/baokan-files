Title: Argentine Sierra Returns Home

Author:
Date: 1960-12-06
Page: 4

    Xinhua News Agency, 5th.  José Sierra, member of the Central Committee of the Argentine Communist Youth League, concluded his visit to our country and left Beijing by plane today to return home.

### 阿根廷西爱拉回国

1960-12-06
第4版()
专栏：

    阿根廷西爱拉回国
    新华社5日讯　阿根廷共产主义青年联盟中央委员会委员何塞·西爱拉，结束了在我国的访问，今天乘飞机离开北京回国。
