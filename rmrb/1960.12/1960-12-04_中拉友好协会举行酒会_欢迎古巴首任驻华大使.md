Title: Sino-Latin American Friendship Association Holds Reception to Welcome First Cuban Ambassador to China
Author:
Date: 1960-12-04
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency, December 3rd: The China-Latin America Friendship Association held a reception tonight to warmly welcome the envoy from the heroic nation, Cuba's first Ambassador to China, Oscar Pino Santos.

The Ambassador met with people from all walks of life in our country tonight, and everyone gathered together in a warm and friendly atmosphere at the reception.

Chu Tunan, President of the China-Latin America Friendship Association, spoke at the reception, saying that the formal establishment of diplomatic relations between China and Cuba will bring the friendship between the people of China and Cuba into a new stage of development. The people of the two countries will always encourage and support each other in the struggle against U.S. imperialism and in defense of world peace.

Cuban Ambassador Oscar Pino Santos warmly greeted the Chinese people on behalf of the Cuban people at the reception. He said that the people of China and Cuba have had friendly, brotherly relations for a long time. Now we also have the common demand to oppose U.S. imperialism, and the friendship between the people of the two countries will undoubtedly be further developed. He concluded by enthusiastically proposing a toast to the strength and growth of the socialist camp, to the victory of the struggle against imperialism and colonialism, to the further development of the friendship between the people of China, Cuba and Latin America.

At the reception, actors from the Chinese art troupe who had visited Cuba performed Chinese and Cuban music, song and dance programs, which were warmly welcomed by the audience.

Among those present at today's reception were people from all walks of life in the capital: Zhang Xiruo, Zhang Hanfu, Kong Yuan, Zhang Yan, Lu Xuzhang, Xia Yan, Hu Yuzhi, Ji Chaoding, Dong Chuncai, Chen Kehan, Ding Xilin, Rong Gaotang, Zhou Erfu, Wu Han, Shen Jian, China's ambassador to Cuba, and people who had visited Cuba.

Foreign guests and students from Latin American countries in Beijing were also invited to attend the reception. (Attached photo)

The photo shows President Chu Tunan (center), Ambassador Pino Santos (left), and Vice Director Kong Yuan (right) at the reception. Xinhua News Agency reporter Liu Qingrui.

### 中拉友好协会举行酒会  欢迎古巴首任驻华大使

1960-12-04
第4版()
专栏：

    中拉友好协会举行酒会
    欢迎古巴首任驻华大使
    据新华社3日讯　中国拉丁美洲友好协会今晚举行酒会，热烈欢迎来自英雄国家的使节、古巴首任驻华大使奥斯卡·皮诺·桑托斯。
    大使今晚同我国各界人士会见，大家欢聚一堂，酒会上充满了热烈友好的气氛。
    中国拉丁美洲友好协会会长楚图南在酒会上讲话，他说，中古两国正式建立外交关系，将使中古两国人民之间的友谊进入一个新的发展阶段。两国人民在反对美帝国主义、保卫世界和平的斗争中，将永远相互鼓舞、相互支持。
    古巴大使奥斯卡·皮诺·桑托斯在酒会上代表古巴人民热烈地向中国人民致意。他说，在很早以前，中古两国人民就有着友好的兄弟般的关系。现在我们又有着反对美帝国主义的共同要求，两国人民的友谊无疑将得到进一步的发展。他最后热情地提议为社会主义阵营的壮大，为反对帝国主义和殖民主义斗争的胜利，为中国古巴和中国拉丁美洲人民的友谊进一步发展，干杯。
    在酒会上，曾经访问过古巴的中国艺术团的演员们，表演了中国和古巴的音乐歌舞节目，受到了全场热烈的欢迎。
    出席今天酒会的有首都各方面人士：张奚若、章汉夫、孔原、张彦、卢绪章、夏衍、胡愈之、冀朝鼎、董纯才、陈克寒、丁西林、荣高棠、周而复、吴晗，中国驻古巴大使申健以及曾经访问过古巴的人士。
    在北京的拉丁美洲国家的外宾和留学生，也应邀出席了酒会。（附图片）
