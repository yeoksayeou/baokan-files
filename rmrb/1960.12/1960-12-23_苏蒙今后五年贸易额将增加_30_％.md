Title: Sino-Mongolian Trade Volume to Increase by 30% in the Next Five Years
Author: 
Date: 1960-12-23
Page: 6
Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, 21st -- According to TASS, representatives from the Soviet Union and Mongolia signed a goods exchange agreement for 1961-1965 and a trade protocol for 1961 in Moscow on the 20th.

    The agreement stipulates that the trade volume between the Soviet Union and Mongolia will increase by 30% in the next five years, totaling more than three billion rubles.

    The Soviet Union will provide Mongolia with machinery and equipment and building materials needed for the construction of industrial enterprises, housing and cultural facilities, as well as petroleum products, steel, chemical products, industrial goods and food.

    In addition, according to past Soviet credit, the Soviet Union will provide Mongolia with a large number of tractors, combine harvesters and other agricultural machinery, automobiles, mobile power stations, etc.

    In addition to providing the Soviet Union with traditional commodities such as livestock, wool, and raw hides, Mongolia will also export woolen fabrics and leather shoes to the Soviet Union.

### 苏蒙今后五年贸易额将增加30％

1960-12-23
第6版()
专栏：

    苏蒙今后五年贸易额将增加30％
    新华社21日讯  据塔斯社莫斯科讯：苏联和蒙古两国的代表20日在莫斯科签订了两国之间1961—1965年的换货协定和1961年的贸易议定书。
    协定规定，苏联和蒙古之间的贸易额，在今后五年内将增加30％，总计为三十多亿卢布。
    苏联将向蒙古提供建设工业企业、民房和文化生活机构所需要的机器装备和建筑材料以及石油制品、钢材、化学制品、工业品和食品等。
    此外，根据过去苏联提出的信贷，苏联将向蒙古提供许多拖拉机、联合收割机和其他农业机器、汽车、流动电站等。
    蒙古除了向苏联提供牲畜、羊毛、原皮等传统商品外，还将向苏联输出毛织品和皮鞋等。
