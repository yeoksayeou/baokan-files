Title: Hanoi Public Security Bureau Arrests French Vice-Consul in Hanoi
Author: 
Date: 1960-12-05
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, Hanoi, December 2nd. According to a report by the Vietnam News Agency, the Hanoi Public Security Bureau, acting on the order of the Hanoi Municipal Procuratorate, arrested Paul Depremonville, a staff member of the French General Delegation in Hanoi and Vice-Consul, at 2:00 PM today.

    The interrogation is underway.

### 河内市公安局逮捕法驻河内副领事

1960-12-05
第5版()
专栏：

    河内市公安局逮捕法驻河内副领事
    新华社河内2日电　据越南通讯社报道，河内市公安局执行河内市检察院的命令，今天下午二时逮捕了法国驻河内总代表团人员、副领事保罗·德普雷蒙维勒。
    审讯正在进行中。
