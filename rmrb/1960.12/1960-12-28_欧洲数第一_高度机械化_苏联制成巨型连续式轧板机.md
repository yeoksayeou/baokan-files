Title: Europe's Number One, Highly Mechanized: Soviet Union Makes Giant Continuous Plate Rolling Mill
Author:
Date: 1960-12-28
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, December 27th: According to a report in *Pravda*, Europe's largest continuous plate rolling mill, the "2500," has been put into operation at the Soviet Magnitogorsk Iron and Steel Works.

This machine weighs five times as much as an ordinary 6,000-ton primary rolling mill and can roll steel plates with a thickness of 1.5 mm to 10 mm and a width of up to 2,350 mm. It is a highly mechanized and automated machine with a total engine power of 100,000 kilowatts. It surpasses similarly sized American machines in terms of both rolling speed and designed production capacity. The completion of this machine is the result of the joint creative efforts of many machine manufacturing plants, electrical machinery plants, design institutes, and scientific research institutions. Its main designer and manufacturer is the New Kramatorsk Machine Building Plant.

The "2500" is one of the modern continuous rail plate mills scheduled to be put into operation during the Seven-Year Plan. (Photo attached)

At the Soviet Magnitogorsk Iron and Steel Works, workers watch the "2500" continuous rolling mill roll the first steel plate.

### 欧洲数第一　高度机械化  苏联制成巨型连续式轧板机

1960-12-28
第5版()
专栏：

    欧洲数第一　高度机械化
    苏联制成巨型连续式轧板机
    在轧制速度和生产能力方面都超过同样大小的美国机器
    据新华社莫斯科27日电　据《真理报》报道：欧洲最大的连续式轧板机“二五○○”号，已在苏联马格尼托哥尔斯克钢铁联合工厂投入生产。
    这台机器的重量为普通的六千吨初轧机的五倍，可以轧厚一点五毫米到十毫米，宽达二千三百五十毫米的钢板。这是一种高度机械化和自动化的机器，它的发动机总功率达十万瓩。它无论在轧制的速度方面和设计生产能力方面都超过同样大小的美国机器。这台机器的制成是许多机器制造厂、电机厂、设计机构和科学研究机构共同的创造性活动的结果。它的主要设计者和制造者是新克拉马托哥尔斯克机器制造厂。
    “二五○○”号是七年计划规定投入生产的现代化连续式轨板机之一。（附图片）
    在苏联马格尼托哥尔斯克钢铁联合工厂，工人们在观看“二五○○”号连续式轧板机轧制第一块钢板。
                                  塔斯社稿（新华社发）
