Title: U.S. Plane Again Intrudes into Our Airspace; We Issue the 129th Serious Warning
Author:
Date: 1960-12-16
Page: 2
Xinhua News Agency, 15th -- On December 15th, from 13:01 to 13:17, a U.S. military aircraft intruded into the airspace over the Shanwei area of Guangdong Province. Regarding this military provocation by the U.S. military aircraft, the spokesperson of our Ministry of Foreign Affairs was instructed to issue the 129th serious warning.

### 美机又侵入我领空  我提出第129次严重警告

1960-12-16
第2版()
专栏：

    美机又侵入我领空
    我提出第129次严重警告
    新华社15日讯　12月15日十三时零一分至十三时十七分，美国军用飞机一架，侵入我广东省汕尾地区上空。对于美国军用飞机的这种军事挑衅，我外交部发言人奉命提出第一百二十九次严重警告。
