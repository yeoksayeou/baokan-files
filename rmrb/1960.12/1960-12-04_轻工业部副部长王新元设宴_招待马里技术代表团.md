Title: Vice Minister of Light Industry Wang Xinyuan Hosts Banquet for Malian Technical Delegation
Author: 
Date: 1960-12-04
Page: 4

    Vice Minister of Light Industry Wang Xinyuan Hosts Banquet
    for Malian Technical Delegation
    Xinhua News Agency, December 3rd. Vice Minister of Light Industry Wang Xinyuan hosted a banquet tonight for the members of the Malian Republic Technical Delegation: construction engineer Silla Fodi and agricultural technician Diala Tidiani.
    Among those present at the banquet were Chen Zhifang, Director of the Science and Technology Research Department of the Ministry of Light Industry, He Ying, Director of the Asian-African Department of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs, Ren Puzhai, Director of the Construction Bureau of the Ministry of Construction Engineering, and Zhu Suichu, Deputy Director of the General Office of the Ministry of Agriculture.
    Cuban Textile Workers' Union Delegation Returns Home
    Xinhua News Agency, December 3rd. The Cuban Textile Workers National Federation Delegation, led by Hector Capone, Deputy Secretary of the Cuban Workers' Federation Organization and Secretary-General of the Havana Province Textile Workers' Union, left Beijing by plane this morning to return home after visiting Beijing, Zhengzhou, Wuhan, Shanghai, Hangzhou and other cities.
    Three Cuban and Brazilian Legal Workers Return Home
    Xinhua News Agency, December 2nd. Santiago Cuba, head of the Cuban legal workers delegation, Jose de Aguiar Dias, head of the Brazilian legal workers delegation, and Francisco Julio, a member of the delegation, who were invited by the China Political Science and Law Society to visit our country, left Beijing by plane today to return home after visiting Beijing, Nanjing, Shanghai, Wuhan, Luoyang and other places.
    Wu Defeng and Zhang Zhirang, vice presidents of the China Political Science and Law Society, Huang Huoxing, deputy procurator-general of the Supreme People's Procuratorate, and Wu Xinyu, director of the China Political Science and Law Society, went to the airport to see them off.
    Chinese Trade Union Delegation Returns Home
    Xinhua News Agency, December 1st. The Chinese Trade Union Delegation, led by Kang Yonghe, Secretary of the Secretariat of the All-China Federation of Trade Unions, returned to Beijing by plane on the 30th after a month-long visit to Cuba.
    The Chinese Trade Union Delegation was invited by the Cuban Workers' Federation and some industrial trade unions to pay a friendly visit to Cuba. They also participated in the third meeting of the International Trade Union Support Algeria Workers and People's Committee held in Cuba.
    PLA Art Troupe Returns Home
    Xinhua News Agency, December 1st. The Chinese People's Liberation Army Art Troupe, which accompanied our country's military friendly delegation to North Korea for a friendly visit and performance, has returned home after concluding its visit and performance activities.
    Chinese Youth Delegation Returns to Beijing
    Xinhua News Agency, December 1st. The Chinese Youth Delegation of twelve people, invited by the Central Committee of the Korean Democratic Youth League to visit North Korea, returned to Beijing by train today, led by Li Qitao, the head of the delegation and secretary of the Central Committee of the Communist Youth League. Wang Zhaohua, secretary of the Secretariat of the Central Committee of the Communist Youth League, and others greeted them at the station. Tae Ho, Second Secretary of the Korean Embassy in China, also greeted them at the station.
    Swedish Ballet Departs Shanghai for Nanjing
    Xinhua News Agency, Shanghai, December 1st. The Swedish Royal Opera Ballet concluded its visit and performance in Shanghai and left Shanghai by special train this morning for Nanjing. Chen Yusun, Vice President of the Shanghai Branch of the Chinese People's Association for Friendship with Foreign Countries, and many people from Shanghai's literary and art circles went to the station to see them off.
    The Swedish artists performed a total of four performances in Shanghai, with nearly 10,000 spectators enjoying their performances of excerpts from "Swan Lake" and "Don Quixote," "The Prodigal Son," "Symphony in C Major," and "Les Sylphides." Jin Zhonghua, Deputy Mayor of Shanghai, Meng Bo, Director of the Shanghai Municipal Bureau of Culture, Zhang Chunqiao, President of the Shanghai Branch of the Chinese People's Association for Friendship with Foreign Countries, and others attended and watched the first performance of the Swedish artists. Deputy Mayor Jin Zhonghua also met with Heloton, the head of the ballet troupe and manager of the Swedish Royal Opera, and Skjöldebrand, artistic director and head of the Swedish Royal Opera Ballet, and the main actors.
    Hungarian Men's and Women's Table Tennis Teams Compete in Guangzhou
    Xinhua News Agency, Guangzhou, December 3rd. The Hungarian national men's and women's table tennis team players formed the Budapest men's and women's table tennis team two in Guangzhou on the evening of the 2nd, and held a friendly match with the Guangdong men's and women's table tennis team two. As a result, the visiting men's and women's teams won both in the team competition. The score for the men's team was five to one, and the women's team was three to zero.
    The Hungarian national men's and women's table tennis teams have concluded their visit and competition in Guangzhou and will take the train to Shanghai on the 3rd. On the 8th and 9th, they will hold friendly matches with table tennis players in Shanghai. As of now, the visiting team's record in friendly matches after coming to our country is: one win and four losses for the men's team; three wins and two losses for the women's team.
    Xinhua News Agency, Guangzhou, December 2nd. The Hungarian national men's and women's table tennis team players formed the Hungarian Budapest men's and women's table tennis team one in Guangzhou on the evening of the 1st, and held separate team friendly matches with the Guangdong men's and women's table tennis team one. As a result, the visiting men's team lost two to five, and the women's team won three to zero.
    Polish "Metallurgist" Football Team Competes in Shanghai
    Xinhua News Agency, Shanghai, December 2nd. The Polish Sosnowiec City "Metallurgist" football team held a friendly match with the Shanghai football team in Shanghai on the 1st. As a result, the home team won three to one. So far, the result of the five matches the visiting team has played after coming to our country is: one win, one draw, and three losses. More than 20,000 spectators watched the exciting performances of both sides at the Hongkou Stadium.

### 轻工业部副部长王新元设宴  招待马里技术代表团

1960-12-04
第4版()
专栏：

    轻工业部副部长王新元设宴
    招待马里技术代表团
    新华社3日讯　轻工业部副部长王新元今晚设宴招待马里共和国技术代表团成员：建筑工程师西拉·福迪和农业技师迪亚拉·蒂迪亚尼。
    出席宴会的有轻工业部科学技术研究司司长陈智方，外交部亚非司司长何英，建筑工程部施工局局长任朴斋，农业部办公厅副主任朱遂初等。
    古巴纺织工会代表团回国
    新华社3日讯　由古巴工人联合会组织副书记、哈瓦那省纺织工会总书记埃克托·卡波内利率领的古巴纺织工人全国联合会代表团一行四人，在访问了北京、郑州、武汉、上海、杭州等城市后，今天上午乘飞机离开北京回国。古巴巴西三位法律工作者回国
    新华社2日讯　应中国政治法律学会邀请来我国访问的古巴法律工作者代表团团长圣地亚哥·古巴，巴西法律工作者代表团团长若泽·德阿基亚尔·迪亚斯和团员弗朗西斯科·儒利奥等三人，在访问了北京、南京、上海、武汉、洛阳等地后，今天乘飞机离开北京回国。
    中国政治法律学会副会长吴德峰、张志让，最高人民检察院副检察长黄火星，中国政治法律学会理事武新宇等到机场送行。
    中国工会代表团回国
    新华社1日讯　由中华全国总工会书记处书记康永和率领的中国工会代表团，在古巴进行了一个多月的访问后，已经在30日乘机回到北京。
    中国工会代表团是应古巴工人联合会及一些产业工会的邀请去古巴进行友好访问的，他们还参加了在古巴举行的国际工会支援阿尔及利亚工人及人民委员会第三次会议。
    解放军文工团回国
    新华社1日讯　随同我国访朝军事友好代表团到朝鲜进行友好访问演出的中国人民解放军文工团，在结束了访问演出活动后，已经回国。
    中国青年代表团回到北京
    新华社1日讯　应朝鲜民主青年同盟中央委员会邀请去朝鲜访问的中国青年代表团一行十二人，在团长、共青团中央书记处书记李琦涛率领下，今天乘火车回到北京。到车站迎接的有共青团中央书记处书记王照华等。朝鲜驻华大使馆二等秘书太虎也到车站迎接。
    瑞典芭蕾舞团离上海去南京
    据新华社上海1日电　瑞典皇家歌剧院芭蕾舞团结束了在上海的访问演出，今天上午乘专车离开上海去南京。中国人民对外文化协会上海市分会副会长陈虞孙和上海市文艺界人士多人到车站欢送。
    瑞典艺术家们在上海共演出四场，有近一万观众欣赏了他们演出的“天鹅湖”和“唐·吉呵德”片断、“浪子”、“C大调交响曲”和“仙女们”等节目。上海市副市长金仲华、上海市文化局局长孟波、中国人民对外文化协会上海市分会会长张春桥等出席观看了瑞典艺术家们的首次演出，金仲华副市长并接见了芭蕾舞团团长、瑞典皇家歌剧院经理海洛通，艺术指导、瑞典皇家歌剧院芭蕾舞团团长史吉平和主要演员。
    匈男女乒乓球队在广州比赛
    据新华社广州3日电　匈牙利国家男女乒乓球队选手2日晚在广州组成布达佩斯男女乒乓球二队，同广东男女乒乓球二队举行友谊比赛，结果客队男女队在团体赛中双双获胜。男子队的比数是五比一，女子队是三比零。
    匈牙利国家男女乒乓球队已经结束了在广州的访问比赛，3日乘火车去上海。8日和9日他们将同上海的乒乓球选手举行友谊比赛。截至目前，客队来我国后友谊比赛的战绩是：男子队一胜四负；女子队三胜二负。
    据新华社广州2日电　匈牙利国家男女乒乓球队选手1日晚在广州组成匈牙利布达佩斯男女乒乓球一队，同广东省男女乒乓球一队分别举行团体友谊比赛，结果客队男子队以二比五失败，女子队以三比零获胜。
    波“冶金”足球队在上海比赛
    据新华社上海2日电　波兰索斯诺维赤市“冶金”足球队1日在上海与上海市足球队举行友谊比赛。结果主队以三比一获胜。至此，客队来我国后进行的五次比赛结果是：一胜一平三负。有两万多观众在虹口体育场观看了双方的精采表演。
