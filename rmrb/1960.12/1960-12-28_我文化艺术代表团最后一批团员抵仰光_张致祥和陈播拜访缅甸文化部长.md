Title: Last Group of Members of Our Cultural and Art Delegation Arrives in Rangoon; Zhang Zhixiang and Chen Bo Visit Burmese Minister of Culture
Author:
Date: 1960-12-28
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, Rangoon, December 26th: Zhang Zhixiang, head of the Chinese Friendship Delegation to Burma's Cultural and Art Delegation and Deputy Director of the Commission for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries, led the third (and last) group of members of the delegation to Rangoon by plane this morning. They were warmly welcomed at the airport by Burmese government and cultural figures.

The Chinese Cultural and Art Delegation is part of the Friendship Delegation of the People's Republic of China visiting the Union of Burma, led by Premier Zhou Enlai. It will participate in the celebrations of the 13th anniversary of the independence of the Union of Burma and the exchange of ratification instruments for the Sino-Burmese Boundary Treaty.

Those who welcomed the delegation at the airport included U Ba Soe, Minister of Social Welfare and Religious Affairs, Federal Culture, and Health of the Union of Burma; U Ba Sein, Secretary of the Ministry of Culture; Colonel So Min, head of the Burmese Cultural Delegation to China; U Soe U, deputy head of the Burmese Cultural Delegation to China; and approximately four hundred well-known figures from the Burmese cultural world. Li Yimang, the Chinese Ambassador to Burma, Xiao Ming, the Cultural Counselor of the Embassy, and other officials also welcomed them at the airport.

Li Wei, Deputy Head of the Chinese Cultural and Art Delegation, Colonel Peng Hua, and Colonel Lu Ting, Head of the Chinese Sports Delegation, who had arrived earlier, also welcomed the delegation at the airport.

As Zhang Zhixiang and his party stepped off the plane, thunderous applause erupted from the crowd gathered at the airport to welcome the Chinese guests. The welcoming crowd waved bouquets and shouted, "Welcome to the Chinese Cultural and Art Delegation." A group of Burmese young women ran forward and presented flowers to Zhang Zhixiang and the Chinese artists. The entire airport was filled with a friendly and warm atmosphere.

Xinhua News Agency, Rangoon, December 27th: Zhang Zhixiang, head of the Chinese Friendship Delegation to Burma's Cultural and Art Delegation, Chen Bo, head of the Film Delegation, and key members of the two delegations, accompanied by Li Yimang, the Chinese Ambassador to Burma, and Xiao Ming, the Cultural Counselor, visited U Ba Soe, the Burmese Minister of Culture, this afternoon.

All members of the Chinese Cultural and Art Delegation and the Chinese Film Delegation visited the Shwedagon Pagoda in Rangoon this morning, paid their respects at the Burmese Martyrs' Mausoleum, and laid a wreath at the tomb of the Burmese national hero, General Aung San. The Chinese Sports Delegation to Burma, led by Colonel Lu Ting, visited Bago, about fifty-two miles from Rangoon, today. The Bago Municipal Committee hosted a luncheon for the Chinese Sports Delegation.

### 我文化艺术代表团最后一批团员抵仰光  张致祥和陈播拜访缅甸文化部长

1960-12-28
第5版()
专栏：

    我文化艺术代表团最后一批团员抵仰光
    张致祥和陈播拜访缅甸文化部长
    新华社仰光26日电　中国访缅友好代表团的文化艺术代表团团长、对外文化联络委员会副主任张致祥，率领代表团的第三批（最后一批）团员今天上午乘飞机到达仰光。他们在机场上受到缅甸政府和文化界人士的热烈欢迎。
    中国文化艺术代表团是周恩来总理率领的中华人民共和国访问缅甸联邦友好代表团的一部分。它将参加缅甸联邦独立十三周年和互换中缅边界条约批准书的庆典。
    到机场欢迎代表团的有缅甸联邦社会福利和宗教事务、联邦文化、卫生部长吴巴素，文化部秘书吴巴盛，缅甸访华文化代表团团长苏敏上校，缅甸访华文化代表团副团长吴苏吴和缅甸文化界的大约四百名知名人士。中国驻缅甸大使李一氓、大使馆文化参赞萧明和其他官员也到机场欢迎。
    早些时候已经到达这里的中国文化艺术代表团副团长李伟大校和彭华以及中国体育代表团团长鲁挺上校也到机场欢迎。
    当张致祥及其一行走下飞机时，聚集在机场上欢迎中国客人的人群中间响起了暴风雨般的掌声。欢迎群众挥舞花束，高呼：“欢迎中国文化艺术代表团”。一群缅甸女青年跑上前去，向张致祥和中国艺术家献花。整个机场充满了友好和热烈的气氛。
    新华社仰光27日电　中国访缅友好代表团文化艺术代表团团长张致祥、电影代表团团长陈播和两个代表团的主要团员在中国驻缅甸大使李一氓和文化参赞萧明陪同下，今天下午拜访了缅甸文化部长吴巴素。
    中国文化艺术代表团和中国电影代表团的全体团员今天上午在仰光参观了瑞光大金塔，拜谒了缅甸烈士墓，并向缅甸民族英雄昂山将军的陵墓献了花圈。由鲁挺上校率领的中国访缅友好代表团体育代表团，今天参观了离仰光约五十二英里的勃固。勃固市政委员会设午宴招待了中国体育代表团。
