    Sparks of Thought
    Why are we working so hard? It's because we know what today's hardships are for! If we weed quickly today, we'll harvest more grain tomorrow.
              — Wu Zhixiong, Gangkou Commune, Zhongshan County, Guangdong

    It doesn't matter if people are poor, the worst thing is to be poor in spirit; if the spirit isn't poor, people won't be poor.
                — Ma He, Xiashan Commune, Chaoyang, Guangdong

    If we are poor, we must turn it into wealth; if we are backward, we must turn it into advancement. No difficulty, however great, can stop our determination.
            — Chen Shulan, Gaotaizi People's Commune, Xinmin, Liaoning

    The countryside is a treasure trove of science, and the countryside is a crucible for forging people; it will temper you to be stronger and more powerful.
                — Zhang Shilan, Erhe Commune, Yidu, Shandong

### 思想的火花

1960-12-04
第8版()
专栏：

    思想的火花
    为什么我们干得这么猛啊？就是因为我们知道，目前的艰苦是为了什么？我们今天快除草，明天就会多打粮。
              ——广东中山县港口公社　吴志雄
    人穷不要紧，最怕是志穷；志不穷，人就不会穷。
                ——广东潮阳峡山公社　马　河
    穷，咱要把它变成富；落后，咱要把它变成先进，天大的困难，也挡不住咱们的决心。
            ——辽宁新民高台子人民公社　陈淑兰
    农村是一个科学的宝库，农村是炼人的熔炉，它会把你锻炼得更坚强、有力。
                ——山东益都洱河公社　张世兰
