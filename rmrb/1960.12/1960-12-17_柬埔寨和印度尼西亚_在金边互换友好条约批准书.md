Title: Cambodia and Indonesia Exchange Instruments of Ratification of Treaty of Friendship in Phnom Penh
Author:
Date: 1960-12-17
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, Phnom Penh, December 15th: The Kingdom of Cambodia and the Republic of Indonesia today exchanged instruments of ratification of the Cambodia-Indonesia Treaty of Friendship here. This Treaty of Friendship was signed when Prince Sihanouk visited Indonesia in February 1959.

During the ceremony, both sides emphasized that this treaty is the result of the Five Principles of Peaceful Coexistence and will further consolidate the good relations between the two countries.

### 柬埔寨和印度尼西亚  在金边互换友好条约批准书

1960-12-17
第6版()
专栏：

    柬埔寨和印度尼西亚
    在金边互换友好条约批准书
    新华社金边15日电　柬埔寨王国和印度尼西亚共和国今天在这里互换了柬埔寨和印度尼西亚友好条约批准书。这个友好条约是西哈努克亲王在1959年2月访问印度尼西亚时签订的。
    在举行仪式的时候，双方强调，这个条约是和平共处五项原则的成果，它将进一步巩固两国之间的良好关系。
