Title: Peace Delegation from Gunma Prefecture, Japan, Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1960-12-08
Page: 3

Xinhua News Agency, December 7th. The Japanese Peace Delegation from Gunma Prefecture, led by Chairman Torakame Ishiguro and Vice Chairmen Yasujiro Ando and Mitsuhiro Kaneko, arrived in Beijing by train today at the invitation of the Chinese People's Committee for World Peace.

Wang Mingyuan, Zheng Senyu, and Yang Chunsong, leading members of the Chinese People's Committee for World Peace and other organizations, were present at the station to welcome them.

Japanese peace advocate Kinkazu Saionji, who is currently in Beijing, also came to the station to greet them.

### 日本群马县和平代表团到京

1960-12-08
第3版()
专栏：

    日本群马县和平代表团到京
    新华社7日讯　应中国人民保卫世界和平委员会邀请前来我国访问的日本群马县和平代表团一行九人，在团长石黑寅龟和副团长安藤安次郎、金子满广率领下，今天乘火车到达北京。
    前往车站迎接的，有中国人民保卫世界和平委员会等团体的负责人王明远、郑森禹、杨春松等。
    在京的日本和平人士西园寺公一也到车站迎接。
