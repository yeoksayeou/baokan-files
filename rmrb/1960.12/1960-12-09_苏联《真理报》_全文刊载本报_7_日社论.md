Title: Soviet "Pravda" Publishes Full Text of This Newspaper's Editorial of the 7th

Author:
Date: 1960-12-09
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, December 8th: The Soviet newspaper "Pravda" today published the full text of the Chinese "People's Daily" editorial of December 7th, "The Victorious Banner, the Banner of Unity," on the fourth and fifth pages in the lower columns.

### 苏联《真理报》  全文刊载本报7日社论
