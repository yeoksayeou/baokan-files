Title: Chairman Liu Shaoqi Sends Congratulations on Finland's National Day
Author: Liu Shaoqi
Date: 1960-12-06
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, December 5th. Chairman of the People's Republic of China, Liu Shaoqi, today sent a telegram to the President of the Republic of Finland, Urho Kaleva Kekkonen, congratulating him on the 43rd anniversary of the founding of the Republic of Finland. The full text of the congratulatory telegram is as follows:
To His Excellency Urho Kaleva Kekkonen, President of the Republic of Finland:

	On the occasion of the 43rd anniversary of the founding of the Republic of Finland, I would like to extend my sincere congratulations to Your Excellency and the Finnish people on behalf of the Chinese people and in my own name. I wish that the friendly relations between China and Finland will be further consolidated and developed. I wish the Republic of Finland prosperity and its people happiness.
					Chairman of the People's Republic of China, Liu Shaoqi
					December 5, 1960

### 刘少奇主席电贺芬兰国庆
刘少奇
1960-12-06
第1版()
专栏：

    刘少奇主席电贺芬兰国庆
    新华社5日讯　中华人民共和国主席刘少奇今天打电报给芬兰共和国总统乌尔霍·卡勒瓦·吉科宁，祝贺芬兰共和国成立四十三周年。贺电全文如下：芬兰共和国总统乌尔霍·卡勒瓦·吉科宁阁下：
    值此芬兰共和国成立四十三周年之际，我谨代表中国人民，并且以我个人的名义向阁下和芬兰人民致热诚的祝贺。祝中芬两国间的友好关系进一步巩固和发展。祝芬兰共和国繁荣昌盛，人民幸福。
                 中华人民共和国主席　刘少奇　1960年12月5日
