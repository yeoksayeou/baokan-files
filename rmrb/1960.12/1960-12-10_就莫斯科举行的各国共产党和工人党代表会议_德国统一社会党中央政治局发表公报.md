Title: Communiqué Issued by the Politburo of the Central Committee of the Socialist Unity Party of Germany Regarding the Meeting of Representatives of Communist and Workers' Parties Held in Moscow
Author:
Date: 1960-12-10
Page: 6
Xinhua News Agency, Berlin, 9th. The Politburo of the Central Committee of the Socialist Unity Party of Germany issued a communiqué on December 7 regarding the meeting of representatives of Communist and Workers' Parties held in Moscow in November 1960, stating: "The Politburo suggests that the Party's district committees and grassroots organizations fully discuss the statement of the meeting of representatives of Communist and Workers' Parties at district activists' meetings and Party members' meetings. For this purpose, immediately begin studying the documents of the Moscow meeting and disseminate information about the Moscow meeting as widely as possible among the people." The communiqué stated, "The Politburo decided to submit a detailed report to the upcoming plenary session of the Central Committee and to propose a draft resolution."

### 就莫斯科举行的各国共产党和工人党代表会议  德国统一社会党中央政治局发表公报

1960-12-10
第6版()
专栏：

    就莫斯科举行的各国共产党和工人党代表会议
    德国统一社会党中央政治局发表公报
    新华社柏林9日电　德国统一社会党中央委员会政治局7日就1960年11月在莫斯科举行的各国共产党和工人党代表会议发表公报说：“政治局建议党的区委和基层组织在区的积极分子会议上和党员会议上充分讨论各国共产党和工人党代表会议声明，为此目的，立即开始学习莫斯科会议的文件和尽可能广泛地在人民中间进行关于莫斯科会议的传达。”公报说，“政治局决定向即将举行的中央委员会全体会议提交一份详细报告，并且提出一项决议草案。”
