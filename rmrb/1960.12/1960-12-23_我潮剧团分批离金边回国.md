Title: My Chaozhou Opera Troupe Leaves Phnom Penh for Home in Batches
Author: 
Date: 1960-12-23
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, Phnom Penh, 21st. Qi Feng, the deputy head of the Chinese Chaozhou Opera Troupe, and some members of the troupe, left here this afternoon to return to China after completing their visit and performance in Cambodia. The first group of troupe members, led by head Wang Kunlun, had already left here on the 9th.

The Chinese Chaozhou Opera Troupe arrived in Phnom Penh on October 28th for a friendly visit and gave a total of twenty-three performances during their month-long stay. Among those who went to the airport to see them off were the Director of the Cambodian Arts Bureau, Mann Mouly, and other Cambodian officials. Ye Jinghao, the chargé d'affaires of the Chinese Embassy, also went to the airport to see them off.

### 我潮剧团分批离金边回国

1960-12-23
第6版()
专栏：

    我潮剧团分批离金边回国
    新华社金边21日电中国潮剧团副团长祁峰和部分团员在结束了在柬埔寨的访问演出后今天下午离开这里回国。第一批团员在团长王昆仑率领下已经在9日离开这里。
    中国潮剧团是在10月28日到达金边作友好访问的，在一个多月的访问中，共演出了二十三场。前往机场欢送的有柬埔寨艺术局局长曼·穆里和柬埔寨其它官员。中国大使馆临时代办叶景灏也前往机场欢送。
