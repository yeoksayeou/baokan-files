Title: Korean People's Army Ensemble Arrives in Shenyang
Author:
Date: 1960-12-01
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Shenyang, November 30th. The Korean People's Army Ensemble, led by Major General Zhang Ruihuan, arrived in Shenyang this morning by special train from Tianjin, accompanied by Major General Wei Chuantong, representative of the Chinese Ministry of National Defense. More than two thousand people from the Shenyang Troops of the Chinese People's Liberation Army and various circles in Shenyang warmly welcomed the distinguished guests.

	Zeng Siyu, Lieutenant General, Zeng Shaoshan, Lieutenant General, Du Ping, Lieutenant General, responsible persons of the Shenyang Troops, Hu Yimin, Standing Committee Member and Secretary-General of the Liaoning Provincial Committee of the Chinese Communist Party, Che Xiangchen, Deputy Governor, Tang Hongguang, Vice President of the Liaoning Branch of the Sino-Korean Friendship Association, Wu Tieming, Secretary of the Shenyang Municipal Committee of the Chinese Communist Party, Song Guang, Deputy Mayor of Shenyang, and other party, government, and military leaders, as well as responsible persons from provincial and municipal cultural and artistic departments, welcomed the distinguished guests at the station. Former Volunteer Army heroes Zheng Qi, Huang Chouhe, Jin Kezhi, former representatives of Volunteer Army martyrs' families, and Liu Zhenyuan, director of the Sino-Korean People's Friendship Commune, also welcomed the distinguished guests.

	In the afternoon, the distinguished guests, accompanied by Li Boqiu, Major General, and others responsible for the Shenyang Troops, visited the Liaoning Industrial Exhibition Hall.

	In the evening, the Shenyang area troops of the Chinese People's Liberation Army, the Liaoning Provincial Committee of the Chinese Communist Party, the Liaoning Provincial People's Committee, the Shenyang Municipal Committee of the Chinese Communist Party, the Shenyang Municipal People's Committee, and the Liaoning Branch of the Sino-Korean Friendship Association jointly held a banquet to welcome the friendly envoys of the heroic Korean people. Chen Xilian, General, responsible person of the Shenyang area troops, Huang Huoqing, First Secretary of the Liaoning Provincial Committee of the Chinese Communist Party, and Zhang Ruihuan, Major General and head of the ensemble, all spoke. Before the banquet, Chen Xilian, General, Lai Chuanzhu, General, Huang Huoqing, First Secretary of the Liaoning Provincial Committee of the Chinese Communist Party, Che Xiangchen, Deputy Governor of Liaoning Province, Jiao Ruoyu, First Secretary of the Shenyang Municipal Committee of the Chinese Communist Party, and Song Guang, Deputy Mayor of Shenyang, received all the distinguished guests.

### 朝鲜人民军协奏团到沈阳

1960-12-01
第4版()
专栏：

    朝鲜人民军协奏团到沈阳
    据新华社沈阳30日电　朝鲜人民军协奏团在团长张瑞焕少将率领下，今天上午由我国国防部代表魏传统少将陪同，乘专车从天津到沈阳。中国人民解放军沈阳部队和沈阳各界两千多人热烈欢迎了贵宾。
    沈阳部队负责人曾思玉中将，曾绍山中将，杜平中将，中共辽宁省委常委、秘书长胡亦民，副省长车向忱，中朝友好协会辽宁省分会副会长唐宏光，中共沈阳市委书记吴铁鸣，沈阳市副市长宋光等党政军领导人和省市文化、艺术部门等负责人到车站欢迎贵宾。欢迎贵宾的还有前志愿军英雄郑起、黄丑和、金克智，前志愿军烈属代表和中朝人民友谊公社社长刘振远等。
    下午，贵宾们由沈阳部队负责人李伯秋少将等人陪同，参观了辽宁工业展览馆。
    晚上，中国人民解放军沈阳地区部队、中共辽宁省委员会、辽宁省人民委员会、中共沈阳市委员会、沈阳市人民委员会、中朝友好协会辽宁省分会联合举行宴会，欢迎英雄朝鲜人民的友好使者。沈阳地区部队负责人陈锡联上将、中共辽宁省委第一书记黄火青和协奏团团长张瑞焕少将都讲了话。宴会前，陈锡联上将、赖传珠上将、中共辽宁省委第一书记黄火青、辽宁省副省长车向忱、中共沈阳市委第一书记焦若愚、沈阳市副市长宋光接见了全体贵宾。
