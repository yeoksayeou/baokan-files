Title: Vice Premier Li Xiannian Hosts Banquet to Bid Farewell to Cuban Guests; Premier Zhou Enlai and Vice Premier Chen Yi Attend Banquet; Hosts and Guests Warmly Wish for the Continuous Development of the Fraternal Friendship Between the Chinese and Cuban People
Author:
Date: 1960-12-01
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, 30th December. Vice Premier Li Xiannian hosted a banquet in the Anhui Hall of the Great Hall of the People tonight to bid farewell Major Ernesto "Che" Guevara, President of the National Bank of Cuba and the Cuban revolutionary Government Economic Delegation which he leads.

Premier Zhou Enlai and Vice Premier Chen Yi attended the banquet.

Cuban Ambassador to China, Óscar Pino Santos, was invited to attend the banquet.

On the eve of their departure, representatives from all walks of life in the capital and from the heroic Cuban people gathered once again, and everyone seemed very happy. In an atmosphere of warmth and friendship, the hosts and guests warmly wished for the continuous development of the fraternal friendship between the people of China and Cuba.

Vice Premier Li Xiannian and Major Guevara spoke at the banquet successively. They enthusiastically expressed that the Chinese people and the Cuban people will always join hands in the struggle to build their respective beautiful countries and oppose imperialist aggression, and move from victory to greater victory.

Attending the banquet were Ye Jizhuang, Minister of Foreign Trade; Vice Ministers Li Qiang and Lu Xuzhang; Vice Minister of Foreign Affairs Geng Biao; Deputy Director of the State Planning Commission Song Yangchu; President of the People's Bank of China Cao Juru; Chinese Ambassador to Cuba Shen Jian; President of the Chinese-Latin American Friendship Association Chu Tunan; Vice Mayor of Beijing Wan Li and other responsible persons from all walks of life. Cuban foreign guests who are visiting Beijing were invited to attend the banquet. (Attached picture)

　　A banquet to bid farewell to the Cuban Revolutionary Government Economic Delegation. 　Our reporter Lü Xiangyou

### 李先念副总理设宴欢送古巴贵宾  周恩来总理、陈毅副总理出席宴会  宾主热情祝愿中古两国人民兄弟友谊不断发展

1960-12-01
第1版()
专栏：

    李先念副总理设宴欢送古巴贵宾
    周恩来总理、陈毅副总理出席宴会
    宾主热情祝愿中古两国人民兄弟友谊不断发展
    新华社30日讯　李先念副总理今晚在人民大会堂安徽厅举行宴会，欢送古巴国家银行行长埃·切·格瓦拉少校和由他率领的古巴革命政府经济代表团。
    周恩来总理、陈毅副总理出席了宴会。
    古巴驻中国大使奥斯卡·皮诺·桑托斯应邀出席了宴会。
    在临别的前夕，首都各方面的人士和英雄的古巴人民的代表们，再次相聚一堂，大家显得十分高兴。在一片热烈、友好的气氛中，宾主热情地祝愿中国和古巴两国人民的兄弟友谊不断发展。
    李先念副总理和格瓦拉少校先后在宴会上讲话，他们热情地表示，中国人民和古巴人民在建设各自美好的国家和反对帝国主义侵略的斗争中，将永远携手前进，从胜利走向更大的胜利。
    出席宴会作陪的有，对外贸易部部长叶季壮，副部长李强、卢绪章，外交部副部长耿飚，国家计划委员会副主任宋养初，中国人民银行行长曹菊如，中国驻古巴大使申健，中国拉丁美洲友好协会会长楚图南，北京市副市长万里等各方面的负责人士。正在北京访问的古巴外宾应邀出席了宴会。（附图片）
