Title: Chinese People Cherish Warm Feelings Towards the Soviet People - Soviet-Chinese Friendship Association Delegation Discusses Soviet-Chinese Friendship
Author:
Date: 1960-12-11
Page: 2

Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, 10th - According to TASS: Members of the Soviet-Chinese Friendship Association delegation, who visited China to participate in the celebration of the 43rd anniversary of the October Socialist Revolution at the invitation of the Sino-Soviet Friendship Association headquarters and have now returned home, pointed out that the Chinese people have always cherished warm feelings and sincere respect for their friends, the Soviet people. The delegation members gave talks on the visit at an enlarged meeting of the Council of the Soviet-Chinese Friendship Association on the 9th.

The head of the delegation, Stoletov, Minister of Higher and Secondary Specialized Education of the Russian Federation, said that the People's Republic of China had made great achievements in training cadres. He said that many higher technical schools are now equipped with the most modern equipment. Chinese friends all spoke of the great assistance given to them by Soviet experts.

Mitrofanov, the principal of the Higher Party School of the Central Committee of the CPSU, spoke of the delegation's meetings with Chinese national leaders and people from all walks of life. Mitrofanov emphasized that the Sino-Soviet Friendship Association plays a significant role in promoting knowledge about the Soviet Union.

The Council of the Soviet-Chinese Friendship Association also sent a telegram to the Council of the Sino-Soviet Friendship Association headquarters, expressing its heartfelt gratitude for the friendly reception of the delegation. The telegram said that the exchange of delegations contributes to the further development of the unbreakable and fraternal friendship between the Soviet Union and China.

### 中国人民对苏联人民怀着热烈感情  苏中友协代表团谈苏中友谊

1960-12-11
第2版()
专栏：

    中国人民对苏联人民怀着热烈感情
    苏中友协代表团谈苏中友谊
    新华社10日讯　据塔斯社莫斯科讯：应中苏友好协会总会邀请，到中国参加十月社会主义革命四十三周年庆祝活动后回国的苏中友好协会代表团的团员们指出，中国人民对自己的朋友——苏联人民，一向怀着热烈感情和真挚的敬意。代表团团员们9日在苏中友好协会理事会扩大会议上就这次访问发表了谈话。
    代表团团长、俄罗斯联邦高等和中等专业教育部部长斯托列托夫说，中华人民共和国在培养干部方面取得了巨大的成就。他说，许多高等技术学校现在都配备有最现代化的设备。中国朋友们都谈到苏联专家给予他们的巨大援助。
    苏共中央高级党校校长米特罗诺夫谈到代表团同中国国家领导人和各界人士的会见。米特罗诺夫强调指出，中苏友好协会在宣传有关苏联的知识方面起着巨大的作用。
    苏中友好协会理事会还致电中苏友好协会总会理事会，衷心地感谢它对代表团的友好接待。电报说，互派代表团有助于牢不可破的和兄弟般的苏中友谊的进一步发展。
