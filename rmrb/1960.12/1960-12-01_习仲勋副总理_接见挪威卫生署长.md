Title: Vice Premier Xi Zhongxun Receives the Norwegian Director of Health

Author:
Date: 1960-12-01
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, 30th — Vice Premier Xi Zhongxun received the Norwegian Director of Health, Karl Evang, and his wife this afternoon, and had a friendly conversation with them.

Present at the reception were Minister of Public Health Li Dequan, Director of the General Office Ouyang Jing, and Deputy Director of the Department of Western European Affairs of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs, Song Zhiguang.

### 习仲勋副总理  接见挪威卫生署长

1960-12-01
第4版()
专栏：

    习仲勋副总理
    接见挪威卫生署长
    新华社30日讯　习仲勋副总理今天下午接见挪威卫生署长卡尔·埃万和他的夫人，同他们进行了友好的谈话。
    接见时在座的有卫生部部长李德全、办公厅主任欧阳竞，外交部西欧司副司长宋之光。
