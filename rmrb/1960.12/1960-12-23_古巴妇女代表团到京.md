Title: Cuban Women's Delegation Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1960-12-23
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency, 22nd. A five-member Cuban women's delegation, led by the head of the delegation, Elena Hill, arrived in Beijing by plane this afternoon at the invitation of the All-China Women's Federation of the People's Republic of China for a visit. Those who welcomed them at the airport included Hu Naiqiu, Secretary of the All-China Women's Federation Secretariat, and Li Shuying, Vice Chairman of the National Committee of the China Light Industry Trade Union.
The wife of the Cuban Ambassador to China, Oscar Pino Santos, also welcomed them at the airport.

### 古巴妇女代表团到京

1960-12-23
第4版()
专栏：

    古巴妇女代表团到京
    新华社22日讯　应中华人民共和国全国妇女联合会邀请来我国参观访问的古巴妇女代表团一行五人，在团长埃伦娜·希尔率领下，今天下午乘飞机到达北京。到机场欢迎的有全国妇联书记处书记胡耐秋和中国轻工业工会全国委员会副主席李淑英等。
    古巴驻华大使奥斯卡·皮诺·桑托斯的夫人也到机场欢迎。
