Title: Malfunction Upon Takeoff, Shattered Bones in Mid-Air: US Satellite Launch Failure
Author:
Date: 1960-12-02
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, December 1st, New York dispatch: The United States Air Force suffered another failure in launching a satellite at Cape Canaveral on November 30th.

The United States Air Force authorities originally planned to use a rocket in this launch to send two satellites, weighing 203 pounds and 40 pounds respectively, into orbit at once. However, shortly after the launch, the rocket malfunctioned, and American officials had to push the button, blowing the rocket to pieces in the high sky.

### 离地生故障  空中碎身骨  美国发射卫星失败

1960-12-02
第6版()
专栏：

    离地生故障  空中碎身骨
    美国发射卫星失败
    新华社1日讯   纽约消息：美国空军11月30日在卡纳维拉尔角发射卫星又一次遭到失败。
    美国空军当局本来打算在这次发射中用一支火箭一次把两个各重二百零三磅和四十磅重的卫星送上轨道。但在发射后不久火箭就出了故障，美国官员不得不揿动按钮，让火箭在高空炸得粉碎。
