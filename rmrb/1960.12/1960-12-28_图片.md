    Xiang Yantong, the Party Secretary of the Zushidian People's Commune in Xupu County, Hunan Province, has consistently adhered to the "Four Togethernesses" (eating, living, working and consulting together with the masses) and adopted a method of combining leading cadres, technicians, and experienced farmers to lead production, achieving great results. The picture shows Xiang Yantong (right) taking advantage of the winter slack season to visit experienced farmers and summarize production experience.
                           Xinhua News Agency Reporter Wang Ping and Song Yunfeng
