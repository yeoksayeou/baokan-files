Title: U.S. Department of Labor Announces U.S. Unemployment to Reach Five Million
Author:
Date: 1960-12-17
Page: 6

    U.S. Department of Labor Announces
    U.S. Unemployment to Reach Five Million
    Xinhua News Agency, 13th dispatch from Washington: The U.S. Department of Labor announced on the 12th that the number of unemployed in the United States in November has exceeded four million, an increase of 452,000 over October.
    This figure is the highest November unemployment figure in the United States since the capitalist world economic crisis of 1930.
    The Department of Labor said that the number of unemployed in the United States will reach at least five million in December.
    Previously, the "Wall Street Journal" estimated that the number of unemployed in the United States in November was 4.2 million.

### 美国劳工部宣布  美失业人数将达五百万

1960-12-17
第6版()
专栏：

    美国劳工部宣布
    美失业人数将达五百万
    新华社13日讯　华盛顿消息：美国劳工部12日宣布，11月份美国失业人数已经超过四百万人，比10月份又增加了四十五万两千人。
    这个数字是1930年资本主义世界经济危机以来美国11月份的最高失业数字。
    劳工部说，12月份美国失业人数至少将达到五百万人。
    前据《华尔街日报》估计，11月份美国失业人数达四百二十万人。
