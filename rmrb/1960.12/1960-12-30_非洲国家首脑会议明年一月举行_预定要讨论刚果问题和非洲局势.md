Title: African Heads of State Conference to be Held Next January; Expected to Discuss the Congo Question and the African Situation
Author:
Date: 1960-12-30
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, Rabat, December 27th: Moroccan Minister of Information and Tourism, Alaoui, announced at a press conference tonight that the African Heads of State Conference is scheduled to be held in Casablanca on January 3rd next year. He said that the upcoming conference will not only study the Congo issue, but also the African situation. France's nuclear test in the Sahara, disarmament issues, and Africa's relations with other parts of the world will also be on the conference agenda.

The Minister pointed out that the Congo issue not only concerns the Congo itself, but also all the people of Africa.

Xinhua News Agency, Cairo, December 28th: UAR President Nasser left Alexandria this afternoon aboard the UAR ship "Liberty" for Rabat to attend the African Heads of State Conference to be held in Casablanca on January 3rd.

Accompanying Nasser were UAR Central Government Minister of Interior Zakaria Mohieddin, Minister of State for Presidential Affairs Sabri, and Foreign Minister Fawzi.

### 非洲国家首脑会议明年一月举行  预定要讨论刚果问题和非洲局势

1960-12-30
第6版()
专栏：

    非洲国家首脑会议明年一月举行
    预定要讨论刚果问题和非洲局势
    新华社拉巴特27日电　摩洛哥新闻和游历大臣阿劳维今晚在记者招待会上宣布，非洲国家首脑会议定于明年1月3日在卡萨布兰卡召开。他说，即将举行的会议不仅要研究刚果问题，并且还要研究非洲局势问题。法国在撒哈拉试验原子弹问题、裁军问题和非洲同世界其他地方的关系问题也将列入会议议程。
    这位大臣指出，刚果问题不仅关系到刚果本身，而且还关系到全体非洲人民。
    新华社开罗28日电　阿联总统纳赛尔今天下午乘阿联船只“自由号”离开亚历山大港前往拉巴特，以便出席将于1月3日在卡萨布兰卡举行的非洲国家首脑会议。
    陪同纳赛尔前往的有阿联中央政府内政部长查卡里亚·毛希丁、负责总统事务的国务部长萨布里和外交部长法齐。
