Title: Swedish Royal Opera Ballet Returns Home
Author:
Date: 1960-12-17
Page: 4
　
Xinhua News Agency, December 15th: The Swedish Royal Opera Ballet, a group of fifty-one people led by团长 Hailuotong and artistic director Shijiping, visited Beijing, Hangzhou, Shanghai, Nanjing, Tianjin and other cities, and took a flight back to their country from Beijing this morning.

Among those who went to the airport to see them off were Ding Xilin, Vice President of the Chinese People's Association for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries, and others.

Chargé d'affaires Volveous of the Swedish Embassy in China also went to the airport to see them off.

At noon on the 14th, Chu Tunan, President of the Chinese People's Association for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries, hosted a luncheon to bid farewell to the artists of the Swedish Royal Opera Ballet.

### 瑞典皇家歌剧院芭蕾舞团回国

1960-12-17
第4版()
专栏：

    瑞典皇家歌剧院芭蕾舞团回国
    据新华社15日讯　瑞典皇家歌剧院芭蕾舞团一行五十一人，在团长海洛通、艺术指导史吉平的率领下访问了北京、杭州、上海、南京、天津等城市后，于今日上午乘飞机离京返国。
    前往机场欢送的有中国人民对外文化协会副会长丁西林等。
    瑞典驻中国大使馆代办沃尔威欧斯也到机场欢送。
    14日中午，中国人民对外文化协会会长楚图南设便宴欢送瑞典皇家歌剧院芭蕾舞团艺术家。
