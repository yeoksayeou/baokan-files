Title: Concert of the Korean People's Army Ensemble Performed in Pyongyang
Author:
Date: 1960-12-17
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, Pyongyang Dispatch: The Korean People's Army Ensemble, led by Major General Zhang Ruihuan, which returned from a friendly performance tour in China, gave a report-back performance at the Pyongyang Grand Theatre on the evening of the 12th.

Among those who watched the performance were Vice Minister of Foreign Affairs of Korea, Park Kwang-seon, Deputy Director of the General Political Bureau of the Korean People's Army, Major General Li Chang-soo, as well as more than two thousand people, including officers and soldiers of the People's Army and people from cultural and artistic circles in Pyongyang.

Chinese Ambassador to Korea Qiao Xiaoguang, Military Attache Colonel Zhang Leting, other diplomatic officials of the Embassy, and personnel from various countries' embassies in Korea also watched the performance.

The Korean People's Army Ensemble performed many songs and dances from both China and Korea, such as "March of the Volunteers," "Marching to the Decisive Battlefield," "The Day of Distribution," and "Spring Comes to the Tea Mountain." When the ensemble performed the dances "Comrades-in-Arms" and "Reunion on the Highland," reflecting the militant friendship forged in blood between the Chinese and Korean armies, the whole audience burst into prolonged and warm applause. After the performance, personnel from the Chinese Embassy in Korea presented flowers to the performers.

### 朝鲜人民军协奏团在平壤作汇报演出

1960-12-17
第5版()
专栏：

    朝鲜人民军协奏团在平壤作汇报演出
    新华社平壤电　从中国作友好访问演出归来的、由张瑞焕少将率领的朝鲜人民军协奏团，12日晚上在平壤大剧场举行了汇报演出。
    观看演出的有朝鲜外务省副相朴光善、朝鲜人民军总政治局副局长李长秀少将，以及人民军官兵、平壤市文化艺术界人士等二千多人。
    中国驻朝鲜大使乔晓光、武官张乐亭上校和大使馆其他外交官员以及各国驻朝鲜大使馆人员也观看了演出。
    朝鲜人民军协奏团演出了“志愿军战歌”、“走向决战场”、“分配的日子”、“春到茶山”等中朝两国的许多歌曲和舞蹈节目。当协奏团演出反映中朝两国军队以鲜血凝成的战斗友谊的舞蹈“战友”和“高地重逢”时，全场响起长时间的热烈的掌声。演出后，中国驻朝鲜大使馆人员向演员们献了花。
