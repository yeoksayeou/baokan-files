Title: Ethiopian Authorities Decide to Ban Sales of Two American Magazines
Author: 
Date: 1960-12-29
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, Addis Ababa, December 26th, TASS: Ethiopian authorities have decided to ban the sale of the latest issues of the American magazines "Time" and "Newsweek" in Ethiopia because they contained false reports about the recent events in Ethiopia.

    The "Ethiopian Herald" wrote in an editorial that "the articles in these magazines are sensationalist nonsense concocted with an unfriendly attitude towards the Ethiopian people."

### 埃塞俄比亚当局决定  禁售两家美国杂志

1960-12-29
第6版()
专栏：

    埃塞俄比亚当局决定
    禁售两家美国杂志
    新华社27日讯塔斯社亚的斯亚贝巴26日讯：埃塞俄比亚当局决定禁止最近一期的美国《时代》周刊和《新闻周刊》在埃塞俄比亚出售，因为它们对埃塞俄比亚不久前发生的事件作了虚假的报道。
    《埃塞俄比亚先驱报》在社论中写道，“这些杂志的文章是怀着对埃塞俄比亚人民不友好的态度编造出来的耸人听闻的胡言乱语”。
