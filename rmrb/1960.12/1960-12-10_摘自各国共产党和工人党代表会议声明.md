Title: Statement from the Meeting of Representatives of Communist and Workers' Parties of All Countries

Author:
Date: 1960-12-10
Page: 7

    The complete and thorough elimination of all forms and manifestations of the colonialist system is required by the entire course of world history in recent decades. All possible support should be given to all peoples still under colonialist enslavement in their struggle for national independence. All forms of colonial oppression must be abolished. The elimination of colonialism will also be of extremely great significance in easing international tensions and consolidating universal peace.
    ——Excerpt from the Statement of the Meeting of Representatives of Communist and Workers' Parties of All Countries

### 摘自各国共产党和工人党代表会议声明

1960-12-10
第7版()
专栏：

    完全彻底消灭殖民主义制度的一切形式和一切表现，是近几十年来整个世界历史进程所要求的。对于那些还处在殖民主义奴役下的所有人民，在他们争取民族独立方面应当给予全力的支持。必须废除一切形式的殖民主义压迫。消灭殖民主义，对缓和国际紧张局势和巩固普遍和平，也将具有极其伟大的意义。
    ——摘自各国共产党和工人党代表会议声明
