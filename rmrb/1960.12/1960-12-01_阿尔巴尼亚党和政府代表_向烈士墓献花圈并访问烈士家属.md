Title: Albanian Party and Government Representatives Lay Wreaths at Martyrs' Graves and Visit Families of Martyrs
Author:
Date: 1960-12-01
Page: 5
Xinhua News Agency, Tirana, 29th. On the occasion of the 16th anniversary of the liberation of Albania, representatives of the Central Committee of the Albanian Labor Party, the Council of Ministers, the Presidium of the People's Assembly, and mass organizations laid wreaths at the graves of Albanian martyrs and at the martyrs' monuments.

The remains of Stafa, Spiru, Shanto, Kondi, and others, former members of the Central Committee of the Albanian Labor Party, are buried in the martyrs' cemetery. Albanian Party and government leaders and representatives of mass organizations also visited the families of the martyrs who sacrificed themselves for the liberation of Albania.

### 阿尔巴尼亚党和政府代表  向烈士墓献花圈并访问烈士家属

1960-12-01
第5版()
专栏：

    阿尔巴尼亚党和政府代表
    向烈士墓献花圈并访问烈士家属
    新华社地拉那29日电　在阿尔巴尼亚解放十六周年的时候，阿尔巴尼亚劳动党中央委员会、部长会议、人民议会主席团和群众团体的代表到阿尔巴尼亚烈士墓及烈士纪念碑前献花圈。
    烈士墓中安葬着前阿尔巴尼亚劳动党中央委员会委员斯塔法、斯比卢、尚托、康迪等的遗体。阿尔巴尼亚党和政府领导人及群众团体的代表还访问了为阿尔巴尼亚解放而牺牲的烈士们的家属。
