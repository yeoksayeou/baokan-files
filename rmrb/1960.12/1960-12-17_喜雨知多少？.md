Welcome Rain: How Much Do You Know?
	Zhi Guang
This winter, North China has generally experienced auspicious snow, while the southern regions have again received widespread welcome rain (as reported in the "People's Daily" on November 25th). This is equivalent to a winter irrigation, which can prevent sudden weather changes and cooling from freezing winter wheat, and is very beneficial for the tillering, growth, sowing, and emergence of winter wheat.

So, how much rain has actually fallen? Please see the table below:
	Name			1-Minute Rainfall (Water Depth)			Condition
	Drizzle			Below 0.02 mm					Ground not wet or slightly wet; rain almost unnoticeable if not paying attention
	Light Rain		0.02 - 0.05 mm					Ground completely wet, but no standing water
	Moderate Rain	0.05 - 0.25 mm					Standing water on the ground, sound of rain audible
	Heavy Rain		0.25 - 1 mm						Standing water everywhere, loud rain sound, thin cloth umbrellas soaked through and leaking
	Torrential Rain	Above 1 mm						Heavy rain pouring down; drainage system unable to cope; water overflows; opening the window makes it impossible to hear people talking inside

The above refers to the intensity of rain "bursts" in a short period (1 minute). However, the current rain grade standards issued by Chinese meteorological observatories are based on the total rainfall within a day: 0.1-10 mm is considered light rain, 10-25 mm is moderate rain, 25-50 mm is heavy rain, and above 50 mm is called torrential rain. If we know the duration of a certain grade of rain, we can roughly determine whether it is heavy rain or light rain today using the table above.

### 喜雨知多少？
之光
1960-12-17
第8版()
专栏：小常识

    喜雨知多少？
    之光
    今冬华北地区已普降瑞雪，南方地区则又遍获喜雨（11月25日《人民日报》载）。这等于进行了一次冬灌，可以防止天气骤然变化降温冻坏冬小麦，对冬小麦的分蘖、生长、播种和出苗有很大好处。
    那末究竟降了多少雨水呢？请看下表：名称　1分钟雨量（水深）　状况微雨　0.02毫米以下　地面不湿或稍湿，如不加注意似无雨小雨　0.02—0.05毫米　地面已全湿，但无积水中雨　0.05—0.25毫米　地面积水，可闻雨声大雨　0.25—1毫米　遍地积水，雨声激烈可闻，薄的布伞湿透并渗进水滴暴雨　1毫米以上　大雨倾盆而下，下水道不及排水常有外溢，一开窗子，室内即听不见说话声以上是指短时间（1分钟）内雨“暴”的程度，而现在我国气象台发布的雨级标准是按一天内雨量的总量来划分的：0.1—10毫米为小雨，10—25毫米为中雨，25—50毫米则是大雨，50毫米以上则称暴雨了。我们如果知道某级雨下的时间，利用上表就可以大致知道今天是大雨或者是小雨了。
