Title: Cuban Confederation of Labor's Foreign Relations Secretary Cables Liu Ningyi: Welcoming the Signing of the Economic Cooperation Agreement; Cuban-Chinese Friendship Association Thanks China for its Aid to Cuba
Author:
Date: 1960-12-10
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, December 6th: Liu Ningyi, Chairman of the All-China Federation of Trade Unions, received a cable yesterday from Odon Alvarez de la Campa, Foreign Relations Secretary of the Cuban Workers' Confederation, welcoming the Sino-Cuban economic cooperation agreement. The telegram stated: "The Cuban Workers' Confederation, on behalf of the Cuban proletariat and people, thanks the Chinese government and people, under the leadership of their leader Mao Zedong, for signing an economic cooperation agreement with Cuba to purchase one million tons of sugar and provide an interest-free loan of sixty million pesos. This is a great demonstration of the Chinese workers and people's support for the Cuban revolution and proletarian internationalism."

Xinhua News Agency, Havana, December 8th: The Cuban-Chinese Friendship Association issued a statement today, paying tribute to China for providing assistance to Cuba under the recent economic cooperation agreement signed between the two countries.

The statement pointed out that at a time when imperialism is trying to destroy Cuba's glorious liberation movement, China's aid to Cuba has moved all Cubans who regard their beautiful motherland as their highest ideal.

The statement said that the Chinese government provides factory equipment and interest-free loans to this distant American island nation; a government like the developing China is our friend, and we cannot but express our gratitude.

### 古巴工联对外关系书记致电刘宁一  欢迎两国签订经济合作协定  古中友协感谢中国对古巴的援助

1960-12-10
第6版()
专栏：

    古巴工联对外关系书记致电刘宁一
    欢迎两国签订经济合作协定
    古中友协感谢中国对古巴的援助
    新华社6日讯　中华全国总工会主席刘宁一昨天收到了古巴工人联合会对外关系书记奥东·阿耳瓦雷斯·德拉坎帕发来的电报，对中古经济合作协定表示欢迎。电报说：“古巴工人联合会谨代表古巴无产阶级和人民，感谢中国政府和人民在他们的领袖毛泽东的领导下，与古巴签订了一项购买一百万吨糖和给予六千万比索的无息贷款的经济合作协定。这是中国工人和人民对古巴革命的支持和无产阶级国际主义的伟大证明。”
    新华社哈瓦那8日电　古巴—中国友好协会今天发表声明，为中国根据两国最近签订的经济合作协定对古巴提供援助向中国致敬。
    声明指出，在帝国主义伺机摧毁古巴光荣的解放运动的时候，中国对古巴采取的援助行动感动了古巴所有那些把美丽的祖国看作是他们最高理想的人。
    声明说，中国政府为这个遥远的美洲岛国提供工厂设备和无息贷款，一个像正在充分发展着的中国这样的政府是我们的朋友，它使我们不能不表示感谢。
