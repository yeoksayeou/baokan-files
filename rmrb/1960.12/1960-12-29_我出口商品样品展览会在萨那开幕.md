Title: My Export Commodities Sample Exhibition Opens in Sana'a
Author:
Date: 1960-12-29
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, Taiz, December 27th: After being exhibited to the public in Hodeidah, the Chinese Export Commodities Sample Exhibition opened yesterday in Sana'a, the first capital of Yemen.

Attending the opening ceremony were the representative of Yemeni Crown Prince Badr, Yemeni Health Minister Muhammad Ali Uthman, some ministers, government officials, and businessmen.

After being exhibited here, the exhibition will move to Taiz.

### 我出口商品样品展览会在萨那开幕

1960-12-29
第5版()
专栏：

    我出口商品样品展览会在萨那开幕
    据新华社塔兹27日电　中国出口商品样品展览会在荷台达向也门公众展出后，昨天在也门第一首都萨那开幕。
    参加这一开幕典礼的有也门王太子巴德尔的代表、也门卫生大臣穆罕默德·阿里·乌斯曼，一些大臣、政府官员和商人。
    展览会在这里展出后，还将迁往塔兹展出。
