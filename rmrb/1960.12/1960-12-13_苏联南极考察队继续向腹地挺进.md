Title: Soviet Antarctic Expedition Continues Inland
Author:
Date: 1960-12-13
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, December 9th. TASS Moscow, December 8th reported: The Soviet Fifth Antarctic Expedition's sledge-tractor train has successfully completed the first stage of its long march from the Davis Coast to the Antarctic interior, arriving at the "Komsomolskaya" station. The train departed from "Mirny" station on October 24th and has traveled 1,100 kilometers to date.

    Different from previous long marches, the sledge-tractor train, starting 135 kilometers from "Mirny", deviated southwest towards 72 degrees south latitude and 87 degrees east longitude, taking a new route to "Komsomolskaya" station. The new route is 200 kilometers longer than the old route and passes through previously unexplored areas.

    After a short rest and equipment inspection, the train will continue south towards the interior.

### 苏联南极考察队继续向腹地挺进

1960-12-13
第5版()
专栏：

    苏联南极考察队继续向腹地挺进
    据新华社9日讯　据塔斯社莫斯科8日报道：苏联第五南极考察队的雪撬——牵引车队已经顺利地完成了从戴维斯海岸前往南极腹地长征的第一阶段，抵达“共青团”站。车队是10月24日从“和平村”出发，到目前为止已经走了一千一百公里。
    与过去历次长征不同的是：雪撬——牵引车队从离开“和平村”一百三十五公里起，就偏向南纬七十二度、东经八十七度的西南方向，沿着新路前往“共青团”站。新路比旧路长二百公里，是从未被考察过的地区。
    在短期休息和检查机件后，车队将继续向南前往腹地。
