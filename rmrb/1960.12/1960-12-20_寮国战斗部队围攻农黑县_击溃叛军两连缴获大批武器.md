Title: Lao Patriotic Armed Forces Besiege Nong Het County, Annihilate Two Rebel Battalions, Seize Large Quantities of Weapons

Author:

Date: 1960-12-20
Page: 7

Xinhua News Agency, Hanoi, December 19th: The Lao Patriotic Armed Forces radio station broadcasted today that the Lao Patriotic Armed Forces, acting on the instructions of the Phouma legitimate government, have surrounded and attacked the Phoumi Nosavan rebel forces in Nong Het County, Xieng Khouang Province, and on the 16th, annihilated two rebel battalions reinforcing Nong Het County. The Lao Patriotic Armed Forces killed and wounded many enemy troops and seized a large number of weapons and military supplies.

### 寮国战斗部队围攻农黑县  击溃叛军两连缴获大批武器

1960-12-20
第7版()
专栏：

    寮国战斗部队围攻农黑县
    击溃叛军两连缴获大批武器
    新华社河内19日电　据寮国战斗部队电台今天广播，寮国战斗部队执行富马合法政府的指示，已包围和进攻川圹省农黑县的富米·诺萨万叛军，并在16日击溃了农黑县增援的二连叛军。寮国战斗部队击毙击伤敌军多人，并缴获了大批武器和军用品。
