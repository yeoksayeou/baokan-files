Title: Vice Premier Bo Yibo Holds Banquet Welcoming Vietnamese Economic and Trade Delegation
Author:
Date: 1960-12-04
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, December 3rd: Vice Premier Bo Yibo held a banquet tonight in the Xinjiang Hall of the Great Hall of the People to warmly welcome all the distinguished guests of the Economic and Trade Delegation of the Government of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam, headed by Vice Premier Nguyen Duy Trinh.

At the banquet, which was filled with a friendly atmosphere, comrades from China and Vietnam repeatedly raised their glasses, wishing for the unbreakable friendship between the people of China and Vietnam to develop forever, and for Chairman Mao Zedong and President Ho Chi Minh to enjoy health and longevity.

Vice Premier Bo Yibo, on behalf of the Chinese government and the Chinese people, extended the warmest welcome to the distinguished Vietnamese guests from the fraternal neighboring country.

In his speech, Vice Premier Bo Yibo warmly praised the brotherly friendship between the people of China and Vietnam, which is based on Marxism-Leninism and proletarian internationalism. Vice Premier Bo Yibo said happily that under the leadership of the Vietnam Workers' Party headed by President Ho Chi Minh, the hardworking and brave Vietnamese people have made remarkable achievements in socialist revolution and socialist construction, and are making unremitting efforts for the unification of their motherland. He said that the Chinese people regard the brilliant achievements made by the Vietnamese people as joyful and inspiring as the victories of our own people.

Vice Premier Bo Yibo emphasized: The Chinese people will always stand with the Vietnamese people to strengthen the solidarity of the socialist camp, to strengthen the economic cooperation between China and Vietnam, and to make greater efforts to promote the continuous upsurge of socialist economic construction in both countries.

Vice Premier Nguyen Duy Trinh spoke at the banquet. He said that the Vietnamese government's economic and trade delegation feels very honored to come to China for negotiations on economic cooperation and trade between the two countries.

Vice Premier Nguyen Duy Trinh said that President Ho Chi Minh often said that Vietnam and China have a relationship as close as lips and teeth. This long-standing friendship has been continuously developed and consolidated on the basis of proletarian internationalism.

In his speech, Vice Premier Nguyen Duy Trinh thanked the Chinese people for their help to the Vietnamese people. He pointed out that the Vietnamese people are struggling to further promote socialist construction and industrialization in North Vietnam. He finally proposed a toast to the eternal friendship between the people of Vietnam and China.

Vietnamese Ambassador to China Tran Tu Binh and diplomatic officials of the embassy were invited to attend the banquet.

Also present at the banquet were Ye Jiuzhuang, Minister of Foreign Trade, Li Qiang, Vice Minister of Foreign Trade, Ji Pengfei, Vice Minister of Foreign Affairs, Cao Lu, Vice Minister of Light Industry, Li Su, Vice Minister of Chemical Industry, Fang Yi, China's Economic Representative to Vietnam, Yang Xiufeng, President of the China-Vietnam Friendship Association, and Feng Jiping, Deputy Mayor of Beijing.

Xinhua News Agency, December 3rd: Nguyen Duy Trinh, Vice Premier of the Government of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam and head of the Economic and Trade Delegation of the Government of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam, who came to visit our country, arrived in Beijing by special plane this afternoon.

At the Capital Airport, which was fluttering with the national flags of China and Vietnam, Vice Premier Nguyen Duy Trinh was warmly welcomed by Vice Premier Bo Yibo and received flowers from young women.

Also at the airport to welcome him were Ye Jiuzhuang, Minister of Foreign Trade, Li Qiang, Vice Minister of Foreign Trade, Ji Pengfei, Vice Minister of Foreign Affairs, Cao Lu, Vice Minister of Light Industry, Li Su, Vice Minister of Chemical Industry, Fang Yi, China's Economic Representative to Vietnam, and Yang Xiufeng, President of the China-Vietnam Friendship Association, and Feng Jiping, Deputy Mayor of Beijing.

Li Ban, Deputy Head of the Economic and Trade Delegation of the Government of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam and Vice Minister of Foreign Trade, Ngo Minh Loan, Vice Minister of Light Industry, and members and staff of the delegation, who had arrived in Beijing earlier, were also at the airport to greet him.

Also at the airport to greet him were Vietnamese Ambassador to China Tran Tu Binh and diplomatic officials from the embassy.

Xinhua News Agency, December 3rd: Vice Premier of the State Council Bo Yibo met tonight with the distinguished guests of the Economic and Trade Delegation of the Government of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam, headed by Vice Premier Nguyen Duy Trinh, and had a cordial conversation with them.

Also present at the meeting were Ye Jiuzhuang, Ji Pengfei, Li Qiang, and Fang Yi.

Vietnamese Ambassador to China Tran Tu Binh was also present at the meeting. (Attached Pictures)

At the airport, he was warmly welcomed by Vice Premier Bo Yibo and others. (Xinhua News Agency reporter Li Changyong)

### 薄一波副总理举行宴会  欢迎越南经济贸易代表团

1960-12-04
第4版()
专栏：

    薄一波副总理举行宴会
    欢迎越南经济贸易代表团
    新华社3日讯　薄一波副总理今晚在人民大会堂新疆厅举行宴会，热烈欢迎以阮维桢副总理为首的越南民主共和国政府经济、贸易代表团的全体贵宾。
    在充满一片友好气氛的宴会上，中越两国同志不断举杯，祝中越两国人民牢不可破的友谊永远发展，祝毛泽东主席和胡志明主席健康长寿。
    薄一波副总理代表中国政府和中国人民向来自兄弟邻邦的越南贵宾，表示最热烈的欢迎。
    薄一波副总理在讲话中热情称颂建立在马克思列宁主义和无产阶级国际主义基础上的中越两国人民的兄弟友谊。薄一波副总理高兴地说，勤劳勇敢的越南人民在以胡志明主席为首的越南劳动党的领导下，在社会主义革命和社会主义建设中取得了卓越成就，为祖国的统一进行着坚持不懈的努力。他说，中国人民把越南人民所取得的光辉成就，看成同我国人民的胜利一样而感到欢欣鼓舞。
    薄一波副总理强调地说：中国人民将永远和越南人民一道，为加强社会主义阵营的团结，为加强中越两国的经济合作，促进两国社会主义经济建设不断高涨的共同事业而作出更大的努力。
    阮维桢副总理在宴会上讲话。他说，越南政府经济、贸易代表团到中国来进行两国经济合作和贸易的谈判，我们感到十分荣幸。
    阮维桢副总理说，胡志明主席经常说，越中两国有着唇齿相依的关系。这种悠久的友谊关系在无产阶级国际主义的基础上，不断得到了发展和巩固。
    阮维桢副总理在讲话中感谢中国人民对越南人民的帮助。他指出越南人民正在为进一步推进越南北方的社会主义建设和工业化而斗争。他最后提议为越中两国人民永恒的友谊而干杯。
    越南驻中国大使陈子平和大使馆外交官员应邀出席了宴会。
    出席宴会作陪的有，对外贸易部部长叶季壮、副部长李强，外交部副部长姬鹏飞，轻工业部副部长曹鲁，化学工业部副部长李苏，中国驻越南经济代表方毅，中国越南友好协会会长杨秀峰，北京市副市长冯基平等。
    新华社3日讯　前来我国访问的越南民主共和国政府副总理、越南民主共和国政府经济、贸易代表团团长阮维桢，今天下午乘专机到达北京。
    在飘扬着中越两国国旗的首都机场上，阮维桢副总理受到薄一波副总理的热烈欢迎，并且接受了女青年的献花。
    到飞机场欢迎的，还有对外贸易部部长叶季壮、副部长李强，外交部副部长姬鹏飞，轻工业部副部长曹鲁，化工部副部长李苏，中国驻越南经济代表方毅、中国越南友好协会会长杨秀峰，北京市副市长冯基平等。
    先期到达北京的越南民主共和国政府经济、贸易代表团副团长、外贸部副部长李班，轻工业部副部长吴明鸾，和代表团团员、工作人员，也到机场迎接。
    到机场迎接的还有越南驻中国大使陈子平和大使馆的外交官员。
    新华社3日讯　国务院副总理薄一波今晚会见以阮维桢副总理为首的越南民主共和国政府经济、贸易代表团的贵宾，并且同他们进行了亲切的谈话。
    会见时在座的，有叶季壮、姬鹏飞、李强、方毅等。
    会见时，越南驻中国大使陈子平也在座。（附图片）
