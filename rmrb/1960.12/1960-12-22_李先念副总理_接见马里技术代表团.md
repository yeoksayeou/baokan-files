Title: Vice Premier Li Xiannian Receives Malian Technical Delegation
Author:
Date: 1960-12-22
Page: 4

    Xinhua News Agency, December 21st. Vice Premier of the State Council Li Xiannian received the members of the Republic of Mali technical delegation visiting China this afternoon: Construction Engineer Silla Fodi and Agricultural Technician Dialla Tidiani, and had a friendly conversation with them.
    Present at the reception were: Vice Minister of Light Industry Wang Xinyuan, Director of the West Asia and Africa Department of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs He Ying, Deputy Director of the Foreign Trade Department Li Yan, etc. (Attached photo)
    December 21, Vice Premier Li Xiannian received Silla Fodi, construction engineer, and Dialla Tidiani, agricultural technician, of the Republic of Mali technical delegation.
   Vice Premier Li Xiannian talking with Silla Fodi (center) and Dialla Tidiani (right)
                                  Xinhua News Agency reporter Niu Weiyu

### 李先念副总理  接见马里技术代表团

1960-12-22
第4版()
专栏：

    李先念副总理
    接见马里技术代表团
    新华社21日讯　国务院副总理李先念在今天下午接见了来我国访问的马里共和国技术代表团的成员：建筑工程师西拉·福迪和农业技师迪亚拉·蒂迪亚尼，并且同他们进行了友好的谈话。
    接见时在座的有：轻工业部副部长王新元，外交部西亚非洲司司长何英，对外贸易部副局长李炎等。（附图片）
    12月21日，李先念副总理接见了马里共和国技术代表团建筑工程师西拉·福迪和农业技师迪亚拉·蒂迪亚尼。
