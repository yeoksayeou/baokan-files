Title: China's Ministry of National Defense Presents Gifts to Myanmar's Defense Forces
Author:
Date: 1960-12-30
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, Yangon, December 28th: Li Wei, Deputy Head of the Chinese Cultural and Art Delegation and Deputy Director of the Propaganda Department under the General Political Department of the Chinese People's Liberation Army, with the rank of Senior Colonel, presented a set of special effects lighting equipment for stage use to the Myanmar Defense Forces on behalf of the Ministry of National Defense of the People's Republic of China.

Brigadier General Aung Gyi, Deputy Chief of Staff of the Myanmar Defense Forces, accepted the gift on behalf of the Myanmar Defense Forces and expressed gratitude to China's Ministry of National Defense.

Zhang Zhixiang, Head of the Chinese Cultural and Art Delegation, Peng Hua, Deputy Head of the delegation, Xiao Ming, Cultural Counselor of the Chinese Embassy in Myanmar, and Senior Colonel Jiang Keding, Military Attaché, also attended the presentation ceremony.

### 我国防部向缅国防军赠礼

1960-12-30
第5版()
专栏：

    我国防部向缅国防军赠礼
    新华社仰光28日电　中国文化艺术代表团副团长、中国人民解放军总政治部宣传部副部长李伟大校，代表中华人民共和国国防部把一套舞台上使用的特技效果灯光设备赠送给缅甸国防军。
    缅甸国防军副总参谋长昂季准将代表缅甸国防军对中国国防部赠送礼物表示感谢。
    中国文化艺术代表团团长张致祥、副团长彭华，中国驻缅甸大使馆文化参赞萧明、武官蒋克定大校也出席了赠礼仪式。
