Title: Chinese Delegation of the China-Myanmar Boundary Joint Committee Arrives in Myoma

Author:

Date: 1960-12-22
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, Rangoon, December 20th: The Chinese delegation of the China-Myanmar Boundary Joint Committee, a party of twenty led by Chief Representative Yao Zhongming, arrived in Myoma, Burma on December 20th from Kunming via Mandalay by plane. The delegation will participate in the fifth meeting of the China-Myanmar Boundary Joint Committee to be held in Myoma.

At the Mandalay airport, the Chinese delegation received a grand and solemn welcome from over 3,000 people, including members of the Burmese delegation of the China-Myanmar Boundary Joint Committee headed by Brigadier General Aung Gyi, Mandalay Division Commissioner U Ba La, as well as local residents and overseas Chinese leaders. Colonel Jiang Keding, Military Attaché of the Chinese Embassy in Burma, also went to the airport to welcome the delegation.

Upon arrival in Myoma, the Chinese delegation was also greeted by Colonel Khun Nong, Commandant of the Burmese Defence Services Academy, and over 2,000 local residents lining the streets.

That noon, Brigadier General San Yu, Commander of the Northern Burmese Military Region, hosted a banquet to entertain the Chinese delegation.

### 中缅边界联合委员会我方代表团抵眉苗

1960-12-22
第5版()
专栏：

    中缅边界联合委员会我方代表团抵眉苗
    新华社仰光20日电　中缅边界联合委员会中方代表团一行二十人在首席代表姚仲明率领下，于20日从昆明乘飞机经曼德勒到达缅甸的眉苗。代表团将参加即将在眉苗举行的中缅边界联合委员会第五次会议。
    中方代表团在曼德勒机场上受到以昂季准将为首的中缅边界联合委员会缅方代表团人员、曼德勒专区专员吴巴拉以及当地居民和华侨领袖等三千多人的盛大而隆重的欢迎。中国驻缅甸大使馆武官蒋克定大校也到机场上欢迎代表团。
    中方代表团到达眉苗时，还受到缅甸国防军事学院院长孔农上校和当地居民二千多人的夹道欢迎。
    当天中午，缅北军区司令山友准将设宴招待中方代表团。
