Title: New Construction
Author: Baoyin Chaoketu
Date: 1960-12-26
Page: 3

    New Construction
    The Chasqi Nitrogen Fertilizer Plant, a modern nitrogen fertilizer plant in Tumote Banner, Ulanqab League, Inner Mongolia Autonomous Region, is about to be completed and put into production. Construction workers are braving the severe cold to rush to build the final project, striving to put it into production as soon as possible to support increased agricultural production next year.
          Xinhua News Agency reporter Baoyin Chaoketu photographed

    新建设
    内蒙古自治区乌兰察布盟土默特旗的一座现代化的氮肥厂——察素齐氮肥厂即将建成投入生产。建设职工正在冒着严寒赶建结尾工程，争取早日投入生产，支援明年农业增产。
          新华社记者　宝音朝克图摄
