Title: US Military Aircraft Intrudes into China's Airspace, I Issue the 127th Serious Warning
Author:
Date: 1960-12-01
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, 30th — At 11:30 to 11:45 on November 30th, a U.S. Navy patrol plane intruded into the airspace over Qilian Island, Shidao Island, and Yongxing Island in the Xisha Islands of Guangdong Province. Regarding this military provocation by the U.S. military aircraft, the spokesperson of our Ministry of Foreign Affairs was instructed to issue the 127th serious warning.

### 美国军用飞机侵入我国领空  我提出第127次严重警告

1960-12-01
第1版()
专栏：

    美国军用飞机侵入我国领空
    我提出第127次严重警告
    新华社30日讯　11月30日十一时三十分至十一时四十五分，美国海军巡逻机一架，侵入我广东省西沙群岛的七连岛、石岛、永兴岛上空。对于美国军用飞机的这种军事挑衅，我外交部发言人奉命提出第127次严重警告。
