Title: Cadres Settle Down in the Countryside
Author: Yu Bo
Date: 1960-12-28
Page: 4

Cadres settling down in the countryside, everyone praises it: cadres say the team has a good advisor; commune members say farming has gained a good worker; the canteen has a good helper, who can also help commune members learn culture. The countryside is vast and wide, and the commune will forever bloom with happiness.

### 干部农村来安家
豫波
1960-12-28
第4版()
专栏：

    干部农村来安家
    豫波干部农村来安家，人人说来个个夸：干部说队里来了好参谋；社员说种地又添好干家；食堂里有了好帮手，还能帮社员学文化。农村天地宽又广，公社永开幸福花。
