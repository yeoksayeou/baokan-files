Title: After Attending the October Revolution Celebration, Comrades Deng Xiaoping and Others Return to Beijing; CCP Central Committee Vice Chairman Zhou Enlai and Others Welcome Them at the Airport
Author:
Date: 1960-12-04
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, December 3rd. Deng Xiaoping, Deputy Head of the Chinese Party and Government Delegation and General Secretary of the CPC Central Committee, and some members of the delegation, who were invited to Moscow to participate in the 43rd anniversary celebration of the October Socialist Revolution, returned to Beijing by plane this afternoon. They were warmly welcomed at the airport.

Zhou Enlai, Vice Chairman of the CPC Central Committee, was at the airport to welcome them.

The members of the Chinese Party and Government Delegation who returned on the same plane were: Peng Zhen, Kang Sheng, Hu Qiaomu, and Liao Chengzhi.

Also at the airport to welcome them today were: members of the Political Bureau of the CPC Central Committee Luo Ronghuan, Chen Yi, Li Fuchun, He Long, Li Xiannian, alternate members of the Political Bureau Chen Boda, Bo Yibo, secretaries of the Secretariat of the CPC Central Committee Tan Zheng, Li Xuefeng, members of the CPC Central Committee Luo Ruiqing, Deng Yingchao, Nie Rongzhen, Wu Xiuquan, Xi Zhongxun. S.V. Sudarikov, Charge d'Affaires ad interim of the Soviet Embassy in China, also greeted them at the airport.

Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, December 2nd. Deng Xiaoping, General Secretary of the CPC Central Committee and Deputy Head of the Chinese Party and Government Delegation, and delegation members Peng Zhen, Kang Sheng, Hu Qiaomu, and Liao Chengzhi, left Moscow by plane on the evening of the 2nd to return to their country. Among those who went to the airport to see them off were M.A. Suslov, member of the Presidium of the CPSU Central Committee, and P.Y. Paletskis, Deputy Chairman of the Presidium of the USSR Supreme Soviet, and others. Zhang Dequn, Counselor of our Embassy in the Soviet Union, and staff members of the embassy also saw them off at the airport.

### 应邀参加十月革命节庆典后  邓小平等同志回到北京  中共中央副主席周恩来等到机场欢迎

1960-12-04
第1版()
专栏：

    应邀参加十月革命节庆典后
    邓小平等同志回到北京
    中共中央副主席周恩来等到机场欢迎
    新华社3日讯　应邀去莫斯科参加十月社会主义革命四十三周年庆祝典礼的中国党政代表团副团长、中共中央总书记邓小平及部分团员，今天下午乘飞机回到北京。他们在飞机场上受到热烈欢迎。
    中共中央副主席周恩来到机场欢迎。
    同机回国的中国党政代表团团员有：彭真、康生、胡乔木、廖承志。
    今天到机场欢迎的还有：中共中央政治局委员罗荣桓、陈毅、李富春、贺龙、李先念，政治局候补委员陈伯达、薄一波，中共中央书记处书记谭政、李雪峰，中共中央委员罗瑞卿、邓颖超、聂荣臻、伍修权、习仲勋。苏联驻华大使馆临时代办苏达利柯夫也到机场迎接。
    新华社莫斯科2日电　中共中央总书记、中国党政代表团副团长邓小平，团员彭真、康生、胡乔木、廖承志，在2日晚乘飞机离开莫斯科返国。前往机场送行的有苏共中央主席团委员穆希金诺夫、苏斯洛夫和苏联最高苏维埃主席团副主席帕列茨基斯等人。我国驻苏联大使馆参赞张德群和使馆工作人员也到机场送行。
