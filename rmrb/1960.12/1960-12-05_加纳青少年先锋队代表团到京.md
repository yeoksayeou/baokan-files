Title: Ghana Young Pioneers Delegation Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1960-12-05
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, 4th — At the invitation of the All-China Youth Federation, the Ghana Young Pioneers delegation, headed by Nii Ashitey Akonfra, arrived in Beijing by plane this afternoon.

Those who welcomed them at the airport included Wang Chuanbin, Deputy Secretary-General of the All-China Youth Federation, and Kong Candong, Deputy Director of the Children's Work Department of the Central Committee of the Chinese Communist Youth League.

### 加纳青少年先锋队代表团到京

1960-12-05
第4版()
专栏：

    加纳青少年先锋队代表团到京
    新华社4日讯　应中华全国青年联合会邀请来我国访问的加纳青少年先锋队代表团一行九人，在团长尼·阿希提·阿科姆弗拉率领下，今天下午乘飞机到达北京。
    到机场欢迎的，有中华全国青年联合会副秘书长王传斌，中国共产主义青年团中央委员会少年儿童工作部副部长孔灿东等。
