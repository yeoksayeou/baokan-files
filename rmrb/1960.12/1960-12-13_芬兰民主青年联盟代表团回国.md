Title: Delegation of the Democratic Youth League of Finland Returns Home
Author:
Date: 1960-12-13
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, 12th — The delegation of the Democratic Youth League of Finland, headed by Olavi Hänninen, General Secretary of the Democratic Youth League of Finland, left Beijing by plane today to return home after completing its visit to our country.

On the evening of the 11th, the Central Committee of the Communist Youth League held a banquet to see off the guests. Hu Yaobang, First Secretary of the Central Committee of the Communist Youth League, attended the banquet.

### 芬兰民主青年联盟代表团回国

1960-12-13
第4版()
专栏：

    芬兰民主青年联盟代表团回国
    新华社12日讯　以芬兰民主青年联盟总书记奥拉维·汉尼宁为首的芬兰民主青年联盟代表团一行四人，结束了在我国的访问，今天乘飞机离开北京回国。
    11日晚，共青团中央曾设宴欢送客人。共青团中央第一书记胡耀邦出席了宴会。
