Title: Souphanouvong Visits Various Places in Sam Neua Province, Points Out Current Task is National Unity Against US Interference
Author:
Date: 1960-12-10
Page: 7
Xinhua News Agency, Hanoi, December 9th: Radio of the Laotian Fighting Forces broadcast tonight that Prince Souphanouvong, Chairman of the Central Committee of the Lao Patriotic Front, and Vice Chairman Sithon Kommadam visited So Lun, Chuang Ku, Meng Ai, and other places in Sam Neua Province from November 29th to December 4th. They were warmly welcomed by more than 8,000 people in these areas. The masses also held traditional ethnic blessing ceremonies for their leaders, tying a red thread on their wrists.

Prince Souphanouvong thanked the masses for their warm welcome in his speech.

At the same time, he talked about the current situation in Laos. He said that the common task of the whole nation now is to unite more broadly, oppose the interference of US imperialism, support the Phouma government in thoroughly implementing the policies of peaceful neutrality, national harmony, and a unified country, and struggle to eliminate the Phoumi Nosavan-Boun Oum-Naciamphak Bassac traitorous clique, so that Laos can embark on the path of peaceful neutrality and achieve independence, democracy, unity, and prosperity.

Prince Souphanouvong also encouraged the people to help and cooperate with each other and increase production to improve their lives.

Representatives of the local people also spoke. They expressed their confidence in the leadership of Prince Souphanouvong and the Central Committee of the Lao Patriotic Front, and pledged to follow the teachings of Prince Souphanouvong.

### 苏发努冯到桑怒省各地访问  指出当前任务是全民团结反对美国干涉

1960-12-10
第7版()
专栏：

    苏发努冯到桑怒省各地访问
    指出当前任务是全民团结反对美国干涉
    新华社河内9日电　据寮国战斗部队电台今晚广播，老挝爱国战线党中央委员会主席苏发努冯亲王和副主席西吞·库马丹在11月29日到12月4日访问了桑怒省索伦、川库、芒艾等地。他们受到上述地方的八千多群众的热烈欢迎。群众还为自己的领袖举行了民族传统的祝福仪式，在领袖们的手腕上系上一根红线。
    苏发努冯亲王在向群众讲话时，对大家的热烈欢迎表示感谢。
    同时，他谈到老挝当前的局势。他说，现在全民的共同任务是更加广泛地团结，反对美帝国主义的干涉，支持富马政府彻底实行和平中立、民族和睦和统一国家的政策，为消灭富米·诺萨万—文翁·纳占巴塞叛国集团、使老挝走上和平中立道路、获得独立、民主、统一和兴盛而斗争。
    苏发努冯亲王还勉励人民要互助合作，增加生产，以改善生活。
    当地人民的代表也讲了话。他表示信赖苏发努冯亲王和老挝爱国战线党中央委员会的领导，并保证遵循苏发努冯亲王的教导。
