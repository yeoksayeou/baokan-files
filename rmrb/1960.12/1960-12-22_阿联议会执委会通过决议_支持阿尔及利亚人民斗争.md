Title: UAE Parliamentary Executive Committee Passes Resolution Supporting Algerian People's Struggle
Author:
Date: 1960-12-22
Page: 6
Xinhua News Agency, Cairo, December 20th: The Executive Committee of the National Assembly of the United Arab Republic passed several resolutions yesterday calling on the parliaments and governments of all Arab countries to sever relations with France; to nationalize French property in the Arab region; to provide military and economic assistance to the Algerian National Liberation Army; and to abolish NATO military bases on Arab territory. These resolutions will be submitted to the upcoming plenary session of the UAE National Assembly.

The resolutions also called on those free countries that have not yet recognized the Provisional Government of the Algerian Republic to do so immediately. The Executive Committee of the National Assembly also decided to send a parliamentary delegation to various Arab countries to make contact in order to implement these resolutions.

### 阿联议会执委会通过决议  支持阿尔及利亚人民斗争

1960-12-22
第6版()
专栏：

    阿联议会执委会通过决议
    支持阿尔及利亚人民斗争
    新华社开罗20日电　阿拉伯联合共和国国民议会执行委员会昨天通过了几项决议，呼吁所有阿拉伯国家的议会和政府同法国断绝关系；把阿拉伯地区的法国财产收归国有；给阿尔及利亚民族解放军以军事和经济援助；取消北大西洋公约组织在阿拉伯国家领土上的军事基地。这些决议将提交即将召开的阿联国民议会全体会议。
    决议还呼吁那些到现在还没有承认阿尔及利亚共和国临时政府的自由国家立即承认它。国民议会执行委员会还决定派遣一个议会代表团到各阿拉伯国家去为实现这些决议进行接触。
