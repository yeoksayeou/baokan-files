Title: Our Sports Delegation and Part of the Art Troupe Arrive in Yangon to Participate in the Burmese Independence Day Celebration and the Exchange of Instruments of Ratification of the Sino-Burmese Boundary Treaty; Burmese Minister of Health U Ba Swe Hosts Banquet for Our Sports Delegation
Author:
Date: 1960-12-24
Page: 5

　　Xinhua News Agency, Yangon, December 22nd. Burmese Minister of Health U Ba Swe hosted a banquet tonight for all members of the Chinese Sports Delegation led by Colonel Lu Ting.
　　Among those attending the banquet were U Tun Tin, Secretary of the Burmese Ministry of Health; Lieutenant Colonel Maung Wun, head of the Burmese Sports Delegation to China; U Gaw Diang, Deputy Secretary of the Burmese Ministry of Health; U Suwe, President of the National Fitness Council; U Tu Kin Shwe, Chief Organizer of the National Fitness Council; and prominent figures from the Burmese sports community.
　　Li Yimang, Chinese Ambassador to Burma; Xiao Ming, Cultural Counselor of the Embassy; Colonel Jiang Keding, Military Attaché; and Lieutenant Colonel Lu Zhan, Deputy Military Attaché, also attended the banquet.
　　In his speech at the banquet, U Ba Swe warmly welcomed all members of the Chinese Sports Delegation. U Ba Swe said, "China and Burma are not only friendly neighbors, but also countries with kinship."
　　U Ba Swe said, "In recent years, the friendly relations between China and Burma have seen satisfactory development. Last time, Burmese Prime Minister U Nu led a delegation of more than 300 people to visit China, where they received a warm welcome and grand reception from the Chinese people. Now, Premier Zhou Enlai will lead a delegation of more than 400 people to our country.This represents the great friendship of 650 million Chinese people towards the Burmese people. We extend a warm welcome and gratitude for the sincere friendship of the Chinese people."
　　U Ba Swe emphasized, "Mutual visits between our two countries' leaders and various delegations will not only help mutual understanding, but also further deepen the friendship between our two peoples."
　　The Chinese guests welcomed U Ba Swe's speech with warm applause.
　　The banquet took place in a friendly and cordial atmosphere. Hosts and guests frequently toasted to the further consolidation of the Sino-Burmese friendship edifice.
　　Xinhua News Agency, Yangon, December 22nd. The Chinese Sports Delegation, consisting of forty-eight people and led by Colonel Lu Ting, arrived in Yangon by plane on the morning of the 22nd. The Chinese guests were warmly welcomed by high-ranking Burmese military and government officials and figures from the Yangon sports community. The Chinese Sports Delegation is part of the "People's Republic of China Friendship Delegation to the Union of Burma" led by Premier Zhou Enlai. During its visit to Burma, the Chinese Sports Delegation will participate in the celebrations for the 13th anniversary of the independence of the Union of Burma and will also participate in the ceremony for the exchange of instruments of ratification of the Sino-Burmese Boundary Treaty.
　　The Chinese Sports Delegation is composed of a renowned football team and a Chinese martial arts team of national style. The Chinese martial arts team is led by Wang Ziping, a renowned martial artist, Vice Chairman of the Chinese Wushu Association, and octogenarian.
　　Among those who welcomed the delegation at the airport were Colonel Su Min, head of the Burmese Cultural Delegation to China; Colonel Deng Mao; Lieutenant Colonel Maung Wun, head of the Burmese Sports Delegation to China; Lieutenant Colonel Lun; U Suwe, President of the Burmese National Fitness Council; and approximately 300 prominent figures from the Burmese sports community.
　　Xinhua News Agency, Yangon, December 22nd. At the invitation of the Burmese government, the first group of 50 members of the Chinese Cultural and Art Delegation, which will participate in the celebrations for the 13th anniversary of the independence of the Union of Burma and conduct a friendly visit, arrived here by plane this morning. They were warmly welcomed at the airport by Burmese government officials and prominent figures from the cultural community.
　　The Chinese Cultural and Art Delegation is part of the "People's Republic of China Friendship Delegation to the Union of Burma" led by Premier Zhou Enlai. During its visit to Burma, the delegation will also participate in the ceremony for the exchange of instruments of ratification of the Sino-Burmese Boundary Treaty in Yangon, and will conduct friendly visits to other parts of Burma. The first group of 50 members of the Chinese Cultural and Art Delegation arrived in Yangon under the leadership of Peng Hua, Deputy Head of the Delegation, and Yu Guang, Deputy Head of the Second Sub-delegation of the Chinese Cultural and Art Delegation. The first group of members includes members of the Peking Opera troupe and some members of the Yunnan Song and Dance Troupe.
　　Among those who went to the airport to welcome the Chinese Cultural and Art Delegation were Colonel Su Min, head of the Burmese Cultural Delegation to China; Colonel Deng Mao; Lieutenant Colonel Lun; U Su Wu, Deputy Head of the Burmese Cultural Delegation to China; U Din Oh, Permanent Deputy Secretary of the Burmese Ministry of Culture; and approximately 300 prominent figures from the Yangon cultural community.
　　Xiao Ming, Cultural Counselor of the Chinese Embassy in Burma, and his wife, as well as representatives of the Burmese Chinese community, also went to the airport to welcome the delegation.
　　The Chinese Cultural and Art Delegation consists of 246 people, including personnel in Peking Opera, music, dance, and acrobatics. The remaining members of the Cultural and Art Delegation will arrive here by plane in batches in the next few days.

### 参加缅甸庆祝独立大典和互换中缅边界条约批准书仪式  我体育代表团和艺术团部分团员到仰光  缅甸卫生部长吴巴素欢宴我体育代表团

1960-12-24
第5版()
专栏：

    参加缅甸庆祝独立大典和互换中缅边界条约批准书仪式
    我体育代表团和艺术团部分团员到仰光
    缅甸卫生部长吴巴素欢宴我体育代表团
    新华社仰光22日电　缅甸卫生部长吴巴素今天晚上设宴招待鲁挺上校率领的中国体育代表团全体团员。
    出席宴会的有缅甸卫生部秘书吴吞东，缅甸访华体育代表团团长貌温中校，缅甸卫生部副秘书高迪昂，国民健身委员会会长吴素韦，国民健身委员会首席组织者都庆瑞，以及缅甸体育界著名人士。
    中国驻缅甸大使李一氓、大使馆文化参赞萧明、武官蒋克定大校和副武官路展上校也出席了宴会。
    吴巴素在宴会上讲话时表示热烈欢迎中国体育代表团全体团员。吴巴素说，“中国和缅甸不仅是友好的邻邦，而且也是有亲戚关系的国家。”
    吴巴素说，“最近几年来，中国和缅甸之间的友好关系有了令人满意的发展。上次缅甸总理吴努率领三百多人组成的代表团访问中国的时候，他们受到中国人民热烈欢迎和盛大接待。现在，周恩来总理将率领四百多人组成的代表团前来我国。这是六亿五千万中国人民对缅甸人民的伟大友谊。我们热烈欢迎并感谢中国人民的真诚的友谊。”
    吴巴素着重指出，“我们两国领导人和各种代表团之间的相互访问，不仅将有助于相互了解，而且将进一步加深我们两国人民之间的友谊。”
    中国客人以热烈的掌声欢迎吴巴素的讲话。
    宴会是在友好和亲切的气氛中进行的。宾主双方频频为中缅友好大厦进一步的巩固而干杯。
    新华社仰光22日电　由四十八人组成的中国体育代表团在鲁挺上校的率领下于22日上午乘飞机到达仰光。中国客人受到缅甸高级军政官员和仰光体育界人士的热烈欢迎。中国体育代表团是由周恩来总理率领的“中华人民共和国访问缅甸联邦友好代表团”的一部分。中国体育代表团在缅甸访问期间，将参加缅甸联邦独立十三周年的庆典，并将参加互换中缅边界条约批准书的仪式。
    中国体育代表团是由著名的足球队和民族形式的中国武术队组成的。中国武术队是由著名的武术家、中国武术协会副主席、八十高龄的王子平率领的。
    到机场欢迎代表团的有缅甸访华文化代表团团长苏敏上校，登貌上校，缅甸访华体育代表团团长貌温中校，伦中校，缅甸国民健身委员会会长吴素韦以及缅甸体育界著名人士共三百人。
    新华社仰光22日电　应缅甸政府的邀请，参加缅甸联邦独立十三周年庆祝典礼和进行友好访问的中国文化艺术代表团第一批团员五十人今天上午乘飞机到达这里。他们在机场受到缅甸政府官员和文化界著名人士的热烈欢迎。
    中国文化艺术代表团是由周恩来总理率领的“中华人民共和国访问缅甸联邦友好代表团”的一部分。代表团在缅甸访问期间，还将参加在仰光举行的互换中缅边界条约批准书的仪式，并将到缅甸其他地方进行友好访问。中国文化艺术代表团第一批五十名团员是在副团长彭华和中国文化艺术代表团第二分团副团长于光率领下到达仰光的。第一批团员包括京剧队队员和云南歌舞团部分团员。
    前往机场欢迎中国文化艺术代表团的有缅甸访华文化代表团团长苏敏上校、登貌上校、伦中校、缅甸访华文化代表团副团长吴苏吴、缅甸文化部常务副秘书吴丁奥和仰光文化界著名人士约三百人。
    中国驻缅甸大使馆文化参赞萧明及夫人以及缅甸华侨代表也前往机场欢迎。
    中国文化艺术代表团共有二百四十六人，其中包括京剧、音乐、舞蹈和杂技等方面的人员。文化艺术代表团的其余团员将在这几天内分批乘飞机来到这里。
