Title: Burmese National Assembly Approves Sino-Burmese Boundary Treaty
Author:
Date: 1960-12-09
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, Rangoon, December 7th: The Burmese National Assembly, in its meeting today, discussed and approved the Sino-Burmese Boundary Treaty. The Sino-Burmese Boundary Treaty was submitted to the National Assembly by Burmese Foreign Minister Sao Khun Hkio on behalf of the Union of Burma government on the 5th.

### 缅甸民族院批准中缅边界条约

1960-12-09
第1版()
专栏：

    缅甸民族院批准中缅边界条约
    新华社仰光7日电　缅甸国会民族院在今天举行的会议上讨论并批准了中缅边界条约。中缅边界条约是由缅甸外交部长藻昆卓代表缅甸联邦政府在5日向民族院提出的。
