Title: Hungarian Men's and Women's Table Tennis Teams Compete in Shanghai, Women Win, Men Lose
Author: 
Date: 1960-12-10
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Shanghai, December 9th. The Hungarian national men's and women's table tennis team players, under the name of the Budapest men's and women's table tennis team, held a friendly match with the Shanghai men's and women's table tennis team one in Shanghai on the evening of the 8th. As a result, the visiting men's team lost three to five, and the women's team won three to zero.
    More than 3,000 spectators in the Shanghai Gymnasium applauded the wonderful performances of both sides from time to time.
    Before the match, Jin Zhonghua, Deputy Mayor of Shanghai, met with the leader of the visiting team, Shaigar Gyula.
    The visiting team arrived in Shanghai on the 5th, and they will also have a friendly match with the Shanghai men's and women's table tennis team two on the evening of the 9th.

### 匈男女乒乓球队在上海比赛女胜男败

1960-12-10
第4版()
专栏：

    匈男女乒乓球队在上海比赛女胜男败
    据新华社上海9日电　匈牙利国家男女乒乓球队选手8日晚在上海以布达佩斯男女乒乓球队的名义与上海市男女乒乓球一队举行友谊比赛，结果客队男队以三比五失败，女队以三比零获胜。
    上海市体育馆里的三千多观众不时对双方运动员的精彩表演热烈鼓掌。
    比赛前，上海市副市长金仲华接见了客队领队沙伊加尔·久拉。
    客队是5日到上海的，他们在9日晚还将与上海市男女乒乓球二队举行友谊比赛。
