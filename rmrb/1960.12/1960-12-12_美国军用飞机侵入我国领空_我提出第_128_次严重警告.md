Title: U.S. Military Aircraft Intrudes into China's Airspace; 128th Serious Warning Issued

Author:
Date: 1960-12-12
Page: 4

    Xinhua News Agency, 11th. On December 11th, from 11:17 to 11:30, a U.S. Navy patrol plane intruded into the airspace over Yongxing Island and Shidao Island in the Xisha Islands of Guangdong Province. Regarding this military provocation by U.S. military aircraft, a spokesman for the Chinese Ministry of Foreign Affairs was instructed to issue the 128th serious warning.

### 美国军用飞机侵入我国领空  我提出第128次严重警告

1960-12-12
第4版()
专栏：

    美国军用飞机侵入我国领空
    我提出第128次严重警告
    新华社11日讯　12月11日十一时十七分至十一时三十分，美国海军巡逻机一架，侵入我广东省西沙群岛的永兴岛、石岛上空。对于美国军用飞机的这种军事挑衅，我外交部发言人奉命提出第128次严重警告。
