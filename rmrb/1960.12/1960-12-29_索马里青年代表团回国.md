Title: Somali Youth Delegation Returns Home
Author:
Date: 1960-12-29
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency, December 28th. The Somali youth delegation headed by Abukar Haji Mou, Chairman of the Somali Progressive Youth League, left Beijing by train on the evening of the 27th via Guangzhou to return home after completing their visit to China.

### 索马里青年代表团回国

1960-12-29
第4版()
专栏：

    索马里青年代表团回国
    新华社28日讯　以索马里进步青年联盟主席阿布卡尔·哈吉·茅为首的索马里青年代表团一行三人，结束了在我国的参观访问，27日晚乘火车离开北京经广州回国。
