Title: Leaders of the Polish-Chinese Friendship Association Meet with Chinese Women's Delegation
Author:
Date: 1960-12-10
Page: 6
Xinhua News Agency, Warsaw, December 8th: Leading members of the Polish-Chinese Friendship Association (PCFA) held a meeting on the evening of the 7th with the Chinese women's delegation attending the International Democratic Women's Federation Council meeting. Those participating in the meeting included PCFA Secretary-General Bulkin, the wife of Mr. Wender, Vice Chairman of the Polish Democratic Party and member of the PCFA Presidium, the wife of the Minister of Higher Education Goranski, and the wife of former Ambassador to China Kirilok, among others. The wife of Ambassador Wang Bingnan also participated in the meeting.

### 波中友协领导人会见我妇女代表团

1960-12-10
第6版()
专栏：

    波中友协领导人会见我妇女代表团
    新华社华沙8日电　波中友好协会总会负责人7日晚和出席国际民主妇女联合会理事会会议的中国妇女代表团举行了会见。参加会见的有波中友协秘书长布尔金，波兰民主党副主席、波中友协主席团委员温德的夫人，高等教育部长戈兰斯基的夫人和前任驻中国大使基里洛克的夫人等。王炳南大使夫人也参加了会见。
