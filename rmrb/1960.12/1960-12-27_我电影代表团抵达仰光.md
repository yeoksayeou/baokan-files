Title: Our Film Delegation Arrives in Rangoon
Author:
Date: 1960-12-27
Page: 5
Xinhua News Agency, Rangoon, December 26th. The Chinese Film Delegation of the Chinese Goodwill Delegation to Burma, led by Chen Bo, director of the August First Film Studio, arrived here by plane this morning. The Chinese guests were warmly welcomed by senior Burmese government officials and people from cultural circles in Rangoon.

    The Chinese Film Delegation is part of the Goodwill Delegation of the People's Republic of China to the Union of Burma, led by Premier Zhou Enlai. During its visit, the Chinese Film Delegation will participate in the celebrations for the 13th anniversary of the independence of the Union of Burma and will also participate in the ceremony for the exchange of instruments of ratification of the Sino-Burmese Boundary Treaty.

    When the delegation stepped out of the cabin, the crowd gathered at the airport to welcome the Chinese guests erupted in thunderous applause and cheers. Burmese artists presented flowers to the Chinese guests. The entire airport was filled with an atmosphere of friendship and cordiality. Those who came to the airport to welcome the delegation included Burmese Minister of Culture U Ba So, Colonel So Min, head of the Burmese cultural delegation to China, famous film actors, and 400 prominent figures from Burmese cultural circles.

### 我电影代表团抵达仰光

1960-12-27
第5版()
专栏：

    我电影代表团抵达仰光
    新华社仰光26日电　中国访缅友好代表团中国电影代表团今天早晨在八一电影制片厂厂长陈播率领下乘飞机到达这里。中国客人们受到缅甸政府高级官员和仰光文化界人士的热烈欢迎。
    中国电影代表团是由周恩来总理率领的中华人民共和国访问缅甸联邦友好代表团的一部分。在访问期间，中国电影代表团将参加缅甸联邦独立十三周年的庆祝典礼，并将参加互换中缅边界条约批准书的仪式。
    当代表团走出机舱的时候，聚集在机场上欢迎中国客人的群众发出雷动的掌声和欢呼。缅甸艺术家们向中国客人们献了花。整个机场充满了友好和亲切的气氛。到机场欢迎的有缅甸文化部长吴巴素、缅甸访华文化代表团团长苏敏上校、著名电影演员以及缅甸文化界著名人士等四百人。
