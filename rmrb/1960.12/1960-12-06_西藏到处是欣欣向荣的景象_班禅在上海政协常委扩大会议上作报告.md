Title: Thriving Scene Everywhere in Tibet: Panchen Lama Delivers Report at Enlarged Meeting of Shanghai CPPCC Standing Committee
Author:
Date: 1960-12-06
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency, Shanghai, December 5th. Panchen Erdeni Qoigyijabu, Vice Chairman of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress and Acting Director of the Preparatory Committee for the Tibet Autonomous Region, and Lhamin Yishey Tsultrim, Standing Committee Member of the National Committee of the CPPCC, Standing Committee Member and Deputy Secretary-General of the Preparatory Committee for the Tibet Autonomous Region, who are currently visiting Shanghai, were invited this afternoon to report on the democratic reforms and production construction in Tibet at an enlarged meeting of the Standing Committee of the Shanghai Municipal Committee of the CPPCC.

In his report, Panchen Erdeni focused on the situation in Tibet since the beginning of this year. He said: Under the wise and correct leadership of the Party Central Committee and Chairman Mao, on the basis of the great victory achieved in quelling the rebellion and carrying out democratic reforms in Tibet last year, all work in Tibet has been further developed and great achievements have been made since the beginning of this year. After introducing the achievements made in industry, agriculture, and animal husbandry in Tibet this year, he said: At present, Tibet is in an excellent situation, with a scene of active production and prosperity everywhere, both in rural areas and cities. He emphasized: Through the practical work of the past year, we have deeply realized that the leadership of the Party is the most important thing. As long as we closely rely on the leadership of the Party, conscientiously implement the Party's policies and guidelines and various instructions, we will be able to overcome all difficulties and be invincible. Mao Zedong Thought is the red flag guiding us from victory to victory. Further study of Chairman Mao's works, using Mao Zedong Thought to arm our minds, and earnestly implementing Chairman Mao's instructions in practical work, will enable us to victoriously push our work forward continuously.

In his report, Panchen Erdeni severely condemned and protested against the illegal acts of U.S. imperialism in shamelessly interfering in China's internal affairs. He said: The people of Tibet are extremely angry at the shameful acts of U.S. imperialism in plotting to use the United Nations to interfere in our country's internal affairs. We will use practical actions and new victories in building a new democratic and socialist Tibet to fight back against imperialism and foreign reactionaries.

In his speech, Lhamin Yishey Tsultrim exposed the crimes of the Tibetan feudal ruling class and introduced the Party and government's work in uniting, educating, and reforming upper-class figures in Tibet.

After Lhamin's speech, Vice Chairman Panchen Erdeni gave detailed answers to the questions raised at the meeting. Chen Pixian, Chairman of the Shanghai Municipal Committee of the CPPCC, expressed his gratitude on behalf of the attendees to Panchen Erdeni and Lhamin for their reports.

Panchen Erdeni and others arrived in Shanghai from Hangzhou on November 22nd, accompanied by Li Weihan, Minister of the United Front Work Department of the CPC Central Committee and Vice Chairman of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress; Chen Shutong, Vice Chairman of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress; Liu Chun, Deputy Director of the Nationalities Affairs Commission; Guo Xilan, Deputy Secretary of the CPC Tibet Working Committee; and Liang Hong, Deputy Minister of the United Front Work Department of the CPC Tibet Working Committee. During their stay in Shanghai, Panchen Erdeni and others visited the Shanghai State-owned No. 2 Woolen Mill, Pengpu Machine Factory, Shanghai Industrial Exhibition, Liyuan Neighborhood Committee, the newly built workers' residential area Zhangmiao Yitiaojie, and the Conservatory of Music.

On November 27th, Panchen Erdeni and others also went to Suzhou and Wuxi for visits. In Suzhou, they visited the Suzhou Arts and Crafts Research Institute and Suzhou Electric Porcelain Factory, and toured scenic spots and historical sites such as Lingyan Mountain, Xiyuan, Tiger Hill, Humble Administrator's Garden, and Lingering Garden. In Wuxi, they visited the local state-owned Wuxi No. 1 Silk Reeling Factory, Huishan Clay Figurine Factory, Jiangsu Provincial Workers' Taihu Sanatorium, and toured the Taihu Lake scenic area. They returned to Shanghai from Wuxi on the 2nd.

### 西藏到处是欣欣向荣的景象  班禅在上海政协常委扩大会议上作报告

1960-12-06
第4版()
专栏：

    西藏到处是欣欣向荣的景象
    班禅在上海政协常委扩大会议上作报告
    新华社上海5日电　正在上海参观访问的全国人民代表大会常务委员会副委员长、西藏自治区筹备委员会代理主任委员班禅额尔德尼
  ·却吉坚赞和政协全国委员会常务委员、西藏自治区筹备委员会常务委员、副秘书长拉敏·益西楚臣，今天下午应邀在政协上海市委员会常务委员扩大会议上就西藏民主改革和生产建设等问题作了报告。
    班禅额尔德尼在报告中着重介绍了今年以来西藏的情况。他说：在党中央和毛主席的英明正确领导下，西藏在去年平息叛乱和民主改革取得伟大胜利的基础上，今年以来的各项工作都有了进一步的发展，取得了很大的成绩。他在介绍了今年西藏工、农业和畜牧业所取得的成就以后说：目前西藏是一片大好形势，不论在农村和城市，到处都是积极生产、欣欣向荣的景象。他强调指出：通过一年来的工作实践，我们深深体会到千条万条，党的领导第一条。只要紧紧依靠党的领导，认真贯彻党的方针政策和各项指示，就能够克服一切困难，无往不胜。毛泽东思想是引导我们从胜利走向胜利的红旗。进一步学习毛主席著作，用毛泽东思想武装我们的头脑，并在实际工作中切切实实贯彻毛主席的指示，我们就能够胜利地不断把工作推向前进。
    班禅额尔德尼在报告中严厉谴责和抗议美帝国主义无耻干涉我国内政的非法行为。他说：西藏人民对于美帝国主义阴谋利用联合国干涉我国内政的可耻勾当感到无比愤怒。我们将用建设民主和社会主义新西藏的实际行动和新的胜利来回击帝国主义和外国反动派。
    拉敏·益西楚臣在讲话中揭露了西藏封建统治阶级的罪恶，并且介绍了党和政府对西藏上层人士进行团结教育和改造的情况。
  在拉敏讲话结束后，班禅额尔德尼副委员长就大家在会上提出的问题，一一作了详细的解答。政协上海市委员会主席陈丕显代表出席会议的人对班禅额尔德尼和拉敏的报告，表示感谢。
    班禅额尔德尼等是在11月22日由中共中央统一战线工作部部长、全国人民代表大会常务委员会副委员长李维汉，全国人民代表大会常务委员会副委员长陈叔通，民族事务委员会副主任刘春，中共西藏工委副书记郭锡兰，中共西藏工委统一战线工作部副部长梁洪等陪同，由杭州到达上海的。在上海期间，班禅额尔德尼等曾参观访问了上海国毛二厂、彭浦机器厂、上海市工业展览会、丽园街道委员会、新建的工人住宅区张庙一条街和音乐学院等。
    11月27日班禅额尔德尼等还去苏州、无锡等地参观访问。他们在苏州参观访问了苏州市工艺美术研究所、苏州电瓷厂，游览了灵岩山、西园、虎丘、拙政园、留园等名胜古迹。在无锡，他们参观访问了地方国营无锡市缫丝第一厂、惠山泥人厂、江苏省工人太湖疗养院，游览了太湖风景区。他们在2日由无锡回到上海。
