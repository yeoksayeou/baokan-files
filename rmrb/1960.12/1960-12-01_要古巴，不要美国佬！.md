Title: Cuba Yes, Yankees No!
Author: Zhang Zhixiang
Date: 1960-12-01
Page: 8

Cuba Yes, Yankees No!
Zhang Zhixiang

"Cuba yes, Yankees no!" This is a slogan shouted from the heart, this is the revolutionary storm of Latin America.

	You stole our resources,
	Sucked dry our blood and sweat,
	Filled your pockets,
		You Yankee!

	You export counter-revolution,
	Interfere in our internal affairs,
	Rampage on our land,
		You Yankee!

	You seize our land,
	Establish your military bases,
	Airplanes loaded with hydrogen bombs fly everywhere,
		You Yankee!

	Get out of Laos and South Vietnam, get out of Japan and South Korea, get out of the Taiwan Strait,
		You Yankee!

	Get out of Europe, get out of the Mediterranean, get out of everywhere,
		You Yankee!

	We want independence, no Yankees! We want democracy, no Yankees! We want prosperity, no Yankees! We want peace, no Yankees! Plague god, Yankees get out!

November 25, 1960
