Title: Premier Zhou Enlai's Reply Telegram to Deputy Prime Minister Gizenga: China Continues to Support the Legitimate Government of the Congo and Resolutely Opposes US Imperialist Aggression and Interference in the Congo
Author:
Date: 1960-12-18
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, December 17th: Premier Zhou Enlai of the State Council of the People's Republic of China, in a telegram sent today to Congolese Deputy Prime Minister Gizenga, expressed continued support for the legitimate government of the Congo headed by Prime Minister Lumumba.

Premier Zhou Enlai's telegram was in reply to Deputy Prime Minister Gizenga's telegram of the 13th from Stanleyville, in which Gizenga said that the central government of the Congo had moved to Stanleyville.

The full text of Premier Zhou Enlai's telegram is as follows: To His Excellency Deputy Prime Minister Gizenga, Stanleyville, Republic of the Congo:

Your Excellency's telegram of December 13th has been received. The Chinese government and people will adhere to their consistent position, continue to support the legitimate government of the Republic of the Congo with Lumumba as Prime Minister and Your Excellency as Deputy Prime Minister, continue to support the Congolese people who are fighting heroically against colonialism and safeguarding national independence, and resolutely oppose US imperialist aggression and interference in the Congo.

Please accept my highest respects.

Zhou Enlai
Premier of the State Council of the People's Republic of China
December 17, 1960, in Beijing

### 周恩来总理电复基赞加副总理  中国继续支持刚果合法政府  坚决反对美帝国主义侵略和干涉刚果

1960-12-18
第1版()
专栏：

    周恩来总理电复基赞加副总理
    中国继续支持刚果合法政府
    坚决反对美帝国主义侵略和干涉刚果
    新华社17日讯　中华人民共和国国务院总理周恩来在今天致刚果共和国副总理基赞加的电报中表示继续支持以卢蒙巴总理为首的刚果合法政府。
    周恩来总理的电报是为了答复基赞加副总理13日自斯坦利维尔发来的电报，基赞加在电报中说，刚果中央政府已经迁到斯坦利维尔。
    周恩来总理的电报全文如下：刚果共和国斯坦利维尔基赞加副总理阁下：
    阁下12月13日来电收到。中国政府和人民，将坚持一贯的立场，继续支持以卢蒙巴为总理和阁下为副总理的刚果共和国合法政府，继续支持为反对殖民主义、维护民族独立而英勇斗争的刚果人民，坚决反对美帝国主义对刚果的侵略和干涉。
    谨致最崇高的敬意。
    中华人民共和国
      国务院总理
              周恩来1960年12月17日于北京
