Evidence of the US Intensifying Preparations for Armed Aggression Against Cuba

Since the victory of the Cuban Revolution, the US authorities, in addition to carrying out provocations and economic "blockades" against Cuba in many ways, have also recruited and trained a large number of Cuban counter-revolutionaries and some mercenaries in preparation for launching an armed invasion of Cuba. The following photos, originally published in the American magazines "Life" and "U.S. News & World Report," fully expose the aggressive ambitions of the US authorities. (Photos attached)
