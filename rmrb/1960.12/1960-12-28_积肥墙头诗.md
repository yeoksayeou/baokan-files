Page: 8

Fertilize Accumulation Wall Poem
    Tang Qinglin, Wanbailin, Taiyuan, Shanxi: To catch fierce tigers up the mountain, to cast a good net to catch big fish, to have a bumper harvest of crops, the manure basket must not leave the shoulder. Bees love flowers, fish love water, crops love land, love fertilizer. If enough fertilizer is applied this winter, next year grain will pile up like a mountain!

### 积肥墙头诗
唐庆霖
1960-12-28
第8版()
专栏：

    积肥墙头诗
    山西太原万柏林　唐庆霖要捉猛虎上山岗，要打大鱼结好网，要想庄稼大丰收，粪筐不能离肩膀。蜂爱花，鱼爱水，庄稼土地，爱的肥。今冬肥料上得足，明年粮食堆成山！
