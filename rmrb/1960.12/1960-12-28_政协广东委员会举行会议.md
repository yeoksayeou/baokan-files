Title: Guangdong Provincial Committee of the CPPCC Holds Meeting
Author:
Date: 1960-12-28
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Guangzhou, December 26th. The Third Plenary Session of the Second Guangdong Provincial Committee of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference was held in Guangzhou from the 20th to the 24th.

The meeting passed a resolution, calling on people of all ethnic groups, local organizations of all democratic parties, people's organizations, and all patriotic people in the province to further strengthen unity, hold high the three red banners even higher under the leadership of the Guangdong Provincial Committee of the Communist Party of China, implement the policy of taking agriculture as the foundation of the national economy, do everything possible to support agriculture, and strive to win bumper harvests of various crops and the early rice crop next year.

The meeting elected Qu Mengjue as Chairman of the Provincial CPPCC, and Liang Guang and Huang Youmou as Vice Chairmen. Tao Zhu, the former Chairman of the Provincial CPPCC, and Wen Minsheng, the former Vice Chairman, could no longer serve concurrently due to work reasons. Vice Chairman Zhang Wen recently passed away due to illness.

The meeting also newly elected Qu Mengjue, Liang Guang, Tian Xin and fifty other people as members, among whom three are people from Hong Kong and Macau.

### 政协广东委员会举行会议

1960-12-28
第4版()
专栏：

    政协广东委员会举行会议
    新华社广州26日电　中国人民政治协商会议广东省第二届委员会第三次全体会议，20日到24日在广州市举行。
    会议通过决议，号召全省各族人民、各民主党派地方组织、各人民团体和一切爱国人士，进一步加强团结，在中共广东省委的领导下，更高地举起三面红旗，贯彻国民经济以农业为基础的方针，千方百计地支援农业，为夺取各种作物和明年早造的丰收而奋斗。
    会议选举区梦觉为省政协主席，梁广、黄友谋为副主席。原任省政协主席陶铸、副主席文敏生，因工作关系不能兼任。副主席张文最近病逝。
    会议还增选了区梦觉、梁广、田心等五十三人为委员，其中有三人是香港、澳门人士。
