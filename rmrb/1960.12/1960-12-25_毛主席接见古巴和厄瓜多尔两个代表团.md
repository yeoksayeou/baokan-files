Title: Chairman Mao Meets with Cuban and Ecuadorian Delegations
Author:
Date: 1960-12-25
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, December 24th – Chairman Mao Zedong received all members of the Cuban Women's Delegation headed by Elena Hill this evening and had a cordial and friendly conversation with them.

Present at the meeting were Liu Qingyang, Vice-Chairman of the All-China Women's Federation, and Qi Yun, Deputy Director of the International Liaison Department of the All-China Women's Federation.

Xinhua News Agency, December 24th – Chairman Mao Zedong received all members of the Ecuadorian Cultural Delegation headed by Ogenes Valledes this evening and had a cordial and friendly conversation with them.

Present at the meeting were Chu Tunan, President of the Chinese-Latin American Friendship Association, Zhou Erfu, Vice President, and others. (Attached Pictures)

Top left picture: Chairman Mao Zedong and the Cuban Women's Delegation. The fourth person from the left is Elena Hill, the head of the delegation.

Top right picture: Chairman Mao Zedong and the Ecuadorian Cultural Delegation. The fourth person from the left is Ogenes Valledes, the head of the delegation.
　　　　Xinhua News Agency reporters Hou Bo, Meng Qingbiao

### 毛主席接见古巴和厄瓜多尔两个代表团

1960-12-25
第1版()
专栏：

    毛主席接见古巴和厄瓜多尔两个代表团
    新华社24日讯　毛泽东主席今晚接见了以埃伦娜·希尔为首的古巴妇女代表团全体成员，并且同她们进行了亲切友好的谈话。
    接见时在座的，有全国妇联副主席刘清扬，全国妇联国际联络部副部长戚云。
    新华社24日讯　毛泽东主席今晚接见了以奥赫内斯·巴莱德斯为首的厄瓜多尔文化代表团全体成员，并且同他们进行了亲切友好的谈话。
    接见时在座的，有中国拉丁美洲友好协会会长楚图南、副会长周而复等。（附图片）

    上右图：毛泽东主席和厄瓜多尔文化代表团合影。左起第四人是奥赫内斯·巴莱德斯团长。
