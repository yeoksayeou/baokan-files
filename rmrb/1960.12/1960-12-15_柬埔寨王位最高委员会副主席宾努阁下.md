Title: His Excellency Penn Nouth, Vice-Chairman of the Supreme Royal Council of Cambodia
Author:
Date: 1960-12-15
Page: 5

    His Excellency Penn Nouth, Vice-Chairman of the Supreme Royal Council of Cambodia, was born in Phnom Penh on April 1, 1906.
    In 1946, he served as the Mayor of Phnom Penh. In 1947, he served as the Minister of State of the Royal Government of Cambodia. He served as Prime Minister from September 1948 to January 1949. In 1951, he served as the King's Personal Advisor.
    In 1953, he served as Prime Minister again, and in 1955, he served as an advisor to the Supreme Royal Council. He served as Prime Minister again from 1954 to 1955.
    In 1958, he served as the Cambodian Royal Kingdom's Ambassador Extraordinary and Plenipotentiary to France. In the same year, he was recalled to serve as the Prime Minister of the Cabinet, and after resigning, he continued to serve as the Ambassador to France.
    In June 1960, he served as the Vice-Chairman of the Supreme Royal Council. He is also the General Secretary of the Cambodian People's Socialist Community and the Vice-Chairman of the Khmer Royal Socialist Youth League.
    Under the leadership of Prince Norodom Sihanouk, His Excellency Penn Nouth actively participated in the struggle to safeguard Cambodia's national independence and adhere to a policy of peace and neutrality, and has consistently advocated for Sino-Cambodian friendship, making significant efforts to develop friendly relations between China and Cambodia.

### 柬埔寨王位最高委员会副主席宾努阁下

1960-12-15
第5版()
专栏：

    柬埔寨王位最高委员会副主席宾努阁下
    柬埔寨王位最高委员会副主席宾努阁下1906年4月1日生于金边。
    1946年任金边市市长。1947年任柬埔寨王国政府国务大臣。1948年9月至1949年1月任首相。1951 年任国王私人顾问。
    1953年又任首相，1955年任皇廷最高会议顾问。1954年至1955年再次任首相。
    1958年任柬埔寨王国驻法国特命全权大使。同年被召回任内阁首相，辞职后仍任驻法国大使。
    1960年6月任王位最高委员会副主席。他又是柬埔寨人民社会同盟总书记和高棉王家社会主义青年团副主席。
    在诺罗敦·西哈努克亲王的领导下，宾努阁下积极参加了维护柬埔寨的民族独立和坚持和平中立政策的斗争，并一贯主张中柬友好，为发展中柬友好关系作了重大努力。
