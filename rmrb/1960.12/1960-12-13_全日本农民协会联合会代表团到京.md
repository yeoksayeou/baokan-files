Title: Delegation of the All-Japan Farmers Association Federation Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1960-12-13
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, December 12th: A delegation of twelve members from the All-Japan Farmers Association Federation, led by Chairman Shigeichi Saiki, arrived in Beijing on the 11th.

The delegation is visiting China at the invitation of the National Committee of the Agricultural and Forestry Workers' Union of China.

Prior to arriving in Beijing, the delegation visited Guangzhou, Wuhan, Zhengzhou, and other places.

### 全日本农民协会联合会代表团到京

1960-12-13
第4版()
专栏：

    全日本农民协会联合会代表团到京
    新华社12日讯　全日本农民协会联合会代表团一行十二人，在团长齐木重一率领下11日到达北京。
    代表团是应中国农林工会全国委员会邀请来我国访问的。
    在到达北京以前，代表团曾访问了广州、武汉、郑州等地。
