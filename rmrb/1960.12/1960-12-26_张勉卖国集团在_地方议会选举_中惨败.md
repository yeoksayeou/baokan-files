Title: The Chang Myon Traitorous Clique Suffers Miserable Defeat in "Local Assembly Elections"
Author:
Date: 1960-12-26
Page: 6

　　Xinhua News Agency, Pyongyang, 22nd. Seoul dispatch: The Chang Myon clique, running dogs of U.S. imperialism in South Korea, suffered a miserable defeat in the recent "local assembly elections."
　　Despite the Chang Myon's Democratic Party clique adopting all sorts of despicable methods such as deception, suppression, and rigging, they only managed to obtain 192 seats out of a total of 487.
　　According to United Press International, due to the voters' distrust of South Korean politicians and their party organizations, "voter turnout was insufficient." Throughout South Korea, the average voter turnout was 67%. In Seoul, only 45% of eligible voters cast their ballots, "the lowest figure in Korean history."

### 张勉卖国集团在“地方议会选举”中惨败

1960-12-26
第6版()
专栏：

    张勉卖国集团在“地方议会选举”中惨败
    新华社平壤22日电　汉城消息：美帝国主义在南朝鲜的走狗张勉集团在最近举行的“地方议会选举”中遭到惨败。
    张勉的民主党集团尽管采取了种种欺骗、镇压和舞弊等卑鄙手法，但结果在全部四百八十七个席位中仅得到一百九十二个席位。
    合众国际社说，由于选民对南朝鲜政客和他们的党组织的不信任，“投票人数不足”。在全南朝鲜，平均投票率是67％。在汉城，只有45％的选民投了票，“这是韩国历史上最低的数字”。
