Title: Lao Fighting Forces Destroy Rebel Lang Khang Stronghold
Author:
Date: 1960-12-22
Page: 6
Xinhua News Agency, Hanoi, December 21st: The Lao Fighting Forces radio station broadcast tonight: The General Command of the Lao Fighting Forces issued a communique on the 21st, stating that after the Lao Fighting Forces surrounded two companies of traitorous troops in the Lang Khang stronghold in Ganmon Province from the 16th to the 18th, they launched an attack on the 19th, immediately destroying the Lang Khang stronghold, routing two companies of enemy troops, killing twenty-two enemies, and wounding some enemies. The Lao Fighting Forces also seized many weapons and military supplies.
The communique stated, "On the 17th, our army also attacked the Ba Gui stronghold, routing one company of enemy troops, inflicting more than ten casualties on the enemy. Our army captured one 60mm mortar, one heavy machine gun, two light machine guns, and seven rifles."

### 寮国战斗部队摧毁叛军郎康据点

1960-12-22
第6版()
专栏：

    寮国战斗部队摧毁叛军郎康据点
    新华社河内21日电　寮国战斗部队电台今晚广播：寮国战斗部队总指挥部21日发表公报说：寮国战斗部队武装力量在16日至18日包围甘蒙省郎康据点的两连叛国军队后，在19日展开进攻，当即将郎康据点摧毁，击溃敌军两个连，击毙敌人二十二名，击伤一部敌人。寮国战斗部队武装力量还缴获许多武器和军用物资。
    公报说，“17日，我军还进攻巴桂据点，敌军一个连被击溃，敌人伤亡十多人。我军缴获六十毫米迫击炮一门，重机枪一挺，轻机枪两挺，步枪七枝。”
