Title: Ambassador Xie Fusheng calls on Sihanouk
Author:
Date: 1960-12-13
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, Ulan Bator, December 12th: Chinese Ambassador to Mongolia, Xie Fusheng, paid a visit to Cambodian Head of State Prince Sihanouk, who is currently visiting Mongolia, this afternoon.
	During the meeting, they had a cordial and friendly conversation.

### 谢甫生大使拜会西哈努克

1960-12-13
第5版()
专栏：

    谢甫生大使拜会西哈努克
    新华社乌兰巴托12日电　中国驻蒙古大使谢甫生今天下午拜会了正在蒙古访问的柬埔寨国家元首西哈努克亲王。
    拜会时，双方进行了亲切友好的谈话。
