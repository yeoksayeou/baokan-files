Title: Chinese and Foreign News Comrades Welcome the New Year: China Journalists Association and Other Units Entertain Journalists from Fraternal Countries and Brotherly Party Newspapers
Author:
Date: 1960-12-31
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, December 30th: News workers in the capital city and journalists stationed in Beijing from fraternal countries and brotherly party newspapers joyously gathered together tonight to warmly celebrate the upcoming year of 1961.

Wu Lengxi, Chairman of the All-China Journalists Association, said at the beginning of the New Year's celebration that, looking back at the past year, it has been a year of great changes. Looking forward to the new year, we are all filled with confidence and joy. He said that in the past year, journalists from fraternal countries and brotherly party newspapers have done a lot of work in strengthening the ties between our brotherly party newspapers and news agencies, in enhancing mutual understanding between the people of our countries, and in strengthening friendship and unity between us. In this regard, he expressed his heartfelt gratitude to the journalist comrades from various countries and the news officials from various embassies in China who were present. Wu Lengxi finally raised a toast to the journalists from fraternal countries and brotherly party newspapers for achieving even greater accomplishments in the new year, and to the friendship and unity between the brotherly party newspapers and news agencies.

The reception was filled with a warm, cordial, and friendly atmosphere.

People from the capital's journalistic circles, journalists and their wives stationed in Beijing from fraternal countries and brotherly party newspapers, and news officials from embassies of fraternal countries in China attended tonight's reception.

Tonight's reception was jointly held by the All-China Journalists Association, People's Daily, Xinhua News Agency, Broadcasting Administration Bureau, News Department of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs, and other units.

### 中外新闻战友共迎新岁  记协等单位招待兄弟国家和兄弟党报记者

1960-12-31
第4版()
专栏：

    中外新闻战友共迎新岁
    记协等单位招待兄弟国家和兄弟党报记者
    新华社30日讯　首都新闻工作者同各兄弟国家和兄弟党报驻北京记者，今晚欢乐地共聚一堂，热烈祝贺即将到来的1961年。
    中华全国新闻工作者协会主席吴冷西在联欢招待会开始的时候说，回顾过去的一年是很大变化的一年，瞻望新的一年，我们大家充满了信心和喜悦。他说，在过去的一年，各兄弟国家和兄弟党报的记者，在加强我们兄弟党报刊和通讯社的联系方面，在增进我们各国人民之间的相互了解，以及加强我们之间的友谊和团结方面，作了很多工作。对此，他对到会的各国记者同志以及各国驻华使馆的新闻官员们，表示衷心的感谢。吴冷西最后为兄弟国家和兄弟党报的记者在新的一年获得更好的成就，为各兄弟党报刊、通讯社之间的友谊和团结，干杯。
    招待会上充满了热烈、亲切和友好的气氛。
    首都新闻界人士、各兄弟国家和兄弟党报驻北京的记者和夫人、各兄弟国家驻华使馆的新闻官员，出席了今晚的招待会。
    今晚的招待会是由中华全国新闻工作者协会、人民日报、新华通讯社、广播事业局、外交部新闻司等单位联合举行的。
