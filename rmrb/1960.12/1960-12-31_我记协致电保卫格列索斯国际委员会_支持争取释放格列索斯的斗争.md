Title: All-China Journalists Association Sends a Telegram to the International Committee for the Defense of Glezos in Support of the Struggle to Free Glezos
Author:
Date: 1960-12-31
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, 30th - The All-China Journalists Association today sent a telegram to the International Committee for the Defense of Glezos in Paris, firmly supporting their struggle to free the Greek national hero Manolis Glezos.
    The telegram said that Chinese journalists are extremely concerned about the health of the Greek national hero Manolis Glezos, who is currently suffering from inhumane torture in prison, and express utmost anger and the strongest protest against the Greek authorities' continued unreasonable imprisonment of Manolis Glezos. Chinese journalists firmly support the struggle of the International Committee for the Defense of Manolis Glezos to secure the release of Manolis Glezos.

### 我记协致电保卫格列索斯国际委员会  支持争取释放格列索斯的斗争

1960-12-31
第5版()
专栏：

    我记协致电保卫格列索斯国际委员会
    支持争取释放格列索斯的斗争
    新华社30日讯　中华全国新闻工作者协会今天致电巴黎保卫格列索斯国际委员会，坚决支持他们争取释放希腊民族英雄曼诺里斯·格列索斯而进行的斗争。
    电报说，中国新闻工作者对目前在狱中因遭受非人道折磨而患病的希腊民族英雄曼诺里斯·格列索斯的健康极为关切，对希腊当局至今仍无理囚禁曼诺里斯·格列索斯的罪恶行为，表示无比的愤怒和最强烈的抗议。中国新闻工作者坚决支持保卫曼诺里斯·格列索斯国际委员会为争取释放曼诺里斯·格列索斯而进行的斗争。
