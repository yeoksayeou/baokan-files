Title: The Second Group of Members of Our Cultural and Art Delegation Arrives in Rangoon
Author:
Date: 1960-12-25
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, Rangoon, December 24th. The second group of members of the Chinese Friendly Delegation to Burma, a cultural and art delegation of 114 people led by Liwei, Deputy Director of the Propaganda Department of the General Political Department of the Chinese People's Liberation, with the military rank of Senior Colonel, arrived here by plane this morning.

They were warmly welcomed at Rangoon Airport by senior Burmese civil and military officials and prominent figures in the cultural circle.

Among those who greeted the delegation at the airport were Colonel Su Min, leader of the Burmese Cultural Delegation to China, Lieutenant Colonel Wu Su Wu, Deputy Leader, Lieutenant Colonel Lun, Wu Ding Ao, Permanent Secretary of the Burmese Ministry of Culture, and about 400 prominent figures in the Rangoon cultural circle.

Xiao Ming, Cultural Counselor of the Chinese Embassy in Burma, Senior Colonel Jiang Keding, Military Attaché, other diplomatic personnel of the Embassy, and representatives of the Burmese Chinese community also welcomed them at the airport.

### 我文化艺术代表团第二批团员到仰光

1960-12-25
第6版()
专栏：

    我文化艺术代表团第二批团员到仰光
    新华社仰光24日电　中国访缅友好代表团的文化艺术代表团第二批团员一百一十四人在代表团副团长、中国人民解放军总政治部宣传部副部长李伟大校率领下，于今天上午乘飞机到达这里。
    他们在仰光机场受到缅甸高级文武官员和文化界著名人士的热烈欢迎。
    到机场迎接代表团的有缅甸访华文化代表团团长苏敏上校、副团长吴苏吴、伦中校、缅甸文化部常务副秘书吴丁奥和仰光文化界著名人士约四百人。
    中国驻缅甸大使馆文化参赞萧明、武官蒋克定大校和大使馆其它外交人员和缅甸华侨代表，也到机场欢迎。
