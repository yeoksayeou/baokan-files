Title: Poland and Albania Sign Trade Agreement
Author:
Date: 1960-12-23
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, Warsaw, December 20th. According to today's report in the Polish newspaper "People's Tribune," the government trade delegations of Poland and Albania recently signed a trade agreement for 1961-1965, a payment agreement, and a 1961 exchange of goods protocol in Warsaw.

The new trade agreement stipulates that the trade volume between the two sides will increase by about 20% in the next five years. Poland will import chromium ore, copper ore, cotton, tobacco, sardines, etc. from Albania.

Poland's exports to Albania will include industrial machinery and equipment, coke, steel, hardware, chemical products, coal mining equipment, textile machines, sugar, etc.

### 波兰和阿尔巴尼亚签订贸易协定

1960-12-23
第6版()
专栏：

    波兰和阿尔巴尼亚签订贸易协定
    新华社华沙20日电据波兰《人民论坛报》今天报道，波兰和阿尔巴尼亚政府贸易代表团最近在华沙签订了两国1961—1965年贸易协定、支付协定以及1961年换货议定书。
    新的贸易协定规定，今后五年内双方的贸易额将增长20％左右。波兰将从阿尔巴尼亚进口铬矿、铜矿、棉花、烟草、沙丁鱼等。
    波兰向阿尔巴尼亚出口的货物将包括工业机器设备、焦炭、钢材、五金器材、化学产品、采煤设备、纺织机、砂糖等。
