Title: Cuban Government Delegation Visits Mongolia; Cuban Economic Delegation Receives Warm Welcome in Hamhung, North Korea
Author:
Date: 1960-12-06
Page: 6
Xinhua News Agency, Ulaanbaatar, December 5th: A delegation of the Cuban Revolutionary Government, headed by Hector Rodriguez Llompart, Administrative Vice Minister of the Cuban Ministry of Foreign Affairs, arrived in Ulaanbaatar by special plane this afternoon, and was warmly welcomed by the Mongolian government and people from all walks of life.

Those who welcomed the delegation at the airport included Mongolian Deputy Chairman of the Council of Ministers Maidar, Chairman of the State Planning Committee Ragcha, Chairman of the Foreign Economic Liaison Committee Barjide, Minister of Foreign Affairs Shagdaruren, Minister of People's Army Affairs Lehagvasuren, and others.

Chinese Ambassador to Mongolia Xie Fusheng and diplomatic envoys from various countries to Mongolia also greeted the delegation at the airport.

In his welcoming speech, Maidar said that the Mongolian people and the Mongolian government warmly welcome the envoys of the heroic Cuban people, and extend their greetings to the Cuban people and the government headed by the hero of the Cuban people, Castro. He said that the Mongolian people celebrate the victory of the Cuban revolution and the establishment of Cuba's independent and autonomous state with the warmest feelings, and pay close attention to the heroic struggle of the Cuban people to defend the achievements of the revolution. The Mongolian people resolutely support the just struggle of the heroic Cuban people. Maidar said that the visit of the Cuban delegation is an important event in the establishment and development of friendly relations between Mongolia and Cuba; it is also an important event to expand political, economic and cultural cooperation between the two countries. This visit will help consolidate the friendship between the people of the two countries and strengthen world peace.

Hector Rodriguez Llompart said in his reply that we came to your country with the desire to consolidate and develop the friendship between Cuba and the Mongolian people. He said that the people of our two countries have undergone the same struggles. He expressed his belief that the purpose of the delegation's visit to Mongolia will surely be achieved.

This afternoon, Mongolian Deputy Chairman of the Council of Ministers Jagvaral received the delegation.

Today, the Mongolian newspaper "Unen" also published an editorial entitled "The Just Cause of the Cuban People Will Surely Triumph" to welcome the Cuban government delegation.

Xinhua News Agency, Pyongyang, December 4th: According to a report from the Korean Central News Agency in Hamhung, the economic delegation of the Cuban Revolutionary Government, led by Major Guevara, President of the National Bank of Cuba, accompanied by Korean Minister of Finance Han Sang-doo and Director of the First Bureau of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs Hong Dong-cheol, arrived in Hamhung by special train this morning for a visit. The people of Hamhung City warmly welcomed the Cuban guests, holding large banners that read "Yes to Cuba, No to the Yankees."

Those who welcomed the delegation at the station included: Hyun Moo-kwang, Chairman of the Hamgyongnam-do Committee of the Workers' Party of Korea, Park In-ha, Chairman of the Hamgyongnam-do People's Committee, and other local party and government leaders.

Park In-ha delivered a speech at the station, expressing a warm welcome to the Cuban guests on behalf of the people of Hamgyongnam-do and Hamhung City.

Then, Major Guevara spoke amid warm applause.

The delegation drove to Hamhung City amid the cheers of the masses. Welcoming crowds used colored ribbons to form a colorful river on the streets and showered the guests with colored confetti.

### 古巴政府代表团赴蒙古访问  古巴经济代表团在朝鲜咸兴访问时受到热烈欢迎

1960-12-06
第6版()
专栏：

    古巴政府代表团赴蒙古访问
    古巴经济代表团在朝鲜咸兴访问时受到热烈欢迎
    新华社乌兰巴托5日电　以古巴外交部行政副部长埃克托·罗德里格斯·略姆帕尔特为首的古巴革命政府代表团，今天下午乘专机到达乌兰巴托，受到了蒙古政府和当地各界人民的热烈欢迎。
    到机场欢迎的有蒙古部长会议副主席迈达尔、国家计划委员会主席拉格查、对外经济联络委员会主席巴尔日德、外交部长沙格达尔苏伦、人民军事务部部长勒哈格瓦苏伦等。
    中国驻蒙古大使谢甫生和各国驻蒙古使节也到机场迎接。
    迈达尔在致欢迎词时说，蒙古人民和蒙古政府热烈欢迎英勇的古巴人民的使者，并向古巴人民和以古巴人民的英雄——卡斯特罗为首的政府致意。他说，蒙古人民怀着最热烈的心情欢庆古巴革命的胜利和古巴独立自主的国家的建立，并且十分关切地注视着古巴人民保卫革命成果的英勇斗争。蒙古人民坚决支持英勇的古巴人民的正义斗争。迈达尔说，古巴代表团的访问，是蒙古、古巴两国友好关系建立和发展的重要事件；也是扩大两国政治、经济和文化合作的重要事件。这次访问将有助于巩固两国人民的友谊和加强世界和平。
    埃克托·罗德里格斯·略姆帕尔特在答词中说，我们怀着巩固和发展古巴同蒙古人民的友谊的愿望来到你们的国家。他说，我们两国人民进行过相同的斗争。他表示相信，代表团访问蒙古的目的一定能够实现。
    今天下午，蒙古部长会议副主席扎格瓦拉尔接见了代表团。
    今天蒙古《真理报》还发表了题为《古巴人民的正义事业一定能够胜利》的社论，欢迎古巴政府代表团。
    据新华社平壤4日电　据朝鲜中央通讯社咸兴讯：正在朝鲜访问的由古巴国家银行行长格瓦拉少校率领的古巴革命政府经济代表团，由朝鲜财政相韩相斗和外务省第一局局长洪东喆陪同，今天上午乘专车到达咸兴访问。咸兴市人民高举写着“要古巴，不要美国佬”的大幅标语牌热烈欢迎古巴贵宾。
    到车站欢迎的有：朝鲜劳动党咸镜南道委员会委员长玄武光、咸镜南道人民委员会委员长朴仁河等地方党政领导人。
    朴仁河在车站上致词，代表咸镜南道和咸兴市人民向古巴客人表示热烈的欢迎。
    接着，格瓦拉少校在热烈的掌声中讲了话。
    代表团在群众的欢呼声中，乘车前往咸兴市。欢迎群众用彩色的纸条在街道上组成了一条彩色的河流，并向贵宾抛撒彩色的纸屑。
