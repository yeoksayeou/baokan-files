Title: Chinese Medical Association Delegation Concludes Visit to Britain
Author:
Date: 1960-12-22
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, London, 20th - The Chinese Medical Association delegation has concluded its friendly visit to Britain, and its four members left here today for Copenhagen. Prior to this, the head of the delegation, Xue Gongchuo, and another member had already left London on December 15th.

The delegation arrived in London on November 22nd at the invitation of the British Royal College of Physicians. During its stay in Britain, the delegation visited hospitals, medical schools, and research institutions in several important cities in Britain. Members of the delegation also gave reports on the development of Chinese medicine and public health work. The audience was deeply impressed by the outstanding medical achievements of New China, especially since the Great Leap Forward.

### 中华医学会代表团在英国访问结束

1960-12-22
第5版()
专栏：

    中华医学会代表团在英国访问结束
    新华社伦敦20日电　中华医学会代表团结束了在英国的友好访问，它的四个团员已经在今天离开这里去哥本哈根。在这以前，代表团团长薛公绰和另一个团员已经在12月15日离开伦敦。
    代表团是应英国皇家医师协会的邀请，在11月22日到达伦敦的。在逗留英国期间，代表团访问了英国几个重要城市的医院、医学院和研究机关。代表团成员还作了关于中国医学发展和公共卫生工作情况的报告。听众对新中国特别是大跃进以来的卓越的医学成就，有深刻的印象。
