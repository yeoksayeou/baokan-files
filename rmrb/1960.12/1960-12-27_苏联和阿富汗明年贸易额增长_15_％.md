Title: Soviet Union and Afghanistan Trade Volume to Increase by 15% Next Year
Author:
Date: 1960-12-27
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, December 22nd: According to TASS, the protocol on the exchange of goods between the Soviet Union and Afghanistan for 1961 was signed in Moscow on the 22nd. The protocol stipulates that the trade volume between the two countries will increase by approximately 15% in 1961.

　　The Soviet Union will provide Afghanistan with machinery, equipment, petroleum products, rolled ferrous metals, cement, fabrics, etc.

　　Afghanistan will provide the Soviet Union with wool, cotton, raw hides, oilseeds, dried fruits, etc.

### 苏联和阿富汗明年贸易额增长15％

1960-12-27
第5版()
专栏：

    苏联和阿富汗明年贸易额增长15％
    新华社讯　据塔斯社莫斯科22日讯：苏联和阿富汗1961年换货议定书22日在莫斯科签字。议定书规定，1961年两国贸易额将增长约15％。
    苏联将向阿富汗提供机器、设备、石油制品、黑色金属轧材、水泥、布匹等。
    阿富汗将向苏联提供羊毛、棉花、生皮、油籽、干果等。
