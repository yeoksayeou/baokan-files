Title: Yugoslav Government Slanders Chinese Leaders, Chargé d'affaires Zhou Qiuye Protests Against the Unreasonable Behavior of the Yugoslav Authorities
Author:
Date: 1960-12-12
Page: 6
Xinhua News Agency, 11th — The Yugoslav government rudely slandered Chinese Premier Zhou Enlai in a note on the 8th.

    The Assistant Secretary-General of the Yugoslav State Secretariat, Vujvoda, met with Zhou Qiuye, Chargé d'affaires of the Chinese Embassy in Yugoslavia, at the State Secretariat for Foreign Affairs on the 8th. He read out the Yugoslav government's so-called "protest" note regarding Premier Zhou Enlai's speech at the reception held by the Albanian Ambassador to China on November 24, exposing Yugoslav revisionism. The note slandered Premier Zhou Enlai's exposure of the Yugoslav leadership group as "defamation," "fabrication," and "contrary to the facts," and claimed that Premier Zhou's speech was aimed at "worsening" Albanian-Yugoslav relations, etc. Therefore, Yugoslavia lodged the "strongest protest" with the Chinese government.

    Chargé d'affaires Zhou Qiuye forcefully refuted the Yugoslav side's unreasonable slander and resolutely rejected its "protest."

    During the meeting with Chargé d'affaires Zhou Qiuye, Vujvoda disregarded diplomatic etiquette and unreasonably interrupted Zhou Qiuye's speech several times. Chargé d'affaires Zhou Qiuye rebuked and protested Vujvoda's unreasonable behavior of rudely interrupting his speech.

### 南斯拉夫政府诬蔑我国领导人  周秋野代办抗议南当局的无理行为

1960-12-12
第6版()
专栏：

    南斯拉夫政府诬蔑我国领导人
    周秋野代办抗议南当局的无理行为
    新华社11日讯　南斯拉夫政府8日在一份照会中粗暴地诬蔑中国国务院总理周恩来。
    南斯拉夫国务秘书处秘书长助理维伊沃达8日在外交国务秘书处接见中国驻南斯拉夫大使馆临时代办周秋野时，就周恩来总理11月24日在阿尔巴尼亚驻中国大使馆举行的招待会上揭露南斯拉夫修正主义的讲话，宣读了南斯拉夫政府的所谓的“抗议”照会。照会诬蔑周恩来总理对南斯拉夫领导集团的揭露是对他们的“诽谤”、“捏造”、“与事实相‘违背’”，说什么周总理的讲话是旨在‘恶化’阿南关系等。因此，南斯拉夫向中国政府提出“最强硬的抗议”。
    周秋野代办对南斯拉夫方面的无理诬蔑给予了有力的驳斥，坚决拒绝其“抗议”。
    维伊沃达在接见周秋野代办时竟不顾外交礼节，几次不讲理地阻挠周秋野代办讲话。周秋野代办斥责并抗议了维伊沃达粗暴地阻挠他讲话的无理行为。
