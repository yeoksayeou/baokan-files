Title: Singapore Prime Minister Lee Kuan Yew States Laotian Internal Affairs Allow No Foreign Interference
Author:
Date: 1960-12-23
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, Hanoi, December 20th: According to a report today from the Vietnam News Agency: Singapore Prime Minister Lee Kuan Yew stated that the Singapore government is concerned about the current serious situation in Laos caused by foreign interference. He said, "We firmly believe that the Laotian people are capable of resolving their own affairs without foreign interference."

Lee Kuan Yew's remarks were made in response to a telegram from Prime Minister Pham Van Dong of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam, dated December 15th.

### 新加坡总理李光耀表示  老挝内政不容外国干涉

1960-12-23
第5版()
专栏：

    新加坡总理李光耀表示
    老挝内政不容外国干涉
  据新华社河内20日电　据越南通讯社今天报道：新加坡总理李光耀表示，新加坡政府关心当前老挝的由于外国干涉而引起的严重局势。他说：“我们坚信，老挝人民能够在没有外国干涉的情况下，解决他们自己的事情。”
    李光耀的这番话是在他对越南民主共和国总理范文同12月15日的电报的答复中说的。
