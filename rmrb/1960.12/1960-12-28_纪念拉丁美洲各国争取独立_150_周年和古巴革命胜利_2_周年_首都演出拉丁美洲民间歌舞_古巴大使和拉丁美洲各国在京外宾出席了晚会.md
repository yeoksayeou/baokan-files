Title: Commemorating the 150th Anniversary of Latin American Countries' Struggle for Independence and the 2nd Anniversary of the Cuban Revolution Victory: Capital Performs Latin American Folk Song and Dance; Cuban Ambassador and Latin American Foreign Guests in Beijing Attended the Evening Party
Author:
Date: 1960-12-28
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, December 27th. The Chinese-Latin American Friendship Association, the Chinese Musicians Association, and the Chinese Dancers Association jointly held a music and dance evening party today to commemorate the 150th anniversary of Latin American countries' struggle for national independence and to celebrate the 2nd anniversary of the victory of the Cuban Revolution. Artists who recently returned from visiting Latin America performed wonderful Latin American and Chinese folk songs and dances, which were warmly welcomed by audiences from all walks of life in the capital.

Bao Erhan, Vice Chairman of the National Committee of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference, Zhang Hanfu, Vice Minister of Foreign Affairs, Chu Tunan, President of the Chinese-Latin American Friendship Association, Lü Ji, Chairman of the Chinese Musicians Association, Ouyang Yuqian, Chairman of the Chinese Dancers Association, and more than a thousand people from all walks of life in the capital attended the evening party. Oscar Pino Santos, the Cuban Ambassador to China, and foreign guests from various Latin American countries in Beijing were invited to attend the evening party.

The Cuban, Venezuelan, Colombian, and Mexican songs and dances performed by the capital's artists tonight were all learned during their recent visit to Latin America. When the "July 26th" anthem was sung and the "Bambuco" dance was performed on stage, warm applause erupted from the audience. When the singer Guo Shuzhen sang songs from Cuba and other countries in Spanish, she was once again greeted with enthusiastic applause.

After the performance, Oscar Pino Santos, the Cuban Ambassador to China, went on stage to present flower baskets to the artists, congratulating them on the success of the performance.

### 纪念拉丁美洲各国争取独立150周年和古巴革命胜利2周年  首都演出拉丁美洲民间歌舞  古巴大使和拉丁美洲各国在京外宾出席了晚会

1960-12-28
第1版()
专栏：

    纪念拉丁美洲各国争取独立150周年和古巴革命胜利2周年
    首都演出拉丁美洲民间歌舞
    古巴大使和拉丁美洲各国在京外宾出席了晚会
    新华社27日讯　中国拉丁美洲友好协会、中国音乐家协会、中国舞蹈工作者协会，今天联合举办了纪念拉丁美洲各国争取民族独立一百五十周年和庆祝古巴革命胜利二周年音乐舞蹈晚会，由访问拉丁美洲后最近归国的艺术家们演出了精彩的拉丁美洲和中国的优秀民间歌舞，受到首都各界观众极其热烈的欢迎。
    政协全国委员会副主席包尔汉、外交部副部长章汉夫、中国拉丁美洲友好协会会长楚图南、中国音乐家协会主席吕骥、中国舞蹈工作者协会主席欧阳予倩和首都各界一千多人参加了晚会。应邀参加晚会的，有古巴驻中国大使奥斯卡·皮诺·桑托斯和在北京的拉丁美洲各国外宾。
    首都艺术家们今晚演出的古巴、委内瑞拉、哥伦比亚、墨西哥等国的歌舞，都是不久前在访问拉丁美洲的时候学到的，当舞台上唱完《七·二六》颂歌和跳完《班布戈》舞的时候，台下响起热烈的掌声，当歌唱家郭淑珍用西班牙语演唱古巴等国歌曲的时候，又一次受到热烈的鼓掌。
    演出结束以后，古巴驻中国大使奥斯卡·皮诺·桑托斯走上舞台，向艺术家赠送了花篮，祝贺演出成功。
