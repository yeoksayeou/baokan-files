Title: End of the Third Meeting of Chinese and Indian Officials; Chinese Officials Leave Rangoon for Home
Author:
Date: 1960-12-15
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Rangoon, December 14th: Zhang Wenjin, Director of the First Asian Department of the Chinese Ministry of Foreign Affairs, Yang Gongsu, Director of the Tibetan Foreign Affairs Office, and advisors, left here by plane this morning to return home after concluding the third meeting with Indian officials on the Sino-Indian border issue and signing the report to be submitted to the two governments.

During their stay in Rangoon, they called on Burmese Prime Minister U Nu, Foreign Minister Sao Khun Kyi, Permanent Secretary of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs James Barrington, and others.

U Nu and Sao Khun Kyi hosted banquets for officials of the Chinese and Indian governments respectively. The Burmese government also arranged for them to visit Taunggyi, the capital of Shan State. Li Yimang, the Chinese Ambassador to Burma, and other members of the embassy saw them off at the airport today. Meda, Director of the Northern Division of the Indian Ministry of Foreign Affairs, Gopal, Director of the Historical Division, and advisors who participated in the meeting of Chinese and Indian officials, diplomatic officials of the Indian Embassy in Burma, and Burmese government officials also came to the airport to see them off.

### 中印官员第三次会晤结束  中国官员离仰光回国

1960-12-15
第4版()
专栏：

    中印官员第三次会晤结束
    中国官员离仰光回国
    新华社仰光14日电　中国外交部第一亚洲司司长章文晋、西藏外事处处长杨公素和顾问在结束了同印度官员就中印边界问题举行的第三次会晤并签署了递交两国政府的报告后，今天上午乘飞机离开这里回国。
    在仰光期间，他们曾拜会缅甸总理吴努、外交部长藻昆卓、外交部常任秘书詹姆斯·巴林顿等。
    吴努和藻昆卓曾分别设宴招待中国和印度两国政府的官员。缅甸政府还替他们安排访问掸邦首府东枝。今天到机场送行的有中国驻缅甸大使李一氓和大使馆其他人员。参加中印官员会晤的印度外交部北方司司长梅达、历史司司长戈帕尔和顾问、印度驻缅甸大使馆的外交官员以及缅甸政府官员也到机场欢送。
