Title: Pakistani Communist Leader Hassan Nasir Murdered; Indian Communist Party Issues Statement Condemning the Crimes of the Pakistani Authorities
Author:
Date: 1960-12-04
Page: 6

    Xinhua News Agency, December 2nd (New Delhi dispatch from Bulgarian Telegraph Agency, November 30) -- Hassan Nasir, one of the leaders of the Communist Party of Pakistan, was murdered by police authorities on November 13th.
    The message said that Hassan Nasir had been arrested in Karachi four months ago.
    The Pakistani authorities announced the news of his murder only after the police had buried him.
    Bulgarian Telegraph Agency said that the Secretariat of the National Council of the Communist Party of India recently issued a statement condemning the Pakistani authorities responsible for the crime of murdering Hassan Nasir and demanded that the murderers be punished.

### 巴共领导人哈桑·纳西尔被害  印共发表声明谴责巴当局的罪行

1960-12-04
第6版()
专栏：

    巴共领导人哈桑·纳西尔被害
    印共发表声明谴责巴当局的罪行
    新华社2日讯　据保加利亚通讯社11月30日新德里消息，巴基斯坦共产党领导人之一哈桑·纳西尔在11月13日被警察当局杀害。
    消息说，哈桑·纳西尔是在四个月前在卡拉奇被逮捕的。
    巴基斯坦当局在警察把他埋葬之后才宣布了他的被害消息。
    保加利亚通讯社说，印度共产党全国委员会书记处书记最近发表声明，谴责应对杀害哈桑·纳西尔罪行负责的巴基斯坦当局，并要求惩办凶手。
