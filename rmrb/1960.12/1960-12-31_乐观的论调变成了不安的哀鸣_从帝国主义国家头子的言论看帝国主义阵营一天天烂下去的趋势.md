Title: Optimistic Tunes Turn into Anxious Laments: Observing the Imperialist Camp's Gradual Decline from the Statements of Imperialist Heads
Author:
Date: 1960-12-31
Page: 6

The year 1960, the first year of the sixties, is about to pass.

At the beginning of this year, even as early as the end of the previous year, officials of imperialist countries made various optimistic estimates for 1960. "Years of prosperity," "good prospects"... such pleasant words filled their speeches.

What was the result? For imperialist countries, 1960 was a year of further accelerated decline and disintegration: economic conditions became increasingly bleak, aggressive policies continued to fail, the colonial system collapsed rapidly, internal contradictions developed sharply... In short, it was rotting day by day.

Of course, officials of imperialist countries tried their best to conceal their unfavorable situation; but even in their remarks, they could not help but reflect that their past "hopes" for 1960 had fallen through.

The following are excerpts from some of the initial optimistic pronouncements and current anxious laments of U.S. President Eisenhower, British Prime Minister Macmillan, French President de Gaulle, and West German Chancellor Adenauer.

Eisenhower: From "Surging Strength" to "Very Distressed"

    "Today, our surging strength is clear to everyone. 1960 holds the promise of becoming the most prosperous year in our nation's history."
(State of the Union Address to Congress, January 7, 1960)

    "The prospects for continued economic growth throughout 1960 and beyond are good."
(Economic Report to Congress, January 20, 1960)

### 乐观的论调变成了不安的哀鸣  从帝国主义国家头子的言论看帝国主义阵营一天天烂下去的趋势

1960-12-31
第6版()
专栏：

    乐观的论调变成了不安的哀鸣
    从帝国主义国家头子的言论看帝国主义阵营一天天烂下去的趋势
    1960年，六十年代的头一年，即将过去了。
    在这一年的年初，甚至早在上一年的年末，帝国主义国家的官方人士对1960年作了各种各样乐观的估计。“繁荣的年代”，“良好的前景”……诸如此类动听的词句，充满在他们的演说中。
    结果怎样呢？1960年对于帝国主义国家来说是进一步加速走向衰落和瓦解的一年：经济情况日益暗淡，侵略政策不断失败，殖民体系急剧崩溃，内部矛盾发展尖锐……总之，是一天天烂下去。
    当然，帝国主义国家的官方人士竭力企图掩饰他们的不利处境；但是，即使在他们的言论中，也不能不反映出他们过去对1960年的“希望”已经落空了。
    下面选录美国总统艾森豪威尔、英国首相麦克米伦、法国总统戴高乐和西德总理阿登纳的一些当初的乐观论调和目前的不安哀鸣。
