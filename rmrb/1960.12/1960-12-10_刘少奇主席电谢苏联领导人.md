Title: Chairman Liu Shaoqi Sends Telegram Thanking Soviet Leaders
Author: Liu Shaoqi
Date: 1960-12-10
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, Irkutsk, 9th. Liu Shaoqi, Vice Chairman of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of China, Chairman of the People's Republic of China, and Head of the Chinese Party and Government Delegation, sent a telegram to the Soviet leaders today upon leaving Irkutsk to return to China, expressing his gratitude. The telegram reads as follows:

    Dear Comrade Khrushchev, Comrade Brezhnev:

    As we are about to leave your great country, dear comrades, please allow me, on behalf of the Chinese Party and Government Delegation, to once again express our heartfelt thanks for your invitation and warm reception, and through you to extend our best wishes to the Soviet people, the Communist Party of the Soviet Union, and the Soviet Government. We wish the Soviet people new and greater achievements under the leadership of the glorious Communist Party of the Soviet Union in the noble cause of building communism and safeguarding world peace. May the brotherly friendship between the Chinese and Soviet peoples last forever!

    Liu Shaoqi
    December 9, 1960, on the plane

### 刘少奇主席电谢苏联领导人
刘少奇
1960-12-10
第1版()
专栏：

    刘少奇主席电谢苏联领导人
    新华社伊尔库次克9日电　中共中央副主席、中华人民共和国主席、中国党政代表团团长刘少奇，今天在离开伊尔库次克回国的时候致电苏联领导人表示感谢，电文如下：亲爱的赫鲁晓夫同志，勃列日涅夫同志：
    在我们就要离开你们伟大国土的时候，亲爱的同志们，请允许我以中国党政代表团的名义对你们的邀请和盛情接待，再一次表示衷心的感谢，并且通过你们向苏联人民、苏联共产党和苏联政府表示最良好的祝愿，祝苏联人民在光荣的苏联共产党的领导下，在共产主义建设和维护世界和平的崇高事业中取得新的更大的成就，祝中苏两国人民的兄弟友谊万古长青！　刘少奇　1960年12月9日在飞机上
