Title: Likhachev Automobile Plant Warmly Welcomes Chairman Liu; Chairman Liu Watches Ballet in Moscow
Author:
Date: 1960-12-08
Page: 2
Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, December 6th. Liu Shaoqi, Vice Chairman of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of China, Chairman of the People's Republic of China, and head of the Chinese Party and Government Delegation, visited the Likhachev Automobile Plant in Moscow this afternoon, accompanied by Brezhnev, Member of the Presidium of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of the Soviet Union and Chairman of the Presidium of the Supreme Soviet of the Soviet Union. Chairman Liu Shaoqi was warmly received and welcomed by the factory workers.

Today, the members of the Chinese Party and Government Delegation who accompanied Chairman Liu Shaoqi on his visit to the Likhachev Automobile Plant included Li Jingquan, Yang Shangkun, and Liu Ningyi.

Those accompanying Chairman Liu Shaoqi on his visit to the factory included Soviet Ambassador to China Chervonenko, Deputy Director of the Far East Department of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs Gutikov, Deputy Director of the Protocol Department of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs Nikiforov, and First Secretary of the Moscow City Committee of the Communist Party of the Soviet Union Demichev.

Today, the national flags of China and the Soviet Union were hung at the entrance of the Likhachev Automobile Plant. At 3 p.m., when Chairman Liu Shaoqi and other distinguished guests arrived at the factory, they were warmly welcomed by the factory director Krylov, the factory party secretary Sofonov, and the factory workers, who were waiting at the entrance. Factory Director Krylov introduced the factory's general situation and the friendly connection between the factory and China's Changchun Automobile Plant to Chairman Liu Shaoqi and the delegation members, and presented gifts to the distinguished guests.

Then, Chairman Liu Shaoqi and others, accompanied by Factory Director Krylov, visited the tool, foundry, and final assembly workshops. In the workshops, large banners written in both Chinese and Russian were hung, reading: "Welcome dear Comrade Liu Shaoqi!"
	"Long live the eternal and unbreakable friendship between the great peoples of the Soviet Union and China!" When Chairman Liu Shaoqi and others entered the workshops, the masses shouted "Long live Soviet-Chinese friendship" and "Hurrah", and applauded enthusiastically. Many workers surged toward the workshop aisles, vying to shake hands and greet the Chinese guests.

Chairman Liu Shaoqi frequently waved to the workers, and had cordial conversations with some of them.

Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, December 6th. Liu Shaoqi, Vice Chairman of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of China, Chairman of the People's Republic of China, and head of the Chinese Party and Government Delegation, watched a ballet performance at the Bolshoi Theatre in Moscow on the evening of the 6th. This performance was specially organized by the Soviet Ministry of Foreign Affairs for the Chinese Party and Government Delegation.

Soviet party and government leaders Brezhnev, Kozlov, Kosygin, Mikoyan, Suslov, Furtseva, Deputy Minister of Foreign Affairs of the Soviet Union Firyubin, Soviet Ambassador to China Chervonenko, Member of the Board of the Soviet Ministry of Foreign Affairs Tugalinov, and Director of the Protocol Department of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs Molochkov, among others, accompanied Chairman Liu Shaoqi to watch the performance.

The members of the Chinese Party and Government Delegation who accompanied Chairman Liu Shaoqi to watch the performance included: Li Jingquan, Lu Dingyi, Yang Shangkun, Liu Ningyi, and Liu Xiao.

Diplomatic envoys from various countries to the Soviet Union also watched today's performance.

Today, the national flags of China and the Soviet Union were hung in the Bolshoi Theatre. When Chairman Liu Shaoqi and the members of the Chinese Party and Government Delegation entered the theatre accompanied by Soviet party and government leaders, the entire audience stood up and applauded enthusiastically.

At the beginning of the performance, the orchestra played the national anthems of China and the Soviet Union.

The programs performed today were: Chopin's "Les Sylphides," Rachmaninoff's "Paganini," and Gounod's "Walpurgis Night."

The wonderful performances by famous Soviet actors such as Ulanova and Lepeshinskaya received warm praise from the Chinese guests and the audience.

After the performance, Chairman Liu Shaoqi and others shook hands cordially with the actors and presented them with flower baskets. (Attached pictures)

### 李哈乔夫汽车厂热烈欢迎刘主席  刘主席在莫斯科观看芭蕾舞

1960-12-08
第2版()
专栏：

    李哈乔夫汽车厂热烈欢迎刘主席
    刘主席在莫斯科观看芭蕾舞
    新华社莫斯科6日电　中国共产党中央委员会副主席、中华人民共和国主席、中国党政代表团团长刘少奇，今天下午在苏联共产党中央委员会主席团委员、苏联最高苏维埃主席团主席勃列日涅夫陪同下，访问了莫斯科李哈乔夫汽车厂，刘少奇主席受到了工厂职工亲切热烈的接待和欢迎。
    今天，随同刘少奇主席访问李哈乔夫汽车厂的中国党政代表团团员有李井泉、杨尚昆、刘宁一。
    陪同刘少奇主席访问工厂的有苏联驻中国大使契尔沃年科、外交部远东司副司长顾悌科夫、外交部礼宾司副司长尼基弗罗夫、苏共莫斯科市委第一书记杰米契夫。
    今天，李哈乔夫汽车厂门口悬挂着中苏两国国旗。下午三点钟，当刘少奇主席等贵宾们到达工厂时，受到了正在门口等候迎接的工厂厂长克雷洛夫、工厂党委书记索弗诺夫和工厂工人们的热烈欢迎。厂长克雷洛夫向刘少奇和代表团团员们介绍了工厂概况和这家工厂同我国长春汽车厂的友谊联系，并且向贵宾们赠送了礼物。
    然后，刘少奇主席等在厂长克雷洛夫陪同下，参观了工具、铸造和总装配三个车间。车间里，悬挂着用中、俄两种文字写的大幅标语：“欢迎亲爱的刘少奇同志！”
  “苏中两国伟大人民永恒的、牢不可破的友谊万岁！”当刘少奇主席等走入车间时，群众高呼“苏中友好万岁”和“乌拉”，并且热烈鼓掌，许多工人涌向车间通道，争向中国贵宾们握手问好。
    刘少奇主席频频向工人们招手致意，并和一些工人亲切地谈了话。
    新华社莫斯科6日电　中国共产党中央委员会副主席、中华人民共和国主席、中国党政代表团团长刘少奇，6日晚在莫斯科大剧院观看了芭蕾舞演出。这次演出是苏联外交部为我国党政代表团特别举办的。
    陪同刘少奇主席观看演出的有苏联党政领导人勃列日涅夫，科兹洛夫，柯西金，米高扬，苏斯洛夫，福尔采娃，苏联外交部副部长费留宾，苏联驻中国大使契尔沃年科，苏联外交部部务委员图加林诺夫，外交部礼宾司司长莫洛奇科夫等人。
    随同刘少奇主席观看演出的有我国党政代表团团员：李井泉、陆定一、杨尚昆、刘宁一、刘晓。
    观看今天演出的还有各国驻苏联的外交使节。
    今天，大剧院里悬挂着中苏两国国旗。当刘少奇主席和我国党政代表团团员们在苏联党政领导人陪同下进入剧场时，观众全体起立，热烈鼓掌。
    在演出开始时，乐队高奏中苏两国国歌。
    今天演出的剧目有：萧邦的《仙女们》，拉赫玛尼诺夫的《帕加尼尼》和古诺的《瓦尔普基之夜》。
    苏联著名演员乌兰诺娃、列皮辛斯卡娅等人的精彩表演受到了中国贵宾和观众们的热烈赞扬。
    演出结束后，刘少奇主席等和演员们亲切握手，并向他们赠送了花篮。（附图片）
