Title: Ministry of Culture and Sino-Cambodian Friendship Association Hold Art Performance to Welcome Prince Sihanouk and Cambodian Guests; Vice Chairman Dong and Premier Zhou, Vice Premier Chen Yi Accompany and Enjoy Ballet

Author: 
Date: 1960-12-18
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, December 17th. The Cambodian Head of State, Prince Norodom Sihanouk, and his wife, accompanied by Vice Chairman Dong Biwu, Premier Zhou Enlai, and Vice Premier Chen Yi, attended an art performance held by the Ministry of Culture and the China-Cambodia Friendship Association tonight and enjoyed the ballet "Le Corsaire" performed by the Experimental Ballet Troupe of the Beijing Dance School.

　　Tonight’s event was held to welcome Prince Sihanouk and the accompanying Cambodian guests.

　　Among those attending the evening party with Prince Sihanouk were: Vice Chairman of the Cambodian High Council of the Throne, Penn Nouth, Prime Minister Pho Proeung, First Deputy Prime Minister Nhiek Tioulong and other distinguished guests.

　　Also invited to the evening party were Prince Sihanouk's princesses and princes, some senior Cambodian officials, members of the Cambodian Economic Delegation, and some artists from the Royal Dance Troupe.

　　Cambodian Ambassador to China, Leng Ngeth, was also invited to the evening party.

　　Also present at the evening party were: Vice Premiers Ulanhu and Li Xiannian, Vice Chairmen of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress Bainqen Erdini Qoigyi Gyaincain and Lin Feng, Minister of Culture Shen Yanbing, President of the China-Cambodia Friendship Association Qi Yanming, Director of the Commission for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries Zhang Xiruo, and Chinese Ambassador to Cambodia Wang Youping, and others.

　　After the performance, Prince Sihanouk, accompanied by Vice Chairman Dong Biwu, Premier Zhou Enlai, Vice Premier Chen Yi and others, walked onto the stage and warmly shook hands with the performers. Prince Sihanouk said to them: "Thank you for your admirable and wonderful performance." He also presented them with silver gifts.

### 文化部和中柬友协举行文艺晚会  欢迎西哈努克亲王和柬贵宾  董副主席周总理陈毅副总理陪同欣赏芭蕾舞

1960-12-18
第1版()
专栏：

    文化部和中柬友协举行文艺晚会
    欢迎西哈努克亲王和柬贵宾
    董副主席周总理陈毅副总理陪同欣赏芭蕾舞
    新华社17日讯　柬埔寨国家元首诺罗敦·西哈努克亲王和夫人，由董必武副主席、周恩来总理、陈毅副总理陪同，出席了今晚文化部和中国柬埔寨友好协会举行的文艺晚会，欣赏了北京舞蹈学校实验芭蕾舞剧团演出的芭蕾舞剧“海侠”。
    今天的晚会是为欢迎西哈努克亲王和随同的柬埔寨贵宾们举行的。
    同西哈努克亲王一起出席晚会的有：柬埔寨王位最高委员会副主席宾努，首相福·波伦，第一副首相?·刁隆等贵宾。
    应邀出席晚会的柬埔寨贵宾，还有西哈努克亲王的公主、王子，柬埔寨的一些高级官员，柬埔寨经济代表团的成员，皇家舞蹈团的部分艺术家等。
    柬埔寨驻中国大使兰·?特也应邀出席了晚会。
    出席晚会的还有：乌兰夫、李先念副总理，班禅额尔德尼·却吉坚赞、林枫副委员长，文化部部长沈雁冰，中国柬埔寨友好协会会长齐燕铭，对外文化联络委员会主任张奚若，中国驻柬埔寨大使王幼平等。
    舞剧演出结束后，西哈努克亲王由董必武副主席、周恩来总理、陈毅副总理等陪同走上舞台，同演员们热烈地握手。西哈努克亲王对他们说：“感谢你们令人钦佩的精采的演出。”并且还赠送给他们银质的礼品。
