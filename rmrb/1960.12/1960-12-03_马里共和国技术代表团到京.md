Title: Technical Delegation from the Republic of Mali Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1960-12-03
Page: 4

    Xinhua News Agency, December 2nd -- The technical delegation from the Republic of Mali, invited by the Ministry of Light Industry to visit China, consisting of construction engineer Silla Fodi and agricultural technician Diarra Tidiani, arrived in Beijing by plane today. They were greeted at the airport by Chen Zhifang, Director of the Science and Research Department of the Ministry of Light Industry, Gu Yuan, Deputy Director of the Foreign Construction Bureau of the Ministry of Construction Engineering, and Zhu Suichu, Deputy Director of the General Office of the Ministry of Agriculture.

### 马里共和国技术代表团到京

1960-12-03
第4版()
专栏：

    马里共和国技术代表团到京
    新华社2日讯　应轻工业部邀请来我国访问的马里共和国技术代表团二人：建筑工程师西拉·福迪和农业技师迪亚拉·蒂迪亚尼今天乘飞机到达北京。到机场欢迎的有轻工业部科学研究司司长陈智方、建筑工程部对外施工局副局长古原、农业部办公厅副主任朱遂初等。
