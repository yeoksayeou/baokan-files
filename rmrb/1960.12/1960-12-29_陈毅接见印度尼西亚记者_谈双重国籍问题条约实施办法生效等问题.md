Title: Chen Yi Meets Indonesian Reporter to Discuss the Implementation of the Treaty on Dual Nationality Issues and Other Matters

Author: 
Date: 1960-12-29
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, December 28th: Vice Premier of the State Council and Minister of Foreign Affairs Chen Yi, at the request of Suwito, a reporter from the Indonesian Antara News Agency stationed in Beijing, met with him this afternoon and gave a speech regarding the publication and entry into force of the implementation measures of the treaty between the Chinese and Indonesian governments on the issue of dual nationality.

Also present at the meeting was Shao Zonghan, Deputy Director of the Information Department of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs.

### 陈毅接见印度尼西亚记者  谈双重国籍问题条约实施办法生效等问题

1960-12-29
第1版()
专栏：

    陈毅接见印度尼西亚记者
    谈双重国籍问题条约实施办法生效等问题
    新华社28日讯　国务院副总理兼外交部长陈毅应印度尼西亚安塔拉通讯社驻北京记者苏维多的请求，今天下午接见了他，并且就中国和印度尼西亚两国政府关于双重国籍问题的条约的实施办法公布生效等问题，发表了谈话。
    接见时在座的有外交部新闻司副司长邵宗汉。
