Title: Ministry of National Defense Hosts Farewell Banquet for Korean People's Army Ensemble; Korean People's Army Ensemble Holds Closing Ceremony for China Performance Tour in Shenyang
Author:
Date: 1960-12-04
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Shenyang, December 3rd: The Ministry of National Defense hosted a banquet in Shenyang tonight to bid farewell to all the guests of the Korean People's Army Ensemble who are about to leave our country.

General Chen Xilian, General Lai Chuanzhu, leaders of the Chinese People's Liberation Army Shenyang Military Region, Huang Huoqing, First Secretary of the CPC Liaoning Provincial Committee, Che Xiangchen, Vice Governor of Liaoning Province, Jiao Ruoyu, First Secretary of the CPC Shenyang Municipal Committee, and others attended the banquet.

Major General Wei Chuantong, representative of the Ministry of National Defense of our country, and Zheng Fenggui, Consul General of Korea in Changchun, also attended the banquet.

General Chen Xilian delivered a speech on behalf of the Ministry of National Defense at the banquet. He said that the friendship between the Chinese and Korean people has been tested over time and has been established and grown on the basis of Marxism-Leninism and proletarian internationalism, and in the common struggle against American imperialist aggression. Therefore, this friendship is eternal and unbreakable. In his speech, General Chen Xilian repeatedly expressed his gratitude for the visit of the Korean People's Army Ensemble and the deep friendship of the Korean people and their army towards the Chinese people.

Major General Zhang Ruihuan also spoke at the banquet. He expressed his gratitude for the warm reception the Korean People's Army Ensemble received during its visit to China. He said: Our people and army cherish the friendship between the Chinese and Korean people very much. The people and officers and soldiers of the Korean People's Army were, are, and will always be with the Chinese people and the officers and soldiers of the Chinese People's Liberation Army, sharing life and death, and advancing side by side for the final victory of the cause of socialism and communism, for opposing imperialist aggression, and defending peace in the Far East and the world.

Huang Huoqing, First Secretary of the CPC Liaoning Provincial Committee, raised his glass at the banquet and proposed a toast to the success of the Korean People's Army Ensemble's visit to China.

Zheng Fenggui, Consul General of Korea in Changchun, spoke at the banquet on behalf of the Korean Embassy in China.

The banquet was filled with a brotherly and cordial atmosphere. The hosts and guests repeatedly stood up and toasted to the friendship cemented with blood between the Chinese and Korean people and armies, to the solidarity of the socialist camp headed by the Soviet Union, and to world peace.

After the banquet, the guests participated in a soiree.

Xinhua News Agency, Shenyang, December 3rd: The Korean People's Army Ensemble solemnly held the closing ceremony for its China performance tour at the Shenyang Bayi Theater today.

Every program at the closing ceremony performance won thunderous applause from the audience. Many programs were repeatedly re-performed due to the audience's warm welcome. The dances "Comrades-in-Arms" and "Meeting on the Highland," which fully expressed the militant friendship between the Chinese and Korean people cemented with blood, brought the audience's emotions to a climax.

Attending the closing ceremony were Lieutenant General Zeng Shaoshan, Lieutenant General Du Ping, Major General Zhang Jiecheng, Major General Li Boqiu, Major General Li Guilin, leaders of the Chinese People's Liberation Army Shenyang Military Region, Tang Hongguang, Vice President of the Liaoning Provincial Branch of the Sino-Korean Friendship Association, Song Guang, Deputy Mayor of Shenyang, and Major General Wei Chuantong, representative of the Ministry of National Defense of our country, who accompanied the Korean People's Army Ensemble on its visit and performance tour.

Zheng Fenggui, Consul General of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea in Changchun, also attended the closing ceremony.

Also participating in the closing ceremony were representatives of former Volunteer Army combat heroes, families of martyrs, families of servicemen, and more than 2,000 representatives from provincial and municipal literary and art groups and people from all walks of life.

Du Ping delivered a speech at the closing ceremony. On behalf of the Ministry of National Defense of the People's Republic of China and all officers and soldiers of the Chinese People's Liberation Army, he warmly congratulated the Korean People's Army Ensemble on the great achievements it had made in its visit and performance tour, and expressed his sincerest gratitude to all the comrades of the Ensemble. He said that your performances have not only developed the cultural exchanges between the Chinese and Korean people, which have a long history, but also further consolidated and developed the militant friendship between the Chinese and Korean people cemented with blood.

Major General Zhang Ruihuan, head of the Korean People's Army Ensemble, expressed his sincere gratitude to the Chinese Communist Party, the Chinese government, and the officers and soldiers of the Chinese People's Liberation Army for their warm welcome and hospitality. He said that during our visit and performance tour in China, we have further realized how deep the friendship between the Chinese and Korean people is. The unity of the Chinese and Korean people cannot be destroyed by any force. The Korean people and army will forever stand with the Chinese people and their army and fight to the end for our common cause.

Amid joyful military music and warm applause, Lieutenant General Du Ping presented a flag to the Korean People's Army Ensemble on behalf of the General Political Department of the Chinese People's Liberation Army. A group of young female literary and art workers presented flowers to the guests.

The closing ceremony ended in the majestic music of "The Hearts of People All Over the World Are One." (Attached picture)

### 国防部欢送朝鲜人民军协奏团  朝鲜人民军协奏团在沈阳举行访华演出闭幕式

1960-12-04
第4版()
专栏：

    国防部欢送朝鲜人民军协奏团
    朝鲜人民军协奏团在沈阳举行访华演出闭幕式
    新华社沈阳3日电　国防部今晚在沈阳举行宴会，欢送即将离开我国的朝鲜人民军协奏团全体客人。
    中国人民解放军沈阳部队首长陈锡联上将、赖传珠上将，中共辽宁省委第一书记黄火青，辽宁省副省长车向忱，中共沈阳市委第一书记焦若愚等出席了宴会。
    我国国防部代表魏传统少将和朝鲜驻长春总领事郑凤珪也出席了宴会。
    陈锡联上将代表国防部在宴会上致词。他说，中朝两国人民的友谊是久经考验的，是在马克思列宁主义和无产阶级国际主义的基础上，在反对美帝国主义侵略的共同斗争中建立和成长起来的。因而这种友谊是永恒的、牢不可破的。陈锡联上将在讲话中，一再对朝鲜人民军协奏团的访问和朝鲜人民及其军队对中国人民的深厚友谊表示感谢。
    张瑞焕少将也在宴会上讲了话。他对朝鲜人民军协奏团在中国访问期间所受到的热情接待表示感谢。他说：我国人民和军队十分珍视朝中两国人民的友谊。我国人民和人民军官兵过去是、现在是、今后还将永远同中国人民和中国人民解放军官兵一起，同生死、共甘苦，为反对帝国主义侵略、保卫远东和世界和平，为社会主义和共产主义事业的最后胜利而并肩前进。
    中共辽宁省委第一书记黄火青在宴会上举杯，提议为朝鲜人民军协奏团在中国的访问演出成功干杯。
    朝鲜驻长春总领事郑凤珪，代表朝鲜驻中国大使馆在宴会上讲了话。
    宴会上充满了兄弟般的亲切气氛，宾主不断起立，为中朝两国人民和军队用鲜血凝成的友谊；为以苏联为首的社会主义阵营的团结；为世界和平干杯。
    宴会后，客人们参加了联欢晚会。
    新华社沈阳3日电　朝鲜人民军协奏团，今天在沈阳八一剧场隆重举行访华演出闭幕式。
    闭幕式演出的每一个节目都博得全场经久不息的掌声，许多节目经观众热烈欢迎一再重演。“战友”、“高地相逢”这两个充分表现中朝两国人民用鲜血凝成的战斗友谊的舞蹈，使场内观众的情绪达到了高峰。
    出席闭幕式的有中国人民解放军沈阳部队首长曾绍山中将、杜平中将、张竭诚少将、李伯秋少将、李桂林少将，中朝友好协会辽宁省分会副会长唐宏光，沈阳市副市长宋光，以及陪同朝鲜人民军协奏团前来访问演出的我国国防部代表魏传统少将等。
    朝鲜民主主义人民共和国驻长春总领事郑凤珪也参加了闭幕式。
    参加闭幕式的还有前志愿军战斗英雄、烈属、军属代表和省市各文艺团体、各界人民代表二千多人。
    杜平中将在闭幕式上讲话，他代表中华人民共和国国防部和中国人民解放军全体官兵热烈祝贺朝鲜人民军协奏团在访问演出中所取得的巨大成就，向协奏团的全体同志们表示最诚挚的谢意。他说，你们的演出，不仅发展了有着悠久历史的中朝两国人民的文化交流，而且更进一步地巩固和发展了中朝两国人民用鲜血凝成的战斗友谊。
    朝鲜人民军协奏团团长张瑞焕少将对热烈欢迎和盛情接待他们的中国共产党、中国政府和中国人民解放军官兵表示诚挚的感谢。他说，我们在中国的访问演出中，进一步体会到中朝人民的友谊是多么深厚。中朝两国人民的团结，是任何力量也破坏不了的。朝鲜人民和军队将永远地和中国人民及其军队站在一起，为我们共同的事业而斗争到底。
    在欢乐的军乐声和热烈的掌声中，杜平中将代表中国人民解放军总政治部向朝鲜人民军协奏团赠旗。一群女青年文艺工作者向客人们献了鲜花。
    闭幕式在“全世界人民心一条”的雄壮乐曲声中结束。（附图片）
    12月3日，正在我国访问的朝鲜人民军协奏团在沈阳举行访华友好访问演出闭幕式。中国人民解放军杜平中将（前排左起第一人）代表中国人民解放军总政治部向协奏团赠旗。前排左起第二人是协奏团团长张瑞焕少将。　新华社稿（传真照片）
