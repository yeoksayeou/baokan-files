Title: Chairman Liu Meets with Chinese Students and Interns; Wishes Them Good Health, Good Studies, and Good Work in His Speech; Encourages Them to Study Hard and Strengthen Friendship with Soviet and International Students

Author:
Date: 1960-12-03
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, December 1st: Liu Shaoqi, Vice Chairman of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of China, Chairman of the People's Republic of China, and head of the Chinese Party and Government Delegation, met with more than 2,500 Chinese students, interns, and embassy staff studying in Moscow at the Chinese Embassy in the Lenin Hills at 6 p.m. today and spoke to them.

Also present at the embassy to meet with the Chinese students and interns today were members of the Chinese Party and Government Delegation Lu Dingyi, Kang Sheng, Hu Qiaomu, Liao Chengzhi, and Liu Ningyi.

When Chairman Liu Shaoqi and others arrived at the embassy, they were warmly welcomed by the embassy staff and students. Thunderous applause erupted in the hall, and shouts of "Hello, Chairman Liu!" and "Wishing the comrades of the delegation good health!" rose one after another. Everyone shouted "Long live the Communist Party of China!" "Long live the People's Republic of China!" "Long live Chairman Mao!" Chairman Liu Shaoqi waved to them in acknowledgement.

In his speech, Chairman Liu Shaoqi first extended his greetings to the students and interns, wishing them good health, good studies, and good work. Chairman Liu Shaoqi encouraged them to continue to study hard and further strengthen their friendship with Soviet classmates and students from other countries.

Chairman Liu's speech was repeatedly interrupted by long periods of enthusiastic applause.

Members of the Chinese Party and Government Delegation Lu Dingyi and Kang Sheng also spoke to everyone.

After the meeting, the students and interns again shouted slogans: "Long live the Communist Party of China!" "Long live the People's Republic of China!" "Long live Chairman Mao!" and sang "The East is Red" in unison.

### 刘主席同我国留学生实习生会面  在讲话中祝他们身体好、学习好、工作好  勉励他们努力学习，加强同苏联学生及各国留学生的友谊

1960-12-03
第1版()
专栏：

    刘主席同我国留学生实习生会面
    在讲话中祝他们身体好、学习好、工作好
    勉励他们努力学习，加强同苏联学生及各国留学生的友谊
    新华社莫斯科1日电　中共中央副主席、中华人民共和国主席、中国党政代表团团长刘少奇今天下午六点在列宁山上的中国驻苏联大使馆同在莫斯科学习的中国留学生、实习生以及使馆工作人员二千五百多人见了面，并且向他们讲了话。
    今天来到使馆与我国留学生、实习生等见面的还有中国党政代表团团员陆定一、康生、胡乔木、廖承志、刘宁一。
    刘少奇主席等到达使馆时，受到使馆人员和留学生的热烈欢迎。大厅里响起了雷鸣般的掌声，“刘主席好！”“代表团同志们身体健康！”的欢呼声此起彼伏，大家高呼“中国共产党万岁！”“中华人民共和国万岁！”“毛主席万岁！”刘少奇主席向他们挥手致意。
    刘少奇主席在讲话中首先向留学生和实习生问好，祝他们身体好、学习好、工作好。刘少奇主席勉励他们继续努力学习，进一步加强同苏联同学和各国留学生的友谊。
    刘主席的讲话不断为长时间的热烈掌声所打断。
    中国党政代表团团员陆定一和康生也向大家讲了话。
    会见结束后，留学生和实习生再次高呼口号：“中国共产党万岁！”“中华人民共和国万岁！”“毛主席万岁！”并齐声高唱《东方红》。
