Title: Communist Party of Ceylon Holds Congress
Author:
Date: 1960-12-30
Page: 5

    Xinhua News Agency, December 28th, dispatch from Colombo: The Sixth Congress of the Communist Party of Ceylon opened in Colombo on the 26th.
    More than two hundred delegates attended the congress. The congress will discuss the draft outline on political issues (reporter - Party General Secretary Keuneman) and organizational issues (reporter - Party Central Committee member Silva). The congress will also revise the Party Constitution and elect the Party's central organs.
    The Chairman of the Communist Party of Ceylon, Wickremasinghe, who attended the recent meeting of representatives of Communist and Workers' Parties in Moscow, addressed the participants at the opening of the congress.
    The congress received congratulatory letters and telegrams from fraternal parties of various countries.

### 锡兰共产党召开代表大会

1960-12-30
第5版()
专栏：

    锡兰共产党召开代表大会
    新华社28日讯　塔斯社科伦坡讯：锡兰共产党第六次代表大会26日在科伦坡开幕。
    出席大会的有二百多名代表。大会将讨论关于政治问题的提纲草案（报告人——党的总书记克尼曼）和组织问题（报告人——党的中央委员会委员席尔瓦）。大会还将修改党章，并选举党的中央机构。
    曾经出席最近在莫斯科举行的各国共产党和工人党代表会议的锡兰共产党主席魏克马沁格在主持大会开幕时向与会者致词。
    大会收到各国兄弟党的贺信和贺电。
