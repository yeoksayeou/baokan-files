Title: Tens of Thousands Demonstrate in Iraq to Support the Algerian People; Rally Passes Resolution Strongly Condemning the Atrocities of French Colonialists
Author:
Date: 1960-12-22
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, Baghdad, December 21st. Tens of thousands of Iraqi people held a massive demonstration in Baghdad on the 20th to support the just struggle of the Algerian people.

　　Carrying Algerian flags and banners with slogans supporting the Algerian people's anti-imperialist struggle, the demonstrators marched through Rashid Street and other main streets of Baghdad.

　　As the demonstration began, thousands of people chanted Algerian songs to the beat of drums. Demonstrators shouted: "Algeria is free, down with Britain, rotten France and shameless America, down with imperialism!" The demonstrators then held a rally in front of the Ministry of Defense and passed several resolutions. The resolution stated: "We, the people of all walks of life, are holding a rally to support the heroic struggle of the Algerian people" and "strongly condemn the atrocities committed by the French government in Algiers, Oran and other cities in Algeria."

　　The resolution also stated: "We strongly condemn the dirty imperialist war launched by France against the Algerian people for the past six years with the military and political support of the North Atlantic Treaty Organization group headed by the United States."

　　The resolution reiterated: "Eliminate French imperialism, overthrow the aggressive North Atlantic Treaty Organization group headed by the United States—the enemy of the Arab people and the enemy of freedom and peace."

　　According to reports from Beirut, the Lebanese Women's Rights League issued a statement in Beirut on the 20th saying that Lebanese women "strongly condemn the bloody massacres carried out by French imperialists in Algeria with the support of the United States and the North Atlantic Treaty Organization."

　　The statement said, "Together with all Arab women and women all over the world, we demand that Algeria be granted complete independence."

### 伊拉克数万人示威支持阿尔及利亚人民  集会通过决议强烈谴责法国殖民者的暴行

1960-12-22
第6版()
专栏：

    伊拉克数万人示威支持阿尔及利亚人民
    集会通过决议强烈谴责法国殖民者的暴行
    新华社巴格达21日电　伊拉克人民数万人20日在巴格达举行声势浩大的示威，支持阿尔及利亚人民的正义斗争。
    示威者高举着阿尔及利亚的旗帜和上面写着支持阿尔及利亚人民反帝国主义斗争口号的横幅标语，通过拉希德大街和巴格达的其它主要街道。
    当示威游行开始的时候，数以千计的人们随着鼓声高唱阿尔及利亚歌曲。示威群众高呼：“阿尔及利亚是自由的，打倒英国，腐朽的法国和无耻的美国，打倒帝国主义”。示威者然后在国防部前面举行集会，通过若干决议。决议说：“我们各阶层人民举行集会，支持英勇的阿尔及利亚人民的斗争”和“强烈谴责法国政府在阿尔及尔、奥兰和阿尔及利亚的其他城市所犯的暴行”。
    决议又说：“我们强烈谴责法国六年来在以美国为首的北大西洋公约组织集团的军事和政治支持下，对阿尔及利亚人民所发动的肮脏的帝国主义战争”。
    决议重申：“消灭法帝国主义、打倒以美国——阿拉伯人民的敌人以及自由与和平的敌人——为首的侵略性的北大西洋公约组织集团”。
    据来自贝鲁特的消息说，黎巴嫩妇女权利同盟20日在贝鲁特发表声明说，黎巴嫩妇女“强烈谴责法帝国主义者在美国和北大西洋公约组织的支持下在阿尔及利亚进行的血腥屠杀”。
    声明说，“我们同全体阿拉伯妇女和全世界妇女一起，要求给予阿尔及利亚完全的独立。”
