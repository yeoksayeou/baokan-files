Title: Czechoslovak Machine Tool Exhibition Closes in Wuhan
Author:
Date: 1960-12-12
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Wuhan, December 11th: The Czechoslovak Machine Tool Exhibition, which lasted for three weeks, closed here on the 10th. During the exhibition, it received a total of 68,000 visitors. People showed great interest in the sensitivity and precision of the various machine tools on display.

### 捷工作母机展览会在武汉闭幕

1960-12-12
第4版()
专栏：

    捷工作母机展览会在武汉闭幕
    据新华社武汉11日电　历时三周的捷克斯洛伐克工作母机展览会10日在这里闭幕。展览会展出期间，共接待了六万八千多名观众。人们对展出的各式机床的灵敏和精确表现了浓厚的兴趣。
