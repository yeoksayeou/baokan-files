Title: The War Drums Resound
Author: Jean-Marie Kitichie
Date: 1960-12-19
Page: 8

    The War Drums Resound
    〔Congo〕 Jean-Marie Kitichie
    Forward, heroic Congolese people,
    Masters of the free land!
    In your fiery hearts
    The sun forever radiates its light.
    In that dense forest,
    On that mysterious river valley,
    Dong, dong, dong, the war drums thunder,
    Dong, dong, dong, the war drums thunder,
    Dong, dong, dong, the war drums thunder!
    Like that joyous silver glow,
    Sparkling on the rushing waterfall,
    Above my beloved Africa,
    The eagerly awaited dawn glows red!
    Freedom! You are the warrior's cloak,
    What finery is more beautiful than you.
    Freedom! You are the warm sunshine,
    Warmly shining on my face.
    I am but a hunter,
    On this boundless tropical grassland,
    For the sake of the motherland's happy destiny,
    Chasing beasts and jackals.
    I am but a fisherman,
    Using that heavy, shimmering net,
    From the days of the future
    Catching endless treasures.
    Although I am but a shepherd,
    Waiting for me is a series of battles,
    For the motherland's independence and freedom
    We have scheduled victory.
    Exciting hope
    Clings tightly to my heartstrings,
    Just like in my coconut shell
    The bubbling coconut juice sings joyfully.
    Forward, heroic Congolese people,
    Masters of the free land!
    In your fiery hearts
    The sun forever radiates its light.
    In that dense forest,
    On that mysterious river valley,
    Dong, dong, dong, the war drums thunder,
    Dong, dong, dong, the war drums thunder,
    Dong, dong, dong, the war drums thunder!
              〔Translated by He Zhuang〕

### 战鼓咚咚响
让—马里·基奇
1960-12-19
第8版()
专栏：

    战鼓咚咚响
    〔刚果〕　让—马里·基奇
    前进，英雄的刚果人民，
    自由土地的主人！
    在你火热的心里
    太阳永远放射着光芒。
    在那茂密的森林里，
    在那神秘的河谷上，
    咚，咚，咚，战鼓震天响，
    咚，咚，咚，战鼓震天响，
    咚，咚，咚，战鼓震天响！
    像那欢乐的银色光辉，
    闪烁在奔腾的瀑布上，
    在我亲爱的阿非利加上空，
    殷殷期待的朝霞放红光！
    自由啊！你是勇士的披风，
    还有什么华服比你更美丽。
    自由啊！你是和煦的阳光，
    暖暖照在我脸上。
    我不过是个猎人，
    在这无边无际的热带草原上，
    为了祖国幸福的命运，
    追逐着野兽和豺狼。
    我不过是个渔夫，
    用那沉重的闪着鳞光的渔网，
    从那未来的日子里
    捕捉无穷无尽的宝藏。
    尽管我是个牧人，
    等着我的却是连续的战斗，
    为了祖国的独立和自由
    我们安排了胜利的日程。
    激动人心的希望
    紧扣着我的心弦，
    就像在我那椰子瓢里
    起泡的椰汁在欢乐地歌唱。
    前进，英雄的刚果人民，
    自由土地的主人！
    在你火热的心里
    太阳永远放射着光芒
    在那茂密的森林里，
    在那神秘的河谷上，
    咚，咚，咚，战鼓震天响，
    咚，咚，咚，战鼓震天响，
    咚，咚，咚，战鼓震天响！
              〔贺庄译〕
