Title: US Establishes Nuclear Submarine Base in Iceland, Netherlands Confirms Storage of US Nuclear Warheads
Author:
Date: 1960-12-06
Page: 6

    US Establishes Nuclear Submarine Base in Iceland
    Netherlands Confirms Storage of US Nuclear Warheads
    Xinhua News Agency, Reykjavik: According to Icelandic radio broadcasts, the US and Icelandic governments have decided to transfer the US Air Force base in Keflavik, Iceland, to the US Navy.
    Icelandic newspapers have repeatedly revealed that the US plans to establish a nuclear submarine base in Hvalfjord, western Iceland. The realization of this plan will pose a serious threat to the Icelandic people.
    Xinhua News Agency, December 1st, The Hague: According to a Dutch Defense Ministry spokesman's admission yesterday, US "Honest John" missile nuclear warheads are stored in the Netherlands.
    This news confirms that the Netherlands is becoming a military warehouse for the North Atlantic Treaty Organization.

### 美在冰岛建立核潜艇基地  荷兰证实储有美原子弹头

1960-12-06
第6版()
专栏：

    美在冰岛建立核潜艇基地
    荷兰证实储有美原子弹头
    据新华社讯　据塔斯社雷克雅未克讯：据冰岛电台广播，美国和冰岛政府决定把美国在冰岛凯夫拉维克的空军基地转交给美国海军。
    冰岛报纸在这方面曾一再透露，美国计划在冰岛西部的赫瓦耳港建立原子潜艇基地。这个计划的实现将是对冰岛人民的严重威胁。
    新华社1日讯　海牙消息：据荷兰国防部发言人昨天承认，在荷兰存储着美国“诚实的约翰”式导弹的原子弹头。
    这一消息证实，荷兰正在变成北大西洋集团的军事仓库。
