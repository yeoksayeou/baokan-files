Title: Prince Sihanouk Arrives in Our Country; Grand Entry Ceremony Held at Jining Station; Vice Premier Ulanhu and Others Make a Special Trip to Welcome and Accompany Him to Beijing
Author:
Date: 1960-12-15
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Jining, December 14th. At the invitation of Liu Shaoqi, Chairman of the People's Republic of China, and Zhou Enlai, Premier of the State Council, the Head of State of Cambodia, Prince Norodom Sihanouk, and other Cambodian distinguished guests, who are on a state visit to our country, arrived in Jining City, Inner Mongolia Autonomous Region, today by special train from Ulaanbaatar, the capital of Mongolia. A grand entry welcome ceremony was held at the city's railway station. The distinguished guests were warmly welcomed by Ulanhu, Vice Premier of the State Council and Chairman of the Inner Mongolia Autonomous Region, and Ji Pengfei, Vice Minister of Foreign Affairs, who made a special trip to Jining.

Accompanying Prince Sihanouk on the visit were His Excellency Prince Penn Nouth, Vice-Chairman of the High Council of the Throne of Cambodia, and His Excellency Pho Proeung, Prime Minister of the Royal Government of Cambodia, and First Deputy Prime Minister Nhiek Tioulong, who were invited by Zhou Enlai, Premier of the State Council of the People's Republic of China, and Vice Premier Chen Yi. The distinguished guests arrived in Jining today by the same train after visiting Czechoslovakia, the Soviet Union, and Mongolia.

Today, the Jining Railway Station was decorated like a festival. A huge red banner reading "Welcome His Royal Highness Prince Norodom Sihanouk, Head of State of Cambodia, and All Cambodian Distinguished Guests" was hung high on the platform. People of all ethnic groups dressed in their festive best came to the station. With great enthusiasm, they braved the severe cold of minus seventeen degrees Celsius, stood in the biting wind, and warmly welcomed Prince Sihanouk and other Cambodian distinguished guests from the friendly neighboring country.

At 5:30 p.m., the special train with the flags of China and Cambodia arrived in Jining. Amidst enthusiastic cheers, Prince Sihanouk and his entourage stepped off the train with smiles on their faces. Vice Premier Ulanhu and Vice Minister Ji Pengfei stepped forward and warmly shook hands with Prince Sihanouk and other distinguished guests, expressing their warm welcome. At this time, the welcoming crowd burst into warm applause, constantly shouting in Mongolian and Chinese: "Welcome Prince Sihanouk!" "Welcome Cambodian distinguished guests!" "Long live the friendship between the Chinese and Cambodian people!"

Prince Sihanouk's wife and his daughter, Princess Bouthumala Norodom, some senior Cambodian officials, and some artists from the Royal Ballet also arrived in Jining by the same train today.

After the band played the national anthems of Cambodia and China, Prince Sihanouk, accompanied by Vice Premier Ulanhu, reviewed the guard of honor. A group of children presented flowers to Prince Sihanouk and other distinguished guests.

Also coming to Jining from Beijing to welcome the distinguished guests today were: Wang Youping, Chinese Ambassador to Cambodia, and his wife; and Leng Ngeth, Cambodian Ambassador to China.

Wang Zaitian and Peng Sike, Vice Chairmen of the Inner Mongolia Autonomous Region, Kong Fei, Major General and Deputy Commander of the Inner Mongolia Military Region of the Chinese People's Liberation Army, and Wu Tao, Major General and Deputy Political Commissar, came from Hohhot to welcome the distinguished guests. They warmly shook hands and greeted the distinguished guests at the station.

Also at the Jining Railway Station to welcome the distinguished guests were Li Wenjing, League Leader of Ulanqab League, and Qi Baoshan, Mayor of Jining City, heads of various people's organizations of the Inner Mongolia Autonomous Region, other heads of Ulanqab League and Jining City, as well as people from all walks of life in the local area.

Yu Peiwu, Director of the Protocol Department of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs, and others made a special trip from Beijing to Erenhot on the Sino-Mongolian border earlier to welcome the distinguished guests and accompanied them to Jining.

After a short rest at Jining Railway Station, Prince Sihanouk and his entourage, accompanied by Vice Premier Ulanhu, Vice Minister Ji Pengfei, Ambassador Wang Youping and his wife, Director Yu Peiwu, and others, continued their journey to Beijing by special train at 7 p.m.

### 西哈努克亲王到达我国  集宁车站上举行了隆重的入境欢迎仪式  乌兰夫副总理等专程前往迎接陪同来京

1960-12-15
第1版()
专栏：

    西哈努克亲王到达我国
    集宁车站上举行了隆重的入境欢迎仪式
    乌兰夫副总理等专程前往迎接陪同来京
    新华社集宁14日电　应中华人民共和国主席刘少奇和国务院总理周恩来的邀请，前来我国进行国事访问的柬埔寨国家元首诺罗敦·西哈努克亲王殿下及其他柬埔寨贵宾，今天从蒙古首都乌兰巴托乘专车到达内蒙古自治区的集宁市。在这个城市的车站上举行了隆重的入境欢迎仪式。贵宾们受到专程来集宁的国务院副总理、内蒙古自治区主席乌兰夫和外交部副部长姬鹏飞的热烈欢迎。
    同西哈努克亲王一起前来访问的，有应中华人民共和国国务院总理周恩来和副总理陈毅邀请的，柬埔寨王位最高委员会副主席宾努阁下、柬埔寨王国政府首相福·波伦阁下、第一副首相?·刁隆阁下。贵宾们一行都是在访问了捷克斯洛伐克、苏联和蒙古以后今天同车到达集宁的。
    今天，集宁车站装饰得如同节日一般，月台上高悬着“欢迎柬埔寨国家元首诺罗敦·西哈努克亲王殿下和全体柬埔寨贵宾们”红色巨幅标语。欢迎的各族人民盛装来到了车站，他们怀着巨大的热情，冒着零下十七度的严寒，伫立在凛冽的寒风中，热烈地欢迎来自友好邻邦的西哈努克亲王和柬埔寨的其他贵宾们。
    下午五时三十分，插着中柬两国国旗的专车到达集宁。在一片热烈的欢呼声中，西哈努克亲王一行贵宾满面笑容地走下火车，乌兰夫副总理和姬鹏飞副部长迎上前去，同西哈努克亲王及其他贵宾们亲切地握手，表示热烈欢迎。这时，欢迎的人群响起了热烈的掌声，他们用蒙、汉语不断高呼：“欢迎西哈努克亲王！”“欢迎柬埔寨的贵宾们！”“中柬人民友好万岁！”
    西哈努克亲王的夫人和他的女儿索丽娅·伦赛公主、柬埔寨的一些高级官员和皇家芭蕾舞团的部分艺术家们，今天也同车到达集宁。
    乐队高奏柬中两国国歌后，西哈努克亲王由乌兰夫副总理陪同检阅了仪仗队。一群少年儿童向西哈努克亲王以及其他贵宾们献花。
    今天，从北京来集宁迎接贵宾的还有：中国驻柬埔寨大使王幼平和他的夫人；柬埔寨驻中国大使兰·?特。
    内蒙古自治区副主席王再天、朋斯克，中国人民解放军内蒙古军区副司令员孔飞少将和副政委吴涛少将等从呼和浩特赶来欢迎贵宾，他们在车站上同贵宾们亲切地握手问好。
    到集宁车站迎接贵宾的还有，乌兰察布盟盟长李文精和集宁市市长齐保山，内蒙古自治区各人民团体负责人，乌兰察布盟和集宁市的其他负责人，以及当地的各族各界人民。
    外交部礼宾司司长俞沛文等早些时候专程从北京到中蒙边境的二连迎接贵宾，并且陪同贵宾们到达集宁。
    西哈努克亲王一行贵宾在集宁车站稍事休息以后，于下午七时在乌兰夫副总理、姬鹏飞副部长、王幼平大使和夫人、俞沛文司长等陪同下，继续乘专车去北京。
