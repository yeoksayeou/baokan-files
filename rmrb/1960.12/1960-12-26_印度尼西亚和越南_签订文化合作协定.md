Title: Indonesia and Vietnam Sign Cultural Cooperation Agreement
Author:
Date: 1960-12-26
Page: 5
Xinhua News Agency, Jakarta. Indonesia and the Democratic Republic of Vietnam signed a cultural cooperation agreement here on the 19th. Indonesian Foreign Minister Subandrio and the head of the Vietnamese cultural delegation currently visiting here, Vietnamese Minister of Culture Hoang Minh Giam, signed the agreement on behalf of their respective governments.

At today's signing ceremony, Indonesian Minister of Culture and Education Prijono and Hoang Minh Giam also signed the 1961 executive plan for the cultural cooperation agreement on behalf of their respective governments.

### 印度尼西亚和越南  签订文化合作协定

1960-12-26
第5版()
专栏：

    印度尼西亚和越南
    签订文化合作协定
    新华社雅加达电　印度尼西亚和越南民主共和国19日在这里签订了文化合作协定。印度尼西亚外交部长苏班德里约和目前在这里访问的越南文化代表团团长、越南文化部长黄明鉴分别代表两国政府在协定上签字。
    在今天的签字仪式上，印度尼西亚文化和教育部长普里约诺和黄明鉴还分别代表他们各自的政府签署了文化合作协定1961年执行计划。
