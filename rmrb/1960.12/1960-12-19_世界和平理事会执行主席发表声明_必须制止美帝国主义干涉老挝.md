Title: World Peace Council Executive Chairman Issues Statement: U.S. Imperialist Intervention in Laos Must Be Stopped
Author:
Date: 1960-12-19
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, Hanoi, December 17th: The Vietnam News Agency reported on December 17th that the Executive Chairman of the Presidium of the World Peace Council, Bernal, issued a statement on December 15th condemning U.S. imperialist overt intervention in Laos.

The statement condemned U.S. imperialism for providing a large number of weapons to the Phoumi Nosavan rebel group and for the aggressive Southeast Asia Treaty Organization's conspiracy to intervene in Laos.

The statement said that all these activities are in complete violation of the 1954 Geneva Agreements on Indochina, which stipulate the prohibition of any foreign interference in the internal affairs of Laos.

The statement called for urgent measures to ensure the correct implementation of the Geneva Agreements and to stop U.S. interference in Laos, which endangers peace in Southeast Asia and the world.

### 世界和平理事会执行主席发表声明  必须制止美帝国主义干涉老挝

1960-12-19
第5版()
专栏：

    世界和平理事会执行主席发表声明
    必须制止美帝国主义干涉老挝
    新华社河内17日电　据越南通讯社17日报道，世界和平理事会主席团执行主席贝尔纳15日发表声明，谴责美帝国主义公开干涉老挝。
    声明谴责美帝国主义向富米·诺萨万叛乱集团大量提供武器以及侵略性的东南亚条约组织正在阴谋干涉老挝。
    声明说，所有这些活动都是完全违反规定禁止外国对老挝内政进行任何干涉的1954年关于印度支那的日内瓦协议的。
    声明要求采取紧急措施来保证日内瓦协议的正确执行，制止美国干涉老挝、危害东南亚的和世界的和平。
