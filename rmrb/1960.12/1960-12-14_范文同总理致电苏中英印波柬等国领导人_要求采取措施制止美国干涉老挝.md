Title: Premier Pham Van Dong Sends Telegrams to Leaders of the Soviet Union, China, Britain, India, Poland, and Cambodia, Requesting Measures to Stop U.S. Interference in Laos
Author:
Date: 1960-12-14
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, Hanoi, December 12th: According to Vietnam News Agency, Premier Pham Van Dong of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam yesterday requested that the governments of those countries responsible for maintaining peace in Laos, as well as many Southeast Asian countries, take urgent and effective measures to stop foreign, particularly American, interference in Laos' internal affairs.

The Premier of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam sent telegrams to the Prime Ministers of the Soviet Union, China, Britain, India, Poland, Canada, France, Burma, Cambodia, Indonesia, Ceylon, and Singapore.

The telegram stated that the Government of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam is deeply disturbed by the recent developments in Laos. The telegram said that these developments exposed the blatant interference of the United States, Thailand, and the Ngo Dinh Diem elements and Chiang Kai-shek cliques in Laos, who provided military assistance to the Phoumi Nosavan traitorous group to carry out rebellion against the government of Prince Souvanna Phouma.

Premier Pham Van Dong's telegram emphasized: "On behalf of the Government of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam, as a signatory to the Geneva Agreements on Laos, I am issuing this urgent appeal to you to take all urgent and effective measures to stop foreign, particularly American, interference, effectively support the legitimate government of Prince Souvanna Phouma, ensure respect for the Geneva Agreements, and safeguard security and peace in Indochina and Southeast Asia."

Xinhua News Agency, Hanoi, December 13th: Premier Pham Van Dong of the Government of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam today sent a telegram to the Royal Government of Laos stating that the Government of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam sincerely pursues a policy of peace and friendship towards all nations, especially its neighbors, and continues to regard the government headed by Prince Souvanna Phouma as the only legitimate government of the Kingdom of Laos. The Government of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam strongly condemns the conspiracy of U.S. imperialism to create a puppet government headed by Boun Oum and Phoumi.

The telegram stated, "The establishment of this illegal government, which serves as a tool of U.S. imperialism, aims to intensify the civil war in Laos and disregard peace in Indochina and Southeast Asia. We firmly believe that the Lao people, united and supported by peace-loving people all over the world, will be able to overcome the current difficulties, smash the interference of U.S. imperialism, and safeguard the independence, peace, and neutrality of the Kingdom of Laos in the spirit of the Geneva Agreements.

The telegram expressed support for the heroic struggle of the Lao people for the interests of the Kingdom, for the friendly relations between Vietnam and Laos, and for the interests of peace in the region.

### 范文同总理致电苏中英印波柬等国领导人  要求采取措施制止美国干涉老挝

1960-12-14
第6版()
专栏：

    范文同总理致电苏中英印波柬等国领导人
    要求采取措施制止美国干涉老挝
    新华社河内12日电　据越南通讯社报道：越南民主共和国总理范文同昨天要求对维护老挝和平负有责任的那些国家以及很多东南亚国家的政府采取紧急的和有效的措施，来制止外国、特别是美国对老挝内政的干涉。
    越南民主共和国总理向苏联、中国、英国、印度、波兰、加拿大、法国、缅甸、柬埔寨、印度尼西亚、锡兰和新加坡的总理发出了电报。
    这封电报说，越南民主共和国政府对老挝最近的发展深感不安。电报说，这些事态发展暴露了美国、泰国以及在老挝的吴庭艳分子和蒋介石分子的露骨的干涉，他们对富米·诺萨万叛国集团提供军事援助，用来进行反对梭发那·富马亲王的政府的叛乱。
    范文同总理的电报着重指出：“我代表作为关于老挝的日内瓦协议的签字国的越南民主共和国政府，向你们发出这个紧迫的呼吁，以便采取一切紧急的和有效的措施，来制止外国、特别是美国的干涉，有效地支持梭发那·富马亲王的合法政府，确保尊重日内瓦协议，并保障印度支那和东南亚的安全与和平。”
    新华社河内13日电　越南民主共和国政府总理范文同今天打电报给老挝王国政府说，越南民主共和国政府真诚奉行对所有各个民族、首先是各邻国和平和友好政策，继续把以梭发那·富马亲王为首的政府看作是老挝王国唯一合法的政府，越南民主共和国政府强烈谴责美帝国主义正在制造以文翁—富米为首的傀儡政府的阴谋。
    电报中说，“成立这个充当美帝国主义的工具的不合法的政府，其目的在于加剧老挝内战，无视印度支那和东南亚的和平。我们坚信，老挝人民团结一致，并得到全世界爱好和平人民的支持，将能够克服目前的困难，粉碎美帝国主义的干涉，根据日内瓦协议的精神而维护老挝王国的独立、和平和中立。
    电报表示支持老挝人民为王国的利益，为越老两国的友好关系，为这一地区的和平利益而进行的英勇斗争。
