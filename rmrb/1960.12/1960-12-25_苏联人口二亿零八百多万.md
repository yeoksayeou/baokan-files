Title: Soviet Population Exceeds 208 Million
Author: 
Date: 1960-12-25
Page: 6

　　Soviet Population Exceeds 208 Million
　　Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, December 24th: According to supplementary materials on the results of the January 15, 1959 census published by the Soviet Central Statistical Bureau, the total population of the Soviet Union is 208,827,000.

### 苏联人口二亿零八百多万

1960-12-25
第6版()
专栏：

    苏联人口二亿零八百多万
    新华社24日讯　据塔斯社莫斯科讯：根据苏联中央统计局发表的对1959年1月15日人口调查结果的补充材料，苏联全国人口总数为二亿零八百八十二万七千人。
