Title: Prince Sihanouk Departs Guangzhou for Home; Vice Premier Chen Yi and Thousands from Guangdong Province and Guangzhou Municipality See Him Off at the Airport
Author: 
Date: 1960-12-27
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, Guangzhou, December 26th. Cambodian Head of State, Prince Norodom Sihanouk, and his wife and other distinguished guests, concluded their state visit to our country and left Guangzhou by special plane this morning, laden with the fruitful results of the friendship between the Chinese and Cambodian people, to return home.

The distinguished guests were seen off at the airport by Vice Premier Chen Yi and his wife, Guangdong Province Governor Chen Yu and his wife, Guangzhou Mayor Zeng Sheng, and thousands of people from all walks of life. A grand send-off ceremony was held at the airport.

Returning home with Prince Sihanouk were: Deputy Chairman of the Cambodian Throne Supreme Council, Penn Nouth, Prime Minister Pho Proeung, First Deputy Prime Minister Nhiek Tioulong, as well as Prince Sihanouk’s daughter, Princess Soriya Lonnsy, some senior Cambodian officials, and performers from the Cambodian Royal Ballet Troupe.

During their visit to our country, Prince Sihanouk and his party of distinguished guests were warmly welcomed by the Chinese people everywhere. As they left our country today, the people of Guangzhou, holding flowers, banners, Chinese and Cambodian national flags, and portraits of Prince Sihanouk, gathered at Baiyun Airport early in the morning to see off the distinguished guests.

An honor guard was lined up at the airport. The national flags of China and Cambodia fluttered in the morning breeze. When the distinguished guests arrived by car at 6:40 a.m., a burst of cheers erupted at the airport.

After the band played the national anthems of Cambodia and China, Prince Sihanouk, accompanied by Deputy Chairman Penn Nouth, Prime Minister Pho Proeung, First Deputy Prime Minister Nhiek Tioulong, Vice Premier Chen Yi, and Governor Chen Yu, reviewed the honor guard.

Prince Sihanouk and other distinguished guests walked to the front of the crowd of well-wishers to meet and warmly bid farewell to the people from all walks of life. At this time, people shouted slogans: “Wishing the distinguished Cambodian guests a safe journey!” “May the friendship between the Chinese and Cambodian people last forever!” Young women waved handkerchiefs and scarves, constantly running out of the crowd to offer flowers to the distinguished guests. Prince Sihanouk and other distinguished guests frequently waved to the crowd and put their palms together to express their reluctance to part.

Vice Premier Chen Yi and his wife, Governor Chen Yu and his wife, Mayor Zeng Sheng, and Major General Wen Niansheng, commander of the People's Liberation Army troops stationed in Guangzhou, escorted the distinguished guests to the side of the plane and shook hands with them one by one to say goodbye. Prince Sihanouk warmly said, holding Vice Premier Chen Yi’s hand: "Thank you for accompanying us on our visit. Thank you to the Chinese people for their welcome. Please believe that the Cambodian people will always stand with the Chinese people." Vice Premier Chen Yi expressed his gratitude to His Royal Highness for bringing friendship to the Chinese people and for the honor bestowed upon him, and warmly wished His Royal Highness a safe journey. As Prince Sihanouk boarded the plane and waved goodbye to the crowd of well-wishers at the airport, cheers and the sound of gongs and drums rang out throughout the field. The special plane carrying Prince Sihanouk took off at exactly seven o'clock.

Cambodian Ambassador to China, Leng Ngeth, accompanied the distinguished guests on their return home today.

Also at the airport to see off the distinguished guests were: Chinese Ambassador to Cambodia, Wang Youping, and his wife, and Yu Peiwen, Director of the Protocol Department of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs.

Also at the airport to see off were: Guangdong Province Vice Governor Lin Qiangyun, Vice Chairman of the Guangdong Provincial Committee of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference Zhang Boquan, responsible persons of various departments and organizations of Guangdong Province and Guangzhou City, local organizations of various democratic parties, and people's organizations, as well as the council members of the Sino-Cambodian Friendship Association in Guangzhou. Wang Kunlun, head of the Chinese Chaozhou Opera Troupe, and all members of the troupe, who recently returned from a visit to Cambodia, also came to the airport to see them off.

Prince Norodom Naradipo and Prince Norodom Kemarunuak, the two sons of Prince Sihanouk who are studying in Beijing and accompanied the distinguished guests on their visit to various places, also came to the airport to see them off.

Also at the airport to see them off were consuls from various countries stationed in Guangzhou, as well as Cambodian interns.

### 西哈努克亲王离广州回国  陈毅副总理和广东省、广州市各界数千人到机场欢送

1960-12-27
第1版()
专栏：

    西哈努克亲王离广州回国
    陈毅副总理和广东省、广州市各界数千人到机场欢送
    新华社广州26日电　柬埔寨国家元首诺罗敦·西哈努克亲王和他的夫人等一行贵宾，结束了在我国的国事访问，今天上午满载着中柬两国人民友谊的丰硕果实，乘专机离开广州回国。
    贵宾们在机场上受到了陈毅副总理和夫人，广东省省长陈郁和夫人，广州市市长曾生，及各界人民数千人的欢送。机场上举行了隆重的欢送仪式。
    随同西哈努克亲王一起回国的有：柬埔寨王位最高委员会副主席宾努，首相福·波伦，第一副首相涅·刁龙，以及西哈努克亲王的女儿索丽娅·伦赛公主，柬埔寨的一些高级官员，柬埔寨皇家舞蹈团的演员。
    西哈努克亲王一行贵宾在访问我国期间，到处受到中国人民亲切热烈的欢迎。今天离开我国的时候，广州市人民拿着鲜花、标语、中柬两国国旗和西哈努克亲王的肖像，一大早便汇集到白云机场欢送贵宾。
    机场上排列着仪仗队。中柬两国的国旗在晨风中飘扬。当贵宾们乘车在早晨六时四十分到达的时候，机场上发出了一片欢呼声。
    音乐队奏过柬中两国国歌以后，西哈努克亲王和宾努副主席、福·波伦首相、涅·刁龙第一副首相，由陈毅副总理和陈郁省长陪同，检阅了仪仗队。
    西哈努克亲王和其他贵宾们走到欢送群众队伍的前面，同各界人民会面并热情地告别。这时，人们高呼口号：“祝柬埔寨贵宾们一路平安！”“祝中国和柬埔寨人民的友谊万古长青！”女青年挥舞着手帕和头巾，不断地从人群中跑出来，把鲜花献给贵宾。西哈努克亲王等贵宾频频向群众挥手、合十，表示惜别之意。
    陈毅副总理和夫人，陈郁省长和夫人，曾生市长，人民解放军驻广州部队首长文年生中将等送贵宾们到机旁，和贵宾们一一握手话别。西哈努克亲王握着陈毅副总理的手热情地说：“感谢您一路陪同我们参观访问。感谢中国人民对我们的欢迎。请您相信，柬埔寨人民永远同中国人民站在一起。”陈毅副总理表示感谢亲王殿下给中国人民带来了友谊和给予他的荣誉，并热情地祝亲王殿下一路平安。当西哈努克亲王登上飞机向机场上的欢送群众挥手告别时，全场响起了一片欢呼声和锣鼓声。西哈努克亲王乘坐的专机，于七时正起飞。
    柬埔寨驻华大使兰·涅特今天陪同贵宾返国。
    到机场欢送贵宾的还有：我国驻柬埔寨大使王幼平和夫人，外交部礼宾司司长俞沛文。
    到机场欢送的还有：广东省副省长林锵云，政协广东省委员会副主席张?村，广东省和广州市各机关、各民主党派地方组织、各人民团体的负责人，以及中柬友好协会在广州的理事。不久前访问柬埔寨回国的中国潮剧团团长王昆仑和全体团员也到机场欢送。
    这次随同贵宾到各地访问的在北京学习的西哈努克亲王的二位王子诺罗敦·纳拉迪波和诺罗敦·克马努拉克，都到机场欢送。
    到机场欢送的还有各国驻广州的领事，以及柬埔寨的实习生。（附图片）
