Title: Chairman Mao Accompanies President Ho to Watch a Performance, Le Duan and Other Comrades, Accompanied by Bo Yibo and Others, Visit the Beijing Railway Station
Author:
Date: 1960-12-05
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, 4th December: Chairman Ho Chi Minh, accompanied by Chairman Mao Zedong and other Party and state leaders including Zhou Enlai, Zhu De, and Deng Xiaoping, attended an evening performance held by the Ministry of Culture at Huairentang today. They enjoyed brilliant programs of Peking Opera, music, dance, acrobatics and other performances by Chinese artists.

    Today's evening party was held to welcome Chairman Ho Chi Minh.

    Also invited to attend the evening party were Le Duan, First Secretary of the Central Committee of the Vietnam Workers' Party; Nguyen Chi Thanh and Hoang Van Hoan, members of the Central Political Bureau; and Tran Quang Huy, alternate member of the Central Committee. Vietnamese Ambassador to China, Tran Tu Binh, was also invited to attend the evening party.

    Other Party and state leaders attending the evening party included Peng Zhen, Chen Yi, Li Fuchun, He Long, Li Xiannian, Tan Zhenlin, Kang Sheng, Nie Rongzhen, Luo Ruiqing, and Hu Qiaomu.

    After the performance, Chairman Ho Chi Minh, accompanied by Chairman Mao Zedong and others, went on stage, presented flower baskets to the performers, and shook hands with them cordially.

    This afternoon, Le Duan, First Secretary of the Central Committee of the Vietnam Workers' Party, and Nguyen Chi Thanh, member of the Central Political Bureau, accompanied by Bo Yibo, alternate member of the Political Bureau of the CPC Central Committee and Vice Premier of the State Council, and Wu Xiuquan, member of the CPC Central Committee, visited the Beijing Railway Station. Comrade Nguyen Chi Thanh also visited the Great Hall of the People. (Photo attached)
    Right: After the performance, Chairman Ho Chi Minh, Chairman Mao Zedong, and others went on stage to shake hands with the performers.
　　　　Xinhua News Agency reporter Du Xiuxian

### 毛主席陪同胡主席观看文艺演出  黎笋等同志由薄一波等陪同参观了北京车站

1960-12-05
第1版()
专栏：

    毛主席陪同胡主席观看文艺演出
    黎笋等同志由薄一波等陪同参观了北京车站
    新华社4日讯　胡志明主席今天由毛泽东主席和党和国家的其他领导人周恩来、朱德、邓小平等陪同，出席了文化部在怀仁堂举行的文艺晚会，欣赏了中国文艺工作者演出的京剧、音乐、舞蹈、杂技等精采节目。
    今天的晚会是为欢迎胡志明主席而举行的。
    应邀出席晚会的，还有越南劳动党中央委员会第一书记黎笋，中央政治局委员阮志清、黄文欢，中央候补委员陈光辉等。越南驻中国大使陈子平也应邀出席了晚会。
    出席晚会的党和国家的其他领导人，还有彭真、陈毅、李富春、贺龙、李先念、谭震林、康生、聂荣臻、罗瑞卿、胡乔木等。
    文艺演出结束以后，胡志明主席由毛泽东主席等陪同走上舞台，向演员们赠花篮，并且同演员们亲切握手。
    今天下午，越南劳动党中央委员会第一书记黎笋和中央政治局委员阮志清，在中共中央政治局候补委员、国务院副总理薄一波和中共中央委员伍修权的陪同下，参观了北京车站。阮志清同志还参观了人民大会堂。（附图片）
    右图：演出结束后，胡志明主席、毛泽东主席等上台和演员握手
　　　　新华社记者　杜修贤摄
