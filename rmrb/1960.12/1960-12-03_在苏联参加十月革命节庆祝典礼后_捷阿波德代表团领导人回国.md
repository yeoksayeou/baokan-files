Title: Leaders of Czechoslovakian Delegation Return Home After Attending October Revolution Celebration in Soviet Union
Author: 
Date: 1960-12-03
Page: 5
Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, December 2nd: According to TASS, the leaders of the Czechoslovak, Albanian, Polish, and German Democratic Republic party and government delegations, who were invited to Moscow to attend the 43rd anniversary celebrations of the Great October Socialist Revolution, have successively left Moscow for home.

    Władysław Gomułka, First Secretary of the Central Committee of the Polish United Workers' Party and head of the Polish party and government delegation, and Józef Cyrankiewicz, Chairman of the Council of Ministers of Poland and member of the Political Bureau of the Central Committee of the United Workers' Party, left Moscow for home on December 1st.

    The German Democratic Republic party and government delegation, headed by Walter Ulbricht, First Secretary of the Central Committee of the Socialist United Party of Germany and Chairman of the Council of State of the German Democratic Republic, left Moscow for home on December 2nd.

  Prior to this, Enver Hoxha, head of the Albanian party and government delegation and First Secretary of the Central Committee of the Albanian Party of Labour, and delegation member Mehmet Shehu, Chairman of the Council of Ministers of Albania, as well as Antonín Novotný, head of the Czechoslovak party and government delegation, First Secretary of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of Czechoslovakia and President of the Republic, had left Moscow for home at the end of November.

### 在苏联参加十月革命节庆祝典礼后  捷阿波德代表团领导人回国

1960-12-03
第5版()
专栏：

    在苏联参加十月革命节庆祝典礼后
    捷阿波德代表团领导人回国
    新华社2日讯　据塔斯社莫斯科讯：应邀到莫斯科参加十月社会主义革命四十三周年庆祝典礼的捷克斯洛伐克、阿尔巴尼亚、波兰和德意志民主共和国党政代表团领导人已先后离开莫斯科回国。
    波兰党政代表团团长、波兰统一工人党中央委员会第一书记哥穆尔卡和波兰部长会议主席、统一工人党中央政治局委员西伦凯维兹，已于12月1日离开莫斯科回国。
    以德国统一社会党中央委员会第一书记、德意志民主共和国国务委员会主席乌布利希为首的德意志民主共和国党政代表团已于12月2日离莫斯科回国。
  在这以前，阿尔巴尼亚党政代表团团长、阿尔巴尼亚劳动党中央委员会第一书记霍查和代表团团员、阿尔巴尼亚部长会议主席谢胡，以及捷克斯洛伐克党政代表团团长、捷克斯洛伐克共产党中央委员会第一书记、共和国总统诺沃提尼，已经分别于11月底离开莫斯科回国。
