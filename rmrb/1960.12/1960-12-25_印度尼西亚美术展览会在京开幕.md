Title: Indonesian Art Exhibition Opens in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1960-12-25
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, December 24th: An Indonesian Art Exhibition, jointly sponsored by the China-Indonesia Friendship Association and the Chinese Artists Association, opened this afternoon at the Cultural Palace of the Working People in the capital.

Hu Yuzhi, Vice President of the China-Indonesia Friendship Association, Liu Kaiqu, Vice Chairman of the Chinese Artists Association, and Ding Xilin, Vice President of the Chinese People's Association for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries, attended the opening ceremony.

The Indonesian Artists Delegation visiting our country, Chargé d'Affaires ad interim of the Indonesian Embassy in China, Suleiman, and First Secretary and Cultural Attaché, Gifu Dadasoeno, were invited to attend the opening ceremony.

Hu Yuzhi delivered the opening speech, thanking the Indonesian Artists Delegation for bringing the rich and colorful works of Indonesian artists, and expressed his belief that through the exhibition of these works, the traditional friendship between the two peoples will be further promoted.

Liu Kaiqu spoke at the opening ceremony, welcoming the Indonesian Artists Delegation visiting our country on behalf of the China-Indonesia Friendship Association and the Chinese Artists Association, and congratulating the exhibition of Indonesian paintings. He said that the people of China and Indonesia have a long history of cultural and friendly relations. We are very pleased that the Indonesian Art Exhibition is opening in Beijing at the time when the implementation measures for the treaty between the two governments on the issue of dual nationality have been announced. The visit of the Indonesian Artists Delegation and this exhibition will further promote mutual understanding and friendship between our two peoples.

Suroso, the head of the Indonesian Artists Delegation, and Gifu Dadasoeno, First Secretary and Cultural Attaché of the Indonesian Embassy in China, also spoke at the opening ceremony, congratulating the opening of the Indonesian Art Exhibition. On behalf of the Indonesian Artists Delegation, Suroso conveyed the friendly greetings of Indonesian artists, cultural workers, and the Indonesian people to Chinese artists, cultural workers, and the Chinese people. He said: "When it comes to consolidating the friendship between the people of our two countries, we welcome the recent important event, which is the approval by the two governments of the implementation measures for the treaty on the issue of dual nationality. This event means a further close relationship between the people of our two countries."

The Indonesian Art Exhibition features nearly 300 oil paintings, watercolors, cartoons, posters, and woodcuts created by more than 80 Indonesian painters, reflecting the development and new achievements of Indonesian painting art.

Also attending the opening ceremony were figures from the capital's literary and art circles such as Hua Junwu, Wang Chaowen, Chen Banding, Li Hua, Gu Yuan, Lin Lin, and Du Zhanchao.

### 印度尼西亚美术展览会在京开幕

1960-12-25
第4版()
专栏：

    印度尼西亚美术展览会在京开幕
    新华社24日讯　中国印度尼西亚友好协会和中国美术家协会联合举办的印度尼西亚美术展览会，今天下午在首都劳动人民文化宫开幕。
    中国印度尼西亚友好协会副会长胡愈之、中国美术家协会副主席刘开渠、中国人民对外文化协会副会长丁西林都参加了开幕式。
    来我国访问的印度尼西亚美术家代表团，印度尼西亚驻中国大使馆临时代办苏雷曼、一等秘书兼文化专员吉甫多达梭诺，应邀参加了开幕式。
    胡愈之致开幕词，他感谢印度尼西亚美术家代表团带来了印度尼西亚美术家们丰富多采的作品，并表示相信通过这些作品的展览，将会进一步促进两国人民之间的传统友谊。
    刘开渠在开幕式上讲话，他代表中国印度尼西亚友好协会和中国美术家协会，对来我国访问的印度尼西亚美术家代表团表示欢迎，并祝贺印度尼西亚绘画艺术作品的展出。他说，中国和印度尼西亚两国人民有着悠久的文化友好关系。我们很高兴，正当两国政府关于双重国籍问题的条约的实施办法公布的时候，印度尼西亚美术展览会在北京开幕。通过印度尼西亚美术家代表团的访问和这次展览，将进一步促进我们两国人民的相互了解和友谊。
    印度尼西亚美术家代表团团长苏罗莫和印度尼西亚驻中国大使馆一等秘书兼文化专员吉甫多达梭诺也在开幕式上讲了话，他们祝贺印度尼西亚美术展览会的开幕。苏罗莫以印度尼西亚美术家代表团的名义，向中国美术家、文化工作者和中国人民转达印度尼西亚美术家、文化工作者和印度尼西亚人民友好的问候。他说：“在谈到巩固两国人民之间的友谊时，我们对最近的重要事件表示欢迎，这就是两国政府批准了关于双重国籍问题的条约的实施办法。这件事意味着更进一步地密切了我们两国人民之间的关系。”
    印度尼西亚美术展览会展出了包括印度尼西亚八十多位画家创作的近三百幅油画、水彩画、漫画、招贴画和木刻，反映了印度尼西亚绘画艺术的发展和新的成就。
    参加展览会开幕式的，还有华君武、王朝闻、陈半丁、李桦、古元、林林、杜展潮等首都文艺界人士。
