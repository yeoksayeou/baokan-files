Title: Vice Premier Xi Zhongxun Receives Brazilian Teixeira Couple
Author:
Date: 1960-12-21
Page: 4

    Xinhua News Agency, December 20th — Vice Premier Xi Zhongxun received the Brazilian Congressman and newly elected Governor of Goiás, Mauro Borges Teixeira, and his wife, who are currently visiting our country, this morning, and had a friendly conversation with them.
    Present at the reception were Hu Yuzhi, Vice President of the Chinese People's Institute of Foreign Affairs, and Wu Xiaoda, Deputy Secretary-General. Wang Disan, Deputy Director of the Department of American and Australian Affairs of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs, was also present at the reception. (Photo attached)
    Vice Premier of the State Council, Xi Zhongxun, received Brazilian Congressman and newly elected Governor of Goiás, Mauro Borges Teixeira, and his wife on December 20th. The picture shows Vice Premier Xi Zhongxun shaking hands warmly with Mauro Borges Teixeira. Xinhua News Agency reporter Lü Houmin.

### 习仲勋副总理  接见巴西蒂希拉夫妇

1960-12-21
第4版()
专栏：

    习仲勋副总理
    接见巴西蒂希拉夫妇
    新华社20日讯　习仲勋副总理今天上午接见了正在我国访问的巴西众议员、果亚斯州新当选的州长摩洛·博热斯·蒂希拉及夫人，并且同他们进行了友好的谈话。
    接见时在座的有中国人民外交学会副会长胡愈之和副秘书长吴晓达。接见时外交部美澳司副司长王第三也在座。（附图片）
