Title: Burmese Government Withdraws Six Officers from Congo
Author:
Date: 1960-12-21
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, Rangoon, December 20th: According to a report in today's *The Guardian*, the Burmese government has decided to withdraw the six Burmese officers currently serving in the Congo "United Nations Force" from the Congo.

Prime Minister U Nu confirmed this at a press conference held this morning.

The Burmese military team, led by Lieutenant Colonel Ye Gwon, left Rangoon for the Congo on August 7th.

### 缅甸政府从刚果撤回六名军官

1960-12-21
第6版()
专栏：

    缅甸政府从刚果撤回六名军官
    新华社仰光20日电　据《卫报》今天报道，缅甸政府已决定从刚果撤出目前在刚果“联合国军”中的六名缅甸军官。
    吴努总理在今天上午举行的记者招待会上证实了这件事。
    由耶贡中校率领的缅甸军事小组是在8月7日离开仰光前往刚果的。
