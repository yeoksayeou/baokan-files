Title: Shen Jian Lays Wreath at José Martí Monument
Author:
Date: 1960-12-31
Page: 5

    Xinhua News Agency, Havana, December 29th: Chinese Ambassador to Cuba, Shen Jian, laid a wreath at the monument to Cuban national hero José Martí here on the afternoon of the 28th.
    Present at the wreath-laying ceremony were Manuel Yépez, Director of the Protocol Department of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs, and Captain Vélez, Military Aide to the President.
    Hundreds of people gathered in Central Park, applauding warmly to pay their respects to the Chinese Ambassador.

### 申健向何塞·马蒂纪念碑献花圈

1960-12-31
第5版()
专栏：

    申健向何塞·马蒂纪念碑献花圈
    新华社哈瓦那29日电　中国驻古巴大使申健28日下午在这里向古巴民族英雄何塞·马蒂纪念碑献花圈。
    献花圈时在场的有外交部礼宾司司长曼努埃尔·耶佩斯和总统军事副官贝莱斯上尉。
    数以百计的人聚集在中央公园，以热烈的掌声向中国大使致敬意。
