Title: Haitian Poet René Depestre Leaves Beijing
Author:
Date: 1960-12-02
Page: 4

    Haitian Poet Depestre Leaves Beijing
    Xinhua News Agency, December 1st - Haitian poet René Depestre, who came to China at the invitation of the Chinese-Latin American Friendship Association, left Beijing by plane today after visiting Beijing, Luoyang, Wuhan, Shanghai, Hangzhou, Nanjing and other cities. Among those who saw him off at the airport were Wang Puqing, a council member of the Chinese-Latin American Friendship Association, and others.
    Before his departure, Zhou Erfu, Vice President of the Chinese-Latin American Friendship Association, hosted a farewell banquet for the guest.

### 海地诗人德贝斯特莱离京

1960-12-02
第4版()
专栏：

    海地诗人德贝斯特莱离京
    新华社1日讯　应中国拉丁美洲友好协会邀请前来我国访问的海地诗人莱乃·德贝斯特莱，在访问了北京、洛阳、武汉、上海、杭州、南京等城市后，今天乘飞机离开北京。到机场送行的有中国拉丁美洲友好协会理事汪普庆等。
    行前，中国拉丁美洲友好协会副会长周而复曾设宴为客人饯行。
