Title: His Excellency Pho Proeung, Prime Minister of the Kingdom of Cambodia

Author: 
Date: 1960-12-15
Page: 5

    His Excellency Pho Proeung, Prime Minister of the Kingdom of Cambodia, was born in Phnom Penh on August 12, 1903. From 1928 to 1938, he successively served as Deputy District Chief and District Chief. From 1939 to 1948, he successively served as Governor of Pursat Province, Prey Veng Province, Kampot Province, and Kampong Cham Province. In 1949, he served as Minister of National Defense. From 1950 to 1952, he served as the Royal Representative of Battambang Province. In 1953, he served as the King's Personal Advisor, concurrently Minister of Information and Religion. From 1954 to 1958, he served as Minister of Finance, Principal of the Royal School of Administration, Minister of Education, and Minister of Social Welfare and Labor. In August 1958, he was elected as a member of the National Assembly. At the end of 1958, he served as Minister of Interior and Religion. In 1955, he served as an advisor to the Supreme Council of the Throne. In April 1960, he served as a member of the Regency Council, and in the same month, he became Prime Minister. He stated that he would resolutely implement the various policies and plans formulated by Prince Sihanouk. When Premier Zhou Enlai visited Cambodia in May 1960, he and Premier Zhou signed a joint statement, reaffirming that the Royal Government of Cambodia would continue to pursue a policy of peace and neutrality and further develop friendly and cooperative relations with China on the basis of the Five Principles of Peaceful Coexistence.

### 柬埔寨王国政府首相福·波伦阁下

1960-12-15
第5版()
专栏：

    柬埔寨王国政府首相福·波伦阁下
    柬埔寨王国政府首相福·波伦阁下1903年8月12日生于金边。1928年至1938年先后任副县长和县长，1939年至1948年先后任菩萨省、波萝勉省、唝吥省、磅湛省省长。1949年任国防部长。1950年至1952年任马德望省皇家代表。1953年任国王私人顾问兼新闻和宗教大臣、1954年至1958年任财政大臣、王家行政学校校长、教育大臣、社会福利和劳工大臣。1958年8月被选为国民议会议员。1958年底任内政和宗教大臣。1955年任皇廷最高会议顾问，1960年4月任摄政委员会委员，同月任首相。他表示将坚决奉行西哈努克亲王所制订的各项政策和计划。1960年5月周恩来总理访问柬埔寨时，他同周总理签署联合声明，重申柬埔寨王国政府将继续奉行和平中立政策，进一步在和平共处五项原则基础上发展同中国的友好合作关系。
