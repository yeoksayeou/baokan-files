Plywood (Two Poems)
    Liu Lanshan

    [To Celebrate the Commissioning of the Sihanouk-Zhou Enlai Plywood Factory]

    I stand before the bright mirror in the morning,
    Looking at the distant Golden Lake and green mountains;
    In the bright lake and mountain scenery,
    Soars a rainstorm-like cheer;
    This cheer and the clanging sound of felling trees
    In the Elephant Mountain forests soar together;
    This cheer and the rolling Golden Phnom Penh lakeside
    The plywood workers' songs soar together;
    This cheer travels to the Northeast forest farms,
    Arousing even greater leaps forward among the carpenters;
    This cheer travels to the Hongshui River bank,
    Inspiring new song moods for the new Liu Sanjies.
    Oh, this is not a mirror,
    This is a piece of plywood radiating light,
    It condenses the friendship between the Chinese and Cambodian people,
    Shining on the green ten-thousand-mile clear sky.

    A Chinese

    [In Memory of the Chinese Engineer Luo Jinchun Who Sacrificed Himself in Cambodia]

    A young Chinese engineer,
    Walks on the high mountains of Cambodia,
    He obeys the orders of his beloved motherland,
    Dedicates his enthusiasm to the friendly country;
    He knocks on every stone in Cambodia,
    Making visits to every place,
    Oh, the mountains and village roads of Cambodia,
    How familiar you are with his sturdy footsteps!

    He walks and walks tirelessly,
    Sowing the seeds of people's friendship along the way:
    Everything the motherland taught him,
    He teaches entirely to his Cambodian worker brothers;
    To make the high mountain area yield water,
    He and the mountain people blasted open a drinking well together;
    For the friendly country's locomotives to run fast,
    He carried detonators and explosives over mountains and ridges;
    With the style of a Chinese from the Mao Zedong era,
    He bravely climbs the majestic peaks;
    Ah, right on this high cliff,
    He planted a row of green pines with his blood!…

    Good son of the motherland, rest in peace,
    The green pines you planted have grown into lush forests;
    Under that giant umbrella-like green shade,
    People are singing of you and your mother!

### 胶合板
刘岚山
1960-12-17
第8版()
专栏：

    胶合板（二首）
    刘岚山
    〔祝西哈努克——周恩来胶合板厂投入生产〕
    我早晨站在明镜前，
    望远方金湖水和绿山林；
    在明媚的湖光山色里，
    飞腾着季雨般的欢呼声；
    这欢呼和象山丛林内
    丁丁冬冬伐木声一齐飞腾；
    这欢呼和荡荡的金边湖畔
    胶合板工人的歌声一齐飞腾；
    这欢呼传到东北林场，
    激起木工们更大的跃进；
    这欢呼传到红水河边，
    引起了新刘三姐们的新歌兴。
    哦，这不是一面镜子，
    这是块胶合板透出的光明，
    它凝结着中柬人民的情谊，
    照耀着绿色的万里晴天空。
    一个中国人
    〔纪念在柬埔寨牺牲的中国工程师罗锦春〕
    一个年轻的中国工程师，
    走在柬埔寨高峻的山岭，
    他奉着亲爱祖国的命令，
    向友邦献出自己的热情；
    他敲打着柬埔寨每块石头，
    向每一个地方作着访问，
    柬埔寨的山岭和村路哟，
    你多熟悉他那结实的脚步声！
    他永不疲倦地走着走着，
    一路上播种着人民的友情：
    他把祖国教给他的一切，
    全部教给柬埔寨工人弟兄；
    要高山地带交出水来，
    他同山民一起炸开吃水井；
    为了友邦的火车头飞快奔跑，
    他背起雷管和炸药翻山越岭；
    他以毛泽东时代中国人的风格，
    勇敢地去攀登巍峨的高峰；
    啊，就在这高高的山崖上，
    他蘸着鲜血栽下一路青松！……
    祖国的好儿子，安眠吧，
    你栽的青松已蓊郁成林；
    在那巨伞一般的绿荫下，
    人们正在歌唱你和你的母亲！
