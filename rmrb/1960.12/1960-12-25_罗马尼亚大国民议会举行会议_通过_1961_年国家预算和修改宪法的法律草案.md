Title: Romanian Grand National Assembly Holds Meeting, Adopts 1961 National Budget and Draft Law to Amend the Constitution
Author:
Date: 1960-12-25
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, Bucharest, December 24th – The ninth session of the Romanian Grand National Assembly was held here from December 22nd to 24th.

Romanian Workers' Party and government leaders Gheorghe Gheorghiu-Dej, Chivu Stoica, and Maurer attended the meeting.

Romanian Finance Minister Viroel delivered a report on the draft 1961 national budget at the meeting on the 22nd.

Viroel said that the 1959 national budget revenue was 51,058 million lei (Romanian currency), and the expenditure was 48,260 million lei. The estimated revenue for the 1960 national budget is 57,470 million lei, and the expenditure is 55,120 million lei.

The report said that the planned revenue for the 1961 national budget is 65,651 million lei, an increase of 14.2% compared to 1960; the expenditure is 64,801 million lei, an increase of 17.6% compared to the estimated expenditure of 1960. In the 1961 budget revenue, income from the socialist economic sector will account for 94% of the total revenue. The total national investment in 1961 will reach 27.9 billion lei, an increase of 24% compared to 1960. Industrial expenditure is 18.708 billion lei, and agricultural expenditure is 7.346 billion lei.

After discussion, the meeting unanimously adopted the 1961 national budget and the draft law on adjusting administrative divisions and amending the Constitution. The amended Constitution stipulates that when changes occur in the regional divisions below the state level, the relevant provisions in the Constitution will no longer be amended, and it can be decided by ordinary law. The meeting also approved the decrees issued by the Presidium of the Grand National Assembly during the two sessions.

### 罗马尼亚大国民议会举行会议  通过1961年国家预算和修改宪法的法律草案

1960-12-25
第6版()
专栏：

    罗马尼亚大国民议会举行会议
    通过1961年国家预算和修改宪法的法律草案
    新华社布加勒斯特24日电　罗马尼亚大国民议会第九次会议于22日至24日在这里举行。
    罗马尼亚工人党和政府领导人乔治乌—德治、基伏·斯托伊卡和毛雷尔等出席了会议。
    罗马尼亚财政部长维若利于22日的会议上作了关于1961年国家预算草案的报告。
    维若利说，1959年国家预算收入为五百一十亿五千八百万列伊（罗币名），支出为四百八十二亿六千万列伊。1960年国家预算预计收入为五百七十四亿七千万列伊，支出为五百五十一亿二千万列伊。
    报告说，1961年国家预算计划收入为六百五十六亿五千一百万列伊，比1960年增加14.2％；支出为六百四十八亿零一百万列伊，比1960年预计支出增加17.6％。在1961年的预算收入中，来自社会主义经济部门的收入将占总收入的94％。1961年国家总投资额达二百七十九亿列伊，比1960年增加24％。工业支出为一百八十七亿零八百万列伊，农业支出为七十三亿四千六百万列伊。
    会议经过讨论后，一致通过了1961年国家预算，通过了关于调整行政区划和修改宪法的法律草案。修改后的宪法规定，州以下的地区划分有更动时，将不再修改宪法中的有关规定，它可以通过普通法律作出决定。会议还批准了在两次会议期间大国民议会主席团所颁布的法令。
