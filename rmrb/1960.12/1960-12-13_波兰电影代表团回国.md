Title: Polish Film Delegation Returns Home
Author:
Date: 1960-12-13
Page: 4

    Xinhua News Agency, 11th. The Polish film delegation, headed by Acting Head of Delegation Kubiecz, returned home by plane today after a month-long visit to China. The head of the delegation, Kawalerowicz, had already returned home on November 24th.
    The delegation visited Beijing, Shanghai, Hangzhou, Guangzhou and other cities in our country.

### 波兰电影代表团回国

1960-12-13
第4版()
专栏：

    波兰电影代表团回国
    新华社11日讯　以库比耶茨代理团长为首的波兰电影代表团，结束了在我国为期一月的访问，今天乘飞机回国。代表团团长卡瓦莱罗维奇已经在11月24日先期回国。
    代表团在我国先后访问了北京、上海、杭州、广州等城市。
