Title: Meeting of the Standing Committee on Foreign Trade of the Council for Mutual Economic Assistance
Author:
Date: 1960-12-05
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, December 2nd, TASS reported: The Standing Committee on Foreign Trade of the Council for Mutual Economic Assistance, composed of the Soviet Union and European People's Democratic countries, held a meeting in Moscow. Delegations from all member states of the Council for Mutual Economic Assistance participated in the meeting. Representatives from Vietnam, China, North Korea, and Mongolia attended the meeting as observers.
    The committee discussed the results of bilateral negotiations and the signing of long-term goods exchange agreements between member states of the Council for Mutual Economic Assistance for the years 1961-1965. The committee noted with satisfaction that the well-coordinated and extensive plan for mutual supply of goods will promote the further high tide of the socialist countries' economies and the improvement of the living standards of the working people. The committee also discussed other issues related to the development of foreign trade among the member states of the Council for Mutual Economic Assistance.

### 经济互助委员会对外贸易常设委员会举行会议

1960-12-05
第5版()
专栏：

    经济互助委员会对外贸易常设委员会举行会议
    新华社4日讯　塔斯社莫斯科2日讯：由苏联和欧洲各人民民主国家组成的经济互助委员会对外贸易常设委员会在莫斯科举行了会议。参加这次会议的有经济互助委员会所有成员国的代表团。越南、中国、朝鲜和蒙古的代表以观察员身份出席了会议。
    委员会讨论了关于双边谈判结果和经济互助委员会各成员国之间签订1961—1965年长期换货协定的问题。委员会满意地指出，妥善协调互相供货的广泛计划，将会促进社会主义各国经济的进一步高涨和劳动人民生活水平的提高。委员会还讨论了有关发展经济互助委员会各成员国的对外贸易的其他一些问题。
