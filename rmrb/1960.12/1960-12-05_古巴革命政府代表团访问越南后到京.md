Title: Cuban Revolutionary Government Delegation Arrives in Beijing After Visiting Vietnam
Author:
Date: 1960-12-05
Page: 4

    Xinhua News Agency, December 4th. The Cuban Revolutionary Government Delegation, led by Héctor Rodríguez Llompart, Administrative Vice Minister of the Cuban Ministry of Foreign Affairs, arrived in Beijing by plane this afternoon after concluding its visit to the Democratic Republic of Vietnam.
    Those who welcomed the delegation at the airport included Lu Xuzhang, Vice Minister of Foreign Trade, Chen Ming, Bureau Director, Shen Jian, Chinese Ambassador to Cuba, and Lin Ping and Wang Disan, Deputy Directors of the Department of American and Australian Affairs of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs. Oscar Pino Santos, Cuban Ambassador to China, and diplomatic officials from the embassy, as well as diplomatic envoys and officials from Vietnam and Mongolia in China, also greeted the delegation at the airport.
    This evening, Geng Biao, Vice Minister of Foreign Affairs, had dinner with the distinguished Cuban guests. Lu Xuzhang, Shen Jian, Chen Ming, Lin Ping, and Wang Disan were present as escorts. Oscar Pino Santos, Cuban Ambassador to China, was also invited to attend.

### 古巴革命政府代表团访问越南后到京

1960-12-05
第4版()
专栏：

    古巴革命政府代表团访问越南后到京
    新华社4日讯　结束了对越南民主共和国访问的古巴革命政府代表团，在团长、古巴外交部行政副部长埃克托·罗德里格斯·略姆帕尔特率领下，今天下午乘飞机到达北京。
    到机场欢迎的有对外贸易部副部长卢绪章、局长陈明，中国驻古巴大使申健，外交部美澳司副司长林平、王第三等。古巴驻中国大使奥斯卡·皮诺·桑托斯和大使馆外交官员，以及越南、蒙古驻中国使节和外交官员也到机场迎接。
    今晚，外交部副部长耿飚同古巴贵宾共进晚餐。出席作陪的有卢绪章、申健、陈明、林平、王第三。古巴驻中国大使奥斯卡·皮诺·桑托斯也应邀出席。
