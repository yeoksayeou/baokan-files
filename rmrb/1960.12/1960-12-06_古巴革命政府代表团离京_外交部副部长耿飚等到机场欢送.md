Title: Cuban Revolutionary Government Delegation Departs Beijing; Vice Foreign Minister Geng Biao and Others See Them Off at the Airport
Author:
Date: 1960-12-06
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, December 5th. The Cuban Revolutionary Government Delegation, headed by Hector Rodriguez Llompart, Administrative Vice Minister of the Cuban Ministry of Foreign Affairs, left Beijing by special plane this morning to visit the Mongolian People's Republic. The delegation arrived in Beijing yesterday after visiting the Democratic Republic of Vietnam.

Those who saw them off at the airport today included Vice Foreign Minister Geng Biao, Vice Minister of Foreign Trade Lu Xuzhang, Bureau Director Chen Ming, Deputy Directors of the American and Australian Affairs Department of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs Lin Ping and Wang Disan, and others.

Cuban Ambassador to China Oscar Pino Santos and diplomatic officials of the embassy, as well as Vietnamese and Mongolian envoys and diplomatic officials to China, also went to the airport to see them off.

### 古巴革命政府代表团离京  外交部副部长耿飚等到机场欢送

1960-12-06
第4版()
专栏：

    古巴革命政府代表团离京
    外交部副部长耿飚等到机场欢送
    新华社5日讯　以古巴外交部行政副部长埃克托·罗德里格斯·略姆帕尔特为首的古巴革命政府代表团，今天上午乘专机离开北京前往蒙古人民共和国访问。代表团是访问越南民主共和国以后在昨天到达北京的。
    今天到机场欢送的，有外交部副部长耿飚，对外贸易部副部长卢绪章、局长陈明，外交部美澳司副司长林平、王第三等。
    古巴驻中国大使奥斯卡·皮诺·桑托斯和大使馆外交官员，以及越南、蒙古驻中国的使节和外交官员，也到机场送行。
