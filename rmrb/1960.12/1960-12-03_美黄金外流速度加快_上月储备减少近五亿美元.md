Title: US Gold Outflow Accelerates; Reserves Decrease by Nearly $500 Million Last Month
Author:
Date: 1960-12-03
Page: 6

    Xinhua News Agency, December 2nd – The U.S. government announced yesterday that a total of $492.2 million in gold flowed out of the United States in November.

    The U.S. government also announced that in the past week, foreign countries redeemed another $76 million worth of gold from the United States, thus reducing U.S. gold reserves to $17.91 billion, which is $90 million less than the "danger point" of $18 billion.

    In the past five months of the second half of this year, U.S. gold reserves have outflowed a total of $1.4122 billion, and the outflow is accelerating: the annual rate was $3.8 billion in July, $4.1 billion in August, $4.7 billion in September, and $5.9 billion in October.

### 美黄金外流速度加快  上月储备减少近五亿美元

1960-12-03
第6版()
专栏：

    美黄金外流速度加快
    上月储备减少近五亿美元
    新华社2日讯　美国政府昨天宣布，在11月份，美国黄金一共外流了四亿九千二百万美元。
    美国政府同时宣布，在过去一个星期内，外国又向美国兑走了价值七千六百万美元的黄金，因此美国的黄金储备已下降到一百七十九亿一千万美元，即比一百八十亿美元的“危险点”还少九千万美元。
    今年下半年五个月来，美国的黄金储备已一共外流了十四亿一千二百万美元，而且外流的速度越来越快：7月份为年率三十八亿美元，8月份为四十一亿美元，9月份为四十七亿美元，10月份为五十九亿美元。
