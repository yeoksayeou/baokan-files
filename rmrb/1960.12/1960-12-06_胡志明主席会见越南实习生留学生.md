Title: President Ho Chi Minh Meets with Vietnamese Interns and Students Studying Abroad
Author:
Date: 1960-12-06
Page: 1

    Xinhua News Agency, December 5th – President Ho Chi Minh, along with all members of the delegation from the Vietnamese Democratic Republic's Party and government, visited the Embassy of the Vietnamese Democratic Republic in China today to meet with Vietnamese interns and students studying in our country.
    This morning, Le Duan, First Secretary of the Central Committee of the Vietnamese Workers' Party, accompanied by Bo Yibo, Alternate Member of the Political Bureau of the CPC Central Committee and Vice Premier of the State Council, visited the National Industrial and Transportation Exhibition.

### 胡志明主席会见越南实习生留学生

1960-12-06
第1版()
专栏：

    胡志明主席会见越南实习生留学生
    新华社5日讯　胡志明主席今天同越南民主共和国党政代表团全体成员，去越南民主共和国驻华大使馆，会见了越南民主共和国在我国的实习生和留学生。
    今天上午，越南劳动党中央委员会第一书记黎笋，在中共中央政治局候补委员、国务院副总理薄一波的陪同下，参观了全国工业交通展览会。
