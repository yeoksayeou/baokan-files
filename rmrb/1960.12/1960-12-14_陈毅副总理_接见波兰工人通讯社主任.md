Title: Vice Premier Chen Yi Receives the Director of the Polish Workers' News Agency
Author:
Date: 1960-12-14
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, December 13th: Vice Premier Chen Yi today received Mr. Walery Śliwiński-Szczerbic, Director of the International Department of the Polish Workers' News Agency, who is currently visiting China, and had a cordial conversation with him.

Present at the reception were Mr. Boratynski, Counselor of the Polish Embassy in China, and Mr. Swolkowski, Beijing correspondent for the Polish People's Tribune.

Kang Maozhao, Deputy Director of the Information Department of the Chinese Ministry of Foreign Affairs, was also present at the reception.

### 陈毅副总理  接见波兰工人通讯社主任

1960-12-14
第4版()
专栏：

    陈毅副总理
    接见波兰工人通讯社主任
    新华社13日讯　陈毅副总理今天接见正在我国访问的波兰工人通讯社国际部主任瓦·希利夫卡—什采尔比茨，同他进行了亲切的谈话。
    接见时，在座的有波兰驻中国大使馆参赞博拉托和波兰人民论坛报驻北京记者斯沃姆科夫斯基。
    接见时，我国外交部新闻司副司长康矛召也在座。
