Title: Austrian Communist Party Adds Nearly Three Thousand New Members
Author:
Date: 1960-12-23
Page: 6
Column: Life of Fraternal Parties

    The "Austrian People's Voice" newspaper published on the 18th a statement from the Central Committee of the Communist Party of Austria regarding the results of the autumn campaign to recruit new members. The statement pointed out that recently 2,942 people joined the party, and one-third of the new members are under the age of thirty.

### 奥共增加新党员近三千人

1960-12-23
第6版()
专栏：兄弟党的生活

     奥共增加新党员近三千人
    《奥地利人民之声报》18日发表了奥地利共产党中央委员会关于秋季征集新党员运动的结果的声明。声明指出，最近有二千九百四十二人入了党，三分之一的新党员的岁数不到三十。
