Title: Leaders of the Chao Opera Troupe Bid Farewell to the Queen of Cambodia; Cambodian Minister of State for National Education Receives the Chao Opera Troupe
Author:
Date: 1960-12-10
Page: 6
Xinhua News Agency, Phnom Penh, 8th: Wang Kunlun, head of the Chinese Chao Opera Troupe, and other leaders, went to the Royal Palace on the 7th to bid farewell to the Queen of Cambodia. Ye Jinghao, Chargé d'Affaires ad interim of the Chinese Embassy in Cambodia, was also present at the farewell.

The Queen of Cambodia told the Chinese guests that she was pleased with the success of the Chinese Chao Opera Troupe's performance. She was also pleased that the Chinese actors had learned the Cambodian Royal Ballet in a very short time.

Troupe leader Wang Kunlun expressed his gratitude to the Queen for her concern for the Chinese Chao Opera Troupe and presented her with some gifts.

Yesterday morning, the Queen of Cambodia watched the Chinese artists rehearse a section of the Royal Ballet that they had just learned from Cambodian dancers. Afterwards, she gave each of them a set of Cambodian national costumes. The Queen of Cambodia told the Chinese artists that the silk sarong was made in Cambodia, but the blouse came from China, so this national costume is the crystallization of Sino-Cambodian friendship.

Yesterday, the leaders of the Chinese Chao Opera Troupe also bid farewell to Cambodian Minister of State for National Education, Xie Zhenguo, Secretary of State for National Education, Gen Tao De Montello, Deputy Mayor of Phnom Penh, Weng En, and Chief of Protocol of the Royal Palace, Qing Ha. Overseas Chinese in Phnom Penh held a reception for the Chinese Chao Opera Troupe last night. Acting Prime Minister of Cambodia and Mayor of Phnom Penh, Tai Pan, and Chargé d'Affaires ad interim of the Chinese Embassy in Cambodia, Ye Jinghao, attended the reception.

During its visit to Cambodia for more than a month, the Chinese Chao Opera Troupe performed a total of twenty-three performances in Phnom Penh, Kampong Cham, Kampong Cham, and Battambang. Approximately 24,000 spectators watched the performances.

Xinhua News Agency, Phnom Penh, 9th: Cambodian Minister of State for National Education, Xie Zhenguo, held a reception here on the evening of the 8th to welcome the Chinese Chao Opera Troupe, which is about to return home.

Among those present at the reception were Cambodian Secretary of State for National Education, Gen Tao De Montello, other senior government officials, and members of parliament. Ye Jinghao, Chargé d'Affaires ad interim of the Chinese Embassy in Cambodia, and embassy staff also attended the reception.

The reception was held in a very cordial and friendly atmosphere.

Later that day, the Chinese Chao Opera Troupe was invited to attend an artistic evening at the Royal Palace. This evening was specially arranged for the Chinese Chao Opera Troupe according to the instructions of the Queen of Cambodia.

### 我潮剧团领导人向柬埔寨王后告别  柬国民教育国务大臣招待我潮剧团

1960-12-10
第6版()
专栏：

    我潮剧团领导人向柬埔寨王后告别
    柬国民教育国务大臣招待我潮剧团
    新华社金边8日电　中国潮剧团团长王昆仑和其它领导人7日到王宫向柬埔寨王后告别。告别时在座的还有中国驻柬埔寨大使馆临时代办叶景灏。
    柬埔寨王后对中国客人说，她对于中国潮剧团的演出成功表示高兴。她对于中国演员在很短时间内学会了柬埔寨皇家芭蕾舞也表示高兴。
    王昆仑团长对于王后对中国潮剧团的关怀，表示感谢，并向她赠送了一些礼品。
    昨天上午，柬埔寨王后观看了中国艺术家排练刚从柬埔寨舞蹈家那里学来的一段皇家芭蕾舞。随后，她送给他们每人一套柬埔寨民族服装。柬埔寨王后对中国艺术家们说，丝围裙是在柬埔寨做的，但是，上衣是从中国来的，所以，这套民族服装是柬中友谊的结晶。
    中国潮剧团领导人昨天还向柬埔寨国民教育国务大臣谢振国、国民教育国务秘书根陶·德蒙特罗、金边副市长翁恩和皇宫礼宾司司长清哈告别。金边的华侨昨晚为中国潮剧团举行了一次招待会。柬埔寨代理首相、金边市长泰潘和中国驻柬埔寨大使馆临时代办叶景灏出席了招待会。
    中国潮剧团在柬埔寨一个多月的访问中，在金边、唝吩、磅湛和马德望等地一共演出了二十三场。大约有两万四千余观众观看了演出。
    据新华社金边9日电　柬埔寨国民教育国务大臣谢振国8日晚在这里举行招待会，招待即将动身回国的中国潮剧团。
    出席招待会的有柬埔寨国民教育国务秘书根陶·德蒙特罗和其他高级政府官员以及议员们。中国驻柬埔寨大使馆临时代办叶景灏和大使馆人员也出席了招待会。
    招待会是在十分亲密友好的气氛中进行的。
    当天晚些时候，中国潮剧团应邀到王宫参加了一个艺术晚会。这个晚会是根据柬埔寨王后的指示为中国潮剧团特别安排的。
