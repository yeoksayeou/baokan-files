Title: Brezhnev Returns to Moscow
Author:
Date: 1960-12-14
Page: 5

    Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, December 12th -- TASS reported from Moscow on the 12th: Brezhnev, member of the Presidium of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of the Soviet Union and Chairman of the Presidium of the Supreme Soviet of the Soviet Union, returned to Moscow from Irkutsk on the 12th.
    Brezhnev accompanied the Vice-Chairman of the Central Committee of the Chinese Communist Party, Chairman of the People's Republic of China Liu Shaoqi and the Chinese Party and Government Delegation he led on a visit to various parts of the Soviet Union. He arrived in Irkutsk when he accompanied the distinguished Chinese guests when they left Moscow to return to their country.
