Title: Chinese Chaozhou Opera Troupe Continues Performances in Cambodia
Author:
Date: 1960-12-01
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, Phnom Penh, 28th -- The Chinese Chaozhou Opera Troupe, at the invitation of the Royal Representative of Phnom Penh, continued its public performances here last night. Cambodian Acting Prime Minister and Mayor of Phnom Penh, Thai Phan, and Chinese Embassy in Cambodia Chargé d'Affaires ad interim, Ye Jinghao, attended the performance.

### 中国潮剧团继续在柬埔寨演出

1960-12-01
第5版()
专栏：

    中国潮剧团继续在柬埔寨演出
    据新华社金边28日电　中国潮剧团应金边皇家代表的邀请昨晚继续在这里举行公演。柬埔寨代理首相金边市长泰潘和中国驻柬埔寨大使馆临时代办叶景灏出席观看了这次演出。
