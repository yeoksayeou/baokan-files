Title: Burmese House of Representatives Approves Sino-Burmese Boundary Treaty; Representatives Cheer Sino-Burmese Friendly Relations Entering New Stage
Author:
Date: 1960-12-10
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Rangoon, 8th December. The Burmese House of Representatives, at its meeting today, discussed and approved the Boundary Treaty signed in Peking on 1st October by the governments of the Union of Burma and the People's Republic of China.

During the discussion, the representatives warmly hailed the traditional friendship between China and Burma, and unanimously agreed that the signing of the Sino-Burmese Boundary Treaty marked a new stage in the friendly relations between the two countries.

U Nu Myo, member of the Federal Party House of Representatives and editor of the "Peacock Voice" newspaper, said that the long-standing Sino-Burmese boundary issue had been satisfactorily resolved through friendly consultation. This is a great victory for the people of China and Burma, and a heavy blow to the imperialist schemes of provocation.

U Nu Myo stressed that the resolution of the Sino-Burmese boundary issue greatly developed the Five Principles of Peaceful Coexistence, setting a brilliant example for countries around the world to resolve their disputes amicably.

U Tha Zan, Shan State representative in the Federal Party House of Representatives, said that the Sino-Burmese boundary issue has been resolved freely and justly. Both sides value friendship. The people of Shan State are very happy about this. U Tha Zan also said that the resolution of this issue will further develop the friendly relations between the two countries, and the Sino-Burmese border will become a border of peace and friendship.

Bo Mhu Bala, a member of the Federal Party House of Representatives, said in his speech that the Sino-Burmese Boundary Treaty is a treaty of peace and friendship. He pointed out that this treaty reflects the strong desire of the people of the two countries for peaceful coexistence. He said that the Sino-Burmese boundary issue has been rationally resolved. There is no question of the strong oppressing the weak in this matter. This treaty represents the high interests of the people of China and Burma. It sets an example for other countries to resolve their disputes.

Meng Bo Qiao, representative of the Mon People's Front in the House of Representatives, said that the long-standing Sino-Burmese boundary issue left over by British imperialism has now been satisfactorily resolved. The Mon people are particularly pleased about this. Meng Bo Qiao, on behalf of the Mon people, expressed his gratitude to those who have worked to resolve the Sino-Burmese boundary issue. He also hopes that the friendship between China and Burma will be further developed.

### 缅甸国会代表院批准中缅边界条约  议员欢呼中缅友好关系进入新阶段

1960-12-10
第1版()
专栏：

    缅甸国会代表院批准中缅边界条约
    议员欢呼中缅友好关系进入新阶段
    新华社仰光8日电　缅甸国会代表院在今天举行的会议上讨论并批准了缅甸联邦和中华人民共和国政府10月1日在北京签订的边界条约。
    议员们在讨论中热情欢呼中国和缅甸之间的传统友谊，并且一致认为，中缅边界条约的签订使两国之间的友好关系进入了新的阶段。
    联邦党代表院议员、《孔雀之声报》主编吴纽妙说，长期以来悬而未决的中缅边界问题已经通过友好协商获得令人满意的解决。这是中国和缅甸人民的伟大胜利，是对帝国主义挑衅阴谋的沉重打击。
    吴纽妙着重指出，中缅边界问题的解决大大发展了和平共处的五项原则，这为世界各国友好地解决它们的争端树立了一个光辉的榜样。
    联邦党的掸邦代表院议员吴塔赞说，中缅边界问题已经自由地和公正地取得了解决。双方都重视友谊。掸邦人民为此感到十分高兴。吴塔赞又说，这个问题的解决将进一步发展两国之间的友好关系，中缅边界将成为和平与友谊的边界。
    联邦党代表院议员波木巴拉在讲话中说，中缅边界条约是和平和友好的条约。他指出，这项条约反映了两国人民对友好共处的强烈愿望。他说，中缅边界问题已经获得合理的解决。在这个问题上丝毫不存在任何强者对弱者的欺压。这项条约代表了中缅两国人民的高度利益。它为其他国家解决它们之间的纠纷树立了榜样。
    孟族人民阵线的代表院议员孟波乔说，英帝国主义遗留下来的长期存在的中缅边界问题现在得到了满意的解决。孟族人民对此特别感到高兴。孟波乔代表孟族人民向那些为解决中缅边界问题作出努力的人表示感谢。他还希望中缅两国的友谊得到进一步的发展。
