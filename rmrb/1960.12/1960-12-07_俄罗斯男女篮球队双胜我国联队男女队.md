Title: Russian Men's and Women's Basketball Teams Both Win Against Chinese Combined Teams

Author:

Date: 1960-12-07
Page: 2

Xinhua News Agency, December 6th - The valiant and tenacious Soviet Russian Federation men's and women's basketball teams, in their final friendly matches during their visit to China tonight in Beijing, both defeated the combined Chinese men's and women's basketball teams. The score for the men's teams was 88 to 81; the score for the women's teams was 59 to 56.

The Russian Federation men's and women's basketball teams have concluded all eight of their sixteen friendly matches in China. The men's team won six and lost two; the women's team won five and lost three. They are about to leave Beijing to return home.

### 俄罗斯男女篮球队双胜我国联队男女队

1960-12-07
第2版()
专栏：

    俄罗斯男女篮球队双胜我国联队男女队
    据新华社6日讯　勇猛、顽强的苏联俄罗斯联邦男女篮球队，今晚在北京进行访华最后一次友谊比赛时，双双战胜了我国的联队男女篮球队。男子队的比数是八十八比八十一；女子队是五十九比五十六。
    俄罗斯联邦男女篮球队已经结束了在我国的全部八次十六场友谊比赛，男子队六胜二负；女子队五胜三负。他们即将离北京回国。
