Title: People from All Circles in the Capital Warmly Hold Symposium, Supporting the Declaration of the Meeting of Representatives of Communist and Workers' Parties of All Countries
Author:
Date: 1960-12-07
Page: 2
Xinhua News Agency, 6th: People from all circles in the capital warmly listened to and read the declaration of the meeting of representatives of Communist and Workers' Parties of all countries today. Many units held discussions, and people expressed their warm support for the declaration.

    People's Daily, Ta Kung Pao, Guangming Daily, Worker's Daily, China Youth Daily and Beijing Daily all published the full text of the declaration today. In front of the business departments of various newspaper offices and the post and telecommunications bureaus distributing newspapers, people were crowding to buy newspapers this morning. All the retail newspapers in the business department of People's Daily were snapped up in half an hour, and the sales volume doubled compared with usual. In cultural centers, reading rooms, and in front of street bulletin boards, many people were attentively reading the declaration all day long.

    The Central People's Broadcasting Station broadcast the summary of the declaration for eighty minutes this morning, and then broadcast it three more times continuously.

    Workers from the Shijingshan Iron and Steel Company and the Beijing No. 1 Machine Tool Plant, teachers and students from the Central Advanced Party School of the Chinese Communist Party, Peking University, and Beijing Normal University, and cadres from the China-Soviet Friendship People's Commune expressed their warm support after reading the declaration. Many people said that the declaration put forward the future combat tasks of the communist and working class and progressive mankind all over the world, and pointed out the path to victory. They felt greatly encouraged after reading it. They believe that this declaration will further promote the struggle of the people of all countries for world peace, national liberation, democratic freedom and socialism to achieve greater victories, and will further strengthen the unity of the socialist camp and the Communist and Workers' Parties of all countries in the world. They expressed their determination to respond to the call of the declaration and wage unremitting struggle against the aggressive and war policies of imperialism and to safeguard world peace.

### 首都各界人民热情举行座谈  拥护各国共产党和工人党代表会议声明

1960-12-07
第2版()
专栏：

    首都各界人民热情举行座谈
    拥护各国共产党和工人党代表会议声明
    新华社6日讯　首都各界人民今天热情地收听和阅读了各国共产党和工人党代表会议声明，许多单位进行了座谈，人们纷纷表示热烈拥护和支持这个声明。
    人民日报、大公报、光明日报、工人日报、中国青年报和北京日报，今天都刊登了声明的全文。各报社营业部和发行报纸的各邮电局门前，今晨挤满了争购报纸的人。人民日报营业部的全部零售报，在半个小时内就被人们争购一空，销售量比平日增加了一倍。在文化馆、阅览室和街头贴报栏前，整日都有许多人在聚精会神地阅读着声明。
    中央人民广播电台在今天清晨，用八十分钟的时间广播了声明的摘要，以后又连续广播了三次。
    石景山钢铁公司和北京第一机床厂的工人，中共中央高级党校、北京大学、北京师范大学的师生，中苏友好人民公社的干部们在读了这个声明以后，纷纷表示热烈拥护。许多人说，这个声明提出了全世界共产党和工人阶级以及进步人类今后的战斗任务，指明了达到胜利的途径，读了以后受到很大的鼓舞。他们认为，这个声明将进一步促进各国人民为世界和平、民族解放、民主自由和社会主义的斗争获得更加伟大的胜利，将进一步加强社会主义阵营和全世界各国共产党和工人党的团结。他们表示坚决响应声明的号召，为反对帝国主义的侵略政策和战争政策、维护世界和平，进行不懈的斗争。
