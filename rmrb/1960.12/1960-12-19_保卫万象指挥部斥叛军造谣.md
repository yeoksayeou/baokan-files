Title: Vientiane Defense Headquarters Denounces Rebel Army for Spreading Rumors
Author:
Date: 1960-12-19
Page: 5

    Xinhua News Agency, Hanoi, December 18th: The Lao legal government radio station broadcast on the evening of December 17th: The Vientiane Defense Headquarters strongly refuted a message broadcast by the Savannakhet radio station on December 15th. The message said that the traitorous Savannakhet army had captured 900 prisoners, including two "Viet Minh," and seized some artillery pieces.
    The Vientiane Defense Headquarters pointed out: This message is completely unfounded. The purpose of fabricating this message is to use it as a pretext for the United States, Thailand, South Vietnam, the Philippines, and the Chiang Kai-shek clique to intensify their interference and aggression against Laos.

### 保卫万象指挥部斥叛军造谣

1960-12-19
第5版()
专栏：

    保卫万象指挥部斥叛军造谣
    新华社河内18日电　老挝合法政府广播电台17日晚上广播：保卫万象指挥部强烈驳斥沙湾拿吉电台12月15日广播的一项消息。消息说，叛国的沙湾拿吉军队抓了九百名俘虏，其中有两名是“越盟”，并缴获一些大炮。
    保卫万象指挥部指出：这项消息完全是没有根据的。他们捏造这项消息的目的，是以此作为美国、泰国、南越、菲律宾和蒋介石集团加紧干涉和侵略老挝的借口。
