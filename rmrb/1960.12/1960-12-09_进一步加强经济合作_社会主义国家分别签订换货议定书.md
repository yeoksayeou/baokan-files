Title: Further Strengthen Economic Cooperation Socialist Countries Sign Barter Protocols Respectively
Author:
Date: 1960-12-09
Page: 5
	To further strengthen economic cooperation, some socialist countries have recently signed trade and barter protocols with each other for the coming year and several years ahead.
	Mongolia and Poland recently signed a barter protocol for 1961 to 1965 and a barter protocol for 1961.
	The protocols stipulate that Mongolia will export furs, meat and other livestock products to Poland.
	Poland will provide Mongolia with tractors, automobiles and daily necessities, etc.
	Czechoslovakia and the German Democratic Republic recently signed a barter protocol for 1961. According to the protocol, the volume of barter between the two countries will increase by 13% compared to 1960.
	Czechoslovakia will provide the German Democratic Republic with products of the machine manufacturing industry, anthracite, coking coal, rolled materials, textiles, motorcycles, etc.
	The German Democratic Republic will provide Czechoslovakia with machine manufacturing industrial products, electrical equipment, precision machinery, optical instruments, chemical industrial products such as artificial fertilizers, synthetic rubber, textiles, and consumer goods.
	Hungary and the German Democratic Republic recently signed a trade protocol for 1961. The protocol stipulates that the trade volume between the two countries next year will increase by 16% compared to this year.
	According to the protocol, the German Democratic Republic will provide Hungary with machine tools, cement factory equipment, chemical industrial equipment, precision machinery, optical instruments and mining industrial products, etc.
	Hungary will provide the German Democratic Republic with agricultural products, raw materials for the aluminum industry, chemical industrial products, automobiles, television sets and other consumer goods, etc.

### 进一步加强经济合作  社会主义国家分别签订换货议定书

1960-12-09
第5版()
专栏：

    进一步加强经济合作
    社会主义国家分别签订换货议定书
    本报讯　为了进一步加强经济合作，一些社会主义国家最近相互签订了今后一年和几年间的贸易议定书和换货议定书。
    蒙古和波兰最近签订了1961年到1965年换货议定书和1961年换货议定书。
    议定书规定，蒙古将向波兰输出毛皮、肉类和其他畜产品。
    波兰将向蒙古提供拖拉机、汽车和日用品等。
    捷克斯洛伐克和德意志民主共和国最近签订了1961年换货议定书。根据议定书，两国换货额将比1960年增加13％。
    捷克斯洛伐克将向德意志民主共和国提供机器制造业产品、无烟煤、炼焦煤、压延材料、纺织品、摩托车等。
    德意志民主共和国将向捷克斯洛伐克提供机器制造工业产品、电工器材、精密机器、光学仪器以及人造肥料、合成橡胶、纺织品等化学工业产品和消费品。
    匈牙利和德意志民主共和国最近签订了1961年的贸易议定书，议定书规定，两国明年贸易额将比今年增加16％。
    根据议定书，德意志民主共和国将向匈牙利提供机床、水泥工厂设备、化学工业设备、精密机器、光学仪器和采矿工业品等。
    匈牙利将向德意志民主共和国提供农产品、制铝工业原料、化学工业产品、汽车、电视机和其他消费品等。
