    The Panyu Shipyard and the Panyu Agricultural Machinery Factory in Guangdong Province cooperated to successfully manufacture a mud dredging boat that can dig more than 8,000 catties of mud fertilizer per minute, which can save a lot of labor for rural areas in digging mud fertilizer. This is the first mud dredging boat launched. Xinhua News Agency reporter Wang Nan photographed
