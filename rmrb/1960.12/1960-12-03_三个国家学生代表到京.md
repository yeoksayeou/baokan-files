Title: Student Representatives from Three Countries Arrive in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1960-12-03
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, December 2nd.  At the invitation of the All-China Students' Federation, Indonesian student representative Bambang Kalibata, Brazilian student representative Niomar Veigas de Carvalho Oliveira, and Argentine student representatives Jorge Wiskin and Mario L. Buaida arrived in Beijing by plane on November 30th and today, respectively.

### 三个国家学生代表到京

1960-12-03
第4版()
专栏：

    三个国家学生代表到京
    新华社2日讯　应中华全国学生联合会邀请来我国访问的印度尼西亚学生代表邦邦·卡雷贝特，巴西学生代表尼奥马尔·维艾加斯·德卡尔瓦罗·奥利维罗，阿根廷学生代表何尔亥·威斯金德和马利欧·勒·布艾达，已分别在11月30日和今天乘飞机到达北京。
