Title: Two Cuban Guests Leave Beijing for Home

Author:
Date: 1960-12-23
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, December 22nd: Mario Puente Ferro, National Organizing Secretary of the Cuban "July 26th" Movement, and Eladio Enrique Martinez, Havana Province Organizing Secretary of the Cuban Farmers' Union, concluded their visit to our country and left Beijing for home today. They came to our country for a visit on November 24th at the invitation of the Chinese-Latin American Friendship Association.

Chu Tunan, President of the Chinese-Latin American Friendship Association, hosted a farewell banquet for the guests on the 21st. Zhou Erfu, Vice President of the Chinese-Latin American Friendship Association, and Oscar Pino Santos, Cuban Ambassador to China, and others attended the banquet.

### 古巴两位客人离京回国

1960-12-23
第4版()
专栏：

    古巴两位客人离京回国
    新华社22日讯　古巴“七·二六”运动全国组织书记马里奥·普恩特·费罗和古巴农民联合会哈瓦那省组织书记艾拉第欧·恩利盖·马尔第内斯，结束了在我国的访问，今天离开北京回国。他们是应中国拉丁美洲友好协会邀请在11月24日来我国访问的。
    中国拉丁美洲友好协会会长楚图南曾在21日设宴欢送客人。中拉友协副会长周而复和古巴驻华大使奥斯卡·皮诺·桑托斯等出席了宴会。
