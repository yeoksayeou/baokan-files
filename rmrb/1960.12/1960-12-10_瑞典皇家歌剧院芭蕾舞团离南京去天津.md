Title: Swedish Royal Opera Ballet Departs Nanjing for Tianjin
Author:
Date: 1960-12-10
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency: The Swedish Royal Opera Ballet concluded its performance tour in Nanjing and departed Nanjing by train on the morning of the 7th for Tianjin.
    The ballet troupe performed three shows in Nanjing and was warmly welcomed by the audience.

### 瑞典皇家歌剧院芭蕾舞团离南京去天津

1960-12-10
第4版()
专栏：

    瑞典皇家歌剧院芭蕾舞团离南京去天津
    据新华社讯　瑞典皇家歌剧院芭蕾舞团结束了在南京的访问演出，7日上午乘火车离开南京去天津。
    芭蕾舞团在南京共演出三场，受到了观众的热烈欢迎。
