Title: Chinese Railway Delegation Arrives in Ulaanbaatar
Author:
Date: 1960-12-20
Page: 7

    Our Railway Delegation Arrives in Ulaanbaatar
    (Dispatch) A Chinese railway delegation, headed by Meng Hua, director of the Cultural and Educational Bureau of the Ministry of Railways, arrived in Ulaanbaatar on the 19th, having been invited to attend the celebration of the tenth anniversary of the establishment of the Ulaanbaatar Railway Bureau of Mongolia.
    The delegation was greeted at the station by Danbadarjaa, Deputy Director of the Ulaanbaatar Railway Bureau of Mongolia, and Sai Zishuang, Commercial Counselor of the Chinese Embassy in Mongolia.

### 我铁路代表团到达乌兰巴托

1960-12-20
第7版()
专栏：

    我铁路代表团到达乌兰巴托
    本报讯　应邀参加蒙古乌兰巴托铁路局成立十周年庆祝典礼的中国铁路代表团，在铁道部文化教育局局长孟华的率领下，在19日到达乌兰巴托。
    到车站迎接的，有蒙古乌兰巴托铁路局副局长丹巴达尔扎和中国驻蒙古大使馆商务参赞赛自爽。
