Title: Prague Publishes Communiqué on Visit of Cuban Economic Delegation to Czechoslovakia; Czechoslovakia and Cuba Decide to Develop Economic Cooperation
Author:
Date: 1960-12-24
Page: 5
Xinhua News Agency, Prague, December 22nd: A communiqué was released here today regarding the visit of the economic delegation of the Republic of Cuba's revolutionary government, headed by National Bank President Guevara, to Czechoslovakia.

The communiqué points out that the Cuban delegation received a very warm welcome from the Czechoslovak people. During their meetings with the Cuban guests, Czechoslovak representatives expressed sincere sympathy and support for the people of the Republic of Cuba, led by Prime Minister Castro, and indignation at the interference and hostile actions of American imperialism against Cuba.

The Czechoslovakian representatives also expressed high praise for Cuba's foreign policy, which resolutely and consistently defends the interests of Cuba's freedom and independence, and bravely opposes all forms of neo-colonialism and old colonialism.

During the talks, the two sides signed a protocol on the exchange of goods for 1961, stipulating the further expansion of trade relations and the volume of exchanged goods.

Based on the economic agreement of June 10, 1960, the Czechoslovak government will increase its loan to Cuba by 20 million U.S. dollars, to be used by Cuba to purchase complete sets of factory equipment from Czechoslovakia. At the same time, Czechoslovakia will accept a considerable number of Cuban students and workers for internships in Czechoslovakia.

At the conclusion of the talks, an economic cooperation agreement was signed, which establishes the main principles on the basis of absolute equality.

### 布拉格发表古巴经济代表团访捷公报  捷古决定发展经济合作

1960-12-24
第5版()
专栏：

    布拉格发表古巴经济代表团访捷公报
    捷古决定发展经济合作
    据新华社布拉格22日电　这里今天发表了关于以国家银行行长格瓦拉为首的古巴共和国革命政府经济代表团访问捷克斯洛伐克的公报。
    公报指出，古巴代表团受到了捷克斯洛伐克人民非常热诚的接待。捷克斯洛伐克代表在会见古巴客人时，表示真挚地同情和支持以卡斯特罗总理为首的古巴共和国的人民，并对美帝国主义对古巴的干涉和敌对行动表示愤慨。
    捷克斯洛伐克的代表还对古巴的外交政策表示高度的评价，这种政策坚决和一贯地保卫古巴的自由和独立的利益，并英勇地反对各种形式的新老殖民主义。
    在会谈期间，双方签订了关于1961年换货议定书，规定进一步扩大贸易关系和换货量。
    在1960年6月10日经济协定的基础上，捷克斯洛伐克政府向古巴提供的贷款将增加二千万美元，供古巴购买捷克斯洛伐克的整套工厂设备之用。捷克斯洛伐克同时将接受相当数量的古巴学生和工人在捷克斯洛伐克实习。
    在会谈结束时签订了经济合作协定，这项协定在绝对平等的基础上确定了主要的原则。
