Title: Nepalese King Dissolves Cabinet and Parliament; Prime Minister Koirala, Speaker of the House, and Others Arrested
Author:
Date: 1960-12-18
Page: 5

    Xinhua News Agency, Kathmandu, 17th: It is reported that King Mahendra of Nepal dissolved the Nepalese Congress Party cabinet headed by Koirala, as well as the upper and lower houses of parliament, on the 15th, and personally took over all administrative power of the country.
    The report said that Prime Minister Koirala had been arrested on the same day. Also arrested were the Minister of the Interior, the Minister of Transportation, the Minister of Agriculture and Food, the Speaker of the Lower House, and the Secretary-General of the Nepalese Congress Party, among others.
    In an announcement broadcast over the radio, King Mahendra declared that Nepal "will adhere to our principle of neutrality and will maintain a policy of mutual goodwill and friendship with all friendly countries."
    Prime Minister Koirala was appointed by the King on May 18th of last year to form a cabinet after the Nepalese Congress Party won the first general election held in the country from the end of February to mid-April last year.

### 尼泊尔国王解散内阁和议会  柯伊拉腊首相和下院议长等被逮捕

1960-12-18
第5版()
专栏：

    尼泊尔国王解散内阁和议会
    柯伊拉腊首相和下院议长等被逮捕
    新华社17日讯　加德满都消息：尼泊尔国王马亨德拉在15日解散了以柯伊拉腊为首的尼泊尔大会党内阁以及议会上下两院，亲自执掌了这个国家的全部行政权。
    消息说，柯伊拉腊首相已经在当天被逮捕。同时被捕的还有内政大臣、交通大臣、农粮大臣、下议院议长和尼泊尔大会党总书记等。
    马亨德拉国王在通过电台广播的一项公告中，宣布尼泊尔“将坚持我们的中立原则，并且将保持同一切友好国家互相亲善和友好的政策。”
    柯伊拉腊首相是在尼泊尔大会党在去年2月底到4月中这个国家举行的第一次大选中获胜后，由国王在去年5月18日授命组阁的。
