Title: Chinese Embassy in the United Arab Republic Holds Reception Welcoming Algerian Art Troupe
Author:
Date: 1960-12-08
Page: 6
Xinhua News Agency, Cairo, December 5th. The Cultural Office of the Chinese Embassy in the United Arab Republic held a reception here tonight to welcome the Algerian Art Troupe, which had just arrived here after visiting China. Belhoussine, Deputy Secretary-General of the Provisional Government of the Republic of Algeria, attended the reception.

Chen Jiakang, Chinese Ambassador to the United Arab Republic, and other officials from the embassy also attended the reception.

The reception was held in a warm and friendly atmosphere. The hosts and guests frequently toasted the friendship between the people of China and Algeria.

### 我驻阿联大使馆举行招待会  欢迎阿尔及利亚艺术团

1960-12-08
第6版()
专栏：

    我驻阿联大使馆举行招待会
    欢迎阿尔及利亚艺术团
    新华社开罗5日电　中国驻阿联大使馆文化处今晚在这里举行招待会，欢迎访问中国后刚来到这里的阿尔及利亚艺术团。阿尔及利亚共和国临时政府副秘书长贝勒·侯赛因出席了招待会。
    中国驻阿联大使陈家康和大使馆的其他官员也出席了招待会。
    招待会是在热烈友好的气氛中进行的。宾主双方频频为中国和阿尔及利亚两国人民的友谊干杯。
