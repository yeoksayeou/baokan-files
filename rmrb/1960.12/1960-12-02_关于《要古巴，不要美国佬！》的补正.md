Title: Correction Regarding "Cuba Si, Yankee No!"
Author: Zhang Zhixiang
Date: 1960-12-02
Page: 8

    Correction Regarding "Cuba Si, Yankee No!"
    The poem "Cuba Si, Yankee No!" (author: Zhang Zhixiang), published on page eight of this newspaper on December 1st, omitted the following four stanzas between the fourth and fifth stanzas. We hereby correct this and apologize to the author and readers.
    You run rampant everywhere,
    Waving the flag of the United Nations,
    Treating the whole world as your own,
        You Yankee!
    "Cuba si, Yankee no!"
    Translate it into a thousand languages,
    Let the whole world shout it!
    Roll back from the Caribbean,
    Roll back from the Panama Canal,
    Roll back from Latin America,
      You Yankee!
    Roll back from the Congo and Africa,
    Africa doesn't want old colonialism,
    Nor does it want neo-colonialism,
        You Yankee!

### 关于《要古巴，不要美国佬！》的补正

1960-12-02
第8版()
专栏：

    关于《要古巴，不要美国佬！》的补正
    12月1日本报八版刊登的《要古巴，不要美国佬！》（作者张致祥）一诗，在第四小节与第五小节之间，漏排以下四节，特补正，并向作者读者致歉。
    你到处横行霸道，
    还打着联合国旗号，
    把全世界看成是你的，
        你这美国佬！
    “cuba si，Yankee no！”
    把它翻成千种语言，
    全世界都来喊啊！
    从加勒比海滚回去，
    从巴拿马运河滚回去，
    从拉丁美洲滚回去，
      你这美国佬！
    从刚果和非洲滚回去，
    非洲不要老殖民主义，
    也不要新殖民主义，
        你这美国佬！
