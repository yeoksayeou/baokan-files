Title: Kim Il-sung Hosts Banquet for Cuban Government Economic Delegation; Delegation Calls on Premier Kim Il-sung; Grand Gathering in Pyongyang Welcomes Cuban Guests
Author:
Date: 1960-12-03
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, Pyongyang, December 2nd: Premier of the Cabinet of North Korea, Kim Il-sung, held a grand banquet in Pyongyang tonight to welcome Major Ernesto "Che" Guevara, President of the National Bank of Cuba, and the Cuban revolutionary Government Economic Delegations led by him.

At 7:30 PM, Major Guevara and all members of the delegation, accompanied by Premier Kim Il-sung and others, entered the brilliantly lit banquet hall, where they were greeted with enthusiastic applause. Subsequently, the banquet commenced with the solemn playing of the national anthems of Cuba and North Korea.

In his address at the banquet, Premier Kim Il-sung extended a warm welcome to the distinguished Cuban guests. He stated that this visit to North Korea by the Cuban guests would make a significant contribution to strengthening the friendship and solidarity between the peoples of North Korea and Cuba. Premier Kim Il-sung emphasized that, in order to oppose American imperialism, the people of North Korea would advance hand-in-hand with the people of Cuba.

In his speech, Major Guevara praised the victory of the North Korean people in the Korean War against American imperialist aggression, and the great achievements they had made in post-war reconstruction.

Heads of diplomatic missions of various countries stationed in North Korea, Chinese Ambassador to North Korea Qiao Xiaoguang, and diplomatic envoys from various countries stationed in North Korea, toasted the Cuban guests at the banquet.

Attending today's banquet were North Korean party and government leaders including Vice-Premiers of the Cabinet Hong Myong-hui, Jeong Il-ryong, Kim Kwang-hyup, and Ri Jong-ok, Vice-Chairman of the Central Committee of the Workers' Party of Korea Park Kum-chol, Standing Committee Member of the Central Committee of the Workers' Party of Korea Rim Hae, heads of various ministries of the Cabinet, heads of social organizations, senior generals of the People's Army, and prominent figures from all walks of life.

Xinhua News Agency, Pyongyang, December 2nd:  The Cuban revolutionary Government Economic Delegation, led by Major Ernesto "Che" Guevara, president of the National Bank of Cuba, who are currently visiting North Korea, paid a courtesy call on Premier Kim Il-sung of the North Korean Cabinet this morning.

All members of the delegation went to pay their respects. Present at the meeting were Vice-Chairman of the Central Committee of the Workers' Party of Korea Park Kum-chol, Vice-Premiers of the Cabinet Jeong Il-ryong and Kim Kwang-hyup, Minister of Foreign Affairs Pak Song-chol, and Minister of Finance Han Sang-du.

Premier Kim Il-sung and Head of Delegation Guevara had a cordial conversation.

Xinhua News Agency, Pyongyang, December 1st: More than 2,000 people from all walks of life in Pyongyang held a grand rally at the Grand Theater tonight, warmly welcoming the representatives of the heroic Cuban people - the President of the National Bank of Cuba, Major Ernesto "Che" Guevara, and the Cuban revolutionary Government Economic Delegation led by him.

Major Guevara and other distinguished Cuban guests, accompanied by Vice-Premier of the North Korean Cabinet Jeong Il-ryong and others, took the stage amidst enthusiastic applause at around 6 p.m. Also on the stage were: Vice-Chairman of the Central Committee of the Workers' Party of Korea Park Kum-chol, Vice-Premiers Kim Kwang-hyup and Ri Ju-yeon, Minister of Finance Han Sang-du, Minister of Foreign Affairs Park Song-chol, Vice-Chairman of the Standing Committee of the Supreme People's Assembly of North Korea Han Seol-ya, Chairman of the Central Committee of the North Korean Democratic Party Kang Ryang-uk, Chairman of the Central Committee of the Chondoist Chongu Party Park Sin-deok, etc. The rally began with the playing of the national anthems of Cuba and North Korea. Vice-Premier Jeong Il-ryong and Major Guevara then delivered speeches at the rally.

### 金日成欢宴古巴政府经济代表团  代表团拜会金日成首相　平壤盛会欢迎古巴贵宾

1960-12-03
第5版()
专栏：

    金日成欢宴古巴政府经济代表团
    代表团拜会金日成首相　平壤盛会欢迎古巴贵宾
    据新华社平壤2日电　朝鲜内阁首相金日成今晚在平壤举行盛大宴会，欢迎古巴国家银行行长埃·切·格瓦拉少校和由他率领的古巴革命政府经济代表团。
    晚七时半，格瓦拉少校和代表团全体团员由金日成首相等陪同进入灯火辉煌的宴会大厅时，全场响起热烈的掌声。接着，宴会在高奏古巴和朝鲜两国国歌声中开始。
    金日成首相在宴会上致词时，对古巴贵宾表示热烈的欢迎。他说，古巴客人们这次对朝鲜的访问，将对加强朝古两国人民的友谊团结作出重大贡献。金日成首相说，为了反对美帝国主义，朝鲜人民将同古巴人民手携手地前进。
    格瓦拉少校在讲话中赞扬了朝鲜人民在反对美帝国主义侵略的朝鲜战争中取得的胜利和在战后恢复建设中取得的伟大成就。
    各国驻朝鲜外交使团团长、中国驻朝鲜大使乔晓光和各国驻朝使节在宴会上向古巴贵宾敬了酒。
    参加今天宴会的有朝鲜内阁副首相洪命熹、郑一龙、金光侠、李钟玉，朝鲜劳动党中央委员会副委员长朴金喆，朝鲜劳动党中央常务委员林海等朝鲜党和政府负责人，内阁各相，社会团体的负责人，人民军高级将领和各界著名人士。
    新华社平壤2日电　正在朝鲜访问的以古巴国家银行行长埃·切·格瓦拉少校为首的古巴革命政府经济代表团，今天早晨拜会了朝鲜内阁首相金日成。
    前往拜会的有代表团全体团员。拜会时在座的，有朝鲜劳动党中央委员会副委员长朴金喆，内阁副首相郑一龙、金光侠，外务相朴成哲，财政相韩相斗。
    金日成首相和格瓦拉团长进行了亲切的谈话。
    据新华社平壤1日电　平壤市各界二千多人今天晚上在大剧场隆重举行大会，热烈欢迎英雄的古巴人民的代表——古巴国家银行行长埃·切·格瓦拉少校和由他率领的古巴革命政府经济代表团。
    格瓦拉少校等古巴贵宾由朝鲜内阁副首相郑一龙等陪同，六时许在热烈的掌声中登上主席台。主席台上还有：朝鲜劳动党中央委员会副委员长朴金喆，副首相金光侠、李周渊，财政相韩相斗，外务相朴成哲，朝鲜最高人民会议常任委员会副委员长韩雪野，北朝鲜民主党中央委员会委员长康良煜，天道教青友党中央委员会委员长朴信德等。大会在奏古巴、朝鲜两国国歌声中开始。郑一龙副首相和格瓦拉少校先后在大会上讲了话。
