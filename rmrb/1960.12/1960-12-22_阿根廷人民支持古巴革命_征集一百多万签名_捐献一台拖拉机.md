Title: Argentine People Support Cuban Revolution, Collect Over One Million Signatures, Donate a Tractor
Author:
Date: 1960-12-22
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, Buenos Aires, December 20th: The Argentine Committee in Support of the Cuban Revolution announced that it has collected 1.1 million signatures in support of the Havana Declaration and raised funds for Cuban farmers to purchase a tractor.

The committee stated that it convened a national congress of organizations participating in the signature and donation campaign and analyzed the progress of the aforementioned campaign. Representatives from eighty organizations supporting the movement attended the congress. These organizations include the Argentine Trade Union Solidarity and Cooperation Movement, the Agricultural Workers' Union, the Argentine University Federation, the Secondary School Student Federation, the Argentine Socialist Party, the Communist Party, and other youth groups.

The congress concluded that 556,000 pesos had been raised and 1.1 million signatures had been collected. The congress elected a delegation to travel to Havana to participate in the Cuban celebration of the anniversary of the overthrow of the Batista dictatorship on January 1, 1961, and will bring the aforementioned signatures and donations.

### 阿根廷人民支持古巴革命  征集一百多万签名　捐献一台拖拉机

1960-12-22
第6版()
专栏：

    阿根廷人民支持古巴革命
    征集一百多万签名　捐献一台拖拉机
    据新华社布宜诺斯艾利斯20日电　阿根廷声援古巴革命委员会宣布，它已征集了一百一十万支持哈瓦那宣言的签名，并为古巴农民募集了购买一台拖拉机的款项。
    委员会说，它召开了参加签名和捐款运动的组织的全国代表大会，并且分析了上述运动的进展情况。参加这次代表大会的有支持这个运动的八十个组织的代表。这些组织包括阿根廷工会团结合作运动、农业工人工会、阿根廷大学生联合会、中学生联合会、阿根廷社会党、共产党以及其他青年团体。
    大会的总结指出，已经募集了五十五万六千比索和征集了一百一十万个签名。大会选出一个代表团以便前往哈瓦那参加1961年1月1日古巴庆祝推翻巴蒂斯塔独裁政权的纪念活动，并将带去上述签名和捐款。
