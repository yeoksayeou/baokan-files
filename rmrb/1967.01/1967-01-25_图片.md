Title: The Heroic Vietnamese People's Love and Admiration for Chairman Mao
Author:
Date: 1967-01-25
Edition: 5

The heroic Vietnamese people have boundless love and admiration for Chairman Mao. The image shows Vietnamese youth hanging a large banner inside a room that reads "Long Live Chairman Mao!" to express their affection for Chairman Mao.
