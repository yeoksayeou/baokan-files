Title: Our Oath
Author:
Date: 1967-01-06
Edition: 6

We dedicate our beautiful youth and our blood to defend the dignity of our motherland and uphold its honor! We—forty-one overseas Chinese youth imprisoned in a foreign land—write this resolute oath with our own blood. We are the overseas children of the Chinese nation, and we are the youth of the era of Chairman Mao. In the past, we fought side by side with the Indonesian people; now, we continue our unyielding struggle in prison. Loving our great motherland is a "crime." Striving for our legal rights is also a "crime." This is the devil's own logic, this is the fascist's "humanity"!

The whip lashes our bodies, fists strike our heads, rifle butts smash our skulls, and gun barrels are pointed at our chests. Even if they use bayonets to rip open our chests, what they will find is only hearts burning with loyalty to our motherland and Chairman Mao! Torture can leave us covered in wounds; beatings can make our blood flow. But it cannot shake our fighting spirit or sway our patriotism!

Motherland! You are our strongest support. To defend your dignity, we are waging a heroic hunger strike. Every time hunger torments us, thinking of you—Chairman Mao—all suffering vanishes. Chairman Mao! You are the brightest red sun in our hearts! We follow your teachings and fight face-to-face with the enemy. Every time our bodies are brutally beaten, thinking of you, all physical pain disappears!

We are undergoing an international class struggle, a bloody test. Under the great, invincible guidance of Mao Zedong Thought, we have become stronger and braver. We stand taller and see farther. Today, in the prison cells of a foreign land, we solemnly vow: "We will dedicate our beautiful youth and our blood to defend the dignity of our motherland and uphold its honor!"

October 13, 1966
In the prison of the Medan Base Command, Indonesia

### 我们的誓言  四十一名华侨青少年在印尼狱中集体创作

1967-01-06
第6版()
专栏：

    我们的誓言
    四十一名华侨青少年在印尼狱中集体创作以自己美丽的青春，用自己的鲜血，誓死保卫祖国的尊严，维护祖国的荣誉！我们——四十一名身在异国囹圄中的“囚
  徒”，用自己身上流下的血，写下了豪迈的誓言。我们是中华民族的海外儿女，我们是毛泽东时代的青少年。过去，我们和印尼人民并肩战斗在一起；如今，我们在狱中坚持着不屈的斗争。热爱自己伟大的祖国，
  有“罪”。争取我们的合法权益，
  也有“罪”。这就是魔鬼的现身说法，这就是法西斯的“人道”逻辑！皮鞭抽在身上，拳头打在头上，枪托砸在脑袋上，枪口对准了胸膛，即使用刺刀来剖开胸腹吧，挖出来的，也只有一颗颗对祖国对毛主席无比忠诚的红心！严刑，可以使人遍体鳞伤；拷打，可以使人鲜血流淌。可是，丝毫也动摇不了我们的战斗意志，撼动不了我们的爱国立场！祖国啊！你是我们最强大的靠山，为了维护你的尊严，我们在作着英勇的绝食斗争。每当饥饿的痛苦在不断折磨我们时，想起了您——毛主席，一切苦难都不复存在。毛主席啊！您是我们心中最红最红的红太阳！我们遵循您的教导，与敌人进行面对面的斗争。每当我们的躯体横遭毒打时，想起了您，我们肉体上的痛苦就消失净尽！我们正在经受一场国际阶级斗争，我们正在经受一场鲜血淋淋的考验。在伟大的、战无不胜的毛泽东思想
  指导下，我们变得更坚强，更勇敢了，我们站得更高，看得更远了。今天，在异国的囚室里，我们庄严宣誓：“我们将以自己美丽的青春，用自己的鲜血，誓死保卫祖国的尊严，维护祖国的荣誉！”
              一九六六年十月十三日于印尼
                  棉兰基地司令部狱中
