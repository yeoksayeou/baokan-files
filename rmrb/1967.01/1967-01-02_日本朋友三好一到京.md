Title: Japanese Friend Sanzo Ichiro Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1967-01-02
Edition: 4

At the invitation of the China-Japan Friendship Association, Sanzo Ichiro, Director of the General Office of the Headquarters of the Japan-China Friendship Association (Orthodox), arrived in Beijing by plane on the 30th.

### 日本朋友三好一到京

1967-01-02
第4版()
专栏：

    日本朋友三好一到京
    新华社三十一日讯　应中日友好协会邀请前来我国访问的日中友好协会（正统）总部事务局长三好一，三十日乘飞机到达北京。
