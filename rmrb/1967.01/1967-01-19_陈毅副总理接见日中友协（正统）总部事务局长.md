Title: Vice Premier Chen Yi Meets with the Director of the General Affairs Bureau of the Japan-China Friendship Association (Orthodox)
Author:
Date: 1967-01-19
Edition: 6

Vice Premier Chen Yi met with the Director of the General Affairs Bureau of the Japan-China Friendship Association (Orthodox) today in the afternoon. He had a cordial and friendly conversation with him.

Japanese peace activist Nishisonji Koichi, who is in Beijing, was also present.

	January 18, Vice Premier Chen Yi met with the Director of the General Affairs Bureau of the Japan-China Friendship Association (Orthodox), Miyoshi Kazuyoshi (second from the right).

### 陈毅副总理接见日中友协（正统）总部事务局长

1967-01-19
第6版()
专栏：

    陈毅副总理接见日中友协（正统）总部事务局长
    新华社十八日讯　陈毅副总理今天下午接见日中友好协会（正统）总部事务局长三好一，同他进行了亲切友好的谈话。
    在北京的日本和平人士西园寺公一也在座。
    （附图片）
    一月十八日，陈毅副总理接见日中友好协会（正统）总部事务局长三好一（右二）。
