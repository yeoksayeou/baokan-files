Title: Vice Premier Chen Yi Meets with Lao Friends
Author:
Date: 1967-01-31
Edition: 5

Vice Premier Chen Yi met with Lao friends today. The New China News Agency reported on the 30th that Vice Premier Chen Yi met with Vice Chairman of the Central Committee of the Lao Patriotic Front, Sithon Kommadam, and Central Committee member Sisana Sisane this afternoon and had a cordial conversation with them.

Present at the meeting were Vice Minister of Foreign Affairs Han Nianlong and others.

After the meeting, Vice Premier Chen Yi hosted a banquet to entertain the Lao friends.

### 陈毅副总理接见老挝朋友

1967-01-31
第5版()
专栏：

　　陈毅副总理接见老挝朋友
    新华社三十日讯　陈毅副总理今天下午接见老挝爱国战线党中央委员会副主席西吞·库马丹、中央委员西萨纳·西山，同他们进行了亲切的谈话。
    接见时在座的，有外交部副部长韩念龙等。
    接见后，陈毅副总理设宴招待老挝朋友。
