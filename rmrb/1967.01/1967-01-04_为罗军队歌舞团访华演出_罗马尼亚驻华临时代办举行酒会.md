Title: Reception for the Romanian Military Song and Dance Troupe's Visit to China
Author:
Date: 1967-01-04
Edition: 5

	The chargé d'affaires of the Romanian People's Republic Embassy in China, Istrade, held a reception tonight for the Romanian military song and dance troupe's visit to China for performances.

	Zhang Qixiao, Qiao Guanhua, Peng Shaohui, Yuan Ziqin, Zhou Yiping, Jia Ruoyu, and Pan Zhenwu, among other relevant officials, were invited to attend the reception.

	The military attaché of the Romanian Embassy in China, Colonel Sorescu, and the deputy director of the Cultural Department of the General Political Department of the Chinese People's Liberation Army, Yu Ji, both spoke at the reception. They both expressed their belief that the Romanian military song and dance troupe's visit to China for performances would make a beneficial contribution to strengthening the friendship between the peoples and the armies of China and Romania.

### 为罗军队歌舞团访华演出  罗马尼亚驻华临时代办举行酒会

1967-01-04
第5版()
专栏：

    为罗军队歌舞团访华演出
    罗马尼亚驻华临时代办举行酒会
    新华社三日讯　罗马尼亚社会主义共和国驻我国大使馆临时代办伊斯特拉代，今晚为罗马尼亚军队歌舞团访华演出举行酒会。
    张奚若、乔冠华、彭绍辉、袁子钦、周一萍、贾若瑜、潘振武等有关方面负责人应邀出席了酒会。
    罗马尼亚驻我国大使馆武官索列斯库上校和中国人民解放军总政治部文化部副部长虞棘，先后在酒会上讲话。他们在讲话中一致表示深信，通过罗马尼亚军队歌舞团这次访华演出，对加强中罗两国人民、两国军队之间的友谊，做出了有益的贡献。
