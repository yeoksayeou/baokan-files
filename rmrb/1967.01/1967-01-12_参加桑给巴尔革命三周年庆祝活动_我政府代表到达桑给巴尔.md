Title: Participating in the Third Anniversary Celebration of the Zanzibar Revolution
Author:
Date: 1967-01-12
Edition: 5

The government representative of the People's Republic of China, Chinese Ambassador to Tanzania He Ying, arrived here by plane on the 10th. He was invited by the Zanzibar government of Tanzania to participate in the celebration of the third anniversary of the Zanzibar Revolution (January 12th).

The representative of the Chinese People's Liberation Army, Zhou De-li, who was also invited to participate in the celebration, arrived on the same plane.

Those who welcomed them at the airport included Zanzibar Protocol Chief Ahmed Maulid, Chief of Staff of the Tanzanian People's Defense Force Ali Mahfuz, and Chinese Consul to Zanzibar Liu Gan.

The Chinese trade union representative Gao Guang, women's representative Xu Ke-li, and youth representative Liao Chujiang, who were invited by the Zanzibar trade unions, women's organizations, and youth groups, arrived in Zanzibar on the 9th.

### 参加桑给巴尔革命三周年庆祝活动  我政府代表到达桑给巴尔

1967-01-12
第5版()
专栏：

    参加桑给巴尔革命三周年庆祝活动
    我政府代表到达桑给巴尔
    新华社桑给巴尔十日电　中华人民共和国政府代表、中国驻坦桑尼亚大使何英十日乘飞机到达这里。他是应坦桑尼亚桑给巴尔政府的邀请，前来参加桑给巴尔革命三周年（一月十二日）的庆祝活动的。
    应邀前来参加庆祝活动的中国人民解放军代表周德礼也同机到达。
    在机场欢迎他们的有桑给巴尔礼宾局局长艾哈迈德·毛利德，在桑给巴尔的坦桑尼亚人民国防军总参谋长阿里·马赫福兹和中国驻桑给巴尔领事刘干。
    中国的工会代表高光、妇女代表徐克立和青年代表廖初江应桑给巴尔工会、妇女和青年的邀请，已在九日到达桑给巴尔。
