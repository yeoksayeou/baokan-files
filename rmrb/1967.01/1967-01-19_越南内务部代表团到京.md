Title: Vietnamese Ministry of Interior Delegation Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1967-01-19
Edition: 6

A delegation from the Ministry of Interior of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam, led by Minister Ung Van Khien, arrived in Beijing today by airplane at the invitation of the Chinese Ministry of Interior. Relevant officials from our country and Charge d'Affaires Ly Chung Chi of the Vietnamese Embassy in China were at the airport to welcome them.

### 越南内务部代表团到京

1967-01-19
第6版()
专栏：

    越南内务部代表团到京
    新华社十八日讯　由越南民主共和国内务部长雍文谦率领的越南内务部代表团，应我国内务部的邀请，今天乘飞机到达北京。我国有关方面负责人和越南驻中国大使馆临时代办黎终始，到机场欢迎。
