Title: The Albanian National Song and Dance Ensemble Performs for PLA Soldiers and Officers
Author:
Date: 1967-01-17
Edition: 4

The Albanian National Song and Dance Ensemble, coming from the frontline of the anti-imperialist and anti-revisionist struggle in Europe, performed tonight in the capital for the soldiers and officers of the Chinese People's Liberation Army (PLA) Navy, Army, and Air Force, and was warmly received.

Members of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of China (CPC), including Political Bureau member and Vice Chairman of the National Defense Council Ye Jianying, Central Committee member and Director of the General Political Department of the PLA Xiao Hua, and Alternate Central Committee member and Acting Chief of the General Staff of the PLA Yang Chengwu, attended the performance. Also present were Deputy Chief of the General Staff of the PLA Peng Shaohui and Deputy Director of the General Political Department of the PLA Yuan Ziqin.

Bekir Balluku, member of the Political Bureau of the Central Committee of the Party of Labor of Albania and Deputy Prime Minister and Minister of Defense, and Hito Cako, member of the Central Committee of the Party of Labor of Albania and Director of the Political Department of the Albanian People's Army, who are currently visiting China, along with members of the Albanian military delegation they lead, and Natanaeli, Albanian Ambassador to China, also attended the performance.

When the Albanian comrades arrived at the theater escorted by Ye Jianying and others, the soldiers and officers enthusiastically applauded, waved copies of "Quotations from Chairman Mao," and shouted slogans such as "Long live Chairman Mao!" "Long live Comrade Enver Hoxha!" and "Long live the unbreakable fighting friendship between the peoples of China and Albania!"

The Albanian National Song and Dance Ensemble performed more than 20 songs and dances full of revolutionary spirit. When the Albanian artists sang in praise of the Party of Labor of Albania and Comrade Enver Hoxha, and our most beloved great leader Chairman Mao, the theater was filled with joy and prolonged applause.

After the performance, Ye Jianying, Xiao Hua, Yang Chengwu, and others, along with Balluku and Cako, went on stage and shook hands with the performers, congratulating them on their successful performance. At that moment, shouts of "Mao Zedong—Enver" and "Enver—Mao Zedong" echoed throughout the venue.

### 阿国家歌舞团为解放军指战员演出  叶剑英萧华杨成武和巴卢库等同志出席观看

1967-01-17
第4版()
专栏：

    阿国家歌舞团为解放军指战员演出
    叶剑英萧华杨成武和巴卢库等同志出席观看
    新华社十六日讯　来自欧洲反帝反修斗争最前线的阿尔巴尼亚国家歌舞团，今晚在首都为中国人民解放军陆海空三军指战员演出，受到热烈欢迎。
    中共中央政治局委员、国防委员会副主席叶剑英，中共中央委员、中国人民解放军总政治部主任萧华，中共中央候补委员、中国人民解放军代总参谋长杨成武，中国人民解放军副总参谋长彭绍辉，中国人民解放军总政治部副主任袁子钦等，观看了演出。
    正在我国访问的阿尔巴尼亚劳动党中央政治局委员、部长会议副主席兼国防部长贝基尔·巴卢库，阿尔巴尼亚劳动党中央委员、阿尔巴尼亚人民军政治部主任希托·恰科，以及由他们率领的阿尔巴尼亚军事代表团的成员，阿尔巴尼亚驻中国大使纳塔奈利，也出席观看表演。
    当阿尔巴尼亚同志们在叶剑英等同志陪同下来到剧场时，全体指战员热烈鼓掌，高举《毛主席语录》，连声高呼“毛主席万岁！”“恩维尔·霍查同志万岁！”“中阿两国人民牢不可破的战斗友谊万岁！”
    阿尔巴尼亚国家歌舞团今晚演出了二十多个充满革命激情的歌舞。当阿尔巴尼亚文艺工作者放声歌颂阿尔巴尼亚劳动党和恩维尔·霍查同志，歌颂我们最最敬爱的伟大领袖毛主席的时候，剧场一片欢腾，长时间热烈鼓掌。
    演出结束后，叶剑英、萧华、杨成武等同志和巴卢库、恰科等同志走上舞台，同演员亲切握手，祝贺他们演出成功。这时，“毛泽东—恩维尔”“恩维尔—毛泽东”的欢呼声响彻全场。
