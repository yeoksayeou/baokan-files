Title: Vice Premier Chen Yi Receives Delegation of the Chinese Welding Society
Author:
Date: 1967-01-09
Edition: 5

Vice Premier Chen Yi, who is also the Minister of Foreign Affairs, met with the entire delegation of the Chinese Welding Society on the evening of the 7th. The delegation had been unjustly prevented from returning home by the Dutch government for an extended period while attending the 19th Annual Meeting of the International Institute of Welding. Chen Yi had a cordial conversation with them, expressing his concern and consolation.

Vice Premier Chen Yi praised the delegation for their firm stance and resolute struggle against the Dutch government's unjust persecution. Chen Yi encouraged them to continue to hold high the great red banner of Mao Zedong Thought, to study and apply Chairman Mao's works actively, and to participate enthusiastically in the Great Proletarian Cultural Revolution.

### 陈毅副总理接见我焊接学会代表团团员  赞扬代表团在反对荷兰政府无理迫害中的坚定立场  勉励团员活学活用毛主席著作积极参加文化大革命

1967-01-09
第5版()
专栏：

    陈毅副总理接见我焊接学会代表团团员
    赞扬代表团在反对荷兰政府无理迫害中的坚定立场  勉励团员活学活用毛主席著作积极参加文化大革命
    据新华社八日讯　国务院副总理兼外交部长陈毅，七日晚间接见了被荷兰政府长期无理阻挠回国的我国出席国际焊接学会第十九届年会的中国焊接学会代表团全体团员，同他们进行了亲切的谈话，表示了关怀和慰问。陈毅副总理赞扬代表团在反对荷兰政府的无理迫害中表现了坚定的立场，进行了坚决的斗争。陈毅副总理勉励他们今后要更高地举起毛泽东思想伟大红旗，活学活用毛主席著作，积极参加无产阶级文化大革命。
