Title: The Book "Seize the Revolution, Promote Production, Thoroughly Smash the New Counterattack of the Bourgeois Reactionary Line" Published
Author:
Date: 1967-01-25
Edition: 2

    The book "Seize the Revolution, Promote Production, Thoroughly Smash the New Counterattack of the Bourgeois Reactionary Line" has been published. The New China News Agency reported on the 24th that the "Letter to the People of Shanghai" and the "Urgent Notice" issued by various revolutionary rebel organizations in Shanghai, which were decided by our great leader Chairman Mao to be broadcast nationwide, as well as the congratulatory telegram from the Central Committee of the Communist Party of China, the State Council, the Central Military Commission, and the Central Cultural Revolution Group to various revolutionary rebel groups in Shanghai, and the eleven important editorials and commentaries published in the "People's Daily" and "Red Flag" magazine, have been compiled by the People's Publishing House into the book "Seize the Revolution, Promote Production, Thoroughly Smash the New Counterattack of the Bourgeois Reactionary Line." This book will soon be distributed in the capital and various places across the country.

### 《抓革命，促生产，彻底粉碎资产阶级反动路线的新反扑》一书出版

1967-01-25
第2版()
专栏：

　　《抓革命，促生产，彻底粉碎资产阶级反动路线的新反扑》一书出版
    新华社二十四日讯　我们伟大领袖毛主席亲自决定向全国广播的上海各革命造反组织发表的《告上海全市人民书》和《紧急通告》，中共中央、国务院、中央军委、中央文革小组给上海市各革命造反团体的贺电，以及《人民日报》《红旗》杂志发表的有关重要社论和评论员文章共十一篇，已由人民出版社编成《抓革命，促生产，彻底粉碎资产阶级反动路线的新反扑》一书出版。这本书即将在首都和全国各地陆续发行。
