Title: Comrade Liu Changsheng Passes Away
Author:
Date: 1967-01-29
Edition: 2

Comrade Liu Changsheng, member of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of China, member of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress, vice-chairman of the All-China Federation of Trade Unions, and president of the China-Africa Friendship Association, passed away on January 20 due to illness. He was 64 years old.

### 刘长胜同志逝世

1967-01-29
第2版()
专栏：

　　刘长胜同志逝世
    新华社二十八日讯　中共中央委员、人大常委会委员、中华全国总工会副主任、中非友好协会会长刘长胜同志，于一月二十日因病逝世，年六十四岁。
