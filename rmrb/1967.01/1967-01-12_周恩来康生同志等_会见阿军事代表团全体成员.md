Title: Premier Zhou Enlai and Comrade Kang Sheng Meet with the Entire Albanian Military Delegation
Author:
Date: 1967-01-12
Edition: 3

Party and state leaders Zhou Enlai, Kang Sheng, Li Xiannian, and Ye Jianying held a reception this evening for Albanian Labor Party Central Committee Political Bureau member, Vice Premier and Minister of Defense Bekir Balluku, and Labor Party Central Committee member, Chief of the Political Department of the Albanian People's Army Hito Cako, along with the entire Albanian military delegation led by them. The hosts and guests had a very warm and friendly conversation.

Present at the reception were Xiao Hua, Yang Chengwu, Wang Shusheng, Peng Shaohui, Li Tianyou, Wang Xinting, Xu Licheng, Qiao Guanhua, and Chu Tu-nan, among other relevant officials.

Albanian Ambassador to China Vasili Nataneli was also present at the reception.

	January 11, Party and state leaders Zhou Enlai, Kang Sheng, Li Xiannian, and Ye Jianying met with Albanian Labor Party Central Committee Political Bureau member, Vice Premier and Minister of Defense Bekir Balluku, and Labor Party Central Committee member, Chief of the Political Department of the Albanian People's Army Hito Cako, along with the entire Albanian military delegation led by them. The photo shows a group photo taken during the reception.

### 周恩来康生同志等  会见阿军事代表团全体成员

1967-01-12
第3版()
专栏：

    周恩来康生同志等
    会见阿军事代表团全体成员
    新华社十一日讯　党和国家领导同志周恩来、康生、李先念、叶剑英，今晚会见了阿尔巴尼亚劳动党中央政治局委员、部长会议副主席兼国防部长贝基尔·巴卢库和劳动党中央委员、阿尔巴尼亚人民军政治部主任希托·恰科以及由他们率领的阿尔巴尼亚军事代表团全体成员。宾主进行了极其亲切友好的交谈。
    会见时在座的，有萧华、杨成武、王树声、彭绍辉、李天佑、王新亭、徐立清、乔冠华、楚图南等有关方面的负责人。
    会见时，阿尔巴尼亚驻中国大使瓦西里·纳塔奈利也在座。
    （附图片）
    一月十一日，党和国家领导同志周恩来、康生、李先念、叶剑英会见阿尔巴尼亚劳动党中央政治局委员、部长会议副主席兼国防部长贝基尔·巴卢库和劳动党中央委员、阿尔巴尼亚人民军政治部主任希托·恰科以及由他们率领的阿尔巴尼亚军事代表团全体成员。图为会见时合影。
