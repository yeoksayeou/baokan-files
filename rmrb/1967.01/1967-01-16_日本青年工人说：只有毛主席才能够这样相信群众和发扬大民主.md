Title: A Japanese Young Worker Says: Only Chairman Mao Can Trust the Masses and Promote Great Democracy
Author:
Date: 1967-01-16
Edition: 4

A young worker from Tokyo recently spoke to a Chinese reporter, stating that the Great Proletarian Cultural Revolution in China is a necessary revolution to prevent capitalist restoration, maintain proletarian dictatorship, and consolidate the proletarian dictatorship in a socialist country. This is a great victory for Mao Zedong Thought.

He said regarding the great democracy under the dictatorship of the proletariat, "Only Chairman Mao can trust the masses so much, fully mobilize the masses, and promote great democracy, thereby sweeping away the old culture, old ideas, old customs, and old habits that exist in China. The modern revisionist clique in the Soviet Union will not and dare not promote such great democracy among the Soviet people."

Recently, a member of the Japan Art Academy and renowned scholar, Toshiki Zenjo, wrote in the monthly publication *Nichi-Chu Bunka Koryu* of the Japan-China Cultural Exchange Association that the Great Proletarian Cultural Revolution in China "has achieved great and unprecedented results in world history." He said, "The 700 million Chinese people, nurtured by Mao Zedong Thought, are rising up and actively striving for socialist construction. All of this shows an immeasurable force."

### 日本青年工人说：只有毛主席才能够这样相信群众和发扬大民主

1967-01-16
第4版()
专栏：

    日本青年工人说：
    只有毛主席才能够这样相信群众和发扬大民主
    东京的一位青年工人最近对中国记者发表谈话指出，中国无产阶级文化大革命是社会主义国家防止资本主义复辟，保持无产阶级政权，巩固无产阶级专政所必需的一次大革命。这是毛泽东思想的伟大胜利。
    他在谈到无产阶级专政下的大民主时说，只有毛主席才能这样相信群众，充分发动群众，发扬大民主，从而扫除了存在于中国的旧文化、旧思想、旧风俗、旧习惯。苏联现代修正主义集团不会而且也不敢在苏联人民中间发扬这样的大民主。
    日本艺术院会员、著名学者土歧善麿最近在日中文化交流协会的月刊《日中文化交流》上写道，中国无产阶级文化大革命“获得了在世界历史上值得大书特书的伟大成果”。他说：“七亿中国人民在毛泽东思想哺育下，正在奋起，积极地为社会主义建设而努力，这一切使人们看到不可估计的力量。”
