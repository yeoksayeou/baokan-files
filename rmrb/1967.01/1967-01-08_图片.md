Title: Beijing's Large Timber Factories Produce Economical and Practical Furniture
Author:
Date: 1967-01-08
Edition: 2

This is some economical and practical furniture produced by large timber factories in Beijing using scraps that were previously only used as firewood. The price of this furniture is about half that of ordinary furniture, and it is sturdy and durable, making it very popular with customers.
