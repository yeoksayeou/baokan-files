Title: Shanghai Port Bureau's Fifth District Revolutionary Rebels Uphold Chairman Mao's Directive
Author:
Date: 1967-01-17
Edition: 1

The revolutionary rebels of the Fifth District of the Shanghai Port Bureau, holding high the banner of Chairman Mao's directive to "grasp revolution, promote production," have steadfastly remained at their production posts and foiled a plot by a handful of capitalist-roaders in power to halt port operations.
