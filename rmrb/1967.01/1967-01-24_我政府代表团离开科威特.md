Title: Chinese Government Delegation Leaves Kuwait
Author:
Date: 1967-01-24
Edition: 5

The Chinese government delegation, led by China's Ambassador to Iraq, Cao Chi, left Kuwait by plane on the 20th after presiding over the opening ceremony of the Chinese Economic and Trade Exhibition. During their visit, Kuwait's Prime Minister Jaber Al-Ahmad Al-Jaber Al-Sabah and Minister of Commerce Abdullah Jaber received the delegation separately.

### 我政府代表团离开科威特

1967-01-24
第5版()
专栏：

　　我政府代表团离开科威特
    新华社科威特二十日电　由中国驻伊拉克大使曹痴率领的中国政府代表团在主持了中国经济贸易展览会的开幕式后，二十日乘飞机离开这里。在访问期间，科威特首相贾比尔·艾哈迈德·贾比尔·萨巴赫和工商大臣阿卜杜拉·贾比尔曾分别接见了代表团。
