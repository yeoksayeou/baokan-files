Title: The Albanian National Song and Dance Ensemble Returns to Beijing
Author:
Date: 1967-01-10
Edition: 6

The Albanian National Song and Dance Ensemble, led by Ba·Peyo, returned to Beijing by special train this morning after their visit and performances in Taiyuan. Vice Minister of the Foreign Cultural Liaison Committee Zhou Yiping and others welcomed them at the station. The Albanian anti-imperialist and anti-revisionist cultural comrades will continue their visit and performances in Beijing.

According to a report from Taiyuan by Xinhua News Agency on the 7th, the Albanian National Song and Dance Ensemble, who were warmly welcomed by the people of Taiyuan, held their final performance in Taiyuan on the evening of the 7th.

The cultural comrades from the anti-imperialist and anti-revisionist front performed three times in Taiyuan, attracting more than twenty thousand Red Guards, workers, cadres, PLA soldiers, and revolutionary students to watch their passionate and excellent performances.

When the members of the Albanian National Song and Dance Ensemble visited the Taiyuan Weaving Machinery Factory on the 6th, they were warmly welcomed by more than ten thousand workers and their families from this factory and nearby factories. Ba·Peyo, the leader of the ensemble, warmly praised China's Great Proletarian Cultural Revolution to the welcoming crowd. He said: Your Great Proletarian Cultural Revolution is being carried out under the correct guidance of Marxism-Leninism thought of Chairman Mao. Its influence is expanding. You have already achieved great victories, and your victories are our victories. At the welcoming rally attended by more than ten thousand people, the Albanian comrades were invited to perform many excellent songs and dances.

### 阿尔巴尼亚国家歌舞团回到北京  在太原演出时受到两万多观众热烈欢迎

1967-01-10
第6版()
专栏：

    阿尔巴尼亚国家歌舞团回到北京
    在太原演出时受到两万多观众热烈欢迎
    新华社九日讯　由巴·佩约率领的阿尔巴尼亚国家歌舞团在太原访问演出以后，今天上午乘专车回到北京。对外文委副主任周一萍等到车站迎接。阿尔巴尼亚反帝反修文艺战友们将在北京继续访问演出。
    据新华社太原七日电　深受太原人民热烈欢迎的阿尔巴尼亚国家歌舞团，七日晚在太原举行了最后一场演出。
    来自反帝反修前线的文艺战友们在太原共演出了三场，有两万多红卫兵小将、工人、机关干部、解放军指战员和革命师生，观看了他们充满着革命激情的精采表演。
    阿尔巴尼亚国家歌舞团的同志们六日到太原经纬纺织机械厂参观时，受到这个工厂和附近工厂上万名职工及家属的热烈欢迎。歌舞团团长巴·佩约对欢迎群众热烈地赞扬了中国的无产阶级文化大革命。他说：你们的无产阶级文化大革命，是在毛主席的正确的马克思列宁主义思想指导下进行的。它的影响正在扩大。你们已经取得伟大胜利，你们的胜利就是我们的胜利。在有上万人参加的欢迎大会上，阿尔巴尼亚同志应邀演出了许多精采的歌舞节目。
