Title: Mao Zedong Thought is the Red Sun in the Hearts of the World's Revolutionary People
Author: Shandong Cartoon Group
Date: 1967-01-04
Edition: 4

The Shandong Cartoon Group has created a powerful and inspiring cartoon that captures the essence of Mao Zedong Thought. This artwork vividly depicts Mao Zedong Thought as the guiding light for the world's revolutionary people. The cartoon illustrates how Mao Zedong Thought illuminates the path forward for the oppressed and the exploited, providing them with the strength and courage to fight against imperialism, revisionism, and all forms of reactionary forces.

The cartoon features a radiant sun, symbolizing Mao Zedong Thought, shining brightly over a landscape filled with people from various countries and backgrounds. These individuals, representing the global revolutionary movement, are united in their struggle for liberation and justice. The sun's rays extend far and wide, reaching every corner of the world, signifying the universal applicability and relevance of Mao Zedong Thought.

The artwork emphasizes the profound impact of Mao Zedong Thought on the international revolutionary movement. It highlights how Mao Zedong Thought has inspired and guided countless people in their fight against oppression and injustice. The cartoon serves as a call to action, urging revolutionary people around the world to embrace Mao Zedong Thought and use it as a powerful weapon in their struggle for a better future.

The Shandong Cartoon Group's creation is a testament to the enduring influence of Mao Zedong Thought. It stands as a beacon of hope and inspiration for all those who seek to challenge the status quo and strive for a more just and equitable world. The cartoon is a visual representation of the unwavering commitment of the world's revolutionary people to the principles and teachings of Mao Zedong Thought.
