Title: The Campaign to Carry the Proletarian Cultural Revolution Through to the End Begins Today
Author:
Date: 1967-01-08
Edition: 2

To meet the needs of a wide readership for study, the People's Publishing House has published the book "The Campaign to Carry the Proletarian Cultural Revolution Through to the End." The contents include: a portrait of the great teacher, great leader, great supreme commander, and great helmsman Chairman Mao, and a photograph of Chairman Mao with his close comrade-in-arms Comrade Lin Biao, the New Year's editorial from the People's Daily "The Campaign to Carry the Proletarian Cultural Revolution Through to the End," the New Year's editorial from the Liberation Army Daily "Raise the great red banner of Mao Zedong Thought higher, push the mass movement of studying and applying Chairman Mao's works to a new stage, and make our army truly a great school of Mao Zedong Thought," and the New Year's editorial from the Guangming Daily "Intellectuals unite with the workers and peasants, and carry the cultural revolution through to the end."

This book will be distributed in Beijing starting from the 8th and will be distributed nationwide in the coming days.

### 《把无产阶级文化大革命进行到底》一书今日开始发行

1967-01-08
第2版()
专栏：

    《把无产阶级文化大革命进行到底》一书今日开始发行
    新华社七日讯　为了满足广大读者学习的需要，人民出版社出版了《把无产阶级文化大革命进行到底》一书。内容包括：伟大的导师、伟大的领袖、伟大的统帅、伟大的舵手毛主席像和毛主席同他的亲密战友林彪同志在一起的照片各一幅，《人民日报》《红旗》杂志元旦社论《把无产阶级文化大革命进行到底》，《解放军报》元旦社论《更高地举起毛泽东思想伟大红旗把活学活用毛主席著作群众运动推向新阶段使我军真正成为毛泽东思想的大学校》，以及《光明日报》元旦社论《知识分子同工农群众相结合，把文化大革命进行到底》等文。
    这本书从八日起在北京发行，并将在全国各地陆续发行。
