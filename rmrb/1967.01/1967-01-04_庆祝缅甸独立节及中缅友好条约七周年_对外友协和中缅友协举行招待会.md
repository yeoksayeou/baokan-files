Title: Celebrating Myanmar's Independence Day and the Seventh Anniversary of the China-Myanmar Friendship and Non-Aggression Treaty
Author:
Date: 1967-01-04
Edition: 5

The China International Friendship Association and the China-Myanmar Friendship Association held a reception this afternoon to celebrate the 19th anniversary of Myanmar's independence and the seventh anniversary of the signing of the China-Myanmar Friendship and Non-Aggression Treaty. Chu Tu-nan, president of the China International Friendship Association, and U Tha Tun, Myanmar's ambassador to China, both spoke at the reception. They wished for the continuous growth of friendly cooperation and cultural exchanges between China and Myanmar, and for the further strengthening and development of the fraternal friendship between the peoples of the two countries.

Relevant officials Han Nianlong and Shi Huibing attended the reception.

### 庆祝缅甸独立节及中缅友好条约七周年  对外友协和中缅友协举行招待会

1967-01-04
第5版()
专栏：

    庆祝缅甸独立节及中缅友好条约七周年
    对外友协和中缅友协举行招待会
    新华社三日讯　对外友协和中缅友协今天下午举行招待会，庆祝缅甸联邦独立十九周年及中缅友好和互不侵犯条约签订七周年。对外友协会长楚图南和缅甸驻中国大使沙马杜瓦·信瓦瑙先后在招待会上讲话。他们祝愿中缅两国的友好合作关系和文化交流与日俱增，两国人民的胞波友谊进一步巩固、发展。
    有关方面负责人韩念龙、史怀璧出席了招待会。
