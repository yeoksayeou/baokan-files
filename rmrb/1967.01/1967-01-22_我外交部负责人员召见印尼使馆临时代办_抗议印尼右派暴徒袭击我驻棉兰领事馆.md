Title: Chinese Foreign Ministry Official Summons Indonesian Chargé d'Affaires to Protest Attack on Chinese Consulate in Medan
Author:
Date: 1967-01-22
Edition: 6

The Chinese Foreign Ministry official summoned the Chargé d'Affaires of the Indonesian Embassy on January 21 to protest the attack on the Chinese Consulate in Medan by Indonesian right-wing thugs.

New China News Agency, January 21: A responsible official of China's Foreign Ministry urgently summoned the Chargé d'Affaires of the Indonesian Embassy in China on January 21 to protest the attack on the Chinese Consulate in Medan by Indonesian right-wing thugs on January 18.

The Chinese Foreign Ministry official pointed out that this incident is particularly serious as it occurred just as China's third repatriation ship was about to depart for Medan. The official demanded that the Indonesian government immediately take effective measures to stop similar incidents, ensure the safety of Chinese consular officials and the consulate, allow Chinese consular officials to perform their duties normally, and ensure the smooth progress of China's third repatriation operation.

### 我外交部负责人员召见印尼使馆临时代办　抗议印尼右派暴徒袭击我驻棉兰领事馆

1967-01-22
第6版()
专栏：

　　我外交部负责人员召见印尼使馆临时代办
　　抗议印尼右派暴徒袭击我驻棉兰领事馆
    新华社二十一日讯　我国外交部负责人员一月二十一日紧急召见印度尼西亚驻华大使馆临时代办，就我国驻棉兰领事馆于一月十八日遭受印尼右派暴徒袭击事，提出抗议。
    我国外交部负责人员指出，正当我国第三次接侨船只业已启航，不久即可到达棉兰的时候，发生这一事件的性质是特别严重的，要求印尼政府立即采取有效措施制止类似事件的发生，保证我国领事和领事馆的安全，保证我国领事能够正常履行自己的职务，保证我国第三次接侨工作的顺利进行。
