Title: Premier Zhou Enlai and Other Leaders Receive the Romanian Army Song and Dance Troupe and Watch Their Performance
Author:
Date: 1967-01-03
Edition: 1

Premier Zhou Enlai, Marshal Zhu De, Vice Chairman Xiao Hua, and Vice Chief of Staff Yang Chengwu, among other leaders, received the leaders and some members of the Romanian People's Republic Army Song and Dance Troupe this evening. They had a cordial and friendly conversation.

Present at the reception were Xu Licheng, Zhang Zhaoru, Qiao Guanhua, and others.

The Chargé d'Affaires of the Romanian Embassy in China, Istrade, and Military Attaché Colonel Sorescu were also present.

Premier Zhou Enlai, Marshal Zhu De, Vice Chairman Xiao Hua, and Vice Chief of Staff Yang Chengwu watched the performance of the Romanian People's Republic Army Song and Dance Troupe this evening.

The performance was held at the Great Hall of the People. The hall was adorned with a large banner in both Chinese and Romanian that read, "Long Live the Friendship Between the Peoples of China and Romania." Over ten thousand spectators enthusiastically applauded the Romanian artists' performances, which were rich in national and folk characteristics.

The Chargé d'Affaires of the Romanian Embassy in China, Istrade, and Military Attaché Colonel Sorescu watched the performance.

Also present were officials from various sectors, including Xu Licheng, Zhang Zhaoru, Qiao Guanhua, Wu Faxian, Zhou Xihan, Jia Ruoyu, and Pan Zhenwu.

As the performance concluded, amidst thunderous applause, Premier Zhou Enlai, Marshal Zhu De, and other leaders walked onto the stage, shook hands with the performers, and posed for a group photo.

After the performance, Colonel Apostol Emil, the leader of the Romanian People's Republic Army Song and Dance Troupe, on behalf of the Romanian People's Republic Ministry of National Defense, presented a documentary film about the activities of the Chinese People's Liberation Army Song and Dance Troupe during their 1965 visit to Romania to the Chinese Ministry of National Defense. Deputy Director of the General Political Department of the Chinese People's Liberation Army, Xu Licheng, accepted this meaningful gift on behalf of the Ministry of National Defense. In his speech, Xu Licheng expressed heartfelt thanks to the Romanian Ministry of National Defense and believed that this gift would significantly contribute to enhancing mutual understanding and brotherly friendship between the peoples and armies of China and Romania.

### 周恩来等领导同志  接见罗军队歌舞团并观看演出

1967-01-03
第1版()
专栏：

    周恩来等领导同志
    接见罗军队歌舞团并观看演出
    新华社二日讯　周恩来、朱德、萧华、杨成武等领导同志，今晚接见罗马尼亚社会主义共和国军队歌舞团领队阿波斯托尔·埃米尔上校、团长迪努·斯太利安上校，以及歌舞团部分成员，同他们进行了亲切友好的谈话。
    接见时在座的，有徐立清、张奚若、乔冠华等。
    罗马尼亚驻中国大使馆临时代办伊斯特拉代，武官索列斯库上校也在座。
    新华社二日讯　周恩来、朱德、萧华、杨成武等领导同志，今晚观看了罗马尼亚社会主义共和国军队歌舞团的演出。
    今晚的演出，是在人民大会堂举行的。大会堂里挂着用中罗两国文字写的“中罗两国人民友好万岁”的巨幅标语。一万多名观众对罗马尼亚艺术家们富有民族、民间特色的表演，不时报以热烈的掌声。
    罗马尼亚驻中国大使馆临时代办伊斯特拉代，武官索列斯库上校，观看了今晚的演出。
    观看演出的还有各方面的负责人徐立清、张奚若、乔冠华、吴法宪、周希汉、贾若瑜、潘振武等。
    演出结束时，在全场热烈的掌声中，周恩来、朱德等同志走上舞台，和演员们亲切握手，并合影留念。
    在演出结束以后，罗马尼亚军队歌舞团领队阿波斯托尔·埃米尔上校代表罗马尼亚社会主义共和国武装部队部，把一部纪录中国人民解放军歌舞团一九六五年访问罗马尼亚时活动的影片，赠送给我国国防部。中国人民解放军总政治部副主任徐立清代表国防部接受了这件有意义的礼品。徐立清在致词中对罗马尼亚武装部队部表示衷心感谢，他深信，这个礼品对增进中罗两国人民、两国军队之间的相互了解和兄弟友谊，一定会产生很好的作用。
