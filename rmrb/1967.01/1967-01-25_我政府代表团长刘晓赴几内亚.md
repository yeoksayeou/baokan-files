Title: Chinese Government Delegation Head Liu Xiao Visits Guinea
Author:
Date: 1967-01-25
Edition: 6

At the invitation of the government of the Republic of Guinea, the head of the Chinese government delegation, Vice Minister of Foreign Affairs Liu Xiao, departed Beijing by airplane this afternoon to attend the completion ceremony of the Jin Kang Hydropower Station, which was built with the aid of our country, in Guinea.

The delegation is scheduled to arrive in Conakry, the capital of Guinea, on January 26th.

### 我政府代表团长刘晓赴几内亚

1967-01-25
第6版()
专栏：

　　我政府代表团长刘晓赴几内亚
    新华社二十四日讯　应几内亚共和国政府邀请，前往几内亚参加我国援助兴建的金康水电站落成典礼的中国政府代表团团长、外交部副部长刘晓，今天下午乘飞机离开北京前往科纳克里。
