Title: Exhibition of Photos Reflecting China's Cultural Revolution Warmly Welcomed in Laos
Author:
Date: 1967-01-07
Edition: 5

    Recently, at the Vientiane Lantern Festival Expo, photographs reflecting China's Great Proletarian Cultural Revolution were exhibited. They were warmly welcomed by the broad masses of the Lao people, who enthusiastically praised Chairman Mao's personally led Great Proletarian Cultural Revolution.

    (Photo by Xinhua News Agency reporter)
