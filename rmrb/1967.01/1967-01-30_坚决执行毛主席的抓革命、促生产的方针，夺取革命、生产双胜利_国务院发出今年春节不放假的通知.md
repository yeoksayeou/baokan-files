Author:
Date: 1967-01-30
Edition: 1

Resolutely implement Chairman Mao's policy of grasping revolution and promoting production to achieve double victory in revolution and production
State Council issues notice on not taking holidays for the Spring Festival this year

New China News Agency, January 29th: The State Council issued a notice to all parts of the country on the 29th regarding not taking holidays for the Spring Festival in 1967. The full text of the notice is as follows: "Our country's Great Proletarian Cultural Revolution has entered a new stage. At present, we are at a critical moment when the revolutionary masses of the proletariat are uniting in large numbers to launch a comprehensive struggle to seize power from a small handful of power-holders within the Party who are taking the capitalist road. In response to the demands of the broad revolutionary masses, in order to resolutely implement the policy of grasping revolution and promoting production proposed by our great leader Chairman Mao, to achieve double victory in revolution and production, and to push the Great Proletarian Cultural Revolution to a new high, the State Council has decided: The Spring Festival in 1967 will not be celebrated; leave for visiting relatives will be suspended during the Cultural Revolution and will be made up later."

### 坚决执行毛主席的抓革命、促生产的方针，夺取革命、生产双胜利　国务院发出今年春节不放假的通知

1967-01-30
第1版()
专栏：

　　坚决执行毛主席的抓革命、促生产的方针，夺取革命、生产双胜利
　　国务院发出今年春节不放假的通知
　　新华社二十九日讯　国务院二十九日向全国各地发出了关于一九六七年春节不放假的通知。通知全文如下：“我国无产阶级文化大革命开始了新阶段。当前正处在无产阶级革命派大联合，以排山倒海之势，向党内一小撮走资本主义道路的当权派展开全面夺权斗争的关键时刻。根据广大革命群众的要求，为了坚决执行我们伟大领袖毛主席提出的抓革命、促生产的方针，夺取革命、生产双胜利，将无产阶级文化大革命推向新的高潮，国务院决定：一九六七年春节不放假；职工探亲假在文化大革命期间暂停执行，以后再补。”
