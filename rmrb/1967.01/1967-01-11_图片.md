Title: In the Old Laotian Battlefield, the Blazing Anti-American Fury
Author:
Date: 1967-01-11

On the battlefields of Laos, where anti-American fury burns, heroic soldiers hold boundless admiration for Mao Zedong Thought. The Lao People's Liberation Army is diligently studying the works of Chairman Mao, arming themselves with the invincible spiritual atomic bomb.
