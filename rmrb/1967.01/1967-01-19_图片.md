Title: Japanese People's Love for Chairman Mao's Quotations
Author:
Date: 1967-01-19
Edition: 5

The Japanese people are extremely fond of "Quotations from Chairman Mao Zedong." The image shows Japanese bindery workers diligently binding batches of printed "Quotations from Chairman Mao Zedong," preparing to ship them to various parts of Japan to meet the demands of the broad masses of the Japanese people.  Photo by Xinhua News Agency.
