Title: Prince Sihanouk Writes to Our Ambassador Expressing Gratitude for Chinese Aid
Author:
Date: 1967-01-08
Edition: 5

	Prince Sihanouk Writes to Our Ambassador Expressing Gratitude for Chinese Aid
	Asian Peoples Admire China's Nuclear Achievements
	Prime Minister Lon Nol: Chinese Aid Demonstrates Sincere Friendship Between Two Countries

	Xinhua News Agency, Phnom Penh, January 6th - According to a report by the Cambodian News Agency on the 5th, on the 3rd, Cambodian Head of State Prince Sihanouk wrote a letter to the Chinese Ambassador to Cambodia, Chen Shuliang, expressing gratitude for China's aid in building a medical college and a hospital in Cambodia.

	The letter pointed out that China's aid to Cambodia in building a medical college and hospital at the Royal University of Phnom Penh is a "new testament to the reliable friendship between the great Chinese people and the Cambodian people, who are united by the common ideals of justice, peace, and opposition to imperialism."

	In this letter, Prince Sihanouk once again congratulated China on its recent successful nuclear test.

	The letter said: "Under the leadership of the outstanding and glorious Chairman Mao Zedong, the Chinese people have achieved success in mastering atomic energy, which has earned the admiration of the peoples of various Asian countries fighting against American imperialism and colonialism, and has become their hope."

	According to a report by Xinhua News Agency, Phnom Penh, January 4th - The handover ceremony for the stage set donated by the Chinese People's Association for Friendship with Foreign Countries to the Queen of Cambodia for the decoration of the Cambodian National Day Hall was held here this morning. Cambodian Prime Minister Lon Nol, on behalf of the Queen and the Head of State, presided over the ceremony. Lon Nol and the Chinese Ambassador to Cambodia, Chen Shuliang, both spoke at the ceremony. Lon Nol, on behalf of the Queen, the Head of State, the government, and the people of Cambodia, expressed gratitude for the gift from China. He said that Cambodia has consistently received support and aid from Chinese friends in political, military, and economic aspects. All the aid provided by the Chinese people to Cambodia is unconditional. This shows the sincere friendship between our two countries.

	Chen Shuliang said in his speech that with the growth of friendship between our two countries, cultural and artistic exchanges between China and Cambodia will continue to develop, and our two countries' close cooperation in all aspects will definitely be further strengthened.

### 西哈努克亲王致函我大使感谢中国援助  亚洲人民钦佩中国的核成就  朗诺首相说中国援助表明了两国之间真挚友谊

1967-01-08
第5版()
专栏：

    西哈努克亲王致函我大使感谢中国援助
    亚洲人民钦佩中国的核成就
    朗诺首相说中国援助表明了两国之间真挚友谊
    新华社金边六日电　据柬埔寨新闻社五日报道，柬埔寨国家元首西哈努克亲王三日写信给中国驻柬埔寨大使陈叔亮，感谢中国将援助柬埔寨兴建一座医学院和一所医院。
    信件指出，由中国援助柬埔寨在茶胶—唝吥王家大学兴建医学院和医院，这是“伟大的中国人民对柬埔寨人民的可靠友谊的新的例证，而中柬两国人民是以正义、和平和反对帝国主义的共同思想团结在一起的。”
    西哈努克亲王在这封信里，再一次祝贺中国最近又成功地进行了一次新的核爆炸。
    信件说：“中国人民在卓越和光荣的领袖毛泽东主席的领导下，在掌握原子能方面获得了成功，受到为反对美帝国主义和殖民主义而斗争的亚洲各国人民的钦佩，并且已成为他们的希望。”
    据新华社金边四日电　中国对外文化联络委员会赠给柬埔寨王后供布置柬埔寨节日大厅用的舞台装置交接仪式，今天上午在这里举行。柬埔寨首相朗诺受王后和国家元首的委托，主持了这个仪式。朗诺和中国驻柬埔寨大使陈叔亮在仪式上讲了话。朗诺代表柬埔寨王后、国家元首、政府和人民，对中国赠送的礼物表示感谢。他说，柬埔寨在政治方面以及军事和经济方面，都一贯得到中国朋友的支持和援助。中国人民给予柬埔寨的一切援助都是无条件的。这表明了我们两国之间的真挚友谊。
    陈叔亮在讲话中说，随着我们两国之间友谊的增长，中国和柬埔寨之间的文化艺术交流将会日益发展，我们两国在各方面的紧密合作一定会日益增强。
