Title: Chairman Ho Chi Minh Addresses New Year Greetings to the Nation's Military and Civilians, Celebrating the Brilliant Victories of the 1966 Anti-American National Salvation Struggle
Author:
Date: 1967-01-02
Edition: 4

	Chairman Ho Chi Minh addressed New Year greetings to the military and civilians of the entire nation, celebrating the brilliant victories of the 1966 anti-American national salvation struggle.

	Xinhua News Agency, Hanoi, January 1st: According to reports from Hanoi newspapers, Chairman Ho Chi Minh of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam addressed New Year greetings to the military and civilians of the entire nation, celebrating the brilliant victories achieved in the 1966 anti-American national salvation struggle.

	In his greetings, Chairman Ho said that last year, the entire nation was closely united, actively fighting and producing, and achieved many brilliant victories. By November 1966, the heroic military and civilians of the South had annihilated and disintegrated 340,000 enemy troops, including the annihilation of over 230,000 enemy troops, among whom more than 100,000 were American invaders; they shot down and destroyed over 1,900 enemy aircraft. The brave military and civilians of the North shattered over 1,600 American aggressor aircraft.

	Chairman Ho said, "Our victories are achieved mainly through our people's self-reliance, but they are inseparable from the enthusiastic support of the people of the world."

	In his greetings, Chairman Ho also presented a New Year's poem to the brave military and civilians of Vietnam who are fighting to the end:

	Spring returns to the earth with a new song,
	I extend my greetings to the entire nation,
	North and South fight against America well,
	Victories are frequent, more than flowers!

### 胡志明主席向全国军民发表新年祝词  祝贺一九六六年抗美救国斗争的辉煌胜利

1967-01-02
第4版()
专栏：

    胡志明主席向全国军民发表新年祝词
    祝贺一九六六年抗美救国斗争的辉煌胜利
    新华社河内一日电　据河内报纸一日报道，越南民主共和国主席胡志明向越南全国军民发表新年祝词，祝贺一九六六年的抗美救国斗争取得了辉煌胜利。
    胡主席在祝词中说，去年，我国全民紧密团结，积极战斗和生产，取得了许多辉煌的胜利。到一九六六年十一月为止，英雄的南方军民共消灭和瓦解敌人三十四万名，其中消灭敌人二十三万多名，内有十万多名美国侵略军；击落和炸毁敌人飞机一千九百多架。英勇的北方军民把一千六百多架美国强盗飞机打得粉身碎骨。
    胡主席说，我们的胜利是由我国人民自力更生为主而取得的，但是同全世界人民的热烈支持是分不开的。
    胡主席在祝词中还向英勇的决战决胜的越南军民赠送了一首祝贺新年的诗：
    春回大地谱新歌，　谨向全民表祝贺，
    北南抗美打得好，　捷报频传赛花多！
