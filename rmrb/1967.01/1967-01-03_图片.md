Title: Central Leaders Watch Romanian Military Song and Dance Troupe Performance
Author:
Date: 1967-01-03
Edition: 6

On January 2, central leaders including Zhou Enlai watched a performance by the Romanian Military Song and Dance Troupe. After the performance, Zhou Enlai and other leaders went on stage to pose for a group photo with the performers.  Xinhua News Agency photographer.
