Title: Comrade Baluku Leads Albanian Military Delegation Back to Beijing
Author:
Date: 1967-01-29
Edition: 1

Comrade Bekir Baluku, Member of the Political Bureau of the Central Committee of the Party of Labour of Albania, Vice-Chairman of the Council of Ministers and Minister of Defence, Comrade Hito Cako, Member of the Central Committee of the Party of Labour of Albania and Head of the Political Department of the Albanian People's Army, and the Albanian military delegation led by them, returned to Beijing today by special plane from Guangzhou after their visit to southern China. They were accompanied by Wang Shusheng, Member of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of China and Vice-Minister of Defence.

Comrade Ye Jianying, Member of the Political Bureau of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of China and Vice-Chairman of the National Defence Council, Comrade Xiao Hua, Member of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of China and Director of the General Political Department of the Chinese People's Liberation Army, Comrade Peng Shaohui, Deputy Chief of the General Staff of the Chinese People's Liberation Army, and other responsible comrades from various relevant departments of the Liberation Army, such as Zhang Xianyao, Zhao Qimin, Tan Jiaxu, and Pan Zhenwu, went to the airport to welcome them.

Comrade Vasili Natanaili, Albanian Ambassador to China, also went to the airport to welcome the delegation.

At the airport, Comrades Baluku, Cako, and others warmly shook hands and had friendly conversations with Comrades Ye Jianying, Xiao Hua, and others. Comrade Baluku thanked them for arranging this visit, saying, "During this visit to Wuhan and Guangzhou, we had extensive contact with the soldiers and officers of the Liberation Army, the Red Guards, workers, and commune members, and everywhere we saw high political enthusiasm." Comrade Ye Jianying said, "Your visit has greatly inspired them." Comrade Baluku continued, "They have greatly inspired us. Throughout the visit, what touched us the most was the revolutionary masses, who are closely linked with Comrade Mao Zedong."

The Albanian comrades departed from Guangzhou this morning by special plane. Responsible comrades from the Guangzhou Military Region of the Chinese People's Liberation Army, responsible comrades from the leadership of the Guangzhou Military Region, and over 800 officers and soldiers from the Army, Navy, and Air Force stationed in Guangzhou went to the airport to give them a warm send-off.

Comrade Vasco Lyuporia, Manager of the Albanian side of the Sino-Albanian Shipping Company, and Comrade Namvan Lanca, Deputy Manager, who were in Guangzhou, also went to the airport to see them off.

### 巴卢库同志率阿军事代表团回到北京　叶剑英等同志到机场迎接阿尔巴尼亚战友

1967-01-29
第1版()
专栏：

　　巴卢库同志率阿军事代表团回到北京
　　叶剑英等同志到机场迎接阿尔巴尼亚战友
    新华社二十八日讯　阿尔巴尼亚劳动党中央政治局委员、部长会议副主席兼国防部长贝基尔·巴卢库，阿尔巴尼亚劳动党中央委员、阿尔巴尼亚人民军政治部主任希托·恰科和由他们率领的阿尔巴尼亚军事代表团，在去我国南方参观访问后，今天，在中共中央委员、国防部副部长王树声陪同下，乘专机从广州回到北京。
    中共中央政治局委员、国防委员会副主席叶剑英，中共中央委员、中国人民解放军总政治部主任萧华，中国人民解放军副总参谋长彭绍辉，以及解放军各有关方面负责同志张贤约、赵启民、谭家述、潘振武等到机场迎接。
    阿尔巴尼亚驻中国大使瓦西里·纳塔奈利也到机场迎接。
    在机场上，巴卢库、恰科等同志同叶剑英、萧华等同志热情握手，亲切交谈。巴卢库同志感谢安排这次参观访问，他说，这次到武汉、广州，和解放军指战员、红卫兵、工人、公社社员进行了广泛接触，到处看到高昂的政治热情。叶剑英同志说：“你们的参观访问对他们鼓舞很大。”巴卢库同志接着说，他们对我们鼓舞很大，在整个参观访问中，最使我们感动的是革命群众，他们和毛泽东同志密切地联系在一起。
    阿尔巴尼亚战友们是今天上午乘专机离开广州的。中国人民解放军广州部队负责人，广州部队领导机关负责人，以及驻广州陆海空三军指战员八百多人，到机场热烈欢送。
    在广州的中阿轮船公司阿方经理瓦斯科·留包里亚、处长南万·兰恰利等也到机场欢送。
