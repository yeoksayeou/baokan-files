Title: Chairman Mao's Words Warm Our Hearts
Author:
Date: 1967-01-01
Edition: 6

We Bai people, we think of Chairman Mao.
Chairman Mao is the red sun in our hearts.
Chairman Mao's words, forever warm our hearts.

The sea will dry up someday,
The rocks will crumble someday,
But Chairman Mao's works, forever shine with golden light.

### 毛主席的话暖心窝（白族民歌）

1967-01-01
第6版()
专栏：

    毛主席的话暖心窝
    （白族民歌）我们白族人民呵，想念的是毛主席。毛主席是我们心中的红太阳，毛主席的话呵，永远暖心窝。海水会有干的时候，岩石会有烂的时候，毛主席的著作呵，永远闪金光。
