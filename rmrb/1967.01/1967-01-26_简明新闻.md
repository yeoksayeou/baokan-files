	Patriotic military and civilians in Khammouane Province, Laos, shoot down two U.S. planes

	According to a broadcast by the Pathet Lao radio station on the 20th, the patriotic military and civilians of Khammouane Province, Laos, shot down two invading U.S. F-105 jet fighter-bomber planes and damaged one on the 10th.

	New Zealand people protest against Nguyen Cao Ky's activities

	On the 23rd, 150 people demonstrated at the airport in Christchurch, the second-largest city in New Zealand, to protest against the arrival of Nguyen Cao Ky, the puppet regime leader of South Vietnam. Nguyen Cao Ky had come to New Zealand after conducting activities in Australia.

	The demonstrators held up placards protesting against the U.S. government's aggression in Vietnam.

	Brazilian parliament passes two fascist laws

	Under the manipulation and control of the pro-American dictator Castelo Branco, the Brazilian parliament passed two fascist laws on the 22nd: a new Brazilian Federal Constitution and a press law.

	The new constitution openly implements naked dictatorship. The new press law, under the guise of "ensuring national security" and "maintaining state secrets," grants the pro-American dictatorship the power to fully control news organizations. (According to Xinhua News Agency)

### 简明新闻

1967-01-26
第6版()
专栏：简明新闻

　　简明新闻
　  老挝川圹爱国军民击落美机两架
    据巴特寮电台二十日广播，老挝川圹省爱国军民十日击落入侵的美国F—105型喷气式强盗飞机两架，击伤一架。
  新西兰人民抗议阮高其到新活动
    新西兰第二大城市克赖斯特彻奇一百五十人二十三日下午在机场举行示威，抗议南越傀儡政权头子阮高其到达这个城市。阮高其是在到澳大利亚进行活动后来到新西兰的。
    示威者高举抗议美国政府在越南进行侵略战争的标语牌。
    巴西议会通过两项法西斯法律
    在巴西亲美独裁者布朗库的操纵和控制下，巴西议会在二十二日通过两项法西斯法律——新的巴西联邦宪法和新闻法。
    新的宪法公开实行赤裸裸的独裁统治。新的新闻法以保障“国家安全”和保持“国家机密”为名，赋予亲美独裁政权以全面控制新闻机构的权力。（据新华社讯）
