Title: Vice Premier Chen Yi Meets Yemen's First Ambassador to China
Author:
Date: 1967-01-28
Edition: 5

Vice Premier and Minister of Foreign Affairs Chen Yi met with the first Ambassador Extraordinary and Plenipotentiary of the Yemen Arab Republic to the People's Republic of China, Muhammad Abdul Wahid Hamidi, to discuss the presentation of the ambassador's credentials. Chen Yi Vice Premier had a friendly conversation with the ambassador.

### 陈毅副总理接见也门首任驻华大使

1967-01-28
第5版()
专栏：

　　陈毅副总理接见也门首任驻华大使
    新华社二十七日讯　国务院副总理兼外交部长陈毅，今天接见了阿拉伯也门共和国首任驻中华人民共和国特命全权大使穆罕默德·阿布杜勒·瓦西·哈米德，商谈大使递交国书事宜。陈毅副总理同大使进行了友好的谈话。
