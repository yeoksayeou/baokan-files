Title: Celebrating the 19th Anniversary of Myanmar's Independence
Author:
Date: 1967-01-06
Edition: 4

The Chairman of the Revolutionary Council of the Union of Burma, General Ne Win, and his wife held a grand reception tonight here to celebrate the 19th anniversary of Myanmar's independence.

Attending the reception were members of the Revolutionary Council, ministers of the government, and representatives from various sectors.

Including China's Ambassador to Myanmar, Geng Biao, diplomatic envoys from various countries were also invited to attend the reception.

The week-long activities in Yangon to celebrate Independence Day officially began on the evening of the 3rd. Other cities in Myanmar also held celebratory activities.

### 庆祝缅甸独立十九周年  奈温将军举行招待会

1967-01-06
第4版()
专栏：

    庆祝缅甸独立十九周年
    奈温将军举行招待会
    新华社仰光四日电　缅甸联邦革命委员会主席奈温将军和夫人今晚在这里举行盛大招待会，庆祝缅甸独立十九周年。
    出席招待会的有革命委员会委员，政府部长和各界代表。
    包括中国驻缅甸大使耿飚在内的各国外交使节也应邀出席了招待会。
    仰光庆祝独立日的一星期的活动是从三日晚上正式开始的。缅甸其他城市也举行了庆祝活动。
