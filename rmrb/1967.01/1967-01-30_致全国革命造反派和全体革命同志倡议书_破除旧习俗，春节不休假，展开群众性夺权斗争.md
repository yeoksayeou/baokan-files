Title: Appeal to All Revolutionary Rebels and Comrades Across the Country – Abolish Old Customs, No Holiday for the Spring Festival, Launch Massive Power Seizure Struggles
Author:
Date: 1967-01-30
Edition: 3

The Shanghai Workers' Revolutionary Rebel Headquarters, the Railway Joint Command, and 57 other revolutionary organizations jointly issued an appeal to abolish old customs, not to take a holiday for the Spring Festival, and to launch a massive power seizure struggle. The full text is as follows:

"The world belongs to us. The country belongs to us. Society belongs to us. If we do not speak, who will? If we do not act, who will?"

Currently, the Great Proletarian Cultural Revolution has entered a new stage: the revolutionary rebels of the proletariat have united to seize power from the handful of power-holders within the Party who are taking the capitalist road and the few stubborn elements who persist in the bourgeois reactionary line. The momentum is like a sudden storm, swift and powerful. The revolutionary situation is excellent! Excellent! But those power-holders within the Party who are taking the capitalist road and the few stubborn elements who persist in the bourgeois reactionary line, though still alive, are not reconciled. They are playing new tricks. They intend to use the upcoming Spring Festival to continue their counter-revolutionary economism, to give workers advance holidays, and to extend the holiday period, etc., to buy off the revolutionary rebels and lure workers away from the battlefront and production posts, to sabotage the Cultural Revolution, sabotage production, and save their dying fate. This is another new counterattack, new provocation, and new conspiracy of the bourgeois reactionary line! This is intolerable!

The broad revolutionary rebels must quickly see through this, thoroughly expose it, and smash it without mercy.

We must shout at these bastards: Put away your tricks! We will not take a holiday for the Spring Festival. We will launch a massive power seizure struggle and resolutely carry the Great Proletarian Cultural Revolution to the end.

For this purpose, we appeal to all revolutionary rebels and comrades across the country as follows:

1. Abolish the old feudal custom of taking a holiday for the Spring Festival, firmly guard the battlefront and production posts, and strictly implement Chairman Mao's great call to "grasp revolution, promote production." We must make up our minds, fear no sacrifice, and despise all difficulties. We will overcome all enemies and thoroughly defeat the new counterattack of the bourgeois reactionary line. We will not stop until we achieve complete victory. We will launch a new high tide of revolution and production and achieve double victories in revolution and production.

2. "Without destruction, there is no construction. Without blocking, there is no flow. Without stopping, there is no progress." We must destroy and construct on a large scale. Around the Spring Festival, we will launch a massive criticism of the old customs and habits of feudalism and capitalism that have existed for thousands of years. We will establish new customs and habits of the proletariat and uphold Mao Zedong Thought. All the filthy habits of worshiping gods, paying New Year visits, treating guests, giving gifts, and eating, drinking, and playing will all go to hell! The working class has never had these dirty habits. We have the power to transform the old world, and we have the revolutionary spirit to smash all old systems. The decadent remnants left by the landlord class and the bourgeoisie, we will uproot and burn them completely. We are the critics of the old world, and we are the creators of the new world. Let us, at this crucial moment of the great unity of the revolutionary rebels of the proletariat across the country, launch a power seizure struggle and spend a Spring Festival that is most meaningful in history.

3. Be vigilant against the sabotage activities of the class enemies. The handful of power-holders within the Party who are taking the capitalist road, the few stubborn elements who persist in the bourgeois reactionary line, and the landlords, rich peasants, counter-revolutionaries, bad elements, and rightists who have not been reformed are not reconciled to their failure. They will use every opportunity to spread poisonous winds, shoot dark arrows, and spread rumors, and carry out sabotage activities politically and economically. The revolutionary rebels and the broad revolutionary masses must keep their eyes open, strengthen their will, and be highly vigilant. Wherever the enemy carries out sabotage, we will annihilate them there.

4. Launch massive propaganda and agitation. During the Spring Festival, mobilize all propaganda machines and use all propaganda tools to loudly propagate Mao Zedong Thought, propagate the proletarian revolutionary line represented by Chairman Mao; propagate the great historical significance of the great unity of the revolutionary rebels of the proletariat and the seizure of power from the power-holders who are taking the capitalist road; propagate Chairman Mao's great call to "grasp revolution, promote production"; propagate the great significance of destroying the old and establishing the new.

"Time flies, and the world changes. A thousand years is too long, we strive for every moment." All revolutionary rebels of the proletariat across the country, unite under the invincible guidance of Mao Zedong Thought, and fight tirelessly to smash the old world completely and create a bright red new world! Fight! Fight! Fight! Dare to let the old world die in the struggle and welcome the new world to be born in the struggle!

Let the great red flag of Mao Zedong Thought fly high forever in the world.

Smash all old systems and establish new systems of the proletariat!

Long live the proletarian revolutionary line represented by Chairman Mao!

Long live the great unity of the revolutionary rebels of the proletariat!

Long live the dictatorship of the proletariat!

Long live the victory of the proletarian seizure of power!

Long live the great Communist Party of China!

Long live our great teacher, great leader, great helmsman, and great commander, Chairman Mao! Ten thousand years! Ten thousand times ten thousand years!

### 致全国革命造反派和全体革命同志倡议书——破除旧习俗，春节不休假，展开群众性夺权斗争

1967-01-30
第3版()
专栏：

　　致全国革命造反派和全体革命同志倡议书
　　——破除旧习俗，春节不休假，展开群众性夺权斗争
    本报讯　上海工人革命造反总司令部铁路联合指挥部等五十七个革命组织联合发出破除旧风俗，春节不休假，开展群众性夺权斗争的倡议书，全文如下：
    “天下者我们的天下。国家者我们的国家。社会者我们的社会。我们不说，谁说？我们不干，谁干？”
    当前，无产阶级文化大革命进入了一个崭新的阶段：无产阶级革命造反派联合起来，向党内一小撮走资本主义道路当权派和极少数执行资产阶级反动路线的顽固分子夺权，其势如暴风骤雨，迅猛异常。革命的形势好得很！好得很！可是那些党内走资本主义道路的当权派和极少数顽固执行资产阶级反动路线的混蛋们，人还在，心不死，又在玩弄新的诡计。他们妄图利用即将来临的春节，继续搞反革命经济主义，给职工提前放假，延长假期等等，以收买革命造反派，诱使工人离开战斗前线，离开生产岗位，破坏文化大革命，破坏生产，挽救他们垂死的命运。这是资产阶级反动路线又一次新反扑、新挑衅、新阴谋！是可忍，孰不可忍！
    广大的革命造反派必须迅速识破之，彻底戳穿之，奋力砸烂之，毫不留情。
    我们要向这群混蛋们大喝一声：收起你们那套鬼把戏吧，我们春节不休假，要展开群众性的夺权，坚决把无产阶级文化大革命进行到底。
    为此，我们向全国革命造反派和全体革命同志们提出如下倡议：
    一、破除春节休假的旧的封建习俗，坚守战斗岗位和生产岗位，不折不扣地贯彻执行毛主席提出的“抓革命，促生产”的伟大号召。下定决心，不怕牺牲，藐视一切困难，压倒一切敌人，彻底打倒资产阶级反动路线的新反扑。不获全胜，决不收兵。掀起一个革命和生产的新高潮，夺取革命、生产双胜利。
    二、“不破不立，不塞不流，不止不行”。我们要大破大立。围绕春节，大造几千年来封建主义、资本主义的旧风俗、旧习惯的反。大立无产阶级的新风俗、新习惯，大立毛泽东思想。什么敬神、拜年、请客、送礼、吃喝玩乐，都统统见鬼去吧！我们工人阶级从来没有这些肮脏的习惯。我们有的是改造旧世界的力量，我们有的是砸烂一切旧制度的革命造反精神。地主阶级、资产阶级遗留下来的糜烂货色，我们要连根铲除，彻底焚烧。我们是旧世界的批判者，我们是新世界的创造者，让我们在全国无产阶级革命造反派大联合，展开夺权斗争的关键时刻，度过一个最最具有历史意义的春节。
    三、警惕阶级敌人的破坏活动。一小撮党内走资本主义道路的当权派、极少数坚持资产阶级反动路线的顽固分子和没有改造好的地、富、反、坏、右决不甘心他们的失败，他们会利用一切机会刮阴风、放暗箭、造谣言，从政治上经济上进行破坏活动，猖狂反扑。我们革命造反派和广大革命人民，必须擦亮眼睛，增强斗志，百倍提高警惕。敌人从那里进行破坏，我们就把他们消灭在那里。
    四、大宣传，大鼓动。在春节期间，开动一切宣传机器，利用一切宣传工具，大张旗鼓地宣传毛泽东思想，宣传以毛主席为代表的无产阶级革命路线；宣传无产阶级革命造反派大联合，夺走资本主义道路当权派的权的伟大历史意义；宣传毛主席提出的“抓革命，促生产”的伟大号召；宣传破旧立新的重大意义。
      “天地转，光阴迫。一万年太久，只争朝夕。”全国无产阶级革命造反派联合起来，在战无不胜的毛泽东思想指引下，为彻底砸烂旧世界，创造一个红彤彤的新世界而不懈的战斗！战斗！！战斗！！！敢叫旧世界在战斗中死亡，欢呼新世界在战斗中诞生！
    让毛泽东思想的伟大红旗，在全世界高高飘扬，永远飘扬。
    砸烂一切旧制度，建立无产阶级新制度！
    以毛主席为代表的无产阶级革命路线胜利万岁！
    无产阶级革命造反派大联合万岁！
    无产阶级专政万岁！
    无产阶级夺权胜利万岁！
    伟大的中国共产党万岁！
    我们伟大的导师、伟大的领袖、伟大的统帅、伟大的舵手毛主席万岁！万岁！！万万岁！！！
