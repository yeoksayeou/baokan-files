Title: A New Year of Victory for Mao Zedong Thought
Author: Shengong
Date: 1967-01-27
Edition: 6

The year 1966 was a great year of new victories for the great Mao Zedong Thought. The Great Proletarian Cultural Revolution, personally initiated and led by the great leader Chairman Mao, has opened up a new path for the world, overthrown a handful of revisionists and those who dream of restoring capitalism, and launched a comprehensive great revolution.

Mao Zedong Thought is the highest form of Marxism-Leninism in contemporary times, the most powerful weapon for fighting against imperialism, revisionism, and dogmatism, and the universal truth applicable to all. I am convinced that under the guidance of Mao Zedong Thought, the year 1967 will be a year of continuous development and victory for China, and a year of victory for the struggles of all oppressed nations around the world to achieve liberation. China's new successful nuclear test is a great gift for 1967, proving that when Mao Zedong Thought is grasped by the masses, it will turn into a tremendous material force.

Long live Sino-Japanese friendship!

Long live the great teacher, the great leader, the great commander, and the great helmsman Chairman Mao! Ten thousand years! Ten thousand ten thousand years!

### 毛泽东思想胜利的新的一年
神宫
1967-01-27
第6版()
专栏：国外来信

　　毛泽东思想胜利的新的一年
    一九六六年是伟大的毛泽东思想取得新胜利的一年。伟大的领袖毛主席亲自发动和领导的无产阶级文化大革命给世界开辟了一条新的道路，打垮了一小撮修正主义分子和梦想复辟资本主义的人，全面地掀起一场大革命。
    毛泽东思想是当代最高的马列主义，是与帝国主义、修正主义、教条主义进行斗争的最强有力的武器，是用于天下而皆准的真理。我确信在毛泽东思想指引下，一九六七年将是中国不断发展取得胜利的一年，将是世界各被压迫民族争取解放的斗争胜利的一年。中国的新的核爆炸成功是一九六七年的大好礼物，它证实当毛泽东思想为群众所掌握，就会变成巨大的物质力量。
    日中友好万岁！
    伟大的导师、伟大的领袖、伟大的统帅、伟大的舵手毛主席万岁！万万岁！
