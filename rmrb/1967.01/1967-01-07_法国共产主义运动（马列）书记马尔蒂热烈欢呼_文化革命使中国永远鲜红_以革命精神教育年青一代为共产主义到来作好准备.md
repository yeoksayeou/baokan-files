Title: French Communist Movement (Marxist-Leninist) Secretary Marti Warmly Hails Cultural Revolution Keeps China Ever Red and Prepares the Young Generation for Communism with Revolutionary Spirit
Author:
Date: 1967-01-07
Edition: 5

	French Communist Movement (Marxist-Leninist) Central Committee Secretariat member Francois Marti published an article on January 5, enthusiastically praising China's Great Proletarian Cultural Revolution, pointing out: This great revolution is "unique in the world." The article said, China's cultural revolution is "consolidating the dictatorship of the proletariat, educating and tempering the younger generation with a revolutionary spirit, successfully resisting bourgeois and revisionist ideology, fostering new people who have cast off the selfishness and all the muck and mire left over from feudal and capitalist society, and thus preparing for the advent of a communist society."

	The article highly praised the great democracy under the dictatorship of the proletariat in China, especially the big-character posters of the revolutionary masses. The article said: This is indeed a democratic movement initiated by the people themselves, opposing a handful of counter-revolutionary revisionists, "because these people are scheming to restore the capitalist system in China sooner or later."

	The article finally stressed that China's Cultural Revolution will certainly be carried through to the end, "China will forever be red, and it will advance unyieldingly towards socialism and communism!"

### 法国共产主义运动（马列）书记马尔蒂热烈欢呼  文化革命使中国永远鲜红  以革命精神教育年青一代为共产主义到来作好准备

1967-01-07
第5版()
专栏：

    法国共产主义运动（马列）书记马尔蒂热烈欢呼
    文化革命使中国永远鲜红
    以革命精神教育年青一代为共产主义到来作好准备
    新华社巴黎五日电　法国共产主义运动（马克思列宁主义）中央委员会书记处书记弗朗索瓦·马尔蒂五日发表文章，热情赞扬中国无产阶级文化大革命，指出：这场大革命是“世界上独一无二的”。文章说，中国的文化革命正在“巩固中国的无产阶级专政，以革命精神教育和锻炼年青一代，胜利地抗击资产阶级和修正主义思想，培养摆脱掉封建社会和资本主义社会遗留下来的自私自利和一切污泥浊水的新人，从而为共产主义社会的到来作好准备”。
    文章高度赞扬中国无产阶级专政下的大民主，特别是革命群众的大字报。文章说：这的确是人民自己掀起的民主（运动），反对一小撮反革命修正主义分子，“因为这一小撮人妄图迟早使资本主义制度在中国复辟。”
    文章最后强调说，中国的文化大革命必将进行到底，“中国将永远是红色的，它将百折不回地向着社会主义和共产主义前进！”
