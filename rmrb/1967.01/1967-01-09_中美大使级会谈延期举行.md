Title: Sino-American Ambassadorial-Level Talks Postponed
Author:
Date: 1967-01-09
Edition: 5

The ambassadors of China and the United States agreed that, due to administrative reasons, the 132nd meeting of the Sino-American ambassadorial-level talks, originally scheduled for January 11, 1967, will be postponed to January 25, 1967.

### 中美大使级会谈延期举行

1967-01-09
第5版()
专栏：

    中美大使级会谈延期举行
    新华社华沙七日电　中美两国大使协议，由于行政性原因，原订于一九六七年一月十一日举行的中美大使级会谈第一百三十二次会议延期至一九六七年一月二十五日举行。
