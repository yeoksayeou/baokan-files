Jakarta Military Authorities Illegally Seize Chinese Embassy Residence
Chinese Embassy in Indonesia Lodges Strong Protest

	BEIJING, Jan. 30 (Xinhua) -- The Chinese Embassy in Indonesia sent a note to the Indonesian Foreign Ministry on Jan. 27, protesting strongly against the Jakarta military authorities' brazen violation of the privileges of the Chinese diplomatic mission and the illegal seizure of the embassy residence.

	The note said that on the afternoon of Jan. 25, around 4 p.m., the Fifth Military District sent a staff officer, Lieutenant Benis Sulodomo, leading four armed soldiers, who, despite the objections of the embassy staff, forcibly occupied the Chinese Embassy residence at No. 19-21 Jl. Cimanuk. They also openly posted a sign with the words "Under the control of the local authorities" on the embassy residence to indicate their seizure.

	The note sternly pointed out that the embassy expresses its utmost indignation at the Jakarta military authorities' brazen violation of the privileges of the Chinese diplomatic mission and the illegal seizure of the embassy residence. It strongly protests to the Indonesian government and demands that the Indonesian government immediately return the seized embassy residence and take effective measures to prevent similar incidents from happening again.

### 雅加达军事当局非法霸占我大使馆宿舍　我驻印尼大使馆提出强烈抗议

1967-01-31
第5版()
专栏：

　　雅加达军事当局非法霸占我大使馆宿舍
　　我驻印尼大使馆提出强烈抗议
    新华社三十日讯　雅加达消息：中国驻印度尼西亚大使馆二十七日照会印度尼西亚外交部，就雅加达军事当局悍然侵犯中国外交机构的特权，非法霸占大使馆宿舍的严重事件，向印度尼西亚政府提出强烈抗议。
    照会说，一九六七年一月二十五日下午四时许，第五军区派出参谋军官倍尼斯·苏洛多莫准尉，率领武装军人四名，不顾大使馆雇员的阻止，强行侵占了芝曼迪里街十九——二十一号中国大使馆的宿舍。他们还公然在大使馆宿舍张贴写有“地方两项命令掌权者管制之下”字样的标记，以示侵占。
    照会严正指出：大使馆对雅加达军事当局悍然侵犯中国外交代表机构的特权，非法霸占大使馆宿舍这一严重事件，表示极大的愤慨，特向印度尼西亚政府提出强烈抗议，并要求印度尼西亚政府立即归还上述被占的大使馆宿舍，切实保证不再发生类似事件。
