Title: Vice Premier Chen Yi Receives Guests from Bechuanaland
Author:
Date: 1967-01-07
Edition: 4

Vice Premier Chen Yi today received the General Secretary of the Botswana People's Party, Marupe, the Propaganda Secretary, Musi, and the Administrative Secretary, Raseko. He had a cordial and friendly conversation with them.

### 陈毅副总理接见贝专纳客人

1967-01-07
第4版()
专栏：

    陈毅副总理接见贝专纳客人
    新华社六日讯　陈毅副总理今天接见贝专纳人民党总书记马鲁平，宣传书记穆西，行政书记拉策科，同他们进行了亲切友好的谈话。
