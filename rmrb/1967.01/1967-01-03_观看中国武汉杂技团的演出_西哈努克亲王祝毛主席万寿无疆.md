Title: Prince Sihanouk Watches Wuhan Acrobatic Troupe Performance and Wishes Chairman Mao Eternal Life
Author:
Date: 1967-01-03
Edition: 6

Prince Sihanouk, Head of State of Cambodia, watched a special performance by the Wuhan Acrobatic Art Troupe of China on the evening of December 30. After the performance, he had a cordial conversation with the troupe members, enthusiastically praising Cambodian-Chinese friendship.

The Prince told the troupe members, "Your visit has greatly encouraged the Cambodian people, the Cambodian government, and myself. Cambodia regards China as its number one friend. I believe that the friendship between Cambodia and China will be consolidated and further developed in the future."

He said, "I once again express my gratitude to China and the Chinese people for sending this famous acrobatic troupe to Cambodia to promote Cambodian-Chinese friendship." The performance by the troupe for the First Asian New Force Games has contributed to further strengthening the forces against American imperialism.

He wished the troupe success and victory in all aspects and wished Chairman Mao Zedong eternal life.

At the end of the performance, Prince Sihanouk presented a flower basket and silver medals to the troupe and personally awarded them to each member.

Those who watched the performance on the evening of the 30th included: Private Advisor to the Head of State Bin Nu and Song Duong, Deputy Chairman of the Supreme Council of the Throne and Director of Royal Affairs Kantol Prince, President of the National Assembly Mundana, Prime Minister Lon Nol, Deputy Prime Ministers Shen Fa and Mao Sai, Director of the Office of the Head of State Huang Hongsa, and other prominent figures in Cambodia such as Gouren.

Chinese Ambassador to Cambodia Chen Shuliang watched the performance on the evening of the 30th. Envoys from several countries to Cambodia also watched the performance.

The troupe performed in Phnom Penh for the Cambodia-China Friendship Association on the evening of the 29th.

### 观看中国武汉杂技团的演出  西哈努克亲王祝毛主席万寿无疆

1967-01-03
第6版()
专栏：

    观看中国武汉杂技团的演出
    西哈努克亲王祝毛主席万寿无疆
    据新华社金边电　柬埔寨国家元首西哈努克亲王十二月三十日晚上，在观看了中国武汉杂技艺术团为他举行的专场演出后，同杂技团团员们进行亲切的交谈，热情赞扬柬—中友谊。
    亲王对杂技团团员们说：“你们的访问给予柬埔寨人民、柬埔寨政府以及我本人极大的鼓舞。柬埔寨把中国当作头号朋友。我相信柬中两国的友谊今后将得到巩固和日益发展。”
    他说：“我再一次向中国和中国人民表示感谢，因为，他们派遣了著名的杂技团来柬埔寨以便增进柬中友谊。”杂技团为第一届亚洲新兴力量运动会进行的演出，对进一步增强反对美帝国主义的力量作出了贡献。
    他祝愿杂技团在各方面取得成就和胜利，祝毛泽东主席万寿无疆。
    演出结束时，西哈努克亲王向杂技团献了花篮和银质奖章，并且给杂技团的团员一一佩戴上。
    三十日晚上观看演出的有：国家元首私人顾问宾努和宋双，王位最高委员会副主席和王宫事务总监康托尔亲王，王国议会议长蒙达那，内阁首相朗诺，副首相沈法和毛赛，国家元首办公厅主任黄鸿萨以及古伦等柬埔寨知名人士。
    中国驻柬埔寨大使陈叔亮观看了三十日晚上的演出。一些国家驻柬埔寨的使节也观看了演出。
    杂技团二十九日晚上在金边为柬埔寨—中国友好协会演出。
