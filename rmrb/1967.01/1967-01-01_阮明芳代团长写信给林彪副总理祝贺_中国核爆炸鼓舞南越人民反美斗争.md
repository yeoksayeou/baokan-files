Title: Deputy Head of Delegation Nguyen Minh Huong Writes to Vice Premier Lin Biao to Congratulate
Author:
Date: 1967-01-01
Edition: 3

Vice Premier and Minister of Defense Lin Biao has received a congratulatory letter from Nguyen Minh Huong, acting head of the delegation of the National Liberation Front of South Vietnam stationed in China, expressing warm congratulations on China's latest successful nuclear test.

The letter states: "This is a new achievement in the development of modern national defense science under the leadership of the Party and government headed by Chairman Mao. It strengthens China's national defense capabilities and the revolutionary forces of the people of the world. It delivers a heavy blow to the war policy and nuclear blackmail of U.S. imperialism. It is a powerful encouragement to the people of South Vietnam who are fighting against U.S. imperialist aggression and to the people of the world who are struggling for independence, democracy, and the defense of world peace."

### 阮明芳代团长写信给林彪副总理祝贺  中国核爆炸鼓舞南越人民反美斗争

1967-01-01
第3版()
专栏：

    阮明芳代团长写信给林彪副总理祝贺
    中国核爆炸鼓舞南越人民反美斗争
    新华社三十一日讯　国务院副总理兼国防部长林彪收到了越南南方民族解放阵线常驻中国代表团代理团长阮明芳的贺信，对我国新的核爆炸成功表示热烈祝贺。
    贺信说：“这是在以毛主席为首的党和政府的领导下，不断巩固和发展着的中国现代国防科学事业所取得一个新的进展。它起着加强中国的国防力量和世界人民的革命力量的作用。它给予美帝国主义的战争政策和核讹诈政策以迎头痛击，它对正在反对美帝国主义侵略的越南南方人民和为独立、民主和保卫世界和平而斗争的世界人民是一个强有力的鼓舞。”
