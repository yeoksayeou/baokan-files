Title: Cambodian Head of State Norodom Sihanouk Cables Premier Zhou Enlai: China's New Nuclear Test Achievement is the Most Effective Support for the People of Asia
Author:
Date: 1967-01-03
Edition: 5

    Premier Zhou Enlai received a cable from Norodom Sihanouk, Head of State of Cambodia, warmly congratulating our country on the success of a new nuclear explosion. The full text of the cable is as follows:

    To Premier Zhou Enlai, Beijing:

    The great and brave people of China, together with their leaders and the outstanding and glorious Chairman Mao Zedong, are celebrating their new brilliant achievement in mastering nuclear energy. The people of Cambodia and the Cambodian people wholeheartedly and unreservedly share in the immense joy of the Chinese people. The people of Cambodia and the Cambodian people, like the people of Asia and the world who will never submit to the will of American imperialism, are greatly encouraged by this.

    Now, while American imperialism is shamelessly and arrogantly using bombs and napalm to ravage our brave Vietnamese brothers, it dares to brazenly, proudly, and haughtily claim to be the true benefactors of humanity.

    The great achievement of the Chinese people is an achievement for Asia, especially for the peoples of Asia who are rising up to oppose American imperialism. This achievement is the most effective support for their struggle to save their independence, freedom, and the national treasures left by their thousand-year history from the savage aggression of the United States, and it is their greatest hope for final victory. I express my heartfelt admiration and sincere congratulations to China, and I extend to you my highest and most fraternal respects.

    Head of State of Cambodia
    Norodom Sihanouk

    January 1, 1967
    Phnom Penh

### 柬埔寨国家元首西哈努克致电周恩来总理  中国核试验的新成就是对亚洲人民最有效支援

1967-01-03
第5版()
专栏：

    柬埔寨国家元首西哈努克致电周恩来总理
    中国核试验的新成就是对亚洲人民最有效支援
    新华社二日讯　周恩来总理收到了柬埔寨国家元首诺罗敦·西哈努克发来的电报，热烈祝贺我国新的核爆炸成功，电报全文如下：北京周恩来总理先生阁下：
    伟大英勇的中国人民和他们的各位领导人以及他们的杰出的、光荣的毛泽东主席一起，正在庆祝自己在掌握核能方面所取得的新的辉煌成就。柬埔寨和柬埔寨人民毫无保留地、衷心地分享中国人民这种无比愉快的心情。柬埔寨和柬埔寨人民和永不屈从于美帝国主义旨意的亚洲和世界各个国家和人民一样，对此感到莫大的欢欣鼓舞。现在，美帝国主义在用炸弹、凝固汽油弹肆无忌惮地摧残我们英勇的越南兄弟的同时，竟敢厚颜无耻、洋洋得意和傲慢地自称是人类中真正慈善的人。
    伟大的中国人民所取得的成就是亚洲的成就，特别是全力起来反对美帝国主义的亚洲各国人民的成就。这一成就，对他们为了拯救自己的独立、自由和千年的光荣历史所遗留下来的民族财富而进行的反对美国野蛮侵略罪行的斗争来说，是最有效的支援，也是他们争取最后胜利的最大的希望。我向中国表示衷心的钦佩之意和诚挚的祝贺，并向阁下致以最崇高的、兄弟般的敬意。
    柬埔寨国家元首
    诺罗敦·西哈努克
    一九六七年一月一日
                    于金边
