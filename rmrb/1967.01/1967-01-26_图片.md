Title: Albanian Comrades Kabo and Shytura Visit Tsinghua University
Author:
Date: 1967-01-26
Edition: 5

    Comrades Kabo and Shytura from Albania, along with Comrade Kang Sheng, were warmly welcomed by revolutionary teachers and students upon their arrival at Tsinghua University.  Photographed by Xinhua News Agency reporter.
