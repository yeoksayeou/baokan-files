Title: Quotations from Chairman Mao
Author:
Date: 1967-01-20
Edition: 1

The newspapers run by our Party, and all the propaganda work conducted by our Party, should be lively, vivid, pointed, and forthright. This is the fighting style that our revolutionary proletariat should have.

*From the Talk with the Editorial Staff of the Jin-Sui Daily*

### 毛主席语录

1967-01-20
第1版()
专栏：

    毛主席语录
    我们党所办的报纸，我们党所进行的一切宣传工作，都应当是生动的，鲜明的，尖锐的，毫不吞吞吐吐。这是我们革命无产阶级应有的战斗风格。
    《对晋绥日报编辑人员的谈话》
