Title: North Vietnam Holds Competition for Anti-American National Salvation Youth Shock Brigades
Author:
Date: 1967-01-19
Edition: 6

North Vietnam recently held the first competition for the Anti-American National Salvation Youth Shock Brigades, enthusiastically commending a number of youth shock brigade units and members who have achieved brilliant results in the anti-American national salvation war.

President Ho Chi Minh of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam attended the opening ceremony of the conference. In the midst of enthusiastic applause, Ho Chi Minh spoke, saying, "I am very satisfied because our youth have achieved many good results and are truly heroic youth of a heroic nation."

He said that in the anti-American national salvation struggle, the Vietnamese military and people are fighting stronger and stronger, achieving many great victories.

Ho Chi Minh encouraged the attendees to be humble and to strive even harder.

The Secretary of the Central Committee of the Vietnam Labor Youth League and the Chairman of the Guidance Committee of the Anti-American National Salvation Youth Shock Brigades, Xie Guangzhan, gave a work report at the conference. The conference passed a resolution to the Central Committee of the Vietnam Labor Party, the government, and President Ho Chi Minh. The resolution states that regardless of how complex and dangerous the situation is, and regardless of how many difficulties are encountered, all members of the Anti-American National Salvation Youth Shock Brigades are determined to highly carry forward the spirit of revolutionary heroism, bravely advance, and resolutely defeat the American aggressors, liberate the South, defend the North, and unify the country.

The Vietnam "People's Daily" and "Pioneer Daily" on January 18th published an editorial on the first competition for the Anti-American National Salvation Youth Shock Brigades in North Vietnam, calling on the Anti-American National Salvation Youth Shock Brigades in North Vietnam to further carry forward the spirit of revolutionary heroism, push the "three preparations" movement to a new high, fearing neither hardship nor sacrifice, and together with the people of the whole country, lead the sacred struggle of anti-American national salvation to victory.

According to reports released here tonight, the military and people of Hai Phong City and Hai Duong Province in North Vietnam shot down two invading American aggressor planes this afternoon.

### 越南北方举行抗美救国青年突击队竞赛大会  胡志明主席勉励青年努力前进  大会决心书表示要高度发扬革命英雄主义精神战胜美国侵略强盗

1967-01-19
第6版()
专栏：

    越南北方举行抗美救国青年突击队竞赛大会
    胡志明主席勉励青年努力前进
    大会决心书表示要高度发扬革命英雄主义精神战胜美国侵略强盗
    新华社河内十八日电　据这里报纸十八日报道，越南北方最近举行了抗美救国青年突击队第一次竞赛大会，热烈表彰一批在抗美救国战争中创立了辉煌成绩的青年突击队单位和青年突击队员。
    越南民主共和国主席胡志明出席了大会的开幕式。胡志明主席在热烈掌声中讲了话。他说，我感到非常满意，因为我们的青年创立了许多好成绩，不愧为英雄民族的英雄青年。
    他说，在抗美救国的战斗中，越南军民越战越强，取得了许多巨大胜利。
    胡志明主席勉励到会代表要谦虚，要进一步努力前进。
    越南劳动青年团中央书记处书记、抗美救国青年突击队指导委员会主任谢光战在大会上作了工作报告。大会通过了给越南劳动党中央、政府和胡志明主席的决心书。决心书表示，不管情况怎样复杂和险恶，不管遇到多大的困难，全体抗美救国青年突击队都决心高度发扬革命英雄主义精神，奋勇向前，坚决战胜美国侵略强盗，解放南方，保卫北方，统一祖国。
    越南《人民报》和《前锋报》十八日就越南北方举行抗美救国青年突击队第一次竞赛大会发表社论，号召越南北方抗美救国青年突击队进一步发扬革命英雄主义精神，把“三准备”运动推向新的高潮，不怕艰苦和牺牲，同全国人民一起，把抗美救国的神圣战斗引向胜利。
    新华社河内十八日电　据这里今晚公布：越南北方海防市和海阳省军民，今天下午击落入侵的美国强盗飞机两架。
