Title: Congratulations from the Foreign Ministry of the Congo (Brazzaville) on China's New Nuclear Explosion Success
Author:
Date: 1967-01-12
Edition: 5

The Foreign Ministry of the Congo (Brazzaville) recently sent a letter to the Chinese Embassy in the Congo (Brazzaville), expressing warm congratulations from the Congolese government to the Chinese people on the success of China's new nuclear explosion.

The congratulatory message states: The Congolese people, together with the heroic and fraternal Chinese people, joyfully celebrate this achievement. This is an effective contribution to the protection of progressive peoples engaged in anti-imperialist struggles.

### 刚果（布）外交部致函我大使馆  热烈祝贺中国新的核爆炸成功

1967-01-12
第5版()
专栏：

    刚果（布）外交部致函我大使馆
    热烈祝贺中国新的核爆炸成功
    新华社讯　刚果（布）外交部最近给我国驻刚果（布）大使馆写了一封信，代表刚果（布）政府向中国人民热烈祝贺我国新的核爆炸成功。
    贺信说，刚果人民同英雄的兄弟的中国人民一起，兴高采烈地庆祝这一成就，这是对保护正在进行反帝斗争的进步人民的有效贡献。
