Title: People of Mali Enthusiastically View Photos of Chairman Mao Reviewing the Cultural Revolution Army
Author:
Date: 1967-01-15
Edition: 6

The people of Mali are enthusiastically viewing photographs of Chairman Mao reviewing the Cultural Revolution Army. The photos were taken by a reporter from the New China News Agency.
