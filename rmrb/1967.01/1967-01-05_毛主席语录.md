This army possesses an indomitable spirit. It is determined to vanquish all enemies and will never yield to them. No matter how difficult and arduous the circumstances, as long as there is one person left, that person will continue to fight on.

### 毛主席语录

1967-01-05
第1版()
专栏：

    毛主席语录
    这个军队具有一往无前的精神，它要压倒一切敌人，而决不被敌人所屈服。不论在任何艰难困苦的场合，只要还有一个人，这个人就要继续战斗下去。
                                            《论联合政府》
