Title: Salute to Heroes from the Frontline of Anti-Imperialism and Anti-Revisionism! Albanian Military Delegation Led by Comrade Baluku Arrives in Shanghai
Author:
Date: 1967-01-11
Edition: 1

Salute to heroes from the frontline of anti-imperialism and anti-revisionism! Albanian comrades led by Comrade Baluku arrive in Shanghai

Wang Shousheng and over a thousand soldiers from the army, navy, and air force welcome them at the airport

The entire crowd chants: "Long live Chairman Mao!" "Long live Comrade Hoxha!" "Long live Sino-Albanian friendship!"

Xinhua News Agency, Shanghai, January 10 – The Albanian military delegation, led by Bekir Baluku, member of the Political Bureau of the Central Committee of the Party of Labour of Albania, Vice Premier and Minister of Defense, and Deputy Head of the delegation, member of the Central Committee of the Party of Labour of Albania, Chief of the Political Department of the Albanian People's Army, Hito Cako, arrived in Shanghai by plane tonight.

Wang Shousheng, member of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of China, Vice Minister of Defense, and Pan Zhenwu, Deputy Director of the Office of the Ministry of Defense, along with responsible personnel of the People's Liberation Army stationed in Shanghai, Liu Wenxue, Yu Guangmao, Zhou Renjie, and Wang Weiguo, as well as over a thousand soldiers from the army, navy, and air force, braved the cold to welcome the Albanian comrades at the airport.

As soon as Comrade Baluku and the delegation appeared at the aircraft door, the welcoming crowd waved red copies of "Quotations from Chairman Mao" to salute the heroic Albanian comrades from the frontline of anti-imperialism and anti-revisionism. When Comrade Baluku came to the front of the welcoming soldiers, everyone repeatedly shouted: "Warmly welcome the Albanian military delegation!" "Long live Sino-Albanian friendship!" "Long live Chairman Mao!" "Long live Enver Hoxha!" Comrade Baluku enthusiastically shook hands with the soldiers and shouted in Chinese: "Long live Mao Zedong!" The welcoming crowd immediately responded: "Long live Comrade Hoxha!" The slogans and applause echoed through the airport, filling it with the combative fraternal feelings between the peoples and armies of China and Albania.

Albanian Ambassador to China, Vasili Nataneli, and the delegation members, military attaché to the Albanian Embassy in China, Felik Hatu, also came to the airport to welcome them.

Tonight, the responsible personnel of the People's Liberation Army stationed in Shanghai held a banquet to entertain the entire delegation led by Comrade Baluku.

Brief Biography of Comrade Bekir Baluku

Comrade Bekir Baluku was born in 1917 in Tirana.

In April 1939, when the fascists occupied the country, Comrade Bekir Baluku led anti-occupation demonstrations and continued revolutionary activities in the old army.

After the founding of the Communist Party of Albania, he was recruited as a party member. In 1942, Comrade Bekir Baluku was appointed commander of the Tirana guerrilla squad. In March 1943, he was appointed a member of the Tirana District Party Committee. That year, he was arrested and imprisoned, but after his release, he continued revolutionary activities, organizing the Dajti and Kruja guerrilla squads and expanding the guerrilla camps in the Tirana area, later serving as the commander of the guerrilla camp.

On September 18, 1943, the Third Brigade was established, and he was appointed political commissar of the Third Brigade. Later, he served as the commander of the Second Brigade. Under the leadership of Comrade Bekir Baluku, the Second Brigade and other units fought together, making significant contributions to the liberation of Korça. Due to his bravery and tenacity in battle, Comrade Bekir Baluku was appointed commander of the Second, Ninth, and Twentieth Brigade battle command.

Due to his outstanding achievements, he was elected as a representative to the Berat Anti-Fascist National Liberation Assembly. In April 1944, he was elected a member of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of Albania. After the liberation of Albania, Comrade Bekir Baluku served as the deputy commander of the First Army, political commissar of the Second Army, and political commissar of the General Rear Services. In January 1948, he was appointed Chief of the General Staff.

Due to his significant contributions in the political and military fields, Comrade Bekir Baluku has been elected as a representative to the People's Assembly since 1945 and a member of the Political Bureau of the Central Committee of the Party of Labour of Albania since the First Congress of the Party of Labour of Albania.

Since 1953, Comrade Bekir Baluku has been appointed Vice Premier and Minister of Defense. He has done a great deal of work in strengthening the country's defense capabilities, educating all military personnel, and further strengthening the revolutionary nature of the army, establishing outstanding merits.

### 向来自反帝反修最前线的英雄的阿尔巴尼亚战友致敬！巴卢库同志率军事代表团到上海

1967-01-11
第1版()
专栏：

    向来自反帝反修最前线的英雄的阿尔巴尼亚战友致敬！
    巴卢库同志率军事代表团到上海
    王树声同志等和陆海空三军指战员千余人到机场热烈欢迎
    全场欢呼：“毛主席万岁！”“霍查同志万岁！”“中阿友好万岁！”
    新华社上海十日电　由团长、阿尔巴尼亚劳动党中央政治局委员、部长会议副主席兼国防部长贝基尔·巴卢库，副团长、劳动党中央委员、阿尔巴尼亚人民军政治部主任希托·恰科率领的阿尔巴尼亚军事代表团，今晚乘飞机到达上海。
　  专程来上海迎接的中共中央委员、国防部副部长王树声，国防部办公厅副主任潘振武和人民解放军驻上海部队负责人刘文学、余光茂、周仁杰、王维国等，以及陆海空三军指战员一千多人，冒着严寒，前往机场热烈欢迎阿尔巴尼亚战友。
    巴卢库团长等在机舱口一出现，欢迎的人们就挥动红彤彤的《毛主席语录》，向来自反帝反修最前线的英雄的阿尔巴尼亚战友致敬。巴卢库团长来到欢迎的指战员们面前时，大家不断高呼：“热烈欢迎阿尔巴尼亚军事代表团！”“中阿友好万岁！”“毛主席万岁！”“恩维尔·霍查万岁！”巴卢库团长热情地同指战员握手，还用中国话高呼：“万岁，毛泽东！”欢迎的人们紧接着回答：“霍查同志万岁！”口号声和掌声此起彼伏，机场上充满了中阿两国人民和军队之间的战斗的兄弟情谊。
    阿尔巴尼亚驻中国大使瓦西里·纳塔奈利和军事代表团团员、驻中国大使馆武官费利克·哈图今晚也到机场欢迎。
    今晚，人民解放军驻上海部队负责人设便宴招待由巴卢库团长率领的阿尔巴尼亚军事代表团全体同志。
    贝基尔·巴卢库同志简历
    贝基尔·巴卢库同志一九一七年生于地拉那。
    一九三九年四月法西斯占领时，贝基尔·巴卢库同志领导了反占领者的游行示威，以后在旧军队中继续从事革命活动。
    阿尔巴尼亚共产党一成立，他就被吸收为党员。一九四二年，贝基尔·巴卢库同志被任命为地拉那游击小队队长。一九四三年三月，任地拉那区党委会委员。同年被捕入狱，出狱后继续从事革命活动，组成达伊特和克鲁雅游击小队，并从事扩充地拉那地区的游击营，后任游击营长。
    一九四三年九月十八日，第三旅成立，他被任命为第三旅政委，后任第二旅旅长。在贝基尔·巴卢库同志的领导下，第二旅和其他部队联合作战，对科尔察的解放作出了巨大的贡献。由于他在战斗中表现了英勇顽强的精神，贝基尔·巴卢库同志被任命为第二、第九、第二十旅战役司令部司令。
    由于他屡立战功，被选为贝尔梅特反法西斯代表大会代表。一九四四年四月当选为阿尔巴尼亚共产党中央委员会委员。阿尔巴尼亚解放后，贝基尔·巴卢库同志曾任第一军副军长，第二军政委，总后勤部政委。一九四八年一月，任总参谋长。
    由于贝基尔·巴卢库同志在政治、军事方面作出了重大贡献，一九四五年起被选为人民议会代表，自阿尔巴尼亚劳动党第一次代表大会起，他就是中央委员会政治局委员。
    自一九五三年起，贝基尔·巴卢库同志被任命为部长会议副主席兼国防部长。他对加强本国国防能力，对教育全军指战员，对进一步加强军队革命化，做了大量工作，建立了卓越的功勋。　（新华社）
