Title: Telegram of Congratulations from the Central Committee of the Communist Party of China, the State Council, the Central Military Commission, and the Central Cultural Revolution Group to the Revolutionary Rebel Organizations of Shanghai
Author:
Date: 1967-01-25
Edition: 3

You have implemented the great alliance of revolutionary mass organizations of the proletariat, becoming the core that unites all revolutionary forces. You have firmly grasped in your hands the destiny of the dictatorship of the proletariat, the destiny of the Great Proletarian Cultural Revolution, and the destiny of the socialist economy.
