Title: Comrade Grivas Cables the CPC Central Political Bureau: China's New Nuclear Test is a New Victory for Mao Zedong Thought
Author:
Date: 1967-01-03
Edition: 5

	The CPC Central Political Bureau has received a cable from Jacques Grivas, Secretary of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of Belgium, warmly congratulating China on the success of its new nuclear test, which is a new victory for Mao Zedong Thought. The full text of the cable is as follows:

	To the Political Bureau of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of China:

	Our party extends fraternal greetings to you, and through you to the Communist Party of China, to the workers, engineers, technicians, and scientists who have undertaken this task, to the Chinese People's Liberation Army, and to the Chinese people, for the new nuclear test conducted on December 28, 1966.

	This successful test has dealt another blow to the imperialists, with U.S. imperialism, the number one enemy of the people of the world, at their head. This successful test has also dealt another blow to the modern revisionists, with the Soviet revisionist clique, which has usurped power in the Soviet Union, using nuclear threats and blackmail, colluding with U.S. imperialism, opposing the people of all countries, and attempting to dominate the world, at their center.

	The success of this nuclear test is a new victory for the Chinese people, the People's Republic of China, and its defensive forces. It is a new victory for socialist science and technology, for the Great Proletarian Cultural Revolution, for the socialist revolution in China, for Marxism-Leninism, and for Mao Zedong Thought. It is a victory for the revolutionary classes, peoples, and nations of the world.

### 格里巴同志致电中共中央政治局  中国新的核爆炸成功是毛泽东思想的新胜利

1967-01-03
第5版()
专栏：

    格里巴同志致电中共中央政治局
    中国新的核爆炸成功是毛泽东思想的新胜利
    新华社二日讯　中共中央政治局收到了比利时共产党中央委员会书记雅克·格里巴发来的电报，热烈祝贺我国新的核爆炸成功是毛泽东思想的新胜利。电报全文如下：中国共产党中央委员会政治局：
    我们党为一九六六年十二月二十八日进行的新的核爆炸，向你们，并通过你们向中国共产党，向从事这一任务的工人、工程技术人员和科学工作者，向中国人民解放军和中国人民致以兄弟般的祝贺。
    由于这一次成功的试验，以全世界人民的头号敌人美帝国主义为首的帝国主义者又遭到了一次新的失败。由于这一次成功的试验，以苏联修正主义集团——这个集团在苏联篡夺了政权，利用核威胁和核讹诈，同美帝国主义勾结起来，反对各国人民，企图主宰世界——为中心的现代修正主义者又遭到了一次新的失败。
    这次核试验的成功，是中国人民、中华人民共和国及其防御力量的新胜利，是社会主义科学技术的新胜利，是无产阶级文化大革命的新胜利，是中国社会主义革命的新胜利，是马克思列宁主义的新胜利，是毛泽东思想的新胜利。这是全世界革命的阶级、人民和民族的胜利。
    比利时共产党中央委员会书记雅克·格里巴
                        一九六六年十二月三十日
