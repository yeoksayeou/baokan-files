Title: Communiqué of the Eleventh Plenary Session of the Eighth Central Committee of the Communist Party of China
Author:
Date: 1967-01-08
Edition: 1

Arming the masses of workers, peasants, soldiers, revolutionary intellectuals, and cadres with Mao Zedong Thought, and further promoting the revolutionization of people's thinking, is the most reliable and fundamental guarantee to prevent revisionism, prevent the restoration of capitalism, and ensure the victory of our socialist and communist cause.

### 《中国共产党第八届中央委员会第十一次全体会议公报》

1967-01-08

专栏：
