Title: A Powerful Response
Author:
Date: 1967-01-21
Edition: 6

The Central Committee of the Chinese Communist Party and the State Council have issued a notice on the publication of the "Decision on the Great Proletarian Cultural Revolution." This decision is a powerful response to the urgent needs of the current class struggle and the revolution. It is a great document that reflects the will of the proletariat and the broad masses of the people.

The notice emphasizes that the "Decision" is a major political document that has been carefully drafted and revised by the Central Committee of the Chinese Communist Party. It is a comprehensive and systematic exposition of the great proletarian cultural revolution. The notice calls on all party members, cadres, and the broad masses of the people to study and implement the "Decision" thoroughly.

The notice points out that the great proletarian cultural revolution is a profound revolution that touches on the very roots of the superstructure. It is a revolution that aims to overthrow the bourgeoisie and all other exploiting classes and to establish the dictatorship of the proletariat. The notice calls on all revolutionary comrades to take up the banner of the great proletarian cultural revolution and to fight resolutely against all reactionary forces.

The notice stresses that the great proletarian cultural revolution is a long-term and complex struggle. It requires the united efforts of the entire party, the entire army, and the broad masses of the people. The notice calls on all revolutionary comrades to strengthen their revolutionary vigilance, to enhance their class consciousness, and to stand firmly on the side of the proletariat.

The notice also calls on all revolutionary comrades to pay close attention to the study and implementation of Mao Zedong Thought. It emphasizes that Mao Zedong Thought is the guiding principle of the great proletarian cultural revolution. The notice calls on all revolutionary comrades to study Mao Zedong Thought diligently, to apply it to their work, and to use it as a weapon to fight against all reactionary forces.

The notice concludes by expressing the firm belief that, under the leadership of the Central Committee of the Chinese Communist Party and Chairman Mao Zedong, the great proletarian cultural revolution will surely achieve great victories. The notice calls on all revolutionary comrades to unite and advance, to overcome all difficulties, and to create a new situation in the great proletarian cultural revolution.
