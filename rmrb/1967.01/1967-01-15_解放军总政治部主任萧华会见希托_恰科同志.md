Title: General Political Department Director Xiao Hua Meets Comrade Hito Cako
Author:
Date: 1967-01-15
Edition: 5

    General Political Department Director Xiao Hua Meets Comrade Hito Cako
    New China News Agency, January 14th
    Xiao Hua, a member of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of China and Director of the General Political Department of the Chinese People's Liberation Army, met with Hito Cako, a member of the Central Committee of the Party of Labor of Albania, Director of the Political Department of the Albanian People's Army, and Deputy Head of the Albanian Military Delegation, this morning. The host and guest had a very warm and friendly conversation.

    Present at the meeting were Deputy Director of the General Political Department of the Chinese People's Liberation Army Yuan Ziqin, and relevant responsible comrades from the Chinese People's Liberation Army, Liu Xiyuan, Zhang Boxiang, Liu Han, Li Wei, and Pan Zhenwu.

### 解放军总政治部主任萧华会见希托·恰科同志

1967-01-15
第5版()
专栏：

    解放军总政治部主任萧华会见希托·恰科同志
    新华社十四日讯　中共中央委员、中国人民解放军总政治部主任萧华，今天上午会见了阿尔巴尼亚劳动党中央委员、阿尔巴尼亚人民军政治部主任、阿尔巴尼亚军事代表团副团长希托·恰科。宾主进行了非常亲切友好的交谈。
    会见时在座的，有中国人民解放军总政治部副主任袁子钦，以及中国人民解放军有关方面负责同志刘西元、张伯祥、刘汉、李伟、潘振武等。
