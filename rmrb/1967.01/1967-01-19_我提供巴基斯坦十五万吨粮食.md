Title: China Provides 150,000 Tons of Grain to Pakistan
Author:
Date: 1967-01-19
Edition: 6

After friendly consultations between both sides, the Chinese government has decided to provide Pakistan with 150,000 tons of wheat and rice.

These grains are provided as a long-term interest-free loan to Pakistan.

On the 17th, China's Ambassador to Pakistan, Zhang Wenjin, on behalf of the Chinese government, and I.A. Khan, Secretary of the Economic Section of the Pakistani Presidential Secretariat, on behalf of the Pakistani government, exchanged documents regarding China's provision of grain to Pakistan in Rawalpindi.

### 我提供巴基斯坦十五万吨粮食

1967-01-19
第6版()
专栏：

    我提供巴基斯坦十五万吨粮食
    新华社拉瓦尔品第十八日电　经过双方友好协商以后，中国政府决定向巴基斯坦提供十五万吨小麦和大米。
    这些粮食是作为长期无息贷款提供给巴基斯坦的。
    中国驻巴基斯坦大使章文晋代表中国政府，巴基斯坦总统秘书处经济司秘书伊·阿·汗代表巴基斯坦政府十七日在这里交换了有关中国向巴基斯坦提供粮食的文件。
