Title: Tanzanian Newspaper Says U.S. Imperialism's Arbitrariness Forces People of All Countries to Rise Up in Resistance
Author:
Date: 1967-01-11
Edition: 5

TANZANIA — The organ of the Tanganyika African National Union, *Uhuru*, published a lengthy article on January 6, calling on the people of Africa to resolutely fight imperialism in the new year.

The article stresses: Those who oppose imperialism are our friends; those who stand with imperialism in any way are enemies of the people and must be fought mercilessly.

The article calls on African youth and the people to be vigilant against imperialist plots in the new year and to be prepared to counter imperialist tactics.

The article points out: "Imperialism, especially U.S. imperialism, is rampant worldwide, which inevitably infuriates the people of the world, especially the workers and peasants who are most severely exploited by imperialism, and forces them to unite and take up armed resistance."

The article emphasizes that the heroic struggle of the Vietnamese people shows that U.S. imperialism is a paper tiger.

The article says that the people of Tanzania must be determined to "wage an unyielding struggle against imperialist rule, repression, and exploitation."

The article highly praises the revolutionary youth of Morogoro Province in Tanzania for smashing a portrait of the British Queen in a club and replacing it with a portrait of President Nyerere. The article says: "The commendable action of the Morogoro youth clearly and unambiguously warns the imperialists: Tanzanian youth are fearless and resolute, and they dare to take action. We are determined to fight imperialism to the end."

The article also points out that in the new year, the people of Tanzania must develop the national economy with the spirit of self-reliance.

### 坦桑报纸说美帝横行霸道迫使各国人民起来反抗  非洲人民反帝必须一反到底  谁同帝国主义站在一起谁就是人民的敌人

1967-01-11
第5版()
专栏：

    坦桑报纸说美帝横行霸道迫使各国人民起来反抗
    非洲人民反帝必须一反到底
    谁同帝国主义站在一起谁就是人民的敌人
    新华社达累斯萨拉姆电　坦噶尼喀非洲民族联盟机关报《民族主义者报》六日发表长篇文章，要求非洲人民在新的一年中，坚决地同帝国主义进行斗争。
    文章强调指出：谁反对帝国主义，谁就是我们的朋友；谁以任何方式同帝国主义站在一起，谁就是人民的敌人，就必须同他进行无情的斗争。
    文章要求非洲青年和人民在新的一年中对帝国主义的阴谋提高警惕，并准备对付帝国主义的各种手段。
    文章指出：“帝国主义，特别是美帝国主义，在世界各地横行霸道，这必然激怒世界人民，首先是受帝国主义残酷剥削最严重的工人和农民，并迫使他们团结起来进行武装反抗。”
    文章强调说，英雄的越南人民的斗争表明，美帝国主义是纸老虎。
    文章说，坦桑尼亚人民必须下定决心，“对帝国主义的统治、镇压和剥削展开毫不妥协的针锋相对的斗争”。
    文章高度赞扬坦桑尼亚莫洛戈洛省的革命青年打碎了莫洛戈洛省一个俱乐部内的英国女王画像，换上尼雷尔总统的画像。文章说：“莫洛戈洛青年的值得称赞的行动清楚地毫不含糊地向帝国主义者发出警告：坦桑尼亚青年是无所畏惧的、坚决的，他们敢于采取行动。我们决心同帝国主义斗争到底。”
    文章还指出，在新的一年里，坦桑尼亚人民必须以自力更生的精神发展民族经济。
