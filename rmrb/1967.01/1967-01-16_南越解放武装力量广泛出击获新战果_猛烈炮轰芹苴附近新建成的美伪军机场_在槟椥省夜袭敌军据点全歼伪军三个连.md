Title: Liberation Armed Forces in South Vietnam Achieve New Victories
Author:
Date: 1967-01-16
Edition: 5

The liberation armed forces in South Vietnam launched extensive attacks and achieved new victories. They heavily shelled a newly built U.S.-puppet military airfield near Can Tho to the southwest of Saigon. In the province of Binh Tuy, they launched a night raid on an enemy stronghold and annihilated three puppet army companies.

Xinhua News Agency, Saigon: On the 12th, the liberation armed forces of South Vietnam launched a fierce attack on a newly built U.S.-puppet military airfield near Can Tho to the southwest of Saigon in the early morning.

According to a report from Western news agencies quoting a spokesman from the U.S. command in Vietnam, the liberation armed forces' mortar and recoilless rifle shells rained down on the airfield. Aircraft and buildings on the airfield were damaged in the shelling.

Xinhua News Agency, Hanoi: According to a report from the South Vietnam Liberation News Agency, the liberation armed forces in the provinces of Binh Tuy and Quang Ngai in South Vietnam have launched continuous attacks, annihilating a large number of U.S.-puppet troops and South Korean mercenary troops.

On the night of the 6th, the liberation armed forces in Binh Tuy Province launched a fierce attack on the enemy stronghold of Thua Dinh in Phu My District, annihilating three puppet army companies and severely damaging another company. They killed and wounded over 300 enemy troops and seized a large amount of weapons and military supplies. In Quang Ngai Province, the local forces and guerrillas in De Phu District killed and wounded over 150 invading puppet troops in the eastern part of the district from December 27 to 30. They destroyed four M-113 type armored vehicles and two other military vehicles. In Quang Nam Province, the guerrillas blew up an enemy military train on the Hai Van to Thua Thien railway line on December 5, killing and wounding 60 enemy troops. All the military supplies on the train were destroyed. On December 9, the guerrillas also blew up five bridges in the Hai Van Pass area. In Ninh Thuan Province, the local forces and guerrillas in the Bo Ai area killed and wounded 24 U.S. troops and South Korean mercenaries who were "sweeping" through Dong Thong and Da La villages on December 18.

### 南越解放武装力量广泛出击获新战果  猛烈炮轰芹苴附近新建成的美伪军机场  在槟椥省夜袭敌军据点全歼伪军三个连

1967-01-16
第5版()
专栏：

    南越解放武装力量广泛出击获新战果
    猛烈炮轰芹苴附近新建成的美伪军机场
    在槟椥省夜袭敌军据点全歼伪军三个连
    新华社讯　西贡消息：越南南方解放武装力量十二日清晨猛烈袭击了西贡西南芹苴市附近的一个新建成的美伪军机场。
    据西方通讯社援引侵越美军司令部的发言人的话说，解放武装力量的迫击炮和无后座力炮炮弹纷纷落到了机场上。机场上的飞机和建筑物在炮轰中都遭到了损失。
    新华社河内电　据越南南方解放通讯社报道：越南南方槟椥、广义等省解放武装力量连续出击，歼灭大批美伪军和南朝鲜帮凶军。
    槟椥省解放武装力量六日夜间猛烈袭击平大县州香敌军据点，全歼伪军三个连，重创另一个连，共打死、打伤敌人三百多名，并缴获大批武器和军用物资。广义省德普县地方部队和游击队，在十二月二十七日到三十日的四天中，共打死打伤窜犯这个县东部地区的伪军一百五十多名，击毁M—113型装甲车四辆和其他军车二辆。广南省游击队去年十二月五日，在海云至顺化的铁路线上，炸毁敌人的一列军用火车，炸死、炸伤敌人六十名，列车上运载的军用物资全被炸毁。十二月九日，游击队还炸毁了海云岭地区的五座桥梁。宁顺省博爱地区地方部队和游击队，十二月十八日在东通、答拉两个村毙伤前去“扫荡”的美军和南朝鲜帮凶军二十四名。
