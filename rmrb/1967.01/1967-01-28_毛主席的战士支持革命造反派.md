Title: Chairman Mao's Soldiers Support the Revolutionary Rebels
Author: Shi Xin
Date: 1967-01-28
Edition: 6

	Revolutionary winds sweep the land, and the revolutionary tide rolls on. The People's Liberation Army resolutely carries out Chairman Mao's instructions and resolutely supports the proletarian revolutionary rebels! What Chairman Mao opposes, we will resolutely overthrow! What Chairman Mao approves, we love without limit! The bourgeoisie attempts to restore the old order, and revisionism seeks to take the stage. We will expose these villains one by one. We must overthrow those in power who are taking the capitalist road and seize back the leadership! Comrades in the revolutionary rebel ranks, Chairman Mao's soldiers resolutely support you! With rifles to defend the revolution in hand and the great cause of the proletariat in our hearts, Chairman Mao is our supreme commander! Whoever tries to sabotage the dictatorship of the proletariat, whoever tries to sabotage the Great Proletarian Cultural Revolution, we will strike them down and trample them underfoot! Overcome all enemies, break through all obstacles! We hold the guns to defend the revolutionary mountains, we grasp the power of the socialist mountains, and we raise high the great red banner of Mao Zedong Thought! Revolutionary winds sweep the land, and the revolutionary tide rolls on. Fight! Fight! All proletarian revolutionaries unite! Never leave Chairman Mao, the train of history forever moves forward!

### 毛主席的战士支持革命造反派
世新
1967-01-28
第6版()
专栏：

　　毛主席的战士支持革命造反派
　　解放军海军某部　世新造反风雷遍地起，革命怒涛滚滚来。解放军最坚决地执行毛主席的指示，解放军最坚决地支持无产阶级革命造反
  派！毛主席反对的，我们坚决打倒！毛主席赞成的，我们无限热爱！资产阶级妄图复辟，修正主义想要上台。我们就把这些坏蛋一个个地揪出来。我们要斗倒走资本主义道路当权派，把领导大权夺回来！革命造反派的战友们，毛主席的战士最坚决地支持你们！保卫革命的钢枪在手，无产阶级的壮志满怀，毛主席是我们的最高统帅！谁要破坏无产阶级专政，谁要破坏文化大革命，我们就把他打翻在地脚下踩！压倒一切敌人，冲破重重障碍！保卫革命江山的枪杆子我们握，社会主义江山的印把子我们掌，毛泽东思想伟大红旗高高地举起来！造反风雷遍地起，革命怒涛滚滚来。战斗！战斗！一切无产阶级革命派联合起来！一步不离毛主席，时代列车永远向前开！
