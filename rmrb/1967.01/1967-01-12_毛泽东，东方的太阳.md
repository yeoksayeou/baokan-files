Title: Mao Zedong, the Sun of the East
Author: Ānwǎ'ěr Hākèmǔ
Date: 1967-01-12
Edition: 4

Look! The radiance of Beijing is brilliant and resplendent,
Sending out countless beams of golden light.
That is the glory of the giant,
Illuminating the entire East.
Mao Zedong! The Sun of the East!
It is you who have given the world hope.
It is you who have shown us the direction to advance.
We follow Mao Zedong in making revolution,
Sweeping away all harmful pests together!
Look!
The dawn of victory is breaking through the darkness.
The moment of victory is about to arrive,
Tomorrow, the world will be bright!

### 毛泽东，东方的太阳
安瓦尔·哈基姆
1967-01-12
第4版()
专栏：

    毛泽东，东方的太阳
    巴勒斯坦　安瓦尔·哈基姆
    看！北京的光芒灿烂辉煌，
    放射出万道金光。
    那是巨人的光辉，
    映红了整个东方。
    毛泽东！东方的太阳！
    是您，使全世界有了希望。
    是您，给我们指出了前进的方向。
    我们紧跟着毛泽东闹革命，
    把害人虫一齐扫光！
    看！
    胜利的曙光正在冲破夜幕。 
    胜利的时刻就要到来，
    明天，世界将是一片光明！
