Title: Sino-American Ambassadorial-Level Talks Continue
Author:
Date: 1967-01-27
Edition: 5

The 132nd session of Sino-American ambassadorial-level talks began at 2 p.m. on the 25th in Warsaw and lasted for three hours and ten minutes.

A statement issued after the meeting said the next session will be held at 2 p.m. on June 7.

### 中美大使级会谈继续举行

1967-01-27
第5版()
专栏：

　　中美大使级会谈继续举行
    新华社华沙二十五日电　中美大使级会谈第一百三十二次会议二十五日下午两点钟开始在华沙举行。会议进行了三小时十分钟。
    会后发表的公告说，下次会议将在六月七日下午两点举行。
