Title: Grasp the Revolution, Promote Production
Author:
Date: 1967-01-23
Edition: 6

	Grasp the Revolution, Promote Production
	(Drums and Clappers)

	甲: Beat the drums of revolution,
	乙: Blow the red counterattack bugle.
	丙: Grasp the revolution, promote production,
	丁: Resolutely and thoroughly eliminate the new tricks of the bourgeois reactionary line.
	戊:
	甲: The revolutionary spirit of Shanghai workers is magnificent,
	乙: The great red flag of Mao Zedong Thought is held high.
	丙: Actively participate in the Cultural Revolution,
	丁: Determine to do well in both the Cultural Revolution and production,
	戊: Report double victories in revolution and production.
	甲: The capitalist-roaders in the Party,
	乙: Fear the masses stirring up the revolution.
	丙: To protect their own official hats,
	丁: They play new tricks and schemes again.
	戊: This, we will not allow for a million reasons.
	甲: They use material incentives to attack,
	乙: Attempting to lead the direction of struggle astray.
	丙: They want to incite the masses to fight the masses,
	丁: Create power outages, water shortages, and traffic disruptions,
	戊: We firmly refuse.
	甲: We are the red rebels,
	乙: With red hearts, bright eyes, and clear direction.
	丙: We closely follow Chairman Mao.
	丁: If the reactionaries dare to move,
	戊: We will rebel against them and seize their power.
	甲: We are the red rebels,
	乙: Dare to speak, dare to do, dare to struggle.
	丙: Whoever dares to sabotage the revolution,
	丁: Whoever dares to sabotage production,
	戊: We will rebel against them and overthrow them.
	甲: We are the red rebels,
	乙: We listen to every word of Chairman Mao's teachings.
	丙: Grasp the revolution, promote production,
	丁: We will resolutely respond,
	戊: We will earnestly carry it out.
	甲: We are the red rebels,
	乙: Great unity, great alliance, together against the enemy,
	丙: Thoroughly smash the new counterattacks of the bourgeois reactionary line,
	丁: Carry the Cultural Revolution of the proletariat to the end.
	戊: Achieve double victories in revolution and production!

	Beijing First Machine Tool Factory Red Rebel Propaganda Troupe

### 抓革命，促生产（锣鼓快板）

1967-01-23
第6版()
专栏：

　　抓革命，促生产
　　（锣鼓快板）甲：擂起革命战鼓，乙：吹起红色反击号。丙：抓革命，促生产，丁：把资产阶级反动路线的新花招，戊：坚决彻底消灭掉。甲：上海革命工人志气豪，乙：毛泽东思想伟大红旗举得高。丙：积极参加文化大革命，丁：决心把文化革命、生产都搞好，戊：革命生产双双传喜报。甲：党内走资本主义道路的当权派，乙：害怕群众把革命闹。丙：为了保自己的乌纱帽，丁：又玩弄新的阴谋和花招。戊：这，一万个办不到。甲：他们用物质刺激来进攻，乙：企图把斗争方向往邪道领。丙：他们想挑动群众斗群众，丁：制造停电、停水、停交通，戊：我们坚决不答应。甲：我们是红色造反者，乙：心红眼亮方向明。丙：我们紧紧跟着毛主席。丁：反动家伙要敢动，戊：就造他的反，专他的政。甲：我们是红色造反者，乙：敢说、敢干、敢斗争。丙：谁敢破坏革命，丁：谁敢破坏生产，戊：我们就造他的反，革他的命。甲：我们是红色造反者，乙：毛主席教导句句听。丙：抓革命，促生产，丁：我们坚决来响应，戊：我们认真来执行。甲：我们是红色造反者，乙：大团结，大联合，共同来对敌，丙：彻底粉碎资产阶级反动路线的新反
    扑，丁：把无产阶级文化大革命进行到底。戊：夺取革命生产双胜利！
    北京第一机床厂红色造反宣传队
