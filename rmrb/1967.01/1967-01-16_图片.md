Title: The People of Zanzibar Love Chairman Mao and Warmly Praise China's Great Proletarian Cultural Revolution
Author:
Date: 1967-01-16
Edition: 4

The revolutionary people of Zanzibar have an unbounded love for Chairman Mao and enthusiastically praise China's Great Proletarian Cultural Revolution. The picture shows the people of Zanzibar intently viewing a photograph of Chairman Mao with the Red Guards.
