	Chairman Mao's Quotations
		All revolutionary struggles in the world are aimed at seizing power and consolidating it. The counter-revolutionaries' desperate struggle against the revolutionary forces is entirely for the purpose of maintaining their power.

### 毛主席语录

1967-01-31
第1版()
专栏：

    毛主席语录
    世界上一切革命斗争都是为着夺取政权，巩固政权。而反革命的拚死同革命势力斗争，也完全是为着维持他们的政权。
