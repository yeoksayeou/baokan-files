Title: Comrade Shehu Praises the Decisive Significance of China's Cultural Revolution for the World Revolution
Author:
Date: 1967-01-31
Edition: 5

	Comrade Shehu, a member of the Political Bureau of the Central Committee of the Albanian Labor Party and Chairman of the Council of Ministers, said in a speech at the celebration of the 20th anniversary of the reconstruction of the November 7th Polytechnic School in Tirana on January 28 that the victory of the Chinese Cultural Revolution of the proletariat has decisive significance for the fate of the world revolution.

	Comrade Shehu said, the Chinese Cultural Revolution of the proletariat has great significance. The Cultural Revolution of the proletariat in China, which erupted like a volcano with rolling lava and is being carried out under the guidance of the great Marxist-Leninist thought of Comrade Mao Zedong, has historical and world significance because this great revolution guarantees the socialist path of great China, smashes and buries the dreams of the bourgeoisie and revisionism, sweeps away the garbage of the bourgeoisie, reactionaries, and revisionism, and revolutionizes the ideological consciousness of the Chinese people who make up one-fourth of the world's population, thereby inspiring all revolutionaries and oppressed peoples in the world.

	Comrade Shehu expressed that the Albanian Labor Party and the Albanian people support the Cultural Revolution of the proletariat in China and cheer for the victory of the great Marxist-Leninist thought of Comrade Mao Zedong.

### 谢胡同志赞扬中国文化大革命对世界革命具有决定性意义  把资产阶级和修正主义垃圾一扫光

1967-01-31
第5版()
专栏：

    谢胡同志赞扬中国文化大革命对世界革命具有决定性意义
    把资产阶级和修正主义垃圾一扫光
    新华社地拉那三十日电　阿尔巴尼亚劳动党中央政治局委员、部长会议主席谢胡同志二十八日在地拉那十一月七日综合技术学校庆祝学校重建二十周年大会上讲话时说，中国无产阶级文化大革命的胜利，对于世界革命的命运具有决定性的意义。
    谢胡同志说，中国的无产阶级文化大革命具有伟大的意义。象岩浆滚滚的火山一样爆发并在毛泽东同志伟大的马克思列宁主义思想指导下进行的中国无产阶级文化大革命，具有历史和世界意义，因为这场大革命保证了伟大的中国的社会主义道路，粉碎和埋葬了资产阶级、修正主义的梦想，扫除了资产阶级、反动派、修正主义的垃圾，使占人类四分之一的中国人民的思想意识革命化，从而鼓舞了世界上的所有革命者和被压迫人民。
    谢胡同志表示，阿尔巴尼亚劳动党和阿尔巴尼亚人民支持中国的无产阶级文化大革命，欢呼毛泽东同志的伟大的马克思列宁主义思想的胜利。
