Title: Celebrating the Fourth Anniversary of the Malian National Army
Author:
Date: 1967-01-23
Edition: 5

	Malian capital Bamako held a grand military parade on the morning of January 20 to celebrate the fourth anniversary of the Malian National Army.

	Before the parade, President and Minister of Defense Modibo Keita said that the Malian army is a strong defender of the country's independence, territorial integrity, and revolutionary achievements. It also participates in the country's economic construction to promote the development of the national economy.

	Subsequently, Keita, in an open-top car, inspected the troops. Then, paratroopers, ground forces, artillery, and tank units marched past the reviewing stand and accepted the inspection of Keita and other Malian leaders.

### 庆祝马里国家军队建军四周年　巴马科隆重举行阅兵式

1967-01-23
第5版()
专栏：

　　庆祝马里国家军队建军四周年
　　巴马科隆重举行阅兵式
    据新华社巴马科二十日电　马里首都巴马科二十日上午举行隆重阅兵仪式，庆祝马里国家军队建军四周年。
    总统兼国防部长凯塔在阅兵式之前发表讲话说，马里军队是我国独立与领土完整以及革命成果的坚强保卫者，它也参加国家的经济建设，以促进国家经济的发展。
    随后，凯塔总统乘敞篷汽车检阅了军队。接着，伞兵、地面部队、炮兵和坦克部队列队走过检阅台，接受凯塔总统和马里其他领导人的检阅。
