Title: Johnson Faces a Year of Pain and Difficulty
Author:
Date: 1967-01-13
Edition: 5

Editor's Note: At the end of the year and the beginning of the new year, American bourgeois newspapers have published numerous articles discussing the insurmountable predicaments faced by the Johnson administration in both domestic and foreign policies. What they reflect is not only the pitiful state of Johnson, the ringleader of the bandits, but also the true picture of American imperialism's declining fortunes.

	“…(Johnson) is burdened with worries, facing enormous problems, and needs to make painful decisions… He feels that things are overwhelming him.”
     January 6, *New York Herald Tribune*

	“Johnson is facing a year of pain and difficulty… The cost of the war has far exceeded the target, and the deficit is astonishingly high. Obviously, taxes need to be increased to cover the expenses, but the President is concerned that increasing taxes might exacerbate the economic recession he is trying to avoid.”
	December 31, 1966, *Chicago Daily News*

	“In the intense war zones of Vietnam, there is nothing to be joyful about in 1967. It seems that the war is intensifying for the Americans. This means more troops will be sent, and there will be more casualties.”
	January 9, *U.S. News & World Report*

	“High-ranking U.S. officials, when studying the world situation, found that as 1966 came to a close, there was nothing inspiring… Even without the Vietnam problem, the United States still has major headaches—Africa, France, the Middle East, and especially Communist China.”
	December 26, 1966, *U.S. News & World Report*

### 约翰逊面临痛苦和困难的一年

1967-01-13
第5版()
专栏：外论摘要

    约翰逊面临痛苦和困难的一年
    编者按：岁尾年首，美国资产阶级报刊发表了不少文章，谈论约翰逊政府在对内对外政策上面临的无法解救的困境。它们所反映的不仅是约翰逊这个强盗头子的狼狈相，也是美帝国主义日暮途穷的真实写照。
    “……（约翰逊）是心事重重的，面临着巨大的问题，需要做出令人痛苦的决定。……他感到事情使他穷于应付。”
     一月六日《纽约先驱论坛报》
    “约翰逊正面临着痛苦和困难的一年。……战争费用的计算已经超过目标很多，赤字高得惊人。显然需要用增税来支付开支，但是总统担心增税可能助长他正在设法避免的经济衰退。”
        一九六六年十二月三十一日
                  《芝加哥每日新闻》
    “在越南的战争激烈地区，人们看不出在一九六七年有什么可高兴的。看起来，对美国人来说，战争似乎要加强。这意味着要派更多的人，有更大的伤亡。”
  一月九日《美国新闻与世界报道》
    “美国高级官员在研究世界局势的时候，发现在一九六六年接近结束的时候没有什么情况是使人感到欢欣鼓舞的。……即使没有越南问题，美国仍然有着令人头痛的重大问题——非洲、法国、中东、特别是共产党中国。”
    一九六六年十二月二十六日
          《美国新闻与世界报道》
