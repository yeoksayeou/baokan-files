Revolutionaries in the Countryside Have Just Cause
The Canary Islands Struggle for Self-Determination and Independence Supports the Shanghai Revolutionaries in the Countryside

New Xinhua News Agency, Algiers: The General Secretariat of the Canary Islands Struggle for Self-Determination and Independence sent a telegram on the 26th from here to the revolutionaries in the countryside of Shanghai, expressing fervent support for the revolutionary movement of the Shanghai countryside.

The telegram states: "We hail the revolutionary movement of the Shanghai countryside, which is acting according to the instructions of Chairman Mao Zedong to 'grasp revolution and promote production.' All revolutionaries around the world stand with you, because we know that you are completely justified, and you will achieve victory in the victorious struggle."

The telegram points out that the revisionist elements who engage in counter-revolutionary economism have abandoned the revolutionary road of socialism and the fundamental principles of Marxism-Leninism. The telegram condemns the counter-revolutionary revisionist clique for promoting economism, aiming to extinguish the revolutionary flames of the Chinese people.

The telegram expresses confidence that: the vast revolutionary peasant masses in China, together with the vast revolutionary workers, revolutionary cadres, revolutionary students, and revolutionary intellectuals, will quickly unite to form a great alliance of revolutionary rebels of the proletariat, to carry the Cultural Revolution through to the end, and to carry out the large-scale class struggle within the country to the end.

The telegram states: "Our eyes are now turned towards your country, towards you, and we have full confidence in your revolutionary movement, because we know that the great thoughts and teachings of Mao Zedong guide you, leading you through the Long March, straight to the victory of socialism."

(Editor's note: The Canary Islands are a colony of Spain. Located in the Atlantic Ocean northwest of the African continent, they consist of thirteen volcanic islands. The population is over 900,000.)

### 革命农民造反有理　加那利群岛争取自决和独立运动支持上海革命农民运动

1967-01-31
第5版()
专栏：

　　革命农民造反有理
　　加那利群岛争取自决和独立运动支持上海革命农民运动
    新华社阿尔及尔电　加那利群岛争取自决和独立运动总书记处二十六日从这里打电报给上海的革命农民，表示热烈支持上海的革命农民运动。
    电文说：“我们欢呼上海的革命农民运动按照毛泽东主席提出的‘抓革命，促生产’的指示办事”。“全世界革命者同你们站在一起，因为我们知道，你们是完全有理的，你们将在胜利的战斗中取得胜利。”
    电文指出，搞反革命经济主义的修正主义分子，放弃了社会主义的革命道路和马克思列宁主义的根本原则。电报谴责反革命修正主义集团搞经济主义，其目的在于扑灭中国人民的革命烈火。
    电文表示确信：中国广大的革命农民群众与广大的革命工人、革命干部、革命学生和革命知识分子群众将很快地联合起来，组成无产阶级革命造反派的大联合，把文化大革命进行到底，把国内大规模的阶级斗争进行到底。
    电文说：“我们的眼睛现在转向着你们的国家，转向着你们，而且我们对你们的革命运动具有充分的信心，因为我们知道，伟大的毛泽东的思想和教导指引着你们，引导着你们通过长征，一直走向社会主义的胜利。”
        （编者注：加那利群岛是西班牙的殖民地。位于非洲大陆西北的大西洋上，由十三个火山岛组成。有九十多万人口。）
