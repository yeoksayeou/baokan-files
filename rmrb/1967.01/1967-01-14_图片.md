Title: Revolutionary Workers at Wuhan Diesel Engine Factory Advance Production During the Cultural Revolution
Author:
Date: 1967-01-14
Edition: 3

Revolutionary workers at the Wuhan Diesel Engine Factory, during the Great Proletarian Cultural Revolution, are following Chairman Mao's instructions, seizing the revolution, and promoting production, thereby powerfully advancing the development of production. This is a scene of workers in Workshop 5 assembling the 195 diesel engine.
