Title: General Vo Nguyen Giap Congratulates Vice Premier Lin Biao on China's Rapid Development in Nuclear Weapons Science and Technology
Author:
Date: 1967-01-01
Edition: 3

Vice Premier and Minister of National Defense Lin Biao received a telegram from Vice Premier and Minister of National Defense of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam, General Vo Nguyen Giap, congratulating China on its latest successful nuclear test.

The telegram expresses warm congratulations to the Chinese people for achieving more and greater victories under the leadership of the glorious Communist Party of China headed by Chairman Mao. It states: "China's nuclear test this time is just two months after China successfully conducted a missile and nuclear weapons test, marking a new and great victory for the Chinese people. This demonstrates the extremely rapid development of China's nuclear weapons science and technology. It is a powerful encouragement for the Vietnamese military and people in their struggle against the United States for national salvation and for the national liberation movements in Asia, Africa, and Latin America. It is also a great contribution to the cause of safeguarding world peace."

### 武元甲大将致电林彪副总理  祝贺我核武器科学技术发展极快

1967-01-01
第3版()
专栏：

    武元甲大将致电林彪副总理
    祝贺我核武器科学技术发展极快
    新华社三十一日讯　国务院副总理兼国防部长林彪收到了越南民主共和国副总理兼国防部长武元甲大将发来的电报，热烈祝贺我国新的核爆炸成功。
    电报祝兄弟的中国人民在以毛主席为首的光荣的中国共产党领导下取得更多、更巨大的胜利。电报说：“中国的这次核试验，是在中国成功地进行导弹核武器试验刚两个月之后进行的，是中国人民的新的巨大的胜利。这件事证明了中国的核武器科学技术发展极为迅速。这对越南军民的抗美救国事业和对亚洲、非洲、拉丁美洲人民的民族解放斗争运动是一个强有力的鼓舞，同时对保卫世界和平事业是一个大贡献。”
