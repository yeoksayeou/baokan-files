Title: Revolutionaries Rebel for the Public, Not for Themselves
Author: Běijīng Dì'èr Jīqìlǎng Chǎng Gémìng Zàofǎn Zǒngbù Wényì Xuānchuán Zǔ
Date: 1967-01-21
Edition: 6

	Revolutionaries rebel, for the public, not for themselves
	[Duet]

	Speaker A: The principles of Marxism
	Speaker B: Are countless and intricate,
	Speaker A: But ultimately, they boil down to one phrase:
	Speaker B: "Rebellion is justified."

	Speaker A: We revolutionaries,
	Speaker B: Hold high the red flag,
	Speaker A: We revolutionaries,
	Speaker B: Follow Chairman Mao closely,
	Speaker A: Stride forward with heads held high,
	Speaker B: Bravely fight to achieve victory!

	Speaker A: Class struggle is fierce and complex,
	Speaker B: Revolutionaries must stay vigilant.
	Speaker A: Thunder and lightning are welcome,
	Speaker B: They test whether you are an eagle or a chicken;
	Speaker A: We fear not sugar-coated bullets,
	Speaker B: Money and benefits cannot sway our resolve.
	Speaker A: A few flies buzzing and whimpering,
	Speaker B: Cannot soften our iron will;
	Speaker A: The class enemy is stubbornly resisting,
	Speaker B: We must expose their deceit.

	Speaker A: Completely crush the new counterattack of the capitalist road,
	Speaker B: Resolutely oppose economism.
	Speaker A: Whoever opposes Chairman Mao,
	Speaker B: Smash their dog's head.
	Speaker A: Whoever opposes Chairman Mao,
	Speaker B: Knock them down to the ground!

	Speaker A: Rebel, rebel, rebel thoroughly,
	Speaker B: Revolution, revolution, revolution to the end.
	Speaker A: With four volumes of brilliant texts shining,
	Speaker B: Our hearts turn to the great leader Chairman Mao.
	Speaker A: March forward,
	Speaker B: Hold high the red flag of revolution;
	Speaker A: Grasp revolution, promote production,
	Speaker B: Never leave the battle station.
	Speaker A: Revolutionaries rebel,
	Speaker B: For the public, not for themselves.
	Speaker A: Charge, charge, charge,
	Speaker B: Charge through the currents and rapids;
	Speaker A: Break through, break through, break through,
	Speaker B: Break through to a fiery new world!

	Together: Long live the Communist Party!
	Long live Chairman Mao!

### 革命造反派　为公不为己〔对口词〕
北京第二机床厂革命造反总部文艺宣传组
1967-01-21
第6版()
专栏：

　　革命造反派　为公不为己
　　〔对口词〕
　　北京第二机床厂革命造反总部文艺宣传组甲：马克思主义的道理乙：千条万绪，甲：归根结底，就是一句
    话：乙：“造反有理。”甲：我们革命造反派，乙：高高举起大红旗，甲：我们革命造反派，乙：紧紧跟着毛主席，甲：昂首阔步朝前迈，乙：勇猛战斗夺取胜利！甲：阶级大搏斗，乙：尖锐复杂；甲：革命造反派，乙：要提高警惕。甲：电闪雷鸣来得好，乙：考验你是鹰还是鸡；甲：糖衣炮弹也不怕，乙：金钱利诱志不移。甲：几只苍蝇嗡嗡抽泣，乙：软化不了咱的钢铁意
      志；甲：阶级敌人负隅顽抗，乙：咱一定要戳穿他的画
    皮。甲：彻底打垮资产阶级反
    动路线的新反扑，乙：坚决反对经济主义。甲：谁反对毛主席，乙：就砸烂他的狗头。甲：谁反对毛主席，乙：就把他打翻在地！甲：造反，造反，造的透，乙：革命，革命，革到底。甲：怀抱着四卷雄文金光
    闪闪，乙：心向着伟大领袖毛主
    席。甲：向前进，乙：革命红旗高高举；甲：抓革命，促生产，乙：战斗岗位决不离。甲：革命造反派，乙：为公不为己。甲：冲，冲，冲，乙：冲过逆流和险滩；甲：闯，闯，闯，乙：闯出个火红的新世
    界！合：万岁万岁共产党！
    万岁万岁毛主席！
