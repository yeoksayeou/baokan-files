Title: At the Invitation of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of China, Comrades Kabo and Shytura Arrive in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1967-01-14
Edition: 1

At the invitation of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of China, Comrade Hysni Kapo, a member of the Political Bureau and Secretary of the Central Committee of the Party of Labour of Albania, and Central Committee member Behar Shytura, arrived in Beijing by special plane today.

Comrades Hysni Kapo and Behar Shytura stopped over in Shanghai on their way to Beijing. Comrade Liu Xiao, a member of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of China, went to Shanghai to welcome them and accompanied them to Beijing.

The Albanian comrades were warmly welcomed at the Beijing Capital Airport by Comrade Kang Sheng, a member of the Political Bureau and Secretary of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of China, Comrade Liu Ningyi, a Secretary of the Central Committee, Comrade Wu Xiuquan, a member of the Central Committee, and Comrade Wang Li, a member of the Central Group for the Cultural Revolution. The Chinese comrades and the Albanian comrades warmly embraced each other and exchanged cordial greetings. Young men and women presented flowers to the Albanian comrades.

Comrade Bekir Balluku, a member of the Political Bureau and Vice Premier and Minister of Defense of the Council of Ministers of Albania, and head of the Albanian Military Delegation, who is currently visiting Beijing, and Comrade Vasili Natanaeli, the Albanian Ambassador to China, also went to the airport to welcome the Albanian comrades.

### 应中国共产党中央委员会的邀请  卡博和什图拉同志到达北京  康生、巴卢库、纳塔奈利等同志到机场热烈欢迎

1967-01-14
第1版()
专栏：

    应中国共产党中央委员会的邀请
    卡博和什图拉同志到达北京
    康生、巴卢库、纳塔奈利等同志到机场热烈欢迎
    新华社十三日讯  应中国共产党中央委员会的邀请，阿尔巴尼亚劳动党中央政治局委员、书记处书记希斯尼·卡博，中央委员贝哈尔·什图拉，今天乘专机到达北京。
    希斯尼·卡博同志和贝哈尔·什图拉同志在来京途中路过上海。中共中央委员刘晓同志专程前往上海迎接，并陪同来京。
    阿尔巴尼亚同志们在首都机场，受到中共中央政治局常委、书记处书记康生，中共中央书记处书记刘宁一，中共中央委员伍修权，中央文化革命小组成员王力等同志最热烈的欢迎。中国同志和阿尔巴尼亚同志热烈拥抱，互致亲切问候。男女少年向阿尔巴尼亚同志献了花。
    正在北京访问的阿尔巴尼亚劳动党中央政治局委员、部长会议副主席兼国防部长、阿尔巴尼亚军事代表团团长贝基尔·巴卢库同志，阿尔巴尼亚驻中国大使瓦西里·纳塔奈利同志，也到机场迎接。
