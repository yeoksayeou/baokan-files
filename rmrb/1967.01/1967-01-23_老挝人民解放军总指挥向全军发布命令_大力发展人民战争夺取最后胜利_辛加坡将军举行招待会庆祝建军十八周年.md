Title: Lao People's Liberation Army Commander Issues Order to the Entire Army to Intensify People's War to Achieve Final Victory; Singapore General Holds Reception to Celebrate 18th Anniversary of the Army
Author:
Date: 1967-01-23
Edition: 5

The commander-in-chief of the Lao People's Liberation Army issues an order to the entire army

Intensify the people's war to achieve final victory

Singapore General holds a reception to celebrate the 18th anniversary of the army

According to Xinhua News Agency, Khamkeo, January 21: According to the Pathet Lao radio broadcast on the 20th, the commander-in-chief of the Lao People's Liberation Army Supreme Command, Khamtai Siphandon, issued an order to the entire army to celebrate the 18th anniversary of the Lao People's Liberation Army. The order calls on the main forces, local forces, and guerrilla units of the Lao People's Liberation Army to intensify guerrilla warfare nationwide, promote the people's war, actively and proactively annihilate the enemy, and seize the final victory from American imperialism and its lackeys.

The order states that over the past 18 years, the Lao people's army, under the wise leadership of the Party Central Committee, with the help and care of people of all ethnic groups, has grown and strengthened rapidly in the crucible of revolutionary struggle, making significant contributions to the cause of national liberation. The Lao People's Liberation Army has developed from scattered guerrilla units and small armed propaganda teams into a powerful revolutionary armed force with the traditions and qualities of a people's army, excellent cadres and soldiers, and a high degree of patriotism and hatred for the enemy. The order calls on the Lao People's Liberation Army to: mobilize the people to establish and consolidate revolutionary organizations, build revolutionary villages, combat villages, and combat zones; enhance self-reliance, increase production, and practice economy to ensure good results in military building and combat; actively cultivate revolutionary qualities, carry forward the excellent traditions of the Lao nationality and people's army, and deepen hatred for the enemy; strengthen unity with patriotic neutral forces to jointly defeat American imperialism and its lackeys.

According to Xinhua News Agency, Khamkeo, January 21: The command of the Xieng Khouang Military Region of the Lao People's Liberation Army held a reception on January 16 to warmly celebrate the 18th anniversary of the Lao People's Liberation Army. Representatives of the Lao People's Liberation Army Supreme Command, General Singapore, and other relevant department heads such as Samane attended the reception.

The heads of the Chinese Economic and Cultural Mission to Laos and the Vietnamese Democratic Republic's Economic and Cultural Mission to Laos were also invited to attend the reception.

General Singapore said in his speech at the reception that over the past 18 years, the Lao People's Liberation Army has grown and strengthened in the fire of revolutionary struggle and achieved great victories in the current anti-American patriotic struggle.

General Singapore, in his speech, thanked the Chinese and Vietnamese peoples for their active support for the anti-American patriotic struggle of the Lao people. He said that the 700 million Chinese people have given multi-faceted support to the revolutionary struggle of the Lao people.

The head of the Chinese Economic and Cultural Mission to Laos, Zhu Ying, spoke at the reception, extending warm congratulations to all officers and soldiers of the Lao People's Liberation Army who are on the front line of the anti-American struggle.

He said: The Chinese people and the Chinese People's Liberation Army, armed with the great thought of Mao Zedong, firmly support the anti-American patriotic struggle of the Lao people and firmly support the Vietnamese people in carrying the anti-American national salvation struggle to the end.

### 老挝人民解放军总指挥向全军发布命令　大力发展人民战争夺取最后胜利　辛加坡将军举行招待会庆祝建军十八周年

1967-01-23
第5版()
专栏：

    老挝人民解放军总指挥向全军发布命令
　　大力发展人民战争夺取最后胜利
　　辛加坡将军举行招待会庆祝建军十八周年
    据新华社康开二十一日电　据巴特寮电台二十日广播：老挝人民解放军最高指挥部总指挥坎代·西番顿，为庆祝老挝人民解放军建军十八周年向全军发布命令，号召老挝人民解放军主力部队、地方部队和民兵游击队在全国范围内大力发展游击战争，大力促进人民战争，积极地、主动地消灭敌人，向美帝国主义及其走狗夺取最后的胜利。
    命令说，十八年来，老挝人民军队在党中央的英明领导下，在各族人民的帮助和爱护下，在革命斗争的熔炉中，得到了迅速的成长和壮大，为民族的解放事业作出了重大的贡献。老挝人民解放军已经从零星的游击队和弱小的武装宣传队，发展成为一支具有人民军队传统和素质，有着优秀的干部和战士以及具有高度的爱国热忱和对敌人刻骨仇恨的雄厚的革命武装力量。命令号召老挝人民解放军：努力动员人民建立和巩固革命组织，建设革命村、战斗村和战斗区；提高自力更生精神，大力增加生产和厉行节约，保证建军和战斗获得良好的成果；积极培养革命品质，发扬老挝民族和人民军队的优良传统，加深对敌人的仇恨；加强同爱国中立力量的团结，共同战胜美帝国主义及其走狗。
    据新华社康开二十一日电　老挝人民解放军川圹军区指挥部一月十六日举行招待会，热烈庆祝老挝人民解放军建军十八周年。老挝人民解放军最高指挥部代表辛加坡将军以及其他有关部门负责人沙曼等出席了招待会。
    中国驻老挝经济文化代表团和越南民主共和国驻老挝经济文化代表团负责人都应邀出席了招待会。
    辛加坡将军在招待会上讲话说，十八年来，老挝人民解放军在革命斗争的烈火中不断发展、壮大，在当前的抗美爱国斗争中取得了很大的胜利。
    辛加坡将军在讲话中感谢中国人民和越南人民对老挝人民的抗美爱国斗争的积极支持。他说，七亿中国人民对老挝人民的革命斗争给予了多方面的支持。
    中国驻老挝经济文化代表团负责人朱英在招待会上讲话，向站在反美斗争前线的老挝人民解放军全体指战员致以热烈的祝贺。
    他表示：用伟大的毛泽东思想武装起来的中国人民和中国人民解放军坚决支持老挝人民的抗美爱国斗争，坚决支持越南人民把抗美救国斗争进行到底。（附图片）
