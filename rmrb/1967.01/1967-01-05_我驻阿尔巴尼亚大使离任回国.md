Title: Chinese Ambassador to Albania Completes Term and Returns to China
Author:
Date: 1967-01-05
Edition: 4

Chinese Ambassador to Albania, Xu Jianguo, completed his term and returned to China on January 3.

Albanian Deputy Foreign Minister Maliqi and the Director of the Protocol Department of the Foreign Ministry, Skolo Voti, and others went to the Tirana Airport to see him off.

The Chinese Embassy's Chargé d'Affaires, Wang Jincao, and other diplomatic staff, as well as representatives of Chinese experts and students in Albania, also went to the airport to bid farewell. Various foreign diplomats stationed in Albania also went to the airport to see him off.

### 我驻阿尔巴尼亚大使离任回国

1967-01-05
第4版()
专栏：

    我驻阿尔巴尼亚大使离任回国
    新华社地拉那四日电　中华人民共和国驻阿尔巴尼亚大使许建国三日离任回国。
    阿尔巴尼亚外交部副部长马利列和外交部礼宾司司长斯科洛伏蒂等到地拉那机场送行。
    中国驻阿尔巴尼亚大使馆临时代办王锦川和其他外交官员以及在阿尔巴尼亚的中国专家和留学生的代表也到机场欢送。到机场送行的还有各国驻阿尔巴尼亚的外交使节。
