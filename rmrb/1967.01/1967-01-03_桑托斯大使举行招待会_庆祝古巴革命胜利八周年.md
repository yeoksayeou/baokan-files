Title: Ambassador Santos Holds Reception to Celebrate the Eighth Anniversary of the Cuban Revolution
Author:
Date: 1967-01-03
Edition: 6

Ambassador Santos of Cuba holds a reception to celebrate the eighth anniversary of the Cuban Revolution.

BEIJING, Jan. 2 (Xinhua) - Cuban Ambassador to China, Pino Santos, held a reception tonight to celebrate the eighth anniversary of the victory of the Cuban Revolution.

Vice Premier Chen Yi, Vice Chairman of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress Yang Mingxuan, Vice Minister of Foreign Affairs Qiao Guanhua, and other officials were invited to attend the reception. Ambassador Pino Santos and Vice Premier Chen Yi made toasts at the reception. Ambassadors from various countries to China were also invited to attend.

### 桑托斯大使举行招待会  庆祝古巴革命胜利八周年

1967-01-03
第6版()
专栏：

    桑托斯大使举行招待会
    庆祝古巴革命胜利八周年
    新华社二日讯　古巴共和国驻我国大使皮诺·桑托斯今晚举行招待会，庆祝古巴革命胜利八周年。
    国务院副总理陈毅、人大常委会副委员长杨明轩、外交部副部长乔冠华和各方面负责人应邀出席招待会。皮诺·桑托斯大使和陈毅副总理先后在招待会上祝酒。各国驻我国使节也应邀出席了招待会。
