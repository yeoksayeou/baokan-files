Title: Yemen's First Ambassador to China Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1967-01-27
Edition: 5

The first ambassador extraordinary and plenipotentiary of the Yemen Arab Republic to the People's Republic of China, Muhammad Abdul-Wasi Hamid, arrived in Beijing by airplane this evening.

### 也门首任驻华大使到京

1967-01-27
第5版()
专栏：

　　也门首任驻华大使到京
    新华社二十六日讯　阿拉伯也门共和国首任驻中华人民共和国特命全权大使穆罕默德·阿布杜勒·瓦西·哈米德，今晚乘飞机到达北京。
