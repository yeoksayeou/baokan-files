	Brief News

	North Vietnam Shoots Down Three U.S. Intruder Aircraft

	According to Hanoi, on the 4th, the military and civilians of Ninh Binh Province in North Vietnam shot down one invading U.S. aggressor aircraft, while the military and civilians of Thua Thien Province shot down two.

	Yemen President Meets with Afro-Asian Journalists' Association Delegation

	On the evening of the 3rd, Yemeni President Abdullah al-Sallal received the delegation of the Afro-Asian Journalists' Association in Taiz. He wished the second Afro-Asian Journalists' Conference success and said: "We should unite with all the peoples of the world who are fighting for freedom, stand shoulder to shoulder, and eliminate imperialism."

	Thai Authorities to Send More Cannon Fodder to South Vietnam

	Under the orders of U.S. imperialism, the Thanom-Kittikachorn traitorous clique of Thailand announced on the 3rd that Thailand will soon send another thousand mercenary troops to South Vietnam to serve as cannon fodder for the U.S. aggression against Vietnam. This mercenary force will be a mixed brigade including infantry, artillery, armored vehicles, and logistics units.

	Indonesian Rightists Return Four Companies to the United States

	The rightist military regime of Indonesia, which is a lackey of U.S. imperialism, on the 3rd brazenly ordered the return of four American oil companies, which the previous Indonesian government had taken over under strong public demand, to U.S. imperialism. These companies are Shell Oil Company, Texaco Oil Company, Standard Oil Company, and Pan American Oil Company. This is another crime committed by the Indonesian reactionaries in betraying national interests.

### 简明新闻

1967-01-06
第4版()
专栏：简明新闻

    简明新闻
    越南北方又击落入侵美机三架
    据河内公布，四日，越南北方宁平省军民击落入侵的美国强盗飞机一架，太平省军民击落两架。
    也门总统接见亚非记协代表团
    也门总统萨拉勒三日晚在塔伊兹接见亚非新闻工作者协会代表团。他预祝第二次亚非新闻工作者会议成功，并说：我们应当同世界上一切为自由而斗争的人民联合起来，并肩站在一起，消灭帝国主义。
    泰国当局又要增派炮灰到南越
    在美帝国主义的指使下，泰国他侬—巴博卖国集团三日宣布，泰国不久要增派一千名帮凶军到南越去为美国侵越战争充当炮灰。这支帮凶军将是一个包括有步兵、炮兵、装甲车和后勤部队的混合营。
    印尼右派把四家公司交还美国
    美帝国主义的走狗印度尼西亚右派军人政权，三日又悍然下令把前印度尼西亚政府在人民的强烈要求下进行管制的四家美国石油公司，即壳牌石油公司、德士古石油公司、美孚石油公司和泛美石油公司交还给美帝国主义。这是印尼反动派犯下的又一出卖民族利益的罪行。
                          （据新华社讯）
