Title: Vice Premier Xie Fuqi Meets with Vietnamese Guests
Author:
Date: 1967-01-24
Edition: 5

Vice Premier Xie Fuqi met with Vietnamese guests today.

New China News Agency, January 23rd: Vice Premier of the State Council Xie Fuqi, this afternoon, met with the delegation from the Ministry of Interior of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam, headed by Minister Vuong Van Kien. They had a cordial and friendly conversation. Charge d'affaires of the Vietnamese Embassy in China, Ly Chung Chi, was also present.

### 谢富治副总理接见越南客人

1967-01-24
第5版()
专栏：

　　谢富治副总理接见越南客人
    新华社二十三日讯　国务院副总理谢富治，今天下午接见了以越南民主共和国内务部长雍文谦为首的越南内务部代表团，同他们进行了亲切友好的谈话。越南驻中国大使馆临时代办黎终始也在座。
