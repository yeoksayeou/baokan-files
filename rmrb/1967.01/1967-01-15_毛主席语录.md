Title: Quotations from Chairman Mao
Author:
Date: 1967-01-15
Edition: 1

	The Chinese and Albanian parties and peoples should unite, all Marxist-Leninists in the world should unite, and all revolutionary peoples of the world should unite. We must overthrow imperialism, modern revisionism, and all reactionaries. A new world without imperialism, without capitalism, and without systems of exploitation must be established.

	From the Telegram of Congratulations to the Fifth Congress of the Party of Labour of Albania

### 毛主席语录

1967-01-15
第1版()
专栏：

    毛主席语录
    中阿两党、两国人民团结起来，全世界马克思列宁主义者团结起来，全世界革命人民团结起来，打倒帝国主义，打倒现代修正主义，打倒各国反动派。一个没有帝国主义、没有资本主义、没有剥削制度的新世界，一定要建立起来。
    《致阿尔巴尼亚劳动党第五次代表大会贺电》
