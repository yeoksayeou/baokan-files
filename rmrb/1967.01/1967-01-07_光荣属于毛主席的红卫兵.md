Title: Glory Belongs to Chairman Mao's Red Guards
Author: Aikewala Luobai'er
Date: 1967-01-07
Edition: 5

The red sun rises over the vast land,
Illuminating the giant standing in the East.
The waves of the Cultural Revolution surge,
The power of Mao Zedong Thought is boundless,
The filth and mud of the old world
Will be washed away completely.
The predecessors have passed the great red flag
Into the hands of the younger generation.
Chairman Mao's Red Guards,
You are the loyal defenders of the proletarian revolutionary cause.
You have been nurtured by revolutionary milk,
Serving the people wholeheartedly,
You fear no hardships,
And strive to seize all the positions of the bourgeoisie.
Our beloved Red Guards,
Your revolutionary actions are glorious,
In the hearts of the people of the world,
You are the hope of the new generation.
You are laying the foundation for the future society,
A society that will forever remain youthful.

### 光荣属于毛主席的红卫兵
艾克瓦拉·罗贝尔
1967-01-07
第5版()
专栏：

    光荣属于毛主席的红卫兵
    喀麦隆劳工总联合会书记　艾克瓦拉·罗贝尔
    红日在广阔的大地上升起，
    照耀着屹立在东方的巨人。
    文化大革命波涛汹涌，
    毛泽东思想威力无穷，
    旧世界的污泥浊水，
    必将被涤荡一空。
    先辈们把伟大的红旗，
    传到了年青一代的手里。
    毛主席的红卫兵啊，
    是无产阶级革命事业忠实捍卫者。
    你们由革命奶汁哺育成长，
    为人民服务全心全意，
    你们不怕一切困难，
    要夺取资产阶级的一切阵地。
    我们心爱的红卫兵啊，
    你们的革命行动战果辉煌，
    在世界人民的心目中，
    你们是新生一代的希望。
    你们在为未来的社会奠定基础，
    这个社会啊，将永葆青春。
