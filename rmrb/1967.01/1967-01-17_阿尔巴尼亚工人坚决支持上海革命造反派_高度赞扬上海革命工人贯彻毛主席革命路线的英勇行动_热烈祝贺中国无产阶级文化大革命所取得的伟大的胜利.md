Title: Albanian Workers Resolutely Support Shanghai Revolutionary Rebels
Author:
Date: 1967-01-17
Edition: 1

Albanian workers resolutely support the Shanghai revolutionary rebels
Highly praise the Shanghai revolutionary workers for implementing Chairman Mao's revolutionary line of action
Warmly congratulate the great victory achieved in the Chinese Cultural Revolution

NEW CHINA NEWS AGENCY TIRANA, January 15 - On January 14, the broad masses of workers in Albania held meetings to express their most fervent and resolute support for the Shanghai revolutionary rebels in carrying out Chairman Mao's revolutionary line and launching a general offensive against the bourgeois reactionary line. They also expressed their heartfelt congratulations on the great victories already achieved and those that will be achieved in their heroic struggle. The workers of the Mao Zedong Textile Combine in Berat, numbering over 2,300, held a meeting on the afternoon of January 14 to express their enthusiastic support for the statement issued by the Shanghai revolutionary rebel organizations, "To the People of Shanghai," and the "Urgent Notice." They highly praised the Shanghai working class for their loyalty to Mao Zedong Thought and their resolute implementation of the proletarian revolutionary line represented by Mao Zedong.

At the meeting, the editorial of the Albanian newspaper "Voice of the People" published on January 14, titled "Mao Zedong's Revolutionary Line Shatters the Reactionary Conspiracy of a Handful of Revisionists and Conspirators," and the "Urgent Notice" issued by 32 revolutionary rebel organizations in Shanghai, as well as other relevant documents on the Chinese Cultural Revolution, were read.

The chairman of the trade union of this factory, Eta Mebeli, and three workshop trade union chairmen spoke at the meeting.

The meeting unanimously adopted a letter to the Shanghai working class. The entire factory selected two representatives to travel from Berat to Tirana to deliver the letter to the Chinese Embassy in Albania, asking it to be forwarded to the Shanghai workers.

The workers of the Enver Hoxha Machine Factory, after reading with great enthusiasm the Albanian newspapers' reprints of the telegram of congratulations from the Central Committee of the Communist Party of China, the State Council, the Central Military Commission, and the Central Cultural Revolution Group to the revolutionary rebel organizations in Shanghai, and the "Urgent Notice" issued by 32 revolutionary rebel organizations in Shanghai, also held a meeting for all the workers in the factory on the afternoon of January 14. At the meeting, the factory director, Cheriku, and some workers spoke, all expressing their enthusiastic support for the revolutionary actions of the Shanghai workers and revolutionary rebel organizations. They said that the Shanghai working class is a close comrade-in-arms of the Albanian working class, and we will always support their heroic struggle to defend the revolutionary line of Mao Zedong. The veteran worker and technical innovation expert, Barbulushi, said that the struggle of the revolutionary workers in Shanghai to defend Mao Zedong Thought is also a struggle to defend Marxism-Leninism. We firmly support their revolutionary actions. The branch secretary, Aliki, said that we firmly support the revolutionary actions taken by the Shanghai workers to advance the Chinese Cultural Revolution.

The workers of the Tirana Tractor Parts Factory, on January 14, rushed to read the newspapers as soon as they got up in the morning. They excitedly discussed the telegram of congratulations from the Central Committee of the Communist Party of China, the State Council, the Central Military Commission, and the Central Cultural Revolution Group, and the revolutionary actions taken by the Shanghai workers and revolutionary rebel organizations. At the meeting of all the workers in the factory, the workers enthusiastically shouted, "Long live the Communist Party of China!" "Long live Mao Zedong!" They celebrated the victory of the proletarian revolutionary line represented by Mao Zedong.

The members of the Albania-China Friendship Association delegation who had just returned from visiting China, the foreman of the smelting workshop, Koco, and some worker representatives spoke. Koco detailed the situation of the Chinese Cultural Revolution to the workers. He expressed his firm belief that the proletarian revolutionary line formulated by the great Mao Zedong will thoroughly smash the frenzied counterattack of the bourgeois reactionary line and all the ghosts and monsters.

The female worker, Kurti, said, "I am extremely happy to see the Shanghai workers and revolutionary rebel organizations holding high the great red banner of Mao Zedong Thought, defending the revolutionary line of Mao Zedong, and fighting heroically to overthrow and eliminate a handful of revisionists and reactionaries, and the great victories they have achieved."

The workshop foreman, Vesel Bona, who had studied in China, said, "We Albanian workers fully support the Shanghai workers and revolutionary rebel organizations in taking all revolutionary actions to defend the great Mao Zedong Thought and the unprecedented proletarian cultural revolution. The situation in Shanghai shows that class struggle does exist in a socialist society, and class enemies must be eliminated through class struggle." He also said, "The workers of our factory, like the Albanian working class, sincerely congratulate the great achievements of the Chinese Cultural Revolution. This achievement will further promote the great friendship between the two parties and peoples of Albania and China, forged by Comrade Enver Hoxha and Chairman Mao."

The workers of the Stalin Textile Combine also unanimously expressed their resolute support for the revolutionary actions of the Shanghai workers and revolutionary rebel organizations, and highly praised their great victories achieved in the Cultural Revolution.

At the meetings of these factories, letters of congratulation were also unanimously adopted in the name of all the workers to the revolutionary workers and revolutionary rebel organizations in Shanghai.

### 阿尔巴尼亚工人坚决支持上海革命造反派  高度赞扬上海革命工人贯彻毛主席革命路线的英勇行动  热烈祝贺中国无产阶级文化大革命所取得的伟大的胜利

1967-01-17
第1版()
专栏：

    阿尔巴尼亚工人坚决支持上海革命造反派
    高度赞扬上海革命工人贯彻毛主席革命路线的英勇行动
    热烈祝贺中国无产阶级文化大革命所取得的伟大的胜利
    新华社地拉那十五日电　阿尔巴尼亚的广大职工十四日分别举行集会，最热烈最坚决地支持上海革命造反派执行毛主席的革命路线，向资产阶级反动路线发起总攻击的革命行动，并表示衷心祝贺他们在英勇的斗争中已经取得和必将取得的伟大胜利。阿尔巴尼亚培拉特毛泽东纺织联合工厂的两千三百多名职工在十四日下午举行的集会上，热烈支持上海革命造反团体发表的《告上海全市人民书》和《紧急通告》，高度赞扬上海市工人阶级忠于毛泽东思想，坚决贯彻以毛泽东同志为代表的无产阶级革命路线。
    在集会上，宣读了阿尔巴尼亚《人民之声报》十四日发表的题为《毛泽东的革命路线粉碎了一小撮修正主义者和阴谋家的反动阴谋》的编辑部文章和上海市三十二个革命造反团体的《紧急通告》以及其它有关中国无产阶级文化大革命的文件。
    这家工厂的工会主席埃塔·梅贝利和三位车间工会主席在会上讲了话。
　　会上一致通过了一封给上海市工人阶级的信。全厂职工推选两位代表专程从培拉特来到地拉那，把信交给中国驻阿尔巴尼亚大使馆，托它转给上海工人。
    恩维尔·霍查机器厂的职工们，在满怀激情地阅读了阿尔巴尼亚报纸上转载的中共中央、国务院、中央军委、中央文革小组给上海市各革命造反团体的贺电和上海市三十二个革命造反组织的《紧急通告》后，十四日下午也举行了全厂职工的集会。在大会上，厂长切里库和一些工人讲了话，他们一致表示热烈地支持上海工人和革命造反组织的革命行动。他们说，上海工人阶级是阿尔巴尼亚工人阶级的亲密战友，我们永远支持他们为保卫毛泽东同志的革命路线所进行的英勇斗争。老工人、技术革新能手巴尔布鲁希说，上海市革命工人保卫毛泽东思想的斗争，也就是保卫马克思列宁主义的斗争，我们坚决支持他们的革命行动。车间支部书记阿利基说，我们坚决支持上海工人为把中国无产阶级文化大革命推向前进，所采取的革命行动。
    地拉那拖拉机配件工厂的工人，十四日一清早就争先恐后地抢着阅读报纸，他们兴奋地交谈中共中央、国务院、中央军委、中央文革小组的贺电和上海工人和革命造反组织采取的革命行动。在全厂职工大会上，职工们热情地高呼“中国共产党万岁！”、“毛泽东同志万岁！”欢呼以毛泽东同志为代表的无产阶级革命路线的胜利。
    刚刚访问中国归来的阿尔巴尼亚中国友好协会代表团团员、冶炼车间主任科乔和一些工人代表讲了话。科乔详细地向职工们介绍了中国文化大革命的情况。他表示坚决地相信：由伟大的毛泽东同志制定的无产阶级革命路线必将彻底粉碎资产阶级反动路线和一切牛鬼蛇神的疯狂反扑。
    这个工厂的女工库尔蒂说，我万分高兴地看到上海工人和革命造反组织高举毛泽东思想伟大红旗，捍卫毛泽东同志的革命路线，为推翻和消灭一小撮修正主义分子、反动分子所进行的英勇斗争和他们取得的巨大胜利。
    曾经在中国学习过的工段长韦塞尔·博纳伊说，我们阿尔巴尼亚工人阶级全心全意地支持上海工人和革命造反组织为保卫伟大的毛泽东思想和史无前例的无产阶级文化大革命所采取的一切革命行动。上海的情况说明，在社会主义社会的确存在着阶级斗争，必须以阶级斗争去消灭阶级敌人。他还说，我厂职工同阿尔巴尼亚工人阶级一样，衷心祝贺中国文化大革命所取得的伟大成就。这一成就必将进一步推进由恩维尔·霍查同志和毛主席所缔造的阿中两党、两国人民的伟大友谊。
    斯大林纺织联合工厂的职工们也一致表示坚决支持上海工人和革命造反组织的革命行动，热烈赞扬他们在无产阶级文化大革命中所取得的伟大胜利。
    在这几个工厂的职工大会上，也一致通过了以全厂职工的名义致上海革命工人和革命造反组织的祝贺信。
