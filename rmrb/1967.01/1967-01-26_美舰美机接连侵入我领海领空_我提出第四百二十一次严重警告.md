Edition: 6

U.S. Ships and Aircraft Repeatedly Intrude into Our Territorial Waters and Airspace; We Issue the 420th Severe Warning

	New China News Agency, January 25th
	On January 25th, from 9:48 AM to 1:37 PM, a U.S. military vessel intruded into our territorial waters south of Pinghai, Guangdong Province. On the same day, from 11:07 AM to 11:10 AM, 11:20 AM to 11:40 AM, and 11:50 AM to 11:54 AM, a U.S. military aircraft intruded into our airspace over the territorial waters north of Huoniao Island, Jiangsu Province, and east of Yancheng and Lianyungang. From 12:05 PM to 12:24 PM, another U.S. military aircraft intruded into our airspace over Yongxing Island and Dong Island in the Xisha Islands, Guangdong Province. In response to this military provocation by U.S. military vessels and aircraft, the spokesperson of our Ministry of Foreign Affairs, on behalf of the Ministry, issues the 420th severe warning.

### 美舰美机接连侵入我领海领空　我提出第四百二十一次严重警告

1967-01-26
第6版()
专栏：

　　美舰美机接连侵入我领海领空
　　我提出第四百二十一次严重警告
    新华社二十五日讯　一月二十五日九时四十八分至十三时三十七分美国军舰一艘，侵入我广东省平海以南地区领海。同日十一时零七分至十一时十分，十一时二十分至十一时四十分和十一时五十分至十一时五十四分，美国军用飞机一架，先后三次侵入我江苏省的花鸟岛以北、盐城和连云港以东地区的领海上空。十二时零五分至十二时二十四分又有美国军用飞机一架，侵入我广东省西沙群岛的永兴岛、东岛地区领空。对于美国军舰和军用飞机的这种军事挑衅，我外交部发言人奉命提出第四百二十一次严重警告。
