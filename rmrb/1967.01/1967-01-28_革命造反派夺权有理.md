Title: The Revolutionary Rebels Have the Right to Seize Power
Author: Wáng Wànqí
Date: 1967-01-28
Edition: 6

	Chairman Mao teaches us,
	The fundamental question of the revolution is the question of power.		A: The east wind blows ten thousand miles,
		B: The sunshine is brilliant.
		Both: The Great Proletarian Cultural Revolution has entered a new stage.
		A: The proletarian revolutionary factions unite,
		B: To overthrow those in power who are taking the capitalist road.
		A: For seventeen years, class struggle has never ceased,
		B: Those in power who are taking the capitalist road have done many evil deeds.
		Both: Today, the revolutionary rebels must seize power from their hands.
		A: The revolutionary rebels have formed a vast proletarian cultural revolutionary army,
		B: To fight against those in power who are taking the capitalist road!
		A: Listen, the war drums beat,
		B: Look, the red flags are flying.
		A: The army firmly supports the revolutionary rebels!
		B: The army firmly supports the seizure of power by the revolutionary rebels!
		A: Sweep away all harmful elements,
		B: Rebel against all those in power who are taking the capitalist road,
		And against all stubborn elements who insist on the bourgeois reactionary line!
		A: Some people say, "You are dictatorial!"
		B: We cannot give power to the bourgeoisie.
		Both: Proletarian revolutionaries must seize all power!
		A: The bourgeoisie wants to change the color of our country,
		B: How can we revolutionaries stand idly by!
		A: Smash the new counterattack of the bourgeois reactionary line,
		B: We must safeguard the proletarian state!
		A: Who will manage state affairs if we do not?
		B: Who will seize proletarian power if we do not?
		Both: Chairman Mao says, "The world belongs to us. The country belongs to us. Society belongs to us.
		A: If we do not speak, who will?
		B: If we do not act, who will?"
		A: The revolution has reason,
		B: The revolutionary rebels have reason to seize power!
		A: Seizing power is a life-and-death struggle,
		B: It is the key to the victory of the Cultural Revolution.
		A: The enemy will not perish on its own,
		B: Those in power who are taking the capitalist road will not relinquish power on their own!
		A: Abandon all illusions,
		B: Seize power from those in power who are taking the capitalist road.
		A: To seize power,
		B: One must make a firm decision and overcome all difficulties!
		A: To seize power,
		B: One must rely on the masses and follow the mass line!
		A: To seize power,
		B: One must not compromise or negotiate peacefully.
		A: To seize power,
		B: One must not be half-hearted or straddle two boats!
		A: To seize power,
		B: One must cast aside selfish thoughts and stand firmly on "dare"!
		A: To seize power,
		B: One must be thorough and not hesitate.
		A: Whoever opposes Chairman Mao's revolutionary line,
		B: We will firmly seize their power.
		A: To seize power,
		B: We will seize it completely and firmly!
		A: The proletarian revolutionary rebels,
		B: See seizing power, think seizing power, do seizing power!
		A: This is not about the revolutionary faction having personal ambitions,
		B: This is about seizing power for the proletariat, seizing power for communism!
		A: Look!
		B: The proletarian revolutionary faction has seized the power of those in power who are taking the capitalist road!
		Both: The proletarian revolutionary factions unite to rebel!
		Rebel! The revolutionary rebels unite to seize power!
		Seize power!
		A: Mao Zedong Thought is our guide to action,
		B: Billions of revolutionaries unite under the red banner of Mao Zedong Thought and advance bravely!
		A: We will smash the old world to pieces,
		B: A brand new world is about to appear!
		A: With great aspirations, we dare to change the heavens and the earth!
		B: Look forward to the day when the revolutionary rebels will definitely seize power!

### 革命造反派夺权有理
王万旗
1967-01-28
第6版()
专栏：

　　革命造反派夺权有理
　　战士　王万旗合：毛主席教导我们，
    革命的根本问题是政权问题。甲：东风万里，乙：阳光灿烂。合：无产阶级文化大革命进入了崭新的
    阶段。甲：无产阶级革命派大联合，乙：把走资本主义道路当权派打翻。甲：十七年来阶级斗争从不停息，乙：走资本主义道路当权派作恶多端。合：今天，革命造反派要从他们手中夺
    回大权。甲：革命造反派组成了一支浩浩荡荡的
    无产阶级文化革命大军，乙：向走资本主义道路的当权派开战！甲：听，战鼓咚咚，乙：看，红旗漫卷。甲：解放军坚决支持革命派造反！乙：解放军坚决支持革命派夺权！甲：横扫一切害人虫，乙：向一切走资本主义道路的当权派，
    向坚持资产阶级反动路线的顽固分
    子大造反！甲：有人说：“你们独裁！”乙：我们就是不能把大权分给资产阶
    级。合：无产阶级革命者就是要独揽大权！甲：资产阶级要我们国家变色，乙：我们革命者怎能坐视旁观！甲：粉碎资产阶级反动路线的新反扑，乙：我们要确保无产阶级江山！甲：国家大事我们不管谁管？乙：无产阶级大权我们不揽谁揽？合：毛主席说：“天下者我们的天下。国
    家者我们的国家。社会者我们的社
          会。甲：我们不说，谁说？乙：我们不干，谁干？”甲：革命造反有理，乙：革命造反派有理夺权！甲：夺权是一场你死我活的阶级斗争，乙：是文化大革命胜利的关键。甲：敌人不会自行消灭，乙：资产阶级当权派不会自行放弃大
    权！甲：丢掉一切幻想，乙：从走资本主义道路当权派手中夺
    权。甲：要夺权，乙：就得下定决心，排除万难！甲：要夺权，乙：必须依靠群众，走群众路线！甲：要夺权，乙：就不能和稀泥，和平谈判。甲：要夺权，乙：就不能搞折衷，脚踏两只船！甲：要夺权，乙：必须去掉私心杂念，一切立足于
      “敢”！甲：要夺权，乙：就要彻底，不能手软。甲：谁反对毛主席的革命路线，乙：就坚决夺他的权。甲：要夺权，乙：就一夺到底，彻底掌权！甲：无产阶级革命造反派，乙：看的是夺权，想的是夺权，干的是
    夺权！甲：这不是革命派有什么个人野心，乙：这是为无产阶级夺权，为共产主义
    夺权！甲：你看！乙：无产阶级革命派夺了一个个走资本
    主义道路当权派的权！合：无产阶级革命派联合起来造反！造
    反！革命造反派团结起来夺权！夺
    权！甲：毛泽东思想是我们行动的指南，乙：亿万革命者团结在毛泽东思想红旗
    下奋勇向前！甲：我们要把旧世界砸个粉碎，乙：一个崭新的新世界即将出现！甲：为有牺牲多壮志，乙：敢教日月换新天！合：喜看今日天下一定是革命造反派掌
    权！
