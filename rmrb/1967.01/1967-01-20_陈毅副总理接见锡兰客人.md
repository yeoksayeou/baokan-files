Title: Vice Premier Chen Yi Meets with Sri Lankan Guests
Author:
Date: 1967-01-20
Edition: 5

Vice Premier Chen Yi met with the General Secretary of the Sri Lankan Journalists' Association, Jayakody, this morning and had a friendly and cordial conversation with him. Present at the meeting were the General Secretary of the Asian-African Journalists' Association, Charito, and others.

### 陈毅副总理接见锡兰客人

1967-01-20
第5版()
专栏：

    陈毅副总理接见锡兰客人
    新华社十九日讯　陈毅副总理今天上午接见了锡兰新闻工作者协会总书记贾亚科迪，同他进行了亲切友好的谈话。接见时在座的有亚非新闻工作者协会总书记查禾多等。
