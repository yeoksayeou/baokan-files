Title: The Radiant Light of Mao Zedong's Thought on Arts Shines Across the World
Author:
Date: 1967-01-16
Edition: 6

The dance-drama "Beijing-Tirana" is a symbol of fraternal combat friendship. The cultural warriors sent by Chairman Mao have sung the hearts of the Chinese and Albanian peoples, united and fighting together: Long live our close friend Enver! Long live the great leader Mao Zedong!

"It is you who have brought us confidence, it is you who have brought us strength." The Japanese people warmly welcome and unanimously praise: "Mao Zedong's cultural warriors, Mao Zedong's Red Guards."

Palestinian youth learn to wield guns, determined to rebuild their homeland. The cultural soldiers of Mao Zedong have brought the sincere hearts of 700 million people, firmly supporting you in the struggle for national liberation.

Chinese cultural warriors go to Yemen, turning construction sites, fields, and valleys into performance venues. Yemen friends enthusiastically praise Chinese dance and song, saying it shines with the radiance of Mao Zedong Thought.

The hearts of the Chinese and Guinean peoples are connected, united against imperialism, and advancing side by side. Artists from both countries perform together, dancing and drumming to boost morale, vowing to dedicate their art to the people.

### 毛泽东文艺思想的光辉照耀全世界（图片）

1967-01-16
第6版()
专栏：

    毛泽东文艺思想的光辉照耀全世界
    歌舞《北京—地拉那》，是兄弟般战斗友谊的象征。毛主席派来的文艺战士，唱出了中阿两国人民团结、战斗的心声：万岁，我们亲密的朋友恩维尔！万岁，伟大的领袖毛泽东。
    “是你们给我们带来了信心，是你们给我们带来了力量。”日本人民热烈欢迎，日本人民同声赞扬：“毛泽东的文艺战士，毛泽东的红卫兵小将。”
    巴勒斯坦青年学拿枪，重建家园意志强。毛泽东的文艺兵带来了七亿颗赤诚的心，坚决支持你们，争取民族解放。
    中国文艺战士到也门，工地，田野，山谷变成了演出场，也门朋友热烈赞扬中国歌舞，说它闪耀着毛泽东思想的光芒。
    中国—几内亚人民心连心，团结反帝，并肩前进。两国艺术家同台欢舞鼓斗志，誓把艺术献人民。
