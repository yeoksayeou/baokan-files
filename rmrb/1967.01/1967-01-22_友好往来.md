Date: 1967-01-22
Edition: 6

Kuwaiti Prime Minister Receives Chinese Government Delegation

Kuwaiti Prime Minister Jaber Ahmad Jaber Al-Sabah received a Chinese government delegation led by Cao Chi on the 19th. The delegation is in Kuwait to preside over the opening ceremony of the People's Republic of China Economic and Trade Exhibition.

In his speech, the Kuwaiti Prime Minister welcomed the Chinese exhibition. He said: Our two countries have the same goals in the common struggle against imperialism and colonialism. We should unite to deal with our common enemies.

Albanian Comrades Celebrate with Red Guards in Wuhan

The Albanian National Song and Dance Ensemble held a celebration with hundreds of Red Guards and revolutionary cultural workers in Wuhan on the afternoon of the 20th.

At the celebration, the young Red Guards and revolutionary cultural workers sang and danced with their Albanian comrades, enthusiastically praising our most beloved great leader Chairman Mao, and praising the beloved leader of the Albanian people and close friend of the Chinese people, Comrade Enver Hoxha.

Vietnamese Writers' Delegation Arrives in Beijing

A Vietnamese writers' delegation, led by Huu Thanh, arrived in Beijing by train on the 20th, according to the execution plan of the Sino-Vietnamese cultural cooperation agreement. Vice Chairman of the Chinese Federation of Literary and Art Circles Xu Guangping hosted a banquet for the delegation on the evening of the 21st.

Chinese Delegation Returns from Zanzibar

Chinese representatives from trade unions, women's organizations, and youth groups returned to China by plane on the 19th after participating in the celebrations for the third anniversary of the Zanzibar Revolution.

### 友好往来

1967-01-22
第6版()
专栏：友好往来

　　友好往来
　　科威特首相接见我政府代表团
    科威特首相贾比尔·艾哈迈德·贾比尔·萨巴赫十九日接见了由曹痴率领的中国政府代表团。这个代表团是来科威特主持中华人民共和国经济贸易展览会的开幕式的。
    科威特首相在讲话中对中国展览会表示欢迎。他说：我们两国在反对帝国主义、反对殖民主义的共同斗争中，目标是相同的。我们应当团结起来对付共同的敌人。
    阿尔巴尼亚战友在武汉同红卫兵联欢
    阿尔巴尼亚国家歌舞团二十日下午在武汉同数百名红卫兵和革命文艺工作者联欢。
    在联欢会上，红卫兵小将和革命文艺工作者们同阿尔巴尼亚的战友们欢歌畅舞，热情歌颂我们最最敬爱的伟大领袖毛主席，歌颂阿尔巴尼亚人民敬爱的领袖、中国人民的亲密朋友恩维尔·霍查同志。
    越南作家代表团到北京
    根据中越文化合作协定执行计划来中国访问的越南作家代表团，由团长怀青率领，二十日乘火车到达北京。中国文联副主席许广平二十一日晚设宴招待代表团。
    我国代表离桑给巴尔回国
    中国工会、妇女和青年代表在参加了桑给巴尔革命三周年的庆祝活动以后，于十九日乘飞机回国。
                                    （据新华社讯）
