Title: Red Guards and Albanian Artists Hold Joint Celebration
Author:
Date: 1967-01-18
Edition: 5

On January 15th, the Red Guards of the capital held a joint celebration with comrades from the Albanian National Song and Dance Troupe, who are on the front lines of the anti-imperialist and anti-revisionist struggle. At the end of the celebration, they sang "The East is Red" together in unison.
