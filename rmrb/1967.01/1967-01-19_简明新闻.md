	Brief News

	Patriotic Military and Civilians of Laos Shoot Down Two Invading U.S. Planes

	According to a broadcast by the Pathet Lao radio station on the 16th: On the 7th, the U.S. imperialists dispatched bandit planes to bomb and strafe the villages around the city of Phonsavan in Laos. The alert anti-aircraft defense forces of Phonsavan Province immediately opened fire, giving the invading enemy planes a fierce blow, shooting down two U.S. F-105 type aircraft.

	The People of Jordan Persist in Struggle Against U.S.-Israeli Aggression

	On the 13th, nearly 40,000 students and workers held a demonstration in Nablus, the third-largest city in Jordan, shouting slogans against imperialism and the reactionary authorities, protesting against the aggression of U.S. imperialism and Israel.

	The Jordanian security forces used tear gas to suppress the demonstrators and arrested many people.

	It is reported that leaflets calling on people to rise up and fight for the liberation of Palestine were distributed in the cities of Amman, Nablus, Hebron, and Irbid in Jordan.

	Australian Crowd Protests Visit by South Vietnamese Puppet Leader

	On the 18th, several hundred Australian citizens held a demonstration in the capital Canberra, angrily protesting the visit of the U.S. imperialists' running dog, the puppet "prime minister" of South Vietnam, Nguyen Cao Ky, who arrived in Australia for a five-day "visit." The demonstrators gathered along the road from the airport to the hotel in the city, shouting "Nguyen Cao Ky, go back!"

	On the evening of the 17th, important streets and working-class areas in Sydney, another important city in Australia, also saw a large number of leaflets and slogans strongly protesting Nguyen Cao Ky's "visit" to Australia.

	Indian Air Force Brutally Suppresses Minority Struggle

	The reactionary government of India brazenly dispatched the air force to savage bomb the brave armed struggle of the Mizos.

	According to a recent report by the _Pakistan Times_, the Indian army, which is suppressing the Mizos, suffered fierce resistance from the armed forces of the Mizos near Lunglei in the state of Assam. The reactionary government of India actually sent helicopters to destroy the villages there, "with hundreds of Mizos killed in the fighting and bombing."

### 简明新闻

1967-01-19
第6版()
专栏：简明新闻

    简明新闻
    老挝爱国军民击落入侵美机两架
    据巴特寮电台十六日广播：美帝国主义七日出动强盗飞机轰炸、扫射老挝丰沙里市周围的乡村。保持高度警惕的丰沙里省防空部队，立即开炮给来犯敌机以迎头痛击，击落美国F－105型飞机两架。
    约旦人民坚持斗争反对美以侵略
    十三日，近四万名学生和工人在约旦第三大城市纳布卢斯举行示威，高呼反对帝国主义和反动当局的口号，抗议美帝国主义和以色列的侵略。
    约旦保安部队用催泪弹镇压示威群众并逮捕了许多人。
    消息说，在约旦首都安曼、纳布卢斯、哈利勒和伊尔比德等城市散发的传单号召人们起来斗争，争取巴勒斯坦的解放。
    澳群众抗议南越傀儡头子“访问”
    数百名澳大利亚群众十八日在首都坎培拉举行示威，愤怒抗议美帝国主义的走狗、南越傀儡“总理”阮高其到达澳大利亚进行为期五天的“访问”。示威群众聚集在从机场到市内宾馆的路旁，高呼“阮高其滚回去！”
    十七日晚上，澳大利亚另一个重要城市悉尼市的重要街道和工人区也出现了大批强烈抗议阮高其“访问”澳大利亚的传单和标语。
    印度空军残酷镇压少数民族斗争
    印度反动政府悍然出动空军，疯狂轰炸正在英勇进行武装斗争的米佐部族。
    据《巴基斯坦时报》最近报道，镇压米佐部族的印度陆军在阿萨姆邦隆莱附近遭到米佐部族武装力量的英勇抵抗，印度反动政府竟然派遣直升飞机去摧毁那里的村庄，“有数以百计的米佐人在战斗和轰炸中被杀害”。
                          （据新华社讯）
