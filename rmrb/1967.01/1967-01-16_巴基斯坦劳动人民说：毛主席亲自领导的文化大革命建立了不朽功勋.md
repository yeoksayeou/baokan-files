Title: Pakistani Workers Say: Chairman Mao's Personally Led Cultural Revolution Has Achieved Immortal Merits
Author:
Date: 1967-01-16
Edition: 4

A member of a county agricultural association in West Pakistan said: The Great Proletarian Cultural Revolution, personally initiated and led by Chairman Mao Zedong and currently being carried out vigorously in China, has achieved immortal merits. It will uproot the source of modern revisionism in China. He pointed out that U.S. imperialism and Soviet revisionism are trembling with fear at China's cultural revolution and are making all sorts of slanders against it. However, this in no way diminishes the brilliant radiance emanating from this great revolution.

A worker from Rawalpindi told a reporter from Xinhua News Agency: "Because U.S. imperialism and its lackeys are fiercely slandering China's Cultural Revolution, we Pakistani workers must support it. Whatever U.S. imperialism and its lackeys hate must be good and should be welcomed; whatever U.S. imperialism and its lackeys welcome must be bad and should be opposed." He enthusiastically shouted: "Long live the Chinese Cultural Revolution!"

### 巴基斯坦劳动人民说：毛主席亲自领导的文化大革命建立了不朽功勋

1967-01-16
第4版()
专栏：

    巴基斯坦劳动人民说：
    毛主席亲自领导的文化大革命建立了不朽功勋
    西巴基斯坦某个县农会的一个会员说：毛泽东主席亲自发动和领导的、现在正在中国轰轰烈烈开展的无产阶级文化大革命，建立了不朽的功勋，它将挖掉在中国产生现代修正主义的根子。他指出，美帝国主义和苏联修正主义对中国的文化革命怕得发抖，对它进行种种诬蔑，但是这丝毫也不能减弱这场伟大革命放射出来的灿烂光芒。
    拉瓦尔品第的一个工人对新华社记者说：“由于美帝国主义及其仆从拚命地诽谤中国文化大革命，我们巴基斯坦工人就必须支持它。凡是美帝国主义及其仆从憎恨的事一定是好事，应当受到欢迎；凡是美帝国主义及其仆从所欢迎的事一定是坏事，应该反对。”他热情地高呼：“中国文化大革命万岁！”
