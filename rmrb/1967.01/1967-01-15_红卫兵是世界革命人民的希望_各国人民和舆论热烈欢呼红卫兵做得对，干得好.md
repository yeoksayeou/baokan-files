Title: Red Guards Are the Hope of the World's Revolutionary People
Author:
Date: 1967-01-15
Edition: 6

The Chinese Red Guards are truly the revolutionary youth of the great era of Chairman Mao. They follow Chairman Mao's words and act according to his instructions, bravely making revolution. They are right! They are doing well! Let the imperialists, revisionists, and all reactionaries tremble before the Red Guard movement! Their curses and hatred only show that the revolutionary spirit of the Red Guard youth is excellent!

	Japan	Shan Yuan

The Red Guards, under the guidance of Chairman Mao Zedong, have set an unprecedented and exemplary model. They hold high the great red banner of Mao Zedong Thought and dedicate themselves wholeheartedly to the construction of socialism.

	Sri Lanka	Li Yanage

Imperialism has always placed its hopes for restoration on the younger generation of China. Now, the younger generation of China is becoming the steadfast successors of the proletarian revolutionary cause. The hopes of imperialism and all ghosts and monsters have been dashed.

	Singapore	People's Forum

The Red Guards are the hope of the world's revolutionary people. Therefore, imperialism, revisionism, and reactionaries are all very afraid of them. Revolutionary people all love the Red Guards because they are correct and revolutionary.

	Nepal	A Friend

The Chinese Red Guards are a new generation of people with proletarian ideology and socialism. They stand in sharp contrast to the Soviet youth who pursue decadent and licentious lives. They are the models for Arab youth to learn from.

	Syria	An Arab Friend

When the laboring people of the whole world hear the news that the Chinese Red Guard youth joyfully met the great leader Chairman Mao at Tiananmen Square, they feel exhilarated. The Chinese Red Guards have become the vanguard of the world's youth.

	Sudan	Mohammed

The Red Guards we see are the most lovable people, the vanguard of the revolution.

	Algeria	A Friend

The Chinese Red Guards are an unprecedented revolutionary shock brigade in the history of progress. All revolutionary people of the world salute the young and brave Red Guards of the Great Proletarian Cultural Revolution.

	South West Africa People's Organization	Kan Ju

The Chinese Red Guards are advancing; they are the vanguard of the revolution against imperialism.

	Azania	Leballo

The Red Guards are a constructive force; they work for the welfare of the people. As a people who love peace, we must highly respect the Red Guards, support, and praise their work.

	Somalia	Mu Mu Chu Mohammed

The power of the Red Guards is the great power of the mobilized masses. They represent the great power of the masses who have been mobilized. I greatly admire the unyielding spirit of the Red Guard youth in the face of the enemy.

	Mexico	Vila

Hail the glorious appearance of the Red Guards. The brave revolutionary actions of the Chinese Red Guards have shaken the entire world, making all reactionaries on Earth tremble. The young warriors of the Red Guards, nurtured by Mao Zedong Thought, have made the Great Proletarian Cultural Revolution not only in China but also in the world a firm guarantee of the sacred revolutionary cause.

	Colombia	Klaweihao

The revolutionary youth of China have never been as spirited as they are today. They have both revolutionary enthusiasm and Mao Zedong Thought as their guide. They have the revolution of China and the entire world in their hearts.

	United States	Epstein

The actions of the Chinese Red Guards are the succession of the revolution. The Red Guards are cultivating new people and paving the way for China's great leap forward. Their experiences, difficulties, and victories will contribute to the international communist movement of Marxism-Leninism.

	France	New Humanity

The Red Guards are the defenders and successors of the revolution. Mao Zedong Thought is a powerful revolutionary driving force. Once it is grasped by the people, it will unleash tremendous power.

	Italy	New Unity

The Chinese Red Guards are the shock troops of the cultural revolution. It is they who prevent the restoration of capitalism in China and ensure that China continues to advance on the path of socialism and communism.

	Greece	Regeneration

The Red Guards are the finest standard-bearers of the Great Proletarian Cultural Revolution in China. The revolutionary actions of the Red Guards make imperialism and modern revisionism tremble with fear.

	Spain	Worker's Vanguard

### 红卫兵是世界革命人民的希望  各国人民和舆论热烈欢呼红卫兵做得对，干得好

1967-01-15
第6版()
专栏：

    红卫兵是世界革命人民的希望 
    各国人民和舆论热烈欢呼红卫兵做得对，干得好
    中国红卫兵不愧是伟大的毛泽东时代的中国革命青年。他们听毛主席的话，照毛主席的指示办事，勇敢地闹革命。他们做得对！干得好！让美帝国主义、修正主义和一切反动派在红卫兵运动面前发抖吧！它们的谩骂、痛恨只能说明红卫兵小将们的革命造反精神好得很！
                                                日本　尚原
    红卫兵在毛泽东主席指引下，树立了史无前例的光辉榜样，值得学习的榜样。他们高举毛泽东思想伟大红旗，全心全意为建设社会主义服务。
                                              锡兰　李亚纳格
    帝国主义一直把复辟的希望寄托在中国年轻一代的身上。现在，中国年轻一代正在成为无产阶级革命事业的坚强接班人。帝国主义和一切牛鬼蛇神的希望统统落空了。
                        新加坡《人民论坛》
    红卫兵是世界革命人民的希望。所以，帝国主义、修正主义和反动派都非常害怕他们。革命人民都非常爱红卫兵，因为他们做得很正确，很革命。
                          尼泊尔　一位朋友
    中国红卫兵是具有无产阶级思想的社会主义一代新人。他们同那些追求糜烂、放荡生活的苏联青年恰成鲜明的对比。他们是阿拉伯青年学习的榜样。
                      叙利亚　一位阿拉伯朋友
    全世界的劳动人民，一听到中国红卫兵青年欢天喜地在天安门广场见到伟大的领袖毛主席的消息时，他们就感到欢欣鼓舞。中国的红卫兵已成为世界青年的先锋。
                              苏丹　穆罕默德
    我们看到的红卫兵是最可爱的人，是革命的先锋。
                      阿尔及利亚　一位朋友

                  西南非洲民族联盟　坎　朱
