Title: People of Shashiya, Hebei Province, Study Chairman Mao's Quotations and Work to Transform the Mountains
Author:
Date: 1967-01-06
Edition: 3

The people of Shashiya, Zunhua County, Hebei Province, study Chairman Mao's quotations every day and strive to be "modern-day Yu Gong," working to transform the mountains for the revolution by clearing paths and creating fields.
