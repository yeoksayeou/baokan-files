Title: Comrade Baluku Praises Shanghai Revolutionary Rebels for Crushing New Economicism Counterattack
Author:
Date: 1967-01-24
Edition: 5

By Xinhua News Agency, Wuhan, January 23rd

The Albanian military delegation led by Albanian Labor Party Central Committee Political Bureau member, Deputy Chairman of the Council of Ministers and Minister of Defense Bekir Balluku, and Albanian Labor Party Central Committee member, Chief of the Political Department of the Albanian People's Army Hito Chako, accompanied by Central Committee member of the Chinese Communist Party and Deputy Minister of Defense Wang Shousheng, departed Wuhan by special plane this morning to visit Guangzhou.

On the afternoon of the 22nd, the entire delegation visited a certain uniform factory in Wuhan and was warmly welcomed by several thousand revolutionary workers. When receiving representatives from this factory, Comrade Balluku strongly criticized revisionist economicism. He emphasized that we must all follow Chairman Mao's revolutionary line. He praised the revolutionary rebels of Shanghai, saying they did well in repelling and crushing the new counterattack of revisionist economicism. He pointed out that the Chinese working class, laboring peasants, soldiers, and youth always follow the Chinese Communist Party and Chairman Mao. No force can shake their determination to march forward victoriously under the leadership of the Party and Chairman Mao. Comrade Balluku concluded by shouting in Chinese, "Long live Chairman Mao! Ten thousand years! Ten thousand, ten thousand years!"

By Xinhua News Agency, Guangzhou, January 23rd

The Albanian military delegation led by Albanian Labor Party Central Committee Political Bureau member, Deputy Chairman of the Council of Ministers and Minister of Defense Bekir Balluku, and Albanian Labor Party Central Committee member, Chief of the Political Department of the Albanian People's Army Hito Chako, accompanied by Central Committee member of the Chinese Communist Party and Deputy Minister of Defense Wang Shousheng, arrived in Guangzhou by special plane today. Responsible comrades of the Guangzhou Military Region of the People's Liberation Army, as well as several thousand officers and soldiers of the army, navy, and air force stationed in Guangzhou, militia members, and Red Guards braved the drizzle to warmly welcome their heroic Albanian comrades from the front lines of anti-imperialism and anti-revisionism.

In the afternoon, the Albanian comrades, accompanied by responsible comrades of the Guangzhou Military Region, visited the former site of the Peasant Movement Training Institute, which was established by Chairman Mao. They also watched a cultural performance and military display by the militia.

### 巴卢库同志赞扬上海革命造反派粉碎经济主义的新反扑　沿着毛主席革命路线前进　阿尔巴尼亚军事代表团离武汉到广州访问

1967-01-24
第5版()
专栏：

　　巴卢库同志赞扬上海革命造反派粉碎经济主义的新反扑
　　沿着毛主席革命路线前进
　　阿尔巴尼亚军事代表团离武汉到广州访问
    据新华社武汉二十三日电　由阿尔巴尼亚劳动党中央政治局委员、部长会议副主席兼国防部长贝基尔·巴卢库和阿尔巴尼亚劳动党中央委员、阿尔巴尼亚人民军政治部主任希托·恰科率领的阿尔巴尼亚军事代表团，在中共中央委员、国防部副部长王树声陪同下，今天上午由武汉乘专机前往广州参观访问。
    二十二日下午，代表团全体同志参观了武汉某被服厂，受到全厂数千名革命职工热烈欢迎。巴卢库同志在接见这个工厂的革命职工代表时，强烈地批判了修正主义的经济主义。他强调说，我们大家都要跟着毛主席革命路线前进。他赞扬上海革命造反派的工人做得好，他们反击并粉碎了修正主义经济主义的新反扑。他指出，中国工人阶级、劳动农民、战士、青年始终跟着中国共产党，跟着毛主席，任何力量都丝毫动摇不了他们在党和毛主席领导下胜利前进的决心。巴卢库同志最后用中国话高呼：“毛主席万岁！万岁！万万岁！”
    据新华社广州二十三日电　由阿尔巴尼亚劳动党中央政治局委员、部长会议副主席兼国防部长贝基尔·巴卢库和阿尔巴尼亚劳动党中央委员、阿尔巴尼亚人民军政治部主任希托·恰科率领的阿尔巴尼亚军事代表团，在中共中央委员、国防部副部长王树声陪同下，今日乘专机到达广州。中国人民解放军广州部队负责人以及驻广州陆海空三军指战员和民兵、红卫兵小将数千人，冒着细雨，在机场上热烈欢迎来自反帝反修最前线的英雄的阿尔巴尼亚战友。
    下午，阿尔巴尼亚同志在广州部队负责人陪同下，参观了当年毛主席主办的农民运动讲习所旧址。还观看了民兵们的文艺演出和军事表演。
