Title: Vice Chairman of the Cambodian-Chinese Friendship Association Fu Ning Says: The Chinese Cultural Revolution Provides New Inspiration for National Liberation Movements
Author:
Date: 1967-01-16
Edition: 4

Vice Chairman of the Cambodian-Chinese Friendship Association Fu Ning wrote in the journal *Cambodian-Chinese Friendship Association* that the Chinese Cultural Revolution is a new and highest stage of scientific socialist thought, and is a necessary stage in the development of the world socialist revolution.

The article further points out that imperialism and reactionary forces in various countries hope that China will abandon the socialist revolution, restore capitalism, or turn to revisionism. However, due to the continuous victories of the Chinese Cultural Revolution, a heavy blow has been dealt to U.S. imperialism and its lackeys, providing new inspiration for the national liberation movements in Asia, Africa, and Latin America, especially for the struggle of the Vietnamese people.

The article also states that Mao Zedong Thought is the highest thought of contemporary Marxism-Leninism. The people of China, who make up a quarter of the world's population, united under the leadership of the Communist Party of China and its wise leader Chairman Mao, form a great revolutionary force of unity. No force can destroy it. This force awakens the people of the world to the path of socialist revolution.

The article concludes by congratulating the great victories of the Chinese Cultural Revolution and wishing that the brilliance of Mao Zedong Thought will forever shine brightly.

### 柬中友协副主席符宁说：中国文化大革命给民族解放运动新的鼓舞力量

1967-01-16
第4版()
专栏：

    柬中友协副主席符宁说：
    中国文化大革命给民族解放运动新的鼓舞力量
    柬中友好协会副主席符宁在《柬中友协》杂志上发表文章说，中国无产阶级文化大革命是科学的社会主义思想的一个新的最高阶段，是世界社会主义革命发展的另一个必要的阶段。
    文章接着指出，帝国主义与各国反动派希望中国放弃社会主义革命，复辟资本主义或变成修正主义。但是，由于中国文化大革命不断获得胜利，狠狠地痛击了美帝国主义及其走狗，给亚洲、非洲、拉丁美洲民族解放运动，特别是越南人民的斗争增加了新的鼓舞的力量。
    文章还说，毛泽东思想是当代马克思列宁主义的最高思想。占世界人口四分之一的中国人民团结在中国共产党及其英明的领袖毛主席的周围，是一种伟大的革命的统一力量，任何力量都不能消灭它。这种力量，唤醒世界人民走向社会主义革命发展的道路。
    文章最后还祝贺中国无产阶级文化大革命获得巨大的胜利，祝贺毛泽东思想光芒永远红光四射。
