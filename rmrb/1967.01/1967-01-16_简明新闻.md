    Brief News

    Vietnamese People and Soldiers Shoot Down Two More U.S. Planes

    The people and soldiers of Nghe An Province in North Vietnam shot down one invading U.S. aggressor plane on the 12th.

    According to a supplementary report, the people and soldiers of Ha Tinh Province shot down one U.S. aggressor plane on the 1st.

    Cambodian Government Protests Thai Invaders' Landmines

    The Cambodian Ministry of Foreign Affairs sent a note on January 10 to the Thanom-Kittikachorn clique in Thailand, strongly protesting the laying of landmines by Thai invaders on Cambodian territory, which has caused serious crimes resulting in the deaths of Cambodian people and material losses.

    The note firmly demands that the Thanom-Kittikachorn clique in Thailand immediately stop these crimes.

    U.S. and Britain Prepare to Reward Indonesian Rightists with Large Sums of Money

    The U.S. government recently agreed to provide $80 million in "aid" to its Indonesian right-wing military regime this year.

    According to the Indonesian newspaper "Hope Light," the British Ministry of Overseas Development is also preparing to provide 360,000 pounds of "aid" to the Indonesian right-wing military regime.

    The funds for this "aid" are restricted to the purchase of British goods and machinery.

    Over 5,000 Philadelphia Transit Workers Strike

    More than 5,000 drivers and maintenance workers of the "Philadelphia Transportation Company" began a strike at midnight on the 15th, causing all public buses, trolleybuses, subways, and elevated railways in the fourth-largest U.S. city of Philadelphia to come to a standstill.

### 简明新闻

1967-01-16
第5版()
专栏：简明新闻

    简明新闻
    越南北方军民又击落美机两架
    越南北方义安省军民，十二日击落入侵的美国强盗飞机一架。
    另据补充报道，广平省军民在一日曾击落美国强盗飞机一架。
    柬政府抗议泰国入侵者埋地雷
    柬埔寨外交部一月十日给泰国他侬—巴博集团一个照会，强烈抗议泰国入侵者在柬埔寨领土上埋设地雷，造成柬埔寨人民的死亡和物质损失的严重罪行。
    照会坚决要求泰国他侬—巴博集团立即制止这种罪行。
    美英准备犒赏印尼右派大笔钱
    美国政府最近同意今年提供给它的走狗印尼右派军人政权八千万美元的“援助”。
    据印尼《希望之光报》报道，英国海外发展部也准备向印尼右派军人政权提供三十六万英镑的“援助”。
    这项“援助”的款项限用于购买英国货物和机器。
    美国费城五千多运输工人罢工
    美国“费城运输公司”的五千多名乘务和维修工人从十五日凌晨开始举行罢工，使美国第四大城市费城市内的所有公共汽车、无轨电车、地下铁道和高架铁道的交通全部陷于瘫痪。　（据新华社）
