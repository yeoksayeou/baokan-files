Title: Vice Premier Chen Yi Meets with Vietnamese Guests
Author:
Date: 1967-01-15
Edition: 5

Vice Premier Chen Yi today met with Nguyen Thanh Chinh, Deputy Chief Editor of the Vietnam News Agency, who is currently visiting Beijing. They had a friendly and cordial conversation.

Present at the meeting were Qin Jialin, Director of the News Department of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs, and Wang Weizhen, Deputy Director of Xinhua News Agency.

### 陈毅副总理接见越南客人

1967-01-15
第5版()
专栏：

    陈毅副总理接见越南客人
    新华社十四日讯　国务院副总理陈毅今天接见了正在北京访问的越南通讯社副总编辑陈青春，同他进行了亲切友好的谈话。
    接见时在座的，有外交部新闻司司长秦加林、新华社副社长王唯真。
