Title: Marshall Flies to Nanjing Again
Author: 
Date: 1946-08-03
Page: 1

　　Marshall Flies to Nanjing Again
    Xinhua News Agency, Yan'an, 31st - Central News Agency, Nanjing reported: Marshall flew from Jiujiang to Nanjing at 7:00 PM on the 30th.
