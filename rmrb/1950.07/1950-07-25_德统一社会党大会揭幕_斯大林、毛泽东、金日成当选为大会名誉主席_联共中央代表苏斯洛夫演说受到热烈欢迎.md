Title: SED Congress Opens; Stalin, Mao Zedong, Kim Il-sung Elected Honorary Chairmen of the Congress; CPSU Central Committee Representative Suslov's Speech Receives Warm Welcome
Author:
Date: 1950-07-25
Page: 4
　　Xinhua News Agency, 24th: Berlin news: The Third Congress of the German Socialist Unity Party (SED) opened in Berlin on the 20th. Many communist parties from countries in Europe and Asia have sent representatives to participate in this grand event, including Suslov, Secretary of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of the Soviet Union; Wang Jiaxiang, member of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of China; Lehmann, Chairman of the German Communist Party; Togliatti, Secretary-General of the Italian Communist Party; Pollitt, Secretary-General of the British Communist Party; and Duclos, Secretary of the Central Committee of the French Communist Party.

　　The congress elected Stalin, Mao Zedong, and Kim Il-sung as honorary chairmen.

　　SED Chairman Pieck delivered a report on the work of the party at the congress on the 20th. The congress then discussed Pieck's report. During the discussion, the representatives' speeches focused on the issue of strengthening the party ideologically and organizationally, making it a new type of party capable of successfully implementing all-German policies.

　　Suslov, head of the CPSU Central Committee delegation, delivered a speech at the congress on the 21st. He pointed out that the growing friendship between the German and Soviet peoples in the noble struggle for peace is a factor of great international importance. The Soviet people pay great attention to and sympathize with the German people's struggle to build a unified, democratic, and peace-loving Germany, and sincerely congratulate them on every achievement in this regard. The appearance of the CPSU Central Committee delegation and Suslov's speech were warmly welcomed by all the representatives.

　　Speaking at the meeting on the 21st were Lehmann, Chairman of the German Communist Party; Cyrankiewicz, Chief Representative of the Polish United Workers' Party; and Wang Jiaxiang, Representative of the CPC Central Committee.

　　SED Chairman Grotewohl delivered a lengthy report at the meeting on the evening of the 21st, entitled "Struggle for a National Front for a Peaceful and Democratic Germany."

　　The congress is continuing.

### 德统一社会党大会揭幕  斯大林、毛泽东、金日成当选为大会名誉主席  联共中央代表苏斯洛夫演说受到热烈欢迎

1950-07-25
第4版()
专栏：

　　德统一社会党大会揭幕
    斯大林、毛泽东、金日成当选为大会名誉主席
    联共中央代表苏斯洛夫演说受到热烈欢迎
    【新华社二十四日讯】柏林消息：德国统一社会党第三次代表大会，已于二十日在柏林开幕。欧亚两洲许多国家的共产党都派有代表参加这一盛会，其中有苏联共产党中央委员会书记苏斯洛夫，中国共产党中央委员王稼祥，德国共产党主席雷曼，意大利共产党总书记托里亚蒂，英国共产党总书记波立特，法国共产党中央委员会书记杜克洛等。
    大会选出了斯大林、毛泽东、金日成等为名誉主席。
    统一社会党主席皮克于二十日在大会上作了关于党的工作的报告。大会随即讨论了皮克的报告。在讨论中，代表们的发言集中在从思想上和组织上加强党，使它成为一个能够胜利执行全德政策的新型的党的问题。
    联共中央代表团团长苏斯洛夫于二十一日在大会发表演说。他指出：德国人民和苏联人民在争取和平的崇高斗争中日益增长的友谊，是具有极大国际重要性的因素。苏联人民以巨大的注意和同情关心德国人民为建立统一、民主和爱好和平的德国而进行的斗争，并热诚祝贺他们在这一方面的每一成就。联共中央代表团的出现及苏斯洛夫的演说，受到了全体代表的热烈欢迎。
    在二十一日会议上发言的，有德国共产党主席雷曼、波兰统一工人党首席代表西伦凯维兹和中共中央代表王稼祥。
    统一社会党主席格罗提渥于二十一日晚在会议上作了长篇报告，题为《为和平及民主德国的全国阵线而斗争》。
    大会正在继续进行中。
