Title: Jilin Federation of Trade Unions and Other People's Organizations Prepare to Launch Anti-American Aggression Movement
Author:
Date: 1950-07-18
Page: 1

　　[News from this newspaper] According to the Jilin Daily, representatives from eight people's organizations, including the Jilin Provincial Federation of Trade Unions, the Committee for the Signature Campaign to Defend World Peace, the Democratic Youth Federation, the Youth League Working Committee, the Democratic Women's Federation, the Student Federation, the Literary Federation, and the Sino-Soviet Friendship Association, gathered on the 13th to discuss how to launch a movement against American aggression in Taiwan and Korea throughout the province. The representatives unanimously expressed that they would use all possible means to carry out propaganda and take active actions to oppose American aggression in Taiwan and Korea. Su Sheng, a representative from the Provincial Federation of Trade Unions, said: Workers throughout the province are willing to enthusiastically launch production competitions to support the liberation of Taiwan and support the Korean people's war of liberation. The attending representatives decided to launch Anti-American Aggression in Taiwan and Korea Movement Week starting on the 17th.

### 吉林总工会等人民团体筹备展开反美侵略运动

1950-07-18
第1版()
专栏：

　　吉林总工会等人民团体筹备展开反美侵略运动
    【本报讯】据吉林日报载称：吉林省总工会、保卫世界和平签名运动委员会、民主青联、青年团工作委员会、民主妇联、学联、文联及中苏友协八个人民团体的代表，于十三日集会讨论如何在全省展开反对美国侵略台湾朝鲜运动，代表们一致表示将利用一切可能条件进行宣传，并以积极的行动来反对美国侵略台湾和朝鲜。省工会代表苏生说：全省工人愿以热烈展开生产竞赛，来支援解放台湾和声援朝鲜人民解放战争。与会代表决定十七日起展开反对美国侵略台湾朝鲜运动周。
