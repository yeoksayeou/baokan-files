Title: US Executes Innocent Black Worker; Our People's Organizations Issue Protest Telegram
Author:
Date: 1950-07-25
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency, July 22nd: After receiving an appeal from the Civil Rights Congress of America, the All-China Federation of Trade Unions, the China Peace Committee and eight other people's organizations sent a telegram today to US President Truman and the Governor of Mississippi, protesting the scheduled execution of innocent black worker Willie Ocgee. According to the appeal recently received by the China Peace Committee from the Civil Rights Congress of America, it stated: Innocent black worker Willie Ocgee is scheduled to be executed in Jackson, Mississippi, on July 27. Please support the innocent. The full text of the protest telegram from our All-China Federation of Trade Unions and other people's organizations is as follows:
President Truman, Governor of Mississippi,

Innocent black worker Willie Ocgee will be executed in Jackson, Mississippi, on July 27. The Chinese people hereby lodge a serious protest with you, hoping that you will immediately stop the execution.

　　　　　　　　　　　　All-China Federation of Trade Unions
　　　　　　　　　　　　China Peace Committee
　　　　　　　　　　　　All-China Federation of Democratic Youth
　　　　　　　　　　　　All-China Democratic Women's Federation
　　　　　　　　　　　　Central Committee of the China New Democratic Youth League
　　　　　　　　　　　　All-China Students' Federation
　　　　　　　　　　　　All-China Federation of Literary and Art Circles
　　　　　　　　　　　　Beijing YMCA

### 美国处死无辜黑人工人  我人民团体发出抗议电

1950-07-25
第4版()
专栏：

　　美国处死无辜黑人工人
    我人民团体发出抗议电
    【新华社二十二日讯】我中华全国总工会、中国保卫世界和平大会委员会等八个人民团体于接获美国民权保障大会的呼吁后，已于今日致电美国总统杜鲁门及美国密西西比州州长，抗议无辜黑人工人威利·奥基被定期处死的事件。按中国保卫世界和平大会委员会最近接获美国民权保障大会的呼吁，内称：无辜黑人工人威利·奥基定于七月二十七日在密西西比州杰克逊城被处死，请中国人民对无辜者给予声援。我全国总工会等人民团体的抗议电全文如下：杜鲁门总统密西西比州长
    无辜黑人工人威利·奥基（Willie Ocgee）将于七月二十七日在密西西比州杰克逊城被处死。中国人民特向你们严重抗议，望即停止执行。
                              中华全国总工会
                            中国保卫世界和平大会委员会
                            中华全国民主青年联合会
                            中华全国民主妇女联合会
                            中国新民主主义青年团中央委员会
                            中华全国学生联合会
                            中华全国文学艺术界联合会
                            北京市青年会
