Title: Beijing Postal Administration Extends Business Hours
Author: Chen Xin
Date: 1950-07-27
Page: 7

Beijing Postal Administration
    Extends Business Hours

    To facilitate remittances for citizens, the Beijing Postal Administration has extended the business hours of its remittance department from 9:00 to 17:00 to 19:00, effective from the 27th of this month. There will be no break at noon. On Sundays and holidays, remittance services will be provided as usual from 10:00 to 12:00. Furthermore, the Dongdan Post Office, to better serve the public, has automatically extended the hours for remittances and parcel services to 20:00, effective from July 24th.
                                         (Chen Xin)

### 北京邮政管理局  延长营业时间
陈新
1950-07-27
第7版()
专栏：

　　北京邮政管理局
    延长营业时间
    北京邮政管理局为了便利市民汇兑款项，自本月二十七日起将该局汇兑部门营业时间，由九点至十七点延长到十九点止。中午也不休息。星期例假日上午十点到十二点，照常办理汇兑业务。又东单邮局为了便利群众自七月二十四日起将办理汇兑和包裹时间自动延长到二十点止。
                                         （陈新）
