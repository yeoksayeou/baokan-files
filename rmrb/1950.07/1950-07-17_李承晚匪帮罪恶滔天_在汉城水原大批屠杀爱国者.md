Title: Syngman Rhee Gang's Heinous Crimes: Massacring Patriots in Seoul and Suwon
Author:
Date: 1950-07-17
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Pyongyang, 16th - According to a report from the Korean Central News Agency: Before fleeing from Seoul and Suwon, the Syngman Rhee gang massacred a large number of imprisoned patriots and innocent residents. According to residents on the outskirts of Seoul who witnessed this atrocity: On the evening of the 27th, on the eve of the Korean People's Army's liberation of Seoul, the Syngman Rhee gang's puppet army military police escorted a large number of patriotic people imprisoned in the "Army Prison" on Itaewon Street, Seoul, to a mountain in Xibingku area on the outskirts of Seoul, and carried out a mass shooting. A group of patriots were taken in small boats to the center of the Han River and drowned in pairs. On that day, more than two hundred patriotic people were murdered.

Before the liberation of Suwon, the bandits slaughtered even more patriots and innocent residents. According to preliminary statistics, more than 1,000 people were machine-gunned to death.

### 李承晚匪帮罪恶滔天  在汉城水原大批屠杀爱国者

1950-07-17
第4版()
专栏：

　　李承晚匪帮罪恶滔天
    在汉城水原大批屠杀爱国者
    【新华社平壤十六日电】据朝鲜中央通讯社报道：李承晚匪帮在从汉城与水原逃走前，曾大批屠杀被监禁的爱国者与无辜居民。据目睹这一暴行的汉城市郊居民说：二十七日晚，在朝鲜人民军解放汉城的前夕，李承晚匪帮的伪军宪兵把监禁在汉城梨泰院街“陆军刑务所”的大批爱国志士，押解到汉城市郊的西冰库地方的一个山上，进行集体枪杀。一批爱国者被用小船载到汉江江心，两个两个地推到江里溺死。那天，被残杀的爱国志士在二百人以上。
    水原解放前，匪徒们所屠杀的爱国者与无辜居民尤多。据初步统计，被机枪扫射而死的有一千余人。
