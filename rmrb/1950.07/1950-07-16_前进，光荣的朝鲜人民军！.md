Title: Forward, Glorious Korean People's Army!
Author: Ai Qing
Date: 1950-07-16
Page: 5

　　Forward, Glorious Korean People's Army!
    Ai Qing
    Forward, glorious Korean People's Army—
    Break through the American army's positions!
    Destroy the enemy's fortresses!
    Surround the invaders!
    Wipe out the traitors!
    Forward, glorious Korean People's Army—
    Liberate the motherland's land!
    Liberate the suffering people!
    Fight for independence and freedom!
    Plant the glorious flag throughout the territory!
                                          July 14, 1950

### 前进，光荣的朝鲜人民军！
艾青
1950-07-16
第5版()
专栏：

　　前进，光荣的朝鲜人民军！
    艾青
    前进，光荣的朝鲜人民军——
    冲破美军的阵地！
    摧毁敌人的堡垒！
    把侵略者包围起来！
    把卖国贼消灭干净！
    前进，光荣的朝鲜人民军——
    解放祖国的土地！
    解放苦难的人民！
    为独立自由而战斗！
    把光荣的旗帜插遍全境！
                                          一九五○年七月十四日
