Title: Revolutionary Committee of the Chinese Kuomintang (RCCK) Beijing Municipal Branch Holds Meeting to Oppose U.S. Aggression Against Taiwan and Korea
Author:
Date: 1950-07-25
Page: 3

    [This newspaper dispatch] The Revolutionary Committee of the Chinese Kuomintang (RCCK) Beijing Municipal Branch, in response to the movement to oppose U.S. aggression against Taiwan and Korea, convened a city-wide党员 (Party member) meeting on the 23rd, with over a hundred Party members in attendance. The meeting was presided over by Ning Wu, a member of the Standing Committee. Shao Lizi reported on the recent general international situation and the reactionary attempts of U.S. imperialism to undermine world peace by using the illegal resolutions of the United Nations. Chen Qiyuan attended and reported on the reasons for U.S. imperialism's launching of a war of aggression and its doomed future of inevitable failure. Ning Wu called on all Party members to strengthen the propaganda against U.S. aggression.

### 民革京市分部举行大会反对美国侵略台湾朝鲜

1950-07-25
第3版()
专栏：

　　民革京市分部举行大会反对美国侵略台湾朝鲜
    【本报讯】民革北京市分部，为响应反对美国侵略台湾朝鲜运动，于二十三日召开全市党员大会，到党员百余人。大会由常委宁武主持。邵力子报告了最近国际一般情势及美帝利用联合国机构非法决议破坏世界和平的反动企图。陈其瑗出席报告了美帝发动侵略战争的原因及注定其必然失败的前途。宁武号召全体党员加强反对美国侵略的宣传。
