Title: The 42nd Meeting of the Government Administration Council: Approved Instructions on Suppressing Counter-Revolutionary Activities, Instructions on Relieving Unemployed Teachers and Handling the Problem of Students Dropping Out of School, and the Decision to Convene a Meeting of Model Representatives
Author:
Date: 1950-07-22
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, July 21st—The 42nd meeting of the Central People's Government Administration Council was held at 3:00 PM on the 21st. The meeting discussed and approved the instructions on suppressing counter-revolutionary activities, the instructions on relieving unemployed teachers and handling the problem of students dropping out of school, and the decision to convene a National Congress of Combat Heroes and a National Congress of Worker-Peasant-Soldier Labor Models. After listening to the report by Minister of Education of the Central People's Government, Ma Xulun, on the First National Higher Education Conference, the meeting decided to organize a special group to review the decisions, regulations, and methods adopted by the conference. In addition, the meeting approved several appointments and submitted them to the Central People's Government Committee for approval.

### 政务院四十二次会议  通过镇压反革命活动的指示、救济失业教师处理学生失学问题的指示、召开英模代表会议的决定

1950-07-22
第1版()
专栏：

　　政务院四十二次会议
    通过镇压反革命活动的指示、救济失业教师处理学生失学问题的指示、召开英模代表会议的决定
    【新华社二十一日讯】中央人民政府政务院第四十二次政务会议于二十一日下午三时举行。会议讨论和通过了关于镇压反革命活动的指示、救济失业教师与处理学生失学问题的指示、召开全国战斗英雄代表会议和全国工农兵劳动模范代表会议的决定。会议在听取了中央人民政府教育部马叙伦部长所作的关于第一次全国高等教育会议的报告后，决定组织专门小组审查该会议通过的各项决定、规程和办法。此外，会议通过任命及提请中央人民政府委员会批准任命案多起。
