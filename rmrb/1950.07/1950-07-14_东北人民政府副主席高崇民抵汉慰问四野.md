Title: Northeast People's Government Vice Chairman Gao Chongmin Arrives in Hankou to Console the Fourth Field Army
Author:
Date: 1950-07-14
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Hankou, July 13th: Gao Chongmin, Vice Chairman of the Northeast People's Government, representing the Northeast People's Government and people from all walks of life in the Northeast, made a special trip to Hankou to extend greetings to the People's Liberation Army's Fourth Field Army, which liberated the Northeast. He also brought the Northeast People's Government's letter of condolences to the entire army and commemorative medals for the liberation of the Northeast, to be awarded to commanders and fighters who contributed to the liberation of the Northeast. After arriving in Wuhan on the 4th of this month, Vice Chairman Gao was warmly received and welcomed by the commanders and fighters of the Fourth Field Army. A grand award ceremony was held on the morning of the 9th, with Deng Zihui, Political Commissar of the Fourth Field Army, and Tao Zhu, Director of the Political Department, representing all the commanders and fighters in accepting the awards. Director Tao Zhu stated in his welcoming speech: "We must continue to improve and strengthen our military strength, protect the interests of the people of the whole country and the people of the Northeast, and deal a head-on blow to the imperialists who dare to invade us." Vice Chairman Gao introduced the great achievements in economic construction in the Northeast after liberation in his speech. He said: These achievements are inseparable from the heroic fighting and hard work of all the commanders and fighters of the Fourth Field Army. He also pointed out: The people of the Northeast hope that their sons and soldiers, under the leadership of Chairman Mao and the Central People's Government, will build the army into a strong national defense force to defend the motherland and protect the peaceful construction of the people. Political Commissar Deng Zihui pointed out in his reply speech: The Fourth Field Army grew up under the care of the people of the Northeast. The people of the Northeast deserve great credit for our ability to achieve victory and glory. He called on all commanders and fighters to prevent complacency, make persistent efforts, and complete the glorious task of building the Central South Region.
	In response to the condolences and encouragement from the people of the Northeast, the leaders and all the commanders and fighters of the Fourth Field Army sent a telegram to the Northeast Party, government, army, and people to express their gratitude and presented a huge brocade banner to the Northeast People's Government and the Northeast Bureau of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of China a few days ago.

### 东北人民政府副主席高崇民抵汉慰问四野

1950-07-14
第1版()
专栏：

　　东北人民政府副主席高崇民抵汉慰问四野
    【新华社汉口十三日电】东北人民政府副主席高崇民代表东北人民政府和东北各界人民专程来汉慰问解放东北的人民解放军第四野战军，并携带有东北人民政府给全军的慰问信和解放东北纪念章，赠给解放东北有功的指战员们。高副主席于本月四日抵达武汉后，即受到第四野战军指战员们的热诚招待和欢迎。九日上午举行了隆重的授奖典礼，由第四野战军政治委员邓子恢、政治部主任陶铸等代表全军指战员受奖。陶铸主任在欢迎词里表示：“要继续提高与加强我们的军事力量，保护全国人民和东北人民的利益，给敢于侵犯我们的帝国主义者以迎头痛击。”高副主席在讲话里介绍了东北解放后在经济建设上的伟大成就，他说：这些成就是与第四野战军全体指战员们的英勇善战艰苦奋斗分不开的。他并且指出：东北人民希望他们的子弟兵在毛主席和中央人民政府的领导下，把部队建设成为强大的国防军，保卫祖国，保护人民的和平建设。邓子恢政委在致答词中指出：第四野战军是在东北人民抚养下长大的，我们所以能获得胜利和光荣，东北人民是有很大功劳的。他号召全体指战员要防止骄傲自满情绪，再接再厉，完成建设中南区的光荣任务。
    为回答东北人民的慰问和鼓励，第四野战军首长暨全体指战员特于日前致电东北党、政、军、民致谢，并献给东北人民政府和中共中央东北局巨幅锦旗一面。
