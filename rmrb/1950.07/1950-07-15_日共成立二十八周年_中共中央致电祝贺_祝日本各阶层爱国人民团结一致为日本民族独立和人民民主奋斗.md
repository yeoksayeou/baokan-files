Title: 28th Anniversary of the Founding of the Japanese Communist Party; The Central Committee of the Chinese Communist Party Sends a Congratulatory Telegram; Wishing the Patriotic People of All Strata in Japan to Unite and Strive for Japan's National Independence and People's Democracy
Author:
Date: 1950-07-15
Page: 1
　　
　　Xinhua News Agency, 14th: The Central Committee of the Chinese Communist Party today sent a telegram to the Central Committee of the Japanese Communist Party, celebrating the 28th anniversary of the founding of the Japanese Communist Party. The telegram stated:
    Dear Comrades of the Central Committee of the Japanese Communist Party:
    The Chinese Communist Party warmly celebrates the 28th anniversary of the founding of the Japanese Communist Party, the vanguard of the patriotic people of Japan. The Japanese Communist Party has suffered and is still suffering from tyrannical oppression, but the Japanese people and the Chinese people know that justice belongs to you, and believe that final victory will belong to you. We wish you to unite as one, and unite with the patriotic people of all strata in Japan, and strive for Japan's national independence and people's democracy.
                                       The Central Committee of the Chinese Communist Party
                                 July 14, 1950

### 日共成立二十八周年  中共中央致电祝贺  祝日本各阶层爱国人民团结一致为日本民族独立和人民民主奋斗

1950-07-15
第1版()
专栏：

　　日共成立二十八周年
    中共中央致电祝贺
    祝日本各阶层爱国人民团结一致为日本民族独立和人民民主奋斗
    【新华社十四日讯】中国共产党中央委员会本日致电日本共产党中央委员会，庆祝日本共产党成立二十八周年。电称：
    日本共产党中央委员会亲爱的同志们：
    中国共产党热烈地庆祝日本爱国人民的前卫日本共产党成立的二十八周年。日本共产党曾经受过并且现在又受着横暴的压迫，但是日本人民和中国人民都知道正义是属于你们，都相信最后胜利将属于你们。祝你们团结一致，并且和日本各阶层爱国人民团结一致，为日本的民族独立和人民民主而奋斗。
                                       中国共产党中央委员会
                                 一九五○年七月十四日
