Title: Demonstrate to the Warmongers
Author: Yang Weiyu
Date: 1950-07-03
Page: 5

　　Demonstrate to the Warmongers
　　Yang Weiyu

　　The peace signature movement is not something to join in on just for fun. It represents a struggle, a demonstration, by the people of the world against the imperialist warmongers. It is to let the warmongers know that the people of the world are definitely not as foolish as they are. If they launch another world war, they will inevitably fail.

### 向战争贩子们示威
杨卫玉
1950-07-03
第5版()
专栏：

　　向战争贩子们示威
    杨卫玉
    和平签名运动，不是凑热闹的玩意儿，它是表示全世界人民对帝国主义战争贩子们的一种斗争，一种示威。要使战争贩子们知道全世界的人民决不像他们一样的糊涂，如果他们发动另一次世界战争，他们必然会失败的。
