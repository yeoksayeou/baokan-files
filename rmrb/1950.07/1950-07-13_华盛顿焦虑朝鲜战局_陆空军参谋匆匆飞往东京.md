Title: Washington Anxious About Korean War Situation; Army and Air Force Chiefs of Staff Hastily Fly to Tokyo
Author:
Date: 1950-07-13
Page: 1

　　Washington Anxious About Korean War Situation
    Army and Air Force Chiefs of Staff Hastily Fly to Tokyo
    [Xinhua News Agency, 12th] U.S. Army Chief of Staff Collins and Air Force Chief of Staff Vandenberg flew from Washington to Tokyo on the evening of the 10th. According to the Domei News Agency, the hurried dispatch of these two individuals to Tokyo for urgent consultations with MacArthur is seen in London as an expression of Washington's anxiety regarding the rapidly deteriorating military situation of the American invading forces in Korea.

### 华盛顿焦虑朝鲜战局   陆空军参谋匆匆飞往东京

1950-07-13
第1版()
专栏：

　　华盛顿焦虑朝鲜战局
    陆空军参谋匆匆飞往东京
    【新华社十二日讯】美陆军参谋柯林斯与空军参谋长范顿伯两人，于十日晚自华盛顿飞往东京。据电通社称，这两个人被匆匆派赴东京与麦克阿瑟作紧急商谈一事，在伦敦被认为是表现了华盛顿方面对于美国侵略军在朝鲜所处的急转直下的军事形势的焦虑。
