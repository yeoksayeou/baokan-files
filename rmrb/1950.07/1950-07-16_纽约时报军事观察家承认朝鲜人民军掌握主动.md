Title: New York Times Military Observer Admits Korean People's Army Holds Initiative
Author:
Date: 1950-07-16
Page: 1

　　Xinhua News Agency, New York, July 14th: The New York Times military observer, Baldwin, commenting on the Korean War, admitted that the North Korean army maintained strategic and tactical initiative on the entire front, continuing its southward advance towards Busan, an important port still in American hands. Baldwin continued that all units of the American 24th Division were now probably in Korea, and troops from another American division were also on their way to Korea.

### 纽约时报军事观察家承认朝鲜人民军掌握主动

1950-07-16
第1版()
专栏：

　　纽约时报军事观察家承认朝鲜人民军掌握主动
    【新华社十五日讯】据塔斯社纽约十四日讯：纽约时报军事观察家鲍尔温在评论朝鲜战事时承认，北朝鲜军队在全线保持了战略与战术的主动权，继续南进，向仍在美国手中的重要港口釜山前进。鲍尔温继称，美国第二十四师的所有部队目前大概已至朝鲜，美国另一个师的部队也正在赴朝鲜途中。
