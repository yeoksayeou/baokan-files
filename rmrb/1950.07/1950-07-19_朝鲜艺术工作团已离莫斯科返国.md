Title: Korean Art Troupe Leaves Moscow for Home

Author:
Date: 1950-07-19
Page: 4

　　Korean Art Troupe Leaves Moscow for Home
    [Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, 18th] Comprehensive TASS Moscow dispatch: The delegation of Korean art workers, invited by the Art Committee of the Soviet Council of Ministers to visit the Soviet Union, left Moscow for home on the 16th.
    The delegation of Korean art workers arrived in the Soviet Union on June 17th and stayed for a month, performing in Moscow and Leningrad. After returning to Moscow from Kyiv on the 13th of this month, the troupe held a final concert at the summer theater of the Moscow Cultural Relics Park on the 15th. Before leaving, a film evening was held, showing the famous Korean film "Blast Furnace," which depicts the life and work of the Korean people.

### 朝鲜艺术工作团已离莫斯科返国

1950-07-19
第4版()
专栏：

　　朝鲜艺术工作团已离莫斯科返国
    【新华社十八日讯】综合塔斯社莫斯科消息：应苏联部长会议艺术委员会邀请访问苏联的朝鲜艺术工作者代表团，已于十六日离莫斯科返国。
    朝鲜艺术工作者代表团系于六月十七日抵苏，在苏逗留一月，曾先后在莫斯科和列宁格勒等地表演。该团于本月十三日由基辅返抵莫斯科后，曾于十五日在莫斯科文物公园夏季剧场举行最后一次演奏会。行前并曾举行电影晚会，放映描写朝鲜人民生活及工作的朝鲜名片《鼓风炉》。
