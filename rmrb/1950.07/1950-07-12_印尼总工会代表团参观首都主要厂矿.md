Title: Indonesian General Confederation of Labor Delegation Visits Major Factories and Mines in the Capital
Author:
Date: 1950-07-12
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, 11th. The Indonesian General Confederation of Labor delegation visited major factories and mines in the capital from May 27th to June 21st. The delegation visited the Shijingshan Power Plant and Iron and Steel Plant, the People's Printing Factory, the Changxindian Railway Factory, the Clothing Factory, and the Agricultural Machinery Factory. The delegation received a warm welcome from the workers wherever they went. The workers all wished the Indonesian people victory.

### 印尼总工会代表团参观首都主要厂矿

1950-07-12
第1版()
专栏：

　　印尼总工会代表团参观首都主要厂矿
    【新华社十一日讯】印尼总工会代表团于五月二十七日到六月二十一日参观首都的各主要厂矿。代表团先后到过石景山发电厂和钢铁厂、人民印刷厂、长幸店铁路工厂、被服厂及农业机械厂。代表团每到一处都受到工人们的热烈欢迎。工人们都预祝印尼人民的胜利。
