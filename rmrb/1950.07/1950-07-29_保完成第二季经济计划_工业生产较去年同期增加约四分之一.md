Title: Ensuring the Completion of the Second Quarter Economic Plan: Industrial Production Increased by Approximately One-Quarter Compared to the Same Period Last Year
Author:
Date: 1950-07-29
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Sofia, July 26th: TASS reports that the Bulgarian State Planning Committee has released a communiqué regarding the implementation of the national economic plan for the second quarter of 1950.

　　The national economic plan for the second quarter of this year has been completed. Industrial production increased by 23% compared to the same period last year. Electricity production increased by 21%, and coal production increased by 15%.

　　The machine manufacturing industry achieved particularly outstanding results, with the production of tractor-drawn seed drills doubling and the production of electrical equipment increasing by 50%.

　　Light industrial enterprises have increased the output of basic necessities. For example, the production of cotton fabrics increased by 18%, woolen fabrics by 33%, and shoes and boots more than doubled. In the food industry, the production of cheese and butter nearly doubled.

　　Significant achievements have also been made in agriculture. This year, the area planted with industrial crops has increased by 5% to 25% compared to last year. The sowing plan has been exceeded, with the wheat sowing plan exceeding it by 20%.

　　Retail trade in the second quarter of this year increased by 19% compared to the same period last year. Compared to last year, the consumption of fats increased by 44%, vegetables by 13%, and sugar and sweets by 18%.

### 保完成第二季经济计划  工业生产较去年同期增加约四分之一

1950-07-29
第4版()
专栏：

　　保完成第二季经济计划
    工业生产较去年同期增加约四分之一
    【新华社二十七日讯】塔斯社索非亚二十六日讯：保加利亚国家计划委员会发表关于一九五○年第二季国家经济计划执行情形的公报。
    今年第二季国家经济计划已经完成。工业生产较去年同期增加了百分之二十三。发电量增加了百分之二十一，煤产量增加了百分之十五。
    机器制造工业的成绩特别优异，以拖拉机曳引的条播机的生产增加了一倍，电工器材的生产增加了百分之五十。
    轻工业企业已经提高了基本必需品的产量。例如，棉织物的生产增加了百分之十八，毛织物增加了百分之三十三，靴鞋增加了一倍以上。在食品工业中，乳酪与黄油的产量几乎增加了一倍。
    农业方面也获得重大成就。今年种植工业作物的面积，已较去年增加了百分之五到百分之二十五。播种计划已被超过，其中小麦播种计划超过了百分之二十。
    今年第二季零售贸易较去年同期增加了百分之十九。与去年相较，脂肪的消费量增加了百分之四十四，蔬菜增加百分之十三，糖与糖果增加百分之十八。
