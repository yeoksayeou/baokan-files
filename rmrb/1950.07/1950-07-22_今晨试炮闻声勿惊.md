Title: Do Not Be Alarmed by Artillery Test Firing This Morning
Author:
Date: 1950-07-22
Page: 6

Do Not Be Alarmed by Artillery Test Firing This Morning
　　A certain unit of the People's Liberation Army will conduct live-fire mountain artillery shooting exercises near Lugou Bridge at 10:00 AM today (July 22nd). It is hoped that people from all walks of life will not be alarmed upon hearing the sounds.

### 今晨试炮闻声勿惊

1950-07-22
第6版()
专栏：

　　今晨试炮闻声勿惊
    人民解放军某部定今日（七月二十二日）上午十时于芦沟桥附近进行山炮实弹射击演习。望各界闻声勿惊。
