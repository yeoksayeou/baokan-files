Title: Soviet Minister of the Army Issues Order to Celebrate Aviation Day; Zhigarev Delivers Speech at Moscow Celebration Rally, Points Out Soviet Air Power is Constantly Being Strengthened with First-Rate Technology
Author:
Date: 1950-07-18
Page: 4
　
　　Soviet Minister of the Army Issues Order to Celebrate Aviation Day
    Zhigarev Delivers Speech at Moscow Celebration Rally
    Points Out Soviet Air Power is Constantly Being Strengthened with First-Rate Technology
    [Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, 17th] TASS, Moscow, 16th: The Soviet Minister of the Army issued the following order on Aviation Day, the 16th:
    Comrade Pilots and Navigators, Gunners and Radio Operators, Aircraft Engineers and Mechanics, Workers of the Aircraft Manufacturing Industry!
    Comrade Soldiers and Non-Commissioned Officers!
    Comrade Lieutenants, Captains, and Generals!
    Today, the Soviet people and their glorious army are celebrating the festival of the Soviet Air Force, and celebrating the new achievements of the Soviet aviation industry in fulfilling the tasks put forward by our great leader and teacher, Marshal Stalin.
    On this national holiday – the festival of the Soviet Air Force – I salute you and extend my congratulations to you.
    I expect all the shining personnel of the Soviet aviation world and all the personnel of the aircraft manufacturing industry to obtain further success in strengthening the aviation power of our country.
    In commemoration of the festival of the Soviet Air Force, I order: today, July 16, 1950, in Moscow, the capital of our motherland, and in the capitals of the various Union republics, twenty salvos shall be fired in salute.
    Long live the Soviet Air Force!
    Long live our powerful motherland!
    Long live the Communist Party of the Soviet Union (Bolsheviks)!
    Long live our victorious inspirer and organizer – the great Stalin!
              Soviet Minister of the Army, Marshal of the Soviet Union, Vasilevsky (signed)
    [Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, 17th] According to TASS, Moscow: The Soviet Ministry of the Army, together with representatives of the working people of Moscow, held a celebration rally in the Moscow Trade Union Building on the 15th to commemorate Soviet Aviation Day. More than 2,000 people attended the rally, including Soviet Minister of the Army, Marshal of the Soviet Union Vasilevsky, Marshals of the Soviet Union Sokolovsky and Govorov, Secretary of the Central Committee of the Communist Youth League of the Soviet Union, Mikhailov, Hero of Socialist Labor, aircraft designers Tupolev, Yakovlev, and Ilyushin, "Grandfather of Russian Aviation," the oldest Russian aviator, Rossinsky, generals and officers of the navy and army, workers in the aircraft industry, and representatives of the Party, Soviets, and people's organizations of Moscow.
    Commander-in-Chief of the Soviet Air Force, Zhigarev, delivered a speech stating: While the Soviet people are celebrating Aviation Day, they are conducting peaceful signature campaigns throughout the country. The Soviet people are marching forward at the forefront of the warriors for peace and democracy. Zhigarev pointed out: The creative thinking of the Russian people has always far surpassed the aviation science and technology of Western Europe and the United States. The power of the Soviet aviation industry has been powerfully demonstrated during the Great Patriotic War and is constantly being strengthened with first-rate technology. Soviet pilots were the first in the world to master the astonishing flying techniques of jet aircraft. On this Aviation Day, the Soviet people and combat personnel first express their gratitude and love to the great Stalin, who created and nurtured the Soviet Air Force.

### 苏陆军部长颁令庆祝航空节  日加列夫在莫斯科庆祝大会上发表演说  指出苏航空威力正以头等技术不断加强

1950-07-18
第4版()
专栏：

　　苏陆军部长颁令庆祝航空节
    日加列夫在莫斯科庆祝大会上发表演说
    指出苏航空威力正以头等技术不断加强
    【新华社十七日讯】塔斯社莫斯科十六日讯：苏联陆军部长于十六日航空节发布命令如下：
    飞行员和领航员、射击手和无线电报务员、飞机工程师和机械士、飞机制造工业工人同志们！
    战士和士官同志们！
    尉官、校官和将军同志们！
    今天，苏联人民和他们光荣的陆军，正在庆祝苏联航空队的节日，并庆祝苏联航空事业在履行我们伟大的领袖和导师、大元帅斯大林同志所提出的任务当中的新成就。
    值此全国性的节日——苏联航空队的节日之际，我向你们致敬并向你们致贺。
    我期望光辉的苏联航空界全体人员和飞机制造工业的全体人员，在加强我们国家的航空力量中获得进一步的成功。
    为了纪念苏联航空队的节日，我命令：今天，一九五○年七月十六日，在我们祖国的首都莫斯科，及各加盟共和国首都各鸣礼炮二十响。
    苏联航空队万岁！
    我们强大的祖国万岁！
    苏联共产党（布）万岁！
    我们胜利的鼓舞者及组织者——伟大的斯大林万岁！
              苏联陆军部长、苏联元帅华西列夫斯基（签字）
    【新华社十七日讯】据塔斯社莫斯科讯：苏联陆军部会同莫斯科劳动人民的代表于十五日在莫斯科工会大厦举行纪念苏联航空节的庆祝大会。出席大会的有二千余人，包括苏联陆军部部长、苏联元帅华西列夫斯基，苏联元帅索科洛夫斯基与戈沃罗夫，苏联共产主义青年团中央委员会书记米哈依洛夫，社会主义劳动英雄、飞机设计家杜波列夫，雅科夫列夫和伊尔友新，“俄国航空之祖父”、最老的俄国飞行家罗辛斯基，海陆军将官，飞机工业的工人，和莫斯科的党、苏维埃与人民团体的代表们。
    苏联空军总司令日加列夫发表演说称：苏联人民在庆祝航空节之时，正在全国范围内进行和平签名，苏联人民站在和平与民主战士的最前列向前迈进。日加列夫指出：俄罗斯人民的创造思想，一直都远远胜过西欧和美国的航空科学和技术。苏联航空事业的威力，已在伟大爱国战争时期有力地表现出来，并不断地用头等技术加强起来。苏联飞行员在世界上首先掌握了喷气式飞机的惊人飞行技术。值此航空节日，苏联人民及战斗人员首先向创造了并且培养了苏联航空队的伟大斯大林表示感激与爱戴。
