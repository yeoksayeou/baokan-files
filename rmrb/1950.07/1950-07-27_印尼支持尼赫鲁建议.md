Title: Indonesia Supports Nehru's Proposal
Author:
Date: 1950-07-27
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, July 27th: After the Indonesian government announced on June 30th that it would not interfere in the Korean War, it has recently expressed its support for Indian Prime Minister Nehru's proposal for a peaceful resolution of the Korean issue. According to a UPI report from Jakarta, Indonesian Information Minister Mononutu declared on the 22nd that the Indonesian government fully supports Indian Prime Minister Nehru's efforts to mediate the Korean dispute. The Indonesian government has been constantly receiving information about the development of the situation through diplomatic channels.

### 印尼支持尼赫鲁建议

1950-07-27
第1版()
专栏：

　　印尼支持尼赫鲁建议
    【新华社二十七日讯】印尼政府于六月三十日宣布不干涉朝鲜战事后，近又表示支持印度总理尼赫鲁和平解决朝鲜问题的建议。据合众社雅加达讯，印尼宣传部长莫诺吐二十二日宣称，印尼政府充分支持印度总理尼赫鲁调解朝鲜纠纷的努力。印尼政府已通过外交途径不断获悉事态发展的消息。
