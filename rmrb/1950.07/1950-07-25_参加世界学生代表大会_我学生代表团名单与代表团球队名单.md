Title: Attending the World Student Congress: List of Our Student Delegation and Team Rosters

Author:

Date: 1950-07-25
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, 24th. The list of the Chinese student delegation and the Chinese student delegation's team rosters for the Second World Student Congress are as follows:

(I) List of the Chinese Student Delegation:

Head of Delegation: Yang Cheng, Alternate Member of the Central Committee of the Communist Youth League, Deputy Director of the Student Department of the Central Committee of the Communist Youth League. Secretary-General: Ke Zaishuo, Secretary-General of the All-China Students' Federation. Members: Liang Geng, Representative of the All-China Students' Federation to the International Union of Students, Vice President of the International Union of Students (currently in Prague). Wu Xueqian, Representative of the All-China Youth Federation to the World Federation of Democratic Youth (currently in Prague). Li Rongchun, student at Renmin University of China in Beijing, Chairman of the Student Union. Qian Dawei, Student Minister of the Nanjing Municipal Committee of the Communist Youth League. Feng YinFu, Chairman of the Northeast Students' Federation. Cheng Zhenqiu, staff member of the International Department of the All-China Students' Federation. Huang Zumin, staff member of the International Department of the All-China Students' Federation. Jin Xiuling (female), student at Renmin University of China in Beijing. Liu Kunzun, student at Sun Yat-sen University in Guangzhou. Huan Juecai Lang (Tibetan), student worker in Qinghai. Eriba La (Mongolian), student at the Inner Mongolia Cadre School. Li Botian, student at Sun Yat-sen University in Guangzhou, Chairman of the Guangdong Students' Federation. Sun Yuwu, student at Wuhan University. Gao Jing, student at Harbin Foreign Language College, Vice Chairman of the Harbin Students' Federation. Ding Yongning (female), student at Aurora University in Shanghai. Dong Xuelong, staff member of the Kunming Students' Federation. Le Daiyun (female), student at Peking University. Huang Tongyu (female), student at Chongqing Women's Normal College, Vice Chairman of the Chongqing Students' Federation. Li Tianxiang, student at the Central Academy of Fine Arts in Beijing, Chairman of the Student Union. Wu Zuqiang, student at the Tianjin Central Conservatory of Music, Chairman of the Student Union. Liang Huaqun (female), student at the Central Academy of Drama in Beijing. Farida (female, Hui), student at the Xinjiang Uyghur School. (Six more representatives are abroad)

(II) Roster of the Chinese Student Delegation's Team: Leader: Wu Gongjun, Secretary-General of the Tianjin Students' Federation, Secretary-General of the Tianjin Sports Association. Basketball Instructor: Song Junfu, Professor of the Department of Physical Education, Beijing Normal University. Volleyball Instructor: Ma Qiwei, Lecturer of the Department of Physical Education, Tsinghua University, Beijing. Basketball Team Captain: Huang Bailing, student at Datong University in Shanghai. Players: Le Jiazhou, student at Datong University in Shanghai. Xu Zhongyi, student at Datong University in Shanghai. Wang Qi, student at the Beijing Pharmaceutical Training Institute. Zhou Baoen, student at Yenching University in Beijing. Li Hanting, student at JinGu University in Tianjin. Chen Wenbin, student at Peking University in Beijing. Zhang Changlu, student at Lixin Accounting School in Tianjin.

Volleyball Team Captain: Du Keli, student at Fu Jen Catholic University in Beijing. Players: Xu Yinghong, graduate of Yucai Middle School in Shanghai. Li Zhang, student at Yenching University in Beijing. Li Angge, student at Peiyang University in Tianjin. Sun Zhian, graduate of Andong High School. Liu Baowu, student at Northeast Normal University. Rao Yurong, student at Zhaoguang Middle School in Shanghai.
