Title: Our Country's Youth Delegation Returns to Beijing; Receives Warm Welcome from Broad Youth in Germany and Czechoslovakia
Author:
Date: 1950-07-18
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency Report: Xu Liqun, head of our country's youth delegation, combat hero Li Guoying, a member of the delegation, and Professor Feng Zhi of Peking University, who was invited to be a language consultant for the delegation to Germany, have returned to Beijing recently after attending the All-German Youth Congress and the Czechoslovak Youth League Congress. Our country's youth delegation received a warm welcome from the broad masses of youth in various parts of Germany and Czechoslovakia. The Chinese people's leader Mao Zedong, and the great contribution of the Chinese people and youth to world peace, have won the sincere respect of the youth of the two countries.

### 我国青年代表团返京  在德捷受到广大青年热情欢迎

1950-07-18
第1版()
专栏：

　　我国青年代表团返京
    在德捷受到广大青年热情欢迎
    【本报讯】据新华社讯：参加全德青年大会和捷克青年联盟代表大会的我国青年代表团团长许立群、团员战斗英雄李国英及应邀为赴德代表团语文顾问的北大教授冯至等已于日前先后返抵北京。我国青年代表团在德国和捷克各地曾受到广大青年的热情欢迎。中国人民领袖毛泽东，中国人民和青年对世界和平的伟大贡献，获得两国青年的衷心崇敬。
