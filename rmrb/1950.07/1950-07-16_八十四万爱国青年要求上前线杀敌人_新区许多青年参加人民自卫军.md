Title: 840,000 Patriotic Youths Request to Go to the Front Lines to Kill the Enemy, Many Youths in Newly Liberated Areas Join the People's Self-Defense Forces
Author:
Date: 1950-07-16
Page: 4

　　Xinhua News Agency, Pyongyang, 15th. (TASS dispatch) A large number of patriotic Korean youths are enthusiastically joining the People's Army. As of July 10th, the Council of Ministers of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea has received applications from 740,000 young workers, peasants, and students in the northern part of the Republic, requesting to join the People's Army and be sent to the front lines. More than 100,000 of them are young women.
　　In the liberated areas of South Korea, many young people have joined the People's Self-Defense Forces. In Seoul, 80,000 people have volunteered to join the army. After a mobilization meeting was held in Seoul to organize the People's Self-Defense Forces, several thousand workers wanted to join. Another 17,000 students in Seoul also expressed their desire to join the People's Self-Defense Forces.

### 八十四万爱国青年要求上前线杀敌人  新区许多青年参加人民自卫军

1950-07-16
第4版()
专栏：

　　八十四万爱国青年要求上前线杀敌人
    新区许多青年参加人民自卫军
    【新华社十五日讯】综合塔斯社平壤讯：大批朝鲜爱国青年热烈参加人民军。截至七月十日止，朝鲜民主主义人民共和国部长会议共收到共和国北部七十四万青年工人、农民和学生的申请书，要求参加人民军并送他们上前线。内有十多万是女青年。
    在南朝鲜解放的地区，许多青年参加了人民自卫军。汉城已有八万人自愿要求参军。汉城的工人在举行了组织人民自卫军的动员大会之后，就有好几千工人要参加人民自卫军。汉城另有一万七千学生也表示要参加人民自卫军。
