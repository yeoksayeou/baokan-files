Title: YMCA to Hold Soviet Photo Exhibition
Author:
Date: 1950-07-31
Page: 6

　　YMCA to Hold Soviet Photo Exhibition
　　To celebrate the "August 1st" Army Day and oppose American imperialism's aggression against Taiwan and Korea, the YMCA of this city will hold a Soviet economic construction photo exhibition from July 30th to August 3rd. The exhibition will be open from 9:00 AM to 7:00 PM daily. All are welcome to visit.

### 基督教青年会举办苏联图片展览

1950-07-31
第6版()
专栏：

　　基督教青年会举办苏联图片展览
    本市基督教青年会为庆祝“八一”建军节，反对美帝国主义侵略台湾朝鲜。特定于七月三十日起，至八月三日止。每日上午九时至下午七时在该会举行苏联经济建设图片展览。欢迎各界前往参观。
