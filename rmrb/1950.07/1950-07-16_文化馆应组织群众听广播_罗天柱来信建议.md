Date: 1950-07-16
Page: 4

Cultural Centers Should Organize the Masses to Listen to the Radio
    A Letter of Suggestion from Luo Tianzhu

To the Editors:
    The Wudong Banner Xiaying Cultural Center in Suiyuan Province installed a radio in May of this year, but has not organized the masses to listen. Only ten or so cadres from the government go to sit for a while each day, listening to songs and Peking opera. The local masses and most of the cadres don't even know if they can go to listen. Our main purpose in installing radios in rural areas is to convey government orders, help cadres learn policies, and strengthen propaganda work among the masses. I suggest that cultural centers should organize the masses to listen to the radio, set listening times, and post the daily broadcast schedule. Only in this way can rural broadcasting work play its due role.
                                                       Luo Tianzhu

### 文化馆应组织群众听广播  罗天柱来信建议
罗天柱
1950-07-16
第4版()
专栏：

　　文化馆应组织群众听广播
    罗天柱来信建议编辑同志：
    绥远省武东旗下营文化馆，于今年五月间安装了收音机，但并没有组织群众收听。每天只有十个八个机关干部去坐一会，收听一些歌曲、京戏。该地群众及大部分干部根本不知道是否可以去听。我们在农村安装收音机，主要目的是传达政令，帮助干部学习政策，加强群众的宣传工作。我建议文化馆应当组织群众听广播，定出听播时间，每天把收听的节目贴出来，这样农村广播工作才能发挥它的应有作用。
                                                       罗天柱
