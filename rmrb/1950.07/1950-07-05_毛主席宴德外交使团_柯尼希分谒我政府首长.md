Title: Chairman Mao Hosts the German Diplomatic Mission; Koenig Pays Respects to Our Government Leaders
Author:
Date: 1950-07-05
Page: 1

　　Xinhua News Agency, 4th: Chairman Mao Zedong of the Central People's Government hosted a banquet at 7:00 PM on the 4th for Mr. Koenig, Head of the Diplomatic Mission of the German Democratic Republic to China with the rank of Ambassador Extraordinary and Plenipotentiary, and all the diplomatic officials of the mission. Invited to attend were Vice Chairmen of the Central People's Government Zhu De, Liu Shaoqi, Li Jishen, and Zhang Lan; Secretary-General Lin Boqu; Premier and concurrently Foreign Minister of the Government Administration Council Zhou Enlai; Director of the General Office of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs Wang Bingnan; Director of the Bureau of Ceremonies of the Central People's Government Yu Xinqing; and Director of the Soviet and Eastern European Affairs Department of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs Wu Xiuquan, Deputy Director Xu Yixin, and Chief of the Reception Office Wang Zhuoru.
　　Xinhua News Agency, 4th: After presenting his credentials, Mr. Koenig, Head of the Diplomatic Mission of the German Democratic Republic to China with the rank of Ambassador Extraordinary and Plenipotentiary, paid respects separately to Vice Chairmen Zhu De, Liu Shaoqi, Li Jishen, and Zhang Lan; Vice Premiers Guo Moruo and Huang Yanpei; and visited Minister of Trade Ye Jizhuang, Minister of Culture Shen Yanbing, Mayor of Beijing Nie Rongzhen, and Vice Mayor Wu Han.

### 毛主席宴德外交使团  柯尼希分谒我政府首长

1950-07-05
第1版()
专栏：

　　毛主席宴德外交使团
    柯尼希分谒我政府首长
    【新华社四日讯】中央人民政府毛泽东主席于四日下午七时宴德意志民主共和国驻我国外交使团特命全权大使衔团长柯尼希及该使团全体外交官员。应邀作陪者有中央人民政府朱德副主席，刘少奇副主席，李济深副主席，张澜副主席，林伯渠秘书长，政务院周恩来总理兼外交部长，外交部办公厅王炳南主任，中央人民政府典礼局余心清局长及外交部苏联东欧司伍修权司长，徐一新副司长，交际处王倬如处长。
    【新华社四日讯】德意志民主共和国驻我国外交使团特命全权大使衔团长柯尼希于呈递国书后，曾分谒朱德副主席，刘少奇副主席，李济深副主席，张澜副主席，郭沫若副总理，黄炎培副总理，并分访贸易部叶季壮部长，文化部沈雁冰部长，及北京市聂荣臻市长，吴晗副市长。
