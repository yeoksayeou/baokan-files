Title: Fuzhou General Trade Union Established, Uniting All City Workers to Overcome Difficulties, Supporting the Liberation of Taiwan and Jinmen, and Building a New Fuzhou
Author:
Date: 1950-07-14
Page: 2

　　Xinhua News Agency, Shanghai, 13th. The Second Fuzhou Municipal Workers' Representative Conference was held from July 1st to 7th. The Fuzhou General Trade Union was established at the meeting, and the general policy for the Fuzhou workers' movement in the future was adopted. The general policy is: The working class of the city should further unite, concentrate its efforts to overcome difficulties, maintain production, provide relief to unemployed workers, and strive to support the liberation of Taiwan and Jinmen and build a new Fuzhou. The city has now established 108 grassroots trade unions with a total of 31,500 trade union members, accounting for approximately 70 percent of the city's total number of employees.

### 福州市总工会成立  团结全市工人努力克服困难  支援解放台湾金门建设新福州

1950-07-14
第2版()
专栏：

　　福州市总工会成立
    团结全市工人努力克服困难  支援解放台湾金门建设新福州
    【新华社上海十三日电】福州市第二届工人代表大会于七月一日至七日举行。会上成立了福州市总工会，并通过了今后福州工人运动的总方针，总方针是：全市工人阶级进一步团结起来，集中力量克服困难，维持生产，救济失业工人，为支援解放台湾、金门，建设新福州而奋斗。该市现已建立基层工会一百零八个，共有工会会员三万一千五百人，约占全市职工总数的百分之七十。
