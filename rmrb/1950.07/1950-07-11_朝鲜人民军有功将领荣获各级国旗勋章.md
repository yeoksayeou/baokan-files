Title: Meritorious Generals of the Korean People's Army Awarded National Flag Orders of Various Classes
Author:
Date: 1950-07-11
Page: 1

　　Xinhua News Agency, Pyongyang, July 10th: The Standing Committee of the Supreme People's Assembly of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea issued an order on the 8th stating: The Standing Committee of the Supreme People's Assembly of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea, in recognition of the exceptional meritorious service rendered by the following generals of the Korean People's Army in the righteous war for the unification, freedom, and independence of the fatherland, hereby awards them the following orders: First Class National Flag Order to Lee Kwon-mu, Lee Hoon, Ryu Kyung-su, Fang Hushan, Choi Kwang, Choi Hyun, and Wu Bailong, a total of seven people; Second Class National Flag Order to Kang Byung-chan and Liu Yuanquan, two people.

### 朝鲜人民军有功将领荣获各级国旗勋章

1950-07-11
第1版()
专栏：

　　朝鲜人民军有功将领荣获各级国旗勋章
    【新华社平壤十日电】朝鲜民主主义人民共和国最高人民会议常任委员会八日发布命令称：朝鲜民主主义人民共和国最高人民会议常任委员会，对于在争取祖国统一、自由、独立的正义战争中建立特殊功勋的朝鲜人民军下列将领，分别授予勋章：第一级国旗勋章授予李权武、李勋、柳京洙、方虎山、崔光、崔贤及吴伯龙等七人；第二级国旗勋章授予姜炳灿、刘源泉二人。
