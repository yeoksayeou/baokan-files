　　Harbin No. 2 Middle School Civil Engineering Students and Teachers Complete Waterway Surveying Practice
　　The faculty and students of the Civil Engineering Department of Harbin No. 2 Middle School High School went to the Muling River for surveying practice and completed it on June 13th. The total practice lasted 44 days, completing 505 kilometers of waterway surveying from the Hulin County Hubei Sluice to the Muji Bridge in Jixi County, exceeding the original plan of 405 kilometers, and also completing the survey of the paddy field area in the middle section of the Muling River.
　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　(Harbin No. 2 Middle School Communications Group)
　　North China University of Technology Faculty and Staff Declare Support for Foreign Minister Zhou's Statement
　　The faculty and staff of North China University of Technology issued a declaration on the 3rd, supporting Foreign Minister Zhou's statement. It stated: Truman's dispatch of troops to invade Korea and our country's Taiwan "shows nothing else but that he is a warmonger, a group of despicable robbers." Truman's statement "did not scare us, on the contrary, it only aroused our greater anger. We firmly support Foreign Minister Zhou's statement." The declaration concluded: "We have unlimited confidence in defeating this gang of outwardly strong but inwardly weak robbers."
　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　(North China University of Technology Labor Union, Student Union)
　　Condemning Truman's Statement
　　Beijing-Suiyuan Railway Workers Issue Circular Telegram
　　A Certain Unit Holds Cadre Symposium
　　All employees of the Beijing-Suiyuan Railway issued a circular telegram on the 3rd, condemning the U.S. President Truman's arrogant statement on June 27th that undermined world peace and invaded Korea and the Chinese people. They are determined to carry out rationalization proposals, create new records, and participate in the peace signature campaign to smash the imperialist conspiracy.
　　The Political Department of a certain division of the Liaodong Military Region held a cadre symposium on the 3rd regarding the South Korean puppet government's attack on the Korean people and Truman's declaration of aggression. The cadres present unanimously condemned the South Korean puppet government's traitorous behavior and the U.S. imperialism's aggression; and firmly expressed their support for Foreign Minister Zhou Enlai's statement.
　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　(Min Guoan)
　　Revolutionary University Academic Affairs Office Sponsors Exhibition of Heroic Models' Deeds
　　The "Exhibition of Heroic Models' Deeds" organized by the Academic Affairs Office of the North China People's Revolutionary University to introduce the deeds of party member cadres of worker-peasant origin studying in one of the university's departments in the War of Resistance Against Japan and the War of Liberation is about to begin. The exhibits include written records, photos, awards, as well as comic strips and illustrations. These materials vividly demonstrate the heroic struggles of the broad masses of worker-peasant cadres under the leadership of the Communist Party.
　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　(Revolutionary University Communications Group)

　　哈市二中土木科师生完成水路测量实习
    哈尔滨市立第二中学土木科高中部全体师生赴穆棱河作测量实习，已于六月十三日完成。共计实习四十四天，完成由虎林县湖北水闸至鸡西县木桥为止的五百零五公里的水路测量，超过了原计划四百零五公里，并完成了穆棱河中段水田区的调查测量任务。
                              （哈市第二中学通讯组）
　　华大工院全体教职员宣言拥护周外长声明
    华北大学工学院全体教职员于三日发表宣言拥护周外长的声明。内称：杜鲁门的派兵侵略朝鲜与我国的台湾，“这不说明别的，只说明他是战争贩子，是一群可恶的强盗”。杜鲁门的声明“并没有吓倒我们，相反的只引起我们更加愤怒。我们坚决的拥护周外长的声明”。宣言最后说：“我们有无限信心，战胜这一伙外强中干的强盗”。
                                      （华大工学院工会、学生会）
　　斥责杜鲁门声明
    京绥路职工发表通电
    某部召开干部座谈会
    京绥铁路全体职工，于三日发出通电，斥责美总统杜鲁门在六月二十七日发表的破坏世界和平、侵略朝鲜与中国人民的狂妄声明。决心开展合理化建议、创造新纪录和参加和平签名运动，粉碎帝国主义的阴谋。
    辽东军区某师政治部，就南朝鲜傀儡政府向朝鲜人民发动进攻及杜鲁门发表侵略声明事件，于三日召开了干部座谈会。到会干部一致斥责南朝鲜傀儡政府的卖国行为与美帝国主义的侵略行为；并坚决表示拥护周恩来外长的声明。
                                          （闵国安）
　　革大教务处主办  英模史迹展览
    华北人民革命大学教务处为介绍在该校一部学习的工农出身的党员干部在抗日战争和解放战争中的事迹，而举办的“英模史迹展览会”，即将开始展览。展览品有文字记载、照片、奖品以及连环画和插画等。这些材料，生动地表现了在共产党的领导下，广大工农干部英勇斗争的情形。
                                       （革大通讯组）
