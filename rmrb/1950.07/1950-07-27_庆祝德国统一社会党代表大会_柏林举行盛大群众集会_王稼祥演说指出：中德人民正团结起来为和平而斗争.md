Title: Grand Mass Rally Held in Berlin to Celebrate the German Socialist Unity Party Congress; Wang Jiaxiang's Speech Points Out: The Chinese and German People are United to Fight for Peace
Author:
Date: 1950-07-27
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Berlin, 26th: According to TASS, a grand mass rally was held in Berlin on the night of the 24th to celebrate the Third Congress of the German Socialist Unity Party.

　　Wang Jiaxiang, member of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of China, delivered a speech at the meeting, extending congratulations on behalf of the Communist Party of China and the Chinese people to the attendees, and pointed out that the Chinese people are paying close attention to the development of the German Democratic Republic. He said: "The establishment of the People's Republic of China and the German Democratic Republic makes it easier for the two countries to establish new friendly relations. Economic cooperation and trade ties will be smoothly developed in the future. We have united to fight for a common cause, for the cause of peace and democracy throughout the world. This consolidates our friendship."

　　Okhaba, Secretary of the Central Committee of the Polish United Workers' Party, Togliatti, General Secretary of the Italian Communist Party, Pollitt, General Secretary of the British Communist Party, and Duclos, Secretary of the French Communist Party, also delivered speeches at the meeting.

　　Pieck, Chairman of the Socialist Unity Party, expressed his gratitude to the fraternal parties of various countries that sent delegations to attend the Third Congress of the Socialist Unity Party, especially to the Communist Party of the Soviet Union. Pieck pointed out that the Congress has decided to fight for peace with greater determination. Pieck declared that the people of West Germany demand that the Americans get out of West Germany immediately, just as the people of Korea demand that the Americans leave Korea.

　　The highlight of the Congress was the grand parade of the working people.

### 庆祝德国统一社会党代表大会  柏林举行盛大群众集会  王稼祥演说指出：中德人民正团结起来为和平而斗争

1950-07-27
第4版()
专栏：

　　庆祝德国统一社会党代表大会
    柏林举行盛大群众集会
    王稼祥演说指出：中德人民正团结起来为和平而斗争
    【新华社二十六日讯】据塔斯社柏林讯：柏林市于二十四日夜举行盛大的群众集会，庆祝德国统一社会党第三次代表大会。
    中国共产党中央委员王稼祥曾在会上演说，他代表中国共产党和中国人民向会众致贺，并指出，中国人民非常关怀地注意着德意志民主共和国的发展。他说：“中华人民共和国和德意志民主共和国的成立，使两国更容易地建立新的友好关系。经济合作和贸易联系将于今后顺利地展开。我们已团结起来为共同事业、为全世界的和平与民主事业而奋斗。这巩固了我们的友谊。”
    波兰统一工人党中央委员会书记奥哈巴、意大利共产党总书记托里亚蒂、英国共产党总书记波立特及法国共产党书记杜克洛，均在会上发表演说。
    统一社会党主席皮克向派遣代表团出席统一社会党第三次代表大会的各国兄弟党，表示感谢，特别对苏联共产党表示感谢。皮克指出：大会已决定以更大的决心为争取和平而斗争。皮克宣称：西德人民要求美国人立刻滚出西德去，这正像朝鲜人民要求美国人离开朝鲜一样。
    大会的最高峰是劳动人民的大游行。
