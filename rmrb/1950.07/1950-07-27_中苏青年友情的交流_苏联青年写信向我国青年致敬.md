Title: Exchanges of Sino-Soviet Youth Friendship, Soviet Youth Write Letters to Pay Tribute to Chinese Youth
Author:
Date: 1950-07-27
Page: 4

　　Exchanges of Sino-Soviet Youth Friendship
    Soviet Youth Write Letters to Pay Tribute to Chinese Youth
    [Xinhua News Agency, 26th] After the Soviet youth delegation returned to the Soviet Union from its visit to China, rallies of Soviet Youth League members and youth were held in Moscow, Leningrad, Kiev, Minsk, Tbilisi, Alma-Ata, Baku, Gorky, Sverdlovsk, Yerevan, Saratov and many other cities, in factories, collective farms, universities and secondary schools to hear reports from the delegation on their visit to China. Peslyak, a member of the delegation and Vice-Chairman of the Soviet Youth Anti-Fascist Committee, recently wrote to Feng Wenbin, Secretary of the Central Committee of the Chinese New Democratic Youth League, informing him of the situation of the above-mentioned conferences and forwarding letters from the youth of Moscow, Leningrad, Gorky, Tbilisi and Alma-Ata to the Chinese Youth League. These letters are full of the spirit of internationalism and express the Soviet youth's warm and fraternal respect for the Chinese youth.
    At the youth rallies in the above-mentioned Soviet cities, the Soviet youth delegation reported that the Chinese people, under the leadership of the Communist Party and Chairman Mao Zedong, are building a new and free life; the delegates told of the great love and friendly feelings of the Chinese youth towards the Soviet people and towards Stalin, the leader and teacher of all progressive mankind. The youth of Moscow and the Moscow region said in a letter to the Central Committee of the Chinese Youth League: "Soviet youth, like all our people, rejoice at the victories won by the Chinese people under the leadership of the Chinese Communist Party and its leader Comrade Mao Zedong. The Soviet people, under the leadership of Comrade Stalin, are full of confidence and are advancing towards the construction of a communist society." "The friendship between the youth of the two great peoples of China and the Soviet Union is a guarantee for the further consolidation of the international democratic youth movement. We will spare no effort to consolidate this friendship." The Youth League members and youth of Leningrad said in a letter to the Central Committee of the Chinese Youth League: "The Youth League members and youth of Leningrad, together with you, are overjoyed by the victories won by the Chinese people in the struggle to destroy the shackles of imperialism and in the struggle to restore and develop the national economy of the country. We assure you that in this great struggle, you will always see the Soviet people as your reliable friends and comrades."

### 中苏青年友情的交流  苏联青年写信向我国青年致敬

1950-07-27
第4版()
专栏：

　　中苏青年友情的交流
    苏联青年写信向我国青年致敬
    【新华社二十六日讯】苏联青年代表团于访问我国回国后，在莫斯科、列宁格勒、基辅、明斯克、特比里西、阿拉木图、巴库、高尔基城、斯维尔德洛夫斯克、耶里温、萨拉托夫等城市以及其他许多城市里，在工厂里、集体农场里、高等学校和中等学校里，都举行了苏联青年团员和青年的大会，听取代表团访问中国的报告。该团团员、苏联青年反法西斯委员会副主席裴斯良克日前致函中国新民主主义青年团中央委员会书记冯文彬，告知上述大会的情况并转来莫斯科、列宁格勒、高尔基城、特比里西和阿拉木图等城市青年给中国青年团的信。在这些信里都充满了国际主义的精神，并表示了苏联青年对中国青年的热烈的、兄弟的敬意。
    在上述苏联各城市的青年大会中，苏联青年代表团报告了我国人民在共产党和毛泽东主席的领导下，正在建设新的自由的生活；代表们讲述了我国青年对苏联人民、对一切进步人类的领袖和导师斯大林所表示的巨大的敬爱和友好的感情。莫斯科市和莫斯科州的青年在给我国青年团中央的信中说：“苏联青年，和我们的全体人民一样，因中国人民在中国共产党和它的领袖毛泽东同志的领导下所获得的胜利而欢欣。苏联的人民在斯大林同志的领导下，正充满着信心，向建设共产主义社会前进。”“中苏两国伟大人民的青年间的友谊，是更进一步巩固国际民主青年运动的保证。为了巩固这一友谊，我们决不吝惜任何力量。”列宁格勒的青年团员和青年给我国青年团中央的信中说：“列宁格勒的青年团员和青年，同你们一道，因中国人民在摧毁帝国主义的束缚的斗争中，在恢复和发展国家的国民经济的斗争中所获得的胜利，而感到莫大的欢欣。我们向你们保证，你们在这个伟大的斗争里，将永远看到苏联人民是你们可靠的朋友和同志。”
