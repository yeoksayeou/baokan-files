Title: Beijing Department Store Production and Sales Conference Concludes, Broadly Discussing Production, Sales, and Management Issues

Author:

Date: 1950-07-30
Page: 3

Beijing City News

　　Beijing Department Store Production and Sales Conference Concludes
    Broadly Discussing Production, Sales, and Management Issues
    [Beijing City News Agency] The Beijing Department Store Production and Sales Conference concluded on the 25th after a summary meeting held in the auditorium of the Preparatory Committee of the Federation of Industry and Commerce. The conference opened on the 13th of this month, with a total of 41 representatives from relevant parties attending. The conference held two and a half days of extensive discussions on some of the current issues in the department store industry regarding production, sales, and management. In order to effectively solve the problems raised at the meeting, relevant parties earnestly studied ways to solve these problems during the adjournment. At the closing meeting, Peng Cheng, Deputy Director of the Commercial Bureau, gave a summary report, Li Yizan, representative of the Preparatory Committee of the Federation of Industry and Commerce, spoke, and Ma Diru of Wangfu Department Store delivered the closing remarks on behalf of the Presidium of the Conference.

### 京百货产销会议闭幕  广泛讨论了产销经营等问题

1950-07-30
第3版()
专栏：京市新闻

　　京百货产销会议闭幕
    广泛讨论了产销经营等问题
    【京市新闻处讯】京市百货产销会议已于二十五日在工商联合会筹委会礼堂举行总结大会后闭幕。这个会议是本月十三日开幕的，出席各有关方面代表共四十一人。会议曾就百货业目前在生产、推销及经营上的一些问题，进行了两天半广泛的讨论。为切实解决会中提出的问题，在休会期间，各有关方面认真地研究了解决这些问题的办法。在闭幕会上，由商业局彭城副局长作总结报告，工商联合会筹委会代表李贻赞讲话，王府百货公司马棣如代表会议主席团致闭幕词。
