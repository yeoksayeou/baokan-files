Title: The Peace Signature in Zhangzhuang Village
Author: Bi Yaocai
Date: 1950-07-25
Page: 4

　　The Peace Signature in Zhangzhuang Village
　　Bi Yaocai
　　People shout, drums beat,
　　The Zhangzhuang signature campaign begins!
　　The village chairman shouts loudly:
　　"Oppose new wars,
　　We want peace!
　　Whoever agrees, come sign quickly."

　　Old Zhang, anxious and sweating,
　　Carries his hoe back to the village.
　　He walks through the streets and alleys,
　　And soon arrives at the meeting place.
　　The crowd surrounds it tightly,
　　Millions of people shout in unison:
　　"I want to sign!..."
　　Those who can write, write with a pen,
　　Those who can't write, register verbally.

　　Old Zhang with his pale beard,
　　Enters the meeting place and shouts:
　　"Folks, make way,
　　Let me sign my name!
　　My surname is Zhang, my name is Yushan,
　　I've done long-term labor, begged for food,
　　Since the land reform, I've turned my life around,
　　I'm wholeheartedly engaged in production.
　　If the imperialists want to provoke a war,
　　That's not going to happen! (Note)
　　I'm not afraid of the atomic bomb!
　　I'll pick up my hoe,
　　And fight back against the American aggressors,
　　My eldest son was a businessman.
　　American planes dropped bombs,
　　My eldest son met his end in Shijiazhuang,
　　Died without even seeing his bones.
　　My second son angrily went to the front lines,
　　To avenge his brother in Jiangnan.
　　We defeated the Japanese devils,
　　And the bandit Chiang is finished too.
　　I want to live peacefully,
　　I want to get rich and engage in production.
　　The people of Taiwan also want liberation!"

　　Old Zhang puts down his pen after speaking.
　　His daughter-in-law holds her grandson behind him.
　　His daughter-in-law shouts:
　　"Dad! Write down my name!
　　Avenge my man's death!"
　　Her voice changes,
　　Tears fall like rain.
　　"Dad! Remember to write down your grandson's name,
　　His name is Victory."
　　"Right! Write down all seven members of the family,
　　Many people, great strength like a mountain!"

　　Thousands write, ten thousands speak,
　　The signature book is filled.
　　The liberated peasants have a high level of awareness,
　　They care about world affairs!
　　"Oppose new wars,
　　Resolutely defend world peace!"
　　Note: "That won't do" meaning "That's not going to happen."

### 张庄村的和平签名
毕耀彩
1950-07-25
第4版()
专栏：

　　张庄村的和平签名
    毕耀彩
    人嘶喊，锣鼓敲，
    张庄签名开始了！
    村主席大声喊：
    “反对新战争，
    我们要和平！
    谁赞成快来签名。”
　
    张老汉急的通身汗，
    扛起锄头转回庄。
    穿过大街走小巷，
    马上走到会场上。
    群众围的严又严，
    千万人民齐声喊：
    “我要签名！……”
    会写的用笔写，
    不会写的用嘴来报名。
　
    苍白胡子张老汉，
    走进会场喊一声：
    “乡亲们，闪一闪，
    让我签上名！
    姓张，叫玉山，
    扛过长活，要过饭，
    土改以后把身翻，
    一心一意搞生产。
    帝国主义要想挑动战争
    那可不沾！（注）
    我不怕原子弹！
    拿起锄头，
    打退美国侵略者，
    我的大儿是个买卖人。
    美国飞机扔炸弹，
    大儿在石家庄遭了难，
    死了尸骨都没见。
    二儿生气上前线，
    替兄报仇到江南。
    打倒日本鬼，
    蒋贼也完蛋。
    我要和平过日子，
    我要发家搞生产。
    台湾的人民也要得解放！”
　
    张老汉把笔放下话说完。
    儿媳妇抱着孙子在后边。
    儿媳妇叫一声：
    “爹！写上我！
    给我男人报仇恨！”
    说话声音变，
    眼泪雨一般。
    “爹！记着写上你孙子的名字，
    他的名字叫胜利。”
    对！全家七口都写上，
    人多力量大如山！”
　
    千人写，万人说，
    签名簿上都写满。
    翻身农民觉悟高，
    世界大事都要管！
    “反对新战争，
    坚决保卫世界和平！”
    注：“那可不成”的意思。
