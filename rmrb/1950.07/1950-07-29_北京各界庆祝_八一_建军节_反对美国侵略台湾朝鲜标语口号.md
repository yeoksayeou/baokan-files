Title: Beijing Celebrates "August 1st" Army Day: Slogans Opposing American Aggression Against Taiwan and Korea
Author:
Date: 1950-07-29
Page: 1

Beijing Celebrates "August 1st" Army Day
Slogans Opposing American Aggression Against Taiwan and Korea

[This newspaper dispatch] The Preparatory Committee for the Beijing All-Circles Celebration of "August 1st" Army Day and Demonstration Against American Aggression Against Taiwan and Korea has released the following slogans:

	I. Celebrate the anniversary of the founding of the Chinese People's Liberation Army!
	II. Celebrate the great victory of the Chinese People's Revolutionary War!
	III. Pay tribute to the officers and soldiers of the National People's Liberation Army!
	IV. Pay tribute to the powerful Chinese People's Army!
	V. Pay tribute to the Chinese People's Air Force!
	VI. Pay tribute to the Chinese People's Navy!
	VII. Pay tribute to the Chinese People's Public Security Forces!
	VIII. Eternal glory to the martyrs who sacrificed themselves in the People's Revolutionary War!
	IX. The whole nation, army and people, fight to defend the fruits of revolutionary victory!
	X. Strengthen the unity of all democratic classes and all democratic parties!
	XI. Strive for a fundamental improvement in the economic and financial situation!
	XII. Prepare to implement land reform throughout the country!
	XIII. Thoroughly eliminate special agents and bandits, and consolidate revolutionary order!
	XIV. Thoroughly eliminate the remnants of the Chiang Kai-shek bandit gang!
	XV. The whole nation, army and people, fight to prepare for the liberation of Taiwan and Tibet!
	XVI. Oppose American aggression against Taiwan!
	XVII. The whole nation, army and people, fight to defend world peace!
	XVIII. Oppose American imperialism, which is undermining peace in the Far East!
	XIX. Oppose American imperialism, which is undermining the United Nations Charter!
	XX. Oppose American imperialism, which is preparing for a world war!
	XXI. Oppose the American attack on Korea!
	XXII. Pay tribute to the heroic Korean army and people!
	XXIII. Pay tribute to General Kim Il-sung, the leader of the Korean people!
	XXIV. Pay tribute to the Vietnamese army and people and their leader, President Ho Chi Minh, who are resisting aggression!
	XXV. Asian people unite to oppose American imperialist interference in Asian affairs!
	XXVI. Asian people unite to restore peace in the Far East and achieve national liberation!
	XXVII. Support the Soviet Union, the bulwark of world peace!
	XXVIII. Long live Marshal Stalin, the leader of the people of the world!
	XXIX. Long live the People's Republic of China!
	XXX. Long live the Chinese People's Liberation Army!
	XXXI. Long live Chairman Mao!
	XXXII. Long live Commander-in-Chief Zhu!
	XXXIII. Long live the Chinese Communist Party!
	XXXIV. Long live the great unity of the Chinese people!
	XXXV. Long live the great unity of the people of the world!

		Beijing All-Circles Celebration of "August 1st" Army Day
		Preparatory Committee for the Demonstration Against American Aggression Against Taiwan and Korea
		July 26th
