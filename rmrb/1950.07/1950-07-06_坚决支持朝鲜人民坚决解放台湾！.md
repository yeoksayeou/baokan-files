Title: Resolutely Support the Korean People's Determination to Liberate Taiwan!
Author:
Date: 1950-07-06
Page: 4

　　People's Forum

    Reader Group Response
　　Resolutely Support the Korean People's Determination to Liberate Taiwan!
To the Editor:
    The American imperialists have consistently instigated their puppet, the Syngman Rhee bandit gang, to implement a reactionary and traitorous policy in southern Korea, persecuting the people. On the 25th of this month, the American imperialists again instigated Syngman Rhee to brazenly launch a full-scale offensive against the Democratic People's Republic of Korea, creating a civil war. The American imperialists' armed forces openly participated in the war, attempting to obstruct the liberation of all of Korea. At the same time, the American imperialists ordered their Seventh Fleet to sail to Taiwan, attempting to obstruct our People's Liberation Army from liberating Taiwan. This naked act of armed aggression will only intensify the hatred of the Korean and Chinese people, accelerate the complete demise of the Syngman Rhee and Chiang Kai-shek bandit gangs, and lead to the American imperialists' own disastrous defeat. The people of China and the world see this very clearly. We cannot tolerate the American and Rhee bandit gangs' acts of aggression that undermine world peace. We resolutely support the 28 million Korean people and the people's democratic regime under the leadership of Premier Kim Il-sung. We resolutely support our People's Liberation Army in liberating Taiwan. We believe that victory will always belong to the people who oppose imperialism and traitors!
    Reading Group, Group 1, Class 1, Department 2, North China People's Revolutionary University

### 坚决支持朝鲜人民坚决解放台湾！

1950-07-06
第4版()
专栏：人民园地

    读报小组反应
　　坚决支持朝鲜人民坚决解放台湾！编辑同志：
    美帝国主义一贯主使其傀儡李承晚匪帮在朝鲜南部执行反动卖国政策，残害人民。本月二十五日美帝又唆使李承晚悍然发动对朝鲜民主主义人民共和国的全面进攻，造成内战。美帝国主义的武装公然参战，企图阻挠全朝鲜的解放。美帝国主义同时命令其第七舰队驶往台湾，妄图阻挠我人民解放军解放台湾。这种赤裸裸的武装侵略的暴行除了加强朝鲜和我国人民的愤恨，促使李承晚蒋介石等匪帮加速彻底灭亡和美帝自身的惨败之外，决不会有其他的结局。这是全中国、全世界人民看得清清楚楚的。我们不能容忍美李匪帮这种破坏世界和平的侵略罪行。我们坚决支持朝鲜二千八百万人民和金日成总理领导下的人民民主政权。我们坚决支持我国人民解放军解放台湾。我们相信胜利永远属于反帝反卖国贼的人民！
    华北人民革命大学二部一班一组读报小组
