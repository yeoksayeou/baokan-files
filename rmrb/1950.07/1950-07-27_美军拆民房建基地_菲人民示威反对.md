Title: U.S. Military Dismantles Civilian Housing to Build Base; Filipino People Demonstrate in Opposition
Author:
Date: 1950-07-27
Page: 4

　　Xinhua News Agency, July 26th: According to a United Press report from Manila on July 25th, approximately 2,500 residents near Cavite, south of Manila, held a mass demonstration to oppose the demolition of their homes to allow the U.S. Navy to build a maritime aircraft base. The report stated that the U.S. Navy, in accordance with its measures to "intensify the defense of the Philippines," decided to convert 40,000 square meters of land near Cavite into a maritime aircraft base, and ordered the poor residents of the area to move out within twenty days.

### 美军拆民房建基地  菲人民示威反对

1950-07-27
第4版()
专栏：

　　美军拆民房建基地
    菲人民示威反对
    【新华社二十六日讯】据合众社马尼拉二十五日讯：马尼拉以南甲美地附近的居民二千五百人举行群众示威，反对拆除他们的家园，让美国海军建筑海上飞机基地。该消息称：美国海军按照其“加紧菲律宾防御”的措施，决定把甲美地附近四万方公尺的地区改建为海上飞机基地。并限令附近地区的贫苦居民在二十天内搬走。
