Title: People of All Countries Unite in Support; Korean People Warmly Welcome; Confidence in Victory Further Strengthened
Author:
Date: 1950-07-18
Page: 4

　　Xinhua News Agency, Pyongyang, July 17th: The Pyongyang Radio Station broadcast that the entire Korean people warmly welcome the call by the World Federation of Trade Unions for the immediate withdrawal of the American aggressor troops from Korea. Workers in Pyongyang's enterprises held meetings one after another to discuss the call from the World Federation of Trade Unions.
    The entire staff of the Pyongyang Power Plant passed a resolution stating: The active Support Korea Week initiated by the World Federation of Trade Unions demonstrates the fighting spirit and determination of the more than 70 million workers belonging to the World Federation of Trade Unions to fight for peace. This is a week of struggle against the American imperialists (who have already carried out armed intervention in Korea), and a week of support for the just struggle of the Korean people. The Korean people's struggle has won the sympathy of all freedom-loving people in the world. Our fight against American imperialism is a just war, a war for the unification of the motherland and for people's democracy.
    Female worker Jin Aosheng said at the meeting: The support of peace-loving people all over the world for the Korean people will further strengthen the Korean people's determination to fight to the final victory.
    At meetings and gatherings held everywhere, the working people unanimously passed resolutions stating that they have made a firm resolution to work hard to meet all the needs of the front and ensure victory over the enemy.

### 世界各国人民齐心声援  朝鲜人民热烈欢迎  胜利信心更加坚强

1950-07-18
第4版()
专栏：

　　世界各国人民齐心声援
    朝鲜人民热烈欢迎
    胜利信心更加坚强
    【新华社十七日讯】塔斯社平壤十七日讯：平壤电台广播：朝鲜全体人民热烈欢迎世界工会联合会要求美国侵略者的军队立即撤出朝鲜的号召。平壤各企业的职工纷纷举行大会，讨论世界工联的号召。
    平壤电力厂全体人员通过决议称：世界工联所发起的积极声援朝鲜人民周，表现着世界工联所属七千余万工人，为和平而斗争的战斗精神与决心。这是反对美帝国主义者（他们已对朝鲜进行了武装干涉）的斗争周，是支援朝鲜人民正义的斗争周。朝鲜人民的斗争获有全世界爱好自由的人民的同情。我们反对美帝国主义者的战争是正义的战争，是为了祖国的统一、为了人民民主的战争。
    女工金敖生在大会上说：全世界爱好和平的人民对朝鲜人民的声援，将更加强朝鲜人民战斗到最后胜利的决心。
    在各处举行的大会与集会上，劳动人民一致通过了决议说：他们已立下坚定的决心，将竭力工作，以满足前线的一切需要，保证战胜敌人。
