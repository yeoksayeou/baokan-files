Author:
Date: 1950-07-29
Page: 6

Mass Current Affairs Lecture to be Held Tomorrow at the Cultural Palace

The 14th Mass Current Affairs Lecture sponsored by the Youth Service Department, in order to welcome the "August 1st" Army Day and respond to the "Movement Against American Aggression in Taiwan and Korea," is jointly organized with the Beijing Municipal Labor People's Cultural Palace. Comrade Xiao Hua, Deputy Director of the General Political Department of the Military Commission and member of the Central Committee of the Communist Youth League, is specially invited to speak on "We Will Always Stand on the Forefront of National Defense" at the Labor People's Cultural Palace at 7:00 PM on July 30th (Sunday). A film will be shown after the meeting. People from all walks of life are welcome to attend. (Free admission, groups please bring a letter of introduction.)

### 大众时事讲座明在文化宫举行

1950-07-29
第6版()
专栏：

　　大众时事讲座明在文化宫举行
    青年服务部主办的大众时事讲座第十四讲，为了迎接“八一”建军节，响应“反对美国侵略台湾、朝鲜运动”，特与北京市劳动人民文化宫联合请军委总政治部副主任、团中央委员萧华同志于七月三十日（星期日）下午七时在劳动人民文化宫主讲“我们永远站在国防最前线”。会后放映电影，欢迎各界前往听讲。（自由入场，团体请带介绍信。）
