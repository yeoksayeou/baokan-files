Title: International Conference of Textile and Garment Workers' Unions Condemns US Imperialism's Armed Intervention in Korea
Author:
Date: 1950-07-13
Page: 4
　　Xinhua News Agency, 11th: A comprehensive TASS dispatch reports: The Administrative Committee of the International Textile and Garment Workers' Union (an industrial department of the World Federation of Trade Unions) recently held an expanded meeting in Lodz, Poland, to discuss the current situation and tasks of textile and garment workers, and passed a resolution condemning the aggressive actions of US imperialism in its armed intervention in Korea.

　　The meeting heard a report by World Federation of Trade Unions Secretary Gilbert and passed a resolution stating that striving for peace is the primary task of textile and garment unions in all countries, calling on textile and garment workers to intensify the activities of peace committees in all enterprises. After the meeting, textile workers in Lodz held a mass rally to express solidarity with the delegates attending the meeting.

### 纺织缝纫工人工会国际会议  抨击美帝武装干涉朝鲜

1950-07-13
第4版()
专栏：

　　纺织缝纫工人工会国际会议
    抨击美帝武装干涉朝鲜
    【新华社十一日讯】综合塔斯社消息：纺织缝纫工人工会国际（世界工联产业部之一）的行政委员会，最近曾在波兰罗兹城举行扩大会议，讨论纺织与缝纫工人目前的情况和任务，并通过决议，抨击美国帝国主义武装干涉朝鲜的侵略行为。
    会议听取了世界工联书记吉伯特的报告，并通过决议，指明争取和平是各国纺织和缝纫业工会的首要任务，号召纺织与缝纫工人在各企业内加紧进行和平委员会的活动。会议结束后，罗兹纺织工人曾举行群众大会，与参加会议的代表们表示联欢。
