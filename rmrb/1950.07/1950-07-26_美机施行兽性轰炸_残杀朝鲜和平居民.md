Title: American Aircraft Carry Out Bestial Bombing, Slaughtering Peaceful Korean Residents
Author:
Date: 1950-07-26
Page: 4

　　Xinhua News Agency, Pyongyang, July 24th: A communiqué issued on the evening of July 23rd by the General Headquarters of the Korean People's Democratic Republic People's Army stated: The People's Army continues its offensive, inflicting heavy blows on the American army on all fronts.

　　The American air force barbarously and savagely bombs the peaceful towns and villages in the north of the Republic and the liberated south. On July 23rd, American aircraft raided the Pyongyang area and nearby villages, dropping hundreds of bombs ranging from 500 to 1,000 kilograms. As a result of this large-scale bombing, many houses and cultural institutions were destroyed, and residents were killed and wounded.

### 美机施行兽性轰炸  残杀朝鲜和平居民

1950-07-26
第4版()
专栏：

　　美机施行兽性轰炸  残杀朝鲜和平居民
    【新华社二十四日讯】塔斯社平壤二十四日讯：朝鲜民主主义人民共和国人民军总司令部七月二十三日晚发表公报称：人民军各部队继续进行攻势，在各前线予美军以痛击。
    美国空军残暴野蛮地轰炸共和国北部以及南部解放了的和平城镇与村庄。七月二十三日，美机空袭平壤地区及附近各村庄，投掷五百到一千公斤的炸弹数百枚。由于这次大规模的轰炸，结果许多房舍和文化机关被毁，居民亦有死伤。
