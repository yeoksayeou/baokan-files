Title: Poland Completes 106% of its First Half Economic Plan

Author:

Date: 1950-07-21
Page: 4

TASS, Warsaw, July 17th: The Polish State Economic Planning Commission recently announced that Poland has completed 106% of its national economic plan for the first half of 1950. Production of electricity, coal, lignite, coking coal, oil, steel, and rolled metals all exceeded planned targets. The spring sowing plan was completed by 103%. The area sown in spring increased by 688,000 hectares compared to last year. The retail trade volume of state-owned and cooperative enterprises increased by 75% compared to the second quarter of last year, totaling 325 billion zlotys (Polish currency). The supply to the people has improved.

### 波上半年经济计划完成百分之一○六

1950-07-21
第4版()
专栏：

　　波上半年经济计划完成百分之一○六
    【新华社二十日讯】塔斯社华沙十七日讯：波兰国家经济计划委员会顷宣布：波兰一九五○年上半年的国民经济计划已完成了百分之一○六。电力、煤、褐煤、焦煤、石油、钢、辗金属等，均超过了生产计划。春季播种计划完成了百分之一○三。春季播种的面积较去年增加了六十八万八千公顷。国家与合作社的零售贸易额较去年第二季增加了百分之七十五，总数达三千二百五十亿兹罗提（波币）。对人民的供应已有改善。
