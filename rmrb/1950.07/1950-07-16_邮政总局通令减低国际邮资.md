Title: Post Office General Administration Issues Circular to Reduce International Postage
Author:
Date: 1950-07-16
Page: 6

The Post Office General Administration of the Ministry of Posts and Telecommunications of the Central People's Government has issued a circular to post offices in all regions (excluding the Northeast) to reduce international postage and international airmail postage as follows, effective July 15th:

　　(1) Letters: 2,800 yuan for the first 20 grams, 1,700 yuan for each additional 20 grams.
　　(2) Postcards: 1,700 yuan for a single card, 3,400 yuan for a double card.
　　(3) Trade contracts, printed matter, samples: 1,100 yuan for the first 50 grams, 600 yuan for each additional 50 grams, with a minimum charge of 2,800 yuan for trade contracts.
　　(4) Literature for the blind: 300 yuan per kilogram.
　　(5) Small packets (limited to acceptance and dispatch by Shanghai, Tianjin, and Guangzhou post offices, subject to customs inspection and release): 1,100 yuan per 50 grams, with a minimum charge of 5,700 yuan; delivery fee for small packets is 4,700 yuan per item; inspection fee for small packets is 4,700 yuan per item.
　　(6) Registration fee: 4,700 yuan per item.
　　(7) Priority fee: 7,100 yuan per item.
　　(8) Insurance fee (limited to insured letters sent to the Soviet Union): 6,000 yuan per 300 Gold Francs or fraction thereof.
　　(9) Return receipt: 3,500 yuan per item.
　　(10) Inquiry: 4,700 yuan per item.
　　(11) Withdrawal and change of address: 4,700 yuan per item.

International Airmail Postage: An additional 7,200 yuan per 10 grams for mail sent to Asian countries, and an additional 11,700 yuan per 10 grams for mail sent to other continents (the entire territory of the Soviet Union is charged according to this rate); international airmail letters, including ordinary postage, are 6,500 yuan per item for mail sent to Asian countries, and 8,500 yuan per item for mail sent to the entire territory of the Soviet Union and Eastern European New Democratic countries.

### 邮政总局通令减低国际邮资

1950-07-16
第6版()
专栏：

　　邮政总局通令减低国际邮资
    中央人民政府邮电部邮政总局通令各地邮局（东北除外）自七月十五日起将国际邮资及国际航空邮资减低如下：（一）信函：起重二十公分二千八百元，续重每二十公分一千七百元。（二）明信片：单一千七百元，双三千四百元。（三）贸易契、印刷物、货样：起重五十公分一千一百元，续重每五十公分六百元，贸易契起算数二千八百元。（四）瞽者文件：每重一公斤仍为三百元。（五）小包邮件（仅限上海、天津、广州邮局收寄，以经由海关查验放行者为限）：每重五十公分一千一百元，起算数五千七百元，小包投递费每件四千七百元，小包验阅费每件四千七百元。（六）挂号费：每件四千七百元。（七）平快费：每件七千一百元。（八）保价费（以寄苏联之保价信函为限）：每三百金佛郎或其畸零六千元。（九）回执每件三千五百元。（十）查询每件四千七百元。（十一）撤回及更改地址每件四千七百元。国际航空邮费：寄往亚洲各国每重十公分加收七千二百元，寄往其他各洲每重十公分加收一万一千七百元（苏联全境均按此例收费）；国际航空邮简连普通邮资在内，寄往亚洲各国每件六千五百元，寄往苏联全境及东欧新民主主义国家每件八千五百元。
