Title: Our Army Liberated Beijishan Island Outside Oujiangkou in Southern Zhejiang
Author:
Date: 1950-07-23
Page: 1

　　Xinhua News Agency, Nanjing, 22nd. A unit of the People's Liberation Army's Third Field Army liberated Beijishan Island outside Oujiangkou in southern Zhejiang on the 15th of this month, annihilating all the remaining bandits entrenched on the island. According to preliminary statistics, more than 500 bandits, including bandit leaders Li Dongfan and Li Dongming, were captured. The results of the battle are still being verified.

### 浙江南部瓯江口外我军解放北麂山岛

1950-07-23
第1版()
专栏：

　　浙江南部瓯江口外我军解放北麂山岛
    【新华社南京二十二日电】人民解放军第三野战军一部于本月十五日解放浙江南部瓯江口外的北麂山岛，将盘踞该岛的残匪全部歼灭。据初步统计，计俘匪首李东藩、李东明等以下五百余人，战果正清查中。
