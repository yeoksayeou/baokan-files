Title: US Army Invading Korea Begins Attacking Korean People's Army; First Battle Reveals Panic and Embarrassment; Opens Fire for 17 and a Half Hours on Thirty Guerrillas
Author:
Date: 1950-07-06
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, 5th. According to Tokyo sources, the US army of armed interventionists invading southern Korea began attacking the Korean People's Army on July 3rd. The first battle demonstrated how the US armed interventionists fight. This battle took place twenty miles south of Suwon on the morning of the 3rd. After the US military encountered thirty Korean People's guerrillas, they opened fire on them. The guerrillas only threw some hand grenades, while the US military panicked and "fired continuously from 11:30 AM on Monday (July 3rd) until 5:00 AM on Tuesday (4th)" (United Press dispatch from the US military defense zone in Korea, 4th). On the second day after the US military joined the war (4th), MacArthur issued a "special statement" in Tokyo, stating: "I hope that American citizens and members of the American armed forces who may fall into the hands of North Korea at any time" will be treated according to "humanitarian principles."

### 侵入朝鲜的美国陆军开始进犯朝鲜人民军  第一次战斗就表现了慌张丑态  遇到三十名游击队就开炮十七个半钟头

1950-07-06
第1版()
专栏：

　　侵入朝鲜的美国陆军开始进犯朝鲜人民军
    第一次战斗就表现了慌张丑态
    遇到三十名游击队就开炮十七个半钟头
    【新华社五日讯】据东京消息，侵入朝鲜南部的美国武装干涉者的陆军部队已于七月三日开始向朝鲜人民军进犯。第一次战斗表明了美国武装干涉者是怎样作战的。这次战斗发生在水原以南二十英里的地方，时间是三日上午。美军遇到了朝鲜人民游击队三十名后便向他们开炮。游击队只投掷了一些手榴弹，而美军则慌张地“从星期一（即三日）上午十一时三十分起，一直开炮到星期二（四日）上午五时止”（合众社朝鲜美军防区四日电）。就在美军参战后的第二天（四日），麦克阿瑟就在东京发表了一项“特别声明”，内称：“我希望对于可能在任何时候落入北朝鲜手中的美国国民和美国武装部队的人员”，能被按照“人道的原则处理”。
