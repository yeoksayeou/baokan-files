Title: People of the World Support the Korean People; International Democratic Women's Federation Responds to "Week of Support for the Korean People" and Calls on Women of All Countries to Participate in Demonstrations

Author:

Date: 1950-07-11
Page: 1

　　Xinhua News Agency, Paris, July 9th: The International Democratic Women's Federation has announced its full support for the "Worldwide Week of Active Support for the Korean People" initiated by the World Federation of Trade Unions, starting from July 10th.

　　The Federation calls upon: "Women's organizations in all countries, the wives of all workers, and all working women, to participate in mass demonstrations on a large scale. Fight to force the immediate withdrawal of American troops from Korea!"

### 世界各国人民声援朝鲜人民  国际妇联响应“支援朝鲜人民周”  号召各国妇女参加示威运动

1950-07-11
第1版()
专栏：

　　世界各国人民声援朝鲜人民
    国际妇联响应“支援朝鲜人民周”
    号召各国妇女参加示威运动
    【新华社十日讯】塔斯社巴黎九日讯：国际民主妇女联合会顷宣布全力支持世界工会联合会所发起的自七月十日起的“全世界积极支援朝鲜人民周”。
    该会号召：“世界各国妇女团体，一切工人的妻子和一切劳动妇女们，大规模地参加群众示威运动。为迫使美军立刻自朝鲜撤退而奋斗”。
