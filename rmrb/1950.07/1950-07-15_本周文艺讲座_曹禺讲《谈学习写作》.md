Title: This Week's Literary Lecture: Cao Yu on "Talking About Learning to Write"
Author:
Date: 1950-07-15
Page: 6

　　This Week's Literary Lecture
    Cao Yu on "Talking About Learning to Write"
    The Mass Literature and Art Creation and Research Association and the Youth Service Department are jointly hosting this week's literary lecture, and have invited Comrade Cao Yu to be the main speaker. The topic is: "Talking About Learning to Write". After the meeting, Mr. Cai Liangui and Yao Zhenzhong will perform the Xihe Dagu "Shi Bulan Drives a Cart". The address is the Youth Palace. The time is 9:30 am on Sunday. Admission by long-term or temporary ticket. Temporary tickets can be collected from the Youth Service Department on Saturday. (Group tickets can be collected on Friday)

### 本周文艺讲座  曹禺讲《谈学习写作》

1950-07-15
第6版()
专栏：广播节目

　　本周文艺讲座
    曹禺讲《谈学习写作》
    大众文艺创研会与青年服务部合办的星期文艺讲座本周聘请曹禺同志主讲，讲题：《谈学习写作》。会后由蔡连贵、姚振忠先生表演西河大鼓《石不烂赶车》。地址在青年宫。时间星期日上午九时半。凭长期券临时券入场。临时券可于星期六至青年服务部领取。（团体票可于星期五往领）
