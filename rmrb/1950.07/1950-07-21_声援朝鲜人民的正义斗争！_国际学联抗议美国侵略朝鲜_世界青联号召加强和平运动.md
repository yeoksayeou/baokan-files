Title: Support the Just Struggle of the Korean People! International Student Union Protests American Aggression Against Korea. World Federation of Democratic Youth Calls for Strengthening the Peace Movement
Author:
Date: 1950-07-21
Page: 4

　　Xinhua News Agency, Twentieth: According to a TASS report from Prague on the 19th: The Secretariat of the International Student Union has issued a statement calling on all student organizations and students around the world to resolutely protest against American imperialism's aggression against Korea and to do everything possible to stop this criminal act.
　　The statement also urged all students to intensify the struggle against the instigators of new wars, actively participate in the movement to collect signatures for the Stockholm Appeal, and expand the scope of preparations for the Second World Peace Congress.
　　The Secretariat of the International Student Union also sent a telegram to the democratic youth and students of Korea, expressing support for the heroic struggle of the Korean people against the armed aggression of American imperialism.
　　The Secretariat of the International Student Union also sent telegrams to US President Truman and UN Secretary-General Trygve Lie, protesting their violation of the UN Charter and demanding that they stop American aggression against Korea.
　　Xinhua News Agency, Twentieth: TASS, Paris, 19th: The World Federation of Democratic Youth has sent a telegram to all its affiliated youth organizations, calling on them to expand and intensify the movement to win peace in light of recent international events, especially the Korean event.
　　The World Federation of Democratic Youth calls on all democratic youth organizations to resolutely demand an end to American aggression against Korea, demand the withdrawal of American troops from Korea, and launch movements to support Korean youth in various places.
　　The telegram declares: "At the same time, we must intensify the movement to demand the prohibition of atomic weapons, which many crazy warmongers have suggested using in Korea and elsewhere. ... We must do everything in our power to mobilize middle-class and peasant youth to sign the peace declaration." The World Federation of Democratic Youth finally called on all its affiliated youth organizations to immediately begin preparations for the Second World Peace Congress.

### 声援朝鲜人民的正义斗争！  国际学联抗议美国侵略朝鲜  世界青联号召加强和平运动

1950-07-21
第4版()
专栏：

　　声援朝鲜人民的正义斗争！
    国际学联抗议美国侵略朝鲜
    世界青联号召加强和平运动
    【新华社二十日讯】据塔斯社布拉格十九日讯：国际学生联合会秘书处顷发表声明，号召全世界一切学生团体和学生，坚决抗议美国帝国主义侵略朝鲜，并竭力制止此种犯罪行为。
    该声明同时敦促所有学生加紧进行反对新战争挑拨者的斗争，积极参加为斯德哥尔摩宣言征集签名的运动，并扩大第二届世界拥护和平大会筹备工作的范围。
    国际学联秘书处同时致电朝鲜的民主青年与学生，对朝鲜人民反对美国帝国主义武装侵略的英勇斗争表示支援。
    国际学联秘书处也曾致电美国总统杜鲁门和联合国秘书长赖伊，对于他们违背联合国宪章一事提出抗议，并且要求他们停止美国对朝鲜的侵略。
    【新华社二十日讯】塔斯社巴黎十九日讯：世界民主青年联盟顷致电所属一切青年组织，号召它们结合最近的国际事件，特别是朝鲜事件，来扩大并加紧进行争取和平的运动。
    世界民主青联号召所有民主青年组织：坚决要求制止美国侵略朝鲜的行动，要求美军撤出朝鲜，并在各地发动支援朝鲜青年的运动。
    电文宣称：“与此同时，我们要加紧进行要求禁止原子武器的运动，许多疯狂的战争贩子竟已建议在朝鲜等地使用原子武器了。……我们必须竭尽一切力量来发动中等阶级及农民青年在和平宣言上签名。”世界民主青联最后号召所属一切青年组织立即开始第二届世界拥护和平大会的筹备工作。
