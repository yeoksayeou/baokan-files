Title: US Gross National Product Decreased Last Year; National Income Also Decreased Significantly
Author:
Date: 1950-07-27
Page: 4

　　US Gross National Product Decreased Last Year
    National Income Also Decreased Significantly
    [Xinhua News Agency, July 26th dispatch from New York by Telegraph] According to figures released by the US Department of Commerce on the 23rd, the US national income and gross national product decreased significantly last year compared to the previous year. The US gross national product last year was $255.5 billion, $3.5 billion less than in 1948. Last year's national income decreased from $223.5 billion in 1948 to $217 billion, a decrease of $6.5 billion.

### 美去年生产总值减少  国民收入也下降很多

1950-07-27
第4版()
专栏：

　　美去年生产总值减少
    国民收入也下降很多
    【新华社二十六日讯】电通社纽约讯：美国去年的国民收入和生产总值比前年下降很多。根据美国商务部二十三日所发表的数字，美国去年的生产总值为二千五百五十五亿美元，比一九四八年少三十五亿美元。去年的国民收入，已从一九四八年的二千二百三十五亿美元降至二千一百七十亿美元，减少了六十五亿美元。
