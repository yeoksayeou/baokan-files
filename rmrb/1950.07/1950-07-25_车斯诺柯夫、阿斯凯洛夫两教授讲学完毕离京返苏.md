Title: Professors Chesnokov and Askeyev Complete Lectures and Return to Soviet Union from Beijing
Author:
Date: 1950-07-25
Page: 3

Xinhua News Agency, July 21st: Professor Chesnokov, Deputy Director of the Institute of Philosophy of the Soviet Academy of Sciences and Editor-in-Chief of the Soviet journal "Problems of Philosophy," and Professor Askeyev, Deputy Director of the Department of Law and Director of the Law Lecture at the Moscow State University, who were invited to lecture in China, left Beijing for their home country on the morning of the 21st. The two professors arrived in Beijing on May 28th and gave academic lectures in Beijing, Jinan, Shanghai, Hangzhou, Guangzhou, Changsha, Wuhan, Xi'an, Shenyang and other places. Professor Chesnokov gave a total of 25 lectures, with approximately 38,200 listeners; he participated in eight symposiums, with 905 participants. Professor Askeyev gave a total of 28 lectures, with approximately 44,200 listeners; he participated in eight symposiums, with 423 participants. The two professors were warmly welcomed by all circles in the local areas they visited. Local government agencies and organizations presented the two professors with banners, gifts, and other items. To thank the two professors for their great contribution to Sino-Soviet cultural exchange, the Political and Legal Committee of the Government Administration Council and the Sino-Soviet Friendship Association jointly hosted a farewell banquet on the evening of the 19th. More than 40 people attended, including Dong Biwu, Guo Moruo, and Shen Yanbing. Mr. Byev, Counselor of the Soviet Embassy, and Mr. Seriyekine, the representative of the Soviet Foreign Cultural Association in China, were also invited. On the evening of the 20th, the representative of the Soviet Foreign Cultural Association in China also hosted a farewell banquet for the two professors.

### 车斯诺柯夫、阿斯凯洛夫两教授讲学完毕离京返苏

1950-07-25
第3版()
专栏：

　　车斯诺柯夫、阿斯凯洛夫两教授讲学完毕离京返苏
    【新华社二十一日讯】应邀来我国讲学的苏联科学院哲学研究院副院长、苏联《哲学问题》杂志总编辑车斯诺柯夫教授和苏联国立莫斯科大学法学系副主任、法学讲座主任阿斯凯洛夫教授已于二十一日上午离京返国。两教授于五月二十八日抵京，先后在北京、济南、上海、杭州、广州、长沙、武汉、西安、沈阳等地作学术讲演。车斯诺柯夫教授共讲学二十五次，听众约三万八千二百人；参加座谈会八次，与会者九百零五人。阿斯凯洛夫教授共讲学二十八次，听众约四万四千二百人；参加座谈会八次，与会者四百二十三人。两教授每到一地，都受到当地各界热烈欢迎。各地机关、团体赠送两教授锦旗、礼品等多种。为了感谢两位教授此次对中苏文化交流的伟大贡献，政务院政治法律委员会及中苏友好协会总会曾于十九日晚联合设宴欢送。到会的有董必武、郭沫若、沈雁冰等四十余人。苏联大使馆白夫参事和北京苏联对外文化协会驻我国代表谢烈金也应邀参加。二十日晚，苏联对外文化协会驻我国代表亦设宴欢送两教授。
