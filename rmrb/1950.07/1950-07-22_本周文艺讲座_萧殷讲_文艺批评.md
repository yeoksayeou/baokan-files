Author:
Date: 1950-07-22
Page: 6

　　This Week's Literary and Art Lecture
    Xiao Yin on "Literary Criticism"
    The Mass Literature Creation and Research Association and the Youth Service Department are jointly hosting this week's Literary and Art Lecture. This week, Xiao Yin is invited to speak on the topic of "Literary Criticism." After the lecture, Wei Changlin and Luo Shihai will perform Leting Drum and sing two excerpts of "New Things and New Ways." Venue: Youth Palace, Time: Sunday (23rd) at 9:30 AM. Admission by long-term ticket or temporary ticket. Temporary tickets can be obtained from the Youth Service Department from Saturday.

### 本周文艺讲座  萧殷讲“文艺批评”

1950-07-22
第6版()
专栏：

　　本周文艺讲座
    萧殷讲“文艺批评”
    大众文艺创研会与青年服务部联合主办的星期文艺讲座，本周聘请萧殷主讲，讲题是“文艺批评”。会后由魏长林、罗世海表演乐亭大鼓，接唱二段“新事新办”。会址：青年宫，时间：礼拜日（二十三日）上午九时半。凭长期券或临时券入场，临时券可于星期六至青年服务部领取。
