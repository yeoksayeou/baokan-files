Title: China Electricity Workers' Union Established, First Congress Concluded the Day Before Yesterday
Author:
Date: 1950-07-24
Page: 2

　　China Electricity Workers' Union Established
    First Congress Concluded the Day Before Yesterday
    [Xinhua News Agency, 22nd] Following the establishment of industrial unions for railways, posts and telecommunications, ordnance, and transport, the unified organization of Chinese electricity workers – the China Electricity Workers' Union – who control the power of the nation's industrial construction, was established at the First Congress of the China Electricity Workers' Union held in Beijing from July 7th to 22nd.

    This congress exchanged experiences of local electricity workers' union work, heard the report by Bao Guobao, Director of the Electricity Management Bureau of the Central Fuel Industry Ministry, on the electricity production tasks for the second half of 1950, and the report by Cai Shubin, Director of the Electricity Workers' Union Work Committee of the All-China Federation of Trade Unions, on the summary of past electricity workers' union work and the future work guidelines and tasks. The congress focused on discussing how the electricity workers' union should improve grassroots organizations to focus on production, and adopted the draft constitution of the China Electricity Workers' Union, the draft outline of collective contracts, the draft resolution on carrying out spare-time education, and the draft interim measures on national electricity workers' disease and disability subsidies. Wang Jiazhen, Liu Dezhen and other thirty-five people were elected as members of the National Committee of the China Electricity Workers' Union, Li Yantai, Tao Ruiping and other eleven people were elected as alternate members, and Feng Dawen, Yang Lin and other seven people were elected as members of the Finance Review Committee, to be submitted to the Standing Committee of the All-China Federation of Trade Unions for approval.

    During the meeting, Vice Chairman of the Central People's Government Zhu De, Minister of Fuel Industry Chen Yu, Vice Chairman of the All-China Federation of Trade Unions Li Lisan, and Vice Chairman Liu Ningyi all attended and gave reports to the congress. In his report, Vice Chairman Zhu De analyzed the current political situation and encouraged everyone to strive to increase production, improve quality, and reduce costs. In his report, Minister Chen Yu called on electricity workers, staff, technicians, and administrative management personnel across the country to unite more closely, improve their cultural and technical levels, and conscientiously implement the overhaul system, striving to improve power generation efficiency and ensure safe power generation. In his report, Vice Chairman of the All-China Federation of Trade Unions Li Lisan called on electricity workers across the country to widely launch production competitions, and to make production competitions the mode of labor and mode of work for electricity workers. In his report, Vice Chairman Liu Ningyi also emphasized that the task of the electricity workers' union in the future is to improve grassroots organizations, focus on production, and eliminate accidents caused by negligence. He also called on workers in various regions to continuously report to Chairman Mao with victories in production.

    During the meeting, the representatives visited power plants in Tianjin, Beijing, Tangshan and other places to study advanced production and work experiences.

    A total of one hundred and forty-three representatives attended this meeting, including labor heroes, model workers, plant protection heroes, workers, engineers, administrative management personnel and trade union workers from electricity companies and power plants across the country.

### 中国电业工会成立  第一次代表大会前日闭幕

1950-07-24
第2版()
专栏：

　　中国电业工会成立
    第一次代表大会前日闭幕
    【新华社二十二日讯】继铁路、邮电、兵工、搬运等产业工会成立之后，掌握国家工业建设的动力——电力的中国电业工人的统一组织——中国电业工会已于七月七日至二十二日在北京举行的中国电业工会第一次代表大会上成立。
    这个代表大会交流了各地电业工会工作的经验、听取了中央燃料工业部电业管理总局鲍国宝局长关于一九五○年下半年电业生产任务的报告，全国总工会电业工会工作委员会主任蔡书彬关于过去电业工会工作的总结及今后工作方针任务的报告，着重讨论了电业工会如何健全基层组织面向生产的问题，并通过了中国电业工会组织章程草案、集体合同纲要草案、开展业余教育的决议草案以及关于全国电业职工疾病伤残补助暂行办法草案。选出了王家珍、刘德珍等三十五人为中国电业工会全国委员会委员，李彦泰、陶瑞平等十一人为候补委员，冯大文、杨林等七人为经费审查委员会委员，提请全国总工会常务委员会批准。
    会议期间，中央人民政府朱德副主席、燃料工业部陈郁部长、全国总工会李立三副主席、刘宁一副主席均出席向大会作报告。朱德副主席在报告中分析了目前的政治形势，勉励大家努力增加产量，提高质量，降低成本。陈郁部长在报告中号召全国电业工人、职员、技术人员与行政管理人员更紧密地团结起来，提高文化水平和技术水平，认真执行检修制度，为提高发电效率，保证安全发电而奋斗。中华全国总工会李立三副主席报告中号召全国电业职工普遍开展生产竞赛，并使生产竞赛成为电业职工的劳动方式与工作方式。刘宁一副主席在报告中也着重说明了今后电业工会的任务是健全基层组织，面向生产，消灭责任事故。他并号召今后各地职工以生产上的胜利成果不断向毛主席作报告。
    会议期间，代表们曾分赴天津、北京、唐山等地的电厂参观，研究先进的生产工作经验。
    出席此次会议的代表计有全国各地电业公司、发电厂的劳动英雄、模范工作者、护厂功臣、工人、工程师、行政管理人员和工会工作者共一百四十三人。
