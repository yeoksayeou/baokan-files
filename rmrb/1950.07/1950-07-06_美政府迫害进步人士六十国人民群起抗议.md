Title: US Government Persecutes Progressives, People of Sixty Countries Protest

Author:

Date: 1950-07-06
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, New York, June 29th (TASS): World opinion firmly condemns the US government's persecution of progressives. The Joint Anti-Fascist Refugee Committee issued a statement stating that in the past two weeks, Truman has received protests from sixty countries regarding the imprisonment of eleven leaders of the organization. According to the Mexico City publication "People's Daily," the Latin American Confederation of Labor has urged all trade unions and other organizations throughout Latin America to send telegrams to Truman demanding the release of anti-fascist political prisoners. A mass meeting was held in Mexico City to defend Dr. Bardski, Chairman of the Joint Anti-Fascist Refugee Committee, the famous writer Fast, and other imprisoned leaders of the committee, as well as Hollywood writers Lawson and Trumbo. Speakers at the meeting included the famous Chilean poet Neruda and the Mexican artist Serivera.

### 美政府迫害进步人士六十国人民群起抗议

1950-07-06
第4版()
专栏：

　　美政府迫害进步人士六十国人民群起抗议
    【新华社二日讯】塔斯社纽约六月二十九日讯：全世界舆论坚决谴责美国政府迫害进步人士。反法西斯难民联合委员会今发表声明称：在过去两周内，杜鲁门接到来自六十个国家对于该组织十一位领袖被监禁一事的抗议。据墨西哥城出版的《人民报》消息：拉丁美洲总工会敦促全拉丁美洲的各工会和其他团体致电杜鲁门，要求释放反法西斯的政治犯。墨西哥城曾举行保卫反法西斯难民联合委员会主席巴尔斯基博士、名作家法斯特和其他被监禁的该委员会的领袖以及好莱坞的作家劳森和特鲁波的群众大会。会上演说者有智利著名诗人聂鲁达、墨西哥艺术家谢里维拉等人。
