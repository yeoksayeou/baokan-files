Title: The Sino-Soviet Friendship Association Holds Lecture to Celebrate Soviet Sports Day
Author: Chen Yingnan
Date: 1950-07-18
Page: 6

　　The Sino-Soviet Friendship Association Holds Lecture to Celebrate Soviet Sports Day

　　Today (July 18th) is Soviet Sports Day. The Beijing Sino-Soviet Friendship Association and the Soviet Foreign Cultural Association have decided to hold a lecture at 7:30 PM today (July 18th) at 19 Nanheyan Street to celebrate this festival. Comrade Gorchgladze will lecture on "Soviet Sports Day." Anyone with an admission ticket or a "Soviet Introduction" long-term listening pass can attend the lecture at that time.

　　If any branch of the Friendship Association wishes to attend the lecture, they can obtain admission tickets by presenting a letter of introduction from the branch to the association at 2 Yuhe Bridge.
                                                (Chen Yingnan)

### 苏对外文协举行讲演会庆祝苏联体育节
陈应楠
1950-07-18
第6版()
专栏：

　　苏对外文协举行讲演会庆祝苏联体育节
    今日（七月十八日）是苏联体育节。北京市中苏友好协会与苏联对外文化协会为庆祝这个节日，决定今日（十八日）下午七时半在南河沿十九号举行讲演会。由戈尔节拉则讲“苏联体育节”。凡持有入场券或“苏联介绍”长期听讲证者，届时均可前往听讲。
    友协各级支会，如有前往听讲者，可持支会介绍信，往御河桥二号该会索取入场券。
                                                （陈应楠）
