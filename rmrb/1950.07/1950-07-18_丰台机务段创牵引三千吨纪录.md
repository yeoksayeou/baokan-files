Title: Fengtai Locomotive Depot Creates 3,000-Ton Traction Record
Author:
Date: 1950-07-18
Page: 2

　　Fengtai Locomotive Depot Creates 3,000-Ton Traction Record
　　[Our Newspaper] The Fengtai Locomotive Depot's ㄇㄎ1-1751 locomotive successfully hauled 3,000 tons on a trial run between Fengtai and Tianjin on June 24, breaking the large locomotive traction record. The entire train consisted of 73 cars including the locomotive. It departed Fengtai Station at 13:10 that day and arrived safely at Tianjin North Station at 16:18, taking two hours and fifty-eight minutes en route, with an average technical speed of 41.4 kilometers. The successful 3,000-ton trial traction of this locomotive exceeded the bureau-set traction standard by 983.9 tons and exceeded the length by 26.4 cars. This has saved the people's railway a huge amount of manpower and material resources.
　　　　　　　　　　(People's Railway News)

### 丰台机务段创牵引三千吨纪录

1950-07-18
第2版()
专栏：

　　丰台机务段创牵引三千吨纪录
    【本报讯】丰台机务段冂丂1一七五一号机车，六月二十四日在丰台、天津间牵引三千吨试运成功，打破大型机车牵引纪录。全列车连车头共七十三辆，当日十三点十分开出丰台站，十六点十八分安抵天津北站，中途计用去了两点五十八分，技术速度平均四十一点四公里。该机车三千吨试验牵引成功，超过局定牵引标准九百八十三点九吨，超长二十六点四辆。给人民铁路节省了巨大的人力和物力。
                        （人民铁道报）
