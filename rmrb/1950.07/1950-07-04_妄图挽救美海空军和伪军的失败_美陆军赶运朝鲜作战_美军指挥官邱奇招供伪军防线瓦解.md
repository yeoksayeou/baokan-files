Title: Attempting to Salvage the Failure of the U.S. Air and Naval Forces and the Puppet Army: The U.S. Army Rushes to Korea for Combat; U.S. Military Commander Church Confesses the Collapse of the Puppet Army's Defense Line
Author:
Date: 1950-07-04
Page: 4

　　Xinhua News Agency, July 3rd: The United States is rushing regular army troops to South Korea in an attempt to salvage the failure of the U.S. air and naval forces and a portion of its ground troops fighting in South Korea, as well as the collapse of the South Korean puppet army. According to a United Press report on July 1st: "A unit of the U.S. Army was airlifted to a base in Korea today and immediately moved to the front lines. Within the next three days, a division of the U.S. Army, including 15,000 officers and soldiers, will arrive in Korea." In addition, according to a United Press dispatch from Tokyo on July 3rd: MacArthur Headquarters announced that it has appointed Major General Dean, commander of the U.S. 24th Infantry Division, as the commander-in-chief of U.S. forces in Korea.

　　Under the heroic blows of the Korean People's Army, the South Korean puppet army has completely disintegrated. According to a United Press report on July 1st: Brigadier General Church, commander of the U.S. advance troops who fled in panic from Suwon to Daejeon, stated: "We had to retreat. The South Koreans' defense line has collapsed."

### 妄图挽救美海空军和伪军的失败  美陆军赶运朝鲜作战  美军指挥官邱奇招供伪军防线瓦解

1950-07-04
第4版()
专栏：

　　妄图挽救美海空军和伪军的失败
    美陆军赶运朝鲜作战
    美军指挥官邱奇招供伪军防线瓦解
    【新华社三日讯】美国的陆军正规部队赶运南朝鲜，以挽救在南朝鲜作战的美国海空军及一部分地面部队的失败和南朝鲜傀儡军的崩溃。据合众社七月一日报道：“美国陆军部队一部今日空运至朝鲜某基地，并随即开往前线。在此后三日内，运抵朝鲜的美国陆军将有一个师，包括官兵一万五千名”。另据合众社东京三日电称：麦克阿瑟总部宣布已任命美军第二十四师师长迪安少将为朝鲜美军总司令。
    在朝鲜人民军的英勇打击下，南朝鲜傀儡军已溃不成军。据合众社一日电称：自水原张慌逃抵大田的美军先头部队指挥官邱奇准将称：“我们不得不撤退。南朝鲜人的防线已瓦解。”
