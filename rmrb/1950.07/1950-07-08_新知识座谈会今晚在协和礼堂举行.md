Title: New Knowledge Symposium to be Held Tonight at Xiehe Auditorium
Author:
Date: 1950-07-08
Page: 6

The 77th gathering of the New Knowledge Symposium, sponsored by the Beijing Branch of the China Democratic National Construction Association, will be held at 7:30 PM today at the Xiehe Auditorium on Dongdan Santiao. Vice Premier Huang Yanpei of the Government Administration Council has been specially invited to speak on the Korean issue. Additionally, Mr. Hu Ziying, Deputy Secretary-General of the Central Financial and Economic Committee and Secretary-General of the Shanghai Federation of Industry and Commerce, will report on the industrial and commercial situation in Shanghai over the past year. In addition to members entering with their admission tickets, anyone wishing to attend can obtain an admission ticket through an introduction from a member or their employing organization.

### 新知识座谈会今晚在协和礼堂举行

1950-07-08
第6版()
专栏：

　　新知识座谈会今晚在协和礼堂举行
    民主建国会北京市分会主办的新知识座谈会第七十七次集会，订于今日下午七时半在东单三条协和礼堂举行，这次特邀请政务院黄炎培副总理主讲朝鲜问题，同时又请中央财经委员会副秘书长兼上海工商联秘书长胡子婴先生报告上海一年来的工商业情况。除会友凭听讲证入场外，凡愿参加听讲者可由会友或其服务单位介绍来会洽领入场证。
