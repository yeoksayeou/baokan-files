Title: French General Confederation of Labor Calls for Support for the Korean People
Author:
Date: 1950-07-10
Page: 1

　　French General Confederation of Labor Calls for Support for the Korean People
　　[Xinhua News Agency, eighth dispatch from Paris] TASS Paris dispatch: The Executive Bureau of the French General Confederation of Labor announced its active response to the call of the World Federation of Trade Unions to hold a worldwide week of active support for the Korean people starting on July 10.
　　The Executive Bureau called on all trade union organizations and active members of the General Confederation of Labor to hold meetings in all enterprises and institutions and demonstrations in various places to defend the Korean people who are fighting for their freedom and independence, and to demand the immediate withdrawal of American troops from Korea.

### 法总工会号召声援朝鲜人民

1950-07-10
第1版()
专栏：

　　法总工会号召声援朝鲜人民
    【新华社八日讯】塔斯社巴黎讯：法国总工会执行局宣布积极响应世界工会联合会的呼吁——自七月十日起举行全世界范围的积极声援朝鲜人民周。
    执行局号召总工会所属的一切工会组织及工会会员全体积极分子，在一切企业和机关中举行大会，在各地举行示威，维护正在为其自由和独立战斗的朝鲜人民，并要求美国军队立即撤出朝鲜。
