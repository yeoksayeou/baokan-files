Title: Cai Chang, Chairman of the All-China Women's Federation, Cables the Polish Women's Federation
Author:
Date: 1950-07-22
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, 19th: July 22nd marks the 6th anniversary of the founding of the Polish People's Republic. Cai Chang, Chairman of the All-China Democratic Women's Federation, sent a congratulatory telegram to the Polish Women's Federation. The telegram reads as follows: To Mrs. Stechwska, Polish Women's League:
    On the occasion of the 6th anniversary of your country's National Day, I extend warm congratulations to the Polish people and women on behalf of the Chinese women. I wish you even greater achievements in the Six-Year Plan movement. I hope that Poland and China will unite even more closely and strive for world peace.

### 我全国妇联主席蔡畅电波兰妇联

1950-07-22
第1版()
专栏：

　　我全国妇联主席蔡畅电波兰妇联
    【新华社十九日讯】七月二十二日为波兰人民共和国六周年国庆纪念日，中华全国民主妇女联合会主席蔡畅特电波兰人民共和国妇女祝贺。电文如下：波兰妇女联盟石华特斯加夫人：
    欣逢贵国六周年国庆纪念，谨代表中国妇女向波兰人民与妇女致热烈祝贺。并祝你们在六年计划运动中取得更伟大的成就。希望波兰和中国将更紧密的团结一致，为争取世界和平而奋斗。
