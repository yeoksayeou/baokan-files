Title: Pravda Publishes Our Land Reform Law
Author:
Date: 1950-07-05
Page: 1

　　Xinhua News Agency, 4th. TASS, Moscow: Pravda published on the 3rd a number of foreign news items, including the Land Reform Law of the People's Republic of China, which came into effect on June 30th this year, and a joint telegram from various democratic parties and organizations in China to Chairman Mao Zedong and the Central Committee of the Chinese Communist Party, declaring their determination to fight to thoroughly ensure the integrity of China's territory and sovereignty, and so on.

### 真理报刊载我土地改革法

1950-07-05
第1版()
专栏：

　　真理报刊载我土地改革法
    【新华社四日讯】塔斯社莫斯科讯：真理报于三日刊载许多国外消息，其中包括今年六月三十日开始生效的中华人民共和国土地改革法，中国各民主党派团体联合致电毛泽东主席及中国共产党中央委员会，宣布他们准备为彻底保障中国领土与主权的完整而斗争的决心等。
