Title: Korean Art Workers Delegation Performs in Leningrad and Kiev, Receiving Heartfelt Welcome from Audiences Everywhere
Author:
Date: 1950-07-15
Page: 4

　　Xinhua News Agency, July 14th: A comprehensive TASS dispatch reports that the art workers delegation from the Democratic People's Republic of Korea, currently visiting the Soviet Union, held a concert in Leningrad on July 1st. The representatives of the freedom-loving Korean people, who are bravely fighting against the American aggressors, received warm receptions everywhere. On the 3rd, the delegation visited the town of Petrodvorets, and the head of the delegation, Korean Minister of Culture and Propaganda, Heo Jeong-suk, delivered a speech to the welcomers. On the 4th, the delegation visited the Lenin monument in Razliv.

　　The delegation bid farewell to Leningrad on the 8th and proceeded to Kiev, the capital of Ukraine. There they stayed for five days, holding three concerts, which were warmly welcomed by Ukrainian audiences.

　　After traveling along the Dnieper River, the Korean artists left Kiev on July 12th.

### 朝鲜艺术工作者代表团在列城和基辅举行演出  到处都受到观众衷心的欢迎

1950-07-15
第4版()
专栏：

　　朝鲜艺术工作者代表团在列城和基辅举行演出
    到处都受到观众衷心的欢迎
    【新华社十四日讯】综合塔斯社消息：访问苏联的朝鲜民主主义人民共和国的艺术工作者代表团，七月一日在列宁格勒举行演奏会。对美国侵略者英勇作战的、爱好自由的朝鲜人民的代表们，到处都受到热诚的招待。三日，代表团参观了彼得洛得佛列茨镇，代表团团长朝鲜文化宣传部长许贞淑向欢迎者发表了演说。四日，代表团参观了拉兹利夫地方的列宁纪念碑。
    代表团于八日辞别列宁格勒，前往乌克兰的首府基辅。在那儿逗留五天，举行了三次音乐会，受到乌克兰观众衷心的欢迎。
    朝鲜艺术家们在第聂伯河沿岸旅行之后，已于七月十二日离开了基辅。
