Title: Berlin Holds 100,000-Strong Rally Against U.S. Imperialist Military Intervention in Korea; Australian Masses Gather to Protest U.S. Imperialist Aggression
Author:
Date: 1950-07-02
Page: 4
　
    Xinhua News Agency, 1st dispatch: TASS, Berlin, June 30th: On June 29th, the Berlin Peace Committee held a rally under the slogan "Truman Wants War! Strive for Peace!" with nearly 100,000 participants. The Minister of Foreign Affairs of the German Democratic Republic, Döttinger, and the former Reuters Berlin Bureau Chief, Peet, delivered speeches opposing the U.S. imperialist military intervention in Korea.
    Xinhua News Agency, 1st dispatch: TASS, Sydney, June 29th: On June 28th, a mass rally of 2,000 people was held in Melbourne to protest against the American military intervention in Korea. The conference unanimously adopted a resolution demanding the immediate cessation of American armed intervention in Korea.
    Bird, the secretary of the Victorian branch of the Australian Seamen's Union, said in his speech at the conference: "All trade unions will do everything possible to prevent the transportation of armaments, ammunition and troops from Australia."

### 柏林举行十万人大会  反对美帝军事干涉朝鲜  澳群众集会抗议美帝侵略

1950-07-02
第4版()
专栏：

　　柏林举行十万人大会
    反对美帝军事干涉朝鲜       
    澳群众集会抗议美帝侵略
    【新华社一日讯】塔斯社柏林六月三十日讯：六月二十九日，柏林和平委员会在“杜鲁门要战争！为和平而努力！”的口号下举行的大会，参加者将近十万人。德意志民主共和国外交部长窦丁格尔和前路透社驻柏林分社主任皮特发表演说，反对美帝国主义对朝鲜的军事干涉。
    【新华社一日讯】塔斯社悉尼六月二十九日讯：六月二十八日，墨尔本曾举行有两千人参加的群众大会，抗议美国对朝鲜的军事干涉。大会一致通过决议，要求立即停止美国对朝鲜的武装干涉。
    澳大利亚海员工会维多利亚分会秘书伯德在大会中致辞称：“各职工会将尽一切力量阻止把军械、弹药和军队等从澳大利亚运去。”
