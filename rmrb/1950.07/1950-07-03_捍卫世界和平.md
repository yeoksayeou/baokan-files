Title: Defend World Peace
Author: Shao Lizhi
Date: 1950-07-03
Page: 5

　　Defend World Peace
    Shao Lizhi
    The prohibition of atomic weapons and the defense of world peace are in accordance with the wishes of the Chinese people. The Chinese people have long struggled for peace. The birth of New China has laid the foundation for domestic peace; but without world peace, China will still not have peace. Faced with the imperialists' clamor for atomic war, the entire Chinese people should unanimously sign their names to express their determination to defend world peace.

### 捍卫世界和平
邵力子
1950-07-03
第5版()
专栏：

　　捍卫世界和平
    邵力子
    禁止原子武器，保卫世界和平，是符合中国人民的愿望的。中国人民长久为和平奋斗，新中国诞生，国内和平已有基础；但世界没有和平，中国仍不会有和平的。全中国人民面对帝国主义者叫嚣原子战争，确应一致签名，表示自己捍卫世界和平的决心。
