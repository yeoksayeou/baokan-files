Title: Our Opinion
Author: Bai Shouyi
Date: 1950-07-03
Page: 5

　　Our Opinion
    Bai Shouyi
    People all over the world want people's democracy and lasting peace. Only inhuman reactionaries, in order to carry out their fascist rule, clamor for war every day and vainly try to use atomic weapons to suppress the power of the people.
    Our ongoing peace signature campaign is a warning to them: the voice of the people has resounded throughout the world, the people have not been intimidated by atomic weapons, and the people want to transfer the control of atomic weapons from the hands of reactionaries to the hands of the people.
    We, the Hui people, have just been liberated after hundreds of years of long-term bloody rule. We have profound feelings and understanding of the defense of world peace. We want to contribute all possible strength to strive for the success of defending world peace.

### 我们的意见
白寿彝
1950-07-03
第5版()
专栏：

　　我们的意见
    白寿彝
    全世界的人民，都要人民民主，都要持久和平。只有丧失了人性的反动派，为了遂行他们的法西斯统治，才天天叫嚣着战争，才妄想要用原子武器来镇压人民的力量。
    我们正在展开中的和平签名运动，是警告他们：人民的声音已响亮了全世界，人民并没有被原子武器吓倒，人民要把原子武器的管制，从反动派手里移到人民手里。
    我们回族人民，在数百年的长期的血腥统治下，刚刚获得解放。我们对于世界和平的保卫，具有深刻的感情和认识。我们要提贡一切可能的力量，争取保卫世界和平的成功。
