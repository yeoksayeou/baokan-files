Popular Science Lecture Tenth Lecture to be Held Tomorrow

The Popular Science Lecture, jointly organized by the Youth Service Department and the Science Popularization Bureau, will hold its tenth lecture at 4:00 PM on July 15th (Saturday) at the Sino-Soviet Friendship Association (19 Nanheyan). Dr. Huang Xinmin will speak on "What is Chemistry? What Problems Can It Solve for Us?" Admission tickets can be obtained at the Youth Service Department during opening hours on July 14th (Friday) (groups can obtain them with a letter of introduction).

(China Youth News Agency)

### 大众科学讲座明日举行第十讲

1950-07-14
第6版()
专栏：

　　大众科学讲座明日举行第十讲
    青年服务部与科学普及局合办的大众科学讲座，定于七月十五日（星期六）下午四时在苏联对外文协（南河沿十九号）举行第十讲。由黄新民博士讲“什么是化学？它能替我们解决那些问题？”入场券可于七月十四日（星期五）在青年服务部开放时间内在青年服务部领取（团体凭介绍信领取）。
                                                  （中国青年社）
