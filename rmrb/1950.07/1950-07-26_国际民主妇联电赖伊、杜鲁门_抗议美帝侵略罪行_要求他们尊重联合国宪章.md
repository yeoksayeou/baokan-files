Title: International Democratic Women's Federation Cables Trygve Lie, Truman Protesting US Imperialist Crimes, Demanding They Respect the UN Charter
Author:
Date: 1950-07-26
Page: 4
　
　　International Democratic Women's Federation Cables Trygve Lie, Truman
    Protesting US Imperialist Crimes
    Demanding They Respect the UN Charter
    [Xinhua News Agency, 25th] The International Democratic Women's Federation recently sent cables to UN Secretary-General Trygve Lie and US President Truman, lodging solemn protests against the US's brazen armed aggression against Korea and Taiwan. The texts of the two cables are as follows:
    (1) Cable to UN Secretary-General Trygve Lie
    The International Democratic Women's Federation strongly protests the Security Council's illegal resolution supporting US military intervention in the Korean people. We demand respect for the UN Charter and an immediate end to US intervention.
    (2) Cable to US President Truman
    The International Democratic Women's Federation is extremely indignant at your armed aggression against Korea and Taiwan, and the bombing of Korea. We condemn this shameful infringement of people's rights and violation of UN principles. We demand that you immediately cease your crimes.

### 国际民主妇联电赖伊、杜鲁门  抗议美帝侵略罪行  要求他们尊重联合国宪章

1950-07-26
第4版()
专栏：

　　国际民主妇联电赖伊、杜鲁门
    抗议美帝侵略罪行   
    要求他们尊重联合国宪章
    【新华社二十五日讯】国际民主妇女联合会最近分别致电联合国秘书长赖伊及美国总统杜鲁门，对美国悍然武装侵略朝鲜、台湾的行为，提出严正抗议。两电电文如下：
    （一）致联合国秘书长赖伊电
    国际民主妇女联合会强硬地抗议安全理事会的非法决议，支持美国军事干涉朝鲜人民。我们要求尊重联合国宪章，并立即停止美国的干涉。
    （二）致美国总统杜鲁门电
    国际民主妇女联合会对你的武装侵略朝鲜、台湾的行为及对朝鲜的轰炸，极为愤慨。我们谴责这种可耻的侵害人民权利和违背联合国原则的行为。我们要求你立即停止你们的罪行。
