Title: The Soviet People Wholeheartedly Defend Peace - Editorial by Izvestia
Author:
Date: 1950-07-15
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, July 14th (via TASS): Soviet newspapers recently published commentaries on the Soviet Peace Committee's report that the number of Soviet citizens who have signed peace appeals has reached over 96 million. The newspapers unanimously pointed out that the Soviet peace signature campaign has become a truly national movement.

	Izvestia published an editorial titled "The Soviet People Wholeheartedly Defend Peace," stating: "All the Soviet people are eagerly signing the peace declaration, which demonstrates their irresistible desire to defend peace. The Soviet people have formed a powerful united front, standing up to defend the sacred cause of peace." The editorial pointed out: "The Soviet signature campaign has set off a new high tide in the peace movements of all countries in the world. More and more people are signing, and millions of people have joined the peace camp."

### 苏联人民全心全意保卫和平  消息报社论

1950-07-15
第4版()
专栏：

　　苏联人民全心全意保卫和平
    消息报社论
    【新华社十四日讯】据塔斯社莫斯科十二日讯：苏联各报顷就苏联和平委员会报告苏联和平签名者已达九千六百余万人一事发表评论，各报一致指出：苏联的和平签名已经是真正具有全国性的运动。
    消息报以《苏联人民全心全意保卫和平》为题，发表社论称：苏联全体人民都争先恐后地在和平宣言上签名，这表示了他们保卫和平的不可抗拒的意愿。苏联人民已结成一个强大的统一战线，挺身保卫神圣的和平事业。社论指出：苏联的签名运动，已在世界各国的和平运动中掀起了新的高潮。签名的人愈来愈多，数以百万计的人民已加入和平阵营中来了。
