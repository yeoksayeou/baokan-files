Title: Taiwan Democratic Self-Government League Issues Statement: Determined to Fight to the End for the Liberation of Taiwan

Author:

Date: 1950-07-11
Page: 1

　　Taiwan Democratic Self-Government League Issues Statement
    Determined to Fight to the End for the Liberation of Taiwan
    [Xinhua News Agency, Shanghai, 10th] The Taiwan Democratic Self-Government League issued a statement supporting Foreign Minister Zhou's statement to UN Secretary-General Trygve Lie on July 6th. The statement said: The people of Taiwan resolutely oppose the American armed aggression, firmly support the heroic struggle of the Korean brothers, and will continue to strengthen their unity and fighting spirit, defeat American provocations, and liberate Taiwan. The statement emphasized: The people of Taiwan are determined to spare no sacrifice, and under the leadership of the Central People's Government of the People's Republic of China, to wage a resolute struggle against the American aggressors and the Chiang Kai-shek bandit gang until complete victory.

### 台湾民主自治同盟发表声明  决为解放台湾奋斗到底

1950-07-11
第1版()
专栏：

　　台湾民主自治同盟发表声明
    决为解放台湾奋斗到底
    【新华社上海十日电】台湾民主自治同盟发表声明，拥护周外长七月六日向联合国秘书长赖伊提出的声明。声明说：台湾人民坚决反对美国武装侵略行为，坚决支持朝鲜兄弟的英勇斗争，并将继续加强自己的团结和斗志，击败美国的挑衅，解放台湾。声明力称：台湾人民决心不惜任何牺牲，在中华人民共和国中央人民政府领导下，对美国侵略者及蒋介石匪帮进行坚决的斗争，直到完全胜利为止。
