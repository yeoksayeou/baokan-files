Title: Measures for Rewarding Meritorious Personnel Stipulated by the Supreme People's Assembly of North Korea
Author:
Date: 1950-07-10
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Pyongyang, 3rd. The Supreme People's Assembly of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea issued an order on June 30th stipulating that the highest honor of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea is the title of Hero of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea. This title of hero will be awarded to those who have made heroic achievements for the country. All those who have been awarded the title of Hero of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea will simultaneously receive the Republic's highest medal—the First Class National Flag Medal, the Gold Star Medal, and a certificate from the Standing Committee of the Supreme People's Assembly of North Korea. The right to award and deprive the title of Hero of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea belongs to the Standing Committee of the Supreme People's Assembly of North Korea.

### 朝鲜最高人民会议规定奖励立功人员办法

1950-07-10
第4版()
专栏：

　　朝鲜最高人民会议规定奖励立功人员办法
    【新华社平壤三日电】朝鲜民主主义人民共和国最高人民会议于六月三十日发布命令：规定朝鲜民主主义人民共和国的最高荣誉，为朝鲜人民共和国的英雄称号。这种英雄称号将授与对国家立了英雄的功勋者。凡荣获朝鲜人民共和国英雄称号者，将同时领授共和国最高勋章——一级国旗勋章、金星奖章及朝鲜最高人民会议常任委员会的奖状。朝鲜人民共和国英雄称号的授与权与剥夺权，属于朝鲜最高人民会议常任委员会。
