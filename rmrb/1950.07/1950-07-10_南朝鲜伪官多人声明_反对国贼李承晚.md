Title: Multiple South Korean Puppet Officials Declare Opposition to Traitor Li Seung-man
Author:
Date: 1950-07-10
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Pyongyang, 6th: According to the Korean Central News Agency, Seoul, 4th: Kim Hyo-seok, former "Minister of Home Affairs" of the Li Seung-man puppet government, Ryu Dong-yeol, former head of the security department of the US occupation army government, and several puppet national assembly members have issued statements expressing their opposition to the traitor Li Seung-man and actively participating in the work of striving for the unified independence of Korea.

### 南朝鲜伪官多人声明  反对国贼李承晚

1950-07-10
第4版()
专栏：

　　南朝鲜伪官多人声明
    反对国贼李承晚
    【新华社平壤六日电】据朝鲜中央通讯社汉城四日讯：李承晚傀儡政府的前“内务长官”金孝锡，和曾任美占领军军政府保卫部部长的柳东悦，以及伪国会议员若干人，顷发表声明，表示反对卖国贼李承晚，并积极参加争取朝鲜统一独立的工作。
