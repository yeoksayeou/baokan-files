Title: I Hate Warmongers! A Letter from a Soviet Soldier to the Editors of "New Times"
Author: You Ren
Date: 1950-07-07
Page: 4

People's Garden

　　I Hate Warmongers!
    A Letter from a Soviet Soldier to the Editors of "New Times" To the Editors of "New Times":
    If possible, please publish this letter. Love for the motherland and hatred for the warmongers compel me to write this letter.
    I grew up among ordinary Soviet working people. I am an ordinary soldier. I want to tell the warmongers how we soldiers think and feel.
    Messrs. Truman and Acheson, Churchill and Bevin, I hope you can see this letter. You are instigating another world war. Your old capitalist system is cracking, and the days of its collapse and disintegration are not far off. A brand-new socialist order will take its place. You are trying to stop the advance of the wheels of history. You are furious, you are building military bases, cultivating bacteria, manufacturing atomic bombs, preparing to drown the world in the blood of millions, in order to stop the progress of history and escape your doom.
    But you will never succeed! The people of the world will not allow another war. They will not allow their children to bleed again.
    I am a soldier, but neither I nor my comrades want another war. We are young people, and young people have ideals. We also have ideals. In our ideals, there is peaceful labor in the fields, factories, mines, and workshops of the motherland, and learning in primary schools, specialized schools, and universities.
    Our future is solid, and we look forward to tomorrow with confidence. The road to happiness lies before each of us. We Soviet soldiers do not hate Americans or Britons, Turks, or people of any other nationality. But we hate you—the warmongers.
    I extend my hand to the American soldiers who support peace, whether they are Black or white.
    I pray for the British workers and peasants to achieve the greatest success in their peaceful endeavors. I take off my hat to the French priest who fights for peace. I respect his gray hair, even though I myself am an atheist.
    My country does not need expansion. We Soviet people yearn for peace.
    But if you start another world war, then we will fight resolutely without mercy. Our purpose in fighting is to make the world no longer know war, so that our children will forget the word "war."
      N. K. Saiduv
      April 17, 1950
    (Translated by You Ren from "New Times" Issue 19)

### 我憎恨战争贩子！  一个苏联士兵给《新时代》编者的信
右仁
1950-07-07
第4版()
专栏：人民园地

　　我憎恨战争贩子！
    一个苏联士兵给《新时代》编者的信《新时代》编辑们：
    如果可能的话，请发表这一封信。对祖国的热爱和对战争贩子们的憎恨，促使我写这封信。
    我是在普通的苏联劳动人民中长大的。我是一个普通的兵士。我要告诉战争贩子们：我们兵士们是怎样思想和怎样感觉的。
    杜鲁门和艾奇逊、邱吉尔和贝文诸先生，我希望你们能看到这封信。你们正在鼓动另一次世界大战。你们古老的资本主义制度正在破裂着，它的崩溃瓦解的日子已经不远了。一种崭新的社会主义秩序将取而代之。你们正在阻挡历史车轮的前进。你们勃然大怒，你们建立军事基地，培养细菌，制造原子弹，准备把世界淹没在千百万人的鲜血中，借以阻挡历史的前进，躲避你们的末日。
    但是你们决不会成功！全世界的人民不容许有另一次战争。他们不容许自己的孩子们再流血了。
    我是一个兵士，但我和我的同志们都不愿再有战争。我们是年青人，年青人都有理想。我们也有理想。在我们的理想中，是祖国的田野上、工厂里、矿山上和作坊里的和平劳动，是中小学里，专科学校里和大学里的学习。
    我们的前途是稳固的，我们充满信心地眺望着明天。在我们每个人的面前，都是幸福之路。我们苏维埃兵士们并不憎恨美国人或英国人、土耳其人或其他任何国籍的人。但我们憎恨你们——战争贩子们。
    我伸手给拥护和平的美国士兵，不论他是黑人或白人。
    我祷祝英国工人和农民在他们的和平事业上获得最大的成功。我脱帽向为和平而战斗的法国神父致敬。我尊敬他的灰白的头发，虽然我自己是一个无神论者。
    我的祖国不需要扩张。我们苏维埃人民渴望和平。
    但是如果你们发动另一次世界大战，那末我们将毫不留情的坚决作战。我们作战的目的是要使全球不再知道战争，使我们的孩子们忘记了“战争”这个字眼。
      Ｎ·Ｋ·赛杜夫
      一九五○年四月十七日
    （右仁译自《新时代》十九期）
