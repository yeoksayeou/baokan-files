Title: International Botanical Congress Held in Sweden Specifically Discusses Michurin's Theory
Author:
Date: 1950-07-30
Page: 4

　　Xinhua News Agency, 28th: A comprehensive TASS dispatch: The Seventh International Botanical Congress was held in Stockholm, the capital of Sweden, on the 19th. A delegation of Soviet scientists attended the meeting and returned to Moscow on the 25th. Sukachev, the head of the delegation, told TASS reporters: This congress was the first international conference of botanists held after the war. Prominent scientists from Sweden, Norway, France, the United States, Britain, Canada, South America, and other countries attended the meeting. At the conference, the Soviet representatives reported on seven papers. The trends in Soviet botanical research and the northward migration of agricultural crops attracted great attention from the participating scholars. A special meeting, attended by a large audience, specifically discussed the achievements of Michurin's theory. Many representatives believed that Michurin's direction is the most progressive direction in the world.

### 在瑞举行的国际植物学者大会  专门讨论了米丘林学说

1950-07-30
第4版()
专栏：

　　在瑞举行的国际植物学者大会
    专门讨论了米丘林学说
    【新华社二十八日讯】综合塔斯社消息：第七届国际植物学者大会于十九日在瑞典京城斯德哥尔摩举行。苏联科学家代表团出席了这一会议，并已于二十五日返抵莫斯科。代表团团长苏卡切夫对塔斯社记者发表谈话称：此次大会系战后第一次举行的植物学者的国际会议。与会者有瑞典、挪威、法国、美国、英国、加拿大、南美洲及其他国家的卓越科学家们。在大会上，苏联代表报告了七篇论文。关于苏联植物学研究的趋势和农业农作物北移的问题，引起了与会学者极大注意。由广大听众参加的特别会议，专门讨论了米丘林学说的成就。许多代表都认为米丘林的方向是世界上最进步的方向。
