Title: The 40th Meeting of the Government Administration Council Hears Work Reports from the Supervision Committee, etc.
Author:
Date: 1950-07-08
Page: 1

　　Xinhua News Agency, July 7th—The 40th Government Administration Council meeting of the Central People's Government was held at 3:00 PM on the 7th. The meeting first heard a preliminary summary of the work of the People's Supervision Committee of the Government Administration Council for the past six months, and a report on future work tasks, given by Deputy Director Liu Jingfan. After discussion, the report was approved. The meeting then heard a report from Wu De, the head of the Central Delegation sent to Pingyuan Province in March, regarding the inspection of work in Pingyuan Province. The meeting expressed satisfaction with the work of the delegation, approved the delegation's work report, and agreed with the delegation's recommendation to dissolve the Central Delegation sent to Pingyuan Province, effective immediately. In addition, the meeting passed several appointment cases and submitted several appointment cases to the Central People's Government Committee for approval.

### 政务院第四十次会议  听取监察委会等工作报告

1950-07-08
第1版()
专栏：

　　政务院第四十次会议
    听取监察委会等工作报告
    【新华社七日讯】中央人民政府政务院第四十次政务会议于七日下午三时举行。会议首先听取了政务院人民监察委员会副主任刘景范所作的该委半年工作初步总结及今后工作任务的报告，经讨论后批准了该报告。会议继听取了于三月间派往平原省的中央代表团主任吴德关于平原省工作的视察报告。会议对该团的工作认为满意，批准了该团的工作报告，并同意该团的建议，即日起结束派往平原省的中央代表团组织。此外，会议通过任命案及提请中央人民政府委员会批准任命案多起。
