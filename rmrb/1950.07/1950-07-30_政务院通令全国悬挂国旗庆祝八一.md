Title: Government Administration Council Issues Circular Nationwide to Hang National Flags in Celebration of August 1st Army Day
Author:
Date: 1950-07-30
Page: 1

　　Government Administration Council Issues Circular Nationwide to Hang National Flags in Celebration of August 1st Army Day
    Xinhua News Agency, July 30th. The Government Administration Council of the Central People's Government issued a circular on July 30th regarding the commemoration of the August 1st Army Day. The full text is as follows:
    In order to commemorate August 1st Army Day, it is decided that governments at all levels, all agencies, all enterprises, all schools, and people's organizations of all sectors throughout the country shall hang the national flag for one day on August 1st to celebrate.

### 政务院通令全国悬挂国旗庆祝八一

1950-07-30
第1版()
专栏：

　　政务院通令全国悬挂国旗庆祝八一
    【新华社三十日讯】中央人民政府政务院于七月三十日发出关于纪念八一建军节的通令，全文如下：
    为了纪念八一建军节，决定全国各级政府、各机关、各企业、各学校、各界人民团体于八月一日悬挂国旗一天，以资庆祝。
