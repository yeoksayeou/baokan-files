Title: China Democratic League Commemorates Martyred Pioneers
Author:
Date: 1950-07-16
Page: 1

　　China Democratic League Commemorates Martyred Pioneers
    [This Newspaper’s Dispatch] July 15th was the China Democratic League's Memorial Day for Martyred Pioneers. In addition to notifying all general branches and provincial and municipal sub-branches to hold memorial meetings on this day, the headquarters of the China Democratic League, together with the Beijing Municipal Branch, held a joint memorial ceremony in the headquarters auditorium at 9:00 am on the 15th. Zhang Lan, chairman of the League, and 75 members from the headquarters and municipal branches attended. First, Shen Junru, vice chairman of the League, gave a speech as chairman, explaining the significance of the meeting. Then, Huang Yanpei, Zhang Bojun, Li Helin, and others spoke successively, emphasizing that commemoration of the martyrs should be expressed through action. Finally, Tang Hongren reported the heroic circumstances of the more than 20 China Democratic League martyrs who died generously in the Chongqing Ciqikou massacre. According to the headquarters, in addition to Li Gongpu, Wen Yiduo, Tao Xingzhi, Du Bincheng, Yang Bokai, and others, nearly 90 members of the China Democratic League have sacrificed in Sichuan, Shaanxi, Gansu, Shanxi, Guangdong, Shanghai and other places during this people's revolution. Investigations are still underway in other areas.
    Furthermore, according to news from Chongqing, the Provisional Working Committee of the Chongqing Municipal Branch of the China Democratic League held a memorial meeting on the 14th at the Victory Building to commemorate the League's martyred pioneers, Li Gongpu, Wen Yiduo, Tao Xingzhi, Du Bincheng, and others. Representatives from various democratic parties and democratic figures, totaling more than 200 people, attended the meeting. Xiao Huaqing, head of the Chongqing China Democratic League, delivered a speech, and Yang Yibo reported a brief history of the martyrs. Then, Cheng Zijian, Deputy Minister of the United Front Work Department of the Southwest Bureau of the CPC Central Committee, Ren Baige, Minister of Propaganda of the Chongqing Municipal Committee of the CPC, Xu Chonglin, representative of the China National Democratic Construction Association, Deng Houyan, representative of the Revolutionary Committee of the Kuomintang, Huang Penghao, representative of the Chinese Peasants' and Workers' Democratic Party, and Sun Mingdong, Principal of Yucai Middle School, who had followed Mr. Tao Xingzhi for more than 20 years, spoke successively. The speakers expounded on the spirit of the martyrs in pursuing truth and serving the people, and encouraged each other to strengthen unity, consolidate the united front, and advance victoriously under the banner of Chairman Mao.

### 民盟纪念殉难先烈

1950-07-16
第1版()
专栏：

　　民盟纪念殉难先烈
    【本报讯】七月十五日为民盟殉难先烈纪念日。中国民主同盟总部除通告各总支部及各省市支分部于是日一致举行纪念会外，十五日上午九时特与北京市支部在总部礼堂联合举行纪念仪式，到有该盟主席张澜及总部与市支部所属盟员七十五人。首由该盟副主席沈钧儒主席致辞，说明开会纪念意义后，继由黄炎培、章伯钧、李何林等相继讲话，强调纪念先烈，应以行动表示。末由唐弘仁报告重庆磁器口大屠杀中民盟殉难烈士二十余人慷慨就义时之英勇情形。按民盟盟员在此次人民革命中牺牲者，除李公朴、闻一多、陶行知、杜斌丞、杨伯恺等外，近经总部调查，在川、陕、甘、晋、粤、沪等地牺牲者，已在九十人以上，其他各地尚在调查中。
    又据重庆消息：民盟重庆市支部临时工作委员会为纪念该盟殉难烈士李公朴、闻一多、陶行知、杜斌丞等，曾于十四日在胜利大厦举行纪念大会，到会各民主党派代表及民主人士二百余人。大会由重庆民盟负责人萧华清致词、杨一波报告烈士史略后，中共中央西南局统一战线工作部副部长程子健、中共重庆市委会宣传部长任白戈、民主建国会代表徐崇林、国民党革命委员会代表邓后炎，农工民主党代表黄朋豪及追随陶行知先生二十余年的育才中学校长孙铭动先后讲话。讲话者阐述了诸烈士追求真理、为人民服务的精神，并互勉加强团结，巩固统一战线，在毛主席旗帜下胜利前进。
