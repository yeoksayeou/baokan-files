Title: Beijing Municipal Committee for the Movement to Resist U.S. Aggression Invites All Parties to Participate in Work; Held Third Meeting Yesterday to Arrange In-Depth Propaganda

Author:

Date: 1950-07-26
Page: 1

　　Beijing Municipal Committee for the Movement to Resist U.S. Aggression Against Taiwan and Korea
    Invites All Parties to Participate in Work
    Held Third Meeting Yesterday to Arrange In-Depth Propaganda
    [Beijing News Agency Dispatch] The Beijing Municipal Committee for the Movement to Resist U.S. Aggression Against Taiwan and Korea has decided to invite the Chinese Communist Party and various democratic parties to participate in the work of the committee. The committee held its third plenary meeting on the afternoon of the 25th. Representatives from the Beijing Municipal Committee of the Chinese Communist Party, the Beijing Branch of the China Democratic League, the Beijing Branch of the Revolutionary Committee of the Chinese Kuomintang, the Beijing Branch of the China National Democratic Construction Association, and the Beijing Party Affairs Consolidation Committee of the Chinese Peasants' and Workers' Democratic Party all participated. The meeting discussed and arranged how to combine the Beijing community's celebration of the August 1st Army Day demonstration against U.S. aggression against Taiwan and Korea to further carry out in-depth propaganda work.

### 京市反对美国侵略运动委会  邀请各党派参加工作  昨开三次会议布置深入宣传

1950-07-26
第1版()
专栏：

　　京市反对美国侵略运动委会
    邀请各党派参加工作
    昨开三次会议布置深入宣传
    【京市新闻处讯】北京市反对美国侵略台湾朝鲜运动委员会决定邀请中国共产党及各民主党派参加委员会工作。该委员会于二十五日下午举行第三次全体会议，中国共产党北京市委员会、中国民主同盟北京市支部、中国国民党革命委员会北京市分部、中国民主建国会北京市分会、中国农工民主党北京市党务整理委员会都派有代表参加。会上讨论并布置了如何结合北京各界庆祝八一建军节反对美国侵略台湾朝鲜示威大会，进一步展开深入的宣传工作。
