Title: Korean People's Army Captures Major US Military Strategic Defense Point: Liberates Daejeon, Annihilates All Enemy Troops; Air Force Downs Five US Aircraft, Liberates Yeongdeok in North Gyeongsang Province
Author:
Date: 1950-07-22
Page: 1

　　Xinhua News Agency, Pyongyang, 21st: The General Headquarters of the Korean People's Army of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea announced in a battle report this morning, July 21st, that the People's Army attacking Daejeon, after crossing the Geum River, annihilated the US military units and the remnant troops of the Syngman Rhee puppet army that concentrated large forces on the south bank of the Geum River in an attempt to resist stubbornly. At 12:00 noon on the 20th, they completely liberated Daejeon City, the capital of South Chungcheong Province and the enemy's main strategic defense point (Note: Daejeon was the temporary capital of the Syngman Rhee puppet government after the People's Army liberated Seoul). In this battle, all the enemy troops defending Daejeon were annihilated, and a large number of prisoners and captured spoils were seized. Detailed battle results are being investigated.
　　The People's Army Air Force displayed a high degree of heroism in this battle. The People's Army Air Force, led by pilot Kim Ki-ok, who was awarded the title of Republic Hero, engaged in fierce battles with US aircraft formations that were five times superior in number over Daejeon and Gimpo (Gyeonggi Province) on the 19th. As a result, five US aircraft (including B-29 bombers) were shot down, inflicting heavy casualties on the enemy's rear transport and manpower.
　　Currently, various units of the People's Army are continuing to advance.
　　Xinhua News Agency, Pyongyang, 21st: The General Headquarters of the Korean People's Army of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea reported on the evening of the 20th that the People's Army on the east coast liberated Yeongdeok County in North Gyeongsang Province on the 19th. The residents of the county warmly welcomed their liberators, the People's Army, into the city.

### 朝鲜人民军攻克美军主要战略防御地  解放大田全歼守敌  空军击落美机五架，庆尚北道解放盈德

1950-07-22
第1版()
专栏：

　　朝鲜人民军攻克美军主要战略防御地
    解放大田全歼守敌       
    空军击落美机五架，庆尚北道解放盈德
    【新华社平壤二十一日电】朝鲜民主主义人民共和国人民军总司令部二十一日晨发表战报称：进攻大田的人民军在强渡锦江后，歼灭了集中大军于锦江南岸企图顽抗的美军部队与李承晚残余伪军，于二十日中午十二时完全解放了敌主要战略防御地、忠清南道的省会大田市（按：大田是人民军解放汉城后李承晚傀儡政府的临时首都）。在这一战斗中，防守大田的敌军全部被歼，俘掳与缴获的战利品甚多，详细战果正清查中。
    人民军空军在这一战斗中发挥了高度的英勇性。以荣获共和国英雄称号的驾驶员金基玉为首的人民军的空军，十九日在大田与金浦（京畿道）上空，与在数量上占五倍优势的美军机群，展开激战，结果击落美机（其中包括Ｂ二十九型轰炸机）五架，予敌后方运输与有生力量以重大杀伤。
    现人民军各部正继续挺进中。
    【新华社平壤二十一日电】朝鲜民主主义人民共和国人民军总司令部二十日晚报道：东海岸方面的人民军，十九日解放了庆尚北道的盈德县。该县居民热烈欢迎自己的解放者——人民军入城。
