Title: US Violates Geneva Convention, Indiscriminately Bombs Peaceful Korean Residents, Czechoslovak Red Cross Protests
Author:
Date: 1950-07-17
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, July 13th: The Executive Committee of the Czechoslovak Red Cross has sent a statement to the International Committee of the Red Cross, requesting the committee to immediately protest to the United States government against the barbarous act of American aircraft bombing peaceful Korean residents. It pointed out that this barbarous act by the United States has seriously violated the Convention for the Protection of Civilian Persons in Time of War signed in Geneva on December 8, 1949.

### 美破坏日内瓦公约  滥炸朝鲜和平居民  捷红十字会提抗议

1950-07-17
第4版()
专栏：

　　美破坏日内瓦公约
    滥炸朝鲜和平居民
    捷红十字会提抗议
    【新华社十五日讯】塔斯社莫斯科十三日讯：捷克斯洛伐克红十字会执行委员会，顷以一项声明送致国际红十字会委员会，要求委员会立即向美国政府抗议美国飞机轰炸朝鲜和平居民的暴行。并指出美国此种暴行，已严重破坏了一九四九年十二月八日在日内瓦签定的战时平民保护公约。
