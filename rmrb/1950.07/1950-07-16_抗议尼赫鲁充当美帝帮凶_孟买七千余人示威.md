Title: Protesting Nehru's Role as an Accomplice of U.S. Imperialism: Over 7,000 People Demonstrate in Mumbai
Author:
Date: 1950-07-16
Page: 4

　　Protesting Nehru's Role as an Accomplice of U.S. Imperialism
　　Over 7,000 People Demonstrate in Mumbai
　　[Xinhua News Agency, Prague, 15th] TASS, Prague, 13th, citing Telepress, Mumbai: Over 7,000 people in Mumbai participated in a demonstration protesting the Indian government's support for U.S. aggression in Korea. A resolution passed at a rally held after the demonstration stated that the Indian government "tore off its mask of neutrality, exposing its true colors as an agent of U.S. imperialism." The demonstration was held under the slogan of supporting the Korean people with fraternal friendship. Demonstrators urged Indian Prime Minister Nehru to change his government's policy and demanded that U.S. troops withdraw from Korea. The demonstration was jointly organized by several political parties and trade unions.

### 抗议尼赫鲁充当美帝帮凶  孟买七千余人示威

1950-07-16
第4版()
专栏：

　　抗议尼赫鲁充当美帝帮凶
    孟买七千余人示威
    【新华社十五日讯】据塔斯社布拉格十三日电引电通社孟买讯：孟买有七千多人参加了抗议印度政府支持美国侵略朝鲜的示威。在示威后举行的大会上通过决议称：印度政府“撕毁了自己中立的假面具，暴露出美帝国主义代理人的真面目”。示威是在以兄弟般的友谊支援朝鲜人民的口号下举行的。示威者力促印度总理尼赫鲁改变他的政府的政策，并要求美军从朝鲜撤兵。这次示威是由好几个政党和工会联合组织的。
