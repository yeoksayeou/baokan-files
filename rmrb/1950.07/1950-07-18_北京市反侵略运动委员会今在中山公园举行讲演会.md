Title: Beijing Anti-Aggression Movement Committee to Hold Lecture Meeting in Zhongshan Park Today
Author: Chen Yingnan
Date: 1950-07-18
Page: 1

　　The Beijing People's Committee Against American Aggression in Taiwan and Korea has decided to hold a "Lecture Meeting Against American Aggression in Taiwan and Korea" at the Zhongshan Park Concert Hall at 7:30 PM on the 18th, in preparation for actively and widely launching the propaganda activities for the Week of the Movement Against American Aggression in Taiwan and Korea. Fan Changjiang will report on "The Situation of the Korean War," and Xiao Ming, the director of the committee, will report on "The Significance of the Week of the Movement Against American Aggression in Taiwan and Korea and the Work Plan of the City during the Week of the Movement." If it rains, the meeting will be postponed.

### 北京市反侵略运动委员会今在中山公园举行讲演会

1950-07-18
第1版()
专栏：

　　北京市反侵略运动委员会今在中山公园举行讲演会
    【本报讯】北京市人民反对美国侵略台湾朝鲜运动委员会，为准备积极广泛展开反对美国侵略台湾朝鲜运动周的宣传活动，决定十八日下午七时半在中山公园音乐堂举行“反对美国侵略台湾朝鲜讲演会”，由范长江报告“朝鲜战争形势”，并由该会主任萧明报告“反对美国侵略台湾朝鲜运动周的意义及运动周内本市工作计划”。如遇天雨，改期举行。
                                                      （陈应楠）
