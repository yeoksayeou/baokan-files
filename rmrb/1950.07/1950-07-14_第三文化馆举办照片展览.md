Title: The Third Cultural Center Holds Photo Exhibition
Author: Xuan Huaiyuan
Date: 1950-07-14
Page: 6
Radio Program

    The Beijing Municipal Third Cultural Center (Xixiejie, Xidan North Avenue) will hold a "Soviet Photos" exhibition from the 15th of this month to July 28th. The photos include "Today's Soviet Industry and Commerce," "Soviet Production and Construction," "Soviet Price Reductions," and "The Situation of Soviet Elections," among others. All sectors are welcome to visit. (Xuan Huaiyuan)

### 第三文化馆举办照片展览
宣怀远
1950-07-14
第6版()
专栏：广播节目

　　第三文化馆举办照片展览
    北京市立第三文化馆（西单北大街西斜街）自本月十五日起至七月二十八日止，举办“苏联照片”展览。照片内容有：“今日苏联工商业”、“苏联生产建设”、“苏联减低物价”和“苏联选举的情形”等多幅，欢迎各界前往参观。（宣怀远）
