Title: Poland and North Korea Negotiate Mutual Exchange of Diplomatic Representatives
Author: 
Date: 1950-07-12
Page: 4

　　Xinhua News Agency, Warsaw, 9th (via TASS): The Polish News Agency reports that the Polish government has instructed its ambassador to China, Mr. Burkin, to negotiate with representatives of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea regarding the mutual exchange of diplomatic representatives between Poland and Korea.

### 波朝进行谈判互派外交代表

1950-07-12
第4版()
专栏：

　　波朝进行谈判互派外交代表
    【新华社十一日讯】塔斯社华沙九日电引波兰通讯社消息：波兰政府已训令驻华大使布尔金，与朝鲜民主主义人民共和国代表进行谈判波兰与朝鲜互派外交代表的事宜。
