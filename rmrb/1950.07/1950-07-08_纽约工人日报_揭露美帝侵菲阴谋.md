Title: New York Worker's Daily Exposes US Imperialist Conspiracy to Invade the Philippines
Author:
Date: 1950-07-08
Page: 4

　　New York Worker's Daily
    Exposes US Imperialist Conspiracy to Invade the Philippines
    [Xinhua News Agency, dated 4th] According to a United Press dispatch from New York on June 28th: The New York *Worker's Daily*, in a Washington dispatch titled "Winds of Liberation Sweep the Pacific," revealed that the U.S. State Department is taking all forms of further "forcible intervention" against the national liberation movement in the Philippines. The article pointed out: Major events are also developing in the Philippines. According to a reactionary journalist's estimate, the membership of the Philippine "Farmers' Association" has reached about 200,000. They can no longer endure poverty.
    The dispatch also quoted the American *New Republic* weekly as admitting that the Philippine Islands are on the verge of bankruptcy due to the Quirino government's squandering, and the strength of the people's armed forces is growing.

### 纽约工人日报  揭露美帝侵菲阴谋

1950-07-08
第4版()
专栏：

　　纽约工人日报
    揭露美帝侵菲阴谋
    【新华社四日讯】据合众社纽约六月二十八日讯：纽约《工人日报》在题为《解放的风吹遍太平洋》的华盛顿电讯中，揭露了美国国务院正在采取一切形式，对菲律宾的民族解放运动实行进一步的“强制干涉”。该文指出：菲律宾的重大事件也正在发展中。据一反动记者估计，菲律宾“农民协会”的会员人数已达二十万左右。他们已不能再忍受贫困。
    该电又引美国新共和周刊承认：菲律宾群岛由于季里诺政府的浪费已濒于破产，人民武装的力量正在壮大中。
