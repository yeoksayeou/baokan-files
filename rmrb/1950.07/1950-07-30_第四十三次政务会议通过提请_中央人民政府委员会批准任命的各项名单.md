Title: The 43rd Government Affairs Council Approves List of Appointments Submitted to the Central People's Government Committee for Approval
Author:
Date: 1950-07-30
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, 28th -- The 43rd Government Affairs Council approved the following list of appointments to be submitted to the Central People's Government Committee for approval:

　　I. List of Chairman, Vice-Chairmen, and Members of the Chahar Provincial People's Government
　　Chairman: Zhang Su, formerly Chairman of the Chahar Provincial People's Government.
　　Vice-Chairmen: Wang Ping, currently Commander of the People's Liberation Army Chahar Military Region.
　　　　　　　　Lü Fu, veteran member of the Tongmenghui.
　　Members: Seventeen persons (in order of stroke count of their surnames)
　　　　　　　　Wang Junkui, currently Professor at the Engineering College of Peking University.
　　　　　　　　Wang Mengling, currently Advisor to the Yongxing Electric Factory in Tianjin.
　　　　　　　　Li Peizhi (female), currently Deputy Director of the Academic Affairs Department of the Chinese People's University, Executive Committee Member of the All-China Democratic Women's Federation.
　　　　　　　　Li Guanying, currently Deputy Mayor of Zhangjiakou City People's Government, Chahar Province.
　　　　　　　　Li Shunqin, currently Director of the Department of Culture and Education of the Chahar Provincial People's Government.
　　　　　　　　Li Jiesan, formerly a member of the North China People's Government Council.
　　　　　　　　Du Xiangguang, currently Director of the Department of Finance of the Chahar Provincial People's Government.
　　　　　　　　Xing Yuhong, currently Secretary-General of the Chahar Provincial People's Government.
　　　　　　　　Sun Ming, currently Chairman of the Chahar Provincial Federation of Trade Unions.
　　　　　　　　Sun Jingwen, currently Mayor of Zhangjiakou City People's Government, Chahar Province.
　　　　　　　　Zhang Xianjin, currently Director of the Public Security Department of the Chahar Provincial People's Government.
　　　　　　　　Guo Yinnan, currently Chairman of the Board of Baoxing Coal Mine, Chahar Province.
　　　　　　　　Yang Shijie, currently Director of the Organization Department of the Chahar Provincial Committee of the Chinese Communist Party.
　　　　　　　　Yang Gengtian, currently Secretary of the Chahar Provincial Committee of the Chinese Communist Party.
　　　　　　　　Jia Yibo, currently Director of the Department of Commerce of the Chahar Provincial People's Government.
　　　　　　　　Zhao Ziqian, currently Standing Committee Member of the Chahar Provincial People's Representative Conference Consultative Committee.
　　　　　　　　Han Yijun, currently Director of the Department of Agriculture of the Chahar Provincial People's Government.

　　II. List of President of Guangxi University
　　President: Yang Dongchun, currently Member of the Cultural and Educational Committee of the Central-South Military and Administrative Committee.
