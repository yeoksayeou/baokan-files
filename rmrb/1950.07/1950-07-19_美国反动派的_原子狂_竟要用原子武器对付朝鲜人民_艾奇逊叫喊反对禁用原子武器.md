Title: American Reactionaries' "Atomic Mania": Actually Wanting to Use Atomic Weapons Against the Korean People; Acheson Shouts Against Banning Atomic Weapons
Author:
Date: 1950-07-19
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, New York, 16th TASS: American reactionaries are currently putting forward various wild plans, including barbaric suggestions to use atomic weapons against the Korean people. These suggestions are encouraged by Acheson's statement at a press conference on July 12th, which specified that the United States opposes declaring atomic weapons illegal. Acheson's statement reflects a frantic "desire" to stop the growing mass movement in support of the Stockholm Appeal.

Acheson's subordinates later clarified Acheson's statement more clearly, telling reporters that the United States retains the right to use atomic bombs like any other weapon.

In the current atmosphere, Congress is more willing than before to approve any proposals that deprive people of democratic rights. On July 12th, the House of Representatives passed a bill by a vote of 327 to 14, granting eight government agencies the arbitrary power to dismiss any employee they deem "may endanger security"; and rejected an amendment that would grant dismissed employees the right to appeal.

"Atomic hysteria" and reactionary legislation are arousing the indignation of American democrats.

### 美国反动派的“原子狂”  竟要用原子武器对付朝鲜人民  艾奇逊叫喊反对禁用原子武器

1950-07-19
第4版()
专栏：

　　美国反动派的“原子狂”
    竟要用原子武器对付朝鲜人民
    艾奇逊叫喊反对禁用原子武器
    【新华社十八日讯】塔斯社纽约十六日讯：美国反动派目前正在提出各种狂妄的计划，包括使用原子武器对付朝鲜人民的野蛮建议。这些建议得到艾奇逊七月十二日在记者招待会上发表的声明的鼓励，该声明指明，美国反对宣布原子武器为非法。艾奇逊的声明反映了一种要阻止日益增长的拥护斯德哥尔摩宣言的群众运动的疯狂“愿望”。
    艾奇逊的下属后来把艾奇逊的声明阐明得更为清楚，他们告记者称：美国保有使用原子弹一如其他任何武器的权利。
    在目前的气氛下，国会是比以前更乐于赞成任何剥夺民主权利的建议的。七月十二日，众院以三百二十七票对十四票通过一项法案，赋予八个政府机关以开除任何它们认为“可能危害治安”的雇员的专横权力；并否决了建议给予解雇雇员以上诉权的修正案。
    “原子歇斯底里”与反动立法正激起美国的民主分子的愤慨。
