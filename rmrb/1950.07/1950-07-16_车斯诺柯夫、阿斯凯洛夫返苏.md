Title: Chesnokov and Askelov Return to the Soviet Union
Author:
Date: 1950-07-16
Page: 1

　　Xinhua News Agency, Shenyang, 15th. Soviet philosopher Professor Chesnokov and legal scholar Professor Askelov have completed their lecture schedule in our country and left Shenyang for the Soviet Union on the 14th and 15th respectively. The two professors gave a total of six lectures in Shenyang, with an audience of 8,000 people. The Education Department of the Northeast People's Government and the Northeast General Branch of the Sino-Soviet Friendship Association presented two banners to the two professors respectively to thank them for their lectures. During their stay in Shenyang, the two professors visited factories, city streets, and the Northeast Museum in Shenyang.

### 车斯诺柯夫、阿斯凯洛夫返苏

1950-07-16
第1版()
专栏：

　　车斯诺柯夫、阿斯凯洛夫返苏
    【新华社沈阳十五日电】苏联哲学家车斯诺柯夫及法学家阿斯凯洛夫两教授已完成在我国的讲学日程，分别于十四、十五两日离沈阳返苏。两教授在沈阳共讲演六次，听众达八千人。东北人民政府教育部及中苏友好协会东北总分会为感谢两教授的讲学，曾分别向两教授献赠锦旗两幅。两教授留沈期间，曾参观沈阳市工厂、市街及东北博物馆。
