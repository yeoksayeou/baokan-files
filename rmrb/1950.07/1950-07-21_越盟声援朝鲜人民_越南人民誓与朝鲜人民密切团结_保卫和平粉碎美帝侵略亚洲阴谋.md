Title: Viet Minh Voices Support for the Korean People; Vietnamese People Vow Close Solidarity with the Korean People; Defend Peace and Crush the American Imperialist Conspiracy to Invade Asia
Author:
Date: 1950-07-21
Page: 4

　　Viet Minh Voices Support for the Korean People
    Vietnamese People Vow Close Solidarity with the Korean People
    Defend Peace and Crush the American Imperialist Conspiracy to Invade Asia
    [Xinhua News Agency, Shanghai, July 20th] (TASS dispatch from Shanghai, July 17th): According to the Vietnam News Agency: The Secretary-General of the Central Executive Committee of the Viet Minh Front, representing the Vietnam National United Front, sent a telegram to the army and people of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea, stating: "The Vietnamese people are extremely angry at the American imperialists' aggression in Korea." The telegram strongly stated that the Vietnamese people vow to closely unite with the Korean people and all Asian people to fight courageously for the complete liberation of their motherland, to crush the American imperialist conspiracy to invade Asia, and to defend world peace and democracy.

### 越盟声援朝鲜人民  越南人民誓与朝鲜人民密切团结  保卫和平粉碎美帝侵略亚洲阴谋

1950-07-21
第4版()
专栏：

　　越盟声援朝鲜人民
    越南人民誓与朝鲜人民密切团结 
    保卫和平粉碎美帝侵略亚洲阴谋
    【新华社二十日讯】塔斯社上海十七日电：据越南通讯社讯：越盟阵线中央执行委员会总书记，代表越南民族统一阵线向朝鲜民主主义人民共和国军队和人民致电称：“越南人民对美帝国主义者在朝鲜的侵略异常愤怒”。电文力言越南人民誓与朝鲜人民以及所有亚洲人民密切团结起来，为争取祖国的完全解放、粉碎美帝国主义在亚洲的侵略阴谋及保卫世界和平与民主，进行果敢的战斗。
