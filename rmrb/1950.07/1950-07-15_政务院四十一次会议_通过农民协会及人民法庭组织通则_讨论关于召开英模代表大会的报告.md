Title: The 41st Meeting of the Government Administration Council Passed the "General Regulations for the Organization of Farmers' Associations and People's Courts" and Discussed the Report on Convening a Conference of Model Workers and Heroes

Author:
Date: 1950-07-15
Page: 1

　　The 41st Meeting of the Government Administration Council
    Passed the "General Regulations for the Organization of Farmers' Associations and People's Courts"
     Discussed the Report on Convening a Conference of Model Workers and Heroes
    [Xinhua News Agency, 14th] The 41st Political Affairs Meeting of the Government Administration Council of the Central People's Government was held at 3 p.m. on the 14th. The meeting passed the "General Regulations for the Organization of Farmers' Associations" and the "General Regulations for the Organization of People's Courts." After listening to the report by Comrade Li Lisan on the issues related to convening the National Congress of Combat Heroes and the National Congress of Model Workers, the meeting discussed the matter and assigned specific individuals to revise the relevant resolutions and plans. In addition, the meeting passed several appointment cases and submitted several appointment cases to the Central People's Government Committee for approval.

### 政务院四十一次会议  通过农民协会及人民法庭组织通则  讨论关于召开英模代表大会的报告

1950-07-15
第1版()
专栏：

　　政务院四十一次会议
    通过农民协会及人民法庭组织通则       
     讨论关于召开英模代表大会的报告
    【新华社十四日讯】中央人民政府政务院第四十一次政务会议于十四日下午三时举行。会议通过了农民协会组织通则及人民法庭组织通则。会议在听取了李立三委员关于召开全国战斗英雄代表大会与全国劳动模范代表大会问题的报告后，曾予讨论，并指定专人修改有关这一问题的决议和计划。此外，会议通过任命案及提请中央人民政府委员会批准任命案多起。
