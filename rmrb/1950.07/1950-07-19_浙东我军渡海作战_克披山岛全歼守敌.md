Title: Our East Zhejiang Troops Cross the Sea to Fight: Capturing Pishan Island and Annihilating All Enemy Troops
Author:
Date: 1950-07-19
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, Nanjing, 18th. A unit of the Third Field Army of the People's Liberation Army launched an attack on the Kuomintang remnants entrenched on Pishan Island, outside Nan'ai Wan in Wenling County, Zhejiang, at 6:00 PM on the 15th of this month. The People's Liberation Army successfully landed at 4:00 AM on the 16th, annihilating all the defending enemy and capturing the island. A total of over 400 officers and soldiers, including the bandit Ou Jiang Commander Lin Zhuo, were captured, and over 50 officers and soldiers, including the bandit Lin Lai, the head of Yuhuan County, were killed. According to preliminary statistics, our army seized eight machine guns, one anti-aircraft machine gun, thirty-one submachine guns, twenty-six carbines, more than twenty short guns, more than two hundred rifles, more than 55,000 rounds of ammunition, and a portion of other military supplies. In addition, one bandit "Xin Qiaoshi" gunboat was sunk, one was damaged, and one gunboat was captured.

### 浙东我军渡海作战  克披山岛全歼守敌

1950-07-19
第1版()
专栏：

　　浙东我军渡海作战
    克披山岛全歼守敌
    【新华社南京十八日电】人民解放军第三野战军一部，于本月十五日下午六时渡海向盘踞于浙江温岭县南隘顽湾外披山岛的国民党残匪发动攻击。解放军于十六日上午四时胜利登陆，将守敌全部歼灭后攻占该岛。计俘匪瓯江指挥官林卓以下官兵四百余人，毙匪玉环县长林涞等以下官兵五十余名。据初步统计，我军缴获机枪八挺、高射机枪一挺、冲锋枪三十一支、卡宾枪二十六支、短枪二十余支、步枪二百余支、子弹五万五千余发及其他军用物资一部。此外并击沉匪“新峭石”号炮艇一艘，击伤一艘，俘炮艇一艘。
