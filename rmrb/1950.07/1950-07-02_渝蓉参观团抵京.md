Title: Chongqing-Chengdu Visiting Group Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1950-07-02
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, July 1st -- The Chongqing-Chengdu visiting group, composed of representatives from industrial, commercial, cultural, and educational circles of Chongqing and Chengdu, arrived in Beijing a few days ago. The group consists of 52 people, led by Cheng Zhanbiao, Director of the Enterprise Bureau of the Chongqing Municipal People's Government, as the head, and Liao Jiamin, Secretary-General of the Chengdu Municipal People's Government, and Li Zhiqin, Deputy Chairman of the Preparatory Committee of the Chongqing Federation of Industry and Commerce, as the deputy heads. The group plans to visit Beijing and Tianjin for twenty days to a month, and then go to Northeast China for a visit.

### 渝蓉参观团抵京

1950-07-02
第1版()
专栏：

　　渝蓉参观团抵京
    【新华社一日讯】重庆、成都两市工商、文教界人士合组的渝蓉参观团已于日前抵京。该团一行五十二人，由重庆市人民政府企业局局长程占彪任团长，成都市人民政府秘书长廖家岷和重庆市工商联合会筹备委员会副主任委员李志亲任副团长。该团计划在京津两市参观二十天至一月，然后转赴东北参观。
