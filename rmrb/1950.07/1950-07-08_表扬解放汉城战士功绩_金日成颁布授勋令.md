Title: Kim Il-sung Issues Order Conferring Medals to Commend Soldiers for Liberating Seoul
Author:
Date: 1950-07-08
Page: 4

　　Xinhua News Agency, Pyongyang, July 7th: Kim Il-sung, Supreme Commander of the Korean Democratic People's Republic People's Army, issued an order on the 5th regarding conferring honorary titles to units, stating: Officers and soldiers of the Third Division, Fourth Division, and 105th Tank Brigade of the Korean People's Army engaged in heroic combat during the attack to liberate the capital, Seoul, and were the first to enter Seoul. To commend their great achievements, the following honorary titles are hereby conferred to each of the units: 1. The Third Division and the Fourth Division are respectively awarded the titles of Seoul Third Division and Seoul Fourth Division. 2. The 105th Tank Brigade is elevated to a Tank Division and awarded the title of Seoul 105th Tank Division. 3. The commanders of the Seoul Third Division, Seoul Fourth Division, and Seoul 105th Tank Division should recommend officers, non-commissioned officers, and soldiers from their original units who have made great contributions in the battle to liberate Seoul, so that they may be awarded national citations.

### 表扬解放汉城战士功绩  金日成颁布授勋令

1950-07-08
第4版()
专栏：

　　表扬解放汉城战士功绩
    金日成颁布授勋令
    【新华社平壤七日电】朝鲜民主主义人民共和国人民军最高司令官金日成于五日发布关于授予部队称号的命令称：朝鲜人民军第三师团、第四师团及第一零五坦克旅团的官兵，在解放祖国首都汉城的攻击战中，进行了英勇的战斗，并首先攻入汉城市。为表扬他们的伟大功勋，兹特授予各该部队的称号如下：一、第三师团第四师团，分别授予汉城第三师团与汉城第四师团的称号。二、第一零五坦克旅团提升为坦克师团，并授予汉城第一零五坦克师团的称号。三、汉城第三师团、汉城第四师团、汉城第一零五坦克师团的指挥官，应就原来所属的军官、下士及战士中推荐在解放汉城的战斗中建立伟大功勋者，以便授予国家奖状。
