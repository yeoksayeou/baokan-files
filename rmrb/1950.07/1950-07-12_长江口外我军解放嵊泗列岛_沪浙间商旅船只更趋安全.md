Title: Our Army Liberates the Shengsi Islands Outside the Yangtze River Estuary; Merchant and Traveler Ships Between Shanghai and Zhejiang Become Safer
Author:
Date: 1950-07-12
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, 11th dispatch: A unit of the People's Liberation Army's Third Field Army, in coordination with the People's Navy, completely occupied the Shengsi Islands outside the Yangtze River Estuary on the 7th and 8th. The remnants of the Kuomintang bandits who fled to the islands have been mostly annihilated. Detailed battle results are still being tallied. Note: The Shengsi Islands include more than 80 islands of varying sizes, including Shengshan, Dayangshan, Xiaoyangshan, Sijiaoshan, and Huanglongshan. After the liberation of Zhoushan, some remnant Kuomintang bandits and secret agents entrenched themselves here, robbing coastal ships and ravaging the island residents. After the liberation of these islands, the passage of merchant and traveler ships between Shanghai and Zhejiang has become safer. Nearby fishermen have been going out to sea to fish in droves.

### 长江口外我军解放嵊泗列岛  沪浙间商旅船只更趋安全

1950-07-12
第1版()
专栏：

　　长江口外我军解放嵊泗列岛
    沪浙间商旅船只更趋安全
    【新华社十一日讯】人民解放军第三野战军一部，在人民海军配合下，已于七、八两日全部占领长江口外的嵊泗列岛。亡命海岛的国民党残匪已大部就歼。详细战果正清查中。按：嵊泗列岛包括嵊山、大洋山、小洋山、泗嶕山、黄龙山等大小岛屿共八十余。舟山解放后，国民党部分残余土匪特务即盘踞于此，抢劫沿海船只，蹂躏海岛居民。该岛解放后，上海、浙江间商旅船只行驶已益趋安全。附近渔民已纷纷下海捕鱼。
