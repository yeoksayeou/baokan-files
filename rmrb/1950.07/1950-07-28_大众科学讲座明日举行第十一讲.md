Date: 1950-07-28

Mass Science Lecture No. 11 to be Held Tomorrow
　　The mass science lecture co-sponsored by the Youth Service Department and the Science Popularization Bureau is scheduled to hold its eleventh lecture at the Sino-Soviet Friendship House (19 South River Bank) at 4:00 p.m. on Saturday, July 29th. Peking University Associate Professor Zhu Guangya will speak on "The Light and Heat of the Sun." A film related to the lecture topic will be shown after the meeting. Admission tickets can be collected during youth opening hours on July 28th (Friday). (Group tickets require a letter of introduction) (China Youth Agency)

### 大众科学讲座明日举行第十一讲

1950-07-28
第6版()
专栏：

　　大众科学讲座明日举行第十一讲
    青年服务部与科学普及局合办的大众科学讲座定于七月二十九日（星期六）下午四时在苏联对外文化协会（南河沿十九号）举行第十一讲。请北大副教授朱光亚讲“太阳的光和热”。会后放映配合讲题的电影。入场券可于七二十八日（星期五）在青年开放时间内领取。（团体票要凭介绍信）        （中国青年社）
