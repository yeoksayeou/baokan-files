Author: Zhang Xiruo
Date: 1950-07-03
Page: 5

　　Effective Ways to Defend World Peace
　　Zhang Xiruo
　　The world is now divided into two major camps: peace and aggression. The peace camp seeks to protect the interests of the broad masses of people, while the aggressive camp seeks to maintain the interests of a few capitalists. One aims to preserve human culture, while the other aims to destroy it. One wants the people to live, while the other wants them to die.
　　The most effective way to oppose death, to oppose the destruction of human culture, and to oppose being slaves to international capitalists is: to defend world peace. The peace signature campaign is a very good way to defend world peace.

### 保卫世界和平有效办法
张奚若
1950-07-03
第5版()
专栏：

　　保卫世界和平有效办法
    张奚若
    现在世界分为和平和侵略两大阵营：和平阵营要保障广大人民的利益，侵略阵营要维持少数资本家的利益；一个要保全人类文化，一个要毁灭人类文化；一个要人民活，一个要人民死。
    反对死，反对毁灭人类文化，反对为国际资本家做奴隶的最有效的办法是：保卫世界和平。和平签名运动就是保卫世界和平的一个很好的办法。
