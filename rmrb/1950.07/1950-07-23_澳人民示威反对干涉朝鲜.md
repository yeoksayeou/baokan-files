Title: Australian People Demonstrate Against Intervention in Korea
Author:
Date: 1950-07-23
Page: 4

　　Xinhua News Agency, Sydney, July 14th (Tass): Under the auspices of the Queensland Peace Council, a mass demonstration was held in the city of Brisbane on the 13th to protest against American intervention in Korea. Many demonstrators held signs reading: "No intervention in Korea!" Police arrested Eaton, secretary of the Queensland Returned Servicemen's Association, for delivering a speech at the rally.

### 澳人民示威反对干涉朝鲜

1950-07-23
第4版()
专栏：

　　澳人民示威反对干涉朝鲜
    【新华社讯】塔斯社悉尼十四日电：在昆士兰拥护和平委员会的主持下，布利斯班城于十三日举行了一个群众示威大会，抗议美国在朝鲜的干涉。许多示威者手拿标语牌，上书：“不要干涉朝鲜！”警察逮捕了昆士兰退伍军人协会秘书伊顿，因他曾在大会上发表演说。
