Title: China Democratic Party Strengthens Anti-Aggression Propaganda
Author:
Date: 1950-07-24
Page: 2

　　The Beijing Municipal Party Consolidation Committee of the China Democratic Party, in response to the call of the "Committee for the Movement Against American Aggression in Taiwan and Korea," issued a notice on the 17th, calling on all comrades of the party to conduct in-depth and extensive propaganda to the masses at any time and any place, using all possible opportunities, based on the propaganda points issued by the "Committee for the Movement Against American Aggression in Taiwan and Korea," and in conjunction with the discussion summary of the "Crushing American Imperialist Armed Aggression" forum held by the association on July 4. The notice also called on them to mobilize family members and friends to participate in the propaganda work during the propaganda week.

### 农工民主党加强反侵略宣传

1950-07-24
第2版()
专栏：

　　农工民主党加强反侵略宣传
    【本报讯】中国农工民主党北京市党务整理委员会为响应“反对美国侵略台湾朝鲜运动委员会”的号召，于十七日发出通知，号召该党全体同志根据“反美侵略台湾朝鲜运动委员会”发布的宣传要点，并结合七月四日该会举行“粉碎美帝武装侵略”座谈会的讨论总结等，在宣传周内，随时随地，利用一切可能机会，向群众进行深入的、广泛的宣传，并动员家属亲友参加宣传工作。
