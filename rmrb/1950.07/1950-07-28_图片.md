Title: Ding County Zhaicheng Women's Mutual Aid Hoeing Seedlings
Author:
Date: 1950-07-28
Page: 2

Ding County Zhaicheng women's mutual aid teams actively carried out seedling hoeing work. The women in the village enthusiastically participated in the mutual aid teams, braving the scorching sun and working hard to hoe the seedlings. They have now hoeing more than 1,000 mu of seedlings, ensuring the healthy growth of the seedlings.
