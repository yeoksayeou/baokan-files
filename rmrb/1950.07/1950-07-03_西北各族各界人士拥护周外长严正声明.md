Title: People of All Ethnic Groups and All Walks of Life in Northwest China Support Foreign Minister Zhou's Solemn Statement
Author:
Date: 1950-07-03
Page: 3
Xinhua News Agency, Xi'an, 30th. People of all ethnic groups and all walks of life in Northwest China unanimously support Minister Zhou Enlai's solemn statement of the 28th of this month, and have strongly condemned Truman's statement of aggression against other countries' sovereignty. The Northwest General Branch of the China Democratic League, the Shaanxi-Gansu-Ningxia Border Region Cultural Association, the Preparatory Committee of the Northwest Democratic Women's Federation, the Northwest Working Committee of the New Democratic Youth League, and representatives of various ethnic groups such as Uyghur, Hui, Tibetan, Salar, and Uzbek who recently came to Xi'an to attend the Northwest Ethnic Affairs Committee meeting, all held separate symposiums on this matter, calling on members of their respective groups and people of all ethnic groups to rise up together to expel the American aggressors and support the People's Liberation Army in recovering Taiwan Province. Buzadin (Uyghur), Ye Futian (Hui), Huang Zhengqing (Tibetan), Mu Huaxi (Kazak), Han Youlu (Salar), Jia Rao (Tibetan), Wu Hongye (Hui), Fa He Li Ding He Jia (Uzbek), and others all unanimously stated that all ethnic minorities have full confidence in the liberation of all of China's territory, and they will continue to support the People's Liberation Army with all their strength to plant the five-star national flag all over the motherland's territory. Yang Mingxuan, Chairman of the Xi'an Branch of the China Peace Committee, Kang Xinzai, Chairman of the Xi'an People's Representative Conference Consultative Committee, Tohubatihai, a representative of the Xinjiang ethnic youth delegation attending the meeting, and Pan Yuanquan, Chairman of the Xi'an Federation of Industry and Commerce, all issued written statements, unanimously believing that this shameless provocative act of American imperialism can only represent their dying struggle and will absolutely not be able to stop the liberation movement of the Asian people. The Chinese people, in order to win independence and integrity of sovereignty and territory, should give a severe blow to this shameless bandit behavior.

### 西北各族各界人士拥护周外长严正声明

1950-07-03
第3版()
专栏：

　　西北各族各界人士拥护周外长严正声明
    【新华社西安三十日电】西北各族各界人士一致拥护周恩来部长本月二十八日的严正声明，和纷纷指斥杜鲁门侵略他国主权的声明。中国民主同盟西北总支部、陕甘宁边区文化协会、西北民主妇女联合会筹委会、新民主主义青年团西北工作委员会及最近来到西安出席西北民族事务委员会议的维吾尔、回、藏、撒拉、乌兹别克等各民族代表人士，均为此分别举行座谈会，号召各该团体成员和各族人民一致奋起，驱逐美国侵略者，支援人民解放军收复台湾省。布札丁（维）、冶福天（回）、黄正清（藏）、穆华希（哈萨克）、韩有禄（撒拉）、嘉饶（藏）、吴鸿业（回）、法和李丁和加（乌兹别克）等，都一致表示，各少数民族对解放全中国的领土有着充分信心，他们将以全力继续支援人民解放军，把五星国旗插遍祖国的全部领土。中国保卫世界和平大会西安分会主席杨明轩、西安市各界人民代表会议协商委员会主席亢心哉、来此参加会议的新疆各族青年代表团代表托乎巴提海、西安工商联合会主席潘源泉等，均发表书面谈话，一致认为美帝国主义这种无耻的挑衅行为，只能表示他们垂死的挣扎，绝对阻止不了亚洲人民的解放运动。中国人民为了争取主权领土的独立和完整，对这种无耻的强盗行为，当给以严重的打击。
