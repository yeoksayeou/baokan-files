Title: General Post Office Expands Anti-Aggression Propaganda; Circular Orders Localities to Print and Distribute Propaganda Slogan Strips
Author:
Date: 1950-07-18
Page: 1

　　General Post Office Expands Anti-Aggression Propaganda
    Circular Orders Localities to Print and Distribute Propaganda Slogan Strips
    [Reported by this newspaper] In response to the call for "Oppose American Aggression Against Taiwan and Korea Movement Week," the General Post Office of the Ministry of Posts and Telecommunications has decided to expand propaganda and enable the masses to gain a deeper understanding of the significant meaning of Movement Week. A telegram has been sent to post offices of the first class and above in all localities, ordering them to print the propaganda slogans uniformly stipulated by the General Post Office into small strips. Starting from July 17th, for seven days, these strips should be given to senders to be affixed to letters together with postage stamps.

### 邮政总局扩大反侵略宣传  通令各地印发宣传口号条

1950-07-18
第1版()
专栏：

　　邮政总局扩大反侵略宣传
    通令各地印发宣传口号条
    【本报讯】邮电部邮政总局为响应“反对美国侵略台湾朝鲜运动周”的号召，扩大宣传，使群众更深入了解运动周的重大意义，特电通知各地一等以上邮局将总局统一规定的宣传口号，印成小条，从七月十七日起七天内发给寄件人将口号和邮票一同贴在信件上。
