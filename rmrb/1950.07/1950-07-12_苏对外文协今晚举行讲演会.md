Title: Sino-Soviet Friendship Association to Hold Lecture Tonight
Author: Chen Yingnan
Date: 1950-07-12
Page: 6

　　Sino-Soviet Friendship Association to Hold Lecture Tonight
    Twenty-six years ago, on this day in 1924 (July 12th), the Sixth National Congress of the Russian Communist Youth League renamed the Russian Communist Youth League to the Russian Lenin Communist Youth League. The Beijing Sino-Soviet Friendship Association and the Soviet Foreign Cultural Association, in celebration of this glorious day, have scheduled a lecture at 8:00 PM today (the 12th) at 19 Nanheyan Street. Aniskin will speak on "The Soviet Communist Youth League." All those holding admission tickets or "Introduction to the Soviet Union" long-term lecture passes are welcome to attend.
                          Chen Yingnan
    (Note: It was changed to the Soviet Lenin Communist Youth League starting in March 1926)

### 苏对外文协今晚举行讲演会
陈应楠
1950-07-12
第6版()
专栏：

　　苏对外文协今晚举行讲演会
    二十六年前，一九二四年的今天（七月十二日），俄国共产主义青年团第六次全国代表大会将俄国共产主义青年团改名为俄国列宁共产主义青年团。北京市中苏友协与苏联对外文化协会为庆祝这个光辉的日子，定于今日（十二日）下午八时在南河沿十九号举行讲演会。由阿尼斯金讲：“苏联共产主义青年团”。凡持有入场券或“苏联介绍”长期听讲证者，届时均可前往参加。
                          陈应楠
    （按：于一九二六年三月始改为苏联列宁共产主义青年团）
