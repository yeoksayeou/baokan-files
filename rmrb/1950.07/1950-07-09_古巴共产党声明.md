Title: Statement by the Communist Party of Cuba
Author: 
Date: 1950-07-09
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, New York, 2nd (TASS): The Cuban newspaper "Hoy" published a statement by Marinello, Chairman of the People's Socialist Party of Cuba (i.e., the Communist Party), strongly condemning Truman's decision to order the US armed forces to carry out aggression in Korea. The statement said: The aggression of the Washington imperialist government directly threatens the independence of the people of all countries. The people of Cuba, Latin America and other countries who advocate peace must unite more strongly than ever before. If the people of all countries can show a firm determination not to allow war at this critical moment, then the despicable provocations of American imperialism will surely be repelled.

### 古巴共产党声明

1950-07-09
第1版()
专栏：

     古巴共产党声明
    【新华社四日讯】塔斯社纽约二日讯：古巴《今日新闻报》刊载古巴人民社会党（即共产党）主席马利涅洛的声明，猛烈抨击杜鲁门下令美国的武装力量在朝鲜实行侵略的决定。声明说：华盛顿帝国主义政府的侵略，直接威胁世界各国人民的独立。古巴、拉丁美洲和其他各国主张和平的人民，必须比已往更为有力地团结起来。如果在这严重关头各国人民能够表现出不准战争的坚定决心，那么，美国帝国主义的卑劣的挑衅就一定会被击退。
