Title: Opposition to Armed Intervention in Korea: Growing Mass Movement in the United States; People of Australia, Norway, and Denmark Protest American Imperialist Aggression
Author:
Date: 1950-07-07
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, July 6th: Opposition to Armed Intervention in Korea

Growing Mass Movement in the United States
People of Australia, Norway, and Denmark Protest American Imperialist Aggression

[Xinhua News Agency, July 6th] A growing mass movement opposing American armed intervention in Korea and defending peace is underway in the United States. According to a TASS dispatch from New York on July 5th: The Daily Worker reported that a rally protesting armed intervention in Korea was held in the Harlem district of New York under the slogan "Hands Off Korea." The rally was organized by the Council on African Affairs, the Harlem Trade Union Council, the Democratic Far Eastern Policy Committee, and the Harlem Provisional Committee for Peace and Democracy. Over 1,500 participants greeted Paul Robeson, member of the National Committee of the Communist Party of the United States and chairman of the Council on African Affairs, with thunderous applause. Robeson said that black people knew what was happening in Korea was the same as what their people were encountering in Africa... The imperialists want our children to die for their outrageous profits.

Davis stated in his speech that we need peace and freedom, and we are prepared to fight for it. The participants passed a resolution demanding that American troops withdraw from Korea and give the Korean people the opportunity to decide their own destiny.

In Wisconsin, two “Electrical Workers Union District Councils” met and passed resolutions demanding the withdrawal of American troops from Korea and a ban on the use of atomic bombs.

Meanwhile, the National Labor Conference for Peace, which is currently organizing a signature campaign of 1.5 million people, declared that the signature campaign has progressed rapidly since the outbreak of the Korean War. Sherer, the New York representative attending the Labor Conference for Peace, stated that according to those responsible for collecting signatures, the general American public does not know why American blood should be shed on Korean soil.

According to a United Press dispatch from San Francisco on July 1st: The Marine Cooks and Stewards Union, affiliated with the CIO, opposes the war policies currently pursued by the American government. The union has passed a resolution demanding that the Truman government immediately cease armed intervention in Korea.

[Xinhua News Agency, July 6th] TASS, Sydney: On July 4th, the Australian people held demonstrations in Melbourne and Sydney against American intervention in Korea. Demonstrators carried signs with slogans such as "Hands Off Asia" and distributed leaflets calling on the people to "Stop the war of aggression against Asian independence."

Demonstrators held a rally near the American Consulate in Sydney under the slogan "Hands Off Korea." Police arrested one speaker.

[Xinhua News Agency, July 6th] Compiled TASS reports: The Danish branch of the World Federation of Democratic Youth sent a telegram to the Korean Democratic Youth League expressing its indignation at the open aggression of American imperialists against Korea, and declaring that it would oppose the enemies of peace and stop the threat of atomic war with even greater force.

A mass meeting was held in Oslo, the capital of Norway, on July 3rd to protest American imperialist aggression against Korea. The meeting unanimously passed a resolution stating that American imperialists are responsible for the Korean War and demanding that Norway not support or participate in any way in the imperialist war against the peoples of the East. The resolution called on all peace advocates to sign the Stockholm Appeal.

### 反对武装干涉朝鲜  美国群众运动增长  澳、挪、丹人民抗议美帝侵略

1950-07-07
第1版()
专栏：

　　反对武装干涉朝鲜
    美国群众运动增长
    澳、挪、丹人民抗议美帝侵略
    【新华社六日讯】反对美国武装干涉朝鲜、保卫和平的群众运动正在美国增长。据塔斯社纽约五日电：工人日报报道，纽约哈莱姆区在“不许干涉朝鲜”的口号下举行抗议武装干涉朝鲜的集会。这次集会是非洲事务委员会、哈莱姆工会会议、民主远东政策委员会及哈莱姆争取和平与民主临时委员会所组织的。参加集会的一千五百余人以暴风雨般鼓掌欢迎美国共产党全国委员会委员戴维斯和非洲事务委员会主席、歌唱家罗伯逊。罗伯逊说，黑人知道朝鲜国内正在发生的事情是与他们人民在非洲所遭遇的一样……帝国主义者要我们的儿女为他们的惊人利润而死。
    戴维斯的发言称，我们需要和平与自由，而且我们准备为此进行斗争。参加集会的人们通过了一项决议，要求美军退出朝鲜并给朝鲜人民以机会，让他们自己决定他们的命运。
    在威斯康辛州，两个“电气工人工会区委员会”开会通过决议，要求美军撤出朝鲜和禁用原子弹。
    同时，目前正在组织一百五十万人签名运动的全国劳工和平会议宣称：自朝鲜战事爆发以来，签名运动进展迅速。出席劳工和平会议的纽约代表谢勒尔声称：据负责征集签名的人员说，美国一般人民不知道为什么美国人的血要洒在朝鲜的土地上。
    另据合众社旧金山一日电：属于产联的海上厨师与侍者工会反对美国政府目前所奉行的战争政策。该工会已通过决议，要求杜鲁门政府立即停止武装干涉朝鲜。
    【新华社六日讯】塔斯社悉尼讯：澳大利亚人民七月四日在墨尔本和悉尼举行了反对美国干涉朝鲜的示威。示威者拿着“不准干涉亚洲”的标语牌，并散发传单，号召人民“制止侵犯亚洲独立的战争”。
    示威者在悉尼美国领事馆附近在“不准干涉朝鲜”的口号下举行了大会。警察逮捕了一名演说者。
    【新华社六日讯】综合塔斯社消息：世界民主青年联盟丹麦分会致电朝鲜民主青年联盟，对美国帝国主义者公开侵犯朝鲜一事表示愤慨，并声明当以更大的力量来反对和平敌人和制止原子战争威胁。
    挪威京城奥斯陆于七月三日举行群众大会，抗议美帝侵略朝鲜。大会一致通过决议，指出美国帝国主义者应对朝鲜战争负责，并要求挪威不以任何方式支持或参加反对东方各族人民的帝国主义战争。决议号召一切和平拥护者在斯德哥尔摩宣言上签名。
