Title: Captured Syngman Rhee Puppet Army Officers and Soldiers Hold Meeting Demanding to Go to the Front Lines to Fight the Aggressors
Author:
Date: 1950-07-31
Page: 4

　　【Xinhua News Agency, Pyongyang, July 29th, TASS】Captured officers and soldiers of Syngman Rhee's so-called "National Defense Army" recently held a conference in Jeonju, expressing their willingness to fight against the US imperialists and Syngman Rhee's army.

　　Prisoner of war Jin Tangsheng said: "We were deceived by Syngman Rhee and used our weapons to fight against the motherland and the people. We feel ashamed of having served in the 'National Defense Army' in the past. We want to participate in the fight against the American imperialists and the Syngman Rhee bandit gang to atone for our sins to the motherland and the people."

　　Lieutenant Lin Zhangdan, Jin Dangning and other prisoners of war also expressed their anger at the crimes of the US imperialists and the Syngman Rhee bandits, and requested to be sent to the front lines to fight the invaders.

　　Participants in the conference wrote a letter to Chairman Kim Il-sung, requesting to be sent to the front lines. They also passed a declaration advising the "National Defense Army" to stop resisting.

### 被俘李承晚伪军官兵集会要求上前线对侵略者作战

1950-07-31
第4版()
专栏：

　　被俘李承晚伪军官兵集会要求上前线对侵略者作战
    【新华社三十日讯】塔斯社平壤二十九日讯：被俘的李承晚的所谓“国防军”官兵们，最近在全州举行大会，表示他们愿意对美帝国主义者和李承晚军队作战。
    战俘金汤生说：“我们受了李承晚的欺骗，把我们的武器拿来打祖国和人民。我们对于过去在‘国防军’里面当兵，感到羞耻。我们要参加对美国帝国主义者和李承晚匪帮作战，向祖国和人民赎罪。”
    林璋丹、金当宁中尉和其他的战俘也对美帝国主义者和李承晚匪徒们的罪行，表示愤怒，并要求派往前线和侵略者作战。
    参加大会的人写信给金日成主席，要求派往前线。他们并通过劝告“国防军”停止抵抗的宣言。
