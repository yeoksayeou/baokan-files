Title: Two Soviet Professors Arrive in Shenyang to Lecture

Author:
Date: 1950-07-14
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Shenyang, July 13th—Two Soviet professors, philosopher Chesnokov and jurist Askelov, who were invited to lecture in our country, arrived in Shenyang on the afternoon of the 11th from Beijing. Those who welcomed them at the station included Liu Zhiming, Deputy Director of the Propaganda Department of the Northeast Bureau of the CPC Central Committee, Che Xiangchen, Minister of Education of the Northeast People's Government, and Li Junfu, Secretary-General of the Northeast Branch of the Sino-Soviet Friendship Association. The two professors are scheduled to lecture in Shenyang for three days.

### 苏联两教授抵沈阳讲学

1950-07-14
第1版()
专栏：

　　苏联两教授抵沈阳讲学
    【新华社沈阳十三日电】应邀来我国讲学的苏联哲学家车斯诺柯夫和法学家阿斯凯洛夫两教授于十一日午后由北京抵沈阳。到车站欢迎者有中共中央东北局宣传部副部长刘芝明、东北人民政府教育部部长车向忱、中苏友好协会东北总分会秘书长李俊夫等。两教授预定在沈阳讲学三日。
