Title: The Korean People's Army Continues to Advance on All Fronts, Liberating Chungju and Annihilating Many Enemies; Launching a New Offensive Against American Troops South of Pyeongtaek; American Troops Flee in Panic, Two American Aircraft Shot Down
Author:
Date: 1950-07-10
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Pyongyang, 9th – The General Headquarters of the Korean People's Army of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea reported on the morning of the 8th: On the 7th, the People's Army units continued to pursue and annihilate the fleeing enemy on all fronts, advancing southward. As a result of the air combat between the People's Army Air Force and the U.S. Air Force fleet, two U.S. B-29 bombers were shot down.

On the 7th, the U.S. Air Force fleet carried out barbaric bombings and strafing of workers' dormitory areas in Nampo in South Pyongan Province, Heungnam in South Hamgyong Province, and Wonsan in Gangwon Province, resulting in the destruction of many workers' dormitories and numerous casualties among workers' families, the elderly, women, and children.

Xinhua News Agency, Pyongyang, 9th – The General Headquarters of the Korean People's Army of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea announced on the evening of the 8th: The People's Army units continued to advance on all fronts, liberating vast towns and villages. One unit of the People's Army liberated Chungju in North Chungcheong Province on the evening of the 7th. In this battle, the People's Army annihilated and captured a great number of enemy troops and seized many spoils of war. On the same day, another unit of the People's Army launched a new offensive against U.S. ground forces in the area south of Pyeongtaek at the southern end of Gyeonggi Province, annihilating and capturing many U.S. troops. The People's Army's offensive caused the U.S. ground forces to abandon large quantities of weapons and military supplies and flee in panic. The People's Army is now continuing the pursuit.

### 朝鲜人民军在各线继续挺进   解放忠州市歼敌甚众  在平泽以南对美军展开新攻势  美军仓惶逃窜美机两架被击落

1950-07-10
第1版()
专栏：

　　朝鲜人民军在各线继续挺进
    解放忠州市歼敌甚众
    在平泽以南对美军展开新攻势  美军仓惶逃窜美机两架被击落
    【新华社平壤九日电】朝鲜民主主义人民共和国人民军总司令部八日晨报道：人民军各部队七日在各战线上继续追歼逃敌，向南挺进。人民军空军部队与美空军机群进行空战的结果，击落美Ｂ二十九型轰炸机两架。
    美空军机群七日对平安南道的南浦、咸镜南道的兴南和江原道的元山等城市的工人宿舍地区，进行了野蛮的轰炸与扫射，致使极多的工人宿舍遭到破坏，工人的家属、老弱妇孺伤亡者甚众。
    【新华社平壤九日电】朝鲜民主主义人民共和国人民军总司令部八日晚公布：人民军各部队在各个战线上继续进军，解放了广大的城镇与农村。人民军某部于七日晚解放了忠清北道的忠州市。在这一战斗中，人民军歼俘敌军甚众，并缴获许多战利品。同日，人民军另部在京畿道南端的平泽以南地区，对美军地面部队展开新攻势。歼俘美军甚多。人民军的进攻，使美军地面部队丢弃大批武器与军需品而仓惶逃窜。现在人民军正继续追击中。
