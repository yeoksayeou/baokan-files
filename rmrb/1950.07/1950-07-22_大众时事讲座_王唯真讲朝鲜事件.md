Title: Mass Current Affairs Lecture: Wang Weizhen on the Korean Event
Author:
Date: 1950-07-22
Page: 6

　　Mass Current Affairs Lecture
    Wang Weizhen on the Korean Event
    The mass current affairs lecture organized by the Youth Service Department is scheduled for the thirteenth lecture on Saturday, July 22nd, at 4:00 PM at the Soviet Foreign Culture Association (19 South River Bank). Comrade Wang Weizhen of Xinhua News Agency will speak on "The Korean Event." A film will be shown after the meeting. Admission tickets can be obtained from the Youth Service Department. (Groups require a letter of introduction.)
                                      (China Youth Press)

### 大众时事讲座  王唯真讲朝鲜事件

1950-07-22
第6版()
专栏：

　　大众时事讲座
    王唯真讲朝鲜事件
    青年服务部主办的大众时事讲座，定于七月二十二日（星期六）下午四时在苏联对外文化协会（南河沿十九号）举行第十三讲，请新华社王唯真同志讲“朝鲜事件”。会后放映电影。入场券可到青年服务部领取。（团体凭介绍信）
                                      （中国青年社）
