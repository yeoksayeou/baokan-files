Title: Resolutely Oppose Warmongers
Author: Xu Beihong
Date: 1950-07-21
Page: 5

　　Resolutely Oppose Warmongers
    Xu Beihong
    Scientists rack their brains to unlock the secrets of nature; their purpose must be to benefit the lives of the people. Conversely, those who use scientific achievements to threaten the survival of the people must be the vermin of mankind! We very much hope that mankind can use scientific achievements to improve our lives in the shortest possible time by utilizing atomic energy; and we resolutely oppose warmongers from manufacturing atomic weapons, threatening our survival, and destroying world civilization. This is a responsibility that everyone has.

### 坚决反对战争贩子
徐悲鸿
1950-07-21
第5版()
专栏：保卫世界和平，反对使用原子武器！

　　坚决反对战争贩子
    徐悲鸿
    科学家绞尽脑汁，启发造化之秘；其目的必将为福利于人群生活。相反的，利用科学的造就，威胁人群生存的人，必是人类的蟊贼！我们很希望人类能借科学上之造就，在最短时间内，利用原子能来改善我们大家的生活；而坚决反对战争贩子们制造原子武器，威胁我们的生存；并毁灭世界的文明。这个责任是大家都有的。
