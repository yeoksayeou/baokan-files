Title: Over Ten Million People in France Participate in Peace Signature Drive; Peace Conference Held in Iceland Launches Signature Campaign; British Students Gather to Intensify Collection of Peace Signatures
Author:
Date: 1950-07-11
Page: 4

　　【Report from this newspaper】Xinhua News Agency dispatch: As of July 5th, over ten million signatures have been collected in France for the Stockholm Appeal, demanding the prohibition of atomic weapons.

    Over 500,000 Finnish citizens have signed the peace declaration. An entire small village in western Finland, with a population of 220 residents, has signed.

    A peace conference was recently held in Reykjavik, the capital of Iceland, with participants including representatives from various labor unions, women's organizations, and youth organizations, as well as Icelandic cultural and artistic workers. In its resolution, the conference called on the Icelandic people to launch a campaign to collect signatures for the Stockholm Appeal.

    The National Conference of British Student Peace Advocates, hosted by students from the University of London Institute of Education, was held in London on the 1st and 2nd. The conference passed a resolution declaring unconditional support for the Stockholm Peace Appeal and calling on all British students to redouble their efforts to collect signatures for the peace declaration. The conference resolved to establish a permanent organization of student peace advocates and to send representatives to the British National Peace Congress to be held in the latter half of July.

### 法国千余万人参加和平签名  冰岛举行和平大会展开签名运动  英国学生集会加紧征集和平签名

1950-07-11
第4版()
专栏：

　　法国千余万人参加和平签名
    冰岛举行和平大会展开签名运动
    英国学生集会加紧征集和平签名
    【本报讯】据新华社讯：至七月五日为止，在法国已为要求禁止原子武器的斯德哥尔摩宣言征集了一千万以上的签名。
    芬兰公民中已有五十余万人在和平宣言上签名。芬兰西部有一个小村庄全体二百二十个居民都签了名。
    冰岛首府雷克雅维克最近举行和平大会，参加者有各工会、各妇女组织和各青年组织的代表们，以及冰岛的文化和艺术工作者们。大会在决议中号召冰岛人民展开征集在斯德哥尔摩宣言上签名的运动。
    英国学生和平拥护者全国大会，在敦伦大学教育学院的学生们主持之下，于一日和二日在伦敦举行。大会通过决议：宣布无保留地拥护斯德哥尔摩和平宣言，号召所有的英国学生们为征集在和平宣言上签名而加倍努力。大会决议建立学生和平拥护者常设组织，并派遣代表出席在七月下半月召开的英国全国和平大会。
