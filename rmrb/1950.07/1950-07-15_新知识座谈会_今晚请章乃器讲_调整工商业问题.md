Title: New Knowledge Forum: Tonight, Zhang Naiqi to Speak on "Adjusting Industrial and Commercial Issues"
Author:
Date: 1950-07-15
Page: 6

　　New Knowledge Forum
    Tonight, Zhang Naiqi to Speak on "Adjusting Industrial and Commercial Issues"
    The New Knowledge Forum, organized by the Beijing Branch of the China Democratic National Construction Association, will hold its 78th session at 7:30 PM this evening (the 15th) at the Xiehe Auditorium on Dongdan Santiao. This time, economic expert and Government Administration Council Member Zhang Naiqi will be the main speaker on "Adjusting Industrial and Commercial Issues." In addition to members, other individuals who wish to attend the lecture can obtain admission tickets through introductions from their organizations, groups, or enterprises at No. 24 Wuliang Daren Hutong.

### 新知识座谈会  今晚请章乃器讲“调整工商业问题”

1950-07-15
第6版()
专栏：广播节目

　　新知识座谈会
    今晚请章乃器讲“调整工商业问题”
    民主建国会北京市分会主办的新知识座谈会，订于今（十五）日下午七时半在东单三条协和礼堂举行七十八次会。这次是请经济学专家、政务院政务委员章乃器主讲“调整工商业问题”除会员外其他人士如要听讲者，可由机关团体或企业单位介绍，到无量大人胡同二十四号领取入场券。
