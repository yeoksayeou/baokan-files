Title: New Democracy Lecture Series to Hold its Ninety-Fifth Lecture Today
Author:
Date: 1950-07-29
Page: 6

The ninety-fifth lecture of the New Democracy Lecture Series, sponsored by the YMCA of this city, will be held at 7:30 PM on Saturday, July 29th. We have specially invited Mr. Chen Qiyuan, Vice Minister of the Ministry of Internal Affairs of the Central People's Government, to speak on "Defending the Motherland and Defending Peace." All sectors are welcome to attend and listen.

### 新民主主义讲座今举行第九十五讲

1950-07-29
第6版()
专栏：

　　新民主主义讲座今举行第九十五讲
    本市青年会主办之新民主主义讲座第九十五讲，定于七月二十九日（星期六）晚七时半特请中央人民政府内务部副部长陈其瑗先生讲《保卫祖国与保卫和平》。欢迎各界来会听讲。
