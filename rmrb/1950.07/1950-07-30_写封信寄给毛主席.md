Title: Write a Letter to Chairman Mao
Author: Zhang Mingquan
Date: 1950-07-30
Page: 5

　　Write a Letter to Chairman Mao
　　Zhang Mingquan
　　I pick up my pen and spread out the paper,
　　To write a letter to Chairman Mao.
　　The migrating geese fly overhead,
　　My letter, hurry and catch up!
　　Fly to our post office,
　　Fly to our train;
　　Fly all the way to Beijing city,
　　Fly into your palm.

　　Plowing our own land,
　　We think of you in our hearts.
　　Eating our own food,
　　We think of you in our hearts.
　　Attending the people's school to elect cadres,
　　We think of you in our hearts.
　　Facing the good wheat seedlings everywhere,
　　We think of you in our hearts.

　　Looking at the newly bought ox in the trough,
　　Looking at the newly built house,
　　Looking at the red auspicious couplets on the door,
　　Looking at the newborn child,
　　A flame burns in our hearts,
　　Thinking of you every moment.

　　During the day we are in the fields,
　　Talking about you, singing about you;
　　At night we dream of you,
　　The next day we work even more happily.

　　We can't express all our gratitude,
　　We can't write down all the joy in our hearts,
　　We can't say it all, we can't write it all,
　　The thousands of words and tens of thousands of phrases in our hearts:

　　"Now the spring plowing is finished,
　　The seedlings have all sprouted;
　　We must guarantee more weeding,
　　We must guarantee more fertilizer;
　　We swear to protect them,
　　Until the grain enters the granary.

　　"Even if insects are born again this year,
　　Even if there is flooding again this year;
　　Even if the special agents want to sabotage,
　　Even if there is a long drought without rain;
　　Even if the difficulties are bigger than mountains,
　　Our confidence is a hundred times greater;
　　Just relying on our own two hands,
　　The struggle for increased production will surely be victorious!"
　　May 10th, Xinxiang

### 写封信寄给毛主席
张明权
1950-07-30
第5版()
专栏：

　　写封信寄给毛主席
    张明权
    提起笔来铺开纸，
    写封信寄给毛主席。
    南来的大雁头上飞，
    我的信呀赶上去！
    飞到我们的邮局里，
    飞到我们的火车里；
    一直飞到北京城，
    飞到你的手心里。
　
    耕着我们自己的地，
    我们心里想起了你。
    吃着我们自己的饭，
    我们心里想起了你。
    上民校来选干部，
    我们心里想起了你。
    对着遍地好麦苗，
    我们心里想起了你。
　
    看着槽上新买的牛，
    看着才盖的新房子，
    看着门上的红喜对，
    看着刚生的小孩子，
    我们心里一团火，
    一时三刻想着你。
　
    白天我们在地里，
    谈着你来唱着你；
    到夜晚我们梦见你，
    第二天干活更美气。
　
    说不完我们的感激意，
    写不完我们的心头喜，
    说不完呀写不尽，
    心里的千言和万语：
　
    “现在春耕已完毕，
    禾苗已经都出齐；
    我们要保证多锄草，
    我们要保证多施肥；
    我们要起誓保住他，
    直到粮食进囤里。
　
    “那怕他今年再生虫，
    那怕他今年再上水；
    那怕他特务想捣乱，
    那怕他久旱不下雨；
    那怕他困难比山大，
    我们的信心大百倍；
    就凭我们一双手，
    增产斗争一定能胜利！”
        五月十日  新乡
