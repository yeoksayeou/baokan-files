Title: From the Banks of the Heilongjiang River to the Liaodong Peninsula: People's Just Voices Resound Everywhere Protesting American Aggression Against Taiwan and Korea
Author:
Date: 1950-07-23
Page: 1
    Xinhua News Agency, Shenyang, July 21st: The people of Northeast China are launching a movement against American imperialist aggression against Taiwan and Korea. Anti-American aggression against Taiwan and Korea movement committees have been established throughout the region, and the movement week in large and medium-sized cities began on the 17th. In recent days, various gatherings have been held in various places to expose the aggressive crimes of American imperialism. From the banks of the Heilongjiang River to the Liaodong Peninsula, the people's just voices resound everywhere.

    Shenyang held a city-wide cadre meeting on the 16th. On the 17th, all units held general seminars and organized studies of this major event. Cultural centers throughout the city have published articles on blackboards condemning the crimes of American imperialist aggression and news of the defeat of the American army by the Korean People's Army.

    On the 16th and 17th, cities and counties in Liaodong Province successively held mass demonstrations, with a total of more than 260,000 participants. Demonstrators rejoiced for the victory of the Korean people. The Yingkou City People's Representative Conference in the province also sent a telegram to the China Peace Committee, expressing its determination to support the People's Liberation Army in liberating Taiwan with all its strength.

    Heilongjiang, Songjiang, and Rehe provinces have also enthusiastically launched various anti-aggression activities. Chengde City, Rehe, began its movement week on the 15th. When the propaganda team took to the streets that evening to carry out propaganda, more than 4,000 listeners rushed to sign at the newly established peace signing station, expressing their determination to oppose aggressive war and support world peace.

### 从黑龙江边到辽东半岛  到处响彻人民正义呼声抗议美国侵略台湾朝鲜

1950-07-23
第1版()
专栏：

　　从黑龙江边到辽东半岛
    到处响彻人民正义呼声抗议美国侵略台湾朝鲜
    【新华社沈阳二十一日电】东北人民正展开反对美帝国主义侵略台湾、朝鲜的运动。全区各地都已成立反对美国侵略台湾、朝鲜运动委员会，大、中城市的运动周都已从十七日开始。日来各地纷纷举行各种集会，揭露美帝国主义的侵略罪行。从黑龙江边到辽东半岛，到处响彻着人民的正义呼声。
    沈阳市于十六日举行了全市干部大会。各单位十七日普遍召开座谈会，并组织对这一重大事件的学习。全市各文化馆都在黑板报上刊出了斥责美帝国主义侵略罪行的文章和美军被朝鲜人民军击溃的消息。
    辽东省各市、县在十六、十七两日，相继举行了群众示威大会，参加示威的共达二十六万余人。示威者为朝鲜人民的胜利而欢欣鼓舞。该省营口市各界人民代表会议并致电中国保卫世界和平大会委员会，表示誓以全力支援人民解放军解放台湾。
    黑龙江、松江和热河等省也热烈地展开了各种反侵略的活动。热河承德市自十五日起即开始了运动周，当晚宣传队出动街头进行宣传时，四千余听众纷纷在新设的和平签名处争着签名，表示反对侵略战争，拥护世界和平的决心。
