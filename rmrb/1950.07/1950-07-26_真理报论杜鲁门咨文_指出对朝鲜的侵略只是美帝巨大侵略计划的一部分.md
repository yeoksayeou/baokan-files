Title: "Pravda" on Truman's Message: Points Out that the Aggression Against Korea is Only Part of the US Imperialists' Grand Scheme of Aggression
Author:
Date: 1950-07-26
Page: 4
　
Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, July 24th: The "Pravda" observer, Viktorov, wrote, commenting on President Truman's lengthy message to Congress on July 19th: The content of this message shows that Truman is wasting his efforts in using diplomatic forms to conceal the resolute rejection of Nehru's peace proposals for the sake of America's military purposes. Truman bluntly and rashly stated this in his message. Furthermore, Truman's message shows that the aggression against Korea is only part of the US imperialists' grand scheme of aggression.

Truman's message to Congress stipulates a new, frenzied arms race, reflecting the increasingly aggressive nature of American policy. At the same time, it reflects a desire to use further arms races to save the American economy from major turmoil, and to further attack the living standards of the working people in order to ensure the huge profits of the Wall Street bosses. As for the warlike and exaggerated clamor that fills Truman's message, its purpose is to conceal the failure of the American gamble.

The US government's rejection of Nehru's peace initiative, and the plan to expand the arms race stipulated in Truman's message, are new proofs of the fact that the American war-mongers intend to further expand the scope of their aggression. The task now facing peace advocates is to further strengthen the struggle to defend peace, oppose aggression, and oppose the criminal plans of the arrogant imperialist gamblers.

### 真理报论杜鲁门咨文  指出对朝鲜的侵略只是美帝巨大侵略计划的一部分

1950-07-26
第4版()
专栏：

　　真理报论杜鲁门咨文
    指出对朝鲜的侵略只是美帝巨大侵略计划的一部分
    【新华社二十五日讯】塔斯社莫斯科二十四日讯：真理报观察家维克托罗夫在论及美总统杜鲁门七月十九日给国会的长篇咨文时写道：这一咨文的内容表明，杜鲁门枉费心机地为了美国的军事目的，而以外交形式来掩盖对尼赫鲁和平建议的坚决拒绝。杜鲁门在他的咨文中直率而且卤莽地表明了这一点。此外，杜鲁门的咨文表明，对朝鲜的侵略只是美帝国主义巨大侵略计划的一部分。
    杜鲁门对国会的咨文规定了新的疯狂的军备竞赛，这反映出美国的政策愈益带有侵略性质。同时，它又反映出一种愿望，要经由进一步的军备竞赛使美国经济免于大动乱，进一步进攻劳动人民的生活标准，借以确保华尔街老板们的巨大的利润。至于杜鲁门咨文中充满了的好战的夸张的叫嚣，它们的目的是要掩饰美国赌博的失败。
    美国政府之拒绝尼赫鲁的和平创议，杜鲁门咨文中所规定的扩大军备竞赛的计划，乃是美国战争贩子意图进一步扩大他们侵略的范围这一事实的新的明证。和平拥护者现在面临的任务，就是进一步加强为保卫和平、反对侵略、反对骄横的帝国主义赌徒的犯罪计划而进行的斗争。
