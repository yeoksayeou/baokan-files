Title: Responding to the World Peace Preservation Signature Campaign
Author: Mao Dun
Date: 1950-07-03
Page: 5

　　Responding to the World Peace Preservation Signature Campaign
    Mao Dun
    The international aggressive bloc headed by U.S. imperialism is妄想征服世界 (desperately attempting to conquer the world), so they are frantically preparing for a new war. The Chinese people, to defend the fruits of the revolution, must unleash their great strength and struggle together with the hundreds of millions of people throughout the world who love peace and democracy, to crush the sinister schemes of the warmongers. Defending world peace is also defending China's peace.

### 响应保卫世界和平签名运动
茅盾
1950-07-03
第5版()
专栏：

　　响应保卫世界和平签名运动
    茅盾
    美帝为首的国际侵略集团妄想征服世界，所以疯狂地准备新战争，中国人民要保卫革命的果实，就必须发挥伟大的力量，和全世界爱好和平与民主的亿万人民共同奋斗，粉碎战争贩子的罪恶企图。保卫世界和平也就是保卫中国的和平。
