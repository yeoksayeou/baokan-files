Title: The U.S. Military Will Be Driven Out of Korea: Public Prediction by the President of the U.S. Chamber of Commerce
Author:
Date: 1950-07-24
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Spring Lake, Michigan, 23rd: According to the Associated Press, the President of the U.S. Chamber of Commerce, Mr. Siefers, said: He believes that the American troops will be "driven out of Korea." He made this prediction after attending a meeting of "senior military leaders and cabinet members" in Washington. He said, "The situation is much more serious than most of us realize."

### 美军将被赶出朝鲜  美国商会会长公开的预言

1950-07-24
第4版()
专栏：

　　美军将被赶出朝鲜
    美国商会会长公开的预言
    【新华社二十三日讯】据美联社密执安州春湖二十一日讯：美国商会会长赛弗尔斯说：他相信美国军队将被“赶出朝鲜”。他是在华盛顿旁听过“高级军事领袖及内阁阁员”的会议后，作此预言的。他说：“情况比起我们大多数人所认识的要严重得多。”
