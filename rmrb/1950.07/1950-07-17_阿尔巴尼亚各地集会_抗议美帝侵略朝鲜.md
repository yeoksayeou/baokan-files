Title: Rallies Held Across Albania Protesting American Imperialist Aggression Against Korea
Author:
Date: 1950-07-17
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Tirana, July 11th (TASS): The Albanian Telegraph Agency reports that rallies are currently being held throughout Albania to protest the armed intervention of American imperialists in Korea.

　　On July 9th, working people in various industrial enterprises held rallies under the auspices of the Albanian National Committee for the Defense of Peace. Speakers expressed deep anger at the American armed intervention in Korea's internal affairs.

　　The Albanian Writers' Union has sent a telegram to Korean writers, expressing the Albanian writers' deep indignation at the American imperialist aggression against Korea.

### 阿尔巴尼亚各地集会  抗议美帝侵略朝鲜

1950-07-17
第4版()
专栏：

　　阿尔巴尼亚各地集会
    抗议美帝侵略朝鲜
    【新华社讯】塔斯社地拉那十一日电：阿尔巴尼亚通讯社报道，阿尔巴尼亚全国各地现正举行集会，抗议美国帝国主义者武装干涉朝鲜。
    七月九日，各工业企业中的劳动人民，在阿尔巴尼亚拥护和平全国委员会主催下举行集会。发言者对美国武装干涉朝鲜内政深表愤怒。
    阿尔巴尼亚作家联盟已致电朝鲜作家，表明阿尔巴尼亚的作家们对美国帝国主义者侵略朝鲜深感愤怒。
