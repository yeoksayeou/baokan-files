Title: Memorial Concert for Nie Er Postponed Due to Rain, Held Today
Author: Renmin Art Theater Communications Group
Date: 1950-07-20
Page: 6
Radio Program

　　Memorial Concert for Nie Er Postponed Due to Rain, Held Today
    The Memorial Concert for Nie Er, jointly organized by the Beijing literary and art circles, and the inaugural meeting of the Beijing Xinghai Chorus, were postponed to today (the 20th) due to rain. The event will begin at 8:00 PM. The location remains the Zhongshan Park Concert Hall. Tickets for the previous day (the 17th) will remain valid.
                                            (Renmin Art Theater Communications Group)

### 纪念聂耳音乐晚会因雨延至今天举行
人民艺术剧院通讯组
1950-07-20
第6版()
专栏：广播节目

　　纪念聂耳音乐晚会因雨延至今天举行
    北京市文艺界联合主办的纪念聂耳音乐晚会及北京星海大合唱团成立大会，因天下雨，顺延至今日（二十日）举行。时间下午八时开始。地点仍在中山公园音乐堂。前日（十七日）的入场券全部有效。
                                            （人民艺术剧院通讯组）
