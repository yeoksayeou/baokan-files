Title: Huaihe River Railway Bridge Repair: Trial Run Successfully Completed
Author:
Date: 1950-07-12
Page: 1

　　Huaihe River Railway Bridge Repair
    Trial Run Successfully Completed
    [Xinhua News Agency, 11th] The Huaihe River Railway Bridge repair project on the Tianjin-Pukou Railway was completed on the 5th of this month, and the trial run was successful on the 7th. The bridge is located between Bengbu and Bengbu North stations. Last January, when the Kuomintang bandit troops fled, they blew up the bridge. In May, the Railway Corps was responsible for starting the construction of a temporary pontoon bridge, which was completed in two months. In November, the Jinan Railway Bureau organized the Bridge Engineering Department to begin repairing the main bridge.

### 淮河铁桥修复  试车已告成功

1950-07-12
第1版()
专栏：

　　淮河铁桥修复
    试车已告成功
    【新华社十一日讯】津浦路淮河铁桥修复工程，已于本月五日完竣，七日试车成功。该桥位于蚌埠与蚌北车站间。去年一月国民党匪军逃溃时，曾将该桥炸毁。五月间由铁道兵团负责动工修建临时便桥，两个月修建完竣。十一月，济南铁路局即组织桥工处着手修复该桥正桥。
