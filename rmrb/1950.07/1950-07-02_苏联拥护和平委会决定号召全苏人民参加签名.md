Title: Soviet Peace Committee Decides to Call on All Soviet People to Participate in Signing

Author:

Date: 1950-07-02
Page: 4

　　Xinhua News Agency, Moscow: The Soviet Peace Committee held a plenary session on the 29th. The plenary session unanimously decided to launch a signature campaign for the Stockholm Appeal of the Standing Committee of the World Peace Congress in the Soviet Union starting from June 30th.
    The resolution pointed out: All the Soviet people have unanimously approved the declaration made by the Supreme Soviet of the Soviet Union on the proposal of the Standing Committee on the Protection of Peace and are making every effort to consolidate peace and ensure the victory of the just and noble cause of friendship among all nations. The Soviet Peace Committee called on all Soviet people to sign the Stockholm Appeal, thereby expressing their determination to safeguard world peace, and is confident that all the people will respond to this call.
    The Soviet Peace Committee members who signed the resolution included Committee Chairman Tikhonov, President of the Soviet Academy of Sciences Vavilov, Secretary of the Central Committee of the Communist Youth League Mikhailov, Academician Lysenko, and many others.

### 苏联拥护和平委会决定号召全苏人民参加签名

1950-07-02
第4版()
专栏：

　　苏联拥护和平委会决定号召全苏人民参加签名
    【新华社二十九日讯】据塔斯社莫斯科讯：苏联拥护和平委员会于二十九日召开全体会议。全会一致决定于六月三十日起，在苏联为世界拥护和平大会常设委员会斯德哥尔摩宣言征集签名。
    决议指出：全体苏联人民已一致赞同苏联最高苏维埃就和大常委会的建议所作的声明，并且正在以全力巩固和平与保证各民族友好的正义与高尚事业的胜利。苏联拥护和平委员会号召全苏人民在斯德哥尔摩宣言上签名，借以表示他们维护全世界和平的决心。并坚信全体人民必将响应这一号召。
    在决议上签署的苏联拥护和平委员会委员有委员会主席吉洪诺夫，苏联科学院院长瓦维洛夫，共产主义青年团中委会书记米哈依洛夫，科学院院士李森科等多人。
