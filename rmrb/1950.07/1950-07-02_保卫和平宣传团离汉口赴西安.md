Title: Peace Preservation Propaganda Group Leaves Hankou for Xi'an
Author:
Date: 1950-07-02
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, Hankou, 30th: The Chinese Peace Preservation Propaganda Group, including Xiao San and five others, left for Xi'an by train on the 27th. During their five-day stay in Hankou, the group attended a total of 19 lectures and symposiums and gave two radio speeches, with a total audience of over 70,000 people. The six films they brought, reflecting the heroic struggle of people all over the world to defend peace, were screened 28 times, with an audience of 95,000 people.

### 保卫和平宣传团离汉口赴西安

1950-07-02
第1版()
专栏：

　　保卫和平宣传团
    离汉口赴西安
    【新华社汉口三十日电】中国保卫世界和平宣传团萧三等一行六人，已于二十七日搭车赴西安。该团在留汉的五天中，曾出席讲演会和座谈会共十九次，广播讲演二次，听讲者共七万余人。该团所带反映全世界人民保卫和平英勇斗争的六部影片，共放映二十八场，观众达九万五千人。
