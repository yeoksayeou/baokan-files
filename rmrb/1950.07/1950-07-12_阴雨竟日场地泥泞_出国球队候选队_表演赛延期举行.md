Title: Rainy Days and Muddy Grounds: Overseas Teams Candidate Team Exhibition Match Postponed
Author:
Date: 1950-07-12
Page: 6

    Rainy Days and Muddy Grounds
    Overseas Teams Candidate Team
    Exhibition Match Postponed
    The exhibition match of the overseas student basketball and volleyball team candidates, originally scheduled to be held at 4:00 PM this afternoon and tomorrow afternoon at the Capital Laboring People's Cultural Palace, has been postponed due to the rain all day yesterday and the muddy ground. The date of the event will be announced in the newspaper after a decision is made.

### 阴雨竟日场地泥泞  出国球队候选队  表演赛延期举行

1950-07-12
第6版()
专栏：

    阴雨竟日场地泥泞
    出国球队候选队
    表演赛延期举行
    出国学生篮排球队候选队表演赛，原定今明两日下午四时在首都劳动人民文化宫举行，因昨天整日下雨，场地泥泞，决予延期。举行日期俟决定后另在报上发表。
