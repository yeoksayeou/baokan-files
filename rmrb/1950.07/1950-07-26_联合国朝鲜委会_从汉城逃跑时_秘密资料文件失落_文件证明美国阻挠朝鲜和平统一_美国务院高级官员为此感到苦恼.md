Title: "United Nations Commission on Korea" Loses Secret Documents While Fleeing Seoul: Documents Prove the United States Obstructed Peaceful Unification of Korea, Senior U.S. State Department Officials Distressed

Author: 
Date: 1950-07-26
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Prague, July 24th (TASS): A Telepress correspondent in Washington reports: Senior officials at the U.S. State Department are distressed about the fate of extremely secret materials and documents belonging to the so-called United Nations Commission on Korea. According to sources in Washington, the members of the "United Nations Commission on Korea" abandoned these secret documents when they fled Seoul.

The reason for American concern is that the documents contain instructions from the State Department to the "United Nations Commission on Korea." These instructions contain a clear "recommendation": to obstruct the peaceful unification of Korea by any and all means.

The documents prove that the "United Nations Commission on Korea" completely obeyed American orders, and demonstrate the role of the "United Nations Commission on Korea" in the American plan to turn all of Korea into a bridgehead for aggression against the Soviet Union.

### “联合国朝鲜委会”从汉城逃跑时  秘密资料文件失落  文件证明美国阻挠朝鲜和平统一  美国务院高级官员为此感到苦恼

1950-07-26
第1版()
专栏：

　　“联合国朝鲜委会”从汉城逃跑时
    秘密资料文件失落         
    文件证明美国阻挠朝鲜和平统一
    美国务院高级官员为此感到苦恼
    【新华社二十五日讯】塔斯社布拉格二十四日讯：据电通社驻华盛顿记者报道：美国国务院高级官员们对于所谓联合国朝鲜委员会的极端秘密的资料和文件的命运感到苦恼。据华盛顿方面所传，“联合国朝鲜委员会”的委员们在他们从汉城逃走的时候，把那些秘密文件抛弃了。
    美国人感到忧虑的缘故，是因为在文件中有国务院给“联合国朝鲜委员会”的指示。该指示包含着明确的“建议”：用任何及一切手段去阻挠朝鲜的和平统一。
    各项文件证明“联合国朝鲜委员会”完全唯美国之命是从，并显示“联合国朝鲜委员会”在美国把全部朝鲜变成侵略苏联的桥头堡的计划中所起的作用。
