Title: Korean People's Army Continues to Advance on All Fronts; Liberates the Three Cities of Wonju, Yeoju, and Yangpyeong; People's Army Air Force Shoots Down Three More Enemy Planes
Author:
Date: 1950-07-05
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency, Pyongyang, July 4th: The General Headquarters of the Korean People's Army of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea reported on the morning of the 4th that units of the Republic's People's Army had liberated the county seat of Wonju in Gangwon Province and the county seat of Yeoju in Gyeonggi Province on the 3rd. They are now pursuing and annihilating the fleeing enemy and continuing to advance south. On the east coast, the People's Army has liberated hundreds of villages. The People's Army has captured a large amount of spoils on all fronts and is being warmly welcomed by the people everywhere.

Xinhua News Agency, Pyongyang, July 4th: The General Headquarters of the Korean People's Army of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea reported on the morning of the 3rd that on the Gangwon Province front, a unit of the People's Army, while advancing south, liberated Wonju Township and its surrounding areas belonging to Wonju County at about 1:00 PM on the 2nd. They then entered Yangpyeong County in Gyeonggi Province, sweeping up the routed and fleeing enemy troops, and liberated Yangpyeong County seat on the morning of the 2nd.

On the 2nd, a certain detachment of the People's Army Air Force alternately bombed enemy infantry and motor vehicle unit positions in the area between Seoul and Suwon. After bombing Suwon Airport, they also destroyed one enemy military train locomotive, twenty train cars, and enemy warehouses and military vehicles. Another unit of the People's Army Air Force bombed enemy artillery positions and shot down one enemy plane.

### 朝鲜人民军在各战线继续前进  解放原州骊州杨平三城  人民军空军又击落敌机三架

1950-07-05
第4版()
专栏：

　　朝鲜人民军在各战线继续前进
    解放原州骊州杨平三城
    人民军空军又击落敌机三架
    【新华社平壤四日电】朝鲜民主主义人民共和国人民军总司令部四日晨报道：共和国人民军部队已于三日解放江原道的原州县城与京畿道的骊州县城，现正追歼逃敌，继续南进。东海岸方面，人民军业已解放了数百个村庄。人民军在各战线上缴获战利品甚伙，到处受到人民的热烈欢迎。
    【新华社平壤四日电】朝鲜民主主义人民共和国人民军总司令部三日晨报道：江原道方面，南进中的人民军某部于二日下午一时许解放原州县所属的原州邑及其附近地区后，又攻入京畿道的杨平县境，扫荡溃散逃窜的敌军，并于二日晨解放杨平县城。
    二日，人民军空军某区分队在汉城水原之间的地区轮番轰炸敌军步兵与汽车部队阵地。在轰炸水原机场后，复炸毁敌军用列车的机车一台、车皮二十辆以及敌军用仓库、军用汽车等。人民军空军另部并轰炸敌军炮兵阵地，击落敌机一架。
