Title: New Democracy Lecture Series To Hold Lecture Ninety-Four Today
Author:
Date: 1950-07-22
Page: 6

　　New Democracy Lecture Series
    To Hold Lecture Ninety-Four Today
    The ninety-fourth lecture of the New Democracy Lecture Series, sponsored by the YMCA of this city, is scheduled to be held at 7:30 PM on July 22nd (Saturday). Mr. Li Yunliang, former professor of Jiaotong University, will speak on "New China's Water Transportation." All circles are welcome to attend.

### 新民主主义讲座  今举行第九十四讲

1950-07-22
第6版()
专栏：

　　新民主主义讲座
    今举行第九十四讲
    本市青年会主办之新民主主义讲座第九十四讲。定七月二十二日（星期六）晚七时半举行。由前交通大学教授李云良先生讲：“新中国的水上交通”。欢迎各界听讲。
