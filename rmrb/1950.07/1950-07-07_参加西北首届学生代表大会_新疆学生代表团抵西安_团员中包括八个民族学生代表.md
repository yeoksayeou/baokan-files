Title: Xinjiang Student Delegation Arrives in Xi'an to Attend the First Northwest Student Congress; Delegation Includes Student Representatives from Eight Ethnic Groups
Author:
Date: 1950-07-07
Page: 3

　　Attending the First Northwest Student Congress
　　Xinjiang Student Delegation Arrives in Xi'an
　　Delegation Includes Student Representatives from Eight Ethnic Groups
　　[Our newspaper's Xi'an dispatch] The Xinjiang Student Delegation, attending the first Northwest Student Congress, arrived in Xi'an on June 18th and received a warm welcome from youth and students from all circles in the Northwest. The Xinjiang Student Delegation consists of 46 people, led by the head of the delegation, Mukan (Kazakh). The delegation members include student representatives from eight ethnic groups: Uyghur, Kazakh, Hui, Tatar, Mongolian, Uzbek, Russian, and Han. (Attached image)
　　Members of the Xinjiang Student Delegation attending the First Northwest Student Congress receive flowers offered by Northwest students.

### 参加西北首届学生代表大会  新疆学生代表团抵西安  团员中包括八个民族学生代表

1950-07-07
第3版()
专栏：

　　参加西北首届学生代表大会
    新疆学生代表团抵西安
    团员中包括八个民族学生代表
    【本报西安讯】出席西北学生第一届代表大会的新疆学生代表团于六月十八日抵达西安时，受到西北各界青年与学生的热烈欢迎。新疆学生代表团一行共四十六人，由团长穆坎（哈萨克族）率领。团员中包括维吾尔族、哈萨克族、回族、塔塔尔族、蒙族、乌孜别克族、俄罗斯族和汉族等八个民族的学生代表。（附图片）
