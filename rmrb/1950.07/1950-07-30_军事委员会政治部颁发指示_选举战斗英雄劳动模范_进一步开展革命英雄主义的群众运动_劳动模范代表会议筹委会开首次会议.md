Title: The General Political Department of the Military Commission Issues Instructions to Elect Combat Heroes and Model Workers, Further Promoting the Mass Movement of Revolutionary Heroism; Preparatory Committee for the Labor Model Representatives Conference Holds First Meeting
Author:
Date: 1950-07-30
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, July 28th: Following the decision of the Government Administration Council of the Central People's Government to convene a National Combat Heroes Conference and a National Conference of Worker-Peasant-Soldier Labor Model Representatives, the General Political Department of the Central People's Government's People's Revolutionary Military Commission issued instructions to the entire People's Liberation Army (including the militia) on preparing for the National Combat Heroes Conference and electing labor models to attend the National Conference of Worker-Peasant-Soldier Labor Model Representatives. The instructions stipulate that the scope of this election of combat heroes and labor models should include the most typical heroes, model figures, and representatives of model units from all branches of the People's Liberation Army's land, sea, and air forces. The conditions for being selected as a combat hero are: (1) Being brave and technically proficient in combat, combining theory with practice, possessing special merits, and being representative of the entire army; (2) Possessing excellent political qualities, being good at connecting with the masses, and having a high reputation among the masses; (3) Having special merits in staff work, political work, war service, military training, bandit suppression, mass work, battlefield rescue, and other work. The instructions, after allocating specific numbers for army combat heroes and labor models as stipulated by the Government Administration Council of the Central People's Government to each branch and military region, require all units to convene a conference at the military or provincial military region level to select heroes and models. The instructions point out that in carrying out this work, a highly serious and cautious attitude should be adopted, and a serious evaluation combining leadership and the masses should be implemented, so that through this large-scale election, the entire army can be educated and encouraged by vivid heroic deeds to further carry out mass movements of revolutionary heroism in all aspects. Concurrent with the convening of the National Combat Heroes Conference, an exhibition of the People's Liberation Army's battle achievements will be held in the capital.

Xinhua News Agency, July 29th: The Preparatory Committee for the National Conference of Worker-Peasant-Soldier Labor Model Representatives held its first meeting on the 29th. The members of the preparatory committee have been designated by the Government Administration Council, consisting of Chen Yun, Li Lisan, Zhu Xuefan, Liu Lantao, Xiao Hua, Mao Qihua, Wang Xinyuan, He Chang Gong, Song Yuhe, Wu Jue Nong, Wu Gengmei, Li Jiebo, Li Fanwu, Li Baohua, Ji Fang, Chen Yu, Xu Zhizhen, Zhang Qinqiu, Yang Xiandong, Dong Xin, Liu Jingfan, Xue Muqiao, Luo Shuzhang and other twenty-six people. It was also decided that Chen Yun would be the director, Li Lisan, Zhu Xuefan, Liu Lantao, and Xiao Hua would be the deputy directors, Mao Qihua would be the secretary-general, and Li Fanwu and Dong Xin would be the deputy secretaries-general. The meeting discussed and approved the plan for convening the National Conference of Worker-Peasant-Soldier Labor Model Representatives, and appointed special personnel to be responsible for studying the reward methods for the labor model representatives attending the conference, and formulating the reward methods for the national production labor models to be discussed at the Labor Model Representatives Conference. The Central Ministry of Agriculture, the All-China Federation of Trade Unions and other units will specifically guide their subordinates on the precautions for electing representatives based on the plan passed by this meeting.

### 军事委员会政治部颁发指示  选举战斗英雄劳动模范  进一步开展革命英雄主义的群众运动  劳动模范代表会议筹委会开首次会议

1950-07-30
第1版()
专栏：

　　军事委员会政治部颁发指示
    选举战斗英雄劳动模范           
    进一步开展革命英雄主义的群众运动
    劳动模范代表会议筹委会开首次会议
    【新华社二十八日讯】在中央人民政府政务院发布关于召开全国战斗英雄会议及全国工农兵劳动模范代表会议的决定以后，中央人民政府人民革命军事委员会政治部特向人民解放军全军（包括民兵）发出筹备召开全国战斗英雄代表会议及选举劳动模范出席全国工农兵劳动模范代表会议的指示。指示规定此次选举战斗英雄和劳动模范的范围，应包括人民解放军陆、海、空军各兵种中最典型的英雄、模范人物和模范单位的代表。当选战斗英雄的条件为：（一）在战斗中勇敢和技术兼备，理论与实际结合，具有特殊功绩，并在全军有代表性者；（二）政治品质优秀，善于联系群众并在群众中有很高威望者；（三）在参谋、政治工作、战争勤务、练兵、剿匪、群众工作、战场救护等工作方面有特殊功绩者。指示就中央人民政府政务院规定的军队战斗英雄、劳动模范的总名额按各兵种、各军区分配具体数字后，要求各部队以军或省军区为单位召开大会选拔英雄和模范。指示指出：在进行此项工作时，应采取高度严肃慎重的态度，实行领导与群众相结合的认真评选，以便经过此次巨大规模的选举，以生动的英雄事迹教育和鼓舞全军进一步开展各方面的革命英雄主义的群众运动。与召开全国战斗英雄代表会议的同时，将在首都举行人民解放军的战绩展览。
    【新华社二十九日讯】全国工农兵劳动模范代表会议筹备委员会，于二十九日召开第一次会议。该筹委会委员业经政务院指定，由陈云、李立三、朱学范、刘澜涛、萧华、毛齐华、王新元、何长工、宋裕和、吴觉农、吴羹梅、李颉伯、李范五、李葆华、季方、陈郁、许之桢、张琴秋、杨显东、董昕、刘景范、薛暮桥、罗叔章等二十六人组成，并决定以陈云为主任，李立三、朱学范、刘澜涛、萧华为副主任，毛齐华为秘书长，李范五、董昕为副秘书长。这次会议讨论并通过关于召开全国工农兵劳动模范代表会议的计划，推定专人分别负责研究对参加大会的劳动模范代表的奖励办法，制订在劳动模范代表会议上讨论的对全国各地生产劳动模范的奖励办法。中央农业部全国总工会等单位，将根据这次会议通过的计划，具体指导所属关于推选代表的注意事项。
