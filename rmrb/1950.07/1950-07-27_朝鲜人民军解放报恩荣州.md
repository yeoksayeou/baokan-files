Title: Korean People's Army Liberates Boeun and Yeongju
Author:
Date: 1950-07-27
Page: 1

　　Xinhua News Agency, Pyongyang, 26th. The Korean People's Army High Command announced in two communiques issued on the morning and evening of the 25th that the People's Army had liberated Boeun County, northeast of Daejeon, North Chungcheong Province, on the 23rd, and Yeongju County in northern North Gyeongsang Province on the 24th. Detailed battle results are still being investigated.

### 朝鲜人民军解放报恩荣州

1950-07-27
第1版()
专栏：

　　朝鲜人民军解放报恩荣州
    【新华社平壤二十六日电】朝鲜人民军总司令部二十五日晨、晚发表的两次公报中宣布：人民军已于二十三日解放了忠清北道大田东北的报恩县，二十四日解放了庆尚北道北部的荣州县。详细战果正在清查中。
