Title: Pakistani Newspapers Oppose Illegal UN Security Council Resolution, Condemn US Imperialism's Interference in Korean Internal Affairs, Strongly Criticize Pakistani Government's Aid to South Korea
Author:
Date: 1950-07-11
Page: 1

　　Xinhua News Agency, Karachi, 8th: Most Pakistani newspapers directly or indirectly oppose the Security Council's resolution on the Korean issue, considering it illegal, and accuse the United States of interfering in Korean internal affairs. On June 28th, three major Pakistani newspapers - the Pakistan Times, the Imroze, and the Lutfakt - publicly condemned the Security Council's resolution on the Korean issue and the United States' open interference.

　　The Pakistan Maghribi, published in Lahore, pointed out on June 29th: "The Security Council's resolution on the Korean issue was passed in great haste. The Security Council was unwilling to even listen to the explanation of the North Korean representative. There is no Soviet representative in the Security Council. Therefore, this resolution is one-sided and illegal."

　　The Lahore Pakistan Times editorial strongly criticized the United States' armed intervention in Korea and the Pakistani government's decision to comply with the Security Council's resolution to aid South Korea.

### 巴基斯坦报纸反对安理会非法决议  谴责美帝干涉朝鲜内政  强烈批评巴政府援助南朝鲜

1950-07-11
第1版()
专栏：

　　巴基斯坦报纸反对安理会非法决议
    谴责美帝干涉朝鲜内政
    强烈批评巴政府援助南朝鲜
    【本报讯】新华社讯：塔斯社喀喇蚩八日讯：大多数巴基斯坦报纸均直接或间接地反对安全理事会关于朝鲜问题的决议，认为它是非法的，并指责美国干涉朝鲜内政。六月二十八日，三家巴基斯坦大报——巴基斯坦时报、伊姆洛斯报和赖弗法克特报均曾公开地谴责安全理事会关于朝鲜问题的决议、和美国公开的干涉。
    在拉合尔发行的巴基斯坦马根比报于六月二十九日指出：“安全理事会关于朝鲜问题的决议是在非常仓促当中通过的。安全理事会甚至不愿听一听北朝鲜代表的解释。安全理事会中没有苏联的代表。因此这个决议是片面的，也是非法的”。
    拉合尔巴基斯坦时报社论强烈批评美国武装干涉朝鲜，以及巴基斯坦政府决定遵照安全理事会的决议援助南朝鲜。
