Title: Korean People's Army: Kim Il-sung Appointed Supreme Commander

Author:

Date: 1950-07-07
Page: 1

　　Korean People's Army
    Kim Il-sung Appointed Supreme Commander
    [Xinhua News Agency, Pyongyang, 6th] The Standing Committee of the Supreme People's Assembly of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea issued an order on the 4th, appointing Premier Kim Il-sung as the Supreme Commander of the Korean People's Army.

### 朝鲜共和国人民军  金日成任最高司令官

1950-07-07
第1版()
专栏：

　　朝鲜共和国人民军
    金日成任最高司令官
    【新华社平壤六日电】朝鲜民主主义人民共和国最高人民会议常任委员会于四日发布命令，任命部长会议主席金日成为朝鲜民主主义人民共和国人民军最高司令官。
