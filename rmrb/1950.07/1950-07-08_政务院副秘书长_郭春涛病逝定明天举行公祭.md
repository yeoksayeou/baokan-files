Title: Deputy Secretary-General of the Government Administration Council, Guo Chuntao, Passes Away; Public Memorial Service Scheduled for Tomorrow
Author:
Date: 1950-07-08
Page: 1

　　Deputy Secretary-General of the Government Administration Council
    Guo Chuntao Passes Away; Public Memorial Service Scheduled for Tomorrow
    [Our Newspaper] Guo Chuntao, Deputy Secretary-General of the Government Administration Council of the Central People's Government and Standing Committee Member of the Central Committee of the Revolutionary Committee of the Chinese Kuomintang, passed away at 3:58 PM on June 30, 1950, at the Peking Union Medical College Hospital in Beijing. The body was placed in a coffin on July 1st and is currently lying in state at Jiaxing Temple. A public memorial service will be held at 8:00 AM on July 9th, after which the coffin will be moved to the Wan'an Public Cemetery in the Western Suburbs for burial. Mr. Guo suffered from bladder cancer and, despite undergoing surgery, succumbed to the illness due to its advanced stage. The Government Administration Council, in conjunction with the Revolutionary Committee of the Chinese Kuomintang and other relevant organizations, immediately established a funeral committee composed of 47 members, including Zhou Enlai, Lin Boqu, Dong Biwu, Li Jishen, He Xiangning, Zhang Lan, Shen Junru, Guo Moruo, Chen Shutong, Huang Yanpei, Zhang Bojun, and Ma Xulun.
    Mr. Guo was a native of Ling County, Hunan Province, and was 55 years old. He participated in the May Fourth Movement in his early years. During the Great Revolution, he served as a member of the Central Committee of the Kuomintang and Director of the Political Department of the Northwest National Army. During the War of Resistance Against Japan, he participated in initiating and organizing the Association of Comrades of the Three People's Principles and devoted himself to the anti-Chiang movement within the Kuomintang's democratic faction. Before the liberation of Shanghai, he made significant contributions to underground defection work. He represented the Association of Comrades of the Three People's Principles of the Chinese Kuomintang at the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference and was elected as a member of the National Committee. After the establishment of the Central People's Government, he was appointed as Deputy Secretary-General of the Government Administration Council and concurrently as a member of the Political and Legal Committee of the Government Administration Council. In the winter of 1949, the representative meeting of the democratic faction of the Kuomintang elected him as a Standing Committee Member of the Central Committee of the Revolutionary Committee of the Chinese Kuomintang.

### 政务院副秘书长  郭春涛病逝定明天举行公祭

1950-07-08
第1版()
专栏：

　　政务院副秘书长
    郭春涛病逝定明天举行公祭
    【本报讯】中央人民政府政务院副秘书长、中国国民党革命委员会中央常务委员郭春涛于一九五零年六月三十日下午三时五十八分在北京北京医院病逝后已于七月一日入殓，现停柩嘉兴寺，定于七月九日上午八时起举行公祭并移柩至西郊万安公墓安葬。郭氏系患膀胱癌，在施行手术后，以患病时日过久，终于不治。政务院会同中国国民党革命委员会等有关方面当即组织治丧委员会，由周恩来、林伯渠、董必武、李济深、何香凝、张澜、沈钧儒、郭沫若、陈叔通、黄炎培、章伯钧、马叙伦等四十七人任治丧委员。
    郭氏系湖南酃县人，年五十五岁。早年参加五四运动。大革命时代任国民党中委及西北国民军政治部主任。抗战中参加发起组织三民主义同志联合会，致力于国民党民主派反蒋运动；上海解放前，对于地下策反工作，颇多贡献。人民政协开会时代表中国国民党三民主义同志联合会参加，被选为全国委员会委员；中央人民政府成立后，被任为政务院副秘书长兼政务院政法委员会委员；一九四九年冬，国民党民主派代表会议推选为中国国民党革命委员会中央常务委员。
