Title: Democratic Parties and Industry and Commerce Representatives of Beijing Congratulate the CCP on its 29th Anniversary; Comrade Peng Zhen Personally Hosted and Sent Letters of Thanks
Author:
Date: 1950-07-03
Page: 1

Beijing Municipal Democratic Parties and Industry and Commerce Representatives Congratulate the CCP on its 29th Anniversary
	Comrade Peng Zhen Personally Hosted and Sent Letters of Thanks
	[Our Newspaper] To commemorate the 29th anniversary of the founding of the Communist Party of China, representatives from various democratic parties in Beijing, including Yang Jixian, Wang Maichu, Ling Qijun, Qiu Elun, and Xu Yueru, representative of the Preparatory Committee of the Beijing Federation of Industry and Commerce, visited the Beijing Municipal Committee of the Communist Party of China on that day to offer congratulations and present congratulatory messages, banners, and letters. Peng Zhen, Secretary of the Beijing Municipal Committee of the CPC, personally hosted them and sent letters of thanks in return.
	The congratulatory messages from the Beijing Branch of the Revolutionary Committee of the Chinese Kuomintang, the Beijing Branch of the China Democratic League, the Beijing Branch of the China National Democratic Construction Association, and the Beijing Party Affairs Consolidation Committee of the Chinese Peasants' and Workers' Democratic Party stated:
	China has the Communist Party, and with the leadership of Mao Zedong, the foundation for the certain victory of the Chinese revolution was laid, and the certain success of China's revolutionary construction was guaranteed. We are willing to follow the Communist Party and advance together forever under the banner of Mao Zedong.
	The congratulatory letter from the Preparatory Committee of the Beijing Federation of Industry and Commerce stated:
	On the occasion of the great anniversary of the 29th anniversary of the founding of the Communist Party of China, we respectfully offer a small gift to celebrate your glorious victory. Long live the New China! Long live your party and the leader of the people of the whole country, Comrade Mao Zedong!
	The original wording of the congratulatory banner from the same association is as follows:
	Commemorating the 29th Anniversary of the Founding of the Communist Party of China
	Our city's industry and commerce, united as one, follow your leadership and strive for the rapid development of economic construction!
					Respectfully presented by the Preparatory Committee of the Beijing Federation of Industry and Commerce

### 京市各民主党派及工商界代表祝贺中共诞生廿九周年  彭真同志亲予招待并复函致谢

1950-07-03
第1版()
专栏：

　　京市各民主党派及工商界代表祝贺中共诞生廿九周年
    彭真同志亲予招待并复函致谢
    【本报讯】前日为中国共产党成立二十九周年纪念，京市各民主党派代表杨集贤、王麦初、凌其峻、丘锷仑和北京市工商业联合会筹备委员会代表徐月如等，曾于当日分别到中国共产党北京市委员会致贺，并送贺词、贺幛、贺函等。中共北京市委书记彭真亲自招待，并分别复函致谢。
    中国国民党革命委员会北京市分部，中国民主同盟北京市支部，中国民主建国会北京市分会，中国农工民主党北京市党务整理委员会贺词称：
    中国有了共产党，有了毛泽东的领导，才奠定了中国革命一定胜利的基础，才保证中国革命建设的一定成功。我们愿在毛泽东的旗帜下，永远跟着共产党一致前进。
    北京市工商业联合会筹备委员会贺函称：
    欣逢中国共产党成立二十九周年伟大的纪念日，我们谨备薄礼，庆祝你们光荣的胜利。庆祝新中国万岁！庆祝你们党及全国人民的领袖毛泽东同志万岁！
    又该会贺幛原辞如下：
    中国共产党成立二十九周年纪念
    我们全市的工商业，团结一致，追随着你们的领导，为经济建设的突飞猛进而奋斗！
                                  北京市工商业联合会筹备委员会敬贺
