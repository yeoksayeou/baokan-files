Title: Launch a Signature Campaign to Defend World Peace!
Author:
Date: 1950-07-08
Page: 6

Launch a Signature Campaign to Defend World Peace!
    The people of London shouted at a meeting to welcome the World Peace Congress Executive Bureau: "Welcome international peace fighters!" "Strive for friendship and understanding among all countries!"
    Workers at the China People's Printing Factory in Beijing enthusiastically signed in the factory.
    Commanders and fighters of the Chinese People's Liberation Army sign the peace declaration. (Photo by Meng Zhaorui, North China Pictorial Agency)
    Children from various countries present flowers to the World Peace Congress. Behind the children are Czech representatives Hordynova and Mrs. Spurna.
    Masses attending the conference
    French General Confederation of Labor Secretary Lellep (right) speaks at a mass rally in London welcoming the World Peace Congress Executive Bureau.
    Liberated old farmers from Balizhuang in the suburbs of Beijing sign the peace declaration.
    Representatives of various countries attending the World Peace Congress Executive Bureau (from left to right): Liu Ningyi (China), Alexander Korneichuk (Soviet Union), Paul Robeson (United States), D'Arboussier (Africa), Bernal (United Kingdom).
    Representatives from various countries at the London meeting of the Standing Committee Executive Bureau of the World Congress of Supporters of Peace.
    Yang Shoutang, director of the state-owned Jiheng Farm in Nanliangzhuang, Jixian County, Hebei, Lu Tianlin and female tractor drivers sign the peace declaration.
    Men, women, old and young in Balizhuang, a suburb of Beijing, enthusiastically participate in the peace signature campaign.
    An old lady holds her baby and also comes to sign, and puts her fingerprint under her name. She said: "We hope for lasting world peace"
    Yan'an children sign the peace declaration. (Contribution from the All-China Women's Federation)
    News Photography Bureau Qian Sijie Ge Liqun photographed

### 开展保卫世界和平的签名运动！（图片）

1950-07-08
第6版()
专栏：

　　开展保卫世界和平的签名运动！
    伦敦人民开会欢迎世界和大执行局会议时高呼：“欢迎国际和平斗士！”“争取世界各国友好谅解！”
    北京中国人民印刷厂的工人在厂房里热烈签名。
    中国人民解放军指战员在和平宣言上签名。（华北画报社孟昭瑞摄）
    各国儿童向世界和大献花。在儿童后面的是捷克代表荷弟诺娃，斯普尔纳夫人。
    参加大会的群众
    法国总工会秘书莱勒普（右）在伦敦人民欢迎世界大执行局会议群众大会上演说。
    京郊人八里庄的翻身老农民在和平宣言上签名。
    出席和大执行局的各国代表（自左至右）：刘宁一（中国），亚力山大·科耳涅海克（苏联），保尔·罗伯逊（美国），达波赛（非洲），伯尔拿（英国）。
    世界拥护和平大会常设委员会执行局伦敦会议的各国代表们。
    河北冀县南良庄的国营冀衡农场场长杨寿堂、卢田林和女拖拉机手在和平宣言上签名。
    京郊八里庄的男女老幼热烈地参加和平签名。
    老太太抱着他的小孩子也来签名，并在她自己的名字下按上手印。她说：“我们希望世界永久和平”
    延安儿童在和平宣言上签名。（全国妇联稿）
    新闻摄影局  钱嗣杰  葛力群摄
