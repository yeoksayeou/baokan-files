Title: Women Activists in Four Districts of Beijing Gather for a Symposium to Oppose American Imperialist Aggression
Author:
Date: 1950-07-24
Page: 1

　　Beijing – The Fourth District Women's Work Group of Beijing convened a symposium at 2:00 PM on July 20th for women activists from various public security police stations in the district to discuss opposition to American imperialist aggression in Taiwan and Korea. More than 100 women attended. The women unanimously expressed their indignation at the aggressive actions of American imperialism, and all signed their names on the peace declaration on the spot. One woman said, "After we go back, we must tell everyone about these things and let everyone rise up to oppose American imperialist aggression in Taiwan and Korea." (Fourth District Propaganda Department)

### 京市四区妇女积极分子集会座谈反对美帝侵略

1950-07-24
第1版()
专栏：

　　京市四区妇女积极分子集会座谈反对美帝侵略
    【本报讯】京市第四区妇女工作组于七月二十日下午二时召集该区各公安派出所的妇女积极分子座谈反对美帝侵略台湾朝鲜问题，到有妇女一百余人。妇女们对美帝的侵略行为一致表示愤慨，当场在和平宣言上全体签上自己的姓名。一个妇女说：“我们回去后一定把这些事都告诉大家，让大家都起来反对美帝侵略台湾朝鲜。”          （四区宣传部）
