Title: Coordinating with the People's Army's Heroic Advance, South Korean People's Guerrillas Launch Extensive Attacks; People's Air Force Shoots Down Four American Planes Over Osan and Incheon
Author:
Date: 1950-07-15
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency, Pyongyang, 14th. The General Headquarters of the Korean People's Army of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea announced in a war report on the evening of the 13th that the People's Army units continued their offensive on all fronts. The People's Army Air Force units provided cover for the People's Army ground forces on the entire front and fiercely attacked the enemy's land and air forces. On the 12th, People's Army Air Force squadrons launched a fierce attack on a formation of more than 20 American bombers and fighters over Osan. As a result, two American B-17 bombers and one fighter plane were shot down. On the same day, one American B-25 bomber was shot down over Incheon.

Xinhua News Agency, Pyongyang, 14th. According to a comprehensive report by the Korean Central News Agency: People's guerrillas in Gyeongsangnam-do, Gyeongsangbuk-do, Jeollanam-do, and Jeollabuk-do are actively launching operations, threatening the enemy's rear and intercepting fleeing remnants of the puppet army, in coordination with the People's Army's heroic advance. People's guerrillas in the Daebaek Mountains of Gyeongsangbuk-do are launching extensive attacks in areas such as Sangju, Gimcheon, Uiseong (all three located on railway lines), Seonsan, and Gunwi. People's guerrillas in the Bohyeon Mountains, with the assistance of the local people, are constantly attacking puppet troops in the Yeongcheon area and disrupting the puppet army's communication networks and power plants in the triangle area between Daegu, Yeongcheon, and Gyeongju (all three located on railway lines).

People's guerrillas in the Jirisan Mountains of Gyeongsangnam-do are actively conducting propaganda work and purging reactionary police and secret agents in areas such as Busan, Hamyang, and Miryang. Many patriotic youths in the area have joined the guerrillas.

People's guerrillas in Jeollanam-do attacked rural puppet police branch offices in Gwangju, a transportation hub in the province, as well as Hwasun, Gokseong, Suncheon, and other areas for three days from the 8th to the 10th, killing and wounding many puppet police officers.

A unit of the People's guerrillas in the Jirisan Mountains of Jeollabuk-do attacked Guchu District of Sunchang County on the night of the 10th and engaged in a fierce battle with the Sunchang County police force. As a result, more than a dozen puppet police officers, including the chief of the puppet police station, were killed, and many weapons were seized. In addition, People's guerrillas in the Deogyu Mountains are also actively operating in the Muju County area of Jeollabuk-do.

### 配合人民军的英勇进军  南朝鲜人民游击队广泛出击  人民空军在鸟致院和仁川上空击落美机四架

1950-07-15
第4版()
专栏：

　　配合人民军的英勇进军
    南朝鲜人民游击队广泛出击
    人民空军在鸟致院和仁川上空击落美机四架
    【新华社平壤十四日电】朝鲜民主主义人民共和国人民军总司令部十三日晚发表战报称：人民军部队在各战线上继续进攻。人民军空军部队在整个战线上掩护人民军地上部队前进，并猛烈攻击敌陆空军部队。十二日，人民军空军机群在鸟致院上空对美轰炸机、战斗机二十余架的编队施以猛烈攻击。结果，击落美Ｂ十七型轰炸机两架，战斗机一架。同日，在仁川附近上空击落美Ｂ二十五型轰炸机一架。
    【新华社平壤十四日电】综合朝鲜中央通讯社报道：庆尚南道、庆尚北道和全罗南道、全罗北道的人民游击队，刻正配合人民军的英勇进军，积极展开活动，威胁敌后，并截击溃逃的残余伪军。庆尚北道大白山区的人民游击队，在尚州、金泉、义城（以上三地均在铁道线上）、善山、军威等地区中，正广泛出击。普贤山区的人民游击队，在当地人民的协助下，正不断袭击永川地区的伪军，并在大邱、永川、庆州（以上三地均位于铁道线上）之间的三角地带，破坏着伪军的通信联络网与发电所。
    庆尚南道智异山区的人民游击队，在釜山、咸阳、密阳等地区，积极进行宣传工作，肃清反动警察与特务分子。该区许多爱国青年，已参加了游击队。
    全罗南道的人民游击队，从八日到十日的三天中，曾先后袭击了该道交通中枢光州及和顺、谷城、顺天等地区的乡区伪警察支署，毙伤伪警甚多。
    全罗北道智异山区的人民游击队一部，于十日夜攻入淳昌县的龟秋区，并与淳昌县的警察队展开激战。结果，毙伪警察署长以下十余名，缴获武器多件。此外，德裕山区的人民游击队，也在全罗北道的茂朱县境积极活动。
