Title: Promote the Peace Signature Campaign
Author: Ma Xulun
Date: 1950-07-03
Page: 5

　　Promote the Peace Signature Campaign
    Ma Xulun
    The peace signature campaign is a political action of peace-loving people around the world. The liberated Chinese people, to express their infinite loyalty and strong will to defend world peace, have written their names on the declaration banning atomic weapons by the hundreds of thousands.
    Promote this movement through all organizational forces and using various forms of propaganda and education. Strive for hundreds of millions of Chinese people to participate in the peace signature, so that men and women, young and old, in every corner of the country have the opportunity to write out their strong will to love peace. Let the imperialist war-mongers tremble before our powerful peace camp!

### 推广和平签名运动
马叙伦
1950-07-03
第5版()
专栏：

　　推广和平签名运动
    马叙伦
    和平签名运动是全世界爱好和平的人民的政治行动。解放了的中国人民为表示对保卫世界和平的无限忠诚与坚强意志，已成千成万地在禁用原子武器的宣言上写下了自己的名字。
    通过一切组织力量，运用各种宣传教育方式来推广这一运动。争取成万万的中国人民来参加和平签名，使全国每个角落里的男女老幼都有机会写出自己热爱和平的坚强意志。让帝国主义的战争贩子们在我们强大的和平阵营面前发抖吧！
