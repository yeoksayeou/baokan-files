Title: Indonesian Circles Condemn US Imperialist Aggression Against Korea
Author:
Date: 1950-07-31
Page: 4

　　Indonesian Circles Condemn US Imperialist Aggression Against Korea
    [Xinhua News Agency, The Hague, 30th] TASS, The Hague, 29th: The Dutch-Indian newspaper "News and People" reported: Indonesian public opinion is expressing anger at the American intervention in Korea. The "People's Daily" published a statement by the proletarian party leader, Indonesian parliamentarian, and engineer, Soekarni, strongly condemning American aggression in Korea. Soekarni stated: The United States is masking its aggression under the banner of the United Nations, while the United Nations organization has been transformed into a tool of American plans.
    The newspaper also reported: Indonesian trade unions have expressed solidarity with the Korean people who are fighting against the American aggressors.

### 印尼各界斥美帝侵略朝鲜

1950-07-31
第4版()
专栏：

　　印尼各界斥美帝侵略朝鲜
    【新华社三十日讯】塔斯社海牙二十九日讯：荷印新闻和人民报报道：印度尼西亚公众对美国干涉朝鲜表示愤怒。人民报发表了无产阶级党领袖、印尼议会议员、工程师苏加尼痛斥美国在朝鲜的侵略的声明。苏加尼称：美国正在联合国组织的旗帜之下，掩护他们的侵略，而联合国组织已被改变为美国计划的工具。
    报纸同时报道：印尼各工会表示声援正在同美国侵略者作战的朝鲜人民。
