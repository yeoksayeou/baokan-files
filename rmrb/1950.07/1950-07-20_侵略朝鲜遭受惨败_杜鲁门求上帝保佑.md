Title: Crushing Defeat in the Korean Invasion: Truman Prays for God's Blessing
Author:
Date: 1950-07-20
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, July 19th: U.S. President Truman, only a little over twenty days after issuing a menacing statement on armed intervention in Korea, is now seeking God's blessing. According to a French News Agency dispatch from Washington on the 14th, Truman prayed with a preacher named Graham on the 14th, asking for God's blessing to help him and his country through the current difficult period. Graham also said that Truman is considering a national day of prayer and repentance.

### 侵略朝鲜遭受惨败  杜鲁门求上帝保佑

1950-07-20
第1版()
专栏：

　　侵略朝鲜遭受惨败
    杜鲁门求上帝保佑
    【新华社十九日讯】美总统杜鲁门在发表了气势汹汹的武装干涉朝鲜的声明仅仅二十多天之后，现在却求神保佑了。据法新社华盛顿十四日电称，杜鲁门在十四日曾与一位名叫格拉汉的布道员一同祈求上帝的保佑，以帮助他与他的国家度过目前困难的时期。格拉汉并说：杜鲁门正在考虑一个全国性的祈祷与忏悔日。
