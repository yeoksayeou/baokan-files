Title: Soviet Foreign Cultural Association to Hold Lecture Tonight
Author: Chen Yingnan
Date: 1950-07-08
Page: 6

　　The Soviet Foreign Cultural Association to Hold Lecture Tonight
　　Today, July 8th, 1944, the Presidium of the Supreme Soviet of the Soviet Union issued an order: "To strengthen assistance to pregnant women, mothers with many children, and single mothers, to strengthen the protection of motherhood and children, to establish the honorable title of 'Mother Heroine,' the 'Mother's Glory' Order, and the 'Mother's Medal.'" To celebrate the sixth anniversary of this glorious day, the Beijing Sino-Soviet Friendship Association and the Soviet Foreign Cultural Association are scheduled to hold a lecture at 7:30 PM today at 19 Nanheyan Street, with Sufulonova speaking on "The Concern of the Soviet State and Party for Mothers and Children." All those holding admission tickets and "Soviet Introduction" long-term lecture passes are welcome to attend.
　　(Chen Yingnan)

### 苏对外文协今晚举行讲演会
陈应楠
1950-07-08
第6版()
专栏：

　　苏对外文协今晚举行讲演会
    一九四四年的今天（七月八日），苏联最高苏维埃主席团下令：“加强对孕妇、多子女的母亲、单身的母亲等的帮助、加强保护母性与儿童，设立尊贵的‘母亲英雄’称号，‘母亲之光’勋章及‘母亲奖章’”。北京市中苏友协与苏联对外文化协会为庆祝这个光辉日子的六周年，定于今日下午七时半在南河沿十九号举行讲演会，由苏夫洛诺娃讲《苏联国家与党对于母亲和儿童的关怀”。凡持有入场券和“苏联介绍”长期听讲证者，届时均可前往参加。
    （陈应楠）
