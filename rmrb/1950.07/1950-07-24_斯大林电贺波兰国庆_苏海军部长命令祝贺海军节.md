Title: Stalin Sends Congratulations on Polish National Day, Soviet Naval Minister Orders Congratulations on Navy Day
Author:
Date: 1950-07-24
Page: 4

　　Xinhua News Agency, July 23rd: Warsaw, Polish News Agency, July 21st: Marshal Stalin sent a telegram to the Polish Prime Minister on the occasion of the Polish National Day, the telegram reads:
    "On the occasion of your National Day - the anniversary of the rebirth of the Polish Republic, please accept my warm congratulations to the Polish people, to the Government of the Polish Republic, and to yourself. The Soviet people wish the fraternal Polish people further achievements in their efforts to build a democratic People's Poland."
    Xinhua News Agency, July 23rd: TASS, Moscow: Soviet Naval Minister Yumashev issued the following order on July 23rd, Soviet Navy Day:
    Comrades sailors, soldiers, and non-commissioned officers! Comrades lieutenants, captains, and generals!
    Today our country is celebrating Navy Day, celebrating the new achievements made by naval personnel and shipbuilders in carrying out the tasks they face.
    In the struggle against the enemies of our motherland, the Soviet Navy has proven that they are worthy of the trust the people have placed in them. Together with the Soviet Army, they vigilantly guard the security and national interests of our great Soviet motherland.
    The Soviet people, the Bolshevik Party, and Comrade Stalin himself are tirelessly concerned with strengthening our navy. The responsibility of the Soviet naval personnel is to do everything possible to master combat technology and improve the military preparedness of ships and vessels.
    On the occasion of this festival - Navy Day, I salute you and extend my congratulations.
    I wish all personnel of the Soviet Navy and all workers of the shipbuilding industry further achievements in fulfilling the tasks entrusted to them by the Soviet government and our teacher and leader, Comrade Stalin.
    In commemoration of Soviet Navy Day, I order: Today, July 23rd, warships of all fleets and flotillas and coastal batteries shall fire a 20-gun salute.
    Long live the Soviet Navy!
    Long live the heroic Soviet people!
    Long live our government!
    Long live the glorious All-Union Communist Party (Bolsheviks)!
    Long live our teacher and leader - the great Stalin!
         Soviet Naval Minister, Admiral Yumashev (signed)

### 斯大林电贺波兰国庆  苏海军部长命令祝贺海军节

1950-07-24
第4版()
专栏：

　　斯大林电贺波兰国庆
    苏海军部长命令祝贺海军节
    【新华社二十三日讯】波兰通讯社华沙二十一日讯：斯大林大元帅于波兰国庆日致电波兰总理祝贺，电文如下：
    “欣逢贵国国庆日——波兰共和国的再生纪念日，请接受我对于波兰人民、对于波兰共和国政府和对于阁下的热烈的祝贺。苏联人民祝贺兄弟的波兰人民在致力于建设民主的人民波兰中获得进一步的成就。”
    【新华社二十三日讯】塔斯社莫斯科讯：苏联海军部部长尤马谢夫于二十三日苏联海军节发布命令如下：
    水手、士兵、士官同志们！尉官、校官、将军同志们！
    今天我国正在庆祝海军节，正在庆祝海军人员和造船工人在执行他们面临的任务中所获得的新成就。
    在反对我们祖国的敌人的斗争中，苏联海军已证明他们无愧于人民给他们的信任。他们和苏联陆军一道警惕地警卫着我们伟大的苏维埃祖国的安全和国家利益。
    苏联人民、布尔什维克党和斯大林同志本人，都孜孜不倦地关怀着加强我国的海军。苏联海军人员的责任，就是用尽一切方法去精通战斗技术，并提高船只与舰只的军事准备。
    欣逢这个节日——海军节，我向你们致敬并致贺。
    谨祝苏联海军全体人员和造船工业全体工作人员在完成苏维埃政府和我们的导师与领袖斯大林同志所交付的任务中，获得进一步的成就。
    为了纪念苏联海军节，我命令：今天，七月二十三日，各舰队与艇队的军舰与各港湾炮台鸣礼炮二十响。
    苏联海军万岁！
    英勇的苏联人民万岁！
    我们的政府万岁！
    光荣的联共（布）党万岁！
    我们的导师与领袖——伟大的斯大林万岁！
         苏联海军部长、海军上将尤马谢夫（签字）
