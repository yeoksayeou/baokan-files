Title: Our Army Captured More Than 300 Bandits in the Battle to Liberate the Shengsi Islands
Author:
Date: 1950-07-14
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Nanjing, 13th. The results of the liberation of the Shengsi Islands by a unit of the Third Field Army of the People's Liberation Army on the 7th and 8th of this month are as follows: more than 300 bandits, including Zhang Guanjun, the Shengsi Detachment Leader, were captured; 15 light machine guns, 44 pistols, more than 500 rifles and carbines, more than 5,000 rounds of ammunition, and 3 radio stations were seized.

### 解放嵊泗列岛之役我军俘匪三百多人

1950-07-14
第1版()
专栏：

　　解放嵊泗列岛之役我军俘匪三百多人
    【新华社南京十三日电】人民解放军第三野战军某部于本月七、八日两日解放嵊泗列岛的战果如下：俘匪嵊泗支队长张冠军以下三百余人，缴获轻机枪十五挺，短枪四十四支，步枪、卡宾枪五百余支，子弹五千余发，电台三部。
