Title: Coastal Areas Not Affected by Typhoon
Author:
Date: 1950-07-20
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, Shanghai, 19th—According to a report from the Shanghai Meteorological Observatory: At 8:00 AM this morning, the typhoon was located in the sea southwest of Kyushu, Japan, at 29.5 degrees north latitude and 128.5 degrees east longitude. It is turning north-northeast, heading towards Kyushu, Japan. The lower reaches of the Yangtze River and the Shanghai area are no longer affected. Other coastal areas of China will also be spared from the typhoon's impact.

### 我沿海地区不受台风影响

1950-07-20
第1版()
专栏：

　　我沿海地区不受台风影响
    【新华社上海十九日电】据上海气象台报告：今晨八时台风位于日本九州西南洋面，该处为北纬二十九度半，东经一百二十八度半，转向北东北，往日本九州进行。长江下游及上海地区已无影响。中国其他沿海地区，亦可免于该台风的袭击。
