Title: Strengthen Anti-Spy and Anti-Special Agent Propaganda: Films Like "The Invisible Front" Screened on Tour in Various Factories and Mines
Author:
Date: 1950-07-18
Page: 3

To strengthen the anti-spy and anti-special agent propaganda and education for the broad masses of workers and staff in various industrial and mining enterprises in the capital, and to raise their vigilance, the Beijing Municipal Public Security Bureau, in addition to holding touring anti-special agent safety picture exhibitions, also toured seventeen units, including the Shijingshan Iron and Steel Plant, the Power Plant, the Changxindian Machine Factory, the Beijing General Machinery Factory, the Mentougou Coal Mine, the People's Printing Factory, the 70th Arsenal, the Qinghe Woolen Mill, the Electric Power Company, and the Telecommunications Bureau, from June 25 to July 6, successively screening the films "The Invisible Front" and "Radiant Light." More than 13,000 workers and staff participated. This had a great educational significance for the broad masses of workers and staff.

### 加强防奸防特宣传  《无形的战线》等影片在各厂矿巡回放映

1950-07-18
第3版()
专栏：

　　加强防奸防特宣传
    《无形的战线》等影片在各厂矿巡回放映
    【本报讯】京市公安局为加强对首都各工矿企业广大职工的防奸防特宣传教育，及提高其警惕性，除举办巡回的防特安全图片展览外，并自六月二十五日至七月六日，分别在石景山钢铁厂、发电厂、长辛店机厂、北京机器总厂、门头沟煤矿、人民印刷厂、七十兵工厂、清河制呢厂、电业公司、电信总局等十七个单位中先后巡回放映了《无形的战线》及《光芒万丈》两电影片。共有职工一万三千余人参加。对广大职工教育意义很大。
