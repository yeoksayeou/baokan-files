Title: Celebrating Army Day·Opposing American Aggression: Beijing to Hold Demonstration Rally on "August 1st"; Preparatory Committee Established Yesterday and Actively Carrying Out Various Preparatory Work
Author:
Date: 1950-07-25
Page: 1

【Beijing News Agency News】Beijing will hold a demonstration rally on August 1st to celebrate Army Day and oppose American aggression against Taiwan and Korea. Representatives from more than 20 units, including democratic parties, people's organizations, and organs, troops, and central organs in Beijing, established the Preparatory Committee for the Beijing All-Circles Celebration of Army Day and Opposition to American Aggression Against Taiwan and Korea Demonstration Rally yesterday (24th) and are actively carrying out various preparatory work.

The preparatory meeting was chaired by Comrade Peng Zhen, who explained the significance of holding the demonstration rally: one is to commemorate "August 1st" Army Day, and the other is to oppose American aggression against Taiwan and Korea. This demonstration rally is of extremely great significance. Subsequently, Zhang Zhixiang, Deputy Director of the Political Department of the North China Military Region, Xue Zizheng, Secretary-General of the Municipal People's Government, Wang Changjiang, Deputy Chief of Staff of the North China Military Region, Zhang Minghe, Deputy Director of the Public Security Bureau, Liu Ren, Vice Chairman of the Municipal Federation of Trade Unions, and representatives of other organizations discussed issues such as the name of the conference, venue layout, the number of participants in the demonstration, and propaganda work, and assigned the work of various departments of the preparatory committee. The preparatory committee established a secretariat, with Xue Zizheng as the secretary-general; the general command of the meeting was in charge of Tang Yanjie, Liu Ren, Xiao Ming, Gu Qizong, etc.; the propaganda department was in charge of Zhang Zhixiang and Liao Mosha, and Comrade Peng Zhen was publicly elected as the chairman of the preparatory committee. The preparatory committee for the conference will officially start work from today, located in the east wing room outside the Meridian Gate, telephone numbers are Wuju 3609 and Wuju 3715.

### 庆祝建军节·反对美国侵略  北京将于“八一”举行示威大会  筹委会昨成立正积极进行各项筹备工作

1950-07-25
第1版()
专栏：

　　庆祝建军节·反对美国侵略
    北京将于“八一”举行示威大会
    筹委会昨成立正积极进行各项筹备工作
    【京市新闻处讯】北京各界将于八月一日举行示威大会，庆祝八一建军节、反对美国侵略台湾朝鲜。北京市各民主党派、人民团体、及机关、部队和中央各机关等二十余单位的代表于昨（二十四）日成立北京各界庆祝八一建军节反对美国侵略台湾朝鲜示威大会的筹备委员会，积极进行各项筹备工作。
    筹备会由彭真同志主席，说明举行示威大会的意义，一个是纪念“八一”建军节，一个是反对美国侵略台湾朝鲜。这个示威大会具有极重大的意义。次由华北军区政治部副主任张致祥、市人民政府秘书长薛子正、华北军区副参谋长王长江、公安局副局长张明河、市总工会副主席刘仁及其他团体代表，就大会名称、会场布置、参加示威的人数、宣传工作等问题进行讨论，并分配筹备会各部门工作。筹备会分设秘书处、由薛子正任秘书长；大会指挥部，由唐延杰、刘仁、萧明、古奇踪等负责；宣传部由张致祥、廖沫沙负责，并公推彭真同志为筹备会主席。大会筹备会从今日起正式办公，地点在午门外东朝房，电话号码为五局三六零九和五局三七一五。
