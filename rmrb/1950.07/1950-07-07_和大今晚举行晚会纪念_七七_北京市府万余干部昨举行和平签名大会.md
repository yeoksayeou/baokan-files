Title: Peace Conference to Be Held Tonight in He Da Commemorating “July 7th”; Over Ten Thousand Cadres from Beijing Municipal Government Held Peace Signature Rally Yesterday

Author:
Date: 1950-07-07
Page: 1

　　He Da to Hold Evening Party Tonight to Commemorate "July 7th"
　　Over Ten Thousand Cadres from Beijing Municipal Government Held Peace Signature Rally Yesterday
　　[Reported by this newspaper] The China Committee for the Defense of World Peace, in order to commemorate the 13th anniversary of the "July 7th" Resistance War and further promote the peace signature campaign nationwide, has decided to hold a lecture meeting at the Zhongshan Park Concert Hall on the evening of "July 7th". Liu Ningyi, the representative of the China Committee for the Defense of World Peace, will convey the resolutions of the London meeting of the Executive Bureau of the World Peace Congress. Bai Yun will convey the resolutions of the Third Standing Committee of the International Democratic Women's Federation for the World Peace Conference, and Ai Qing will report on the work of the Peace Propaganda Team.
　　The lecture meeting is scheduled to be held at 7:00 PM on the evening of "July 7th" at the Zhongshan Park Concert Hall. The evening program is rich and includes dramas, Yangko dances, and musical performances by the Central Academy of Drama. If it rains, the date will be changed. Please pay attention to the news in this newspaper and keep your tickets.
　　[Another News] Xiao San, the head of the China Peace Preservation Propaganda Group, and members Ai Qing, Zhao Zhongchi, Huang Zhensheng, and others returned to Beijing on July 5th. The group departed from Beijing on May 24th and has propagandized in eight cities including Nanjing, Shanghai, Hangzhou, Guangzhou, Changsha, Wuhan, Zhengzhou, and Xi'an. The propaganda group was warmly welcomed by the local people everywhere. In more than a month of work, they gave 104 lectures and held symposiums with over 160,000 listeners from all walks of life; gave 11 radio speeches; and screened more than 50 peace preservation propaganda films, with more than 150,000 viewers.
　　[Beijing News Agency] More than 10,000 cadres of the Beijing Municipal People's Government held a World Peace Preservation Signature Campaign meeting at the Labor People's Cultural Palace on the evening of the 5th. Vice Mayor Wu Han gave a report at the meeting. Vice Mayor Wu's report was divided into three parts: 1. The current international situation; 2. The development of the world peace and democracy movement in the past year; 3. The fundamental understanding of the issue of war and peace. A movie was also shown after the meeting.

### 和大今晚举行晚会纪念“七七”  北京市府万余干部昨举行和平签名大会

1950-07-07
第1版()
专栏：

　　和大今晚举行晚会纪念“七七”
    北京市府万余干部昨举行和平签名大会
    【本报讯】中国保卫世界和平大会工作委员会为纪念“七七”抗战十三周年，并进一步在全国范围内推进和平签名运动，决定于“七七”晚在中山公园音乐堂举行讲演会，请中国和大代表刘宁一传达世界拥护和平大会执行局伦敦会议的决议，白芸传达国际民主妇联执行世界保卫和平大会第三次常委会决议，并由艾青报告和平宣传团的工作。
    演讲会定“七七”晚七时在中山公园音乐堂举行，晚会节目丰富，有话剧、秧歌剧及音乐演奏，由中央戏剧学院演出，如天雨改期举行。请注意本报消息，保存入场券。
    【又讯】中国保卫世界和平宣传团团长萧三及团员艾青、赵仲池、黄振声等一行已于七月五日返抵北京。该团于五月二十四日由京出发，曾在南京、上海、杭州、广州、长沙、武汉、郑州、西安等八个城市宣传。宣传团在各地都受到当地人民的热烈欢迎。一个多月的工作中共讲演和召开座谈会一百零四次，各界听众在十六万人以上；作了十一次广播演讲；放映宣传保卫和平的影片五十余场，观众在十五万人以上。
    【京市新闻处讯】京市人民政府干部一万余人五日晚在劳动人民文化宫举行保卫世界和平签名运动大会。会上由吴晗副市长作报告。吴副市长的报告分三部分：一、目前的国际形势；二、一年来的世界和平民主运动的发展；三、对战争与和平问题的根本认识。会后并放映电影。
