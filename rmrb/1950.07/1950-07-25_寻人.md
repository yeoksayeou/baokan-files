Seeking a Person

　　Yan Zong, son of Guo Mantu (from Donglai), is seeking his father and his uncle Guo Bantu (from Donghai). Anyone who knows of their whereabouts, please write to Liu Gongqin, Central Primary School, Dongluodai Town, Chengdu, Sichuan, to be forwarded to Guo Yanzong, or send a message via the Central People's Broadcasting Station.

### 寻人

1950-07-25
第6版()
专栏：

　　寻人
    郭曼图（东莱）之子彦宗寻父及叔郭版图（东海）。有知下落者，请函四川成都东落带镇中心小学刘公勤转郭彦宗或寄中央人民广播电台转达。
