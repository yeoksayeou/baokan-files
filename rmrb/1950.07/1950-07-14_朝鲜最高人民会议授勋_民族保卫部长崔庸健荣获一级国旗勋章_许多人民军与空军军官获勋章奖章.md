Title: Korean Supreme People's Assembly Awards Medals: Minister of National Defense Choi Yong-kun Awarded First-Class National Flag Order; Many People's Army and Air Force Officers Awarded Medals and Decorations
Author:
Date: 1950-07-14
Page: 1

　　Xinhua News Agency, Pyongyang, 11th—On July 10th, the birthday of General Choi Yong-kun, Minister of National Defense of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea, the Standing Committee of the Supreme People's Assembly of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea, in view of General Choi Yong-kun's outstanding contributions in the past anti-Japanese struggle and in the establishment of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea, especially in the construction of the Republic's armed forces, issued an order on the 9th, awarding him the First-Class National Flag Order.
　　Xinhua News Agency, Pyongyang, 13th—The Standing Committee of the Supreme People's Assembly of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea issued an award order on the 12th, stating: The Standing Committee of the Supreme People's Assembly of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea, in recognition of the following People's Army and Air Force officers and non-commissioned officers who have performed meritorious service in annihilating the enemy in the just war for the unification and independence of the motherland and against the armed aggression of American imperialists, awards the Third-Class National Flag Order and Military Service Medal respectively: The Third-Class National Flag Order is awarded to Kim Dal-won, Kim Hee-kyung, Lee Sung-yoon, Ahn Sung-deuk, and Choi Sang-yuk. The Military Service Medal is awarded to Ko Kyu-seop, Kim Kuk-cheol, Lee Ju-hwan, Shin Eung-yoon, Kim Jong-hwa, Jung Beom-kyung, Ji Ha-bong, and Ham Bong-cheol.

### 朝鲜最高人民会议授勋  民族保卫部长崔庸健荣获一级国旗勋章  许多人民军与空军军官获勋章奖章

1950-07-14
第1版()
专栏：

　　朝鲜最高人民会议授勋
    民族保卫部长崔庸健荣获一级国旗勋章
    许多人民军与空军军官获勋章奖章
    【新华社平壤十一日电】七月十日是朝鲜民主主义人民共和国部长会议民族保卫部部长崔庸健将军的五十寿辰，朝鲜民主主义人民共和国最高人民会议常任委员会鉴于崔庸健将军在过去抗日斗争中与在朝鲜民主主义人民共和国的建立中，特别是在共和国武装部队的建设中功勋卓著，特于九日发布命令，授予他以共和国一级国旗勋章一枚。
    【新华社平壤十三日电】朝鲜民主主义人民共和国最高人民会议常任委员会十二日发布授勋命令称：朝鲜民主主义人民共和国最高人民会议常任委员会，对于在为祖国的统一、独立和反对美帝国主义者武装侵略的正义战争中歼敌立功的下列人民军、空军军官与下士官，分别授予三级国旗勋章与军功奖章：三级国旗勋章授予金达元、金熙庆、李成允、安圣得及崔相育等五人。军功奖章授予高奎燮、金国哲、李舟焕、申应允、金钟化、郑范庆、池夏峰及咸奉哲等八人。
