Title: Central South Military Region and Fourth Field Army Units Actively Prepare to Celebrate "August 1st"
Author:
Date: 1950-07-28
Page: 1

　　Central South Military Region and Fourth Field Army Units
    Actively Prepare to Celebrate "August 1st"
    [Xinhua News Agency, Hankou, 27th] The Central South Military Region of the People's Liberation Army and the various units of the Fourth Field Army are actively preparing to celebrate the 23rd anniversary of the founding of the army. The entire army will, during the two weeks from August 1st to 15th, conduct education on the glorious history and traditions of the People's Liberation Army, and launch a movement against the American invasion of Taiwan and Korea. In addition to deciding to invite local masses to hold army-founding commemoration meetings on August 1st, Wuhan City will hold a grand parade and demonstration against American aggression. In Nanchang, the birthplace of the Chinese People's Liberation Army, a review assembly of various sports performances will be held on this day. The troops stationed in Hunan have successively collected and sorted out various weapons seized in past wars, and are preparing to hold a grand exhibition of war trophies in Changsha on August 1st. The military and civilians of Haikou City will also hold a grand celebration of the Army Day and a demonstration against American aggression in Taiwan and Korea on the Army Day holiday.

### 中南军区暨四野各部  积极筹备庆祝“八一”

1950-07-28
第1版()
专栏：

　　中南军区暨四野各部
    积极筹备庆祝“八一”
    【新华社汉口二十七日电】人民解放军中南军区和第四野战军各部正积极筹备庆祝建军二十三周年的节日。全军将在八月一日到十五日的两周内，进行人民解放军的光荣历史和传统的教育，并展开反对美国侵略台湾、朝鲜的运动。各部除了决定在八月一日邀请当地群众分别召开建军纪念大会外，武汉市将举行盛大的反对美国侵略的游行示威。在中国人民解放军诞生地的南昌，这一天将举行各种体育表演的检阅大会。湖南驻军已陆续搜集和整理了历年战争中所缴获的各种武器，预备于八月一日在长沙举行盛大的战利品展览会。海口市军民也将于建军节日举行盛大的庆祝建军节大会，并举行反对美国侵略台湾朝鲜的示威游行。
