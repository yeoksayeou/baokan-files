Title: Lost and Found

Author:

Date: 1950-07-21
Page: 6

　　Lost and Found
    A comrade from the Reception Center of the Political Department of the North China Military Region found a small wallet at 8:00 AM on the 20th on West Chang'an Street, Xidan. It contained several tens of thousands of RMB. The owner can go to No. 10 Hongmiao Hutong, West Xiejie, North Street, Xidan, to find the person in charge of the center to claim it.

### 招领

1950-07-21
第6版()
专栏：

　　招领
    华北军区政治部招待所某同志于二十日上午八点在西单西长安街拾得小钱包一个。内有人民币数万。失者可至西单北大街西斜街宏庙胡同十号找该所负责人认领。
