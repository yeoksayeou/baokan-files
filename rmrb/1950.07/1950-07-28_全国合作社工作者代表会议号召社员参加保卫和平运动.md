Title: National Cooperative Workers' Representatives Conference Calls on Members to Participate in the Movement to Defend Peace
Author:
Date: 1950-07-28
Page: 1
　　
　　Xinhua News Agency: The National Cooperative Workers' Representatives Conference passed a resolution calling on more than 20 million members and over 100,000 working cadres nationwide to enthusiastically respond to the call of the National Committee of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference and actively participate in the world peace signature campaign. The conference called on every cooperative worker across the country to take immediate action, participate in and organize peace signature campaigns, mobilize every cooperative member to sign, and unite with local organizations, groups, and troops, or independently expand this movement to the surrounding people, using various opportunities and methods to educate members and the broad masses of people in patriotism and internationalism.

### 全国合作社工作者代表会议号召社员参加保卫和平运动

1950-07-28
第1版()
专栏：

　　全国合作社工作者代表会议号召社员参加保卫和平运动
    【本报讯】据新华社讯：中华全国合作社工作者代表会议通过决议，号召全国两千多万社员和十万以上的工作干部热烈响应人民政协全国委员会的号召，积极参加保卫世界和平签名运动。会议号召全国各地每一合作社工作者立即行动起来，参加并组织和平签名运动，除动员每一个合作社员签名外，并要联合本地各机关、团体、部队或单独地向周围人民群众扩大这一运动，利用各种机会和方式向社员及广大人民进行爱国主义和国际主义的教育。
