Title: Over 30,000 People in Tianjin Hold a Rally Against American Aggression in Taiwan and Korea; Anti-American Imperialism Aggression Movement Week Begins Today in Northeast and Other Regions
Author:
Date: 1950-07-17
Page: 1

　　Tianjin Holds Rally of Over 30,000 People
    Opposing American Aggression Against Taiwan and Korea
    Anti-American Imperialism Aggression Movement Week Begins in Northeast and Other Regions Today
    [Comprehensive report from this newspaper] The people of Tianjin, Northeast China, Beijing, and Chongqing have begun an Anti-American Aggression Against Taiwan and Korea Movement Week successively from the 16th.
    The Tianjin People's Anti-American Imperialism Aggression Against Taiwan and Korea Movement Week began on the 16th. At 8 p.m., more than 30,000 people from all walks of life held a rally against American imperialism's aggression against Taiwan and Korea. At the meeting, Huang Songling, chairman of the Tianjin Branch of the All-China Federation of Trade Unions, spoke. He pointed out that American imperialism openly invaded Taiwan and Korea, and openly exposed its bandit and fascist faces to the people of the world. He also pointed out that American imperialism's armed intervention cannot prevent the Chinese people from liberating Taiwan, nor can it prevent the Korean people from liberating Korea. He called on the people of the city to step up production, study hard, and work actively to support the People's Liberation Army in liberating Taiwan with practical actions. Subsequently, Qiu Jin, vice chairman of the Tianjin Federation of Trade Unions, Li Zhuchen, director of the Federation of Industry and Commerce, and Yang Shixian of the University Teachers' Association spoke, unanimously expressing their firm opposition to American imperialism's aggression against Taiwan and Korea, and calling on people from all walks of life to support the liberation of Taiwan and the liberation struggle of the Korean and all Asian people with practical actions. Finally, a telegram was passed protesting the American imperialist atrocities of aggression against Taiwan and Korea.
    The Northeast People's Anti-American Aggression Against Taiwan and Korea Movement Week began on the 17th. This was the resolution of a meeting of representatives of people's organizations stationed in Shenyang, presided over by the Northeast Federation of Trade Unions on the afternoon of the 14th. At the meeting, everyone unanimously supported the decision of the All-China Federation of Trade Unions and other eleven people's organizations to hold this Movement Week throughout the country, and formed the "Northeast People's Anti-American Aggression Against Taiwan and Korea Movement Committee" in order to deeply promote this movement to the broad masses of people in all provinces and cities of the Northeast. The committee includes seventeen people's organizations and schools from all walks of life in Northeast China and Shenyang, including workers, women, youth, students, and literary and art circles. The meeting also decided to convene a cadre meeting of various units in Shenyang on the 16th, and the Shenyang Federation of Trade Unions will organize a mass rally on the 19th. All government agencies, schools, and factories will hold report meetings or forums during this Movement Week. The Northeast People's Broadcasting Station will continuously invite people to give radio lectures.
    The Shanghai People's Anti-American Aggression Against Taiwan and Korea Movement Week will be held from July 17th to 24th. The Shanghai Federation of Trade Unions, the Shanghai Branch of the China Committee for the Defense of World Peace, the Shanghai Branch of the Sino-Soviet Friendship Association, and seventeen people's organizations including farmers, youth, women, students, religious groups, science and technology, culture and education, news, and industry and commerce, jointly formed the Shanghai Branch of the Chinese People's Anti-American Aggression Against Taiwan and Korea Movement Committee on the 15th, and elected Liu Changsheng, chairman of the Shanghai Federation of Trade Unions, as director, and Sheng Pihua, president of the Shanghai Peace Branch, and Xia Yan, secretary-general of the Shanghai Branch of the Sino-Soviet Friendship Association, as deputy directors. During the Movement Week, in addition to holding a representative conference of people from all walks of life in the city against aggression, various methods such as forums, speeches, discussions, evening chats, studying Chairman Mao's relevant speeches, Foreign Minister Zhou's relevant statements, writing letters of condolences, and through newspapers, radio broadcasts, Korean war reports, war maps, films, exhibitions, songs, and paintings. and other activities to widely carry out in-depth publicity and education to the people of the city, so that people from all walks of life can understand the situation, strengthen their work, do a good job in production, expand peace signatures, support the just struggle of the Korean people with practical actions, and prepare all the conditions for liberating Taiwan from the American aggressors.
    In order to launch a movement against American aggression against Taiwan and Korea, people's organizations in Chongqing held a preparatory meeting on the 15th, presided over by the Chongqing Branch of the Committee for the Defense of World Peace, and decided to start holding the "Anti-American Aggression Against Taiwan and Korea Movement Week" from the 17th of this month, and resolved to organize the "Chongqing People's Anti-American Aggression Against Taiwan and Korea Movement Committee."

### 天津市三万余人举行大会  反对美国侵略台湾朝鲜  东北等地今起举行反对美帝侵略运动周

1950-07-17
第1版()
专栏：

　　天津市三万余人举行大会
    反对美国侵略台湾朝鲜
    东北等地今起举行反对美帝侵略运动周
    【本报综合报道】天津、东北、北京及重庆人民反对美国侵略台湾、朝鲜运动周自十六日起陆续开始。
    天津市人民反对美帝侵略台湾、朝鲜运动周，已于十六日开始。晚八时，各界人民三万余人举行了反对美帝侵略台湾、朝鲜的大会。会中，和大天津分会主席黄松龄讲话。他指出：美帝公开地侵略台湾、朝鲜，公开地把强盗面目、法西斯面目揭露在全世界人民面前。他并指出：美帝的武装干涉，决不能阻止中国人民解放台湾，决不能阻止朝鲜人民解放朝鲜。他号召全市人民，加紧生产，努力学习，积极工作，以实际行动来支援人民解放军解放台湾。继由天津市总工会副主席邱金、工商联合会主任李烛尘，大学教联杨石先等讲话，一致表示坚决反对美帝侵略台湾、朝鲜，并号召各界人民以实际行动支援解放台湾，支援朝鲜人民和全亚洲人民的解放斗争。最后并通过通电抗议美帝侵略台湾、朝鲜的暴行。
    东北人民反对美国侵略台湾、朝鲜的运动周，于十七日开始举行。这是十四日下午由东北总工会主持的驻沈各人民团体代表会议的决议。会上大家一致拥护全国总工会等十一个人民团体关于在全国举行这一运动周的决定，并组成了“东北人民反对美国侵略台湾朝鲜运动委员会”，以便把这一运动深入推广到全东北各省、市广大人民群众中去。参加这个委员会的有东北及沈阳市的工人、妇女、青年、学生、文艺等各界十七个人民团体和学校。会议并决定十六日在沈阳市召开各单位干部大会，十九日由沈阳市总工会组织一次群众性的大会。各机关、学校、工厂均将在这一运动周中分别举行报告会或座谈会。东北人民广播电台将连续请人作广播讲演。
    上海人民反对美国侵略台湾朝鲜运动周将于七月十七日至二十四日举行。上海总工会、中国保卫世界和平大会委员会上海分会、中苏友好协会上海分会及农民、青年、妇女、学生、宗教、科学技术、文化教育、新闻、工商等十七个人民团体已于十五日联合组成中国人民反对美国侵略台湾朝鲜运动委员会上海分会，推选上海总工会主席刘长胜为主任，上海和平分会会长盛丕华和中苏友好协会上海分会总干事夏衍为副主任。运动周中，除举行全市各界人民反侵略运动代表大会外，并将用各种方式，如座谈、演讲、讨论、乘凉漫谈、学习毛主席有关讲话、周外长有关声明、写慰问信，以及通过报章、广播、朝鲜战报、战事地图、电影、展览、歌曲、绘画等活动，广泛地向全市人民进行深入的宣传教育，使各界人民认清局势，加强工作，作好生产，扩大和平签名，以实际行动支援朝鲜人民的正义斗争，并准备好从美国侵略者手中解放台湾的一切条件。
    重庆各人民团体为展开反对美国侵略台湾朝鲜运动，十五日由保卫和平大会委员会重庆分会主持召开筹备会议，决定自本月十七日起，开始举行“反对美国侵略台湾朝鲜运动周”，并决议组织“重庆市各界人民反对美国侵略台湾朝鲜运动委员会”。
