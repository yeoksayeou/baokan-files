Title: American Bandit Aircraft Groups Bomb Pyongyang City
Author:
Date: 1950-07-01
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Pyongyang, 30th: TASS Pyongyang, 29th: At 5:30 PM on June 29th, 27 American B-29 bombers carried out a bandit-like attack on Pyongyang. The aircraft groups dropped nearly three hundred bombs of various sizes on different areas of the city. The bombing destroyed many houses and caused heavy casualties among the city's peaceful residents.

### 美国强盗机群空袭平壤城区

1950-07-01
第4版()
专栏：

　　美国强盗机群空袭平壤城区
    【新华社三十日讯】塔斯社平壤二十九日电：六月二十九日下午五时三十分，二十七架美国Ｂ——２９轰炸机向平壤作盗匪式的袭击。机群在该市各个区域投掷了将近三百枚各种大小不同的炸弹。轰炸使很多房屋被毁，该城和平居民牺牲甚多。
