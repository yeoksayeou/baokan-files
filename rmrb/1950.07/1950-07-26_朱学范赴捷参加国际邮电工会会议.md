Title: Zhu Xuefan to Attend International Postal, Telegraph and Telephone Workers' Conference in Czechoslovakia
Author:
Date: 1950-07-26
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency: Zhu Xuefan, Vice Chairman of the All-China Federation of Labor, left Beijing on July 24th to attend the International Administrative Committee meeting of the World Federation of Trade Unions (WFTU) Postal, Telegraph and Telephone Workers' Trade Department, to be held in Prague from August 6th to 10th, the All-German Postal Workers' Congress to be held in Germany in August, and the Third Congress of the Free German Trade Union Federation. Over a hundred people, including Liu Ningyi, Vice Chairman of the All-China Federation of Labor, were at the station to see him off. In addition, Lu Xiangxian and Huang Zongxi, the other two representatives of the Chinese Postal and Telecommunications Workers' Union attending the International Administrative Committee meeting of the WFTU Postal, Telegraph and Telephone Workers' Trade Department, will depart on the 27th.

### 朱学范赴捷参加国际邮电工会会议

1950-07-26
第1版()
专栏：

　　朱学范赴捷参加国际邮电工会会议
    【本报讯】据新华社消息：中华全国总工会朱学范副主席已于七月二十四日离京前往布拉格出席八月六日至十日召开的世界工联产业部邮电工会国际行政会议、八月间在德国召开的全德邮电工人代表大会与自由德国工会第三次代表大会。到车站欢送者有全国总工会刘宁一副主席等百余人。又出席世界工联产业部邮电工会国际行政会议的另两位中国邮电工会代表陆象贤、黄宗熙将于二十七日起程。
