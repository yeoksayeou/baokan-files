Title: Free German Youth League Sixth Congress Opens; Sends Greetings to Korean Youth
Author:
Date: 1950-07-16
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Berlin, 13th dispatch from TASS: The Sixth Plenary Session of the Central Committee of the Free German Youth League opened in Berlin on July 12th. The congress heard the report of the League's Secretariat and sent a telegram of greetings to the Central Committee of the Korean Democratic Youth League. The delegates to the congress, on behalf of the German youth fighting for peace, lodged a firm protest against the aggressive actions of the American imperialists in Korea, and expressed their support for the Korean people's struggle for freedom and independence.

### 自由德国青联六届大会揭幕  电朝鲜青年致敬

1950-07-16
第4版()
专栏：

　　自由德国青联六届大会揭幕
    电朝鲜青年致敬
    【新华社十四日讯】塔斯社柏林十三日讯：自由德国青年联盟中央委员会第六届全体大会，于七月十二日在柏林揭幕。大会听取联盟秘书处的报告，并向朝鲜民主青年联合会中央委员会去电致敬。大会代表为和平而奋斗的德国青年，对美帝国主义者在朝鲜的侵略行动提出坚决的抗议，并表示支援朝鲜人民为自由和独立而进行的斗争。
