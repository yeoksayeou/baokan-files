Author:
Date: 1950-07-17
Page: 1

　　The National Flag of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea
    [Explanation] As the nationwide movement against American aggression in Taiwan and Korea begins, many readers still do not know what the national flag of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea looks like. We are now introducing it. The top and bottom of the flag are blue, and the middle part and the five-pointed star are red. The middle part of red and blue, and the area around the five-pointed star, are all white. In this diagram, diagonal lines represent blue, and black represents red.
