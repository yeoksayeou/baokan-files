　　Important Correction

　　In yesterday's edition, on page one, in the second item of "People's Mailbox," within the "People's Daily Editorial Department's Note," the following three phrases should have the words "newspaper" added after "收回" (shouhui, meaning recall/take back): in the third line "决不应当采取收回的办法 (jué bù yīngdāng cǎiqǔ shōuhuí de bànfǎ, meaning "should absolutely not adopt the method of recalling")," in the eighth line "固然不应当收回 (gùrán bù yīngdāng shōuhuí, meaning "of course should not recall")," and in the ninth line "而不应当收回 (ér bù yīngdāng shōuhuí, meaning "and should not recall")." We hereby make this correction.

　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　People's Daily Editorial Department
　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　July 18th

### 重要补正

1950-07-19
第1版()
专栏：

　　重要补正
    昨日本报第一版“人民信箱”第二条的“人民日报编辑部按”中，第三行“决不应当采取收回的办法”、第八行“固然不应当收回”、第九行“而不应当收回”等三句中的“收回”下面，均应加“报纸”二字。特此补正。
                                   本报编辑部
                                    七月十八日
