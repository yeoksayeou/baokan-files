Title: Thanks for Congratulations on Polish National Day; Beirut Replies to Chairman Mao by Telegram; Cyrankiewicz Replies to Premier Zhou by Telegram
Author:
Date: 1950-07-27
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, 26th -- President Beirut of the Republic of Poland and Premier Cyrankiewicz respectively telegraphed their thanks to Chairman Mao Zedong of the Central People's Government and Premier Zhou Enlai of the Government Administration Council for their congratulations on the sixth anniversary of the National Day of the Republic of Poland. The texts of the two telegrams are as follows:

(1) President Beirut's telegram to Chairman Mao:

Mr. Mao Zedong, Chairman of the Central People's Government of the People's Republic of China:

I extend my heartfelt thanks for your congratulations on the Polish National Day. I believe that the great Chinese people, liberated from the shackles of imperialism, will confidently move towards a new and happy future.

Beirut
Warsaw, 21st, 21:00

(2) Premier Cyrankiewicz's telegram to Premier Zhou:

Mr. Zhou Enlai, Premier and Minister of Foreign Affairs of the Central People's Government of the People's Republic of China:

Please accept my heartfelt thanks in my own name and in the name of the government of the Republic of Poland. Thank you for your country's congratulations on our National Day, for the genuine and fraternal friendship that the great Chinese people have expressed to the Polish people, for the valuable gifts that express the sincere feelings of the Chinese people, and for the high respect you have personally shown.

Cyrankiewicz
Warsaw, 21st, 21:00

### 感谢对波兰国庆的祝贺  贝鲁特电复毛主席  西伦凯维兹复周总理

1950-07-27
第1版()
专栏：

　　感谢对波兰国庆的祝贺
    贝鲁特电复毛主席
    西伦凯维兹复周总理
    【新华社二十六日讯】波兰共和国贝鲁特总统和西伦凯维兹总理，分别电谢我中央人民政府毛泽东主席和政务院周恩来总理于波兰共和国六周年国庆纪念日的祝贺，两电电文如下：
    （一）贝鲁特总统致毛主席电
    中华人民共和国中央人民政府主席毛泽东先生：
    对您在波兰国庆节的祝贺，谨致衷心谢意。我相信从帝国主义束缚下解放了的伟大的中国人民，将满怀信心地走向新的幸福的未来。
                                          贝鲁特
                                  华沙二十一日二十一时
    （二）西伦凯维兹总理致周总理电
    中华人民共和国中央人民政府总理兼外交部部长周恩来先生：
    请接受以我个人及波兰共和国政府名义对您衷心的感谢。对贵国在我国国庆日的祝贺，对伟大的中国人民对波兰人民所表示的真实与兄弟的友情，对表示了中国人民真挚感情的贵重的礼物以及对您个人所表示的崇高敬意，谨致谢意。
                                                西伦凯维兹
                     华沙二十一日二十一时
