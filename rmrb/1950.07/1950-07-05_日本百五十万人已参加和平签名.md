Title: 1.5 Million People in Japan Have Signed Peace Petition
Author:
Date: 1950-07-05
Page: 4

　　Xinhua News Agency, 3rd dispatch from Tass on the 2nd: According to the Japanese "New Culture Daily", as of June 25th, approximately 1.5 million people in Japan have signed the Stockholm Appeal of the Standing Committee of the World Congress of Supporters of Peace. A student organization federation has decided to collect one million signatures for this declaration. The workers of the Tsurumi factory of the "Hodogaya" Chemical Industry Company have named their factory the "Factory for the Defense of Peace". The peace signature campaign is being actively conducted among members of the railway workers and postal and telecommunications workers unions.

### 日本百五十万人已参加和平签名

1950-07-05
第4版()
专栏：

　　日本百五十万人已参加和平签名
    【新华社三日讯】塔斯社二日电引日本新文化报消息：截至六月二十五日为止，日本已约有一百五十余万人在世界拥护和平大会常设委员会斯德哥尔摩宣言上签名。学生组织的联合会决定征集一百万人签署这个宣言。“保土谷”化学工业公司的鹤见工厂人员把他们的工厂叫作“保卫和平工厂”。和平签名运动正在铁道工人和递信工人工会会员中积极地进行着。
