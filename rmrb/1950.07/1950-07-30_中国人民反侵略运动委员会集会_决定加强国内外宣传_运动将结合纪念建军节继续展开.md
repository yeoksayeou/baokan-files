Title: Chinese People's Anti-Aggression Movement Committee Meeting Decides to Strengthen Domestic and Foreign Propaganda; Movement Will Continue in Conjunction with Army Day Commemoration

Author:

Date: 1950-07-30
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, July 29th: The Chinese People's Anti-American Aggression Against Taiwan and Korea Movement Committee held its fourth meeting at the All-China Federation of Trade Unions on the 28th. The meeting decided to strengthen domestic and foreign propaganda and reporting work, widely introduce the work experience of various countries and regions in China in opposing American aggression, and closely liaise with the World Federation of Trade Unions, the Women's International Democratic Federation, the World Federation of Democratic Youth, and the International Union of Students to report to the world the determination of the Chinese people to oppose American aggression against Taiwan and their will to support the just struggle of the Korean people. The committee also heard reports on the national anti-American aggression against Taiwan and Korea movement, as well as work reports from the Sino-Soviet Friendship Association, the Democratic Women's Federation, and the Student Federation in Beijing. Since the Chinese People's Anti-American Aggression Against Taiwan and Korea Movement Committee issued a call to launch a propaganda campaign against American imperialist aggression against Taiwan and Korea, the people across the country have responded enthusiastically. People's organizations, democratic parties, and people from all walks of life in major administrative regions, provinces, and cities have launched propaganda work, which has achieved great results. This movement will continue to unfold enthusiastically throughout the country in conjunction with the demonstration and parade rallies commemorating "August 1st" Army Day.

### 中国人民反侵略运动委员会集会  决定加强国内外宣传  运动将结合纪念建军节继续展开

1950-07-30
第1版()
专栏：

　　中国人民反侵略运动委员会集会
    决定加强国内外宣传         
    运动将结合纪念建军节继续展开
    【新华社二十九日讯】中国人民反对美国侵略台湾朝鲜运动委员会于二十八日假中华全国总工会召开第四次会议。会议决定加强国内外宣传报道工作，广泛介绍各国及国内各地反对美国侵略运动的工作经验，并与世界工联、国际民主妇联、世界民主青联及学联密切联系，将我国人民反对美国侵略台湾的决心，及支援朝鲜人民正义斗争的意志，向全世界作报道。委员会并听取了全国反对美国侵略台湾朝鲜运动情况的报告，北京市中苏友协及民主妇联、学联等方面的工作汇报。按自中国人民反对美国侵略台湾、朝鲜运动委员会发出开展反对美帝侵略台湾朝鲜宣传运动的号召后，全国各地人民热烈响应。各大行政区和各省、市的人民团体、民主党派及各阶层人民纷纷展开宣传工作，已收到很大效果。这一运动，将结合纪念“八一”建军节的示威游行大会，在全国各地继续热烈展开。
