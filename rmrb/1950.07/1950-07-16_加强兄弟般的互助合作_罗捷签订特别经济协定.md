Title: Strengthening Brotherly Mutual Assistance and Cooperation: Romania and Czechoslovakia Sign Special Economic Agreement
Author:
Date: 1950-07-16
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency, Bucharest, July 13th: TASS reports that Romania and Czechoslovakia have recently signed a special economic agreement here. According to this agreement, Czechoslovakia will supply Romania with heavy industrial equipment, particularly equipment for thermal, power, and hydroelectric power plants, in exchange for Romanian agricultural products, timber products, and petroleum products over the next few years.

The two countries also signed a protocol to increase the volume of trade between the two countries by 40 percent, as well as an agreement on scientific and technical cooperation between the two countries.

### 加强兄弟般的互助合作  罗捷签订特别经济协定

1950-07-16
第4版()
专栏：

　　加强兄弟般的互助合作
    罗捷签订特别经济协定
    【新华社十四日讯】塔斯社布加勒斯特十三日讯：罗马尼亚和捷克斯洛伐克两国顷在此间签订一项特别经济协定。根据这项协定，捷克斯洛伐克在今后数年内将以重工业设备，特别是热力和动力及水力发电厂的设备供给罗马尼亚，以换取罗马尼亚的农产品、木材产品和石油产品。
    两国同时签定了增加两国贸易额百分之四十的议定书，及两国进行科学与技术合作的协定。
