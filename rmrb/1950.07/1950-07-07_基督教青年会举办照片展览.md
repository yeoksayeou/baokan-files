Title: YMCA Holds Photo Exhibition
Author:
Date: 1950-07-07
Page: 6

　　The Metropolitan YMCA's annual education conference, in commemoration of "July 7th" and to enhance friendship and cooperation between China and the Soviet Union, will hold a photo exhibition commemorating the 32nd anniversary of the Soviet socialist revolution at the YMCA from 9:00 AM to 7:00 PM daily from July 5th to 8th. All are welcome to visit.

### 基督教青年会举办照片展览

1950-07-07
第6版()
专栏：

　　基督教青年会举办照片展览
    大市基的教育年会为纪念“七七”，及增进中苏两国友好与合作；自七月五日起至八日止每日早九时至下午七时在该会举办苏联社会主义革命三十二周年纪念图片展览，欢迎各国前往参观。
