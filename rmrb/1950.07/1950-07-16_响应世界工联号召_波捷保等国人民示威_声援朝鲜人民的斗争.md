Title: Responding to the Call of the World Federation of Trade Unions: Demonstrations by People in Poland, Czechoslovakia, and Other Countries in Support of the Korean People's Struggle
Author:
Date: 1950-07-16
Page: 4

　　Responding to the Call of the World Federation of Trade Unions
    Demonstrations by People in Poland, Czechoslovakia, and Other Countries
    In Support of the Korean People's Struggle
    [Xinhua News Agency, 15th] People in Poland, Czechoslovakia, Hungary, Romania, Bulgaria, and other countries have unanimously responded to the call of the World Federation of Trade Unions to promote the Week of Support for the Korean People.

　　In Warsaw, the capital of Poland, 35,000 citizens held a demonstration rally on the 13th to protest the American imperialist aggression against Korea. Mass protest demonstrations were also held in other Polish cities. The National Assembly of Czechoslovakia passed a statement on the 13th, strongly condemning the American imperialists' acts of aggression in Korea and expressing warm support for the Korean people's struggle for freedom and independence. The Central Council of Trade Unions of Czechoslovakia has issued a statement on behalf of three million Czech trade union members, responding to the World Federation of Trade Unions' message on supporting the Korean people. The statement declared: The Czech working people will respond to the evil plans of the enemies of peace with greater creative labor. Every overfulfilled task and every achievement in shock work marks the consolidation of peace.

　　Budapest, the capital of Hungary, held a mass rally on the 12th to protest American aggression against Korea. The rally, representing 1.65 million organized workers in Hungary, sent a telegram of support to the Korean people and demanded that American troops withdraw from Korea.

　　Workers in Romania conducted large-scale political activities during the Week of Support for the Korean People. At mass rallies held in various places, workers unanimously declared: No interference in Korea!

　　The Bulgarian National Peace Committee has sent a telegram to the Secretary-General of the United Nations, strongly protesting the American armed intervention in Korea. The National Peace Committee also sent a telegram to the Korean National Peace Committee, expressing warm support for the Korean people's struggle.

### 响应世界工联号召  波捷保等国人民示威  声援朝鲜人民的斗争

1950-07-16
第4版()
专栏：

　　响应世界工联号召
    波捷保等国人民示威
    声援朝鲜人民的斗争
    【新华社十五日讯】波、捷、匈、罗、保等国人民一致响应世界工联推行支援朝鲜人民周的号召。
    在波兰首都华沙，三万五千公民于十三日举行了抗议美帝国主义侵略朝鲜的示威大会。在波兰其他城市，亦举行了群众性的抗议示威大会。捷克斯洛伐克国民议会于十三日通过一项声明，强烈斥责美帝国主义者在朝鲜的侵略行为，并对争取自由与独立的朝鲜人民表示热烈的声援。捷克斯洛伐克工会中央理事会已发表声明，代表捷克三百万工会会员响应世界工联关于支援朝鲜人民的文告。声明宣称：捷克劳动人民将以更伟大的创造性劳动回答和平敌人的罪恶计划。劳动人民的每一个超额完成的工作，每一个突击工作的成就，都标志着和平的巩固。
    匈牙利首都布达佩斯于十二日举行了抗议美国侵略朝鲜的群众大会。大会代表匈牙利一百六十五万有组织的劳动人民，致电声援朝鲜人民，并要求美军撤出朝鲜去。
    罗马尼亚的工人在支援朝鲜人民周内，进行了巨大规模的政治活动。在各地举行的群众大会上，工人一致宣称：不准干涉朝鲜！
    保加利亚全国和平委员会已致电联合国秘书长，强硬抗议美国武装干涉朝鲜。全国和平委员会并电朝鲜全国和平委员会，表示热烈声援朝鲜人民的斗争。
