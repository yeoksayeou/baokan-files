Title: Having the Strength to Strive for Peace
Author: Huang Xinmin
Date: 1950-07-21
Page: 5

Defend World Peace, Oppose the Use of Atomic Weapons!

　　Having the Strength to Strive for Peace
    Huang Xinmin
    When we say, "Whoever uses atomic weapons first is a war criminal," it not only shows that we will definitely not use the atomic bomb first, but it also means that we have the strength to strive for peace and severely punish war criminals. Through this signature campaign, we have united the people of the world who are willing to fight for peace, causing the warmongers to tremble.

### 有力量争取和平
黄新民
1950-07-21
第5版()
专栏：保卫世界和平，反对使用原子武器！

　　有力量争取和平
    黄新民
    我们说：“谁先使用原子武器，谁就是战犯”，这不但说明我们一定不先使用原子弹，而且还意味着：我们是有力量争取和平，严惩战犯的。通过这次的签名运动，我们团结了全世界愿为和平而斗争的人民，使战争贩子们在发抖。
