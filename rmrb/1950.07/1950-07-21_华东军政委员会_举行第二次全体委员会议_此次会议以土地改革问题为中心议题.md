Title: East China Military and Political Committee Holds Second Plenary Session; Land Reform is the Central Topic of This Meeting
Author:
Date: 1950-07-21
Page: 3

Xinhua News Agency, Shanghai, 16th. The second plenary session of the East China Military and Political Committee opened in Shanghai on the 14th. Attending the meeting were Rao Shushi, Chairman of the East China Military and Political Committee, Vice Chairmen Zeng Shan and Ma Yinchu, and a total of sixty committee members. Also present at the meeting were the heads of various departments of the East China Military and Political Committee, staff members of the people's governments of various provinces and cities in East China, representatives of various democratic parties, representatives of the consultative committees of the people's representatives conferences of various provinces and cities, and specially invited patriotic democrats, totaling 220 people. The meeting began with an opening speech by Rao Shushi, Chairman of the East China Military and Political Committee. Following this, the meeting listened to the report by Committee Member Pan Hannian on the resolutions and spirit of the Second Session of the First National Committee of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference, and Chairman Rao's report "Striving to Complete Land Reform in East China."

In his opening speech, Chairman Rao pointed out that the focus of this meeting should be the issue of land reform. He said, "Land relations in the East China region are relatively complex, so leadership and control over land reform work must be even more cautious. In order to resolutely implement the land reform laws and policies of the Central People's Government and ensure that land reform in the East China region can be carried out in a planned, led, and orderly manner in the future, our meeting should give special and serious discussion to this issue." Chairman Rao also said, "This meeting should review our past work based on the resolutions and spirit of the Second Session of the National Committee of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference so that future work can be improved on the existing foundation." Chairman Rao explained that this meeting invited people from all walks of life to attend and participate in the discussion, which was done according to the instructions of Chairman Mao of the Central People's Government. Because this approach allows us to gather broader opinions, examine our past work, and decide on future directions.

After Chairman Rao's opening speech, the entire audience stood up and observed a moment of silence for the late Vice Chairman Yan Huiqing of the East China Military and Political Committee.

### 华东军政委员会  举行第二次全体委员会议  此次会议以土地改革问题为中心议题

1950-07-21
第3版()
专栏：

　　华东军政委员会
    举行第二次全体委员会议         
    此次会议以土地改革问题为中心议题
    【新华社上海十六日电】华东军政委员会第二次全体委员会议于十四日在上海揭幕。出席这次会议的有华东军政委员会主席饶漱石，副主席曾山、马寅初暨委员共六十人。列席会议的有华东军政委员会各部门负责人，华东各省、市人民政府工作人员，各民主党派代表，各省、市各界人民代表会议协商委员会的代表及特邀爱国民主人士等共二百二十人。会议首由华东军政委员会饶漱石主席致开幕词。接着，会议听取了潘汉年委员关于中国人民政治协商会议第一届全国委员会第二次会议的决议与精神的传达报告和饶主席“为完成华东土地改革而奋斗”的报告。
    饶主席在开幕词中指出：此次会议应以土地改革问题为中心议题。他说：“华东地区土地关系较为复杂，因此对土地改革工作的领导与掌握更须慎重。为了坚决贯彻中央人民政府的土地改革法令与政策，保证华东地区今后的土地改革能够有计划、有领导、有秩序地进行，我们这次会议就应该对这一问题特别加以郑重的讨论。”饶主席并且说：“这次会议应该根据人民政协全国委员会第二次会议的决议和精神，来检讨我们过去的工作，使今后的工作能够在现有的基础上提高一步。”饶主席说明这次会议邀请了各方面人士列席参加讨论，这是根据中央人民政府毛主席的指示来做的。因为这种做法，使我们更加能够集中广泛的意见，检查我们过去的工作，决定今后的方针。
    会议在饶主席致开幕词后，曾全场起立，为华东军政委员会故副主席颜惠庆静默致哀。
