Title: Ministry of Labor Revises Article 4 of the "Organizational and Working Rules of the Municipal Labor Dispute Arbitration Committee"
Author:
Date: 1950-07-14
Page: 2

　　Xinhua News Agency, 13th: The Ministry of Labor of the Central People's Government has revised Article 4 of the "Organizational and Working Rules of the Municipal Labor Dispute Arbitration Committee," promulgated on June 15th, to read: "The day-to-day work of the arbitration committee shall be handled by the relevant departments of the Labor Bureau." Note: The original text of this article was "The day-to-day work of the arbitration committee shall be handled by the relevant departments of the Labor Bureau designated by the chairman of the arbitration committee."

### 劳动部修改“市劳动争议仲裁委员会组织及工作规则”第四条

1950-07-14
第2版()
专栏：

　　劳动部修改“市劳动争议仲裁委员会组织及工作规则”第四条
    【新华社十三日讯】中央人民政府劳动部已将六月十五日颁布的“市劳动争议仲裁委员会组织及工作规则”第四条修改为“仲裁委员会的日常工作，由劳动局有关部门办理之。”按：该条原文为“仲裁委员会的日常工作，由仲裁委员会主席指定劳动局有关部门办理之。”
