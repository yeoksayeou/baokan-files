　　Broadcast Programs
    Beijing People's Broadcasting Station today (July 24th) important broadcast programs: 8:45-9:00 City Voice: The Public Service Group of the Beijing Telecommunications Bureau discusses some problems with telephone obstructions during the rainy season. 9:00-10:00 Youth Program: Broadcast of the Central Academy of Drama Opera Troupe's recording of the opera "The White-Haired Girl." 12:25-13:00 Workers' Program: (1) Domestic and international events. (2) Comrade Xiao Ming, Chairman of the Beijing People's Committee for Opposing the American Aggression Against Taiwan and Korea, speaks on: "We should expand the movement against American aggression against Taiwan and Korea in a timely manner." 17:00-17:30 On the hour, Youth Program: Appreciation of new records produced by the People's Broadcasting Equipment Factory. 17:30-18:00 Children's Program: Mr. Sun Jingxiu tells "The Story of Peterka." 18:00-18:30 Folk Art Program: Zhao Yingpo broadcasts the storytelling "Registration" (written by Zhao Shuli). 18:30-18:45 Women's Program: Broadcast of the recorded speeches from the Beijing Women's Conference against American Aggression against Taiwan and Korea.
    Central People's Broadcasting Station tomorrow (July 25th) important broadcast programs: 6:00 to 7:00 Social Science Lecture: The station's announcer will broadcast the third lecture of the first chapter of "The History of Social Development": The Working Masses Create History (replayed from 7:30 to 8:30 and 19:00 to 20:00). 18:45 Natural Science Lecture: Huang Shuze talks about: The Heart and Blood Circulation. 20:00 Program sponsored by the Chinese People's Committee for Opposing American Aggression Against Taiwan and Korea: Cao Yu speaks on: "The Death Knell of American Imperialism is Ringing." 21:45 Book Review Time: Introducing several new books from the Xinhua Current Affairs Series and the contents of the first issue of "The Soviet Union" pictorial. 20:30 First Literary and Art Program: The Beijing People's Art Theater performs the drama: "Victory Train" (theater recording) (written by Qiao Yu and Lu Fei). 22:00 Second Literary and Art Program: Tan Fuying and Qiu Shengrong lead the Taiping Opera Troupe in performing the Peking Opera: "The Meeting at Mianchi" (excerpt from "Generals and Ministers Reconciled," theater recording).

### 广播节目

1950-07-24
第7版()
专栏：

　　广播节目
    北京市人民广播电台今天（七月二十四日）重要广播节目：八·四五——九·○○市政之声：由北京电信局公众服务组谈雨季电话障碍的一些问题。九·○○——一○·○○青年节目：播送中央戏剧学院歌剧团演播歌剧白毛女的录音。一二·二五——一三·○○工人节目：（１）国内外大事。（２）北京市人民反对美国侵略台湾朝鲜运动委员会主任委员萧明同志讲：《应及时扩大反对美国侵略台湾及朝鲜的运动》。一七·○○——一七·三○对时，青年节目：人民广播器材厂新出唱片欣赏。一七·三○——一八·○○儿童节目：孙敬修先生讲《彼蒂加的故事》。一八·○○——一八·三○曲艺节目：赵英颇播讲评书《登记》（赵树理编写）。一八·三○——一八·四五妇女节目：播送北京市妇女反对美国侵略台湾朝鲜运动大会的讲演录音。
    中央人民广播电台明天（七月二十五日）重要广播节目：六点到七点社会科学讲座：由该台播音员播送社会发展史第一章第三讲：劳动群众创造历史（在七点三十分到八点三十分及十九点到二十点重播）。十八点四十五分自然科学讲座：黄树则讲：心脏和血液循环。二十点中国人民反对美国侵略台湾朝鲜运动委员会主办节目：曹禺讲：《美帝的丧钟响了》。二十一点四十五分书刊介绍时间：介绍新华时事丛刊里的几种新书和《苏联》画报第一期的内容。二十点三十分第一次文艺节目：北京人民艺术剧院演出话剧：《胜利列车》（剧场录音）（乔羽、逯斐编剧）二十二点第二次文艺节目：谭富英、裘盛戎领导太平剧社演唱京剧：《渑池会》（将相和摘录，剧场录音）
