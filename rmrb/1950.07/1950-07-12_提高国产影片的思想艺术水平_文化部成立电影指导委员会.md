Title: To Improve the Ideological and Artistic Level of Domestic Films: Ministry of Culture Establishes Film Steering Committee
Author:
Date: 1950-07-12
Page: 3

Xinhua News Agency, July 11th: To improve the ideological and artistic level of domestic films, the Central Ministry of Culture has specially invited experts from relevant departments, social organizations, and the film and theater industry to jointly form the "Central People's Government Ministry of Culture Film Steering Committee." The tasks of the committee are to provide opinions on matters related to promoting the film industry, as well as film scripts, story outlines, production and distribution plans of state-owned factories, and films of private film enterprises; and to jointly review and evaluate them with the Ministry of Culture. All opinions put forward by the Film Steering Committee shall be handled and answered by the Central People's Government Ministry of Culture. The members of the committee are: Shen Yanbing, Zhou Yang, Ding Xielin, Shakoff, Yuan Muzhi, Cai Chusheng, Shi Dongshan, Chen Boer, Li Lisan, Lu Dingyi, Qian Junrui, Liao Chengzhi, Xiao Hua, Jiang Nanxiang, Xu Bing, Deng Tuo, Liu Geping, Zhang Zhixiang, Shen Zijiu, Ding Ling, Ai Qing, Lao She, Zhao Shuli, Yang Hansheng, Tian Han, Hong Shen, Ouyang Yuqian, Cao Yu, Li Bozhao, Jiang Qing, Zhou Weizhi, Wang Bin, and others, totaling thirty-two people. The chairman is Shen Yanbing.

### 提高国产影片的思想艺术水平  文化部成立电影指导委员会

1950-07-12
第3版()
专栏：

　　提高国产影片的思想艺术水平
    文化部成立电影指导委员会
    【新华社十一日讯】为提高国产影片的思想艺术水平，中央文化部特邀请各有关部门、各社会团体与影剧界专家，共同组成“中央人民政府文化部电影指导委员会”。该会的工作任务是：对有关推进电影事业，及国营厂的电影剧本、故事梗概、制片和发行计划及私营电影企业的影片提出意见，并会同文化部共同审查和评议。电影指导委员会所提出的各项意见，均由中央人民政府文化部负责处理，并予回答。该会委员为：沈雁冰、周扬、丁爕林、沙可夫、袁牧之、蔡楚生、史东山、陈波儿、李立三、陆定一、钱俊瑞、廖承志、萧华、蒋南翔、徐冰、邓拓、刘格平、张致祥、沈兹九、丁玲、艾青、老舍、赵树理、阳翰笙、田汉、洪深、欧阳予倩、曹禺、李伯钊、江青、周巍峙、王滨等三十二人，主任委员为沈雁冰。
