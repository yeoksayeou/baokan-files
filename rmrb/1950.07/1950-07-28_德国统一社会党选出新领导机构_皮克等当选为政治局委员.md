Title: Socialist Unity Party of Germany Elects New Leadership; Pieck and Others Elected to Politburo

Author:
Date: 1950-07-28
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Berlin: TASS reported that the press office of the Socialist Unity Party of Germany issued a communique stating that on the 25th, the newly elected Central Committee of the Socialist Unity Party of Germany held its first meeting at the Central Unity Building. The Central Committee elected Wilhelm Pieck, Otto Grotewohl, Walter Ulbricht, Franz Dahlem, Heinrich Rau, Friedrich Ebert, Wilhelm Zaisser, Alfred Eisler, and Hermann Matern as members of the Politburo. Ulbricht was elected General Secretary of the Central Committee of the Socialist Unity Party of Germany. Ulbricht, Dahlem, Eisler and others were elected Secretaries of the Secretariat of the Central Committee. Matern was elected Chairman of the Central Control Commission of the Party. Eisler was elected Chairman of the Central Auditing Commission.

### 德国统一社会党选出新领导机构  皮克等当选为政治局委员

1950-07-28
第4版()
专栏：

　　德国统一社会党选出新领导机构
    皮克等当选为政治局委员
    【新华社二十七日讯】据塔斯社柏林讯：德国统一社会党新闻处发表公报称：二十五日，新选出的德国统一社会党中央委员会在中央统一大厦举行首次会议。中央委员会选出威廉·皮克、奥托·格罗提渥、瓦尔特·乌布里希、德兰士·达赖姆、享利劳、佛烈德利克·艾伯特、威廉·柴塞尔、亚佛里德·艾斯勒和赫尔曼·马吞等为政治局委员。乌布里希当选为德国统一社会党中央委员会总书记。乌布里希、达赖姆、艾斯勒等当选为中央委员会书记处书记。马吞当选为党的中央监察委员会主席。艾斯勒当选为中央审计委员会主席。
