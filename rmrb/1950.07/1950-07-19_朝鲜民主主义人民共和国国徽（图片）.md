Title: National Emblem of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea (Picture)
Author:
Date: 1950-07-19
Page: 1

    National Emblem of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea
   [Explanation] The Chinese people's movement against the United States' aggression against Taiwan and Korea is proceeding enthusiastically, and the Chinese people have shown great concern for the fraternal country of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea. This newspaper has already published the design of the national flag of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea at the request of the general readership. Today, we will introduce the national emblem of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea to everyone:
  The design of the national emblem of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea is a majestic hydroelectric power plant surrounded by two bundles of rice ears tied with ribbons inscribed with the words "Democratic People's Republic of Korea," with a radiant red star above it. (According to Article 101 of Chapter IX of the Constitution of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea.)

### 朝鲜民主主义人民共和国国徽（图片）

1950-07-19
第1版()
专栏：

    朝鲜民主主义人民共和国国徽
   【说明】中国人民反对美国侵略台湾朝鲜运动周正热烈进行，中国人民表现了对兄弟国家朝鲜民主主义人民共和国的极大关心。本报前已应广大读者的要求刊载了朝鲜共和国国旗的样式，今天再把朝鲜共和国的国徽介绍给大家：
  朝鲜民主主义人民共和国的国徽图案是写有“朝鲜民主主义人民共和国”字样的带子所扎住的两束稻穗围绕着雄大的发电所，其上有放射光芒的红星。（依据朝鲜民主主义人民共和国宪法第九章第一○一条。）
