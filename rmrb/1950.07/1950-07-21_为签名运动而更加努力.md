Title: Strive Harder for the Signature Campaign
Author: Zhang Yunchuan
Date: 1950-07-21
Page: 5

　　To Strive Harder for the Signature Campaign
    Zhang Yunchuan
    A group of war-mongering adventurers serving Wall Street in the United States are planning to instigate a third world war and use atomic bombs to save dying capitalism. The rapid and enthusiastic development of the peace signature campaign around the world is sufficient proof of the greatness and continued strengthening of the forces of peace, enough to stop and eliminate wars of aggression and achieve lasting world peace. We must work harder to launch a peace signature campaign for the banning of atomic weapons!

### 为签名运动而更加努力
张云川
1950-07-21
第5版()
专栏：保卫世界和平，反对使用原子武器！

　　为签名运动而更加努力
    张云川
    服务于美国华尔街的一批战争冒险家，打算挑动世界第三次大战，使用原子弹，以挽救垂死的资本主义。由于和平签名运动在世界各地迅速而热烈地展开，正足证明和平力量的伟大和继续增强，足够制止并消灭侵略战争，获致永久的世界和平。我们要为禁用原子武器展开和平签名运动而更加努力！
