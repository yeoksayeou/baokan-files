Title: Chairman Mao Hosts Banquet for Hungarian Ambassador
Author: 
Date: 1950-07-21
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, 20th: Chairman Mao Zedong of the Central People's Government hosted a banquet at 7:00 PM on the 20th for the first Hungarian Ambassador to China, Extraordinary and Plenipotentiary, Sándor Sziklai, and all members of the embassy. Invited to accompany him were Vice Chairmen of the Central People's Government Zhu De, Li Jishen, and Zhang Lan, Secretary-General Lin Boqu, Premier of the State Administration Council and Minister of Foreign Affairs Zhou Enlai, Vice Minister of Foreign Affairs Zhang Hanfu, our Ambassador to Hungary Huang Zhen, Director of the General Office of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs Wang Bingnan, Deputy Director Yan Baohang, Director of the Bureau of Protocol of the Central People's Government Yu Xinqing, Deputy Director of the Soviet and Eastern European Department of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs Xu Yixin, Director of the Protocol Department Wang Zhuoru, and Deputy Director Shen Ping.

### 毛主席欢宴匈大使

1950-07-21
第1版()
专栏：

　　毛主席欢宴匈大使
    【新华社二十日讯】中央人民政府毛泽东主席于二十日下午七时宴匈牙利驻我国首任特命全权大使夏法朗柯及大使馆全体馆员，应邀作陪者有中央人民政府朱德副主席，李济深副主席，张澜副主席，林伯渠秘书长，政务院周恩来总理兼外交部部长，外交部章汉夫副部长，我驻匈牙利大使黄镇，外交部办公厅王炳南主任，阎宝航副主任，中央人民政府典礼局余心清局长，及外交部苏联东欧司徐以新副司长，交际处王倬如处长，沈平副处长。
