Title: Over Two-Thirds of Shanghai's Population Have Signed the Peace Declaration

Author: 
Date: 1950-07-17
Page: 1

　　Xinhua News Agency, Shanghai: The people of Shanghai are enthusiastically responding to the call by the National Committee of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference to hold a Peace Signature Campaign Week. As of the afternoon of the 13th, according to statistics from the Shanghai Branch of the China Committee for the Defense of World Peace, more than 2.3 million people in the city have signed the Peace Declaration. Adding the 1,009,014 signatures the branch had already received before the Signature Campaign Week, the total number of signatories in Shanghai has reached 3,311,514, accounting for more than two-thirds of the city's population of over 5 million. Among the signatories, workers number 1,278,000, accounting for 38.5% of the city's total number of signatories; suburban farmers number 240,310, a twenty-fourfold increase compared to the 11,000 on the eve of the Campaign Week.

### 沪三分之二以上人口已在和平宣言上签名

1950-07-17
第1版()
专栏：

　　沪三分之二以上人口已在和平宣言上签名
    【本报讯】据新华社上海电：上海人民热烈响应人民政协全国委员会举行和平签名运动周的号召。截至十三日下午止，根据中国保卫世界和平大会委员会上海分会统计，全市已有二百三十余万人在和平宣言上签名，连签名运动周前该会已收到的一百万零九千零十四人的签名，上海签名者已达三百三十一万一千五百十四人，占全市五百多万人口的三分之二以上。签名者中，工人有一百二十七万八千人，占全市签名人数百分之三十八点五；市郊农民有二十四万零三百十人，比运动周前夕的一万一千人增加了二十四倍。
