Title: Citywide Education Workers' Union to Hold Representative Meeting
Author:
Date: 1950-07-07
Page: 3

　　Beijing News

　　Citywide Education Workers' Union to Hold Representative Meeting
　　[Report from this newspaper] The preparatory committee of the Beijing Education Workers' Union held its third plenary meeting on June 29th and decided to convene the Beijing Education Workers' Union Representative Congress starting from July 20th. To this end, the preparatory committee has already issued a notice, pointing out that the tasks of this congress are: 1. Summarize work and exchange work experiences of the union in various school districts. 2. Formulate the charter of the Beijing Education Workers' Union. 3. Determine the current tasks of the Beijing Education Workers' Union. 4. Officially establish the Beijing Education Workers' Union and produce its leadership. 5. Elect representatives from Beijing to attend the National Education Workers' Union Representative Congress, and prepare proposals to be submitted to the congress, etc. The notice also stipulates that representatives of the unions from various school districts attending the congress should complete registration procedures from the 15th to the 17th of this month, and all proposals must be submitted to the congress preparatory office before the 17th.

### 全市教育工作者工会将举行代表会议

1950-07-07
第3版()
专栏：京市新闻

　　全市教育工作者工会将举行代表会议
    【本报讯】北京市教育工作者工会筹备委员会在六月二十九日举行了第三次全体会议，决定从七月二十日起召开北京市教育工作者工会代表大会。为此筹委会顷已发出通知，指出此次大会的任务为：一、总结工作，交流各校区工会工作经验。二、制订北京市教育工作者工会的章程。三、决定北京市教育工作者工会的当前任务。四、正式成立北京市教育工作者工会并产生其领导机构。五、选举北京市出席全国教育工作者工会代表大会的代表，并准备向该大会提出的提案等项。通知并规定各校区工会出席大会之代表，应于本月十五至十七日办理报到手续，所有提案亦须于十七日前送交大会筹备处。
