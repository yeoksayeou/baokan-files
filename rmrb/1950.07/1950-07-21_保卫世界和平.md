Title: Defend World Peace
Author: Jian Bozan
Date: 1950-07-21
Page: 5

Defend World Peace, Oppose the Use of Atomic Weapons!

　　Defend World Peace
    Jian Bozan
    Since the end of World War II, American imperialism has begun to instigate and organize a new world war, and is preparing to apply the achievements of human science—atomic energy—as a weapon to exterminate mankind.
    History has presented an urgent task to the people of all countries around the world, and that is to defend world peace. The movement to defend world peace has already unfolded in countries around the world, and tens of millions of people have already signed the declaration supporting world peace. This proves that the force of defending peace is great.
    The signature movement is a great review of the world's peace forces. This review has at least made American imperialism aware of one fact, namely that those who oppose war are not only the people of the Soviet Union, China, and other new democratic countries, but also the people of all capitalist countries, including the American people.
    However, American imperialism will never stop manufacturing war because of this. Therefore, we must expand the movement to defend peace more widely and deeply, so that this movement becomes a force, a force that disintegrates and eliminates the war provocation of American imperialism.
    Arise, people of the world! Regardless of race or nation, let us unite around the peace fortress of the Soviet Union and fight to defend world peace.

### 保卫世界和平
翦伯赞
1950-07-21
第5版()
专栏：保卫世界和平，反对使用原子武器！

　　保卫世界和平
    翦伯赞
    自从第二次世界大战结束以后，美帝即开始挑拨并组织新的世界战争，并且准备把人类科学的成就——原子能，应用为屠灭人类的武器。
    历史已向全世界各国的人民，提出了一个紧急任务，这就是保卫世界和平。保卫世界和平的运动，已经在世界各国展开，数以万万计的人民，已经在拥护世界和平的宣言上签了字，这证明了保卫和平的力量是伟大的。
    签名运动，是全世界和平力量的大检阅，这一检阅，至少使美帝了解了一件事实，即反对战争的，不仅是苏联中国和其他新民主主义国家的人民，而且是包括各资本主义国家的人民，美国人民也在内。
    但是美帝决不因此而停止战争的制造，因此，我们必须把保卫和平的运动，更广泛更深入地展开，使这种运动变成一种力量，变成一种瓦解和消灭美帝战争挑拨的力量。
    起来，全世界的人民，我们不分种族国家，大家团结在和平堡垒苏联的周围，为了保卫世界和平而斗争。
