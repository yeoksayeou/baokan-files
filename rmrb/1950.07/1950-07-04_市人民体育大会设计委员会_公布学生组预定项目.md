Title: Municipal People's Sports Meet Design Committee Announces Preliminary Student Group Events
Author:
Date: 1950-07-04
Page: 3
【Beijing News Agency】In order to prepare for the 1950 Municipal People's Sports Meet as soon as possible, the Beijing Municipal People's Government Bureau of Culture and Education, in conjunction with the Municipal Sports Association, initiated the establishment of the Sports Meet Design Committee on June 19th. The committee has now completed the design of the principles for the content of student group performances and the competition events. The events for the worker, farmer, military, and general groups are expected to be completed around July 5th (and will be announced separately in the future). To enable participating units to prepare early, the student group event content is announced as follows:

　　(1) Group Performances:

　　(I) Content: Each unit can choose and create performance content based on the following four items:

　　　　A. Gymnastics (freehand exercises, light apparatus, apparatus exercises, formation changes, character formation, figure formation, etc.)
　　　　B. Dance (group dance, Yangge dance, border region dance, singing games, etc.)
　　　　C. Traditional Chinese Martial Arts (group boxing, swordsmanship, etc.)
　　　　D. Folk Skills (various national skills and techniques with sports value, with group performance as the principle)

　　　　Appendix: Apparatus exercises may be used as competition items in addition to group performance content.

　　(II) Precautions: All group performances must pay attention to the following principles:

　　　　A. Attention should be paid to sports value (the content of the movements and the amount of exercise should be suitable for the physiological and psychological needs of the performers).
　　　　B. The political meaning should be profound (avoid being too formal or theatrical).
　　　　C. Pay attention to neatness, seriousness, liveliness, and intensity.
　　　　D. Coordinate with music as much as possible (preferably with musical accompaniment, and the instruments used are not limited).
　　　　E. Creativity (the performance content should strive for novelty, hoping to gradually improve in quality).
　　　　F. Entering and exiting the venue should be as quick as possible, and attention should be paid to lively and majestic marching steps.

　　(2) Competitions:

　　(I) Groups: The following seven groups will be established:

　　　　Men's University Group, Women's Senior Group (merger of women's university and women's high school), Men's Middle School Group, Men's Junior High School Group, Women's Junior Group (women's junior high school), Men's Elementary School Group, Women's Elementary School Group

　　(II) Events (Track and Field):

　　　　Men's University Group: 100 meters, 400 meters, 800 meters, 1500 meters, 5000 meters, 110 meters hurdles, Medley Relay (four runners, the first runs 100 meters, the second runs 200 meters, the third runs 300 meters, the fourth runs 400 meters), high jump, long jump, triple jump, 16-pound shot put, discus, hand grenade throw (wooden handle hand grenade, 9.5 inches long, 12 taels in weight, iron head circumference 5.2 inches, iron head length 2.11 inches)

　　　　Women's Senior Group: 100 meters, 200 meters, 400 meters relay, 80 meters low hurdles, high jump, long jump, shot put, discus, softball throw

　　　　Men's Middle School Group: 100 meters, 400 meters, 1500 meters, 3000 meters, Medley Relay (same as Men's University), high jump, long jump, 12-pound shot put, discus, hand grenade (same as Men's University)

　　　　Men's Junior High School Group: 100 meters, 400 meters relay, high jump, long jump, hand grenade (same as Men's University)

　　　　Women's Junior Group: 100 meters, 200 meters relay, long jump, softball throw

　　　　Men's Elementary School Group: 100 meters, 400 meters relay (eight runners, 50 meters each), long jump, softball throw

　　　　Women's Elementary School Group: 60 meters, 400 meters relay (same as Men's Elementary), long jump, softball throw

　　　　Appendix: The methods for ball games (soccer, basketball, volleyball, baseball, softball) will be determined separately based on the schedule. All units can prepare.

　　(III) Preliminary Selections: Preliminary selections will be held in districts before the official meeting. The preliminary selection methods will be determined separately.

　　(3) Special Performances: In addition to inviting the military to perform military demonstrations, farmers' children in suburban areas to perform fire-fighting skills demonstrations, and ethnic minorities to perform special projects, the conference plans to use the summer vacation to coordinate student cultural activities to organize and train joint group exercises, collective waist drums, collective dance, collective apparatus exercises, and other performance projects.

　　(4) Opening Ceremony: Detailed methods will be determined separately.

### 市人民体育大会设计委员会  公布学生组预定项目

1950-07-04
第3版()
专栏：京市新闻

　　市人民体育大会设计委员会
    公布学生组预定项目
    【京市新闻处讯】北京市人民政府文教局为争取早日筹备一九五○年本市人民体育大会，特于六月十九日联合本市体育分会，发起成立体育大会设计委员会。现该会已将学生组集体表演内容原则及竞赛项目设计完毕；至于工人、农民、部队及普通部之各组项目，约于七月五日可设计完毕（将来另行公布）。为使各参加单位能早日准备，兹将学生组的项目内容公布于后：
    （１）集体表演：
    （一）内容项目：各单位可根据下列四项选择创造为表演内容
    甲、体操（徒手操、轻器械、器械、队形变换、组字、组人形等）
    乙、舞蹈（团体舞、秧歌舞、边疆舞、唱游等）
    丙、国术（团体的拳术、刀枪、击剑等）
    丁、民间技艺（各种国技及有体育价值的技艺以团体表演为原则）
    附：器械操除作为集体表演内容外可能另作比赛
    （二）注意事项：集体表演均须注意下列各项原则：
    甲、应注意体育价值（动作内容与运动量要适合表演者的生理心理需要）
    乙、政治含意要深刻（应避免过于形式化或戏剧化）
    丙、要注意整齐、严肃、活泼、紧张
    丁、尽可能配合音乐（最好要有音乐伴奏所用乐器可不限）
    戊、创造性（表演内容应力求新颖希在质的方面逐步提高）
    己、出入场要力求迅速注意行列步伐活泼雄壮
    （２）竞赛：
    （一）组别：共设下列七组：
    男子大学组、女子高级组（女大、女高中合并）、男子中学组、男子初中组、女子初级组（女初中）、男子小学组、女子小学组
    （二）项目（田径）：
    男子大学组：一○○公尺、四○○公尺、八○○公尺、一五○○公尺、五○○○公尺、一一○公尺高栏、异程接力（四人跑，第一人跑一○○，第二人跑二○○，第三人跑三○○，第四人跑四○○），跳高，跳远，三级跳远，十六磅铅球，铁饼，手榴弹掷远（木把手榴弹，全长九·五英寸重十二市两，铁头周径五·二英寸，铁头长二·一一英寸）
    女子高级组：一○○公尺、二○○公尺、四○○公尺接力，八○公尺低栏，跳高、跳远、铅球、铁饼、垒球掷远
    男子中学组：一○○公尺、四○○公尺、一五○○公尺、三○○○公尺、异程接力（同男大），跳高、跳远、十二磅铅球、铁饼、手榴弹（同男大）
    男子初中组：一○○公尺、四○○公尺接力，跳高、跳远，手榴弹（同男大）
    女子初级组：一○○公尺、二○○公尺接力，跳远、垒球掷远
    男子小学组：一○○公尺、四○○公尺接力（八人跑每人五○公尺），跳远、垒球掷远
    女子小学组：六○公尺、四○○公尺接力（同男小），跳远、垒球掷远
    附：关于球类比赛（足、篮、排、棒、垒）办法，拟根据时间另行规定，各单位可作准备
    （三）预选：正式会期前先分区举行预选。预选办法另定。
    （３）特约表演：大会除约请军队作军事表演，郊区农民子弟作消防技能表演及少数民族作特殊项目表演外，并拟利用暑期，配合学生文艺活动组织训练联合性之团体操、集体腰鼓、集体舞蹈、集体器械操等表演项目。
    （４）入场式：详细办法另行规定
