Title: British Labour MP Brockway Advocates for US Navy Withdrawal from Taiwan, Recognition of Korea's Right to Unity and Independence
Author:
Date: 1950-07-20
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, July 19th: According to a Reuters dispatch from London on July 16th, British Labour MP Brockway wrote in the "Reynold News" that an effective way to prevent the Korean conflict from escalating into a world war is for the US Navy to withdraw from Taiwan. Britain should make it clear that it will not participate in any wider war. Brockway said: "We should now think that Korea is not a stake in the Cold War, but a country that should enjoy unity and independence."

### 英工党议员布罗克威  主张美国海军撤出台湾  承认朝鲜应享有统一和独立

1950-07-20
第1版()
专栏：

　　英工党议员布罗克威
    主张美国海军撤出台湾
    承认朝鲜应享有统一和独立
    【新华社十九日讯】据路透社伦敦十六日电，英工党议员布罗克威在雷诺新闻撰文说：防止朝鲜冲突扩大为世界大战的有效方法是美国海军撤出台湾。英国应当明白宣布不参加任何较为广泛的战争。布罗克威说：我们现在应该想一想，朝鲜不是冷战中的赌注，而是应享有统一和独立的国家。
