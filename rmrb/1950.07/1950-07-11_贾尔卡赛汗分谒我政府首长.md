Title: Jalkhjaa Sain called on our government leaders

Author:
Date: 1950-07-11
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, 10th. After submitting his credentials, Jalkhjaa Sain, the Ambassador of the Mongolian People's Republic to China, separately called on Vice Chairman Zhu De, Vice Chairman Liu Shaoqi, Vice Chairman Li Jishen, Vice Chairman Zhang Lan, Vice Premier Guo Moruo, Vice Premier Huang Yanpei, and separately visited Minister Ye Jizhuang of the Ministry of Trade, Minister Shen Yanbing of the Ministry of Culture, Mayor Nie Rongzhen of Beijing, and Deputy Mayor Wu Han.

### 贾尔卡赛汗分谒我政府首长

1950-07-11
第1版()
专栏：

　　贾尔卡赛汗分谒我政府首长
    【新华社十日讯】蒙古人民共和国驻我国大使贾尔卡赛汗于呈递国书后，曾分谒朱德副主席，刘少奇副主席，李济深副主席，张澜副主席，郭沫若副总理，黄炎培副总理，并分访贸易部叶季壮部长，文化部沈雁冰部长，及北京市聂荣臻市长，吴晗副市长。
