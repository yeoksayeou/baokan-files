Title: Peasants in Newly Liberated Areas of Korea Warmly Support Land Reform; People in Various Areas Hold Meetings and Pledge to Increase Production and Support the Front

Author: 
Date: 1950-07-15
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Pyongyang, July 14th: The decree issued by the Standing Committee of the Supreme People's Assembly of Korea regarding the implementation of land reform through uncompensated confiscation and uncompensated distribution in southern Korea has been warmly supported by peasants in the newly liberated areas. Farmers in Nanyuan Bai County are actively preparing for the implementation of land reform. Farmers in various districts have held mass meetings in succession. Farmers spoke enthusiastically at the meetings and unanimously expressed their determination to increase grain production to support the front and sent telegrams to General Kim Il-sung to pay their respects. Farmers in Ongjin County also expressed at a mass meeting that they were determined to support the land reform decree with practical actions of increasing grain production to supply the front. Under the leadership of the People's Committee, the county has rapidly established revolutionary order.

### 朝鲜新解放地区农民  热烈拥护土地改革  各地群众集会宣誓增产支前

1950-07-15
第4版()
专栏：

　　朝鲜新解放地区农民
    热烈拥护土地改革
    各地群众集会宣誓增产支前
    【新华社平壤十四日电】朝鲜最高人民会议常任委员会发布的关于在朝鲜南部实行无偿没收、无偿分配的土地改革的命令，受到新解放地区农民的热烈拥护。南延白县的农民正积极进行实施土地改革的准备工作。各区农民连日纷纷举行群众大会，农民在会上热烈发言，一致表示以增产粮食来支援前线，并先后电金日成将军致敬。瓮津县的农民们也在群众大会上表示，他们决心以增产粮食供应前线的实际行动，来支持土地改革的法令。该县在人民委员会的领导下，已迅速建立了革命秩序。
