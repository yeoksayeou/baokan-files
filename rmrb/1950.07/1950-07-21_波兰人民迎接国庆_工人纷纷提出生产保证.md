Title: Polish People Welcome National Day; Workers Propose Production Guarantees
Author:
Date: 1950-07-21
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency, 20th. Compiled from Polish News Agency reports: The working people of Poland are striving to fulfill the production guarantees put forward to welcome Polish National Day (July 22). Metallurgical workers throughout Poland are guaranteeing to save the country more than 2 billion złoty (Polish currency). After the workers of the Bierawka cotton textile factory fulfill their guarantees, they will save the country more than 1 billion złoty. Construction workers in Primorze are guaranteeing to save more than 17 million złoty. Agricultural workers in the three provinces of Gdańsk, Zdziecin, and Koszalin have also proposed guarantees to save more than 37 million złoty.
　　To welcome National Day and the second anniversary of the founding of the Polish Youth League, the vast number of young workers in Poland have put forward production guarantees one after another. The "Młoda Gwardia" youth tractor team of the Lukowis State Farm in Warsaw Province guarantees to complete 125 percent of the quota in this year's harvesting work, and has challenged youth tractor teams across the country.

### 波兰人民迎接国庆  工人纷纷提出生产保证

1950-07-21
第4版()
专栏：

　　波兰人民迎接国庆
    工人纷纷提出生产保证
    【新华社二十日讯】综合波兰通讯社消息：波兰全国的劳动人民正在争取完成为迎接波兰国庆日（七月二十二日）所提出的生产保证。全波兰的冶金工人在保证要为国家节省二十多亿兹罗提（波币）。俾拉维棉织品工厂的工人完成他们的保证后，将给国家节省十多亿兹罗提。普利莫尔兹的建筑工人保证要节省一千七百余万兹罗提。格丹斯克、兹杰辛和柯斯萨林三省的农业工人也提出保证，要节省三千七百余万兹罗提。
    波兰广大的劳动青年，为了迎接国庆日和波兰青年联盟成立两周年纪念日，已纷纷提出生产保证。华沙省鲁克威斯国营农场的“姆拉达·吉浦蒂的亚”青年拖拉机队，保证在今年收获工作中完成定额百分之一百二十五，并已向全国青年拖拉机队提出挑战。
