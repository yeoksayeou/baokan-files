Title: People's Representatives of the Fifth District Fully Elected
Author:
Date: 1950-07-08
Page: 3

　　Beijing News

　　People's Representatives of the Fifth District Fully Elected
    [Report from this newspaper] The Preparatory Committee for the People's Representatives Conference of the Fifth District, in coordination with the election propaganda work, has been deeply involved with the masses to collect and reflect opinions. From June 11th to the present, it has received more than 600 pieces of opinions from the district people on public welfare, cadre work style, and other aspects. The district representatives were all elected on June 29th, and it was decided to hold forums for district people's representatives and worker representatives separately in the coming days. In addition to exchanging opinions and explaining the tasks of the representatives and the precautions in the conference, the representatives will be called upon to cooperate with the city-wide summer sanitation campaign and carry out extensive publicity among the masses.
    (Secretariat of the Preparatory Committee for the People's Representatives of the Fifth District)

### 五区人民代表会议全部代表已选出

1950-07-08
第3版()
专栏：京市新闻

　　五区人民代表会议全部代表已选出
    【本报讯】第五区各界人民代表筹委会配合选举宣传工作，深入群众收集反映，从六月十一日起截至目前止，已先后收到区民对公共福利、干部作风等方面的意见六百余件。该区区代表已于六月二十九日全部选出，并决定日内分别召开区民代表及工人代表座谈会。除交换意见、说明代表任务和大会中注意事项外，将号召代表配合全市展开的夏季卫生运动，在群众中进行广泛宣传。
    （五区人民代表筹委会秘书处）
