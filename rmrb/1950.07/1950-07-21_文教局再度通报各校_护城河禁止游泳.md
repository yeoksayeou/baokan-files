Title: Bureau of Culture and Education Re-issues Notice to All Schools: Swimming in the Moat is Prohibited
Author:
Date: 1950-07-21
Page: 3

　　Bureau of Culture and Education Re-issues Notice to All Schools
    Swimming in the Moat is Prohibited
    [Our Newspaper] This month on the 2nd, Zhang Liu (17 years old), a junior middle school student at the private Pingmin Middle School, went swimming in the moat outside Fuxingmen with his classmates and drowned. The Municipal Bureau of Culture and Education has re-issued notices to all schools, earnestly educating students to prevent such unfortunate events from happening again.

### 文教局再度通报各校  护城河禁止游泳

1950-07-21
第3版()
专栏：

　　文教局再度通报各校
    护城河禁止游泳
    【本报讯】本月二日，私立平民中学初三学生张留（十七岁）和同学到复兴门外护城河去游泳，溺死后，市文教局已再通报各校向学生恳切进行教育，勿再使这类不幸事件发生。
