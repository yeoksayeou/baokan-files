Title: People of All Countries Protest U.S. Imperialist Aggression Against Korea and Unanimously Support the Just Struggle of the Korean People
Author:
Date: 1950-07-10
Page: 4

　　People of All Countries Protest U.S. Imperialist Aggression Against Korea and Unanimously Support the Just Struggle of the Korean People

    Xinhua News Agency, July 9th dispatch: News of the working people of Eastern European People's Democratic countries protesting against U.S. imperialist armed aggression against Korea continues to pour in. The Hungarian people have sent telegrams to the Hungarian Workers' Party and the Central Council of Hungarian Trade Unions, expressing their anger at the despicable acts of U.S. imperialists in attacking the Korean people, and declaring that they will promote the victory of the Korean people's just struggle by intensifying labor and intensifying the struggle against reactionaries. The Central Council of Hungarian Trade Unions, as well as women's and youth organizations, have sent telegrams to support the Korean people and protest against American aggression in the Far East.
    
    In Bulgaria, the Worker's Cause newspaper published many resolutions from the working people of cities such as Plovdiv and Dimitrovgrad protesting against U.S. imperialist intervention in Korea. Newspapers published editorials condemning U.S. imperialist acts of aggression in the Far East. The Bulgarian National Peace Committee sent telegrams to UN Secretary-General Trygve Lie and peace organizations such as the World Peace Congress, protesting against American aggression in Korea.
    
    Following the Romanian General Confederation of Labor and youth organizations, the Romanian Democratic Women's League also sent telegrams to Korean women, guaranteeing that Romanian women will fight more resolutely against the warmongers to support the just struggle of the Korean people.
    
    The Catholic Patriotic Priests Conference held in Hradec Králové, Czechoslovakia, also resolved to condemn the U.S. aggression against Korea.
    
    Xinhua News Agency, Ulaanbaatar, July 8th dispatch: Mass rallies were held in various parts of Mongolia. At the meetings, livestock breeders, workers, and intellectuals angrily protested against U.S. armed intervention in Korea.
    
    The Unen newspaper published an editorial entitled "Mongolian People Denounce American Aggressors," saying: "By launching armed intervention against the Korean people, the U.S. imperialists have exposed themselves to the world as aggressors, enemies of the Korean people, and enemies of world peace. Now, it is an open secret known to all: the Korean civil war was prepared and instigated by the U.S. imperialists; they are trying to turn the entire Korea into their colony through armed intervention in Korea, enslave the Korean people, and turn Korea into a military springboard for attacking the Soviet Union and the People's Republic of China."
    
    The newspaper declared: "Like all progressive mankind, the Mongolian people denounce the U.S. imperialists' military aggression against Korea, China, and some other Asian countries. They demand that the U.S. military immediately withdraw from Korea and stop interfering in the internal affairs of China, Vietnam, and the Philippines."
    
    Xinhua News Agency, July 9th dispatch: The working people of the capitalist world, from Northern Europe to Australia, from the Near East to Central America, have unanimously expressed their opposition to U.S. imperialist armed intervention in Korea and their support for the just struggle of the Korean people. According to a comprehensive TASS report, a grand protest rally with 30,000 people participating was held in Helsinki, the capital of Finland, on the 6th. Speakers at the rally unanimously emphasized the dire consequences caused by U.S. imperialist aggression in Korea, and unanimously decided to send a telegram to Chairman Kim Il-sung of Korea, wishing the Korean people victory in their struggle against the imperialists and their running dogs.
    
    Stockholm and Gothenburg, Sweden, both held grand protest rallies on the 6th, protesting against U.S. imperialist aggression against Korea and the Swedish government's position of participating in the U.S. government's gambling policy; demanding that the Korean people resolve their own affairs without foreign interference. At the same time, the Swedish Communist Party's organ, Ny Dag, published a statement issued by the Political Bureau of the Central Committee of the Swedish Communist Party, which called on all those who support national self-determination to uphold the just struggle of the Korean people.
    
    In the Near East, the Central Committee of the Israeli Communist Party issued a statement to the people entitled "Stop American Aggression Against Korea," demanding an end to the imperialists' armed intervention in the Far East, and calling on the Israeli people to intensify their struggle for peace and for the banning of atomic weapons. The Syrian Al-Ba'ath newspaper pointed out that the Security Council's decision on Korea, passed without the presence of representatives from the Soviet Union and the People's Republic of China, was illegal.
    
    In Pakistan, a trade union conference held in Karachi on July 1 passed a resolution protesting against U.S. imperialist interference in Korea's internal affairs, and demanding the withdrawal of all U.S. troops from Korea. At the meeting, Faiz Ahmed Faiz, editor of The Pakistan Times, also denounced the illegality of the Security Council's resolution on Korea.
    
    Some of the largest progressive organizations in South American country Cuba - the Cuban National Peace Committee, the Cuban Socialist Youth League, and the Cuban Democratic Women's Federation - all issued declarations strongly condemning the U.S. military's war of aggression against Korean patriots, and calling on the Cuban people to intensify their campaign to collect signatures on the declaration demanding the banning of atomic weapons.
    
    According to a United Press Sydney dispatch, the Australian people held a demonstration in front of the U.S. Consulate in Sydney on the 4th. They held signs that read "No interference in Korea," "No troops to Korea," and "Down with the American aggressors."

### 各国人民抗议美帝侵略朝鲜一致声援朝鲜人民正义斗争

1950-07-10
第4版()
专栏：

　　各国人民抗议美帝侵略朝鲜一致声援朝鲜人民正义斗争
    匈
    【新华社九日讯】综合塔斯社消息：东欧人民民主国家劳动人民抗议美帝国主义武装侵略朝鲜的消息，正继续不断地传来。匈牙利人民纷纷致电匈牙利工人党和匈牙利工会中央理事会，对美帝国主义者进攻朝鲜人民的卑劣行为，表示愤怒，并声明要以加紧劳动和加紧与反动派进行斗争来促成朝鲜人民正义斗争的胜利。匈牙利工会中央理事会以及妇女和青年团体均致电声援朝鲜人民，抗议美国在远东的侵略行为。
    保
    在保加利亚，工人事业报发表了普勒文、季米特洛夫格勒等城市劳动人民抗议美帝国主义者干涉朝鲜的许多决议案，各报纷纷发表社论，斥责美帝国主义者在远东的侵略行为。保加利亚全国拥护和平委员会致电联合国秘书长赖伊及世界拥护和平大会等和平组织，抗议美国侵略朝鲜。
    罗
    罗马尼亚民主妇联亦继罗国总工会和青年团体之后，致电朝鲜妇女，保证罗国妇女将更坚决地和战争贩子们斗争，来支持朝鲜人民的正义斗争。
    捷
    在捷克斯洛伐克佛勒拉德举行的天主教爱国神父大会，也决议斥责美国侵略朝鲜的暴行。
     蒙古
    【新华社九日讯】塔斯社乌兰巴托尔八日讯：蒙古全国各地纷纷举行群众大会。会上，牲畜饲养者、工人和知识分子愤怒抗议美国武装干涉朝鲜。
    乌嫩报以“蒙古人民痛斥美国侵略者”为题，发表社论说：美帝国主义者发动反对朝鲜人民的武装干涉，从而在全世界面前暴露了自己是侵略者，是朝鲜人民的敌人和世界和平的敌人。现在，这已是人所共知的公开的秘密：朝鲜的内战是美帝国主义者所准备并促使其爆发的；他们力图借武装干涉朝鲜而使整个朝鲜沦为他们的殖民地，奴役朝鲜人民，并把朝鲜变为进攻苏联与中华人民共和国的军事跳板。
    该报宣称：正像全体进步人类一样，蒙古人民痛斥美帝国主义者军事侵略朝鲜、中国及其他某些亚洲国家。他们要求美军立即从朝鲜撤退，并停止干涉中国、越南和菲律宾的内政。
    芬兰
    【新华社九日讯】资本主义世界的劳动人民，从北欧到澳大利亚，从近东到中美洲，一致表示反对美国帝国主义武装干涉朝鲜和声援朝鲜人民的正义斗争。综合塔斯社消息：芬兰京城赫尔辛基六日举行了有三万人参加的盛大抗议集会，大会演说者一致强调指出美帝国主义在朝鲜的侵略行为所造成的恶果，并一致决定致电朝鲜金日成主席，预祝朝鲜人民反对帝国主义者及其走狗的斗争的胜利。
    瑞典
    瑞典斯德哥尔摩城与哥德堡城，在六日都举行了盛大的抗议集会，抗议美帝国主义侵略朝鲜和瑞典政府参与美国政府的赌博政策的立场；要求朝鲜人民不受外国干涉地解决它本国的事务。同时，瑞典共产党机关报《新时代》刊载了瑞典共产党中央委员会政治局所发表的声明，该声明号召所有赞成民族自决的人们来维护朝鲜人民的正义斗争。
    近东
    在近东，以色列共产党中央委员会以“制止美国侵略朝鲜”为题，发表告人民书，要求制止帝国主义者在远东的武装干涉，并号召以色列人民要加紧为和平、禁用原子武器而斗争。叙利亚的《巴斯报》指出：安全理事会没有苏联和中华人民共和国的代表出席而通过的对朝鲜的决定是非法的。
    巴基斯坦
    在巴基斯坦，七月一日在喀喇蚩举行的职工会大会通过决议，抗议美帝国主义干涉朝鲜内政，并要求从朝鲜撤退一切美国军队。会上，《巴基斯坦时报》编辑费斯·阿克默德·费斯也斥责安理会对朝鲜的决议的非法。
    南美
    南美国家古巴的一些最大的进步组织——古巴全国和平委员会，古巴社会主义青年联盟和古巴民主妇女联合会都发表宣言，一致强烈地斥责美国军队对朝鲜爱国者所进行的侵略战争，并号召古巴人民加紧进行征集在为要求禁止原子武器的宣言上签名的运动。
    澳洲
    另据合众社悉尼讯：澳大利亚人民于四日在悉尼美领馆前举行示威。他们手持“不干涉朝鲜”、“不出兵朝鲜”、“打倒美国佬的侵略行为”的标语。
