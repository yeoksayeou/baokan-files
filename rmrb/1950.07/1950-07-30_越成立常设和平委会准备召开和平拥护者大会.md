Title: Vietnam Establishes Standing Peace Committee to Prepare for Peace Supporters' Conference
Author:
Date: 1950-07-30
Page: 4

　　Xinhua News Agency, Yangon, 27th: According to the Điện Tín News Agency, Vietnam recently held a conference where a Standing Peace Committee was established. This committee is tasked with preparing for the Vietnam Peace Supporters' Conference. The conference was convened by the Executive Committee of the Vietnam National United Front, and representatives from various people's organizations, democratic parties, cultural and religious groups attended. Tôn Đức Thắng, Chairman of the Standing Committee of the National Assembly, was elected as Chairman of the Standing Peace Committee. During the preparation for the Peace Conference, the Standing Peace Committee will maintain close contact with the Standing Committee of the World Peace Council.

### 越成立常设和平委会准备召开和平拥护者大会

1950-07-30
第4版()
专栏：

　　越成立常设和平委会准备召开和平拥护者大会
    【新华社二十九日讯】据电通社仰光二十七日讯：越南最近召开了一次会议，会上设立了常设和平委员会，这个委员会负有准备召开越南和平拥护者大会的任务。会议系由越南民族统一战线执行委员会所召开，到会的有各人民团体、民主党派、文化和宗教团体的代表。国民议会常设委员会主席孙德胜当选为常设和平委员会主席。在准备召开和平大会期间，常设和平委员会将与世界和大常设委员会保持密切联系。
