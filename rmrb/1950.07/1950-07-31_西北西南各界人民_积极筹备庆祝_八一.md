Title: People from All Circles in Northwest and Southwest China Actively Prepare to Celebrate "August 1st"
Author:
Date: 1950-07-31
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency: The people of all ethnic groups and all circles in Northwest and Southwest China are enthusiastically preparing to celebrate "August 1st" (Army Day). Xi'an, Lanzhou, Ürümqi and other places will hold grand parades and demonstrations on "August 1st" to celebrate the founding of the People's Liberation Army and oppose American aggression.

The Political Department of the Southwest Military Region instructed all its departments to hold small commemorative meetings and recollection parties on the "August 1st" Army Day to recall the historical achievements of the struggle against the enemy, commend heroic models, and use this as an opportunity to launch various meritorious service campaigns and a signature campaign against American aggression and in defense of world peace. In order to widely launch commemorative activities, the Chongqing Municipal People's Government specially notified all districts to convene symposiums and dinners on August 1st for the families of revolutionary martyrs and soldiers, and for disabled soldiers, to express condolences. The city's Literary and Art Workers Association is mobilizing all members to write letters of appreciation to the People's Liberation Army.

### 西北西南各界人民  积极筹备庆祝“八一”

1950-07-31
第1版()
专栏：

　　西北西南各界人民
    积极筹备庆祝“八一”
    【本报讯】据新华社讯：西北、西南各族各界人民热烈筹备庆祝“八一”。西安、兰州、迪化等地均将于“八一”举行庆祝人民解放军建军节及反对美国侵略的盛大游行示威。
    西南军区政治部指示所属各部，于“八一”建军节召开小型纪念会，和回忆晚会，回忆对敌斗争的史绩，表扬英雄模范，借以展开各项立功运动和展开反对美国侵略、保卫世界和平的签名运动。重庆市人民政府为了广泛展开纪念活动，特通知各区于八月一日召集辖区革命烈士及军人的家属和荣誉军人举行座谈会、聚餐以表示慰问。该市文艺工作者协会正发动全体会员写信慰问解放军。
