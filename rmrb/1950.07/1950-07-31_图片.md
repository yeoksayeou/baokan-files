Title: Picture
Author:
Date: 1950-07-31
Page: 4

A large number of American officers and soldiers captured by the Korean people in the American war of aggression against Korea issued a joint statement stating that they received the most humane treatment in Korea and opposed American armed intervention in Korea. The picture shows the American prisoners of war signing the statement.
(Manuscript from the News Photography Bureau, provided by the Korean Central News Agency's General Branch in China)

### 图片

1950-07-31
第4版()
专栏：

　　在美国侵略朝鲜的战争中被朝鲜人民俘虏的大批美军官兵，发表联合声明，说明他们在朝鲜受到最人道的街遇，反对美国武装干涉朝鲜。照片上是美军俘虏正在声明书上署名的情形。
    （新闻摄影局稿，由朝鲜中央通讯社驻华总分社供稿）
