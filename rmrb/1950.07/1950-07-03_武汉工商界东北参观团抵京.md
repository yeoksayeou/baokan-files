Title: Wuhan Business Community Northeast Visit Group Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1950-07-03
Page: 1

　　Wuhan Business Community
    Northeast Visit Group Arrives in Beijing
    [This newspaper's dispatch] The Wuhan business community's Northeast Visit Group, consisting of twenty-three people, arrived in Beijing a few days ago. The group is headed by Hua Yuqing, Deputy Manager of the Shenxin Fourth Textile Company, with Zhao Houfu and Yu Jintang serving as Deputy Heads. Wang Dezhang, Section Chief of the United Front Work Department of the Central South Bureau, is accompanying the group, and industrialist Li Guowei is serving as an advisor. The group's purpose in visiting North and Northeast China is mainly to absorb advanced experiences in industry and commerce as a reference for the restoration and development of Wuhan's industry and commerce. After visiting major industrial and commercial units in Beijing, the group is scheduled to leave Beijing on the 7th of this month for Tianjin, Shenyang, Fushun, Anshan, Benxi Lake, Xiaofengman, Changchun, Harbin, and Dalian for visits, with an expected return to Wuhan in a month and a half.

### 武汉工商界东北参观团抵京

1950-07-03
第1版()
专栏：

　　武汉工商界
    东北参观团抵京
    【本报讯】武汉工商界东北参观团一行二十三人于日前抵京。该团系由申新第四纺织公司副经理华煜卿担任团长，赵厚甫、余金堂担任副团长，并有中南局统战部汪德彰科长随行，及工业家李国伟担任顾问。该团参观华北与东北的目的，主要为吸收工商业的先进经验，来作为恢复和发展武汉工商业的借镜。预计在京参观各大工商业单位后，将于本月七日离京前往天津、沈阳、抚顺、鞍山、本溪湖、小丰满、长春、哈尔滨及大连等地参观，预计一个半月回返武汉。
