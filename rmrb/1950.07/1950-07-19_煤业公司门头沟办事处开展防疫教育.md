Date: 1950-07-19

The Mentougou Office of the Coal Industry Company Carries Out Epidemic Prevention Education

[This newspaper report] The employees of the Mentougou Office of the Beijing China Coal Industry Construction Materials Company have usually neglected hygiene. From early June to early July, thirty-two people contracted colds and dysentery in one month, and such spread will affect the overall work. Now the administrative side of the office is focusing on the source of the disease and conducting epidemic prevention education for all employees, proposing that they should pay attention to personal and public hygiene; and organizing hygiene teams to carry out mass fly and mosquito eradication campaigns, regularly holding cleaning activities, and maintaining a clean environment. (Mentougou Office of China Coal Industry Construction Materials Company)

### 煤业公司门头沟办事处开展防疫教育

1950-07-19
第3版()
专栏：

　　煤业公司门头沟办事处开展防疫教育
    【本报讯】京市中国煤业建筑器材公司门头沟办事处职工平素疏忽卫生，六月上旬至七月上旬，一月中患感冒、痢疾达三十二人，如此蔓延下去将影响全面工作。现该处行政方面正针对病源，对全体职工进行防疫教育，提出应重视个人及公共卫生；并组织卫生小组，开展群众性的扑蝇灭蚊运动，经常举行扫除，保持环境整洁。（中国煤业建筑器材公司门头沟办事处）
