Title: The Ministry of Education of the Central People's Government to Hold Summer Political Class Teaching Seminar, Notifying Major Administrative Regions to Select Teachers to Participate
Author:
Date: 1950-07-11
Page: 3

　　Xinhua News Agency, July 7th: In order to improve the theoretical level of political teachers and strengthen teaching work in various regions, the Ministry of Education of the Central People's Government will organize a summer political class teaching seminar this summer vacation. The seminar is scheduled to run from July 24th to August 23rd. The content of the discussion will be the history of social development and On New Democracy. The seminar will conclude with three days of exchanging experiences on teaching organization and methods. The seminar will be chaired by Qian Junrui, Vice Minister of Education, Zhang Zonglin, Deputy Director of the Higher Education Department, and Gao Erbai, Deputy Chief of the Second Division of the Department. Ai Siqi and He Ganzhi will be invited as professors to lead the teaching work.

　　On July 1st, the Ministry notified the education departments of major administrative regions to select political teachers with a certain theoretical level and who can serve as backbone personnel to come to Beijing to participate in the seminar.

### 中央人民政府教育部  举办暑期政治课教学讨论会已通知各大行政区选派教师参加

1950-07-11
第3版()
专栏：

　　中央人民政府教育部
    举办暑期政治课教学讨论会已通知各大行政区选派教师参加
    【新华社七日讯】中央人民政府教育部为提高政治教师理论水平，加强各地教学工作，特于今年暑假期间组织暑期政治课教学讨论会。讨论会预定自七月二十四日起，至八月二十三日止。讨论的内容为社会发展史和新民主主义论。讨论会最后将以三天时间交流关于教学组织和方法的经验。讨论会将由教育部副部长钱俊瑞、高等教育司副司长张宗麟及该司第二处副处长高尔柏主持，同时聘请艾思奇、何干之两位教授分别领导教学工作。
    该部已于七月一日通知各大行政区教育部门选派具备一定理论水平和能做骨干的政治课教师来京参加讨论会。
