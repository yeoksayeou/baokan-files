Title: Representatives of the Fourth District Election Area
Author: Huang Xueyan
Date: 1950-07-22
Page: 3

　　Representatives of the Fourth District Election Area
    [Our Newspaper] The Preparatory Committee of the People's Representatives Conference of All Circles in the Fourth District of Beijing has, since July 5th, mobilized more than 50 night school students, female workers, and housewives in the district to vigorously promote the work, and has now completed the key pilot election work of representatives in two police station election areas.
                                                  (Huang Xueyan)

### 四区选举区代表
黄学彦
1950-07-22
第3版()
专栏：

　　四区选举区代表
    【本报讯】京市四区各界人民代表会筹委会从七月五日起，经动员该区内五十多名夜校学生、女工、家庭妇女大力宣传后，目前已完成两派出所选举区代表的重点试选工作。
                                                  （黄学彦）
