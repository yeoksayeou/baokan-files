Title: TASS Authorized Statement: Britain Did Not Propose a Peaceful Solution to the Korean War to the Soviet Union
Author:
Date: 1950-07-08
Page: 4

TASS Authorized Statement
    Britain Did Not Propose a Peaceful Solution to the Korean War to the Soviet Union
    [Xinhua News Agency, July 7th, Moscow] TASS, Moscow, July 7th: TASS has issued the following statement: A representative of the British Foreign Office stated at a press conference in London on the 5th that Britain had already submitted a proposal to the Soviet government through its ambassador in Moscow regarding a peaceful resolution to the Korean War, and is awaiting a reply from the Soviet government.
    TASS is authorized to declare: The British Ambassador to Moscow, in his meeting with a representative of the Soviet Foreign Ministry, did not submit any proposals to the Soviet government regarding a peaceful resolution to the Korean War, but merely expressed the hope that the Korean conflict would gradually resolve itself. Obviously, the British Ambassador to Moscow's remarks do not require any response from the Soviet government.

### 塔斯社受权声明　英并未向苏提出和平解决朝鲜问题的建议

1950-07-08
第4版()
专栏：

　　塔斯社受权声明
    英并未向苏提出和平解决朝鲜问题的建议
    【新华社七日讯】塔斯社莫斯科七日讯：塔斯社顷发表声明如下：英国外交部代表于五日在伦敦记者招待会上声称：英国业已经由其驻莫斯科大使向苏联政府提出关于和平解决朝鲜问题的建议，并且等待着苏联政府的答复。
    塔斯社兹受权声明：英国驻莫斯科大使在其与苏联外交部代表晤谈时，并未向苏联政府提交任何关于和平解决朝鲜问题的建议，只是表示希望朝鲜冲突会逐渐解决而已。显然的，英国驻莫斯科大使的谈话并不需要苏联政府的任何答复。
