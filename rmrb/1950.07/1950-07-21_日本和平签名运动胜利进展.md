Title: Victorious Progress of the Japanese Peace Signature Campaign
Author:
Date: 1950-07-21
Page: 4

　　Xinhua News Agency, Tokyo, 20th dispatch via Tass, 19th: The signature campaign to defend peace and ban atomic weapons is progressing victoriously in Japan. The Communist Party Committee of Yamaguchi Prefecture (located at the southwestern tip of Honshu) is actively conducting the signature campaign. The mayor of Oguchi Town (located in Aichi Prefecture) has also signed the Stockholm Appeal.

　　The Osaka Branch Committee of the League of Defenders of Democracy (formerly translated as the League to Defend Democracy) recently held a meeting and passed a resolution to strengthen the signature campaign. The Kanto Private Railway Workers' Union Federation decided to hold a peace vote among its 33,000 members. The Fuse District Trade Union Council in Osaka collected 469 signatures for the Stockholm Appeal within two days. The Tokyo Sumida District Signature Collection Committee had collected 2,000 signatures by July 9th and will collect 200,000 signatures by August 5th.

### 日本和平签名运动胜利进展

1950-07-21
第4版()
专栏：

　　日本和平签名运动胜利进展
    【新华社二十日讯】塔斯社十九日电引东京消息：征集保卫和平与禁止原子武器的签名运动，正在日本胜利地进展着。山口县（按在本州西南端）共产党委员会正积极地进行签名运动。大口镇（按在爱知县）镇长也在斯德哥尔摩宣言上签了名。
    民主主义拥护同盟（前译保卫民主同盟）大阪分会委员会最近举行会议时，通过加强签名运动的决定。关东区私营铁路工人工会联合会决定在三万三千名会员中举行和平投票。大阪的布施区工会协议会，在两天以内征集了四百六十九个斯德哥尔摩宣言的签名。东京隅田区征集签名委员会到七月九日为止已征集了二千人的签名，到八月五日将征集到二十万人的签名。
