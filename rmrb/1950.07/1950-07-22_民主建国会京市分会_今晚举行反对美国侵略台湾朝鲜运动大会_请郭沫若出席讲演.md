Title: Beijing Municipal Branch of the China Democratic National Construction Association to Hold Rally Against U.S. Aggression in Taiwan and Korea; Guo Moruo Invited to Speak

Author:
Date: 1950-07-22
Page: 6

　　Beijing Municipal Branch of the China Democratic National Construction Association
    Tonight to Hold Rally Against U.S. Aggression in Taiwan and Korea
    Guo Moruo Invited to Speak
    The Beijing Municipal Branch of the China Democratic National Construction Association has scheduled a rally against U.S. aggression in Taiwan and Korea for 7:00 PM on July 22nd at the Xiehe Auditorium on Dongdan Santiao. Mr. Guo Moruo has been invited to speak, and the newsreel "Korean August 15th Liberation Day" will be shown. Those from all walks of life who wish to participate may obtain admission tickets through introductions from organizations, groups, members, or friends by first contacting No. 24 Wuliang Daren Hutong.

### 民主建国会京市分会  今晚举行反对美国侵略台湾朝鲜运动大会  请郭沫若出席讲演

1950-07-22
第6版()
专栏：

　　民主建国会京市分会
    今晚举行反对美国侵略台湾朝鲜运动大会
    请郭沫若出席讲演
    民主建国会北京市分会定二十二日下午七时，在东单三条协和礼堂举行反对美国侵略台湾朝鲜运动大会，请郭沫若先生演讲，并放映“朝鲜八一五解放日”新闻影片。各界如愿意参加，可由机关团体或会员会友介绍，先到无量大人胡同二十四号洽领入场券。
