Title: Life Education Society Holds Gathering in Beijing to Commemorate the Fourth Anniversary of Tao Xingzhi's Death
Author:
Date: 1950-07-27
Page: 1
　　
　　Life Education Society Holds Gathering in Beijing
　　Commemorates the Fourth Anniversary of Tao Xingzhi's Death

　　[Our newspaper reports] The Life Education Society held a gathering in Beijing on the 25th to commemorate the fourth anniversary of the death of the late people's educator, Mr. Tao Xingzhi. More than 500 people attended, including friends of Mr. Tao during his lifetime, members of the Life Education Society, and students from various schools founded by Mr. Tao. At the meeting, Liu Shi gave a report on Mr. Tao's thoughts and theories. Shao Lizi, Yang Weiyu, Tian Han, and Sa Kongliao also spoke. They all agreed that Mr. Tao had struggled for the people's education cause for many years, and his achievements are a valuable legacy in our construction of people's education in New China.

### 生活教育社在京集会  纪念陶行知逝世四周年

1950-07-27
第1版()
专栏：

　　生活教育社在京集会
    纪念陶行知逝世四周年
    【本报讯】生活教育社于二十五日在北京举行集会，纪念已故人民教育家陶行知先生逝世四周年。到会的有陶先生生前友好及生活教育社员和陶先生主办的各学校同学等共五百余人。会上由柳湜作了关于陶先生思想学说的报告，邵力子、杨卫玉、田汉、萨空了也都讲了话。他们一致认为陶先生为人民教育事业奋斗多年，其成果是我们建设新中国人民教育中的宝贵遗产。
