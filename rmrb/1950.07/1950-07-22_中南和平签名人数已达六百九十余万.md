Title: Central South Peace Signature Campaign Reaches 6.9 Million

Author:
Date: 1950-07-22
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Hankou, July 21st: The peace signature campaign in the Central South region continues to expand widely in cities and rural areas. According to incomplete statistics from mid-July: Hunan, Jiangxi, Henan, Hubei, and Guangxi provinces, as well as Wuhan and Guangzhou cities, have seen 6,922,216 people sign the peace declaration. Among them, Hunan Province has 2,035,768 people, Henan Province has over 1.7 million people, Guangxi Province has 492,954 people, Hubei Province has over 600,000 people, Jiangxi Province has 400,000 people from the six cities of Nanchang, Jiujiang, Ganzhou, Linchuan, Pingxiang alone, Guangzhou City has 706,100 people, and Wuhan City has 973,957 people. In addition, the Wuhan branch of the China Committee for the Defense of World Peace has received signature books directly from various places in the Central South region containing 13,437 signatures.

### 中南和平签名人数已达六百九十余万

1950-07-22
第1版()
专栏：

　　中南和平签名人数已达六百九十余万
    【本报讯】据新华社汉口二十一日电：中南区各地和平签名运动继续在城市与农村中广泛展开。据七月中旬不完全统计：湖南、江西、河南、湖北、广西五省和武汉、广州两市已有六百九十二万二千二百一十六人在和平宣言上签名。其中湖南省有二百零三万五千七百六十八人，河南省有一百七十余万人，广西省有四十九万二千九百五十四人，湖北省有六十余万人，江西省仅南昌、九江、赣州、临川、萍乡六个城市即有四十万人，广州市有七十万六千一百余人，武汉市有九十七万三千九百五十七人。此外，中南区各地直接向中国保卫世界和平大会委员会武汉分会投递的签名册中，有一万三千四百三十七人签名。
