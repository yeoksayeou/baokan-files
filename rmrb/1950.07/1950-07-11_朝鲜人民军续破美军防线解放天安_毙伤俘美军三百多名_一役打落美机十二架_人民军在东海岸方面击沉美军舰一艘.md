Title: Korean People's Army Continues to Break Through American Defenses and Liberate Cheonan; More Than 300 American Soldiers Killed, Wounded, or Captured; Twelve American Planes Shot Down in One Battle; People's Army Sinks American Warship on East Coast

Author:

Date: 1950-07-11
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Pyongyang, 10th — The General Headquarters of the Korean People's Army of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea announced in a battle report on the morning of the 9th: The People's Army, which has been advancing south along the Seoul-Mokpo railway line since Seoul, liberated Suwon and Pyeongtaek in Gyeonggi Province on the 4th and 7th, respectively. They continued to advance south, attacking into South Chungcheong Province, and broke through the American defenses and liberated Cheonan County (approximately 50 kilometers from Daejeon) at 11:00 AM on the 8th. In this battle, the People's Army killed or wounded more than 300 American soldiers, captured more than 60 American soldiers, and shot down four American planes, capturing two American pilots alive. The remaining enemy troops retreated in panic, abandoning many weapons and ammunition. In the battle to liberate Cheonan, the People's Army Air Force and anti-aircraft artillery units, in addition to shooting down four American planes, shot down a total of five American Air Force P-80 and P-51 fighters, one Mustang fighter, and two B-29 aircraft in the front and rear areas. The People's Army troops are currently continuing to advance south.

Xinhua News Agency, Pyongyang, 10th — According to a battle report released on the morning of the 10th by the General Headquarters of the Korean People's Army of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea: On the 9th, a unit of the People's Army shelled an American imperialist warship that had invaded Korean territorial waters on the east coast, sinking one American warship.

People's Army units are pursuing American troops and the remnants of the Syngman Rhee puppet army on all fronts, continuing to advance south.

1950-07-11
第1版()
专栏：

　　朝鲜人民军续破美军防线解放天安
    毙伤俘美军三百多名  一役打落美机十二架
    人民军在东海岸方面击沉美军舰一艘
    【新华社平壤十日电】朝鲜民主主义人民共和国人民军总司令部九日晨发表战报称：自汉城沿汉（汉城）浦（全罗南道的木浦）铁道南下的人民军，在四日与七日先后解放京畿道的水原与平泽后，继续南进，攻入忠清南道，并于八日上午十一时突破美军防线，解放天安县城（距大田约五十余公里）。在这一战斗中，人民军毙伤美军三百余名，俘虏美军六十余名，击落美机四架，活捉美驾驶员二人。残敌仓惶败退，丢弃武器弹药甚多。人民军空军与高射炮部队在解放天安的战役中，除击落美机四架外，复在前线和后方击落美空军Ｐ——八十式与Ｐ——五一式战斗机共五架，野马式机一架，及Ｂ——二九式机二架。人民军各部队刻正继续向南挺进中。
    【新华社平壤十日电】据朝鲜民主主义人民共和国人民军总司令部十日晨发表战报称：人民军某部九日在东海岸方面炮击侵入朝鲜领海的美帝国主义军舰，击沉美军舰一艘。
    人民军部队正在各战线上追击着美军部队与李承晚残余伪军，继续南进中。
