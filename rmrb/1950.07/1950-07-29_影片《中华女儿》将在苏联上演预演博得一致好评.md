Title: Film "Daughters of China" to be Screened in Soviet Union, Preview Receives Universal Acclaim
Author:
Date: 1950-07-29
Page: 3

　　Film "Daughters of China" to be Screened in Soviet Union, Preview Receives Universal Acclaim
    [Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, July 27th] TASS reports from Moscow: The Chinese film "Daughters of China" is about to be released in the Soviet Union. The film was previewed on the 26th at the Soviet Film Department and received unanimous praise.

### 影片《中华女儿》将在苏联上演预演博得一致好评

1950-07-29
第3版()
专栏：

　　影片《中华女儿》将在苏联上演预演博得一致好评
    【新华社二十七日讯】据塔斯社莫斯科讯：中国影片《中华女儿》即将在苏上映。该片已于二十六日在苏联电影事业部预映，博得一致好评。
