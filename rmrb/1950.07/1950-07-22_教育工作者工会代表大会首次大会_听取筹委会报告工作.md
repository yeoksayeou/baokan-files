Title: First Congress of Education Workers' Union Representatives: Listening to the Work Report of the Preparatory Committee
Author:
Date: 1950-07-22
Page: 3

　　First Congress of Education Workers' Union Representatives
    Listening to the Work Report of the Preparatory Committee
    [Our Newspaper] The Beijing Education Workers' Union Representative Congress officially opened on the 20th of this month. 462 representatives attended, along with over 100 leading figures from the education sector and representatives from various organizations as guests. After Chairman Qian Duansheng delivered the opening address, Minister of Education Ma Xulun spoke first. Minister Ma pointed out that the school unions had achieved considerable success and accumulated experience in the past six months. He hoped that they could ensure the implementation of the resolutions of the Higher Education Conference and the draft curriculum reform for institutions of higher education, closely cooperating with the education administration to run schools well. Subsequently, Weng Dujian, Director of the Beijing Municipal People's Government Bureau of Culture and Education, spoke, explaining that Beijing's education will enter a stage of consolidation and improvement. He raised several specific issues: (1) Improving teachers' political and professional levels, (2) Improving teaching effectiveness, (3) Strengthening leadership in private schools. Liu Zijiu, Minister of Culture and Education of the All-China Federation of Trade Unions, stated in his speech that over 90% of Beijing education workers have joined the union, and it has been basically organized, entering a stage of normal development. The union will transition from development to consolidation. The future task is to ensure the completion of teaching work on the one hand; on the other hand, attention should be paid to welfare to ensure the stability of members' lives. Xiao Ming, Vice Chairman of the Beijing Municipal Federation of Trade Unions, also pointed out in his speech that everyone should unite to ensure teaching. During the congress, elementary school and kindergarten children presented flowers, and grassroots unions presented flags. The congress adjourned at 1:00 PM in a warm atmosphere.
    The first congress was held on the afternoon of the 20th to hear the work summary report of the preparatory committee, the report of the proposal review committee on proposal review, the report on the organization of the procedural group, and the work report of the financial review committee. In the evening, representatives held group discussions on the reference list of candidates, etc.

### 教育工作者工会代表大会首次大会  听取筹委会报告工作

1950-07-22
第3版()
专栏：

　　教育工作者工会代表大会首次大会
    听取筹委会报告工作
    【本报讯】北京市教育工作者工会代表大会于本月二十日正式开幕，出席代表四六二人，并有教育界首长及各团体代表来宾百余人。主席钱端升致开幕词后，首由教育部长马叙伦讲话，马部长指出半年来各校工会获得了不少的成绩与经验，希望今后能保证高教会议决议和高等学校课程改革草案的实施，应与教育行政密切配合，搞好学校。继由北京市人民政府文教局局长翁独健讲话，他说明北京市的教育今后是巩固与提高的阶段，提出几个具体问题：（一）提高教师的政治业务水平，（二）提高教学效果，（三）加强领导私立学校。全国总工会文教部长刘子久致词中指出北京教育工作者参加工会的已达百分之九十以上，基本上已组织起来了，工作已进入正常发展阶段，工会亦将由发展而进入巩固，今后的任务一面保证教学工作的完成；另一面则应注意福利，谋会员生活的安定。市总工会副主席萧明致词也指出大家团结，保证教学。在大会进行中有小学及幼稚园儿童献花，基层工会献旗等，大会于下午一时在热烈气氛中休会。
    二十日下午举行第一次大会，听取筹委会工作总结报告，提案审查委员会提案审查报告，议事组组织问题的报告，及经费审查委员会工作报告等，晚间代表分组讨论候选人参考名单等。
