Title: Over Five Hundred Korean Cadres Go to New Areas to Lead Land Reform
Author:
Date: 1950-07-16
Page: 4

　　Xinhua News Agency, Pyongyang, July 15th: In order to implement the order of the Standing Committee of the Supreme People's Assembly of Korea to carry out land reform in the liberated areas of southern Korea, so as to rapidly restore and develop the backward agricultural production in southern Korea, the Ministry of Agriculture and Forestry of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea has dispatched more than 500 leading cadres for land reform to work in the newly liberated areas in batches on the 12th and 13th.

### 朝鲜五百余干部赴新区领导土改

1950-07-16
第4版()
专栏：

　　朝鲜五百余干部赴新区领导土改
    【新华社平壤十五日电】为了按照朝鲜最高人民会议常任委员会的命令在朝鲜南部的解放地区实行土地改革，以便迅速恢复与发展朝鲜南部的落后的农业生产，朝鲜民主主义人民共和国农林部已派遣进行土地改革的领导干部五百余人，于十二、十三两日分批前往新解放的地区工作。
