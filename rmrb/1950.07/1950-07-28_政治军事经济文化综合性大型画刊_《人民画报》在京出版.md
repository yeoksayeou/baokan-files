Title: Large-scale Comprehensive Pictorial of Politics, Military Affairs, Economy, and Culture, "People's Pictorial," Published in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1950-07-28
Page: 1

　　Xinhua News Agency, July 25th. A large-scale pictorial reflecting the Chinese people's revolutionary struggles and construction endeavors, "People's Pictorial," was published in Beijing on July 20th. This pictorial is a comprehensive monthly publication, printed in color on octavo paper. Using pictures, artwork, literature, and other forms, it introduces important current developments in our country's politics, military affairs, economy, culture, and other aspects, as well as the international political situation. The inaugural special issue contains approximately 400 large and small pictures, with content covering: the proceedings of the Second Session of the First National Committee of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference; the battle scenes of the Chinese People's Liberation Army's liberation of Hainan Island and the Zhoushan Islands; the enthusiastic nationwide peace signature campaign; and the great ranks of the working people, the ranks of youth, and the happy lives of children during the first Labor Day, Youth Day, and Children's Day after the founding of the People's Republic of China. In addition, the inaugural issue also features photos of workers actively producing in factories such as Anshan, Shijingshan, and Tianjin, as well as the cultural activities of workers and youth in Beijing.

### 政治军事经济文化综合性大型画刊  《人民画报》在京出版

1950-07-28
第1版()
专栏：

　　政治军事经济文化综合性大型画刊
    《人民画报》在京出版
    【新华社二十五日讯】反映中国人民的革命斗争和建设事业的大型画刊《人民画报》，已于七月二十日在北京出版。该画报为综合性的月刊，八开本彩色精印。用图片、美术作品、文艺等形式，介绍我国当前政治、军事、经济、文化等各方面重要的动态和国际政治形势。创刊特大号中有大小图片约四百幅，内容为：中国人民政治协商会议第一届全国委员会第二次会议的经过；中国人民解放军解放海南岛、舟山群岛两战役的战斗场面；全国各地热烈展开的和平签名运动；中华人民共和国开国后第一个劳动节、青年节、儿童节中伟大的劳动人民行列、青年行列和幸福的儿童生活。此外，创刊号并刊载了鞍山、石景山、天津等各工厂工人积极生产和北京市工人、青年文化动态等照片。
