Title: Philippine Liberation Army Launches Major Offensive, Occupies Pinapagan North of Manila
Author:
Date: 1950-07-24
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency, July 22nd—According to a United Press report from Manila on the 18th, the Philippine People's Liberation Army launched its largest attack in recent times on the 16th, striking in the four provinces of Nueva Misamis, Nueva Ecija, Pampanga, and Pangasinan, as well as other areas. A unit of the Liberation Army also occupied Pinapagan City (north of Manila) in Nueva Misamis Province. A week earlier, the Philippine government forces had launched a pincer offensive against the People's Liberation Army in the mountains of Pangasinan Province, but it had failed.

According to an Agence France-Presse report on the 19th, the Pampanga Province People's Liberation Army newspaper, "The Liberator," prominently published news of the Korean People's Army's victory.

### 菲解放军大举出击  占领马尼拉以北品纳帕干

1950-07-24
第4版()
专栏：

　　菲解放军大举出击
    占领马尼拉以北品纳帕干
    【新华社二十二日讯】据合众社马尼拉十八日讯：菲律宾人民解放军于十六日发动了最近以来规模最大的一次攻击，出击新米斯皆耶、新怡西夏、邦邦牙、描东岸等四省及其他地区。解放军一部并占领了新米斯皆耶省的品纳帕干城（马尼拉以北）。在一星期之前，菲政府军曾向描东岸省山地中的人民解放军发动钳形攻势，但已告失败。
    另据法新社十九日讯：邦邦牙省的人民解放军的报纸《解放者》，以显著地位刊载朝鲜人民军胜利的消息。
