Title: Over Four Million People of All Ethnic Groups and Circles in Southwest and Northwest Sign Peace Petition
Author:
Date: 1950-07-21
Page: 1
　　
　　(Xinhua News Agency) According to incomplete statistics as of the beginning of this month, 2,838,000 people in the Southwest Administrative Region have signed the Peace Declaration. This includes 2,529,000 people in Chongqing, Western Sichuan (including Chengdu), Southern Sichuan, Eastern Sichuan, and Northern Sichuan Administrative Regions, 180,000 people in Kunming, 115,000 people in Guiyang, and 14,000 people in units directly under the Southwest Military Region.

　　Furthermore, according to recent incomplete statistics, 1,283,952 people of all ethnic groups and circles in the Northwest have signed the Peace Declaration. At present, the wave of peace signatures in various regions is spreading extensively from cities to rural areas and ethnic minority regions.

### 西南西北各族各界人民和平签名者达四百多万

1950-07-21
第1版()
专栏：

　　西南西北各族各界人民和平签名者达四百多万
    【本报讯】据新华社讯：据截至本月上旬止的不完全统计：西南大行政区已有二百八十三万八千人在和平宣言上签名。其中包括重庆市，川西（成都市在内）、川南、川东及川北行政区共二百五十二万九千人，昆明市十八万人，贵阳市十一万五千人，西南军区直属队一万四千人。
    又，据最近不完全的统计，西北各族各界人民已有一百二十八万三千九百五十二人在和平宣言上签名。目前各地和平签名的浪潮，正由城市向乡村和少数民族地区广泛地展开。
