Title: Soong Ching-ling, Vice Chairman, Sends Telegram to Chairman of the American Civil Rights Congress Protesting U.S. Imperialist Imprisonment of Progressive Leaders
Author:
Date: 1950-07-01
Page: 4

　　Xinhua News Agency, Shanghai, 30th: Soong Ching-ling, Vice Chairman of the Central People's Government, telegraphed William Patterson, Chairman of the American Civil Rights Congress, holding a meeting in New York on the 28th, stating: "The Chinese people and millions of peace fighters around the world protest the American reactionaries' imprisonment of American progressive leaders, destruction of human rights, and promotion of war policies."

### 宋庆龄副主席电美民权保障大会主席  抗议美帝囚禁进步领袖

1950-07-01
第4版()
专栏：

　　宋庆龄副主席
    电美民权保障大会主席  抗议美帝囚禁进步领袖
    【新华社上海三十日电】中央人民政府宋庆龄副主席，二十八日致电于同在纽约举行集会的美国民权保障大会主席威廉·巴特生称：“中国人民与全世界千百万和平战士，对于美国反动派囚禁美国进步领袖、摧残人权，并推行战争政策，表示抗议。”
