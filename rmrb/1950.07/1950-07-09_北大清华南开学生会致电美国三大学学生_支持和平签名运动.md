Title: Peking University, Tsinghua University, and Nankai University Student Unions Send Telegrams to Students at Three American Universities in Support of the Peace Petition Movement
Author: 
Date: 1950-07-09
Page: 3

　　Peking University, Tsinghua University, and Nankai University Student Unions Send Telegrams to Students at Three American Universities in Support of the Peace Petition Movement
    After learning that students at Edinburgh, Queens, and Sheffield Universities in the United Kingdom were launching a peace petition movement, the Peking University Student Union, together with the Tsinghua University and Nankai University Student Unions, sent them a telegram on June 25th to express their support. The telegram read: "To the students of Edinburgh, Queens, and Sheffield Universities, forwarded by the International Student Union: We extend our respect to the righteous students participating in the movement to defend world peace! Let us advance shoulder to shoulder on the road of safeguarding and developing culture and science, and striving for a bright future!"
    (Peking University News Group)

### 北大清华南开学生会致电美国三大学学生  支持和平签名运动

1950-07-09
第3版()
专栏：

　　北大清华南开学生会致电美国三大学学生
    支持和平签名运动
    北京大学学生会于获悉英国爱丁堡、坤斯、希佛尔德三大学同学正在展开和平签名运动后，于六月二十五日与清华大学、南开大学学生会联名致电他们表示支持。电文如下：“国际学联转爱丁堡、坤斯、希佛尔德大学的同学们：谨向参加保卫世界和平运动的正义学生们致敬！让我们在维护与发展文化科学、争取美好将来的道路上并肩前进！”
    （北大通讯组）
