Title: I Hate the New War
Author: Xǔ Guǎngpíng
Date: 1950-07-03
Page: 5

　　I Hate the New War
　　许广平

　　I hate the new war instigated by the U.S. imperialists and their attempts to threaten world peace with atomic weapons. I am willing to sign the declaration to defend world peace, so as to use collective power to defend the permanent peace of the world.

### 我憎恨新战争
许广平
1950-07-03
第5版()
专栏：

　　我憎恨新战争
    许广平
    我憎恨那以美帝为首的阴谋挑拨新战争，和企图以原子武器威胁世界的和平。我愿签名在保卫世界和平的宣言上，以便用集体的力量来保卫世界永久的和平。
