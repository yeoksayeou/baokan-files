Title: All-China Federation of Trade Unions Issues Notice: Donation Drive to Aid Unemployed Workers Ends; National Donations Reach Over 28 Billion

Author:

Date: 1950-07-10
Page: 2

All-China Federation of Trade Unions Issues Notice
    Donation Drive to Aid Unemployed Workers Ends
    National Donations Reach Over 28 Billion
    [Reported by this newspaper] The All-China Federation of Trade Unions issued a notice on the 7th regarding the conclusion of the donation drive to aid unemployed workers. The full text is as follows:

    On April 20th, the Federation issued a letter to all male and female workers and staff across the country, calling on all employed workers and staff to donate one day's wages to aid unemployed workers in Shanghai and across the country. This call, once issued, immediately received enthusiastic responses from workers and staff in factories, enterprises, organizations, and schools across the country, as well as commanders and soldiers of the People's Liberation Army. Everyone actively donated, fully demonstrating the fraternal love of the working class and the deep sympathy of people from all walks of life towards unemployed workers. As of July 5th, according to a report from the Head Office of the People's Bank of China, the total amount of donations received from across the country has reached RMB 28,792,233,617.10. Among them, East China accounted for RMB 20,126,409,887.60 (including approximately 6.2 billion donated from the Northeast); Central South accounted for RMB 2,019,446,719.35; North China accounted for RMB 4,377,666,422.69; Northwest accounted for RMB 360,554,194; Southwest accounted for RMB 466,156,393.46; and Shandong Province accounted for RMB 1,442,000,000.

    It is now decided, in accordance with Article 5 of the Federation's May 6th notice, to allocate the aforementioned donations to the Shanghai Federation of Trade Unions, the East China Office of the All-China Federation of Trade Unions, the Southwest Office, the Central South Federation of Trade Unions, the Northwest Federation of Trade Unions, the Shandong Provincial Federation of Trade Unions, the Beijing and Tianjin Municipal Federations of Trade Unions directly under the central government, and the Federation of Trade Unions of the five provinces in North China for the purpose of aiding unemployed workers. Local trade union organizations must report the detailed situation of the handling to the Federation before the end of August, and must also be responsible for publishing the income and expenditure of donations in the local newspapers.

    Regarding the work of donation drives to aid unemployed workers in various localities, please cease all such activities from today onwards. In the future, regarding work related to aiding unemployed workers, it is hoped that all localities will follow the "Instructions on Aiding Unemployed Workers" promulgated by the Government Administration Council of the Central People's Government on June 17th.

### 全国总工会发出通知  救济失业工人捐款结束  全国捐款达二百八十余亿

1950-07-10
第2版()
专栏：

　　全国总工会发出通知
    救济失业工人捐款结束       
    全国捐款达二百八十余亿
    【本报讯】中华全国总工会于七日发出关于结束救济失业工人捐款的通知，全文如下：
    本会于四月二十日发出告全国男女工人、职员书，号召全体在业工人和职员，捐助一天工资，救济上海及全国各地失业工人。这一号召发布以后，立即得到了全国各地工厂、企业、机关团体、学校的工人、职员以及人民解放军各部队指战员们的热烈响应。大家踊跃捐输，充分表现了工人阶级的兄弟友爱与各界人士对失业工人的深厚同情。截至七月五日止，根据中国人民银行总行报告，收到全国各地捐款总额已达人民币二八、七九二、二三三、六一七·一○元。其中华东区为二○、一二六、四○九、八八七·六○元（内包括东北捐款约六十二亿）；中南区为二、○一九、四四六、七一九·三五元；华北区为四、三七七、六六六、四二二·六九元；西北区为三六○、五五四、一九四元；西南区为四六六、一五六、三九三·四六元；山东省为一、四四二、○○○、○○○元。
    现在决定根据本会五月六日通知第五条之规定，将上列捐款分别拨交上海总工会、全国总工会华东办事处、西南办事处、中南总工会、西北总工会、山东省总工会、京津两直属市总工会以及华北五省总工会作为救济失业工人之用。各地工会组织须将办理的详细情形于八月底以前报告本会，同时必须负责将捐款收支情况在当地报上公布。
    关于各地救济失业工人捐款工作，请自即日起一律停止进行。今后有关救济失业工人的工作，希望各地遵照中央人民政府政务院六月十七日所颁布的“关于救济失业工人的指示”办理。
