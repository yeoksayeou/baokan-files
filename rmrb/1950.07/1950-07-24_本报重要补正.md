Title: Important Correction from This Newspaper
Author:
Date: 1950-07-24
Page: 1

　　Important Correction from This Newspaper
　　In the sixth line of the second paragraph of this newspaper's July 21st, first edition editorial, "Seriously Prepare and Establish People's Courts," the phrase "consolidate the people's democratic dictatorship" was missing "and smoothly complete the land reform." This correction is hereby issued.
