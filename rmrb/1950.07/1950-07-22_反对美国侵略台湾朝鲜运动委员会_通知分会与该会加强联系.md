Title: Committee to Oppose American Aggression Against Taiwan and Korea Notifies Branches to Strengthen Contact with the Committee
Author:
Date: 1950-07-22
Page: 1

　　The Committee to Oppose American Aggression Against Taiwan and Korea
    Notifies Branches to Strengthen Contact with the Committee
    [This newspaper dispatch] The Chinese People's Committee to Oppose American Aggression Against Taiwan and Korea issued a notice to branch committees in various locations on July 21st, the full text of which is as follows:
    "In order to respond to the call of the World Federation of Trade Unions, support the Korean people, and oppose American aggression, this Committee launched a nationwide movement to oppose American aggression against Taiwan and Korea on the 17th of this month. Soon after the launch, major administrative regions and large cities responded one after another, actively展开ing actions. People's organizations in various locations have already organized or are in the process of organizing branch committees to lead the anti-American aggression movement in their respective regions. In order to strengthen the connection between the branch committees and this Committee, better exchange work experiences in various places, and promote international propaganda, please send the work situation and various related propaganda materials to this Committee at any time. This is hereby notified."

### 反对美国侵略台湾朝鲜运动委员会  通知分会与该会加强联系

1950-07-22
第1版()
专栏：

　　反对美国侵略台湾朝鲜运动委员会
    通知分会与该会加强联系
    【本报讯】中国人民反对美国侵略台湾朝鲜运动委员会七月二十一日向各地分委员会发出通知，全文如下：
    “本会为了响应世界工联号召，支援朝鲜人民，反对美国侵略，于本月十七日起发动全国人民反对美国侵略台湾、朝鲜的运动。发动后不久，各大行政区、大城市纷纷响应，积极展开行动。各地人民团体已经或正在组织分委员会，领导各该地区的反美侵略的运动。为了加强各分会与本会的联系，更好地交流各地工作经验，并推广国际宣传起见，请各地分会把工作情况及各种有关宣传品随时送至本会为要。特此通知。”
