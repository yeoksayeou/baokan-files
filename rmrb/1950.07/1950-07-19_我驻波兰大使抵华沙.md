Title: Our Ambassador to Poland Arrives in Warsaw
Author:
Date: 1950-07-19
Page: 1

　　Our Ambassador to Poland Arrives in Warsaw
    [Xinhua News Agency, 18th] Warsaw, July 17th, Polish News Agency: General Peng Mingzhi, Ambassador of the People's Republic of China to Poland, arrived in Warsaw on the 17th.

### 我驻波兰大使抵华沙

1950-07-19
第1版()
专栏：

　　我驻波兰大使抵华沙
    【新华社十八日讯】波兰通讯社华沙十七日讯：中华人民共和国驻波兰大使彭明治将军，已于十七日抵达华沙。
