Title: Sino-British Friendship Association Sends "July 7th" Telegram to Chairman Mao
Author: 
Date: 1950-07-08
Page: 1

　　Xinhua News Agency, Prague, July 7th: The Sino-British Friendship Association sent a telegram to Chairman Mao Zedong on the occasion of the July 7th anniversary. The text of the telegram is as follows:
　　Chinese friends remember the Japanese attack on your country on July 7, 1937. They firmly believe that despite various difficulties and American aggression, the Chinese people, under your wise leadership, will advance toward victory.
　　Deribang of the Sino-British Friendship Association

### 英中友好协会“七七”致电毛主席

1950-07-08
第1版()
专栏：

　　英中友好协会“七七”致电毛主席
    【新华社布拉格七日电】英中友好协会值此七七纪念日之际致电毛泽东主席。电文如下：
    中国的友人们记得一九三七年七月七日日本对您的国家的进攻，他们深信：尽管有各种困难及美国的侵略，中国人民在您的英明领导下将向胜利迈进。
    英中友好协会德里邦
