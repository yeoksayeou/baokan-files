Title: Establish Hygiene and Epidemic Prevention Stations at Major Railway Stations to Prevent Disease Transmission and Protect Passengers' Health
Author:
Date: 1950-07-11
Page: 3

Xinhua News Agency, August 8th: To prevent the occurrence and spread of epidemics, and to protect the health and safety of passengers, railway workers, and their families, the Ministry of Railways of the Central People's Government recently issued a circular to railways in the North and South to establish railway hygiene and epidemic prevention stations at major stations along the lines. The tasks of the hygiene and epidemic prevention stations mainly concern the planning and implementation of epidemic prevention work within their jurisdiction, the prevention and eradication of acute and chronic infectious diseases, traffic quarantine, guidance on maternal and child health, and testing of food hygiene, among other matters. The locations for establishing hygiene and epidemic prevention stations include Beijing, Tianjin, Zhangjiakou, Taiyuan, Shijiazhuang, Zhengzhou, Chang'an, Hankou, Jinan, Xuzhou, Shanghai, Nanjing, Hangzhou, Zhuzhou, Liuzhou, Guangzhou, and sixteen other places.

### 防止疾病传染保护旅客健康  各铁路要站设卫生防疫站

1950-07-11
第3版()
专栏：

　　防止疾病传染保护旅客健康
    各铁路要站设卫生防疫站
    【新华社八日讯】为防止疫病发生与传染蔓延，以保护旅客及铁路职工和家属的健康安全起见，中央人民政府铁道部已于日前通令北方南方各铁路在沿线主要大车站设置铁路卫生防疫站。卫生防疫站的任务主要是关于本管区内防疫业务的计划实施，急、慢性传染疫病的预防扑灭，交通检疫，妇婴卫生的指导，以及饮食卫生的化验等事项。设立卫生防疫站的地点有北京、天津、张家口、太原、石家庄、郑州、长安、汉口、济南、徐州、上海、南京、杭州、株州、柳州、广州等十六处。
