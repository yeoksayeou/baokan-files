Title: Korean Student Delegation Departs to Attend the Second World Student Congress
Author:
Date: 1950-07-29
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Pyongyang, July 28th. The Korean delegation to the Second World Student Congress, scheduled to be held in Prague, led by Kang Buk, Director of the Student Affairs Department of the Central Committee of the North Korean Democratic Youth League, departed from Pyongyang for Prague on the 24th. The Korean student delegation carried a large number of bomb fragments from the American planes' bombing of peaceful Korean cities, rural areas, and fishing villages, as well as various war trophies seized by the People's Army in the war of liberation.

### 出席二届世界学生大会  朝鲜学生代表团出发

1950-07-29
第4版()
专栏：

　　出席二届世界学生大会
    朝鲜学生代表团出发
    【新华社平壤二十八日电】参加即将在布拉格召开的第二届世界学生大会的朝鲜代表团一行，在团长北朝鲜民主青年同盟中央委员会学生工作部部长康北的率领下，已于二十四日自平壤出发赴布拉格。朝鲜学生代表团携带了美机炸射朝鲜和平城市与农村、渔村的大批炸弹破片，与人民军在解放战争中所缴获的各种战利品。
