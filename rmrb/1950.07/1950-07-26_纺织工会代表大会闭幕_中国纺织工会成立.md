Title: Textile Workers' Congress Concludes; China Textile Trade Union Established
Author:
Date: 1950-07-26
Page: 2

　　Textile Workers' Congress Concludes
    China Textile Trade Union Established
    Clearly recognized that: organizing production competitions and improving grassroots organizations are the basic methods and central links for trade unions to do a good job in production.
    [Xinhua News Agency, July 24th] The China Textile Trade Union, with 412,558 members, has been formally established. This was the result of the first National Congress of the China Textile Trade Union held in Beijing from July 15th to 24th. The main agenda of the congress was to discuss and approve the report by Chen Shaomin, Director of the Preparatory Committee of the China Textile Trade Union, on the basic summary of the work of the China Textile Trade Union in the past six months and the future guidelines and tasks, as well as the draft outline of the collective contract, and the constitution of the China Textile Trade Union; at the same time, in accordance with the constitution, the congress elected the members of the National Committee of the China Textile Trade Union, including Chen Shaomin and 49 others, alternate members including Shi Xiaomei and 16 others, members of the Finance Review Committee including Lei Binglin and 7 others, and 2 alternate members. Two hundred and fourteen representatives from thirty regional trade unions attended this meeting.
    In addition to exchanging experiences of grassroots trade union organizations leading the masses in production in various regions, trade union representatives from various regions also conducted work reviews at the meeting with a focus, based on the spirit of rectification. The main gains of the ten-day meeting were: the attending representatives more clearly recognized that organizing production competitions is the basic method for trade union organizations to lead the masses in production work. And improving the grassroots trade union organizations is the basic link to ensure the success of production work.
    The congress also heard reports from the Ministry of Textile Industry of the Central People's Government on the work and future tasks in the past eight months, a report from Liu Zijiu, Minister of the Culture and Education Department of the All-China Federation of Trade Unions, on the Trade Union Law, and a report from Deng Yingchao, Vice-Chairman of the All-China Democratic Women's Federation, on the Marriage Law.
    The congress also passed a telegram supporting the Korean people's war of liberation, and called on textile workers across the country to actively participate in the movement against the American imperialist aggression against Taiwan and Korea, write letters of sympathy, and sew comfort bags to support the Korean People's Army, which is heroically fighting against American imperialism.

### 纺织工会代表大会闭幕  中国纺织工会成立

1950-07-26
第2版()
专栏：

　　纺织工会代表大会闭幕
    中国纺织工会成立
    明确认识到：组织生产竞赛与健全基层组织，是工会做好生产工作的基本方法与中心环节。
    【新华社二十四日讯】拥有会员四一二五五八人的中国纺织工会已正式成立。这是七月十五日至二十四日在北京举行的中国纺织工会第一届全国代表大会所产生的。代表大会的主要议程是讨论并通过了中国纺织工会筹委会主任陈少敏关于半年来中国纺织工会工作的基本总结和今后的方针任务的报告，以及集体合同纲要草案，和中国纺织工会章程；同时根据会章选出了中国纺织工会全国委员会的委员陈少敏等四十九人，候补委员施小妹等十六人，经费审查委员会委员雷炳林等七人，候补委员二人。出席这次会议的有三十个地区工会代表二一四人。
    大会除交流了各地工会基层组织领导群众生产的经验外，各地工会代表还根据整风的精神，在会上有重点地进行了工作检讨。十天会议的主要收获是：到会代表更明确认识到：组织生产竞赛是工会组织领导群众生产工作的基本方法。而健全工会基层组织，乃是保证作好生产工作的基本环节。
    大会还听取了中央人民政府纺织工业部关于八个月来的工作及今后任务的报告和中华全国总工会文教部刘子久部长关于工会法的报告、中华全国民主妇女联合会副主席邓颖超关于婚姻法的报告。
    大会还通过了支援朝鲜人民解放战争的通电，并号召全国纺织工人积极参加反对美帝侵略台湾朝鲜运动，写慰问信，缝慰问袋，以支援正在向美帝国主义进行英勇斗争的朝鲜人民军。
