Title: Eight American Planes Shot Down in Two Days; Air Force Destroys Over Sixty Enemy Vehicles in One Strike
Author:
Date: 1950-07-21
Page: 1

　　Xinhua News Agency, Pyongyang, 20th: The General Headquarters of the Korean People's Democratic Republic announced in a war bulletin on the evening of the 19th that on the front lines, the People's Army Air Force launched fierce attacks on American aircraft and ground forces, shooting down five American planes on the 18th. At 4:30 PM on the 18th, a detachment of the People's Army Air Force bombed over a hundred American trucks fleeing towards Daegu, destroying over sixty vehicles. An anti-aircraft artillery detachment of the People's Army fired at a group of American planes attacking the Hungnam area on the 18th, shooting down one American plane into the sea off Hungnam Port.
    Xinhua News Agency, Pyongyang, 20th: The General Headquarters of the Korean People's Democratic Republic reported on the morning of the 20th that the People's Army units are breaking through the stubborn resistance of American ground forces on all fronts and continuing their southward advance. On the 19th, the People's Army Air Force, undeterred by the numerical superiority of American aircraft, launched fierce attacks on enemy rear transportation and enemy personnel, inflicting heavy casualties on the enemy and shooting down two enemy planes.
    Guerrilla forces in the areas of Kochang and Hamyang in Gyeongsangnam-do Province and Boseong in Jeollanam-do Province are actively launching operations in response to the victorious offensive of the People's Army.

### 两天击落美机八架  空军一次毁敌汽车六十多辆

1950-07-21
第1版()
专栏：

　　两天击落美机八架
    空军一次毁敌汽车六十多辆
    【新华社平壤二十日电】朝鲜民主主义人民共和国人民军总司令部十九日晚发表战报称：在前线，人民军空军对美军机群和地面部队进行猛烈攻击，于十八日击落美机五架。十八日午后四时三十分，人民军空军某区分队对逃往大邱方面的美军载重汽车一百余辆，加以轰炸，结果击毁汽车六十余辆。人民军高射炮区分队，于十八日射击侵袭兴南地区的美军机群，将美机一架击落在兴南港的海里。
    【新华社平壤二十日电】朝鲜民主主义人民共和国人民军总司令部二十日晨报道：人民军部队在各战线上击破美军地面部队的顽抗。继续南进。十九日，人民军空军不顾数量上占优势的美机的顽强抵抗。对敌后交通运输和敌人的有生力量予以猛烈袭击。予敌重创，并击落敌机两架。
    庆尚南道居昌、咸阳一带和全罗南道宝城一带的游击队，响应人民军的胜利进攻，正积极展开活动。
