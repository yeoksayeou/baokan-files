Title: Changes in Participating Institutions and Examination Areas for the North China Higher Education Joint Enrollment

Author:

Date: 1950-07-10
Page: 6

　　Changes in Participating Institutions and Examination Areas for the North China Higher Education Joint Enrollment
    The North China Higher Education Joint Enrollment Committee for 1950 has made slight changes regarding participating institutions and the setup of examination areas. As for participating institutions, Fu Jen Catholic University has been added, and Hebei Medical College has withdrawn. Regarding examination areas, as instructed by the Central Ministry of Education, and to accommodate the needs of various regions and the distribution of candidates, two examination areas have been added in Fuzhou and Changsha, and the originally planned Jinan examination area has been cancelled; a total of fourteen examination areas are set up in Beijing, Tianjin, Nanjing, Shanghai, Wuhan, Guangzhou, Changsha, Chongqing, Fuzhou, Xi'an, Taiyuan, Kaifeng, Tangshan, and Jiaozuo. Enrollment teams from each area will be heading to their respective regions in the coming days to carry out enrollment work. In addition, the Enrollment Committee has made decisions regarding enrollment departments, application preferences, registration procedures, and application methods for the New Student People's Scholarship, and has already issued a joint enrollment prospectus.

### 华北区高等学校联合招生参加单位及考区有变动

1950-07-10
第6版()
专栏：

　　华北区高等学校联合招生参加单位及考区有变动
    华北区高等学校一九五○年度联合招生委员会关于参加单位及考区设置略有变更。参加单位，增加了辅仁大学，退出了河北医学院。考区经中央教育部指示，为照顾各地需要及考生分布情况，增设福州、长沙两个考区，取消原定的济南考区；计设北京、天津、南京、上海、武汉、广州、长沙、重庆、福州、西安、太原、开封、唐山、焦作等十四个考区。各区招生小组，日内即将前往各该地区进行招生工作。另外，关于招生院系、投考志愿、报名手续及新生人民助学金申请办法等，招生委员会均作出决定，并已印发联合招生简章。
