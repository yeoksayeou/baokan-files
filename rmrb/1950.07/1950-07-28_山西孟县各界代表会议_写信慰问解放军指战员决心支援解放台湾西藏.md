Title: Representative Conference of All Circles in Meng County, Shanxi Province: Writing Letters to Comfort the Commanders and Fighters of the People's Liberation Army, Resolving to Support the Liberation of Taiwan and Tibet
Author:
Date: 1950-07-28
Page: 2

　　Shanxi Meng County Representative Conference of All Circles
    Writing Letters to Comfort the Commanders and Fighters of the People's Liberation Army, Resolving to Support the Liberation of Taiwan and Tibet
    [This Newspaper News] The third session of the People's Representatives Conference of All Circles in Meng County, Shanxi Province, was held from June 15th to 18th. During the conference, all representatives wrote letters to express their solicitude to all commanders and fighters of the People's Liberation Army. The letters conveyed cordial greetings and paid lofty respect to all commanders and fighters. The letters also stated: "Now Meng County is entering the summer work season. The people of our entire county will carry forward the glorious tradition of cherishing the people's army during the war years, continue to do a good job in providing preferential treatment to military families and helping them with farming, earnestly solve the difficulties of the families of revolutionary martyrs and soldiers throughout the county, strive to support the front lines, and fight for the early liberation of Taiwan and Tibet!"

### 山西孟县各界代表会议  写信慰问解放军指战员决心支援解放台湾西藏

1950-07-28
第2版()
专栏：

　　山西孟县各界代表会议
    写信慰问解放军指战员决心支援解放台湾西藏
    【本报讯】山西省孟县于六月十五至十八日举行第三届各界人民代表会议。会议中全体代表写信慰问人民解放军全体指战员。信上向全体指战员致亲切的慰问，和崇高的敬礼。并且说：“现在孟县整个步入夏季工作。我们全县人民，将发扬战争时期爱护人民军队的光荣传统，继续做好优军代耕工作，认真解决全县烈军属的困难，努力支援前线，为台湾西藏的早日解放而奋斗！”
