Title: Song of the Korean People's War
Author: Lü Jian
Date: 1950-07-09
Page: 4

People's Garden

　　Song of the Korean People's War
    Lü Jian
    Rising from pools of blood,
    Never to fall into them again!
    Breaking the chains of slavery,
    Never to be slaves again!

    Those who want to light a fire at our door,
    --Beware of being burned to death!
    Those who want to run rampant here,
    --That will not happen!

    The tranquil Daedong River,
    Whips up a huge, angry wave!
    The beautiful Mount Paektu,
    Raises a forest of knives and guns!

    From every window,
    Shoot bullets of hatred!
    From every section of the trench,
    Spew flames of anger!

    General Kim Il-sung,
    Calls for a general mobilization,
    The farms produce more grain,
    The factories rush to produce shells! --

    Thousands of troops march south,
    Red flags fly south,
    Down with American imperialism,
    Capture alive Syngman Rhee!

    Defend the peaceful homeland,
    Oppose fascist aggression,
    Korean people advance, advance!
    All Asian people advance, advance! ...
    July 1, 1950

### 朝鲜人民战歌
吕剑
1950-07-09
第4版()
专栏：人民园地

　　朝鲜人民战歌
    吕剑
    从血泊中站起来，
    不能再跌进血泊！
    斩断了奴隶命运，
    不能再做奴隶！
　
    想在我们门前点火，
    ——当心烧死！
    想在这儿横行霸道，
    ——那办不到！
　
    静静的大同江，
    掀起万丈怒浪！
    美丽的大白山，
    举起刀枪如林！
　
    从每一个窗口，
    射出仇恨的子弹！
    从每一段战壕，
    喷发愤怒的火焰！
　
    金日成将军，
    号召总动员，
    农庄多打粮食，
    工厂赶制炮弹！——
　
    千军万马向南，
    红旗飞卷向南，
    打倒美帝国主义，
    活捉李承晚！
　
    保卫和平的祖国，
    反对法西斯侵略，
    朝鲜人民前进、前进！
    全亚洲人民前进，前进！……
    一九五○年七月一日
