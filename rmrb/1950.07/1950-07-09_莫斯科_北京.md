　　Moscow - Beijing
    By Weiershini, translated by Zhu Ziqi
    This short song was one of the brilliant performances by many famous Soviet artists at the "Celebration of the Liberation of China Evening" held at the Soviet Art Workers' Building in Moscow this spring, singing for the friendship between the Chinese and Soviet people. The singer was the world-renowned composer and Stalin Prize winner, Morateli. The poet is the famous poet Weiershini.
    When the singer finished, more than a thousand male and female art workers in the audience applauded enthusiastically, and shouts of "Bis!" (meaning "encore!") and "You sing it right! You sing it well!" filled the venue.
    I
    Russians and Chinese -
    Eternal brothers!
    Let's consolidate the unity and friendship of the two great nations.
    Pure people stand shoulder to shoulder!
    Pure people sing and move forward!...
    Stalin and Mao Zedong are listening to us,
    Listening to our advancing footsteps!
    Listening to our advancing footsteps!

    Moscow - Beijing!
    Moscow - Beijing!
    Forward, forward, the people are advancing!
    For glorious labor,
    For lasting peace,
    We march forward under the great banner of freedom!
    II
    The rushing sound of the Yangtze River,
    Surges in the Volga River!
    The Chinese people gaze at the red lights of the Kremlin.
    We will never fear the thunder of any war,
    The will of the people is the most powerful thunder!
    The whole earth praises us,
    Singing for our victory!
    Singing for our victory!

    Moscow - Beijing!
    Moscow - Beijing!...
    Forward, forward, the people are advancing!
    For glorious labor,
    For lasting peace,
    We march forward under the great banner of freedom!
    III
    There is no friendship in the world,
    That can unite in love so tightly!
    Our ranks are filled with the joy of May.
    This marching force is the Soviet Union!
    This invincible force is the Soviet Union!
    That advancing side by side is the new China,
    The new People's Republic of China!
    The new People's Republic of China!

    Moscow - Beijing!
    Moscow - Beijing!...
    Forward, forward, the people are advancing!
    For glorious labor,
    For lasting peace,
    We march forward under the great banner of freedom!

### 莫斯科——北京
维尔什尼　朱子奇
1950-07-09
第5版()
专栏：

　　莫斯科——北京
    维尔什尼  作  朱子奇  译
    这首短歌，是今年春天，在莫斯科苏联艺术工作者大厦所举行的“庆祝中国解放晚会”上，许多苏联著名的艺术家们为中苏人民的友谊而歌唱的精采节目中之一。演唱者为世界闻名的大作曲家——斯大林文艺奖金的获得者莫拉特里。诗作者是名诗人维尔什尼。
    当歌者唱完的时候，全场一千多男女艺术工作者热烈鼓掌，“比——斯！”（再来一个的意思）和“你唱得对！你唱得好！”的欢呼充满会场。
    一
    俄罗斯人和中国人——
    永世的弟兄！
    更巩固起两大民族一致的友情。
    纯洁的人民并肩站起来！
    纯洁的人民欢唱向前进！……
    斯大林和毛泽东在听着我们，
    听我们前进的步伐声！
    听我们前进的步伐声！
　
    莫斯科——北京！
    莫斯科——北京！
    前进，前进，人民在前进！
    为光辉的劳动，
    为持久的和平，
    我们排列在自由的大旗下前进！
    二
    扬子江滔滔的流水声，
    在伏尔加河奔腾！
    中国人凝视着克里姆宫的红灯。
    我们决不恐惧任何战争的雷鸣，
    人民的意志就是最强大的雷鸣！
    整个地球在赞美我们，
    为我们的胜利歌颂！
    为我们的胜利歌颂！
　
    莫斯科——北京！
    莫斯科——北京！……
    前进，前进，人民在前进！
    为光辉的劳动，
    为持久的和平，
    我们排列在自由的大旗下前进！
    三
    世上没有什么友情，
    能团结相爱这般紧！
    在我们的行列中充满了五月的欢腾。
    这行走的大队是苏维埃联盟！
    这无敌的大队是苏维埃联盟！
    那并排前进的是新的中国，
    新的中华人民共和国！
    新的中华人民共和国！
　
    莫斯科——北京！
    莫斯科——北京！……
    前进，前进，人民在前进！
    为光辉的劳动，
    为持久的和平，
    我们排列在自由的大旗下前进！
