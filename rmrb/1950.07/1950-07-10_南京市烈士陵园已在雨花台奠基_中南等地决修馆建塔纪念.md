Title: Nanjing Martyrs' Cemetery Groundbreaking Ceremony Held at Yuhuatai; Central South and Other Regions Decide to Build Memorial Halls and Pagodas

Author:

Date: 1950-07-10
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, July 9th: The Nanjing Municipal Committee of the Chinese Communist Party and representatives from all sectors of the people held a groundbreaking ceremony for the Martyrs' Cemetery at Yuhuatai at 8:00 AM on the 2nd, to commemorate the martyrs who were killed by the Kuomintang reactionaries during the Chinese People's Revolutionary Movement. The groundbreaking ceremony was held in a solemn atmosphere. More than a hundred people attended, including members of the Nanjing Municipal Committee of the Chinese Communist Party, representatives of various democratic parties, relatives of the martyrs, and representatives from all sectors of the people. Tang Liang, Secretary of the Nanjing Municipal Committee of the Chinese Communist Party, presided over the groundbreaking ceremony and respectfully read the memorial oration from all sectors of the people of Nanjing. Workers and people from all walks of life in Nanjing also continuously went to the foundation of the tomb to offer wreaths and pay their respects. It is reported that over the past twenty-two years, the Kuomintang reactionaries killed countless Communist Party members and patriotic people at Yuhuatai, including outstanding leaders of the Chinese Communist Party such as Yun Daiying, Deng Zhongxia, Luo Dengxian, and former Secretary of the Nanjing Municipal Committee of the Chinese Communist Party, Sun Jinchuan, as well as patriotic democrats and Deng Yanda, the founder of the Chinese Third Party, and other martyrs.

Furthermore, it is reported that the Political Department of the Central South Military Region of the People's Liberation Army has decided to build a Martyrs' Memorial Hall in Wuhan to commemorate the martyrs who heroically sacrificed themselves for the liberation of the people of Central South. Preparations are currently underway, and various materials related to the martyrs are being collected. At the same time, the Political Department of the Guangdong Military Region has also decided to build a Liberation of Hainan Martyrs' Memorial Pagoda in Haikou City, the capital of Hainan Island.

### 南京市烈士陵园已在雨花台奠基   中南等地决修馆建塔纪念

1950-07-10
第1版()
专栏：

　　南京市烈士陵园已在雨花台奠基
    中南等地决修馆建塔纪念
    【新华社九日讯】中共南京市委会及各界人民代表于二日上午八时在雨花台举行烈士陵园奠基典礼，纪念中国人民革命运动中被国民党反动派杀害的烈士们。奠基典礼在庄严肃穆中举行。到有中共南京市委会、各民主党派、烈士家属及各界人民代表共百余人。中共南京市委会书记唐亮主持奠基，并恭读南京市各界人民的纪念词。南京市的工人和各界人民亦络绎至墓基前献花圈致敬。按二十二年来，国民党反动派在雨花台杀害无数共产党员及爱国人民，其中包括中国共产党优秀的领导者恽代英、邓中夏、罗登贤和中共前南京市委书记孙津川及爱国民主人士、中国第三党创始人邓演达等烈士。
    又讯：人民解放军中南军区政治部为纪念解放中南人民英勇牺牲的烈士，决定在武汉修建烈士纪念馆，现已着手筹备并搜集各种有关烈士材料。同时广东军区政治部亦决定在海南岛首府海口市修建解放海南烈士纪念塔。
