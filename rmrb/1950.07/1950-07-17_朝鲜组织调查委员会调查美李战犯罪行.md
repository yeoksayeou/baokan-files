Title: Korean Organization Establishes Investigation Committee to Investigate War Crimes by the U.S. and Syngman Rhee Clique
Author:
Date: 1950-07-17
Page: 4

　　Xinhua News Agency, Pyongyang, 16th: The Central Standing Committee of the Korean Democratic United Fatherland Front convened a meeting on the 14th, resolving to organize a Fatherland Front Investigation Committee to investigate the various bestial atrocities and criminal acts committed by U.S. imperialism and the Syngman Rhee traitorous clique since the beginning of the Korean civil war, as well as the various facts of the people suffering from them. The committee consists of 25 members, including Heon Heo, Lee Young, and Park Chung-hee, with Heon Heo as Chairman, and Lee Young and Park Chung-hee as Vice Chairmen. For the convenience of its work, the committee is divided into five investigation teams, which will begin work in the near future.

### 朝鲜组织调查委员会调查美李战犯罪行

1950-07-17
第4版()
专栏：

　　朝鲜组织调查委员会调查美李战犯罪行
    【新华社平壤十六日电】朝鲜祖国统一民主主义战线中央常务委员会于十四日召开会议，决定组织祖国战线调查委员会，调查自朝鲜内战开始以来，美帝国主义与李承晚卖国集团的各种野兽暴行与犯罪行为，及人民遭受其害的种种事实。委员会由许宪、李英、朴正爱等二十五人组成，许宪为委员长，李英、朴正爱为副委员长。该委员会为工作便利起见，划分为五个调查班，不日开始工作。
