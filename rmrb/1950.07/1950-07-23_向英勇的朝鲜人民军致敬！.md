Title: Salute to the Valiant Korean People's Army!
Author: Bian Zhilin, Shi Yi, Lü Jian, Yuan Jun
Date: 1950-07-23
Page: 5

　　Salute to the Valiant Korean People's Army!
    Salute to the Korean People

    Bian Zhilin
    Just to facilitate the cleanup of fascist remnants,
    The 38th parallel had to divide you at the waist.
    Your upper body is clean, with unprecedented vigor and evenness;
    But your lower body is wrapped in a fascist apron.
　
    The devilish hand not only continues to cripple half of you,
    But also turns up the apron to cover your eyes.
    You are very alert, blocking their shenanigans,
    And simply take the opportunity to untie the apron and break the apron string.
　
    Once the apron is torn, the devil's claws are revealed underneath,
    You are not afraid, determined to cut it off and eliminate it.
    The people of the whole world thank you for your bravery,
    We are cutting off the devil's claws separately, and we are not relaxing for a moment.
　
    To the Korean Brothers
    Shi Yi
    Shaking the world
    Thunderous footsteps
    Heroically marching to the front line
    You, Korean brothers
　
    Answering fists with fists
    Answering cannons with cannons
    A giant of steel stands up
    Facing the invading robbers
　
    Let the paper tiger
    Be punctured in front of you
    Let the enemies of mankind
    Break their sinful mud legs
    Let the ferocious jackals
    Break their greedy noses
    Yesterday, on the Chinese mainland
    Shamefully rolling out
    Today, on Korean soil
    Running away in a panic again
    Losing jeeps, losing helmets
    In your valiant pursuit
    You, Korean brothers
　
    Let the traitor who betrayed his compatriots
    Syngman Rhee
    This foul-smelling name
    Turn up his ass and run for his life
    Escape, escape all the way to the grave
    In your valiant pursuit
    You, Korean brothers
　
    You fight for the liberation of the motherland
    You fight for world peace
　
    Children holding flowers
    Girls singing in unison
    The whole world cheers for you
    The whole world expects you
    Victory is waiting for you
    You, the valiant
    Dear Korean brothers
　
    Song of the Asian People's War
    Lü Jian
    Valiant Asian people
    Singing songs of revenge,
    Towards the American aggressors,
    Loading shells into the artillery! --
    Don't allow them to invade our motherland,
    If they are presumptuous, open fire on them,
    The Pacific Ocean roars:
    "Drive the invaders out of Asia!"
    All Asian people advance victoriously!
    All the world's people advance victoriously!
　
    The victorious light of the Soviet Union and China,
    Illuminates the path of the Asian people.
    Look, in Korea, in Japan,
    In Vietnam, in the Philippines...
    The blood of the people converges into a raging tide,
    One struggle is connected to another.
    The century of slavery is coming to an end,
    The dawn of liberation is approaching the colonies!
    All Asian people advance victoriously!
    All the world's people advance victoriously!…
　
    Sent to the Korean Brothers
    Yuan Jun
    I am a soldier of Mao Zedong,
    Patrolling the border of the motherland at night.
    The south wind blows the waves of the Bohai Sea,
    Like my boiling heart.
　
    I look at the stars in the northeast corner.
    The starlight twinkles, emitting light.
    I think of the Korean People's Army under the starlight,
    Waging fierce war with the aggressors.
　
    I seem to see General Kim Il-sung,
    Commanding the warriors to advance!
    Truman, Syngman Rhee's tanks, planes, warships…
    Destroyed and sinking in the flames.
　
    I hear the news of your victory,
    My heart is filled with indescribable joy!
    I look at the newspapers and maps every day,
    Calculating the mileage of your advance.
　
    Salute to you!
    Valiant Korean People's Army brothers.
    We were once like you,
    Driving the invaders out of the country.
　
    I am a soldier of Mao Zedong,
    Patrolling the border of the motherland at night,
    I listen to the surging waves,
    It's like hearing the sound of your marching feet.

### 向英勇的朝鲜人民军致敬！
卞之琳　适夷　吕剑　远军
1950-07-23
第5版()
专栏：

　　向英勇的朝鲜人民军致敬！
    向朝鲜人民致敬
    卞之琳
    就为了便于收拾法西斯遗毒，
    三八线不得已把你们拦腰一束。
    上半身干净了，空前的血气和匀；
    下半身却被裹上了法西斯的围裙。
　
    魔手不但继续害你们半身不遂，
    而且翻上围裙来掩你们眼眉。
    你们很警醒，挡住他们的乱来，
    就索性趁势解围裙，挣断围裙带。
　
    围裙一撕裂，底下露出了魔爪，
    你们不怕，定要切断它，消灭它。
    全世界人民感谢你们的英勇，
    分头割魔掌，我们也一刻不放松。
　
    给朝鲜的兄弟们
    适夷
    震动了世界的
    雷一样的脚声
    英勇地走上前线
    你，朝鲜的兄弟们
　
    用拳头还答拳头
    用大炮还答大炮
    钢铁的巨人站起来
    面对着侵略的强盗
　
    让纸老虎
    在你的面前戳穿
    让人类的敌人
    跌断罪恶的泥腿
    让凶恶的豺狼
    碰破贪馋的鼻子
    昨天，在中国大陆上
    可耻地滚出去
    今天，在朝鲜土地上
    又狼狈地逃跑了
    丢了吉普，丢了钢盔
    在你的勇猛的追击里
    你，朝鲜的兄弟们
　
    让出卖同胞的贼
    李承晚
    这个发恶臭的名字
    撅起屁股没命的逃吧
    逃，一直逃进坟墓
    在你勇猛的追击里
    你，朝鲜的兄弟们
　
    你战斗，为祖国的解放
    你战斗，为世界的和平
　
    孩子们捧着鲜花
    少女们齐声歌唱
    全世界欢呼你
    全世界期待你
    胜利在等着你
    你，英勇的
    朝鲜的亲爱的兄弟
　
    亚洲人民战歌
    吕剑
    英勇的亚洲人民
    高唱复仇的战歌，
    朝着美国侵略者，
    把炮弹推进炮膛！——
    不准侵略我们祖国，
    他若放肆就向他开火，
    太平洋发出怒吼：
    “把侵略者赶出亚洲！”
    全亚洲人民胜利前进！
    全世界人民胜利前进！
　
    苏联和中国的胜利光芒，
    照耀着亚洲人民的道路。
    看，在朝鲜、在日本、
    在越南、在菲律宾……
    人民的鲜血汇成怒潮，
    一个斗争连着一个斗争。
    奴隶的世纪就要告终，
    殖民地临到解放的黎明！
    全亚洲人民胜利前进！
    全世界人民胜利前进！……
　
    寄给朝鲜的弟兄
    远军
    我是毛泽东的战士，
    星夜里巡防在祖国边境。
    南风吹着渤海的海浪，
    好像我这颗沸腾的心。
　
    我望着东北角上的繁星。
    星光闪烁，放着光明。
    我想着星光照射下的朝鲜人民军，
    正与侵略者进行着猛烈的战争。
　
    我仿佛看见金日成将军，
    指挥着勇士们前进！
    杜鲁门、李承晚的坦克、飞机、军舰……
    在火焰里毁灭、下沉。
　
    我听到你们胜利的消息，
    心里说不出的欢欣！
    我每天看着报纸、地图，
    计算着你们前进的里程。
　
    向你们致敬！
    英勇的朝鲜人民军弟兄。
    我们曾经和你们一样，
    把侵略者赶出国境。
　
    我是毛泽东的战士，
    星夜里巡防在祖国边境，
    我听着海浪奔腾，
    就好像听到你们进军的足音。
