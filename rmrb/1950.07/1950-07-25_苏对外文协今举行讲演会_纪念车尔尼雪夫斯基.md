Title: Soviet Foreign Cultural Association to Hold Lecture Meeting Commemorating Chernyshevsky
Author: Chen Yingnan
Date: 1950-07-25
Page: 6

　　Soviet Foreign Cultural Association to Hold Lecture Meeting
    Commemorating Chernyshevsky
    July 24, 1828, is the anniversary of the birth of H.F. Chernyshevsky, the great Russian revolutionary democrat, philosopher, writer, and political commentator. To commemorate the 122nd anniversary of the birth of Chernyshevsky, this outstanding Russian genius, scholar, and critic, the Beijing Sino-Soviet Friendship Association and the Soviet Foreign Cultural Association will hold a lecture meeting at 7:30 p.m. today (the 25th) at 19 Nanheyan Street. Ms. Yeleisheva will speak on "Chernyshevsky, the Great Russian Revolutionary Democrat." All those holding admission tickets and long-term "Introduction to the Soviet Union" lecture passes are welcome to attend.
    If branches of the Friendship Association at all levels wish to attend, they may obtain admission tickets by presenting a letter of introduction from their branch to the Association at 2 Yuhe Bridge.
                                         (Chen Yingnan)

### 苏对外文协今举行讲演会  纪念车尔尼雪夫斯基
陈应楠
1950-07-25
第6版()
专栏：

　　苏对外文协今举行讲演会
    纪念车尔尼雪夫斯基
    一八二八年七月二十四是俄国伟大的革命民主主义者、哲学家、作家及政论家Ｈ·Ｆ·车尔尼雪夫斯基诞生纪念日。北京市中苏友好协会与苏联对外文化协会为纪念这位杰出的俄国天才的学者和批评家——车尔尼雪夫斯基诞生一百二十二周年，定于今日（二十五日）下午七时半在南河沿十九号举行讲演会。请伊雷雪娃讲“俄国伟大的革命民主主义者——车尔尼雪夫斯基”。凡持有入场券和“苏联介绍”长期听讲证者，届时均可前往听讲。
    友协各级支会如有前往听讲者，可持支会介绍信，往御河桥二号该会索取入场券。
                                         （陈应楠）
