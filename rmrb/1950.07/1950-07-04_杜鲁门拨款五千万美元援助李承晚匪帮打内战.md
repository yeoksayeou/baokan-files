Title: Truman Allocates 50 Million US Dollars to Aid the Li Chengwan Bandit Gang in Fighting the Civil War
Author:
Date: 1950-07-04
Page: 4

　　Xinhua News Agency, Washington, 3rd—According to the US News Service, after ordering armed aggression against Korea, US President Truman signed a bill on June 29 stipulating the allocation of 50 million US dollars to support the Li Chengwan puppet government. This appropriation bill was quickly passed in Congress after the US government provoked the Korean War. Including the 60 million US dollars passed in February of this year, the US government has allocated 110 million US dollars in "economic assistance" to the Li Chengwan puppet government for preparing for and conducting the civil war in the 1950 fiscal year.

### 杜鲁门拨款五千万美元援助李承晚匪帮打内战

1950-07-04
第4版()
专栏：

　　杜鲁门拨款五千万美元援助李承晚匪帮打内战
    【新华社三日讯】据美国新闻处华盛顿消息：美国总统杜鲁门继下令武装侵略朝鲜之后，又于六月二十九日签署一项法案，规定拨款五千万美元支援李承晚傀儡政府。这一拨款法案是于美国政府挑起朝鲜战事之后，在国会中迅速通过的。加上今年二月间所通过的六千万美元，美国政府在一九五○年度内，拨给李承晚傀儡政府准备与进行内战的“经济援助”的款项，已达一亿一千万美元。
