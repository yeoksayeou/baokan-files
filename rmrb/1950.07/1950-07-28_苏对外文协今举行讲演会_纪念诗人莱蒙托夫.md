The Soviet Foreign Cultural Association to Hold Lecture Commemorating Poet Lermontov

　　July 27, 1841, marked the 109th anniversary of the death of the great Russian poet Mikhail Yuryevich Lermontov in a duel. To commemorate this gifted poet, the Beijing Sino-Soviet Friendship Association and the Soviet Society for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries will hold a lecture at 7:30 PM today (the 28th) at 19 Nanheyan Street. Savelyeva will lecture on "The Great Russian Poet Lermontov." Those holding admission tickets and long-term "Introduction to the Soviet Union" lecture passes are welcome to attend. (Chen Yingnan)

### 苏对外文协今举行讲演会  纪念诗人莱蒙托夫
陈应楠
1950-07-28
第6版()
专栏：

　　苏对外文协今举行讲演会
    纪念诗人莱蒙托夫
    一八四一年七二十七日是俄国伟大诗人Ｍ·Ю·莱蒙托夫决斗遇难一百零九年周年，北京市中苏友好协会与苏联对外文化协会为纪念这位天才的诗人，定于今日（二十八日）下午七时半在南河沿十九号举行讲演会，请萨维雅洛娃讲“俄国伟大诗人莱蒙托夫”。凡持有入场券和“苏联介绍”长期听讲证者，屈时均可前往听讲。（陈应楠）
