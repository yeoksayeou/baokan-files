Title: Beijing to Hold "Oppose American Imperialist Aggression" Movement Week from the 19th to the 25th

Author:

Date: 1950-07-17
Page: 1

　　The "Beijing People's Committee to Oppose American Aggression Against Taiwan and Korea" was established yesterday (the 16th). At the inaugural meeting, it was decided that Beijing would hold an "Oppose American Aggression Against Taiwan and Korea" Movement Week from the 19th to the 25th of this month.

　　The committee is jointly composed of the Beijing Federation of Trade Unions, the Municipal Farmers' Association, the Sino-Soviet Friendship Association of Beijing, the Municipal Committee of the Communist Youth League, the Municipal Youth Federation, the Municipal Women's Federation, the Municipal Student Federation, the Municipal Federation of Literary and Art Circles, and the Taiwan Democratic Self-Government League. Xiao Ming, Vice Chairman of the Municipal Federation of Trade Unions, was elected as the chairman of the committee.

　　The inaugural meeting also decided to convene a representative mass rally of ten thousand people on the 23rd of this month; during the Movement Week, factories, schools, organizations, and groups are called upon to organize large and small lectures through their respective systems, and to conduct propaganda in various forms in nearby streets or villages; all entertainment venues are mobilized to perform operas and films opposing American imperialist aggression; and a Korean photo exhibition will be held at the Labor People's Cultural Palace.

### 京市反对美帝侵略运动周定十九日至二十五日举行

1950-07-17
第1版()
专栏：

　　京市反对美帝侵略运动周定十九日至二十五日举行
    “北京市人民反对美国侵略台湾朝鲜运动委员会”于昨（十六）日成立，成立会上决定京市自本月十九日起到二十五日止举行反对美国侵略台湾、朝鲜运动周。
    该会系由京市总工会、市农会、市中苏友协、青年团市工委、市青联、市妇联、市学联、市文联及台湾民主自治同盟等单位联合组成，公推市总工会副主席萧明为主任委员。
    成立会上并决定于本月二十三日召开一个代表性的万人群众大会；在运动周内号召各工厂、学校、机关、团体通过各自的系统，组织大小型的讲演会，到附近街头或村镇以各种形式进行宣传；发动各游艺场所表演反对美帝侵略的戏曲及电影片；并在劳动人民文化宫举行朝鲜照片展览。
