Title: Mongolia Celebrates the 29th Anniversary of Liberation; Choibalsan Sends Telegram to Kim Il-sung Congratulating Victory
Author:
Date: 1950-07-14
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, July 13th: According to TASS, Ulaanbaatar, July 11th: The Presidium of the Little Khural, the Council of Ministers, and the Central Committee of the Mongolian People's Revolutionary party of the Mongolian People's Republic jointly held a celebration meeting in the National Theater of Ulaanbaatar to commemorate the 29th anniversary of the liberation of Mongolia. On the presidium of the meeting were Marshal Choibalsan, Chairman of the Council of Ministers of the Mongolian People's Republic, Tsendenbal, General Secretary of the Mongolian People's Revolutionary Party, Bumajin, Chairman of the Presidium of the Little Khural of the Republic, as well as Soviet Ambassador Prikhodov and Chinese Ambassador Ji Yatai.

Damba, Secretary of the Central Committee of the Mongolian People's Revolutionary Party, delivered a speech on commemorating the 29th anniversary of the liberation of the Mongolian people. Damba stated: With the fraternal assistance of the great Soviet Union and the great Stalin, the Mongolian People's Republic is marching towards socialism. One of the most important achievements of the Republic in the past year was the increase of 1,315,900 head of livestock. While livestock breeding—the foundation of the Mongolian People's Republic's economy—is developing, the work in other sectors is also gradually developing. Industry completed 101.6 percent of its plan for the first half of the year. Sixty-two point seven percent of adults nationwide are now literate.

Speaking of the Korean situation, Damba said: We, the freedom-loving Mongolian people, are watching the heroic liberation struggle of the Korean people with great sympathy. Large-scale rallies are being held in various parts of our country. At the meetings, the Mongolian people, like all progressive humanity, resolutely condemned the crimes of the American interventionists in Korea and unanimously demanded: "No interference in Korea!"

The participants sent a telegram to pay tribute to Stalin, the great leader of all working people and the best friend of the Mongolian people, and a telegram to pay tribute to Marshal Choibalsan.

Xinhua News Agency, Pyongyang, July 13th: Kim Il-sung, Chairman of the Council of Ministers of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea, sent a telegram to Choibalsan, Chairman of the Council of Ministers of the Mongolian People's Republic, on the 11th, congratulating the 29th anniversary of the establishment of the Mongolian People's Revolutionary Government. Chairman Choibalsan replied to General Kim Il-sung on the same day, expressing his gratitude; and said: "The Mongolian people are watching with deep sympathy and concern the heroic struggle of the Korean people to completely liberate their country from the Syngman Rhee gang and the American imperialists who are carrying out armed intervention against the freedom-seeking Korean people. The Mongolian people are convinced that the complete liberation of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea is not far off, and hope that the friendly Korean people will achieve great success and rapid victory in their heroic struggle."

### 蒙古庆祝解放二十九周年  乔巴山电金日成祝贺胜利

1950-07-14
第4版()
专栏：

　　蒙古庆祝解放二十九周年
    乔巴山电金日成祝贺胜利
    【新华社十三日讯】据塔斯社乌兰巴托尔十一日讯：蒙古人民共和国小呼拉尔主席团、部长会议与蒙古人民革命党中央委员会，为纪念蒙古民族解放二十九周年，在乌兰巴托尔国家剧院联合举行了庆祝大会。在大会主席团席位上，有蒙古人民共和国部长会议主席乔巴山元帅、蒙古人民革命党总书记泽登巴尔、共和国小呼拉尔主席团主席布马增等，以及苏联大使普里霍多夫和中华人民共和国大使吉雅泰。
    蒙古人民革命党中央委员会书记达姆巴在会上作了关于纪念蒙古人民解放二十九周年的演说。达姆巴称：蒙古人民共和国在伟大的苏联与伟大的斯大林的兄弟般的帮助下，正向社会主义迈进。共和国过去一年间最重要的成果之一，是增加了一百三十一万五千九百头牲畜。与牲畜饲养——蒙古人民共和国经济的基础——发展的同时，其他部门的工作也在逐步发展中。工业完成了今年上半年计划的百分之一百零一点六。全国有百分之六十二点七的成年人已能识字。
    在谈到朝鲜事件时，达姆巴说：我们爱好自由的蒙古人民，正以极大的同情来注视朝鲜人民的英勇的解放斗争。我国各地正在纷纷举行大规模的集会。会上，蒙古人民像所有的进步人类一样，坚决痛斥美国干涉主义者在朝鲜的罪行，并且一致要求：“不要干涉朝鲜！”
    与会者致电向一切劳动人民伟大的领袖和蒙古人民最好的朋友斯大林致敬，并电乔巴山元帅致敬。
    【新华社平壤十三日电】朝鲜民主主义人民共和国部长会议主席金日成，于十一日致电蒙古人民共和国部长会议主席乔巴山，祝贺蒙古人民革命政府成立二十九周年。乔巴山主席于同日复电金日成将军，表示谢意；并称：“蒙古人民正以深切的同情和关怀，注视着朝鲜人民为从李承晚匪帮与对争取自由的朝鲜人民进行武装干涉的美帝国主义者手中完全解放自己国家而进行的英勇斗争。蒙古人民深信，朝鲜民主主义人民共和国的完全解放已为期不远，并希望友好的朝鲜人民在其英勇的斗争中，获得巨大的成就和迅速的胜利。”
