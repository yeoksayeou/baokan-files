Title: Youth Art Evening to Be Held at Zhongshan Park Tonight
Author:
Date: 1950-07-21
Page: 6

　　The second Youth Art Evening, sponsored by the Beijing Summer Life Committee, will be held at Zhongshan Park at 8:00 PM on the 21st of this month. The program includes the Xinghai Chorus performing Nie Er's posthumous work, the "Yangtze River Storm" suite, the drama "Improve Unity and Promote Production," and the song and dance drama "Production Grand Song and Dance." If it rains, it will be postponed to the 23rd. Students from various schools who wish to participate can register at the student union and pay a performance fee of 500 yuan.
　　　　　　　　　　　　　　(Summer Committee Publicity and Communication Group)

### 青年文艺晚会今晚在中山公园举行

1950-07-21
第6版()
专栏：

　　青年文艺晚会今晚在中山公园举行
    北京市暑期生活委员会主办第二次青年文艺晚会，将于本月二十一日晚八时在中山公园举行。节目有星海大合唱团演出聂耳遗作《扬子江暴风雨》组曲，话剧《搞好团结闹生产》及歌舞剧《生产大歌舞》。如果下雨改至二十三日举行。各校同学若愿意参加，可至学生会登记，并缴演出费五百元。
                                       （暑期委会宣通组）
