Title: All-China Democratic Women's Federation Sends Telegram of Condolences to Korean Women
Author:
Date: 1950-07-25
Page: 1

　　All-China Democratic Women's Federation Sends Telegram of Condolences to Korean Women
    [Reported by this newspaper] Cai Chang, Chairman of the All-China Democratic Women's Federation, sent a telegram of condolences to Korean women on the 23rd, as follows: To Comrade Park Jung-ae, Chairman of the Korean Women's Union:
    First of all, on behalf of the women of China, I extend warm congratulations to all of your heroic people on the victories you have achieved in the war against the provocations of the Syngman Rhee puppet clique and the armed intervention of the American aggressors.
    At the same time, we denounce with the utmost anger the shameless aggression of the American ruling clique and the barbaric acts of bombing your peaceful residents.
    We, all the women of China, will certainly, together with peace-loving people all over the world, resolutely support your just war, and firmly believe that you will achieve the final victory.

### 中华全国民主妇联致电慰问朝鲜妇女

1950-07-25
第1版()
专栏：

　　中华全国民主妇联致电慰问朝鲜妇女
    【本报讯】中华全国民主妇女联合会主席蔡畅于二十三日向朝鲜妇女发出慰问电如下：朝鲜妇女联合会朴正爱主席：
    我首先代表中国的妇女向你们全体英勇的人民在反击李承晚傀儡集团的挑衅和打击美国侵略者的武装干涉的战争中所获得的胜利致热烈的祝贺。
    同时我们以最大的愤怒痛斥美国统治集团的无耻侵略和残酷轰炸你们和平居民的兽行。
    我们全中国的妇女必定和全世界爱好和平的人民一道，坚决地支援你们所进行的正义的战争，并坚信你们一定会取得最后的胜利。
