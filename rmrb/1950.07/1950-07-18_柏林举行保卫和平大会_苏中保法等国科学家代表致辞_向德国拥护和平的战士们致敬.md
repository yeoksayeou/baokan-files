Title: Berlin Holds Peace Conference; Scientists from the Soviet Union, China, Bulgaria, France, and Other Countries Deliver Speeches; Saluting the German Fighters for Peace

Author:

Date: 1950-07-18
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Berlin, 16th: TASS Berlin reports: On the evening of the 14th, a Peace Conference was held in the Walter Ulbricht Stadium in Berlin. Foreign delegations who came to Berlin to attend the 150th anniversary of the German Academy of Sciences also participated in the conference.

The stadium, with 80,000 seats, could not accommodate all the participants, so thousands of Berliners stayed outside the stadium stands.

Delivering speeches to pay tribute to the German fighters for peace were: Academician Paladin, President of the Ukrainian Academy of Sciences; Wu Youxun, representative of the scientists of the People's Republic of China; the President of the Bulgarian Academy of Sciences; representatives of French scientists; the Minister of Education of Democratic Greece, and many German scientists.

### 柏林举行保卫和平大会  苏中保法等国科学家代表致辞  向德国拥护和平的战士们致敬

1950-07-18
第4版()
专栏：

　　柏林举行保卫和平大会
    苏中保法等国科学家代表致辞
    向德国拥护和平的战士们致敬
    【新华社十六日讯】塔斯社柏林讯：十四日傍晚，柏林瓦尔特·乌尔布里特体育馆中举行保卫和平大会，来柏林出席德国科学院第一百五十周年纪念会的外国代表团，也参加了这一大会。
    有八万座位的体育馆，也不能把所有的参加者们容纳在内，因此，有几千柏林人都停留在体育馆的看台外面。
    向德国拥护和平的战士们致辞表示敬意的，有：乌克兰科学院院长巴拉丁，中华人民共和国科学家代表吴有训，保加利亚科学院院长，法国科学家代表们，民主希腊教育部长以及许多德国科学家们。
