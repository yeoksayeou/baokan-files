Title: Youth Summer Life Committee to Organize Youth Art Evening, First Scheduled for Tomorrow and the Day After Tomorrow in Zhongshan Park

Author: 
Date: 1950-07-13
Page: 6

    Youth Summer Life Committee
    To Organize Youth Art Evening
    First Scheduled for Tomorrow and the Day After Tomorrow in Zhongshan Park
    The Beijing Youth Summer Life Committee will jointly organize youth art evenings with various art groups in Beijing during the summer vacation to liven up the summer life of students and further improve their thinking and culture. The first evening will be held at the Zhongshan Park Concert Hall at 7:30 pm on July 13th and 14th, with the Central Academy of Drama's Drama Troupe performing "Red Flag Song." Students from various schools can purchase tickets through the student union at the offices of the summer committees in each district. Each ticket costs 500 yuan. (Announced by the Propaganda and Communication Group of the Summer Committee)

### 青年暑期生活委员会  筹办青年文艺晚会  首次定于今明两日在中山公园举行

1950-07-13
第6版()
专栏：

    青年暑期生活委员会
    筹办青年文艺晚会
    首次定于今明两日在中山公园举行
    北京市青年暑期生活委员会将联合京市各文艺工作团体，在暑期中经常举办青年文艺晚会，以活跃同学们的暑期生活，并使同学们在思想上、文化上得到进一步的提高。第一次晚会将于七月十三、十四两日下午七时半在中山公园音乐堂举行，由中央戏剧学院话剧团演出“红旗歌”。各校同学可通过学生会到各区暑委会办事处购买入场券。每券五百元。（暑委会宣通组）
