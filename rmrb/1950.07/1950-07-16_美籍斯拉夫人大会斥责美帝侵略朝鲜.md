Title: Congress of American Slavs Condemns US Imperialist Aggression Against Korea
Author:
Date: 1950-07-16
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, dispatch from New York, 15th: The Congress of American Slavs recently issued a statement denouncing the American aggression in Korea and strongly urging Truman and the US Congress to immediately cease military operations in Korea and withdraw American troops. The statement pointed out that the Congress of American Slavs fully supports the demands of some American groups and well-meaning people who are urging Truman and Congress to stop the unjust war against the Korean people and to order the withdrawal of American troops from Korea.

### 美籍斯拉夫人大会斥责美帝侵略朝鲜

1950-07-16
第4版()
专栏：

　　美籍斯拉夫人大会斥责美帝侵略朝鲜
    【新华社十五日讯】塔斯社纽约十四日讯：美籍斯拉夫人大会顷发表声明，斥责美国在朝鲜的侵略行动，并力促杜鲁门与美国国会立即停止在朝鲜作战和撤退美国部队。该声明指出：美籍斯拉夫人大会完全赞同美国一些团体和善良人们的要求，他们促请杜鲁门与国会停止反对朝鲜人民的非正义战争，并下令美军退出朝鲜。
