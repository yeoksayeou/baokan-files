Title: Oppose American Aggression Against Taiwan and Korea: Beijing to Hold Lecture Meeting Tomorrow; All-Circles Women's Anti-Aggression Meeting Scheduled for the 23rd

Author:
Date: 1950-07-21
Page: 1

　　Oppose American Aggression Against Taiwan and Korea
    Beijing to Hold Lecture Meeting Tomorrow
    All-Circles Women's Anti-Aggression Meeting Scheduled for the 23rd
    [Beijing News Agency Dispatch] The Beijing People's Committee Against American Aggression Against Taiwan and Korea held its second meeting on the afternoon of the 20th. In addition to specifically arranging the publicity activities of various people's organizations, the meeting decided to hold a lecture meeting with more than 12,000 people at the Labor People's Cultural Palace at 7:00 pm on the 22nd, inviting Deng Tuo, Deputy Director of the People's Daily, to give a report. The representative mass meeting originally scheduled for the 23rd of this month has been postponed and is planned to be held on a larger scale.
    [Another Dispatch] The Beijing Democratic Women's Federation is scheduled to hold the "Beijing All-Circles Women's Anti-American Aggression Against Taiwan and Korea Movement Lecture Meeting" at the Labor People's Cultural Palace at 6:30 pm on the 23rd (Sunday). The film "Zhao Yiman" will be shown after the meeting.
    [Another Dispatch] The Beijing People's Committee Against American Aggression Against Taiwan and Korea held a lecture meeting at the Zhongshan Park Music Hall on the evening of the 18th. More than 6,000 people from all walks of life in Beijing attended, and five representatives from the Korean Embassy also participated, receiving enthusiastic cheers from the audience.
    Xiao Ming, the chairman of the committee, delivered a speech first, calling on workers and people from all walks of life in Beijing to enthusiastically participate in this movement and to oppose American aggression against Taiwan and support the Korean people's liberation war through hard work in production and active work. Then, Fan Changjiang, Director of the People's Daily, gave a report on the situation of the Korean War. Finally, the meeting ended in enthusiastic slogans of "Oppose American Imperialist Aggression Against Taiwan" and "Support the Korean People's Liberation War."

### 反对美国侵略台湾朝鲜  京市明日举行讲演大会  各界妇女反侵略大会定二十三日举行

1950-07-21
第1版()
专栏：

　　反对美国侵略台湾朝鲜
    京市明日举行讲演大会
    各界妇女反侵略大会定二十三日举行
    【京市新闻处讯】北京市人民反对美国侵略台湾朝鲜运动委员会二十日下午举行第二次会议。会上除具体布置各人民团体的宣传活动外，并决定二十二日下午七时在劳动人民文化宫举行一万二千余人的讲演大会，邀请人民日报副社长邓拓作报告。原订本月二十三日举行的代表性的群众大会，决定延期，拟扩大举行。
    【又讯】京市民主妇女联合会定于二十三日（星期日）下午六时半在劳动人民文化宫举行“北京市各界妇女反对美国侵略台湾朝鲜运动讲演大会”。会后并放映电影《赵一曼》。
    【又讯】北京市人民反对美国侵略台湾朝鲜运动委员会曾于十八日晚在中山公园音乐堂举行讲演会。到会的有京市各界人民六千余人，朝鲜大使馆也有代表五人参加，受到会场群众的热烈欢呼。
    大会首由该会主任委员萧明致词，他号召京市工人及各界人民热烈参加这一运动，并以努力生产，积极工作来反对美国侵略台湾，支援朝鲜人民解放战争。接着即由人民日报社社长范长江作关于朝鲜战争形势的报告。最后大会在“反对美国帝国主义侵略台湾”“援助朝鲜人民解放战争”的热烈口号声中结束。
