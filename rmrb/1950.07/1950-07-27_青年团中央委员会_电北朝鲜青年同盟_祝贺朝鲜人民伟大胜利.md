Title: Central Committee of the Communist Youth League Cables the North Korean Democratic Youth League to Congratulate the Great Victory of the Korean People
Author:
Date: 1950-07-27
Page: 1

　　Xinhua News Agency, dated 26th:

    Central Committee of the Communist Youth League
    Cables the North Korean Democratic Youth League
    Congratulating the Great Victory of the Korean People
    [Xinhua News Agency, 26th] The Central Committee of the China New Democratic Youth League has sent a cable to the Central Committee of the North Korean Democratic Youth League, congratulating the victory of the Korean people. The cable reads as follows: To Comrade Hyun Jung-min, Chairman of the Central Committee of the North Korean Democratic Youth League, and to all the democratic youth of Korea:
    We, the youth and members of the Youth League of China, warmly congratulate the great victory won by the Korean people in the war of fiercely striking the invading Syngman Rhee puppet army and the American aggressors, and extend revolutionary greetings to the Korean youth standing at the forefront of the war.
    The Chinese youth and the Chinese people firmly support the just war you are waging, and will answer the American imperialist aggression against China by liberating Taiwan.
    The Korean people and the Chinese people will surely achieve victory.
              Central Committee of the China New Democratic Youth League
                                       Secretary: Feng Wenbin
                                     Deputy Secretary: Liao Chengzhi
                                             Jiang Nanxiang
                      July 25, 1950

### 青年团中央委员会  电北朝鲜青年同盟  祝贺朝鲜人民伟大胜利

1950-07-27
第1版()
专栏：

　　青年团中央委员会
    电北朝鲜青年同盟
    祝贺朝鲜人民伟大胜利
    【新华社二十六日讯】中国新民主主义青年团中央委员会，致电北朝鲜民主青年同盟，祝贺朝鲜人民的胜利，电文如下：北朝鲜民主青年同盟中央委员会委员长玄正民同志并转全朝鲜民主青年：
    我们中国的青年和青年团员，热烈祝贺朝鲜人民在痛击进犯的李承晚伪军和美国侵略者的战争中所获得的伟大胜利，并向站在战争最前线的朝鲜青年致革命的敬礼。
    中国青年和中国人民，坚决支援你们所进行的正义战争，并将以解放台湾，答复美国帝国主义对中国的侵略。
    朝鲜人民和中国人民一定会取得胜利。
              中国新民主主义青年团中央委员会
                                       书记  冯文彬
                                     副书记  廖承志
                                             蒋南翔
                      一九五○年七月二十五日
