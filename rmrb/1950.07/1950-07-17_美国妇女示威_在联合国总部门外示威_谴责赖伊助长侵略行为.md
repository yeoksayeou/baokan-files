Title: US Women Demonstrate, Demonstrating Outside UN Headquarters, Condemning Lie for Aiding Aggression
Author:
Date: 1950-07-17
Page: 4

　　Xinhua News Agency, Lake Success Dispatch: According to a United Press report, about 150 American women advocating peace held a peace demonstration outside the United Nations Headquarters on the 13th, condemning UN Secretary-General Trygve Lie for abusing the name of the United Nations and supporting US imperialism's intervention in Korea. The demonstrators included the wife of the American black singer Paul Robeson. They entered the UN headquarters and demanded that the UN stop helping the US interfere in Korea.

### 美国妇女示威  在联合国总部门外示威  谴责赖伊助长侵略行为

1950-07-17
第4版()
专栏：

　　美国妇女示威
    在联合国总部门外示威
    谴责赖伊助长侵略行为
    【新华社十六日讯】据合众社成功湖讯：美国拥护和平的妇女约一百五十名，于十三日在联合国总部门外举行和平示威，谴责联合国秘书长赖伊滥用联合国名义，支持美帝国主义干涉朝鲜的行动。示威者包括美国黑人歌王罗伯逊的夫人。她们曾步入联合国总部，要求联合国停止帮助美国干涉朝鲜。
