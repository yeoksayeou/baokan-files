Title: The Soviet Foreign Cultural Association Representative in Beijing Presents Books and Periodicals to the Third Cultural Center
Author: Liu Deyuan
Date: 1950-07-18
Page: 3

　　Soviet Foreign Cultural Association Representative in Beijing
    Presents Books and Periodicals to the Third Cultural Center
    [This Newspaper's report] On July 14th, Comrade Selezhin, the representative of the Soviet Foreign Cultural Association in our country, personally brought more than 250 copies of dozens of books published in Moscow, including "A Brief Biography of Stalin," "A Short Course on the History of the Communist Party of the Soviet Union (Bolsheviks)," "Marx, Engels and Marxism," and "A Concise Chinese-Russian Dictionary" (in Russian, English, and Chinese), as well as twelve portraits of leaders such as Stalin and Molotov, and presented them to the Beijing Third Cultural Center to help the broad masses of readers study Marxism-Leninism. Comrade Selezhin also personally visited the reading rooms and exhibition rooms.
                          (Third Center Communication Group Liu Deyuan)

### 苏联对外文协驻京代表  赠送书刊给第三文化馆
刘德元
1950-07-18
第3版()
专栏：

　　苏联对外文协驻京代表
    赠送书刊给第三文化馆
    【本报讯】七月十四日，苏联对外文化协会驻我国代表谢列金同志，亲携莫斯科出版的斯大林传略、联共（布）党史简明教程、马克思恩格斯及马克思主义、汉俄简略词典等数十种（俄文、英文、中文）书刊共计二百五十余册，及斯大林、莫洛托夫等领袖画像十二幅，赠送北京市第三文化馆，以帮助广大读者学习马列主义。谢列金同志并亲到各阅览室、展览室参观。
                          （三馆通讯组刘德元）
