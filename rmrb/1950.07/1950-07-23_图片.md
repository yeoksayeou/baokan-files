Title: American Prisoners of War Pass Through the Streets of Pyongyang
Author:
Date: 1950-07-23
Page: 4

    American prisoners of war pass through the streets of Pyongyang. The slogans on the flags in Korean read: "The Korean people's struggle to unify their fatherland is just! Immediately stop armed intervention in Korea!"
