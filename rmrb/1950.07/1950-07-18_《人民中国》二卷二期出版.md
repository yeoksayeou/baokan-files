　　《People's China》 Volume II, Issue 2 Published

　　The English semi-monthly magazine "People's China," Volume II, Issue 2, was published on July 16th. Land reform is the theme of this issue. In the "Essays" section, there is a translation of Vice Chairman Liu Shaoqi's "Report on the Land Reform Problem" and "The Chinese Land Revolution," both of which explain the land reform. The "Features" section includes "A Record of Land Reform in Shuanghuaishu" and "Spring After Land Reform," vividly depicting the face of land reform. The pictures in this issue also coordinate with this theme. The editorial is "The People of All Asia Support the Korean People's Struggle." There is also an article in the "Essays" section commemorating the people's musician Nie Er.

### 《人民中国》二卷二期出版

1950-07-18
第6版()
专栏：

　　《人民中国》二卷二期出版
    英文半月刊《人民中国》的二卷二期已于七月十六日出版。土改是本期的主题。专论栏里有刘少奇副主席的《关于土地改革问题的报告》的译文和《中国土地革命》，都是对土改的说明。特写栏里有《双槐树土改记》和《土改后的春天》，生动地描绘了土改的面貌。本期的图片也是配合这一主题的。社论是《全亚人民支持朝鲜人民的斗争》。专论栏里还有一篇纪念人民音乐家聂耳的文章。
