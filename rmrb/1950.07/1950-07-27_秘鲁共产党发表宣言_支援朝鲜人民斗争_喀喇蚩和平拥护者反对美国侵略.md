Title: Communist Party of Peru Issues Declaration Supporting the Struggle of the Korean People; Karachi Peace Supporters Oppose American Aggression
Author:
Date: 1950-07-27
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, July 25th: TASS, New York, July 24th: Continental News Agency reports from Lima: The Communist Party of Peru has issued a declaration calling on the Peruvian people to protest against American armed intervention in Korea. The declaration states: "The Communist Party of Peru reiterates that in the just struggle of mankind, it firmly supports those who oppose imperialism and reactionaries."

Xinhua News Agency, July 26th: TASS, Karachi: A conference of peace supporters was held here on July 23rd. The conference elected the Karachi Peace Committee, including Chairman Bakshi, Secretary Muntaz (progressive writer), and Accountant Newrani. The conference unanimously passed the committee's declaration, which stated: "The Karachi Peace Supporters will fight to protect world peace and support the independence movements of various colonial countries."

In the resolutions passed, the peace supporters demanded the withdrawal of all American troops from Korea, the cessation of American "aid" to the Bao Dai puppet "government" in Indochina, and the cessation of support for the Chiang Kai-shek clique.

### 秘鲁共产党发表宣言  支援朝鲜人民斗争  喀喇蚩和平拥护者反对美国侵略

1950-07-27
第4版()
专栏：

　　秘鲁共产党发表宣言
    支援朝鲜人民斗争           
    喀喇蚩和平拥护者反对美国侵略
    【新华社二十五日讯】塔斯社纽约二十四日讯：大陆新闻社自利马来讯称：秘鲁共产党发表宣言，号召秘鲁人民抗议美国武装干涉朝鲜。宣言说：“秘鲁共产党再度声明，在人类进行的正义的斗争中，它坚决地支持那些反对帝国主义和反动派的人们。”
    【新华社二十六日讯】塔斯社喀喇蚩讯：七月二十三日，此间举行了和平拥护者大会。会上选出包括主席巴克希，秘书蒙塔兹（进步作家）和会计纽拉尼的喀喇蚩和平委员会。大会一致通过了委员会的宣言，该宣言说：“喀喇蚩和平拥护者将为保护世界和平而奋斗，并支持各殖民地国家的独立运动。”
    在通过的决议中，和平拥护者要求自朝鲜撤退所有的美国军队，停止美国对印度支那保大傀儡“政府”的“援助”及对蒋介石集团的支持。
