Title: New Knowledge Forum Today: Chai Zemin on the Land Reform Issue
Author:
Date: 1950-07-29
Page: 6

　　New Knowledge Forum Today: Chai Zemin on the Land Reform Issue
　　The Beijing Branch of the China Democratic National Construction Association will hold its 80th New Knowledge Forum at 7:30 PM on the 29th. Mr. Chai Zemin of the Beijing Suburban District Committee will speak on the land reform issue, focusing on the relationship between land reform and industry and commerce. Those from all walks of life who wish to attend can obtain admission tickets at No. 24 Wuliang Daren Hutong through introductions from members, friends, or organizations, enterprises, and institutions.

### 新知识座谈会今由柴泽民讲土地改革问题

1950-07-29
第6版()
专栏：

　　新知识座谈会今由柴泽民讲土地改革问题
    民建京分会二十九日下午七时半，举行第八十次新知识座谈会，请京市郊区委员会柴泽民先生主讲土地改革问题，着重阐述土改和工商业的关系。各界愿往听讲者，可由会员会友或机关团体、企业单位介绍，到无量大人胡同二十四号领取入场券。
