Title: Strive for Hundreds of Millions of Signatures
Author: Li Da
Date: 1950-07-03
Page: 5

　　Strive for Hundreds of Millions of Signatures
　　Li Da
　　In response to the call of the World Congress of Supporters of Peace to sign in support of world peace, all institutions, troops, parties and organizations in China should immediately and fully implement the peace signature campaign, and penetrate deeply into rural and urban areas to conduct patriotic and internationalist education for the unorganized people, persuade them to participate, and strive for hundreds of millions of signatures in support of lasting world peace.

### 争取亿万人签名
李达
1950-07-03
第5版()
专栏：

　　争取亿万人签名
    李达
    为响应世界拥护和平大会签名拥护世界和平的号召，中国一切的机关、部队、党派和团体，应立即全部实行和平签名，并深入农村和城市，对尚未组织的人民，进行爱国主义与国际主义的教育，劝导他们参加，争取亿万人签名拥护世界持久和平的数字。
