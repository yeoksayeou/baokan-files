Title: "New Construction" Issue No. 11 Published
Author:
Date: 1950-07-18
Page: 6
The bi-weekly "New Construction," Volume 2, Issue 11, has now been published. The contents of this issue include: Liao Gailong's "On the Third Plenary Session of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of China and the Second Session of the National Committee of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference," Chen Tiqiang's "Further Implementation of the US Imperialist Plot to Sabotage the United Nations," Wang Chaowen's "Fine Arts and Drama," Wu Pinjia's "The Suffering Economy of Taiwan," and the Ministry of Heavy Industry's "Outline of the Industrial Economic Plan," as well as several news reports.

专栏：

　　《新建设》第十一期出版
    《新建设》双周刊，二卷十一期现已出版，这一期内容有：廖盖隆的《关于中国共产党三中全会和人民政协全国委员会第二次会议》。陈体强的《美帝破坏联合国阴谋的进一步实施》，王朝闻的《美术与戏剧》，吴频迦的《苦难的台湾经济》及重工业部的《工业经济计划概要》等专文，另外还有通讯报导多篇。
