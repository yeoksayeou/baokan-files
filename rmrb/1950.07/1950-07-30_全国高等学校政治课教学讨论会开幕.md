Title: National Higher Education Political Course Teaching Symposium Opens
Author:
Date: 1950-07-30
Page: 3

Xinhua News Agency, 28th: The National Higher Education Summer Political Course Teaching Symposium, convened by the Ministry of Education of the Central People's Government, officially opened on the 26th of this month at Yenching University. Participants in the meeting include eighty political course teachers from public and private universities and colleges in the North China, East China, Central South, Southwest, and Northwest Administrative Regions. The session will last from July 24th to August 25th. During this symposium, the focus will be on studying the teaching content of political courses, exchanging teaching experiences, and seeking consistency in teaching ideology, content, and methods, so as to further improve the political and ideological education in national universities and colleges in the next academic year on the existing foundation. The opening ceremony was held at 9:00 a.m. on the 26th, with Minister Ma Xulun, Vice Minister Wei Que, and Deputy Director of the Higher Education Department Zhang Zonglin of the Ministry of Education all present and giving speeches.

### 全国高等学校政治课教学讨论会开幕

1950-07-30
第3版()
专栏：

　　全国高等学校政治课教学讨论会开幕
    【新华社二十八日讯】中央人民政府教育部召集的全国高等学校暑期政治课教学讨论会已于本月二十六日在燕京大学正式开幕。该会参加人员包括华北、华东、中南、西南、西北各大行政区公私立高等学校的政治课教师共八十人，会期为自七月二十四日至八月二十五日。在这次讨论会中，将有重点地研讨政治课教学内容，交流教学经验，求得教学思想、教学内容及方法上的一致，使下学年全国高等学校政治思想教育在原有基础上提高一步。该会于二十六日上午九时举行开幕典礼，教育部马叙伦部长，韦悫副部长，高教司张宗麟副司长均曾到会讲话。
