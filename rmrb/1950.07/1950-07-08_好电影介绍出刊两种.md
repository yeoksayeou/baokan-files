Title: Two Kinds of Good Movie Introductions Published
Author:
Date: 1950-07-08
Page: 6

　　Two Kinds of Good Movie Introductions Published
    In order to widely introduce excellent Soviet and Chinese films, the Central Film Bureau has specially compiled "Good Movie Introductions," published by the Popular Bookstore. Two issues have already been released: "The Great Battle of Stalingrad" and "Zhao Yiman." In addition to story and character introductions, they also contain many wonderful photographs.

### 好电影介绍出刊两种

1950-07-08
第6版()
专栏：

　　好电影介绍出刊两种
    中央电影局为了广泛介绍苏联和我国的优良影片，特选编《好电影介绍》，由大众书店出版，已出《斯大林格勒大血战》《赵一曼》两种，除故事人物介绍之外，并有精彩照片多幅。
