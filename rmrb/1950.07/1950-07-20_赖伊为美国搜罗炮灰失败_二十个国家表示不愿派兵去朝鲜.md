Title: Lie's Failure to Scrounge Cannon Fodder for the United States: Twenty Countries Express Unwillingness to Send Troops to Korea
Author:
Date: 1950-07-20
Page: 1

　　Lie's Failure to Scrounge Cannon Fodder for the United States
    Twenty Countries Express Unwillingness to Send Troops to Korea
    [Xinhua News Agency, 19th Dispatch] TASS, London, 18th Dispatch: According to a London broadcast, as of July 18th, twenty member states of the United Nations had responded to Secretary-General Lie's call for military assistance to the American aggressors in Korea. According to an official at Lake Success (the location of the United Nations), none of these countries expressed willingness to send troops to Korea.

### 赖伊为美国搜罗炮灰失败  二十个国家表示不愿派兵去朝鲜

1950-07-20
第1版()
专栏：

　　赖伊为美国搜罗炮灰失败
    二十个国家表示不愿派兵去朝鲜
    【新华社十九日讯】塔斯社伦敦十八日讯：据伦敦广播，截至七月十八日止，联合国已有二十个会员国对联合国秘书长赖伊呼吁给与在朝鲜的美国侵略者军事援助一事，提出答复。据成功湖（联合国所在地）的一位官员声称：在这些国家中，没有一国表示愿意派兵到朝鲜去。
