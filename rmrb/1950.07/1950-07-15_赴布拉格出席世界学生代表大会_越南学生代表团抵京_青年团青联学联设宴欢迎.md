Title: Vietnamese Student Delegation Arrives in Beijing to Attend World Student Congress in Prague; Youth League, Youth Federation, and Student Federation Host Banquet to Welcome Them
Author:
Date: 1950-07-15
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, 14th: The Vietnamese student delegation, led by head of the delegation Huang Mingzheng, arrived in the Chinese capital on the 13th from Vietnam to attend the Second World Student Congress in Prague, Czechoslovakia. Liao Chengzhi, Chairman of the All-China Federation of Democratic Youth, Xie Bangding, Chairman of the All-China Students' Federation, Qu Tangliang, Minister of Foreign Liaison Department of the Central Committee of the Youth League, Shen Ping, Deputy Director of the Protocol Department of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs of the Central People's Government, Wu Han, Deputy Mayor of Beijing, Zhang Dazhong, Deputy Secretary of the Beijing Municipal Committee of the Communist Youth League, and Gu Qicong, Chairman of the Beijing Students' Federation, went to welcome them. Representatives of the Chinese delegation to the Second World Student Congress also went to welcome them. The delegation will depart from Beijing with the Chinese student delegation to attend the congress.

Another Dispatch: The Central Committee of the China New Democratic Youth League , the All-China Youth Federation, and the All-China Students' Federation hosted a banquet on the evening of the 14th to welcome the Vietnamese delegation to the Second World Student Congress. Feng Wenbin, Secretary of the Central Committee of the Youth League, and ministers of various departments, Liao Chengzhi, Chairman of the All-China Youth Federation, Sha Qianli, Vice Chairman, Wu Han, Secretary-General, Xie Bangding, Chairman of the All-China Students' Federation, and Gu Qicong, Chairman of the Municipal Students' Federation, attended as guests.

### 赴布拉格出席世界学生代表大会  越南学生代表团抵京  青年团青联学联设宴欢迎

1950-07-15
第1版()
专栏：

　　赴布拉格出席世界学生代表大会
    越南学生代表团抵京         
    青年团青联学联设宴欢迎
    【新华社十四日讯】前往捷京布拉格出席世界学生第二次代表大会的越南学生代表团，由团长黄明正率领，于十三日由越南抵达中国首都。中华全国民主青年联合总会主席廖承志、全国学生联合会主席谢邦定、青年团中央国外联络部长区棠亮、中央人民政府外交部交际处副处长沈平、北京市副市长吴晗、北京市团工委副书记张大中、北京市学联主席古奇琮等均前往欢迎。中国出席世界学生第二次代表大会代表团的代表们也前往欢迎。该团将与中国学生代表团一同由京启程出席大会。
    【又讯】中国新民主主义青年团中央委员会、中华全国民主青年联合总会、中华全国学生联合会等三单位，十四日晚设宴欢迎出席世界学生第二次代表大会的越南代表团。青年团中央书记冯文彬及各部部长、全国青联主席廖承志、副主席沙千里、秘书长吴晗、全国学联主席谢邦定，市学联主席古奇琮等均出席作陪。
