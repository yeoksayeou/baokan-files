Title: The Korean People Fight to Defeat the American Aggressors! (Photos)
Author:
Date: 1950-07-22
Page: 5

    After the liberation of Seoul, a parade of women.
    The wreckage of an American plane shot down by the Korean People's Army Air Force.
    A Korean People's Army Navy torpedo boat sinking an American naval cruiser.
    The tank unit of the People's Army of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea triumphantly enters the streets of Seoul, receiving a warm welcome from the local people.
    The first batch of American prisoners of war were escorted to Pyongyang on July 11th, shamefully passing through the streets on their way to the prisoner-of-war camp.
