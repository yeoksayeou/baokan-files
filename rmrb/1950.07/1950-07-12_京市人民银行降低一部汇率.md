Title: Beijing People's Bank Reduces Some Exchange Rates
Author:
Date: 1950-07-12
Page: 6

    Beijing People's Bank Reduces Some Exchange Rates
    The Beijing Branch of the People's Bank of China has reduced some exchange rates since the 6th. The telegraphic transfer rate to Shanghai, Nanjing, Hankou, Wuchang, Qingdao, and Jinan is changed to 0.2%; the telegraphic transfer rate to Guangzhou and Shantou is changed to 0.3%. This is a reduction of 50% to 66% compared to before. The mail transfer is calculated at 80% of the telegraphic transfer rate, and the remittance is calculated at 50%.
                          Beijing News Agency

### 京市人民银行降低一部汇率
京市新闻处
1950-07-12
第6版()
专栏：

　　京市人民银行降低一部汇率
    人民银行北京分行自六日起减低一部汇率。对上海、南京、汉口、武昌、青岛、济南六地电汇汇率改为千分之二；对广州、汕头两地电汇汇率改为千分之三。比以前降低百分之五十到六十六。票汇按电汇汇率八折计收，信汇按五折计收。
                          京市新闻处
