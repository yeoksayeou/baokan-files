Title: Australian Youth Leader Wishes to Visit China, Menzies Government Actually Refuses to Issue Passport, Progressive Groups Universally Protest
Author:
Date: 1950-07-27
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Sydney, 26th.  Telecommunications Agency Sydney, 24th dispatch: The Australian government refused to issue a passport to prominent Australian youth leader Kentingham to visit the People's Republic of China. Kentingham is the secretary of the Eureka Youth League of New South Wales, a progressive youth organization in Australia, and an executive member of the New South Wales Peace Committee. He was recently selected as the Australian representative of the World Federation of Democratic Youth delegation invited to visit China in September this year. Progressive groups and youth groups throughout Australia are organizing widespread protests against this unreasonable decision by the Australian government.

### 澳青年领袖欲访问我国  孟席斯政府竟拒发护照  进步团体普遍提出抗议

1950-07-27
第4版()
专栏：

　　澳青年领袖欲访问我国
    孟席斯政府竟拒发护照     
    进步团体普遍提出抗议
    【新华社二十六日讯】电通社悉尼二十四日讯：澳大利亚政府拒绝发给澳著名青年领袖肯丁汉访问中华人民共和国的护照。肯丁汉是澳大利亚进步青年组织——佑里加青年联盟新南威尔士州的书记、新南威尔士和平委员会的执行委员，他在最近被选为今年九月应邀访问中国的世界民主青联代表团的澳大利亚代表。澳大利亚各地进步团体和青年团体都在组织普遍的抗议，反对奥政府的这一无理决定。
