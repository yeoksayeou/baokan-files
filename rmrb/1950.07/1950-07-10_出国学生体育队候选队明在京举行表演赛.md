Title: Student Sports Team Candidates for Overseas Competition to Hold Exhibition Match in Beijing Tomorrow
Author:
Date: 1950-07-10
Page: 6

Candidate teams of basketball and volleyball players from various regions, participating in the selection competition for the All-China Students' Federation's overseas sports team, will hold an exhibition match in Beijing on the 11th and 12th. The location is the Palace of Laboring People's Culture. Games will be held simultaneously, with basketball and volleyball each day starting at 4:00 PM. Tickets are 1,000 yuan, with a half-price discount for workers, students, and cadres holding identification or wearing symbol badges. Ticket sales will begin tomorrow at the Palace of Laboring People's Culture, the Qianmen Municipal First Cultural Center, the Gulou Municipal Second Cultural Center, the East Chang'an Street Youth Service Department, the Rice Market Street YMCA, the Donghuamen Tonglai Sporting Goods Store, and the Xisi Chunhe Sporting Goods Store.
(China Youth News Agency)

### 出国学生体育队候选队明在京举行表演赛

1950-07-10
第6版()
专栏：

　　出国学生体育队候选队明在京举行表演赛
    参加中华全国学生联合会出国体育队选拔赛的各地篮球、排球候选队，将于十一日十二日在京举行表演赛。地点在劳动人民文化宫。每日下午四时起，分篮球、排球两场同时举行。入场券一千元，持有证件或佩带符号证章的工人、学生、干部可享受半价优待。自明日起在劳动人民文化宫、前门市立第一文化馆、鼓楼市立第二文化馆、东长安街青年服务部、米市大街青年会、东华门同来运动品商店、西四春合运动品商店等处开始售票。
    （中国青年社）
