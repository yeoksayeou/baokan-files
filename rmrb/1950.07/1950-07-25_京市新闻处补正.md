Beijing News Agency Correction

Regarding the article "Xingye Co., Ltd. Raising Funds" issued on July 21st (published on the 23rd of this month), the full name of Xingye Co., Ltd. should be "Beijing Xingye Investment Co., Ltd."

### 京市新闻处补正

1950-07-25

　　京市新闻处补正
