Title: Jalkhasaikhan's Resume
Author:
Date: 1950-07-04
Page: 1

　　Xinhua News Agency, 3rd. Biography of Mr. Bayaryn Jalkhasaikhan, Ambassador of the Mongolian People's Republic to China.

　　Born in 1915 in the Khalkh River Sumu (Sumu is equivalent to a county) of Kherlen League, Mongolian People's Republic.

　　After graduating from middle school, he graduated from the Oriental Studies Institute of Moscow, Soviet Union. After that, he served as the Dean of the Academy of Sciences. For a period, he served as the First Secretary of the Embassy of the Mongolian People's Republic in the Soviet Union and worked in the Ministry of Foreign Affairs of his country.

　　In recent years, he has served as the Dean of the Academy of Sciences. While serving in this position, he was dispatched by the Mongolian People's Republic as Ambassador Extraordinary and Plenipotentiary to the People's Republic of China.

### 贾尔卡赛汗简历

1950-07-04
第1版()
专栏：

　　贾尔卡赛汗简历
    【新华社三日讯】蒙古人民共和国驻华大使巴雅伦·贾尔卡赛汗简历。
    一九一五年生于蒙古人民共和国阙巴拉森盟哈拉哈河苏木（苏木相当于县）。
    中学毕业后又在苏联莫斯科东方研究学院毕业，此后即担任科学院院长工作，有一时期曾任蒙古人民共和国驻苏联大使馆一等秘书及在本国的外交部工作。
    近几年来担任科学研究院院长职务，在担任该职务中，由蒙古人民共和国派遣为驻中华人民共和国特命全权大使。
