Title: Polish Prime Minister Cyrankiewicz Receives Ambassador Peng Mingzhi; Chinese Scientific Delegation to Germany Arrives in Warsaw
Author:
Date: 1950-07-28
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, Warsaw, 27th: Polish News Agency, Warsaw reports: Polish Prime Minister Cyrankiewicz received General Peng Mingzhi, Ambassador Extraordinary and Plenipotentiary of the People's Republic of China to Poland, on the 26th.
Xinhua News Agency, Warsaw, 27th: According to the Polish News Agency, Warsaw: The delegation of the Chinese Academy of Sciences, including Wu Youxun, Hua Luogeng, Wang Ganchang, and Yun Ziqiang, which is participating in the celebration of the 250th anniversary of the German Academy of Sciences, arrived in Warsaw on the 26th. Representatives from the Polish Ministry of Higher Education and Science and the Ministry of Foreign Affairs were present at the station to welcome them. In the afternoon of the same day, Labarski, the Minister of Higher Education and Science of Poland, hosted them. The delegation will stay in Poland for sightseeing for four days.

### 波兰总理西伦凯维兹  接见我大使彭明治将军  我赴德科学代表团抵华沙

1950-07-28
第1版()
专栏：

　　波兰总理西伦凯维兹
    接见我大使彭明治将军
    我赴德科学代表团抵华沙
    【新华社二十七日讯】波兰通讯社华沙讯：波兰总理西伦凯维兹，于二十六日接见中华人民共和国驻波兰特命全权大使彭明治将军。
    【新华社二十七日讯】据波兰通讯社华沙讯：中国科学院参加庆祝德意志科学院二百五十周年的代表团吴有训、华罗庚、王淦昌、恽子强等一行，于二十六日抵达华沙。波兰高等教育与科学部和外交部均派代表到车站欢迎。当日下午，波兰高等教育与科学部部长拉巴斯基曾加以招待。代表团将在波兰逗留观光四日。
