Title: Important Correction from This Newspaper
Author:
Date: 1950-07-25
Page: 1

　　Important Correction from This Newspaper
    In this newspaper's editorial on the 24th, "The Forces of Peace are Continuing to Rise" on page four, lines five and six, "according to Articles Five and Seven of the United Nations Charter" should read "according to Article Two, paragraph five, and Article Seven of the United Nations Charter."
