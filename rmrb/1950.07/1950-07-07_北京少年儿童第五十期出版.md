Title: Beijing Juvenile and Children's Magazine, 50th Issue Published
Author:
Date: 1950-07-07
Page: 6

The 50th issue of "Beijing Juvenile and Children's Magazine" was published on July 5th. The main contents of this issue include: a short article against the ambition of American imperialism's armed aggression, "Taiwan Must Be Liberated," and the recent "major event" in Asia - the Korean people, under the call of General Kim Il-sung, crushed the offensive of the Syngman Rhee reactionary clique, instigated and supported by American imperialism, and liberated the Korean capital, Seoul. There is also the good news of domestic construction, "The Chengdu-Chongqing Railway Has Started Construction," with an accompanying map of the distribution of products along the Chengdu-Chongqing Railway. In literature, there is the small story from the War of Resistance Against Japan, "I Want Revenge," and the comic strip "Storm at Sea (Part 2)."

In addition, there are "Ancient Chinese Scientific Inventions," science Q&A, intelligence tests, and five articles and poems by children.

### 北京少年儿童第五十期出版

1950-07-07
第6版()
专栏：

　　北京少年儿童第五十期出版
    《北京少年儿童》第五十期已于七月五日出版。该期主要内容有：反对美国帝国主义武装侵略的野心的短文“台湾一定要解放”及最近亚洲发生的“大事情”——朝鲜人民在金日成将军的号召下，粉碎了美国帝国主义怂恿和支持的李承晚反动派的进攻，并解放了朝鲜首都汉城。还有国内建设的喜报“成渝铁路动工了”，此文并附有成渝铁路沿线物产分布图。在文  抗战时期的小故事“我要报仇”，和连环画《海上风暴（二）》。
    另外还有《中国古代的科学发明》、科学问答、智力测验和五篇小朋友的文章和诗。
