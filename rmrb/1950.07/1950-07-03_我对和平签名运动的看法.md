Title: My Views on the Peace Signature Campaign
Author: Liang Xi
Date: 1950-07-03
Page: 5

　　My Views on the Peace Signature Campaign
　　Liang Xi
　　I believe: The great significance of the Peace Signature Campaign: First, it is a great review and demonstration of the power of peace-loving people all over the world, warning the warmongers that we have the determination and confidence to defend peace. Second, our demand for peace does not mean we are afraid of war. We are issuing a call for peace to the people of the world, not begging for peace from the warmongers. The more peace-loving people we unite, the more we isolate the warmongers. Third, this peace camp is so broad that it transcends nationality, class, color, and creed. As long as you love peace and oppose war, you can clearly express your wish. This is the formation of an international united front for peace. One must be either a friend of peace or an enemy of peace; there is no middle ground. Fourth, the American imperialists, playing with fire and threatening the nervous with atomic bombs, cannot scare the armed people, but atomic bombs are, after all, lethal weapons. From a humanitarian standpoint, we must rise up to stop this cruel war.
　　The people of the world need peace, and the people of New China need peace even more. We—the peace camp led by the Soviet Union—are willing to organize a competition with all imperialists on the battlefield of peace, and we dare to strike back at the aggressors at any time.
　　Peace represents the boundless prosperity of eighty percent of the world's new forces; war represents only the death throes of a few dehumanized and dying beasts.
　　I hope that every citizen of New China will enthusiastically sign your kind name on the peace declaration!

### 我对和平签名运动的看法
梁希
1950-07-03
第5版()
专栏：

　　我对和平签名运动的看法
    梁希
    我认为：和平签名运动的伟大意义：第一，是全世界爱好和平人士力量的大检阅和大示威，正告了战争贩子，我们是有决心和信心来捍卫和平的。第二，我们要求和平，并不等于畏惧战争，我们是对全世界人民发出和平的呼吁，而不是向战争贩子叩求和平。多团结一个爱好和平的人，便愈益孤立了战争贩子。第三，这个和平阵营广泛到这样的程度：不分国籍，不分阶层，不分肤色，不分信仰，只要是爱好和平反对战争，你便明白的表示你的愿望，这是和平的国际统一战线的组成，谁是和平的朋友，谁是和平的敌人，二者必居其一，没有任何中间道路可走。第四，玩火的美帝以原子弹威胁神经衰弱患者，吓不倒人民的武装，但原子弹毕竟是杀人的利器，从人道的立场出发，我们必须奋起阻止这个残酷的战争。
    全世界人民需要和平，新中国的人民更需要和平，我们——以苏联为首的和平阵营，愿意和一切帝国主义者在和平的战场上组织起竞赛，也敢于在任何时间内给打击者以打击。
    和平，代表着世界百分之八十的新生力量的无边无际的繁荣；战争，只代表着少数灭绝人性的垂死的野兽临终的挣扎。
    我愿每一个新中国的人民踊跃地在和平宣言书上签上你的善良的名字！
