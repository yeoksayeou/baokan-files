Title: Japanese Communist Party Calls on Japanese Women to Enthusiastically Participate in Peace Vote
Author:
Date: 1950-07-25
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Tokyo, July 23rd, via TASS: The Japanese Communist Party published a letter to Japanese women a few days ago, calling on them to join the ranks of peace supporters and participate in the peace vote. The letter to women stated: "Let us stop the hands of those who want to use atomic bombs. A government that uses atomic bombs must be regarded as war criminals by the people of the world."

Xinwenhua Bao reported: The Peace Preservation Association of Keio University has collected 2,000 signatures on the Stockholm Peace Appeal as of July 7th. Students from Osaka Commercial College and Osaka University have collected 5,000 signatures on the Stockholm Appeal.

### 日共号召日本妇女热烈参加和平投票

1950-07-25
第4版()
专栏：

　　日共号召日本妇女热烈参加和平投票
    【新华社二十四日讯】塔斯社二十三日电引东京消息：日本共产党数天前发表了告日本妇女书，号召她们加入和平支持者的行列，参加和平投票。告妇女书说：“让我们制止住那些要使用原子弹的人的手。使用原子弹的政府一定要被全世界人民认为战犯。”
    新文化报报道：庆应大学的保卫和平协会，至七月七日为止已征集了两千人在斯德哥尔摩和平宣言上签名。大阪商学院和大阪大学的学生，已征得五千人在斯德哥尔摩宣言上签名。
