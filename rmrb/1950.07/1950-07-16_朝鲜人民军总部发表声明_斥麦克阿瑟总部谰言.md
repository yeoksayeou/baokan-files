Title: Korean People's Army Headquarters Issues Statement Condemning MacArthur Headquarters' Absurdities
Author:
Date: 1950-07-16
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Pyongyang, July 15th: The General Headquarters of the Korean People's Army of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea issued a statement on the 14th refuting the MacArthur Headquarters' slanderous claim that the Korean People's Army was mistreating American prisoners of war. The full text of the statement from the General Headquarters of the People's Army is as follows: According to the American broadcast of July 12th, the MacArthur Headquarters issued a statement falsely claiming that our People's Army of the Republic killed American prisoners of war. This is completely unfounded. The General Headquarters of the Korean People's Army of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea hereby refutes this. The reason why the American imperialists fabricate this fictitious news is to threaten the American soldiers who have been driven by them to Korea to carry out armed interference in internal affairs. It is hereby declared that the Korean People's Army of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea treats prisoners of war of the enemy army in accordance with humanitarian principles and international law.

### 朝鲜人民军总部发表声明  斥麦克阿瑟总部谰言

1950-07-16
第1版()
专栏：

　　朝鲜人民军总部发表声明
    斥麦克阿瑟总部谰言
    【新华社平壤十五日电】朝鲜民主主义人民共和国人民军总司令部十四日发表声明，驳斥麦克阿瑟总部诬蔑朝鲜人民军虐待美军俘虏的谰言。人民军总司令部的声明全文如下：据七月十二日的美国广播，麦克阿瑟总部发表声明，硬说我们共和国的人民军杀害了美国俘虏。这是毫无事实根据的。朝鲜民主主义人民共和国人民军总司令部特予以驳斥。美帝国主义者所以捏造这个虚构的消息，旨在威胁被他们驱迫到朝鲜进行武装干涉内政的美国军人。兹特声明，朝鲜民主主义人民共和国人民军系根据人道主义的原则与国际法的条例，来对待敌军的俘虏。
