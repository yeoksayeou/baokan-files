Title: Central People's Government Ministry of Heavy Industry to Regularly Convene Automobile Industry Conferences
Author:
Date: 1950-07-09
Page: 2

　　Central People's Government Ministry of Heavy Industry
    Regularly Convenes Automobile Industry Conferences
    (Xinhua News Agency, 5th) The Ministry of Heavy Industry of the Central People's Government has decided to convene automobile industry conferences in Beijing on July 27th, gathering responsible individuals from the industrial departments of major administrative regions. The conference will focus on discussing automobile factory construction issues, including vehicle models, production capacity, construction timeline and steps, construction methods, factory location, personnel, equipment, raw materials, accessories, as well as the current supply and demand relationship of automobile parts industry, coordination of production plans, development prospects of various factories, technological improvements, tractor manufacturing, trial manufacturing of new parts, general fuel and substitutes for the automobile industry, the establishment of a national automobile maintenance and repair plan, technical research work, and experience exchange. This conference will guide the new China's automobile industry onto a new path.

### 中央人民政府重工业部  定期召开汽车工业会议

1950-07-09
第2版()
专栏：

　　中央人民政府重工业部
    定期召开汽车工业会议
    【新华社五日讯】中央人民政府重工业部决定于七月二十七日召集各大行政区工业部负责人在京开汽车工业会议。会议将着重讨论汽车厂建造问题，包括车型、产量、建设时间与步骤、建设办法、厂址、人员、设备、原料、附件，以及当前汽车配件工业的配件供求关系，生产计划的配合，各厂发展前途，技术改进，拖拉机制造，新零件试造及汽车工业一般燃料与代用品，全国汽车保养修配计划的建立，技术研究工作，经验交流工作等问题。这一会议将使新中国的汽车工业走向新的途径。
