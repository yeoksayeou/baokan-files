Title: Anti-U.S. Imperialist Aggression Radio Speech Program
Author:
Date: 1950-07-17
Page: 1

　　Xinhua News Agency, 15th: The Chinese People's Committee Against U.S. Aggression in Taiwan and Korea and the Central People's Broadcasting Station will jointly launch a radio speech program "Oppose U.S. Aggression in Taiwan and Korea" starting from the 17th. The content and time of the program are as follows: On the 17th, Zhang Xiruo will speak on: Opposing U.S. Imperialist Aggression in Taiwan and Korea. On the 18th, Bai Yun will speak on: Chinese women and women all over the world unite to defend world peace. On the 20th, Hu Yuzhi will speak on: The Aggressive Actions of U.S. Imperialism and the Liberation Movement of the People of Southeast Asia. On the 21st, Chen Hanbo will speak on: The Korean People Have the Right to Liberate Themselves. In addition, Shen Yanbing, Xie Bangding and others will also give speeches, the dates of which will be determined separately. This program is scheduled to be broadcast every night from 20:00 to 20:30.

### 反对美帝侵略广播演讲节目

1950-07-17
第1版()
专栏：

　　反对美帝侵略广播演讲节目
    【新华社十五日讯】中国人民反对美国侵略台湾朝鲜运动委员会和中央人民广播电台从十七日起联合举办“反对美国侵略台湾朝鲜”的广播演讲节目，节目的内容及时间如下：十七日，张奚若讲：反对美帝侵略台湾和朝鲜。十八日，白芸讲：中国妇女和全世界妇女团结起来，保卫世界和平。二十日，胡愈之讲：美帝国主义的侵略行动与东南亚人民的解放运动。二十一日，陈翰伯讲：朝鲜人民有权解放自己。此外尚有沈雁冰、谢邦定等讲演，日期另定。此项节目定在每晚二十点到二十点三十分广播。
