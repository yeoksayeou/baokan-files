Title: World Federation of Democratic Youth Issues Call, Urges Nine International Youth Organizations to Participate in Peace Signature Campaign
Author:
Date: 1950-07-05
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency, Paris, July 2nd (TASS dispatch): The World Federation of Democratic Youth has called upon nine international youth organizations to sign the Stockholm Appeal. The World Federation of Democratic Youth emphasized the unity shown by young men and women of goodwill on all continents in striving to declare the atomic bomb illegal and to prevent a new war, and urged these nine international youth organizations, for the sake of peace, not to turn a deaf ear to the most distressing problems of the people, but to do their part in the common efforts of peace advocates to strive for victory in the struggle for peace.
    The nine international youth organizations are: the Youth Department of the International Committee of the League of Red Cross Societies, the World Young Women's Christian Association, the World Young Men's Christian Association, the Youth Department of the World Council of Churches, the International Boy Scouts Association, the World Association of Girl Guides and Girl Scouts, the World Student Christian Federation, the International Student Service and the Student Meal Hall Association.

### 世界青联发出号召  敦促九个国际青年组织参加和平签名

1950-07-05
第4版()
专栏：

　　世界青联发出号召
    敦促九个国际青年组织参加和平签名
    【新华社三日讯】塔斯社巴黎二日讯：世界民主青年联盟已号召九个国际青年组织在斯德哥尔摩宣言上签名。世界青联着重指出各大洲善良的青年男女在力求宣布原子弹为非法与阻止新战争上所表现的一致，并且敦促这九个国际青年组织为了和平，不要对人民最苦恼的问题充耳不闻，而要在和平拥护者的共同努力中尽其一分力量，以争取和平斗争的胜利。
    这九个国际青年组织为：万国红十字会协会国际委员会青年部、国际基督教女青年会、国际基督教男青年会、世界基督教会联合会青年部、国际童子军协会、国际少女团协会、世界基督教学生联合会、国际学生服务社和学生餐堂协会。
