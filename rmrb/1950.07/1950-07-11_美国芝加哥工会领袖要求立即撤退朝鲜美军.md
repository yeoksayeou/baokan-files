Title: Chicago Union Leaders in US Demand Immediate Withdrawal of American Troops from Korea
Author:
Date: 1950-07-11
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, New York, July 8th (TASS dispatch): The Chicago Workers' Daily reports that Chicago union leaders have signed a call issued by the Chicago Labor Peace Conference for the immediate withdrawal of American troops from Korea. Signatories include Kali, Gilmore, Lewis, and Corley, leaders of the local branch of the Meat Packing Workers Union, Parks, secretary of the local committee, Langs and McGrail of the Dining Car and Railway Workers Union, Daimeo, chairman of the local branch of the United Electrical Workers Union, Markowski, member of the local executive committee of the International Ladies' Garment Workers Union, and Lucas, chairman of the local branch of the International Longshoremen's and Warehousemen's Union and member of the union's executive committee.

### 美国芝加哥工会领袖要求立即撤退朝鲜美军

1950-07-11
第1版()
专栏：

　　美国芝加哥工会领袖要求立即撤退朝鲜美军
    【新华社十日讯】塔斯社纽约八日电：工人日报芝加哥消息称，芝加哥工会领袖们已经在芝加哥劳工和平会议发出的立即撤退朝鲜美军的呼吁书上签名。签名者有肉食包装工会地方分会领袖卡利、吉尔摩尔、路易斯和柯利门，地方委员会书记派克斯，餐车和铁路工会的朗氏和麦克盖尔，联合电气工会地方分会主席戴美欧，国际妇女服装工会地方执行委员会委员马考斯基，国际码头工人和仓库工人工会地方分会主席兼该工会执行委员会委员鲁卡斯。
