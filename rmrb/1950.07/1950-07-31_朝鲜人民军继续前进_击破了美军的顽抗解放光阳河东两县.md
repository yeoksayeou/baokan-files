Title: Korean People's Army Continues to Advance, Overcoming American Resistance to Liberate Gwangyang and Hadong Counties
Author:
Date: 1950-07-31
Page: 1

　　Korean People's Army Continues to Advance
    Overcoming American Resistance to Liberate Gwangyang and Hadong Counties
    [Xinhua News Agency, Pyongyang, 30th] The General Headquarters of the Korean People's Army of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea announced in a war report on the morning of the 29th that the People's Army units at the front continued to advance in fierce warfare against the American troops.

    The People's Army, having liberated Suncheon and Yeosu, captured Gwangyang County in South Jeolla Province on the 28th after overcoming the stubborn resistance of the American troops.

    American air force squadrons carried out frantic and repeated bombings of Pyongyang on the 27th and 28th, causing extensive damage to many residences and buildings. Many citizens were killed and wounded.

    An American aircraft, damaged by the ground forces of the People's Army on the 27th in a certain area of the southern half, was forced to land at Daejeon Airport, and all the enemies inside were captured.
    [Xinhua News Agency, Pyongyang, 30th] The General Headquarters of the Korean People's Army of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea announced in a war report on the evening of the 29th that the People's Army troops continued to engage in fierce battles with the American troops. The People's Army troops attacking towards Hadong in South Gyeongsang Province annihilated the American troops attempting to resist stubbornly, and completely liberated Hadong County on the 28th. The People's Army inflicted heavy blows on the enemy in the battle of Hadong, and captured many spoils of war.

### 朝鲜人民军继续前进  击破了美军的顽抗解放光阳河东两县

1950-07-31
第1版()
专栏：

　　朝鲜人民军继续前进
    击破了美军的顽抗解放光阳河东两县
    【新华社平壤三十日电】朝鲜民主主义人民共和国人民军总司令部二十九日晨发表战报称：前线人民军各部在对美军展开强烈的战争中继续前进。
    解放顺天、丽水的人民军，在击破美军的顽抗后，于二十八日攻克了全罗南道的光阳县。
    美空军编队于二十七、八两日对平壤进行疯狂的轮番轰炸，致使极多的住宅与建筑物遭到破坏。市民死伤甚众。
    二十七日在南半部某地被人民军地面部队击伤的美机一架，被迫降落在大田机场，机内敌人全部被俘。
    【新华社平壤三十日电】朝鲜民主主义人民共和国人民军总司令部二十九日晚发表战报称：人民军部队继续和美军部队展开猛烈战斗。向庆尚南道河东方面进攻的人民军部队击灭了企图顽抗的美军部队后，二十八日完全解放了河东县。人民军在河东的战斗中予敌军以沉重打击，缴获战利品甚多。
