Title: International Student Union Calls for Support of the All-China Youth Federation's Proposal to Hold a Day to Ban Atomic Bombs
Author:
Date: 1950-07-31
Page: 4

　　Xinhua News Agency, Prague, July 26th, dispatch from Telegraph Agency: The Secretariat of the International Student Union, after studying the current world peace movement at a recent meeting, decided that students worldwide should use all means of propaganda to expose those professors, student leaders, and others who refuse to sign the Stockholm Appeal. Outstanding student workers who support the Stockholm Appeal will receive peace scholarships from the International Student Union. The International Student Union has notified all national student delegations participating in the Second World Student Congress to be held in Prague in August to report to the Congress on the latest developments in the peace signature campaign in their respective countries. The International Student Union also calls on students to do their best to support the proposal of the World Federation of Democratic Youth to hold a day for striving for peace and banning atomic bombs on August 6th—the fifth anniversary of the United States dropping the first atomic bomb on Hiroshima, Japan, in 1945.

### 国际学联号召支持青联建议  举行禁用原子弹日

1950-07-31
第4版()
专栏：

　　国际学联号召支持青联建议
    举行禁用原子弹日
    【新华社三十日讯】电通社布拉格二十六日讯：国际学生联合会书记处最近的一次会议，在研究了当前的世界和平运动以后，决定全世界学生应利用一切宣传方法，揭露那些拒绝在斯德哥尔摩宣言上签名的教授、学生领袖及其他的人们。拥护斯德哥尔摩宣言的杰出的学生工作者，将获得国际学联的和平奖学金。国际学联已通知所有参加八月间在布拉格举行的第二次世界学生代表大会的各国学生代表团，向大会报告各该国和平签名运动的最近情况。国际学联并号召学生尽力支持世界民主青年联盟的建议，在八月六日——一九四五年美国在日本广岛投第一颗原子弹的五周年纪念日，举行争取和平、禁用原子弹日。
