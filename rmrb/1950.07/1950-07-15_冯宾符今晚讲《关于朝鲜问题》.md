Broadcast Program

　　Feng Binfu to Lecture on "The Korean Question" Tonight
    The New Democracy Lecture Series, organized by the YMCA of this city, will specially invite Mr. Feng Binfu, editor-in-chief of World Knowledge Press, to lecture on "The Korean Question" at 7:30 PM on Saturday, July 15th. All are welcome to attend.
