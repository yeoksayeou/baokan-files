Title: Overseas Chinese Association of Bandung, Java Telegraphed Respects to Chairman Mao and Commander-in-Chief Zhu, Hoping to Expedite the Liberation of Taiwan and Tibet
Author:
Date: 1950-07-21
Page: 1

　　Xinhua News Agency, 19th: It is learned here that the 12th Member Congress of the Overseas Chinese Association of Bandung, Java, passed a resolution on July 1st to send a telegram of respect to Chairman Mao, Commander-in-Chief Zhu, and the People's Liberation Army, and respectfully requested that the frontline soldiers be instructed to expedite the liberation of Taiwan and Tibet, in order to comfort the hopes of the overseas Chinese.

### 爪哇万隆华侨公会  电毛主席朱总司令致敬  希望加紧解放台湾西藏

1950-07-21
第1版()
专栏：

　　爪哇万隆华侨公会
    电毛主席朱总司令致敬
    希望加紧解放台湾西藏
    【新华社十九日讯】此间获悉：爪哇万隆华侨公会第十二次会员大会，曾于七月一日通过致电毛主席、朱总司令及人民解放军致敬，并恳请转饬前线战士，加紧解放台湾、西藏，以慰侨望。
