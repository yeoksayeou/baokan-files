Title: Kim Il-sung Issues Order Congratulating the Liberation of Daejeon
Author:
Date: 1950-07-26
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Pyongyang, July 25th: Kim Il-sung, Supreme Commander of the Korean People's Army, issued an order on the 24th stating: The units of the People's Army fighting in the Daejeon area, at 12:00 on July 20th, broke through the strong defense line of the U.S. ground forces and the remaining troops of the Syngman Rhee puppet army, and liberated Daejeon City, an important military strategic stronghold and political and administrative center of the enemy.

In the battle to liberate Daejeon, the People's Army units completely annihilated the U.S. 24th Infantry Division and the remnants of the Syngman Rhee puppet army's 1st and 7th Divisions, capturing hundreds of enemy soldiers and seizing a large number of various spoils of war.

In the battle to liberate Daejeon, the units commanded by Comrades Kim Ung, Li Xun, Liu Jingshu, and Li Quanwu made outstanding contributions. In the name of the Military Committee and the Supreme Command, I thank the soldiers, non-commissioned officers, and commanders of the People's Army who demonstrated noble patriotism and boundless heroism for the motherland and the people and achieved glorious victory in the battle to liberate Daejeon! Glory belongs to the officers and soldiers of the People's Army who bravely sacrificed themselves in the battle for the unification and independence of the motherland! Glory belongs to the heroic entire People's Army! Advance to eliminate and expel the U.S. military bandits from the territory of our motherland!

### 金日成发布命令  祝贺解放大田

1950-07-26
第1版()
专栏：

　　金日成发布命令
    祝贺解放大田
    【新华社平壤二十五日电】朝鲜人民军最高司令官金日成将军二十四日发布命令称：在大田方面作战的人民军各部，于七月二十日十二时突破美军地面部队与李承晚残余伪军的强固防线，解放了敌人重要的军事战略据点与政治行政的中心——大田市。
    人民军各部在解放大田的战斗中，完全歼灭了美军第二十四师团与李承晚伪军第一师团及第七师团的残余部队，俘虏敌军数百名，缴获各种战利品极多。
    在解放大田的战斗中，金雄、李勋、柳京洙、李权武等同志所指挥的部队，功勋卓著。我以军事委员会和最高司令部的名义，向在解放大田的战斗中发挥了为祖国与人民的高尚的爱国主义和无限英勇性而取得光辉胜利的人民军战士、下士军官及指挥官致谢！光荣属于在为祖国统一独立的战斗中英勇牺牲的人民军官兵！光荣属于英勇的全体人民军队！要为从我祖国疆土上消灭并驱逐美军强盗而向前挺进！
