Title: All-China Federation of Trade Unions Issues Notice Soliciting Opinions on Implementing the Trade Union Law
Author:
Date: 1950-07-05
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, July 4th: The All-China Federation of Trade Unions, in order to formulate a document on matters needing attention when implementing the Trade Union Law as a basis for trade union organizations in various regions and industries to implement the Trade Union Law, issued a notice on the 3rd, soliciting opinions from local trade union organizations. The notice requires trade union organizations in various regions and industries to promptly report to the All-China Federation of Trade Unions any difficulties and unclear points in the implementation of the Trade Union Law.

### 全国总工会发出通知征求实施工会法意见

1950-07-05
第1版()
专栏：

　　全国总工会发出通知征求实施工会法意见
    【新华社四日讯】中华全国总工会为拟制实施工会法应注意事项的文件，作为各地区与各产业工会组织实施工会法的依据，于三日发出通知，向各地工会组织征求意见。通知要求各地区与各产业工会组织将实施工会法中的困难与不明了处，迅速报告全国总工会。
