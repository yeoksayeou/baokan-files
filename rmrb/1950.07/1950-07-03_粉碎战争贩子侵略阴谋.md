Title: Smash the Warhawks' Aggression Conspiracy
Author: Xǔ Déhéng
Date: 1950-07-03
Page: 5

　　Smash the Warhawks' Aggression Conspiracy
　　Xu Deheng

　　Respond to the call to ban atomic weapons, actively launch the peace signature campaign, and use the immense power of our peace-loving people around the world to smash the aggression conspiracy of the warhawks headed by American imperialism.

### 粉碎战争贩子侵略阴谋
许德珩
1950-07-03
第5版()
专栏：

　　粉碎战争贩子侵略阴谋
    许德珩
    响应禁用原子武器的号召，积极的展开和平签名运动，用我们全世界爱好和平的人民巨大无比的力量，粉碎以美帝为首的战争贩子的侵略阴谋。
