Title: Soviets Prepare to Celebrate Aviation Day; Moscow to Hold Aviation Review
Author:
Date: 1950-07-15
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency, Moscow: The Soviet people will celebrate Aviation Day on the 16th. Various enterprises, institutions, collective farms, and state farms are preparing reports and speeches recounting the role of Lenin and Stalin in establishing Soviet aviation, the advanced role of the Soviet Union in developing aviation, and the glorious history of Stalin's eagles in the Great Patriotic War. Working people in many places will meet heroic Soviet pilots.

The Aviation Day celebration assembly is scheduled to be held on the 15th at the Green Theater of the Central Culture and Recreation Park in Moscow. The most exciting program of the festival is the aviation review to be held at Tushino Airfield on the 16th. The review program includes three parts: 1. Athletes from the All-Union Voluntary Society for Assisting the Development of Aviation demonstrate flight techniques. 2. Air Force performance. 3. Parachute jump performance.

### 苏筹备庆祝航空节  莫斯科将举行航空检阅

1950-07-15
第4版()
专栏：

　　苏筹备庆祝航空节
    莫斯科将举行航空检阅
    【新华社十四日讯】塔斯社莫斯科讯：苏联人民将在十六日庆祝航空节。各企业、机关、集体农场与国家农场，正筹备各种报告与演讲，讲述列宁和斯大林在建立苏联航空事业上所起的作用和苏联在发展航空事业中的先进作用，并讲述斯大林之鹰在伟大的爱国战争中的光荣史迹。劳动人民将在许多地方会晤英勇的苏联飞行员。
    航空节的庆祝大会定于十五日在莫斯科的中央文化与休憩公园绿荫剧院举行。节日的最精彩节目是十六日在土希诺飞机场举行的航空检阅。检阅节目包括三部分：一、全苏发展航空事业志愿协会的运动员表演飞行技术。二、空军表演。三、跳伞表演。
