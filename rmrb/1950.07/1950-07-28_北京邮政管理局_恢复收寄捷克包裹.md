Title: Beijing Postal Administration Resumes Accepting Parcels to Czechoslovakia
Author:
Date: 1950-07-28
Page: 6

　　Beijing Postal Administration
    Resumes Accepting Parcels to Czechoslovakia
    The Beijing Postal Administration, upon order, will resume accepting parcels to Czechoslovakia starting from July 27th. Parcel rates are set as follows:
    For parcels sent from postal offices in Northeast China, Beijing, Tianjin, Shanghai, Dihua, Shufu, Tacheng, Yili, and other locations, the rate is 54,000 RMB per item not exceeding one kilogram, 75,000 RMB for over one kilogram but not exceeding three kilograms, 140,000 RMB for over three kilograms but not exceeding five kilograms, and 260,000 RMB for over five kilograms but not exceeding ten kilograms. For parcels sent from postal offices in other locations, the rate is 66,000 RMB per item not exceeding one kilogram, 88,000 RMB for over one kilogram but not exceeding three kilograms, 160,000 RMB for over three kilograms but not exceeding five kilograms, and 300,000 RMB for over five kilograms but not exceeding ten kilograms.
    The dimensions of each parcel must not exceed one and a half meters on any side, and the volume must not exceed three meters.
                            (Social Service Section of the Beijing Postal Administration)

### 北京邮政管理局  恢复收寄捷克包裹

1950-07-28
第6版()
专栏：

　　北京邮政管理局
    恢复收寄捷克包裹
    北京邮政管理局奉令自七月二十七日起恢复收寄捷克包裹，包裹资费订定如下：
    由东北各地及北京、天津、上海、迪化、疏附、塔城、伊犁等地邮局收寄者，每件重不逾一公斤人民币五万四千元，一公斤以上不逾三公斤七万五千元，三公斤以上不逾五公斤十四万元，五公斤以上不逾十公斤二十六万元。其他各地邮局收寄者：每件重不逾一公斤六万六千元。一公斤以上不逾三公斤八万八千元，三公斤以上不逾五公斤十六万元，五公斤以上不逾十公斤三十万元。
    每件包裹尺寸任何一面不得超过一公尺半，体积不得超过三公尺。
                            （京邮政管理局社会服务股）
