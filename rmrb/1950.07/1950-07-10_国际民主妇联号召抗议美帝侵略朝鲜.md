Title: International Democratic Women's Federation Calls for Protest Against U.S. Imperialist Aggression in Korea
Author:
Date: 1950-07-10
Page: 1

　　International Democratic Women's Federation Calls for Protest Against U.S. Imperialist Aggression in Korea
    [Xinhua News Agency, July 9th] TASS, Paris, 8th: The International Democratic Women's Federation has received a telegram from the Korean Democratic Women's League regarding the American aggression in Korea. The Korean women solemnly guarantee that the armed intervention of the American imperialists will never prevent them from fighting for the unification and independence of their motherland.
    The International Democratic Women's Federation has forwarded this telegram to women's organizations in various countries, urging them to quickly organize representative conferences, meetings, and mass rallies to protest against American intervention in Korea, urging them to send delegations to the American embassies and consulates in their respective countries to lodge protests, to send delegations to resolutely demand that their governments submit protests to the United Nations against American aggression in Korea, and to intensify the signature campaign for the Stockholm Appeal.

### 国际民主妇联号召抗议美帝侵略朝鲜

1950-07-10
第1版()
专栏：

　　国际民主妇联号召抗议美帝侵略朝鲜
    【新华社九日讯】塔斯社巴黎八日讯：国际民主妇女联合会已收到朝鲜民主妇女总同盟发来的关于美国侵略朝鲜的电报。朝鲜妇女郑重保证：美帝国主义者的武装干涉，决阻止不了她们为祖国的统一与独立所进行的斗争。
    国际民主妇联已把这个电报转发所属各国妇女团体，敦促它们迅速组织代表大会、会议及群众大会，以抗议美国在朝鲜的干涉，敦促它们派遣代表团到驻在本国的美国大使馆及领事馆去提出抗议，派遣代表团坚决要求本国政府向联合国提出反对美国侵略朝鲜的抗议，并加紧进行关于斯德哥尔摩宣言的签名运动。
