Title: Beijing Municipal Consultative Committee Holds Seventh Meeting, Hearing Various Work Reports
Author:
Date: 1950-07-31
Page: 1

Beijing News Agency Report: The Beijing Municipal People's Representative Conference Consultative Committee held its seventh meeting at 3:00 PM on the 30th in the conference room of the committee at Zhongshan Park. Thirty-two committee members were present. The meeting was presided over by Peng Zhen, Chairman of the Consultative Committee. The meeting listened to and discussed the municipal government's semi-annual work report to the Second Session of the Third Beijing Municipal People's Representative Conference, and adopted the "List of Changes in Representatives of Various Units of the Second Session of the Third Beijing Municipal People's Representative Conference." It was also decided that another Consultative Committee meeting would be held on the 3rd of next month to hear and discuss the municipal government's report on financial and economic work for the past six months and the Consultative Committee's report on relieving unemployed workers and the district People's Representative Conference.

　　At the meeting, Deputy Mayor Zhang Youyu made a report on the "Municipal Government's Implementation of the 1950 Work Plan," Deputy Mayor Wu Han made a report on the "Implementation of the 1950 Amateur Education, Culture, Education, and Health Work Plan," and Secretary-General Xue Zizheng, Director of the Suburban Work Committee Chai Zemin, and President of the Municipal People's Court Wang Feiran made reports on the "Implementation of the 1950 Municipal Construction Plan," "Handling of the Fuhua Mining and Pharmaceutical Factory Explosion," "Land Reform, Agricultural Production, and Production Disaster Relief Work," and "The Court's Work on Clearing Backlogged Cases," respectively. The meeting held a preliminary discussion on these reports. Those who spoke successively included: Yu Xinqing, Vice-Chairman of the Consultative Committee, Qian Duansheng, and members Chen Mingde, Fu Huating, Liu Yifeng, Zhu Futang, Zhang Yunchuan, and Gu Qizong. They unanimously agreed that Beijing had made great achievements in its construction work in the past six months, fully demonstrating the great power of the People's Government and the people. At the same time, they put forward some suggestions on municipal construction, culture, education, and health work.

### 京市协商委员会举行第七次会议  听取各项工作报告

1950-07-31
第1版()
专栏：

　　京市协商委员会举行第七次会议
    听取各项工作报告
    【京市新闻处讯】北京市各界人民代表会议协商委员会于三十日下午三时在中山公园该会会议室举行第七次会议。出席委员三十二人。会议由协商委员会彭真主席主持，听取并讨论了市人民政府准备向京市第二届第三次各界人民代表会议所做的半年工作报告，通过了“北京市第二届第三次各界人民代表会议各单位代表变动名单”，并决定于下月三日再举行一次协商委员会议，以便听取与讨论市政府半年来财经工作的报告和协商委员会关于救济失业员工、区各界人民代表会议的报告。
    本次会议由张友渔副市长作了关于《市政府执行一九五○年度工作计划》的报告，吴晗副市长作了关于《执行一九五○年度业余教育、文教卫生工作计划》的报告，并由薛子正秘书长、郊区工作委员会柴泽民主任、市人民法院王斐然院长分别作了关于《执行一九五○年市政建设计划》、《辅华矿药厂爆炸事件的处理》、《土地改革、农业生产、生产救灾工作》及《法院清理积案工作》等报告，会议对这几项报告做了初步的讨论，相继发言的有：协商委员会副主席余心清、钱端升及委员陈铭德、傅华亭、刘一峰、诸福棠、张云川、古奇踪等，一致认为北京市这半年来的建设工作有很大成绩，充分表现了人民政府与人民伟大的力量。同时对市政建设、文教卫生等工作，提出了一些建议。
