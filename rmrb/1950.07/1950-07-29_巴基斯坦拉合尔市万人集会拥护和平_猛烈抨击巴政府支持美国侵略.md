Title: Lahore, Pakistan Holds Mass Rally Supporting Peace, Vigorously Condemning the Pakistani Government's Support for American Aggression
Author:
Date: 1950-07-29
Page: 4

　　TASS, Karachi, 26th: According to the "Pakistan Times", ten thousand people participated in a rally hosted by the Lahore Peace Committee. Political leaders, writers, journalists, farmers and lawyers who spoke at the meeting urged the Pakistani people to fight to maintain world peace.

　　Ahmed Faiz, Vice-Chairman of the Pakistan Trade Union Federation and Secretary of the Lahore Peace Committee, said at the meeting: "It is the sacred duty of everyone to fight for peace, to save mankind and civilization from destruction and extinction. All people with different political opinions must reach a common goal of stopping the war."

　　Hayat Khan, a member of the Pakistan Constituent Assembly, strongly condemned the Pakistani government for supporting the Security Council's illegal resolution on the Korean issue without obtaining the consent of the Pakistani people.

### 巴基斯坦拉合尔市万人集会拥护和平  猛烈抨击巴政府支持美国侵略

1950-07-29
第4版()
专栏：

　　巴基斯坦拉合尔市万人集会拥护和平
    猛烈抨击巴政府支持美国侵略
    【新华社二十八日讯】塔斯社喀喇蚩二十六日讯：据《巴基斯坦时报》消息：有一万人参加了拉合尔市和平委员会所主持的大会。在大会上发言的政治领袖、作家、新闻记者、农民和律师们，敦促巴基斯坦人民为保持世界和平而斗争。
    巴基斯坦工会联合会副主席、拉合尔和平委员会秘书阿默德·腓兹在会上说：“为和平，为拯救人类和文明免于破坏和灭亡而进行斗争，是每一个人的神圣天职。一切政治上意见不同的人们，必须达成阻止战争的共同目的。”
    巴基斯坦制宪议会议员海阿特·汗，猛烈抨击巴基斯坦政府没有取得巴基斯坦人民的同意，就擅自支持安全理事会关于朝鲜问题的非法决议。
