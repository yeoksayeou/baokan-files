Title: Minister of Water Resources Fu Zuoyi and Others Complete Survey of Yellow River Reservoir Sites; Construction to Begin on One Site Soon
Author:
Date: 1950-07-25
Page: 2

　　Xinhua News Agency, Kaifeng, 23rd -- Minister of Water Resources of the Central People's Government, Fu Zuoyi, Vice Minister Zhang Hanying, Deputy Director of the Yellow River Water Conservancy Commission, Zhao Mingfu, along with more than sixty water conservancy and geology experts, successfully completed the survey of reservoir sites, dam sites, and irrigation and Jiwei (relief of the Wei River) canal locations in the Tongguan-Mengjin section of the Yellow River from July 1st to 14th. Among the five reservoir and dam sites surveyed on the Yellow River, construction will begin on one site soon to achieve the purposes of flood storage, disaster prevention, and promotion of benefits. The Yellow River irrigation and Jiwei projects have also been surveyed, and it has been decided to build a gate on the northwest bank of the Yellow River Iron Bridge to divert water. This project will begin soon and is expected to be completed next year.

### 水利部傅作义部长等  勘查黄河蓄水库址完毕  有一处即将开始修建

1950-07-25
第2版()
专栏：

　　水利部傅作义部长等
    勘查黄河蓄水库址完毕       
    有一处即将开始修建
    【新华社开封二十三日电】中央人民政府水利部傅作义部长、张含英副部长、黄河水利委员会赵明甫副主任，偕同水利、地质专家等一行六十余人，已于七月一日至十四日胜利完成黄河潼关至孟津段的蓄水库址、坝址和引黄灌溉、济卫的渠道位置的勘查工作。经过勘查的黄河五个库址和坝址中，有一处即将开始修建，以达蓄洪防患兴利的目的。引黄灌溉、济卫工程亦经查勘，确定于黄河铁桥以西北岸筑闸引水。此一工程即将施工，预计明年完成。
