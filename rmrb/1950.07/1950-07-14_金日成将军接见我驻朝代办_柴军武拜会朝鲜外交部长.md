Title: General Kim Il-sung Receives Chinese Chargé d'Affaires; Chai Junwu Pays Courtesy Visit to Korean Foreign Minister
Author:
Date: 1950-07-14
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Pyongyang, 13th. Kim Il-sung, Chairman of the Cabinet of Ministers of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea, received Chai Junwu, Chargé d'Affaires of the Chinese Embassy in Korea, at 8:00 p.m. on the 11th. Park Dong-chu, Vice Minister of Foreign Affairs of Korea, was also present.
Xinhua News Agency, Pyongyang, 12th. Chai Junwu, Chargé d'Affaires of the Chinese Embassy in Korea, accompanied by Counselor Ni Weiting, paid his first courtesy visit to Korean Foreign Minister Park Heon-yeong at 5:00 p.m. on the 10th. Park Dong-chu, Vice Minister of Foreign Affairs of Korea, was also present.

### 金日成将军接见我驻朝代办  柴军武拜会朝鲜外交部长

1950-07-14
第1版()
专栏：

　　金日成将军接见我驻朝代办
    柴军武拜会朝鲜外交部长
    【新华社平壤十三日电】朝鲜民主主义人民共和国部长会议主席金日成将军，于十一日晚八时接见我国驻朝鲜大使馆代办柴军武。在座者尚有朝鲜外交部副部长朴东楚。
    【新华社平壤十二日电】我国驻朝鲜大使馆代办柴军武，于十日下午五时偕参赞倪蔚庭，首次拜会朝鲜外交部部长朴宪永。在座者尚有朝鲜外交部副部长朴东楚。
