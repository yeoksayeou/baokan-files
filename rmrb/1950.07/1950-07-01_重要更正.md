　　Important Correction

　　In the editorial "Strive to Realize Land Reform Throughout China" published on page two of this newspaper on June 30th, in the ninth line of the fourth column, "没收" (confiscate) should be "征收" (requisition), and the first character "使" (make) in the eighteenth line should be "便" (then).

### 重要更正

1950-07-01
第3版()
专栏：

　　重要更正
    六月三十日本报第二版所载社论《为实现全中国土地改革而斗争》内，第四栏第九行中“没收”应为“征收”，第十八行第一字“使”字应为“便”字。
