Title: Soviet-Polish Sign New Agreement: Trade Volume to Increase by Sixty Percent Next Year; Soviet Loan of 400 Million Rubles for Polish Industrial Equipment
Author:
Date: 1950-07-14
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, July 7th: The Soviet and Polish governments signed a protocol in Moscow on June 29th, regarding increasing the trade volume between the two countries in 1951 and 1952, a barter agreement for 1953 to 1958, and an agreement for the Soviet Union to provide Poland with industrial equipment on credit from 1951 to 1958.

The Soviet Union has agreed to lend the Polish Republic 400 million rubles as partial payment for Polish industrial equipment deliveries.

Due to the signing of the new agreement, the average annual trade volume between the Soviet Union and Poland from 1951 to 1958 is expected to increase by 60 percent compared to the actual average annual trade volume from 1948 to 1950.

### 苏波签订新协定  明年贸易额提高百分之六十  苏贷与波工业装备四亿卢布

1950-07-14
第4版()
专栏：

　　苏波签订新协定
    明年贸易额提高百分之六十  苏贷与波工业装备四亿卢布
    【新华社七日讯】据塔斯社莫斯科讯：苏联与波兰两国政府，已于六月二十九日在莫斯科签订了增加两国在一九五一年和一九五二年贸易额的议定书，和一九五三年至一九五八年的易货协定，以及一九五一年至一九五八年由苏联以工业装备贷与波兰的协定。
    苏联已同意贷与波兰共和国四亿卢布，作为波兰偿付工业装备交货的一部分费用。
    由于新协定的签订，预计苏波两国间一九五一年至一九五八年每年平均贸易额，将较一九四八年至一九五○年每年实际平均贸易额增加百分之六十。
