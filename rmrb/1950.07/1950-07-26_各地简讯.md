　　Local Briefs
　　Guangdong College and University Delegation Goes North for Visits
　　Under the leadership of the Cultural and Educational Department of the Guangdong Provincial People's Government, a delegation of 145 people from five institutions, including Guangdong College of Law and Commerce, College of Arts and Sciences, Private Lingnan University, National University, and College of Traditional Chinese Medicine, has arrived in Beijing on the 19th of this month for a northern tour. After visiting Renmin University of China, North China Revolutionary University, Peking University, Tsinghua University, Beijing Library and other places in Beijing, they will go to the Northeast for visits.
　　　　　　　　　　　　　(Guangdong College and University Northern Visit Delegation)
　　The Shanxi Provincial Government's Department of Culture and Education Holds Art Training Class
　　In order to cultivate rural art cadres and carry out rural recreational activities, the Department of Culture and Education of the Shanxi Provincial People's Government has held an art training class. There were a total of 259 students, most of whom were art workers recommended by various special districts. The class ended on the 8th of this month, and they have now been assigned to various places to engage in rural recreational work.
　　　　　　　　　　　　(Department of Culture and Education of Shanxi Provincial People's Government)
　　National Advanced Technical School Summer Internship Begins
　　More than 240 students from the Mechanical, Electrical, Metallurgical, and Mining Departments of the National Advanced Industrial School have gone to the 70th Arsenal, Beijing Tram Company, Shijingshan Iron and Steel Plant, Tianjin Machine Factory, Kailuan Coal Mine and other factories and mines on the 17th and 18th of this month for summer internships. The maximum internship period is two months.
　　　　　　　　　　　　(Zhu Guisen)

### 各地简讯
朱桂森
1950-07-26
第3版()
专栏：

　　各地简讯
    广东专科以上学校组参观团北上参观
    广东省人民政府文教厅领导下由广东省立法商学院、文理学院、私立岭南大学、国民大学、中医专校等五个单位组成的广东省专科以上学校北上参观团一行一百四十五人，已于本月十九日抵达北京。在京市参观中国人民大学、华北革大、北京大学、清华大学、北京图书馆等地后，转赴东北参观。
                      （广东省专上学校北上参观团）
    山西省府文教厅举办艺术训练班
    山西省人民政府文教厅为培养乡村文艺干部，以开展乡村文娱活动，特举办了艺术训练班。学员共二百五十九名，大部为各专区保送的文艺工作者。该班已于本月八日结束，现均已分发各地从事乡村文娱工作。
                              （山西省人民政府文教厅）
    国立高级工校暑期实习开始
    国立高级工业学校，机械、电机、冶金、采矿等系同学二百四十余人，已于本月十七、十八两日分赴七十兵工厂，北京电车公司，石景山钢铁厂，天津机器厂，开滦煤矿等厂矿作暑期实习。实习时间最多者为两月。
                          （朱桂森）
