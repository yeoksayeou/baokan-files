Title: South Korean Socialist Party Leader, Cho So-ang, Defects to the People
Author:
Date: 1950-07-17
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Pyongyang, July 14th: Cho So-ang, the leader of the Socialist Party in South Korea who formerly advocated for a "middle road," and a new member of the puppet National Assembly, came to Pyongyang shortly after the liberation of Seoul. On the evening of the 12th, he delivered a broadcast speech on the Korean Central Broadcasting Station. After reviewing his past mistakes, he expressed his willingness to lead all members of the Socialist Party in opposing the Li Seung-man bandit gang and the American imperialist aggression, and to fight for the freedom and independence of the motherland. He called on the puppet National Assembly members in South Korea to stand on the side of the people and participate in the just struggle to win the unification and independence of the motherland.

### 南朝鲜社会党领袖  赵素昂投向人民

1950-07-17
第4版()
专栏：

　　南朝鲜社会党领袖
    赵素昂投向人民
    【新华社平壤十四日电】原在南朝鲜提倡“中间路线”的社会党领袖、新的伪国会议员赵素昂，在汉城解放后不久来到平壤，并于十二日晚在朝鲜中央广播电台发表广播演说。他在检讨了自己过去的错误之后，表示愿率领社会党全体党员反对李承晚匪帮与美帝国主义的侵略，为祖国的自由独立而奋斗。他号召南朝鲜的伪国会议员们站到人民方面来，参加争取祖国统一独立的正义斗争。
