Title: New York Labor Peace Council Launches Million Signature Campaign and Will Hold Peace Demonstration on August 2nd
Author:
Date: 1950-07-30
Page: 4
　　New York Labor Peace Council
    Launches Million Signature Campaign
    and Will Hold Peace Demonstration on August 2nd
    [Xinhua News Agency, 21st Dispatch] TASS, New York: The New York branch of the American National Labor Peace Conference has decided to launch a broad campaign starting July 20th to collect signatures for the Stockholm Peace Appeal. The branch has set itself the task of collecting one million signatures before August 2nd. On August 2nd, a peace demonstration will be held in Union Square, New York. The branch has received many letters from representatives of all walks of life expressing their willingness to assist in the signature campaign.
    The Workers Daily pointed out: Despite the terror implemented by the police in an attempt to obstruct the peace movement, and despite the arrest of many people collecting signatures on the Stockholm Peace Appeal, the movement for peace is still expanding in the United States.

### 纽约劳工和平分会  发动百万人签名运动  并将于八月二日举行和平示威

1950-07-30
第4版()
专栏：

　　纽约劳工和平分会
    发动百万人签名运动
    并将于八月二日举行和平示威
    【新华社二十一日讯】塔斯社纽约讯：美国全国劳工和平会议纽约分会决定自七月二十日起展开广泛的运动，为斯德哥尔摩和平宣言征集签名。分会为自己规定一项任务，要在八月二日前征集一百万人的签名。在八月二日，将在纽约联合广场举行和平示威。该分会接到各阶层居民代表的许多来信，他们都表示愿意协助进行签名运动。
    工人日报指出：尽管警察实行恐怖，力图阻挠和平运动，尽管有许多征集在斯德哥尔摩和平宣言上签名的人被捕，但争取和平的运动在美国仍在扩大中。
