Title: Northwest Nationalities Joyfully Celebrate "Eid al-Fitr"
Author:
Date: 1950-07-22
Page: 3

Xinhua News Agency, Xi'an, 20th—The Uyghur, Hui, Kazakh, Uzbek, Tatar, Tajik, Kirgiz, Sala and other ethnic groups in the Northwest region who believe in Islam, celebrated the annual "Eid al-Fitr" (that is, the day of breaking the fast after the end of Ramadan, also known as Little New Year) on the 16th and 17th.

The Xi'an Municipal People's Government hosted a banquet during the festival to entertain the imams of various mosques and representatives of the Hui people from all walks of life, to express congratulations. More than 14,000 Hui people in the city sent gifts to each other to celebrate, and held celebrations in fourteen mosques. The ethnic committee members, youth, and student representatives from all ethnic groups who are currently attending the Northwest Military and Political Committee's Nationalities Affairs Committee and the Northwest Youth and Student Congress also attended. Imam Ma Zhijun of the Daxuexi Lane Mosque in the city called on everyone at the celebration to unite, defend world peace, and oppose wars of aggression.

Nearly 10,000 Hui people in Xining, the capital of Qinghai Province, solemnly celebrated "Eid al-Fitr" at the Dongguan Grand Mosque on that day, and prayed for lasting world peace. The number of Hui people participating in the celebration doubled compared to previous years. Responsible persons of the provincial and municipal people's governments, as well as representatives of the Han, Mongolian, Tibetan, Tu, and other ethnic groups, were invited to participate. Ma Letian (Hui nationality), Deputy Secretary-General of the Provincial People's Government, called on the Hui compatriots in his speech to strengthen unity with all fraternal ethnic groups, and strive to build a new Qinghai and defend lasting world peace.

People of all ethnic groups in Xinjiang Province who believe in Islam greeted the first "Eid al-Fitr" after liberation with joy. The vast rural areas and pastures of the Uyghur and Kazakh ethnic groups in southern and northern Xinjiang were filled with festive joy. The leaders of the Xinjiang Military Region sent a telegram to the commanders and fighters of the Fifth Army (formerly the national army of the three districts of Ashan, Tacheng, and Yili) to congratulate them, and distributed two catties of beef and mutton to each person.

### 西北各族人民欢度“尔得节”

1950-07-22
第3版()
专栏：

　　西北各族人民欢度“尔得节”
    【新华社西安二十日电】西北区信奉伊斯兰教的维吾尔族、回族、哈萨克族、乌兹别克族、塔塔尔族、塔吉克族、柯尔克斯族、萨拉族等各族人民，于十六、十七两日欢度一年一度的“尔得节”（即斋月期满开斋的日子，又称过小年）。
    西安市人民政府在节日中设宴招待各清真寺阿訇及各界回民代表，表示祝贺。全市一万四千余回民均相互送礼庆贺，并分别在十四个清真寺中举行庆祝典礼。现在西安出席西北军政委员会民族事务委员会、西北青年和学生代表大会的各族委员和各族青年、学生代表们，均前往参加。该市大学习巷清真寺的阿訇马志俊在典礼会上号召大家团结一心，保卫世界和平，反对侵略战争。
    青海省会西宁市回民近万人，是日在东关清真大寺隆重庆祝“尔得节”，并祈祷世界永久和平。参加庆祝的回民比往年增加了一倍。省、市人民政府负责人，及汉、蒙、藏、土各族代表人士均应邀参加。省人民政府副秘书长马乐天（回族）在讲话中号召回胞加强和各兄弟民族间的团结，为建设新青海和保卫世界永久和平而奋斗。
    新疆省各地信仰伊斯兰教的各族人民，均以愉快的心情迎接解放后的第一度“尔得节”。南、北疆维吾尔族、哈萨克族广大的农村中和牧场上，都充满了节日的欢腾。新疆军区首长并致电第五军指战员（原阿山、塔城、伊犁三区民族军）祝贺，并发给每人牛羊肉二斤。
