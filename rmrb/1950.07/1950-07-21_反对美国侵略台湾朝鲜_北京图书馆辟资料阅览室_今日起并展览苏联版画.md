Title: Oppose American Aggression in Taiwan and Korea: Beijing Library Opens Reading Room for Materials and Exhibits Soviet Prints Starting Today
Author: Chen Zhongdi
Date: 1950-07-21
Page: 6

　　Oppose American Aggression in Taiwan and Korea
    Beijing Library Opens Reading Room for Materials and Exhibits Soviet Prints Starting Today
    In order to cooperate with the development of the movement to oppose American aggression against Taiwan and Korea, the Beijing Library has decided to open a special reading room during this movement week to display relevant materials, so that the broad masses of people can further understand the crimes of American imperialism in invading Taiwan and Korea, and the great strength of the Chinese and Korean people in resisting American aggression.
    At the same time, the library will hold a Soviet print exhibition starting on the 21st. These exhibits were produced by the Department of Fine Arts of the Soviet Union's Foreign Cultural Association. They include more than 130 works in pencil, charcoal, colored pencil, watercolor, woodcut, lead plate, lithograph, linoleum cut, and watercolor. All circles are welcome to visit.
                            (Chen Zhongdi)

### 反对美国侵略台湾朝鲜  北京图书馆辟资料阅览室  今日起并展览苏联版画
陈仲递
1950-07-21
第6版()
专栏：

　　反对美国侵略台湾朝鲜
    北京图书馆辟资料阅览室   
    今日起并展览苏联版画
    北京图书馆为了配合反对美国侵略台湾和朝鲜运动的开展，决定在这运动周内特开辟一阅览室，陈列有关资料，供广大人民进一步认识美帝侵略台湾朝鲜的罪恶，和中国人民及朝鲜人民反抗美国侵略的伟大力量。
    同时该馆从二十一日起举行苏联版画展览。这些展览品是苏联对外文化协会造型艺术部所制成的。其中包括铅笔画、炭笔画、色铅笔画、淡彩画、木刻、铅版、石版、麻胶版、水彩画等一百三十余幅。欢迎各界前往参观。
                            （陈仲递）
