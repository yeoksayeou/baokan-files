Title: Japan Trade Union Liaison Council Issues Letter to Korean Workers: To Guarantee the Independence of Both Countries, We Are Determined to Launch a Joint Struggle with Concrete Actions

Author: 
Date: 1950-07-14
Page: 4

　　Xinhua News Agency, 13th dispatch: According to TASS's 12th dispatch: The Japanese labor front newspaper recently published the "Letter to Korean Workers" adopted by the Central Committee Meeting of the Japan Trade Union Liaison Council. The letter reads: The Japan Trade Union Liaison Council, representing 1.2 million organized workers, sends fraternal greetings to you who have risen up to fight for the unification and independence of your motherland. Your victory is beyond doubt, because your struggle is not only supported by the entire Korean people, but also by the democratic forces of the world.

　　The letter also stated: Fascist suppression has now been added to the heads of us Japanese workers, this suppression is similar to the suppression imposed on the Korean people by Japanese imperialists in the past. We have a common enemy. In coordination with your heroic struggle, we are launching a joint struggle with concrete actions. We guarantee to strengthen this struggle to thwart the imperialists' plans to launch wars and destroy peace, to guarantee the independence of our two countries, and to guarantee peace and prosperity.

### 日本工会联络协议会  发表告朝鲜工人书  为了保证两国的独立，决用具体的行动展开共同的斗争

1950-07-14
第4版()
专栏：

　　日本工会联络协议会
    发表告朝鲜工人书
    为了保证两国的独立，决用具体的行动展开共同的斗争
    【新华社十三日讯】据塔斯社十二日讯：日本劳动战线报顷发表日本工会联络协议会中央委员会会议所通过的《致朝鲜工人书》。该书写道：日本工会联络协议会代表一百二十万有组织的工人，向您们这些起来为自己的祖国的统一与独立而斗争的人们致兄弟的敬礼。您们的胜利是毫无疑问的，因为您们的斗争不但受到全体朝鲜人民的支持，而且受到全世界民主力量的支持。
    该书又说：法西斯的镇压现在已加到我们日本工人的头上，这种镇压和过去日本帝国主义者加于朝鲜人民的镇压相似。我们有着共同的敌人。配合着您们的英勇的斗争，我们正在用具体的行动展开共同的斗争。我们保证加强这种斗争，以挫败帝国主义者旨在发动战争、破坏和平的计划，以保证我们两国的独立，并保证和平与繁荣。
