Title: Visiting Soviet Union and Germany Delegation Returns to Beijing; Li Jishen, Guo Moruo, and Others Greet Them at the Station
Author:
Date: 1951-07-06
Page: 1

　　The Visiting Soviet Union and Germany Delegation Returns to Beijing
    Li Jishen, Guo Moruo, and Others Greet Them at the Station

    Xinhua News Agency, July 5th: The China-Soviet Friendship Association delegation that went to the Soviet Union to participate in the May Day celebrations and the Chinese People's delegation participating in the China-Germany Friendship Month returned to Beijing at 7:00 AM on the 5th. Those who went to the station to welcome them included Li Jishen, Guo Moruo, Zhang Lan, and Huang Yanpei, vice presidents of the China-Soviet Friendship Association; Yan Baohang, Lai Yali, and Zhao Zhongchi, deputy secretaries-general; Dong Biwu, Cai Tingkai, Zhang Bojun, Shi Liang, Peng Zemin, Zhu Xuefan, Wu Han, Liang Xi, and Xin Zhichao, directors; Peng Zhen and Chen Shutong, vice chairmen of the Chinese People's Anti-American Aggression and Aid Korea General Association; Liu Guanyi, secretary-general; Wu Xiuquan, vice minister of the Central People's Government Ministry of Foreign Affairs; Wang Bingnan, director of the General Office; and representatives of various organizations and groups in the capital, totaling more than 300 people.
    Soviet Ambassador to China Roshchin and Koenig, head of the diplomatic mission of the German Democratic Republic with the rank of ambassador, also went to the station to welcome them.
    After the delegates got off the train, they accepted flowers. Guo Moruo, vice president of the China-Soviet Friendship Association and chairman of the Chinese People's Anti-American Aggression and Aid Korea General Association, delivered a welcome speech. Subsequently, Shen Junru, deputy head of the China-Soviet Friendship Association delegation to the Soviet Union for the May Day celebrations and head of the Chinese People's delegation participating in the China-Germany Friendship Month, delivered a reply. He said: The delegation participated in the May Day celebrations in the Soviet Union, visited the Soviet Union's production and construction, and some delegates went to Berlin to participate in the founding meeting of the China-Germany Friendship Association, and were warmly welcomed by the people of the Soviet Union and Germany. This trip by the delegation greatly promoted the friendship between the Chinese, Soviet, and German people.
     Note: The delegation left Beijing for the Soviet Union on April 16th, and some members returned to China successively on June 3rd and 14th. The delegation now plans to widely disseminate their impressions of the Soviet Union and Germany.

### 访苏访德代表团返京    李济深郭沫若等赴站欢迎

1951-07-06
第1版()
专栏：

　　访苏访德代表团返京
    李济深郭沫若等赴站欢迎
    【新华社五日讯】中苏友好协会赴苏参加“五一”节观礼代表团、中国人民参加德中友好月代表团于五日晨七时返抵北京。到车站欢迎的有中苏友好协会总会副会长李济深、郭沫若、张澜、黄炎培，副总干事阎宝航、赖亚力、赵仲池，理事董必武、蔡廷锴、章伯钧、史良、彭泽民、朱学范、吴晗、梁希、辛志超，中国人民抗美援朝总会副主席彭真、陈叔通，秘书长刘贯一，中央人民政府外交部副部长伍修权、办公厅主任王炳南，以及首都各机关团体代表等共三百余人。
    苏联驻华大使罗申、德意志民主共和国外交使团大使衔团长柯尼希等亦到车站欢迎。
    代表团各代表下车后，接受献花。中苏友好协会总会副会长、中国人民抗美援朝总会主席郭沫若致欢迎词。继由中苏友好协会赴苏参加“五一”节观礼代表团副团长兼中国人民参加德中友好月代表团团长沈钧儒致答词。他说：代表团这次在苏联参加了“五一”节观礼，参观了苏联的生产建设，一部分代表并赴柏林参加了德中友好协会成立大会，受到苏联和德国人民的热烈欢迎。代表团此行，大大促进了中苏、中德人民的友谊。
     按：代表团在四月十六日离京赴苏，部分团员已于六月三日及十四日先后返国。现该代表团将计划把在苏联和德国的观感进行广泛传达。
