Title: General Kim Il-sung Replies to the Chinese People's Volunteers, Thanking Them for Their Congratulations on the First Anniversary of the Korean War of Liberation
Author:
Date: 1951-07-02
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, Korean Front, 1st: After all the commanders and fighters of the Chinese People's Volunteers sent a telegram to General Kim Il-sung and all the officers and soldiers of the Korean People's Army congratulating them on the first anniversary of the Korean People's Liberation War, General Kim Il-sung replied on June 26th to express his gratitude. The reply said: "In the past eight months of your participation in the war, you have assisted the Korean People's Army in bravely fighting, dealing fatal blows to the American, British, and other invading forces and the Li puppet army, achieving brilliant results, further consolidating the fraternal friendship between the Korean and Chinese peoples, and further inspiring the world's democratic camp with the confidence of inevitable victory. We are even more impressed by the lofty revolutionary spirit and exemplary actions you have shown in combining patriotism and internationalism. We hope that you will continue to work hard on the front line and fight alongside our Korean People's Army to continue to strike at the enemy with heroic battles. I believe that final victory will surely belong to us."

### 金日成将军复电中国志愿军  感谢对朝鲜解放战争一周年的祝贺

1951-07-02
第1版()
专栏：

　　金日成将军复电中国志愿军
    感谢对朝鲜解放战争一周年的祝贺
    【新华社朝鲜前线一日电】中国人民志愿军全体指挥员战斗员在朝鲜人民解放战争一周年时，电贺金日成将军和朝鲜人民军全体官兵以后，金日成将军在六月二十六日复电表示谢意。复电说：“你们在这八个月的参战中协助朝鲜人民军英勇作战，予美、英等侵略军及李伪军以致命的打击，得到辉煌的战果，更加巩固了朝、中人民间的兄弟友谊，更加鼓舞了世界民主阵营的必胜信心。你们所表现的爱国主义与国际主义互相结合的高度革命精神及模范行动，使我们更加钦佩。希望你们在前线再接再厉，与我朝鲜人民军共同作战，以英勇的战斗继续打击敌寇。我相信最后胜利一定属于我们的。”
