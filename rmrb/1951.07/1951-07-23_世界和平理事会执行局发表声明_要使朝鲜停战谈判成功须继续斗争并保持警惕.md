Title: World Peace Council Executive Bureau Issues Statement: To Ensure the Success of the Korean Armistice Negotiations, Struggle Must Continue and Vigilance Must Be Maintained
Author:
Date: 1951-07-23
Page: 1

　　Xinhua News Agency, Helsinki: The Executive Bureau of the World Peace Council, currently meeting in Helsinki, issued the following statement on the 21st:
    "The Executive Bureau of the World Peace Council, holding its regular meeting, congratulates the preliminary victory in the method of resolving the Korean issue, where the principle of negotiation has prevailed over the principle of force. The will of the people of all countries to strive for peace has made this contact possible; if the negotiations are to achieve a successful conclusion and the issue is to be resolved peacefully, unwavering struggle and vigilance must be maintained. In this way, the suffering of the Korean people, who have won their right to life at such a high price, can be ended, and the people of other countries, who are uneasy day and night, will understand that the conflicts that divide the world can find a peaceful solution."

### 世界和平理事会执行局发表声明  要使朝鲜停战谈判成功须继续斗争并保持警惕

1951-07-23
第1版()
专栏：

　　世界和平理事会执行局发表声明
　  要使朝鲜停战谈判成功
　  须继续斗争并保持警惕
    【新华社二十二日讯】塔斯社赫尔辛基讯：目前正在赫尔辛基举行会议的世界和平理事会执行局于二十一日发表声明如下：
    “在解决朝鲜问题的方法上，谈判的原则压倒了武力的原则，取得了初步的胜利，世界和平理事会执行局在举行例会的时候特向这个胜利表示庆贺。各国人民争取和平的意志已使这种接触得以建立；如果要使谈判得到成功的结局，使问题得到和平的解决，就必须进行始终不渝的斗争并保持警惕。这样，就可以结束以那样高的代价取得了他们的生活权利的朝鲜人民的苦难，而日夜不安的其他国家的人民也将懂得：使世界四分五裂的冲突是可以找到和平解决的办法的。”
