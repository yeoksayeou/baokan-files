Title: All-China Women's Federation and Beijing Women's Federation Hold Rally to Welcome Korean Delegation
Author:
Date: 1951-07-03
Page: 1

　　Xinhua News Agency, 29th: The All-China Democratic Women's Federation and the Beijing Democratic Women's Federation jointly held a rally at 9:00 a.m. on the 29th of this month to welcome the Korean People's Delegation to China. Participants included Cai Chang, Chairman of the All-China Women's Federation, Vice-Chairman Xu Guangping, Zhang Xiaomei, Chairman of the Beijing Women's Federation, Vice-Chairman Yang Yunyu, and representatives from all sectors of the capital's women, totaling more than 900 people.

　　At the meeting, Yang Yunyu delivered a warm welcome speech on behalf of the women of the whole country and Beijing to the Korean People's Delegation to China.

　　The Korean People's Delegation to China presented a banner to the Chinese women and gifts to the Chairman and Vice-Chairman of the All-China Women's Federation. Cai Chang said after accepting the banner and gifts: These banners and gifts represent the unbreakable friendship between the Chinese and Korean peoples. We firmly believe that the just struggle of the Korean people will surely be victorious, and we must use practical actions to support you and jointly strive for the early arrival of the final victory in the war against America.

　　Next, Wu Tanlie, Minister of the Central Organization Department of the Korean Democratic Women's League, reported on the brutal atrocities of the American aggressors in Korea. She also introduced many heroic deeds of Korean women fighting the enemy. Finally, children from the capital presented flowers to the Korean People's Delegation to China.

### 全国妇联和北京市妇联  集会欢迎朝鲜代表团

1951-07-03
第1版()
专栏：

　　全国妇联和北京市妇联
    集会欢迎朝鲜代表团
    【新华社二十九日讯】中华全国民主妇女联合会、北京市民主妇女联合会于本月二十九日上午九时，联合举行欢迎朝鲜人民访华代表团大会。参加大会的有全国妇联主席蔡畅，副主席许广平及北京市妇联主席张晓梅，副主席杨蕴玉以及首都各界妇女代表等九百余人。
    会上杨蕴玉代表全国妇女及北京市妇女向朝鲜人民访华代表团致热烈的欢迎辞。
    朝鲜人民访华代表团向中国妇女献旗，并向全国妇联正、副主席献礼。蔡畅在接受献旗献礼后致词说：这些锦旗和礼物，代表了中朝两国人民牢不可破的友谊。我们深信朝鲜人民的正义斗争一定胜利，我们一定要用实际行动来支援你们，共同争取抗美战争最后胜利的早日到来。
    接着，朝鲜民主女性同盟中央组织部长吴贪烈报告美国侵略者在朝鲜的残暴兽行。她并介绍了许多朝鲜妇女与敌人斗争的英勇事迹。最后由首都儿童向朝鲜人民访华代表团献花。
