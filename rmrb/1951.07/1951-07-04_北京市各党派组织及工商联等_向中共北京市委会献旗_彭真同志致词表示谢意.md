Title: Party Organizations and Industry & Commerce Federation of Beijing Present Banner to Beijing Municipal Committee of the CPC; Comrade Peng Zhen Expresses Thanks in Speech
Author:
Date: 1951-07-04
Page: 1

　　Organizations of various democratic parties in Beijing and the Federation of Industry and Commerce, etc.
    Present Banner to Beijing Municipal Committee of the CPC
    Comrade Peng Zhen Expresses Thanks in Speech
    [Our Newspaper] The democratic parties of Beijing Municipality, the Beijing Federation of Industry and Commerce, and the Beijing Machine Tool High-Speed Cutting Research Promotion Committee presented a banner to the Beijing Municipal Committee of the Communist Party of China on the 2nd, to celebrate the 30th anniversary of the founding of the Communist Party of China.
    The Beijing Branch of the Revolutionary Committee of the Chinese Kuomintang and others jointly presented a banner to the Beijing Municipal Committee of the Communist Party of China. During the presentation, Jiang Guangnai first read a congratulatory message on behalf of the democratic parties. Subsequently, Fu Huating and others representing the Municipal Federation of Industry and Commerce, and Hu Guang and others from the Beijing Machine Tool High-Speed Cutting Research Promotion Committee presented banners respectively. The banner presented by the Beijing Machine Tool Factory was decorated with iron filings from high-speed cutting. At the same time, the factory also wrote a letter to the Beijing Municipal Committee of the CPC to be forwarded to Chairman Mao, reporting that in order to welcome the 30th anniversary of the founding of the Communist Party of China, they had worked hard to learn advanced Soviet high-speed cutting methods, greatly improving production efficiency.
    Peng Zhen, Secretary of the Beijing Municipal Committee of the CPC, delivered a speech after accepting the banner, expressing his gratitude. Finally, a forum was held to exchange views on future work. The meeting was cordial between hosts and guests, fully demonstrating the solid unity of the democratic parties and people from all walks of life in Beijing.
    Congratulatory message from the organizations of the democratic parties in Beijing Municipality: Beijing Municipal Committee of the Communist Party of China:
    On the occasion of the 30th anniversary of the founding of the great Communist Party of China, we, on behalf of all party, league, association, society, and group members of the democratic parties in Beijing Municipality, extend our heartfelt congratulations.
    For thirty years, under the leadership of the Communist Party of China and Chairman Mao, the Chinese people have carried out heroic revolutionary struggles, unyielding, and making persistent efforts, and finally achieved the great victory of the Chinese People's Liberation War and the Chinese People's Revolution, overthrowing the reactionary rule of imperialism, feudalism, and bureaucratic capitalism, and establishing the New Democratic People's Republic of China, opening up an infinitely bright and happy future for our nation.
    Today, while celebrating this great festival, we would like to assure you that we will earnestly study the glorious history of the arduous struggle of the Communist Party of China, we will strengthen the self-education of Marxism-Leninism and Mao Zedong Thought, we will forever unite under the banner of Mao Zedong, and strive for the victorious completion of the three major movements of resisting U.S. aggression and aiding Korea, land reform, and suppressing counter-revolutionaries, for consolidating the people's democratic dictatorship, and for defending world peace.
    With revolutionary greetings,

### 北京市各党派组织及工商联等  向中共北京市委会献旗   彭真同志致词表示谢意

1951-07-04
第1版()
专栏：

　　北京市各党派组织及工商联等
    向中共北京市委会献旗
    彭真同志致词表示谢意
    【本报讯】北京市各民主党派、北京市工商界联合会、北京市机器厂高速切削研究推进委员会，二日向中国共产党北京市委员会献旗，祝贺中国共产党成立三十周年。
    中国国民党革命委员会北京市分部等，联合向中国共产党北京市委员会献旗一面，献旗时，首由蒋光鼐代表各民主党派宣读祝词。继由市工商联合会代表傅华亭等，北京机器厂高速切削研究推进委员会胡光等分别献旗。北京市机器厂献的锦旗上，并饰以高速切削下的铁屑。同时，该厂还写了一封信，请中共北京市委员会转致毛主席，信上报告他们为了迎接中国共产党成立三十周年，努力学习苏联先进高速切削方法，大大提高了生产效率。
    中共北京市委员会书记彭真，于接受献旗后致词，表示谢意。最后并举行座谈，对今后工作交换意见。会上，宾主欢洽，充分表现了北京市各民主党派及各界人民巩固的大团结。
    北京市各民主党派组织的祝词：中国共产党北京市委员会：
    欣逢伟大的中国共产党建党三十周年纪念日，我们谨代表北京市各民主党派的全体党、盟、会、社、团员致以衷心的祝贺。
    三十年来，在中国共产党和毛主席的领导下，中国人民进行了英勇的革命斗争，百折不挠，再接再厉，终于取得了中国人民解放战争和中国人民革命的伟大胜利，推翻了帝国主义、封建主义和官僚资本主义的反动统治，建立了新民主主义的中华人民共和国，为我们民族开辟了无限光明和幸福的前途。
    今天，在庆贺这个伟大的节日时，我们愿向你们保证我们要认真学习中国共产党艰苦斗争的光荣历史，我们要加强马克思列宁主义和毛泽东思想的自我教育，我们要永远团结在毛泽东的旗帜下，为胜利地完成抗美援朝、土地改革和镇压反革命这三大运动，为巩固人民民主专政，为保卫世界和平而奋斗。
