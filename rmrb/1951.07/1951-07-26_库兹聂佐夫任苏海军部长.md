Title: Kuznetsov Appointed Soviet Navy Minister
Author:
Date: 1951-07-26
Page: 4

　　Kuznetsov Appointed Soviet Navy Minister
    [Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, 25th] TASS Moscow, 24th: The Presidium of the Supreme Soviet of the Soviet Union orders: The request of Soviet Navy Minister Yumashev to resign has been approved, and Vice Admiral Kuznetsov has been appointed as the Navy Minister.

### 库兹聂佐夫任苏海军部长

1951-07-26
第4版()
专栏：

　　库兹聂佐夫任苏海军部长
    【新华社二十五日讯】塔斯社莫斯科二十四日讯：苏联最高苏维埃主席团命令：苏联海军部长尤马谢夫呈请辞职，已予照准，海军部长由海军中将库兹聂佐夫继任。
