Title: Comrade Ren Bishi Laid to Rest in the People's Cemetery; Comrade Liu Shaoqi Presents Wreath on Behalf of the Party Central Committee
Author:
Date: 1951-07-19
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, 18th. The remains of Comrade Ren Bishi, member of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of China, member of the Political Bureau of the Central Committee, and Secretary of the Secretariat of the Central Committee, were interred at the Babaoshan Revolutionary Cemetery in the western suburbs of Beijing on July 18th. Those who assisted in carrying the coffin included members of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of China Liu Shaoqi, Zhou Enlai, Zhu De, Chen Yun, Dong Biwu, Peng Zhen, Li Fuchun, Li Lisan, Cai Chang, Lu Dingyi, Nie Rongzhen, Wu Yuzhang, Teng Daiyuan, Bo Yibo, Liao Chengzhi, Wang Jiaxiang, Chen Boda, alternate members of the Central Committee Deng Yingchao, Cheng Zihua, Li Baohua, Chen Yu, Lü Zhengcao, Luo Ruiqing, Wang Congwu, Liu Lantao, and other senior cadres, and Comrade Ren Bishi's family members Chen Congying, Ren Yuanzhi, Ren Yuanzheng, Ren Yuanfang, Ren Yuanyuan, and others, totaling over two hundred people. The funeral service for Comrade Ren Bishi's remains began at seven o'clock on the morning of the 18th. After the music was played and the coffin was raised, the coffin, led by those assisting in carrying it, was moved from the memorial hall of the People's Cemetery to the burial site, with soldiers standing solemnly on both sides of the road awaiting the arrival of the remains. The burial site was surrounded by wreaths. At seven thirty, the coffin was lowered into the grave. Comrade Liu Shaoqi presented a wreath to Comrade Ren Bishi's remains on behalf of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of China. Subsequently, all those attending the funeral bowed in respect to Comrade Ren Bishi's remains, and the band played the Internationale. (Attached Pictures)

### 任弼时同志安葬人民公墓  刘少奇同志代表党中央献花圈

1951-07-19
第1版()
专栏：

　　任弼时同志安葬人民公墓
　  刘少奇同志代表党中央献花圈
    【新华社十八日讯】中国共产党中央委员会委员、中央政治局委员、中央书记处书记任弼时同志的灵柩，在七月十八日在北京西郊八宝山人民公墓安葬。前往执绋的有中国共产党中央委员刘少奇、周恩来、朱德、陈云、董必武、彭真、李富春、李立三、蔡畅、陆定一、聂荣臻、吴玉章、滕代远、薄一波、廖承志、王稼祥、陈伯达，候补中央委员邓颖超、程子华、李葆华、陈郁、吕正操、罗瑞卿、王从吾、刘澜涛，及其他高级干部，和任弼时同志家属陈琮英、任远志、任远征、任远芳、任远远等共二百余人。任弼时同志灵柩葬仪在十八日早晨七时开始。在奏乐起灵后，灵柩在执绋者引导下，由人民公墓灵堂移到墓地，道路两旁肃立着候灵的战士。墓地四周，布满了花圈。七时半灵柩下降墓穴。刘少奇同志代表中国共产党中央委员会向任弼时同志灵柩献了花圈。随后，全体送葬者向任弼时同志灵柩鞠躬致敬，乐队奏国际歌。（附图片）
