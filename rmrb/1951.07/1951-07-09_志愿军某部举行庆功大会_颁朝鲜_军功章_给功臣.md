Title: A Certain Unit of the Chinese People's Volunteer Army Holds Celebration Meeting, Awards Korean "Military Merit Medals" to Meritorious Personnel
Author: 
Date: 1951-07-09
Page: 4

　　Xinhua News Agency, Korean Front, 8th -- A certain unit of the Chinese People's Volunteer Army recently held a grand celebration meeting in a certain area on the front lines to award "Military Merit Medals" of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea to meritorious personnel. The recipients were meritorious personnel of the unit who had performed two or more major meritorious services since entering Korea to fight. They and representatives of various units rushed from various positions overnight to attend this grand ceremony. When the head of the unit personally pinned the dazzling "Military Merit Medals" on the chests of the meritorious personnel, the whole audience burst into stormy cheers. Meritorious representatives Gao Zhende, Zhang Qunsheng, and others unanimously stated in their speeches that they would strive to add merit upon merit and respond to the encouragement and love of the Korean people and government with even greater victories. Representatives of various units stated in their speeches that they would learn from the meritorious personnel and strive to perform meritorious service in future struggles. The conference unanimously passed a letter to the leaders of the Volunteer Army, expressing their determination to create new honors. The chairman of a certain county committee of the Korean Workers' Party made a special trip to attend the meeting and pay tribute and thanks to the meritorious personnel on behalf of the people of the county. The whole audience enthusiastically shouted slogans such as "Long live Chairman Mao" and "Long live General Kim Il-sung." The grand ceremony ended in an atmosphere full of patriotism and internationalism.

### 志愿军某部举行庆功大会  颁朝鲜“军功章”给功臣

1951-07-09
第4版()
专栏：

　　志愿军某部举行庆功大会
    颁朝鲜“军功章”给功臣
    【新华社朝鲜前线八日电】中国人民志愿军某部最近在前线某地隆重举行庆功大会，颁发朝鲜民主主义人民共和国的“军功章”给功臣们。受奖的人是该部入朝作战以来立过两次大功以上的功臣。他们和各单位的代表，都连夜从各处阵地赶来参加这个盛典。当该部首长将光耀夺目的“军功章”亲自佩戴在功臣们的胸前时，全场发出了暴风雨似的欢呼。功臣代表高振德、张群生等在发言时一致表示要争取功上加功，用更大胜利来回答朝鲜人民和政府对他们的勉励和热爱。各单位代表在发言中都表示要向功臣们学习，在今后的斗争中争取立功。大会一致通过上书志愿军首长，表示创造新的荣誉的决心。朝鲜劳动党某郡委员会委员长特远道赶来参加大会，代表该郡人民向功臣们致敬和致谢。全场热烈欢呼“毛主席万岁”、“金日成将军万岁”等口号。盛典在充满着爱国主义和国际主义气氛中结束。
