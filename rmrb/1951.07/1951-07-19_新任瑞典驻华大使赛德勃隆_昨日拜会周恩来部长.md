Title: Newly Appointed Swedish Ambassador to China, Mr. Soderblom, Pays a Visit to Minister Zhou Enlai Yesterday
Author:
Date: 1951-07-19
Page: 1

　　Xinhua News Agency, July 18th: The newly appointed Swedish Ambassador Extraordinary and Plenipotentiary to China, Mr. Soderblom, visited Minister Zhou Enlai at the Ministry of Foreign Affairs at 4:00 PM today to discuss matters related to the presentation of his credentials. Also present were Director Huan Xiang of the Department of European and African Affairs, and Director Wang Zhuoru of the Bureau of Protocol.

### 新任瑞典驻华大使赛德勃隆  昨日拜会周恩来部长

1951-07-19
第1版()
专栏：

　　新任瑞典驻华大使赛德勃隆
    昨日拜会周恩来部长
    【新华社十八日讯】新任瑞典驻华特命全权大使赛德勃隆于今日下午四时赴外交部拜会周恩来部长，商谈呈递国书事宜。在座者尚有欧非司宦乡司长，交际处王倬如处长。
