Title: The Government Administration Council Holds Meeting, Adopts Decisions on Improving Student Health, etc.
Author:
Date: 1951-07-14
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, July 13th: The 93rd meeting of the Central People's Government Administration Council was held on the afternoon of the 13th. The meeting listened to and discussed Education Minister Ma Xulun's report on improving the health of students at all levels of schools and Deputy Minister of Health He Cheng's report on enriching health personnel in national defense construction. Following the reports, the meeting adopted the "Decision on Improving the Health of Students at All Levels of Schools" and the "Decision on Enriching Health Personnel in National Defense Construction." The meeting also approved several personnel appointments and removals and submitted several proposed appointments to the Central People's Government for approval.

### 政务院举行会议  通过改善学生健康状况等的决定

1951-07-14
第1版()
专栏：

　　政务院举行会议
    通过改善学生健康状况等的决定
    【新华社十三日讯】中央人民政府政务院第九十三次政务会议于十三日下午举行。会议在听取和讨论了中央人民政府教育部马叙伦部长关于改善各级学校学生健康状况的报告和中央人民政府卫生部贺诚副部长关于充实国防建设中的卫生人员问题的报告后，通过了关于改善各级学校学生健康状况的决定和关于充实国防建设中的卫生人员的决定。会议并通过任免案及提请中央人民政府批准任命案多起。
