    Our Great Motherland
    Many state-owned factories are paying attention to training female technicians. This is Song Jie, a female worker at the Dalian Metal Machinery Factory. She has already learned how to manufacture gears. The gears in front of her are all made by her.

### 我们伟大的祖国（图片）

1951-07-16
第1版()
专栏：

    我们伟大的祖国
    国营的许多工厂，正在注意培养女技术工人。这是大连金属机械工厂的女工宋杰，她已经学会了制造齿轮。在她面前摆着的这些齿轮，都是她做成的。
