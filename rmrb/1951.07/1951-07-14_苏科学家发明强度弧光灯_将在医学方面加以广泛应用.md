Title: Soviet Scientists Invent High-Intensity Arc Lamp, To Be Widely Applied in Medicine
Author:
Date: 1951-07-14
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Moscow: Soviet Doctor of Mathematical Physics, Katsenelanbogen, has recently invented a high-intensity combustion carbon arc lamp. The light intensity of this arc is six times that of an arc emitted by a carbon electrode. Soviet scientists say that applying this arc to special devices creates the best "artificial sunlight" available. Several experiments have shown that this arc has a high therapeutic effect. After being irradiated with this arc, the human body can enhance its resistance to various infectious diseases. The Optical Technology Committee directly under the Soviet Academy of Sciences has approved this new invention and recommended its widespread application in medicine.

### 苏科学家发明强度弧光灯  将在医学方面加以广泛应用

1951-07-14
第4版()
专栏：

　　苏科学家发明强度弧光灯
    将在医学方面加以广泛应用
    【新华社十三日讯】据塔斯社莫斯科讯：苏联数理学博士卡采尼兰波根最近发明了高度强烈燃烧的炭精弧光灯。这种弧光的光度相当于炭精电极所发出的弧光的六倍。苏联科学家说，把这种弧光应用于特殊的装置之上，乃是现有的最好的一种“人工太阳光”。若干试验已经表明，这种弧光具有高度的治病效能。人体经过这种弧光的照射后可以增强对于各种传染病的抵抗力。苏联科学院直属光学技术委员会已批准此项新发明，并推荐到医学方面去加以广泛应用。
