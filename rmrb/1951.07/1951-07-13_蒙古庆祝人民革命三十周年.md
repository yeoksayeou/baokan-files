Title: Mongolia Celebrates the 30th Anniversary of the People's Revolution
Author:
Date: 1951-07-13
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Ulan Bator, July 11th: A grand meeting was held at the State Music and Drama Theatre in Ulan Bator on the evening of July 10th to celebrate the 30th anniversary of the Mongolian People's Revolution.

Participating in the celebration were deputies of the Great People's Khural of the Mongolian People's Republic, ministers of various departments, social leaders, workers in culture, science, and art, the most outstanding laborers in industry, transportation, and agriculture, and officers and generals of the Mongolian army. When the Party and government leaders of the Mongolian People's Republic, headed by Marshal Choibalsan, and government delegations from the Soviet Union, China, North Korea, and other countries entered the venue, the entire audience burst into prolonged applause and cheers. Diplomatic representatives from the Soviet Union and other people's democratic countries also attended the celebration.

Chairman Bumazhin of the Presidium of the Small Khural of the Mongolian People's Republic delivered the opening speech. Amidst the applause of all attendees, he warmly welcomed the government delegations from various countries who had come to participate in the 30th anniversary of the Mongolian People's Revolution. The assembly, filled with surging enthusiasm, elected a honorary presidium composed of the Political Bureau of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of the Soviet Union (Bolsheviks) headed by the great Stalin, the Political Bureau of the Central Committee of the Mongolian People's Revolutionary Party headed by Choibalsan, Comrade Mao Zedong, Comrade Kim Il-sung, Comrade Pieck, Comrade Bierut, Comrade Gottwald, Comrade Chervenkov, Comrade Rakosi, Comrade Hoxha, Comrade Gheorghiu-Dej, and Comrade Ho Chi Minh.

Zedenbal, General Secretary of the Central Committee of the Mongolian People's Revolutionary Party, delivered a report on the commemoration of the 30th anniversary of the Mongolian People's Revolution.

Heads of government delegations from various countries then delivered congratulatory speeches.

With the highest enthusiasm, the participants passed a telegram of tribute to the great Stalin—the best friend of the Mongolian people, the genius who leads and educates the working people of the world.

### 蒙古庆祝人民革命三十周年

1951-07-13
第4版()
专栏：

　　蒙古庆祝人民革命三十周年
    【新华社十二日讯】塔斯社乌兰巴托尔十一日讯：七月十日晚，在乌兰巴托尔的国家音乐戏剧院举行盛会，庆祝蒙古人民革命三十周年。
    参加庆祝大会的有：蒙古人民共和国大人民呼拉尔的议员，各部部长，社会领袖，文化、科学与艺术工作者，工业、运输业和农业中最杰出的劳动者，蒙古军队的军官和将军。当以乔巴山元帅为首的蒙古人民共和国党、政首长和苏、中、朝等国的政府代表团进入会场时，全场响起了一片历久不绝的掌声与欢呼声。苏联和各人民民主国家的外交代表也都参加了这个庆祝大会。
    蒙古人民共和国小呼拉尔主席团主席布马增致开会词。他在全体与会者的掌声中热烈欢迎前来参加蒙古人民革命三十周年的各国政府代表团。大会在澎湃的热情中选出了名誉主席团，由以伟大的斯大林为首的联共（布）党中委会政治局、以乔巴山为首的蒙古人民革命党中委会政治局、毛泽东同志、金日成同志、皮克同志、贝鲁特同志、哥特瓦尔德同志、契尔文科夫同志、拉科西同志、霍查同志、乔治乌—德治同志和胡志明同志组成。
    蒙古人民革命党中委会总书记泽登巴尔作了关于蒙古人民革命三十周年纪念的报告。
    各国政府代表团团长相继致贺词。
    与会者以最高度热情通过了向伟大的斯大林——蒙古人民最好的朋友、领导与教育全世界劳动人民的天才——致敬的电文。
