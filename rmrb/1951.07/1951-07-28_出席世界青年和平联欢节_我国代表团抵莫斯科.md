Title: Chinese Delegation Arrives in Moscow to Attend World Youth Peace Festival
Author:
Date: 1951-07-28
Page: 4

　　Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, July 27th: The Chinese Youth Delegation, led by Deputy Head Rong Gaotang, to the Third World Youth and Student Peace Festival in Berlin, arrived in Moscow on the morning of the 26th. The delegation was warmly welcomed by more than 2,000 Soviet youth representatives at the Yaroslavsky Railway Station. Selipin and Semichastny, Secretaries of the All-Union Leninist Young Communist League, Kozimasov, Chairman of the Soviet Youth Anti-Fascist Committee, Sizov, Secretary of the Moscow City Committee of the All-Union Leninist Young Communist League, and personnel from the Chinese Embassy in the Soviet Union were all at the station to welcome them.
　　Huge banners written in both Chinese and Russian were hung on the platform, reading: "Long live the great Chinese people who have won freedom and independence and are victoriously building a new life," "Youth unite to defend peace and break the threat of a new war," "May the eternal friendship and cooperation between the Soviet and Chinese people be developed and consolidated," etc. The train slowly entered the station to the tune of the Democratic Youth March. As the Chinese youth representatives got off the train, they were immediately surrounded by people holding flowers to greet them and photographers.
　　At the welcoming meeting held at the station, Selipin, on behalf of all Soviet youth, and Sizov, on behalf of Moscow youth, warmly welcomed the Chinese youth representatives. Selipin said: Soviet youth, filled with deep friendship for Chinese youth, sincerely hope that the youth of the People's Republic of China will succeed in their labor and study, and wish the Chinese youth representatives success at the festival.
　　In his reply, Rong Gaotang paid tribute to the Soviet youth on behalf of the youth of New China, and said that they were bringing to the festival the determination and strength of the Chinese youth to resolutely defend peace. He said: The great unity of the 700 million people of China and the Soviet Union is a powerful guarantee for world peace. The welcoming meeting ended with the Democratic Youth March and continuous applause.
　　The Chinese Youth Delegation received the same warm welcome along the way during their ten-day journey. The delegation will proceed to Berlin in two or three days.

### 出席世界青年和平联欢节  我国代表团抵莫斯科

1951-07-28
第4版()
专栏：

　　出席世界青年和平联欢节
　  我国代表团抵莫斯科
    【新华社莫斯科二十七日电】出席柏林第三届世界青年与学生和平联欢节的中国青年代表团，在副团长荣高棠率领下已于二十六日上午抵达莫斯科。代表团在雅罗斯拉夫斯基火车站受到苏联青年代表两千多人的热烈欢迎。苏联共产主义青年团书记谢利平和赛米恰斯尼，苏联青年反法西斯委员会主席科齐马索夫，苏联共产主义青年团莫斯科市委员会书记西佐夫，和中国驻苏大使馆人员均到车站欢迎。
    月台上悬着用中文和俄文写的巨幅标语：“赢得自由独立和胜利建设着新生活的伟大中国人民万岁”，“青年团结起来保卫和平打破新战争威胁”，“祝苏中两国人民的永久友好合作关系发扬巩固”等。列车在世界民主青年进行曲乐声中徐徐入站。中国青年代表下车后，马上被抱着鲜花来迎接的人们和摄影师包围了。
    在车站举行的欢迎大会上，谢利平代表全苏联青年，西佐夫代表莫斯科青年热烈欢迎中国青年代表。谢利平说：苏联青年满怀着对中国青年的深厚友情，热烈希望中华人民共和国青年在劳动和学习中成功，并祝中国青年代表在联欢节上获得成功。
    荣高棠致答词时代表新中国青年向苏联青年致敬，并表示带到联欢节去的是中国青年坚决保卫和平的决心与力量。他说：中苏两国七亿人民的伟大团结，是世界和平的有力保证。欢迎会在民主青年进行曲和不断的鼓掌声中结束。
    中国青年代表团在十天旅程中，沿途都受到同样热烈的欢迎。代表团将于两三天内转赴柏林。
