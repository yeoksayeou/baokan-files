Title: Returning Representatives of Aid-Korea Railway Workers Report on Heroic Deeds of Railway Workers in Korea

Author:
Date: 1951-07-18
Page: 1

　　
Xinhua News Agency, Shanghai, July 13th: The returning representatives of the Chinese Railway Workers Volunteer Aid-Korea Brigade, Guan Changhai, Liu Enzhong, Wang Yonggui, Liu Fuze, Han Diange, and Tian Yaozhong, accompanied by Li Yong, Vice Chairman of the National Committee of the Chinese Railway Workers' Union, arrived in Shanghai on the afternoon of the 12th. The returning representatives left Beijing on June 28th, passing through Jinan, Qingdao, Xuzhou, Nanjing, and other places. Along the way, they conveyed news of victory from the front lines to railway workers and reported on the heroic deeds of the Chinese Railway Workers Volunteer Aid-Korea Brigade in Korea, as well as the brutal acts of the American aggressors in Korea.

### 援朝铁路工人归国代表  报告赴朝铁路职工英雄事迹

1951-07-18
第1版()
专栏：

　　援朝铁路工人归国代表
　  报告赴朝铁路职工英雄事迹
    【新华社上海十三日电】中国铁路工人志愿援朝大队的归国代表关长海、刘恩忠、王永贵、刘福泽、韩殿阁、田耀忠六人，在中国铁路工会全国委员会副主席李永陪同下，于十二日下午到达上海。归国代表一行系于六月二十八日离北京，途经济南、青岛、徐州、南京等地。他们沿途向铁路职工传达了前方胜利消息，报告了中国铁路工人志愿援朝大队在朝鲜的英雄事迹以及美国侵略者在朝鲜的残暴行为。
