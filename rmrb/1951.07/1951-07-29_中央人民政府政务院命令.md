Author: 
Date: 1951-07-29
Page: 2

　　Order of the State Administrative Council of the Central People's Government
    The "Decision on the Work Tasks of Secretaries-General of Government Organs at All Levels and Directors of General Offices Without Secretaries-General, and the Secretarial Work Organization" proposed by the First National Secretaries-General Conference convened by this Council may be implemented on a trial basis and is hereby promulgated.
          This order.
              July 26, 1951
