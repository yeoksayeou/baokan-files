Title: Strengthen the Friendship Between the Chinese and Hungarian People! Promote Sino-Hungarian Cultural Exchange! China and Hungary Sign Cultural Cooperation Agreement
Author:
Date: 1951-07-13
Page: 4

　　Strengthen the Friendship Between the Chinese and Hungarian People! Promote Sino-Hungarian Cultural Exchange!
    China and Hungary Sign Cultural Cooperation Agreement
    [Xinhua News Agency, 12th] The Sino-Hungarian Cultural Cooperation Agreement, after friendly negotiations between representatives of both sides, was signed in Beijing on July 12, 1951. The signatories to the agreement were: on behalf of the Central People's Government of the People's Republic of China, Shen Yanbing, Minister of the Ministry of Culture of the Central People's Government; on behalf of the Government of the Hungarian People's Republic, Dr. Erik Molnar, Minister of Justice of the Hungarian People's Republic. Participants in the signing ceremony included: on the Chinese side, Guo Moruo, Vice Premier of the State Administration Council of the Central People's Government; Wu Xiuquan, Deputy Minister of Foreign Affairs; Ma Xulun, Vice Chairman of the Cultural and Educational Committee and Minister of Education; Yang Hansheng and Shao Quanlin, Deputy Secretaries-General of the Cultural and Educational Committee; Zhou Yang and Ding Xielin, Deputy Ministers of the Ministry of Culture; Hong Shen, Director of the Bureau of Foreign Cultural Relations of the Cultural and Educational Committee, etc.; on the Hungarian side, Mr. Mihályfi, Deputy Minister of Culture of the Hungarian People's Republic, Ambassador Sarlanko, Counselor Fekete, etc. (Attached picture)
    The picture shows the scene of the signing ceremony of the Sino-Hungarian Cultural Cooperation Agreement, with Minister Shen Yanbing, the plenipotentiary representative of the Central People's Government of China, and Minister Molnar, the plenipotentiary representative of the Government of the Hungarian People's Republic, signing the agreement.
    Cao Xinghua, News Photography Bureau.  Nar

### 加强中匈人民友谊！促进中匈文化交流！  中匈签订文化合作协定

1951-07-13
第4版()
专栏：

　　加强中匈人民友谊！促进中匈文化交流！
    中匈签订文化合作协定
    【新华社十二日讯】中匈文化合作协定经过双方代表友好谈判，已于一九五一年七月十二日在北京签字。协定签字者：中华人民共和国中央人民政府全权代表为中央人民政府文化部部长沈雁冰；匈牙利人民共和国政府全权代表为匈牙利人民共和国司法部部长埃里克·莫尔那尔博士。参加签字仪式者：我方有中央人民政府政务院副总理郭沫若，外交部副部长伍修权，文化教育委员会副主任兼教育部部长马叙伦，文化教育委员会副秘书长阳翰笙、邵荃麟，文化部副部长周扬、丁燮林，文化教育委员会对外文化联络事务局局长洪深等；匈方有匈牙利人民共和国文化部副部长米哈依费先生，夏法朗柯大使，费凯尔特参赞等。（附图片）
