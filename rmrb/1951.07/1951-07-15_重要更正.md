　　Important Correction
    In footnote 8 of the article "Why Can China's Red Political Power Exist?" published on page 3 of this newspaper on July 14th, "towns such as Liuyang, Pingjiang, Liling, and Zhuzhou" should read "the area of Liuyang, Pingjiang, Liling, and Zhuzhou."

### 重要更正

1951-07-15
第1版()
专栏：

　　重要更正
    本报七月十四日第三版所载《中国的红色政权为什么能够存在？》一文的第八条附注中，“浏阳、平江、醴陵、株州等城镇”应为“浏阳、平江、醴陵、株州一带”。
