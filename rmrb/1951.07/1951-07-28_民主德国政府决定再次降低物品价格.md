Title: Government of the German Democratic Republic Decides to Reduce Prices Again
Author:
Date: 1951-07-28
Page: 4

　　Democratic Germany Government decided
　  To reduce commodity prices again
    Xinhua News Agency, Berlin, July 27th: The Council of Ministers of the German Democratic Republic has decided to further reduce the prices of food and industrial products from July 30th to improve the living conditions of the working people. The resolution stipulates: sugar will be reduced by about 45%, candy will be reduced by 30%, rice will be reduced by 25%, socks will be reduced by 18%, leather products except shoes will be reduced by 20%, radios will be reduced by 25%, and household soap will be reduced by about 20%.

### 民主德国政府决定再次降低物品价格

1951-07-28
第4版()
专栏：

　　民主德国政府决定
　  再次降低物品价格
    【新华社二十七日讯】据塔斯社柏林二十七日讯：德意志民主共和国部长会议决定，自七月三十日起，再度降低食品和工业品的价格，以改善劳动人民的生活条件。决议规定：食糖减价约百分之四十五，糖果减价百分之三十，大米减价百分之二十五，袜子减价百分之十八，皮革制品除鞋子以外均减价百分之二十，收音机减价百分之二十五，家用肥皂减价约百分之二十。
