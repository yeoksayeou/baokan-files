Title: The National Committee of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference and Various Party Groups Hold a Grand Reception to Celebrate "July 1st"; Comrades Mao Zedong and Liu Shaoqi Participate Personally
Author:
Date: 1951-07-02
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, 1st. - To celebrate the 30th anniversary of the founding of the Communist Party of China, the National Committee of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Congress, the Communist Party of China, the Revolutionary Committee of the Chinese Kuomintang, the China Democratic League, the China National Democratic Construction Association, non-party democrats of the Chinese People's Political Consultive Conference, the China Association for Promoting Democracy, the Chinese Peasants' and Workers' Democratic Party, the China Zhi Gong Party, the Jiusan Society, the Taiwan Democratic Self-Government League, the Chinese New Democratic Youth League, the All-China Federation of Trade Unions, the All-China Democratic Women's Federation, the All-China Democratic Youth Federation, the All-China Students' Federation, and the All-China Federation of Literary and Art Circles, held a grand reception at the Huairen Hall in Zhongnanhai at 7 p.m. on July 1.

Participating in this grand celebration were members of the Central People's Government Committee and the heads of all subordinate academies, commissions, ministries, associations, bureaus and banks; members of the National Committee of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference; senior officers of the Army, Navy, and Air Force of the Chinese People's Liberation Army; representatives of the Chinese People's Volunteers returning from Korea; leaders of various democratic parties; non-party democrats of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference; heads of various people's organizations; university presidents; scientists; writers; artists; members of the North China Bureau of the CPC Central Committee; leaders of the North China Military Region of the People's Liberation Army; and responsible persons from the Beijing Municipal People's Government, the Beijing Municipal Consultative Committee, the Beijing Municipal Democratic Parties, the Beijing Municipal People's Organizations, and local gentry in Beijing, totaling more than 600 people.

At 7 p.m., Chairman Mao Zedong and CPC Central Committee leaders Liu Shaoqi, Zhu De, and others arrived at the venue. At this moment, the participants stood up to pay their respects. The entire venue erupted in thunderous applause.

The celebratory reception took place in an atmosphere of close unity. After the reception, a celebratory evening party was held.

### 人民政协全国委员会及各党派团体   举行盛大庆祝“七一”酒会  毛主席刘少奇等同志均亲参加

1951-07-02
第1版()
专栏：

　　人民政协全国委员会及各党派团体
    举行盛大庆祝“七一”酒会
    毛主席刘少奇等同志均亲参加
    【新华社一日讯】为庆祝中国共产党成立三十周年纪念日，中国人民政治协商会议全国委员会、中国共产党、中国国民党革命委员会、中国民主同盟、民主建国会、中国人民政协无党派民主人士、中国民主促进会、中国农工民主党、中国致公党、九三学社、台湾民主自治同盟、中国新民主主义青年团、中华全国总工会、中华全国民主妇女联合会、中华全国民主青年联合会、中华全国学生联合会和中华全国文学艺术界联合会，在七月一日下午七时，在中南海怀仁堂举行了盛大的酒会。
    参加这一盛大庆祝酒会的，有中央人民政府委员会委员及所属各院、委、部、会、署、行首长，人民政协全国委员会委员，中国人民解放军陆海空军高级军官，中国人民志愿军归国代表，各民主党派的领袖，人民政协无党派民主人士，各人民团体负责人，各大学的校长，科学家，文学家，艺术家，中共中央华北局委员，人民解放军华北军区首长，以及北京市人民政府、北京市协商委员会、北京市民主党派、北京市人民团体负责人，北京市地方绅耆等共六百余人。
    下午七时，毛泽东主席和中共中央负责人刘少奇、朱德等莅临会场。这时，与会者起立致敬。全场掌声雷动。
    庆祝酒会在亲密团结的空气中进行。酒会后，举行了庆祝晚会。
