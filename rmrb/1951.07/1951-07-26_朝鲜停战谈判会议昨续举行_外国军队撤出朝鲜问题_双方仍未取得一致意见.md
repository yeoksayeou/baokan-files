Title: Korean Armistice Talks Continue Yesterday; No Agreement Yet Reached on the Issue of the Withdrawal of Foreign Troops from Korea
Author:
Date: 1951-07-26
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Pyongyang, July 25th. This reporter learned from the headquarters on the Korean front that after a three-day recess, the ninth meeting of the Korean armistice talks began on time at 10:00 AM (Korean time) on July 25th. During the meeting, the two sides still failed to reach an agreement on how to handle the issue of withdrawing all foreign troops from Korea. At 2:15 PM, the United Nations Command delegation proposed adjourning the meeting until 1:00 PM on July 26th for the tenth meeting.

### 朝鲜停战谈判会议昨续举行  外国军队撤出朝鲜问题  双方仍未取得一致意见

1951-07-26
第1版()
专栏：

　　朝鲜停战谈判会议昨续举行
　  外国军队撤出朝鲜问题
　  双方仍未取得一致意见
    【新华社平壤二十五日电】本社记者自朝鲜前线总部获悉：朝鲜停战谈判会议休会三日后，第九次会议在七月二十五日上午十时（朝鲜时间）按时开始。在会议中双方对于从朝鲜撤退一切外国军队的问题应如何处理，仍未取得一致意见。下午二时十五分，联合国军代表团建议休会至七月二十六日下午一时再举行第十次会议。
