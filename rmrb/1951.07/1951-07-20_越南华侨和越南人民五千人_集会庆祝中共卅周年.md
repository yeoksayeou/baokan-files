Title: Five Thousand Overseas Chinese and Vietnamese People in Vietnam Gather to Celebrate the 30th Anniversary of the CCP
Author:
Date: 1951-07-20
Page: 1

　　Vietnamese Overseas Chinese and Vietnamese People Five Thousand People
　  Gather to Celebrate the 30th Anniversary of the CCP
    [Xinhua News Agency News] According to a dispatch from the Vietnam News Agency on the 17th: Overseas Chinese in the Fifth Military Region of Central and Southern Vietnam held a meeting on July 1st to celebrate the 30th anniversary of the founding of the Chinese Communist Party. The meeting was convened by the Democratic Overseas Chinese Federation of Central and Southern Vietnam, and was attended by five thousand Overseas Chinese and Vietnamese people. A representative of the Democratic Overseas Chinese Federation reported at the meeting on the history of the Chinese Communist Party and Chairman Mao Zedong leading the Chinese people in revolution, overcoming numerous difficulties, and achieving brilliant victories over the past thirty years. Representatives of Overseas Chinese groups and Vietnamese guests unanimously hailed the achievements of the Chinese Communist Party in leading the Chinese people in construction and defending world peace in their speeches. The meeting finally unanimously passed a telegram to the Central Committee of the Chinese Communist Party, expressing gratitude and support for the Chinese Communist Party, and guaranteeing to actively participate in the movement to resist America and aid Korea, and contribute their own strength to the cause of defending world peace and democracy.

### 越南华侨和越南人民五千人  集会庆祝中共卅周年

1951-07-20
第1版()
专栏：

　　越南华侨和越南人民五千人
　  集会庆祝中共卅周年
    【新华社讯】据越南通讯社十七日讯：中越南部第五军区的华侨于七月一日举行大会，庆祝中国共产党成立三十周年。大会由中越南部民主华侨联合会召集，到会的有华侨和越南人民五千人。民主华侨联合会的代表在大会上报告了中国共产党和毛泽东主席三十年来领导中国人民进行革命、克服重重困难获得光辉胜利的历史。华侨团体的代表和越南人来宾们在发言中一致欢呼中国共产党领导中国人民进行建设和保卫世界和平的成就。大会最后一致通过致电中国共产党中央委员会，表示感谢和拥护中国共产党，并保证积极参加抗美援朝运动，在保卫世界和平与民主的事业中贡献出自己的力量。
