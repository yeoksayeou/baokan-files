Title: More Than 500,000 in Britain Sign in Support of Concluding a Peace Pact
Author:
Date: 1951-07-27
Page: 4

　　Xinhua News Agency, London, July 25th (Telegraph) – More than 500,000 signatures have now been collected in Britain for the declaration demanding the conclusion of a five-power peace pact. The British Peace Committee reports that an average of 70 percent of those invited to sign have agreed to do so.
　　The British Peace Committee is currently carrying out a massive Peace Crusade, which will continue until the end of the year. During this period, the collection of signatures will be intensified, funds will be raised, and twelve large-scale street rallies will be held in major cities in October, November, and December.
　　The British Peace Committee issued a special statement on the 24th, calling on the British government to step up efforts to seek peace in Korea.

### 拥护缔结和平公约  英国五十多万人已签名

1951-07-27
第4版()
专栏：

　　拥护缔结和平公约
　  英国五十多万人已签名
    【新华社二十六日讯】电通社伦敦二十五日讯：英国现在已为要求缔结五大国和平公约的宣言征集到五十多万个签名。英国和平委员会的报告说：被邀签名的人平均有百分之七十愿意签名。
    英国和平委员会现在正在推行一个巨大的和平十字军运动，这个运动将进行到年底。在这个期间，将加紧征集签名，筹募经费，十月，十一月和十二月间并将在各主要城市召开十二次规模宏大的街头群众大会。
    英国和平委员会二十四日特别发表声明，要求英国政府加紧努力以谋取朝鲜的和平。
