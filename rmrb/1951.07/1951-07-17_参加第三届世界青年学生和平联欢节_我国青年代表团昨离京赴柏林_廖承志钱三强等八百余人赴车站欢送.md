Author:
Date: 1951-07-17
Page: 1

　　Our Youth Delegation Left Beijing for Berlin Yesterday to Participate in the 3rd World Youth and Student Festival for Peace
    Liao Chengzhi, Qian Sanqiang, and More Than 800 Others Went to the Station to See Them Off
    [Dispatch from this newspaper] The Chinese Youth Delegation, led by Deputy Head Rong Gaotang, left Beijing on the 16th to participate in the 3rd World Youth and Student Festival for Peace, which will be held in Berlin, Germany in early August (Head Feng Wenbin and Deputy Head Wu Han will set off in the coming days). Those who went to the station to see them off included: Liao Chengzhi, Chairman of the All-China Federation of Democratic Youth; Qian Sanqiang, Vice Chairman; Jiang Nanxiang, Deputy Secretary of the Central Committee of the China New Democratic Youth League; Li Chang and Song Yiping, Standing Committee Members; Song Xiheng, Vice Chairman of the All-China Students' Federation; Xiao Hua, Deputy Director of the General Political Department of the People's Revolutionary Military Commission; Wang Zonghuai, Director of the Youth Department; Chen Yi, Director of the Culture Department; Yan Baohang, Deputy Director of the General Office of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs of the Central People's Government; Ding Xilin, Deputy Minister of the Ministry of Culture; Sha Kefu, Director of the General Office; Zheng Zhenduo, Director of the Bureau of Foreign Cultural Relations of the Cultural and Educational Committee; Jiang Ling, Deputy Director of the Department of Social Education of the Ministry of Education; representatives from units such as the Central Committee of the Youth League, the All-China Federation of Trade Unions, the All-China Democratic Women's Federation, the All-China Federation of Literary and Art Circles, the Central Academy of Drama, the Beijing Municipal Committee of the Youth League, and some student representatives from various regions attending the 15th National Student Congress, totaling more than 800 people.
    The Chinese Youth Delegation is composed of 300 people, with Feng Wenbin, Secretary of the Central Committee of the Youth League, serving as the head, and Wu Han, Secretary-General of the All-China Federation of Democratic Youth, and Rong Gaotang, Secretary-General of the Central Committee of the Youth League, serving as deputy heads. The entire delegation includes: youth workers from the Central Committee of the Youth League and various regions across the country, youth representatives and combat heroes from the Army, Navy, Air Force, and the Chinese People's Volunteers, model workers in industry and agriculture, representatives of ethnic minority youth, representatives of Christian youth, representatives of Young Pioneers, and performing arts groups, basketball and volleyball teams, staff, etc.
    Among them are the famous combat hero Liu Zilin, Zhao Xiao'an, the 21-year-old gunboat captain, Guo Zhongtian and Zhou Wenjiang, combat heroes of the Chinese People's Volunteers, and youth labor models from the industrial and agricultural production fronts, such as Tong Junshan, who created 11 new records in the Ma Hengchang Advanced Production Team, Qiao Yuyan, the train driver who set the national record for the highest daily mileage, Wang Jingbin, the model Youth League member from the telephone station of the Andong Telecommunications Section who persisted in working under enemy bombing, Gao Xiurong, a special-class labor model from Tianjin Hengyuan Yarn Factory, and Jin Xiulan, a model water conservancy worker for the Huai River project.
    The delegation includes a performing arts group and an acrobatics troupe led by Zhou Weizhi, Deputy Director of the Arts Administration Bureau of the Ministry of Culture of the Central People's Government, all of whom were selected from the National Literary and Art Program Conference performances during the "May Fourth" Youth Day this year. The works that will be performed abroad include: China's famous operas "The White-Haired Girl" and "Dong Cunrui," as well as various music and dance performances. In addition, there will be performances of various acrobatics and Peking Opera programs. The men's basketball team and men's volleyball team in the delegation, with a total of 24 athletes, are all national players selected from the 1951 National Basketball and Volleyball Tournament, including volleyball players Huang Fuyan and Huang Xiang, who come from worker and peasant backgrounds, and will also perform abroad.
    The Vietnamese Youth Delegation of 19 people and the Thai Youth Delegation of 4 people, who passed through our country, also traveled to Berlin with the Chinese Youth Delegation. (Attached picture)
    Rong Gaotang, Deputy Head of the Youth Delegation, accepts flowers from well-wishers.

### 参加第三届世界青年学生和平联欢节  我国青年代表团昨离京赴柏林  廖承志钱三强等八百余人赴车站欢送

1951-07-17
第1版()
专栏：

　　参加第三届世界青年学生和平联欢节
    我国青年代表团昨离京赴柏林
    廖承志钱三强等八百余人赴车站欢送
    【本报讯】参加德国柏林八月初举行的第三届世界青年学生和平联欢节的中国青年代表团，在副团长荣高棠率领下，于十六日离开北京（团长冯文彬、副团长吴晗将于日内启程）。到车站欢送的有：中华全国民主青年联合总会主席廖承志、副主席钱三强，中国新民主主义青年团中央委员会副书记蒋南翔、常务委员李昌、宋一平，中华全国学生联合会副主席宋锡恒，人民革命军事委员会总政治部副主任萧华、青年部长王宗槐、文化部长陈沂，中央人民政府外交部办公厅副主任阎宝航，文化部副部长丁西林、办公厅主任沙可夫，文教委员会对外文化联络事务局长郑振铎，教育部社会教育司副司长江凌，及青年团中央、中华全国总工会、中华全国民主妇女联合会、中华全国文学艺术界联合会、中央戏剧学院、青年团北京市委会等单位代表，和出席中华全国学生第十五届代表大会的一部分各地学生代表，共约八百余人。
    中国青年代表团由三百人组成，由青年团中央委员会书记冯文彬担任团长，中华全国民主青年联合总会秘书长吴晗和青年团中央秘书长荣高棠担任副团长。全团包括有：青年团中央和全国各地青年工作者，陆、海、空军和人民志愿军的青年代表战斗英雄，工农劳动模范，少数民族青年代表，基督教青年代表、少年儿童队代表，和文艺工作团、篮排球队、工作人员等。
    其中有著名战斗英雄刘子林，二十一岁的炮艇艇长赵孝庵，中国人民志愿军战斗英雄郭忠田、周文江，和工农等生产战线上的青年劳动模范：如马恒昌先进生产小组里连创十一次新纪录的佟俊山，创全国每日行车最高纪录的火车司机长乔玉岩，坚持在敌机轰炸下工作的安东电务段电话所的模范青年团员王静彬，天津恒源纱厂特等劳动模范高秀蓉和治淮模范金秀兰等。
    代表团中有中央人民政府文化部艺术事业管理局副局长周巍峙率领下的文艺工作团和杂技团，他们都是从今年“五四”青年节全国文艺节目大会演出选拔出来的。这次即将在国外演出的有：中国著名的歌剧《白毛女》、《董存瑞》，和各种音乐舞蹈，另外表演的还有各种杂技和京剧等节目。代表团中的男子篮球队和男子排球队，共有二十四名运动员，也都是从一九五一年全国篮、排球比赛大会中选出的全国选手，其中工农出身的排球健将黄福彦、黄享等，都将在国外表演。
    路经我国的越南青年代表团十九人，和泰国青年代表团四人，亦与中国青年代表团同行赴柏林。（附图片）
