Title: British "Daily Worker" Publishes Article Pointing Out that U.S. Avoids Discussing Troop Withdrawal from Korea Because it Dreams of Maintaining a Base for Attacking China
Author:
Date: 1951-07-24
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Prague, July 22nd: The London *Daily Worker* published an article on the 19th, discussing the Korean armistice negotiations, saying: The Kaesong talks for arranging a Korean armistice seem to be creating a climate far more serious than generally expected. "Why not stop the war?" This question can be heard almost everywhere in Britain.

The article pointed out: Despite the extremely reasonable negotiation agenda put forward by the representatives of the Korean People's Army and the Chinese People's Volunteer Army, the American representatives are making various excuses to hinder a satisfactory conclusion to the negotiations. The United States avoids discussing the withdrawal of foreign troops from Korea. Of course, the withdrawal of foreign troops would mean losing a base for attacking China in the future – a dream that some American military leaders have never forgotten, as clearly demonstrated by the recent Senate debate on MacArthur's dismissal.

The article said: The U.S. authorities not only want to maintain a foothold in Korea, but also want to occupy Taiwan and other Far Eastern bases. To do so, the U.S. authorities do not hesitate to use the lives of Koreans, Britons and Americans as bargaining chips to realize its world hegemony plan.

### 英《工人日报》著论指出  美国避免讨论自朝撤军问题是梦想保持进攻中国的基地

1951-07-24
第1版()
专栏：

　　英《工人日报》著论指出
　  美国避免讨论自朝撤军问题
　  是梦想保持进攻中国的基地
    【新华社布拉格二十二日电】十九日的伦敦工人日报发表一篇论文，该文论及朝鲜停战谈判时说：安排朝鲜停战的开城谈判似乎在造成一种远比一般人的预料严重的气候。“为什么不停止战争呢？”几乎在英国任何地方都可以听得到这个问题。
    该文指出：尽管朝鲜人民军和中国人民志愿军代表团提出极其合理的谈判议程，但是美国代表却制造种种借口，阻碍谈判的完满结局。美国避免讨论外国军队退出朝鲜的问题。当然，如果实行外国军队撤退，就等于说他们要失去了将来进攻中国的一个基地——这是某些美国军人头子念念不忘的梦想，美国参院最近关于麦克阿瑟被撤换的辩论就清楚地证明了这一点。
    该文说：美国当局不仅要在朝鲜保持立足点，同时也要占有台湾及其他远东基地。为了这样做，美国当局不惜以朝鲜人、英国人和美国人的生命当作实现它的世界霸权计划的讨价还价的本钱。
