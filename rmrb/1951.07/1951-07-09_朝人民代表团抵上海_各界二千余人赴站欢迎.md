Title: Korean People's Delegation Arrives in Shanghai; Over Two Thousand People from All Circles Go to the Station to Welcome Them
Author:
Date: 1951-07-09
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Shanghai, July 7th: The 120-member Korean People's Delegation to China, led by head of the delegation Won Dong-geun, arrived in Shanghai at 9:45 AM on the 7th from Beijing. Those who went to the station to welcome them included Shu Tong and Shen Zhiyuan, Vice Chairmen of the East China Branch of the Resist America, Aid Korea Association; Liu Changsheng, Chairman of the Shanghai Branch of the Resist America, Aid Korea Association; Sheng Pihua, Feng Ding, and Jin Zhonghua, Vice Chairmen; Pan Hannian, Deputy Mayor of the Shanghai Municipal People's Government; Huang Hua, Director of the Foreign Affairs Office; Hu Juewen, Vice Chairman of the Shanghai Municipal People's Representative Conference Standing Committee; and representatives from the local organizations of various democratic parties in East China and Shanghai, and from various people's organizations in East China and Shanghai, totaling more than 2,000 people. After disembarking from the train to the sound of military music and receiving flowers presented by people from all walks of life, the delegation participated in a welcoming ceremony held at the station by the people of East China and Shanghai. At the meeting, Liu Changsheng, on behalf of the people of East China and Shanghai, expressed a warm welcome to the Korean People's Delegation's visit to Shanghai. Won Dong-geun, the head of the delegation, delivered a speech in reply, expressing his gratitude for the great support given by the Chinese people to the Korean people. On the evening of the 7th, the East China Branch of the Resist America, Aid Korea Association, the Shanghai Branch of the Resist America, Aid Korea Association, and the Shanghai Municipal People's Representative Conference Standing Committee jointly hosted a banquet to welcome the Korean People's Delegation to China.

### 朝人民代表团抵上海  各界二千余人赴站欢迎

1951-07-09
第4版()
专栏：

　　朝人民代表团抵上海
    各界二千余人赴站欢迎
    【新华社上海七日电】朝鲜人民访华代表团一行一百二十人，在团长元东根率领下，于七日晨九时四十五分由北京抵上海，前往车站欢迎的，有华东抗美援朝总分会副主席舒同、沈志远，上海市抗美援朝分会主席刘长胜，副主席盛丕华、冯定、金仲华，上海市人民政府副市长潘汉年，外侨事务处处长黄华，上海市各界人民代表会议协商委员会副主席胡厥文及各民主党派在华东暨上海的地方组织、华东暨上海各人民团体的代表等共二千余人。代表团在军乐声中下车和接受各界人民的献花后，即参加华东暨上海人民在车站举行的欢迎仪式。会上，刘长胜代表华东及上海人民对朝鲜人民访华代表团访问上海表示热烈欢迎。代表团团长元东根致答词，对中国人民给朝鲜人民的伟大支援表示感谢。七日晚，华东抗美援朝总分会、上海市抗美援朝分会及上海市各界人民代表会议协商委员会联合欢宴朝鲜人民访华代表团。
