Title: Korean Armistice Talks: Agenda Continued to be Discussed Yesterday
Author:
Date: 1951-07-18
Page: 1

　　Korean Armistice Talks
　　Agenda Continued to be Discussed Yesterday
    Xinhua News Agency, Pyongyang, 17th -- This reporter learned from the headquarters at the Korean front that the fifth meeting of the Korean armistice talks was held on time at 10:00 AM (Korean time) on July 17th and ended at 1:40 PM. The agenda issue was further discussed during the meeting. The sixth meeting is scheduled to be held at 9:00 AM on July 18th.

### 朝鲜停战谈判会议  昨日继续讨论议程

1951-07-18
第1版()
专栏：

　　朝鲜停战谈判会议
　　昨日继续讨论议程
    【新华社平壤十七日电】本社记者由朝鲜前线总部获悉：朝鲜停战谈判会议第五次会议在七月十七日上午十时（朝鲜时间）按时举行，下午一时四十分结束。会议中继续地讨论了议程问题。第六次会议预定在七月十八日上午九时举行。
