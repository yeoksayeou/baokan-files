Title: Korean People's Delegation to China Leaves Shanghai for Nanjing
Author:
Date: 1951-07-16
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Shanghai, 15th—The Korean People's Delegation to China and the Art Troupe, led by Head of Delegation Won Dong-geun and Deputy Head of Delegation Kim In-chun, left Shanghai for Nanjing by train on the afternoon of the 14th. Shu Tong and Chen Yisheng, Vice Chairmen of the East China Branch of the Resist America, Aid Korea Association, Liu Changsheng, Chairman of the Shanghai Branch of the Resist America, Aid Korea Association, Vice Chairmen Sheng Pihua, Ba Jin, Liu Hongsheng, Jin Zhonghua, Pan Hannian, Deputy Mayor of the Shanghai Municipal People's Government, and representatives from various democratic parties' Shanghai local organizations and people's organizations, numbering over 500, were at the station to see them off.

The Korean People's Delegation to China attended grand welcome rallies held by people from all walks of life in East China and Shanghai, and held symposiums with various democratic parties, workers, peasants, women, industrialists and businessmen, literary and art circles, and religious circles in Shanghai to convey the Korean people's gratitude to the Chinese people and exchange experiences in revolutionary struggle. In addition, the delegation also visited the Shanghai Native Produce Exhibition and Exchange Fair, the Resist America, Aid Korea Exhibition, and the Shanghai Electric Power Company and factories. On the eve of the delegation's departure, nineteen people's organizations in East China and Shanghai jointly hosted a farewell banquet.

Also reported: The Korean People's Delegation to China and the Art Troupe arrived in Nanjing on the afternoon of the 14th and were welcomed by more than 2,000 representatives from all walks of life in Nanjing. At 8:00 p.m. that day, the Nanjing Branch of the Resist America, Aid Korea Association and the Nanjing People's Representative Conference Consultative Committee hosted a banquet for the entire delegation.

### 朝鲜人民访华代表团自上海赴南京

1951-07-16
第4版()
专栏：

　　朝鲜人民访华代表团自上海赴南京
    【新华社上海十五日电】朝鲜人民访华代表团及艺术团全体人员，在团长元东根、副团长金仁春率领下，于十四日午乘车离上海赴南京。华东抗美援朝总分会副主席舒同、陈巳生，上海市抗美援朝分会主席刘长胜、副主席盛丕华、巴金、刘鸿生、金仲华，上海市人民政府副市长潘汉年，以及各民主党派上海地方组织，各人民团体代表五百余人到车站欢送。
    朝鲜人民访华代表团人员在上海曾出席了华东区和上海市各界人民分别举行的盛大欢迎会，并和上海市各民主党派、工人、农民、妇女、工商界、文艺界、宗教界等举行座谈会，传达朝鲜人民对中国人民的谢意，交流革命斗争经验。此外，代表团还参观了上海市土产展览交流会、抗美援朝展览会以及上海电力公司、工厂等。该代表团人员临行前夕，华东及上海十九个人民团体联合设宴欢送。
    又讯：朝鲜人民访华代表团及艺术团全体人员，已于十四日下午抵南京，受到南京市各界代表二千多人的欢迎。当日晚八时，南京市抗美援朝分会，南京市各界人民代表会议协商委员会曾设宴招待代表团全体人员。
