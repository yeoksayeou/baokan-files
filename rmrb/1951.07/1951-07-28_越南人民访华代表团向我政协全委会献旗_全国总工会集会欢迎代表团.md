Title: Vietnamese People's Delegation to China Presents Banner to the CPPCC National Committee, All-China Federation of Trade Unions Holds Rally to Welcome Delegation
Author:
Date: 1951-07-28
Page: 1

　　Xinhua News Agency, July 27th: The entire Vietnamese People's Delegation to China, led by head of delegation Hoang Quoc Viet, presented a banner and gifts to the National Committee of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference (CPPCC) at 9:00 AM today. Those attending the ceremony included Chen Shutong, Vice Chairman of the National Committee, and staff members of the National Committee's working conference Xing Xiping, Zheng Zhenduo, Zhu Zaoguan, Chen Qiyuan, Mei Gongbin, Xu Guangping, and others. At the beginning of the ceremony, Head of Delegation Hoang Quoc Viet delivered a congratulatory speech and then presented the banner and gifts. Chen Shutong, Vice Chairman of the National Committee of the CPPCC, accepted the banner and gifts on behalf of the National Committee and delivered a reply. Head of Delegation Hoang Quoc Viet presented a total of three silk banners, two of which were presented by the Vietnam National United Front, inscribed in both Chinese and Vietnamese with "Long Live Vietnamese-Chinese Solidarity" and "A Shining Example of the Asian National Liberation Movement." The other banner was presented by the Vietnam-China Friendship Association, inscribed with "Long Live Vietnamese-Chinese Friendship."

　　At 8:00 PM this evening, the entire Vietnamese People's Delegation to China attended a welcome rally held by the All-China Federation of Trade Unions at their invitation. Those present included Li Lisan, Vice Chairman of the All-China Federation of Trade Unions, Secretary-General Xu Zhizhen, chairmen of the national committees of various industrial trade unions, and approximately three hundred staff members of the Federation. After Li Lisan delivered a welcoming speech, Hoang Quoc Viet, head of the Vietnamese delegation, presented two silk banners to the All-China Federation of Trade Unions on behalf of the Vietnam General Confederation of Labor. Head of Delegation Hoang Quoc Viet also reported on the role of the Vietnamese working class in the Vietnamese national liberation struggle.

### 越南人民访华代表团向我政协全委会献旗  全国总工会集会欢迎代表团

1951-07-28
第1版()
专栏：

　　越南人民访华代表团
　  向我政协全委会献旗
　  全国总工会集会欢迎代表团
    【新华社二十七日讯】越南人民访华代表团全体人员在黄国越团长率领下，于今日上午九时向中国人民政治协商会议全国委员会献旗献礼。参加典礼者有全国委员会副主席陈叔通及全国委员会工作会议工作人员邢西萍、郑振铎、朱早观、陈其瑗、梅龚彬、许广平等人。典礼开始时，黄国越团长首先致祝词，然后献旗献礼。中国人民政协全国委员会副主席陈叔通代表全国委员会接受献旗献礼，并致答词。黄国越团长共献锦旗三面，其中两面是越南国民联协战线献赠的锦旗，上面用中、越两国文字写着“越华团结万岁”“亚洲民族解放运动的明镜”另一面是越华友好协会献赠的，上面写着“越华友谊万岁”。
    今日晚间八时，越南人民访华代表团全体人员应邀出席中华全国总工会举行的欢迎会。到会的有中华全国总工会副主席李立三、秘书长许之桢、各产业工会全国委员会主席及总工会工作人员约三百人。在李立三致欢迎辞后，越南代表团团长黄国越代表越南总工会向中华全国总工会献锦旗两面。黄国越团长并在会上报告了越南工人阶级在越南民族解放斗争中的作用。
