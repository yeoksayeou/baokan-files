Title: Spanish Communist Party Congratulatory Telegram on the 30th Anniversary of the CCP
Author:
Date: 1951-07-13
Page: 1

　　Congratulatory Telegram on the 30th Anniversary of the CCP
　  Spanish Communist Party Congratulatory Telegram to Chairman Mao Zedong:
    On the occasion of the glorious and heroic 30th anniversary of the founding of the Communist Party of China, I extend my respectful and warm congratulations to you on behalf of the Spanish Communist Party.
    We wish you good health and wish the Communist Party of China, which has achieved historically significant victories in the selfless struggle against the imperialist oppressors and their lackeys, thereby opening the road for the Chinese people to socialism, new victories in the struggle for the prosperity of the great People's Republic of China and for peace.
    Long live the Communist Party of China!
    Long live Mao Zedong!
        Dolores Ibárruri (signed)
          July 1951

### 祝贺中共三十周年  西班牙共产党贺电

1951-07-13
第1版()
专栏：

　　祝贺中共三十周年
　  西班牙共产党贺电毛泽东主席：
    欣逢光荣而英雄的中国共产党建党三十周年，我谨代表西班牙共产党向你致以崇敬而热诚的祝贺。
    我们谨祝你健康，并祝在反对帝国主义压迫者及其走狗的忘我斗争中取得了具有历史意义的胜利从而开辟了中国人民走向社会主义的道路的中国共产党，在争取伟大的中华人民共和国的繁荣和争取和平的斗争中获得新的胜利。
    中国共产党万岁！
    毛泽东万岁！
        多洛列斯·伊巴露丽（签字）
          一九五一年七月
