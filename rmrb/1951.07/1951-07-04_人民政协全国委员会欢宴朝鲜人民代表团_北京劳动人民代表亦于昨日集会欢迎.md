Title: National Committee of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference Hosts Banquet for Korean People's Delegation; Beijing Laboring People Also Held Rally Yesterday to Welcome Them
Author:
Date: 1951-07-04
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, 3rd: The National Committee of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference (CPPCC) hosted a banquet at Huairen Hall in Zhongnanhai at 6 p.m. on the 3rd to welcome Won Dong-geun, head of the Korean People's Delegation to China, Kim In-chun, deputy head, and all members of the delegation, as well as Han Byung-ok, head of the art troupe, and all its members.

Invited to the banquet were: Li Joo-yeon, Ambassador of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea to China, and Choi Young, Counselor of the Embassy, and others.

Attending this grand banquet were: Li Jishen, Guo Moruo, Chen Shutong, vice chairmen of the National Committee of the CPPCC, and members in Beijing; Liu Shaoqi, Zhu De, Dong Biwu, Chen Yun, Zhang Lan, Huang Yanpei, Shao Lizi, Zhang Bojun, Liu Yazi, Zhang Zhizhong, Ma Xulun, Xu Deheng, Shen Yanbing, Liang Xi, Chen Shaoxian, Chen Qiyuan, Cai Tingkai, Jiang Guangnai, Huang Shaohong, Fu Zuoyi, Luo Longji, Shi Liang, Zhou Xinmin, Shi Fuliang, Wang Shao'ao, Peng Zemin, Yan Xichun, Zhu Xuefan, Xu Guangping, Qian Sanqiang, Sha Qianli, Liao Chengzhi, head of the Chinese People's Delegation to Korea, Wang Jiaxiang and Zhang Hanfu, vice ministers of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs of the Central People's Government, and more than 300 art workers from Beijing.

During the banquet, Li Jishen, Vice Chairman of the National Committee of the CPPCC, Liu Shaoqi, Secretary of the Secretariat of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of China, Won Dong-geun, Head of the Korean People's Delegation to China, Han Byung-ok, Head of the Korean People's Art Troupe, and Li Joo-yeon, Korean Ambassador to China, delivered speeches successively.

The democratic parties, people's organizations, and the Korean People's Delegation to China repeatedly stood up to toast the health of Chairman Mao Zedong and General Kim Il-sung, the eternal friendship and unity between the Chinese and Korean people, and the final victory of the Chinese and Korean people in the war against American aggression.

The banquet proceeded in an atmosphere of friendship and unity from beginning to end.

After the banquet, the Korean People's Delegation to China presented gifts to Zhu De, Liu Shaoqi, Li Jishen, and Zhang Lan, Vice Chairmen of the Central People's Government, and Zhou Enlai, Premier of the State Council, and Dong Biwu, Chen Yun, Guo Moruo, and Huang Yanpei, Vice Premiers. The hall resounded with prolonged applause during the presentation of gifts.

In the evening, a grand welcome party was held.

[Our newspaper dispatch] According to Xinhua News Agency: The laboring people of Beijing held a meeting on the afternoon of the 3rd to welcome the Korean People's Delegation to China. Attending the meeting were representatives from factories, mines, trade unions, and suburban farmers in Beijing, totaling more than 700 people.

After Zhang Hongshun, Vice Chairman of the Beijing Federation of Trade Unions, delivered a welcome speech, Kim In-chun, Deputy Head of the Korean People's Delegation to China, presented a banner on behalf of the Korean people to the laboring people of Beijing, and spoke at the meeting. Jang Je-geun, a representative of the Korean People's Army and a combat hero, reported on his glorious combat deeds. Afterwards, representatives of the laboring people of Beijing presented flowers to the Korean People's Delegation to China. Finally, Xiao Ming, Vice Chairman of the Municipal Federation of Trade Unions, delivered a speech.

### 人民政协全国委员会欢宴朝鲜人民代表团    北京劳动人民代表亦于昨日集会欢迎

1951-07-04
第1版()
专栏：

　　人民政协全国委员会
    欢宴朝鲜人民代表团
    北京劳动人民代表亦于昨日集会欢迎
    【新华社三日讯】中国人民政治协商会议全国委员会于三日下午六时在中南海怀仁堂欢宴朝鲜人民访华代表团团长元东根、副团长金仁春和该团全体团员，以及艺术团团长韩炳钰和该团全体人员。
    应邀赴宴的，还有：朝鲜民主主义人民共和国驻华大使李周渊及大使馆参赞崔英等。
    参加这个盛大宴会的有：中国人民政治协商会议全国委员会副主席李济深、郭沫若、陈叙通及在京委员，各民主党派和人民团体负责人刘少奇、朱德、董必武、陈云、张澜、黄炎培、邵力子、章伯钧、柳亚子、张治中、马叙伦、许德珩、沈雁冰、梁希、陈劭先、陈其瑗、蔡廷锴、蒋光鼐、黄绍竑、傅作义、罗隆基、史良、周新民、施复亮、王绍鏊、彭泽民、严希纯、朱学范、许广平、钱三强、沙千里、中国人民赴朝慰问团团长廖承志，中央人民政府外交部副部长王稼祥、章汉夫、以及北京的艺术工作者等三百余人。
    宴会进行时，中国人民政协全国委员会副主席李济深，中共中央书记处书记刘少奇、朝鲜人民访华代表团团长元东根、朝鲜人民艺术团团长韩炳钰、朝鲜驻华大使李周渊曾先后致词。
    各民主党派、各人民团体和朝鲜人民访华代表团再三地起立为毛泽东主席、金日成将军的健康而干杯，为中朝人民的永恒友谊和团结而干杯，为中朝人民抗美战争最后胜利而干杯。
    宴会始终在友好与团结的空气中进行。
    宴后，朝鲜人民访华代表团向中央人民政府副主席朱德、刘少奇、李济深、张澜，政务院总理周恩来，副总理董必武、陈云、郭沫若、黄炎培献礼。献礼时，全场掌声经久不息。
    在晚上，举行了盛大的欢迎晚会。
    【本报讯】据新华社讯：北京市劳动人民三日下午举行欢迎朝鲜人民访华代表团大会。参加大会的有北京市各工厂、矿山、行业工会的代表和郊区农民代表等共七百余人。
    会议由北京市总工会副主席张鸿舜致欢迎词后，朝鲜人民访华代表团副团长金仁春代表朝鲜人民向北京市劳动人民献旗，他并在会上讲话。朝鲜人民军代表、战斗英雄张济根报告他的光荣战斗事迹。接着，北京市劳动人民代表向朝鲜人民访华代表团献花。最后，由市总工会副主席萧明致词。
