Title: After Attending the 30th Anniversary of the Mongolian People's Revolution, Our Delegation Returned to Beijing Yesterday by Special Plane
Author:
Date: 1951-07-18
Page: 1

　　After Attending the 30th Anniversary of the Mongolian People's Revolution
　  Our Delegation Returned to Beijing Yesterday by Special Plane
    [Xinhua News Agency, 17th] The special delegation of the Central People's Government to attend the 30th anniversary of the victory of the Mongolian People's Republic's revolution, a group of five people led by head of delegation Wang Zaitian, returned to Beijing by special plane at 2:30 pm on the 17th. Those who welcomed them at the airport were: Qu Wu, Deputy Secretary-General of the State Council of the Central People's Government, Yan Baohang, Deputy Director of the General Office of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs, Chen Jiakang, Acting Director of the Asian Department, Wang Zhuoru, Director of the Protocol Department, Peng Sike, Deputy Director of the General Office of the Central Nationalities Affairs Commission, and Jin Ziming, Minister of the International Liaison Department of the All-China Federation of Trade Unions. Mongolian Ambassador to China, Jalkhasaikhan, also came to welcome them.

### 参加蒙古人民革命三十周年纪念后  我代表团昨日专机返京

1951-07-18
第1版()
专栏：

　　参加蒙古人民革命三十周年纪念后
　  我代表团昨日专机返京
    【新华社十七日讯】我中央人民政府特派参加蒙古人民共和国革命胜利三十周年纪念代表团，一行五人，由团长王再天率领，已于十七日下午二时三十分专机返京。到机场欢迎者有：中央人民政府政务院副秘书长屈武，外交部办公厅副主任阎宝航，亚洲司代司长陈家康，交际处处长王倬如，中央民族事务委员会办公厅副主任朋斯克，中华全国总工会国际联络部部长金子明等。蒙古驻华大使贾尔卡赛汗亦莅场欢迎。
