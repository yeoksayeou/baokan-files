Title: Swedish New Ambassador Cedarborg Presents Credentials to Our Government
Author:
Date: 1951-07-22
Page: 1

　　Xinhua News Agency, 21st: The newly appointed Ambassador Extraordinary and Plenipotentiary of the Kingdom of Sweden to our country, Mr. Cedarborg, accompanied by First Secretary Mr. Karlqvist and Third Secretary Mr. Malmer, presented his credentials to Vice Chairman Zhang Lan of the Central People's Government of our country at 4:00 pm today. Those present included Vice Minister Zhang Hanfu of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs of the Central People's Government, Acting Chief of General Staff of the Chinese People's Liberation Army Nie Rongzhen, Director of the General Office of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs Wang Bingnan, Director of the Bureau of Ceremonies of the Central People's Government Yu Xinqing, Director of the Department of Western European and African Affairs of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs Huan Xiang, and Director of the Protocol Department Wang Zhuoru. Ambassador Cedarborg delivered a eulogy to Vice Chairman Zhang Lan when presenting his credentials. Vice Chairman Zhang Lan delivered a reply.
    Ambassador Cedarborg was born on July 14, 1900. In 1921, he served as an attaché in the Swedish Ministry of Foreign Affairs. From 1937 to 1943, he served as the Director of the Political Department of the Swedish Ministry of Foreign Affairs. From 1943 to 1944, he served as the Secretary-General of the Swedish Ministry of Foreign Affairs. From 1944 to 1946, he served as the Minister to Moscow. From 1946 to the present, he has served as the Minister to Bern, Switzerland.

### 瑞典新任大使赛德勃隆向我政府呈递国书

1951-07-22
第1版()
专栏：

　　瑞典新任大使赛德勃隆向我政府呈递国书
    【新华社二十一日讯】瑞典王国新任驻我国特命全权大使赛德勃隆于今日下午四时偕一等秘书高罗士、三等秘书马尔末，向我国中央人民政府张澜副主席呈递国书。在场者有中央人民政府外交部副部长章汉夫、中国人民解放军代总参谋长聂荣臻、外交部办公厅主任王炳南、中央人民政府典礼局局长余心清、外交部西欧非洲司司长宦乡、交际处处长王倬如。赛德勃隆大使呈递国书时曾向张澜副主席致颂词。张澜副主席致答词。
    赛德勃隆大使生于一九○○年七月十四日。一九二一年任瑞典外交部随员。一九三七年至一九四三年任瑞典外交部政治司司长。一九四三年至一九四四年任瑞典外交部秘书长。一九四四年至一九四六年任驻莫斯科公使。一九四六年至目前任驻瑞士伯尔尼公使。
