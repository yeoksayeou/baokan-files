Title: General Political Department of the People's Revolutionary Military Commission Issues Slogans Celebrating Army Day
Author:
Date: 1951-07-29
Page: 1
　　People's Revolutionary Military Commission General Political Department
    Issues Slogans Celebrating Army Day

    [Xinhua News Agency, 28th] The General Political Department of the People's Revolutionary Military Commission of the Central People's Government has issued the following slogans to celebrate the 24th anniversary of the founding of the Chinese People's Liberation Army:

    1. Celebrate the great Army Day of the Chinese People's Liberation Army!

    2. Pay tribute to the Communist Party of China and Chairman Mao, the creators and leaders of the Chinese People's Liberation Army!

    3. Pay tribute to the commanders, fighters, political workers, and logistical workers of the Chinese People's Liberation Army!

    4. Pay tribute to the Chinese People's Volunteer Army for resisting America, aiding Korea, and defending our homes and country!

    5. Pay tribute to the revolutionary disabled soldiers! Pay tribute to the glorious wounded and sick soldiers!

    6. Pay tribute to the families of revolutionary martyrs and the families of revolutionary soldiers!

    7. Eternal glory to the martyrs who gloriously sacrificed in the revolutionary war!

    8. Oppose American imperialism's aggression against Korea and Taiwan! Oppose American imperialism's arming of Japan!

    9. Resolutely defend the motherland, defend peace in the East and the world!

    10. Build a regularized and modernized national defense army!

    11. Strengthen the military and political training of the troops! Strengthen logistical work! Improve technology! Improve discipline!

    12. Promote revolutionary heroism! Promote the spirit of hard struggle and overcoming difficulties!

    13. Long live Chairman Mao!

    14. Long live Commander-in-Chief Zhu!

    15. Long Live the Communist Party of China!

    16. Long live the powerful Chinese People's Liberation Army
