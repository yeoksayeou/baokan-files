Title: Ten British Labour Party MPs Propose Motion Welcoming the Cessation of the Korean War
Author:
Date: 1951-07-09
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, London, July 6th: TASS, London, July 6th: The political correspondent of the *Daily Worker* reports: Ten Labour MPs jointly submitted a motion on July 5th, expressing their welcome for the cessation of hostilities in Korea and requesting the government to put forward a program including general recognition of the Central People's Government of the People's Republic of China and its admission to the United Nations, holding a foreign ministers' conference to resolve all current issues that divide the world into mutually hostile blocs, gradually reducing armaments to prevent further currency inflation, alleviating the crisis of war, and granting Korea freedom and independence.

The MPs who jointly signed the motion include Scholefield Allen, Strachey, Brown, Yates, Jeger, Langdon, Julius Silverman, Sydney Silverman, and Craddock.

### 英国工党议员十人提出动议  表示欢迎停止朝鲜战争

1951-07-09
第4版()
专栏：

　　英国工党议员十人提出动议
    表示欢迎停止朝鲜战争
    【新华社七日讯】塔斯社伦敦六日讯：《工人日报》政治记者报道：工党议员十人于七月五日联名提出一项动议，表示欢迎停止朝鲜的战事，要求政府提出一项纲领，这项纲领中包括普遍承认中华人民共和国中央人民政府并接纳它出席联合国、举行外长会议以求解决目前一切使世界分裂为互相敌对的两个集团的问题、逐步裁减军备以防止通货进一步膨胀、缓和战争的危机、使朝鲜获得自由和独立等等。
    联合签署这项动议的议员有施米斯、司特罗斯、卜劳恩、叶芝、杰格、郎登、朱理亚斯·席福曼，悉尼·锡尔佛曼和克拉多克等人。
