Title: Korean Armistice Talks Continue Discussion of Meeting Agenda
Author:
Date: 1951-07-12
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Pyongyang, July 11th: This reporter learned from the General Headquarters on the Korean front that the second meeting of the Korean armistice talks began at 9:00 AM (Korean time) on July 11th and ended at 2:45 PM, lasting approximately four hours excluding breaks. The second meeting continued the discussion of the meeting agenda proposed by both sides on July 10th. During the first meeting on July 10th, after speeches by our representative General Nam Il, General Deng Hua, and United Nations Command representative General Joy, both sides presented draft agendas and initially exchanged views.

### 朝鲜停战谈判会议继续讨论会议议程

1951-07-12
第1版()
专栏：

　　朝鲜停战谈判会议继续讨论会议议程
    【新华社平壤十一日电】本社记者自朝鲜前线总司令部获悉：朝鲜停战谈判的第二次会议，在七月十一日上午九时（朝鲜时间）开始，在下午二时四十五分结束，其中除休会时间外，历时共约四小时。第二次会议继续讨论了双方在七月十日所提出的会议议程。在七月十日的第一次会议中，在我方代表南日将军，邓华将军和联合国军方代表乔埃将军致词后，双方即提出了会议的议程草案，并初步地交换了意见。
