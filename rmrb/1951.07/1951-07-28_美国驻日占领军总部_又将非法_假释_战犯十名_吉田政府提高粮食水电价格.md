Title: US Occupation Headquarters in Japan Illegally "Paroles" Ten More War Criminals / Yoshida Government Raises Food and Utility Prices
Author:
Date: 1951-07-28
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, July 27th: The US Occupation Authorities in Japan continue to illegally "parole" Japanese war criminals. According to TASS, Tokyo, July 26th: The US Occupation Headquarters in Japan announced that on August 1st, it will release ten more Japanese war criminals whose sentences have not yet been completed, including Lieutenant General Shibayama, former commander of the 26th Division.

The total number of war criminals released in this way will reach 301.

Xinhua News Agency, July 27th: TASS, July 25th: According to the Japanese newspaper "Japan Times," the Yoshida government has decided to increase the price of rice and other grains by 18.5 percent starting August 1st.

The newspaper pointed out that in the near future, electricity bills will increase by 30 percent, gas bills by 20 percent, water bills by 20 to 30 percent, and train fares by 30 percent.

### 美国驻日占领军总部  又将非法“假释”战犯十名  吉田政府提高粮食水电价格

1951-07-28
第4版()
专栏：

　　美国驻日占领军总部
　  又将非法“假释”战犯十名
　  吉田政府提高粮食水电价格
    【新华社二十七日讯】美国驻日占领当局继续非法“假释”日本战犯。据塔斯社东京二十六日讯：美国驻日占领军总部宣称：八月一日将再释放刑期未满的日本战犯十名，其中有前第二十六师团的师团长柴山中将。
    这样被释放的战犯人数合计将达三百零一名。
    【新华社二十七日讯】塔斯社二十五日讯：据日本报纸《日本时报》载称：吉田政府已决定从八月一日起把食米和其他谷类的价格增加百分之十八点五。
    该报指出：在不久的将来，电费将增加百分之三十，煤气费增加百分之二十，自来水费增加百分之二十至三十，火车运费增加百分之三十。
