Title: People from All Walks of Life in Wuxi City Decide to Donate Thirty-Three Aircraft. Yongding, Fujian, Donates a "Henlu" Aircraft

Author:
Date: 1951-07-11
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Wuxi, 10th. People from all walks of life in Wuxi City held a representative meeting on July 6th to Resist U.S. Aggression and Aid Korea to discuss how to implement the July 4th notice of the China People's Committee to Resist US Aggression and Aid Korea. At the meeting, representatives from all walks of life unanimously agreed that the movement to resist U.S. Aggression and Aid Korea and defend our homes and country must not be relaxed in the slightest due to the Korean armistice negotiations. Worker representatives reported that they had decided to donate five fighter planes; representatives from the industrial and commercial sectors reported that they had decided to donate twenty-seven fighter planes; representatives from other sectors also decided to jointly donate one fighter plane, for a total of thirty-three planes. More than 600 representatives present at the meeting unanimously passed a resolution calling on the city's 400,000 people to resolutely complete the five tasks outlined in the July 4th notice of the China People's Committee to Resist US Aggression and Aid Korea.
    
Xinhua News Agency, Fuzhou, 10th. The people of Yongding County, Fujian Province, the hometown of martyr Liao Henlu, deputy head of the Second Sub-Group of the Chinese People's Delegation to Korea, have decided to purchase and donate a "Henlu" fighter plane. More than 5,000 representatives from all walks of life in the county solemnly held a memorial meeting for martyr Liao Henlu on June 22nd. At the meeting, a resolution to donate a "Henlu" fighter plane was unanimously adopted. The people's currency collected on the spot by the representatives attending the meeting reached more than 20 million yuan. In other news: The All-China Federation of Returned Overseas Chinese Association of Jinjiang County, Fujian Province, held a joint meeting of all committee members and heads of branch associations in Quanzhou City on July 2nd, and decided to mobilize all returned overseas Chinese and their relatives in the county to actively expand production, increase income, and save expenditures to donate and purchase a "Jinjiang Overseas Chinese" fighter plane.

### 无锡市各界人民  决定献机卅三架  福建永定献“亨禄号”飞机一架

1951-07-11
第1版()
专栏：

　　无锡市各界人民
    决定献机卅三架
    福建永定献“亨禄号”飞机一架
    【新华社无锡十日电】无锡市各界人民于六日举行抗美援朝代表会议，讨论如何贯彻执行中国人民抗美援朝总会七月四日的通知。会上，各界人民代表一致认为：抗美援朝保家卫国运动决不能因朝鲜停火谈判而有丝毫松懈。工人代表报告他们决定捐献战斗机五架；工商界代表报告他们决定捐献战斗机二十七架；其他各界代表也决定联合捐献战斗机一架，共三十三架。到会六百余代表并一致通过决议，号召全市四十万人民坚决完成中国人民抗美援朝总会七月四日通知中指出的五项任务。
    【新华社福州十日电】中国人民赴朝慰问团第二分团副团长廖亨禄烈士的故乡——福建省永定县的人民，决定购献“亨禄号”战斗机一架。该县各界人民代表五千余人曾于六月二十二日隆重举行廖亨禄烈士追悼大会。会上，全体通过捐献“亨禄号”战斗机一架的决议。到会代表当场捐集的人民币即达二千多万元。另讯：福建省晋江县归国华侨联谊会于七月二日在泉州市举行全体委员暨各区分会负责人联席会议，决定动员全县归国华侨与侨眷，积极扩大生产，增加收入和节约支出，捐献购置“晋江华侨号”战斗机一架。
