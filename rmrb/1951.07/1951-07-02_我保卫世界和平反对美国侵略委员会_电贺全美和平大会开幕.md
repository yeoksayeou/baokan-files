Title: China Peace Committee Sends Congratulations to the All-America Peace Assembly

Author: 
Date: 1951-07-02
Page: 4

　　China Peace Committee Sends Congratulations to the All-America Peace Assembly

　　[Xinhua News Agency, 29th] The China Peace Committee cabled congratulations on the opening of the All-America Peace Assembly. The text of the cable is as follows: All-America Peace Assembly, Chicago, Illinois, USA:

　　On behalf of the entire Chinese people, we warmly congratulate you on the opening of the All-America Peace Assembly. The US government's continued pursuit of policies of aggression and expansion of war violates the will and interests of the peace-loving American people. We firmly believe that your Assembly will further mobilize the American people to unite with the peace-loving people of the world to strive for a swift peaceful resolution of the Korean question, the signing of comprehensive and just peace treaties with democratic Germany and Japan, and the establishment of a Five-Power Peace Pact. We celebrate the success of the Assembly.
                         China Peace Committee
                                      June 29, Beijing

### 我保卫世界和平反对美国侵略委员会  电贺全美和平大会开幕

1951-07-02
第4版()
专栏：

　　我保卫世界和平反对美国侵略委员会
    电贺全美和平大会开幕
    【新华社二十九日讯】中国人民保卫世界和平反对美国侵略委员会电贺全美和平大会开幕，电文如下：美国伊利诺斯州芝加哥城全美和平大会：
    我们代表全中国人民，热烈祝贺全美和平大会的开幕。美国政府继续推行侵略和扩大战争的政策，是违反爱好和平的美国人民的意志和利益的。我们深信，贵会将会进一步动员美国人民，和全世界爱好和平的人民团结一致，为争取迅速和平解决朝鲜问题，与民主的德国和日本签订全面的公正和约，并成立五大国和平公约而斗争。我们庆祝大会的成功。
                        中国人民保卫世界和平反对美国侵略委员会
                                      六月二十九日于北京
