Title: Our Great Motherland (Picture)
Author:
Date: 1951-07-24
Page: 1
    Our Great Motherland
    The Beijing Democratic Women's Federation has organized family working women to engage in handicraft production. This is a scene of female workers in the embroidery factory in Haidian, a suburb of Beijing, at work. Due to the development of their business, they are preparing to organize 6,000 family working women to participate in this production throughout the year.

### 我们伟大的祖国（图片）

1951-07-24
第1版()
专栏：

    我们伟大的祖国
    北京市民主妇女联合会组织了家庭劳动妇女进行手工艺生产。这是京郊海甸的挑补花工厂的女工们正在工作的情形。由于业务的发展，她们正准备全年组织六千个家庭劳动妇女参加这种生产。
