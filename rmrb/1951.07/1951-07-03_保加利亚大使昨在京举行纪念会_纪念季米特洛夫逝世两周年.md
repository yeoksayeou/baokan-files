Title: Bulgarian Ambassador Holds Memorial Service in Beijing Yesterday Commemorating the Second Anniversary of Dimitrov's Death
Author:
Date: 1951-07-03
Page: 1

　　Xinhua News Agency, July 2nd—The Embassy of the People's Republic of Bulgaria in China held a memorial service in Beijing at 8:00 PM today to commemorate the second anniversary of the death of the Bulgarian people's leader, Dimitrov. Among those attending the memorial service were Guo Moruo, Vice Premier of the Government Administration Council of the Central People's Government, Wu Xiuquan, Deputy Minister of Foreign Affairs, and other government leaders, Peng Zhen, member of the Political Bureau of the Central Committee of the Chinese Communist Party, Lu Dingyi, Xu Teli and Liao Chengzhi, members of the Central Committee, and heads of various people's organizations, totaling over 200 people. The ambassadors of the Soviet Union, Czechoslovakia, North Korea, India, the German Democratic Republic, Mongolia, and Hungary, the minister of Switzerland, and embassy personnel from Romania, Poland, Sweden, Burma, Indonesia, and Pakistan also attended the memorial service.
    Bulgarian Ambassador Petkov delivered a brief memorial speech at the meeting, followed by the screening of a film about Dimitrov's funeral.

### 保加利亚大使昨在京举行纪念会  纪念季米特洛夫逝世两周年

1951-07-03
第1版()
专栏：

　　保加利亚大使昨在京举行纪念会
    纪念季米特洛夫逝世两周年
    【新华社二日讯】保加利亚人民共和国驻华大使馆于今日下午八时在北京举行纪念会，纪念保加利亚人民领袖季米特洛夫逝世两周年。出席纪念会的有中央人民政府政务院副总理郭沫若，外交部副部长伍修权等政府首长，中国共产党中央委员会政治局委员彭真，中央委员陆定一、徐特立和廖承志，以及各人民团体负责人等二百余人。苏联、捷克斯洛伐克、朝鲜、印度、德意志民主共和国、蒙古和匈牙利等国的大使及瑞士公使、以及罗马尼亚、波兰、瑞典、缅甸、印尼和巴基斯坦等国使馆人员也都参加了纪念会。
    保加利亚大使彼得科夫在会上发表了简短的纪念演说，接着放映了关于季米特洛夫葬礼的影片。
