Title: British Peace Committee Demands Facilitation of Korean Armistice
Author:
Date: 1951-07-29
Page: 4

　　British Peace Committee
　　Demands Facilitation of Korean Armistice
　　Xinhua News Agency, Prague, July 28th: The London "Daily Worker" reported: The British Peace Committee requested the British government on July 24th to intensify efforts to bring about a genuine armistice in Korea. The committee said: "Although 'ceasefire' negotiations are underway, the slaughter in Korea continues, planes continue to bomb indiscriminately, and battlefield attacks have not ceased. All honest people are indignant about this situation."

### 英国和平委员会  要求促成朝鲜停战

1951-07-29
第4版()
专栏：

　　英国和平委员会
　  要求促成朝鲜停战
    【新华社布拉格二十八日电】伦敦《工人日报》报道：英国和平委员会于七月二十四日要求英国政府加紧努力，以在朝鲜促成真正的停战。该委员会说：“虽然‘停火’谈判正在进行，可是，朝鲜境内的屠杀仍在继续着，飞机仍在滥施轰炸，战场上的攻击行动还没有停止，一切正直的人们对这种情形都感到愤慨。”
