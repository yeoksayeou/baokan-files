Title: The People of the Whole Country Send Telegrams to Congratulate the Party on Its Thirtieth Anniversary
Author:
Date: 1951-07-12
Page: 1

　　The people of the whole country, including individuals and groups, sent telegrams or letters to the Central Committee of the Communist Party of China and Chairman Mao to express their warm congratulations on the 30th anniversary of the Party. The names of these groups and individuals, in addition to those already published, are published successively as follows:
　　The entire students of the Second Group of the Teachers' Research Class of the Nanjing Municipal Bureau of Culture and Education, the entire students of the Ninth Group, the entire students of the Fourteenth Group, the entire students of the Eighth Group, and the entire students of the Fifteenth Group. All members of the General Branch of the Wuxi Girls' Middle School of the Communist Youth League of China in Sunan District. All non-Party staff members of the Shandong Provincial People's Court. All members of the Reading Group of Xiaojie Sections 7 and 8 in the Sixth District of Nanjing. Lou Caihua and other seven people from the Nanjing Unemployed Workers Relief Committee. All teachers and students of the English Group of the Foreign Languages Department of Nanjing University. Twenty-eight students of the Political Science Department of Nanjing University. Fifteen students of the History Department of Nanjing University. Shen Qi and other seven faculty members of the Nanjing School of the Cadre School of the Ministry of Communications of the Central People's Government. All forty-two second-year students of the Department of Mechanical Engineering of Nanjing University. All employees of Nanjing Daxing Machinery and Automobile Parts Manufacturing Factory. All students of Hangzhou Normal School of Zhejiang Province. All teachers, students and workers of Suzhou Municipal Staff and Workers' Children School. All students of the first, second, and third groups of the first brigade of the Workers' Cadre Training Class of Sijing Town, Songjiang Special Zone, Sunan District. All comrades of the Dangtu Office of the Nanjing Branch of the China Salt Industry Company. All employees of the Caishi Post Office in Anhui Province. Celebration of the 30th Anniversary of the Founding of the Communist Party of China by Zhejiang Medical College, Zhejiang Provincial Hangzhou Senior Medical Vocational School, and Guangji Nursing School. All students of the Fourth Study Group of the State-owned Shanghai No. 18 Cotton Textile Factory. All members of the Shanghai Private Nanping Primary School of the Chinese Young Pioneers. Celebration of the 30th Anniversary of the Founding of the Communist Party of China by the Guangzhou Municipal Committee of the China Textile Workers' Union. All members of the Shanghai Xujiahui Post Office Committee. The Preparatory Committee of the Federation of Industry and Commerce and the entire business community of Shenzhen Town, Baoan County, Guangdong Province. All teachers, students and staff of Guilin Private Yixian Middle School. All employees of the Jiujiang Special Zone People's Hospital of Jiangxi Province. Celebration of the 30th Anniversary of the Founding of the Communist Party of China by the East China Branch of the Central Academy of Fine Arts. The Preparatory Committee of the Wuhu Municipal Federation of Industry and Commerce. Farmers of Qingtan Village, Kong'ao Township, Yongquan District, Linhai County, Zhejiang Province.

### 全国人民电贺党的三十周年

1951-07-12
第1版()
专栏：

　　全国人民电贺党的三十周年
    在党的三十周年纪念日，全国各地人民，包括个人和团体，纷纷打电报或写信给中国共产党中央委员会和毛主席，表示热烈的祝贺。这些团体和个人的名单，除已发表者外，陆续发表如下：          
    南京市文教局教师研究班第二组全体同学、第九组全体学员、第十四小组全体学员、第八组全体学员、第十五小组全体学员。中国新民主主义青年团苏南区无锡市女中总支部全体团员。山东省人民法院全体非党工作人员。南京市第六区晓街第七、八段读报组全体组员。南京市失业工人救济委员会楼才华等七人。南京大学外文系英文组全体师生员工。南京大学政治系学生二十八人。南京大学历史系学生十五人。中央人民政府交通部干部学校南京校教职员沈圻等七人。南京大学机械系二年级全体四十二人。南京大兴机械汽材制造厂全体职工。浙江省立杭州师范全体同学。苏州市市立职工子弟学校全体师生工友。苏南区松江专区泗泾镇工人干部训练班第一大队第一、二、三组全体学员。中国盐业公司南京分公司当涂办事处全体同志。安徽省采石邮局全体职工。浙江医学院、浙江省立杭州高级医事职业学校、广济护士学校庆祝中国共产党成立三十周年纪念大会。国营上海第十八棉纺织厂学习第四大组全体学员。中国少年儿童队上海市私立南屏小学全体队员。中国纺织工会广州市委员会庆祝中国共产党成立三十周年大会。上海徐家汇邮局委员会全体会员。广东省宝安县深圳镇工商联筹委会暨全体工商界。桂林市私立逸仙中学全体师生员工。江西省立九江专区人民医院全体职工。中央美术学院华东分院庆祝中国共产党成立三十周年大会。芜湖市工商业联合会筹备委员会。浙江省临海县涌泉区孔岙乡清潭村农民。
