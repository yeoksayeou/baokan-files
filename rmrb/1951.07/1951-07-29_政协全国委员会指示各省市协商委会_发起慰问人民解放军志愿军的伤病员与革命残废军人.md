Title: CPPCC National Committee Directs Provincial and Municipal Consultative Committees to Launch Comfort Campaign for Wounded and Sick Soldiers and Revolutionary Disabled Soldiers of the People's Liberation Army and the Chinese People's Volunteers

Author:

Date: 1951-07-29
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, July 28th: The National Committee of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference (CPPCC) issued a directive on the 27th to the consultative committees of all provinces and municipalities, instructing them to launch a comfort campaign for the wounded and sick soldiers and revolutionary disabled soldiers of the Chinese People's Liberation Army and the Chinese People's Volunteers around the "August 1st" Army Day. The telegram reads as follows:
To the People's Governments of all provinces and municipalities, forwarded to the consultative committees of all provinces and municipalities:
In commemoration of the "August 1st" Army Day, we hereby request that you invite local democratic parties and people's organizations to cooperate with the local branches of the Resist America, Aid Korea Federation to launch a comfort campaign for the wounded and sick soldiers of the Chinese People's Liberation Army and the Chinese People's Volunteers, as well as revolutionary disabled soldiers in various regions, on or around August 1st.
The National Committee of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference
July 27th

### 政协全国委员会指示各省市协商委会  发起慰问人民解放军志愿军的伤病员与革命残废军人

1951-07-29
第1版()
专栏：

　　政协全国委员会指示各省市协商委会
　  发起慰问人民解放军志愿军的伤病员与革命残废军人
    【新华社二十八日讯】中国人民政治协商会议全国委员会于二十七日指示各省、市协商委员会于“八一”前后对中国人民解放军，中国人民志愿军的伤病员与革命残废军人应发起慰问。电文如下：各省市人民政府转各省、市协商委员会：
    为纪念“八一”建军节，特电请你会邀集当地各民主党派、各人民团体配合地方抗美援朝分会，于八月一日或其前后，对中国人民解放军、中国人民志愿军的伤病员，与各地革命残废军人发起慰问。
      中国人民政治协商会议全国委员会
      七月二十七日
