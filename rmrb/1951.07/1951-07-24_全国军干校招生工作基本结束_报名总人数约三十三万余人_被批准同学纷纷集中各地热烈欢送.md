Title: National Military Cadre School Enrollment Basically Completed; Total Number of Applicants Approximately 330,000; Approved Students Gather in Various Locations for Enthusiastic Send-offs

Author:

Date: 1951-07-24
Page: 1

[Report from this newspaper] The national military cadre school enrollment work has now basically concluded. According to incomplete statistics from the six major administrative regions of Northeast, North China, East China, Central South, Southwest, and Northwest, the number of applicants reached over 330,000. Students approved from various regions are gathering, preparing to study at various military cadre schools. Students remaining at schools in Beijing, Tianjin, Shanghai, Wuhan, Guangzhou, Chongqing, Xi'an, and other places, along with people from all walks of life, held grand send-off meetings. Representatives from various military cadre schools were also dispatched to welcome students to the schools. Beijing held a send-off meeting for more than 4,000 people on the 12th. Many teachers and parents came to participate. Tianjin held a send-off meeting on the 13th. At the meeting, Wang Laowu, vice chairman of the Tianjin Federation of Trade Unions, represented the workers of Tianjin in expressing that they would use practical actions of increasing production to send off the students participating in national defense construction. Various regions in Northeast China are enthusiastically sending off the admitted students to school. At the send-off meeting in Harbin, Tao Wenzhong's mother, Zhou Yingxue, read aloud a letter she wrote to Chairman Mao, saying: "I am sending my seventeen-year-old child to a military school, handing him over to our people's army, handing him over to you, and I feel very relieved." Wuhan held a send-off meeting on the morning of the 15th, after which the students participating in the military cadre school paraded through the streets in a large convoy of "Glory Cars." Citizens braved the rain, crowding both sides of the road, warmly applauding and cheering the students participating in the military cadre school. Students in Xi'an assured the students participating in the military cadre school: "We who remain in school must study hard and dedicate ourselves to the motherland at any time." Many students in Shanghai checked their patriotic pacts and completed their study plans to send off the students participating in the military cadre school. Students from various regions participating in the military cadre school also expressed to the teachers, students, parents, and people from all walks of life who remained in school when leaving: We will never fail to live up to everyone's expectations, and after arriving at the military cadre school, we will definitely study hard and defend our great motherland.

### 全国军干校招生工作基本结束  报名总人数约三十三万余人  被批准同学纷纷集中各地热烈欢送

1951-07-24
第1版()
专栏：

　　全国军干校招生工作基本结束
　  报名总人数约三十三万余人
　  被批准同学纷纷集中各地热烈欢送
    【本报讯】全国各地军事干部学校招生工作，现已基本结束。根据东北、华北、华东、中南、西南、西北六大行政区的不完全统计，报名人数达三十三万余人。各地被批准同学正纷纷集中，准备到各种军事干部学校学习。北京、天津、上海、武汉、广州、重庆、西安等地的留校同学们和各界人民都举行了盛大的欢送会。各军事干部学校也派出代表迎接同学入校。北京市于十二日举行四千多人的欢送会。许多老师和家长都赶来参加。天津市在十三日举行了欢送会。会上天津市总工会副主席王老五代表天津市工人向参加军事干部学校同学表示，要以增加生产的实际行动来欢送参加国防建设的同学。东北各地正热烈欢送被录取的学生入校。在哈尔滨市的欢送大会上，陶文中的母亲周英学朗读了她给毛主席写的信，信上说：“我把十七岁的孩子送到军事学校，把他交给咱们人民的队伍，把他交给您，我非常放心。”武汉市在十五日上午举行欢送会后，参加军事干部学校的同学们乘着大队的“光荣车”在街上游行。市民们冒着雨，拥挤在马路两边，向参加军干校同学热烈鼓掌欢呼。西安市的同学们向参加军事干部学校的同学们保证：“我们留在学校里一定要努力学习，随时献身祖国。”上海市很多同学以检查爱国公约，完成学习计划来欢送参加军事干部学校同学。各地参加军事干部学校同学在离开学校时也向留校的老师、同学、家长及各界人民表示：决不辜负大家的期望，到了军事干部学校后，一定努力学习，保卫伟大的祖国。
