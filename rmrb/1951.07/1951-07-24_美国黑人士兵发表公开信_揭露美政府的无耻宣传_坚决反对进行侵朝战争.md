Title: American Black Soldiers Publish Open Letter Exposing the Shameless Propaganda of the U.S. Government and Resolutely Opposing the War of Aggression Against Korea
Author:
Date: 1951-07-24
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, New York, 23rd: According to a dispatch from TASS, the largest Black newspaper in the United States, the *Pittsburgh Courier*, published a letter on July 14th written by fifty-four American Black soldiers from Massachusetts. This letter sharply exposed the U.S. government's shameless deception of the people into fighting in Korea for "freedom," and it has aroused widespread attention among Black Americans and progressive whites.

The letter reads: "Why are we soldiers? Why is America fighting in Korea? We know we are not alone in asking these questions. More people in the American army are asking this question, and so are others, many mothers, fathers, wives, lovers, children, brothers, and sisters who want the right answers."

The letter continues: "When some of us get leave to go home, uniforms don't matter much to white people in the South or the North, the color of our skin does. So we are still discriminated against...still living in slums and earning low wages. Mothers and children are still wandering around the country. We have no freedom."

"Thinking about all this, what are we fighting for? …Can America give freedom to other colored people in other countries when we don't have freedom at home? Hardly. You can't give what you don't have."

"In our opinion, this war is entirely a war for wealthy white supremacists. He sits comfortably in a big Cadillac, chauffeured around. He sunbathes on Miami Beach. He talks about freedom, but he does everything he can to trample on us and prevent us from getting the same freedom at home."

The letter concludes: "We Black people are being asked to fight hard in Asia and Europe, but we don't enjoy freedom at home; we believe we Black people should express our opinions before it's too late. If enough of us unite, we believe we can achieve peace and freedom, because there is strength in unity."

### 美国黑人士兵发表公开信  揭露美政府的无耻宣传  坚决反对进行侵朝战争

1951-07-24
第4版()
专栏：

　　美国黑人士兵发表公开信
　  揭露美政府的无耻宣传
　  坚决反对进行侵朝战争
    【新华社二十三日讯】据电通社纽约讯：美国最大的黑人报纸《匹兹堡新闻报》在七月十四日登载了马萨诸塞州五十四个美国黑人兵士写的一封信。这封信尖锐地揭露了美国政府欺骗人民到朝鲜为“自由”而战的无耻嘴脸，它已在美国黑人和进步白人中间引起了广泛的注意。
    这封信写道：“为什么我们要当兵？为什么美国要在朝鲜作战？我们知道不但我们有这种问题。美国军队中有更多人在问这个问题，其他的人也是如此，许多母亲、父亲、妻子、爱人、孩子、兄弟、姊妹也希望得到正确的答案。”
    这封信接着又说：“当我们中间有些人请假回家时，无论对于南方或北方的白人说来，军服并不很重要，重要的是我们皮肤的颜色。因此我们仍旧受到歧视……仍旧是陋巷和低工资。母亲们和孩子们仍旧在全国流浪。我们没有自由。”
    “想到这一切，我们去作战是为了什么呢？……当我们在国内还没有自由的时候，美国还能把自由给予其他国家的其他有色人民吗？很难。你不能把你所没有的东西给人家。”
    “在我们看来，这个战争完全是富有的白种优越主义者的战争。他安稳地坐在大卡迪拉克汽车里游荡，有车夫给他开车。他在迈阿密海滩作日光浴。他嘴上说着自由，脚下却尽力践踏我们，使我们在国内得不到同样的自由”。
    这封信最后说：“我们黑人被人要求到亚洲和欧洲去艰苦作战，在国内却享不到自由；我们认为我们黑人应该发表我们的意见，免得为时太晚。如果我们有足够的人团结在一起，我们相信我们便能够得到和平和自由，因为团结就有力量。”
