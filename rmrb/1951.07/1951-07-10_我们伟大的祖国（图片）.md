Title: Our Great Motherland (Picture)
Author:
Date: 1951-07-10

    Our Great Motherland
    Among the Chinese People's Volunteers on the Korean front, many female comrades, like their male counterparts, undertake various demanding tasks. Here are several female nurses of the Chinese People's Volunteers, washing the bandages of wounded Volunteers in the icy river water.

### 我们伟大的祖国（图片）

1951-07-10
第1版()
专栏：

    我们伟大的祖国
    在朝鲜前线的中国人民志愿军中，有许多女同志和男同志一样，担任着各种紧张的工作。这里是中国人民志愿军的几位女护士，她们正在冰冷的河水中洗涤志愿军伤员的绷带。
