Title: Korean Armistice Talks Continue Discussion on Military Demarcation Line Issue
Author:
Date: 1951-07-31
Page: 1

　　Xinhua News Agency, Pyongyang, July 30th: This reporter learned from the headquarters on the Korean front that the fourteenth meeting of the Korean armistice talks began at 10:00 am (Korean time) on July 30th. At 1:05 pm, the United Nations Command delegation proposed a recess. The meeting continued to discuss the issue of establishing a military demarcation line to create a demilitarized zone. The fifteenth meeting is scheduled to be held at 10:00 am on July 31st.

### 朝鲜停战谈判会议续讨论军事分界线问题

1951-07-31
第1版()
专栏：

　　朝鲜停战谈判会议续讨论军事分界线问题
    【新华社平壤三十日电】本社记者自朝鲜前线总部获悉：朝鲜停战谈判会议第十四次会议在七月三十日上午十时（朝鲜时间）开始。下午一时零五分联合国军代表团提议休会。会议中继续讨论确立军事分界线以建立非军事地区的问题。第十五次会议定于七月三十一日上午十时举行。
