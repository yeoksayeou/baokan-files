Title: Long Live the Chinese Communist Party! Long Live Chairman Mao! (Picture)
Author:
Date: 1951-07-04
Page: 5

Long Live the Chinese Communist Party! Long Live Chairman Mao!
    Chairman Mao arrived at the venue at 6:00 PM. The entire audience stood up to pay their respects. Slogans of "Long Live Chairman Mao!" and "Long Live the Chinese Communist Party!" along with cheers and applause resounded for a long time.
    Chen Shutong, Vice Chairman of the National Committee of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference, spoke at the meeting.
    The National Committee of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference and various parties and organizations held a grand reception at Huairentang, Zhongnanhai, at 7:00 PM on July 1st to celebrate the 30th anniversary of the founding of the Chinese Communist Party.
    Liu Shaoqi, Secretary of the Secretariat of the CPC Central Committee, spoke at the meeting.
    The grand celebration of the 30th anniversary of the founding of the Chinese Communist Party was solemnly held at the Beijing Xianongtan Stadium at 6:00 PM on June 30th. More than 40,000 people attended the meeting.
    A corner of the meeting venue.
    Railway workers participating in the meeting raised their hands and shouted slogans of "Long Live Chairman Mao!" and "Long Live the Chinese Communist Party!" to welcome the arrival of Chairman Mao.
    Communist Party members and Communist Youth League members in the Chinese People's Liberation Army participating in the meeting.
    The Beijing Machine General Factory of the Central People's Government's Ministry of Heavy Industry, the职工学校 of the Beijing Federation of Trade Unions, the Central Institute for Nationalities, the Beijing Municipal Second Women's Middle School, the directly affiliated spare-time training class of the Railway Bureau, and the Beijing Fangjia Hutong Elementary School jointly held a celebration party on June 29th to celebrate "July 1st."
    Workers at the parts factory of the Changxindian Locomotive Factory produced a large number of parts as a gift for "July 1st." The picture shows the inspection of parts.
    During the meeting, 300 Young Pioneers presented flowers to Chairman Mao. The picture shows the Young Pioneers' flower-presenting team heading towards the podium.

### 中国共产党万岁！毛主席万岁！（图片）

1951-07-04
第5版()
专栏：

　　中国共产党万岁！毛主席万岁！
    毛主席于下午六时莅临会场。全场起立致敬。“毛主席万岁！”“中国共产党万岁！”的口号声、欢呼声和鼓掌声经久不息。
    中国人民政治协商会议全国委员会副主席陈叔通在大会上讲话。
    中国人民政治协商会议全国委员会及各党派团体于七月一日下午七时，在中南海怀仁堂举行了盛大的酒会，庆祝中国共产党成立三十周年纪念日。
    中共中央书记处书记刘少奇在大会上讲话。
    中国共产党成立三十周年庆祝大会于六月三十日下午六时在北京先农坛体育场隆重举行，出席大会的共有四万余人。
    大会会场之一角。
    参加大会的铁路工人举手高呼“毛主席万岁！”“中国共产党万岁！”的口号，欢迎毛主席的莅临。    
    参加大会的中国人民解放军中的共产党员和青年团员。
    中央人民政府重工业部北京机器总厂、北京市总工会职工学校、中央民族学院、北京市立第二女子中学、铁路局直属业余训练班、北京市方家胡同小学等于六月二十九日联合举行庆祝“七一”联欢大会。
    长辛店机车厂的零件厂工人做出大批的零件，作为“七一”的献礼。图为检查零件时的情形。
    大会进行中，三百名少年儿童队员向毛主席献花。图为少年儿童队的献花队伍走向主席台时的情形。
