Title: Ngapoi and His Party Arrive in Qamdo
Author:
Date: 1951-07-24
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Chongqing, July 23rd: Qamdo news: Ngapoi, the chief plenipotentiary representative of the Tibetan local government, and his party arrived in Qamdo on the 17th of this month.

### 阿沛等一行抵达昌都

1951-07-24
第1版()
专栏：

　　阿沛等一行抵达昌都
    【新华社重庆二十三日电】昌都消息：西藏地方政府首席全权代表阿沛等一行，已于本月十七日抵达昌都。
