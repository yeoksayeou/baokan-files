Title: Panchen Thanks Mayor Peng Zhen by Telegram

Author: 
Date: 1951-07-08
Page: 1

　　[Beijing News Agency] Panchen Erdeni sent a telegram to Mayor Peng Zhen to express his gratitude. The telegram read: "To Mayor Peng Zhen of the Beijing Municipal People's Government: During Panchen's visit to Beijing to pay respects to Chairman Mao, I received your kind care and warm hospitality for over a month. The courtesy was utmost, and the consideration was thorough. I will never forget your great friendship and profound affection. After departing, I arrived safely in Xining on the 23rd of this month (June) and returned to the Ta'er Monastery on the 26th. I hereby send this telegram to express my thanks and pay my respects. Panchen Erdeni, June 26th."
    Mayor Peng replied by telegram on July 2nd, saying: "To Mr. Panchen Erdeni: I am delighted to hear that you have safely arrived at Ta'er Monastery. Mayor of Beijing, Peng Zhen, July 2nd."

### 班禅电谢彭真市长

1951-07-08
第1版()
专栏：

　　班禅电谢彭真市长
    【京市新闻处讯】班禅额尔德尼先生电彭真市长致谢，电文说：“北京市人民政府彭市长：班禅此次晋京致敬毛主席，在月余当中备承亲切爱护，热烈招待，既礼遇之备至，复照顾之周详，盛谊隆情，永矢不忘，别后于本月（六月）漾（二十三日）日安抵西宁，宥（二十六日）日返回塔尔寺，谨电申谢并致敬礼。班禅额尔德尼巳（六月）宥（二十六日）”
    彭市长于七月二日复电说：“班禅额尔德尼先生：接巳宥来电敬悉已安抵塔尔寺，无任欣慰。北京市市长彭真七月二日”
