Title: The Greatest Honor for Warriors Defending World Peace! Joliot-Curie and Mrs. Gordon Receive Stalin Peace Prize in Soviet Capital

Author:

Date: 1951-07-09
Page: 4

　　The Greatest Honor for Warriors Defending World Peace!
    Joliot-Curie and Mrs. Gordon Receive Stalin Peace Prize in Soviet Capital
    [Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, 8th] According to TASS, a ceremony was held in the Kremlin, Moscow, on the 7th to award the International Stalin Prize "For Strengthening Peace Among Nations" to Joliot-Curie, Chairman of the World Peace Council. The ceremony was attended by Tikhonov, Chairman of the Soviet Peace Committee, and representatives from all walks of life. Mrs. Gordon and General Hara, also recipients of the International Stalin Prize "For Strengthening Peace Among Nations," attended the grand ceremony. Skobeltsyn, Chairman of the International Stalin Peace Prize Committee, presented Joliot-Curie with a certificate and a gold medal bearing Stalin's image. Nesmeyanov, President of the Soviet Academy of Sciences, and Tikhonov delivered congratulatory speeches.
    In his acceptance speech, Joliot-Curie said that all honest people around the world believe that the establishment of the Stalin Peace Prize is a concrete demonstration of the Soviet people's strong will for peace. Receiving the Peace Prize is the greatest honor of my life. It strengthens my confidence and gives me new strength to persevere in my work, so that our children will never know the horrors of a new war, so that science will not criminally betray its goals, and so that the fruits of all working people's efforts will become the highest happiness. Joliot-Curie's speech was met with extremely warm applause.
    [Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, 5th] According to TASS, a ceremony was held in the Kremlin, Moscow, on the 2nd to award the International Stalin Prize "For Strengthening Peace Among Nations" to Mrs. Gordon, President of the Women's International Democratic Federation and an active fighter for the defense of world peace. The ceremony was attended by Vice-Chairman Grekov of the Soviet Peace Committee and representatives from all walks of life. Skobeltsyn, Chairman of the International Stalin Peace Prize Committee, presented Mrs. Gordon with a certificate and a gold medal and delivered a congratulatory speech. Vice-Chairman of the Soviet Peace Committee and Academician Grekov, Chairman of the Soviet Women's Anti-Fascist Committee Popova, and Professor Kishelev delivered congratulatory speeches. In her acceptance speech, Mrs. Gordon expressed her gratitude for receiving the prize and expressed her determination to contribute even greater efforts to the cause of defending peace. She said, "Awarding this noble prize to me is a recognition of the heroic actions of women around the world in the struggle to defend world peace. Women in various countries are now further展开ing the signature campaign for the Peace Covenant Declaration. Recently, the Executive Committee of the Women's International Democratic Federation Sofia Conference called on women from various countries to actively sign the declaration. I believe that my winning the Stalin International Peace Prize will help to further expand the influence of the Women's International Democratic Federation and further strengthen the struggle of women around the world to defend peace."
    [Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, 8th] According to TASS, the Soviet Stalin International Peace Prize Committee held a grand reception in Moscow on the 6th for the recipients of the International Stalin Prize "For Strengthening Peace Among Nations" who are currently in Moscow: Joliot-Curie, Chairman of the World Peace Council, Mrs. Gordon, President of the Women's International Democratic Federation, and General Hara, former Minister of the Navy of Mexico. The meeting was attended by many well-known Soviet scientists, writers, artists, and representatives from all walks of life. The reception proceeded in a warm and friendly atmosphere.

### 保卫世界和平战士的最大荣誉！约里奥—居里与戈登夫人在苏京接受斯大林和平奖金

1951-07-09
第4版()
专栏：

　　保卫世界和平战士的最大荣誉！
    约里奥—居里与戈登夫人在苏京接受斯大林和平奖金
    【新华社八日讯】据塔斯社莫斯科讯：七日苏联克里姆林宫举行授奖典礼，以“加强国际和平”斯大林国际奖金授与世界和平理事会主席约里奥—居里。参加典礼的有苏联拥护和平委员会主席吉洪诺夫和各界代表多人。“加强国际和平”斯大林国际奖金得奖人戈登夫人和哈拉将军也参加了盛典。斯大林国际和平奖金委员会主席斯科贝尔琴把奖状一纸和有斯大林雕像的金质奖章一枚授与约里奥—居里。苏联科学院院长涅斯米扬诺夫和吉洪诺夫等相继致贺词。
    约里奥—居里致答词说：全世界的正直人民都认为设置斯大林和平奖金是苏联人民要求和平的坚强意志的具体证明。领受和平奖金是我一生中最大的荣誉，它增强了我的信心，使我获得新的力量来把我的工作坚持到底，以便使我们的孩子永远不知道新战争的恐怖，使科学不致罪恶地背弃其目标，使一切劳动人民努力的成果成为最高的幸福。约里奥—居里的演说受到极为热烈的欢迎。
    【新华社五日讯】据塔斯社莫斯科讯：二日在苏联克里姆林宫举行了授奖典礼，以“加强国际和平”斯大林国际奖金授予保卫世界和平的积极战士、国际民主妇女联合会主席戈登夫人。参加典礼的有苏联拥护和平委员会副主席格列科夫和各界代表多人。斯大林国际和平奖金委员会主席斯科贝尔琴以奖状一纸和金质奖章一枚授予戈登夫人，并致贺辞。苏联拥护和平委员会副主席、科学院院士格列科夫、苏联妇女反法西斯委员会主席波波娃和基舍列夫教授相继致贺辞。戈登夫人致答辞时对获得奖金表示感谢，并表示决心为保卫和平的事业尽更大的力量。她说：“把这个崇高的奖金授给我，就是表扬全世界妇女在保卫世界和平斗争中的英勇行为。现在各国妇女正进一步展开和平公约宣言的签名运动，最近国际民主妇联执行委员会索非亚会议已号召各国妇女在宣言上踊跃签名。我相信我荣获斯大林国际和平奖金，将有助于进一步扩大国际民主妇联的影响，并进一步加强全世界妇女保卫和平的斗争。”
    【新华社八日讯】据塔斯社莫斯科讯：苏联斯大林国际和平奖金委员会于六日在莫斯科举行盛会，招待刻在莫斯科的“加强国际和平”斯大林国际奖金得奖人世界和平理事会主席约里奥—居里、国际民主妇女联合会主席戈登夫人和墨西哥前海军部长哈拉将军。到会的有苏联著名的科学家、作家、艺术家和各界代表多人。招待会在热烈友好的气氛中进行。
