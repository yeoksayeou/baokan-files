Title: American Seamen's Strike Ends in Victory; Capitalists Forced to Increase Wages and Shorten Working Hours
Author:
Date: 1951-07-05
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, New York, 2nd. The strike launched by 60,000 seamen on the American East Coast on June 16th ended victoriously on June 27th. The capitalists were forced to agree to increase wages and shorten working hours. The striking seamen belonged to the CIO's National Maritime Union, the National Marine Engineers' Beneficial Association, and the American Radio Telegraphists Association. The strike affected twenty-three ports along the American East Coast and prevented hundreds of ships from sailing.

### 美国海员罢工胜利结束  资方被迫增加工资和缩短工时

1951-07-05
第4版()
专栏：

　　美国海员罢工胜利结束
    资方被迫增加工资和缩短工时
    【新华社二日讯】纽约消息：美国东海岸六万海员于六月十六日发动的罢工，已于六月二十七日胜利结束，资方已被迫同意增加工资和缩短工作时间。此次罢工海员属于产联全国海员工会、全国海船司机福利协会和美国无线电报务员工会。罢工波及美国东海岸二十三个港口，曾使数百艘船只无法开出。
