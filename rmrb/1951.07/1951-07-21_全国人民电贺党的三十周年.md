Title: The People of the Whole Country Send Telegrams of Congratulations on the 30th Anniversary of the Party

Author: 
Date: 1951-07-21
Page: 1

On the occasion of the 30th anniversary of the Party, people from all over the country, including individuals and organizations, sent telegrams or letters to the Central Committee of the Communist Party of China and Chairman Mao, expressing their warm congratulations. The list of these organizations and individuals, in addition to those already published, is published successively as follows:

　　All members of the Pingxiang County Tongmu Industry and Commerce Federation in Jiangxi Province. Wuzhou Import and Export Trade Association of Guangxi Province. Wuzhou Lighterage Association of Guangxi Province. Wuzhou Incense and Firecracker Trade Association of Guangxi Province. Workers' Group of Changsha Shipping Company. All residents of the Second Village, Yangming District, Yuyao County, Zhejiang Province. All the people of the Sixth Village, Daixi Township, Daishan District, Dinghai County, Zhejiang Province. All the people of Jianshe Street, First District, Linhe County, Suiyuan Province. All the people of the Dianbei Residents Committee, Third and Fourth Groups, Luqiao Town, Huangyan County, Zhejiang Province. Jiang Baiyu and three others from the Third Group, Yejia Village, Xiazhou Township, Hangbu District, Qu County, Zhejiang Province; Zheng Gengsheng and three others from the First Group; Jiang Zhangfu, Director of the Yejia Village Farmers Association. Doufu Trade Association of Xinhua County, Hunan Province, Preparatory Committee of the South Goods Commercial Trade Association, Bamboo Ware Trade Association, Preparatory Committee of the Native Products and Timber Trade Association, Preparatory Committee of the Itinerant Merchants Maoban Trade Association, Zhang Yueqing, Executive Director of the Slaughter and Commercial Trade Association and all merchants, Wei Dingjian, Zeng Chunan, and You Keyun, representatives of urban food stall vendors. The Conference of People from All Walks of Life in the Second Guidance Area of the Eleventh District of Hengshan County, Hunan Province, Celebrates the 30th Anniversary of the Communist Party of China. Oil, Salt and Food Trade Association of Xinhua County, Hunan Province. Teachers, students, staff and workers of the Hunan Provincial Xinhua Senior Industrial School. Sun Huanzhang and five other students from the second year of the Normal School, Chifeng Junior High School, Rehe Province. All staff, students and workers of the private Songtou Junior High School, Wuhua County, Guangdong Province. Preparatory Committee of the Jinan Municipal Federation of Industry and Commerce, Shandong Province. All workers of Tailai Flour Mill, Taizhou City, Subei District. All recuperating members of the Jinan Workers' Sanatorium of the Jinan District Committee of the China Railway Trade Union. All members of the Fourth Group of the Fifth Branch of the Wuxi Girls' Middle School in the South Jiangsu District of the Chinese New Democratic Youth League. All students of the Qufu County Xiaoxue Elementary School, Shandong Province. All farmers of Yucun Village, Minhe Township, Zhanggang District, Jiangdu County, Subei District. All colleagues of Baolun Pharmacy, Shanghai Medical Workers Union. All members of the Southwest Land Reform Work Group. Model workshops of Shandong Xinhua Pharmaceutical Factory - Organic Pharmaceutical Room, Preparation Room, Furnace Worker Room, Large Furnace Room, Instrument Group, Labor Model Sun Yushan and 20 others.

### 全国人民电贺党的三十周年

1951-07-21
第1版()
专栏：

　　全国人民电贺党的三十周年
    在党的三十周年纪念日，全国各地人民，包括个人和团体，纷纷打电报或写信给中国共产党中央委员会和毛主席，表示热烈的祝贺。这些团体和个人的名单，除已发表者外，陆续发表如下：
    江西省萍乡县桐木业工商业联合会全体。广西省梧州市出进口业公会。广西省梧州市趸驳公会。广西省梧州市香炮业同业公会。长沙轮船公司职工小组。浙江省余姚县阳明区第二村全体居民。浙江省定海县岱山区岱西乡第六村全体人民。绥远省临河县第一区建设街全体人民。浙江省黄岩县路桥镇殿北居民委员会全体人民、第三、四组全体。浙江省衢县杭埠区下洲乡叶家村第三小组江白于等四人、第一小组郑根生等四人、叶家村农民协会主任江章甫。湖南省新化县豆腐业同业公会、南货商业同业公会筹备委员会、竹器业同业公会、土产木材业同业公会筹备委员会、行商毛板业同业公会筹备委员会、屠宰商业同业会常务理事张越卿暨全体商户、城区食品摊贩业代表魏定坚、曾楚南、游克云。湖南省衡山县第十一区镇第二辅导区各界人民庆祝中国共产党三十周年纪念大会。湖南省新化县油盐食品业同业公会。湖南省立新化高级工业学校师、生、员、工。热河省立赤峰初级中学校初师二年孙焕章等六人。广东省五华县私立嵩头初级中学全体员生工。山东省济南市工商业联合会筹备会。苏北区泰州市泰来面粉厂全体工人。中国铁路工会济南区委员会济南职工业余休养所全体休养员。中国新民主主义青年团苏南区无锡市女子中学第五分支第四小组全体团员。山东省曲阜县立小雪小学校全体同学。苏北区江都县张纲区民和乡渔樵村全体农民。上海医务工作者工会宝仑药房全体同人。西南土地改革工作团全体团员。山东新华制药总厂模范车间——有机制药室、制剂室、炉工室、大炉室、仪器组，劳动模范孙玉善等二十人。
