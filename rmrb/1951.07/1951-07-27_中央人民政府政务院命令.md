Title: Order of the State Administration Council of the Central People's Government

Author:
Date: 1951-07-27
Page: 1

　　Order of the State Administration Council of the Central People's Government
    The "Measures for Handling Christian Organizations Receiving American Subsidies" have been formulated and are hereby promulgated for implementation.
    This order.
                  Premier Zhou Enlai
              July 24, 1951
