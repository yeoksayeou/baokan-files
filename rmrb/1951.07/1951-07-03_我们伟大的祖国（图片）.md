Title: Our Great Motherland (Picture)
Author:
Date: 1951-07-03
Page: 1

    Our Great Motherland
    The glorious representatives of the Chinese People's Volunteer Army returning home are welcomed everywhere in the motherland. Here, representatives of the Youth Cultural Troupe of the Chinese People's Volunteer Army, who are participating in the National Cultural Troupe Conference, are dancing the Korean "Tractor" dance at a get-together at Beijing Normal University. They were warmly welcomed by the audience.

### 我们伟大的祖国（图片）

1951-07-03
第1版()
专栏：

    我们伟大的祖国
    光荣的中国人民志愿军回国代表，到处受到祖国的欢迎。在这里，参加全国文工团会议的中国人民志愿军青年文工团代表正在北京师范大学联欢会上跳着朝鲜的“拖拉机”舞。他们受到了观众的热烈欢迎。
