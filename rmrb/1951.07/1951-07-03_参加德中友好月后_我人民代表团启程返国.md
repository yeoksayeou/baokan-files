Title: People's Delegation Departs for Home After Participating in the Sino-German Friendship Month
Author:
Date: 1951-07-03
Page: 4

　　After Participating in the Sino-German Friendship Month
    Our People's Delegation Departs for Home
    Xinhua News Agency, Berlin, 30th. The Chinese People's Delegation, led by Shen Junru, which participated in the Sino-German Friendship Month, departed from here on the 29th by plane to Moscow en route to return home. Chairman Correns of the National Front of Democratic Germany, Premier Grotewohl, and representatives of the government of the German Democratic Republic, including Geyer, Director of the Government Office, as well as Ji Pengfei, head of our country's diplomatic mission to the German Democratic Republic, saw them off at the airport. Correns and Shen Junru both delivered short speeches at the airport, emphasizing that the Sino-German Friendship Month is a good start to further strengthen the friendship between the people of Germany and China.

### 参加德中友好月后  我人民代表团启程返国

1951-07-03
第4版()
专栏：

　　参加德中友好月后
    我人民代表团启程返国
    【新华社柏林三十日电】沈钧儒率领的参加德中友好月的中国人民代表团，已于二十九日离此乘飞机赴莫斯科转道返国。民主德国全国阵线主席柯伦斯，总理格罗提渥和德意志民主共和国政府的代表、政府办公厅主任盖耶，以及我国驻德意志民主共和国外交使团团长姬鹏飞等，都到机场送行。柯伦斯和沈钧儒在机场都发表简短演说，强调指出德中友好月是进一步加强德中两国人民友谊的良好开端。
