Title: Molotov Departs Poland for Home

Author:

Date: 1951-07-26
Page: 4

　　Xinhua News Agency, Warsaw, July 25th: According to TASS, the Soviet government delegation composed of Molotov, Vice-Chairman of the Council of Ministers of the Soviet Union, and Marshal Zhukov of the Soviet Union, which participated in the Polish National Day celebrations, left Warsaw on the 24th to return to Moscow. Those who went to see them off included: Polish Parliament Speaker Kowalski, Premier Cyrankiewicz and government officials, members of the Political Bureau of the Polish United Workers' Party, Marshal Rokossovsky and generals of the Polish army, representatives of workers from various enterprises in Warsaw, as well as Soviet Ambassador to Poland Sobolev and diplomatic envoys from various People's Democratic countries.

### 莫洛托夫离波返国

1951-07-26
第4版()
专栏：

　　莫洛托夫离波返国
    【新华社二十五日讯】据塔斯社华沙讯：由苏联部长会议副主席莫洛托夫和苏联元帅朱可夫所组成的参加波兰国庆庆祝会的苏联政府代表团，已于二十四日离华沙返莫斯科。前往送行的有：波兰议会主席科瓦尔斯基，西伦凯维兹总理和政府官员，波兰统一工人党政治局的委员，罗科索夫斯基元帅和波兰军队的将领，华沙各企业的工人代表，以及苏联驻波兰大使索波列夫和各人民民主国家的外交使节。
