Title: 30th Anniversary of the Mongolian People's Revolution: Central People's Government Special Delegation Travels to Participate in Celebrations
Author:
Date: 1951-07-09
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, July 8th:

　　To commemorate the 30th anniversary of the victory of the Mongolian People's Revolution on July 11th, the Central People's Government has dispatched a delegation consisting of Wang Zaitian, a member of the Nationalities Affairs Commission; Gao Bozebu, a member of the Inner Mongolia Autonomous Region People's Government; Chen Xiwen, First Deputy Minister of the Culture and Education Department of the All-China Federation of Trade Unions; Xu Wentian, a Counselor of the Government Administration Council; and Ye Shangzhi, Director of the Personnel Office of the Nationalities Affairs Commission, to the Mongolian People's Republic to participate in the celebrations. The delegation departed by special plane for Ulaanbaatar at 7:00 AM on the 8th. Those who saw them off at the airport included: Qu Wu, Deputy Secretary-General of the Government Administration Council of the Central People's Government; Yan Baohang, Deputy Director of the General Office of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs; Peng Sike, Deputy Director of the General Office of the Nationalities Affairs Commission of the Central People's Government; and Liu Ziju, Minister of the Culture and Education Department of the All-China Federation of Trade Unions. Mongolian Ambassador to China, Jalkhasaikhan, also attended to see them off.

### 蒙古人民革命胜利三十周年纪念  中央人民政府特派代表团前往参加庆典

1951-07-09
第1版()
专栏：

　　蒙古人民革命胜利三十周年纪念
    中央人民政府特派代表团前往参加庆典
    【新华社八日讯】七月十一日为蒙古人民共和国人民革命胜利三十周年纪念，我中央人民政府特派民族事务委员会委员王再天、内蒙古自治区人民政府委员高博泽布、中华全国总工会文教部第一副部长陈希文、政务院参事许闻天、民族事务委员会人事室主任叶尚志等组成代表团，赴蒙古人民共和国参加庆祝大典。代表团一行已于八日晨七时乘专机飞乌兰巴托尔。到机场欢送者有：中央人民政府政务院副秘书长屈武、外交部办公厅副主任阎宝航、中央民族事务委员会办公厅副主任朋斯克、中华全国总工会文教部部长刘子久等。蒙古驻华大使贾尔卡赛汗亦莅场欢送。
