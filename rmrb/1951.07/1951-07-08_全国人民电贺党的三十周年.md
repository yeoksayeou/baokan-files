Title: The People of the Whole Country Send Congratulatory Telegrams for the Party's 30th Anniversary
Author:
Date: 1951-07-08
Page: 1

　　On the 30th anniversary of the Party, people from all over the country, including individuals and groups, have sent telegrams or letters to the Central Committee of the Communist Party of China and Chairman Mao, expressing their warm congratulations. The list of these groups and individuals, in addition to those already published, is published successively as follows:
    
　　The entire membership of the Tianjin Seventh District Shop Assistants Branch of the Chinese New Democratic Youth League. Wenhou Village, Fu Village, Guojiazhuang, Yangjia Yard, Song Wenzhuang, Tan Jiazhuang in the First District of Xinjiang County, Shanxi Province. All comrades of the Fifth Group of the Hefei Postal and Telecommunications Trade Union in Anhui. All employees of the Fourth Group of the Postal Section of the Anhui Postal and Telecommunications Administration. The Young Pioneers Detachment of Guli Town Primary School in Yongkang County, Zhejiang Province. All propagandists of Jiefang Street, Datong District, Tongling County, Chizhou Special District, Southern Anhui Region. All accounting and statistics personnel of the Datong Engineering Team of the General Administration of Electricity. All employees of the Tenth Group of the Anhui Postal and Telecommunications Administration Committee of the Chinese Postal and Telecommunications Trade Union. All students of the Fifth Class of the Suzhou Sulun Yarn Factory Shutdown Study Class. The Twelfth Group of the Grassroots Committee of the Anhui Postal and Telecommunications Administration of the Chinese Postal and Telecommunications Trade Union. The Laboring Fishmongers Organization Office of Datong District, Tongling County, Southern Anhui Region. All employees of the Chemical Department of the Yongli Chemical Industry Company Ammonium Factory. The Committee of the Nanjing Municipal No. 2 Junior Middle School of the Chinese Education Trade Union. All teachers and students of the Nanjing Private Dazhong Women's Sewing Cooperative. All female students of Class A of Shanghai Municipal Shanghai High School. The grassroots group of Shanghai Dahua Industrial Factory. The Sixteenth Group of the Anhui Postal and Telecommunications Administration Committee of the Chinese Postal and Telecommunications Trade Union. All peasants of the Eastern Suburb Township, Chengguan District, Xiangxiang County, Hunan Province. The entire membership of the Yibuqi Village Branch of the Tenth District of Longjiang County, Heilongjiang Province of the Chinese New Democratic Youth League. All workers of the Natural Science Subject Group of Zhenjiang Junior Middle School, Southern Jiangsu Region. A group of old workers from Shanghai Changfeng Dyeing and Weaving Factory. The Teacher Research Class Branch of the Nanjing Municipal Bureau of Culture and Education of the Chinese New Democratic Youth League. The Jinguo Pen Factory Branch of the Shanghai Light Industry Trade Union of the Chinese New Democratic Youth League.

### 全国人民电贺党的三十周年

1951-07-08
第1版()
专栏：

　　全国人民电贺党的三十周年
    在党的三十周年纪念日，全国各地人民，包括个人和团体，纷纷打电报或写信给中国共产党中央委员会和毛主席，表示热烈的祝贺。这些团体和个人的名单，除已发表者外，陆续发表如下：     
    中国新民主主义青年团天津市第七区店员支部全体团员。山西省新绛县第一区文侯村、符村、郭家庄、杨家院、宋温庄、谭家庄。安徽合肥邮电工会第五小组全体同志。安徽邮电管理局邮政科第四小组全体职工。中国少年儿童队浙江省永康县古丽镇小学队部。皖南区池州专区铜陵县大通区解放街全体宣传员。电业管理总局大同工程队会计、统计全体。中国邮电工会安徽邮电管理局委员会第十小组全体职工。苏州市苏纶纱厂停工学习班第五班全体学员。中国邮电工会安徽邮电管理局基层委员会第十二小组。皖南区铜陵县大通区肩挑鱼贩劳动工人组织处。永利化学工业公司铔厂化学部全体职工。中国教育工会南京市立第二初级中学委员会。南京市私立大众妇女缝纫社全体师生。上海市立上海中学理一甲全体女同学。上海市大华实业工厂基层小组。中国邮电工会安徽邮电管理局委员会第十六小组。湖南省湘乡县城关区东郊乡全体农民。中国新民主主义青年团黑龙江省龙江县第十区依布气村支部全体。苏南区镇江市初级中学自然学科小组全体工人。上海市长丰染织厂一群老工人。中国新民主主义青年团南京市文教局教师研究班支部。中国新民主主义青年团上海轻工业工会金国笔厂支部。
