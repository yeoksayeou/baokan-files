Title: Korean Armistice Talks: Preliminary Discussion on Military Demarcation Line Issue Yesterday
Author:
Date: 1951-07-28
Page: 1

　　Korean Armistice Talks
　  Preliminary Discussion on Military Demarcation Line Issue Yesterday
    Xinhua News Agency, Pyongyang, July 27th: This reporter learned from the headquarters on the Korean front that the eleventh meeting of the Korean armistice talks began at 9:00 AM (Korean time) on July 27th and ended at 10:10 AM. The meeting preliminarily discussed the issue of determining the military demarcation line between the two sides to establish a demilitarized zone. The twelfth meeting is scheduled to be held at 9:00 AM on July 28th.

### 朝鲜停战谈判会议  昨初步讨论军事分界线问题

1951-07-28
第1版()
专栏：

　　朝鲜停战谈判会议
　  昨初步讨论军事分界线问题
    【新华社平壤二十七日电】本社记者自朝鲜前线总部获悉：朝鲜停战谈判会议第十一次会议，在七月二十七日上午九时（朝鲜时间）开始，十时十分结束。会议中初步地讨论了确定双方军事分界线以建立非军事地区的问题。第十二次会议定于七月二十八日上午九时举行。
