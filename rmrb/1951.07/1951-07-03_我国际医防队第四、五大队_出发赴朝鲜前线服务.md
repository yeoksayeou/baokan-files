Title: Our International Medical Defense Teams Fourth and Fifth Battalions Depart to Serve on the Korean Front

Author:

Date: 1951-07-03
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, dated the 29th: The fourth and fifth battalions of the International Medical Defense Service Team, recently formed by the Chinese People's Committee to Resist America and Aid Korea and the Red Cross Society of China, totaling 155 people, departed for Korea on the 28th.

　　The Chinese People's Committee to Resist America and Aid Korea held a grand send-off meeting on the eve of the departure of these two battalions. Attending the send-off meeting were representatives from various people's organizations, the entire delegation of the Korean people visiting China led by Won Dong-geun, and Choe Young, a counselor of the Korean Embassy in China, totaling more than 300 people.

　　At the meeting, Chen Shutong, Vice Chairman of the Chinese People's Committee to Resist America and Aid Korea, delivered a speech encouraging patriotic medical workers to actively participate in the combat mission to resist America and aid Korea. Ryu Gi-chun, Vice Minister of the Korean Ministry of Public Health and representative of the Korean people's delegation visiting China, delivered a speech on behalf of the Korean people, warmly welcoming the Chinese medical workers to Korea.

### 我国际医防队第四、五大队  出发赴朝鲜前线服务

1951-07-03
第1版()
专栏：

　　我国际医防队第四、五大队
    出发赴朝鲜前线服务
    【新华社二十九日讯】中国人民抗美援朝总会与中国红十字会总会最近组成的国际医防服务队第四、五两个大队共一百五十五人，已于二十八日出发赴朝。
    抗美援朝总会在这两个大队出发前夕举行了盛大的欢送会。欢送会上出席的有各人民团体代表和以元东根为首的朝鲜人民访华团全体团员、朝鲜驻华大使馆参赞崔英等三百余人。
    会上抗美援朝总会副主席陈叔通致词，他鼓励爱国的医务工作者踊跃参加抗美援朝的战斗任务。朝鲜人民访华团代表朝鲜保健省副相柳基春致辞，他代表朝鲜人民热烈欢迎中国的医务工作者到朝鲜去。
