Title: Ma Hengchang Group Formulates Patriotic Competition Agreement
Author: Cai Bai
Date: 1951-07-26
Page: 1

　　Ma Hengchang Group Formulates Patriotic Competition Agreement

　　[This newspaper dispatch] The Ma Hengchang Group, responding to the Three Great Calls of the Resist America, Aid Korea General Association, has revised its patriotic agreement and formulated a plan for long-term donation through increased production.

　　In order to formulate a long-term plan for increased production and donation, the Ma Hengchang Group earnestly studied and discussed the Three Great Calls and relevant documents of the Resist America, Aid Korea General Association. They also reviewed the challenge conditions issued to workers nationwide in January and the previously established patriotic and factory protection agreements. In accordance with the decisions and instructions of the All-China Federation of Trade Unions and the Northeast Federation of Trade Unions, and after internal deliberation and discussion, it was decided to merge the challenge competition conditions, the patriotic agreement, and the factory protection agreement. These were unified and revised into a six-item patriotic competition agreement to serve as the action program for the Ma Hengchang Group's patriotic competition in the second half of the year. The content of the patriotic competition agreement is as follows:

　　1. Actively respond to the Three Great Calls of the Resist America, Aid Korea General Association, strive to overfulfill the production task by 10% in the second half of the year, and donate 50% of the bonus from overproduction and one day's wages per month to purchase airplanes, artillery, and tanks.

　　2. Strictly abide by the technical operating procedures and machine maintenance and quality inspection systems to ensure that the product quality reaches 99% or more as excellent products.

　　3. Ensure that all members of the group can learn the basic operating methods of high-speed cutting and promote the Kovalev work method.

　　4. Ensure that all members of the group participate in cultural and technical night school studies, adhere to newspaper reading group activities, strengthen current affairs study, and conduct monthly tests.

　　5. Abide by the confidentiality system, do not talk carelessly, and actively participate in factory protection work.

　　6. Conduct weekly inspections and monthly summaries of the implementation of the patriotic agreement.

　　To ensure the concrete realization of the patriotic competition agreement, the whole group has assigned specific personnel to be responsible for each item, conducting regular inspections and supervision, with the overall inspection being the responsibility of Deputy Group Leader Dong Zhenyuan. In addition, it is stipulated that a group inspection meeting will be held once a week.

　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　(Cai Bai)

### 马恒昌小组订立爱国竞赛公约
蔡柏
1951-07-26
第1版()
专栏：

　　马恒昌小组订立爱国竞赛公约
    【本报讯】马恒昌小组响应抗美援朝总会三大号召，修订爱国公约，订出增加生产长期捐献的计划。
    马恒昌小组为了订出长期增产捐献计划，认真学习讨论了抗美援朝总会的三大号召与有关文件，并结合检查了一月份的向全国职工挑战条件和过去订的爱国公约护厂公约等，根据全国总工会及东北总工会的决定和指示，经过组内酝酿讨论，已决定将挑战竞赛条件、爱国公约、护厂公约等合并，统一修订为六项爱国竞赛公约，作为马恒昌小组下半年爱国竞赛的行动纲领。爱国竞赛公约内容如下：
    一、积极响应抗美援朝总会三大号召，争取下半年内超额完成生产任务百分之十，捐献超额奖金的百分之五十和每月献工一天的工资，购买飞机、大炮、坦克。
    二、严格遵守技术操作规程和机器保养、质量检查制度，保证产品质量达到百分之九十九以上为优良品。
    三、保证全组都能学会高速切削初步操作方法和推行郭瓦廖夫工作方法。
    四、保证全组参加文化技术夜校学习，坚持读报组活动，加强时事学习，每月测验一次。
    五、遵守保密制度，不乱说乱讲，积极参加护厂工作。
    六、做到每周检查一次，每月总结一次爱国公约执行情况。
    全组为了保证爱国竞赛公约的具体实现，每一项都确定了专人负责，随时检查督促，总的检查由副组长董振远负责。另外，并规定每周召开小组检查会议一次。
                  （蔡柏）
