Title: All-China Youth Federation Holds Grand Meeting to Welcome Vietnamese People's Delegation
Author:
Date: 1951-07-31
Page: 1

　　Xinhua News Agency, July 30th: The All-China Youth Federation held a welcome meeting for the Vietnamese People's Delegation to China at 8 p.m. this evening. Present at the meeting were Liao Chengzhi, Chairman of the All-China Democratic Youth Federation, Li Chang, Standing Committee Member of the Central Committee of the China New Democratic Youth League, and more than 300 young people from all walks of life.

　　The welcome meeting began with the music of "March of the World Democratic Youth." Chairman Liao Chengzhi delivered a welcome speech on behalf of the All-China Democratic Youth Federation. Huang Guo Yue, head of the Vietnamese People's Delegation to China, delivered a reply. On behalf of the Vietnam Youth Federation, Head Huang Guo Yue presented the All-China Democratic Youth Federation with a silk banner; on the banner was written in both Chinese and Vietnamese: "Long Live the Unity of Vietnamese and Chinese Youth." Chairman Liao Chengzhi accepted the banner on behalf of the All-China Democratic Youth Federation.

　　Nguyen Chong, member of the Vietnamese People's Delegation to China and Deputy Regiment Commander of the Vietnamese People's Army, who was awarded the Third-Class Combatant Medal, reported at the meeting on the heroic combat deeds of Vietnamese youth against French imperialism in the Liberation War. Pham Van Hanh, a worker at the Vietnamese Arsenal, reported at the meeting on how Vietnamese workers and peasants overcame various difficulties, worked hard to increase production, and supported the front lines. Their reports received warm applause from the audience.

　　The Vietnamese People's Delegation to China visited the Summer Palace this morning, and separately visited the Cultural Palace, Zhongshan Park, and Beihai on the 26th. They visited Peking University on the 29th.

### 全国青联举行盛会欢迎越南人民代表团

1951-07-31
第1版()
专栏：

　　全国青联举行盛会欢迎越南人民代表团
    【新华社三十日讯】中华全国民主青年联合总会今晚八时举行欢迎越南人民访华代表团大会。到会的有中华全国民主青年联合总会主席廖承志、中国新民主主义青年团中央委员会常务委员李昌及各界青年三百余人。
    欢迎会在世界民主青年进行曲的乐声中开始，廖承志主席代表中华全国民主青年联合总会致欢迎辞。越南人民访华代表团团长黄国越致答辞。黄国越团长代表越南青年联合会向中华全国民主青年联合总会献锦旗一面；旗上用中越两国文字写着：“越华青年团结万岁”。廖承志主席代表中华全国民主青年联合总会接受献旗。
    越南人民访华代表团团员、荣获第三等战士勋章的越南人民军副中团长沅创在大会上报告了越南青年在解放战争中对法帝国主义英勇作战的事迹。越南兵工厂工人范文幸在会上报告了越南工人和农民克服各种困难、努力增加生产、支援前线的情形。他们的报告获得全场热烈的鼓掌。
    越南人民访华代表团今日上午游览了颐和园，二十六日分别游览文化宫、中山公园和北海，二十九日曾去北京大学参观。
