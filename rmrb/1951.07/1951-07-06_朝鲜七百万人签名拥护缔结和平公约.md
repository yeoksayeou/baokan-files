Title: Seven Million Koreans Sign in Support of Concluding a Peace Pact
Author:
Date: 1951-07-06
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Pyongyang, 2nd - The signature campaign of the Korean people in support of the World Peace Council's declaration calling for the five major powers to conclude a peace pact ended victoriously on June 25th. A total of 7,040,000 people across the country (including enemy-occupied areas) participated in the signing.

　　The Korean National Committee for the Defense of Peace convened a central committee meeting on the 30th. The meeting unanimously passed a statement.

　　The statement said: In the harsh combat environment of the Fatherland Liberation War, from April 13th to June 25th, the Korean people actively launched a signature campaign in support of the World Peace Council's declaration calling for the five major powers to conclude a peace pact. A total of 25,282 mass rallies were held in factories, workshops, villages, fishing villages, schools, government offices, and on the streets, with 4,629,464 people participating. A total of 7,047,821 people across the country signed the World Peace Council's declaration, including 433,563 people in enemy-occupied areas. The statement pointed out that the victorious conclusion of this signature campaign demonstrates the Korean people's firm determination to fight for the victory of the Fatherland Liberation War and to defend world peace.

### 朝鲜七百万人签名拥护缔结和平公约

1951-07-06
第4版()
专栏：

　　朝鲜七百万人签名拥护缔结和平公约
    【新华社平壤二日电】朝鲜人民拥护世界和平理事会关于要求五大国缔结和平公约宣言的签名运动，已于六月二十五日胜利结束。全国各地（包括敌占区）人民参加签名的共达七百零四万人。
    朝鲜拥护和平全国民族委员会于三十日召开中央委员会。会议一致通过了一项声明。
    声明说：朝鲜人民在进行祖国解放战争的残酷战斗环境中，从四月十三日到六月二十五日，积极展开了支持世界和平理事会关于要求五大国缔结和平公约宣言的签名运动。各地工厂、制作所、农村、渔村、学校、机关和街头，共举行了二万五千二百八十二次群众大会，参加大会的达四百六十二万九千四百六十四人。全国各地在世界和平理事会宣言上签名的，共达七百零四万七千八百二十一人，其中包括敌占区人民四十三万三千五百六十三人。声明指出这一签名运动的胜利结束，表明了朝鲜人民争取祖国解放战争胜利和保卫世界和平的坚强斗争意志。
