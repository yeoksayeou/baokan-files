    Photo Description
    On July 8th, the Soviet Aviation Day celebration was held at the Tushino Airfield in Moscow, where outstanding "Stalin's Eagles" gave wonderful performances. Here are three photos: (Bottom) A group of aircraft formed the words "Glory to Stalin" flying across the sky. (Left) Parachute troops performing a parachute jump. (Bottom left) Helicopter performance.
