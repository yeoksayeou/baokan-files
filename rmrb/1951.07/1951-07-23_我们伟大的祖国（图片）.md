Our Great Motherland
The Miao people of Xingren, Guizhou, have generally improved their political awareness and their lives have been initially improved through movements such as suppressing bandits, rent reduction and deposit refunds, and opposing bullies. Therefore, they are eager to improve their culture and learn politics. This is a scene where the Miao people are selling local products at the market and then buying farmer's literacy textbooks and the elementary edition of "Study" at a Xinhua Bookstore open-air sales booth.

### 我们伟大的祖国（图片）

1951-07-23
第1版()
专栏：

    我们伟大的祖国
    贵州兴仁的苗族人民经过了清剿土匪、减租退押、反对恶霸等运动，政治觉悟普遍提高，生活得到了初步改善，因此迫切地要求提高文化和学习政治。这是苗族人民在赶集时出卖了土产之后，在新华书店露天推销摊上购买农民文化课本和“学习”初级版的情形。
