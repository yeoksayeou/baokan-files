Title: Polish Industry Achieves New Successes: Exceeds Production Plan for the First Half of the Year
Author:
Date: 1951-07-24
Page: 4

TASS, Warsaw dispatch: The Polish State Planning Commission published a communique on the results of the implementation of the national economic plan for the second quarter of 1951 on the 18th. The communique shows that Poland's socialist industry has completed 102.3 percent of the second quarter's production plan and 102 percent of the plan for the first half of this year. The total production value for the second quarter of this year increased by 29 percent compared with the same period last year.

The communique pointed out that the industrial sectors manufacturing consumer goods achieved the best results in implementing the second quarter's production plan. Light industrial products increased by 27 percent compared with the second quarter of last year, the food industry increased by 34 percent, and small-scale industry and handicrafts increased by 95 percent. The mining industry and heavy industry have achieved significant accomplishments. The output of the mining industry exceeded that of the second quarter of last year by 19 percent, and heavy industry exceeded it by 21 percent. Coal production in the second quarter increased by 10 percent compared with the same period last year; pig iron production also increased by 10 percent; steel increased by 11 percent, rolled metal increased by 19 percent; industrial equipment and construction machinery increased by 129 percent; and agricultural machinery and implements increased by 30 percent.

The communique also pointed out that spring sowing throughout Poland was very successful. Various transportation services have developed smoothly, and freight volume in the second quarter of this year increased by 19 percent compared with the same period last year. Sales in the second quarter have seen a significant increase. The total retail trade volume increased by 13 percent compared with the same period last year. At the same time, the total trade volume of state-owned and cooperative enterprises increased by 29 percent.

The communique concluded by saying that the number of people employed in Polish state-owned industries increased by 7 percent compared with the second quarter of last year, and the average monthly wage of workers increased by 6 percent.

### 波兰工业获新成就  超额完成上半年生产计划

1951-07-24
第4版()
专栏：

　　波兰工业获新成就
　  超额完成上半年生产计划
    【新华社二十日讯】据塔斯社华沙讯：波兰国家计划委员会于十八日发表关于一九五一年第二季国民经济计划执行结果的公报。公报表明：波兰的社会主义工业已完成第二季生产计划的百分之一百零二点三，今年上半年计划已完成百分之一百零二，今年第二季生产总值比去年同期增加了百分之二十九。
    公报指出：在执行第二季生产计划方面，制造消费品的工业部门成绩最好。轻工业产品比去年第二季增加了百分之二十七，食品工业增加了百分之三十四，小型工业和手工业增加了百分之九十五。采矿工业和重工业获得了重大成就。采矿工业的产量超过去年第二季百分之十九，重工业超过百分之二十一。第二季煤产量比去年同期增加了百分之十；生铁产量也增加了百分之十；钢增加百分之十一，辗金属增加百分之十九；工业设备和建筑机器增加百分之一百二十九；农业机器和农具增加了百分之三十。
    公报又指出：波兰全国各地春季播种工作很为成功。各种运输业务都顺利发展，今年第二季货运比去年同期增加了百分之十九。第二季售货额已有显著的增加。零售贸易总额比去年同期增加了百分之十三。同时，国家与合作社的贸易总额增加了百分之二十九。
    公报最后说：波兰国营工业中就业人数比去年第二季增加了百分之七，工人每月平均工资增加了百分之六。
