Title: US Aircraft Continue Indiscriminate Bombing of Korean Cities and Villages
Author:
Date: 1951-07-11
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Tenth dispatch. TASS, Pyongyang, Ninth dispatch: American aircraft continue to attack peaceful cities and villages in Korea. The aerial bandits have repeatedly bombed cities such as Pyongyang, Wonsan, and Anju.

According to the Korean Central News Agency, four American aircraft strafed and bombed Seokwang Temple during an air raid on Wonsan. The next day, the aerial bandits returned to strafe and bomb nearby temples and other historical sites. Seokwang Temple and the nearby resort have been destroyed. About fifty other historical sites cherished by the Korean people have also been destroyed.

### 美机续滥炸朝鲜城乡

1951-07-11
第1版()
专栏：

　　美机续滥炸朝鲜城乡
    【新华社十日讯】塔斯社平壤九日讯：美国飞机继续袭击朝鲜的和平城市和乡村。空中强盗们一再轰炸平壤、元山、安州等城市。
    据朝鲜中央通讯社讯，四架美国飞机在空袭元山时曾扫射并轰炸释王庙。第二天空中强盗又来扫射和轰炸附近的庙宇和其他古迹。释王庙和附近的休养所已被炸毁。朝鲜人民所珍爱的其他约五十处古迹，也已被炸毁。
