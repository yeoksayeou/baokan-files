Title: Military Cadre School Students Return to Shanghai to Visit Classmates at Various Schools
Author: Wen
Date: 1951-07-11
Page: 3

　　Military Cadre School Students Return to Shanghai to Visit Classmates at Various Schools

　　The students who previously participated in the Military Cadre School from Shanghai, at the invitation of the Shanghai Military Cadre School Enrollment Committee, the Shanghai Municipal Committee of the Communist Youth League, and the Shanghai Student Federation, organized a visiting group to return to Shanghai for visits. After arriving in Shanghai, they introduced the learning and living conditions at the Military Cadre School to students from various schools, in order to encourage Shanghai students to actively participate in the country's national defense construction. (Wen)

### 军事干部学校同学回上海访问各校同学
文
1951-07-11
第3版()
专栏：文化生活动态

　　军事干部学校同学回上海访问各校同学
    上海市上次参加军事干部学校的同学，应上海市军事干部学校招生委员会、青年团上海市工作委员会、上海市学联的邀请，组织了访问团回上海访问。他们到上海以后即分别向各校同学介绍军事干部学校学习与生活情况，以鼓励上海同学踊跃参加祖国的国防建设。（文）
