Title: Our Great Motherland (Picture)
Author:
Date: 1951-07-06
Page: 1

    Our Great Motherland
    In the fields of our motherland, agricultural products are becoming more abundant year by year. This is the famous Hami melon of Xinjiang, a product of a bountiful harvest. After the liberation of Xinjiang, the People's Government helped farmers solve the problem of irrigation, and the yield of Hami melons increased from 2,000 per mu to 2,600 per mu, each weighing more than ten catties. Look! How happy the Uighur farmers who grow melons are!

### 我们伟大的祖国（图片）

1951-07-06
第1版()
专栏：

    我们伟大的祖国
    在祖国的田野上，农产品一年比一年丰盛了。这是丰收的新疆名产哈密瓜。新疆解放后，人民政府帮助农民解决了水利问题，哈密瓜的产量由每亩二千颗增加到二千六百颗，每颗重十余斤。看！种瓜的维吾尔族的农妇们多么愉快！
