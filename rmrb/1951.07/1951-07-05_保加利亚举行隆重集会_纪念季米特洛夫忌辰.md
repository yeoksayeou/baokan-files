Title: Bulgaria Holds Solemn Gathering to Commemorate Dimitrov's Death Anniversary
Author:
Date: 1951-07-05
Page: 4
　　
　　Bulgaria Holds Solemn Gathering
    Commemorates Dimitrov's Death Anniversary

    [Xinhua News Agency, July 3rd] According to TASS, Sofia, July 2nd: The Central Committee of the Bulgarian Communist Party, the National Committee of the Bulgarian Fatherland Front, and the National Agricultural Workers' Union, together with representatives of various people's organizations, held a solemn memorial meeting in the People's Theater in Sofia on July 1st to commemorate the second anniversary of the death of Dimitrov, the great leader and teacher of the Bulgarian people. The Bulgarian flag was hung on the rostrum, with a huge portrait of Dimitrov in the middle, set off by fresh flowers. Many huge slogans called for the realization of the great words of this fighter for the happiness and peace of the working people. Personnel from the diplomatic missions of various People's Democratic countries, headed by Soviet Ambassador Bodrov, attended the memorial meeting. At 7 p.m., members of the Politburo of the Bulgarian Communist Party Central Committee, headed by Bulgarian Communist Party Central Committee General Secretary and Chairman of the Council of Ministers Chervenkov, and members of the Government of the People's Republic of Bulgaria walked onto the presidium seats. The memorial meeting was opened by Birchev, Secretary of the Sofia Municipal Committee of the Bulgarian Communist Party, who proposed a moment of silence for Dimitrov. The audience stood in silence, and the orchestra played the national anthems of the People's Republic of Bulgaria and the Soviet Union in succession.
    Naidev, a member of the Politburo of the Bulgarian Communist Party, gave a report on Dimitrov's life and revolutionary deeds, recounting the exploits of Dimitrov—a loyal student of Lenin and Stalin—in the struggle against fascism, the most vicious enemy of mankind, and in the struggle to build a people's democratic Bulgaria. Naidev called for the realization of Dimitrov's legacy, and for maintaining and consolidating friendship with the Soviet Union. On the 2nd, tens of thousands of Sofia's working people visited Dimitrov's mausoleum to pay their respects.

### 保加利亚举行隆重集会  纪念季米特洛夫忌辰

1951-07-05
第4版()
专栏：

　　保加利亚举行隆重集会
    纪念季米特洛夫忌辰
    【新华社三日讯】据塔斯社索非亚二日讯：保加利亚共产党中央委员会、保加利亚祖国阵线全国委员会和全国农业工人联盟会同各人民团体代表于七月一日在索非亚人民剧院举行隆重纪念会，纪念保加利亚人民的伟大领袖与导师季米特洛夫逝世二周年。主席台上挂着保加利亚的国旗，中间是衬着鲜花的巨幅季米特洛夫画像。许多巨幅标语号召实现这位争取劳动者幸福与和平的斗士的伟大遗言。以苏联大使保德罗夫为首的各人民民主国家外交使团的人员都参加了纪念会。下午七时，以保加利亚共产党中央委员会总书记、部长会议主席契尔文科夫为首的保共中央政治局委员及保加利亚人民共和国政府委员走入主席团席位。纪念会由保加利亚共产党索非亚市委会书记比尔契夫致开幕词，他建议为季米特洛夫致哀，全场肃立，乐队先后奏起保加利亚人民共和国及苏联国歌。
    保加利亚共产党政治局委员奈捷夫作了关于季米特洛夫的生平与革命事迹的报告，叙述了季米特洛夫——列宁与斯大林的忠实学生——在反对人类最凶恶的敌人法西斯主义的斗争中，在建设人民民主的保加利亚的斗争中的功勋。奈捷夫号召实现季米特洛夫遗言，应保持和巩固与苏联的友谊。二日，成千成万的索非亚劳动人民谒季米特洛夫陵墓致敬。
