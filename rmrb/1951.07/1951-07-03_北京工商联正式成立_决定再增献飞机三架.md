Title: Beijing Federation of Industry and Commerce Formally Established; Decides to Donate Three More Airplanes

Author:
Date: 1951-07-03
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency: The Beijing Federation of Industry and Commerce was formally established at a representative meeting held by the Beijing business community from June 26th to 28th. The meeting focused on how to further the Resist America, Aid Korea movement, promote patriotic covenants, and expand the patriotic donation campaign. The attending representatives unanimously guaranteed to strive to overfulfill the plan to donate airplanes and cannons, and to donate three more airplanes in addition to the originally planned 27 airplanes. The meeting also sent letters of respect to Chairman Mao and the Beijing Municipal Committee of the Chinese Communist Party, expressing their intention to celebrate the 30th anniversary of the founding of the Chinese Communist Party by overfulfilling the donation task.

### 北京工商联正式成立  决定再增献飞机三架

1951-07-03
第1版()
专栏：

　　北京工商联正式成立 
    决定再增献飞机三架
    【本报讯】据新华社讯：北京市工商界于六月二十六日至二十八日举行代表会议，正式成立北京市工商联合会。会议曾着重研究了如何深入抗美援朝、推行爱国公约与扩大爱国捐献运动。到会代表一致保证：争取超额完成捐献飞机大炮计划，除原定捐献飞机二十七架外，再增献飞机三架。会议并致函毛主席及中国共产党北京市委会致敬，并表示要以超额完成捐献来庆贺中国共产党建党三十周年。
