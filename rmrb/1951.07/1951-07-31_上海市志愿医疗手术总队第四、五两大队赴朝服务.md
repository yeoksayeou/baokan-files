Title: Shanghai Volunteer Medical Surgical Corps' Fourth and Fifth Detachments Depart for Service in Korea
Author:
Date: 1951-07-31
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency report:

　　Shanghai Volunteer Medical Surgical Corps' Fourth and Fifth Detachments Depart for Service in Korea
    [This newspaper report] According to Xinhua News Agency: The Shanghai Resist-America Aid-Korea Volunteer Medical Surgical Corps' Fourth and Fifth Detachments departed from Shanghai on the afternoon of the 27th to serve on the Korean front. The majority of these two detachments are comprised of physicians, nurses, and practicing Chinese and Western medicine doctors from missionary hospitals. Many are renowned medical experts, such as Ni Baochun, a plastic surgery expert and dean of St. John's University Medical School; Nie Chuanxian, an ophthalmology expert, vice president of Aurora University Medical School, and director of the ophthalmology department at Guangci Hospital; and Liu Qicheng, a surgery expert and director of the Shanghai Health Sanatorium. Before the team's departure, Sheng Pihua, Deputy Mayor of the Shanghai Municipal People's Government, representatives from various people's organizations, medical workers, and over two thousand others gathered at the station to see them off.
    The Shanghai Resist-America Aid-Korea Volunteer Medical Surgical Corps is currently organizing a technical advisory group, headed by Shen Kefei, a renowned Chinese surgical expert and president of Shanghai Zhongshan Hospital. The group will include experts in orthopedics, thoracic surgery, neurosurgery, ophthalmology, X-ray, and other specialties. After its formation, the group will also depart regularly to serve on the Korean front.

### 上海市志愿医疗手术总队第四、五两大队赴朝服务

1951-07-31
第1版()
专栏：

　　上海市志愿医疗手术总队第四、五两大队赴朝服务
    【本报讯】据新华社讯：上海市抗美援朝志愿医疗手术总队第四、第五大队已于二十七日下午由上海出发赴朝鲜前线。这两个大队大部分是由教会医院的医师、护士和开业的中西医师组成的。有许多是著名的医学专家，如整形外科专家、圣约翰大学医学院院长倪葆春，眼科专家、震旦大学医学院副院长、广慈医院眼科主任聂传贤，外科专家、上海卫生疗养院院长刘启承等。该队出发前，上海市人民政府副市长盛丕华，各人民团体代表，医务工作者等二千余人到车站欢送。
    上海市抗美援朝志愿医疗手术总队正在组织一个技术顾问团，由中国著名的外科专家、上海中山医院院长沈克非任团长。团员包括骨科、胸腔、脑神经、眼科、爱克司光等科专家。该团组成后亦将定期出发赴朝鲜前线服务。
