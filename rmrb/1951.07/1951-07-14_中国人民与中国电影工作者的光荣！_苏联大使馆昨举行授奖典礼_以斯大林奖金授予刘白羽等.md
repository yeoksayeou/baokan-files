Title: Glory of the Chinese People and Chinese Filmmakers! Soviet Embassy Held Award Ceremony Yesterday, Stalin Prizes Awarded to Liu Baiyu and Others
Author:
Date: 1951-07-14
Page: 4

　　The Glory of the Chinese People and Chinese Filmmakers!
    Soviet Embassy Held Award Ceremony Yesterday
    Stalin Prizes Awarded to Liu Baiyu and Others
    [Xinhua News Agency, 13th] The Soviet Embassy, representing the Stalin Prize Committee, held an award ceremony this afternoon to award prizes to Chinese filmmakers Liu Baiyu, Wu Benli, Zhou Feng, He Shide, Xu Xiaobing, Su Heqing, Zhou Libo, and Li Hua for their joint production with Soviet filmmakers of the films "The Victory of the Chinese People" and "Liberated China". For the award winners Hao Yusheng, Li Bingzhong, Xu Lai, and Ye Hui, who were unable to attend the award ceremony, the prizes will be awarded at a later date. The award ceremony was presided over by Soviet Ambassador Roshchin. Attendees included Guo Moruo, Vice Premier of the Central People's Government's Government Administration Council; Shen Yanbing, Minister of the Ministry of Culture; Zhou Yang and Ding Xilin, Vice Ministers; Wu Xiuquan, Vice Minister of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs; and figures from the cultural sphere such as Yang Hansheng, Shao Quanlin, Qian Junrui, Shakov, Zheng Zhenduo, Hong Shen, Ouyang Yuqian, Shu Sheyu, Song Zhi's, and Zhang Junxiang.
    In his speech at the award ceremony, Soviet Ambassador Roshchin said: The joint creation by Chinese and Soviet filmmakers of the films "The Victory of the Chinese People" and "Liberated China" is not only a great achievement in the film art of our two countries, but also another proof of the unbreakable friendship and close cooperation between the two great peoples. The Soviet people have given high praise to these two films, which is why the Stalin Prize is awarded to the filmmakers of these two films.
    Vice Premier Guo Moruo and Minister Shen Yanbing also delivered speeches at the meeting. Guo Moruo said: The awarding of the Stalin Prize to Chinese filmmakers this time is not only the glory of the winners themselves, but also the glory of all the Chinese people. Because only the victory of the Chinese people's revolution can create these two films.
    Finally, Liu Baiyu, representing the Chinese filmmakers who participated in the production of "The Victory of the Chinese People", and Xu Xiaobing, representing the Chinese filmmakers who participated in the production of "Liberated China", delivered acceptance speeches. They unanimously stated: It is a supreme honor to receive the prize named after the great Stalin. In the future, we must work harder to learn from the high achievements of Soviet film art, learn from the patriotism and internationalism of Soviet film work, and strive to build a great motherland and defend world peace.

### 中国人民与中国电影工作者的光荣！  苏联大使馆昨举行授奖典礼  以斯大林奖金授予刘白羽等

1951-07-14
第4版()
专栏：

　　中国人民与中国电影工作者的光荣！
    苏联大使馆昨举行授奖典礼
    以斯大林奖金授予刘白羽等
    【新华社十三日讯】苏联驻华大使馆代表斯大林奖金委员会于今日下午举行授奖典礼，以奖金授予与苏联电影工作者联合摄制《中国人民的胜利》和《解放了的中国》两片的中国电影工作者刘白羽、吴本立、周峰、何士德、徐肖冰、苏河清、周立波和李华等八人。对于未能参加此次授奖典礼的得奖人郝玉生、李秉忠、徐来和叶惠，将另行订期颁发奖金。授奖典礼由苏联大使罗申主持，出席典礼的有：中央人民政府政务院郭沫若副总理、文化部沈雁冰部长，周扬、丁西林副部长，外交部伍修权副部长，以及文化界人士阳翰笙、邵荃麟、钱俊瑞、沙可夫、郑振铎、洪深、欧阳予倩、舒舍予、宋之的、张骏祥等人。
    苏联大使罗申在颁发奖金时致词说：中苏两国电影工作者的共同创作《中国人民的胜利》和《解放了的中国》两部影片，不仅是我们两国电影艺术上的巨大成绩，也是两国伟大人民之间牢不可破的友谊和紧密合作的又一个证明。苏联人民对这两部影片给予了高度的评价，这就是对这两部影片的摄制者授予斯大林奖金。
    郭沫若副总理和沈雁冰部长在会上也发表了演说。郭沫若说：中国电影工作者此次荣获斯大林奖金，不仅是得奖者个人的光荣，也是全中国人民的光荣。因为只有中国人民革命的胜利，才能创造出这两部影片。
    最后，由刘白羽代表参加摄制《中国人民的胜利》、徐肖冰代表参加摄制《解放了的中国》两片的中国电影工作者致答词。他们一致表示：荣获以伟大的斯大林为名的奖金是无上光荣，今后要更加努力学习苏联电影艺术的高度成就，学习苏联电影工作的爱国主义和国际主义精神，为建设伟大的祖国和保卫世界和平而奋斗。
