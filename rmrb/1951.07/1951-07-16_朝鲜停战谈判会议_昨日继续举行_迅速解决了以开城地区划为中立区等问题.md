Title: Korean Armistice Talks Continued Yesterday; Issues Such as Designating the Kaesong Area as a Neutral Zone Were Quickly Resolved
Author:
Date: 1951-07-16
Page: 1
　
　　Korean Armistice Talks
    Continued Yesterday
    Issues Such as Designating the Kaesong Area as a Neutral Zone Were Quickly Resolved
    [Xinhua News Agency, Pyongyang, July 15th] This reporter learned from the Korean front headquarters: After a three-day recess, the Korean armistice talks resumed for the third meeting at 1:10 PM (Korean time) on July 15th. During the meeting, the issue of designating the Kaesong area as a neutral zone, the size of the meeting area, and other related specific issues were quickly resolved, and discussions continued on the agendas proposed by both sides. The meeting concluded at 3:00 PM. The fourth meeting is scheduled to be held at 9:00 AM on July 16th.

### 朝鲜停战谈判会议  昨日继续举行  迅速解决了以开城地区划为中立区等问题

1951-07-16
第1版()
专栏：

　　朝鲜停战谈判会议
    昨日继续举行
    迅速解决了以开城地区划为中立区等问题
    【新华社平壤十五日电】本社记者由朝鲜前线总部获悉：朝鲜停战谈判会议休会三日后，在七月十五日下午一时十分（朝鲜时间）继续举行第三次会议。会议中迅速解决了以开城地区划为中立地区的问题，会址区域大小的问题，以及其他有关的具体问题，并继续讨论了双方所提出的议程。会议在下午三时结束。预定第四次会议将在七月十六日上午九时举行。
