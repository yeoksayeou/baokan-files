Title: Eighth Meeting of Korean Armistice Talks Scheduled for Today
Author:
Date: 1951-07-21
Page: 1

　　Xinhua News Agency, Pyongyang, 20th: This reporter has learned from the forward headquarters in Korea that the eighth meeting of the Korean armistice talks was originally scheduled to be held at 10:00 AM (Korean time) on July 20th. However, due to rain and blocked roads on the morning of July 20th, the United Nations Command delegation was unable to come to Kaesong. In the afternoon, with the consent of the Korean People's Army and the Chinese People's Volunteer Army delegation, the United Nations Command delegation rescheduled the eighth meeting to be held at 9:00 AM on July 21st.

### 朝鲜停战谈判会议第八次会议定今举行

1951-07-21
第1版()
专栏：

　　朝鲜停战谈判会议第八次会议定今举行
    【新华社平壤二十日电】本社记者自朝鲜前线总部获悉：朝鲜停战谈判会议第八次会议原定于七月二十日上午十时（朝鲜时间）举行，但因二十日上午天雨，道路阻塞，联合国军代表团未能前来开城。下午联合国军代表团，征得朝鲜人民军及中国人民志愿军代表团同意，将第八次会议改于七月二十一日上午九时举行。
