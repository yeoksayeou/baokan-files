Title: Capital Cultural Circles Gather to Commemorate Dimitrov
Author:
Date: 1951-07-04
Page: 1

　　Xinhua News Agency, 3rd. The capital's cultural figures, over two hundred people, gathered at 8:00 PM today for a memorial meeting to commemorate the leader of the Bulgarian people and great warrior of the international communist movement, Dimitrov. The memorial meeting was presided over by Hong Shen, Director of the Bureau of Foreign Cultural Relations of the Cultural and Educational Committee of the Central People's Government's Government Administration Council. The Bulgarian Ambassador to China, Petkov, was invited to attend the memorial meeting. Zhou Yang, Deputy Minister of the Ministry of Culture, delivered a speech at the meeting, emphasizing that in commemorating Dimitrov, it is necessary to learn from Dimitrov's brilliant example of closely combining internationalism and patriotism, and to learn from his fearless spirit and brilliant revolutionary personality. After the meeting, a documentary film of Dimitrov's funeral was screened.

### 首都文化界人士集会纪念季米特洛夫

1951-07-04
第1版()
专栏：

　　首都文化界人士集会纪念季米特洛夫
    【新华社三日讯】首都文化界人士二百余人于今日下午八时举行纪念会，纪念保加利亚人民领袖、国际共产主义的伟大战士季米特洛夫。纪念会由中央人民政府政务院文化教育委员会对外文化联络事务局洪深局长主持。保加利亚驻华大使彼得科夫应邀出席了纪念会。文化部周扬副部长在会上发表演说，强调指出纪念季米特洛夫，要学习季米特洛夫把国际主义与爱国主义密切结合的光辉榜样，学习他的大无畏的精神和光辉的革命人格。会后放映了季米特洛夫葬礼的纪录影片。
