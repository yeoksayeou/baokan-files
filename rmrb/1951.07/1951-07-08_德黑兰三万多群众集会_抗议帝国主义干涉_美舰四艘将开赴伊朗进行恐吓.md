Title: More Than 30,000 People Gather in Tehran to Protest Imperialist Interference; Four US Warships to Sail to Iran to Intimidate
Author:
Date: 1951-07-08
Page: 4

　　Tehran, TASS dispatch, July 7th: On the evening of June 29th, the "National Iranian Anti-Imperialist Oil Company Struggle Association" held a mass rally in the square opposite the Iranian Parliament in Tehran. More than 30,000 people participated in the rally. The conference passed the following resolutions: "To expedite the removal of all Iranian oil enterprises from the management of the former Anglo-Iranian Oil Company; to resolutely resist the interference of the International Court of Justice in The Hague, an instrument of imperialism; to resolutely oppose Britain's threat of military intervention and the United States' interference in Iran's internal affairs; and to unite all anti-imperialist forces in the country to fight for Iran's freedom and independence."
    The conference also demanded freedom for political parties and trade unions, the release of political prisoners, and the lifting of martial law in Khuzistan.
    Xinhua News Agency dispatch: New Delhi News: According to the Colombo English weekly *People's Voice* of June 29th, four American destroyers, transferred from the Korean front to Iran to intimidate the Iranian people, were berthed in Colombo, Ceylon, from June 29th to July 2nd for a "operational visit".
    These four destroyers are: the "Fiske", the "William S. Ruch", the "Joseph P. Kennedy" and the "Hawkins".

### 德黑兰三万多群众集会  抗议帝国主义干涉  美舰四艘将开赴伊朗进行恐吓

1951-07-08
第4版()
专栏：

　　德黑兰三万多群众集会
    抗议帝国主义干涉
    美舰四艘将开赴伊朗进行恐吓
    【新华社七日讯】塔斯社德黑兰讯：六月二十九日晚，“伊朗全国反帝国主义石油公司斗争协会”在德黑兰国会对面广场召开群众大会。参加大会的有三万多人。大会通过了下列决议案：“尽速使伊朗一切石油企业摆脱前英伊石油公司的管理；坚决反抗作为帝国主义工具的海牙国际法庭的干涉；坚决反对英国出兵的威胁和美国对伊朗内政的干涉；联合全国一切反帝国主义的力量为争取伊朗的自由与独立而斗争。”
    大会并要求政党与工会的自由、要求释放政治犯和解除库齐斯坦的戒严令。
    【新华社讯】新德里消息：据科伦坡英文《人民呼声》周刊六月二十九日载称：从朝鲜前线调往伊朗去恐吓伊朗人民的四艘美国驱逐舰，自六月二十九日至七月二日停泊在锡兰的科伦坡作“行动访问”。
    这四艘驱逐舰是：“费斯克”号，“威廉·斯·鲁希”号，“约瑟夫·伯·凯尼第”号和“霍金丝”号。
