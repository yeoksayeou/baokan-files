Title: Generals Kim Il-sung and Peng Dehuai Notify Ridgway of Agreement to Hold Korean Armistice Talks, Suggesting Kaesong Area as Venue for Representatives' Meeting
Author:
Date: 1951-07-02
Page: 1

  　Kim Il-sung and Peng Dehuai Notify Ridgway
    Agree to Hold Korean Armistice Talks
    Suggest Kaesong Area as Venue for Representatives' Meeting
    [Xinhua News Agency, 1st] Important news from the Korean front: General Kim Il-sung, Commander-in-Chief of the Korean People's Army, and General Peng Dehuai, Commander of the Chinese People's Volunteers, jointly sent a reply to General Ridgway, Commander-in-Chief of the United Nations Command, after consultation today. This was in response to General Ridgway's broadcast notification yesterday (June 30th), in which he stated his willingness to send representatives to meet with representatives of the Korean People's Army and the Chinese People's Volunteers for negotiations on an armistice.
    The notification from General Kim Il-sung and General Peng Dehuai is as follows:
    "To General Ridgway, Commander-in-Chief of the United Nations Command:
    We have received your statement of June 30th of this year regarding peace talks. We are authorized to declare to you that we agree to meet with your representatives for negotiations on the cessation of military action and the establishment of peace. We suggest the Kaesong area on the 38th parallel as the meeting place. If you agree, our representatives are prepared to meet with your representatives between July 10th and 15th, 1951.
                       (Signed) Commander-in-Chief of the Korean People's Army, Kim Il-sung
                              Commander of the Chinese People's Volunteers, Peng Dehuai
                        July 1st, 1951"
    [Xinhua News Agency, 1st] The notification issued by General Ridgway, Commander-in-Chief of the United Nations Command, on June 30th is as follows:
    "In my capacity as Commander-in-Chief of the United Nations Command, I am ordered to negotiate the following matters with your forces: Because I have learned that your side may wish to hold an armistice conference to cease all hostilities and armed action in Korea, and is willing to appropriately guarantee the implementation of this armistice agreement. After receiving your reply to this document, I will send my representatives and propose a date for a meeting with your representatives. I further propose that this meeting be held on a Danish hospital ship in Wonsan Harbor. (Signed) Commander-in-Chief of the United Nations Command, Ridgway."

### 金日成彭德怀两将军通知李奇微  同意举行朝鲜停战谈判  建议以开城地区为双方代表会晤地点

1951-07-02
第1版()
专栏：

  　金日成彭德怀两将军通知李奇微
    同意举行朝鲜停战谈判
    建议以开城地区为双方代表会晤地点
    【新华社一日讯】朝鲜前线重要消息：朝鲜人民军总司令金日成将军，和中国志愿军司令员彭德怀将军，本日在会商之后，联名向联合国军总司令李奇微将军，发出回答的通知。因为李奇微将军在昨天（六月三十日）发出了广播通知，在此通知中声明愿意派遣代表与朝鲜人民军和中国志愿军方面的代表举行关于停战的谈判。
    金日成将军和彭德怀将军的通知如下：
    “联合国军总司令李奇微将军：
    你在本年六月三十日关于和平谈判的声明收到了。我们受权向你声明，我们同意为举行关于停止军事行动和建立和平的谈判而和你的代表会晤。会晤地点，我们建议在三八线上的开城地区。若你同意，我们的代表准备于一九五一年七月十日至十五日和你的代表会晤。
                       （签字）  朝鲜人民军总司令  金日成
                              中国志愿军司令员  彭德怀
                        一九五一年七月一日”
    【新华社一日讯】六月三十日联合国军总司令李奇微发出的通知如下：
    “本人以联合国军总司令的资格，奉命与贵军谈判下列的事项：因为我得知贵方可能希望举行一停战会议，以停止在朝鲜的一切敌对行为及武装行动，并愿适当保证此停战协议的实施。我在获得贵方对本文的答复以后，将派出我方代表并提出一会议的日期，以便与贵方代表会晤。我更提议此会议可在元山港一只丹麦伤兵船上举行。（签字）联合国军总司令李奇微。”
