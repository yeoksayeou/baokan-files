Title: Our Great Motherland (Picture)
Author:
Date: 1951-07-07
Page: 1

    Our Great Motherland
    Students from all over the country responded to the call of the motherland and rushed to sign up for military cadre schools. Major universities and middle schools in Beijing established student recommendation committees for military cadre schools one after another starting from June 27th. This is the scene of students from Peking University enthusiastically signing up after the recommendation committee announced the registration.

### 我们伟大的祖国（图片）

1951-07-07
第1版()
专栏：

    我们伟大的祖国
    各地学生响应祖国的号召，争先报名投考军事干部学校，北京市各大、中学校自六月二十七日起，先后成立了军事干部学校学生保送委员会。这是北京大学学生在保送委员会宣布报名后踊跃报名的情形。
