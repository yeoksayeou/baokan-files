Title: Headquarters of the China Democratic League Holds Memorial Service and Mourning Ritual to Commemorate the League's Martyred Heroes; Wen Yiduo Buried in Beijing Revolutionary Martyrs' Cemetery
Author:
Date: 1951-07-18
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, July 17th: The headquarters of the China Democratic League held a memorial service and mourning ritual in Beijing on the 15th of this month—the China Democratic League Martyrs' Memorial Day—to commemorate the League's martyred heroes. After the service, the Wen Yiduo Martyr Burial Committee held a funeral for Martyr Wen Yiduo and a burial ceremony at the Beijing Revolutionary Martyrs' Cemetery.

The memorial service and mourning ritual were held at 8:30 AM at the Cultural Club of the National Committee of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference (CPPCC). Attendees included representatives from the National Committee of the CPPCC, representatives from the Chinese Communist Party and other democratic parties, representatives of non-party democratic figures from the CPPCC, representatives from the cultural and educational circles of the capital, heads and professors from various universities, and relatives and friends of the martyrs, totaling approximately 200 people. Zhang Lan, Chairman of the China Democratic League, attended the memorial service in person.

At the meeting, Shen Junru, Vice Chairman of the China Democratic League, delivered a speech. After recounting the heroic deeds of martyrs such as Wen Yiduo and Li Gongpu, he called on all League members to better inherit the unfinished aspirations of the martyrs under the leadership of the Chinese Communist Party and Chairman Mao, to continue strengthening the anti-imperialist and patriotic struggle, to completely eliminate the remnants of the Chiang Kai-shek bandit gang, and to strive to build a great motherland to the very end!

Representatives of the guests who spoke in succession included Li Jishen, Vice Chairman of the National Committee of the CPPCC and Chairman of the Revolutionary Committee of the Chinese Kuomintang; Xu Bing, Deputy Minister of the United Front Work Department of the Central Committee of the CPC; Huang Yanpei, Convenor of the China National Democratic Construction Association; Guo Moruo, representative of non-party democratic figures from the CPPCC; Ma Xulun, Chairman of the China Association for Promoting Democracy; and Xu Deheng, Chairman of the Jiusan Society. In their speeches, they expressed their high admiration and deep condolences for the heroic spirit shown by martyrs such as Li Gongpu and Wen Yiduo in their struggle against Chiang Kai-shek's dictatorial and traitorous regime. They also expressed sincere condolences to the families of the deceased martyrs.

The memorial service and mourning ritual concluded after Shen Junru offered a sacrifice. Following this, a funeral was held for Martyr Wen Yiduo, with the procession proceeding to the Babaoshan Revolutionary Martyrs' Cemetery in the western suburbs of Beijing. Over one hundred people served as pallbearers.

The burial ceremony concluded at approximately 12:00 noon.

### 民盟总部举行纪念会及祭灵仪式  纪念民盟殉难烈士  闻一多安葬于北京革命烈士公墓

1951-07-18
第1版()
专栏：

　　民盟总部举行纪念会及祭灵仪式
    纪念民盟殉难烈士
    闻一多安葬于北京革命烈士公墓
    【新华社十七日讯】中国民主同盟总部于本月十五日——民盟殉难烈士纪念日——在北京举行该盟殉难烈士纪念会和祭灵仪式。会后，由闻一多烈士安葬委员会为闻一多烈士举殡，并在北京革命烈士公墓举行安葬仪式。
    纪念会和祭灵仪式于上午八时半在人民政协全国委员会文化俱乐部举行。参加追悼的来宾有：人民政协全国委员会代表、中国共产党及其他各民主党派代表、人民政协无党派民主人士代表、首都文教界代表、各大学负责人和教授以及烈士生前亲友等共约二百余人。民盟主席张澜曾亲临祭奠。
    会上，首由民盟副主席沈钧儒致词，他在叙述了闻一多、李公朴等烈士英勇斗争的事迹后，号召全盟盟员，在中共和毛主席的领导下，更好地继承先烈们的未竟之志，为继续加强反帝爱国斗争，彻底消灭蒋介石残余匪帮，建设伟大的祖国而奋斗到底！
    来宾代表中相继讲话的有人民政协全国委员会副主席、中国国民党革命委员会主席李济深，中共中央统战部副部长徐冰，民主建国会召集人黄炎培，人民政协无党派民主人士代表郭沫若，中国民主促进会主席马叙伦，九三学社主席许德珩等人。在讲话中，他们对李公朴、闻一多等烈士为反对蒋介石独裁卖国而表现的英勇斗争精神，表示崇高的敬佩和深切的悼念。对于死难烈士家属表示真挚的慰问。
    纪念会和祭灵仪式在沈钧儒献祭后结束。接着为闻一多烈士举殡，启灵至北京西郊八宝山革命烈士公墓，执绋者达一百余人。
    葬仪于十二时许宣告结束。
