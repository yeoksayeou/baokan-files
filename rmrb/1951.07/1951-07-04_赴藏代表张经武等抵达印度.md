Title: Representative Zhang Jingwu and Others Arrive in India
Author:
Date: 1951-07-04
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, New Delhi, 3rd—Zhang Jingwu, representative of the Central People's Government to Tibet, and Keimo Soan Wangdui, Tudan Danda, Sangpo Dengzeng Dunzhu and other fully empowered representatives of the Tibetan local government, arrived in Calcutta, India, by plane at 2 p.m. on the 1st. Yuan Zhongxian, China's ambassador to India, Yao Zhongkang, Consul General in Calcutta, and representatives of overseas Chinese went to the airport to welcome them. That evening, Consul General Yao Zhongkang hosted a banquet to welcome them.

### 赴藏代表张经武等抵达印度

1951-07-04
第1版()
专栏：

　　赴藏代表张经武等抵达印度
    【新华社新德里三日电】中央人民政府赴西藏代表张经武及西藏地方政府全权代表凯墨·索安旺堆、土丹旦达、桑颇·登增顿珠等一行，已于一日下午二时飞抵印度加尔各答。前往机场欢迎者有我国驻印度大使袁仲贤、驻加尔各答总领事姚仲康和华侨代表等。当晚，姚仲康总领事设宴欢迎。
