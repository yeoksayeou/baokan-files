Title: People in Various Parts of the Korean Rear Area Prepare to Celebrate Our Army Day, Will Go to the Front to Console Our Volunteer Army
Author:
Date: 1951-07-26
Page: 1

　　The People in Various Parts of the Korean Rear Area
　　Prepare to Celebrate Our Army Day
　　Will Go to the Front to Console Our Volunteer Army
    [Xinhua News Agency, Pyongyang, 24th] The people in various parts of the Korean rear area are preparing to celebrate the founding anniversary of the Chinese People's Liberation Army, and are enthusiastically supporting and comforting the Chinese People's Volunteers who are fighting bravely on the Korean battlefield. Letters of gratitude and comfort letters from the Korean working people to the Chinese People's Liberation Army and the Chinese People's Volunteers are sent like snowflakes to the local committees of the Korean Fatherland Defense Support Association every day. Workers, farmers, students, women, and Young Pioneer members in various places are actively organizing people's delegations and art troupes, preparing to go to the front to comfort the volunteer army units around the "August 1st" holiday. People's organizations such as the General Federation of Trade Unions, the Farmers' Alliance, the Democratic Youth League, the Democratic Women's League, and the General Federation of Literary and Art Circles, as well as staff members of various agencies, factories, and enterprises, are making pennants to dedicate to the Volunteer Army. Personnel from various agencies and organizations in Pyongyang are preparing to present the Chinese People's Volunteers with various photos, albums, books and periodicals, and other large quantities of gifts that express the friendship between China and Korea. Up to now, farmers in the suburbs of Pyongyang alone have collected more than 2,600 items of various comfort goods; women in the northern district of Pyongyang have collected more than 1,800 items of comfort goods such as gramophones and harmonicas.

### 朝鲜后方各地人民  筹备庆祝我建军节  将赴前线慰问我志愿军

1951-07-26
第1版()
专栏：

　　朝鲜后方各地人民
　  筹备庆祝我建军节
　  将赴前线慰问我志愿军
    【新华社平壤二十四日电】朝鲜后方各地人民正准备庆祝中国人民解放军建军节，并热烈支援与慰劳在朝鲜战场上英勇斗争的中国人民志愿军。朝鲜劳动人民写给中国人民解放军、中国人民志愿军的感谢信和慰问信，每天都雪片似地送到朝鲜祖国保卫后援会的各地委员会。各地工人、农民、学生、妇女、少年团员们正积极组织人民代表团和文工团，准备在“八一”节前后到前线慰问志愿军各部队。职业总同盟、农民同盟、民主青年同盟、民主妇女同盟、文学艺术总同盟等人民团体，及各机关、工厂、企业的工作人员，正纷纷制作献给志愿军的锦旗。平壤市各机关、团体人员，准备以表现中朝友谊的各种照片、画册、书刊及其他大批礼物，赠送给中国人民志愿军。截至目前止，仅平壤郊区农民就已募集了各种慰劳品二千六百余件；平壤北区妇女已募集了留声机、口琴等慰劳品一千八百余件。
