Title: Our Great Motherland (Picture)
Author: 
Date: 1951-07-31
Page: 1

    Our Great Motherland
    The enrollment work for military cadre schools across the country has basically concluded. According to incomplete statistics from the six major administrative regions of Northeast, North China, East China, Central South, Southwest, and Northwest, the number of applicants reached over 330,000. The students approved in various regions have been gathering at various military cadre schools to study. Here, the educated youths who previously participated in military cadre schools are introducing the situation of military life to the students who are participating this time.

### 我们伟大的祖国（图片）

1951-07-31
第1版()
专栏：

    我们伟大的祖国
    全国各地军事干部学校的招生工作，基本上已经结束。根据东北、华北、华东、中南、西南、西北六大行政区的不完全统计，报名人数达三十三万余人。各地被批准的同学已纷纷集中到各种军事干部学校学习。在这里，前次参加军事干部学校的知识青年，正在向这次参加军事干部学校的同学介绍军中生活的情形。
