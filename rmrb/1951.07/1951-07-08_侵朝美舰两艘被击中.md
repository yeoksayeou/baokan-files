Title: Two Invading US Warships Hit in Korea
Author:
Date: 1951-07-08
Page: 1

　　Xinhua News Agency, Washington, July 7th: TASS, Washington, July 6th: The United States Navy Department announced that the heavy escort frigate "Eversole" was hit by shore-based artillery fire outside Wonsan Harbor on July 4th. The Navy Department also announced that the destroyer "Duke" was hit by shore-based artillery fire outside Wonsan Harbor last week, and part of the deck was damaged.

### 侵朝美舰两艘被击中

1951-07-08
第1版()
专栏：

　　侵朝美舰两艘被击中
    【新华社七日讯】塔斯社华盛顿六日讯：美国海军部宣布：重型护航快舰“埃弗尔斯号”，七月四日在元山港外被岸上炮火击中。海军部又宣布驱逐舰“杜克尔号”上周在元山港外被岸上炮火击中，甲板部分受伤。
