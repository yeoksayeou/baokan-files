Title: Shanghai Military Control Commission Requisitions Property of American Oil Companies; Employees of American Mobil, Texaco, and China America Companies Pledge to Assist the Government in Completing the Requisition Work
Author:
Date: 1951-07-22
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Shanghai, July 20th: For the sake of national interests, the Shanghai Military Control Commission of the Chinese People's Liberation Army has decided to requisition all property, except for the headquarters and branch offices, of the American Mobil, Texaco, and China America Oil Companies, which were previously under control, and to purchase their existing oil stocks. On the morning of the 18th, the Foreign Affairs Office of the Military Control Commission convened managers and employee representatives of the three oil companies to announce this requisition order. After the news reached the three companies, all employees enthusiastically supported it, hung five-star national flags on all oil depots and ships, and lined up to welcome the arrival of the Military Control Commission commissioners to carry out the requisition task. Employees of the Texaco oil storage depot expressed at the welcoming meeting that they would resolutely assist the government in completing the requisition work, protecting oil and property, and ensuring that they would carry forward the achievements made in production since the implementation of control, implement the "June 1st" call of the China People's Anti-American Aggression and Aid Korea General Association, and complete the task of increasing production and making donations.

### 上海市军事管制委员会征用美商油公司财产  美商美孚、德士古和中美三公司职工保证协助政府做好征用工作

1951-07-22
第1版()
专栏：

　　上海市军事管制委员会征用美商油公司财产
　  美商美孚、德士古和中美三公司职工保证协助政府做好征用工作
    【新华社上海二十日电】中国人民解放军上海市军事管制委员会为国家利益起见，决定征用前已实行管制的美商美孚、德士古和中美三油公司除其总公司和分支机构之办公处以外的全部财产，并征购其所存油料。十八日上午由军事管制委员会外侨事务处召集该三油公司经理人员及职工代表，宣布此项征用命令。消息传到三公司以后，全体职工热烈拥护，都在各油库船只上悬挂了五星国旗，并列队欢迎军事管制委员会专员到达执行征用任务。德士古储油所职工在欢迎会上表示坚决协助政府做好征用工作，保护油料及财产，并保证要发扬管制以来在生产上所创造的成绩，贯彻中国人民抗美援朝总会“六一”号召，完成增产捐献的任务。
