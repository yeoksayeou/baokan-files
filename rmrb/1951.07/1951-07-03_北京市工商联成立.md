Title: Beijing Federation of Industry and Commerce Established
Author:
Date: 1951-07-03
Page: 2

　　The Beijing Federation of Industry and Commerce Established

　　The industrial and commercial circles of Beijing held a representative meeting from the 26th to the 28th, formally establishing the Beijing Federation of Industry and Commerce. The meeting focused on how to deepen the resistance against U.S. aggression and aid Korea, promote patriotic pacts, and expand the patriotic donation movement. The attending representatives unanimously guaranteed that they would strive to overfulfill the plan to donate airplanes and cannons, and would donate three more airplanes in addition to the original plan to donate 27 airplanes. The meeting also sent letters of respect to Chairman Mao and the Beijing Municipal Committee of the Chinese Communist Party, expressing their intention to celebrate the 30th anniversary of the founding of the Chinese Communist Party by overfulfilling the donations.

　　The meeting elected Fu Huating, Shi Jinkui, Zhao Jiwu, Yuan Songting and 91 others as executive committee members, and adopted the organizational charter of the Beijing Federation of Industry and Commerce. The charter stipulates that the purpose of the Municipal Federation of Industry and Commerce is to unite industry and commerce in Beijing, under the leadership of the People's Government, in accordance with the Common Program, to implement the general policy of taking into consideration both public and private interests, benefiting both labor and capital, mutual assistance between urban and rural areas, and internal and external exchanges, in order to achieve the goal of developing production and prospering the economy.
　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　(Xinhua News Agency)

### 北京市工商联成立

1951-07-03
第2版()
专栏：

　　北京市工商联成立
    北京市工商界于二十六日至二十八日举行代表会议，正式成立北京市工商联合会。会议曾着重研究了如何深入抗美援朝、推行爱国公约与扩大爱国捐献运动。到会代表一致保证：争取超额完成捐献飞机大炮计划，除原定捐献飞机二十七架外，再增献飞机三架。会议并致函毛主席及中国共产党北京市委会致敬，并表示要以超额完成捐献来庆贺中国共产党建党三十周年。
    会议选举傅华亭、石金奎、赵辑五、袁松亭等九十五人为执行委员，并通过北京市工商联合会组织章程。章程规定：市工商联合会以团结北京市工商业，在人民政府领导下，依据共同纲领，贯彻公私兼顾、劳资两利、城乡互助、内外交流的总方针，达到发展生产，繁荣经济之目的为自己的宗旨。
                                                      （新华社）
