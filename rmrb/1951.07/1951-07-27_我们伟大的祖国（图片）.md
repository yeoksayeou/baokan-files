Title: Our Great Motherland (Picture)
Author:
Date: 1951-07-27
Page: 1

    Our Great Motherland
    Under the leadership and education of the Communist Party, the employees of the Chengdu-Chongqing Railway have displayed a high level of labor enthusiasm, creating a record of laying track at a rate of one kilometer every three hours, and realizing the goal of opening the Chongqing (Chongqing) to Yongchuan (Yongchuan) section to traffic by "July 1st." This is the scene of the "30th Anniversary of the Communist Party of China Commemorative Train" being welcomed by the masses when it arrived in Yongchuan from Chongqing.

### 我们伟大的祖国（图片）

1951-07-27
第1版()
专栏：

    我们伟大的祖国
    成渝铁路的员工们，在共产党的领导和教育下，发挥了高度的劳动热情，创造了每三小时铺轨一公里的纪录，实现了渝（重庆）永（永川）段“七一”通车的愿望。这是“庆祝中国共产党三十周年纪念专车”由重庆开抵永川时受到广大群众欢迎的情形。
