Title: Korean People's Delegation to China Leaves Beijing for Shanghai; Guo Moruo, Peng Zhen, and over 500 Others See Them Off at the Station
Author:
Date: 1951-07-06
Page: 1

　　Xinhua News Agency, 5th: The entire Korean People's Delegation to China and the Art Troupe left Beijing for Shanghai on the evening of the 5th. Those who went to the station to see them off included: Guo Moruo, Chairman of the China People's Anti-American Aid Korea General Association; Vice-Chairmen Peng Zhen and Chen Shutong; Secretary-General Liu Guanyi; Liao Chengzhi, Head of the China People's Delegation to Korea for Comfort; Deputy Head Tian Han; Zhang Hanfu, Deputy Minister of Foreign Affairs of the Central People's Government; Yan Baohang, Deputy Director of the General Office; Chen Jiakang, Acting Director of the Asian Department; Wang Zhuoru, Director of the Protocol Department; representatives of various democratic parties and people's organizations Huang Yanpei, Xu Deheng, Peng Zemin, Mei Gongbin, Li Bozhao, Xie Bangding; and art workers from Beijing, totaling more than 500 people.
    Li Zhouyuan, Ambassador of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea to China, and personnel from the embassy also went to the station to see them off.
    A brief and warm send-off meeting was held at the station. After Guo Moruo, Chairman of the China People's Anti-American Aid Korea General Association, delivered a farewell speech, Won Tong-gun, Head of the Korean People's Delegation to China, gave a reply, expressing gratitude for the warm reception from the China People's Anti-American Aid Korea General Association and the people of all circles in Beijing. He said: The great Chinese people are redoubling their efforts to carry out the Anti-American Aid Korea and Defend the Homeland movement, which has made the Korean people's determination and confidence in victory in the struggle for national independence, freedom, and peace even more firm.
    The entire delegation accepted flowers amidst cheering crowds and applause, boarded the train, and departed. The China People's Anti-American Aid Korea General Association specially sent Tang Mingzhao, Deputy Director of the Liaison Department of the Association, to accompany them along the way and take care of them.

### 朝鲜人民访华代表团离京赴上海    郭沫若彭真等五百余人赴车站欢送

1951-07-06
第1版()
专栏：

　　朝鲜人民访华代表团离京赴上海
    郭沫若彭真等五百余人赴车站欢送
    【新华社五日讯】朝鲜人民访华代表团及艺术团全体人员于五日晚离北京赴上海。到车站欢送的有：中国人民抗美援朝总会主席郭沫若、副主席彭真、陈叔通、秘书长刘贯一，中国人民赴朝慰问团团长廖承志、副团长田汉，中央人民政府外交部副部长章汉夫、办公厅副主任阎宝航、亚洲司代理司长陈家康、交际处长王倬如，各民主党派、人民团体的代表黄炎培、许德珩、彭泽民、梅龚彬、李伯钊、谢邦定，以及北京的艺术工作者等共五百余人。
    朝鲜民主主义人民共和国驻华大使李周渊及大使馆人员亦到车站送行。
    在车站举行了简短热烈的欢送会。中国人民抗美援朝总会主席郭沫若致欢送词后，朝鲜人民访华代表团团长元东根致答词，对中国人民抗美援朝总会及北京各界人民的热烈招待表示感谢。他说：伟大的中国人民再接再厉地开展抗美援朝保家卫国运动，使朝鲜人民为民族的独立、自由与和平而斗争的决心和胜利信心更加坚定。
    该代表团全体人员在群情欢腾、掌声齐鸣中接受献花，登车启程。中国人民抗美援朝总会特派该会联络部副部长唐明照陪行沿途照料。
