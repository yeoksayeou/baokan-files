Title: Mongolian People Warmly Welcome 30th Anniversary of the Revolution, Soviet Delegation Arrives in Mongolian Capital to Participate in Celebrations, People's Democratic Countries Send Delegations to Offer Congratulations
Author:
Date: 1951-07-11
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Ulaanbaatar, July 10th: TASS Ulaanbaatar, July 10th: The Soviet government delegation led by Tarasov, Vice-Chairman of the Presidum of the Supreme Soviet of the Soviet Union and Chairman of the Presidium of the Supreme Soviet of the Russian Soviet Federative Socialist Republic, arrived in Ulaanbaatar on July 9th to participate in the celebrations of the 30th anniversary of the Mongolian People's Revolution. The members of the delegation were Tong Jin, Director of the First Far East Department of the Soviet Ministry of Foreign Affairs, and Zelempilon, Chairman of the Council of Ministers of the Buryat-Mongol Autonomous Soviet Socialist Republic.

Those who welcomed the Soviet government delegation at the airport included: Marshal Choibalsan, Prime Minister of the Mongolian People's Republic, Bumazeng, Chairman of the Presidium of the Little Khural, Zedanbal, General Secretary of the Central Committee of the Mongolian People's Revolutionary Party and Chairman of the Central Preparatory Committee for the 30th Anniversary Commemoration of the People's Revolution, Damba and Susobama, Secretaries of the Central Committee of the Mongolian People's Revolutionary Party, Zedib, Vice-Chairman of the Presidium of the Little Khural, Vice-Premiers Suranjab, Sharap, and Lubusang, Foreign Minister Lhamsuren, Mongolian army generals Janchiv and Denkhayab, senior officials of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs, and other officials.

Also welcoming the Soviet government delegation were diplomatic personnel from the Soviet Embassy in the Mongolian People's Republic, headed by Soviet Ambassador Extraordinary and Plenipotentiary Prikhodov, the Ambassadors of the People's Republic of China and the Democratic People's Republic of Korea, and the delegations from the governments of the various People's Democratic countries who had come to Ulaanbaatar to participate in the celebrations.

On July 9th, shortly before the arrival of the Soviet delegation, government delegations from the Republic of Poland, the Czechoslovak Republic, the German Democratic Republic, the People's Republic of Bulgaria, the Romanian People's Republic, and the People's Republic of Albania arrived in Ulaanbaatar. Prior to this, the government delegation of the Hungarian People's Republic and many Soviet artists had also arrived.

Xinhua News Agency, Ulaanbaatar, July 9th: TASS Ulaanbaatar, July 9th: The Mongolian people are enthusiastically preparing to celebrate the upcoming (July 11th) 30th anniversary of the Mongolian People's Revolution. They are summarizing the results of the labor emulation campaign that has been widely launched throughout the country to commemorate the glorious 30th anniversary. In the first half of this year, the production achievements of the employees of the country's largest production enterprises—the Stalin Meat Packing Plant, the Choibalsan Industrial Enterprise, the factory manufacturing automatic machinery, and many other factories—have greatly exceeded the prescribed targets. The entire industrial sector exceeded the half-year plan by 4.5 percent, and the enterprises of the artisan production cooperatives exceeded the half-year plan by 7 percent. The herdsmen of many villages, their production cooperatives, and the employees of many state-owned farms are working to increase the number of livestock and have achieved new successes in the development of animal husbandry.

### 蒙古人民热烈迎接革命三十周年  苏代表团抵蒙古首都参加庆典  各人民民主国家均派代表团前往祝贺

1951-07-11
第1版()
专栏：

　　蒙古人民热烈迎接革命三十周年
    苏代表团抵蒙古首都参加庆典
    各人民民主国家均派代表团前往祝贺
    【新华社十日讯】塔斯社乌兰巴托尔十日讯：苏联最高苏维埃主席团副主席、俄罗斯苏维埃联邦社会主义共和国最高苏维埃主席团主席塔拉索夫所率领的参加蒙古人民革命三十周年庆祝大典的苏联政府代表团，于七月九日抵达乌兰巴托尔。代表团的团员有苏联外交部第一远东司司长童金和布利亚蒙古自治苏维埃社会主义共和国部长会议主席泽列姆皮隆。
    到机场欢迎苏联政府代表团的有：蒙古人民共和国总理乔巴山元帅，小呼拉尔主席团主席布马增，蒙古人民革命党中央委员会总书记兼人民革命三十周年纪念庆祝会中央筹备委员会主席泽登巴尔，蒙古人民革命党中央委员会书记达姆巴和苏索巴马，小呼拉尔主席团副主席泽狄布，副总理苏兰扎布、沙拉普和鲁布桑，外交部长拉姆苏伦，蒙古军队的将领詹契夫和登哈雅布，外交部高级官员和其他官员等。
    欢迎苏联政府代表团的还有以苏联特命全权大使普里霍多夫为首的苏联驻蒙古人民共和国大使馆的外交人员、中华人民共和国与朝鲜民主主义人民共和国的大使以及前来乌兰巴托尔参加庆祝大典的各人民民主国家政府的代表团。
    七月九日，在苏联代表团抵达前不久，波兰共和国、捷克斯洛伐克共和国、德意志民主共和国、保加利亚人民共和国、罗马尼亚人民共和国和阿尔巴尼亚人民共和国各国的政府代表团到达乌兰巴托尔。在此以前抵达的还有：匈牙利人民共和国政府代表团和苏联艺术工作者多人。
    【新华社十日讯】塔斯社乌兰巴托尔九日讯：蒙古人民热烈筹备庆祝即将来到（七月十一日）的蒙古人民革命第三十周年。他们正在总结为纪念光荣的三十周年而在全国广泛展开的劳动竞赛的结果。在今年上半年，全国最大的生产企业——斯大林肉类包装厂、乔巴山工业企业、制造自动机械的工厂和其它许多工厂等——的职工们的生产成绩，都已大量超过规定的目标。整个工业部门超过半年计划的百分之四点五，技工生产合作社各企业超过半年计划的百分之七。许多村庄的牧人，他们的生产合作社以及许多国营农场的职工，为增加牲畜的头数而工作，并在发展畜牧业方面获得了新的成就。
