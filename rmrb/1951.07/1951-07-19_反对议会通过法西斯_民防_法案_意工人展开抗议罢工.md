Title: Italian Workers Launch Protest Strikes Against Parliament's Passage of Fascist "Civil Defense" Bill
Author:
Date: 1951-07-19
Page: 4

　　Xinhua News Agency Dispatch: Under the manipulation of the Gasperi government, the Italian parliament's lower house has already passed the fascist "Civil Defense" bill on July 11. However, the circumstances surrounding the passage of this bill reveal a serious crisis in the Gasperi government's rule. It has triggered another major split within the Christian Democratic Party.

　　This so-called "Civil Defense" bill authorizes the government to declare a state of national emergency, issue martial law, subject the people to military conscription, arbitrarily conscript people and requisition any property, and authorizes the Ministry of the Interior to organize and command fascist-style volunteer corps. The Gasperi government's actions in forcing the parliament to pass this fascist "Civil Defense" bill have aroused great anger among the people nationwide. A wave of protest strikes has unfolded across the country.

### 反对议会通过法西斯“民防”法案  意工人展开抗议罢工

1951-07-19
第4版()
专栏：

　　反对议会通过法西斯“民防”法案
    意工人展开抗议罢工
    【本报讯】据新华社讯：在加斯贝利政府操纵下，意大利议会下院已经在七月十一日通过了法西斯的“民防”法案，但是，这个法案通过的情形，显示了加斯贝利政府统治的严重危机。它在基督教民主党引起了又一次重大的分裂。
    这个所谓“民防”法案授权政府宣布国家处于紧急状态、颁布戒严令、把人民实行军事编制、任意征调人民和征用任何财产，并且授权内政部组织并指挥法西斯型的义勇兵团。加斯贝利政府强使议会通过这个法西斯“民防”法案的行径，使全国人民感到极大的愤怒。罢工抗议的浪潮已在全国各地展开。
