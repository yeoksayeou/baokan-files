Title: Poland, Hungary, Romania, Bulgaria and other countries publish large quantities of works by Chinese Communist Party leaders; People of India generally welcome the works of Chairman Mao and Liu Shaoqi
Author:
Date: 1951-07-03
Page: 4

　　Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, 30th -- Poland, Hungary, Romania, and Bulgaria have published large quantities of works by Chinese Communist Party leaders. Poland has published Mao Zedong's "Talks at the Yan'an Forum on Literature and Art" (published by the Polish United Workers' Party's "Books and Knowledge" Publishing House in 1950), "Strategic Issues of China's Revolutionary War," and "On Practice" (published in 1951).
　　The Hungarian Spark Publishing House published the collection "The Road to Victory of the Chinese Revolution" in 1950, which collected Mao Zedong's "The Chinese Revolution and the Chinese Communist Party," "On New Democracy," "Strategic Issues of China's Revolutionary War," the section on the policies of the Chinese Communist Party from "On Coalition Government," "The Current Situation and Our Tasks," "Speech at the Jin-Sui Cadre Conference," "On the People's Democratic Dictatorship," and "Message of Greetings at the Celebration of Stalin's 70th Birthday," etc. The Spark Publishing House's "People's Republic of China" collection includes Mao Zedong's "Struggle to Achieve a Fundamental Turn for the Better in the Nation's Financial and Economic Situation," Liu Shaoqi's "On the Party," "Report on the Land Reform Problem," and his speech at the cadres' meeting celebrating May Day in Beijing in 1950, Zhou Enlai's political report at the National Congress of Literary and Art Workers, "Striving to Consolidate and Develop the Victory of the People" published on October 1st of last year, and Ren Bishi's political report at the First National Congress of the Chinese New Democratic Youth League. In addition, the Hungarian Working People's Party's central magazine "Social Science Review" published Mao Zedong's "On Practice" in its second issue this year.
　　Romania has published Mao Zedong's "On the People's Democratic Dictatorship" in both Romanian and Hungarian versions, as well as a separate edition of "On Practice" (the Romanian Workers' Party Central Committee's theoretical and political magazine "Class Struggle" published this article in the first issue of this year, and the history and philosophy magazine "Research" also published this article in the second issue of this year), Liu Shaoqi's "On Internationalism and Nationalism," and Zhou Enlai's "Striving to Consolidate and Develop the Victory of the People."
　　The Bulgarian "Philosophy Magazine" also published Mao Zedong's "On Practice" in its thirty-fourth issue of 1950.
　　Xinhua News Agency, Prague, 30th -- The Swedish Communist Party's theoretical magazine "Our Time" has published many works by Chinese Communist Party leaders since 1948, such as Chairman Mao Zedong's "The Current Situation and Our Tasks," "Unite All Revolutionary Forces of the World to Combat Imperialist Aggression" (an essay written for the special issue commemorating the 31st anniversary of the October Revolution of "For Lasting Peace, For People's Democracy!"), "On Practice," and Liu Shaoqi's "On Internationalism and Nationalism," "On the Party."
　　The Swedish Communist Party attaches great importance to the rich revolutionary experience of the Chinese Communist Party. The 15th Congress of the Swedish Communist Party, held in March of this year, particularly emphasized the importance of "going to the masses"; the important reports and discussions of the Congress often quoted the words of the leaders of the Chinese people.
　　Xinhua News Agency, New Delhi, 30th -- Chairman Mao Zedong's works are extremely popular in India. All major bookstores in major Indian cities display Chairman Mao's works.
　　Indian publishers have published translations of many of Chairman Mao's works in various Indian languages and English. According to publishers in Bombay, Calcutta, and Madras, Chairman Mao's works have been published in Bengali, Hindi, Urdu, Marathi, Tamil, Telugu, Malayalam, and English, totaling as many as 46 kinds, with a total of 175,000 copies.
　　Chairman Mao's famous book "On Practice" has been published in English by the People's Publishing House in Bombay. "On New Democracy" has been published in English in four editions, totaling 20,000 copies, and in Hindi and Marathi in two editions each, and in Bengali and Urdu in 5,000 copies each. "The Chinese Revolution and the Chinese Communist Party" has been published in English and various major Indian languages, totaling 30,000 copies. Another popular work by Chairman Mao, "On the People's Democratic Dictatorship," has been published in English and several Indian languages, totaling 26,000 copies. The Bengali translation has been published in three editions, totaling 11,000 copies. Chairman Mao's "Talks at the Yan'an Forum on Literature and Art" has been published in English and several Indian languages, totaling about 16,000 copies. The first English edition was published in June 1950 and sold out by mid-December; the second edition was published in February 1951.
　　In addition, Comrade Liu Shaoqi's famous book "On the Party" has been translated into Bengali, Hindi, Urdu, Marathi, and Malayalam. Several of his essays have been translated and published in Indian language periodicals and magazines. In June of this year, another of his books, "On Inner-Party Struggle," was published in English, and translations into other languages will soon be published. Comrade Liu Shaoqi's opening speech at the Asian-Australian Trade Union Conference in Beijing in 1949 has also been translated into various Indian languages and widely disseminated, and published in various periodicals and magazines.

### 波兰匈牙利罗马尼亚保加利亚等国  大量出版中国共产党领袖著作   印度人民普遍欢迎毛主席刘少奇著作

1951-07-03
第4版()
专栏：

　　波兰匈牙利罗马尼亚保加利亚等国
    大量出版中国共产党领袖著作
    印度人民普遍欢迎毛主席刘少奇著作
    【新华社莫斯科三十日电】波、匈、罗、保四国大量出版了中国共产党领袖的著作。波兰出版了毛泽东的《在延安文艺座谈会上的讲话》（波兰统一工人党《书与知识》出版社一九五○年出版）、《中国革命战争的战略问题》和《实践论》（一九五一年出版）。
    匈牙利火花社一九五○年出版了《中国革命胜利的道路》文集，收集了毛泽东的《中国革命与中国共产党》、《新民主主义论》、《中国革命战争的战略问题》、《论联合政府》中关于中国共产党的政策部分、《目前形势和我们的任务》、《在晋绥干部会议上的讲话》、《论人民民主专政》、《在斯大林七十寿辰庆祝大会上的祝词》等。火花社出版的《中华人民共和国》文集，收集了毛泽东的《为争取国家财政经济状况的基本好转而斗争》，刘少奇的《论党》、《关于土地改革问题的报告》和一九五○年在北京庆祝五一劳动节干部大会上的演说，周恩来在中华全国文学艺术工作者代表大会上的政治报告，去年十月一日发表的《为巩固和发展人民的胜利而奋斗》，任弼时在中国新民主主义青年团第一次全国代表大会上的政治报告。此外，匈牙利劳动人民党中央杂志《社会科学评论》今年第二期刊载了毛泽东的《实践论》。
    罗马尼亚出版了毛泽东的《论人民民主专政》罗马尼亚文和匈牙利文两种版本，《实践论》的单行本（罗马尼亚工人党中央理论和政治杂志《阶级斗争》今年第一期，历史与哲学杂志《研究》今年第二期亦刊载这篇文章），刘少奇的《论国际主义与民族主义》、周恩来的《为巩固和发展人民的胜利而奋斗》。
    保加利亚的《哲学杂志》一九五○年第三十四期也刊载了毛泽东的《实践论》。
    【新华社布拉格三十日电】瑞典共产党出版的理论杂志《我们的时代》，从一九四八年以来就刊载了中国共产党领袖们的许多著作，例如毛泽东主席的《目前形势与我们的任务》、《全世界革命力量团结起来反对帝国主义的侵略》（给《争取持久和平与人民民主》十月革命三十一周年纪念专号所写的论文）、《实践论》，和刘少奇的《论国际主义与民族主义》、《论党》。
    瑞典共产党非常重视中国共产党丰富的革命经验。今年三月举行的瑞典共产党第十五次代表大会，曾特别强调“到群众中去”的重要性；大会的重要报告和讨论常常引用中国人民领袖们的言论。
    【新华社新德里三十日电】毛泽东主席的著作在印度极受欢迎。印度各大城市的所有大书店几乎都陈列着毛主席的著作。
    印度出版家已以印度的各种语文和英文出版了毛主席许多著作的译本。据孟买、加尔各答与马德拉斯的出版家估计，毛主席的著作已用孟加拉文、印地文、乌尔都文、马拉提文、泰米尔文、泰鲁固文、马来雅冷文以及英文出版，多达四十六种，总共十七万五千册。
    毛主席的名著《实践论》已由孟买人民出版社用英文出版。《新民主主义论》已用英文发行四版，共二万册，用印地文和马拉提文各发行了两版，用孟加拉文与乌尔都文各出版了五千册。《中国革命与中国共产党》用英文和印度各种主要语文发行，总数已达三万册。毛主席的另一受欢迎的著作《论人民民主专政》已用英文和印度若干种语文出版，共达二万六千册。孟加拉文译本已发行三版，共一万一千册。毛主席《在延安文艺座谈会上的讲话》已用英文和印度若干种语文出版了一万六千册左右。英文译本于一九五○年六月发行第一版，到十二月中旬即销售一空，第二版已于一九五一年二月发行。
    此外，刘少奇同志的名著《论党》已译成孟加拉文、印地文、乌尔都文、马拉提文、马来雅冷文。他的若干论文均经译出刊在印度各种语文的期刊和杂志上。今年六月，他的另一本书《论党内斗争》已用英文出版，其他语文的译本也即将出版。刘少奇同志一九四九年在北京亚洲澳洲工会代表会议上的开幕词，也已被译成印度的各种语文广为传布，并刊载在各种期刊和杂志上。
