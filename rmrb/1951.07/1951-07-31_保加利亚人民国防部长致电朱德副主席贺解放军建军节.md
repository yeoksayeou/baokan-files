Title: Bulgarian People's Defense Minister Sends Telegram to Vice Chairman Zhu De to Congratulate the Anniversary of the Founding of the People's Liberation Army
Author:
Date: 1951-07-31
Page: 1

　　Xinhua News Agency, 29th: Bulgarian People's Defense Minister, Lieutenant General Panchevski, sent a telegram to Vice Chairman Zhu De, congratulating the 24th anniversary of the founding of the Chinese People's Liberation Army. The telegram is as follows:

To General Zhu De, Vice Chairman of the Revolutionary Military Commission of the Central People's Government of the People's Republic of China:

On the occasion of the 24th anniversary of the founding of the heroic Chinese People's Liberation Army, I extend warm combat greetings to you and the brave soldiers of the great Chinese people on behalf of all commanders and fighters of the Bulgarian People's Army. I wish the fraternal Chinese army new achievements on the road of learning from the rich experience of the invincible Soviet army, and in building it into a stronger force to defend peace, democracy and socialism, to defend the inviolability of the People's Republic of China and peaceful socialist construction, and in opposing the conspiracies of the Anglo-American imperialists and their running dogs, the Kuomintang.

Long live the combat unity between the Chinese People's Liberation Army and the Bulgarian People's Army!

Long live the great Chinese people and their glorious leader Mao Zedong!

Long live the greatest marshal of our time, the beloved leader, the teacher of all peace-loving people - Marshal Stalin!

Bulgarian People's Republic People's Defense Minister, Lieutenant General Panchevski
July 28

### 保加利亚人民国防部长致电朱德副主席贺解放军建军节

1951-07-31
第1版()
专栏：

　　保加利亚人民国防部长致电朱德副主席贺解放军建军节
    【新华社二十九日讯】保加利亚人民共和国人民国防部长潘琪夫斯基中将，致电朱德副主席，祝贺中国人民解放军二十四周年。电文如下：中华人民共和国中央人民政府革命军事委员会副主席朱德将军：
    值此英勇的中国人民解放军建军二十四周年之际，谨以保加利亚人民军全体指战员的名义，向您及伟大的中国人民的勇敢的战士们，致以热烈的战斗的敬礼。谨祝兄弟般的中国军队在学习无敌的苏联军队的丰富经验的道路上，以及在将其建设成为一支更强大的队伍以保卫和平、民主及社会主义，保卫中华人民共和国的不可侵犯性及和平的社会主义建设，以及在反对美英帝国主义者及其走狗国民党的阴谋的道路上，获得新的成就。
    中国人民解放军与保加利亚人民军之间的战斗团结万岁！
    伟大的中国人民及其光辉的领袖毛泽东万岁！
    我们的时代中最伟大的元帅，亲爱的领袖，全体爱好和平的人类的导师——斯大林大元帅万岁！
      保加利亚人民共和国人民国防部长  潘琪夫斯基中将
              七月二十八日
