Title: Government Administration Council Holds Meeting
Author:
Date: 1951-07-21
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, 20th—The 94th meeting of the Central People's Government Administration Council was held on the afternoon of the 20th. The meeting, after hearing the report by Sun Qimeng, Deputy Minister of Personnel of the Central People's Government, on the draft of the Provisional Regulations on the Appointment and Removal of State Organ Personnel by the Central People's Government, adopted the "Draft Provisional Regulations on the Appointment and Removal of State Organ Personnel by the Central People's Government." This draft will be submitted to the Central People's Government Committee for approval and promulgation. Subsequently, the meeting, after hearing the explanation by Rong Zihe, Deputy Minister of Finance of the Central People's Government, on the Provisional Regulations on Budget and Final Accounts, adopted the "Draft Provisional Regulations on Budget and Final Accounts." This draft will be submitted to the Chairman of the Central People's Government for approval and promulgation. The meeting also heard a report by Dong Biwu, Vice Premier of the Government Administration Council and Director of the Political and Legal Affairs Committee, on the issue of cultivating political cadres, and approved the "Plan of the Political and Legal Affairs Committee of the Government Administration Council of the Central People's Government on Preparing to Establish a Central Political and Legal Cadre School." In addition, the meeting approved several appointment and removal cases.

### 政务院举行会议

1951-07-21
第1版()
专栏：

　　政务院举行会议
    【新华社二十日讯】中央人民政府政务院第九十四次政务会议于二十日下午举行。会议在听取了中央人民政府人事部副部长孙起孟所作的关于草拟中央人民政府任免国家机关工作人员暂行条例的报告后，通过了《中央人民政府任免国家机关工作人员暂行条例草案》。这一草案将提请中央人民政府委员会批准后公布施行。会议继在听取中央人民政府财政部副部长戎子和所作的关于预算决算暂行条例的说明后，通过了《预算决算暂行条例草案》。这一草案将呈请中央人民政府主席批准后公布施行。会议并听取了政务院副总理兼政治法律委员会主任董必武所作的关于培养政治干部问题的报告，批准了《中央人民政府政务院政治法律委员会关于筹设中央政法干部学校方案》。此外，会议通过了任免案多起。
