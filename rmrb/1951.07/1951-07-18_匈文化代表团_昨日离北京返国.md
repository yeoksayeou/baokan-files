Title: Hungarian Cultural Delegation Departs Beijing for Home Yesterday
Author: 
Date: 1951-07-18
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, July 17th: The head of the Hungarian People's Republic Cultural Delegation, Molnár, and member Mihályi, after signing the Sino-Hungarian Cultural Cooperation Agreement, were accompanied by the Hungarian Ambassador to China, Sándor, to pay a visit to Zhou Enlai, Premier and Minister of Foreign Affairs of the Central People's Government, on the afternoon of July 16th. The meeting was very pleasant. On July 17th, the delegation left Beijing by plane to return home. Those who went to the airport to see them off included Shen Yanbing, Minister of Culture of the Central People's Government, Yan Baohang, Deputy Director of the General Office of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs, Wang Zhuoru, Director of the Reception Department, Hong Shen, Deputy Director of the Foreign Cultural Liaison Bureau of the Ministry of Culture, and Zhao Feng, Counselor of the Cultural and Educational Committee. Also present were the Soviet Ambassador to China, Roshchin, and the Hungarian Ambassador to China, Sándor.

During its stay in China, in addition to conducting Sino-Hungarian cultural negotiations, the delegation had extensive contact with cultural circles in Beijing, and visited schools, cultural and artistic institutions, factories, and suburban villages in Beijing. They also watched various song and dance dramas and film performances, and gave special lectures on the situation of Hungarian farmers and the peace movement. On July 13th, they went to Tianjin to visit light industrial factories and learn about the lives of workers. The delegation received a warm welcome from our government agencies and people from all walks of life everywhere they went.

### 匈文化代表团  昨日离北京返国

1951-07-18
第1版()
专栏：

　　匈文化代表团
    昨日离北京返国
    【新华社十七日讯】匈牙利人民共和国文化代表团团长莫尔那尔及团员米哈依费于签订中匈文化合作协定后，在七月十六日下午由匈牙利驻华大使夏法朗柯陪同拜会了我中央人民政府政务院周恩来总理兼外交部长，畅谈甚欢。七月十七日该代表团乘机离北京返国。前往机场送行者有我中央人民政府文化部部长沈雁冰、外交部办公厅副主任阎宝航、交际处处长王倬如、文化部对外文化联络事务局副局长洪深及文化教育委员会参事赵沨等，此外尚有苏联驻华大使罗申及匈牙利驻华大使夏法朗柯等。
    代表团留华期间除进行中匈文化谈判外曾与北京的文化界有多方接触，并访问了北京的学校、文化艺术机关、工厂、近郊农村，参观了各种歌舞剧及电影的演出，作了有关匈牙利农民情况及和平运动的专题演讲；并于七月十三日前往天津参观轻工业工厂及工人生活情况。代表团在各处均受到我政府机关及各界人民的热烈欢迎。
