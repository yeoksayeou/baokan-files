Our Great Motherland
　　The Jiangyanchi Reservoir is located 6 kilometers southwest of Dihua, Xinjiang Province, and is the second largest reservoir on the Chinese mainland (the other is in the Northeast). After the peaceful liberation of Xinjiang, the People's Government has accelerated this huge water conservancy project in order to expand the area of ​​cultivated land in Xinjiang and increase grain production. This is the Heping Canal flume leading to the reservoir, which can irrigate 100,000 mu of newly reclaimed farmland and increase grain production by 15 million jin per year.

### 我们伟大的祖国（图片）

1951-07-25
第1版()
专栏：

    我们伟大的祖国
  江雁池蓄水库位于新疆省迪化西南六公里，是我国大陆第二个大蓄水库（另一个在东北）。新疆和平解放以后，人民政府为了扩大新疆耕地的面积和增产粮食，加紧进行了这项巨大的水利工程。这是通往水库的和平渠水槽，所输水量能灌溉新垦农田十万亩，每年可增产食粮一千五百万斤。
