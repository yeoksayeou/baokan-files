Title: Celebrating the 30th Anniversary of the Victory of the Mongolian People's Revolution, Mongolian Ambassador Holds Reception in Beijing Yesterday; Chairman Mao and Premier Zhou Send Cables to Bumazhav and Choibalsan Respectively to Offer Congratulations
Author:
Date: 1951-07-12
Page: 1
	
	Celebrating the 30th Anniversary of the Victory of the Mongolian People's Revolution
	Mongolian Ambassador Holds Reception in Beijing Yesterday
	Chairman Mao and Premier Zhou Send Cables to Bumazhav and Choibalsan Respectively to Offer Congratulations

	[Xinhua News Agency, 11th] To celebrate the 30th anniversary of the victory of the Mongolian People's Revolution, the Ambassador of the Mongolian People's Republic to China, Jalkhasaikhan, held a reception in Beijing tonight.

	Those invited to attend were: Vice Chairmen of the Central People's Government Zhu De and Li Jishen, Vice Premier of the Government Administration Council Huang Yanpei, Deputy Ministers of Foreign Affairs Zhang Hanfu and Wu Xiuquan, and Zhang Xiruo, Peng Zemin, Ma Yinchu, Ma Xulun, Shen Yanbing, Xie Juezai, Luo Ruiqing, Zhang Bojun, Li Shucheng, Fu Zuoyi, Shi Liang and other heads of departments of the Central People's Government, heads of various people's organizations, and Vice Mayor of the Beijing Municipal People's Government Wu Han, totaling more than 300 people.

	Foreign diplomatic envoys to China who were invited to attend included: Soviet Ambassador Roshchin, Czechoslovak Ambassador Weiskopf, Korean Ambassador Lee Joo-yeon, Indian Ambassador Panikkar, Head of the Diplomatic Mission of the German Democratic Republic König, Hungarian Ambassador Saffronko, Bulgarian Ambassador Petkov, Chargé d'Affaires ad interim of the Romanian Embassy Şerlad, Chargé d'Affaires ad interim of the Polish Embassy Dowding, Chargé d'Affaires ad interim of the Burmese Embassy U Pyi, First Secretary of the Vietnamese Delegation Zhou Liang, and embassy staff from various countries. The head of the Hungarian Cultural Delegation, Molnar, was also invited to attend. The reception proceeded in a warm and friendly atmosphere.

	On the 7th, Chairman Mao Zedong of the Central People's Government of the People's Republic of China and Premier Zhou Enlai sent telegrams to Bumazhav, Chairman of the Presidium of the Little Khural of the Mongolian People's Republic, and Marshal Choibalsan, Premier of the Mongolian People's Republic, respectively, to offer congratulations.

### 庆祝蒙古人民革命胜利三十周年  蒙古大使昨在京举行招待会  毛主席和周总理分别电布马增及乔巴山祝贺

1951-07-12
第1版()
专栏：

　　庆祝蒙古人民革命胜利三十周年
    蒙古大使昨在京举行招待会
    毛主席和周总理分别电布马增及乔巴山祝贺
    【新华社十一日讯】蒙古人民共和国驻我国大使贾尔卡赛汗为庆祝蒙古人民革命胜利三十周年纪念，于今晚在北京举行招待会。
    应邀出席者有：中央人民政府副主席朱德、李济深，政务院副总理黄炎培，外交部副部长章汉夫、伍修权，及张奚若、彭泽民、马寅初、马叙伦、沈雁冰、谢觉哉、罗瑞卿、章伯钧、李书城、傅作义、史良等中央人民政府各部门首长，各人民团体负责人，以及北京市人民政府副市长吴晗等共三百多人。
    各国驻华外交使节应邀出席者有：苏联大使罗申、捷克斯洛伐克大使魏斯柯普夫、朝鲜大使李周渊、印度大使潘尼迦、德意志民主共和国外交使团团长柯尼希、匈牙利大使夏法朗柯、保加利亚大使彼得科夫、罗马尼亚大使馆临时代办席拉德、波兰大使馆临时代办道定、缅甸大使馆临时代办吴辟、越南代表团一等秘书周亮及各国使馆人员。匈牙利文化代表团团长莫尔那尔亦应邀出席。招待会在热烈友好气氛中进行。
    七日，中华人民共和国中央人民政府毛泽东主席和周恩来总理曾分别致电蒙古人民共和国小呼拉尔主席团主席布马增和蒙古人民共和国总理乔巴山元帅祝贺。
