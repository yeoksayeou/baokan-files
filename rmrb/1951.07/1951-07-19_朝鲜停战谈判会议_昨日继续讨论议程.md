Title: Korean Armistice Talks: Agenda Discussion Continued Yesterday
Author:
Date: 1951-07-19
Page: 1

　　Korean Armistice Talks
　  Agenda Discussion Continued Yesterday
    [Xinhua News Agency, Pyongyang, 18th] This reporter learned from the headquarters on the Korean front that the sixth meeting of the Korean armistice talks began at 9:00 AM (Korean time) on July 18th and ended at 2:30 PM. The agenda issue was further discussed during the meeting. The seventh meeting is scheduled to be held at 10:00 AM on July 19th.

### 朝鲜停战谈判会议  昨日继续讨论议程

1951-07-19
第1版()
专栏：

　　朝鲜停战谈判会议
　  昨日继续讨论议程
    【新华社平壤十八日电】本社记者由朝鲜前线总部获悉：朝鲜停战谈判会议第六次会议在七月十八日上午九时（朝鲜时间）开始，下午二时三十分结束。会议中继续讨论了议程问题。第七次会议预定在七月十九日上午十时举行。
