Title: National Student Fifteenth Congress Opens Today in the Capital
Author:
Date: 1951-07-20
Page: 1

　　National Student Fifteenth Congress
　  Opens Today in the Capital
　　[News from this newspaper] The Fifteenth Congress of the All-China Students' Federation opened in Beijing today (20th). This congress will summarize the experiences of the Chinese student movement over the past two years, determine the current specific tasks of Chinese students, and revise the Constitution of the All-China Students' Federation and re-elect the Executive Committee of the All-China Students' Federation. As of the 19th, a total of 420 representatives had registered, including representatives from major administrative regions, various ethnic groups, and various religious beliefs. The congress also has invited representatives, including students studying abroad, returned overseas Chinese students, students who have recently left school and are serving with excellent results in various construction posts in the motherland's national defense, economy, and culture, and students who are studying at school but have participated in social services and achieved results.

### 全国学生第十五届代表大会  今日在首都开幕

1951-07-20
第1版()
专栏：

　　全国学生第十五届代表大会
　  今日在首都开幕
    【本报讯】中华全国学生第十五届代表大会于今（二十）日在北京开幕。这次大会将总结两年来中国学生运动的经验，确定中国学生当前的具体任务，并将修改中华全国学生联合会章程，改选中华全国学生联合会执行委员会。报到的代表截至十九日止共有四百二十人，其中有各大行政区、各民族、各种宗教信仰等代表，大会并有邀请代表，计有留学生、归国华侨学生、离校不久而正在祖国国防、经济和文化等各种建设岗位上服务成绩优良的学生，和在校学习但曾参加社会服务而有成绩的学生。
