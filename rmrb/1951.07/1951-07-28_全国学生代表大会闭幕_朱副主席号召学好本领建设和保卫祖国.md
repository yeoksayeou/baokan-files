Title: National Student Congress Concludes: Vice Chairman Zhu Calls on Students to Master Skills to Build and Defend the Motherland
Author:
Date: 1951-07-28
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency report: The 15th National Student Congress of the All-China Students' Federation concluded on the 26th after seven days of meetings.

　　On the final day, all delegates passed the resolution of the 15th National Student Congress of the All-China Students' Federation. The resolution pointed out that the main tasks of the current student movement in China are: to popularize and deepen patriotic ideological education among students nationwide, improve students' knowledge level, pay attention to physical exercise, and strengthen the work of student unions at all levels and school student associations. The Congress also revised the Constitution of the All-China Students' Federation and passed the election law of the Executive Committee of the All-China Students' Federation in order to elect members of the 15th Executive Committee of the All-China Students' Federation.

　　During the Congress, Peng Zhen, a member of the Political Bureau of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of China, delivered a political report to the student representatives. Jiang Nanxiang, Deputy Secretary of the Central Committee of the China New Democratic Youth League, delivered a report on "Several Issues in the Current Student Movement." Li Fuchun, Deputy Director of the Financial and Economic Committee of the Central People's Government, delivered a report on "The Direction of New China's Construction." Qian Junrui, Deputy Minister of Education of the Central People's Government, delivered a report on "New China's Education Construction and Patriotic Education." All delegates held enthusiastic group discussions on these reports.

　　The closing ceremony of the Congress was held at 8 p.m. on the 26th. Zhu De, Vice Chairman of the Central People's Government, attended the closing ceremony. Vice Chairman Zhu congratulated the Congress and delivered a speech (the original text is published separately). After the speech, amidst military music and applause, he accepted the silk banner presented by the Congress to Chairman Mao, as well as gifts presented to Chairman Mao and the Central Committee of the Communist Party of China by students of various ethnic minorities from East China, Northwest China, Southwest China, and overseas Chinese students.

　　Amidst warm applause, the Congress passed the congratulatory telegrams to Chairman Mao, Commander-in-Chief Zhu, the Chinese People's Liberation Army, the Chinese People's Volunteers, the International Union of Students, and the World Youth Student Festival. It also passed letters of greetings to students attending military cadre schools, students in Taiwan, and overseas Chinese students.

　　Finally, Guan Ping, student representative of the Renmin University of China and Executive Chairman of the Presidium of the Congress, delivered the closing speech. He pointed out that this Congress was united and successful. In the future, students across the country should continue to unite and advance under the banner of patriotism.

　　During the Congress, it received congratulatory telegrams from the International Union of Students and the Soviet Youth Anti-Fascist Committee, and it received 124 silk banners from various democratic parties, people's organizations, local student federations, and local organizations of the Youth League.

### 全国学生代表大会闭幕  朱副主席号召学好本领建设和保卫祖国

1951-07-28
第1版()
专栏：

　　全国学生代表大会闭幕
　  朱副主席号召学好本领建设和保卫祖国
    【本报讯】据新华社讯：中华全国学生第十五届代表大会经过七天的会议，已于二十六日闭幕。
    大会最后一日，全体代表通过了中华全国学生第十五届代表大会决议。决议指出现在中国学生运动的主要任务是：在全国学生中普及与深入爱国主义的思想教育，提高学生的知识水平，注意锻炼身体，并加强各级学生联合会和各校学生会的工作。大会并修改了中华全国学生联合会章程，通过了中华全国学生联合会执行委员会选举法，以便选举中华全国学生联合会第十五届执行委员会委员。
    大会期间，中国共产党中央政治局委员彭真向学生代表们作了政治报告，中国新民主主义青年团中央委员会副书记蒋南翔向代表们作了“目前学生运动中几个问题”的报告，中央人民政府财政经济委员会副主任李富春向代表们作了《新中国建设的方向》的报告，中央人民政府教育部副部长钱俊瑞向代表们作了《新中国教育建设与爱国主义教育问题》的报告。全体代表对这些报告都分组进行了热烈的讨论。
    大会闭幕式在二十六日晚八时举行。中央人民政府副主席朱德参加了闭幕典礼。朱副主席向大会致贺并致词（原词见另文）。致辞毕，他在军乐与掌声中接受了大会献给毛主席的锦旗以及华东、西北、西南等地各少数民族学生和华侨学生献给毛主席和中国共产党中央委员会的礼物。
    大会在热烈的掌声中通过了向毛主席、朱总司令和中国人民解放军、中国人民志愿军、国际学联、世界青年学生联欢节的致敬电，并通过了给参加军事干部学校的学生、台湾学生和华侨学生的慰问信。
    最后，由中国人民大学学生代表、大会主席团执行主席管平致闭幕词。他指出：这次大会是团结的，成功的。今后全国学生要在爱国主义的旗帜下继续团结前进。
    大会在开会期间，收到了国际学联和苏联青年反法西斯委员会给大会的贺电，并收到了各民主党派、各人民团体、各地学联和青年团各地组织送来的锦旗一百二十四面。
