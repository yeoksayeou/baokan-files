Title: Guatemalan Workers' Revolutionary Party Congratulatory Telegram
Author:
Date: 1951-07-27
Page: 4

To the Central Committee of the Communist Party of China and Chairman Mao Zedong, esteemed comrades:

　　On the occasion of the glorious 30th anniversary celebration of the founding of the Communist Party of China on July 1st, our Central Executive Committee of the Party extends warm congratulations and respects to the Communist Party of China. The Communist Party of China, due to its outstanding achievements and correct leadership, has brought happiness to millions of Chinese people.

　　For this reason, our party has printed the book "Strive More and Better for the Cause of the Chinese Revolution" and will publish and distribute it to all party comrades and sympathizers on this day.

　　Long live the Communist Party of China and its great and wise leaders!

　　General Secretary of the Central Executive Committee of the Guatemalan Workers' Revolutionary Party: Gutiérrez
　　Guatemala, July 1, 1951

### 危地马拉工人革命党贺电

1951-07-27
第4版()
专栏：

　　危地马拉工人革命党贺电中国共产党中央委员会毛泽东主席敬爱的同志：
    值此光荣的中国共产党成立三十周年七一庆祝纪念之际，我们的党中央执行委员会，谨向中国共产党致以热烈的庆贺敬礼。中国共产党，由于它的卓越的成就及其正确的领导，使千万的中国人民获得了幸福。
    为此，我们的党，曾刊印了《更多更好地为中国革命事业而努力》一书，并在这一天出版散发全党同志及党的同情者。
    中国共产党及其伟大而英明的领袖们万岁！
          危地马拉工人革命党中央执行委员会总书记：古蒂雷斯
        危地马拉，一九五一年七月一日
