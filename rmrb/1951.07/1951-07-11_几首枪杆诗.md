Title: Several Gun Poems
Author: Sun Zhishen, Liu Yurong, Cao Shuli
Date: 1951-07-11
Page: 4

Column: On the Korean Front

　　Several Gun Poems
    Editor's Note: The following poems are original creations by comrades of the Chinese People's Volunteer Army and are widely loved by the soldiers.
           I. Rocket Launcher          Sun Zhishen
    Rocket launcher, what a hoot,
    Its origin is American-made.
    Transportation Captain Chiang Kai-shek,
    Brought it to us without a receipt.
    This time to resist America and aid Korea,
    American soldiers will suffer from American cannons.
           II. Hand Grenade          Liu Yurong
    Hand grenade, truly strong,
    Resisting America and aiding Korea to protect our national defense,
    A loud bang when it sees the American army,
    Settles the dog's food bill.
           III. Steel Helmet  Sun Zhishen
    Little steel helmet, two jin heavy,
    Volunteer soldiers wear it to kill enemies,
    Bayonet pointed, shouting words,
    Capture a large group of American soldiers.
           IV. Carrying Pole  Cao Shuli
    Small carrying pole, seven chi long,
    Carries oil drums to the front,
    Comrades eat and become strong,
    Kill more American soldiers and seize more guns.

### 几首枪杆诗
孙志深　刘玉容　曹树理
1951-07-11
第4版()
专栏：在朝鲜前线

　　几首枪杆诗
    编者按：下面几首诗是中国人民志愿军同志自己的创作，为战士们普遍喜爱。
           一、火箭炮          孙志深
    火箭炮，呱呱叫，
    它的来源美国造。
    运输队长蒋介石，
    给咱送来不要收条。
    这回抗美来援朝，
    美国兵要挨美国炮。
           二、手榴弹          刘玉容
    手榴弹，真是强，
    抗美援朝保国防，
    见了美军一声响，
    算了狗日的伙食账。
           三、钢盔  孙志深
    小钢盔，二斤沉，
    志愿军戴上杀敌人，
    刺刀一端把话喊，
    俘虏美军一大群。
           四、扁担  曹树理
    小扁担，七尺长，
    挑起油桶上前方，
    同志们吃得身体壮，
    多杀美军多缴枪。
