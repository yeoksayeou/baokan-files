Title: Korean Armistice Negotiation Conference Continues Discussion on Military Demarcation Line Issue
Author:
Date: 1951-07-29
Page: 1
　　
　　Korean Armistice Negotiation Conference
　　Continues Discussion on Military Demarcation Line Issue
    [Xinhua News Agency, Pyongyang, July 28] This reporter learned from the headquarters at the Korean front: The twelfth meeting of the Korean armistice negotiation conference began at 9:00 AM (Korean time) on July 28 and ended at 2:45 PM. The meeting continued to discuss the second item on the agenda, namely, the establishment of a military demarcation line to create a demilitarized zone. The thirteenth meeting is scheduled to be held at 10:00 AM on July 29.

### 朝鲜停战谈判会议  续讨论军事分界线问题

1951-07-29
第1版()
专栏：

　　朝鲜停战谈判会议
　  续讨论军事分界线问题
    【新华社平壤二十八日电】本社记者自朝鲜前线总部获悉：朝鲜停战谈判会议第十二次会议在七月二十八日上午九时（朝鲜时间）开始，下午二时四十五分结束，会议中继续地讨论了议程中的第二项议题，即确立双方军事分界线以建立非军事地区的问题。第十三次会议定于七月二十九日上午十时举行。
