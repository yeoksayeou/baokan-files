    Our Great Motherland
    Lychees are one of the specialties of Guangdong. This is a scene from Pazhou Township, Xinzhou District, Guangzhou, after the land reform, where farmers received many lychee trees and harvested the fruits of their labor for the first time. The male and female farmers here are sorting the picked lychees.

### 我们伟大的祖国（图片）

1951-07-29
第1版()
专栏：

    我们伟大的祖国
    荔枝是广东的特产之一。这是广州新洲区琶洲乡经过土地改革后的农民，他们分得了许多荔枝树，第一次收到自己劳动的果实。这里的男女农民正在把摘下来的荔枝加以挑选。
