Title: Pakistan Appoints Ambassador to Our Country
Author:
Date: 1951-07-20
Page: 1

　　Pakistan Appoints Ambassador to Our Country

    [Xinhua News Agency, 19th] Following the successful conclusion of negotiations between our country and Pakistan on the establishment of diplomatic relations, and after our government appointed Han Nianlong as our ambassador to Pakistan, the Pakistani government, with the consent of our Central People's Government, has appointed General Nawabzada Agha Muhammad Raza as the Pakistani ambassador to our country.

### 巴基斯坦任命驻我国大使

1951-07-20
第1版()
专栏：

　　巴基斯坦任命驻我国大使
    【新华社十九日讯】继我国与巴基斯坦建立外交关系谈判顺利结束，我政府任命韩念龙为我国驻巴基斯坦大使之后，巴基斯坦政府已征得我中央人民政府同意，任命那瓦查达·阿格哈·穆罕默德·罗查将军为巴基斯坦驻我国大使。
