Title: Citations in Translated Books Should Indicate the Chinese Edition
Author: Jiang Yun
Date: 1951-07-27
Page: 2

Column: Reader's Letter

    Citations in Translated Books Should Indicate the Chinese Edition

    To the Editor:
    In our translated works, there are often citations of Marx, Engels, Lenin, and Stalin's original works. The citations always directly translate the reference from the translated text. For example, when quoting Lenin's "Left-Wing" Communism: an Infantile Disorder, the citation is: "Lenin's Collected Works, Third Edition, Volume 25, Page ×××," instead of "Selected Works of Lenin, Two-Volume Chinese Edition, Volume 2, Page ×××," or other common Chinese edition page numbers. This makes it impossible or very difficult for readers to consult the cited works to understand the context of the cited words. Therefore, I suggest that when translators translate foreign books, whenever the cited words already have a relatively reliable Chinese edition, it is best to use the page numbers of the Chinese edition for citation. The Chinese edition of The History of the Communist Party of the Soviet Union (Bolsheviks) Short Course adopts a double annotation method, using both the direct translation source and brief notes at the end of the book to indicate the page numbers of the Chinese edition. I think this is worth adopting by other translators.
    Such a small problem can save readers some time and increase the convenience of readers checking the original works with a little effort in translation.
              Jiang Yun

    Editor's Note: We have forwarded Comrade Jiang Yun's letter to the People's Publishing House for reference. The People's Publishing House has replied agreeing and decided to implement it on a trial basis in the publications of the publishing house. In order to attract the attention of general translators and publishers, it is hereby published again.

### 翻译书籍的引用文字应注明中译本出处
江云
1951-07-27
第2版()
专栏：读者来信

    翻译书籍的引用文字应注明中译本出处编辑同志：
    在我们所翻译过来的著作中，常有引用一些马克思、恩格斯、列宁、斯大林原著的话，在文中注明出处也总是直接从译文中翻过来。如引用列宁的《共产主义运动中“左派”幼稚病》的原文，注上：“《列宁全集》第三版，第二五卷，第×××页”，而不是注上：“《列宁文选》两卷集中文版第二卷，第×××页”，或其他常见的中文版页数。这就使读者不能或很困难去翻阅所引用的著作，以便了解所引用的话的前因后果。因此，建议翻译工作者在翻译外文书时，凡书中所引用的话，已经有比较可靠的中文版的，最好改用中文版的页数来注明。《联共（布）党史简明教程》中文版采用双重注明的办法，既用直译的出处，又于书末用简注来标明中文版的页数。我认为这是值得其他翻译工作者采用的。
    这样的小问题，只要在翻译时稍费功夫，就可省去读者一些时间，增加读者查原著的方便。
              江云
    编者按：江云同志来信所提建议，我们曾先转请人民出版社参考。人民出版社并复信表示同意，决定在该社出版物中试行。为了引起一般翻译工作者及出版家的注意，特再予发表。
