Title: The People of the Whole Country Send Telegrams to Congratulate the Party on its Thirtieth Anniversary
Author:
Date: 1951-07-14
Page: 1

　　On the occasion of the 30th anniversary of the founding of the Party, people from all over the country, including individuals and organizations, have sent telegrams or letters to the Central Committee of the Communist Party of China and Chairman Mao to express their warm congratulations. The list of these organizations and individuals, in addition to those already published, is published successively as follows:
