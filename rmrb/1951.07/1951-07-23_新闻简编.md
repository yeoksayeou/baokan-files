　　News Briefs
  The Revolutionary Committee of the Kuomintang Establishes Organizations in the Northeast
    The Northeast Provisional Working Committee of the Revolutionary Committee of the Chinese Kuomintang held its first standing committee meeting in Shenyang on the 10th of this month. Ning Wu, the chairman of the committee, reported on the history and current tasks of the RCCK at the meeting. The meeting also discussed the development of organizations and the implementation of propaganda and education in the Northeast region.
    The RCCK Northeast Provisional Working Committee was formally established in Shenyang on the 18th of last month, consisting of five members. Ning Wu is the chairman, and Liu Qixin, Li Guisen, Bai Xiqing, and Huang Fanggang are members.
  Enrollment Work Completed at Military Cadre Schools in Various Parts of the Northeast
    The enrollment work at military cadre schools in various parts of the Northeast has basically been completed. According to preliminary statistics from the five provinces of Songjiang, Heilongjiang, Jilin, Liaodong, and Liaoxi, and Shenyang City: the number of applicants reached more than 22,000. In Shenyang City, 7,798 students from twenty middle schools successively applied, exceeding the city's planned enrollment quota by more than fifty times.
  All Circles in Tianjin Welcome the Korean People's Delegation
    The Korean People's Delegation and Art Troupe arrived in Tianjin from Nanjing on the morning of the 20th. More than a thousand representatives from all circles in Tianjin went to the station to welcome them. A grand welcome party was held by representatives from all walks of life that evening.

　　新闻简编
  民革在东北建立组织
    中国国民党革命委员会东北临时工作委员会，于本月十日在沈阳举行首次常务委员会。该会主任委员宁武在会上报告了民革的历史及目前的工作任务，会议并讨论了民革在东北地区发展组织和进行宣传教育等问题。
    民革东北临时工作委员会是上月十八日在沈阳正式成立的，由五人组成，宁武为主任委员，刘启新、李桂森、白希清、黄方刚等为委员。
  东北各地军干校招生工作结束
    东北各地军事干部学校招生工作已基本结束。据松江、黑龙江、吉林、辽东、辽西五省及沈阳市的初步统计：报名人数约达两万两千余名。沈阳市二十个中学的学生有七千七百九十八人先后报了名，超过全市预定招生名额的五十倍。
  天津各界欢迎朝人民代表团
    朝鲜人民访华代表团及艺术团二十日上午由南京抵天津。天津市各界代表千余人到车站欢迎。当晚各界代表又举行了盛大的欢迎会。
