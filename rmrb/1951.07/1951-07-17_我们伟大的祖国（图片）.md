Author:
Date: 1951-07-17
Page: 1

    Our Great Motherland
    In the patriotic labor competition to resist America and aid Korea, the Wang Yonglu riveting team of the Taiyuan Machine Factory in Shanxi Province won the red flag for three consecutive months and became an advanced production team in the style of Ma Hengchang. This is the Wang Yonglu production team carefully inspecting every rivet on the machine.

### 我们伟大的祖国（图片）

1951-07-17
第1版()
专栏：

    我们伟大的祖国
    山西太原机器厂的王永禄铆工组，在抗美援朝爱国主义劳动竞赛中，连得三个月的红旗，成为马恒昌式的先进生产小组。这是王永禄生产小组正在细心地检查机器上的每一个铆钉。
