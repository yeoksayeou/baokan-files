Title: People of All Nationalities in Xinjiang and Garrison Troops Launch Activities to Welcome Army Day
Author:
Date: 1951-07-22
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, Dihua, 20th—People of all nationalities and all walks of life in Xinjiang Province are actively preparing to celebrate the Chinese People's Liberation Army's Army Day. Representatives from all nationalities and all sectors of society in Xinjiang Province and Dihua City formed a preparatory committee for celebrating "August 1st" Army Day on the 11th. The Xinjiang Branch of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of China and the Party Committee of the Xinjiang Military Region of the Chinese Communist Party jointly issued instructions for celebrating "August 1st" Army Day. The instructions stated: "Celebrating Army Day must be linked with the study and propaganda of the glorious 30-year history of the Chinese Communist Party, to publicize the heroic fighting history of the Chinese People's Liberation Army; to publicize that the Chinese People's Liberation Army is invincible because of the correct leadership of the Chinese Communist Party and Chairman Mao, and because of the long-term education of Chairman Mao's brilliant military thought." The instructions also stipulated that a military parade would be held in Dihua on August 1st, and all units would hold joint military-civilian celebration meetings or military personnel meetings in their garrisons.

　　In order to welcome Army Day, all units of the People's Liberation Army stationed in Xinjiang that are carrying out production and construction tasks, in addition to further launching patriotic production increase and merit-seeking campaigns in agricultural production, are also preparing to step up the construction of modern factories such as automobile repair factories and actively excavate open-pit coal mines.

　　In order to thank the People's Liberation Army for thoroughly suppressing bandits over the past year and for their enthusiastic assistance in all aspects of politics, economy, and health, the people of all nationalities in Xinjiang have widely launched a campaign to write letters of appreciation. People's governments at all levels will also inspect preferential treatment work. From central cities such as Dihua, Kashgar, and Yili to vast rural and pastoral areas, people of all nationalities are enthusiastically launching donation competitions, striving to complete the donation plan of 27 fighter planes and 6 cannons (one newly added in Tacheng) ahead of schedule to welcome Army Day.

### 新疆各族人民和驻军展开迎接建军节活动

1951-07-22
第1版()
专栏：

　　新疆各族人民和驻军展开迎接建军节活动
    【新华社迪化二十日电】新疆省各族、各界人民积极准备迎接中国人民解放军建军节。新疆省暨迪化市各族各界代表，已于十一日组成庆祝“八一”建军节的筹备会。中共中央新疆分局及中共新疆军区党委会联名发出庆祝“八一”建军节的指示。指示说：“庆祝建军节，必须联系着对中国共产党光荣斗争三十年历史的学习和宣传工作，来宣传中国人民解放军的英勇斗争历史；宣传中国人民解放军之所以战无不胜，攻无不克，是由于中国共产党和毛主席的正确领导，由于毛主席光辉的军事思想的长期教养。”指示并规定八月一日在迪化举行阅兵，各部队在驻地举行军民庆祝大会或军人大会。
    为迎接建军节，驻新疆人民解放军执行生产建设任务的各部队，除在农业生产上进一步展开爱国增产立功运动外，并准备加紧修建汽车修理厂等现代化工厂的建筑工程和积极挖掘露天煤矿。
    新疆各族人民为感谢人民解放军一年多来彻底剿灭土匪，和在政治、经济、卫生等各方面对他们的热情援助，现已广泛展开了写慰问信的运动。各级人民政府并将分别检查优抚工作。从迪化、喀什、伊犁等中心城市到广大的农村和牧区，各族人民正热烈展开捐献竞赛，争取提早完成二十七架战斗机、六门大炮（新增塔城一门）的捐献计划，来迎接建军节。
