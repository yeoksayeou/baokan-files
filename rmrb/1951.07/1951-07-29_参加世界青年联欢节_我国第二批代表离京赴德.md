Title: Our Country's Second Group of Representatives Leaves Beijing for Germany to Participate in the World Youth Festival
Author:
Date: 1951-07-29
Page: 4

　　To Participate in the World Youth Festival
　　Our Country's Second Group of Representatives Leaves Beijing for Germany
    [Xinhua News Agency, July 27th] The second group of representatives and staff members of the Chinese Youth Delegation to the Third World Youth and Student Peace Festival to be held in Berlin, Germany in early August this year, 14 people in total, led by Feng Wenbin, head of the delegation, and Wu Han, deputy head of the delegation, left Beijing for Germany on the 27th. More than 200 people, including heads of units and representatives from the All-China Federation of Democratic Youth, the Central Committee of The China New Democratic Youth League, the All-China Students Federation, the Ministry of Foreign Affairs of the Central People's Government, the General Political Department of the People's Revolutionary Military Commission, the Beijing Municipal People's Government, and the Beijing Municipal Committee of the Youth League, went to see them off. In addition, personnel from the diplomatic mission of the German Democratic Republic in China were also present to see them off. The first group of the delegation, led by Deputy Head Rong Gaotang, had already set off on the 16th.

### 参加世界青年联欢节  我国第二批代表离京赴德

1951-07-29
第4版()
专栏：

　　参加世界青年联欢节
　  我国第二批代表离京赴德
    【新华社二十七日讯】出席今年八月初在德国柏林举行的第三届世界青年学生和平联欢节的中国青年代表团第二批代表和工作人员十四人，在代表团团长冯文彬、副团长吴晗率领下，于二十七日离京赴德。前往欢送的有中华全国民主青年联合总会、中国新民主主义青年团中央委员会、中华全国学生联合会、中央人民政府外交部、人民革命军事委员会总政治部、北京市人民政府、青年团北京市委会等单位负责人和代表共二百余人。此外，德意志民主共和国驻华外交使团人员亦到场欢送。该代表团第一批在副团长荣高棠率领下，已于十六日启程。
