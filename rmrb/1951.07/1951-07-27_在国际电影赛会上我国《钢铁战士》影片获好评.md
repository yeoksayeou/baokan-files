Title: Our Film "Steel Fighter" Receives Acclaim at International Film Festival
Author:
Date: 1951-07-27
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency, Prague, 26th—The Chinese feature film "Steel Fighter," screened on the evening of the 19th at the Karlovy Vary International Film Festival in Czechoslovakia, was unanimously praised by film representatives from eighteen countries. They believed that this film, compared to the Chinese film that participated in the competition last year, fully demonstrates the progress of China's film industry over the past year. Semenov, head of the Soviet delegation, said: "This film powerfully and realistically portrays the noble qualities of Chinese revolutionary fighters. With such soldiers full of patriotism, this army will always win battles. This work deepens the Soviet people's understanding of the Chinese people." The Bulgarian representative said: "The progress of your film industry over the past year has become a brilliant model for us." The famous French progressive director Daquin said: "This film strengthens our confidence in the victory of the struggle." Our other two films participating in the competition, "The White Haired Girl" and "New Sons and Daughters of China," will be screened successively.

### 在国际电影赛会上我国《钢铁战士》影片获好评

1951-07-27
第4版()
专栏：

　　在国际电影赛会上我国《钢铁战士》影片获好评
    【新华社布拉格二十六日电】十九日晚在捷克的卡罗维发利城国际电影赛会上放映的中国故事片《钢铁战士》受到十八个国家电影界代表的一致赞扬，认为这部影片和去年参加比赛的中国影片相比，充分说明了一年来中国电影事业的进展。苏联代表团团长塞米诺夫说：“这部电影有力而真实地表现了中国革命战士的崇高品质，有了这样的充满爱国主义精神的战士，这支军队将永远打胜仗。这部作品使苏联人民对中国人民的认识更深刻了。”保加利亚代表说：“你们电影事业一年来的进步，已成为我们光辉的模范。”法国著名的进步导演达根说：“这部影片加强了我们对斗争胜利的信心。”我国参加竞赛的另外两部影片《白毛女》和《新儿女英雄传》将相继放映。
