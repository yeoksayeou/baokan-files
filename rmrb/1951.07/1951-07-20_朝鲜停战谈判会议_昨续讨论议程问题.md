Title: Korean Armistice Negotiation Meeting Continued Discussion on Agenda Issues Yesterday
Author:
Date: 1951-07-20
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Pyongyang, July 19th: The seventh meeting of the Korean armistice negotiations was held on time at 10:00 AM (Korean time) on July 19th and ended at 12:40 PM. The agenda issue was further discussed during the meeting. The eighth meeting will be held at 10:00 AM on July 20th.

### 朝鲜停战谈判会议  昨续讨论议程问题

1951-07-20
第1版()
专栏：

　　朝鲜停战谈判会议
    昨续讨论议程问题
    【新华社平壤十九日电】朝鲜停战谈判会议第七次会议，在七月十九日上午十时（朝鲜时间）按时举行，中午十二时四十分结束。会议中继续讨论了议程问题。第八次会议将在七月二十日上午十时举行。
