Title: Bumajin Sends Telegram to Chairman Mao to Thank Him for Congratulations on the Mongolian People's Republic, Choibalsan Sends Telegram to Premier Zhou

Author:
Date: 1951-07-15
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, July 14th:

　　Bumajin Sends Telegram to Chairman Mao to Thank Him for Congratulations on the Mongolian People's Republic
    Choibalsan Sends Telegram to Premier Zhou
    [Xinhua News Agency, 14th] G. Bumajin, Chairman of the Presidium of the Little Khural of the Mongolian People's Republic, sent a telegram to Chairman Mao Zedong to thank him for his congratulations on the 30th anniversary of the Mongolian People's Republic. The telegram reads as follows:
    Beijing, Comrade Chairman Mao Zedong, Chairman of the Central People's Government of the People's Republic of China, I sincerely thank you for your warm congratulations and wishes on the 30th anniversary of the Mongolian People's Republic, and I sincerely hope that the great Chinese people will achieve new and greater success in the country's economic and cultural construction and in the struggle to defend common peace.
      G. Bumajin
      Chairman of the Presidium of the Little Khural of the Mongolian People's Republic
                  July 14, 1951, Ulaanbaatar
    [Xinhua News Agency, 14th] Marshal H. Choibalsan, Chairman of the Council of Ministers of the Mongolian People's Republic, sent a telegram to Premier Zhou Enlai to thank him for his congratulations on the 30th anniversary of the Mongolian People's Republic. The telegram reads as follows:
    Beijing, Comrade Zhou Enlai, Premier of the State Council of the Central People's Government of the People's Republic of China:
    On the occasion of the 30th anniversary of the Mongolian People's Republic, please accept my heartfelt thanks for your sincere congratulations to the Mongolian people, Comrade Premier.
    On behalf of the entire Mongolian people, I express my sincere friendship to the great neighbor—the heroic Chinese people—and hope that the government and people of the People's Republic of China will achieve further great success in building their free and independent country and in the struggle to defend peace.
            Marshal H. Choibalsan
            Chairman of the Council of Ministers of the Mongolian People's Republic
            July 13, 1951

### 感谢对蒙古人民共和国的祝贺  布马增致电毛主席  乔巴山致电周总理

1951-07-15
第1版()
专栏：

　　感谢对蒙古人民共和国的祝贺
    布马增致电毛主席
    乔巴山致电周总理
    【新华社十四日讯】蒙古人民共和国小呼拉尔主席团主席格·布马增电谢毛泽东主席对蒙古人民共和国三十周年纪念的祝贺。电文如下：北京，中华人民共和国中央人民政府毛泽东主席主席同志，真挚地感谢你对于蒙古人民共和国三十周年纪念的热诚祝贺与愿望，并衷心地希望伟大中国人民在国家的经济和文化建设上以及在保卫共同和平的斗争上获得新的更大的成就。
      蒙古人民共和国小呼拉尔主席团主席
                  格·布马增
    一九五一年七月十四日乌兰巴托尔
    【新华社十四日讯】蒙古人民共和国部长会议主席郝·乔巴山元帅电谢周恩来总理对蒙古人民共和国三十周年的祝贺。电文如下：北京，中华人民共和国中央人民政府政务院总理周恩来同志：
    在蒙古人民共和国三十周年之际，承蒙对蒙古人民致以真实的祝贺，总理同志，请接受我对您的衷心的感谢。
    我代表蒙古全体人民向伟大的邻邦——英勇的中国人民——表示真实的友谊之情，并希望中华人民共和国政府和人民在建设其自由独立的国家及在保卫和平的斗争中获得进一步的巨大成就。
            蒙古人民共和国部长会议主席
              郝·乔巴山元帅
                一九五一年七月十三日
