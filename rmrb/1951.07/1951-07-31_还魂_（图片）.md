Author: Hua Junwu

　　“Resurrection”
    Hua Junwu's work—The American conspiracy to revive the Japanese fleet. The Japanese government, under orders, is reorganizing the Maritime Safety Agency along the lines of the former Japanese Ministry of the Navy and is preparing to recruit another 25,000 former officers and sailors who served in the Japanese fleet for the Maritime Safety Agency.

1951-07-31

专栏：

　　“还魂”
