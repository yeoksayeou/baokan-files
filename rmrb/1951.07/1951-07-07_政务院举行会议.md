Title: Government Administration Council Holds Meeting
Author:
Date: 1951-07-07
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, 6th. The 92nd Government Administration Council meeting of the Central People's Government was held on the afternoon of the 6th. The meeting discussed the "Provisional Regulations on Disciplining Public Servants Who Violate the Law or Neglect Their Duties" and approved several personnel appointments and removals.

### 政务院举行会议

1951-07-07
第1版()
专栏：

　　政务院举行会议
    讨论惩戒违法失职公务人员暂行条例
    【新华社六日讯】中央人民政府政务院第九十二次政务会议于六日下午举行。会议讨论了惩戒违法失职公务人员暂行条例，并通过了任免案多起。
