Date: 1951-07-02

Our Great Motherland
Workers at the Changxindian Locomotive Factory, who have a revolutionary tradition, used their spare time on the 30th anniversary of the founding of the Communist Party of China to make a locomotive tender and present it to Chairman Mao as a gift to celebrate "July 1st." Here, the workers are pasting slogans on the finished locomotive tender and are preparing to drag it out of the factory.

### 我们伟大的祖国（图片）

1951-07-02
第1版()
专栏：

    我们伟大的祖国
    具有革命传统的长辛店机车厂工人，在中国共产党成立三十周年的纪念日，利用工余时间制出机车水柜一台献给毛主席，作为庆祝“七一”的献礼。在这里，工人们在做好的机车水柜上贴了标语，正在准备拖出车厂去。
