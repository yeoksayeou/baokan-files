Title: Government Administration Council Held Meeting Yesterday
Author:
Date: 1951-07-28
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, 27th—The 95th meeting of the Government Administration Council of the Central People's Government was held yesterday afternoon. The meeting listened to and approved the report by Li Shucheng, Minister of Agriculture of the Central People's Government, on agricultural production work in the first half of 1951. After listening to and approving the proposal by Liang Xi, Minister of Forestry and Land Reclamation of the Central People's Government, on resolving the difficulties in timber supply, the meeting passed an instruction on conserving timber. In addition, the meeting approved several personnel appointments and removals, and submitted several personnel appointments and removals to the Central People's Government Committee for approval.

### 政务院昨举行会议

1951-07-28
第1版()
专栏：

　　政务院昨举行会议
    【新华社二十七日讯】中央人民政府政务院第九十五次政务会议于二十七日下午举行。会议听取和批准了中央人民政府农业部李书城部长所作的关于一九五一年上半年农业生产工作的报告，会议并在听取和批准了中央人民政府林垦部梁希部长关于解决木材供应困难问题的建议后，通过了关于节约木材的指示。此外，会议通过任免案及提请中央人民政府委员会批准任免案多起。
