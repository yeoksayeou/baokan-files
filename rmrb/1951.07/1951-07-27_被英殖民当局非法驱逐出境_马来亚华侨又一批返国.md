Title: Overseas Chinese from Malaya Illegally Deported by British Colonial Authorities Return to the Country in Another Batch
Author:
Date: 1951-07-27
Page: 1

Overseas Chinese from Malaya Illegally Deported by British Colonial Authorities Return to the Country in Another Batch

[Report from this newspaper] Xinhua News Agency dispatch: The ninth group of 750 overseas Chinese illegally deported by the British colonial authorities in Malaya returned to Guangzhou on July 22nd. Eighty percent of these deported overseas Chinese are women and children. These deported overseas Chinese have all been subjected to insults and abuse by British imperialism and have been imprisoned in concentration camps for long periods. After arriving in Guangzhou, they received warm condolences and care from overseas Chinese affairs agencies and people from all walks of life.

### 被英殖民当局非法驱逐出境  马来亚华侨又一批返国

1951-07-27
第1版()
专栏：

　　被英殖民当局非法驱逐出境
　  马来亚华侨又一批返国
    【本报讯】据新华社讯：被马来亚英国殖民当局非法驱逐出境的第九批华侨七百五十人，于七月二十二日回到广州。在这批被逐华侨中，百分之八十是妇女和儿童。这批被逐华侨都曾受到英帝国主义的侮辱与虐待，并曾被长期囚禁在集中营里。他们到广州后受到侨务机关和各界人民的热情慰问和照顾。
