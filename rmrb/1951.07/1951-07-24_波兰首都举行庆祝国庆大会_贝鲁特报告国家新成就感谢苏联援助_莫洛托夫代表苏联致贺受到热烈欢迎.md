Title: Polish Capital Holds National Day Celebration; Beirut Reports New National Achievements, Thanks Soviet Aid; Molotov Represents Soviet Union in Offering Congratulations, Receives Warm Welcome
Author: 
Date: 1951-07-24
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency, July 23rd dispatch from Warsaw: On July 21st, the people of Warsaw, the Polish capital, held a National Day celebration at the Polish Theater. Participants included leaders of various political parties, representatives of the working people and the army, prominent social figures, cultural and artistic workers, and members of foreign diplomatic missions. Polish President Beirut and other government leaders, leaders of the United Workers' Party, the Soviet government delegation headed by Molotov, delegations from various Communist and Workers' Parties, and delegations from various People's Democratic countries were warmly welcomed by the audience upon entering the venue.

The celebration was chaired by Prime Minister Cyrankiewicz. Beirut delivered a report, emphasizing the decisive role of the Soviet Union in the liberation and revitalization of Poland. He stated: Poland has gained strength from the example and assistance of the Soviet Union and has now victoriously embarked on the path of socialist construction. The Polish people have achieved great victories in developing industry and agriculture, opening up limitless prospects for further prosperity in Poland. Poland has become a strong and consolidated nation. Beirut then pointed out that the Soviet-Polish Treaty of Friendship, Mutual Assistance, and Post-War Cooperation, signed by the great Stalin, consolidated the unbreakable and eternal friendship between the peoples of the two countries, which is of great importance for Poland's development. The Polish people regard the Soviet Union as a bulwark of peace and progress for the entire world. The Polish people are willing to do everything in their power to fight for peace under the leadership of the great Stalin. Beirut concluded by shouting "Long live the great Stalin, the best friend of the Polish people!" The audience cheered endlessly.

Molotov then delivered a speech. The hall repeatedly resounded with cheers and applause paying tribute to the great Stalin, interrupting Molotov's speech many times. Soviet delegation member and Soviet Marshal Zhukov, as well as representatives of the governments of Germany, Czechoslovakia, Romania, Hungary, Albania, and Mongolia, also delivered speeches at the meeting.

Xinhua News Agency, July 23rd dispatch from Warsaw: The Soviet government delegation, headed by Soviet Deputy Chairman of the Council of Ministers Molotov, attended a reception given by Polish Chairman of the Council of Ministers Cyrankiewicz on July 22nd to celebrate Polish National Day. Also present at the reception were Polish President Beirut and other government leaders, leaders of the United Workers' Party, delegations from various People's Democratic countries attending the celebrations, and members of foreign diplomatic missions in Warsaw.

On the afternoon of the 22nd, Molotov visited the progress of housing construction in Warsaw and visited the radio equipment factory and the automobile factory under construction. He was accompanied by Polish Deputy Chairman of the Council of Ministers Minc and Warsaw's chief architect Sigalin.

On the same day, the Soviet government delegation laid a wreath at the monument to the Soviet soldiers who sacrificed their lives in the battle to liberate Poland. (Picture attached)

### 波兰首都举行庆祝国庆大会  贝鲁特报告国家新成就感谢苏联援助  莫洛托夫代表苏联致贺受到热烈欢迎

1951-07-24
第4版()
专栏：

　　波兰首都举行庆祝国庆大会
　  贝鲁特报告国家新成就感谢苏联援助
  　莫洛托夫代表苏联致贺受到热烈欢迎
    【新华社二十三日讯】据塔斯社华沙讯：波兰首都华沙的人民于二十一日在波兰剧院举行国庆日庆祝大会，参加大会的有各政党的领袖，劳动人民和军队的代表，著名的社会人士，文化和艺术工作者以及各国外交使团。波兰总统贝鲁特和其他政府领袖、统一工人党的领袖，以莫洛托夫为首的苏联政府代表团、各国共产党与工人党的代表团及各人民民主国家的代表团进入会场时，受到会众的热烈欢迎。
    庆祝大会由西伦凯维兹总理担任主席，贝鲁特在会上作了报告，指出苏联在波兰的解放和复兴中起了决定性的作用。他说：波兰从苏联的榜样和援助中获得了力量，现在已胜利地走上了社会主义建设的道路。波兰人民在发展工业和农业方面已赢得了巨大的胜利，为波兰展开了进一步繁荣的无限前途。波兰已经成为一个强大巩固的国家。贝鲁特接着指出：伟大的斯大林签字的波苏友好互助条约巩固了波苏两国人民之间牢不可破的永恒友谊，这种友谊对于波兰的发展有很大的重要性。波兰人民把苏联看做全世界的和平进步堡垒。波兰人民愿意在伟大的斯大林领导下尽一切力量为争取和平而斗争。贝鲁特最后高呼波兰人民的最好的朋友、伟大的斯大林万岁，会众欢呼不止。
    莫洛托夫接着发表了演说，会场上一再响起向伟大的斯大林致敬的欢呼声和掌声，把莫洛托夫的演说打断了许多次。苏联代表团团员、苏联元帅朱可夫及德、捷、罗、匈、阿尔巴尼亚和蒙古政府代表团代表都在会上发表了演说。
    【新华社二十三日讯】据塔斯社华沙讯：以苏联部长会议副主席莫洛托夫为首的苏联政府代表团，在二十二日出席了波兰部长会议主席西伦凯维兹为庆祝波兰国庆日举行的招待会。出席招待会的并有：波兰共和国总统贝鲁特和政府其他首长，统一工人党领袖，以及前来参加庆典的各人民民主国家代表团和各国驻华沙外交使团人员。
    二十二日下午，莫洛托夫参观了华沙房屋建筑工作的进展情形，并参观了无线电器材厂和正在建立中的汽车厂。陪同他去参观的是波兰部长会议副主席明兹和华沙首席建筑师西格林。
    苏联政府代表团曾于同日向在解放波兰战役中牺牲的苏军将士纪念碑献花圈。（附图片）
