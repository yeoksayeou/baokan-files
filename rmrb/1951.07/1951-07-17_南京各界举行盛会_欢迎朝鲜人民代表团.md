Title: Nanjing Circles Hold Grand Meeting to Welcome Korean People's Delegation

Author:
Date: 1951-07-17
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Nanjing, 16th. A grand meeting was held in Nanjing on the evening of the 15th by 3,500 representatives from all walks of life in Nanjing to warmly welcome the Korean People's Delegation to China upon their arrival in Nanjing. The welcome meeting began with the orchestra playing the national anthems of China and Korea. Jin Shanbao, Chairman of the Nanjing Branch of the Resist America, Aid Korea Association, delivered a welcoming speech, expressing warm welcome on behalf of the Nanjing Branch of the Resist America, Aid Korea Association and the million people of Nanjing to the Korean People's Delegation to China, and paying high tribute to the Korean People's Army and the Chinese People's Volunteers. Jin Shanbao called on the people of Nanjing to continue to deepen the movement to increase production and donations, to widely establish patriotic covenants, to do a good job in giving preferential treatment to the families of martyrs and revolutionary soldiers, and to vigorously support the front.

Amid music and applause, delegation leader Yuan Donggen presented a flag to the working people of Nanjing on behalf of all the employees of the Pyongyang Si Tong Charcoal Mine in Korea, with the inscription "Long live the eternal friendship between the Chinese and Korean people cemented with blood." Subsequently, people's organizations from all walks of life in Nanjing presented flowers to the Korean People's Delegation. In his reply, delegation leader Yuan Donggen denounced the various atrocities committed by U.S. imperialism in Korea and introduced the heroic struggle of the Korean people for national independence and freedom. He expressed heartfelt thanks to the Chinese people for their strong assistance to the Korean people in their most difficult times. Yuan Donggen stated that with the brotherly assistance of peace-loving people all over the world, especially the Chinese people, the Korean people will surely achieve final victory.

### 南京各界举行盛会  欢迎朝鲜人民代表团

1951-07-17
第1版()
专栏：

　　南京各界举行盛会
    欢迎朝鲜人民代表团
    【新华社南京十六日电】南京市各界人民代表三千五百余人，十五日晚举行盛会，隆重欢迎抵南京的朝鲜人民访华代表团。欢迎会在乐队吹奏中、朝两国国歌声中开始。南京抗美援朝分会主席金善宝致欢迎词，他代表南京市抗美援朝分会及南京市百万人民对朝鲜人民访华代表团的来临表示热烈欢迎，并向朝鲜人民军与中国人民志愿军致崇高的敬意。金善宝号召南京人民继续深入开展增产捐献运动，广泛订立爱国公约，做好优待烈士和革命军人的家属工作，大力支援前线。
    在乐声与掌声中，元东根团长代表朝鲜平壤寺烔炭矿全体从业员向南京市劳动人民献旗，旗上写着“以血结成的中朝两国人民永恒的友谊万岁”。继由南京各界人民团体向朝鲜人民访华代表团献花。元东根团长致答词时，控诉了美帝国主义在朝鲜种种暴行，并介绍了朝鲜人民为争取祖国独立自由而英勇斗争的情形。对中国人民在朝鲜人民最困难的时候，给予有力援助，表示衷心感谢。元东根团长表示：在世界爱好和平的人民尤其是中国人民兄弟般的援助下，朝鲜人民一定能取得最后胜利。
