Title: Telegram of Congratulations from the Mongolian People's Revolutionary Party
Author:
Date: 1951-07-01
Page: 2

To the Central Committee of the Chinese Communist Party, Beijing:

　　On the occasion of the 30th anniversary of the founding of the Chinese Communist Party, the Central Committee of the Mongolian People's Revolutionary Party extends warm congratulations to the great Chinese people’s glorious leader – the Chinese Communist Party. The Chinese Communist Party's unyielding and heroic struggle has led to the victory of the Chinese people's national liberation movement and the Chinese revolution, and established the People's Republic of China – the great friendly neighbor of the Mongolian People's Republic. The Central Committee of the Mongolian People's Revolutionary Party extends its highest respect to the Chinese Communist Party, which is building a new China, further expanding the glorious achievements of the Chinese people, developing its economy and culture, and fighting for peace, democracy, and socialism.

　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　Central Committee of the Mongolian People's Revolutionary Party

### 蒙古人民革命党贺电

1951-07-01
第2版()
专栏：

　　蒙古人民革命党贺电北京  中国共产党中央委员会
    兹逢中国共产党成立三十周年，蒙古人民革命党中央委员会谨向伟大的中国人民光荣的领导者——中国共产党致热烈的祝贺。中国共产党百折不回的英勇斗争使中国人民民族解放运动和中国革命获得胜利，并建立了中华人民共和国——蒙古人民共和国的伟大友好邻邦。蒙古人民革命党中央委员会对正在建设新中国，正在更加扩展中国人民的光辉成就，正在发展自己的经济和文化，以及正在为和平、民主与社会主义而斗争的中国共产党，致以最崇高的敬礼。
                                蒙古人民革命党中央委员会
