Title: Our Great Motherland (Picture)
Author:
Date: 1951-07-18
Page: 1

    Our Great Motherland
    Our People's Air Force has its first female parachutist - Comrade Lin Li, a 19-year-old member of the Communist Youth League and Communist Party member. She performed a formal parachute jumping exercise at a certain location at 5:00 PM on April 11th, and it was a complete success. This is a picture of her fully dressed and preparing to board the plane before the exercise.

### 我们伟大的祖国（图片）

1951-07-18
第1版()
专栏：

    我们伟大的祖国
    我国人民空军出现了第一个女跳伞员——十九岁的青年团员和共产党员林莉同志。她在四月十一日午后五时在某地作正式跳伞演习，完全成功。这是她在演习前全副装束，准备登机的情形。
