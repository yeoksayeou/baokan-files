Title: Embarking on the Glorious Post of National Defense Construction (Pictures)
Author:
Date: 1951-07-18
Page: 5

Embracing the Glorious Post of National Defense Construction

	Thirty-eight students from Peking University who participated in the military cadre school concentrated at Northern Jiaotong University Beijing Railway College on the afternoon of the 7th of this month. This is the scene of teachers and students of Peking University seeing off students from the school participating in the military cadre school in a car amidst the sound of firecrackers.

	After seeing the "Decision on Enrolling Students in Various Military Cadre Schools" issued by the State Council of the Central People's Government, the students of the Senior 3 class of Beijing No. 6 Middle School applauded in unison to show their response to the call of the motherland.

	The students of Northern Jiaotong University Beijing Railway College who were approved to participate in the military cadre school were warmly loved by their classmates. Before getting into the car and leaving the school, the students of Beijing Normal University who were approved to participate in the military cadre school excitedly shouted: "Long live Chairman Mao!"

	Many young people from society with primary and secondary school education in Beijing are vying to sign up to participate in the military cadre school. This is the scene of young people from Beijing society going to the Enrollment Committee of the Military Cadre School of the Beijing Municipal People's Government to sign up.

	Teachers of the Affiliated Women's Middle School of Beijing Normal University are encouraging students to participate in the military cadre school.

	The mother of Wang Yuning, a student of the Affiliated Women's Middle School of Beijing Normal University, is encouraging Wang Yuning to sign up to participate in the military cadre school.

	Trainees of the military cadre school are studying flight subjects.

	Trainees of the military cadre school are practicing semaphore signals.

	Trainees of the military cadre school are listening to instructors explain howitzers.

	Beijing No. 2 Middle School established a Military Cadre School Enrollment Recommendation Review Committee to carry out review work.

	The students of Beijing No. 6 Middle School are enthusiastically vying to sign up to participate in the military cadre school.

	Trainees of the military cadre school are practicing vaulting horses.

	Trainees of the military cadre school are listening to the radio in their spare time.

	Trainees of the military cadre school are conducting physical training suitable for the needs of the air force.

### 走上国防建设的光荣岗位（图片）

1951-07-18
第5版()
专栏：

    走上国防建设的光荣岗位
    北京大学参加军事干部学校的三十八位同学，于本月七日下午前往北方交通大学北京铁道学院集中。这是北京大学师生在鞭炮声中欢送该校参加军事干部学校同学上汽车的情形。
    北京市第六中学高三班的同学看到中央人民政府政务院“关于各种军事干部学校招收学生的决定”后，一致鼓掌表示响应祖国的号召。
    北方交通大学北京铁道学院被批准参加军事干部学校的同学，受到同学们的热烈爱戴。北京师范大学被批准参加军事干部学校的同学，在上汽车离校之前，兴奋地高呼：毛主席万岁！
    北京市许多中小学程度的社会青年，争先报名参加军事干部学校。这是北京市社会青年前往北京市人民政府军事干部学校招生委员会报名的情形。
    北京师范大学附属女子中学的教员，鼓励同学们参加军事干部学校。
    北京师范大学附属女子中学学生王毓宁的母亲，正在鼓励王毓宁报名参加军事干部学校。
    军事干部学校的学员正在研究飞行科目。
    军事干部学校的学员正在实习手旗信号。
    军事干部学校的学员正在听教官讲解榴弹炮。
    北京市第二中学成立了军事干部学校招生保送审查委员会，进行审查工作。
    北京市第六中学的同学们正在热烈地争先报名参加军事干部学校。
    军事干部学校的学员正在练习跳木马。
    军事干部学校的学员在课余时收听广播。
    军事干部学校的学员正在进行适合空军需要的体格锻炼。
