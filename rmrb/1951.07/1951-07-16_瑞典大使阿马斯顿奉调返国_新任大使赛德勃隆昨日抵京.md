Title: Swedish Ambassador Amelston Recalled Home; New Ambassador Soderblom Arrives in Beijing Yesterday
Author:
Date: 1951-07-16
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, 15th: Swedish Ambassador to China, Amelston, was recalled home on May 12th. With the agreement of the Chinese government, the Swedish government has appointed Mr. Soderblom as the Swedish Ambassador to China.

Ambassador Soderblom arrived in Beijing by car at 6:29 PM today. Those who went to the station to greet him included Yan Baohang, Deputy Director of the General Office of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs; Huan Xiang, Director of the Department of European and African Affairs; Wang Zhuoru, Director of the Protocol Department; Wu Han, Deputy Mayor of the Beijing Municipal People's Government; as well as the Indian Ambassador, the Danish Minister, the Swiss Minister, the Chargé d'affaires of the Indonesian and Burmese Embassies, personnel from the Soviet and Swedish Embassies, and negotiation representatives from the British, Norwegian, and Dutch governments.

### 瑞典大使阿马斯顿奉调返国  新任大使赛德勃隆昨日抵京

1951-07-16
第4版()
专栏：

　　瑞典大使阿马斯顿奉调返国
    新任大使赛德勃隆昨日抵京
    【新华社十五日讯】瑞典首任驻华特命全权大使阿马斯顿已于五月十二日奉调返国。瑞典王国政府于征得我国政府同意后，任命赛德勃隆为瑞典驻我国特命全权大使。
    赛德勃隆大使于今日下午六时二十九分乘车抵京。赴站迎接者有我外交部办公厅副主任阎宝航，欧非司司长宦乡，交际处处长王倬如，北京市人民政府副市长吴晗，此外尚有印度大使，丹麦公使，瑞士公使，印尼和缅甸使馆的临时代办，苏联，瑞典等使馆人员及英国、挪威、荷兰政府谈判代表等。
