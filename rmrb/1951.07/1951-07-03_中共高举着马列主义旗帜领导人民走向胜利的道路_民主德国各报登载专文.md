Title: The Chinese Communist Party, Holding High the Banner of Marxism-Leninism, Leads the People Towards the Road to Victory: Articles Published in Various Newspapers of Democratic Germany
Author:
Date: 1951-07-03
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency, Berlin, 1st.

The newspapers of Berlin, the capital of the German Democratic Republic, published news today commemorating the 30th anniversary of the founding of the Chinese Communist Party. Both *Neues Deutschland* and *Tägliche Rundschau* published the congratulatory telegram from the Central Committee of the Socialist Unity Party of Germany to the Central Committee of the Chinese Communist Party in prominent positions.

*Neues Deutschland* published photos and biographies of Chairman Mao Zedong and other CCP leaders such as Liu Shaoqi, Zhou Enlai, and Zhu De, and also published excerpts of the Chinese Communist Party's constitution. The third page of the newspaper published a full-page article entitled "The Chinese Communist Party, Under the Banner of Marx, Engels, Lenin, and Stalin, and Under the Leadership of Chairman Mao Zedong, Marches Towards the Road of Victory."

### 中共高举着马列主义旗帜领导人民走向胜利的道路  民主德国各报登载专文

1951-07-03
第4版()
专栏：

　　中共高举着马列主义旗帜领导人民走向胜利的道路
    民主德国各报登载专文
    【新华社柏林一日电】德意志民主共和国首都柏林各报今天都登载了关于中国共产党成立三十周年纪念的新闻。《新德意志报》和《每日评论报》都以显著地位刊载了德国统一社会党中央委员会致中国共产党中央委员会的贺电。
    《新德意志报》登载了毛泽东主席以及中共其他领袖刘少奇、周恩来、朱德等人的照片和简历，并节要登载了中国共产党的党章。该报第三版以全版地位发表长文一篇，题为“中国共产党在马恩列斯的旗帜下，在毛泽东主席领导下，走向胜利的道路”。
