Title: First Sub-Group of the慰问团 (Comfort Mission) to Korea Visits Northwest Provinces for Publicity; Third Sub-Group Conveys Reports in Wuhu and Qingdao
Author:
Date: 1951-07-09
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Xi'an, July 7th: The entire delegation of the First Sub-Group of the Chinese People's Comfort Mission to Korea departed from Xi'an on the 2nd of this month, heading to Shaanxi, Gansu, Qinghai, Xinjiang, and Ningxia provinces for publicity work.

Since arriving in Xi'an on the 20th of last month, the entire delegation had given 67 reports to people from all walks of life in Xi'an, with an audience of over 168,500 people.

After listening to the delegates' reports, people from all walks of life actively responded to the three major appeals of the China Resist America, Aid Korea General Association. Family members of workers at the Chang'an Engineering Section of the Longhai Railway established laundry groups to donate the proceeds from laundry to purchase airplanes and artillery. Farmers in the third township of the eleventh district not only expressed their active intention to increase production and donate weapons, but also resolved to do a good job in substitute farming, and to dry the new public grain and put it into storage on time. The elderly couple, Ge Fayi, in the twelfth district, who made a living by growing vegetables, also donated 300,000 yuan of their savings after hearing the report.

After listening to the report, students at Southeast Middle School immediately organized a comfort group to regularly visit the families of revolutionary martyrs and revolutionary soldiers. Business and industrial circles also became more concerned about the families of soldiers and martyrs after listening to the report. At the report meeting, the handicraft industry donated over 8 million yuan in preferential treatment funds on the spot.

Furthermore, Li Furen, head of the First Sub-Group of the Chinese People's Comfort Mission to Korea, deputy heads Zhou Jingwen, Xu Baoqi, and Zakharov, and 81 members of the group arrived in Lanzhou on the afternoon of the 5th and were warmly welcomed by people of all ethnic groups in the city. On the afternoon of the same day, people of all ethnic groups jointly held a grand meeting of 100,000 people to express their welcome.

Xinhua News Agency, Shanghai, July 7th: From the end of last month to the beginning of this month, representatives of the Third Sub-Group of the Chinese People's Comfort Mission to Korea conducted report meetings in Wuhu and Qingdao, which were warmly welcomed by the local people and promoted the local Resist America, Aid Korea movement.

Huang Shuying, a family member of a volunteer soldier in Wuhu, immediately wrote a letter to her son, who was recuperating in Korea, after listening to the report, saying: "It is an honor for you to bravely fight and be wounded on the Korean front. I hope you take good care of yourself and strive to return to the front line as soon as possible to eliminate more enemies!" After listening to the report, the workers of the Mingyuan Power Plant, who had successfully realized the guarantee of no accidents for 120 days, now proposed a new safety guarantee for another 120 days. The farmers of Qingshuihe in the suburbs of the city donated more than 10 million yuan on the spot when listening to the report, and guaranteed to realize this year's bumper harvest plan and do a good job in treating the families of revolutionary martyrs and revolutionary soldiers preferentially. After listening to the report by the representatives of the Comfort Mission on the heroic deeds of Korean youth defending their motherland, more than 30 students from Cuiwen Middle School applied for military cadre schools in half a day on June 30th. After the report, all sectors of industry and commerce immediately gathered to discuss concrete plans for increasing production and donating weapons. Everyone unanimously guaranteed to exceed the donation target of one airplane and one artillery piece.

Chen Yi, deputy head of the General Group of the Comfort Mission to Korea, led 33 representatives of the Third Sub-Group to report to people from all walks of life in Qingdao more than 60 times from June 30th to the 3rd of this month, with an audience of over 200,000 people. Their reports played a great promoting and encouraging role for people from all walks of life in Qingdao to further develop the patriotic Resist America, Aid Korea movement, complete the donation of weapons plan, and consolidate the great motherland's national defense.

### 赴朝慰问团第一分团  赴西北各省进行宣传  第三分团在芜湖青岛传达报告

1951-07-09
第4版()
专栏：

　　赴朝慰问团第一分团
    赴西北各省进行宣传         
    第三分团在芜湖青岛传达报告
    【新华社西安七日电】中国人民赴朝慰问团第一分团全体代表已于本月二日离西安转赴陕西、甘肃、青海、新疆、宁夏等省进行宣传。
    该团全体代表自上月二十日抵达西安后，曾分别向西安市各界人民作了六十七次报告，听众达十六万八千五百余人。
    各界人民听了代表们的报告后，纷纷以实际行动响应抗美援朝总会的三大号召。陇海铁路长安工务段的职工家属成立了洗衣小组，以洗衣所得捐献购买飞机大炮。十一区三乡的农民除表示积极增产捐献武器外，并决心做好代耕工作，同时将新的公粮晒干，全部按时入仓。十二区以种菜为生的葛发义老夫妇，听了报告后也捐出积蓄三十万元。
    东南中学的学生在听了报告后，立即组织慰问组准备经常慰问革命烈士家属和革命军人家属。工商界人士听了报告后也更加关心军属和烈属。在报告会上手纺业当场捐出优抚款八百余万元。
    又讯：中国人民赴朝慰问团第一分团李敷仁团长、周鲸文、许宝骙、扎克洛夫副团长及团员等八十一人，已于五日下午抵达兰州，受到该市各族人民的热烈欢迎。当日下午各民族人民联合举行十万人的盛会，表示欢迎。
    【新华社上海七日电】中国人民赴朝慰问团第三分团代表，自上月底至本月初，分别在芜湖、青岛两市进行传达报告，受到当地人民的热烈欢迎，并推动了当地的抗美援朝运动。
    芜湖市志愿军家属黄淑英听了报告后，当即写信鼓励在朝鲜养伤的儿子说：“你在朝鲜前线英勇战斗负伤是光荣的，望你好好休养，争取早日回到前线，消灭更多的敌人！”明远电厂工人们听了报告后，已经顺利实现了一百二十天不出事故的保证的机间工人，现在又提出一个一百二十天的安全保证。市郊清水河的农民在听报告时，当场就捐献出一千多万元，并保证实现今年的丰产计划，和做好优待革命烈士和革命军人家属的工作。萃文中学学生听了慰问团代表报告朝鲜青年保卫祖国的各种英雄事迹后，在六月三十日的半天中就有三十多个学生报名投考军事干部学校。工商界各行业在报告后立即集会讨论工商界增产捐献武器的具体计划。大家一致保证要超过一架飞机和一门大炮的捐献目标。
    赴朝慰问团总团陈沂副团长率第三分团代表三十三人，于六月三十日至本月三日在青岛向各界人民报告六十多次，听众超过二十万人。他们的报告，对于青岛市各界人民进一步开展抗美援朝爱国运动，完成捐献武器计划，巩固伟大祖国的国防，起了很大的推动和鼓舞作用。
