Title: My Ambassador to North Korea Hosts Banquet to Celebrate "July 1st"
Author:
Date: 1951-07-06
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Pyongyang, 5th — Our ambassador to North Korea, General Ni Zhiliang, hosted a banquet at the embassy on the evening of the 1st to celebrate the 30th anniversary of the founding of the Communist Party of China. Heads of the North Korean government and diplomatic envoys to North Korea from the Soviet Union, Poland, Mongolia, Hungary, Czechoslovakia, and Romania were invited. North Korean government leaders who attended included Zheng Junze, Chairman of the State Planning Commission; Zhang Shiyu, Minister of Commerce; Park Yi-wan, Minister of Transportation; Choi Chang-ik, Minister of Finance; Heo Jeong-suk, Minister of Culture and Propaganda; and Fang Xueshi, Minister of Social Security. The banquet proceeded in a warm atmosphere of unity and friendship. During the banquet, the hosts and guests repeatedly raised their glasses to the Communist Party of China, to the Chinese people and their leader Chairman Mao Zedong, to the Communist Party of the Soviet Union, to the leader of the world people, Marshal Stalin, and to the Korean Workers' Party, the Korean people and their leader General Kim Il-sung.

### 我驻朝鲜大使设宴庆祝“七一”

1951-07-06
第1版()
专栏：

　　我驻朝鲜大使
    设宴庆祝“七一”
    【新华社平壤五日电】我国驻朝鲜大使倪志亮将军为庆祝中国共产党诞生三十周年，于一日晚在大使馆设宴招待朝鲜政府首长和苏联、波兰、蒙古、匈牙利、捷克斯洛伐克、罗马尼亚等国驻朝使节。朝鲜政府首长应邀出席者有国家计划委员会委员长郑准泽、商业相张时雨、交通相朴义琓、财政相崔昌益、文化宣传相许贞淑、社会安全相方学世等。宴会在团结友好的热烈气氛中进行。席间，宾主频频起立为中国共产党，为中国人民及其领袖毛泽东主席，为苏联共产党，为世界人民领袖斯大林大元帅，为朝鲜劳动党、朝鲜人民及其领袖金日成将军而干杯。
