Title: Korean Armistice Talks Continue Discussion of Meeting Agenda
Author:
Date: 1951-07-17
Page: 1

　　Korean Armistice Talks
    Continue Discussion of Meeting Agenda
    [Xinhua News Agency, Pyongyang, 16th] This reporter has learned from the headquarters at the Korean front that the fourth meeting of the armistice talks began at 9:00 AM (Korean time) on July 16th and ended at 12:50 PM. The meeting continued to discuss the issue of the meeting agenda. The fifth meeting is scheduled to be held at 10:00 AM on July 17th.

### 朝鲜停战谈判会议  继续讨论会议议程

1951-07-17
第1版()
专栏：

　　朝鲜停战谈判会议
    继续讨论会议议程
    【新华社平壤十六日电】本社记者由朝鲜前线总部获悉：停战谈判会议第四次会议在七月十六日上午九时（朝鲜时间）开始，中午十二时五十分结束。会议中继续讨论了会议议程问题，第五次会议预定在七月十七日上午十时举行。
