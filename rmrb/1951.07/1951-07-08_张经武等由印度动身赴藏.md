Title: Zhang Jingwu and Others Depart from India for Tibet
Author:
Date: 1951-07-08
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, New Delhi, 7th: Zhang Jingwu, the representative of the Central People's Government to Tibet, and Keimo, the plenipotentiary representative of the Tibetan local government, and their party, departed from Calcutta, India, by plane on the 4th for Siliguri in northern Bengal Province. They will travel from there to Tibet via Jalpaiguri, Sikkim, and other places.

### 张经武等由印度动身赴藏

1951-07-08
第1版()
专栏：

　　张经武等由印度动身赴藏
    【新华社新德里七日电】中央人民政府赴西藏代表张经武及西藏地方政府全权代表凯墨等一行，已于四日搭机离印度加尔各答赴孟加拉省北部的锡里格。他们将自该地经由加邻旁、锡金等地赴西藏。
