Title: Hubei Donates Sixty Airplanes
Author:
Date: 1951-07-03
Page: 1

　　Hubei Donates Sixty Airplanes
    [This newspaper, Hankou, 2nd Dispatch] The Hubei Provincial Anti-American and Aid-Korea Branch and the Hubei Provincial People's Representative Conference held a joint meeting on the afternoon of the 2nd, and unanimously resolved to strive to donate sixty fighter planes before the end of the year. Representatives from the industrial and commercial sectors immediately pledged to donate thirty fighter planes.

### 湖北献机六十架

1951-07-03
第1版()
专栏：

　　湖北献机六十架
    【本报汉口二日电】湖北省抗美援朝分会与湖北省各界人民代表会议，于二日下午举行联席会议，一致决议，争取在年底以前捐献战斗机六十架，工商界代表当场认捐了战斗机三十架。
