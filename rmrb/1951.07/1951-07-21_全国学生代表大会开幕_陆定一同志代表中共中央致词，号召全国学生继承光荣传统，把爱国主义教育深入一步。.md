Title: National Student Congress Opens; Comrade Lu Dingyi Delivers Speech on Behalf of the CCP Central Committee, Calling on Students Nationwide to Inherit Glorious Traditions and Deepen Patriotic Education

Author: 
Date: 1951-07-21
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, July 20th: The 15th National Student Congress of the All-China Students' Federation grandly opened in Beijing on July 20th. The tasks of this congress are to summarize the experiences of the Chinese student movement over the past two years, determine the current specific tasks of Chinese students, revise the Constitution of the All-China Students' Federation, and re-elect the Executive Committee of the All-China Students' Federation. A total of 363 delegates attended the congress, with 60 invited delegates. The delegates include students from various regions, ethnic groups, religious beliefs, overseas Chinese, students studying abroad, and students from schools of various natures. The invited delegates are students who have recently left school and are serving with excellent achievements in various construction posts, and students from military cadre schools.

Guests attending the opening ceremony included: Lu Dingyi, member of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of China; Shen Junru, Vice Chairman of the National Committee of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference; Guo Moruo, Vice Premier of the State Administration Council and Director of the Cultural and Educational Committee of the Central People's Government; Ma Xulun, Minister of Education of the Central People's Government; Li Lisan, Vice Chairman of the All-China Federation of Trade Unions; Wu Yuzhang, Chairman of the China Educational Workers' Union; Cai Chang, Chairman of the All-China Democratic Women's Federation; Liao Chengzhi, Deputy Secretary of the Central Committee of the China New Democratic Youth League and Chairman of the All-China Federation of Democratic Youth; Jiang Nanxiang, Deputy Secretary of the Central Committee of the China New Democratic Youth League; Xiao Hua, Deputy Director of the General Political Department of the Central People's Government's Revolutionary Military Commission; Peng Zemin, Chairman of the Central Supervisory Committee of the Chinese Peasants' and Workers' Democratic Party, and others.

After the Chairman of the Congress, Xie Bangding, delivered the opening address, Comrade Lu Dingyi delivered a speech on behalf of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of China. He pointed out that Chinese students should inherit the glorious tradition of past patriotic struggles and, under the banner of patriotism, deepen patriotic education. He said: In the past, under the leadership of the Communist Party of China, Chinese students fought heroically with the people and achieved the victory of the revolution. In the future, under the leadership of the Communist Party, they will continue to fight with the people to build a beautiful socialist and communist society. Subsequently, Shen Junru and Guo Moruo spoke, encouraging students to unite more broadly, combine with workers and peasants, learn their skills well, and prepare to participate in the construction of the motherland. In his speech, Ma Xulun hoped that students would continue to help educational institutions and school administrations reform the education system, and proposed that in order to better serve the motherland, they should pay attention to physical exercise. Following them, Li Lisan, Wu Yuzhang, Cai Chang, and Liao Chengzhi spoke, unanimously encouraging students to strengthen their political studies, master science and technology, and prepare to serve the motherland!

During the congress, representatives from the Beijing Young Pioneers and the Youth Department of the General Political Department of the Central People's Government's Revolutionary Military Commission presented flowers and flags to the congress. In the evening, the General Political Department of the Central People's Government's Revolutionary Military Commission, the Central Committee of the China New Democratic Youth League, the All-China Federation of Democratic Youth, and the North China Working Committee of the Youth League jointly hosted a banquet for all the delegates attending the congress.

### 全国学生代表大会开幕  陆定一同志代表中共中央致词，号召全国学生继承光荣传统，把爱国主义教育深入一步。

1951-07-21
第1版()
专栏：

　　全国学生代表大会开幕
　  陆定一同志代表中共中央致词，号召全国学生继承光荣传统，把爱国主义教育深入一步。
    【新华社二十日讯】中华全国学生第十五届代表大会七月二十日在北京隆重开幕。这次大会的任务是总结两年来中国学生运动的经验，确定中国学生当前具体任务，修改中华全国学生联合会章程，改选中华全国学生联合会执行委员会。出席大会的代表共三百六十三人，邀请代表六十人。代表中有各地区、各民族、各种宗教信仰、海外华侨、留学生和各种性质学校的学生等。邀请代表为离校不久而正在各种建设岗位上服务成绩优良的学生和军事干部学校学生。
    参加大会开幕式的来宾有：中国共产党中央委员陆定一，中国人民政治协商会议全国委员会副主席沈钧儒，中央人民政府政务院副总理兼文化教育委员会主任郭沫若，中央人民政府教育部长马叙伦，中华全国总工会副主席李立三，中国教育工作者工会主席吴玉章，中华全国民主妇女联合会主席蔡畅，中国新民主主义青年团中央委员会副书记、中华全国民主青年联合会主席廖承志，中国新民主主义青年团中央委员会副书记蒋南翔，中央人民政府人民革命军事委员会总政治部副主任萧华，中国农工民主党中央监察委员会主席彭泽民等。
    大会主席谢邦定致开幕词后，即由陆定一同志代表中国共产党中央委员会致词。他指出中国学生要继承过去爱国斗争的光荣传统，在爱国主义的旗帜下，把爱国主义教育深入一步。他说：中国学生过去在中国共产党的领导下，和人民在一起，英勇奋斗，取得了革命的胜利，将来还要在共产党的领导之下，和人民在一起，继续奋斗，建设美好的社会主义、共产主义社会。继由沈钧儒、郭沫若讲话，他们勉励学生更广泛地团结起来，与工农结合，学好本领，准备参加祖国的建设。马叙伦讲话中希望学生们继续帮助教育机关与学校行政进行教育事业的改革，并提出为了更好地为祖国服务，应重视体格的锻炼。接着讲话的有李立三、吴玉章、蔡畅、廖承志，他们一致勉励学生加强政治学习，掌握科学技术，准备为祖国服务！
    大会进行时，北京市少年儿童队、中央人民政府人民革命军事委员会总政治部青年部等代表向大会献花献旗。晚间，中央人民政府人民革命军事委员会总政治部、中国新民主主义青年团中央委员会、中华全国民主青年联合会和青年团华北工作委员会，联合欢宴了出席大会的全体代表。
