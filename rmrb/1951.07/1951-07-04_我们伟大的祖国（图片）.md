    Our Great Motherland
    The abundant wheat harvest has brought universal joy to farmers in wheat-producing areas. Yu Yanfa, a liberated farmer from Duandian Town in the suburbs of Jinan City, is holding a bundle of wheat, expressing unspeakable joy because he is getting such a good harvest for the first time.

### 我们伟大的祖国（图片）

1951-07-04
第1版()
专栏：

    我们伟大的祖国
    夏收丰盛的小麦，引起了各地产麦区农民普遍的欢悦。济南市郊段店镇的翻身农民于延法，正抱着一捆麦子，表现了说不出的快乐，因为他第一次得到这样的丰收。
