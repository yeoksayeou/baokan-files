Title: Soviet Newspapers and the Organ of the Communist Information Bureau Publish Articles and News Extensively to Commemorate the 30th Anniversary of the Founding of the Communist Party of China
Author:
Date: 1951-07-02
Page: 1

　　Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, 1st. Soviet newspapers today all published articles commemorating the 30th anniversary of the Communist Party of China, as well as news of the Beijing celebration meeting and Liu Shaoqi's speech. "Pravda" published Zhu De's "How the Chinese People Defeated Chiang Kai-shek's Reactionary Clique Armed by US Imperialism." "Trud" published "The CCP is the Inspirer and Organizer of the Construction of New China" by Chinese economic researcher Kovalev, which focused on introducing the achievements of the Chinese working class in economic construction under the leadership of the Communist Party of China. "Moscow Pravda" reprinted the editorial from "For Lasting Peace, For People's Democracy," entitled "The Glorious Thirty Years of the Communist Party of China." "Izvestia" published "New Chinese Literature" by Speransky, which introduced the state of Chinese people's literature under the leadership of the Communist Party of China.
　　Xinhua News Agency, Prague, 1st. The organ of the Communist Information Bureau, "For Lasting Peace, For People's Democracy!" published on June 29th, under the banner headline "Thirty Years of the Communist Party of China," published an editorial and articles commemorating the 30th anniversary of the Communist Party of China, filling three pages. The first page of the publication published an editorial entitled "The Glorious Thirty Years of the Communist Party of China," and published the full text of Zhu De's "How the Chinese People Defeated Chiang Kai-shek's Reactionary Clique Armed by US Imperialism," Chen Yun's "The Communist Party of China Leads National Construction," Lu Dingyi's "The World Significance of the Chinese Revolution," and Deng Xiaoping's "Close Contact with the Masses is the Glorious Tradition of Our Party," and other articles. The second page excerpted Chairman Mao Zedong's "On the People's Democratic Dictatorship" and the congratulatory speech delivered at the celebration of Stalin's 70th birthday in Moscow in 1949. The third page published photos of Mao Zedong, Zhu De, Liu Shaoqi, and Zhou Enlai reviewing the "May Day" International Labor Day parade on Tiananmen Square in Beijing this year.

### 苏各报和共产党情报局机关报  以大量篇幅刊登文章和消息纪念中国共产党建党三十年

1951-07-02
第1版()
专栏：

　　苏各报和共产党情报局机关报
    以大量篇幅刊登文章和消息
    纪念中国共产党建党三十年
    【新华社莫斯科一日电】苏联各报今日都登载纪念中国共产党三十周年的文章，北京庆祝大会和刘少奇讲话的消息。真理报登载了朱德的《中国人民怎样打败了美帝国主义武装的蒋介石反动派》。劳动报登载了中国经济研究家柯沃廖夫作的《中共是新中国建设的鼓舞者和组织者》，该文着重介绍中国共产党领导下中国工人阶级在经济建设上的成就。莫斯科真理报转载了《争取持久和平，争取人民民主》的社论《中国共产党的光荣的三十年》。消息报登载了斯柏兰斯基作的《新中国文学》，该文介绍在中国共产党领导下中国人民文学的情况。
    【新华社布拉格一日电】六月二十九日出版的共产党情报局机关报《争取持久和平，争取人民民主！》在《中国共产党的三十年》的通栏标题下，以三版篇幅发表了纪念中国共产党三十周年的社论和文章。该刊的第一版发表了题为《中国共产党的光荣的三十周年》的社论，并全文刊登朱德的《中国人民怎样击败了美帝国主义武装的蒋介石反动派》、陈云的《中国共产党领导着国家建设》、陆定一的《中国革命的世界意义》，和邓小平的《紧密地联系群众是我党的光荣传统》等文章。第二版摘录了毛泽东主席的《论人民民主专政》和一九四九年在莫斯科庆祝斯大林七十寿辰的大会上发表的祝辞。第三版刊登了毛泽东、朱德、刘少奇和周恩来今年在北京天安门上检阅“五一”国际劳动节示威游行的照片。
