    Our Great Motherland (Picture)
    Under the leadership of the People's Government, the cultural and educational undertakings of all ethnic groups in Xinjiang are developing rapidly. Here, students of the 20th Primary School in Dihua are engaged in cultural and recreational activities in their spare time. The two female students standing are Russian children's team members, and they are playing the six-string guitar.

### 我们伟大的祖国（图片）

1951-07-15
第1版()
专栏：

    我们伟大的祖国（图片）
    新疆各族人民的文化教育事业，在人民政府领导下，正在迅速发展。在这里，迪化市立第二十小学校的学生，正在课余进行文化娱乐。站着的两位女学生是俄罗斯族的少年儿童队员，她们正在演奏六弦琴“吉他”。
