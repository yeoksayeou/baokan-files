Title: Our Great Motherland (Picture)
Author:
Date: 1951-07-21
Page: 1

    Our Great Motherland
    The "Mao Zedong" locomotive crew of the Fengtai Locomotive Depot, famous throughout the country, is enthusiastically responding to the call of the China Association to Resist US Aggression and Aid Korea to increase production and make donations. This is a picture of the crew members discussing their planned production increase and donations.

1951-07-21
第1版()
专栏：

    我们伟大的祖国
    闻名全国的丰台机务段毛泽东号机车包乘组，热烈响应抗美援朝总会号召的增产捐献运动，这是该组组员们正在研究自己订立的增产捐献计划。
