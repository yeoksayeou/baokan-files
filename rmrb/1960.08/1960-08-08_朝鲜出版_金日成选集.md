Title: Korean Publication of "Kim Il-sung's Selected Works"

Author:

Date: 1960-08-08
Page: 6

    Korean Publication of Second Edition of Six Volumes of "Kim Il-sung's Selected Works"
    Xinhua News Agency, Pyongyang, 6th - According to the Korean Central News Agency, the second edition of the sixth volume of "Kim Il-sung's Selected Works" has recently been published.
    The sixth volume includes important works by Comrade Kim Il-sung during the period from August 1958 to December 1959.
    KCNA said that during this period, inspired by the letter from the Central Committee of the Korean Workers' Party to all party members, published in September 1958, all party members and working people displayed great patriotic enthusiasm. They expanded and developed the Chollima Movement, which symbolizes the great upsurge in socialist construction in Korea.
    KCNA said, "The programmatic documents in 'Kim Il-sung's Selected Works' help to arm the minds of all party members and working people with Marxism-Leninism. In this volume, the Marxist-Leninist theory has been creatively applied and developed according to the actual conditions of Korea. It points out the path the Korean people should take."

### 朝鲜出版“金日成选集

1960-08-08
第6版()
专栏：

    朝鲜出版“金日成选集”六卷第二版
    新华社平壤6日电　据朝鲜中央通讯社报道，金日成选集第六卷第二版最近出版。
    第六卷包括金日成同志在1958年8月到1959年12月期间的重要著作。
    朝中社说，在这期间，在1958年9月发表的朝鲜劳动党中央委员会致全体党员书的鼓舞下，全体党员和劳动人民发挥了巨大的爱国热情。他们扩大并发展了象征着朝鲜社会主义建设大高涨的千里马运动。
    朝中社说，“金日成选集中带有纲领性的文件，有助于用马克思列宁主义来武装全体党员和劳动人民的思想。在这卷选集中，马克思列宁主义的理论根据朝鲜的实际情况得到了创造性的运用和发展。它指出了朝鲜人民应走的道路。”
