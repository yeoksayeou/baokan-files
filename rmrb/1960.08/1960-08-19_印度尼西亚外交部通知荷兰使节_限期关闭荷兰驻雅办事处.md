Title: Indonesian Foreign Ministry Notifies Dutch Envoy: Deadline to Close Dutch Office in Jakarta
Author:
Date: 1960-08-19
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, Jakarta, August 18th: The Indonesian Foreign Ministry announced today that Foreign Minister Subandrio informed the head of the Dutch representative office in Indonesia early on the morning of August 17th that Indonesia is severing diplomatic relations with the Netherlands, effective August 17th. The Dutch representative office has been given fourteen days to close its Jakarta office and leave Indonesia. The Indonesian Foreign Ministry has instructed the Indonesian representative office in the Netherlands to close within this month.

### 印度尼西亚外交部通知荷兰使节  限期关闭荷兰驻雅办事处

1960-08-19
第5版()
专栏：

    印度尼西亚外交部通知荷兰使节
    限期关闭荷兰驻雅办事处
    新华社雅加达18日电　印度尼西亚外交部今天宣布，外交部长苏班德里约17日清晨通知荷兰驻印度尼西亚代表机构负责人说，印度尼西亚从8月17日起同荷兰断绝外交关系。荷兰代表机构被限令在十四天内关闭它在雅加达的办事处并且离开印度尼西亚。印度尼西亚外交部已指示印度尼西亚驻荷兰的代表机构在本月内结束。
