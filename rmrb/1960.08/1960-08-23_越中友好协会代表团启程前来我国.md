Title: Vietnam-China Friendship Association Delegation Departs for China
Author:
Date: 1960-08-23
Page: 6
Xinhua News Agency, Hanoi, August 22nd. At the invitation of the China-Vietnam Friendship Association to participate in China's celebrations of the 15th anniversary of the founding of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam, a six-member delegation of the Vietnam-China Friendship Association, led by Tran Huy Lieu, Vice-President of the Vietnam China Friendship Association, departed for China this afternoon.

Those who saw them off at the station included Nguyen Xuan Thuy, Vice-President of the Vietnam-China Friendship Association, and Yoon Ke Sun, Chairman of the Hanoi Fatherland Front.

Wang Tao, Charge d'Affaires ad interim of the Chinese Embassy in Vietnam, and others also saw them off at the station.

### 越中友好协会代表团启程前来我国

1960-08-23
第6版()
专栏：

    越中友好协会代表团启程前来我国
    新华社河内22日电　应中越友好协会邀请参加中国庆祝越南民主共和国建国十五周年活动的越中友好协会代表团一行六人，在越中友好协会副会长陈辉燎的率领下，今天下午启程前往中国。
    到车站送行的有越中友好协会副会长阮春水、河内祖国战线主席尹继善等人。
    中国驻越南大使馆临时代办汪滔等也到车站送行。
