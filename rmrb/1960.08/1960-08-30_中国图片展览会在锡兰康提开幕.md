Title: Chinese Photo Exhibition Opens in Kandy, Ceylon
Author:
Date: 1960-08-30
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, Colombo, August 29th. A Chinese photo exhibition opened in Kandy, Ceylon on August 26.

These photos were previously exhibited in Colombo last November.

The former Ceylon Ambassador to China and Chairman of the Ceylon-China Friendship Association, Mr. Perera, inaugurated the exhibition.

Yao Dengshan, Chargé d'Affaires ad interim of the Chinese Embassy in Ceylon, attended the opening ceremony.

The exhibition was organized by the Kandy branch of the Ceylon-China Friendship Association.

### 中国图片展览会在锡兰康提开幕

1960-08-30
第5版()
专栏：

    中国图片展览会在锡兰康提开幕
    新华社科伦坡29日电　中国图片展览会8月26日在锡兰的康提开幕。
    这些图片去年11月曾在科伦坡展览过。
    前锡兰驻中国大使、锡中友好协会主席佩雷拉为展览会揭幕。
    中国驻锡兰大使馆临时代办姚登山出席了开幕式。
    展览会是锡中友好协会康提分会组织的。
