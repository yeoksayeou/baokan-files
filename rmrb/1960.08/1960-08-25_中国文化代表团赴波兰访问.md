Title: Chinese Cultural Delegation Visits Poland
Author:
Date: 1960-08-25
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, August 24th. A four-member Chinese cultural delegation, led by Shen Yanbing, Minister of Culture and President of the Sino-Polish Friendship Association, departed for Poland on the morning of the 24th according to the 1960-1961 implementation plan of the Sino-Polish Cultural Cooperation Agreement. The members of the delegation included Wang Kuang, Director of the Propaganda Department of the Guangdong Provincial Party Committee, Hua Yingshen, Director of the General Office of the Ministry of Culture, and Hua Junwu, Secretary-General of the Artists Association.

Those who went to the airport to see them off included Qian Junrui and Xu Guangxiao, Vice Ministers of Culture, Zhang Zhixiang, Deputy Director of the Commission for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries, Wang Yi, Secretary-General of the Sino-Polish Friendship Association, and Zhang E, Deputy Secretary-General of the Artists Association.

Chargé d'Affaires ad interim of the Polish Embassy in China, Mr. Fularato, and his wife also went to the airport to see them off.

### 中国文化代表团赴波兰访问

1960-08-25
第4版()
专栏：

    中国文化代表团赴波兰访问
    新华社24日讯　根据中波文化合作协定1960—1961年执行计划赴波访问的中国文化代表团一行四人，在文化部部长、中波友好协会会长沈雁冰的率领下，24日早晨启程前往波兰。代表团团员有广东省委宣传部长王匡、文化部办公厅主任华应申、美术家协会秘书长华君武。
    前往机场送行的有文化部副部长钱俊瑞、徐光霄，对外文委副主任张致祥，中波友好协会秘书长王仪和美术家协会副秘书长张谔等人。
    波兰驻华使馆临时代办傅拉托和夫人也到机场送行。
