Title: Bankruptcies Increasing in Canada, Up Approximately 30% in the First Half of This Year Compared to the Same Period Last Year
Author:
Date: 1960-08-08
Page: 6

Canada's bankrupt businesses are increasing daily.

According to a report by the Canadian "Globe and Mail" on July 27, an increasing number of businesses in Canada are going bankrupt.

The newspaper cited statistics from Dun & Bradstreet of Canada, stating that the number of business failures in Canada this year is almost 30% higher compared to last year. The number of businesses that failed throughout last year was 1,830.

The newspaper stated that in June of this year, the debts of bankrupt companies increased by 60% compared to June 1959, reaching 8.3 million Canadian dollars.

In the first half of this year, a total of 918 companies went bankrupt, while the figure for the same period in 1959 was 706.

### 加拿大破产企业日增  今年上半年比去年同期约增三成

1960-08-08
第6版()
专栏：

    加拿大破产企业日增
    今年上半年比去年同期约增三成
    据加拿大“环球邮报”7月27日报道，加拿大破产的企业越来越多。
    这家报纸援引加拿大邓—布列德斯特里统计所的统计资料说，今年加拿大企业倒闭的数字同去年相比几乎多30％。去年全年倒闭的企业数是一千八百三十家。
    报纸说，今年6月，倒闭的公司的债务比1959年6月增加了60％，而达到八百三十万加元。
    今年上半年，倒闭的公司总共有九百一十八家，而1959年同期为七百零六家。
