Title: Chinese Academy of Sciences Delegation Leaves England for Home

Author:

Date: 1960-08-01
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, London, 30th - The delegation of the Chinese Academy of Sciences, led by Wu Youxun, Vice President of the Chinese Academy of Sciences, left here by plane today to return home after attending the celebrations for the 300th anniversary of the founding of the Royal Society of the United Kingdom at the invitation of the Society.
　　
The British Minister of Science and Technology, Viscount Hailsham, received the delegation on July 28.
　　
Wu Youxun, head of the delegation of the Chinese Academy of Sciences, held a reception on the 27th. Among those present at the reception were Sir Cyril Hinshelwood, President of the Royal Society, and many British scientists. Huan Xiang, Chargé d'Affaires of the Chinese Embassy in the United Kingdom, also attended the reception.
　　
The delegation of the Chinese Academy of Sciences arrived in London on July 18.

### 中国科学院代表团离英返国

1960-08-01
第6版()
专栏：

    中国科学院代表团离英返国
    新华社伦敦30日电　应英国皇家学会邀请参加庆祝这个学会成立三百周年纪念的中国科学院代表团今天乘飞机离开这里返国。代表团是由中国科学院副院长吴有训率领的。
    英国科学技术大臣黑尔什姆子爵在7月28日接见了代表团。
    中国科学院代表团团长吴有训在27日举行了招待会。出席招待会的有英国皇家学会会长钦歇耳伍德和许多英国科学家。中国驻英国代办处代办宦乡也出席了招待会。
    中国科学院代表团是在7月18日抵达伦敦的。
