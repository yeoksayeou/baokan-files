Title: Representatives from Various Countries Speak at Latin American Youth Congress, Fully Supporting the Cuban People's Anti-Imperialist Struggle, Angrily Condemning US Imperialist Aggression Against Cuba
Author: 
Date: 1960-08-09
Page: 6

    Xinhua News Agency, Havana, 6th - The first Latin American Youth Congress continued its plenary sessions on the 5th and 6th. Representatives from the World Federation of Democratic Youth, China, the Democratic People's Republic of Korea, Czechoslovakia, and the United States spoke at the meeting on the 5th.
    Echard, General Secretary of the World Federation of Democratic Youth, extended greetings on behalf of 87 million members to the Latin American youth who are fighting against imperialism and for the realization of the wishes of the Latin American people, and expressed full support for their struggle. He said that the World Federation of Democratic Youth has always supported young people all over the world who are fighting against imperialism, and now the World Federation of Democratic Youth stands on the side of Cuba.
    Zhang Chao, head of the Chinese youth delegation, solemnly warned US imperialism against interfering in and invading Cuba in his speech to the conference. He expressed that the Chinese youth will firmly stand with the Cuban people and fully support the just struggle of the Cuban people and youth against the invaders. (For detailed speech, see page 5 of this newspaper on the 8th)
    The representative of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea said that the Korean people angrily condemn US imperialism's aggression against Cuba. He said that US imperialism is attempting to prevent the influence of the Cuban revolution from spreading in Latin America in order to continue to maintain its rule over the region. US imperialism is the main enemy of Cuba and Latin America, but it can no longer enslave Cuba.
    The representative of Czechoslovakia said that socialist countries support the people of all countries who are fighting against imperialism and support the Cuban youth who are fighting.
    An American representative attending the meeting said: The Cuban revolution reflects the Cuban people's feelings of demanding national sovereignty. We should unite the American people to support the Cuban revolution. The American people should demand that the government not interfere in Cuba's internal affairs.
    The representative of the International Union of Students also spoke at the meeting that day. At the meeting held on the morning of the 6th, Nuñez Jiménez, Director of the National Land Reform Committee of Cuba, spoke, condemning US imperialism's aggression and plunder against Cuba and Latin America. He cited numerous facts to prove that the United States has always been an intervener and aggressor against Cuba. He said that the US policy is an imperialist policy.

### 各国代表在拉丁美洲青年代表大会上发言  全力支持古巴人民反帝斗争  愤怒谴责美帝国主义对古巴的侵略

1960-08-09
第6版()
专栏：

    各国代表在拉丁美洲青年代表大会上发言
    全力支持古巴人民反帝斗争
    愤怒谴责美帝国主义对古巴的侵略
    据新华社哈瓦那6日电　拉丁美洲第一次青年代表大会5日和6日继续举行全体会议。世界民主青年联盟、中国、朝鲜民主主义人民共和国、捷克斯洛伐克以及美国的代表在5日会议上发了言。
    世界民主青年联盟总书记埃夏尔代表八千七百万会员对为反对帝国主义和争取实现拉丁美洲人民的愿望而斗争的拉丁美洲青年致意，并且表示全力支持他们的斗争。他说，世界民主青年联盟一贯支持世界上所有为反对帝国主义而斗争的青年，现在，世界民主青年联盟站在古巴这一边。
    中国青年代表团团长张超在向大会发言时严正警告美帝国主义不得干涉和侵略古巴。他表示中国青年将坚定地同古巴人民站在一起，全力支持古巴人民和青年反抗侵略者的正义斗争。（详细发言见8日本报第五版）
    朝鲜民主主义人民共和国的代表说，朝鲜人民愤怒地谴责美帝国主义侵略古巴。他说，美帝国主义企图阻止古巴革命的影响在拉丁美洲传播，以此来继续保持它对这一地区的统治。美帝国主义是古巴和拉丁美洲的主要敌人，但是它决不能再奴役古巴。
    捷克斯洛伐克的代表说，社会主义国家支持为反对帝国主义而进行斗争的各国人民，支持正在进行斗争的古巴青年。
    出席会议的一位美国代表发言说：古巴革命反映了古巴人民要求国家主权的感情。我们应该团结美国人民支持古巴革命。美国人民应该要求政府不要干涉古巴内政。
    在这一天会议上发言的，还有国际学生联合会的代表。在6日上午举行的会议上，古巴土地改革全国委员会主任努涅斯·希门尼斯发言，谴责美帝国主义对古巴和拉丁美洲进行的侵略和掠夺。他列举了无数的事实，证明美国一贯是古巴的干涉者和侵略者。他说，美国的政策是帝国主义的政策。
