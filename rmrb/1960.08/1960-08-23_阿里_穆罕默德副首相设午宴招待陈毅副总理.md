Title: Deputy Prime Minister Ali Mohammed Hosts Luncheon in Honor of Deputy Premier Chen Yi
Author:
Date: 1960-08-23
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, Kabul, August 22nd: Afghan Deputy Prime Minister Ali Mohammed held a luncheon today in the Paghman Royal Garden here in honor of Chen Yi, Marshal of the People's Republic of China and Vice Premier of the State Council and Minister of Foreign Affairs. Afghan Deputy Prime Minister and Foreign Minister Sardar Mohammad Naim attended the banquet. Also attending the banquet were Kong Yuan, Deputy Director of the Foreign Affairs Office of the State Council of the People's Republic of China, Geng Biao, Vice Minister of Foreign Affairs, and other members of Marshal Chen Yi's entourage, as well as Hao Ting, the Chinese Ambassador to Afghanistan, and personnel from the Chinese Embassy. Also attending the banquet were Mohammad Nuruzai, Speaker of the Afghan National Assembly, cabinet ministers, senior military officers, and diplomatic envoys from various countries to Afghanistan.

The lush Paghman Royal Garden is located on a small hillside eighteen kilometers from Kabul. Within the garden, neat lawns, adorned with countless flowers, appear extremely charming, with streams murmuring beside the lawns. Guests and hosts engaged in cordial and friendly conversations in this tranquil and beautiful environment.

After the luncheon, Marshal Chen Yi and his entourage, accompanied by Afghan Deputy Foreign Minister Ghulam Mohammad Suleiman and Afghan Ambassador to China Abdul Samad, paid a visit to His Royal Highness Marshal Shah Wali Khan Ghazi, uncle of the King.

### 阿里·穆罕默德副首相设午宴招待陈毅副总理

1960-08-23
第1版()
专栏：

    阿里·穆罕默德副首相设午宴招待陈毅副总理
    新华社喀布尔22日电　阿富汗副首相阿里·穆罕默德今天在这里的帕格曼皇家花园中举行午宴，招待中华人民共和国国务院副总理兼外交部长陈毅元帅。阿富汗副首相兼外交大臣萨达尔·穆罕默德·纳伊姆出席了宴会。出席宴会的还有：中华人民共和国国务院外事办公室副主任孔原，外交部副部长耿飚和陈毅元帅的其它随行人员，以及中国驻阿富汗大使郝汀和中国大使馆人员。出席宴会的还有阿富汗国民议会议长穆罕默德·努鲁塞，内阁大臣们，高级军官和各国驻阿富汗外交使节。
    树木葱郁的帕格曼皇家花园位于离喀布尔十八公里的一个小山坡上。花园内，很整齐的草地在无数的花朵的点缀下，显得十分妩媚动人，草地旁边溪水潺潺作响。宾主们在这种恬静而美丽的环境中进行亲切和友好的交谈。
    在午宴结束后，陈毅元帅和随行人员，在阿富汗外交副大臣吴拉姆·穆罕默德·苏莱曼和阿富汗驻中国大使阿卜杜勒·萨马德的陪同下，拜会了国王的叔父沙阿·华利汗·加齐元帅殿下。
