Title: Wu Nu Receives Chinese Representatives of the Sino-Burmese Boundary Joint Committee
Author:
Date: 1960-08-25
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Rangoon, August 24th: Burmese Prime Minister Wu Nu received Yao Zhongming, the chief Chinese representative of the Sino-Burmese Boundary Joint Committee, along with other Chinese representatives and advisors, this morning.

During the meeting, Prime Minister Wu Nu expressed his extreme satisfaction with the progress of the Sino-Burmese Boundary Joint Committee's work. He said that the Sino-Burmese Boundary Treaty will be officially signed in Beijing on October 1st, which is an event of great historical significance.

Present at the meeting was Lei Yang, Chargé d'affaires ad interim of the Chinese Embassy in Burma.

Burmese Prime Minister's Advisor U Wang, Burmese Ambassador to China Kyao Winn, and Burmese Acting Chief Representative of the Sino-Burmese Boundary Joint Committee, Colonel Soe Min, were also present.

The conversation took place in an extremely cordial and friendly atmosphere.

Burmese Foreign Minister Sao Khun Hko also received the Chinese delegation of the Sino-Burmese Boundary Joint Committee, headed by Yao Zhongming, this morning.

Minister Sao Khun Hko greatly praised the progress of the work of the Sino-Burmese Boundary Joint Committee. He said that the friendly relations between China and Burma are now continuing to be consolidated and developed, which is extremely gratifying.

### 吴努接见中缅边界联合委员会中国代表

1960-08-25
第4版()
专栏：

    吴努接见中缅边界联合委员会中国代表
    新华社仰光24日电　缅甸总理吴努今天上午接见了中缅边界联合委员会中国首席代表姚仲明、其他中国代表和顾问。
    吴努总理在接见时对中缅边界联合委员会工作的进展表示极其满意。他说，中缅边界条约将于10月1日在北京正式签字，这是一件具有巨大历史意义的事。
    接见时在座的有中国驻缅甸大使馆临时代办雷扬。
    缅甸总理顾问吴旺、缅甸驻中国大使叫温、中缅边界联合委员会缅甸代理首席代表苏敏上校也在座。
    谈话是在极为亲切和友好的气氛中进行的。
    缅甸外交部长藻昆卓今天上午也接见了以姚仲明为首的中缅边界联合委员会的中国代表团。
    藻昆卓部长大大赞扬中缅边界联合委员会的工作的进展。他说，中国和缅甸之间的友好关系现在继续得到巩固和发展，这是极其令人满意的。
