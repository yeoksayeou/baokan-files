Title: Chairman Liu Receives Hungarian Ambassador to China
Author:
Date: 1960-08-16
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, 15th. Chairman of the People's Republic of China, Liu Shaoqi, received the newly appointed Hungarian Ambassador to China, Martin Ferenc, this afternoon. They held a cordial and friendly conversation. Present at the meeting were Vice Minister of Foreign Affairs Luo Guibo, Director of the Protocol Department Yu Peiwen, Deputy Director of the Department of Soviet and Eastern European Affairs Yu Zhan, and others.

### 刘主席接见匈牙利驻华大使

1960-08-16
第1版()
专栏：

    刘主席接见匈牙利驻华大使
    新华社15日讯　中华人民共和国主席刘少奇今天下午接见了匈牙利新任驻中国大使马尔丁·费伦茨。并进行了亲切友好的谈话。接见时在座的有，外交部副部长罗贵波、礼宾司司长俞沛文、苏联东欧司副司长余湛等。
