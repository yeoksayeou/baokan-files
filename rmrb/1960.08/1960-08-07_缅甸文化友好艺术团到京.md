Title: Burmese Cultural Friendship Art Troupe Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1960-08-07
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, August 6th. The Burmese Federal Cultural Friendship Art Troupe, consisting of sixty-three members led by head of the troupe U Ba Sein, arrived in Beijing by special plane from Kunming on the morning of the 6th, at the invitation of the Chinese People's Association for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries.

Those who went to the airport to welcome them included Zhang Zhixiang, Vice-Chairman of the Commission for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries; Xia Yan, Vice-Minister of Culture; Zhang Youyu, President of the Sino-Burmese Friendship Association; and people from all walks of life, including Zhou Weizhi, Zhao Feng, Lin Lin, Wang Yuanfang, Liu Tieshan, as well as actors from the Central National Song and Dance Ensemble who had visited the Union of Burma, and more than a hundred actors from various art troupes in the capital.

The Ambassador of the Union of Burma to China, Kyao Wynn, and diplomatic officials also went to the airport to welcome them.

### 缅甸文化友好艺术团到京

1960-08-07
第6版()
专栏：

    缅甸文化友好艺术团到京
    新华社6日讯　应中华人民共和国对外文化联络委员会邀请，来我国访问演出的缅甸联邦文化友好艺术团一行六十三人，在团长吴巴盛的率领下，6日上午由昆明乘专机到达北京。
    前往机场欢迎的有，对外文化联络委员会副主任张致祥、文化部副部长夏衍、中缅友好协会会长张友渔，各界人士周巍峙、赵沨、林林、王元方、刘铁山等以及曾访问过缅甸联邦的中央民族歌舞团的演员和首都各文艺团体的演员一百多人。
    缅甸联邦驻我国大使叫温和外交官员也到机场欢迎。
