Title: Two Cameroonian Trade Union Representatives Arrive in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1960-08-23
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, August 22nd: At the invitation of the All-China Federation of Trade Unions, Ekala Robert, secretary of the Cameroonian General Confederation of Labor and general secretary of the Commercial Union, and Ndu Isaac, head of the Construction Union and chairman of the Cameroonian Committee for the Protection of Unemployed Workers, arrived in Beijing by plane on the 21st for a visit to our country.
    Cai Yingping, deputy director of the International Liaison Department of the All-China Federation of Trade Unions, and others were at the airport to welcome them.

### 两位喀麦隆工会代表到京

1960-08-23
第6版()
专栏：

    两位喀麦隆工会代表到京
    新华社22日讯　应中华全国总工会邀请来我国访问的喀麦隆劳工总联合会书记、商业工会总书记埃克瓦拉·罗贝尔和建筑工会负责人、喀麦隆保卫失业工人委员会主席恩杜·伊萨克，21日乘飞机到达北京。
    到机场欢迎的有中华全国总工会国际联络部副部长蔡英平等人。
