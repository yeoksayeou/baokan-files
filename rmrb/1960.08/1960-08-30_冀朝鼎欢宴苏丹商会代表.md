Title: Ji Chao Ding Hosts Banquet for Sudanese Chamber of Commerce Representative
Author:
Date: 1960-08-30
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, August 29th: Ji Chao Ding, Vice Chairman of the China Council for the Promotion of International Trade, hosted a banquet this evening to welcome Sayed Ibrahim Othman Ishaq, representative of the Sudanese Chamber of Commerce.

The Sudanese guest arrived in Beijing on the 28th at the invitation of the China Council for the Promotion of International Trade.

### 冀朝鼎欢宴苏丹商会代表

1960-08-30
第5版()
专栏：

    冀朝鼎欢宴苏丹商会代表
    新华社29日讯　中国国际贸易促进委员会副主席冀朝鼎今天晚上设宴欢迎苏丹商会代表赛义德·易卜拉欣·欧斯曼·伊沙克。
    苏丹外宾是应中国国际贸易促进委员会邀请于28日到达北京的。
