Title: International Tennis Tournament Begins in Moscow
Author:
Date: 1960-08-22
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, August 21st. The traditional international tennis tournament began in Moscow at 11:00 a.m. on the 21st. Players from fourteen countries, including the Soviet Union, China, Czechoslovakia, Bulgaria, Hungary, Poland, Romania, India, Britain, Australia, and the United States, are participating in the competition. The two Chinese athletes participating in the competition are Zhu Zhenhua and Mei Fuji. The vast majority of those participating are young players. Many of them are their country’s most outstanding tennis players.

### 国际网球赛在莫斯科开始

1960-08-22
第5版()
专栏：

    国际网球赛在莫斯科开始
    新华社莫斯科21日电　传统的国际网球赛21日上午十一时起在莫斯科举行，参加这次比赛的有苏联、中国、捷克斯洛伐克、保加利亚、匈牙利、波兰、罗马尼亚、印度、英国、澳大利亚、美国等十四个国家的选手。中国参加比赛的两名运动员是朱振华和梅福基。参加这次比赛的绝大部分都是年青的选手。其中有许多都是本国最优秀的网球选手。
