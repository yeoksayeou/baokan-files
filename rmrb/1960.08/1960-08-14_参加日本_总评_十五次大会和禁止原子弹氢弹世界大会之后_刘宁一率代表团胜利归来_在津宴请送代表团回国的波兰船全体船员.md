Title: After Attending the 15th Congress of the Japanese "Sohyo" and the World Conference Against Atomic and Hydrogen Bombs, Liu Ningyi Led a Delegation to a Triumphant Return. Held a Banquet in Tianjin to Entertain All Crew Members of the Polish Ship That Returned the Delegation Home
Author:
Date: 1960-08-14
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Tianjin, August 13th. The Chinese delegation of thirteen members, led by Liu Ningyi, which attended the 15th National Congress of the General Council of Trade Unions of Japan (Sohyo) and the Sixth World Conference against Atomic and Hydrogen Bombs and for Disarmament, returned to Tianjin from Japan today aboard a Polish ship.

Liao Chengzhi, Chairman of the Chinese Committee for Asian-African Solidarity, Kang Yonghe, Secretary of the All-China Federation of Trade Unions, Gao Shuxun, Chairman of the Hebei Branch of the Chinese People's Committee for World Peace, Wang Kangzhi, Secretary of the Tianjin Municipal Committee, Zhou Shutao, Deputy Mayor of Tianjin, and Wang Weiqi, Vice Chairman of the Hebei Provincial Federation of Trade Unions, warmly welcomed the delegation's triumphant return at Tianjin New Port.

Polish Chargé d'Affaires ad interim to China, Mr. Flato, and Mr. and Mrs. Kenzo Nakajima, Director-General of the Japan-China Cultural Exchange Association, who are currently visiting our country, also came to New Port to welcome the delegation.

Zheng Senyu and another representative from the World Peace Council who went to Tokyo to attend the Sixth World Conference against Atomic and Hydrogen Bombs and for Disarmament, also returned to Tianjin on the same ship.

Xinhua News Agency, Tianjin, August 13th. Liu Ningyi, head of the Chinese delegation to the 15th Congress of the Japanese "Sohyo" and the Sixth World Conference against Atomic and Hydrogen Bombs and for Disarmament, hosted a banquet in Tianjin tonight to entertain the captain and all crew members of the Polish ship that returned the delegation home.

Polish Chargé d'Affaires ad interim to China, Mr. Flato, and Mr. and Mrs. Kenzo Nakajima, Director-General of the Japan-China Cultural Exchange Association, who are currently visiting China, were invited to attend the banquet. (Picture attached)

Liu Ningyi shaking hands with Liao Chengzhi, Chairman of the Chinese Committee for Asian-African Solidarity, who came to welcome him.

### 参加日本“总评”十五次大会和禁止原子弹氢弹世界大会之后  刘宁一率代表团胜利归来  在津宴请送代表团回国的波兰船全体船员

1960-08-14
第1版()
专栏：

    参加日本“总评”十五次大会和禁止原子弹氢弹世界大会之后
    刘宁一率代表团胜利归来
    在津宴请送代表团回国的波兰船全体船员
    新华社天津13日电　由刘宁一率领的参加日本工会总评议会第十五次全国大会和第六届禁止原子弹氢弹和争取裁军世界大会的中国代表团一行十三人，今天从日本乘波兰船回到天津。
    中国亚非团结委员会主席廖承志、中华全国总工会书记康永和以及中国人民保卫世界和平委员会河北分会主席高树勋、天津市委书记王亢之、天津市副市长周叔弢、河北省总工会副主席王维奇等到天津新港热烈欢迎代表团的胜利归来。
    波兰驻华大使馆临时代办傅拉托和正在我国访问的日中文化交流协会理事长中岛健藏夫妇也到新港欢迎代表团。
    前往东京参加第六届禁止原子弹氢弹和争取裁军世界大会的世界和平理事会代表郑森禹等二人也同船回到天津。
    新华社天津13日电　参加日本“总评”十五次大会和第六届禁止原子弹氢弹和争取裁军世界大会的中国代表团团长刘宁一今晚在天津设宴招待送代表团回国的波兰船船长和全体船员。
    波兰驻华临时代办傅拉托和正在访华的日中文化交流协会理事长中岛健藏夫妇应邀出席了宴会。（附图片）
