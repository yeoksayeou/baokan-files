Title: Leaders of the Japan Socialist Party Meet with Chinese Delegation; All-Japan Buddhist Association Hosts Banquet for Zhao Puchu
Author:
Date: 1960-08-11
Page: 5
Xinhua News Agency, Tokyo, August 10th: As Tokyo dispatches report: Asanuma Inejirō, Chairman of the Japan Socialist Party, advisors Suzuki Mosaburō and Kazami Akira, International Bureau Chief Sata Tadataka, Propaganda Bureau Chief Katsumada Seiichi, and National Movement Committee Chairman Tanaka Toshiomi, met today with Liu Ningyi, head of the Chinese delegation attending the World Conference Against Atomic and Hydrogen Bombs, deputy head Zhao Anbo, and deputy secretary-general Sun Pinghua.

During the meeting, the Japanese friends discussed with Liu Ningyi the issues of opposing the Japan-US military alliance treaty and strengthening friendly relations between the peoples of Japan and China.

The meeting took place in a friendly atmosphere.

Xinhua News Agency, Tokyo, August 10th: The All-Japan Buddhist Association held a grand banquet today to entertain Zhao Puchu, deputy head of the Chinese delegation to the Sixth World Conference Against Atomic and Hydrogen Bombs and for General Disarmament, and vice president of the Buddhist Association of China.

Leaders of various sects of Japanese Buddhism attended the banquet.

Prominent Japanese Buddhist leader Otani Eijun made a special trip from Kyoto to attend this welcoming banquet.

The banquet took place in a very warm and friendly atmosphere.

### 日本社会党领导人  会见中国代表团  全日本佛教会盛宴招待赵朴初

1960-08-11
第5版()
专栏：

    日本社会党领导人
    会见中国代表团
    全日本佛教会盛宴招待赵朴初
    新华社10日讯　东京消息：日本社会党委员长浅沼稻次郎，顾问铃木茂三郎和风见章，国际局长佐多忠隆，宣教局长胜间田清一，国民运动委员长田中稔男等今天会见了出席禁止原子弹氢弹世界大会的中国代表团团长刘宁一、副团长赵安博和副秘书长孙平化。
    会见时，日本朋友同刘宁一交谈了反对日美军事同盟条约和加强日中人民友好关系的问题。
    会见在友好气氛中进行。
    新华社10日讯　东京消息：全日本佛教会今天举行盛大宴会，招待出席第六届禁止原子弹氢弹和争取全面裁军世界大会的中国代表团副团长、中国佛教协会副会长赵朴初。
    日本佛教的各派领袖出席了宴会。
    日本著名佛教界领袖大谷莹润特地从京都赶来参加这次欢迎宴会。
    宴会在十分热烈友好的气氛中进行。
