Title: Romanian Armed Forces Minister General Sălăjan Sends Congratulatory Message on China's Army Day
Author: 
Date: 1960-08-02
Page: 6
Xinhua News Agency, August 1st. Comrade Lin Biao, Vice Premier of the State Council and Minister of National Defense of the People's Republic of China, today received a telegram from General Leontin Sălăjan, Minister of the Armed Forces of the Romanian People's Republic, congratulating the 33rd anniversary of the founding of the Chinese People's Liberation Army. The full text is as follows:
To Comrade Lin Biao, Vice Premier of the State Council and Minister of National Defense of the People's Republic of China, Marshal of the People's Republic of China:

Dear Comrade Marshal,

On the occasion of the glorious 33rd anniversary of the founding of the Chinese People's Liberation Army, I extend to you the warmest congratulations on behalf of the soldiers of the Armed Forces of the Romanian People's Republic and in my own name.

The solid friendship based on common interests and goals unites the people and armies of Romania and China with the armies of all countries in the great socialist camp, firmly defending the independence and sovereignty of our countries and world peace.

I wish the glorious soldiers of the Chinese People's Liberation Army new achievements in military training and political education, and in the cause of enhancing the defense capabilities of the People's Republic of China.

Leontin Sălăjan,
Minister of the Armed Forces of the Romanian People's Republic
Bucharest, July 31, 1960

### 罗武装部队部长萨拉然大将  电贺我国建军节

1960-08-02
第6版()
专栏：

    罗武装部队部长萨拉然大将
    电贺我国建军节
    新华社1日讯　我国防部长林彪元帅，今天接到罗马尼亚人民共和国武装部队部长列·萨拉然大将祝贺中国人民解放军建军三十三周年的电报。全文如下：中华人民共和国国务院副总理兼国防部长中华人民共和国元帅林彪同志：
    敬爱的元帅同志：
    值此光荣的中国人民解放军建军三十三周年之际，我代表罗马尼亚人民共和国武装部队的军人们，并以我个人名义向您致以最热烈的祝贺。
    以共同利益和目标为基础的巩固的友谊把罗、中两国人民及其军队同伟大的社会主义阵营各国军队连结在一起，坚强地保卫我们各国的独立和主权及世界和平。
    祝中国人民解放军的光荣的军人们在军事训练和政治教育中，在提高中华人民共和国国防威力的事业中取得新的成就。
    罗马尼亚人民共和国武装部队
      部长　列·萨拉然大将
    1960年7月31日于布加勒斯特
