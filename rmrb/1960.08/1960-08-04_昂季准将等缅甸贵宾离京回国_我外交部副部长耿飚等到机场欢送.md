Title: Brigadier General Aung Gyi and Other Burmese Guests Leave Beijing for Home; Vice Foreign Minister Geng Biao and Others See Them Off at the Airport
Author:
Date: 1960-08-04
Page: 6

    Xinhua News Agency, August 3rd. Brigadier General Aung Gyi, the chief representative of Burma attending the second meeting of the Sino-Burmese Joint Boundary Committee, along with the representatives, advisors, and staff of the Burmese delegation, left Beijing by plane this morning, heading home via Guangzhou.

    Seeing them off at the airport were Vice Foreign Minister Geng Biao; General Zhang Aiping, Deputy Chief of the General Staff of the Chinese People's Liberation Army; General Gan Siqi, Deputy Director of the General Political Department of the Chinese People's Liberation Army; Major General Wang Xinggang, Deputy Director of the General Office of the Ministry of National Defense; Li Yimang, our country's ambassador to Burma; Shen Ping, Deputy Director of the First Asian Affairs Department of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs; Zhang Youyu, President of the Sino-Burmese Friendship Association; and Xin Yi, Director of the Foreign Affairs Office of the Beijing Municipal People's Committee, among others.

    Also seeing them off at the airport were Yao Zhongming, the chief representative of China on the Sino-Burmese Joint Boundary Committee, as well as representatives and advisors of the Chinese delegation.

    The Burmese Ambassador to our country, Kyaw Winn, and diplomatic officials from the embassy also came to the airport to see them off.

    On the morning of the 2nd, the chief representatives of both sides signed the documents adopted at the second meeting of the Sino-Burmese Joint Boundary Committee.

    During their meeting in Beijing, the Burmese delegation visited people's communes, schools, the Military Museum, the Ming Tombs Reservoir, as well as new buildings and scenic spots in Beijing.

### 昂季准将等缅甸贵宾离京回国  我外交部副部长耿飚等到机场欢送

1960-08-04
第6版()
专栏：

    昂季准将等缅甸贵宾离京回国
    我外交部副部长耿飚等到机场欢送
    新华社3日讯　出席中缅边界联合委员会第二次会议的缅甸首席代表昂季准将，以及缅甸代表团的代表、顾问和工作人员，今天上午乘飞机离开北京，取道广州回国。
    到飞机场欢送的，有外交部副部长耿飚，中国人民解放军副总参谋长张爱萍上将，中国人民解放军总政治部副主任甘泗淇上将，国防部办公厅副主任王兴纲少将，我国驻缅甸大使李一氓，外交部第一亚洲司副司长沈平，中缅友好协会会长张友渔和北京市人民委员会外事处处长辛毅等。
    到飞机场欢送的，还有中缅边界联合委员会中国首席代表姚仲明，以及中国代表团代表、顾问等。
    缅甸驻我国大使叫温和大使馆的外交官员也到飞机场送行。
    2日早晨，双方首席代表在中缅边界联合委员会第二次会议所通过的文件上签了字。
    缅甸代表团在北京参加会议期间，曾经参观访问了人民公社、学校、军事博物馆、十三陵水库，以及北京市的新建筑物和名胜古迹。
