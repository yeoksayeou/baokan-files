Title: Chinese Cultural Delegation Arrives in Warsaw
Author:
Date: 1960-08-27
Page: 5
    Xinhua News Agency, Warsaw, August 26th. At the invitation of the Polish Minister of Culture and Art, the Chinese Cultural Delegation headed by Shen Yanbing, Minister of Culture of China and President of the Sino-Polish Friendship Association, arrived in Warsaw by plane at noon today for a friendly visit.  Galiński, the Polish Minister of Culture and Art, Makowski, the Director of the Polish Foreign Cultural Cooperation Bureau, Żukiewicz, a member of the Presidium of the Polish-Chinese Friendship Association, and others greeted the delegation at the airport. Wang Bingnan, the Chinese Ambassador to Poland, also went to the airport to welcome them.

### 中国文化代表团抵华沙

1960-08-27
第5版()
专栏：

    中国文化代表团抵华沙
    新华社华沙26日电　应波兰文化艺术部长的邀请，以中国文化部长、中波友协主席沈雁冰为首的中国文化代表团，今天中午乘飞机到达华沙进行友好访问。到机场迎接代表团的有波兰文化艺术部部长加林斯基，波兰对外文化合作局局长马科夫斯基，波中友协主席团委员茹凯夫斯基等人。中国驻波兰大使王炳南也前往机场欢迎。
