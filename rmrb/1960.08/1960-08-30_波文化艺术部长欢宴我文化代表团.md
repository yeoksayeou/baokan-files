Title: Polish Minister of Culture and Art Hosts Chinese Cultural Delegation

Author: 
Date: 1960-08-30
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, Warsaw, August 29th: The Chinese Cultural Delegation, led by Minister Shen Yanbing, is currently visiting Poland. In recent days, they have been touring and visiting Warsaw, and have been warmly received by the Polish side.

The Chinese Cultural Delegation arrived in Warsaw on the 26th. That afternoon, the Polish Minister of Culture and Art, Galinski, hosted a banquet to welcome the delegation. Minister Galinski and Minister Shen Yanbing delivered speeches during the banquet.

Galinski expressed a heartfelt welcome to the Chinese Cultural Delegation. He also spoke about his recent visit to China. He said, "During our visit to China, we were deeply impressed by the tremendous achievements and great work of the Chinese people in economic construction, as well as the warm reception the Chinese people extended to us." Shen Yanbing said in his speech, "We are very excited to be able to visit our brother country, the heroic and beautiful city of Warsaw." He said: Poland has a long and glorious culture, and in the past sixteen years, Poland's socialist culture and art have greatly developed. He congratulated Poland on the greater prosperity of its socialist culture and art.

On the 28th, the delegation visited the National Museum and the Polish Art Photography Exhibition. In the evening, Galinski hosted a special musical evening for the delegation at the Stanisław Theatre in Łazienki Park, where the Warsaw City People's Chorus and Polish singers and pianists gave excellent performances.

### 波文化艺术部长欢宴我文化代表团

1960-08-30
第5版()
专栏：

    波文化艺术部长欢宴我文化代表团
    新华社华沙29日电　正在波兰访问的中国文化代表团在沈雁冰部长的率领下连日来在华沙进行参观访问，受到了波兰方面的热烈招待。
    中国文化代表团是在26日到达华沙的。当天下午，波兰文化艺术部部长加林斯基就设宴招待了代表团，加林斯基部长和沈雁冰部长在席间相继发表了讲话。
    加林斯基表示衷心地欢迎中国文化代表团的访问。他还谈到了前不久访问中国的情况。他说：“我们在中国访问时，中国人民在经济建设方面的巨大成就和巨大工作以及中国人民给予我们的热情接待，给我们留下了深刻的印象。”沈雁冰致词说：“我们能够到我们的兄弟国家，到英雄而美丽的城市——华沙来访问，感到十分兴奋。”他说：波兰有着悠久的辉煌的文化，十六年来，波兰社会主义的文化艺术更有了很大的发展。他祝贺波兰的社会主义文化艺术更加繁荣昌盛。
    28日代表团参观了民族博物馆和波兰艺术摄影展览会。晚间，加林斯基在拉津卡公园的斯坦尼斯拉夫剧院专门为代表团举办了音乐晚会，会上由华沙市人民合唱团及波兰的歌唱家、钢琴家们作了精湛的表演。
