Title: The Secretary Comes to Our Team

Author:

Date: 1960-08-11
Page: 4

The secretary comes to our team,
He's just like us in everything.
We go to weed, he weeds,
We go to transplant rice seedlings, he transplants rice seedlings.
We go to the canteen to eat,
The secretary sits at our table.
The whistle for study blows,
The secretary explains the policies.
When labor ends, we have an evening party,
The secretary sings a Han Erhuang opera.
The secretary comes to our team,
He gives us ideas on everything.
        (Transferred from Shaanxi Daily)

### 书记来到咱队上

1960-08-11
第4版()
专栏：

    书记来到咱队上
    书记来到咱队上，
    处处和咱一个样。
    咱去锄草他锄草，
    咱去薅秧他薅秧。
    咱到食堂去吃饭，
    书记坐在咱席上。
    学习哨子一声响，
    书记就把政策讲。
    劳动结束开晚会，
    书记唱起汉二簧。
    书记来到咱队上，
    事事给咱出主张。
        （转自陕西日报）
