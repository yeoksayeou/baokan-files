Title: Korean Film Delegation Leaves Beijing for Northeast Visit
Author:
Date: 1960-08-29
Page: 4

    Xinhua News Agency, August 28th: The five-member Korean film delegation, invited to China to participate in the Korean Film Week activities, left by train for Northeast China this afternoon. Xia Yan, Vice Minister of Culture, Si Tu Huimin and Wang Dong, Deputy Directors of the Film Bureau of the Ministry of Culture, relevant department heads from the film industry, and film industry personnel saw them off at the station.
    The delegation visited the four cities of Beijing, Jinan, Shanghai, and Hangzhou. They participated in various celebration activities for the 15th anniversary of the liberation of Korea and the opening ceremony of Korean Film Week, and held meetings with Chinese film audiences. They visited film enterprises in various places, held discussions with leaders of China's film industry and film industry personnel, and exchanged experiences. On the evening of August 25, the delegation also signed the "China-Korea Mutual Purchase of Film Distribution Rights Contract" with the China Film Distribution and Projection Corporation. Vice Minister of Culture Xia Yan and others attended the signing ceremony. Vice Minister Xia Yan also held a farewell banquet for the Korean film delegation.
    The delegation will return home after visiting Changchun and Shenyang in Northeast China.

### 朝鲜电影代表团离京去东北访问

1960-08-29
第4版()
专栏：

    朝鲜电影代表团离京去东北访问
    新华社28日讯　应邀来我国参加朝鲜电影周活动的朝鲜电影代表团一行五人，已于今天下午乘火车去东北。前往车站送行的有文化部副部长夏衍、文化部电影局副局长司徒慧敏和王栋及电影界各有关部门的负责人和电影界人士。
    代表团曾访问了北京、济南、上海、杭州四个城市。参加了庆祝朝鲜解放十五周年的各项庆祝活动和朝鲜电影周开幕式，并和我国电影观众举行了见面会。他们在各地参观了电影事业，和我国电影事业领导人及电影界人士进行了座谈，交流了经验。8月25日晚，代表团还与中国电影发行放映公司签订了“中朝互购电影发行权合同”，文化部夏衍副部长等出席了签字仪式。夏衍副部长还为朝鲜电影代表团举行了欢送宴会。
    代表团将在东北访问长春和沈阳之后回国。
