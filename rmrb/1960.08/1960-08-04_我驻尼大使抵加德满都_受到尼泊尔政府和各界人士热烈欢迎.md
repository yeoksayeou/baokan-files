Title: Our Ambassador to Nepal Arrives in Kathmandu, Warmly Welcomed by the Nepalese Government and People from All Walks of Life
Author:
Date: 1960-08-04
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, Kathmandu, August 3rd: Zhang Shijie, the Chinese Ambassador to the Kingdom of Nepal, arrived in Kathmandu by plane on the 2nd and was warmly welcomed by the Nepalese government and people from all walks of life. Ambassador Zhang Shijie is also the chief representative of the Chinese side of the Sino-Nepalese Joint Boundary Commission.
    Brigadier General Mo. Wei. Xia, the military secretary of the King of Nepal, and the Director of Protocol of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs greeted Ambassador Zhang Shijie at the airport. Purna Bahadur, President of the Nepal-China Friendship Association, and members of the Association, representatives of Nepalese farmers, youth students, women's organizations, and the Kathmandu Municipal Committee, as well as Nepalese people from all walks of life who had visited China, all went to the airport to welcome him.
    Soviet Ambassador Zabrokin and the First Secretary of the Indian Embassy were also present at the airport to welcome him.
    Ambassador Zhang Shijie reviewed the guard of honor at the airport and accepted bouquets from people from all walks of life.

### 我驻尼大使抵加德满都  受到尼泊尔政府和各界人士热烈欢迎

1960-08-04
第1版()
专栏：

    我驻尼大使抵加德满都
    受到尼泊尔政府和各界人士热烈欢迎
    新华社3日讯　加德满都消息：中国驻尼泊尔王国大使张世杰在2日乘飞机到达加德满都，受到了尼泊尔政府和各界人士的热烈欢迎。张世杰大使也是中尼边界联合委员会中国方面的首席代表。
    尼泊尔国王军事秘书莫·维·夏准将和外交部交际司长在机场迎接张世杰大使。尼中友好协会会长普尔纳·巴哈杜尔和友协的委员，尼泊尔的农民、青年学生、妇女组织和加德满都市政委员会的代表，以及曾经访问过中国的尼泊尔各界人士都到机场欢迎。
    到机场欢迎的还有苏联大使扎勃罗金和印度大使馆的一等秘书。
    张世杰大使在机场上检阅了仪仗队，并且接受了各界人士的献花。
