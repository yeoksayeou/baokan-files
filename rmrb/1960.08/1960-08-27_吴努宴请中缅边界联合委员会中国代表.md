Title: Wu Nu Hosts Chinese Representatives of the Sino-Burmese Boundary Joint Committee
Author:
Date: 1960-08-27
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency, Rangoon, August 25th: Burmese Prime Minister Wu Nu hosted a banquet here tonight to entertain the Chinese delegation, headed by Yao Zhongming, attending the third meeting of the Sino-Burmese Boundary Joint Committee.

    Among those present at the banquet were Burmese Chief Justice of the Supreme Court U Myint Thein, Speaker of the Chamber of Nationalities Sao Khun Kyi, Speaker of the Chamber of Deputies Mann Ba Sae, Minister of Finance Thakin Tin, Minister of Justice U E Maung, Minister of Trade Development U Tun, Minister of Kachin State Affairs Sama Duwa Sinwa Nawng, former Foreign Minister U Chan Tun Aung, and other senior government officials. Burmese Ambassador to China Kyaw Winn, Burmese Chief Representative to the Sino-Burmese Boundary Joint Committee Brigadier General Aung Gyi, as well as Burmese representatives and advisors participating in the Joint Committee, also attended the banquet. Lei Yang, Chargé d'Affaires ad interim of the Chinese Embassy in Burma, Colonel Jiang Keding, Military Attaché, and other officials of the Chinese Embassy also attended the banquet. The banquet was held in a warm and friendly atmosphere.

    After the banquet, the Chinese guests, accompanied by Prime Minister Wu Nu and the Burmese hosts, watched a Burmese film.

### 吴努宴请中缅边界联合委员会中国代表

1960-08-27
第4版()
专栏：

    吴努宴请中缅边界联合委员会中国代表
    新华社仰光25日电　缅甸总理吴努今天晚上在这里举行宴会，招待以姚仲明为首的参加中缅边界联合委员会第三次会议的中国代表团。
    出席宴会的有缅甸最高法院首席法官吴敏登、民族院议长藻昆基、代表院议长曼巴赛、财政部长德钦丁、司法部长吴埃貌、贸易发展部长吴敦、克钦邦事务部长沙马杜瓦信瓦瑙、前外交部长吴千吞昂，以及其他政府高级官员。缅甸驻中国大使叫温，参加中缅边界联合委员会的缅甸首席代表昂季准将以及参加联合委员会的缅甸代表和顾问也出席了宴会。出席宴会的还有中国驻缅甸大使馆临时代办雷扬、武官蒋克定上校和中国大使馆的其他官员。宴会是在热烈和友好的气氛中举行的。
    在宴会结束后，中国客人在吴努总理和缅甸主人陪同下观看了缅甸影片。
