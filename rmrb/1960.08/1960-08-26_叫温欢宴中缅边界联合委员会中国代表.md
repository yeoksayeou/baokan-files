Title: Kyaw Winn Hosts Banquet for Chinese Representatives of the Sino-Burmese Joint Boundary Committee
Author:
Date: 1960-08-26
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Rangoon, August 24th: Kyaw Winn, Deputy Head of the Burmese Delegation to the Sino-Burmese Joint Boundary Committee and Burmese Ambassador to China, hosted a banquet here on the evening of the 24th to welcome Yao Zhongming, Chief Chinese Representative to the Third Meeting of the Sino-Burmese Joint Boundary Committee, as well as other Chinese representatives and advisors.

    The Burmese side present at the banquet included Colonel So Min, Director of the Border Areas Administration Bureau; Colonel La Aung, Director of the Survey Bureau; Colonel Ba Than, Director of the Army Psychological Warfare and Education Bureau; U Khin Nyo, Joint Secretary of the Border Areas Administration Bureau; U San Lun, Secretary of the Kachin State Affairs Department; and U Kun Aung Kya, Commissioner of the Northern Shan State.

    Lei Yang, Chargé d'affaires ad interim of the Chinese Embassy in Burma, Colonel Jiang Keding, Military Attaché, and other staff members also attended the banquet.

    The banquet was filled with a cordial and friendly atmosphere, with guests frequently toasting Sino-Burmese friendship and the comprehensive settlement of the Sino-Burmese border issue. After the banquet, the Chinese guests were invited to watch a movie.

### 叫温欢宴中缅边界联合委员会中国代表

1960-08-26
第4版()
专栏：

    叫温欢宴中缅边界联合委员会中国代表
    新华社仰光24日电　中缅边界联合委员会缅甸代表团副团长、缅甸驻中国大使叫温24日晚在这里举行宴会，欢迎出席中缅边界联合委员会第三次会议的中国首席代表姚仲明以及其他中国代表和顾问。
    缅甸方面出席宴会的有边境地区管理局局长苏敏上校、勘察局局长拉昂上校、陆军心理战和教育局局长巴丹上校、边境地区管理局联合秘书吴钦纽、克钦邦事务部秘书吴山伦和北掸邦专员吴昆翁佳。
    中国驻缅甸大使馆临时代办雷扬、武官蒋克定上校和其他工作人员也出席了宴会。
    宴会上，充满亲切友好的气氛，宾主双方频频为中缅友谊和中缅边界问题的全面解决而干杯。在宴会以后，中国客人应邀看了电影。
