Title: Chinese Art Troupe Continues Performances in Canada
Author: 
Date: 1960-08-23
Page: 6
Xinhua News Agency, Calgary, 22nd: The Chinese Art Troupe held two performances at the Jubilee Auditorium in Calgary, Canada's oil city, on the 17th and 18th. This is the second city the Chinese Art Troupe has visited in Canada. The troupe's performances here were very successful.

    The largest local newspaper, the "Calgary Herald," gave high praise to every program and the artists' performances. The newspaper said that the Chinese people broke down all barriers—political, ideological, and racial. Another newspaper, "The Albertan," said, "The language of art is universal, and no artificial curtain can prevent it from conveying ideas."

    After the first performance, the Chinese Art Troupe held a reception at the Calgary Allied Arts Centre, attended by Calgary municipal officials, the president of the University of Alberta, prominent local figures from cultural and artistic circles, industrialists, and overseas Chinese. The guests unanimously and enthusiastically praised the excellent performances of the Chinese Art Troupe.

    The Chinese Art Troupe arrived in Calgary from Vancouver on the 15th. The next day, Calgary Mayor Harry Hays hosted a luncheon to entertain the head of the art troupe, Chen Zhongjing, and other leaders.

    During its visit to Calgary, the art troupe visited a famous large ranch and the local Museum of Practical Art of Indians and Eskimos, and also visited Canada's beautiful Banff National Park and Lake Louise. The art troupe's next performance location is Edmonton.

### 中国艺术团继续在加拿大演出

1960-08-23
第6版()
专栏：

    中国艺术团继续在加拿大演出
    新华社22日讯　卡尔加利（加拿大）消息：中国艺术团17日和18日在加拿大的石油城市卡尔加利的欢乐礼堂举行了两场演出。这是中国艺术团在加拿大访问的第二个城市。艺术团在这里的演出非常成功。
    当地最大的报纸“卡尔加利先驱报”对每一项节目和艺术家的表演都给予了很高的评价。报纸说，中国人打破了一切壁垒——政治、思想意识和种族的壁垒。另一家报纸“阿尔伯达人报”说，“艺术的语言是普遍的，任何人为的帷幕都不能阻止它传递思想。”
    在第一次演出结束以后，中国艺术团在卡尔加利联合艺术中心举行了招待会，出席的有卡尔加利市政官员，阿尔伯达大学校长，当地的著名文化界和艺术界人士、工业家和华侨。来宾一致热烈地赞扬中国艺术团的优美表演。
    中国艺术团是在15日从温哥华到达卡尔加利的。第二天卡尔加利市长哈里·海斯设午宴招待艺术团团长陈忠经和其它领导人。
    艺术团在卡尔加利访问期间参观了一个著名的大牧场和当地的印地安人和爱斯基摩人实用艺术博物馆，还参观了加拿大美丽的班夫国家公园和路易斯湖。艺术团下一个访问演出地点是埃德蒙吞。
