Title: Sihanouk Discusses the Laotian Situation: Laotian People Demand Peace and Neutrality
Author:
Date: 1960-08-18
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, Phnom Penh, August 16th: Prince Sihanouk presided over the groundbreaking ceremony for the Phnom Penh-Sihanoukville railway project this morning. In his speech to the railway workers, Sihanouk emphasized that it is necessary to strive for economic independence, thereby enabling Cambodia to break away from the dependency caused by foreign aid.

Speaking of the Laotian situation, Sihanouk said that the Laotian coup demonstrates the Laotian people's desire for peace and neutrality.

### 西哈努克谈老挝局势  老挝人民要求和平中立

1960-08-18
第5版()
专栏：

    西哈努克谈老挝局势
    老挝人民要求和平中立
    新华社金边16日电　西哈努克亲王今天上午主持金边—西哈努克城铁路工程开工典礼。在向筑路工人的讲话时，西哈努克强调指出，必须争取实现经济独立，从而使柬埔寨摆脱外援所造成的从属地位。
    谈到老挝局势时，西哈努克说，老挝政变表明了老挝人民的和平和中立的愿望。
