Title: World Conference Against Atomic and Hydrogen Bombs Grandly Opens on the 15th Anniversary of the US Atomic Bombing of Hiroshima
Author:
Date: 1960-08-07
Page: 1

    On the 15th Anniversary of the US Atomic Bombing of Hiroshima
    World Conference Against Atomic and Hydrogen Bombs Grandly Opens
    Xinhua News Agency, Tokyo, 6th - The Sixth World Conference Against Atomic and Hydrogen Bombs and for Complete Disarmament officially opened in Tokyo today—the 15th anniversary of the US atomic bombing of Hiroshima City, Japan.
    11,000 Japanese delegates and 130 foreign delegates attended the conference.
    The conference heard the general report by Ikuo Yasui, Executive Director of the Japan Council Against Atomic and Hydrogen Bombs; speeches by foreign delegates; reports submitted to the conference by the three group meetings of the preparatory meeting; and reports from representatives of the Japanese People's Peace March participants and Japanese atomic bomb victims.
    The conference also read congratulatory telegrams and messages from various organizations at home and abroad.

### 在广岛遭受美国原子弹轰炸十五周年纪念日  禁止原子弹氢弹世界大会隆重开幕

1960-08-07
第1版()
专栏：

    在广岛遭受美国原子弹轰炸十五周年纪念日
    禁止原子弹氢弹世界大会隆重开幕
    新华社6日讯　东京消息：第六届禁止原子弹氢弹和争取全面裁军世界大会正式会议今天——日本广岛市遭受美国原子弹轰炸十五周年纪念日——在东京隆重开幕。
    出席大会的有一万一千名日本代表和一百三十名外国代表。
    大会听取了日本禁止原子弹和氢弹协议会理事长安井郁作的总报告、外国代表的发言、预备会议的三个分组会向大会提出的报告以及日本人民和平大游行参加者的代表、日本原子弹受害者的报告。
    大会还宣读国内外各种组织的贺电和贺词。
