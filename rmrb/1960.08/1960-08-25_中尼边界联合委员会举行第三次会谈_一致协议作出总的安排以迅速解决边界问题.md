Title: Sino-Nepalese Joint Boundary Committee Holds Third Meeting; Unanimously Agrees on Overall Arrangements to Quickly Resolve Boundary Issues
Author: 
Date: 1960-08-25
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Kathmandu, August 24th: The third meeting of the first session of the Sino-Nepalese Joint Boundary Committee was held at the Singha Durbar in Kathmandu, the capital of the Kingdom of Nepal, from 10:00 AM to 12:00 PM on August 22nd. The various issues were discussed in a friendly and cordial atmosphere.

The two sides reached a unanimous agreement on making overall arrangements to quickly resolve the boundary issue, and decided to appoint experts to meet before the next meeting of the Joint Committee to discuss how to implement specific tasks and make recommendations to the Joint Committee.

The next meeting will be held at 10:00 AM on Tuesday, August 30th.

### 中尼边界联合委员会举行第三次会谈  一致协议作出总的安排以迅速解决边界问题

1960-08-25
第4版()
专栏：

    中尼边界联合委员会举行第三次会谈
    一致协议作出总的安排以迅速解决边界问题
    新华社24日讯　加德满都消息：中尼边界联合委员会第一次会议第三次会谈22日上午十时到十二时在尼泊尔王国首都加德满都的狮宫举行，并在友好融洽的气氛中讨论了各个问题。
    双方就作出总的安排以迅速解决边界问题一事取得了一致协议，并且决定任命专家在联合委员会下次举行会谈之前会晤，以讨论如何执行具体任务并向联合委员会提出建议的问题。
    下次会谈将于8月30日（星期二）上午十时举行。
