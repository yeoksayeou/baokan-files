Title: Brigadier Aung Gyi and his party return to Rangoon
Author:
Date: 1960-08-09
Page: 5
Xinhua News Agency, Rangoon, August 8th: Brigadier Aung Gyi, the chief Burmese representative to the Second Meeting of the Sino-Burmese Joint Boundary Committee, as well as the representatives, advisors, and staff of the Burmese delegation, returned here this morning. The Second Meeting of the Sino-Burmese Joint Boundary Committee was held in Beijing from July 25th to August 1st.
    Among those welcoming the Burmese representatives at the airport were Brigadier General Dan Pe (Navy) Deputy Chief of Staff of Burma, and Colonel Kin Nyunt, Lieutenant Colonel Kyi Han, Lieutenant Colonel La Min, Lieutenant Colonel Ye Gaung; Permanent Secretary of the Burmese Ministry of Foreign Affairs James Barrington, Director of the Asian Department U Ba Chit, Director of the Protocol Department U Ba Maung, Director of the Consular Department U Soe Saing Toe, and others.
    Also welcoming the Burmese representatives at the airport were Lei Yang, Chargé d'affaires ad interim of the Chinese Embassy in the Union of Burma, Colonel Jiang Keding, Military Attaché, and the counselors of the Chinese Embassy in Burma.

### 昂季准将一行回到仰光

1960-08-09
第5版()
专栏：

    昂季准将一行回到仰光
    新华社仰光8日电　出席中缅边界联合委员会第二次会议的缅甸首席代表昂季准将，以及缅甸代表团的代表、顾问和工作人员，今天上午回到这里。中缅边界联合委员会第二次会议是从7月25日到8月1日在北京举行的。
    在机场欢迎缅甸代表回国的有缅甸（海军）副参谋长丹佩准将和钦纽上校、季汉中校、拉敏中校、耶埃中校；缅甸外交部常任秘书詹姆斯·巴林顿、亚洲司司长吴巴漆、礼宾司司长吴巴茂、领事司司长吴索多赛托等。
    在机场上欢迎缅甸代表的还有中国驻缅甸联邦大使馆临时代办雷扬、武官蒋克定上校和中国驻缅甸大使馆的参赞们。
