Title: Zhou Erfu Hosts a Banquet for Latin American Guests
Author:
Date: 1960-08-19
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, August 18th: Zhou Erfu, Vice President of the Chinese Latin American Friendship Association, hosted a banquet this evening to welcome Latin American guests who are currently visiting China. These guests, invited by the Chinese Latin American Friendship Association, are Professor Caio da Silva Prado Júnior, an economist from Brazil, and his wife; a Brazilian teachers' union delegation consisting of V. Borborema Bôrdo, First Secretary of the Rio de Janeiro Teachers' Union, and Eurico Baptista Aranha Miranda, Secretary of the Brazilian Inter-State Teachers' Federation, who are visiting China at the invitation of the National Committee of the Educational Workers' Union of China; a delegation from the Cuban National Institute for Teachers and Equivalent Teachers' Society headed by Leslie Rodriguez Aguilera, President of the Institute; Ciro Maldonado, President of the Ecuadorian National Union of Educational Workers; and a Mexican teachers' union delegation consisting of Jorge Cruickshank García, President of the National Publishing Committee of the National Union of Education Workers of Mexico and Vice President of the International Teachers' Union, and Francisco Mora Pérez of the National Union of Education Workers of Mexico; and Lidia Díaz, President of the Montevideo Teachers' Union of Uruguay.

At the banquet, Zhou Erfu said that the storm of Latin American revolution has risen. The Cuban Revolution is a brilliant example for Latin America and has received the support of the people of China and the world.

Leslie Rodriguez Aguilera, head of the Cuban National Teachers' Society delegation, also spoke at the banquet. He praised the achievements of China's socialist revolution and socialist construction, and said that the Cuban people and the Chinese people are advancing on the same road against U.S. imperialism, and the friendship between the people of Cuba and China is unbreakable.

### 周而复欢宴拉丁美洲客人

1960-08-19
第6版()
专栏：

    周而复欢宴拉丁美洲客人
    据新华社18日讯　中国拉丁美洲友好协会副会长周而复今晚举行宴会，欢迎正在我国访问的拉丁美洲客人。这些客人是应中国拉丁美洲友好协会邀请的巴西经济学家卡约·达席尔瓦·普拉多·尤尼沃尔教授夫妇；应中国教育工会全国委员会邀请前来我国访问的由巴西里约热内卢教师工会第一书记维·波尔波莱玛·波尔多和巴西洲际教师联合会书记埃乌里克·巴布蒂斯塔·阿兰巴·米兰达组成的巴西教师工会代表团；以古巴全国师范及同级教师学会主席莱斯列·罗德里格斯·阿吉莱拉为首的古巴全国师范及同级教师学会代表团；厄瓜多尔全国教育工会工作者联盟主席西罗·马尔东纳；由墨西哥全国教育工作者工会全国出版委员会主席、教育工会国际副主席何赫·克鲁伊克显克·加尔西亚和墨西哥全国教育工作者工会的弗兰西斯科·莫拉·佩莱斯组成的墨西哥教师工会代表团；乌拉圭蒙得维的亚教师联盟主席丽达·迪亚斯。
    周而复在宴会上说，拉丁美洲革命的风暴已经兴起。古巴革命是拉丁美洲的光辉榜样，得到了中国和全世界人民的支持。
    古巴全国教师学会代表团团长罗德里格斯·阿吉莱拉也在宴会上讲话，他盛赞中国社会主义革命和社会主义建设的成就，并说古巴人民和中国人民在反对美帝国主义的同一条道路上前进，古中两国人民的友谊是牢不可破的。
