Title: Yanji People of All Nationalities and Circles Gather to Warmly Celebrate the 15th Anniversary of the Liberation of Korea
Author:
Date: 1960-08-17
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, Yanji, August 16th: One thousand two hundred people of all nationalities and circles in Yanji City, the capital of the Yanbian Korean Autonomous Prefecture in Jilin Province, held a grand gathering on the evening of the 14th to warmly celebrate the 15th anniversary of the liberation of Korea.

Zhu Dehai, First Secretary of the Yanbian Korean Autonomous Prefecture Committee of the Chinese Communist Party and Governor of the Prefecture, Deputy Governor Nan Mingxue, Jin Jixi, First Secretary of the Yanji Municipal Committee of the Chinese Communist Party, Jiang Laibin, Secretary of the Secretariat and Deputy Mayor, Du Xiuzhang, Commander of the Yanbian Military Sub-district, Chen Biao, Deputy Political Commissar, and other party, government and military leaders attended the celebration.

Zhu Dehai spoke at the meeting. He mentioned that after the American imperialists suffered a shameful defeat in the Korean War, the glorious achievements created by the Korean people in the great struggle against American imperialist aggression and in defense of world peace will forever radiate in human history. Then, he listed a large number of facts to praise the brilliant achievements made by the Korean people in the fifteen years after liberation. Finally, he pointed out that the traditional friendship between the Chinese and Korean people has been newly developed in the struggle against American imperialist aggression. The Chinese people have always been, are now, and will always be with the Korean people, resolutely opposing American imperialist aggression and defending peace in the Far East and the world.

After the meeting, a film was shown.

### 延吉各族各界人民集会  热烈庆祝朝鲜解放十五周年

1960-08-17
第5版()
专栏：

    延吉各族各界人民集会
    热烈庆祝朝鲜解放十五周年
    新华社延吉16日电　吉林省延边朝鲜族自治州首府——延吉市各族各界一千二百人，14日晚举行盛大集会，热烈庆祝朝鲜解放十五周年。
    中共延边朝鲜族自治州委员会第一书记、州长朱德海、副州长南明学，中共延吉市委第一书记金己锡，书记处书记、副市长姜耒斌，延边军分区司令员杜秀章，副政治委员陈彪等党政军负责人出席了庆祝大会。
    会上朱德海讲了话。他谈到美帝国主义在侵朝战争中遭到可耻失败后说，朝鲜人民在反对美帝国主义侵略、保卫世界和平的伟大斗争中所创立的光辉功绩，在人类历史上将永远放射光芒。接着，他列举大量事实，赞扬朝鲜人民在解放后十五年内取得的光辉成就。最后他指出，中朝两国人民的传统友谊在反对美帝国主义侵略的斗争中，得到了新的发展。中国人民过去、现在和将来永远和朝鲜人民一起，坚决反对美帝国主义侵略，保卫远东和世界和平。
    大会结束后放映了电影。
