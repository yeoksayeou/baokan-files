Title: Li Fuchun Delivers Report on Economic Construction Issues at the National Congress of Literary and Art Workers

Author: 
Date: 1960-08-06
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, August 5th. The Third National Congress of Chinese Literary and Art Workers continued its sessions today. Vice Premier of the State Council, Li Fuchun, delivered a report on national economic construction issues to the congress. All delegates will discuss this report on the morning of August 6th.

### 在全国文学艺术工作者代表大会上  李富春作经济建设问题的报告

1960-08-06
第1版()
专栏：

    在全国文学艺术工作者代表大会上
    李富春作经济建设问题的报告
    新华社5日讯　中国文学艺术工作者第三次代表大会今天继续举行大会。国务院副总理李富春在大会上作了关于国家经济建设问题的报告。全体代表将在6日上午讨论这个报告。
