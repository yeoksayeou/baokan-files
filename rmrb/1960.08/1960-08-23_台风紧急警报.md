Title: Typhoon Emergency Alert
Author:
Date: 1960-08-23
Page: 4
    Xinhua News Agency, August 22nd. The Central Meteorological Science Research Institute issued a typhoon emergency alert at 6:00 AM on the 22nd:

    The 14th typhoon of 1960, at 2:00 AM this morning, had its center located approximately 250 kilometers east of Zhejiang, near 28.3 degrees North latitude and 124.5 degrees East longitude. The maximum wind force near the center was level 12. The typhoon center will move northwest at a speed of 12-14 knots per hour and is expected to approach the coastal areas of southern Jiangsu and northern Zhejiang this afternoon to evening, and then turn to the north-northeast. Affected by the typhoon, there will be force 8-11 winds in the coastal areas of northern Zhejiang and central and southern Jiangsu in the next 48 hours; force 6-8 winds in the coastal areas of northern Jiangsu and southern Zhejiang; force 7-9 winds in the central and northern Yellow Sea; and heavy to torrential rain in central and southern Jiangsu, northern Zhejiang, eastern Shandong, and eastern Liaoning.

### 台风紧急警报

1960-08-23
第4版()
专栏：

    台风紧急警报
    新华社22日讯　中央气象科学研究所22日早晨六时发布台风紧急警报：
    1960年第十四号台风，今天早晨二时，中心位置在浙江以东二百五十公里左右的海面上，即北纬二十八点三度，东经一百二十四点五度附近，中心附近最大风力有十二级，未来台风中心将以每小时十二—十四浬的速度，向西北方向移动，预计今天下午到晚上靠近苏南、浙北一带沿海，而后转向北偏东方向移动。受台风影响，未来四十八小时内，浙江北部沿海、江苏中部及南部沿海将有八—十一级大风；江苏北部沿海、浙江南部沿海将有六—八级大风；黄海中部、北部将有七—九级大风；江苏中部、南部、浙江北部、山东东部、辽宁东部将有大雨到暴雨。
