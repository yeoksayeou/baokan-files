Title: Iraqi Student Union President Hafez Leaves Beijing for Home

Author: 
Date: 1960-08-25
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, August 20th. Mahdi Ahmed Hafez, Chairman of the General Union of Students of the Republic of Iraq, left by plane on the 20th to return home after visiting China at the invitation of the All-China Students' Federation.

### 伊拉克学联主席  哈菲兹离京回国

1960-08-25
第5版()
专栏：

    伊拉克学联主席
    哈菲兹离京回国
    新华社20日讯　伊拉克共和国学生总联合会主席马哈迪·阿赫默德·哈菲兹，应中华全国学生联合会邀请来我国进行访问后，已在20日乘飞机回国。
