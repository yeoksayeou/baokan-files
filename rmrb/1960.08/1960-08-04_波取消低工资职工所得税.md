Title: Poland Cancels Income Tax for Low-Wage Workers
Author: 
Date: 1960-08-04
Page: 6
    Xinhua News Agency, Warsaw, 2nd. It is reported here that the Central Committee of the Polish United Workers' Party and the Council of Ministers, based on the recommendation of the Central Council of Polish Trade Unions in July this year, recently decided to completely abolish the wage income tax for 500,000 employees with monthly salaries below 850 zlotys, starting from August 1 this year.
    In addition, it was also decided that from August 1st to November this year, the wage income tax for staff (approximately 2.19 million people in total) in education, agriculture and forestry, transportation, local industry, science, culture, medical care, public utilities, and local administration departments will be gradually reduced.
    The wage income tax for other employees with higher wages will also be gradually reduced in the future.

### 波取消低工资职工所得税

1960-08-04
第6版()
专栏：

    波取消低工资职工所得税
    新华社华沙2日电　据此间报道，波兰统一工人党中央和部长会议根据波兰工会中央理事会今年7月份的建议，最近决定从今年8月1日起完全取消月薪在八百五十兹罗提以下的五十万名职工的工资所得税。
    此外，还决定从今年8月1日开始到11月期间，将陆续降低教育工作者、农林业、运输、地方工业、科学、文化、医务、公用事业、地方行政等部门的工作人员（共约二百一十九万人）的工资所得税。
    其他一些工资较高的职工的工资所得税以后也将陆续降低。
