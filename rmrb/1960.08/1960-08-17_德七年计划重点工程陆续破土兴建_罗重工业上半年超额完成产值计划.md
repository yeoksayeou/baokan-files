Title: Key Projects of the German Seventh Five-Year Plan Break Ground One After Another; Romanian Heavy Industry Overfulfills Production Value Plan for the First Half of the Year
Author:
Date: 1960-08-17
Page: 7

    Xinhua News Agency Dispatch: Some important projects of the German Democratic Republic's Seven-Year Plan (1959-1965) are under construction.

    The construction of the sister factory of the largest chemical enterprise in the country, the Leuna Chemical Factory No. 1—the Leuna Chemical Factory No. 2—has been continuing since it broke ground last October. The factory's basic task is to extract raw materials needed for the manufacture of plastics and chemical fibers from petroleum. These two chemical products are at the core of the development of the chemical industry in the German Democratic Republic. Therefore, the completion of the new factory will be of great significance to the development of the Republic's chemical industry.

    The basic construction project of the Vetschau Power Station, located southeast of Berlin, began in the second half of last year. The new power station will be fully completed in 1966, with a generating capacity of 1.2 million kilowatts, sufficient to supply electricity to sixteen cities the size of the large industrial center of Dresden.

    Xinhua News Agency, Bucharest, August 13th: In the first half of this year, the Romanian heavy industry sector overfulfilled the total output value plan by 3.6% and the labor productivity plan by 2.6%. Enterprises saved a total value of more than 146.6 million lei (Romanian currency) in the first half of the year. A large-scale socialist production competition and technological innovation movement is an important reason for the major achievements of the Romanian heavy industry sector. In the first half of this year, the number of innovators in the heavy industry sector increased by 21.5% compared with the same period last year, the number of innovation proposals increased by 41%, and the actual savings in value increased by 86%.

    Improving equipment utilization is an important aspect of the socialist competition in the heavy industry sector. The "Brotherhood Friendship" machine tool factory in Oradea produced 19.9% more machine tools in the first half of this year than in the same period last year with the same machinery and production area.

### 德七年计划重点工程陆续破土兴建  罗重工业上半年超额完成产值计划

1960-08-17
第7版()
专栏：

    德七年计划重点工程陆续破土兴建
    罗重工业上半年超额完成产值计划
    据新华社讯　德意志民主共和国七年计划（1959—1965年）的一些重要工程正在建设中。
    全国最大的化学企业洛伊纳化学工厂第一厂的姐妹厂——洛伊纳化学工厂第二厂自去年10月破土兴建后，建设工作正在继续展开。这个工厂的基本任务是从石油中提炼制造塑料和化学纤维所需的原料，而这两项化学产品是发展德意志民主共和国化学工业的核心。因此，新工厂的建成对发展共和国的化学工业将有重要意义。
    在柏林东南部的韦乔发电站的基本建设工程，是在去年下半年开始的。新发电站将在1966年全部建成，它的发电能力将达到一百二十万瓩，它的电力足够供应十六个像大工业中心德累斯顿那样的城市之用。
    新华社布加勒斯特13日电　今年上半年，罗马尼亚重工业部门超额完成了总产值计划3.6％和劳动生产率计划2.6％，各企业上半年节约总值达一亿四千六百多万列伊（罗币）大规模的社会主义生产竞赛和技术革新运动，是罗马尼亚重工业部门取得重大成就的一个重要原因。今年上半年，重工业部门涌现的革新者比去年同期多21.5％，提出的革新建议多41％，而实际节约的价值多86％。
    提高设备利用率是重工业部门社会主义竞赛中的一个重要方面。俄拉第亚“兄弟友谊”机床制造厂今年上半年用同样的机器设备和生产面积，比去年同期多生产了19.9％的机床。
