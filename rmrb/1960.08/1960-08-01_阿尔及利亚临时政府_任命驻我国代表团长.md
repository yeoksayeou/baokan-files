Title: Algerian Provisional Government Appoints Head of Delegation to Our Country
Author:
Date: 1960-08-01
Page: 1

    Algerian Provisional Government
    Appoints Head of Delegation to Our Country
    Xinhua News Agency, 31st — Cairo dispatch: The Provisional Government of the Republic of Algeria recently issued a statement saying that, with the consent of the Government of the People's Republic of China, Mustapha Ferroukhi has been appointed as the Head of the Delegation of the Provisional Government of the Republic of Algeria to the People's Republic of China.
    The statement said that Ferroukhi will soon travel to Beijing.

### 阿尔及利亚临时政府  任命驻我国代表团长

1960-08-01
第1版()
专栏：

    阿尔及利亚临时政府
    任命驻我国代表团长
    新华社31日讯　开罗消息：阿尔及利亚共和国临时政府最近发表声明说，经中华人民共和国政府的同意，穆斯塔法·菲鲁基被任命为阿尔及利亚共和国临时政府驻中华人民共和国代表团团长。
    声明说，菲鲁基不久将前往北京。
