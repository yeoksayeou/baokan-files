Title: US Unreasonably Refuses Return of Hungarian Military Attaché to Post; Hungary Revokes Residence Rights of US Military Attaché

Author:

Date: 1960-08-20
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, Budapest, August 17th: The "People's Freedom Daily" published a communiqué from the Hungarian Ministry of Foreign Affairs today stating that after Lieutenant Colonel László Károly, military attaché of the Hungarian People's Republic to the United States, returned home for vacation, the US authorities groundlessly refused to issue him a visa to return to Washington.

The communiqué stated that, in response, Hungary has revoked the permission for Lieutenant Colonel Carl Miller, the US military attaché to Hungary, to continue residing in Hungary.

### 美无理拒绝匈驻美武官返回任所  匈取消美国武官居留权利

1960-08-20
第6版()
专栏：

    美无理拒绝匈驻美武官返回任所
    匈取消美国武官居留权利
    新华社布达佩斯17日电　“人民自由报”今天刊登了匈牙利外交部的公报说，匈牙利人民共和国驻美国公使馆武官拉斯洛·卡罗伊上校在回国休假后，美国当局竟毫无理由地不发给他返回华盛顿的签证。
    公报说，为此，匈牙利方面取消了美国驻匈牙利公使馆武官卡尔·米勒上校继续在匈牙利居留的许可。
