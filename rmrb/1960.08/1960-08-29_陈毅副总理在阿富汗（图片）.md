Title: Vice Premier Chen Yi in Afghanistan (Photos)
Author:
Date: 1960-08-29
Page: 5

Vice Premier and Foreign Minister Marshal Chen Yi arrived in Kabul on August 21 for a friendly visit and to attend the Afghan Independence Day celebrations on August 23. The photo shows Afghan children in national costumes presenting flowers to Vice Premier Chen Yi. Xinhua News Agency reporter Liu Dong'ao photographed.

Vice Premier and Foreign Minister Marshal Chen Yi attended the military parade of the Afghan Independence Day celebrations on August 23. The photo shows His Majesty King Mohammed Zahir of Afghanistan (left) and Vice Premier Chen Yi in friendly conversation on the reviewing stand.
Xinhua News Agency reporter Liu Dong'ao photographed.

Vice Premier and Foreign Minister Chen Yi was invited on August 24 to attend the opening ceremony of the Afghan Exhibition held to celebrate Afghan Independence Day. The photo shows King Mohammed Zahir of Afghanistan and Vice Premier Chen Yi visiting the exhibition together.
Xinhua News Agency reporter Liu Dong'ao photographed.

Prime Minister of the Kingdom of Afghanistan, Sardar Mohammed Daoud, hosted a grand banquet on August 21 for Vice Premier of the State Council and Foreign Minister of the People's Republic of China, Marshal Chen Yi. The photo shows Prime Minister Sardar Mohammed Daoud and Vice Premier Chen Yi in cordial conversation before the banquet. Xinhua News Agency reporter Liu Dong'ao photographed.

### 陈毅副总理在阿富汗（图片）

1960-08-29
第5版()
专栏：

    陈毅副总理在阿富汗
    副总理兼外交部长陈毅元帅8月21日到达喀布尔进行友好访问并参加8月23日阿富汗独立节庆典。图为穿着民族服装的阿富汗儿童向陈毅副总理献花。　新华社记者　刘东鳌摄副总理兼外交部长陈毅元帅8月23日参加了阿富汗王国独立节庆典的阅兵式。图为阿富汗国王穆罕默德·查希尔陛下（左）和陈毅副总理在检阅台上亲切交谈。
      新华社记者   刘东鳌摄
    副总理兼外交部长陈毅8月24日应邀参加了为庆祝阿富汗独立节而举办的阿富汗展览会的开幕式。图为阿富汗国王穆罕默德·查希尔和陈毅副总理一起参观展览会。
      新华社记者  刘东鳌摄
    阿富汗王国首相萨达尔·穆罕默德·达乌德8月21日为中华人民共和国国务院副总理兼外交部长陈毅元帅举行盛大宴会。图为萨达尔·穆罕默德·达乌德首相和陈毅副总理在宴会前亲切交谈。　新华社记者　刘东鳌摄
