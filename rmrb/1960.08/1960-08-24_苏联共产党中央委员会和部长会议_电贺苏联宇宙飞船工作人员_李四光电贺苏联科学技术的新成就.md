Title: The Central Committee of the Communist Party of the Soviet Union and the Council of Ministers Send Congratulations to the Staff of the Soviet Spaceship; Li Siguang Sends Congratulations on the New Achievements of Soviet Science and Technology
Author:
Date: 1960-08-24
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, August 23rd: TASS reports: The Central Committee of the Communist Party of the Soviet Union and the Council of Ministers sent a telegram warmly congratulating the scientists, designers, engineers, technicians, workers and all staff who participated in the manufacture and launch of the satellite spaceship and its return to Earth.

The congratulatory telegram stated that this outstanding achievement is a remarkable feat of the Soviet people in science, a triumph of Soviet science, technology and industry, and an epoch-making great contribution to the world's scientific and cultural treasure trove of conquering the universe. The congratulatory telegram pointed out that the practical possibility of mankind entering outer space has been created.

The congratulatory telegram emphasized that this glorious feat once again showed the power and might of the scientific and technological achievements of socialist countries to the world, showed the indisputable superiority of the socialist system, and showed the creative talent of the great Soviet people.

The congratulatory telegram finally wished Soviet scientists, designers, engineers, technicians, and workers new and outstanding achievements.

Xinhua News Agency, August 23rd: Li Siguang, Chairman of the Science and Technology Association of the People's Republic of China, sent a telegram on the 22nd to Gorevkov, Chairman of the Soviet Higher Education Trade Union, Semyonov, Chairman of the All-Union Knowledge Society, and Siruianov, Chairman of the All-Soviet Science and Technology Association, warmly congratulating the successful launch of the second Soviet spaceship.

The telegram said: "We are delighted to learn that the Soviet Union successfully launched its second spaceship again on August 19th, and after a day and night of navigation, safely and accurately returned to Earth in the designated area. The passengers of mankind's first space flight - 'Squirrel' and 'Little Arrow' - returned safely after traveling in space. This fact shows the great progress of the Soviet Union in space flight research. The new achievements of Soviet science and technology demonstrate the incomparable superiority of the socialist system and will powerfully encourage and strengthen the struggle of the people of all countries for world peace and human progress. On behalf of all members of the Science and Technology Association of the People's Republic of China, I extend my warmest congratulations to you and, through you, to all members of your associations."

### 苏联共产党中央委员会和部长会议  电贺苏联宇宙飞船工作人员  李四光电贺苏联科学技术的新成就

1960-08-24
第5版()
专栏：

    苏联共产党中央委员会和部长会议
    电贺苏联宇宙飞船工作人员
    李四光电贺苏联科学技术的新成就
    新华社23日讯　据塔斯社莫斯科讯：苏联共产党中央委员会和苏联部长会议致电热烈祝贺参加制造、发射卫星式宇宙飞船和使它返回地球的科学家、设计师、工程师、技术员、工人和全体工作人员。
    贺电中说，这个杰出的成就是苏联人在科学上的卓越功绩，是苏联的科学、技术和工业的凯歌，是对世界科学和文化宝库所作的征服宇宙的划时代的伟大贡献。贺电指出，人类进入宇宙空间的实际可能性已经创造出来。
    贺电强调指出，这一光荣的事迹再一次向全世界显示了社会主义国家科学技术成就的力量和威力，显示了社会主义制度无可争辩的优越性，显示了伟大的苏联人民的创造才能。
    贺电最后祝苏联的科学家、设计师、工程师、技术员和工人们取得新的卓越的成就。
    新华社23日讯　中华人民共和国科学技术协会主席李四光，22日打电报给苏联高教工会主席戈里夫柯夫、全苏科普主席谢苗诺夫、全苏科协主席西鲁扬诺夫，热烈祝贺苏联发射第二个宇宙飞船成功。
    电文说：欣闻苏联于8月19日又一次成功地发射了第二个宇宙飞船，并且经过一昼夜的航行之后，在预定地区安全而准确地返回地球，人类历史上第一次宇宙航行的乘客——“松鼠”和“小箭”在遨游太空之后安然返航。这个事实说明苏联在宇宙飞行方面研究的巨大进展。苏联科学技术的新成就，显示了社会主义制度的无比优越性，将有力地鼓舞和加强各国人民争取世界和平和人类进步的斗争。谨代表中华人民共和国科学技术协会全体会员向您并通过您向贵会全体会员致以最热烈的祝贺。
