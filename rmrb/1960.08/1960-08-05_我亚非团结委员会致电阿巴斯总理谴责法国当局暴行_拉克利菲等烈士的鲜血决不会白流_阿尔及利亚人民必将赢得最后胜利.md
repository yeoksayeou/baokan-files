Title: My Asian-African Solidarity Committee Cables Prime Minister Abbas Condemning the French Authorities' Brutal Acts; The Blood of Martyrs Like Lakhlif Will Never Be Shed in Vain; The Algerian People Will Surely Win Final Victory

Author:

Date: 1960-08-05
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, 4th. The Chinese Asian-African Solidarity Committee cabled Abbas, Prime Minister of the Provisional Government of the Republic of Algeria, on the 3rd, strongly condemning the French authorities' barbaric acts of murdering Algerian national hero Lakhlif and four other Algerian patriots, and expressing that the Chinese people will forever stand with the Algerian people.

The cable said that the French authorities, disregarding the opposition of people all over the world, have heinously murdered Algerian national hero Lakhlif and four other Algerian patriots. This is another new blood debt owed by French imperialism to the Algerian people. The Chinese people severely condemn this barbaric act by the French authorities. The cable pointed out that the blood of patriots like Lakhlif will never be shed in vain. Their patriotic spirit of living for the people and dying for the motherland will be immortal. The Chinese people will forever stand with the heroic Algerian people. We firmly believe that the tide of historical progress cannot be stopped or bound by any force. With the strong support of the Asian-African people and progressive people all over the world, the just cause of the Algerian people for national independence will surely win final victory.

### 我亚非团结委员会致电阿巴斯总理谴责法国当局暴行  拉克利菲等烈士的鲜血决不会白流  阿尔及利亚人民必将赢得最后胜利

1960-08-05
第5版()
专栏：

    我亚非团结委员会致电阿巴斯总理谴责法国当局暴行
    拉克利菲等烈士的鲜血决不会白流  阿尔及利亚人民必将赢得最后胜利
    新华社4日讯　中国亚非团结委员会3日致电阿尔及利亚共和国临时政府总理阿巴斯，严厉谴责法国当局杀害阿尔及利亚民族英雄拉克利菲和其他四位阿尔及利亚爱国者的野蛮暴行，表示中国人民将永远和阿尔及利亚人民站在一起。
    电报说，法国当局不顾全世界人民的反对，竟穷凶极恶地杀害了阿尔及利亚民族英雄拉克利菲和其他四位阿尔及利亚爱国者，这是法帝国主义对阿尔及利亚人民欠下的又一笔新血债，中国人民严厉谴责法国当局这一野蛮暴行。电报指出，拉克利菲等爱国者的鲜血决不会白流，他们为人民而生，为祖国而死的爱国精神将永垂不朽。中国人民将永远和英勇的阿尔及利亚人民站在一起。我们深信：历史前进的潮流是任何力量阻挡不住和束缚不了的。阿尔及利亚人民争取民族独立的正义事业，在亚非人民和全世界进步人民的强大支持下，必将赢得最后胜利。
