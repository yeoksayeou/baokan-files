Title: King of Jordan Appoints New Prime Minister
Author: 
Date: 1960-08-31
Page: 6

    Xinhua News Agency, Amman, August 30th: Following the assassination of former Jordanian Prime Minister Majali by a time bomb on the morning of the 29th, King Hussein of Jordan appointed former Chief of the Royal Court, Bahjat al-Talhouni, as Prime Minister that evening and announced the list of the new cabinet.

    It is reported that Talhouni, at a press conference held after forming the cabinet, said that his government will continue to implement the internal and external policies of the previous government.

### 约旦国王任命新首相

1960-08-31
第6版()
专栏：

    约旦国王任命新首相
    据新华社30日讯　安曼消息：在前约旦首相马贾利在29日上午被定时炸弹炸死以后，约旦国王侯赛因当天晚上任命前宫廷总管巴哈贾特·塔尔霍尼担任首相，并且宣布了新内阁的名单。
    据报道，塔尔霍尼在组成内阁以后举行的一次记者招待会上说，他的政府将继续执行前政府的内外政策。
