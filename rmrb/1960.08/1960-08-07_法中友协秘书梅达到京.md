Title: Secretary of the France-China Friendship Association, Mayda, Arrives in Beijing
Author: 
Date: 1960-08-07
Page: 6

    Xinhua News Agency, 5th. Jacques Mayda, Secretary of the France-China Friendship Association, arrived in Beijing by plane this morning at invitation of the Chinese People's Association for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries.

### 法中友协秘书梅达到京

1960-08-07
第6版()
专栏：

    法中友协秘书梅达到京
    新华社5日讯　应中国人民对外文化协会邀请来我国访问的法国法中友好协会秘书雅克·梅达，今天上午乘飞机到达北京。
