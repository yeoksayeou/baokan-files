Title: Premier Zhou Enlai Receives Delegation of Costa Rican Parliamentarians
Author: 
Date: 1960-08-27
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, August 26th: Premier Zhou Enlai received the entire delegation of Costa Rican parliamentarians, who are currently visiting our country, this afternoon and held friendly talks.

The parliamentarians in the delegation are: Marcial Aguiluz and his wife, José Rafael Cordero and his wife, Noel Hernández and his wife, Néstor López Gutiérrez, José Ángel Jara, César O. Solano, and the accompanying reporter Julio César Zúñiga. Present at the reception were Hu Yuzhi, Vice President of the Chinese People's Institute of Foreign Affairs, and Wu Xiaoda, Deputy Secretary-General. Also present were Lei Renmin, Vice Minister of Foreign Trade, and Shen Jian, Director of the Department of American and Australian Affairs of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs. (Attached picture)

Premier Zhou Enlai receives the Costa Rican Parliamentary Delegation. The picture shows a group photo after the reception.

### 周总理接见哥斯达黎加议员代表

1960-08-27
第1版()
专栏：

    周总理接见哥斯达黎加议员代表
    新华社26日讯　周恩来总理今天下午接见了正在我国访问的哥斯达黎加议员代表团全体成员，并进行了友好的谈话。
    代表团中的议员们是：马西亚尔·阿吉卢斯和夫人，何塞·拉斐尔·科尔德罗和夫人，诺埃尔·埃尔南台斯和夫人，内斯托尔·洛佩兹·古铁雷斯，何塞·安赫尔·哈拉，赛萨·奥·索拉诺，以及随团记者胡利奥·赛萨·苏诺耳。接见时在座的，有外交学会副会长胡愈之，副秘书长吴晓达。在座的还有对外贸易部副部长雷任民，外交部美澳司司长申健。（附图片）
