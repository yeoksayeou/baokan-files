Title: Burmese National United Front Welcomes Success of Sino-Burmese Boundary Talks, Further Strengthening and Consolidating Friendly Relations Between the Two Countries
Author:
Date: 1960-08-11
Page: 6
Xinhua News Agency, Rangoon, August 8th. The Burmese National United Front, in a statement issued today, said that the rapid progress and fruitful results of the second meeting of the Sino-Burmese Joint Boundary Committee clearly indicate that the Sino-Burmese boundary issue will be resolved satisfactorily, favorably, and peacefully between the two countries in the near future.

The statement from the Burmese National United Front pointed out that the achievements of the second meeting of the Sino-Burmese Joint Boundary Committee are the result of friendly relations between the two peoples based on the Five Principles of Peaceful Coexistence.

The statement said that in the future, friendly relations, mutual interests, and the maintenance of peace in Asia between the two countries should be further strengthened and consolidated on the basis of the Five Principles of Peaceful Coexistence.

### 缅甸民族团结阵线欢迎中缅边界会谈成功  两国友好关系将进一步加强和巩固

1960-08-11
第6版()
专栏：

    缅甸民族团结阵线欢迎中缅边界会谈成功
    两国友好关系将进一步加强和巩固
    新华社仰光8日电　缅甸民族团结阵线在今天发表的一项声明中说，中缅边界联合委员会第二次会议的迅速的进展和富有成效的结果清楚地表明，中缅边界问题将在最近的将来在两国之间获得令人满意的、有利的和和平的解决。
    缅甸民族团结阵线的声明指出，中缅边界联合委员会第二次会议所取得的成就是两国人民在和平共处五项原则基础上的友好关系的结果。
    声明说，在将来，两国之间的友好关系、相互间的利益和维护亚洲的和平应在和平共处五项原则的基础上进一步加强和巩固。
