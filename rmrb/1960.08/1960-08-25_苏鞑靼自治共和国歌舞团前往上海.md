Title: Tatar Autonomous Soviet Socialist Republic Song and Dance Ensemble Heads to Shanghai
Author:
Date: 1960-08-25
Page: 5

    Xinhua News Agency, August 24th. After giving six performances in Beijing, the Tatar Autonomous Soviet Socialist Republic State Song and Dance Ensemble left Beijing this morning for a performance tour in Shanghai. The song and dance ensemble will also visit Hangzhou, Nanjing, Tianjin and other cities.

### 苏鞑靼自治共和国歌舞团前往上海

1960-08-25
第5版()
专栏：

    苏鞑靼自治共和国歌舞团前往上海
    新华社24日讯　苏联鞑靼自治共和国国家歌舞团在北京进行了六场访问演出之后，于今天上午离开北京去上海访问演出。歌舞团还将访问杭州、南京、天津等城市。
