Title: Korea Holds Korean-Soviet Friendship Month
Author:
Date: 1960-08-13
Page: 6
Xinhua News Agency, Pyongyang, August 11th: More than 1,000 people from all walks of life in Pyongyang held an opening ceremony for "Korean-Soviet Friendship Month" at the National Art Theater on the evening of the 10th. This "Korean-Soviet Friendship Month," scheduled to run from August 10th to September 20th, is being held to celebrate the 15th anniversary of Korea's liberation on "August 15th."

Among those attending the meeting were Korean Deputy Prime Ministers Hong Myong-hui and Jeong Il-yong, Foreign Minister Park Sung-chul, and leaders of various democratic political parties and social organizations. The Soviet-Korean Friendship Association delegation, headed by A. Andoshin, Secretary of the Primorsky Krai Committee of the Communist Party of the Soviet Union, which is currently visiting Korea, Soviet Ambassador to Korea Puzanov, and diplomatic envoys of various countries to Korea were invited to attend the meeting.

Ri Ki-young, Chairman of the Central Committee of the Korean-Soviet Friendship Association, spoke at the meeting. He said that the holding of "Korean-Soviet Friendship Month" will further strengthen the friendly unity between the people of Korea and the Soviet Union. He warmly praised the achievements of the Soviet people in communist construction.

A. Andoshin, head of the Soviet-Korean Friendship Association delegation, said that he was grateful to the Korean people for holding the "Korean-Soviet Friendship Month" activities. He praised the achievements of the Korean people in socialist construction. He said that the heroic struggle of the South Korean people is a heavy blow to the US imperialists who are occupying South Korea.

### 朝鲜举办朝苏友好月

1960-08-13
第6版()
专栏：

    朝鲜举办朝苏友好月
    新华社平壤11日电　平壤市各界一千多人，10日晚上在国立艺术剧场举行“朝苏友好月”开幕仪式。这个预定从8月10日开始进行到9月20日的“朝苏友好月”是为庆祝“八·一五”朝鲜解放十五周年而举办的。
    参加大会的有朝鲜内阁副首相洪命熹、郑一龙，外务相朴成哲和各民主政党、社会团体的负责人等。正在朝鲜访问的以苏联共产党滨海边疆区委员会书记安道辛为首的苏朝友好协会代表团，苏联驻朝鲜大使普扎诺夫和各国驻朝使节应邀参加了大会。
    朝苏友好协会中央委员会委员长李箕永在会上讲了话。他说，“朝苏友好月”的举行，将进一步加强朝苏两国人民的友好团结。他热烈赞扬苏联人民在共产主义建设中取得的成就。
    苏朝友好协会代表团团长安道辛讲话说，他对朝鲜人民举办“朝苏友好月”活动表示感谢。他赞扬了朝鲜人民在社会主义建设中所取得的成就。他说，南朝鲜人民的英雄斗争，给予强占着南朝鲜的美帝国主义以沉重的打击。
