Title: Liao Chengzhi Hosts Banquet for Suzuki Kazuo
Author:
Date: 1960-08-23
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, August 22nd: Liao Chengzhi, Chairman of the China Asian-African Solidarity Committee, hosted a banquet this evening in honor of Suzuki Kazuo, Standing Director of the Japan Asian-African Solidarity Committee and Executive Director of the Japan-China Trade Promotion Association, who is currently visiting China.

Among those present at the banquet were Wang Mingyuan, Xiao Fangzhou, Yang Jin, Xiao Xiangqian, Wang Xiaoyun, and other individuals from various circles.

### 廖承志设宴招待铃木一雄

1960-08-23
第6版()
专栏：

    廖承志设宴招待铃木一雄
    新华社22日讯　中国亚非团结委员会主席廖承志今天晚间举行宴会，招待正在我国访问的日本亚非团结委员会常务理事、日中贸易促进会专务理事铃木一雄。
    出席宴会的，有王明远、萧方洲、杨进、萧向前、王晓云等各界人士。
