Title: Hungarian Artisan Kalman Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1960-08-14
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, August 13th: According to the 1960 executive plan of the Sino-Hungarian Cultural Cooperation Agreement, Hungarian artisan Konya Kalman arrived in Beijing by plane this afternoon for a visit to China.

Those who welcomed her at the airport included Mei Jianying, director of the Ceramics Department of the Central Academy of Arts and Crafts, and others.

### 匈工艺美术家卡尔曼妮到京

1960-08-14
第6版()
专栏：

    匈工艺美术家卡尔曼妮到京
    新华社13日讯　根据中匈文化合作协定1960年执行计划，来我国访问的匈牙利工艺美术家科尼亚·卡尔曼妮，今天下午乘飞机到达北京。
    到机场欢迎的有中央工艺美术学院陶瓷系主任梅健鹰等人。
