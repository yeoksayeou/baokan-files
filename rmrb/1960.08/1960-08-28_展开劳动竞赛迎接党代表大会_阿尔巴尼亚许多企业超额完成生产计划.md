Title: Launching Labor Emulation Drives to Greet the Party Congress: Many Albanian Enterprises Exceed Production Plans
Author:
Date: 1960-08-28
Page: 6
Xinhua News Agency, Tirana — Central and local enterprises in Albania are using practical actions of completing and over fulfilling production plans to greet the Fourth Congress of the Albanian Labor Party, which will be held at the end of this year. The Albanian industrial sector completed 101% of the plan for the first seven months. All thirty-five existing business units in Tirana have also completed their plans for the same period.
    According to local newspapers, enterprises in the Shkodër District of northern Albania had already over fulfilled the total industrial output value of the Second Five-Year Plan (1956-1960) by 106% by the end of June this year, producing 66.75 million lek worth of products for the country. The district's cement plant raised its targets twice this year and completed the annual plan to increase cement production by 600 tons by the end of July.
    In the Korçë District in the south, the state-owned logging company's sawmills have overfulfilled their tasks for the first seven months of this year in the production competition with the slogan "Greeting the Fourth Party Congress." The Gërmenj sawmill in the district completed 142% of the plan for the first seven months and won the mobile red flag. The labor competition launched by enterprises in the two coastal areas of Vlorë and Durrës to greet the Party Congress has entered a new high tide. (Attached photo)
    The first coal block was mined in the newly excavated coal mine in Arar, Pukë District, Albania. Albanian Telegraph Agency draft (Xinhua News Agency)

### 展开劳动竞赛迎接党代表大会  阿尔巴尼亚许多企业超额完成生产计划

1960-08-28
第6版()
专栏：

    展开劳动竞赛迎接党代表大会
    阿尔巴尼亚许多企业超额完成生产计划
    据新华社地拉那讯　阿尔巴尼亚中央和地方企业正在用完成和超额完成生产计划的实际行动来迎接将在今年年底召开的阿尔巴尼亚劳动党第四次代表大会。阿尔巴尼亚工业部门以101％完成了前七个月的计划。地拉那现有的三十五个企业单位也都完成了同期的计划。
    据此间报纸报道，阿尔巴尼亚北部的斯库台区的企业已在今年6月底以106％超额完成第二个五年（1956年—1960年）计划的工业总产值，为国家多生产了价值六万六千七百五十万里克的产品。这个区水泥厂今年曾两度提高指标，并在7月底就完成了全年的增产六百吨水泥的计划。
    在南方的科尔察区，国营伐木公司各锯木场在以“迎接党的四大”为口号的生产竞赛中，今年前七月的任务也已超额完成。这个区的盖尔曼尼锯木场以142％完成了前七个月的计划，获得了流动红旗。发罗那和都拉斯两个沿海地区的企业与企业之间，为迎接党代会而发起的劳动竞赛已进入了新的高潮。（附图片）
