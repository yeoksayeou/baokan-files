Title: China's First Ambassador to Ghana, Huang Hua, Arrives in Accra
Author:
Date: 1960-08-26
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, Accra, 25th. Huang Hua, China's first ambassador to Ghana, arrived here by plane today from Beijing.

Among those who went to the airport to welcome Ambassador Huang Hua were Sam, the Chief of Protocol of the Ghanaian Ministry of Foreign Affairs, diplomatic envoys from the Soviet Union and other socialist countries to Ghana, and the staff of the Chinese Embassy.

### 我国驻加纳第一任大使  黄华到达阿克拉

1960-08-26
第5版()
专栏：

    我国驻加纳第一任大使
    黄华到达阿克拉
    新华社阿克拉25日电　中国驻加纳第一任大使黄华今天从北京乘飞机到达这里。
    前往机场欢迎黄华大使的有加纳外交部礼宾司司长萨姆、苏联和其它社会主义国家驻加纳的外交使节以及中国大使馆的工作人员。
