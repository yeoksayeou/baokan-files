Title: Song and Dance Troupe of the Soviet Tatar Autonomous Republic Receives Warm Welcome in Shanghai Performances
Author:
Date: 1960-08-29
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency, Shanghai, August 28th: The National Song and Dance Troupe of the Soviet Tatar Autonomous Republic held its premiere in Shanghai on the evening of the 27th, performing 27 programs, including Tatar and Russian folk songs, dances, the chorus "Ode to Lenin," "My Republic," "Friendship Suite," and the famous Chinese song "The East is Red," receiving a warm welcome from more than two thousand Shanghai audience members.

　　Cao Diqiu, Secretary of the Shanghai Municipal Committee of the Communist Party of China and Deputy Mayor of Shanghai, watched the artists' wonderful performance.

　　During the intermission, Cao Diqiu met with the head of the troupe, B.M. Zhichatulin, and the main actors of the song and dance troupe, and had a cordial conversation. Present at the meeting were Meng Bo, Director of the Shanghai Municipal Bureau of Culture, Liu Simu, Deputy Secretary-General of the Shanghai Municipal Sino-Soviet Friendship Association, and Chekunov, Deputy Consul of the Soviet Consulate General in Shanghai.

　　After the performance, Cao Diqiu, Meng Bo, and other Shanghai literary and art figures went on stage to warmly congratulate all the artists of the song and dance troupe on their successful performance.

　　The song and dance troupe arrived in Shanghai from Beijing by special train on the 25th.

### 苏联鞑靼自治共和国歌舞团  在上海演出受到观众热烈欢迎

1960-08-29
第4版()
专栏：

    苏联鞑靼自治共和国歌舞团
    在上海演出受到观众热烈欢迎
    新华社上海28日电　苏联鞑靼自治共和国国家歌舞团27日晚在上海举行首次公演，演出了鞑靼和俄罗斯民歌、舞蹈、合唱“列宁颂”“我的共和国”“友谊组舞”以及中国著名歌曲“东方红”等二十七个节目，受到二千多名上海观众的热烈欢迎。
    中共上海市委书记处书记、上海市副市长曹荻秋观看了艺术家们的精彩演出。
    演出休息时，曹荻秋接见了布·米·吉柴图林团长和歌舞团主要演员，并且进行了亲切的谈话。接见时在座的有上海市文化局局长孟波、上海市中苏友好协会副秘书长刘思慕，还有苏联驻上海总领事馆副领事切库诺夫。
    演出结束后，曹荻秋、孟波等和上海文艺界人士一起登台，热烈祝贺歌舞团全体艺术家们演出成功。
    歌舞团是25日乘专车从北京来上海的。
