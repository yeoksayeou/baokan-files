Title: Chinese Painting Exhibition Held in Ecuador
Author:
Date: 1960-08-13
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, Guayaquil, 12th: A Chinese painting exhibition was recently held in Loja City, the capital of Loja Province, Ecuador.

The exhibition, held from July 22nd to 28th, was jointly organized by the Loja branch of the Ecuadorian House of Culture and the University of Loja. The exhibition featured reproductions of paintings by famous Chinese painters Qi Baishi and Xu Beihong, as well as works by some modern painters, totaling about forty pieces.

Bolivar Solis Castro, the organizer of the exhibition, said in his speech at the opening ceremony that in China, "art not only receives the necessary support from the government, but especially draws more and more nourishment from the experience, achievements, and efforts made by the people in the victorious and unstoppable socialist construction."

It is reported that the Ecuadorian House of Culture is currently organizing a large-scale Chinese painting exhibition to be held on tour in various places.

### 厄瓜多尔举办中国画展

1960-08-13
第6版()
专栏：

    厄瓜多尔举办中国画展
    新华社12日讯　瓜亚基尔消息：厄瓜多尔乐哈省省会乐哈城最近举办了中国名画展览会。
    从7月22日到28日举行的这次展览会是由厄瓜多尔文化之家乐哈分部和乐哈大学联合举办的。展览会上展出了中国著名画家齐白石和徐悲鸿绘画的复制品和一些现代画家的作品，总共约四十幅。
    这次展览会的组织者博利瓦尔·索利斯·卡斯特罗在展览会开幕式上讲话时说，在中国，“艺术不但得到政府必要的支持，而且特别是从人民在胜利的和不可遏止的社会主义建设中所取得的经验、成就和所作的努力中吸取到越来越多的滋养”。
    据报道，厄瓜多尔文化之家目前正在组织一个到各地巡回展出的大型中国画展览会。
