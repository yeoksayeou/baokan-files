Title: British "Worker's Daily" Comments on Premier Zhou's Peace Initiative: The Whole World Sees Socialist Countries Want Peace; Condemns Eisenhower's "Warmongering Message"
Author:
Date: 1960-08-10
Page: 6
Xinhua News Agency, London, August 9th. The British "Worker's Daily" said in an editorial published on the 8th: "Contrary to Eisenhower's clamoring, (China's) Premier Zhou Enlai has proposed a nuclear-free zone in the Pacific. The whole world sees once again that it is the socialist countries that want peace, while the imperialists are preparing for war."

The "Worker's Daily" made this statement after comparing the recent peace initiatives proposed by socialist countries with Eisenhower's bellicose message to the US Congress on the 8th. The editorial pointed out that Eisenhower's special message was a "warmongering message." This message clamored for the United States to "have more bombers, more aircraft carriers, more missiles, more missile-launching submarines, more military exercises, intensified efforts to establish continuous airborne alerts, and spend more money buying arms."

The newspaper called on the British people to rise up against the Western imperialists' war plans. It pointed out that currently, British "Conservatives are in serious danger of becoming even more anti-Soviet and more subservient to Washington and Bonn than before."

### 英国“工人日报”评周总理和平倡议  全世界看到社会主义国家要和平  谴责艾森豪威尔的“黩武主义的咨文”

1960-08-10
第6版()
专栏：

    英国“工人日报”评周总理和平倡议
    全世界看到社会主义国家要和平
    谴责艾森豪威尔的“黩武主义的咨文”
    新华社伦敦9日电　英国“工人日报”在8日发表的一篇社论中说：“与艾森豪威尔的叫嚣相反，（中国）周恩来总理提出了太平洋无核武器地区的建议。全世界又一次看到，正是社会主义国家要和平，而帝国主义者却正在进行战争准备。”
    “工人日报”是在把社会主义国家最近提出的和平倡议同艾森豪威尔8日向美国国会发表的好战的咨文相对比后这样说的。这篇社论指出，艾森豪威尔的特别咨文是一篇“黩武主义的咨文”。这篇咨文叫嚣美国“要有更多的轰炸机、更多的航空母舰、更多导弹、更多发射导弹的潜水艇、更多军事演习，加紧努力设置连续空中警戒、花更多钱购买军火”。
    这家报纸号召英国人民起来反对西方帝国主义者的战争计划。它指出，目前英国“保守党人有变得甚至比以前更加反苏、更加屈从于华盛顿和波恩的严重危险”。
