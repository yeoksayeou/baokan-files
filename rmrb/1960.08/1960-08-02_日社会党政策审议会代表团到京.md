Title: Delegation from the Japanese Socialist Party Policy Deliberation Council Arrives in Beijing
Author: 
Date: 1960-08-02
Page: 6

    Xinhua News Agency, 1st. The ten-member delegation from the Japanese Socialist Party Policy Deliberation Council, led by Hiroshi Teshima, Secretary of the Japanese Socialist Party Headquarters and head of the delegation, arrived in Beijing by train this afternoon.
    Greeting them at the station were Xie Nanguang, Yang Chunsong, Deputy Secretary-General Xiao Xiangqian, of the Chinese People's Institute of Foreign Affairs, Japanese peace activist Kinkazu Saionji, and Korean peace activist Hwang Bong-koo, among others.
    After arriving in Beijing, Hiroshi Teshima told reporters that the delegation brought the heartfelt friendship of the patriotic Japanese people and the Japanese Socialist Party who are waging an unyielding struggle against the signing of the Japan-U.S. military alliance treaty; and expressed gratitude for the strong support given by the Chinese people to the just struggle of the Japanese people. He said that the delegation hopes that this visit will contribute to the promotion of Japan-China friendship.

### 日社会党政策审议会代表团到京

1960-08-02
第6版()
专栏：

    日社会党政策审议会代表团到京
    新华社1日讯　日本社会党政策审议会访华代表团一行十人，在团长、日本社会党本部书记手岛博率领下，在今天下午乘火车到达北京。
    到车站欢迎的，有中国人民外交学会理事谢南光、杨春松、副秘书长萧向前和在京日本和平人士西园寺公一，朝鲜和平人士黄凤九等。
    手岛博到达北京后，对记者发表谈话说，代表团此次访华，带来了反对签订日美军事同盟条约而进行不屈不挠斗争的日本爱国人民以及日本社会党的衷心的友谊；并感谢中国人民对日本人民正义斗争所给予的强有力的支持。他说，代表团希望这次访问能对促进日中友好有所贡献。
