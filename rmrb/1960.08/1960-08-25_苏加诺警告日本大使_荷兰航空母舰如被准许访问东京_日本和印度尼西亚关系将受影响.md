Title: Sukarno Warns Japanese Ambassador: If Dutch Aircraft Carrier is Allowed to Visit Tokyo, Relations Between Japan and Indonesia Will Be Affected
Author:
Date: 1960-08-25
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, Jakarta, August 24th: According to a report by the Indonesian News Agency on August 23rd, President Sukarno told the Japanese Ambassador to Indonesia, Okita Takita, on August 23rd: "If the Dutch aircraft carrier 'Karel Doorman' is allowed to visit Tokyo, the feelings of the Indonesian government and people will be greatly affected."

Sukarno further warned Okita Takita that this issue was "very serious" and could affect the "good relations" between the two countries.

President Sukarno issued this warning during a meeting with the Japanese Ambassador at the Independence Palace.

### 苏加诺警告日本大使  荷兰航空母舰如被准许访问东京  日本和印度尼西亚关系将受影响

1960-08-25
第6版()
专栏：

    苏加诺警告日本大使
    荷兰航空母舰如被准许访问东京
    日本和印度尼西亚关系将受影响
    新华社雅加达24日电　据印度尼西亚新闻社23日报道，苏加诺总统23日对日本驻印度尼西亚大使黄田多喜多说：“如果荷兰航空母舰‘卡雷耳·杜尔曼号’被准许访问东京，印度尼西亚政府和人民的感情将受到很大的影响。”
    苏加诺进一步警告黄田多喜多说，这个问题是“非常严重的”，而且可能影响两国的“良好关系”。
    苏加诺总统是在独立宫同日本大使会谈的时候提出这个警告的。
