Title: CPC Beijing Municipal Committee Holds Graduates' Report Meeting
Author:
Date: 1960-08-21
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, 20th - The CPC Beijing Municipal Committee held a report meeting this afternoon at the Great Hall of the People for more than 20,000 graduating students from universities and secondary vocational schools in the capital.

Comrade Zhou Enlai, Vice-Chairman of the CPC Central Committee and Premier of the State Council, also attended the meeting and met with the graduates.

Comrade Peng Zhen, member of the Political Bureau of the CPC Central Committee, First Secretary of the CPC Beijing Municipal Committee, and Mayor of Beijing, delivered an important report at the meeting.

### 中共北京市委举行毕业生报告会

1960-08-21
第4版()
专栏：

    中共北京市委举行毕业生报告会
    新华社20日讯　中共北京市委员会今天下午在人民大会堂为首都高等院校、中等专业学校二万多名应届毕业生举行报告会。
    中共中央副主席、国务院总理周恩来同志也到会同毕业生们见了面。
    中共中央政治局委员、中共北京市委第一书记、北京市市长彭真同志在会上做了重要报告。
