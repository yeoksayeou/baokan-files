Title: Ceylon Education Delegation Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1960-08-14
Page: 6
Xinhua News Agency, August 13th: A joint delegation from the Ceylon education sector, consisting of Fernando, Vice-Chairman of the Ceylon National Teachers' Union; Das, Secretary of the Ceylon Public School Teachers' Union; Subramanian, Chairman of the Northern Province Branch of the All Ceylon Teachers' League; and Rajendram, Chairman of the All Ceylon Tamil Teachers' Union, arrived in Beijing by train from Guangzhou today.

They are visiting China at the invitation of the National Committee of the Chinese Education Workers' Union.

Sheng Jin, member of the Presidium of the National Committee of the Chinese Education Workers' Union, and others welcomed them at the station.

### 锡兰教育界联合代表团到京

1960-08-14
第6版()
专栏：

    锡兰教育界联合代表团到京
    新华社13日讯　由锡兰全国教师工会副主席费尔南多，锡兰公立学校教师工会秘书达斯，全锡教师同盟北方省分会主席苏布拉曼尼安和全锡泰米尔教师工会主席拉金德拉姆等四人组成的联合代表团，今天从广州乘火车抵达北京。
    他们是应中国教育工会全国委员会邀请来我国访问的。
    到车站欢迎的，有中国教育工会全国委员会主席团委员盛瑾等。
