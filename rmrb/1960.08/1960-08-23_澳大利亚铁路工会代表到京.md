Title: Australian Railway Union Representatives Arrive in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1960-08-23
Page: 6

    Australian Railway Union Representatives Arrive in Beijing

    Xinhua News Agency, August 22nd: Two representatives of the Australian Railway Union arrived in Beijing by plane on the 21st for a visit to China at the invitation of the National Committee of the Railway Union of China. They are Richard James Boling, Chairman of the Victorian Branch of the Australian Railway Union, and William Raymond Busen, Secretary of the Southwest Queensland Region of the Australian Railway Union. Xiao Hongqi, Vice Chairman of the National Committee of the Railway Union of China, and others welcomed them at the airport.

### 澳大利亚铁路工会代表到京

1960-08-23
第6版()
专栏：

    澳大利亚铁路工会代表到京
    新华社22日讯　澳大利亚铁路工会代表二人，应中国铁路工会全国委员会的邀请来我国访问，21日乘飞机到达北京。他们是澳大利亚铁路工会维多利亚分会主席理查德·詹姆斯·波林和澳大利亚铁路工会昆士兰西南地区书记威廉·雷蒙·布森。到机场欢迎的有中国铁路工会全国委员会副主席萧洪启等人。
