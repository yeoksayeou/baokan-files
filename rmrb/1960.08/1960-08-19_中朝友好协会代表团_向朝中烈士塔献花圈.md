Title: Sino-Korean Friendship Association Delegation Lays Wreaths at the Tower of Korean-Chinese Martyrs
Author:
Date: 1960-08-19
Page: 6
Xinhua News Agency, Pyongyang.

    The delegation of the Sino-Korean Friendship Association, headed by Li Dequan and Li Jiebo, laid wreaths on the 18th at the Memorial Tower to the Martyrs of the Korean People's Army and the Tower of Korean-Chinese Friendship, which commemorates the exploits of the Chinese People's Volunteers and the martyrs of the Volunteers. Choi Ki-chul, Vice-Chairman of the Korean Committee for Foreign Cultural Relations, and Qiao Xiaoguang, Chinese Ambassador to Korea, also attended the wreath-laying ceremony.

### 中朝友好协会代表团  向朝中烈士塔献花圈

1960-08-19
第6版()
专栏：

    中朝友好协会代表团
    向朝中烈士塔献花圈
    据新华社平壤电　以李德全、李颉伯为首的中朝友好协会代表团，18日向朝鲜人民军烈士纪念塔和纪念中国人民志愿军的功勋和志愿军烈士的朝中友谊塔敬献了花圈。朝鲜对外文化联络委员会副委员长崔基哲和中国驻朝鲜大使乔晓光也参加了献花圈的仪式。
