Title: Vice Premier Chen Yi Returns to Yining; People of All Ethnic Groups and Circles in Ili District Give a Warm Welcome at the Airport
Author:
Date: 1960-08-29
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Yining, August 28th. After concluding a friendly visit to the Kingdom of Afghanistan, Vice Premier of the State Council and Minister of Foreign Affairs Marshal Chen Yi returned to Yining City, the capital of the Ili Kazakh Autonomous Prefecture in the Xinjiang Uyghur Autonomous Region, by special plane on the afternoon of the 27th.

Marshal Chen Yi received a warm welcome from all ethnic groups and circles at the airport. Among those who went to the airport to welcome him were Lü Jianren, Secretary of the Secretariat of the Xinjiang Uyghur Autonomous Region Committee of the Chinese Communist Party; Zhang Shigong, First Secretary of the Ili District Committee of the Chinese Communist Party; Zhao Huaibi, Secretary; Kuerban Ali, Governor of the Ili Kazakh Autonomous Prefecture, and other leaders from the Party, government, military, and people's organizations. Also welcoming him at the airport was Sholokhov, Deputy Consul of the Soviet Consulate in Yining. A group of Young Pioneers of Kazakh, Uyghur, Han, and other ethnic groups presented flowers to Marshal Chen Yi.

In the evening, the Ili District Committee of the Chinese Communist Party and the People's Committee of the Ili Kazakh Autonomous Prefecture held a banquet to welcome Marshal Chen Yi's return from his visit.

Accompanying Marshal Chen Yi were Kong Yuan, Deputy Director of the Foreign Affairs Office of the State Council, and Geng Biao, Vice Minister of Foreign Affairs.

### 陈毅副总理回到伊宁  伊犁区各族各界人士到机场热烈欢迎

1960-08-29
第1版()
专栏：

    陈毅副总理回到伊宁
    伊犁区各族各界人士到机场热烈欢迎
    新华社伊宁28日电　国务院副总理兼外交部长陈毅元帅在结束对阿富汗王国的友好访问后，27日下午乘专机回到新疆维吾尔自治区伊犁哈萨克自治州首府伊宁市。
    陈毅元帅在机场受到了各族各界的热烈欢迎。到机场欢迎的有中共新疆维吾尔自治区委员会书记处书记吕剑人，中共伊犁区委员会第一书记张世功、书记赵怀璧，伊犁哈萨克自治州州长库尔班阿里等党、政府、军队和人民团体的负责人。到机场欢迎的还有苏联驻伊宁市领事馆副领事肖洛科夫。一群哈萨克族、维吾尔族、汉族等各民族的少先队员向陈毅元帅献了花。
    晚间，中共伊犁区委员会、伊犁哈萨克自治州人民委员会举行宴会，欢迎陈毅元帅访问归来。
    随同陈毅元帅一起到达此间的有国务院外事办公室副主任孔原，外交部副部长耿飚。
