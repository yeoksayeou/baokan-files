Title: Delegation of the Korean-Chinese Friendship Association Leaves Beijing for Wuhan
Author:
Date: 1960-08-17
Page: 5

    Xinhua News Agency, 16th. The delegation of the Korean-Chinese Friendship Association, headed by Kim Jong-heng and Deputy Head Park Bung-shik, accompanied by Xie Bingyan, Council Member of the Sino-Korean Friendship Association, left Beijing by train today to visit Wuhan.
    Among those who went to the station to see them off were Lao She, Vice President of the Sino-Korean Friendship Association, and Zhang Zhixiang, Deputy Director of the Commission for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries. Li Yong-ho, Ambassador of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea to China, Counselor Ma Dong-shan and others also saw them off at the station.

### 朝中友协代表团离京去武汉

1960-08-17
第5版()
专栏：

    朝中友协代表团离京去武汉
    新华社16日讯　朝中友好协会代表团一行十人，在团长金钟恒、副团长朴丰植的率领下，由中朝友好协会理事谢冰岩陪同，于今日乘火车离京去武汉访问。
    前往车站欢送的有中朝友好协会副会长老舍，对外文化联络委员会副主任张致祥等。朝鲜民主主义人民共和国驻华大使李永镐、参赞马东山等人也到车站送行。
