Title: China and Cuba Sign Two Trade Contracts: China Sells 100,000 Tons of Rice, Buys 350,000 Tons of Sugar
Author:
Date: 1960-08-10
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, Havana, 7th. To implement the trade agreement between China and Cuba, the trade organizations of the two countries signed two trade contracts here on the evening of the 5th, totaling 13 million pounds.

    The two contracts are: the contract between China National Cereals, Oils and Foodstuffs Import and Export Corporation and the National Bank of Cuba for China to sell 100,000 tons of Chinese rice to Cuba; and the contract between China National Cereals, Oils and Foodstuffs Import and Export Corporation and the National Bank of Cuba for the sale of 350,000 tons of Cuban sugar to China.

### 中国古巴签订两项贸易合同  我售出十万吨大米，购进三十五万吨食糖

1960-08-10
第1版()
专栏：

    中国古巴签订两项贸易合同
    我售出十万吨大米，购进三十五万吨食糖
    新华社哈瓦那7日电　为执行中国和古巴的贸易协定，两国贸易机构5日晚上在这里签订了总数为一千三百万英镑的两项贸易合同。
    这两项合同是：中国粮谷油脂出口公司和古巴对外贸易银行签订的售给古巴十万吨中国大米的合同；中国食品出口公司和古巴对外贸易银行签订的售给中国三十五万吨古巴食糖的合同。
