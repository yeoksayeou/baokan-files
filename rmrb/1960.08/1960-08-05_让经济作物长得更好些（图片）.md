Let Economic Crops Grow Better

    In the Eight-Seven People's Commune of Xiangcheng County, Henan, the early tobacco is growing tall and leafy, promising a good harvest. This is a commune member pruning tobacco in the tobacco field. Yu Huixi (Xinhua News Agency)
    The Shitou People's Commune of Guangsi County, Guangdong Province, makes use of scattered wasteland to widely plant miscellaneous grains and cassava. This is a commune member cultivating and weeding in the cassava field. Tan Shaoshu (Xinhua News Agency)
    Since the Chenxiang People's Commune of Siyang County, Jiangsu Province, launched a campaign to meticulously manage cotton fields, most cotton plants have grown more than a dozen fruiting branches. The picture shows commune members applying fertilizer in the cotton field. Zhu Junjie (Xinhua News Agency)
    The hemp in Wenjiang County, Sichuan Province, is growing tall and lush this year. This is Huang Jinan (right), the Party Secretary of Yongxing Commune, happily viewing the abundant hemp. Xinhua News Agency reporter Sun Zhongjing

### 让经济作物长得更好些（图片）

1960-08-05
第4版()
专栏：

    让经济作物长得更好些
    河南襄城县八七公社的早烟长得株高叶大，丰收有望。这是社员在烟地里打杈。              于惠喜摄（新华社稿）
    广东广四县石狗公社利用零散荒地广种杂粮和木薯。这是社员们在木薯地中耕除草。                        谭少枢摄（新华社稿）
    江苏泗阳县城厢公社开展精管棉田运动以来，一般棉株都长有十几个果枝。图为社员们正在棉田里施肥。
                          朱俊杰摄（新华社稿）
    四川温江县今年的大麻长得株高茂盛。这是永兴公社党委书记黄继南（右）在喜看丰盛的大麻。新华社记者    孙忠靖摄
