Title: Bulgarian Folk Song and Dance Troupe Departs for Performance Tour of Five Countries: USSR, Mongolia, China, North Korea, and Vietnam. Will Visit and Perform in China for One Month Starting in Early September.
Author:
Date: 1960-08-22
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, Sofia, August 19th: The Bulgarian National Folk Song and Dance Troupe left Bulgaria by plane on August 17th and 19th in succession to visit and perform in five countries: the Soviet Union, Mongolia, China, North Korea, and Vietnam.

The song and dance troupe consists of more than one hundred people. The head of the troupe is Bulgarian People's Artist, Dimitrov Prize winner, and Secretary-General of the Composers Association, Filip Kutev. After visiting the Soviet Union and Mongolia, the song and dance troupe will visit and perform in China for one month starting in early September.

The Bulgarian National Folk Song and Dance Troupe is the largest national song and dance troupe in Bulgaria, with a high level of performing skills, and is very popular with the Bulgarian people. It is visiting China in accordance with the 1960 executive plan of the Sino-Bulgarian Cultural Cooperation Agreement.

Those who went to the airport to see off the song and dance troupe included: Stefan Stanchev, Vice Chairman of the Bulgarian Committee for Foreign Friendship and Cultural Relations, Wang Peng, Chargé d'Affaires ad interim of the Chinese Embassy in Bulgaria, and Wang Xixiong, First Secretary, etc.

Before the song and dance troupe left Bulgaria, Wang Xixiong held a reception at the embassy on the evening of August 16 to see them off. Stefan Stanchev also attended the reception. After the reception, the Chinese dance drama film "Lotus Lantern" was screened.

### 到苏蒙中朝越五国访问演出  保加利亚民间歌舞团启程  将于九月初来我国访问演出一个月

1960-08-22
第5版()
专栏：

    到苏蒙中朝越五国访问演出
    保加利亚民间歌舞团启程
    将于九月初来我国访问演出一个月
    新华社索非亚19日电　保加利亚国家民间歌舞团8月17、19两日陆续乘飞机离开保加利亚，前往苏联、蒙古、中国、朝鲜、越南等五个国家进行访问演出。
    歌舞团共一百余人，团长是保加利亚人民演员、季米特洛夫奖金获得者和作曲家协会总书记菲·库捷夫。歌舞团将在访问苏联和蒙古后，于9月初到中国访问演出一个月。
    保加利亚国家民间歌舞团是保加利亚最大的国家歌舞团，有着高度的表演技巧，深受保加利亚人民的欢迎。它是根据中保文化合作协定1960年执行计划前往中国访问的。
    到机场欢送歌舞团的有：保加利亚对外友好和文化联络委员会副主席斯特凡·斯坦契夫、中国驻保加利亚大使馆临时代办王彭、一等秘书王西雄等。
    歌舞团离保加利亚前，王西雄于8月16日晚上在使馆举行了招待会，为歌舞团饯行。斯特凡·斯坦契夫也参加了招待会。会后放映了中国舞剧影片“宝莲灯”。
