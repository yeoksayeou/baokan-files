Title: Liu Shaoqi, Premier Zhou Enlai, and Foreign Minister Chen Yi Send Telegrams to Congratulate Indonesia on the 15th Anniversary of Independence
Author:
Date: 1960-08-17
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, August 16th: Chairman Liu Shaoqi of the People's Republic of China today sent a telegram to President Sukarno of the Republic of Indonesia, congratulating him on the 15th anniversary of the Republic of Indonesia's independence. The telegram reads as follows:
    To His Excellency President Sukarno of the Republic of Indonesia:
    On the occasion of the 15th anniversary of the independence of the Republic of Indonesia, I, on behalf of the state and the Chinese people, and in my own name, extend my warm congratulations to Your Excellency and the Indonesian people.
    May the profound friendship between the peoples of China and Indonesia continue to consolidate and develop. I wish Your Excellency and the Indonesian people new achievements in the cause of safeguarding national independence and building your own country. I wish the Republic of Indonesia prosperity and strength.
                  Liu Shaoqi, Chairman of the People's Republic of China
                                  August 16, 1960

    Xinhua News Agency, August 16th: Premier Zhou Enlai of the State Council of the People's Republic of China today sent a telegram to President and Premier Sukarno of the Republic of Indonesia, congratulating him on the 15th anniversary of the Republic of Indonesia's independence. The telegram reads as follows:
    To His Excellency President and Premier Sukarno of the Republic of Indonesia:
    On the occasion of the 15th anniversary of the independence of the Republic of Indonesia, I, on behalf of the Government of the People's Republic of China, and in my own name, extend my sincere congratulations to Your Exellency and the Indonesian government.
    A deep friendship has long existed between the peoples of China and Indonesia. The Chinese government and people have always believed that cherishing this friendship is in line with the wishes and fundamental interests of the two peoples. I sincerely hope that, through the joint efforts of both sides, the friendly and cooperative relations between our two countries will be maintained and developed under the guidance of the Five Principles of Peaceful Coexistence and the Bandung Conference spirit. I wish our two countries jointly make new contributions to the cause of promoting solidarity and friendship among the peoples of Asia and Africa and defending world peace.
            Zhou Enlai, Premier of the State Council of the People's Republic of China
                                August 16, 1960

    Xinhua News Agency, August 16th: Foreign Minister Chen Yi of the People's Republic of China today sent a telegram to Foreign Minister Subandrio of the Republic of Indonesia, congratulating him on the 15th anniversary of the Republic of Indonesia's independence. The telegram reads as follows:
    To His Excellency Dr. Subandrio, Foreign Minister of the Republic of Indonesia:
    On the occasion of the National Day of the Republic of Indonesia, I would like to extend my sincere congratulations to Your Excellency. I wish the friendly relations between China and Indonesia continue to develop. I wish Your Excellency new achievements in the noble cause of safeguarding Asian-African and world peace.
                Chen Yi, Foreign Minister of the People's Republic of China
                                August 16, 1960

### 刘少奇主席周恩来总理陈毅外长  电贺印度尼西亚独立十五周年

1960-08-17
第1版()
专栏：

    刘少奇主席周恩来总理陈毅外长
    电贺印度尼西亚独立十五周年
    新华社16日讯　中华人民共和国主席刘少奇今天打电报给印度尼西亚共和国总统苏加诺，祝贺印度尼西亚共和国独立十五周年。电文如下：印度尼西亚共和国苏加诺总统阁下：
    值此印度尼西亚共和国独立十五周年之际，我谨代表国家和中国人民，并且以我个人的名义，向阁下和印度尼西亚人民表示热诚的祝贺。
    祝中国和印度尼西亚两国人民之间的深厚友谊继续巩固和发展。祝阁下和印度尼西亚人民在维护民族独立和建设自己国家的事业中取得新的成就。祝印度尼西亚共和国繁荣富强。
                  中华人民共和国主席　刘少奇
                                  1960年8月16日
    新华社16日讯　中华人民共和国国务院总理周恩来今天打电报给印度尼西亚共和国总统兼总理苏加诺，祝贺印度尼西亚共和国独立十五周年。电文如下：印度尼西亚共和国苏加诺总统兼总理阁下：
    值此印度尼西亚共和国独立十五周年之际，我谨代表中华人民共和国政府，并且以我个人的名义，向阁下和印度尼西亚政府表示诚挚的祝贺。
    中国和印度尼西亚两国人民间长期存在着深厚的友谊。中国政府和人民一向认为，珍惜这种友谊是符合两国人民的愿望和根本利益的。我衷心地希望，通过双方共同的努力，我们两国间的友好合作关系，在和平共处五项原则和万隆会议精神的指导下，将会得到维护和发展。祝贵我两国在促进亚非各国人民团结友好和保卫世界和平的事业中共同作出新的贡献。
            中华人民共和国国务院总理　周恩来
                                1960年8月16日
    新华社16日讯　中华人民共和国外交部长陈毅今天打电报给印度尼西亚共和国外交部长苏班德里约，祝贺印度尼西亚共和国独立十五周年。电文如下：印度尼西亚共和国外交部长苏班德里约博士阁下：
    值此印度尼西亚共和国国庆之际，我谨向阁下表示诚挚的祝贺。祝中国和印度尼西亚两国的友好关系继续发展。祝阁下在维护亚非和世界和平的崇高事业中取得新的成就。
                中华人民共和国外交部长　陈毅
                                1960年8月16日
