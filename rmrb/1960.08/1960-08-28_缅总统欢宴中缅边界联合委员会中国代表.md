Title: Burmese President Hosts Banquet for Chinese Representatives of the Joint Sino-Burmese Boundary Committee
Author:
Date: 1960-08-28
Page: 2
Xinhua News Agency, Rangoon, August 26th: Burmese President U Win Maung hosted a banquet here tonight in honor of the Chinese representatives and advisors of the Joint Sino-Burmese Boundary Committee, headed by Yao Zhongming.

Among those present at the banquet were the Chief Justice of the Burmese Supreme Court, U Myint Thein, Foreign Minister Sao Khun Kyi, and Permanent Secretary of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs, James Barrington. Also present were the Burmese Ambassador to China, Kyaing Wynn, the Burmese Chief Representative of the Joint Sino-Burmese Boundary Committee, Brigadier Aung Gyi, and other Burmese representatives and advisors. The Chinese Embassy in Burma's Chargé d'affaires ad interim, Lei Yang, also attended the banquet.

The banquet was filled with a warm and friendly atmosphere. After the banquet, Yao Zhongming and other Chinese representatives and advisors of the Joint Sino-Burmese Boundary Committee, accompanied by President U Win Maung, watched a traditional Burmese puppet show.

President U Win Maung received the Chinese Chief Representative Yao Zhongming at the Presidential Palace this afternoon.

During the meeting, President U Win Maung expressed great pleasure at the smooth resolution of the Sino-Burmese boundary issue and highly praised the spirit of the surveying staff in dealing with difficult tasks. President U Win Maung said that the smooth resolution of the Sino-Burmese boundary issue greatly encouraged the entire Burmese people.

Also present at the meeting was the Chinese Embassy in Burma's Chargé d'affaires ad interim, Lei Yang.

### 缅总统欢宴中缅边界联合委员会中国代表

1960-08-28
第2版()
专栏：

    缅总统欢宴中缅边界联合委员会中国代表
    新华社仰光26日电　缅甸总统吴温貌今天晚上在这里举行宴会，欢迎以姚仲明为首的中缅边界联合委员会的中国代表和顾问。
    出席宴会的有缅甸最高法院首席法官吴敏登、外交部长藻昆卓和外交部常任秘书詹姆斯·巴林顿。出席宴会的还有缅甸驻中国大使叫温、中缅边界联合委员会缅甸首席代表昂季准将以及其他缅甸代表和顾问。中国驻缅甸大使馆临时代办雷扬也出席了宴会。
    宴会上充满了热烈友好的气氛。宴会以后，姚仲明和中缅边界联合委员会的其他中国代表和顾问在吴温貌总统的陪同下观看了一出缅甸传统的木偶戏。
    吴温貌总统今天下午在总统府接见了中国首席代表姚仲明。
    在接见时，吴温貌总统对中缅边界问题的顺利解决表示非常高兴，并且盛赞勘察工作人员对待困难任务的精神。吴温貌总统说，中缅边界问题的顺利解决使全体缅甸人民受到很大的鼓舞。
    接见时在座的有中国驻缅甸大使馆临时代办雷扬。
