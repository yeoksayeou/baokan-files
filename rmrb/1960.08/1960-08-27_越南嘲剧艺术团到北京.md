Title: Vietnamese Cheo Art Troupe Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1960-08-27
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, August 26th: The Vietnamese Democratic Republic Cheo Art Troupe, a group of fifty-five people led by troupe leader Vu Thu, arrived in Beijing by train this morning. The art troupe is visiting our country according to the 1960 implementation plan of the Sino-Vietnamese cultural cooperation agreement.

Greeting them at the station were Zhang Zhixiang, acting director of the Commission for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries; Wang Yi, deputy director of the First Bureau of the Commission for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries; Ma Yanxiang, deputy director of the Art Bureau of the Ministry of Culture; Shi Lemeng, director of the Sino-Vietnamese Friendship Association; and people from literary and art circles in the capital.

Tran Trong Quat, Charge d'Affaires ad interim of the Vietnamese Democratic Republic in China, and diplomatic officials of the embassy were also at the station to welcome them.

### 越南嘲剧艺术团到北京

1960-08-27
第4版()
专栏：

    越南嘲剧艺术团到北京
    新华社26日讯　越南民主共和国嘲剧艺术团一行五十五人，在团长武秋率领下，于今天上午乘火车到达北京。艺术团是根据中越文化合作协定1960年执行计划来我国访问的。
    到车站欢迎的，有对外文委代理主任张致祥，对外文委一司副司长王仪，文化部艺术局副局长马彦祥，中越友好协会理事时乐蒙以及首都文艺界人士等。
    越南民主共和国驻中国临时代办陈仲括和大使馆外交官员，也到车站欢迎。
