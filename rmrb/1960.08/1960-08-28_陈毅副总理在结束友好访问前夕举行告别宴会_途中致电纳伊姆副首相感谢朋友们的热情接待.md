Title: Vice Premier Chen Yi Holds Farewell Banquet Before Concluding Friendly Visit; Sends Telegram to Deputy Prime Minister Naim en Route, Thanking Friends for Their Warm Reception
Author:
Date: 1960-08-28
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Kabul, August 26th: Chen Yi, Vice Premier of the State Council and Minister of Foreign Affairs of the People's Republic of China, who is about to conclude his friendly visit to the Kingdom of Afghanistan, held a farewell banquet here at the Kabul Hotel tonight.

Among those attending the banquet were Afghan Prime Minister Daud, Deputy Prime Minister Ali Muhammad, Deputy Prime Minister and Minister of Foreign Affairs Sardar Mohammad Naim, and Marshal Shah Wali Khan Ghazi, uncle of the King.

Also attending the banquet were senior government officials and military officers, and Abdul Samad, the Afghan Ambassador to China.

Diplomatic envoys from various countries were also invited to attend the banquet.

Also attending the banquet were Kong Yuan, Deputy Director of the Foreign Affairs Office of the State Council, Geng Biao, Vice Minister of Foreign Affairs, and other members of Vice Premier Chen Yi's entourage. Hao Ting, the Chinese Ambassador to Afghanistan, also attended the banquet.

The banquet proceeded in a friendly and cordial atmosphere. Hosts and guests repeatedly proposed toasts to the lasting friendship between China and Afghanistan.

Xinhua News Agency, Kabul, August 27th: Marshal Chen Yi, Vice Premier of the State Council and Minister of Foreign Affairs of the People's Republic of China, sent a telegram from his special plane to Naim, Deputy Prime Minister and Minister of Foreign Affairs of the Kingdom of Afghanistan, before leaving Afghan territory. The telegram is as follows:

To His Royal Highness Sardar Mohammad Naim, Deputy Prime Minister and Minister of Foreign Affairs of the Kingdom of Afghanistan, Kabul

Respected Your Royal Highness Sardar Mohammad Naim, as I am about to leave Afghan territory, please allow me to once again express my heartfelt gratitude to you and the government of the Kingdom of Afghanistan for your warm reception. Please convey my thanks to His Royal Highness the Prime Minister and all the leaders of the government of the Kingdom of Afghanistan, as well as to our Afghan friends, and please convey my respects to His Majesty the King.

I wish the Kingdom of Afghanistan prosperity and its people happiness. I wish the friendly and cooperative relations between our two countries, affirmed in treaty form, will continue to be consolidated and developed.

Chen Yi
Vice Premier of the State Council and Minister of Foreign Affairs of the People'

### 陈毅副总理在结束友好访问前夕举行告别宴会  途中致电纳伊姆副首相感谢朋友们的热情接待

1960-08-28
第1版()
专栏：

    陈毅副总理在结束友好访问前夕举行告别宴会
    途中致电纳伊姆副首相感谢朋友们的热情接待
    新华社喀布尔26日电　即将结束对阿富汗王国友好访问的中华人民共和国国务院副总理兼外交部长陈毅今天晚上在这里的喀布尔饭店举行告别宴会。
    出席宴会的有阿富汗首相达乌德，副首相阿里·穆罕默德，副首相兼外交大臣萨达尔·穆罕默德·纳伊姆，国王的叔父沙阿·华利汗·加齐元帅。
    出席宴会的还有政府高级官员和军官，阿富汗驻中国大使阿卜杜勒·萨马德。
    各国外交使节也应邀出席了宴会。
    出席宴会的还有我国国务院外事办公室副主任孔原，外交部副部长耿飚，以及陈毅副总理的其他随行人员。中国驻阿富汗大使郝汀也出席了宴会。
    宴会在友好和亲切的气氛中进行。宾主双方一再建议为中国和阿富汗之间的持久友谊干杯。
    新华社喀布尔27日电　中华人民共和国国务院副总理兼外交部长陈毅元帅，在离开阿富汗国境之前，从他所乘的专机上打电报给阿富汗王国副首相兼外交大臣纳伊姆。电文如下：喀布尔阿富汗王国副首相兼外交大臣萨达尔·穆罕默
  德·纳伊姆亲王殿下
    尊敬的萨达尔·穆罕默德·纳伊姆亲王殿下，当我就要离开阿富汗国土的时候，请允许我再次对您和阿富汗王国政府这次对我的热情接待表示衷心的感谢。请转达我对首相亲王殿下和阿富汗王国政府全体领导人以及阿富汗朋友们的谢意，并请转达我对国王陛下的敬意。
    祝阿富汗王国繁荣，人民幸福，祝我们两国之间用条约形式肯定下来的友好合作关系不断巩固和发展。
  中华人民共和国国务院副总理兼外交部长
                                      陈　毅
