Title: Haitian Cultural Friendship Delegation Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1960-08-20
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, 19th. The Haitian Cultural Friendship Delegation, led by head of delegation Jean Laratte, arrived in Beijing by plane this morning at the invitation of the Chinese-Latin American Friendship Association.

    Zhu Boshen, Executive Director of the Chinese-Latin American Friendship Association, and others welcomed them at the airport.

### 海地文化友好代表团到京

1960-08-20
第6版()
专栏：

    海地文化友好代表团到京
    新华社19日讯　应中国拉丁美洲友好协会邀请来我国访问的海地文化友好代表团，在团长让·拉脱率领下，今天上午乘飞机到达北京。
    到机场欢迎的有中国拉丁美洲友好协会常务理事朱伯深等人。
