Title: Bolivian Guests Leave Beijing for Home

Author:

Date: 1960-08-13
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, August 12th: Oscar Donoso López, First Vice-President of the Bolivian Senate, and Jaime Arellano Casaneda, Member of the Bolivian Chamber of Deputies, concluded their visit to China and left Beijing by plane this morning to return home.

They were seen off at the airport by Hu Yuzhi, Vice-President of the Chinese People's Institute of Foreign Affairs, Yu Xinqing, Deputy Secretary-General of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress, and others.

The members of the Bolivian Senate delegation to China, led by Donoso, had returned home earlier.

### 玻利维亚客人离京回国

1960-08-13
第6版()
专栏：

    玻利维亚客人离京回国
    新华社12日讯　玻利维亚参议院第一副议长奥斯卡·多诺索·洛佩斯和玻利维亚众议院议员海梅·阿雷利亚诺·卡萨内达，结束了在中国的参观访问后，于今天上午乘飞机离开北京回国。
    在机场欢送的有，中国人民外交学会副会长胡愈之，全国人民代表大会常务委员会副秘书长余心清等。
    由多诺索率领的玻利维亚参议院访华代表团的团员们已先期回国。
