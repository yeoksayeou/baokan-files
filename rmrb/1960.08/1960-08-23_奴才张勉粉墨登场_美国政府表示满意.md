Title: Lackey Chang Myon Takes the Stage; US Government Expresses Satisfaction
Author:
Date: 1960-08-23
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, Washington: The United States government is extremely satisfied that Chang Myon, with American support, has finally obtained the position of "Prime Minister" in the South Korean puppet cabinet. White, a spokesman for the U.S. State Department, issued a statement on the 19th, stating that the U.S. government intends to "continue to maintain the most friendly relations" with Chang Myon's "government" and wants it to "cooperate" with the United States for the so-called "security and progress of the free world."

### 奴才张勉粉墨登场  美国政府表示满意

1960-08-23
第6版()
专栏：

    奴才张勉粉墨登场
    美国政府表示满意
    据新华社讯　华盛顿消息：美国政府对张勉终于在美国的支持下获得南朝鲜伪内阁“总理”职位表示极为满意。美国国务院发言人怀特19日发表声明，表示美国政府打算同张勉的“政府”“继续保持最友好的关系”，要它为所谓“自由世界的安全和进步”而同美国“合作”。
