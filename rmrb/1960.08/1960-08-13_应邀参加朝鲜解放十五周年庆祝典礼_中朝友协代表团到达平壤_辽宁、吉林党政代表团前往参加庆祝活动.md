Title: Invited to Attend the 15th Anniversary Celebration of the Liberation of Korea, Chinese-Korean Friendship Association Delegation Arrives in Pyongyang; Liaoning and Jilin Party and Government Delegations Go to Participate in Celebration Activities
Author:
Date: 1960-08-13
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, August 12th: The delegation of the Chinese-Korean Friendship Association, invited to the Democratic People's Republic of Korea to participate in the 15th anniversary celebration of the liberation of Korea, carrying the profound friendship of the Chinese people, left Beijing by plane this morning for Pyongyang.

The Chinese-Korean Friendship Association delegation consists of five people. The head of the delegation is Li Dequan, President of the Chinese-Korean Friendship Association and Minister of Health, and the deputy head is Li Jiebo, Director of the Chinese-Korean Friendship Association, Alternate Member of the CPC Central Committee, and Vice Chairman of the All-China Federation of Trade Unions.

More than 30 people, including Chu Tunan, Lao She, Cui Yitian, Xu Zhizhen, and Li Qiangfen, went to the airport to see them off. Korean Ambassador to China Li Yong-ho and diplomatic officials of the embassy, as well as Kim Jong-heng, head of the Korean-Chinese Friendship Association delegation in Beijing, and Park Poong-shik, deputy head, also went to the airport to see them off. A group of Korean children presented flowers to the members of the Chinese-Korean Friendship Association delegation.

Zhao Yang, Deputy Editor-in-Chief of the Heilongjiang Daily, who was invited to visit Korea under the Sino-Korean Cultural Cooperation Agreement and will also participate in the celebration activities, also went to Pyongyang on the same plane as the Chinese-Korean Friendship Association delegation today.

Xinhua News Agency, Pyongyang, August 12th: The delegation of the Chinese-Korean Friendship Association, invited to Korea to participate in the "August 15th" 15th Anniversary Celebration of the Liberation of Korea, arrived in Pyongyang by plane at noon today, led by Li Dequan, President of the Chinese-Korean Friendship Association and Minister of Health of China, and Li Jiebo, Director of the Chinese-Korean Friendship Association, Alternate Member of the CPC Central Committee, and Vice Chairman of the All-China Federation of Trade Unions.

After Li Dequan, Li Jiebo, and others got off the plane, they warmly shook hands with Ha Yang-cheon, Chairman of the Korean-Chinese Friendship Association, and others who welcomed them at the airport. A team of Korean children in colorful national costumes presented flowers to the delegation.

Among those who welcomed the delegation at the airport were: Song Young, Chairman of the Korean Committee for Foreign Cultural Relations; Choi Chang-seok, Minister of Public Health; Hyun Pil-hoon, Vice Chairman of the Korean-Chinese Friendship Association; and Ryu Jang-shik, Vice Minister of Foreign Affairs.

Liu Xianglun, Chargé d'Affaires ad interim of the Chinese Embassy in Korea, and other embassy staff members welcomed them at the airport.

Xinhua News Agency, Shenyang, August 12th: The Liaoning Provincial Party and Government Delegation, invited by the North Pyongan Provincial Committee of the Korean Workers' Party and the North Pyongan Provincial People's Committee of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea to participate in the "August 15th" 15th Anniversary Celebration of the Liberation of Korea, led by Li Huang, Secretary of the Liaoning Provincial Committee of the CPC and President of the Liaoning Branch of the Chinese-Korean Friendship Association, departed from Shenyang by train for Sinuiju at 7:00 p.m. today.

Accompanying the Liaoning Provincial Party and Government Delegation is an art troupe composed of the Liaoning Provincial Youth Peking Opera Troupe and the Shenyang Acrobatic Troupe, who will perform celebratory performances in North Pyongan Province of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea.

Xinhua News Agency, Changchun, August 11th: An 18-member delegation of the Jilin Provincial Party and Government, led by Fu Zhensheng, Head of the Delegation, Secretary of the Jilin Provincial Party Committee, and Cui Cifeng and Zhang Wenhai, Vice Governors of Jilin Province, took a train tonight separately to North Hamgyong Province, Jagang Province, and Ryanggang Province of Korea to participate in the 15th anniversary celebration of the liberation of Korea and celebrate the festival with the Korean people. The delegation was invited by the Labor Parties and governments of North Hamgyong Province, Jagang Province, and Ryanggang Province of Korea.

### 应邀参加朝鲜解放十五周年庆祝典礼  中朝友协代表团到达平壤  辽宁、吉林党政代表团前往参加庆祝活动

1960-08-13
第1版()
专栏：

    应邀参加朝鲜解放十五周年庆祝典礼
    中朝友协代表团到达平壤
    辽宁、吉林党政代表团前往参加庆祝活动
    新华社12日讯　应邀到朝鲜民主主义人民共和国参加朝鲜解放十五周年庆祝活动的中朝友好协会代表团，带着中国人民的深厚情谊，今天上午乘飞机离开北京前往平壤。
    中朝友好协会代表团共由五人组成。团长是中朝友好协会会长、卫生部部长李德全，副团长是中朝友好协会理事、中共中央候补委员、中华全国总工会副主席李颉伯。
    到飞机场欢送的，有楚图南、老舍、崔义田、许之桢、李强奋等三十余人。朝鲜驻中国大使李永镐和大使馆外交官员，在北京的朝中友协代表团团长金钟恒，副团长朴丰植也到机场欢送。一群朝鲜儿童向中朝友协代表团的成员们献了鲜花。
    根据中朝文化合作协定应邀前往朝鲜访问并将参加庆祝活动的黑龙江日报副总编辑赵扬，今天也和中朝友协代表团同机前往平壤。
    新华社平壤12日电　应邀前来朝鲜参加“八·一五”朝鲜解放十五周年庆祝典礼的中朝友好协会代表团，在中朝友好协会会长、中国卫生部长李德全和中朝友好协会理事、中共中央候补委员、中华全国总工会副主席李颉伯率领下，今天中午乘飞机到达平壤。
    李德全和李颉伯等下飞机后，同到机场欢迎的朝中友好协会委员长河仰天等热烈握手，一队穿着彩色民族服装的朝鲜儿童向代表团献了鲜花。
    到机场欢迎代表团的还有：朝鲜对外文化联络委员会委员长宋影，保健相崔昌锡，朝中友好协会副委员长玄弼勋，外务省副相柳章植等。
    我国驻朝鲜大使馆临时代办刘祥纶和使馆其它人员到机场欢迎。
    新华社沈阳12日电　应朝鲜劳动党平安北道委员会和朝鲜民主主义人民共和国平安北道人民委员会邀请，前往参加朝鲜“八·一五”解放十五周年庆祝活动的辽宁省党政代表团，在中共辽宁省委书记、中朝友好协会辽宁省分会会长李荒率领下，于今天下午七时，乘火车从沈阳出发去新义州。
    随辽宁省党政代表团前往的，还有一个由辽宁省青年京剧团和沈阳市杂技团组成的艺术团，他们将在朝鲜民主主义人民共和国平安北道，进行庆祝演出。
    据新华社长春11日电　吉林省党政代表团一行十八人，在团长、中共吉林省委书记处书记富振声，吉林省副省长崔次丰、张文海率领下，今晚乘车分别前往朝鲜咸镜北道、慈江道、两江道，参加朝鲜解放十五周年庆祝活动，同朝鲜人民一起欢度节日。代表团是应朝鲜咸镜北道、慈江道、两江道劳动党和政府的邀请前往的。
