Title: West German Peace Activists Walter Diehl and Others Arrive in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1960-08-28
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, August 27th. At the invitation of the Chinese People's Committee for World Peace, West German peace activists Walter Diehl, a member of the World Peace Council, and Kurt Beiersdorf, executive member of the West German Committee Against Atomic Armament, who attended the World Conference Against Atomic and Hydrogen Bombs and for Disarmament in Tokyo, arrived in Beijing by plane on the 27th for a visit to our country.

### 西德和平人士华尔特·迪尔等抵京

1960-08-28
第6版()
专栏：

    西德和平人士华尔特·迪尔等抵京
    新华社27日讯　应中国人民保卫世界和平委员会邀请，于出席东京禁止原子弹氢弹和争取裁军世界大会后来我国访问的西德和平人士、世界和平理事会理事华尔特·迪尔，和西德反对原子武装行动委员会执行委员克尔脱·贝维尔斯道夫，于27日乘飞机到达北京。
