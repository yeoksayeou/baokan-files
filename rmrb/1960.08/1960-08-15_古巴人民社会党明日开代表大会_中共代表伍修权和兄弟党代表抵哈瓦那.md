Title: Cuban People's Socialist Party to Hold Congress Tomorrow; Chinese Communist Party Representative Wu Xiuquan and Representatives of Fraternal Parties Arrive in Havana
Author:
Date: 1960-08-15
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, Havana, August 13th: According to Cuba's "Hoy" newspaper, delegations from communist and workers' parties of various countries, invited to attend the Ninth National Congress of the Cuban People's Socialist Party, have begun arriving in Havana.

Yesterday afternoon, representatives and delegations from eleven communist and workers' parties arrived, including: Wu Xiuquan, representative of the Chinese Communist Party; Pastirik Zlatko Tula, representative of the Czechoslovak Communist Party; Han Sang-du, representative of the Korean Workers' Party; Jacques Duclos, representative of the French Communist Party; Rubin Avramov, representative of the Bulgarian Communist Party; Paul Verner, representative of the German Socialist Unity Party; Joko Sujono Handojo, representative of the Indonesian Communist Party; Andrzej Werblan, representative of the Polish United Workers' Party; Arnoldo Ferreto, representative of the People's Vanguard Party of Costa Rica; Vilio Spano, representative of the Italian Communist Party; and Montes, representative of the Chilean Communist Party.

The representatives were welcomed at the airport by party leaders headed by Aníbal Escalante, Executive Secretary of the Cuban People's Socialist Party.

The Ninth Congress of the Cuban People's Socialist Party will open in Havana on August 16th. This is the first congress held since the party was forced underground by the Batista tyranny.

The first item on the Congress agenda is to hear the report of Blas Roca, General Secretary of the National Committee. Subsequent agenda items include discussion of the new party program and constitution, and the election of the party's leading bodies.

### 古巴人民社会党明日开代表大会  中共代表伍修权和兄弟党代表抵哈瓦那

1960-08-15
第5版()
专栏：

    古巴人民社会党明日开代表大会
    中共代表伍修权和兄弟党代表抵哈瓦那
    新华社哈瓦那13日电　据古巴“今日报”报道，应邀参加古巴人民社会党第九次全国代表大会的各国共产党和工人党的代表团开始抵达哈瓦那。
    昨天下午到达这里的有十一个国家共产党和工人党的代表和代表团，其中有：中国共产党代表伍修权；捷克斯洛伐克共产党代表帕斯蒂里克·兹拉特科·图拉；朝鲜劳动党代表韩相斗；法国共产党代表雅克·杜克洛；保加利亚共产党代表鲁宾·阿弗拉莫夫；德国统一社会党代表保罗·维尔纳；印度尼西亚共产党代表佐科苏佐诺·汉多约；波兰统一工人党代表安杰伊·维尔布兰；哥斯达黎加人民先锋党代表阿诺耳多·费雷托；意大利共产党代表维利奥·斯巴诺和智利共产党代表蒙特斯。
    代表们在机场上受到以古巴人民社会党执行书记阿尼瓦耳·埃斯卡兰特为首的党的领导人的欢迎。
    古巴人民社会党第九次代表大会将于8月16日在哈瓦那开幕，这是党被巴蒂斯塔暴政打入地下之后召开的第一次代表大会。
    代表大会第一项议程是听取全国委员会总书记布拉斯·罗加的报告，以后几项议程是讨论新的党纲和党章和选举党的领导机构。
