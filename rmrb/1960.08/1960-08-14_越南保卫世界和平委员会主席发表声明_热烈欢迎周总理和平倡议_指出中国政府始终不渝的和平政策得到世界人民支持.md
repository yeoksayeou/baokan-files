Title: Chairman of the Vietnam Committee for the Defense of World Peace Issues Statement: Warmly Welcomes Premier Zhou's Peace Initiative, Points Out That the Chinese Government's Consistent Policy of Peace Has the Support of the People of the World
Author:
Date: 1960-08-14
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, Hanoi, August 13th: Le Dinh Tham, Chairman of the Vietnam Committee for the Defense of World Peace, issued a statement today, warmly welcoming Premier Zhou Enlai's peace initiative on the conclusion of a treaty of non-aggression and peace among countries in Asia and the Pacific coast, and on turning the region into a nuclear-weapon-free zone. He also welcomed the victory of the Sixth World Conference Against Atomic and Hydrogen Bombs and for Total Disarmament.

The statement said, "Under the current situation in Asia, Premier Zhou Enlai's initiative is an important peaceful undertaking that embodies the wishes of the peace-loving people of Asia and the world. It is not only a strong guarantee for peace and national independence in Asia and the Pacific coastal countries, but it will also strengthen the unity of the peoples of all countries that adhere to the Five Principles of Peaceful Coexistence."

The statement said, "The fact that the Tokyo Conference unanimously adopted a resolution supporting Premier Zhou Enlai's initiative proves that the Chinese government's consistent policy of peace has been warmly welcomed, sympathized with, and supported by the people of the world."

The statement pointed out that the victory of the Sixth World Conference Against Atomic and Hydrogen Bombs marks a new development in the movement of the people of the world to oppose US imperialism, which is conspiring to enslave all nations and push mankind into the abyss of atomic war.

The statement expressed that the Vietnamese people, excited by the great victory of the Tokyo Conference, will strengthen the struggle against the U.S.-Ngo Dinh Diem clique for peaceful reunification of the fatherland, strengthen solidarity with socialist countries and peace-loving people in Asia and the world, oppose imperialism headed by the United States, demand comprehensive and complete disarmament, and contribute to defending peace in Asia and the world.

### 越南保卫世界和平委员会主席发表声明  热烈欢迎周总理和平倡议  指出中国政府始终不渝的和平政策得到世界人民支持

1960-08-14
第6版()
专栏：

    越南保卫世界和平委员会主席发表声明
    热烈欢迎周总理和平倡议
    指出中国政府始终不渝的和平政策得到世界人民支持
    新华社河内13日电　越南保卫世界和平委员会主席黎廷探今天发表声明，热烈欢迎周恩来总理的关于亚洲和太平洋沿岸各国缔结一项互不侵犯的和平公约和把这个地区建成为无核武器地区的和平倡议，欢迎第六届禁止原子弹氢弹和争取全面裁军世界大会所取得的胜利。
    声明说，“在亚洲当前的局势下，周恩来总理的倡议，是体现亚洲和世界爱好和平人民愿望的重要的和平创举。它不但是亚洲和太平洋沿岸国家的和平和民族独立的有力保证，而且还将加强奉行和平共处五项原则的各国人民的团结。”
    声明说，“东京会议一致通过决议支持周恩来总理的倡议这件事，证明中国政府始终不渝的和平政策得到世界人民的热烈欢迎、同情和支持。”
    声明指出，第六届禁止原子弹氢弹世界大会的胜利，标志着世界人民反对正在阴谋奴役各民族、把人类推进原子战争深渊的美帝国主义的斗争运动的新进展。
    声明表示，为东京会议的巨大胜利感到兴奋的越南人民，将加强反对美国—吴庭艳集团、争取和平统一祖国的斗争，加强同社会主义国家、亚洲和世界爱好和平人民的团结，反对以美国为首的帝国主义，要求全面和彻底裁军，为保卫亚洲和世界和平作出贡献。
