Title: Our Acrobatic Troupe Ends Visit to the Soviet Union; Left Moscow on the 16th for Czechoslovakia
Author:
Date: 1960-08-18
Page: 6
    Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, 15th - The Chinese Chongqing Acrobatic Troupe, currently on a performance tour in the Soviet Union, gave its final performance this evening at the Gorky Park Open-Air Theater in Moscow, receiving a warm welcome from the audience.

    Since June 21st, the Chongqing Acrobatic Troupe has performed a total of 59 shows in the Soviet Union, with 140,000 audience members. In Novosibirsk and Tyumen, the acrobatic troupe also held gatherings with representatives of local Communist Labor Brigades, workers, farmers, Soviet soldiers, and Young Pioneers, and performed programs for them. The Novosibirsk Film Studio also filmed a documentary about the acrobatic troupe's performances. Many of the acrobatic troupe's wonderful programs have been praised by Soviet audiences.

    At noon on the 15th, Soviet Deputy Minister of Culture Tsvetkov hosted a banquet for the Chongqing Acrobatic Troupe. At the banquet, Tsvetkov said that the Chongqing Acrobatic Troupe's performance tour had received a good response from Soviet audiences, and he wished the acrobatic troupe greater achievements in the future. Li Shaoyan, head of the Chongqing Acrobatic Troupe, said that during the troupe's visit to the Soviet Union, they deeply felt the enthusiasm of the Soviet people. The fraternal friendship between the Chinese and Soviet people forged in revolutionary struggles is eternal and unbreakable. The Chongqing Acrobatic Troupe will leave Moscow on the 16th for a performance tour in Czechoslovakia.

### 我杂技团结束访苏演出  十六日离莫斯科前往捷克斯洛伐克

1960-08-18
第6版()
专栏：

    我杂技团结束访苏演出
    十六日离莫斯科前往捷克斯洛伐克
    新华社莫斯科15日电　正在苏联进行访问演出的中国重庆杂技团今晚在莫斯科高尔基公园露天剧场进行了最后一次演出，受到了观众的热烈欢迎。
    重庆杂技团自从6月21日开始一共在苏联演出了五十九场，有十四万观众观看了演出。在新西伯利亚和秋明时，杂技团还与当地的共产主义劳动队代表、工人、农民、苏军战士和少先队员进行了联欢，并且为他们演出了节目。新西伯利亚电影制片厂还将杂技团的演出拍成了记录片。杂技团的许多精彩节目都得到了苏联观众的赞扬。
    15日中午，苏联文化部副部长茨维特科夫曾设宴招待重庆杂技团。茨维特科夫在宴会上说，重庆杂技团的访问演出在苏联观众中得到了良好的反应，他祝杂技团演员今后获得更大的成就。重庆杂技团团长李少言说，重庆杂技团在访苏演出中深深感到苏联人民的热情。中苏两国人民在革命斗争中结成的兄弟般的友谊是永恒的和牢不可破的。重庆杂技团将在16日离开莫斯科前往捷克斯洛伐克进行访问演出。
