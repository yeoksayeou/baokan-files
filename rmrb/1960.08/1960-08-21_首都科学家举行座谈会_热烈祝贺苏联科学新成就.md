Title: Capital Scientists Hold Symposium, Warmly Congratulating the New Achievements of Soviet Science
Author:
Date: 1960-08-21
Page: 6
Xinhua News Agency, August 20th: The Chinese Academy of Sciences held a symposium today to celebrate the successful launch of the second Soviet cosmic spaceship. Scientists in their speeches unanimously pointed out that this is another great contribution to human scientific endeavors and expressed warm congratulations to Soviet scientists and working people for this achievement.

    Wu Youxun, Vice President of the Chinese Academy of Sciences and physicist, said in his speech that the mission of the Soviet Union in launching the second cosmic spaceship is to further study the safety system for human flight and return to Earth, which shows that human interstellar flight will be realized in the near future. In the past three years, the research progress of the Soviet Union in cosmic flight has been very rapid, which proves the unparalleled superiority of the socialist system and the rapid progress of Soviet scientific undertakings.

    Mechanics expert Guo Yonghuai pointed out that the Soviet Union's research on cosmic flight is planned and step-by-step, with new purposes each time and new technological leaps each time. The second cosmic spaceship will study the guarantee of life for organisms in outer space. The sealed cabin replicates the living conditions on the ground and can conduct experiments on the life of organisms under long-term weightlessness. Through these experiments, humans may soon be able to fly into outer space.

    Biologist Tong Dizhou said that the Soviet Union's launch of the second cosmic spaceship is not only a great achievement in technical science, but also a great achievement in medicine and biology. It is not simple to ensure normal life in a sealed cabin in outer space. This experiment has created favorable conditions for human cosmic flight.

    Biologist Bei Shizhang pointed out that the launch of the second Soviet cosmic spaceship marks a new stage in the Soviet Union's research on outer space. At the same time, it has opened up broad prospects for the development of space biology and created favorable conditions for the development of space medicine.

    Astronomer Cheng Maolan believes that the launch of cosmic spaceships is of great significance for the development of astronomy. Cosmic spaceships can be used to study hot stars, interstellar matter, and the rotation period of Venus, and whether there is life on Mars.

    Zoologist Zhang Zhiyi said that the Soviet Union's cosmic spaceship will bring new materials for biological research, giving us new understanding of the origin of life and some mysteries in biology. We can expect newer and greater discoveries in biology.

    Geophysicist Ye Duzheng said that the Soviet Union's cosmic spaceship has broadened the research field of meteorology. We can use cosmic spaceships to directly observe the atmospheric conditions of other planets, which will help solve many theoretical problems of the Earth's atmospheric operation.

### 首都科学家举行座谈会  热烈祝贺苏联科学新成就

1960-08-21
第6版()
专栏：

    首都科学家举行座谈会
    热烈祝贺苏联科学新成就
    新华社20日讯　中国科学院今天为苏联成功地发射第二个宇宙飞船举行座谈会，科学家们在发言中一致指出，这是对人类科学事业的又一次巨大贡献，并对苏联科学家和劳动人民的这一成就表示热烈祝贺。
    中国科学院副院长、物理学家吴有训在发言中谈到，苏联发射第二个宇宙飞船的任务是进一步研究人的飞行和返回地球的安全系统，这说明人类的星际飞行在不久的将来就会实现。三年来，苏联在宇宙飞行方面的研究进展是非常迅速的，这证明社会主义制度的无比优越性和苏联科学事业正在突飞猛进。
    力学家郭永怀指出，苏联对宇宙飞行的研究是有计划、有步骤的，每一次都有新的目的，每一次在技术上都有新的跃进。第二个宇宙飞船将要研究生物在宇宙空间生活的保证，密封舱里复制了地面生活条件，可以进行生物在长期失重条件下生活的实验，通过这些实验，就会使人类可能很快地飞入宇宙空间。
    生物学家童第周说，苏联发射第二个宇宙飞船，不仅是技术科学方面的伟大成就，而且也是医学和生物学上的伟大成就。保证在宇宙空间中密封舱里的正常生活是很不简单的，这次试验为人类的宇宙飞行创造了有利条件。
    生物学家贝时璋指出，苏联第二个宇宙飞船的发射，标志着苏联对宇宙空间的研究又进入了一个新的阶段，同时，给宇宙生物学的发展开辟了广阔的道路，为宇宙医学的发展也创造了优越的条件。
    天文学家程茂兰认为，宇宙飞船的发射对天文学的发展有很大意义，宇宙飞船可以利用来研究热恒星、星际物质和金星的自转周期、火星的有无生物等问题。
    动物学家张致一说，苏联的宇宙飞船将会给生物学的研究带来新的材料，使我们对生命起源和生物学上的一些奥秘问题带来新的认识，可以期待生物学上会有更新的伟大发现。
    地球物理学家叶笃正说，苏联的宇宙飞船使气象学的研究境界更加开阔了，我们可以利用宇宙飞船来直接观测其它行星大气运行的状况，这有助于解决地球大气运行的许多理论问题。
