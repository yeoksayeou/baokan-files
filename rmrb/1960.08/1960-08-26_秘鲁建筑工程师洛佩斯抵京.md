Title: Peruvian Architectural Engineer López Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1960-08-26
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, August 25th—Manuel Chambi López, a Peruvian architectural engineer and chairman of the Cusco Film Club, arrived in Beijing by plane today at the invitation of the Chinese-Latin American Friendship Association.

Wang Jiabin, Deputy Secretary-General of the Chinese-Latin American Friendship Association, and others welcomed him at the airport.

### 秘鲁建筑工程师洛佩斯抵京

1960-08-26
第5版()
专栏：

    秘鲁建筑工程师洛佩斯抵京
    新华社25日讯　应中国拉丁美洲友好协会邀请来中国访问的秘鲁建筑工程师、库斯科电影俱乐部主席马努埃尔·昌比·洛佩斯，今日乘飞机到达北京。
    到机场欢迎的有中国拉丁美洲友好协会副总干事王家宾等人。
