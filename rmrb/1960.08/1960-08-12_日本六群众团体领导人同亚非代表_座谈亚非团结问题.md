Title: Japanese Six Mass Organizations Leaders Discuss Asian-African Solidarity with Asian-African Representatives
Author: 
Date: 1960-08-12
Page: 5
Xinhua News Agency, Tokyo, August 11th: The Japan Asia-Africa Solidarity Committee and five other mass organizations jointly held a symposium in Tokyo today to discuss the issue of strengthening solidarity between the people of Asia and Africa with representatives from Asia and Africa attending the Sixth World Conference Against Atomic and Hydrogen Bombs.

Eight representatives from the Soviet Union, Vietnam, India, Indonesia, and Sudan participated in the symposium. On the Japanese side, leaders from various mass organizations such as Akira Kazami, Ikuo Yasui, Hajime Mishima, Natsu Kawasaki, Katsumi Kubushiro, Koshirō Okakura, and Yūichi Horie participated.

The foreign representatives put forward concrete suggestions for strengthening Asian-African solidarity. Sudanese representative Mohammed Khair urged everyone to be wary of actions that would isolate the Soviet Union and China from other Asian-African countries. He emphasized that such attempts should be crushed because the Soviet Union and China are the true friends of the people of Asia and Africa.

Indonesian representative Isa and Indian representative Jilani spoke about the importance of the Bandung spirit.

### 日本六群众团体领导人同亚非代表  座谈亚非团结问题

1960-08-12
第5版()
专栏：

    日本六群众团体领导人同亚非代表
    座谈亚非团结问题
    新华社11日讯　东京消息：日本亚非团结委员会等六个群众团体今天在东京联合举行座谈会，同参加第六届禁止原子弹氢弹世界大会的亚洲和非洲代表座谈加强亚洲和非洲人民之间的团结问题。
    参加座谈会的有苏联、越南、印度、印度尼西亚和苏丹的八名代表。日本方面参加座谈的有风见章、安井郁、三岛一、河崎夏、久布白落实、冈仓古志郎和堀江邑一等各界群众团体的领导人。
    外国代表们提出了加强亚非团结的具体建议。苏丹代表穆罕默德·凯尔要求大家提防把苏联和中国同其他亚非国家隔离开来的行动。他着重指出，应当粉碎这种企图，因为苏联和中国是亚洲和非洲人民的真诚的朋友。
  印度尼西亚代表伊萨和印度代表吉拉尼谈到万隆精神的重要性。
