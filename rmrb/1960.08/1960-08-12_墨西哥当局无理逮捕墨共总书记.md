Title: Mexican Authorities Unreasonably Arrest General Secretary of the Mexican Communist Party
Author:
Date: 1960-08-12
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, 11th. According to a Mexico City dispatch from the Associated Press: Mexican police authorities arrested David Alfaro Siqueiros, General Secretary of the Mexican Communist Party, on the 9th. Siqueiros was elected as the party's General Secretary three months ago.

It is reported that Siqueiros was arrested after a demonstration held in the downtown area of Mexico City on the evening of the 9th.

Eight hundred students from the Mexican National Teachers' College also participated in the demonstration. Prior to this, Siqueiros had spoken to the demonstrators at this college.

### 墨西哥当局无理逮捕墨共总书记

1960-08-12
第5版()
专栏：

    墨西哥当局无理逮捕墨共总书记
    新华社11日讯　据美联社墨西哥城消息：墨西哥警察当局9日逮捕了墨西哥共产党总书记戴维·阿耳法罗·西克罗斯。西克罗斯是三个月前当选为党的总书记的。
    据报道，西克罗斯是在9日晚上墨西哥城闹区举行的示威游行以后被捕的。
    墨西哥全国教师学院的八百名学生也参加了这次示威游行。在这以前，西克罗斯曾在这个学院里向示威游行者讲了话。
