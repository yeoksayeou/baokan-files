Title: Andong Party and Government Delegation Goes to Sinuiju, North Korea, to Participate in the 15th Anniversary Celebration of the Liberation of Korea
Author:
Date: 1960-08-15
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, Andong, 14th. The Andong City Party and Government Delegation, a group of five people led by Li Zhankui, Secretary of the Andong Municipal Party Committee Secretariat, departed by automobile this afternoon for Sinuiju, North Korea, to participate in the 15th anniversary celebration of the Liberation of Korea.

### 参加朝鲜解放十五周年庆祝活动  安东党政代表团去朝鲜新义州

1960-08-15
第6版()
专栏：

    参加朝鲜解放十五周年庆祝活动
    安东党政代表团去朝鲜新义州
    新华社安东14日电　安东市党政代表团一行五人，在团长、中共安东市委书记处书记李占魁率领下，于今天下午乘汽车前往朝鲜新义州参加朝鲜解放十五周年庆祝活动。
