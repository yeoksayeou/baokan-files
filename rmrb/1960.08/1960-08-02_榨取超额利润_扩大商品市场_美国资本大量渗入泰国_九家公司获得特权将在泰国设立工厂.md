Title: Extracting Super Profits, Expanding Commodity Markets: American Capital Infiltrates Thailand; Nine Companies Granted Privileges to Establish Factories in Thailand
Author: 
Date: 1960-08-02
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, Bangkok, August 1st: Nine American companies have reached agreements with the Thai authorities to invest in and establish factories in Thailand. These American companies will obtain various privileges under the regulations of Thailand encouraging foreign investment, enabling them to obtain super profits from extracting cheap Thai labor and paving the way for expanding the market for American goods in Thailand.

The factories invested in and established by American companies include a tire factory, three dairy factories, two battery factories, a pharmaceutical factory, and a paint factory, with a total capital of 210.4 million baht (approximately over 10 million US dollars).

### 榨取超额利润　扩大商品市场  美国资本大量渗入泰国  九家公司获得特权将在泰国设立工厂

1960-08-02
第5版()
专栏：

    榨取超额利润　扩大商品市场
    美国资本大量渗入泰国
    九家公司获得特权将在泰国设立工厂
    据新华社1日讯　曼谷消息：已经有九家美国公司同泰国当局达成了在泰国投资设立工厂的协议。这些美国公司将在泰国鼓励外国投资的条例下取得种种特权，能够从榨取泰国廉价劳动力中得到超额利润，并且为扩大美国商品在泰国的市场开辟道路。
    美国投资设立的工厂包括有一家汽车轮胎厂、三家乳品厂、两家电池厂、一家制药厂和一家油漆厂等，资金共计二亿一千零四十万铢（约合一千万多美元）。
