Title: Sofia University President Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1960-08-20
Page: 6
Xinhua News Agency, 19th. Academician Daki Yordanov Vichev, President of Sofia University in Bulgaria, and Associate Professor Mihail Zolov Michev, Head of the Department of Biology, Geology, and Geography, arrived in Beijing by train this morning for a visit to China according to the 1960 executive plan of the Sino-Bulgarian Cultural Cooperation Agreement.
    Liu Ziyu, Deputy Director of the General Office of the Ministry of Education, Ma Jianmin, Vice President of Beijing Normal University, and Professor Zhang Jingyue, Head of the Department of Biology of Peking University, were among those who greeted them at the station.

### 索非亚大学校长等到京

1960-08-20
第6版()
专栏：

    索非亚大学校长等到京
    新华社19日讯　根据中保文化合作协定1960年执行计划来我国考察的保加利亚索非亚大学校长达基·约尔丹诺夫·维切夫院士和生物、地质、地理系主任米哈依尔·佐洛夫·米切夫副教授，今天上午乘火车到达北京。
    前往车站欢迎的有教育部办公厅副主任刘子余、北京师范大学副校长马健民、北京大学生物系主任张景钺教授等。
