Title: Castro Firmly Declares While Inspecting Militia: Cuban People Fully Armed, Prepared to Crush Aggressors; "Revolución" Exposes U.S. Imperialism's Conspiracy to Use Guatemala to Plot Aggression Against Cuba
Author:
Date: 1960-08-24
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, Havana, August 22nd: Cuban Prime Minister Castro yesterday condemned the U.S. conspiracy to invade Cuba at a militia rally, saying, "Our answer to all their conspiracies and all their aggression plans is: whether or not the Organization of American States exists, (we) will surely achieve victory. ...Our only answer is: a fully armed nation using its own homeland as a trench, ready to defeat its enemies completely."

Castro spoke at the graduation ceremony of the militia in San Julián de Guane, Pinar del Río Province. This group of 1,500 militiamen underwent 45 days of intensive military training, learning how to operate guns and artillery, guerrilla warfare, marching, and other military skills and knowledge. They held a military parade at yesterday's graduation.

Castro said that when imperialism makes threats, when imperialism engages in conspiracies, we will do what we should do, which is to organize ourselves and train ourselves. He warned that the enemy must know that the revolution has the support of the broad masses of the people, and the enemy cannot realize their illusions. "Although the enemy has used conspiracies against the revolution, the revolution is not weakening every day, but becoming stronger."

Castro asked, "Can U.S. soldiers withstand (Cuban) citizens?" "Can the U.S. Marines withstand the peasants in our mountains?" The inspected militia answered loudly and decisively, "Absolutely not!"

Castro said that when the entire island of Cuba is organized, and every farmer knows how to use weapons, every worker, student, and every Cuban man and woman knows how to use weapons, we will see who can destroy our motherland, no matter how strong he is.

Xinhua News Agency, Havana: A news item published in "Revolución" on the 19th stated that Guatemala has become a base for U.S. imperialism to plot aggression against Cuba in recent months.

The news from Guatemala said that the twenty-five airfields of the United Fruit Company have prepared all the conditions so that all types of aircraft can take off and land there. One of these airfields has been handed over to Marcos, the brother of the Cuban traitor and former Air Force Commander Díaz Lanz, and a group of anti-Cuban air force personnel.

The Guatemalan government recently purchased nine B-26 bombers. At the same time, the Petén Lake area of Guatemala has become a guerrilla warfare training center.

The newspaper also pointed out that the troops training in Petén Lake have been ordered to grow beards to imitate the appearance of the Cuban rebel army. This is a Guatemalan conspiracy to cooperate with its earlier false accusations against Cuba in order to present so-called "evidence of Cuban invasion."

The Guatemalan government, which follows the United States, recently submitted a written material to the Peace Committee of the Organization of American States, "accusing" Cuba of "illegally interfering in Guatemala's internal affairs." This "accusation" against Cuba is part of the U.S.-instigated conspiracy to interfere in Cuba. (Attached picture)

### 卡斯特罗在检阅民兵时坚定宣布  古巴人民全面武装准备痛击侵略者  “革命报”揭露美帝国主义利用危地马拉策划侵略古巴的阴谋

1960-08-24
第6版()
专栏：

    卡斯特罗在检阅民兵时坚定宣布
    古巴人民全面武装准备痛击侵略者
    “革命报”揭露美帝国主义利用危地马拉策划侵略古巴的阴谋
    新华社哈瓦那22日电　古巴总理卡斯特罗昨天在一次民兵集会上谴责美国对古巴的侵略阴谋时说，“我们对他们所有的阴谋诡计和所有的侵略计划的回答是：不管有没有美洲国家组织，（我们）必将取得胜利。……我们的回答只有一个：一个全面武装起来的民族以自己的祖国作为战壕，随时准备把自己的敌人打得一败涂地。”
    卡斯特罗是在比那尔德里奥省的圣胡利安德古亚内举行的民兵毕业典礼上讲话的。这批为数一千五百名的民兵经过了四十五天的紧张的军事训练，学习了包括操纵枪炮、游击战、行军以及其他军事技术和知识，在昨天结业时举行了军事检阅。
    卡斯特罗说，当帝国主义进行威胁的时候，当帝国主义搞阴谋的时候，我们就进行我们应该进行的工作，那就是把我们自己组织起来，加以训练。他警告说，敌人必须知道，革命拥有广大人民的支持，敌人不能实现他们的幻想。“虽然敌人施展了阴谋诡计反对革命，但是革命每经过一天不是更弱了，而是更强大了”。
    卡斯特罗问道：“美国兵能抵得过（古巴）公民么？”“美国海军陆战队能抵得过我们山区的庄稼汉么？”受检阅的民兵高声断然回答说，“那个也不行！”
    卡斯特罗说，当整个的古巴岛组织起来，每个农民都会使用武器，每个工人、学生和古巴每个男人和女人都知道怎样使用武器的时候，我们倒要看看谁能摧毁我们的祖国，不管他多强大。
    据新华社哈瓦那电　“革命报”19日登载的一条消息说，最近几个月来危地马拉变成了美帝国主义策划侵略古巴的基地。
    来自危地马拉的这条消息说，美国联合果品公司的二十五个飞机场已经准备好一切条件，以便能够在那里起飞和降落一切类型的飞机，这些飞机场中的一个，已经交给了古巴叛徒前空军司令迪亚斯·兰斯的兄弟马科斯和一批反古巴的空军人员。
    危地马拉政府最近购买了九架B—26型轰炸机。同时，危地马拉的佩腾湖地区已经成了游击战的训练中心。
    报纸还指出，在佩腾湖受训的军队被命令留起胡子模仿古巴起义军的样子，这是危地马拉阴谋配合它早先对古巴的诬告，以便提出所谓“古巴入侵”的证据。
    追随美国的危地马拉政府最近曾向美洲国家组织和平委员会提出一份“指控”古巴的书面材料，诬蔑古巴“对危地马拉进行非法的干涉活动”。这个对古巴的“指控”是美国策动干涉古巴阴谋的一部分。（附图片）
