Title: Premier Zhou and Foreign Minister Chen Send Telegrams to Congratulate the Independence of the Republic of Niger; China Recognizes the Republic of Niger
Author:
Date: 1960-08-03
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, August 2nd -- Premier of the State Council of the People's Republic of China, Zhou Enlai, today sent a telegram to Mr. Hamani Diori, Prime Minister of the Republic of Niger, congratulating the Republic of Niger on its declaration of independence. The telegram reads as follows:

Niger
Niamey
His Excellency Mr. Hamani Diori, Prime Minister of the Republic of Niger:

On the occasion of the declaration of independence of the Republic of Niger I extend, on behalf of the government and people of the People's Republic of China, our sincere congratulations to the government and people of the Republic of Niger. I wish the Niger people further achievements in their struggle to safeguard national independence and oppose imperialism and colonialism. I wish your country prosperity and your people happiness.

Zhou Enlai
Premier of the State Council of the People's Republic of China
August 2, 1960, Beijing

Xinhua News Agency, August 2nd -- Minister of Foreign Affairs of the People's Republic of China, Chen Yi, today sent a telegram to Mr. Hamani Diori, Prime Minister of the Republic of Niger, congratulating the Republic of Niger on its declaration of independence and informing him that the Government of the People's Republic of China has decided to recognize the Republic of Niger. The telegram reads as follows:

Niger
Niamey
His Excellency Mr. Hamani Diori, Prime Minister of the Republic of Niger:

I am delighted to learn of the declaration of independence of the Republic of Niger, and on behalf of the Government of the People's Republic of China, I extend my heartfelt congratulations to the Government of the Republic of Niger. I am honored to inform Your Excellency that the Government of the People's Republic of China has decided to recognize the Republic of Niger. I hope that our government's recognition of your country will lead to the further development of relations between China and Niger.

Chen Yi
Minister of Foreign Affairs of the People's Republic of China
August 2, 1960, Beijing

### 周总理陈外长电贺尼日尔共和国独立  我国承认尼日尔共和国

1960-08-03
第1版()
专栏：

    周总理陈外长电贺尼日尔共和国独立
    我国承认尼日尔共和国
    新华社2日讯　中华人民共和国国务院总理周恩来今天打电报给尼日尔共和国总理阿马尼·迪奥里，祝贺尼日尔共和国宣告独立。电文如下：尼日尔　尼亚美尼日尔共和国总理阿马尼·迪奥里先生阁下：
    值此尼日尔共和国宣告独立之际，我谨代表中华人民共和国政府和人民，向尼日尔共和国政府和人民致以诚挚的祝贺。祝尼日尔人民在维护民族独立、反对帝国主义和殖民主义的斗争中取得进一步的成就。祝贵国国家繁荣，人民幸福。
            中华人民共和国国务院总理　周恩来
                        1960年8月2日于北京
    新华社2日讯　中华人民共和国外交部长陈毅今天打电报给尼日尔共和国总理阿马尼·迪奥里，祝贺尼日尔共和国宣告独立，并通知他，中华人民共和国政府已经决定承认尼日尔共和国。电文如下：尼日尔　尼亚美尼日尔共和国总理阿马尼·迪奥里先生阁下：
    欣闻尼日尔共和国宣告独立，我谨代表中华人民共和国政府向尼日尔共和国政府致以衷心的祝贺。我荣幸地通知阁下，中华人民共和国政府已经决定承认尼日尔共和国。我希望，我国政府对贵国的承认将导致中、尼两国关系的进一步发展。
              中华人民共和国外交部长　陈　毅
                          1960年8月2日于北京
