Title: Burmese Ambassador to China Holds Reception for Visit of Burmese Cultural Friendship Art Troupe to China
Author:
Date: 1960-08-15
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, 14th August. The Burmese Ambassador to China, U Hla Maw, held a reception this afternoon for the Burmese Cultural Friendship Art Troupe, led by U Ba Swe, on their visit to China.

Invited to the reception were Shen Yanbing, Minister of Culture; Chu Tunan, Deputy Director of the Commission for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries; Li Yimang, our Ambassador to Burma; General Gan Siqi, Deputy Director of the General Political Department of the Chinese People's Liberation Army; Jian Bozan, Vice President of the Sino-Burmese Friendship Association; and figures from the capital's cultural and artistic circles, including Ouyang Yuqian, Lin Lin, Tian Hua, Liu Tieshan, Jia Zuoguang, and others.

Diplomatic envoys from various countries to China also attended the reception.

At the reception, the hosts and guests engaged in cordial conversations, and Burmese artists and Chinese literary and art workers also exchanged experiences.

### 为缅甸文化友好艺术团访华  缅甸驻华大使举行招待会

1960-08-15
第5版()
专栏：

    为缅甸文化友好艺术团访华
    缅甸驻华大使举行招待会
    新华社14日讯　缅甸驻中国大使叫温今天下午为以吴巴盛团长为首的缅甸文化友好艺术团访问中国举行招待会。
    应邀出席招待会的，有文化部部长沈雁冰；对外文化联络委员会副主任楚图南；我国驻缅甸大使李一氓；中国人民解放军总政治部副主任甘泗淇上将；中缅友好协会副会长翦伯赞；以及首都文化艺术界人士欧阳予倩、林林、田华、刘铁山、贾作光等。
    各国驻中国外交官员也出席了招待会。
    会上，宾主亲切地进行了交谈，缅甸艺术家和中国文艺工作者还互相交流了经验。
