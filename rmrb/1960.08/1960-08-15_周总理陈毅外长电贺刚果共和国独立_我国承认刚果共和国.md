Title: Premier Zhou Enlai and Foreign Minister Chen Yi Send Congratulatory Messages on the Independence of the Republic of the Congo; China Recognizes the Republic of the Congo
Author:
Date: 1960-08-15
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, August 14th. Premier Zhou Enlai of the People's Republic of China today sent a telegram to President Fulbert Youlou of the Republic of the Congo, congratulating the Republic of the Congo on its declaration of independence. The telegram reads as follows:

To His Excellency Fulbert Youlou, President of the Republic of the Congo, Brazzaville, Congo:

On the occasion of the declaration of independence of the Republic of the Congo, I, on behalf of the Government and people of the People's Republic of China, extend warm congratulations to the Government and people of the Republic of the Congo. I wish the people of the Republic of the Congo further achievements in their struggle to uphold national independence and development, and oppose imperialism and colonialism. I wish your country prosperity and your people happiness.

\t\t\tZhou Enlai
\t\t\tPremier of the State Council of the People's Republic of China
\t\t\tBeijing, August 14, 1960

Xinhua News Agency, August 14th. Chen Yi, Minister of Foreign Affairs of the People's Republic of China, today sent a telegram to President Fulbert Youlou of the Republic of the Congo, congratulating the Republic of the Congo on its declaration of independence, and informing him of the Chinese government's decision to recognize the Republic of the Congo. The telegram reads as follows:

To His Excellency Fulbert Youlou, President of the Republic of the Congo, Brazzaville, Congo:

I am pleased to learn of the declaration of independence of the Republic of the Congo. I, on behalf of the Government of the People's Republic of China, extend sincere congratulations to the Government of the Republic of the Congo. It is my honor to inform Your Excellency that the Government of the People's Republic of China have decided to recognize the Republic of the Congo. I hope that our government's recognition of your country will lead to the further development of relations between our two countries.

\t\t\tChen Yi
\t\t\tMinister of Foreign Affairs of the People's Republic of China
\t\t\tBeijing, August 14, 1960

### 周总理陈毅外长电贺刚果共和国独立  我国承认刚果共和国

1960-08-15
第1版()
专栏：

    周总理陈毅外长电贺刚果共和国独立
    我国承认刚果共和国
    新华社14日讯　中华人民共和国总理周恩来今天打电报给刚果共和国总统富耳贝·尤卢，祝贺刚果共和国宣告独立。电文如下：刚果　布拉柴维尔刚果共和国总统富耳贝·尤卢先生阁下：
  在此刚果共和国宣告独立的时候，我谨代表中华人民共和国政府和人民向刚果共和国政府和人民致以热烈的祝贺。祝刚果共和国人民在坚持民族独立发展的道路、反对帝国主义和殖民主义的斗争中取得进一步的成就。祝贵国国家繁荣，人民幸福。
          中华人民共和国国务院总理　周恩来
                      1960年8月14日于北京
    新华社14日讯　中华人民共和国外交部长陈毅今天打电报给刚果共和国总统富耳贝·尤卢，祝贺刚果共和国宣告独立，并通知他中国政府决定承认刚果共和国。电文如下：刚果　布拉柴维尔刚果共和国总统富耳贝·尤卢先生阁下：
    欣悉刚果共和国宣告独立，我谨代表中华人民共和国政府向刚果共和国政府致以诚挚的祝贺。我荣幸地通知阁下，中华人民共和国政府已经决定承认刚果共和国。我希望，我国政府对贵国的承认将导致我们两国关系的进一步发展。
            中华人民共和国外交部长　陈毅
                        1960年8月14日于北京
