Title: "Miracle Water"
Author: Huang Huixin, Yang Peirong
Date: 1960-08-29
Page: 3

    "Miracle Water"
    Recently, eleven communes in our county have universally manufactured and used humic acid. This is a chemical fertilizer that can promote crop growth. In early August, we conducted an experiment on one mu of land in the Youth Production Team of the Jia Hui Brigade in Shizui Commune. We applied one jin of 10% pro-humic ammonium fertilizer mixed with 250 jin of phosphate rock powder. The seedlings grew very quickly, far surpassing the crops fertilized with ammonium sulfate and ordinary farm manure; the tillering was also particularly good. The commune members all call it "miracle water."
    The raw materials for this chemical fertilizer are peat, wild fiber, caustic soda, or wood ash water. The method of preparation is also very simple, requiring only ordinary iron pots and water tanks. This fertilizer was first trial-produced by the county chemical plant. This is a new achievement of our county's industry supporting agriculture.
        Guangxi Guiping County Huang Huixin, Yang Peirong

### “神仙水”
黄惠新  杨培荣
1960-08-29
第3版()
专栏：

    “神仙水”
    最近，我县的十一个公社普遍制造和使用了胡敏酸。这是一种能促进农作物生长的化学肥料。8月初，我们在石咀公社加惠大队青年生产队的一亩田里作试验，用一斤含量10％的丙胡敏酸铵搭配上二百五十斤磷矿粉施肥，禾苗长得很快，远远胜过了施硫酸铵、普通农家肥的庄稼；分孽情况也特别好。社员们都管它叫“神仙水”。
    这种化学肥料的原料是坭炭、野生纤维、烧碱或草木灰水。制法也很简单，有普通的铁锅、水缸就行了。这种肥料是县化工厂首先试制的。这是我们县工业支援农业的一项新成就。
        广西桂平县　黄惠新、杨培荣
