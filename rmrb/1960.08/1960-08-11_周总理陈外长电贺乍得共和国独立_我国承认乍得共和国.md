Title: Premier Zhou and Foreign Minister Chen Send Congratulations on the Independence of the Republic of Chad; China Recognizes the Republic of Chad
Author:
Date: 1960-08-11
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, 10th August. Premier of the State Council of the People's Republic of China, Zhou Enlai, today sent a telegram to the Prime Minister of the Republic of Chad, François Tombalbaye, congratulating the Republic of Chad on its declaration of independence. The telegram reads as follows:

To His Excellency François Tombalbaye, Prime Minister of the Republic of Chad, Fort-Lamy, Chad:

On the occasion of the declaration of independence of the Republic of Chad, I extend warm congratulations to the Government and people of the Republic of Chad on behalf of the Government and people of the People's Republic of China. I wish the people of the Republic of Chad further achievements in their struggle to uphold national independence and development, and to oppose imperialism and colonialism. I wish your country prosperity and your people happiness.

Zhou Enlai, Premier of the State Council of the People's Republic of China
August 10, 1960

Xinhua News Agency, 10th August. Chen Yi, Minister of Foreign Affairs of the People's Republic of China, today sent a telegram to the Prime Minister of the Republic of Chad, François Tombalbaye, congratulating the Republic of Chad on its declaration of independence, and informing him of the Chinese government's decision to recognize the Republic of Chad. The telegram reads as follows:

To His Excellency François Tombalbaye, Prime Minister of the Republic of Chad, Fort-Lamy, Chad:

I am pleased to learn of the declaration of independence of the Republic of Chad, and I extend sincere congratulations to the Government of the Republic of Chad on behalf of the Government of the People's Republic of China. I have the honor to inform Your Excellency that the Government of the People's Republic of China have decided to recognize the Republic of Chad. I hope that our government's recognition of your country will lead to the further development of relations between our two countries.

Chen Yi, Minister of Foreign Affairs of the People's Republic of China
August 10, 1960

### 周总理陈外长电贺乍得共和国独立  我国承认乍得共和国

1960-08-11
第1版()
专栏：

    周总理陈外长电贺乍得共和国独立
    我国承认乍得共和国
    新华社10日讯　中华人民共和国国务院总理周恩来今天打电报给乍得共和国总理弗朗索瓦·托姆巴巴耶，祝贺乍得共和国宣告独立。电文如下：乍得　拉密堡乍得共和国总理弗朗索瓦·托姆巴巴耶先生阁下：
    值此乍得共和国宣告独立之际，我谨代表中华人民共和国政府和人民，向乍得共和国政府和人民致以热烈的祝贺。祝乍得共和国人民在坚持民族独立发展的道路、反对帝国主义和殖民主义的斗争中取得进一步的成就。祝贵国国家繁荣，人民幸福。
            中华人民共和国国务院总理　周恩来
                                1960年8月10日
    新华社10日讯　中华人民共和国外交部长陈毅今天打电报给乍得共和国总理弗朗索瓦·托姆巴巴耶，祝贺乍得共和国宣告独立，并通知他中国政府决定承认乍得共和国。电文如下：乍得　拉密堡乍得共和国总理弗朗索瓦·托姆巴巴耶先生阁下：
    欣闻乍得共和国宣告独立，我谨代表中华人民共和国政府向乍得共和国政府致以诚挚的祝贺。我荣幸地通知阁下，中华人民共和国政府已经决定承认乍得共和国。我希望，我国政府对贵国的承认将导致我们两国关系的进一步发展。
              中华人民共和国外交部长　陈毅
                                1960年8月10日
