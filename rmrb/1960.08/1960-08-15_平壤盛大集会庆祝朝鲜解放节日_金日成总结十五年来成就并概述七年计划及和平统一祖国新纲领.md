Title: Grand Rally in Pyongyang Celebrates Korean Liberation Day; Kim Il-sung Summarizes Fifteen Years of Achievements and Outlines Seven-Year Plan and New Program for Peaceful Reunification of the Fatherland
Author:
Date: 1960-08-15
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, Pyongyang, August 14th: According to the Korean Central News Agency, all the Korean people are jubilantly celebrating the fifteenth anniversary of their liberation from the yoke of Japanese imperialist colonial rule by the great Soviet army.

A grand celebration rally was held this evening at the Grand Theater in Pyongyang to celebrate the festival of all the Korean people - the fifteenth anniversary of the "August 15th" Liberation.

As the festival approached, the Grand Theater, which opened yesterday, was decorated in festive attire.

Attending the rally were figures from Korean party and government organs, social organizations, figures from the scientific, cultural, artistic, and journalistic circles, cadres of the Korean People's Army, and people from all parts of Korea.

Also attending the rally were personnel from various countries' embassies in Pyongyang, delegations from the Soviet-Korean Friendship Association, the Chinese-Korean Friendship Association, the Japan-Korea Association, the Policy Deliberation Council of the Japan Socialist Party, and foreign guests, as well as foreigners residing in Pyongyang.

At 6:00 PM, Premier Kim Il-sung and Chairman of the Presidium of the Supreme People's Assembly, Choe Yong-gon, ascended the rostrum amidst warm applause.

Attending the celebration ceremony were party and government leaders such as Kim Il, Hong Myung-hui, Park Jung-ae, Park Kum-chul, Kim Chang-man, Jung Il-ryong, and Nam Il.

Also on the rostrum were diplomatic representatives of various countries in Korea, the head of the Soviet-Korean Friendship Association delegation, A.I. Andosienko, and the head of the Chinese-Korean Friendship Association delegation, Li Dequan.

Young Pioneers, dressed in their finest festive attire, stepped onto the rostrum and presented flowers to Premier Kim Il-sung, other party and government leaders, and Soviet Ambassador to Korea, Puzanov.

Comrade Choe Yong-gon announced the commencement of the commemoration rally.

The national anthems of Korea and the Soviet Union were solemnly played.
Comrade Kim Il-sung, Chairman of the Central Committee of the Workers' Party of Korea and Prime Minister of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea, delivered a report at the meeting.

In his report, he summarized the great achievements made by the Korean people in the fifteen years since liberation, and outlined a grand seven-year plan for developing the national economy and a new program for promoting the peaceful reunification of the fatherland. His report was repeatedly interrupted by stormy applause from the participants.

Next, Soviet Ambassador to Korea, Puzanov, delivered a congratulatory speech amidst warm applause. The rally then featured music and dance performances.

### 平壤盛大集会庆祝朝鲜解放节日  金日成总结十五年来成就并概述七年计划及和平统一祖国新纲领

1960-08-15
第6版()
专栏：

    平壤盛大集会庆祝朝鲜解放节日
    金日成总结十五年来成就并概述七年计划及和平统一祖国新纲领
    新华社平壤14日电　据朝鲜中央通讯社消息：全体朝鲜人民欢欣鼓舞地庆祝伟大的苏联军队把他们从日本帝国主义殖民统治的枷锁下解放出来的第十五周年。
    今晚在平壤的大剧院举行了盛大的庆祝大会，庆祝朝鲜全体人民的节日——“八·一五”解放十五周年。
    随着节日将临，昨天开幕的大剧院披上了节日的盛装。
    参加大会的有朝鲜党政机关、社会团体的人士，科学、文化、艺术和新闻界人士，朝鲜人民军的干部和朝鲜各地人士。
  参加大会的还有各国驻平壤大使馆的人员、正在访问的苏朝友好协会、中朝友好协会、日朝协会、日本社会党政策审议会的代表团和外国来宾以及住在平壤的外国人士。
    下午六时，金日成首相和最高人民会议常任委员会委员长崔庸健以及苏联驻朝鲜大使普扎诺夫在热烈的掌声中登上主席台。
    参加庆祝仪式的有：金一、洪命熹、朴正爱、朴金喆、金昌满、郑一龙、南日等党和政府的领导人。
    在主席台上的还有：各国驻朝鲜的外交代表，苏朝友好协会代表团团长安道辛，中朝友好协会代表团团长李德全。
    少先队员穿着最漂亮的节日服装走上主席台，向金日成首相，其他党政领导人和苏联驻朝鲜大使普扎诺夫献花。
    崔庸健同志宣布纪念大会开始。
    庄严地演奏了朝鲜和苏联的国歌。
    朝鲜劳动党中央委员会委员长、朝鲜民主主义人民共和国内阁首相金日成同志在会上作了报告。
    他在报告中总结了朝鲜人民解放以后十五年来所取得的巨大成就，并且概述了发展国民经济的宏伟的七年计划和一项促进祖国的和平统一的新纲领。他的报告不断地为与会者的暴风雨般的掌声所打断。
    接着，苏联驻朝鲜大使普扎诺夫在热烈的掌声中致祝词。大会然后演出了音乐和舞蹈。
