Title: Vice Premier Chen Yi Receives Kenzo Nakajima
Author:
Date: 1960-08-13
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, August 12th: Vice Premier Chen Yi received Kenzo Nakajima, Director-General of the Japan-China Cultural Exchange Association, his wife, and his accompanying staff member Goofu Shirato this afternoon. The hosts and guests held cordial talks.

Present at the meeting were Liao Chengzhi, Zhou Erfu, Lin Lin, and others. Japanese peace advocate Kinkazu Saionji, who is in Beijing, was also present.

### 陈毅副总理接见中岛健藏

1960-08-13
第6版()
专栏：

    陈毅副总理接见中岛健藏
    新华社12日讯　陈毅副总理在今天下午接见了日本日中文化交流协会理事长中岛健藏和夫人以及他的随行人员白土吾夫。宾主进行了亲切的交谈。
    接见时在座的，有廖承志、周而复、林林等人。在京的日本和平人士西园寺公一也在座。
