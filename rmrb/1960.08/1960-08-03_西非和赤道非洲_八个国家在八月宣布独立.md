Title: Eight Countries in West and Equatorial Africa Declare Independence in August
Author:
Date: 1960-08-03
Page: 6
Xinhua News Agency, Paris, August 1st: According to Paris newspapers, Dahomey, Niger, Upper Volta, and Ivory Coast in French West Africa will all declare independence within the first week of August. Chad, the Central African Republic (Ubangi-Shari), (Central) Congo, and Gabon in French Equatorial Africa will also become independent in August.

    Apart from the Republic of Dahomey, which declared independence on August 1st, the dates for the other countries to declare independence are: Niger – August 3rd; Upper Volta – August 5th; Ivory Coast – August 7th; Chad – August 11th; Central African Republic (Ubangi-Shari) – August 13th; (Central) Congo – August 15th; Gabon – August 17th.

    These countries are all member states of the "French Community," with a total area of 4.54 million square kilometers and a population of approximately 15.6 million. France has been forced to agree to their independence, but it is still attempting to maintain its interests there by signing "cooperation agreements" with them.

### 西非和赤道非洲  八个国家在八月宣布独立

1960-08-03
第6版()
专栏：

    西非和赤道非洲
    八个国家在八月宣布独立
    据新华社巴黎1日电　据巴黎报纸报道，法属西非的达荷美、尼日尔、上沃尔特和象牙海岸都要在8月第一周内宣告独立。法属赤道非洲的乍得、中非共和国（乌班吉沙立）、（中央）刚果和加蓬也将在8月份内独立。
    除了达荷美共和国已在8月1日宣告独立之外，其他国家宣布独立的日期是：尼日尔——8月3日；上沃尔特——8月5日；象牙海岸——8月7日；乍得——8月11日；中非共和国（乌班吉沙立）——8月13日；（中央）刚果——8月15日；加蓬——8月17日。
    这些国家都是“法兰西共同体”成员国，其面积总和为四百五十四万平方公里，人口约一千五百六十余万。法国被迫同意让它们独立，但仍企图通过同签订“合作协定”来继续维持它在那里的利益。
