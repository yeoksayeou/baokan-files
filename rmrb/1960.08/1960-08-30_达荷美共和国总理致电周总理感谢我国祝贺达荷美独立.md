Title: Prime Minister of the Republic of Dahomey Cables Premier Zhou to Thank China for Congratulating Dahomey on its Independence
Author:
Date: 1960-08-30
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, August 29th: Hubert Maga, Prime Minister of the Republic of Dahomey in West Africa, wrote to Zhou Enlai, Premier of the State Council of the People's Republic of China, on August 18th, thanking Premier Zhou for congratulating the Republic of Dahomey on its declaration of independence. The full text of the letter is as follows:

To His Excellency Zhou Enlai, Premier of the State Council of the People's Republic of China, Beijing

Your Excellency:

I have the honor to receive your telegram of July 31, 1960.

On behalf of the people and government of Dahomey, I convey to you that we are deeply moved by the sentiments you expressed to us at the birth of our young republic.

Please accept, Your Excellency, my highest respects.

Hubert Maga
Prime Minister of the Republic of Dahomey
Porto-Novo, August 18, 1960

### 达荷美共和国总理致电周总理感谢我国祝贺达荷美独立

1960-08-30
第1版()
专栏：

    达荷美共和国总理致电周总理感谢我国祝贺达荷美独立
    新华社29日讯　西非达荷美共和国总理余贝尔·马加在8月18日写信给中华人民共和国国务院总理周恩来，感谢周总理对达荷美共和国宣告独立的祝贺。信的全文如下：北京中华人民共和国国务院总理周恩来阁下阁下：
    我荣幸地收到了你1960年7月31日的来电。
    我代表达荷美人民和达荷美政府向你转达：在我们年轻的共和国诞生的时候，你对我们所表达的感情使我们十分感动。
    阁下，请接受我最崇高的敬意。
            达荷美共和国总理　余贝尔·马加
                      1960年8月18日于波多诺伏
