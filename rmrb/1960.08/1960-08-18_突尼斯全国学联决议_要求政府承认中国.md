Title: Tunisian National Student Union Resolution Demands Government Recognition of China
Author:
Date: 1960-08-18
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, Rabat, August 15th: According to reports from Tunis, Tunisian students are demanding that the government recognize the People's Republic of China. This demand was put forward in a resolution passed by the Tunisian National Student Union Congress. The Congress opened in Tunis on August 8th and closed on the 13th.

The resolution passed by the Congress expressed support for the Algerian people's struggle for independence and endorsed the Tunisian government's policy of non-alignment. The Congress stated that it would strive to achieve the unity of the Arab Maghreb.

### 突尼斯全国学联决议  要求政府承认中国

1960-08-18
第6版()
专栏：

    突尼斯全国学联决议
    要求政府承认中国
    新华社拉巴特15日电　据来自突尼斯的消息，突尼斯学生要求政府承认中华人民共和国。这个要求是突尼斯全国学生联合会代表大会通过的决议中提出的。代表大会于8月8日在突尼斯开幕，于13日闭幕。
    代表大会通过的决议表示支持为争取独立而斗争的阿尔及利亚人民，并拥护突尼斯政府的不参加集团的政策。代表大会表示将争取实现阿拉伯马格里布的统一。
