Title: At the Invitation of Deputy Prime Minister Naim, Vice Premier Chen Yi Will Visit Afghanistan
Author:
Date: 1960-08-15
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, August 14th: At the invitation of His Royal Highness Sardar Mohammad Naim, Deputy Prime Minister and Minister of Foreign Affairs of the Kingdom of Afghanistan, Chen Yi, Vice-Premier of State Council and Minister of Foreign Affairs of the People's Republic of China, will pay an amicable visit to the Kingdom of Afghanistan in late August and attend the Afghan Independence Day celebrations on August 23.

### 应纳伊姆副首相的邀请  陈毅副总理将访问阿富汗

1960-08-15
第1版()
专栏：

    应纳伊姆副首相的邀请
    陈毅副总理将访问阿富汗
    新华社14日讯　中华人民共和国国务院副总理兼外交部长陈毅元帅应阿富汗王国副首相兼外交大臣萨达尔·穆罕默德·纳伊姆殿下的邀请，将于8月下旬前往阿富汗王国进行友好访问，并参加8月23日阿富汗独立节庆典。
