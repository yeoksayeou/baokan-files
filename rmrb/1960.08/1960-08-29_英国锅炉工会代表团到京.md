Title: British Boilermakers' Union Delegation Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1960-08-29
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, August 28th. At the invitation of the National Committee of the Chinese Mechanical Workers' Union, the British Boilermakers' Union delegation of four, led by its chairman John Heppellwhite, arrived in Beijing by plane this morning. Greeting them at the airport was He Ruilin, Vice-Chairman of the National Committee of the Chinese Mechanical Workers' Union, and others.

### 英国锅炉工会代表团到京

1960-08-29
第5版()
专栏：

    英国锅炉工会代表团到京
    新华社28日讯　应中国机械工会全国委员会邀请来我国访问的英国锅炉工会代表团一行四人，在团长约翰·赫波怀德率领下，于今日上午乘机到达北京。前往欢迎的有中国机械工会全国委员会副主席贺瑞林等。
