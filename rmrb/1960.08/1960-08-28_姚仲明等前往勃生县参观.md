Title: Yao Zhongming and Others Visit Bassein

Author: 
Date: 1960-08-28
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, Rangoon, August 27th: Yao Zhongming, the chief representative of the Chinese side of the Sino-Burmese Boundary Joint Committee, along with other Chinese representatives, advisors, and staff, left here this morning aboard the Burmese President's special yacht to visit Bassein County, Burma's second largest port, famous for its rice production.

Accompanying them were Brigadier General Aung Gyi, the chief representative of the Burmese side of the Sino-Burmese Boundary Joint Committee and Deputy Chief of Staff of the Burmese Defense Forces, as well as other Burmese representatives and advisors.

Also accompanying them were Sun Shengwei, Counselor of the Chinese Embassy in Burma, and Colonel Jiang Keding, Military Attaché.

Lei Yang, Chargé d'Affaires ad interim of the Chinese Embassy in Burma, and other members of the embassy saw them off at the Rangoon wharf.

### 姚仲明等前往勃生县参观

1960-08-28
第6版()
专栏：

    姚仲明等前往勃生县参观
    新华社仰光27日电　中缅边界联合委员会中国首席代表姚仲明以及其他中国代表、顾问和工作人员，今天上午乘缅甸总统专用汽艇离开这里前往缅甸的第二大港口、盛产大米的勃生县参观。
    陪同他们前往的有中缅边界联合委员会的缅甸首席代表、缅甸国防军副总参谋长昂季准将，以及其他缅甸代表和顾问等。
    陪同前往的还有中国驻缅甸大使馆参赞孙盛渭和武官蒋克定上校。
    在仰光码头为他们送行的有中国驻缅甸大使馆临时代办雷扬和大使馆的其他人员。
