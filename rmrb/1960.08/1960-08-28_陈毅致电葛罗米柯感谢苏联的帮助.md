Title: Chen Yi Sends Telegram to Gromyko Thanking the Soviet Union for its Assistance
Author: Chen Yi
Date: 1960-08-28
Page: 2

Xinhua News Agency, August 27th: Chen Yi, Vice Premier and Minister of Foreign Affairs of the People's Republic of China, sent a telegram to Soviet Foreign Minister Gromyko today from the Soviet Union on his way back home after visiting Afghanistan, expressing his gratitude. The text of the telegram is as follows:

    To Comrade A.A. Gromyko, Minister of Foreign Affairs of the Union of Soviet Socialist Republics:
    Dear Comrade Gromyko: As I conclude my friendly visit to the Kingdom of Afghanistan and pass through the territory of our great ally, the Soviet Union, once again on my way home, please allow me to express once again to you, and through you to the Soviet Government and the Soviet people, my sincere respect and gratitude for all the assistance and warm reception you have given us. May the unbreakable brotherly friendship between the Chinese and Soviet peoples continue to be consolidated and developed!
            Chen Yi, Minister of Foreign Affairs of the People's Republic of China
          August 27, 1960, en route from Alma-Ata to China

### 陈毅致电葛罗米柯感谢苏联的帮助
陈毅
1960-08-28
第2版()
专栏：

    陈毅致电葛罗米柯感谢苏联的帮助
    新华社27日讯　中华人民共和国国务院副总理兼外交部长陈毅今天在访问阿富汗以后的返国途中经苏联时给苏联外交部长葛罗米柯拍发了感谢电，电文如下：
    苏维埃社会主义共和国联盟外交部长安·安·葛罗米柯同志
    敬爱的葛罗米柯同志：当我结束了在阿富汗王国的友好访问，在返国途中，再度经过我们伟大的盟邦——苏联的国土的时候，请允许我再一次向您并且通过您向苏联政府和苏联人民表示诚挚的敬意，感谢你们给予我们的各种帮助和热情的接待。祝中苏两国人民牢不可破的兄弟友谊不断巩固和发展！
            中华人民共和国外交部长　陈毅
          1960年8月27日在从阿拉木图返国途中
