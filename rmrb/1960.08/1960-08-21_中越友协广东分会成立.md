Title: China-Vietnam Friendship Association Guangdong Branch Established
Author:
Date: 1960-08-21
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, Guangzhou, August 20th. The Guangdong branch of the China-Vietnam Friendship Association was officially established today. This branch was jointly initiated and organized by ten organizations, including the provincial and municipal trade unions, women's federations, and youth federations. Tonight, the Guangdong branch of the China-Vietnam Friendship Association held an establishment ceremony at the International Club and approved the list of directors. Lin Qiangyun was appointed as the president of the Guangdong branch of the China-Vietnam Friendship Association, and Zeng Zhi and Chen Nengxing were appointed as vice presidents. More than 140 people, including the heads and representatives of the ten initiating units, attended the ceremony.

### 中越友协广东分会成立

1960-08-21
第6版()
专栏：

    中越友协广东分会成立
    新华社广州20日电　中越友好协会广东分会在今天宣告成立。这个分会是由省、市工会、妇联、青联等十个团体联合发起组织的。今晚，中越友好协会广东分会，在国际俱乐部举行了成立仪式。并通过了理事名单。中越友好协会广东分会由林锵云任会长，曾志、陈能兴任副会长。参加仪式的，有十个发起单位的负责人和代表共一百四十多人。
