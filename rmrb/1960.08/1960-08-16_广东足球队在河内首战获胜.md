Title: Guangdong Football Team Wins First Match in Hanoi
Author:
Date: 1960-08-16
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, Hanoi, August 14th. The Guangdong football team from China played its first match in Vietnam against the Vietnamese People's Army "Sports Team" at the Hang Đẫy Stadium in Hanoi on the evening of August 14th. The Guangdong team won with a score of four to one.

### 广东足球队在河内首战获胜

1960-08-16
第5版()
专栏：

    广东足球队在河内首战获胜
    据新华社河内14日电　中国广东足球队14日晚在河内行底运动场同越南人民军“体工队”进行了访越首次比赛。广东队以四比一获胜。
