Title: Exhibition on Achievements Under the Leadership of the Labor Party to Celebrate the 15th Anniversary of the Founding of Vietnam Opens
Author:
Date: 1960-08-30
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, Hanoi, August 29th. The exhibition on achievements in celebration of the 15th anniversary of the founding of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam solemnly opened this afternoon.

	Prime Minister Pham Van Dong of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam, Chairman Truong Chinh of the Standing Committee of the National Assembly, Deputy Prime Minister Vo Nguyen Giap, Vice Chairman Nguyen Xien of the Standing Committee of the National Assembly, and diplomatic officials from various countries stationed in Vietnam attended the opening ceremony.

	Prime Minister Pham Van Dong cut the ribbon for the opening of the exhibition hall. The large-scale exhibition hall, located at Bay Mau Lake in Hanoi, the capital of Vietnam, has forty-eight exhibition rooms, comprehensively introducing the brilliant achievements made by the Vietnamese people in the past fifteen years under the leadership of the Vietnamese Labor Party headed by President Ho Chi Minh.

	The exhibition hall has four parts. The first part introduces Vietnam's rich natural resources and the heroic revolutionary struggles of the Vietnamese people before the August Revolution; the second part introduces the glorious victories of the Vietnamese army and people in resisting French and Japanese aggressors; the third part introduces the achievements of socialist transformation and socialist construction in North Vietnam after the restoration of peace; and the fourth part introduces the prospects of Vietnam's first five-year plan.

### 介绍劳动党领导下取得的辉煌成就  越建国十五周年成绩展览馆开幕

1960-08-30
第5版()
专栏：

    介绍劳动党领导下取得的辉煌成就
    越建国十五周年成绩展览馆开幕
    新华社河内29日电　越南民主共和国成立十五周年成绩展览馆，今天下午隆重开幕。
    越南民主共和国政府总理范文同，国会常务委员会主席长征，副总理武元甲，国会常务委员会副主席阮阐以及各国驻越南的外交官员参加了开幕仪式。
    范文同总理为展览馆开幕剪彩。位于越南首都河内七亩湖的这所规模巨大的展览馆设有四十八个展览室，全面地介绍了越南人民十五年来在以胡志明主席为首的越南劳动党领导下所取得的辉煌成就。
    展览馆共四个部分。第一部分介绍越南富饶的自然资源和八月革命以前越南人民英勇的革命斗争；第二部分介绍越南军民抗击法、日侵略者的光辉胜利；第三部分介绍越南北方在和平恢复后进行社会主义改造和社会主义建设的成就；第四部分介绍越南第一个五年计划的远景。
