Title: Chinese Officials Arrive in Delhi to Attend Sino-Indian Officials' Meeting
Author:
Date: 1960-08-20
Page: 6

    Attending the Sino-Indian Officials' Meeting
    Chinese Officials Arrive in Delhi
    Xinhua News Agency, New Delhi, August 19th: Chinese officials participating in the Sino-Indian officials' meeting, namely, Zhang Wenjin, Director of the First Asian Department of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs, Yang Gongsu, Director of the Foreign Affairs Office of Tibet, and advisors, arrived in Delhi by plane on the evening of the 17th.
    They were greeted at the airport by Meda, Director of the Northern Division of the Indian Ministry of External Affairs, Gopal, Director of the Historical Division, and De Jya, Deputy Director of the Protocol Division of the Indian Ministry of External Affairs, and others. Pan Zili, Chinese Ambassador to India, also welcomed them at the airport.

### 参加中印官员会晤  我方官员到达德里

1960-08-20
第6版()
专栏：

    参加中印官员会晤
    我方官员到达德里
    新华社19日讯　新德里消息：参加中印官员会晤的我方官员：外交部第一亚洲司司长章文晋、西藏外事处处长杨公素和顾问等，已于17日晚乘飞机到达德里。
    前往机场迎接的，有印度外交部北方司司长梅达、历史司司长戈帕尔和印度外交部礼宾司副司长德贾等。我驻印度大使潘自力也到机场迎接。
