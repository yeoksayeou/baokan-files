Title: Delegations from Portuguese Angola and Guinea Arrive in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1960-08-06
Page: 6

    Xinhua News Agency, 5th - Delegations from the People's Movement for the Liberation of Angola and the African Independence Party of Portuguese Guinea arrived in Beijing by plane this morning. They are visiting China at the invitation of the Chinese People's Institute of Foreign Affairs.
    Those who welcomed them at the airport included Wang Yinpu, Deputy Secretary-General of the Chinese People's Institute of Foreign Affairs, He Gongkai, Director, and responsible persons from the Chinese Committee for Afro-Asian Solidarity and the Chinese-African People's Friendship Association.

### 葡属安哥拉和几内亚两代表团到京

1960-08-06
第6版()
专栏：

    葡属安哥拉和几内亚两代表团到京
    新华社5日讯　葡属安哥拉人民解放运动访华代表团和葡属几内亚非洲独立党访华代表团，今天上午乘飞机到达北京。他们是应中国人民外交学会邀请来我国访问的。
    到机场欢迎的，有中国人民外交学会副秘书长王荫圃、理事何功楷，中国亚非团结委员会及中国非洲人民友好协会负责人等。
