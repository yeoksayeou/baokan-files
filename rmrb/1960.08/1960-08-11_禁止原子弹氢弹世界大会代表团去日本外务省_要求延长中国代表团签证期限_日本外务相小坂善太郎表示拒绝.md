Title: World Conference Against Atomic and Hydrogen Bombs Delegation Barred from Visiting Japanese Foreign Ministry; Request to Extend Visas for Chinese Delegation Refused by Japanese Foreign Minister Zentaro Kosaka
Author:
Date: 1960-08-11
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, Tokyo, 10th: The Sixth World Conference Against Atomic and Hydrogen Bombs dispatched a delegation of fifty people to the Japanese Foreign Ministry on the afternoon of August 9th to request the Japanese government to extend the visa期限 (qíxiàn) [time limit] for the Chinese representatives and to allow the Korean representatives to enter Japan.

    This protest delegation was sent according to the resolution passed by the World Conference on August 6th.

    Ikuo Yasui, representing the delegation, met with Foreign Minister Zentaro Kosaka.

    Kosaka refused Ikuo Yasui's request to extend the visa期限 (qíxiàn) [time limit] for the Chinese representatives. He also stated that the Foreign Ministry would not allow the Korean representatives to enter the country.

    Although the other members of the delegation requested to meet with Kosaka, Kosaka did not accept the request.

### 禁止原子弹氢弹世界大会代表团去日本外务省  要求延长中国代表团签证期限  日本外务相小坂善太郎表示拒绝

1960-08-11
第5版()
专栏：

    禁止原子弹氢弹世界大会代表团去日本外务省
    要求延长中国代表团签证期限
    日本外务相小坂善太郎表示拒绝
    新华社10日讯　东京消息：第六届禁止原子弹氢弹世界大会9日下午派了一个由五十人组成的代表团到日本外务省去，要求日本政府延长中国代表的签证期限和准许朝鲜代表进入日本。
    这个抗议代表团是根据世界大会8月6日通过的决议派出的。
    安井郁代表代表团会见了外务相小坂善太郎。
    小坂拒绝了安井郁提出的延长中国代表的签证期限的要求。他并且说，外务省不准朝鲜代表入境。
    虽然代表团的其他团员要求同小坂会面，但是小坂没有接受这个要求。
