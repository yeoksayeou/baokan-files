Title: Soviet Union Captures American Spy

Author:
Date: 1960-08-05
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, August 2nd (TASS dispatch): The Soviet State Security organs have arrested Slavnov, a special agent of the American spy agency. This June, he was detained while attempting to cross the Soviet border into Iran at a border section.

The task Slavnov received from the American spy agency was: to settle in Leningrad, collect secret materials regarding important Soviet defense and industrial targets, primarily secret materials about the locations of intercontinental ballistic missiles and Soviet air defense facilities, and transmit these materials to the spy center via radio and letters (using steganography).

At the time of Slavnov's arrest, some secret intelligence that he had prepared to send to the American spy agency and some stolen Soviet citizen identity cards were found on his person. (Attached picture)

### 苏联捕获美国特务一名

1960-08-05
第6版()
专栏：

    苏联捕获美国特务一名
    新华社2日讯　塔斯社莫斯科2日讯：苏联国家保安机关逮捕了美国间谍机关的特务斯拉夫诺夫。今年6月，他在边境的一个地段企图越过苏联国境转移到伊朗去时被拘留了。
    斯拉夫诺夫从美国间谍机关接受到的任务是：在列宁格勒定居，搜集关于苏联重要的国防目标和工业目标的秘密材料，而首先是关于洲际弹道火箭设置的地方和苏联防空工具设置的地方的秘密材料，并将这些材料通过无线电和信件（用隐写的方法）转发给间谍中心。
    在逮捕斯拉夫诺夫时，在他身上查获他准备给美国间谍机关发出的一些秘密情报和他窃取的一些苏联公民身份证。（附图片）
