Title: At the Meeting of Secretaries of the Korean Armistice Commission, Our Side Protests US Aircraft Intrusion

Author:

Date: 1960-08-13
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, Pyongyang, August 11th. According to the Korean Central News Agency, the two sides of the Korean Military Armistice Commission held their 214th Secretaries' Meeting today at the suggestion of the Korean-Chinese side.

At the meeting, Colonel Lin Jiji, Secretary of the Korean-Chinese side, lodged a serious protest with the US side regarding the recent hostile actions of US military aircraft entering the airspace over the Korean-Chinese side's area.

The Secretary of the Korean-Chinese side pointed out that at approximately 9:55 AM on August 3, 1960, two US F-86 jet aircraft intruded from the south into the airspace over Yeli Mountain (transliteration), which is under the military control of the Korean-Chinese side, for reconnaissance activities.

On the same day, at approximately 11:55 AM, a US L-19 military aircraft intruded from the direction of Ganghwa Island into the airspace near Fengchuanli (transliteration), flying over Baima Mountain, which is under the military control of the Korean-Chinese side. The Secretary of the Korean-Chinese side resolutely demanded at the meeting that the US side severely punish those personnel involved in these incidents.

### 在朝鲜停战委员会秘书长会议上  我方抗议美机侵犯事件

1960-08-13
第6版()
专栏：

    在朝鲜停战委员会秘书长会议上
    我方抗议美机侵犯事件
    新华社平壤11日电　据朝鲜中央通讯社报道，朝鲜军事停战委员会双方秘书长会议在朝中方面建议下，今天举行第二百十四次会议。
    会上，朝中方面秘书长林箕济上校就最近美方军用飞机进入朝中方面地区上空的敌对行动，向美方提出严重抗议。
    朝中方面秘书长指出，1960年8月3日九时五十五分左右，美方F—86型喷气飞机两架，从南方侵入朝中方面军事控制下的耶里山（译音）上空进行侦察活动。
    同一天十一时五十五分左右，美方L—19型军用飞机一架从江华岛方向侵入枫川里（译音）附近上空，飞越朝中方面军事控制下的白马山上空。朝中方面秘书长在会上坚决要求美方严惩同这些事件有关的人员。
