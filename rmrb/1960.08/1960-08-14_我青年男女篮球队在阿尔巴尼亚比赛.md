Title: Our Youth Men's and Women's Basketball Teams Compete in Albania
Author: 
Date: 1960-08-14
Page: 6
    Xinhua News Agency, Tirana, August 12th. The Chinese youth men's and women's basketball teams competed against the local men's and women's "Skanderbeg" teams in Korçë on the 11th and 12th, respectively. The Chinese youth men's and women's teams won with scores of 76-35 and 67-24, respectively.
    Prior to this, the Chinese youth men's team played against the Albanian champion team "Partizan" in Tirana on the 7th, winning with a score of 93-34. On the same day, the Chinese youth women's team defeated the Shkodër women's team in Shkodër with a score of 63-18.

### 我青年男女篮球队在阿尔巴尼亚比赛

1960-08-14
第6版()
专栏：

    我青年男女篮球队在阿尔巴尼亚比赛
    新华社地拉那12日电　中国青年男、女篮球队11日、12日两日在科尔察分别同当地的男、女“斯坎德培”队进行了比赛。中国青年男、女队分别以七十六比三十五和六十七比二十四获胜。
    在此以前，中国青年男子队7日曾在地拉那同阿尔巴尼亚冠军队“游击队”进行了比赛，结果以九十三比三十四获胜。同日中国青年女子队在斯库台以六十三比十八胜斯库台女子队。
