Title: Premier Zhou and Foreign Minister Chen Send Congratulatory Telegrams on the Independence of the Gabonese Republic; China Recognizes the Gabonese Republic
Author:
Date: 1960-08-17
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, August 16th: Premier Zhou Enlai of the People's Republic of China today sent a telegram to Mr. Léon M'ba, Prime Minister of the Gabonese Republic, congratulating the Gabonese Republic on its declaration of independence. The telegram reads as follows:
To His Excellency Mr. Léon M'ba, Prime Minister of the Gabonese Republic, Libreville, Gabon:

    On the occasion of the Gabonese Republic's declaration of independence, I, on behalf of the government and people of the People's Republic of China, extend warm congratulations to the government and people of the Gabonese Republic. I wish the people of the Gabonese Republic further achievements in their struggle to uphold national independent development and oppose imperialism and colonialism. I wish your country prosperity and your people happiness.

            Zhou Enlai, Premier of the State Council of the People's Republic of China
                         August 16, 1960, Beijing

Xinhua News Agency, August 16th: Chen Yi, Minister of Foreign Affairs of the People's Republic of China, today sent a telegram to Mr. Léon M'ba, Prime Minister of the Gabonese Republic, congratulating the Gabonese Republic on its declaration of independence and informing him of the Chinese government's decision to recognize the Gabonese Republic. The telegram reads as follows:
To His Excellency Mr. Léon M'ba, Prime Minister of the Gabonese Republic, Libreville, Gabon:

    I am delighted to learn of the Gabonese Republic's declaration of independence, and I, on behalf of the government of the People's Republic of China, extend sincere congratulations to the government of the Gabonese Republic. I am honored to inform Your Excellency that the government of the People's Republic of China has decided to recognize the Gabonese Republic. I hope that my government's recognition of your country will lead to further development of relations between our two countries.

              Chen Yi, Minister of Foreign Affairs of the People's Republic of China
                                August 16, 1960

### 周总理陈外长电贺加蓬共和国独立  我国承认加蓬共和国

1960-08-17
第1版()
专栏：

    周总理陈外长电贺加蓬共和国独立
    我国承认加蓬共和国
    新华社16日讯　中华人民共和国总理周恩来今天打电报给加蓬共和国总理莱昂·姆巴，祝贺加蓬共和国宣告独立。电文如下：加蓬　利伯维尔加蓬共和国总理莱昂·姆巴先生阁下：
    在此加蓬共和国宣告独立的时候，我谨代表中华人民共和国政府和人民向加蓬共和国政府和人民致以热烈的祝贺。祝加蓬共和国人民在坚持民族独立发展的道路、反对帝国主义和殖民主义的斗争中取得进一步的成就。祝贵国国家繁荣，人民幸福。
            中华人民共和国国务院总理　周恩来
                          1960年8月16日于北京
    新华社16日讯　中华人民共和国外交部长陈毅今天打电报给加蓬共和国总理莱昂·姆巴，祝贺加蓬共和国宣告独立，并通知他中国政府决定承认加蓬共和国。电文如下：加蓬　利伯维尔加蓬共和国总理莱昂·姆巴先生阁下：
    欣悉加蓬共和国宣告独立，我谨代表中华人民共和国政府向加蓬共和国政府致以诚挚的祝贺。我荣幸地通知阁下，中华人民共和国政府已经决定承认加蓬共和国。我希望，我国政府对贵国的承认将导致我们两国关系的进一步发展。
              中华人民共和国外交部长　陈毅
                                1960年8月16日
