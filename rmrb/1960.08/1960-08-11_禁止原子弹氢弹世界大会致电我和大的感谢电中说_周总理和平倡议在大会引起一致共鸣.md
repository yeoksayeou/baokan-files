Title: World Conference Against Atomic and Hydrogen Bombs Sends Telegram to Me and the Great Hall, Expressing Thanks, Stating that Premier Zhou's Peace Initiative Resonated Universally at the Conference
Author:
Date: 1960-08-11
Page: 5
Xinhua News Agency, August 10th -- The Chinese People's Committee for World Peace has received a telegram of thanks from the Sixth World Conference Against Atomic and Hydrogen Bombs and for General Disarmament. The full text is as follows:

To the Chinese People's Committee for World Peace:

The Sixth World Conference Against Atomic and Hydrogen Bombs and for General Disarmament deeply thanks the Chinese people for sending an excellent delegation, unseen for three years, to participate in the conference and for providing great assistance to the conference, and for expressing indescribably strong support for the relief movement for atomic and hydrogen bomb victims. The suggestion made by Premier Zhou Enlai of your country in his congratulatory message to the conference, that all countries in Asia and the Pacific region conclude a treaty of mutual non-aggression and make this region a nuclear-weapon-free zone, has received unanimous resonance from the conference participants and has been accepted by the conference.

We also deeply thank your committee, the Chinese Committee for Afro-Asian Solidarity, the All-China Federation of Trade Unions, the All-China Women's Federation, and other organizations for jointly hosting a meeting in Beijing on the 5th to support this conference, for sending us a congratulatory message, and for demonstrating our solidarity with your conference. The representatives of thirty-one countries and ten international organizations attending this conference, especially the 11,000 Japanese representatives, are determined to join the Chinese people in the future to do their utmost to fight against imperialism, for national independence, and for peaceful coexistence.

World Conference Against Atomic and Hydrogen Bombs and for General Disarmament
August 8, 1960

### 禁止原子弹氢弹世界大会致电我和大的感谢电中说  周总理和平倡议在大会引起一致共鸣

1960-08-11
第5版()
专栏：

    禁止原子弹氢弹世界大会致电我和大的感谢电中说
    周总理和平倡议在大会引起一致共鸣
    新华社10日讯　中国人民保卫世界和平委员会收到了第六届禁止原子弹氢弹和争取全面裁军世界大会发来的感谢电报，全文如下：
    中国人民保卫世界和平委员会：
    第六届禁止原子弹氢弹和争取全面裁军世界大会深深地感谢中国人民，感谢中国人民派遣了三年未见的、优秀的代表团来参加大会，并且给大会以巨大的协助，和对原子弹氢弹受害者的救援运动表示了言语难以形容的强有力的支援。贵国国务院总理周恩来阁下在给大会的贺电中，提出了亚洲及太平洋地区一切国家缔结一项互不侵犯条约，并且使这个地区成为无核武器地区的建议，这项建议得到了大会与会者一致的共鸣，并为大会所接受。
    我们还要深深感谢贵委员会、中国亚非团结委员会、中华全国总工会、中华人民共和国全国妇女联合会等团体于5日在北京共同主办的支持本大会的大会给我们的贺电，并表明我们同贵大会的团结。出席本大会的三十一个国家和十个国际组织的代表，特别是一万一千名日本代表决心在今后和中国人民一道，为反对帝国主义、为民族独立和和平共处而竭尽全力进行斗争。
    禁止原子弹氢弹和争取全面裁
      军世界大会
              1960年8月8日
