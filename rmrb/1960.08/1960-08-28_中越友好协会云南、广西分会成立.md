Title: Yunnan and Guangxi Branches of the China-Vietnam Friendship Association Established
Author:
Date: 1960-08-28
Page: 2

Xinhua News Agency, Kunming, August 26th: The Yunnan branch of the China-Vietnam Friendship Association was officially established on the 25th. The branch was jointly initiated and organized by nine people's organizations, including the Provincial Federation of Trade Unions, the Women's Federation, and the Youth Federation.

On the afternoon of the 25th, the Yunnan branch of the China-Vietnam Friendship Association held its inaugural ceremony in Kunming and approved the list of理事 (lǐshì, council members). At the meeting, Liu Zhuofu, Deputy Governor of Yunnan Province, was elected as the President of the Yunnan Branch of the China-Vietnam Friendship Association, with Liu Linyuan, Xu Jiarui, and Jing Zhuru as Vice Presidents.

Xinhua News Agency, Nanning, August 27th: The Guangxi Zhuang Autonomous Region branch of the China-Vietnam Friendship Association was established in Nanning on the 26th, and a celebration meeting was held. More than 600 people, including the heads of the eight initiating units of the Guangxi Branch of the China-Vietnam Friendship Association and representatives from all ethnic groups in Guangxi, attended the celebration meeting. Le Quang Nhan, Acting Consul of the Vietnamese Consulate in Nanning, and other consulate staff were also invited to attend the meeting.

The Guangxi branch of the China-Vietnam Friendship Association is headed by Qin Yingji as President, and Mo Naiqun, Ge Zhen, and Lin Kewu as Vice Presidents.

Qin Yingji spoke at the celebration meeting. He said that there is a long history of friendly relations between the people of China and Vietnam, and the establishment of the Guangxi branch of the China-Vietnam Friendship Association will surely promote the further development of friendly and cooperative relations between the two countries. Le Quang Nhan also spoke at the meeting. He said that the Vietnam-China Friendship Association of Vietnam and the China-Vietnam Friendship Association of China are the most beautiful flowers in the friendship garden of the two peoples.

### 中越友好协会云南、广西分会成立

1960-08-28
第2版()
专栏：

    中越友好协会云南、广西分会成立
    新华社昆明26日电　中越友好协会云南分会在25日宣告成立。分会是由省工会、妇联、青联等九个人民团体联合发起组织的。
    25日下午，中越友好协会云南分会，在昆明举行了成立仪式，并通过了协会理事名单。会上推选了云南省副省长刘卓甫担任中越友好协会云南分会会长，刘林元、徐嘉瑞、经竹如担任副会长。
    据新华社南宁27日电　中越友好协会广西僮族自治区分会26日在南宁成立，并举行了成立庆祝大会。参加庆祝大会的有中越友好协会广西分会八个发起单位的负责人和广西各族各界代表共六百多人。越南驻南宁领事馆代理领事黎光仁和领事馆的其他工作人员也应邀参加了大会。
    中越友好协会广西分会由覃应机任会长，莫乃群、葛震和林克武任副会长。
    覃应机在庆祝大会上讲了话。他说，中越两国人民之间有着历史悠久的友好关系，中越人民友好协会广西分会的成立，必将促进两国之间友好合作关系的进一步发展。黎光仁也在会上讲了话。他说，越南的越中友好协会和中国的中越友好协会都是两国人民友谊花园中最鲜艳的花朵。
