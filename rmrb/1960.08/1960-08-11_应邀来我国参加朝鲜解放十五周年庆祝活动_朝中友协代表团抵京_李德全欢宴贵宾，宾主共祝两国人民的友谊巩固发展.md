Title: In response to the invitation to come to our country to participate in the 15th Anniversary of the Liberation of Korea celebrations, the China-Korea Friendship Association Delegation Arrives in Beijing; Li Dequan Hosts a Banquet for the Distinguished Guests, Hosts and Guests Jointly Wish for the Consolidation and Development of Friendship Between the People of the Two Countries
Author:
Date: 1960-08-11
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, 10th. The China-Korea Friendship Association delegation, invited to our country to participate in the celebrations of the 15th anniversary of the liberation of Korea, arrived in Beijing by train from Shenyang this morning.

The head of the China-Korea Friendship Association delegation is Kim Jong-heng, Minister of Higher Education of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea and Standing Committee Member of the China-Korea Friendship Association. The deputy head is Park Fung-sik, Deputy Director of the Heavy Industry Department of the Central Committee of the Workers' Party of Korea. Members include Han Soo-dong, Acting President of Kim Il-sung University of Korea; Moon Han-bong, Party Committee Chairman of the Korean State Planning Commission; Han Cha-nam (female), Director of the First Bureau of the Korean Committee for Foreign Cultural Liaison; Yang Gi-jeong, Section Chief of the Central Committee of the Workers' Party of Korea; Kim Bong-il, Editor-in-Chief of the "Democratic Youth" newspaper of the Central Committee of the Korean Democratic Youth League; Park Sung-joon, Minister of the Korean Central News Agency; and Li Yul, Instructor of the Central Committee of the Workers' Party of Korea.

As the envoys of the heroic Korean people stepped off the train, enthusiastic young women rushed forward to present flowers to the distinguished guests. The welcomers applauded continuously and warmly, expressing the Chinese people's high respect for the fraternal Korean people.

People from all walks of life in our country went to the station to welcome the distinguished guests, including Li Dequan, President of the China-Korea Friendship Association, Vice Presidents Lao She and Wei Chuantong, Vice Ministers of the Commission for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries Chu Tunan and Zhang Zhixiang, Vice Minister of Foreign Affairs Ji Pengfei, Vice Minister of the Industrial Work Department of the CPC Central Committee Yu Jiangzhen, Vice Minister of Education Dong Chuncai, our Ambassador to Korea Qiao Xiaoguang, Vice Mayor of Beijing Wu Han, Huang Jiguang's mother Deng Fangzhi, and people from the capital's education, news, and literary and art circles.

Ma Dongshan, Charge d'Affaires ad interim of the Korean Embassy in China, and diplomatic officials of the embassy also went to the station to greet them.

Xinhua News Agency, 10th. Li Dequan, President of the China-Korea Friendship Association, hosted a banquet tonight to warmly welcome the China-Korea Friendship Association delegation headed by Kim Jong-heng and deputy head Park Fung-sik.

On the eve of the glorious "August 15th" 15th Anniversary of the Liberation of Korea, people from all walks of life in China and the distinguished guests of the China-Korea Friendship Association delegation gathered together cordially. Everyone was particularly happy, and the hosts and guests repeatedly rose to toast the unbreakable fighting friendship between the Chinese and Korean people, and the strong unity of the socialist camp headed by the Soviet Union.

Li Yong-ho, Ambassador of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea to China, his wife, diplomatic officials of the embassy, and Korean person Huang Feng-jiu in Beijing were also invited to attend the banquet.

Li Dequan spoke at the banquet, warmly welcoming the envoys of the heroic Korean people. She said that the Chinese people regard the great festival of the 15th anniversary of the liberation of Korea as their own festival. This visit by the China-Korea Friendship Association delegation to our country will add splendor to the China-Korea Friendship Week celebrations held in our country and contribute to the development of the friendship between the people of our two countries.

Head of delegation Kim Jong-heng conveyed the warm greetings of the Korean people to the Chinese people in his speech. He said that the friendship between the people of Korea and China has a long history. In particular, the friendship forged with blood by the people of the two countries in the struggle against US imperialism cannot be destroyed by anyone. He said that this visit of the China-Korea Friendship Association delegation to China will contribute to further consolidating and developing the friendship between the people of the two countries.

Those present at the banquet included Zhang Xiruo, Director of the Commission for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries, Vice Directors Chu Tunan and Zhang Zhixiang, Vice Minister of Foreign Affairs Ji Pengfei, Vice Minister of the Industrial Work Department of the CPC Central Committee Yu Jiangzhen, Vice Minister of Education Dong Chuncai, our Ambassador to Korea Qiao Xiaoguang, Vice Presidents of the China-Korea Friendship Association Lao She and Wei Chuantong, Directors Li Jiebo, Liu Baiyu, and Huang Luofeng, Vice Mayor of Beijing Wu Han, Huang Jiguang's mother Deng Fangzhi, and people from the capital's education, news, and literary and art circles Zhou Peiyuan, Hu Jiwei, and Yang Shuo, etc.

### 应邀来我国参加朝鲜解放十五周年庆祝活动  朝中友协代表团抵京  李德全欢宴贵宾，宾主共祝两国人民的友谊巩固发展

1960-08-11
第1版()
专栏：

    应邀来我国参加朝鲜解放十五周年庆祝活动
    朝中友协代表团抵京
    李德全欢宴贵宾，宾主共祝两国人民的友谊巩固发展
    新华社10日讯　应邀来我国参加朝鲜解放十五周年庆祝活动的朝中友好协会代表团，今天上午乘火车由沈阳到达北京。
    朝中友好协会代表团团长是朝鲜民主主义人民共和国高等教育相、朝中友协常务委员金钟恒，副团长是朝鲜劳动党中央委员会重工业部副部长朴丰植。团员有朝鲜金日成综合大学代总长韩秀东，朝鲜国家计划委员会党委员长文韩峰，朝鲜对外文化联络委员会第一局局长韩次男（女），朝鲜劳动党中央委员会科长杨淇汀，朝鲜民主青年同盟中央委员会机关报“民主青年”社总编辑金奉一，朝鲜中央通讯社部长朴圣俊和朝鲜劳动党中央委员会指导员李聿。
    英雄的朝鲜人民的使者走下火车后，热情的女青年，奔上前去向贵宾们献了鲜花。欢迎的人们不断热烈鼓掌，表达中国人民对兄弟的朝鲜人民的崇高敬意。
    我国各方面人士到车站欢迎贵宾，他们当中有中朝友好协会会长李德全、副会长老舍、魏传统，对外文委副主任楚图南、张致祥，外交部副部长姬鹏飞，中共中央工业工作部副部长于江震，教育部副部长董纯才，我国驻朝鲜大使乔晓光，北京市副市长吴晗，黄继光烈士的母亲邓芳芝，以及首都教育、新闻、文艺界人士等。
    朝鲜驻中国大使馆临时代办马东山和大使馆外交官员也到车站迎接。
    新华社10日讯　中朝友好协会会长李德全今晚举行宴会，热烈欢迎以团长金钟恒和副团长朴丰植为首的朝中友好协会代表团。
    在“八·一五”朝鲜解放十五周年光荣节日的前夕，中国各方面的人士同朝中友协代表团的贵宾们亲切欢聚，大家显得格外欢乐，宾主一再起立，为中朝两国人民的牢不可破的战斗友谊，为以苏联为首的社会主义阵营的坚强团结而干杯。
    朝鲜民主主义人民共和国驻中国大使李永镐和夫人，大使馆的外交官员，在北京的朝鲜人士黄凤九都应邀出席了宴会。
    李德全在宴会上讲话，她热烈欢迎英雄的朝鲜人民的使者。她说，中国人民把朝鲜解放十五周年这个伟大节日看做是自己的节日。朝中友协代表团这次来我国访问，将给我国举行的中朝友好周庆祝活动增添光采，并对发展我们两国人民的友谊做出贡献。
    金钟恒团长在讲话中，热情地转达了朝鲜人民对中国人民的问候。他说，朝中两国人民的友谊有着悠久的历史。特别是两国人民在反对美帝国主义斗争中用鲜血凝结成的友谊是任何人破坏不了的。他说，朝中友协代表团这次访问中国，将为进一步巩固和发展两国人民的友谊做出贡献。
    出席宴会的，有对外文化联络委员会主任张奚若，副主任楚图南、张致祥，外交部副部长姬鹏飞，中共中央工业工作部副部长于江震，教育部副部长董纯才，我国驻朝鲜大使乔晓光，中朝友好协会副会长老舍、魏传统，理事李颉伯、刘白羽、黄洛峰，北京市副市长吴晗，黄继光烈士的母亲邓芳芝，以及首都教育、新闻、文艺界人士周培源、胡绩伟、杨朔等。（附图片）
