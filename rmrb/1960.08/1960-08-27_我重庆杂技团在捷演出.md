Title: Chongqing Acrobatic Troupe Performs in Czechoslovakia
Author:
Date: 1960-08-27
Page: 5
    Xinhua News Agency, Prague, August 26th: The Chinese Chongqing Acrobatic Troupe, currently on a visit and performance tour in Czechoslovakia, gave its opening performance in Prague last night. More than six thousand spectators enjoyed the acrobatic troupe's sixteen wonderful programs. Each program won enthusiastic applause from the entire audience on multiple occasions.

### 我重庆杂技团在捷演出

1960-08-27
第5版()
专栏：

    我重庆杂技团在捷演出
    据新华社布拉格26日电　目前正在捷克斯洛伐克作访问演出的中国重庆杂技团，昨晚在布拉格作了在这个首都的开幕演出。六千多观众欣赏了杂技团的十六个精彩节目。每一个节目都赢得了全场观众多次的热烈鼓掌。
