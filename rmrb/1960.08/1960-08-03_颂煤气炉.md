Title: Ode to the Coal Gas Stove
Author: Zhen Ji
Date: 1960-08-03
Page: 2

    Ode to the Coal Gas Stove
      Zhen Ji
    Coal gas stove,
    Is a treasure,
    Saves coal with a great shout;
    Operation is light and clever,
    Production greatly improved.
