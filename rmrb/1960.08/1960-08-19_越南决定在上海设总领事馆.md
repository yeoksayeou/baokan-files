Title: Vietnam Decides to Establish Consulate General in Shanghai
Author:
Date: 1960-08-19
Page: 6

    Vietnam Decides to Establish Consulate General in Shanghai
    Xinhua News Agency, August 18th – The government of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam, with the consent of our government, has decided to establish a Consulate General in Shanghai.

### 越南决定在上海设总领事馆

1960-08-19
第6版()
专栏：

    越南决定在上海设总领事馆
    新华社18日讯　越南民主共和国政府征得我国政府同意以后，已决定在上海设立总领事馆。
