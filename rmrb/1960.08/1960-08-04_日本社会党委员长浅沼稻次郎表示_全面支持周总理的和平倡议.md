Title: Japanese Socialist Party Chairman Inejiro Asanuma States: Fully Supports Premier Zhou's Peace Initiative
Author:
Date: 1960-08-04
Page: 5
    Xinhua News Agency, Tokyo, 3rd. Japanese Socialist Party Chairman Inejiro Asanuma stated that he fully supports Premier Zhou Enlai's proposal for Asian and Pacific coastal countries to sign a treaty of mutual non-aggression and to build the entire region into a nuclear-weapon-free zone.

    Asanuma made these remarks at a press conference held in Kumamoto.

    Asanuma emphasized that Premier Zhou Enlai's proposal is very important and that the Ikeda Cabinet should also seriously consider this matter.

    Regarding Sino-Japanese relations, Asanuma said that they should be normalized as soon as possible. He said that in view of the growing strength within the conservative parties willing to work to break the deadlock in Sino-Japanese relations, he would propose that after the general election, a delegation beyond party lines be sent to visit China to conduct concrete consultations with the Chinese side.

### 日本社会党委员长浅沼稻次郎表示  全面支持周总理的和平倡议

1960-08-04
第5版()
专栏：

    日本社会党委员长浅沼稻次郎表示
    全面支持周总理的和平倡议
    新华社3日讯　东京消息：日本社会党委员长浅沼稻次郎表示全面支持周恩来总理关于亚洲和太平洋沿岸各国签订互不侵犯的和平公约，把整个地区建成为没有核武器的地区的建议。
    浅沼的这番话是在熊本举行记者招待会时发表的。
    浅沼强调指出，周恩来总理这项建议是很重要的，池田内阁也应该慎重考虑这件事。
    浅沼说关于日中关系，应该早日趋于正常化。他说，鉴于在保守政党内部愿意努力打开日中关系僵局的力量逐渐强大的情况，他要建议在大选之后派遣一个超越党派的使节团访问中国，同中国方面进行具体洽商。
