Title: Two Guatemalan Student Union Representatives Arrive in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1960-08-07
Page: 6
Xinhua News Agency, August 6th. Two representatives from the Guatemalan National Student Union, Cultural Secretary Julio César Segura Tucuherro and Executive Committee Member Leones Roldán Salguero, arrived in Beijing by plane today at the invitation of the All-China Students' Federation to visit our country.
    Zhang Yin'an, Vice-Chairman of the All-China Students' Federation, and others welcomed them at the airport.

### 两位危地马拉学联代表到京

1960-08-07
第6版()
专栏：

    两位危地马拉学联代表到京
    新华社6日讯　应中华全国学生联合会邀请来我国访问的危地马拉全国学生联合会代表：文化书记胡利欧·赛沙尔·塞古拉·图虚赫罗和执行委员会委员累欧内斯·罗尔当·沙尔逵罗二人，今天乘飞机到达北京。
    到机场欢迎的有中华全国学生联合会副主席张寅安等人。
