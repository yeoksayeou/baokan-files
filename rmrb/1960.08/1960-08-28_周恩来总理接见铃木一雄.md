Title: Premier Zhou Enlai Receives Kazuo Suzuki

Author: 
Date: 1960-08-28
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, August 27th: Premier Zhou Enlai received Kazuo Suzuki, the Standing Director of the Japan Afro-Asian Solidarity Committee and Managing Director of the Japan-China Trade Promotion Association, and his party of three this afternoon. Present at the reception were Liao Chengzhi, Xiao Xiangqian, and Wang Xiaoyun. (Attached picture)

Premier Zhou Enlai receives Kazuo Suzuki. The picture shows a cordial conversation during the meeting. From left: Zhou Enlai, Kazuo Suzuki, Shujiro Tanaka, Kazuyo Morikawa.

### 周恩来总理接见铃木一雄

1960-08-28
第1版()
专栏：

    周恩来总理接见铃木一雄
