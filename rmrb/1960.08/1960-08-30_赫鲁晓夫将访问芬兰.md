Title: Khrushchev to Visit Finland
Author: 
Date: 1960-08-30
Page: 5

    Khrushchev to Visit Finland
    Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, August 29th: TASS, Moscow, August 29th, reports: Khrushchev, Chairman of the Soviet Council of Ministers, will visit Finland in early September this year.

### 赫鲁晓夫将访问芬兰

1960-08-30
第5版()
专栏：

    赫鲁晓夫将访问芬兰
    新华社29日讯　据塔斯社莫斯科29日讯：苏联部长会议主席赫鲁晓夫将于今年9月上旬访问芬兰。
