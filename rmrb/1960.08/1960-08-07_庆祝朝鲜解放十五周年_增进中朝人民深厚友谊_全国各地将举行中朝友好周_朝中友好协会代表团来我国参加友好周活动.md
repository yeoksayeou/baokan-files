Title: Celebrating the 15th Anniversary of the Liberation of Korea, Enhancing the Profound Friendship Between the Chinese and Korean People, China Will Hold Sino-Korean Friendship Week Throughout the Country, Delegation of the China-Korea Friendship Association Will Come to Our Country to Participate in Friendship Week Activities
Author:
Date: 1960-08-07
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, 6th dispatch: The 15th anniversary of Korea's "August 15th" liberation is approaching. Our capital and other cities are preparing to hold grand celebrations, and through extensive celebration activities, the profound friendship between the people of China and Korea cemented with blood will be further enhanced.

From August 8th, Sino-Korean Friendship Week celebrations will be held successively in cities such as the capital, Shenyang, Changchun, Harbin, Changsha, Wuhan, Shanghai, Guangzhou, Chongqing, Xi'an, Andong, and Yanji. The celebrations in the capital are scheduled to begin on the 11th, when people from all walks of life in the capital will hold a grand celebration meeting, host the "Construction Picture Exhibition of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea," and some schools, factories, people's communes and other units will hold celebration meetings, get-togethers, and report meetings. The country will also issue commemorative stamps for the "15th Anniversary of the Liberation of Korea."

During the Sino-Korean Friendship Week, a "Film Week of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea" will be held in sixteen cities including the capital, and Korean films will also be screened in other cities across the country.

At the invitation of the China-Korea Friendship Association, the delegation of the Korea-China Friendship Association, as friendship envoys of the Korean people, will come to our country to celebrate the glorious national festival of the Korean people with the people of various places.

Xinhua News Agency, Shenyang, 6th dispatch: The delegation of the Korea-China Friendship Association, consisting of nine people led by Kim Chong-hyon, Minister of Higher Education of the Korean Cabinet and Standing Committee Member of the Korea-China Friendship Association, arrived in Shenyang by special plane from Pyongyang at 10:00 this morning to participate in the commemorative activities celebrating the 15th anniversary of the liberation of Korea in our country. Those who welcomed them at the airport included: Li Huang, Secretary of the Liaoning Provincial Committee of the Communist Party of China and President of the Liaoning Branch of the China-Korea Friendship Association, Qiu Youwen, Ning Wu, and Gong Tianmin, Vice Governors of Liaoning Province, Lieutenant General Zeng Siyu, Head of the Shenyang Garrison, Zhang Panxin, Deputy Mayor of Shenyang, and Jin Zhifu, Chairman of the Liaoning Provincial Federation of Trade Unions and Vice President of the Liaoning Branch of the China-Korea Friiendship Association, and others.

This afternoon, the distinguished guests visited the Liaoning Industrial Exhibition Hall.

In the evening, Li Huang, President of the Liaoning Branch of the China-Korea Friendship Association, held a welcome banquet for the distinguished guests. Before the banquet, Huang Oudong, Second Secretary of the Liaoning Provincial Committee of the Communist Party of China, and Wu Tieming, Secretary of the Shenyang Municipal Committee of the Communist Party of China, met with the entire delegation of distinguished guests.

Xinhua News Agency, Pyongyang, 6th dispatch: The delegation of the Korea-China Friendship Association, which was invited to participate in the "Sino-Korean Friendship Week" activities held in our country to celebrate the 15th anniversary of the liberation of Korea, left Pyongyang by plane for Beijing today under the leadership of Kim Chong-hyon, the head of the delegation, the Minister of Higher Education of Korea, and a member of the Standing Committee of the Korea-China Friendship Association. Ha Ang-chon, Chairman of the Korea-China Friendship Association, Song Yong, Chairman of the Korean Committee for Foreign Cultural Relations, Liu Xianglun, Chargé d'Affaires ad interim of the Chinese Embassy in Korea, Li Lin, Cultural Counselor, and others saw them off at the airport.

### 庆祝朝鲜解放十五周年　增进中朝人民深厚友谊  全国各地将举行中朝友好周  朝中友好协会代表团来我国参加友好周活动

1960-08-07
第1版()
专栏：

    庆祝朝鲜解放十五周年　增进中朝人民深厚友谊
    全国各地将举行中朝友好周
    朝中友好协会代表团来我国参加友好周活动
    新华社6日讯　朝鲜“八·一五”解放十五周年即将到来。我国首都和其他一些城市正在准备举行规模盛大的庆祝活动，通过广泛的庆祝活动将进一步增进中朝两国人民用鲜血凝结成的深厚友谊。
    从8月8日起，首都、沈阳、长春、哈尔滨、长沙、武汉、上海、广州、重庆、西安、安东、延吉等城市，将先后举行中朝友好周庆祝活动。首都的庆祝活动准备在11日开始，届时首都各界人民将举行隆重的庆祝大会，举办“朝鲜民主主义人民共和国建设图片展览会”，一些学校、工厂、人民公社等单位将举行庆祝会、联欢会、报告会。全国还将发行“朝鲜解放十五周年”纪念邮票。
    在中朝友好周期间，首都等十六个城市要举行“朝鲜民主主义人民共和国电影周”，全国其他城市也将放映朝鲜影片。
    应中朝友好协会的邀请，作为朝鲜人民的友谊使者的朝中友好协会代表团，将前来我国，同各地人民一起庆祝光荣的朝鲜人民的全民节日。
    新华社沈阳6日电　前来我国参加庆祝朝鲜解放十五周年纪念活动的朝中友好协会代表团一行九人，在朝鲜内阁高等教育相、朝中友好协会常务委员金钟恒率领下，今天上午十时由平壤乘专机到达沈阳。到机场欢迎的有：中共辽宁省委书记、中朝友好协会辽宁省分会会长李荒，辽宁省副省长仇友文、宁武、巩天民，沈阳驻军负责人曾思玉中将，沈阳市副市长张盘新，辽宁省总工会主席、中朝友好协会辽宁省分会副会长金直夫等。
    今天下午，贵宾们参观了辽宁工业展览馆。
    晚上，中朝友好协会辽宁省分会会长李荒为贵宾们举行了欢迎宴会。宴会前，中共辽宁省委第二书记黄欧东和中共沈阳市委书记吴铁鸣会见了代表团全体贵宾。
    新华社平壤6日电　应邀参加我国为庆祝朝鲜解放十五周年举行的“中朝友好周”活动的朝中友好协会代表团，在团长、朝鲜高等教育相、朝中友好协会常务委员金钟恒率领下，今天乘飞机离平壤前往北京。到机场送行的有朝中友好协会委员长河仰天、朝鲜对外文化联络委员会委员长宋影、中国驻朝鲜大使馆临时代办刘祥纶、文化参赞李林等。
