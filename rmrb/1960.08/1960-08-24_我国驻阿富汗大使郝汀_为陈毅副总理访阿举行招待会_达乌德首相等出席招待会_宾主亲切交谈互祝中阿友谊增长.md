Title: Chinese Ambassador to Afghanistan Hao Ting Holds Reception for Vice Premier Chen Yi's Visit to Afghanistan; Prime Minister Daoud and Others Attend Reception; Hosts and Guests Chat Cordially and Wish Each Other Growing Sino-Afghan Friendship
Author:
Date: 1960-08-24
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Kabul, August 23rd. Chinese Ambassador to the Kingdom of Afghanistan, Hao Ting, held a grand reception last night for the visit of Marshal Chen Yi, Vice Premier and Minister of Foreign Affairs of the State Council of China, to Afghanistan.

Afghan Prime Minister Daoud, Deputy Prime Minister Ali Muhammad, and Deputy Prime Minister and Foreign Minister Naim attended the reception.

King's uncle, Marshal Shah Wali Khan Ghazi, also attended the reception.

Among the more than two hundred guests were Afghan cabinet ministers, senior military officers, and foreign diplomatic envoys.

The reception was held in the brightly lit garden of the Chinese Embassy. Vice Premier Chen Yi, Afghan Prime Minister Daoud and his wife, Deputy Prime Minister Ali Muhammad, Deputy Prime Minister Naim and his wife, and King's uncle Marshal Shah Wali Khan Ghazi sat together and chatted cordially with each other.

Members of the Chinese Youth Art Troupe, who are visiting here, were introduced to these Afghan dignitaries.

Kong Yuan, Deputy Director of the Foreign Affairs Office of the State Council, and Geng Biao, Vice Minister of Foreign Affairs, also talked with many guests in a friendly atmosphere. The hosts and guests expressed their hope that the friendship between China and Afghanistan would grow day by day. (Attached picture)
August 22nd, Vice Premier Chen Yi held talks with Prime Minister Sardar Muhammad Daoud (second from right). Xinhua News Agency reporter Liu Dongao (facsimile photo)

### 我国驻阿富汗大使郝汀  为陈毅副总理访阿举行招待会  达乌德首相等出席招待会　宾主亲切交谈互祝中阿友谊增长

1960-08-24
第1版()
专栏：

    我国驻阿富汗大使郝汀
    为陈毅副总理访阿举行招待会
    达乌德首相等出席招待会　宾主亲切交谈互祝中阿友谊增长
    新华社喀布尔23日电　中华人民共和国驻阿富汗王国大使郝汀昨天晚上为中国国务院副总理兼外交部长陈毅元帅访问阿富汗举行盛大的招待会。
    阿富汗首相达乌德，副首相阿里·穆罕默德，副首相兼外交大臣纳伊姆出席了招待会。
    国王的叔父沙阿·华利汗·加齐元帅也出席了招待会。
    在两百多位客人中，有阿富汗的内阁大臣们，高级军官和各国外交使节。
    招待会是在中国大使馆的灯光灿烂的花园中举行的。陈毅副总理，阿富汗首相达乌德和夫人，副首相阿里·穆罕默德，副首相纳伊姆和夫人，国王的叔父沙阿·华利汗·加齐元帅坐在一起，相互亲切地交谈。
    正在这里访问的中国青年艺术团团员们被介绍给这些阿富汗贵宾们。
    国务院外事办公室副主任孔原和外交部副部长耿飚也在友好的气氛中同许多客人交谈。宾主们表示希望中国和阿富汗之间的友谊一天天增长。（附图片）
    8月22日，陈毅副总理同萨达尔·穆罕默德·达乌德首相（右二）举行会谈。　新华社记者　刘东鳌摄（传真照片）
