Title: China-Nepal Joint Boundary Committee Holds Second Meeting; Meeting Discusses Overall Arrangements for Resolving Boundary Issues
Author:
Date: 1960-08-19
Page: 6
Xinhua News Agency, Kathmandu, 18th: The second session of the first meeting of the China-Nepal Joint Boundary Committee was held in the Lion Palace in Kathmandu, the capital of the Kingdom of Nepal, from 10:00 AM to 12:00 PM on the 17th. The meeting, in a friendly and warm atmosphere, discussed the overall arrangements for resolving the boundary issue and some specific issues. The next meeting of the China-Nepal Joint Boundary Committee will be held on August 22nd.

### 中尼边界联合委员会举行第二次会谈  会议讨论了解决边界问题的总安排问题

1960-08-19
第6版()
专栏：

    中尼边界联合委员会举行第二次会谈
    会议讨论了解决边界问题的总安排问题
    新华社18日讯　加德满都消息：中尼边界联合委员会第一次会议第二次会谈17日上午十时至十二时在尼泊尔王国首都加德满都的狮宫举行。会谈在友好和热诚的气氛中，讨论了解决边界问题的总的安排以及某些具体问题。中尼边界联合委员会的下一次会谈将在8月22日举行。
