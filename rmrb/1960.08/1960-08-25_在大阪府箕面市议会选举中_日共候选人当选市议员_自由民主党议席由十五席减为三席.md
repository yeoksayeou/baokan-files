Title: In the Minoo City Assembly Election in Osaka Prefecture, a Japanese Communist Party Candidate Was Elected as City Councilor; The Liberal Democratic Party's Seats Decreased from Fifteen to Three
Author:
Date: 1960-08-25
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, Tokyo, August 24th: According to a report in today's "Akahata," in the Minoo City Assembly election held in Osaka Prefecture on August 21st, a Communist Party candidate was elected as a city councilor. This is the first time that a Communist Party candidate has been elected as a city councilor in this city.

The Communist Party candidate, Hara Toru, received almost three times as many votes as last time.

"Akahata" said that Hara Toru received the support of ten local mass organizations in the election campaign, including the local railway union and other trade unions.

The Liberal Democratic Party suffered a disastrous defeat in this election, with its seats in the city assembly reduced from fifteen to three.

### 在大阪府箕面市议会选举中  日共候选人当选市议员  自由民主党议席由十五席减为三席

1960-08-25
第6版()
专栏：

    在大阪府箕面市议会选举中
    日共候选人当选市议员
    自由民主党议席由十五席减为三席
    新华社24日讯　东京消息：据“赤旗报”今天报道，8月21日在大阪府箕面市举行的市议会选举中，共产党候选人当选为市议会议员。共产党候选人在这个城市当选市议员，这还是第一次。
    共产党候选人原亨得票数比上一次几乎增加为三倍。
    “赤旗报”说：原亨在竞选运动中得到十个当地群众团体的支持，包括当地铁道工会和其他工会的支持。
    自由民主党在这次选举中遭受了惨重的失败，它在市议会的议席从十五席减少到三席。
