Title: Turning Barren Land into Fertile Fields: Albania's Land Reclamation Work Progresses Smoothly
Author:
Date: 1960-08-01
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, August 31st: According to the Albanian Telegraph Agency, land reclamation work is progressing smoothly in Albania.

    Reclamation workers recently completed the reclamation work on the Myzeqe Plain, reclaiming 35,000 hectares of land. In the Mallesh Plain, they reclaimed 2,000 hectares of land. In addition, the reclamation work on the Vurg and Seman plains is nearing completion, and the reclamation work in Roskovec is also being accelerated. In this way, the task of reclaiming the above-mentioned plains stipulated in Albania's Second Five-Year Plan (1956-1960) is being successfully completed.

    Large-scale irrigation projects have been built on the plains that have been reclaimed and are being reclaimed. A reservoir with a capacity of 65 million cubic meters has been built in Myzeqe. Two other large reservoirs are under construction in Roskovec and Tarine.

    During the fourth and fifth years of the Second Five-Year Plan, reclamation workers made great contributions, opening up a total of 15,000 hectares of cultivated land and irrigating another 15,000 hectares of land. Before the war, Albania had only 270,000 hectares of cultivated land. Land reclamation work began after the war. During the Second Five-Year Plan alone, the state allocated 3.5 billion lek for land reclamation. During the Third Five-Year Plan, reclamation will begin on the plains of Hoxharë, Karavasta, and Durrës. During this period, the reclamation of the Maliq Plain will be fully completed, thereby opening up and improving 37,300 hectares of land. (Photo attached)
    
    In recent years, Albania has vigorously reclaimed land, greatly promoting the increase in agricultural production. The picture shows a bulldozer of the Albanian land reclamation team at work.

### 变荒地为良田　扩大耕地面积  阿尔巴尼亚垦荒工作顺利进行

1960-08-01
第6版()
专栏：

    变荒地为良田　扩大耕地面积
    阿尔巴尼亚垦荒工作顺利进行
    新华社31日讯　据阿尔巴尼亚通讯社报道，阿尔巴尼亚的垦荒工作正在顺利进行。
    垦荒者最近完成了穆泽克平原上的开垦工作，开垦了三万五千公顷土地。在毛尔什平原，他们开垦了两千公顷土地。此外，伍尔格和素曼平原的开垦工作接近完成，罗斯科维茨的开垦工作也正在加速进行。这样，阿尔巴尼亚第二个五年计划（1956—1960年）规定的开垦上述平原的任务正在胜利完成。
    在已经开垦的平原上以及正在开垦的地方，都兴建了巨大的灌溉工程，在穆泽克兴建了一个容积达六千五百万立方米的水库。在罗斯科维茨和塔里内，正在兴建另外两个大水库。
    在第二个五年计划的四、五两年期间，垦荒者作出了巨大的贡献，他们共开辟了一万五千公顷耕地，并使另外一万五千公顷土地得到灌溉。在战前阿尔巴尼亚耕地面积只有二十七万公顷。垦荒工作是战后开始的。单是在第二个五年计划期间，国家就为开荒工作拨出了三十五亿列克。在第三个五年计划期间，将在霍贾拉斯、卡拉瓦斯塔斯和杜雷斯等平原开始进行开垦。在这个时期，马利克平原的开垦工作将完全告成，从而能开拓和改善三万七千三百公顷土地。（附图片）
