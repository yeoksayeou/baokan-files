Title: Our Officials Leave for Delhi to Attend Sino-Indian Officials' Meeting
Author:
Date: 1960-08-16
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, 15th. In accordance with the provisions of the Sino-Indian Joint Communiqué of April 25, 1960, the Sino-Indian officials' meeting, after the conclusion of the Beijing stage, will continue in Delhi. Our officials participating in the Sino-Indian officials' meeting: Zhang Wenjin, Director of the First Asian Department of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs, Yang Gongsu, Director of the Foreign Affairs Office of Tibet, and advisors, left Beijing by plane this morning for Delhi.

### 参加中印官员会晤  我方官员前往德里

1960-08-16
第5版()
专栏：

    参加中印官员会晤
    我方官员前往德里
    新华社15日讯　根据1960年4月25日中印两国总理联合公报的规定而举行的中印官员会晤，在北京阶段的工作结束后，将在德里继续进行。参加中印官员会晤的我方官员：外交部第一亚洲司司长章文晋、西藏外事处处长杨公素和顾问等，已于今天上午乘飞机离开北京前往德里。
