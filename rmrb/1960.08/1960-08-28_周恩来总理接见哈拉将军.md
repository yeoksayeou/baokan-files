Title: Premier Zhou Enlai Receives General Hara
Author:
Date: 1960-08-28
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, August 27th: Premier Zhou Enlai received Mr. Heriberto Jara, a prominent Mexican social activist and Chairman of the Mexican National Peace Council, and Mr. José Rosales, editor of the Mexican magazine "Eternal," who accompanied him, this afternoon. They had a cordial and friendly conversation.

Present at the reception were Chu Tunan, President of the Chinese-Latin American Friendship Association, Wang Mingyuan, Deputy Secretary-General of the Chinese People's Committee for World Peace, and Shen Jian, Director of the Department of American and Australian Affairs of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs. Also present at the reception were Chilean peace activist Venturelli and his wife, who are currently in Beijing. (Attached image)

Premier Zhou receives Hara. The photo shows the group at the reception. The fourth from the right is Heriberto Hara, and the second is José Rosales. The third from the left is Chilean peace activist Venturelli, and the fourth is Venturelli's wife. (Photo by Du Xiuxian, Xinhua News Agency)

### 周恩来总理接见哈拉将军

1960-08-28
第1版()
专栏：

    周恩来总理接见哈拉将军
    新华社27日讯　周恩来总理今天下午接见了墨西哥著名社会活动家、墨西哥全国和平理事会主席埃里维尔托·哈拉将军和随他同来的墨西哥“永恒”杂志编辑何塞·罗萨莱斯，并同他们进行了亲切友好的谈话。
    接见时在座的有中国拉丁美洲友好协会会长楚图南，中国人民保卫世界和平委员会副秘书长王明远，外交部美澳司司长申健。接见时在座的还有在京的智利和平人士万徒勒里和他的夫人。（附图片）
