Title: President of the Republic of Cyprus Cables Premier Zhou, Thanking China for Congratulating Cypriot Independence
Author:
Date: 1960-08-21
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, August 20th. Archbishop Makarios III, President of the Republic of Cyprus, cabled Premier Zhou Enlai of the People's Republic of China on the 19th, thanking the Chinese government and people for their congratulatory message on the independence of Cyprus. The full text of the cable is as follows:

His Excellency Zhou Enlai, Premier of the State Council of the People's Republic of China, Beijing:

I am deeply moved by the kind congratulatory message sent on the occasion of the declaration of independence of the Republic of Cyprus. I am pleased to express, on behalf of the government and people of Cyprus, and on my own behalf, my heartfelt thanks to Your Excellency and, through you, to the government and people of the People's Republic of China for your sincere wishes. We are pleased to reciprocate the same wishes.

Archbishop Makarios III, President of the Republic of Cyprus
Nicosia, August 19, 1960

### 塞浦路斯共和国总统致电周总理  感谢我祝贺塞浦路斯独立

1960-08-21
第1版()
专栏：

    塞浦路斯共和国总统致电周总理
    感谢我祝贺塞浦路斯独立
    新华社20日讯　塞浦路斯共和国总统大主教马卡里奥斯三世19日致电中华人民共和国总理周恩来，对中国政府和人民祝贺塞浦路斯独立的贺电表示感谢。电报全文如下：北京中华人民共和国国务院总理周恩来阁下：
    在塞浦路斯共和国宣布独立的时候，承蒙发来亲切的贺电，使我深受感动。我愉快地以塞浦路斯政府和人民以及我个人的名义，向阁下，并通过你向中华人民共和国政府和人民衷心感谢你们的热诚的祝愿。我们高兴地回报同样的祝愿。
      塞浦路斯共和国总统大主教马卡里奥斯三世
                      1960年8月19日于尼科西亚
