Title: New Hungarian Ambassador to China Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1960-08-08
Page: 6

    Xinhua News Agency, 7th August: Ferenc Martin, the newly appointed Ambassador of the Hungarian People's Republic to China, arrived in Beijing by plane today.
    Yu Peiwen, Director of the Protocol Department of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs, and Yu Zhan, Deputy Director of the Department of Soviet and Eastern European Affairs, were at the airport to welcome him. Szigeti, Chargé d'affaires ad interim of the Hungarian Embassy in China, and embassy staff were also at the airport to welcome him.

### 匈牙利新任驻华大使抵京

1960-08-08
第6版()
专栏：

    匈牙利新任驻华大使抵京
    新华社7日讯　匈牙利人民共和国新任驻华大使马尔丁·费伦茨今天乘飞机到达北京。
    到机场欢迎的，有外交部礼宾司司长俞沛文，苏联东欧司副司长余湛。匈牙利驻华大使馆临时代办西格蒂和使馆人员也到机场欢迎。
