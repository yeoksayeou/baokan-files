Title: Premier Zhou Watches Performance by Burmese Artists
Author: 
Date: 1960-08-13
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, 12th August. Premier Zhou Enlai watched a wonderful performance by the artists of the Burmese Federal Cultural Friendship Art Troupe at the Capital Theatre tonight. He also met with the head of the Art Troupe, U Ba Swe, and the main actors, and had a cordial conversation with them.

The Burmese artists gave their last performance in Beijing tonight, entertaining officers and soldiers of the People's Liberation Army stationed in Beijing. After the performance, literary and art workers from the troops presented flowers to the Burmese artists. Premier Zhou Enlai walked onto the stage amidst thunderous applause to congratulate the artists. At this moment, the Burmese artists waved bouquets and repeatedly shouted in Chinese: "Long live Sino-Burmese friendship!"

When Premier Zhou Enlai met the Burmese artists, the Burmese Ambassador to China, Kyaw Winn, was present. The Ambassador accompanied Premier Zhou Enlai in watching the performance.

Also watching the performance tonight were Vice-Director of the Commission for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries, Chu Tunan; Vice-Minister of Culture, Xu Guangxiao; Deputy Chief of the General Staff of the Chinese People's Liberation Army, General Zhang Aiping; Deputy Director of the General Political Department, General Gan Siqi; Director of the General Office of the Ministry of National Defense, Lieutenant General Xiao Xiangrong; as well as Lieutenant General Xu Shenji of the Air Force, Rear Admiral Duan Dezhang of the Navy, Major General Zhang Zhengguang, and others. Military attachés from various countries' embassies in China also watched the performance. (Attached picture)

August 12, Premier Zhou Enlai watched the performance of the Burmese Federal Cultural Friendship Art Troupe. After the performance, Premier Zhou Enlai cordially shook hands with the actors. (Photo by Gu Songnian, Xinhua News Agency)

### 周总理观看缅艺术家演出

1960-08-13
第6版()
专栏：

    周总理观看缅艺术家演出
    新华社12日讯　周恩来总理今晚在首都剧场观看了缅甸联邦文化友好艺术团艺术家们的精采演出，并接见了艺术团团长吴巴盛和主要演员，和他们进行了亲切的谈话。
    缅甸艺术家们今晚举行在北京的最后一场演出，招待中国人民解放军驻北京部队官兵。在演出全部结束后，部队文艺工作者向缅甸艺术家们献了鲜花。周恩来总理在雷动的掌声中走上舞台向艺术家们致贺。这时，缅甸艺术家们挥动花束，一再用中国话高呼：“中缅友好万岁”。
    周恩来总理接见缅甸艺术家们的时候，缅甸驻中国大使叫温在座。大使陪同周恩来总理观看了演出。
    观看今晚演出的，还有对外文委副主任楚图南，文化部副部长徐光霄，中国人民解放军副总参谋长张爱萍上将，总政治部副主任甘泗淇上将，国防部办公厅主任萧向荣中将以及徐深吉空军中将，段德彰海军少将，张正光少将等。各国驻中国大使馆武官也观看了演出。（附图片）
    8月12日，周恩来总理观看了缅甸联邦文化友好艺术团的演出。演出结束后，周恩来总理和演员亲切握手　　　新华社记者　顾松年摄
