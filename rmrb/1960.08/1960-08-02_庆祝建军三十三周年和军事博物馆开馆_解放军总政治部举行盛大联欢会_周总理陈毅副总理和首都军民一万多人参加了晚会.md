Title: Grand Celebration Held by the General Political Department of the PLA to Celebrate the 33rd Anniversary of the Founding of the Army and the Opening of the Military Museum; Premier Zhou, Vice Premier Chen Yi, and Over 10,000 Soldiers and Civilians from the Capital Participate in the Evening Gathering
Author:
Date: 1960-08-02
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency, August 1st. The General Political Department of the Chinese People's Liberation Army held a grand evening gathering at the Chinese People's Revolutionary Military Museum tonight to celebrate the 33rd anniversary of the founding of the Chinese People's Liberation Army and the grand opening of the Chinese People's Revolutionary Military Museum.

Zhou Enlai, Vice Chairman of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of China and Premier of the State Council, and Chen Yi, member of the Political Bureau of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of China and Vice Premier, attended tonight's evening gathering.

Also attending the evening gathering were Senior General Luo Ruiqing, Senior General Wang Shusheng, Senior General Xu Guangda, General Xiao Hua, General Yang Chengwu, General Zhang Aiping, General Peng Shaohui, General Li Da, General Wang Zhen, General Li Juikui, Air Force Senior General Liu Yalou, Navy Senior General Su Zhenhua, General Chen Shiju, and General Huang Yongsheng, among other senior generals.

More than 10,000 people, including prominent figures from the capital, representatives of families of revolutionary martyrs and soldiers, people from all walks of life, and officers and soldiers, participated in the evening gathering.

Tonight, the Chinese People's Revolutionary Military Museum was brightly lit, and all the exhibition halls were open. Various cultural programs, such as Peking opera, song and dance, acrobatics, folk art, and film, were performed simultaneously on the platforms and in the lounges of each building. (Photo attached)

The Chinese Revolutionary Military Museum displays a charger. Old Red Army soldier Zhong Qihan is telling the Young Pioneers about its story. (Photo by Xinhua News Agency reporter Li Jilu)

The Chinese Revolutionary Military Museum officially opened. Qiu Huiying, the wife of martyr Li Bai, is introducing visitors to the deeds of martyr Li Bai during his lifetime, based on the martyr's relics displayed in the museum. (Photo by Xinhua News Agency reporter Li Jilu)

### 庆祝建军三十三周年和军事博物馆开馆  解放军总政治部举行盛大联欢会  周总理陈毅副总理和首都军民一万多人参加了晚会

1960-08-02
第4版()
专栏：

    庆祝建军三十三周年和军事博物馆开馆
    解放军总政治部举行盛大联欢会
    周总理陈毅副总理和首都军民一万多人参加了晚会
    新华社1日讯　中国人民解放军总政治部今晚在中国人民革命军事博物馆举行盛大联欢晚会，庆祝中国人民解放军建军三十三周年和中国人民革命军事博物馆的隆重开馆。
    中共中央副主席、国务院总理周恩来，中共中央政治局委员、副总理陈毅元帅参加了今晚的联欢晚会。
    参加联欢晚会的还有罗瑞卿大将、王树声大将、许光达大将、萧华上将、杨成武上将、张爱萍上将、彭绍辉上将、李达上将、王震上将、李聚奎上将、刘亚楼空军上将、苏振华海军上将、陈士榘上将、黄永胜上将等高级将领。
    首都著名人士和烈军属代表以及各界人民和部队官兵共一万多人参加了晚会。
    今晚，中国人民革命军事博物馆灯火辉煌，各馆陈列大厅一起开放。各楼平台和休息厅里，京剧、歌舞、技艺、曲艺、电影等各种文艺节目同时上演。（附图片）
    中国革命军事博物馆陈列着一台充电机。老红军战士钟其汉正在给少先队员们讲关于它的故事。　新华社记者　李基禄摄
    中国革命军事博物馆正式开馆，李白烈士的爱人裘会英，就博物馆陈列的烈土遗物，向参观者介绍李白烈士生前的事迹。　新华社记者　李基禄摄
