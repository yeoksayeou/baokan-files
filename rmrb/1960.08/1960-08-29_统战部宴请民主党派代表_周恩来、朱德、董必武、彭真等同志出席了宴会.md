Title: United Front Work Department Hosts Banquet for Representatives of Democratic Parties; Comrades Zhou Enlai, Zhu De, Dong Biwu, Peng Zhen, and Others Attend the Banquet
Author:
Date: 1960-08-29
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, August 28th: The United Front Work Department of the CPC Central Committee held a banquet in the banquet hall of the Great Hall of the People tonight, hosting representatives attending the enlarged plenary sessions of the central committees of six democratic parties, namely the Revolutionary Committee of the Chinese Kuomintang, the China Democratic League, the China Association for Promoting Democracy, the Chinese Peasants' and Workers' Democratic Party, the China Zhi Gong Party, and the Jiu San Society, as well as other responsible persons of democratic parties and non-party democratic figures.

Comrades Zhou Enlai and Zhu De, Vice Chairmen of the CPC Central Committee, and Comrades Dong Biwu and Peng Zhen, members of the Political Bureau of the CPC Central Committee, attended the banquet tonight.

Comrade Zhou Enlai, Vice Chairman of the CPC Central Committee, and Xu Bing, Deputy Minister of the United Front Work Department of the CPC Central Committee, proposed toasts at the banquet. Cheng Qian, Vice Chairman of the Revolutionary Committee of the Chinese Kuomintang, also proposed a toast at the banquet on behalf of the democratic parties.

The banquet proceeded in a very cordial and warm atmosphere.

Present at the banquet were chairmen, vice chairmen, and central committee members of various democratic parties, as well as responsible persons of local organizations of the democratic parties. (Attached picture)

Comrade Zhou Enlai, Vice Chairman of the CPC Central Committee, proposing a toast at the banquet. (Xinhua News Agency reporter Zhang Bin)

### 统战部宴请民主党派代表  周恩来、朱德、董必武、彭真等同志出席了宴会

1960-08-29
第1版()
专栏：

    统战部宴请民主党派代表
    周恩来、朱德、董必武、彭真等同志出席了宴会
    新华社28日讯　中共中央统一战线工作部今晚在人民大会堂宴会厅举行宴会，招待出席中国国民党革命委员会、中国民主同盟、中国民主促进会、中国农工民主党、中国致公党、九三学社等六个民主党派中央全会扩大会议的代表和其他民主党派负责人以及无党派民主人士。
    中共中央副主席周恩来、朱德，中共中央政治局委员董必武、彭真出席了今晚的宴会。
    中共中央副主席周恩来，中共中央统一战线工作部副部长徐冰在宴会上祝了酒，中国国民党革命委员会副主席程潜代表各民主党派也在宴会上祝了酒。
    宴会在十分亲切热烈的气氛中进行。
    出席宴会的有各民主党派主席、副主席、中央委员，以及各民主党派地方组织的负责人等。（附图片）
