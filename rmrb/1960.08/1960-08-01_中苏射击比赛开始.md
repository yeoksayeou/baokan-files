Title: China-Soviet Shooting Competition Begins
Author: 
Date: 1960-08-01
Page: 6

　　Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, July 30th. The Chinese People's National Defense Sports Association shooting team held a shooting competition with the Soviet Union's Voluntary Association for Assisting the Army, Navy, and Air Force shooting team in Moscow on the 30th. In the women's 50-meter small-bore rifle 60-shot prone shooting competition held today, the Chinese team won first place in the team event with a score of 1732 rings. The Soviet team was second (1729 rings).

　　Cao Jingfen of the Chinese team won first place in the individual event with a score of 584 rings, Leng Guiying was second with a score of 582 rings, and Kirillova of the Soviet team was third with a score of 581 rings.

　　The competition started on the 30th and will continue until August 5th. There will be a rest day on the 31st. In addition to the competition held today, six more events will be held.

### 中苏射击比赛开始

1960-08-01
第6版()
专栏：

    中苏射击比赛开始
    新华社莫斯科30日电　中国人民国防体育协会射击队30日在莫斯科同苏联支援陆海空军志愿协会射击队举行射击比赛。在今天举行的女子小口径步枪五十米六十发卧射比赛中，中国队获得这一项目的团体第一名，成绩是一七三二环。苏联队第二名（一七二九环）。
    中国队曹靖芬获得这一项的个人第一名，成绩是五八四环，第二名是冷桂英，成绩五八二环，苏联队的基里洛娃第三名，成绩五八一环。
    比赛从30日开始，一直进行到8月5日，31日休息一天，除今天举行的一项比赛外，还将举行六个项目的比赛。
