Title: Two Members of the Political Bureau of the Congolese African Solidarity Party Arrive in Beijing
Author: 
Date: 1960-08-08
Page: 6

    Two Members of the Political Bureau of the Congolese African Solidarity Party Arrive in Beijing
    Xinhua News Agency, July 7th. At the invitation of the All-China Youth Federation, Momanki Dounasian Felix and Ongezagasa Serestine, two members of the Political Bureau of the Congolese African Solidarity Party, arrived in Beijing by plane on the afternoon of the 6th for a visit to our country.
    Wang Chuanbin, Deputy Secretary-General of the All-China Youth Federation, and others were at the airport to welcome them.

### 刚果非洲团结党政治局委员二人到京

1960-08-08
第6版()
专栏：

    刚果非洲团结党政治局委员二人到京
    新华社7日讯　应中华全国青年联合会邀请，前来我国访问的刚果非洲团结党政治局委员莫芒基·杜纳西安·菲力斯和昂盖萨加·塞勒斯坦二人，于6日下午乘飞机到达北京。
    到机场欢迎的，有中华全国青年联合会副秘书长王传斌等人。
