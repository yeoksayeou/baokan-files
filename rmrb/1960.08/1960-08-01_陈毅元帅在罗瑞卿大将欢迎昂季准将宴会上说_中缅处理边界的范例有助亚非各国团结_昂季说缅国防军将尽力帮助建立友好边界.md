Title: Marshal Chen Yi Says at the Banquet Hosted by General Luo Ruiqing in Honor of Brigadier General Aung Gyi: The Example of China and Myanmar Handling the Border Issue is Conducive to the Unity of Asian and African Countries; Aung Gyi Says the Myanmar Defense Forces Will Do Their Best to Help Establish a Friendly Border
Author:
Date: 1960-08-01
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, 31st - General Luo Ruiqing, Chief of the General Staff of the Chinese People's Liberation Army, held a banquet at noon today to warmly welcome Brigadier General Aung Gyi, Deputy Chief of Staff of the Myanmar Defense Forces, and the Myanmar delegation of the China-Myanmar Joint Boundary Committee led by him.

Marshal Chen Yi, Vice Premier of the State Council, attended the banquet and spoke at the banquet.

General Luo Ruiqing spoke first at the banquet, extending a warm welcome to Brigadier General Aung Gyi and other distinguished guests. He said that the negotiations between China and Myanmar on the boundary issue went very smoothly and the issue was resolved fairly and reasonably. We are very happy and satisfied. He proposed a toast to the friendship between the people of China and Myanmar, to the friendship between the two armies, and to the victory of creating a friendly border between China and Myanmar!

Brigadier General Aung Gyi then spoke, warmly praising the friendship between the people and armies of Myanmar and China. He said that the Chinese People's Liberation Army and the Myanmar Defense Forces have consistently maintained friendly and close relations based on the wishes of the two governments and peoples. He said that in order to establish a peaceful and friendly border based on the Five Principles of Peaceful Coexistence, the Myanmar Defense Forces will do everything possible to help. At the same time, we are pleased to see that the Chinese People's Liberation Army has also done everything possible for this. This active cooperative relationship between the two armies is a glorious page in the history of our army.

Marshal Chen Yi, Vice Premier of the State Council, spoke at the banquet. After praising the glorious history of the Myanmar Defense Forces in the anti-imperialist struggle, he said that now that China and Myanmar have a peaceful and friendly border, setting a good example, it will help the unity and understanding of Southeast Asian countries, Asian and African countries, and help us oppose imperialist aggression. Vice Premier Chen Yi said that the Myanmar delegation headed by Brigadier General Aung Gyi has made a good contribution to enhancing the friendship between the people of China and Myanmar. He proposed a toast to the health of General Ne Win and General Aung Gyi of Myanmar.

Myanmar delegation representatives: Myanmar Ambassador to China U Hla Mawng, U San Lun, Secretary of Kachin State Affairs, Colonel So Min, Director of the Border Region Administration, U Khun Ohn Kya, Commissioner of North Shan State, and the delegation's advisors and staff, as well as Colonel Kyin Shwe Mon, Myanmar Military Attaché to China, were all invited to attend the banquet.

Those present at the banquet included Geng Biao, Vice Minister of Foreign Affairs, General Zhang Aiping of the Chinese People's Liberation Army, General Gan Siqi, Lieutenant General Xiao Xiangrong, Lieutenant General Tan JiaShu of the Air Force, Vice Admiral Zhao Qimin of the Navy, Major General Wang Xinggang, Li Yimang, Chinese Ambassador to Myanmar, Yao Zhongming, Chief Representative of the Chinese side of the China-Myanmar Joint Boundary Committee, and representatives and advisors.

Before the banquet, General Luo Ruiqing met with Brigadier General Aung Gyi and other distinguished Myanmar guests and had a friendly conversation.

### 陈毅元帅在罗瑞卿大将欢迎昂季准将宴会上说  中缅处理边界的范例有助亚非各国团结  昂季说缅国防军将尽力帮助建立友好边界

1960-08-01
第6版()
专栏：

    陈毅元帅在罗瑞卿大将欢迎昂季准将宴会上说
    中缅处理边界的范例有助亚非各国团结
    昂季说缅国防军将尽力帮助建立友好边界
    新华社31日讯　中国人民解放军总参谋长罗瑞卿大将今天中午举行宴会，热烈欢迎缅甸国防军副总参谋长昂季准将和由他率领的中缅边界联合委员会的缅甸代表团。
    国务院副总理陈毅元帅出席了宴会，并在宴会上讲了话。
    罗瑞卿大将在宴会上首先讲话，他对昂季准将等贵宾表示热烈欢迎。他说，这次中缅双方关于边界问题的谈判，谈得很顺利，公平合理地解决了问题，我们感到很高兴，很满意。他提议，为中缅两国人民之间的友谊和两国军队之间的友谊，为中缅双方创造了一条友好边界的胜利，干杯！
    昂季准将接着讲话，他热情称颂缅中两国人民和两国军队的友谊。他说，中国人民解放军和缅甸国防军，根据两国政府和人民的愿望，一贯进行了友好亲密的来往。他说，为在和平共处五项原则的基础上，建立一条和平友好的边界，缅甸国防军将尽一切力量给予帮助，同时我们愉快地看到中国人民解放军也为此尽了一切力量。两国军队这种积极合作的关系，是我们军队历史上的光荣一页。
    国务院副总理陈毅元帅在宴会上讲了话。他在赞扬缅甸国防军反帝斗争的光荣历史后说，现在中缅两国有了一条和平友好的边界，树立了一个良好的范例，它将有助于东南亚各国、亚非各国的团结和了解，有助于我们反对帝国主义的侵略。陈毅副总理说，以昂季准将为首的缅甸代表团为增进中缅两国人民的友谊，作出了很好的贡献。他提议为缅甸奈温将军和昂季将军的健康干杯。
    缅甸代表团代表：缅甸驻中国大使叫温，克钦邦事务部秘书吴山伦，边境地区管理局局长苏敏上校，北掸邦专员吴昆翁佳和代表团的顾问、工作人员，缅甸驻中国武官景沙门上校等，都应邀出席了宴会。
    出席宴会的，有外交部副部长耿飚，中国人民解放军张爱萍上将、甘泗淇上将、萧向荣中将、谭家述空军中将、赵启民海军中将、王兴纲少将，中国驻缅甸大使李一氓，中缅边界联合委员会中国首席代表姚仲明以及代表、顾问等。
    宴会前，罗瑞卿大将会见了昂季准将等缅甸贵宾，进行了友好的谈话。
