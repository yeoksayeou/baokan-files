Title: Excerpt from the Declaration of the Moscow Meeting of Representatives of Communist and Workers' Parties of Socialist Countries
Author:
Date: 1960-08-08
Page: 6

    In the imperialist countries, the contradiction between productive forces and relations of production is sharpening. In these countries, modern science and technology are in many respects not used to serve social progress and human welfare, because capitalism shackles the development of social productive forces and distorts this development. The world capitalist economy remains volatile and unstable. In some countries of the capitalist world, the temporary phenomenon of relatively prosperous conditions is largely based on the unstable foundation of the arms race and other temporary factors. However, the capitalist economy cannot avoid new and profound shocks and crises.	——Excerpt from the Declaration of the Moscow Meeting of Representatives of Communist and Workers' Parties of Socialist Countries

### 摘自社会主义国家共产党和工人党代表莫斯科会议宣言

1960-08-08
第6版()
专栏：

    在帝国主义各国，生产力和生产关系之间的矛盾尖锐化了。在这些国家里，现代的科学技术在许多方面都没有用来服务于社会进步和人类福利，因为资本主义束缚着社会生产力的发展并且使这种发展畸形化。世界资本主义经济仍然是动荡不稳的。在资本主义世界的一些国家里，暂时仍然保持着的比较景气的现象在很大程度上是在军备竞赛和其他暂时因素的不巩固的基础上产生的。但是，资本主义经济不能避免新的、深刻的震动和危机。	——摘自社会主义国家共产党和工人党代表莫斯科会议宣言
