Title: Vice Premier Xi Zhongxun Receives Woods Worth
Author:
Date: 1960-08-24
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, August 23rd. Vice Premier Xi Zhongxun received Woods Worth, the famous Canadian lawyer who is currently visiting China, and his wife this afternoon, and had a friendly conversation with them.

Present at the reception were Wu Defeng and Zhang Youyu, vice presidents of the China Political Science and Law Association, and director Ke Bonian.

Woods Worth and his wife arrived in Beijing by plane on the 18th at the invitation of the China Political Science and Law Association. Tonight, Wu Defeng, vice president of the China Political Science and Law Association, hosted a banquet to bid farewell to the guests.

### 习仲勋副总理接见伍兹华斯

1960-08-24
第4版()
专栏：

    习仲勋副总理接见伍兹华斯
    新华社23日讯　习仲勋副总理今天下午接见了正在我国访问的加拿大著名律师伍兹华斯和他的夫人，并进行了友好的交谈。
    接见时在座的有中国政治法律学会副会长吴德峰、张友渔，理事柯柏年。
    伍兹华斯和他的夫人是应中国政治法律学会邀请，于18日乘飞机到达北京的。今天晚上，中国政法学会副会长吴德峰设宴欢送客人。
