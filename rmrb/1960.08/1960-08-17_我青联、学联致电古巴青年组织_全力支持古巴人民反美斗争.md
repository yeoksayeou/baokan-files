Title: All-China Youth Federation and All-China Students' Federation Send Telegrams to Cuban Youth Organizations to Fully Support the Cuban People's Anti-American Struggle
Author:
Date: 1960-08-17
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, 16th August: The All-China Youth Federation and the All-China Students' Federation sent telegrams to the Cuban Socialist Youth League, the Cuban 26th of July Movement Student Department, the Cuban University Students Federation, the Cuban Revolutionary Directorate, and the Cuban Rebel Army Youth Federation, expressing full support for the Cuban people and youth in their struggle against U.S. imperialism.

The telegram stated that while U.S. imperialism is brazenly employing economic blockades and plotting armed intervention to strangle the Cuban revolution, it is now using the Washington's tool—the Organization of American States (OAS) meeting of foreign ministers to launch international intervention in Cuba. The Chinese people and youth express great indignation at this. The Cuban people and youth, in order to strive for and safeguard national independence and defend world peace, are bravely and unyieldingly carrying out the struggle against U.S. imperialism, striking a blow at U.S. imperialism's war and aggression policies, and setting a brilliant example for the people and youth of Latin America.

The telegram stated that the Chinese people and youth fully support the struggle of the Cuban people and youth against U.S. imperialism and will forever stand on the side of the Cuban and Latin American people and youth. The Chinese people and youth congratulate the Cuban people and youth on the victories they have achieved in their struggle against U.S. imperialism, and congratulate you on the victories you have achieved in the struggle of the Cuban government to nationalize all the property of twenty-six U.S. monopoly companies. We believe that the heroic Cuban people, with the firm support of the righteous people of Latin America and the world, will persist in their struggle and will surely be able to crush any aggressive conspiracy of U.S. imperialism.

### 我青联、学联致电古巴青年组织  全力支持古巴人民反美斗争

1960-08-17
第6版()
专栏：

    我青联、学联致电古巴青年组织
    全力支持古巴人民反美斗争
    新华社16日讯　中华全国青年联合会、中华全国学生联合会致电古巴社会主义青年联盟、古巴7月26日运动学生部、古巴大学生联合会、古巴革命指导委员会、古巴起义军青年联合会，表示全力支持古巴人民和青年反对美帝国主义的斗争。
    电文说，美帝国主义在悍然采用经济封锁和策划武装干涉以达到扼杀古巴革命的阴谋的同时，现在又利用华盛顿的驯服工具——美洲国家组织召开的美洲国家外长会议来发动对古巴的国际干涉，中国人民和青年对此表示极大的愤慨。古巴人民和青年为了争取和维护民族独立，保卫世界和平，英勇不屈地进行着反对美帝国主义的斗争，打击了美帝国主义的战争政策和侵略政策，为拉丁美洲人民和青年树立了光辉的榜样。
    电文说，中国人民和青年全力支持古巴人民和青年反对美帝国主义的斗争，永远站在古巴和拉丁美洲的人民和青年的一边。中国人民和青年祝贺古巴人民和青年在反对美帝国主义斗争中所取得的胜利，祝贺你们在古巴政府将二十六家美国垄断公司全部财产收归国有的斗争中所取得的胜利。我们相信英勇的古巴人民在拉丁美洲和全世界正义人民的坚决支持下，坚持斗争，一定能够粉碎美帝国主义的任何侵略阴谋。
