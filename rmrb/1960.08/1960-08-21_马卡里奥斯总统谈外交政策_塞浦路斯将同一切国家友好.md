Title: President Makarios Discusses Foreign Policy: Cyprus Will Be Friendly to All Countries
Author:
Date: 1960-08-21
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, Nicosia: Archbishop Makarios, President of the Republic of Cyprus, held his first press conference in the capital on the 17th. He stated at the meeting that his government would seek to establish friendly relations with the people of all countries, especially with the people of neighboring countries.

A reporter asked what attitude he would take if Britain used its bases in Cyprus to store nuclear weapons. Makarios replied: "Although Britain has complete sovereignty over its bases in Cyprus, if Britain attempts to use these bases in such a way as to affect the rest of the island of Cyprus, then the people of Cyprus will certainly not agree."

In response to a question about accepting foreign aid, Makarios said: "We will accept assistance from any country that does not attach conditions and does not interfere in our policies." At the same time, Makarios said that Cyprus must continue to remain within the Commonwealth. The Cyprus House of Representatives will make a decision on this issue soon.

### 马卡里奥斯总统谈外交政策  塞浦路斯将同一切国家友好

1960-08-21
第5版()
专栏：

    马卡里奥斯总统谈外交政策
    塞浦路斯将同一切国家友好
    据新华社讯　尼科西亚消息：塞浦路斯共和国总统马卡里奥斯大主教17日在首都举行首次记者招待会。他在会上发表声明说，他的政府将谋求同一切国家的人民，特别是同邻国人民，建立友好关系。
    有记者问，如果英国利用它在塞浦路斯的基地储存核武器，他将采取什么态度。马卡里奥斯回答说：“虽然英国对它在塞浦路斯的基地拥有完全的主权，但是，如果英国企图在使塞浦路斯岛其余部分受到影响的这样一种方式下使用这些基地，那么，塞浦路斯人民是一定不答应的。”
    在回答关于接受外援的问题时马卡里奥斯说：“我们将接受不附带条件和不干涉我们政策的任何国家的援助。”同时，马卡里奥斯说，塞浦路斯必须继续留在英联邦内。关于这个问题，塞浦路斯众议院不久将作出决定。
