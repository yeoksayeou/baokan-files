Title: Every Performance Touched People's Hearts: Burmese Art Troupe Performs in Shanghai
Author:
Date: 1960-08-19
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, Shanghai, August 18th: The Burmese Federal Cultural Friendship Art Troupe gave its first performance in Shanghai on the evening of the 17th. Every performance was warmly welcomed, and many performances were repeated at the enthusiastic request of nearly a thousand spectators.

　　The performance was held at the Friendship Cinema of the Sino-Soviet Friendship Building in the city center. It began with the Burmese traditional instrumental ensemble "Overture", followed by the chorus "Song of Sino-Burmese Friendship." As soon as the song ended, applause rang out. At the audience's repeated request, the song was replayed twice, and the famous Chinese song "The East is Red" was added. Fragments from the "Golden Peacock" play performed by Burmese famous dancers Wu Ge Nai Sheng, Wu Rui Man Ding Mao, etc., as well as "Bell Board Dance", "Farming Dance", and "National Unity Dance" all received thunderous applause. Shanghai Vice Mayor Jin Zhonghua and others attended and watched the performance. During the intermission, Jin Zhonghua met with the head of the art troupe, Wu Ba Sheng, and other leaders and principal actors of the art troupe.

### 每个节目都打动了人们的心弦  缅甸艺术团在上海演出

1960-08-19
第6版()
专栏：

    每个节目都打动了人们的心弦
    缅甸艺术团在上海演出
    新华社上海18日电　缅甸联邦文化友好艺术团17日晚在上海举行首次演出，每一个节目都受到热烈欢迎，许多节目在近千观众的热情要求下一再重演。
    演出是在市中心的中苏友好大厦友谊电影院举行的。以缅甸传统的器乐合奏“序曲”开始，紧接着表演的是合唱“中缅友谊之歌”。歌声一落，掌声四起。这首歌在观众再三鼓掌要求下，重演了两次，并且加演中国著名歌曲“东方红”。缅甸著名舞蹈家吴葛内盛、吴瑞曼丁貌等表演的“金孔雀”剧中的片断，以及“铃板舞”、“农作舞”、“民族团结舞”等都获得了雷动的掌声。上海市副市长金仲华等出席观看了表演。演出休息时，金仲华接见了艺术团团长吴巴盛等艺术团领导人和主要演员。
