Title: Chinese Delegation of the Sino-Burmese Joint Boundary Committee Visits; Pathein, Burma, Permeated with Sino-Burmese Friendship; Hosts and Guests at Local Reception Jointly Celebrate Achievements in Solving Boundary Issues Between the Two Countries
Author:
Date: 1960-08-31
Page: 5
Xinhua News Agency, Rangoon, August 29th: The Chinese delegation of the Sino-Burmese Joint Boundary Committee, headed by Yao Zhongming, arrived in Pathein, Burma's second largest port, on the morning of the 28th aboard the Burmese President's special yacht "Federal," for a visit. The delegation was warmly welcomed by the local people and graciously hosted by Burmese officials.

Accompanying the Chinese delegation were the Burmese Chief Representative of the Sino-Burmese Joint Boundary Committee, Deputy Chief of Staff of the Defense Forces Brigadier General Aung Gyi, as well as other Burmese representatives and advisors.

Welcoming the Chinese delegation at the Pathein docks were the Commissioner of the Irrawaddy Administrative Region, U Kharlot, Colonel Tin Aung, Commander of the 8th Brigade stationed in Pathein, and other senior military and government officials, as well as people from all walks of life in the area. The entire city was filled with an atmosphere of Sino-Burmese friendship. The Chinese delegation was greeted by tens of thousands of Pathein citizens lining the streets.

U Nyu, Chairman of the Pathein Municipal Committee, held a reception for the Chinese delegation. In his speech at the reception, U Nyu warmly praised the contributions made by the leaders of China and Burma to Sino-Burmese friendship. He pointed out that the continuous development and strengthening of the friendly relations and cultural exchanges between China and Burma since ancient times would not only further consolidate the foundation of Sino-Burmese friendship but would also be respected by peace-loving people all over the world.

In his reply, Yao Zhongming, the Chief Representative of the Chinese side of the Sino-Burmese Joint Boundary Committee, particularly emphasized the role played by the Five Principles of Peaceful Coexistence, initiated by Premier Zhou Enlai and Prime Minister U Nu, in developing the friendly relations between China and Burma. He also emphasized that "in the entire negotiation process regarding the Sino-Burmese boundary issue, our two governments demonstrated a spirit of mutual understanding and mutual accommodation, as well as a sincere desire for cooperation."

In his speech at the reception, U Kharlot, Commissioner of the Irrawaddy Administrative Region, said, "The rapid resolution of the Sino-Burmese boundary issue will become the best example for other countries. We sincerely hope that other countries will also resolve their existing problems according to the same approach we have adopted, based on the Five Principles of Peaceful Coexistence."

At noon, the Chinese and Burmese delegations of the Sino-Burmese Joint Boundary Committee attended a reception held for them by the Chinese community in Pathein.

In the afternoon, U Kharlot, Commissioner of the Irrawaddy Administrative Region, held a welcome meeting for the Chinese delegation at "Aung San Park" on the lakeshore. In the evening, Colonel Tin Aung, Commander of the Burmese 8th Brigade stationed in Pathein, held a banquet for the Chinese and Burmese delegations of the Sino-Burmese Joint Boundary Committee.

After concluding its two-day visit to Pathein, the Chinese delegation of the Sino-Burmese Joint Boundary Committee returned to Rangoon on the evening of the 29th.

### 欢迎中缅边界联合委员会中国代表团访问  缅甸勃生市洋溢中缅友好气氛  在当地的招待会上宾主同祝两国解决边界问题的成就

1960-08-31
第5版()
专栏：

    欢迎中缅边界联合委员会中国代表团访问
    缅甸勃生市洋溢中缅友好气氛
    在当地的招待会上宾主同祝两国解决边界问题的成就
    据新华社仰光29日电　以姚仲明为首的中缅边界联合委员会中国代表团在28日上午乘缅甸总统专用汽艇“联邦号”到达缅甸第二大港口勃生市访问。代表团受到当地人民热烈欢迎和缅甸官员们的盛情款待。
    陪同中国代表团前往的有中缅边界联合委员会缅甸首席代表、国防军副总参谋长昂季准将以及其他缅甸代表和顾问。
    在勃生码头上欢迎中国代表团的有伊洛瓦底行政区专员吴卡罗特、驻勃生的第八旅旅长丁昂上校和其他高级军政官员以及当地各界人士。全市洋溢着中缅友好的气氛。中国代表团受到数以万计的勃生市民的夹道欢迎。
    勃生市政委员会主席吴纽为中国代表团举行了招待会。吴纽在招待会上讲话时，热烈赞扬中缅两国领导人对中缅友好所作的贡献。他指出，中缅两国自古以来的友好关系和文化交流的不断发展和加强，不仅将进一步巩固中缅友谊的基础，而且将得到全世界爱好和平的人民的尊敬。
    中缅边界联合委员会中国首席代表姚仲明在致答词时特别强调指出周恩来总理和吴努总理倡议的和平共处五项原则对发展中缅两国的友好关系所起的作用。他还强调说，“在关于中缅边界问题的整个谈判过程中，我们两国政府表现了互谅互让的精神以及真诚合作的愿望。”
    伊洛瓦底行政区专员吴卡罗特在招待会上讲话时说，“中缅边界问题的迅速解决，将成为其它国家的最好的榜样。我们真挚地希望，其它国家也按照我们所采取的同样的方式，根据和平共处五项原则来解决它们现有的问题。”
    中午，中缅边界联合委员会的中国和缅甸代表团出席了勃生市华侨为他们举行的招待会。
    下午，伊洛瓦底行政区专员吴卡罗特在湖滨的“昂山公园”为中国代表团举行了欢迎会。晚间，驻勃生的缅甸第八旅旅长丁昂上校为中缅边界联合委员会中国和缅甸代表团举行了宴会。
    中缅边界联合委员会中国代表团在结束了对勃生市的两天访问后，已于29日晚间回到仰光。
