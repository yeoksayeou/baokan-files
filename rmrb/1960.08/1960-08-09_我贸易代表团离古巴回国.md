Title: Our Trade Delegation Leaves Cuba for Home
Author:
Date: 1960-08-09
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, Havana – The Chinese trade delegation headed by Lu Xuzhang left Havana by plane on the 7th to return home. On the 6th, Lu Xuzhang paid a visit to Mr. Chaumont, Secretary-General of the Cuban Revolutionary Steering Committee, and visited the "Combat Newspaper." Lu Xuzhang also visited the Cuba-China Friendship Association.

On the evening of the 5th, Lu Xuzhang, head of the Chinese trade delegation, held a farewell reception at the Riviera Hotel in Havana. Those present at the reception included Che Guevara, President of the National Bank of Cuba; José Naranjo, Minister of the Interior; Pedro Miret, Minister of Agriculture; José Ramón Machado, Minister of Health; Carlos Olivares and Rodriguez Lumparte, Deputy Ministers of Foreign Affairs; William Galvez Rodriguez, Inspector General of the Armed Forces; and Mora, President of the Cuban Foreign Trade Bank, and Jacinto Torras, Vice President.

Also present at the reception were Rogga, Secretary-General of the Cuban People's Socialist Party, and Escalante, Executive Secretary; Chaumont, Secretary-General of the Revolutionary Steering Committee; Mata Freide, Chairman of the Movement for the Defense of Peace and National Sovereignty of All Countries; the poet Guillen, newspaper editors, business people, and representatives of overseas Chinese.

### 我贸易代表团离古巴回国

1960-08-09
第5版()
专栏：

    我贸易代表团离古巴回国
    据新华社哈瓦那讯　以卢绪章为首的中国贸易代表团7日乘飞机离开哈瓦那回国。6日，卢绪章拜会了古巴革命指导委员会总书记肖蒙，并访问了“战斗报”。卢绪章还访问了古巴—中国友好协会。
    5日晚，中国贸易代表团团长卢绪章在哈瓦那的里维埃拉饭店举行告别招待会。出席招待会的有古巴国家银行行长格瓦拉，内政部长何塞·纳兰霍，农业部长佩德罗·米雷特，卫生部长何塞·拉蒙·马丘多，外交部副部长卡洛斯·奥利瓦雷斯和罗德里格斯·路姆帕尔特，武装部队总督察威廉·加尔维斯·罗德里格斯和古巴对外贸易银行行长莫拉和副行长哈辛托·托拉斯。
    出席招待会的还有古巴人民社会党总书记罗加和执行书记埃斯卡兰特，革命指导委员会总书记肖蒙，保卫和平和各国人民主权运动主席马塔·弗赖德，诗人纪廉，报社社长，商业界人士和华侨代表。
