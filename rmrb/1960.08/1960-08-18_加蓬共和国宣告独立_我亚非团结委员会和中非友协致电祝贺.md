Title: Republic of Gabon Declares Independence; Afro-Asian Solidarity Committee and Chinese-African People's Friendship Association Send Congratulations

Author:
Date: 1960-08-18
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, Libreville, August 17th: The Republic of Gabon, located in Equatorial Africa, declared its independence today. It is the eighth African country to be born in August of this year.

This morning, Gabonese Prime Minister Léon M'ba announced the independence of the Republic of Gabon at a ceremony held in the capital, Libreville. A salute was fired at that moment.

Xinhua News Agency, August 16th: The Chinese Afro-Asian Solidarity Committee sent a telegram to Gabonese Prime Minister Léon M'ba, congratulating the Republic of Gabon on its independence.

The telegram stated: On the occasion of the declaration of independence of the Republic of Gabon, the Chinese Afro-Asian Solidarity Committee extends its warm congratulations to the Gabonese people.

The declaration of independence of the Republic of Gabon is the result of the Gabonese people's long-term struggle against imperialism and colonialism. The Chinese people have always supported your people's just struggle against colonialism and for national independence. We sincerely wish the Gabonese people further achievements in consolidating independence, developing the national economy, and fighting against imperialism and colonialism, and we wish the friendship between the Chinese and Gabonese people to develop day by day.

Xinhua News Agency, August 16th: Liu Changsheng, President of the Chinese-African People's Friendship Association, sent a telegram to Gabonese Prime Minister Léon M'ba, extending his warm congratulations to him and the Gabonese people on the independence of the Republic of Gabon.

The telegram stated that the independence of the Republic of Gabon is the result of the Gabonese people's continuous struggle against imperialism and colonialism. The 650 million Chinese people, who have always sympathized with and supported the African people's struggle for national independence, sincerely wish the Gabonese people continuous new victories in the struggle to consolidate national independence and oppose imperialist and colonialist forces! May the friendly relations between the Chinese and Gabonese people develop day by day!

### 加蓬共和国宣告独立  我亚非团结委员会和中非友协致电祝贺

1960-08-18
第5版()
专栏：

    加蓬共和国宣告独立
    我亚非团结委员会和中非友协致电祝贺
    据新华社17日讯　利伯维尔消息：位于赤道非洲的加蓬共和国今天宣告独立。这是今年8月份以来诞生的第八个非洲国家。
    今天清晨，加蓬共和国总理莱昂·姆巴在首都利伯维尔举行的仪式上宣布加蓬共和国获得独立。这时，鸣放了礼炮。
    新华社16日讯　中国亚非团结委员会致电加蓬共和国总理莱昂·姆巴，祝贺加蓬共和国的独立。
    电文说：值此加蓬共和国宣告独立之际，中国亚非团结委员会谨向加蓬人民表示热烈的祝贺。
    加蓬共和国宣告独立，是加蓬人民长期进行反对帝国主义、殖民主义斗争的结果。中国人民一向支持贵国人民反对殖民主义、争取民族独立的正义斗争。我们衷心祝加蓬人民在巩固独立、发展民族经济及反对帝国主义和殖民主义的斗争，取得进一步的成就，并祝中加两国人民的友谊日益发展。
    新华社16日讯　中国非洲人民友好协会会长刘长胜致电加蓬共和国总理莱昂·姆巴，向他和加蓬人民热烈祝贺加蓬共和国的独立。
    电文说，加蓬共和国的独立是加蓬人民不断向帝国主义和殖民主义进行斗争的结果。一贯同情和支持非洲人民争取民族独立斗争的六亿五千万中国人民，衷心祝愿加蓬人民在巩固民族独立和反对帝国主义和殖民主义势力的斗争中不断取得新的胜利！祝中国和加蓬人民的友好关系日益发展！
