Title: Mongolia Decides to Hold Mongolian-Chinese Friendship Fortnight; Mongolian-Vietnamese Friendship Association Established in Ulaanbaatar
Author:
Date: 1960-08-31
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, Ulaanbaatar, August 30th: According to a report in today's "Truth" newspaper of Mongolia, the Mongolian-Chinese Friendship Association held a presidium meeting and decided to hold a Mongolian-Chinese Friendship Fortnight from September 20th to October 1st. The meeting also approved the activity plan for the Friendship Fortnight.

Xinhua News Agency, Ulaanbaatar, August 30th: The Mongolian Peace and Friendship Organization held a gathering in Ulaanbaatar on the 30th and decided to establish the Mongolian-Vietnamese Friendship Association. The Deputy Minister of Agriculture and Animal Husbandry of Mongolia, B. Badamdash, was elected as the chairman of the association.

B. Badamdash and Nguyen Hoa, the Chargé d'Affaires ad interim of the Embassy of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam in Mongolia, who was invited to the meeting, spoke at the meeting.

In his speech, B. Badamdash pointed out that the Mongolian people regard the achievements of the fraternal Vietnamese people's construction over the past fifteen years as their own achievements and wish them greater achievements. He also said that the cooperation between the Mongolian and Vietnamese people in the economic and cultural fields is constantly developing.

In his speech, Nguyen Hoa expressed his gratitude for the establishment of the Mongolian-Vietnamese Friendship Association in Mongolia on the eve of the 15th anniversary of the founding of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam. He said that this is a sign of the consolidation of the fraternal friendship between the Vietnamese and Mongolian people.

### 蒙古决定举行蒙中友好旬  蒙越友好协会在乌兰巴托成立

1960-08-31
第5版()
专栏：

    蒙古决定举行蒙中友好旬
    蒙越友好协会在乌兰巴托成立
    新华社乌兰巴托30日电　据蒙古“真理报”今天报道，蒙中友好协会召开主席团会议，决定从9月20日到10月1日举行蒙中友好旬。会议并通过了友好旬的活动计划。
    新华社乌兰巴托30日电　蒙古和平和友好组织30日在乌兰巴托举行集会，决定成立蒙越友好协会。蒙古农牧业部副部长巴登达西当选为协会主席。
    巴登达西和应邀到会的越南民主共和国驻蒙古大使馆临时代办阮和先后在会上讲了话。
    巴登达西在讲话中指出，蒙古人民把兄弟的越南人民十五年来的建设成就看作是自己的成就，并祝他们取得更大的成就。他还说，蒙越两国人民在经济、文化方面的合作在不断发展。
    阮和在讲话中对蒙古在越南民主共和国建国十五周年前夕成立蒙越友好协会表示感谢。他说，这是越蒙两国人民兄弟友谊巩固的标志。
