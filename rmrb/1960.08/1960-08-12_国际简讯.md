International Briefs

    Ceylon's First Sugar Factory Starts Production
    Colombo dispatch: Ceylon's Minister of Agriculture, Land, Irrigation and Power, Mr. de Silva, announced the commencement of operations at Ceylon's first sugar factory in Gal Oya on the 8th.
    The sugar factory is expected to produce 2,500 tons of sugar this year. It is expected that by 1963 it will produce 30,000 tons annually, accounting for one-sixth of Ceylon's total sugar consumption.

    West German Prices Rise
    Bonn dispatch: According to a report just released by the Federal Bank, the West German cost of living index increased by 2.6 points in June this year compared to the same period last year, and the food price index rose by 3.6 points.

### 国际简讯

专栏：国际简讯

    国际简讯
    锡兰第一个糖厂开工
    科伦坡讯　锡兰农业、土地、灌溉和电力部长德席尔瓦8日在加娄亚宣布锡兰第一个糖厂开工。
    这个糖厂预计今年将生产二千五百吨糖。预料到1963年时它每年将生产三万吨，即占锡兰食糖总消费量的六分之一。
    西德物价上涨
    波恩讯　据联邦银行刚刚发表的报告，西德生活费用指数今年6月份比去年同期增加了二·六点，食品价格指数上升了三·六点。
