Title: Mongolian Art Troupe's First Performance in Beijing; Vice Premier Li Xiannian Watches Performance and Talks Cordially with Leaders of Art Troupe
Author:
Date: 1960-08-14
Page: 6
Xinhua News Agency, 13th. The Kobdo Provincial Music and Drama Theatre Art Troupe of the Mongolian People's Republic gave its premiere performance at the Nationalities Palace Auditorium tonight.

Vice Premier of the State Council Li Xiannian watched the art troupe's wonderful performance, and during the intermission, he met with the head of the troupe, Guurlagcha, and other leaders and principal actors of the art troupe, and had a cordial conversation.

Toiv, Chargé d'Affaires ad interim of the Mongolian Embassy in China, also watched tonight's performance.

Also watching the performance were Zhang Zhixiang, Deputy Director of the Commission for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries and President of the Sino-Mongolian Friendship Association, Qi Yanming, Vice Minister of Culture, Kong Yuan, Deputy Director of the Foreign Affairs Office of the State Council, Ji Pengfei, Vice Minister of Foreign Affairs, and people from the cultural and artistic circles of the capital, as well as heads of diplomatic missions and diplomats from various countries stationed in China.

After the performance ended, Li Xiannian, Zhang Zhixiang, Qi Yanming, Kong Yuan, Ji Pengfei, and others went on stage to congratulate the Mongolian artists on their successful performance.

Vice Premier Li Xiannian posed for a photo with the actors after watching the performance of the Mongolian Art Troupe. Photo by Zheng Xiaozhen, Xinhua News Agency

### 蒙古艺术团在京首次演出  李先念副总理观看演出并和艺术团领导人亲切谈话

1960-08-14
第6版()
专栏：

    蒙古艺术团在京首次演出
    李先念副总理观看演出并和艺术团领导人亲切谈话
    新华社13日讯　蒙古人民共和国科布多省音乐话剧院艺术团，今晚在民族宫礼堂举行首次公演。
    国务院副总理李先念观看了艺术团精彩的演出，并且在演出休息期间接见了古尔拉格查团长等领导人和艺术团的主要演员，进行了亲切的谈话。
    蒙古驻中国大使馆临时代办托依夫等也观看了今晚的演出。
    观看演出的还有对外文化联络委员会副主任、中蒙友好协会会长张致祥，文化部副部长齐燕铭，国务院外事办公室副主任孔原，外交部副部长姬鹏飞和首都文艺界人士，以及各国驻中国使节和外交官。
  演出全部结束后，李先念、张致祥、齐燕铭、孔原、姬鹏飞等走上舞台，祝贺蒙古艺术家们成功的演出。
    李先念副总理观看了蒙古艺术团的演出后和演员们合影　新华社记者　郑小箴摄
