Title: U.S. Military Ships Invade Guangdong and Fujian Territorial Waters; I Issue the 119th Serious Warning
Author: 
Date: 1960-08-22
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, 21st - At 5:30 a.m. to 8:30 a.m. on August 21st, one U.S. military ship intruded into the sea area south of Pinghai Peninsula, Guangdong Province. On the same day, from 7:33 a.m. to 11:01 a.m., another U.S. military ship intruded into the sea area southeast of Matsu, Fujian Province. Regarding this military provocation by U.S. military ships, a spokesperson for our Ministry of Foreign Affairs was instructed to issue the 119th serious warning.

### 美军事舰侵入我广东福建领海  我提出第119次严重警告

1960-08-22
第1版()
专栏：

    美军事舰侵入我广东福建领海
    我提出第119次严重警告
    新华社21日讯　8月21日五时三十分至八时三十分，美国军舰一艘。侵入我广东省平海半岛以南海域。同日七时三十三分至十一时零一分，又有美国军舰一艘，侵入我福建省马祖东南海域。对于美国军舰的这种军事挑衅，我外交部发言人奉命提出一百一十九次严重警告。
