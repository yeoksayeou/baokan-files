Title: Liu Ningyi, Burhan Host Banquet for Australian, West German, and Japanese Peace Activists
Author:
Date: 1960-08-30
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, August 29th. Liu Ningyi, Chairman of the All-China Federation of Trade Unions and Vice-Chairman of the Chinese People's Committee for World Peace, and Burhan, Vice-Chairman of the Chinese People's Committee for World Peace, held a banquet this evening to warmly welcome peace activists from Australia, West Germany, and Japan who are currently visiting China.

The Australian delegation, led by Geoffrey Ronald Anderson, Joint Secretary of the New South Wales Peace Committee of Australia; West German peace activists Walter Diehl, member of the World Peace Council, and Kurt Bevilssdorf, Executive Member of the West German Committee Against Nuclear Armament; and Masao Kitazawa, Japanese Secretary of the Secretariat of the Asian-African Solidarity Council, who arrived in Beijing yesterday, attended the banquet.

Among those present at the banquet were Zhu Xuefan, Zhang Su, Xu Guangxiao, Wu Xinyu, Qu Tangliang, Zhao Puchu, Zhao Anbo, Wu Maosun, Wang Mingyuan, Chen Yu, Zheng Senyu, Zhu Ziqi, Ma Shaobo, Zhao Fusan, Huang Ganying, and Zheng Xingli.

Peace activists from New Zealand, Japan, Chile, and Sudan in Beijing: Rewi Alley, Kinkazu Saionji, Mr. and Mrs. Venturelli, and Mr. and Mrs. Kyle were also invited to attend the banquet.

### 刘宁一、包尔汉  宴请澳大利亚、西德和日本和平人士

1960-08-30
第4版()
专栏：

    刘宁一、包尔汉
    宴请澳大利亚、西德和日本和平人士
    新华社29日讯　中华全国总工会主席、中国人民保卫世界和平委员会副主席刘宁一，中国人民保卫世界和平委员会副主席包尔汉，今天晚上举行宴会，热烈欢迎正在我国访问的澳大利亚、西德和日本和平人士。
    以澳大利亚新南威尔士州和平委员会联合书记乔弗雷·罗纳德·安德逊为首的澳大利亚代表团；西德和平人士：世界和平理事会理事华尔特·迪尔，和西德反对原子武装行动委员会执行委员克尔脱·贝维尔斯道夫；以及昨天到达北京的日本和平人士、亚非团结理事会书记处日本书记北泽正雄出席了宴会。
    参加宴会作陪的有各有关方面人士：朱学范、张苏、徐光霄、武新宇、区棠亮、赵朴初、赵安博、吴茂荪、王明远、陈宇、郑森禹、朱子奇、马少波、赵复三、黄甘英、郑兴丽等。
    在北京的新西兰、日本、智利、苏丹和平人士：艾黎、西园寺公一、万徒勒里夫妇、凯尔夫妇也应邀出席了宴会。
