Title: Guests from Uruguay and Ecuador Arrive in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1960-08-15
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, August 13th: Rita Díaz, President of the Montevideo Teachers' Union of Uruguay, and Ciro Maldonado, President of the National Federation of Educational Workers' Unions of Ecuador, arrived in Beijing by plane today.

They are visiting China at the invitation of the National Committee of the Educational Workers' Union of China after attending the Third World Teachers' Conference.

Among those who welcomed them at the airport were Sheng Jin, a member of the Presidium of the National Committee of the Educational Workers' Union of China, and others.

### 乌拉圭和厄瓜多尔两位客人到京

1960-08-15
第5版()
专栏：

    乌拉圭和厄瓜多尔两位客人到京
    新华社13日讯　乌拉圭蒙得维的亚教师联盟主席丽达·迪亚斯及厄瓜多尔全国教育工会工作者联盟主席西罗·马尔东纳多二人于今日乘飞机抵达北京。
    他们是出席第三届世界教师代表大会后应中国教育工会全国委员会邀请来我国访问的。
    到机场欢迎的，有中国教育工会全国委员会主席团委员盛瑾等。
