Title: Geologists' Exploration Shows Bulgaria Has Rich Mineral Deposits
Author:
Date: 1960-08-04
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, 2nd dispatch: According to the Bulgarian Telegraph Agency, Bulgarian geologists have determined that Bulgaria is rich in mineral deposits. It ranks first among the Balkan countries in terms of iron ore reserves, and it has also become one of the richest countries in Europe in terms of zinc-lead mineral deposits. Bulgaria's coal reserves are approximately 4.34 billion tons, iron ore approximately 300 million tons, and lead-zinc approximately 112.2 million tons.

### 地质学家的勘查表明  保加利亚矿藏丰富

1960-08-04
第6版()
专栏：

    地质学家的勘查表明
    保加利亚矿藏丰富
    据新华社2日讯　据保加利亚通讯社报道：保加利亚的地质学家查明，保加利亚蕴藏着丰富的矿藏。在铁矿蕴藏方面它居巴尔干各国的第一位，在锌—铅矿藏方面它也成为欧洲最丰富的国家之一。保加利亚煤的蕴藏量大约为四十三亿四千万吨、铁矿大约为三亿吨、铅锌大约为一亿一千二百万吨。
