Title: The National Revolutionary Committee of the Chinese Kuomintang Central Committee Holds Plenary Session; He Xiangning Elected Chairman of the National Revolutionary Committee of the Chinese Kuomintang Central Committee
Author:
Date: 1960-08-16
Page: 2
Xinhua News Agency, 15th: The Second Plenary (Enlarged) Session of the Fourth Central Committee of the Revolutionary Committee of the Chinese Kuomintang opened this morning. The meeting pointed out that this session will discuss the domestic and international situation, exchange and summarize experiences, determine the guiding principles and tasks for future work, and enhance the ideological awareness of all members.
    At today's Central Committee plenary session, He Xiangning was unanimously elected Chairman of the Central Committee of the National Revolutionary Committee of the Chinese Kuomintang.
    At the beginning of the meeting, Cheng Qian, Vice Chairman of the National Revolutionary Committee, delivered the opening speech. He said that this meeting was convened under the kind concern of the Chinese Communist Party. Cheng Qian emphasized that before the opening of the meeting, the report by Zhou Enlai, Vice Chairman of the CPC Central Committee, on the domestic and international situation, and the report by Li Weihan, Minister of the United Front Work Department of the CPC Central Committee, on "Studying Chairman Mao's Works and Gradually Remolding the World Outlook," deeply educated and greatly encouraged the committee members and representatives attending the meeting. Cheng Qian said that the current situation is excellent, and we must, together with the broad masses of working people across the country, actively contribute to the socialist cause. Cheng Qian emphatically pointed out that members of the National Revolutionary Committee should continuously improve their consciousness of accepting socialist transformation, actively participate in labor practice and work practice, earnestly study Chairman Mao's works, gradually remold their world outlook, keep up with the pace of the working people, and bravely advance on the bright road of socialism.
    After the election of the chairman at the meeting, He Xiangning gave a speech. She pointed out that the comrades of the National Revolutionary Committee inherited and carried forward Dr. Sun Yat-sen's revolutionary spirit, accepted the leadership of the Chinese Communist Party, and participated in the people's democratic revolution. Now, the motherland's construction cause is developing rapidly. She emphasized: "Of all the things, the leadership of the Communist Party is the first. Our comrades of the National Revolutionary Committee must listen to Chairman Mao's words, follow the Communist Party, and take the road of socialism."
    He Xiangning said that the report on the current situation by Vice Chairman Zhou Enlai of the CPC Central Committee gave us great encouragement and education. We must actively participate in socialist construction, unite with the people of the world, resolutely oppose the most ferocious enemy, U.S. imperialism, and fully support the national democratic movements of the people of Asia, Africa, and Latin America. We support socialism and communism and resolutely oppose imperialism, colonialism, fascism, and modern revisionism. He Xiangning finally expressed her hope that everyone at this meeting would speak freely, say everything they know, pool their wisdom, and work together to make this meeting a success.

### 民革中央委员会全体会议开幕  何香凝当选为民革中央委员会主席

1960-08-16
第2版()
专栏：

    民革中央委员会全体会议开幕
    何香凝当选为民革中央委员会主席
    新华社15日讯　中国国民党革命委员会第四届中央委员会第二次全体会议扩大会议，今天上午开幕。会议指出，这次会议将通过讨论国内外形势，交流和总结经验，确定今后工作的方针任务，提高全体成员的思想觉悟。
    在今天的中央全会上，一致选举了何香凝为民革中央委员会主席。
    会议开始时，民革副主席程潜致开幕词。他说，这次会议是在中国共产党的亲切关怀下召开的。程潜强调，在会议开幕之前，中共中央副主席周恩来关于国内外形势的报告，中共中央统一战线工作部部长李维汉关于“学习毛主席著作，逐步改造世界观”的报告，使到会委员和代表受到很深刻的教育，得到极大的鼓舞。程潜说，目前正是大好形势，我们必须同全国广大劳动人民一道，积极为社会主义事业贡献力量。程潜着重指出，民革成员要不断提高接受社会主义改造的自觉性，积极参加劳动实践和工作实践，认真学习毛主席著作，逐步改造世界观，跟上劳动人民的步伐，在社会主义的光明大道上奋勇前进。
    在会议选举主席之后，何香凝讲了话，她指出，民革同志继承和发扬孙中山先生的革命精神，接受了中国共产党的领导，参加了人民民主革命，现在祖国建设事业正在飞跃发展。她强调：“千条万条，共产党的领导第一条。我们民革同志一定要听毛主席的话，跟着共产党走，走社会主义的道路。”
    何香凝说，中共中央周恩来副主席关于当前形势的报告，给了我们极大的鼓舞和教育。我们要积极参加社会主义建设，要同全世界人民团结起来，坚决反对最凶恶的敌人美帝国主义，全力支持亚洲、非洲、拉丁美洲人民的民族民主运动。我们拥护社会主义、共产主义，坚决反对帝国主义、殖民主义、法西斯主义和现代修正主义。何香凝最后表示，希望大家在这次会议中，都能做到知无不言，言无不尽，集思广益，群策群力地把这次会议开好。
