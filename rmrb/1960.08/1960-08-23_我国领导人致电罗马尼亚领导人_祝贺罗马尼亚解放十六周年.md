Title: Chinese Leaders Send Congratulatory Message to Romanian Leaders on the 16th Anniversary of Romania's Liberation
Author:
Date: 1960-08-23
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, August 22nd – Chinese Party and state leaders sent a telegram on the 22nd to the Romanian Workers' Party and state leaders, congratulating them on the 16th anniversary of Romania's liberation. The full text of the telegram is as follows:

To Comrade Gheorghe Gheorghiu-Dej, First Secretary of the Central Committee of the Romanian Workers' Party, Comrade Ion Gheorghe Maurer, Chairman of the Presidium of the Grand National Assembly of the Romanian People's Republic, Comrade Chivu Stoica, Chairman of the Council of Ministers of the Romanian People's Republic:

On the occasion of the 16th anniversary of Romania's liberation, we, on behalf of the Communist Party of China, the government of the People's Republic of China, and the Chinese people, extend our fraternal congratulations to the Romanian Workers' Party, the government of the Romanian People's Republic, and the Romanian people.

Under the leadership of the Romanian Workers' Party and the government of the Romanian People's Republic, the Romanian people have achieved great successes in socialist construction. Romania's national economy is now flourishing. The Six-Year Plan for the Development of the National Economy from 1960 to 1965 and the long-term economic program adopted by the Third Congress of the Romanian Workers' Party recently have further encouraged the Romanian people's enthusiasm and confidence in the cause of socialist construction.

The Romanian people have made significant contributions to opposing the imperialist policies of aggression and war and to defending world peace.

In recent years, the fraternal friendship between the Chinese and Romanian peoples and the friendly and mutually beneficial cooperation between our two countries have developed on the basis of great proletarian internationalism, which will surely promote the common prosperity of our two countries' socialist cause and strengthen the socialist camp headed by the Soviet Union.

We wish the Romanian people new and greater achievements in the struggle to build socialism, defend world peace, and promote the progress of mankind. May the fraternal friendship between the Chinese and Romanian peoples last forever.

　　Mao Zedong, Chairman of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of China
　　Liu Shaoqi, Chairman of the People's Republic of China

　　Zhu De, Chairman of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress of the People's Republic of China
　　Zhou Enlai, Premier of the State Council of the People's Republic of China

August 22, 1960

Xinhua News Agency, August 22nd – Chinese Foreign Minister Chen Yi sent a telegram on the 22nd to Romanian Foreign Minister Corneliu Mănescu, congratulating him on the 16th anniversary of Romania's liberation. The full text of the telegram is as follows:

To the Foreign Minister of the Romanian People's Republic, Dear Comrade A. Bunaciu:

On the occasion of the 16th anniversary of Romania's liberation, I would like to extend my warm congratulations to you. I sincerely wish you new achievements in implementing the peaceful foreign policy of the Romanian People's Republic, further consolidating the unity of the socialist camp headed by the Soviet Union, and safeguarding world peace.

　　Chen Yi, Minister of Foreign Affairs of the People's Republic of China
August 22, 1960

### 我国领导人致电罗马尼亚领导人  祝贺罗马尼亚解放十六周年

1960-08-23
第1版()
专栏：

    我国领导人致电罗马尼亚领导人
    祝贺罗马尼亚解放十六周年
    新华社22日讯　中国共产党和国家领导人22日打电报给罗马尼亚工人党和国家领导人，祝贺罗马尼亚解放十六周年。电报全文如下：罗马尼亚工人党中央委员会第一书记格·乔
  治乌—德治同志，罗马尼亚人民共和国大国民议会主席团主席
  扬·格·毛雷尔同志，罗马尼亚人民共和国部长会议主席基伏·斯
  托伊卡同志：
    欣逢罗马尼亚解放十六周年，我们谨代表中国共产党、中华人民共和国政府和中国人民向罗马尼亚工人党、罗马尼亚人民共和国政府和罗马尼亚人民致以兄弟般的祝贺。
    罗马尼亚人民，在罗马尼亚工人党和罗马尼亚人民共和国政府的领导下，在社会主义建设中取得了巨大的成就。现在罗马尼亚的国民经济正在蓬勃地发展。最近召开的罗马尼亚工人党第三次代表大会所通过的1960年至1965年发展国民经济的六年计划和远景经济纲要，更加鼓舞了罗马尼亚人民对于社会主义建设事业的热忱和信心。
    罗马尼亚人民为反对帝国主义的侵略政策和战争政策，为保卫世界和平，作出了重大的贡献。
    近年来，中罗两国人民的兄弟友谊和我们两国之间的友好互助合作关系在伟大的无产阶级国际主义的基础上得到了发展。这必将促进我们两国社会主义事业的共同繁荣和增进以苏联为首的社会主义阵营的力量。
    祝罗马尼亚人民在建设社会主义、保卫世界和平和促进人类进步事业的斗争中获得新的更大的成就。祝中罗两国人民的兄弟友谊万古长青。
    中国共产党中央委员会主席　毛泽东
    中华人民共和国主席　刘少奇中华人民共和国全国人民代表大会常务委员会委员长朱　德
    中华人民共和国国务院总理　周恩来
                          1960年8月22日
    新华社22日讯　我国外交部长陈毅22日打电报给罗马尼亚外交部长布纳丘，祝贺罗马尼亚解放十六周年。电报全文如下：罗马尼亚人民共和国外交部长，亲爱的阿·布纳丘同志：
    当罗马尼亚解放十六周年的时候，谨向你表示热烈的祝贺。衷心地祝你在执行罗马尼亚人民共和国的和平外交政策，进一步巩固以苏联为首的社会主义阵营的团结和保卫世界和平的事业中获得新的成就。
      中华人民共和国外交部长　陈　毅
                        1960年8月22日
