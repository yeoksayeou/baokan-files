Title: Kim Il-sung Receives Sino-Korean Friendship Association Delegation; Sino-Korean Friendship Association Delegation Returns to Pyongyang After Visiting Wonsan
Author:
Date: 1960-08-30
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Pyongyang, August 29th: Premier Kim Il-sung of the Korean Cabinet received the delegation of the Sino-Korean Friendship Association in Hamhung on the 28th, headed by Li Dequan, President of the Sino-Korean Friendship Association and Minister of Public Health, and Li Jiebo, Director of the Sino-Korean Friendship Association, Alternate Member of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of China, and Vice-Chairman of the All-China Federation of Trade Unions.

Present at the reception were: Lim Kye-chol, Chairman of the State Planning Commission of Korea; Choi Ki-chol, Standing Committee Member of the Korean-Chinese Friendship Association and Vice-Chairman of the Korean Committee for Foreign Cultural Relations; Hyun Moo-kwang, Chairman of the South Hamgyong Provincial Committee of the Korean Workers' Party; and Park In-ha, Chairman of the South Hamgyong Provincial People's Committee.

During the reception, Premier Kim Il-sung held talks with the members of the Sino-Korean Friendship Association delegation in a friendly and cordial atmosphere.

Xinhua News Agency, Pyongyang, August 29th: The delegation of the Sino-Korean Friendship Association, headed by Li Dequan and Li Jiebo, visited Wonsan, Hamhung, and other places from the 23rd to the 28th, and toured the Kumgangsan scenic area. They returned to Pyongyang on the morning of the 29th.

When the delegation visited the heroic port of Wonsan on the 23rd, Park Jae-geun’s wife, the internationalist martyr who sacrificed his life to rescue wounded Volunteer soldiers, and Volunteer Mother Gu Fuli, specially traveled a long distance from afar to Wonsan to meet with the delegation.

On the 27th and 28th, the delegation visited and toured the Hungnam Fertilizer Factory, Bongung Chemical Factory, and Ryongsong Machinery Factory in Hamhung.

In Wonsan and Hamhung, the delegation received a warm welcome from local party and government leaders and the people. (Attached photo)

### 金日成接见中朝友协代表团  中朝友协代表团访问元山后回到平壤

1960-08-30
第4版()
专栏：

    金日成接见中朝友协代表团
    中朝友协代表团访问元山后回到平壤
    新华社平壤29日电　朝鲜内阁首相金日成28日在咸兴接见了以中朝友好协会会长、卫生部部长李德全和中朝友好协会理事、中共中央候补委员、中华全国总工会副主席李颉伯为首的中朝友好协会代表团。
    接见时在座的有：朝鲜国家计划委员会委员长林桂哲，朝中友好协会常务委员会委员、朝鲜对外文化联络委员会副委员长崔基哲，朝鲜劳动党咸镜南道委员会委员长玄武光，咸镜南道人民委员会委员长朴仁夏等。
    接见时，金日成首相与中朝友好协会代表团成员在友好亲切的气氛中进行了谈话。
    新华社平壤29日电　以李德全和李颉伯为首的中朝友好协会代表团从23日到28日访问了元山、咸兴等地，并游览了金刚山风景区后，已在29日早晨回到了平壤。
    当代表团23日访问英雄港元山时，为救护志愿军伤员献出了自己生命的国际主义烈士朴在根的妻子和志愿军妈妈具富力特地从远路赶到元山来同代表团会面。
    27日和28日代表团在咸兴参观访问了兴南肥料工厂、本工化学工厂和龙城机械厂。
    在元山和咸兴，代表团受到当地党政领导人和人民的热烈欢迎。（附图片）
    8月28日，朝鲜内阁首相金日成（左起第三人）在咸兴接见了中朝友好协会代表团　平壤传真照片（新华社发）
