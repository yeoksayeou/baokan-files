Title: Fully Loaded Express Transport of "Steel Grain"
Author:
Date: 1960-08-01
Page: 2

    Rapid News Bulletin

    Fully Loaded Express Transport of "Steel Grain"
    Under the leadership of Party committees at all levels, the transportation departments of Zhejiang Province are working together with the production, supply, and sales departments to accelerate the transportation of "steel grain." At present, the province has organized 72 one-stop transportation lines for coal, coke, and ore. As of July 20, the average daily intake of "three materials" at major steel plants throughout the province increased by 26% compared to June.

### 满载快运“钢粮”

1960-08-01
第2版()
专栏：要闻快报

    满载快运“钢粮”
    浙江省交通运输部门，在各级党委领导下，同产、供、销各部门拧成一股绳，加速运输“钢粮”。目前，全省已组织了煤、焦、矿石一条龙运输线七十二条，到7月20日止，全省主要钢铁厂的“三料”日平均进厂量，比6月份提高了26％。
