Title: Beijing Teachers Celebrate with Teachers from Cuba and Other Countries
Author:
Date: 1960-08-18
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, 17th. The Cuban, Brazilian, Mexican, Uruguayan, and Ecuadorian teacher delegations attending the Third World Teachers' Conference, along with the Sri Lankan teacher delegation visiting China at the invitation of the National Committee of the Educational Workers' Union of China, held a celebration this evening with more than 300 teachers from Beijing at the Beijing Municipal Trade Union Council Auditorium.

He Wushuang, Acting Chairman of the Beijing Educational Workers' Union, extended a warm welcome on behalf of all teachers in the city to colleagues from Latin America and Asia, and expressed deep admiration for the heroic spirit of the Cuban people in resolutely opposing the aggression of U.S. imperialism. He wished the friends from various countries continuous new victories in the struggle against imperialist aggression and in defense of world peace.

Rodriguez, head of the Cuban delegation, spoke on behalf of the Latin American delegations. He expressed his gratitude for the support of the Chinese people and government for the Cuban people's struggle. He said, "China and Cuba have become important components of the worldwide anti-American united front." Fernando, head of the Sri Lankan Teachers' Union delegation, also spoke, praising the great achievements of China's socialist construction.

Teachers and students from the Beijing Art Normal College and the Twenty-first Middle School and other schools performed wonderful cultural programs at the party.

Fang Ming, Vice Chairman of the National Committee of the Educational Workers' Union of China, and Presidium member Sheng Jin also attended the party.

### 北京教师和古巴等国教师联欢

1960-08-18
第6版()
专栏：

    北京教师和古巴等国教师联欢
    新华社17日讯　出席第三届世界教师代表大会的古巴、巴西、墨西哥、乌拉圭和厄瓜多尔教师代表团以及应中国教育工会全国委员会邀请来华访问的锡兰教师代表团，今晚在北京市总工会礼堂与北京市三百多名教师举行了联欢会。
    北京市教育工会代理主席何戊双代表全市教师对来自拉丁美洲和亚洲的同行表示热烈欢迎，并且对古巴人民坚决反对美帝国主义侵略的英勇气概表示十分敬佩。他祝各国朋友们在反对帝国主义侵略和保卫世界和平事业的斗争中不断取得新的胜利。
    古巴代表团团长罗德里格斯代表各拉丁美洲国家代表团讲话。他对中国人民和政府给予古巴人民斗争的支持表示感谢。他说，“中国和古巴已成为全世界反美统一战线的重要构成部分”。锡兰教师工会代表团团长费南多也讲了话，他称赞了中国社会主义建设的巨大成就。
    北京市艺术师范学院及第二十一中学等学校的师生在联欢会上表演了精彩的文娱节目。
    中国教育工会全国委员会副主席方明及主席团委员盛瑾也出席了联欢会。
