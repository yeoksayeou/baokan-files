Title: African Foreign Ministers Conference Holds Secret Meeting
Author:
Date: 1960-08-28
Page: 6
Xinhua News Agency, Leopoldville, August 27th: The African Foreign Ministers Conference, which opened on August 25th in Leopoldville, capital of the Republic of Congo, held a secret meeting on the morning of August 26th to discuss the appointment of several committees to study political, economic, social, and cultural issues, and to draft the conference's agenda.

The conference elected Congolese Foreign Minister Bomboko as the Chairman of the conference, and the heads of delegations from Tunisia, Guinea, Liberia, and Ethiopia, among others, as members of the conference's Standing Committee.

### 非洲国家外长会议举行秘密会议

1960-08-28
第6版()
专栏：

    非洲国家外长会议举行秘密会议
    新华社27日讯　利奥波德维尔消息：25日在刚果共和国首都利奥波德维尔开幕的非洲国家外长会议，26日早上举行秘密会议，讨论了任命几个委员会来研究政治、经济、社会和文化等问题，并起草会议的议程。
    会议选出刚果外交部长邦博科为会议主席，突尼斯、几内亚、利比里亚和埃塞俄比亚等国的代表团团长为会议的常务委员会委员。
