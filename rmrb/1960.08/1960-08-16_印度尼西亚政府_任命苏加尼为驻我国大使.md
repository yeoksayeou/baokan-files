Title: Indonesian Government Appoints Sukarni as Ambassador to Our Country
Author:
Date: 1960-08-16
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, Jakarta, 13th — According to a report by the Indonesian news agency Antara, the government of the Republic of Indonesia has decided to appoint Mr. Sukarni as the new Ambassador Extraordinary and Plenipotentiary to the People's Republic of China.

### 印度尼西亚政府  任命苏加尼为驻我国大使

1960-08-16
第5版()
专栏：

    印度尼西亚政府
    任命苏加尼为驻我国大使
    新华社雅加达13日电　据印度尼西亚新闻社报道，印度尼西亚共和国政府已经决定任命苏加尼先生为新任驻中华人民共和国特命全权大使。
