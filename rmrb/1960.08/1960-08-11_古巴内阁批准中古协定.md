Title: Cuban Cabinet Approves Sino-Cuban Agreements
Author:
Date: 1960-08-11
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, Havana, August 9th. The Cuban Cabinet this morning approved the cultural cooperation agreement, the scientific and technical cooperation agreement, and the trade payment agreement signed between the governments of the Republic of Cuba and the People's Republic of China on July 23 this year.

The cabinet also approved some agreements signed between the Cuban government and the governments of the United Arab Republic, Poland, and other countries, and passed laws establishing the Cuban Mining Commission, the National Electricity Commission, and the Farmers' Housing Construction Fund.

### 古巴内阁批准中古协定

1960-08-11
第5版()
专栏：

    古巴内阁批准中古协定
    新华社哈瓦那9日电　古巴内阁今天清晨批准了古巴共和国政府和中华人民共和国政府今年7月23日签订的文化合作协定、科学和技术合作协定及贸易支付协定。
    内阁还批准了古巴政府和阿拉伯联合共和国、波兰等国政府签订的一些协定，并且通过了建立古巴矿业委员会、全国电力委员会和建立农民住宅建设基金的法律。
