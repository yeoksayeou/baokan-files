Title: Chinese Art Troupe Arrives in Canada to Attend Vancouver Drama Festival
Author:
Date: 1960-08-10
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, Vancouver, 9th: The Chinese Art Troupe, invited to participate in the Vancouver Drama Festival, arrived in Vancouver, a western Canadian city, on the afternoon of the 8th, led by its head, Chen Zhongjing. They were warmly welcomed by the local mayor, other administrative officials, and heads of the theater community.

Upon the art troupe's arrival, the Mayor of Vancouver, wearing a gold chain and carrying the city's seal, expressed a solemn welcome to the Chinese guests. He stated that Vancouver felt honored to host the first Chinese art troupe to visit Canada.

The art troupe was also warmly welcomed by over a hundred patriotic overseas Chinese. They presented flowers to each member of the art troupe and excitedly sang the song "Ode to the Motherland."

That afternoon, the art troupe held a press conference. The art troupe will hold five performances in Vancouver starting August 10th.

### 应邀参加温哥华戏剧节  我国艺术团抵加拿大

1960-08-10
第6版()
专栏：

    应邀参加温哥华戏剧节
    我国艺术团抵加拿大
    新华社9日讯   温哥华消息：应邀参加温哥华戏剧节的中国艺术团，8日下午在团长陈忠经的率领下到达加拿大西部城市温哥华，受到了当地市长、其他行政长官和戏剧界负责人的热烈欢迎。
    艺术团到达时，温哥华市长佩着金链，持着市的印信，表示对中国客人的隆重欢迎。他表示，温哥华为能接待第一个访问加拿大的中国艺术团体而感到光荣。
    艺术团还受到了一百多名爱国华侨的热烈欢迎。他们把鲜花献给了艺术团的每个成员，并兴奋地唱起“歌唱祖国”的歌曲。
    当天下午，艺术团举行了记者招待会。艺术团将从8月10日起在温哥华进行五场演出。
