Title: Cuban Teachers' Association Delegation Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1960-08-13
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, 12th - A four-member delegation from the Cuban National Association of Normal and Equivalent Teachers, led by the association's president, Leslie Rodriguez Aguilera, arrived in Beijing by plane today.

They are visiting China at the invitation of the National Committee of the Educational Workers' Union of China after attending the Third World Teachers' Conference in Guinea.

Those who welcomed them at the airport included Li Zaiwen, Secretary of the Secretariat of the All-China Federation of Trade Unions, and Sheng Jin, member of the Presidium of the National Committee of the Educational Workers' Union of China.

### 古巴教师学会代表团到京

1960-08-13
第6版()
专栏：

    古巴教师学会代表团到京
    新华社12日讯　古巴全国师范及同级教师学会代表团一行四人，在该会会长莱斯列·罗德里格斯·阿吉莱拉的率领下，于今日乘机抵达北京。
    他们是出席在几内亚举行的第三届世界教师代表大会后，应中国教育工会全国委员会的邀请来我国访问的。
    到机场欢迎的，有中华全国总工会书记处书记栗再温和中国教育工会全国委员会主席团委员盛瑾等。
