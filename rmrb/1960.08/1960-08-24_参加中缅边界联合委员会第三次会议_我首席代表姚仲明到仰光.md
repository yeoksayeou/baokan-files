Title: My Chief Representative Yao Zhongming Arrives in Rangoon to Attend the Third Meeting of the Sino-Burmese Boundary Joint Committee
Author:
Date: 1960-08-24
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Rangoon, August 23rd. Yao Zhongming, the Chinese Chief Representative of the Sino-Burmese Boundary Joint Committee, arrived here by plane this afternoon to attend the third meeting of the committee, which will be held in Rangoon soon.

Accompanying Yao Zhongming were Chinese representatives, advisors, and staff members of the Sino-Burmese Boundary Joint Committee.

Those who went to the airport to welcome the Chinese representatives included Colonel So Min, Director of the Burmese Border Areas Administration Bureau; Colonel La Ang, Director of the Survey Bureau; Colonel Ba Dan, Director of the Army Psychological Warfare and Education Bureau; Colonel Qing Niu of the General Staff Department; U San Lun, Secretary of the Kachin State Affairs Department; U Khin Nyo, Joint Secretary of the Border Areas Administration Bureau; and former Attorney General U Ba Sheng. The Burmese Ambassador to China, Kyaw Winn, also went to the airport to welcome them.

Also at the airport to greet them were Lei Yang, Chargé d'affaires ad interim of the Chinese Embassy in Burma, Colonel Jiang Keding, Military Attaché, and counselors from the Chinese Embassy.

Xinhua News Agency, Kunming, August 23rd. The third meeting of the Sino-Burmese Boundary Joint Committee will be held in Rangoon in the fourth week of this month. The Chinese representatives and advisors, led by Chief Representative Yao Zhongming, left Kunming by plane for Rangoon this morning.

Those who went to the airport to see them off today included Zhang Zizhai, Secretary-General of the Yunnan Provincial People's Committee, and Li Ping, Director of the Provincial Foreign Affairs Office. U Maw Maw So, Consul General of the Union of Burma in Kunming, also went to the airport to see them off today.

### 参加中缅边界联合委员会第三次会议  我首席代表姚仲明到仰光

1960-08-24
第4版()
专栏：

    参加中缅边界联合委员会第三次会议
    我首席代表姚仲明到仰光
    新华社仰光23日电　中缅边界联合委员会的中国首席代表姚仲明今天下午乘飞机到达这里，他将参加委员会即将在仰光举行的第三次会议。
    随同姚仲明前来的还有中缅边界联合委员会的中国代表、顾问和工作人员等。
    前往机场欢迎中国代表的有缅甸边境地区管理局局长苏敏上校、勘察局局长拉昂上校、陆军心理战和教育局局长巴丹上校、总参谋部的庆牛上校、克钦邦事务部秘书吴山伦、边境地区管理局联合秘书吴钦纽和前检察总长吴巴盛。缅甸驻中国大使叫温也到机场欢迎。
    到机场迎接的还有中国驻缅甸大使馆临时代办雷扬、武官蒋克定上校和中国大使馆的参赞等。
    新华社昆明23日电　中缅边界联合委员会第三次会议将于本月第四个星期在仰光举行。中国方面的代表和顾问们在首席代表姚仲明率领下，已于今天上午由昆明乘飞机前往仰光。
    今天到机场欢送的，有云南省人民委员会秘书长张子斋，省外事处处长李萍等。缅甸联邦驻昆明总领事吴茂茂梭，今天也到机场欢送。
