Title: Korea Announces Communiqué Khrushchev to Visit Korea
Author:
Date: 1960-08-15
Page: 6

    Korea Announces Communiqué
    Khrushchev to Visit Korea
    Xinhua News Agency, Pyongyang, August 13th. Pyongyang newspapers today published a Korean communiqué regarding Khrushchev's upcoming visit to Korea:

    The Central Committee of the Workers' Party of Korea and the Government of the Democratic People's of Korea have extended an invitation to the Central Committee of the Communist Party of the Soviet Union and the Soviet Government, inviting Comrade Khrushchev, First Secretary of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of the Soviet Union and Chairman of the Council of Ministers of the Soviet Union, to visit the Democratic People's Republic of Korea.

### 朝鲜发表公报  赫鲁晓夫将访问朝鲜

1960-08-15
第6版()
专栏：

    朝鲜发表公报
    赫鲁晓夫将访问朝鲜
    新华社平壤13日电　平壤报纸今天发表了朝鲜关于赫鲁晓夫将访问朝鲜的公报：
    朝鲜劳动党中央委员会和朝鲜民主主义人民共和国政府向苏联共产党中央委员会和苏联政府发出邀请，邀请苏联共产党中央委员会第一书记、苏联部长会议主席赫鲁晓夫同志访问朝鲜民主主义人民共和国。
    苏联共产党中央委员会和苏联政府很满意地接受了这一邀请。
    赫鲁晓夫同志将在今年10月初访问朝鲜民主主义人民共和国。
