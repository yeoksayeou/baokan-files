Title: Warring States Period Ruins and Relics Discovered in Liangcheng
Author:
Date: 1960-08-30
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Hohhot, August 29th: The Cultural Relics Work Group of Ulanqab League in Inner Mongolia Autonomous Region recently discovered a Warring States to Han Dynasty site covering approximately 1,000 square meters at the Xishuang Ancient City water conservancy construction site in Liangcheng County. Among the cultural relics unearthed from this site are rectangular earthen houses and tile-roofed houses inhabited by people during the Warring States period. Most of the houses are five meters long and four and a half meters wide, and generally have kitchens and bedrooms.

### 凉城发现战国遗址和文物

1960-08-30
第4版()
专栏：

    凉城发现战国遗址和文物
    据新华社呼和浩特29日电　内蒙古自治区乌兰察布盟文物工作组，最近在凉城县西双古城水利工地，发现一处面积约一千平方米的战国至汉遗址。在这处遗址中发掘出来的文物，有战国时代人类居住的长方形的土房和瓦房，房屋大部分每间长五米，宽四米五，一般都有厨房和卧室。
