Title: Jordanian Prime Minister Majali Assassinated by Bomb

Author:
Date: 1960-08-30
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, Amman, August 29th: According to a broadcast by Amman Radio today, a bomb exploded at the Jordanian Ministry of Foreign Affairs on the morning of August 29th, killing Jordanian Prime Minister Majali. The message stated that the bomb explosion severely damaged the Foreign Ministry building (which also houses the Prime Minister's office). It is reported that after the incident, martial law was immediately imposed in the Jordanian capital of Amman, and Jordanian troops are patrolling the streets. According to the radio station, a bomb explosion also occurred in the office of the Director-General of the Jordanian Publishing Bureau.

### 约旦首相马贾利被炸死

1960-08-30
第6版()
专栏：

    约旦首相马贾利被炸死
    新华社29日讯　安曼消息：据安曼电台今天广播，约旦外交部29日上午发生炸弹爆炸，约旦首相马贾利被炸死。消息说，炸弹的爆炸使外交部的房屋（也是首相办公室）遭到严重毁坏。据报道，在事件发生后，约旦首都安曼立即实行戒严，约旦军队在街道上巡逻。据这家电台说，约旦出版局总局长的办公室内也发生了炸弹爆炸。
