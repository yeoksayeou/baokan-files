Title: Chinese Men's Tennis Team to Participate in International Competition in Soviet Union
Author: 
Date: 1960-08-21
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, August 20th: The Chinese men's tennis team, composed of Zhu Zhenhua and Mei Fuji, left Beijing by plane today for Moscow to participate in the international tennis competition to be held there.

### 中国男子网球队去苏参加国际比赛

1960-08-21
第6版()
专栏：

    中国男子网球队去苏参加国际比赛
    新华社20日讯　由朱振华、梅福基组成的中国男子网球队，今天乘飞机离北京前往莫斯科，参加即将在那里举行的国际网球比赛。
