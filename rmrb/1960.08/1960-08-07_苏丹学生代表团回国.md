Title: Sudanese Student Delegation Returns Home
Author: 
Date: 1960-08-07
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, 6th — The Sudanese student delegation, invited by the All-China Students' Federation to visit our country, returned home by plane today from Beijing after visiting various parts of China.
　　Those who saw them off at the airport included Pan Lihua, Vice-Chairman of the All-China Students' Federation, and others.

### 苏丹学生代表团回国

1960-08-07
第6版()
专栏：

    苏丹学生代表团回国
    新华社6日讯　应中华全国学生联合会邀请来我国访问的苏丹学生代表团，在访问了中国各地以后，今天乘飞机离开北京回国。
    到机场送行的有中华全国学生联合会副主席潘丽华等人。
