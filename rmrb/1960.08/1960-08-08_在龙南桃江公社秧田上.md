Title: On the Rice Paddy Fields of Taojiang Commune, Longnan
Author: Cai Qin Yuan, Xi Lin
Date: 1960-08-08
Page: 3

Poetry and Painting

    On the Rice Paddy Fields of Taojiang Commune, Longnan
    The rice transplanter, goes "gua gua,"
    One person drags it all over the field;
    Running one pass after another,
    Ridge after ridge of green seedlings sway in the wind.
        Poem by Production Team Leader Cai Qinyuan
        Painting by Xi Lin

### 在龙南桃江公社秧田上
蔡勤媛  夕林
1960-08-08
第3版()
专栏：诗画配

    在龙南桃江公社秧田上
    插秧机，呱呱叫，
    一人拖着满田跑；
    跑了一趟又一趟，
    丘丘绿秧随风飘。
        生产队长蔡勤媛诗
        夕　林画
