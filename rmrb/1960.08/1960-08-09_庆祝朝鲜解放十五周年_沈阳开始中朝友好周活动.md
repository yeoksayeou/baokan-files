Title: Celebrating the 15th Anniversary of the Liberation of Korea, Shenyang Begins Sino-Korean Friendship Week Activities
Author:
Date: 1960-08-09
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Shenyang, August 8th. To celebrate the 15th anniversary of the liberation of Korea, the Sino-Korean Friendship Week, held by the people of Shenyang, opened today.

This afternoon, people from all walks of life in Liaoning Province and Shenyang City held a grand meeting to celebrate the 15th anniversary of the liberation of Korea. Liaoning People's Broadcasting Station broadcast the meeting live to the people of the province. Shop windows in the prosperous areas of Shenyang displayed pictures about the construction achievements of the Korean people and Sino-Korean friendship. All major cinemas in the city will hold a Korean Film Week starting from the 9th, screening Korean films "Nine O'Clock Sharp," "Mount Kumgang," and "Cherish the Future."

Articles celebrating the 15th anniversary of the liberation of Korea have begun to be published in newspapers. Cai Zhoushan, a member of the Sino-Korean People's Friendship Commune who had visited Korea, and Wang Xuanzhi and Zhao Zongcheng, former volunteers who fought alongside the Korean people, published commemorative articles in the newspapers saying that the friendship between the Chinese and Korean people has been tested in the flames of war and is unbreakable.

The delegation of the Korea-China Friendship Association, which is currently visiting Shenyang, attended the Shenyang No. 1 Machine Tool Plant's celebration of the 15th anniversary of the liberation of Korea yesterday afternoon. More than 1,200 employees present welcomed the distinguished guests with warm applause. At the meeting, Wang Mingde, deputy director of Shenyang No. 1 Machine Tool Plant, and Kim Jong-heng, head of the delegation, spoke respectively. At the celebration meeting, the employees of the No. 1 Machine Tool Plant also performed songs and dances such as "Song of Kim Il-sung," "Scented Hairpin Dance," and "Xinjiang Dance" for the distinguished Korean guests.

### 庆祝朝鲜解放十五周年  沈阳开始中朝友好周活动

1960-08-09
第4版()
专栏：

    庆祝朝鲜解放十五周年
    沈阳开始中朝友好周活动
    新华社沈阳8日电　沈阳市人民为庆祝朝鲜解放十五周年而举行的中朝友好周，在今天开幕。
    今天下午，辽宁省和沈阳市各界人民举行了盛大的庆祝朝鲜解放十五周年大会。辽宁人民广播电台向全省人民转播了大会实况。沈阳市繁华地区的商店橱窗展出了有关朝鲜人民建设成就和中朝友谊的图片。全市各大影院将从9日开始举行朝鲜电影周，上映朝鲜影片“九点正”、“金刚山”、“要爱未来”。
    报纸上开始发表有关庆祝朝鲜解放十五周年的文章。曾经到朝鲜访问过的中朝人民友谊公社社员蔡周善和曾经同朝鲜人民并肩作战的前志愿军人员王宣之、赵宗城，都在报纸上发表纪念文章说，中朝人民的友谊是在战火中受到考验的，是牢不可破的。
    正在沈阳访问的朝中友好协会代表团，昨天下午参加了沈阳第一机床厂庆祝朝鲜解放十五周年大会。到会的一千二百多名职工以热烈的掌声对贵宾的来临表示欢迎。会上，沈阳第一机床厂副厂长王明德和代表团团长金钟恒分别讲了话。庆祝大会上，第一机床厂的职工们还为朝鲜贵宾演出了“金日成之歌”、“香钗舞”、“新疆舞”等歌舞节目。
