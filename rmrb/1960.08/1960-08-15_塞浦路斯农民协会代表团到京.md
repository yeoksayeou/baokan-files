Title: Cyprus Farmers Association Delegation Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1960-08-15
Page: 5
Xinhua News Agency, August 13th. A four-member delegation from the Cyprus Farmers Association arrived in Beijing by plane today. Song Chuan, Vice Chairman of the Chinese Agricultural and Forestry Workers' Union, and others welcomed them at the airport.

### 塞浦路斯农民协会代表团到京

1960-08-15
第5版()
专栏：

    塞浦路斯农民协会代表团到京
    新华社13日讯　塞浦路斯农民协会代表团一行四人于今日乘飞机抵达北京。中国农林工会副主席宋川等到机场欢迎。
