Title: Southwest Africa National Union Chairman Kozonguizi Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1960-08-16
Page: 5

    Southwest Africa National Union Chairman Kozonguizi Arrives in Beijing
    Xinhua News Agency, August 14th. At the invitation of the Chinese People's Institute of Foreign Affairs and the Chinese Asian-African Solidarity Committee, Kozonguizi, Chairman of the Southwest Africa National Union, arrived in Beijing on the afternoon of the 14th.

### 西南非民族联盟主席科章吉济到京

1960-08-16
第5版()
专栏：

    西南非民族联盟主席科章吉济到京
    新华社14日讯　西南非洲民族联盟主席科章吉济应中国人民外交学会和中国亚非团结委员会联合邀请，于14日下午抵达北京。
