Title: Further Research on Human Flight and the Safety System for Returning to Earth: Soviet Union Launches Second Spaceship

Author:
Date: 1960-08-20
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, August 19th, TASS dispatch from Moscow on August 19th: According to the plan for studying outer space, the Soviet Union launched its second spaceship on August 19, 1960, which has entered Earth's orbit. The primary mission of the launch is to further study systems that ensure human life support, as well as the safety of human flight and return to Earth.

　　The sealed cabin of the satellite is equipped with everything necessary for future human flight. The cabin carries test subjects, including two dogs, "Belka" and "Strelka."

　　During the satellite-spaceship flight, a series of medical and biological experiments will be conducted, and a plan for scientific exploration of outer space will be implemented.

　　The Soviet Union's second spaceship has entered a near-circular orbit with an altitude of approximately 320 kilometers.

　　The initial orbital period of the spaceship is 90.6 minutes, and the angle of inclination of its orbit to the Earth's equatorial plane is 65 degrees. The weight of the satellite-spaceship (excluding the last stage of the carrier rocket) is 4,600 kilograms.

　　The satellite-spaceship is equipped with a "Signal" radio transmitter operating at a frequency of 19.995 megahertz, as well as radio telemetry equipment for transmitting materials to the ground regarding the condition of the test animals and the operation of various systems installed on the satellite. To observe the behavior of the animals, a radio television system is installed on the satellite-spaceship.

　　Preliminary data indicate that the various systems installed on the satellite-spaceship are functioning normally.

　　The times (Moscow time) when the satellite-spaceship passed over the following regions are as follows:

　　Rome – 14:49, Moscow – 14:55, Hailar – 15:07, Tokyo – 15:12, Wellington – 15:35, Rio de Janeiro – 16:01, Paris – 16:22, Leningrad – 16:28, Irkutsk – 16:38, Shanghai – 16:44, Sydney – 17:04, Santiago – 17:28, Oslo – 17:58, Novosibirsk – 18:08, Hanoi – 18:18.

### 进一步研究人的飞行和返回地球的安全系统  苏联发射第二个宇宙飞船

1960-08-20
第1版()
专栏：

    进一步研究人的飞行和返回地球的安全系统
    苏联发射第二个宇宙飞船
    新华社19日讯　塔斯社莫斯科19日讯：根据研究宇宙空间的计划，1960年8月19日苏联发射了第二个宇宙飞船，它已进入地球卫星轨道。发射的基本任务是进一步研究保证人的生命活动以及人的飞行和返回地球的安全的系统。
    在卫星的密封舱中，装备了未来的人的飞行所必需的一切。舱内载有试验用的生物，其中包括两只狗“小箭”和“松鼠”。
    规定在卫星式飞船飞行时，要进行一系列的医学生物学实验和实现宇宙空间科学考察的计划。
    苏联第二个宇宙飞船进入了高约三百二十公里的接近圆形的轨道。
    宇宙飞船初始运行周期是九十点六分钟，它的运行轨道同地球赤道平面的交角是六十五度。卫星式飞船的重量（不算运载火箭的最后一级）为四千六百公斤。
    卫星式飞船上装有“信号”无线电发射机，它工作的频率是十九点九九五兆周，还装有向地面发射关于试验动物的状况和关于卫星上安装的各种系统的工作情况的材料用的无线电遥测仪器。为了观测动物的举动，在卫星式飞船上安装了一个无线电电视系统。
    初步材料表明，安装在卫星式飞船上的各种系统工作正常。
    卫星式飞船通过的地区时间（莫斯科时间）如下：
