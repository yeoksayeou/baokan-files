Title: At the Invitation of the International Exposition, Sichuan Song and Dance Troupe Performs in Damascus
Author:
Date: 1960-08-18
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, Damascus, 16th - At the invitation of the Seventh Damascus International Exposition, the Chinese Sichuan Song and Dance Troupe, which is visiting, gave its premiere performance at the Exposition Theater tonight.

The premiere was a great success. All thirteen performances were warmly welcomed by more than 1,700 spectators. Some of the programs were even repeated at the request of the audience.

The audience was deeply impressed by the traditional Chinese folk dances and music. Many viewers commented that the performance was "excellent."

Before the performance, a representative of the General Administration of the Exposition spoke, warmly welcoming the visiting Chinese Song and Dance Troupe and praising the high level of Chinese art. Zhang Tiesheng, head of the Chinese Sichuan Song and Dance Troupe, gave a reply.

Among those who attended the performance today were the Ministers of Industry, Agriculture, Justice, Health, and Economy, and the Minister of Culture and Guidance of the Syrian Region of the United Arab Republic. Chen Jiakang, our Ambassador to the United Arab Republic, also watched the performance.

The Chinese Sichuan Song and Dance Troupe will perform four more shows here.

### 应国际博览会的邀请  四川歌舞团在大马士革演出

1960-08-18
第6版()
专栏：

    应国际博览会的邀请
    四川歌舞团在大马士革演出
    新华社大马士革16日电　应第七届大马士革国际博览会的邀请前来访问的中国四川歌舞团，今晚在博览会剧场作首次公演。
    首次演出非常成功。上演的十三个节目，每一个都受到一千七百多名观众的热烈欢迎。其中有些节目还应观众的要求而重演。
    观众们对中国传统的民间舞蹈和音乐获得极为深刻的印象。许多观众反映，这次演出“好极了”。
    在演出之前，博览会总管理处的代表讲了话，热烈欢迎正在访问的中国歌舞团并赞扬中国艺术的高度水平。中国四川歌舞团团长张铁生致了答词。
    今天出席观看演出的有阿拉伯联合共和国叙利亚地区的工业部长、农业部长、司法部长、卫生部长、经济部长及文化和指导部长。我国驻阿拉伯联合共和国大使陈家康也观看了演出。
    中国四川歌舞团还将在这里演出四场。
