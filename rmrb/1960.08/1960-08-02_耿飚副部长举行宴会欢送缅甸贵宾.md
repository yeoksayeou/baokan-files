Title: Geng Biao, Vice Minister, Holds Banquet to Bid Farewell to Burmese Guests
Author:
Date: 1960-08-02
Page: 1
    Xinhua News Agency, August 1st — Geng Biao, Vice Minister of Foreign Affairs, held a banquet at Beihai Park this noon to bid farewell to the Burmese delegation of the Sino-Burmese Joint Boundary Committee, headed by Brigadier General Aung Gyi.
    The Burmese Ambassador to China, U Hla Maw, the representative of the Burmese delegation, and diplomatic officials from the embassy were invited to attend the banquet.
    Li Yimang, China's Ambassador to Burma, Yao Zhongming, the Chief Representative of the Chinese side of the Sino-Burmese Joint Boundary Committee, and representatives and advisors attended the banquet.

### 耿飚副部长举行宴会欢送缅甸贵宾

1960-08-02
第1版()
专栏：

    耿飚副部长举行宴会欢送缅甸贵宾
    新华社1日讯　外交部副部长耿飚今天中午在北海公园举行宴会，欢送以昂季准将为首的中缅边界联合委员会缅甸代表团。
    缅甸驻中国大使、缅甸代表团代表叫温，和大使馆外交官员应邀出席了宴会。
    我国驻缅甸大使李一氓，中缅边界联合委员会中国首席代表姚仲明和代表、顾问出席了宴会。
