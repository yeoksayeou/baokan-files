Title: Delegation of the China-Korea Friendship Association Leaves Shenyang for Beijing
Author:
Date: 1960-08-10
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Shenyang, August 9th. The delegation of the China-Korea Friendship Association, headed by Kim Jong-heng, Minister of Higher Education of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea, left Shenyang by train at 7:00 p.m. today for Beijing.

Those who saw them off at the station included Li Huang, Secretary of the Liaoning Provincial Committee of the Chinese Communist Party and President of the Liaoning Provincial Branch of the China-Korea Friendship Association, Qiu Youwen, Deputy Governor of Liaoning Province, Jin Zhifu, Chairman of the Liaoning Provincial Federation of Trade Unions and Vice President of the Liaoning Provincial Branch of the China-Korea Friendship Association, Lieutenant General Zeng Siyu, head of the Shenyang Garrison, and Wu Tieming, Secretary of the Shenyang Municipal Committee of the Chinese Communist Party, and others.

During their stay in Shenyang, the distinguished Korean guests attended the celebration meeting of all circles in Liaoning Province and Shenyang City celebrating the 15th anniversary of the liberation of Korea, as well as the celebration activities of the Shenyang No. 1 Machine Tool Plant and the Northeast Institute of Technology. The delegation also visited the Liaoning Industrial Exhibition Hall, the Shenyang Cable Factory, and the Workers' Village, and toured the city of Shenyang.

### 朝中友好协会代表团离沈阳来京

1960-08-10
第4版()
专栏：

    朝中友好协会代表团离沈阳来京
    新华社沈阳9日电　以朝鲜民主主义人民共和国高等教育相金钟恒为首的朝中友好协会代表团，今天下午七时乘火车离开沈阳前往北京。
    到车站欢送的，有中共辽宁省委书记、中朝友好协会辽宁省分会会长李荒，辽宁省副省长仇友文，辽宁省总工会主席、中朝友好协会辽宁省分会副会长金直夫，沈阳驻军负责人曾思玉中将，中共沈阳市委书记吴铁鸣等。
    朝鲜贵宾们在沈阳期间，参加了辽宁省和沈阳市各界人民庆祝朝鲜解放十五周年大会，以及沈阳第一机床厂、东北工学院的庆祝活动。代表团还参观了辽宁工业展览馆、沈阳电缆厂、工人村，游览了沈阳市容。
