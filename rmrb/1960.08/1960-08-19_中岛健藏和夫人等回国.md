Title: Nakajima Kenzo and Wife Return Home

Author:
Date: 1960-08-19
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, 17th - Nakajima Kenzo, Chairman of the Japan-China Cultural Exchange Association, his wife, and accompanying personnel Goto Shirato and two others, returned home by plane via Guangzhou this morning.

Those who went to the airport to see them off included Liao Chengzhi, Chairman of the Chinese Asian-African Solidarity Committee; Yang Hansheng, Vice President of the Chinese People's Association for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries; Zhou Erfu, Secretary-General; as well as Ouyang Yuqian, Tian Han, Zha Fuxi, Lü Ji, Zhao Puchu, Zhao Anbo, Chen Yu, Zheng Senyu, Ye Qianyu, Yang Jin, Wang Xiaozhi, Sun Pinghua, Yang Chunsong, Xie Nanguang, and more than twenty others.

Lin Lin, the Deputy Secretary-General of the Chinese People's Association for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries accompanied guests to Shenzhen.

### 中岛健藏和夫人等回国

1960-08-19
第6版()
专栏：

    中岛健藏和夫人等回国
    新华社17日讯　日中文化交流协会理事长中岛健藏和夫人以及随行人员白土吾夫等三人，今天早晨乘飞机经广州返国。
    前往机场欢送的有中国亚非团结委员会主席廖承志，中国人民对外文化协会副会长阳翰笙、秘书长周而复以及欧阳予倩、田汉、查阜西、吕骥、赵朴初、赵安博、陈宇、郑森禹、叶浅予、杨进、王晓之、孙平化、杨春松、谢南光等二十多人。
    中国人民对外文化协会副秘书长林林陪同客人前往深圳。
