Title: Kazuo Suzuki Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1960-08-20
Page: 6

    Xinhua News Agency, 19th. Kazuo Suzuki, Standing Director of the Japan Asian-African Solidarity Committee and Executive Director of the Japan-China Trade Promotion Association, arrived in Beijing by plane on the afternoon of the 19th at the invitation of the Chinese Asian-African Solidarity Committee.
    Those who went to the airport to welcome him included Qi Muguang, Xie Nanguang, Xiao Xiangqian, Wang Xiaoyun, Dong Chao and other people from all walks of life.

### 铃木一雄到京

1960-08-20
第6版()
专栏：

    铃木一雄到京
    新华社19日讯　日本亚非团结委员会常务理事、日本日中贸易促进会专务理事铃木一雄应中国亚非团结委员会邀请，19日下午乘飞机到达北京。
    前往机场欢迎的有：戚慕光、谢南光、萧向前、王晓云、董超等各界人士。
