Title: Liu Chairman Appoints Huang Hua as China's Ambassador to Ghana
Author:
Date: 1960-08-06
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, August 5th: Chairman of the People's Republic of China, Liu Shaoqi, has appointed Huang Hua as the Plenipotentiary Ambassador of the People's Republic of China to the Republic of Ghana.
