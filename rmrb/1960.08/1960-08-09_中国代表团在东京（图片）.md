Title: Chinese Delegation in Tokyo (Pictures)
Author:
Date: 1960-08-09
Page: 6

On the presidium of the Sixth World Conference Against Atomic and Hydrogen Bombs is Liu Ningyi, head of the Chinese delegation. He is one of the thirty-one chairmen of the conference.

Liu Ningyi addressed the 15th Regular Convention of the General Council of Trade Unions of Japan (Sohyo).

On July 31, at the celebration of the 10th anniversary of the founding of Sohyo, Liu Ningyi and Sohyo Chairman Kaoru Ota shook hands cordially.

On August 5, Liu Ningyi and representatives of various foreign delegations participating in the conference entered the grand rally venue held in Hibiya Park together with the peace marchers, and were warmly welcomed.

On July 29, at Haneda Airport in Tokyo, the Japanese people warmly welcomed the Chinese delegation attending the Sixth World Conference Against Atomic and Hydrogen Bombs. The first person on the left in the front row is Liu Ningyi, head of the Chinese delegation.

The welcoming crowd waved their arms and shouted slogans.

(Xinhua News Agency稿)

### 中国代表团在东京（图片）

1960-08-09
第6版()
专栏：

    中国代表团在东京
    在第六届禁止原子弹氢弹世界大会主席台上的中国代表团团长刘宁一。他是大会三十一位主席之一。
    刘宁一向日本总评第十五次定期大会致词。
    在7月31日总评成立十周年纪念会上，刘宁一和总评议长太田熏握手言欢。
    8月5日刘宁一和参加大会的各外国代表团与和平大游行的队伍一起进入在日比谷公园举行的盛大集会会场，受到人们热烈的欢迎。
    7月29日在东京羽田机场上，日本人民热烈欢迎了出席第六届禁止原子弹氢弹世界大会的中国代表团。前左第一人为中国代表团团长刘宁一。
    欢迎的人群挥舞着双臂，高呼口号。
                           （新华社稿）
