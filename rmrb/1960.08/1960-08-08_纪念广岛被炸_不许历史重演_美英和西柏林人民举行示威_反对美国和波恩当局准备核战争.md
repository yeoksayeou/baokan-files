Title: Commemorating the Hiroshima Bombing: Let History Not Repeat Itself - People in the US, Britain, and West Berlin Hold Demonstrations Against the US and Bonn Authorities' Preparations for Nuclear War
Author:
Date: 1960-08-08
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, 7th dispatch from New York: Over two thousand Americans demonstrated in front of the United Nations headquarters in New York yesterday to commemorate the fifteenth anniversary of the US dropping the atomic bomb on Hiroshima and to protest the government's nuclear arms race policy.

　　Demonstrators carrying signs opposing the expansion of nuclear armaments and the preparation for nuclear war, sang American folk songs as they marched to the front of the United Nations headquarters, and formed a picket line around the building.

　　Among the demonstrators were a group from Trenton, New Jersey. They set out from Trenton on August 3rd, starting a "peace march" towards New York.

　　In Groton, Connecticut, people also held similar demonstrations. Demonstrators distributed leaflets protesting the manufacture of nuclear submarines that launch "Polaris" missiles.

　　Demonstrators shouted: "Don't repeat Hiroshima! Oppose any submarines equipped with 'Polaris' missiles!"

　　Xinhua News Agency, Berlin, 6th dispatch: The people of West Berlin held a demonstration today to commemorate the fifteenth anniversary of the US dropping the atomic bomb on Hiroshima, Japan.

　　Demonstrators held signs opposing US imperialism and distributed leaflets condemning West Germany's nuclear arms race policy.

　　West Berlin police even arrested more than thirty demonstrators.

　　Xinhua News Agency, London, 6th dispatch: Approximately two hundred residents of Manchester held a demonstration march in the city center today to commemorate the fifteenth anniversary of the atomic bombing of Hiroshima by the United States.

　　The demonstrators eventually laid wreaths and observed a moment of silence at the city's war memorial. (Attached picture)

### 纪念广岛被炸　不许历史重演  美英和西柏林人民举行示威  反对美国和波恩当局准备核战争

1960-08-08
第5版()
专栏：

    纪念广岛被炸　不许历史重演
    美英和西柏林人民举行示威
    反对美国和波恩当局准备核战争
    新华社7日讯　纽约消息：两千多名美国人昨天在纽约联合国总部前面举行示威，纪念美国在广岛投掷原子弹十五周年，并且抗议政府的核军备竞赛政策。
    示威群众举着反对扩充核军备、准备核战争的标语牌，唱着美国的民歌来到联合国总部前面，并且围绕着这座建筑物布置了纠察线。
    示威群众当中有一批人是来自新泽西州特伦敦地方的。他们在3日从特伦敦出发，开始向纽约的“和平进军”。
    在康涅狄格州的格鲁顿，人们也举行了同类性质的示威游行。示威者散发了传单，抗议制造发射“北极星”导弹的核潜水艇。
    示威者高呼：“不要重演广岛事件。反对装备‘北极星’导弹的任何潜水艇！”
    新华社柏林6日电　西柏林人民今天举行示威，纪念美国向日本的广岛投掷原子弹十五周年。
    示威者拿着反对美帝国主义的标语牌，并且分发传单，谴责西德的核军备竞赛政策。
    西柏林的警察竟逮捕了三十多个示威者。
    新华社伦敦6日电　曼彻斯特市的大约二百多个居民今天在市中心举行示威游行，纪念广岛被美国原子弹轰炸十五周年。
    示威者最后在这个城市的战争纪念碑前献了花圈并默悼。（附图片）
