Title: Argentine Cultural Delegation Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1960-08-07
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, 6th. The Argentine cultural delegation, led by Professor Carlos Astrada, arrived in Beijing by plane this afternoon at the invitation of the Chinese-Latin American Friendship Association.

Wang Jiabin, Deputy Secretary-General of the Chinese-Latin American Friendship Association, and others were at the airport to welcome them.

### 阿根廷文化代表团到京

1960-08-07
第6版()
专栏：

    阿根廷文化代表团到京
    新华社6日讯　应中国拉丁美洲友好协会邀请来我国访问的阿根廷文化代表团，在卡洛斯·阿斯特拉达教授率领下，今天下午乘飞机到达北京。
    到机场欢迎的有中国拉丁美洲友好协会副总干事王家宾等人。
