Title: Li Fuchun Leads Party and Government Delegation to Hanoi to Attend the 15th Anniversary of the Founding of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam
Author:
Date: 1960-08-31
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, August 30th: At the invitation of the Central Committee of the Vietnam Workers' Party and the Government of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam, the Chinese Communist Party and Chinese Government delegation, carrying the friendship and congratulations of the people of the whole country, left Beijing by special plane this morning for Hanoi to attend the 15th anniversary celebration of the founding of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam.

The head of the Chinese Party and Government Delegation is Li Fuchun, member of the Political Bureau of the CPC Central Committee and Vice Premier of the State Council. The members are: Ye Jianying, member of the CPC Central Committee and Vice Chairman of the National Defense Council; and Liao Chengzhi, member of the CPC Central Committee and Deputy Director of the Foreign Affairs Office of the State Council.

Those who saw them off at the airport today included Peng Zhen and Li Xiannian, members of the Political Bureau of the CPC Central Committee; Bo Yibo, alternate member of the Political Bureau; Yang Shangkun, alternate member of the Secretariat of the CPC Central Committee; Luo Ruiqing and Xi Zhongxun, Vice Premiers of the State Council; Shen Junru and Lin Feng, Vice Chairmen of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress; Zhang Zhizhong, Vice Chairman of the National Defense Council; and Zhang Hanfu and Ji Pengfei, Vice Ministers of Foreign Affairs.

Before Comrades Li Fuchun, Ye Jianying, and Liao Chengzhi boarded the plane, the people who saw them off presented them with flowers.

Tran Tu Binh, Ambassador of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam to China, and officials of the Embassy were also at the airport to see them off. (Attached photo)

Peng Zhen (right one), Li Xiannian (left one), Li Fuchun (left two), Ye Jianying (right two), and Liao Chengzhi (right three) and other comrades enter the airport.

### 应邀参加越南民主共和国成立十五周年庆典  李富春率党政代表团赴河内

1960-08-31
第1版()
专栏：

    应邀参加越南民主共和国成立十五周年庆典
    李富春率党政代表团赴河内
    新华社30日讯　应越南劳动党中央委员会和越南民主共和国政府的邀请，前往参加越南民主共和国成立十五周年庆典的中国共产党和中国政府代表团，带着全国人民的友谊和祝贺，今天上午乘专机离开北京前往河内。
    中国党政代表团的团长是，中共中央政治局委员、国务院副总理李富春，团员有：中共中央委员、国防委员会副主席叶剑英元帅，中共中央委员、国务院外事办公室副主任廖承志。
    今天到机场欢送的，有中共中央政治局委员彭真、李先念，政治局候补委员薄一波，中共中央书记处候补书记杨尚昆，国务院副总理罗瑞卿、习仲勋，全国人民代表大会常务委员会副委员长沈钧儒、林枫，国防委员会副主席张治中，外交部副部长章汉夫、姬鹏飞等。
    在李富春、叶剑英、廖承志同志登上飞机之前，欢送的人们向他们献了鲜花。
    越南民主共和国驻中国大使陈子平和大使馆官员也到机场欢送。（附图片）
    彭真（右一）、李先念（左一）和李富春（左二）叶剑英（右二）廖承志（右三）等同志步入机场
            新华社记者　李基禄摄
