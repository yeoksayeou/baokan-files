Title: Secretary-General of the Bolivian Miners' Union, Torres, Leaves Beijing for Home
Author: 
Date: 1960-08-25
Page: 5
    Xinhua News Agency, August 20th—Mario Torres Calleja, Secretary-General of the Bolivian Miners' Union, who came to China for a visit at the invitation of the National Committee of the Chinese Coal Miners' Union, left Beijing by plane on the morning of the 19th to return home.

### 玻矿工联合会总书记  托莱斯离京回国

1960-08-25
第5版()
专栏：

    玻矿工联合会总书记
    托莱斯离京回国
    新华社20日讯　应中国煤矿工会全国委员会邀请，前来我国访问的玻利维亚矿工联合会总书记马里奥·托莱斯·卡耶哈，在19日上午乘飞机离开北京回国。
