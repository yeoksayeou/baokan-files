Title: Resolutely Oppose Imperialism to Defend Peace: Preparatory Meeting of the World Conference Against Atomic and Hydrogen Bombs Holds Group Discussions
Author:
Date: 1960-08-05
Page: 5
Xinhua News Agency, Tokyo, August 4th: The preparatory meeting of the Sixth World Conference Against Atomic and Hydrogen Bombs was divided into two groups on the 3rd. They held enthusiastic and in-depth discussions on defending world peace through opposing arms expansion and war preparations, prohibiting nuclear weapons, striving for disarmament, and realizing peaceful coexistence.
    A total of 140 people, including representatives from various countries and Japanese representatives, attended the first group meeting. Sixty people, including representatives from various countries and Japanese representatives, attended the second group meeting.
    On the morning of the 4th, the third group began discussing "How can we further promote the anti-nuclear weapons movement and the movement to aid atomic bomb victims?"
    After the group discussions, a plenary session of the preparatory meeting will be held starting this afternoon.

### 坚决反对帝国主义才能保卫和平  禁止原子弹氢弹世界大会预备会议进行分组讨论

1960-08-05
第5版()
专栏：

    坚决反对帝国主义才能保卫和平
    禁止原子弹氢弹世界大会预备会议进行分组讨论
    新华社4日讯　东京消息：第六届禁止原子弹和争取全面裁军世界大会预备会议在3日分成两组，热烈、深入地讨论了反对扩军备战、禁止核武器、争取裁军和实现和平共处等保卫世界和平的问题。
    出席第一分组会议的有来自各国的代表和日本代表共一百四十人。出席第二分组会议的有各国和日本代表六十人。
    4日上午，第三分组开始讨论“怎样才能更加促进反对核武器运动和救济原子弹受害者运动？”
    分组讨论结束后，将从今天下午开始举行预备会议全体会议。
