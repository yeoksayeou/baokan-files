Title: "Old Never-Satisfied" - One of the Heroes of the Mine
Author: Yan Yi
Date: 1960-08-18
Page: 8

"Old Never-Satisfied"
——One of the Heroes of the Mine
Yan Yi

I
　　Since you were ten years old, you came here,
　　Carrying baskets of coal, bending your back,
　　The coal was heavier than your body,
　　You used all your hands and feet together.
　　Climb, climb, climb,
　　Straight waist bent over,
　　Your sweat, your blood, your strength,
　　Filled the capitalist's belly.
　　The capitalist was still not satisfied,
　　Brought over a donkey,
　　The donkey carried coal, and you carried coal,
　　You led the donkey, and the donkey led you.
　　You and the donkey ate and slept together,
　　The donkey was you, and you were the donkey,
　　Climb, climb, climb,
　　Going back and forth in the deep well.
　　Climb, climb, climb,
　　Thirty years of time passed like this,
　　You, this ten-year-old urchin,
　　Grew a white beard.

II
　　With a booming sound, the cannon of liberation rang,
　　You straightened your back and greeted the red flag,
　　From then on, you and the donkey were distinguished,
　　You are a person, no longer a donkey!
　　You became the master of the mine,
　　Labor is no longer for the sake of the stomach,
　　You built a rubber-wheeled cart,
　　And shaved off your white beard.
　　Head held high, waving the whip,
　　Chest out, singing a little tune,
　　Holding the cart, driving the horse,
　　Life is full of poetry.
　　You, you old never-satisfied,
　　You said that such output is too low,
　　On the night of your wedding,
　　You were indifferent to your beloved wife.
　　You treated the white tin wine pot as a horse,
　　You treated the four dishes as carriages,
　　You used chopsticks to form the tracks,
　　You said, this is the best wedding.

III
　　Now you sit in the driver's seat,
　　Become the driver of the battery car,
　　Like riding a steed,
　　Man not off the saddle, horse doesn't stop.
　　You, beard unintentionally shaved,
　　Often murmuring in your dreams,
　　Your wife says, "You old never-satisfied,
　　What are you getting angry about again?"
　　You said you are not satisfied with yourself,
　　Such output is too low, too low,
　　The Iron Marshal is in a hurry for supplies,
　　How can you not be anxious.
　　You said you want to modify the machine,
　　To increase the horsepower of the locomotive,
　　Your wife said, "Are you crazy?
　　How can modifying the machine be you?"
　　After speaking, she brought paper and pen,
　　And handed the compass to you,
　　You, old never-satisfied,
　　Hugged your wife tightly.

IV
　　You drive the modified locomotive,
　　The carriages are half a mile long,
　　But you, still on the locomotive,
　　Thinking of even better ideas.
　　At a Communist Youth League meeting,
　　You revealed your inner secrets:
　　"In the past, I was a donkey,
　　Now, I have become a driver!
　　"Some people call me 'old never-satisfied' again,
　　Being satisfied means no progress,
　　Thinking of the happiness of future generations,
　　My whole body is filled with inexhaustible strength!
　　"I know my culture is too low,
　　I can't think of a better machine,
　　I believe in the intelligent successors,
　　I believe in you, my children!
　　"Yes, I am an old never-satisfied,
　　Fantasizing that coal can automatically flow out of the earth,
　　Because in my heart,
　　Is filled with communism!"

August 7, 1960, at Zhongliangshan Coal Mine

### “老不知足”——矿山英雄谱之一
雁翼
1960-08-18
第8版()
专栏：

    “老不知足”
    ——矿山英雄谱之一
    雁翼
    一
    从十岁你就来到这里，
    一篓篓煤炭弯腰驮起，
    煤炭比你的身体还重啊，
    你只有手和脚一齐用力。
    爬呀，爬呀，爬呀，
    笔直的腰身压成弯曲，
    你的汗，你的血，你的力，
    填进了资本家的肚皮。
    资本家还不满意，
    牵来了一头毛驴，
    毛驴驮着煤你也驮着煤，
    你牵着驴呀，驴牵着你。
    你和驴吃睡在一起，
    驴就是你呀，你就是驴，
    爬呀，爬呀，爬呀，
    在深深的井下来来去去。
    爬呀，爬呀，爬呀，
    三十年的光阴就这样过去，
    你这个十岁的顽童，
    长出了花白的胡须。
    二
    轰隆一声解放的炮响，
    你直起腰来，迎着红旗，
    从此你和驴区别开来，
    你是人呀，再也不是驴！
    你成了矿山的主人，
    劳动再不是为了肚皮，
    你造了一辆胶轱辘大车，
    也剃掉了花白的胡须。
    昂着头摇着鞭儿，
    挺着胸唱着小曲，
    扶着车，赶着马，
    生活充满了诗意。
    你呀，你这个老不知足，
    你说这样的产量太低，
    在你结婚的那天夜里，
    你冷淡了你的爱妻。
    你把白锡酒壶当作马，
    你把四个菜盘当车皮，
    你用筷子摆成了轨道，
    你说呀，这是最好的婚礼。
    三
    现在你坐在驾驶台上，
    成了电瓶列车的司机，
    像骑着一匹骏马，
    人不下鞍，马不停蹄。
    你呀，胡子无心修剃，
    梦里也常常自语，
    老伴说“你这个老不知足，
    又为啥子事情生气？”
    你说你不满意自己，
    这样的产量太低太低，
    钢铁元帅急着要粮草，
    你怎能不着急。
    你说你要改装机器，
    要加大机车的马力，
    老伴说“你疯了吗？
    改造机器怎能是你？”
    说罢拿来了纸和笔，
    又把圆规递在你手里，
    你呀，老不知足，
    把老伴猛抱在怀里。
    四
    你驾驶着改装的机车，
    车皮拉长了半里，
    可是你呀，仍在机车上，
    想着更妙的主意。
    在一次共青团会上，
    你说出了内心的秘密：
    “过去呀，我是条驴，
    现在呀，我成了司机！
    “有人又叫我‘老不知足’，
    知足了就没有出息，
    想起子孙万代的幸福，
    浑身就有了用不完的力气！
    “我知道我的文化太低，
    不能想出更妙的机器，
    我相信聪明的接班人呀，
    我相信你们呀，我的子女！
    “是呀，我是个老不知足，
    幻想着煤能自动流出大地，
    因为在我的心底呀，
    装着一个共产主义！”
    1960年8月7日于中梁山煤矿
