Title: Premier Zhou and Foreign Minister Chen Send Congratulatory Telegrams on the Independence of the Republic of Ivory Coast; I Recognize the Republic of Ivory Coast
Author:
Date: 1960-08-07
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, 6th. Premier Zhou Enlai of the People's Republic of China today sent a telegram to President Houphouët-Boigny of the Republic of Ivory Coast, congratulating the Republic of Ivory Coast on its declaration of independence. The telegram reads as follows:

To His Excellency Félix Houphouët-Boigny, President of the Republic of Ivory Coast, Abidjan, Ivory Coast:

On the occasion of the declaration of independence of the Republic of Ivory Coast, I would like to extend my sincere congratulations to the government and people of the Republic of Ivory Coast on behalf of the government and people of the People's Republic of China. I wish the people of Ivory Coast further achievements in their struggle to safeguard national independence and oppose imperialism and colonialism. I wish your country prosperity and your people happiness.

	Zhou Enlai
	Premier of the State Council of the People's Republic of China
	August 6, 1960

Xinhua News Agency, 6th. Chen Yi, Minister of Foreign Affairs of the People's Republic of China, today sent a telegram to President Houphouët-Boigny of the Republic of Ivory Coast, congratulating Ivory Coast on its declaration of independence and informing him that the Chinese government has decided to recognize the Republic of Ivory Coast. The telegram reads as follows:

To His Excellency Félix Houphouët-Boigny, President of the Republic of Ivory Coast, Abidjan, Ivory Coast:

I am pleased to learn that the Republic of Ivory Coast has declared its independence, and I would like to extend my heartfelt congratulations on behalf of the government of the People's Republic of China. I am honored to notify Your Excellency that the government of the People's Republic of China has decided to recognize The Republic of Ivory Coast. I hope that our government's recognition of your country will lead to further development of relations between our two countries.

	Chen Yi
	Minister of Foreign Affairs of the People's Republic of China
	August 6, 1960

### 周总理陈外长电贺象牙海岸共和国独立  我承认象牙海岸共和国

1960-08-07
第1版()
专栏：

    周总理陈外长电贺象牙海岸共和国独立
    我承认象牙海岸共和国
    新华社6日讯　中华人民共和国国务院总理周恩来今天打电报给象牙海岸共和国总统乌弗埃—博瓦尼，祝贺象牙海岸共和国宣告独立。电文如下：象牙海岸　阿比让象牙海岸共和国总统费利克斯·乌弗埃—博瓦尼先生阁下：
    在此象牙海岸共和国宣告独立的时候，我谨代表中华人民共和国政府和人民，向象牙海岸共和国政府和人民致以诚挚的祝贺。祝象牙海岸人民在维护民族独立、反对帝国主义和殖民主义的斗争中取得进一步的成就。祝贵国国家繁荣，人民幸福。
          中华人民共和国国务院总理　周恩来
                                1960年8月6日
    新华社6日讯　中华人民共和国外交部长陈毅今天打电报给象牙海岸共和国总统乌弗埃—博瓦尼，祝贺象牙海岸宣告独立，并通知他，中国政府决定承认象牙海岸共和国。电文如下：象牙海岸　阿比让象牙海岸共和国总统费利克斯·乌弗埃—博瓦尼先生
  阁下：
    欣悉象牙海岸共和国宣告独立，我谨代表中华人民共和国政府致以衷心的祝贺。我荣幸地通知阁下，中华人民共和国政府已经决定承认象牙海岸共和国。我希望，我国政府对贵国的承认将导致我们两国关系的进一步发展。
              中华人民共和国外交部长　陈　毅
                                1960年8月6日
