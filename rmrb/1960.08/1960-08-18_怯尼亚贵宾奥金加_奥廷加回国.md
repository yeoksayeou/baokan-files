Title: Kenyan Guest Oginga Odinga Returns Home
Author:
Date: 1960-08-18
Page: 6

    Kenyan Guest Oginga Odinga Returns Home
    Xinhua News Agency, 17th - Oginga Odinga, Vice President of the Kenya African National Union, and his assistant, Wera Ambiso, Secretary of the Kenyan Office in Cairo, who came to our country at the invitation of the Chinese Committee for Afro-Asian Solidarity, concluded their visit to our country and left Beijing by plane on the 16th to return home.
    Those who saw them off at the airport included Liao Chengzhi, Chairman of the Chinese Committee for Afro-Asian Solidarity, Zhu Ziqi, Secretary-General, Liu Changsheng, President of the Chinese-African People's Friendship Association, and Wu Qing, Director-General, among others.

### 怯尼亚贵宾奥金加·奥廷加回国

1960-08-18
第6版()
专栏：

    怯尼亚贵宾奥金加·奥廷加回国
    新华社17日讯　应中国亚非团结委员会邀请，前来我国访问的怯尼亚非洲民族联盟副主席奥金加·奥廷加和他的助手怯尼亚驻开罗办事处秘书韦腊·安比梭，结束了在我国的访问，在16日乘飞机离北京回国。
    到机场欢送的，有中国亚非团结委员会主席廖承志、秘书长朱子奇，中国非洲人民友好协会会长刘长胜、总干事吴青等人。
