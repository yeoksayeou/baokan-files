Title: Premier Zhou and Foreign Minister Chen Send Telegrams to Congratulate the Independence of the Republic of Cyprus; China Recognizes the Republic of Cyprus
Author:
Date: 1960-08-16
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, August 15th -- Premier Zhou Enlai of the People's Republic of China sent a telegram today to Archbishop Makarios III, President of the Republic of Cyprus, congratulating Cyprus on its declaration of independence. The text of the telegram is as follows:
Cyprus, Nicosia
His Excellency Archbishop Makarios III, President of the Republic of Cyprus:

    I am pleased to learn of the declaration of independence of Cyprus. On behalf of the Government and people of the People's Republic of China, I extend my heartfelt congratulations to Your Excellency and, through Your Excellency, to the Government and people of the Republic of Cyprus. I wish the Cypriot people further achievements in their struggle to strengthen national unity and uphold national independence, and I wish your country prosperity and your people happiness.
            Zhou Enlai
            Premier of the State Council of the People's Republic of China
                          August 15, 1960, Beijing

Xinhua News Agency, August 15th -- Chen Yi, Minister of Foreign Affairs of the People's Republic of China, sent a telegram today to Nikos Kranidiotis, Minister of Foreign Affairs of the Republic of Cyprus, congratulating Cyprus on its declaration of independence and notifying him that the Chinese government has decided to recognize the Republic of Cyprus. The text of the telegram is as follows:
Cyprus, Nicosia
His Excellency Nikos Kranidiotis, Minister of Foreign Affairs of the Republic of Cyprus:

    On the occasion of the declaration of independence of Cyprus, I extend my sincere congratulations to the Government of the Republic of Cyprus on behalf of the Government of the People's Republic of China. I am honored to notify Your Excellency that the Government of the People's Republic of China has decided to recognize the Republic of Cyprus. I hope that my government's recognition of your country will help to further develop the friendly relations between China and Cyprus.

              Chen Yi
              Minister of Foreign Affiars of the People's Republic of China
                          August 15, 1960, Beijing

### 周总理陈外长电贺塞浦路斯共和国独立  我承认塞浦路斯共和国

1960-08-16
第1版()
专栏：

    周总理陈外长电贺塞浦路斯共和国独立
    我承认塞浦路斯共和国
    新华社15日讯　中华人民共和国总理周恩来今天打电报给塞浦路斯共和国总统大主教马卡里奥斯三世，祝贺塞浦路斯宣告独立。电文如下：塞浦路斯　尼科西亚塞浦路斯共和国总统大主教马卡里奥斯三世阁下：
    欣闻塞浦路斯宣告独立，我谨代表中华人民共和国政府和人民，向阁下并通过阁下向塞浦路斯共和国政府和人民致以衷心的祝贺。祝塞浦路斯人民在增强民族团结和坚持民族独立的斗争中取得进一步的成就，并祝贵国国家繁荣，人民幸福。
            中华人民共和国国务院总理　周恩来
                          1960年8月15日于北京
    新华社15日讯　中华人民共和国外交部长陈毅今天打电报给塞浦路斯共和国外交部长尼科斯·克拉尼迪奥蒂斯，祝贺塞浦路斯宣告独立，并通知他中国政府决定承认塞浦路斯共和国。电文如下：塞浦路斯　尼科西亚塞浦路斯共和国外交部长尼科斯·克拉尼迪奥蒂斯先生阁下：
    值此塞浦路斯宣告独立的时候，我谨代表中华人民共和国政府向塞浦路斯共和国政府致以诚挚的祝贺。我荣幸地通知阁下，中华人民共和国政府已经决定承认塞浦路斯共和国。我希望，我国政府对贵国的承认将有助于中、塞两国友好关系的进一步发展。
              中华人民共和国外交部长　陈毅
                          1960年8月15日于北京
