Title: African Foreign Ministers' Conference Adopts Agenda, Decides to Discuss Situation in Congo and Aid to Congo
Author:
Date: 1960-08-29
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, Leopoldville, August 28th: The heads of the delegations of the African Foreign Ministers' Conference held a closed-door meeting on the morning of the 27th and adopted the conference agenda. According to a communique issued after the meeting, the agenda adopted by the conference includes studying the situation in the Congo, African countries' aid to the Congo, cooperation between African countries, and preparations for the African Heads of State Conference.

The communique stated that the conference will pay particular attention to the situation in the Congo.

### 非洲外长会议通过议程  决定讨论刚果局势和对刚果的援助

1960-08-29
第6版()
专栏：

    非洲外长会议通过议程
    决定讨论刚果局势和对刚果的援助
    新华社28日讯　利奥波德维尔消息：非洲国家外长会议各代表团团长27日上午举行秘密会议，通过了会议的议程。根据会后发表的公报，会议通过的议程包括研究刚果局势、非洲国家对刚果的援助、非洲国家之间的合作，和筹备非洲国家首脑会议等。
    公报说，这次会议将特别注意刚果局势。
