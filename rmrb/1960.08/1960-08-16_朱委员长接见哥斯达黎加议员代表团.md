Title: Chairman Zhu Receives Delegation of Costa Rican Parliamentarians
Author:
Date: 1960-08-16
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, August 15th: Zhu De, Chairman of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress, received the entire delegation of Costa Rican parliamentarians visiting China this morning and had friendly talks with them.

    The members of the Costa Rican parliamentary delegation included: parliamentarian Marcial Aguiluz and his wife, parliamentarian José Rafael Cordero and his wife, parliamentarian Noel Hernández and his wife, parliamentarian Néstor López Gutiérrez, parliamentarian José Ángel Jara, parliamentarian César O. Solano, and journalist Julio César Zunol.

    Present at the reception were Zhang Su and Wu Xinyu, members of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress; Lian Guan, Deputy Secretary-General; Hu Yuzhi, Vice President of the Chinese People's Institute of Foreign Affairs; and Wu Xiaoda, Deputy Secretary-General. Shen Jian, Director of the Department of American and Australian Affairs of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs, was also present.

### 朱委员长接见哥斯达黎加议员代表团

1960-08-16
第1版()
专栏：

    朱委员长接见哥斯达黎加议员代表团
    新华社15日讯　全国人民代表大会常务委员会委员长朱德今天上午接见了正在我国访问的哥斯达黎加议员代表团全体成员，并进行了友好的交谈。
    哥斯达黎加议员代表团的成员包括：议员马西亚尔·阿吉卢斯和夫人，议员何塞·拉斐尔·科尔德罗和夫人，议员诺埃尔·埃尔南台斯和夫人，议员内斯托尔·洛佩兹·古铁雷斯，议员何塞·安赫尔·哈拉，议员赛萨·奥·索拉诺，以及记者胡利奥·赛萨·苏诺耳。
    接见时在座的有：全国人民代表大会常务委员会委员张苏、武新宇，副秘书长连贯；中国人民外交学会副会长胡愈之，副秘书长吴晓达。外交部美澳司司长申健也在座。
