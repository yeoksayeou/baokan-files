Title: Delegation of Japanese General Council of Trade Unions Youth and Women Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1960-08-17
Page: 7

    Delegation of Japanese General Council of Trade Unions Youth and Women Arrives in Beijing

    Xinhua News Agency, August 16th—The delegation of the Japanese General Council of Trade Unions Youth and Women, consisting of fourteen members led by head of the delegation Shigeru Tanabashi, arrived in Beijing by train on the afternoon of August 16th for a visit to our country at the invitation of the All-China Youth Federation.

### 日本总评青年与妇女代表团抵京

1960-08-17
第7版()
专栏：

    日本总评青年与妇女代表团抵京
    据新华社16日讯　应中华全国青年联合会邀请来我国访问的日本总评青年与妇女代表团一行十四人，在团长种桥茂率领下，已于8月16日下午乘火车抵达北京。
