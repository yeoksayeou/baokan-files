Title: Mongolian People's Army Holds Evening Party to Celebrate the Anniversary of the Founding of the Chinese Army; Chinese Military Attaches in India, Burma, and Other Countries Hold "August 1st" Reception; Chinese Embassy in Albania Holds Film Reception
Author:
Date: 1960-08-04
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, Ulaanbaatar, August 3rd: According to a report in the "Red Star Newspaper" (published on the 3rd), the newspaper of the Ministry of People's Army Affairs and the Ministry of Public Security of Mongolia, the troops of the Mongolian People's Army stationed in the capital held an evening party on the evening of July 30 to celebrate the 33rd anniversary of the founding of the Chinese People's Liberation Army.
    Mongolian People's Army Colonel Danzan, Lieutenant Colonel Chaodun, and Lieutenant Colonel Nasujile gave reports at the meeting on the fighting experience of the Chinese People's Liberation Army. In their reports, they all talked about the birth and development of the Chinese People's Liberation Army and its contributions to the cause of peaceful construction.
    Lieutenant Colonel Chaodun said that since the day it was born, the Chinese People's Liberation Army has fought heroically for the happiness and liberation of the Chinese people under the leadership of its glorious Communist Party. The Chinese People's Liberation Army overcame difficulties such as poor weapons and equipment and the Long March, and made great contributions. For thirty-three years, it has shown infinite loyalty to the Communist Party and the people.
    Lieutenant Colonel Chaodun said: "The Chinese People's Liberation Army is not only the heroic defender of the Chinese people, but also an internationalist force that is watched and loved by progressive people all over the world."
    Xinhua News Agency, August 2nd: Military attachés of our country stationed in India, Burma, Indonesia, the United Arab Republic, and Iraq held receptions in the capitals of their respective host countries on August 1 to celebrate the 33rd anniversary of the founding of the Chinese People's Liberation Army.
    Among those who attended the reception held by Lieutenant Colonel Xu Jiguang, acting military attaché of the Chinese Embassy in India, were Indian Defense Minister Krishna Menon, Secretary-General of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs Pillai, senior generals of the three armed forces, and prominent figures. Pan Zili, Chinese Ambassador to India, also attended the reception.
    Among those who attended the reception held by Colonel Jiang Keding, military attaché of the Chinese Embassy in Burma, were Brigadier General (Air Force) Clifton, Deputy Chief of Staff of the Burmese Defense Forces, Colonel Ji Mao, Colonel Babar (Navy), Colonel Deng Mao, Colonel Deng Han, and senior government officials.
    Among those who attended the reception held by Colonel Mao Chen, military attaché of the Chinese Embassy in Indonesia, were Brigadier General Abrachmat, First Assistant Minister of the Indonesian Air Force, Colonel Kritato, Military Secretary to the President, Colonel Mokochinta, Assistant Minister of National Security, Colonel Mursjid, Director of the Army Operations Department, and Colonel Sudamon, Assistant Chief of Staff of the Navy.
    Also present at the reception were Jakarta Mayor Sumarno, Susanto, Acting Secretary-General of the Indonesian Ministry of Foreign Affairs, Indonesian Communist Party leaders Aidit, Yudo, Lukman, Sutisman, Civilian Party Chairman Sugani, as well as members of parliament and members of the Supreme Advisory Council.
    Huang Zhen, Chinese Ambassador to Indonesia, and Colonel Chen Jianwu, the new military attaché, also attended the reception. Chen Jianwu will succeed Colonel Mao Chen as military attaché.
    About 150 guests attended the reception held by Colonel Li Dazhong, military attaché of the Chinese Embassy in the United Arab Republic. Among them was Lieutenant General Afifi, Chief of Staff of the Air Force of the United Arab Republic.
    Qasim, Prime Minister and Minister of Defense and Commander-in-Chief of the Armed Forces of Iraq, attended the reception held by Lieutenant Colonel Xu Yimin, military attaché of the Chinese Embassy in Iraq.
    In addition, the military attachés of the Chinese Embassy in Sweden and the Chinese Embassy in Denmark also held receptions respectively.
    Xinhua News Agency, Tirana, August 2nd: The Chinese Embassy in Albania held a film reception at the Tirana Palace of Culture tonight to celebrate the 33rd anniversary of the founding of the Chinese People's Liberation Army.
    At the reception, the Chinese art film "Thousands of Rivers and Mountains" and the documentary "Storm Over Asia" were shown. Among those who attended the film reception were Lieutenant General Bektashi, Director of the Political Department of the Albanian People's Army, other generals, and Butun, Deputy Minister of Foreign Affairs of Albania.

### 蒙古人民军举行晚会庆祝我建军节  我国驻印缅等国武官举行“八一”节招待会  我国驻阿尔巴尼亚使馆举行电影招待会

1960-08-04
第6版()
专栏：

    蒙古人民军举行晚会庆祝我建军节
    我国驻印缅等国武官举行“八一”节招待会  我国驻阿尔巴尼亚使馆举行电影招待会
    新华社乌兰巴托3日电　据蒙古人民军事务部和公安部机关报“红星报”（3日刊）今天报道，蒙古人民军驻首都部队7月30日晚举行晚会，庆祝中国人民解放军建军三十三周年。
    蒙古人民军上校丹赞、中校朝敦和中校那术吉勒分别在会上作了有关中国人民解放军斗争经历的报告。他们在报告中都谈到中国人民解放军的诞生和发展以及它对和平建设事业所建立的功勋。
    朝敦中校说，中国人民解放军从诞生的那天起，就在自己的光荣的共产党的领导下为中国人民的幸福和解放而英勇斗争。中国人民解放军克服了武器装备不良和长征等困难，建立了功勋。三十三年来，它对共产党和人民表现了无限的忠诚。
    朝敦中校说：“中国人民解放军不仅是中国人民的英勇的捍卫者，也是得到全世界进步人类注视和爱戴的一支国际主义部队。”
    据新华社2日讯　我国驻印度、缅甸、印度尼西亚、阿拉伯联合共和国和伊拉克的武官，8月1日分别在各驻在国的首都举行招待会，庆祝中国人民解放军建军三十三周年。
    出席我国驻印度大使馆代理武官徐寄光中校举行的招待会的，有印度国防部长克里希纳·梅农、外交部秘书长皮莱、三军高级将领和著名人士。我国驻印度大使潘自力也出席了招待会。
    出席我国驻缅甸大使馆武官蒋克定上校举行的招待会的，有缅甸国防军副总参谋长克利夫特准将（空军）、季貌上校、巴巴尔上校（海军）、登貌上校、登汉上校和政府高级官员。
    出席我国驻印度尼西亚大使馆武官茅琛上校举行的招待会的有印度尼西亚空军部第一助理部长阿布腊赫马特准将、总统军事秘书克里塔托上校、国家安全部长助理莫科琴塔上校、陆军作战处处长穆尔斯吉德上校、海军参谋长助理苏达蒙上校等。
    出席招待会的还有雅加达市长苏马诺、印度尼西亚外交部代理秘书长苏桑托、印度尼西亚共产党领导人艾地、约多、鲁克曼、苏蒂斯曼、平民党主席苏加尼以及国会议员和最高评议院委员。
    我国驻印度尼西亚大使黄镇和新任武官陈建吾上校也出席了招待会。陈建吾即将接替茅琛上校担任武官。
    出席我国驻阿联大使馆武官李大中大校举行的招待会的约有一百五十位客人。其中有阿联空军参谋长阿菲菲中将。
    伊拉克总理兼国防部长和武装部队总司令卡塞姆，出席了我国驻伊拉克大使馆武官徐毅民中校举行的招待会。
    此外，我国驻瑞典大使馆和驻丹麦大使馆的武官也分别举行了招待会。
    据新华社地拉那2日电　中国驻阿尔巴尼亚大使馆今晚为庆祝中国人民解放军建军三十三周年在地拉那文化宫举行了电影招待会。
    在招待会上，放映了中国艺术片“万水千山”和纪录片“亚洲风暴”。出席电影招待会的有阿尔巴尼亚人民军政治部主任别克特希中将和其他将领，以及阿尔巴尼亚外交部副部长布敦。
