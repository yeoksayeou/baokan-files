Title: U.S. Government Intensifies "Cold War," Again Demands UN General Assembly Discuss So-Called "Hungarian Question"
Author:
Date: 1960-08-24
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, New York, August 23rd: The United States government has taken another step to poison the international atmosphere -- demanding that the so-called "Hungarian question" be placed again on the agenda of the Fifteenth Session of the United Nations General Assembly to be held in mid-September this year.

U.S. Permanent Representative to the United Nations Lodge made this request in a letter and a memorandum to United Nations Secretary-General Hammarskjöld on the 22nd. Lodge absurdly accused the Soviet Union and the Hungarian government of refusing to implement the resolution on interfering in Hungary's internal affairs illegally passed by the Fourteenth Session of the United Nations General Assembly under the manipulation of the United States, and used this as an excuse to demand that the Fifteenth Session of the United Nations General Assembly discuss this issue again.

### 美国政府加紧进行“冷战”  又要求联大讨论所谓“匈牙利问题”

1960-08-24
第5版()
专栏：

    美国政府加紧进行“冷战”
    又要求联大讨论所谓“匈牙利问题”
    新华社23日讯　纽约消息：美国政府又采取了一个毒化国际气氛的步骤——要求再次把所谓“匈牙利问题”列入将在今年9月中旬举行的联合国大会第十五届会议的议程。
    美国常驻联合国代表洛奇22日在给联合国秘书长哈马舍尔德的一封信件和一个备忘录中提出了这个要求。洛奇荒谬地指责苏联和匈牙利政府拒绝执行联合国大会第十四届会议在美国的操纵下非法通过的干涉匈牙利内政的决议，并且以此作为借口，要求联合国大会第十五届会议再次讨论这个问题。
