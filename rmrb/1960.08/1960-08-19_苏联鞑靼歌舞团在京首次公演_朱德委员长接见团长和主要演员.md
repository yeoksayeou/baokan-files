Title: Soviet Tatar Song and Dance Ensemble's First Performance in Beijing; Chairman Zhu De Receives the Head of the Ensemble and Principal Performers
Author:
Date: 1960-08-19
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, August 18th: The Soviet Tatar Autonomous Republic State Song and Dance Ensemble gave its first performance at the Tianqiao Theater tonight. Beijing audiences welcomed the beautiful song and dance art and the friendship of the Soviet people with brotherly affection and warm applause.

Zhu De, Chairman of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress, watched the ensemble's wonderful performance.

Tonight's program began with the ensemble's chorus "Ode to Lenin" and ended with the "Friendship Suite" performed by all the artists. A total of thirty-one programs including Tatar, Russian, and Chinese folk songs, dances, and choruses were performed. The artists' chorus of "To Chinese Friends," "The East is Red," "My Republic," and "Knight's Dance," as well as all the other programs, were warmly welcomed by the audience.

During the intermission, Chairman Zhu De received the head of the ensemble, B.M. Gizzatullin, and other leaders and principal performers of the troupe, and had a cordial conversation with them.

Soviet Ambassador to China, Chervonenko, also watched tonight's performance. Among those who watched the performance were Minister of Culture Shen Yanbing, Vice Chairman of the Commission for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries Zhang Zhixiang, Deputy Secretary-General of the Sino-Soviet Friendship Association, Ge Baoquan, Vice Minister of Foreign Affairs Ji Pengfei, and people from the capital's literary and art circles.

After the entire performance, Zhu De, Shen Yanbing, Zhang Zhixiang, Ge Baoquan, and Ji Pengfei stepped onto the stage to shake hands with the artists of the song and dance ensemble, congratulating them on their successful performance. (Attached picture)

### 苏联鞑靼歌舞团在京首次公演  朱德委员长接见团长和主要演员

1960-08-19
第6版()
专栏：

    苏联鞑靼歌舞团在京首次公演
    朱德委员长接见团长和主要演员
    新华社18日讯　苏联鞑靼自治共和国国家歌舞团今晚在天桥剧场举行首次公演。北京观众以兄弟般的情谊和热烈的掌声，欢迎歌舞团带来的优美歌舞艺术和苏联人民的友谊。
    全国人民代表大会常务委员会委员长朱德观看了歌舞团精彩的演出。
    今晚的节目以歌舞团的合唱“列宁颂”开始，到全体艺术家们演出的“友谊组舞”结束，共演出鞑靼、俄罗斯、中国民歌、舞蹈、合唱等三十一个节目，艺术家们合唱的“致中国朋友”、“东方红”、“我的共和国”和“骑士舞”，以及演出的所有节目，都受到观众热烈的欢迎。
    在演出休息期间，朱德委员长接见了布·米·吉柴图林团长等领导人和艺术团的主要演员，并且进行了亲切的谈话。
    苏联驻中国大使契尔沃年科也观看了今晚的演出。观看演出的，还有文化部部长沈雁冰，对外文委副主任张致祥，中苏友好协会总会副秘书长戈宝权，外交部副部长姬鹏飞，和首都文艺界人士等。
    演出全部结束后，朱德、沈雁冰、张致祥、戈宝权和姬鹏飞等步上舞台同歌舞团的艺术家们握手，祝贺他们成功的演出。（附图片）
    苏联鞑靼自治共和国国家歌舞团在天桥剧场举行首次公演结束后，朱德委员长等和歌舞团的艺术家们合影。前排右第一人是布·米·吉柴图团长，第二人是苏联驻中国大使契尔沃年科。　新华社记者　张彬摄
