Title: Delegation of the Federation of Black African Students in France Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1960-08-01
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, 31st. The delegation of the Federation of Black African Students in France, led by head Dico Amadou Abdoul, arrived in Beijing by plane today at the invitation of the All-China Students' Federation for a visit to our country.
	Zhang Yin'an, vice chairman of the All-China Students' Federation, and others were at the airport to greet them.

### 黑非洲留法学生联合会代表团抵京

1960-08-01
第6版()
专栏：

    黑非洲留法学生联合会代表团抵京
    新华社31日讯　应中华全国学生联合会邀请来我国访问的黑非洲留法学生联合会代表团一行九人，在团长迪柯·阿马杜·阿卜杜勒率领下，已于今天乘飞机抵达北京。
    到机场迎接的有中华全国学生联合会副主席张寅安等人。
