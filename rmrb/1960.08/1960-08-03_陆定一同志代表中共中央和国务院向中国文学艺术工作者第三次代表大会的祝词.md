Title: Comrade Lu Dingyi's Congratulatory Speech on Behalf of the CCP Central Committee and the State Council to the Third Congress of Chinese Literary and Art Workers
Author:
Date: 1960-08-03
Page: 7

    In order to carry out our country's socialist revolution on the economic, political, ideological, and cultural fronts to the end and achieve complete victory, in order to build our country into a powerful socialist country with modern industry, modern agriculture, and a modern science and culture, and to prepare the material and spiritual conditions for entering a communist society in the future, we still have to make long-term and arduous efforts. Our literary and art workers must play an important role in the struggle to achieve these tasks.
  　——Comrade Lu Dingyi's Congratulatory Speech on Behalf of the CCP Central Committee and the State Council to the Third Congress of Chinese Literary and Art Workers

### 陆定一同志代表中共中央和国务院向中国文学艺术工作者第三次代表大会的祝词

1960-08-03
第7版()
专栏：

    为了把我国的社会主义革命在经济战线上、政治战线上、思想战线上和文化战线上进行到底，取得彻底的胜利，为了把我国建设成为一个具有现代工业、现代农业和现代科学文化的强大的社会主义国家，并且为将来进入共产主义社会准备物质的和精神的条件，我们还要进行长期的艰巨的努力。我国的文学艺术工作者，一定要在实现这些任务的斗争中发挥自己的重要作用。
  　——陆定一同志代表中共中央和国务院向中国文学艺术工作者第三次代表大会的祝词
