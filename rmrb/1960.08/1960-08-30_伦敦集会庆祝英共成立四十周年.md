Title: London Rally Celebrates Fortieth Anniversary of the Founding of the British Communist Party
Author:
Date: 1960-08-30
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, London, August 28th: A celebration meeting for the fortieth anniversary of the founding of the British Communist Party was held here at St. Pancras Town Hall tonight.

　　The meeting was presided over by the Central Committee of the British Communist Party. 1,200 people attended the meeting, including 138 veteran party members who participated in the founding of the party, representatives from party organizations across Britain, and members of the British Communist Youth League.

　　General Secretary of the British Communist Party, Gollan, spoke at the meeting.

　　Vice-Chairman of the British Communist Party, Dutt, read out congratulatory messages from the Soviet Union, the People's Republic of China, France, Australia, India, Spain and South Africa.

　　Honorary Chairman of the British Communist Party and member of the Party's Executive Committee, Gallacher, also spoke at the meeting.

　　Today, a photo exhibition reflecting the past forty years of struggle of the British Communist Party was also held at St. Pancras Town Hall.

### 伦敦集会庆祝英共成立四十周年

1960-08-30
第6版()
专栏：

    伦敦集会庆祝英共成立四十周年
    新华社伦敦28日电　今天晚上在这里的圣潘克拉斯市政厅举行了英国共产党成立四十周年庆祝大会。
    大会由英国共产党中央委员会主持。参加大会的有一千二百人，其中包括参加建党的一百三十八个老党员、英国各地党组织的代表和英国共产主义青年团的团员。
    英国共产党总书记高兰在会上讲了话。
    英国共产党副主席杜德在会上宣读了来自苏联、中华人民共和国、法国、澳大利亚、印度、西班牙和南非的贺电。
    英国共产党名誉主席、党的执行委员会委员加拉赫也在会上讲了话。
    今天在圣潘克拉斯市政厅还举行了反映英国共产党过去四十年来的斗争情况的图片展览。
