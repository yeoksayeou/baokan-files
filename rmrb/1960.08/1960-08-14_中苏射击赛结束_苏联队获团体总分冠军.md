Title: China-Soviet Shooting Competition Concludes; Soviet Team Wins Overall Team Championship
Author:
Date: 1960-08-14
Page: 6
Xinhua News Agency, Moscow - The six-day shooting competition between the Chinese People's National Defense Sports Association Shooting Team and the Soviet Voluntary Society for Assistance to the Army, Navy, and Air Force Shooting Team concluded here on the 5th. The Soviet team won the overall team championship. In the ten events, the Chinese team won three team championships and two individual championships. The Soviet team won seven team championships, for a total of seventeen points. The Chinese team won a total of thirteen points. In the two women's events, the Chinese team won the team championships for the 50-meter small-bore rifle prone shooting with 60 shots and the 50-meter small-bore rifle 3x20.
    In the eight men's events, the Chinese team won the team championship in rapid-fire pistol (2281), and Dong Guangrong of the Chinese team won the individual championship in the 50-meter small-bore rifle kneeling position (386).

### 中苏射击赛结束  苏联队获团体总分冠军

1960-08-14
第6版()
专栏：

    中苏射击赛结束
    苏联队获团体总分冠军
    据新华社莫斯科电　中国人民国防体育协会射击队同苏联支援陆海空军志愿协会射击队的历时六天射击比赛已于5日在这里全部结束。苏联队获团体总分冠军。在十个项目中，中国队获得三个项目团体冠军和两个项目个人冠军。苏联队赢得七个项目团体冠军，共获十七分。中国队共获十三分。女子的两个项目，小口径步枪五十米六十发卧射和小口径步枪五十米3×20，团体冠军都为中国队获得。
    在八个男子项目中，中国队获手枪速射团体冠军（二二八一），中国队董光荣获小口径步枪五十米跪射个人冠军（三八六）。
