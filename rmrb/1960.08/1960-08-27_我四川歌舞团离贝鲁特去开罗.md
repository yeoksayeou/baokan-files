Title: Sichuan Song and Dance Troupe Leaves Beirut for Cairo
Author:
Date: 1960-08-27
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, Beirut, August 26th: The Chinese Sichuan Song and Dance Troupe, at the invitation of the Lebanese Minister of Education, Kamal Joumblatt, held a performance here on the evening of the 25th, which was a great success.

　　More than 1,600 people from all walks of life in Lebanon filled the theater. Among these people were members of parliament, military and government officials, and diplomatic envoys from countries friendly to China. Kamal Joumblatt also attended the performance.

　　The various programs performed by the Sichuan Song and Dance Troupe were warmly welcomed by the audience. Many programs were interrupted by thunderous applause, and the performers repeatedly took bows amid the audience's applause. Many audience members were seeing Chinese song and dance for the first time. They were very happy to be able to see the artistic performances of People's China. They said it was "real art." Many audience members praised it, saying, "Wonderful," "Excellent," and "Fantastic."

　　The song and dance troupe left here today via Damascus for Cairo. Representatives of the Lebanese Ministry of Education accompanied the song and dance troupe to the border between Lebanon and the Syrian region of the United Arab Republic.

### 我四川歌舞团离贝鲁特去开罗

1960-08-27
第5版()
专栏：

    我四川歌舞团离贝鲁特去开罗
    新华社贝鲁特26日电　中国四川省歌舞团应黎巴嫩教育部长卡迈勒·琼卜拉特的邀请，25日晚在这里举行演出，获得极大成功。
    一千六百多黎巴嫩各界人士把演出的剧院坐得满满的。这些人士中包括议员，军政官员和一些对中国友好的国家的外交使节。卡迈勒·琼卜拉特也出席观看了表演。
    四川省歌舞团表演的各项节目都受到观众的热烈欢迎，许多节目为雷动的掌声所打断，演员们在观众的鼓掌声中一再谢幕。许多观众还是第一次欣赏中国的歌舞。他们对于能够看到人民中国的艺术表演感到非常高兴。他们说，这是“真正的艺术”。很多观众赞不绝口地说，“太好了”、“十分出色”、“妙极了”。
    歌舞团已经在今天离开这里取道大马士革前往开罗。黎巴嫩教育部的代表陪送歌舞团到黎巴嫩和阿联叙利亚地区边境。
