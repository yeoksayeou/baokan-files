Title: Cuban Ambassador Discusses Agenda of OAS Foreign Ministers' Meeting: Demands Discussion of US Aggression Against Cuba, Points Out the Real Threat to the Americas Comes From US Aggression
Author:
Date: 1960-08-09
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, Washington, August 7th: Cuban Ambassador to the Organization of American States (OAS), Carlos Lechuga, stated on the 6th, at a committee meeting preparing the agenda for the OAS Foreign Ministers' Meeting, that Cuba demands the inclusion of the issue of condemning US military and economic aggression against Cuba on the agenda of the meeting, and opposes discussing the so-called "threat of communism" at this meeting. Ambassador Lechuga said that the Cuban revolutionary government "sees no threat to the Americas from any country outside the continent."

On the same day, according to Agence France-Presse, the Cuban government submitted a note to the OAS through its delegation to the organization. The note solemnly pointed out that the real threat to the Americas comes from US economic and military aggression.

The note also condemned the proposal, instigated by the US and put forward by Peru, to convene the Foreign Ministers' Meeting to discuss the so-called threat of "communism." The note emphasized that Latin American countries are facing the constant aggression of the United States and the threat of interference from the US government. They should focus on discussing the issue of unity among Latin American countries at this Foreign Ministers' Meeting.

The note also requested that the Latin American foreign ministers consider "US economic penetration and exploitation of Latin America" and the resulting instability, economic recession, and poverty in Latin American countries.

### 古巴大使谈美洲国家组织外长会议议程  要求讨论美国侵略古巴问题  指出对美洲大陆的真正威胁来自美国的侵略

1960-08-09
第6版()
专栏：

    古巴大使谈美洲国家组织外长会议议程
    要求讨论美国侵略古巴问题
    指出对美洲大陆的真正威胁来自美国的侵略
    新华社7日讯　华盛顿消息：古巴驻美洲国家组织的大使卡洛斯·莱丘加6日在一个为美洲国家组织外长会议准备议程的委员会会议上说，古巴要求把谴责美国对古巴的军事和经济侵略问题列入这次会议的议程，而反对在这次会议上讨论所谓“共产主义的威胁”的问题。莱丘加大使说，古巴革命政府“看不出任何来自大陆以外的国家对美洲的威胁。”
    同一天，据法新社报道，古巴政府通过它的驻美洲国家组织的代表团，把一项照会提交这个组织。这项照会严正指出，对美洲大陆的真正威胁来自美国的经济和军事侵略。
    这项照会还谴责在美国指使下由秘鲁出面提出的关于召开这次外长会议来讨论所谓对付“共产主义威胁”的建议。照会强调说，拉丁美洲国家面临着美国不断进行的侵略和美国政府对它们施加的干涉威胁，他们应该在这次外长会议上着重讨论拉丁美洲国家之间的团结问题。
    这项照会还要求拉丁美洲国家外长们考虑“美国对拉丁美洲的经济渗透和剥削”以及由此而造成的拉丁美洲国家的不稳定、经济衰退、贫困等问题。
