Title: Typhoon Emergency Alert
Author:
Date: 1960-08-08
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, 7th: The Central Meteorological Research Institute issued a typhoon emergency alert at 6:00 PM on the 7th:

The eighth typhoon of 1960 in the Pacific Ocean was centered at 2:00 PM today approximately 150 kilometers southwest of Okinawa Island, Japan, near 25 degrees North latitude and 126.9 degrees East longitude. The maximum wind force near the center is Force 12 or above. The typhoon's central movement speed is now rapidly accelerating, moving in a west-northwesterly direction at a speed of 18 to 22 knots per hour. It is expected that the typhoon center will make landfall along the coast from Zhejiang to northern Fujian tomorrow morning. In the next 24 hours, the wind force along the central and northern coast of Fujian, the coast of Zhejiang, and the southern coast of Jiangsu will increase to Force 7 to 10, with wind forces of Force 12 or above near the typhoon's landfall point. Heavy rain is expected in central and northern Fujian, as well as southern Zhejiang, southern Anhui, and eastern Jiangxi. After making landfall, the typhoon will continue to penetrate inland, with strong winds of Force 6 to 9 in northern Fujian and inland Zhejiang.

### 台风紧急警报

1960-08-08
第4版()
专栏：

    台风紧急警报
    新华社7日讯　中央气象科学研究所7日下午六时发布台风紧急警报：
    太平洋上的1960年第八号台风，今天下午二时中心位置在日本的冲绳岛西南方一百五十公里左右的洋面上，就是北纬二十五度、东经一百二十六点九度附近。中心附近最大风力有十二级以上。目前台风中心移速急剧加快，正以每小时十八至二十二浬的速度向西北偏西方向移动。预计台风中心将于明天早晨到上午在浙江到福建北部一带沿海登陆。未来二十四小时内，福建中部北部沿海、浙江沿海和江苏南部沿海风力将加大到七至十级，其中，台风登陆地点附近风力有十二级以上；福建中部北部和浙江、安徽南部、江西东部并有暴雨。台风登陆以后将继续深入内陆，福建北部、浙江内陆将有六至九级的大风。
