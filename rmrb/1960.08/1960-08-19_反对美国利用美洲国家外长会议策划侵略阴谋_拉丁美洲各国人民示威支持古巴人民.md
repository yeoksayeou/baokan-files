Title: Oppose the US Using the Meeting of Ministers of Foreign Affairs of American States to Plot Aggression Conspiracy; People of Latin American Countries Demonstrate to Support the Cuban People
Author: 
Date: 1960-08-19
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, 18th dispatch: People in countries such as Costa Rica, Uruguay, and Venezuela have held rallies and demonstrations to protest the United States' conspiracy to interfere in Cuba through the Meeting of Ministers of Foreign Affairs of the Americas.

　　In San José, the capital of Costa Rica and the location of the meeting, when the Meeting of Ministers of Foreign Affairs of the Americas began on the 16th, local people held rallies and demonstrations near the Cuban embassy to support Cuba and oppose US interference. The rally lasted for several hours. The participants constantly shouted slogans such as "Down with US imperialism!" "We want Cuba, not the Yankees!" "Yankees get out of Costa Rica!"

　　On the evening of the 16th, a demonstration of more than 20,000 people was held in Montevideo, the capital of Uruguay, to express support for Cuba and condemn the Meeting of Ministers of Foreign Affairs of the Americas instigated by the United States with the aim of interfering in Cuba.

　　The procession passed through twenty-five streets, and signs in the procession read: "US imperialism get out of Latin America!" "Long live Cuba!" The demonstrators shouted "We want Cuba, not the Yankees!" "The robbers of the Organization of American States will not defeat Cuba!" and slogans against the US-controlled International Monetary Fund.

　　Before the march, the United Workers' Confederation of Uruguay, the University Students' Federation, and other political groups organized a mass rally. Speakers at the rally emphasized the Uruguayan people's will to stand in solidarity with the Cuban people. They pointed out that Cuba's path is the path of all of Latin America.

　　On the 16th, demonstrations in support of the Cuban revolution were also held in the Venezuelan capital and the oil center of Maracaibo. The demonstrators also opposed US support for the dictatorial rule of Trujillo in the Dominican Republic.

　　Thousands of people participated in the demonstration in the capital, Caracas, and chanted slogans such as "Long live Cuba" along the way. A women's picket line carrying signs against the US Pentagon and in support of the Cuban revolution marched with the demonstration. A motorcade was also held in the city that day, with hundreds of cars driving through the streets of Caracas, the cars constantly honking their horns in rhythm with the slogan "We want Cuba, not the Yankees." People in the cars distributed leaflets along the way, which read: "Oppose Trujillo and the Pentagon, support the Cuban revolution, long live Venezuela! Long live Cuba!"

　　Xinhua News Agency, 18th dispatch: San Salvador report: On the evening of the 16th, on the eve of the formal opening of the Meeting of the Organization of American States instigated by the United States, more than 1,000 residents of the capital of El Salvador held a demonstration in front of the US Embassy, ​​angrily protesting the US conspiracy to interfere in Cuba.

　　Before demonstrating at the US Embassy, ​​residents of San Salvador held a rally at a university in support of the Cuban revolutionary government, and then they took to the streets to march.

　　This is the largest anti-American demonstration held by the people of El Salvador to support the Cuban revolution since the victory of the Cuban revolution.

### 反对美国利用美洲国家外长会议策划侵略阴谋  拉丁美洲各国人民示威支持古巴人民

1960-08-19
第5版()
专栏：

    反对美国利用美洲国家外长会议策划侵略阴谋
    拉丁美洲各国人民示威支持古巴人民
    新华社18日讯　哥斯达黎加、乌拉圭、委内瑞拉等国人民纷纷举行集会和示威游行，抗议美国通过美洲外长会议策划干涉古巴的阴谋。
    在会议所在地哥斯达黎加首都圣约瑟，在16日美洲外长会议开始举行的时候，当地人民群众在古巴大使馆附近举行了支持古巴、反对美国干涉的集会和示威，大会进行了几小时。与会群众不断高呼“打倒美帝国主义！”“要古巴、不要美国佬！”“美国佬滚出哥斯达黎加！”等口号。
    16日晚上，在乌拉圭首都蒙得维的亚举行了有两万多人参加的示威游行，表示支持古巴，谴责美国策动的目的在于干涉古巴的美洲外长会议。
    游行队伍穿过二十五条街道，队伍中的标语牌上写着：“美帝国主义滚出拉丁美洲！”“古巴万岁！”游行者高呼“要古巴，不要美国佬！”“美洲国家组织的强盗们不会战胜古巴！”以及反对美国所控制的国际货币基金组织的口号。
    游行之前，乌拉圭工人统一工会、大学生联合会以及其他政治团体组织了一个群众集会。在集会上讲话的人强调了乌拉圭人民同古巴人民团结在一起的意志。他们指出，古巴的道路就是整个拉丁美洲的道路。
    16日在委内瑞拉首都和石油中心马拉开波也举行了支持古巴革命的示威游行，示威者同时还反对美国支持多米尼加特鲁希略的独裁统治。
    有成千上万的人参加了首都加拉加斯的示威游行，他们在游行时一路高呼“古巴万岁”等口号。妇女纠察队拿着反对美国五角大楼和支持古巴革命的标语随着示威的队伍行进。这一天在这个城市还举行了车队游行，几百辆汽车驶遍加拉加斯街道，汽车配合着“要古巴不要美国佬”的口号的节奏不断地响着喇叭。在汽车上的人们沿途散发传单，传单上写着：“反对特鲁希略和五角大楼，支持古巴革命，委内瑞拉万岁！古巴万岁！”
    据新华社18日讯　圣萨尔瓦多消息：16日晚，在美国策动的美洲国家组织外长会议正式开幕的前夕，萨尔瓦多首都居民一千多人在美国大使馆前举行了示威游行，愤怒抗议美国对古巴的干涉阴谋。
    在到美国大使馆举行示威以前，圣萨尔瓦多居民曾在一座大学里举行支持古巴革命政府的集会，然后他们走到大街上去游行。
    这是古巴革命胜利以后，萨尔瓦多人民为支持古巴革命而举行的规模最大的反美示威。
