Title: Hungarian Ambassador to China Meets with Luo Guibo
Author:
Date: 1960-08-09
Page: 5

    Xinhua News Agency, August 8th: Ferenc Martin, the newly appointed Ambassador of the Hungarian People's Republic to China, met with Luo Guibo, Vice Minister of Foreign Affairs, this morning to discuss matters related to the presentation of his credentials. Present at the meeting were Yu Peiwen, Director of the Protocol Department of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs, and Yu Zhan, Deputy Director of the Department of Soviet Union and Eastern European Affairs.

### 匈驻华大使会见罗贵波

1960-08-09
第5版()
专栏：

    匈驻华大使会见罗贵波
    新华社8日讯　匈牙利人民共和国新任驻华大使马尔丁·费伦茨，今天上午会见了外交部副部长罗贵波，商谈递交国书事宜。会见时在座的，有外交部礼宾司司长俞沛文，苏联东欧司副司长余湛。
