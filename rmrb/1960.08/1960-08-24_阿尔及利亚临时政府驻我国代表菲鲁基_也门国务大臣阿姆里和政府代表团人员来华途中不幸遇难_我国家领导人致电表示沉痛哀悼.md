Title: Algerian Provisional Government Representative to China, Firouzi, and Yemeni Minister of State Amri and Government Delegation Members Killed in Accident En Route to China; Chinese National Leaders Send Condolences

Author:
Date: 1960-08-24
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, August 22nd -- Algerian Republic Provisional Government Representative to China, Mustapha Firouzi, and his family, as well as Yemeni Kingdom Minister of State Qadi Muhammad Abdullah Amri and his entire Yemeni Kingdom government delegation, who were invited to visit China, tragically died in a plane crash en route from Cairo to China.

Our national leaders sent telegrams of condolences to the Algerian Republic Provisional Government and the Yemeni Kingdom government on the 21st to express their deep sorrow.

Xinhua News Agency, August 22nd -- Chairman Liu Shaoqi and Premier Zhou Enlai sent a telegram to Ferhat Abbas, Premier of the Algerian Republic Provisional Government, on the 21st, expressing their deep sorrow over the tragic death of Mustapha Firouzi, the Algerian Republic Provisional Government Representative to China, and his family in a plane crash en route to China. The full text of the telegram is as follows:

To His Excellency Ferhat Abbas, Premier of the Algerian Republic Provisional Government:

We are shocked to learn that Mustapha Firouzi, the Algerian Republic Provisional Government Representative to China, and his family died in a plane crash en route to China. On behalf of the Chinese people and the Chinese government, we extend our deepest condolences to the deceased and ask the Algerian government to convey our most sincere condolences to the families of the victims.

President of the People's Republic of China Liu Shaoqi
Premier of the State Council of the People's Republic of China Zhou Enlai
August 21, 1960

Xinhua News Agency, August 22nd -- Vice Premier and Foreign Minister Chen Yi sent a telegram to Karim Belkacem, Deputy Premier and Foreign Minister of the Algerian Republic Provisional Government, on the 21st, expressing his deep sorrow over the tragic death of Mustapha Firouzi, the Algerian Republic Provisional Government Representative to China, and his family in a plane crash en route to China. The full text of the telegram is as follows:

To His Excellency Karim Belkacem, Deputy Premier and Foreign Minister of the Algerian Republic Provisional Government:

We are shocked to learn that Mustapha Firouzi, the Algerian Republic Provisional Government Representative to China, and his family died in a plane crash en route to China. I extend my deepest condolences to the deceased and ask Your Excellency to convey my deepest condolences to the families of the victims.

Vice Premier and Foreign Minister of the State Council of the People's Republic of China Chen Yi
August 21, 1960

Xinhua News Agency, August 22nd -- Chairman Liu Shaoqi and Premier Zhou Enlai sent a telegram to Imam Ahmad bin Yahya Hamidaddin Nasser Liddinillah, King of the Mutawakkilite Kingdom of Yemen, on the 21st, expressing their condolences over the tragic death of Qadi Muhammad Abdullah Amri, Minister of State of the Yemeni Kingdom, and all members of the Yemeni government delegation he led in a plane crash en route to China. The full text of the telegram is as follows:

To His Majesty Imam Ahmad bin Yahya Hamidaddin Nasser Liddinillah, King of the Mutawakkilite Kingdom of Yemen:

The Chinese people and the Chinese government are shocked to learn that Qadi Muhammad Abdullah Amri, Minister of State of the Yemeni Kingdom, and all members of the Yemeni government delegation he led, died in a plane crash en route to China. On behalf of the Chinese people and the Chinese government, we extend our most sincere condolences to Your Majesty, the Yemeni Kingdom government, and the families of the victims. We firmly believe that the people of China and Yemen will redouble their efforts to enhance the friendship between the two countries to express their condolences to the deceased.

President of the People's Republic of China Liu Shaoqi
Premier of the State Council of the People's Republic of China Zhou Enlai
August 21, 1960

Xinhua News Agency, August 22nd -- Vice Premier and Foreign Minister Chen Yi sent a telegram to Saif al-Islam Muhammad al-Badr, Deputy Prime Minister and Foreign Minister of the Mutawakkilite Kingdom of Yemen, on the 21st, expressing his deep condolences over the tragic death of Qadi Muhammad Abdullah Amri, Minister of State of the Yemeni Kingdom, and all members of the government delegation he led in a plane crash en route to China. The full text of the telegram is as follows:

To His Royal Highness Prince Saif al-Islam Muhammad al-Badr, Deputy Prime Minister and Foreign Minister of the Mutawakkilite Kingdom of Yemen:

I am shocked and saddened to learn of the tragic news that all members of the government delegation led by Qadi Muhammad Abdullah Amri, Minister of State of the Yemeni Kingdom, died in a plane crash en route to China. I extend my deepest condolences to the deceased and ask Your Highness to convey my condolences to their families.

Vice Premier and Foreign Minister of the State Council of the People's Republic of China Chen Yi
August 21, 1960

### 阿尔及利亚临时政府驻我国代表菲鲁基  也门国务大臣阿姆里和政府代表团人员来华途中不幸遇难  我国家领导人致电表示沉痛哀悼

1960-08-24
第1版()
专栏：

    阿尔及利亚临时政府驻我国代表菲鲁基  也门国务大臣阿姆里和政府代表团人员来华途中不幸遇难
    我国家领导人致电表示沉痛哀悼
    新华社22日讯　阿尔及利亚共和国临时政府驻我国代表穆斯塔法·菲鲁基和他的家属，应邀来我国访问的也门王国国务大臣卡迪·穆罕默德·阿卜杜拉·阿姆里和他所率领的也门王国政府代表团全体成员，在从开罗来华途中，因座机失事，不幸遇难。
    我国家领导人已于21日分别致电阿尔及利亚共和国临时政府和也门王国政府表示沉痛的哀悼。
    新华社22日讯　刘少奇主席、周恩来总理21日致电阿尔及利亚共和国临时政府总理费尔哈特·阿巴斯，对阿尔及利亚共和国临时政府驻我国代表穆斯塔法·菲鲁基和他的家属在来华途中因飞机失事不幸遇难，表示沉痛的哀悼。唁电全文如下：阿尔及利亚共和国临时政府总理费尔哈
