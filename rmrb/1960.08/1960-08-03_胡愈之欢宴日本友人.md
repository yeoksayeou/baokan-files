Title: Hu Yuzhi Hosts Banquet for Japanese Friends
Author: 
Date: 1960-08-03
Page: 6
Xinhua News Agency, August 2nd. Hu Yuzhi, Vice President of the Chinese People's Institute of Foreign Affairs, hosted a banquet tonight in honor of the Japanese Socialist Party Policy Deliberation Council Delegation to China, headed by Hiroshi Teshima. Among those present at the banquet were Xie Nanguang, Yang Chunsong, Xiao Xiangqian, Yong Longgui, Gao Shoushan and other figures from various circles, as well as Japanese peace activist Kinkazu Saionji and Korean peace activist Hwang Bong-koo, who are in Beijing.

### 胡愈之欢宴日本友人

1960-08-03
第6版()
专栏：

    胡愈之欢宴日本友人
    新华社2日讯　中国人民外交协会副会长胡愈之今晚设宴招待以手岛博为首的日本社会党政策审议会访华代表团。出席宴会的有谢南光、杨春松、萧向前、勇龙桂、高守善等各界人士，以及在京的日本和平人士西园寺公一、朝鲜和平人士黄凤九。
