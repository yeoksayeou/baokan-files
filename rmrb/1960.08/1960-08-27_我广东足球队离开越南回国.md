Title: Guangdong Football Team Leaves Vietnam for Home
Author:
Date: 1960-08-27
Page: 5

    Guangdong Football Team Leaves Vietnam for Home
    Xinhua News Agency, Hanoi, August 26th: After concluding its visit to the Democratic Republic of Vietnam, the Guangdong Province Football Team left Hanoi by plane this morning to return home. During its visit to Vietnam, the Guangdong Football Team played three friendly matches with Vietnamese football teams.

### 我广东足球队离开越南回国

1960-08-27
第5版()
专栏：

    我广东足球队离开越南回国
    新华社河内26日电　广东省足球队结束了在越南民主共和国的访问后，今天上午乘飞机离开河内回国。广东省足球队在越南访问期间，同越南的足球队进行了三场友谊比赛。
