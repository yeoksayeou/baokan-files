    Jiangsu Province, Wujiang County, utilizes the lake and river harbor surfaces to cultivate aquatic green manure such as water hyacinth and duckweed. The county has 380,000 mu of water surface, and more than 64,000 mu have been planted with aquatic green manure. This is a scene of members of the Hongqi Brigade of the Meiyan Commune planting aquatic green manure.
