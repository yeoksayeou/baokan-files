Title: Lebanese "Racing Star" Football Team Plays in Guangzhou
Author:
Date: 1960-08-16
Page: 5
Xinhua News Agency, Guangzhou, August 14th: The Lebanese "Racing Star" football team drew with the Guangzhou Youth Team 2-2 this afternoon at the Yuexiushan Stadium in Guangzhou. This was the visiting team's last match in China. The visiting team's overall record in China was two wins, two draws, and four losses.

### 黎“雷星”足球队在广州比赛

1960-08-16
第5版()
专栏：

    黎“雷星”足球队在广州比赛
    据新华社广州14日电　黎巴嫩“雷星”足球队14日下午在广州市越秀山体育场与广州青年队以二比二战和。这是客队在华的最后一场比赛，总计客队在华的战绩是二胜二平四负。
