Title: Vice Chairman Song and Premier Zhou Receive Delegation of the Sino-Korean Friendship Association, Also Receive Korean Film Delegation and Korean National Volleyball Team
Author:
Date: 1960-08-15
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, August 14th. Vice Chairman Song Qingling and Premier Zhou Enlai received the delegation of the China-Korea Friendship Association headed by Kim Jong-heng, the Korean Film Delegation headed by Kim Han-kyu, and the entire Korean National Volleyball Team led by Park Chan-song this morning.

Present at the reception were: Vice Premiers of the State Council Li Xiannian and Xi Zhongxun, Vice Chairmen of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress Guo Moruo and Chen Shutong, Vice Chairman of the Chinese People's Committee for World Peace Liao Chengzhi, Minister of Culture Shen Yanbing, Deputy Directors of the Commission for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries Chu Tunan and Zhang Zhixiang, Vice Minister of Foreign Affairs Ji Pengfei, Vice President of the Sino-Korean Friendship Association Lao She, Deputy Mayor of Beijing Wu Han, former Commander of the Chinese People's Volunteers General Yang Yong, Political Commissar General Wang Ping, and Deng Fangzhi, the mother of martyr Huang Jiguang.

Also present at the reception was the Korean Ambassador to China, Li Yong-ho, and others.

### 宋副主席周总理接见朝中友协代表团  同时接见朝鲜电影代表团和朝鲜国家排球队

1960-08-15
第1版()
专栏：

    宋副主席周总理接见朝中友协代表团
    同时接见朝鲜电影代表团和朝鲜国家排球队
    新华社14日讯　宋庆龄副主席、周恩来总理今天上午接见了以金钟恒为首的朝中友好协会代表团，以金汉奎为首的朝鲜电影代表团、以朴赞松率领的朝鲜国家排球队全体人员。
    接见的时候，在座的有：国务院副总理李先念、习仲勋，全国人民代表大会常务委员会副委员长郭沫若、陈叔通，中国人民保卫世界和平委员会副主席廖承志，文化部部长沈雁冰，对外文化联络委员会副主任楚图南、张致祥，外交部副部长姬鹏飞，中朝友好协会副会长老舍，北京市副市长吴晗，前中国人民志愿军司令员杨勇上将、政治委员王平上将，以及黄继光烈士的母亲邓芳芝等。
    接见的时候，在座的还有朝鲜驻我国大使李永镐等。
