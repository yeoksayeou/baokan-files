Title: Successor's Blessing Poem - Greetings from the Young Pioneers of Art Academies in the Capital to the Literary and Art Congress
Author: Shao Yu
Date: 1960-08-14
Page: 8

The Successor's Blessing Poem
——Greetings from the Young Pioneers of Art Academies in the Capital to the Literary and Art Congress

Respected literary and art seniors, uncles and aunts!
　　We are the "Red Scarves" of New China,
　　We read your books,
　　Sing your songs,
　　Appreciate your paintings,
　　Watch your plays and movies.
　　We are determined to learn the revolutionary spirit of the Red Children,
　　We will never forget Haiwa delivering letters to the Eighth Route Army,
　　The characters in the works are like living around us,
　　Sublime thoughts will always inspire us to move forward.
　　It is you who have helped the motherland educate us to grow up,
　　Shaping our souls.
　　Let us sincerely pay tribute to you!
　　We wish you, under the leadership of Chairman Mao,
　　Advance every day,
　　Climb the highest peak of the revolutionary literary and art Qomolangma!
　　Respected literary and art seniors, uncles and aunts!
　　We are the Young Pioneers of art academies and art groups,
　　We are elementary school students of drama, music, fine arts, and dance,
　　We are your successors,
　　We are your reserve force.
　　It was Chairman Mao who opened up the direction of literature and art for workers, peasants, and soldiers,
　　It was you who created the first batch of good works in this direction,
　　We, this new generation, must inherit your cause.
　　Letting a hundred flowers bloom in the direction of workers, peasants, and soldiers,
　　Is the glorious road of socialist literature and art,
　　It will become our glorious literary and art tradition.
　　The magnificent Great Hall is brightly lit,
　　No, the lights are not as bright as this,
　　What shines on us is the light of Mao Zedong Thought!
　　The grand venue is fragrant with flowers,
　　No, the flowers are not as fragrant as this,
　　This is a myriad of colors and a hundred flowers blooming in the motherland's literary and art garden!
　　　　　　　　　　〔Shao Yu Sketch〕 (Attached picture)

### 接班人的祝诗——首都艺术院校团体少先队员向文代大会的祝词
邵宇
1960-08-14
第8版()
专栏：

    接班人的祝诗
    ——首都艺术院校团体少先队员向文代大会的祝词
    敬爱的文艺界前辈们，叔叔、阿姨们！
    我们是新中国的“红领巾”，
    我们读你们的书，
    唱你们的歌，
    欣赏你们的画，
    看你们的戏和电影。
    我们决心学习红孩子的革命精神，
    我们忘不了海娃送信给八路军，
    作品里的人物就像生活在我们身边，
    崇高的思想永远鼓舞我们前进。
    是你们帮助祖国教育我们长大成人，
    塑造着我们的灵魂。
    让我们衷心地向你们致敬！
    祝你们在毛主席的领导下，
    天天向上，
    攀登革命文艺的珠穆朗玛最高峰！
    敬爱的文艺界前辈们，叔叔、阿姨们！
    我们是艺术院校和艺术团体的少先队员，
    我们是戏剧、音乐、美术、舞蹈的小学生，
    我们是你们的接班人，
    我们是你们的后备军。
    是毛主席开辟了工农兵文艺方向，
    是你们创造了第一批这个方向下的好作品，
    我们这新的一代一定要把你们的事业继承。
    在工农兵方向下百花齐放，
    是社会主义文艺的光辉道路，
    它将成为我们光荣的文艺传统。
    壮丽的大会堂灯光辉煌，
    不，灯光哪有这样辉煌，
    照耀着我们的是毛泽东思想的光芒！
    宏伟的会场上鲜花芬芳，
    不，鲜花哪有这样芬芳，
    这是祖国文艺园地里万紫千红，百花齐放！
                  〔邵宇速写〕（附图片）
