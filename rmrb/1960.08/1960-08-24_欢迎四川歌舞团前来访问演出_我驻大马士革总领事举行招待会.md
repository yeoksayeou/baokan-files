Title: Chinese Consul General in Damascus Holds Reception Welcoming Sichuan Song and Dance Troupe's Visit and Performance
Author:
Date: 1960-08-24
Page: 5
Xinhua News Agency, Damascus, August 21st: Chinese Consul General in Damascus, Wang Chongli, held a reception this evening to welcome the Chinese Sichuan Song and Dance Troupe's visit.

    The Sichuan Song and Dance Troupe arrived here on the 11th of this month at the invitation of the 7th Damascus International Fair. Their performances have been warmly welcomed by the audience.

    More than 300 people from all walks of life attended the reception, including the Minister of Economy of the Syrian Region of the United Arab Republic, Husni Sawwaf, the General Manager of the 7th Damascus International Fair, Darati, and consuls from various countries stationed here. Zhang Ziquan, the curator of the Chinese Pavilion at the International Fair, also attended the reception. At the reception, the song and dance troupe performed programs for the guests.

### 欢迎四川歌舞团前来访问演出  我驻大马士革总领事举行招待会

1960-08-24
第5版()
专栏：

    欢迎四川歌舞团前来访问演出
    我驻大马士革总领事举行招待会
    新华社大马士革21日电　中国驻大马士革总领事王崇理今晚举行招待会欢迎前来访问的中国四川歌舞团。
    四川歌舞团是应第七届大马士革国际博览会的邀请，于本月11日到达这里的。他们的演出受到了观众的热烈欢迎。
    出席招待会的有各界人士三百多人，其中有阿拉伯联合共和国叙利亚地区经济部长胡斯尼·萨瓦夫，第七届大马士革国际博览会总经理达拉提和各国驻这里的领事。参加国际博览会的中国馆馆长张子泉也出席了招待会。在招待会上，歌舞团为客人们演出了节目。
