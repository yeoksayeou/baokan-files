Title: Our Youth Basketball Team Continues to Compete in Romania
Author:
Date: 1960-08-28
Page: 6
    Xinhua News Agency, Bucharest, August 27th: The Chinese Youth Men's and Women's Basketball Teams held two games with Romanian basketball players on the 26th in Oradea and Târgu Mureș, Romania, respectively. The result was one win and one loss.

### 我青年篮球队在罗继续比赛

1960-08-28
第6版()
专栏：

    我青年篮球队在罗继续比赛
    据新华社布加勒斯特27日电　中国青年男女篮球队26日分别在罗马尼亚的俄拉第亚和塔古尔谟勒斯同罗马尼亚篮球运动员举行了两场比赛。结果一胜一负。
