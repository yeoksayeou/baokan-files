Title: Delegation of the China-Korea Friendship Association Returns to Beijing After Visiting Wuhan, Shanghai and Other Places
Author: 
Date: 1960-08-26
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, August 25th—The delegation of the China-Korea Friendship Association, led by Kim Jong-heng, head of the delegation, and Park Poong-sik, deputy head of the delegation, returned to Beijing by train from Shanghai today after visiting Wuhan, Shanghai, Hangzhou and other places at the invitation of our country.

    Those who went to the station to welcome the delegation included Lao She, Vice President of the Sino-Korean Friendship Association, Zhang Zhixiang, Acting Director of the Commission for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries, Dong Chuncai, Vice Minister of Education, Huang Luofeng, Wang Yi, Xia Ding and other council members of the Sino-Korean Friendship Association.

    Korean Ambassador to China Li Yong-ho and embassy staff also went to the station to welcome them. During their visit to Hangzhou from the 22nd to the 24th, the delegation visited the Hangzhou Dujinsheng Silk Weaving Factory and toured the scenic spots and historical sites of West Lake. On the 23rd, Wu Xian, Secretary of the Zhejiang Provincial Party Committee Secretariat, Vice Governor of Zhejiang Province, First Secretary of the Hangzhou Municipal Party Committee, and Mayor of Hangzhou, hosted a banquet to entertain the guests. On the 24th, the delegation left Hangzhou for Shanghai, and took a train from Shanghai back to Beijing that night. When the delegation left Hangzhou and Shanghai, Ren Yili, Vice Governor of Zhejiang Province, Cao Diqiu, Secretary of the Shanghai Municipal Party Committee Secretariat, and Vice Mayor of Shanghai, and other party and government leaders went to the station to see them off.

### 在访问武汉上海等地以后  朝中友协代表团返回北京

1960-08-26
第1版()
专栏：

    在访问武汉上海等地以后
    朝中友协代表团返回北京
    新华社25日讯　应邀前来我国访问的朝中友协代表团在团长金钟恒、副团长朴丰植的率领下，在访问了武汉、上海、杭州等地后，今日乘火车从上海回到北京。
    前往车站欢迎的有中朝友协副会长老舍、对外文委代主任张致祥、教育部副部长董纯才、中朝友协理事黄洛峰、王益、夏鼎等。
    朝鲜驻华大使李永镐及使馆人员也到车站欢迎。代表团22日到24日在杭州访问时，参观了杭州都锦生丝织厂，游览了西湖的名胜古迹。23日，中共浙江省委书记处书记、浙江省副省长、中共杭州市委第一书记、杭州市市长吴宪设宴招待客人。24日，代表团离开杭州去上海，当晚从上海乘车回北京。在代表团离开杭州和上海的时候，浙江省副省长任一力，中共上海市委书记处书记、上海市副市长曹荻秋等党和政府的负责人曾到车站送行。
