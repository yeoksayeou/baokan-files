Title: Contemporary Hero (Picture)
Author: Wang Ping
Date: 1960-08-05
Page: 3

    Contemporary Hero
    Cao Shusheng (second from right), a young technical innovation expert from the Lianhua Iron Mine in Changsha, Hunan, an advanced producer in Changsha City, and a Communist Party member, is a new worker from a peasant background who only entered the mine in 1959. Under the cultivation and education of the Party, he has continuously studied technology diligently and made rapid progress. In the mine-wide movement of "everyone contributes an idea, and carries out large-scale mine technical transformation," he successively created and improved more than ten new tools, such as the triple-use foot-operated drilling machine, round steel track, double aerial cableway, automatic unloading truck, and dual-purpose western hoe, which increased work efficiency by one to thirty times respectively. This is him working with his colleagues to study the problem of improving the automatic reciprocating transport line track.
                                 Xinhua News Agency reporter Wang Ping

### 当代英雄（图片）
王平
1960-08-05
第3版()
专栏：

    当代英雄
    湖南长沙莲花铁矿青年技术革新能手、长沙市先进生产者、共产党员曹树生（右二），是一个1959年才进矿的农民出身的新工人。他在党的培养教育下，不断刻苦钻研技术，进步很快。在全矿“人人献一计，大搞矿山技术改造”运动中，先后创造和改进了三用脚踏打眼机、圆钢轨道、双条高空索道、自动卸土车、两用洋锄等十多种新工具，使工效分别提高一倍到三十倍。这是他正在和同伴们一起研究改进来回自动运输线轨道问题。
                                 新华社记者　王平摄
