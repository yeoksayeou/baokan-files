Title: Chen Yi Congratulates African Foreign Ministers' Conference, Expresses Firm Support of Chinese People for the Just Struggle of the Congolese People, Cables Congolese Foreign Minister to Notify China's Dispatch of Chen Jiakang as Observer to the Conference
Author:
Date: 1960-08-26
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, August 25th. Foreign Minister Chen Yi of the People's Republic of China sent a telegram on the 24th to congratulate the African Foreign Ministers' Conference held in Leopoldville, the capital of Congo, and expressed the firm support of the Chinese people for the just struggle of the Congolese people. The text of the telegram is as follows:
To the African Foreign Ministers' Conference in Leopoldville, Congo:
    On the occasion of the convening of the African Foreign Ministers' Conference, I would like to extend my heartfelt congratulations to the conference on behalf of the government of the People's Republic of China and its people. At present, the people of the Republic of Congo are waging a heroic struggle against the aggression and interference of the imperialist bloc headed by the United States and to safeguard the country's sovereignty, independence and unity. The Chinese people resolutely support this just struggle of the Congolese people. I wish the conference and the people of African countries success in the struggle to win and safeguard national independence.
    Chen Yi
    Minister of Foreign Affairs of the People's Republic of China
    August 24, 1960
Xinhua News Agency, August 25th. Foreign Minister Chen Yi of the People's Republic of China telegraphed Mr. Bomboko, Minister of Foreign Affairs of the Republic of Congo, on the 24th, notifying him that the Chinese government, at the invitation of the Republic of Congo, had sent Chen Jiakang, Chinese Ambassador to the United Arab Republic, as China's observer to the African Foreign Ministers' Conference. The text of the telegram is as follows:
To His Excellency Bomboko, Minister of Foreign Affairs of the Republic of Congo, Leopoldville, Congo:
    I was pleased to receive your telegram of August 23rd regarding welcoming our country to send observers to the African Foreign Ministers' Conference, and I am honored to notify Your Excellency on behalf of the government of the People's Republic of China that the Chinese government has decided to send Chen Jiakang, Ambassador of the People's Republic of China to the United Arab Republic, as an observer to attend the African Foreign Ministers' Conference. I have instructed Ambassador Chen Jiakang to set off as soon as possible and notify Your Excellency of his date of arrival. I would be very grateful if Your Excellency could provide assistance.
    Chen Yi
    Minister of Foreign Affairs of the People's Republic of China
    August 24, 1960

### 陈外长电贺非洲国家外长会议  表示中国人民坚决支持刚果人民的正义斗争  致电刚果外长通知中国派陈家康为出席会议观察员

1960-08-26
第1版()
专栏：

    陈外长电贺非洲国家外长会议
    表示中国人民坚决支持刚果人民的正义斗争
    致电刚果外长通知中国派陈家康为出席会议观察员
    新华社25日讯　中华人民共和国外交部长陈毅24日致电祝贺非洲国家外长会议在刚果首都利奥波德维尔召开，并且表示中国人民坚决支持刚果人民的正义斗争。电文如下：刚果利奥波德维尔非洲国家外长会议：
    值此非洲国家外长会议召开的时候，我谨代表中华人民共和国政府和中国人民向大会表示衷心的祝贺。目前，刚果共和国人民正在为反对以美国为首的帝国主义集团的侵略和干涉、维护国家的主权独立和统一而进行着英勇的斗争。中国人民坚决支持刚果人民的这一正义斗争。祝大会和非洲各国人民在争取和维护民族独立的斗争中取得成功。
    中华人民共和国外交部长
                        陈　毅
                1960年8月24日
    新华社25日讯　中华人民共和国外交部长陈毅24日打电报给刚果共和国外交部长邦博科，通知他中国政府已应刚果共和国的邀请，派中国驻阿拉伯联合共和国大使陈家康为我国出席非洲国家外长会议的观察员。电文如下：刚果　利奥波德维尔刚果共和国外交部长邦博科阁下：
    我高兴地收到了阁下于8月23日关于欢迎我国派观察员出席非洲国家外长会议的来电，并且荣幸地代表中华人民共和国政府通知阁下，中国政府已决定派中华人民共和国驻阿拉伯联合共和国大使陈家康为观察员，出席非洲国家外长会议。我已指示陈家康大使尽速启程并将他到达的日期通知阁下，如蒙阁下惠予协助，则不胜感谢。
    中华人民共和国外交部长
                        陈　毅
                1960年8月24日
