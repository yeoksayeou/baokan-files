    Ultrasonic Welding of Metals
    The Soviet Design Bureau for Electric Heating Equipment has developed a special ultrasonic welding machine. Using this method to weld metal parts results in strong welds and is easy to operate.
    The main parts of this machine are an ultrasonic generator, several vibrating plates, and a rod-shaped waveguide connected to the vibrating plates. Ultrasonic waves are transmitted from the powerful generator to the vibrating plates and then to the waveguide. At the end of the waveguide, the entire power of the ultrasonic waves can be concentrated at one point. If the end of the waveguide touches metal parts that are close together, the metals will weld together.
    When welding using this method, the metal hardly heats up at all. As a result of local plastic deformation, it seems that metal particles interpenetrate each other at the welding point. The weld is very strong. There are no dazzling sparks during welding, and the welder does not need to wear a protective mask.

### 超声波焊接金属

1960-08-04
第6版()
专栏：

    超声波焊接金属
    苏联电热装备设计局制成了专门的超声波焊接机。利用这种方法焊接金属零件，焊缝坚固而且操作简便。
    这部机器的主要部分是一个超声波发生器、一些振动片和一个连接到振动片上的棒形波导管。超声波从强大的发生器传到振动片，然后再到波导管。在波导管末端可以把超声波的全部力量集中在一点上。如果把波导管末端触到彼此紧靠在一起的金属零件上，金属就会焊在一起。
    利用这种方法焊接时金属几乎一点都不会变热。由于局部塑性变形结果，在焊接点上似乎发生了金属微粒相互渗和现象。焊接十分坚固。焊接时不再出现炫目的火花，焊接者也无须戴上保护面罩了。
