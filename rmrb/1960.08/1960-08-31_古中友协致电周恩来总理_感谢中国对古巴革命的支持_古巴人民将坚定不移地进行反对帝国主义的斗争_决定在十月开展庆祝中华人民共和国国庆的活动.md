Title: Cuba-China Friendship Association Sends Telegram to Premier Zhou Enlai, Thanking China for its Support of the Cuban Revolution; Cuban People Will Unswervingly Wage the Struggle Against Imperialism; Decision Made to Carry Out Activities in October to Celebrate the National Day of the People's Republic of China
Author:
Date: 1960-08-31
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Havana, 29th - The Cuba-China Friendship Association today sent a telegram to Premier Zhou Enlai, thanking the People's Republic of China for its support of the Cuban Revolution.

The telegram said: "The Executive Committee of the Cuba-China Friendship Association has unanimously decided to express the feelings of the Cuban people and convey to you our deep gratitude, thanking you for your speech in support of the Cuban Revolution. Such expressions of support from you encourage the patriotism of the Cuban people, who are determined to unwaveringly wage the struggle against brutal imperialism and defend the sovereignty and independence of the country. Faced with wicked aggression, the promise of unconditional assistance automatically offered by the Chinese government is eternally appreciated and further strengthens the fraternal friendship between our two peoples."

The thank-you telegram was signed by Baltolomero Alvarez Rios, Chairman of the Executive Committee of the Cuba-China Friendship Association.

The thank-you telegram was adopted at the Association's Executive Committee meeting held on the 27th. The meeting also decided to carry out activities in October this year to celebrate the National Day of the People's Republic of China.

### 古中友协致电周恩来总理  感谢中国对古巴革命的支持  古巴人民将坚定不移地进行反对帝国主义的斗争  决定在十月开展庆祝中华人民共和国国庆的活动

1960-08-31
第1版()
专栏：

    古中友协致电周恩来总理
    感谢中国对古巴革命的支持
    古巴人民将坚定不移地进行反对帝国主义的斗争
    决定在十月开展庆祝中华人民共和国国庆的活动
    新华社哈瓦那29日电　古巴—中国友好协会今天致电周恩来总理，感谢中华人民共和国对古巴革命的支持。
    电文说：“古巴—中国友好协会执行委员会一致决定表达古巴人民的感情，向您表示深切的谢意，感谢您声援和支持古巴革命的讲话。像您这样表示支持的行动鼓舞了古巴人的爱国主义，古巴人决心坚定不移地进行反对残暴的帝国主义的斗争，保卫国家的主权和独立。面对罪恶的侵略，中国政府自动提出的无条件援助的诺言使人永远感谢，并使得我们两国人民之间兄弟般的友好关系更加紧密了。”
    在感谢电上签名的是古巴—中国友好协会执行委员会主席巴耳多梅罗·阿耳瓦雷斯·里奥斯。
    感谢电是在27日举行的协会执行委员会的会议上通过的。会议还决定在今年10月开展庆祝中华人民共和国国庆的活动。
