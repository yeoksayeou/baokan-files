Title: Our Youth Basketball Team Wins First Game in Romania
Author:
Date: 1960-08-24
Page: 5
Xinhua News Agency, Bucharest, August 22nd: The Chinese youth men's and women's basketball teams, currently visiting Romania, achieved victory in their first game in Stalin City on the evening of the 21st.

    The Chinese youth women's basketball team defeated the Romanian youth women's basketball team with a score of 53 to 49; the Chinese youth men's basketball team played against the Stalin City representative team and also won with a score of 69 to 43.

### 我青年篮球队在罗马尼亚首战获胜

1960-08-24
第5版()
专栏：

    我青年篮球队在罗马尼亚首战获胜
    新华社布加勒斯特22日电　正在罗马尼亚访问的中国青年男女篮球队，21日晚上在斯大林城首战告捷。
    中国青年女子篮球队以五十三比四十九战胜了罗马尼亚青年女子篮球队；中国青年男子篮球队和斯大林城代表队交锋，结果也以六十九比四十三获得胜利。
