Title: Moscow International Tennis Tournament in Full Swing; Chinese Athletes Secure Spots in Singles Quarter-Finals

Author: 
Date: 1960-08-25
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, August 24th: The largest tennis tournament held in the Soviet Union, the Moscow Thirteen-Nation International Tennis Tournament, is in full swing. Chinese athletes Zhu Zhenhua and Mei Fuji have successfully passed the third round in the men's singles competition and entered the quarter-finals. A total of forty-five athletes are participating in the men's singles.

The competition is using a knockout system. The two Chinese athletes are also participating in the doubles competition, and successfully passed the first round, winning against the Soviet Union's Virulava and Mtsinarishvili with a score of 6-2, 6-2.

### 莫斯科国际网球赛热烈进行  我国选手已获单打半复赛权

1960-08-25
第5版()
专栏：

    莫斯科国际网球赛热烈进行
    我国选手已获单打半复赛权
    新华社莫斯科24日电　在苏联举行的最大一次网球赛——莫斯科十三国国际网球赛，正在热烈地进行。中国运动员朱振华和梅福基在男子单打比赛中，已经顺利通过第三轮进入半复赛。参加这次男子单打的一共有四十五名选手。
    这次比赛是采用淘汰制。中国的两名选手还参加双打比赛，并且顺利地通过了第一轮，以六比二，六比二胜苏联的维鲁拉瓦和姆齐纳里什维利。
