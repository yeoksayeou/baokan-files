Title: Carrier Rocket of Spaceship Continues to Orbit Earth, Aiding Research into Conditions for Descent of Cosmic Devices
Author:
Date: 1960-08-25
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, August 24th: The carrier rocket of the Soviet Union's second satellite spaceship continues to orbit the Earth, according to an article published in the "Soviet Russia Newspaper" by Sternfeld, a famous Soviet spaceflight scientist.

Sternfeld said that the carrier rocket is orbiting along the trajectory of the second satellite spaceship. Its operation is very meaningful, especially in helping to study the conditions for the descent of cosmic devices.

### 飞船的运载火箭继续绕地球运行  有助于研究宇宙装置下降的条件

1960-08-25
第5版()
专栏：

    飞船的运载火箭继续绕地球运行
    有助于研究宇宙装置下降的条件
    新华社24日讯　据塔斯社莫斯科讯：苏联第二个卫星式宇宙飞船的运载火箭，目前仍继续在围绕地球运行，这是苏联著名宇宙飞行学家斯特恩菲尔德在“苏维埃俄罗斯报”上发表的一篇文章中说的。
    斯特恩菲尔德说，运载火箭是沿着第二个卫星式飞船的轨道运行的。它的运行是很有意义的，特别有助于研究宇宙装置下降的条件。
