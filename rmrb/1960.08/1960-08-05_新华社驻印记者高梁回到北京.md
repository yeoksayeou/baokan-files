Title: Xinhua News Agency Correspondent Gao Liang Returns to Beijing
Author:
Date: 1960-08-05
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency: Beijing, August 4th. Xinhua News Agency correspondent Gao Liang, who was unreasonably refused continued residency in India by the Indian government, returned to Beijing this afternoon. He was warmly welcomed at the airport by Mei Yi, Vice Chairman of the All-China Journalists Association, Wang Yi, Deputy Editor-in-Chief of the People's Daily, Miao Haileng, Deputy Director of Xinhua News Agency, and other people from the capital's press circles.

Other staff members of the Xinhua News Agency's New Delhi branch also left New Delhi and returned to China because the Indian government unreasonably refused to allow them to continue residing in India.

### 新华社驻印记者高梁回到北京

1960-08-05
第5版()
专栏：

    新华社驻印记者高梁回到北京
    新华社4日讯　被印度政府无理拒绝继续在印度居留的新华社驻印度记者高梁，已经在今天下午回到北京，他在机场上受到中华全国新闻工作者协会副主席梅益、人民日报副总编辑王揖、新华社副社长缪海棱及其他首都新闻界人士的热烈欢迎。
    新华社新德里分社其他工作人员，也因印度政府无理拒绝他们继续居留印度而离开新德里回国。
