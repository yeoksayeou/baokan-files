Title: Black African Teachers' Union Delegation Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1960-08-26
Page: 5
Xinhua News Agency, August 25th. At the invitation of the National Committee of the Educational Workers' Union of China, the 11-member delegation of the Black African Teachers' Union, led by Ndaw Amadou, Deputy Secretary-General of the Secular Education Unified Trade Union of Senegal, arrived in Beijing today.
    The delegation is composed of heads of educational trade unions from four countries: Guinea, Mauritania, Senegal, and Sudan.

### 黑非洲教师联合会代表团抵京

1960-08-26
第5版()
专栏：

    黑非洲教师联合会代表团抵京
    新华社25日讯　应中国教育工会全国委员会邀请前来我国访问的黑非洲教师联合会代表团一行十一人，在团长塞内加尔非宗教教育统一工会副总书记恩多·阿玛杜率领下，今日到达北京。
    代表团由几内亚、毛里塔尼亚、塞内加尔、苏丹四个国家教育工会的负责人共同组成的。
