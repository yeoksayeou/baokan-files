Title: National Literary and Art Congress Continues System Meetings; Representatives Speak on Specialized Issues such as Literature, Drama, Music, Fine Arts, and Film; Ministry of Culture and Others Hold Diverse and Colorful Exhibitions; Folk Poets and Singers of All Ethnic Groups Attending the Literary and Art Congress Hold a Grand Gathering

Author:
Date: 1960-08-03
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, 2nd August -- During the Third National Literary and Art Congress, the member representative conferences or expanded council meetings of the Chinese Writers Association, Chinese Dramatists Association, Chinese Musicians Association, Chinese Artists Association, China Film Workers Association, Chinese Ballad Singers Association, Chinese Dance Research Association, Chinese Folk Literature and Art Research Association, and Chinese Photographic Society have been ongoing for four days. The group discussions in each meeting have now concluded, and the plenary speeches for some meetings have also finished.

In the plenary speeches over the past four days, some representatives spoke on specialized issues in literature, drama, music, fine arts, film, ballad singing, dance, folk literature and art, and photography. Some representatives introduced the achievements and experiences in literary and artistic activities in their regions or units. Some representatives engaged in professional or amateur literary and artistic activities focused on discussing their experiences in creation and performance.

In their speeches and discussions, the representatives unanimously expressed their warm support for the congratulatory speech made by Lu Dingyi on behalf of the CCP Central Committee and the State Council at the Third National Literary and Art Congress, as well as Zhou Yang's report. They also conducted heated discussions on the reports of the responsible persons of various associations, research associations, federations, and societies at the system meetings. The representatives unanimously felt very excited about the great achievements made in China's literary and artistic work in recent years, and expressed their determination to continue to follow Mao Zedong's literary and artistic thought, and strive to implement the literary and artistic direction of serving the workers, peasants, and soldiers and the policy of letting a hundred flowers bloom and a hundred schools of thought contend.

The various meetings will be adjourned for one day tomorrow and will continue the day after tomorrow.

Xinhua News Agency, 1st August -- Representatives attending the Third National Congress of Chinese Literary and Art Workers have visited many exhibitions held by the Ministry of Culture and various literary and artistic organizations in recent days. These exhibitions are: the "Exhibition of Literary Books and Periodicals" sponsored by the Chinese Writers Association and the Chinese Folk Literature and Art Research Association; the "Exhibition of Chinese Film Work" sponsored by the Film Bureau of the Ministry of Culture and the China Film Workers Association; the "Small Exhibition of Technological Innovation and Technological Revolution in Cultural Undertakings" sponsored by the Ministry of Culture; the "National Art Exhibition" sponsored by the Chinese Artists Association; the "Exhibition of Achievements in Drama Work Over the Past Ten Years" sponsored by the Chinese Dramatists Association; the "Exhibition of the General Situation of the Work of the Chinese Musicians Association and its Branches in Various Regions" sponsored by the Chinese Musicians Association; and the "Exhibition of Photographic Art" sponsored by the Chinese Photographic Society, etc.

Through exhibits such as physical objects, pictures, models, and clipping materials, the exhibitions reflect the great achievements made in various aspects of Chinese literature and art under the guidance of Mao Zedong's literary and artistic line in the past ten years, especially since the Great Leap Forward, demonstrating the vigorous development of the literary and artistic undertakings of the new China under the leadership of the Party.

Xinhua News Agency, 2nd August -- More than 150 folk poets and singers of all ethnic groups attending the Third National Literary and Art Congress held a get-together on the evening of the 1st at the Chinese Folk Literature and Art Research Association.

The get-together was hosted by Lao She, Vice Chairman of the Chinese Folk Literature and Art Research Association. Zhou Yang, Vice Chairman of the Research Association, and famous writers, poets, and directors of the Research Association such as Zhao Shuli, Ouyang Shan, Ruan Zhangjing, Xu Chi, and Jia Zhi attended the get-together. At the get-together, folk artists enthusiastically praised the Party and Chairman Mao with bold verses and beautiful songs.

Wang Lao Jiu, a famous peasant poet from Shaanxi, recited his new work "Working People Love the Party," Pajie, a famous Mongolian storyteller, sang "Ode to Chairman Mao," and Bao Yindelegeer, a Mongolian soprano, sang "Hai Liu Horse" with her clear voice. Kang Langshuai, known as the Dai ethnic group's king of songs, sang "Chairman Mao's Sun Shines on Xishuangbanna" accompanied by the sound of the flute. Li Siyi, a young Lisu poet from the mountains of the Nujiang River, expressed the enthusiastic feelings of people attending this National Literary and Art Congress with his vivid and simple verses, which he recited at the meeting:
    "I crossed seventy-seven high mountains to Beijing,
    Not for anything else,
    Just to visit my dear Chairman Mao;
    I crossed ninety-nine rapids to the capital,
    Not for anything in particular,
    Just to listen to the Party's instructions and call."

Yunnan representative, known as the Dai ethnic group's king of songs, Kang Langshuai, sang his new work praising the Party and Chairman Mao at the get-together. (Xinhua News Agency reporter Li Jilu photo)

### 全国文代会按系统会议继续举行  代表们就文学、戏剧、音乐、美术、电影等方面的专门问题作了发言  文化部等举办了多种丰富多彩的展览会  出席文代会的各族民间诗人和歌手大联欢

1960-08-03
第4版()
专栏：

    全国文代会按系统会议继续举行
    代表们就文学、戏剧、音乐、美术、电影等方面的专门问题作了发言
    文化部等举办了多种丰富多彩的展览会
    出席文代会的各族民间诗人和歌手大联欢
    新华社2日讯　在全国第三次文代大会期间，中国作家协会、中国戏剧家协会、中国音乐家协会、中国美术家协会、中国电影工作者联谊会、中国曲艺工作者协会、中国舞蹈艺术研究会、中国民间文艺研究会、中国摄影学会召开的会员代表大会或理事扩大会，已经进行了四天。现在各个会议的分组讨论已经结束，有些会议的大会发言也已经结束。
    在四天来的大会发言中，有的代表就文学、戏剧、音乐、美术、电影、曲艺、舞蹈、民间文艺、摄影等方面的一些专门问题作了发言，有的代表介绍了本地区或本单位文艺活动中的成就和经验，有些从事专业或业余文艺活动的代表着重谈到了他们在创作与表演方面的一些体会。
    代表们在发言和讨论中，一致表示热烈拥护陆定一代表中共中央和国务院在全国第三次文代会上所作的祝词和周扬的报告。并且对各协会、研究会、联谊会、学会负责人在各系统会议上的报告，进行了热烈的讨论。代表们对于我国文学艺术工作几年来所取得的巨大成就一致感到非常兴奋，并且纷纷表示要在今后继续遵循毛泽东文艺思想，努力贯彻为工农兵服务的文艺方向和百花齐放、百家争鸣的文艺方针。
    各个会议明天将休会一天，后天继续举行。
    新华社1日讯　出席中国文学艺术工作者第三次代表大会的代表，连日来参观了由文化部和文学艺术界各组织举办的许多展览会。这些展览会是：中国作家协会和中国民间文艺研究会主办的“文学书籍和期刊展览会”、文化部电影事业管理局和中国电影工作者联谊会主办的“中国电影工作展览会”、文化部主办的“文化事业技术革新技术革命小型展览会”、中国美术家协会主办的“全国美术展览会”、中国戏剧家协会主办的“十年来戏剧工作成就展览会”、中国音乐家协会主办的“中国音乐协会及各地分会工作概况展览会”以及中国摄影学会主办的“摄影艺术展览会”等。
    各展览会通过实物、图片、模型、剪报资料等展品，反映了十年来，特别是大跃进以来，我国文学艺术的各个方面在毛泽东文艺路线的指导下所取得的伟大成就，显示了新中国的文学艺术事业在党的领导下，蓬勃发展的情况。
    据新华社2日讯　出席全国第三届文代大会的各族民间诗人和歌手一百五十多人，1日晚在中国民间文艺研究会举行了联欢晚会。
    晚会由中国民间文艺研究会副主席老舍主持。研究会副主席周扬，著名作家、诗人和研究会理事赵树理、欧阳山、阮章竞、徐迟、贾芝等都出席了晚会。晚会上民间艺术家们，用豪放的诗句和优美的歌唱热情地歌颂了党和毛主席。
    陕西著名农民诗人王老九朗诵了他的新作“劳动人民热爱党”，蒙古族著名说唱诗人帕杰演唱了“歌颂毛主席”，蒙古族女高音歌唱家宝音德力格尔用她嘹亮的歌喉唱了“海骝马”，被称为傣族歌王的康朗甩在笛声伴奏下唱了“毛主席的太阳照到了西双版纳”。一位来自怒江边高山上的傈僳族青年诗人李四益用他生动、朴实的诗句表达了人们出席这次全国文代会的热烈情绪，他在会上朗颂道：
    “我越过了七十七座高山来北京，
    不是为了什么，
    就是为了探望亲人毛主席；
    我跨过了九十九条急流来首都，
    不是为了哪样，
    就是为了听党的嘱咐和召唤。”
    云南代表、被称为傣族歌王的康朗甩，在联欢晚会上，唱了他歌颂党和毛主席的新作　新华社记者　李基禄摄
