Title: The Central Committee of the Vietnam Workers' Party Announces That a National Congress Will Be Held
Author:
Date: 1960-08-17
Page: 7

    Xinhua News Agency, Hanoi, August 15th: The Vietnam News Agency reported that a communiqué from the Central Committee of the Vietnam Workers' Party, published here today, stated that the Vietnam Workers' Party will hold its Third National Congress on September 5, 1960.
    The communiqué also stated that the date of the Third National Congress was decided by the Eighteenth Meeting of the Central Committee.
    The communiqué stated that the Eighteenth Meeting of the Central Committee heard reports on the congresses of party organizations at all levels, discussed and adopted the political report and the report on revising the Party Constitution to be submitted by the Central Committee to the Third National Congress. The meeting also adopted the draft revision of the Party Constitution.

### 越南劳动党中央宣布  将举行全国代表大会

1960-08-17
第7版()
专栏：

    越南劳动党中央宣布
    将举行全国代表大会
    新华社河内15日电　据越南通讯社报道，今天在这里公布的越南劳动党中央委员会的一项公报说，越南劳动党将在1960年9月5日举行第三次全国代表大会。
    公报又说，举行第三次全国代表大会的日期是党中央委员会第十八次会议决定的。
    公报说，中央委员会第十八次会议听取了关于各级党组织代表大会的报告，讨论并通过了中央委员会将向第三次全国代表大会提出的政治报告和修改党章的报告。这次会议还通过了党章的修改草案。
