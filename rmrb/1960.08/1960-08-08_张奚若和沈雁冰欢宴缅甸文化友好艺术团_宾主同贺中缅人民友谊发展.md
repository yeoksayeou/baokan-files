Title: Zhang Xiruo and Shen Yanbing Host Banquet for Burmese Cultural Friendship Art Troupe: Guests Join in Celebrating the Development of Sino-Burmese Friendship
Author:
Date: 1960-08-08
Page: 6
Xinhua News Agency, July 7th: Zhang Xiruo, Director of the Commission for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries, and Shen Yanbing, Minister of Culture, hosted a banquet at the Beijing Hotel tonight to warmly welcome the Burmese Cultural Friendship Art Troupe, headed by U Ba Swe, which brought the friendly sentiments of the Burmese people.

The peoples of China and Burma are good neighbors, “Pauk Phaw”. Tonight, Chinese and Burmese people and artists gathered together, and everyone seemed particularly happy. Zhang Xiruo and U Ba Swe spoke at the banquet, both warmly congratulating the continuous development of the traditional friendship between the Chinese and Burmese people on the basis of the Five Principles of Peaceful Coexistence. Their speeches aroused bursts of warm applause. The banquet was filled with a strong atmosphere of friendship from beginning to end.

Burmese Ambassador to China, Kyaw Winn, and embassy diplomatic officials were invited to attend the banquet.

Also present at the banquet were Xia Yan, Vice Minister of Culture; Chu Tunan and Zhang Zhixiang, Deputy Directors of the Commission for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries; Li Yimang, Chinese Ambassador to Burma; Zhang Youyu, President of the Sino-Burmese Friendship Association; Jian Bozan, Vice President; Zhou Erfu, Secretary-General of the Foreign Cultural Association; and Shen Ping, Deputy Director of the First Asian Department of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs.

In his welcoming speech at the banquet, Zhang Xiruo warmly praised the development of the traditional friendship between the Chinese and Burmese people on the basis of the Five Principles of Peaceful Coexistence. He pointed out that the signing of the Sino-Burmese Treaty of Friendship and Mutual Non-Aggression and the Sino-Burmese Boundary Question Agreement, as well as the recent successful talks between the representatives of the Sino-Burmese Joint Boundary Committee, have brought the traditional friendly relations between China and Burma into a new historical stage.

Zhang Xiruo said that with the increasing development of Sino-Burmese friendly relations, cultural exchanges between the two countries are also becoming more frequent. Zhang Xiruo called the Burmese artists envoys of cultural friendship. He said, we believe that through our joint efforts, the flowers of Sino-Burmese friendship will bloom more brightly and beautifully.

In his speech,团长 U Ba Swe said that the Burmese Cultural Friendship Art Troupe came to China at a very auspicious time. He said that recently, our two countries are friendly resolving the border issues between China and Burma, which shows that the friendship between China and Burma is being consolidated and developed.

U Ba Swe said that although it was his first time to China, the warm welcome and hospitality from the Chinese side made him feel as if he had returned to his home after a long separation. He said that this also shows that the two countries are truly abiding by the Five Principles of Peaceful Coexistence jointly advocated by the two Prime Ministers, and the friendly relations between the two countries are very solid.

U Ba Swe also talked about the development of cultural and artistic exchanges between the two countries, and he expressed his happiness that China is learning Burmese art and dance.

Shen Yanbing, Ambassador Kyaw Winn, and Ambassador Li Yimang also made enthusiastic and friendly speeches at the banquet.

People from the capital's music, drama, and dance circles attended the banquet.

### 张奚若和沈雁冰欢宴缅甸文化友好艺术团  宾主同贺中缅人民友谊发展

1960-08-08
第6版()
专栏：

    张奚若和沈雁冰欢宴缅甸文化友好艺术团
    宾主同贺中缅人民友谊发展
    新华社7日讯　对外文化联络委员会主任张奚若和文化部部长沈雁冰今晚在北京饭店举行宴会，热烈欢迎带来了缅甸人民友好情谊的以团长吴巴盛为首的缅甸文化友好艺术团。
    中缅两国人民是好邻居，是“胞波”。今晚，中缅两国人士和两国艺术家欢聚一起，大家显得格外欢乐。张奚若和吴巴盛在宴会上讲了话，都热情地祝中缅两国人民的传统友谊在和平共处五项原则的基础上不断发展，他们的讲话激起一阵阵热烈的掌声。宴会自始至终洋溢着浓厚的友好气氛。
    缅甸驻中国大使叫温和大使馆外交官员应邀出席了宴会。
    出席宴会的，有文化部副部长夏衍，对外文化联络委员会副主任楚图南、张致祥，我国驻缅甸大使李一氓，中缅友好协会会长张友渔、副会长翦伯赞，对外文化协会秘书长周而复，外交部第一亚洲司副司长沈平等。
    张奚若在宴会上致欢迎词时，热烈称颂中缅两国人民的传统友谊在和平共处五项原则基础上的发展。他指出，中缅友好互不侵犯条约和中缅边界问题协定的签订，中缅边界联合委员会双方代表最近会谈的成功，使中缅两国传统友好关系进入到一个新的历史阶段。
    张奚若说，随着中缅友好关系的日益发展，两国的文化交流也日益频繁。张奚若称缅甸艺术家们是文化友好的使者。他说，我们相信，通过我们的共同努力，中缅友谊的花朵会开得更加鲜艳美丽。
    吴巴盛团长在讲话中说，缅甸文化友好艺术团是在一个非常吉祥的时间里来到中国的。他说，最近我们两国正在友好地解决中缅两国的边界问题，这说明中缅两国的友谊正在巩固和发展。
    吴巴盛说，他虽然是第一次到中国来，但是中国方面的热情欢迎和款待，使他感到好像回到离别很久的家里一样。他说，这也说明两国真正遵守着两国总理所共同倡议的和平共处五项原则，两国的友好关系非常巩固。
    吴巴盛还讲到了两国文化艺术交流的发展，他表示他对中国学习缅甸的艺术、舞蹈感到很高兴。
    沈雁冰、叫温大使和李一氓大使，也都在宴会上作了热情、友好的讲话。
    首都音乐、戏剧、舞蹈界人士出席了宴会。
