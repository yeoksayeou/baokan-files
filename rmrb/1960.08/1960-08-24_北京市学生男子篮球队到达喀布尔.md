Title: Beijing Student Men's Basketball Team Arrives in Kabul
Author:
Date: 1960-08-24
Page: 5

    Xinhua News Agency, Kabul, August 23rd: The Beijing Student Men's Basketball Team, headed by Ren Chao, a group of fifteen people, arrived in Kabul, the capital of the Kingdom of Afghanistan, yesterday at the invitation of the Afghan Olympic Federation. This basketball team will participate in friendly matches with the Afghan basketball team during the Afghan Independence Day sports meet.

### 北京市学生男子篮球队到达喀布尔

1960-08-24
第5版()
专栏：

    北京市学生男子篮球队到达喀布尔
    新华社喀布尔23日电　以任超为首的北京市学生男子篮球队一行十五人，应阿富汗奥林匹克联合会的邀请，于昨天到达阿富汗王国首都喀布尔。这个篮球队将在阿富汗独立节运动会上同阿富汗篮球队进行友谊比赛。
