Title: Delegation of the Korean-Chinese Friendship Association Leaves Beijing for Home; People from All Walks of Life in the Capital Gather at the Station to Bid Farewell to the Distinguished Guests
Author:
Date: 1960-08-30
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency, August 29th. The distinguished guests of the delegation of the Korean-Chinese Friendship Association, headed by Chairman Kim Jong-heng and Vice-Chairman Park Fung-shik, after celebrating the 15th anniversary of the liberation of Korea with the Chinese people and visiting various places, left Beijing by train this morning to return home.

Today, more than 200 people from all walks of life in the capital went to the station to bid farewell to the distinguished guests. Girls presented flowers to all the distinguished guests, and people sang in unison and applauded for a long time, thanking the envoys of the fraternal Korean people for coming with the great friendship of the Korean people and leaving with the friendship of the Chinese people. Chairman Kim Jong-heng waved his hands to greet everyone and excitedly shouted "Goodbye" in Chinese.

Those who went to the station to see them off today included Vice-Presidents of the Sino-Korean Friendship Association, Lao She and Wei Chuantong; Acting Director of the Commission for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries, Zhang Zhixiang; Vice-Director, Chu Tunan; Vice Minister of Education, Dong Chuncai; Vice Mayor of Beijing, Wu Han; as well as council members of the Sino-Korean Friendship Association and people from various fields, Zhou Peiyuan, Hu Jiwei, Cao Mengjun, Zhou Weizhi, Huang Luofeng, Xia Nai, Wang Jiong, Deng Fangzhi, the mother of martyr Huang Jiguang, and others. They cordially shook hands with Kim Jong-heng and other distinguished guests to bid farewell.

Ma Dongshan, Counselor of the Embassy of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea in China, and other officials also went to the station to see them off.

### 朝中友协代表团离京回国  首都各界群众到车站欢送贵宾

1960-08-30
第4版()
专栏：

    朝中友协代表团离京回国
    首都各界群众到车站欢送贵宾
    新华社29日讯　以团长金钟恒、副团长朴丰植为首的朝中友协代表团的贵宾们，同我国人民一起欢庆朝鲜解放十五周年和在各地访问之后，于今天上午乘火车离开北京回国。
    今天首都各界群众二百多人到车站欢送贵宾。姑娘们向全体贵宾献了鲜花，人们齐声歌唱并且长时间地鼓掌，感谢兄弟的朝鲜人民的使者带着朝鲜人民的盛意而来，带着中国人民的友谊而去。金钟恒团长挥动着双手向大家致意，激动地用中国话高呼“再见”。
    今天到车站欢送的，有中朝友协副会长老舍、魏传统，对外文委代主任张致祥、副主任楚图南，教育部副部长董纯才，北京市副市长吴晗，以及中朝友协理事和各方面的人士周培源、胡绩伟、曹孟君、周巍峙、黄洛峰、夏鼐、王炯，黄继光烈士的母亲邓芳芝等。他们和金钟恒等贵宾们亲切地一一握手告别。
    朝鲜民主主义人民共和国驻中国大使馆参赞马东山等官员，也到车站送行。
