Title: Guangdong Provincial Party and Government Delegation Invited to Visit Vietnam
Author:
Date: 1960-08-17
Page: 7

Xinhua News Agency, Guangzhou, 16th. At the invitation of the Hai Ninh Provincial Committee of the Vietnamese Labor Party and the government, the Guangdong Provincial Party and Government Delegation, headed by Lin Qiangyun, Standing Committee Member of the Guangdong Provincial Committee of the Chinese Communist Party and Vice Governor of Guangdong Province, left for Vietnam on the 15th to attend the National Day celebrations for the 15th anniversary of the founding of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam.

### 我广东省党政代表团应邀赴越南

1960-08-17
第7版()
专栏：

    我广东省党政代表团应邀赴越南
    新华社广州16日电　应越南劳动党海宁省委和政府的邀请，前往参加越南民主共和国成立十五周年国庆观礼的广东省党政代表团，在团长、中共广东省委常委、广东省副省长林锵云的率领下，于15日启程前往越南。
