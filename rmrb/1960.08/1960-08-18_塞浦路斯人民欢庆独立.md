Title: Cypriot People Celebrate Independence
Author:
Date: 1960-08-18
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, Nicosia, August 17th: Cyprus, which has suffered long and brutal rule by British colonialists, declared its independence at dawn on August 16th amidst the roar of cannons, establishing the Republic of Cyprus. On the independence day, Nicosia, the capital of Cyprus, was filled with a joyful atmosphere. Flags fluttered in the streets and squares, decorated with colorful lights, and citizens gathered in groups to cheer for the independence in the streets. Late on the 15th, more than 20,000 people from all walks of life gathered outside the House of Representatives to hear Archbishop Makarios, the President of the Republic, officially declare independence.

### 塞浦路斯人民欢庆独立

1960-08-18
第5版()
专栏：

    塞浦路斯人民欢庆独立
    据新华社17日讯　尼科西亚消息：经受了英国殖民主义者长期残酷统治的塞浦路斯，8月16日凌晨在隆隆礼炮声中宣告独立，建立为塞浦路斯共和国。在独立节日，塞浦路斯的首都尼科西亚充满着欢乐气氛，在大街上和广场上旗帜飘扬，装饰着五颜六色的灯，市民们成群结队在街上为庆祝独立而欢呼。15日深夜，两万多各阶层人民聚集在众议院外面听共和国总统马卡里奥斯大主教正式宣布独立。
