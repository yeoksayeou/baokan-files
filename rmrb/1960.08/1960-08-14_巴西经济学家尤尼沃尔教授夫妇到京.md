Title: Brazilian Economist Professor Eunival and Wife Arrive in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1960-08-14
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, 13th -- Brazilian economist Professor Caio da Silva Prado Eunival and his wife, Professor Helena Maria Niuaak, arrived in Beijing this afternoon for a visit to China at the invitation of the Chinese Latin American Friendship Association.

Wang Jiabin, Deputy Secretary-General of the Chinese Latin American Friendship Association, and others were at the airport to welcome them.

### 巴西经济学家尤尼沃尔教授夫妇到京

1960-08-14
第6版()
专栏：

    巴西经济学家尤尼沃尔教授夫妇到京
    新华社13日讯　应中国拉丁美洲友好协会邀请来我国访问的巴西经济学家卡约·达席尔瓦·普拉多·尤尼沃尔教授及其夫人埃莱娜·玛丽亚·尼奥阿克教授，今天下午到达北京。
    到机场欢迎的有中国拉丁美洲友好协会副总干事王家宾等人。
