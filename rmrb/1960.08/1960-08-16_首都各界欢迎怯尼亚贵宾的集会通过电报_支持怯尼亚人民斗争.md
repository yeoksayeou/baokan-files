Title: Capital's Circles Hold Rally to Welcome Kenyan Guests and Pass Telegram Supporting Kenyan People's Struggle
Author:
Date: 1960-08-16
Page: 5
Xinhua News Agency, 15th - The rally held today in the capital by people from all walks of life to welcome Mr. Oginga Odinga, Vice President of the Kenya African National Union, passed a telegram supporting the Kenyan people's struggle. The full text of the telegram is as follows:

To the Kenya African National Union and to
The People of Kenya:

The Chinese Afro-Asian Solidarity Committee, the Chinese-African People's Friendship Association, and various Chinese people's organizations held a rally on August 15th to warmly welcome Mr. Oginga Odinga, Vice President of the Kenya African National Union. Through Mr. Odinga, we extend our heartfelt and fraternal respect to the Kenyan people who are bravely fighting against imperialism and for national independence.

The Chinese people have always deeply sympathized with and firmly supported the Kenyan people's just struggle for national independence and against imperialism. The Chinese people firmly demand the immediate removal of all imperialist military bases and military forces in Kenya! We firmly demand the release of Kenyan national leader Jomo Kenyatta! We firmly demand the immediate return to the Kenyan people of all the land seized by the colonialists! We firmly demand that all imperialist forces get out of Kenya!

The Chinese people firmly believe that, under the strong encouragement and support of the anti-imperialist struggle of the people of the world, through unity and perseverance in struggle, the Kenyan people's struggle for national independence will surely achieve final and complete victory at an early date.

Long live the heroic Kenyan people and African people!
Long live the friendship and solidarity between the Chinese and Kenyan people!

August 15, 1960

### 首都各界欢迎怯尼亚贵宾的集会通过电报  支持怯尼亚人民斗争

1960-08-16
第5版()
专栏：

    首都各界欢迎怯尼亚贵宾的集会通过电报
    支持怯尼亚人民斗争
    新华社15日讯　首都各界人民在今天欢迎怯尼亚非洲民族联盟副主席奥金加·奥廷加的集会上，通过了支持怯尼亚人民斗争的电报。电报全文如下：
    怯尼亚非洲民族联盟并转
    怯尼亚人民：
    中国亚非团结委员会和中国非洲人民友好协会及中国各人民团体于八月十五日举行集会，热烈欢迎怯尼亚非洲民族联盟副主席奥金加·奥廷加先生，并通过奥廷加先生，向正在反对帝国主义和争取民族独立而英勇斗争的怯尼亚人民，致以衷心的、兄弟的敬意。
    中国人民一向深切同情和坚决支持怯尼亚人民争取民族独立和反对帝国主义的正义斗争。中国人民坚决要求立即撤销帝国主义在怯尼亚的一切军事基地和军事力量！坚决要求释放怯尼亚民族领袖乔莫·肯雅塔！坚决要求立即把殖民主义者所抢夺的全部土地归还怯尼亚人民！坚决要求一切帝国主义的势力从怯尼亚滚出去！
    中国人民深信，在全世界人民反帝斗争的强大鼓舞和支持下，怯尼亚人民通过团结一致、坚持斗争，怯尼亚人民争取民族独立的斗争，一定能早日获得最后的、彻底的胜利。
    英勇的怯尼亚人民和非洲人民万岁！
    中国和怯尼亚人民友好团结万岁！
                        1960年8月15日
