Title: Ambassador Huang Hua Calls on Ghanaian Foreign Minister
Author:
Date: 1960-08-28
Page: 6
Xinhua News Agency, Accra, August 26th: Chinese Ambassador to Ghana, Huang Hua, called on the Ghanaian Minister of Foreign Affairs, Ako Adjei, today.

Ambassador Huang Hua discussed the presentation of his credentials with the Ghanaian Foreign Minister.

### 黄华大使拜会加纳外长

1960-08-28
第6版()
专栏：

    黄华大使拜会加纳外长
    新华社阿克拉26日电　中国驻加纳大使黄华今天拜会了加纳外交部长阿科·阿杰伊。
    黄华大使同加纳外交部长讨论了呈递国书的事项。
