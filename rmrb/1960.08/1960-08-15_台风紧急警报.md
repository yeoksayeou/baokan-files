Title: Typhoon Emergency Alert
Author:
Date: 1960-08-15
Page: 3

    Typhoon Emergency Alert
    Xinhua News Agency, 14th. The Central Meteorological Research Institute issued a typhoon emergency alert at 8:00 AM on the 14th: The twelfth typhoon of 1960 was centered about 300 kilometers east of northern Taiwan at 2:00 AM this morning, at approximately 24.5 degrees north latitude and 125 degrees east longitude. The maximum wind force near the center is level 8 to 9. The typhoon center will move in a west-northwest direction at a speed of 10 to 14 knots per hour. It will make landfall along the coast of southern Fujian and eastern Guangdong tomorrow during the day. There will be level 6 to 8 gales in the eastern Guangdong coastal areas, the Fujian coastal areas, and the Zhejiang coastal areas, with winds of level 8 to 10 near the landfall point. After landfall, the typhoon will continue to move in a west-northwest direction. From the 14th to the 16th, eastern Zhejiang, eastern Fujian, eastern and northern Guangdong will experience continuous heavy to torrential rains.

### 台风紧急警报

1960-08-15
第3版()
专栏：

    台风紧急警报
    新华社14日讯　中央气象科学研究所14日上午八时发布台风紧急警报：1960年第十二号台风，今天早晨二时中心位置在台湾北部以东三百公里左右的海面上，就是北纬二十四·五度东经一百二十五度附近，中心附近最大风力有八至九级，未来台风中心将以每小时十至十四浬的速度向偏西方向移动，明天白天将在福建南部、广东东部一带沿海登陆。广东东部沿海、福建沿海、浙江沿海，将有六至八级大风，其中登陆地点附近风力可达八至十级。台风登陆后将继续向偏西方向移动，14—16日浙江东部、福建东部、广东东部、北部将连续有大雨到暴雨。
