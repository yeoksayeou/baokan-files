Title: Moscow Tennis Tournament Continues; Chinese Athletes Obtain Doubles Quarterfinal Berth
Author:
Date: 1960-08-26
Page: 5

    Moscow Tennis Tournament Continues
    Chinese Athletes Obtain Doubles Quarterfinal Berth
    Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, August 25th. The Moscow Thirteen-Nation International Tennis Tournament continued on the 24th. Chinese athletes have secured a spot in the doubles quarterfinals.
    After Chinese athletes Zhu Zhenhua and Mei Fooki won their first-round doubles match, they competed against Soviet athletes Leyus and Palmus on this day. They won with scores of 10-8 and 6-4, thus securing their place in the quarterfinals.

### 莫斯科网球赛继续举行  我国选手取得双打复赛权

1960-08-26
第5版()
专栏：

    莫斯科网球赛继续举行
    我国选手取得双打复赛权
    新华社莫斯科25日电　莫斯科十三国国际网球赛24日继续举行。中国选手取得了双打复赛权。
    中国选手朱振华和梅福基取得了双打第一轮的胜利后，这一天同苏联选手列尤斯和帕尔马斯举行了比赛。结果以十比八，六比四获胜而取得了复赛权。
