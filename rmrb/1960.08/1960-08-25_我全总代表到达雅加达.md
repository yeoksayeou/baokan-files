Title: ACFTU Representative Arrives in Jakarta
Author:
Date: 1960-08-25
Page: 5

    Xinhua News Agency, Jakarta, August 24th: Zhang Xiuzhu, representative of the All-China Federation of Trade Unions (ACFTU), and his party arrived in Jakarta at midnight on August 23rd. He was invited to attend the Third National Congress of the All-Indonesia Central Organization of Trade Unions. He will leave for Solo tomorrow.

### 我全总代表到达雅加达

1960-08-25
第5版()
专栏：

    我全总代表到达雅加达
    新华社雅加达24日电　中华全国总工会代表张修竹等一行人在8月23日午夜抵达雅加达。他是应邀出席全印度尼西亚中央职工会第三次全国代表大会的。他将在明天动身前往梭罗。
