Title: Our Ambassador Presents Credentials to the King of Nepal; King Mahendra Says the Friendly Relations Between China and Nepal Will Always Be a Brilliant Example of Cooperation and Peaceful Coexistence Between Two Neighboring Countries
Author:
Date: 1960-08-07
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Kathmandu, 6th - Zhang Shijie, the Ambassador Extraordinary and Plenipotentiary of the People's Republic of China to the Kingdom of Nepal, presented his credentials to King Mahendra of Nepal on the afternoon of August 5th.

Accompanying Ambassador Zhang Shijie were Liu Yamin, First Secretary of the Chinese Embassy, and Li Zhao, Attaché.

Present at the presentation of credentials were Nepalese Prime Minister Koirala, the Speaker of the Lower House, the Chief Justice, the Commander-in-Chief of the Army, the Deputy Prime Minister, cabinet ministers, and important civil and military officials of the Nepalese government.

When presenting his credentials, Ambassador Zhang Shijie, on behalf of Chairman Liu Shaoqi of the People's Republic of China and the Chinese people, extended sincere respect to King Mahendra and the people of Nepal, and expressed heartfelt wishes for the prosperity and strength of the Kingdom of Nepal and the well-being and happiness of the Nepalese people.

Ambassador Zhang Shijie said, "China and Nepal are neighbors with contiguous borders and have a long history of close historical and cultural ties. Since the establishment of diplomatic relations between our two countries and the establishment of the Five Principles of Peaceful Coexistence as the basic principles guiding our bilateral relations, the traditional friendship between the two countries has been further developed. In particular, the recent mutual visits between the Prime Ministers and Premiers of the two countries have further enhanced mutual understanding and friendship between the peoples of China and Nepal. The treaties and agreements signed by both sides during the mutual visits mark a new stage in the friendly relations between China and Nepal. The Chinese government and people cherish the friendship with Nepal very much and are willing to continue to work for the maintenance and development of the traditional friendly relations between our two countries."

Ambassador Zhang Shijie finally stated that he will devote himself to consolidating and developing the friendly and cooperative relations between China and Nepal.

In his reply, King Mahendra expressed great pleasure in welcoming Zhang Shijie as the first ambassador of China to the Kingdom of Nepal.

The King of Nepal said, "The friendly relations between Nepal and China, as Your Excellency has rightly pointed out, have a long and close connection." He said, "We attach great importance to Your Excellency's statement regarding the desire of your government to maintain friendly relations with Nepal, and we sincerely convey this desire of our government to your government. Your country's generous assistance to Nepal's development, as well as the mutual visits between your Prime Minister and our Prime Minister, are clear evidence of our common desire to maintain and strengthen this traditional friendship."

The King of Nepal said, "We are sure that the establishment of your embassy in our capital will bring our two countries closer together. We are convinced that during your tenure as ambassador to our country, the friendly relations between our two countries will be greatly strengthened. We hope that the friendly relations between Nepal and the People's Republic of China will always be a shining example of cooperation and peaceful coexistence between two neighboring countries."

Finally, the King of Nepal, on behalf of the Nepalese government, people, and in his own name, extended greetings and wishes to the Chairman, government, and people of the People's Republic of China, and wished Ambassador Zhang Shijie's mission complete success.

### 我大使向尼泊尔国王递交国书  马亨德拉国王说，尼中两国的友好关系将永远是两个邻国合作和和平共处的光辉榜样

1960-08-07
第1版()
专栏：

    我大使向尼泊尔国王递交国书
    马亨德拉国王说，尼中两国的友好关系将永远是两个邻国合作和和平共处的光辉榜样
    新华社6日讯　加德满都消息：中华人民共和国驻尼泊尔王国特命全权大使张世杰8月5日下午向尼泊尔国王马亨德拉递交国书。
    随同张世杰大使递交国书的有中国大使馆一等秘书刘亚民和随员李钊。
    递交国书时在场的有尼泊尔首相柯伊拉腊、下议院议长、首席法官、陆军总司令和副首相、内阁大臣以及尼泊尔政府重要文武官员等。
    张世杰大使在递交国书时代表中华人民共和国主席刘少奇和中国人民向尼泊尔国王马亨德拉和人民表示诚挚的敬意，并且对尼泊尔王国的繁荣富强和尼泊尔人民的兴旺幸福表示衷心的祝愿。
    张世杰大使说，“中尼两国国境毗连，在历史上和文化上都有悠久密切的联系。自从我们两国建立外交关系和确立以和平共处五项原则为指导两国关系的基本原则以来，两国间的传统友谊得到了新的发展。特别是不久前两国总理和首相的相互访问，进一步增进了中尼两国人民的相互了解和友谊。在相互访问期间双方签订的各项条约和协定，标志着中尼两国的友好关系进入了一个新的阶段。中国政府和人民非常珍视同尼泊尔的友谊，并且愿意继续为维护和发展我们两国之间的传统友好关系而不断努力。”
    张世杰大使最后表示，将致力于巩固和发展中尼两国间的友好合作关系。
    马亨德拉国王在致答辞时，表示以非常高兴的心情欢迎张世杰就任中国驻尼泊尔王国的首任大使。
    尼泊尔国王说，“尼泊尔和中国的友好关系，正如阁下所正确指出的那样，是有悠久密切的联系。”他说，“我们非常重视阁下所谈到的贵国政府要同尼泊尔保持友好关系的愿望，我们也真诚地向贵国政府转达我国政府的这种愿望。贵国对尼泊尔发展方面的慷慨援助以及贵国总理和我国首相的互相访问是我们要维护和加强这种传统友好关系的共同愿望的明证。”
    尼泊尔国王说，“我们肯定，贵国在我国首都建立大使馆将使我们两国的关系更为密切。我们深信，在你就任我国大使期间，我们两国的友好关系将大大加强。我们希望，尼泊尔和中华人民共和国的友好关系将永远是两个邻国合作和和平共处的光辉榜样。”
    尼泊尔国王最后代表尼泊尔政府、人民和以他本人的名义对中华人民共和国主席、政府和人民致意和祝愿，并且祝张世杰大使的使命获得全面成功。
