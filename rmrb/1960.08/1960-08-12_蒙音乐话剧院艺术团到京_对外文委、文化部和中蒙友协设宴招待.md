Title: Mongolian Music and Drama Troupe Arrives in Beijing; Commission for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries, Ministry of Culture, and Sino-Mongolian Friendship Association Host Banquet

Author:

Date: 1960-08-12
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency dispatch: The Mongolian People's Republic's Khovd Province Music and Drama Troupe, consisting of fifty-two members and led by Head of the Troupe Guulagcha and Deputy Head of the Troupe Tsebeng Pürbü, arrived in Beijing by train on the evening of the 10th, at the invitation of the 1960 executive plan of the Sino-Mongolian Cultural Cooperation Agreement.

The Commission for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries, the Ministry of Culture, and the Sino-Mongolian Friendship Association held a banquet at the Qianmen Hotel on the evening of the 11th to warmly welcome the Mongolian People's Republic's Khovd Province Music and Drama Troupe headed by Guulagcha to visit and perform in our country.

Toiv, Charge d'Affaires ad interim of the Mongolian People's Republic's Embassy in China, was also invited to attend the banquet. Zhang Zhixiang, Vice-Chairman of the Commission for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries and President of the Sino-Mongolian Friendship Association, delivered a speech at the banquet, warmly welcoming the Mongolian Khovd Province Music and Drama Troupe to visit and perform in our country. He said, we regard you not only as envoys of art, but also as envoys of friendship between the Chinese and Mongolian people. Your visit will not only provide learning opportunities for our country's literary and art workers and contribute to the cultural exchange between our two countries, but will also contribute to further deepening the friendship between our two peoples.

In his speech, Guulagcha, the head of the art troupe, enthusiastically praised the achievements of the Chinese people in politics, economy, and culture, and said that this visit to China provides us with a good opportunity to learn from Chinese artists.

The speeches of Zhang Zhixiang and Guulagcha were continuously met with warm applause.

The banquet was filled with brotherly affection. Qi Yanming, Vice Minister of Culture, Toiv, Charge d'Affaires ad interim of the Mongolian Embassy in China, and others enthusiastically invited everyone to raise their glasses and toast to the unbreakable friendship between the Chinese and Mongolian people, to the development of cultural exchange between China and Mongolia, to the unity of the socialist camp headed by the Soviet Union, and to world peace!

Also present at the banquet were Cao Yu, Vice President of the Sino-Mongolian Friendship Association, Ma Yanxiang, Deputy Director of the Bureau of Arts Administration of the Ministry of Culture, Zhao Feng, Secretary of the Secretariat of the Chinese Musicians Association, and well-known figures from the capital's literary and art circles such as Chen Yading, Wang Yuanfang, and Li Chao.

### 蒙音乐话剧院艺术团到京  对外文委、文化部和中蒙友协设宴招待

1960-08-12
第6版()
专栏：

    蒙音乐话剧院艺术团到京
    对外文委、文化部和中蒙友协设宴招待
    据新华社讯　根据中蒙文化合作协定1960年执行计划，应邀来我国访问演出的蒙古人民共和国科布多省音乐话剧院艺术团一行五十二人，在团长古尔拉格查、副团长策本普尔布的率领下，于10日晚乘火车到达北京。
    对外文化联络委员会、文化部、中蒙友好协会11日晚在前门饭店举行宴会，热烈欢迎来我国访问演出的以古尔拉格查为首的蒙古人民共和国科布多省音乐话剧院艺术团。
    蒙古人民共和国驻中国大使馆临时代办托依夫也应邀出席了宴会。对外文委副主任、中蒙友好协会会长张致祥在宴会上首先致词，热烈欢迎蒙古科布多省音乐话剧院艺术团前来我国访问演出。他说，我们不但把你们看成是艺术的使者，而且看成是中蒙两国人民友谊的使者。你们的访问，不但对我国的文艺工作者提供了学习的机会，对我们两国的文化交流事业作出贡献，而且将对进一步加深我们两国人民的友谊做出贡献。
    艺术团团长古尔拉格查在讲话中热情地赞扬了中国人民在政治、经济、文化各方面所取得的成就后说，这次来中国访问，提供了我们向中国艺术家们学习的良好机会。
    张致祥和古尔拉格查的讲话不断地获得热烈掌声。
    宴会上，洋溢着兄弟般的情谊。文化部副部长齐燕铭、蒙古驻中国大使馆临时代办托依夫等相互热情地请大家举杯，为中蒙两国人民牢不可破的友谊，为中蒙两国文化交流的发展，为以苏联为首的社会主义阵营的团结，为世界和平干杯！
    出席宴会的还有中蒙友好协会副会长曹禺，文化部艺术事业管理局副局长马彦祥，中国音乐家协会书记处书记赵沨，以及首都文艺界著名人士陈亚丁、王元方、李超等。
