Title: Myanmar's "Han Jiao Yue Li Bao" Says Sino-Burmese Border Meeting is Encouraging, Cambodian Weekly Praises the Friendly and Mutual Understanding Attitude of China and Myanmar

Author:

Date: 1960-08-09
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, Rangoon, August 4th: An article published in today's "Han Jiao Yue Li Bao" of Myanmar stated that "the agreement reached by both sides on important issues at the second meeting of the Sino-Burmese Joint Boundary Committee in Beijing is encouraging." The article pointed out, "The smooth and successful progress of the boundary issue discussion is due to the clear belief of both sides' representatives in the Five Principles of Peaceful Coexistence, which enabled them to overcome difficulties and reach satisfactory agreements in the talks." The article concluded by saying that China and Myanmar will continue to use the Five Principles of Peaceful Coexistence to handle bilateral relations in the future.

Xinhua News Agency, Phnom Penh, August 8th: The Cambodian weekly "Nationalist" said that China and Myanmar's resolution of complex border issues on the basis of friendship and understanding is a significant achievement. Any complex problem can be solved as long as both sides are sincere and earnest. The newspaper said that the Chinese and Myanmar delegations held negotiations in a spirit of cooperation and understanding, and the negotiations were conducted in a friendly atmosphere.

### 缅甸“罕礁越利报”说  中缅边界会议令人鼓舞  柬一周刊赞扬中缅双方的友好互谅态度

1960-08-09
第6版()
专栏：

    缅甸“罕礁越利报”说
    中缅边界会议令人鼓舞
    柬一周刊赞扬中缅双方的友好互谅态度
    新华社仰光4日电　“罕礁越利报”今天刊载的一篇文章说，“在北京举行的中缅边界联合委员会第二次会议上双方就重要问题达成了协议，这是令人鼓舞的。”这篇文章指出，“边界问题的讨论能顺利和成功地进行，是由于双方代表对和平共处的五项原则有明确的信念，从而在会谈中克服了困难并取得令人满意的协议。”文章最后说，中缅两国在将来将继续运用和平共处的五项原则来处理两国关系问题。
  据新华社金边8日电　柬埔寨“民族主义者”周刊说，中国和缅甸在友好和谅解的基础上解决复杂的边界问题，这是有重大意义的成就。只要双方都有诚意而且认真，任何复杂的问题都能够得到解决。报纸说，中缅两国代表团本着合作和谅解的精神举行谈判，谈判是在友好气氛中进行的。
