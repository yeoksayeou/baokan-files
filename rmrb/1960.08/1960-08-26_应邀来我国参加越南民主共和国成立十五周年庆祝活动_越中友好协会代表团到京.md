Title: Vietnamese-Chinese Friendship Association Delegation Arrives in Beijing to Attend Celebrations for the 15th Anniversary of the Founding of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam
Author:
Date: 1960-08-26
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, August 25th: At the invitation of the Chinese-Vietnamese Friendship Association to attend the celebrations for the 15th anniversary of the founding of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam, the Vietnamese-Chinese Friendship Association delegation, carrying the deep friendship of the Vietnamese people, arrived in Beijing by train this morning.

The head of the Vietnamese-Chinese Friendship Association delegation is Tran Huy Lieu, Vice President of the Vietnam-China Association, member of the Standing Committee of the National Assembly, and Director of the Institute of History of the Vietnam National Science Committee. The members include Nguyen Xuan Thuyet, Executive Committee member of the Thanh Hoa Provincial Branch of the Vietnam-China Friendship Association and Chairman of the Thanh Hoa Provincial Fatherland Front Committee; Hoang Van Tang, Vice President of the Nghe An Provincial Branch of the Vietnam-China Friendship Association and Vice Chairman of the Nghe An Provincial Fatherland Front Committee; Nguyen Huu Tan, Executive Committee member of the Hong Quang District Branch of the Vietnam-China Friendship Association and Deputy Director of the Hong Quang District Cultural Bureau; and Luong Thi Khiem (female), Executive Committee member of the Lang Son Branch of the Vietnam-China Friendship Association, member of the Van Uyen County Administrative Committee, and Chairman of the County Women's Federation.

The envoys of the Vietnamese people were warmly welcomed by people from all walks of life in the capital at the Beijing Railway Station. Young girls presented bouquets to all the distinguished guests.

Among those who welcomed them at the station were Sun Xiao Cun, Vice President of the Chinese-Vietnamese Friendship Association; Zhang Zhixiang, Acting Director of the Commission for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries; Ji Pengfei, Vice Minister of Foreign Affairs; Xia Yan, Vice Minister of Culture; Feng Jiping, Deputy Mayor of Beijing; Deng Gang, Jin Zhao, Zhang E, and Yang Shaoren, members of the Council of the Chinese-Vietnamese Friendship Association; and people from all walks of life in the capital.

Tran Trong Quat, Charge d'Affaires ad interim of the Embassy of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam in China, and diplomatic officials of the Embassy were also at the station to welcome them. (Attached picture)

The head of the Vietnamese-Chinese Friendship Association delegation, Tran Huy Lieu (center), walks out of the station accompanied by Sun Xiao Cun (right), Vice President of the Chinese-Vietnamese Friendship Association, and others. The first person on the left is Tran Trong Quat, Charge d'Affaires ad interim of the Embassy of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam in China. (Xinhua News Agency reporter Ta Jigu Le)

### 应邀来我国参加越南民主共和国成立十五周年庆祝活动  越中友好协会代表团到京

1960-08-26
第1版()
专栏：

    应邀来我国参加越南民主共和国成立十五周年庆祝活动
    越中友好协会代表团到京
    新华社25日讯　应中越友好协会邀请前来我国参加越南民主共和国成立十五周年庆祝活动的越南中国友好协会代表团，带着越南人民的深厚情谊，于今天上午乘火车到达北京。
    越中友协代表团团长是越中协会副会长、国会常务委员会委员、越南国家科学委员会史学院院长陈辉燎，团员有越中友协清化省分会执行委员、清化省祖国战线委员会主席阮春邃，越中友协宜安省分会副会长、宜安省祖国战线委员会副主席黄文唐，越中友协鸿广区分会执行委员、鸿广区文化局副局长阮友新，越中友协谅山分会执行委员、文渊县行政委员会委员、县妇联会长梁氏谦（女）。
    越南人民的使者在北京车站受到首都各方面人士的热烈欢迎。年青的姑娘们向全体贵宾献了鲜花。
    到车站欢迎的，有中越友好协会副会长孙晓村，对外文委代主任张致祥，外交部副部长姬鹏飞，文化部副部长夏衍，北京市副市长冯基平，中越友好协会理事邓岗、金照、张谔、杨少任等，以及首都各方面的人士。
    越南民主共和国驻中国大使馆临时代办陈仲括和大使馆外交官员，也到车站迎接。（附图片）
    越中友协代表团团长陈辉燎（中）在中越友协副会长孙晓村（右）等陪同下步出车站。左起第一人是越南民主共和国驻中国大使馆临时代办陈仲括。新华社记者　塔吉古勒摄
