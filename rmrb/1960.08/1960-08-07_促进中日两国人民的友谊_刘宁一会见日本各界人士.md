Title: Promoting Friendship Between the Chinese and Japanese People: Liu Ningyi Meets with People from All Walks of Life in Japan
Author: 
Date: 1960-08-07
Page: 6
    Xinhua News Agency, Tokyo, August 6th: Japanese Communist Party Chairman Nozaka Sanzo, Secretary-General Miyamoto Kenji, and JCP Central Committee members Shiga Yoshio and Hakamada Satomi met with Liu Ningyi, head of the Chinese delegation attending the Sixth World Conference Against Atomic and Hydrogen Bombs and for General Disarmament, this evening for a cordial conversation. Zhao Anbo, deputy head of the delegation, was present at the meeting.

    Xinhua News Agency, Tokyo, August 6th: Japan Socialist Party Chairman Asanuma Inejirō and Socialist Party leading cadres Tanaka Minoru, Kuroda Hisao, and Hozumi Shichiro visited Liu Ningyi, head of the Chinese delegation attending the Sixth World Conference Against Atomic and Hydrogen Bombs and for General Disarmament, today.

    Liu Ningyi had a cordial and friendly conversation with his Japanese friends. Sun Pinghua, deputy secretary-general of the delegation, was present at the meeting.

    Xinhua News Agency, Tokyo, August 6th: Liu Ningyi, Chairman of the All-China Federation of Trade Unions, and Chen Yu, Director of the International Liaison Department, held discussions with Sohyo Chairman Kaoru Ota and Secretary-General Akira Iwai today on strengthening the friendship and unity of the working class of Japan and China.

    They also discussed issues such as promoting the normalization of Sino-Japanese relations and strengthening the unity of the working classes of Asia and Africa.

    Xinhua News Agency, Tokyo, August 5th: Liu Ningyi, head of the Chinese delegation attending the Sixth World Conference Against Atomic and Hydrogen Bombs and for General Disarmament, visited Matsumoto Jiichiro, President of the Japan-China Friendship Association, today. Liu Ningyi and Matsumoto Jiichiro had a cordial conversation on further deepening the friendly relations between the peoples of Japan and China.

    Liu Ningyi also attended a reception banquet held by the National Federation of Farmers Unions, at the invitation of the National Federation of Farmers Unions President, Nozoi Masaru, today.

    Prior to this, Liu Ningyi, Chairman of the All-China Federation of Trade Unions, and other trade union representatives from various countries visited the headquarters of the General Council of Trade Unions of Japan (Sohyo). Sohyo Chairman Ota Kaoru, Secretary-General Iwai Akira, and other leaders met with the trade union representatives from various countries.

    Xinhua News Agency, Tokyo, August 4th: Kaoru Ota, Chairman of the General Council of Trade Unions of Japan (Sohyo), hosted a banquet today in honor of Liu Ningyi, Chairman of the All-China Federation of Trade Unions, and Chen Yu, Director of the International Liaison Department.

    Also present at today's banquet were Okamoto Akiharu, Vice Chairman of the Japan Synthetic Chemical Industry Workers' Union Federation, Irie Shoji, Secretary-General Nishino Rokuro, and other Japanese trade union leaders.

    Representatives from various countries attending the World Conference Against Atomic and Hydrogen Bombs on the rostrum of the Japan Peace March rally. The second person from the left in the picture is Liu Ningyi, head of the Chinese delegation.

### 促进中日两国人民的友谊  刘宁一会见日本各界人士

1960-08-07
第6版()
专栏：

    促进中日两国人民的友谊
    刘宁一会见日本各界人士
    新华社6日讯　东京消息：日本共产党主席野坂参三、总书记宫本显治和日共中央干部会委员志贺义雄、袴田里见今天晚上会见了出席第六届禁止原子弹氢弹和争取全面裁军世界大会的中国代表团团长刘宁一，进行了亲切的交谈。会见时在座的有代表团副团长赵安博。
    新华社6日讯　东京消息：日本社会党委员长浅沼稻次郎和社会党领导干部田中稔男、黑田寿男、穗积七郎今天访问了出席第六届禁止原子弹氢弹和争取全面裁军世界大会的中国代表团团长刘宁一。
    刘宁一和日本朋友进行了亲切友好的交谈。会见时在座的有代表团副秘书长孙平化。
    新华社6日讯　东京消息：中华全国总工会主席刘宁一和国际联络部部长陈宇今天和总评议长太田熏、事务局长岩井章就加强日中两国工人阶级的友好和团结进行了商谈。
    他们还交谈了促进中日邦交的正常化和加强亚非工人阶级的团结等问题。
    新华社5日讯　东京消息：出席第六届禁止原子弹氢弹和争取全面裁军世界大会的中国代表团团长刘宁一今天访问了日中友好协会会长松本治一郎。刘宁一和松本治一郎就进一步加深日中两国人民的友好关系进行了亲切的交谈。
    刘宁一今天还应全日本农民协会联合会会长野沟胜的邀请，出席了全日本农民协会联合会的招待宴会。
    在这以前，中华全国总工会主席刘宁一和其他各国工会代表访问了日本工会总评议会本部。“总评”议长太田熏、事务局长岩井章等领导人会见了各国工会代表。
    据新华社4日讯　东京消息：日本工会总评议会议长太田熏今天举行宴会，招待中华全国总工会主席刘宁一和国际联络部长陈宇。
    出席今天宴会的还有日本合成化学产业工会联合会副委员长冈本明保、入江正治，书记长西野六郎和其他日本工会领导人。
