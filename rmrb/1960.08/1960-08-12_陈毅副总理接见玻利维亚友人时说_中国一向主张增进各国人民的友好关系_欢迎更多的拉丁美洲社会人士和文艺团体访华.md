Title: Vice Premier Chen Yi Meets Bolivian Friends, Says China Always Advocates Enhancing Friendly Relations Between Peoples of All Countries, Welcomes More Latin American Social Figures and Performing Arts Groups to Visit China
Author:
Date: 1960-08-12
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, 11th - Vice Premier Chen Yi met this morning with Oscar Donoso Lopez, First Vice President of the Bolivian Senate and Chairman of the Senate Foreign Affairs Committee, and Jaime Arellano Casaneda, Member of the Bolivian Chamber of Deputies and Member of the Chamber of Deputies Foreign Affairs Committee, who are currently visiting China. The hosts and guests had a cordial and friendly conversation.

Donoso introduced the situation in Bolivia. He said that the Bolivian government has nationalized the enterprises of American, British, and other imperialists in Bolivia. Currently, 80% of the enterprises in the country are state-owned. Bolivia has also implemented land reform. Donoso also praised the achievements of the Chinese people in economic construction. He expressed hope that the relations between Bolivia and the People's Republic of China will be increasingly enhanced.

Arellano said that after returning to their country, they will make efforts to enhance the relations between Bolivia and China. He believes that their efforts will be fruitful.

Vice Premier Chen Yi thanked the Bolivian guests for their good wishes regarding the enhancement of relations between China and Bolivia. Vice Premier Chen Yi said that the Chinese people have always advocated enhancing friendly relations between the people of all countries and safeguarding world peace. He expressed welcome to more social figures and cultural and artistic groups from Bolivia and other Latin American countries to visit China in order to better enhance mutual understanding and friendship. Vice Premier Chen Yi expressed hope that the relations between China and Bolivia will be further developed.

Present at the meeting were Shen Jian, Director of the Department of American and Australian Affairs of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs, and Wu Xiaoda, Deputy Secretary-General of the Chinese People's Institute of Foreign Affairs.

### 陈毅副总理接见玻利维亚友人时说  中国一向主张增进各国人民的友好关系  欢迎更多的拉丁美洲社会人士和文艺团体访华

1960-08-12
第6版()
专栏：

    陈毅副总理接见玻利维亚友人时说
    中国一向主张增进各国人民的友好关系
    欢迎更多的拉丁美洲社会人士和文艺团体访华
    新华社11日讯　陈毅副总理今天上午接见了正在我国访问的玻利维亚参议院第一副议长、参议院外交委员会主席奥斯卡·多诺索·洛佩斯，玻利维亚众议院议员、众议院外交委员会委员海梅·阿雷利亚诺·卡萨内达。宾主进行了亲切友好的交谈。
    多诺索介绍了玻利维亚的情况。他说，玻利维亚政府已经把美、英等帝国主义在玻利维亚的企业收归国有。目前，全国已有80％的企业是国营的。玻利维亚还实行了土地改革。多诺索还赞扬了中国人民经济建设的成就。他希望玻利维亚和中华人民共和国之间的关系日益增进。
    阿雷利亚诺说，他们回国后将为增进玻、中两国之间的关系而作出努力。他相信他们的努力将取得成果。
    陈毅副总理感谢玻利维亚客人们关于增进中、玻两国关系的良好意愿。陈毅副总理说，中国人民一向主张增进各国人民之间的友好关系，维护世界和平。他表示欢迎有更多的玻利维亚和拉丁美洲其他国家的社会人士和文化艺术团体来中国访问，以便更好地增进相互间的了解和友谊。陈毅副总理希望中、玻两国之间的关系得到进一步的发展。
    接见时在座的，有外交部美澳司司长申健，中国人民外交学会副秘书长吴晓达等。
