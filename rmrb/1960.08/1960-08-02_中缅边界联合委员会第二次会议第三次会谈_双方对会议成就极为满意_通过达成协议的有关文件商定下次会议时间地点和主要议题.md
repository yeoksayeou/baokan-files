Title: The Third Talk of the Second Meeting of the Sino-Burmese Joint Boundary Committee: Both Sides Extremely Satisfied with the Meeting's Achievements; Approved Relevant Documents of Agreed Upon Matters; Agreed on Time, Location, and Main Agenda for the Next Meeting
Author:
Date: 1960-08-02
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, 1st dispatch—News communique of the Third Talk of the Second Meeting of the Sino-Burmese Joint Boundary Committee.
The Second Meeting of the Sino-Burmese Joint Boundary Committee held its final, third talk on the morning of August 1st. Participating in this talk were the Chinese Chief Representative Yao Zhongming and the Burmese Chief Representative Brigadier General Aung Gyi, as well as other representatives and advisors from both sides. The talk approved the relevant documents of the agreements reached during the Second Meeting; agreed upon the time, location, and main agenda for the Third Meeting of the Joint Committee, and sincerely exchanged views on other issues. Both sides were extremely satisfied with the significant achievements made at this meeting. The talks concluded in a warm and friendly atmosphere.

### 中缅边界联合委员会第二次会议第三次会谈  双方对会议成就极为满意  通过达成协议的有关文件商定下次会议时间地点和主要议题

1960-08-02
第1版()
专栏：

    中缅边界联合委员会第二次会议第三次会谈
    双方对会议成就极为满意
    通过达成协议的有关文件商定下次会议时间地点和主要议题
    新华社1日讯　中缅边界联合委员会第二次会议第三次会谈新闻公报
    中缅边界联合委员会第二次会议于8月1日上午举行最后一次会谈。参加这次会谈的，有中方首席代表姚仲明和缅方首席代表昂季准将以及双方的其他代表和顾问。会谈通过了第二次会议期间所达成协议的有关文件；商定了联合委员会第三次会议的时间、地点和主要议题，并就其他问题诚恳地交换了意见。双方对这次会议所取得的重大成就极为满意，会谈在热情友好的气氛中结束。
