Title: African Countries' Foreign Ministers Conference Opens; Lumumba Demands Withdrawal of Imperialist Troops from Africa
Author:
Date: 1960-08-27
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, August 26th, Leopoldville dispatch: The African Countries' Foreign Ministers Conference opened in Leopoldville, capital of the Republic of Congo, on the afternoon of the 25th.

The representatives who have arrived in Leopoldville and attended the opening ceremony include: Ghana, Guinea, Morocco, the United Arab Republic, Sudan, Tunisia, Algeria, Ethiopia, Liberia, Togo, and Cameroon.

Congolese Prime Minister Lumumba delivered a speech at the opening ceremony, pointing out that the purpose of the conference is: 1. To express full support for African countries striving for independence and unity; 2. To support the policy of neutralism necessary for complete independence; 3. To strive to eliminate the barriers created by colonialist countries between African countries; 4. To discuss trade agreements between African countries; 5. To carefully formulate African countries' claims regarding the European Common Market; 6. To discuss military cooperation between African countries; 7. To consider establishing a radio station in Leopoldville owned by African countries; 8. To establish a scientific research center in Leopoldville shared by all African countries. Lumumba pointed out in his speech that African countries must reiterate their rejection of imperialist countries stationing troops on their territory, and foreign military bases must be removed.

At the beginning of the meeting, some people caused trouble outside the venue. Lumumba pointed out on the spot: "This is an imperialist conspiracy."

Subsequently, the heads of various delegations spoke one after another. Guinean representative Diallo Telli said in his speech that he brought the fraternal respect of the Guinean people, who are determined to give the Congolese people unconditional assistance to strengthen respect for this territory.

### 非洲国家外长会议开幕  卢蒙巴要求帝国主义军队从非洲撤走

1960-08-27
第6版()
专栏：

    非洲国家外长会议开幕
    卢蒙巴要求帝国主义军队从非洲撤走
    据新华社26日讯　利奥波德维尔消息：非洲国家外长会议25日下午在刚果共和国首都利奥波德维尔开幕。
    业已到达利奥波德维尔并出席了开幕式的代表有：加纳、几内亚、摩洛哥、阿联、苏丹、突尼斯、阿尔及利亚、埃塞俄比亚、利比里亚、多哥、喀麦隆。
    刚果总理卢蒙巴在开幕式上致词，指出这次会议的目的是：一、表示对那些为独立和统一而努力的非洲国家全面支持；二、支持为完全独立所必需的中立主义政策；三、为铲除殖民主义国家在非洲国家之间制造的壁垒而努力；四、讨论非洲国家之间的贸易协定；五、仔细拟订非洲国家对于欧洲共同市场的主张；六、讨论非洲国家之间的军事合作；七、考虑在利奥波德维尔建立一座由非洲国家所拥有的广播电台；八、在利奥波德维尔建立一个为所有非洲国家共有的科学研究中心。卢蒙巴在讲话中指出，非洲国家必须重申拒绝帝国主义国家在他们的领土上驻扎军队，外国军事基地必须撤除。
    在会议开始时，曾有一些人在会场外面捣乱。卢蒙巴当场指出：“这是帝国主义者的预谋”。
  随后，各国代表团团长相继发言。几内亚代表迪亚洛·特利在发言中说，他带来了几内亚人民兄弟般的敬意，他们决心给予刚果人民以无条件的援助，以加强人们对这个领土的尊重。
