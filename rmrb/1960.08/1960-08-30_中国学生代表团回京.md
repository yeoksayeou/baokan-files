Title: Chinese Student Delegation Returns to Beijing
Author: 
Date: 1960-08-30
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, August 29th: The Chinese student delegation, led by Hu Qili, returned to Beijing by plane on the 28th after participating in the Fifth Moroccan Student Congress, the Fourth Congress of the General Association of Algerian Muslim Students, and the Congress of the Tunisian Student Union.

### 中国学生代表团回京

1960-08-30
第5版()
专栏：

    中国学生代表团回京
    新华社29日讯　由胡启立率领的中国学生代表团，在参加了摩洛哥第五届学生代表大会、阿尔及利亚穆斯林学生总会第四届代表大会和突尼斯学生联合会代表大会以后，已于28日乘飞机回到北京。
