Title: Delegation from the Japan Central Metal Joint Struggle Council Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1960-08-20
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, 19th. A twelve-member delegation from the Japan Central Metal Joint Struggle Council, led by Sakao Masuo, Vice Chairman of the Central Executive Committee of the Japan National Metal Labor Union, arrived in Beijing by plane this afternoon at the invitation of the National Committee of the Chinese Machinery Trade Union.

They were welcomed at the airport by He Ruilin and Yu Huan, Vice Chairmen of the National Committee of the Chinese Machinery Trade Union, and others.

### 日中央金属共斗会议访华团到京

1960-08-20
第6版()
专栏：

    日中央金属共斗会议访华团到京
    新华社19日讯　应中国机械工会全国委员会邀请来我国访问的日本中央金属共斗会议访华代表团一行十二人，在日本全国金属劳动组合中央执行委员会副委员长坂尾增雄率领下，今天下午乘飞机到达北京。
    到机场欢迎的有中国机械工会全国委员会副主席贺瑞林、于桓等。
