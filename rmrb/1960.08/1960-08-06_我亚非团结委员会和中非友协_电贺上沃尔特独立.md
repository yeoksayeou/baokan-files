Title: China-Africa People's Friendship Association and the Afro-Asian Solidarity Committee Cable Congratulations on Upper Volta's Independence
Author:
Date: 1960-08-06
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, August 4th—The Chinese Afro-Asian Solidarity Committee today sent a telegram to Maurice Yaméogo, President of the Republic of Upper Volta, warmly congratulating Upper Volta on its independence.

The telegram said: "The declaration of independence by Upper Volta is the result of the long-term struggle of the people of Upper Volta against imperialism and colonialism. The Chinese people have always sympathized with and supported the just struggle of the people of Upper Volta and the African people against imperialism, colonialism, and for national independence. We sincerely congratulate the people of Upper Volta on their continuous new victories in the cause of consolidating independence and developing the national economy. We wish prosperity and happiness to the people of the Republic of Upper Volta. We wish the friendship between the people of China and Upper Volta to develop day by day."

Xinhua News Agency, August 4th—Liu Changsheng, President of the Chinese People's Association for Friendship with Africa, today sent a telegram to Maurice Yaméogo, President of the Republic of Upper Volta, warmly congratulating Upper Volta on its independence.

The telegram said: "Your country's independence is the result of your people's struggle against imperialism and colonialism. The 650 million Chinese people resolutely support your people's struggle against imperialism and colonialism and earnestly hope that your people will continue to achieve new victories in this struggle. We wish your country prosperity and strength, and your people happiness. May the friendly relations between the people of China and Upper Volta continue to develop."

### 我亚非团结委员会和中非友协  电贺上沃尔特独立

1960-08-06
第6版()
专栏：

    我亚非团结委员会和中非友协
    电贺上沃尔特独立
    据新华社4日讯　中国亚非团结委员会今天致电上沃尔特共和国总统莫里斯·亚梅奥果，热烈祝贺上沃尔特独立。
    电文说：“上沃尔特的宣布独立，是上沃尔特人民长期反对帝国主义、殖民主义斗争的结果。中国人民一贯同情和支持上沃尔特人民和非洲人民反对帝国主义、殖民主义和争取民族独立的正义斗争。我们衷心祝贺上沃尔特人民在巩固独立和发展民族经济的事业中，不断取得新的胜利。祝上沃尔特共和国人民繁荣、幸福。祝中国和上沃尔特两国人民的友谊日益发展。”
    据新华社4日讯　中国非洲友好协会会长刘长胜今天致电上沃尔特共和国总统莫里斯·亚梅奥果，热烈祝贺上沃尔特独立。
    电文说：“贵国的独立是贵国人民向帝国主义和殖民主义进行斗争的结果，六亿五千万中国人民坚决支持贵国人民反对帝国主义和殖民主义的斗争，并热切希望贵国人民在这一斗争中不断取得新的胜利。祝贵国繁荣昌盛，人民幸福。愿中上两国人民的友好关系不断发展。”
