Title: Chairman of the Burmese Trade Union Congress Leaves Beijing for Home
Author:
Date: 1960-08-02
Page: 6
Xinhua News Agency, August 1st. After attending the 11th Council Meeting of the World Federation of Trade Unions, and at the invitation of the All-China Federation of Trade Unions, the Chairman of the Burmese Trade Union Congress, Tekin Lun, left Beijing by plane this morning to return home after his visit to our country.
Those who went to the airport to see him off included Li Jiebo, Vice-Chairman of the All-China Federation of Trade Unions, and others.

### 缅甸工会大会主席离京回国

1960-08-02
第6版()
专栏：

    缅甸工会大会主席离京回国
    新华社1日讯　出席世界工联第十一次理事会会议代表、 缅甸工会大会主席德钦伦应中华全国总工会的邀请，在我国访问后，于今晨乘飞机离开北京回国。
    前往机场欢送的，有中华全国总工会副主席李颉伯等。
