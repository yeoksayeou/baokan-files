Title: Bashkilo, Chargé d'Affaires ad interim, Holds Reception Celebrating the 16th Anniversary of the Liberation of Romania; Chairman Zhu and Premier Zhou Attended the Reception
Author:
Date: 1960-08-24
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, August 23rd: Bashkilo, the Chargé d'Affaires ad interim of the Romanian Embassy in China, held a reception at the embassy this evening to celebrate the 16th anniversary of the liberation of Romania.

    Chairman Zhu De and Premier Zhou Enlai attended today's reception.

    The reception proceeded in a friendly atmosphere.

    Also present at the reception were Vice Premiers of the State Council Li Xiannian and Xi Zhongxun, Vice Chairman of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress Shen Junru, Vice Chairman of the National Committee of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference Burhan, Vice Ministers of Foreign Affairs Zhang Hanfu and Luo Guibo, responsible persons from various departments of the government, people's organizations, and Beijing Municipality, senior generals of the Chinese People's Liberation Army, and people from all walks of life in the capital. Heads of diplomatic missions and diplomatic officials from various countries to China also attended the reception.

### 巴什基洛临时代办举行招待会  庆祝罗马尼亚解放十六周年  朱委员长周总理出席了招待会

1960-08-24
第1版()
专栏：

    巴什基洛临时代办举行招待会
    庆祝罗马尼亚解放十六周年
    朱委员长周总理出席了招待会
    新华社23日讯　罗马尼亚驻中国大使馆临时代办巴什基洛今晚在大使馆举行招待会，庆祝罗马尼亚解放十六周年。
    朱德委员长、周恩来总理出席了今天的招待会。
    招待会在友好的气氛中进行。
    出席招待会的还有，国务院副总理李先念、习仲勋，人大常委会副委员长沈钧儒，政协全国委员会副主席包尔汉，外交部副部长章汉夫、罗贵波，政府各部门、各人民团体和北京市的负责人，中国人民解放军高级将领，以及首都各界人士。各国驻中国使节和外交官员也出席了招待会。
