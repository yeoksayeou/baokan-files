Title: American Bandit Planes Bomb Cuba Again
Author:
Date: 1960-08-29
Page: 6
Xinhua News Agency, Havana, August 27th: While U.S. Secretary of State Herter was hypocritically denying U.S. imperialist aggression against Cuba at the Organization of American States Foreign Ministers Conference in San José, bandit planes from the United States continuously dropped incendiary bombs yesterday and the day before, burning Cuban sugarcane fields. Yesterday, a plane dropped incendiary bombs over the sugarcane plantations of the Violeta Sugar Mill and the Cunagua Sugar Mill in Camagüey Province, but the rapid action of the militia prevented the spread of the fire. The Cunagua Sugar Mill is one of the thirty-six American-owned sugar mills recently nationalized by the Cuban Revolutionary Government.

### 美国强盗飞机又轰炸古巴

1960-08-29
第6版()
专栏：

    美国强盗飞机又轰炸古巴
    新华社哈瓦那27日电　当美国国务卿赫脱在圣约瑟举行的美洲国家组织外长会议上虚伪地否认美帝国主义对古巴的侵略的时候，来自美国的强盗飞机在昨天和前天连续投下燃烧弹，焚烧古巴的甘蔗园。昨天有一架飞机在卡马圭省维奥莱塔糖厂和古纳瓜糖厂的甘蔗种植园上空投下燃烧弹，但由于民兵的迅速行动阻止了火势的蔓延。古纳瓜糖厂是古巴革命政府最近收归国有的三十六家美国糖厂之一。
