Title: At the invitation of Deputy Prime Minister and Foreign Minister Naim, Vice Premier Chen Yi Embarks on Visit to Afghanistan; Premier Zhou Enlai, Vice Premiers Li Xiannian and Xi Zhongxun, Vice Chairman of the Standing Committee Guo Moruo and Others See Him Off at the Airport

Author: 
Date: 1960-08-16
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, 15th. At the invitation of His Royal Highness Naim, Deputy Prime Minister and Foreign Minister of the Kingdom of Afghanistan, Vice Premier of the State Council and Foreign Minister Marshal Chen Yi left Beijing by special plane this morning for a friendly visit to Afghanistan.

Premier of the State Council Zhou Enlai, Vice Premiers Li Xiannian, Xi Zhongxun, Vice Chairman of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress Guo Moruo, Vice Chairman of the National Defense Council Fu Zuoyi, Deputy Directors of the Foreign Affairs Office of the State Council Liao Chengzhi, Liu Ningyi, Vice Ministers of Foreign Affairs Zhang Hanfu, Luo Guibo, Ji Pengfei, all went to the airport to see him off.

Before boarding the plane, Vice Premier Chen Yi, together with Kong Yuan, Deputy Director of the Foreign Affairs Office of the State Council, and Geng Biao, Vice Minister of Foreign Affairs, who accompanied him on the visit, shook hands warmly with the people who came to see them off. Everyone applauded warmly, wishing Vice Premier Chen Yi a successful and safe visit abroad. Young Pioneers presented flowers to Vice Premier Chen Yi.

Those who went to the airport to see him off today also included responsible persons from various departments of state organs, people's organizations, and Beijing Municipality, senior generals of the Chinese People's Liberation Army, and people from all walks of life in the capital.

Akim, Charge d'Affaires ad interim of the Afghan Embassy in China, and envoys and diplomatic officials from various countries to China, also went to the airport to see him off.

### 应纳伊姆副首相兼外交大臣的邀请  陈毅副总理启程访问阿富汗  周总理李先念习仲勋副总理郭沫若副委员长等到机场欢送

1960-08-16
第1版()
专栏：

    应纳伊姆副首相兼外交大臣的邀请
    陈毅副总理启程访问阿富汗
    周总理李先念习仲勋副总理郭沫若副委员长等到机场欢送
    新华社15日讯　国务院副总理兼外交部长陈毅元帅，应阿富汗王国副首相兼外交大臣纳伊姆殿下邀请，于今天上午乘专机离开北京，前往阿富汗进行友好访问。
    国务院总理周恩来，副总理李先念、习仲勋，全国人民代表大会常务委员会副委员长郭沫若，国防委员会副主席傅作义，国务院外事办公室副主任廖承志、刘宁一，外交部副部长章汉夫、罗贵波、姬鹏飞，都到机场欢送。
    陈毅副总理和随同前往访问的国务院外事办公室副主任孔原，外交部副部长耿飚，在走上飞机前，同欢送的人们一一亲切握手。大家热烈鼓掌，祝陈毅副总理出国访问成功，一路平安。少年先锋队员向陈毅副总理献了鲜花。
    今天到机场欢送的，还有国家机关各部门、各人民团体和北京市的负责人，中国人民解放军高级将领，以及首都各界人士。
    阿富汗驻中国大使馆临时代办阿基姆，各国驻中国的使节和外交官员，也到机场欢送。
