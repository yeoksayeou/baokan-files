Title: China Donates Rice to Guinea, Arrives in Conakry
Author:
Date: 1960-08-28
Page: 2

Xinhua News Agency, Conakry, August 26th: The People's Republic of China has shipped five thousand tons of rice to Conakry, the capital of the Republic of Guinea. This is part of the ten thousand tons of rice donated by the Chinese government to the Guinean government.

### 我赠几内亚大米运到科纳克里

1960-08-28
第2版()
专栏：

    我赠几内亚大米运到科纳克里
    新华社科纳克里26日电　中华人民共和国已经把五千吨大米运到几内亚共和国首都科纳克里。这是中国政府赠送给几内亚政府的一万吨大米的一部分。
