Title: Two Representatives of the Ghana National Union of Students Arrive in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1960-08-16
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, 14th August: At the invitation of the All-China Students' Federation, Robert Adam Ambermah and David Christian Asan, representatives of the Ghana National Union of Students, arrived in Beijing by plane on the 14th for a visit to our country.

### 加纳全国学联代表两人到京

1960-08-16
第5版()
专栏：

    加纳全国学联代表两人到京
    新华社14日讯　应中华全国学生联合会邀请，来我国访问的加纳全国学生联合会代表罗勃·阿当姆·阿姆伯马和大卫·克里斯狄安·阿山在14日乘飞机到达北京。
