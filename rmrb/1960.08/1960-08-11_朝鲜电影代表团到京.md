Title: Korean Film Delegation Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1960-08-11
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, 10th. A five-member Korean film delegation, led by Kim Han-gyu, Director of the Film Bureau of the Korean Ministry of Education and Culture, arrived in Beijing by plane this afternoon from Pyongyang to participate in the "Democratic People's Republic of Korea Film Week" activities held during the Sino-Korean Friendship Week.

Those who welcomed the delegation at the airport included Xia Yan, Vice Minister of the Ministry of Culture, Wang Dong, Deputy Director of the Film Bureau of the Ministry of Culture, and Li Mu, Deputy Director of the Beijing Municipal Film Bureau.

### 朝鲜电影代表团到京

1960-08-11
第1版()
专栏：

    朝鲜电影代表团到京
    新华社10日讯　前来我国参加在中朝友好周期间举行的“朝鲜民主主义人民共和国电影周”活动的朝鲜电影代表团一行五人，在朝鲜教育文化省电影局局长金汉奎率领下，今天下午由平壤乘飞机到达北京。
    到机场欢迎的，有文化部副部长夏衍、文化部电影事业管理局副局长王栋、北京市电影事业管理局副局长李牧等。
