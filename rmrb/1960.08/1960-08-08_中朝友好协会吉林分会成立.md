Title: Establishment of the China-Korea Friendship Association Jilin Branch
Author:
Date: 1960-08-08
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Changchun, 7th. To enhance the friendship between the Chinese and Korean people and develop the friendly relations between the two peoples, the Jilin Branch of the China-Korea Friendship Association was established in Jilin Province. On the 6th, 1,500 people from all walks of life in Jilin Province and Changchun City held a grand gathering to celebrate the establishment of the Jilin Branch of the China-Korea Friendship Association.

Fu Zhensheng, Secretary of the Jilin Provincial Committee of the Communist Party of China, Cui Cifeng, Vice Governor of Jilin Province, Wang Dajing, Secretary of the Secretariat of the Changchun Municipal Committee of the Communist Party of China, Cheng Guanglie, Vice Mayor of Changchun City, and other provincial and municipal leaders, as well as the Jilin Provincial Branch of the Chinese People's Committee for World Peace, the Jilin Provincial Committee of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference, and heads of various democratic parties and people's organizations in Jilin Province attended the celebration meeting.

Jeong Bong-gyu, Consul General of North Korea in Changchun, and personnel from the Consulate General were invited to participate in the celebration meeting.

Fu Zhensheng, Secretary of the Secretariat of the Jilin Provincial Committee of the Communist Party of China and President of the Jilin Branch of the China-Korea Friendship Association, spoke at the celebration meeting. He expressed his heartfelt congratulations on the establishment of the Jilin Branch of the China-Korea Friendship Association on behalf of the people of Jilin Province. He cited numerous facts to illustrate that the friendship between the Chinese and Korean people is unbreakable and that this friendship is developing day by day. He pointed out that the establishment of the Jilin Branch of the China-Korea Friendship Association will contribute to further consolidating and developing the profound friendship between the two peoples, jointly opposing U.S. imperialism, safeguarding world peace, and promoting mutual learning and exchange of construction experience.

Jeong Bong-gyu, Consul General of North Korea in Changchun, also spoke. He expressed his great pleasure and warm congratulations on the establishment of the Jilin Branch of the China-Korea Friendship Association as the Korean people welcome their upcoming glorious festival, the 15th anniversary of the "August 15th" Liberation. In his speech, he described the solidarity and friendship between the Korean and Chinese peoples and the construction achievements of the Korean people. He also said that the Korean people will maintain a high degree of vigilance against the war provocation conspiracies of U.S. imperialism and are determined to wage a tenacious struggle against U.S. imperialism.

### 中朝友好协会吉林分会成立

1960-08-08
第4版()
专栏：

    中朝友好协会吉林分会成立
    新华社长春7日电　为增进中朝两国人民的友谊，发展中朝两国人民的友好关系，吉林省成立了中朝友好协会吉林省分会。6日吉林省和长春市各界人民一千五百人举行盛大集会，庆祝中朝友好协会吉林省分会成立。
    中共吉林省委书记富振声、吉林省副省长崔次丰、中共长春市委书记处书记王大经、长春市副市长程光烈等省、市负责人，以及中国人民保卫世界和平委员会吉林省分会、政协吉林省委员会、吉林省各民主党派和各人民团体负责人出席了庆祝会。
    朝鲜驻长春总领事郑凤珪和总领事馆人员应邀参加了庆祝会。
    中共吉林省委书记处书记、中朝友好协会吉林省分会会长富振声在庆祝会上讲了话。他代表吉林省人民衷心地祝贺中朝友好协会吉林省分会的成立。他列举大量事实，说明中朝两国人民的友谊是牢不可破的，这种友谊正在日益发展。他指出，中朝友好协会吉林省分会的成立，在进一步巩固和发展两国人民的深厚友谊、共同反对美帝国主义、维护世界和平、促进互相学习和交流建设经验等方面，将会做出贡献。
    朝鲜驻长春总领事郑凤珪也讲了话。他对于在朝鲜人民迎接自己即将到来的光荣节日——“八·一五”解放十五周年的时候，中朝友好协会吉林省分会成立，感到非常高兴，并表示热烈祝贺。他在讲话中叙述了朝中两国人民的团结与友谊以及朝鲜人民的建设成就。他还说，朝鲜人民对于美帝国主义的战争挑衅阴谋将保持高度的警惕性，并且具有同美帝国主义展开顽强斗争的决心。
