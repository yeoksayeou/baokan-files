Title: Mrs. Valdés of Cuba Leaves Beijing for Home
Author:
Date: 1960-08-15
Page: 5
Xinhua News Agency, August 12th: Mrs. Hilda Felipe Valdés, a Cuban foreign guest who came to China for a tour and visit at the invitation of the All-China Women's Federation, left Beijing by plane this morning to return to her country after visiting Harbin, Shanghai and other places.
    Yang Bin, Director of the General Office of the All-China Women's Federation, and others were at the airport to see her off.

### 古巴瓦尔台斯夫人离京回国

1960-08-15
第5版()
专栏：

    古巴瓦尔台斯夫人离京回国
    新华社12日讯　应全国妇联邀请来我国参观访问的古巴外宾希尔达·费利佩·瓦尔台斯夫人，在访问了哈尔滨、上海等地后，今天上午乘飞机离开北京回国。
    到机场欢送的，有全国妇联办公厅主任杨彬等。
