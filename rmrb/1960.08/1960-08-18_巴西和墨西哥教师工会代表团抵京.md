Title: Brazilian and Mexican Teachers' Union Delegations Arrive in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1960-08-18
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, August 17th: The Brazilian Teachers' Union delegation, composed of V. Borbolema Bordo, First Secretary of the Rio de Janeiro Teachers' Union in Brazil, and Eurico Baptista Aranha Miranda, Secretary of the Interstate Teachers' Federation of Brazil; and the Mexican Teachers' Union delegation, composed of Jorge Cruickshank García, Chairman of the National Publishing Committee of the National Union of Education Workers of Mexico and Vice President of the International of Education Trade Unions, and Francisco Mora Peláez of the National Union of Education Workers of Mexico, arrived in Beijing by plane today.

They are visiting China at the invitation of the National Committee of the Chinese Education Union after attending the Third World Teachers' Conference held in Guinea.

### 巴西和墨西哥教师工会代表团抵京

1960-08-18
第6版()
专栏：

    巴西和墨西哥教师工会代表团抵京
    新华社17日讯　由巴西里约热内卢教师工会第一书记维·波尔波莱玛·波尔多和巴西州际教师联合会书记埃乌里克·巴布蒂斯塔·阿兰巴·米兰达组成的巴西教师工会代表团；由墨西哥全国教育工作者工会全国出版委员会主席、教育工会国际副主席何赫·克鲁伊克显克·加尔西亚和墨西哥全国教育工作者工会的弗兰西斯科·莫拉·佩莱斯组成的墨西哥教师工会代表团，于今日乘机抵达北京。
    他们是出席在几内亚举行的第三届世界教师代表大会后，应中国教育工会全国委员会的邀请来我国访问的。
