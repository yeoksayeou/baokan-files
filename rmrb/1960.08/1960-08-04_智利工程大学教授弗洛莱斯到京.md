Title: Chilean Engineering University Professor Flores Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1960-08-04
Page: 5

    Chilean Engineering University Professor Flores Arrives in Beijing
    (Our report) Rodrigo Flores, professor of structural engineering at the Chilean Engineering University, and his wife, Ma. Gao. Xiziwa, arrived in Beijing at 4:00 PM on August 2nd, at the invitation of the Architectural Society of China. Liu Yunhe, director of the Technical Information Bureau of the Ministry of Construction Engineering, Wu Huaqing, vice president of the Beijing Institute of Architectural Engineering, Chen Jin, director-general of the Chinese Association for Latin American Friendship, and others were at the airport to welcome them.

### 智利工程大学教授弗洛莱斯到京

1960-08-04
第5版()
专栏：

    智利工程大学教授弗洛莱斯到京
    本报讯　应中国建筑学会邀请来我国访问的智利工程大学教授、结构工程师罗德里戈·弗洛莱斯和他的夫人玛·高·西兹娃于8月2日下午四时抵京。前往机场欢迎的有建筑工程部技术情报局局长刘云鹤、北京建筑工程学院副院长吴华庆、中国拉丁美洲友协总干事陈金等人。
