Title: Our Youth Basketball Team Continues Visit in Romania
Author:
Date: 1960-08-27
Page: 5
    Xinhua News Agency, Bucharest, August 26th: The Chinese Youth Men's and Women's Basketball Teams played four friendly matches in the Romanian cities of Sibiu, Târgu Mureș, and Cluj on the 23rd, 24th, and 25th respectively, resulting in two wins and two losses.

### 我青年篮球队继续在罗访问

1960-08-27
第5版()
专栏：

    我青年篮球队继续在罗访问
    新华社布加勒斯特26日电　中国青年男女篮球队于23日、24日、25日分别在罗马尼亚的锡比乌、特尔古—木雷希和克罗什三个城市进行了四场访问比赛，结果两胜两负。
