Title: Contemporary Hero (Photo)
Author: Tan Liguang
Date: 1960-08-03
Page: 2

    Contemporary Hero
    Huang Huating (front), Deputy Secretary of the Party Committee of the Shantou City Construction Engineering Bureau in Guangdong Province, is a person with a high degree of class consciousness and communist style, and a standard-bearer of the technological revolution. He came from a carpentry background and has improved many woodworking machines over the years. After 103 designs and 18 experiments, he successfully created the first electric ramming machine in the country.

    Recently, in order to break through the final manual process in door and window production and achieve complete mechanization, he is working with workers to modify a large-scale precision grinding machine.
                                     Our reporter Tan Liguang took the photo

### 当代英雄（图片）
谭理光
1960-08-03
第2版()
专栏：

    当代英雄
    广东省汕头市建筑工程局党委副书记黄华庭（前），是一个具有高度阶级觉悟和共产主义风格的人，是技术革命的红旗手。他是木工出身，数年来改进了许多木工机具。他曾经过一百零三次的设计和十八次试验，创造成功了全国第一台电动夯墙机。
    最近，他为了攻破门窗生产最后的一道手工工序，实现门窗生产全部机械化，正在和工人一起改制大型精光机。
                                     本报记者　谭理光摄
