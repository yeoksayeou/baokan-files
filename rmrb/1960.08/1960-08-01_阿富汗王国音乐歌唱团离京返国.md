Title: Afghan Royal Music Troupe Departs Beijing for Home
Author:
Date: 1960-08-01
Page: 6
Xinhua News Agency, July 31st: The seventeen-member Afghan Royal Music Troupe, led by its leader Abdul Breshna, departed by plane this morning for their home country after visiting Beijing and Tianjin. Those who went to the airport to see them off included Ding Xilin, Vice-Chairman of the Commission for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries, Lin Lin, Director of the Department, Huang Hui, Deputy Manager of the China Performing Arts Agency, and members of the music community in the capital.
    Afghan Royal Ambassador to China, Abdul Samad, also went to the airport to see them off.

### 阿富汗王国音乐歌唱团离京返国

1960-08-01
第6版()
专栏：

    阿富汗王国音乐歌唱团离京返国
    新华社31日讯　阿富汗王国音乐歌唱团一行十七人，在团长阿卜杜勒·布里希纳的率领下，访问了北京、天津以后，于今天上午乘飞机回国。前往机场欢送的有对外文化联络委员会副主任丁西林、司长林林，中国演出经理公司副经理黄回以及首都音乐界人士。
    阿富汗王国驻华大使阿卜杜勒·萨马德也到机场欢送。
