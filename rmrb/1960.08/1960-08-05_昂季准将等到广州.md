Title: Brigadier General Aung Gyi Arrives in Guangzhou
Author:
Date: 1960-08-05
Page: 6

    Xinhua News Agency, Guangzhou, August 4th: Brigadier General Aung Gyi, the chief Burmese representative of the Sino-Burmese Joint Boundary Committee, along with representatives, advisors, and accompanying personnel, arrived in Guangzhou today from Beijing. Colonel Khin Saw Min, the Burmese military attaché to China, also accompanied the delegation to Guangzhou.
    Welcoming them at the airport were Sun Leyi, Vice Mayor of Guangzhou; He Dongsheng and Hu Jicheng, Major Generals of the Chinese People's Liberation Army stationed in Guangzhou; Luo Peiyuan, Vice Chairman of the Guangzhou Municipal Committee of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference, and other leading figures.
    Tonight, Vice Mayor Sun Leyi hosted a banquet to bid farewell to the distinguished guests. In his speech at the banquet, Sun Leyi warmly praised the efforts made by the Burmese representatives in resolving the Sino-Burmese boundary issue and the achievements already made. He said that the negotiations on the Sino-Burmese boundary issue have been so satisfactory and smooth, providing an example for countries with different systems to resolve boundary issues through consultation under the principle of peaceful coexistence, and at the same time making contributions to safeguarding peace in Asia and the world.
    Brigadier General Aung Gyi also spoke at the banquet. He said that the Sino-Burmese boundary issue was originally a very complicated one, but thanks to the joint consultation between the two governments based on the principles of peaceful coexistence, the problem was satisfactorily resolved. He wished that the friendship between the peoples of the two countries would grow and be strengthened day by day.
    The distinguished guests are scheduled to leave Guangzhou on the 5th to return to their country.

### 昂季准将等到广州

1960-08-05
第6版()
专栏：

    昂季准将等到广州
    新华社广州4日电　中缅边界联合委员会缅甸首席代表昂季准将和代表、顾问以及随行人员等，今天从北京到达广州。随同代表团到广州的还有缅甸驻中国武官景沙门上校。
    到机场欢迎的，有广州市副市长孙乐宜，中国人民解放军驻广州部队首长贺东生少将、胡继成少将，政协广州市委员会副主席罗培元等负责人。
    今天晚上，孙乐宜副市长设宴欢送贵宾。孙乐宜在宴会上讲话，热烈赞扬缅甸代表们在解决中缅两国边界问题上所作的努力，和已经取得的成绩。他说，中缅两国边界问题的谈判进行得这样圆满和顺利，给不同制度的国家在和平共处的原则下，用协商的方法解决边界问题提供了榜样，同时对保卫亚洲和世界和平作出了贡献。
    昂季准将也在宴会上讲了话。他说，中缅两国边界问题本来是很复杂的问题，但由于双方政府根据和平共处的原则，共同协商，使问题得到圆满解决。他祝两国人民的友谊日益增长和巩固。
    贵宾们定于5日离开广州回国。
