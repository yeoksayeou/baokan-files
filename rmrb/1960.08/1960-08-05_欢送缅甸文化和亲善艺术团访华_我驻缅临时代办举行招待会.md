Title: Farewell Reception for the Burmese Cultural and Goodwill Art Troupe's Visit to China Held by Our Chargé d'Affaires ad interim in Burma
Author:
Date: 1960-08-05
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, Rangoon, August 3rd: Lei Yang, Chargé d'Affaires ad interim of the Chinese Embassy in the Union of Burma, held a reception here tonight to bid farewell to the members of the Burmese Cultural and Goodwill Art Troupe, led by U Ba Swe, Secretary of the Burmese Ministry of Culture, who are about to visit China. They will depart for China on August 5th at the invitation of the Government of the People's Republic of China.

Among those present at the reception were officials from the Burmese Ministry of Culture, prominent figures from the literary and art circles, and representatives of the overseas Chinese in Rangoon.

Members of the Burmese art troupe performed songs and dances at the reception that expressed the friendship between China and Burma. The guests and hosts attending the reception welcomed these performances with loud applause.

The reception was held in a warm and friendly atmosphere.

### 欢送缅甸文化和亲善艺术团访华  我驻缅临时代办举行招待会

1960-08-05
第6版()
专栏：

    欢送缅甸文化和亲善艺术团访华
    我驻缅临时代办举行招待会
    新华社仰光3日电　中国驻缅甸联邦大使馆临时代办雷扬今晚在这里举行招待会，欢送即将到中国访问的，由缅甸文化部秘书吴巴盛率领的缅甸文化和亲善艺术团团员。他们应中华人民共和国政府的邀请将在8月5日前往中国。
    出席招待会的有缅甸文化部官员，文艺界著名人士和仰光的华侨代表。
    缅甸艺术团团员在招待会上表演了表现中缅友谊的歌舞，参加招待会的宾主们不断以响亮的掌声欢迎这些表演。
    招待会是在热烈和友好的气氛中进行的。
