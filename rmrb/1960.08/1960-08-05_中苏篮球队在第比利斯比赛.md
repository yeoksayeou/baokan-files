Title: Chinese and Soviet Basketball Teams Compete in Tbilisi
Author:
Date: 1960-08-05
Page: 6
Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, August 3rd. The Chinese men's and women's basketball teams, currently visiting the Soviet Union, played two games against local basketball players in Tbilisi on the 2nd, with one win and one loss.

    The Chinese men's team defeated the Tbilisi "Dynamo" team with a score of 77 to 69, while the women's team lost to the Georgian Institute of Technology team with a score of 51 to 63.

### 中苏篮球队在第比利斯比赛

1960-08-05
第6版()
专栏：

    中苏篮球队在第比利斯比赛
    新华社莫斯科3日电　正在苏联访问的中国男、女篮球队2日在第比利斯同当地的篮球运动员进行了两场比赛。一胜一负。
    中国男子队以七十七比六十九战胜了第比利斯“狄纳莫”队，女子队以五十一比六十三失败，对手是格鲁吉亚工艺学院队。
