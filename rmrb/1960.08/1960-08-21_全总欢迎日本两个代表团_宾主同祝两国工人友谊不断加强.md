Title: All-China Federation of Trade Unions Welcomes Two Japanese Delegations; Guests and Hosts Jointly Wish Continuous Strengthening of Friendship Between Workers of the Two Countries
Author:
Date: 1960-08-21
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, August 20th: The All-China Federation of Trade Unions (ACFTU) held a reception this afternoon to warmly welcome the Japan General Council of Trade Unions (Sohyo) Youth and Women Delegation and the Japan Central Metal Industry Workers' Union Joint Struggle Council Delegation.

Kang Yonghe, Secretary of the Secretariat of the ACFTU, proposed a toast at the reception, saying that your visit to China will further strengthen the friendship and unity between the workers of China and Japan. The Chinese workers and the entire Chinese people will always stand by your side and support your struggle against American imperialism.

The head of the Japan General Council of Trade Unions (Sohyo) Youth and Women Delegation, Shigeru Tanemura, and the head of the Japan Central Metal Industry Workers' Union Joint Struggle Council Delegation, Masuo Iio, delivered speeches at the reception. They expressed their heartfelt gratitude for the great support given by the Chinese workers and the Chinese people to the struggle of the Japanese workers and people against the Japan-U.S. Security Treaty and against the common enemy, U.S. imperialism. They said that Chairman Liu Ningyi's visit to Japan not long ago was a great support from the Chinese workers and people to the anti-American struggle of the Japanese people.

They said that on May 1st, International Labor Day, a joint statement was signed by the trade unions of Japan, China, and North Korea. This statement is a valuable document, and we will strive to fulfill the tasks outlined in the joint statement in the future.

They emphasized that there is a deep friendship between the people of Japan and China. No matter how high the mountains are or how wide the sea is, the people of the two countries will unite and wage unremitting struggles to achieve common aspirations.

### 全总欢迎日本两个代表团  宾主同祝两国工人友谊不断加强

1960-08-21
第6版()
专栏：

    全总欢迎日本两个代表团
    宾主同祝两国工人友谊不断加强
    据新华社20日讯　中华全国总工会今天下午举行招待会，热烈欢迎日本总评青年与妇女访华代表团、日本中央金属共斗会议访华代表团。
    中华全国总工会书记处书记康永和在招待会上祝酒说，你们这次来中国访问将进一步加强中日两国工人的友谊和团结。中国工人和全国人民永远站在你们这一边，支持你们反对美帝国主义的斗争。
    日本总评青年与妇女代表团团长种桥茂和日本中央金属共斗会议访华代表团团长饭尾增雄先后在招待会上致词。他们对中国工人和中国人民在日本工人和人民反对日美安全条约、反对共同的敌人美帝国主义的斗争中所给予的巨大支持，表示衷心感谢。他们说，刘宁一主席不久前到日本访问，这是中国工人和人民对日本人民的反美斗争的极大支持。
    他们说，今年“五一”国际劳动节，日、中、朝签订了三国工会联合声明，这个声明是一宝贵的文献，我们今后要为贯彻联合声明的任务而努力。
    他们最后强调地说，日本和中国人民之间有着深厚的友谊，不管山有多高，海有多宽，两国人民将为实现共同的愿望团结起来进行不懈的斗争。
