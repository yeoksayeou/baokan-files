Title: Sichuan Song and Dance Troupe Arrives in Damascus
Author:
Date: 1960-08-13
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, Damascus, August 11th: The Chinese Sichuan Song and Dance Troupe, led by troupe leader Zhang Tiesheng, arrived in Damascus this morning.

The song and dance troupe will perform at the Damascus International Fair in the United Arab Republic at the invitation of the fair.

### 四川歌舞团到大马士革

1960-08-13
第6版()
专栏：

    四川歌舞团到大马士革
    新华社大马士革11日电　中国四川歌舞团在张铁生团长率领下今天上午到达大马士革。
    歌舞团应阿联大马士革国际博览会的邀请将在博览会上演出。
