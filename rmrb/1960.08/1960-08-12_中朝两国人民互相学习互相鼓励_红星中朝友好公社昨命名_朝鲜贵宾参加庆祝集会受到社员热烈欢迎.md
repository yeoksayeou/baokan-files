Title: Chinese and Korean People Learn from and Encourage Each Other: Red Star China-Korea Friendship Commune Named Yesterday; Korean Guests Attended the Celebration and Were Warmly Welcomed by Commune Members
Author:
Date: 1960-08-12
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, 11th. The Red Star People's Commune in Daxing County, Beijing, was named the Red Star China-Korea Friendship People's Commune today. More than a thousand commune members held a grand rally this morning to warmly celebrate the 15th anniversary of the liberation of Korea on August 15th and the naming of the Red Star China-Korea Friendship Commune.

Korean Ambassador to China, Li Yong-ho, and his wife, Kim Jong-heng, head of the Korea-China Friendship Association delegation, and all members of the delegation, as well as all members of the Korean film delegation headed by Kim Han-kyu, attended the celebration meeting. When the Korean guests arrived at the Red Star China-Korea Friendship People's Commune, male and female commune members gathered on the road by the village, waving bouquets, beating gongs and drums, and setting off firecrackers to warmly welcome them.

Li Dequan, President of the China-Korea Friendship Association, Wei Zhenwu, Vice Minister of Agriculture, Wu Han, Deputy Mayor of Beijing, Li Qiangfen, Deputy Director of the Second Asian Department of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs, Lu Ming, Deputy Director of the First Department of the Foreign Cultural Committee, and Deng Fangzhi, mother of martyr Huang Jiguang, also attended the celebration meeting.

Wu Han spoke at the celebration meeting, extending a warm welcome to the Korean guests and praising the profound fighting friendship between the Chinese and Korean people. He said that the people of Beijing, along with the people of the whole country, warmly congratulate and cheer the great victories achieved by the fraternal Korean people in the socialist revolution and socialist construction. Every victory of the Korean people marks the continuous growth of the strength of our socialist camp, and is a powerful encouragement and support for the Chinese people and the people of the world.

Wu Han said: "The naming of the Red Star People's Commune as the Red Star China-Korea Friendship People's Commune is an honor for the entire commune and an encouragement to all commune members. Under the leadership of the Party, all commune members must hold high the three red flags of the General Line, the Great Leap Forward, and the People's Commune, exert greater revolutionary vigor, and use more outstanding achievements to thank the profound friendship of the Korean people and advance hand in hand with the Korean people."

Kim Jong-heng, head of the Korea-China Friendship Association delegation, spoke at the meeting, extending warm congratulations to all members of the China-Korea Friendship People's Commune. He said: "Our two peoples have a profound traditional friendship, and our friendship has been further consolidated and developed in the struggle against Japanese imperialism and American imperialism for the freedom and independence of our motherland. Today, our two peoples are heart-to-heart, cooperating closely, learning from each other, encouraging each other, exchanging advanced experiences, and advancing bravely towards a communist society." He said: "The China-Korea Friendship People's Commune named today symbolizes the friendship and unity of the two peoples, and this will make a great contribution to further consolidating and developing the friendship and unity between the Chinese and Korean peoples." Kim Jong-heng's speech was warmly welcomed. After the speech, amidst thunderous applause, he presented the commune with a banner bearing the words "Long Live the Friendship and Unity of the Chinese and Korean People."

Zhao Biao, Director of the Red Star China-Korea Friendship People's Commune, extended festive congratulations to the guests and, through the guests, to the Korean people on behalf of more than 39,000 commune members at the meeting. He said that today we are gloriously named "Red Star China-Korea Friendship People's Commune," and we feel that this is not only an honor, but also an encouragement to us. We must answer the great friendship of the Korean people with more outstanding achievements.

The meeting read the festive congratulatory letter from the Red Star China-Korea Friendship People's Commune to the China-Korea Friendship Agricultural Cooperative of Shunan County, South Pyongan Province, Korea. After the meeting, literary and artistic programs were performed.

The Korean guests had lunch at the commune.

After lunch, the guests, accompanied by Guo Fang, Secretary of the Commune Party Committee, and Zhao Biao, Director, visited the commune's agricultural machinery repair and manufacturing plant, plastic factory, stud farm, pig farm, dairy farm, duck farm, chicken farm, and rice fields. The guests were warmly welcomed by the commune members wherever they went.

The guests also visited the China-Korea Friendship Rice Field today, where excellent rice seeds sent by members of the China-Korea Friendship Agricultural Cooperative of Shunan County, South Pyongan Province, Korea, are planted. These rice plants are growing vigorously and a bumper harvest is in sight under the careful cultivation of the commune members. After the visit, Kim Jong-heng, head of the China-Korea Friendship Association delegation, happily said to the director of the commune: "I sincerely congratulate you on the achievements made by your commune, and I hope that you will achieve even greater results under the leadership of the Party."

Tonight, the China-Korea Friendship Association and the Foreign Cultural Committee held a film soiree to entertain all the distinguished guests of the China-Korea Friendship Association delegation. At the soiree, Chu Tunan, Deputy Director of the Foreign Cultural Committee, and others accompanied the guests and enjoyed the Chinese films "Nie Er" and "Climbing the World's Highest Peak." ### 中朝两国人民互相学习互相鼓励  红星中朝友好公社昨命名  朝鲜贵宾参加庆祝集会受到社员热烈欢迎

1960-08-12
第1版()
专栏：

    中朝两国人民互相学习互相鼓励
    红星中朝友好公社昨命名
    朝鲜贵宾参加庆祝集会受到社员热烈欢迎
    新华社11日讯　北京市大兴县红星人民公社，今天命名为红星中朝友好人民公社。一千多社员在今天上午举行隆重集会，热烈庆祝“八·一五”朝鲜解放十五周年和庆祝红星中朝友好公社的命名。
    朝鲜驻我国大使李永镐和他的夫人、朝中友好协会代表团团长金钟恒和代表团全体团员、以金汉奎为首的朝鲜电影代表团全体人员，都参加了庆祝大会。当朝鲜贵宾们来到红星中朝友好人民公社的时候，齐集在村边马路上的男女社员们挥舞花束，敲锣打鼓，燃放鞭炮，热情欢迎。
    中朝友好协会会长李德全、农业部副部长魏震五、北京市副市长吴晗、外交部第二亚洲司副司长李强奋、对外文委第一司副司长鲁明、黄继光烈士的母亲邓芳芝都参加了庆祝大会。
    吴晗在庆祝大会上讲话，他对朝鲜贵宾的来临表示热烈的欢迎，并且赞颂中朝两国人民深厚的战斗友谊。他说，北京市人民和全国人民一道，热烈祝贺和欢呼兄弟的朝鲜人民在社会主义革命和社会主义建设中取得的伟大胜利。朝鲜人民的每一个胜利，都标志着我们社会主义阵营力量的不断壮大，对于中国人民和全世界人民都是有力的鼓舞和支持。
    吴晗说：“红星人民公社命名为红星中朝友好人民公社，这是全社的荣誉，也是对全体社员的鼓舞。全体社员要在党的领导下，高举总路线、大跃进、人民公社三面红旗，发挥更大的革命干劲，以更出色的成绩，答谢朝鲜人民的深厚友谊，同朝鲜人民携手前进。”
    朝中友协代表团团长金钟恒在会上讲话，向中朝友好人民公社的全体社员表示热烈的祝贺。他说：“我们两国人民有着深厚的传统友谊，我们的友谊是在反对日本帝国主义和美帝国主义，争取祖国的自由、独立的斗争中进一步巩固和发展起来的。今天我们两国人民心连心，亲密合作，互相学习，互相鼓励，交流先进经验，向着共产主义社会奋勇直前。”他说：“今天命名的中朝友好人民公社象征两国人民的友谊和团结，这将对进一步巩固和发展朝中两国人民的友谊团结作出巨大的贡献。”金钟恒的讲话，受到热烈的欢迎，在讲话后，他在雷动的掌声中把一面写着“朝中两国人民友好团结万岁”的锦旗赠给公社。
    红星中朝友好人民公社主任赵彪在大会上代表三万九千多社员向贵宾并通过贵宾向朝鲜人民致以节日的祝贺。他说，今天我们光荣地命名为“红星中朝友好人民公社”，我们感到这不但是一种荣誉，也是对我们的鼓舞，我们要以更出色的成绩来回答朝鲜人民的伟大友谊。
    大会上宣读了红星中朝友好人民公社给朝鲜平安南道顺安郡朝中友谊农业社的节日贺信。会后演出了文艺节目。
    朝鲜贵宾们在公社进了午餐。
    午餐后，贵宾们由公社党委书记郭方、主任赵彪等陪同，参观了公社的农业机械修造厂、塑料厂、种马场、猪场、奶牛场、鸭场、鸡场和稻田。贵宾们每到一处都受到社员们的热烈欢迎。
    贵宾们今天还参观了中朝友谊稻田，这里种着朝鲜平安南道顺安郡朝中友谊农业合作社社员寄来的优良稻种。这些水稻在公社社员的精心培植下，生长茁壮，丰收在望。参观以后，朝中友协代表团团长金钟恒高兴地对公社主任说：“衷心祝贺你们公社取得的成就，希望你们在党的领导下取得更大的成绩。”
    今天晚上，中朝友好协会和对外文委举行电影晚会，招待朝中友协代表团的全体贵宾。晚会上，对外文委副主任楚图南等陪同贵宾们，欣赏了中国影片“聂耳”和“攀登世界最高峰”。（附图片）
