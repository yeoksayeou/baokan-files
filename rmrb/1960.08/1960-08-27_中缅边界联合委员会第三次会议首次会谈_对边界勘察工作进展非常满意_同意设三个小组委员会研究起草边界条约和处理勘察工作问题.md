Title: The Third Meeting of the Sino-Burmese Joint Boundary Committee Holds its First Talks: Extremely Satisfied with the Progress of Boundary Survey Work; Agrees to Establish Three Subcommittees to Study and Draft Boundary Treaties and Handle Survey Work Issues
Author:
Date: 1960-08-27
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Rangoon, August 26th: The first meeting of the third session of the Sino-Burmese Joint Boundary Committee was held here yesterday morning. The following is the full text of the press communiqué issued today concerning this meeting:

    The Sino-Burmese Joint Boundary Committee held its first meeting of the third session at the Ministry of Foreign Affairs Guest House at 9:30 a.m. yesterday (August 25), and agreed to establish three subcommittees to study and draft boundary treaties and handle survey work issues. The meeting was conducted in an extremely cordial and friendly atmosphere.

    Present at the meeting were Brigadier General Aung Gyi, the Chief Representative of Burma, Yao Zhongming, the Chief Representative of China, and representatives and advisors from both sides.

    The two Chief Representatives exchanged greetings, expressing the satisfaction and appreciation of the people and governments of both countries for the rapid progress made by the Joint Committee and the Joint Survey Team.

    The Joint Committee reviewed the work of the Joint Survey Team based on reports submitted by both sides and unanimously expressed satisfaction with the progress of the survey team's work.

    They commended the personnel from both sides participating in the survey work for their spirit of enduring hardship, overcoming difficulties, and cooperating with each other. They also expressed gratitude for the contributions made by the national defense service personnel, local governments, and border area administration officials, as well as the people in the border areas of both sides, in providing active assistance to the survey work.

    The meeting also noted that the remarkable progress made by the Joint Survey Team is the result of the spirit of mutual understanding, concession, and friendly cooperation between the personnel of both sides. Their cooperation in handling all issues has helped to further strengthen the feelings of amity, friendship, and understanding between the two countries.

### 中缅边界联合委员会第三次会议首次会谈  对边界勘察工作进展非常满意  同意设三个小组委员会研究起草边界条约和处理勘察工作问题

1960-08-27
第1版()
专栏：

    中缅边界联合委员会第三次会议首次会谈
    对边界勘察工作进展非常满意
    同意设三个小组委员会研究起草边界条约和处理勘察工作问题
    新华社仰光26日电　中缅边界联合委员会第三次会议的第一次会谈昨天上午在这里举行。今天发表有关这次会谈的新闻公报全文如下：
    中缅边界联合委员会昨天（8月25日）上午九时半在外交部宾馆举行第三次会议的第一次会谈，并同意设立三个小组委员会来研究起草边界条约和处理勘察工作的问题。会议是在极其亲切和友好的气氛中进行的。
    出席会议的有缅甸首席代表昂季准将、中国首席代表姚仲明以及双方的代表和顾问们。
    两位首席代表相互致意，表示两国人民和政府对于联合委员会和联合勘察队所取得的迅速进展感到非常满意并加以赞许。
    联合委员会根据双方提出的报告检查了联合勘察队的工作，并且对勘察队的工作的进展一致表示满意。
    他们赞扬参加勘察工作的双方人员的忍受艰苦、克服困难和相互合作的精神。他们还对国防服务人员、地方政府和边境地区管理局官员以及双方边境地区人民对勘察工作提供积极援助所作出的贡献表示感谢。
    这次会谈还注意到，联合勘察队所取得的显著的进展，是双方人员充满互谅互让和友好合作的精神的结果。他们在处理一切问题时的合作有助于进一步加强两国之间的亲善、友好和谅解的感情。
