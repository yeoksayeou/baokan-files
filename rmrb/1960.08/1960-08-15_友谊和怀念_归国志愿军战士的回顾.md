Title: Friendship and Remembrance – Reflections of Returning Volunteer Army Soldiers
Author: Gu Gong
Date: 1960-08-15
Page: 8

Friendship and Remembrance
——Reflections of Returning Volunteer Army Soldiers
Gu Gong

    Bowl

    Bowl after bowl of bronze,
    Passed down generation after generation,
    Every grandfather of the Korean people,
    Used it to swallow bitterness.

    Bowl after bowl of bronze,
    Passed down generation after generation,
    Until General Kim Il-sung raised the red flag,
    The bronze bowls gleamed with golden light.

    Bowl after bowl of bronze,
    Filled with white rice,
    The Korean people hold them with both hands,
    And offer them to the Volunteer Army.

    Take it! Dear brothers,
    Take it! Brave comrades,
    You are returning to your homeland,
    Take this bronze bowl as a souvenir.

    Take it! Dear children,
    Take it! Unforgettable comrades,
    This shining bronze bowl,
    Will forever be filled with friendship and remembrance...

    Farewell

    In the dense rain,
    We hurried on our way;
    Goodbye, hilltops with red flags planted,
    Goodbye, heroic land.

    Here we bombed American tanks,
    Here we escorted American prisoners of war,
    Here we once used hand grenades,
    To blow up MacArthur's bunker...

    Here we spread out battle maps,
    Here we made attack deployments,
    Here, the blood of soldiers,
    Dyed the azalea bushes red.

    In the dense rain,
    We hurried on our way;
    Military boots gently tread the mud,
    Unwilling to disturb every window.

    The Korean people, with tears in their eyes,
    Pushed open their doors and poured out,
    Fighting to carry backpacks! Fighting to carry ammunition boxes,
    Following the ranks closely, very closely.

    Village after village,
    All followed our moving footsteps,
    The ranks grew longer and longer, larger and larger,
    Crossing bridges, crossing valleys...

### 友谊和怀念——归国志愿军战士的回顾
顾工
1960-08-15
第8版()
专栏：

    友谊和怀念
    ——归国志愿军战士的回顾
    顾工
    碗
    一只一只铜碗，
    一辈一辈相传，
    朝鲜人民的每一位祖父，
    都用它来吞咽苦难。
    一只一只铜碗，
    一辈一辈相传，
    直到金日成将军扬起红旗，
    铜碗才金光闪闪。
    一只一只铜碗，
    盛满白白米饭，
    朝鲜人民用双手捧着，
    捧到志愿军面前。
    收下吧！亲爱的子弟，
    收下吧！英勇的同伴，
    你们要回祖国了，
    收下这铜碗作为纪念。
    收下吧！亲爱的儿女，
    收下吧！难忘的同伴，
    这闪光的铜碗，
    永远盛满着友谊和怀恋……。
    别
    在密密的雨丝中，
    我们匆匆地赶路；
    再见了，插红旗的山头，
    再见了，英雄的国土。
    在这里炸过美国坦克，
    在这里押过美国战俘，
    在这里曾经用手雷，
    炸飞过麦克阿瑟的掩蔽部……
    在这里摊开过作战地图，
    在这里作过进攻的部署，
    在这里战士的鲜血，
    染红过金达莱的花株。
    在密密的雨丝中，
    我们匆匆地赶路；
    军鞋轻轻地踏着泥泞，
    不愿惊动每一扇窗户。
    朝鲜人民流着热泪，
    纷纷推开屋门涌出，
    抢背背包啊！抢背弹药箱，
    紧紧地紧紧地跟随着队伍。
    一个村庄又一个村庄，
    都跟随我们移动脚步，
    队伍越来越长，越来越大，
    穿过了大桥，穿过了山谷……
