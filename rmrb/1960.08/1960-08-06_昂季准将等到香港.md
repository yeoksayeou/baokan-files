Title: Brigadier General Aung Gyi Arrives in Hong Kong
Author:
Date: 1960-08-06
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, Hong Kong, 5th. Brigadier General Aung Gyi, the chief Burmese representative to the second meeting of the Sino-Burmese Joint Boundary Committee, along with representatives, advisors, and staff of the Burmese delegation, arrived in Hong Kong today from Guangzhou.

At a press conference, the delegation spokesman, Colonel Ba Than, told reporters that the resolution of the Sino-Burmese border issue "sets a brilliant example of peaceful coexistence." He emphasized that the boundary treaty between the two countries was drafted on a very reasonable and fair basis. He also said, "We have now reached a satisfactory agreement."

The delegation is scheduled to leave Hong Kong for Yangon on August 7.

### 昂季准将等到香港

1960-08-06
第6版()
专栏：

    昂季准将等到香港
    新华社香港5日电　出席中缅边界联合委员会第二次会议的缅甸首席代表昂季准将，以及缅甸代表团的代表、顾问和工作人员，今天从广州到达香港。
    代表团发言人巴丹上校在记者招待会上对报界说，中缅两国边界问题的解决“树立了一个和平共处的光辉榜样”。他强调说，两国边界条约是在非常合理和公平的基础上拟订的。他还说，“我们现在已经达成圆满的协议。”
    代表团预定在8月7日离开香港前往仰光。
