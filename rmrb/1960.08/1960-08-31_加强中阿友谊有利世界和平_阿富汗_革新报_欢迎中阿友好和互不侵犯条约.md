Title: Strengthening Sino-Afghan Friendship is Conducive to World Peace: Afghanistan's "Islah" Newspaper Welcomes the Sino-Afghan Treaty of Friendship and Non-Aggression
Author:
Date: 1960-08-31
Page: 5
Xinhua News Agency, 30th dispatch from Kabul: Afghanistan's "Islah" newspaper published the Joint Communiqué between the People's Republic of China and the Kingdom of Afghanistan on the 28th and published an editorial for this purpose, saying that the Afghan people welcome the signing of the Treaty of Friendship and Non-Aggression between the People's Republic of China and the Kingdom of Afghanistan and regard this treaty as a vivid example of peace and friendship in this region of the world, and believe that it is in line with the people's wishes.

The editorial also expressed gratitude to Chairman Liu Shaoqi and the Government of the People's Republic of China for inviting King Mohammed Zahir of Afghanistan to pay a friendly visit to China.

The editorial commented on the content of the Sino-Afghan Joint Communiqué, stating, "From this, it seems that the spirit of this communiqué constitutes a lofty hope. The signing of the Treaty of Friendship and Non-Aggression between the two countries is certainly the best representative of the friendly relations between the two countries and the desire to maintain world peace."

The editorial said that the Afghan people and government have good relations with all countries in the world, especially neighboring countries in Asia. It takes respecting the United Nations Charter, accepting the Bandung Principles, supporting the right to national self-determination, and striving for the settlement of issues that have a direct impact on world peace as national policies. Under these conditions, it never refuses any cooperation with all countries in the world for lasting peace. The editorial said, "Our relationship with the People's Republic of China is consolidated on this basis."

The editorial reviewed the long-standing friendly relations between the Chinese and Afghan people and said, "The Chinese and Afghan people have fought against foreign invaders and succeeded in defending their independence, maintaining their unity, and safeguarding the sovereignty of the people. The current friendship between the two countries is becoming stronger day by day due to the ideals and desire for peace of the leaders of Afghanistan and China."

The editorial pointed out that in the future, due to the peaceful policies adopted by the two countries, they will play a valuable role in guaranteeing and strengthening world peace.

The editorial concluded by saying that the Afghan people express their wishes for the future relations between the two friendly countries of China and Afghanistan, and wish them great achievements in their continued struggle to stabilize world peace.

### 加强中阿友谊有利世界和平  阿富汗“革新报”欢迎中阿友好和互不侵犯条约

1960-08-31
第5版()
专栏：

    加强中阿友谊有利世界和平
    阿富汗“革新报”欢迎中阿友好和互不侵犯条约
    新华社30日讯　喀布尔消息：阿富汗“革新报”28日公布了中华人民共和国和阿富汗王国联合公报，并为此发表社论说，阿富汗人民对中华人民共和国和阿富汗王国友好和互不侵犯条约的签订表示欢迎，并且把这个条约看成是在世界这一地区的和平友谊的生动例子，认为它是符合人民愿望的。
    社论并且对刘少奇主席和中华人民共和国政府邀请阿富汗国王穆罕默德·查希尔到中国进行友好访问，表示感谢。
    社论评述了中阿联合公报的内容，它说，“由此看来，这个公报的精神组成了一个崇高的希望。两国友好和互不侵犯条约的签订当然是两国友好关系和维护世界和平愿望的最好代表。”
    社论说，阿富汗人民和政府同世界各国尤其是亚洲邻国具有良好的关系，它以尊重联合国宪章，接受万隆原则，支持民族自决权，为了对世界和平有直接影响的问题的解决而奋斗等作为国家的政策。它在这种条件下，同世界各国为持久和平绝不拒绝各种合作。社论说，“我们同中华人民共和国的关系，便是巩固在这种基础上的。”
    社论在回顾了中阿两国人民悠久的友好关系后说，“阿中人民在为保卫独立、维护自己的统一和人民的主权上，同外国进犯者进行过斗争，并且获得了成功。两国现在的友谊，由于阿中两国领导人的理想和要求和平，一天天趋于加强。”
    社论指出，在将来由于两国所采取的和平政策，对保证和加强世界和平，将起有价值的作用。
    社论最后说，阿富汗人民对中阿两个友好国家的未来关系表示祝愿，并且祝它们在为继续稳定世界和平的奋斗中获得伟大的成就。
