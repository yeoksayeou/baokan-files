Title: Chairman Liu Shaoqi Sends Congratulatory Telegram on Swiss National Day
Author: 
Date: 1960-08-01
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, July 31st: Chairman of the People's Republic of China, Liu Shaoqi, sent a telegram to the President of the Swiss Confederation, Mr. Petitpierre, on July 30th, congratulating him on Swiss National Day. The telegram reads as follows:

To His Excellency Mr. Petitpierre, President of the Swiss Confederation:

On the occasion of the Swiss Confederation's National Day, please allow me to extend my sincere congratulations to Your Excellency and the Swiss people. I wish the Swiss Confederation

### 刘少奇主席电贺瑞士国庆

1960-08-01
第1版()
专栏：

    刘少奇主席电贺瑞士国庆
    新华社31日讯　中华人民共和国主席刘少奇30日致电瑞士联邦主席彼蒂彼爱，祝贺瑞士国庆。电文如下：瑞士联邦主席彼蒂彼爱先生阁下：
    在瑞士联邦国庆纪念的时候，谨向阁下和瑞士人民致以诚挚的祝贺。祝瑞士联邦繁荣，人民幸福。
                 中华人民共和国主席　刘少奇　1960年7月30日
