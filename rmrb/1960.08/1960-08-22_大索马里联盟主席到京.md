Title: Greater Somalia League Chairman Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1960-08-22
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, August 21st—Haji Mohamed and Hussein, chairman of the Greater Somalia League, and Abdulehi Hashi, a member of the Central Committee of the Greater Somalia League, arrived in Beijing by plane today at the invitation of the Chinese Committee for Afro-Asian Solidarity. Those who went to the airport to welcome them included Liao Chengzhi, Chairman of the Chinese Committee for Afro-Asian Solidarity, Bao Erhan, Vice President of the Chinese-African People’s Friendship Association and Director of the China Islamic Association, and Zhu Ziqi, Secretary-General of the Chinese Committee for Afro-Asian Solidarity.

### 大索马里联盟主席到京

1960-08-22
第5版()
专栏：

    大索马里联盟主席到京
    新华社21日讯　应中国亚非团结委员会邀请前来我国访问的大索马里联盟主席哈吉·穆罕默德、侯赛因及大索马里联盟中央委员阿布杜勒希·哈希，今天乘飞机到达北京。前往机场欢迎的有中国亚非团结委员会主席廖承志，中国非洲人民友好协会副会长、中国伊斯兰教协会主任包尔汉，中国亚非团结委员会秘书长朱子奇等人。
