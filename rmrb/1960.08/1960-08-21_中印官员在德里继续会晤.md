Title: Sino-Indian Officials Continue Meeting in Delhi
Author:
Date: 1960-08-21
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, New Delhi, August 20th: Chinese and Indian officials continued their meeting in Delhi on the 19th. Their last meeting was in Beijing until the adjournment on July 25, 1960. They are now engaged in completing the discussion of the second agenda item, namely treaties and agreements, traditions and customs.

Under normal circumstances, they will hold three meetings a week.

### 中印官员在德里继续会晤

1960-08-21
第6版()
专栏：

    中印官员在德里继续会晤
    新华社20日讯　新德里消息：中国和印度官员19日在德里继续了他们的会晤。双方上次会晤是在1960年7月25日在北京休会的。他们现在正在从事于完成对第二项议程，即，条约和协定、传统和习惯的讨论。
    在通常情况下，他们每周将举行三次会晤。
