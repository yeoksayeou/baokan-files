Title: Using Loan Conditions to Control the Philippine Economy: U.S. Monopoly Capital's Ruthless Tactics; Philippine Government Forced to Sell Fertilizer Plant in Exchange for U.S. Loan
Author:
Date: 1960-08-02
Page: 5

　　Xinhua News Agency, Manila Dispatch: U.S. financial monopoly consortiums are maliciously using loans with various conditions attached to further exploit and control the Philippine economy.

　　The news says that in order to adapt to the conditions put forward by the "International Bank for Reconstruction and Development" eighteen months ago, on July 13, the Philippine government sold its state-owned Maria Cristina Fertilizer Plant in Iligan City, worth 12 million pesos, to a private enterprise in order to obtain a $21 million loan from this American-controlled bank for a hydroelectric power project in the Philippines.

　　It is revealed that U.S. monopoly capitalists put forward such loan conditions to hinder the Philippine national subsidy fertilizer industry in order for American fertilizers to control the Philippine fertilizer market. According to figures from the Philippine Bureau of Statistics, the Philippines imported $8 million worth of fertilizer from the United States in 1957, which decreased to $3 million in 1958.

　　The news says that the U.S. Export-Import Bank is delaying approval of a loan to the Iligan Steel Mill controlled by the Philippine government because it opposes the Philippine government's plan to control 51% of the shares.

　　Valencia, a columnist for the "Manila Times," commented on the ugly nature of U.S. "aid" and loans to the Philippines, saying, "The United States does not consider the Philippines as an equal, but as a beggar who knows nothing but how to beg for food."

### 利用贷款条件控制菲律宾经济  美国垄断财团手段毒辣  菲政府被迫出卖肥料厂换取美国贷款

1960-08-02
第5版()
专栏：

    利用贷款条件控制菲律宾经济
    美国垄断财团手段毒辣
    菲政府被迫出卖肥料厂换取美国贷款
    据新华社讯　马尼拉消息：美国金融垄断财团正恶毒地利用附加各种条件的贷款来进一步剥削和控制菲律宾的经济。
    消息说，菲律宾政府为了适应“国际复兴开发银行”十八个月前提出的条件，7月13日曾把它在怡里彦市价值一千二百万比索的国营玛丽亚·克里斯蒂安娜肥料厂卖给私营企业以获取这家美国控制的银行给予菲律宾一个水力发电工程两千一百万美元的贷款。
    据透露，美国垄断资本家提出这样的贷款条件，目的是要阻挠由菲律宾国家津贴的肥料工业，以便美国肥料控制菲律宾的肥料市场。据菲律宾统计局的数字，菲律宾1957年从美国输入价值八百万美元的肥料，1958年减少到三百万美元。
    消息说，美国进出口银行延不批准给菲律宾政府控制的怡里彦钢铁厂的贷款，因为它反对菲律宾政府要控制51％股票的计划。
    “马尼拉时报”专栏作家巴伦西亚在评论美国给菲律宾的“援助”和贷款的丑恶性质时说，“美国认为菲律宾不是平等的，而是一个除要饭以外什么也不知道的乞丐。”
