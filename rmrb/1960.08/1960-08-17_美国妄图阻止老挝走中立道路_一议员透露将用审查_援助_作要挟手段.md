Title: U.S. Attempts to Prevent Laos from Following a Neutral Path; A Congressman Reveals that He Will Use the Threat of Reviewing "Aid" as a Means of Coercion
Author:
Date: 1960-08-17
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, Washington, 16th: U.S. Representative Zablocki, Chairman of the Far East Subcommittee of the House Foreign Affairs Committee, once again brandished the economic stick on the 15th, clamoring for a "re-examination" of the U.S. "aid" program to Laos, attempting to use this to coerce the new Lao government.

Zablocki was very uneasy about the new Lao government's announcement of pursuing a neutral policy. He clamored that, in view of the current trend of Laos moving towards neutralism, "a thorough re-examination and inspection of our aid program to Laos is necessary."

Zablocki announced that the U.S. Assistant Secretary of State for Far Eastern Affairs, Parsons, will attend the subcommittee on the 16th to discuss the Lao situation together.

### 美国妄图阻止老挝走中立道路  一议员透露将用审查“援助”作要挟手段

1960-08-17
第6版()
专栏：

    美国妄图阻止老挝走中立道路
    一议员透露将用审查“援助”作要挟手段
    新华社16日讯　华盛顿消息：美国众议院外交委员会远东小组委员会主席扎布洛基15日又挥舞经济大棒，叫嚷要“重新审查”美国对老挝的“援助”计划，企图以此来要挟老挝新政府。
    扎布洛基对老挝新政府宣布奉行中立政策很感不安。他叫嚷说，鉴于老挝目前走向中立主义的趋势，“必须对我们援助老挝的计划作一彻底的重新审查和检查”。
    扎布洛基宣布，美国负责远东事务的助理国务卿帕森斯将在16日出席这个小组委员会，一同来讨论老挝局势。国际简讯
