Title: Typhoon Emergency Alert: Strong Winds and Heavy Rain Expected in Eastern Heilongjiang and Jilin
Author:
Date: 1960-08-31
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, August 30th—The Central Meteorological Research Institute issued a typhoon emergency alert on the morning of the 30th:

The center of Typhoon No. 17 of 1960 was located in the central part of the Sea of Japan at 2:00 AM this morning, near 39 degrees north latitude and 133.5 degrees east longitude. The typhoon center will move in a due north direction at a speed of 24 to 30 knots per hour in the future. It is expected to pass through the southern part of the Soviet Primorsky Krai this afternoon to tonight and brush the eastern edge of Heilongjiang Province to the north. Eastern Heilongjiang and eastern Jilin will experience strong winds of level six to eight, as well as heavy rain to torrential rain.

### 台风紧急警报  黑龙江、吉林东部将有大风、大雨

1960-08-31
第4版()
专栏：

    台风紧急警报
    黑龙江、吉林东部将有大风、大雨
    新华社30日讯　中央气象科学研究所30日早晨，发布台风紧急警报：
    1960年第十七号台风，今天早晨二时中心位置在日本海中部，即北纬三十九度，东经一百三十三点五度附近。未来台风中心将以每小时二十四到三十浬的速度向正北方向移动，预计今天下午到晚上将经过苏联东海滨省南部向北擦过黑龙江省东部边缘，黑龙江东部、吉林东部将有六到八级大风，并有大雨到暴雨。
