Title: To Celebrate the 15th Anniversary of the Founding of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam, China Will Hold Sino-Vietnamese Friendship Week in Various Places

Author:

Date: 1960-08-27
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, August 26th: The glorious festival of the Vietnamese people – the 15th anniversary of the founding of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam – is approaching. At that time, grand celebrations will be held in the capital and many cities across the country to enhance the unbreakable brotherly friendship between the peoples of China and Vietnam.

Beginning on August 29th, ten cities, including Beijing, Tianjin, Shanghai, Wuhan, Guangzhou, Xi'an, Nanjing, Zhengzhou, Nanning, and Kunming, will successively hold "Sino-Vietnamese Friendship Week." During Friendship Week, people from all walks of life in the capital will hold a grand gathering to celebrate the 15th anniversary of the founding of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam. Workers, students, and people from all walks of life in various places will also hold a series of celebrations and get-togethers to celebrate the festival. The capital will also host a photo exhibition to showcase the great achievements of the construction of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam. During Friendship Week, a Vietnamese Film Week will be held in the capital and fifteen other cities across the country, screening films from the Democratic Republic of Vietnam.

The distinguished guests of the Vietnam-China Friendship Association delegation, the envoys of friendship of the Vietnamese people who have arrived in Beijing, will celebrate the great festival of the Vietnamese people together with our people.

### 庆祝越南民主共和国成立十五周年  我国各地将举行中越友好周

1960-08-27
第1版()
专栏：

    庆祝越南民主共和国成立十五周年
    我国各地将举行中越友好周
    新华社26日讯　越南人民的光辉节日——越南民主共和国成立十五周年即将到来，届时首都和全国许多城市都将举行规模盛大的庆祝活动，以增进中越两国人民牢不可破的兄弟友谊。
    从8月29日开始，北京、天津、上海、武汉、广州、西安、南京、郑州、南宁、昆明十个城市，将先后举行“中越友好周”。在友好周期间，首都各界人民要举行隆重集会庆祝越南民主共和国成立十五周年。各地工人、学生和各界群众也要举行一系列庆祝会、联欢会等庆祝节日的活动。首都还将举办图片展览会，介绍越南民主共和国建设的巨大成就。在友好周期间，首都和全国其它十五个城市要举行越南民主共和国电影周，放映越南民主共和国的影片。
    已经到达北京的越南人民的友谊使者——越中友好协会代表团的贵宾，将和我国人民一起，欢庆越南人民的伟大节日。
