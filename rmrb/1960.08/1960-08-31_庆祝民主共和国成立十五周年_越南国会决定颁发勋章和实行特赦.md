Title: Celebrating the 15th Anniversary of the Founding of the Democratic Republic, Vietnamese National Assembly Decides to Award Medals and Implement Amnesty
Author:
Date: 1960-08-31
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, Hanoi, August 30th. According to the Vietnam News Agency, the Standing Committee of the Vietnamese National Assembly, at its third meeting on the 29th, decided, based on the proposal of the Vietnamese government meeting, to award the Gold Star Medal, Ho Chi Minh Medal, Independence Medal, and Resistance War Medal to individuals and collectives who have made contributions during the War of Resistance on the occasion of celebrating the 15th anniversary of the founding of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam. The meeting also decided to grant amnesty to a group of criminals.

At this meeting, a decision was also passed to award the second and third class Labor Medals to units on the Dong Anh-Thai Nguyen railway construction site, belonging to the General Railway Administration of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam, for completing the construction task ahead of schedule. This railway is scheduled to officially open to traffic on the 30th.

### 庆祝民主共和国成立十五周年  越南国会决定颁发勋章和实行特赦

1960-08-31
第5版()
专栏：

    庆祝民主共和国成立十五周年
    越南国会决定颁发勋章和实行特赦
    新华社河内30日电　据越南通讯社报道，越南国会常务委员会在29日举行的第三次会议上根据越南政府会议的提议，决定在庆祝越南民主共和国成立十五周年之际，对在抗战期间有功的个人和集体颁发金星勋章、胡志明勋章、独立勋章和抗战勋章。会议还决定特赦一批罪犯。
    在这次会议上还通过了对越南民主共和国铁路总局所属东英—太原铁路建设工地上提前完成建设任务的各单位授于二、三级劳动勋章的决定。这条铁路定于30日正式通车。
