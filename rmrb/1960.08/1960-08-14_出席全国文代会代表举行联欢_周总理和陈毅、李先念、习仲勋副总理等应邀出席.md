Title: Representatives Attending the National Literary and Art Congress Hold Get-Together; Premier Zhou Enlai and Vice Premiers Chen Yi, Li Xiannian, and Xi Zhongxun Attend by Invitation
Author:
Date: 1960-08-14
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, August 13th: On the evening of the successful conclusion of the Third National Congress of Literary and Art Workers of China, the National Federation of Literary and Art Circles held a get-together for the representatives who were full of confidence and about to return to their work posts.
    Premier of the State Council Zhou Enlai, Vice Premiers Chen Yi, Li Xiannian, Xi Zhongxun and other central leaders were invited to join the representatives in the get-together. Guo Moruo, Chairman of the National Federation of Literary and Art Circles, Zhou Yang, Vice Chairman, and others attended the evening party.
    The artists among the representatives showed their talents in the get-together, performing more than ten wonderful programs such as singing, folk art, folk song singing, and opera a cappella, which won warm applause. Peking opera, Hebei Bangzi opera, film and other programs were also performed at the party.

### 出席全国文代会代表举行联欢  周总理和陈毅、李先念、习仲勋副总理等应邀出席

1960-08-14
第5版()
专栏：

    出席全国文代会代表举行联欢
    周总理和陈毅、李先念、习仲勋副总理等应邀出席
    新华社13日讯　在中国文学艺术工作者第三次代表大会胜利闭幕的当晚，全国文联为满怀信心即将返回工作岗位的代表们举行了联欢晚会。
    国务院总理周恩来、副总理陈毅、李先念、习仲勋等中央负责人应邀和代表们一起进行了联欢。全国文联主席郭沫若、副主席周扬等都出席了晚会。
    代表中的艺术家们在联欢中大显身手，表演了歌唱、曲艺、民歌演唱、戏曲清唱等十多个精彩节目，博得热烈掌声。晚会上还演出了京剧、河北梆子、电影等节目。
