Title: Vietnam Cheo Opera Troupe Performs in Beijing for the First Time; Chairman Zhu De and Others Watch Performance and Meet with Head of Troupe
Author: 
Date: 1960-08-29
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, August 28th. On the eve of celebrating the 15th anniversary of the founding of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam, the artists of the Cheo Opera Troupe of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam, envoys of friendship and art of the Vietnamese people, who came to visit our country, gave their premiere performance at the Capital People's Theater tonight.

Chairman Zhu De and Vice Premier Xi Zhongxun watched the performance and, during the intermission, met with the head of the troupe, Wu Qiu, the deputy head, Gao Jindian, and other responsible persons and the main actors of the troupe, and had cordial talks with them.

Vietnamese Cheo opera has a long history and is a type of opera with a strong Vietnamese national style. The content of Cheo opera is mostly satirical and humorous, with the theme of reflecting the thoughts, feelings, and aspirations of the working people. Cheo opera is deeply loved by the people of Vietnam.

The Vietnam Cheo Opera Troupe was founded in the northern base area of Vietnam in 1952. It is now one of the main Cheo opera troupes in Vietnam. Today, the Vietnamese artists successfully performed the historical drama "Magic Water Bottle," which was warmly welcomed by the audience in the capital.

After the performance, Chairman Zhu De, Vice Premier Xi Zhongxun, and others went on stage, shook hands cordially with the artists, and congratulated them on the success of their performance.

Also present at the performance were: Zhang Zhixiang, Acting Director of the Commission for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries, Deputy Directors Chu Tunan, Ding Xilin, Qu Wu, Luo Jun, Vice Minister of Foreign Affairs Zhang Hanfu, Vice Minister of Culture Xu Guangxiao, and well-known figures in the capital's cultural and artistic circles, such as Tian Han, Mei Lanfang, Lü Ji, and Jian Bozan.

The Ambassador of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam to China, Tran Tu Binh, watched the performance. Ambassadors and diplomatic officials from various countries stationed in China also attended the performance. (Attached picture)

### 越南嘲剧艺术团在京首次演出  朱德委员长等观看演出并接见艺术团团长

1960-08-29
第4版()
专栏：

    越南嘲剧艺术团在京首次演出
    朱德委员长等观看演出并接见艺术团团长
    新华社28日讯　在欢庆越南民主共和国成立十五周年的前夕，来我国访问的越南人民的友谊和艺术使者——越南民主共和国嘲剧艺术团的艺术家们，今天晚上在首都人民剧场举行首次演出。
    朱德委员长，习仲勋副总理观看了演出，并且在演出休息期间接见了艺术团团长武秋、副团长高金典等负责人和艺术团的主要演员，和他们进行了亲切的谈话。
    越南嘲剧艺术具有悠久历史，它是具有浓厚的越南民族风格的一种戏曲。嘲剧的内容大都是讽刺和诙谐的，以反映劳动人民的思想感情和愿望为主题。嘲剧在越南深受人民群众喜爱。
    越南嘲剧艺术团1952年成立于越南北部根据地。现在它是越南嘲剧的主要团体之一。今天，越南艺术家成功地演出了历史题材的剧目“神水瓶”，受到了首都观众的热烈欢迎。
    演出结束后，朱德委员长、习仲勋副总理等，走上舞台，和艺术家们亲切握手，祝贺他们演出的成功。
    出席观看演出的还有：对外文化联络委员会代主任张致祥、副主任楚图南、丁西林、屈武、罗俊，外交部副部长章汉夫，文化部副部长徐光霄，以及首都文化艺术界著名人士田汉、梅兰芳、吕骥、翦伯赞等。
    越南民主共和国驻中国大使陈子平观看了演出。各国驻中国的使节、外交官员也出席观看了演出。（附图片）
