Title: Soviet Scientists Discuss Spaceflight: The Problem of Spacecraft Returning to Earth is a Watershed; Once this Problem is Solved, Humans Can Directly Venture into Space
Author:
Date: 1960-08-22
Page: 5

    Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, August 21st: The news of the successful return to Earth of the Soviet Union's second satellite spacecraft and its test animals has been met with unanimous jubilation by the Soviet people. Soviet newspapers today published articles and interviews with scientists, expounding on the enormous significance of this victory.

    Professor Poloskov, in a statement published in "Pravda," said that this remarkable victory of the Soviet Union's space conquerors once again proves that Soviet experts have achieved brilliant success in ensuring the safe return of spacecraft and their passengers to Earth. This achievement marks the entry into a new era in the conquest of outer space. The professor said that human flight will be realized when the conditions needed to safely achieve human spaceflight are fully understood.

    Poloskov said that although there are many planets and natural satellites with near-circular orbits in the solar system, people have never known of such a celestial body whose orbit is closer to circular than that of the Soviet Union's second spacecraft. Soviet scientists and engineers have done something that nature itself has not done. Achieving such an orbit requires extremely precise control over the magnitude and direction of the spacecraft's initial velocity.

    Grigoriev, Deputy Doctor of Technical Sciences, published an article in "Komsomolskaya Pravda" saying that the problem of returning a spacecraft to Earth is a watershed; once this problem is solved, humans can directly venture into space. Grigoriev also said that the solution to the problem of spacecraft returning to Earth will not only enable human spaceflight, but also make it possible to establish huge artificial satellite stations around the Earth. These satellite stations could become manned space experimenters and also serve as fuel depots for rockets. From such satellite stations, rockets can carry people to other planets in the solar system, which is easier and more economical than launching rockets from Earth.

    Dr. Pokrovsky of Technical Sciences said that the television equipment on the second spacecraft made it possible for scientists on the ground to observe the condition of the experimental animals during spaceflight. However, it is still necessary to realize human spaceflight, because instruments can only record phenomena. If we rely only on instruments, then humanity's understanding of the universe will be very slow.

### 苏联科学家畅谈宇宙飞行  宇宙飞船返回地面的问题是个分界线，这个问题解决了，人就可以直接走向宇宙

1960-08-22
第5版()
专栏：

    苏联科学家畅谈宇宙飞行
    宇宙飞船返回地面的问题是个分界线，这个问题解决了，人就可以直接走向宇宙
    新华社莫斯科21日电　苏联第二个卫星式宇宙飞船及试验动物顺利返回地面的消息，引起了苏联人民的一致欢呼。苏联各报今天发表了科学家们的文章和谈话，阐述这一胜利的巨大意义。
      “真理报”发表的波洛斯科夫教授的谈话说，苏联宇宙征服者的这一卓越胜利再次证明，苏联专家在保证宇宙飞船及其乘客安全回到地球方面已经取得了辉煌的成就。这个成就标志着在征服宇宙空间方面已经进入新的时代。这位教授说，当为了安全地实现人的宇宙飞行所需要的条件被完全研究透彻以后，就要实现人的飞行了。
    波洛斯科夫说，虽然在太阳系中存在着许多行星和具有接近于圆形轨道的天然卫星，但人们从来还不知道有这样的天体，它的轨道像苏联第二个宇宙飞船的轨道那样更接近于圆形。苏联科学家和工程师们作出了大自然本身也没有做出的事。获得这样的轨道，要求对飞船的初始速度的大小和方向控制得极精确。
    技术科学副博士格里戈里耶夫在“共青团真理报”上发表文章说，宇宙飞船返回到地面的问题是个分界线，这问题解决了，人就可以直接走向宇宙了。格里戈里耶夫还说，宇宙飞船返回地面问题解决后不只可以使人类实现宇宙飞行，并且使人们有可能在地球外围建立巨大的人造卫星站，这些卫星站可以成为有人居住的宇宙试验家，并且也可以成为火箭的燃料贮藏室，从这样的卫星站上，火箭可以带着人飞向太阳系的其他星球，这要比从地球上发射火箭容易而且经济得多。
    技术科学博士波克罗夫斯基说，第二个宇宙飞船上的电视设备，使得科学家们有可能在地面上观察试验动物在宇宙飞行中的情况。但是，实现人的宇宙飞行还是有必要的，因为仪器只能记录现象，如果只靠仪器，那么人类对宇宙的了解的速度将是很缓慢的。
