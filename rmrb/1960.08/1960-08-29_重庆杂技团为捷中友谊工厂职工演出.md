Title: Chongqing Acrobatic Troupe Performs for Workers at the Czech-Chinese Friendship Factory
Author:
Date: 1960-08-29
Page: 5
Xinhua News Agency, Prague, August 26th: The Chinese Chongqing Acrobatic Troupe, currently visiting Czechoslovakia, performed today for the workers of the Czech-Chinese Friendship Factory in Tábor County.

　　Under the clear sky, the acrobats performed wonderful traditional Chinese acrobatic acts. The spacious stadium was filled with bursts of applause, laughter, and cheers. After the performance, Novotny, the director of the Czech-Chinese Friendship Factory, and Li Shaoyan, the head of the acrobatic troupe, exchanged gifts. A group of Young Pioneers swarmed the stage to present flowers. To the sound of "Socialism is Good," the audience waved goodbye to their Chinese brothers with lingering applause.

### 重庆杂技团为捷中友谊工厂职工演出

1960-08-29
第5版()
专栏：

    重庆杂技团为捷中友谊工厂职工演出
    新华社布拉格26日电　正在捷克斯洛伐克访问的中国重庆杂技团，今日到塔波尔县为捷中友谊工厂的职工们作了表演。
    在晴朗的天空下，杂技团的演员们演出了精采的中国传统杂技节目。广阔的体育场上不断爆发出一阵阵掌声、欢笑声和赞叹声。演出完毕后，捷中友谊工厂厂长诺沃提尼和杂技艺术团团长李少言互赠礼品。一群少先队员拥上舞台献花。在“社会主义好”的乐声中，观众以经久不息的掌声依依不舍地送别了来自中国的兄弟们。
