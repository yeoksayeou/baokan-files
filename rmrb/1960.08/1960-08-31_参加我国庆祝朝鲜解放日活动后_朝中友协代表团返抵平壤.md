Title: Korean-Chinese Friendship Association Delegation Returns to Pyongyang After Participating in China's Celebration of Korean Liberation Day

Author:

Date: 1960-08-31
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, Pyongyang, August 30th: The delegation of the Korean-Chinese Friendship Association, led by its head Kim Jong-heng, returned to Pyongyang today by international train after being invited to China to participate in the celebrations of the 15th anniversary of the liberation of Korea.

Those who greeted the delegation at the station included: Ha Ang-cheon, Chairman of the Korean-Chinese Friendship Association; Song Young, Chairman of the Korean Committee for Foreign Cultural Relations; and Li Le-yan, Vice Minister of Higher Education.

Li Dequan, head of the Chinese-Korean Friendship Association delegation currently visiting Korea, and deputy head Li Jiebo, as well as Liu Xianglun, chargé d'affaires ad interim of the Chinese Embassy in Korea, also welcomed them at the station.

### 参加我国庆祝朝鲜解放日活动后  朝中友协代表团返抵平壤

1960-08-31
第5版()
专栏：

    参加我国庆祝朝鲜解放日活动后
    朝中友协代表团返抵平壤
    新华社平壤30日电　应邀到中国参加朝鲜解放十五周年庆祝活动的朝中友好协会代表团在团长金钟恒率领下，今天乘国际列车回到平壤。
    到车站迎接代表团的有：朝中友好协会委员长河仰天、朝鲜对外文化联络委员会委员长宋影、高等教育省副相李乐彦。
    正在朝鲜访问的中朝友好协会代表团团长李德全和副团长李颉伯以及中国驻朝鲜大使馆临时代办刘祥纶也到车站欢迎。
