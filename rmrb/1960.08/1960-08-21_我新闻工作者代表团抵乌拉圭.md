Title: Our Journalist Delegation Arrives in Uruguay
Author:
Date: 1960-08-21
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, Montevideo, 20th - A Chinese journalist delegation led by Zhu Muzhi, Vice-Chairman of the All-China Journalists Association, arrived in Montevideo, the capital of Uruguay, on the 18th for a friendly visit at the invitation of the Uruguayan Journalists Association.

### 我新闻工作者代表团抵乌拉圭

1960-08-21
第6版()
专栏：

    我新闻工作者代表团抵乌拉圭
    新华社20日讯　蒙得维的亚消息：由中华全国新闻工作者协会副主席朱穆之率领的中国新闻工作者代表团，应乌拉圭新闻工作者协会的邀请，已经在18日抵达乌拉圭首都蒙得维的亚作友好访问。
