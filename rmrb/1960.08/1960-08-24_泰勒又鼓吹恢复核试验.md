Title: Taylor Again Advocates Resuming Nuclear Tests
Author:
Date: 1960-08-24
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, Washington, August 23rd: The American atomic scientist Edward Teller has again publicly advocated that the United States resume nuclear testing and step up the development of nuclear weapons. In a television interview on the 21st, Teller advocated that the U.S. government "resume underground nuclear testing, and if possible, resume nuclear testing in outer space." He clamored that the United States should "develop our nuclear weapons" and establish a so-called "retaliatory force." He said that the American moratorium on testing has caused "us (the United States) to lose time, and we are losing time week after week, month after month."

### 泰勒又鼓吹恢复核试验

1960-08-24
第6版()
专栏：

    泰勒又鼓吹恢复核试验
    据新华社23日讯　华盛顿消息：美国原子科学家爱德华·泰勒又公开鼓吹美国恢复核试验和加紧发展核武器。泰勒21日在电视谈话中主张美国政府“恢复地下核试验，如果可能的话，恢复在宇宙空间的核试验”。他叫嚷美国要“发展我们的核武器”，要建立所谓“回击力量”。他说，美国的暂停试验已经使“我们（美国）失去了时间，而且我们正在一个星期又一个星期、一个月又一个月地失去时间”。
