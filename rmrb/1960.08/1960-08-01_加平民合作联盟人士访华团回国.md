Title: Canadian Co-operative Commonwealth Federation Delegation Returns Home
Author:
Date: 1960-08-01
Page: 6
　
    Xinhua News Agency, July 31st. The Canadian Co-operative Commonwealth Federation delegation, led by William Irvine, a member of the National Council of the Canadian Co-operative Commonwealth Federation and a member of the World Peace Council, concluded its visit to China and left Beijing and Guangzhou today to return home.

### 加平民合作联盟人士访华团回国

1960-08-01
第6版()
专栏：

    加平民合作联盟人士访华团回国
    新华社31日讯　由加拿大平民合作联盟全国理事会理事、世界和平理事会理事威廉·欧文率领的加拿大平民合作联盟人士访华团一行六人结束了在我国的参观访问后，于今天分别离开北京和广州回国。
