Title: Concluding Friendly Visit to Afghanistan, Vice Premier Chen Yi Departs Kabul for Home; Prime Minister Daoud and Government Officials See Him Off at the Airport
Author:
Date: 1960-08-28
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Kabul, August 27th: After concluding his week-long friendly visit to Afghanistan, Vice Premier and Foreign Minister of the People's Republic of China, Marshal Chen Yi, left Kabul by plane this morning to return home.

Those seeing him off at the airport included Afghan Prime Minister Sardar Mohammad Daoud, Deputy Prime Minister Ali Mohammad, Deputy Prime Minister and Foreign Minister Sardar Mohammad Naim, Chairman of the National Assembly Mohammad Nuruzai, and Chief of the General Staff of the Army, General Mohammad Hassan. Cabinet ministers, senior military officers, and diplomatic envoys from various countries were also present to see him off.

Chinese Ambassador to Afghanistan Hao Ting and embassy staff members also went to the airport to see him off.

Members of the Chinese Youth Art Troupe and the Beijing Student Men's Basketball Team were also at the airport.

Vice Premier Chen Yi issued a written statement at the airport. Accompanied by Prime Minister Daoud, he reviewed the guard of honor. Before boarding the plane, he warmly exchanged good wishes with the Afghan leaders and shook hands with all those who came to see him off.

### 结束对阿富汗友好访问  陈毅副总理离喀布尔回国  达乌德首相及政府官员等到机场欢送

1960-08-28
第1版()
专栏：

    结束对阿富汗友好访问
    陈毅副总理离喀布尔回国
    达乌德首相及政府官员等到机场欢送
    新华社喀布尔27日电　中华人民共和国国务院副总理兼外交部长陈毅元帅，在结束了对阿富汗历时一星期的友好访问以后，今天上午乘飞机离喀布尔回国。
    在机场送行的有阿富汗首相萨达尔·穆罕默德·达乌德，副首相阿里·穆罕默德，副首相兼外交大臣萨达尔·穆罕默德·纳伊姆，国民议会议长穆罕默德·努鲁塞，陆军总参谋长穆罕默德·哈桑将军。前往送行的还有内阁大臣，高级军官，以及各国外交使节。
    中国驻阿富汗大使郝汀和大使馆人员也到机场送行。
    在机场送行的还有中国青年艺术团团员和北京市学生男子篮球队队员。
    陈毅副总理在机场发表了书面谈话。他在达乌德首相陪同下检阅了仪仗队。他在登上飞机以前，热烈地同阿富汗的领导人互致良好的祝愿，并同全体送行者一一握手。
