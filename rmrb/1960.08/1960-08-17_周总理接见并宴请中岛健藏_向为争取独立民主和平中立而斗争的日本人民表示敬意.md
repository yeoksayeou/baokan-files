Title: Premier Zhou Meets and Hosts Nakajima Kenzo, Expresses Respect to the Japanese People Fighting for Independence, Democracy, Peace and Neutrality
Author:
Date: 1960-08-17
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, August 16th: Premier Zhou Enlai met and hosted Nakajima Kenzo, Chairman of the Japan-China Cultural Exchange Association, his wife, and his entourage, Mr. Shirato Gofu, at noon today.

During a toast at the banquet, Premier Zhou first expressed admiration and respect to the Japanese people who are fighting against the Japan-U.S. military alliance treaty and striving for independence, democracy, peace, and neutrality. He said that although the Japanese people are still suffering from the aggression and oppression of U.S. imperialism, the great Japanese people, with their national self-esteem and long tradition of independence, are invincible, and Japan's destiny is in the hands of the Japanese people. The development of friendship between the Chinese and Japanese people and the normalization of relations between the two countries depend on the joint efforts of the two peoples. Premier Chou congratulated the Chinese People's Association for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries and the Japan-China Cultural Exchange Association on the signing of a joint statement on cultural exchange between the people of China and Japan in Beijing today. Premier Zhou also said that the Chinese delegation led by Comrade Liu Ningyi to the Sixth World Conference against Atomic and Hydrogen Bombs and for General Disarmament was warmly welcomed and supported by the broad masses of the Japanese people. Premier Zhou asked Mr. Nakajima Kenzo to convey the Chinese people's friendship and gratitude to the Japanese people.

Present at the meeting were Liao Chengzhi, Chairman of the Chinese Committee for Afro-Asian Solidarity, Vice Chairman Liu Ningyi, Yang Hansheng, Vice President of the Chinese People's Association for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries, as well as Zhao Puchu, Zhou Erfu, Zheng Senyu, Chen Yu, Zhao Anbo, Lin Lin and others. ### 周总理接见并宴请中岛健藏  向为争取独立民主和平中立而斗争的日本人民表示敬意

1960-08-17
第1版()
专栏：

    周总理接见并宴请中岛健藏
    向为争取独立民主和平中立而斗争的日本人民表示敬意
    新华社16日讯　周恩来总理在今天中午接见并设宴招待日本中国文化交流协会理事长中岛健藏和夫人以及他的随行人员白土吾夫。
    周总理在宴会上祝酒的时候，首先向反对日美军事同盟条约和争取独立、民主、和平、中立而斗争的日本人民表示钦佩和敬意。他说，尽管日本人民现在还遭受着美帝国主义的侵略和压迫，但是，有着民族自尊心和长期独立传统的伟大日本人民是不可战胜的，日本的命运是掌握在日本人民手里的。中日两国人民友好的发展和两国关系的正常化取决于两国人民的共同努力。周总理庆贺中国人民对外文化协会和日本中国文化交流协会今天在北京签订关于中日两国人民间文化交流的联合声明。周总理还说，刘宁一同志率领的参加第六届禁止原子弹氢弹和争取全面裁军世界大会的中国代表团受到日本广大人民的热烈欢迎和支持，周总理请中岛健藏先生把中国人民的友好和感谢的心情带给日本人民。
    在座的有：中国亚非团结委员会主席廖承志、副主席刘宁一，中国人民对外文化协会副会长阳翰笙，以及赵朴初、周而复、郑森禹、陈宇、赵安博、林林等人。（附图片）
