Title: Liao Chengzhi Hosts Kenyan Guests
Author:
Date: 1960-08-15
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, August 14th: Liao Chengzhi, Chairman of the Chinese Committee for Asian-African Solidarity, hosted a luncheon today for Oginga Odinga, Vice-President of the Kenya African National Union, and Vera Ambiso, Secretary of the Kenyan Office in Cairo, who are visiting China.

Liao Chengzhi spoke first, warmly welcoming the distinguished Kenyan guests, calling them representatives of the fighting Kenyan people. He expressed his belief that their visit would further enhance the militant friendship between the Chinese and Kenyan peoples.

Liao Chengzhi said that the Chinese people, who have always admired the struggle of the Kenyan people, have consistently supported the Kenyan people in the past and will always fully support them. He proposed a toast to driving imperialism out of Kenya, to Kenya's independence, and to the freedom of Kenyan people's leaders.

Oginga Odinga then spoke, warmly recalling the sincere friendship forged between the Chinese and Kenyan peoples in the struggle for world peace. He thanked the Chinese people for their warm reception and said that China had left a good impression on him during his short visit.

Liu Changsheng, President of the Chinese-African People's Friendship Association, said that the Chinese and Kenyan people support each other in the common anti-imperialist struggle. The Chinese people have always been concerned about the struggle of the Kenyan people and believe that Kenya will soon achieve independence and freedom.

Vera Ambiso said that the Chinese and Kenyan people are both fighting against imperialism. The friendship between the two peoples is eternal. Zhang Su, Deputy Secretary-General of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress, and heads of various people's organizations also attended the banquet. (Attached picture)

### 廖承志欢宴怯尼亚客人

1960-08-15
第5版()
专栏：

    廖承志欢宴怯尼亚客人
    新华社14日讯　中国亚非团结委员会主席廖承志今天设午宴招待前来中国访问的怯尼亚非洲民族联盟副主席奥金加·奥廷加和怯尼亚驻开罗办事处秘书韦腊·安比梭。
    廖承志首先讲话，热烈欢迎怯尼亚贵宾，称他们为怯尼亚战斗人民的代表。他表示相信他们的访问将进一步增进中、怯两国人民之间的战斗友谊。
    廖承志说，一向对怯尼亚人民的斗争十分钦佩的中国人民在过去一贯支持怯尼亚人民，并且将永远全力支持怯尼亚人民。他建议为把帝国主义从怯尼亚赶出去，为怯尼亚独立，为怯尼亚人民领袖获得自由而干杯。
    奥金加·奥廷加接着讲话，他满怀热情地回顾了中怯两国人民之间在争取世界和平斗争中结成的真诚的友谊。他感谢中国人民给予他的热诚的接待，并且说，在短短的访问期间，中国已经给他留下了良好的印象。
    中非友好协会会长刘长胜说，中国和怯尼亚人民在共同的反帝斗争中是互相支援的。中国人民一向关心怯尼亚人民的斗争，并且相信怯尼亚一定能够很快地获得独立和自由。
    韦腊·安比梭说，中国和怯尼亚人民都在与帝国主义进行斗争。两国人民之间的友谊是永恒的。全国人民代表大会常务委员会副秘书长张苏和各人民团体的负责人也出席了宴会。（附图片）
