Title: President Sukarno Discusses the Overseas Chinese Issue: There are Some Minor Difficulties Between Indonesia and China; These Difficulties Can Be Resolved Through the Goodwill of Both Countries
Author:
Date: 1960-08-24
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, Jakarta, August 23rd - Indonesian radio broadcasted on the evening of August 22nd a recording of President Sukarno's speech at the swearing-in ceremony of Indonesia's new ambassador to China, Sukarni. Sukarno said, "We have some difficulties with China, namely difficulties regarding the issue of overseas Chinese, but these are not major difficulties." He said, "With the goodwill of both countries, these difficulties can be resolved. In any case, these minor difficulties will not change our position and desire to maintain the closest relationship with China."

Sukarno said that he firmly believes that the new ambassador to China will be able to maintain the friendly relations between Indonesia and China very well, "and help resolve these minor difficulties." He said, "These minor difficulties are only slight injuries to the skin and do not harm the body - the friendly relations between Indonesia and China."

Those present at the swearing-in ceremony included First Minister Djuanda, Foreign Minister Subandrio, Deputy Chief of the Army Staff Subroto, and other cabinet ministers.

### 苏加诺总统谈华侨问题  印度尼西亚和中国之间有些小困难  依靠两国的善意这些困难能够解决

1960-08-24
第6版()
专栏：

    苏加诺总统谈华侨问题
    印度尼西亚和中国之间有些小困难
    依靠两国的善意这些困难能够解决
    新华社雅加达23日电　印度尼西亚电台22日晚间广播了苏加诺总统在印度尼西亚驻中国新任大使苏卡尼宣誓就职仪式上讲话的录音。苏加诺说，“我们同中国有一些困难，即在华侨问题上的困难，但是这些不是重大的困难。”他说，“依靠两国的善意，这些困难是能够解决的。无论如何，这些小困难丝毫不会改变我们要同中国保持最密切的关系的立场和愿望。”　
    苏加诺说，他深信新任驻华大使能够很好地保持印度尼西亚同中国的友好关系，“帮助解决这些小困难”。他说，“这些小困难只是皮肤上的轻伤，并不损害躯体——印度尼西亚和中国之间的友好关系。”
    出席宣誓仪式的有第一部长朱安达、外交部长苏班德里约、陆军副总参谋长苏布罗托以及其他的内阁部长。
