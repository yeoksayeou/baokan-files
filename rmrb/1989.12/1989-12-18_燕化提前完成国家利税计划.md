Title: Yanhuang Completes National Tax Revenue Plan Ahead of Schedule
Author: Liupuqian
Date: 1989-12-18
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, December 16th. (Reporter Liupuqian) The largest petrochemical integrated enterprise in China, the Beijing Yanshan Petrochemical Company, has completed the national tax revenue plan for the year ahead of schedule. By the 15th, it had realized a tax revenue of 1.69 billion yuan, exceeding the plan by 40 million yuan. By the middle of December, the production of major products such as high-pressure polyethylene, polypropylene, ethylene glycol, pure benzene, para-xylene, and synthetic rubber had all completed the national plan.

### 燕化提前完成国家利税计划
刘浦泉
1989-12-18
第1版(要闻)
专栏：

    燕化提前完成国家利税计划
    据新华社北京十二月十六日电　（记者刘浦泉）我国最大的石油化工联合企业——北京燕山石油化工公司提前完成国家下达的全年利税计划。截至十五日，已实现利税十六点九亿元，超计划四千万元。到十二月上旬，高压聚乙烯、聚丙烯、乙二醇、纯苯、对二甲苯、合成橡胶等主要产品产量已完成国家计划。
