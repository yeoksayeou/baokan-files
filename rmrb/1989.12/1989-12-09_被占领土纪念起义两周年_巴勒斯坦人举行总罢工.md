Title: Palestinians Hold General Strike to Mark Two Years of Uprising in Occupied Territories
Author:
Date: 1989-12-09
Edition: 4

Palestinians in the occupied territories held a general strike today to commemorate the second anniversary of the Palestinian uprising. According to a report by the Palestinian News Agency, the Palestinian people in the occupied territories held a general strike today to commemorate the second anniversary of the Palestinian uprising.

In the West Bank and Gaza Strip, Palestinians hung hundreds of Palestinian national flags and portraits of President Arafat. Palestinian rebels also posted slogans on the walls of cities, villages, and refugee camps.

The Israeli authorities, in order to suppress the uprising of the Palestinians, sealed off all the occupied territories and declared them military zones, cutting off external telephone communications.

In the Gaza Strip, Palestinians clashed with Israeli soldiers. A 17-year-old Palestinian youth was killed in the clash by the Israeli military.

The report said that in response to the call of the Palestinian Uprising National Unified Command, the Palestinian people will start a four-day general strike from today.

### 被占领土纪念起义两周年  巴勒斯坦人举行总罢工

1989-12-09
第4版(国际新闻)
专栏：

    被占领土纪念起义两周年  巴勒斯坦人举行总罢工
    新华社突尼斯12月7日电  据巴勒斯坦通讯社报道，被占领土上的巴勒斯坦人民群众今天举行总罢工，纪念巴勒斯坦人发动起义两周年。
    在约旦河西岸和加沙地带，巴勒斯坦人悬挂了数百面巴勒斯坦国国旗和总统阿拉法特的画像。巴勒斯坦起义者还在一些城市、村庄和难民营的墙壁上张贴了标语口号。
    以色列当局为了镇压起义的巴勒斯坦人，封锁了全部被占领土，并宣布被占领土为军事区，切断了对外电话联络。
    在加沙地带，巴勒斯坦人同以色列士兵发生激烈冲突。一名17岁的巴勒斯坦青年在冲突中被以军打死。
    报道说，巴勒斯坦人民群众为响应巴勒斯坦起义全国统一指挥部的号召，从今天起举行4天的总罢工。
