Title: Zhang Xinyue Calligraphy Exhibition Opens in Beijing
Author: Lǐ Chūnlín, Jiāng Zàizhōng
Date: 1989-12-14
Page: 3

Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, December 12th.

The Zhang Xinyue Calligraphy Exhibition opened today at the China Art Museum. Vice Chairman of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress, Liao Hansheng and Wang Hanbin, attended the opening ceremony. Chen Pishan, Zhang Pinghua, and renowned calligraphers Qi Gong and Shen Peng, among others, were also present to celebrate the occasion.

Zhang Xinyue currently serves as the Deputy Secretary-General of the Legislative Affairs Commission of the National People's Congress. He has a deep love for calligraphy and calligraphy theory, and in his spare time, he wrote the article "An Exploration of the True Face of Wang Xizhi's Calligraphy," which, due to its unique insights, was translated into a textbook by Tsukuba University in Japan.

### 张昕若书法展在京开幕
李春林  姜在忠
1989-12-14
第3版(综合)
专栏：

    张昕若书法展在京开幕
    据新华社北京十二月十二日电  （记者李春林、姜在忠）张昕若书法展览今天在中国美术馆开幕。全国人大常委会副委员长廖汉生、王汉斌出席了开幕式。陈丕显、张平化及著名书法家启功、沈鹏等泼墨祝贺。
    张昕若现任全国人大常委会法制委员会副秘书长。他酷爱书法和书论研究，利用业余时间写的《王羲之书法真面的探讨》一文，因见地独特，被日本筑波大学译作教材。
