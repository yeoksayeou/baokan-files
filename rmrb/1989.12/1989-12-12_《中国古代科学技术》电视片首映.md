Title: Premiere of the Television Series "Ancient Chinese Science and Technology"
Author: Jiang Jianke
Date: 1989-12-12
Edition: 3

The television series "Ancient Chinese Science and Technology," produced by the China Association for Science and Technology's Audio-Visual Center, premiered in Beijing today. The series introduces the brilliant ancient scientific and cultural achievements of the Chinese nation and features outstanding scientific experts. The series consists of over 100 episodes, with six episodes completed so far.

### 《中国古代科学技术》电视片首映
蒋建科
1989-12-12
第3版(综合)
专栏：

    《中国古代科学技术》电视片首映
    本报北京十二月十一日讯　记者蒋建科报道：由中国科协声像中心组织拍摄的《中国古代科学技术》系列电视片，今天在京举行首映式。该片介绍了中华民族创造的灿烂的古代科学文化，并介绍了杰出的科技专家。本系列片共一百多集，至今已完成六集。
