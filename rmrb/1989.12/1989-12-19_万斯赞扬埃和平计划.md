Title: Vance Praises Egypt's Peace Plan
Author:
Date: 1989-12-19
Edition: 4

Former U.S. Secretary of State Cyrus Vance, who is visiting Egypt, said today in Cairo that Egyptian President Hosni Mubarak's 10-point plan for ending the Arab-Israeli conflict is important for promoting the Middle East peace process.

Vance made the remarks at a press conference after meeting with Mubarak. He said that without this plan, it would be difficult to make any progress in the Middle East peace process.

He expressed the hope that the Arab-Israeli dialogue would succeed and emphasized the importance of direct dialogue between the conflicting parties.

### 万斯赞扬埃和平计划

1989-12-19
第4版(国际新闻)
专栏：

    万斯赞扬埃和平计划
    新华社开罗12月16日电　正在埃及访问的美国前国务卿赛勒斯·万斯今天在这里说，埃及总统穆巴拉克为结束阿以冲突提出的10点计划，对推动中东和平进程是重要的。
    万斯在同穆巴拉克会见后举行的记者招待会上说，没有这项计划，要想在中东和平进程方面取得任何进展是困难的。
    他希望阿以对话取得成功，并强调了冲突双方进行直接对话的重要性。
