Title: China International Trust and Investment Corporation Hong Kong Company Opens Representative Office in London
Author: Chen Te'an
Date: 1989-12-07
Page: 4

People's Daily, London, December 6th - Reporter Chen Te'an: The Hong Kong branch of China International Trust and Investment Corporation's Chiyu Bank Limited officially opened its representative office in the City of London yesterday. Over 180 people, including the Deputy Governor of the Bank of England and other financial figures from China and abroad, attended the opening ceremony.

### 中信集团香港公司在伦敦开设代表处
陈特安
1989-12-07
第4版(国际新闻)
专栏：

    中信集团香港公司在伦敦开设代表处
    本报伦敦12月6日电　记者陈特安报道：中信集团香港嘉华银行有限公司昨天正式在伦敦金融城开设代表处。英国中央银行常务董事以及其他中外金融界人士180多人出席了开业仪式。
