Title: State Council Appoints and Dismisses National Officials
Author:
Date: 1989-12-29
Edition: 2

The State Council appointed and dismissed a batch of national officials from December 2 to December 19, 1989.

Guishen Tan was appointed as Vice Chairman of the State Planning Commission, Zhang Youcai as Vice Minister of Finance, Zhao Zongrui as Vice Minister of the Ministry of Personnel, Shi Xiyu as Vice Minister of the Ministry of Railways, and Liu Shucui as Director of the State Council's Office of Foreign Affairs.

Qian Yongnian was relieved of his position as Director of the State Council's Office of Foreign Affairs.

### 国务院任免国家工作人员

1989-12-29
第2版(国内新闻)
专栏：

    国务院任免国家工作人员
    新华社北京12月28日电　国务院1989年12月2日至19日任免一批国家工作人员。
    任命桂世镛为国家计划委员会副主任，张佑才为财政部副部长，赵宗鼐兼任人事部副部长，石希玉为铁道部副部长，刘述卿兼任国务院外事办公室主任。
    免去钱永年的国务院外事办公室主任职务。
