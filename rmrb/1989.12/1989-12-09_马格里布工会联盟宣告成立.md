Title: The Maghreb Trade Union Confederation Announces Its Establishment
Author:
Date: 1989-12-09
Edition: 4

The founding conference of the Maghreb Trade Union Confederation was held in Casablanca, Morocco, from December 6 to 7. The conference elected Sadiq, the General Secretary of the Moroccan Workers' Union, as the Chairman of the Maghreb Trade Union Confederation, and determined that the capital of Tunisia would be the location of the Confederation's headquarters.

The five member countries of the Confederation—Morocco, Tunisia, Mauritania, Libya, and Algeria—along with more than fifty foreign trade union delegations, including a Chinese trade union delegation led by Xu Hongfu, Deputy Director of the International Liaison Department of the All-China Federation of Trade Unions, attended the conference.

### 马格里布工会联盟宣告成立

1989-12-09
第4版(国际新闻)
专栏：

    马格里布工会联盟宣告成立
    马格里布工会联盟成立大会十二月六日至七日在摩洛哥卡萨布兰卡举行。会议选举摩洛哥工人联合会总书记塞迪克为马格里布工会联盟主席，并确定突尼斯首都为该联盟会址。
    该联盟的五个成员国：摩洛哥、突尼斯、毛里塔尼亚、利比亚、阿尔及利亚和五十多个外国工会代表团以及由中华全国总工会国际联络部副部长徐洪福率领的中国工会代表团出席了会议。
