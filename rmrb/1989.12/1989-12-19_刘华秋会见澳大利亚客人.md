Title: Liu Huaqiu Meets with Australian Guests
Author:
Date: 1989-12-19
Edition: 2

Vice Foreign Minister Liu Huaqiu held consultations today with Michael Costello, Deputy Secretary of Australia's Department of Foreign Affairs and Trade, on major international issues of common concern and bilateral relations. The consultations were conducted in a friendly and frank atmosphere.

Before the consultations, Liu Huaqiu hosted a luncheon for the Australian guests.

Australian Ambassador to China, Stephen S. FitzGerald, attended the consultations and the luncheon.

The Costello delegation arrived in Beijing on the evening of the 17th.

### 刘华秋会见澳大利亚客人

1989-12-19
第2版(国内新闻)
专栏：

    刘华秋会见澳大利亚客人
    新华社北京12月18日电　外交部副部长刘华秋今天下午同澳大利亚外交贸易部副秘书长迈克尔·科斯特洛就共同关心的重大国际问题和双边关系举行了磋商。磋商是在友好和坦率的气氛中进行的。
    磋商前，刘华秋设午宴招待澳大利亚客人。
    澳大利亚驻华大使沙德伟参加了磋商和宴会。
    科斯特洛一行是17日晚抵达北京的。
