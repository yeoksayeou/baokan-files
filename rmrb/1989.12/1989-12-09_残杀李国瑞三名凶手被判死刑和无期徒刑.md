Title: Three Murderers of Li Guorui Sentenced to Death and Life Imprisonment

Author:

Date: 1989-12-09

Page: 3

Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, December 8th.

The three murderers who killed the national hero Li Guorui during the counter-revolutionary riot in Beijing have been sentenced to death and life imprisonment. Meng Duo and Zhou Jiguo were sentenced to death and deprivation of political rights for life, while Chen Yong was sentenced to life imprisonment and deprivation of political rights for life.

This is the first-instance verdict handed down by the Beijing Municipal Intermediate People's Court.

The Beijing Municipal Intermediate People's Court found that on the morning of June 4th, unemployed individual Meng Duo, worker at the Huihui Restaurant Zhou Jiguo, and worker at the Tangshan Electric Construction Company Chen Yong, along with other rioters, attacked and beat the soldier Li Guorui, who was on duty enforcing martial law. The three murderers used clubs, shovels, and other weapons to brutally beat Li Guorui on his body, head, and back, killing him on the spot.

### 残杀李国瑞三名凶手被判死刑和无期徒刑

1989-12-09
第3版(综合)
专栏：

    残杀李国瑞三名凶手被判死刑和无期徒刑
    据新华社北京12月8日电　在北京反革命暴乱中残杀共和国卫士李国瑞烈士的凶手孟多、周继国今天被依法判处死刑，剥夺政治权利终身；另一名凶手陈勇被依法判处无期徒刑，剥夺政治权利终身。
    这是北京市中级人民法院作出的一审判决。
    北京市中级人民法院审理查明：今年6月4日晨5时许，无业人员孟多、荟萃餐厅工人周继国、唐山陡电中建二局一公司工人陈勇，窜至已实行戒严的阜成门立交桥附近，伙同其他暴徒围攻毒打奉命执行戒严任务的武警北京总队二支队一大队战士李国瑞。这3名凶手分别用木棍、铁锹等凶器，朝李国瑞的身上和头部及背部等猛打狠砸，当场将李国瑞毒打致死。
