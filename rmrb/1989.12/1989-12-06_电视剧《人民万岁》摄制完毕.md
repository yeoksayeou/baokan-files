Title: The Television Drama "Long Live the People" Completed
Author: Zhao Xiangru
Date: 1989-12-06
Page: 3

The television drama "Long Live the People," which sings the praises of the deep bond between the people and the army and promotes the spirit of loyalty to the nation, has been completed. It was recently screened in Nanchang and other places, receiving positive feedback from relevant individuals.

### 电视剧《人民万岁》摄制完毕
赵相如
1989-12-06
第3版(综合)
专栏：

    电视剧《人民万岁》摄制完毕
    本报讯　记者赵相如报道：讴歌人民与军队鱼水深情，宣扬精忠报国的民族精神的电视剧《人民万岁》已经摄制完毕，最近在南昌等地试映，受到有关人士的好评。
