Title: Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference Delegation Visits Yemen
Author:
Date: 1989-12-06
Edition: 4

The Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference delegation concluded a five-day visit to the Republic of Yemen today and departed for Damascus to continue their friendly visit to Syria. During the visit, Han Kehua, a member of the Standing Committee of the National Committee of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference and the delegation leader, held talks with Hussein Abdullah Magdami, a member of the Yemeni Consultative Council.

### 我政协代表团访问阿拉伯也门

1989-12-06
第4版(国际新闻)
专栏：

    我政协代表团访问阿拉伯也门
    新华社萨那12月3日电　中国人民政治协商会议代表团对阿拉伯也门共和国进行了5天访问以后今天离开这里前往大马士革，继续对叙利亚进行友好访问。访问期间，全国政协常务委员会委员、代表团团长韩克华同阿拉伯也门协商议会议员侯赛因·阿卜杜拉·马格达米举行了会谈。
