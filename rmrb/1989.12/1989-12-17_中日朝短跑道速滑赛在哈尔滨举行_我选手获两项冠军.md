Title: China, Japan, and North Korea Short Track Speed Skating Competition Held in Harbin
Author: Jǐ Yúnchéng
Date: 1989-12-17
Edition: 3

In the 500-meter final of the China-Japan-North Korea short track speed skating friendly competition held this afternoon, Chinese speed skating star Li Yan won the women's title, and Japanese athlete Akasaka Yuichi took the men's first place.

Li Yan's time was 50.07 seconds. She, along with two other Chinese athletes—Gao Tian from the Heilongjiang Provincial Team and Li Changxiang from the National Team, secured the top three spots in this event.

In the 1000-meter final held this afternoon, Zhang Yanmei from the Chinese National Team won the women's title with a time of 1 minute 44.86 seconds.

Japanese athlete Wahei Koji secured the men's first place with a time of 1 minute 41.70 seconds.

### 中日朝短跑道速滑赛在哈尔滨举行  我选手获两项冠军
姬云程
1989-12-17
第3版(综合)
专栏：体育

    中日朝短跑道速滑赛在哈尔滨举行
    我选手获两项冠军
    新华社哈尔滨12月15日电  （记者姬云程）在今天下午进行的中日朝短跑道速滑友谊赛500米决赛中，中国速滑名将李琰获得女子组冠军，日本选手赤坂雄一获男子组第一名。
    李琰的成绩是50秒07。她与另外两名中国选手——黑龙江一队的高天和国家一队的李长香，囊括这项前三名。
    新华社哈尔滨12月16日电（记者姬云程）今天下午在这里进行的中、日、朝短跑道速滑友谊赛1000米决赛中，中国国家一队的张艳梅以1分44秒86的成绩获得女子组冠军。
    日本选手和合季信取得男子组第一名，成绩是1分41秒70。
