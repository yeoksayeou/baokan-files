Title: Beijing's Foreign Trade Completes Annual Plan Two Months Ahead of Schedule
Author: Lai Renqiong
Date: 1989-12-18
Page: 1

Beijing's foreign trade exports for the first ten months of the year reached $928 million, completing the state's export plan two months ahead of schedule. Additionally, 159 new foreign-invested enterprises were approved, attracting $78.35 million in foreign investment. Among these, 16 wholly foreign-owned enterprises were approved, marking a 420% increase compared to the same period last year.

### 北京外贸提前两月完成计划
赖仁琼
1989-12-18
第1版(要闻)
专栏：

    北京外贸提前两月完成计划
    本报北京十二月十七日讯　记者赖仁琼报道：一至十月北京外贸出口九点二八亿美元，提前两个月完成国家出口计划。新批外商投资企业一百五十九家，吸收外资七千八百三十五万美元。新批独资企业十六家，比去年同期增长四点二倍。
