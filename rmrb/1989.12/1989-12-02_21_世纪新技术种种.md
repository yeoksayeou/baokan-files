Title: Various New Technologies of the 21st Century
Author: Cheng Zhang
Date: 1989-12-02
Page: 7

The Japanese newspaper *Tokyo Shimbun* recently published an article stating that in the 21st century, humanity will face the challenge of simultaneously achieving development and environmental protection. Building on the scientific, technological, and production advancements of this century, the next century will see a series of new technologies that seem like myths today, all aimed at achieving these two major goals.

In the realm of the environment:

1.	Biotechnology will play a significant role in environmental purification and soil improvement.
2.	To green the deserts, projects such as artificial lakes and man-made mountain ranges will be promoted.
3.	The use of superconducting materials will double energy efficiency, reducing pollution.

In the realm of production:

1.	High technology will industrialize agriculture, animal husbandry, and aquaculture.
2.	The practical application of high polymer membranes and biotechnology will revolutionize refining, separation, compounding, and synthesis processes, transitioning the petrochemical system to a biochemical system.
3.	The emergence of new synthetic materials will enable the development of artificial organ materials that do not cause rejection reactions.
4.	The application of light will advance further. Lasers may become a propulsion force for rockets.
5.	The technology of smelting iron with nuclear energy will be widely applied.

In the realm of energy:

1.	Nuclear fusion reactions will be achieved. Nuclear energy use, including nuclear waste treatment, will be absolutely safe.
2.	The utilization of nuclear energy, bioenergy, wind energy, solar energy, and other environmentally friendly energy sources will significantly increase.
3.	Humans will be able to control typhoons and manage and utilize typhoon energy.
4.	Superconducting materials can be used to create "electric cans," making electricity a convenient commodity for transportation, storage, and trade.

In the realm of daily life:

1.	Developed countries will enter an era where people live to be 100 years old. Significant progress will be made in the treatment of cancer, AIDS, and other diseases.
2.	Translation phones and translation machines will become widely used.
3.	Banking and securities businesses will be fully electronic. Electronic newspapers will enter the practical stage. The development of electronic shopping will increase the proportion of shopless sales.
4.	Space travel will become possible. People will widely use engine-less electromagnetic ships and electromagnetic cars. Non-polluting cars using solar and nuclear energy will become popular.
5.	Household electrical appliances will use light as their energy source. Fibers that do not require washing and food preservatives will appear, rendering washing machines and refrigerators obsolete.

### 21世纪新技术种种
成章
1989-12-02
第7版(国际专页)
专栏：

    21世纪新技术种种
    日本《东京新闻》最近发表一篇文章认为，21世纪人类将面临同时实现发展和环保这两大目的的各种挑战。在本世纪科技、生产发展的基础上，下世纪在实现这两大目的中将出现一系列今天看来如同神话般的新技术。
    在环境方面：
    1、生物工程将在净化环境、改良土壤等方面发挥作用。
    2、为绿化沙漠，将推进人工湖和人造山脉等工程。
    3、利用超导材料将使能源利用率倍增，减少污染。
    在生产方面：
    1、利用高技术将使农业、畜牧业、水产业生产工业化。
    2、高分子膜和生物工程的实用化将改变精炼、分离、化合、合成等工艺，将石油化学体系转变成生物化学体系。
    3、新合成材料的出现将使人们可能研制出不会引起排斥反应的人工脏器材料。
    4、光的应用将进一步发展。激光可能成为火箭推动力。
    5、用核能炼铁的技术将得到广泛应用。
    在能源方面：
    1、将实现核聚合反应。包括核废料处理等各方面，核能的使用将绝对安全。
    2、核能、生物能源、风能、太阳能等对地球环境无害的能源利用将大大增加。
    3、人类将可以控制台风，实现对台风能源的管理和利用。
    4、利用超导材料可制成“电罐头”，使电可以成为便于运输、储存、贸易的商品。
    在生活方面：
    1、发达国家将进入人生百岁时代。癌症、艾滋病及其他病症的治疗将取得长足进步。
    2、将出现翻译电话。翻译机将被广泛应用。
    3、银行、证券业务将实现电子化。电子报纸将进入实用化阶段。电子购物的发展将使无店铺贩卖比例扩大。
    4、宇宙旅行将成为可能。人们将广泛使用无引擎的电磁船和电磁汽车。使用太阳能和核能的无公害汽车将得到普及。
    5、家庭内的电化制品将以光为能源。将出现无须洗涤的纤维和食品保存剂，从而洗衣机、电冰箱的作用将被取代。
