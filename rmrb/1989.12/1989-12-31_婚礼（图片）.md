    Wedding Ceremony
    (Photographed by Yuán Xuéjūn, published in the 12th issue of 1989's "News Front")

    This photograph captures a moment of joy and celebration, showcasing the traditional and cultural significance of a wedding ceremony. The image, taken by Yuán Xuéjūn, beautifully encapsulates the essence of the event, highlighting the customs and rituals that are deeply rooted in Chinese heritage. The details in the photograph, from the attire of the bride and groom to the decorations and the expressions of the attendees, all contribute to the rich tapestry of this significant life event. The photograph serves as a timeless record of the love and commitment shared between the couple, as well as the communal support and blessings from their family and friends.
