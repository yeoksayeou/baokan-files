Title: The Second Plenary Session of the 10th Central Committee of the China Democratic League of Self-Employment and Small Business Concludes
Author:
Date: 1989-12-18
Page: 2

Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, December 17th.

The second plenary session of the 10th Central Committee of the China Democratic League of Self-Employment and Small Business concluded this afternoon in Beijing. In his closing remarks, Vice Chairman Fang Rongxin of the China Democratic League of Self-Employment and Small Business stated that the main theme of this meeting was to maintain stability, boost morale, and work hard. All levels of the party's organizations and party members must fully recognize their historical mission and responsibilities, with a strong sense of revolutionary duty and responsibility, overcome difficulties, and make selfless contributions to strive for the victory of rectification and deepening of reforms.

The meeting unanimously adopted a resolution. The resolution pointed out that history has proven that only socialism can save and develop China, and only the Communist Party can lead the Chinese people on the socialist path. The China Democratic League of Self-Employment and Small Business has a revolutionary tradition of patriotism, democracy, and adherence to socialism. In the future, no matter what storms and difficulties we encounter on the road ahead, we will stand firmly and clearly accept the leadership of the Communist Party of China and adhere to the socialist path.

The meeting called on all levels of the China Democratic League of Self-Employment and Small Business and its members to further study and implement the spirit of the Fourth and Fifth Plenary Sessions of the 13th Central Committee of the Communist Party of China, actively participate in political consultations, and give full play to the characteristics and advantages of the China Democratic League of Self-Employment and Small Business. Conduct investigations and research, propose opinions and suggestions, and assist the Communist Party of China and the people's government in their work. Continue to face society, carry out various forms of consultation services, and strengthen overseas liaison work. At the same time, strengthen the party's own construction. Chairman Lu Jiasi presided over the meeting.

### 农工民主党十届二中全会闭幕  会议主旨是稳定、鼓劲、出力

1989-12-18
第2版(国内新闻)
专栏：

    农工民主党十届二中全会闭幕
    会议主旨是稳定、鼓劲、出力
    新华社北京12月17日电中国农工民主党第十届中央委员会第二次全体会议今天下午在京闭幕。农工民主党副主席方荣欣在闭幕词中说，这次会议的主旨是稳定、鼓劲、出力，全党各级组织和党员要充分认识自己的历史使命和职责，以强烈的革命事业心和责任感，克服困难，无私奉献，为争取治理整顿和深化改革的胜利而奋斗。
    会议一致通过决议。决议指出，历史已雄辩地证明，只有社会主义才能救中国和发展中国，只有共产党才能领导中国人民走社会主义道路。农工民主党具有爱国、民主、坚持社会主义的革命传统，今后在前进的道路上，不论遇到什么风浪和困难，都将立场坚定，旗帜鲜明地接受中国共产党的领导，坚持走社会主义道路。
    会议要求农工民主党各级组织和党员进一步深入学习贯彻中共十三届四中、五中全会精神，积极参政议政，发挥农工民主党的特点和优势，开展调查研究，提出意见和建议，协助中国共产党和人民政府做好工作。要继续面向社会，开展多种形式的咨询服务，大力加强海外联谊工作，同时，要切实加强自身建设。农工民主党主席卢嘉锡主持了会议。
