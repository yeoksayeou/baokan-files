Title: Grenada Prime Minister Bligh Passes Away
Author:
Date: 1989-12-21
Edition: 4

Grenada Prime Minister Herbert Bligh passed away on December 19th at the age of 71.

The official cause of Bligh's death has not been released by Grenada. Reports indicate that Bligh had been suffering from prostate cancer for several years.

Bligh was elected Prime Minister in December 1984, following the U.S. military invasion of Grenada in 1983.

### 格林纳达总理布莱兹逝世

1989-12-21
第4版(国际新闻)
专栏：

    格林纳达总理布莱兹逝世
    据加勒比通讯社报道，格林纳达总理赫伯特·布莱兹于十九日病逝，终年七十一岁。
  格林纳达官方未公布布莱兹的死因。据报道，布莱兹患前列腺癌已有多年。
    布莱兹是在一九八三年美国武装入侵格林纳达后于一九八四年十二月三日当选为总理的。
