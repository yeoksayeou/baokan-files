Title: Soviet People's Congress Concludes, Condemns Invasion of Afghanistan
Author: Tang Xiuzhe
Date: 1989-12-27
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, December 24th.

The second Soviet People's Congress concluded this afternoon in the Kremlin. The congress passed a message to the nation stating that ethnic conflicts have caused "anxiety and pain," pushing Soviet society to the "brink of danger." It called for an immediate halt to these conflicts.

In his closing remarks, Soviet Supreme Soviet Chairman Gorbachev emphasized the need to strengthen discipline and order, warning that political activism should not devolve into anarchism.

The congress also made a political assessment of the Soviet Union's 1979 invasion of Afghanistan, stating that the decision by then-leaders Brezhnev, Ustinov, Andropov, and Gromyko to invade should be "morally and politically condemned."

The second Soviet People's Congress opened on December 12th. The meeting discussed and approved the government's measures for the revitalization of the Soviet economy, steps for economic reform, and the principles for drafting the 13th Five-Year Plan (1991-1995).

This congress also amended parts of the Soviet Constitution related to the electoral system.

### 苏联人代会闭幕  认为出兵阿富汗应受到谴责
唐修哲
1989-12-27
第4版(国际新闻)
专栏：

    苏联人代会闭幕
    认为出兵阿富汗应受到谴责
    新华社莫斯科12月24日电　（记者唐修哲）第二次苏联人民代表大会24日下午在克里姆林宫闭幕。代表大会通过的告全国公民书指出，民族冲突引起了人们的“不安和痛苦”，使苏联社会走到了“危险的边缘”，因此应该立即停止。
    苏联最高苏维埃主席戈尔巴乔夫在致闭幕词时强调，要加强纪律和秩序，政治积极性不应变成无政府主义。
    代表大会还对1979年12月苏联出兵阿富汗作出了政治评价，认为当时苏联的主要领导人勃列日涅夫、乌斯季诺夫、安德罗波夫和葛罗米柯作出的出兵阿富汗的决定应受到“道义上和政治上的谴责”。
    第二次苏联人民代表大会是12月12日开幕的。会议讨论并通过了政府提出的关于苏联经济健康化的措施、经济改革的步骤和制订第13个5年计划（1991—1995）的原则纲要。
    本次代表大会对苏联宪法有关选举制度的部分进行了修改。
