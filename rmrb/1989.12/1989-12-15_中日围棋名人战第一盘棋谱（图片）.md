Title: The First Game of the China-Japan Go Championship
Author:
Date: 1989-12-15
Edition: 3

The first game of the China-Japan Go Championship was played on December 15, 1989. The black player was Ma Xiaochun, who used 2 hours and 57 minutes. The white player was Kobayashi Koichi, who used 2 hours and 55 minutes. The total number of moves was 274.
