Title: Iranian Military Conducts Drills in the Gulf
Author:
Date: 1989-12-11
Page: 4

Iranian regular military forces and the Islamic Revolutionary Guards Corps conducted a joint military exercise in the Gulf and the Sea of Oman on December 9th.

This is the first time since the establishment of the Islamic Republic of Iran in 1979 that the regular military and the Revolutionary Guards have conducted a joint military exercise. Participants included the air force, marine corps, and frogmen, making it the largest-scale exercise in a decade.

The report stated that the exercise will last for one week, aiming to enhance the combat capabilities of the armed forces and promote coordination between the regular military and the Revolutionary Guards in times of war.

### 伊朗军队在海湾举行演习

1989-12-11
第4版(国际新闻)
专栏：

    伊朗军队在海湾举行演习
    据德黑兰电台报道，伊朗正规军和伊斯兰革命卫队九日在海湾至阿曼海一带举行联合军事演习。
    这是自一九七九年伊朗伊斯兰共和国成立以来，正规军与革命卫队第一次举行联合军事演习。参加人员包括空军、海军陆战队和蛙人，是十年来规模最大的一次演习。
    报道说，这次演习为期一周，目的是提高武装部队的战斗能力，促进正规军和革命卫队战时协调。
