Title: Hainan Special Economic Zone Opens International Air Cargo Routes
Author:
Date: 1989-12-12
Edition: 2

Hainan Special Economic Zone has recently opened direct international air cargo routes to Hong Kong and, via Hong Kong, to various countries around the world. Through this route, goods from Hainan can reach Hong Kong in one day and be transshipped to various parts of the world the same day. This changes the long-standing situation where air cargo from Hainan had to be transshipped via Guangzhou.

### 海南特区已开通国际航空货运线

1989-12-12
第2版(国内新闻)
专栏：四面八方

    海南特区已开通国际航空货运线
    新华社海口12月9日电  海南特区近日开通直达香港及通过香港中转到世界各国的国际航空货运线。通过这条货运线，海南的货物一天可到香港，当天可转运世界各地。它改变了海南岛长期以来航空货物出口经广州转口的局面。
