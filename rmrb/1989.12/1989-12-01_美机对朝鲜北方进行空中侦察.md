Title: U.S. Aircraft Conducts Aerial Reconnaissance Over North Korea
Author:
Date: 1989-12-01
Edition: 4

The North Korean Central News Agency, citing a well-informed source from the military, reported that on the 29th, the U.S. military dispatched a U-2 type high-altitude strategic reconnaissance aircraft to conduct reconnaissance activities over North Korea.

At around 7 AM on the 29th, this reconnaissance aircraft flew back and forth multiple times from the western coastal area along the military demarcation line to the eastern coastal area, conducting reconnaissance over a wide and deep area of North Korea.

### 美机对朝鲜北方进行空中侦察

1989-12-01
第4版(国际新闻)
专栏：

    美机对朝鲜北方进行空中侦察
    据朝鲜中央通讯社引用军方消息灵通人士的话报道，美军二十九日派遣U—2型高空战略侦察机对朝鲜北方进行了侦察活动。
    二十九日晨七时前后，这架侦察机自西部海岸上空沿军事分界线至东部海岸上空多次往复飞行，侦察了朝鲜北方的广阔地区和纵深地区。
