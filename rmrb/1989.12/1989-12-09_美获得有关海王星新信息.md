Title: The United States Obtains New Information About Neptune
Author:
Date: 1989-12-09
Edition: 4

The United States National Aeronautics and Space Administration announced that they recently obtained photographs from "Voyager 2" which revealed that Triton, a moon of Neptune, has at least four active ice volcanoes. Neptune itself is being struck by winds reaching speeds of 2400 kilometers per hour.

According to the Associated Press, scientists at the U.S. space agency, Torrence Johnson, pointed out that the photographs transmitted back show at least two ice volcanoes erupting on Triton. Since the photographs only show part of Triton, it is estimated that there may be more active ice volcanoes yet to be discovered.

The U.S. "Voyager 2" space probe was launched on August 20, 1977. It flew by Neptune and Triton at the closest distance on August 24 this year, discovering for the first time an active volcano on Triton and transmitting information back to Earth about the volcano ejecting solid nitrogen and nitrogen gas to a height of 8 kilometers. Prior to this, astronomers had only discovered active volcanoes on Earth and Io, a moon of Jupiter, with the ejected materials primarily being sulfur.

Analysis of the photographs transmitted by "Voyager 2" shows that the wind speed on Neptune reaches 2400 kilometers per hour, making it the fastest wind speed discovered so far in the solar system. The giant winds carry enormous dark spots.

### 美获得有关海王星新信息

1989-12-09
第4版(国际新闻)
专栏：

    美获得有关海王星新信息
    新华社北京12月8日电　美国宇航局宣布，他们最近从“旅行者2”号发回的照片中发现，海卫一上至少有四个活冰山，海王星本身正在遭受时速2400公里巨风的袭击。
    据美联社报道，美国宇航局的科学家托伦斯·约翰逊指出，从传回的照片中至少可以看出海卫一上有两座冰火山正在喷发；由于照片只显示了海卫一的一部分，估计还可能有更多的活冰火山尚待发现。
    美国“旅行者2”号太空探测器是1977年8月20日发射的。它于今年8月24日以最近的距离掠过海王星和海卫一时，第一次发现了海卫一上的一座活火山，并向地面传回了火山喷发出高达8公里的固态氮和氮气的信息。在此之前，天文学家仅在地球上和木卫一上发现活动的活火山，且喷发物中主要是硫。
    对“旅行者2”号传回的照片分析，海王星上的风速高达每小时2400公里，这是迄今在太阳系内发现的速度最快的风。巨风中卷带着巨大的黑斑。
