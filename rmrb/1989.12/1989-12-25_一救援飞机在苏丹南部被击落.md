Title: A Relief Plane Shot Down in Southern Sudan
Author:
Date: 1989-12-25
Edition: 4

A relief plane was shot down in southern Sudan.

According to reports from Khartoum, the Sudanese government and the rebel forces have accused each other of shooting down a plane engaged in relief work in southern Sudan. It is reported that the twin-engine plane, which can carry eight people, was shot down on December 21 while completing a relief mission. Four passengers on board were killed. United Nations officials said that among the four victims were three French nationals and one Sudanese relief worker.

### 一救援飞机在苏丹南部被击落

1989-12-25
第4版(国际新闻)
专栏：

    一救援飞机在苏丹南部被击落
    据来自喀土穆的消息，苏丹政府和反政府武装相互指责对方在苏丹南部击落一架负责救济工作的飞机。据报道，这架可乘八人的双引擎飞机是二十一日在完成一次救济任务时被击落的，机上四名乘客丧生。联合国人士说，这四名遇难者中有三名法国人和一名苏丹救援人员。
