Title: East and West Germany Reach Visa Agreement
Author:
Date: 1989-12-09
Edition: 4

East and West Germany Reach Visa Agreement

New China News Agency, Bonn, December 5th - West Germany and East Germany reached an agreement in Berlin on December 5th regarding West German citizens traveling to East Germany without needing a visa. According to the agreement reached by East German Council of Ministers Chairman Modrow and West German Chancellor's Office Minister Seiters, West German citizens will, for the first time in 40 years, be able to visit and travel in East Germany without a visa.

### 两德达成签证协议

1989-12-09
第4版(国际新闻)
专栏：

    两德达成签证协议
    新华社波恩12月5日电　联邦德国和民主德国5日在柏林就联邦德国公民前往民主德国旅行无需办理签证一事达成协议。根据民主德国部长会议主席莫德罗和联邦德国总理府部长赛特斯达成的这一协议，联邦德国公民40年来将第一次无需签证就可以前往民主德国访问和旅行。
