Title: The Burmese Military to Attack the Karen Ethnic Anti-Government Forces
Author:
Date: 1989-12-28
Page: 4

According to reports from Bangkok newspapers on the 27th, the Burmese military will soon launch a military attack on two camps of the Karen ethnic anti-government forces in the Burmese territory bordering Thailand's Mae Sot district.

The Burmese military has notified Thai border police that Burmese troops will take military action in the Burmese-Thai border area, and therefore requests that 200 Thai workers logging in the vicinity quickly withdraw.

### 缅军将进攻克伦族反政府武装

1989-12-28
第4版(国际新闻)
专栏：

    缅军将进攻克伦族反政府武装
    据曼谷报纸二十七日报道，缅甸军方近日将在与泰国湄索县交界的缅甸境内向克伦族反政府武装的两个营地发动军事进攻。
    缅甸军方已通知泰国边防警察，缅军将在缅泰边境地区采取军事行动，因此要求在附近伐木的二百名泰国工人迅速撤离。
