Author:
Date: 1989-12-29
Edition: 4

Romania Requests United Nations Assistance

	New York, Dec. 27 (Xinhua) — Romania today requested the United Nations to consider providing emergency humanitarian aid.

	This request was formally made by Romania's Ambassador to the United Nations, Petre Tanasie, in a letter to UN Secretary-General Javier Perez de Cuellar. In the letter, Tanasie proposed that the current session of the General Assembly include an agenda item on providing emergency humanitarian aid to Romania.

	Tanasie stated that due to recent events, Romania is in a difficult situation and urgently needs the assistance of all countries, the international community, and the United Nations.

### 罗马尼亚要求联合国提供援助

1989-12-29
第4版(国际新闻)
专栏：

    罗马尼亚要求联合国提供援助
    新华社联合国12月27日电　罗马尼亚今天要求联合国考虑提供紧急人道主义援助。
    这一要求是罗马尼亚驻联合国大使彼得·特纳谢在给联合国秘书长佩雷斯·德奎利亚尔的一封信中正式提出的。特纳谢在信中要求本届联大增加一项关于向罗马尼亚提供紧急人道主义援助的议案。
    特纳谢说，由于最近发生的事件，罗马尼亚陷入了困难的境地，迫切需要所有国家、国际社会和联合国的援助。
