Title: Foreign Affairs Briefing
Author:
Date: 1989-12-17
Edition: 2

	Member of the Political Bureau of the CPC Central Committee and Vice Premier of the State Council Wu Xueqian held a friendly talk with a delegation led by Wlodzimierz Natolf, a member of the Central Committee of the Polish United Workers' Party, in the afternoon of December 15. Both sides exchanged views on issues of common concern, including domestic and international affairs and the development of friendly relations between the two parties. The delegation, invited by the Communist Party of China, arrived in Beijing on December 12 for a friendly visit. Zhu Liang, a member of the Central Committee of the CPC and head of the International Liaison Department, held talks with Natolf. The delegation is scheduled to return home on December 16.

	State Councilor and Minister of the Ministry of Machine Building and Electronics Industry Zhou Jiahuai met with a delegation led by Okada Seiichi, vice president of the Japanese company Kenmoku Corporation, at the Diaoyutai State Guesthouse on December 15.

	Zhou Jiahuai welcomed and congratulated the delegation on their participation in the opening ceremony of the Beijing branch of Kenmoku Corporation. He said that on the basis of equality and mutual benefit, relevant companies of China and Japan have the possibility of better cooperation. Okada Seiichi said he was pleased to be able to contribute to China's economic development. China is a very large market.

	Zhou Jiahuai and Okada Seiichi also exchanged views on production and technological cooperation between China and Kenmoku Corporation in the fields of communication technology, audio equipment, and testing instruments.

	State Councilor and Minister of the State Science and Technology Commission Song Jian met with a delegation led by Deputy Chairman Ashinov of the Bulgarian National Committee for Science and Higher Education at the Great Hall of the People on December 16. The Bulgarian delegation was invited by the National Natural Science Foundation of China for a visit. During the visit, the two sides will sign a memorandum of understanding between the two countries' science funds and discuss the implementation plan for bilateral scientific cooperation projects in 1990.

### 外事简讯

1989-12-17
第2版(国内新闻)
专栏：

    外事简讯
    ▲中共中央政治局委员、国务院副总理吴学谦15日下午会见了波兰统一工人党中央书记弗沃吉米日·纳托尔夫一行，同他们进行了热情友好的谈话。双方就国内国际和发展中波两党友好关系等共同关心的问题交换了意见。纳托尔夫一行是应中国共产党的邀请于12日抵京，来华进行友好访问的。中共中央委员、中联部部长朱良同纳托尔夫进行了会谈。纳托尔夫一行定于16日回国。
    ▲国务委员兼机械电子工业部部长邹家华15日在钓鱼台国宾馆会见了日本建伍株式会社副社长冈诚一行。
    邹家华对冈诚一行前来参加日本建伍株式会社北京事务所开业典礼表示欢迎和祝贺。他说，在平等互利的基础上中国与日本的有关公司完全有可能合作得更好。冈诚说，中国是个很大的市场，能为中国的经济发展作出贡献他感到高兴。
    邹家华和冈诚还就中国与建伍株式会社在通讯技术、音响设备和测试仪器等方面的生产和技术合作问题交换了意见。
    ▲国务委员兼国家科委主任宋健16日在人民大会堂会见了由副主任阿申诺夫率领的保加利亚国家科学与高等教育委员会代表团。保加利亚代表团应中国国家自然科学基金委员会的邀请前来访问。访问期间，将同中国续签两国科学基金组织之间的谅解备忘录并商定1990年度双方科学合作项目执行计划。
