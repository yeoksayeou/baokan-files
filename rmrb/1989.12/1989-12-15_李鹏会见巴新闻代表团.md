Title: Premier Li Peng Meets with Pakistani News Delegation
Author: Gù Yùqīng
Date: 1989-12-15
Edition: 1

Premier Li Peng Meets with Pakistani News Delegation

	Xinhua News Agency reporter Gù Yùqīng reports: Premier Li Peng met with a Pakistani news delegation led by A. S. Awan, Minister of State for Information and Broadcasting of Pakistan, this afternoon at the Zhongnanhai's Ziguang Pavilion.

	Li Peng said, "Just a month ago, I visited your country and received a warm and friendly welcome from the Pakistani people. I would like to express my gratitude once again for this. He said, "The friendship between China and Pakistan is built on a solid foundation. No matter how the world changes, the friendship between China and Pakistan will endure."

	Awan said, "Premier, your visit to Pakistan last month was very successful and left a deep impression on the Pakistani people, strengthening the already existing friendly cooperative relations between the two countries. He hoped that the news sectors of the two countries would further strengthen friendly exchanges and cooperation in the future."

	Premier Li Peng agreed with this view.

	Xinhua News Agency President Mù Qīng was present at the meeting.

### 李鹏会见巴新闻代表团
顾玉清
1989-12-15
第1版(要闻)
专栏：

    李鹏会见巴新闻代表团
    本报北京十二月十四日讯　记者顾玉清报道：国务院总理李鹏今天下午在紫光阁会见了由巴基斯坦新闻广播部国务部长艾·赛·阿旺率领的新闻代表团。
    李鹏说，一个月前，我访问了贵国，受到了巴基斯坦人民的热情、友好的接待。对此，我再次表示感谢。他说，中巴友好关系是有牢固基础的，不管世界风云如何变化，中巴友谊是长存的。
    阿旺说，总理阁下上月对巴基斯坦的访问非常成功，给巴基斯坦人民留下了深刻的印象，加强了巴中业已存在的友好合作关系。他希望两国新闻界今后要进一步加强友好交往与合作。
    李鹏总理对此表示赞同。
    新华社社长穆青会见时在座。
