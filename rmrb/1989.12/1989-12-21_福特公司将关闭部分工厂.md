Title: Ford to Close Some Factories
Author:
Date: 1989-12-21
Page: 4

Ford Motor Company of the United States announced on the 18th that due to sluggish car sales, the company will temporarily close thirteen of its seventeen assembly plants in the United States and Canada starting next January.

The other two of the three major American automobile companies are also forced to temporarily close some of their automobile assembly plants. General Motors has already closed twenty-eight of its thirty-four factories, while Chrysler will close four of its twelve plants.

### 福特公司将关闭部分工厂

1989-12-21
第4版(国际新闻)
专栏：

    福特公司将关闭部分工厂
    美国福特汽车公司十八日宣布，因汽车销售不景气，公司将于明年一月暂时关闭它在美国和加拿大十七家组装厂中的十三家。
    美国三大汽车公司中的另外两家也不得不暂时关闭部分汽车组装厂，其中通用公司已关闭三十四家工厂中的二十八家，克莱斯勒将关闭十二家工厂中的四家。
