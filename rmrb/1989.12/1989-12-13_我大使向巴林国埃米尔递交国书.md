Title: China's Ambassador Presents Credentials to the Emir of Bahrain
Author:
Date: 1989-12-13
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Kuwait, December 11th.

China's Ambassador to Kuwait and Special Envoy to Bahrain, Guan Zihuai, presented his credentials to the Emir of Bahrain, Isa bin Sulman Al Khalifa, this morning. Both sides engaged in friendly and cordial discussions.

The Emir stated that the establishment of diplomatic relations between Bahrain and China is a significant step worth celebrating. Bahrain is willing to develop friendly and cooperative relations with China in all areas.

China and Bahrain established diplomatic relations on April 18th of this year.

### 我大使向巴林国埃米尔递交国书

1989-12-13
第4版(国际新闻)
专栏：

    我大使向巴林国埃米尔递交国书
    新华社科威特12月11日电　中华人民共和国驻科威特兼驻巴林国特命全权大使管子怀今天上午向巴林国埃米尔伊萨·本·苏莱曼·哈利法递交了国书。双方进行了亲切、友好的交谈。
    埃米尔说，巴、中两国建交是值得庆贺的步骤，巴林愿意在各个方面同中国发展友好合作关系。
    中、巴两国是今年4月18日建交的。
