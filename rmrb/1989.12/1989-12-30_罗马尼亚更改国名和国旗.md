Title: Romania Changes Its Name and Flag
Author:
Date: 1989-12-30
Edition: 4

Romania Changes Its Name and Flag

Xinhua News Agency, Bucharest, December 28 - According to a report from the local television station, the Romanian National Salvation Front Council today officially announced the renaming of the Socialist Republic of Romania to Romania, retaining the republican system. The national flag will consist of blue, yellow, and red colors, removing the emblem from the original flag.

The National Salvation Front Council is currently the highest authority in Romania, consisting of 145 members, who pass resolutions and decrees by a simple majority. The council elects an executive bureau consisting of a chairman and vice-chairmen, totaling 11 people, to handle daily affairs.

The Romanian National Salvation Front Council also appointed two deputy prime ministers and several ministers today.

### 罗马尼亚更改国名和国旗

1989-12-30
第4版(国际新闻)
专栏：

    罗马尼亚更改国名和国旗
    新华社布加勒斯特12月28日电　据此间电视台报道，罗马尼亚救国阵线委员会今天正式宣布把罗马尼亚社会主义共和国改名为罗马尼亚，保留共和制。国旗由蓝、黄、红三色组成，去掉了原国旗中的国徽图案。
    救国阵线委员会是罗马尼亚目前的最高权力机构，由145名委员组成，以简单多数通过各项决议和法令。委员会选出由主席和副主席等11人组成的执行局，主持日常工作。
    罗救国阵线委员会今天还任命了两个政府副总理和一些部的部长。
