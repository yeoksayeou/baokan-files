Title: Italian Prime Minister Visits North and South Yemen
Author:
Date: 1989-12-28
Edition: 4

Italian Prime Minister Andreotti departed on the morning of the 27th by airplane for Sana'a, beginning a four-day official visit to the Arab Republic of Yemen and the People's Democratic Republic of Yemen. This is the first time that a leader of the Italian government has visited both Yemens.

It is understood that the situation in the Gulf, the Middle East, and the Horn of Africa will be the main topics of discussion between the Italian Prime Minister and the leaders of North and South Yemen. The two sides will also explore the possibility of Italian participation in the development cooperation of North and South Yemen and strengthening bilateral trade relations.

### 意大利总理出访南北也门

1989-12-28
第4版(国际新闻)
专栏：

    意大利总理出访南北也门
    意大利总理安德烈奥蒂二十七日上午乘飞机前往萨那，对阿拉伯也门共和国和也门民主人民共和国进行为期四天的正式访问。这是意大利政府首脑首次出访南北也门。
    据悉，海湾、中东和非洲之角的形势将是意大利总理与南北也门领导人会谈的主要议题。两国还将探讨意大利参加南北也门发展合作和加强双边贸易关系的可能性。
