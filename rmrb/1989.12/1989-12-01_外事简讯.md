Title: Foreign Affairs Brief
Author:
Date: 1989-12-01
Edition: 2

Guangdong Provincial Governor Ye Xuanping met with Norwegian Ambassador to China Holvik, Swedish Ambassador to China Skal and his wife, Danish Ambassador to China Ana Berling and his wife, and Finnish Chargé d'Affaires to China Buren on November 30 in Guangzhou. They had a friendly conversation.

Ambassador Holvik, speaking on behalf of the four ambassadors, said that he hoped their visit to Guangdong for inspection and exchange would further promote the already good cooperation between the four Nordic countries and Guangdong.

### 外事简讯

1989-12-01
第2版(国内新闻)
专栏：

    外事简讯
    ▲广东省长叶选平11月30日在广州会见了挪威驻华大使霍尔维克，瑞典驻华大使史凯乐和夫人，丹麦驻华大使阿纳·贝林和夫人，以及芬兰驻华使馆临时代办布里宁，并同他们进行了友好交谈。
    霍尔维克大使代表四国使节说，希望通过他们这次到广东进行考察访问，促进北欧四国同广东业已存在的良好合作关系进一步发展。
              （新华社）
