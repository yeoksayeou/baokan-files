Title: Li Peng Meets Pakistani Guests
Author: Zhū Yúnlóng
Date: 1989-12-27
Edition: 1

Li Peng Meets Pakistani Guests

New China News Agency, Beijing, December 26 (Reporter: Zhu Yunlong) - Premier Li Peng met with Pakistani Defense Minister Chima and his delegation this afternoon in the Zhongnanhai Purple Light Pavilion. Both hosts and guests highly praised the friendly cooperative relationship between China and Pakistan.

Minister Chima said that he brought the friendly sentiments of the Pakistani government and people to the Chinese government and people. The friendship between Pakistan and China is world-renowned. Premier Li Peng's recent visit to Pakistan greatly enhanced the friendly cooperative relationship between the two countries.

Li Peng said that the relationship between China and Pakistan is a model of friendly coexistence between countries with different social systems. No matter what changes occur internationally, the friendship between China and Pakistan has always been solid. He hopes that cooperation and exchanges between the two countries in all fields will be further strengthened.

Li Peng asked Minister Chima to convey to President Ishaq Khan and Prime Minister Benazir Bhutto upon his return: The current political and economic situation in China is stable.

Attending the meeting were relevant department heads such as He Qizong, Ding Henggao, and Zhang Lai.

### 李鹏会见巴基斯坦客人
朱云龙
1989-12-27
第1版(要闻)
专栏：

    李鹏会见巴基斯坦客人
    新华社北京12月26日电（记者朱云龙）国务院总理李鹏今天下午在中南海紫光阁会见了巴基斯坦国防国务部长契玛一行。宾主高度赞扬中巴之间的友好合作关系。
    契玛部长说，他带来了巴基斯坦政府和人民对中国政府和人民的友好情谊。巴中友谊是举世闻名的。李鹏总理不久前对巴基斯坦的访问大大增进了两国的友好合作关系。
    李鹏说，中巴关系堪称不同社会制度国家之间友好相处的楷模。不管国际上发生什么变化，中巴友谊一直是牢固的。他希望两国在各个领域的合作和交流进一步加强。
    李鹏请契玛部长回国后向伊沙克·汗总统和贝·布托总理转告：当前中国的政治、经济形势都是稳定的。
    参加会见的有何其宗、丁衡高、张来等有关部门负责人。
