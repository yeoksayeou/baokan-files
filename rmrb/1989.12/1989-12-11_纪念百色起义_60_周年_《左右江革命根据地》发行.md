Title: Commemorating the 60th Anniversary of the Baise Uprising
Author: Zhu Ming, Li Shiyuan
Date: 1989-12-11
Page: 3

Xinhua News Agency, Nanning, December 9th.

To commemorate the 60th anniversary of the Baise Uprising and the Longzhou Uprising led by comrades Deng Xiaoping, Zhang Yunyi, and others, the book "The Left and Right River Revolutionary Base Area" was released this afternoon in Nanning.

The book is divided into two volumes, totaling 1.1 million words. It includes 87 historical documents, biographies of 36 leaders of the Baise and Longzhou Uprisings, including Deng Xiaoping and Zhang Yunyi, and 48 reminiscences written by comrades such as Zhang Yunyi and Li Tianyou. It also includes a chronology and charts of major events in the Baise and Longzhou Uprisings and the Left and Right River Revolutionary Base Area.

### 纪念百色起义60周年  《左右江革命根据地》发行
朱明  李石元
1989-12-11
第3版(综合)
专栏：

    纪念百色起义60周年
    《左右江革命根据地》发行
    新华社南宁12月9日电　（记者朱明、李石元）为了纪念邓小平、张云逸等同志领导的百色起义、龙州起义60周年，《左右江革命根据地》一书今天下午在南宁首次发行。
    这部书分上、下两册，共110万字，书内收有历史文献87篇，以及百色起义、龙州起义领导人邓小平、张云逸等36位同志的传略，张云逸、李天佑等同志撰写的48篇回忆文章，并附有百色、龙州起义和左右江革命根据地大事记及图表。
