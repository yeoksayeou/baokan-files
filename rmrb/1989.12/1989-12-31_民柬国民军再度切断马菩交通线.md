Title: Khmer People's National Liberation Armed Forces Again Cut Off the Ma-Po Transportation Line
Author:
Date: 1989-12-31
Edition: 4

The Khmer People's National Liberation Armed Forces on December 26 launched a fierce attack on enemy strongholds along the railway and National Highway 5 between Battambang and Pursat, once again disrupting the enemy's supply lines.

The broadcast said that early on the 26th, the Khmer People's National Liberation Armed Forces captured three enemy battalion-level strongholds and two company-level strongholds along a 60-kilometer stretch of railway and highway between Mong District in Battambang Province and Pursat City. They also destroyed one 80-meter and one 20-meter long bridge, seized and destroyed 34 various weapons, thereby cutting off the supply line from Phnom Penh to the western battlefield.

According to the same radio station, from November 20 to December 20, the Cambodian resistance forces cleared all enemy strongholds set up by the Vietnamese and Phnom Penh puppet forces along an 80-kilometer stretch of National Highway 69 south of Phnom Voar. This included two regimental-level strongholds and 13 battalion-level strongholds. The enemy's long-prepared defensive line south of Phnom Voar was completely destroyed. In this month of fighting, the resistance forces killed more than 300 enemy soldiers and captured more than 200.

### 民柬国民军再度切断马菩交通线

1989-12-31
第4版(国际新闻)
专栏：

    民柬国民军再度切断马菩交通线
    新华社北京12月30日电  据民主柬埔寨电台今天广播，民柬国民军12月26日向马德望市到菩萨市之间的铁路和5号公路沿线的敌军据点群发起猛攻，使敌军运输线再次中断。
    广播说，26日凌晨，民柬国民军一举拔除了马德望省蒙县到菩萨市之间60公里铁路和公路线上的敌军3个营级据点和两个连级据点，炸毁长80米和20米公路桥各一座，缴获和击毁各种武器34件，从而切断了金边到西部战场的运输线。
    另据这家电台广播，从11月20日到12月20日，柬埔寨抵抗力量扫除了扁担山南侧69号公路长达80公里路段上的全部越军和金边伪军设置的据点，其中包括两个团级据点、13个营级据点。敌军长期苦心经营。扁担山南侧防线被全部摧毁。在这一个月的战斗中，抵抗力量打死敌军300多人，俘获200多人。
