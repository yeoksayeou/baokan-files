Title: Australian Government Provides Loan to Ningbo
Author: Liú Zàoxiáng
Date: 1989-12-16
Edition: 2

The Australian government provided a loan for the first phase of a 150,000-line digital telephone project in Ningbo City. The loan agreement, amounting to $14.28 million, was signed in Beijing on December 14. This is the 14th preferential loan that the Australian government has provided to China since April of last year. Ericsson Australia is the supplier for the above agreement. Deputy Minister of the Ministry of Foreign Economic Relations and Trade Liu Yan and Australian Ambassador to China Mr. Shadwick attended the signing ceremony.

### 澳政府向宁波提供贷款
刘兆祥
1989-12-16
第2版(国内新闻)
专栏：

    澳政府向宁波提供贷款
    本报讯　澳大利亚政府向宁波市提供15万门程控电话项目第一期合同贷款协议14日在京签字，贷款金额为1428万美元。这是自去年4月以来澳政府向中国提供的第14笔优惠贷款。澳大利亚爱立信公司是上述协议的供货厂商。经贸部部长助理刘岩和澳大利亚驻华大使沙德伟先生出席了签字仪式。（刘兆祥）
