Title: Romanian Leader Receives Chinese Ambassador
Author:
Date: 1989-12-29
Edition: 4

Romanian leader receives Chinese ambassador

	Both sides express willingness to further develop friendly relations

	Xinhua News Agency, Bucharest, December 28 — The chairman of Romania's National Salvation Front Council, Ion Iliescu, received Chinese Ambassador to Romania, Wang Jinqing, on December 28 at the Ministry of Foreign Affairs.

	Both sides expressed their willingness to further develop Sino-Romanian bilateral friendly relations during the talks. During the meeting, Wang Jinqing conveyed a congratulatory message from Chairman of the People's Republic of China, Yang Shangkun, and a congratulatory message from Chinese Premier Li Peng to the new Romanian Prime Minister, Petre Roman. Romanian Foreign Minister Sergiu Celac was also present during the reception.

### 罗新领导人接见我大使  双方表示进一步发展友好关系

1989-12-29
第4版(国际新闻)
专栏：

    罗新领导人接见我大使
    双方表示进一步发展友好关系
    新华社布加勒斯特12月28日电　罗马尼亚救国阵线委员会主席伊利埃斯库28日在外交部接见了中国驻罗大使王荩卿。
    双方在谈话中都表示愿意进一步发展中罗双边友好关系。接见中，王荩卿向伊利埃斯库转交了中华人民共和国主席杨尚昆的贺电，及中国政府总理李鹏给罗政府新总理彼得·罗曼的贺电。罗外长塞·切拉克接见时也在座。
