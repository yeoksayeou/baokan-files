Title: International Antarctic Expedition Reaches the Pole
Author:
Date: 1989-12-13
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, December 12th.

**International Antarctic Expedition Reaches the Pole**

**Chinese Team Member Qin Dahé is the First Chinese Person to Reach the Pole**

According to information provided by the National Antarctic Expedition Committee, an international expedition team consisting of six explorers from China, the United States, France, the Soviet Union, the United Kingdom, and Japan successfully reached the South Pole at 3:00 AM Beijing time on December 12.

Qin Dahé, a mid-career glaciologist and deputy researcher at the Lanzhou Institute of Glaciology and Frozen Soil, was dispatched by the Chinese National Antarctic Expedition Committee to participate in this globally significant expedition. The expedition team set out from the tip of the Antarctic Peninsula on July 27, 1989, and after 137 days and a journey of 3,205 kilometers, they finally reached the pole, overcoming countless difficulties and dangers. Qin Dahé is the first Chinese person in history to reach the South Pole on foot.

After a brief rest at the pole, the expedition team will continue their journey westward, aiming to reach the final destination of this expedition—the Soviet Union's Mirny Station on the western edge of the Antarctic continent—by March 1, 1990.

### 国际南极探险队抵达极点  我队员秦大河是第一个到达极点的中国人

1989-12-13
第4版(国际新闻)
专栏：

    国际南极探险队抵达极点
    我队员秦大河是第一个到达极点的中国人
    新华社北京12月12日电  据国家南极考察委员会提供的消息，由中国、美国、法国、苏联、英国、日本6国6名探险家组成的国际徒步横穿南极探险队，于北京时间12月12日凌晨3时胜利抵达南极点。
    中国科学院兰州冰川冻土研究所副研究员、中年冰川学家秦大河受中国国家南极考察委员会的派遣，参加了此次全球瞩目的探险活动，探险队于1989年7月27日从南极半岛端部出发，历时137天，行程3205公里，历尽千难万险，终于胜利地到达极点。秦大河是历史上第一位徒步登上南极点的中国人。
    探险队行程过半，他们在极点稍息后将继续西征，计划于1990年3月1日前到达此次探险队的终点——位于南极大陆西部边缘的苏联和平站。
