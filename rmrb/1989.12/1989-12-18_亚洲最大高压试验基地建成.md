Title: Asia's Largest High-Voltage Test Facility Completed
Author: Chen De, Du Hua Ju
Date: 1989-12-18
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Wuhan, December 16th — (Correspondent Chen De, Reporter Du Hua Ju) Asia's largest comprehensive high-voltage test and research facility, after 15 years of effort by the scientific and technical personnel of the Wuhan High Voltage Research Institute under the Ministry of Energy, was recently completed in Wuhan and passed national acceptance today. This facility includes an ultra-high voltage transmission station and a national high-voltage metrology station, covering an area of 190,000 square meters, with a building area of 57,000 square meters, and a total investment of 53.13 million yuan.

### 亚洲最大高压试验基地建成
陈德  杜华举
1989-12-18
第1版(要闻)
专栏：

    亚洲最大高压试验基地建成
    据新华社武汉十二月十六日电　（通讯员陈德、记者杜华举）亚洲最大的综合性高电压试验研究基地，经过能源部武汉高压研究所科技人员十五年的努力，最近在武汉全部建成，并于今天通过国家验收。这个基地包括超高压输电站和国家高电压计量站，占地十九万平方米，建筑面积五万七千平方米，总投资五千三百一十三万元。
