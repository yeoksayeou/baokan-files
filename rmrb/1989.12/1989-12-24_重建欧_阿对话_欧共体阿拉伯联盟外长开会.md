Title: Rebuilding Euro-Arab Dialogue: Foreign Ministers of the European Community and the Arab League Meet
Author: Shēn Xiàoquán
Date: 1989-12-24
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Paris, December 22nd

The first ministerial meeting between the 12 member states of the European Community and the 22 member states of the Arab League was held today in Paris. The meeting made important decisions on rebuilding the Euro-Arab dialogue and promoting Euro-Arab cooperation.

The President of the European Commission, Jacques Delors, and the Secretary-General of the Arab League, Chedli Klibi, attended today's meeting. The Chairman of the Arab Summit, King Hassan II of Morocco, and the President of the European Council, French President François Mitterrand, delivered speeches at the closing ceremony this afternoon. King Hassan II stated that this meeting is a "decisive step" in the development of Euro-Arab dialogue relations. Mitterrand said that developing relations with the Arab world is a priority for Western Europe.

The meeting made specific decisions on economic cooperation, mutual investment, and cultural exchanges between the two sides. The meeting also decided that the Euro-Arab Dialogue Committee will hold its 6th meeting in the first half of next year in Dublin, the capital of Ireland.

### 重建欧—阿对话  欧共体阿拉伯联盟外长开会
沈孝泉
1989-12-24
第4版(国际新闻)
专栏：

    重建欧—阿对话
    欧共体阿拉伯联盟外长开会
    新华社巴黎12月22日电（记者沈孝泉）由欧洲共同体12国和阿拉伯联盟22国外长参加的第一届欧—阿部长会议今天在巴黎举行，会议就重建欧—阿对话和推动欧—阿合作等问题作出了重要决定。
    欧共体委员会主席德洛尔和阿盟秘书长卡利比参加了今天的会议。阿拉伯首脑会议主席、摩洛哥国王哈桑二世和欧洲理事会本届主席、法国总统密特朗在今天下午举行的闭幕式上发表了讲话。哈桑二世认为，这次会议是欧—阿对话关系发展中“决定性的一步”。密特朗说，发展同阿拉伯世界的关系是西欧优先考虑的问题。
    这次会议对双方的经济合作、相互投资和文化交流等具体计划作出了决定。会议还决定，欧—阿对话总委员会明年上半年在爱尔兰首都都柏林举行第6次会议。
