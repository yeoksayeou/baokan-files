Title: Vatican Spokesman Says Noriega Will Not Be Handed Over to the United States
Author: Guo Weicheng
Date: 1989-12-26
Edition: 4

Vatican spokesman Joaquin Navarro said the Vatican will not hand Noriega over to the United States.

The Vatican spokesman said the Vatican does not consider Noriega a political refugee, but rather accepts his wish to end the internal turmoil in Panama.

Spanish Prime Minister Felipe Gonzalez and the Foreign Minister held an emergency meeting to discuss the possibility of accepting Noriega's asylum. However, the Spanish Foreign Minister said that because Spain and the United States have an extradition treaty, Spain is not a suitable place for Noriega to seek asylum.

### 梵蒂冈教廷发言人说  不把诺列加交给美国
郭伟成
1989-12-26
第4版(国际新闻)
专栏：本报最后消息

    梵蒂冈教廷发言人说
    不把诺列加交给美国
    本报墨西哥城12月25日电　记者郭伟成报道：梵蒂冈教廷发言人华金·纳瓦罗说，教廷不会把诺列加交给美国支配。
    梵蒂冈发言人说，教廷不是把诺列加看作政治避难，而是接受他要求结束巴拿马内乱的愿望。
    西班牙首相费利佩·冈萨雷斯与外长紧急磋商接受诺列加避难的可能。但是，西班牙外长说，由于西班牙与美国之间订有《引渡条约》，因此，西班牙不是接受诺列加避难的适当地点。
