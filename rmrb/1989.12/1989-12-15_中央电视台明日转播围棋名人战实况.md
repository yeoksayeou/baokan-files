Title: CCTV to Broadcast Live Coverage of the Go Name Match Tomorrow
Author:
Date: 1989-12-15
Page: 3

Transmitted by People's Daily

The second round of the China-Japan Go Name Match will take place on December 16. This match is crucial for Ma Xiaochun, the Chinese player who has already lost one game. If he loses again, he will not be able to fulfill his promise to at least win one game against Kobayashi Koichi. CCTV will broadcast the live coverage of the match on the second channel (Channel 8 in Beijing) starting at 3:30 PM. During the broadcast, Weiqi Saint Nie Weiping and Yang Hui will provide on-site commentary.

### 中央电视台明日转播围棋名人战实况

1989-12-15
第3版(综合)
专栏：体育

    中央电视台明日转播围棋名人战实况
    本报讯　12月16日将进行中日围棋名人战第二盘比赛。这一战对于已输一盘的中方马晓春九段十分关键，如果再失利他就无法实现自己至少拚下小林光一一盘的誓言。中央电视台定于16日下午3点半用第二套节目（北京地区为8频道）转播对局实况。届时棋圣聂卫平和杨晖将在现场担任讲解。
