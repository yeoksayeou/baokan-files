Title: Comrade Guan Xuewen Passes Away
Author:
Date: 1989-12-28
Page: 2

Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, December 27th.

Comrade Guan Xuewen, former advisor to the People's Bank of China and retired cadre, passed away in Beijing on December 16 due to illness, at the age of 92. A farewell ceremony for Comrade Guan Xuewen was held today at the Babaoshan Revolutionary Cemetery. Comrades Yao Yilin, Peng Zhen, Nie Rongzhen, Chen Muhua, Yang Chengwu, and Lü Zhengcao, as well as the Organization Department, United Front Work Department, the Secretariat of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference, and the People's Bank of China sent wreaths.

Leaders such as Li Guixian and Wang Peng, along with friends who knew him in life, attended the farewell ceremony.

Comrade Guan Xuewen was from Liaoyang County, Liaoning Province, and was of Manchu ethnicity. He joined the revolution in the autumn of 1937 and became a member of the Communist Party of China in June 1940. Throughout his more than 50 years of revolutionary career, Comrade Guan Xuewen remained loyal to the Party and the people, firmly believed in Marxism-Leninism, upheld communist ideals, and steadfastly implemented the Party's lines, principles, and policies. He dedicated himself wholeheartedly to the interests of the Party and the people, exemplifying the noble qualities of a communist.

Comrade Guan Xuewen was one of the founders and a key leader of the early banking institution in the enemy-occupied areas—the Jin-Cha-Ji Border Region Bank. He also participated in the leadership work during the early establishment of the People's Bank of China, playing an important role in its founding and development. After the founding of New China, Comrade Guan Xuewen served in various positions, including Director of the Office of the People's Bank of China, Director of the North China Business Management Bureau, Director of the Organizational and Budgetary Department, Director of the North China Branch, Deputy Director of the Agricultural Bank of China, and Advisor to the Headquarters of the People's Bank of China.

Over the past 50 years, Comrade Guan Xuewen adhered to Party principles, was strict with himself, honest and upright, and maintained fairness and justice. He worked diligently and effectively, united with his comrades, cared for cadres, was approachable, lived a simple and frugal life, opposed extravagance and waste, and always upheld and carried forward the Party's fine traditions and styles. He was highly praised by a wide range of cadres and the masses.

### 关学文同志逝世

1989-12-28
第2版(国内新闻)
专栏：

    关学文同志逝世
    新华社北京12月27日电  原中国人民银行顾问、离休老干部关学文同志因病医治无效，于12月16日在北京逝世，终年92岁。向关学文同志遗体告别仪式今天在八宝山革命公墓举行。姚依林、彭真、聂荣臻、陈慕华、杨成武、吕正操同志和中组部、统战部、政协办公厅及中国人民银行送了花圈。
    李贵鲜、王平等领导同志及生前友好等参加了遗体告别仪式。
    关学文同志是辽宁省辽阳县人，满族，1937年秋参加革命，1940年6月加入中国共产党。在50余年的革命生涯中，关学文同志一贯忠于党，忠于人民，坚信马列主义，坚持共产主义信念，坚定不移地执行党的路线、方针和政策，忠心耿耿地为党和人民的利益而奋斗，出色地表现了共产党人的高贵品质。
    关学文同志是我敌后根据地早期的银行——晋察冀边区银行的创始人和主要领导人。他还参加了人民银行初建时期的领导工作，为人民银行的创建和发展起了重要的作用。新中国成立后，关学文同志曾任中国人民银行办公厅主任、华北业务管理局局长、组织预算司司长、华北区行行长，中国农业银行副行长，中国人民银行总行顾问等职。
    50多年来，关学文同志坚持党性原则，严于律己，廉洁奉公、主持公道正义；工作勤奋努力，讲求实效；他团结同志，爱护干部，平易近人；他生活艰苦朴素，反对铺张浪费，始终保持和发扬了党的优良传统和作风，深受广大干部和群众的好评。
