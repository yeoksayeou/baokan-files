Title: The "China Senior Practical Encyclopedia" Published
Author: Zhang Zhuji
Date: 1989-12-31
Edition: 3

The first comprehensive reference book for the elderly in China, "China Senior Practical Encyclopedia," was recently published by Huaxia Press. Comrade Li Peng inscribed the book's title.
