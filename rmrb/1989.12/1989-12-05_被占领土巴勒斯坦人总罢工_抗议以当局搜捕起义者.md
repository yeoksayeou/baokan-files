Title: Palestinians in Occupied Territories Hold General Strike to Protest Israeli Arrests of Uprising Participants
Author:
Date: 1989-12-05
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Cairo, December 3rd.

Palestinians in the occupied territories of the West Bank, Gaza Strip, and East Jerusalem held a general commercial strike today to protest the large-scale arrests of uprising participants by Israeli authorities.

According to reports from Jerusalem, approximately 2,000 Israeli soldiers, after arresting dozens of people and killing four in Nablus, continued their third day of searching for uprising participants there.

It is reported that this is the largest-scale arrest operation Israel has conducted in Nablus since occupying the West Bank and Gaza Strip in the 1967 Middle East War.

Strikers and Israeli soldiers engaged in fierce clashes. In the Gaza Strip, dozens of young people burned tires and threw stones and glass bottles at Israeli soldiers, who responded with tear gas and gunfire.

The Palestinians' protest action came after uprising leaders, to commemorate the second anniversary of the uprising, distributed leaflets calling for a "higher level of struggle" against Israel.

### 被占领土巴勒斯坦人总罢工  抗议以当局搜捕起义者

1989-12-05
第4版(国际新闻)
专栏：

    被占领土巴勒斯坦人总罢工
    抗议以当局搜捕起义者
    新华社开罗12月3日电　在被占领土约旦河西岸、加沙地带和东耶路撒冷的巴勒斯坦人今天举行商业总罢工，抗议以色列大规模搜捕起义参加者。
    据来自耶路撒冷的报道说，大约2000名以色列士兵在纳布卢斯逮捕几十人并杀害4人以后，今天连续第三天在那里继续搜捕起义参加者。
    据报道，这是以色列自1967年中东战争中占领约旦河西岸和加沙地带以来，在纳布卢斯进行的最大一次规模的逮捕行动。
    罢工者和以色列士兵发生了激烈冲突。在加沙地带，几十名青年焚烧轮胎并向以色列士兵投掷石块和玻璃瓶，以色列军队则向他们施放催泪弹，并向他们开枪射击。
    巴勒斯坦人的这次抗议行动是在起义领导者为纪念起义两周年而散发传单，号召对以色列进行“更高层次的斗争”后展开的。
