Title: Yang Shangkun Leaves Cairo for Alexandria
Author: Fú Bólín
Date: 1989-12-22
Edition: 1

Chinese President Yang Shangkun left Cairo this morning to continue his visit to the Egyptian coastal city of Alexandria.

At 9:10 a.m., President Hosni Mubarak came to the presidential palace, Qubba Palace, to bid farewell to Yang Shangkun. Yang Shangkun welcomed Mubarak in the grand hall of Qubba Palace, and the two leaders shook hands and exchanged pleasantries.

Following this, a farewell ceremony was held. The band played the national anthems of both China and Egypt. Yang Shangkun shook hands and bid farewell to the Egyptian People's Assembly Speaker Rifaat El-Mahgoub, Prime Minister Atef Sedki, Shura Council President Sayed Marei, and other Egyptian government officials who had come to see him off. Two Egyptian children presented flowers to Yang Shangkun and Mubarak. Yang Shangkun, accompanied by Mubarak, inspected the honor guard and warmly shook hands in farewell.

The head of the Egyptian delegation, Minister of Industry Muhammad Wahib, accompanied Yang Shangkun to Alexandria. Also traveling to Alexandria were Vice Premier and Minister of Finance Wang Baoqian, Deputy Foreign Minister Qi Huaiyuan, Deputy Minister of Foreign Economic Relations and Trade Lü Xuejin, and Chinese Ambassador to Egypt Zhan Shiliang.

Yang Shangkun arrived in Cairo on December 18 at the invitation of President Mubarak for an official friendly visit. During his stay, Yang Shangkun and Mubarak exchanged views on international issues of mutual concern and on further enhancing friendship and cooperation between the two countries.

After visiting Alexandria, Yang Shangkun will proceed to the United Arab Emirates for a visit.

### 杨主席离开罗赴亚历山大  穆巴拉克总统在库巴宫举行欢送仪式
伏柏林
1989-12-22
第1版(要闻)
专栏：

    杨主席离开罗赴亚历山大
    穆巴拉克总统在库巴宫举行欢送仪式
    新华社开罗十二月二十一日电　（记者伏柏林）中国国家主席杨尚昆今天上午离开开罗前往埃及滨海城市亚历山大继续进行访问。
    上午九点十分，穆巴拉克总统来到杨主席下榻的国宾馆库巴宫话别。杨主席在库巴宫大厅迎接了穆巴拉克总统，双方亲切握手和交谈。
    接着，举行了欢送仪式。乐队先后奏起中、埃两国国歌。杨主席同前来欢送的埃人民议会议长马哈古卜、总理西德基、协商会议主席希勒米及其他埃及政府官员一一握手话别。两名埃及儿童向杨主席和穆巴拉克总统献花后，杨主席在穆巴拉克总统的陪同下检阅了仪仗队，热烈握手道别。
    埃及陪同团团长、工业部长穆罕默德·瓦哈卜陪同杨主席前往亚历山大。一同前往亚历山大的还有国务委员兼财政部长王丙乾，外交部副部长齐怀远，对外经济贸易部副部长吕学俭，中国驻埃大使詹世亮等。
    杨主席是应穆巴拉克总统的邀请，于十八日到达开罗进行正式友好访问的。在访问期间，杨主席和穆巴拉克总统就两国共同关心的国际问题及进一步增进两国的友谊和合作交换了意见。
    杨主席在访问亚历山大之后将前往阿拉伯联合酋长国访问。
