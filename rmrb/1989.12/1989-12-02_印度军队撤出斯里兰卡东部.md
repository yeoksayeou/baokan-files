Title: Indian Troops Withdraw from Eastern Sri Lanka
Author:
Date: 1989-12-02
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Colombo, November 29th.

    Indian troops withdraw from eastern Sri Lanka
    According to a message received in Colombo on November 29, 200 vehicles loaded with Indian soldiers and equipment drove out of the eastern Sri Lankan town of Batticaloa on the 29th.
    This batch of Indian troops will board ships in the eastern Sri Lankan port of Trincomalee to return to their country.
    An Indian senior official confirmed that Indian troops will leave Sri Lanka tomorrow. However, he refused to disclose the exact number of troops.
    It is estimated that this withdrawal involves about 6,000 troops.

### 印度军队撤出斯里兰卡东部

1989-12-02
第4版(国际新闻)
专栏：

    印度军队撤出斯里兰卡东部
    据十一月二十九日在科伦坡收到的消息说，二百辆满载印度士兵及设备的卡车二十九日已驶出斯里兰卡东部的拜蒂克洛镇。
    这批印度军队将在斯里兰卡东部港口亭可马里登船回国。
    一位印度高级专员证实，印度军队将在明天离开斯里兰卡。但他拒绝透露这批军队的具体数字。
    据估计，此次撤出的军队达六千人。
