Title: Foreign Affairs Briefs
Author:
Date: 1989-12-06
Edition: 2

State Councilor and Minister of the State Education Commission Li Tieying returned to Beijing by plane on December 4th after successfully concluding his visits to Chad, the Central African Republic, Congo, and Zaire. Li Tieying led the Chinese government delegation to depart from Beijing on November 13th to visit the aforementioned four countries for friendly exchanges.

Vice Chairman of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress Wang Hanbin met with the delegation led by Chairman Eugene T. Boak of the Carnegie Corporation of New York in the Great Hall of the People on the afternoon of December 4th. The delegation, invited by the State Education Commission, arrived in Beijing on November 26th.

State Councilor Chen Junsheng met with the Soviet Forestry Delegation led by Chairman Isayev of the Soviet State Forestry Committee in the Great Hall of the People on the afternoon of December 4th. Both sides exchanged views on the issue of Sino-Soviet forestry cooperation. Minister of Forestry Gao Dezhang was present at the meeting.

President of the Supreme People's Court Ren Jianxin returned to Beijing on the afternoon of December 5th after attending the Third Asia-Pacific Chief Justices Conference held in Manila from November 27th to December 1st.

Thai Ambassador to China Dej Bunnag held a reception in Beijing on December 5th to celebrate the birthday of King Bhumibol Adulyadej of Thailand, which is also Thailand's National Day. Guests invited to the reception included Prince Norodom Sihanouk and his wife, Vice Chairman of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress Liao Hansheng, Minister of Radio, Film, and Television Ai Zhisheng, and more than 300 officials from relevant departments and foreign diplomats stationed in China.

Vice Chairman of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress Lei Jieqiong held a banquet and met with the delegation led by Zhou Juanjuan, Chairperson of the Women's Committee of the Chinese General Chamber of Commerce in Hong Kong, in the Great Hall of the People on the evening of December 5th. The delegation, invited by the All-China Women's Federation, arrived in Beijing on December 4th to begin a week-long visit to Beijing, Fuzhou, and Xiamen.

Major General Zhou Wenyuan, Deputy Director of the General Political Department of the Chinese People's Liberation Army, held a banquet in Beijing on the evening of December 5th to welcome the delegation led by Major General Yuan Mingjun, Deputy Director of the Political Bureau of the Korean People's Army. The delegation, invited by the General Political Department of the Chinese People's Liberation Army, arrived in Beijing on the morning of December 5th. The purpose of this visit is to exchange political work experiences between the two countries' armies. The Korean guests will also visit Nanjing, Guangzhou, and other places.

### 外事简讯

1989-12-06
第2版(国内新闻)
专栏：

    外事简讯
    ▲国务委员兼国家教委主任李铁映在圆满结束了对乍得、中非、刚果和扎伊尔4国的访问后，于4日乘飞机回到北京。李铁映是率中国政府代表团于11月13日离京前往上述4国进行友好访问的。
    ▲全国人大常委会副委员长王汉斌4日下午在人民大会堂会见了由主席欧内基特·鲍尔率领的美国卡内基促进教学基金会代表团。代表团应国家教委邀请访华，于11月26日抵京。
    ▲国务委员陈俊生4日下午在人民大会堂会见了由苏联国家森林委员会主席伊沙耶夫率领的苏联林业代表团。双方就中苏两国林业合作问题交换了意见。林业部部长高德占会见时在座。
    ▲最高人民法院院长任建新出席于11月27日至12月1日在马尼拉召开的第三届亚太地区首席大法官会议后，5日下午回到北京。
    ▲泰王国驻华大使德·汶纳5日在北京举行招待会，庆祝泰王国国王普密蓬·阿杜德生日，即泰国国庆日。应邀出席招待会的有民主柬埔寨主席西哈努克亲王和夫人、中国全国人大常委会副委员长廖汉生、广播电影电视部部长艾知生以及有关部门负责人和外国驻华使节共300多人。
    ▲全国人大常委会副委员长雷洁琼5日晚上在人民大会堂会见并宴请了以香港中华总商会妇女委员会主任周娟娟为团长的香港工商界妇女访问团。访问团应中华全国妇女联合会的邀请于4日抵京开始对北京、福州、厦门作为期一周的访问。
    ▲中国人民解放军总政治部副主任周文元少将5日晚上在北京举行宴会，欢迎由总政治局副局长袁明均少将率领的朝鲜人民军政工代表团一行。朝鲜人民军政工代表团是应中国人民解放军总政治部邀请于5日上午抵达北京的。此行的目的是交流两国军队之间的政治工作经验。朝鲜客人还将到南京、广州等地参观访问。
              （新华社）
