Title: Corazon Aquino to Exercise "Emergency Powers" Next Week
Author:
Date: 1989-12-17
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Manila, December 15th.

The joint committee of the Philippine Senate and House of Representatives has proposed a bill granting President Corazon Aquino "emergency powers." According to this bill, Aquino will begin exercising these powers for a period of six months starting next week.

The bill aims to mitigate the adverse effects of the December 1st coup attempt by restricting political and economic activities.

Before June 7th of next year, the powers Aquino can exercise include:

* Prohibiting hoarding, speculation, price manipulation, and the formation of monopolies;
* Taking over private public utilities and commercial sectors that affect public interest;
* Seizing unlicensed firearms, explosives, and other weapons;
* Opening up the import of vehicles and ships to ease the transportation crisis;
* Reducing expenditures of government agencies and government-controlled sectors, and reorganizing official institutions without affecting the employment of civil servants.

The joint committee will submit this bill to the legislature for approval by the 18th.

### 根据菲参众两院联委会所提法案  科·阿基诺下周开始行使“非常权力”

1989-12-17
第4版(国际新闻)
专栏：

    根据菲参众两院联委会所提法案
    科·阿基诺下周开始行使“非常权力”
    新华社马尼拉12月15日电　菲律宾参、众两院联席会议委员会提出关于授予科·阿基诺总统“非常权力的”法案。根据这项法案，科·阿基诺从下星期开始将行使为期6个月的“非常权力”。
    这项法案旨在通过限制政治、经济方面的活动以消除12月1日发生的政变所造成的不良影响。
    在明年6月7日之前科·阿基诺总统可以行使的权力主要有：
    禁止囤积居奇、投机倒把、垄断价格及组织垄断集团；
    接管私营公共事业部门及涉及公共利益的商业部门；
    搜缴未经官方许可而持有的火器、爆炸物及其他武装设备；
    开放车辆及船舶的进口以缓解运输危机；
    削减政府机关及政府控制部门开支，在不影响国家公务员任职的前提下改组官方机构。
    联席会议委员会将在18日之前将这项法案提交议会通过。
