Title: Fengzhen Power Plant Officially Starts Operation
Author: Qū Zhènyè
Date: 1989-12-08
Edition: 2

    Fengzhen Power Plant Officially Starts Operation
    According to Xinhua News Agency, Hohhot, December 7th (Reporter Qū Zhènyè): Under the ancient Great Wall, by the banks of the Yinjima River, a new power plant has risen. The first unit of the Fengzhen Power Plant in Inner Mongolia, a key project of the country's "Seventh Five-Year Plan," officially started operation yesterday. This 200,000-kilowatt unit is the first power generation unit in the Inner Mongolia Autonomous Region to be designed and installed independently.

    The Fengzhen Power Plant is a key power station connecting the western part of Inner Mongolia and the North China Power Grid. Its completion is of great significance for China's implementation of the strategy of sending electricity from the west to the east.

### 丰镇电厂正式投产
瞿振业
1989-12-08
第2版(国内新闻)
专栏：四面八方

    丰镇电厂正式投产
    据新华社呼和浩特12月7日电  （记者瞿振业）古长城下，饮马河畔，如今已崛起一座新电厂。我国“七五”重点项目内蒙古丰镇电厂一号机组昨天正式投产。这台20万千瓦的机组是内蒙古自治区第一台自行设计、安装的发电机组。
    丰镇电厂是连结内蒙古西部和华北电网的枢纽电站。它建成后，对我国实施西电东送战略有重要意义。
