Title: A Large Granite Mine Discovered in Shandong
Author:
Date: 1989-12-01
Edition: 2

A large, high-quality granite mine has recently been discovered in Changqing County, Shandong Province. Relevant provincial departments have begun planning its exploitation. Surveys indicate that the granite reserve amounts to 1 billion cubic meters. Experts believe that the granite here has excellent color and variety, strong integrity, and gives people a sense of dignity, naturalness, and comfort, surpassing the world-famous "Indian Red" granite. Experts have named it "Luqing Red."

### 山东发现大型花岗石矿

1989-12-01
第2版(国内新闻)
专栏：四面八方

    山东发现大型花岗石矿
    新华社济南11月29日电　山东省最近在长清县发现全国罕见的特大型优质花岗石矿山，省有关部门已着手规划开采。勘察表明，花岗石储量达10亿立方米，专家认为，这里的花岗石花色品种好，整体性强，能给人以庄重、自然、舒适之感，优于世界驰名的“印度红”花岗石。专家们命名它为“鲁青红”。
