Title: Foreign Affairs Brief
Author:
Date: 1989-12-16
Page: 2

At the invitation of the Zimbabwe African National Union (Patriotic Front) and the National Resistance Movement of Uganda, Vice Minister of the International Liaison Department, Jiang Guanghua, and his delegation departed Beijing on the 15th for Zimbabwe to attend the National Congress of the ZANU (PF). Subsequently, they will visit Uganda.

### 外事简讯

1989-12-16
第2版(国内新闻)
专栏：

    外事简讯
    ▲应津巴布韦非洲民族联盟（爱国阵线）和乌干达全国抵抗运动的邀请，中联部副部长蒋光化一行15日离京前往津巴布韦参加津民盟（爱阵）全国代表大会，然后将访问乌干达。
