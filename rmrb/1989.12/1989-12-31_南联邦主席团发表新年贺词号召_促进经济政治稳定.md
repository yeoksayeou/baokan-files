Title: The Presidency of the SFRY Issues a New Year's Message Calling for the Promotion of Economic and Political Stability
Author: Yan Zheng
Date: 1989-12-31
Edition: 4

The Presidency of the Socialist Federal Republic of Yugoslavia (SFRY) issued a New Year's message today, calling on the people of the country to work together to make 1990 a year of turning the tide in overcoming the economic crisis, achieving social reform, and promoting economic and political stability.

The message said, "Let us work together to make our country join the European democratization movement and integration process, while striving to play the role of Yugoslavia as a non-aligned country, promoting international cooperation and lasting world peace."

### 南联邦主席团发表新年贺词号召  促进经济政治稳定
严正
1989-12-31
第4版(国际新闻)
专栏：

    南联邦主席团发表新年贺词号召
    促进经济政治稳定
    本报贝尔格莱德12月30日电　记者严正报道：今天发表的南斯拉夫联邦主席团新年贺词号召全国人民共同努力，使1990年成为向克服经济危机、实现社会改革、促进经济和政治稳定转折的年头。
    贺词说，让我们一起努力，使自己的国家加入欧洲民主化运动和一体化进程，同时争取发挥南斯拉夫作为不结盟国家的作用，促进国际合作和世界持久和平。
