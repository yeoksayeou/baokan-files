Title: The Chinese Learning Machine Enters the Home
Author: Xiao Liang
Date: 1989-12-17
Edition: 2

The Chinese Learning Machine Enters the Home

    The China Educational Electronics Company recently successfully developed the Chinese Learning Machine using super large-scale integrated circuits, as well as a Russian version for export. The development of the Chinese Learning Machine is part of the national "Seventh Five-Year Plan" key technological research project and the "Spark" program project. To date, more than 100,000 units have been introduced into domestic schools and urban and rural households.

### 中华学习机进入家庭
晓亮
1989-12-17
第2版(国内新闻)
专栏：四面八方

    中华学习机进入家庭
    本报讯　中国教育电子公司最近研制成功采用超大规模集成电路的中华学习机和专供出口用的俄文版中华学习机。中华学习机的研制属国家“七五”科技攻关项目和“星火”计划项目，迄今已有十余万台进入国内学校和城乡人民家庭。
          （晓　亮）
