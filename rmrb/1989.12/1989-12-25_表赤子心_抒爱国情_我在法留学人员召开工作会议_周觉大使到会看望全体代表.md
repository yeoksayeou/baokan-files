Title: Expressing Filial Piety and Patriotism
Author: Wáng Yùnjiǔ
Date: 1989-12-25
Edition: 4

The two-day 1989 All-France Chinese Students and Scholars Association (AFCSSA) work meeting concluded this afternoon in Paris.

The meeting heard reports from the chairman of the third session of the AFCSSA and Li Haiji, Counselor of the Education Section of the Chinese Embassy in France, on the work of Chinese students.

Li Haiji, in discussing the ideological situation of Chinese students in France, pointed out that although the political storm that occurred this past summer caused some fluctuations and changes in the thoughts of some students, the vast majority of students have not changed their filial piety and patriotism. Patriotism has always been our common banner. Loving the socialist motherland, supporting the leadership of the Communist Party of China, and supporting the policy of reform and opening up are the mainstream thoughts of the vast majority of students.

At the conclusion of the meeting, Ambassador Zhou Jue visited all the student representatives.

### 表赤子心　抒爱国情  我在法留学人员召开工作会议  周觉大使到会看望全体代表
王运久
1989-12-25
第4版(国际新闻)
专栏：

    表赤子心　抒爱国情
    我在法留学人员召开工作会议
    周觉大使到会看望全体代表
    新华社巴黎12月23日电　（记者王运久）为期两天的1989年全法中国留学人员工作会议今天下午在巴黎结束。
    会议听取了全法中国学联第三届委员会主席的工作报告和我驻法大使馆教育处李海绩参赞关于留学生工作的报告。
    李海绩在谈到我在法留学人员的思想状况时指出，尽管今年夏天出现的那场政治风暴使部分留学人员的思想产生过一些起伏和变化，但是就绝大多数人来说，大家的赤子之心、爱国之情并没有变，爱国主义始终是我们共同的旗帜。热爱社会主义祖国，拥护中国共产党的领导，拥护改革开放的政策，是绝大多数留学人员思想的主流。
    会议结束时，我驻法大使周觉看望了全体学生代表。
