Title: The Chairman of the CCP Shanxi Provincial Advisory Committee, Jia Jun, Passes Away
Author: Chi Maohua  Wu Yan
Date: 1989-12-17
Edition: 2

The Chairman of the CCP Shanxi Provincial Advisory Committee, Jia Jun, passed away in Taiyuan on December 2 due to illness, at the age of 72. Jia Jun's funeral was held today at the Double Pagoda Martyrs' Cemetery in Taiyuan.

Wreaths were sent by Peng Zhen, Wang Zhen, Bo Yibo, Song Renqing, and Cheng Zihua.

Jia Jun was a native of Yicheng County, Shanxi Province. He joined the Communist Party of China in 1938. During the War of Resistance Against Japan and the War of Liberation, he held various positions, including Deputy Director of the Yicheng County Anti-Japanese Allied Association, Director of the Organization Department of the Jin-Yu Special Committee, Director of the Organization Department of the Tai-Nan District Committee, Secretary of the Huquan County Committee, Secretary of the Lucheng County Committee, Director of the Propaganda Department of the Taihang Three District Committee, and Director of the Organization Department. After the founding of the People's Republic, he served as Deputy Secretary and First Secretary of the Changzhi District Committee, Deputy Director of the Industrial and Transportation Department of the Shanxi Provincial Committee, Secretary of the Yuxian District Committee, Deputy Director of the Provincial Rural Work Leading Group, Director of the Provincial Agricultural and Industrial Department, and Member of the Provincial Committee. In 1985, he became the Vice Chairman and Chairman of the Provincial Advisory Committee. He was a representative of the Eighth and Thirteenth National Congresses of the Party.

### 中共山西省顾问委员会主任贾俊逝世
池茂花  武岚
1989-12-17
第2版(国内新闻)
专栏：

    中共山西省顾问委员会主任贾俊逝世
    新华社太原12月14日电　（记者池茂花、武岚）中共山西省顾问委员会主任贾俊，因病医治无效，于12月2日在太原逝世，终年72岁。贾俊遗体告别仪式今天在太原双塔烈士陵园举行。
    彭真、王震、薄一波、宋任穷、程子华等送了花圈。
    贾俊是山西省翼城县人，1938年加入中国共产党。在抗日战争和解放战争中，历任翼城县牺盟会协理员、晋豫特委组织部干部科长、太南地委组织部干部科长、壶关县委书记、潞城县委书记、太行三地委宣传部长、组织部长等职。建国后，历任长治地委副书记，第一书记，山西省委工交部常务副部长、中共榆次地委书记、省农村工作领导小组副组长、省委农工部部长，省委常委等职。1985年，担任省顾委副主任、主任。他是党的第八次、第十三次全国代表大会代表。
