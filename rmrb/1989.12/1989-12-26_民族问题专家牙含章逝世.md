Title: Renowned Expert on Ethnic Issues, Ya Hanchang, Passes Away
Author:
Date: 1989-12-26
Edition: 2

The farewell ceremony for the late Ya Hanchang, a renowned theorist on ethnic issues, religious scholar, and Tibetologist, as well as the former president of the Chinese Association for Ethnic Theory and the former director of the Institute of Ethnic Studies at the Chinese Academy of Social Sciences, was held today at the Baobao Mountain Revolutionary Cemetery Hall.

Ya Hanchang passed away on December 19 due to illness, at the age of 73.

Ya Hanchang was born in October 1916 in Hezheng County, Gansu Province, to a peasant family. From 1936, he studied Tibetan history and Lamaism at Labrang Monastery in Gannan and the Drepung Monastery in Lhasa. In 1938, he went to Yan'an to participate in revolutionary work and joined the Communist Party of China the same year. In 1939, he worked at the Fourth Bureau of the Research Office of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of China, focusing on ethnic issues and ethnic policies. Ya Hanchang held various positions, including deputy director of the Fourth Bureau of the Research Office of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of China, deputy director of the United Front Work Department of the Gansu Provincial Committee of the Communist Party of China, secretary-general of the Central Tibetan Work Committee, and vice-president of Inner Mongolia University. In his work in Tibet and his research on Tibetan studies, Ya Hanchang made significant contributions to strengthening national unity and safeguarding the unity of the motherland. His major works include "The Biography of the Dalai Lama," "The Biography of Panchen Erdeni," "A New Chapter in Tibetan History," "Ethnic Issues and Religious Issues," "Research on the Formation of Ethnic Groups," "Atheism and Religious Issues," "An Initial Exploration of Chinese Atheism," and "Underground Struggles in Longyou."

In accordance with Ya Hanchang's wishes, no obituary was issued, and no memorial service was held.

Hu Qiaomu, Ngapoi Ngawang Jigme, Saifuddin Azizi, Fei Xiaotong, Yang Jingren, Hu Sheng, and Sima Yimaiti sent wreaths, as did the National People's Congress Ethnic Affairs Committee, the United Front Work Department, the State Ethnic Affairs Commission, the Chinese Academy of Social Sciences, the Tibet Autonomous Region Party Committee, the Tibet Autonomous Region People's Government, and the Inner Mongolia Autonomous Region People's Government. Deng Ligong, Pengcuo Wangjie, Zhao Yinian, Jiang Ping, Hu Sheng, and Duoje Cairen attended the farewell ceremony.

### 民族问题专家牙含章逝世

1989-12-26
第2版(国内新闻)
专栏：

    民族问题专家牙含章逝世
    新华社北京12月25日电　向我国著名民族问题理论家、宗教学家和藏学家，中国民族理论学会理事长，中国社会科学院民族研究所原所长牙含章同志的遗体告别仪式今天在八宝山革命公墓礼堂举行。
    牙含章同志因病于12月19日逝世，终年73岁。
    牙含章同志1916年10月出生于甘肃省和政县的一个农民家庭。1936年起在甘南拉卜楞寺和拉萨哲蚌寺研究藏族历史与喇嘛教。1938年奔赴延安参加革命工作，同年加入中国共产党，1939年在中共中央西北工作委员会从事民族问题、民族政策的研究。牙含章同志曾任中共中央调研局第四分局民族研究室副主任、中共甘肃省委统战部副部长、中央西藏工委秘书长、内蒙古大学副校长等职。牙含章同志在西藏工作和从事藏学研究中，为加强民族团结、维护祖国的统一，作出了重要贡献。其主要著作有《达赖喇嘛传》、《班禅额尔德尼传》、《西藏历史的新篇章》、《民族问题与宗教问题》、《民族形成问题研究》、《无神论和宗教问题》、《中国无神论初探》、《陇右地下斗争》等。
    遵照牙含章同志的遗言，身后不发讣告、不开追悼会。
    胡乔木、阿沛·阿旺晋美、赛福鼎·艾则孜、费孝通、杨静仁、胡绳、司马义·艾买提等同志和全国人大民委、统战部、国家民委、中国社会科学院、西藏自治区党委、西藏自治区人民政府、内蒙古自治区人民政府等单位送来了花圈。邓力群、平措汪杰、赵延年、江平、胡绳、多杰才旦等前往向牙含章同志的遗体告别。
