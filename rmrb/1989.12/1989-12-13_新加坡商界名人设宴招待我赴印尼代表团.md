Title: Singapore Business Leaders Host Dinner for Chinese Delegation Visiting Indonesia
Author: Lǐ Yǒngmíng
Date: 1989-12-13
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Singapore, December 11th.

Singapore business leader Tang Yu hosted a dinner tonight to welcome the Chinese delegation that had participated in talks to restore diplomatic relations between China and Indonesia in Jakarta and were passing through Singapore on their way back to China.

Chinese Commercial Counselor to Singapore Wang Jiu'an, Singapore Trade Development Board Chairman Yang Zhiyao, former president of the Singapore Chinese Chamber of Commerce Chen Gongcun, and several prominent figures from Singapore's business community were invited to attend the dinner.

### 新加坡商界名人设宴招待我赴印尼代表团
李永明
1989-12-13
第4版(国际新闻)
专栏：

    新加坡商界名人设宴招待我赴印尼代表团
    新华社新加坡12月11日电　（记者李永明）新加坡商界名人唐裕先生今天晚上在这里设宴，招待在印尼首都雅加达参加中国与印尼恢复外交关系会谈后途经新加坡回国的中国代表团。
    中国驻新加坡商务代表王久安、新加坡贸易发展局主席杨至耀、新加坡中华总商会前会长陈共存以及新加坡商界一些知名人士应邀出席了宴会。
