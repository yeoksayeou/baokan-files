Title: Foreign Ministry Spokesperson Answers Journalist's Questions: China's Attitude Towards Selling Medium-Range Missiles is Serious and Responsible
Author:
Date: 1989-12-12
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, December 11th.

The Foreign Ministry Spokesperson said here today that China has always taken a serious and responsible attitude towards the issue of selling medium-range missiles.

A journalist asked: According to foreign media reports, China is preparing to sell M-9 missiles to Syria. What is your comment?

The Spokesperson replied: This report is groundless. China has always taken a serious and responsible attitude towards the issue of selling medium-range missiles. Except for selling a small quantity of missiles to Saudi Arabia, China has not and will not sell medium-range missiles to Middle Eastern countries.

### 外交部发言人答记者问  中国出售中导态度严肃认真

1989-12-12
第1版(要闻)
专栏：

    外交部发言人答记者问
    中国出售中导态度严肃认真
    新华社北京12月11日电  外交部发言人今天在这里说，中国对出售中程导弹问题一向持严肃认真的态度。
    有记者问：据外电报道，中国准备向叙利亚出售M—9导弹。请问有何评论？
    发言人答：这一报道毫无根据。中国对出售中程导弹问题一向持严肃认真的态度。除向沙特出售少量导弹外，中国没有也不准备向中东国家出售中导。
