Title: Heilongjiang Recovers Excessively Occupied Housing
Author: Zhai Jingyao
Date: 1989-12-30
Edition: 2

The housing rectification work that began in June in Heilongjiang Province has made rapid progress. So far, more than 500 households of cadres at the bureau level and above have been found to have housing areas exceeding the regulations. A total of 491 units of excessively occupied housing have been recovered; 21 million yuan of excess subsidies for low-priced sales and excess public-assisted construction funds have been recovered, as well as 19.6 million yuan for high-standard renovations of housing with public funds and other types of disciplinary funds.

### 黑龙江收回多占住房
翟景耀
1989-12-30
第2版(国内新闻)
专栏：四面八方

    黑龙江收回多占住房
    新华社哈尔滨12月28日电（记者翟景耀）黑龙江省从6月开始的清房工作进展迅速。目前，全省在处以上干部中清理出有500多户住房面积超规定；收回多占住房491套；收回多补贴的低价售房款及私建公助超额款2100万元，收回用公款高标准装修住房款及其他各种违纪款196万元。
