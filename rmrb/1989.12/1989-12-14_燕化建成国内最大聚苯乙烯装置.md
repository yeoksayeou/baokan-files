Title: Yanshan Petrochemical Completes Largest Polystyrene Facility in China
Author: Wang Guorui Liu Qingzheng
Date: 1989-12-14
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, December 13th.

China's largest polystyrene production facility has recently been completed and put into operation at the Yanshan Petrochemical Company in Beijing. This large-scale polystyrene production facility, which was imported from the United States with an investment of 150 million yuan, has an annual designed capacity of 50,000 tons.

### 燕化建成国内最大聚苯乙烯装置
王国瑞  刘清正
1989-12-14
第1版(要闻)
专栏：

    燕化建成国内最大聚苯乙烯装置
    据新华社北京十二月十三日电  （通讯员王国瑞、刘清正）我国最大的聚苯乙烯生产装置，日前在北京燕山石油化工公司建成投产。由美国引进、投资一点五亿元建成的这套大型聚苯乙烯生产装置，年设计能力为五万吨。
