Title: PLO Leader Khalif Says PLO Has Determined Representatives for Palestinian-Israeli Dialogue
Author:
Date: 1989-12-17
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Kuwait, December 15th.

PLO leader Salah Khalif said the PLO has determined the list of Palestinian representatives to participate in the Palestinian-Israeli dialogue. The list will be announced at an appropriate time.

Khalif (also known as Abu Iyad) revealed this information during an interview with the Kuwaiti newspaper "Al-Watan."

He emphasized that the selection of the Palestinian delegation is purely an internal matter for the Palestinians, and only the PLO has the right to choose the delegation to participate in the dialogue with Israel.

Khalif believes that Egypt's mediation role between the PLO and the United States will promote the dialogue or negotiations between the Palestinians and Israelis. However, the United States and Israel are attempting to have Egypt represent the Palestinian people.

He said, "The representative rights of the Palestinian people are non-transferable, and no Arab country has the right to represent the PLO."

When discussing the relationship between the Palestinians and Syria, Khalif said that normalizing relations with Syria is a set policy of the PLO. When the time is right, the PLO will send a delegation to visit Damascus.

### 巴解领导人哈利夫说  巴解已确定参加巴以对话代表

1989-12-17
第4版(国际新闻)
专栏：

    巴解领导人哈利夫说
    巴解已确定参加巴以对话代表
    新华社科威特12月15日电巴解组织法塔赫运动中央委员萨利赫·哈利夫说，巴解组织已经确定了参加巴以对话的巴勒斯坦代表名单。这个名单将在适当的时候公布。
    哈利夫（又名阿布·伊亚德）是在接受科威特《祖国报》的采访时，透露这一消息的。
    他强调，拟定巴勒斯坦代表名单完全是巴勒斯坦的内部事务，只有巴解组织有权选定代表团参加与以色列的对话。
    哈利夫认为，埃及在巴解组织和美国之间所起的调解作用，将推动巴以之间的对话或谈判。但是，美国和以色列妄图让埃及作为巴勒斯坦人民的代表。
    他说：“巴勒斯坦人民的代表权是不可转让的，任何阿拉伯国家都无权代表巴解组织”。
    在谈到巴勒斯坦与叙利亚关系时，哈利夫说，实现与叙利亚关系正常化是巴解组织的既定方针。在时机成熟的时候，巴解组织将派遣代表团访问大马士革。
