Title: The First Shanghai Farmers' Film Festival Opens
Author: Zhao Lan Ying
Date: 1989-12-12
Edition: 3

The first Shanghai Farmers' Film Festival opened at the Jiading County Theater in Shanghai's suburbs. This film festival, which will last for one month, will screen 12 new films submitted by production studios for competition and 8 exhibition films in various counties and towns across Shanghai.

### 首届上海农民电影节开幕
赵兰英
1989-12-12
第3版(综合)
专栏：

    首届上海农民电影节开幕
    据新华社上海十二月十一日电　（记者赵兰英）首届上海农民电影节在上海市郊嘉定县影剧院拉开大幕。这届农民电影节，将在一个月的时间内，在上海各县城、乡镇展映制片厂送来参赛的十二部新片和八部展览影片。
