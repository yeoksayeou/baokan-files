Title: Standing Committee of the NPC Holds 11th Meeting
Author:
Date: 1989-12-21
Edition: 1

The 11th meeting of the Standing Committee of the Seventh National People's Congress began this morning in the Great Hall of the People.

Commissioner-in-Chief Wan Li presided over the meeting.

At the beginning of the meeting, Vice Commissioner-in-Chief Peng Chong explained the draft agenda for this meeting. The commissioners approved this agenda.

According to the opinions on the "Draft Law on Urban Residents' Committees" discussed at the previous meeting of the Standing Committee, Vice Chairman of the Law Committee Lin Jianqing reported today on whether the law should stipulate that residents' committees can engage in "production" service industries.

The previous meeting of the Standing Committee also discussed the "Draft Amendments to the Environmental Protection Law" and the "Draft Urban Planning Law." Today, Vice Chairman of the Law Committee Gu Ming and Song Ruwei reported on the results of the deliberations on these two draft laws.

To protect the safety of military facilities, ensure the effectiveness of military facilities, and guarantee the normal conduct of military activities, the State Council and the Central Military Commission submitted a proposal to this meeting of the Standing Committee to review the "Draft Law on the Protection of Military Facilities." At the meeting, Central Military Commission member and Chief of the General Staff of the People's Liberation Army, He Hetao, explained the draft on behalf of the State Council and the Central Military Commission.

Premier Li Peng submitted to this meeting of the Standing Committee a proposal to review the "Draft Copyright Law" and a proposal to review the "Draft Amendments to the Law on Sino-Foreign Joint Ventures." At the meeting, Director of the State Copyright Bureau Song Muwen and Minister of the Ministry of Foreign Economic Relations and Trade Zheng Tuobin explained these two draft laws on behalf of the State Council.

Premier Li Peng also submitted to this meeting of the Standing Committee a proposal to review the "Consular Convention between the People's Republic of China and the Republic of Turkey." At the meeting, Vice Minister of Foreign Affairs Tian Zengpei explained the convention on behalf of the State Council.

Vice Commissioners-in-Chief Apu Aluang Jinmei, Saifuding Aizisi, Yan Jici, Rong Yiren, Ye Fei, Liao Hansheng, Chen Mu Hua, Fei Xiaotong, Sun Qimeng, Lei Jieqiong, and Wang Hanbin attended the meeting.

President of the Supreme People's Court Ren Jianxin attended the meeting as a non-voting delegate.

### 人大常委会举行第11次会议  万里主持会议　通过会议议程  听取有关法律草案的审议报告和说明

1989-12-21
第1版(要闻)
专栏：

    人大常委会举行第11次会议
    万里主持会议　通过会议议程
    听取有关法律草案的审议报告和说明
    新华社北京12月20日电　七届全国人大常委会第11次会议今天上午在人民大会堂开始举行。
    万里委员长主持了会议。
    会议开始时，彭冲副委员长就这次会议的议程草案作了说明。委员们通过了这个议程。
    根据上次常委会会议对《城市居民委员会组织法（草案）》的审议意见，法律委员会副主任委员林涧青今天就这部法律草案是否规定居民委员会可以兴办“生产”服务事业问题作了汇报。
    上一次常委会会议还对《环境保护法（修改草案）》、《城市规划法（草案）》进行了审议。今天的会上，法律委员会副主任委员顾明、宋汝棼分别作了关于这两部法律草案的审议结果的报告。
    为了保护军事设施的安全，保障军事设施的使用效能和军事活动的正常进行，国务院、中央军委向这次常委会议提交了关于提请审议《军事设施保护法（草案）》的议案。会上，中央军委委员、人民解放军总参谋长迟浩田受国务院、中央军委的委托，就草案作了说明。
    国务院总理李鹏向这次常委会会议提交了关于提请审议《著作权法（草案）》的议案、关于提请审议《中外合资经营企业法修正案（草案）》的议案。会上，国家版权局局长宋木文，对外经济贸易部部长郑拓彬，受国务院委托，分别就这两个法律草案作了说明。
    李鹏总理还向这次常委会议提交了关于提请审议《中华人民共和国和土耳其共和国领事条约》的议案。会上，外交部副部长田曾佩受国务院委托，就条约作了说明。
    副委员长阿沛·阿旺晋美、赛福鼎·艾则孜、严济慈、荣毅仁、叶飞、廖汉生、陈慕华、费孝通、孙起孟、雷洁琼、王汉斌，出席了会议。
    最高人民法院院长任建新列席会议。
