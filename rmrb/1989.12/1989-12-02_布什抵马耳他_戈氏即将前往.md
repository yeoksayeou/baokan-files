Title: Bush Arrives in Malta; Gorbachev to Follow
Author:
Date: 1989-12-02
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Valletta, Malta, December 1st.

U.S. President Bush arrived in Malta's capital, Valletta, this morning to participate in the informal summit between the U.S. and the Soviet Union scheduled for the next two days. He was greeted at the airport by Malta's Prime Minister, Adami. According to the published schedule, the leaders of the U.S. and the Soviet Union will hold three rounds of talks, focusing on the situation in Eastern Europe, disarmament, and regional conflicts.

Gorbachev will fly to Valletta tonight after concluding his visit to Italy.

### 布什抵马耳他  戈氏即将前往

1989-12-02
第4版(国际新闻)
专栏：

    布什抵马耳他  戈氏即将前往
    据新华社马耳他瓦莱塔12月1日电　美国总统布什今天上午抵达马耳他首都瓦莱塔，参加将于明后两天举行的美苏首脑非正式会晤。他在机场受到了马耳他总理阿达米的迎接。根据这里公布的日程，美苏领导人将举行3轮会谈，着重讨论东欧局势、裁军和地区冲突等问题。
    戈尔巴乔夫将在结束对意大利访问后于今晚飞抵瓦莱塔。
