Title: U.S. Military Conducts Exercises in the Panama Canal Zone
Author:
Date: 1989-12-10
Edition: 4

The U.S. Southern Command's troops in the Panama Canal Zone conducted large-scale military exercises on December 7th and 8th.

A convoy consisting of more than ten U.S. tanks and military vehicles passed through the Americas Bridge on the 8th, heading straight for the Amador Fortress, a jointly administered military area by Panama and the U.S.

On the 7th, two U.S. military aircraft and a helicopter transported a batch of soldiers to the Chilibre Water Treatment Plant in the Canal Zone for military exercises.

### 美军在巴拿马运河区演习

1989-12-10
第4版(国际新闻)
专栏：

    美军在巴拿马运河区演习
    美国南方司令部驻巴拿马运河区的军队七日和八日在巴拿马运河区进行了大规模的军事演习。
      一个由十多辆美国坦克和军车组成的车队八日通过美洲桥，直向巴、美两国军队共管的军事区阿马多尔堡驶去。
    七日，二架美国军用飞机和一架直升机运送一批士兵到运河区奇利布雷自来水厂附近进行军事演习。
