Title: Achieving Domestic and International Leading Levels: The Huaguang Computer System Passes Inspection
Author: Wei Yanan
Date: 1989-12-27
Page: 3

People's Daily, Beijing, December 26th - Reporter Wei Yanan reports: The national key project of the "Seventh Five-Year Plan" — the Huaguang IV Computer — Laser Chinese Character Editing and Typesetting System — passed the national design, production, and type approval inspection today in Beijing. The inspection results show that the system's hardware, software technology level, typesetting software functions, page quality, graphic typesetting functions, graphic quality, and system adaptability have all reached domestic and international leading levels, achieving full-page, multi-language typesetting functions. In recent years, the Huaguang system has been used by more than 500 units in the fields of news, publishing, and printing, and has also been sold to Hong Kong, Macau, Taiwan, Singapore, and other places.

In 1974, the late Premier Zhou Enlai personally arranged for the development and production of this project to bring China's news publishing and printing industry into the era of computerization. Over the past 15 years, Beijing University, Weifang Computer Company, Hangzhou Communication Equipment Factory of the Ministry of Posts and Telecommunications, Changchun Institute of Optics and Fine Mechanics of the Chinese Academy of Sciences, Kunlun Electronic Printing Equipment Service Company, and other units have worked together, adopting large-scale integrated circuit technology. The Huaguang IV has become the world's first system to use large-screen typesetting and directly modify true-to-life large samples of Chinese newspaper typesetting.

### 达到国内外领先水平  华光电脑系统通过鉴定
魏亚南
1989-12-27
第3版(综合)
专栏：

    达到国内外领先水平
    华光电脑系统通过鉴定
    本报北京12月26日讯　记者魏亚南报道：国家“七五”重点攻关项目——华光IV型计算机——激光汉字编辑排版系统，今天在京通过了国家设计生产定型鉴定。鉴定结果表明，该系统硬件、软件技术水平、排版软件的功能、版面质量、图形排版功能和图形质量以及系统的适应范围等主要方面均已达到国内外领先水平，达到了全版式、多文种排版功能。近年来，华光系统已有500多套用于新闻、出版、印刷单位，并已销往香港、澳门、台湾、新加坡等地。
    1974年，已故周恩来总理亲自安排开发研制这一项目，以使我国新闻出版印刷行业进入电脑化。15年来，经过北京大学、潍坊计算机公司、邮电部杭州通信设备厂、中国科学院长春光机所、昆仑电子印刷设备服务公司等单位团结一致，通力合作，采用超大规模集成电路技术，华光IV型成为世界上第一个采用大屏幕组版，并直接修改逼真大样的中文报纸组版系统。
