Title: The 15th African Men's Basketball Championship Concludes
Author:
Date: 1989-12-28
Edition: 3

The 15th African Men's Basketball Championship concluded on December 26 in Luanda. The Angolan team won the championship, the Egyptian team took the runner-up position, and the Senegalese team finished third.

The championship began on December 16, with 11 teams participating in the competition.

### 非洲男篮锦标赛结束

1989-12-28
第3版(综合)
专栏：体育

    非洲男篮锦标赛结束
    新华社罗安达12月26日电　第15届非洲男子篮球锦标赛26日在这里结束，安哥拉队夺得冠军，埃及队获亚军，塞内加尔队名列第三。
    这届非洲锦标赛是本月16日开始的，共有11个队参加比赛。
