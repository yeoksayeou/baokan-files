Title: Chinese Players Achieve Three Consecutive Wins in the Fifth China-Japan Go Tournament
Author:
Date: 1989-12-24
Page: 3

Xinhua News Agency, Singapore, December 23rd.

The fifth match of the fifth China-Japan Go Tournament concluded this afternoon here. Chinese young player Zhang Wendi, a 7th dan, defeated Japanese player Ohiara Shusha, a 9th dan, in a middle-game victory. The game started at 9 AM and ended at 6 PM when Ohiara Shusha conceded defeat. The match lasted 201 moves.

Zhang Wendi, a 20-year-old player from Beijing, has achieved three consecutive wins in this tournament. As a result, the Chinese team leads with a score of 4:1.

The sixth match will be held on the 25th here, with Zhang Wendi facing Japanese player Yamashiro Hiroshi, a 9th dan.

### 中日围棋擂台赛我方新人三连胜  张文东击败大平修三

1989-12-24
第3版(综合)
专栏：体育

    中日围棋擂台赛我方新人三连胜
    张文东击败大平修三
    据新华社新加坡12月23日电（记者李永明）第五届中日围棋擂台赛第五场比赛今天下午在这里结束。中国年轻棋手张文东七段中盘战胜日本大平修三九段。这盘棋从上午9点开始，下午6点，大平修三投子认输。比赛共弈201手。
    北京20岁棋手张文东在本届中日围棋擂台赛中已获“三连胜”。至此，中方以4∶1领先。
    第六场比赛将于25日在这里举行，由张文东对日本山城宏九段。
