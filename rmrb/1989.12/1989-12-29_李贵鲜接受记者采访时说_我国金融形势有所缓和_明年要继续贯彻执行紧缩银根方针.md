Title: Li Guixian Says in an Interview with a Reporter that the Financial Situation in Our Country Has Eased and That Next Year We Will Continue to Implement the Policy of Tightening Monetary Supply

Author:

Date: 1989-12-29

Edition: 1

	Li Guixian said in an interview with a reporter that the financial situation in our country has eased. Next year, we will continue to implement the policy of tightening monetary supply.

	The People's Daily reported today that the second page of today's issue carries a report on Li Guixian, State Councilor and Governor of the People's Bank of China, discussing the country's financial situation this year and the financial policy for next year.
