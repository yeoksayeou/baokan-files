Title: Northwest University Publishes High-Quality Books
Author: Meng Xi'an Yuan Zi
Date: 1989-12-13
Edition: 3

Northwest University Press adheres to the principles of serving teaching and research and publishing fewer but better books. It produces high-level, high-quality publications, some of which have attracted the attention of the international academic community.

The press prioritizes social benefits above all else. Since its establishment in 1984, it has published over 200 books, none of which are of poor quality or have low content standards.

### 西北大学出版图书质量高
孟西安　垣子
1989-12-13
第3版(综合)
专栏：

    西北大学出版图书质量高
    本报西安电　西北大学出版社坚持为教学科研服务和少而精的原则，高层次高质量出书，部分图书已为国际学术界瞩目。
    这个出版社坚持把社会效益放在第一位。出版社一九八四年成立以来，出版图书二百多种，没一本坏书和内容格调低下的书。
                  （孟西安　垣　子）
