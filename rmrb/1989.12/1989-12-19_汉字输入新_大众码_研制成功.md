Title: New "Dazhong Code" for Chinese Character Input Successfully Developed
Author: Cheng Jian
Date: 1989-12-19
Page: 2

Xinhua News Agency, Chengdu, December 18th

A new "Dazhong Code" that combines sound and shape has been successfully developed by national-level experts from the Sichuan Provincial Economic Information Center, including Chen Daiyu. This advancement marks a significant step forward in computer Chinese character input technology. The new "Dazhong Code" can uniformly encode 60,000 characters, including simplified Chinese, traditional Chinese, Korean, and Japanese Kanji, making it the largest character set for encoding in the country at present.

### 汉字输入新“大众码”研制成功
成建
1989-12-19
第2版(国内新闻)
专栏：四面八方

    汉字输入新“大众码”研制成功
    新华社成都12月18日电　（记者成建）四川省经济信息中心国家级专家陈代于等人最近研制成功音形结合的新“大众码”，使计算机汉字输入技术又迈上了一个新台阶。新“大众码”能对简体、繁体、朝鲜文、日文汉字共6万字进行统一编码，是目前国内容量最大的汉字编码。
