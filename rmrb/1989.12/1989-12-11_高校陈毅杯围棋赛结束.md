Title: The Second "Chen Yi Cup" Go Tournament for Universities in Beijing Concludes
Author: Lü Changhong
Date: 1989-12-11
Edition: 3

The second "Chen Yi Cup" Go tournament for universities in Beijing concluded on December 10 at Beijing University of Aeronautics and Astronautics. This event was jointly organized by the Beijing University Students' Go Association, the "Go World" magazine, and other organizations.

A total of 144 players participated in the final round, selected from more than 2,000 Go enthusiasts who participated in the preliminary rounds.

### 高校陈毅杯围棋赛结束
吕长宏
1989-12-11
第3版(综合)
专栏：体育

    高校陈毅杯围棋赛结束
    本报讯第二届首都高校“陈毅杯”围棋赛十二月十日在北京航空航天大学结束。这项赛事是由北京大学生围棋协会、《围棋天地》杂志社等联合主办的。
    参加决赛阶段的共有一百四十四名棋手，他们是从两千多名围棋爱好者参加的预赛中选拔来的。（吕长宏）
