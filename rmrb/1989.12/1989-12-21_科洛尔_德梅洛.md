Title: Color de Mello
Author: Cai Ji
Date: 1989-12-21
Edition: 4

Fernando Collor de Mello, the newly elected President of Brazil, is 40 years old. He was born in Rio de Janeiro and studied journalism at the Federal University of Alagoas in northeastern Brazil and economics at the University of Brasilia. In 1979, he was appointed mayor of Maceió, the capital of Alagoas State, thus entering the political arena. From 1983 to 1987, he served as a federal deputy in Brazil and participated in the Brazilian Democratic Movement Party. Since March 1987, he has been the governor of Alagoas State. He resigned from his position as governor in June this year to run for the presidency. His Brazilian Young Party was reorganized into the National Reconstruction Party in February this year. He has a lifelong love for football and other sports. Collor is married and has two sons. In December 1987, Collor visited China as the governor of Alagoas.

### 科洛尔·德梅洛
才基
1989-12-21
第4版(国际新闻)
专栏：人物介绍

    科洛尔·德梅洛
    巴西新当选总统费尔南多·科洛尔·德梅洛，40岁，出生于里约热内卢，曾在巴西东北部阿拉戈亚斯州联邦大学新闻系和首都巴西利亚大学经济系就读。1979年被任命为阿拉戈亚斯州州府马塞约市市长，从此步入政坛。1983—1987年任巴西联邦众议员，并参加过巴西民主运动党。自1987年3月份开始任阿拉戈亚斯州州长，今年6月辞去州长职务，参加总统竞选。他所属的巴西青年党于今年2月改组为国家复兴党。他从小爱好足球等各项体育运动。科洛尔已婚，有两个儿子。科洛尔曾在1987年12月以州长身份访问中国。　·才基·
