Title: Adding Momentum to the National Economy's Development
Author: Zhao Mingliang, Wang Xianguang
Date: 1989-12-31
Edition: 1

The State Energy Investment Corporation has completed a number of large and medium-sized coal and power projects this year, adding significant momentum to China's energy industry and national economic development.

By December 29, the corporation had completed 34 coal mine projects, increasing coal production capacity by 23.15 million tons, exceeding the national plan by 1.56 million tons. This is equivalent to building two more super-large mines. Additionally, 38 power generation units in the corporation's investment or joint-venture power engineering projects have been completed and put into operation.

### 为国民经济发展添后劲  一批大中煤电项目建成
赵明亮  王显光
1989-12-31
第1版(要闻)
专栏：

    为国民经济发展添后劲
    一批大中煤电项目建成
    据新华社北京12月29日电（记者赵明亮、通讯员王显光）国家能源投资公司今年又投资建成一大批大中型煤、电项目，为我国能源工业和国民经济发展增添了后劲。
    截至12月29日，这个公司投资建设的煤炭项目，已有34处矿井投产，新增煤炭生产能力2315万吨，超过国家计划156万吨，等于多建了两个特大型矿井；这个公司投资或合资建设的电力工程项目中，已有38台机组建成投产。
