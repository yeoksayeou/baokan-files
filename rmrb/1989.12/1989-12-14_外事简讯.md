Title: Foreign Affairs Brief
Author:
Date: 1989-12-14
Edition: 2

Vice Premier and Minister of the Ministry of Machine Building and Electronics, Zhuo Jiahua, met with a delegation led by Shahid Javed Burki, Director of the World Bank's China Division in the Asia Region, in Beijing on the afternoon of December 13. Zhuo Jiahua briefed the guests on China's policies and plans in the field of mechanical and electronic industries.

At the invitation of the interior ministries of Thailand and Pakistan, a Chinese public security delegation led by Vice Premier and Minister of Public Security Wang Fang began a friendly visit to the two countries on December 13.

Admiral Zhang Lianzhong, Commander of the Chinese People's Liberation Army Navy, departed Beijing by plane on December 13, at the invitation of Thailand, Bangladesh, and Pakistan, for a friendly visit to these countries.

### 外事简讯

1989-12-14
第2版(国内新闻)
专栏：

    外事简讯
    ▲国务委员兼机电部部长邹家华13日下午在北京会见世界银行亚洲地区中国局局长沙希德·贾维德·伯基一行。邹家华向客人们介绍了中国在机械电子工业方面的政策和规划。
    ▲应泰国和巴基斯坦内政部的邀请，由国务委员兼公安部长王芳率领的中国公安代表团，从13日起对泰、巴两国进行友好访问。
    ▲中国人民解放军海军司令员张连忠中将13日乘飞机离开北京，应邀前往泰国、孟加拉国、巴基斯坦进行友好访问。
            （新华社发）
