Title: Foreign Affairs Brief
Author:
Date: 1989-12-20
Edition: 2

Vice Premier Li Guixian hosted a dinner for Ethiopian Vice Premier Wolde Chikolet and his delegation at the Diaoyutai State Guesthouse on the evening of December 19.

The delegation arrived in Beijing in the afternoon of December 19.

Mayor of Beijing Chen Xitong met with American Chinese Mr. Huang Jinbo in the Great Hall of the People in the afternoon of December 19.

Mr. Huang is the former mayor and current council member of Sherwood, California.

### 外事简讯

1989-12-20
第2版(国内新闻)
专栏：

    外事简讯
    ▲国务委员李贵鲜19日晚在钓鱼台国宾馆会见并宴请了埃塞俄比亚副总理沃利·契科勒及其一行。
    契科勒一行是19日下午抵京的。
    ▲国务委员、北京市市长陈希同19日下午在人民大会堂会见了美籍华人黄锦波先生。
    黄先生是美国加州舍瑞图市前任市长、现任市议员。
