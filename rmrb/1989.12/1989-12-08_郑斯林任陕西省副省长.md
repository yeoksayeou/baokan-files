Title: Zheng Silin Appointed as Vice Governor of Shaanxi Province
Author:
Date: 1989-12-08
Edition: 2

    Zheng Silin Appointed as Vice Governor of Shaanxi Province

    Xinhua News Agency, Xi'an, December 6th - The 10th meeting of the Seventh Standing Committee of the Shaanxi Provincial People's Congress, which concluded yesterday, decided to appoint Zheng Silin as the Vice Governor of Shaanxi Province.

    Zheng Silin was born in Wuxian County, Jiangsu Province, in 1940. He started working in September 1963 and joined the Communist Party in 1965. He has a university education. After working in enterprises for many years, he was appointed as the Vice Mayor of Dandong in 1983. In 1985, he became the Assistant to the Governor of Liaoning Province and the Director of the Provincial Foreign Economic and Trade Commission.

### 郑斯林任陕西省副省长

1989-12-08
第2版(国内新闻)
专栏：

    郑斯林任陕西省副省长
    新华社西安12月6日电　昨天结束的陕西省七届人大常委会10次会议，决定任命郑斯林为陕西省副省长。
    郑斯林1940年生于江苏省吴县，1963年9月参加工作，1965年入党，大学文化程度。他在企业工作多年，1983年任丹东市副市长，1985年任辽宁省省长助理、省外经贸委主任。
