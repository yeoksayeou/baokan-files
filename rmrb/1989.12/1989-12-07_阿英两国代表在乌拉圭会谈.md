Title: Representatives of Argentina and Britain Hold Talks in Uruguay
Author:
Date: 1989-12-07
Edition: 4

Representatives of the Argentine and British governments held talks in Montevideo, the capital of Uruguay, on December 5 to discuss the establishment of a security mechanism in the Malvinas Islands region. The aim is to prevent potential military conflicts between the two countries in the South Atlantic.

According to Argentine diplomatic sources in Buenos Aires on December 5, the main topic of this round of talks was the possibility of lifting restrictions on communication, navigation, and aviation between the Malvinas Islands and the Argentine continental shelf.

### 阿英两国代表在乌拉圭会谈

1989-12-07
第4版(国际新闻)
专栏：

    阿英两国代表在乌拉圭会谈
    阿根廷和英国政府代表五日在乌拉圭首都蒙得维的亚就在马尔维纳斯群岛地区建立安全机构举行了会谈，目的在于避免双方在南大西洋地区可能发生的军事冲突。
    据阿根廷外交人士五日在布宜诺斯艾利斯透露，这次会谈主要讨论了取消双方在马岛和阿根廷大陆架之间的通讯、航海和航空的限制的可能性。
