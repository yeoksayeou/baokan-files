Title: Bondy Tubes Now Produced Domestically
Author: Liǔ Xièyáng
Date: 1989-12-04
Page: 2

    Reporter Liǔ Xièyáng reports: The Bondy tube, a specialized automotive tubing material long considered indispensable by the world, has been successfully developed by the Sino-foreign joint venture Huá Yàn Bāngdí Company in Qinhuangdao. This marks the end of China's reliance on imported Bondy tubes. The Bondy tube is made from copper-plated cold-rolled strips, processed through unique rolling and brazing techniques to produce high-precision, small-diameter double-walled spiral-welded tubes. It has extensive applications in the refrigeration industry, particularly in the automotive sector.

### 邦迪管已能国产
刘燮阳
1989-12-04
第2版(国内新闻)
专栏：四面八方

    邦迪管已能国产
    本报讯　记者刘燮阳报道：一直被世界公认难以取代的汽车专用管材——邦迪管，在我国秦皇岛中外合资企业华燕邦迪公司研制成功，从而结束了我国依赖进口邦迪管的历史。邦迪管是采用镀铜的冷轧带，经独特的轧制成型和钎焊处理制得的一种高精度小口径的双层卷焊管，在制冷工业特别是汽车工业中有极其广泛的用途。
