Title: The Rock-Sand Hydroelectric Power Station Under Construction
Author: Luo Ancheng
Date: 1989-12-04
Edition: 1

The workers of the Guangxi Hydropower Engineering Bureau, who are responsible for the concrete construction of the dam and powerhouse of the Rock-Sand Hydroelectric Power Station, a key national project, have carried forward the spirit of hard struggle and tenacious struggle. In October, the concrete pouring for the dam and powerhouse exceeded 110,000 cubic meters, significantly accelerating the project's progress. The image shows the front dam of the power station.

### 建设中的岩滩水电站（图片）
罗安诚
1989-12-04
第1版(要闻)
专栏：

    建设中的岩滩水电站
