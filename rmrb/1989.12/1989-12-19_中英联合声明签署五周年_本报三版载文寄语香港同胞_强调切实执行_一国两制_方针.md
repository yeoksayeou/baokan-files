Title: Fifth Anniversary of the Signing of the Sino-British Joint Declaration
Author:
Date: 1989-12-19
Page: 1

The 19th of December marks the fifth anniversary of the formal signing of the Sino-British Joint Declaration on the Hong Kong issue in Beijing by the governments of China and Britain. The People's Daily published an article on its third page, addressed to Hong Kong compatriots, emphasizing the need to genuinely implement the "one country, two systems" policy. (Full text on the third page).

### 中英联合声明签署五周年  本报三版载文寄语香港同胞  强调切实执行“一国两制”方针

1989-12-19
第1版(要闻)
专栏：

    中英联合声明签署五周年
    本报三版载文寄语香港同胞
    强调切实执行“一国两制”方针
    本报讯　十二月十九日是中英两国政府在北京正式签署关于香港问题联合声明五周年的日子，本报三版发表郭刚寄语香港同胞的文章，强调切实执行“一国两制”的方针政策（全文见第三版）。
