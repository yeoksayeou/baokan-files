Title: Major Calligraphy Exhibition of Grass Script Held in Beijing
Author: Shao Jianwu
Date: 1989-12-14
Edition: 3

The largest-ever exhibition of grass script calligraphy opened in Beijing today, showcasing 409 works by Chinese and foreign calligraphers. The exhibition highlights the current standards and scope of contemporary grass script art.

The Standard Grass Script Society, the Chinese Artists Association, and the Chinese People's Association for Friendship with Foreign Countries jointly called for submissions from both domestic and international artists in 1987. From over 10,000 entries, 409 works were selected for display.

### 中外草书展览在京举行
邵建武
1989-12-14
第3版(综合)
专栏：

    中外草书展览在京举行
    据新华社北京十二月十二日电  （记者邵建武）有史以来规模最大的草书展览今天在京举行，参展的四百零九件中外书法家的作品展示了当代草书艺术的水准和格局。
    标准草书学社、中国美术家协会和中国国际友谊促进会等单位于一九八七年联合向海内外征稿，在一万多件应征作品中，遴选出四百零九件作品。
