Title: Yuxi Cigarette Factory Surpasses 15 Billion Yuan in Tax Revenue and Profits This Year
Author: Qianjiang
Date: 1989-12-13
Edition: 1

Yuxi Cigarette Factory in Yunnan has exceeded its annual plan, producing over 11 million cases of cigarettes, with a total production increase of 4.8% compared to last year. Among these, 7.25 million cases are filtered cigarettes, marking a 25% increase from the previous year. The total tax revenue and profits have surpassed 15 billion yuan, an increase of over 3 billion yuan compared to last year. The per capita tax revenue and profit creation is 301,000 yuan, a 15.7% increase from last year. The economic benefits rank first in the national light industry, making new contributions to the national fiscal revenue.

### 玉溪卷烟厂今年上缴税利逾15亿
钱江
1989-12-13
第1版(要闻)
专栏：

    玉溪卷烟厂今年上缴税利逾15亿
    本报云南玉溪12月12日电　记者钱江报道：截至今天，云南玉溪卷烟厂已超额完成全年计划，生产卷烟110多万箱，总产量比去年增长4.8％。其中滤嘴烟72.5万箱，比上年增长25％。上缴税利总额超过15亿元，比上年净增3亿多元。人均创税利30.1万元，比上年增长15.7％。经济效益居全国轻工行业第一，为国家财政增收做出了新的贡献。
