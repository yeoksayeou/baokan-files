Title: Conflict Between Tamil Military Organizations
Author:
Date: 1989-12-15
Page: 4

Police in Colombo reported that on the morning of the 13th, the Tamil Eelam Liberation Tigers (LTTE) and the illegal Tamil National Army clashed violently in the eastern town of Batticaloa.

In the conflict, one LTTE fighter was killed, and the Tamil National Army lost eighteen officers.

Police said that the LTTE surrounded the town of Batticaloa on the night of the 12th and entered the town on the morning of the 13th.

The Tamil National Army is an illegal armed force established by the Eelam People's Revolutionary Liberation Front. The Eelam People's Revolutionary Liberation Front is the ruling party of the provisional Northern-Eastern Provincial Council.

### 斯泰米尔军事组织间发生冲突

1989-12-15
第4版(国际新闻)
专栏：

    斯泰米尔军事组织间发生冲突
    据科伦坡的警察说，斯里兰卡敌对的泰米尔伊拉姆猛虎解放组织和非法的泰米尔国民军十三日上午在东部地区的巴的加罗亚镇发生激烈冲突。
    在冲突中，泰米尔伊拉姆猛虎解放组织的一名战士被打死，泰米尔国民军有十八名军官身亡。
    警察说，泰米尔伊拉姆猛虎解放组织十二日晚包围了巴的加罗亚镇，十三日晨进入该镇。
    泰米尔国民军是由伊拉姆人民革命解放阵线建立的一支非法武装力量。伊拉姆人民革命解放阵线是临时成立的北方—东方省委员会的执政党。
