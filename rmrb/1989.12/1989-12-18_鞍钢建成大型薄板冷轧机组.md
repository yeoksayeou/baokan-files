Title: Anshan Iron and Steel Group Completes Large Thin Plate Cold Rolling Mill
Author: chen guang ming
Date: 1989-12-18
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Shenyang, December 16th.  (Reporter: Chen Guangming) A continuous cold rolling mill with an annual production capacity of 800,000 tons of thin cold-rolled steel plates was officially completed and put into operation today at Anshan Iron and Steel Group. This increases China's production capacity of this important industrial raw material by 20%.

This cold rolling mill is a second-hand set of equipment imported from the Federal Republic of Germany. After being modified and matched by Anshan Iron and Steel Group and the Beijing Automation Research Institute of the Ministry of Metallurgy, it is equipped with advanced facilities such as thyristor power supply, microcomputer control, hydraulic AGC, and quick roll change. The entire set of equipment has reached the world's 1980s level.

### 鞍钢建成大型薄板冷轧机组
陈光明
1989-12-18
第1版(要闻)
专栏：

    鞍钢建成大型薄板冷轧机组
    据新华社沈阳十二月十六日电　（记者陈光明）年产八十万吨冷轧薄钢板的连续冷轧机组，今天在鞍钢正式竣工投产。这使我国重要工业原料冷轧薄板的生产能力增加了百分之二十。
    这套冷轧机组是从联邦德国引进的二手设备，经鞍钢与冶金部北京自动化研究院等单位的改造配套后，具有可控硅供电、微机控制、液压AGC、快速换辊等先进装置，整个机组达到八十年代世界水平。
