Title: Shenzhen Huannan Pharmaceutical Co., Ltd. Opens
Author: Lǐ Méi, Jiāo Rán
Date: 1989-12-23
Edition: 2

A joint venture company aimed at promoting traditional Chinese medicine with modern science—Shenzhen Huannan Pharmaceutical Co., Ltd.—opened today in Shenzhen. National Committee of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference Vice Chairmen Wang Renhe and Wang Guangying attended the opening ceremony and cut the ribbon.

Shenzhen Huannan Pharmaceutical Co., Ltd. is a comprehensive pharmaceutical manufacturing and operating entity jointly established by the Chinese side and Hong Kong investors.

The "Chinese Divine Formula" series of capsule medicines produced by this company have shown significant effects in treating and preventing liver diseases, influenza, gonorrhea, syphilis, and other conditions.

### 深圳华南制药有限公司开业
李玫  焦然
1989-12-23
第2版(国内新闻)
专栏：四面八方

    深圳华南制药有限公司开业
    新华社深圳12月21日电（记者李玫、焦然）一家旨在用现代化科学光大祖国传统医学的合资企业——深圳华南制药有限公司今天在深圳开业。全国政协副主席王任重、王光英等为这家企业开业剪了彩。深圳华南制药有限公司是中方与港商等合资兴办的生产、经营综合性制药实体。
    这家公司生产的“中国神方”系列胶囊药，在治疗、预防肝病、流感、淋病、梅毒等方面的疗效显著。
