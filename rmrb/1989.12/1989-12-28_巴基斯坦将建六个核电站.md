Title: Pakistan to Build Six Nuclear Power Plants
Author:
Date: 1989-12-28
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency,

Pakistan is set to build six more nuclear power plants, according to a senior Pakistani official in Islamabad. Prime Minister Benazir Bhutto has approved the construction of these plants, which will have a total generating capacity of 2.4 million kilowatts, to address the country's severe power shortage.

The power shortage has been costing the nation $1 billion annually in economic losses.

Currently, Pakistan is in negotiations with France, China, and the Soviet Union regarding the construction of these nuclear power plants.

### 巴基斯坦将建六个核电站

1989-12-28
第4版(国际新闻)
专栏：

    巴基斯坦将建六个核电站
    据巴基斯坦高级官员最近在伊斯兰堡说，贝·布托总理已批准另建六个核电站，总发电量为二百四十万千瓦，以解决严重的电力短缺问题。
    因为供电短缺，每年给国家造成十亿美元的经济损失。
    目前，巴基斯坦正同法国、中国、苏联谈判建设核电站事宜。（据新华社）
