Title: President of the Supreme People's Court of China Meets Chief Justice of the Philippines
Author:
Date: 1989-12-06
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Manila, December 4th.

President of the Supreme People's Court of China, Ren Jianxin, today called on the Chief Justice of the Philippines, Fernan. The two had a sincere and friendly conversation.

Ren Jianxin expressed the hope to strengthen friendly exchanges and cooperation between judicial workers of China and the Philippines. Fernan praised the friendship between judicial workers of the two countries.

### 我最高法院院长会见菲律宾首席法官

1989-12-06
第4版(国际新闻)
专栏：

    我最高法院院长会见菲律宾首席法官
    新华社马尼拉12月4日电　中国最高人民法院院长任建新今天拜见了菲律宾最高法院首席法官费尔南，两人进行了诚挚友好的谈话。
    任建新表示，希望加强中菲两国司法工作者之间的友好往来和合作。费尔南赞扬了两国司法工作者之间的友谊。
