Title: Gorbachev Reports on Soviet-American Summit to Warsaw Pact
Author:
Date: 1989-12-06
Edition: 4

Leaders of the Warsaw Pact member countries met in Moscow on December 4 to hear Soviet leader Gorbachev report on his summit with U.S. President Bush in Malta.

Gorbachev said he and Bush had constructive discussions on all major international issues and bilateral relations between the Soviet Union and the United States. He revealed that recent changes in Eastern Europe were a significant topic in the summit. Both sides agreed that maintaining stability was crucial.

Gorbachev emphasized the importance of further reducing military confrontation and respecting existing European borders. He stated that realism dictated the need to maintain the existence of NATO and the Warsaw Pact in the foreseeable future. Under current conditions, Warsaw Pact member countries should cooperate closely and update the organization's activities and methods.

The leaders of Bulgaria, Hungary, East Germany, Poland, and Romania attended the summit, including Todor Zhivkov, János Kádár, Egon Krenz, Wojciech Jaruzelski, and Nicolae Ceaușescu, respectively.

On the same day, the leaders of Bulgaria, Hungary, East Germany, Poland, and the Soviet Union jointly issued a statement declaring that the 1968 invasion of Czechoslovakia by these five countries was an intervention in the internal affairs of a sovereign state and should be condemned. The Soviet government also issued a separate statement acknowledging that the decision to invade was a mistake.

### 戈尔巴乔夫向华约通报苏美首脑会晤情况  华约五国声明68年出兵捷是对主权国家内政的干涉

1989-12-06
第4版(国际新闻)
专栏：

    戈尔巴乔夫向华约通报苏美首脑会晤情况
    华约五国声明68年出兵捷是对主权国家内政的干涉
    新华社莫斯科12月4日电  华沙条约组织成员国领导人4日在莫斯科举行会晤，听取苏联领导人戈尔巴乔夫就他在马耳他同美国总统布什会晤情况作的通报。
    戈尔巴乔夫说，他同布什就所有重大国际问题建设性地交换了意见并讨论了苏美双边关系问题。他透露，东欧最近发生的变化在苏美首脑会晤中占有很大地位。苏美双方认为，保持稳定具有重要意义。
    戈尔巴乔夫指出，在会晤过程中，他强调了进一步降低军事对抗水平和尊重欧洲现存边界的重要性。他说，现实主义要求可预见的将来保持北约和华约组织。在当前条件下，华约各成员国应当紧密合作，并更新华约的活动形式和方法。
    保共中央总书记、保加利亚国务委员会主席姆拉德诺夫，匈牙利社会党主席涅尔什，民主德国国务委员会主席克伦茨，波兰总统雅鲁泽尔斯基，罗共总书记、共和国总统齐奥塞斯库，捷共中央总书记乌尔班内克率领本国代表团参加了这次首脑会晤。
    同一天，参加这次会晤的保、匈、民德、波兰和苏联5国领导人联合发表声明，宣布这5个国家1968年8月出兵捷克斯洛伐克是对一个主权国家内政的干涉，这一行动应当受到谴责。同时，苏联政府还就这一问题单独发表声明，承认“当时作出出兵的决定是错误的”。
