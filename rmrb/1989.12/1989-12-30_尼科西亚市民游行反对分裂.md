Title: Citizens of Nicosia Protest Against Division
Author:
Date: 1989-12-30
Edition: 4

Thousands of Greek Cypriots marched in a candlelight procession in Nicosia for one hour on the evening of the 28th to protest against the continued division of Nicosia.

The President of Cyprus, Vasiliou, as well as party leaders, government ministers, and members of parliament participated in this activity.

### 尼科西亚市民游行反对分裂

1989-12-30
第4版(国际新闻)
专栏：

    尼科西亚市民游行反对分裂
    数千名塞浦路斯希腊族人二十八日晚在尼科西亚举行一小时的秉烛示威游行，以抗议尼科西亚市继续处于分裂状态。
    塞浦路斯总统瓦西利乌以及政党领导人、政府部长、议会议员等参加了这次活动。
