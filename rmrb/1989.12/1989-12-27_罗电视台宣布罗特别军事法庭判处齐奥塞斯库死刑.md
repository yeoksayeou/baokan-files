Title: Romanian Television Announces Special Military Court Sentences Ceausescu to Death
Author:
Date: 1989-12-27
Edition: 4

Romanian television announced at 20:30 local time on December 25 (02:30 Beijing time on December 26) that the Romanian Special Military Court had sentenced Nicolae Ceausescu and Elena Ceausescu to death and that the sentence had been carried out.

### 罗电视台宣布罗特别军事法庭判处齐奥塞斯库死刑

1989-12-27
第4版(国际新闻)
专栏：

    罗电视台宣布罗特别军事法庭判处齐奥塞斯库死刑
    新华社布加勒斯特12月25日电　罗马尼亚电视台当地时间25日20时30分（北京时间26日凌晨2时30分）宣布，罗特别军事法庭25日判处尼·齐奥塞斯库和埃列娜·齐奥塞斯库死刑并已执行。
