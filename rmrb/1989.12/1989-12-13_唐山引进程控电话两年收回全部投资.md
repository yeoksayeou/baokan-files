Title: Tangshan Recovers Full Investment in Program Control Telephones in Two Years
Author: Xǔ Guìyuán Fāng Lìníng
Date: 1989-12-13
Edition: 2

Tangshan, Hebei Province, has raised 14 million yuan through social financing to import a 500-channel long-distance and 12,000-line urban digital program-controlled telephone exchange from Japan. After two years of expanding business opportunities and vigorously developing telephone users, the city has recovered 18.65 million yuan in funds, exceeding the total investment by 4.65 million yuan.

### 唐山引进程控电话两年收回全部投资
许贵元  方立宁
1989-12-13
第2版(国内新闻)
专栏：四面八方

    唐山引进程控电话两年收回全部投资
    新华社石家庄12月12日电　（通讯员许贵元、方立宁）河北省唐山市依靠社会集资1400万元，从日本引进的500路长途及市内1.2万门程控数字电话交换机，经过两年广辟业务门路，大力发展电话用户，到目前已收回资金1865万元，超过引进投资总额465万元。
