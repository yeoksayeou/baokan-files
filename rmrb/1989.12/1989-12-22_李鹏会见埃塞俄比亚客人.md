Author:
Date: 1989-12-22
Edition: 1

Li Peng Meets with Ethiopian Guests

BEIJING, Dec. 21 (Xinhua) — Premier Li Peng met with Ethiopian Deputy Prime Minister Wolde Yeshake Chiko and his delegation this afternoon in Zhongnanhai.

Li Peng welcomed Wolde Yeshake's visit. He said that China-Ethiopia relations are good. Mengistu Haile Mariam's successful visit to China last year has helped promote the further development of relations between the two countries. Li Peng also expressed his satisfaction with the achievements made by the Ethiopian government and people under the leadership of Mengistu Haile Mariam, and appreciated the Ethiopian government's efforts to improve relations with neighboring countries.

Wolde Yeshake thanked Li Peng for the reception and expressed his gratitude for the warm and friendly reception given to Mengistu Haile Mariam by the Chinese government last year. He also hoped to further develop friendly relations between Ethiopia and China.

### 李鹏会见埃塞俄比亚客人

1989-12-22
第1版(要闻)
专栏：

    李鹏会见埃塞俄比亚客人
    本报北京12月21日讯　国务院总理李鹏今天下午在中南海会见埃塞俄比亚副总理沃利·契科勒一行。
    李鹏对沃利的来访表示欢迎。他说，中埃两国的关系是好的，去年，门格斯图总统成功地访问我国，这有助于推动中埃两国关系的进一步发展。李鹏还对埃塞俄比亚政府和人民在门格斯图总统领导下取得的成绩表示高兴，对埃塞俄比亚政府致力于改善同周边国家的关系表示赞赏。
    沃利对李鹏总理的接见，对中国政府去年热情友好地接待门格斯图总统表示感谢，并希望进一步发展埃中两国的友好关系。
