Title: The Political Executive Committee of the Romanian Communist Party Holds a Meeting and Elects an Eight-Member Standing Bureau
Author:
Date: 1989-12-03
Edition: 4

The Political Executive Committee of the Romanian Communist Party held a meeting today and elected an eight-member Standing Bureau.

According to a report by the Romanian News Agency, the Political Executive Committee of the Romanian Communist Party held a meeting today and elected an eight-member Standing Bureau.

The Standing Bureau consists of Nicolae Ceaușescu, Constantin Dăscălescu, Elena Ceaușescu, Manea Mănescu, Gheorghe Rădulescu, Emil Bob, Gheorghe Oprea, and Ion Dincă.

The Executive Committee also discussed the measures to be taken to complete the December 1989 and the full-year plan for 1989, as well as the draft of the unified national economic and social development plan for 1990.

### 罗共中央政治执委会举行会议  选出由8人组成的常设局

1989-12-03
第4版(国际新闻)
专栏：

    罗共中央政治执委会举行会议
    选出由8人组成的常设局
    新华社布加勒斯特12月1日电　据罗通社报道，罗共中央政治执委会今天举行会议，选出了由8人组成的常设局。
    常设局由尼·齐奥塞斯库、康·德斯克列斯库、埃列娜·齐奥塞斯库、马·曼内斯库、格·勒杜列斯库、埃·博布、格·奥普雷亚和杨·丁卡组成。
    执委会还讨论了为完成今年12月份和1989年全年计划应采取的措施及1990年国家经济和社会发展统一计划草案。
