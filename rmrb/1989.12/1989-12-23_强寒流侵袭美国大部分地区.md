Title: A Severe Cold Wave Sweeps Across Most of the United States
Author:
Date: 1989-12-23
Page: 4

A severe cold wave from the Arctic struck most of the United States on the 21st. The temperature in the central and northern parts of Montana and Chicago dropped sharply to -40°C and -25°C, respectively.

In the Washington D.C. area, the temperature also fell to -15°C, the lowest in decades. It is reported that three people have died from the cold.

According to meteorological estimates, the low temperatures will continue for another two days.

### 强寒流侵袭美国大部分地区

1989-12-23
第4版(国际新闻)
专栏：

    强寒流侵袭美国大部分地区
    来自北极的一股特大寒流二十一日侵袭了美国大部分地区，蒙大拿州中北部和芝加哥的气温分别骤降到摄氏零下四十度和零下二十五度。
    首都华盛顿地区的气温二十一日也降到摄氏零下十五度，为几十年来的最低纪录。据报道，已有三人被冻死。
    据气象部门估计，低温还将持续两天。
                                      （本报专电）
