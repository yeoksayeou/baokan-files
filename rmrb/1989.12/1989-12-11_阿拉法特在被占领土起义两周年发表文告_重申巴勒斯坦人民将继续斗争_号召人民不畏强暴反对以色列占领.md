Title: Arafat Issues Statement on the Second Anniversary of the Uprising in the Occupied Territories
Author:
Date: 1989-12-11
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Tunis, December 9th

According to the Palestinian News Agency, on the occasion of the second anniversary of the uprising by Palestinians in the occupied territories, Palestinian President Yasser Arafat issued a statement to the Palestinian people, reaffirming that the Palestinian uprising will continue until the end of Israeli occupation.

Arafat called on the Palestinian uprising fighters in the occupied territories to strike even harder against the Israeli invaders, resolutely stop Israeli aggression, and make active efforts to develop the national economy.

Arafat demanded that the Palestinian uprising fighters strictly adhere to iron discipline. He believed that the Palestinian uprising is not a rootless tree, but a just revolutionary action.

Xinhua News Agency, Baghdad, December 9th

Palestinian President Yasser Arafat today emphasized at a mass rally held at the University of Mustansiriya in Iraq to commemorate the second anniversary of the Palestinian uprising in the occupied territories that the Palestinian people will not be deterred by any pressure and will continue to fight against Israeli occupation.

Arafat told the thousands of Palestinians present that since the outbreak of the Palestinian uprising in the occupied territories in December 1987, the Israeli army has killed 1,003 Palestinians, injured 53,000, and detained 64,000.

In his speech, Arafat criticized the five-point Middle East peace plan proposed by U.S. Secretary of State Baker in October. He pointed out, "Baker's plan cannot bring peace." He stressed that only through the efforts of the Palestinians themselves can peace be achieved.

Today, Iraqi Minister of Culture and Information Jassim also spoke at the rally, reaffirming the Iraqi government's support for the PLO and the Palestinian uprising.

### 阿拉法特在被占领土起义两周年发表文告  重申巴勒斯坦人民将继续斗争  号召人民不畏强暴反对以色列占领

1989-12-11
第4版(国际新闻)
专栏：

    阿拉法特在被占领土起义两周年发表文告
    重申巴勒斯坦人民将继续斗争
    号召人民不畏强暴反对以色列占领
    新华社突尼斯12月9日电  据巴勒斯坦通讯社报道，在纪念被占领土上的巴勒斯坦人起义两周年之际，巴勒斯坦总统阿拉法特发表告巴勒斯坦人民书，重申巴勒斯坦人起义斗争将继续进行下去，直到结束以色列的占领。
    阿拉法特呼吁被占领土上的巴勒斯坦起义者更加严厉地打击以色列侵略者，坚决制止以色列的侵略行径，为发展民族经济作出积极的努力。
    阿拉法特要求，巴勒斯坦起义者严守铁一般的纪律。他认为，巴勒斯坦起义斗争不是无根之木，而是正当的革命行动。
    新华社巴格达12月9日电巴勒斯坦国总统阿拉法特今天在伊拉克穆斯坦西里亚大学召开的纪念被占领土巴勒斯坦人民起义两周年的群众大会上强调，巴勒斯坦人民将不畏各种压力，坚持反对以色列占领的斗争。
    阿拉法特对与会的数千名巴勒斯坦人说，自1987年12月被占领土巴勒斯坦起义以来，以色列军杀害了1003名巴勒斯坦人，打伤53000人，拘禁64000人。
    阿拉法特在讲话中批评了美国国务卿贝克10月提出的中东和平5点计划。他指出，“贝克的计划不可能赢得和平。”他强调，只有通过巴勒斯坦人的自身努力，才能实现和平。
    今天，伊拉克文化和新闻部长贾西姆也在大会上发言，重申伊拉克政府对巴解组织和巴勒斯坦人民起义的支持。
