Title: Lhasa Publicly Judges Eleven Counter-Revolutionary Criminals
Author: Lú Xiǎofēi
Date: 1989-12-02
Edition: 3

This afternoon, the Lhasa Intermediate People's Court held a public trial in the Lhasa Grand Hall, sentencing 11 individuals involved in counter-revolutionary groups, counter-revolutionary propaganda, and espionage cases. Over 1300 local residents, monks, and nuns from temples attended the trial as spectators.

The defendants Awang Pingqiong, Awang Weise, Gesang Tuduo, and Jiangbai Qiangjiu began conspiring together in August 1988. They drafted, printed, and distributed counter-revolutionary leaflets advocating for "Tibet independence," attacking the socialist system, and inciting the overthrow of the people's democratic government. In January 1989, defendants Awang Pingqiong and Awang Weise planned to establish a reactionary organization dedicated to the cause of "Tibet independence." On January 15, they held a founding meeting at Awang Pingqiong's quarters. The organization consisted of 10 members, all monks from the Jokhang Temple. Besides the aforementioned four defendants, the members included Awang Jianzang, Jiangbai Mulang, Jiangbai Ciren, Jiangbai Luose, Awang Renqin, and Awang Gongga.

The above facts are well-documented, and the defendants have confessed. The Lhasa Intermediate People's Court sentenced Awang Pingqiong, Awang Weise, Gesang Tuduo, Jiangbai Qiangjiu, and Awang Jianzang to imprisonment ranging from 17 to 19 years, with a deprivation of political rights for 5 years.

In another case, defendant Dunzhu Duoji printed and prepared to distribute counter-revolutionary leaflets advocating for "Tibet independence." He was sentenced to 5 years of imprisonment and a deprivation of political rights for 2 years.

### 拉萨公开宣判十一名反革命案犯
卢小飞
1989-12-02
第3版(综合)
专栏：

    拉萨公开宣判十一名反革命案犯
    本报拉萨11月30日电　记者卢小飞报道：今天下午，拉萨市中级人民法院在拉萨市大礼堂，依法对涉及反革命集团、反革命宣传煽动和特务案的11人进行公开宣判。拉萨市民及寺院僧、尼1300多人旁听。
    被告阿旺平穷、阿旺维色、格桑土多、江白强久自1988年8月起，纠合在一起，起草、油印、散发了以鼓吹“西藏独立”、攻击社会主义制度、煽动推翻人民民主政权为内容的反革命传单。1989年元月，被告阿旺平穷、阿旺维色预谋成立一个从事“西藏独立”事业的反动组织，并于元月15日在阿旺平穷的僧舍召开成立大会。该组织10名成员均为哲蚌寺僧侣，除上述4名被告外，还有阿旺坚赞、江白木朗、江白次仁、江白洛色、阿旺仁钦、阿旺贡嘎。
    上述事实，证据确凿，被告人供认不讳。拉萨市中级人民法院依法分别判处阿旺平穷、阿旺维色、格桑土多、江白强久、阿旺坚赞有期徒刑17年到19年，剥夺政治权利5年。
    另一案被告顿珠多吉油印并准备散发鼓吹“西藏独立”的反革命传单，被判处有期徒刑5年，剥夺政治权利2年。
