Title: Arafat Meets with Chinese Ambassador
Author:
Date: 1989-12-04
Edition: 4

    Arafat Meets with Chinese Ambassador

    Xinhua News Agency, Tunis, December 2—Palestinian President Yasser Arafat met with Chinese Ambassador to Tunisia Zhu Yinglu on December 2 in Tunis and briefed him on the latest developments in the Middle East and the Palestinian situation.

    During the meeting, Zhu Yinglu reaffirmed China's government's stance of supporting the PLO's proactive peace initiatives and advocating for a comprehensive and just resolution to the Palestinian issue in international forums.

### 阿拉法特接见我大使

1989-12-04
第4版(国际新闻)
专栏：

    阿拉法特接见我大使
    新华社突尼斯12月2日电　巴勒斯坦国总统阿拉法特2日在突尼斯接见了中国驻突尼斯大使朱应鹿，并向他通报了中东和巴勒斯坦形势的最新发展情况。
    在接见中，朱应鹿重申了中国政府在国际场合支持巴解组织的主动和平行动和推动巴勒斯坦问题全面公正解决的立场。
