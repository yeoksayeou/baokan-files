Title: Vice Premier Yao Yilin Meets with Thai Guests
Author:
Date: 1989-12-14
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, December 13th.

Vice Premier Yao Yilin met with a delegation from the Thai Charoen Pokphand Group led by its chairman, Sithiporn Charoen, this afternoon at the Great Hall of the People.

Yao Yilin welcomed the investment by the Charoen Pokphand Group in China.

The Charoen Pokphand Group delegation arrived in Beijing on December 12th at the invitation of the Chinese People's Association for Friendship with Foreign Countries to visit China.

### 姚依林会见泰国客人

1989-12-14
第1版(要闻)
专栏：

    姚依林会见泰国客人
    新华社北京12月13日电　国务院副总理姚依林今天下午在人民大会堂会见了由董事长谢国民率领的泰国正大集团访华团。
    姚依林对泰国正大集团在华投资表示欢迎。
    正大集团访华团应中国国际友好联络会邀请于12日到京，对中国进行访问。
