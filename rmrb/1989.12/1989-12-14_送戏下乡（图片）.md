Title: Bringing Performances to the Countryside
Author: Zhang Yaxin
Date: 1989-12-14
Page: 1

The Xiangyang County Yu Opera Troupe in Hubei Province has long been performing in factories and rural areas. This year alone, they have performed over 200 shows in rural areas. The image shows national first-class performer Li Xihua singing for the farmers working on the water conservancy project.

		Photographed by People's Daily reporter Zhang Yaxin.
