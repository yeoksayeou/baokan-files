Title: British Government Issues White Paper Stating It Will Continue Dialogue with China on Hong Kong Issue
Author:
Date: 1989-12-07
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, London

The British government's white paper released on December 1 states that the UK will continue to engage in dialogue with China regarding the Hong Kong issue.

The white paper asserts that the British government is unaware of any serious proposition suggesting that abandoning the 1984 Sino-British Joint Declaration on the return of Hong Kong to China would provide a better future for Hong Kong. The white paper also defends the UK's stance on repatriating the current Vietnamese boat people in Hong Kong, stating that this position has received international recognition.

### 英政府发表白皮书说  将同我继续就香港问题对话

1989-12-07
第4版(国际新闻)
专栏：

    英政府发表白皮书说
    将同我继续就香港问题对话
    新华社伦敦电　英国政府12月1日发表的白皮书说，英国将继续就香港问题同中国保持对话。
    白皮书说，英政府不知道有这样一种认真的说法，即只有放弃中英1984年签署的关于将香港归还中国的联合声明，香港才能有更好的前途。白皮书还对英国坚持遣返目前在港的越南船民回国的立场进行了辩护，说英国的这一立场得到了国际社会的认可。
