Title: Northwest Airlines of China and Northwest Administration Bureau of Civil Aviation Officially Established
Author: Zhu Jingruo
Date: 1989-12-06
Edition: 2

Northwest Airlines of China and Northwest Administration Bureau of Civil Aviation Officially Established

Xian, December 5 (Xinhua) - Northwest Airlines of China and the Northwest Administration Bureau of Civil Aviation, after more than eight months of operation, were officially established today in Xian.

Northwest Airlines of China is the latest state-owned backbone airline following the establishment of Southwest Airlines, Eastern Airlines, and Air China. It operates 43 domestic routes connecting more than 30 cities including Beijing, Shanghai, Guangzhou, and Kunming, as well as one regional route.

The Northwest Administration Bureau of Civil Aviation will oversee the management of Northwest Airlines of China. Their establishment is part of the reform of the Civil Aviation Administration of China.

### 中国西北航空公司  民航西北管理局正式成立
朱竞若
1989-12-06
第2版(国内新闻)
专栏：四面八方

    中国西北航空公司  民航西北管理局正式成立
    本报西安12月5日电　记者朱竞若报道：中国西北航空公司和中国民航西北管理局，经过8个多月的运转，今天在西安正式成立。
    中国西北航空公司是继中国西南航空公司、东方航空公司、中国国际航空公司之后的又一国家骨干航空公司，它经营由西北通达北京、上海、广州、昆明等30多个城市的43条航线和一条地区航线。
    中国民航西北管理局对中国西北航空公司实施行业管理，它们的组建是中国民航局体制改革的一个组成部分。
