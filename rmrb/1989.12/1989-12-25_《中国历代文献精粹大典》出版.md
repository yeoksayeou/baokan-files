Title: The Publication of "Compendium of Chinese Historical Documents"
Author:
Date: 1989-12-25
Edition: 6

The "Compendium of Chinese Historical Documents" has been published. This book is a comprehensive large-scale reference work that compiles the essence of various historical documents from different dynasties. The content is quite rich, with detailed and extensive materials. It includes five sections: politics, classics and history, literature and art, science and technology, and biographies. The book is published by Learning Garden Press.
