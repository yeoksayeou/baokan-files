Title: Yixing Ceramics Exports Increase
Author: Jiang Yaoji
Date: 1989-12-06
Edition: 2

Yixing Ceramics Exports Increase

	People's Daily reports that Jiangsu Yixing Ceramics Company has actively adjusted its product structure and expanded its overseas markets. This year, its foreign trade exports have shown a continuous growth trend. By the end of October, the export value reached 14 million yuan, an increase of 80 percent compared to the same period last year.

### 宜兴陶瓷出口增大
蒋尧基
1989-12-06
第2版(国内新闻)
专栏：四面八方

    宜兴陶瓷出口增大
    本报讯　江苏宜兴陶瓷公司积极调整产品结构，拓宽国外市场，今年外贸出口出现持续增长的好势头。截至十月底，外贸出口额达一千四百万元，比去年同期增长百分之八十。　（蒋尧基）
