Title: Egypt Discovers Ancient Egyptian Stone Block
Author:
Date: 1989-12-14
Edition: 7

Egyptian archaeologists recently unearthed a black stone block bearing the likeness of the ancient Egyptian Pharaoh Ramesses II in the southern part of the capital Cairo.

The stone block, which dates back over 3,200 years, is 160 centimeters long, 60 centimeters wide, and 10 to 20 centimeters thick, weighing over 3 tons.

At the same excavation site, the Egyptian archaeological team also discovered 13 stone sarcophagi, each weighing over 40 tons.

### 埃及发现古埃及岩石块

1989-12-14
第7版(国际专页)
专栏：

    埃及发现古埃及岩石块
    埃及考古学家最近在首都开罗城南发掘出一块刻有古埃及拉美西斯二世头像的黑色岩石。
    这块3200多年以前的岩石长160厘米，宽60厘米，厚10至20厘米，重量超过3吨。
    在同一发掘地点，埃及考古队还发现了13具石棺，其中每具石棺重量都超过40吨。（华）
