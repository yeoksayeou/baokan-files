Title: Jiangsu's Silk Cocoon Production and Procurement Both Hit Record Highs
Author: Sun Can
Date: 1989-12-12
Edition: 1

Jiangsu's silk cocoon production and procurement both hit record highs this year. According to the Xinhua News Agency in Nanjing on December 11, Jiangsu Province's total silk cocoon production is expected to reach 2.1 million piculs, an increase of 15% over last year. This marks the 11th consecutive year of increased production since 1979, and the first time the province has surpassed the 2 million piculs mark. This year, the province's silk cocoon procurement is expected to reach 1.85 to 1.9 million piculs, an increase of about 20% over last year.

### 江苏蚕茧产量收购量均创纪录
孙参
1989-12-12
第1版(要闻)
专栏：

    江苏蚕茧产量收购量均创纪录
    据新华社南京12月11日电　（记者孙参）江苏省今年蚕茧产量及收购量创历史最高纪录。据省丝绸总公司提供的数字，全省蚕茧总产量预计可达210万担，比去年增产15％。这是江苏蚕茧自1979年以来连续第11年增产，并首次突破200万担大关。今年预计全省蚕茧收购量可达185—190万担，比去年增加20％左右。
