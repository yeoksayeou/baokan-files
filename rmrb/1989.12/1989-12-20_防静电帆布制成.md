Title: Anti-Static Canvas Fabric Developed
Author: Wan Qiguo Li Rongzu
Date: 1989-12-20
Edition: 2

    Anti-static canvas fabric, a crucial sealing material for dry coal gas storage tanks, has been successfully developed by the Hubei Sand Market Cotton Mill. This innovation fills a gap in domestic technology. Anti-static canvas fabric is not only an ideal protective material for the coal gas industry but also for various production equipment handling flammable gases, liquids, and powders.

### 防静电帆布制成
万启国　李荣祖
1989-12-20
第2版(国内新闻)
专栏：四面八方

    防静电帆布制成
    本报讯　干式煤气贮罐所急需的密封材料——防静电帆布在湖北沙市棉织厂研制成功，填补了国内一项空白。防静电帆布不仅是煤气工业理想的防护材料，也是各种可燃气体、可燃液体、可燃粉末生产设备的防护材料。　（万启国　李荣祖）
