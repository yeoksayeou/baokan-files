Title: The Kaifeng Yellow River Highway Bridge Opens to Traffic
Author: Yin Daofu
Date: 1989-12-02
Edition: 2

The Kaifeng Yellow River Highway Bridge opened to traffic today. President Yang Shangkun inscribed the name of the bridge.

The completion of this bridge allows National Highway 106, which runs from Beijing to Guangzhou, to overcome the obstacle of the Yellow River, facilitating north-south connectivity. This will play an important role in the development of China's economy.

The Kaifeng Yellow River Highway Bridge is the second-longest highway bridge in China. It is located 20 kilometers east of Kaifeng, with a total length of 4,475 meters and 108 spans. The bridge has a width of 18.5 meters, with 12.3 meters designated for motor vehicles and 3.1 meters on each side for non-motor vehicles and pedestrians.

### 开封黄河公路大桥通车
尹道夫
1989-12-02
第2版(要闻)
专栏：四面八方

    开封黄河公路大桥通车
    新华社郑州12月1日电　（记者尹道夫）我国公路第二座长桥——河南开封黄河公路大桥今天建成通车。国家主席杨尚昆为大桥题了桥名。
    这座大桥建成通车后，使国道由北京至广州106公路从此克服黄河天险障碍，南北沟通，对发展我国经济将起重要作用。
    开封黄河公路大桥的规模仅次于郑州黄河公路大桥。它位于开封东20公里处，全长4475米，有108孔。这座大桥桥面宽18.5米，其中机动车道12.3米，两侧各3.1米为非机动车和人行道。
