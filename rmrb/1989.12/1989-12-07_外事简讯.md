Title: Foreign Affairs Brief
Author:
Date: 1989-12-07
Edition: 2

	State Councilor and Minister of National Defense Qin Jiwei hosted a dinner for a delegation from the Korean People's Army Political Bureau led by Lieutenant General Yuan Mingjun on the evening of December 6 in Beijing.

	The Nepalese economic and trade delegation concluded its six-day visit to Tibet and departed from Lhasa on December 6. The Nepalese delegation and the Tibetan delegation held talks on issues such as import and export goods, bilateral border trade meetings, opening additional border trade ports, transit transportation of commercial goods, and economic and tourism cooperation. Both sides reached a consensus. Both sides agreed to further consolidate and develop the existing trade exchanges and economic cooperation on the basis of mutual benefit.

	On the evening of December 6, Han Xu, President of the Chinese People's Association for Friendship with Foreign Countries, hosted a dinner at the Great Hall of the People to welcome a delegation from the American International Cooperation Committee led by Mrs. Chen Xiangmei. The delegation arrived in Beijing on December 5 at the invitation of the Chinese People's Association for Friendship with Foreign Countries. The guests will also visit Shanghai, Fuzhou, Xiamen, and Guangzhou.

### 外事简讯

1989-12-07
第2版(国内新闻)
专栏：

    外事简讯
    ▲国务委员兼国防部部长秦基伟6日晚上在北京会见并宴请了由总政治局副局长袁明均少将率领的朝鲜人民军政工代表团。
    ▲尼泊尔王国经济贸易代表团结束了对西藏为期6天的访问，于6日离开拉萨回国。尼泊尔代表团与西藏代表团就进出口商品、双方边境地区官员贸易会晤问题、增设边境贸易开放口岸、商业货物车辆过境运输和双方经济合作和旅游合作等问题进行了会谈并达成了一致协议。双方同意在互利的基础上，进一步巩固和发展现行的贸易往来和经济合作。
    ▲中国人民对外友好协会会长韩叙6日晚上在人民大会堂举行宴会，欢迎由陈香梅女士率领的美国国际合作委员会代表团。陈香梅一行是应对外友协邀请于5日抵京的。客人们还将去上海、福州、厦门和广州等地访问。　（新华社）
