Title: The 14th Meeting of the Sino-British Joint Liaison Group
Author:
Date: 1989-12-06
Edition: 1

The 14th meeting of the Sino-British Joint Liaison Group was held this morning at 9:30 AM at the High Cloud Building in Golden, Hong Kong Island. The meeting is expected to last four days.

The Chinese representatives attending the meeting today include Chief Representative Ke Zaishuo, Resident Representative Zheng Weirong, Deputy Director of the Xinhua News Agency Hong Kong Branch Qiao Zonghui, Resident Representative Wang Jiaji, and Counselor Zhang Youyun of the Chinese Embassy in the UK. The British representatives include Chief Representative Gordon S. Young, Permanent Secretary of the Hong Kong Government Koo Pong, Head of the Hong Kong Department of the British Foreign Office Paul Boyn, Political Advisor to the Hong Kong Government William O'Brien, and Counselor of the British Representative Office of the Liaison Group, Mark D. Smith.

Governor Wilson, Baron of Cowley, hosted a dinner last night at the Governor's Residence to entertain the members of the Sino-British Joint Liaison Group from both sides.

### 中英联合联络小组举行第十四次会议

1989-12-06
第1版(要闻)
专栏：

    中英联合联络小组举行第十四次会议
    本报香港12月5日电　中英联合联络小组第14次会议于今天上午9时30分在港岛金钟高云楼举行。会议预计举行4天。
    今天出席会议的中方成员有首席代表柯在铄、常驻代表郑伟荣、新华社香港分社副社长乔宗淮、常驻代表王家骥和中国驻英使馆参赞张幼云。英方成员有首席代表高德年、港府常务司屈珩、英国外交部香港司主管鲍雅伦、港府政治顾问欧威廉及联络小组英方代表处参赞马德思。
    港督卫奕信爵士昨晚在港督府设宴款待中英联合联络小组中英双方成员。
