Title: China Proposes Tasks for Next Year's Economic Special UN General Assembly
Author: Zhao Yingnan
Date: 1989-12-05
Edition: 4

The Chinese representative, Wang Baoliu, today at the 44th UN General Assembly's Second Committee meeting, pointed out that the main task of the economic special session to be held next April should be to engage in global consultations on the revitalization and development of the economies of developing countries, thereby creating a more favorable international environment.

Wang Baoliu said that since the 1980s, the growth of the world economy has mainly come from developed countries, while most developing countries have experienced a "lost decade" of development, and the economic gap between them and the developed countries has further widened.

Wang Baoliu hopes that the preparatory committee for the special session will quickly reach a consensus on the outline of the final document for the conference. To this end, he proposed three points of view from the Chinese delegation:

1. The profound changes in the world economy since the 1980s and their impact, opportunities, and challenges for the economic growth and development of developing countries in the 1990s should be seriously studied and analyzed to promote their economic development.

2. Given the increasing interdependence of national economies and the interconnection of various economic issues due to the evolution of the world economy, the special session should focus on how to address issues in the areas of debt, commodities, trade, currency, and finance from a comprehensive perspective.

3. The final document of the special session should reflect the international community's commitment to strengthening international economic cooperation, particularly to revitalizing the economic growth and development of developing countries, and the broad, clear, and practical policies and measures that should be taken to achieve this goal.

Wang Baoliu emphasized that to achieve the above objectives, the major developed countries should show sufficient political will and adopt flexible and realistic approaches.

### 我代表提出明年经济特别联大任务  为发展中国家经济振兴创造有利环境
赵颖楠
1989-12-05
第4版(国际新闻)
专栏：

    我代表提出明年经济特别联大任务
    为发展中国家经济振兴创造有利环境
    新华社联合国11月24日电  （记者赵颖楠）中国代表王保流今天在44届联大第二委员会会议上发言时指出，将于明年4月召开的经济特别联大的中心任务应该是就发展中国家的经济振兴与发展进行全球性协商，以创造一个更加有利的国际环境。
    王保流说，80年代以来世界经济的增长主要来自发达国家，而大多数发展中国家却度过了“失去发展的十年”，同发达国家之间的经济差距进一步扩大了。
    王保流希望特别联大筹委会尽快就会议最后文件的提纲协商并达成一致。为此，他提出了中国代表团的三点意见：
    1，应该认真研究和分析80年代以来世界经济的深刻演变对90年代发展中国家的经济增长和发展的影响、机遇以及挑战，以促进它们的经济发展。
    2，鉴于世界经济的演变使各国经济之间的联系、各类经济问题之间的联系更加紧密，特别联大应集中考虑如何从综合的高度来解决债务、商品、贸易、货币和金融等领域的问题。
    3，特别联大的最后文件应集中反映国际社会对加强国际经济合作、尤其是振兴发展中国家经济增长和发展的承诺，以及为达到这一目标所应采取的广泛、明确而切合实际的政策和措施。
    王保流强调，为达到上述目的，主要发达国家应该显示出足够的政治意愿，采取灵活和现实的做法。
