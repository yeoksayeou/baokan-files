Title: Books Delivered to the Military Camp
Author: Dong Lizhong
Date: 1989-12-08
Page: 1

The People's Liberation Army unit is vigorously promoting the study of Marxism-Leninism and Mao Zedong Thought, and has formulated measures to guide soldiers in their studies. The image shows military leaders and staff members delivering "Selected Works of Mao Zedong" and "Selected Works of Deng Xiaoping" to the soldiers on December 2.
