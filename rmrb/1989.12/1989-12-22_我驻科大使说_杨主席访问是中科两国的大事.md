Title: Chinese Ambassador to Kuwait Says Yang's Visit is a Major Event for China and Kuwait
Author:
Date: 1989-12-22
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Kuwait, December 18th.

Chinese Ambassador to Kuwait Guan Zihuai today spoke to the local press about Chairman Yang Shangkun's visit to Kuwait, highly praising the Sino-Kuwaiti friendship and the friendly cooperative relations between the two countries.

Ambassador Guan said that Chairman Yang will officially visit Kuwait from December 24th to 26th. This is the first visit by a Chinese head of state to Kuwait since the two countries established diplomatic relations in 1971, marking a significant event in the history of Sino-Kuwaiti relations.

During the visit, Chairman Yang will exchange views with Kuwait's Emir (Head of State) Sheikh Jaber Al-Ahmad Al-Jaber Al-Sabah on the development of bilateral relations and international issues of mutual concern.

### 我驻科大使说  杨主席访问是中科两国的大事

1989-12-22
第4版(国际新闻)
专栏：

    我驻科大使说
    杨主席访问是中科两国的大事
    新华社科威特12月18日电　中国驻科威特大使管子怀今天在这里就杨尚昆主席访问科威特向当地报界发表谈话，高度赞扬了中科友谊和两国的友好合作关系。
    管大使说，杨主席将于12月24日至26日正式访问科威特。这是两国1971年建交以来中国第一位国家主席访问科威特，是中科关系中具有历史意义的一件大事。
    访问期间，杨主席将同科威特埃米尔（国家元首）萨巴赫殿下就发展双边关系和共同关心的国际问题广泛交换意见。
