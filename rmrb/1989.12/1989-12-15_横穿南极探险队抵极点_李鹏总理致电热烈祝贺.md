Title: Chinese Expedition Team Reaches the South Pole
Author:
Date: 1989-12-15
Edition: 1

Chinese Expedition Team Reaches the South Pole
Li Peng Premier Sends Congratulatory Message

Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, December 14th: Premier Li Peng of the People's Republic of China sent a telegram on the 13th to the International Trans-Antarctic Expedition Team, expressing warm congratulations on their arrival at the South Pole. The full text of the telegram is as follows:

International Trans-Antarctic Expedition Team:

I am delighted to hear that, with your courage, tenacity, and fearless spirit of self-sacrifice, you have overcome difficulties and trekked across the Antarctic continent on foot, reaching the South Pole. On behalf of the Chinese government, I extend my warmest congratulations and heartfelt greetings to you! Your endeavor is a great feat in the history of human Antarctic exploration. I wish you continued unity, cooperation, and strenuous efforts to successfully reach your final destination. May you contribute to humanity's understanding of Antarctica, to the preservation of its environment from pollution, and to the peaceful use of the continent.

### 横穿南极探险队抵极点  李鹏总理致电热烈祝贺

1989-12-15
第1版(要闻)
专栏：

    横穿南极探险队抵极点
    李鹏总理致电热烈祝贺
    新华社北京12月14日电  中华人民共和国国务院总理李鹏，13日打电报给国际横穿南极大陆科学探险队，热烈祝贺探险队到达南极点。电报全文如下：横穿南极探险队：
    欣闻你们以英勇顽强和大无畏的献身精神，战胜困难，徒步横穿南极大陆，已经到达了南极点。我谨代表中国政府向你们表示热烈的祝贺和衷心的问候！你们的活动堪称人类南极考察史上的一大壮举，预祝你们团结合作，努力拚搏，胜利地到达最终目的地，为人类认识南极、维护南极环境不被污染和和平利用南极做出贡献。
