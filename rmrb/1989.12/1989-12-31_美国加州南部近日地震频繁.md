Title: Frequent Earthquakes in Southern California
Author:
Date: 1989-12-31
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency

	Southern California has experienced ten small earthquakes in the past two days, leading experts to worry about the possibility of a major earthquake in the region.

	California, located on the eastern shore of the Pacific Ocean, is a well-known earthquake zone. Over the past century, it has experienced more than ten major earthquakes. This year on October 17, a 7.1 magnitude earthquake struck San Francisco, causing the most severe damage in the city's history. The recent ten small earthquakes occurred near the San Jacinto Fault. Experts predict that this fault line may experience a 6.5 to 7.0 magnitude earthquake by the year 2018.

### 美国加州南部近日地震频繁

1989-12-31
第4版(国际新闻)
专栏：

    美国加州南部近日地震频繁
    美国加利福尼亚州南部在过去两天内发生十次小地震，地震专家因此担心在那里可能发生一次大地震。
    处于太平洋东岸的加州是世界上著名的地震带，近百年来发生过十余次大地震。今年十月十七日在旧金山发生了历史上损失最惨重的七点一级大地震。近日的十次小震发生于圣哈辛托地震带附近。专家们预测，该地震带可能在二○一八年爆发里氏六点五级至七级的大地震。（据新华社）
