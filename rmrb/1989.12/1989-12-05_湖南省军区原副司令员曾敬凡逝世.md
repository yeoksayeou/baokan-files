Title: Former Deputy Commander of Hunan Provincial Military Region, Zeng Jingfan, Passes Away
Author:
Date: 1989-12-05
Edition: 2

Former Deputy Commander of Hunan Provincial Military Region, Zeng Jingfan, passed away due to illness on October 7 in Changsha, aged 74.

Zeng Jingfan was a native of Cha'ling County, Hunan Province. He joined the revolution in 1927, became a member of the Communist Youth League of China in 1929, and joined the Communist Party of China in 1933. He held various positions including platoon leader, political instructor, regiment political commissar, division political department organization section chief, brigade commander, independent division commander, military sub-district commander, deputy corps commander, and deputy military region commander. In 1955, he was awarded the rank of Major General.

### 湖南省军区原副司令员曾敬凡逝世

1989-12-05
第2版(国内新闻)
专栏：

    湖南省军区原副司令员曾敬凡逝世
    新华社长沙12月3日电　湖南省军区原副司令员曾敬凡同志因病于10月7日在长沙逝世，终年74岁。
    曾敬凡同志是湖南省茶陵县人。1927年参加革命，1929年加入中国共产主义青年团，1933年转为中国共产党党员。历任排长、政治指导员、团政委、旅政治部组织科长、支队司令员、独立师师长、军分区司令员、副军长兼参谋长等职。1955年被授予少将军衔。
