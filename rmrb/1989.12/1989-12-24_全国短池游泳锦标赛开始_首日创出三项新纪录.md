Title: National Short Course Swimming Championships Begin
Author: Tang Yuqin
Date: 1989-12-24
Edition: 3

The National Short Course Swimming Championships began today at the heated swimming pool in Xuhui District. Two individuals and two teams broke three national short course swimming records on the first day.

In the men's 100-meter breaststroke, Anhui's Chen Jianhong set a new national record with a time of 1:00.68. Shen Jianqiang narrowly missed breaking his own national record of 22.93 seconds in the men's 50-meter freestyle final, finishing with a time of 22.75 seconds. The Shanghai and Jiangsu teams both broke the national record in the women's 4x50-meter medley relay, with times of 1:57.77 and 1:58.82, respectively. The other champions were:

Women's 50-meter breaststroke: Hebei's Huang Xiaomin, 32.26 seconds;
Women's 800-meter freestyle: Hebei's Yan Ming, 8:52.88;
Women's 100-meter freestyle: Beijing's Zhang Chunling, 56.73 seconds;
Women's 400-meter individual medley: Guangdong's Chen Xiaorong, 5:00.56;
Men's 200-meter freestyle: Hebei's Wang Dali, 1:50.54;
Men's 200-meter backstroke: Hebei's Wei Xing, 2:06.52.

### 全国短池游泳锦标赛开始  首日创出三项新纪录
唐玉琴
1989-12-24
第3版(综合)
专栏：体育

    全国短池游泳锦标赛开始
    首日创出三项新纪录
    本报上海12月23日电　今天在徐汇区温水游泳池开赛的全国短池游泳锦标赛，有2人2队打破3项全国短池游泳纪录。
    男子100米蛙泳，安徽陈剑虹以1分0秒68打破全国纪录。沈坚强在男子50米自由泳决赛中，以22秒75险破22秒93他自己保持的全国纪录。上海队和江苏队在女子4×50米混合泳接力赛中，双双打破这个项目全国纪录，成绩分别为1分57秒77、1分58秒82。其他项目冠军为：女子50米蛙泳，黑龙江的黄晓敏，32秒26；女子800米自由泳，黑龙江阎明，8分52秒88；女子100米自由泳，北京张春莅，56秒73，女子400米个人混合泳，广东陈晓蓉，5分0秒56；男子200米自由泳，河北王大力，1分50秒54；男子200米仰泳，河北魏星，2分6秒52。
　（唐玉琴）
