Title: World Bank Report: Developing Countries' External Debt Exceeds $1 Trillion
Author: Zhang Liang
Date: 1989-12-19
Edition: 4

The World Bank today released a report estimating that by the end of this year, the total external debt of developing countries will reach $1.29 trillion, an increase of $6 billion from 1988.

The report states that the results of the "Brady Plan" proposed by the U.S. Secretary of the Treasury in March this year have been "encouraging." The debt agreements reached by Mexico, the Philippines, and Costa Rica with creditor banks also prove that the "case-by-case resolution" approach is feasible. However, this new debt strategy also faces some challenges: the official guarantees for debt relief are insufficient; commercial banks are reluctant to provide new loans to debtor countries; commercial banks lack cooperation in debt negotiations; some commercial banks are unwilling to support debt relief plans.

The report points out that for the new debt strategy to succeed, industrialized countries should first create a favorable and stable external environment for debtor countries, in which maintaining an open international trade system, stable exchange rates, and interest rates are crucial. Secondly, the economic adjustment and stable development of developing countries are prerequisites for the success of debt relief strategies.

The report also predicts that the total external debt of developing countries will reach $1.319 trillion in 1990, an increase of approximately 2% from this year.

### 世界银行发表报告  发展中国家外债逾万亿美元
张亮
1989-12-19
第4版(国际新闻)
专栏：

    世界银行发表报告
    发展中国家外债逾万亿美元
    本报华盛顿12月18日电记者张亮报道：世界银行今天发表的一份报告估计，到今年底发展中国家的外债总额将达12900亿美元，比1988年增加60亿美元。
    报告认为，美国财政部长今年3月提出的“布雷迪计划”迄今取得的成果是“令人鼓舞”的。墨西哥、菲律宾和哥斯达黎加与债权银行达成的债务协议也证明“逐个解决”办法是可行的。但是，这一新的债务战略也面临一些难题：用于支持减免债务的官方保证金不足；商业银行不愿向债务国提供新贷款；商业银行在债务谈判中合作不够；一些商业银行不愿支持减免债务计划。
    报告指出，为使新的债务战略获得成功，工业国家首先应当为债务国创造一个有利和稳定的外部环境，其中保持开放的国际贸易体系、稳定汇率及利率至关重要。其次，发展中国家经济调整和稳定发展是减免债务战略成功的先决条件。
    报告还预测，1990年发展中国家的外债总额将达13190亿美元，比今年增加大约2％。
