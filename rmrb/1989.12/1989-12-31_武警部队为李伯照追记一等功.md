Title: The People's Armed Police Force Posthumously Awards Li Bozhao the First-Class Merit
Author:
Date: 1989-12-31
Edition: 3

The People's Armed Police Force announced at an expanded meeting of the Party Committee this afternoon that it would posthumously award the "Lei Feng-style good cadre" Li Bozhao the First-Class Merit.

### 武警部队为李伯照追记一等功

1989-12-31
第3版(综合)
专栏：

    武警部队为李伯照追记一等功
    本报北京12月30日讯　中国人民武装警察部队在今天下午举行的党委扩大会议上宣布，给“雷锋式好干部李伯照”追记一等功。
