Title: Chairman Yang Meets Chinese Personnel in Kuwait
Author:
Date: 1989-12-26
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Kuwait, December 24th.

Chairman Yang Shangkun, who is on an official friendly visit to Kuwait, met with the entire staff of the Chinese Embassy in Kuwait and representatives of Chinese business, contracting companies, medical teams, sports coaching groups, and students studying in Kuwait this evening at the residence of the Chinese Ambassador to Kuwait.

Accompanied by Ambassador Guan Zihuai, Chairman Yang shook hands with each Chinese colleague present and inquired warmly about their work and living conditions, encouraging everyone to work hard. Chairman Yang also took group photos with more than 80 Chinese colleagues in batches.

### 杨主席会见我在科人员
许昌
1989-12-26
第4版(国际新闻)
专栏：

    杨主席会见我在科人员
    新华社科威特12月24日电　（记者许昌）正在这里进行正式友好访问的杨尚昆主席，今晚在中国驻科威特大使官邸会见了中国使馆全体人员和在科威特工作的中国商务、承包公司、医疗队、体育教练组和留学生代表。
    杨主席由管子怀大使陪同，同在这里工作的中国同志一一握手，并亲切询问大家的工作和生活情况，勉励大家努力工作。杨主席还分批同80多位中国同志合影留念。
