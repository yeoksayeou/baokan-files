Title: Sweden Widely Uses Computers
Author:
Date: 1989-12-15
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Stockholm

According to statistics from the Swedish Central Statistics Bureau, currently 1.5 million Swedes use computers in their work, accounting for 32% of the total workforce.

In comparison, in 1984, the number of Swedes using computers was 1 million, accounting for 23% of the workforce. This indicates that the use of computers in Sweden is becoming increasingly widespread, with a 50% increase over five years.

Statistics show that the use of computers varies significantly across different industries. Less than 10% of people in agriculture and forestry use computers, while in the banking and insurance sectors, 90% of people use computers.

### 瑞典广泛使用电脑

1989-12-15
第4版(国际新闻)
专栏：

    瑞典广泛使用电脑
    新华社斯德哥尔摩电　据瑞典中央统计局统计，目前150万瑞典人在工作中使用电脑，占全部就业人数的32％。
    与此相比，1984年瑞典使用电脑的人数为100万人，占就业人数的23％。这表明电脑的使用在瑞典日趋广泛，5年中增加了50％。
    据统计，不同行业中使用电脑情况很不一样。农业和林业中使用电脑者不足10％，而在银行和保险业中，90％的人都使用电脑。
