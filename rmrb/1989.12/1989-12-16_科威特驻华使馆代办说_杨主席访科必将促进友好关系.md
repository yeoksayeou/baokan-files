Title: Kuwaiti Chargé d'Affaires Says Yang's Visit Will Promote Friendly Relations
Author: Yang Guoqiang
Date: 1989-12-16
Edition: 2

Kuwaiti Chargé d'Affaires Says Yang's Visit Will Promote Friendly Relations

New China News Agency, Beijing, December 15 (Reporter Yang Guoqiang) The Chargé d'Affaires of the Kuwaiti Embassy to China, Hisham Al-Khudairi, said in an exclusive interview with our reporter yesterday that Yang Shangkun's upcoming visit to Kuwait is a major historic event in Kuwait-China relations and will elevate bilateral friendly cooperation to a new height.

Yang Shangkun, at the invitation of Kuwait's Emir Jaber Al-Ahmad Al-Sabah, will visit Kuwait from December 24 to 26. Al-Khudairi noted that this is the first visit by a Chinese head of state to Kuwait since the two countries established diplomatic relations in 1971, and it will promote the development of friendly relations between the two countries in many fields.

He said that the people of Kuwait will be very happy to receive Chairman Yang Shangkun and will give him a warm welcome. This will enhance the relationship and mutual understanding between the two peoples. Al-Khudairi finally emphasized that Chairman Yang Shangkun's visit to Kuwait will continue to promote the development of bilateral relations and open up new areas of cooperation for the two countries.

### 科威特驻华使馆代办说  杨主席访科必将促进友好关系
杨国强
1989-12-16
第2版(国内新闻)
专栏：

    科威特驻华使馆代办说
    杨主席访科必将促进友好关系
    新华社北京12月15日电  （记者杨国强）科威特驻华使馆临时代办休克里昨天在接受本社记者单独采访时说，杨尚昆主席即将对科威特的访问是科中关系史上的重大的历史性事件，必将把双边友好合作关系推向新的高度。
    杨尚昆应科威特国埃米尔贾比尔·艾哈迈德·萨巴赫的邀请，将于12月24—26日访问科威特。休克里代办指出，这是科中两国1971年建交以来，中国国家元首首次访问科威特，它将推动两国友好关系在许多领域的发展。
    他说，科威特人民将十分高兴地接待杨尚昆主席，并给予他热烈的欢迎。这将增进两国人民之间的关系和相互了解。休克里最后强调说，杨尚昆主席对科威特的访问必将继续促进双方关系的发展，并为两国开辟新的合作领域。
