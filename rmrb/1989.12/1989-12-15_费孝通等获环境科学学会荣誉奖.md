Title: Fei Xiaotong and Others Receive Honor Award from the Environmental Science Society
Author:
Date: 1989-12-15
Edition: 2

	Fei Xiaotong and 13 other senior comrades who have made outstanding contributions to promoting environmental protection were awarded the "Honor Award" by the Environmental Science Society today. Jiang Xiaoke, Director of the Beijing Municipal Environmental Protection Bureau, and 134 others were simultaneously awarded the "Excellent Environmental Science and Technology Worker Award."

	These outstanding environmental scientists and activists have made remarkable contributions in establishing Chinese environmental science and in pollution prevention and control, as well as engineering construction. Vice Chairman of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress Huang Hua and Vice Chairman of the National Committee of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference Kang Keqing attended the ceremony held today to commemorate the 10th anniversary of the founding of the Chinese Environmental Science Society and presented the awards to the recipients. Over the past decade, the Chinese Environmental Science Society has undertaken a great deal of work.

### 费孝通等获环境科学学会荣誉奖

1989-12-15
第2版(国内要闻)
专栏：

    费孝通等获环境科学学会荣誉奖
    新华社北京12月14日电　全国人大常委会副委员长费孝通等13位为推动环境保护事业做出卓越贡献的老同志今天获得了环境科学学会“荣誉奖”。北京市环保局局长江小珂等134人同时被授予“优秀环境科技工作者奖”。
    这些优秀的环境科学工作者和活动家，在创建中国环境科学，以及防治污染、工程建设等方面做出了突出的贡献。全国人大常委会副委员长黄华、政协全国副主席康克清出席今天召开的中国环境科学学会成立十周年纪念会，并向获奖者颁奖。中国环境科学学会成立十年来，做了大量的工作。
