Title: U.S. Requests Extension of U.S.-Greek Defense Cooperation Agreement
Author:
Date: 1989-12-12
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency,

The U.S. Ambassador to Greece, Sotirios, on the 8th requested the extension of the U.S.-Greek defense cooperation agreement, signed in 1983, for an additional six months.

It is reported that Sotirios met with the leader of the New Democracy Party, Mitsotakis, on the 8th, conveying the U.S. government's wish.

This five-year agreement expired on December 21st of last year. According to the agreement, if a new agreement cannot be signed between the two countries, the U.S. military bases in Greece must be dismantled by May of 1990.

### 美要求延长希美防务合作协议

1989-12-12
第4版(国际新闻)
专栏：

    美要求延长希美防务合作协议
    美国驻希腊大使索蒂罗斯八日要求把一九八三年签定的希美防务合作协议期限延长六个月。
    据悉，索蒂罗斯八日会见了新民主党领导人米佐塔基斯，向他转达了美国政府的这一愿望。
    这项为期五年的协议已于去年十二月二十一日到期。根据协议精神，如果两国之间不能签定一项新协议，美国驻希腊的军事基地就必须在一九九○年五月份之前拆除。
                                      （据新华社）
