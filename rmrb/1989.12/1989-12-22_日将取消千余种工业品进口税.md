Title: Japan to Cancel Import Taxes on Over a Thousand Industrial Products
Author:
Date: 1989-12-22
Edition: 4

Japan's Ministry of Finance recently decided to abolish import tariffs on 1,004 industrial products starting from next April.

These industrial products include artificial satellites, records, cassette tapes, optical fibers, medical equipment, film, inner tubes for wheels, mineral products, chemical products, reinforced glass and glass fiber for automobiles, precious metal products, iron tools, newsprint, furniture, and sports goods. Currently, most of these products are subject to import tariffs of less than 5%.

In addition, Japan will also reduce the import tariffs on four other industrial products.

This measure is aimed at alleviating the dissatisfaction of some countries and regions that trade with Japan.

### 日将取消千余种工业品进口税

1989-12-22
第4版(国际新闻)
专栏：

    日将取消千余种工业品进口税
    新华社东京12月20日电  日本大藏省最近决定从明年4月起取消1004种工业产品的进口关税。
    这些工业产品包括人造卫星、唱片、录音带、光导纤维、医疗器械、胶卷、车轮内胎、矿产品、化学产品、汽车用强化玻璃和玻璃纤维、贵金属产品和铁制工具、新闻纸、家具、体育用品等。目前这些产品在进口时大部分要上5％以下的关税。
    除此以外，日本还将降低4种工业产品的进口关税。
    采取这种措施是为了缓和同日本进行贸易的一些国家与地区的不满。
