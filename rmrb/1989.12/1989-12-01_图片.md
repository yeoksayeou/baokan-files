Title: PLA Border Defense Forces in Zhejiang Respect Local Customs and Strengthen Police-Public Unity
Author: Zhong Changhong
Date: 1989-12-01
Page: 5

The People's Armed Police Zhejiang Provincial Corps Jingning She Autonomous County Detachment respects the local ethnic customs, promotes police-public unity, and has been praised by the She ethnic minority.

The image shows ethnic minority residents having a friendly conversation with cadres and soldiers.
