Title: Jiang Zemin and Yang Shangkun Meet with Thai Guests
Author:
Date: 1989-12-15
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, December 14th.

CPC General Secretary Jiang Zemin this afternoon met with a delegation from Thailand's Charoen Pokphand Group led by Chairman Sookchai Lee in the Great Hall of the People. They had a cordial and friendly discussion on issues related to economic cooperation.

During the meeting, Sookchai Lee introduced the cooperation between the Charoen Pokphand Group and China. The Charoen Pokphand Group is Thailand's largest multinational corporation and one of the world's largest agricultural enterprise groups. It began investing in China to establish factories in 1979, and has now established 21 enterprises in China. Sookchai Lee said that over the past 10 years, the investment environment in China has significantly improved, and he expressed hope to further expand the scope of cooperation.

Jiang Zemin briefed the guests on China's economic development and expressed appreciation for the Charoen Pokphand Group's active cooperation with China. He said, "We hope that the Charoen Pokphand Group will set an example for cooperation with China in the 1990s."

Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, December 14th.

President Yang Shangkun this afternoon met with a delegation from Thailand's Charoen Pokphand Group led by Chairman Sookchai Lee in the Great Hall of the People.

During the meeting, Yang Shangkun expressed appreciation for the Charoen Pokphand Group's active investment in China.

### 江泽民杨尚昆会见泰国客人  赞赏正大集团积极在华投资

1989-12-15
第1版(要闻)
专栏：

    江泽民杨尚昆会见泰国客人
    赞赏正大集团积极在华投资
    新华社北京12月14日电　中共中央总书记江泽民今天下午在人民大会堂会见由董事长谢国民率领的泰国正大集团访华团，就发展经济合作等问题同客人进行了亲切友好的交谈。
    会见时，谢国民介绍了泰国正大集团同中国开展合作的情况。正大集团是泰国最大的跨国公司，也是世界上最大的农业企业集团之一。它从1979年起即开始在中国投资办厂，在华投资的企业迄今已达21家。谢国民说，10年来中国的投资环境有了很大改善，他表示希望进一步扩大合作领域。
    江泽民向客人介绍了中国经济发展的情况，并对正大集团积极同中国发展合作表示赞赏。他说，希望正大集团在90年代做对华合作的榜样。
    新华社北京12月14日电　国家主席杨尚昆今天下午在人民大会堂会见了由董事长谢国民率领的泰国正大集团访华团。
    会见时，杨尚昆对泰国正大集团积极在华投资表示赞赏。
