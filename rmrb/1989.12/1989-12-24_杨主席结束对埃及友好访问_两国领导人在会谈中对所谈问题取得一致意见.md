Title: Yang Shangkun Ends Friendly Visit to Egypt
Author: Cheng Yuansheng
Date: 1989-12-24
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Alexandria, December 23rd.

Yang Shangkun Chairman today at 11:20 a.m. (local time) concluded his official friendly visit to Egypt and boarded a special plane to leave Alexandria for a formal friendly visit to the United Arab Emirates.

Egyptian Vice Premier and Minister of Planning and International Cooperation Gamal Mubarak, on behalf of President Hosni Mubarak, specially came from Cairo to the airport to warmly see off Chairman Yang. Chairman Yang said that this visit was very successful. Local military and political officials also came to the airport to see him off.

Before leaving Alexandria, Chairman Yang accepted an interview with Egyptian journalists.

Chairman Yang said, "In my talks with President Mubarak, we reached a consensus on the issues discussed. The Cairo International Conference Center is a joint effort of the Chinese and Egyptian sides and a symbol of Sino-Egyptian friendship." He expressed his gratitude to the Egyptian media for their coverage of his visit.

### 杨主席结束对埃及友好访问  两国领导人在会谈中对所谈问题取得一致意见
成元生
1989-12-24
第4版(国际新闻)
专栏：

    杨主席结束对埃及友好访问
    两国领导人在会谈中对所谈问题取得一致意见
    本报亚历山大12月23日电  记者成元生报道：杨尚昆主席今天中午11时20分（当地时间）结束对埃及的正式友好访问，乘专机离开亚历山大前往阿联酋进行正式友好访问。
    埃及副总理兼计划和国际合作部部长甘祖里受穆巴拉克总统委托专程从开罗到机场热烈欢送杨主席。杨主席说，这次访问很成功。当地军政官员也到机场欢送。
    杨主席在离开亚历山大时接受了埃及记者的采访。
    杨主席说，我同穆巴拉克总统在会谈中，对所谈到的问题取得一致意见。开罗国际会议中心是中埃双方共同努力的结果，也是中埃友谊的象征。他对埃及新闻界对他来访的报道表示感谢。
