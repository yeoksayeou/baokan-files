Title: Publication of "Biophysics of Acupuncture Meridians"
Author: Ke Wen
Date: 1989-12-06
Edition: 3

The book "Biophysics of Acupuncture Meridians," which compiles the latest achievements of both ancient and modern research on acupuncture meridians in China and international acupuncture meridian research, has recently been published by the Beijing Publishing House.
