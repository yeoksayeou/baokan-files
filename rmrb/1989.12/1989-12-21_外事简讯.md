Author:
Date: 1989-12-21
Page: 2

Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, December 21st.

	National Committee of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference Vice Chairman Fang Yi met and hosted a banquet for Japanese Senator Tana Tsuyoshi on December 20th. Tana Tsuyoshi arrived in Beijing on December 19th at the invitation of Fang Yi.

### 外事简讯

1989-12-21
第2版(国内新闻)
专栏：外事简讯

    外事简讯
    新华社北京电　全国政协副主席方毅20日在这里会见并宴请了日本参议员田英夫。田英夫应方毅邀请于19日抵京访华。
