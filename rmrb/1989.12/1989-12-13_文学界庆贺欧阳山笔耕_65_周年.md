Title: Literary Circles Celebrate 65 Years of Eu Yang Shan's Literary Work
Author: Wang Chuan Zhen
Date: 1989-12-13
Edition: 3

A celebration of renowned writer Eu Yang Shan's 65 years of literary creation and a symposium on the "Eu Yang Shan Collection" began yesterday and will continue for four days in Guangzhou.

Lin Mo Han, Liu Bai Yu, Wu Leng Xi, and the Secretary of the Guangdong Provincial Committee of the Communist Party of China, Lin Ruo, as well as nearly 100 writers, critics, and professors from Beijing, Shanghai, Liaoning, Shaanxi, and Guangdong attended the meeting.

The Vice Minister of the Propaganda Department and Acting Minister of Culture, He Jing Zhi, sent a congratulatory telegram. He praised Eu Yang Shan as a great writer who created a series of successful works that hold an important position in the history of Chinese revolutionary literature. He also noted that Eu Yang Shan demonstrated the valuable steadfastness of a proletarian writer and a clear, Marxist sense of right and wrong in the extremely complex historical conditions of China's rapid social changes.

### 文学界庆贺欧阳山笔耕65周年
王传真
1989-12-13
第3版(综合)
专栏：

    文学界庆贺欧阳山笔耕65周年
    据新华社广州12月12日电　（记者王传真）庆贺著名作家欧阳山从事文学创作65周年暨《欧阳山文集》研讨会，昨日起一连4天在广州举行。
    林默涵、刘白羽、吴冷西和中共广东省委书记林若以及来自北京、上海、辽宁、陕西、广东等地的作家、评论家、教授近百人出席了会议。
    中宣部副部长、文化部代部长贺敬之专门发来贺电。他称赞欧阳山是创作了在我国革命文学史上占有重要地位的一系列成功作品的大作家，并在中国社会急剧变化的极端复杂的历史条件下，表现了无产阶级作家可贵的坚定性和鲜明、清醒的马克思主义是非观念。
