Title: Heads of State Meeting of the East and Southern African Preferential Trade Area Held
Author:
Date: 1989-12-04
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Nairobi, November 30th.

A two-day summit of the East and Southern African Preferential Trade Area (PTA) began today in Nairobi. The outgoing chairman of the PTA, Tanzanian President Julius Nyerere, presided over the opening ceremony. Attendees included Zimbabwean President Robert Mugabe, Zambian President Kenneth Kaunda, Ugandan President Yoweri Museveni, Mozambican President Joaquim Chissano, Djiboutian President Hassan Gouled Aptidon, and Rwandan President Juvénal Habyarimana, as well as representatives from other member states.

Kenyan President Daniel arap Moi, in his opening speech, urged member states to do everything possible to accelerate the economic integration process in East and Southern Africa.

Xinhua News Agency, Nairobi, December 1st.

The two-day summit of the East and Southern African Preferential Trade Area concluded today. The organization has now shifted its focus to implementing its various plans within a clearly defined timeframe.

### 东、南非优惠贸易区举行首脑会议  强调加速经济一体化进程

1989-12-04
第4版(国际新闻)
专栏：

    东、南非优惠贸易区举行首脑会议
    强调加速经济一体化进程
    新华社内罗毕11月30日电  为期两天的东部和南部非洲优惠贸易区第8次首脑会议今天在这里开幕。
    即将卸任的东部和南部非洲优惠贸易区主席、坦桑尼亚总统姆维尼主持了开幕式。津巴布韦总统穆加贝、赞比亚总统卡翁达、乌干达总统穆塞韦尼、莫桑比克总统希萨诺、吉布提总统哈桑·古莱德和卢旺达总统哈比亚利马纳，以及该组织的其他成员国代表出席了会议。
    肯尼亚总统莫伊在开幕式上发表讲话，呼吁成员国尽一切努力加快东部和南部非洲地区的经济一体化进程。
    新华社内罗毕12月1日电  为期两天的东部和南部非洲优惠贸易区第8次首脑会议今天闭幕。该组织已经把精力转向在明确规定的时间表内实施其各项方案方面。
