Title: Jiang Zemin Looks Ahead to the 1990s on Eve of New Year
Author:
Date: 1989-12-31
Edition: 1

CCTV and Radio to Broadcast Live Coverage Tonight

BEIJING, Dec. 30 (Xinhua) — CPC Central Committee General Secretary Jiang Zemin accepted an interview with a reporter from CCTV on the eve of the New Year.

In answering the reporter's questions, Jiang Zemin reviewed the glorious achievements made by the Chinese people in fulfilling the three major tasks of the 1980s, looked ahead to the prospects for the 1990s, and shared his views on current international issues.

Jiang Zemin's interview with CCTV will be broadcast on the "News Simulcast" program on Dec. 31. The China National Radio will also broadcast the recording of this interview on the same evening.

### 新年前夕接受记者采访  江泽民展望九十年代  中央电视台、广播电台今晚将播实况

1989-12-31
第1版(要闻)
专栏：

    新年前夕接受记者采访
    江泽民展望九十年代
    中央电视台、广播电台今晚将播实况
    新华社北京12月30日电　中共中央总书记江泽民，新年前夕接受了中央电视台记者的采访。
    江泽民总书记在回答记者提出的问题的时候回顾了我国人民在实现80年代三大任务方面取得的辉煌成就，展望了90年代的前景，并就当今国际问题发表了看法。
    江泽民总书记答中央电视台记者问，12月31日在中央电视台《新闻联播》节目中播出。中央人民广播电台也将在同一天晚上广播这次采访的录音实况。
