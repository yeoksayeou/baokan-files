Title: Daqing Industrial Output Exceeds 10 Billion Yuan
Author: Wei Guozhi
Date: 1989-12-31
Edition: 1

Daqing City has seen steady growth in its industrial production this year. By December 26, the city's annual total industrial output value exceeded 10 billion yuan for the first time, marking a 3.6% increase over the previous year.

Daqing City is a modern industrial city primarily focused on oil and petrochemical production. Despite facing challenges such as rising raw material prices and severe shortages of funds and electricity, the city's oil production is on track to fully complete its annual targets for crude oil, natural gas, and other products. The petrochemical sector has processed 7 million tons of crude oil this year, achieving a historic high.

### 大庆工业年产值超百亿  原油加工创历史最高水平
魏国志
1989-12-31
第1版(要闻)
专栏：

    大庆工业年产值超百亿
    原油加工创历史最高水平
    新华社哈尔滨12月29日电　（通讯员魏国志）大庆市今年工业生产稳步增产，到12月26日，年总产值首次突破百亿元，比去年增长3.6％。
    大庆市是一座以石油和石油化工生产为主的新型工业城市。今年石油生产在原材料涨价、资金电力严重不足的情况下，广大职工克服困难，即将全面完成原油、天然气等各项生产任务。石油化工今年以来已加工原油700万吨，创历史最高水平。
