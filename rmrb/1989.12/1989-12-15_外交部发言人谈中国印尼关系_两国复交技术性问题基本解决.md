Title: Foreign Ministry Spokesperson on China-Indonesia Relations: Technical Issues for Resumption of Diplomatic Ties Basically Resolved
Author: Héchóngyuán
Date: 1989-12-15
Page: 2

Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, December 14th.

Foreign Ministry Spokesperson on China-Indonesia Relations: Technical Issues for Resumption of Diplomatic Ties Basically Resolved

Reporter Héchóngyuán reports from Beijing: The Foreign Ministry spokesperson said at today's afternoon press conference that after friendly negotiations, China and Indonesia have basically resolved the technical issues related to the resumption of diplomatic ties.

The Foreign Ministry spokesperson made these remarks in response to a question about China-Indonesia relations. He said that the next step will be for the two countries' foreign ministers to finalize the process of resuming diplomatic ties. The time for the foreign ministers' meeting will be determined through bilateral consultations.

### 外交部发言人谈中国印尼关系  两国复交技术性问题基本解决
何崇元
1989-12-15
第2版(国内要闻)
专栏：

    外交部发言人谈中国印尼关系
    两国复交技术性问题基本解决
    本报北京12月14日讯　记者何崇元报道：中国外交部发言人在今天下午举行的新闻发布会上说，中国与印尼双方经过友好商谈，有关两国复交的技术性问题已经基本解决。
    外交部发言人是在回答有关中国与印尼的关系时说这番话的。他说，下一步将由两国外长来最终完成复交的进程。两国外长会晤的时间将由双方协商确定。
