Title: Yang Shangkun Visits Rusayl Industrial Zone
Author: Yú Kāiyuán, Zhōu Cípǔ
Date: 1989-12-29
Edition: 4

Chinese President Yang Shangkun visited the Rusayl Industrial Zone in the afternoon today and said that the achievements made by the Omani people in developing their national economy have left a deep impression on him.

The Rusayl Industrial Zone is located 45 kilometers west of Muscat. Five years ago, it was a barren wasteland, but now it has new factories, infrastructure, and service facilities. So far, more than 40 factories have started production.

President Yang saw food, clothing, building materials, and daily necessities produced by the Omani people in the industrial zone. He praised the efforts made by the Omani government to develop medium and small-scale industries, reduce the severe dependence of the national economy on oil resources, and achieve economic diversification.

At a textile factory with a beautiful environment, President Yang had a friendly chat with workers operating the machines, shook hands warmly, and praised the quality and design of the fabric they produced.

Today, President Yang also visited the Bustan Palace Hotel and toured the Jalali Fort.

The Bustan Palace Hotel is situated by the mountain and the sea, with a beautiful environment and luxurious decorations. Not long ago, the 10th Summit of the Gulf Cooperation Council was held in this hotel.

### 杨主席参观鲁赛尔工业区
喻开元  周慈朴
1989-12-29
第4版(国际新闻)
专栏：

    杨主席参观鲁赛尔工业区
    新华社马斯喀特12月27日电　（记者喻开元、周慈朴）中国国家主席杨尚昆今天下午在这里参观鲁赛尔工业区时说，阿曼人民发展民族经济所取得的成就，给他留下了“深刻的印象”。
    鲁赛尔工业区位于马斯喀特以西45公里处。5年前，那里还是一片荒地，而今已建起了崭新的厂房、基础设施、服务设施等。迄今，已有40多家工厂投产。
    杨主席在工业区看到了阿曼人民生产的食品、服装、建筑材料、日用品等。杨主席对阿曼政府为发展中、小工业，改变国民经济严重依赖石油资源，实现经济多样化所作的努力表示赞赏。
    在一家环境优美的纺织厂，杨主席同正在操作机器的工人亲切交谈，热情握手，称赞他们生产出来的布匹“质量很好，图案很好看”。
    今天，杨主席还参观了布斯坦宫饭店，游览了贾拉里古城堡。
    布斯坦宫饭店倚山傍海，环境幽美，装饰豪华。不久前，海湾合作委员会第10届首脑会议就在这个饭店举行。
