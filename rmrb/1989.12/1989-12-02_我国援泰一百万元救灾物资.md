Title: China Provides One Million Yuan in Disaster Relief to Thailand
Author:
Date: 1989-12-02
Edition: 4

China Provides One Million Yuan in Disaster Relief to Thailand

Xinhua News Agency, Bangkok, December 1 - Chinese Ambassador to Thailand, Li Shichun, today handed over disaster relief materials worth one million yuan on behalf of the Chinese government to Thai Foreign Minister Siddhi Savetsila. This aid is intended to assist the people of southern Thailand who suffered severe losses due to a typhoon in November.

Siddhi expressed gratitude for China's valuable assistance, saying, "Despite its own difficulties, China has provided material aid to the Thai people. This reflects the deep friendship between the Chinese and Thai peoples. China is our best friend."

The relief materials provided by China include canned food, medicine, and building materials.

In November, a typhoon struck southern Thailand, causing approximately 2.98 billion US dollars in damage, resulting in the deaths of 530 people and the disappearance of 2,000 others.

### 我国援泰一百万元救灾物资

1989-12-02
第4版(国际新闻)
专栏：

    我国援泰一百万元救灾物资
    新华社曼谷12月1日电　中国驻泰国大使李世淳今天代表中国政府在泰国外交部向西提·沙卫西拉外长转交了100万元人民币的救灾物资，以援助今年11月因台风遭受严重灾害的泰国南部灾民。
    西提外长对中国政府提供的宝贵援助表示感谢。他说：“尽管中国有它自己的困难，但中国仍然向泰国灾民提供物资援助，这反映了中国人民对泰国人民的深厚感情。中国是我们的最好朋友。”
    中国援泰的救灾物资包括罐装食品、药品和建筑材料。
    今年11月，台风袭击了泰国南部地区，致使泰国遭受了约2.98亿美元的损失，530人丧生，2000人失踪。
