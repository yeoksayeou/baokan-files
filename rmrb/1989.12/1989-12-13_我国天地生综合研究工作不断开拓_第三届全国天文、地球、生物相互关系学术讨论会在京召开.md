Title: China's Interdisciplinary Research on the Relationships Between Astronomy, Geology, and Biology Continues to Advance
Author: Yang Zhaobo
Date: 1989-12-13
Page: 3

Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, December 12th. (Reporter Yang Zhaobo) The Third National Academic Conference on the Relationships Between Astronomy, Geology, and Biology was held in Beijing today.

Renowned scientist Qian Xuesen attended the conference and pointed out that the interdisciplinary research on the relationships between astronomy, geology, and biology is based on the development of modern science. It treats astronomy, geology, and biology as an interconnected and interactive system, conducting comprehensive research across multiple disciplines. This interdisciplinary research, along with the in-depth development of interdisciplinary and marginal sciences, will inevitably lead to further advancements in various sub-disciplines, bring about significant progress in natural sciences, and drive the progress of human society.

It is reported that China's interdisciplinary research on the relationships between astronomy, geology, and biology started relatively early. It inherits the ancient Chinese concept of the unity of heaven, earth, and humanity, and on the basis of modern science, treats the earth, astronomy, and biology as an interconnected whole, conducting comprehensive research across multiple disciplines. So far, China's interdisciplinary research on the relationships between astronomy, geology, and biology has made pioneering work in new disciplines such as astronomical geology, astronomical meteorology, astronomical seismology, seismological meteorology, and historical natural sciences.

This academic conference received a total of 300 papers. The conference was jointly initiated by 14 national academic societies, including the China Meteorological Society, the China Seismological Society, the Chinese Astronomical Society, the Chinese Paleontological Society, the Chinese Geophysical Society, the China Oceanic Society, and the Chinese Society for Environmental Sciences.

### 我国天地生综合研究工作不断开拓  第三届全国天文、地球、生物相互关系学术讨论会在京召开
杨兆波
1989-12-13
第3版(综合)
专栏：

    我国天地生综合研究工作不断开拓
    第三届全国天文、地球、生物相互关系学术讨论会在京召开
    新华社北京12月12日电　（记者杨兆波）第三届全国天文、地球、生物相互关系学术讨论会今天在北京举行。
    著名科学家钱学森到会指出，天地生综合研究是在现代科学发展的基础上，将天文、地球、生物三者做为一个相互联系、相互作用的整个系统，进行多学科的综合研究，这种综合研究以及交叉学科和边缘学科的深入发展，必将导致各分支学科的进一步发展，引起自然科学的重大进展，推动人类的社会前进。
    据介绍，我国的天地生综合研究起步较早，它继承了我国古代的天地人统一观，在现代科学的基础上，将地球、天文、生物三者视为相互联系的整体，进行多学科的综合研究。迄今我国天地生综合研究已在天文地质学、天文气象学、天文地震学、地震气象学、历史自然学等新兴学科中，做出了开拓性的工作。
    这届学术讨论会共收到论文300篇。讨论会是由中国气象学会、中国地震学会、中国天文学会、中国古生物学会、中国地球物理学会、中国海洋学会、中国环境科学学会等14个全国性学会共同发起的。
