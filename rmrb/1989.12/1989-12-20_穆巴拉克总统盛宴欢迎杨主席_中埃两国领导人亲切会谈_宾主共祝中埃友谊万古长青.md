Title: President Mubarak Hosts a Grand Banquet for Chairman Yang
Author: Cheng Yuansheng, Lin Jiaoming
Date: 1989-12-20
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Cairo, 19th December

President Mubarak of Egypt hosted a grand banquet at the Presidential Palace on the evening of December 18 to welcome Chairman Yang Shangkun. The hosts and guests delivered passionate speeches at the banquet (see page 4 for details).

Xinhua News Agency, Cairo, 19th December

Chinese President Yang Shangkun and Egyptian President Hosni Mubarak held a private meeting this morning at the new Presidential Palace in Cairo and jointly presided over a high-level bilateral government officials' meeting.

During the meeting, Chairman Yang invited President Mubarak to visit China again at his convenience.

President Mubarak said in the high-level meeting that the feelings between Egypt and China are those of loyal brotherhood, of mutual reliance in times of difficulty. China is one of Egypt's best friends. He wished Chairman Yang a pleasant visit and hoped that friends would meet frequently.

Chairman Yang said, "As soon as I set foot on Egyptian soil, I felt the deep friendship of the Egyptian people towards China. This friendship is a cooperative brotherhood that deeply moves us." He said that President Mubarak is a familiar old friend of the Chinese people, a renowned political activist. We admire his policies of diligent governance and economic development in Egypt. Under the President's leadership, Egypt's economy will definitely develop, and the people's lives will definitely improve. China supports Egypt's independent foreign policy and supports Egypt's various active efforts to resolve the Middle East issue.

Chairman Yang congratulated Egypt on formally resuming its membership in the Arab League this year and on President Mubarak's election as Chairman of the Organization of African Unity. He said that this shows the trust of African countries and Arab nations in Egypt. He believed that President Mubarak will achieve even greater success.

Chairman Yang said, "The relationship between China and Egypt can only be good, can only be mutually cooperative, and can only develop together. As two major ancient countries in Asia and Africa, we should play our due roles in the world." Chairman Yang wished for the everlasting friendship between China and Egypt.

Egyptian Prime Minister Atif Sidqi and Wang Bingqian participated in the high-level meeting.

### 穆巴拉克总统盛宴欢迎杨主席  中埃两国领导人亲切会谈  宾主共祝中埃友谊万古长青
成元生  林皎明
1989-12-20
第1版(要闻)
专栏：

    穆巴拉克总统盛宴欢迎杨主席
    中埃两国领导人亲切会谈
    宾主共祝中埃友谊万古长青
    本报开罗12月19日电　记者成元生、林皎明报道：埃及总统穆巴拉克12月18日晚在总统府举行盛大宴会，欢迎杨尚昆主席来访。宾主在宴会上发表了热情洋溢的讲话（详见第四版）。
    本报开罗12月19日电　记者成元生、林皎明报道：中国国家主席杨尚昆和埃及总统穆巴拉克今天上午在新开罗总统府举行单独会谈，并共同主持了双边政府高级官员的大组会谈。
    杨尚昆主席在会谈中邀请穆巴拉克总统在方便的时候再次访问中国。
    穆巴拉克总统在大组会谈中说，埃中之间的感情是忠诚的兄弟感情，是困难时相互依靠的感情。中国是埃及最好的朋友之一。他祝愿杨主席访问愉快，希望朋友之间经常见面。
    杨主席说，“我一踏上埃及国土，就感到埃及人民对中国的深厚友谊。这种友谊，是兄弟之间的合作友谊，使我们深受感动。”他说，穆巴拉克总统是中国人民熟悉的老朋友，著名的政治活动家。他在埃及实行励精图治、发展经济的政策，我们表示敬佩。在总统的领导下，埃及经济一定会发展，人民生活一定会改善。中国支持埃及独立自主的外交政策，支持埃及为解决中东问题所做的各种积极努力。
    杨主席祝贺埃及今年正式恢复阿盟成员国资格，祝贺穆巴拉克当选非统组织主席。他说，这表明非洲各国和阿拉伯国家对埃及的信任。他相信穆巴拉克总统会取得更大成就。
    杨主席说，中埃两国关系只能搞好，只能相互合作，共同发展。中国和埃及作为亚非两大古国，应该在世界上发挥应有的作用。杨主席祝愿中埃友谊万古长青。
    埃及总理阿提夫·西德基和王丙乾等参加大组会谈。
