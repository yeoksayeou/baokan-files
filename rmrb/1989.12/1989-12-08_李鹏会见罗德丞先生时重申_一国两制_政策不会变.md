Title: Premier Li Peng Reiterates "One Country, Two Systems" Policy Will Not Change During Meeting with Mr. Roger Loin
Author: Liang Riyao
Date: 1989-12-08
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, December 7th.

Premier Li Peng today reiterated that China's "One Country, Two Systems" policy will not change during a meeting with Mr. Roger Loin, Vice Chairman of the Basic Law Drafting Committee for the Hong Kong Special Administrative Region, and his wife.

Li Peng elaborated on China's principles and policies regarding the Hong Kong issue during the meeting. He emphasized that China's "One Country, Two Systems" policy will not change. He stated that China will continue to implement and execute the Sino-British Joint Declaration on the Hong Kong issue, maintaining Hong Kong's stability and prosperity. Li Peng said, "We do not want to see this historically significant joint declaration harmed in any way."

Roger Loin expressed his confidence in the future of China and the Hong Kong region. He fully agreed with Premier Li Peng's views and said he would strive to ensure the implementation and execution of the Sino-British Joint Declaration and the drafting of a Basic Law that will benefit the long-term stability and prosperity of Hong Kong.

Deputy Director of the State Council's Hong Kong and Macau Affairs Office, Lu Ping, was present during the meeting.

Roger Loin and his delegation of seven arrived in Beijing yesterday evening for a visit. Tonight, Director of the State Council's Hong Kong and Macau Affairs Office, Ji Pengfei, met and hosted a dinner for Roger Loin and his delegation.

### 李鹏会见罗德丞先生时重申  “一国两制”政策不会变
黄日尧
1989-12-08
第1版(要闻)
专栏：

    李鹏会见罗德丞先生时重申
    “一国两制”政策不会变
    新华社北京12月7日电　（记者黄日尧）国务院总理李鹏今天下午在人民大会堂会见香港特别行政区基本法咨询委员会副主任罗德丞先生和夫人一行时重申，中国关于“一国两制”的政策不会改变。
    李鹏在会见时阐述了中国对香港问题的原则立场和有关政策，并强调中国关于“一国两制”的政策不会改变。他表示，中国将一如既往地贯彻和执行中英关于香港问题的联合声明，维护香港的稳定和繁荣。李鹏说，我们不希望看到这一具有重大历史意义的联合声明受到任何损害。
  　罗德丞表示对中国和香港地区的前途充满信心，他完全赞同李鹏总理的看法，并说，他将为中英联合声明的贯彻和执行，以及制定一部有利于保持香港长期稳定繁荣的基本法作出努力。
    国务院港澳办公室副主任鲁平会见时在座。
    罗德丞一行七人是昨天傍晚抵达北京访问的。今晚，国务院港澳办公室主任姬鹏飞会见并宴请了罗德丞一行。
