Title: Honduras' President-Elect Carias Expresses Support for Dispersing Nicaraguan Anti-Government Forces
Author: Guo Weicheng
Date: 1989-12-05
Edition: 4

	Honduras' President-Elect Carias Expresses Support for Dispersing Nicaraguan Anti-Government Forces

	Mexico City, November 30 (Xinhua) - Reporter Guo Weicheng: Honduras' President-Elect Carias announced that he will support the dispersal of the Nicaraguan anti-government forces (Contras) currently stationed in Honduras.

	Carias stated in a declaration, "The process of dispersing the Contras should be short; however, it should also be linked to the democratic opening process in Nicaragua."

	The 46-year-old Carias is a candidate from the conservative National Party. In the November 27 election, he defeated the incumbent Liberal Party candidate Flores with 51% of the vote. Honduras' current President Azcona acknowledged Carias' victory.

### 洪都拉斯当选总统卡列哈斯表示  支持遣散尼反政府武装
郭伟成
1989-12-05
第4版(国际新闻)
专栏：

    洪都拉斯当选总统卡列哈斯表示
    支持遣散尼反政府武装
    本报墨西哥城11月30日电  记者郭伟成报道：洪都拉斯当选总统卡列哈斯宣布，他将支持遣散目前驻扎在洪都拉斯境内的尼加拉瓜反政府武装（康特拉）。
    卡列哈斯发表声明说，“遣散康特拉的过程应当短一些；但是，也应当与尼加拉瓜的民主开放过程相联系”。
    今年46岁的卡列哈斯是保守派国家党候选人。他在11月27日的大选中以51％的得票击败了执政的自由党候选人弗洛雷斯。洪都拉斯现总统阿斯科纳承认卡列哈斯已经获胜。
