Title: Chinese Ambassador Speaks on Yang Shangkun's Visit to Egypt
Author:
Date: 1989-12-14
Edition: 4

Chinese Ambassador Speaks on Yang Shangkun's Visit to Egypt
Visit Will Enhance Sino-Egyptian Friendship

Xinhua News Agency, Cairo, December 12 - Chinese Ambassador to Egypt, Zhan Shiliang, said at a press conference held today that Chairman Yang Shangkun's visit to Egypt next week will further strengthen the already solid friendly cooperative relations between China and Egypt.

Zhan Shiliang said that this visit will be a significant event in the history of Sino-Egyptian relations.

He said, "We firmly believe that Chairman Yang's visit will elevate the relationship between the two long-term reliable friends to a new level."

At the invitation of Egyptian President Hosni Mubarak, Chairman Yang Shangkun will begin his official friendly visit to Egypt on December 18. This will be Yang Shangkun's first visit to the Middle East as the head of state of the People's Republic of China.

Zhan Shiliang said that during his visit, Chairman Yang will hold talks with President Mubarak on bilateral relations and international issues of mutual concern.

Chairman Yang will also attend the opening ceremony of the Cairo International Conference Center, which was built with Chinese aid. Zhan Shiliang said that this center is a vivid example of the cooperation between the two countries.

Egypt is the first stop on Chairman Yang's Middle East tour. He will also visit the United Arab Emirates, Kuwait, and Oman. China and Egypt established diplomatic relations in 1956. Egypt was the first country in Africa and the Arab world to establish diplomatic ties with China.

### 我大使就杨主席访埃发表谈话  访问将增进中埃友好关系

1989-12-14
第4版(国际新闻)
专栏：

    我大使就杨主席访埃发表谈话
    访问将增进中埃友好关系
    新华社开罗12月12日电　中国驻埃及大使詹世亮今天在这里举行的记者招待会上说，杨尚昆主席下周对埃及的访问将增进中国和埃及业已存在的坚实的友好合作关系。
    詹世亮说，这次访问将成为中埃关系史上的“一个重要事件”。
    他说：“我们坚信杨主席的访问将把中埃两个长期可靠的朋友之间的关系提高到一个新阶段。”
    杨尚昆主席应埃及总统穆巴拉克的邀请将于12月18日起对埃及进行正式友好访问。这是杨尚昆作为中华人民共和国国家元首首次访问中东。
    詹世亮说，杨主席在访问期间将同穆巴拉克总统就双边关系和共同关心的国际问题进行会谈。
    杨主席还将同穆巴拉克总统共同为中国援建的开罗国际会议中心剪彩。詹世亮说，这个中心是两国合作的生动体现。
    埃及是杨尚昆主席中东之行的第一站。随后，他还将赴阿拉伯联合酋长国、科威特和阿曼进行访问。中埃两国于1956年建交。埃及是非洲大陆和阿拉伯世界中第一个同中国建交的国家。
