Title: China Secures Women's Singles Title at Badminton Grand Finals
Author:
Date: 1989-12-10
Edition: 3

China's Han Aiping and Xiong Guobao today won their respective semifinals in the 1989 World Badminton Grand Prix Finals held in Singapore, securing their spots in the finals. Han Aiping will face Indonesia's Rung Rung of Indonesia for the championship. Han Aiping won a hard-fought three-set match 2-1. Since the other semifinalists are both from China, China has effectively secured both the championship and the runner-up positions in the women's singles event. Xiong Guobao, China's sole representative in the men's singles semifinals, also won a grueling three-set match, defeating Indonesia's Supriyanto 2-1 to advance to the finals.

World number one badminton player Yang Yang and Denmark's top player Morten Frost lost their final round-robin matches in the 1989 World Badminton Grand Prix Finals held in Singapore on December 8. Yang Yang was defeated by Denmark's Bo Larsen in 35 minutes, with a score of 17-14 and 15-6, thus losing his chance to advance to the semifinals. Frost's match against Supriyanto was one-sided, with Frost losing both sets 7-15.

Chinese women's singles players dominated the top four spots, with world number one Han Aiping defeating Sweden's Magnuson 11-3 and 11-2. She will face the new World Cup champion Rung Rung of Indonesia for a spot in the finals. Another semifinal will feature Chinese players Tang Jiuhong and Huang Hua. Both easily defeated their opponents on December 8. The men's doubles final will feature China's Li Yongbo/Tian Bingyi against Malaysia's Razif Sidek/Jalani Sidek. In the women's doubles, Chinese players Zhou Lei/Sun Xiaqing lost to Indonesian players.

### 羽毛球总决赛我国稳获女单冠军  熊国宝获决赛权  杨阳失去半决赛资格

1989-12-10
第3版(综合)
专栏：体育

    羽毛球总决赛我国稳获女单冠军
    熊国宝获决赛权  杨阳失去半决赛资格
    新华社北京12月9日电　据外电报道，中国选手韩爱平和熊国宝今天在新加坡进行的1989年世界羽毛球大奖赛总决赛的半决赛中分别战胜各自的对手，进入决赛。与韩爱平争夺决赛权的是印度尼西亚选手王莲香。韩爱平苦战3局，以2∶1战胜了对手。因另一对争夺决赛权的均为中国选手，实际上中国已获得该赛事女单的冠、亚军。中国唯一进入男单半决赛的熊国宝，也是经过3局苦战才赢得决赛权的。他以2∶1战胜印尼的苏普里扬托。
    新华社北京12月9日电　据外电报道，世界羽坛头号选手杨阳和丹麦名将弗罗斯特8日在新加坡进行的1989年世界羽毛球大奖赛总决赛最后一轮循环赛中，分别负于丹麦选手波·拉尔森和印度尼西亚的苏普里扬托，双双失去进入男子单打半决赛的资格。拉尔森用35分钟，以17∶14、15∶6战胜杨阳，获得半决赛权。
    弗罗斯特同苏普里扬托的比赛呈一边倒形势，最后弗罗斯特以两个7∶15失利。
    中国女选手则占了女单4强中的3强。世界头号选手韩爱平以11∶3和11∶2战胜瑞典的马格努松，将同新世界杯赛冠军王莲香（印尼）争夺决赛权。另一场半决赛在中国选手唐九红和黄华间进行。她俩8日都轻松地淘汰了对手。男子双打已进入决赛。将由中国选手李永波／田秉毅和马来西亚的拉·西德克／贾·西德克争夺冠军。女子双打中国选手周雷／孙小青负于印尼选手。
