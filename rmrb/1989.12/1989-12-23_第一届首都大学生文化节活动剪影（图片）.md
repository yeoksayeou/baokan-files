Title: Snapshots of the First Capital College Students' Cultural Festival
Author: Wang Jingren
Date: 1989-12-23
Page: 5

The first Capital College Students' Cultural Festival, themed "Love for the Country, Spirited, Healthy, and Lively," kicked off on December 7. Colleges and universities in the capital city organized a series of cultural and artistic activities, igniting the patriotic enthusiasm of college students. The festival concluded on December 30.

The bottom left image shows the Capital College Students' Choir performing "I and My Motherland."

The bottom right image shows college Go (Weiqi) players competing for the "Chen Yi Cup."

The left image shows students practicing calligraphy at an on-site calligraphy event.

Reported and photographed by Wang Jingren of this newspaper.

### 第一届首都大学生文化节活动剪影（图片）
王景仁
1989-12-23
第5版(国内专页（教科文）)
专栏：

    第一届首都大学生文化节活动剪影
    以“爱国、振奋、健康、活泼”为主题的第一届首都大学生文化节于十二月七日拉开帷幕，首都各高等院校开展了一系列的文化艺术活动，激发了大学生蓬勃向上的爱国热情。这次文化节于十二月三十日结束。
    左下图首都大学生合唱团演唱《我和我的祖国》。
    右下图高校围棋选手为争夺“陈毅杯”进行角逐。
    左图在书法笔会上，大学生们临场挥毫。
