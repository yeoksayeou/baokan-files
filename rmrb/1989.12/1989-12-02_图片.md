Title: A German Businessman Builds an Energy-Efficient Glass Tower
Author:
Date: 1989-12-02
Page: 7

Xinhua News Agency

A businessman named Karl Otterpohl from the Federal Republic of Germany has constructed a tower-like energy-efficient glass house. The three sides of the house that receive sunlight are equipped with 98 high-quality insulated glass windows, which can make full use of solar energy.
