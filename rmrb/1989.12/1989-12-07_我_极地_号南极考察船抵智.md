Title: China's "Jidi" Ship Arrives in Chile
Author:
Date: 1989-12-07
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency

China's "Jidi" Antarctic expedition ship arrived in Valparaiso, Chile's largest port, on the 4th. The Chinese Antarctic expedition team will then proceed to the Antarctic for their sixth expedition.

The "Jidi" expedition ship departed from Qingdao on October 30th, after a 34-day, 10,800-nautical-mile journey, it arrived in Chile.

After a brief stop in Valparaiso, the "Jidi" will sail to the Changcheng Station and Zhongshan Station in the Antarctic. It will transport 1,300 tons of supplies to these two stations and then conduct a 15-day scientific expedition in the Indian Ocean. It is expected to return to Qingdao on April 5, 1990.

Chinese Ambassador to Chile, Huang Shikang, welcomed the entire Antarctic expedition team at Valparaiso Port.

### 我“极地”号南极考察船抵智

1989-12-07
第4版(国际新闻)
专栏：

    我“极地”号南极考察船抵智
    中国南极考察队乘坐“极地”号考察船四日抵达智利第一大港瓦尔帕莱索，然后前往南极进行第六次考察。
    “极地”号考察船是十月三十日离开青岛港，经过三十四天、一万零八百海里的航行后来到智利的。
    “极地”号在瓦尔帕莱索港稍作停留后将驶往南极的长城站和中山站。它将把一千三百吨给养物资运送到这两个考察站，然后在印度洋进行十五天的科学考察。预计在一九九○年四月五日返回青岛。
    中国驻智利大使黄士康到瓦尔帕莱索港迎接南极考察队全体队员。
              （据新华社）
