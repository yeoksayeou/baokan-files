Title: Shaanxi Completes Export Plan Ahead of Schedule
Author: Jing Xianfeng
Date: 1989-12-18
Page: 2

The Shaanxi Provincial Commission of Foreign Economic Relations and Trade has reported that as of November 25, the province (including the separately planned city of Xi'an) had achieved an export volume of $334 million. This figure surpasses the annual export earning target set by the state by 2.45 percent, completing the goal 36 days ahead of schedule. Compared to the same period last year, this year's export earnings have increased by more than 5 percent.

### 陕西提前完成出口计划  比去年同期创汇增百分之五
景险峰
1989-12-18
第2版(国内新闻)
专栏：

    陕西提前完成出口计划
    比去年同期创汇增百分之五
    本报讯　记者景险峰报道：陕西省对外经济贸易委员会提供的消息表明，截至十一月二十五日，全省（含计划单列的西安市）外贸出口达三点三四亿美元，提前三十六天超额百分之二点四五完成了全年出口创汇国家计划，比上年同期的出口创汇实绩增长百分之五以上。
