Title: Yang Shangkun to Visit Four Asian and African Countries
Author: Gù Yùqīng
Date: 1989-12-08
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, December 7th.

Today, the spokesperson for the Ministry of Foreign Affairs announced at a press conference that at the invitation of President Muhammad Hosni Mubarak of the Arab Republic of Egypt, President Zayed bin Sultan Al Nahyan of the United Arab Emirates, Emir Jaber Al-Ahmad Al-Sabah of Kuwait, and Sultan Qaboos bin Said of Oman, Chairman Yang Shangkun will embark on a formal friendly visit to the four countries starting from December 18th.

China has good relations with Egypt, the UAE, Kuwait, and Oman. Chairman Yang Shangkun's upcoming visit will further enhance mutual understanding and friendship between the Chinese people and the peoples of the four countries, and strengthen the existing friendly cooperative relations between China and the four countries.

### 杨主席将出访亚非四国
顾玉清
1989-12-08
第1版(要闻)
专栏：

    杨主席将出访亚非四国
    本报北京12月7日讯　记者顾玉清报道：今天下午，外交部发言人在新闻发布会上宣布，应阿拉伯埃及共和国总统穆罕默德·胡斯尼·穆巴拉克、阿拉伯联合酋长国总统扎耶德·本·苏尔坦·阿勒纳哈扬、科威特国埃米尔贾比尔·艾哈迈德·萨巴赫、阿曼苏丹国苏丹卡布斯·本·赛义德的邀请，国家主席杨尚昆将从12月18日起对上述4国进行正式友好访问。
    中国同埃及、阿联酋、科威特和阿曼四国都有着良好的关系。杨尚昆主席即将进行的访问必将进一步增进中国人民同4国人民之间的了解和友谊，加强中国同4国之间业已存在的友好合作关系。
