Title: Construction Begins on the Second Phase of the Zhenjiang Great Harbor Port Area
Author: Gong Yongquan
Date: 1989-12-13
Page: 2

Xinhua News Agency, Zhenjiang, December 12th.

The second phase of the Zhenjiang Great Harbor Port Area, the third-largest port on the 10,000-li Yangtze River, officially began construction today. The second phase is designed with an annual throughput capacity of 26.4 million tons and is scheduled to be completed by 1992.

### 镇江大港港区二期工程开工
龚永泉
1989-12-13
第2版(国内新闻)
专栏：四面八方

    镇江大港港区二期工程开工
    本报镇江12月12日电　记者龚永泉报道：万里长江上第三大港——镇江港大港港区二期工程，今日正式开工。二期工程设计年吞吐量为264万吨，将于1992年建成。
