Title: Fertilizing and Weeding in One Go
Author: Xin Minghua
Date: 1989-12-29
Edition: 2

A new type of paddy field herbicide—Paddy Field Herbicide Fertilizer No. 1—has been successfully developed by the Jilin City Agricultural Science Institute. This herbicide can both weed and fertilize.

Paddy Field Herbicide Fertilizer No. 1 contains excellent herbicides and nutrients needed during the tillering period of rice. Field trials and demonstrations have shown that it has a comprehensive weed control effect of 95% to 100%. Field yield tests indicate that it can generally increase rice yield by 10% to 20%.

### 施肥除草一次完
辛明华
1989-12-29
第2版(国内新闻)
专栏：四面八方

    施肥除草一次完
    新华社长春12月28日电（记者辛明华）一种既能除草又能施肥的新型水田除草剂——水田除草化肥1号，由吉林市农科所研制成功。
    水田除草化肥1号含有优良的除草剂和水稻分蘖期所需要的营养成分。田间试验、示范证明，它对水田杂草的综合防除效果均达95％至100％。经田间测产表明，一般可提高水稻产量10％至20％。
