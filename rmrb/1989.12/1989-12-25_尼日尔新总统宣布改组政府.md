Title: Niger's New President Announces Government Reshuffle
Author:
Date: 1989-12-25
Page: 4

Niger's newly elected President Ali Seibou announced on the 20th that he would reshuffle the government and form a new cabinet without a prime minister. The new cabinet consists of 22 members, including 20 ministers and 2 state secretaries. Former Secretary-General of the Organization of African Unity, Idé Oumarou, was appointed as a state minister and presidential advisor. Additionally, five new members have joined the new cabinet. Foreign Minister Sani Bako continues to hold his position.

### 尼日尔新总统宣布改组政府

1989-12-25
第4版(国际新闻)
专栏：

    尼日尔新总统宣布改组政府
    新当选的尼日尔总统阿里·赛义布二十日宣布对政府进行改组，成立一个不设总理职务的新内阁。新内阁由二十二名成员组成，其中包括二十名部长和两名国务秘书。非洲统一组织前秘书长伊德·奥马鲁被任命为国务部长兼总统府顾问。另有五人进入了新内阁。外交部长萨尼·巴科继续留任。
