Title: National Fair of Products from Joint Ventures and Export Enterprises Held in Guangzhou
Author: Shi Meisi
Date: 1989-12-01
Page: 2

Xinhua News Agency, Guangzhou, November 28th

The 1989 National Fair of Products from Joint Ventures and Export Enterprises is being held from November 28 to December 4 at the Guangzhou International Exhibition Center. Over 380 joint ventures and export enterprises from across the country are showcasing thousands of products in more than ten categories, including electronics, light industry, textiles, machinery, chemicals, building materials, handicrafts, and food. The fair highlights some of the achievements made in developing an export-oriented economy over the past decade of China's opening up to the outside world.

More than 2,000 domestic and foreign businesspeople attended the opening ceremony and engaged in business negotiations.

### 三资企业  出口企业产品博览会在广州举行
史美泗
1989-12-01
第2版(国内新闻)
专栏：

    三资企业  出口企业产品博览会在广州举行
    本报讯　新华社记者史美泗报道：1989年全国三资企业及出口企业产品博览会11月28日至12月4日在广州国际展览馆举行。全国380多家三资企业及出口创汇企业在会上展出了电子、轻工、纺织、机械、化工、建材、工艺品、食品等十几个门类的数千种产品，展示了我国对外开放十年来发展外向型经济所取得的部分成果。
    国内外2000多客商参加了开幕式，并进行了业务洽谈。
