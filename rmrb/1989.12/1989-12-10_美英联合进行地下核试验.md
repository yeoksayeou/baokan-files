Title: U.S. and UK Conduct Joint Underground Nuclear Test
Author:
Date: 1989-12-10
Page: 4

The United States and the United Kingdom jointly conducted an underground nuclear test in the Nevada Desert of California on December 8. The explosion had a yield equivalent to 150,000 tons of TNT.

So far, the two countries have carried out 21 joint nuclear explosion tests under the cooperation agreement signed in 1958.

### 美英联合进行地下核试验

1989-12-10
第4版(国际新闻)
专栏：

    美英联合进行地下核试验
    美国和英国八日在加利福尼亚州的内华达沙漠中联合进行了一次当量为十五万吨梯恩梯炸药的地下核爆炸试验。
    迄今为止，美英两国根据双方一九五八年签署的合作协议已进行了二十一次核爆炸联合试验。
