Title: Warsaw Pact Defense Ministers' Meeting Concludes
Author:
Date: 1989-12-01
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Budapest, November 29th.

The Warsaw Pact defense ministers' meeting, which began on November 28th, concluded on November 29th after signing a resolution document.

Hungarian Defense Minister Karpati Ferenc held a press conference after the meeting, stating that the main topic of this meeting was the defense issues of the Warsaw Pact.

He said that the seven member countries of the Warsaw Pact believe that the unilateral disarmament measures they took earlier can accelerate the disarmament process. The participating countries all hope that the Vienna arms control negotiations will soon produce specific results.

Warsaw Pact Commander-in-Chief Lushkov also pointed out to reporters that although the domestic situations of the Warsaw Pact member countries are different at present, the views on the issues discussed at this meeting in Budapest are consistent.

### 华约国防部长会议闭幕

1989-12-01
第4版(国际新闻)
专栏：

    华约国防部长会议闭幕
    新华社布达佩斯11月29日电　28日召开的华沙条约国国防部长会议在签署一项决议性文件后于29日结束。
    匈牙利国防部长卡尔帕蒂·费伦茨会后在记者招待会上介绍，这次会议主要讨论了华约防御问题。
    他说，华约7个成员国都认为，它们早些时候单方面采取的裁军措施可以加速裁军进程。与会国都希望维也纳裁军谈判不久将由此产生具体结果。
    华约总司令卢舍夫也向记者指出，尽管当前华约成员国的国内情况各不相同，但在布达佩斯举行的这次会议上，各成员国对所讨论的问题看法是一致的。
