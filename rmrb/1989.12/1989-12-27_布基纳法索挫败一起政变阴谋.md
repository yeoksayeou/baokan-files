Title: Burkina Faso Foils a Coup Plot
Author:
Date: 1989-12-27
Edition: 4

Burkina Faso officials announced on the 25th that the Burkina Faso government recently foiled a coup plot aimed at overthrowing the head of state, Blaise Compaoré.

Burkina Faso's Minister of Justice, Antoine Sambou Komi, said at a press conference that some foreign mercenaries were involved in this coup plot. He said that authorities have arrested several individuals and will put them on trial.

### 布基纳法索挫败一起政变阴谋

1989-12-27
第4版(国际新闻)
专栏：

    布基纳法索挫败一起政变阴谋
    布基纳法索官方二十五日宣布，布基纳法索政府最近粉碎了一起旨在推翻国家元首孔波雷的政变阴谋。
    布基纳法索司法部长安托万·桑博·科米在记者招待会上说，一些外国雇佣人员与这起政变阴谋有牵连。他说，当局已逮捕了一些人，并将审判他们。
