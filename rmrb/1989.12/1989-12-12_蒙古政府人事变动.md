Title: Mongolian Government Personnel Changes
Author:
Date: 1989-12-12
Edition: 4

The Presidium of the Great People's Khural of Mongolia issued an order on the 10th, appointing the former First Deputy Chairman of the Council of Ministers, Zaslay, as the First Deputy Chairman of the Council of Ministers, and appointing the former Director of the Council of Ministers' Cadres Training Institute, Bimbasauren, as the Deputy Chairman of the Council of Ministers. It also relieved Altangerel of his duties as Deputy Chairman of the Council of Ministers.

Zaslay is currently 56 years old. Since 1984, he has served as the First Deputy Chairman of the Council of Ministers and the Chairman of the State Planning Commission.

### 蒙古政府人事变动

1989-12-12
第4版(国际新闻)
专栏：

    蒙古政府人事变动
    蒙古大人民呼拉尔主席团十日发布命令，任命原部长会议副主席扎斯莱为部长会议第一副主席，任命原部长会议干部进修学院院长宾巴苏伦为部长会议副主席，解除阿勒坦格尔勒的部长会议副主席职务。
    扎斯莱现年五十六岁，从一九八四年起，任部长会议副主席兼计划经济委员会主席。
