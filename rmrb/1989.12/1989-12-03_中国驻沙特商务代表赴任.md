Title: Chinese Commercial Representative to Saudi Arabia Assumes Duty
Author:
Date: 1989-12-03
Page: 2

Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, December 2nd.

The People's Republic of China's Commercial Representative to the Kingdom of Saudi Arabia, Sun Bigan, departed Beijing today to assume his duties.
