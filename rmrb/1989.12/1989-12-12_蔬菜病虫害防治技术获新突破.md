Title: Breakthroughs in Vegetable Pest and Disease Control Technology
Author: Sun Hongxiang
Date: 1989-12-12
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Shanghai, December 11th.

Chinese biologists and agricultural technologists have made new breakthroughs in vegetable pest and disease control technology after four years of research and practice.

The new integrated pest management (IPM) technology for vegetables focuses on crops as the primary target for controlling multiple pests and diseases. It emphasizes biological control methods, utilizing genetic resistance of varieties, pest-resistant and high-yield cultivation techniques, and the selection and application of high-efficiency, low-toxicity new pesticides. This approach represents a significant advancement over the previous method of focusing on individual diseases or pests and relying primarily on chemical pesticides.

Practical experience has shown that the new IPM technology, which prioritizes biological control, achieves an 80% control efficacy. It can reduce pesticide usage by 20% to 30% and increase vegetable yields by 10% to 20%.

### 蔬菜病虫害防治技术获新突破
孙洪祥
1989-12-12
第1版(要闻)
专栏：

    蔬菜病虫害防治技术获新突破
    据新华社上海12月11日电　（记者孙洪祥）我国生物学家和农业科技工作者经过四年研究和实践，在蔬菜病虫害防治技术方面取得了新的突破。
    蔬菜病虫害综合防治新技术在指导思想上以作物为对象开展对多种病虫的防治，在方法手段上则以生物防治为主开展利用品种遗传抗性、抗害丰产栽培技术和筛选应用高效低毒新农药的综合防治。这比过去以“病”或“虫”
  （单病单虫）为对象，并以化学农药为主的综合防治技术提高了一大步。生产实践表明，以生物防治为主的蔬菜病虫综合防治新技术，其防治效率达80％，可减少农药使用量20％至30％，并可提高蔬菜产量10％至20％。
