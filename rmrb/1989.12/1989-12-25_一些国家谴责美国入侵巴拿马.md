Title: Some Countries Condemn U.S. Invasion of Panama
Author:
Date: 1989-12-25
Edition: 4

	Some Countries Condemn U.S. Invasion of Panama

	According to a summary from Xinhua News Agency: In recent days, some countries' governments and political parties have condemned the U.S. invasion of Panama, and some countries' citizens have held protest rallies.

	On December 22, U Saw Maung, the leader of the State Law and Order Restoration Council's News Commission in Burma, stated that the U.S. invasion of Panama violates the United Nations Charter and the Five Principles of Peaceful Coexistence, and is a regrettable incident.

	On December 21, the Central Committee of the Workers' Party of Korea sent a telegram to the National Committee of the Democratic Revolutionary Party of Panama, expressing support for the Panamanian people's resistance against the U.S. armed invasion. The telegram stated that the U.S. aggression against Panama is a trampling of a sovereign state and a challenge to world peace.

	On December 22, New Zealand's Acting Foreign Minister, Jeffries, expressed regret over the casualties caused by the U.S. invasion of Panama and reiterated New Zealand's firm support for a peaceful resolution to the Panamanian issue.

	On December 23, a spokesperson for Nepal's Ministry of Foreign Affairs issued a statement saying that the U.S. invasion of Panama violates the basic principles of the United Nations Charter. Nepal has always opposed the use of force to resolve disputes.

	On December 22, the Guyanese government issued a statement expressing "extreme dissatisfaction" with the U.S. military invasion of Panama.

	The government of Trinidad and Tobago announced that it does not recognize the new Panamanian government established after the U.S. military invasion of Panama.

	On December 22, Turkish Prime Minister Yıldırım Akbulut expressed concern over the U.S. invasion of Panama and hoped that it would not affect world peace.

	On December 23, the Qatari newspaper Al-Raya published an article stating that the U.S. invasion of Panama shows to the world that the U.S. talks about freedom, democracy, and human rights are merely lies. The newspaper expressed opposition to the U.S. aggression that disregards international law and norms.

	The Kuwaiti newspaper Al-Watan condemned the U.S. invasion of Panama, stating that Panama is a sovereign state, not a U.S. state.

	On December 22, the official newspaper of Uganda, New Vision, published an editorial saying that the U.S. actions set a "dangerous precedent" because they violate a country's sovereignty and cause instability in the region.

	On December 20, tens of thousands of Nicaraguans and people from other Central American countries gathered in front of the U.S. Embassy in Managua, the capital of Nicaragua, demanding that the U.S. respect Panama's sovereignty and cease all military actions in Panama.

	On December 20, thousands of people in Uruguay gathered in the capital, Montevideo, to protest the U.S. invasion of Panama.

	On December 20, tens of thousands of students and people from various sectors in Havana, the capital of Cuba, marched to the office representing U.S. interests in Cuba to condemn the U.S. invasion of Panama.

	On December 22, approximately 15,000 Argentine youths held a massive demonstration in the main streets of Buenos Aires, the capital, to protest the U.S. armed invasion of Panama.

### 一些国家谴责美国入侵巴拿马

1989-12-25
第4版(国际新闻)
专栏：

    一些国家谴责美国入侵巴拿马
    本报讯　综合新华社报道：近日，一些国家政府、政党谴责美国入侵巴拿马，一些国家的公众举行了抗议集会。
    缅甸国家治安建设委员会新闻委员会领导人吴觉山22日发表谈话说，美国入侵巴拿马违反联合国宪章和和平共处五项原则，是令人遗憾的事件。
    朝鲜劳动党中央委员会21日打电报给巴拿马民主革命党全国委员会，声援巴拿马人民抵抗美国武装入侵的斗争。电报说，美国对巴拿马的侵略是对一个主权国家的践踏，是对世界和平的挑战。
    新西兰代外长杰弗里斯22日说，他对美国入侵巴拿马造成的伤亡表示遗憾，重申新西兰坚决支持巴拿马问题的和平解决。
    尼泊尔外交部发言人23日发表声明说，美国入侵巴拿马违犯了联合国宪章的基本原则。尼泊尔一贯反对使用武力解决争端。
    圭亚那政府22日发表声明，对美军入侵巴拿马表示“极为不满”。
    特立尼达和多巴哥政府宣布，不承认美军入侵巴拿马之后建立的巴拿马新政府。
    土耳其总理耶尔德勒姆·阿克布卢特22日对美国入侵巴拿马的事件表示关注，并希望不要因此而影响世界和平。
    卡塔尔《旗帜报》23日发表文章说，美国入侵巴拿马向全世界表明，美国口头上侈谈自由、民主和人权，只不过是在撒弥天大谎。该报表示反对美国置国际法和准则于不顾的侵略行径。
    科威特《祖国报》谴责美国入侵巴拿马时说，巴拿马是一个主权国家而不是美国的一个州。
    乌干达官方报纸《新视野》22日发表社论说，美国的行径是“一个危险的先例”，因为它侵犯了一个国家的主权，造成了这一地区的不稳定。
    12月20日，尼加拉瓜首都马那瓜成千上万的尼加拉瓜人和其他中美洲国家人士聚集在美国驻尼加拉瓜大使馆前面，要求美国尊重巴拿马主权的完整和停止在巴拿马的一切军事行动。
    乌拉圭数以千计的群众20日下午在首都蒙得维的亚举行集会，抗议美国入侵巴拿马。
    古巴首都哈瓦那数以万计的学生和社会各界人士20日中午列队前往代表美国在古巴利益的办事处，谴责美国入侵巴拿马。
    22日约1.5万名阿根廷青年在首都布宜诺斯艾利斯市区的主要街道上举行了声势浩大的示威游行，抗议美国武装入侵巴拿马。
