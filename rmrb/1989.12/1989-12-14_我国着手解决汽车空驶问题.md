Title: China Takes Steps to Address the Issue of Empty Truck Trips
Author:
Date: 1989-12-14
Edition: 1

China is taking steps to address the long-standing issue of empty truck trips. Representatives from 56 road freight enterprises in 14 provinces and municipalities recently signed an agreement to jointly operate long-distance freight services.

Statistics show that China has 1.6 million trucks engaged in road freight operations, with an average empty trip rate of 47.9%. This results in an annual waste of approximately 5 billion yuan.

### 我国着手解决汽车空驶问题

1989-12-14
第1版(要闻)
专栏：

    我国着手解决汽车空驶问题
    据新华社石家庄十二月十日电  我国公路汽车长期空驶的现象可望得到解决，来自全国十四个省市的五十六家公路货运企业代表最近签定了联合经营长途货运业务协议。
    据统计，我国长期从事公路货运的汽车有一百六十万辆，车辆空驶率达百分之四十七点九，每年造成浪费约五十亿元。
