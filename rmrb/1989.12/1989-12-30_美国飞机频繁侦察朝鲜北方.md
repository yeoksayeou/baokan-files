Title: U.S. Aircraft Frequently Spy on North Korea
Author:
Date: 1989-12-30
Edition: 4

The Central News Agency of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea reported on December 27 that a military source had revealed that U.S. aircraft had conducted multiple aerial reconnaissance missions over North Korea in December.

The report stated that at around 8:50 AM on December 27, a U.S. E-3 AWACS aircraft flew from a base in Okinawa to South Korea and conducted reconnaissance along the Military Demarcation Line in an east-west direction over a wide area in North Korea. This type of reconnaissance activity has occurred eight times in December.

### 美国飞机频繁侦察朝鲜北方

1989-12-30
第4版(国际新闻)
专栏：

    美国飞机频繁侦察朝鲜北方
    新华社平壤12月27日电　朝鲜中央通讯社27日援引军事消息灵通人士提供的资料报道说，美国飞机在12月份曾多次对朝鲜北方进行空中侦察活动。
    消息说，27日上午8时50分左右，美国的一架E—3型预警机从冲绳基地飞入南朝鲜，在军事分界线附近上空沿东西方向往返飞行，对朝鲜北方纵深的广大地区进行了空中侦察活动。12月份以来，同类侦察活动共进行了8次。
