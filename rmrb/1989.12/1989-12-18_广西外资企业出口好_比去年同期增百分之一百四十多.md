Title: Guangxi Foreign-Invested Enterprises See Significant Export Growth
Author:
Date: 1989-12-18
Page: 2

Xinhua News Agency, December 18th

	Guangxi's foreign-invested enterprises have achieved remarkable export performance this year. By December 10th, the total export value had reached $27.51 million, marking an increase of 140.7% compared to the same period last year.

### 广西外资企业出口好  比去年同期增百分之一百四十多

1989-12-18
第2版(国内新闻)
专栏：

    广西外资企业出口好
    比去年同期增百分之一百四十多
    广西外商投资企业今年出口创汇好，截至十二月十日止，出口总金额已达二千七百五十一万美元，比去年同期增长百分之一百四十点七。
                                      （据新华社）
