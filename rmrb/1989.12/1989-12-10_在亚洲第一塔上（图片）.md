Title: On the Tallest Tower in Asia
Author: Liu Jiemin
Date: 1989-12-10
Page: 2

The revolving restaurant and observation deck of the Liaoning Color Television Transmission Tower, which stands at a total height of 305 meters and is considered the tallest in Asia, have been completed. From the 200-meter-high revolving deck, one can overlook the entire cityscape of Shenyang.

### 在亚洲第一塔上（图片）
刘杰敏
1989-12-10
第2版(国内新闻)
专栏：

    在亚洲第一塔上
    堪称亚洲之最的辽宁彩色电视发射塔（总高305米），旋转餐厅及观览厅已装修完毕。在200米的旋转厅向下眺望，沈阳城风光尽收眼底。          刘杰敏摄
