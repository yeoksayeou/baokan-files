Title: A Chinese International Flight is Hijacked and Lands in Japan
Author:
Date: 1989-12-17
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, December 16th.

Today, a China International Airlines flight CA-981 from Beijing to the United States via Shanghai was hijacked in mid-flight. The plane landed safely at Fukue Airport in Japan at 13:53. All passengers and the aircraft are safe, and the hijacker has been subdued.

### 我一国际航班遇劫降落日本  旅客皆安全　歹徒被制服

1989-12-17
第1版(要闻)
专栏：

    我一国际航班遇劫降落日本
    旅客皆安全　歹徒被制服
    新华社北京12月16日电　今天上午由北京经上海去美国的中国国际航空公司CA—981航班，飞行中遇歹徒劫持，已于13点53分降落日本福岗机场，旅客及飞机都安全，歹徒已被制服。
