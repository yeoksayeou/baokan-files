Title: Chairman Yang Meets Chinese Personnel in Alexandria
Author: Lǐ Sīzhì
Date: 1989-12-23
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Alexandria, December 22nd.

Chairman Yang Shangkun today met with all the staff of the Chinese Consulate General in Alexandria and representatives of Chinese personnel working in the Alexandria region at the Consulate General.

Chairman Yang shook hands with each of them, inquired about the work and living conditions of the Chinese personnel from Consul-General Wu Decheng, and posed for a group photo with them.

Accompanying Chairman Yang for the meeting were State Councilor and Minister of Finance Wang Boping, Vice Minister of Foreign Affairs Qi Huaiyuan, Vice Minister of Foreign Economic Relations and Trade Lü Xuejin, and Chinese Ambassador to Egypt Zhan Shiliang.

### 杨主席会见我在亚历山大人员
李思治
1989-12-23
第4版(国际新闻)
专栏：

    杨主席会见我在亚历山大人员
    新华社亚历山大12月22日电　（记者李思治）杨尚昆主席今天上午在中国驻亚历山大总领事馆会见了总领事馆的全体工作人员和其他在亚历山大地区工作的中国人员代表。
    杨主席同他们一一握手，向总领事吴德成询问了中国人员的工作和生活情况，并同他们一起合影留念。
    陪同杨主席会见的有国务委员兼财政部长王丙乾、外交部副部长齐怀远、对外经济贸易部副部长吕学俭、中国驻埃及大使詹世亮。
