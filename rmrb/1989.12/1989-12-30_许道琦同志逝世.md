Title: Comrade Xu Daoji Passes Away
Author: Fang Zhengjun
Date: 1989-12-30
Edition: 2

A memorial service for Comrade Xu Daoji, former chairman of the Advisory Committee of Hubei Province, was held this morning in Wuchang.

The Central Advisory Commission of the Communist Party of China, and the Party, government, and military leadership of Hubei Province, as well as Comrades Li Xiannian, Bo Yibo, Wang Zhen, Song Renqiong, Yang Dezhi, and Wang Renzhong, sent wreaths.

Comrade Xu Daoji was born in Guangde County, Anhui Province, on April 29, 1914. He passed away in Wuchang on December 22, 1989, at the age of 75 due to illness.

In his youth, Comrade Xu Daoji dedicated himself to the revolutionary cause. He joined the Communist Youth League of China in 1933 and the Communist Party of China in 1938. After the outbreak of the War of Resistance Against Japanese Aggression, he studied at the Anti-Japanese Military and Political University in Yan'an. Upon graduation, he served as the Political Department Director of the Thirteenth Brigade of the Fifth Division of the New Fourth Army, and later as the Director of the Security Department of the Political Department of the Fifth Division. During the War of Liberation, he held positions such as the Director of the Security Department, the Director of the Organization Department of the Second Column of the Central Plains Field Army, the Political Department Director of the Third Military Sub-district of the Shannan Military Region, and the Political Department Director of the Twelfth Column of the Second Field Army. After the founding of the People's Republic, he served as the Director of the Propaganda Department of the Hubei Provincial Committee of the Communist Party of China, the Secretary of the Provincial Committee, the Secretary-General of the Provincial Committee, the President of the Provincial Party School, the Chairman of the Provincial Federation of Literature and Art Circles, and the Vice-Chairman of the Provincial Federation of Social Sciences. After the Third Plenary Session of the Eleventh Central Committee of the Party, he served as the Deputy Secretary of the Provincial Committee, the First Secretary of the Provincial Discipline Inspection Commission, the Chairman of the Fourth Session of the Provincial Committee of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference, and the Chairman of the Provincial Advisory Committee. He also served as the President of the Provincial Academy of Social Sciences. Comrade Xu Daoji was a delegate to the Thirteenth National Congress of the Party. The Communist Party of Anhui Province, the Provincial Advisory Committee, the Prefectural Committee and Government of Xuancheng, the County Government of Guangde, and the Prefectural Committee and Government of Shangluo in Shaanxi Province also sent wreaths.

### 许道琦同志逝世
方政军
1989-12-30
第2版(国内新闻)
专栏：

    许道琦同志逝世
    新华社武汉12月27日电（记者方政军）湖北省原顾问委员会主任许道琦同志追悼会今天上午在武昌举行。
    中共中央顾问委员会、湖北省党政军领导机关和李先念、薄一波、王震、宋任穷、杨得志、王任重等同志送了花圈。
    许道琦同志是安徽省广德县人，1914年4月29日出生，1989年12月22日因病在武昌逝世，享年75岁。
    许道琦同志青年时期投身革命，1933年加入中国共产主义青年团，1938年加入中国共产党。抗日战争爆发后，他在延安抗大学习，结业后曾任新四军五师十三旅政治部主任、五师政治部保卫部部长等职。解放战争时期，他任中原军区第二纵队政治部保卫部长、组织部长、陕南军区第三军分区政治部主任兼地委宣传部长、二野十二纵队政治部宣传部长。建国后，他历任中共湖北省委宣传部部长、省委书记处书记兼省委秘书长、省委党校校长、省文联主席、省社联副主席。党的十一届三中全会以后，他任省委副书记兼省纪律检查委员会第一书记、省政协第四届委员会主席，省顾问委员会主任，还兼任过省社会科学院院长等职。许道琦同志是党的十三次全国代表大会代表。中共安徽省委、省顾委、安徽宣城地委、行署和广德县、陕西省商洛地委、行署等单位也送了花圈。
