Title: Bank of Communications Shanghai Branch Loans 700 Million to Support Foreign Trade
Author:
Date: 1989-12-09
Edition: 2

Yesterday, the Shanghai branch of the Bank of Communications provided 9 import and export companies with a special loan of 700 million yuan. This will help these enterprises, which are in urgent need of funds, to purchase raw materials from abroad and arrange for export production in early next year.

Prior to this, the bank had already issued a cumulative total of 2.5 billion yuan in loans to foreign trade enterprises this year.

### 交通银行上海分行贷款七亿支持外贸

1989-12-09
第2版(国内新闻)
专栏：四面八方

    交通银行上海分行贷款七亿支持外贸
    新华社上海12月8日电　昨天，交通银行上海分行向9家进出口公司提供专项贷款7亿元，帮助这些急需资金的企业从国外购买原材料，安排好明年年初的出口生产。
    在这之前，这家银行今年来已向外贸企业累计发放贷款25亿元。
