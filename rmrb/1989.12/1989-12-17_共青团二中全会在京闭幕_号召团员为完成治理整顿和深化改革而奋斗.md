Title: The Second Plenary Session of the 12th National Congress of the Communist Youth League of China Concludes in Beijing
Author: Yuán Jiāndá, Hé Píng
Date: 1989-12-17
Edition: 1

The second plenary session of the 12th National Congress of the Communist Youth League of China concluded in Beijing today after a four-day meeting.

The resolution unanimously adopted by the plenary session stated that it is the duty of the Communist Youth League to carry out the spirit of the Fifth Plenary Session of the 13th Central Committee of the Communist Party of China, mobilize and organize young people in various sectors to actively participate in socialist labor competitions, strive to be vanguards of the new Long March, and work hard to complete the tasks of rectification and deepening reform.

The resolution calls on all levels of Youth League organizations to guide young people to understand the situation, build confidence, educate young people to consider the overall situation, consciously maintain social stability, lead young people to work honestly and strive for excellence in their respective positions; organize young people to actively carry out the "double increase, double save" movement, and make due contributions in improving the quality of young people, organizing them to participate in reform management and social supervision, and assisting relevant departments to open up more job opportunities for young people.

Another decision passed by the plenary session proposed to strengthen the construction of the youth league membership, improve the quality of members, enhance the combat effectiveness of the Communist Youth League, and excellently complete various tasks assigned by the Party to the Communist Youth League.

The meeting also passed a decision on the procedure for amending the constitution of the Young Pioneers. Considering that the National Congress of the Young Pioneers will be held next year, the Central Committee of the Communist Youth League decided that the work of amending the constitution will be carried out under the leadership of the Secretariat of the Central Committee of the Communist Youth League. The amended constitution will be submitted to the National Congress of the Young Pioneers for deliberation and approval.

Song Defu and Liu Yandong both spoke at the meeting.

### 共青团二中全会在京闭幕  号召团员为完成治理整顿和深化改革而奋斗
袁建达  何平
1989-12-17
第1版(要闻)
专栏：

    共青团二中全会在京闭幕
    号召团员为完成治理整顿和深化改革而奋斗
    本报北京12月16日讯　本报记者袁建达、新华社记者何平报道：为期4天的共青团十二届二中全会今天闭幕。
    全会一致通过的决议认为，贯彻党的十三届五中全会精神，动员和组织全国各行各业团员青年大力开展社会主义劳动竞赛，争当新长征突击手，为完成治理整顿和深化改革的任务而努力奋斗，是共青团组织义不容辞的职责。
    决议要求各级团组织要引导团员青年认清形势，树立信心，教育团员青年顾全大局，自觉维护社会稳定，带领团员青年在本职岗位上诚实劳动，创优达标；组织团员青年积极开展双增双节运动以及在提高团员青年素质、组织他们参与改革管理和社会监督、协助有关部门广开青年就业门路等方面做出应有的贡献。
    全会通过的另一项决定提出要加强团员队伍建设，提高团员素质，增强共青团的战斗力，出色完成党赋予共青团的各项任务。
    这次会议还通过了关于少先队章程修改程序的决定。鉴于明年将召开少先队全国代表大会，团中央决定队章修改工作在团中央书记处主持下进行，修改后的队章提交少先队全国代表大会审议通过。
    宋德福、刘延东分别在会上讲了话。
