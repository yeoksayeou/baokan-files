Title: Ministry of Foreign Affairs Holds New Year Reception
Author:
Date: 1989-12-30
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, December 29th.

The Ministry of Foreign Affairs held a New Year reception tonight at the Great Hall of the People to welcome ambassadors from various countries and their spouses.

At the beginning of the reception, Vice Premier and Minister of Foreign Affairs Qian Qichen, on behalf of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs, extended New Year greetings to the guests. He wished everyone to make new efforts to promote friendship among the peoples of the world and China, and to develop relations between various countries and China.

More than 300 guests, including ambassadors from over 100 countries and representatives of international organizations in Beijing, as well as their spouses, were invited to attend the reception.

After the reception, the Oriental Song and Dance Troupe performed for the foreign guests.

### 外交部举行新年招待会  百余国家使节国际组织驻京代表等出席

1989-12-30
第1版(要闻)
专栏：

    外交部举行新年招待会
    百余国家使节国际组织驻京代表等出席
    新华社北京十二月二十九日电　外交部今天晚上在人民大会堂举行新年招待会，招待各国驻华使节和他们的夫人。
    招待会开始时，钱其琛外长代表外交部向客人们祝贺新年。他祝愿大家为增进世界各国人民和中国人民的友谊以及世界各国和中国之间关系的发展作出新的努力。
    一百多个国家的使节、国际组织驻京代表以及他们的夫人共三百多人应邀出席了招待会。
    招待会后，东方歌舞团为各国朋友表演了节目。
