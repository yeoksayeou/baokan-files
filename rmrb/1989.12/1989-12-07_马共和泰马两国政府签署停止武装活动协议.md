Title: Governments of Malaysia and Thailand Sign Agreement to Cease Armed Activities
Author:
Date: 1989-12-07
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, December 5th.

The governments of Thailand and Malaysia, along with the Malayan Communist Party, signed two agreements in the Thai southern city of Hat Yai on December 2. These agreements pertain to the cessation of all armed activities in the border regions of Thailand and Malaysia, as well as the achievement of peace.

Attending the signing ceremony were Chen Ping, the General Secretary of the Malayan Communist Party, Abdul Rahman, the representative of the Malaysian government, and Chavalit Yongchaiyudh, the representative of the Thai government and acting commander-in-chief of the armed forces.

### 马共和泰马两国政府签署停止武装活动协议

1989-12-07
第4版(国际新闻)
专栏：

    马共和泰马两国政府签署停止武装活动协议
    新华社北京12月5日电　泰国合艾消息：泰国和马来西亚政府同马来亚共产党2日在泰国南部的合艾市签署了两项关于在泰、马边境地区和马来西亚停止一切武装活动和实现和平的共同协议。
    出席这两项协议签字仪式的有马来亚共产党总书记陈平，马来西亚政府代表阿卜杜勒·拉赫曼和泰国政府代表、武装部队代理最高司令差瓦立·永猜裕。
