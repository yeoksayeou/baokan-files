Title: Opening of the National Folk Art and Craft Excellence Selection Exhibition
Author: Zhí Bó Bó Fēng
Date: 1989-12-23
Page: 2

The heartfelt work of folk artisans and the treasures of national culture have made today's opening of the first national folk art and craft excellence selection exhibition in Beijing a notable event. Guizhou's "Weaving" and Xinjiang's "Ethnic Embroidery" have won first prizes.

### 民间工艺美术佳品选展开幕
智博　伯锋
1989-12-23
第2版(国内新闻)
专栏：

    民间工艺美术佳品选展开幕
    本报北京12月20日讯　民间艺人的心血，民族文化的瑰宝，使今天在京开幕的全国首届民间工艺美术佳品奖评选展引人注目。贵州的《织锦》、新疆的《民族绣品》等获一等奖。
          （智博　伯锋）
