Title: Spokesperson of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs Expresses Belief that Romania Can Handle Its Own Affairs
Author:
Date: 1989-12-22
Edition: 2

The spokesperson of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs said at today's press conference in response to a reporter's question regarding Romania's domestic affairs, "China has always believed that the current events in some Eastern European countries are their internal affairs. China does not interfere in the internal affairs of other countries."

He said, "We believe that Romania can handle its own affairs."

### 外交部发言人表示相信  罗马尼亚能处理好自己事务

1989-12-22
第2版(国内新闻)
专栏：

    外交部发言人表示相信
    罗马尼亚能处理好自己事务
    新华社北京12月21日电　外交部发言人在今天的新闻发布会上回答记者有关罗马尼亚国内事务的问题时说，中国一贯认为东欧一些国家当前所发生的事情是他们的内部事务，中国不干涉别国的内政。
    他说：“我们相信罗马尼亚能够处理好自己的事务。”
