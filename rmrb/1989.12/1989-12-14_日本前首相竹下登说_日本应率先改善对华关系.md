Title: Former Japanese Prime Minister Takeshita Noboru Says Japan Should Take the Lead in Improving Relations with China
Author:
Date: 1989-12-14
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Tokyo, December 12th.

Former Japanese Prime Minister Takeshita Noboru said today at a lecture hosted by Kyodo News that Japan should take the lead in improving relations with China.

Takeshita said, "In the policies implemented by Western countries, Japan should play a pioneering role in improving relations with China."

He said that Japan and China are neighboring countries. Japan should implement the third round of yen loans to China as soon as possible.

Regarding the visit of U.S. National Security Advisor Brent Scowcroft to China, Takeshita said this was a substantive contact between high-level officials of the two countries. He gave this visit high praise.

### 日本前首相竹下登说  日本应率先改善对华关系

1989-12-14
第4版(国际新闻)
专栏：

    日本前首相竹下登说
    日本应率先改善对华关系
    新华社东京十二月十二日电  日本前首相竹下登今天在共同社主持的讲演会上说，日本应该率先努力改善同中国的关系。
    竹下说：“在西方各国实施的政策中，日本应该发挥改善对华关系的突破口和率先的作用。”
    他说，日本和中国是邻国。日本应该尽早实施第三次对华日元贷款。
    在谈到美国总统国家安全事务助理斯考克罗夫特访华时，竹下说，这是中美两国高级官员的一次实质性接触。他对此给予高度评价。
