Title: Ali Seibou Elected President of Niger
Author:
Date: 1989-12-13
Page: 4

The results of the Niger presidential election were announced on the 10th. Ali Seibou was elected as the President of the Second Republic of Niger for a seven-year term with 99.60 percent of the votes. Ninety-three candidates who participated in the parliamentary elections were also elected as members of parliament, securing 99.52 percent of the valid votes.

### 阿里·赛义布当选尼日尔总统

1989-12-13
第4版(国际新闻)
专栏：

    阿里·赛义布当选尼日尔总统
    尼日尔大选十日揭晓。阿里·赛义布以百分之九十九点六零的赞成票当选为任期七年的尼日尔第二共和国总统。　九十三名参加议会选举的议员候选人也同时当选为议员。他们获得了百分之九十九点五二的有效选票。
