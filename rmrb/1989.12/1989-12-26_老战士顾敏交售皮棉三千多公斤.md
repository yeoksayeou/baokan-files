Title: Veteran Met Min Sells Over 3,000 Kilograms of Cotton
Author: Jiāng Huà
Date: 1989-12-26
Edition: 2

Veteran Met Min Sells Over 3,000 Kilograms of Cotton

Xinhua News Agency, Nanjing, December 23 (Correspondent Jiang Hua) - Met Min, a 68-year-old veteran of the New Fourth Army and a retired cadre from Kangzhuang Township, Xinghua County, Jiangsu Province, drove a tractor to the township cotton purchasing station to sell the last batch of cotton for this year. With this, Met Min has sold a total of 3,100 kilograms of cotton to the state this year.

Met Min joined the revolution in 1942. In 1984, he retired and returned to Kangzhuang Village, Kangzhuang Township. However, he did not rest on his past laurels. In 1988 and 1989, he consecutively sold over 3,000 kilograms of cotton to the state. This spring, he was awarded the title of "Cotton Planting Champion" by the Jiangsu Provincial Supply and Marketing Cooperative.

### 老战士顾敏交售皮棉三千多公斤
姜桦
1989-12-26
第2版(国内新闻)
专栏：四面八方

    老战士顾敏交售皮棉三千多公斤
    新华社南京12月23日电（通讯员姜桦）江苏省响水县康庄乡68岁的新四军老战士、离休干部顾敏，坐着拖拉机到乡收花站卖出了今年交售的最后一批棉花。至此，顾敏今年已向国家交售皮棉3100公斤。
    顾敏是1942年参加革命的。1984年，他离休回到康庄乡康庄村，但他没躺在过去的功劳簿上享清福。1988、1989连续两年向国家交售皮棉超过3000公斤，今春被江苏省供销合作社授予“植棉大户”称号。
