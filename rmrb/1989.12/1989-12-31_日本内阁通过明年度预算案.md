Title: Japanese Cabinet Approves Budget for Next Fiscal Year
Author:
Date: 1989-12-31
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Tokyo, December 29th.

The Japanese government held an emergency cabinet meeting early today and approved the budget for the 1990 fiscal year. The total budget for the next fiscal year is 6627.36 billion yen, an increase of 9.7% from the previous year.

In the 1990 fiscal year budget, the defense budget is 415.93 billion yen, an increase of 6.1% from the previous year. This is the first time Japan's defense budget has exceeded 400 billion yen.

In this substantial budget, the Japanese government will no longer issue deficit bonds, thus ending the 15-year practice of supplementing fiscal expenditures through deficit bonds. In the new budget, the government's official development assistance budget has increased by 8.2% from the previous year, amounting to approximately 817.5 billion yen.

This budget will be submitted to the Diet for deliberation in late January of next year.

### 日本内阁通过明年度预算案

1989-12-31
第4版(国际新闻)
专栏：

    日本内阁通过明年度预算案
    新华社东京12月29日电  日本政府今天凌晨召开临时内阁会议，通过了1990年度政府预算案。下一年度的预算总额为662736亿日元，比上一年度增加9.7％。
    在1990年度政府预算案中，防卫预算为41593亿日元，比前一年度增加6.1％，这是日本防卫预算首次突破40000亿日元。
    在这个庞大的预算案中，日本政府不再发行赤字国债，从而结束了15年来一直靠发行赤字国债补充财政开支的状况。在新的预算案中，政府开发援助预算比上一年度增加8.2％，约为8175亿日元。
    这一政府预算案将于明年1月下旬提交国会审议。
