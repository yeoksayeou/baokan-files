Title: Summit of the Andean Group Opens
Author: ZHU Manting
Date: 1989-12-19
Edition: 4

The summit of the Andean Group, comprising Bolivia, Colombia, Ecuador, Peru, and Venezuela, was held yesterday in Ecuador's famous nature reserve, the Galapagos Islands. This is the second summit this year for the group.

The main agenda of this meeting is to discuss economic integration among Andean countries and to promote cooperation among the five nations in areas such as the fight against drugs. The heads of state will review a statement drafted by the five foreign ministers over the past two days for the meeting.

Ecuadorian President Borja stated that this meeting is of great significance and will revitalize the Andean Group, setting a new direction for the integration of the Andean region. Venezuelan President Perez hopes the meeting will reach specific agreements to inject new momentum into the Andean Pact.

Perez believes, "If we do not pursue integration, we will not achieve economic independence." Both Perez and Peruvian President Garcia welcomed the possibility of Chile rejoining the Andean Group.

The Galapagos Islands are located 1,000 kilometers off the coast of Ecuador and are famous for their unique animal species, such as giant tortoises. In the last century, Darwin's scientific expedition here had a significant impact on his theory of species evolution. The five presidents first arrived in Ecuador's largest city, Guayaquil, and then flew to the Galapagos. The summit was held on a warship.

### 安第斯集团首脑会议开幕  将为安第斯地区一体化确定新的方向
朱满庭
1989-12-19
第4版(国际新闻)
专栏：

    安第斯集团首脑会议开幕
    将为安第斯地区一体化确定新的方向
    本报加拉加斯12月18日电  记者朱满庭报道：由玻利维亚、哥伦比亚、厄瓜多尔、秘鲁和委内瑞拉组成的安第斯集团5国首脑会议昨天在厄瓜多尔著名自然保护区加拉帕戈斯群岛举行。这是该集团在今年举行的第2次首脑会议。
    这次会议的主要议题是讨论安第斯国家的经济一体化，并推动5国之间在反毒品斗争等领域内的合作。首脑们将审议5国外长在头两天为会议起草的一项声明。
    厄瓜多尔总统博尔哈表示，这次会具有重要意义，将推动安第斯集团重新活跃，为安第斯地区一体化确定新的方向。委内瑞拉总统佩雷斯希望会议能达成具体协议，给安第斯条约注入动力。
    佩雷斯认为，“如果我们不搞一体化，就不会有经济独立”。佩雷斯总统和秘鲁总统加西亚都对智利可能重返安第斯集团表示欢迎。
    加拉帕戈斯群岛距厄瓜多尔海岸1000公里，以其独特的动物种类如大龟等闻名于世。上个世纪达尔文在这里的科学考察对他建立物种进化理论有重大意义。5国总统先抵达厄瓜多尔第一大城市瓜亚基尔，然后乘飞机飞往加拉帕戈斯。首脑会议则是在一条军舰上进行的。
