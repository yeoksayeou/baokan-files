Title: China Wins the King's Cup Sepak Takraw Newcomer's Cup
Author: Huang Yong
Date: 1989-12-09
Edition: 3

China Wins the King's Cup Sepak Takraw Newcomer's Cup

New China News Agency, Bangkok, December 7th (Reporter Huang Yong) The Chinese team defeated the Sri Lankan team and the South Korean team today in the sixth King's Cup Sepak Takraw Invitational Tournament Newcomer's Cup, advancing to the finals.

The Chinese team defeated the American team 2:0 yesterday evening and this morning defeated the Sri Lankan team 2:0 (15:7 and 15:9), securing first place in Group A of the Newcomer's Cup. In the afternoon, they defeated the South Korean team 2:0 (15:7 and 15:9) in the semifinals, advancing to the finals.

In another semifinal match, the Indian team defeated Sri Lanka 2:0 (15:11 and 15:10), also advancing to the finals.

Flash News: The Chinese team won 2:1 against India on the 8th, securing the championship.

### 我获泰王杯藤球赛新秀杯赛冠军
黄勇
1989-12-09
第3版(综合)
专栏：体育

    我获泰王杯藤球赛新秀杯赛冠军
    新华社曼谷12月7日电  （记者黄勇）中国队今天在第六届泰王杯藤球邀请赛新秀杯赛中战胜斯里兰卡队和南朝鲜队后进入决赛。
    中国队昨晚以2∶0战胜美国队，今天上午又以2∶0（15∶7和15∶9）战胜斯里兰卡队，取得新秀杯赛A组第一名。下午在同南朝鲜队进行的半决赛中以2∶0（15∶7和15∶9）获胜，进入决赛。
    在另一场半决赛中，印度队以2∶0（15∶11和15∶10）胜斯里兰卡队，也进入了决赛。
    快讯：中国队8日以2∶1胜印度队，获得冠军。
