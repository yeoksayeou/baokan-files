Title: Joint Open Laboratory for Sensor Technology Established
Author: Zhang Xuequan
Date: 1989-12-18
Page: 2

Xinhua News Agency, Shanghai, December 17th.

The state key joint open laboratory for sensor technology has recently been completed and is now officially open to the public.

The laboratory is located within the Shanghai Institute of Metallurgy of the Chinese Academy of Sciences and the Beijing Semiconductor Research Institute. Relevant personnel introduced that this laboratory focuses on researching and developing sensors with broad significance and substantial application prospects. Visiting scientists can collaborate with the laboratory for research.

### 传感技术联合开放实验室建成
张学全
1989-12-18
第2版(国内新闻)
专栏：四面八方

    传感技术联合开放实验室建成
    新华社上海12月17日电　（记者张学全）国家重点传感技术联合开放实验室新近建成已正式对外开放。
    该实验室分设于中科院上海冶金研究所和北京半导体研究所内。有关人士介绍说，这个实验室研究与开发具有普遍意义和重大应用前景的传感器，客座科学家可与实验室合作研究。
