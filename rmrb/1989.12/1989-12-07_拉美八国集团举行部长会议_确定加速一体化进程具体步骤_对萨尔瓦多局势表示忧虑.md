Title: Latin American Eight-Nation Group Holds Ministerial Meeting
Author: Lǐ Zhìmíng
Date: 1989-12-07
Edition: 4

The Latin American Eight-Nation Group (excluding Panama) held a two-day ministerial meeting here, after which they signed the "Buenos Aires Declaration," outlining specific steps to accelerate Latin American integration.

This ministerial meeting was convened in accordance with the decisions of the third summit of the Eight-Nation Group. The meeting discussed specific steps to accelerate Latin American integration by 1992. The "Buenos Aires Declaration" issued after the meeting announced that in the area of trade, member countries have agreed on the following actions: 1) Eliminate mutual non-tariff barriers in trade (no new restrictions will be added by July 1990, and non-tariff restrictions on products currently enjoying tariff preferences will be lifted starting April 1); 2) Expand tariff preferences for all products (the current level of tariff preferences will be increased by 50% starting next April); 3) Expand and deepen bilateral trade agreements; 4) Reduce tariff levels through negotiations. The first round of trade negotiations of the Latin American Integration Association will be held in the second half of 1990 to discuss the gradual elimination of non-tariff restrictions and the reduction of tariff rates.

The foreign ministers of the Latin American Eight-Nation Group (excluding Panama) issued a news statement here today, expressing concern over the situation in El Salvador. They called on all parties involved to cease hostile actions, urged foreign countries to refrain from intervention, and hoped that the peace process would be restored based on the spirit of the second summit of Central American heads of state in Esquipulas.

### 拉美八国集团举行部长会议  确定加速一体化进程具体步骤  对萨尔瓦多局势表示忧虑
李志明
1989-12-07
第4版(国际新闻)
专栏：

    拉美八国集团举行部长会议
    确定加速一体化进程具体步骤
    对萨尔瓦多局势表示忧虑
    本报布宜诺斯艾利斯12月5日电　记者李志明报道：拉美8国集团（巴拿马暂缺）外交、经济、计划部长在这里举行两天会议之后，今天签署了《布宜诺斯艾利斯宣言》，就加速拉美一体化进程提出了一系列具体步骤。
    这次部长会议是根据8国集团第三次首脑会议的决定召开的。会议讨论了在1992年以前加速实现拉美一体化的具体步骤。会后发表的《布宜诺斯艾利斯宣言》宣布，在贸易方面，各成员国就下列行动达成协议：1、取消相互间贸易的非关税壁垒（1990年7月之前不再增加新的限制，4月1日起取消对现在享有关税优惠的产品的非关税限制）；2、扩大对所有产品的关税优惠（明年4月1日起现有关税优惠水平再扩大50％）；3、扩大和深化双边贸易协议；4、通过谈判降低关税水平。1990年下半年召开拉美一体化协会第一轮贸易谈判，讨论逐步取消非关税限制和降低关税率问题。
    本报布宜诺斯艾利斯12月5日电　记者李志明报道：拉美8国集团（巴拿马暂缺）的外交部长今天在这里发表一项新闻公报，对萨尔瓦多局势表示忧虑，呼吁当事各方停止敌对行动，要求外国放弃干涉行动，希望在中美洲国家元首第二次埃斯基普拉斯会议精神基础上恢复和平进程。
