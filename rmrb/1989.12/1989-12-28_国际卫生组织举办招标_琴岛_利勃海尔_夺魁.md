Title: International Health Organization Holds Bidding Event, "Qin Island—Liebert Haier" Takes the Crown
Author:
Date: 1989-12-28
Edition: 2

The International Health Organization recently held an international bidding event in Beijing. "Qin Island—Liebert Haier" refrigerators emerged victorious in the intense competition among global refrigeration powerhouses, securing the top spot and bringing honor to China's refrigerator industry.

In the bidding event organized by the International Health Organization using World Bank loans to procure medical refrigeration equipment, three models produced by Qingdao Refrigerator Factory—"Qin Island—Liebert Haier" refrigerators—were successful: the 212-liter and 220-liter luxury models, both of which won the National Quality Gold Award, as well as the 132-liter energy-efficient upright freezer.

### 国际卫生组织举办招标  “琴岛—利勃海尔”夺魁

1989-12-28
第2版(国内新闻)
专栏：四面八方

    国际卫生组织举办招标
    “琴岛—利勃海尔”夺魁
    本报北京12月27日讯记者许正中报道：国际卫生组织举办的国际招标活动，日前在京揭标：“琴岛—利勃海尔”电冰箱在世界制冷强国竞争激烈的角逐中，一举中标夺魁，为我国冰箱行业争得了荣誉。
    在国际卫生组织利用世界银行贷款为选购医用制冷器械而举办的国际招标活动中，青岛电冰箱总厂生产的“琴岛—利勃海尔”有三个规格的品种中标：获国优金牌的212升和豪华型220升电冰箱以及节能型132升立式冷冻箱。
