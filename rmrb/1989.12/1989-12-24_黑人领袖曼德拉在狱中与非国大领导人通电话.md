Title: Black Leader Mandela Makes Phone Call from Prison to ANC Leaders
Author:
Date: 1989-12-24
Edition: 4

    Black leader Mandela makes phone call from prison to ANC leaders

    Xinhua News Agency, Gaborone, December 22 — According to Radio Botswana, the South African authorities allowed the imprisoned South African black leader Nelson Mandela to make a phone call from prison to the leaders of the African National Congress in exile in Zimbabwe on December 21.

    The report said this is an "interesting change" in the situation in South Africa.

    According to the South African "Star" newspaper, Mandela met with 84-year-old human rights activist Helen Joseph on December 21 and had a conversation lasting several hours.

### 黑人领袖曼德拉在狱中与非国大领导人通电话

1989-12-24
第4版(国际新闻)
专栏：

    黑人领袖曼德拉在狱中与非国大领导人通电话
    新华社哈博罗内12月22日电  据博茨瓦纳电台报道，南非当局21日首次容许被监禁的南非黑人领袖曼德拉，从狱中打电话与在津巴布韦流亡的南非非洲人国民大会领导人进行谈话。
    报道说，这是南非形势一个“有趣的变化”。
    另据南非《星报》报道，曼德拉21日会见了84岁的人权活动家海伦·约瑟夫夫人，并进行了数小时的谈话。
