Title: Britain to Grant British Citizenship to 225,000 Hong Kong Residents
Author:
Date: 1989-12-22
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, London, December 20th.

	Britain will grant British citizenship to approximately 225,000 Hong Kong residents before 1997, announced British Foreign Secretary Douglas Hurd today. This plan will include about 50,000 Hong Kong families.

	Hurd stated that a points system will be used to select the Hong Kong residents who will receive British citizenship, and they will come from various industries in Hong Kong.

	Gerald Kaufman, the shadow foreign secretary of the opposition Labour Party, criticized the government's move as "impractical." He said that if the Labour Party comes to power in the future, it will not be obligated to continue this policy.

### 英将给22.5万香港人英国国籍

1989-12-22
第4版(国际新闻)
专栏：

    英将给22.5万香港人英国国籍
    新华社伦敦12月20日电  英国外交大臣道格拉斯·赫德今天宣布，英国将在1997年以前向约22.5万香港人发放英国国籍，将有5万个香港家庭被纳入这一计划。
    赫德说，将采用积分制的方法选择那些将获得英国国籍的香港人，他们将来自香港的各个行业。
    英国反对党工党的影子外交大臣杰拉尔德·考夫曼称政府的这一举动“难以实行”。他说，如果工党今后执政，它将认为没有义务继续执行这一政策。
