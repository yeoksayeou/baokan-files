Title: Chinese Economic and Trade Delegation Visits Saudi Arabia
Author:
Date: 1989-12-10
Edition: 4

A Chinese high-level economic and trade delegation led by the president of the China Council for the Promotion of International Trade, Zheng Hongye, concluded its visit to the Saudi Arabian capital of Riyadh today and departed for Saudi industrial cities including Dammam, Jubail, and Dhahran to continue their visit.

The delegation arrived in Riyadh on December 3 at the invitation of the Saudi Chamber of Commerce and Industry.

Saudi Arabia's Minister of Commerce and Industry, Sulaiman Salim, met with the entire delegation. Both sides exchanged views on the prospects for Sino-Saudi trade and economic and technological cooperation. They agreed that the prospects for economic and trade relations between China and Saudi Arabia are broad.

### 我经贸代表团访问沙特

1989-12-10
第4版(国际新闻)
专栏：

    我经贸代表团访问沙特
    新华社科威特12月8日电　由中国国际商会会长郑鸿业率领的中国高级经济贸易代表团今天结束了在沙特阿拉伯首都利雅得的访问，前往沙特工业城市达兰、朱拜勒、达曼等地继续进行访问。
    该代表团是应沙特商工联合会的邀请于12月3日到达利雅得访问的。
    沙特政府贸易大臣苏莱曼·萨利姆会见了代表团全体成员。双方就中国沙特贸易前景、经济技术合作等问题广泛地交换了意见，一致认为中国沙特两国的经济贸易前景广阔。
