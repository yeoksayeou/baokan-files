Title: Discuss Regional Issues to Advance Peace Process: Mexico and Guatemala Call for Central American Summit

Author: Guo Weicheng

Date: 1989-12-02

Edition: 4

	Mexico City, November 28 (Xinhua) — Mexican Foreign Minister Fernando Solana today called for an emergency summit of the five Central American presidents to resume dialogue and seek reconciliation.

	Solana made the remarks while presenting his foreign policy report to the Mexican Senate. He said, "Central America is the basic space and priority region of Mexico's foreign policy." Mexico believes it is urgent to rebuild the reconciliation process in El Salvador, engage in dialogue, and stop hostilities. Mexico is concerned about Nicaragua, respects the electoral process in Nicaragua, and is optimistic about it. Mexico reiterated its demand to resolve the Panama issue in the "Panamanian way" and the effectiveness of the new U.S.-Panama Canal Treaty.

	Solana also reiterated that due to the huge differences in development among North American countries, "establishing a Mexico-Canada-United States common market is inappropriate."

	Mexico City, November 29 (Xinhua) — Guatemalan President Cerezo today proposed a summit of Central American presidents to discuss the Central American peace process and the internal conflict in El Salvador.

	President Cerezo suggested holding the summit of the five Central American countries from December 10 to 15 in Guatemala. According to the resolution signed by the five presidents at the August summit in Tegucigalpa, Honduras, the next summit was originally scheduled for December 9 in Nicaragua. Due to the Salvadoran government's suspension of diplomatic relations with Nicaragua and its refusal to attend the summit in Nicaragua, the original plan was scrapped. Therefore, Guatemalan President Cerezo proposed changing the venue to Guatemala. He also said he had agreed with Costa Rican President Arias to persuade Salvadoran President Cristiani to attend the next summit.

### 讨论地区问题　推进和平进程  墨危两国要求召开中美洲首脑会议
郭伟成
1989-12-02
第4版(国际新闻)
专栏：

    讨论地区问题　推进和平进程
    墨危两国要求召开中美洲首脑会议
    本报墨西哥城11月28日电　记者郭伟成报道：墨西哥外交部长费尔南多·索拉纳今天要求中美洲5国首脑召开紧急会议，重新对话，争取和解。
    索拉纳外长是在向墨西哥参议院提交外交政策报告及作证时作出上述表示的。他指出，中美洲是“墨西哥外交政策的基本空间及优先地区”。墨西哥认为，有必要刻不容缓地在萨尔瓦多重建和解过程，进行对话，停止敌对。墨西哥关注尼加拉瓜，尊重尼加拉瓜的大选进程，并对此感到乐观。墨西哥重申要求以“巴拿马方式”解决巴拿马问题以及美巴运河新条约的有效性。
    索拉纳外长还重申，由于北美洲各国在发展方面存在的巨大差异，“建立墨西哥—加拿大—美国共同市场是不合适的”。
    本报墨西哥城11月29日电　记者郭伟成报道：危地马拉总统塞雷索今天要求召开中美洲首脑会议，讨论中美洲和平进程及萨尔瓦多内部冲突问题。
    塞雷索总统建议12月10日至15日在危地马拉召开中美洲5国首脑会议。按照5国首脑今年8月在洪都拉斯特拉会议签署的决议，下次首脑会议原定12月9日在尼加拉瓜举行。由于萨尔瓦多政府中止了与尼加拉瓜的外交关系，并拒绝出席在尼加拉瓜召开的会议，原计划告吹。危地马拉总统塞雷索因此建议把会议改在危地马拉举行。他还说他已同哥斯达黎加总统阿里亚斯商定，一同劝说萨尔瓦多总统克里斯蒂亚尼出席下次会议。
