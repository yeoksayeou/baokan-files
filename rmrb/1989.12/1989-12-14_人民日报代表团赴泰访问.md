Title: People's Daily Delegation Visits Thailand
Author:
Date: 1989-12-14
Page: 2

A delegation from the People's Daily, led by Editor-in-Chief Shao Huaze, consisting of 5 people, departed for Thailand today at 10:00 AM. The delegation was invited by the Thai newspaper "Xin Zhongyuan Bao."

### 人民日报代表团赴泰访问

1989-12-14
第2版(国内新闻)
专栏：

    人民日报代表团赴泰访问
    本报北京12月13日讯　由总编辑邵华泽率领的《人民日报》代表团一行5人，应泰国《新中原报》邀请，今天上午乘飞机赴泰国访问。
