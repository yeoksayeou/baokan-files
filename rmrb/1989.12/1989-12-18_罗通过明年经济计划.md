Title: Romania Approves Economic Plan for Next Year
Author:
Date: 1989-12-18
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Bucharest, December 14th.

The 10th session of the 9th Great National Assembly of Romania discussed and approved the economic plan and state budget for 1990.

According to this plan, Romania's national income will grow by 9% to 10% in 1990, social production value will increase by 7% to 8%, total industrial output will rise by 7% to 8.5%, and foreign trade will grow by more than 11%.

The plan stipulates that Romania will continue to implement a high accumulation policy, with next year's investment exceeding 290 billion lei. The focus of investments will be on expanding the production of domestic raw materials and the development of energy resources, as well as the technological renovation of factories and mines.

### 罗通过明年经济计划

1989-12-18
第4版(国际新闻)
专栏：

    罗通过明年经济计划
    新华社布加勒斯特12月14日电　据此间报道，今天举行的罗马尼亚第9届大国民议会第10次会议讨论并通过了1990年经济计划和国家预算。
    根据这一计划1990年罗马尼亚国民收入将增长9％至10％，社会产值增长7％至8％，工业总产值增长7％至8.5％，外贸增长11％以上。
    计划规定罗将继续执行高积累政策，明年的投资额将超过2900亿列伊。投资的重点是扩大本国原材料的生产和能源的开发，对厂矿企业进行技术改造。
