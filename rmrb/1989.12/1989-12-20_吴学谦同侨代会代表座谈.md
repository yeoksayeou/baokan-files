Title: Wu Xueqian Holds a Discussion with Representatives of the Fourth Overseas Chinese Congress
Author:
Date: 1989-12-20
Edition: 2

Wu Xueqian, a member of the Political Bureau of the CPC Central Committee and Vice Premier of the State Council, today held a discussion with representatives attending the Fourth Overseas Chinese Congress. He said that in the current situation of rectification and deepening reform, the work of the Overseas Chinese Federation should adhere to the principle of local conditions, coordinate various relationships, and strive to give full play to the role of returned overseas Chinese, relatives of overseas Chinese, and overseas Chinese.

During the discussion, representatives from provinces, municipalities, and autonomous regions such as Hunan, Fujian, Zhejiang, Taiwan, Guangdong, Shanghai, Guangxi, and Hainan spoke on issues such as further implementing policies for overseas Chinese, legislation for overseas Chinese affairs, how the work of the Overseas Chinese Federation can serve economic construction, and how to strengthen ties with overseas Chinese.

After listening to the representatives' speeches, Wu Xueqian said that from the representatives' speeches, it can be seen that everyone is very enthusiastic about doing a good job in the future work of the Overseas Chinese Federation. Many representatives have long been engaged in overseas Chinese work and have rich experience. He believes that through this conference, the work of the Overseas Chinese Federation will definitely usher in a new situation. He hopes that the Overseas Chinese Federation will fully play its role in democratic supervision and play a role in the four modernizations and the unification of the motherland.

After the discussion, Wu Xueqian also visited the representatives from Hong Kong and Macao attending the conference.

### 吴学谦同侨代会代表座谈

1989-12-20
第2版(国内新闻)
专栏：

    吴学谦同侨代会代表座谈
    新华社北京12月19日电　中共中央政治局委员、国务院副总理吴学谦今天在与出席四届侨代会的代表座谈时说，在目前治理整顿、深化改革的形势下，侨联的工作要坚持因地制宜的原则，要协调好各方面的关系，努力发挥归侨、侨眷和海外侨胞的作用。
    座谈会上，湖南、福建、浙江、台湾、广东、上海、广西、海南等省、市、自治区的代表分别就进一步落实侨务政策、侨务立法、侨联工作如何为经济建设服务、如何加强与海外侨胞的联络等问题发表了意见。
    吴学谦在听取代表们的发言后说，从代表们的发言中可以看出，大家对搞好今后侨联的工作干劲很足，许多代表长期从事侨务工作，经验很丰富，相信通过这次会议，侨联工作一定会出现一个新的局面。他希望侨联充分发挥民主监督职能，在四化建设和祖国统一中发挥作用。
    座谈会后，吴学谦还看望了参加大会的港澳代表。
