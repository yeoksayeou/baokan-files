Title: Hong Kong Phone Dialing to Be Simplified
Author:
Date: 1989-12-05
Edition: 2

From December 30 of this year, the Hong Kong Telephone Company will adopt a simplified dialing method. At that time, all Hong Kong telephone numbers will be uniformly changed to 7 digits. The current area codes of 5, 3, and 0, which respectively represent Hong Kong Island, Kowloon, and the New Territories, will be abolished. The response numbers for local facsimile machines will also be adjusted accordingly.

### 香港电话拨号开始简化

1989-12-05
第2版(国内新闻)
专栏：四面八方

    香港电话拨号开始简化
    新华社香港12月4日电　从今年12月30日起，香港电话公司将采用简便拨号方式，届时香港所有电话号码均一律改为7位数字，现在分别代表港岛、九龙和新界的5、3和0地区字头都将取消。本地的图文传真机的回应号也将作相应改动。
