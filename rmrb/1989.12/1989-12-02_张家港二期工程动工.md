Title: Construction Begins on Zhangjiagang Phase II Project
Author:
Date: 1989-12-02
Page: 2

Xinhua News Agency, Nanjing, December 1st.

The Zhangjiagang Phase II Project, approved by the State Council, officially began construction today. This "Seventh Five-Year Plan" national key construction project, with a total investment of 2.6 billion yuan, will build five 10,000-ton deep-water berths for containers, minerals, timber, coal, and general cargo. It will also open a new inland port basin and its supporting facilities, covering an area of over 1,000 acres. After completion, the port will increase its handling capacity by 6.4 million tons. By then, the port's total production capacity will exceed 10 million tons, making it a major national port and a significant international commercial port. This will further promote the economic prosperity of the Suzhou, Wuxi, and Changzhou regions.

### 张家港二期工程动工

1989-12-02
第2版(要闻)
专栏：四面八方

    张家港二期工程动工
    本报南京12月1日电　经国务院批准的张家港二期工程，今天正式开锤动工。这项总投资达2.6亿元的“七五”国家重点建设工程，将新建集装箱、矿建、木材、煤炭、件杂5个万吨级深水泊位，新开内河港池一座及其配套设施，占地面积1000多亩。码头建成后，新增吞吐能力640万吨。届时，全港生产能力将突破1000万吨，步入全国大港行列，成为一个规模可观的国际商港，进一步推进苏、锡、常地区经济的繁荣。
