Title: Exhibition of Li Renyi's New Fourth Army Portraits
Author:
Date: 1989-12-30
Edition: 8

The exhibition of Li Renyi's New Fourth Army portraits is currently on display at the China Art Gallery in Beijing. The exhibition is jointly organized by the China Art Gallery, the Cultural Department of the Shenyang Military Region, the Propaganda Department of the CPC Salt City Committee, the 81043 Unit of the People's Liberation Army, and the New Fourth Army Memorial Hall. The portrait of Comrade Liu Shaoqi is shown above.
