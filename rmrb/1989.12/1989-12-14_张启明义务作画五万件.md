Title: Zhang Qiming Volunteers to Paint 50,000 Pieces
Author: Hu Youheng  Yang Junfeng
Date: 1989-12-14
Page: 3

Xinhua News Agency, Taiyuan, December 13th

Zhang Qiming, a member of the Chinese Calligraphers Association, vice chairman of the Shanxi Province Artists Association, and director of the Cultural Bureau of Xinzhou Region, Shanxi Province, has volunteered to write and paint more than 50,000 pieces for the public over the past 26 years. When invited to write plaques or banners for people, he also does not charge a single cent.
