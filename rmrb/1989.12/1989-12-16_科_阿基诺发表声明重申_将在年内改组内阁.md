Title: Corazon Aquino Reiterates Commitment to Cabinet Reshuffle by Year-End
Author:
Date: 1989-12-16
Edition: 4

Philippine President Corazon Aquino on December 14 reiterated in a statement that she will reshuffle her cabinet before the end of the year.

Aquino's decision comes amid growing public calls for reform following an attempted coup in the Philippines on December 1.

Aquino stated, "All cabinet members have either individually or collectively expressed that their fate will be decided by me, and I can replace anyone at any time." She emphasized that actions in such circumstances must be taken with caution and not hastily.

Reports indicate that all 25 members of Aquino's cabinet have offered to resign voluntarily, allowing the president to freely reshuffle the cabinet.

### 科·阿基诺发表声明重申  将在年内改组内阁

1989-12-16
第4版(国际新闻)
专栏：

    科·阿基诺发表声明重申
    将在年内改组内阁
    新华社马尼拉12月15日电　菲律宾总统科·阿基诺14日在一份声明中重申，她将在年底之前改组内阁。
    科·阿基诺总统的这一决定是在12月1日菲律宾发生未遂政变后人民要求改革的呼声日高的情况下作出的。
    科·阿基诺说：“所有内阁成员都只是单独地或集体地表示，他们的去留将由我决定，我可以在任何时候调换任何人。”她强调，在这种情况下行事必须谨慎而不能匆忙。
    据报道，科·阿基诺内阁的25个成员表示自愿辞职，以便让科·阿基诺总统能够自由地改组内阁。
