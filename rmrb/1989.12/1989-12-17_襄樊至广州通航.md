Title: Shiyan to Guangzhou Air Route Opens
Author: Zhang Hongzhi
Date: 1989-12-17
Page: 2

From Four Corners:

A civil airport primarily funded by local resources—Shiyan Liuji Airport—was completed and began operations in December 1989. On the 14th, a Boeing 737 aircraft carrying 126 passengers successfully completed its maiden voyage from Shiyan to Guangzhou.

The completion of this airport will play a significant role in promoting Shiyan's economic development and the economic opening of northwestern Hubei.

### 襄樊至广州通航
张鸿志
1989-12-17
第2版(国内新闻)
专栏：四面八方

    襄樊至广州通航
    本报讯　一家主要由地方筹资兴建的民用机场——襄樊刘集机场1989年12月建成通航。14日，一架满载126名旅客的波音737客机圆满完成了襄樊至广州的首航。
    这个机场的建成对襄樊的经济建设，及鄂西北的经济开发，将起到促进作用。
              （张鸿志）
