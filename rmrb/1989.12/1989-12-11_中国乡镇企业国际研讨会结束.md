Title: The First International Symposium on China's Township Enterprises Concludes
Author: Han Xiaofeng
Date: 1989-12-11
Edition: 2

The rapid development of China's township enterprises over the past decade has drawn significant attention from the international economic community. Today, the first international symposium on China's township enterprises concluded here, sending a message that some developing countries are considering formulating their own rural economic development strategies based on China's experiences in developing township enterprises. The symposium was organized by the United Nations Economic and Social Commission for Asia and the Pacific.

### 中国乡镇企业国际研讨会结束
韩晓烽
1989-12-11
第2版(国内新闻)
专栏：

    中国乡镇企业国际研讨会结束
    据新华社杭州12月10日电　（记者韩晓烽）在10年改革中迅猛发展起来的中国乡镇企业，引起了国际经济界的极大关注。今天在这里结束的首次中国乡镇企业国际研讨会传出的信息表明，一些发展中国家已考虑根据中国乡镇企业发展的经验，制定本国乡村经济发展的战略规划。研讨会由联合国亚洲及太平洋地区经济及社会委员会召开。
