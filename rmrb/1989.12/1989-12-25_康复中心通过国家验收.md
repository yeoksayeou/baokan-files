Title: Rehabilitation Center Passes National Inspection
Author: Xú Yùnpíng, Zhāng Míngwàng
Date: 1989-12-25
Page: 2

    Four Corners

    The China Rehabilitation Research Center recently passed a national inspection in Beijing. The establishment of this center brings good news to the more than 51.64 million disabled people in China and provides an excellent venue for the exchange between China's rehabilitation industry and the international rehabilitation community.

    Deng Pufang, Chairman of the China Disabled Persons' Federation, attended the inspection and expressed his congratulations.

    As the first and largest rehabilitation center in China and Asia, this center comprehensively utilizes modern technologies in medicine, engineering, social work, psychology, and vocational rehabilitation, along with traditional Chinese medicine, to provide comprehensive rehabilitation services that integrate medical treatment, education, and research. It is designed to handle 300 outpatient visits per day.

### 康复中心通过国家验收
徐运平　张明旺
1989-12-25
第2版(国内新闻)
专栏：四面八方

    康复中心通过国家验收
    本报北京12月24日讯　中国康复研究中心近日在北京通过国家验收。该中心的建成给我国5164万多残疾人带来了福音，也给我国康复事业与国际康复界的交流提供了良好的场所。
    中国残联主席邓朴方参加验收会并表示祝贺。
    作为中国第一家也是亚洲最大的康复中心是综合运用医学、工程、社会、心理、职业康复等现代技术和祖国传统医学对残疾人进行全面康复的医教研三结合的机构，设计门诊量300人次。
      （徐运平　张明旺）
