Title: Daughter of Indian Home Minister Released After Kidnapping
Author:
Date: 1989-12-15
Edition: 4

The daughter of Indian Home Minister Mufti Muhammad Sayeed, Rabia, who was kidnapped on December 8 in the Kashmir region, was released on December 13.

Following the incident, negotiations were conducted through a third party. The government made concessions and agreed to the kidnappers' demands, releasing five detained members of the "Kashmir Liberation Front" in the afternoon of December 13. Subsequently, Rabia was released and flew from Srinagar to New Delhi on an Indian Air Force special aircraft.

### 被绑架的印内政部长女儿获释

1989-12-15
第4版(国际新闻)
专栏：

    被绑架的印内政部长女儿获释
    八日在克什米尔地区被绑架的印度内政部长穆夫蒂·穆罕默德·赛义德的女儿拉比亚十三日获释。
    事发后，双方通过第三者进行了紧张的谈判。政府方面作了让步，同意绑架者提出的条件，在十三日下午释放了被关押的五名“克什米尔解放阵线”活动分子。之后拉比亚被释，并乘坐印度空军专机从斯内那加飞往新德里。（本报专电）
