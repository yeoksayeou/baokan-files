Title: Experts from the US, Japan, and Europe Hold a Seminar
Author:
Date: 1989-12-05
Edition: 4

	Experts from the US, Japan, and Europe Hold a Seminar
	Stress Strengthening of Japan-Europe Defense Ties

	Xinhua News Agency, Tokyo, December 2nd - Defense experts from the United States, Japan, and Western Europe concluded a two-day seminar in Tokyo today, emphasizing the need to establish a new security consultation mechanism. They stressed the importance of strengthening the weak link among the three parties—the Japan-Europe relationship—to place the three sides in an equal "circular" relationship.

	At this seminar, organized by the Japanese Ministry of Foreign Affairs, former U.S. Assistant Secretary of Defense Richard Armitage said that new channels of communication are needed among the U.S., Japan, and Europe to "coordinate and act together on the defense stage." He pointed out that the weak Japan-Europe link prevents the three parties from fully coordinating their actions.

	Pierre Lellouche, an aide to French conservative leader Jacques Chirac, admitted that Europe currently "ignores Japan" in defense matters. He advocated finding new channels of communication for Japan-Europe relations beyond the annual summit of the Seven Western Nations.

	West German defense expert Uwe Nerrlich also emphasized the need to increase high-level contacts between Japan and Europe to strengthen this weak link.

	Professor Nishihara of the Japan Defense Research Institute said that the security consultation mechanism between Japan and the U.S. also needs to be strengthened. He suggested that the two countries could jointly propose initiatives on Pacific regional security to adjust the current passive response strategies toward the Soviet Union.

### 美日欧专家举行研讨会  强调加强日欧防务联系

1989-12-05
第4版(国际新闻)
专栏：

    美日欧专家举行研讨会
    强调加强日欧防务联系
    新华社东京12月2日电  美国、日本和西欧的防卫问题专家今天结束了在东京举行的为期两天的研讨会，强调要建立新的安全协商机制，加强美、日、欧三边中的薄弱环节——日欧关系，使三方处于等同的“圆周状”关系之中。
    在这次由日本外务省主办的研讨会上，美国前助理国防部长理查德·阿米塔奇说，在美、日、欧三边中需要寻求新的联系渠道，使三方“协调起来，在防卫舞台上共同发挥作用”。他指出，薄弱的日欧联系，使三方不能完满地协调动作。
    法国保守党领袖雅克·希拉克的助手皮埃尔·勒鲁什承认目前欧洲在防卫问题上“十分忽视日本”。他主张日欧关系需要在每年举行西方7国首脑会议以外寻找新的联系途径。
    西德防卫问题专家乌韦·内尔利希也强调要增加日欧之间的最高层接触，以加强日欧关系这个薄弱环节。
    日本防卫研究所西原教授说，日美之间的安全协商体制也要得到加强。他建议双方可以就太平洋地区的安全提出共同倡议，以调整两国目前在对苏战略上采取的被动反应态势。
