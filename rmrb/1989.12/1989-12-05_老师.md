Title: Teacher
Author: Pan Jianli
Date: 1989-12-05
Edition: 8

	Teacher
	Pan Jianli
	You are the sun that rises on the podium,
	Illuminating the hopes of tomorrow.
	Therefore,
	Generation after generation of young pioneers
	Follow the path bathed in your wisdom,
		—Filled with the era's
		Faith and profound meaning—
		And step into the dreams and aspirations
		You have long cherished.
