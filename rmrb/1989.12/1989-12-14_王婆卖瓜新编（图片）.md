Title: A New Version of Wang Po Selling Melons
Author: Wang Chunsheng
Date: 1989-12-14
Page: 5

Originally published in the *Tianjin Daily*

In the bustling streets of Tianjin, a familiar figure has emerged once again—Wang Po, the renowned melon seller. However, this time, Wang Po is not just selling melons; she is selling a new kind of melon, one that embodies the spirit of reform and innovation.

Wang Po's melons are not ordinary. They are the result of years of experimentation and dedication. She has crossed different varieties, selected the best traits, and nurtured them with care. The melons she sells are not only sweet and juicy but also have a unique texture and flavor that sets them apart from the rest.

Wang Po's melons have become a sensation in Tianjin. People from all walks of life come to her stall, eager to taste the fruit of her labor. Her melons are not just a treat for the palate; they are a symbol of progress and achievement. They represent the hard work, creativity, and perseverance of the Chinese people in their quest for a better future.

Wang Po's success story is a testament to the power of innovation and the spirit of entrepreneurship. She has shown that with determination and hard work, one can achieve great things. Her melons are a reminder that the path to success is not always easy, but with perseverance and a willingness to take risks, anything is possible.

As Wang Po continues to sell her melons, she inspires others to follow in her footsteps. She is a beacon of hope and a symbol of the new China—a China that embraces change, values innovation, and strives for excellence. Her story is a new chapter in the annals of Chinese entrepreneurship, a chapter that promises a bright and prosperous future for all.
