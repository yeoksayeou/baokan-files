Title: Shanghai People's Congress Standing Committee 13th Session Elects Jiang Zemin as a Representative
Author: Xiao Guan Gen
Date: 1989-12-29
Edition: 1

Shanghai, December 28 (Xinhua) - The 13th session of the Standing Committee of the 9th Shanghai People's Congress today elected Jiang Zemin as a representative to the 7th National People's Congress.

The Communist Party of China Central Committee, in accordance with the Election Law, recently nominated Jiang Zemin as a candidate for the 7th National People's Congress. The Standing Committee of the Shanghai People's Congress, in accordance with the 3rd paragraph of Article 42 of the Election Law, which states that "during the recess of a local people's congress at or above the county level, the standing committee of the people's congress at the same level may fill vacancies for deputies to the people's congress at the next higher level," included the proposal to fill a vacancy for a National People's Congress representative in the agenda for this session of the Standing Committee of the Shanghai People's Congress. The members of the Standing Committee present voted by secret ballot and unanimously elected Jiang Zemin as a representative to the National People's Congress.

### 上海人大常委会13次会议  补选江泽民为人大代表
萧关根
1989-12-29
第1版(要闻)
专栏：

    上海人大常委会13次会议
    补选江泽民为人大代表
    本报上海12月28日电　记者萧关根报道：上海市第九届人大常委会第13次会议，今天补选江泽民同志为第七届全国人民代表大会代表。
    中共中央根据《选举法》规定，最近提名江泽民同志为七届全国人民代表大会代表候选人。上海市人大常委会按照《选举法》第42条第3款关于“县级以上的地方各级人民代表大会闭会期间，可以由本级人民代表大会常务委员会补选上一级人民代表大会代表”的规定，将补选全国人大代表的议案列入市人大常委会本次会议的议程，参加会议的常委会组成人员以无记名投票方式，一致选举江泽民同志为全国人大代表。
