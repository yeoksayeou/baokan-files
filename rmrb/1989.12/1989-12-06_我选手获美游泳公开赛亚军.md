Title: Chinese Athlete Wins Silver at U.S. Open Swimming Championships
Author:
Date: 1989-12-06
Page: 3

Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, December 5th.

Chinese athletes achieved notable results at the U.S. Open Swimming Championships held in Orlando on December 4th. Wang Xiaohong, a Chinese swimmer, secured the silver medal in the women's 100-meter butterfly event with a time of 1 minute and 3 seconds.

The gold medal in this event was won by American swimmer J. Jorgensen, who completed the race in 1 minute and 2.12 seconds.

American swimmers also won gold medals in the men's 100-meter butterfly, men's 400-meter individual medley, women's 100-meter backstroke, women's 200-meter freestyle, and women's 400-meter individual medley. Soviet and Swedish athletes won the gold medals in the men's 100-meter backstroke and men's 200-meter freestyle, respectively.

Chinese female swimmers Zhang Yong and Lin Li participated in the 200-meter freestyle and 400-meter individual medley events, finishing in seventh and sixth place with times of 2 minutes and 5.03 seconds and 4 minutes and 55.49 seconds, respectively.

### 我选手获美游泳公开赛亚军

1989-12-06
第3版(综合)
专栏：体育

    我选手获美游泳公开赛亚军
    新华社北京12月5日电　据外电报道，美国游泳公开赛4日在奥兰多又进行了几个项目的决赛，中国运动员王晓红在女子100米蝶泳比赛中，以1分03秒的成绩获得亚军。
    获得该项目第一名的是美国选手贾·乔根森，她的成绩为1分02秒12。
    美国选手今天还获得了男子100米蝶泳和400米混合泳，女子100米仰泳、200米自由泳和400米混合泳金牌。苏联选手和瑞典选手分别夺得男子100米仰泳和200米自由泳冠军。
    中国女选手庄泳和林莉参加了200米自由泳和400米混合泳的比赛，分别以2分05秒03和4分55秒49列第七和第六名。
