Title: China and Iran Sign Cooperation Memorandum
Author:
Date: 1989-12-01
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Tehran, November 28th.

China and Iran signed an economic, trade, scientific, and technological cooperation memorandum today. The memorandum was signed by visiting Chinese Minister of Foreign Economic Relations and Trade Zheng Tuobin and Iranian Minister of Economy and Finance Nouri Bahesh at the conclusion of the fifth session of the China-Iran Economic, Trade, Scientific, and Technological Cooperation Joint Committee.

According to the memorandum, starting from January 1, 1990, all payments between the two countries will be settled in convertible currencies.

The two ministers expressed satisfaction with the two-day meeting and hoped that the cash settlement method would resolve the problems caused by the barter trade system that has been in place since 1973. The cash settlement method will also apply to economic and technological cooperation between the two countries.

### 中国伊朗签订合作备忘录  中伊将改变贸易结算方式

1989-12-01
第4版(国际新闻)
专栏：

    中国伊朗签订合作备忘录
    中伊将改变贸易结算方式
    新华社德黑兰11月28日电  中国和伊朗今天签订经济、贸易、科学和技术合作备忘录。
    备忘录是在中伊经济、贸易、科学和技术合作联合委员会第5次会议结束时，由来访的中国对外经贸部长郑拓彬和伊朗经济和财政部长努尔巴赫什共同签署的。
    根据这份备忘录，从1990年1月1日起，两国间所有支付都将以可兑换货币结算。
    两国部长对为期两天的会议感到满意，并希望现金结算方式将会解决从1973年开始实行的易货贸易所带来的问题。
    现金结算方式还适用于两国的经济和科技领域的合作。
