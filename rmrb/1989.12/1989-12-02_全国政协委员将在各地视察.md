Title: National CPPCC Members to Conduct Inspections Across the Country
Author:
Date: 1989-12-02
Page: 2

Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, December 1st.

The Office of the National Committee of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference (CPPCC) recently issued a notice stating that CPPCC members in various regions should conduct inspections in their respective areas before the convening of the third session of the Seventh National Committee of the CPPCC next year.

The notice specifies that the focus of this inspection will be to understand the implementation of the spirit of the Fourth and Fifth Plenary Sessions of the Thirteenth Central Committee of the Communist Party of China, the progress of further rectification and deepening of reforms, and the activities of the CPPCC in conducting political consultations and democratic supervision.

### 全国政协委员将在各地视察

1989-12-02
第2版(要闻)
专栏：

    全国政协委员将在各地视察
    新华社北京12月1日电　全国政协办公厅最近发出通知提出，各地政协委员于明年七届全国政协第三次会议召开之前，在所在地进行视察。
    通知提出的这次视察的内容是：着重了解本地区贯彻中共十三届四中全会和五中全会精神，进一步治理整顿和深化改革的情况和政协开展政治协商、民主监督活动的情况。
