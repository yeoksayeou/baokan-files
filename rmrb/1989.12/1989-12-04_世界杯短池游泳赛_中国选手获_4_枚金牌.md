Title: Chinese Swimmers Win 4 Gold Medals at World Cup Short Course Swimming Championships
Author:
Date: 1989-12-04
Edition: 3

Chinese swimmers won 4 gold medals and 1 silver medal at the World Cup Short Course Swimming Championships held in Montreal, Canada. Lin Li performed exceptionally, securing two first-place finishes. In the women's 100-meter and 400-meter individual medley finals, Lin Li won with times of 1 minute 3 seconds and 4 minutes 45.45 seconds, respectively. Zhang Yong claimed the gold medal in the women's 200-meter freestyle with a time of 2 minutes 1.52 seconds. In the women's 100-meter backstroke final, Yang Wenyi won with a time of 1 minute 4.11 seconds.

Another Chinese female swimmer, Wang Xiaohong, secured the silver medal in the women's 100-meter butterfly with a time of 1 minute 1.91 seconds. The gold medal in this event went to British swimmer M. Scalabro with a time of 1 minute 1.47 seconds. The gold medalists in the other events decided yesterday were:

- Men's 100-meter individual medley: G. Robbins (UK) with a time of 57.22 seconds
- Men's 200-meter freestyle: T. Werner (Sweden) with a time of 1 minute 47.74 seconds
- Men's 100-meter backstroke: M. Tuxbury (Canada) with a time of 54.77 seconds
- Women's 100-meter breaststroke: K. Dugan (Canada) with a time of 1 minute 9.15 seconds
- Men's 100-meter breaststroke: D. Volkov (Soviet Union) with a time of 1 minute 0.58 seconds

### 世界杯短池游泳赛  中国选手获4枚金牌

1989-12-04
第3版(综合)
专栏：体育

    世界杯短池游泳赛
    中国选手获4枚金牌
    新华社北京12月2日电  据外电报道，在加拿大蒙特利尔举行的世界杯短池游泳赛昨天进行了14个项目的决赛，中国选手获4枚金牌和1枚银牌。中国选手林莉表现出色，一人夺两项第一。在女子100米和400米个人混合泳决赛中，林莉分别以1分3秒和4分45秒45的成绩获得冠军。庄泳以2分1秒52的成绩，赢得女子200米自由泳决赛冠军。在女子100米仰泳决赛中，杨文意以1分4秒11的成绩夺魁。
    参赛的另一名中国女选手王晓红获得女子100米蝶泳银牌，成绩为1分1秒91。英国选手玛·斯卡尔伯劳以1分1秒47的最快成绩获这个项目金牌。昨天决出的其余几个项目的金牌得主是：男子100米个人混合泳，57秒22，格·罗宾斯（英国）；男子200米自由泳，1分47秒74，托·韦尔纳（瑞典）；男子100米仰泳，54秒77，马·图克斯伯里（加拿大）；女子100米蛙泳，1分9秒15，凯·杜根（加拿大）；男子100米蛙泳，1分0秒58，德·沃尔科夫（苏联）等。
