Title: The 19th Asian Youth Football Championship to be held in Shenyang next year
Author: Wang Ke
Date: 1989-12-09
Page: 3

Xinhua News Agency, Shenyang, December 7th

The 19th Asian Youth Football Championship will be held in Shenyang, Liaoning Province, China, from July 22nd to 30th next year. This was announced by the chairman of the organizing committee for this championship and vice minister of the State Education Commission, Zhou Shiyang, at a press conference held this afternoon.

The Asian Youth Football Championship was initiated by the Asian School Football Union in 1971. China joined the union in 1984 and has participated in four championships, achieving good results.

### 亚洲中学生足球锦标赛明年在沈阳举行
王珂
1989-12-09
第3版(综合)
专栏：体育

    亚洲中学生足球锦标赛明年在沈阳举行
    据新华社沈阳12月7日电　（记者王珂）第十九届亚洲中学生足球锦标赛将于明年7月22日至30日在中国辽宁省沈阳市举行。这是本届锦标赛组委会主任、国家教委副主任邹时炎今天下午在这里举行的新闻发布会上宣布的。
    亚洲中学生足球锦标赛是1971年由亚洲中学生足球联合会发起的，中国于1984年加入该联合会，并参加了4届比赛，获得良好成绩。
