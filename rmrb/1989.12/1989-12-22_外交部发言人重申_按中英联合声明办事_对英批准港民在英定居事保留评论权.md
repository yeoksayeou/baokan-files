Title: Spokesperson of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs Reiterates Adherence to the Sino-British Joint Declaration
Author:
Date: 1989-12-22
Page: 2

Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, December 21st.

At today's afternoon press conference, the spokesperson of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs responded to questions from journalists regarding the British government's approval of certain Hong Kong residents to settle in Britain.

He said, "The fundamental position of the Chinese government is to handle matters according to the Sino-British Joint Declaration and the memoranda exchanged at the time of signing the declaration. Regarding the relevant plans announced by the British government, we are currently studying them and reserve the right to make further comments."

### 外交部发言人重申  按中英联合声明办事  对英批准港民在英定居事保留评论权

1989-12-22
第2版(国内新闻)
专栏：

    外交部发言人重申
    按中英联合声明办事
    对英批准港民在英定居事保留评论权
    本报北京12月21日讯　在今天下午的新闻发布会上，外交部发言人就英国政府批准部分香港居民在英定居一事，回答了记者的提问。
    他说，中国政府的基本立场是，按照中英联合声明和签署声明时双方互换的备忘录办事。对英国政府宣布的有关计划，我们正在研究，并且保留进一步评论的权利。
