Title: United Nations Commemorates International Day of Solidarity with the Palestinian People
Author: 
Date: 1989-12-01
Edition: 4

The United Nations held an event today to commemorate the International Day of Solidarity with the Palestinian People, calling for Israel to withdraw completely from the Arab-occupied territories and for the Palestinian state to fully exercise its right to self-determination.

At the commemoration ceremony, the chairman of the Committee on the Exercise of the Inalienable Rights of the Palestinian People, Diallo, said that Israel's occupation of Palestinian territories has forced nearly 5 million Palestinians to live in Israeli-occupied territories such as the West Bank, Gaza Strip, and neighboring Arab countries. She urged Israel to withdraw unconditionally and completely from all Arab-occupied territories immediately.

She also called on the international community to do everything possible to support the Palestinian people in their struggle for self-determination, including the right to return home and establish an independent state.

UN Secretary-General Perez de Cuellar stated at the meeting that the international community unanimously believes that a satisfactory resolution to the Palestinian issue should be based on the recognition of the legitimate rights of the Palestinian people.

He expressed regret over Israel's refusal to engage in dialogue with the PLO, which has affected the progress of resolving the Palestinian issue. He called on the international community to coordinate efforts and work together to help the relevant parties enter into effective negotiations, thereby achieving comprehensive and lasting peace in the Middle East.

The UN General Assembly designated November 29 each year as the International Day of Solidarity with the Palestinian People in 1977.

The African Unity Organization reaffirmed its support for the Palestinian people's struggle for self-determination and independence on November 28.

In a statement issued on the International Day of Solidarity with the Palestinian People, the AUO pledged to continue supporting all those who persist in seeking a just and lasting solution to the Palestinian issue.

The AUO called on the international community to pressure Israel to negotiate with the Palestinian people under the leadership of the Palestine Liberation Organization.

### 联合国纪念声援巴勒斯坦国际日  非统组织重申支持巴人民斗争

1989-12-01
第4版(国际新闻)
专栏：

    联合国纪念声援巴勒斯坦国际日
    非统组织重申支持巴人民斗争
    新华社联合国11月29日电　联合国今天举行声援巴勒斯坦人民国际日活动，呼吁以色列全部撤出阿拉伯被占领土，并使巴勒斯坦国充分行使自决权。
    在纪念仪式上，巴勒斯坦人民行使不可剥夺权利委员会主席迪亚洛说，以色列对巴勒斯坦领土的占领使得近500万巴勒斯坦人至今仍然被迫生活在以色列占领的约旦河西岸、加沙地带以及相邻的阿拉伯国家内。她敦促以色列立即无条件地全部从阿拉伯被占领土撤走。
  她还呼吁国际社会尽一切努力支持巴勒斯坦人民为争取自决权，包括返回家园、建立独立自主的国家而进行的斗争。
    联合国秘书长佩雷斯·德奎利亚尔在大会上指出，国际社会一致认为，巴勒斯坦问题获得令人满意的解决应基于承认巴勒斯坦人民合法权利的基础上。
    他对以色列拒绝同巴解组织对话以至影响了巴勒斯坦问题的解决进程表示遗憾。他呼吁国际社会协调一致，通力合作帮助有关各方进入有效的谈判进程，从而使中东实现全面、持久的和平。
    联合国大会于1977年规定每年的11月29日为声援巴勒斯坦人民国际日。
    新华社亚的斯亚贝巴11月28日电　非洲统一组织28日重申支持巴勒斯坦人民为恢复自决和独立的权利所进行的斗争。
    非统组织在声援巴勒斯坦人民国际日之际发表的新闻公报中保证，继续支持所有坚持不懈地寻求公正和持久地解决巴勒斯坦问题的人。
    非统组织呼吁国际社会迫使以色列同在巴勒斯坦解放组织领导下的巴勒斯坦人民谈判。
