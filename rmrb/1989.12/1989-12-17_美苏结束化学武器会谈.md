Title: U.S. and Soviet Union Conclude Talks on Chemical Weapons
Author:
Date: 1989-12-17
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Geneva, December 14th.

The 13th round of U.S.-Soviet talks on the prohibition of chemical weapons concluded in Geneva on December 14th.

According to the communiqué issued after the talks, the purpose of this round of talks was to eliminate differences between the two countries and promote the progress of multilateral negotiations on the international conference on the prohibition of chemical weapons.

Both sides agreed that the next round of talks will be held in early 1990.

### 美苏结束化学武器会谈

1989-12-17
第4版(国际新闻)
专栏：

    美苏结束化学武器会谈
    新华社日内瓦12月14日电　美苏第13轮禁止化学武器会谈14日在日内瓦结束。
    根据会谈结束后发表的公报，此轮会谈的目的是消除两国间的分歧，促进关于禁止化学武器国际大会多方谈判的进展。
    双方商定，下一轮会谈将于1990年初举行。
