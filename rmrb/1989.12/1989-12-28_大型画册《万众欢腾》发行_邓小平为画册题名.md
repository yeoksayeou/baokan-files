Title: Large-Format Album "Jubilant Crowds" Released
Author: Yan Shigu
Date: 1989-12-28
Page: 3

Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, December 26th.

A large-format color album titled "Jubilant Crowds," with the title written by Comrade Deng Xiaoping, was released today. The album features 230 selected photographs that capture the grandeur of the celebrations for the 40th anniversary of the founding of the nation from various angles. Many readers believe this is a precious historical record.

State Councilor and Mayor of Beijing, Chen Xitong, attended the launch ceremony today.

### 大型画册《万众欢腾》发行  邓小平为画册题名
颜世贵
1989-12-28
第3版(综合)
专栏：

    大型画册《万众欢腾》发行
    邓小平为画册题名
    本报北京12月26日讯　由邓小平同志题写书名的大型彩色画册《万众欢腾》，今天与读者见面。入选的230幅照片，从不同的角度反映了建国40周年庆祝活动的盛况。不少读者认为，这是一本珍贵的历史资料。
    国务委员、北京市长陈希同出席了今天的首发式。（颜世贵）
