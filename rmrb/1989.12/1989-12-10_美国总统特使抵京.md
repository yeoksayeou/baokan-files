Title: U.S. Presidential Envoy Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1989-12-10
Page: 2

Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, December 9th.

U.S. Presidential Envoy, National Security Affairs Assistant Brent Scowcroft, arrived in Beijing this afternoon by special plane.

Accompanying the envoy on his visit were U.S. Deputy Secretary of State Lawrence Sidney Eagleburger, Presidential Personnel Affairs Assistant Richard G. Darman, and National Security Council official in charge of Chinese affairs, Donald P. Gregg. U.S. Ambassador to China James Lilley arrived on the same plane.

Those who went to the airport to welcome the delegation included Vice Minister of Foreign Affairs Liu Huaqiu, Director of the American and Oceanian Affairs Department Zhang Yijun, and Director of the Information Department Li Zhaoxing. Foreign Minister Qian Qichen will hold talks with Scowcroft this afternoon.

### 美国总统特使抵京

1989-12-10
第2版(国内新闻)
专栏：

    美国总统特使抵京
    新华社北京12月9日电　美国总统特使、总统国家安全事务助理布伦特·斯考克罗夫特今天下午乘专机抵达北京。
    随同特使来访的有美国副国务卿劳伦斯·西德尼·伊格尔伯格、总统人事事务助理查·格·昂特迈耶和国家安全委员会负责中国事务的官员道·海·帕尔。美国驻华大使李洁明同机到达。
    前往机场迎接的有中国外交部副部长刘华秋、美大司司长张毅君和新闻司司长李肇星。外交部部长钱其琛将在今天下午同斯考克罗夫特会谈。
