Title: The Standing Committee of the Consultative Committee for the Basic Law of Macao Establishes Special Task Forces
Author:
Date: 1989-12-15
Edition: 2

The Standing Committee of the Consultative Committee for the Basic Law of Macao held a meeting yesterday and decided to establish five special task forces.

Secretary-General Liao Zeyun reported on the third plenary session of the Drafting Committee held in November. He said that the session revised and adopted the "Draft Structure of the Basic Law of the Macao Special Administrative Region" and established five special task forces, marking a new stage in the drafting of the Basic Law.

After discussion, the meeting decided to establish five corresponding special task forces to assist in the drafting of the Basic Law. These five task forces are: the Special Task Force on the Relationship between the Central Government and the Macao Special Administrative Region, the Special Task Force on the Basic Rights and Duties of Residents, the Special Task Force on the Political System, the Special Task Force on the Economy, and the Special Task Force on Culture and Social Affairs.

The meeting also adopted the "Charter of the Special Task Forces."

### 澳门基本法咨委会成立专责小组

1989-12-15
第2版(国内要闻)
专栏：

    澳门基本法咨委会成立专责小组
    新华社澳门12月14日电  昨天举行的澳门基本法咨询委员会常委会议，讨论并成立了5个专责咨询小组。
    咨委会秘书长廖泽云在会上介绍了11月召开的起草委员会第三次全体会议的情况。他说，这次会议修改并通过了《澳门特别行政区基本法结构（草案）》，成立了5个专责小组，使基本法的起草工作进入一个新阶段。
    会议经过讨论，决定成立5个相应的专责小组，以配合基本法的起草工作，这5个小组是：中央与澳门特别行政区的关系专责咨询小组、居民的基本权利与义务专责咨询小组、政治体制专责咨询小组、经济专责咨询小组、文化与社会事务专责咨询小组。
    会议还通过了《专责咨询小组章程》。
