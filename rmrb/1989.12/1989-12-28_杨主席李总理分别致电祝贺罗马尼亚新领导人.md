Title: Yang Chairman and Li Premier Send Congratulatory Messages to New Romanian Leaders
Author:
Date: 1989-12-28
Edition: 1

Chinese President Yang Shangkun and Premier Li Peng on December 27 sent separate messages of congratulations to Chairman Ion Iliescu of Romania's National Salvation Front Council and Romanian Prime Minister Petre Roman.

Yang Shangkun's message to Iliescu said: "On the occasion of your election as Chairman of the Romanian National Salvation Front Council, I, on behalf of the Government of the People's Republic of China and in my personal capacity, extend my congratulations to you.

"May the friendship between the peoples of China and Romania and the friendly relations between the two countries continue to develop."

Li Peng's telegram to Roman said: "On the occasion of your assumption of the office of Prime Minister of Romania, I, on behalf of the Government of the People's Republic of China and in my personal capacity, extend my congratulations to you. I hope that the friendly relations between China and Romania and between the peoples of the two countries will continue to develop."

### 杨主席李总理分别致电祝贺罗马尼亚新领导人

1989-12-28
第1版(要闻)
专栏：

    杨主席李总理分别致电祝贺罗马尼亚新领导人
    新华社北京12月27日电　中华人民共和国主席杨尚昆和国务院总理李鹏27日分别向罗马尼亚救国阵线委员会主席扬·伊利埃斯库和罗马尼亚政府总理彼得·罗曼发出贺电。
    杨尚昆主席给伊利埃斯库主席的贺电说：“在您当选罗马尼亚救国阵线委员会主席的时候，我谨代表中华人民共和国政府并以我个人的名义向您表示祝贺。
    “愿中罗两国人民之间的友谊和两国的友好关系继续得到发展。”
    李鹏总理打给罗曼总理的电报说：“在您就任罗马尼亚总理的时候，我谨代表中华人民共和国政府并以我个人的名义向您表示祝贺，希望中罗两国和两国人民的友好关系继续得到发展。”
