Title: Zhang Deliang Appointed Ambassador to Jordan
Author:
Date: 1989-12-31
Page: 2

Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, December 30th

Zhang Deliang Appointed Ambassador to Jordan

The President of the People's Republic of China, Yang Shangkun, in accordance with the decision of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress, has appointed Zhang Deliang as the Ambassador Extraordinary and Plenipotentiary of the People's Republic of China to the Hashemite Kingdom of Jordan.

### 章德良出任驻约旦大使

1989-12-31
第2版(国内新闻)
专栏：

    章德良出任驻约旦大使
    新华社北京12月30日电　中华人民共和国主席杨尚昆根据全国人民代表大会常务委员会的决定，任命章德良为中华人民共和国驻约旦哈希姆王国特命全权大使。
