Title: U.S. Uses Potato Peelings to Make Biodegradable Plastics
Author:
Date: 1989-12-09
Edition: 4

The Financial Times of Britain reported that scientists at the U.S. Department of Energy have invented a new process to make biodegradable plastics using potato peelings.

This process involves three main stages: converting the carbohydrates in potato peelings into glucose; converting the glucose into lactic acid; and then converting the lactic acid into polymer films.

This technology can produce biodegradable plastics that can be decomposed by bacteria or photodegradable plastics that can be decomposed by sunlight. The raw materials for making biodegradable plastics are easily obtainable from food processing plants.

### 美用土豆皮制造降解塑料

1989-12-09
第4版(国际新闻)
专栏：

    美用土豆皮制造降解塑料
    新华社伦敦电　据英国《金融时报》报道，美国能源部的科学家发明一种用土豆皮制造降解塑料的新工艺。
    这种工艺过程包括3个主要阶段：把土豆皮中的碳水化合物变成葡萄糖；把葡萄糖变成乳酸；再把乳酸变成聚合物薄膜。
    应用这种工艺能够制成生物降解塑料（被细菌分解）或者光降解塑料（被太阳光分解）。制造降解塑料的原料，很容易从食品加工厂取得。
