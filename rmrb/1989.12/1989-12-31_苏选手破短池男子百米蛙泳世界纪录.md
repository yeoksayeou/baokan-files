Title: Soviet Swimmer Breaks Short Course Men's 100m Breaststroke World Record
Author:
Date: 1989-12-31
Page: 3

Sports

Xinhua News Agency, Paris, December 29th.

According to a report by Agence France-Presse today, Soviet athlete Dmitri Volkov broke the short course men's 100-meter breaststroke world record today in a swimming competition held on Réunion Island. He achieved a time of 59.42 seconds.

The previous world record was 59.75 seconds.

### 苏选手破短池男子百米蛙泳世界纪录

1989-12-31
第3版(综合)
专栏：体育

    苏选手破短池男子百米蛙泳世界纪录
    新华社巴黎12月29日电　据法新社今天报道，苏联运动员季米特里·沃尔科夫今天在留尼汪岛上举行的一次游泳比赛中，以59秒42的成绩，打破短池男子100米蛙泳的世界纪录。
    原世界纪录的成绩为59秒75。
