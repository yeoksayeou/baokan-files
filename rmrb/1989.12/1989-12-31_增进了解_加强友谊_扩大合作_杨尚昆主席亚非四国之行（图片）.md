Title: Enhancing Understanding, Strengthening Friendship, Expanding Cooperation – Chairman Yang Shangkun’s Visit to Four Asian and African Countries
Author: Wǔ Jīguó
Date: 1989-12-31
Edition: 4

Chairman Yang Shangkun, at the invitation of the respective countries, paid official friendly visits to the Arab Republic of Egypt, the United Arab Emirates, the State of Kuwait, and the Sultanate of Oman from December 18 to December 28. This visit deepened the understanding and friendship between the people of China and the four Asian and African countries, strengthened the unity and cooperation between China and these countries, and achieved complete success.

On the 19th, Chairman Yang and President Mubarak attended the completion and unveiling ceremony of the Cairo International Conference Center.

On the 17th, Sheikh Sultan of the Sharjah Emirate of the UAE presented a gift to Chairman Yang.

On the 25th, Chairman Yang visited the National Museum of Kuwait.

On the 27th, Chairman Yang visited the Al-Manahil Textile Factory in Oman.

(All photos in this column were taken by Xinhua News Agency reporter Wu Jiguo.)

### 增进了解　加强友谊　扩大合作——杨尚昆主席亚非四国之行（图片）
武基国
1989-12-31
第4版(国际新闻)
专栏：

    增进了解　加强友谊　扩大合作
    ——杨尚昆主席亚非四国之行
    杨尚昆主席自12月18日至12月28日应邀先后对阿拉伯埃及共和国、阿拉伯联合酋长国、科威特国和阿曼苏丹国进行了正式友好访问。这次访问增进了我国人民与亚非四国人民的了解和友谊，加强了我国同亚非四国的团结与合作，取得圆满成功。
    杨主席和穆巴拉克总统19日出席开罗国际会议中心竣工揭幕仪式。
    阿联酋沙迦酋长国酋长苏尔坦17日向杨主席赠礼。
    25日，杨主席参观科威特国家博物馆。
    27日，杨主席参观阿曼纺织厂。
    （本栏照片均为新华社记者　武基国摄）
