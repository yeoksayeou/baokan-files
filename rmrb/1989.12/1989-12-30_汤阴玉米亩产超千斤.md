Title: Yumin County Achieves Over 1,000 Jin of Corn Per Mu
Author: Liu Shumin Li Yangsheng Cheng Lijun
Date: 1989-12-30
Edition: 2

    Yumin County Achieves Over 1,000 Jin of Corn Per Mu

    Our reporter learns that in Henan's Yumin County, 200,000 mu of high-yield corn development fields have achieved good results. The average yield per mu is 555 jin, ranking first in the nation for large-scale corn development, making Yumin County the first in Henan's history to achieve over 1,000 jin of corn per mu.

    At the beginning of this year, the county committee and county government decided to develop 200,000 mu of high-yield corn fields, aiming to achieve over 1,000 jin per mu. They invited agricultural experts as consultants, starting with improved varieties and strict scientific management. The total production of the 200,000 mu of corn development fields reached 113.2 million jin, with an average yield of 555 jin per mu.

### 汤阴玉米亩产超千斤
刘书民　李阳生　程利军
1989-12-30
第2版(国内新闻)
专栏：四面八方

    汤阴玉米亩产超千斤
    本报讯　河南汤阴县20万亩玉米高产开发田取得好收成，平均亩产555公斤，跃居全国大面积开发玉米的前列，成为河南省历史上第一个玉米亩产超千斤的县。
    今年初，县委、县政府决定开发20万亩玉米高产田，力争亩产超千斤。他们聘请农业专家为顾问，从改良品种入手，加以严格的科学管理；20万亩玉米开发田总产达1.132亿公斤，平均亩产555公斤。　（刘书民　李阳生　程利军）
