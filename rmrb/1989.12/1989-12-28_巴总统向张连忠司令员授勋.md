Title: Pakistani President Awards Medal to Admiral Zhang Lianzhong
Author:
Date: 1989-12-28
Edition: 4

Pakistani President Ghulam Ishaq Khan today awarded the First Class Flag Medal to Admiral Zhang Lianzhong, Commander of the Chinese People's Liberation Army Navy, who is currently visiting Pakistan. This honor is in recognition of his contributions to the Chinese revolution and Sino-Pakistani friendship.

During the meeting, President Ghulam Ishaq Khan highly praised the strong friendship between Pakistan and China, expressing his hope that this deep friendship between the two countries will continue to grow.

Admiral Zhang Lianzhong and his delegation arrived here on the evening of the 23rd, beginning a week-long visit to Pakistan.

### 巴总统向张连忠司令员授勋

1989-12-28
第4版(国际新闻)
专栏：

    巴总统向张连忠司令员授勋
    新华社伊斯兰堡12月24日电　巴基斯坦总统吴拉姆·伊沙克·汗今天在新总统府授予正在这里访问的中国人民解放军海军司令员张连忠一级旗帜勋章，以表彰他对中国革命事业和巴中友谊作出的贡献。
    在会见时，吴·伊沙克·汗总统高度赞扬巴中现存的牢固友谊，并希望两国间的这种深厚友谊进一步得到发展。
    张连忠司令员一行是23日晚抵达这里开始对巴基斯坦进行为期一周访问的。
