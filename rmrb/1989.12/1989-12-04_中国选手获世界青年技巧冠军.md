Title: Chinese Athletes Win World Youth Trampoline Championship
Author: Tang Deqiao
Date: 1989-12-04
Page: 3

Xinhua News Agency, Katowice, Poland, December 2nd

    Chinese athletes win the World Youth Trampoline Championship. According to a report from Xinhua News Agency in Katowice, Poland, at the first World Youth Trampoline Championships held in the southern Polish coal mining center of Katowice, Chinese trampoline athletes Xia Minglei and Cai Zhigang achieved an outstanding score of 29.46 points, winning the men's doubles all-around championship.

### 中国选手获世界青年技巧冠军
汤德乔
1989-12-04
第3版(综合)
专栏：体育

    中国选手获世界青年技巧冠军
    据新华社波兰卡托维兹12月2日电　（记者汤德乔）在波兰南方煤炭工业中心——卡托维兹市举行的第一届世界青年技巧锦标赛上，中国技巧运动员夏明磊、蔡志强以29.46分的优异成绩夺得男双全能冠军。
