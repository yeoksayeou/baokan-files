U.S. and Soviet Leaders Hold First Round of Talks
Due to Bad Weather, Meeting Originally Scheduled on Soviet Warship Moved to Passenger Ship

Xinhua News Agency, Valletta, December 2nd: U.S. President Bush and Soviet leader Gorbachev held their first round of talks today on a Soviet passenger ship in the Mediterranean Sea off the coast of Malta.

Soviet Foreign Minister Shevardnadze, Central Committee Secretary Yakovlev, U.S. Secretary of State Baker, and National Security Advisor Scowcroft participated in this three-hour-long discussion.

This informal meeting between the U.S. and Soviet leaders will last for two days, with three rounds of talks planned. The first round of talks was originally scheduled to be held on the Soviet cruiser "Slava," but due to high winds and waves in the Mediterranean today, it was temporarily moved to the Soviet passenger ship "Gorky."

According to Xinhua News Agency, Valletta, Malta, December 1st: Soviet leader Gorbachev arrived in Valletta, the capital of Malta, late on the 1st to hold talks with U.S. President Bush. He said at the airport, "Recent developments require the two superpowers to engage in more active dialogue."

Gorbachev was welcomed at the airport by Maltese President Tabone and Prime Minister Adam.

In his speech at the welcoming ceremony, Gorbachev said that the Soviet Union "attaches great importance to this meeting" and hopes to have "important discussions" on issues of common concern to the U.S., the Soviet Union, and other countries around the world.

Gorbachev arrived in Valletta after completing his visit to Italy. Accompanying him were Soviet Foreign Minister Shevardnadze and others.

U.S. President Bush and Soviet leader Gorbachev began their meeting on December 2nd. The first round of talks was held in the morning. This meeting between the U.S. and Soviet leaders will last for two days.

### 美苏首脑举行首轮会谈  由于天气恶劣原定在苏军舰上举行的会谈改在客轮上

1989-12-03
第4版(国际新闻)
专栏：

    美苏首脑举行首轮会谈
    由于天气恶劣原定在苏军舰上举行的会谈改在客轮上
    新华社瓦莱塔12月2日电  美国总统布什和苏联领导人戈尔巴乔夫今天在马耳他海域一艘苏联客轮上举行了第一轮会谈。
    苏联外长谢瓦尔德纳泽、苏共中央书记雅科夫列夫、美国国务卿贝克和国家安全事务助理斯考克罗夫特参加了这轮长达3小时的会谈。
    这次美苏首脑非正式会晤将持续两天，计划进行3轮会谈。第一轮会谈原定在苏联巡洋舰“光荣”号上举行，但因为今天地中海上风大浪高，临时改在苏联客轮“高尔基”号上举行。
    据新华社马耳他瓦莱塔12月1日电　苏联领导人戈尔巴乔夫1日晚抵达马耳他首都瓦莱塔，与美国总统布什举行会晤。他在机场说，“最近一系列的事态发展要求两个超级大国进行更为积极的对话。”
    戈尔巴乔夫在机场受到了马耳他总统塔博恩和总理阿达米的欢迎。
    戈尔巴乔夫在欢迎仪式上发表的讲话中说，苏联“对这次会晤给予极大的重视”，并希望能就美苏两国和世界各国所共同关心的问题进行“重要的讨论”。
    戈尔巴乔夫是在结束对意大利访问后飞抵瓦莱塔的。与他同机到达的还有苏联外长谢瓦尔德纳泽等。
