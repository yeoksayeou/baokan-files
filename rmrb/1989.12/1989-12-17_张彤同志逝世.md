Title: Comrade Zhang Tong Passes Away
Author:
Date: 1989-12-17
Edition: 2

	Comrade Zhang Tong, a loyal communist warrior, a long-tested member of the Communist Party of China, an outstanding political worker, military commander, and leader on the diplomatic front of our military, and the former director of the Foreign Affairs Bureau of the Ministry of National Defense, passed away in Beijing on November 20, 1989, at the age of 69 due to illness.

	Comrade Zhang Tong was from Xinyang City, Henan Province. He joined the revolution in 1938 and became a member of the Communist Party of China in the same year. He held various positions, including instructor, political commissar, county committee secretary, division political commissar, brigade political department director, corps chief of staff, military attaché to the Chinese embassies in Denmark, Sweden, India, and Nepal, acting chargé d'affaires in the Republic of the Congo, director of the First Asian Affairs Bureau of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs, and Chinese ambassador to Pakistan, Egypt, and the Federal Republic of Germany. He made significant contributions to the construction and development of China's diplomatic work.

### 张彤同志逝世

1989-12-17
第2版(国内新闻)
专栏：

    张彤同志逝世
    新华社北京12月15日电　忠诚的共产主义战士，久经考验的中国共产党党员，我军优秀的政治工作者、军事指挥员和外交战线的领导干部，国防部外事局原局长张彤同志，因病于1989年11月20日在北京逝世，终年69岁。
    张彤同志系河南省信阳市人，1938年参加革命，同年加入中国共产党。历任指导员、教导员、县委书记、团政治委员、旅政治部主任、师参谋长，中国驻丹麦、瑞典、印度和尼泊尔大使馆武官，中国驻刚果（利）临时代办，外交部第一亚洲司司长，中国驻巴基斯坦、埃及、联邦德国大使等职。他为我国外交工作的建设和发展做出了重要贡献。
