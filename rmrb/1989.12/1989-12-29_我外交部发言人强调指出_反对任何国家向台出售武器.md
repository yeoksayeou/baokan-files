Title: Spokesperson of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs Emphasizes Opposition to Any Country Selling Weapons to Taiwan
Author:
Date: 1989-12-29
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, December 28th.

Today in the afternoon, Foreign Ministry spokesperson Jin Guihua responded to questions from reporters at a press conference regarding the possibility of France selling warships to Taiwan.

He said, "We firmly oppose any country selling or collaborating with Taiwan to produce any type of weapon or military equipment, whether directly or indirectly, in whole or in parts. Such actions are direct interference in China's internal affairs."

### 我外交部发言人强调指出  反对任何国家向台出售武器

1989-12-29
第1版(要闻)
专栏：

    我外交部发言人强调指出
    反对任何国家向台出售武器
    本报北京12月28日讯　今天下午，外交部发言人金桂华在新闻发布会上就法国可能向台湾出售军舰一事回答了记者的提问。
    他说，我们坚决反对任何国家向台湾出售或同台湾合作生产任何种类的武器和军事装备，不管这是以直接的还是间接的、以整体的还是分部件的方式进行。这样做都是对中国内政的直接干涉。
