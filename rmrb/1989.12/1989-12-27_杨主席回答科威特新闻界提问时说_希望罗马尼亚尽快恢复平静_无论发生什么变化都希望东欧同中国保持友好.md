Title: Yang Shangkun Responds to Questions from Kuwaiti Media: Hopes Romania Quickly Restores Peace
Author: Yǔ Chāng, Zhōu Cí Pǔ
Date: 1989-12-27
Edition: 1

Chinese President Yang Shangkun said this morning, "We hope Romania can quickly end the bloodshed and restore peace and stability."

He made these remarks during a meeting with editors and media representatives from major Kuwaiti newspapers when answering their questions.

Yang Shangkun said that recently, Eastern European countries, including Romania, have undergone some historical changes. China believes these changes have both internal and external causes.

He said that China is closely monitoring the situation there. "We cannot criticize them when they are in extreme difficulty," he said.

Yang Shangkun said that regardless of the changes in Eastern Europe, China hopes that Eastern European countries will maintain friendly relations with China. Ultimately, it is up to the people of Eastern European countries to choose their own systems. China respects the choices made by the peoples of Eastern European countries.

### 杨主席回答科威特新闻界提问时说  希望罗马尼亚尽快恢复平静  无论发生什么变化都希望东欧同中国保持友好
许昌  周慈朴
1989-12-27
第1版(要闻)
专栏：

    杨主席回答科威特新闻界提问时说
    希望罗马尼亚尽快恢复平静
    无论发生什么变化都希望东欧同中国保持友好
    新华社科威特12月26日电（记者许昌、周慈朴）正在这里访问的中华人民共和国主席杨尚昆今天上午说：“我们希望罗马尼亚尽快结束流血冲突，恢复平静与稳定”。
    他是在会见科威特各大报主编和新闻界负责人，回答他们提出的问题时说这番话的。
    杨尚昆说，最近，东欧国家，包括罗马尼亚，发生了一些历史性的变化。中国认为这些变化既有内在原因，也有外部原因。
    他说，中国正在密切注视那里局势的变化。他说：“我们在它们极端困难的时候，不能说三道四。”
    杨尚昆说，无论东欧局势发生了什么样的变化，中国都希望东欧国家同中国保持友好关系。究竟东欧国家采取什么制度，这要由它们的人民自己作出选择。中国尊重东欧各国人民的选择。
