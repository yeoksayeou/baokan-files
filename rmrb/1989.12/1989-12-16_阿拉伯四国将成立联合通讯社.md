Title: Four Arab Countries to Establish a Joint News Agency
Author:
Date: 1989-12-16
Edition: 4

The Arab Cooperation Council, composed of Egypt, Jordan, Iraq, and the Arab Republic of Yemen, will establish a joint news agency. This agency will release daily news and information published by the council's secretariat.

This decision was made during a meeting of the heads of the official news agencies of the four countries, which began ten days ago. The chairman of the meeting, Najib, the head of the Middle East News Agency, stated on the 12th that the joint news agency will be located in the capital of one of the four countries. The Jordan News Agency will coordinate the distribution of news.

### 阿拉伯四国将成立联合通讯社

1989-12-16
第4版(国际新闻)
专栏：

    阿拉伯四国将成立联合通讯社
    由埃及、约旦、伊拉克和阿拉伯也门组成的阿拉伯合作委员会将成立一家联合通讯社，发布每日新闻和该委员会秘书处公布的消息。
    这个决定是由十日开始举行的四国官方通讯社董事长联席会议作出的。这次会议的主席、中东社董事长纳吉布十二日说，联合通讯社将设在四国中的一个首都。约旦通讯社将协调发稿工作。
