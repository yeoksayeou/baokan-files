Title: Latin American Education Ministers Propose Slogan: Education for All, All for Education
Author:
Date: 1989-12-03
Edition: 4

Latin American education ministers have proposed the slogan "Education for All, All for Education" at a conference held in Quito.

The Latin American Development of Education Conference was held from November 28 to December 1 in Quito, the capital of Ecuador. Education ministers from 22 Latin American countries and representatives from some international organizations attended the meeting. The attendees agreed that the slogan "Education for All" is not enough; they also need to advocate for "All for Education." They said that in any country, it is impossible to achieve good education without the concerted efforts of the government, families, social organizations, churches, and other sectors.

The attendees pointed out that the heavy burden of external debt is an obstacle to the development of education in Latin American countries. To develop education, it is necessary to reduce the debt burden.

They also pointed out that it is necessary to improve the education system, improve teaching methods, and raise the level of education.

### 拉美国家教育部长提出口号  教育为大家　大家为教育

1989-12-03
第4版(国际新闻)
专栏：

    拉美国家教育部长提出口号
    教育为大家　大家为教育
    新华社基多12月1日电　“教育为大家，大家为教育”。这是拉美国家教育部长们在基多举行的拉丁美洲发展教育讨论会上提出的口号。
    拉丁美洲发展教育讨论会于11月28日至12月1日在厄瓜多尔首都基多举行。22个拉美国家的教育部长和一些国际组织的代表参加了这次会议。与会者们认为光有“教育为大家”这个口号是不够的，还应提倡“大家为教育”。他们说，在任何一个国家，没有政府、家庭、社会团体、教会等各方面的齐心协力，是不可能办好教育的。
    与会者们指出，沉重的外债负担是妨碍拉美国家发展教育的绊脚石。要发展教育，就要减轻债务负担。
    他们指出，有必要完善教育体制、改进教育方法和提高教育水平。
