Title: Jiang Zemin and Others Watch "Swan Lake"
Author:
Date: 1989-12-13
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, December 12th.

Jiang Zemin, General Secretary, attended tonight as a guest of honor of the Central Ballet Troupe to watch their performance of the traditional classic "Swan Lake" in celebration of the troupe's 30th anniversary.

Five "Odettes" took the stage tonight. The five performers of the White Swans represent three generations of ballet dancers nurtured by New China, from Bai Shuxiang to the recent international award-winning newcomers.

During the intermission, Jiang Zemin and Li Tieming met with the main performers, directors, and conductors, inquiring about their performances and work.

Accompanying the viewing were also Deputy Minister of Culture He Jingzhi and others.

### 江泽民等观看《天鹅湖》

1989-12-13
第1版(要闻)
专栏：

    江泽民等观看《天鹅湖》
    新华社北京12月12日电  江泽民总书记今晚作为中央芭蕾舞团的贵宾，观看了他们为纪念建团30周年演出的传统古典名剧《天鹅湖》。
    有五位“奥杰塔”在今天的晚会上轮流登场。这五个白天鹅的扮演者，代表了新中国造就的三代芭蕾舞演员，从白淑湘到近年在国际上获奖的新秀。
    幕间休息时，江泽民与李铁映等与主要演员、编导及指挥见面，询问了他们的演出和工作情况。
    陪同观看演出的还有文化部代部长贺敬之等。（附图片）
