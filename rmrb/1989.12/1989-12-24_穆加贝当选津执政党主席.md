Title: Mugabe Elected Chairman of Zimbabwe's Ruling Party
Author:
Date: 1989-12-24
Edition: 4

The national congress of the Zimbabwe African National Union (Patriotic Front) concluded at eleven o'clock on the evening of the twenty-second. The congress elected a 160-member central committee, with Mugabe as chairman, Nkomo and Muzenda as vice chairmen, and Msika as the party's national chairman.

At the congress, Chairman Mugabe announced the list of 22 political bureau members and clearly defined their respective responsibilities.

At the congress on the twenty-second, Mugabe and Nkomo jointly announced the formal dissolution of the original ZANU and ZAPU.

### 穆加贝当选津执政党主席

1989-12-24
第4版(国际新闻)
专栏：

    穆加贝当选津执政党主席
    津巴布韦非洲民族联盟（爱国战线）全国代表大会于二十二日晚十一点闭幕。大会选举产生了以穆加贝为主席、恩科莫和穆增达为副主席以及姆西卡为党的全国主席的一百六十人的中央委员会。
    穆加贝主席在大会上宣布了二十二名政治局委员的名单，并明确了各自的分工。
    在二十二日的代表大会上，穆加贝和恩科莫分别宣布正式解散原民盟和人盟。
