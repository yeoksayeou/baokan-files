Title: A Delegation of Chinese Communist Party Workers Visits Romania
Author:
Date: 1989-12-17
Edition: 4

A delegation of Chinese Communist Party workers, led by Chen Zhi-li, a candidate member of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of China and deputy secretary of the Shanghai Municipal Committee, concluded a week-long friendly visit to Romania on December 14.

During the visit, I. Matei, a member of the Executive Committee of the Central Committee of the Romanian Communist Party and the secretary in charge of the Central Propaganda Department, met with the delegation members and held cordial and friendly talks with them.

### 我党的工作者代表团访罗

1989-12-17
第4版(国际新闻)
专栏：

    我党的工作者代表团访罗
    新华社布加勒斯特12月14日电　中共中央候补委员、上海市委副书记陈至立率领的中国共产党党的工作者代表团14日结束了对罗马尼亚为期一周的友好访问。
    访问期间，罗共中央执委、中央书记兼中宣部长伊·马太接见了代表团成员，并同他们进行了亲切友好的谈话。
