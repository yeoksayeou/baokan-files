Title: Tianjin Constructs the Guangang Forest Greening Base
Author: Liáng Xiǎoqín
Date: 1989-12-12
Page: 2

Column: Four Directions

    Tianjin Constructs the Guangang Forest Greening Base
    Our reporter Liáng Xiǎoqín reports from Tianjin: A forest greening base covering 34,332 mu (approximately 2,290 hectares) – the Guangang Forest Greening Base – recently began construction in the Dagang District of Tianjin. The planned base is divided into active zones, quiet zones, and production zones, with forests as the main feature. Inside, there are nurseries, fruit orchards, lotus ponds, lakes, and animal breeding farms.

### 天津兴建官港森林绿化基地
梁小琴
1989-12-12
第2版(国内新闻)
专栏：四面八方

    天津兴建官港森林绿化基地
    本报天津12月11日电　记者梁小琴报道：一座占地34332亩的森林绿化基地——官港森林绿化基地日前在天津大港区动工兴建。规划设计的基地分为动区、静区和生产区，以森林为主，内设苗圃、果木园、荷花塘、湖泊、动物饲养场等。
