Title: Tangshan Sends Large Quantities of Industrial Goods to Rural Areas
Author: Xǔ Guìyuán
Date: 1989-12-29
Page: 2

Xinhua News Agency, Shijiazhuang, December 28th

To ensure a good supply of goods in rural markets during the New Year and Spring Festival, the commercial and supply departments at various levels in Tangshan City, Hebei Province, have actively organized industrial goods to be sent to rural areas.

Since the beginning of winter, Tangshan City's commercial and supply departments have shifted their sales focus to rural markets, dispatching a large number of personnel and vehicles to mountainous and coastal areas.

According to incomplete statistics, in just two months, the entire city has organized over 12,000 people to deliver goods to rural areas, mobilized more than 3,400 vehicle trips, delivering goods worth over 81 million yuan, and achieving a transaction volume of over 46 million yuan through mobile exhibitions and mobile wholesale.

### 唐山送大批工业品下乡
许贵元
1989-12-29
第2版(国内新闻)
专栏：四面八方

    唐山送大批工业品下乡
    新华社石家庄12月28日电　（通讯员许贵元）为了搞好新年和春节期间的农村市场供应，河北省唐山市各级商业、供销部门，积极开展工业品下乡活动。
    入冬以来，唐山市商业供销部门把销售的重点转移到农村市场，抽调大批人力和车辆，奔赴到山区和沿海地区。
    据不完全统计，仅两个月的时间，全市共组织下乡送货1.2万余人次，出动车辆3400多辆次，送去商品8100多万元，巡回展销和流动批发成交额达4600多万元。
