Title: 44th UN General Assembly Adopts Resolution: 1992 as International Space Year, 1994 as International Year of the Family
Author: Liú Kāichén
Date: 1989-12-11
Page: 4

People's Daily, United Nations, December 8th. Correspondent Liu Kaichen reports: The 44th session of the United Nations General Assembly today unanimously endorsed the proposal by international scientific organizations to designate 1992 as the "International Space Year."

The Assembly, without a vote, unanimously adopted the resolution on "International Cooperation in the Peaceful Use of Outer Space." The resolution states that, for the benefit of all countries, especially considering the needs of developing countries, the International Space Year should promote international cooperation in the peaceful use of outer space. It should utilize the educational and training capabilities of the United Nations Space Applications Program to enable the United Nations to play a meaningful role.

The resolution also "urges all countries, especially those with strong space capabilities, to make positive contributions to preventing an arms race in outer space, so as to create the necessary conditions for international cooperation in the peaceful exploration and use of outer space."

The Assembly believes that it is necessary to increase the benefits of space technology and its applications, and to promote orderly increases in space activities that are relevant to the entire human community, especially the socio-economic development of developing countries.

The Assembly also adopted another resolution, declaring 1994 as the "International Year of the Family." The Assembly requested that governments and relevant organizations of all countries organize celebrations at that time, with assistance from the United Nations system, to further public awareness that the family is the natural and fundamental unit of society, and to improve the status of the family and enhance family welfare.

### 第44届联大通过决议  1992年为国际空间年　1994年为国际家庭年
刘开宸
1989-12-11
第4版(国际新闻)
专栏：

    第44届联大通过决议
    1992年为国际空间年　1994年为国际家庭年
    本报联合国12月8日电  记者刘开宸报道：第44届联合国大会今天一致赞同国际科学机构将1992年作为“国际空间年”的倡议。
    大会未经表决，一致通过了“和平利用外层空间的国际合作”的决议。决议指出，为了世界各国的利益、特别是考虑到发展中国家的需要，要通过国际空间年促进和平利用外空的国际合作，要利用联合国空间应用方案的教育和培训能力，使联合国发挥有意义的作用。
    决议还“敦促所有国家，特别是那些拥有强大空间能力的国家，为防止外层空间军备竞赛作出积极贡献，以便为和平探索和利用外空的国际合作创造必要条件”。
    大会认为，需要增加空间技术及其应用的效益，促使各种有关全人类，特别是发展中国家社会经济发展的空间活动有秩序地增加。
    大会还通过另一项决议，宣布1994年为“国际家庭年”。大会请各国政府和有关机构届时组织庆祝活动，由联合国系统提供协助，使公众进一步认识到家庭是社会的自然和基本单位，要改善家庭地位和增进家庭福利。
