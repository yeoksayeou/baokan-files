Title: Chad Begins Presidential Elections
Author:
Date: 1989-12-12
Page: 4

Chad begins presidential elections

Chad's nationwide referendum on a new autonomous constitution and the election of a president began on the 10th. Voters started flocking to the 3,576 polling stations across the country from 5 AM. Diplomatic circles and public opinion believe that the incumbent head of state, President Hissène Habré, the sole candidate, will be elected as the new president.

### 乍得开始总统选举

1989-12-12
第4版(国际新闻)
专栏：

    乍得开始总统选举
    乍得全民投票通过新的自治宪法和选举总统十日在全国拉开序幕。
    选民们从早晨五点起就踊跃地来到设在各地的三千五百七十六个投票站。外交界和舆论认为现任国家元首、总统唯一候选人哈布雷 将当选为新总统。
