Title: 35th Anniversary of National Defense Industry Press
Author: Yu Qingtian
Date: 1989-12-21
Page: 3

The National Defense Industry Press celebrated its 35th anniversary on December 20. Marshal Nie Rongzhen inscribed a message of encouragement: "Do a good job in publishing, disseminate scientific and technological knowledge, and strive for the revitalization of national defense."

The People's Daily reports that on December 20, the National Defense Industry Press marked its 35th anniversary. Marshal Nie Rongzhen inscribed a message of encouragement: "Do a good job in publishing, disseminate scientific and technological knowledge, and strive for the revitalization of national defense."

### 国防工业出版社成立35周年  聂荣臻元帅题词鼓励
于庆田
1989-12-21
第3版(综合)
专栏：

    国防工业出版社成立35周年
    聂荣臻元帅题词鼓励
    本报讯　12月20日，国防工业出版社迎来了它的35岁生日。聂荣臻元帅题词鼓励：“做好出版工作，传播科技知识，为振兴国防事业而努力奋斗。”　（于庆田）
