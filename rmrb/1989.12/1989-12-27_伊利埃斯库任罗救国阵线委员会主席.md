Title: Iliescu Appointed Chairman of Romania's National Salvation Front Council
Author:
Date: 1989-12-27
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Bucharest, December 26th.

Romanian Television announced at noon today that a communiqué from the National Salvation Front Council of Romania stated that Ion Iliescu and Dumitru Mazilu were elected as the chairman and first vice-chairman, respectively, of the council, which was established on December 23rd.

Iliescu issued an order appointing 43-year-old former water engineer Petre Roman as the prime minister until the general election in April next year.

### 伊利埃斯库任罗救国阵线委员会主席

1989-12-27
第4版(国际新闻)
专栏：

    伊利埃斯库任罗救国阵线委员会主席
    新华社布加勒斯特12月26日电　罗马尼亚电视台今天中午宣布的罗马尼亚救国阵线委员会的一项公报说，杨·伊利埃斯库和杜米特鲁·马济卢分别被选为本月23日成立的救国阵线委员会主席和第一副主席。
    伊利埃斯库发布一项命令，任命43岁、曾是水利工程师的彼得·罗曼在明年4月大选前的期间担任政府总理。
