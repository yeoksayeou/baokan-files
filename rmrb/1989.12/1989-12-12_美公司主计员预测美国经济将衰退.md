Title: American Company Chief Accountants Predict U.S. Economic Recession
Author:
Date: 1989-12-12
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Washington, December 5th.

A survey report released by the American Institute of Certified Public Accountants on December 5th stated that 63% of the company chief accountants surveyed by the institute predicted that the U.S. economy would experience a recession next year, and 52% predicted a recession in 1991.

Among the chief accountants who responded to the survey, 43% predicted that the U.S. economy would weaken further in the next 12 months, while only 15% predicted an improvement. 46% predicted that the U.S. inflation rate would rise to over 5% next year.

Xinhua News Agency, Washington, December 8th.

The U.S. Department of Labor announced on December 8th that the U.S. unemployment rate in November was 5.4%, an increase of 0.1 percentage points from October.

The Department of Labor reported that the U.S. added 210,000 new jobs in November, all of which were in the service sector, while the manufacturing sector lost 27,000 jobs, with the automotive manufacturing sector losing 15,000 jobs. From a racial perspective, the unemployment rate for whites in November decreased from 4.6% in October to 4.4%, while the unemployment rate for blacks increased from 11.8% to 12.1%.

### 美公司主计员预测美国经济将衰退

1989-12-12
第4版(国际新闻)
专栏：

    美公司主计员预测美国经济将衰退
    新华社华盛顿12月5日电　美国全国会计师协会5日发表的一份调查报告说，在接受该协会调查的公司主计员中，有63％的人预计明年美国经济将发生衰退，预计1991年发生衰退的占52％。
    在答复调查的主计员中，43％的人预计今后12个月内美国经济将更趋疲软，只有15％的人预测经济向好的方向发展。46％的人预计明年美国的通货膨胀率将上升到5％以上。
    新华社华盛顿12月8日电　美国劳工部8日宣布，今年11月份美国的失业率为5.4％比10月份上升了0.1个百分点。
    劳工部说，11月份美国新增就业岗位21万个，全部属于服务业，制造业部门则失去了2.7万个就业岗位，其中汽车制造业失去的就业岗位达1.5万个。从种族的角度看，11月份美国白人的失业率从10月份4.6％下降到4.4％，而黑人的失业率则从11.8％上升到12.1％。
