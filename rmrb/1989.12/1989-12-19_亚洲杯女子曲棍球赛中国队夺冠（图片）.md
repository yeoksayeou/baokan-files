Title: Chinese Women's Field Hockey Team Wins Asian Cup
Author: Huang Benqiang
Date: 1989-12-19
Page: 3

Xinhua News Agency, Hong Kong, December 17th

On December 17th, the Chinese women's field hockey team defeated South Korea with a score of 1:0 in Hong Kong, securing the championship of the Asian Cup women's field hockey tournament. The image shows a Chinese team member preparing to score a goal.
