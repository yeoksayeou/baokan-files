Title: Japan Develops a Robot Capable of Conversing with Humans
Author:
Date: 1989-12-09
Edition: 4

Japan's Industrial Technology Institute's Electronics Technology Research Institute has recently developed two highly skilled and intelligent industrial robots.

According to the *Nihon Keizai Shimbun*, one of these robots is called the "Artificial Skill Robot." It consists of an operating device, a laser pointer, a stereoscopic camera, a multimedia display, and is equipped with an environmental simulation system, a programming system, and an operation management system. This "Artificial Skill Robot" can perform complex tasks such as disassembling and repairing valves. Its distinctive feature is that it can operate independently in extreme conditions, replacing humans.

The other robot is called the "Human Dialogue Robot." Its main component is a direct-drive operating device for tasks, along with a multimedia display for monitoring and a program controller. This robot can use matches, alcohol lamps, crucibles, and stirring rods to perform flame reaction experiments, replacing humans. Its distinctive feature is that it can converse with humans and coordinate tasks. When it encounters complex situations it cannot handle, it can ask for help verbally.

### 日制成可与人对话机器人

1989-12-09
第4版(国际新闻)
专栏：

    日制成可与人对话机器人
    新华社东京12月4日电  日本工业技术院电子技术综合研究所，最近研制出两个具有高技能和智能的工业用机器人。
    据《日刊工业新闻》报道，这两个机器人一台被称之为“人工技能机器人”。它是由操作器、激光指示器、立体视觉摄像机、多媒质显示器等组成，并配有环境模拟系统、程序编制系统和作业实施管理系统。这个“人工技能机器人”可进行阀门的拆卸和修理等复杂作业。它的特点是可代替人在极限条件下独自作业。
    另一个被称之为“与人对话型机器人”。它的主体是一台作业用直接驱动操作装置，并配有监视用多媒质显示器、程序控制器等系统。这个机器人可使用火柴、酒精灯、乳钵、乳棒等代替人来完成火焰反应试验。它的特点是可与人进行对话，协调作业。当它遇到不能处理的复杂情况时，可用声音向人请教。
