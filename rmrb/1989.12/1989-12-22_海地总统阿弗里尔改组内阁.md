Title: Haitian President Avril Reorganizes Cabinet
Author:
Date: 1989-12-22
Edition: 4

Haitian military president General Avril reorganized the cabinet on the 19th, replacing most of the cabinet members, including the ministers of interior, defense, economy, and finance.

This reorganization involved eight out of the thirteen cabinet departments.

Additionally, General Avril appointed new leaders for the armed forces and the military commander of the capital, Port-au-Prince.

It is believed that this cabinet reorganization is one of the major measures taken by Avril to strengthen control over the interior and defense departments and to seek solutions to the severe economic crisis.

### 海地总统阿弗里尔改组内阁

1989-12-22
第4版(国际新闻)
专栏：

    海地总统阿弗里尔改组内阁
    海地军人总统阿弗里尔将军十九日改组内阁，更换了包括内政和国防部长及经济和财政部长在内的大部分内阁成员。
    这次改组涉及到海地内阁十三个部中的八个部。
    另外，阿弗里尔将军还任命了新的武装部队总监和首都太子港军区领导人。
    据认为，这次内阁改组是阿弗里尔为加强对内政和国防部的控制和寻求解决严重经济危机而采取的重大措施之一。
