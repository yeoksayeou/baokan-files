Title: The National Theatre of Nigeria
Author: Shēn Míng Hé
Date: 1989-12-04
Page: 7

The National Theatre of Nigeria is one of the major buildings in the capital city of Lagos. Inside the theatre, there is the National Museum of Modern Art and the Center for African and African-American Art and Civilization. The photo shows the exterior of the National Theatre.

		By our reporter Shēn Míng Hé.
