Title: Yang Chairman Says Visit to Kuwait Achieves Complete Success
Author: 
Date: 1989-12-27
Edition: 4

Yang Shangkun, President of the People's Republic of China, said today morning during a meeting with editors and news officials from major Kuwaiti newspapers that his visit to Kuwait was a "complete success."

Yang Shangkun particularly mentioned his talks with Kuwaiti Emir Jaber Al-Sabah. He said that during the talks, the leaders of both China and Kuwait expressed satisfaction with the great achievements in friendly cooperation between the two countries and agreed that the relationship between China and Kuwait should be further developed.

He said that China and Kuwait have already cooperated on some projects, but both sides hope that future cooperation will cover a broader range and more levels.

Yang Shangkun said that this visit "achieved the purpose of enhancing mutual understanding and friendship between the peoples of China and Kuwait, and strengthening unity and cooperation between the two countries."

Yang Shangkun expressed his appreciation for the domestic and foreign policies pursued by the Kuwaiti government.

He reiterated that China attaches great importance to friendly relations with Kuwait. It is the unwavering policy of the Chinese government to further develop friendly cooperation between the two countries in all aspects. He said that the Chinese government is willing to strengthen friendly coordination with Kuwait on major international issues.

Yang Shangkun also introduced the current situation in China to the Kuwaiti news community. He said that although China is currently facing some difficulties, as long as the 1.1 billion people unite as one, they will definitely be able to overcome these difficulties and build a prosperous country.

### 杨主席对科新闻界说  访问科威特获圆满成功
周慈朴  许昌
1989-12-27
第4版(国际新闻)
专栏：

    杨主席对科新闻界说
    访问科威特获圆满成功
    新华社科威特12月26日电（记者周慈朴、许昌）中华人民共和国主席杨尚昆今天上午在会见科威特各大报主编和新闻界负责人时说，他这次对科威特的访问获得了“圆满成功”。
    杨尚昆特别提到了他这次同科威特埃米尔贾比尔·萨巴赫的会谈。他说，在会谈中，中科两国领导人对两国友好合作所取得的巨大成就感到欣慰，并一致认为，中科两国关系应该更好地向前发展。
    他说，中科两国已经有了一些项目的合作，但双方都希望今后合作范围更大一些，层次更多一些。
    杨尚昆说，他这次访问“达到了增进中科两国人民之间的了解和友谊，加强中科两国团结与合作的目的。”
    杨尚昆对科威特政府奉行的内外政策表示赞赏。
    他重申，中国十分重视同科威特的友好关系。进一步发展两国在各方面的友好合作是中国政府坚定不移的政策。他说，中国政府愿意在重大国际事务中加强同科威特的友好协调工作。
    杨尚昆还向科威特新闻界朋友介绍了当前中国的形势。他说，尽管当前中国面临一些困难，但只要11亿人民同心同德，一定能克服这些困难，把国家建设好。
