Title: First Large-Screen Flat-Angle Color Display Successfully Developed
Author: Xú Tāo
Date: 1989-12-16
Edition: 2

The first large-screen display using a flat-angle, high-resolution color display tube in China has been successfully developed at the Beijing Dongfeng Television Factory.

During the development of this new type of display, the engineering and technical personnel at the Beijing Dongfeng Television Factory, in response to the current international trend of using spherical display tubes for large-screen color displays over 48 centimeters, have innovated boldly based on the absorption of advanced foreign technology. They have redesigned the circuits and structures, using a 54-centimeter low-transmittance, black-background, anti-glare flat-angle display tube. This gives the display the advantages of a large viewing angle, minimal distortion, and high contrast. During the development process, they overcame several technical challenges, including geometric distortion and nonlinear distortion, and successfully entered the stage of using high-grade flat-angle display tubes in one go.

### 首台大屏幕平面直角彩色显示器研制成功
徐韬
1989-12-16
第2版(国内新闻)
专栏：四面八方

    首台大屏幕平面直角彩色显示器研制成功
    新华社北京12月15日电  （通讯员徐韬）我国第一台以平面直角高分辨率彩色显示管制作的大屏幕显示器，日前在北京东风电视机厂研制成功。
    在研制这种新型显示器的过程中，北京东风电视机厂的工程技术人员，针对当前国际上流行的48厘米以上的大屏幕彩色显示器均采用球面型显示管的情况，在吸收国外先进技术的基础上，大胆创新，重新进行了电路设计和结构设计，采用54厘米低透光率、黑底防眩直角平面显示管，使显示器具有视角大，失真小，对比度高的突出优点。在研制过程中，他们先后攻克了几何失真、非线性失真等方面的技术难关，一次就进入了采用高档平面直角显示管的新阶段。
