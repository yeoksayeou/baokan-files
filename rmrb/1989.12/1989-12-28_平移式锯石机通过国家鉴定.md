Title: The First Domestically Produced Linear Sawing Machine Passes National Inspection
Author: Zhou Mingzhu
Date: 1989-12-28
Page: 2

    Four Corners

    The first domestically produced LHS86-45 type linear sawing machine, developed through the collaborative efforts of Dalian University of Technology, Fujian Huaqiao University, and Factory 7814, has recently been unveiled in Dalian. The machine has successfully passed national-level inspection and is now in mass production.

    This sawing machine, weighing 48 tons, can simultaneously install 45 saw blades, each 2 meters long, and can cut 8-ton blocks of various hardness and patterns of large granite.

### 平移式锯石机通过国家鉴定
邹明珠
1989-12-28
第2版(国内新闻)
专栏：四面八方

    平移式锯石机通过国家鉴定
    本报讯　由大连理工大学、福建华侨大学和7814厂共同消化、吸收国外锯石机械先进技术，设计研制的我国第一台LHS86—45型平移式锯石机最近在大连问世，并通过国家级鉴定，投入批量生产。
    这台锯石机自重48吨，可以同时安装45根2米长的锯条，锯切8吨重的各种硬度和花色品种的巨型花岗石。
            （邹明珠）
