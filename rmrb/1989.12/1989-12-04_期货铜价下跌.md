Title: Copper Futures Prices Fall
Author:
Date: 1989-12-04
Edition: 4

    Copper futures prices on the London Metal Exchange fell from 1686 pounds per ton on November 14 to 1659 pounds per ton on November 15. Meanwhile, prices for tin, aluminum, zinc, nickel, and other non-ferrous metals also experienced varying degrees of decline. The primary reason for the drop in prices of these non-ferrous metals is a decrease in demand, leading to increased inventory.

### 期货铜价下跌

1989-12-04
第4版(国际新闻)
专栏：

    期货铜价下跌
    新华社伦敦电　伦敦五金交易所11月15日的3个月的期货铜价由14日的每吨1686英磅跌至1659英磅。
    与此同时，锡、铝、锌、镍等的价格都有不同程度的下降。这些有色金属价格下跌主要原因是需求衰减，造成库存增加。
