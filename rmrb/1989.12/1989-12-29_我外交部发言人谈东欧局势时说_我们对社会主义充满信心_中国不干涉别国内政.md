Title: Chinese Foreign Ministry Spokesperson on the Situation in Eastern Europe
Author:
Date: 1989-12-29
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, December 28th.

Chinese Foreign Ministry spokesperson Jin Guihua today, at a press conference, responded to questions from reporters by saying that China believes the current situation in some Eastern European countries is their internal affair, and China does not interfere in the internal affairs of other countries.

He said, China sincerely hopes that these countries will achieve social stability and economic development. China will continue to maintain and develop friendly cooperative relations with Eastern European countries on the basis of the Five Principles of Peaceful Coexistence.

In response to a question about the international communist movement, Jin Guihua said, a fundamental experience of the international communist movement is that each country's communist party formulates its own program, line, and policies based on the principle of combining Marxist basic principles with their own country's practice. Throughout history, any social system will encounter twists and turns in its development. We firmly believe that socialism will overcome the difficulties in its progress and continue to develop. We have full confidence in socialism.

Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, December 28th.

Chinese Foreign Ministry spokesperson Jin Guihua today, at a press conference, said that China is paying attention to the developments in Romania and believes this is Romania's internal affair.

When asked about the impact of the Romanian situation on China, Jin Guihua said, each country has its own circumstances. We will continue to proceed from our own national conditions and the fundamental interests of the Chinese people, adhere to the Four Cardinal Principles, and persist in the policy of reform and opening up, building socialism with Chinese characteristics.

### 我外交部发言人谈东欧局势时说  我们对社会主义充满信心  中国不干涉别国内政

1989-12-29
第1版(要闻)
专栏：

    我外交部发言人谈东欧局势时说
    我们对社会主义充满信心
    中国不干涉别国内政
    新华社北京12月28日电　外交部发言人金桂华今天在新闻发布会上回答记者提问时说，中国认为，东欧一些国家当前所发生的事情是它们的内部事务，中国不干涉别国的内政。
    他说，中国衷心希望这些国家社会稳定、经济发展。中国将在和平共处五项原则基础上继续保持和发展同东欧各国的友好合作关系。
    在回答关于国际共运的问题时，金桂华说，国际共产主义运动的一条基本经验是各国共产党按照马克思主义基本原理同本国的实践相结合的原则来制订自己的纲领、路线和方针政策。历史上任何一种社会制度在其发展过程中都会有迂回和曲折。我们坚信社会主义终将克服前进中的困难，继续得到发展。我们对社会主义充满信心。
    新华社北京12月28日电　外交部发言人金桂华今天在新闻发布会上说，中国关注罗马尼亚的事态发展，并认为这是罗马尼亚的内部事务。
    在被问及罗马尼亚局势对中国有何影响时，金桂华说，各国有各国的情况。我们将继续从自己的国情出发，从中国人民的根本利益出发，坚持四项基本原则，坚持改革开放的方针，建设有中国特色的社会主义。
