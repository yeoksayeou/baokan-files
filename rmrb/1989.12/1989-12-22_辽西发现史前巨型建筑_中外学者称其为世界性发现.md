Title: Giant Prehistoric Structure Discovered in Liaoxi
Author: Wei Yunheng, Bu Zhaowen
Date: 1989-12-22
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Shenyang, December 21st — The Hongshan Culture site in Liaoxi, dating back 5,000 years, has made several significant and unusual discoveries in recent years following the 1986 revelation of the Goddess Temple and the Cairn Group, which shocked the world. The most astonishing of these discoveries is the gigantic structure at Niuheliang, the large tomb of a leader from primitive society, and the exquisite jade artifacts unearthed from numerous cairns. Archaeological experts believe that these new important discoveries provide more abundant and detailed firsthand materials for further exploring the origins of Chinese civilization. Scholars from the United States, France, Japan, India, and other countries who have conducted on-site investigations share the view that the Hongshan Culture site in Liaoxi is a world-class discovery comparable to the Egyptian pyramids and the ancient Indus Valley civilization of Mohenjo-Daro.

The first discovery of a prehistoric structure with pyramid-like qualities in China by Liaoning archaeologists at Niuheliang is grand in scale and impressive in appearance. The above-ground portion of the large structure is a man-made mound of rammed earth, with a diameter of nearly 40 meters and surrounded by large stones. The inner stone circle is neatly circular, with a diameter of nearly 60 meters. The entire structure covers an area of nearly 10,000 square meters. Experts have determined that this site is over 5,000 years old.

### 辽西发现史前巨型建筑  中外学者称其为世界性发现
魏运亨  卜昭文
1989-12-22
第1版(要闻)
专栏：

    辽西发现史前巨型建筑
    中外学者称其为世界性发现
    据新华社沈阳12月21日电（记者魏运亨、卜昭文）距今5000年前的辽西红山文化遗址，继3年前发现轰动中外的女神庙和积石冢群之后，近年来陆续又有多项重要奇特的发现。其中最为惊人的是牛河梁的巨型建筑、原始社会首领人物的大型墓葬，以及在众多积石冢中出土的精美的玉器等。考古专家们认为，这一系列的新的重要发现，为继续探讨中华文明起源问题提供了更丰富更翔实的第一手材料。美、法、日、印度等国学者实地考察后的共同观感是，辽西红山文化遗址是可与埃及金字塔、印度河莫亨觉达罗古文明相比的世界性发现。
    辽宁考古工作者在牛河梁首次发现的我国史前具有金字塔性质的巨型建筑，规模宏伟，气势不凡。大型建筑的地上部分，中心是人工夯起的土丘，夯土堆直径近40米，外包巨石。内石圈砌成规整的圆形，直径近60米。整个建筑的范围大约有近万平方米。专家们断定这座遗址距今已有5000多年历史。
