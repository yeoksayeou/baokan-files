Title: The Ministry of Foreign Economic Relations and Trade Computation Center Information Consulting Department Opens
Author: Guosheng
Date: 1989-12-16
Edition: 2

The Ministry of Foreign Economic Relations and Trade Computation Center's Economic Information Consulting Department at the Beijing Lido Holiday Hotel officially opened today. This consulting department serves as a window for foreign businessmen to understand the Chinese market and will provide accurate and timely foreign economic and trade information services to hotel guests.

### 经贸部计算中心信息咨询部开业
国胜
1989-12-16
第2版(国内新闻)
专栏：

    经贸部计算中心信息咨询部开业
    本报北京12月15日讯　经贸部计算中心驻北京丽都假日饭店经济信息咨询部今天正式开业。这个咨询部是外国商人了解中国市场的窗口，将为饭店旅客提供准确、及时的对外经济贸易信息服务。
            （国　胜）
