Title: Comments by Foreign Ministry Spokesperson on the Informal Summit Between U.S. and Soviet Leaders
Author:
Date: 1989-12-07
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, December 6th.

The spokesperson for the Chinese Foreign Ministry stated today, "The leaders of the United States and the Soviet Union held an informal meeting in Malta, where they exchanged views on a wide range of issues, including the situation in Europe and U.S.-Soviet relations. We hope that the outcome of the meeting will genuinely contribute to easing and stabilizing the global situation without harming the interests of third countries."

The spokesperson made these remarks in response to a reporter's question.

### 就美苏首脑非正式会晤  外交部发言人发表评论

1989-12-07
第1版(要闻)
专栏：

    就美苏首脑非正式会晤
    外交部发言人发表评论
    新华社北京12月6日电　中国外交部发言人今天在这里说，“美苏两国领导人在马耳他进行了一次非正式会晤，就欧洲局势和美苏关系等广泛的问题交换了意见。我们希望会晤的结果能真正有利于促进世界局势的缓和与稳定，而不损害第三国的利益。”
    这位外交部发言人是在回答记者的提问时发表上述评论的。
