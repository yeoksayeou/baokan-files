Title: The Leading Enterprise in Intestinal Casing Production in Hebei Province
Author: Wáng Jǐngrén
Date: 1989-12-17
Edition: 5

    Da Han Zhai Joint Venture Intestinal Casing Factory in Gu'an County, Hebei Province, is one of the key enterprises in the national production of intestinal casing. In recent years, it has produced over 1,000 barrels of sheep intestinal casing annually, and 500,000 sheep intestinal casing sleeves. This year, it is expected to achieve a production value of 23 million yuan and earn 4 million US dollars in foreign exchange.

    Photographed by People's Daily reporter Wáng Jǐngrén.
