Title: Salvador Guerrillas Announce Christmas Ceasefire
Author:
Date: 1989-12-24
Edition: 4

Salvadoran guerrillas of the "Farabundo Marti National Liberation Front" announced on the 22nd that they will implement a unilateral ceasefire during the Christmas and New Year period. This ceasefire will begin at midnight on the 23rd and last until January 2, 1990. At the same time, the Salvadoran government announced that the curfew will be lifted from the night of the 24th to New Year's Day.

### 萨游击队宣布圣诞期间停火

1989-12-24
第4版(国际新闻)
专栏：

    萨游击队宣布圣诞期间停火
    萨尔瓦多游击队“法拉本多·马蒂民族解放阵线”二十二日宣布，圣诞新年期间实行单方面停火。这次停火从二十三日零时起，至一九九○年一月二日为止。与此同时，萨尔瓦多政府宣布二十四日夜间与元旦当天取消宵禁。
                                      （本报专电）
