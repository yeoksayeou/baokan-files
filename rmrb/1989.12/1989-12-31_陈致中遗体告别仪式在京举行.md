Title: Funeral of Chen Zhizhong Held in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1989-12-31
Edition: 2

The funeral of Chen Zhizhong, former secretary of the Chinese Academy of Sciences Semiconductor Research Institute, was held this morning at the Babaoshan Revolutionary Cemetery Hall in Beijing. Chen Zhizhong passed away on December 18, 1989, at 5:45 AM in Beijing due to illness. He was 74 years old.

Wreaths were sent by Hab Zhuming, Fang Yi, Ma Wenrui, Zhou Guangzhao, and others. Ma Wenrui and others attended the funeral.

Chen Zhizhong was born in January 1915 in Qingjian County, Shaanxi Province. He joined the revolution in 1934 and the Communist Party of China the same year. During the Second Revolutionary Civil War, he served as the inspector of the Shaanxi Provincial Committee of the Communist Party and the deputy secretary of the Shaanxi Provincial Committee of the Communist Party in Hengshan County, leading local anti-encirclement guerrilla warfare. From December 1938 to 1939, he studied at the Central Party School of the Communist Party in Yan'an and served as the secretary of the branch. After the victory of the War of Resistance Against Japan, Chen Zhizhong voluntarily requested to work in the areas under the rule of the Kuomintang, developing a large number of underground grassroots organizations of the Party in extremely dangerous and difficult environments, and establishing underground revolutionary armed forces. After the founding of the People's Republic of China, Chen Zhizhong successively held important leading positions in the Ministry of Culture and Gansu Province. From 1965 to 1980, he served as the secretary of the Party Committee of the Semiconductor Research Institute of the Chinese Academy of Sciences and the secretary of the Party Committee of the Institute of Vertebrate Paleontology and Paleoanthropology of the Chinese Academy of Sciences. He retired in 1982.

Chen Zhizhong devoted his life to the cause of the Party, fighting all his life. In more than half a century of revolutionary career, he was loyal to the Party and the revolution, fearing neither hardship nor sacrifice. He put the interests of the Party and the revolution first at all times, demonstrating the noble qualities of a Communist.

### 陈致中遗体告别仪式在京举行

1989-12-31
第2版(国内新闻)
专栏：

    陈致中遗体告别仪式在京举行
    新华社北京12月29日电　中国科学院半导体研究所原党委书记陈致中同志，因病于1989年12月18日5时45分在北京逝世，享年74岁。陈致中同志遗体告别仪式今天上午在八宝山革命公墓礼堂举行。
    习仲勋、方毅、马文瑞、周光召等同志送了花圈。马文瑞等参加了今天的遗体告别仪式。
    陈致中同志1915年1月生于陕西省清涧县。1934年参加革命。同年参加中国共产党。在第二次国内革命战争时期，曾任中共陕北省委巡视员，中共陕西省横山县委副书记等职，参加领导了当地的反围剿游击战。1938年12月至1939年，在延安中共中央党校学习，并任支部书记。抗日战争胜利后，陈致中同志主动要求到国民党统治区开展工作，在十分危险与困难的环境下，发展了大量党的地下基层组织，建立了地下革命武装。中华人民共和国成立后，陈致中同志先后在文化部、甘肃省担任重要领导职务。1965年至1980年先后任中国科学院半导体研究所党委书记，中国科学院古脊椎动物与古人类研究所党委书记。1982年离休。
    陈致中同志为党的事业呕心沥血，奋斗一生。他在半个多世纪的革命历程中，忠于党、忠于革命，不怕艰苦与牺牲。他五十年如一日，时时刻刻把党的利益，革命的利益放在第一位，表现了共产党人的高尚品质。
