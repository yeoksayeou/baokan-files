Title: China Focuses on the Development of the Situation in Eastern Europe
Author:
Date: 1989-12-01
Edition: 1

China is closely following the development of the situation in Eastern Europe, including Czechoslovakia, and hopes for their social stability and economic development.

New China News Agency, Beijing, November 30th - Foreign Ministry spokesperson Jin Guihua said at a news conference this afternoon that China is closely following the development of the situation in Eastern Europe, including Czechoslovakia.

In response to a reporter's question, he said that what is currently happening in some Eastern European countries, including Czechoslovakia, is their internal affairs. "We do not interfere in the internal affairs of other countries. We believe that the people of these Eastern European countries will ultimately find a path to building socialism that suits their own national conditions based on the actual situation in their own countries."

He said, "We sincerely hope that these countries will achieve social stability and economic development."

### 我国关注东欧形势发展  希望他们社会稳定经济发展

1989-12-01
第1版(要闻)
专栏：

    我国关注东欧形势发展
    希望他们社会稳定经济发展
    新华社北京十一月三十日电　外交部发言人金桂华在今天下午的新闻发布会上说，中国密切关注东欧包括捷克斯洛伐克形势的发展。
    他在回答记者提问时说，东欧一些国家，包括捷克斯洛伐克，当前发生的一些事情是它们的内部事务。“我们不干涉别国的内政，我们相信这些东欧国家的人民终究会从自己国家的实际情况出发，找到符合本国国情的建设社会主义的道路。”
    他说，“我们衷心希望这些国家社会稳定，经济发展。”
