Title: Organizing Committee of the Asian Games Science Conference Holds Certificate Awarding Ceremony
Author: Jiang Jianke
Date: 1989-12-21
Edition: 3

The organizing committee of the 1990 Beijing Asian Games Science Conference held a certificate awarding ceremony in Beijing today. Correspondent Jiang Jianke reports:

The 1990 Asian Games Science Conference is a high-level sports science event in Asia. As of today, 18 countries and regions, including the United States, the Soviet Union, the United Kingdom, South Korea, and Japan, have registered to participate.

Vice Minister of the State Sports Commission and Chairman of the 1990 Beijing Asian Games Science Conference Organizing Committee Zhang Caizhen, among others, presented certificates to representatives from 19 domestic sponsoring companies. The China Technology Innovation Company received the 1990 Beijing Asian Games Science Conference Sports Science Contribution Award.

### 亚运会科学大会组委会举行颁证式
蒋建科
1989-12-21
第3版(综合)
专栏：体育

    亚运会科学大会组委会举行颁证式
    本报北京12月20日讯　记者蒋建科报道：1990年北京亚运会科学大会组委会今天在京向赞助单位颁发证书。
    1990年亚运会科学大会是亚洲的一次高水平的体育科学盛会。截止到今天，已有18个国家和地区，包括美国、苏联、英国、南朝鲜、日本等报名参加。
    国家体委副主任、1990年北京亚运会科学大会组委会主任张彩珍等向国内19个赞助厂家的代表颁发了证书。中国技术创新公司获1990年北京亚运会科学大会体育科学贡献奖。
