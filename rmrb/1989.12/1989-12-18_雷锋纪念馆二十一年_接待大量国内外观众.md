Title: The 21st Anniversary of the Lei Feng Memorial Hall
Author: Wu Xinghua
Date: 1989-12-18
Edition: 3

The spirit of Lei Feng has always inspired and encouraged the broad masses of young people and the people at home and abroad. The Lei Feng Memorial Hall in Lei Feng's hometown, Wangcheng County, Hunan Province, has received over 5 million visitors from all over the country in the 21 years since its establishment. It has also welcomed more than 14,000 foreign friends from 38 countries and regions around the world.

### 雷锋纪念馆二十一年  接待大量国内外观众
吴兴华
1989-12-18
第3版(综合)
专栏：

    雷锋纪念馆二十一年
    接待大量国内外观众
    本报讯　雷锋精神始终吸引和鼓舞着国内外广大青年和人民群众。伟大的共产主义战士雷锋家乡湖南省望城县雷锋纪念馆建馆二十一年来，接待来自全国各地的观众五百多万人次，接待来自世界三十八个国家和地区的外国朋友一万四千多人次。　（吴兴华）
