Title: Avellaneda Says China Has Good Conditions for Hosting the World Cup
Author:
Date: 1989-12-21
Edition: 3

	Avellaneda Says China Has Good Conditions for Hosting the World Cup

	Xinhua News Agency, Rio de Janeiro, December 19th - The president of FIFA, Avellaneda, said today here that China has all the conditions and means necessary to host the 2002 World Cup.

	This was stated by Avellaneda during a lunch with foreign journalists stationed in Rio de Janeiro.

	The FIFA president believes that China, Japan, and Saudi Arabia all have the conditions to host the World Cup. These countries have good accommodation, sufficient football fields, and transportation, so there is no reason to prevent them from hosting the World Cup.

	Avellaneda told our reporter that he has visited China seven times and likes this country very much. He watched a women's football match in Guangzhou, where over 300,000 spectators watched the game and CCTV broadcasted the match live, all of which left a deep impression on him. He said, "The Chinese government and people have treated me very well. Next October, I will go to China to watch the Asian Games. Before I step down, I will do everything I can to make the 2002 World Cup held in China or Japan."

### 阿维兰热称中国具备良好条件  力促世界杯足球赛在我国举行

1989-12-21
第3版(综合)
专栏：体育

    阿维兰热称中国具备良好条件
    力促世界杯足球赛在我国举行
    新华社里约热内卢12月19日电　国际足联主席阿维兰热今天在这里说，中国具备在2002年主办世界杯足球赛的各种条件和必要的手段。
    这是阿维兰热在和驻此间的外国记者共进午餐时说的。
    这位国际足联主席认为，中国、日本和沙特阿拉伯均具备主办世界杯足球赛的条件，这些国家拥有良好的住宿条件，足够的足球场和交通工具，因此没理由阻止他们主办世界杯赛。
    阿维兰热对本社记者说，他曾7次访问中国，很喜欢这个国家。他曾在广州观看了中国女子足球赛，30多万名观众观看比赛及中央电视台转播比赛实况等都给他留下了深刻的印象。他说：“中国政府和人民待我很好，明年10月我将去中国观看亚运会。在我卸任以前，我将尽一切努力使2002年的世界杯赛在中国或日本举行。”
