Title: Washable Carpets Hit the Market
Author:
Date: 1989-12-18
Page: 2

From Four Corners:

A new type of flooring material known as "washable carpet" – polyvinyl chloride (PVC) elastic roll flooring – has been successfully developed and is now in mass production by Changzhou Architectural Plastics Factory. This innovative flooring material has recently been selected for use in Beijing's Asian Games Village and has also gained popularity in the Soviet Union and Southeast Asia.

### “可清洗地毯”面市

1989-12-18
第2版(国内新闻)
专栏：四面八方

    “可清洗地毯”面市
    本报讯　被称为“可清洗的地毯”的一种新型铺地材料——聚氯乙烯弹性卷材地板，已由常州建筑塑料厂试制成功，并投入批量生产。这种新型铺地材料最近被北京亚运村选用，并在苏联和东南亚地区受到欢迎。
