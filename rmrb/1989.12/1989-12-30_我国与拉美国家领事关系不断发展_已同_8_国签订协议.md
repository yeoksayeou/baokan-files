Title: China's Consular Relations with Latin American Countries Continue to Develop
Author:
Date: 1989-12-30
Page: 2

Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, December 29th.

Our country's consular relations with Latin American countries continue to develop. China has signed agreements with 8 countries to establish consulates or consulates-general, two of which were signed this year.

On June 8th this year, China and the Republic of Cuba reached an agreement to establish consulates-general. On November 8th, China and the Republic of Colombia reached an agreement to establish consulates.

The Latin American countries that have agreements with China to establish consulates or consulates-general are: Ecuador, Brazil, Mexico, Chile, Bolivia, Argentina, Cuba, and Colombia.

### 我国与拉美国家领事关系不断发展  已同8国签订协议

1989-12-30
第2版(国内新闻)
专栏：

    我国与拉美国家领事关系不断发展
    已同8国签订协议
    新华社北京12月29日电　本社记者今天从外交部获悉，中国已同拉美地区8个国家签订了互设总领事馆或领事馆的协议，其中有两项协议是今年签订的。
    今年6月8日，中国与古巴共和国达成了互设总领事馆的协议。11月8日，中国与哥伦比亚共和国就互设领事馆问题达成协议。
    与中国有互设总领事馆或领事馆协议的拉美国家是：厄瓜多尔、巴西、墨西哥、智利、玻利维亚、阿根廷、古巴和哥伦比亚。
