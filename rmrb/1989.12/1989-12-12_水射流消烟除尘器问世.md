Title: Water Jet Smoke and Dust Removal Device Developed
Author:
Date: 1989-12-12
Page: 2

Xinhua News Agency, Hefei, December 11th.

A senior engineer from Pan Yi Mine in Huainan, Anhui Province, Yao Huanwen, has successfully applied water jet technology to environmental protection, creating a water jet smoke and dust removal device. This innovation has recently been granted a national patent.

This device, after trials, has shown a smoke and dust removal efficiency of over 95%, reduced the noise of the induced draft fan to below 50 decibels, and saved up to 40% of electricity.

### 水射流消烟除尘器问世

1989-12-12
第2版(国内新闻)
专栏：四面八方

    水射流消烟除尘器问世
    新华社合肥12月11日电　安徽淮南潘一矿高级工程师姚焕文将水射技术用于环境保护，首创成功水射流消烟除尘器，并于最近获得了国家专利。
    这种除尘器，经试验表明，消烟除尘率达95％以上，引风机的噪音降低到50分贝以下，并可节电40％。
