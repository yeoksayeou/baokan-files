Title: Rail Transport Achieves Good Results Last Month
Author: Lǐ Méi
Date: 1989-12-14
Edition: 1

	Rail transport achieved good results last month. According to Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, December 13th, since entering November, some regions of the national railway have been affected by cold waves and snowstorms. Broad masses of railway workers overcame difficulties and completed the transportation production plan comprehensively. The loading and unloading of cars and coal transportation in November reached a historical high for the same period. According to statistics from the Ministry of Railways, the daily average loading of cars in November was 73,505, exceeding the plan by 2,505 cars; the daily average unloading of cars was 76,009, exceeding the planned unloading by 9,801 cars.

### 铁路运输上月取得好成绩
李玫
1989-12-14
第1版(要闻)
专栏：

    铁路运输上月取得好成绩
    据新华社北京十二月十三日电 （记者李玫）进入十一月份以来，全国铁路部分地区遭受了寒流和风雪的袭扰。广大铁路员工克服困难，全面完成运输生产计划，十一月份的装卸车及煤炭运输创历史同期最高水平。据铁道部统计，十一月份全路日均装车七万三千五百零五车，比计划多二千五百零五车；日均卸车完成七万六千零九车，超应卸九千八百零一车。
