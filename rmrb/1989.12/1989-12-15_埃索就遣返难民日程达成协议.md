Title: Ethiopia and Somalia Agree on Schedule for Repatriation of Refugees
Author:
Date: 1989-12-15
Edition: 4

Ethiopia and Somalia reached an agreement on December 13 regarding the schedule for repatriating Ethiopian refugees within Somalia.

This agreement was reached during the third meeting of the tripartite committee consisting of Ethiopia, Somalia, and the United Nations High Commissioner for Refugees.

According to the schedule, the registration of Ethiopian refugees currently in Somalia should be completed by February 28, 1990.

Ethiopia will begin preparations to receive the refugees in January of next year. The repatriation process will commence in March 1990.

### 埃索就遣返难民日程达成协议

1989-12-15
第4版(国际新闻)
专栏：

    埃索就遣返难民日程达成协议
    埃塞俄比亚和索马里十三日就遣返在索马里境内的埃塞俄比亚难民的日程达成协议。
    这项协议是在埃塞俄比亚、索马里和联合国难民署三方委员会第三次会议上达成的。
    根据这项日程，目前在索马里的埃塞俄比亚难民登记工作应该在一九九○年二月二十八日完成。
    埃塞俄比亚将在明年一月开始进行接受难民的准备工作。遣返工作将于一九九○年三月开始。
