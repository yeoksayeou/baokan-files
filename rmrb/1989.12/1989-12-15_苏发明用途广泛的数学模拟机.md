Title: Soviet Scientists Invent Versatile Mathematical Simulator
Author:
Date: 1989-12-15
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Moscow

Soviet scientists from the Belorussian Republic have invented a versatile mathematical simulator that can be used for environmental protection and weather forecasting.

This simulator can calculate the potential pollution levels of future enterprises during the design phase, providing suggestions for optimal locations to minimize environmental impact.

It can also simulate the behavior of fires under various weather conditions and in different regions, predicting potential fire sites and enabling timely preventive measures. In the event of a fire, it can propose scientific fire-fighting strategies.

Additionally, the simulator can monitor nuclear radiation and predict the long-term movement of metallic elements cesium and strontium, determining where they might concentrate and in which foods they might be found.

### 苏发明用途广泛的数学模拟机

1989-12-15
第4版(国际新闻)
专栏：

    苏发明用途广泛的数学模拟机
    新华社莫斯科电苏联白俄罗斯共和国的科学家发明一种用途广泛的数学模拟机，它既可用于环保，又可用于气象预报。
    这种数学模拟机在企业设计阶段，就能算出未来企业对环境的污染程度，对厂址提出建议，以便把对自然界的危害减少到最低程度。
    它还可以模拟火在各种天气及地带的行为特点，可以预测可能发生火灾的地点，以便及时采取预防措施。一旦发生火灾，即能提出科学灭火方案。
    数学模拟机还可用于对核辐射的监测，对金属元素铯和锶的位移作出长期预报，确定它们可能集中在哪些地方和哪些食品中。
