Title: Castro Hosts Reception for Zheng Tuobin
Author:
Date: 1989-12-16
Edition: 4

Castro Hosts Reception for Zheng Tuobin
China and Cuba Sign Economic Cooperation Agreement

HAVANA, Dec. 13 (Xinhua) — Cuban President Fidel Castro hosted a reception at the Revolution Palace in Havana this evening to welcome the Chinese government's economic and trade delegation led by Zheng Tuobin, Minister of Foreign Economic Relations and Trade.

Before the reception, Castro met with Minister Zheng Tuobin and held friendly talks.

Vice Chairman of the Cuban Council of State Rafael Rodriguez, Osmany Cienfuegos, and Pedro Miret, along with several ministers, attended the reception.

Chinese Ambassador to Cuba Tang Yonggui also attended the reception.

Zheng Tuobin and his delegation arrived in Havana on the 11th to participate in the Sino-Cuban Economic Cooperation Joint Committee meeting.

HAVANA, Dec. 14 (Xinhua) — The governments of China and Cuba signed an economic cooperation agreement in Havana today.

According to the agreement, the two countries will cooperate in the fields of agriculture, animal husbandry, fisheries, sugar industry, light industry, food industry, textile industry, electronics industry, and machinery industry.

Zheng Tuobin, Minister of Foreign Economic Relations and Trade of China, and Ricardo Cabrisas, Minister of Foreign Trade of Cuba, signed the agreement during the Sino-Cuban Economic Cooperation Joint Committee meeting.

### 卡斯特罗为郑拓彬举行招待会  中古签署经济合作协定

1989-12-16
第4版(国际新闻)
专栏：

    卡斯特罗为郑拓彬举行招待会
    中古签署经济合作协定
    新华社哈瓦那12月13日电　古巴国务委员会主席菲德尔·卡斯特罗今晚在哈瓦那革命宫举行招待会，欢迎以对外经贸部部长郑拓彬为首的中国政府经济贸易代表团。
    招待会之前，卡斯特罗会见了郑拓彬部长，双方进行了友好的谈话。
    古巴国务委员会副主席拉斐尔·罗德里格斯、奥斯马尼·西恩富戈斯和佩得罗·米雷特以及一些部长出席了招待会。
    中国驻古巴大使汤永贵也出席了招待会。
    郑拓彬部长一行是11日抵达哈瓦那前来参加中古经济合作混委会会议的。
    新华社哈瓦那12月14日电　中国和古巴政府今天在哈瓦那签署了中古两国经济合作协定。
    根据协定，中古两国将在农业、畜牧业、渔业、制糖业、轻工业、食品工业、纺织业、电子工业和机械工业等方面进行合作。
    正在这里参加中古经济合作混委会会议的中国对外经贸部部长郑拓彬和古巴外贸部长里卡多·卡夫里萨斯在协定上签了字。
