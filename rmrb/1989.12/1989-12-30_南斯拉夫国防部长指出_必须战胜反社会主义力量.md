Title: Yugoslav Defense Minister Says It Is Necessary to Defeat Anti-Socialist Forces
Author: Yan Zheng
Date: 1989-12-30
Edition: 4

Yugoslav Defense Minister Says It Is Necessary to Defeat Anti-Socialist Forces

	BELGRADE, Dec. 28 (Xinhua) — "We are facing the major task of pulling our country out of the crisis. In this decisive battle, we must defeat the forces that oppose our country's unity and socialism," said Yugoslav Defense Minister Veljko Kadijević today when he met with retired generals at the Belgrade Soldiers' Home.

	General Kadijević further pointed out that Yugoslavia's difficult social situation and the extremely sharp conflicts among the leadership over future development programs have become the greatest threat to national security. It must also be noted that related to this are the developments of contradictions in international relations. The roots of threats to peace and security in the world have not been eliminated. The latest events on the turbulent international stage are proving this every day.

### 南斯拉夫国防部长指出  必须战胜反社会主义力量
严正
1989-12-30
第4版(国际新闻)
专栏：

    南斯拉夫国防部长指出
    必须战胜反社会主义力量
    本报贝尔格莱德12月28日电　记者严正报道：“我们正面临着使国家摆脱危机的重大任务，在这决定性的战斗中，必须战胜那些反对我们国家统一和反对社会主义的力量”。这是南斯拉夫国防部长韦·卡迪耶维奇今天在贝尔格莱德军人之家接见南退役将军时说的一番话。
    卡迪耶维奇大将还指出，南斯拉夫艰难的社会局势以及领导人就未来发展纲领所发生的非常尖锐的冲突已成为国家安全的最大威胁，还必须看到与此有关的是国际关系中矛盾的发展，世界上威胁和平与安全的根源并没有被排除，在动荡的国际舞台上发生的最新事件每天都在证实这一点。
