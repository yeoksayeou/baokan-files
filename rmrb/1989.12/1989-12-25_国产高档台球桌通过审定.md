Title: Domestic High-End Billiards Table Passes Inspection
Author:
Date: 1989-12-25
Page: 3

Transmitted by People's Daily, Beijing

The high-end competition billiards equipment series produced by the Beijing Daxing Xinmin Billiards Equipment Factory, known as the "Star" brand, has recently passed inspection by the State Sports Commission in Beijing. The products include English-style snooker, American-style pocket, and Japanese-style carom billiards tables. Relevant authorities believe that the quality of domestically produced billiards equipment is now close to that of world-famous brands. It is necessary to end the practice of spending large amounts of foreign exchange to import luxury billiards tables as soon as possible.

### 国产高档台球桌通过审定

1989-12-25
第3版(综合)
专栏：体育

    国产高档台球桌通过审定
    本报讯　北京市大兴新民台球器械厂的星牌高档比赛用台球器材系列产品，最近在北京通过国家体委审定。产品包括英式斯诺克、美式落袋和日式开轮台球桌等。有关方面认为，国产台球器材质量已接近世界名牌，应当尽快结束动用大量外汇进口豪华台球桌。
