Title: Uruguay's President-Elect Meets Chinese Ambassador
Author:
Date: 1989-12-24
Edition: 4

Uruguay's President-elect Luis Alberto Lacalle met with Chinese Ambassador to Uruguay Yang Xiuqiang today and said that economic and trade relations between Uruguay and China are "very important," and that the new government will strengthen relations with China in this regard.

Ambassador Yang conveyed greetings from the Chinese leadership and said that China is willing to continue to develop relations with Uruguay. The atmosphere of the meeting was friendly.

China and Uruguay established diplomatic relations in 1988. Since then, relations between the two countries have developed smoothly.

Regarding the Taiwan issue, Ambassador Yang reiterated to Lacalle the principle that there is only one China and that Taiwan is a part of Chinese territory.

### 乌拉圭当选总统会见我大使

1989-12-24
第4版(国际新闻)
专栏：

    乌拉圭当选总统会见我大使
    据新华社蒙得维的亚12月22日电　乌拉圭当选总统路易斯·阿尔维托·拉卡列今天在这里会见中国驻乌大使杨许强时表示，乌拉圭同中国的经济贸易关系“非常重要”，新政府将加强同中国发展这方面的关系。
    杨大使向拉卡列转达了中国领导人的问候，并表示中国愿意同乌拉圭继续发展关系。会见时气氛友好。
    中国和乌拉圭于1988年建交。建交后两国关系发展顺利。
    在谈到台湾问题时，杨大使向拉卡列重申了只有一个中国，台湾是中国领土的一部分的原则立场。
