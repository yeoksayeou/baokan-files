Title: Shanghai and Carl Zeiss Jena Exchange Optical Instrument Technology
Author: Chen Yue Shen  Yu Xi
Date: 1989-12-11
Edition: 2

Shanghai Optical Instrument Factory and the East German Carl Zeiss Jena Joint Venture held the 1989 Optical Instrument Product Exhibition and Technical Exchange Meeting in Shanghai today. Local economic circles believe this is a beneficial attempt for the two optical enterprises to join hands and march towards the global market.

During this product exhibition and technical exchange meeting, Carl Zeiss of East Germany displayed eight products, including interference contrast microscopes, and gave five technical lectures. Shanghai Optical Instrument Factory provided 30 products, including biological microscopes and metallographic microscopes.

### 上海与民德企业交流光仪技术
陈月申  余曦
1989-12-11
第2版(国内新闻)
专栏：

    上海与民德企业交流光仪技术
    据新华社上海12月10日电　（通讯员陈月申、记者余曦）上海光学仪器厂与民主德国卡尔蔡司耶拿联合企业合作举办1989年度光学仪器产品陈列、技术交流会，今天在此间正式开幕。此间经济界人士认为，这是两地光学企业携手走向世界市场的有益尝试。
    在这次产品陈列、技术交流会上，民德蔡司公司展出干涉相衬显微镜等8种产品，并主讲5项技术讲座。上海光学仪器厂提供生物显微镜、金相显微镜等30种产品。
