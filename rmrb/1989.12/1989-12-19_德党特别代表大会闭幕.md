Title: The Special Congress of the SED Closes
Author:
Date: 1989-12-19
Edition: 4

The special congress of the Socialist Unity Party of Germany (SED) concluded this afternoon in Berlin.

The special congress, which began on December 8, was suspended after two days of proceedings and resumed on December 16 and 17. During the session on December 16, it was decided to rename the Socialist Unity Party of Germany to the Socialist Unity Party of Germany—Party of Democratic Socialism. On December 17, Party Chairman Egon Krenz delivered a report. In discussing the future development of the German Democratic Republic, Krenz emphasized the need to maintain the independence and sovereignty of the GDR and advocated for the development of a security partnership between the two Germanys. The congress also adopted a new party constitution.

### 德党特别代表大会闭幕

1989-12-19
第4版(国际新闻)
专栏：

    德党特别代表大会闭幕
    新华社柏林12月17日电  德国统一社会党特别代表大会于17日下午在这里闭幕。
    这次特别代表大会是12月8日开幕的。会议进行两天后中断，于16日和17日继续举行。在16日的会议上，决定将德国统一社会党更名为德国统一社会党—民主社会主义党。党的主席居西17日在会上作了报告。在谈到民主德国的未来发展时，居西认为要维护民主德国的独立和主权，主张发展两个德国之间的安全伙伴关系。这次会议还通过了新党章。
