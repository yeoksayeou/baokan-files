Title: China's Foreign Ministry Spokesperson Issues Statement Condemning U.S. Armed Invasion of Panama
Author: Gù Yùqīng
Date: 1989-12-22
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, December 21st.

China's Foreign Ministry spokesperson issued a statement condemning the U.S. armed invasion of Panama.

Today in the afternoon, Foreign Ministry spokesperson Jin Guihua, at a press conference, expressed condemnation of the U.S. invasion of Panama.

In response to a reporter's question, the Foreign Ministry spokesperson pointed out that on December 20th, the United States launched an armed invasion of Panama. This is an infringement on the sovereignty of a third-world country and a violation of international law and the purposes and principles of the United Nations Charter. We are shocked by this and strongly condemn it.

He went on to say that we have always believed that relations between countries should strictly adhere to the Five Principles of Peaceful Coexistence. We firmly oppose any country interfering in the internal affairs of another, especially interference by force. The independence and sovereignty of Panama should be respected. The United States should immediately stop its armed invasion of Panama and prevent the situation from escalating further.

### 我外交部发言人发表谈话  谴责美国武装入侵巴拿马
顾玉清
1989-12-22
第1版(要闻)
专栏：

    我外交部发言人发表谈话
    谴责美国武装入侵巴拿马
    本报北京12月21日讯　记者顾玉清报道：今天下午，外交部发言人金桂华在新闻发布会上，对美国入侵巴拿马一事表示谴责。
    外交部发言人在回答记者提问时指出，12月20日，美国对巴拿马进行武装入侵，这是对一个第三世界主权国的侵犯，是违反国际法准则和联合国宪章的宗旨和原则的。我们对此感到震惊，并予以谴责。
    他接着说，我们一贯认为，国与国之间的关系应该严格遵循和平共处五项原则。我们坚决反对任何国家以任何借口和形式干涉别国内政，特别是反对以武力干涉别国内部事务。巴拿马的独立和主权应当得到尊重。美国应当立即停止对巴拿马的武装入侵，防止事态进一步扩大。
