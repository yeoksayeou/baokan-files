Title: 20 Construction Projects Receive the Luban Award
Author:
Date: 1989-12-18
Edition: 2

20 construction projects have been selected for China's highest honor in construction quality—the Luban Award.

The China Construction Industry Association announced today the 1989 Luban Award-winning projects, which include the National Science and Technology Information Center Main Building, Shanghai New Railway Station, Shanghai Grand View Garden, Tianjin New Railway Station, Tianjin Longmen Tower, and 16 other projects. This is the third time the Luban Award has been selected since 1987.

### 20项建筑工程获鲁班奖

1989-12-18
第2版(国内新闻)
专栏：四面八方

    20项建筑工程获鲁班奖
    新华社北京12月17日电　（记者李安定）20项建筑工程入选我国建筑工程质量的最高荣誉奖——鲁班奖。
    中国建筑业联合会今天公布的1989年度建筑工程鲁班奖的获奖工程是国家科技情报中心主楼、上海新客站、上海大观园、天津新客站、天津龙门大厦等20项。这次鲁班奖评选是自1987年以来的第三次。
