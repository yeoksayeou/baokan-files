Title: Jordanian Senate President Highly Praises Yang Shangkun's Visit to the Middle East
Author:
Date: 1989-12-28
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Amman, December 27th.

	The President of the Jordanian Senate, Ahmad Luzi, praised Chinese Chairman Yang Shangkun's visit to four Arab countries, saying it played a significant role in promoting friendship between China and Arab countries and advancing the Middle East peace process.

	Luzi made these remarks today when he received the Chinese Broadcasting and Television Delegation at the Jordanian Parliament Building.

	Luzi briefed the delegation on the situation of the Palestinians in the occupied territories who are struggling to end the Israeli occupation.

	He emphasized that the relationship between Jordan and China is solid and has developed continuously.

### 约旦参议院议长  高度评价杨主席访问中东

1989-12-28
第4版(国际新闻)
专栏：

    约旦参议院议长
    高度评价杨主席访问中东
    据新华社安曼12月27日电  约旦参议院议长艾哈迈德·卢齐称赞中国主席杨尚昆对阿拉伯四国的访问，为增进中国与阿拉伯国家的友谊以及推进中东和平进程起了重大的作用。
    卢齐是今天在约旦议会大厦接见中国广播电视代表团时说这番话的。
    卢齐向代表团介绍了以占区巴勒斯坦人为结束以色列占领进行斗争的情况。
    他强调，约中两国关系是牢固的，并取得不间断的发展。
