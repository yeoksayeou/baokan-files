Title: Construction Begins on TianSheng Bridge to Guangzhou Power Transmission Project
Author:
Date: 1989-12-07
Page: 2

	Xinhua News Agency, Nanning, December 6th.

	The TianSheng Bridge to Guangzhou 500,000-volt ultra-high-voltage AC power transmission project, a key project in the National "Seventh Five-Year Plan," recently broke ground in Guangxi.

	This power transmission project is 975 kilometers long, with approximately 776 kilometers within Guangxi. It is a joint venture between China and foreign investors, with a total investment of about 900 million yuan.

	The construction of this power transmission project will significantly alleviate the power shortage in Guangdong and accelerate the development of the "West River Corridor" industrial base in the two Guangxi and Guangdong provinces.

### 天生桥至广州输电工程开工

1989-12-07
第2版(国内新闻)
专栏：四面八方

    天生桥至广州输电工程开工
    本报南宁12月6日电  国家“七五”计划重点工程——广西天生桥至广州的50万伏交流超高压输电工程，日前在广西境内破土动工。
    这一输电工程全长975公里，其中广西境内约776公里，系中外合资项目，工程总投资约9亿元。
    这一输电工程的建设，对缓解广东电力紧缺状况，加快两广“西江走廊”工业基地的建设，将产生较大影响。
