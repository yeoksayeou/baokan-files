Title: Qin Huangdao Port's Dingdongnan Wharf Passes Acceptance Inspection, Zou County's 1.2 Million Kilowatt Power Plant Completed
Author: Zhou Jing
Date: 1989-12-01
Edition: 1

The state key project—Qin Huangdao Port's Bingding Wharf, of which Dingdongnan Wharf is a part, was completed and passed the acceptance inspection by the Ministry of Communications recently.

Dingdongnan Wharf is a general cargo wharf designed with an annual throughput capacity of 30 million tons, featuring a 1.5 million-ton general cargo berth and a 202-meter-long wharf.

According to Xinhua News Agency in Jinan on November 30, the 1.2 million kilowatt Zou County Power Plant in Shandong Province was completed today.

### 秦皇岛港丁东南码头通过验收  邹县120万千瓦电厂全部建成
谢敬
1989-12-01
第1版(要闻)
专栏：

    秦皇岛港丁东南码头通过验收  邹县120万千瓦电厂全部建成
    据新华社石家庄11月30日电　国家重点工程——秦皇岛港丙丁码头的组成部分丁东南码头于日前竣工并通过交通部验收。
    丁东南码头是设计年通过能力30万吨的杂货码头，有1.5万吨级杂货泊位1个，码头长202米。
    据新华社济南11月30日电　（记者谢敬）今天上午，设计规模为120万千瓦装机容量的山东邹县电厂全部建成。
