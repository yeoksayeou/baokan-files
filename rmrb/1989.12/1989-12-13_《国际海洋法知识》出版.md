Title: Publication of "International Maritime Law Knowledge"
Author: Li Shengrong
Date: 1989-12-13
Edition: 3

	Publication of "International Maritime Law Knowledge"
		According to a report, the book "International Maritime Law Knowledge" has been officially published by the Navy Press.

		Experts believe that it is currently the first comprehensive and systematic toolbook in China that introduces the basic knowledge of international maritime law.
