Title: Influenza in the UK Claims 102 Lives
Author:
Date: 1989-12-21
Edition: 4

The British authorities announced on the 19th that the influenza epidemic that swept through England and Wales during the first week of this month has resulted in 102 deaths.

A spokesperson from the relevant department stated that the majority of those who died from the flu were elderly individuals aged 65 and above.

### 英国流感造成一百零二人死亡

1989-12-21
第4版(国际新闻)
专栏：

    英国流感造成一百零二人死亡
    据英国有关部门十九日公布的数字，本月第一周里席卷英格兰和威尔士的流行性感冒已造成一百零二人死亡。
    有关部门的发言人说，死于流感的人大部分是六十五岁以上的老人。
