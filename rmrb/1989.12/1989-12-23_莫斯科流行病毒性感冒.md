Title: Influenza Outbreak in Moscow
Author:
Date: 1989-12-23
Edition: 4

According to the *Moscow Evening News*, the AH group of influenza viruses has recently begun to spread in the Soviet capital, with daily cases ranging from 370,000 to 400,000.

This influenza outbreak is characterized by a sudden onset, high fever, and headaches. Some patients may also experience nausea and vomiting.

The Moscow city government has decided to mobilize 1,500 interns and resident doctors to assist in outpatient clinics. If necessary, senior medical students will be called upon for reinforcement. Additionally, 300 taxis will be allocated for doctors to use for house calls.

### 莫斯科流行病毒性感冒

1989-12-23
第4版(国际新闻)
专栏：

    莫斯科流行病毒性感冒
    据《莫斯科晚报》报道，AH组病毒性感冒最近已开始在苏联首都流行，日发病人数三点七万至四万人。
    这次流感的特点是，发病急，高烧、头痛，个别病人还会恶心呕吐。
    莫斯科市政府已决定调动一千五百名实习医生和住院大夫帮助门诊部工作，必要时将调动医学院高年级学生增援，并拨出三百辆出租车供医生出诊用。
                                        （据新华社）
