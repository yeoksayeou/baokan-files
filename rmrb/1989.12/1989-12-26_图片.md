Title: Mexican Government Prioritizes Drug Control and Crackdown on Drug Traffickers
Author: Ma Zhancheng
Date: 1989-12-26
Edition: 7

The Mexican government places great importance on drug control and the eradication of drug traffickers. Over the past 10 months, 22 tons of cocaine have been seized from drug dealers.

The image shows police in Mexico City publicly destroying the confiscated cocaine. Photo by New China News Agency reporter Ma Zhancheng.
