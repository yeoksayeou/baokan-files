Title: Bahrain's Foreign Minister Hopes for Continued Development of Bahrain-China Relations
Author:
Date: 1989-12-07
Edition: 4

Bahrain's Foreign Minister Mohammed bin Mubarak expressed on November 18 that he hopes the friendly cooperative relations between China and Bahrain will continue to develop.

Bin Mubarak made the remarks when receiving the letter of introduction from Zhang Weiqiu, the Chinese chargé d'affaires to Bahrain, in Manama. He said that Bahrain and China had friendly exchanges before the establishment of diplomatic relations, and the Bahraini Foreign Ministry is willing to provide all conveniences for the Chinese Embassy to work in Bahrain.

On April 18 of this year, the governments of China and Bahrain announced the formal establishment of diplomatic relations at the ambassadorial level.

### 巴林外交大臣希望巴中关系继续发展

1989-12-07
第4版(国际新闻)
专栏：

    巴林外交大臣希望巴中关系继续发展
    新华社科威特电　麦纳麦消息：巴林外交大臣穆罕默德·本·穆巴拉克11月18日表示，希望中巴友好合作关系能得到进一步发展。
    本·穆巴拉克大臣是在麦纳麦接受中国驻巴林临时代办张维秋递交代办介绍信时作出上述表示的。他说，巴中两国建交前就有着友好往来，巴林外交部愿为中国大使馆在巴林开展工作提供一切方便。
    今年4月18日，中巴两国政府宣布正式建立大使级外交关系。
