Date: 1989-12-16
Edition: 7

	Chinese Products Abroad

	The small Swan brand microcomputer fully automatic washing machine produced by the Wuxi Washing Machine Factory in Jiangsu Province was highly favored by Soviet businessmen at the international exhibition held in Beijing. A contract for the supply of 3,000 washing machines was signed in one go, marking the first export of washing machines from China to the Soviet Union. The small Swan microcomputer washing machine produced by this factory is a fruit of Sino-Japanese technological cooperation and has reached the international standard of the mid-1980s.

	The 20-inch off-road bicycles produced by the Liuzhou Bicycle Factory in Guangxi Province recently passed the quality inspection conducted according to the new standards of the U.S. Customs. The factory seriously listened to the foreign businessmen's evaluation of the products. After the Skai Company in the United States proposed three rounds of improvements totaling six items, the factory modified the 20-inch off-road bicycles for export to the United States according to the standards required by the U.S. Customs within a few days. Recently, American businessmen have signed a contract with the Liuzhou Bicycle Factory to purchase 34,000 bicycles.

	The blue and white porcelain produced by the Luzhou Porcelain Factory in Sichuan Province, such as the blue and white delicate porcelain and the universe coffee cup and saucer, has seen a continuous stream of foreign orders. This year, more than 200,000 sets have been exported to countries such as Finland, Denmark, and the Netherlands.

### 中国商品在国外

1989-12-16
第7版(国际专页)
专栏：

    中国商品在国外
    无锡小天鹅洗衣机首销苏联江苏无锡洗衣机厂生产的小天鹅牌微电脑全自动洗衣机，在北京举办的国际博览会上，受到苏联客商的宠爱，一次就签订了三千台洗衣机的供货合同，这是我国洗衣机首次出口苏联。该厂生产的小天鹅微电脑洗衣机是中日技术合作的结晶，具有国际八十年代中期水平。
    柳州越野自行车出口美国广西柳州市自行车厂生产的二十英寸越野自行车，最近通过了按美国海关新标准进行的品质鉴定。这个厂认真听取外商对产品的评估，在美国斯凯公司提出三次共六条改进意见后，短短几天后就按美国海关的标准要求，对出口美国的二十英寸越野车进行了修改。前不久，美国商人已同柳州市自行车厂签订了要货三万四千辆的合同。
    泸州青花瓷畅销欧洲四川省泸州市瓷厂生产的青花玲珑瓷、宇宙咖啡杯碟，国外订货合同源源不断。今年已向芬兰、丹麦、荷兰等国出口二十多万套。
                  （据新华社）
