Title: Over 100 Elderly People in Shanxi Resist Birthday Celebration Trend
Author:
Date: 1989-12-11
Edition: 2

Over 100 elderly people in Shanxi resist birthday celebration trend

    New China News Agency, Taiyuan, December 9 — Over 100 elderly people in Chengzilu Village, Yongji County, Shanxi Province, have taken the initiative to resist the trend of extravagant birthday celebrations. The village, with a population of nearly 3,000, has seen an increasing scale of birthday celebrations for the elderly in recent years as production has developed and incomes have risen. Many elderly people, witnessing this trend of extravagance and waste, recalled the central government's call to tighten belts for a few years and felt uneasy. They took active steps, connected with each other, and adopted various measures to resist this trend.

### 晋百名老人抵制祝寿风

1989-12-11
第2版(国内新闻)
专栏：四面八方

    晋百名老人抵制祝寿风
    新华社太原12月9日电　山西省永济县城子埒村崔小法、郭继宗、郭玉林等百余名老人自觉抵制祝寿风。拥有近3000人的城子埒村，近几年来随着生产的发展和收入的增加，出现了给老人祝寿规模越来越大。许多老人看到这种铺张浪费现象，想到中央要求过几年紧日子的号召，心里很不安。他们积极行动，互相串连，采取多种办法加以抵制。
