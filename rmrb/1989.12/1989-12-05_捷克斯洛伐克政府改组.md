Title: Czechoslovakian Government Reshuffled
Author:
Date: 1989-12-05
Edition: 4

The Czechoslovakian government underwent a reshuffle on December 3. Out of the original 24 government members, 10 were replaced. Among the newly appointed personnel, 5 are representatives from parties outside the Communist Party of Czechoslovakia and non-partisan individuals.

This government reshuffle was conducted in accordance with an agreement reached between Federal Government Premier Adamec of Czechoslovakia and representatives of the "Civic Forum" on November 28.

### 捷克斯洛伐克政府改组

1989-12-05
第4版(国际新闻)
专栏：

    捷克斯洛伐克政府改组
    新华社布拉格12月3日电捷克斯洛伐克政府3日进行了改组。在原24名政府成员中，有10人被撤换。新任命的人员中有5人为捷共以外政党和无党派人士代表。
    这次政府改组是根据捷联邦政府总理阿达麦茨与捷“公民论坛”的代表于11月28日达成的一项协议进行的。
