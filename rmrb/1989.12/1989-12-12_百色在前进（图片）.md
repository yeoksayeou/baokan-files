Title: Baise is Advancing
Author: He Guozheng
Date: 1989-12-12
Edition: 1

Baise is advancing in the era of reform and opening up. The Baise National Costume Factory has introduced advanced foreign equipment, establishing a production line capable of producing 650,000 pieces of clothing annually. The image shows workers making national costumes.
