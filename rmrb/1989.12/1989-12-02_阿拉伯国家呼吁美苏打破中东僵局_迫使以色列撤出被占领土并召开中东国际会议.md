Title: Arab States Urge U.S. and Soviet Union to Break Middle East Deadlock
Author: Lín Jiǎomíng
Date: 1989-12-02
Page: 4

On the eve of the informal summit between U.S. and Soviet leaders in Malta, Arab states strongly urged the two superpowers to fulfill their obligations to break the Middle East deadlock, force Israel to withdraw from occupied territories, and resolve the Palestinian issue justly based on relevant UN resolutions.

The Arab League foreign ministers, after concluding a two-day special session of the Arab League Council in Tunis, issued the above appeal and handed it over to officials from the two countries in Tunis.

The Arab League foreign ministers pointed out that the two superpowers have the obligation to resolve the Middle East dispute, which seriously threatens world peace, especially in the face of Israel's obstinate attitude that obstructs the Middle East peace process. The principles for solving the Middle East problem can only be Israel's complete withdrawal from the territories it occupied in 1967, the establishment of an independent Palestinian state, and the convening of an international Middle East conference under the auspices of the United Nations, with the participation of the PLO.

### 阿拉伯国家呼吁美苏打破中东僵局  迫使以色列撤出被占领土并召开中东国际会议
林皎明
1989-12-02
第4版(国际新闻)
专栏：

    阿拉伯国家呼吁美苏打破中东僵局
    迫使以色列撤出被占领土并召开中东国际会议
    本报开罗12月1日电　记者林皎明报道：在美苏首脑马耳他非正式会晤前夕，阿拉伯国家强烈呼吁美苏两个超级大国履行义务打破中东僵局，迫使以色列撤出被占领土，在联合国有关决议的基础上公正解决巴勒斯坦问题。
    阿盟外长昨天在突尼斯结束的为期两天的阿盟理事会特别会议后，向布什和戈尔巴乔夫发出上述呼吁，并把呼吁书递交给两国在突尼斯的官员。
    阿盟外长指出，美苏两个超级大国有义务尽快解决严重威胁世界和平的中东争端，特别是在以色列以顽固态度阻挠中东和平进程的情况下。解决中东问题的原则，只能是以色列从1967年占领土地全部撤出，建立巴勒斯坦独立国，并召开由联合国主持的、有巴解参加的中东国际会议。
