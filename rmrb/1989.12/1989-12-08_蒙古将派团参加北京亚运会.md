Title: Mongolia to Send Delegation to Beijing Asian Games
Author: Wáng Jǐngyīng
Date: 1989-12-08
Page: 3

Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, December 6th.

Mongolia to Send Delegation to Beijing Asian Games

Mongolian People's Republic Sports Committee Chairman and Mongolian Olympic Committee Chairman Galzang Dashzeveg expressed today in the Great Hall of the People that Mongolia will send a delegation to participate in the 11th Asian Games to be held in Beijing next year.

When meeting with the visiting Mongolian sports delegation, Vice Chairman of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress, Liao Hansheng, expressed satisfaction with the development of friendly cooperation between China and Mongolia in various fields in recent years. He welcomed Mongolia's decision to participate in the Asian Games and wished Mongolian athletes good results.

Dashzeveg said that they are preparing for the 11th Asian Games and believe that China has the full capability to successfully host this event.

Wu Shaozu, Minister of the State Sports Commission, was present at the meeting.

The Mongolian sports delegation, consisting of three members, arrived in Beijing on December 4th at the invitation of the Chinese State Sports Commission.

Yesterday, Wu Shaozu and Dashzeveg held talks on strengthening sports exchanges between the two countries and the Asian Games.

### 蒙古将派团参加北京亚运会
王景英
1989-12-08
第3版(综合)
专栏：体育

    蒙古将派团参加北京亚运会
    新华社北京12月6日电　（记者王景英）蒙古人民共和国体育运动委员会主席、蒙古奥委会主席嘎勒桑·达希泽伯格今天下午在人民大会堂会见中国人大常委会副委员长廖汉生时表示，蒙古将派出代表团参加明年在北京举行的第十一届亚运会。
    廖汉生副委员长在会见来访的蒙古体育代表团时对中蒙两国近年来在各个领域内友好合作关系的发展表示满意。他对蒙古决定参加亚运会表示欢迎，并预祝蒙古运动员取得好成绩。
    达希泽伯格说，他们正在为参加第十一届亚运会作准备，相信中国完全有条件把这届亚运会办好。
    国家体委主任伍绍祖会见时在座。
    蒙古体育代表团一行三人是应中国国家体委的邀请，于12月4日到达北京的。
    昨天，伍绍祖主任与达希泽伯格主席就加强两国间体育交往和亚运会等问题进行了会谈。
