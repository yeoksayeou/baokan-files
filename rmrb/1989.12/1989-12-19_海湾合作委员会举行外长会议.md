Title: Gulf Cooperation Council Holds Foreign Ministers' Meeting
Author:
Date: 1989-12-19
Page: 4

The foreign ministers of the six member states of the Gulf Cooperation Council held a meeting in Muscat on the 17th, setting the agenda for the council's 10th summit, scheduled for the 18th.

Abdullah, Oman's Minister of State for Foreign Affairs, who chaired the meeting, told the press afterward that the primary goal of the 10th summit of the Gulf Cooperation Council is to promote coordinated, peaceful, and friendly neighborly relations among the countries in this region.

He said that the Gulf region is at a critical stage in achieving peace and stability.

According to reports, the summit will address issues including the Palestinian question, the situation in Lebanon, and the Iran-Iraq conflict.

### 海湾合作委员会举行外长会议

1989-12-19
第4版(国际新闻)
专栏：

    海湾合作委员会举行外长会议
    海湾合作委员会六国外长十七日在马斯喀特举行会议，确定十八日召开的该委员会第十届首脑会议的议事日程。
    主持这次会议的阿曼外交国务大臣阿卜杜拉会后对新闻界说，海湾合作委员会第十届首脑会议的主要目的是促使这一地区各国之间建立协调、和平和友好的睦邻关系。
    他说，海湾地区正处于实现和平与稳定的关键阶段。
    据报道，这次首脑会议的内容包括巴勒斯坦问题、黎巴嫩局势和两伊争端问题。
