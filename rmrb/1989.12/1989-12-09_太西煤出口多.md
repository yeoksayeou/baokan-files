Title: Export of Taixi Coal Surpasses Target
Author: Wang Cunli
Date: 1989-12-09
Edition: 2

By the end of November, the Ningxia branch of China Coal Import and Export Company had exported 315,400 tons of Taixi coal, completing the annual export earnings plan 31 days ahead of schedule and exceeding the target by 5%, setting a new historical high.

The export earnings from Taixi coal account for more than one-third of Ningxia's total export earnings.

### 太西煤出口多
王存理
1989-12-09
第2版(国内新闻)
专栏：四面八方

    太西煤出口多
    新华社银川12月8日电　（记者王存理）截至11月底，中国煤炭进出口公司宁夏分公司已出口太西煤31.54万吨，提前31天、超额5％完成太西煤的全年出口创汇计划，创历史最高水平。
    太西煤出口创汇占宁夏出口创汇总额1／3以上。
