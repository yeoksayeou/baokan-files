Title: U.S. Population Nears 250 Million
Author:
Date: 1989-12-11
Edition: 4

The U.S. Department of Commerce's Bureau of the Census reported today that as of October 1, 1989, the total population of the United States was approximately 249,511,000 people (including overseas military personnel).

The census bureau's report states that this figure indicates that compared to the same period last year, the U.S. population has increased by 1%, or 2,472,000 people.

### 美总人口近二点五亿

1989-12-11
第4版(国际新闻)
专栏：

    美总人口近二点五亿
    新华社华盛顿12月6日电　美国商务部人口普查局今天报告说，截至1989年10月1日，美国人口总数约计249511000人（海外军事人员包括在内）。
    人口普查局的报告说，这一数字表明，与去年同期相比，美国的人口增加1％，即增加2472000人。
