Title: Hab Zongshun and Chen Muhua Emphasize Further Development of the Economic Special Zone During Inspection of Shenzhen
Author: Jiang Shunchang
Date: 1989-12-11
Edition: 2

New China News Agency, Shenzhen, December 9th - (Reporter Jiang Shunchang) Today, the Vice Chairpersons of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress, Hab Zongshun and Chen Muhua, stated here that further developing the Shenzhen Economic Special Zone is of great practical significance and far-reaching impact in building socialism with Chinese characteristics, fully demonstrating the superiority of the socialist system, and consolidating and perfecting the socialist system.

Vice Chairpersons Hab Zongshun and Chen Muhua recently came to the Shenzhen Special Zone to inspect the work. This afternoon, relevant leaders of the Shenzhen Municipal Party Committee and Municipal Government reported to the two Vice Chairpersons on the situation of Shenzhen Special Zone's rectification and reform this year, as well as economic development. The two Vice Chairpersons expressed satisfaction with Shenzhen's work this year after hearing the report.

They pointed out that our economic special zones are socialist economic special zones. All work in the special zones is first and foremost carried out under socialist conditions. This is a very important principle. Reform should be conducive to the full play of the superiority of the socialist system and help to further consolidate and perfect the socialist system.

### 习仲勋陈慕华视察深圳时强调  进一步办好经济特区  发挥社会主义优越性
蒋顺章
1989-12-11
第2版(国内新闻)
专栏：

    习仲勋陈慕华视察深圳时强调
    进一步办好经济特区
    发挥社会主义优越性
    新华社深圳十二月九日电　（记者蒋顺章）全国人大常委会副委员长习仲勋、陈慕华今天在这里说，进一步办好深圳经济特区，对于建设有中国特色的社会主义，进一步发挥社会主义制度的优越性，坚持和巩固社会主义制度，具有非常重要的现实意义和深远影响。
    习仲勋、陈慕华副委员长是最近先后来到深圳特区视察工作的。今天下午，深圳市委和市政府的有关领导同志向两位副委员长汇报了深圳特区今年治理整顿、深化改革和经济发展等情况。两位副委员长听后对深圳今年的工作表示满意。
    他们指出，我们的经济特区是社会主义的经济特区，特区的一切工作首先是在社会主义条件下进行的，这是个非常重要的原则。改革应当有利于社会主义制度优越性的充分发挥，有助于社会主义制度进一步巩固和完善。
