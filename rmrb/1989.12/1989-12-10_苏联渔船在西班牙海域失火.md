Title: Soviet Fishing Vessel Catches Fire in Spanish Waters
Author:
Date: 1989-12-10
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency

A Soviet fishing vessel caught fire on December 8th near Estaca de Bares in northwestern Spain, resulting in one death and two missing persons. Spanish naval officials stated that the cause of the fire on the Soviet vessel, which had 82 crew members, is unknown.

### 苏联渔船在西班牙海域失火

1989-12-10
第4版(国际新闻)
专栏：

    苏联渔船在西班牙海域失火
    一艘苏联渔船八日在西班牙西北部埃斯塔卡德巴雷斯附近海域发生火灾，造成一人死亡、二人失踪。西班牙海军人士说，这艘载有八十二名船员的苏联渔船失火原因不明。　（据新华社）
