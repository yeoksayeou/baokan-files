Title: Czechoslovak Federal Assembly Elects New Chairman
Author:
Date: 1989-12-14
Page: 4

At the joint session of the two chambers of the Czechoslovak Federal Assembly held on December 12, non-partisan Stanislav Kucera was elected as the chairman of the Federal Assembly.

Kucera is 54 years old. He was elected as a member of the Federal Assembly in 1976 and was appointed as the chairman of the People's Control Committee of the Czech Republic on December 5 this year.

The two chambers of the Federal Assembly also elected six vice-chairmen, among whom the first vice-chairman and another person are members of the Communist Party of Czechoslovakia, while the others are from the People's Party, the Social Democratic Party, the Democratic Party, and the Liberal Party.

### 捷联邦议会选出新主席

1989-12-14
第4版(国际新闻)
专栏：

    捷联邦议会选出新主席
    在十二日举行的捷克斯洛伐克联邦议会两院联席会议上，无党派人士斯塔尼斯拉夫·库克拉尔出任联邦议会主席。
    库克拉尔现年五十四岁，一九七六年当选为联邦议会议员，今年十二月五日被任命为捷克共和国人民检查委员会主席。
    联邦议会两院还选出六名副主席，其中第一副主席和另一人是捷共党员，其他分别是人民党、社会党、民主党和自由党人士。
