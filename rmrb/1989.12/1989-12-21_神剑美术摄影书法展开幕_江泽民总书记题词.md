Title: Opening of the Divine Sword Art, Photography, and Calligraphy Exhibition
Author: Su Kuangpu
Date: 1989-12-21
Edition: 3

The State Council's Commission of Science, Technology and Industry for National Defense and the Divine Sword Literary and Art Association jointly organized the Third Divine Sword Art, Photography, and Calligraphy Exhibition, which opened on December 20 at the Military Museum of the Chinese People's Revolution in Beijing. Jiang Zemin, General Secretary of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of China, inscribed the following words for the exhibition: "The Divine Sword soars to the sky, igniting the spirit of the nine provinces. A billion people unite in their resolve, and the land of Yu becomes even more beautiful."

The exhibition showcases the artistic achievements of the members of the Divine Sword Literary and Art Association, reflecting their deep love for the motherland and their dedication to the cause of national defense. The works on display cover a wide range of themes, including landscapes, portraits, and historical events, and demonstrate the artists' mastery of various techniques and styles. The exhibition is open to the public and will run until January 10, 1990.

### 神剑美术摄影书法展开幕  江泽民总书记题词
苏扩普
1989-12-21
第3版(综合)
专栏：

    神剑美术摄影书法展开幕
    江泽民总书记题词
    本报讯　国防科工委神剑文学艺术学会主办的神剑第三届美术摄影书法展览12月20日在北京军事博物馆开幕。中共中央总书记江泽民为展览题词：“神剑冲天耀，九州生气燃。亿民齐发愤，禹域定增妍。”　（苏扩普）
