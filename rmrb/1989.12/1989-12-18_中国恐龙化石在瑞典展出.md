Title: Chinese Dinosaur Fossils on Display in Sweden
Author: Gù Yàomíng
Date: 1989-12-18
Page: 7

Chinese dinosaur fossils on display in Sweden have attracted a large number of Swedish spectators. The exhibition features a total of seven dinosaur skeletons weighing 30 tons, as well as dinosaur eggs and footprints. The accompanying image shows Swedish students listening to their teacher's explanation at the exhibition. By our reporter, Gù Yàomíng.

### 中国恐龙化石在瑞典展出
顾耀铭
1989-12-18
第7版(国际专页)
专栏：

    中国恐龙化石在瑞典展出
    中国恐龙化石在瑞典展出以来，吸引了大批瑞典观众。展览会上共展出了总重量达30吨的7具中国恐龙骨架，以及恐龙蛋和恐龙脚印等化石。下图为瑞典小学生正在展览会上听老师讲解。　本报记者　顾耀铭（附图片）
