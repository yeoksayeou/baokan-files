Title: China Provides Material Aid to Afghan Refugees
Author:
Date: 1989-12-28
Edition: 4

China has provided a batch of material aid worth 84.5 million US dollars to Afghan refugees residing in Pakistan.

The aid includes blankets, footwear, and canned food.

Today, China's Ambassador to Pakistan, Tian Ding, handed over this batch of aid to Pakistan's Chief Commissioner for Afghan Refugee Affairs, Rahman Khan.

### 我国向阿富汗难民提供物资援助

1989-12-28
第4版(国际新闻)
专栏：

    我国向阿富汗难民提供物资援助
    新华社伊斯兰堡12月18日电　中国向居住在巴基斯坦的阿富汗难民提供了一批价值84.5万美元的物资援助。
    援助物资包括毛毯、鞋类和罐头食品。
    中国驻巴基斯坦大使田丁今天将这批物资移交给巴基斯坦负责阿富汗难民事务的首席专员拉赫曼·汗。
