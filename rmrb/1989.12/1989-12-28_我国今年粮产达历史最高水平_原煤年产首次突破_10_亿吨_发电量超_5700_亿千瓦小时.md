Title: China's Grain Production Reaches Historic High, Coal Output Exceeds 1 Billion Tons, Electricity Generation Surpasses 570 Billion kWh
Author: Liú Xiāoyáng
Date: 1989-12-28
Edition: 1

    China's grain production reaches historic high
    Coal output exceeds 1 billion tons, electricity generation surpasses 570 billion kWh

    Beijing, December 27 (Xinhua) - The State Statistical Bureau revealed: According to preliminary data from sampling and estimation in 28 provinces, municipalities, and autonomous regions, and comprehensive estimates from Hainan and Tibet, despite severe natural disasters in parts of northern China this autumn, the national grain output still exceeded 405 billion kilograms (405 billion jin), an increase of over 10 billion kilograms (100 billion jin) compared to last year, a growth of 3.4%, reaching the highest level since 1984.

    It is understood that this year's planting area is expected to reach 16.86 billion mu, an increase of 29.45 million mu compared to last year, a growth of 1.8%. Most regions in the country have increased production, while some regions have seen significant decreases.

    Beijing, December 27 (Xinhua) - Reporter Liu Xiaoyang learned from the Ministry of Energy: Today, China's coal and electricity production both exceeded the "Seventh Five-Year Plan" targets one year ahead of schedule. The annual coal output surpassed the 1 billion-ton mark, and the annual electricity generation exceeded 570 billion kWh, ranking among the world's top.

    The Ministry of Energy official said: Before the Third Plenary Session of the Eleventh Central Committee, the annual coal output was only 6.18 billion tons, and the annual electricity generation was 256.6 billion kWh. In the past 11 years, coal output has increased by 68%, and electricity generation has more than doubled.

### 我国今年粮产达历史最高水平  原煤年产首次突破10亿吨  发电量超5700亿千瓦小时
刘燮阳
1989-12-28
第1版(要闻)
专栏：

    我国今年粮产达历史最高水平
    原煤年产首次突破10亿吨  发电量超5700亿千瓦小时
    本报北京12月27日讯　国家统计局透露：据全国28个省、区、市抽样实测推算初步数据和海南、西藏两省、区的全面预计表明，尽管今年秋季北方部分地区遭受严重的自然灾害，全国粮食总产量仍超过40500万吨（4050亿公斤），比去年增加1000万吨（100亿公斤）以上，增长3.4％，达到了历史最高年份1984年的水平。
    据悉，今年播种面积预计达16.86亿亩，比去年增2945万亩，增长1.8％。全国大部分地区增产，局部地区减产较多。
    本报北京12月27日讯　记者刘燮阳从能源部获悉：今天，我国煤炭和电力双双提前一年超额完成“七五”计划，原煤年产量突破10亿吨大关，年发电量超过5700亿千瓦小时，居世界前列。
    能源部负责人说：党的十一届三中全会以前，煤炭年产量只有6.18亿吨，年发电量2566亿千瓦小时，11年来，煤炭年产量增加68％，电翻了一番还多。
