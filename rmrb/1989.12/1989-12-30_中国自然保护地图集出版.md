Title: Publication of the "China Natural Reserve Map Collection"
Author: Xiao Tihuan
Date: 1989-12-30
Edition: 3

The "China Natural Reserve Map Collection," compiled under the auspices of the State Environmental Protection Administration and edited by the Changchun Institute of Geography, Chinese Academy of Sciences, was approved in Beijing on December 25 and published by the Science Press.

The "China Natural Reserve Map Collection" comprehensively reflects the superior natural environment and natural resources of China's vast territory, as well as the achievements and existing problems in the natural conservation sector since the founding of the People's Republic. In terms of map compilation, it has reached an advanced international level.

### 中国自然保护地图集出版
萧体焕
1989-12-30
第3版(综合)
专栏：

    中国自然保护地图集出版
    本报讯　记者萧体焕报道：由国家环保局主持、中国科学院长春地理研究所主编的大型地图集《中国自然保护地图集》，十二月二十五日在北京通过鉴定，并由科学出版社出版。
    《中国自然保护地图集》全面地反映了我国辽阔疆域优越的自然环境和自然资源，以及建国以来自然保护事业所取得的成就和存在的问题，在地图编制方面达到国际先进水平。
