Title: Most Gold Medals Go to China in Asian Youth Athletics
Author:
Date: 1989-12-18
Edition: 3

The fifth Asian Youth Athletics Championships concluded today in Hong Kong, wrapping up three days of intense competition. The final 16 gold medals were awarded, bringing the total number of gold medals to 35.

Chinese athletes performed exceptionally well, securing 26 out of the 35 gold medals. The Tokyo team from Japan and the Taoyuan team from Chinese Taipei each won 3 gold medals.

### 亚洲城市青年田径赛  金牌多数归中国

1989-12-18
第3版(综合)
专栏：体育

    亚洲城市青年田径赛
    金牌多数归中国
    据新华社香港12月17日电  第五届亚洲城市青年田径赛今天在这里决出最后16枚金牌，宣告历时3天的比赛结束。
    中国选手表现突出，夺得本届比赛35块金牌中的26块。日本东京队和中国台北桃园队各获3枚金牌。
