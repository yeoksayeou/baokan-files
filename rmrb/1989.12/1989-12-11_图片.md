Title: Shanghai Armed Police Force Continues Blood Donation
Author: Wáng Yùliáng
Date: 1989-12-11
Edition: 5

The Shanghai Armed Police Force has organized nearly 700 officers and soldiers to donate blood voluntarily for four consecutive years, totaling 130,000 milliliters. This makes them the unit with the highest number of blood donors in Shanghai.

The image shows armed police officers donating blood at the Shanghai Blood Center.
