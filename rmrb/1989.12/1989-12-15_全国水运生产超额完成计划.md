Title: Nationwide Water Transport Exceeds Plan
Author: Lǐ Méi
Date: 1989-12-15
Edition: 1

According to a report from Xinhua News Agency in Beijing on December 14, the water transport sector has seen a favorable production situation this year. By the end of November, the cargo volume handled by ships and the throughput of major river and sea ports had already exceeded the annual production plan. The transportation of key commodities such as coal and crude oil has also been completed ahead of schedule.

According to figures provided by the Ministry of Communications, by the end of November, the national cargo volume handled by ships reached 22,707.4 million tons, exceeding the annual plan by 0.9 percent. The throughput of river and sea ports reached 56,493.3 million tons, exceeding the annual plan by 1.2 percent and increasing by 6.5 percent compared to the same period last year.

### 全国水运生产超额完成计划
李玫
1989-12-15
第1版(要闻)
专栏：

    全国水运生产超额完成计划
    据新华社北京十二月十四日电　（记者李玫）记者从交通部获悉，今年水运生产形势喜人。截至十一月底，船舶货运量和江海主要港口吞吐量已提前完成全年生产计划。煤炭、原油等重点物资的运输也超额完成任务。
    据交通部提供的数字，到十一月底，全国船舶货运量完成二万二千七百零七点四万吨，超额年计划的百分之零点九。江海港口吞吐量完成五万六千四百九十三点三万吨，超过年计划的百分之一点二，比去年同期增长百分之六点五。
