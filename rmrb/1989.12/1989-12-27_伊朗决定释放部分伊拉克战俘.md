Title: Iran Decides to Release Some Iraqi Prisoners of War
Author:
Date: 1989-12-27
Page: 4

Iran has decided to release a portion of Iraqi prisoners of war. In response to a request from United Nations Secretary-General Javier Pérez de Cuéllar, Iran announced on the 25th that it will unilaterally release 50 injured and disabled Iraqi prisoners of war.

The report states that Iran's Ministry of Foreign Affairs has notified the International Committee of the Red Cross of this decision. The released Iraqi prisoners of war will be arranged to return home by the committee. The specific time and location for the release of this batch of Iraqi prisoners of war are not yet known.

### 伊朗决定释放部分伊拉克战俘

1989-12-27
第4版(国际新闻)
专栏：

    伊朗决定释放部分伊拉克战俘
    应联合国秘书长佩雷斯·德奎利亚尔的请求，伊朗二十五日宣布将单方面释放五十名伊拉克伤残战俘。
    报道说，伊朗外交部已将这一决定通知红十字国际委员会。被释放的伊拉克战俘将由该委员会安排回国。目前尚不清楚释放这批伊拉克战俘的具体时间和地点。
