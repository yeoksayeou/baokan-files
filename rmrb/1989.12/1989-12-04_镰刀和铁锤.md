Title: The Sickle and the Hammer
Author: He Haitao
Date: 1989-12-04
Page: 8

I love the sickle and the hammer
—The most simple beauty
The sickle harvests the ripened crops
The hammer embodies a firm will
When they are raised together
They radiate the light of justice
Making our passions noble
In the confusion of the clouds, we awaken
A steadfast belief
Fills and permeates our hearts
On the red flag
The golden sickle and hammer

### 镰刀和铁锤
贺海涛
1989-12-04
第8版(副刊)
专栏：

    镰刀和铁锤
    贺海涛
    我喜欢镰刀和铁锤
    ——最朴素的美
    镰刀收获成熟
    铁锤坚实意志
    当它们举在一起
    放射正义的光辉
    使我们的激情趋于崇高
    在迷乱的云中清醒
    一种坚定的信念
    充实并渗透我们的胸怀
    红通通的布上
    金黄的镰刀和铁锤
