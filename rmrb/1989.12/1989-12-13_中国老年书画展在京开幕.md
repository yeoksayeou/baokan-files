Title: Chinese Elderly Calligraphy and Painting Exhibition Opens in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1989-12-13
Page: 3

The Chinese Elderly Calligraphy and Painting Exhibition officially opened on December 9th at the Beijing Nationalities Culture Palace. Vice Chairman of the National Committee of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference, Ma Wenrui, performed the ribbon-cutting ceremony for the opening.

It is reported that there are currently over forty elderly calligraphy and painting organizations nationwide, with more than 4,000 members. This exhibition, co-hosted by the National Committee on Aging and the China Elderly Calligraphy and Painting Research Association, features 582 calligraphy works, 606 paintings, and 15 seal engravings. Among the authors are Liu Ningyi, Shi Jinqian, Li Zhen, Wei Chuantong, and other elderly warriors, cadres, professors, and experts who have dedicated decades to the founding of New China.

### 中国老年书画展在京开幕

1989-12-13
第3版(综合)
专栏：

    中国老年书画展在京开幕
    本报讯　中国老年书画展本月九日在北京民族文化宫正式展出，全国政协副主席马文瑞为开幕式剪彩。
    据悉，目前全国老年书画组织有四十多个，会员四千余名。这次由中国老龄问题全国委员会与中国老龄书画研究会共同举办的书画展，其中有书法五百八十二幅，绘画六百零六幅，篆刻十五幅。作者中有刘宁一、史进前、李真、魏传统等为创建新中国奋斗几十年的老战士、老干部、老教授和专家学者。
