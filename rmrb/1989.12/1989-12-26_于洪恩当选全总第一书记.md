Title: Yu Hong'en Elected as First Secretary of the All-China Federation of Trade Unions
Author: Xú Kèhóng  Huáng Xiǎonán
Date: 1989-12-26
Page: 2

Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, December 25th.

The eleventh session of the second executive committee of the All-China Federation of Trade Unions announced today that Yu Hong'en, former general manager of the China National Coal Industry Bureau, has been elected as the vice-chairman and first secretary of the secretariat of the All-China Federation of Trade Unions.

The 62-year-old Yu Hong'en has worked as a coal miner and has also served as the minister of the State Coal Industry Bureau. He described his life's work as being dedicated to coal mining. He stated that taking on the role of the principal leader of the All-China Federation of Trade Unions is a new challenge for him.

During this executive committee session, Yang Xingfu, former vice-chairman of the Standing Committee of the Shandong Provincial People's Congress, was also elected as vice-chairman of the All-China Federation of Trade Unions. Xue Zhaofeng, former chairman of the Shaanxi Provincial Federation of Trade Unions, and Zhang Guoxiang, who served as the mayor of Chongwen District, Beijing, were elected as secretaries of the secretariat of the All-China Federation of Trade Unions.

The aforementioned four individuals were also elected as members of the eleventh executive committee of the All-China Federation of Trade Unions.

Additionally, the executive committee and the presidium of the All-China Federation of Trade Unions made the decision to relieve Zhu Houze of his positions as vice-chairman and first secretary of the secretariat of the All-China Federation of Trade Unions.

### 于洪恩当选全总第一书记
徐克洪  黄晓南
1989-12-26
第2版(国内新闻)
专栏：

    于洪恩当选全总第一书记
    新华社北京12月25日电　（记者徐克洪、黄晓南）中华全国总工会十一届二次执委会今天宣布，增选原中国统配煤矿总公司总经理于洪恩为全总副主席和全总书记处第一书记。
    今年62岁的于洪恩曾做过煤矿采掘工，并担任过国家煤炭部部长的职务，用他自己的话说，一生从事挖煤工作。他表示，担任全国总工会的主要领导职务对他来说是一个新的考验。
    在这次执委会上，还增选了原山东省人大常委会副主任杨兴富为全总副主席；原陕西省总工会主席薛昭鋆、担任过北京市崇文区区长的张国祥为全总书记处书记。
    上述4人并被增选为全总第十一届执行委员会委员。
    此外，全总执委会和全总主席团还分别作出决定，免去朱厚泽全总副主席及全总书记处第一书记的职务。
