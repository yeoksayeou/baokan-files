Title: Chinese Ambassadors Present Credentials in Hungary and Ireland
Author:
Date: 1989-12-02
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Budapest, November 29th.

China's newly appointed Ambassador to Hungary, Dai Bingguo, presented his credentials to Hungary's Acting President, Szűrös Mátyás, on November 29th in Budapest.

During the credential presentation, both sides engaged in friendly and cordial discussions. Szűrös expressed his hope for the continued development of friendly relations between Hungary and China.

Xinhua News Agency, London, November 30th.

China's newly appointed Ambassador to Ireland, Han Li (female), presented her credentials to Irish President Patrick Hillery in Dublin on November 30th, conveying greetings from Chairman Yang Shangkun. After the credential presentation, both sides engaged in friendly and cordial discussions.

### 我驻匈牙利和爱尔兰大使递交国书

1989-12-02
第4版(国际新闻)
专栏：

    我驻匈牙利和爱尔兰大使递交国书
    新华社布达佩斯11月29日电　中国新任驻匈牙利特命全权大使戴秉国29日在这里向匈牙利共和国临时总统絮勒什·马加什递交了国书。
    递交国书时，双方进行了亲切友好的谈话。絮勒什表示希望继续发展匈中两国之间的友好关系。
    新华社伦敦11月30日电　中国新任驻爱尔兰大使韩琍琍（女）今天在都柏林向爱尔兰总统帕特里克·希勒里递交了国书，并向他转达了杨尚昆主席的问候。递交国书后，双方进行了亲切友好的交谈。
