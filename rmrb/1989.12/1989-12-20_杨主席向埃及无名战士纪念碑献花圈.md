Title: Yang Shangkun Lays Wreath at Egypt's Tomb of the Unknown Soldier
Author: Cheng Yuansheng, Lin Jiaoming
Date: 1989-12-20
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Cairo, December 18th.

President Yang Shangkun of the People's Republic of China laid wreaths at the Tomb of the Unknown Soldier and the tombs of former Egyptian presidents Gamal Abdel Nasser and Anwar Sadat in Cairo today.

Accompanied by the head of the Egyptian reception committee and Minister of Industry, Muhammad Wahib, President Yang and his delegation traveled by car from the Kuba Palace, where they are staying, to the Tomb of the Unknown Soldier in Nasr City.

Amidst military music, President Yang laid a wreath at the 30-meter-high pyramid-shaped monument inscribed with the Arabic date "October 6," commemorating Egypt's fallen heroes in various Middle East wars.

The Tomb of the Unknown Soldier was constructed in 1975 to honor Egypt's martyrs in past conflicts.

President Yang also laid a wreath at the adjacent tomb of former President Anwar Sadat.

Subsequently, President Yang traveled by car to the tomb of former President Gamal Abdel Nasser, where he laid a wreath and signed the guest book.

Accompanying President Yang in laying the wreaths was China's Ambassador to Egypt, Zhan Shiliang.

### 杨主席向埃及无名战士纪念碑献花圈
成元生  林皎明
1989-12-20
第4版(国际新闻)
专栏：

    杨主席向埃及无名战士纪念碑献花圈
    本报开罗12月18日电　记者成元生、林皎明报道：中华人民共和国主席杨尚昆今天下午向埃及无名战士纪念碑和已故埃及总统纳赛尔、萨达特的陵墓献花圈。
    杨主席一行在埃及陪同团长、工业部长穆罕默德·瓦哈卜的陪同下，乘车从下榻的库巴宫前往纳赛尔城无名战士纪念碑。
    在军乐声中，杨主席向刻有“10月6日”阿拉伯文字样、高达30米的金字塔状纪念碑敬献花圈。
    无名战士纪念碑是1975年为纪念埃及在历次中东战争中牺牲的烈士而修建的。
    杨主席还向在同址的已故总统安瓦尔·萨达特墓献了花圈。
    随后，杨主席又乘车前往已故总统纳赛尔墓献花圈，并在留言簿上签名。
    陪同杨尚昆主席献花圈的还有中国驻埃及大使詹世亮。
