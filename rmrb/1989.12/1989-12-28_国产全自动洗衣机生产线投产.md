Title: Domestic Fully Automatic Washing Machine Production Line Commences Operation
Author: Sun Jian
Date: 1989-12-28
Edition: 2

The first fully integrated domestic production line for fully automatic washing machines in China, which combines large-scale plastic mold manufacturing with component processing, assembly, and testing, has recently passed provincial-level acceptance and officially commenced operation in Nanjing. The annual production capacity is 200,000 units.

### 国产全自动洗衣机生产线投产
孙健
1989-12-28
第2版(国内新闻)
专栏：四面八方

    国产全自动洗衣机生产线投产
    本报讯　我国第一条集大型塑料模具制造和零部件加工、总装、检测于一体的国产化全自动洗衣机生产线，日前在南京通过省级验收，并正式投入使用，年产能力为20万台。（孙健）
