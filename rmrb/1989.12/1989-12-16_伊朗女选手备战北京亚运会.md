Title: Iranian Female Athletes Preparing for the Beijing Asian Games
Author:
Date: 1989-12-16
Edition: 3

Iranian women's shooting team will hold a warm-up match with the Bangladeshi women's team, which will visit Iran next March, in preparation for the Beijing Asian Games. It is reported that the Iranian women's shooting team will also visit Bangladesh in April next year.

According to Iranian sources, Iran will invite foreign female coaches to train women in swimming and gymnastics. Currently, Iranian female athletes are busy training in ping-pong, volleyball, basketball, badminton, swimming, gymnastics, track and field, and shooting. However, it has not been officially confirmed whether female athletes will participate in all the above-mentioned events in the Asian Games.

### 伊朗女选手备战北京亚运会

1989-12-16
第3版(综合)
专栏：体育

    伊朗女选手备战北京亚运会
    新华社德黑兰12月14日电　伊朗女子射击队将同明年3月到这里来访的孟加拉国女队进行备战北京亚运会的热身赛。据悉，伊朗女子射击队也将在明年4月回访孟加拉国。
    据伊朗有关方面透露，伊朗将邀请国外女教练来指导女子游泳和体操。目前，伊朗女选手正忙于乒乓球、排球、篮球、羽毛球、游泳、体操、田径、射击等项目的练兵，但官方没有确认女选手是否要参加上述所有项目的亚运会比赛。
