Title: Albania Celebrates 45th Anniversary of Liberation
Author:
Date: 1989-12-04
Page: 4

November 29th marked the 45th anniversary of Albania's liberation and the victory of the people's revolution. At 10 AM, a grand military parade and mass celebration march were held on "National Martyrs Boulevard." Party and government leaders such as Aliya and Çarçani reviewed the troops and watched the parade.

On November 28th, a gathering to celebrate the 45th anniversary of liberation and the victory of the people's revolution was held at the Tirana Conference Hall. Party Central Committee Politburo member and Chairman of the Council of Ministers Çarçani delivered a speech at the event.

### 阿庆祝解放四十五周年

1989-12-04
第4版(国际新闻)
专栏：

    阿庆祝解放四十五周年
    十一月二十九日是阿尔巴尼亚解放和人民革命胜利四十五周年纪念日。上午十时，在“民族烈士大街”举行了隆重的阅兵和群众庆祝游行。阿利雅、查尔查尼等党政领导人检阅了部队并观看了游行。
    二十八日，在地拉那大会堂还举行了庆祝解放和人民革命胜利四十五周年集会，党中央政治局委员、部长会议主席查尔查尼在会上讲了话。
