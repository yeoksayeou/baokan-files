Title: Philippine President Appoints New Head of the National Bureau of Investigation
Author:
Date: 1989-12-25
Page: 4

Philippine President Corazon Aquino on the 22nd appointed retired Major General Alfredo Lim as the head of the National Bureau of Investigation, replacing the former head Jose Capio. Corazon Aquino also appointed him as a presidential assistant in charge of special projects. Lim is 60 years old and was formerly the deputy chief of the Philippine National Police. Corazon Aquino also appointed Andrea Domingo as the acting commissioner of the Bureau of Immigration.

### 菲总统任命新的调查局长

1989-12-25
第4版(国际新闻)
专栏：

    菲总统任命新的调查局长
    菲律宾总统科·阿基诺二十二日任命退休少将阿尔弗雷多·利姆为全国调查局局长，以代替原局长何塞·卡皮奥。科·阿基诺还任命他为负责特别项目的总统助理。利姆现年六十岁，他原是菲律宾国家警察副总监。科·阿基诺还任命了安德烈娅·多明戈为菲律宾移民局代理局长。
