Title: Thai Navy Commander Meets Chinese Navy Commander Zhang Lianzhong
Author: Huang Yong
Date: 1989-12-17
Edition: 4

Thai Navy Commander Meets Chinese Navy Commander Zhang Lianzhong

Xinhua News Agency, Bangkok, December 14 (Reporter Huang Yong) - Thai Navy Commander-in-Chief Bapha Khrisanachinda today met with Zhang Lianzhong, Commander-in-Chief of the Chinese People's Liberation Army Navy, who is visiting Thailand. They exchanged views on strengthening friendly relations between the two navies.

Zhang Lianzhong said during the meeting that the purpose of his visit to Thailand is twofold: to strengthen friendly relations between the two navies and to learn from the Thai Navy. Bapha said, "China's economy is developing rapidly. The development of the Chinese Navy is also fast. The Chinese Navy has its own advantages."

Today, the Thai Ministry of Defense also held a ceremony to award Zhang Lianzhong the "First Class Crown Medal" in recognition of his contributions to world peace and the development of the Chinese Navy.

Zhang Lianzhong arrived here yesterday to begin his official visit to Thailand. After completing his visit to Thailand, he will proceed to Bangladesh and Pakistan for friendly visits.

### 泰海军司令会见我海军司令张连忠
黄勇
1989-12-17
第4版(国际新闻)
专栏：

    泰海军司令会见我海军司令张连忠
    新华社曼谷12月14日电　（记者黄勇）泰国海军总司令巴帕·格里沙纳占今天会见了正在这里访问的中国人民解放军海军司令员张连忠，就加强两国海军间的友好关系问题交换了意见。
    张连忠在会见时说，他来泰国访问的目的一是加强两国海军的友好关系，二是向泰国海军学习。巴帕说，中国经济发展很快。中国海军发展也快。中国海军有自己的优势。
    今天泰国国防部还为张连忠司令员举行了“一级王冠勋章”授勋仪式，表彰他为世界和平和中国海军的发展做出了贡献。
    张连忠是于昨天抵达这里对泰国进行正式访问的。结束访泰后，他将接着对孟加拉国和巴基斯坦进行友好访问。
