    Foreign Affairs Brief
    State Councilor Chen Junsheng met with Professor Emeritus Saburo Tamura of Tokyo University on December 27 at the Great Hall of the People. Chen Junsheng briefed Saburo Tamura on China's policies and arrangements for developing the Loess Plateau and the low-yielding farmlands in the Huang-Huai-Hai region. (Xinhua)

### 外事简讯

1989-12-28
第2版(国内新闻)
专栏：

    外事简讯
    国务委员陈俊生12月27日在人民大会堂会见了日本东京大学名誉教授田村三郎。陈俊生向田村三郎介绍了中国开发黄土高原和黄淮海农业低产田的方针及部署。（据新华社）
