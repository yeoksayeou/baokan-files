Title: China and Egypt Sign Cultural Agreement Implementation Plan
Author: Cheng Yuansheng, Lin Jiaoming
Date: 1989-12-21
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Cairo, December 20th.

China and Egypt signed the implementation plan for the cultural agreement between the two countries. Chinese Vice Minister of Foreign Affairs Qi Huaiyuan and Egyptian Deputy Foreign Minister Salah signed the agreement on behalf of their respective governments this afternoon.

The signing ceremony was held at the Egyptian Ministry of Foreign Affairs.

### 中埃签署文化协定执行计划
成元生  林皎明
1989-12-21
第4版(国际新闻)
专栏：

    中埃签署文化协定执行计划
    本报开罗12月20日电　记者成元生、林皎明报道：中国外交部副部长齐怀远和埃及外交部长助理沙拉勒今天下午代表两国政府签署中埃文化协定执行计划。
    签字仪式在埃及外交部举行。
