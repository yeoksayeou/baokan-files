Title: List of Dismissals by the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress
Author:
Date: 1989-12-27
Edition: 3

The Standing Committee of the Seventh National People's Congress dismissed the following individuals from their positions as judges of the Supreme People's Court at its 11th meeting on December 26, 1989:

1. Fu Kan
2. Shi Tongwen
3. Li Fengchun
4. Li Seng
5. Sun Jiaping
6. Zhang Guodong
7. Cao Xibo

### 全国人大常委会免职名单

1989-12-27
第3版(综合)
专栏：

    全国人大常委会免职名单
