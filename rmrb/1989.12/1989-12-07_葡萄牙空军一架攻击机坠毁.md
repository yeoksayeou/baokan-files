Title: A Portuguese Air Force Attack Aircraft Crashes
Author:
Date: 1989-12-07
Edition: 4

A Portuguese Air Force A-7 attack aircraft crashed near the town of Vicosa in southern Portugal on the 4th. The pilot was killed in the crash.

The A-7 attack aircraft took off from the Montereal Air Force Base for a training flight when the accident occurred. After the incident, a Portuguese Air Force investigation team immediately rushed to the scene. The cause of the accident is currently under investigation.

### 葡萄牙空军一架攻击机坠毁

1989-12-07
第4版(国际新闻)
专栏：

    葡萄牙空军一架攻击机坠毁
    葡萄牙空军一架A—7攻击机四日在葡南部维科萨镇附近坠毁，驾驶员当场身亡。
    这架A—7攻击机是从蒙特雷亚尔空军基地起飞后进行训练飞行时失事的。事故发生后，葡空军一个调查组立即奔赴现场，目前事故原因正在调查中。
