Title: Energy-efficient Lamps to Illuminate the Asian Games Venues
Author: Zhou Ning
Date: 1989-12-07
Edition: 3

A domestically developed energy-efficient high-efficiency projector lamp has recently been selected for use in the Beijiao Sports Stadium for the Asian Games, serving as the primary lighting source.

This new type of columnar projector lamp was developed by Nanjing Anda Electromechanical Technology Co., Ltd. It boasts high light utilization efficiency, excellent heat dissipation, long service life, and effective prevention of salt mist vapor corrosion on the reflective mirror surface. Traditional lamps typically have a light utilization rate of no more than 40%. According to tests conducted by the National Lamp Testing Center, this lamp achieves a utilization rate of nearly 70%, representing an improvement of over 60% compared to traditional lamps.

### 高效节能灯将照耀亚运会赛场
周宁
1989-12-07
第3版(综合)
专栏：体育

    高效节能灯将照耀亚运会赛场
    本报讯　一种国内首创的节能高效投光灯具，最近被亚运会北郊体育馆选用，作为主要灯光光源。
    这种新型的柱面投光灯具是由南京安达机电科技开发有限公司研制开发的，具有光利用率高，散热性能好，使用寿命长，能有效地防止盐雾性蒸气对反射镜面的腐蚀等优点。传统灯具日光利用率通常不超过40％。据全国灯具测试中心测试表明，该灯具利用率接近70％，较传统灯具提高了60％以上。
                                      （周宁）
