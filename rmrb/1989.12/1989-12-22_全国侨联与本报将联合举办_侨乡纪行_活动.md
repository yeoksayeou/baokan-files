Title: The All-China Federation of Returned Overseas Chinese and the Newspaper Will Jointly Hold the "Sojourn in the Homeland of Overseas Chinese" Activity
Author:
Date: 1989-12-22
Edition: 2

The All-China Federation of Returned Overseas Chinese and this newspaper will jointly organize the "Sojourn in the Homeland of Overseas Chinese" reporting activity, which will begin next March. This evening, the All-China Federation of Returned Overseas Chinese and this newspaper jointly invited some representatives attending the Fourth Session of the National Overseas Chinese Congress here to hold a symposium, at which the plan was announced.

Our country has Guangdong, Fujian, Guangxi, Hainan and other key hometowns of overseas Chinese, as well as many small and medium-sized hometowns of overseas Chinese scattered throughout the country. The total number of returned overseas Chinese and relatives of overseas Chinese reaches more than 30 million. The purpose of this reporting activity is to reflect the changes in the above-mentioned places, to further promote the reform and opening up of the hometowns of overseas Chinese, to inspire the broad masses of returned overseas Chinese and relatives of overseas Chinese to actively participate in the modernization drive, and to attract overseas Chinese to participate in the construction of the motherland.

The symposium was presided over by Xiao Gang, Vice Chairman of the All-China Federation of Returned Overseas Chinese. Chen Lan, Vice Chairman of the All-China Federation of Returned Overseas Chinese, and Zhang Yunsheng, Deputy Editor-in-Chief of People's Daily, attended the symposium.

### 全国侨联与本报将联合举办“侨乡纪行”活动

1989-12-22
第2版(国内新闻)
专栏：

    全国侨联与本报将联合举办“侨乡纪行”活动
    本报北京12月21日讯　由全国侨联和本报联合主办的“侨乡纪行”采访活动将于明年3月开始进行。今天晚上，全国侨联和本报联合邀请正在这里参加第四次侨代会的部分代表举行座谈会，会上宣布了这项计划。
    我国有广东、福建、广西、海南等重点侨乡，还有一批遍布于各地的中小侨乡，归侨、侨眷总数达3000多万。这次采访活动的目的就是要反映上述地方的变化，进一步促进侨乡的改革、开放，激发广大归侨、侨眷积极投身四化事业，吸引海外侨胞参加祖国的建设。
    座谈会由全国侨联副主席肖岗主持，全国侨联副主席陈兰通、人民日报副总编辑张云声出席了座谈会。
