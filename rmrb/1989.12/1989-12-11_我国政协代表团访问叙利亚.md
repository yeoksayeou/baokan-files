Title: Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference Delegation Visits Syria
Author:
Date: 1989-12-11
Edition: 4

Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference delegation visits Syria

Xinhua News Agency Damascus, December 6 - The leader of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference delegation, Han Kehua, condemned Israel's barbaric destruction of the Syrian town of Quneitra and the suppression of the uprising by the Palestinian people in the occupied territories.

During a meeting with the Vice Secretary-General of the Syrian Arab Socialist Ba'ath Party, Ahmad, on the evening of the 5th, Han Kehua also expressed that China firmly supports Syria's efforts to defend its independence.

Ahmad stated that Syria firmly supports the just struggle of the Palestinian people and supports the convening of an international peace conference on the Middle East under the auspices of the United Nations.

The Chinese delegation arrived in Damascus on the 3rd for a visit.

### 我国政协代表团访问叙利亚

1989-12-11
第4版(国际新闻)
专栏：

    我国政协代表团访问叙利亚
    新华社大马士革12月6日电　正在这里访问的中国政协代表团团长韩克华谴责以色列野蛮破坏叙利亚城镇库奈特哈和镇压被占领土巴勒斯坦人民的起义。
    在5日晚与叙利亚阿拉伯复兴社会党副总书记阿赫马尔的会谈中，韩克华还表示，中国坚决支持叙利亚为捍卫独立而作的努力。
    阿赫马尔表示，叙利亚坚决支持巴勒斯坦人民的正义斗争，支持召开联合国主持下的中东和平国际会议。
    中国代表团是于3日到达大马士革访问的。
