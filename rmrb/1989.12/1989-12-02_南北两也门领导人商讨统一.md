Title: Leaders of North and South Yemen Discuss Unification
Author:
Date: 1989-12-02
Edition: 4

Leaders from North and South Yemen met to discuss unification. On November 29, Ali Abdullah Saleh, President of the Yemen Arab Republic, and Ali Salem al-Baid, General Secretary of the Yemeni Socialist Party, held a meeting in Aden, the capital of South Yemen, to discuss the issue of unification between the two countries.

The Sana'a radio station quoted Ali Salem al-Baid as saying that the leaders of the two countries have made serious efforts to address the issue of unification on the basis of fraternal democratic dialogue.

### 南北两也门领导人商讨统一

1989-12-02
第4版(国际新闻)
专栏：

    南北两也门领导人商讨统一
    阿拉伯也门总统萨利赫和民主也门社会党总书记比德十一月二十九日在民主也门首都亚丁举行会晤，商讨两国的统一问题。
    萨那电台援引比德的话说，两国领导人在兄弟般的民主对话的基础上对统一问题已经作了认真的努力。
