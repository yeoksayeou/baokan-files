Title: Two Chinese Athletes Advance to the Women's Singles Final in the Asian Youth Table Tennis Championships
Author:
Date: 1989-12-27
Edition: 2

Two Chinese female table tennis players, Wu Na and Fu Pei, secured their spots in the women's singles final of the fourth Asian Youth Table Tennis Championships held in New Delhi, India, on December 25.

The sixth-seeded Wu Na defeated South Korean player Kim Bun-jik in the semifinals with a score of 21-16, 21-11, advancing to the final. Unseeded player Fu Pei defeated the ninth-seeded South Korean player Jang Jae-hee with a score of 21-11, 21-18, also earning a place in the final.

Two players from the Democratic People's Republic of Korea secured spots in the men's singles final.

### 我两选手获亚洲少年乒乓球赛女单决赛权

1989-12-27
第2版(国内新闻)
专栏：体育

    我两选手获亚洲少年乒乓球赛女单决赛权
    新华社新德里12月25日电　中国少年女子乒乓球选手邬娜和付培25日在印度新德里举行的第四届亚洲少年乒乓球赛上获女单决赛权。
    这届比赛的第6号种子邬娜在半决赛中以21∶16、21∶11击败了南朝鲜选手金分植，进入决赛。非种子选手付培以21∶11、21∶18击败第9号种子、南朝鲜的张在熙获得决赛权。
    朝鲜民主主义人民共和国的两名选手获得了男单决赛权。
