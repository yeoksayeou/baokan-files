Title: Five Teams to Compete in the Second Winter Asian Games
Author: Jiǎn Zhàn Guó
Date: 1989-12-15
Page: 3

Xinhua News Agency, Tokyo, December 14th.

The organizing committee of the Second Winter Asian Games recently announced that by the registration deadline of December 12th, nine countries and regions, including China, had registered to participate in this Winter Games.

The Second Winter Asian Games will be held from March 9th to 14th, 1990, in Sapporo, a city in northern Japan. China, North Korea, Japan, Chinese Taipei, and Iran are among the five countries and regions that have registered to compete. India, Mongolia, Hong Kong, and South Korea will only send officials to attend the Games. China will send 109 athletes, making it the largest delegation. The official competition events will include skiing, skating, ice hockey, and modern biathlon, totaling four major events.

### 第二届冬季亚运会将有5队参赛
姜占国
1989-12-15
第3版(综合)
专栏：体育

    第二届冬季亚运会将有5队参赛
    新华社东京12月14日电  （记者姜占国）据第二届冬季亚运会组委会日前宣布，到报名截止日12日为止，中国等9个国家和地区报名参加这届冬运会。
    第二届冬季亚运会将于明年3月9日至14日在日本北部城市札幌举行。中国、朝鲜、日本、中国台北和伊朗等5个国家和地区已报名参赛。印度、蒙古人民共和国、香港和南朝鲜届时只派官员参加大会。我国将有109名运动员参赛，是人数最多的代表团。正式比赛项目包括滑雪、滑冰、冰球和现代冬季两项4大项。
