Title: Recommend Several Wedding Ceremonies
Author: Liu Yulan
Date: 1989-12-31
Edition: 8

For many years, countless young people's marriages have been burdened by the extravagant and wasteful traditional customs, making it a lifelong regret. To help young friends choose a wedding ceremony that is both civilized, thrifty, elegant, and lively, we recommend the following several wedding ceremonies:

1. Tree Planting Wedding. At a pre-arranged time and place, the couple, along with both sets of parents and friends, plant a tree together and take a commemorative photo.

2. Congratulatory Message Wedding. Send out invitations stating: "The wedding will not accept gifts, but congratulatory messages are required." During the wedding, guests write their congratulatory messages in a prepared book or dictate them. The host records all the messages with a tape recorder. In the days to come, whenever you open or play these messages, the wise words and beautiful wishes will remind you of that unforgettable wedding.

3. Dance Party Wedding. This type of wedding is novel, lively, enthusiastic, solemn, elegant, and full of contemporary flavor. It also facilitates the natural and unrestrained interaction among relatives, colleagues, and friends.

4. Movie Wedding. Rent two exciting movies and invite all relatives and friends. After the movie screening, the bride and groom stand at the door to see off the guests.

5. Group Wedding.

6. Newspaper Announcement Wedding. The couple publishes their marriage announcement in a newspaper, informing the world and replacing other traditional ceremonies.

7. Travel Wedding.

### 推荐几种婚礼仪式
刘玉兰
1989-12-31
第8版(每周文摘)
专栏：

    推荐几种婚礼仪式
    多少年来，无数青年的婚姻大事被传统的大操大办风弄得苦不堪言，实为终身憾事。为了帮助广大青年朋友选择一个既文明、节俭，又高雅、热闹的婚礼形式，我们特向青年朋友推荐如下几种婚礼仪式：
    一、植树婚礼。在事先约定好的时间、地点，夫妻与双方父母、亲朋好友一同植树、合影作为永久的纪念。
    二、贺辞婚礼。先发请柬，写明：婚礼不收礼品，但必备新婚贺辞。举行婚礼时，来宾们将贺辞写在准备好的本子上，或口述出来。主持人用录音机将贺辞一一录下。在以后的日子里，每当您翻开或播放这些贺辞时，那金玉良言，美好的祝愿都会使您想起那难忘的婚礼。
    三、舞会婚礼。这种婚礼新颖、活泼、热烈、隆重、高雅、富有时代气息，且有利于亲戚、同事、朋友间无拘束，很自然地相识、交谈。
    四、电影婚礼。租两部精彩影片，遍邀亲友。电影放映结束，新郎、新娘在门口为亲友送行。
    五、集体婚礼。
    六、登报婚礼。当事人将结婚的消息刊登在报纸上，告示天下，以代替其它传统礼仪。
    七、旅游婚礼。
                （12月18日《湖北日报》刘玉兰文）
