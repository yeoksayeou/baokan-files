Title: Chinese Foreign Ministry Spokesperson Says China is Concerned about the Situation in the Philippines
Author:
Date: 1989-12-03
Edition: 1

The spokesperson for the Ministry of Foreign Affairs said today that China is deeply concerned about the events that have taken place in the Philippines.

He said, "We hope that under the leadership of President Corazon Aquino, the situation in the Philippines will return to normal as soon as possible." He made these remarks in response to a reporter's question.

According to reports, a military coup occurred in the Philippines at 1:00 AM on December 1.

### 外交部发言人说  中国关切菲律宾局势

1989-12-03
第1版(要闻)
专栏：

    外交部发言人说
    中国关切菲律宾局势
    新华社北京12月2日电　外交部发言人今天在这里说，中国对菲律宾国内发生的事件深为关切。
    他说“我们希望在科·阿基诺总统领导下，菲律宾局势尽快恢复正常。”他是在回答记者提问时讲这番话的。
    据报道，12月1日凌晨菲律宾发生了军事政变。
