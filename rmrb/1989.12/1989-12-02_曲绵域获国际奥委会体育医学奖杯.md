Title: Qu Mianyu Receives the International Olympic Committee's Sports Medicine Cup
Author: Yuan Wen Zhong
Date: 1989-12-02
Edition: 3

    Qu Mianyu Receives the International Olympic Committee's Sports Medicine Cup
    According to Xinhua News Agency in Jinan on December 1st, the Chinese Olympic Committee today, on behalf of the International Olympic Committee, awarded the "1989 Sports Medicine Cup" to Qu Mianyu, the president and professor of Beijing Medical University, in recognition of his long-term clinical and basic research in sports medicine and sports injury prevention and treatment, and his outstanding achievements in these fields.

### 曲绵域获国际奥委会体育医学奖杯
袁文忠
1989-12-02
第3版(综合)
专栏：体育

    曲绵域获国际奥委会体育医学奖杯
    据新华社济南12月1日电　（记者袁文忠）中国奥委会今天在山东省泰安市代表国际奥委会将“1989年体育医学奖杯”授予北京医科大学校长、教授曲绵域，以表彰他多年来从事运动医学及运动创伤防治的临床和基础研究，并取得卓著成就。
