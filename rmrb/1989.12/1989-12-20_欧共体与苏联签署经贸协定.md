Title: The European Community and the Soviet Union Sign an Economic and Trade Agreement
Author:
Date: 1989-12-20
Edition: 4

The European Community and the Soviet Union formally signed an economic and trade agreement on December 18.

According to this 10-year economic and trade agreement, the areas of cooperation between the European Community and the Soviet Union will include agriculture, raw materials and mining, energy, science and technology, nuclear research, banking, insurance and financial services, transportation, tourism, environmental protection, management, and vocational training.

The President of the Council of Ministers of the European Community, French Foreign Minister Roland Dumas, and Soviet Foreign Minister Eduard Shevardnadze held a joint press conference after the signing of the agreement.

Dumas stated that the signing of the agreement has political significance for repairing East-West relations. Shevardnadze said that this is the result of the great efforts made by the European people after overcoming political and military confrontation.

The agreement guarantees that both sides will cooperate in trade and promote the entry of Soviet products into the European Community market, and will also promote the activities of European Community investors in the Soviet Union.

### 欧共体与苏联签署经贸协定

1989-12-20
第4版(国际新闻)
专栏：

    欧共体与苏联签署经贸协定
    新华社布鲁塞尔12月18日电　欧洲共同体和苏联18日正式签署了一项经济贸易协定。
    根据这份为期10年的经贸协定，欧共体和苏联的合作领域将包括农业、原料及采矿、能源、科技、核研究、银行、保险和金融服务、运输、旅游、环保、管理及职业培训等方面。
    欧共体部长理事会执行主席、法国外长罗朗·迪马和苏联外长谢瓦尔德纳泽在协定签署后联合举行了记者招待会。
    迪马表示，协定的签署对修复东西方关系有政治上的意义。谢瓦尔德纳泽则表示，这是欧洲人民在克服了政治军事对抗之后付出巨大努力的结果。
    协定保证双方将在贸易及推动苏联产品进入欧共体市场方面合作，并将促进欧共体投资者在苏联的活动。
