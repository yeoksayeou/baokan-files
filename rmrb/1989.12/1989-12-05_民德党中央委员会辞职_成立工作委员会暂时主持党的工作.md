Title: Central Committee of the SED Resigns; Work Commission to Temporarily Lead Party
Author: Xǔ Hóngzhì
Date: 1989-12-05
Edition: 4

The Central Committee of the Socialist Unity Party of Germany (SED) announced today that it has decided to resign en masse. The decision was made during the 12th plenary session of the 11th Central Committee held today. The plenary session also decided to establish a work commission to temporarily lead the party and to prepare for a special party congress scheduled to be held from December 15 to 17.

The work commission of the SED consists of 25 members, with the first secretary of the Erfurt District Party Committee, Hans Modrow, serving as the head.

### 民德党中央委员会辞职  成立工作委员会暂时主持党的工作
许宏治
1989-12-05
第4版(国际新闻)
专栏：

    民德党中央委员会辞职
    成立工作委员会暂时主持党的工作
    本报柏林12月3日电　记者许宏治报道：据民德电视台今天下午报道的消息，德国统一社会党中央委员会在今天举行的11届12中全会上决定，中央委员会全体辞职。全会决定由一个工作委员会主持党的工作并负责准备定于12月15—17日举行的特别党代表大会。
    德国统一社会党的这个工作委员会由25名成员组成，爱尔富特专区党委第一书记赫·克罗克尔为负责人。
