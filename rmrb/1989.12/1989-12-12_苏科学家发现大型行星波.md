Title: Soviet Scientists Discover Large Planetary Waves
Author:
Date: 1989-12-12
Edition: 4

Soviet scientists have discovered large planetary waves in the upper layer of the Earth's ionosphere. This discovery has significant practical implications for improving the quality of radio communications and predicting various natural disasters on Earth.

Soviet scientists speculate that these planetary waves occur at the edge of the magnetic field, where the electromagnetic field is uneven, disrupting the relatively stable behavior of the solar wind.

### 苏科学家发现大型行星波

1989-12-12
第4版(国际新闻)
专栏：

    苏科学家发现大型行星波
    新华社莫斯科12月10日电　格鲁吉亚地球物理学家在地球电离层的上层发现了大型行星波，这一发现对改进无线电通信的质量和预报地球上的各种自然灾害有着重大的实际意义。
    苏联科学家推测，行星波发生在磁场边缘，因为这里的电磁场不均匀，太阳风的相对稳定“行为”遭到破坏。
