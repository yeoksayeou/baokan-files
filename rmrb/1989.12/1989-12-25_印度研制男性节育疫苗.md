Title: India Develops Male Contraceptive Vaccine
Author:
Date: 1989-12-25
Edition: 7

Indian scientists have developed a male contraceptive vaccine after more than a decade of effort.

This vaccine works by inhibiting the production of follicle-stimulating hormone, thereby suppressing sperm production and achieving contraception.

Experiments on monkeys have shown that for the first year of contraception, the vaccine needs to be administered 3-4 times, and subsequently, 1-2 times per year is sufficient. If the vaccine injections are stopped, fertility can be restored.

### 印度研制男性节育疫苗

1989-12-25
第7版(国际专页)
专栏：

    印度研制男性节育疫苗
    印度科学家经过10余年努力，研制出男性节育疫苗。
    这种疫苗是通过阻止促卵泡激素的产生，以抑制精子的产生从而达到节育的目的。
    在猴子身上试验表明，节育的第一年需注射疫苗3—4次，之后每年注射1—2次即可。如果停止注射疫苗，还可恢复生育能力。（新）
