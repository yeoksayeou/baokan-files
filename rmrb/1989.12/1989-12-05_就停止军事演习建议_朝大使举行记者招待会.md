Title: North Korean Ambassador Holds Press Conference on Proposal to Halt Military Exercises

Author:

Date: 1989-12-05

Edition: 2

	North Korean Ambassador to China, Chu Changjun, held a press conference today regarding the statement made by the spokesperson of the North Korean Foreign Ministry on November 30. The spokesperson proposed three measures to halt military exercises, aiming to ease tensions on the Korean Peninsula, promote dialogue between the North and the South, and urge the United States and South Korean authorities to agree to a three-party talks on making the Korean Peninsula a nuclear-free zone.

	Ambassador Chu Changjun stated, "We believe that taking this measure immediately will contribute to eliminating the danger of nuclear war on the Korean Peninsula." He expressed the hope that the United States and South Korean authorities will respond positively to this proposal.

### 就停止军事演习建议  朝大使举行记者招待会

1989-12-05
第2版(国内新闻)
专栏：

    就停止军事演习建议
    朝大使举行记者招待会
    新华社北京12月4日电　朝鲜民主主义人民共和国驻华大使朱昌骏今天就11月30日朝鲜外交部发言人发表的谈话举行记者招待会。
    朝鲜外交部发言人在11月30日的谈话中提出了有关停止军事演习的3项建议，以缓和朝鲜半岛的紧张局势，推动南北对话，并敦促美国和南朝鲜当局同意与北方就使朝鲜半岛成为无核区问题举行3方会谈。
    朱昌骏大使指出，“我们认为，立即采取这一措施，将对在朝鲜半岛消除核战争危险作出一定的贡献”。他表示希望美国和南朝鲜当局对这次建议作出肯定的答复。
