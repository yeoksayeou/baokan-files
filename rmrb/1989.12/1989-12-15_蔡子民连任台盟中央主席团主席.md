Title: Cai Zimin Re-elected as Chairman of the Central Committee of the Taiwan Democratic Self-Government League
Author:
Date: 1989-12-15
Page: 2

Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, December 14th.

The ongoing fourth plenary session of the fourth central committee of the Taiwan Democratic Self-Government League today elected Cai Zimin as the chairman of the central committee by secret ballot and equal number of candidates.

Cai Zimin, 70 years old, is from Changhua, Taiwan. He graduated from Waseda University in Japan in 1944, served as the chief editor of the Taipei "Free News" in 1946, and participated in the February 28th uprising of the Taiwanese people in 1947. He later went to the mainland. After the founding of the People's Republic, he served as the deputy director, deputy minister, and cultural counselor in the Central People's Broadcasting Station, the State Council's Foreign Affairs Office, the Chinese People's Association for Friendship with Foreign Countries, the Ministry of Culture, and the Chinese Embassy in Japan. He has long been engaged in Sino-Japanese friendship, foreign cultural exchanges, and work related to Taiwan. He is a representative of the Fourth, Fifth, Sixth, and Seventh National People's Congress, and a member of the Standing Committee of the Sixth and Seventh National People's Congress.

Cai Zimin said after his election that he would, under the leadership of the Communist Party of China, rely on the broad masses of the league members to strengthen the ideological and organizational construction of the Taiwan Democratic Self-Government League, and continue to do a good job in political consultation, legislative participation, and work related to Taiwan.

### 蔡子民连任台盟中央主席团主席

1989-12-15
第2版(国内要闻)
专栏：

    蔡子民连任台盟中央主席团主席
    新华社北京12月14日电　正在进行的台盟中央四届三中全会今天下午以无记名等额选举的方式，选举蔡子民连任台盟中央主席团主席。
    现年70岁的蔡子民，系台湾彰化人，1944年毕业于日本早稻田大学，1946年任台北《自由报》总编辑，1947年参加台湾人民“二·二八”起义，后到大陆。建国后，曾在中央人民广播电台、国家对外文委、中国人民对外友好协会、文化部及中国驻日使馆任科长、处长、副司长、文化参赞，长期从事中日友好、对外文化交流和对台工作。他是四、五、六、七届全国人大代表，第六、七届全国人大常务委员会委员。
    蔡子民在当选后表示，他将在中国共产党的领导下，依靠广大盟员，努力加强台盟的思想组织建设，继续做好参政、议政和对台工作。
