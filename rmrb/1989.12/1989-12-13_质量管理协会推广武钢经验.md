Title: Quality Management Association Promotes Wuhan Steel Experience
Author: Li Niangui
Date: 1989-12-13
Edition: 2

The China Quality Management Association held a meeting in Beijing today and decided that in the first half of next year, a conference will be held at the Wuhan Steel Company to promote the experience of Wuhan Steel's implementation of total quality management. The aim is to drive the deepening of total quality management across various regions and industries nationwide, seeking benefits from product quality and quality management.

Wuhan Steel began implementing total quality management in 1979. After a decade of exploration, it has forged a path of development focused on quality and efficiency. Over the past 10 years, Wuhan Steel's production volume, output value, and labor productivity have doubled, while profits and taxes have quadrupled, growing at an annual rate of 24.6%.

### 质量管理协会推广武钢经验
李年贵
1989-12-13
第2版(国内新闻)
专栏：

    质量管理协会推广武钢经验
    据新华社北京12月12日电　（记者李年贵）今天在北京召开的中国质量管理协会常务理事会决定，明年上半年将在武汉钢铁公司召开现场会议，推广武钢实行全面质量管理的经验，推动全国各地、各行各业深入开展全面质量管理工作。向产品质量和质量管理要效益。
    武钢从1979年开始推行全面质量管理。经过10年探索，开拓出一条质量效益型发展路子。10年来武钢产量、产值、劳动生产率提高了一倍，利税翻了两番，每年以24.6％的速度递增。
