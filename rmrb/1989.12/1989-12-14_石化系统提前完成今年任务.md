Title: Petrochemical System Completes This Year's Task Ahead of Schedule
Author: Zhao Mingliang
Date: 1989-12-14
Edition: 1

The China Petrochemical Corporation has completed the state-assigned plan 48 days ahead of schedule. By the end of November, the total industrial output value reached 38.09 billion yuan, exceeding the annual plan by 2.14 billion yuan and growing by 6.7% compared to the same period last year. Among the 40 chemical, fertilizer, and chemical fiber products evaluated by the state, 33 have already completed their annual tasks ahead of schedule.

### 石化系统提前完成今年任务
赵明亮
1989-12-14
第1版(要闻)
专栏：

    石化系统提前完成今年任务
    据新华社北京十二月十三日电  （记者赵明亮）中国石化总公司已提前四十八天完成国家下达的计划，到十一月底，工业产值总额达三百八十点九亿元，超出全年计划二十一点四亿元，比去年同期增长百分之六点七。国家考核的四十个化工、化肥、化纤产品品种中，有三十三个已提前完成全年任务。
