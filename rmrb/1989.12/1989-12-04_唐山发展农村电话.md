Title: Tangshan Develops Rural Telephone Services
Author: Xǔ Guìyuán, Fāng Lìnìng
Date: 1989-12-04
Page: 2

Xinhua News Agency, Shijiazhuang, December 3rd.

Tangshan City's postal and telecommunications bureau in Hebei Province has made significant progress in improving rural telephone services. As of now, the city has installed 3256 crossbar and electronic program-controlled telephone exchanges in rural areas. Additionally, the original 2500-plus manual telephones have been upgraded to automatic telephones and integrated into the local county city's automatic telephone network. This has made Tangshan the city with the highest number of automatic telephones in rural areas in the province.

### 唐山发展农村电话
许贵元  方立宁
1989-12-04
第2版(国内新闻)
专栏：四面八方

    唐山发展农村电话
    据新华社石家庄12月3日电  （通讯员许贵元、方立宁）河北省唐山市邮电局改善农村电话服务设施取得突破性进展。到目前，全市农村已安装纵横制和程控电子交换机3256门，同时，原有的2500多部人工电话也已改装为自动电话并入当地县城的自动电话网，使全市农村拥有的自动电话数量跃居全省榜首。
