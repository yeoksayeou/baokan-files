Title: Closing of the First Capital University Student Cultural Festival
Author: Wáng Wèi
Date: 1989-12-29
Page: 3

Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, December 28th — (Reporter Wang Wei) The 22-day First Capital University Student Cultural Festival came to a close today. Over 2,000 students from more than 30 universities in the capital attended the closing ceremony.

During this festival, Beijing University's dance "Anse Waist Drum" and 21 other performances won first prizes in the arts performance competition. Tsinghua University's string quartet and 40 other performances won second prizes, and Beijing University of Science and Technology's solo performance and 69 other performances won third prizes. Tsinghua University's military band won a special award.

Since its opening on December 7th, the First Capital University Student Cultural Festival has staged more than 20 performances, showcasing the healthy and positive spirit of contemporary university students.

### 首都大学生文化节闭幕
王蔚
1989-12-29
第3版(综合)
专栏：

    首都大学生文化节闭幕
    据新华社北京12月28日电　（记者王蔚）为期22天的第一届首都大学生文化节今天落下帷幕，来自首都30余所高校的2000多名大学生参加了闭幕式。
    在这届文化节中，北京大学的舞蹈《安塞腰鼓》等22个节目获得文艺汇演一等奖，清华大学的弦乐四重奏等41个节目获二等奖，北京科技大学的独唱等70个节目获三等奖，清华大学军乐合奏获特别奖。
    第一届首都大学生文化节自12月7日开幕以来，共演出20余场，展现了当代大学生健康向上的精神风貌。
