Title: Anshan Iron and Steel Corporation Completes Annual Production Targets Ahead of Schedule
Author: Lǐ Xīnyàn Chén Guāngmíng
Date: 1989-12-26
Edition: 1

Anshan Iron and Steel Corporation, the largest industrial enterprise in China, has completed its annual production targets ahead of schedule. The 400,000 employees of the company have shown great spirit and tenacity, overcoming severe challenges related to raw materials, electricity, and funding. They have completed 7.4 million tons of steel, 6.95 million tons of iron, and 5.02 million tons of steel products, all ahead of schedule by 20 days, 22 days, and 38 days respectively.

### 鞍钢已提前完成全年生产任务
李新彦  陈光明
1989-12-26
第1版(要闻)
专栏：

    鞍钢已提前完成全年生产任务
    据新华社沈阳电　（记者李新彦、陈光明）我国最大工业企业鞍山钢铁公司40万职工振奋精神，顽强拚搏，千方百计克服原材料、电力和资金等严重困难，已提前20天完成740万吨钢、提前22天完成695万吨铁、提前38天完成502万吨钢材的全年国家计划。
