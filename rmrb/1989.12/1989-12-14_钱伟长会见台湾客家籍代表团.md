Title: Qian Weichang Meets with Taiwanese Hakka Delegation
Author:
Date: 1989-12-14
Page: 2

Xinhua News Agency, Guangzhou, December 12th.

National Committee of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference Vice Chairman and Chairman of the China Peaceful Unification Promotion Association Qian Weichang, this morning in Guangdong's Meizhou City, warmly received the leaders and advisors of the Taiwan delegation and various sub-delegations attending the World Hakka Federation Conference, totaling more than 20 people.

Qian Weichang inquired about the situation of the Taiwanese compatriots' phone calls to Taiwan, happily saying, "The phone calls are connected, that's good." "Three links" is just the first step, hoping that there will be more exchanges between the two sides of the Taiwan Strait.

### 钱伟长会见台湾客家籍代表团

1989-12-14
第2版(国内新闻)
专栏：

    钱伟长会见台湾客家籍代表团
    新华社广州12月12日电  全国政协副主席、中国和平统一促进会会长钱伟长，今天上午在广东梅州市亲切接见前来参加世界客属联谊大会的台湾代表团总团和各分团负责人及顾问20多人。
    钱伟长询问了台胞们与台湾通电话的情况，高兴地说，电话通了就好。“三通”仅是第一步，希望海峡两岸有更多的来往。
