Title: Mongolian Foreign Minister Pays Working Visit to the Soviet Union
Author:
Date: 1989-12-24
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, December 22nd.

    Mongolian Foreign Minister Pays Working Visit to the Soviet Union
    Soviet Premier Nikolai Ryzhkov met on the 22nd with Mongolian Foreign Minister Puntsagiin Gansükh, who is visiting Moscow. The two sides exchanged views on improving the economic cooperation mechanism between the two countries and enhancing the effectiveness of economic cooperation.

    According to a report from TASS, Soviet Foreign Minister Eduard Shevardnadze held talks with Gansükh on the 21st. The two sides discussed the issue of the remaining Soviet troops in Mongolia after 75 percent of the Soviet troops stationed there had been withdrawn.

    The Mongolian Foreign Minister pointed out that the complete normalization of Sino-Soviet relations has had a positive effect on the overall political climate in the Asia-Pacific region. Both sides expressed that the two countries will make necessary efforts to further develop various aspects of relations with China on the basis of equality and good-neighborliness.

    Gansükh arrived in the Soviet Union on the 20th for a working visit.

### 蒙古外长对苏进行工作访问

1989-12-24
第4版(国际新闻)
专栏：

    蒙古外长对苏进行工作访问
    苏联部长会议主席雷日科夫二十二日会见了正在莫斯科访问的蒙古外交部长贡布苏伦，双方就完善两国经济合作机制和提高经济合作效益等问题交换了意见。
    另据塔斯社报道，苏联外长谢瓦尔德纳泽二十一日和贡布苏伦进行了会谈，双方讨论了苏联撤出百分之七十五驻蒙苏军后尚留在那里的部分苏军问题。
    蒙古外长指出，中苏关系完全正常化对亚太地区总的政治气候起了良好的作用。双方表示，苏蒙两国将作出必要的努力为在平等和睦邻原则基础上进一步发展与中国的各方面的联系。
    贡布苏伦是二十日抵达苏联进行工作访问的。
