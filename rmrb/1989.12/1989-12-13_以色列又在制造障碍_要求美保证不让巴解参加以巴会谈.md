Israel Creates Obstacles Again, Demands U.S. Ensure PLO Exclusion from Israeli-Palestinian Talks

	BEIRUT, Dec. 11 (Xinhua) -- Israeli officials said today that Israel demands the United States reaffirm its commitment to exclude the Palestine Liberation Organization (PLO) from Palestinian-Israeli talks before the upcoming three-way foreign ministers' meeting in Washington next January.

	Israeli Foreign Minister Arens said he is not in a hurry to go to Washington. Israel will first discuss the conditions for the Palestinian-Israeli talks proposed by its cabinet with relevant parties.

	Israel announced last month that it accepted U.S. Secretary of State Baker's proposal for a three-way foreign ministers' meeting in Washington. Before that, the Israeli cabinet demanded that the United States guarantee its opposition to the participation of PLO members in the talks.

	A spokesman for Israeli Prime Minister Shamir said the cabinet will send officials to the United States for consultations later this week.

	According to reports, Israeli, Egyptian, U.S., and Palestinian officials secretly discussed a list of Palestinian participants over the weekend. It is believed that Israel's latest move will create new obstacles for the proposed Palestinian-Israeli talks.

### 以色列又在制造障碍  要求美保证不让巴解参加以巴会谈

1989-12-13
第4版(国际新闻)
专栏：

    以色列又在制造障碍
    要求美保证不让巴解参加以巴会谈
    新华社开罗12月11日电　耶路撒冷消息：以色列官员今天说，以色列要求美国在美、以、埃及3国明年1月华盛顿外长会议之前重新保证将巴解组织排斥于巴勒斯坦和以色列会谈之外。
    以色列外长阿伦斯表示他不急于赴华盛顿。以色列将首先就其内阁提出的巴以会谈条件同有关方面接触。
    以色列上月宣布接受美国国务卿贝克提出的华盛顿3方外长会晤的建议。在此之前，以色列内阁要求美国保证反对巴解组织人士参加会谈。
    以色列总理沙米尔的发言人说，内阁将于本周晚些时候派遣官员到美国进行磋商。
    据报道，以色列、埃及、美国和巴勒斯坦官员上周末就拟定巴勒斯坦人与会的名单进行了秘密接触。据认为，以色列的最新举动将为拟议中的巴以会谈制造新的障碍。
