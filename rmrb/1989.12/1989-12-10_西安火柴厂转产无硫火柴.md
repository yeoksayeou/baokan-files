Title: Xi'an Match Factory Converts to Producing Sulfur-Free Matches
Author: Zhang Ruoyu
Date: 1989-12-10
Edition: 2

Foreign tourists visiting Xi'an no longer need to bring their own sulfur-free matches for smoking. The Xi'an Match Factory, which produces 500,000 boxes of matches annually, has been manufacturing sulfur-free matches since 1984, gradually increasing the production of these health-friendly matches year by year.

Currently, the factory has fully transitioned to producing sulfur-free matches, becoming the first factory in China to produce entirely sulfur-free matches.

### 西安火柴厂转产无硫火柴
张若愚
1989-12-10
第2版(国内新闻)
专栏：四面八方

    西安火柴厂转产无硫火柴
    据新华社西安12月7日电  （记者张若愚）外国旅游者到西安旅游无需为吸烟而自带无硫火柴了，西安火柴厂生产的无硫火柴为外来“烟民”们提供了方便。这家年产50万件火柴的企业从1984年开始生产无硫火柴以来，逐年扩大对人体无害的无硫火柴生产量。
    目前，工厂已全部生产无硫火柴，成为我国第一家全产无硫火柴的厂家。
