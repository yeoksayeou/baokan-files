Title: Zhang Yunfen Donates Artifacts to the Military Museum
Author: Zhang Minjie
Date: 1989-12-12
Edition: 3

    Zhang Yunfen, daughter of veteran Red Army soldier Zhang Jichun, donated 21 precious revolutionary artifacts, including Zhang Jichun's diaries, notebooks, and a "Red Army 10th Anniversary Commemorative Badge" to the Chinese People's Revolutionary Military Museum at the end of November.

### 张云芬向军事博物馆捐文物
张敏杰
1989-12-12
第3版(综合)
专栏：

    张云芬向军事博物馆捐文物
    本报讯　红军老战士张际春的女儿张云芬于十一月底将张际春的日记、笔记本及“红军十周年纪念章”等二十一件珍贵革命文物捐献给中国人民革命军事博物馆。（张敏杰）
