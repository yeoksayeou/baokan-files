Title: Chinese Women's Team Wins First Match in Asian Youth Table Tennis Championships
Author:
Date: 1989-12-21
Edition: 3

The fourth Asian Youth Table Tennis Championships began today in New Delhi.

In the women's team event, China's team, competing in the second group, defeated Iraq with a score of 3-0.

The defending champions from the Democratic People's Republic of Korea won their first match in the first group, defeating Nepal with a score of 3-0.

In the men's team event, the teams from the Democratic People's Republic of Korea and South Korea won their first matches in the first and second groups, respectively, defeating Iran and Pakistan with scores of 3-0.

The only match that went to four games was between Indonesia and Saudi Arabia in the second group, with Indonesia winning 3-1.

A total of 20 men's teams and 17 women's teams from across Asia are participating in this year's championships.

### 亚洲青少年乒乓球赛  中国女队初战得胜

1989-12-21
第3版(综合)
专栏：体育

    亚洲青少年乒乓球赛
    中国女队初战得胜
    新华社新德里12月20日电　第四届亚洲青少年乒乓球锦标赛今天在这里拉开战幕。
    在女子团体赛中，第二组的中国队以3∶0轻取伊拉克队。
    上届冠军朝鲜民主主义人民共和国队在第一组比赛中以3∶0战胜尼泊尔队。
    在男子团体比赛中，朝鲜民主主义人民共和国队和南朝鲜队分别在第一组和第二组比赛中，以3∶0战胜伊朗队和巴基斯坦队。
    唯一打了四盘才决出胜负的一场是第二组的印度尼西亚队和沙特阿拉伯队的比赛，印尼队以3∶1获胜。
    来自亚洲的20支男队和17支女队参加本届比赛。
