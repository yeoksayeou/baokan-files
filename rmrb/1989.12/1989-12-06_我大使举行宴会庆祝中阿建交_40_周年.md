Title: Chinese Ambassador Hosts Banquet to Celebrate 40 Years of China-Albania Diplomatic Relations
Author:
Date: 1989-12-06
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Tirana, November 23rd.

Chinese Ambassador to Albania Gu Maoxuan hosted a banquet at the embassy on November 23 to celebrate the 40th anniversary of the establishment of diplomatic relations between China and Albania. Guzari, Chairman of the State Planning Commission of Albania, and other relevant officials attended the banquet.

The banquet was held in a friendly atmosphere.

### 我大使举行宴会庆祝中阿建交40周年

1989-12-06
第4版(国际新闻)
专栏：

    我大使举行宴会庆祝中阿建交40周年
    新华社地拉那电　中国驻阿尔巴尼亚大使顾懋萱11月23日在大使馆举行宴会，庆祝中阿建交40周年。阿国家计委主任居扎里及有关方面负责人出席了宴会。
    宴会是在友好的气氛中进行的。
