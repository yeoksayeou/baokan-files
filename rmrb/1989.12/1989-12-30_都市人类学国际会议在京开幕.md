Title: International Conference on Urban Anthropology Opens in Beijing
Author: Liú Zhào Xiáng
Date: 1989-12-30
Edition: 3

The first International Conference on Urban Anthropology opened on December 28 in Beijing. Hu Sheng, President of the Chinese Academy of Sciences, attended the opening ceremony and delivered a speech.

Urban anthropology is a new discipline that studies the characteristics and changes of human culture with the city as its spatial unit. It is closely related to the development of human civilization and urbanization.

This conference was jointly initiated by scholars from China and the United States and organized by the Institute of Nationalities, Chinese Academy of Social Sciences. More than 80 scholars from China, the United States, the Soviet Union, Japan, Canada, Mexico, India, Yugoslavia, Uganda, and Hong Kong attended the conference.

### 都市人类学国际会议在京开幕
刘兆祥
1989-12-30
第3版(综合)
专栏：

    都市人类学国际会议在京开幕
    本报北京讯　记者刘兆祥报道：第一届都市人类学国际会议二十八日在北京开幕。中国科学院院长胡绳出席开幕式并讲了话。
    都市人类学是以都市为空间单位，研究人类文化的特点和变化，同人类文明的发展与都市化建设有着密切关系的一门新的学科。
    这次会议由中美两国学者共同发起，中国社会科学院民族研究所主办。中国、美国、苏联、日本、加拿大、墨西哥、印度、南斯拉夫、乌干达和香港的学者八十多人出席会议。
