Title: World's First Liver, Heart, and Kidney Transplant Successful

Author:

Date: 1989-12-08

Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, December 5th.

According to foreign reports, a transplant specialist at the University of Pittsburgh in the United States successfully performed the world's first combined heart, liver, and kidney transplant on a patient after 21.5 hours of effort on December 3rd.

The patient, a 26-year-old woman named Cindy Martin, underwent her second transplant surgery. Three years ago, she had a heart transplant, but her body rejected the transplanted heart and she developed hepatitis and kidney dysfunction. Martin's condition is normal after the surgery.

### 世界首例肝心肾移植成功

1989-12-08
第4版(国际新闻)
专栏：

    世界首例肝心肾移植成功
    新华社北京12月5日电　据外电报道，美国匹兹堡大学的一位器官移植专家，3日经过21个半小时的努力，成功地为一名患者进行了世界首例心脏、肝脏和肾脏多器官移植手术。
    这位名叫辛迪·马丁的妇女今年26岁，是第二次接受移植手术治疗。三年前她曾做过心脏移植手术，但她体内对移入的心脏产生了排斥作用，并患了肝炎和肾功能障碍。马丁手术后情况正常。
