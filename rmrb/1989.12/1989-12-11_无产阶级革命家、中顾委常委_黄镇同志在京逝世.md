Title: Revolutionary of the Proletariat, Standing Committee Member of the Central Advisory Commission, Comrade Huang Zhen Passes Away in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1989-12-11
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, December 10th.

Comrade Huang Zhen, a revolutionary of the proletariat and a Standing Committee Member of the Central Advisory Commission of the Communist Party of China, passed away in Beijing at 6:40 AM on December 10, 1989, due to illness. He was 80 years old.

During Comrade Huang Zhen's illness, Central leaders Jiang Zemin, Li Peng, Qiao Shi, Song Ping, and other leaders of the Central Advisory Commission, as well as some senior comrades, visited the hospital or sent representatives to express their condolences to the family.

### 无产阶级革命家、中顾委常委  黄镇同志在京逝世

1989-12-11
第1版(要闻)
专栏：

    无产阶级革命家、中顾委常委
    黄镇同志在京逝世
    新华社北京12月10日电　无产阶级革命家、中共中央顾问委员会常委黄镇同志因病医治无效，于1989年12月10日6时40分在北京逝世。终年八十岁。
    黄镇同志病重期间，江泽民、李鹏、乔石、宋平等中央领导同志、中顾委领导同志和一些老同志前往医院看望或派人对家属表示慰问。（附图片）
