Title: Foreign Affairs Briefing
Author:
Date: 1989-12-05
Edition: 2

The Vice Chairman of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress and Chairman of the All-China Federation of Trade Unions, Ni Zhifu, met in the afternoon of December 3 in Beijing with a delegation from the Malagasy Revolutionary Trade Union Federation led by Andrianerianjao Teofil, a member of the Supreme Revolutionary Council, a member of the Revolutionary Vanguard Party's Political Bureau, and Chairman of the Revolutionary Trade Union Federation.

The Malagasy guests arrived in Beijing on December 2 at the invitation of the All-China Federation of Trade Unions for a friendly visit.

The Argentine Tango Ensemble "Buenos Aires" performed on December 3 at the Beijing Concert Hall, showcasing typical Argentine tango music and dance styles to the Chinese audience.

### 外事简讯

1989-12-05
第2版(国内新闻)
专栏：

    外事简讯
    ▲全国人大常委会副委员长、中华全国总工会主席倪志福3日下午在北京会见了由最高革命委员会委员、革命先锋党政治局委员、革命工会联合会主席安德里亚努埃利苏阿·泰奥菲尔率领的马达加斯加革命工会代表团。
    马达加斯加客人是应中华全国总工会邀请于2日到达北京作友好访问的。
    ▲阿根廷布格列塞探戈乐团3日晚在北京音乐厅演出，向中国观众展示了典型的阿根廷探戈音乐和舞蹈风格。
                  （据新华社）
