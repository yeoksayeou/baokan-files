Title: The First Meeting of the Sino-Angolan Joint Committee Concludes
Author:
Date: 1989-12-26
Edition: 4

The first meeting of the Sino-Angolan Joint Committee, which lasted for four days, concluded today in Luanda, the capital of Angola. The two sides signed three documents.

The three documents are: the Minutes of the First Meeting of the Sino-Angolan Joint Committee, the Exchange of Letters on China's Grant of General Materials, and the Exchange of Letters on the Feasibility Study for the Construction of a Senior Party School.

Ulanmu'er, Assistant Minister of China's Ministry of Foreign Economic Relations and Trade, and Domingos das Chagas, Minister of Industry and Trade of Angola, signed the documents.

The Chinese economic and trade delegation, led by Ulanmu'er, and the Angolan delegation, led by Domingos das Chagas, Minister of Industry and Trade of Angola, conducted extensive discussions on cooperation in agriculture, energy, and other areas.

### 中安混合委员会第一次会议结束

1989-12-26
第4版(国际新闻)
专栏：

    中安混合委员会第一次会议结束
    新华社罗安达12月22日电　历时4天的中国—安哥拉混合委员会第一次会议，今天在安哥拉首都罗安达结束，双方签署了3个文件。
    这3个文件是：中国—安哥拉混合委员会第一次会议纪要、中国无偿提供一般物资和关于建设高级党校可行性考察的换文。
    中国对外经济贸易部部长助理乌兰木伦和安哥拉工业和贸易部部长多明戈·达斯沙加斯在文件上签了字。
    以乌兰木伦为首的中国经济贸易代表团和以安哥拉工业和贸易部长多明戈·达斯沙加斯为首的安哥拉代表团，就农业、能源及其他方面的合作问题进行了广泛的讨论。
