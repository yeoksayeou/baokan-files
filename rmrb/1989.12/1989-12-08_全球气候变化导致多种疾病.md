Title: Global Climate Change Leads to Various Diseases
Author:
Date: 1989-12-08
Edition: 4

    Global Climate Change Leads to Various Diseases
    Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, December 7 - According to foreign news reports, a recent conference on climate and health held in Washington pointed out that global warming and the destruction of the atmospheric ozone layer, which has led to increased ultraviolet radiation, have damaged the human body's disease resistance, thereby inducing various diseases.

    Some representatives at the conference said that global warming can lead to the widespread prevalence of encephalitis, rabies, yellow fever, dengue fever, and other infectious diseases transmitted by mosquitoes and parasites. At the same time, it can also damage the human immune system, leading to the development of many diseases.

    Additionally, the increasing threat of ultraviolet radiation to humans is becoming more severe.

### 全球气候变化导致多种疾病

1989-12-08
第4版(国际新闻)
专栏：

    全球气候变化导致多种疾病
    新华社北京12月7日电　据外电报道，最近在华盛顿举行的气候与健康会议指出，全球气候变暖和大气臭氧层破坏而引起的紫外线辐射增加，破坏了人体的抗病能力，从而诱发多种疾病。
  　一些与会代表说，全球气候变暖能导致脑炎、狂犬病、黄热病、登革热以及由蚊子和寄生虫传播的传染病广泛流行。同时还能损害人体的免疫系统，产生许多疾病。
    另外，紫外线辐射增加对人类造成的威胁也越来越严重。
