Title: Premier Li Peng Meets with Madame Chen Xiangmei and Her Delegation, Says China's Door is Open
Author: Zhū Yúnlóng
Date: 1989-12-09
Edition: 1

	Premier Li Peng today afternoon met with a delegation led by Madame Chen Xiangmei of the U.S. International Cooperation Committee in the Zhongnanhai's Ziguang Pavilion. He stressed that "China's door is open."

	During the friendly conversation, Premier Li Peng welcomed the friends' visit to China. He said, "Your visit is very beneficial for enhancing mutual understanding and promoting cooperation and exchanges."

	He emphasized, "China's door is open. We welcome friends to come and see for yourselves."

	When talking about China's foreign relations, Li Peng said, "The difficulties that have emerged in China's relations with some Western countries at present are temporary. As long as everyone adheres to the Five Principles of Peaceful Coexistence and acts accordingly, the friendly cooperative relations between us will gradually be restored and developed. I am full of confidence in this regard."

	Madame Chen Xiangmei and her delegation came to China for friendly exchanges and sightseeing.

### 李鹏会见陈香梅女士一行时说  中国大门是敞开的
朱云龙
1989-12-09
第1版(要闻)
专栏：

    李鹏会见陈香梅女士一行时说
    中国大门是敞开的
    新华社北京12月8日电  （记者朱云龙）国务院总理李鹏今天下午在中南海紫光阁会见由陈香梅女士率领的美国国际合作委员会代表团时强调说，“中国的大门是敞开的。”
    在友好的交谈中，李鹏总理欢迎各位朋友前来参观访问。他说，“你们的访问对增进相互了解、促进合作与交流是很有益处的。”
    他强调说，“中国的大门是敞开的，欢迎朋友们多来看看。”
    在谈到中国的对外关系时，李鹏说，“目前中国同某些西方国家的关系中出现的困难是暂时的。只要大家能切实按照和平共处五项原则办事，相互间的友好合作关系是会逐步得到恢复和发展的，我对此是充满信心的。”
    陈香梅女士一行是来这里进行友好交流活动和参观旅游的。
