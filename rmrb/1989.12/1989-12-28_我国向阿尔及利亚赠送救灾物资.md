Title: China Donates Disaster Relief Supplies to Algeria
Author:
Date: 1989-12-28
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Algiers, December 20th.

China today donated relief supplies worth 1 million yuan to the Algerian government to aid victims of the earthquake that struck the Algiers region at the end of October.

The relief supplies include 7,500 blankets, 10,000 thermos bottles, and 16,000 pieces of acrylic clothing and sweaters.

Today, Chinese Ambassador to Algeria An Huihou and Algerian Deputy Foreign Minister Hafad attended the signing ceremony for the donation, which was held at the Foreign Ministry.

### 我国向阿尔及利亚赠送救灾物资

1989-12-28
第4版(国际新闻)
专栏：

    我国向阿尔及利亚赠送救灾物资
    新华社阿尔及尔12月20日电　中国政府今天向阿尔及利亚政府赠送100万人民币的物资，用于救济今年10月底在阿尔及尔地区发生地震中受害的灾民。
    这批救灾物资有毯子7500条，保温瓶10000个，腈纶衣和毛衣16000件。
    今天，中国驻阿尔及利亚大使安惠侯和阿外交部代理秘书长哈法德出席了在外交部举行的赠物签字仪式。
