Title: China's Export Commodities Exhibition Opens in Los Angeles
Author:
Date: 1989-12-10
Edition: 4

China's Export Commodities Exhibition opened today in Los Angeles, with 76 import and export companies and manufacturing enterprises showcasing their products.

This is the second time the China Council for the Promotion of International Trade has held an export commodities exhibition in Los Angeles. The exhibition covers an area of 2,000 square meters, with main exhibits including machinery, hardware and minerals, chemicals, light industry, handicrafts, silk, agricultural and livestock products, grains and oils, and food.

More than 400 businesspeople from the Los Angeles area attended today's opening ceremony.

### 中国出口商品展览会在洛杉矶开幕

1989-12-10
第4版(国际新闻)
专栏：

    中国出口商品展览会在洛杉矶开幕
    新华社华盛顿12月8日电　中国出口商品展览会今天在洛杉矶市开幕，共有76个进出口公司和生产厂家展出了它们的产品。
    这是中国国际贸易促进委员会在洛杉矶举办的第二次出口商品展览会。展览会展出面积为2000平方米，展品主要有机械设备、五金矿产、化工、轻工、工艺、丝绸、土畜、粮油、食品等。
    洛杉矶地区工商界人士共400多人参加了今天的开幕式。
