Title: Shenzhen's Industrial Output Exceeds 10 Billion Yuan
Author: Xu Feng
Date: 1989-12-14
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Shenzhen, December 12th.

Shenzhen's industrial output has surpassed 10 billion yuan as of December 2nd, completing the annual plan 28 days ahead of schedule. According to statistics, the city's total industrial output value will reach 11.5 billion yuan.

### 深圳工业总产值突破百亿元
许烽
1989-12-14
第1版(要闻)
专栏：

    深圳工业总产值突破百亿元
    据新华社深圳十二月十二日电  （记者许烽）深圳今年工业总产值到十二月二日已突破百亿元大关，提前二十八天超额完成全年计划。据统计，深圳全市工业总产值可达一百一十五亿元。
