Title: Ships Flying the Panamanian Flag Change Registration
Author:
Date: 1989-12-15
Edition: 4

	Ships Flying the Panamanian Flag Change Registration

	According to informed sources in Nicosia, the U.S. decision to sanction ships flying the Panamanian flag has prompted some shipping companies to transfer their vessels to Cypriot registration.

	The U.S. made this decision to punish the hardline Panamanian leader Noriega, prohibiting all ships flying the Panamanian flag from entering U.S. ports. This decision will take effect on February 1, 1990.

	These unnamed sources said that twenty ships originally registered in Panama have already been allowed to fly the Cypriot flag. Owners of another two hundred ships are also inquiring about the procedures for changing registration.

	Currently, there are two thousand ships flying the Cypriot flag worldwide, making Cyprus the seventh-largest shipping nation in the world. (Xinhua)

### 挂巴拿马国旗的船只改变注册

1989-12-15
第4版(国际新闻)
专栏：

    挂巴拿马国旗的船只改变注册
    据尼科西亚消息灵通人士十三日说，美国作出要制裁悬挂巴拿马国旗船只的决定，促使一些海运公司正把它们的船只转向在塞浦路斯注册。
    美国为惩罚巴拿马强硬派领导人诺列加作出决定，禁止一切悬挂巴拿马国旗的船只进入美国港口。这项决定于一九九○年二月一日生效。
    这些不愿透露姓名的人士说，原来在巴拿马注册的二十艘船已经得到塞浦路斯的允许，悬挂塞浦路斯国旗。还有二百艘船的船东也在打听改变注册的程序。
    现在世界上有二千艘船只悬挂塞浦路斯国旗，使塞浦路斯成为全世界第七大海运国。　（据新华社）
