Title: Pakistani President Meets Chinese Public Security Delegation, Expresses Understanding and Appreciation for China's Suppression of Riots
Author: Tang Shui Fu
Date: 1989-12-23
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Islamabad, December 21st. (Reporter Tang Shui Fu) Pakistani President Ghulam Ishaq Khan said today that Pakistan understands and appreciates the Chinese government's firm action in June to suppress the counter-revolutionary riots in Beijing, thereby maintaining political stability within China.

Ishaq Khan made these remarks this morning at the new presidential palace in Islamabad while receiving a Chinese public security delegation led by Wang Fang, State Councilor and Minister of Public Security.

He believes that China's insistence on carrying out reform and opening-up policies while maintaining political stability is correct.

He said that the governments and peoples of Pakistan and China have deep friendship and have developed friendly relations in all aspects for a long time.

Ishaq Khan welcomed the visit of the Chinese public security delegation to Pakistan and said that the visit by the Chinese delegation has opened up new areas of cooperation between the two countries in the fields of internal affairs, police, and public security.

Public Security Minister Wang Fang thanked the Pakistani government for understanding and appreciating the Chinese government's suppression of the counter-revolutionary riots, and said that China and Pakistan are the most reliable true friends because we support each other in times of difficulty.

Wang Fang conveyed Chairman Yang Shangkun's cordial greetings to President Ishaq Khan during the meeting. Ishaq Khan also asked Wang Fang to convey his cordial greetings to Chairman Yang.

Public Security Minister Wang Fang arrived here on December 19th at the invitation of Pakistani Interior Minister Ahsan.

### 巴基斯坦总统接见我公安代表团时说  巴对中国平暴表示理解和赞赏
汤水富
1989-12-23
第4版(国际新闻)
专栏：

    巴基斯坦总统接见我公安代表团时说
    巴对中国平暴表示理解和赞赏
    新华社伊斯兰堡12月21日电　（记者汤水富）巴基斯坦总统伊沙克·汗今天在这里说，巴理解和赞赏中国政府于今年6月坚决制止了在北京发生的反革命暴乱，维护了中国国内的政治稳定。
    伊沙克·汗总统是今天上午在伊斯兰堡新总统府接见由国务委员兼公安部长王芳率领的中国公安代表团时说这番话的。
    他认为，中国在维护政治稳定的前提下坚决实行改革开放的政策是正确的。
    他说，巴中两国政府和人民存在着深厚的友谊，长期以来发展了各方面的友好关系。
    伊沙克·汗对中国公安代表团访问巴基斯坦表示欢迎，并说中国代表团的来访为两国在内政、警察和公安部门开辟了新的合作领域。
    公安部长王芳感谢巴基斯坦政府对中国政府平息反革命暴乱表示理解和赞赏，并说中巴两国是最可靠的真正朋友，因为我们在困难的时候都相互支持。
    王芳在会见时转达了杨尚昆主席对伊沙克·汗总统的亲切问候。伊沙克·汗也请王芳转达他对杨主席的亲切问候。
    公安部长王芳是应巴基斯坦内政部长阿赫桑的邀请于12月19日来这里访问的。
