Foreign Ministry Spokesperson Answers Reporters' Questions, Reiterates China's Stance on the Cambodian Issue

New China News Agency, Beijing, November 30th - The Foreign Ministry spokesperson reiterated China's consistent stance on the Cambodian issue at today's press conference.

A reporter asked, "Australia has proposed establishing a United Nations interim administrative body in Cambodia, and Prince Sihanouk has expressed agreement. What is China's position on this?"

The spokesperson replied, "We have taken note of Australia's proposal. The Chinese government's consistent stance on the Cambodian issue is: Vietnam should withdraw its armed forces from Cambodia under strict and effective international supervision. After the withdrawal of Vietnamese troops, during the transitional period before elections in Cambodia, a temporary joint government should be established, led by Prince Sihanouk and participated in by the four Cambodian factions."

### 外交部发言人答记者问  重申中国对柬问题立场

1989-12-01
第1版(要闻)
专栏：

    外交部发言人答记者问
    重申中国对柬问题立场
    新华社北京11月30日电  外交部发言人在今天的新闻发布会上重申了中国政府在柬埔寨问题上的一贯立场。
    有记者问，澳大利亚提出了在柬组成联合国临时行政机构的建议，西哈努克亲王表示同意。中国对此持何态度？
    发言人回答说，“我们注意到了澳大利亚提出的这一建议。中国政府在柬埔寨问题上的一贯立场是：越南应在严格有效的国际监督之下从柬真正全部撤出其武装力量，在越撤军后到柬实行大选前的过渡时期，成立以西哈努克亲王为首的由柬四方参加的临时联合政府。”
