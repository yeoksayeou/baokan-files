Title: Hotpot "Public Funds"
Author: Yi Ming
Date: 1989-12-24
Edition: 8

	The cartoon titled "Hotpot 'Public Funds'" was published in the December 13 issue of the *Economic Daily*. The cartoon, which is unsigned, depicts a scene where public funds are being used in a way that is both wasteful and inappropriate. The imagery is stark and critical, reflecting the societal concerns of the time regarding the misuse of public resources. The cartoon serves as a commentary on the need for fiscal responsibility and transparency in government spending.
