Title: The Netherlands Invents New Technology for Preserving Books
Author:
Date: 1989-12-15
Page: 4

New Scientist magazine reports that American chemists have completed an 18-month trial of a new book preservation technology.

This new technology was invented by Akzo Chemicals of the Netherlands, with the trial funded by the U.S. Library of Congress.

Acid residues from wood pulp remain in paper, decomposing cellulose polymers, damaging the paper structure, and ultimately ruining books.

The technology uses the compound diethylzinc. Its volatile gas fills the pores in the paper, neutralizing the acids and producing ethane gas. Diethylzinc also reacts with residual moisture in the paper to form zinc oxide, protecting the paper from atmospheric sulfur dioxide and other acidic gases.

This new technology treats entire books, rather than processing them page by page, thus preserving every page. The process involves placing the books in a vacuum chamber and heating them for a day to remove residual moisture. The chamber is then flushed with dry nitrogen gas for five hours to remove any remaining air. Diethylzinc gas is then passed through the chamber at low pressure for about eight hours. Unreacted diethylzinc is recovered and reused at the outlet, while the ethane is burned off. After this process, the chamber is flushed with nitrogen gas to remove any residual diethylzinc. The entire process takes approximately three days.

The U.S. Library of Congress has over 10 million books in danger, requiring treatment. This new technology can process hundreds of books at a time, saving about one million books per year. Books treated with this method can last for hundreds of years.

### 荷兰发明保存图书新技术

1989-12-15
第4版(国际新闻)
专栏：

    荷兰发明保存图书新技术
    新华社伦敦电　据《新科学家》杂志报道，美国化学家用18个月的时间完成了一项图书保存新技术的试验。
    这项新技术是荷兰阿克索化学公司发明的，试验经费由美国国会图书馆提供。
    木材纸浆中的弱酸残留在纸张上，会分解纤维素聚合物，破坏纸张结构，最终损坏图书。
    这项新技术使用的化合物是二乙基锌，它的挥发气体充满纸上的孔，中和纸中的酸，并产生乙烷气体。二乙基锌还能同纸中残留的水分起作用形成氧化锌，防止纸张受空气中二氧化硫等酸性气体的腐蚀。
    这种新技术处理整本的书，不是把书拆开一页一页地处理，所以能保护每一页书。处理时，把书放在真空室内加热一天，除去残留的潮气，用干氮气冲刷真空室5小时，除去剩余的空气，以低压把二乙基锌气体通过真空室约8小时，在出口处把没有起作用的二乙基锌回收再用，把乙烷烧掉。这个过程完成后，用氮气清洗真空室，除去残留的二乙基锌，整个过程约需3天。
    美国国会图书馆有1000多万册书处于危险状态，需要处理。用这种新技术一次可以处理几百本书，一年可以挽救100万册书。处理过的书能够再用几百年。
