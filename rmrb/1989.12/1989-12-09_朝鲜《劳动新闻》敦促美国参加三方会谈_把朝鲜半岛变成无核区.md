Title: North Korea's *Rodong Sinmun* Urges the U.S. to Participate in Three-Party Talks to Make the Korean Peninsula a Nuclear-Free Zone
Author:
Date: 1989-12-09
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Pyongyang, 7th December

North Korea's *Rodong Sinmun* today published an article urging the U.S. to hold three-party talks with North and South Korea to discuss making the Korean Peninsula a nuclear-free zone.

The North Korean government proposed in June 1986 to make the Korean Peninsula a nuclear-free zone. A month ago, the North Korean Foreign Ministry issued a statement calling on the U.S. and South Korea to hold three-party talks with North Korea to realize this proposal. However, the U.S. has yet to respond.

The *Rodong Sinmun* article states that achieving the denuclearization of the Korean Peninsula is not only for maintaining and consolidating peace in Korea but also for safeguarding peace in Asia and the world. As the party responsible for deploying a large number of nuclear weapons in South Korea, the U.S. has an unavoidable responsibility for achieving the denuclearization of the Korean Peninsula.

The article says that if the U.S. truly wants to maintain peace on the Korean Peninsula, it should respond to North Korea's proposal. If they find it difficult to do so immediately, they should create a climate of mutual trust for holding the aforementioned three-party talks and, in military actions, adopt a cautious attitude to positively influence the multi-channel dialogues currently underway between North and South Korea.

### 朝鲜《劳动新闻》敦促美国参加三方会谈  把朝鲜半岛变成无核区

1989-12-09
第4版(国际新闻)
专栏：

    朝鲜《劳动新闻》敦促美国参加三方会谈
    把朝鲜半岛变成无核区
    新华社平壤12月7日电  朝鲜《劳动新闻》今天发表文章，敦促美国同朝鲜南北方举行三方会谈，讨论把朝鲜半岛变为无核区的问题。
    朝鲜政府1986年6月提出了把朝鲜半岛变成无核区的建议。一个月前，朝鲜外交部又发表声明，呼吁美国和南朝鲜同朝鲜北方就实现这一建议进行三方会谈。但是，美国方面至今尚未做出任何答复。
    《劳动新闻》的文章说，实现朝鲜半岛无核化不仅是为维持与巩固朝鲜的和平，而且也是为了保障亚洲与世界的和平。美国作为在南朝鲜部署大量核武器的当事人，它对于实现朝鲜半岛的无核化问题负有不可推卸的责任。
    文章说，如果美国真正希望朝鲜半岛保持和平，就应该响应朝鲜北方的建议。如果他们立即这样做有困难，也应该为举行上述三方会谈造成相互信赖的气氛，并为给目前朝鲜南北方正在进行着的多渠道对话造成良好的影响而在军事行动方面采取谨慎行事的态度。
