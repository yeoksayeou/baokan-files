Title: The Annual Eid al-Adha Festival
Author: Zhang Xiaolong
Date: 1989-12-15
Edition: 7

The annual Eid al-Adha festival is the most solemn traditional celebration in Bangladesh. The photo shows farmers from the outskirts of Dhaka leading their decorated "sacrificial cows" to the city market on the eve of this year's Eid al-Adha.
