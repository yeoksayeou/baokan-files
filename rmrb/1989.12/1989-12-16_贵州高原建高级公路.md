Title: Construction of High-Grade Highway in Guizhou Plateau
Author: Zhang Bing Sen, Zhou Xiao Nong
Date: 1989-12-16
Edition: 2

A wide and smooth high-grade highway has added a new modern look to the Guizhou Plateau.

This high-grade highway, which connects the provincial capital Guiyang with the famous scenic area of Huangguoshu, is 141.7 kilometers long, with a width ranging from 8.5 meters to 27 meters, and a straight route. Among them, 26 kilometers are first-class roads, and 115.7 kilometers are second-class roads.

### 贵州高原建高级公路
张炳森  周晓农
1989-12-16
第2版(国内新闻)
专栏：四面八方

    贵州高原建高级公路
    据新华社贵阳12月15日电　（记者张炳森、周晓农）一条宽阔平整的高等级公路，为贵州高原增添了迈向现代化的新景象。
    这条由省城贵阳通向著名风景区黄果树的高等级公路全长141.7公里，路宽8.5米至27米，路线平直。其中一级路为26公里，二级路为115.7公里。
