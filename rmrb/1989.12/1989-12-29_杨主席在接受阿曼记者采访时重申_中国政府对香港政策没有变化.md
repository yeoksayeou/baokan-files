Title: Yang Reiterates China's Unchanged Policy on Hong Kong
Author: Yang Cibó, Yu Dàbō
Date: 1989-12-29
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Muscat, December 27th.

Chinese President Yang Shangkun, who is currently visiting Oman, reiterated today that China's policy on Hong Kong remains unchanged. He urged Omani journalists not to believe rumors to the contrary.

Yang Shangkun said in response to a journalist's question that, in accordance with the Sino-British Joint Declaration, the Chinese government will resume sovereignty over Hong Kong in 1997. The basic principle for resolving the Hong Kong issue is "one country, two systems," which means that the mainland will practice socialism while Hong Kong can continue with capitalism.

Yang Shangkun stated, "To ensure Hong Kong's prosperity and stability, we have stipulated that this policy will remain unchanged for 50 years." He added, "We are currently drafting the Basic Law for the Hong Kong Special Administrative Region to legally enshrine the 'one country, two systems' policy and the 50-year commitment."

In response to questions about the situation in Eastern Europe, Yang Shangkun said that over the past few months, Eastern European countries have undergone profound changes, driven by both internal and external factors. He stated, "The path these countries will take is for their own people to decide. China has never interfered in the internal affairs of other countries. China hopes to maintain friendly relations with these countries."

### 杨主席在接受阿曼记者采访时重申  中国政府对香港政策没有变化
周慈朴  于大波
1989-12-29
第1版(要闻)
专栏：

    杨主席在接受阿曼记者采访时重申
    中国政府对香港政策没有变化
    新华社马斯喀特12月27日电　（记者周慈朴、于大波）正在这里访问的中国国家主席杨尚昆今天晚上在接受阿曼记者采访时重申，中国政府对香港的政策没有变化。他请阿曼记者不要听信有关这方面的谣传。
    杨尚昆主席在回答记者问题时说，根据中英联合公报，中国政府将于1997年收回对香港的主权。解决香港问题的基本原则是一国两制，这就是说，大陆实行社会主义制度，香港可以实行资本主义制度。
    杨尚昆说：“为了香港的繁荣与稳定，我们规定这一政策50年不变。”“我们正在制定香港特别行政区的基本法，就是要把实行一国两制的方针和50年不变的承诺用法律形式规定下来。”在回答有关东欧局势的问题时，杨尚昆说，最近几个月，东欧国家发生了深刻的变化。这些变化既有内部原因，也有外部原因。他说，东欧国家到底要走什么道路，这由本国人民自己去决定。中国从来不干涉别国的内政。中国希望同这些国家继续保持友好关系。
