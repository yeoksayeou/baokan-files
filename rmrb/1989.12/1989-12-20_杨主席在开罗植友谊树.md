Title: Yang Shangkun Plants a Friendship Tree in Cairo
Author: Jiang Yaping
Date: 1989-12-20
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Cairo, December 19th.

Today, the lush and beautiful Cairo International Park added a magnolia tree symbolizing the friendship between China and Egypt.

At around 1 PM, Yang Shangkun, who is on an official friendly visit to Egypt, planted the magnolia tree with the assistance of Mohamed Abdel Wahab, the Egyptian minister of industry and the head of the Egyptian escort team. The tree was brought from far-away China.

Under the bright sunlight, Yang, dressed in a black suit, said while planting the tree, "I will add more soil. I hope this magnolia tree will grow well and bloom in Egyptian soil."

Egyptian friends present expressed that the Egyptian people will definitely take good care of this tree, ensuring it grows well, as it symbolizes the friendship between the Egyptian and Chinese peoples.

The park already has 14 friendship trees planted by foreign leaders. The magnolia tree planted by Yang today is the 15th, and the first friendship tree from China.

### 杨主席在开罗植友谊树
江亚平
1989-12-20
第4版(国际新闻)
专栏：

    杨主席在开罗植友谊树
    新华社开罗12月19日电（记者江亚平）今天，葱茏秀丽的开罗国际公园增加了一棵象征中埃两国友谊的玉兰树。
    中午1时许，正在埃及进行正式友好访问的杨尚昆主席在埃及陪同团团长、工业部长穆罕默德·阿卜杜勒·瓦哈卜的陪同下，来到位于开罗市东北部纳赛尔城的国际公园，亲手种下了这棵从万里之外的中国运来的玉兰树。
    身穿黑色西服的杨主席在灿烂的阳光下一边培土浇水，一边说：“我要多培些土，希望这棵玉兰在埃及的土地上很好地生长、开花。”
    在场的埃及朋友表示，埃及人民一定会好好照料这棵树，让它顺利地成长，因为它象征着埃中两国人民的友谊。
    国际公园的芳草地上已经生长着14棵外国元首栽种的友谊树。杨主席今天种下的玉兰是第15棵，也是来自中国的第一棵友谊树。
