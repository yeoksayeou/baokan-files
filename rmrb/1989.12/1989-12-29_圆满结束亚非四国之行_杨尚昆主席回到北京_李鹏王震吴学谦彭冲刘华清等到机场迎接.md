Title: Chairman Yang Shangkun Returns to Beijing After Successful Visit to Four Asian and African Countries
Author: Yú Dàbō Yú Kāiyuán
Date: 1989-12-29
Edition: 1

Chairman Yang Shangkun has successfully concluded his official friendly visits to Egypt, the United Arab Emirates, Kuwait, and Oman. He returned to Beijing by special plane tonight.

Premier Li Peng, Vice Chairman Wang Zheng, Vice Premier Wu Xueqian, Vice Chairman of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress Peng Chong, and Vice Chairman of the Central Military Commission Liu Huaqing, among others, went to the airport to welcome Chairman Yang Shangkun.

The ambassadors of the aforementioned four countries to China also went to the airport to welcome Chairman Yang Shangkun.

According to Xinhua News Agency, Masqat, December 28 (reporters Yu Dabo, Yu Kaiyuan), Chairman Yang Shangkun departed from the capital of Oman, Masqat, by special plane this morning.

Chairman Yang's 11-day visit to four countries in the Middle East and North Africa was a complete success. It enhanced the understanding and friendship between the Chinese people and the people of the four countries, and strengthened the unity and cooperation between China and the four countries.

During the visit, Chairman Yang held talks with the leaders of the four countries, exchanged views on bilateral relations and issues of common concern, and reached broad consensus.

### 圆满结束亚非四国之行  杨尚昆主席回到北京  李鹏王震吴学谦彭冲刘华清等到机场迎接
于大波  喻开元
1989-12-29
第1版(要闻)
专栏：

    圆满结束亚非四国之行
    杨尚昆主席回到北京
    李鹏王震吴学谦彭冲刘华清等到机场迎接
    新华社北京12月28日电  国家主席杨尚昆圆满结束了对埃及、阿联酋、科威特和阿曼4国的正式友好访问，于今晚乘专机回到北京。
    国务院总理李鹏、国家副主席王震、副总理吴学谦、全国人大常委会副委员长彭冲、中央军委副主席刘华清等到机场迎接杨尚昆主席。
    上述4国驻华使节也到机场迎接杨尚昆主席。
    据新华社马斯喀特12月28日电　（记者于大波、喻开元）杨尚昆主席是今天上午乘专机离开阿曼首都马斯喀特回国的。
    杨主席历时11天的西亚、北非4国之行取得了圆满成功，增进了中国人民同4国人民的了解和友谊，加强了中国同4国的团结和合作。
    访问期间，杨主席同4国领导人进行了会谈，就双边关系和共同关心的问题交换了意见，取得了广泛一致的看法。
