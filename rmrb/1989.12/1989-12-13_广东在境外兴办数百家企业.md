Title: Guangdong Establishes Hundreds of Enterprises Overseas
Author: Chen Bingguang
Date: 1989-12-13
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Guangzhou, December 12th.

Guangdong has shifted from waiting for customers to come to it to actively exploring international markets. The province has established hundreds of enterprises overseas, bringing its total export trade to over $7.4 billion, ranking first in the nation.

### 广东在境外兴办数百家企业
陈炳光
1989-12-13
第1版(要闻)
专栏：

    广东在境外兴办数百家企业
    据新华社广州12月12日电　（记者陈炳光）广东改变等客上门做生意的办法，积极开拓国际市场，在境外兴办了数百家企业，使全省目前出口贸易已达74亿多美元，居全国首位。
