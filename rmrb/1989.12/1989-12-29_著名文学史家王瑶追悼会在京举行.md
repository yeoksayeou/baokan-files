Title: Renowned Literary Historian Wang Yao's Funeral Held in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1989-12-29
Edition: 2

Renowned literary historian Wang Yao passed away from illness on December 13 in Shanghai's Huadong Hospital at the age of 75. Wang Yao's funeral was held this afternoon at the Baobao Mountain Revolutionary Cemetery in Beijing.

Wang Yao was a member of the second, sixth, and seventh National Committee of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference, a member of the Central Committee of the China Democratic League, a council member of the Chinese Writers' Association, the president of the Chinese Modern Literature Research Association, and a professor at Peking University.

Wang Yao was born in Pingyao, Shanxi Province, in 1914. In the mid-1940s, he studied medieval literature under Professor Zhu Ziqing. After graduation, he has since worked at the Department of Chinese Language and Literature at Tsinghua University and Peking University, conducting research and teaching in classical Chinese literature and modern literature. He has achieved remarkable results in the fields of medieval literature research, Lu Xun studies, and modern literature research.

Wang Yao was upright and pursued progress throughout his life. He consistently supported the Party and socialism, actively participated in the work of the Political Consultative Conference and the Democratic League, and made contributions to the construction of socialist spiritual civilization.

Vice Chairman of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress Fei Xiaotong, Vice Chairman of the National Committee of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference Qian Weichang, Vice Secretary-General Ye Zishan, Peking University President Wu Shuqing, as well as more than 700 literary figures and Peking University faculty and students attended the funeral.

The National Committee of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference, the United Front Work Department of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of China, the National Federation of Literary and Art Circles, the Beijing Municipal Committee of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference, and others, as well as Xia Yan, Cao Yu, and others sent wreaths.

### 著名文学史家王瑶追悼会在京举行

1989-12-29
第2版(国内新闻)
专栏：

    著名文学史家王瑶追悼会在京举行
    新华社北京12月27日电　著名文学史家王瑶因病于12月13日在上海华东医院逝世，终年75岁。王瑶先生追悼会今天下午在北京八宝山革命公墓礼堂举行。
    王瑶是第二、六、七届全国政协委员，中国民主同盟中央委员，中国作家协会理事，中国现代文学研究会会长，北京大学教授。
    王瑶先生1914年生于山西平遥。四十年代中期师从朱自清先生攻读中古文学，毕业后至今，先后在清华大学中文系、北京大学中文系担任中国古典文学、现代文学的研究与教学工作。他在中古文学研究、鲁迅研究及现代文学研究等领域，取得了引人瞩目的成就。
    王瑶先生一生光明磊落，追求进步，一直拥护党和社会主义，积极参与政协和民盟的工作，为社会主义精神文明建设作出了贡献。
    人大常委会副委员长费孝通，全国政协副主席钱伟长、副秘书长叶至善，北大校长吴树青，以及文学界知名人士和北大师生700多人参加了追悼仪式。
    全国政协、中共中央统战部、全国文联、北京市政协等单位和夏衍、曹禺等送了花圈。
