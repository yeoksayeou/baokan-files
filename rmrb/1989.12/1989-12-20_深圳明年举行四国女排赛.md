Title: Shenzhen to Host Four-Nation Women's Volleyball Tournament Next Year
Author:
Date: 1989-12-20
Edition: 3

The 1990 Shenzhen International Women's Volleyball Four-Strong Tournament will be held from May 20 to 22 next year at the Shenzhen Sports Gymnasium. The tournament will feature the world's top four women's volleyball teams: Cuba, the Soviet Union, China, and Japan.

### 深圳明年举行四国女排赛

1989-12-20
第3版(综合)
专栏：体育

    深圳明年举行四国女排赛
    1990年深圳国际女排四强赛定于明年5月20日至22日在深圳体育馆举行，届时将有世界女子排坛四强古巴、苏联、中国和日本队参加。
            （据新华社）
