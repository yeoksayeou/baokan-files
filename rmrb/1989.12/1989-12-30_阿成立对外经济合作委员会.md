Title: Albania Establishes Committee for Foreign Economic Cooperation
Author:
Date: 1989-12-30
Edition: 4

The 28th session of the 11th People's Assembly of Albania, which concluded in Tirana, approved the establishment of the Albanian Committee for Foreign Economic Cooperation.

In recent years, Albania has actively engaged in foreign economic exchanges and cooperation, establishing government-level economic, industrial, and technological cooperation mixed committees with more than ten countries. This year's foreign trade volume has increased by nearly 30% compared to last year. (According to Xinhua News Agency)

### 阿成立对外经济合作委员会

1989-12-30
第4版(国际新闻)
专栏：

    阿成立对外经济合作委员会
    二十八日在地拉那闭幕的阿尔巴尼亚十一届人民议会第六次会议批准成立了阿对外经济合作委员会。
    最近一两年，阿尔巴尼亚积极开展对外经济交流与合作，已同十几个国家建立了政府间经济、工业和技术合作混合委员会，今年的外贸额比去年增长近百分之三十。　（据新华社）
