Title: Salvador Guerrillas Oppose U.S. and Soviet Interference in Domestic Affairs
Author:
Date: 1989-12-04
Page: 4

Salvadoran guerrillas spokesman Hernandez said on the second that the United States and the Soviet Union should not interfere in El Salvador's civil war, as it is an internal matter for El Salvador.

When discussing the possibility of U.S. and Soviet leaders reaching an agreement on resolving armed conflicts in Central America during their summit in Malta, the spokesman said that the civil war in El Salvador can only be resolved by the Salvadoran people themselves and not by the two superpowers.

### 萨游击队反对美苏干涉内政

1989-12-04
第4版(国际新闻)
专栏：

    萨游击队反对美苏干涉内政
    萨尔瓦多游击队发言人埃尔南德斯二日在墨西哥首都墨西哥城说，美国和苏联不应该干涉萨尔瓦多的内战，因为这是萨尔瓦多的内部事务。
    在谈到美苏首脑有可能在马耳他会晤中就解决中美洲武装冲突问题达成协议时，这位发言人说，萨尔瓦多的内战只能由萨尔瓦多人民自己来解决，不能由两个超级大国来解决。
