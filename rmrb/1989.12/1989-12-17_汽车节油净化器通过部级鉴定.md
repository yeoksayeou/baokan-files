Title: Automobile Fuel-Saving Purifier Passes Ministry-Level Appraisal
Author: Zhang Yaxin
Date: 1989-12-17
Edition: 2

	The automobile fuel-saving purifier developed jointly by Beijing Yuan Tong Consulting Company, Chengdu Zhong Yuan Hardware Electrical Appliance Company, and China Machinery and Electrical Advertising Company passed a ministry-level appraisal in Beijing on December 12. This fuel-saving purifier has a stable fuel-saving rate of 10%–15% and has no adverse effects on the engine. It can be widely used in automobiles, tractors, and other vehicles.

### 汽车节油净化器通过部级鉴定
张雅心
1989-12-17
第2版(国内新闻)
专栏：

    汽车节油净化器通过部级鉴定
    本报讯　记者张雅心报道：由北京远通咨询开发公司、成都中源五金交电公司和中国机电广告公司联合研制汽车动力补尝节油净化器12月12日在北京通过部级鉴定。这种节油净化器节油率稳定在10％—15％，对发动机无任何不利影响，可广泛用于汽车、拖拉机等车辆。
