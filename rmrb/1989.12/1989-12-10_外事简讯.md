Title: Foreign Affairs Briefing
Author:
Date: 1989-12-10
Edition: 2

	▲	On the afternoon of December 9, Wang Renzhi, a member of the Standing Committee of the Political Bureau of the CPC Central Committee, met with a delegation from the Democratic Yemen Party and Youth, led by Saleh Shaif Hussein, a member of the Central Committee of the Democratic Yemen Socialist Party and the first secretary of the "Fatah" Socialist Youth Union. The delegation was invited by the International Liaison Department of the CPC Central Committee and the Communist Youth League Central Committee and arrived in Beijing on December 7.

	▲	The minutes of the fifth round of cultural exchange consultations between the Chinese and Japanese governments were signed in Beijing on December 8.

	▲	Wang Hanbin, vice-chairman of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress, met with a delegation from the Burundi-China Friendship Association, led by Chairman Didace Rudaragija, at the Great Hall of the People on December 8. The Burundi guests were in China at the invitation of the Chinese People's Association for Friendship with Foreign Countries. (Xinhua News Agency)

### 外事简讯

1989-12-10
第2版(国内新闻)
专栏：

    外事简讯
    ▲中共中央书记处候补书记温家宝9日下午在人民大会堂会见了以民主也门社会党中央委员、“法塔赫”社会主义青年联合会第一书记萨利赫·沙伊夫·侯赛因为团长的民主也门党和青年代表团。代表团应中联部和团中央的邀请来访，于7日抵京。
    ▲第五次中日政府间文化交流协商会议纪要8日在北京签署。
    ▲全国人大常委会副委员长王汉斌8日在人民大会堂会见由主席迪达斯·鲁达拉吉率领的布隆迪—中国友好协会代表团。
    布隆迪客人是应对外友协邀请来华访问的。　（新华社）
