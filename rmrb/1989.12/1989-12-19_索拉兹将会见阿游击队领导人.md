Title: Solarz to Meet with Afghan Guerrilla Leaders
Author:
Date: 1989-12-19
Page: 4

The chairman of the U.S. House of Representatives' Subcommittee on Asian and Pacific Affairs, Stephen Solarz, who is visiting Islamabad, is scheduled to meet with Afghan guerrilla leaders based in Pakistan on December 18. The meeting will discuss the proposal to establish a new government in the Afghan capital, Kabul. Solarz will also discuss the role of former King Zahir Shah in a new government that includes Afghan guerrilla leaders.

Prior to this, Solarz held in-depth discussions with Pakistani President Ghulam Ishaq Khan and Prime Minister Benazir Bhutto on the Afghan issue and other regional matters.

Diplomatic sources believe that Solarz's visit indicates that the United States is increasingly focusing on a political solution to the Afghan problem.

### 索拉兹将会见阿游击队领导人

1989-12-19
第4版(国际新闻)
专栏：

    索拉兹将会见阿游击队领导人
    正在伊斯兰堡访问的美国众议院亚洲和太平洋事务小组委员会主席斯蒂芬·索拉兹十八日将会见以巴基斯坦为基地的阿富汗游击队领导人并讨论在阿富汗首都喀布尔建立新政府的设想。索拉兹还将讨论前国王查希尔在有阿富汗游击队领导人参加的新政府里发挥作用的问题。
    在此之前，索拉兹分别同巴基斯坦总统伊沙克·汗和总理贝·布托就阿富汗问题以及其它区域性问题进行了深入的会谈。
    外交界人士认为，索拉兹的来访表明美国越来越注重政治解决阿富汗问题。
