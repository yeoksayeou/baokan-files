Title: Pakistan Produces Remote-Controlled High-Altitude Reconnaissance Aircraft
Author:
Date: 1989-12-14
Edition: 4

Pakistan has produced a type of unmanned, remote-controlled reconnaissance aircraft equipped with highly sensitive infrared cameras. This is a significant achievement for Pakistan in the field of battlefield reconnaissance.

The report states that the Pakistani Army's air defense forces successfully manufactured this aircraft in just three months. It can effectively conduct reconnaissance at an altitude of 30,000 meters and is equipped with a real-time data system that connects with ground stations.

The report says that this achievement places Pakistan among the few countries with high-altitude reconnaissance capabilities.

### 巴生产出遥控式高空侦察机

1989-12-14
第4版(国际新闻)
专栏：

    巴生产出遥控式高空侦察机
    据巴基斯坦联合通讯社十二日报道，巴基斯坦生产出一种装备有最敏感的红外线摄影机的无人驾驶遥控式侦察机。这是巴基斯坦在战场侦察方面获得的重大成就。
    报道说，巴基斯坦陆军防空部队在三个月内就成功地制造出这种飞机。它可以在三万米高空有效地进行侦察，机体有与地面站相联系的实时数据系统。
    报道说，这一成就使巴基斯坦进入了拥有高空侦察能力的少数国家之列。
