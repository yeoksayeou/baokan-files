Title: Ten Major Environmental Problems Facing Humanity
Author: Wang Ya
Date: 1989-12-10
Edition: 8

Humanity is facing ten major environmental problems:

1. Desertification of land.
2. Rapid deforestation.
3. Water resource crisis.
4. Species extinction.
5. Acid rain pollution.
6. Intensification of the "greenhouse effect."
7. Destruction of the ozone layer.
8. Soil erosion.
9. Toxic chemical pollution.
10. Waste disaster.
