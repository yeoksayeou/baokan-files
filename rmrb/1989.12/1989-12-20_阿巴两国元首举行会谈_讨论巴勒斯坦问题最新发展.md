Title: Leaders of Algeria and Palestine Hold Talks Discussing Latest Developments in the Palestinian Issue
Author:
Date: 1989-12-20
Edition: 4

Algerian President Chadli and Palestinian President Arafat held talks today in Algiers, discussing the latest developments in the Palestinian issue.

Arafat told the Algerian press after the talks that he and Chadli reviewed the situation in Arab lands, the uprising of the Palestinian people against the Israeli occupation forces, and the necessity of strengthening this uprising through various means. He also said he briefed Chadli on the important actions taken by the seven-nation committee supporting the Palestinian uprising within the Arab League to continue international support for the Palestinian uprising.

Arafat pointed out that both sides also discussed the resolutions passed at the recent special session of the Arab League Council of Ministers in Tunis regarding the Palestinian issue, including the dangerous memorandum distributed by the Israeli foreign minister at the United Nations. In this memorandum, the Israeli foreign minister announced for the first time the annexation of Arab-occupied territories to the Israeli government. Arafat added that both sides believed it was necessary to use the Arab-European dialogue scheduled for December 21 and 22 in Paris to strengthen support for the Palestinian cause and other Arab economic and political issues, to support the Arab three-party committee for resolving the Lebanese crisis, and to promote the work of the seven-nation committee of non-aligned countries responsible for the Palestinian issue.

### 阿巴两国元首举行会谈  讨论巴勒斯坦问题最新发展

1989-12-20
第4版(国际新闻)
专栏：

    阿巴两国元首举行会谈
    讨论巴勒斯坦问题最新发展
    新华社阿尔及尔12月18日电　阿尔及利亚总统沙德利今天在这里同巴勒斯坦国总统阿拉法特举行会谈，讨论了巴勒斯坦问题的最新发展。
    阿拉法特在会谈结束后向阿尔及利亚报界发表谈话说，他同沙德利回顾了阿拉伯领土上的局势、巴勒斯坦人民反对以色列占领军的起义以及通过各种手段加强这种起义的必要性。他还说，他向沙德利通报了阿盟支持巴勒斯坦起义7国委员会所采取的重要行动，以便在国际上继续支持巴勒斯坦起义。
    阿拉法特指出，双方还讨论了最近在突尼斯召开的阿盟部长理事会特别代表大会上就巴勒斯坦问题，其中包括以色列外长在联合国散发的危险备忘录所通过的决议。以色列外长在这份备忘录中首次宣布把阿拉伯被占领土归并以色列政府。阿拉法特还说，双方认为必须利用定于本月21日和22日在巴黎举行的阿拉伯—欧洲对话来加强对巴勒斯坦事业以及其他阿拉伯经济和政治问题的支持，加强对解决黎巴嫩危机的阿拉伯3方委员会的支持，并促进不结盟国家负责巴勒斯坦问题7国委员会的工作。
