Title: Japan's Criminal Cases Hit Post-War High in 1989
Author:
Date: 1989-12-25
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency,

Statistics released by the Japanese National Police Agency on the 22nd indicate that Japan's criminal cases this year will surpass last year's figures, setting a new post-war high. By the end of November, the total number of criminal cases nationwide had reached 1,529,600, an increase of 2.1% compared to the same period last year. The Police Agency estimates that by the end of the year, the total number of criminal cases will reach 1,675,000, thus exceeding the previous post-war high of 1,641,000 cases recorded in 1988.

### 日今年刑事案创战后最高纪录

1989-12-25
第4版(国际新闻)
专栏：

    日今年刑事案创战后最高纪录
    据日本警察厅二十二日发表的统计材料表明，日本今年刑事案件将超过去年，创战后最高纪录。到十一月底，今年日本全国刑事犯罪案件已达一百五十二点九六万起，比去年同期增加了百分之二点一。警察厅估计，到年底，刑事犯罪案件将达一百六十七点五万起，从而超过战后刑事犯罪案件最多的一九八八年的一百六十四点一万起。
                                    （据新华社）
