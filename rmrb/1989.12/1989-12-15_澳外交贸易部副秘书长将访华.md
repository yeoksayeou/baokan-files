Title: Australian Deputy Secretary of the Department of Foreign Affairs and Trade to Visit China
Author:
Date: 1989-12-15
Edition: 2

The spokesperson for the Ministry of Foreign Affairs revealed this afternoon that, at the request of the Australian side, China has agreed to the visit of the Australian Deputy Secretary of the Department of Foreign Affairs and Trade, Costello, from December 17 to 19. During this time, he will engage in consultations with the Chinese side on issues of mutual concern.

### 澳外交贸易部副秘书长将访华

1989-12-15
第2版(国内要闻)
专栏：

    澳外交贸易部副秘书长将访华
    本报北京12月14日讯　外交部发言人今天下午透露，应澳方要求，中国已同意澳大利亚外交贸易部副秘书长科斯特洛于12月17日至19日来华就共同关心的问题同中方进行磋商。
