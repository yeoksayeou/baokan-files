Title: Utilizing Taiyue Nuclear Male-Sterile Wheat to Create a New Breeding Method
Author: Yu Futan
Date: 1989-12-13
Edition: 2

	China has made significant progress in utilizing Taiyue nuclear male-sterile wheat for recurrent selection breeding. Over the past seven years, more than 40 new strains with superior traits such as high yield, drought resistance, and resistance to Fusarium head blight have been developed nationwide.

	Recurrent selection breeding for self-pollinating crops on a large scale has not been attempted internationally. China discovered Taiyue nuclear male-sterile wheat in Taiyue County, Shanxi Province, for the first time in 1972, providing favorable conditions for recurrent selection breeding in wheat. This new breeding method not only facilitates the accumulation of beneficial genes but also helps to break unfavorable trait correlations, promote the expression of latent genes, and optimize gene combinations. Since 1981, when nationwide collaborative research on Taiyue nuclear male-sterile wheat began, a series of new strains with superior traits such as high yield, disease resistance, salt tolerance, drought resistance, and high protein content have emerged.

	This achievement has attracted widespread attention from international peers. On April 4 of this year, Deng Jingyang, the researcher from the Chinese Academy of Agricultural Sciences who led this study, was appointed as a foreign member of the French Academy of Agricultural Sciences by French Prime Minister Jacques Chirac.

### 利用太谷核不育小麦  我首创轮回选择育种  已培育40多个性状优异的新品系
余富棠
1989-12-13
第2版(国内新闻)
专栏：

    利用太谷核不育小麦
    我首创轮回选择育种
    已培育40多个性状优异的新品系
    据新华社北京12月11日电　（记者余富棠）我国利用太谷核不育小麦开展轮回选择育种取得可喜进展。近7年，全国已培育出40多个分别具有丰产、抗旱、耐赤霉病等优异特性的新品系。
    自花授粉作物开展大规模的轮回选择育种，在国际上迄今尚无先例。我国于1972年在山西省太谷县首次发现太谷核不育小麦后，为小麦开展轮回选择育种提供了有利条件。这种新育种方法，不仅有利于有益基因的累加，而且有利于打破不利性状相关、促进潜伏基因表达和优化基因组合。1981年开展全国太谷核不育小麦协作研究以来，一批丰产、抗病、耐盐碱、抗旱、蛋白质含量高的性状优异的新品系相继问世。
    这项成果引起了国际同行们的广泛关注。今年4月4日，主持这项研究的中国农业科学院研究员邓景扬被法国总理罗卡尔批准聘任为法国农业科学院外籍院士。
