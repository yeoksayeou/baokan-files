Title: 1989 Chen Jiageng Award Announcement
Author: Chen Jinwu
Date: 1989-12-01
Edition: 3

The Chen Jiageng Foundation Council today announced the winners of the 1989 Chen Jiageng Award. Three research achievements in the fields of medical science, life science, and earth science have received this honor.

The three winning research achievements are: the "Radical Cure for Choriocarcinoma" research by Song Hongzhao, Wu Baozhen, and Wang Yuanlu from the Peking Union Medical College Hospital of the Chinese Academy of Medical Sciences; the "Quantitative Relationship Between Protein Functional Group Modification and Biological Activity" research by Zhou Chenglu from the Institute of Biophysics, Chinese Academy of Sciences; and the "Uplift of the Qinghai-Tibet Plateau and Its Impact on Natural Environment and Human Activities" research by Liu Dongsheng and Sun Honglie from the Chinese Academy of Sciences.

### 1989年度陈嘉庚奖评奖揭晓
陈金武
1989-12-01
第3版(综合)
专栏：

    1989年度陈嘉庚奖评奖揭晓
    据新华社北京11月30日电　（记者陈金武）陈嘉庚基金会理事会今天宣布，1989年度陈嘉庚奖评奖揭晓。医药科学、生命科学和地球科学领域的3项研究成果获得了这项奖励。
    这3项获奖研究成果是：中国医学科学院协和医院宋鸿钊、吴葆桢、王元萼的“根治绒癌”研究，中国科学院生物物理研究所邹承鲁的“蛋白质功能基团的修饰与其生物活性之间的定量关系”研究，中国科学院刘冬生、孙鸿烈的“青藏高原隆起及其对自然环境与人类活动的影响”的研究。
