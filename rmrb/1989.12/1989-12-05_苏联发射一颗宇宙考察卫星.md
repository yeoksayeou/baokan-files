Title: The Soviet Union Launches an Astronomic Observation Satellite
Author:
Date: 1989-12-05
Page: 4

Tass News Agency, December 2nd.

The Soviet Union's "Proton" carrier rocket successfully launched the "Garnets" astronomical observation satellite into orbit at 23:21 Moscow time on December 1st. The satellite's mission is to observe X-ray and soft gamma-ray sources in the universe.

### 苏联发射一颗宇宙考察卫星

1989-12-05
第4版(国际新闻)
专栏：

    苏联发射一颗宇宙考察卫星
    据塔斯社二日报道，苏联“质子”号运载火箭已于一日莫斯科时间二十三点二十一分将“石榴石”号天体物理考察卫星送上轨道。它的任务是考察伦琴射线和软伽玛射线宇宙源。
                                      （据新华社）
