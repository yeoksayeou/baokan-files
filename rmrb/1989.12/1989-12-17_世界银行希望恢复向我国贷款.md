Title: The World Bank Hopes to Resume Loans to China
Author:
Date: 1989-12-17
Edition: 4

The World Bank hopes to resume lending to China as soon as possible, World Bank President Barber Conable told reporters on December 14.

He said that World Bank experts have already analyzed China's economic development situation up to November 7.

Conable also said that 32 countries have agreed to provide $15.2 billion to the World Bank's affiliate, the International Development Association, over the next three years for lending to the world's poorest countries. China may receive about 15% of this funding.

### 世界银行希望恢复向我国贷款

1989-12-17
第4版(国际新闻)
专栏：

    世界银行希望恢复向我国贷款
    新华社华盛顿12月15日电  世界银行总裁巴伯·科纳布尔14日对记者说，世界银行希望尽快地恢复向中国贷款。
    他说，世界银行的专家已经对中国截至11月7日的经济发展情况进行了分析。
    科纳布尔还说，有32个国家已同意在今后3年内向世界银行分支机构——国际发展协会提供152亿美元，用于今后向世界最穷国家提供贷款，中国将可能得到这笔资金的约15％。
