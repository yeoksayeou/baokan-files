Title: Syrian Military Intelligence Headquarters in Lebanon Attacked, PLO Condemns and Demands Investigation
Author:
Date: 1989-12-16
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Tunis, December 14th.

According to a report by the Palestinian News Agency, the PLO issued a statement today in Tunis condemning an attack by unidentified armed individuals on the Syrian Military Intelligence Headquarters in Sidon, southern Lebanon, early this morning.

The statement said that the PLO has requested an investigation, in cooperation with the Lebanese National Army and Syrian forces stationed in Sidon, to identify the perpetrators and their accomplices.

The statement pointed out that such actions only serve the interests of common enemies. The statement also called on everyone to be vigilant against any attempts to create tension in this strategic region.

The statement noted that Israeli forces are bombing southern Lebanon daily as a prelude to launching a military operation.

According to the Tunisian African News Agency, about 50 armed individuals attacked the Syrian Military Intelligence Headquarters in southern Lebanon early this morning, killing at least 4 people and injuring 19, many of whom were Syrian soldiers.

### 驻黎叙军情报总部遭到袭击  巴解谴责并要求调查此事件

1989-12-16
第4版(国际新闻)
专栏：

    驻黎叙军情报总部遭到袭击
    巴解谴责并要求调查此事件
    新华社突尼斯12月14日电据巴勒斯坦通讯社报道，巴解组织今天在这里发表一项公报，谴责一伙身份不明的武装人员今天凌晨袭击设在黎巴嫩南部西顿的叙利亚情报总部。
    公报说，巴解组织已要求在驻西顿的黎巴嫩国民军和叙利亚部队的配合下进行调查，以查明这次袭击的肇事者及其同伙。
    公报指出，这样的行动只能为共同敌人的利益效劳。公报还说，巴解组织呼吁大家对在这一战略地区制造紧张局势的任何企图保持警惕。
    公报指出，以色列军队每天在黎巴嫩南部进行轰炸，以作为发动一次军事行动的序幕。
    据突尼斯非洲通讯社报道，50来名武装人员今天凌晨袭击了黎巴嫩南部的叙利亚情报总部，至少打死了4人和打伤了19人，其中许多是叙利亚士兵。
