Title: Saudi Arabian Foreign Minister Meets Chinese Commercial Representative
Author:
Date: 1989-12-28
Edition: 4

Saudi Arabian Foreign Minister Saud al-Faisal met with China's commercial representative to Saudi Arabia, Sun Bidigan, in Riyadh on December 26.

During the meeting, Sun Bidigan handed over a letter from Chinese Foreign Minister Qian Qichen to Foreign Minister Saud al-Faisal. Both sides engaged in friendly discussions. Sun Bidigan arrived in Riyadh, the capital of Saudi Arabia, on December 4.

### 沙特外交大臣会见我商务代表

1989-12-28
第4版(国际新闻)
专栏：

    沙特外交大臣会见我商务代表
    新华社北京12月27日电  利雅得消息：沙特阿拉伯外交大臣沙特·费萨尔26日在利雅得会见了中国驻沙特商务代表孙必干。
    会见时，孙必干递交了中国外长钱其琛致费萨尔外交大臣的信件。双方进行了友好交谈。孙必干是12月4日抵达沙特阿拉伯首都利雅得的。
