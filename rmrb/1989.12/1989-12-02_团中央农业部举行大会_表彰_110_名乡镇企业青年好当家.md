Title: The Central Committee of the Communist Youth League and the Ministry of Agriculture Hold a Conference to Honor 110 Outstanding Young Managers of Township Enterprises
Author: Hán Xiǎo Fēng
Date: 1989-12-02
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Suzhou, December 1st

10 outstanding national young township enterprise managers and 100 excellent national young township enterprise managers (directors) were honored today in Suzhou, Jiangsu Province, by the Central Committee of the Communist Youth League and the Ministry of Agriculture.

Vice Chairman of the Central Advisory Commission, Bo Yibo, sent a congratulatory message to those being honored, expressing his hope that they would "firmly implement the Central Committee's decision on further rectification and deepening reform, ensuring the orderly and healthy development of township enterprises, and making new contributions to the Four Modernizations."

Vice Minister of Agriculture Chen Yaobang, Secretary of the Secretariat of the Central Committee of the Communist Youth League Feng Jun, and relevant officials from Jiangsu Province and Suzhou City attended the conference. Feng Jun and Chen Yaobang both spoke at the meeting. They fully affirmed the role and position of young township enterprise managers (directors) in China's rural reform and economic construction, emphasizing the need to seriously implement the spirit of the Fifth Plenary Session of the 13th Central Committee, actively encouraging and guiding the healthy development of township enterprises, and cultivating and fostering a new generation of young agricultural entrepreneurs with the goal of "four haves." They hoped that the broad masses of township enterprise managers (directors) would, at a time when both the country and enterprises face difficulties and new challenges, be more spirited, work hard, and actively unite and lead all employees to overcome difficulties, making due contributions to the sustained, stable, coordinated, and healthy development of the national economy.

The 110 individuals honored today were selected from the "National Excellent Young Township Enterprise Managers (Directors)" evaluation activity jointly organized by the Central Committee of the Communist Youth League and the Ministry of Agriculture. They represent the 6 million young township enterprise managers nationwide. Their average age is 32.8 years, 84% are Communist Party members, and 76.3% have a college or higher education.

The 10 outstanding national young township enterprise managers honored today are: Xu Hanlin, Hu Hao, Wei Guangming, Yang Jikuai, Li Changyou, Liu Shizhong, Chen Jiancheng, Tang Qingyu, Wang Hongyuan, and Zhang Guoshu.

### 团中央农业部举行大会  表彰110名乡镇企业青年好当家
韩晓烽
1989-12-02
第1版(要闻)
专栏：

    团中央农业部举行大会
    表彰110名乡镇企业青年好当家
    新华社苏州12月1日电  （记者韩晓烽）10名全国明星青年乡镇企业家和百名全国优秀青年乡镇企业厂长（经理），今天在江苏省苏州市受到共青团中央和农业部的表彰。
    中顾委副主任薄一波写来贺信，向受到表彰的同志表示祝贺，希望他们“坚决贯彻执行中央关于进一步治理整顿和深化改革的决定，使乡镇企业有秩序地、健康地发展，为实现四化作出新贡献”。
    农业部副部长陈耀邦，共青团中央书记处书记冯军，以及江苏省、苏州市的有关负责同志出席了今天的大会。冯军和陈耀邦先后在会上讲了话。他们在讲话中充分肯定了乡镇企业青年厂长（经理）在我国农村改革和经济建设中的地位和作用，并强调指出，要认真贯彻落实党的十三届五中全会精神，积极鼓励和引导乡镇企业健康发展，以“四有”为目标，培养和造就一代新型的青年农民企业家。他们希望广大乡镇企业厂长（经理）在当前国家和企业都遇到困难，面临新的考验的时候，更要振奋精神，努力工作，积极团结和带领全体职工共渡难关，为国民经济持续、稳定、协调、健康地发展作出应有的贡献。
    今天受到表彰的110名同志，是从团中央和农业部联合举办的“全国优秀青年乡镇企业厂长（经理）”评选活动中评选出来的，在全国600万乡镇企业青年厂长中具有代表性。他们平均年龄32.8岁，共产党员占84％，大中专文化程度的占76.3％。
    10名受到表彰的全国明星青年乡镇企业家是：徐汉林、胡浩、魏光明、杨吉奎、李常友、刘世忠、陈建成、唐庆余、王洪元和张国淑。
