Title: Developing the Hundred-Mile Abandoned Yellow River
Author: Gǔ Línqīng Liú Jīnxīng
Date: 1989-12-09
Edition: 2

    The people of Suyang County in Jiangsu Province have mobilized 100,000 laborers to develop water conservancy projects on the 100-mile stretch of abandoned Yellow River land. In just over a month, they have developed more than 10,000 mu of rice fields and over 5,000 mu of fish ponds. The former wasteland has been transformed into a grid-like pattern of fertile fields.

### 泗阳开发百里废黄河
顾林青　刘金星
1989-12-09
第2版(国内新闻)
专栏：四面八方

    泗阳开发百里废黄河
    本报讯　江苏省泗阳县动员10万民工，在绵延百里的废黄河滩上兴修水利。短短一个多月时间，已开发稻田1万多亩、鱼池5000多亩，昔日荒滩变成了棋盘画格一样的良田。　（顾林青　刘金星）
