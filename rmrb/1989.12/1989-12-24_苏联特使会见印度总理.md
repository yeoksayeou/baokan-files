Title: Soviet Envoy Meets Indian Prime Minister
Author:
Date: 1989-12-24
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency,

Soviet envoy, First Deputy Foreign Minister Yuri Vorontsov, met with Indian Prime Minister V.P. Singh on December 22 in New Delhi, expressing the hope to maintain close relations with India.

According to a spokesman for the Indian Foreign Ministry, the Soviet envoy handed a letter from Soviet leader Gorbachev to the new Indian Prime Minister, requesting strengthened cooperation between the Soviet Union and India in all fields. Vorontsov emphasized the necessity of high-level political cooperation between the two countries on regional and international issues. During his talks with Indian Foreign Minister Gujral, both sides agreed to maintain regular contact between the two foreign ministries. Both sides hope to strengthen cooperation in the economic field, including high-tech fields.

### 苏联特使会见印度总理

1989-12-24
第4版(国际新闻)
专栏：

    苏联特使会见印度总理
    苏联特使、外交部第一副部长尤里·沃龙佐夫十二月二十二日在新德里会见了印度总理维·普·辛格，表示希望继续保持同印度的密切关系。
    据印度外交部发言人说，苏联特使向印度新总理递交了苏联领导人戈尔巴乔夫的一封信，要求在所有领域加强苏印合作。沃龙佐夫强调了两国在区域性和国际问题上进行高层政治合作的必要性。他在同印度外长古杰拉尔举行的会谈中，一致同意两国外交部保持经常性接触。双方希望，在经济领域、包括高科技在内的科技领域加强合作。
                                      （据新华社）
