　　International Briefs

　　Ecuadorian City Opens Chinese Bookstore

　　Quito Dispatch: A bookstore selling Chinese books recently opened in Esmeraldas, in northern Ecuador. The opening of this bookstore, named "Great Light," was welcomed by the masses in Esmeraldas.

　　In this bookstore, people can buy Mao Zedong's works, the Land Reform Law of the People's Republic of China, and other Chinese books, as well as reproductions of Chinese artworks, such as collections of paintings by Qi Baishi and Chen Banding. They can also subscribe to Chinese magazines.

　　U.S. Economic Delegation Visits Damascus

　　Cairo Dispatch: After being active in Cairo for nearly a month, a U.S. economic delegation composed of influential businessmen left Cairo for Damascus on the 12th.

　　Before departing, the head of the delegation, Paul H. Hauk, Director of the U.S. Bureau of Foreign Trade, said at a press conference that "American companies and UAR companies can cooperate in various ways." He said that this "cooperation" could take several forms: establishing joint ventures, U.S. companies participating in UAR construction projects, or granting UAR companies the right to manufacture certain products of U.S. companies.

    国际简讯
    厄瓜多尔一城市开设中国书店
    基多讯　在厄瓜多尔北部的埃斯美拉尔达斯，最近新开设了一家销售中国书籍的书店。这家名叫“大光明”的书店在埃斯美拉尔达斯的开幕受到广大群众的欢迎。
    人们在这家书店里可以买到毛泽东的著作，中华人民共和国土地改革法等中国书籍，中国艺术作品的复制品，例如齐白石和陈半丁的画集等，还可以订阅中国杂志。
    美经济代表团访问大马士革
    开罗讯　由一批有势力的商人组成的美国经济代表团在开罗活动近一个月后，12日离开开罗前往大马士革。
    代表团团长、美国对外贸易局局长保罗·霍克在动身之前举行的记者招待会上说，“美国公司和阿联公司有各种不同的方式可以进行合作。”他说，这种“合作”可以采取这样几种形式：建立合营公司，美国公司参加阿联的建设工程或者给予阿联公司制造美国公司某些产品的权利。
