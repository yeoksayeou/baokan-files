Title: China and Czechoslovakia Sign Trade Protocol for This Year; Vice Premier Xi Zhongxun Receives Czechoslovak Government Trade Delegation
Author:
Date: 1960-02-03
Page: 4

    China and Czechoslovakia Sign Trade Protocol for This Year
    Vice Premier Xi Zhongxun Receives Czechoslovak Government Trade Delegation
    Xinhua News Agency, February 2nd: The 1960 protocol on the exchange of goods and payments between the People's Republic of China and the Czechoslovak Republic was signed in Beijing today.
    Representing the Chinese side in signing the protocol was Lei Renmin, Acting Minister of Foreign Trade of the People's Republic of China; representing the Czechoslovak side in signing the protocol was František Krajčír, Minister of Foreign Trade of the Czechoslovak Republic.
    Attending the signing ceremony on the Chinese side were Lin Haiyun, Vice Minister of Foreign Trade, Chen Boqing, Deputy Director of the Soviet Union and Eastern Europe Department of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs, and others. On the Czechoslovak side were Czechoslovak Ambassador to China, Bušniak, and all members of the Czechoslovak Government Trade Delegation.
    Xinhua News Agency, February 2nd: Vice Premier of the State Council Xi Zhongxun received František Krajčír, Minister of Foreign Trade of the Czechoslovak Republic, and all members of the Czechoslovak Government Trade Delegation led by him this afternoon.
    Present at the reception were Lin Haiyun, Vice Minister of Foreign Trade, Jia Shi, Director of the Second Bureau; Chen Boqing, Deputy Director of the Soviet Union and Eastern Europe Department of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs, and others.
    Czechoslovak Ambassador to China Bušniak was also present.

### 中捷签订今年贸易议定书  习仲勋副总理接见捷政府贸易代表团

1960-02-03
第4版()
专栏：

    中捷签订今年贸易议定书
    习仲勋副总理接见捷政府贸易代表团
    新华社2日讯　中华人民共和国和捷克斯洛伐克共和国1960年交换货物和付款议定书今天在北京签字。
    代表中国方面在议定书上签字的，是中华人民共和国对外贸易部代理部长雷任民；代表捷克斯洛伐克方面在议定书上签字的，是捷克斯洛伐克共和国对外贸易部部长克拉伊契尔。
    参加签字仪式的，中国方面有对外贸易部副部长林海云、外交部苏联东欧司副司长陈伯清等，捷克斯洛伐克方面有捷克斯洛伐克驻中国大使布希尼亚克和捷克斯洛伐克政府贸易代表团的全体人员。
    新华社2日讯　国务院副总理习仲勋今日下午接见了捷克斯洛伐克共和国对外贸易部部长弗·克拉伊契尔和由他率领的捷克斯洛伐克政府贸易代表团全体团员。
    接见时在座的有，对外贸易部副部长林海云、第二局局长贾石；外交部苏联东欧司副司长陈伯清等。
    捷克斯洛伐克驻我国大使布希尼亚克也在座。
