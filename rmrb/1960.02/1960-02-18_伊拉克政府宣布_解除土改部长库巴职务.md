Title: Iraqi Government Announces Dismissal of Land Reform Minister Kuba
Author:
Date: 1960-02-18
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, Baghdad, February 16th: Baghdad Radio broadcast tonight a republican decree announcing the dismissal of Ibrahim Kuba from his posts as Minister of Land Reform and acting Minister of Petroleum.

The decree stated that this decision was made "based on the public interest and the recommendation of the Prime Minister."

The decree appointed Minister of Planning Talat Shebani as acting Minister of Petroleum, and Minister of Interior Ahmed Yehia as acting Minister of Land Reform.

### 伊拉克政府宣布  解除土改部长库巴职务

1960-02-18
第6版()
专栏：

    伊拉克政府宣布
    解除土改部长库巴职务
    新华社巴格达16日电　巴格达电台今晚广播的一项共和国命令宣布解除易卜拉欣·库巴的土地改革部长和代理石油部长的职务。
    这项命令说，这项决定是“根据公众利益和总理的建议”作出的。
    命令任命计划部长塔拉特·谢巴尼为代理石油部长，内政部长艾哈迈德·叶海亚代理土地改革部长。
