Title: Italian Communist Party Holds Ninth Congress, to Hear and Discuss Togliatti's Report
Author:
Date: 1960-02-01
Page: 5

　　Xinhua News Agency, Rome, January 31st: According to TASS, the Ninth Congress of the Italian Communist Party opened in Rome on the 30th. Attending the congress were 1,100 delegates with voting and speaking rights, who will hear and discuss the report by Palmiro Togliatti, General Secretary of the Central Committee of the Italian Communist Party: "Striving for Democratic Renewal of Italian Society and Advancing Towards Socialism," and elect new leading bodies of the party.

　　Representatives of Communist and Workers' Parties from other countries are participating in the congress, including a delegation from the Communist Party of the Soviet Union led by Mikhail Suslov, member of the Presidium of the Central Committee and Secretary of the Central Committee of the CPSU.

　　The congress has attracted great attention from the public. More than 2,000 representatives of various Italian political parties, social organizations, and activists of the workers' movement are attending the congress as guests.

　　The opening of the meeting was presided over by Traccini, the oldest activist in the Italian workers' movement and a member of the leading bodies of the Italian Communist Party.

### 意共举行第九次代表大会  将听取和讨论陶里亚蒂的报告

1960-02-01
第5版()
专栏：

    意共举行第九次代表大会
    将听取和讨论陶里亚蒂的报告
    新华社31日讯　据塔斯社罗马讯：意大利共产党第九次代表大会30日在罗马开幕。出席代表大会的有一千一百名有表决权和发言权的代表，他们将听取和讨论意大利共产党中央委员会总书记陶里亚蒂的报告：“争取意大利社会的民主革新、争取向社会主义前进”，并选举党的新的领导机构。
    参加代表大会的有其他国家共产党和工人党的代表，其中包括以苏共中央委员会主席团委员、中央委员会书记苏斯洛夫为首的苏联共产党代表团。
    代表大会受到社会公众的极大注意。作为客人列席大会的有意大利各政党代表、社会团体代表以及工人运动和积极分子二千多人。
    意大利工人运动最年长的活动家、意大利共产党领导机构的成员特拉齐尼主持会议开幕。
