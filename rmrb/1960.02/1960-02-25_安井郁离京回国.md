Title: Yasui Iku Departs Beijing for Home

Author:

Date: 1960-02-25
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, February 23rd – Yasui Iku, Director of the Japan Council Against Atomic and Hydrogen Bombs, who visited China at the invitation of the Chinese People's Committee for World Peace, and Yoshida Yoshiaki, Deputy Secretary-General of the Council's Secretariat, left Beijing by plane today via Guangzhou to return to their country.

Those who saw them off at the airport included Zheng Senyu, Yang Jin, Yang Chunson, Qi Mugang, and other representatives from the Chinese People's Committee for World Peace and the Chinese Asian-African Solidarity Committee. Japanese peace activist Kinkazu Saionji and Korean peace activist Hwang Bong-koo, who are in Beijing, also saw them off at the airport.

### 安井郁离京回国

1960-02-25
第5版()
专栏：

    安井郁离京回国
    新华社23日讯　应中国人民保卫世界和平委员会邀请来我国访问的日本禁止原子弹和氢弹协议会理事长安井郁，和协议会事务局次长吉田嘉清，今天乘飞机离开北京经广州回国。
    到机场欢送的，有中国人民保卫世界和平委员会和中国亚非团结委员会各方面人士郑森禹、杨进、杨春松、戚慕光等。在北京的日本和平人士西园寺公一和朝鲜和平人士黄凤九也到机场欢送。
