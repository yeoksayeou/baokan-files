Title: Soviet-Chinese Friendship Association Delegation Leaves Beijing for Home
Author:
Date: 1960-02-24
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, February 23rd. The delegation of the Soviet-Chinese Friendship Association, led by Yelyutin, Vice Chairman of the Council of the Soviet-Chinese Friendship Association and Minister of Higher and Secondary Specialized Education of the Soviet Union, left Beijing by plane this morning to return home.

Those who went to the airport to see them off included Li Dequan and Shao Lizi, Vice Presidents of the Sino-Soviet Friendship Association; Yang Xiufeng, Minister of Education; Qian Junrui, Secretary-General of the Sino-Soviet Friendship Association; Ge Baoquan and Li Zhanwu, Deputy Secretaries-General; Yang Shu, Vice President of the Beijing Sino-Soviet Friendship Association; and representatives from relevant departments.

Soviet Ambassador to China Chervonenko and embassy counselors Antonov, Sudarikov, and Safronov also went to the airport to see them off.

### 苏中友协代表团离京回国

1960-02-24
第4版()
专栏：

    苏中友协代表团离京回国
    新华社23日讯　由苏中友好协会理事会副主席、苏联高等教育和中等专业教育部部长叶留金率领的苏中友好协会代表团，今天上午乘飞机离开北京回国。
    到机场欢送的，有中苏友好协会总会副会长李德全、邵力子，教育部部长杨秀峰，中苏友好协会总会秘书长钱俊瑞，副秘书长戈宝权、李沾吾，北京市中苏友好协会副会长杨述，以及有关方面的代表。
    苏联驻中国大使契尔沃年科和大使馆参赞安东诺夫、苏达利柯夫、萨福隆也到机场送行。
