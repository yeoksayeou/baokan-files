Title: Only friends can truly help; jackals and wolves show no mercy.
Author:
Date: 1960-02-20
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, February 19th: According to Soviet newspapers, the Soviet Union's economic cooperation with other countries will have new developments this year, and it will help 22 countries build 383 industrial enterprises and other construction projects.

This year, the economic ties between the Soviet Union and various socialist countries still occupy the main position in the Soviet Union's foreign economic relations. The Soviet Union will help various socialist countries build 288 industrial enterprises and engineering projects this year, and the trade volume with these countries will account for three-quarters of the Soviet Union's total foreign trade.

In 1960, the Soviet Union will provide many complete sets of machinery and equipment to fraternal countries such as China, Poland, Romania, and Bulgaria. The specialization and collaboration of production between the Soviet Union and various fraternal countries will also be carried out on a broader scale. According to the decision of the Council for Mutual Economic Assistance composed of European socialist countries, the Soviet Union will provide the German Democratic Republic, Poland, Czechoslovakia, and other member countries of the Council for Mutual Economic Assistance with increasing amounts of various types of equipment, iron ore and manganese ore, petroleum, coke, steel, steel products, cotton, grain, etc. in this year and in the years to come. The Soviet Union will also import chemical industry equipment, instruments, forging equipment, transportation equipment, and light industrial raw materials from various fraternal countries.

The economic ties between the Soviet Union and economically underdeveloped countries in Asia and Africa will also be further developed this year. This year, the Soviet Union will provide technical assistance to eleven Asian and African countries to help them build 95 industrial enterprises and other construction projects.

### 朋友才能真相助　豺狼不会发慈悲  苏联今年同各国经济合作将有新发展  帮助二十二个国家建设三百八十三个工业企业和其他工程

1960-02-20
第5版()
专栏：

    朋友才能真相助　豺狼不会发慈悲
    苏联今年同各国经济合作将有新发展
    帮助二十二个国家建设三百八十三个工业企业和其他工程
    新华社莫斯科19日电　据苏联报刊报道：苏联今年同其他国家的经济合作将有新的发展，它将帮助二十二个国家建设三百八十三个工业企业和其他建设工程。
    今年，苏联同各社会主义国家的经济联系，仍占苏联对外经济关系中的主要地位。苏联今年帮助各社会主义国家建设的工业企业和工程项目将达二百八十八个，同这些国家的贸易额将占苏联对外贸易总额的四分之三。
    1960年，苏联将向中国、波兰、罗马尼亚和保加利亚等兄弟国家提供许多整套的机器设备。苏联和各兄弟国家之间的生产的专业化和协作也将在更广泛的规模上进行。根据由欧洲社会主义国家组成的经济互助委员会的决定，苏联在今年和往后各年中将向德意志民主共和国、波兰、捷克斯洛伐克以及经济互助委员会的其他成员国提供越来越多的各种类型的设备、铁矿砂和锰矿砂、石油、焦炭、钢铁、钢材、棉花、谷物等。苏联也将从各兄弟国家输入化学工业设备、仪器、锻压设备、交通器材和轻工业原料。
    苏联同亚非经济不发达国家的经济联系，在今年也将获得进一步的发展。苏联今年将向十一个亚非国家提供技术援助，帮助它们建设九十五个工业企业和其他工程项目。
