Title: Khrushchev Leaves Jakarta for Bogor
Author:
Date: 1960-02-20
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, February 19th, TASS dispatch: Chairman of the Council of Ministers of the Soviet Union, Khrushchev, and his entourage went to Indonesian President Sukarno's official residence, Bogor Palace, this afternoon with President Sukarno.

　　From Jakarta to Bogor, the Soviet government leader was warmly welcomed by residents along the way.

　　This morning, Khrushchev and his entourage laid wreaths at the Jakarta Independence Monument. Subsequently, they laid wreaths at the Kalibata Heroes Cemetery. At noon, Khrushchev and Sukarno visited the construction site of the new Jakarta stadium, which is being built with Soviet assistance. They attended the ceremony of driving the 100th pile there.

　　On the 18th, after Khrushchev arrived in Jakarta, he officially paid a visit to President Sukarno.

　　On the same day, Sukarno held a banquet at the Independence Palace to entertain Khrushchev. At the banquet, Sukarno said that he invited Khrushchev to visit Indonesia in order to make the friendship between Indonesia and the Soviet Union more consolidated and strengthened.

　　In his reply, Khrushchev said that the Soviet Union hopes to cooperate with Indonesia on the basis of mutual benefit.

### 赫鲁晓夫离雅加达去茂物

1960-02-20
第5版()
专栏：

    赫鲁晓夫离雅加达去茂物
    新华社19日讯　据塔斯社消息：苏联部长会议主席赫鲁晓夫和他的随行人员今天下午同印度尼西亚总统苏加诺一起到苏加诺的官邸茂物宫。
    从雅加达到茂物，苏联政府领导人受到沿途居民的夹道欢迎。
    今天上午，赫鲁晓夫和他的随行人员在雅加达独立纪念碑前献了花圈。随后，他们到卡里巴达烈士陵墓前献了花圈。中午，赫鲁晓夫和苏加诺参观了正在苏联帮助下修建的雅加达新体育场工地。他们在那里参加了打第一百根桩的典礼。
    18日，赫鲁晓夫到达雅加达以后，正式拜会了苏加诺总统。
    当天，苏加诺在独立宫举行了宴会招待赫鲁晓夫。苏加诺在宴会上说，他邀请赫鲁晓夫访问印度尼西亚，是为了使印度尼西亚和苏联之间的友谊更加巩固和加强。
    赫鲁晓夫致答辞时表示，苏联希望在互利的基础上同印度尼西亚进行合作。
