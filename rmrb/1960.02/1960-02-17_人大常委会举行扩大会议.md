Title: NPC Standing Committee Holds Enlarged Meeting
Author:
Date: 1960-02-17
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, 16th - The Standing Committee of the National People's Congress held its 16th enlarged meeting this afternoon, hearing a report by Liu Xiangsan, Vice Minister of the Coal Industry, on the work of the coal industry in 1959 and the work tasks for 1960.
    Members of the Standing Commitee of the National Committee of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference attended the meeting as observers.

### 人大常委会举行扩大会议

1960-02-17
第1版()
专栏：

    人大常委会举行扩大会议
    新华社16日讯　全国人民代表大会常务委员会在今天下午举行第十六次会议扩大会议，听取了煤炭工业部副部长刘向三所作的关于煤炭工业1959年的工作情况和1960年工作任务的报告。
    政协全国委员会常务委员列席了这次会议。
