Title: Friendship
Author: Ren Dan
Date: 1960-02-14
Page: 8

    Friendship
    Ren Dan
    Soviet comrade Boris Kubalev was herding livestock near the Chinese border when he got lost in a blizzard. He wandered into Chinese territory and collapsed from the cold...
    The wind and snow fill the sky,
    Everywhere is a blur,
    Dear comrade, oh,
    Why are you lying on the ground?
    This is a hospital,
    The room feels like spring;
    Have a glass of milk,
    Quickly regain your strength.
    We've never met,
    But we are not strangers,
    Like old friends for many years,
    Like brothers from the same womb.
    Nothing is colder than wind and snow,
    But friendship is hotter than fire;
    Goodbye, comrade,
    Give our regards to your family.

### 友谊
任丹
1960-02-14
第8版()
专栏：

    友谊
    任丹
    苏联同志鲍里斯·库巴列夫在靠近中国边境的地方放牧，在暴风雪里他迷了路，走到中国境内，冻倒在地上……
    漫天的大风雪，
    到处一片迷茫，
    亲爱的同志呵，
    怎么躺在地上？
    这里是医院，
    屋内好像春天；
    喝杯牛奶吧，
    快恢复强健。
    我们没见过面，
    但是并不陌生，
    好像多年老友，
    好像亲生弟兄。
    寒冷莫过风雪，
    友谊却比火热；
    再见呵同志，
    问候你的亲人。
