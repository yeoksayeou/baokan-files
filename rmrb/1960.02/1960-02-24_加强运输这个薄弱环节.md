Title: Strengthen the Weak Link of Transportation
Author:
Date: 1960-02-24
Page: 2
Local Newspaper Commentary Excerpts

Strengthen the Weak Link of Transportation

The situation of continued leap forward in 1960 has emerged on all fronts in our province. We must further strengthen transportation work to support the continued leap forward of the national economy. How to strengthen transportation work? On the one hand, we must vigorously carry out transportation construction and vigorously promote technological revolution and technical innovation. On the other hand, we must strengthen transportation organization to maximize the potential of various transportation equipment. The one-dragon transportation collaboration is a guideline for mobilizing all positive forces (including factories, mines, communes, and all aspects related to transportation) and strengthening transportation work. Grasping this guideline will bring about a new leap forward in transportation work and improve this weak link in the national economy.

The current situation urgently requires raising the one-dragon transportation collaboration to a new stage and realizing the full "dragon networkization" of transportation in the province.

To realize "dragon networkization" is to break regional boundaries according to the flow of materials, and on the basis of further strengthening regional production-transport-sales one-dragon collaboration, to organize the vast majority of material circulation processes with the one-dragon transportation method, using material distribution centers as contact points and railway, highway, and waterway trunk lines as the backbone, so as to achieve internal and external, vertical and horizontal, interconnected, and closely linked, realizing high transportation to ensure high production.

The fundamental guarantee for realizing the dragon networkization of transportation is the leadership of the Party and the vigorous mass movement. Under the leadership of Party committees at all levels, we must correctly analyze the situation and, on the basis of raising communist ideological consciousness, use the formula of unity-criticism-unity to handle contradictions between various departments. This is the fundamental method for unifying thinking and mobilizing various positive factors. Collaboration is the requirement of social production and also the urgent demand of the masses in production practice. One-dragon transportation collaboration is a comprehensive mass movement; it is a production movement and an ideological movement. If there is a problem in one link, the "one-dragon" may be paralyzed. Realizing "dragon networkization" requires a larger-scale mass movement to overcome all departmentalism, ideas and practices that do not emphasize collaboration, greatly promote the communist spirit of mutual assistance and cooperation, and create greater and better work achievements and richer cooperative experiences. We must universally realize the full "dragon networkization" of transportation in all regions of the province in the first quarter, and let the flower of communist collaboration shine brightly. (Excerpt from Hebei Daily)

### 加强运输这个薄弱环节

1960-02-24
第2版()
专栏：地方报纸评论摘要

    加强运输这个薄弱环节
    1960年继续跃进的局面，已经在我省各个战线上出现，我们必须进一步加强交通运输工作，支持国民经济的继续跃进。如何加强交通运输工作呢？一方面要大力进行交通建设，大搞技术革新技术革命；另一方面，要加强运输组织工作，使各种运输设备发挥最大的潜力。而一条龙运输大协作，正是动员一切积极力量（包括厂矿、公社以及与运输有关的各个方面）、加强交通运输工作的一条纲。抓住这条纲，就会使交通运输工作有个新的跃进，就能使国民经济中的这个薄弱环节有所改观。
    当前的形势迫切要求把一条龙运输大协作提高到新阶段，实现全省交通运输方面的全面“龙网化”。
    实现“龙网化”，就是要根据物资的流向打破地区的界限，在进一步加强地区性的产运销一条龙大协作的基础上，以物资集散点为联络点，以铁路、公路、水运干线为骨干，把绝大部分的物资流通过程，同一条龙运输的办法组织起来，做到内外纵横、脉脉相通、首尾相连、环环紧扣，实现高运保高产。
    实现运输龙网化的根本保证，是党的领导和大搞群众运动。在各级党委的领导下，正确的分析形势，在提高共产主义思想觉悟的基础上，用团结——批评——团结的公式，处理各部门之间的矛盾。这是统一思想，调动各种积极因素的根本方法。协作，是社会生产的要求，也是群众在生产实践中的迫切要求。一条龙运输大协作，这是一个综合性的群众运动，它是生产运动，又是思想运动。一个环节上有问题，“一条龙”就可能陷于瘫痪。实现“龙网化”就要求有更大规模的群众运动，克服一切本位主义、不讲协作的思想和做法，大大发扬共产主义的互助协作精神，创造更大更好的工作成绩，创造更为丰富的协作经验。我们要在第一季度内，在全省各个地区普遍实现交通运输方面的全面“龙网化”，让共产主义协作之花大放光彩。　（摘自河北日报）
