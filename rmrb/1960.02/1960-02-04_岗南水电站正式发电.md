Title: Gangnan Hydropower Station Officially Generates Electricity
Author:
Date: 1960-02-04
Page: 2

Xinhua News Agency Report:

    The first unit of the Gangnan Hydropower Station, which is under construction, has officially generated electricity recently, more than five months ahead of schedule. Its capacity is 15,000 kilowatts. There are two other units with the same capacity as this unit, and they are currently being installed one after another.
    The Gangnan Hydropower Station is built on the Hutuo River in Hebei Province. After the power station is completed, its reservoir, together with the nearby Huangbizhuang Reservoir, will jointly exert benefits and can irrigate 6.2 million mu of farmland.

### 岗南水电站正式发电

1960-02-04
第2版()
专栏：

    岗南水电站正式发电
    据新华社讯　兴建中的岗南水电站第一台机组已提前五个多月在最近正式发电。它的容量是一万五千瓩。同这台机组的容量相等的机组还有两台，目前正在陆续安装中。
    岗南水电站建设在河北省的滹沱河上。电站建成之后，它的水库同临近的黄壁庄水库联合发挥效益，可以灌溉农田六百二十万亩。
