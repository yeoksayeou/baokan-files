Title: Hungarian Population Nears Ten Million
Author:
Date: 1960-02-22
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, Budapest, February 21st: The Hungarian newspaper "People's Freedom" published today a communique from the Hungarian Central Statistical Office regarding population statistics, stating that as of January 1, 1960, Hungary had a total population of 9,977,870. Among them, 4,817,355 were men, and 5,160,515 were women.

### 匈牙利人口近一千万

1960-02-22
第5版()
专栏：

    匈牙利人口近一千万
    新华社布达佩斯21日电　匈牙利“人民自由报”今天发表的匈牙利中央统计局关于人口统计的公报指出，截至1960年1月1日为止，匈牙利共有九百九十七万七千八百七十人。其中，男子为四百八十一万七千三百五十五人，妇女为五百一十六万零五百一十五人。
