Title: French Atomic Device Explosion in Sahara Incites Public Outrage: African Public Opinion Condemns France's Blatant Challenge to World Peace; Asian-African Solidarity Council Calls on African People to Strengthen Struggle; Ghana Freezes French Assets

Xinhua News Agency, Paris, February 13th: Disregarding the vehement condemnation of the local people and world opinion, the French government brazenly exploded its first atomic device at 7:00 AM local time today in the southwestern area of the Reggane settlement in the Sahara Desert.

The atomic device was detonated on top of a metal tower over 100 meters high.

The French President's press office officially announced the news of the atomic device explosion.

Xinhua News Agency, February 14th: African public figures, radio stations, and newspapers issued comments on February 13th condemning France's atomic device explosion in the Sahara, and considering it a challenge to world peace.

He also said that peace-loving people all over the world can never forgive France for this criminal act; France will find that this atomic explosion will arouse the indignation and hatred of people all over the world towards France.

Julius Nyerere, President of the Tanganyika African National Union, said in New York on the 13th that France's atomic explosion in the Sahara shows that France has selfish purposes towards other parts of the world. He said, "Independent Africa cannot allow France's actions."

Kwaye, chairman of the Ghanaian Committee Against Atomic Weapons, said that his committee knows "what steps will be taken to deal with this (French) atrocity."

A Tunisian government spokesman said that France's explosion would make its international position more difficult.

Tunisian Radio pointed out that France's action violates the desire of millions of Africans to live freely and happily in their own land. This once again makes Africans "unite in unease and redetermine to defend their children's lives without nightmares, insults and death."

"Action", the organ of the ruling Neo-Destour Party of Tunisia, said that France would face a world of anger, protest and contempt for its atomic explosion.

Cairo's "Republic" commented on France's atomic explosion and said, "General de Gaulle did not explode an atomic bomb, but exploded himself."

Cairo's "Evening News" also said that France's atomic explosion has not been able to frighten the African people, nor can it change their position, but will only strengthen the African people's determination to fight for their rights.

Moroccan Radio warned that the French government should be fully responsible for the consequences of the atomic explosion.

Xinhua News Agency, Cairo, February 13th: The Permanent Secretariat of the Asian-African People's Solidarity Council issued a statement today on France's explosion of a nuclear device in the Sahara, strongly protesting France's action in the name of the Asian and African people, and calling on the organizations of the Asian-African People's Solidarity Council in various countries to condemn France's inhumane action in unison.

The statement said that because of France's actions, the struggle for independence and the solidarity of Asia and Africa will be further strengthened, and the days of French imperialism being buried are not far away.

The statement also said that de Gaulle and his gang should understand that this crime will subject them to eternal condemnation by history, and they will be responsible for France's eternal notoriety from now on. Exploding nuclear devices did not enhance "France's national prestige" at all, but on the contrary, made France infamous.

The statement said that the nuclear explosion in the Sahara should serve as a final warning to those who still have illusions about the so-called "French Community", explaining that this "Community" is not for maintaining freedom, but as a tool of enslavement and oppression. The statement emphasized that this crazy act of the French general will inevitably further strengthen the great struggle of the African people for freedom.

Xinhua News Agency, Cairo, February 13th: The Provisional Government of the Republic of Algeria issued a statement here today stating that France's explosion of a nuclear device is a blatant contempt for world opinion, especially the United Nations.

The statement said that France's action will remind the people of the world and make them aware of the dangers that French imperialism poses to the world, especially to Africa. The statement also said that this approach also helps the people of the world further understand the struggle of the Algerian people and their determination to permanently eradicate French imperialism.

The statement said that as for the Algerian people, they are not at all surprised by the French government's approach, because for more than five years, France has been using all the destructive weapons at its disposal every day to exterminate the Algerian people.

Finally, the Provisional Government of the Republic of Algeria called for resolute measures to be taken to prevent France from further defying world opinion and threatening the lives of people of all countries and the freedom and peace of the world.

Xinhua News Agency, February 14th: Accra news: Ghanaian Prime Minister Kwame Nkrumah condemned France's atomic device explosion in the Sahara in a radio broadcast on the morning of the 13th, and announced the freezing of all French assets in Ghana.

Nkrumah said: France, completely disregarding the repeated protests of Ghana and other African countries, the resolutions of the United Nations General Assembly, and the conscience of mankind, exploded an atomic device on African soil on the morning of the 13th.

Nkrumah said that until the impact of this French atomic device test and French Prime Minister Debré's so-called future series of nuclear tests are clarified, all French assets in Ghana will be frozen. He said this was the first step to safeguard the interests of the Ghanaian people. He said the Ghanaian government would take any necessary further action.

Nkrumah estimated that French assets in Ghana amounted to five million pounds.

### 法国在撒哈拉爆炸原子装置激起公愤  非洲公众舆论谴责法国公然对世界和平挑战  亚非团结理事会号召非洲人民加强斗争　加纳冻结法国财产

1960-02-15
第6版()
专栏：

    法国在撒哈拉爆炸原子装置激起公愤
    非洲公众舆论谴责法国公然对世界和平挑战
    亚非团结理事会号召非洲人民加强斗争　加纳冻结法国财产
    新华社巴黎13日电　法国政府悍然不顾当地人民和世界舆论的猛烈谴责，今天当地时间七时在撒哈拉沙漠赖加奈居民点西南地区使它的第一个原子装置爆炸。
    这个原子装置是在一百多米高的金属塔上爆炸的。
    法国总统新闻处正式公布了原子装置爆炸的消息。
    据新华社14日讯　非洲公众领袖和电台、报纸13日纷纷发表评论，谴责法国在撒哈拉地区爆炸原子装置，并且认为这是对世界和平的挑战。
    正在开罗的喀麦隆人民联盟主席穆米埃说，法国的这个罪恶行动是对世界和平的严重威胁。他又说，全世界热爱和平的人民决不能原谅法国的这个罪恶行动；法国将会发现，这次原子爆炸将激起全世界的人民对法国的愤慨和憎恨。
    坦噶尼喀民族联盟主席尼雷尔13日在纽约说，法国在撒哈拉进行的原子爆炸表明法国对世界其他地区抱着自私自利的目的。他说，“独立的非洲不能容许法国的这种行动。”
    加纳反对原子武器委员会主席夸耶说，他的委员会知道“将采取什么步骤来对付（法国的）这种暴行。”
    突尼斯政府发言人说，法国的这次爆炸将使它在国际上处境更加困难。
    突尼斯电台指出，法国这个行动是违反千百万非洲人希望自由和愉快地在自己土地上生活的愿望的。这又一次使非洲人“在不安中团结一致，并且再次决心为保卫他们的孩子过没有恶梦、侮辱和死亡的生活”而斗争。
    突尼斯执政党新宪政党机关报“行动报”说，法国将会面临一个对它的原子爆炸表示愤怒、抗议和鄙视的世界。
    开罗“共和国报”在评论法国的原子爆炸时说，“戴高乐将军不是爆炸了原子弹，而是把他自己爆炸了。”
    开罗“晚报”也说，法国的原子爆炸并没有能够吓倒非洲人民，也决不能改变他们的立场，而只会使非洲人民加强他们争取自己权利的决心。
    摩洛哥电台警告说，法国政府应对原子爆炸的后果负完全的责任。
    新华社开罗13日电　亚非人民团结理事会常设书记处今天就法国在撒哈拉爆炸核装置发表声明，以亚非人民的名义强烈抗议法国的这一行动，并号召亚非人民团结理事会所属各国组织同声谴责法国这一不人道的行动。
    声明说，由于法国这一行动，争取独立的斗争和亚非的团结将进一步加强，而且法国帝国主义被埋葬的日子已经不远了。
    声明又说，戴高乐和他的一帮人应该懂得，这个罪行将使他们受到历史的永远谴责，他们将对法国从此永远臭名远扬负责。爆炸核装置根本没有提高“法国的国威”，相反地使法国声名狼籍。
    声明说，撒哈拉的核爆炸应该成为对所谓的“法兰西共同体”仍然存幻想的人们的最后警告，说明这个“共同体”不是为维护自由，而是作为奴役和压迫的工具。声明强调指出，这位法国将军的这一疯狂行为，势必将使非洲人民争取自由的伟大斗争进一步加强。
    新华社开罗13日电　阿尔及利亚共和国临时政府今天在这里发表声明说，法国爆炸核装置是对世界舆论，尤其是对联合国的公然蔑视。
    声明说，法国这一行动将提醒全世界的人民，使他们认识到法帝国主义对世界特别是对非洲造成的危险。声明又说，这种做法也有助于世界人民进一步了解阿尔及利亚人民的斗争以及他们决心永远根除法帝国主义的重要意义。
    声明说，至于阿尔及利亚人民对法国政府这种做法一点也不感到惊奇，因为在这五年多来，法国每天都在使用它手中的一切毁灭性武器来灭绝阿尔及利亚人民。
    最后，阿尔及利亚共和国临时政府号召采取坚决的措施，来阻止法国进一步违抗世界舆论，威胁各国人民的生命和世界的自由与和平。
    据新华社14日讯　阿克拉消息：加纳总理恩克鲁玛13日上午在电台广播中谴责法国在撒哈拉爆炸原子装置，同时宣布冻结法国在加纳的全部财产。
    恩克鲁玛说：法国完全不顾加纳和其他非洲国家的一再抗议，不顾联合国大会的决议，不顾人类的良心，13日上午在非洲土地上爆炸了一个原子装置。
    恩克鲁玛说，在弄清楚法国这次原子装置试验和法国总理德勃雷所说的未来一系列的核试验所产生的影响以前，法国在加纳的全部财产将被冻结。他说，这是为了保障加纳人民的利益的第一步。他说，加纳政府将采取任何必要的进一步行动。
    据恩克鲁玛估计，法国在加纳的财产有五百万英镑。
