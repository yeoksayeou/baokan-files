International Briefs

    Ethiopian Emperor Accepts Soviet Gift of Aircraft
    Addis Ababa dispatch: During Ethiopian Emperor Haile Selassie I's visit to the Soviet Union in July of last year, the Soviet government presented him with a specially equipped "Ilyushin-14" aircraft. Soviet Ambassador to Ethiopia, Budakov, presented the aircraft to Haile Selassie I at Addis Ababa Airport on the 20th on behalf of the Soviet government. The Emperor, in accepting the gift from the Soviet government, expressed his heartfelt gratitude to the Soviet government and Soviet Premier Khrushchev.

    Cambodia Appoints Former Prime Minister Khim Tit as Ambassador to the Soviet Union
    Phnom Penh dispatch: Phnom Penh Radio in Cambodia announced on the 21st that Cambodia has appointed former Prime Minister Khim Tit to succeed Nhiek Tioulong (the current Deputy Prime Minister) as Cambodia's Ambassador to the Soviet Union.

### 国际简讯

专栏：国际简讯

    国际简讯
    埃塞俄比亚皇帝接受苏联赠的飞机
    亚的斯亚贝巴讯　在埃塞俄比亚皇帝海尔·塞拉西一世去年7月间访问苏联时，苏联政府赠给他一架专门装备的“伊尔—十四式”飞机。苏联驻埃塞俄比亚大使布达科夫20日在亚的斯亚贝巴机场代表苏联政府把飞机交给了海尔·塞拉西一世。皇帝在接受苏联政府的礼物时，向苏联政府和苏联政府首脑赫鲁晓夫表示衷心的感谢。
    柬任命前首相钦迪为驻苏大使
    金边讯　柬埔寨金边电台21日宣布，柬埔寨任命前首相钦迪接替涅刁隆（现任副首相）担任柬埔寨驻苏联大使职务。
