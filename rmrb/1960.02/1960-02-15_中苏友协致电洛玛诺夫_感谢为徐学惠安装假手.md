Title: Sino-Soviet Friendship Association Sends Telegram to Romanov to Thank Him for Installing a Prosthetic Hand for Xu Xuehui
Author:
Date: 1960-02-15
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, February 14th. The National Council of the Sino-Soviet Friendship Association and the Beijing Sino-Soviet Friendship Association jointly sent a telegram to Romanov, director of the Chita Orthopedic Equipment Factory in the Soviet Union, to express their heartfelt gratitude to Romanov and the Soviet comrades for installing a prosthetic hand for Xu Xuehui.

The telegram stated, "As the Chinese people warmly celebrate the tenth anniversary of the signing of the Sino-Soviet Treaty of Friendship, Alliance and Mutual Assistance, we invited Comrade Xu Xuehui, who had just returned from the Soviet Union, to give a report to the activists and youth of the Beijing Sino-Soviet Friendship Association. Your noble deed of installing a prosthetic hand for Xu Xuehui, the outstanding daughter of the Chinese people, and the warm care and assistance of the Soviet comrades to Xu Xuehui, are a vivid illustration of the deep friendship between the people of China and the Soviet Union. All the comrades who heard the report were deeply moved by your spirit of internationalism."

The telegram said: "We would like to express our brotherly respect and heartfelt thanks to you, to all the staff of your factory, and to all the Soviet comrades who cared for and helped Xu Xuehui. We wish the great and unbreakable friendship between the people of China and the Soviet Union everlasting!"

### 中苏友协致电洛玛诺夫  感谢为徐学惠安装假手

1960-02-15
第5版()
专栏：

    中苏友协致电洛玛诺夫
    感谢为徐学惠安装假手
    新华社14日讯　中苏友好协会总会和北京市中苏友好协会今天联合打电报给苏联赤塔整形器材厂厂长洛玛诺夫，衷心感谢洛玛诺夫和苏联同志为徐学惠安装假手。
    电报说，“在中国人民热烈庆祝中苏友好同盟互助条约签订十周年的时候，我们邀请刚从苏联归来的徐学惠同志向北京市的中苏友协积极分子和青年们作了报告。你为中国人民的优秀女儿徐学惠安装假手的崇高事迹以及苏联同志们对徐学惠的热情关怀和帮助，是中苏两国人民深情厚谊的一个生动例证，所有听报告的同志都为你们的国际主义精神深深感动。”
    电报说：“我们谨向你，向你厂全体职工、以及所有关怀和帮助徐学惠的苏联同志们，表示兄弟的敬意和衷心的感谢。祝中苏两国人民伟大的牢不可破的友谊万古长青！”
