Title: China and the Soviet Union Sign Forest Fire Prevention Agreement
Author:
Date: 1960-02-01
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, 30th: China and the Soviet Union signed an agreement on forest fire prevention in Moscow on the 29th.

The agreement stipulates that the two countries will take measures to mutually assist in protecting forest resources and preventing fires in the border areas of the two countries and their spread to each other.

Zhang Kexia, Vice Minister of Forestry, signed the agreement on behalf of China, and Petrov, Deputy Minister of Agriculture, signed on behalf of the Soviet Union.

### 中苏签订护林防火协定

1960-02-01
第6版()
专栏：

    中苏签订护林防火协定
    新华社莫斯科30日电　29日在莫斯科签订了中国和苏联关于护林防火协定。
    协定规定，两国采取措施互相协助保护森林资源，防止两国边境地区发生火灾及其相互蔓延。
    在协定上签字的，中国方面是林业部副部长张克侠，苏联方面是农业部副部长彼得罗夫。
