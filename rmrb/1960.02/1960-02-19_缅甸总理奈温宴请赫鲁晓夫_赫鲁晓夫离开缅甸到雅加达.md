Title: Burmese Prime Minister Ne Win Hosts Khrushchev; Khrushchev Leaves Burma for Jakarta
Author:
Date: 1960-02-19
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, February 17th, Rangoon dispatch: According to TASS, Burmese Prime Minister Ne Win hosted a banquet today in honor of Soviet Chairman of the Council of Ministers, Khrushchev.

Khrushchev also visited the Martyrs' Mausoleum today, and laid a wreath at the tomb of Aung San, the Burmese national hero. Afterwards, Khrushchev and his entourage visited the construction site of the Rangoon Institute of Technology. Khrushchev received a warm welcome from the president, professors, Burmese experts and workers involved in the construction of the institute, representatives from the Soviet Ministry of Construction assisting Burma's construction, and Soviet experts.

The construction of the institute is scheduled to be fully completed this year. It will accommodate 1,100 undergraduates and more than 50 graduate students.

Khrushchev then visited the construction site of a hotel. Both the institute and the hotel are gifts from the Soviet Union to the Burmese people.

Xinhua News Agency, Rangoon, February 18th: Soviet Chairman of the Council of Ministers, Khrushchev, and his entourage concluded their three-day visit to Burma and left Rangoon by plane this morning for Jakarta, the capital of Indonesia.

Those who saw Khrushchev off at the airport included the President of the Union of Burma, U Win Maung, Prime Minister Ne Win, government officials, prominent figures from all walks of life, and diplomatic envoys from various countries.

Burmese President U Win Maung held a grand reception for Khrushchev on the 17th. Famous Burmese actors performed at the reception.

On the same day, Soviet Ambassador to Burma, Ledovsky, held a grand reception for Khrushchev.

At the reception, Khrushchev presented replicas of the symbol of the Soviet cosmic rocket that had been sent to the moon to the Burmese President and Prime Minister as gifts.

On this day, Khrushchev held talks with Ne Win. Soviet Foreign Minister Gromyko participated in the friendly talks.

On the afternoons of the 16th and 17th, Khrushchev also met with prominent Burmese political figures U Nu, U Ba Swe, and U Kyaw Nyein.

After Khrushchev's plane left Burmese airspace, he sent a telegram to the Burmese President and Prime Minister. In his telegram, he said, "I am deeply convinced that the meeting and beneficial talks between the leaders of the Soviet Union and Burma will promote the further strengthening of friendship between the two countries and the cause of world peace."

Xinhua News Agency, Jakarta, February 18th: Soviet Chairman of the Council of Ministers, Khrushchev, and his entourage arrived in Jakarta today by plane from Rangoon, the capital of Burma.

Khrushchev is visiting Indonesia for twelve days at the invitation of Indonesian President and Prime Minister Sukarno.

Those who welcomed Khrushchev at the airport included Indonesian President and Prime Minister Sukarno, First Minister Djuanda and cabinet ministers, members of parliament and representatives of various political parties, citizens of Jakarta dressed in festive attire, and diplomatic envoys from various countries.

In his welcoming speech, Sukarno expressed the hope that Khrushchev's visit to Indonesia would lead to greater mutual understanding between the peoples of the two countries and further strengthen the friendship between the two peoples.

Khrushchev said in his speech that personal contact between the leaders of the two countries is of great significance for better mutual understanding and strengthening the friendship between the two peoples.

Khrushchev expressed the hope that his visit would promote the further development of friendship between the two countries.

On his way from the airport to his residence, the Indonesian State Palace, Khrushchev was warmly welcomed by more than one million citizens of Jakarta.

### 缅甸总理奈温宴请赫鲁晓夫  赫鲁晓夫离开缅甸到雅加达

1960-02-19
第5版()
专栏：

    缅甸总理奈温宴请赫鲁晓夫
    赫鲁晓夫离开缅甸到雅加达
    新华社17日讯　据塔斯社仰光消息：缅甸总理奈温今天设宴招待苏联部长会议主席赫鲁晓夫。
    赫鲁晓夫今天还访问了烈士墓，向缅甸民族英雄昂山的墓献了花圈。然后，赫鲁晓夫和他的随行人员参观了仰光工学院的建筑工程，赫鲁晓夫受到工学院院长、教授、建设这个工学院的缅甸专家和工人、帮助缅甸建设的苏联建筑部的代表和苏联专家的热烈欢迎。
    这所工学院的建筑工程将于今年全部完工。它将容纳一千一百名大学生和五十多名研究生。
    赫鲁晓夫接着又参观了一家旅馆的建筑工程。工学院和旅馆这两项建筑工程都是苏联赠送给缅甸人民的。
    新华社仰光18日电　苏联部长会议主席赫鲁晓夫和他的随行人员结束了对缅甸的三天访问，今天上午乘飞机离开仰光前往印度尼西亚首都雅加达。
    到机场欢送赫鲁晓夫的有缅甸联邦总统吴温貌、总理奈温、政府官员、各界著名人士以及各国外交使节等。
    缅甸总统吴温貌曾在17日为赫鲁晓夫举行了盛大的招待会。在招待会上，著名的缅甸演员作了艺术表演。
    同一天，苏联驻缅甸大使列多夫斯基为赫鲁晓夫举行了盛大的招待会。
    赫鲁晓夫在这个招待会上把苏联宇宙火箭送到月球上去的标志的复制品送给出席招待会的缅甸总统和总理作为礼物。
    在这一天，赫鲁晓夫同奈温举行了会谈。苏联外交部长葛罗米柯参加了这次友好的会谈。
    在16日下午和17日，赫鲁晓夫还先后会见了缅甸著名政治活动家吴努以及吴巴瑞和吴觉迎。
    赫鲁晓夫乘飞机离开缅甸领空以后，在飞机上打电报给缅甸总统和总理。他在电报中说，我深信，苏缅两国领导人的会晤和有益的会谈将促进两国友谊的进一步加强，促进世界和平事业。
    新华社雅加达18日电　苏联部长会议主席赫鲁晓夫和他的随行人员今天从缅甸首都仰光乘飞机到达雅加达。
    赫鲁晓夫应印度尼西亚总统兼总理苏加诺的邀请，前来印度尼西亚作为期十二天的访问。
    到机场欢迎赫鲁晓夫的有印度尼西亚总统兼总理苏加诺、第一部长朱安达和内阁部长们、议员和各政党代表，穿着节日盛装的雅加达市民以及各国外交使节。
    苏加诺在致欢迎词时表示希望赫鲁晓夫对印度尼西亚的访问，将导致两国人民更大的相互了解，更加加强两国人民之间的友谊。
    赫鲁晓夫发表讲话说，两国领导人之间的亲自接触对更好地互相了解和加强两国人民的友谊具有很大的意义。
    赫鲁晓夫表示希望，他这次访问将促进两国友谊的进一步发展。
    赫鲁晓夫在离开机场到他的住所——印度尼西亚国家宫的时候，沿途受到一百多万雅加达市民的热烈欢迎。
