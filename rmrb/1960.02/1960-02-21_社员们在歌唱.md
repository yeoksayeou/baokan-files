Title: The Commune Members are Singing
Author:
Date: 1960-02-21
Page: 5

    The clouds and mist shroud the mountaintop,
    No, that is not clouds and mist,
    It is the cooking smoke from the commune's canteen.
    White snow covers the green plain,
    No, that is not white snow,
    It is our commune's flock of sheep.
    Thunder rumbles and shakes the hills,
    No, that is not thunder,
    It is the commune members singing.

### 社员们在歌唱

1960-02-21
第5版()
专栏：

    社员们在歌唱
    云烟笼罩了山尖，
    不，那不是云烟，
    是公社食堂的炊烟。
    白雪盖住了绿原，
    不，那不是白雪，
    是咱们公社的羊群。
    雷声隆隆震山岗，
    不，那不是雷声，
    是社员们在歌唱。
