Title: Chairman Liu and Premier Zhou Send Congratulatory Messages on Ceylon's National Day
Author:
Date: 1960-02-04
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, 3rd - Chairman of the People's Republic of China, Liu Shaoqi, today sent a telegram to Ceylon's Governor-General, Sir Oliver Goonetilleke, to congratulate him on Ceylon's National Day. The telegram reads as follows:
His Excellency Sir Oliver Goonetilleke, Governor-General of Ceylon:
    On the occasion of your country's National Day, I extend my warm congratulations to Your Excellency and the people of Ceylon on behalf of the Chinese people and in my own name. May the traditional friendship between the Chinese and Ceylonese people be further consolidated and developed. May Ceylon be prosperous and its people happy.
                  Liu Shaoqi, Chairman of the People's Republic of China
                              February 3, 1960

Xinhua News Agency, 3rd - Premier of the State Council of the People's Republic of China, Zhou Enlai, today sent a telegram to Ceylon's Prime Minister, W. Dahanayake, to congratulate him on the twelfth anniversary of Ceylon's independence. The telegram reads as follows:
His Excellency Prime Minister W. Dahanayake of Ceylon:
    On the happy occasion of the twelfth anniversary of Ceylon's independence, I extend my warm congratulations to the Government of Ceylon and to Your Excellency on behalf of the Government of the People's Republic of China and in my own name.
    May the friendly relations between China and Ceylon, established on the basis of the Five Principles of Peaceful Coexistence, be increasingly consolidated and developed. May the people of Ceylon achieve new successes in the cause of building their own country and safeguarding peace in Asia and the world.
            Zhou Enlai, Premier of the State Council of the People's Republic of China
                            February 3, 1960

### 刘主席周总理电贺锡兰国庆

1960-02-04
第1版()
专栏：

    刘主席周总理电贺锡兰国庆
    新华社3日讯　中华人民共和国主席刘少奇今天打电报给锡兰总督奥利弗·古?狄莱克祝贺锡兰国庆，电文如下：锡兰奥利弗·古?狄莱克总督阁下：
    在贵国国庆的时候，我谨代表中国人民，并且以我个人的名义向阁下和锡兰人民表示热诚的祝贺。祝中锡两国人民间的传统友谊进一步巩固和发展。祝锡兰国家繁荣，人民幸福。
                  中华人民共和国主席　刘少奇
                              一九六○年二月三日
    新华社3日讯　中华人民共和国国务院总理周恩来今天打电报给锡兰总理达哈纳亚克，祝贺锡兰独立十二周年，电文如下：锡兰达哈纳亚克总理阁下：
    欣逢锡兰独立十二周年，我谨代表中华人民共和国政府，并且以我个人的名义向锡兰政府和阁下表示热烈的祝贺。
    祝中锡两国建立在和平共处五项原则基础上的友好关系日益巩固和发展。祝锡兰人民在建设自己的国家、维护亚洲和世界和平的事业中获得新的成就。
            中华人民共和国国务院总理　周恩来
                            一九六○年二月三日
