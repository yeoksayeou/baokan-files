Title: Chinese Ambassador to Burma Hosts Banquet for Senior Burmese Military Officers; People of Shan and Kachin States Welcome Sino-Burmese Agreement
Author:
Date: 1960-02-22
Page: 5
    Xinhua News Agency, Rangoon, February 20th: Chinese Ambassador to Burma, Li Yimang, held a banquet at the Chinese Embassy yesterday evening to entertain senior officers of the Burmese Defense Forces.
    General Ne Win, Prime Minister of Burma and Chief of Staff of the Burmese Defense Forces, and his wife attended the banquet.
    Senior officers of the Burmese Defense Forces and their wives also attended the banquet.
    The banquet took place in a warm and friendly atmosphere. The hosts and guests repeatedly toasted the conclusion of the historically significant Sino-Burmese Treaty of Friendship and Mutual Non-Aggression and the Agreement on the Sino-Burmese Boundary Question between the two governments, and the further development of friendly relations between China and Burma.
    Xinhua News Agency, Rangoon, February 21st: The people of all ethnic groups in ninety-six villages and towns in Burma's Shan and Kachin States recently held a series of public gatherings to welcome the Sino-Burmese Treaty of Friendship and Mutual Non-Aggression and the Agreement on the Sino-Burmese Boundary Question concluded by Burmese Prime Minister Ne Win and Chinese Premier Zhou Enlai.
    At gatherings held in various places in Burma, people of all ethnic groups pointed out that the signing of these two treaties and agreements not only demonstrates the two peoples' love of peace and cherishing of their traditional friendship, but also embodies China's policy of peaceful coexistence with its neighbors. They expressed their firm belief that the Sino-Burmese border will become a border of friendship and that the friendship between the two peoples will be further developed.
    At a public meeting held in Lege in Kachin State, more than 30,000 participants, including Shan and Kachin people, unanimously welcomed the signing of the Sino-Burmese Treaty of Friendship and Mutual Non-Aggression and the Agreement on the Sino-Burmese Boundary Question, and chanted the slogan "Long live Sino-Burmese friendship."

### 我国驻缅大使宴请缅甸高级军官  掸邦和克钦邦人民欢迎中缅协定

1960-02-22
第5版()
专栏：

    我国驻缅大使宴请缅甸高级军官
    掸邦和克钦邦人民欢迎中缅协定
    新华社仰光20日电　中国驻缅甸大使李一氓昨天晚间在中国大使馆举行宴会，招待缅甸国防军的高级军官。
    缅甸总理兼缅甸国防军参谋长奈温将军和他的夫人出席了宴会。
    缅甸国防军的高级军官和他们的夫人也出席了宴会。
    宴会是在热烈友好的气氛中进行的。宾主频频为中缅两国政府缔结具有历史意义的中缅友好和互不侵犯条约以及关于中缅边界问题的协定，为中缅两国之间的友好关系的进一步发展而干杯。
    新华社仰光21日电　缅甸掸邦和克钦邦的九十六个村镇的各族人民最近举行了一系列公众集会，欢迎缅甸总理奈温和中国总理周恩来缔结的中缅友好和互不侵犯条约以及关于中缅边界问题的协定。
    缅甸各族人民在各地举行的集会上指出，这两项条约和协定的签订不仅表明两国人民热爱和平和珍视他们之间的传统友谊，而且体现了中国同它的邻国和平共处的政策。他们表示深信，中缅之间的边界将成为友好的边界，两国人民之间的友谊将进一步得到发展。
    在克钦邦的雷基举行的一次公众大会上，包括掸族人和克钦族人在内的三万多与会者一致欢迎签订中缅友好和互不侵犯条约以及关于中缅边界问题的协定，并且高呼“中缅友好万岁”的口号。
