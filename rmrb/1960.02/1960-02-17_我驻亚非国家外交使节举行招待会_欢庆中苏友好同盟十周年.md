Title: Our Diplomatic Envoys to Asian and African Countries Hold Receptions to Celebrate the Tenth Anniversary of the Sino-Soviet Treaty of Friendship and Alliance

Author:

Date: 1960-02-17
Page: 5

    Xinhua News Agency, February 16th. Chinese diplomatic envoys to Asian and African countries, including India, Burma, Indonesia, Pakistan, Afghanistan, Yemen, Ceylon, Cambodia, Iraq, Morocco, Sudan, and the acting Consul-General in Damascus, recently held banquets or receptions in their respective locations to commemorate the tenth anniversary of the signing of the Sino-Soviet Treaty of Friendship, Alliance, and Mutual Assistance.

    In Burma, Iraq, and Ceylon, the ambassadors of China and the Soviet Union jointly held celebratory banquets; in other countries, the Chinese and Soviet ambassadors held separate celebratory receptions.

    During the lively banquets and receptions, the Chinese and Soviet envoys, in their speeches, emphasized the significant importance of the Sino-Soviet Treaty of Friendship, Alliance, and Mutual Assistance for the prosperity and development of China and the Soviet Union, for safeguarding world peace, and for promoting the cause of human progress.

### 我驻亚非国家外交使节举行招待会  欢庆中苏友好同盟十周年

1960-02-17
第5版()
专栏：

    我驻亚非国家外交使节举行招待会
    欢庆中苏友好同盟十周年
    新华社16日讯　中国驻印度、缅甸、印度尼西亚、巴基斯坦、阿富汗、也门、锡兰、柬埔寨、伊拉克、摩洛哥、苏丹等亚非国家的外交使节和驻大马士革的代理总领事最近几天在驻在地先后举行了宴会或招待会，庆祝中苏友好同盟互助条约签订十周年。
    在缅甸、伊拉克和锡兰，由中苏两国的大使联合举行了庆祝宴会；在其他国家，中国和苏联的大使分别举行了庆祝招待会。
    中苏使节在这些气氛热烈的宴会和招待会上讲话时，都强调指出中苏友好同盟互助条约对于中苏两国的繁荣和发展、对于维护世界和平和促进人类进步的事业的重大意义。
