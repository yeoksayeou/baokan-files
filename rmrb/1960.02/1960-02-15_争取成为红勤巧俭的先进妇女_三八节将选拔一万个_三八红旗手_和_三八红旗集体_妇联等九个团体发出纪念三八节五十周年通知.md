Title: Strive to Become Advanced Red, Diligent, Clever, and Frugal Women; Ten Thousand "March 8th Red Banner Pacesetters" and "March 8th Red Banner Collectives" to be Selected During the March 8th Festival; Nine Organizations Including the All-China Women's Federation Issue Notice Commemorating the Fiftieth Anniversary of the March 8th Festival
Author: 
Date: 1960-02-15
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, 14th: The All-China Women's Federation and eight other organizations issued a joint notice on February 14th regarding the commemoration of the fiftieth anniversary of International Working Women's Day, "March 8th."

    The notice stated that in order to achieve a better, more comprehensive, and continued leap forward in 1960, women across the country must, together with the people of the whole country, under the leadership of the Party and Chairman Mao, better implement the General Line of going all out, aiming high, and achieving greater, faster, better, and more economical results in building socialism; actively and systematically study Marxism-Leninism and the works of Mao Zedong; and actively participate in the campaign to increase production and practice economy centered on technical innovation and technical revolution. Women across the country must all establish lofty aspirations and set great ambitions, striving to become red, diligent, clever, and frugal socialist working women, and make greater contributions to completing and overfulfilling the 1960 national construction plan, and to building socialism at high speed.

    The notice stated that in order to commend advanced women and women-dominated advanced collectives who made outstanding contributions on all fronts of socialist construction in 1959, the All-China Women's Federation decided to select ten thousand advanced women and advanced collectives from all over the country during the commemoration of the fiftieth anniversary of International Working Women's Day, "March 8th," and award them the titles of "March 8th Red Banner Pacesetter" and "March 8th Red Banner Collective," along with certificates and banners. We firmly believe that through this large-scale commendation and reward activity, women in our country will learn more advanced models and advanced experiences, and more advanced collectives and individuals will emerge, making more outstanding contributions to achieving a continued leap forward in 1960.

    This notice was jointly issued by the All-China Women's Federation, the All-China Federation Of Trade Unions, the Communist Youth League Central Committee, the All-China Youth Federation, the National Committee of the China Association for Science and Technology, the All-China Students' Federation, the All-China Federation of Literary and Art Circles, the China Peace Committee, and the Chinese People's National Committee for the Defence of Children.

### 争取成为红勤巧俭的先进妇女  三八节将选拔一万个“三八红旗手”和“三八红旗集体”  妇联等九个团体发出纪念三八节五十周年通知

1960-02-15
第1版()
专栏：

    争取成为红勤巧俭的先进妇女
    三八节将选拔一万个“三八红旗手”和“三八红旗集体”
    妇联等九个团体发出纪念三八节五十周年通知
    据新华社14日讯　全国妇女联合会等九个团体于2月14日发出关于纪念“三八”国际劳动妇女节五十周年的联合通知。
    通知说，为了实现1960年更好的全面的继续跃进，全国妇女一定要和全国人民一起，在党和毛主席的领导下，更好地贯彻执行鼓足干劲、力争上游、多快好省地建设社会主义的总路线，积极地有计划地学习马克思列宁主义，学习毛泽东著作，积极参加以技术革新、技术革命为中心的增产节约运动。全国妇女一定要人人树雄心、个个立大志，争取成为红、勤、巧、俭的社会主义劳动妇女，为完成和超额完成1960年国家建设计划，为高速度建设社会主义作出更大的贡献。
    通知说，全国妇联为表扬在1959年建设社会主义各个战线上作出卓越贡献的先进妇女和以妇女为主的先进集体，决定在纪念“三八”国际劳动妇女节五十周年的时候，从全国各地选拔一万个先进妇女和先进集体，分别授与“三八红旗手”“三八红旗集体”的奖状和奖旗。我们深信，通过这个大规模的表扬和奖励活动，定将使我国妇女学到更多的先进榜样、先进经验，涌现出更多的先进集体和先进人物，为实现1960年的继续跃进作出更出色的贡献。
    这个通知是由全国妇联、全国总工会、团中央、全国青联、科学技术协会全国委员会、全国学联、全国文联、中国人民保卫世界和平委员会、中国人民保卫儿童全国委员会联合发出的。
                               （通知全文见第四版）
