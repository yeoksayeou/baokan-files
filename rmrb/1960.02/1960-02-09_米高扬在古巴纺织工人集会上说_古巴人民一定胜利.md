Title: Mikoyan Says at Cuban Textile Workers' Rally: The Cuban People Will Surely Win
Author:
Date: 1960-02-09
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, Havana, Febuary 8th: According to TASS, First Deputy Chairman of the Soviet Council of Ministers, Mikoyan, attended a welcoming rally for Cuban textile workers on the morning of February 7th at the invitation of the Cuban Workers' Confederation and other labor unions.

	A union leader, who delivered the welcoming speech at the meeting, extended a heartfelt welcome to Mikoyan. He emphasized that no conspiracy or provocation by domestic and foreign enemies of the Cuban Revolution, nor any plan for armed intervention in Cuba, could frustrate the Cuban people's will to achieve a complete and thorough victory for the revolution.

	In his speech, Mikoyan conveyed warm greetings to the Cuban workers and all the Cuban people on behalf of the Soviet working people. He said that the enthusiasm and sympathy of the Soviet people are on the side of the Cuban people, and with a people like you, Cuba cannot fail to win.

### 米高扬在古巴纺织工人集会上说  古巴人民一定胜利

1960-02-09
第6版()
专栏：

    米高扬在古巴纺织工人集会上说
    古巴人民一定胜利
    据新华社8日讯　据塔斯社哈瓦那8日讯：苏联部长会议第一副主席米高扬7日早晨应古巴工人联合会和其他一些工会的邀请，参加了古巴纺织工人的欢迎会。
    在会上致欢迎词的工会领袖向米高扬表示衷心欢迎。他着重说，古巴革命的内外敌人的任何阴谋和挑衅以及任何武装干涉古巴的计划，都不能挫折古巴人民要革命彻底胜利的意志。
    米高扬在会上致词时，代表苏联劳动人民向古巴工人和全体人民致热情的问候。他说，苏联人的热情和同情心在古巴人民这边，有你们这样人民的古巴，不会不取得胜利的。
