Title: Algerian Prime Minister Abbas Says Algeria Will Inevitably Be Independent; No Foreign Interference Can Solve the Algerian Problem
Author:
Date: 1960-02-20
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, 19th dispatch from Tunis: Algerian Republic Provisional Government Prime Minister Abbas said in a radio address in Tunis on the 17th that Algeria will inevitably gain independence in the end. He pointed out that the Algerian problem can only be solved by faithfully implementing the right to self-determination without any foreign interference. He said, "No imposed regulations can establish peace and guarantee the future."

In this speech to the Europeans in Algeria, Abbas also said that "a just, feasible, and certain solution" to the Algerian problem "depends first and foremost on the Algerians themselves, that is, on you and us."

Abbas expressed his hope that these Europeans "will not continue to destroy opportunities for unity," and said, "In the Algerian Republic that we will build together, everyone has a place, everyone has a job. The new Algeria will have neither racial barriers nor religious hatred. It will respect all moral values and all legitimate interests."

### 阿尔及利亚总理阿巴斯说  阿尔及利亚必然要独立  任何外来干涉都不可能解决阿尔及利亚问题

1960-02-20
第6版()
专栏：

    阿尔及利亚总理阿巴斯说
    阿尔及利亚必然要独立
    任何外来干涉都不可能解决阿尔及利亚问题
    新华社19日讯　突尼斯消息：阿尔及利亚共和国临时政府总理阿巴斯17日在突尼斯发表的一篇广播演说中说，阿尔及利亚最后必然会获得独立。他指出，只有在忠实地实行自决权，没有任何外来的干涉的条件下，才能解决阿尔及利亚问题。他说，“没有任何强加的法规能够建立和平和保障前途。”
    阿巴斯在这篇向在阿尔及利亚的欧洲人发表的演说中还说，阿尔及利亚问题的“一个公正的、可行的和肯定的解决首先取决于阿尔及利亚人自己，这就是说，取决于你们和我们”。
    阿巴斯表示希望这些欧洲人“不要继续破坏团结的机会”，并且说，“在我们将一起建立的阿尔及利亚共和国内，每个人都有地位、每个人都有工作。新的阿尔及利亚将既没有种族的障碍，也没有宗教的仇恨。它将尊重一切道德观念、一切合法利益。”
