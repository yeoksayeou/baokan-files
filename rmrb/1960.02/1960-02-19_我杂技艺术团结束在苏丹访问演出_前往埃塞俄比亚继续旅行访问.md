Title: Our Acrobatic Troupe Ends Visit and Performance in Sudan, Travels to Ethiopia for Continued Visit
Author:
Date: 1960-02-19
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, Khartoum, 18th: The Chinese Acrobatic Troupe held its farewell performance here on the evening of the 17th, with the Entumban National Theatre filled to capacity.

Major General Farid, member of the Supreme Council of the Sudanese Armed Forces, Minister of Information and Minister of Labour, attended the performance on behalf of Chairman Abboud. Also present at the performance were: Major General Bashir, member of the Supreme Council and Deputy Commander-in-Chief; Brigadier General Amin, member of the Supreme Council, Minister of Agriculture, Irrigation and Transport; Minister of Foreign Affairs, Herr; and Brigadier General Othman, Deputy Chief of Staff of the Armed Forces. Wang Yutian, Chinese Ambassador to Sudan, accompanied them to watch the performance.

After the performance, Major General Farid, accompanied by Chinese Ambassador Wang Yutian, went on stage and delivered a speech of thanks. He wished China an increasingly bright and happy future. He announced that the Chinese Acrobatic Troupe had donated the proceeds from the performance to the Sudanese Ministry of Information and Ministry of Labour for the development of Sudanese culture. Major General Farid also presented gifts to the members of the Chinese Acrobatic Troupe on behalf of Chairman Abboud.

Prior to Major General Farid's speech, Qu Wu, head of the Chinese Acrobatic Troupe, spoke. He thanked the Sudanese government and people for their warm hospitality and enthusiastic welcome. He said that they knew that this expressed the deep friendship of the Sudanese people towards the Chinese people. He expressed that they would bring the friendship of the Sudanese people to the Chinese people.

The Chinese Acrobatic Troupe arrived in Sudan on January 19th. During their nearly one-month visit and performance, they performed a total of eighteen shows in Khartoum and other cities, with an audience of over 50,000 people.

The Acrobatic Troupe will leave here today for Ethiopia to continue their tour in Africa.

### 我杂技艺术团结束在苏丹访问演出  前往埃塞俄比亚继续旅行访问

1960-02-19
第5版()
专栏：

    我杂技艺术团结束在苏丹访问演出
    前往埃塞俄比亚继续旅行访问
    新华社喀土穆18日电　中国杂技艺术团17日晚上在这里举行告别演出，演出时恩图曼国立剧院座无虚席。
    苏丹武装部队最高委员会委员、宣传部和劳工部部长法里德少将代表阿布德主席出席了演出晚会。出席演出晚会的还有：最高委员会委员、副总司令贝希尔少将，最高委员会委员、农业灌溉部和交通部长阿明准将，外交部长赫尔，和武装部队副总参谋长欧斯曼准将。中国驻苏丹大使王雨田陪同他们观看了演出。
    演出结束后，法里德少将在中国大使王雨田陪同下走上了舞台，并致了谢词。他祝中国将享有日益光明和幸福的前途。他宣布，中国杂技艺术团已把演出所得捐献给了苏丹宣传部和劳工部，供发展苏丹文化之用。法里德少将还代表阿布德主席向中国杂技艺术团的团员们赠送了礼物。
    在法里德少将讲话以前，中国杂技艺术团团长屈武讲了话。他感谢苏丹政府和人民对他们的热忱款待和热烈欢迎。他说，他们知道，这表达了苏丹人民对中国人民的深厚友谊。他表示将把苏丹人民的友谊带给中国人民。
    中国杂技艺术团是在1月19日到达苏丹的。在将近一个月的访问演出中，他们总共在喀土穆和其他城市演出了十八场，观众超过五万余人。
    杂技艺术团将在今天离开这里前往埃塞俄比亚，继续他们在非洲的旅行演出。
