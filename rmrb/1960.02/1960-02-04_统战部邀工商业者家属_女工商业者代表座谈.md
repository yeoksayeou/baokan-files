Title: United Front Work Department Invites Industrialist and Businessmen's Families, Female Industrialist and Businessmen Representatives to Symposium

Author:

Date: 1960-02-04
Page: 4

    Xinhua News Agency, 3rd. The United Front Work Department of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of China, this afternoon, invited industrialist and businessman's family members and female industrialist and business representatives attending the Chinese Democratic National Construction Association and All-China Federation of Industry and Commerce representative conference to a symposium.

    Li Weihan, Minister of the United Front Work Department of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of China, and Ping Jiesan, Deputy Minister, spoke at the meeting.

    More than one hundred industrialist and businessman's family members and female industrialist and business representatives who attended the symposium spoke enthusiastically at the meeting. They expressed that they must actively participate in street production, service work, and social activities, taking the streets as a base for transforming themselves, striving to raise their awareness, and listening to the words of the Party and Chairman Mao.

    Also participating in the symposium were responsible persons from the All-China Women's Federation.

### 统战部邀工商业者家属  女工商业者代表座谈

1960-02-04
第4版()
专栏：

    统战部邀工商业者家属  女工商业者代表座谈
    新华社3日讯　中共中央统一战线工作部，今天下午邀请出席中国民主建国会和中华全国工商业联合会代表大会中的工商业者家属和女工商业者举行座谈会。
    中共中央统一战线工作部部长李维汉、副部长平杰三在会上讲了话。
    参加座谈会的一百多名工商业者家属和女工商业者，热烈地在会上发言。她们表示：一定要积极参加街道生产、服务工作和社会活动，把街道作为改造自己的基地，努力提高自己的觉悟，听党和毛主席的话。
    参加座谈会的还有全国妇联负责人。
