Title: U.S. War Preparations Aimed at Socialist Camp
Author:
Date: 1960-02-25
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, February 24th, New York dispatch: The United States is stepping up the training of "Special Forces" on Okinawa, preparing to airdrop them behind socialist countries in Asia to carry out sabotage activities.

    In Europe: Establishing a vast military reconnaissance and communication system to spy on the military intelligence of socialist countries.

    The "New York Times" revealed some information about this U.S. armed special agent force in a dispatch from Okinawa on the 23rd. The report said that the U.S. Army is training "guerrilla warfare" leaders on Okinawa so that they can operate behind enemy lines in future wars in Asia. The members of this force, named the "First Special Forces," are selected from American senior non-commissioned officers with "above average education and intelligence, and are usually more mature than the average soldier."

    The report said that in the past, the United States would only conduct "Special Forces" activities after the war, and these people "mostly learned their tasks while doing them in combat." According to the new plan, the "Special Forces" are trained in peacetime, and if war breaks out, "they will be immediately ready to drop behind enemy lines at night and carry out their work with a full understanding of what they must do."

    The report said: The United States is now also training two similar units, preparing to perform the same tasks in other areas where conflicts may occur. They are the "Tenth Special Forces" stationed in Western Europe and the "Seventy-seventh Special Forces" stationed at Camp Lejeune in North Carolina.

    Xinhua News Agency, February 23rd, New York dispatch: The United States is establishing a vast military reconnaissance and communication system on the periphery of the Soviet Union and Eastern European socialist countries to spy on the military intelligence of socialist countries and quickly transmit the obtained intelligence to the "European Allied Command" in Paris. This is part of the United States' active preparation for new war activities. Bartholomew, the president of United Press International, issued a telegram from New York today stating that this system begins 530 miles north of the Arctic Circle and extends in an arc to Asia Minor, connecting Norway, Denmark, West Germany, the United Kingdom, Belgium, France, Italy, Greece, and Turkey. It is also building power stations on the Faroe Islands between Scotland and Iceland, and on Malta, Crete, and Cyprus in the Mediterranean. He said that this system has 86 powerful broadcasting and relay stations in nine NATO member states, forming an "electronic border" facing the Soviet Union and Eastern European socialist countries.

    Bartholomew also revealed that the part of this so-called "electronic early warning system" called "High ACE" in Norway has already been completed and "quietly started operating" in August 1958. He said, "This plan is half completed, and its cost in electronic equipment alone amounts to 50 to 75 million U.S. dollars. In addition, each country where the station is located provides land, buildings, and staff."

    According to the American magazine "Time," published yesterday, the United States is also secretly using radar and other means to spy on Soviet "missile tests" in the Middle East.

### 美国备战矛头指向社会主义阵营  在亚洲：加紧训练“特种部队”，准备空投到社会主义国家后方进行破坏活动  在欧洲：建立庞大军事侦察和通讯系统，准备侦察社会主义国家的军事情报

1960-02-25
第6版()
专栏：

    美国备战矛头指向社会主义阵营
    在亚洲：加紧训练“特种部队”，准备空投到社会主义国家后方进行破坏活动
    在欧洲：建立庞大军事侦察和通讯系统，准备侦察社会主义国家的军事情报
    新华社24日讯　纽约消息：美国陆军正在冲绳岛加紧训练准备空投到亚洲社会主义国家后方进行各种各样特务活动的“特种部队”。
    “纽约时报”23日在发自冲绳的一篇电讯中透露了有关美国这支武装特务部队的一些情况。消息说，美国陆军正在冲绳训练“游击战”领导人，以便在亚洲未来的战争中他们可以在敌后作战。这支被命名为“第一特种部队”的成员从“比平均为高的教育和智力水平，并且通常比一般士兵要成熟”的美国高级士官中选拔。
    消息说，在以前，美国要到战争以后才进行“特种部队”的活动，而且这些人“多半是在战斗的情况下边作边学会他们的任务的”。根据新的计划，“特种部队”在和平时期训练，如果发生战争，“他们将马上准备好在夜间投到敌后，在完全了解他们必须怎样做的情况下进行工作”。
    消息说：美国现在还在训练两支同样的部队，准备在可能发生冲突的其他地区执行同样的任务，它们是驻在西欧的“第十特种部队”和驻在美国北卡罗来纳州勒琼营的“第七十七特种部队”。
    据新华社23日讯　纽约消息：美国正在苏联和东欧社会主义国家的外缘建立一个庞大的军事侦察和通讯系统，以侦察社会主义国家的军事情报，并把所得情报迅速转播给巴黎的“欧洲盟军司令部”。这是美国在积极准备新战争活动的一部分。美国合众国际社社长巴塞洛缪今天从纽约发出的一条电讯说，这个系统从北极圈以北五百三十英里的地方开始，呈一个弧形直达小亚细亚，联结了挪威、丹麦、西德、英国、比利时、法国、意大利、希腊和土耳其，并且正在苏格兰和冰岛之间的法罗群岛和地中海的马耳他、克里特、塞浦路斯建筑供电站。他说，这个系统在九个北大西洋集团的成员国里设有八十六个强大的播送和转播台，构成了面对苏联和东欧社会主义国家的“电子边界”。
    巴塞洛缪还透露，这个名叫“高ACE”的所谓“电子警报系统”在挪威的部分早已完成，并且在1958年8月便“悄悄地开始使用”。他说，“这个计划已经完成一半，它仅在电子装置方面的费用即达五千万到七千五百万美元。此外，每个电台所在国提供土地、建筑物和工作人员。”
    据昨天出版的美国“时代”周刊透露，美国还正在中东地区偷偷用雷达等侦察苏联的“导弹试验”。
