Title: Delivering Manure
Author: Liu Zhang
Date: 1960-02-16
Page: 8

    Delivering Manure
    Spring returns to the earth, hundreds of flowers bloom,
    The manure-delivering girls come out of the village,
    Small carts run along the mountain road,
    Like a row of geese in the blue sky.
    Crushing every drop of dew,
    Tilting the shadow of the Western Hills;
    Who says women have low abilities?
    Ten thousand layers of terraced fields built with one hand!
    Hebei Xinglong Banbishan Commune Liu Zhang

### 送粪
刘章
1960-02-16
第8版()
专栏：

    送粪
    春回大地百花开，
    送粪姑娘出庄来，
    小车沿着山路跑，
    好像蓝天雁一排。
    压的颗颗露珠碎，
    压的西山日影歪；
    谁说妇女能力低？
    万重梯田一手盖！
    河北兴隆半壁山公社  刘章
