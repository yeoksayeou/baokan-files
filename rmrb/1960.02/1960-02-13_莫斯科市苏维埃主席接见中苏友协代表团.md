Title: Chairman of the Moscow City Soviet Receives Delegation from the Sino-Soviet Friendship Association
Author:
Date: 1960-02-13
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, February 12th:  Chairman of the Moscow City Soviet, Bobrovnikov, received the delegation from the Sino-Soviet Friendship Association headed by Liu Changsheng on the morning of the 11th.  The delegation was invited to the Soviet Union to participate in activities commemorating the 10th anniversary of the signing of the Sino-Soviet Treaty of Friendship, Alliance and Mutual Assistance.

During the meeting, Chairman Bobrovnikov briefed the delegation on Moscow's construction situation in recent years. The reception took place in a cordial and friendly atmosphere.

Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, February 12th: Liu Changsheng, head of the delegation from the Sino-Soviet Friendship Association, and Li Zhuchen, deputy head of the delegation, visited the Institute of Chinese Studies of the Soviet Academy of Sciences on the afternoon of the 11th.  They were invited to the Soviet Union to participate in activities commemorating the 10th anniversary of the signing of the Sino-Soviet Treaty of Friendship, Alliance and Mutual Assistance.

Deputy Head of Delegation Li Zhuchen briefed the researchers at the institute on China's socialist construction.  Other members of the delegation participated in activities commemorating the 10th anniversary of the signing of the Sino-Soviet Treaty of Friendship, Alliance and Mutual Assistance at units such as the Moscow Shcherbakov Factory.

In the evening, delegation head Liu Changsheng also attended a commemorative evening held at the Moscow Aviation Institute and spoke at the event.

### 莫斯科市苏维埃主席接见中苏友协代表团

1960-02-13
第5版()
专栏：

    莫斯科市苏维埃主席接见中苏友协代表团
    新华社莫斯科12日电　莫斯科市苏维埃主席波布罗夫尼科夫11日上午接见了以团长刘长胜为首的应邀到苏联参加庆祝中苏友好同盟互助条约签订十周年纪念活动的中苏友好协会代表团。
    接见时，波布罗夫尼科夫主席向代表团介绍了莫斯科近年来的建设情形。接见是在亲切友好的气氛中进行的。
    新华社莫斯科12日电　应邀到苏联参加中苏友好同盟互助条约签订十周年纪念活动的中苏友好协会代表团团长刘长胜、副团长李烛尘11日下午访问了苏联科学院中国问题研究所。
    李烛尘副团长向研究所的工作人员们介绍了中国社会主义建设情况。代表团其他成员分别参加莫斯科谢尔巴科夫工厂等单位的庆祝中苏友好同盟互助条约签订十周年纪念活动。
    傍晚，代表团团长刘长胜还参加了莫斯科航空学院举行的纪念晚会，并在晚会上讲了话。
