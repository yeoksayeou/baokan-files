Title: Head of Polish Government Trade Delegation Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1960-02-09
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, February 8th: Mr. B. Burakiewicz, the head of the Polish Government Trade Delegation and Vice Minister of the Polish Ministry of Foreign Trade, arrived in Beijing by plane this afternoon.

Those who went to the airport to welcome him included Lin Haiyun, Vice Minister of Foreign Trade, Chen Boqing, Deputy Director of the Soviet and Eastern European Department of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs, and others.

Mr. Frat, Chargé d'Affaires ad interim of the Polish Embassy in China, Mr. Kolar, Deputy Head of the delegation who arrived earlier, Mr. Szymanski, Commercial Counselor of the Polish Embassy in China, and others also greeted him at the airport.

### 波兰政府贸易代表团团长到京

1960-02-09
第4版()
专栏：

    波兰政府贸易代表团团长到京
    新华社8日讯　波兰政府贸易代表团团长、波兰对外贸易部副部长布腊凯维兹今天下午乘飞机到达北京。
    前往机场欢迎的有对外贸易部副部长林海云，外交部苏联东欧司副司长陈伯清等人。
    波兰驻华大使馆临时代办傅拉托、先期来华的代表团副团长柯拉尔、波兰驻华商务参赞席曼斯基等人也到机场迎接。
