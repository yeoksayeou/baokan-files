Title: TASS Authorized to Issue Statement: The Soviet Union Cannot Ignore Continued Nuclear Explosions
Author:
Date: 1960-02-15
Page: 6

    TASS Authorized to Issue Statement
    The Soviet Union Cannot Ignore Continued Nuclear Explosions
    Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, February 13th: TASS, Moscow, February 13th, authorized to issue the following statement due to France's atomic bomb explosion in the Sahara:
    The Soviet Union's position on the issue of nuclear explosions is clear to all. The Soviet Union has repeatedly advocated for an immediate cessation of all nuclear tests. The Soviet government is committed to promoting and accelerating the reaching of an international agreement on the permanent cessation of nuclear weapon testing and, on August 29, 1959, unilaterally decided not to resume nuclear explosions if Western countries did not resume atomic and hydrogen weapon testing.
    As everyone knows, at the 14th session of the United Nations General Assembly on November 20, 1959, a resolution was adopted at the initiative of many Asian and African countries, demanding that France abandon atomic weapon testing in the Sahara.
    Therefore, regret must be expressed that the French government, disregarding world public opinion, conducted an atomic explosion on February 13th. The Soviet Union believes that each new nuclear weapon explosion, wherever it takes place, cannot but complicate the solution of the current major issue - comprehensive and complete disarmament - and serves the interests of forces advocating the continuation of the nuclear arms race. If nuclear explosions continue, then the Soviet government certainly cannot ignore this and must draw corresponding conclusions to safeguard its own security.
    In the future, the Soviet Union will continue to strive for the early and complete cessation of all nuclear tests, for the conclusion of an international agreement, and for the permanent cessation of such tests.

### 塔斯社受权发表声明  苏联对继续核爆炸不能置之不理

1960-02-15
第6版()
专栏：

    塔斯社受权发表声明
    苏联对继续核爆炸不能置之不理
    新华社14日讯　塔斯社莫斯科13日讯：由于法国在撒哈拉进行原子弹爆炸，塔斯社受权发表声明如下：
    苏联在核爆炸问题上的立场大家都很清楚。苏联不止一次地主张立即停止一切核试验。苏联政府致力于促进和加速达成关于永远停止试验核武器的国际协议，并在1959年8月29日单方面决定不恢复核爆炸，如果西方国家不恢复原子武器和氢武器试验的话。
    大家知道，在1959年11月20日联合国第十四届大会会议上曾根据许多亚非国家的倡议通过了一项决议，要求法国放弃在撒哈拉试验原子武器。
    因此，对于法国政府不顾世界舆论在2月13日进行了原子爆炸一事不能不表示遗憾。苏联认为，每次新的核武器爆炸，无论在什么地方进行，都不能不使当前的主要问题——全面和彻底裁军的解决复杂化，而符合主张继续核军备竞赛的势力的心意。如果核爆炸继续下去的话，那么，苏联政府当然不能对此置之不理，不能不为保障本国的安全作出相应的结论。
    苏联今后仍将为争取及早和完全停止一切核试验、争取缔结国际协议和永远停止这种试验而斗争。
