Title: Japanese Foreign Minister Fujiyama's Remarks Show Japan is a US Base for Aggression in the Far East; The US Can Deploy US Troops Stationed in Japan to Fight Anywhere

Author:
Date: 1960-02-17
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, Tokyo, February 14th: Japanese Foreign Minister Aiichiro Fujiyama admitted on February 12th that if the United States first transfers US troops stationed in Japan to Okinawa, it can deploy the US troops to fight anywhere without "prior consultation" with the Japanese government. When Fujiyama answered questions from Socialist Party members in the House of Representatives Budget Committee regarding the new Japan-U.S. "Security Treaty," he said that U.S. troops transferred to areas outside of Japanese government jurisdiction are no longer considered U.S. troops stationed in Japan. Therefore, the United States is completely free to use its troops for any purpose.

Fujiyama explained that if US troops stationed in Japan are first transferred to Okinawa and then sent to fight in the Taiwan Strait, then "prior consultation" is not required because the US troops are no longer stationed in Japan.

Fujiyama also said that the United States can use its troops stationed in Japan to "coordinate with its troops stationed in South Korea and Taiwan for combat."

Fujiyama's words exposed the falsehood of "prior consultation" and clearly proved that the new Japan-U.S. "Security Treaty" has made Japan a forward base for U.S. imperialism to invade China and other Asian countries.

### 日本外务相藤山的谈话表明  日本是美国侵略远东基地  美国可以把驻日美军派到任何地方作战

1960-02-17
第6版()
专栏：

    日本外务相藤山的谈话表明
    日本是美国侵略远东基地
    美国可以把驻日美军派到任何地方作战
    新华社14日讯　东京消息：日本外务相藤山爱一郎12日承认，如果美国首先把驻日美军调到冲绳岛，就可以不经过同日本政府“事先协商”，把美军派到任何地方去作战。藤山在众议院预算委员会里答复社会党议员提出的有关新日美“安全条约”的问题时说，调往日本政府管辖以外的地区的美国军队不再被认为是驻日美军。因此，美国完全可以自由把它的军队用于任何目的。
    藤山解释说，如果首先把驻日美军调往冲绳岛，然后派到台湾海峡作战，那就不需要经过“事先协商”，因为美军不再驻在日本。
    藤山还说，美国可以用它驻在日本的军队“同它驻在南朝鲜和台湾的军队协同作战。”
    藤山的话暴露了“事先协商”的虚伪性，并且清楚地证明，新的日美“安全条约”已经使日本成为美帝国主义侵略中国和亚洲其他国家的前沿基地。
