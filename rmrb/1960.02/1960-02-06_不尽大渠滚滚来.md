Title: The Endless Great Canal Rolls In
Author: Wang Dagang
Date: 1960-02-06
Page: 8

    The Endless Great Canal Rolls In
    Wang Dagang, Yuncun Chengguan Printing Factory, Hubei

    斩断老鹰崖，劈开大雁岭，要让两山携起手，大渠桥梁跨云头。
    Cut off Eagle Cliff, split open Wild Goose Ridge, wanting the two mountains to join hands, the great canal bridge to span the cloud tops.

    人在桥下走，水在云上流，飞流直下数千尺，好似白练挂山头。
    People walk under the bridge, water flows above the clouds, the waterfall plunges down thousands of feet, like a white silk hanging on the mountain top.

    迈过千重山，绕过万道湾，条条山溪湍湍下，不尽大渠滚滚来。
    Stepping over thousands of mountains, winding around ten thousand bends, every mountain stream rushes down, the endless great canal rolls in.

    白杨树儿齐拍手，野鸟枝头放歌喉，崇山峻岭两边排，恭恭敬敬迎水帅。
    Poplar trees clap their hands in unison, wild birds sing on the branches, towering mountains and ridges line up on both sides, respectfully welcoming the water commander.

    牧童一声“水来了”大渠两岸人如潮。
    The shepherd boy shouts, "The water is coming!" and people throng the banks of the great canal like a tide.

    齐集渠边迎水头，眼看渠水乐悠悠。
    Gathering together by the canal to welcome the head of the water, watching the canal water flowing joyfully.

    小孩抛起五彩帽，青年哥儿吹吹哨。
    Children throw up colorful hats, young men blow whistles.

    山笑水笑人也笑，笑声充满长渠道。
    The mountains laugh, the water laughs, and the people laugh too, laughter fills the long canal.

    党的好领导，党的“三治”好，眼看穷山变富川，甜水灌溉幸福田。
    The Party's good leadership, the Party's "Three Governance" is good, watching the poor mountains transform into rich fields, sweet water irrigates happy fields.
