Title: Eisenhower Attempts to Resurrect the "Eisenhower Doctrine"
Author:
Date: 1960-02-20
Page: 6

    Xinhua News Agency, Washington, February 19th: U.S. President Eisenhower stated that the U.S. government will continue to push the notorious so-called "Eisenhower Doctrine" on Arab countries. In a report to the U.S. Congress regarding the Middle East situation, he portrayed the U.S.'s aggressive act of armed intervention in Lebanon in 1958 as a good thing. He also maliciously provoked the relationship between Arab countries and socialist countries such as the Soviet Union and China, clamoring about the "danger of international communism." He then, under the guise of "anti-communism," guaranteed that the United States would continue to "aid" Middle Eastern countries and continue to implement the resolution on the Middle East issue passed in 1957—the so-called "Eisenhower Doctrine" of "filling the vacuum."

### 艾森豪威尔为“艾森豪威尔主义”招魂

1960-02-20
第6版()
专栏：

    艾森豪威尔为“艾森豪威尔主义”招魂
    据新华社19日讯　华盛顿消息：美国总统艾森豪威尔表示，美国政府将对阿拉伯国家继续推行臭名远扬的所谓“艾森豪威尔主义”。他在向美国国会提出的一项关于中东局势的报告中，把美国在1958年武装干涉黎巴嫩的侵略行为说成是一件好事，他还恶意挑拨阿拉伯国家同苏联和中国等社会主义国家的关系，大叫“国际共产主义的危险”。他接着在“反共”的幌子下，保证美国要继续“援助”中东国家，继续推行1957年所通过的关于中东问题的决议——所谓“填补真空”的“艾森豪威尔主义”。
