Title: Second Powerful Multistage Ballistic Rocket Successfully Launched; Soviet Union Victoriously Completes Rocket Tests in Pacific Region
Author:
Date: 1960-02-02
Page: 6

    Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, February 1st – TASS Moscow, February 1st: In accordance with the plan to test more powerful multistage ballistic rockets for launching heavy Earth satellites and achieving cosmic flights to planets in the solar system, the second launch of such a rocket was carried out on the evening of January 31st this year.
    The penultimate stage of the rocket, along with a model of the final stage, reached the predetermined area of the Pacific Ocean at 19:58 Moscow time on January 31st.
    The flight of the final stage model in the atmosphere was observed. Its splashdown in the water was recorded by shipborne radar, optical, and acoustic stations.
    The measurement data again confirmed the high accuracy of rocket flight control. The launch on January 31st successfully completed this phase of testing. TASS is authorized to state that the area with coordinates specified in the TASS communique of January 8, 1960, can be opened for navigation and flight ahead of schedule on February 1st.

### 第二个强大多级弹道火箭发射成功  苏联胜利完成太平洋地区火箭试验

1960-02-02
第6版()
专栏：

    第二个强大多级弹道火箭发射成功
    苏联胜利完成太平洋地区火箭试验
    新华社莫斯科1日电　塔斯社莫斯科1日电：根据试验威力更强大的多级弹道火箭以发射重型地球卫星和实现向太阳系行星的宇宙飞行的计划，今年1月31日晚间，第二次发射了这种火箭。
    火箭的倒数第二级连同最后一级的模型，于今年1月31日莫斯科时间十九点五十八分到达了太平洋水域的预定地区。
    火箭最后一级的模型在大气中飞行时的情况已经观测到了。它在落入水中时的情况已由船上的无线电定位站、光学站和音响信号站记录下来了。
    根据测量的数据，又一次证实了控制火箭飞行的高度准确性。今年1月31日的发射，成功地完成了这个试验阶段。塔斯社据此受权声明，1960年1月8日塔斯社公报中指明的座标范围内的地区，提前在2月1日可以让船只航行和飞机飞行。
