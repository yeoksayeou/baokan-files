Title: "New" Proposals, Old Conspiracy: U.S. News Agency Talks About U.S. Tactics at Geneva Conference; Aim is to Improve Nuclear Weapons and Continue the Arms Race
Author:
Date: 1960-02-13
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, Washington, 11th (TASS dispatch): Washington journalists, in commenting on the U.S.’s “new” proposals regarding the prohibition of nuclear weapons testing, noted the following two points:

    First, this U.S. proposal may not really be considered new, because the U.S.'s past attitude has not actually changed (as everyone knows, the U.S.'s past attitude was to not discuss "small-scale" underground explosions in an agreement to ban nuclear weapons testing).

    Second, people here have noted that when Eisenhower answered reporters' questions at a press conference, he avoided discussing the Pentagon's and the U.S. Atomic Energy Commission's plans to conduct underground nuclear weapons tests in Nevada this summer. A United Press International report pointed out that the purpose of these nuclear explosions is to improve the nuclear warheads of the U.S.'s "Polaris" and "Minuteman" missiles, to improve many other types of nuclear weapons produced by the U.S., and what this news agency calls "cumbersome" tactical atomic bombs.

    It can be seen from this news agency's report that the U.S. is preparing to conduct underground nuclear weapons tests, not to improve the instruments for detecting these explosions. When talking about improving operational atomic weapons, there can hardly be any other explanation than to say that the U.S. intends to continue the arms race.

### “新”建议　老阴谋  美通讯社谈美国在日内瓦会议上的手法  目的是改进核武器和继续进行军备竞赛

1960-02-13
第6版()
专栏：

    “新”建议　老阴谋
    美通讯社谈美国在日内瓦会议上的手法
    目的是改进核武器和继续进行军备竞赛
    新华社12日讯　据塔斯社华盛顿11日讯：华盛顿新闻界人士在评论美国关于禁止核武器试验问题的“新”建议时，注意到以下两点：
    第一，美国这个建议未必能算是新的，因为美国过去的态度实际上并没有改变（大家知道，美国过去的态度是在禁止核武器试验的协定中不谈“小规模”的地下爆炸）。
    第二，这里的人们注意到，艾森豪威尔在记者招待会上回答记者的问题时，避而不谈五角大楼和美国原子能委员会打算今年夏天在内华达州进行地下核武器试验的问题。合众国际社的报道中指出，这些核爆炸的目的在于改进美国的“北极星”式和“民兵”式导弹的核弹头，在于改进美国生产的许多其他类型的核武器和这家通讯社称之为“笨重的”战术原子弹。
    从这家通讯社的报道中可以看出，美国准备进行地下核武器试验，不是为了改进发现这些爆炸的仪器。在谈到改进作战的原子武器的时候，除了说明美国打算继续进行军备竞赛之外，未必还能有其他的解释。
