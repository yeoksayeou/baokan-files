Title: Mikoyan Visits Cuba at Invitation, Receives Warm Welcome in Havana, and Holds Friendly Talks with the Cuban President
Author:
Date: 1960-02-06
Page: 5

    Xinhua News Agency, Havana, February 5th (TASS): Soviet First Deputy Chairman of the Council of Ministers Mikoyan paid a courtesy call on Cuban President Dorticos on February 4th.
    Mikoyan and Dorticos held warm and friendly talks.
    Xinhua News Agency, Havana, February 4th: Soviet First Deputy Chairman of the Council of Ministers Mikoyan arrived in Havana this afternoon at the invitation of the Cuban government.
    Those at the airport to welcome Mikoyan included Cuban Prime Minister Castro, Cuban Foreign Minister Raúl Roa, Cuban rebel army leader Guevara, Director of the Cuban National Institute of Agrarian Reform Núñez Jiménez, Chairman of the Cuban Popular Socialist Party Marinello, Chairman of the Cuban Peace Committee Marta Fryde, General Secretary of the Cuban Revolutionary Directorate Faure Chomón, as well as representatives of trade unions and other people's organizations.
    At the airport, the Cuban rebel army and revolutionary navy bands played the Cuban national anthem and the Soviet national anthem. People welcomed Mikoyan with applause, flowers, and shouts of "Long live Free Cuba, long live the Soviet Union, long live the friendship between the two countries." After Mikoyan reviewed the guard of honor, he and Castro traveled by car from the airport to Havana, where they were greeted with cheers and salutes from the masses.
    Many Cuban newspapers published the news and photos of Mikoyan's arrival on their front pages.
    Mikoyan will preside over the opening ceremony of the Soviet Science, Technology, and Culture Achievements Exhibition in Havana on the 5th.

### 米高扬应邀到古巴访问  在哈瓦那受到热烈欢迎　和古巴总统进行了友好交谈

1960-02-06
第5版()
专栏：

    米高扬应邀到古巴访问
    在哈瓦那受到热烈欢迎　和古巴总统进行了友好交谈
    新华社5日讯　塔斯社哈瓦那4日讯：苏联部长会议第一副主席米高扬2月4日拜会了古巴共和国总统多尔蒂科斯。
    米高扬和多尔蒂科斯进行了热烈友好的交谈。
    新华社哈瓦那4日电　苏联部长会议第一副主席米高扬应古巴政府的邀请，在今日下午到达哈瓦那。
    到机场欢迎米高扬的有古巴总理卡斯特罗、古巴外交部长劳尔·罗亚、古巴起义军领导人之一格瓦拉、古巴土地改革全国委员会主任努涅斯·希门尼斯、古巴人民社会党主席马里内略、古巴和平委员会主席玛塔·弗赖德、古巴革命指导委员会总书记福雷·萧蒙以及工会和其他人民团体的代表。
    在机场上，古巴起义军和革命海军的乐队奏起了古巴国歌和苏联国歌。人们用掌声、鲜花和高呼自由古巴万岁，苏联万岁，两国友好万岁来欢迎米高扬。米高扬检阅了仪仗队之后，和卡斯特罗一起乘车由机场赴哈瓦那，他们受到了人民群众的欢呼致敬。
    古巴许多报纸在头版刊登了米高扬到达的消息和照片。
    米高扬将在5日在哈瓦那主持苏联科学、技术和文化成就展览会的开幕式。
