　　International Briefs

　　U.S. Fishing Boats Invade Chilean Territorial Waters
　　Santiago Dispatch: The Chilean Air Force Command announced on the 18th that two U.S. fishing boats had invaded Chilean territorial waters in northern Chile.
　　An Air Force Command statement confirmed a report published in the government newspaper "La Nación," which stated that Chilean aircraft discovered two ships without flags in Chilean territorial waters, and they raised U.S. flags when forced to declare their nationality.

　　Israeli Government Proposes New Draft Budget
　　Jerusalem, Israeli Area Dispatch: Israeli Finance Minister Eshkol presented the draft national budget for 1960-1961 at a parliamentary meeting on the evening of the 15th.
　　The new draft budget estimates revenue at 1,574 million Israeli pounds, an increase of over 140 million pounds compared to last year. Among them, approximately 41% of the funds are allocated for military and police affairs.

### 国际简讯

1960-02-21
第4版()
专栏：国际简讯

    国际简讯
    美国渔船侵犯智利领海
    圣地亚哥讯　智利空军司令部18日宣布，两艘美国渔船在智利北部侵犯了智利的领海。
    空军司令部的一项声明证实了政府报纸“民族报”刊载的一条消息，这条消息说，智利飞机在智利领海内发现了两艘没有国旗的船只，它们在被迫表示自己的国籍时升起了美国国旗。
    以色列政府提出新预算草案
    耶路撒冷以色列区讯　以色列财政部长艾希科尔15日晚在议会会议上提出了1960—1961年度国家预算草案。
    新预算草案预计的岁入是十五亿七千四百万以色列镑，比去年增加一亿四千多万镑。其中，约41％的经费是用于军事和警察方面。
