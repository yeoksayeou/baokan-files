Title: Worker's Poetry and Painting: White Clouds Only Dare to Float Beneath My Feet
Author: Zhong Zhiming
Date: 1960-02-16
Page: 2

    Worker's Poetry and Painting
    White Clouds Only Dare to Float Beneath My Feet
    Mount Everest towers into the clouds,
    But my stature is even taller.
    Reaching out, I can pluck the moon,
    White clouds only dare to float beneath my feet.
    Sichuan Construction Worker, Zhong Zhiming

### 职工诗画  白云只敢脚下飘
钟志明
1960-02-16
第2版()
专栏：

    职工诗画
    白云只敢脚下飘
    珠穆朗玛耸云霄，
    我的架子比它高。
    伸手就能摘月亮，
    白云只敢脚下飘。
    四川建筑工人　钟志明
