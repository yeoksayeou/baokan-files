Title: Park Byong-ryul, Representative of the Korean Cultural Delegation, Returns Home
Author:
Date: 1960-02-09
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, February 8th: Park Byong-ryul, representative of the Korean cultural delegation and Director of the Second Department of the Korean Committee for Foreign Cultural Relations, who came to China at the invitation of the Committee for Foreign Cultural Relations to discuss the 1960 implementation plan of the China-Korea Cultural Cooperation Agreement, left Beijing by train this morning to return home.

Among those who saw him off at the station were Luo Jun, Vice-Chairman of the Committee for Foreign Cultural Relations, and Ma Dong-san, Counselor of the Korean Embassy in China.

### 朝鲜文化代表团代表朴秉律回国

1960-02-09
第4版()
专栏：

    朝鲜文化代表团代表朴秉律回国
    新华社8日讯　应对外文化联络委员会邀请前来我国商订中朝文化合作协定1960年执行计划的朝鲜文化代表团代表、朝鲜对外文化联络委员会第二部部长朴秉律，今天上午乘火车离开北京回国。
    到车站欢送的有对外文化联络委员会副主任罗俊，朝鲜驻华大使馆参赞马东山。
