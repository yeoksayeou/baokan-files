International Briefs

    Tanganyikan African Railway Workers Strike
    Dar es Salaam Dispatch: Due to the insistence of 10,000 Tanganyikan African railway workers on their strike struggle, all land transportation in Tanganyika controlled by the East African Railways and Harbors Administration was paralyzed on the 10th.
    The strike began at midnight on the 8th, with workers demanding increased wages and improved working conditions.

    Cambodian Parliament Passes First Five-Year Plan
    Phnom Penh Dispatch: According to Phnom Penh newspapers, the Cambodian National Assembly unanimously passed the first five-year construction plan (1960-1964) on the 9th.
    This plan is named the "Sihanouk Five-Year Plan," and it stipulates a national investment of 8 billion riels over five years, with an investment of 1.5 billion riels in the first year.

    UAR Nationalizes Two Largest Banks in the Egyptian Region
    Cairo Dispatch: The government of the United Arab Republic nationalized the two largest banks in the Egyptian region—Misr Bank and National Bank of Egypt—on the 11th. However, the nationalization law does not apply to companies affiliated with Misr Bank. The law allows Misr Bank to continue owning shares in joint-stock companies.

### 国际简讯

1960-02-15
第6版()
专栏：国际简讯

    国际简讯
    坦噶尼喀非洲铁路工人罢工
    达累斯萨拉姆讯　由于一万名坦噶尼喀非洲铁路工人坚持罢工斗争，坦噶尼喀境内由东非铁路和港口管理局控制的全部陆地交通在10日陷于瘫痪状态。
    这次罢工是从8日午夜开始的，罢工工人要求增加工资和改善劳动条件。
    柬议会通过第一个五年计划
    金边讯　据金边报纸报道，柬埔寨国民议会在9日一致通过了第一个五年建设计划（1960年——1964年）。
    这项计划定名为“西哈努克五年计划”，它规定在五年中由国家投资八十亿瑞尔，第一年的投资为十五亿瑞尔。
    阿联把埃及地区两家最大银行收为国有
    开罗讯　阿拉伯联合共和国政府在11日把埃及地区最大的两家银行——密斯尔银行和埃及国家银行收为国有。但从属于密斯尔银行的那些公司，国有化法律对它们是不适用的。法律允许密斯尔银行继续拥有股份公司的股票。
