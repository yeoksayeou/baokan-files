Title: Delegation of the Sino-Soviet Friendship Association Joins Beijing Workers in Warmly Celebrating the 10th Anniversary of the Sino-Soviet Alliance

Author:
Date: 1960-02-15
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, 14th - The entire delegation of the Sino-Soviet Friendship Association, today joined workers from many factories in Beijing in warmly celebrating the 10th anniversary of the signing of the Sino-Soviet Treaty of Friendship, Alliance and Mutual Assistance.

    Ye Liujin, head of the delegation of the Sino-Soviet Friendship Association, attended a celebration meeting held at the Beijing Electronic Tube Factory, attended by 1,600 people. Xiong Jie, secretary of the factory's Party committee, and head of the Ye Liujin delegation, both spoke at the meeting. Xiong Jie said that the signing of the Sino-Soviet Treaty of Friendship, Alliance and Mutual Assistance, and the fraternal unity of the peoples of China and the Soviet Union, are a strong fortress for defending world peace. He said that the establishment and development of the Beijing Electronic Tube Factory are inseparable from the fraternal assistance of the Soviet Union. He expressed his heartfelt gratitude to the delegation of the Sino-Soviet Friendship Association and the Soviet experts working in the factory on behalf of all the factory's workers. Head of the Ye Liujin delegation said that the Sino-Soviet friendship alliance has already played a huge role in the construction undertakings of the Soviet Union and China and in defending world peace. He said, we are pleased that you have completed the main indicators of the Second Five-Year Plan ahead of schedule. We are delighted with the achievements made by the Chinese brothers. He said that electronic tube production plays a significant role in the development of the socialist economy. He encouraged all the workers of the electronic tube factory to create greater achievements in production.

    Mkhogin, a Soviet expert working in the factory, also spoke at the meeting. After the meeting, the head of the Ye Liujin delegation, accompanied by Xiong Jie and others, visited the factory.

    Today, members of the delegation of the Sino-Soviet Friendship Association, Pokrovsky, Chikhvinsky, Kerimov and Ajidrova, respectively attended celebration meetings held by workers of the Shijingshan Iron and Steel Company, Xuanwu Iron and Steel Plant, Beijing Automobile Manufacturing Plant and Beijing No. 2 Cotton Textile Factory. They also conducted visits after the meetings.

### 苏中友好协会代表团同北京职工一起  热烈庆祝中苏同盟十周年

1960-02-15
第4版()
专栏：

    苏中友好协会代表团同北京职工一起
    热烈庆祝中苏同盟十周年
    新华社14日讯　苏中友好协会代表团全体同志，今天同北京许多工厂的职工一起，热烈庆祝中苏友好同盟互助条约签订十周年。
    苏中友好协会代表团团长叶留金，出席了北京电子管厂举行的有一千六百人参加的庆祝大会。工厂党委书记熊杰和叶留金团长都在大会上讲了话。熊杰说，中苏友好同盟互助条约的签订，中苏两国人民兄弟般的团结，是保卫世界和平的坚强堡垒。他说，北京电子管厂的建立和发展，是和苏联的兄弟般的帮助分不开的。他代表全厂职工向苏中友协代表团和在这个厂工作的苏联专家表示衷心感谢。叶留金团长说，苏中友好同盟，已经对苏中两国的建设事业和对保卫世界和平起了巨大的作用。他说，我们高兴的是你们已经提前完成了第二个五年计划的主要指标。我们对中国兄弟取得的成就感到欢欣鼓舞。他说，电子管生产对社会主义经济发展有着重大的作用。他勉励电子管厂全体职工在生产上创造更大的成就。
    在这个厂工作的苏联专家姆霍金也在会上讲了话。会后，叶留金团长在熊杰等陪同下参观了工厂。
    今天，苏中友好协会代表团团员波克罗夫斯基、齐赫文斯基、凯里莫夫和阿吉卓娃，分别参加了石景山钢铁公司、宣武钢铁厂、北京汽车制造厂和北京第二棉纺织厂职工举行的庆祝大会，会后也都进行了参观访问。
