Title: Chairman Liu to Visit Albania
Author: 
Date: 1960-02-17
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, 16th. Comrade H. Lleshi, Chairman of the Presidium of the People's Assembly of the People's Republic of Albania, has invited Comrade Liu Shaoqi, Chairman of the People's Republic of China, to pay a friendly visit to the People's Republic of Albania at an appropriate time. Chairman Liu Shaoqi has accepted this invitation.

### 刘主席将访问阿尔巴尼亚

1960-02-17
第1版()
专栏：

    刘主席将访问阿尔巴尼亚
    新华社16日讯　阿尔巴尼亚人民共和国人民议会主席团主席哈·列希同志邀请中华人民共和国主席刘少奇同志在适当时机到阿尔巴尼亚人民共和国进行友好访问。刘少奇主席已经接受了这项邀请。
