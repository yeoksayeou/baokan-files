Title: What a Happy Prairie!
Author:
Date: 1960-02-21
Page: 5

What a happy prairie!
The motherland is like the azure sky!
Chairman Mao is the warm sun.
What a happy prairie!
The commune is like a joyful ocean,
The commune members are free goldfish.
What a happy prairie!
The commune's cattle and sheep are as numerous as green grass,
The herdsmen are flowers among the green grass.

### 多么幸福的草原呵

1960-02-21
第5版()
专栏：

    多么幸福的草原呵
    多么幸福的草原呵！
    祖国像蔚蓝的天空！
    毛主席是温暖的太阳。
    多么幸福的草原呵！
    公社像欢腾的海洋，
    社员是自由的金鱼儿。
    多么幸福的草原呵！
    公社的牛羊像绿草一样多，
    牧人是绿草中的花朵。
