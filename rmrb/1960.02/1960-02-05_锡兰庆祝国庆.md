Title: Ceylon Celebrates National Day
Author:
Date: 1960-02-05
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, Colombo, February 4th: Ceylon celebrated its twelfth anniversary of independence today.

Early in the morning, primary school students held a parade and performed gymnastics at Independence Square in Colombo, the capital. At this ceremony, Prime Minister Dahanayake delivered a short speech. Afterwards, a military review was held near the House of Representatives. Governor-General Goonetileke reviewed the troops. Ministers, the Chief Justice, the Speaker of the Senate, and foreign diplomatic envoys to Ceylon attended both ceremonies.

### 锡兰庆祝国庆

1960-02-05
第5版()
专栏：

    锡兰庆祝国庆
    新华社科伦坡4日电　锡兰今天庆祝它的独立十二周年。
    一清早，小学生举行游行，并在首都科伦坡独立广场上举行体操表演。在这个仪式上，达哈纳亚克总理发表了简短讲话。随后，在众议院附近举行了军事检阅。古?狄莱克总督检阅了军队。出席这两个仪式的有部长们、首席法官、参议院议长和各国驻锡兰外交使节等。
