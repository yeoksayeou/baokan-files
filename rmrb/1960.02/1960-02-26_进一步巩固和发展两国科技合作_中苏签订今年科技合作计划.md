Title: Further Consolidating and Developing Scientific and Technological Cooperation Between the Two Countries: China and the Soviet Union Sign This Year's Scientific and Technological Cooperation Plan
Author:
Date: 1960-02-26
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, 25th: The meeting of delegations from the Chinese Scientific and Technological Commission and the State Scientific and Technological Committee of the Soviet Council of Ministers concluded in Moscow on the 24th.

During the meeting, both sides discussed the implementation results of the 1959 cooperation plan for the "Protocol on Joint Scientific and Technological Research by China and the Soviet Union and Soviet Assistance to China in Major Scientific and Technological Research," signed by the governments of China and the Soviet Union on January 18, 1958. They also discussed and signed the annual scientific and technological cooperation plan for 1960.

The 1960 annual scientific and technological cooperation plan was signed by Wu Heng, Vice Chairman of the Scientific and Technological Commission, on the Chinese side, and by Aleksenko, Vice Chairman of the Soviet State Scientific and Technological Committee, on the Soviet side.

Liu Xiao, the Chinese Ambassador to the Soviet Union, and Petukhov, Chairman of the Soviet State Scientific and Technological Committee, attended the signing ceremony.

The communique issued after the meeting stated that during the meeting, the delegations of the Chinese and Soviet Scientific and Technological Commissions noted with satisfaction that in the past two years, thanks to the joint efforts of relevant departments, scientists, and experts of the two countries, a large amount of scientific research and experimental work related to the joint resolution of 122 major scientific and technological projects had been successfully completed. This has significantly promoted the development of science and technology in China. On this basis, the friendly relations and cooperation between scientific research institutions, scientists, and experts of both sides have been further consolidated and strengthened.

During the meeting, the Chinese representatives pointed out that the sincere and substantial assistance provided by relevant Soviet departments, Soviet scientists, and experts in helping China carry out scientific research, in helping China train scientific and technological cadres, and in providing China with scientific and technological data and samples is of great significance to China. The Soviet side believed that the scientific and technological data and experience obtained from relevant Chinese departments were also helpful to the scientific research work of relevant Soviet departments.

In the discussions, both sides expressed their belief that the scientific and technological cooperation between China and the Soviet Union, which is constantly deepening and expanding, will surely achieve new successes in the future.

### 进一步巩固和发展两国科技合作  中苏签订今年科技合作计划

1960-02-26
第5版()
专栏：

    进一步巩固和发展两国科技合作
    中苏签订今年科技合作计划
    新华社莫斯科25日电　中国科学技术委员会和苏联部长会议国家科学技术委员会的代表团会议，于24日在莫斯科结束。
    双方在这次会议中讨论了关于执行中苏两国政府1958年1月18日签订的“关于中苏两国共同进行和苏联帮助中国进行重大科学技术研究的议定书”的1959年合作计划的执行结果，并商谈和签订了1960年年度科学技术合作计划。
    在1960年年度科学技术合作计划上签字的，中国方面是科学技术委员会副主任武衡，苏联方面是苏联国家科学技术委员会副主席阿列克先科。
    中国驻苏联大使刘晓和苏联国家科学技术委员会主席佩图霍夫参加了签字仪式。
    会后发表的公报指出，在这次会议期间，中苏两国科学技术委员会代表团都满意地指出，在过去两年中，由于两国有关部门、科学家和专家的共同努力，顺利地完成了大量有关共同解决一百二十二项重大科学技术项目的科学研究工作及实验工作，这在相当程度上促进了中国科学技术的发展。在此基础上，双方科学研究机关、科学家和专家之间的友好关系和合作又有了进一步的巩固和加强。
    在会议期间，中国代表指出，苏联有关部门、苏联科学家和专家在帮助中国进行科学研究工作方面，在帮助中国培养科学技术干部方面，在向中国提供科学技术资料和样品方面所给予的真诚的大量的援助，对中国具有重要的意义。苏联方面认为，从中国有关部门取得的科学技术资料和经验对苏联有关部门的科学研究工作也有帮助。
    在交谈中双方都表示相信，正在不断加深和扩大的中苏两国的科学技术合作今后定将取得新的成绩。
