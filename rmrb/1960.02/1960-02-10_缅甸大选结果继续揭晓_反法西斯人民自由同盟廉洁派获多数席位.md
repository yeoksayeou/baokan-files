Title: Burmese General Election Results Continue to be Announced; Anti-Fascist People's Freedom League (Clean Faction) Wins Majority of Seats
Author:
Date: 1960-02-10
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, Rangoon, February 9th: According to the election results announced today for 164 constituencies in Burma, the Clean Anti-Fascist People's Freedom League has already won 134 seats in the House of Representatives in this general election, exceeding the majority of 250 seats in the House of Representatives. The Stable Anti-Fascist People's Freedom League won 20 seats, and the remaining 10 seats were won by the Burma National United Front, the Arakan National United Organization, and others.

According to the Constitution of the Union of Burma, the party that wins more than 126 seats in the House of Representatives will be invited to form a government.

### 缅甸大选结果继续揭晓  反法西斯人民自由同盟廉洁派获多数席位

1960-02-10
第6版()
专栏：

    缅甸大选结果继续揭晓
    反法西斯人民自由同盟廉洁派获多数席位
    新华社仰光9日电　据今天宣布的缅甸一百六十四个选区选举结果，廉洁的反法西斯人民自由同盟在这次大选中已经获得国会代表院中的一百三十四席，超过代表院二百五十席的半数，巩固的反法西斯人民自由同盟获得二十席，其他十席分别由缅甸民族团结阵线、亚拉干民族团结组织等获得。
    根据缅甸联邦宪法，在代表院中获得一百二十六席以上的政党将被邀请组织政府。
