Title: China's National Song and Dance Troupe Performs in Mawlamyine for the First Time
Author:
Date: 1960-02-24
Page: 5
Xinhua News Agency, Yangon, February 22nd – The China National Song and Dance Troupe gave its premiere performance in Mawlamyine, Burma’s third largest city, on the 21st.

    Burmese Minister of Culture U Chit Thoung and head of the Chinese Cultural Friendship Delegation Chu Tunan delivered speeches at the opening ceremony. They emphasized that the signing of the Sino-Burmese Treaty of Friendship and Mutual Non-Aggression and the border agreement between the two countries will further consolidate the friendship between the two countries.

    They expressed their belief that under the guidance of the Five Principles of Peaceful Coexistence, the friendly relations and cultural exchanges between the two countries will be further developed on a new basis.

    Approximately 5,000 citizens of Mawlamyine watched the performance. Chinese Ambassador to Burma Li Yimang, as well as U Soe Ya, Commissioner of the Thanintharyi Division of Burma, Deputy Commissioner U Hla Pe, and Assistant Deputy Commissioner U Wan Maung also attended the performance.

### 我国民族歌舞团  在毛淡棉首次演出

1960-02-24
第5版()
专栏：

    我国民族歌舞团
    在毛淡棉首次演出
    新华社仰光22日电　中国民族歌舞团21日在缅甸第三大城市毛淡棉作首次演出。
    缅甸文化部长吴漆东和中国文化友好代表团团长楚图南在开幕仪式上发表了讲话。他们强调指出，中缅友好和互不侵犯条约和两国边界协定的签订，将进一步巩固两国的友谊。
    他们表示相信，在和平共处五项原则的指导下，两国的友好关系和文化交流将在新的基础上获得进一步的发展。
    观看这次演出的约有毛淡棉五千市民。中国驻缅甸大使李一氓以及缅甸丹那沙林行政区专员吴梭雅、副专员吴拉佩和副专员助理吴旺密等也观看了这次演出。
