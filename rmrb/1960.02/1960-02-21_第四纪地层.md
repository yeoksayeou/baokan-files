    Quaternary Strata

    For the convenience of research, geologists divide the time of surface sedimentary strata of the earth into: Archean, Proterozoic, Paleozoic, Mesozoic, and Cenozoic, according to the characteristics of minerals, paleontology, and rocks in various strata. Each era is further divided into several periods.

    The Cenozoic is the newest era in geological history, and the Quaternary is the newest period in the Cenozoic. Quaternary strata are generally the layer of strata covering the Earth's surface.

### 第四纪地层

1960-02-21
第7版()
专栏：小常识

    第四纪地层
    地质学家为了研究方便起见，根据各种地层中矿物、古生物和岩石的特点，把地球表面沉积地层的时间划分为：太古代、元古代、古生代、中生代和新生代。每个代又划分为若干纪。
    新生代是地质史上最新的一个代，第四纪又是新生代中最新的一个纪。第四纪地层一般是复盖在地球最表面的一层地层。
