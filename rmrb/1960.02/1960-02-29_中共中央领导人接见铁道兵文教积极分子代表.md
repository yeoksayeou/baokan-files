Title: Central CCP Leaders Receive Representatives of Activists in Cultural and Educational Work of the Railway Engineering Corps
Author:
Date: 1960-02-29
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, February 28th: Comrade Liu Shaoqi, Vice-Chairman of the Central Committee of the Chinese Communist Party, Comrade Deng Xiaoping, General Secretary, Comrades Peng Zhen and Li Xiannian, members of the Political Bureau, Comrade Lu Dingyi, alternate member of the Political Bureau, Comrade Liu Lantao, alternate secretary of the Secretariat, and Comrade Xie Fuzhi, member of the Central Committee, received all the representatives of the Conference of Activists in Cultural and Educational Work of the Railway Engineering Corps of the Chinese People's Liberation Army this afternoon.

### 中共中央领导人接见铁道兵文教积极分子代表

1960-02-29
第1版()
专栏：

    中共中央领导人接见铁道兵文教积极分子代表
    新华社28日讯　中共中央副主席刘少奇，总书记邓小平，政治局委员彭真、李先念，政治局候补委员陆定一，书记处候补书记刘澜涛和中央委员谢富治等同志，在今天下午接见了中国人民解放军铁道兵文化教育积极分子代表大会的全体代表。
