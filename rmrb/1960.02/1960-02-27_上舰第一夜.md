Title: The First Night Aboard Ship
Author: Cao Ben
Date: 1960-02-27
Page: 8

    The First Night Aboard Ship
    Cao Ben
    I sleep in the hammock,
    The warship seems to be a cradle, gently rocking me,
    My thoughts take wing,
    Soaring freely across the vast sea and sky.
    I listen to the sounds around me,
    My comrades' snores are warm and sweet,
    The engine's song enchants me,
    I can't help but open the porthole.
    I look at the scene on the sea,
    Waves sparkle with silver moonlight,
    The waves are singing aloud,
    I can't help but catch the splashing waves in my hands.
    Ah! Rolling waves!
    Please carry my heartfelt feelings,
    To the beloved people:
    A new sailor's heart is filled with pride and glory.

### 上舰第一夜
曹奔
1960-02-27
第8版()
专栏：

    上舰第一夜
    曹奔
    我睡在吊铺上，
    战舰好像摇篮把我轻轻摇晃，
    我的思想插上翅膀，
    海阔天空到处飞翔。
    我听一听四周的声响，
    战友的鼾声亲切又甜香，
    轮机的奏鸣使我陶醉神往，
    我不禁打开了舷窗。
    我看一看海上的景况，
    浪花闪烁着银色的月光，
    波浪在纵声歌唱，
    我不禁把飞溅的浪花接在手上。
    啊！滚滚的波浪！
    请你把我满腔衷肠，
    带给亲爱的人民：
    一个新水兵心里充满自豪和荣光。
