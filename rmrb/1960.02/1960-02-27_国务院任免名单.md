Title: State Council Appointment and Dismissal List
Author:
Date: 1960-02-27
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency, February 24th. The State Council appointed and dismissed a number of personnel from state organs, embassies abroad, and universities on February 16th. The list is as follows:

    Appointed Cheng Zihua as Vice Chairman of the State Capital Construction Commission;
    Yang Shaoqiao as Vice Minister of the Ministry of Food;
    Qi Yanming, Xu Guangxiao, and Xu Pingyu as Vice Ministers of the Ministry of Culture;
    Xu Ming (female) as Deputy Director of the Premier's Office of the State Council;
    Li Lin as Cultural Counselor of the Embassy of the People's Republic of China in the Democratic People's Republic of Korea.

    Removed Zhang Dingyi from the post of Vice President of Beijing Petroleum Institute;
    Liu Ang from the post of Deputy Director of the Premier's Office of the State Council;
    Lin Lin from the post of Cultural Counselor of the Embassy of the People's Republic of China in the Republic of India.

### 国务院任免名单

1960-02-27
第4版()
专栏：

    国务院任免名单
    新华社24日讯　国务院在2月16日任免了一批国家机关、驻外使馆和高等学校的工作人员。名单如下：
    任命程子华为国家基本建设委员会副主任；
    杨少桥为粮食部副部长；
    齐燕铭、徐光霄、徐平羽为文化部副部长；
    许明（女）为国务院总理办公室副主任；
    李林为中华人民共和国驻朝鲜民主主义人民共和国大使馆文化参赞。
    免去张定一的北京石油学院副院长职务；
    刘昂的国务院总理办公室副主任职务；
    林林的中华人民共和国驻印度共和国大使馆文化参赞职务。
