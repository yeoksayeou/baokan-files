Title: Missile-Laden Island: US Prepares to Strengthen Okinawa Base
Author:
Date: 1960-02-12
Page: 6

    Small Land, Missile Forest
    US Prepares to Strengthen Okinawa Base
    Xinhua News Agency, 10th - New York Dispatch: The United States is stepping up its war preparations in the Far East. According to a recent issue of the American "Newsweek," the United States, after signing a military treaty with Japan, is now preparing to "strengthen its 'ready' forces in Okinawa." The magazine quoted a senior US Army official as saying that in Okinawa, the United States "will add at least one more Army airborne battle group."

    The United States already has an airborne battle group and four regiments of Marines stationed in Okinawa.

    In addition, according to United Press International, the United States is also preparing to send a new missile battalion to Okinawa. There are already two "Nike-Hercules" missile battalions there. US Assistant Secretary of the Army for Logistics, Johnson, will also visit Okinawa on February 16 to study the facilities for the "Hawk" surface-to-air missile.

### 弹丸之地导弹林立  美准备加强冲绳岛基地

1960-02-12
第6版()
专栏：

    弹丸之地导弹林立
    美准备加强冲绳岛基地
    新华社10日讯　纽约消息：美国正在加紧在远东的备战活动。据最近一期美国“新闻周刊”报道，美国在同日本签订了军事条约之后，现在正准备“加强它在冲绳的‘作好准备的’军队”。这家周刊引一个美国陆军高级官员的话说，在冲绳，美国“至少将再增加一个陆军空运战斗大队”。
    美国目前已经有一个空运战斗大队和四团海军陆战队驻在冲绳。
    此外，据合众国际社透露，美国还准备派一个新的导弹营去冲绳。目前那里已经有两个“奈克—赫尔克里士”导弹营。美国负责后勤的助理陆军部长约翰逊2月16日还将到冲绳活动，研究关于“隼”式地对空导弹的设施问题。
