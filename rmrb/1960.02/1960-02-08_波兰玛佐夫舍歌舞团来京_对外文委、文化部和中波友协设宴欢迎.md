Title: Polish Mazowsze Song and Dance Ensemble Arrives in Beijing; Foreign Cultural Commission, Ministry of Culture, and Sino-Polish Friendship Association Host Banquet to Welcome Them
Author:
Date: 1960-02-08
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, 7th: The Foreign Cultural Liaison Committee, the Ministry of Culture, and the Chinese-Polish Friendship Association jointly held a banquet tonight to warmly welcome all the artists of the Polish Mazowsze Song and Dance Ensemble, led by Bogusław Płusa, representative of the Polish Ministry of Culture and Art, and Mira Zimińska-Sygietyńska, the head of the ensemble. The song and dance ensemble arrived in Beijing by train this morning from Tianjin.

Shen Yanbing, Minister of Culture, Hu Yuzhi, Deputy Minister, Zhang Zhixiang, Deputy Director of the Foreign Cultural Liaison Committee, and He Chengxiang, Vice President of the Chinese-Polish Friendship Association, attended the banquet. Polish Chargé d'Affaires in China, Furato, was also invited to attend the banquet.

Zhang Zhixiang, Deputy Director of the Foreign Cultural Liaison Committee, and Shen Yanbing, Minister of Culture, spoke at the banquet, warmly welcoming the distinguished guests.

Zhang Zhixiang said in his speech that it is a joyous event in our cultural life in the early spring of 1960 that the old friends of the renowned Polish Mazowsze Song and Dance Ensemble are visiting our country for the second time. The ensemble's visit and performances will undoubtedly make important contributions to further enhancing the fraternal friendship between the people of the two countries.

Shen Yanbing said in his speech that the arrival of the Polish Mazowsze Song and Dance Ensemble has made the spring atmosphere in Beijing even stronger. He wished the ensemble success in their performances in China, wished the Sino-Polish friendship further development, and wished the flowers of Sino-Polish cultural exchange flourish even more luxuriantly.

Polish Chargé d'Affaires in China, Furato, said at the banquet that the visit of the Polish Mazowsze Song and Dance Ensemble to China marks a further development of Sino-Polish cultural exchange and Sino-Polish friendship. He said that the ensemble's visit and performances in China will add a cornerstone to the building of Sino-Polish friendship.

Bogusław Płusa, representative of the Polish Ministry of Culture and Art, and Mira Zimińska-Sygietyńska, head of the ensemble, also spoke at the banquet, wishing the Sino-Polish friendship further consolidation and development and thanking the Chinese hosts for their warm welcome.

Also present at tonight's banquet were famous figures from the capital's literary and art circles.

When the artists of the song and dance ensemble arrived in Beijing this morning, they were warmly welcomed by the heads of the Foreign Cultural Liaison Committee, the Ministry of Culture, the Chinese-Polish Friendship Association, and more than 200 people from the capital's literary and art circles.

Xinhua News Agency, Tianjin, 7th: The Polish People's Republic "Mazowsze" Song and Dance Ensemble left Tianjin for Beijing this morning after performing three wonderful Polish folk song and dance art performances in Tianjin.

During the performances, Liu Zihou, Governor of Hebei Province, Zhang Minghe, Deputy Governor, and others received the artists of the song and dance ensemble. Lou Ningxian, Deputy Mayor of Tianjin, held a reception for the song and dance ensemble to congratulate them on their successful performances.

### 波兰玛佐夫舍歌舞团来京  对外文委、文化部和中波友协设宴欢迎

1960-02-08
第4版()
专栏：

    波兰玛佐夫舍歌舞团来京
    对外文委、文化部和中波友协设宴欢迎
    新华社7日讯　对外文化联络委员会、文化部和中国波兰友好协会今晚联合举行宴会，热烈欢迎由波兰文化艺术部代表鲍古斯瓦夫·普瓦萨和团长米拉·齐明斯卡·塞格廷斯卡率领的波兰玛佐夫舍歌舞团的全体艺术家们。歌舞团是在今天上午由天津乘火车到达北京的。
    文化部部长沈雁冰、副部长胡愈之，对外文化联络委员会副主任张致祥，中国波兰友好协会副会长何成湘都出席了宴会。波兰驻中国大使馆临时代办傅拉托也应邀出席了宴会。
    对外文化联络委员会副主任张致祥和文化部部长沈雁冰在宴会上讲话，热烈欢迎贵宾。
    张致祥在讲话中说，久负盛名的波兰玛佐夫舍歌舞团的老朋友们第二次来我国访问，这是1960年初春我们文化生活中的一个喜讯。歌舞团的访问演出，必将为进一步增进两国人民的兄弟友谊，作出重要的贡献。
    沈雁冰在讲话中说，波兰玛佐夫舍歌舞团的到来，使北京的春天气息更加浓厚，他祝歌舞团在中国演出的成功，祝中波友谊更加发展，祝中波文化交流事业的花朵开得更加茂盛。
    波兰驻中国大使馆临时代办傅拉托在宴会上讲话说，波兰玛佐夫舍歌舞团访问中国，标志着波中文化交流事业和波中友谊的进一步发展。他说，歌舞团在中国的访问演出，将为中波友谊大厦添一块基石。
    波兰文化艺术部代表鲍古斯瓦夫·普瓦萨和歌舞团团长米拉·齐明斯卡·塞格廷斯卡也先后在宴会上讲话，祝波中友谊进一步巩固和发展并感谢中国主人对他们的热情欢迎。
    出席今晚宴会的，还有首都文艺界的著名人士。
    歌舞团的艺术家们在今天上午到达北京的时候，受到了对外文化联络委员会、文化部、中波友好协会的负责人，以及首都文艺界人士二百多人的热情欢迎。
    据新华社天津7日电　波兰人民共和国“玛佐夫舍”歌舞团在天津演出三场精采的波兰民间歌舞艺术后，今天上午离开天津去北京。
    在演出期间，河北省省长刘子厚、副省长张明河等接见了歌舞团的艺术家们，天津市副市长娄凝先为歌舞团举行酒会，祝贺他们的演出成功。
