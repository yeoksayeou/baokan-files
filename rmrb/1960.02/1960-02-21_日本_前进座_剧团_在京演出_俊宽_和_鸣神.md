Title: Japanese "Zenshinza" Theater Troupe Performs "Shunkan" and "Narukami" in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1960-02-21
Page: 3

    Xinhua News Agency, February 20th. In response to the request of the capital's audience, the Japanese "Zenshinza" Theater Troupe performed "Shunkan" and "Narukami" at the Capital Theater tonight.

    Guo Moruo, Vice Chairman of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress, watched the performance and received the head of the troupe, Cho Juro Kawarasaki, the deputy head, Utaemon Nakamura, and Gasei Miyagawa, as well as the main actors during the intermission. He also went on stage to shake hands and congratulate the actors after the performance.

    Ding Xilin, Vice President of the Chinese People's Association for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries, Wu Xue, Dean of the Youth Art Theater, and others were present at the reception. Japanese peace activist in Beijing, Kinkazu Saionji, was also present.

### 日本“前进座”剧团  在京演出“俊宽”和“鸣神”

1960-02-21
第3版()
专栏：

    日本“前进座”剧团
    在京演出“俊宽”和“鸣神”
    新华社20日讯　日本“前进座”剧团应首都观众要求，今晚在首都剧场加演一场，演出了“俊宽”和“鸣神”。
    人大常委会副委员长郭沫若观看了演出，并在休息期间接见了剧团团长河原崎长十郎，副团长中村翫右卫门和宫川雅青，以及主要演员。并在演出结束后上台向演员们握手祝贺。
    接见时在座的，有中国人民对外文化协会副会长丁西林、青年艺术剧院院长吴雪等。日本在京和平人士西园寺公一也在场。
