Title: Czechoslovak Academy of Sciences Delegation Arrives in Beijing
Author: 
Date: 1960-02-01
Page: 4

    Xinhua News Agency, January 31st: The delegation from the Czechoslovak Academy of Sciences, led by Academician Prusik, along with members Corresponding Academician Jeliye and Dr. Bilid, arrived in Beijing by train today from Pyongyang. The delegation is in China to sign the 1960 execution plan for scientific cooperation between the two academies, according to the Sino-Czech Academy of Sciences scientific cooperation agreement.
    The delegation's other two members, Academician Burjichka and Academician Silatsky, arrived in Beijing by plane from Prague on the 29th.
    Greeting the delegation at the station today were Xie Xinhe, Deputy Secretary-General of the Chinese Academy of Sciences, Liu Daosheng, Deputy Director of the Department of Philosophy and Social Sciences, Liu Dagang, Director of the Institute of Chemistry, and Wang Tuo, Director of the Liaison Bureau, among others.

### 捷克斯洛伐克科学院代表团到京

1960-02-01
第4版()
专栏：

    捷克斯洛伐克科学院代表团到京
    新华社31日讯　捷克斯洛伐克科学院代表团团长普鲁司克院士和团员耶力业通讯院士、白利德博士，今天乘火车从平壤到达北京。代表团是根据中捷两国科学院科学合作协议来中国与中国科学院签订1960年两国科学院合作执行计划的。
    代表团的另外两位团员布尔吉契卡院士和西拉茨基院士，已经在29日乘飞机从布拉格到达北京。
    今天到车站欢迎代表团的有中国科学院副秘书长谢鑫鹤、哲学社会科学部副主任刘导生、化学研究所所长柳大纲、联络局局长王拓等。
