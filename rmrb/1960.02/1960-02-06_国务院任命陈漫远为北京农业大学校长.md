Title: State Council Appoints Chen Manyuan as President of Beijing Agricultural University
Author:
Date: 1960-02-06
Page: 6

    State Council Appoints Chen Manyuan as President of Beijing Agricultural University
    Xinhua News Agency, 4th—The State Council appointed Chen Manyuan as President of Beijing Agricultural University on January 20th, and Fu Qinghua as Vice President. At the same time, Sun Xiaocun was relieved of his position as President of Beijing Agricultural University.

### 国务院任命陈漫远为北京农业大学校长

1960-02-06
第6版()
专栏：

    国务院任命陈漫远为北京农业大学校长
    新华社4日讯　国务院1月20日任命陈漫远为北京农业大学校长，傅清华为副校长。同时免去孙晓村的北京农业大学校长职务。
