Title: Panchen Leaves Xining for Tibet
Author:
Date: 1960-02-15
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Xining, February 14th. Panchen Erdeni Chökyi Gyaltsen, Vice Chairman of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress, Acting Director of the Preparatory Committee for the Tibet Autonomous Region, and Pagbalha Geleg Namgyal, Vice Chairman of the National Committee of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference and Deputy Director of the Preparatory Committee for the Tibet Autonomous Region, along with accompanying monastic and secular officials, flew from Xining to Tibet by special plane this afternoon. Zhou Renshan, Deputy Secretary of the Tibet Working Committee of the Communist Party of China, also returned to Tibet on the same plane today.

Wang Feng, Deputy Minister of the United Front Work Department of the CPC Central Committee and Deputy Director of the Nationalities Affairs Commission, and Meng Dingjun, Director of the United Front Work Department of the CPC Gansu Provincial Committee and Vice Chairman of the Gansu Provincial Committee of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference, who had accompanied Panchen Erdeni and Pagbalha to Xining, went to the airport to see them off.

Those who went to the airport to see them off also included Gao Feng, First Secretary of the CPC Qinghai Provincial Committee, Yuan Renyuan, Secretary of the Provincial Committee and Governor of Qinghai Province, Colonel Qi Weichuan of the Qinghai Military Region, and other leading Party, government, and military officials, as well as more than 300 representatives from all ethnic groups and walks of life.

During their stay in Xining, Panchen Erdeni and Pagbalha visited Ta'er Monastery in Huangzhong County, the birthplace of Tsongkhapa, the founder of the Yellow Sect of Tibetan Buddhism, and participated in the annual Lantern Festival activities there.

### 班禅离西宁去西藏

1960-02-15
第4版()
专栏：

    班禅离西宁去西藏
    新华社西宁14日电　全国人民代表大会常务委员会副委员长、西藏自治区筹备委员会代理主任委员班禅额尔德尼·却吉坚赞，政协全国委员会副主席、西藏自治区筹备委员会副主任委员帕巴拉·卓列朗杰和随行的僧俗官员等，今天下午从西宁乘专机飞往西藏，中共西藏工委副书记周仁山也在今天同机返回西藏。
    陪同班禅额尔德尼和帕巴拉到西宁的中共中央统战部副部长、民族事务委员会副主任汪锋和中共甘肃省委统战部部长、政协甘肃省委员会副主席蒙定军前往机场欢送。
    到机场欢送的，还有中共青海省委第一书记高峰，省委书记、青海省省长袁任远，青海军区齐渭川大校等党政军负责人和各族各界代表三百多人。
    班禅额尔德尼和帕巴拉在西宁期间，曾去湟中县朝拜黄教始祖宗喀巴的诞生地——塔尔寺，并且在那里参加了一年一度的灯节活动。
