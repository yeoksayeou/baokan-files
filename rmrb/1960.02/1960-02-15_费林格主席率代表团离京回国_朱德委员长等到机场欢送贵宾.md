Title: Chairman Fierlinger Leads Delegation Leaving Beijing for Home; Chairman Zhu De and Others Welcomed the Distinguished Guests at the Airport
Author:
Date: 1960-02-15
Page: 1

    Xinhua News Agency, February 14th: Chairman Fierlinger of the Czechoslovak National Assembly and the Czechoslovak National Assembly delegation he led, left Beijing by plane today to return home, after a three-day stopover in Beijing at the invitation of the Chinese government.
    Chairman Zhu De went to the airport to see off the distinguished guests. Those who also saw them off at the airport included Vice-Chairman Lin Feng, members of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress Wang Weizhou, Shi Liang, Wu Xinyu, Zhang Su, Zhao Shoushan, Nan Hanchen, Vice Minister of Foreign Affairs Zeng Yongquan, and Vice Mayor of Beijing Le Songsheng, among others. Young women of the capital presented flowers to the distinguished guests.
    Czechoslovak Ambassador to China, Bušiňák, as well as diplomatic envoys and officials from India, Indonesia, Cambodia, and the Democratic Republic of Vietnam, which the parliamentary delegation had previously visited, also went to the airport to see them off.
    During their stay in Beijing, Chairman Fierlinger and the other distinguished guests visited the Sino-Czech Friendship People's Commune, toured factories and new buildings in Beijing, and visited Beijing's famous historical sites.

### 费林格主席率代表团离京回国  朱德委员长等到机场欢送贵宾

1960-02-15
第1版()
专栏：

    费林格主席率代表团离京回国
    朱德委员长等到机场欢送贵宾
    新华社14日讯　捷克斯洛伐克国民议会主席费林格和由他率领的捷克斯洛伐克国民议会代表团，应邀在北京作了三天停留以后，今天乘飞机离开北京回国。
    朱德委员长到机场欢送贵宾。到机场欢送的还有林枫副委员长，人大常委会委员王维舟、史良、武新宇、张苏、赵寿山、南汉宸，外交部副部长曾涌泉，北京市副市长乐松生等人。首都的女青年向贵宾们献了鲜花。
    捷克斯洛伐克驻中国大使布希尼亚克，以及议会代表团曾经访问过的印度、印度尼西亚、柬埔寨、越南民主共和国驻中国使节和外交官员，也到机场欢送。
    费林格主席和其他贵宾在北京期间，曾经访问了中捷友好人民公社，参观了工厂和北京的新建筑，并且游览了北京的名胜古迹。
