Title: Wu Yuzhang, Vice President of the Sino-Soviet Friendship Association, Holds a Banquet to Bid Farewell to the Delegation of the Soviet-Chinese Friendship Association
Author:
Date: 1960-02-23
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, February 22nd: Wu Yuzhang, Vice President of the Sino-Soviet Friendship Association, held a banquet this evening to bid farewell to the delegation of the Soviet-Chinese Friendship Association, led by Yelyutin, Vice Chairman of the Council of the Soviet-Chinese Friendship Association and Minister of Higher and Secondary Specialized Education of the Soviet Union. The delegation of the Soviet-Chinese Friendship Association participated in the celebrations of the 10th anniversary of the signing of Sino-Soviet Treaty of Friendship, Alliance and Mutual Assistance in our country and visited various places. They will return to China on the 23rd.

At the banquet, Wu Yuzhang said that in these days, the people of China and the Soviet Union have jointly and enthusiastically celebrated the 10th anniversary of the signing of the Sino-Soviet Treaty of Friendship, Alliance and Mutual Assistance, which is a vivid manifestation of the rock-solid unity of the 850 million people of our two countries. He also said that the visit of the delegation of the Soviet-Chinese Friendship Association to China has made a beneficial contribution to further enhancing the understanding and friendship between the people of China and the Soviet Union.

In his speech, Head of Delegation Yelyutin said that the delegation of the Soviet-Chinese Friendship Association saw the manifestation of the fraternal friendship between the two countries of the Soviet Union and China everywhere in China. He wished that the unbreakable fraternal friendship between the people of the Soviet Union and China would be strengthened forever. When talking about his impressions of visiting China, he said that the socialist construction cause of the Chinese people is advancing victoriously and rapidly, and we are extremely happy about the achievements made by the Chinese people in socialist construction.

At the banquet filled with a friendly atmosphere, the guests and hosts toasted each other, talked enthusiastically, and jointly wished the great Sino-Soviet friendship would last forever.

Antonov and Sudarikov, counselors of the Soviet Embassy in China, were invited to attend the banquet.

Among those present at the banquet were Li Dequan and Shao Lizi, Vice Presidents of the Sino-Soviet Friendship Association; Qian Junrui, Secretary-General; Ge Baoquan and Li Zhanwu, Deputy Secretaries-General; Liu Aifeng, Vice Minister of Education; Yang Shu, Vice President of the Beijing Sino-Soviet Friendship Association; and people from various circles.

### 中苏友协副会长吴玉章举行宴会  欢送苏中友协代表团

1960-02-23
第1版()
专栏：

    中苏友协副会长吴玉章举行宴会
    欢送苏中友协代表团
    新华社22日讯　中苏友好协会总会副会长吴玉章今天晚间举行宴会，欢送由苏中友好协会理事会副主席、苏联高等教育和中等专业教育部部长叶留金率领的苏中友好协会代表团。苏中友好协会代表团在我国参加了中苏友好同盟互助条约签订十周年庆祝活动并在各地访问之后，将在23日回国。
    吴玉章在宴会上讲话说，在这些日子里，中苏两国人民共同热烈庆祝了中苏友好同盟互助条约签订十周年，这是我们两国八亿五千万人民坚如磐石地团结一致的生动表现。他还说，苏中友好协会代表团在中国的访问，对进一步增进中苏两国人民之间的了解和友谊，作出了有益的贡献。
    叶留金团长在讲话中说，苏中友好协会代表团在中国到处看到了苏中两国兄弟般友谊的表现。他祝牢不可破的苏中两国人民的兄弟友谊永远增强。他在谈述访问中国的印象时说，中国人民的社会主义建设事业正在胜利地飞速地前进着，我们对中国人民在社会主义建设中取得的成就感到无比的高兴。
    在充满友好气氛的宴会上，宾主互相敬酒、热情交谈，共同祝伟大的中苏友谊万古长青。
    苏联驻中国大使馆参赞安东诺夫、苏达利柯夫应邀出席了宴会。
    出席宴会作陪的，有中苏友好协会总会副会长李德全、邵力子，秘书长钱俊瑞，副秘书长戈宝权、李沾吾，教育部副部长刘皑风，北京市中苏友好协会副会长杨述等各方面的人士。
