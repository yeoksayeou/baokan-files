Title: A Group of Soviet Civil War Veterans Begins Collecting Materials on Chinese Soldiers
Author:
Date: 1960-02-19
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, Riga, February 17th: According to TASS, Riga, February 15th: A group of veterans who participated in the Soviet Civil War – former Latvian sharpshooters – has formed a group to begin collecting memoirs and archival materials related to the Chinese Wu Changfu (transliteration) platoon, which fought in Latvia in 1919 for the victory of Soviet power.

This group is led by Miller, the deputy company commander of the International Company at the time. The International Company was composed of Latvian, Chinese, and Hungarian platoons.

Miller and other veterans recalled that in all battles, the Chinese comrades demonstrated an extremely firm example. These veterans still remember a battle story in the Luza area of Latvia. A Chinese machine gunner, after establishing a firing position in a haystack, was surrounded. The enemy asked him to surrender, and he replied: Only the Soviet regime can order the abandonment of the position.

### 一批苏联国内战争老战士  开始搜集中国战士的材料

1960-02-19
第5版()
专栏：

    一批苏联国内战争老战士
    开始搜集中国战士的材料
    新华社17日讯　据塔斯社里加15日讯：一批参加苏联国内战争的老战士——前拉脱维亚的狙击兵组成了一个小组，开始搜集有关1919年为争取苏维埃政权的胜利而在拉脱维亚进行战斗的中国吴长福（译音）排的回忆材料和档案材料。
    这个小组由当时的国际连副连长米列尔领导。国际连是由拉脱维亚排、中国排和匈牙利排组成的。
    米列尔和其他老战士追溯说，在所有的战斗中，中国同志都表现出是极其坚定的榜样。这些老战士都还记得在拉脱维亚卢扎市地区的一个战斗故事。一个中国机枪手在干草垛中建立了火力阵地之后被包围了，敌人要他投降，他回答说：只有苏维埃政权下命令，才能放弃阵地。
