Title: Afghan Prime Minister Daoud Khan Expresses Welcome for Khrushchev's Visit to Afghanistan
Author:
Date: 1960-02-19
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, Kabul, February 18th (TASS): Afghan Prime Minister Mohammed Daoud Khan told a TASS correspondent on the 17th that he was pleased that Soviet Chairman of the Council of Ministers Khrushchev had accepted the invitation from the King of Afghanistan and the Afghan government to visit Afghanistan.

He said, "Khrushchev visited our country in 1955. At that time, he received the warmest welcome here." He emphasized, "I believe that the Afghan people, who are eagerly awaiting his visit, will now warmly welcome their distinguished guest."

Speaking on the issue of disarmament, Daoud Khan said, "I read with satisfaction the appeal from the Supreme Soviet of the Soviet Union to the governments and parliaments of all countries in the world, and I welcome the Soviet Union's peace initiatives. The proposal for complete and thorough disarmament put forward by Chairman Khrushchev on behalf of the Soviet government at the United Nations has been welcomed by the people of all countries. The Afghan people and government also welcome with satisfaction the news of the practical steps taken by the Soviet government."

In response to a question from the TASS correspondent, Daoud Khan also praised the work of Soviet experts in Afghanistan.

### 阿富汗首相达乌德汗表示  欢迎赫鲁晓夫访问阿富汗

1960-02-19
第5版()
专栏：

    阿富汗首相达乌德汗表示
    欢迎赫鲁晓夫访问阿富汗
    新华社18日讯　据塔斯社喀布尔17日讯：阿富汗首相穆罕默德·达乌德汗17日对塔斯社记者说，他对苏联部长会议主席赫鲁晓夫接受阿富汗国王和阿富汗政府请他访问阿富汗的邀请表示满意。
    他说，“赫鲁晓夫在1955年访问过我国。当时，他在这里受到了最热烈的欢迎”。他强调指出，“我相信，急切盼望着他来访问的阿富汗人民，现在也会热烈地欢迎他们的贵宾。”
    达乌德汗在谈到裁军问题时说，“我满意地读了苏联最高苏维埃致世界各国政府和议会的呼吁书，并且欢迎苏联的和平倡议。赫鲁晓夫主席代表苏联政府在联合国提出的全面彻底裁军的建议，受到世界各国人民的欢迎。阿富汗人民和政府也满意地欢迎苏联政府采取了实际步骤的消息。”
    达乌德汗在回答塔斯社记者的问题时还赞扬了苏联专家在阿富汗的工作。
