	International Briefs
	Algerian Provisional Government Appoints Ambassador to the UAR
	Cairo Dispatch: The Provisional Government of the Republic of Algeria appointed Ahmed Tewfik El Madani as its ambassador to the United Arab Republic on the 17th. This is the first ambassador dispatched by the Algerian Provisional Government since its establishment.
	Lebanese Cabinet Decides to Increase Parliamentary Seats
	Beirut Dispatch: According to reports in the Lebanese newspapers "Al-Sharq" and "Al-Nahar" on the 18th, the Lebanese Cabinet decided at a meeting on the evening of the 17th to increase the number of parliamentarians from the current sixty-six to ninety-nine. The newspapers stated that the cabinet meeting also agreed on the allocation of the new parliamentary seats as follows: Maronites thirty, Sunnis twenty, Shiites eighteen, Greek Orthodox eleven, Greek Catholics six, Druze six, Armenian Orthodox four, Armenian Catholic one, minorities two, and Protestants one.

### 国际简讯

1960-02-20
第6版()
专栏：国际简讯

    国际简讯
    阿临时政府任命驻阿联大使
    开罗讯　阿尔及利亚共和国临时政府17日指派艾哈迈德·陶菲克·迈达尼为驻阿拉伯联合共和国大使。这是阿尔及利亚临时政府成立以来派出的第一个大使。
    黎巴嫩内阁决定增加议会席位
    贝鲁特讯　据黎巴嫩“东方报”和“日报”18日报道，黎巴嫩内阁在17晚的一次会议上决定将议员人数从目前的六十六人增加到九十九人。报纸说，内阁会议还同意新议员名额的分配如下：马龙派三十人、逊尼派二十人、十叶派十八人、希腊正教派十一人、天主教希腊派六人、德鲁兹派六人、亚美尼亚正教派四人、天主教亚美尼亚派一人、少数民族两人和新教徒一人。
