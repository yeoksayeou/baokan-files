Title: "Giant" Added to Sanmenxia Construction Site
Author:
Date: 1960-02-16
Page: 2

Xinhua News Agency Dispatch:

　　A giant cable crane installed at the Sanmenxia Water Conservancy Project construction site on the Yellow River has recently been officially put into production.

　　This giant cable crane weighs two thousand three hundred tons, spans eight hundred and seventy meters across the Yellow River, and is capable of lifting twenty tons of workpieces. After it is put into production, it will provide tremendous transportation power for accelerating the installation of the dam's metal structures and the pouring of concrete.

### 三门峡工地添“巨人”

1960-02-16
第2版()
专栏：

    三门峡工地添“巨人”
    在黄河三门峡水利枢纽工程工地安装的巨型缆索起重机，最近正式投入生产。
    这架巨型缆索起重机体重二千三百吨，横跨黄河八百七十米，能够起重二十吨的工作物。它投入生产后，将为加快大坝金属结构的安装和混凝土的浇筑，提供巨大的运输力量。（据新华社讯）
