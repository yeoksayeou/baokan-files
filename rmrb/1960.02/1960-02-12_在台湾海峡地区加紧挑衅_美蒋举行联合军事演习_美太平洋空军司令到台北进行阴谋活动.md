Title: Intensifying Provocations in the Taiwan Strait Area: US-Chiang Kai-shek Hold Joint Military Exercises; US Pacific Air Force Commander Conducts Conspiracy Activities in Taipei
Author:
Date: 1960-02-12
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, 11th: Taipei News: The United States and the Chiang Kai-shek clique have recently conducted a series of provocative military exercises in the Taiwan Strait area.

According to a "Pan Asia News Agency" Taipei dispatch, the United States and the Chiang Kai-shek navy held a joint exercise in the Taiwan Strait in early February, with the US Seventh Fleet sending submarines to participate. Prior to this, according to Taiwan newspaper reports, under the command of the US Military Assistance Advisory Group (MAAG) Navy Section in Taiwan, Chiang's naval and air force units conducted a large-scale amphibious assault landing operation exercise on the southern coast of Taiwan on January 17. On January 18, the US MAAG Air Force Section in Taiwan also directed Chiang's paratrooper units to conduct parachute and airdrop exercises in Pingtung. C-46 transport aircraft and C-119 "Flying Boxcar" transport aircraft supplied by the United States to Chiang Kai-shek's air force participated in the exercise. Tens of thousands of Chiang Kai-shek soldiers and police also held a so-called "atomic protection exercise" in Taitung and Hualien on January 12.

According to Taiwan newspaper reports, US Pacific Air Force Commander O'Donnell and US Thirteenth Air Force Commander Mooreman went to Taiwan from February 6 to 9 to carry out war conspiracy activities. O'Donnell and others met with Chiang Kai-shek in Taipei and also held separate meetings with US military generals stationed in Taiwan and Chiang Kai-shek's air force personnel.

### 在台湾海峡地区加紧挑衅  美蒋举行联合军事演习  美太平洋空军司令到台北进行阴谋活动

1960-02-12
第6版()
专栏：

    在台湾海峡地区加紧挑衅
    美蒋举行联合军事演习
    美太平洋空军司令到台北进行阴谋活动
    新华社11日讯　台北消息：美国和蒋介石集团最近在台湾海峡地区接连举行挑衅性的军事演习。
    据“泛亚社”台北消息透露，美国和蒋介石海军2月初曾在台湾海峡举行了一次联合演习，美国第七舰队派出了潜艇参加。在这以前，据台湾报纸消息，在美国驻台湾军事援助顾问团海军组的指挥下，蒋军海空军部队1月17日曾在台湾南部海岸举行大规模的两栖部队突击登陆作战演习。1月18日，美国驻台湾军事援助顾问团空军组又指挥蒋军伞兵部队在屏东举行空降和空投演习。由美国供给蒋介石空军的C—四六式运输机和C—一一九式“飞行车厢”运输机参加了这次演习。几万名蒋军官兵和警察还在1月12日在台东花莲举行了所谓“原子防护演习”。
    据台湾报纸消息，美国太平洋空军司令奥当纳耳和美国第十三航空队司令穆尔曼在2月6日到9日曾到台湾进行战争阴谋活动。奥当纳耳等在台北曾与蒋介石会谈，并且曾与侵台美军将领和蒋介石空军负责人员分别举行会谈。
