Title: Vietnamese Cultural Delegation Leaves Beijing to Visit Various Places
Author:
Date: 1960-02-19
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, 18th – The Vietnamese Cultural Delegation, invited by the Commission for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries to come to China to negotiate the 1960 implementation plan of the Sino-Vietnamese cultural cooperation agreement, left Beijing by train today to visit various places after signing this year's implementation plan with China and visiting Beijing.

Those who saw them off at the station included Zhang Zhixiang and Ding Xilin, Vice-Chairmen of the Commission for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries, Hu Yuzhi, Vice-Minister of Culture, and Liu Ziyu, Deputy Director of the General Office of the Ministry of Education.

Tran Trong Quat, Chargé d'Affaires ad interim of the Vietnamese Embassy in China, also saw them off at the station.

### 越南文化代表团离京去各地访问

1960-02-19
第4版()
专栏：

    越南文化代表团离京去各地访问
    新华社18日讯　应对外文化联络委员会邀请来我国商订中越文化合作协定1960年执行计划的越南文化代表团，在同我国签订了今年度执行计划和在北京访问以后，今天乘火车去各地参观访问。
    到车站欢送的有对外文化联络委员会副主任张致祥、丁西林、文化部副部长胡愈之及教育部办公厅副主任刘子余等。
    越南驻中国大使馆临时代办陈仲括也到车站送行。
