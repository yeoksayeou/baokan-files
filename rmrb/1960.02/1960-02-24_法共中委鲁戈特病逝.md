Title: French Communist Party Central Committee Member Rougé Dies

Author:
Date: 1960-02-24
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, Paris, February 22nd: Gabriel Rougé, a member of the Central Committee of the French Communist Party, passed away yesterday after a long illness at the age of fifty-six.

　　Rougé was an outstanding fighter of the French Communist Party. In 1941, he organized the first "French Volunteer Guerrilla" to resist Hitler in southern France.

　　He was elected as a member of the Central Committee of the French Communist Party from June 1945.

### 法共中委鲁戈特病逝

1960-02-24
第6版()
专栏：

    法共中委鲁戈特病逝
    新华社巴黎22日电　法国共产党中央委员会委员加布里埃尔·鲁戈特在长期患病后在昨天逝世，享年五十六岁。
    鲁戈特是法国共产党的优秀战士，他于1941年在法国南部地区组织了第一支抵抗希特勒的“法国义勇军游击队”。
    他从1945年6月起当选为法国共产党中央委员会委员。
