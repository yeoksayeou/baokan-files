Title: Voroshilov Returns Home After Visiting India and Nepal
Author:
Date: 1960-02-07
Page: 3

Xinhua News Agency, February 6th: After visiting India and Nepal, Voroshilov, Chairman of the Presidium of the Supreme Soviet of the Soviet Union, Kozlov, First Deputy Chairman of the Council of Ministers of the Soviet Union, and Furtseva, representative of the Supreme Soviet of the Soviet Union, returned to Moscow today.

Last night, Chairman Voroshilov delivered a farewell broadcast over All India Radio, thanking the Indian side for their hospitality. He said that the peoples of India and the Soviet Union have a long history of friendship. "The friendship between our two peoples helps to strengthen the cause of peace in Southeast Asia and the world."

Voroshilov, Kozlov, and Furtseva also held a grand reception in New Delhi yesterday. Indian President Prasad, Prime Minister Nehru, cabinet ministers, members of parliament, prominent social figures, and diplomatic envoys from various countries to India attended the reception.

### 伏罗希洛夫访问印度尼泊尔后回国

1960-02-07
第3版()
专栏：

    伏罗希洛夫访问印度尼泊尔后回国
    据新华社6日讯　苏联最高苏维埃主席团主席伏罗希洛夫、苏联部长会议第一副主席科兹洛夫和苏联最高苏维埃代表福尔采娃，在访问了印度和尼泊尔之后，今天回到莫斯科。
    昨天晚间，伏罗希洛夫主席通过全印广播电台发表告别广播演说，感谢印度方面的招待。他说，印苏两国人民有着悠久的友谊。“我们两国人民的友谊有助于加强东南亚和世界和平的事业。”
    伏罗希洛夫、科兹洛夫和福尔采娃昨天还在新德里举行了盛大招待会。印度总统普拉沙德、总理尼赫鲁、内阁部长、议员、著名社会人士和各国驻印度的外交使节出席了招待会。
