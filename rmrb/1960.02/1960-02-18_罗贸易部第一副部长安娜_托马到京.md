Title: Romanian First Deputy Minister of Trade, Anna Toma, Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1960-02-18
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, February 17th - Anna Toma, First Deputy Minister of Trade of the Romanian People's Republic, arrived in Beijing by plane this afternoon. Anna Toma is here to discuss trade issues between China and Romania for the year 1960.

Lin Haiyun, Vice Minister of Foreign Trade, and others went to the airport to welcome her.

Vi Jiannu, Economic Counselor of the Romanian Embassy in China, also went to the airport to greet her.

### 罗贸易部第一副部长安娜·托马到京

1960-02-18
第4版()
专栏：

    罗贸易部第一副部长安娜·托马到京
    新华社17日讯　罗马尼亚人民共和国贸易部第一副部长安娜·托马今天下午乘飞机到达北京。安娜·托马是来我国商谈中罗两国1960年度贸易问题的。
    前往机场欢迎的有我对外贸易部副部长林海云等人。
    罗马尼亚驻中国大使馆经济参赞维强努等也到机场迎接。
