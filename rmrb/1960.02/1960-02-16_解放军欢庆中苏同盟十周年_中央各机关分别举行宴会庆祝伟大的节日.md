Title: People's Liberation Army Celebrates the Tenth Anniversary of the Sino-Soviet Alliance; Central Organs Hold Banquets to Celebrate the Great Festival
Author:
Date: 1960-02-16
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency, February 15th: In recent days, various units of the Chinese People's Liberation Army have held meetings to warmly celebrate the tenth anniversary of the signing of the Sino-Soviet Treaty of Friendship, Alliance, and Mutual Assistance.

At the celebration meetings held by various units, the unit leaders reported to all officers and soldiers the great significance of the Sino-Soviet Treaty of Friendship, Alliance, and Mutual Assistance. Many organs, companies, schools, hospitals, etc., held photo exhibitions, report meetings, organized film evenings or story evenings, and published special columns on "Sino-Soviet Friendship" on wall newspapers to widely promote the friendship between the people and armies of China and the Soviet Union. On the front lines of the Fujian Front, huge slogans such as "Long Live Sino-Soviet Friendship" and "The Sino-Soviet Alliance is a Powerful Fortress of World Peace" were posted. Soldiers joyfully sang songs praising Sino-Soviet friendship at celebration evenings. Troops stationed in Dalian City, who had spent time with the Soviet army, were particularly lively these days. Veteran soldiers showed the gifts given to them by the Soviet army to new soldiers, and officers introduced the heroic deeds of the Soviet Red Army to the soldiers. At the discussion meetings, officers and soldiers unanimously expressed that they would step up their studies, improve their skills, and strive tirelessly to defend the motherland and world peace.

Xinhua News Agency, February 13th: In recent days, central organs have held receptions, banquets, or soirees to warmly commemorate the tenth anniversary of the signing of the Sino-Soviet Treaty of Friendship, Alliance, and Mutual Assistance

On February 10th, the Ministry of Foreign Trade held a banquet to entertain all the staff of the Soviet Trade Representative Office in China, the Economic Counselor's Office, and all members of the Soviet Complete Equipment Distribution Group. Lei Renmin, Vice Minister of Foreign Trade, and Fumin, Soviet Economic Counselor in China, and Seminass, Acting Trade Representative, spoke at the banquet. They repeatedly proposed toasts to the unbreakable and eternal friendship between the people of China and the Soviet Union.

On February 12th, the Ministry of Culture held a banquet to entertain Soviet experts working in the Ministry of Culture. Shen Yanbing, Minister of Culture, Qian Junrui and Lin Mohan, Vice Ministers, Zhang Zhixiang, Deputy Director of the Foreign Cultural Liaison Committee, and others attended the banquet. Minister Shen Yanbing and Soviet dance choreography expert Gusev spoke at the banquet. Shen Yanbing said in his speech that in the past ten years, China's film, drama, music, dance, fine arts, publishing, mass culture, and other undertakings have received assistance and guidance from Soviet experts and experts from other fraternal countries. He expressed his gratitude to the Soviet experts and the experts of other fraternal countries for their hard work.

The State Council's Bureau of Foreign Experts, together with the Soviet Embassy in China, jointly held a report meeting and celebration soiree on February 11th. The Ministry of Finance, the Ministry of Commerce, the Ministry of Posts and Telecommunications, the People's Bank of China, Xinhua News Agency, and the Broadcasting Administration jointly held a soiree on the evening of February 12th.

### 解放军欢庆中苏同盟十周年  中央各机关分别举行宴会庆祝伟大的节日

1960-02-16
第4版()
专栏：

    解放军欢庆中苏同盟十周年
    中央各机关分别举行宴会庆祝伟大的节日
    新华社15日讯　最近几天，中国人民解放军各部队纷纷集会，热烈庆祝中苏友好同盟互助条约签订十周年。
    在各部队举行的庆祝大会上，部队负责人都向全体官兵报告了中苏友好同盟互助条约的伟大意义。许多机关、连队、学校、医院等举办图片展览会、报告会，组织电影晚会或故事晚会，墙报上出刊“中苏友好”专栏等，广泛宣传中苏两国人民和军队的友谊。福建前线部队的前沿阵地上，张贴着“中苏友谊万古长青”、“中苏同盟是世界和平的强大堡垒”等巨幅标语。战士们在庆祝晚会上欢欣鼓舞地唱着歌颂中苏友好的歌曲。曾经与苏军相处过一个时期的驻旅大市部队，这几天特别热闹。老战士把苏军赠送给他们的礼品拿给新战士看，军官向战士们介绍苏联红军英勇斗争的事迹。在座谈会上，官兵们一致表示，要加紧学习，提高本领，为保卫祖国、保卫世界和平奋斗不懈。
    新华社13日讯　中央各机关在最近几天中分别举行酒会、宴会或联欢晚会，热烈庆祝中苏友好同盟互助条约签订十周年。
    对外贸易部在10日举行宴会，招待苏联驻华商务代表处、经济参赞处的全体工作人员和苏联成套设备分交小组的全体成员。对外贸易部副部长雷任民和苏联驻华经济参赞符明、代理商务代表谢米纳斯都在宴会上讲了话，他们都一再提议为中苏两国人民牢不可破的永恒的友谊干杯。
    文化部12日举行宴会招待在文化部工作的苏联专家。文化部部长沈雁冰、副部长钱俊瑞、林默涵，对外文化联络委员会副主任张致祥等都参加了宴会。沈雁冰部长和苏联舞蹈编导专家古谢夫先后在宴会上讲了话。沈雁冰在讲话中说，十年来我国的电影、戏剧、音乐、舞蹈、美术、出版、群众文化等事业，都得到苏联专家和其他各兄弟国家的专家的帮助和指导。他对苏联专家和各兄弟国家专家的辛勤劳动表示感谢。
    国务院外国专家局11日和苏联驻华大使馆联合举行了报告会和庆祝晚会。财政部、商业部、邮电部、中国人民银行、新华社、广播事业局12日晚联合举行了联欢晚会。
