International Briefs

    Sukarno to Visit Iraq
    Jakarta News: The Information Department of the Indonesian Ministry of Foreign Affairs announced on the 22nd that Indonesian President Sukarno has accepted the formal invitation extended by Iraqi Prime Minister Kassem on behalf of the government of the Republic of Iraq, and will visit Iraq in early April this year.

    Burma Bans Import of American Films for One Year
    Rangoon News: According to Rangoon newspapers on the 22nd, the government of the Union of Burma has announced a one-year ban on the import of American films into Burma.

    Earthquakes Occur in Parts of Pakistan, India, and Other Countries
    Karachi News: A strong earthquake occurred in fifteen cities in northern West Pakistan, including Rawalpindi, on the afternoon of the 19th.

    It is also reported that Delhi, the capital of India, parts of the Punjab, and Srinagar in Kashmir also felt the earthquake.

### 国际简讯

1960-02-24
第6版()
专栏：国际简讯

    国际简讯
    苏加诺将访问伊拉克
    雅加达讯　印度尼西亚外交部新闻司22日宣布，印度尼西亚总统苏加诺已经接受伊拉克共和国总理卡塞姆代表伊拉克共和国政府提出的正式邀请，将在今年4月初访问伊拉克。
    缅甸禁止美国影片输入一年
    仰光讯　据仰光各报22日报道，缅甸联邦政府已经宣布禁止美国电影输入缅甸一年。巴、印等国部分地区发生地震
    卡拉奇讯　西巴基斯坦北部拉瓦尔品第等十五个城市在19日下午发生剧烈的地震。
    同时据悉，印度首都德里、旁遮普邦的部分地区以及克什米尔的斯利那加也都感到有地震。
