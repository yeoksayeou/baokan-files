    Table of Industrial Output Value Increase Percentage of Fraternal Countries from 1959 Compared to 1958
    Editor's Note: 1959 was a year of continued rapid progress in the economic construction of socialist countries. Since the beginning of the year, various fraternal countries have successively published communiques on the results of the implementation of last year's national economic plans. The communiques indicate that industrial production in various countries has increased significantly. Now, based on the figures in the communiques of various countries, we have created a chart showing the percentage increase in the total industrial output value of fraternal countries in 1959 compared to 1958.
