	International Briefs
	Yemen Appoints First Ambassador to the Soviet Union
	Taz Report: The King of Yemen announced in an order that the Yemeni government has appointed Sayyid Hassan Ibrahim as Yemen's first ambassador to the Soviet Union.
	More than Seven Thousand People from West Germany
	Relocate to the German Democratic Republic This Year
	Berlin Report: According to the "Neues Deutschland" newspaper, in January and February of this year, more than seven thousand people relocated from West Germany to the German Democratic Republic.

### 国际简讯

1960-02-26
第5版()
专栏：国际简讯

    国际简讯
    也门任命首任驻苏公使
    塔兹讯　也门国王在一项命令中宣布，也门政府任命赛义德·哈桑·易卜拉欣为也门驻苏联第一任公使。
    西德今年又有七千多人
    迁居德意志民主共和国
    柏林讯　据“新德意志报”报道，在今年1月份和2月份又有七千多人从西德迁居德意志民主共和国。
