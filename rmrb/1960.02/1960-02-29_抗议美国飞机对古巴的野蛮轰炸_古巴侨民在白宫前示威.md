Title: Protesting the Savage Bombing of Cuba by American Planes: Cuban Expatriates Demonstrate in Front of the White House
Author:
Date: 1960-02-29
Page: 6

    Protesting the Savage Bombing of Cuba by American Planes
    Cuban Expatriates Demonstrate in Front of the White House
    Xinhua News Agency, February 28th, Washington: A group of Cuban expatriates residing in Washington and New York gathered in front of the White House, the U.S. President's residence, on the 27th to demonstrate against the bombing of Cuba by American planes. The demonstrators held signs that read: "We protest the bombing of Cuba by planes taking off from Florida"; "American planes burned 500,000 tons of Cuban sugar."
    The demonstrators distributed mimeographed leaflets along the way, listing the crimes committed by planes taking off from American bases when bombing Cuba. The leaflet stated, "Since October 18th (last year), Cuba has been bombed at least eighteen times by planes taking off from American bases."

### 抗议美国飞机对古巴的野蛮轰炸  古巴侨民在白宫前示威

1960-02-29
第6版()
专栏：

    抗议美国飞机对古巴的野蛮轰炸
    古巴侨民在白宫前示威
    新华社28日讯　华盛顿消息：一批居住在华盛顿和纽约的古巴侨民27日集合在美国总统府白宫前面举行示威，抗议美国飞机对古巴的轰炸。参加游行示威的人手持标语牌，上面写着：“我们抗议自佛罗里达起飞的飞机轰炸古巴”；“美国飞机烧毁了古巴五十万吨糖”。
    游行示威的人沿途散发了油印传单，列举自美国基地起飞的飞机在轰炸古巴时所犯下的罪行。传单说，“自（去年）10月18日以来，古巴至少被自美国基地起飞的飞机轰炸了十八次。”
