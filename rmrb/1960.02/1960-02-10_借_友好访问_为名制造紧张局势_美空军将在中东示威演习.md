Title: Creating Tension Under the Guise of "Friendly Visits": US Air Force to Conduct Demonstration Exercises in the Middle East
Author:
Date: 1960-02-10
Page: 6

    Creating Tension Under the Guise of "Friendly Visits"
    US Air Force to Conduct Demonstration Exercises in the Middle East
    Xinhua News Agency, Karachi, February 8th: According to an official announcement made here today, at the recently held Seventh Meeting of the Central Treaty Organization's Military Committee, the US representative, the US Chairman of the Joint Chiefs of Staff, stated that a US Air Force mobile unit would make a "friendly visit" to Iran, Turkey, and Pakistan in February. The Central Treaty Organization's military representative group has already announced its agreement to the dates of the "visit."
    It is said that this US Air Force mobile unit will conduct ground exhibitions and aerial demonstration exercises in designated cities of the Central Treaty Organization countries, including Tehran, Karachi, Istanbul, Adana, and Ankara.

### 借“友好访问”为名制造紧张局势  美空军将在中东示威演习

1960-02-10
第6版()
专栏：

    借“友好访问”为名制造紧张局势
    美空军将在中东示威演习
    据新华社卡拉奇8日电　据官方今天在这里宣布，在最近举行的中央条约组织军事委员会第七次会议上，美国代表美国三军参谋长表示，美国的一支空军机动部队将在2月到伊朗、土耳其和巴基斯坦作“友好访问”，中央条约组织军事代表小组已经宣布同意“访问”的日期。
    据说，这支美国空军机动部队将在指定的中央条约组织国家的城市里，其中包括德黑兰、卡拉奇、伊斯坦布尔、亚达那和安卡拉，进行地面展览和空中示威演习。
