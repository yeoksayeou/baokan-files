Title: Canal Transport of Fertilizer Saves Labor (Picture)
Author:
Date: 1960-02-20
Page: 3

    New Developments in Spring Plowing

    Canal Transport of Fertilizer Saves Labor
    The Nanhu Commune in Jiaxing County, Zhejiang Province, uses wooden buckets to transport river mud from the canals. Two people can transport forty dan at a time, saving labor. Many communes have sent people to learn from them.
