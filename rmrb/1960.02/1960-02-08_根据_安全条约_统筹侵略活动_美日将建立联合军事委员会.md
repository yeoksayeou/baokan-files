Title: Coordinating Aggression Based on "Security Treaty": US and Japan to Establish Joint Military Committee
Author:
Date: 1960-02-08
Page: 6

    Coordinating Aggression Based on "Security Treaty"
    US and Japan to Establish Joint Military Committee
    Xinhua News Agency, Tokyo, 6th—According to news from Tokyo: Japanese Foreign Minister Aiichiro Fujiyama said in the Diet today that the United States and Japan will establish a joint military committee based on the newly signed US-Japan "Treaty of Mutual Cooperation and Security" to study the joint military actions of the two countries in the event of "aggression."
    Fujiyama made the statement when answering a question from Socialist Party member Isasumi Isamu in the House of Representatives Budget Committee regarding the US-Japan military alliance treaty.
    Fujiyama also said that the planned joint military committee will determine the scope of responsibility for joint actions taken by the two countries and which side will be responsible for command.

### 根据“安全条约”统筹侵略活动  美日将建立联合军事委员会

1960-02-08
第6版()
专栏：

    根据“安全条约”统筹侵略活动
    美日将建立联合军事委员会
    据新华社6日讯　东京消息：日本外务相藤山爱一郎今天在国会中说，美国和日本将根据新签订的日美“共同合作和安全条约”建立一个联合军事委员会，来研究在遭到“侵略”时两国的联合军事行动。
    藤山是在众议院预算委员会答复社会党议员今澄勇关于日美军事同盟条约的质询时这样说的。
    藤山还说，预定成立的这个联合军事委员会将决定两国采取联合行动时的责任范围和由那一方面负责指挥。
