Title: Czechoslovak Parliament Delegation Arrives in Beijing for a Short Stay After Visiting Southeast Asian Countries
Author:
Date: 1960-02-11
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, 10th. The Czechoslovak National Assembly delegation of eleven people, led by National Assembly Chairman Fierlinger, arrived in Beijing by plane at 7 p.m. tonight from Hanoi. The delegation is making a short stop in Beijing at the invitation of Chairman Zhu De after visiting India, Indonesia, Cambodia, and the Democratic Republic of Vietnam.

Chairman Zhu De welcomed the distinguished guests at the airport. Also present at the airport to welcome them were Vice-Chairman Lin Feng, and members of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress Wang Weizhou, Shi Liang, Wu Xinyu, Zhang Su, Zhao Shoushan, Nan Hanchen, Hu Zi'ang, Vice Minister of Foreign Affairs Zeng Yongquan, and Vice Mayor of Beijing Le Songsheng, among others. Young women of the capital presented the distinguished guests with flowers.

The Czechoslovak Ambassador to China, Bushniak, and the envoys and diplomatic officials of India, Indonesia, Cambodia, and the Democratic Republic of Vietnam, which the parliamentary delegation had previously visited, also welcomed them at the airport.

### 在访问东南亚各国后应邀来京作短期停留  捷议会代表团到北京

1960-02-11
第1版()
专栏：

    在访问东南亚各国后应邀来京作短期停留
    捷议会代表团到北京
    新华社10日讯　由国民议会主席费林格率领的捷克斯洛伐克国民议会代表团一行十一人，今晚七时乘飞机由河内到达北京。代表团是在访问印度、印度尼西亚、柬埔寨和越南民主共和国以后，应朱德委员长的邀请来北京作短期停留的。
    朱德委员长到机场欢迎贵宾。到机场欢迎的，还有林枫副委员长和人大常委会委员王维舟、史良、武新宇、张苏、赵寿山、南汉宸、胡子昂，外交部副部长曾涌泉，北京市副市长乐松生等。首都的女青年向贵宾们献了鲜花。
    捷克斯洛伐克驻中国大使布希尼亚克和议会代表团曾经访问过的印度、印度尼西亚、柬埔寨和越南民主共和国驻中国的使节和外交官员也到机场欢迎。
