Bulgaria's Brotherly Nations' Cultural Life

    Thriving Bulgarian Amateur Cultural Activities
    Currently, Bulgaria has more than 13,000 mass amateur art groups. Almost every enterprise, large construction site, and university has its own amateur art group. There are now 4,700 amateur choirs, more than 560 bands, more than 2,280 dance troupes, more than 4,670 theater groups, and five opera companies.
    The number of people participating in amateur artistic activities exceeds 300,000. On average, about one in every twenty-five people is a member of an amateur art group. (Xinhua News Agency dispatch)

### 蓬勃的保加利亚业余文化活动

1960-02-05
第6版()
专栏：兄弟国家文化生活

    蓬勃的保加利亚业余文化活动
    目前保加利亚已有一万三千多个群众业余艺术团体。几乎在每一个企业、大的建筑工地、高等学校中都有自己的业余艺术团体。现在已经成立的有四千七百个业余合唱团、五百六十多个乐队、二千二百八十多个舞蹈团、四千六百七十多个剧团、五个歌剧团。
    参加业余艺术活动的人数已逾三十万人。平均约在每二十五个人中就有一人是业余艺术团体的成员。　（据新华社讯）
