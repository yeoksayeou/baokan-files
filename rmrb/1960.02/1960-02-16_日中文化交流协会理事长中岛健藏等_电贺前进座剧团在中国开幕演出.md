Title: Japan-China Cultural Exchange Association Chairman Kenzo Nakajima and Others Send Telegrams to Congratulate the Opening Performance of the Zenshinza Theater Troupe in China
Author:
Date: 1960-02-16
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, 15th. Chu Tunan, President of the Chinese People's Association for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries, received telegrams from Kenzo Nakajima, Chairman of the Japan-China Cultural Exchange Association, and Shizue Kawarasaki of the Japanese Zenshinza Theater Troupe, congratulating the opening performance of the Zenshinza Theater Troupe in China. The telegrams stated: "As the Zenshinza Theater Troupe holds its opening performance on a visit to your country, we express our heartfelt joy and gratitude. The friendship and cultural exchange between the people of Japan and China is our unwavering desire. This desire cannot be undermined by any force. The movement to prevent the Japan-U.S. 'Security Treaty' and restore diplomatic relations between Japan and China is rising higher and higher. We would like to convey to you the respect, friendship, and gratitude of all the people who advocate Japan's complete independence, democracy, freedom, peace, and neutrality toward you, your association, and the people of your country."

### 日中文化交流协会理事长中岛健藏等  电贺前进座剧团在中国开幕演出

1960-02-16
第4版()
专栏：

    日中文化交流协会理事长中岛健藏等
    电贺前进座剧团在中国开幕演出
    新华社15日讯　中国人民对外文化协会会长楚图南收到了日本日中文化交流协会理事长中岛健藏和日本前进座剧团河原崎静江发来的祝贺前进座剧团在中国开幕演出的电报。电报说：“当前进座剧团在贵国举行访问演出开幕式之际，我们谨表示衷心的喜悦和感谢。日中两国人民的友好和文化交流是我们坚持不渝的愿望。这一愿望是任何势力也不能破坏的。阻止日美‘安全条约’、恢复日中邦交的运动愈来愈高涨。我们谨向您传达主张日本完全独立、民主、自由、和平、中立的全体人民对您与贵会及贵国人民的尊敬、友谊和感谢的心情。”
