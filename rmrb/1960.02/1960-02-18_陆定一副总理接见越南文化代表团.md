Title: Lu Dingyi, Vice Premier, Receives Vietnamese Cultural Delegation
Author:
Date: 1960-02-18
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, February 17th: Vice Premier Lu Dingyi received the entire Vietnamese Democratic Republic cultural delegation led by Le Liem, Deputy Minister of Culture of the Vietnamese Democratic Republic, this morning. The guests and hosts had a cordial and friendly conversation.

Present at the reception were Zhang Zhixiang and Ding Xilin, Deputy Directors of the Commission for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries.

Also present at the reception were Tran Trong Quat, Chargé d'Affaires ad interim, and Nguyen Tien, First Secretary, of the Vietnamese Embassy in China.

### 陆定一副总理接见越南文化代表团

1960-02-18
第4版()
专栏：

    陆定一副总理接见越南文化代表团
    新华社17日讯　陆定一副总理今天上午接见了由越南民主共和国文化部副部长黎廉率领的越南民主共和国文化代表团全体人员。宾主进行了亲切友好的交谈。
    接见时在座的，有对外文化联络委员会副主任张致祥、丁西林。
    接见时，越南驻中国大使馆临时代办陈仲括、一等秘书阮进也在座。
