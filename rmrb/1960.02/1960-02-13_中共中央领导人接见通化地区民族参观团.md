Title: Central Committee Leaders of the Chinese Communist Party Receive Ethnic Minority Visiting Group from Tonghua Area

Author: 
Date: 1960-02-13
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, 12th. Liu Shaoqi and Zhu De, Vice Chairmen of the CPC Central Committee, and Li Weihan, a member of the CPC Central Committee, received the ethnic minority visiting group from the Tonghua area of Jilin Province this afternoon.

### 中共中央领导人接见通化地区民族参观团

1960-02-13
第1版()
专栏：

    中共中央领导人接见通化地区民族参观团
    新华社12日讯　中共中央副主席刘少奇、朱德和中共中央委员李维汉，今天下午接见了吉林省通化地区各民族参观团。
