Title: Patrol After the Rain
Author: Zhao Guangtao
Date: 1960-02-28
Page: 8

    Patrol After the Rain
    Zhao Guangtao
    Patrolling after the heavy wind and rain,
    One foot high, the other low,
    For the children to sleep soundly,
    I am willing to be covered in mud.
        (Poem by Private First Class Liu Yuqin)

### 雨后出巡
赵光涛
1960-02-28
第8版()
专栏：

    雨后出巡
    赵光涛
    大风雨后巡逻去，
    一脚高来一脚低，
    为了孩子睡得香，
    泥水满身我愿意。
        （上等兵刘玉钦诗）
