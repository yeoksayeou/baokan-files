Title: China and Ceylon Sign This Year's Exchange of Goods Protocol
Author:
Date: 1960-02-09
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, 8th. Representatives of the Government of the People's Republic of China and the Government of Ceylon, in accordance with the provisions of the "Trade and Payments Agreement between the Government of the People's Republic of China and the Government of Ceylon" concluded on September 19, 1957, signed in Beijing today the 1960 Exchange of Goods Protocol between the People's Republic of China and Ceylon after negotiations. Lei Renmin, Vice Minister of Foreign Trade of the People's Republic of China, signed on behalf of the Chinese government, and Mr. Gooneratne, head of the Ceylon government trade delegation and Ceylon's ambassador to China, signed on behalf of the Ceylon government.

### 中锡签订今年换货议定书

1960-02-09
第4版()
专栏：

    中锡签订今年换货议定书
    新华社8日讯　中华人民共和国政府和锡兰政府的代表，根据1957年9月19日两国政府缔结的“中华人民共和国政府、锡兰政府贸易和支付协定”的规定，经过谈判，今天在北京签订了1960年中华人民共和国和锡兰换货议定书。代表中国政府签字的是中华人民共和国对外贸易部副部长雷任民，代表锡兰政府签字的是锡兰政府贸易代表团团长、锡兰驻华大使高伯拉瓦。
