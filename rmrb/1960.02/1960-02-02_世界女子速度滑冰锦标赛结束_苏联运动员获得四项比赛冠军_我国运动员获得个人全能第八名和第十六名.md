Title: World Women's Speed Skating Championships Conclude: Soviet Athletes Win All Four Events, Chinese Athletes Achieve Eighth and Sixteenth Place in All-Around
Author:
Date: 1960-02-02
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, 1st dispatch: Östersund (Sweden) - The 18th World Women's Speed Skating Championships concluded on January 31st. Soviet athletes secured the top four positions in the all-around competition and won all four individual events. Chinese athletes achieved eighth and sixteenth place in the all-around, and also set new national records in the 1000m and 3000m speed skating events.

The top four finishers in the all-around competition were all Soviet athletes. Valentina Stenina, the 1959 world runner-up, won her first world all-around title this year with a score of 208.833 points. Tamara Rylova, the 1959 world champion, finished second this year with a score of 209.633 points. Lidia Skoblikova, who placed third in the all-around last year, maintained her third position this year with a score of 210.600 points. D. Dontchenko finished fourth with a score of 212.083 points. Poland's P. Pileczyk finished fifth with a score of 212.784 points. Finland's E. Hutunen finished sixth with a score of 213.517 points.

Chinese athlete Sun Hongxia achieved a total score of 214.784 points, placing eighth (she finished ninth in last year's world championships). Another Chinese athlete, Liu Fengrong, finished sixteenth in the all-around with a score of 222.417 points.

### 世界女子速度滑冰锦标赛结束  苏联运动员获得四项比赛冠军  我国运动员获得个人全能第八名和第十六名

1960-02-02
第6版()
专栏：

    世界女子速度滑冰锦标赛结束
    苏联运动员获得四项比赛冠军
    我国运动员获得个人全能第八名和第十六名
    据新华社1日讯　厄斯特松德（瑞典）消息：第十八届世界女子速度滑冰锦标赛已经在1月31日结束。苏联运动员获得了个人全能前四名和所有四项比赛的冠军。中国运动员获得了全能第八名和第十六名，并且创造了一千米和三千米速滑的全国最高成绩。
    个人全能前四名都是苏联运动员。1959年世界亚军斯捷尼娜今年第一次获得世界个人全能冠军称号，成绩二百零八点八三三分。1959年世界冠军里洛娃今年获得亚军，成绩是二百零九点六三三分。去年个人全能第三名斯科布利科娃今年仍取得第三名，成绩是二百一十点六零零分。顿钦科以二百一十二点零八三分的成绩获第四名。波兰的皮雷契克列第五名，成绩是二百一十二点七八四分。芬兰的胡图南第六名，成绩是二百一十三点五一七分。
    中国选手孙洪霞个人总成绩为二百一十四点七八四分，列第八名（她去年在世界锦标赛中曾获得第九名）。另一名中国选手刘凤荣以二百二十二点四一七分的成绩列个人全能第十六名。
