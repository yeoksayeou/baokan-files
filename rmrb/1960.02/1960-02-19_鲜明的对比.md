    Sharp Contrast

    Under the leadership of the Labor Party, farmers in North Vietnam have not only rapidly healed the wounds of war, but have also continuously increased agricultural production.

    Under the brutal rule of US imperialism and the Ngo Dinh Diem clique, agricultural production in South Vietnam is increasingly withered. South Vietnam was originally a fertile area rich in rice, but now rice production has plummeted. The following table compares the rice production of North Vietnam and South Vietnam last year with the pre-war production in 1939: (Unit: 10,000 tons)

         1939　1959　Increase/Decrease
    North Vietnam　240　519.4　Increase of 117%
    South Vietnam　430  294　Decrease of 33%

### 鲜明的对比

1960-02-19
第5版()
专栏：小统计

    鲜明的对比
    越南北方农民在劳动党的领导下，不仅迅速地医治了战争创伤，而且使农业生产不断地增长。
    在美帝国主义和吴庭艳集团的残暴统治下，南越的农业生产日益雕敝。越南南方本是土地肥沃、盛产大米的地方，但现在大米产量却急剧下降。现将越南北方和南越去年大米产量和战前1939年产量列表比较如下：（单位：万吨）
         1939年　1959年　增减比例越南北方　240　519．4　增长117％南越　430  294　下降33％
