Title: The Korean Workers' Party Decides to Grandly Celebrate the 15th Anniversary of the "August 15th" Liberation; Calls on the Entire People to Advance Towards New Peaks in Socialist Construction; Strengthen the Struggle to Force US Troops to Withdraw from South Korea and Realize the Peaceful Reunification of the Fatherland
Author:
Date: 1960-02-25
Page: 5
　
Xinhua News Agency, Pyongyang, 24th -- Pyongyang newspapers today published the resolution adopted by the Standing Committee of the Central Committee of the Korean Workers' Party on grandly celebrating the 15th anniversary of the "August 15th" Korean Liberation.

The resolution stated that when celebrating the "August 15th" Korean Liberation fifteenth anniversary this year, the Korean people will summarize the great victories they have achieved in the past fifteen years under the leadership of the Korean Workers' Party in repelling the resistance of all enemies at home and abroad, and in the struggle to defend the independence of the fatherland and build socialism. The resolution pointed out that the Democratic People's Republic of Korea, in a very short period after liberation, has transformed from a backward colonial agricultural country into a socialist industrial and agricultural country with a solid independent national economic foundation. The resolution emphasized that the Korean people will rely on the world socialist system, consolidate and develop the achievements already made, strive to realize the peaceful reunification of the fatherland, and bravely advance towards new peaks in socialist construction by adhering to the advancement of the revolutionary upsurge.

The resolution requires party organizations at all levels to summarize and publicize the tremendous achievements made by the Korean people in all aspects on the occasion of the "August 15th" Liberation fifteenth anniversary, so that all the people can clearly recognize the superiority of the socialist system, and actively struggle to defend the victorious achievements of socialism and build a prosperous fatherland.

The resolution also requires the thorough implementation of the Korean Workers' Party's correct line and policies among the working people, and to conduct education on the party's glorious revolutionary traditions, in order to strengthen the party's unity and solidarity, and to firmly unite all the people around the Party Central Committee.

The resolution emphasized that it is necessary to thoroughly expose the colonial policies of US imperialism and the anti-people and traitorous policies of the Syngman Rhee clique to the working people, and to further strengthen the struggle to force the immediate withdrawal of US troops from South Korea.

The resolution also calls for further strengthening the friendly unity with the Soviet people and further strengthening the education of proletarian internationalism for party members and the working people.

The resolution stipulates the establishment of preparatory committees for celebrating the "August 15th" Liberation fifteenth anniversary at the central level and in each province, city, and county. The resolution calls for the organization and development of a nationwide production increase campaign to effectively complete this year's national economic plan while carrying out various celebratory activities.

The resolution stipulates various commemorative activities to be held in northern Korea before the festival: scientific, cultural, and educational institutions and newspapers will widely organize report meetings and exhibitions summarizing the achievements of the past fifteen years, and conduct theoretical and general propaganda; promote the work of publishing Comrade Kim Il-sung's works; hold a national art festival and a national sports meet; and open the newly built Central Liberation Struggle Museum and the Soviet Exhibition Hall.

The resolution requires the renovation of the tombs of the martyrs of the Soviet Army, the Korean People's Army, and the Chinese People's Volunteers before the festival.

The resolution also requires the launch of activities to further strengthen proletarian internationalist friendly solidarity with the people of socialist countries headed by the Soviet Union before the festival.

### 朝鲜劳动党决定隆重庆祝“八·一五”解放十五周年  号召全民向社会主义建设新高峰迈进  加强斗争迫使美军撤出南朝鲜实现祖国和平统一

1960-02-25
第5版()
专栏：

    朝鲜劳动党决定隆重庆祝“八·一五”解放十五周年
    号召全民向社会主义建设新高峰迈进
    加强斗争迫使美军撤出南朝鲜实现祖国和平统一
    新华社平壤24日电　平壤各报今天刊登了朝鲜劳动党中央委员会常务委员会通过的关于隆重庆祝“八·一五”朝鲜解放十五周年的决议。
    决议说，在今年庆祝“八·一五”朝鲜解放十五周年的时候，朝鲜人民将总结他们十五年来在朝鲜劳动党领导下击退国内外一切敌人的反抗，在保卫祖国独立和建设社会主义的斗争中取得的伟大胜利。决议指出，朝鲜民主主义人民共和国在解放后很短的时期内，已经由落后的殖民地农业国变成为有巩固的独立的民族经济基础的社会主义工农业国家。决议强调说，朝鲜人民将依靠世界社会主义体系，巩固和发展已经取得的成就，为实现祖国的和平统一、坚持推进革命高潮，向社会主义建设的新的高峰奋勇前进。
    决议要求各级党组织在“八·一五”解放十五周年之际总结和宣传朝鲜人民在各个方面所取得的巨大成就，使全体人民清楚地认识到社会主义制度的优越性，为保卫社会主义的胜利果实，为建设富强的祖国进行积极的斗争。
    决议还要求在劳动人民中彻底贯彻执行朝鲜劳动党的正确路线和政策，进行党的光辉的革命传统教育，以加强党的统一和团结，使全体人民牢固地团结在党中央委员会的周围。
    决议强调指出，必须向劳动人民彻底揭露美帝国主义的殖民政策和李承晚集团的反人民的卖国政策，进一步加强迫使美军立即从南朝鲜撤走的斗争。
    决议还要求进一步加强同苏联人民的友好团结，对党员和劳动人民进一步加强无产阶级国际主义的教育。
    决议规定在中央以及各道、市、郡成立庆祝“八·一五”解放十五周年的筹备委员会。决议号召在进行各种庆祝活动的同时，组织和开展全民性的有成效地完成今年的人民经济计划的增产竞赛。
    决议规定了节日前朝鲜北部各地将举行的各种纪念活动：科学、文化、教育机关和报刊将广泛组织总结十五年来的成就的报告会、展览会，进行理论宣传和一般宣传；促进出版金日成同志的著作的工作；举行全国艺术会演和全国体育大会；开放新建的中央解放斗争博物馆和苏联展览馆。
    决议要求在节日前后整修苏军、朝鲜人民军和中国人民志愿军的烈士墓。
    决议还要求在节日前展开进一步加强同以苏联为首的社会主义阵营各国人民之间的无产阶级国际主义友好团结的活动。
