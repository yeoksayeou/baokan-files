Title: State Council Approves Sino-Burmese Boundary Agreement and Treaty of Friendship to be Submitted to the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress for Deliberation and Approval
Author:
Date: 1960-02-17
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, February 16th -- The 96th plenary meeting of the State Council was held this morning. The meeting approved the "Agreement Between the Government of the People's Republic of China and the Government of the Union of Burma on the Question of the Boundary Between the Two Countries" and the "Treaty of Friendship and Non-Aggression Between the People's Republic of China and the Union of Burma" and decided to submit the aforementioned agreement and treaty to the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress for deliberation and approval.

The meeting heard a report by Liao Chengzhi, Director of the Overseas Chinese Affairs Commission, on the reception and resettlement of returned overseas Chinese in 1960.

The meeting also heard and approved a report by Xu Deheng, Minister of Aquatic Products, on the situation of the Fourth Plenary Meeting of the Western Pacific Fisheries Research Committee. This meeting was held in Hanoi, the Democratic Republic of Vietnam, from December 3rd to 10th, 1959, with representatives from China, the Soviet Union, North Korea, Vietnam, and Mongolia participating. Today's meeting also discussed and approved the "Provisional Regulations of the State Council on the Titles of Teachers in Institutions of Higher Education and the Methods for Determining and Promoting Them" and the "Provisional Regulations of the State Council on the Evaluation and Promotion of Wage Grades for Full-Time Primary and Secondary School Teachers."

Finally, the meeting discussed and approved matters concerning changes in the administrative division of counties and cities, and a list of appointments and removals.

### 国务院通过中缅边界协定和友好条约  将提请全国人民代表大会常委会审议批准

1960-02-17
第1版()
专栏：

    国务院通过中缅边界协定和友好条约
    将提请全国人民代表大会常委会审议批准
    新华社16日讯　国务院全体会议第九十六次会议在今天上午举行。会议通过了“中华人民共和国政府和缅甸联邦政府关于两国边界问题的协定”和“中华人民共和国和缅甸联邦之间的友好和互不侵犯条约”，并决定将上述协定和条约提请全国人民代表大会常务委员会审议批准。
    会议听取了华侨事务委员会主任廖承志关于1960年接待和安置归国华侨问题的报告。
    会议还听取并批准了水产部部长许德珩关于太平洋西部渔业研究委员会第四次全体会议情况的报告，这次会议是1959年12月3日至10日在越南民主共和国河内召开的，参加这次会议的有中、苏、朝、越、蒙五个国家的代表。今天的会议还讨论通过了国务院关于高等学校教师职务名称及其确定与提升办法的暂行规定和国务院关于评定和提升全日制中、小学教师工资级别的暂行规定。
    会议最后还讨论通过了关于县、市行政区划变更事项和任免名单。
