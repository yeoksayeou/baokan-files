Title: Our Artists' Performance Troupe Visits and Performs in Various Parts of the Soviet Union
Author:
Date: 1960-02-16
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, February 15th: After participating in the Moscow celebrations of the 10th anniversary of the signing of Sino-Soviet Treaty of Friendship, Alliance and Mutual Assistance, the Chinese Artists' Performance Troupe left Moscow today for a formal tour of fifteen cities, including Tula, in the central region of the Soviet Union.
    The performance troupe participated in festival performances in Moscow and was warmly welcomed by Soviet audiences.

### 我艺术家表演团赴苏联各地访问演出

1960-02-16
第5版()
专栏：

    我艺术家表演团赴苏联各地访问演出
    据新华社莫斯科15日电　中国艺术家表演团参加了莫斯科庆祝中苏友好同盟互助条约签订十周年的纪念活动之后，今天离开莫斯科去苏联中部地区图拉等十五个城市作正式访问演出。
    表演团在莫斯科参加了节日演出，受到了苏联观众的热烈欢迎。
