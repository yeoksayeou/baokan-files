Title: Our Air Force Shoots Down One Chiang Kai-shek Plane
Author:
Date: 1960-02-18
Page: 4
    Xinhua News Agency, Fujian Front, February 17th: On the afternoon of February 16th, a batch of four Chiang gang F86 planes conducted harassment activities in the Dongshan Zhangpu and Chihu areas of Fujian Province. At 16:28, in the airspace south of Zhangpu County, north of Hutou Mountain, they encountered our patrol planes. The Chiang planes opened fire on our planes first and launched two U.S.-made Sidewinder missiles. Our planes immediately returned fire, shot down one Chiang plane, and returned safely.

### 我空军击伤蒋机一架

1960-02-18
第4版()
专栏：

    我空军击伤蒋机一架
    新华社福建前线17日电　2月16日下午，蒋帮F86飞机一批四架，到我福建省东山漳浦、赤湖地区进行袭扰活动。十六时二十八分在漳浦县以南虎头山以北上空，与我巡逻飞机遭遇。蒋机首先向我机开火，并施放美制响尾蛇导弹二发，我机当即还击，击伤蒋机一架后，安全返航。
