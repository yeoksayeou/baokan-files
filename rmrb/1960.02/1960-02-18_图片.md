    On February 17th, the Guangzhou office of the People's Republic of China's Reception and Placement Committee for Returned Overseas Chinese held a farewell ceremony at Huangpu Port in Guangzhou to bid farewell to the Soviet Union's "Russia" and "Yakutia" ships, which were assisting China in transporting Overseas Chinese to Indonesia. This is the scene of Guangzhou citizens bidding farewell.
                    Xinhua News Agency reporter Li Ruigan photo
