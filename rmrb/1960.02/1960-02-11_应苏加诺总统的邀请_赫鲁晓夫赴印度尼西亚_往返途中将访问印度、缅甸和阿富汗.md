Title: At the Invitation of President Sukarno, Khrushchev Visits Indonesia; He Will Visit India, Burma, and Afghanistan on His Way There and Back
Author:
Date: 1960-02-11
Page: 5
Xinhua News Agency, 10th dispatch from Moscow: TASS reports: Soviet Minister of Council Chairman Khrushchev departed from Moscow on an "Il-18" aircraft on the morning of the 10th for a visit to Indonesia.
　　Khrushchev is visiting at the friendly invitation of President and Prime Minister Sukarno of the Republic of Indonesia. On the way to Indonesia, Khrushchev will visit India and Burma at the invitation of the government of the Republic of India and the government of the Union of Burma before going to Jakarta. He will arrive there on February 18.
　　On the way back from Indonesia, Khrushchev will visit Afghanistan at the invitation of King Mohammed Zahir Shah of Afghanistan and the Afghan government.
　　Accompanying Khrushchev on his visit are Soviet Foreign Minister Gromyko, Soviet Minister of Culture Mikhailov, and the chairmen of the two Soviet State Committees, Skachkov and Zhukov, among others. Leaders such as Voroshilov, Kozlov, Suslov, Furtseva, and many representatives of the working people of Moscow were at Vnukovo Airport to see him off.
　　Diplomatic envoys from various countries to the Soviet Union were also present to see him off. At 10:05 AM, the plane took off from the airport and flew to Tashkent.
　　Xinhua News Agency, Rangoon, 9th telegram: The Burmese government announced this evening that Soviet Minister of Council Chairman Khrushchev will make an official visit to Burma from February 16 to 18 at the invitation of the government of the Union of Burma. It was announced that Soviet Foreign Minister Gromyko, Minister of Culture Mikhailov, Chairman of the State Committee for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries Zhukov, and Chairman of the State Committee for Foreign Economic Relations Skachkov, among others, will accompany Khrushchev on his visit to Burma.

### 应苏加诺总统的邀请  赫鲁晓夫赴印度尼西亚  往返途中将访问印度、缅甸和阿富汗

1960-02-11
第5版()
专栏：

    应苏加诺总统的邀请
    赫鲁晓夫赴印度尼西亚
    往返途中将访问印度、缅甸和阿富汗
    新华社10日讯　据塔斯社莫斯科讯：苏联部长会议主席赫鲁晓夫10日上午搭“伊尔—十八式”飞机从莫斯科启程前往印度尼西亚访问。
    赫鲁晓夫是应印度尼西亚共和国总统兼总理苏加诺的邀请去作友好访问的。在前往印度尼西亚的途中，赫鲁晓夫将应印度共和国政府和缅甸联邦政府的邀请，访问印度和缅甸，然后前往雅加达。他将于2月18日到达那里。
    在由印度尼西亚返国途中，赫鲁晓夫将应阿富汗国王穆罕默德·查希尔·沙阿和阿富汗政府的邀请访问阿富汗。
    陪同赫鲁晓夫前往访问的有：苏联外交部长葛罗米柯、苏联文化部长米哈伊洛夫、苏联两个国家委员会的主席斯卡奇科夫和茹科夫等。到伏努科夫机场上送行的有：伏罗希洛夫、科兹洛夫、苏斯洛夫、福尔采娃等领导人，和莫斯科劳动人民的许多代表。
    前往送行的还有各国驻苏联外交使节。十时零五分，飞机从机场起飞，飞往塔什干。
    新华社仰光9日电　缅甸政府今晚宣布，苏联部长会议主席赫鲁晓夫应缅甸联邦政府的邀请，将于2月16日到18日到缅甸作正式访问。据宣布，将陪同赫鲁晓夫到缅甸访问的有苏联外交部长葛罗米柯、文化部长米哈伊洛夫、国家对外文化委员会主席茹科夫、国家对外经济关系委员会主席斯卡奇科夫等。
