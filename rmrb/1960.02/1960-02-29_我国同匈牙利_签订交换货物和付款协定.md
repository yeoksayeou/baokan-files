Title: China and Hungary Sign Goods Exchange and Payment Agreement
Author:
Date: 1960-02-29
Page: 7

Xinhua News Agency, February 28th. The agreement between the People's Republic of China and the Hungarian People's Republic on the exchange of goods and payment for 1960 was signed in Beijing today.

Representing the Chinese side, the agreement was signed by Lin Haiyun, Vice Minister of Foreign Trade of the People's Republic of China; representing the Hungarian side, the agreement was signed by Karádi Gyula, First Deputy Minister of Foreign Trade of the Hungarian People's Republic.

Attending the signing ceremony on the Chinese side were Lei Renmin, Vice Minister of Foreign Trade, Jia Shi, Director of the Second Bureau, Chen Boqing, Deputy Director of the Soviet and European Affairs Department of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs, etc.; on the Hungarian side were Szigeti, Chargé d'affaires ad interim of the Hungarian People's Republic Embassy in China, and all members of the Hungarian People's Republic Government Trade Delegation.

### 我国同匈牙利  签订交换货物和付款协定

1960-02-29
第7版()
专栏：

    我国同匈牙利
    签订交换货物和付款协定
    新华社28日讯　中华人民共和国和匈牙利人民共和国关于1960年交换货物和付款协定，今天在北京签字。
    代表中国方面在协定上签字的，是中华人民共和国对外贸易部副部长林海云；代表匈牙利方面在协定上签字的，是匈牙利人民共和国对外贸易部第一副部长卡拉迪·久洛。
    参加签字仪式的，中国方面有对外贸易部副部长雷任民、第二局局长贾石，外交部苏欧司副司长陈伯清等；匈牙利方面有匈牙利人民共和国驻中国大使馆临时代办西格蒂和匈牙利人民共和国政府贸易代表团全体人员。
