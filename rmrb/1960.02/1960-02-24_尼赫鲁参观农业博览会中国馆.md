Title: Nehru Visits the Chinese Pavilion at the Agricultural Exposition
Author: 
Date: 1960-02-24
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, New Delhi, February 23rd: Indian Prime Minister Nehru, accompanied by Chinese Ambassador to India Pan Zili, visited the Chinese exhibition hall at the World Agricultural Exposition being held here this afternoon.

Prime Minister Nehru visited all parts of the Chinese pavilion. During the visit, Wu Zhen, the person in charge of the Chinese exhibition hall, briefed him on the situation of the Chinese pavilion. Prime Minister Nehru praised the Chinese pavilion as "a very good exhibition hall."

Ambassador Pan Zili presented Prime Minister Nehru with a basket of dried fruits displayed in the Chinese pavilion.

The World Agricultural Exposition, which opened in early December last year, is scheduled to close on February 29. As of today, more than 2.7 million people have visited the Chinese pavilion.

### 尼赫鲁参观农业博览会中国馆

1960-02-24
第5版()
专栏：

    尼赫鲁参观农业博览会中国馆
    新华社新德里23日电　印度总理尼赫鲁今天下午在中国驻印度大使潘自力陪同下，参观了在这里举行的世界农业博展会中的中国展览馆。
    尼赫鲁总理参观了中国馆的所有部分。在参观时，中国展览馆负责人吴振向他介绍中国馆的情况。尼赫鲁总理称赞中国馆是“一个非常好的展览馆”。
    潘自力大使把一篮陈列在中国馆的干果送给尼赫鲁总理。
    在去年12月初开幕的世界农业博览会预定2月29日闭幕。到今天为止，已经有二百七十多万人参观了中国馆。
