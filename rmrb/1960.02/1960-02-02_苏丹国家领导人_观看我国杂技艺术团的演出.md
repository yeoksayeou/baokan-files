Title: Sudanese National Leaders Watch Performance by Chinese Acrobatic Troupe
Author:
Date: 1960-02-02
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, Khartoum, February 1st. Ibrahim Abboud, Chairman of the Supreme Council of the Armed Forces of Sudan and Prime Minister, watched a performance by the Chinese Acrobatic Troupe at the National Theater here last night.

Also watching the performance were Major General Talaat Farid, member of the Supreme Council and Minister of Information and Labor, Brigadier General Bahari, member of the Supreme Council and Minister of Interior, and Ziada Arbab, Minister of Education. Qu Wu, head of the Chinese Acrobatic Troupe, and Li Shi, chargé d'affaires ad interim of the Chinese Embassy, accompanied them to watch the performance.

The Chinese Acrobatic Troupe performed fifteen acts, and the Sudanese leaders and audience gave the Chinese performers enthusiastic applause.

Before the performance, Abboud received Qu Wu, head of the Chinese Acrobatic Troupe, and Li Shi, chargé d'affaires ad interim of the Chinese Embassy.

### 苏丹国家领导人  观看我国杂技艺术团的演出

1960-02-02
第6版()
专栏：

    苏丹国家领导人
    观看我国杂技艺术团的演出
    新华社喀土穆1日电　苏丹武装部队最高委员会主席、总理易卜拉欣·阿布德昨晚在这里的国家剧院观看了中国杂技艺术团的演出。
    同他一起观看表演的有最高委员会委员、宣传和劳工部长塔拉特·法里德少将，最高委员会委员、内政部长巴哈里准将，教育部长齐亚达·阿巴卜。中国杂技艺术团团长屈武和中国大使馆临时代办李石陪同观看演出。
    中国杂技艺术团表演了十五个节目，苏丹领导人和观众们对中国演员的表演报以热烈的鼓掌。
    在演出前，阿布德接见了中国杂技艺术团团长屈武和中国大使馆临时代办李石。
