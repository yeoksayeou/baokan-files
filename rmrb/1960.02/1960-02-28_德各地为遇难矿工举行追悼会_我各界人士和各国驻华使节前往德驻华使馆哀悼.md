Title: Memorial Services Held in Various Parts of Germany for Deceased Miners; People from All Walks of Life in China and Ambassadors to China from Various Countries Visit the German Embassy in China to Express Condolences
Author:
Date: 1960-02-28
Page: 3

Xinhua News Agency, Berlin, 27th: People across the German Democratic Republic held memorial services this morning in Zwickau for the deceased miners of the Karl Marx coal mine. Premier Grotewohl spoke at the memorial service. The entire nation observed two minutes of silence for the deceased and flew flags at half-mast in mourning.

President Pieck of the German Democratic Republic and the Central Committee of the Socialist Unity Party of Germany wrote letters to the families of the deceased miners, expressing condolences and sympathy. The letter from the Central Committee, signed by Comrade Ulbricht, said: "All members of the Socialist Unity Party of Germany and all workers of the German Democratic Republic mourn with you the loss suffered by our ranks and extend our sincere condolences to you." President Pieck's letter said: "We will remember our heroic comrades and will continue to tirelessly strive for peace and socialism."

Compiled report from this newspaper, according to Xinhua News Agency, 27th: Chairman Stoica of the Romanian Council of Ministers and the World Federation of Trade Unions today sent telegrams to Premier Grotewohl and Chairman Waneck of the Free German Trade Union Federation, respectively, expressing deep condolences to the entire working people of the German Democratic Republic and mourning for the victims of the Zwickau coal mine.

Xinhua News Agency, Berlin, 27th: Wang Guoquan, Ambassador of our country to the German Democratic Republic, went to the Ministry of Foreign Affairs of the German Democratic Republic today to convey the condolences and sympathy of the Chinese government for the Zwickau mine disaster in the German Democratic Republic.

Xinhua News Agency, 27th: People from all walks of life in the capital today went to the Embassy of the German Democratic Republic in China to mourn the victims of the unfortunate explosion in a mine shaft of the Karl Marx coal mine in Zwickau.

Those who went to the embassy to express their condolences included Liu Ningyi, Chairman of the All-China Federation of Trade Unions, Li Zaowen, Secretary of the Secretariat, Li Chunming, Vice-Chairman of the National Committee of the China Coal Miners' Union, Zhang Xiruo, Director of the Commission for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries, Zhao Zishang, Vice-Minister of the Ministry of Coal Industry, Zeng Yongquan, Vice-Minister of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs, Zhu Kezhen, President of the China-German Democratic Republic Friendship Association, Feng Jiping and Jia Tingsan, Vice-Mayors of Beijing, Wang Jiong, Chairman of the Beijing Trade Union, miners from the Jingxi Mining Bureau, members of the China-Germany Friendship People's Commune, and hundreds of people from all walks of life in the capital. Government departments, trade unions and other people's organizations sent wreaths to the deceased.

The ambassadors, chargés d'affaires and diplomatic officials of the Soviet Union, Bulgaria, Romania, Hungary, the Democratic People's Republic of Korea, Czechoslovakia, Poland, the Mongolian People's Republic, Albania, the Democratic Republic of Vietnam, India, Indonesia, Yugoslavia, Afghanistan, the United Arab Republic, Ceylon, Cambodia and Iraq also went to the Embassy of the German Democratic Republic in China today to express their condolences and sent letters to the Embassy of the German Democratic Republic in China expressing their condolences.

### 德各地为遇难矿工举行追悼会  我各界人士和各国驻华使节前往德驻华使馆哀悼

1960-02-28
第3版()
专栏：

    德各地为遇难矿工举行追悼会
    我各界人士和各国驻华使节前往德驻华使馆哀悼
    新华社柏林27日电　德意志民主共和国各地人民今天上午在兹维考城为卡尔·马克思煤矿遇难矿工举行追悼会。格罗提渥总理在追悼会上讲了话。全国为死难者默哀两分钟，并且悬挂半旗志哀。
    德意志民主共和国总统皮克和德国统一社会党中央委员会曾分别写信给受难矿工家属，表示悼念和慰问。由乌布利希同志署名的中央委员会的信说：“德国统一社会党全体党员和德意志民主共和国全体工人，同你们一起哀悼我们的队伍所蒙受的损失，并向你们致以亲切的慰问。”皮克总统的信说：“我们将铭记住我们英勇的同志，并且将坚持不懈地继续为和平和社会主义而奋斗。”
    本报综合报道　据新华社27日讯：罗马尼亚部长会议主席斯托伊卡和世界工会联合会今天分别致电格罗提渥总理和致电自由德国工会联合会主席瓦恩克，向德意志民主共和国全体劳动人民表示深切的慰问，并对兹维考城煤矿死难者表示哀悼。
    新华社柏林27日电　我国驻德意志民主共和国大使王国权为德意志民主共和国兹维考矿工遇难事件，今日前往德意志民主共和国外交部，转达了中国政府的悼念和慰问。
    新华社27日讯　首都各界人士今天前往德意志民主共和国驻中国大使馆，哀悼兹维考城卡尔·马克思煤矿的一个矿井不幸发生爆炸事件中的死难者。
    前往大使馆表示哀悼的，有中华全国总工会主席刘宁一、书记处书记栗再温，中国煤矿工会全国委员会副主席李春明，对外文化联络委员会主任张奚若，煤炭工业部副部长赵子尚，外交部副部长曾涌泉，中国德意志民主共和国友好协会会长竺可桢，北京市副市长冯基平、贾庭三，北京市工会主席王炯，京西矿务局的矿工们，中德友好人民公社的社员们，以及首都各界人士等共数百人。政府部门、工会等人民团体向死难者送了花圈。
    苏联、保加利亚、罗马尼亚、匈牙利、朝鲜民主主义人民共和国、捷克斯洛伐克、波兰、蒙古人民共和国、阿尔巴尼亚、越南民主共和国、印度、印度尼西亚、南斯拉夫、阿富汗、阿拉伯联合共和国、锡兰、柬埔寨和伊拉克等国的大使、代办和外交官员，今天也前往德意志民主共和国驻中国大使馆表示哀悼和致函德意志民主共和国驻中国大使馆表示哀悼。
