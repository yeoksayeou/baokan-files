Title: China and North Korea Sign Cultural Cooperation Agreement for This Year's Implementation Plan
Author:
Date: 1960-02-06
Page: 6
Xinhua News Agency, 5th. China and the Democratic People's Republic of Korea government's 1960 implementation plan for the Cultural Cooperation Agreement was signed this afternoon in Beijing.

    Representing the Chinese side was Luo Jun, Deputy Director of the Commission for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries. Representing the Korean side was the Korean Ambassador to China, Li Yong-ho. The 1960 implementation plan for the cultural cooperation agreement between the Chinese and Korean governments covers eight aspects, including science, culture, art, education, health, sports, news, publishing, broadcasting, and exhibitions.
    After the signing ceremony, Luo Jun and Li Yong-ho spoke, warmly congratulating the further development of cultural cooperation between China and North Korea, and the eternal consolidation of the friendly unity between the peoples of China and North Korea.

### 中朝签订文化合作协定今年执行计划

1960-02-06
第6版()
专栏：

    中朝签订文化合作协定今年执行计划
    新华社5日讯　中华人民共和国政府和朝鲜民主主义人民共和国政府文化合作协定1960年执行计划，今天下午在北京签字。
    代表中国方面签字的，是对外文化联络委员会副主任罗俊，代表朝鲜方面签字的，是朝鲜驻中国大使李永镐。中朝两国政府文化合作协定1960年执行计划，包括科学、文化、艺术、教育、卫生，体育、新闻、出版、广播、展览等八个方面。
    签字仪式后，罗俊和李永镐先后讲话，热烈祝贺中朝两国文化合作更加发展，中朝两国人民的友好团结永远巩固。
