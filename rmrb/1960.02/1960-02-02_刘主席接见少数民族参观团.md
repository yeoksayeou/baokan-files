Title: Chairman Liu Receives Minority Nationalities Visiting Group
Author:
Date: 1960-02-02
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, February 1st: Chairman Liu Shaoqi received this afternoon all members of the Tibetan Agricultural Visiting Group, Xinjiang Agricultural Visiting Group, Tibetan Public School Cadre Visiting Group, and Taiyuan and Shijiazhuang Railway Institute Minority Nationalities Students Visiting Group.

Accompanying the reception were Huang Yanpei, Chen Shutong, Panchen Erdeni Chökyi Gyaltsen, Vice Chairmen of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress; Chen Yi, Tan Zhenlin, Lu Dingyi, and Xi Zhongxun, Vice Premiers of the State Council; and Burhan and Pagbalha Geleg Namgyal, Vice Chairmen of the National Committee of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference.

Also present at the reception were Wang Feng, Liu Chun, Sa Kongliao, Yang Jingren, Xie Hechou, and Yu Xinqing, Vice Ministers of the Nationalities Affairs Commission.

### 刘主席接见少数民族参观团

1960-02-02
第1版()
专栏：

    刘主席接见少数民族参观团
    新华社1日讯　刘少奇主席今天下午接见了西藏农业参观团、新疆农业参观团、西藏公学干部参观团、太原和石家庄铁路学院少数民族学员参观团的全体人员。
    陪同接见的有，全国人民代表大会常务委员会副委员长黄炎培、陈叔通、班禅额尔德尼·却吉坚赞，国务院副总理陈毅、谭震林、陆定一、习仲勋，政协全国委员会副主席包尔汉、帕巴拉·卓列朗杰。
    接见时在场的，有民族事务委员会副主任汪锋、刘春、萨空了、杨静仁、谢鹤筹、余心清。
