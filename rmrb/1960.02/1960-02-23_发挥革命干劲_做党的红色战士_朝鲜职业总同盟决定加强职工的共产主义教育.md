Title: Give Play to Revolutionary Drive, Be Red Soldiers of the Party: Korean Professional General League Decides to Strengthen Communist Education of Workers and Staff
Author:
Date: 1960-02-23
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, Pyongyang, 21st: The Pyongyang newspapers reported today that the Korean Professional General League Central Committee's second plenary meeting, which concluded its two-day session on the 19th, passed a resolution emphasizing the need to promote and organize the revolutionary drive of the working people, so that this year's labor productivity will be increased by more than 20%-30% compared to last year.

The resolution emphasized that raising the revolutionary consciousness of the working people is of decisive significance for improving productivity.

To this end, trade union organizations must strengthen communist education for the working people, so that they become red soldiers loyal to the Party.

The resolution also pointed out that it is necessary to make the working people consciously abide by socialist discipline and work systems, rationally utilize the four hundred and eighty minutes of working time, and rapidly improve their technical level.

The resolution pointed out that it is necessary to implement the Party's policy of gradually achieving mechanization in production, first mechanizing heavy labor, and gradually carrying out comprehensive mechanization starting with small mechanization. Broadly adopt advanced technology and new work methods, and further strengthen the invention and innovation and rationalization proposal movement.

The resolution said that trade union organizations at all levels and their leaders must better lead the work of the socialist emulation movement, further expand the "Chollima Work Team" movement on a voluntary basis among the masses, and further strengthen and improve the production agreement meeting work, which is an important form of laboring people participating in production management.

### 发挥革命干劲　做党的红色战士  朝鲜职业总同盟决定加强职工的共产主义教育

1960-02-23
第6版()
专栏：

    发挥革命干劲　做党的红色战士
    朝鲜职业总同盟决定加强职工的共产主义教育
    新华社平壤21日电　平壤报纸今天报道，朝鲜职业总同盟中央委员会第二次全体会议在19日结束了为期两天的会议时通过决议，强调要发扬和组织劳动人民的革命干劲，使今年的劳动生产率比去年提高20％—30％以上。
    决议强调指出，提高劳动人民的革命觉悟对于提高生产率有决定性意义。
    为此，工会组织必须加强对劳动人民的共产主义教育，使他们成为忠于党的红色战士。
    决议还指出要使劳动人民自觉地遵守社会主义的纪律和工作制度，合理利用四百八十分钟的劳动时间，迅速提高技术水平。
    决议指出，必须贯彻党的在生产方面逐步实现机械化的方针，首先使笨重劳动机械化，并且从小机械化开始逐步进行综合机械化。广泛采用先进技术和新的工作法，进一步加强发明创造和合理化建议运动。
    决议说，各级工会组织和它的领导人必须更好地领导社会主义竞赛运动的工作，在群众自愿的基础上进一步扩大“千里马作业班”运动，进一步加强和改善劳动人民参加生产管理的重要形式的生产协议会工作。
