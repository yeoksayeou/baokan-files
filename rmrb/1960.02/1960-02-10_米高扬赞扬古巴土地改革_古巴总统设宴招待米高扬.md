Title: Mikoyan Praises Cuban Land Reform; Cuban President Hosts Banquet for Mikoyan
Author:
Date: 1960-02-10
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, Havana, February 9th - TASS reported from Havana on February 9th: Soviet Council of Ministers First Deputy Chairman Mikoyan visited the Cuban National Institute of Agrarian Reform yesterday. He was warmly received by the Chairman of the Committee, Jiménez.

	Mikoyan congratulated Cuba on its decision to carry out land reform and national industrialization. He pointed out that without industrialization and the kind of enthusiasm he saw throughout Cuba, a new life could not be built. Mikoyan expressed his hope that the Cuban revolution would bring fruitful results to the Cuban people.

	Jiménez, on behalf of all the staff of the committee, expressed his gratitude for Mikoyan's visit and expressed his hope to develop trade relations between the Soviet Union and Cuba.

Xinhua News Agency, Havana, February 9th - TASS reported from Havana on February 9th: Cuban President Dorticos hosted a banquet yesterday in honor of Soviet Council of Ministers First Deputy Chairman Mikoyan.

	Among those attending the banquet were Cuban Prime Minister Castro, Cuban government leaders, and Soviet officials in charge of the Exhibition of Soviet Scientific, Technological, and Cultural Achievements. The banquet was held in a warm and friendly atmosphere.

### 米高扬赞扬古巴土地改革  古巴总统设宴招待米高扬

1960-02-10
第6版()
专栏：

    米高扬赞扬古巴土地改革
    古巴总统设宴招待米高扬
    新华社9日讯　据塔斯社哈瓦那9日讯：苏联部长会议第一副主席米高扬昨天访问了古巴土地改革全国委员会。他受到委员会主席希门尼斯的热情接待。
    米高扬祝贺古巴进行土地改革和国家工业化的决定。他指出，如果没有工业化和他在古巴各地看到的那种热情，就不能建设新生活。米高扬表示希望古巴的革命给古巴人民带来丰硕的果实。
    希门尼斯代表委员会全体工作人员对米高扬的访问表示感谢，并表示希望发展苏联和古巴两国的贸易关系。
    新华社9日讯　塔斯社哈瓦那9日讯：古巴总统多尔蒂科斯昨天设宴招待苏联部长会议第一副主席米高扬。
    出席宴会的有古巴总理卡斯特罗、古巴政府领导人和负责苏联科学、技术和文化成就展览会的苏联官员。宴会在热烈友好的气氛中进行。
