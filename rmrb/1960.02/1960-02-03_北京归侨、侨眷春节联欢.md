Title: Beijing Returned Overseas Chinese, Overseas Chinese Dependents Spring Festival Celebration
Author:
Date: 1960-02-03
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, February 2nd: Thirteen thousand returned overseas Chinese, overseas Chinese dependents, and returned overseas Chinese students in Beijing held a Spring Festival celebration in the Great Hall of the People this afternoon.

Liao Chengzhi, Director of the Overseas Chinese Affairs Commission of the People's Republic of China, attended the celebration and, on behalf of the Overseas Chinese Affairs Commission and all overseas Chinese affairs workers across the country, extended Spring Festival greetings to all returned overseas Chinese, overseas Chinese dependents, and returned overseas Chinese students attending the celebration.

Today's celebration was jointly organized by the All-China Federation of Returned Overseas Chinese, the Beijing Civil Affairs Bureau, the Beijing Education Bureau, the Beijing Federation of Returned Overseas Chinese, the Beijing Municipal Committee of the Communist Youth League, the Beijing Youth Federation, the Beijing Student Federation, and the Overseas Chinese Service Department of the Head Office of the Bank of China. Among those attending the celebration were Yang Xiufeng, Minister of Education, Wu Han and Wang Kunlun, Deputy Mayors of Beijing, Zhang Chao, Vice Chairman of the All-China Youth Federation, and You Yangzu, Vice Chairman of the All-China Federation of Returned Overseas Chinese. Some returned overseas Chinese students from Liaoning, Shanghai, Nanjing and other places who are spending their winter vacation in Beijing also participated in the celebration.

The returned overseas Chinese, overseas Chinese dependents, and returned overseas Chinese students who participated in the celebration enjoyed wonderful performances by art troupes such as the General Political Department Cultural Troupe of the Chinese People's Liberation Army, the Acrobatic Troupe of the China Railway Art Theater, the China Peking Opera Theater, and the Central Experimental Opera House.

### 北京归侨、侨眷春节联欢

1960-02-03
第1版()
专栏：

    北京归侨、侨眷春节联欢
    新华社2日讯　北京市归侨、侨眷和归国华侨学生一万三千人，今天下午在人民大会堂举行春节联欢会。
    中华人民共和国华侨事务委员会主任瘳承志出席了联欢会，并代表华侨事务委员会和全国全体侨务工作者，向出席联欢会的全体归侨、侨眷、归国华侨学生祝贺春节。
    今天的联欢会是由中华全国归国华侨联合会、北京市民政局、北京市教育局、北京市归国华侨联合会、共青团北京市委、北京市青年联合会、北京市学生联合会、中国银行总管理处华侨服务部联合举办的。出席联欢会的，有教育部部长杨秀峰，北京市副市长吴晗、王昆仑，中华全国青年联合会副主席张超，中华全国归国华侨联合会副主席尤扬祖等。正在北京度寒假的辽宁、上海、南京等地部分归国华侨学生，也参加了联欢会。
    参加联欢会的归侨、侨眷和归国华侨学生，在会上欣赏了中国人民解放军总政文工团、中国铁道艺术剧院杂技团、中国京剧院、中央实验歌剧院等艺术团体的精彩演出。
