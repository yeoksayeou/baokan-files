Title: Two Representatives of the Polish Cultural Delegation Arrive in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1960-02-06
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, 3rd – Two representatives of the Polish Cultural Delegation, Pwasza and Bentzki, arrived in Beijing by plane this afternoon at the invitation of the Committee for Foreign Cultural Relations to discuss the 1960 and 1961 executive plans for the Sino-Polish Cultural Cooperation Agreement. Chen Zhongjing, Secretary-General of the Committee for Foreign Cultural Relations, and others welcomed them at the airport.

### 波兰文化代表团的两位代表到京

1960-02-06
第6版()
专栏：

    波兰文化代表团的两位代表到京
    新华社3日讯　应对外文化联络委员会邀请前来我国商订中波文化合作协定1960、1961两年执行计划的波兰文化代表团的两位代表普瓦萨和本茨基，今天下午乘飞机到达北京。到机场欢迎的有对外文化联络委员会秘书长陈忠经等。
