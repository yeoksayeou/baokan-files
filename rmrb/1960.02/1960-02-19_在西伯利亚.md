Title: In Siberia...
Author: Yang Shusheng
Date: 1960-02-19
Page: 8

Small Knowledge

    In Siberia…
    Siberia occupies ten million square kilometers of land, an area equal to the combined total of the United States (excluding Alaska), Britain, France, Italy, and Germany. Even the huge continent of Australia would have to give way in comparison to Siberia.
    Now, on the map of Siberia, you can't find a city without a huge factory. The western part of Siberia (Altai Krai, Kemerovo, Kurgan, Novosibirsk, Omsk, Tomsk, and Tyumen Oblast) has an annual timber increment exceeding 70-80 million cubic meters.
    It is possible to build hydroelectric power stations with a total power of approximately 165 million kilowatts on the rivers of Siberia.
    The total length of navigable and floatable rivers in Siberia is 100,000 kilometers. (Compiled by Yang Shusheng)

### 在西伯利亚……
杨树生
1960-02-19
第8版()
专栏：小常识

    在西伯利亚……
    西伯利亚占有一千万平方公里土地，这块土地的面积等于把美国（除去阿拉斯加）、英国、法国、意大利、德国连在一起的总和。巨大的大陆澳大利亚的面积如果和西伯利亚比较起来，还得让位呢。
    现在在西伯利亚的地图上找不到一个没有巨大工厂的城市。西伯利亚的西部（阿尔泰边区、克麦罗沃、库尔干、诺沃西比尔斯克、鄂木斯克、托姆斯克和秋明地带），每年木材的增殖量超过七——八千万立方米。
    在西伯利亚的河上能建成一个总功率大约为一亿六千五百瓩水力的发电站。
    在西伯利亚可以通航和浮运的河流总长有十万公里。（杨树生编译）
