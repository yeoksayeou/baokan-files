Title: Khrushchev Receives Lodge
Author:
Date: 1960-02-10
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, February 9th: TASS reports: Chairman of the Council of Ministers of the Soviet Union, Khrushchev, received the United States Permanent Representative to the United Nations, Lodge, who is currently visiting the Soviet Union, on the 8th, and held friendly talks with him.

Lodge is visiting the Soviet Union as a guest of the United States Ambassador to the Soviet Union, Thompson.

### 赫鲁晓夫接见洛奇

1960-02-10
第5版()
专栏：

    赫鲁晓夫接见洛奇
    新华社9日讯　据塔斯社莫斯科讯：苏联部长会议主席赫鲁晓夫8日接见了现在苏联访问的美国驻联合国常任代表洛奇，并且同他进行了友好的谈话。
    洛奇是作为美国驻苏联大使汤普森的客人到苏联访问的。
