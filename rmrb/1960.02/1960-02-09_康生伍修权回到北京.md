Title: Kang Sheng and Wu Xiuquan Return to Beijing
Author:
Date: 1960-02-09
Page: 1

    Xinhua News Agency, February 8th: Kang Sheng, alternate member of the Political Bureau of the CPC Central Committee, and Wu Xiuquan, member of the CPC Central Committee, returned to Beijing by plane today from Moscow after participating in the high-level meeting of the Political Consultative Committee of the Warsaw Treaty Organization as observers of the People's Republic of China. Chen Yi, Li Xiannian, and Tan Zhenlin, members of the Political Bureau of the CPC Central Committee and Vice Premiers of the State Council, and Liu Ningyi, member of the CPC Central Committee, were at the airport to welcome them. Antonov, Chargé d'Affaires ad interim of the Soviet Embassy in China, was also at the airport to welcome them.
    Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, February 8th: Kang Sheng, alternate member of the Political Bureau of the CPC Central Committee, and Wu Xiuquan, member of the CPC Central Committee, left Moscow by plane on the evening of the 7th to return home after participating in the meeting of the Political Consultative Committee of the Warsaw Treaty Organization as observers. Pospelov, alternate member of the Presidium of the CPSU Central Committee and Secretary of the Secretariat of the CPSU Central Committee, responsible personnel from the CPSU Central Committee and the Soviet Ministry of Foreign Affairs, and others were at the airport to see them off. Liu Xiao, Chinese Ambassador to the Soviet Union, also went to the airport to see them off.

### 康生伍修权回到北京

1960-02-09
第1版()
专栏：

    康生伍修权回到北京
    新华社8日讯　中共中央政治局候补委员康生，中共中央委员伍修权，以中华人民共和国观察员身份参加了华沙条约政治协商委员会高级会议以后，已于今天乘飞机从莫斯科回到北京。到机场欢迎的有中共中央政治局委员、国务院副总理陈毅、李先念、谭震林，中共中央委员刘宁一。苏联驻华大使馆临时代办安东诺夫也到机场欢迎。
    新华社莫斯科8日电　中共中央政治局候补委员康生和中共中央委员伍修权，以观察员身份参加华沙条约政治协商委员会会议之后，7日晚乘飞机离莫斯科回国。到机场欢送的有苏共中央主席团候补委员、苏共中央书记处书记波斯别洛夫，苏共中央和苏联外交部的负责工作人员等。中国驻苏联大使刘晓也到机场送行。
