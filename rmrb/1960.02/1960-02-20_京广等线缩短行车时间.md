Title: Jingguang and Other Lines Shorten Travel Time
Author:
Date: 1960-02-20
Page: 2

    Jingguang, Longhai, Baoji-Chengdu, Chengdu-Chongqing and other railway lines' passenger trains will implement a new timetable starting from the 25th of this month.
    The main purpose of this change of passenger train schedule is to tap transportation potential and improve transportation efficiency, thereby accelerating the turnover of locomotives and vehicles. According to the new timetable, the No. 15 special express train from Beijing to Guangzhou will increase its average direct speed by 5.1 kilometers per hour, and the train running time will be shortened by 4 hours and 52 minutes compared to the winter schedule.

### 京广等线缩短行车时间

1960-02-20
第2版()
专栏：

    京广等线缩短行车时间
    本报讯　京广、陇海、宝成、成渝等铁路线旅客列车，将自本月25日起，实行新运行图。
    这次旅客列车改用新时刻，主要是为了挖掘运输潜力，提高运输效率，从而加速机车、车辆周转。按照新运行图，北京到广州的十五次特别快车，全程直通速度平均每小时提高五点一公里，列车运行时间比冬季又缩短四小时五十二分。
