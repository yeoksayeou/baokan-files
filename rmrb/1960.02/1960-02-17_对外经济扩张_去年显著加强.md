Title: Overseas Economic Expansion Significantly Strengthened Last Year
Author: 
Date: 1960-02-17
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, Tokyo: Official statistics released by the Japanese Ministry of Finance on January 30 indicate that the economic expansion of Japanese monopoly capital abroad was significantly strengthened last year.

According to the announcement, by the end of last year, Japan's total investment and loans abroad reached 1.217 billion US dollars, the highest record since the war. In the past year, Japan's direct investment abroad increased by 61 million US dollars compared with 1958, and the amount of deferred payment exports increased by 86 million US dollars.

### 对外经济扩张  去年显著加强

1960-02-17
第6版()
专栏：

    对外经济扩张  去年显著加强
    据新华社讯　东京消息：日本大藏省1月30日发表的官方统计数字表明，日本垄断资本在国外的经济扩张去年有了显著的加强。
    据宣布，到去年年底日本在国外的投资和贷款总额达到十二亿一千七百万美元，这是战后的最高纪录。在过去一年中，日本向国外的直接投资比1958年增加了六千一百万美元，延期付款的出口额增加了八千六百万美元。
