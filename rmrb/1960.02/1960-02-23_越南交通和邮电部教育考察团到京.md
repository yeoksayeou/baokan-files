Title: Vietnamese Ministry of Transport and Post Office Education Delegation Arrives in Beijing
Author: 
Date: 1960-02-23
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency, February 22nd. The Vice Minister of the Ministry of Transport and Post Office of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam, Nguyen Nhu Que, and the Vietnamese Ministry of Transport and Post Office Education Delegation he led, a group of seven people, arrived in Beijing by train this afternoon. The delegation is visiting China at our invitation to study higher education and scientific research in the fields of transportation, post and telecommunications, and railways.
    Yu Guangsheng, Vice Minister of Railways, and representatives from the Ministry of Communications and the Ministry of Posts and Telecommunications went to the station to welcome them.
    Dang Thanh Van, Commercial Counselor of the Embassy of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam in China, also went to the station to welcome them.

### 越南交通和邮电部教育考察团到京

1960-02-23
第4版()
专栏：

    越南交通和邮电部教育考察团到京
    新华社22日讯　越南民主共和国交通和邮电部副部长阮如晷和他率领的越南民主共和国交通和邮电部教育考察团一行七人，今天下午乘火车到达北京。考察团是应我国邀请前来考察交通、邮电、铁道的高等教育和科学研究事业的。
    前往车站欢迎的，有铁道部副部长余光生和交通部、邮电部的代表。
    越南民主共和国驻中国大使馆商务参赞邓青云也到车站欢迎。
