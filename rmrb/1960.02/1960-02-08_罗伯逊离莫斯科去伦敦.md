Title: Robeson Leaves Moscow for London
Author:
Date: 1960-02-08
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, 7th. TASS reports from Moscow: The distinguished peace fighter, famous singer and actor Robeson, who was visiting Moscow at the invitation of the Soviet Peace Defense Committee, left here today for London.
    A warm send-off ceremony was held at Vnukovo Airport. Many representatives from the theatrical circles of the Soviet capital, as well as some members of the Soviet Peace Defense Committee, attended the send-off.

### 罗伯逊离莫斯科去伦敦

1960-02-08
第5版()
专栏：

    罗伯逊离莫斯科去伦敦
    新华社7日讯　据塔斯社莫斯科讯：应苏联保卫和平委员会的邀请在莫斯科进行访问的杰出的和平战士、著名的歌唱家和演员罗伯逊今天离开这里去伦敦。
    在伏努科夫机场上举行了热烈的欢送会。参加欢送会的有苏联首都戏剧界的许多代表，以及苏联保卫和平委员会的一些委员。
