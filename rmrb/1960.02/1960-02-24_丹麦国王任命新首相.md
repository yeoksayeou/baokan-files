Title: Danish King Appoints New Prime Minister
Author:
Date: 1960-02-24
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, Copenhagen, 22nd—Danish King Frederik IX appointed Finance Minister Viggo Kampmann as the new Danish Prime Minister yesterday, succeeding the late Prime Minister Hansen.

Kampmann had been serving as the Finance Minister since 1953 and had been acting as Prime Minister since January of this year.

A government announcement stated that Kampmann will continue to serve concurrently as the Finance Minister, and there will be no changes to other cabinet positions.

### 丹麦国王任命新首相

1960-02-24
第6版()
专栏：

    丹麦国王任命新首相
    新华社22日讯　哥本哈根消息：丹麦国王昨天任命财政大臣维戈·坎普曼继已故首相汉森为丹麦首相。
    坎普曼从1953年起一直担任财政大臣，今年1月起代理首相职务。
    政府公告说，坎普曼继续兼任财政大臣，内阁其他职务无变动。
