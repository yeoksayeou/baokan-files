Title: Soviet Union to Publish Physics Encyclopedia
Author:
Date: 1960-02-27
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, 26th - According to TASS reports: The Soviet Union is about to publish a four-volume physics encyclopedia. This is a massive reference book encompassing all branches of modern physics and related subjects, containing over six thousand five hundred entries and more than four thousand illustrations.
	Prominent Soviet physicists have participated in the compilation of the encyclopedia.

### 苏联即将出版物理学百科全书

1960-02-27
第6版()
专栏：

    苏联即将出版物理学百科全书
    新华社莫斯科26日电　据塔斯社报道：苏联即将出版四卷集的物理学百科全书。这是一种包罗了现代物理学中各个部门及其有关科目的巨型参考书，其中一共包括六千五百多个条目和四千多帧插图。
    苏联著名物理学家都参加了百科全书的编纂工作。
