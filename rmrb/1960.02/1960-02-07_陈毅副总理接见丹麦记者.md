Title: Chen Yi, Vice Premier, Receives Danish Journalists
Author: 
Date: 1960-02-07
Page: 2

Xinhua News Agency, February 6th: Vice Premier Chen Yi received Danish "Daily News" reporter An Shengda and Danish "Politiken" reporter Niu Sitai this afternoon who were visiting China.

　　Danish Ambassador to China, Batteson, was present at the meeting.

　　Also present at the meeting were Kang Maozhao, Deputy Director of the Information Department of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs, and Wang Jin, Deputy Director of the Department of Western European Affairs.

### 陈毅副总理接见丹麦记者

1960-02-07
第2版()
专栏：

    陈毅副总理接见丹麦记者
    新华社6日讯　陈毅副总理今天下午接见了来我国访问的丹麦“每日新闻”报记者安升达和丹麦“政治报”记者纽斯太德。
    接见时丹麦驻中国大使巴特森在座。
  接见时在座的，有外交部新闻司副司长康矛召、西欧司副司长王进。
