Title: Soviet Aid is Sincere and Friendly
Author:
Date: 1960-02-20
Page: 5

    Soviet Aid is Sincere and Friendly
    "This form of aid (referring to the Soviet loan to Ethiopia) is of great significance to our progress, and our people should highly appreciate it."
                    —Emperor Haile Selassie I of Ethiopia

    "Cambodia will not forget the friendly and sincere assistance of the Soviet Union. This assistance helps the Kingdom's government ensure our country's economic and political independence."
                          —King Suramarit of Cambodia

    "Soviet experts work diligently and strive to exceed the prescribed targets. We are very satisfied with them and express our gratitude. Special mention should be made of the friendly relationship between Soviet experts and Afghan personnel, as well as their sincere desire to enable Afghan cadres to quickly master complex modern technologies."
        —Malek Khan, Manager of the Afghan Automobile Repair Shop built with Soviet Assistance

    "When the Soviet Union provides assistance to other countries, it does not have the ulterior motives that some other countries have."
                                  —“Nepal News”

    "The purpose of Soviet training is to enable Indian engineers to independently use modern technology as quickly as possible."
                              —“Hindustan Times”

### 苏联援助真诚友好

1960-02-20
第5版()
专栏：

    苏联援助真诚友好
    “这种形式的援助（指苏联给埃塞俄比亚的贷款）对我们的进步有很大的意义，我们的人民应该予以高度的评价。”
                    ——埃塞俄比亚国王塞拉西一世
    “柬埔寨对苏联的友好的、真诚的援助不会忘怀。这种援助有助于王国政府保证我国的经济和政治独立。”
                          ——柬埔寨国王苏拉玛里特
    “苏联专家工作起来既勤恳又努力，尽量提前提前规定的指标。我们对他们十分满意，并向他们表示感谢。特别应该提到苏联专家对阿富汗人员的友好关系，以及他们使阿富汗干部尽快地掌握复杂的现代技术的真诚愿望。”
        ——由苏联帮助建设的阿富汗汽车修配厂经理
                                                  马莱克汗
    “苏联对别的国家进行援助时，没有其他一些国家所具有的不良动机。”
                                  ——“尼泊尔新闻”
    “苏联训练的目的在于，如何使印度工程师尽快地独立使用现代技术。”
                              ——“印度时坦时报”
