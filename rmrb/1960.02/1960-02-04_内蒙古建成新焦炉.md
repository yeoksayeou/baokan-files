Title: New Coke Oven Built in Inner Mongolia
Author:
Date: 1960-02-04
Page: 2

Xinhua News Agency report: A new 30-coke oven in the coking workshop of the Xinsheng Iron and Steel Plant in Hohhot, Inner Mongolia, has recently been completed and officially put into production.

This coke oven can produce 100,000 tons of high-quality coke annually. The workshop can also use the large amount of coal tar recovered from this coke oven to produce more than ten kinds of valuable chemical raw materials to supply the needs of the autonomous region's iron and steel smelting industry and chemical industry.

### 内蒙古建成新焦炉

1960-02-04
第2版()
专栏：

    内蒙古建成新焦炉
    据新华社讯　内蒙古呼和浩特新生钢铁厂焦化车间新建的一座三十孔焦炉，已在最近建成并正式投入生产。
    这座焦炉能年产优质焦炭十万吨。车间还可利用这座焦炉回收的大批焦油生产出十多种贵重的化工原料，供给自治区钢铁冶炼工业和化学工业的需要。
