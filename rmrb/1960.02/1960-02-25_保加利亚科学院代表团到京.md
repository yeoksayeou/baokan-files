Title: Bulgarian Academy of Sciences Delegation Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1960-02-25
Page: 5

    Xinhua News Agency, February 24th. A delegation from the Bulgarian Academy of Sciences, headed by Corresponding Academician Lyubomir Krastanov, Vice President of the Bulgarian Academy of Sciences, arrived in Beijing by plane this afternoon. The delegation members are: Corresponding Academician Boyan Kaminov, Secretary of the Department of Geological, Geographical and Chemical Sciences of the Bulgarian Academy of Sciences, and Professor Asen Beorchev, Director of the Soil Erosion Laboratory of the Soil Institute of the Bulgarian Academy of Sciences.
    The delegation is visiting China to discuss the 1960 scientific cooperation implementation plan between the two academies, according to the scientific cooperation agreement between the Chinese and Bulgarian Academies of Sciences.
    Wu Youxun, Vice President of the Chinese Academy of Sciences, Yin Zanhun, Director of the Department of Earth Sciences of the Chinese Academy of Sciences, Zhao Jiuzhang, Director of the Institute of Geophysics of the Chinese Academy of Sciences, and Wang Tuo, Director of the Liaison Bureau of the Chinese Academy of Sciences, went to the airport to welcome the delegation.
    Personnel from the Bulgarian Embassy in China also went to the airport to welcome them.

### 保加利亚科学院代表团到京

1960-02-25
第5版()
专栏：

    保加利亚科学院代表团到京
    新华社24日讯　以保加利亚科学院副院长柳鲍米尔·克雷斯坦诺夫通讯院士为首的保加利亚科学院代表团，今天下午乘飞机抵达北京。代表团成员有：保加利亚科学院地质地理和化学学部秘书波扬·卡明诺夫通讯院士和保加利亚科学院土壤研究所水土浸蚀室主任阿谢·别奥尔切夫教授。
    代表团是根据中保两国科学院科学合作协议，来我国商谈1960年两院科学合作执行计划的。
    前往机场欢迎代表团的，有中国科学院副院长吴有训、中国科学院地学部主任尹赞勋、中国科学院地球物理研究所所长赵九章、中国科学院联络局局长王拓等。
    保加利亚驻华大使馆人员也到机场欢迎。
