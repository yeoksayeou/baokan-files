Title: Yasui Kaoru and Yoshida Yoshiakira Arrive in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1960-02-13
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, February 12th. Yasui Kaoru, Director of the Japan Council Against Atomic and Hydrogen Bombs, and Yoshida Yoshiakira, Deputy Secretary-General of the Council's Secretariat, arrived in Beijing by plane today. They are visiting China at the invitation of the China Peace Committee.

In the evening, Liao Chengzhi, Vice-Chairman of the China Peace Committee, hosted a banquet to welcome the guests. Other leaders of the China Peace Committee were present. Japanese peace advocate Kinkazu Saionji and his wife, as well as Korean peace advocate Hwang Bong-koo, were also invited to attend.

### 安井郁、吉田嘉清到京

1960-02-13
第4版()
专栏：

    安井郁、吉田嘉清到京
    新华社12日讯　日本禁止原子弹和氢弹协议会理事长安井郁和协议会事务局次长吉田嘉清，今天乘飞机到达北京。他们是应中国人民保卫世界和平委员会邀请来我国访问的。
    晚上，中国人民保卫世界和平委员会副主席廖承志设宴欢迎客人。在座的有中国人民保卫世界和平委员会其他负责人。在京的日本和平人士西园寺公一和他的夫人以及朝鲜和平人士黄凤九也应邀出席。
