Title: Sino-Soviet Friendship Association Delegation Returns to Beijing
Author:
Date: 1960-02-27
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency, February 26th. The Sino-Soviet Friendship Association delegation of six people, led by Secretary-General Hu Weide, returned to Beijing on the 25th, after visiting the Soviet Union to participate in the celebration of the tenth anniversary of the signing of the Sino-Soviet Treaty of Friendship, Alliance and mutual assistance at the invitation of the Union of Soviet Societies for Friendship and Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries and the Soviet-Chinese Friendship Association. Head of the delegation Liu Changsheng and Deputy Head Li Zhuchen are still abroad.
    Those who greeted them at the airport included Deputy Secretary-General of the Sino-Soviet Friendship Association, Ge Baoquan, Li Zhanwu, and others. Sudarikov, Counselor of the Soviet Embassy in China, and Sang Feilou, First Secretary, also greeted them at the airport.
    In addition to participating in various celebratory activities in Moscow, the delegation visited Baku, Sumgait, and other places, and had extensive contact with the Soviet people.

### 中苏友好协会代表团回到北京

1960-02-27
第4版()
专栏：

    中苏友好协会代表团回到北京
    新华社26日讯　应苏联对外友好和文化协会联合会和苏中友好协会的邀请，前往苏联参加中苏友好同盟互助条约签订十周年庆祝活动的中苏友好协会代表团一行六人，在秘书长胡韦德率领下，已经在25日回到北京。团长刘长胜和副团长李烛尘还在国外。
    到机场迎接的有中苏友协总会副秘书长戈宝权、李沾吾等人。苏联驻华大使馆参赞苏达利柯夫、一等秘书桑飞楼也到机场迎接。
    代表团除在莫斯科参加了各项庆祝活动外，并访问了巴库、苏姆盖特等地，同苏联人民进行了广泛的接触。
