Title: Past: Twelve People One Hour to Load a Car Now: One Person Ten Minutes to Load a Car
Author:
Date: 1960-02-19
Page: 2

Xinhua News Agency Report: Tangshan Steel Plant transportation section workers, based on the principle of "high platform, low cargo position," successfully created an "automatic landslide high cargo position" loading method using indigenous methods. Using this loading method to load steel, filling a thirty-ton capacity train car only requires one person for ten minutes; while in the past, manual loading required twelve people for one hour, and loading with a crane also required six or seven people for half an hour.

The equipment used for the "automatic landslide high cargo position" loading method is simple. It only requires making an "automatic landslide" steel frame. First, use a crane to place the steel on it. When the train arrives, release the clip on the steel frame that holds the steel, and the steel will slide down the slope and automatically load onto the car.

### 过去：十二个人一小时装一车  现在：一个人十分钟装一车

1960-02-19
第2版()
专栏：

    过去：十二个人一小时装一车  现在：一个人十分钟装一车
    据新华社讯　唐山钢厂运输科职工根据“高站台、低货位”的原理，用土办法创造成功“自动滑坡高货位”装车法。用这种装车法装钢材，装满一个载重三十吨的车皮，只需一个人装十分钟；而过去用人力装需要十二个人装一小时，用吊车装也要六、七个人装半小时。
    “自动滑坡高货位”装车法所用的设备简单，只需要作一个“自动滑坡”钢架，先用吊车把钢材放在上面，当火车开到以后，把钢架上挡住钢材的卡子扳开，钢材便沿着滑坡滑下去自动装在车上。
