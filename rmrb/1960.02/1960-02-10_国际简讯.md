International Briefs

    Thai King Visits Indonesia
    Jakarta dispatch: The King and Queen of Thailand arrived here by plane on the afternoon of February 8th. They will be on a week-long visit to Indonesia.
    President Sukarno, cabinet ministers and foreign diplomatic envoys to Indonesia greeted them at the airport.

    Jordanian King Visits Saudi Arabia
    Damascus dispatch: According to Amman Radio, King Hussein of Jordan, accompanied by Prime Minister Majali, left Amman by plane on the morning of February 8th for Riyadh. They will be on a two-day visit to Saudi Arabia. Majali announced in Amman on the 7th that King Hussein's visit to the Saudi King was for the purpose of "exchanging views on recent Arab issues."

### 国际简讯

1960-02-10
第6版()
专栏：国际简讯

    国际简讯
    泰国王访问印度尼西亚
    雅加达讯　泰国国王和王后8日下午乘飞机到达这里。他们将在印度尼西亚作为时一周的访问。
    到机场欢迎他们的有苏加诺总统、内阁部长和各国驻印度尼西亚的外交使节。
    约旦国王访问沙特阿拉伯
    大马士革讯　据安曼电台报道，约旦国王侯赛因在马贾利首相陪同下，8日上午乘飞机离开安曼前往利雅得。他们将在沙特阿拉伯作为期两天的访问。马贾利7日在安曼宣布，侯赛因国王拜访沙特国王的目的在于“交换有关最近的阿拉伯问题的看法”。
