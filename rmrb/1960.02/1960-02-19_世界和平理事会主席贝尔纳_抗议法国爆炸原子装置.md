Title: World Peace Council President Bernal Protests French Explosion of Atomic Device
Author:
Date: 1960-02-19
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, London, 17th. Professor Bernal, Executive Chairman of the Presidential Committee of the World Peace Council, issued a statement today protesting France's atomic tests in the Sahara and demanding that the French government cease such tests in the future.

In the statement, Bernal emphasized that "the people of Africa will not easily forgive this crime committed on their land in disregard of their wishes."

The statement said, "The French government's action has been universally condemned. Therefore, the World Peace Council now most earnestly urges the French government to refrain from further atomic bomb tests."

### 世界和平理事会主席贝尔纳  抗议法国爆炸原子装置

1960-02-19
第6版()
专栏：

    世界和平理事会主席贝尔纳
    抗议法国爆炸原子装置
    新华社伦敦17日电　世界和平理事会主席团执行主席贝尔纳教授今天发表声明，抗议法国在撒哈拉进行原子试验，并且要求法国政府今后不再进行这种试验。
    贝尔纳在声明中强调说，“非洲人民决不会轻易放过无视他们的愿望而在他们土地上进行的这一罪行。”
    声明说，“法国政府这一行动受到普遍的谴责。因此，世界和平理事会现在极其恳切地敦促法国政府不要再进行原子弹试验。”
