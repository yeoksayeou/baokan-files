Title: NPC Standing Committee Holds Enlarged Meeting; Liu Aifeng Reports on Educational Construction Achievements in 1959
Author:
Date: 1960-02-23
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, February 22nd. The Standing Committee of the National People's Congress held its twentieth meeting (enlarged) on the afternoon of February 22nd to hear a report by Liu Aifeng, Vice Minister of Education, on the achievements of educational construction in 1959 and the tasks of educational work in 1960. Members of the Standing Committee of the National Committee of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference attended the meeting as observers.

### 人大常委会举行扩大会议  刘皑风报告1959年教育建设成就

1960-02-23
第1版()
专栏：

    人大常委会举行扩大会议
    刘皑风报告1959年教育建设成就
    新华社22日讯　全国人民代表大会常务委员会在22日下午举行第二十次会议扩大会议，听取了教育部副部长刘皑风所作的关于1959年教育建设成就和1960年教育工作任务的报告。政协全国委员会常务委员列席了这次会议。
