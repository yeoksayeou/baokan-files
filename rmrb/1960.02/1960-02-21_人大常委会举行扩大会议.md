Title: NPC Standing Committee Holds Enlarged Meeting
Author: 
Date: 1960-02-21
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, 20th: The Standing Committee of the National People's Congress held its 19th enlarged meeting this afternoon, and listened to the report by Wang Lei, Vice Minister of Commerce, on the commercial work in 1959 and the tasks for 1960. Members of the Standing Committee of the National Committee of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference attended the meeting as observers.

### 人大常委会举行扩大会议

1960-02-21
第1版()
专栏：

    人大常委会举行扩大会议
    新华社20日讯　全国人民代表大会常务委员会今天下午举行第十九次会议扩大会议，听取商业部副部长王磊所作的关于1959年商业工作情况和1960年任务的报告。政协全国委员会常务委员列席了这次会议。
