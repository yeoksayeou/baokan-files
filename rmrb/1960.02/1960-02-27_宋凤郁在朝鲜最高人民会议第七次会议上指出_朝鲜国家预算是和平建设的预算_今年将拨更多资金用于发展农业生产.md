Title: Song Bong-uk Points Out at the Seventh Session of the Supreme People's Assembly of Korea That the Korean National Budget is a Budget for Peaceful Construction, and More Funds Will Be Allocated This Year for Developing Agricultural Production
Author:
Date: 1960-02-27
Page: 6

　　Xinhua News Agency, Pyongyang, February 25th: The Seventh Session of the Second Supreme People's Assembly of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea opened this morning at the National Art Theater in Pyongyang.

　　At this morning's meeting, the three items on the agenda for this session were adopted: 1. On the settlement of the 1958 national budget and the 1960 national budget of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea; 2. On promoting people's health; 3. Approval of the ordinances of the Standing Committee of the Supreme People's Assembly.

　　Subsequently, Song Bong-uk, Minister of Finance of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea, delivered a report on the first item on the agenda.

　　Those present at today's meeting included: Kim Il-sung, Premier of the Cabinet of Korea, Choe Yong-gon, Chairman of the Standing Committee of the Supreme People's Assembly, as well as Kim Il, Hong Myong-hui, Park Jeong-ae, Park Kum-chul, Kim Chang-man, Chung Il-ryong, Nam Il, Chung Joon-taek, Lee Joo-yeon, Lee Jong-ok, and other leaders of the Korean Workers' Party and the Republic's government.

　　A total of 202 deputies attended the meeting.

　　The meeting will be held for three consecutive days.

　　Xinhua News Agency, Pyongyang, February 25th: Song Bong-uk, Minister of Finance of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea and Deputy to the Supreme People's Assembly, delivered a report this morning at the Seventh Session of the Second Supreme People's Assembly of Korea on the 1958 national budget settlement and the draft 1960 national budget.

　　The report stated: The 1960 national budget is expected to have an income of 1,967,522,000 won (Korean currency) and an expenditure of 1,900,840,000 won, with income exceeding expenditure by 67,338,000 won.

　　The report said that the total expenditure of the 1960 national budget increased by 15.1% compared to 1959. Among them, expenditure on the national economy and social culture accounted for an overwhelming proportion, accounting for 87.9% of the total expenditure. In particular, expenditure on social culture has increased significantly. Expenditure on national defense has decreased from 4.8% of the total expenditure in 1958 to 3.2% this year. The report stated that this clearly shows that the Republic's budget is a budget for peaceful construction.

　　Regarding the budget revenue, the report said that the total revenue of the 1960 national budget increased by 14.6% compared to 1959. Among them, revenue from the socialist economy accounted for 95.3% of the total revenue. Residents' tax revenue only accounted for 2.1% of the total revenue.

　　The report stated that the scale and composition of this year's national budget revenue correctly reflect the superiority and strength of the socialist system based on the material and technical foundations of the rapidly developing national economy.

　　The report said that the central task this year is to alleviate the tensions created in some economic sectors during the implementation of the 1959 plan, strengthen weak links, and further improve people's lives.

　　The report said that the 1960 national budget will allocate more funds to promote agricultural mechanization, expand agricultural production, and strengthen rural construction. In addition, it is necessary to build housing and cultural welfare facilities, and develop animal husbandry, fisheries, chemical fiber industry, and people's consumer goods production. In order to expand the source of foreign exchange, more funds than in previous years should also be invested in the mining sector and enterprises producing export commodities.

　　The report said that the huge funds required by the state this year will be mainly solved by tapping potential, improving labor productivity and equipment utilization rate, and rapidly developing production. The report also emphasized that all economic sectors should strengthen economic accounting system, establish a strict saving system, and strengthen saving.

　　Referring to the 1958 national budget settlement and the 1959 preliminary settlement, the report said that as a result of the implementation of the 1958 budget, the income was 1,529,140,000 won, the expenditure was 1,322,141,400 won, and the surplus was 207,726,000 won. The preliminary settlement for 1959 was that the actual income was 100.1% of the income stipulated in the budget, the actual expenditure was 100.5% of the expenditure stipulated in the budget, and the income exceeded the expenditure by 65,987,000 won. The national budgets for both years have been effectively implemented on the basis of the rapid development of the national economy, further strengthening the country's finances.

### 宋凤郁在朝鲜最高人民会议第七次会议上指出  朝鲜国家预算是和平建设的预算  今年将拨更多资金用于发展农业生产

1960-02-27
第6版()
专栏：

    宋凤郁在朝鲜最高人民会议第七次会议上指出
    朝鲜国家预算是和平建设的预算
    今年将拨更多资金用于发展农业生产
    新华社平壤25日电　朝鲜民主主义人民共和国第二届最高人民会议第七次会议今天上午在平壤市国立艺术剧场开幕。
    在今天上午的会议上通过了这次会议的三项议程：一、关于朝鲜民主主义人民共和国1958年度国家预算的决算和1960年度的国家预算；二、关于促进人民保健事业；三、批准最高人民会议常任委员会的政令。
    随后，朝鲜民主主义人民共和国财政相宋凤郁就第一项议程作了报告。
    出席今天的会议的有：朝鲜内阁首相金日成，最高人民会议常任委员会委员长崔庸健以及金一、洪命熹、朴正爱、朴金喆、金昌满、郑一龙、南日、郑准泽、李周渊、李钟玉等朝鲜劳动党和共和国政府的领导人。
    出席会议的议员共有二百零二名。
    会议将连续举行三天。
    新华社平壤25日电　朝鲜民主主义人民共和国财政相、最高人民会议议员宋凤郁今天上午在朝鲜第二届最高人民会议第七次会议上作了关于1958年国家决算和1960年国家预算草案的报告。
    报告说：1960年国家预算预计收入十九亿六千七百五十二万两千元（朝鲜币），支出十九亿十八万四千元，收入超过支出六千七百三十三万八千元。
    报告说，1960年国家预算支出总额比1959年增长了15．1％。其中对国民经济和社会文化方面的支出占压倒的比重，共占支出总额的87．9％。特别是社会文化方面的支出更有显著的增加。对民族保卫费（国防费）的支出则由1958年支出总额的4．8％，减少到今年的3．2％。报告说，这清楚地说明了共和国的预算是和平建设的预算。
    报告谈到预算的收入时说，1960年国家预算收入总额比1959年增长了14．6％。其中来自社会主义经济方面的收入占收入总额的95．3％。居民税收在收入总额中只占2．1％。
    报告说，今年国家预算收入的规模和它的构成正确地反映了立足在飞跃发展的国民经济的物质技术基础上的社会主义制度的优越和强大。
    报告说，今年的中心任务是缓和在执行1959年计划的过程中在一部分经济部门中造成的紧张，加强薄弱环节，进一步改进人民生活。
    报告说，1960年的国家预算要把更多的资金用于促进农业机械化、扩大农业生产、加强农村建设。另外，要建设住宅和文化福利设施，发展畜牧业、水产业、化学纤维工业和人民消费品生产。为了扩大外汇的来源，还要对矿业部门和生产出口商品的企业投下比往年更多的资金。
    报告说，今年国家所需的庞大资金，主要在挖掘潜力、提高劳动生产率和设备利用率以迅速发展生产的基础上加以解决。报告还强调，一切经济部门要加强经济核算制，建立严格的节约制度，加强节约。
    报告谈到1958年国家决算和1959年的预备决算时说，1958年预算执行结果，收入十五亿两千九百十四万元，支出十三亿两千一百四十一万四千元，结余二亿七百七十二万六千元。1959年的预备决算为，实际收入为预算规定的收入的100．1％，实际支出为预算规定的支出的100．5％，收入超过支出六千五百九十八万七千元。这两年的国家预算都在国民经济飞跃发展的基础上有成效地执行了，进一步加强了国家的财政。
