International Briefs

    120,000 Construction Workers in Argentina Strike
    Buenos Aires Dispatch: 120,000 construction workers in the Argentine capital held a 24-hour strike on the 10th, demanding the release of Rubén Iscaro, general secretary of the arrested construction workers' union, and other detainees, and demanding wage increases. Due to the strike, major projects in the city center have come to a standstill.

    Moroccan Prime Minister Leaves Cairo for Home
    Cairo Dispatch: After leading the Moroccan delegation to participate in the opening meeting of the Arab League Council, Moroccan Prime Minister and Foreign Minister Abdallah Ibrahim left here on the 11th to return home.

### 国际简讯

专栏：国际简讯

    国际简讯
    阿根廷十二万建筑工人罢工
    布宜诺斯艾利斯讯　阿根廷首都十二万建筑工人10日举行二十四小时罢工，要求释放被捕的建筑工人工会总书记鲁宾斯·伊斯卡罗及其他被捕者，并要求增加工资。由于罢工，城市中心各大工程陷于停顿。
    摩首相离开罗回国
    开罗讯　摩洛哥首相兼外交大臣阿卜杜拉·易卜拉欣在率领摩洛哥代表团参加阿拉伯联盟理事会开幕会议后，11日离开这里回国。
