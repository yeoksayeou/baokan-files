Title: Land Reform in the Sierra Maestra, Revolutionary Base of Cuba, Nears Completion
Author:
Date: 1960-02-29
Page: 6
Xinhua News Agency, Havana, February 28th: According to reports from here today, the National Land Reform Committee of Cuba has taken over all the large estates in the Sierra Maestra region. Eight hundred farmers will receive land certificates in March, and another six hundred will receive land certificates in April. The work of requisitioning 2,750 caballerias of land (one caballeria equals 201.45 Chinese mu) from three large estates in this region will be completed in the near future. The Sierra Maestra was the base of the rebel army led by Castro in the armed struggle before the victory of the Cuban Revolution.

### 古巴革命根据地马埃斯特腊山区  土地改革工作即将完成

1960-02-29
第6版()
专栏：

    古巴革命根据地马埃斯特腊山区
    土地改革工作即将完成
    据新华社哈瓦那28日电　据今天这里报道，古巴土地改革全国委员会已经接管了马埃斯特腊山地区所有的大庄园。3月份将有八百个农民领到土地证，4月份又将有六百人领到土地证。征用这个地区三个大庄园的二千七百五十卡瓦耶里亚土地（一卡瓦耶里亚等于二百零一点四五中国亩）的工作将在短期内完成。马埃斯特腊山区是古巴革命胜利前卡斯特罗领导的起义军武装斗争的根据地。
