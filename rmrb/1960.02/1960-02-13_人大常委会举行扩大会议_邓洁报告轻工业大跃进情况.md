Title: NPC Standing Committee Holds Enlarged Meeting; Deng Jie Reports on the Great Leap Forward in Light Industry
Author:
Date: 1960-02-13
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, 12th -- The Standing Committee of the National People's Congress held its 15th meeting (enlarged) on the morning of the 12th to hear a report by Deng Jie, Vice Minister of Light Industry, on the Great Leap Forward in Light Industry and current tasks.

The members of the Standing Committee of the National Committee of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference attended the meeting as observers.

### 人大常委会举行扩大会议  邓洁报告轻工业大跃进情况

1960-02-13
第1版()
专栏：

    人大常委会举行扩大会议
    邓洁报告轻工业大跃进情况
    新华社12日讯　全国人民代表大会常务委员会在12日上午举行第十五次会议扩大会议，听取了轻工业部副部长邓洁所作的关于轻工业大跃进的情况和当前任务的报告。
    政协全国委员会的常务委员，列席了这次会议。
