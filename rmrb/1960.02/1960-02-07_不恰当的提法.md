Author: Tang Shengzhi
Date: 1960-02-07
Page: 8

Column: Readers, Authors, Editors

    Inappropriate Formulation
    In the short article "Marching Towards 'High Precision and Cutting Edge'" written by Comrade Wang Pu on the second page of the January 28th edition, it mentions "Our ultimate goal is to build a socialist strong country with modern industry, modern agriculture, and modern science and culture." I believe it is inappropriate to describe our ultimate goal as building a socialist strong country. We are communists, and building a socialist strong country with modern industry, modern agriculture, and modern science and culture is our current task; our ultimate goal is to realize the highest ideal of mankind – communism.
    Nanjing Power College, Tang Shengzhi

### 不恰当的提法
唐省智
1960-02-07
第8版()
专栏：读者、作者、编者

    不恰当的提法
    1月28日二版王朴同志写的“向‘高精尖’进军”的短文中，谈到“我们最后的目标是建设一个具有现代工业、现代农业、现代科学文化的社会主义强国”。把我们的最后奋斗目标说成是建设社会主义强国，我认为是不恰当的。我们是共产主义者，建设具有现代工业、现代农业、现代科学文化的社会主义强国，是我们目前的任务；我们的最后目标是实现人类的最高理想——共产主义。
    南京动力专科学校　唐省智
