Title: Remembering the Friendship of Battle: Soviet Union Publishes New Book Documenting the Deeds of Chinese Soldiers in Defending the Soviet Regime
Author:
Date: 1960-02-09
Page: 5

    Remembering the Friendship of Battle
    Soviet Union Publishes New Book Documenting the Deeds of Chinese Soldiers in Defending the Soviet Regime
    The Soviet Military Publishing House recently published a book called "Chinese Comrades-in-Arms."

    This book meticulously collects the heroic deeds of Chinese soldiers who participated in the Soviet Civil War, fighting shoulder to shoulder with the Soviet Red Army in southern Russia, the Don region, and Astrakhan.

    The authors, Novogrudsky and Dunayevsky, traveled throughout the aforementioned regions to write this book, collecting materials about Chinese soldiers from local residents and archival documents.

    The book also includes many photographs of Chinese soldiers. (Xinhua News Agency)

### 回忆战斗的友谊  苏联出版新书，记录中国战士保卫苏维埃政权的事迹

1960-02-09
第5版()
专栏：友谊之窗

    回忆战斗的友谊
    苏联出版新书，记录中国战士保卫苏维埃政权的事迹
    苏联军事出版局最近出版了一本名叫“中国战士同志”的书。
    这本书详尽地收集了参加苏联国内战争的中国战士在俄罗斯南部、顿河地区和阿斯特拉罕同苏联红军并肩作战的英勇事迹。
    作者诺沃格鲁茨基和杜纳耶夫斯基为写这本书走遍了上述地区，向当地居民和在档案文件中收集有关中国战士的材料。
    这本书还附有许多有关中国战士的照片。　（新华社电）
