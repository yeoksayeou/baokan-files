Title: Khrushchev Arrives in Surabaya
Author:
Date: 1960-02-24
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, February 22nd: According to TASS, Chairman of the Council of Ministers of the Soviet Union, Khrushchev, and his entourage arrived in Surabaya, the capital of East Java, this afternoon by plane from Yogyakarta in Central Java.

Khrushchev was warmly welcomed at the airport. The citizens of Surabaya held a grand mass rally at the city center stadium to welcome the distinguished Soviet guests.

After leaving Bandung on the 21st, Khrushchev and his entourage visited Yogyakarta for two days.

### 赫鲁晓夫到达泗水

1960-02-24
第5版()
专栏：

    赫鲁晓夫到达泗水
    新华社22日讯　据塔斯社报道：苏联部长会议主席赫鲁晓夫和他的随行人员今天下午乘飞机从中爪哇的日惹到达东爪哇首府泗水。
    赫鲁晓夫在机场上受到了热烈欢迎。泗水市民在市中心体育场举行盛大群众集会欢迎苏联贵宾。
    赫鲁晓夫和他的随行人员在21日离开万隆以后，到日惹进行了两天的参观访问。
