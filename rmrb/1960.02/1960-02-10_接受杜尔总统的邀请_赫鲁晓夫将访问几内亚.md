Title: Khrushchev to Visit Guinea After Accepting President Touré's Invitation
Author:
Date: 1960-02-10
Page: 5
Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, 9th - TASS reported from Moscow on the 9th: Soviet Premier Khrushchev has accepted the invitation from President Sékou Touré of the Republic of Guinea to pay an official visit to Guinea. The date of the visit will be determined through consultations between the diplomatic agencies of the two countries.

The Moscow newspapers today published Khrushchev's reply to President Sékou Touré regarding the visit to Guinea.

Khrushchev wrote: "I fully agree with your view on the importance of maintaining and developing personal contacts to strengthen mutual understanding and cooperation between our two countries."

Khrushchev pointed out that President Touré's recent visit to the Soviet Union greatly promoted the development of friendly relations between the Soviet Union and the Republic of Guinea. He expressed his belief that his meeting and talks with President Touré again will be even more beneficial and productive, and will undoubtedly bring the people of Guinea and the people of the Soviet Union closer together.

Khrushchev also said that the Soviet people would be happy to receive President Sékou Touré of the Republic of Guinea on another visit to the Soviet Union at any time convenient for him.

### 接受杜尔总统的邀请  赫鲁晓夫将访问几内亚

1960-02-10
第5版()
专栏：

    接受杜尔总统的邀请
    赫鲁晓夫将访问几内亚
    新华社9日讯　据塔斯社莫斯科9日讯：苏联部长会议主席赫鲁晓夫接受了几内亚共和国总统塞古·杜尔请他前去几内亚作正式访问的邀请。访问日期将由两国外交机构商议确定。
    莫斯科各报今天刊登了赫鲁晓夫给塞古·杜尔总统关于访问几内亚的复信。
    赫鲁晓夫写道：“我完全赞同您关于保持和发展个人之间的接触对于加强我们两国间的相互谅解和合作的重要性的看法”。
    赫鲁晓夫指出，杜尔总统不久前在苏联的访问大大地促进了苏联同几内亚共和国之间友好关系的发展。他表示相信，他同杜尔总统的再次会见和会谈将是更加有益和有成效的，并且无疑将使几内亚人民和苏联人民更加亲近起来。
    赫鲁晓夫还说，苏联人民将高兴地接待几内亚共和国总统塞古·杜尔在他任何合适的时候再次来苏联访问。
