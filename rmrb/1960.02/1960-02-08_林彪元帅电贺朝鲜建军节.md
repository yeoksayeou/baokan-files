Title: Marshal Lin Biao Sends Telegram to Congratulate North Korea on Army Day

Author:
Date: 1960-02-08
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, February 7th. On the occasion of the 12th anniversary of the founding of the Korean People's Army on February 8th, Marshal Lin Biao, Vice Premier of the State Council and Minister of National Defense, sent a telegram to General Kim Kwang-hyup, Minister of National Defense of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea, to express his congratulations. The telegram is as follows:

To Comrade General Kim Kwang-hyup, Minister of National Defense of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea:

On the occasion of the glorious 12th anniversary of the founding of the heroic Korean People's Army, I, on behalf of all commanders and fighters of the Chinese People's Liberation Army and in my own name, extend to you and all commanders and fighters of the Korean People's Army our heartfelt and warm congratulations.

Under the leadership and care of the Korean Workers' Party, the Korean People's Army has made tremendous achievements in various aspects of army building. Today, the heroic Korean People's Army is vigilantly defending the security of its motherland and, together with the armies of fraternal countries, is safeguarding world peace. I wish you and all commanders and fighters of the fraternal Korean People's Army new and brilliant achievements in military training, political education, and the struggle for the reunification of the motherland.

May the close, fraternal, and militant friendship between the peoples and armies of China and Korea last forever.

　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　Lin Biao, Vice Premier of the State Council and Minister of National Defense of the People's Republic of China
                                     February 6, 1960, in Beijing

### 林彪元帅电贺朝鲜建军节

1960-02-08
第1版()
专栏：

    林彪元帅电贺朝鲜建军节
    新华社7日讯　2月8日是朝鲜人民军建军十二周年纪念。国务院副总理兼国防部长林彪元帅特致电朝鲜民主主义人民共和国民族保卫相金光侠大将表示祝贺，电文如下：朝鲜民主主义人民共和国民族保卫相金光侠大将同志：
    值此英勇的朝鲜人民军建军十二周年光荣的节日，我谨代表中国人民解放军全体指战员并以我个人名义向您和朝鲜人民军全体指战员致以衷心的、热烈的祝贺。
    朝鲜人民军在朝鲜劳动党的领导和关怀下，在军队建设的各项工作中，已取得巨大的成就。今天，英雄的朝鲜人民军，正警惕地保卫着自己祖国的安全，并同各兄弟国家的军队一起保卫着世界和平。我祝您和兄弟的朝鲜人民军全体指战员，在军事训练、政治教育和争取统一祖国的斗争中，取得新的光辉的成就。
    愿中、朝两国人民和军队之间亲密的、兄弟般的战斗友谊万古常青。
                  中华人民共和国国务院副总理兼国防部长　林彪
                                     一九六○年二月六日于北京
