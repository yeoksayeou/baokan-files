Title: Cuban Government Note to the United States: Willing to Resume Negotiations Without Prejudice to Cuba's Interests
Author:
Date: 1960-02-25
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, Havana, February 23rd - Cuban Foreign Minister Raúl Roa delivered a note to the U.S. Chargé d'Affaires in Cuba yesterday afternoon. The note stated that the Cuban government has decided to appoint a committee to resume diplomatic negotiations on outstanding issues between the two countries. The note pointed out that the Cuban government wishes to clarify that the necessary conditions for resuming these negotiations are that the U.S. government or Congress not take unilateral measures that may harm the outcome of the talks or harm the Cuban economy and the Cuban people.

### 古巴政府照会美国  愿在无损古巴利益前提下恢复谈判

1960-02-25
第6版()
专栏：

    古巴政府照会美国
    愿在无损古巴利益前提下恢复谈判
    新华社哈瓦那23日电　古巴外交部长劳尔·罗亚昨天下午交给美国驻古巴外交代办一份照会。照会说，古巴政府已经决定任命一个委员会来恢复关于两国间悬而未决的问题的外交谈判。照会指出，古巴政府愿意阐明，恢复这些谈判必需的条件是美国政府或国会不采取可能损害会谈结果或可能损害古巴经济和古巴人民的单方面的措施。
