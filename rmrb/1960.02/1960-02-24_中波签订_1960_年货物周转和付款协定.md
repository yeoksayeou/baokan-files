Title: China and Poland Sign 1960 Goods Turnover and Payment Agreement
Author:
Date: 1960-02-24
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency, February 23rd. The agreement between the government of the People's Republic of China and the government of the People's Republic of Poland regarding the 1960 Goods Turnover and Payment, was signed in Beijing on the 22nd.

	Lin Haiyun, Vice Minister of Foreign Trade of the People's Republic of China, signed the agreement on behalf of the Chinese side; and B. Brackiewicz, Vice Minister of Foreign Trade of the People's Republic of Poland, signed the agreement on behalf of the Polish side.

	Attending the signing ceremony on the Chinese side were Lei Renmin, Vice Minister of Foreign Trade, Yu Zhan, Deputy Director of the Soviet Union and Eastern Europe Department of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs, and others. On the Polish side were Ftorato, Chargé d'Affaires ad interim of the Polish Embassy in China, and all members of the trade delegation of the Government of the People's Republic of Poland.

### 中波签订1960年货物周转和付款协定

1960-02-24
第4版()
专栏：

    中波签订1960年货物周转和付款协定
    新华社23日讯　中华人民共和国政府和波兰人民共和国政府关于1960年货物周转和付款协定，22日在北京签字。
    代表中国方面在协定上签字的，是中华人民共和国对外贸易部副部长林海云；代表波兰方面在协定上签字的，是波兰人民共和国对外贸易部副部长布腊凯维兹。
    参加签字仪式的，中国方面有对外贸易部副部长雷任民，外交部苏联东欧司副司长余湛等；波兰方面有波兰驻中国大使馆临时代办傅拉托以及波兰人民共和国政府贸易代表团全体人员。
