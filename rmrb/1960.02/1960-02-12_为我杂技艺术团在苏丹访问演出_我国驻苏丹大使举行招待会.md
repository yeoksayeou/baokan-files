Title: Chinese Ambassador to Sudan Holds Reception for Chinese Acrobatic Troupe's Visit to Sudan
Author: 
Date: 1960-02-12
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, Khartoum, February 11th. Chinese Ambassador to Sudan, Wang Yutian, held a reception here on the evening of the 10th for the Chinese Acrobatic Troupe, which is currently visiting Sudan.

Among the 300 guests attending the reception were Talat Farid, member of the Supreme Council of the Sudanese Armed Forces and Minister of Information and Labor; Hassan Beshir Nasr, member of the Supreme Council and Deputy Commander-in-Chief of the Armed Forces; Brigadier General Nasr Ousman, Deputy Chief of Staff of the Sudanese Armed Forces; diplomatic envoys from various countries to Sudan; prominent figures from all walks of life; and people who had visited China.

The Sudanese guests warmly praised the performances of the Chinese Acrobatic Troupe and its success. At the reception, the acrobatic troupe's orchestra played Chinese and Sudanese music, performed acrobatics and magic, and Chinese and Sudanese artists sang Chinese and Sudanese folk songs.

In the past four days, the Chinese Acrobatic Troupe has given three performances to approximately 7,000 spectators in Khartoum.

The acrobatic troupe also visited Khartoum University and the Technical College. They were warmly welcomed by the students everywhere they went. The acrobatic troupe departed this afternoon for Al-Ubayyid and Kosti in the west for a few days of performances.

### 为我杂技艺术团在苏丹访问演出  我国驻苏丹大使举行招待会

1960-02-12
第5版()
专栏：

    为我杂技艺术团在苏丹访问演出
    我国驻苏丹大使举行招待会
    新华社喀土穆11日电　中国驻苏丹大使王雨田10日晚上在这里为正在苏丹访问演出的中国杂技艺术团举行招待会。
    参加招待会的三百位客人中，有苏丹武装部队最高委员会委员、宣传和劳工部长塔拉特·法里德，最高委员会委员、武装部队副总司令哈桑·贝希尔·纳斯尔，苏丹武装部队副参谋长纳斯尔·欧斯曼准将，各国驻苏丹使节、各界著名人士和曾经访问过中国的人士。
    苏丹客人们热烈赞扬了中国杂技艺术团的表演和它所取得的成功。在招待会上，杂技艺术团的乐队演奏了中国和苏丹的音乐，表演了杂技和魔术，中国和苏丹的艺术家演唱了中国和苏丹的民歌。
    在过去的四天内，中国杂技艺术团在喀土穆又向七千名左右的观众作了三场演出。
    杂技艺术团在这里还参观了喀土穆大学和技术学院。他们到处受到学生们最热烈的欢迎。杂技艺术团今天下午已动身前往西部的阿卑德和科斯蒂作几天的访问演出。
