Title: Khrushchev Holds Talks with Sukarno
Author:
Date: 1960-02-25
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, February 24th - According to TASS: Soviet Chairman of the Council of Ministers Khrushchev and President Sukarno of the Republic of Indonesia held talks in Batang, the capital of Bali Island, this morning on issues of mutual interest. Soviet Foreign Minister Gromyko, Indonesian First Minister Juanda, and Foreign Minister Subandrio participated in the talks.
	Khrushchev and Sukarno arrived in Batang by plane from Surabaya on the afternoon of the 23rd.

### 赫鲁晓夫同苏加诺会谈

1960-02-25
第5版()
专栏：

    赫鲁晓夫同苏加诺会谈
    新华社24日讯　据塔斯社消息：苏联部长会议主席赫鲁晓夫同印度尼西亚共和国总统苏加诺今天上午在巴厘岛首府巴塘市就双方感到兴趣的问题举行会谈。苏联外交部长葛罗米柯，印度尼西亚共和国第一部长朱安达、外交部长苏班德里约参加了会谈。
    赫鲁晓夫和苏加诺是在23日中午乘飞机从泗水到达巴塘市的。
