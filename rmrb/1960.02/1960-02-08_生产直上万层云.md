Title: Production Soars Straight to the Myriad Clouds
Author: Xǔ Mínghú
Date: 1960-02-08
Page: 2

    Production Soars Straight to the Myriad Clouds
    Xu Minghu, Worker at Guiyang Yunyan Chemical Machinery Factory

　　Spring is warm, flowers bloom in the bright sunshine,
　　Flowers bloom all over the workshop,
　　Soaring drive plus drilling spirit,
　　High quality and high yield advance together.
　　The whole year's work depends on a good start in spring,
　　Create miracles and make innovations,
　　Indigenous and foreign methods combined, great strength,
　　Production soars straight to the myriad clouds.

### 生产直上万层云
许明湖
1960-02-08
第2版()
专栏：

    生产直上万层云
    贵阳云岩化工机械厂工人  许明湖春暖花开艳阳天，鲜花开遍满车间，冲天干劲加钻劲，优质高产齐向前。一年之计在于春，大创奇迹闹革新，土洋结合力量大，生产直上万层云。
