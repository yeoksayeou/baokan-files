Title: Danish Prime Minister Hansen Dies

Author: 
Date: 1960-02-21
Page: 3

Xinhua News Agency, Copenhagen, 20th - Danish Prime Minister H. C. Hansen died of cancer last night.

Hansen was a Social Democrat. He had been Prime Minister since 1955.

The King of Denmark has appointed Finance Minister Viggo Kampmann, who served as acting Prime Minister during Hansen's illness, to continue as acting Prime Minister until a successor is determined.

### 丹麦首相汉森病逝

1960-02-21
第3版()
专栏：

    丹麦首相汉森病逝
    新华社20日讯　哥本哈根消息：丹麦首相贺·西·汉森因患癌症在昨晚逝世。
    汉森是社会民主党人。他从1955年起担任首相。
    丹麦国王已经指定在汉森生病期间代理首相的财政大臣维戈·坎普曼，继续代理首相职务，直到首相继任人选确定为止。
