Title: Soviet Scientist Blagonravov Says the Soviet Union Will Launch a Batch of Artificial Satellites, Some of Which Will Be Equipped with Plants and Higher Organisms
Author:
Date: 1960-02-21
Page: 3

Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, 20th. TASS reports: Soviet scientist Blagonravov said on the 17th that the Soviet Union would launch a series of artificial Earth satellites, some of which will also carry plants and higher organisms.

This was said when he spoke to a reporter from the Soviet magazine "New Times" about the Soviet Union's planned satellite and rocket launches. He said that the Soviet Union would conduct a series of experiments on rockets flying to planets in the solar system, primarily to Mars and Venus.

Speaking about the Soviet Union's recent launch of a powerful multi-stage rocket into the Pacific region, he said that the accuracy of the rocket confirmed the correctness of prior calculations. This is undoubtedly one of the important achievements. Thus, in completing the complex task of launching rockets not only to the vicinity of the moon but also to planets in the solar system, great reliability can be obtained. Blagonravov said that the thrust of Soviet rocket engines is already sufficient to allow rockets to reach any place on Earth and to launch huge and complex automatic stations into outer space. The possibility of launching huge payloads into outer space will enable the implementation of extensive and complex scientific research programs on the universe.

### 苏联科学家布拉冈拉沃夫说  苏联将发射一批人造卫星  其中一些卫星将装有植物和高级生物

1960-02-21
第3版()
专栏：

    苏联科学家布拉冈拉沃夫说
    苏联将发射一批人造卫星
    其中一些卫星将装有植物和高级生物
    新华社莫斯科20日电　据塔斯社报道：苏联著名科学家布拉冈拉沃夫17日说，苏联将要发射一系列人造地球卫星，其中一些卫星还将装着植物和高级生物。
    这是他就苏联将要发射卫星和火箭的问题对苏联“新时代”杂志的记者发表谈话时说的。他说，苏联将对飞往太阳系行星首先是飞往火星和金星的火箭，进行一系列的试验。
    他在谈到苏联不久前向太平洋地区发射威力强大的多级火箭时说，火箭的准确性证实了事先计算的正确。这无疑是重要成就之一。这样，在完成不但向月球附近空间，而且向太阳系行星发射火箭的复杂任务时，将能得到很大的可靠性。布拉冈拉沃夫说，苏联火箭发动机的推力，已经可以使火箭到达地球上的任何地方，并且可以向宇宙空间发射巨大而复杂的自动站。向宇宙空间发射巨大有效载重量的可能性，将使对宇宙进行广泛的复杂的科学研究计划得到实现。
