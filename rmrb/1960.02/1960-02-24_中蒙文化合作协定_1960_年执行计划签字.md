Title: Sino-Mongolian Cultural Cooperation Agreement 1960 Implementation Plan Signed
Author:
Date: 1960-02-24
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency, February 23rd: The 1960 implementation plan of the Cultural Cooperation Agreement between the Government of the People's Republic of China and the Government of the Mongolian People's Republic was signed in Beijing today. Zhang Zhixiang, head of the Chinese cultural delegation and Deputy Director of the Foreign Cultural Liaison Committee, signed on behalf of the Chinese side, and Sharrab, head of the Mongolian cultural delegation and Ambassador of the Mongolian People's Republic to China, signed on behalf of the Mongolian side.
    This implementation plan includes projects in science, education, culture, art, film, news, broadcasting, health, sports, and exhibitions. Zhang Zhixiang and Sharrab spoke after the signing, warmly praising the increasing enhancement and development of Sino-Mongolian cultural exchange.

### 中蒙文化合作协定1960年执行计划签字

1960-02-24
第4版()
专栏：

    中蒙文化合作协定1960年执行计划签字
    新华社23日讯　中华人民共和国政府和蒙古人民共和国政府文化合作协定1960年执行计划，今天在北京签字。代表中国方面签字的是中国文化代表团团长、对外文化联络委员会副主任张致祥，代表蒙古方面签字的是蒙古文化代表团团长、蒙古人民共和国驻中国大使沙拉布。
    这个执行计划包括科学、教育、文化、艺术、电影、新闻、广播、卫生、体育、展览等项目。张致祥和沙拉布在签字以后讲了话，他们热烈赞扬中蒙文化交流的日益增强和发展。
