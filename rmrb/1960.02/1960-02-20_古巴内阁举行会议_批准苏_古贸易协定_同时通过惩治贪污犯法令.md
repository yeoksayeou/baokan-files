Title: Cuban Cabinet Holds Meeting Approving Soviet-Cuban Trade Agreement While Passing Law Punishing Corrupt Officials
Author:
Date: 1960-02-20
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, Havana, 18th - The Cuban cabinet held a meeting yesterday and approved the Soviet-Cuban trade, payment, and loan agreement.

At this meeting, the cabinet also passed a law punishing corrupt officials. This law stipulates that state officials who manage public property and engage in corruption or collude with other corruptors will be sentenced to ten to thirty years in prison or to death.

### 古巴内阁举行会议  批准苏——古贸易协定  同时通过惩治贪污犯法令

1960-02-20
第6版()
专栏：

    古巴内阁举行会议
    批准苏——古贸易协定
    同时通过惩治贪污犯法令
    新华社哈瓦那18日电　古巴内阁会议昨天批准苏联—古巴贸易、支付和贷款协定。
    在这次会议上，内阁还通过一项惩治贪污犯的法令。这条法令规定，管理公共财产而进行贪污或者与其他贪污者勾结的国家官员将被判处十年到三十年的徒刑或死刑。
