Title: Sino-Nepalese Friendship Association Holds Reception Celebrating Kingdom of Nepal National Day
Author:
Date: 1960-02-18
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, February 17th—The Sino-Nepalese Friendship Association held a reception in Beijing this afternoon to celebrate the National Day of the Kingdom of Nepal on February 18th.

Nepalese students in Beijing were invited to attend the reception.

Zhou Shujia, Vice President of the Sino-Nepalese Friendship Association, delivered a speech at the reception, congratulating Nepal on the achievements it has made in safeguarding national interests and building the national economy, and praising the foreign policy of peaceful coexistence pursued by the Nepalese government. He said that because both China and Nepal share a common desire to live in friendship, our two countries have witnessed increasingly frequent friendly exchanges and cultural exchanges in recent years, thus further deepening the mutual understanding and traditional friendly relations between the two peoples. The friendship between the Chinese and Nepalese people will surely be continuously strengthened and developed on the existing basis.

Sharma, a representative of the Nepalese students, delivered a speech at the reception. While recalling the traditional friendship and friendly relations between China and Nepal, he said that the Himalayas link our two countries together, and the friendship between our two peoples is as solid as the Himalayas.

Among those present at the reception were Zhang Shijie, Deputy Director of the First Asian Department of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs, Ju Zan, Vice President of the Buddhist Association of China, directors of the Sino-Nepalese Friendship Association, and people who have visited Nepal.

### 中尼友好协会举行酒会  庆祝尼泊尔王国国庆节

1960-02-18
第4版()
专栏：

    中尼友好协会举行酒会
    庆祝尼泊尔王国国庆节
    新华社17日讯中国尼泊尔友好协会今天下午在北京举行酒会，庆祝2月18日尼泊尔王国国庆节。
    在北京的尼泊尔学生应邀出席了酒会。
    中尼友好协会副会长周叔迦在酒会上致词，祝贺尼泊尔在维护民族利益和国民经济建设中所取得的成就，并且赞扬尼泊尔政府执行的和平共处的外交政策。他说，由于中国和尼泊尔两国都具有友好相处的共同愿望，近年来我们两国的友好往来和文化交流日益频繁，因而进一步地加深了两国人民的相互了解和传统的友好关系。中国和尼泊尔人民的友谊在现有基础上必将不断加强和发展。
    尼泊尔学生代表沙尔玛在酒会上致词，他在追述尼中两国的传统友谊和友好关系时说，喜马拉雅山把我们两国联系在一起，我们两国人民的友谊就像喜马拉雅山一样巩固。
    出席酒会的，有外交部第一亚洲司副司长张世杰，中国佛教协会副会长巨赞，中尼友好协会理事和曾经访问过尼泊尔的人士等。
