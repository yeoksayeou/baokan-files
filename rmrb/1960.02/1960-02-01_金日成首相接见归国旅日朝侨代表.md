Title: Premier Kim Il-sung Receives Representatives of Returning Korean Residents in Japan
Author:
Date: 1960-02-01
Page: 6
Xinhua News Agency, Pyongyang, January 31st: According to Pyongyang newspapers, Premier Kim Il-sung received 28 representatives of the first five groups of returning Korean residents in Japan and personnel who had worked in the General Association of Korean Residents in Japan and its affiliated organizations on the 30th.

Premier Kim Il-sung praised the achievements of the General Association of Korean Residents in Japan in uniting Korean residents in Japan and ensuring their return, and encouraged them to study hard.

According to the Korean Central News Agency, Chongjin, the sixth group of 998 Korean residents in Japan, who returned from Japan aboard two ships, arrived at Chongjin Port at noon today and received a warm welcome from people from all walks of life.

### 金日成首相接见归国旅日朝侨代表

1960-02-01
第6版()
专栏：

    金日成首相接见归国旅日朝侨代表
    新华社平壤31日电　据平壤报纸报道，朝鲜内阁首相金日成在30日接见了前五批归国的旅日朝侨代表和曾在旅日朝鲜人总联合会及其所属团体中工作的人员二十八人。
    金日成首相表扬了旅日朝鲜人总联合会在团结旅日朝侨、保证旅日朝侨归国方面的工作成就，并且勉励他们好好学习。
    另据朝鲜中央通讯社清津讯，由日本归国的第六批旅日朝侨九百九十八名分乘两艘轮船在今天中午到达朝鲜清津港，受到了各界人民的热烈欢迎。
