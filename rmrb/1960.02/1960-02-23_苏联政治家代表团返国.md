Title: Soviet Political Delegation Returns Home
Author:
Date: 1960-02-23
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, February 21st: The Soviet political delegation led by Dmitry Polyansky, Chairman of the Council of Ministers of the Russian Soviet Federative Socialist Republic, returned to Moscow on the evening of the 21st aboard a Tu-114 aircraft after a 24-day visit to the United States.

### 苏联政治家代表团返国

1960-02-23
第6版()
专栏：

    苏联政治家代表团返国
    新华社莫斯科电　由苏联俄罗斯联邦部长会议主席波利扬斯基率领的苏联政治家代表团，在美国进行了二十四天的访问以后，21日晚乘“图—114式”飞机返抵莫斯科。
