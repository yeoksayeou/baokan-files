Title: Chinese Youth Delegation to Chile
Author: 
Date: 1960-02-18
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency, 17th. A Chinese youth delegation of three, led by Zhang Chao, Standing Committee Member of the Central Committee of the Chinese Communist Youth League and Vice-Chairman of the All-China Youth Federation, left Beijing by plane on the 16th for Chile. They are visiting at the invitation of the Chilean Communist Youth League to attend the Fourth Extraordinary Congress of the Chilean Communist Youth League.

### 中国青年代表团去智利

1960-02-18
第4版()
专栏：

    中国青年代表团去智利
    新华社17日讯　由中国共产主义青年团中央常务委员、中华全国青年联合会副主席张超率领的中国青年代表团一行三人，16日乘飞机离开北京去智利。他们是应智利共产主义青年联盟邀请前往参加智利共产主义青年联盟第四届非常代表大会的。
