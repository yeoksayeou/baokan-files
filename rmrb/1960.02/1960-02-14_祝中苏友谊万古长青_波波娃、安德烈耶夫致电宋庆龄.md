Title: May Sino-Soviet Friendship Last Forever! Popova and Andreyev Send Telegram to Soong Ching Ling
Author:
Date: 1960-02-14
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, February 13th: Soong Ching Ling, President of the Sino-Soviet Friendship Association, received a telegram today from N. Popova, Chairman of the Presidium of the Union of Soviet Societies for Friendship and Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries, and A. Andreyev, Chairman of the Council of the Soviet-Chinese Friendship Association, warmly congratulating the 10th anniversary of the signing of the Sino-Soviet Treaty of Friendship, Alliance and Mutual Assistance. The text of the telegram is as follows: To Comrade Soong Ching Ling, President of the Sino-Soviet Friendship Association:

Dear Comrade Soong Ching Ling,

The Presidium of the Union of Soviet Societies for Friendship and Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries and the Council of the Soviet-Chinese Friendship Association extend to you, and through you, to all the Chinese people, warm congratulations on the 10th Anniversary of the Sino-Soviet Treaty of Friendship, Alliance and Mutual Assistance. The Sino-Soviet Treaty has forged an eternal fraternal relationship between the peoples of our two countries, and it is an important factor in strengthening the unity of the great family of socialist countries and in strengthening universal peace and international security. On this great anniversary, we Soviet people are elated by the great achievements made by our dear Chinese friends in socialist construction, and by the victories achieved by the great Chinese people under the leadership of the glorious Chinese Communist Party and the Party Central Committee headed by Comrade Mao Zedong.

The Sino-Soviet Friendship Association, which you lead, plays a huge and fruitful role in strengthening and developing the friendship and cooperation between the peoples of our two countries. The work of this association has won the deep love and respect of the Soviet people, because no task is more glorious or sublime than consolidating the friendship between the peoples of our two countries. We sincerely wish the Chinese people new and greater achievements in the socialist construction of your country.

Long live the eternal and unbreakable friendship between the Soviet and Chinese people! Long live world peace!

N. Popova, Chairman of the Presidium of the Union of Soviet Societies for Friendship and Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries
A. Andreyev, Chairman of the Council of the Soviet-Chinese Friendship Association
Moscow, February 13, 1960

### 祝中苏友谊万古长青  波波娃、安德烈耶夫致电宋庆龄

1960-02-14
第1版()
专栏：

    祝中苏友谊万古长青
    波波娃、安德烈耶夫致电宋庆龄
    新华社13日讯　中苏友好协会总会会长宋庆龄今日收到苏联对外友好和文化协会联合会主席团主席波波娃、苏中友好协会理事会主席安德烈耶夫的电报，热烈祝贺中苏友好同盟互助条约签订十周年。电文如下：中苏友好协会总会会长宋庆龄同志亲爱的宋庆龄同志：
    苏联对外友好和文化协会联合会主席团、苏中友好协会理事会向您，并通过您，向全中国人民热烈地祝贺苏中友好同盟互助条约签订十周年。苏中条约使我们两国人民结成了永恒的兄弟关系，它并且是加强社会主义各国人民伟大家庭的团结一致、加强普遍和平和国际安全的重要因素。在这个重大的纪念日里，我们苏联人，为我们亲爱的中国朋友们在社会主义建设中所取得的巨大成就，为伟大的中国人民在光荣的中国共产党和以毛泽东同志为首的党中央委员会的领导下所取得的胜利而感到欢欣鼓舞。
    您领导的中苏友好协会，在加强与发展我们两国人民的友谊与合作方面，发挥着巨大的富有成效的作用。这个协会的工作，博得了苏联人民的深深爱戴和尊敬，因为再没有任何任务会比巩固我们两国人民的友谊更为光荣，更为崇高。我们衷心地祝愿中国人民在你们国家的社会主义建设中，取得新的更大的成就。
    苏中两国人民的永恒的牢不可破的友谊万岁！世界和平万岁！
    苏联对外友好和文化协会联合会主席团主席  尼·波波娃
    苏中友好协会理事会主席　安·安德烈耶夫
              一九六○年二月十三日莫斯科
