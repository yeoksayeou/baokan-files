Title: Soviet Three Organizations Send Telegrams to China's Three Organizations to Congratulate on the Ever-Growing Consolidation of Sino-Soviet Friendship
Author:
Date: 1960-02-15
Page: 5
    Xinhua News Agency, February 14th -- The All-China Women's Federation received a telegram from the Soviet Women's Committee congratulating the tenth anniversary of the signing of the Sino-Soviet Treaty of Friendship, Alliance, and Mutual Assistance.
    The telegram said: "We wholeheartedly congratulate all the women of the People's Republic of China on the tenth anniversary of the signing of the Sino-Soviet Treaty of Friendship, Alliance, and Mutual Assistance. May the unbreakable friendship and cooperation between the people of China and the Soviet Union, and between the women of the two countries, be further consolidated and developed."
    Xinhua News Agency, February 14th -- The All-China Youth Federation received a telegram from the Soviet Youth Organizations Committee congratulating the tenth anniversary of the signing of the Sino-Soviet Treaty of Friendship, Alliance, and Mutual Assistance.
    The telegram said: "On behalf of the Soviet youth, we warmly congratulate the heroic youth of great China on the tenth anniversary of the signing of the Sino-Soviet Treaty of Friendship, Alliance, and Mutual Assistance. This significant day is a common festival for the people and youth of our two countries.
    "Over the past ten years, the friendship between our two countries has become more developed and more consolidated. We believe that the Chinese youth, who have made great contributions to the early completion of the Second Five-Year Plan, will achieve even greater success in socialist construction and the struggle for world peace under the leadership of the Chinese Communist Party."
    Xinhua News Agency, February 14th -- The All-China Students' Federation received a telegram from the Student Committee of the Soviet Youth Organizations Committee congratulating the tenth anniversary of the signing of the Sino-Soviet Treaty of Friendship, Alliance, and Mutual Assistance.
    The telegram said: “On the occasion of the tenth anniversary of the signing of the Sino-Soviet Treaty of Friendship, Alliance, and Mutual Assistance, we extend our heartfelt congratulations to you and all Chinese university students on behalf of Soviet university students and all Soviet youth. We wish Chinese university students greater achievements in socialist construction and the struggle for peaceful friendship among the people of all countries. May the unbreakable friendship between the people and students of the Soviet Union and China be consolidated and prosper!”

### 苏联三团体致电我国三团体  祝贺中苏人民友谊日益巩固

1960-02-15
第5版()
专栏：

    苏联三团体致电我国三团体
    祝贺中苏人民友谊日益巩固
    新华社14日讯　全国妇联收到了苏联妇女委员会发来的祝贺中苏友好同盟互助条约签订十周年的电报。
    电报说：“我们衷心地向中华人民共和国的全体妇女祝贺苏中友好同盟互助条约签订十周年。让中苏两国人民之间和两国妇女之间牢不可破的友谊与合作更加巩固和发展。”
    新华社14日讯　中华全国青年联合会收到了苏联青年组织委员会发来的祝贺中苏友好同盟互助条约签订十周年的电报。
    电报说：“谨代表苏联青年向伟大中国的英勇青年热烈祝贺苏中友好同盟互助条约签订十周年。这个意义重大的日子是我们两国人民和青年的共同节日。
    “十年来，我们两国的友谊已经更发展、更巩固。我们相信，已为提前完成第二个五年计划作出巨大贡献的中国青年，将在中国共产党的领导下，在社会主义建设和争取世界和平的斗争中取得更大的成就。”
    新华社14日讯　中华全国学生联合会收到了苏联青年组织委员会大学生委员会发来的祝贺中苏友好同盟互助条约签订十周年的电报。
    电报说：“当苏中友好同盟互助条约签订十周年的时候，谨代表苏联大学生和全苏联青年向你们和全中国大学生致以衷心的祝贺。祝中国大学生在社会主义建设和争取各国人民和平友好的斗争中取得更大的成就。祝苏中两国人民和学生之间的牢不可破的友谊巩固和繁荣！”
