Title: Reception and Placement Committee for Returned Overseas Chinese Holds Meeting
Author:
Date: 1960-02-21
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, 20th — The Reception and Placement Committee for Returned Overseas Chinese of the People's Republic of China held its second meeting today. Liao Chengzhi, Chairman of the Reception and Placement Committee for Returned Overseas Chinese, reported on the progress of the preparatory work for the reception and placement of returned overseas Chinese at the meeting.

### 接待和安置归国华侨委员会举行会议

1960-02-21
第1版()
专栏：

    接待和安置归国华侨委员会举行会议
    新华社20日讯　中华人民共和国接待和安置归国华侨委员会今天举行第二次会议。接待和安置归国华侨委员会主任委员廖承志，在会上报告了接待和安置归国华侨准备工作的进展情况。
