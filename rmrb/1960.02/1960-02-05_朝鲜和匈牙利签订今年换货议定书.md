Title: North Korea and Hungary Sign This Year's Goods Exchange Protocol

Author: 
Date: 1960-02-05
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, Pyongyang, 3rd. According to the Korean Central News Agency: The Democratic People's Republic of Korea and the Hungarian People's Republic signed the 1960 Goods Exchange and Payment Protocol here today.

    According to this protocol, North Korea will provide Hungary with non-ferrous metals, cement, ammonium sulfate fertilizer, canned fish, hops, etc., and Hungary will provide North Korea with ship parts, truck parts, telecommunications tools, and medicines, etc.

    The Hungarian government trade delegation has left Pyongyang for home after signing the protocol.

### 朝鲜和匈牙利签订今年换货议定书

1960-02-05
第6版()
专栏：

    朝鲜和匈牙利签订今年换货议定书
    新华社平壤3日电　据朝鲜中央通讯社讯：朝鲜民主主义人民共和国和匈牙利人民共和国今天在这里签订了1960年换货和支付议定书。
    根据这项议定书，朝鲜将向匈牙利提供非铁金属、水泥、硫酸氨肥料、罐头鱼、酒花等，匈牙利将向朝鲜提供轮船零件、卡车零件、电讯工具和医药等。
    匈牙利政府贸易代表团已经在签订议定书以后离平壤回国。
