Title: Sino-Mongolian 1960 Agreement on Mutual Supply of Goods Signed
Author:
Date: 1960-02-24
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, February 23rd. A protocol on the mutual supply of goods between the People's Republic of China and the Mongolian People's Republic for 1960 was signed in Beijing today.

The protocol stipulates that the People's Republic of China will supply the Mongolian People's Republic with silk, wool fabrics, cotton cloth, automobiles, machine parts, chemical raw materials, building materials, and daily necessities, and will assist Mongolia in constructing a cement factory, a match factory, a porcelain factory, a raw fur selection and processing plant, a department store, and a guesthouse. The Mongolian People's Republic will supply the People's Republic of China with horses, casings, and hides.

Acting Minister of Foreign Trade Lei Renmin signed on behalf of the Chinese government; Minister of Foreign Trade O. Dorjgotov, head of the Mongolian government trade delegation, signed on behalf of the Mongolian government.

Attending the signing ceremony on the Chinese side were Jiang Ming, Vice Minister of Foreign Trade, and Li Qiangfen, Deputy Director of the Second Asian Affairs Department of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs. On the Mongolian side were Sh. Sharav, Ambassador of the Mongolian People's Republic to China, and all members of the Mongolian government trade delegation.

### 中蒙1960年互相供应货物议定书签字

1960-02-24
第4版()
专栏：

    中蒙1960年互相供应货物议定书签字
    新华社23日讯　中华人民共和国和蒙古人民共和国1960年互相供应货物议定书今天在北京签字。
    议定书规定，中华人民共和国将供应蒙古人民共和国绸缎、呢绒、棉布、汽车、机器配件、化工原料、建筑材料、日用百货等，并为蒙古建设水泥厂、火柴厂、瓷器厂、毛皮原料挑选整理加工场、百货大楼和招待所。蒙古人民共和国将供应中华人民共和国马匹、肠衣和皮张等。
    代表中国政府签字的，是中华人民共和国对外贸易部代理部长雷任民；代表蒙古政府签字的，是蒙古人民共和国政府贸易代表团团长、对外贸易部部长奥·德勒格尔扎布。
    参加签字仪式的，中国方面有对外贸易部副部长江明，外交部第二亚洲司副司长李强奋；蒙古方面有蒙古人民共和国驻中国大使沙拉布和蒙古人民共和国政府贸易代表团的全体人员。
