Title: Strive for Algeria's Liberation and Independence: Armed Struggle is the Primary Form of Struggle; Algerian "Holy War Fighter Newspaper" Comments on the Current Situation in Algeria
Author:
Date: 1960-02-07
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, February 6th:  The Algerian "Holy War Fighter Newspaper" stated in an editorial regarding the meeting of the Algerian National Revolution Committee held last month in Tripoli, the capital of Libya:  Regardless of how the political and diplomatic situation develops, our primary form of struggle is armed struggle.  The war imposed upon us is a war for liberation and independence.

The Provisional Government of the Republic of Algeria announced in a statement on the 5th that, according to the decision of the Algerian National Revolution Committee, the General Staff of the Algerian National Liberation Army has been established.

### 争取阿尔及利亚的解放和独立  武装斗争是主要斗争形式  阿“圣战者报”评阿尔及利亚当前形势

1960-02-07
第4版()
专栏：

    争取阿尔及利亚的解放和独立
    武装斗争是主要斗争形式
    阿“圣战者报”评阿尔及利亚当前形势
    据新华社6日讯　阿尔及利亚“圣战者报”在一篇关于上个月在利比亚首都的黎波里举行的阿尔及利亚全国革命委员会会议的社论里指出：不管政治和外交形势如何发展，我们主要的斗争形式是武装斗争。强加于我们的战争是一场争取解放和独立的战争。
    阿尔及利亚共和国临时政府5日在一项声明中宣布，根据阿尔及利亚全国革命委员会的决定，已经成立了阿尔及利亚民族解放军总参谋部。
