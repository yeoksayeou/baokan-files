Title: Overworked Technician
Author: Cao Liji
Date: 1960-02-20
Page: 2

    Overworked technician,
    Never idle,
    Running east, rushing west.
    Girls come to ask,
    Masters come to move.
    Helping this one draw blueprints,
    Helping that one offer suggestions,
    Sketches drawn in bundles,
    Suggestions piled into a mountain.
    Oh my!
    The technician is so busy.
    Worker of Qingdao Printing and Dyeing Factory, Cao Liji

### 忙坏了技术员
曹立基
1960-02-20
第2版()
专栏：

    忙坏了技术员
    技术员，不得闲，
    东边跑，西边颠。
    姑娘前来请，
    师傅前来搬。
    帮助这个画图样，
    帮助那个提意见，
    草图画成捆，
    建议堆成山。
    哎呀呀！
    可忙坏了技术员。
    青岛印染厂工人　曹立基
