Title: NPC Standing Committee Holds Enlarged Meeting; Li Baohua Reports on 1959 Achievements in Water Conservancy and Electric Power Construction and This Year's Tasks
Author:
Date: 1960-02-12
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, 11th — The Standing Committee of the National People's Congress held its enlarged fourteenth meeting on the morning of the 11th, and listened to a report by Li Baohua, Vice Minister of the Ministry of Water Conservancy and Electric Power, on the achievements of water conservancy and electric power construction in 1959 and the work tasks for 1960.

### 人大常委会举行扩大会议  李葆华报告1959年水利电力建设成就和今年任务

1960-02-12
第1版()
专栏：

    人大常委会举行扩大会议
    李葆华报告1959年水利电力建设成就和今年任务
    新华社11日讯　全国人民代表大会常务委员会在11日上午举行扩大的第十四次会议，听取了水利电力部副部长李葆华所作的关于1959年水利电力建设成就和1960年工作任务的报告。
    政协全国委员会常务委员列席了这次会议。
