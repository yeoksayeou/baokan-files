Title: Chinese Delegation Arrives in Cairo to Participate in the Asian-African Film Festival
Author:
Date: 1960-02-29
Page: 7

    Xinhua News Agency, Cairo, February 27th: The Chinese delegation, led by head Zhang Wang, arrived here today to participate in the Asian-African Film Festival.
    Members of the delegation include playwright and film director Li Jun, and film actresses Yang Likun and Lu Guilan. The Chinese color feature film "Five Golden Flowers" and other feature films and documentaries will be screened at the film festival, which is scheduled to begin on the 29th of this month.

### 参加亚非电影节  我国代表团抵开罗

1960-02-29
第7版()
专栏：

    参加亚非电影节
    我国代表团抵开罗
    新华社开罗27日电　参加亚非电影节的中国代表团在团长张望率领下，今天到达这里。
    代表团团员有剧作家和电影导演李俊，电影女演员杨丽坤和卢桂兰。中国彩色故事片“五朵金花”和其它故事片、纪录片将在订于本月29日开始的电影节放映。
