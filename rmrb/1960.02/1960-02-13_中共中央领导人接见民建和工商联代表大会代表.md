Title: Central CCP Leaders Receive Representatives of the China Democratic National Construction Association and the All-China Federation of Industry and Commerce Congress

Author:
Date: 1960-02-13
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, February 12th:  CCP Central Committee Vice-Chairmen Liu Shaoqi and Zhu De, CCP Central Committee Political Bureau Members Chen Yi, Li Xiannian, and Tan Zhenlin, and CCP Central Committee Member Li Weihan received all the representatives of the China Democratic National Construction Association and the All-China Federation of Industry and Commerce Congress this afternoon.

(Attached Picture)

The picture shows the scene during the reception.  Xinhua News Agency Reporter Du Xiuxian, Zhang Bin.

### 中共中央领导人接见民建和工商联代表大会代表

1960-02-13
第1版()
专栏：

    中共中央领导人接见民建和工商联代表大会代表
    新华社12日讯　中共中央副主席刘少奇、朱德，中共中央政治局委员陈毅、李先念、谭震林，中共中央委员李维汉今天下午接见了中国民主建国会和中华全国工商业联合会代表大会全体代表。
