International Briefs

    Laos to Hold General Elections
    Hanoi Dispatch: According to a broadcast by Vientiane Radio, the Lao Cabinet decided at a meeting held on January 30th to hold general elections on April 24, 1960. The ultra-pro-American "Committee for the Defense of National Interests" is attempting to unite with Sananikone's Lao People's Union Party to further suppress the Lao Patriotic Front and to legitimize the regime they seized by force through the general election.

    Prime Minister Kassem to Visit India
    Baghdad Dispatch: Iraqi Prime Minister Kassem has accepted an invitation to visit India. This was announced by Iraqi Minister of State Guidance Samir on the evening of January 31st on Baghdad Radio. He said that the date of the visit has not yet been specifically determined.

    Armed Conflict Occurs on the Border Between the UAR and Israel
    Damascus Dispatch: According to a spokesman for the First Army Command of the UAR's Syrian Region, Israeli forces launched a fierce and concentrated attack on the village of Tawafik in the demilitarized zone at midnight on January 31st, resulting in casualties on both sides.

    The First Army spokesman announced on February 1st that air battles had taken place between UAR and Israeli air force planes.

### 国际简讯

1960-02-04
第6版()
专栏：国际简讯

    国际简讯
    老挝将举行大选
    河内讯　据万象电台广播，老挝内阁在1月30日举行的会议上决定在1960年4月24日举行大选。极端亲美的“保卫国家利益委员会”正企图联合萨纳尼空的老挝人联合党，进一步打击老挝爱国战线党，通过大选来使他们以武力攫取的政权取得合法的形式。
    卡塞姆总理将访问印度
    巴格达讯　伊拉克总理卡塞姆已经接受了访问印度的邀请。这是伊拉克国家指导部长萨米尔1月31日晚在巴格达电台宣布的。他说，访问日期还没有具体确定。
    阿联以色列边境发生武装冲突
    大马士革讯　据阿联叙利亚地区第一军司令部发言人宣布，以色列军队在1月31日午夜向非军事区内的塔瓦菲克村发动了一次猛烈的集中攻击，战斗中双方都有伤亡。
    第一军发言人2月1日宣布，阿联和以色列的空军飞机曾经进行了空战。
