Title: Delegation from Romanian Academy of Sciences Arrives in Beijing; President Guo Moruo Receives Delegation
Author:
Date: 1960-02-11
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency, February 9th:

    Delegation from Romanian Academy of Sciences Arrives in Beijing
    President Guo Moruo Receives Delegation

    Xinhua News Agency, February 9th – A delegation from the Romanian Academy of Sciences, headed by Academician Alexandru Codarcea, arrived in Beijing by plane today. The delegation is in China to sign the 1960 executive plan for scientific cooperation between the Chinese and Romanian Academies of Sciences and to pay a friendly visit, in accordance with the provisions of the scientific cooperation agreement between the two academies. The delegation members include Professor Virgil Ianovici, Professor Vasile Vasilescu, and Loheao Astdanianu, Director of the Foreign Liaison Bureau of the Romanian Academy of Sciences.

    Greeting the delegation at the airport were Wu Youxun, Vice President of the Chinese Academy of Sciences, Yin Zanxun, Director of the Department of Earth Sciences, Lin Rong, Deputy Director of the Department of Biological Sciences, Wang Tuo, Director of the Liaison Bureau, and others.

    Baschkiru, First Secretary of the Romanian Embassy in China, also greeted the delegation at the airport.

    Xinhua News Agency, February 10th – Guo Moruo, President of the Chinese Academy of Sciences, received Academician Alexandru Codarcea, head of the Romanian Academy of Sciences delegation, and delegation members Professor Virgil Ianovici, Professor Vasile Vasilescu, and Loheao Astdanianu, Director of the Foreign Liaison Bureau of the Romanian Academy of Sciences, this afternoon.

    Present at the reception were Zhang Jingfu and Wu Youxun, Vice Presidents of the Chinese Academy of Sciences, Yin Zanxun, Director of the Department of Earth Sciences, Lin Rong, Deputy Director of the Department of Biological Sciences, Wang Tuo, Director of the Liaison Bureau, and others.

### 罗马尼亚科学院代表团到京  郭沫若院长接见代表团

1960-02-11
第4版()
专栏：

    罗马尼亚科学院代表团到京
    郭沫若院长接见代表团
    新华社9日讯　以亚力克山特鲁·科达尔查院士为首的罗马尼亚科学院代表团今天乘飞机到达北京。代表团是根据中罗两国科学院科学合作协定的规定，来我国签订中罗两国科学院1960年科学合作的执行计划和进行友好访问的。代表团成员有维尔基尔·雅诺维奇教授，华西列·华西列斯库教授和罗马尼亚科学院对外联络局局长洛海奥·阿斯旦列扬努。
    到机场欢迎的有中国科学院副院长吴有训、地学部主任尹赞勋、生物学部副主任林镕、联络局局长王拓等。
    罗马尼亚驻华大使馆一等秘书巴什基洛也到机场欢迎。
    新华社10日讯　中国科学院院长郭沫若今天下午接见了罗马尼亚科学院代表团团长亚力克山特鲁·科达尔查院士和代表团团员维尔基尔·雅诺维奇教授、华西列·华西列斯库教授和罗马尼亚科学院对外联络局局长洛海奥·阿斯旦列扬努。
    接见时在座的有中国科学院副院长张劲夫、吴有训，地学部主任尹赞勋、生物学部副主任林镕、联络局局长王拓等。
