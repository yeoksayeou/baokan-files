Author:
Date: 1960-02-11
Page: 1

    Sino-Soviet Alliance Deals a Fatal Blow to Imperialism
    Guo Moruo Hosts Reception for Soviet Scientists to Celebrate the Tenth Anniversary of the Sino-Soviet Treaty
    Xinhua News Agency, February 10th. Guo Moruo, President of the Chinese Academy of Sciences, held a reception at the Moscow Restaurant in the Beijing Exhibition Center tonight in celebration of the tenth anniversary of the signing of the Sino-Soviet Treaty of Friendship,Alliance, andMutual Assistance, to entertain Soviet scientists working in the Chinese Academy of Sciences. Scientists from Czechoslovakia and the German Democratic Republic, as well as the Romanian Academy of Sciences delegation currently visiting China, were also invited to attend the reception.
    Also invited to attend the reception were Antonov and his wife, and Sudarikov and his wife, counselors of the Soviet Embassy in China. A total of more than 200 people attended the reception.
    Guo Moruo said at the reception that the Sino-Soviet Treaty of Friendship, Alliance, and Mutual Assistance has played a great role in safeguarding world peace over the past ten years, and has also played a huge role in the development of economic, cultural, and scientific undertakings in both countries. Guo Moruo emphasized that today we must further consolidate the unity of the socialist camp headed by the Soviet Union, and cherish this unity as we cherish the apple of our eye.
    Antonov said that he believes that in the future the Sino-Soviet Treaty of Friendship, Alliance, and Mutual Assistance will play a greater role in the economic, cultural, and scientific construction of the two countries, and will give the imperialist elements a more powerful and fatal blow.
    Academician Alexandru Codarcea, head of the Romanian Academy of Sciences delegation, also spoke to congratulate the tenth anniversary of the signing of the Sino-Soviet Treaty of Friendship, Alliance, and Mutual Assistance, saying that the Sino-Soviet Treaty of Friendship, Alliance, and Mutual Assistance is a guarantee of world peace.
    A wonderful artistic program was performed at the reception.

### 中苏同盟给予帝国主义致命打击  郭沫若举行酒会招待苏联科学家庆祝中苏同盟条约十周年

1960-02-11
第1版()
专栏：

    中苏同盟给予帝国主义致命打击
    郭沫若举行酒会招待苏联科学家庆祝中苏同盟条约十周年
    新华社10日讯　中国科学院院长郭沫若为庆祝中苏友好同盟互助条约签订十周年，今晚在北京展览馆莫斯科餐厅举行酒会，招待在中国科学院帮助工作的苏联科学家。捷克斯洛伐克和德意志民主共和国的科学家以及正在我国访问的罗马尼亚科学院代表团也应邀出席了酒会。
    应邀出席酒会的还有苏联驻华大使馆参赞安东诺夫夫妇和苏达利柯夫夫妇。参加酒会的共有二百多人。
    郭沫若在酒会上说：中苏友好同盟互助条约签订十年来对于保卫世界和平起了很大作用；对于发展两国经济文化建设和科学事业同样起了巨大作用。郭沫若强调说，今天我们要更加巩固以苏联为首的社会主义阵营的团结，要像爱护自己的眼珠一样来爱护这种团结。
    安东诺夫讲话说，相信今后中苏友好同盟互助条约将对两国经济文化建设和科学事业起更大的作用；将给予帝国主义分子以更有力的致命打击。
    罗马尼亚科学院代表团团长亚历克山特鲁·科达尔查院士也讲话祝贺中苏友好同盟互助条约签订十周年，他说中苏友好同盟互助条约是世界和平的保证。
    酒会上演出了精彩的文艺节目。
