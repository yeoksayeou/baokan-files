Title: Panchen and Pagbalha Leave Beijing for Tibet; Vice Premiers Chen Yi, Tan Zhenlin, and Xi Zhongxun See Them Off at the Station
Author:
Date: 1960-02-09
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, February 8th. Panchen Erdeni Qoigyi Gyaltsen, Acting Chairman of the Preparatory Committee for the Tibet Autonomous Region, Pagbalha Geleg Namgyal, Vice Chairman, and their entourage of monks and laypeople, left Beijing for Tibet by special train this morning. Wang Feng, Deputy Director of the United Front Work Department of the CPC Central Committee and Deputy Director of the Nationalities Affairs Commission, accompanied Panchen Erdeni to Xining. Zhou Renshan, Deputy Secretary of the CPC Tibet Working Committee, returned to Tibet on the same train.

Vice Premiers Chen Yi, Tan Zhenlin, and Xi Zhongxun; Huang Yanpei, Zhang Zhizhong, Wang Weizhou, Zhang Su and others, Vice Chairmen of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress; Burhan, and some members of the Standing Committee of the National Committee of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference; Xu Bing, Ping Jiesan, and Zhang Zhiyi, Deputy Directors of the United Front Work Department of the CPC Central Committee; Liu Chun, Sa Kongliao, Yang Jingren, Xie Hechou, and Yu Xinqing, Deputy Directors of the Nationalities Affairs Commission, all went to the station today to see them off.

When Panchen Erdeni and others arrived at the station platform, warm applause broke out in the send-off crowd, and children of the capital presented flowers to Panchen Erdeni and others. Before boarding the train, Panchen Erdeni and Pagbalha presented hadas to Vice Premier Chen Yi and others. Vice Premier Chen Yi and others returned the hadas to Panchen Erdeni and Pagbalha, and boarded the train to bid them farewell. As the special train departed, Panchen Erdeni and others repeatedly waved to the send-off crowd, and a burst of applause rang out again on the platform, expressing a warm send-off.

Also at the station today to see them off were heads of various departments of the State Council, Shi Rao Jiacuo, President of the Buddhist Association of China, representatives from all walks of life in the capital, cadres from various organizations, and students of various nationalities.

### 班禅和帕巴拉离京回西藏  陈毅、谭震林、习仲勋副总理等到车站欢送

1960-02-09
第1版()
专栏：

    班禅和帕巴拉离京回西藏
    陈毅、谭震林、习仲勋副总理等到车站欢送
    新华社8日讯　西藏自治区筹备委员会代理主任委员班禅额尔德尼·却吉坚赞、副主任委员帕巴拉·卓列朗杰和随行的僧俗人员，今天上午乘专车离京回西藏。中共中央统战部副部长、民族事务委员会副主任汪锋陪送班禅额尔德尼等到西宁。中共西藏工委副书记周仁山同车回西藏。
    陈毅、谭震林、习仲勋副总理，全国人民代表大会常务委员会副委员长黄炎培和张治中、王维舟、张苏等委员，政协全国委员会副主席包尔汉和部分常务委员，中共中央统战部副部长徐冰、平杰三、张执一，民族事务委员会副主任刘春、萨空了、杨静仁、谢鹤筹、余心清，今天都到车站欢送。
    当班禅额尔德尼等到达车站月台的时候，欢送行列里响起热烈的掌声，首都少年儿童向班禅额尔德尼等献花。班禅额尔德尼和帕巴拉在上车前，向陈毅副总理等敬送哈达，陈毅副总理等向班禅额尔德尼和帕巴拉回敬了哈达，并且登车同他们告别。专车开动后，班禅额尔德尼等一再向欢送的人们招手致意，月台上又响起一片掌声，表示热烈欢送。
    今天到车站欢送的，还有国务院各部门的负责人，中国佛教协会会长喜饶嘉错，首都各界代表，机关干部和各民族的学生。
