Title: Burmese Steel Inspection Delegation Arrives in Beijing; Vice Minister of Metallurgical Industry Liu Bin Receives and Hosts Burmese Delegation

Author:
Date: 1960-02-27
Page: 4

    Burmese Steel Inspection Delegation Arrives in Beijing
    Vice Minister of Metallurgical Industry Liu Bin Receives and Hosts Burmese Delegation
    Xinhua News Agency, February 25th: The Burmese Steel Inspection Delegation, consisting of four members led by U Khin Maung Kyi, Director of the Burmese State Steel Rolling Mill Management Committee, arrived in Beijing by plane today.
    Welcoming them at the airport were Zhai Ziqiang, Director of the General Office of the Ministry of Metallurgical Industry, and Wang Zhixi, Deputy Director of the Steel Bureau, among others.
    Diplomatic officials from the Burmese Embassy in China also welcomed them at the airport.
    Xinhua News Agency, February 26th: Vice Minister of Metallurgical Industry Liu Bin today received U Khin Maung Kyi, the head of the Burmese Steel Inspection Delegation and Director of the Burmese State Steel Rolling Mill Management Committee, and all members of the delegation.
    In the evening, Liu Bin also hosted a banquet to welcome all members of the delegation.
    Burmese Ambassador to China, Kyaw Winn, was also invited to attend the banquet.

### 缅甸钢铁考察团抵京  冶金工业部副部长刘彬接见并欢宴缅甸考察团

1960-02-27
第4版()
专栏：

    缅甸钢铁考察团抵京
    冶金工业部副部长刘彬接见并欢宴缅甸考察团
    新华社25日讯　缅甸钢铁考察团一行四人，在缅甸国营轧钢厂管理委员会主任吴庆貌季率领下，今天乘飞机到达北京。
    在机场欢迎的有冶金工业部办公厅主任翟自强和钢铁司副司长王之玺等。
    缅甸驻我国大使馆外交官员也到机场欢迎。
    新华社26日讯　冶金工业部副部长刘彬今天接见了缅甸钢铁考察团团长、缅甸国营轧钢厂管理委员会主任吴庆貌季和考察团全体团员。
    晚上，刘彬还设宴欢迎考察团全体成员。
    缅甸驻中国大使叫温也应邀出席了宴会。
