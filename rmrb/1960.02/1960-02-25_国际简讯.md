International Briefs

    Pakistan, Turkey, and Iran Hold Summit
    Karachi Dispatch: Pakistani President Ayub Khan, Turkish President Bayar, and Iranian King Pahlavi began talks in Lahore on the 23rd to discuss strengthening relations between these three aggressive "Central Treaty Organization" Muslim member states. According to Karachi newspapers, the issues discussed by the heads of these three countries during the talks included the establishment of a joint military advisory command and the establishment of a Muslim state "confederation." The King of Iran said in Tehran a few days ago that the establishment of a Muslim state group was a "good idea."
      Nasser to Visit India
    New Delhi Dispatch: New Delhi officially announced on the 23rd that President Nasser of the United Arab Republic, at the invitation of the Indian government, will visit India for about two weeks during the last week of March or the first week of April.

### 国际简讯

专栏：国际简讯

    国际简讯
    巴、土、伊三国首脑会谈
    卡拉奇讯　巴基斯坦总统阿尤布汗、土耳其总统拜亚尔和伊朗国王巴列维23日在拉合尔开始举行会谈，讨论加强这三个侵略性的“中央条约组织”穆斯林成员国之间的关系。据卡拉奇报纸报道，这三个国家的首脑在会谈中所讨论的问题包括成立联合军事谘询司令部，成立一个穆斯林国家“邦联”等。伊朗国王几天前在德黑兰曾说，成立穆斯林国家集团是个“好主意”。
      纳赛尔将访问印度
    新德里讯　新德里官方23日宣布，阿拉伯联合共和国总统纳赛尔应印度政府的邀请，将在3月的最后一个星期或4月的第一个星期到印度作大约两星期的访问。
