Title: Guo Moruo Receives Czechoslovak Academy of Sciences Delegation
Author:
Date: 1960-02-18
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, February 16th: Guo Moruo, President of the Chinese Academy of Sciences, received Academician Prusek, head of the Czechoslovak Academy of Sciences delegation, and all members of the delegation this evening. President Guo Moruo had a cordial conversation with the guests.

Present at the reception were Wu Youxun, Vice President of the Chinese Academy of Sciences, and Wang Tuo, Director of the Liaison Bureau of the Chinese Academy of Sciences.

### 郭沫若接见捷科学院代表团

1960-02-18
第4版()
专栏：

    郭沫若接见捷科学院代表团
    新华社16日讯　中国科学院院长郭沫若，今晚接见了捷克斯洛伐克科学院代表团团长普鲁司克院士和代表团的全体团员。郭沫若院长同客人们进行了亲切的交谈。
    接见时在座的，有中国科学院副院长吴有训和中国科学院联络局局长王拓。
