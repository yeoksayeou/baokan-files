Title: The U.S. Will Continue the "Cold War" for Fifty Years; Allen Dulles Insists on Taking "Effective Actions" to Deal with the Socialist Camp
Author:
Date: 1960-02-20
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, February 19th: A dispatch from the U.S. Information Agency in Washington on the 17th admitted that the director of the U.S. Central Intelligence Agency, Allen Dulles, advocated a long-term "Cold War."

The dispatch said that Allen Dulles stated at a closed-door opinion hearing of the Senate's National Policy Machinery Subcommittee that the "Cold War" would last for another fifty years. He also emphasized that the United States must "take effective actions" to antagonize the socialist camp.

### 美国还要继续“冷战”五十年  艾伦·杜勒斯坚持采取“有效行动”对付社会主义阵营

1960-02-20
第6版()
专栏：

    美国还要继续“冷战”五十年
    艾伦·杜勒斯坚持采取“有效行动”对付社会主义阵营
    新华社19日讯　美国官方宣传机构美国新闻处17日从华盛顿发出的一条消息承认，美国中央情报局局长艾伦·杜勒斯鼓吹长期进行“冷战”。
    消息说，艾伦·杜勒斯在参议院国家政策机构特别小组委员会的秘密意见听取会上说，“冷战”还要持续五十年之久。他还强调美国必须“采取有效行动”来同社会主义阵营为敌。
