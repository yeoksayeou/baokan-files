Title: Busy Delivering Fertilizer
Author:
Date: 1960-02-07
Page: 1

Busy delivering fertilizer, the spring scenery arrives early and the view is good, carts and horses run along the commune roads, every vehicle is loaded with fertilizer, all good nourishment for crops, fertilize the fields fully today, and grain yields will be high in the autumn.
      (Originally published in Heilongjiang Daily)

### 送肥忙

1960-02-07
第1版()
专栏：

     送肥忙春色早到风光好，公社路上车马跑，辆辆车儿装肥料，都是庄稼的好粮草，今天耕地施满肥，秋季粮食产量高。
      （原载黑龙江日报）
