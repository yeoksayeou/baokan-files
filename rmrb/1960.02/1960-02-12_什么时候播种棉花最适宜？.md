Title: When is the Best Time to Plant Cotton?
Author: Wang Youxiang
Date: 1960-02-12
Page: 3

Column: Reader Questions

    When is the best time to plant cotton?
    In the short commentary "Catch Up Head-on, Early in Everything" on page 3 of the February 8th People's Daily, it was mentioned that cotton is generally sown around the "Grain Rain" solar term, when jujube trees sprout. When exactly do jujube trees sprout? From our perspective here, it's about one solar term after "Grain Rain," which is around "Start of Summer." So, when is the most suitable time to sow cotton?
                  Hebei Province, Shijiazhuang City, Wang Youxiang
    The cotton sowing period should be determined based on soil temperature and the last frost date. Cotton can be sown when the local soil temperature reaches a stable rise of 12 degrees Celsius. Specifically, in the main cotton-producing areas of the Yangtze and Yellow River basins, the most suitable cotton sowing period is approximately from early April to mid-April; in the northern regions, the cotton sowing period is around mid-to-late April. In 1958 and 1959, all major cotton-producing areas basically completed sowing around April 20th. This shows that the cotton sowing period should be before the "Grain Rain" solar term. When sowing cotton in the aforementioned areas, regardless of whether in the south or north, the jujube trees have not yet sprouted.
    "When jujube trees sprout, plant cotton" is a farming saying in cotton-producing areas. It is a summary of planting experience from the era of small-scale peasant economy, and it is no longer applicable today. The central idea of this short commentary is to hope that all regions, in the spirit of "early in everything," will make full preparations for timely and early sowing of cotton. However, it is inappropriate to say in the short commentary that "cotton is generally sown around the 'Grain Rain' solar term, when jujube trees sprout."

### 什么时候播种棉花最适宜？
王有相
1960-02-12
第3版()
专栏：答读者问

    什么时候播种棉花最适宜？
    2月8日人民日报三版“迎头赶上，一早百早”短评中提到：棉花一般都在“谷雨”节前后，枣树发芽的时候开始播种。枣树究竟什么时候发芽？从我们这里来看，大约在“谷雨”后一个节令，也就是“立夏”的时候。那么，播种棉花究竟在什么时候最适宜？
                  河北省石家庄市　王有相
    棉花播种期应该根据地温和终霜期来决定。当地温达到摄氏十二度稳定上升的时候，就能播种棉花。从具体地区来说，长江、黄河流域主要棉产区最适宜的棉花播种期，大约是4月初到4月中；北方偏北地区的棉花播种期，约在4月中下旬。1958年和1959年各主要棉产区都在4月20日左右就基本结束了播种。由此可见，棉花播种期应在“谷雨”节以前。而在上述各地播种棉花时，不论南方和北方，枣树都还没有发芽。
    “枣发芽，种棉花”是棉产区的一句农谚。它是小农经济时代植棉经验的总结，在今天已经不适用了。这篇短评的中心思想，是希望各地本着一早百早的精神，为适时提前播种棉花做好充分准备。但是，短评中说“一般都在‘谷雨’节前后、枣树发芽的时候开始播种”，是不恰当的。　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　编者
