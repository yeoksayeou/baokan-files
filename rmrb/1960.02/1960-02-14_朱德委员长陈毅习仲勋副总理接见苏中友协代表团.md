Title: Chairman Zhu De, Vice Premiers Chen Yi and Xi Zhongxun Receive Delegation from the Soviet-Chinese Friendship Association
Author:
Date: 1960-02-14
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, February 13th: Chairman of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress Zhu De, and Vice Premiers of the State Council Chen Yi and Xi Zhongxun, received this evening Comrade V.P. Yelyutin, Vice Chairman of the Council of the Soviet-Chinese Friendship Association and Minister of Higher and Secondary Specialized Education of the Soviet Union, and all members of the delegation from the Soviet-Chinese Friendship Association led by him. The hosts and guests engaged in a cordial and friendly conversation.

Present at the reception were Vice Presidents of the Sino-Soviet Friendship Association, Guo Moruo, Wu Yuzhang, Huang Yanpei, Liu Ningyi, Liao Chengzhi, Li Dequan, Li Siguang, Shen Yanbing, Shao Lizi, Secretary-General Qian Junrui, Vice Minister of Foreign Affairs Zeng Yongquan, Vice Director of the Commission for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries Zhang Zhixiang, and Vice Minister of Education Liu Aifeng.

Also present at the reception were Soviet Ambassador to China S.V. Chervonenko, and Counselor Antonov and Sudarikov of the Embassy.

### 朱德委员长陈毅习仲勋副总理接见苏中友协代表团

1960-02-14
第1版()
专栏：

    朱德委员长陈毅习仲勋副总理接见苏中友协代表团
    新华社13日讯　全国人民代表大会常务委员会委员长朱德，国务院副总理陈毅、习仲勋今晚接见了苏中友好协会理事会副主席、苏联高等教育和中等专业教育部部长叶留金和由他率领的苏中友好协会代表团的全体人员，宾主进行了亲切友好的交谈。
    接见时在座的，有中苏友好协会总会副会长郭沫若、吴玉章、黄炎培、刘宁一、廖承志、李德全、李四光、沈雁冰、邵力子，秘书长钱俊瑞，外交部副部长曾涌泉，对外文委副主任张致祥，教育部副部长刘皑风。
    接见时，苏联驻中国大使契尔沃年科和大使馆参赞安东诺夫、苏达利柯夫也在座。
