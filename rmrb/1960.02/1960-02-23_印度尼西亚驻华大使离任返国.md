Title: Indonesian Ambassador to China Departs for Home

Author:
Date: 1960-02-23
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, 22nd – The Ambassador of the Republic of Indonesia to China, Soekardjo Wirjopranoto, departed for home this morning upon the completion of his term. Yu Peiwen, Director of the Protocol Department of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs, and Zhang Wenjin, Director of the First Asian Affairs Department, were at the airport to see him off.

Diplomatic envoys from various countries to China and diplomats from the Indonesian Embassy in China also went to the airport to see him off.

### 印度尼西亚驻华大使离任返国

1960-02-23
第4版()
专栏：

    印度尼西亚驻华大使离任返国
    新华社22日讯　印度尼西亚共和国驻华大使苏加佐·维约普拉诺托于今日上午离任返国。前往机场送行的有外交部礼宾司司长俞沛文、第一亚洲司司长章文晋。
    各国驻华使节和印度尼西亚驻华大使馆的外交官也到机场送行。
