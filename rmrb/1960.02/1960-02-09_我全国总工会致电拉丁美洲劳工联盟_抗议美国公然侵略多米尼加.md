Title: All-China Federation of Trade Unions Cables the Confederation of Latin American Workers, Protesting the United States' Open Aggression Against the Dominican Republic
Author:
Date: 1960-02-09
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, 8th. The All-China Federation of Trade Unions today sent a cable to the Confederation of Latin American Workers, protesting the United States Marines' landing in the Dominican Republic, an act of aggression.

The cable stated that the Chinese working class expresses its deep anger at this open act of aggression by the United States, which interferes in internal affairs and obstructs the Dominican people's struggle against dictatorship, and demands that the United States fleet and Marines immediately withdraw from the Dominican Republic. The All-China Federation of Trade Unions requests that the above cable be forwarded to the workers of the Dominican Republic.

### 我全国总工会致电拉丁美洲劳工联盟  抗议美国公然侵略多米尼加

1960-02-09
第6版()
专栏：

    我全国总工会致电拉丁美洲劳工联盟
    抗议美国公然侵略多米尼加
    新华社8日讯　中华全国总工会今天打电报给拉丁美洲劳工联盟，表示抗议美国海军陆战队在多米尼加登陆的侵略行为。
    电文说，中国工人阶级对于美国这种干涉内政、阻挠多米尼加人民反独裁斗争的公然侵略行为深表愤怒，并且要求美国舰队和海军陆战队立即撤离多米尼加。中华全国总工会要求把上述电文转达给多米尼加的工人。
