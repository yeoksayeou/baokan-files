Title: China-Hungary and China-Romania Sign This Year's Cultural Cooperation Implementation Plans
Author:
Date: 1960-02-18
Page: 5
Xinhua News Agency, Budapest, February 16th: The 1960 implementation plan for the cultural cooperation agreement between China and Hungary was signed here today.

The plan stipulates that both parties will further expand and strengthen contacts and exchanges of experience in culture, art, science, education, and sports. The two countries will send representatives and experts to visit each other, exchange relevant information, and send students to study abroad.

Xinhua News Agency, Bucharest, February 16th: The 1960 implementation plan for the cultural cooperation agreement between China and Romania was signed here today.

According to the plan, scientific and cultural exchange activities between China and Romania will greatly develop this year. Both sides will send delegations, art troupes, and experts to visit or perform.

The plan also stipulates that both sides will strengthen the exchange of books, periodicals, movies, and pictures, as well as cooperation in news and broadcasting.

### 中匈中罗签订今年文化合作执行计划

1960-02-18
第5版()
专栏：

    中匈中罗签订今年文化合作执行计划
    新华社布达佩斯16日电　中国和匈牙利两国文化合作协定1960年执行计划今天在这里签字。
    计划规定，双方将进一步扩大和加强文化、艺术、科学、教育和体育等方面的联系和经验交流。两国将互派代表和专家进行访问，交换有关资料和互派留学生等。
    新华社布加勒斯特16日电　中国和罗马尼亚文化合作协定1960年执行计划今天在这里签字。
    根据计划，今年中罗两国的科学文化交流活动将有很大发展。双方将互派考察团、艺术团体和专家等进行访问或演出。
    计划还规定，双方将加强书刊、电影、图片等的交换工作和新闻、广播等方面的合作。
