Title: Death of Outstanding Soviet Scientist Kurchatov
Author:
Date: 1960-02-10
Page: 5

    Outstanding Soviet Scientist
    Kurchatov Dies
    Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, 8th. TASS reports: The world-renowned Soviet atomic physicist, Academician I. Kurchatov, died on the 7th after a long illness, at the age of fifty-eight. The Presidium of the Soviet Academy of Sciences and the General Administration for the Utilization of Atomic Energy of the Soviet Council of Ministers issued an obituary stating that Kurchatov was a member of the Communist Party of the Soviet Union, a member of the Presidium of the Soviet Academy of Sciences, and the director of the Institute of Atomic Energy of the Soviet Academy of Sciences. He made outstanding contributions to the research on solving the magnificent task of utilizing atomic internal energy for the benefit of mankind and was hailed as one of the most outstanding physicists of our time.
    Kurchatov was also an active political activist and an enthusiastic fighter for peace. He was repeatedly elected as a deputy to the Supreme Soviet of the Soviet Union.

### 苏联杰出科学家  库尔恰托夫逝世

1960-02-10
第5版()
专栏：

    苏联杰出科学家
    库尔恰托夫逝世
    新华社莫斯科8日电　据塔斯社报道：世界闻名的苏联原子物理学家伊·库尔恰托夫院士，在长期患病后于7日去世，享年五十八岁。苏联科学院主席团和苏联部长会议原子能利用总管理局发表讣告说，库尔恰托夫是苏联共产党党员、苏联科学院主席团委员、苏联科学院原子能研究所所长。他在研究有关解决利用原子内部能源以造福人类这一宏伟任务方面有着杰出的贡献，被誉为当代最杰出的物理学家之一。
    库尔恰托夫同时还是积极的政治活动家、热情的和平战士。他曾经多次当选为苏联最高苏维埃代表。
