Title: Premier Zhou Sends Condolences on the Death of Danish Prime Minister Hansen
Author: 
Date: 1960-02-22
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, February 21st. Premier Zhou Enlai today sent a telegram to Viggo Kampmann, Acting Prime Minister of the Kingdom of Denmark, to express condolences on the death of Danish Prime Minister Hansen. The full text of the message is as follows:

    Copenhagen
    To His Excellency Viggo Kampmann, Acting Prime Minister of the Kingdom of Denmark:

    I was saddened to learn of the unfortunate passing of His Excellency H. C. Hansen, Prime Minister of the Kingdom of Denmark. I hereby send my condolences.

                          Zhou Enlai, Premier of the State Council of the People's Republic of China
                                      February 21, 1960

### 周总理电唁丹麦首相汉森逝世

1960-02-22
第1版()
专栏：

    周总理电唁丹麦首相汉森逝世
    新华社21日讯　周恩来总理今天打电报给丹麦王国代理首相维戈·坎普曼，吊唁丹麦首相汉森的逝世，唁电全文如下：
    哥本哈根
    丹麦王国代理首相维戈·坎普曼阁下：
    顷悉丹麦王国首相贺·西·汉森阁下不幸逝世，特此电唁。
                          中华人民共和国国务院总理　周恩来
                                      一九六○年二月二十一日
