Title: Song of the Dockworkers
Author:
Date: 1960-02-19
Page: 2

　　Flowers of innovation bloom everywhere,
　　Dockworkers operate machines,
　　With a flip of the switch, the motor roars,
　　Thousands of pounds lifted from the ground.
　　Crowbars pressed on me for so many years,
　　Wearing out several new shirts!
　　From now on, my shoulders are liberated,
　　Our ancestors will not forget Chairman Mao!
　　(Attached picture)
　　In the past, people pulled carts; now, carts pull people.
　　Worker: Wang Zhaozhu

### 装卸工人之歌

1960-02-19
第2版()
专栏：

    装卸工人之歌
    革新之花开满地，
    装卸工人开机器，
    电钮一动马达响，
    千斤重量平地起。
    撬杠压我多少年，
    磨破几件新上衣！
    从今肩膀得解放，
    祖辈不忘毛主席！
    （附图片）
    过去人拉车　现在车拉人　工人　王兆珠
