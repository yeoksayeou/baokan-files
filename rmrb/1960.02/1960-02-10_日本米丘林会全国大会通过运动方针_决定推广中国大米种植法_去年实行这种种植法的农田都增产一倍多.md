Title: Japan Michurin Society National Congress Adopts Action Plan: Decides to Promote Chinese Rice Cultivation Methods; Fields Implementing This Method Last Year All Saw More Than Double the Yield

Author:
Date: 1960-02-10
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, Tokyo, 8th - The Japan Michurin Society, at its seventh national congress which concluded on the 7th, adopted an action plan, deciding to continue promoting China's deep plowing, heavy fertilization, and close planting rice cultivation methods to Japanese farmers. The congress confirmed that the experiments in emulating China's deep plowing, heavy fertilization, and close planting rice cultivation methods in the past year have achieved great success.

    Fukushima Yoichi, Secretary-General of the Japan Michurin Society, delivered a general report at the meeting. He pointed out that last year, China's rice cultivation methods were implemented in forty-one locations in nineteen prefectures in Japan. Each experimental field yielded one and a half to two times more rice than before, with the highest reaching 7.5 *shi* per *fan* (approximately 0.66 *mu*), a number far exceeding Japan's highest rice yield last year—6 *shi* per *fan*. (Note: 1 Japanese *shi* is approximately 1.8 Chinese *shi*).

    He emphasized that this planting method must be promoted to the vast number of farmers in the future in order to reap more rice.

    At the meeting, participants exchanged experiences in applying Michurin's theory to cultivate various other crops.

### 日本米丘林会全国大会通过运动方针  决定推广中国大米种植法  去年实行这种种植法的农田都增产一倍多

1960-02-10
第5版()
专栏：

    日本米丘林会全国大会通过运动方针
    决定推广中国大米种植法
    去年实行这种种植法的农田都增产一倍多
    新华社8日讯　东京消息：日本米丘林会在7日结束的第七次全国大会上通过运动方针，决定继续向日本农民推广中国的深耕、多肥、密植的大米种植法。大会确认，过去一年来仿效中国实行深耕、多肥、密植的大米种植法试验获得了很大的成就。
    日本米丘林会干事长福岛要一在会上作了总报告。他指出，去年日本在十九个县的四十一个地方实行了中国的大米种植法，每一个试验农田都收到比从前多一倍半到两倍的大米，其中最多的达每反（约合零点六六市亩）七点五石（每日石约合中国的一点八石），这个数目远远超过日本去年的大米最高产量——每反六石。
    他强调指出，今后必须把这种种植法推广到广大农民中去，以便收到更多的大米。
    会上，与会者交流了应用米丘林学说种植其他各种农作物的经验。
