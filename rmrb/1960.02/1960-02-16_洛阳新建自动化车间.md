Title: New Automated Workshop Under Construction in Luoyang
Author:
Date: 1960-02-16
Page: 2

    New Automated Workshop Under Construction in Luoyang
    The Luoyang Bearing Factory is starting construction on a comprehensive automated workshop. Its production process, from material feeding, turning, and grinding, all the way to oiling and packaging, will be entirely completed by continuous production assembly lines.

### 洛阳新建自动化车间

1960-02-16
第2版()
专栏：

    洛阳新建自动化车间
    洛阳轴承厂正在动工兴建一个综合性自动化车间。它的生产过程，从投料、车、磨加工，直到涂油、包装等，将全部由连续生产自动线来完成。
