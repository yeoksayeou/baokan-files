Title: Friendship Festival Talks Friendship: Sino-Soviet Friendship People's Commune Holds Celebrations
Author:
Date: 1960-02-16
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency, February 15th: The Sino-Soviet Friendship People's Commune held a rally today with 1,000 participants, celebrating the tenth anniversary of the signing of the Sino-Soviet Treaty of Friendship, Alliance and mutual Assistance.

    The commune resounded with the sounds of gongs, drums, and firecrackers. Commune members played joyful folk music, lining the streets to welcome Soviet guests: Soviet Ambassador to China, Chervonenko, and his wife, head of the delegation Ye Liujin and all members of the Soviet-Chinese Friendship Association delegation led by him, as well as staff members of the Soviet Embassy in China.

    Sun Zhenying, Secretary of the Party Committee of the Sino-Soviet Friendship People's Commune, spoke at the celebration meeting, expressing heartfelt gratitude to the Soviet experts for their guidance and assistance in the commune's forestry and animal husbandry production.

    Soviet Ambassador to China, Chervonenko, spoke at the meeting, saying: The name of the Sino-Soviet Friendship People's Commune is a symbol of the brotherly and unbreakable solidarity of the great peoples of China and the Soviet Union. The Soviet people feel boundless joy and heartfelt congratulations for the remarkable achievements of their sincere friends and brothers—the great Chinese people.

    Ye Liujin, head of the Soviet-Chinese Friendship Association delegation, and member Kerimov also spoke at the meeting. They congratulated all members of the Sino-Soviet Friendship People's Commune on the achievements they had made in production.

    Then, Ambassador Chervonenko and head of delegation Ye Liujin presented gifts such as television sets and satellite models to the Sino-Soviet Friendship People's Commune. Sun Zhenying presented peanuts, floss, and Beijing picture albums produced by the commune to Ambassador Chervonenko and head of delegation Ye Liujin.

    Accompanied by Sun Zhenying and other leaders of the commune, the distinguished guests visited the commune's tractor station, fish ponds, livestock farms, kindergartens, and homes for the elderly.

    The distinguished guests had lunch in the commune's farmers' canteen.

    Vice Minister of Agriculture, Cai Ziwei, Director of the Rural Work Department of the Beijing Municipal Committee of the Communist Party of China, Zhao Fan, and Deputy Director of the Soviet-European Department of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs, Chen Boqing, also attended today's celebration meeting.

    In the afternoon, one hundred members of the Sino-Soviet Friendship People's Commune, at the invitation of Soviet Ambassador to China, Chervonenko, went to the Soviet Embassy in China to have dinner with the staff of the Soviet Embassy and watched films produced by China and the Soviet Union.

### 友谊节日话友谊  中苏友好人民公社展开庆祝活动

1960-02-16
第4版()
专栏：

    友谊节日话友谊
    中苏友好人民公社展开庆祝活动
    新华社15日讯　中苏友好人民公社今天举行有一千人参加的大会，庆祝中苏友好同盟互助条约签订十周年。
    公社里锣鼓鞭炮齐鸣。社员们奏着欢乐的民间乐曲，夹道欢迎苏联客人——苏联驻华大使契尔沃年科和他的夫人，叶留金团长和由他率领的苏中友协代表团的全体团员，以及苏联驻华大使馆的工作人员。
    中苏友好人民公社党委书记孙振英在庆祝大会上讲话，对苏联专家指导和帮助他们公社的林业和畜牧业生产，表示衷心的感谢。
    苏联驻华大使契尔沃年科在会上讲了话，他说：中苏友好人民公社的名字，就是中苏两国伟大人民的兄弟般的、牢不可破的团结的象征。苏联人民对于他们真诚的朋友和兄弟——伟大中国人民的卓越成就感到无限的愉快和衷心的祝贺。
    苏中友好协会代表团团长叶留金和团员凯里莫夫也在会上讲了话。他们对中苏友好人民公社全体社员在生产上所取得的成就表示祝贺。
    接着，契尔沃年科大使和叶留金团长把电视机和卫星模型等礼品赠送给中苏友好人民公社。孙振英把公社出产的花生、绒花以及北京画册赠送给契尔沃年科大使和叶留金团长。
    在孙振英和公社其他领导人陪同下，贵宾们参观了公社的拖拉机站、养鱼池、畜牧场、幼儿园和敬老院。
    贵宾们在公社的农民食堂进了午餐。
    农业部副部长蔡子伟，中共北京市委员会农村工作部部长赵凡、外交部苏欧司副司长陈伯清也参加了今天的庆祝大会。
    下午，中苏友好人民公社的一百名社员，应苏联驻华大使契尔沃年科的邀请，到苏联驻华大使馆同苏联大使馆工作人员共进晚餐，并且看了中苏两国拍制的电影。
