Title: Mikoyan Leaves Norway, Returns to Moscow
Author:
Date: 1960-02-18
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, 17th: TASS reports: After a two-day visit to Norway, Soviet First Deputy Premier Mikoyan left Oslo by plane on the afternoon of the 16th and returned to Moscow.

Before leaving Norway, Mikoyan issued a statement at Oslo Airport, thanking the Norwegian government and people for inviting him to visit, and expressed his hope that the friendship between the people of the Soviet Union and Norway would be strengthened and developed.

Norwegian Prime Minister Gerhardsen also spoke at the airport.

### 米高扬离挪威回到莫斯科

1960-02-18
第6版()
专栏：

    米高扬离挪威回到莫斯科
    据新华社17日讯　据塔斯社消息：苏联部长会议第一副主席米高扬在挪威访问了两天后，16日下午乘飞机离开奥斯陆回到了莫斯科。
    米高扬在离开挪威前在奥斯陆机场发表声明，对挪威政府和人民邀请他访问表示感谢，并且表示希望苏联和挪威两国人民的友谊得到加强和发展。
    挪威首相基哈德森也在机场上讲了话。
