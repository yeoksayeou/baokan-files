Title: Natural Gas Power Plant of Gonglu Commune (Picture)
Author: Tang Yunren
Date: 1960-02-21
Page: 7

    Natural Gas Power Plant of Gonglu Commune
    The Gonglu People's Commune in Chuansha County has built a 55-kilowatt natural gas power plant using natural gas. In the evening, the canteen is brightly lit with electric lights. This is a scene of commune members watching their children perform songs and dances after work.
    Xinhua News Agency reporter Tang Yunren

### 龚路公社的天然气电厂（图片）
唐允仁
1960-02-21

专栏：

    龚路公社的天然气电厂
    川沙县龚路人民公社，利用天然气办起了一座五十五瓩的天然气发电厂。晚上，食堂里电灯明亮。这是社员们在劳动之余看孩子们表演歌舞
    新华社记者　唐允仁摄
