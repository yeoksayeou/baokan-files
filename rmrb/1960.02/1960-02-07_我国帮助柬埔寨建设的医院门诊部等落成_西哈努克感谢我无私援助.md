Title: China Helps Cambodia Build Hospital Outpatient Department, etc., Completed; Sihanouk Thanks China for Its Unselfish Assistance
Author:
Date: 1960-02-07
Page: 3
Xinhua News Agency, Phnom Penh, 4th. Cambodian Prime Minister Prince Sihanouk attended the completion ceremonies on the 3rd of the Siem Reap Hospital outpatient department and the primary school superintendent's office, which were built with funds provided by China.

Chinese Ambassador to Cambodia Wang Youping and Commercial Counselor Ye Jinghao were also invited to attend the two ceremonies held in Siem Reap.

At the two ceremonies, Prince Sihanouk and Ambassador Wang Youping both spoke. Prince Sihanouk expressed his gratitude to the People's Republic of China for providing unselfish assistance. He also believed that the Cambodian people must learn from the example of the People's Republic of China and work hard for the people and for peace.

Ambassador Wang Youping pointed out that the friendly cooperative relations between Cambodia and China will continue to develop because the friendly cooperation between China and Cambodia is based on the lofty principles of peaceful coexistence.

Prince Sihanouk held a banquet on the evening of the 3rd to entertain the Chinese guests.

### 我国帮助柬埔寨建设的医院门诊部等落成  西哈努克感谢我无私援助

1960-02-07
第3版()
专栏：

    我国帮助柬埔寨建设的医院门诊部等落成
    西哈努克感谢我无私援助
    新华社金边4日电　柬埔寨首相西哈努克亲王3日参加了用中国提供的资金建造的暹粒医院门诊部和小学督学办公室落成典礼。
    中国驻柬埔寨大使王幼平和商务参赞叶景灏也应邀参加了在暹粒举行的这两个仪式。
    在这两个仪式上，西哈努克亲王和王幼平大使都讲了话。西哈努克亲王表示感谢中华人民共和国提供无私的援助。他还认为，柬埔寨人民必须学习中华人民共和国的榜样，为人民和和平而努力工作。
    王幼平大使指出，柬埔寨和中国之间的友好合作关系将不断得到发展，因为中柬两国的友好合作是基于和平共处的崇高原则的。
    西哈努克亲王在3日晚上曾经举行宴会招待中国客人。
