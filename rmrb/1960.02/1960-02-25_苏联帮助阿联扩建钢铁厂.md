Title: The Soviet Union Helps the United Arab Republic Expand Steel Plant
Author: 
Date: 1960-02-25
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, February 22nd: The United Arab Republic is expanding the Helwan Steel Plant with the technical assistance of the Soviet Union. This will be the largest project in the United Arab Republic after the Aswan Dam.

	In the future, the Helwan Steel Plant will be able to produce 1 million to 1.2 million tons of steel annually.

### 苏联帮助阿联扩建钢铁厂

1960-02-25
第5版()
专栏：

    苏联帮助阿联扩建钢铁厂
    新华社23日讯　据塔斯社莫斯科22日讯：阿拉伯联合共和国正在苏联的技术帮助下扩建赫万钢铁厂。这将是仅次于阿斯旺水坝的阿拉伯联合共和国最大的一项工程。
    将来，赫万钢铁厂每年将能炼出一百万到一百二十万吨钢。
