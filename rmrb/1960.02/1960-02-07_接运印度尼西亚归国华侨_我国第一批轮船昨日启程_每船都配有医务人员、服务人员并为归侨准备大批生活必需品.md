Title: Ships Sent to Indonesia to Bring Back Overseas Chinese; First Group of Ships Departed Yesterday; Each Ship Equipped with Medical Staff, Service Personnel, and Large Quantities of Necessities Prepared for Returned Overseas Chinese

Author:

Date: 1960-02-07
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Guangzhou, 6th: The first group of four ships dispatched by the Chinese government to Indonesia to bring back overseas Chinese have departed from Huangpu Port in Guangdong this morning, with the other two scheduled to depart tomorrow.

Yesterday morning, more than a thousand returned overseas Chinese, relatives of overseas Chinese, and returned overseas Chinese students in Guangzhou held a meeting at Huangpu Port to bid farewell to the staff accompanying the ships. Zhong Qingfa, Director of the General Office of the Overseas Chinese Affairs Commission of the People's Republic of China, and Lin Qiangyun, Vice Governor of Guangdong Province, spoke at the meeting on behalf of the Reception and Settlement Committee for Returned Overseas Chinese of the People's Republic of China and the People's Committee of Guangdong Province, respectively. They requested all accompanying staff members to overcome difficulties, conscientiously do a good job in receiving returned overseas Chinese, and complete the important task entrusted by the motherland. Wang Zhengqian, the representative of the accompanying staff, said in his speech that he would never fail the trust of the motherland and would certainly do a good job in receiving the returnees. After the speeches, returned overseas Chinese, relatives of overseas Chinese, and returned overseas Chinese students participating in the meeting set off firecrackers and danced the lion dance, escorting the accompanying staff all the way from the venue to the dock.

These four ships going to Indonesia to bring back overseas Chinese are all equipped with doctors, nurses, and service personnel, and have prepared a large number of necessities such as cotton clothing, quilts, food, and medicine for the returned overseas Chinese.

### 接运印度尼西亚归国华侨  我国第一批轮船昨日启程  每船都配有医务人员、服务人员并为归侨准备大批生活必需品

1960-02-07
第1版()
专栏：

    接运印度尼西亚归国华侨
    我国第一批轮船昨日启程
    每船都配有医务人员、服务人员并为归侨准备大批生活必需品
    新华社广州6日电　我国政府派往印度尼西亚接运华侨回国的第一批四艘轮船，其中两艘已经在今天早晨从广东黄浦港启程前往印度尼西亚，另外两艘将在明天启程。
    昨天上午，广州市归国华侨、侨眷和归侨学生一千多人在黄浦港举行大会，欢送随船的工作人员。中华人民共和国华侨事务委员会办公厅主任钟庆发和广东省副省长林锵云，分别代表中华人民共和国接待和安置归国华侨委员会和广东省人民委员会，先后在会上讲话，要求全体随船工作人员排除困难，认真做好接待归侨工作，完成祖国托付的重任。随船工作人员代表王正乾在讲话中表示，决不辜负祖国的重托，一定做好接待工作。讲话以后，参加大会的归侨、侨眷和归侨学生，鸣放鞭炮，舞着瑞狮，把随船工作人员一直从会场送到码头。
    这四艘前往印度尼西亚接运华侨回国的轮船，都配备有医生、护士和服务人员，并给归侨准备了大批棉衣、棉被、食物、药物等生活必需品。
