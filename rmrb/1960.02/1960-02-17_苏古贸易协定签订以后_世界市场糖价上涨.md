Title: Sugar Prices Rise in World Market After Signing of Soviet-Cuban Trade Agreement
Author:
Date: 1960-02-17
Page: 6

    Xinhua News Agency, Havana, 15th: According to Havana newspapers, world sugar prices have risen as a result of the signing of the Cuban-Soviet trade agreement in which the Soviet Union purchases Cuban sugar. On the 11th, before the agreement was signed, the listed price of sugar was 2.92 cents per pound. Today, the opening price of sugar is 3.04 cents, an increase of 0.12 cents.

### 苏古贸易协定签订以后  世界市场糖价上涨

1960-02-17
第6版()
专栏：

    苏古贸易协定签订以后
    世界市场糖价上涨
    据新华社哈瓦那15日电　据哈瓦那报纸报道，由于签订了苏联购买古巴食糖的古—苏贸易协定，世界市场上的糖价上涨了。11日，在协定签订之前，糖的牌价为每磅二点九二分美元。今天，糖市场的开盘价格是三点零四分美元，增加了零点一二分美元。
