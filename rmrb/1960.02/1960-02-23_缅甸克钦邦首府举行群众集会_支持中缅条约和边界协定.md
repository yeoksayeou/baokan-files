Title: Mass Rally Held in the Capital of Kachin State, Burma, in Support of the Sino-Burmese Treaty and Border Agreement
Author:
Date: 1960-02-23
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, Rangoon, February 22nd. A mass rally of approximately 3,000 people was held in Myitkyina, the capital of Kachin State, Burma, on the 21st, expressing firm support for the agreement on the Sino-Burmese border issue and the Sino-Burmese Treaty of Friendship and Non-Aggression recently signed by the governments of the two countries.

　　U Shin, the Deputy Commissioner of Myitkyina, emphasized the great significance of the Sino-Burmese agreement and treaty at the meeting.

　　Representatives of the Kachin and Shan ethnic groups, as well as representatives of Burmese and Chinese people in Myitkyina, also expressed their support for the Sino-Burmese treaty and agreement at the meeting. They pointed out that the signing of the treaty and agreement has opened a new chapter in Sino-Burmese history.

### 缅甸克钦邦首府举行群众集会  支持中缅条约和边界协定

1960-02-23
第6版()
专栏：

    缅甸克钦邦首府举行群众集会
    支持中缅条约和边界协定
    新华社仰光22日电　缅甸克钦邦首府密支那在21日举行了有大约三千人参加的群众集会，表示坚决支持缅中两国政府最近签订的关于两国边界问题的协定和缅中友好和互不侵犯条约。
    密支那副专员吴信在大会上强调指出了缅中协定和条约的巨大意义。
    出席这次大会的克钦族和掸族的代表以及在密支那的缅甸人和中国人的代表也在会上表示支持缅中条约和协定。他们指出，条约和协定的签订在缅中历史上揭开了新的一页。
