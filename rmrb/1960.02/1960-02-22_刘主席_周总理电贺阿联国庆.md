Title: Chairman Liu and Premier Zhou Send Congratulatory Messages on United Arab Republic's National Day
Author:
Date: 1960-02-22
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, February 21st—Chairman of the People's Republic of China, Liu Shaoqi, sent a telegram to President Nasser of the United Arab Republic, congratulating him on the UAR's National Day. The telegram reads as follows:

To His Excellency Gamal Abdel Nasser,
President of the United Arab Republic:

On the occasion of the National Day of the United Arab Republic, I would like to extend my heartfelt congratulations to Your Excellency and the people of the United Arab Republic on behalf of the Chinese people and in my own name. I wish your country prosperity and your people happiness.

					Liu Shaoqi
					Chairman of the People's Republic of China
					February 19, 1960

Xinhua News Agency, February 21st—Premier of the State Council of the People's Republic of China, Zhou Enlai, sent a telegram to President Nasser of the United Arab Republic, congratulating him on the UAR's National Day. The telegram reads as follows:

To His Excellency Gamal Abdel Nasser,
President of the United Arab Republic:

On the happy occasion of your country's National Day, I would like to extend my sincere congratulations to Your Excellency and the government of the United Arab Republic on behalf of the government of the People's Republic of China and in my own name.

The people of China and the UAR have a profound tradition of friendship. In recent years, our two governments and peoples have established friendly relations in the common struggle

### 刘主席　周总理电贺阿联国庆

1960-02-22
第1版()
专栏：

    刘主席　周总理电贺阿联国庆
    新华社21日讯　中华人民共和国主席刘少奇打电报给阿拉伯联合共和国总统纳赛尔，祝贺阿联国庆。电文如下：
    阿拉伯联合共和国加麦尔·阿卜杜勒·纳赛尔总统阁下：
    值此阿拉伯联合共和国国庆之际，我谨代表中国人民并且以我个人的名义向阁下和阿拉伯联合共和国人民表示衷心的祝贺。祝贵国国家繁荣，人民幸福。
                                中华人民共和国主席　刘少奇
                                        一九六○年二月十九日
    新华社21日讯　中华人民共和国国务院总理周恩来打电报给阿拉伯联合共和国总统纳赛尔，祝贺阿联国庆。电文如下：
    阿拉伯联合共和国加麦尔·阿卜杜勒·纳赛尔总统阁下：
    欣逢贵国国庆，我谨代表中华人民共和国政府并且以我个人的名义向阁下和阿拉伯联合共和国政府致以诚挚的祝贺。
    中、阿两国人民有着深厚的传统友谊，近几年来，我们两国政府和人民在反对殖民主义和帝国主义的共同斗争中建立了友好的关系，我们热诚地希望，这种关系在万隆会议十项原则的指导下将获得巩固和发展。祝阿拉伯联合共和国人民在建设自己的国家、维护亚洲、非洲和世界和平的事业中获得新的成就。
                            中华人民共和国国务院总理　周恩来
                                        一九六○年二月十九日
