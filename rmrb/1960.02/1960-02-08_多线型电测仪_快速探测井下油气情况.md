Title: Multi-Line Electrical Logging Instrument: Quickly Detect Oil and Gas Conditions Downhole
Author:
Date: 1960-02-08
Page: 2

Xinhua News Agency, Xining Electric: Employees of the Logging Station of the Drilling Division of the Qinghai Petroleum Administration have recently successfully modified the "May 1st Type Electric Logging Instrument" into a "Multi-Line Electrical Logging Instrument."

    After this modification, the understanding of oil, gas, and water layers and formation conditions downhole can be accelerated, so that electrical logging equipment can better play its role and achieve the requirements of economy, safety, and high efficiency.

### 多线型电测仪  快速探测井下油气情况

1960-02-08
第2版()
专栏：

    多线型电测仪
    快速探测井下油气情况
    据新华社西宁电  青海石油管理局钻井处测井总站的职工，最近成功地把“五一型电测仪”改装成了“多线型电测仪”。
    经过这样的改装，可以加速对井下油、气、水层及地层情况的了解，使电测设备更好地发挥作用，达到经济、安全、高工效的要求。
