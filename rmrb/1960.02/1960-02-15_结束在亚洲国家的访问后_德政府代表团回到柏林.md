Title: German Government Delegation Returns to Berlin After Concluding Visit to Asian Countries
Author:
Date: 1960-02-15
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, Berlin, February 13th: The government delegation of the German Democratic Republic, headed by Deputy Prime Minister Heinrich Rau, returned to Berlin on the afternoon of the 13th, after visiting Asian countries including Mongolia, China, North Korea, Vietnam, Burma, and India.

Those who welcomed the delegation at the airport included Deputy Prime Minister and Foreign Minister of the German Democratic Republic, Lothar Bolz. Envoys from China and other socialist countries to the German Democratic Republic also welcomed the delegation at the airport.

### 结束在亚洲国家的访问后  德政府代表团回到柏林

1960-02-15
第5版()
专栏：

    结束在亚洲国家的访问后
    德政府代表团回到柏林
    新华社柏林13日电　以副总理海因里希·劳为首的德意志民主共和国政府代表团，在访问了蒙古、中国、朝鲜、越南、缅甸、印度等亚洲国家之后，13日下午回到柏林。
    到机场欢迎的有德意志民主共和国副总理兼外交部长博尔茨等。中国和其他社会主义国家驻德意志民主共和国使节也到机场欢迎。
