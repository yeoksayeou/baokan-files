Title: China and Poland Sign Cultural Cooperation Agreement Implementation Plan
Author:
Date: 1960-02-12
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, 11th - The 1960-1961 implementation plan for the cultural cooperation agreement between the People's Republic of China and the People's Republic of Poland was signed in Beijing this afternoon. Zhang Zhixiang, head of the Chinese cultural delegation and Deputy Director of the Commission for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries, signed on behalf of the Chinese side, and Fularato, Chargé d'Affaires ad interim of the Polish Embassy in China, signed on behalf of the Polish side.

The 1960-1961 plan includes projects in culture, art, education, science, news, broadcasting, publishing, and exhibitions. After the signing, Zhang Zhixiang and Fularato spoke successively, both warmly praising the increasing enhancement and development of Sino-Polish cultural exchange. A reception was held after the signing.

### 中波签订文化合作协定执行计划

1960-02-12
第5版()
专栏：

    中波签订文化合作协定执行计划
    新华社11日讯　中华人民共和国和波兰人民共和国文化合作协定1960—1961年执行计划，今天下午在北京签字。代表中国方面签字的是中国文化代表团团长、对外文化联络委员会副主任张致祥，代表波兰方面签字的是波兰文化代表团团长、波兰人民共和国驻中国大使馆临时代办傅拉托。
    1960—1961年的计划包括文化、艺术、教育、科学、新闻、广播、出版、展览等项目。签字以后，张致祥和傅拉托先后讲话，他们都热烈赞扬中波文化交流的日益增强和发展。签字后并举行了酒会。
