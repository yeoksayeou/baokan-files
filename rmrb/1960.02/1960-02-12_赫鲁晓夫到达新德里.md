Title: Khrushchev Arrives in New Delhi
Author:
Date: 1960-02-12
Page: 5

    Xinhua News Agency, New Delhi, February 11th: At the invitation of the Indian government, Soviet Chairman of the Council of Ministers Khrushchev arrived in New Delhi from Tashkent this morning aboard an "Il-18" aircraft for a five-day visit to India.
    Welcoming Khrushchev at the airport were Indian President Prasad, Vice President Radhakrishnan, Prime Minister Nehru, ministers, social figures, leaders of various political parties and people's organizations, as well as diplomatic envoys from various countries to India, and several thousand citizens.
    In his welcoming speech at the airport, Prasad emphasized the friendship between India and the Soviet Union, saying, "Our cooperation in economic and industrial development has begun to bear fruit."
    Amid cheers, Khrushchev delivered a reply, expressing his gratitude to the Indian government for inviting him to visit India.
    He said that he hoped that after this visit to India, Indian national activists would also visit the Soviet Union. He emphasized the significance of direct contact between national leaders.
    He hoped that "cooperation between the Soviet Union and India will develop and strengthen year after year, to benefit the people of our two countries and to promote peace throughout the world."
    As Khrushchev and his party drove from the airport along the thirteen-mile road, about one million Delhi citizens and suburban farmers lined the streets to welcome them.
    Xinhua News Agency, February 11th: When Soviet Chairman of the Council of Ministers Khrushchev flew over our country on his way to visit India, he sent a telegram to the leaders of our country and our people, expressing brotherly respect and wishing them new and greater achievements in socialist construction.

### 赫鲁晓夫到达新德里

1960-02-12
第5版()
专栏：

    赫鲁晓夫到达新德里
    新华社新德里11日电　苏联部长会议主席赫鲁晓夫应印度政府的邀请，今天上午乘“伊尔—十八式”飞机从塔什干到达新德里，他将在印度作为期五天的访问。
    到机场欢迎赫鲁晓夫的有印度总统普拉沙德、副总统拉达克里希南、总理尼赫鲁、部长们、社会名流、各政党和人民团体的领导人以及各国驻印度使节以及几千市民。
    普拉沙德在机场致欢迎词时强调印度和苏联之间的友谊，他说，“我们在经济和工业发展方面的合作已经开始结下硕果。”
    赫鲁晓夫在欢呼声中致答词时，表示感谢印度政府邀请他访问印度。
    他说，他希望这次访问印度之后，印度的国家活动家也到苏联去访问。他强调国家领导人直接接触的意义。
    他希望，“苏联和印度之间的合作年复一年地发展和加强，以造福我们两国的人民和有利于全世界的和平。”
    在赫鲁晓夫等一行从机场驱车通过十三英里长的道路时，两旁约有一百万德里市民和郊区农民夹道欢迎。
    据新华社11日讯　苏联部长会议主席赫鲁晓夫在前往印度访问途中飞经我国上空时，打电报向我国国家领导人和我国人民致兄弟的敬意，并祝在社会主义建设中获得新的更大的成就。
