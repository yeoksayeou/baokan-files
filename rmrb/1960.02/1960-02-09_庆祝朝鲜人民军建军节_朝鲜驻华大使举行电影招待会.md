Title: The North Korean Ambassador to China Hosts a Film Reception to Celebrate the Anniversary of the Founding of the Korean People's Army
Author:
Date: 1960-02-09
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, February 8th. The Ambassador of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea to China, Li Yong-ho, held a film reception this afternoon to celebrate the 12th anniversary of the founding of the Korean People's Army. The feature film "Loving Tomorrow," produced by the Korean National Art Film Studio, was screened at the reception.

General Zhang Aiping, Deputy Chief of the General Staff of the Chinese People's Liberation Army, and more than 500 officers from the army, navy, and air force stationed in Beijing were invited to attend the reception.

Also invited to attend the reception were Vice Chairman of the CPPCC, Burhan; Vice Minister of Foreign Affairs, Ji Pengfei; Vice Minister of Foreign Trade, Jiang Ming; Deputy Director of the Commission for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries, Zhang Zhixiang; Vice Mayors of Beijing, Wang Kunlun and Le Songsheng; President of the Sino-Korean Friendship Association, Li Dequan; Vice Presidents Lao She and Wei Chuantong; and more than 1,000 responsible persons from other government departments and mass organizations.

Military attachés from various countries stationed in China also attended the reception.

### 庆祝朝鲜人民军建军节  朝鲜驻华大使举行电影招待会

1960-02-09
第4版()
专栏：

    庆祝朝鲜人民军建军节
    朝鲜驻华大使举行电影招待会
    新华社8日讯　今天下午，朝鲜民主主义人民共和国驻华大使李永镐举行电影招待会，庆祝朝鲜人民军建军十二周年。会上放映了朝鲜国立艺术电影制片厂拍制的故事片“热爱明天”。
    中国人民解放军副总参谋长张爱萍上将和驻京陆海空军五百多名军官应邀出席了招待会。
    应邀出席招待会的还有政协副主席包尔汉，外交部副部长姬鹏飞，对外贸易部副部长江明，对外文化联络委员会副主任张致祥，北京市副市长王昆仑、乐松生，中朝友好协会会长李德全、副会长老舍、魏传统，以及政府其他部门和群众团体的负责人等一千多人。
    各国驻华武官也出席了招待会。
