Title: Lanzhou Begins Construction of Locomotive Plant
Author:
Date: 1960-02-16
Page: 2

Lanzhou Begins Construction of Locomotive Plant

	The Lanzhou Locomotive Plant recently commenced construction.

	After the completion of the first phase of this plant, it will undertake major locomotive overhauls in Northwest China. In the future, it will also manufacture diesel locomotives according to national needs.

### 兰州兴建机车厂

1960-02-16
第2版()
专栏：

    兰州兴建机车厂
    兰州机车厂已在最近动工兴建。
    这座工厂第一期工程完工之后，将担负起我国西北地区的机车大修任务，以后还将根据国家需要制造内燃机车。
