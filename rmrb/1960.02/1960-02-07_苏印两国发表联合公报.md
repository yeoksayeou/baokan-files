Title: Sino-Indian Joint Communiqué Published
Author: 
Date: 1960-02-07
Page: 3

Xinhua News Agency, Delhi, 6th. TASS reported that the Soviet-Indian Joint Communiqué was published on the 6th, at the conclusion of the visit to India by the Chairman of the Presidium of the Supreme Soviet of the USSR, Voroshilov, and his party.

The communiqué stated that Voroshilov, First Deputy Chairman of the Council of Ministers of the USSR, Kozlov, and Soviet Supreme Soviet representative Furtseva, during their visit to India, met with Indian President Prasad, Prime Minister Nehru, and other members of the Indian government, and held talks with them in a friendly and sincere atmosphere. Their talks with Prime Minister Nehru touched upon issues of mutual concern to both countries, including various fundamental issues in the current international situation, and also covered India's current and future development plans.

The communiqué said that both sides expressed satisfaction with the recent easing of international tensions and welcomed the agreement to hold a summit meeting in May of this year. India and the Soviet Union both expressed their heartfelt hope that the upcoming meeting will achieve significant progress in solving the various problems facing the world today, thereby paving the way for further successes in the common efforts of all countries.

The communiqué continued by saying that both sides emphasized the importance of disarmament, considering it an essential prerequisite for achieving lasting peace and eliminating the fear of war. The communiqué said that both sides reviewed the economic and cultural relations between the two countries. In order to further develop cultural relations between the two countries, a cultural and scientific agreement will be signed between the two countries.

The communiqué concluded by saying that Voroshilov, Kozlov, and Furtseva had the opportunity to exchange their views with Nehru and declared their two countries' common determination to work unremittingly for world peace and better international understanding among all nations. The communiqué stated that Voroshilov invited President Prasad to pay a return visit to the Soviet Union in 1960. This invitation was gratefully accepted.

### 苏印两国发表联合公报

1960-02-07
第3版()
专栏：

    苏印两国发表联合公报
    据新华社6日讯　据塔斯社德里讯，苏联最高苏维埃主席团主席伏罗希洛夫等一行6日访问印度结束时发表了苏印联合公报。
    公报说，伏罗希洛夫，苏联部长会议第一副主席科兹洛夫和苏联最高苏维埃代表福尔采娃在访问印度期间曾经会见了印度总统普拉沙德、总理尼赫鲁和印度政府其他成员，并且在友好诚挚的气氛中同他们举行了会谈。他们同尼赫鲁总理的会谈涉及两国共同关心的问题，包括目前国际局势中的各种基本问题，并且还谈到印度目前和将来的发展计划。
    公报说，双方对最近国际紧张局势有了某些缓和表示满意，并且对于就今年5月举行最高级会议达成协议表示欢迎。印度和苏联双方表示它们衷心希望，即将举行的这次会议能在解决世界今天面临的各种问题方面获得重大进展，从而为各国共同努力求得更进一步的成功铺平道路。
    公报接着说，双方强调指出裁军的重要性，认为它是实现持久和平消除战争恐惧的必不可少的先决条件。公报说，双方回顾了两国之间的经济和文化关系。为了进一步发展两国之间的文化关系，两国之间将签订文化和科学协定。
    公报最后说，伏罗希洛夫、科兹洛夫和福尔采娃同尼赫鲁有机会交换了他们的观点，并且宣布他们两国共同决心不中断地为谋求世界和平和各国之间更好的国际谅解而努力。公报说，伏罗希洛夫邀请普拉沙德总统在1960年回访苏联。这个邀请已被欣然接受。
