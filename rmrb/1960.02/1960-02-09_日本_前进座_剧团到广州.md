Title: Japanese "Zenshinza" Theater Troupe Arrives in Guangzhou
Author:
Date: 1960-02-09
Page: 4

　　Xinhua News Agency, Guangzhou, 7th. At the invitation of the Chinese People's Association for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries, the Japanese "Zenshinza" Theater Troupe, a group of sixty-eight people led by troupe leader Chojuro Kawarasaki, deputy troupe leader Kan'emon Nakamura, and Masaaki Miyagawa, arrived in Guangzhou on the afternoon of the 7th.

　　Among those welcoming them at the station were Du Ai, Vice President of the Guangzhou Branch of the Chinese People's Association for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries, Zheng Da, Vice Chairman of the Guangzhou Branch of the Chinese Dramatists Association, Liang Lun, Head of the South China Song and Dance Troupe, and more than one hundred other figures from the literary and art circles.

　　After arriving in Guangzhou, the entire "Zenshinza" Theater Troupe visited scenic spots and historical sites such as Yuexiu Mountain and toured the city of Guangzhou.

　　In the evening, Du Guoxiang, President of the Guangzhou Branch of the Chinese People's Association for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries, held a banquet to welcome the Japanese artists.

　　The entire Japanese "Zenshinza" Theater Troupe will travel to Beijing by train on the 8th.

### 日本“前进座”剧团到广州

1960-02-09
第4版()
专栏：

    日本“前进座”剧团到广州
    新华社广州7日电　应中国人民对外文化协会邀请前来我国访问演出的日本“前进座”剧团一行六十八人，在团长河原崎长十郎、副团长中村翫右卫门、宫川雅睛的率领下，7日下午到达广州。
    到车站欢迎的有中国人民对外文化协会广州分会副会长杜埃、中国戏剧家协会广州分会副主席郑达、华南歌舞团团长梁伦等文艺界人士一百多人。
    “前进座”剧团全体人员到达广州后，曾游览了越秀山等名胜古迹和广州市容。
    晚上，中国人民对外文化协会广州分会会长杜国庠举行宴会，欢迎日本艺术家们。
    日本“前进座”剧团全体人员将在8日乘车赴北京。
