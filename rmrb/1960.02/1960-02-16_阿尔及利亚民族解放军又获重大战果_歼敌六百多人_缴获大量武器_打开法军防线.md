Title: Algerian National Liberation Army Achieves Another Major Victory: Annihilates Over 600 Enemy Soldiers, Seizes Large Quantities of Weapons, Breaches French Army Defenses
Author: 
Date: 1960-02-16
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, Cairo, February 14th: A military communique issued by the Supreme Command of the Algerian National Liberation Army on the night of the 14th stated that in military operations on the 9th and 10th, Algerian National Liberation Army commando units launched a series of attacks on French military barracks, killing 365 French soldiers and wounding 262.

According to the announcement, Algerian armed forces carried out thirteen ambushes against the French army during these two days and launched attacks on French military barracks in thirty-two villages and cities.

According to the announcement, during these two days, the Algerian National Liberation Army shot down five French military aircraft, destroyed an enemy train with landmines, and destroyed twenty-six military vehicles and armored vehicles. At the same time, Algerian nationalists destroyed many enemy utility poles throughout Algeria and opened a 155-meter gap in the power grid of the French "Morice Line" on the eastern border.

According to the announcement, during these two days, Algerian armed forces captured 208 weapons and a large quantity of ammunition, hand grenades, etc., and captured nine French soldiers. In addition, twenty-two French soldiers surrendered to the Algerian National Liberation Army.

### 阿尔及利亚民族解放军又获重大战果  歼敌六百多人　缴获大量武器　打开法军防线

1960-02-16
第6版()
专栏：

    阿尔及利亚民族解放军又获重大战果
    歼敌六百多人　缴获大量武器　打开法军防线
    新华社开罗14日电　阿尔及利亚民族解放军最高司令部在14日夜间发表的军事公报说，阿尔及利亚民族解放军突击队在9日和10日的军事行动中，对法军兵营发动了一连串的袭击，共打死法军三百六十五人，打伤二百六十二人。
    据宣布，阿尔及利亚武装部队在这两天中对法军进行了十三次伏击，并且在三十二个村庄和城市中对法军兵营发动了袭击。
    据宣布，在这两天中，阿尔及利亚民族解放军击落了五架法国军用飞机，用地雷炸毁了敌军的一列火车，并且击毁了二十六辆军用车和装甲车。同时，阿尔及利亚民族主义者在阿尔及利亚全境炸毁了敌军的许多电线杆，把东部边境法军“莫里斯防线”的电网打开了长达一百五十五米的缺口。
    据宣布，在这两天中，阿尔及利亚的武装部队缴获了二百零八件武器和大量弹药、手榴弹等，俘获了九名法军。另外，有二十二名法军向阿尔及利亚民族解放军投诚。
