Title: Presidium of the USSR Supreme Soviet Approves Soviet-Cuban Trade Agreement
Author:
Date: 1960-02-29
Page: 7

    Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, 28th: TASS reported from Moscow that the Presidium of the USSR Supreme Soviet approved on the 27th the agreement on exchange of goods and payments between the Soviet Union and the Republic of Cuba, as well as the agreement on the Soviet Union providing loans to Cuba.

    These two agreements were signed in Havana on February 13 during the visit of Soviet First Deputy Premier Mikoyan to Cuba.

### 苏联最高苏维埃主席团  批准苏古贸易协定

1960-02-29
第7版()
专栏：

    苏联最高苏维埃主席团
    批准苏古贸易协定
    新华社28日讯　据塔斯社莫斯科讯：苏联最高苏维埃主席团27日批准了苏联同古巴共和国的换货和支付协定，以及苏联向古巴提供贷款的协定。
    这两个协定是苏联部长会议第一副主席米高扬访问古巴时，2月13日在哈瓦那签订的。
