Title: Soviet-Chinese Friendship Association Delegation Arrives in Beijing to Attend Celebrations of the 10th Anniversary of the Sino-Soviet Alliance
Author: 
Date: 1960-02-13
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, 12th - The Soviet-Chinese Friendship Association delegation, led by Yelyutin, head of the delegation, Vice-Chairman of the Council of the Soviet-Chinese Friendship Association, and Minister of Higher and Secondary Specialized Education of the Soviet Union, arrived in Beijing by plane from Moscow this afternoon.

The delegation was invited by Song Qingling，President of the Sino-Soviet Friendship Association, to participate in the celebrations of the 10th anniversary of the signing of the Sino-Soviet Treaty of Friendship,Alliance and Mutual Assistance. The delegation members include Pokrovsky, Secretary of the Leningrad Regional Committee of the CPSU; Tikhvinsky, member of the Council of the Soviet-Chinese Friendship Association and Director of the Institute of Chinese Studies of the Soviet Academy of Sciences; Kerimov, drilling team leader of the "Durgyan Oil Company", representative of the Supreme Soviet of the Azerbaijan Soviet Socialist Republic, and Hero of Socialist Labor; and Ajidzrova, Vice-President of the Supreme Court of the Tajik Soviet Socialist Republic and representative of the Supreme Soviet of the Tajik Soviet Socialist Republic.

Those who welcomed the delegation at the airport included Liao Chengzhi, Vice-President of the Sino-Soviet Friendship Association; Li Dequan, Minister of Public Health and Vice-President of the Sino-Soviet Friendship Association; Shao Lizi, Vice-President of the Sino-Soviet Friendship Association; Qian Junrui, Vice-Minister of Culture and Secretary-General of the Sino-Soviet Friendship Association; Liu Aifeng, Vice-Minister of Education; Yang Shu, Vice-President of the Beijing Sino-Soviet Friendship Association; Cao Mengjun, member of the Presidium of the All-China Women's Federation; Ge Baoquan and Li Zhanwu, Deputy Secretaries-General of the Sino-Soviet Friendship Association, etc.

Antonov, V.I. Lichatchev and Sangfylou, First Secretaries of the Soviet Embassy in China, also welcomed the delegation at the airport.

### 应邀来我国参加中苏同盟十周年庆祝活动  苏中友好协会代表团到京

1960-02-13
第1版()
专栏：

    应邀来我国参加中苏同盟十周年庆祝活动
    苏中友好协会代表团到京
    新华社12日讯　由团长、苏中友好协会理事会副主席、苏联高等教育和中等专业教育部部长叶留金率领的苏中友好协会代表团一行五人，今天下午由莫斯科乘飞机到达北京。
    代表团是应中苏友好协会总会会长宋庆龄的邀请，来我国参加中苏友好同盟互助条约签订十周年庆祝活动的。团员有苏共列宁格勒州委会书记波克罗夫斯基，苏中友好协会理事会理事、苏联科学院中国问题研究所所长齐赫文斯基，“久尔格扬石油公司”钻探工段段长、阿塞拜疆苏维埃社会主义共和国最高苏维埃代表、社会主义劳动英雄凯里莫夫，塔吉克苏维埃社会主义共和国最高法院副院长、塔吉克苏维埃社会主义共和国最高苏维埃代表阿吉卓娃。
    到机场欢迎的有中苏友好协会总会副会长廖承志，卫生部部长、中苏友好协会总会副会长李德全，中苏友好协会总会副会长邵力子，文化部副部长、中苏友好协会总会秘书长钱俊瑞，教育部副部长刘皑风，北京市中苏友好协会副会长杨述，全国妇联主席团委员曹孟君，中苏友好协会总会副秘书长戈宝权、李沾吾等。
    苏联驻华大使馆参赞安东诺夫、魏立夏金、一等秘书桑飞楼也到机场欢迎。
