Title: Burmese Prime Minister Ne Win and Wife Host Banquet for Chinese Cultural and Song and Dance Troupes

Author:
Date: 1960-02-07
Page: 3

Xinhua News Agency, Rangoon, February 6th: Burmese Prime Minister General Ne Win and his wife held a banquet last night to entertain the entire delegation of the Chinese Cultural Friendship Delegation, headed by Chu Tunan, and the Chinese National Song and Dance Troupe, who are currently visiting here.

Burmese senior military officers and the Mayor of Rangoon, Colonel Tun Sein, attended the banquet. Chinese Ambassador to Burma, Li Yimang, was also invited to attend.

At the banquet, the hosts and guests had cordial conversations and toasted each other for Sino-Burmese friendship. The banquet proceeded in a very warm atmosphere.

Before the banquet, Prime Minister Ne Win and his wife watched a performance by the Chinese National Song and Dance Troupe for the Burmese Defense Forces, and they greatly appreciated the performance. More than a thousand senior officers and soldiers of the Burmese Defense Forces watched the performance. After each performance, the applause lasted for a long time.

After the performance, Prime Minister Ne Win and his wife went to the stage to present flower baskets to the Chinese artists and posed for photos with them amidst cheers and applause.

### 缅甸总理奈温和夫人  宴请我文化歌舞两代表团

1960-02-07
第3版()
专栏：

    缅甸总理奈温和夫人
    宴请我文化歌舞两代表团
    新华社仰光6日电　缅甸总理奈温将军及其夫人昨晚举行宴会，招待正在这里访问的以楚图南为首的中国文化友好代表团和中国民族歌舞团的全体团员。
    缅甸高级军官们和仰光市长顿盛上校出席了宴会。中国驻缅甸大使李一氓也应邀出席宴会。
    在宴会上，宾主亲切交谈，并且互相为中缅友好干杯。宴会在非常热烈的气氛中进行。
    在宴会前，奈温总理和总理夫人观看了中国民族歌舞团为缅甸国防军演出的节目，他们很欣赏这次演出。观看这次演出的有缅甸国防军的一千多位高级军官和士兵。每一个节目表演完毕后，掌声历久不息。
    在表演结束后，奈温总理和总理夫人到舞台上向中国艺术家们赠送了花篮，并在欢呼和掌声中同他们合影留念。
