Title: Scientists and Industrial Workers of Various Republics Cooperate: Another Soviet Atomic Reactor Begins Operation
Author:
Date: 1960-02-16
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, February 13th: TASS reports that the experimental atomic reactor of the Physics Institute of the Ukrainian Republic Academy of Sciences in the Soviet Union was successfully built with the support of scientists and industrial workers from various Soviet republics and began operation on the 12th. This reactor is considered one of the best in the Soviet Union.

With this reactor, scientists will be able to further develop neutron physics, study the interaction of neutrons with atomic nuclei, the effect of nuclear radiation on metals and semiconductors, and the effect of radioactive radiation on living organisms. The reactor will also be used to produce various isotopes widely used in production and scientific laboratories.

The control of this reactor is fully automated. There are approximately two thousand instruments and devices in its control system, with a total length of fifty-five kilometers of connecting cables and wires.

### 各共和国科学家和工业职工通力合作  苏联又一原子反应堆开始运转

1960-02-16
第5版()
专栏：

    各共和国科学家和工业职工通力合作
    苏联又一原子反应堆开始运转
    据新华社莫斯科13日电　据塔斯社报道：苏联乌克兰共和国科学院物理研究所的实验原子反应堆，在苏联各加盟共和国科学家和工业职工的支援下胜利建成，并在12日开始运转。这座反应堆被认为是苏联优秀反应堆之一。
    有了这座反应堆，科学家们将可以进一步发展中子物理学、研究中子同原子核的相互作用、核辐射对于金属和半导体的作用以及放射性辐射对于活机体的作用等。这座反应堆还将用来生产供生产和科学实验室中广泛运用的各种同位素。
    这座反应堆的控制是完全自动化的。在它的控制系统中一共约有两千个仪表和装置，其中的联结电缆和电线总长度就有五十五公里。
