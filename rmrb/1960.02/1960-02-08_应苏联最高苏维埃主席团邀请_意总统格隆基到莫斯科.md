Title: At the Invitation of the Presidium of the Supreme Soviet of the USSR, Italian President Gronchi Arrives in Moscow
Author:
Date: 1960-02-08
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, February 7th -- TASS reports: Italian President Gronchi arrived in Moscow by plane on the 6th, for an official visit to the Soviet Union at the invitation of the Presidium of the Supreme Soviet of the USSR.

The Soviet state leaders Voroshilov, Khrushchev, Kozlov, Furtseva, Kosygin, Ustinov, Organov, and others were at Vnukovo Airport to welcome him. Voroshilov and Gronchi exchanged welcoming and response speeches at the airport.

On the same day, President Gronchi paid visits to Voroshilov, Chairman of the Presidium of the Supreme Soviet, and Khrushchev, Chairman of the Council of Ministers of the Soviet Union.

On the evening of the 6th, the Presidium of the Supreme Soviet of the USSR held a banquet in the Kremlin to entertain President Gronchi and his wife, as well as their entourage. The Soviet side attending the banquet included Voroshilov, Khrushchev, and other leaders.

Italian Foreign Minister Bella also paid a visit to Soviet Foreign Minister Gromyko on the 6th. Bella came to Moscow as part of President Gronchi's official entourage.

Voroshilov, Chairman of the Presidium of the Supreme Soviet of the USSR, and Italian President Gronchi spoke successively at the banquet held by the Presidium of the Supreme Soviet of the USSR on the evening of the 6th.

### 应苏联最高苏维埃主席团邀请  意总统格隆基到莫斯科

1960-02-08
第5版()
专栏：

    应苏联最高苏维埃主席团邀请
    意总统格隆基到莫斯科
    新华社7日讯　据塔斯社莫斯科讯：意大利总统格隆基6日乘飞机抵达莫斯科。他是应苏联最高苏维埃主席团的邀请，来苏联作正式访问的。
    前往伏努科夫机场欢迎的有苏联国家领导人伏罗希洛夫、赫鲁晓夫、科兹洛夫、福尔采娃、柯西金、乌斯季诺夫、奥尔加诺夫等。伏罗希洛夫和格隆基在机场上互致欢迎词和答词。
    格隆基总统在当天先后拜会了最高苏维埃主席团主席伏罗希洛夫和苏联部长会议主席赫鲁晓夫。
    6日晚苏联最高苏维埃主席团在克里姆林宫设宴招待格隆基总统和夫人以及他们的随行人员。苏联方面出席宴会的有伏罗希洛夫、赫鲁晓夫以及其他领导人。
    意大利外交部长贝拉也在6日拜会了苏联外交部长葛罗米柯。贝拉是作为格隆基总统正式随行人员到莫斯科来的。
    苏联最高苏维埃主席团主席伏罗希洛夫和意大利总统格隆基，6日晚在苏联最高苏维埃主席团举行的宴会上先后讲了话。
