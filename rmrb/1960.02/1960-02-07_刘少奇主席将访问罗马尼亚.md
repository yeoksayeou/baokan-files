Title: Liu Shaoqi to Visit Romania
Author:
Date: 1960-02-07
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, 6th. Comrade Ion Gheorghe Maurer, Chairman of the Presidium of the Grand National Assembly of the Romanian People's Republic, and Comrade Chivu Stoica, Chairman of the Council of Ministers of the Romanian People's Republic, have invited Comrade Liu Shaoqi, Chairman of the People's Republic of China, to pay a friendly visit to the Romanian People's Republic at an appropriate time. Chairman Liu Shaoqi has accepted this invitation.

### 刘少奇主席将访问罗马尼亚

1960-02-07
第1版()
专栏：

    刘少奇主席将访问罗马尼亚
    新华社6日讯　罗马尼亚人民共和国大国民议会主席团主席扬·格·毛雷尔同志和罗马尼亚人民共和国部长会议主席基伏·斯托伊卡同志，邀请中华人民共和国主席刘少奇同志在适当时机到罗马尼亚人民共和国进行友好访问。刘少奇主席已经接受了这项邀请。
