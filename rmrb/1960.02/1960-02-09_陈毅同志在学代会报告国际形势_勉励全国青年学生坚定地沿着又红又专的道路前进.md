Title: Comrade Chen Yi Reports on the International Situation at the Student Congress, Encouraging National Youth and Students to Firmly Advance Along the Path of Being Both Red and Expert
Author:
Date: 1960-02-09
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, 8th. Chen Yi, member of the Political Bureau of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of China and Vice Premier of the State Council, delivered a report on the international situation at the 17th National Congress of the All-China Students' Federation today, and encouraged the national youth and students to firmly advance along the path of being both Red and expert.

More than 600 delegates attending the National Student Congress warmly welcomed Vice Premier Chen Yi's report with continuous applause and cheers.

### 陈毅同志在学代会报告国际形势  勉励全国青年学生坚定地沿着又红又专的道路前进

1960-02-09
第1版()
专栏：

    陈毅同志在学代会报告国际形势
    勉励全国青年学生坚定地沿着又红又专的道路前进
    新华社8日讯　中共中央政治局委员、国务院副总理陈毅今天在中华全国学生第十七届代表大会上作了关于国际形势的报告，并勉励全国青年学生坚定地沿着又红又专的道路前进。
    出席全国学代会的六百多名代表，以不断的掌声和欢呼声热烈欢迎陈毅副总理的报告。
