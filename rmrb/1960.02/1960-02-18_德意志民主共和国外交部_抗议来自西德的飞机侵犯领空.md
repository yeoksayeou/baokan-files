Title: Ministry of Foreign Affairs of the German Democratic Republic Protests West German Aircraft's Intrusion into Airspace
Author:
Date: 1960-02-18
Page: 5
Xinhua News Agency, Berlin, February 17th: According to an announcement by the Ministry of Foreign Affairs of the German Democratic Republic, at 10:26 AM on February 16th, a jet aircraft of unknown nationality intruded into the airspace of the German Democratic Republic from West Germany. This aircraft flew to a point 90 kilometers near Finanburg, at an altitude of 4,000 meters. Subsequently, it continued to fly in the airspace of the German Democratic Republic until 10:40 AM when it departed in the area of ​​North Coburg.
    The Ministry of Foreign Affairs of the German Democratic Republic strongly protested this new provocative violation of the airspace of the German Democratic Republic. The Ministry of Foreign Affairs pointed out that the purpose of such illegal activities can only be to obstruct the easing of international tensions. The Ministry of Foreign Affairs stated that the perpetrators of this intrusion should bear full responsibility for all the consequences arising therefrom.

### 德意志民主共和国外交部  抗议来自西德的飞机侵犯领空

1960-02-18
第5版()
专栏：

    德意志民主共和国外交部
    抗议来自西德的飞机侵犯领空
  　新华社柏林17日电　据德意志民主共和国外交部宣布，2月16日十时二十六分，一架不明国籍的喷气式飞机从西德侵入德意志民主共和国领空。这架飞机飞到了靠近菲南堡的九十公里的地方，飞行高度为四千米。随后，它仍继续在德意志民主共和国的领空飞行，直到十时四十分才在北科堡地区离去。
    德意志民主共和国外交部强烈抗议对德意志民主共和国领空的新的挑衅性的侵犯。外交部指出，这类非法活动的目的只能是阻挠国际紧张局势缓和。外交部说，这一侵犯的肇事者应对由此而产生的一切后果负完全责任。
