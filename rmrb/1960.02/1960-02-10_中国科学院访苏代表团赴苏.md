Title: Chinese Academy of Sciences Delegation Visits Soviet Union
Author:
Date: 1960-02-10
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, 9th. A five-member delegation from the Chinese Academy of Sciences, led by Pei Lisheng, Secretary-General of the Chinese Academy of Sciences, left by plane for Moscow this morning. The delegation will discuss and sign the 1960 cooperation plan between the two Academies of Sciences with the Soviet Academy of Sciences, and visit the research departments of the Soviet Academy of Sciences.

### 中国科学院访苏代表团赴苏

1960-02-10
第4版()
专栏：

    中国科学院访苏代表团赴苏
    新华社9日讯  中国科学院访苏代表团一行五人，在团长、中国科学院秘书长裴丽生率领下，今天早晨乘飞机去莫斯科。代表团将和苏联科学院商谈并签订两国科学院1960年度合作计划，并参观苏联科学院的研究部门。
