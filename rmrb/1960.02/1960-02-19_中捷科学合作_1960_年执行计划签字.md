Title: Sino-Czechoslovak Scientific Cooperation 1960 Implementation Plan Signed
Author:
Date: 1960-02-19
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, February 18th—The signing ceremony for the 1960 execution plan of scientific cooperation between the Chinese Academy of Sciences and the Czechoslovak Academy of Sciences was held in Beijing today.

    Signing on behalf of the Chinese Academy of Sciences was Wu Youxun, head of the Chinese Academy of Sciences delegation and Vice President of the Chinese Academy of Sciences. Signing on behalf of the Czechoslovak Academy of Sciences was Academician Prusík, head of the Czechoslovak Academy of Sciences delegation, Director of the Linguistics Department of the Czechoslovak Academy of Sciences, and Director of the Institute of Oriental Studies. In addition to all members of the two delegations, Guo Moruo, President of the Chinese Academy of Sciences, and Bušiňák, Czechoslovak Ambassador to China, and Lehmann, Cultural Counselor, also attended the signing ceremony.

    In the evening, Wu Youxun, Vice President of the Chinese Academy of Sciences, hosted a banquet to entertain the Czechoslovak Academy of Sciences delegation.

### 中捷科学合作1960年执行计划签字

1960-02-19
第4版()
专栏：

    中捷科学合作1960年执行计划签字
    新华社18日讯　中国科学院和捷克斯洛伐克科学院科学合作1960年执行计划签字仪式，今天在北京举行。
    代表中国科学院签字的是中国科学院代表团团长、中国科学院副院长吴有训，代表捷克斯洛伐克科学院签字的是捷克斯洛伐克科学院代表团团长、捷克斯洛伐克科学院语言学学部主任、东方学研究所所长普鲁司克院士。参加签字仪式的除双方代表团的全体成员外，还有中国科学院院长郭沫若和捷克斯洛伐克驻中国大使布希尼亚克、文化参赞雷曼等。
    晚上，中国科学院副院长吴有训举行宴会招待捷克斯洛伐克科学院代表团。
