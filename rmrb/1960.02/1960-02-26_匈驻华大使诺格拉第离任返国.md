Title: Hungarian Ambassador to China, Nogradi, Departs for Home After Completion of Tenure

Author: 
Date: 1960-02-26
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, February 25th: The Ambassador of the Hungarian People's Republic to China, Nogradi, departed for home this morning after completing his tenure.

　　Yu Peiwen, Director of the Protocol Department of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs, and Chen Boqing, Deputy Director of the Soviet and European Affairs Department, were present at the airport to see him off.

　　Diplomatic envoys from various countries to China and diplomats from the Hungarian Embassy in China also went to the airport to see him off.

### 匈驻华大使诺格拉第离任返国

1960-02-26
第4版()
专栏：

    匈驻华大使诺格拉第离任返国
    新华社25日讯　匈牙利人民共和国驻华大使诺格拉第于今日上午离任返国。
    前往机场送行的有外交部礼宾司司长俞沛文、苏欧司副司长陈伯清。
    各国驻华使节和匈牙利驻华大使馆的外交官也到机场送行。
