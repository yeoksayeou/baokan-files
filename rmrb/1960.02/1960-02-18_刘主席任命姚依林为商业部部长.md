Title: Chairman Liu Appoints Yao Yilin as Minister of Commerce
Author:
Date: 1960-02-18
Page: 4

    Xinhua News Agency, 17th — Chairman of the People's Republic of China, Liu Shaoqi, based on the decision of the 17th meeting of the Standing Committee of the Second National People's Congress, appointed Yao Yilin as Minister of Commerce of the People's Republic of China.

### 刘主席任命姚依林为商业部部长

1960-02-18
第4版()
专栏：

    刘主席任命姚依林为商业部部长
    新华社17日讯　中华人民共和国主席刘少奇根据第二届全国人民代表大会常务委员会第十七次会议的决定，任命姚依林为中华人民共和国商业部部长。
