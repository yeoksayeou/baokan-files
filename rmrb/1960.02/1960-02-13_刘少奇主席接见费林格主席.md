Title: Chairman Liu Shaoqi Receives Chairman Fierlinger
Author:
Date: 1960-02-13
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, February 12th: Chairman Liu Shaoqi received Chairman of the National Assembly of Czechoslovakia, Fierlinger, and all members of the Czechoslovak National Assembly delegation led by him this evening. The hosts and guests had a cordial and friendly conversation. Present at the reception were Zhu De, Chairman of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress, members Zhang Su and Wu Xinyu, Deputy Minister of Foreign Affairs Zeng Yongquan, and others. The Czechoslovak Ambassador to China, Bushniak, was also present at the reception.

### 刘少奇主席接见费林格主席

1960-02-13
第1版()
专栏：

    刘少奇主席接见费林格主席
    新华社12日讯　刘少奇主席今天晚上接见了捷克斯洛伐克国民议会主席费林格和由他率领的捷克斯洛伐克国民议会代表团的全体人员，宾主进行了亲切友好的交谈。接见时在座的，有全国人民代表大会常务委员会委员长朱德、委员张苏、武新宇，外交部副部长曾涌泉等人。接见时，捷克斯洛伐克驻中国大使布希尼亚克也在座。

          新华社记者　孟庆彪、张彬摄
