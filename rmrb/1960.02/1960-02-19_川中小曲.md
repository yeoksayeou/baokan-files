Title: Sichuan Folk Song
Author: Guān Shānyuè
Date: 1960-02-19
Page: 8

　　Sichuan Folk Song
　　Guān Shānyuè
　　The North is still a world of ice and snow,
　　But Chengdu, ah, is a lush green!
　　I brush off the dust from my journey,
　　And take a deep breath of the fresh air!
　　Green fields flutter with red flags,
　　Everywhere are high-yield fields, high-yield lands,
　　Those patches of sturdy seedlings,
　　Arouse the joy of visitors from afar.

　　A long whistle sounds in the fields,
　　Commune members begin to rest,
　　The crowd flies into the bamboo forest,
　　The bamboo forest resounds with songs of youth.

　　The colorful clouds shine on the green bamboo,
　　The green bamboo is so beautiful,
　　So much laughter flies around the bamboo,
　　The bamboo forest is a wonderful place:
　　Some are practicing singing,
　　Some are performing operas,
　　Some are jumping rope,
　　Some are playing chess,
　　Some are reading and learning culture,
　　Some are picking up their pens to write poems. …

　　Two little kids,
　　Come up with a good idea:
　　A smooth stone slab becomes a stone table,
　　Ping-pong balls fly across the table!

　　The auntie is full of strength,
　　The old man refuses to be outdone,
　　He cuts a vine to play tug-of-war,
　　The old couple are comparing martial arts!

　　So many aunties come to help,
　　So many old men come to cheer,
　　The stream beside the field gurgles,
　　The birds on the branches chirp!

　　The spring breeze rises in the fields of Sichuan,
　　The spring breeze is gentle and warms the heart,
　　The People's Commune is infinitely good,
　　The farmers live in happiness.

### 川中小曲
关山月
1960-02-19
第8版()
专栏：

    川中小曲
    关山月
    北国还是冰天雪地，
    成都呵，却是一片葱绿；
    我拂去旅途上的尘灰，
    深深吸一口清新空气！
    绿色的田野飘着红旗，
    到处是高产田，高产地，
    那一片片茁壮的嫩苗，
    引起远方来客的欣喜。
    田间响起一声长笛，
    公社社员开始休息，
    人群飞向竹林里，
    竹林响起青春曲。
    彩霞照翠竹，
    翠竹多秀丽，
    多少欢笑绕竹飞，
    竹林里面好天地：
    有人在练歌，
    有人在唱戏，
    有人在跳绳，
    有人在下棋，
    有人看书学文化，
    有人提笔写诗句。……
    两个小娃子，
    想出个好主意：
    光滑石板摆石桌，
    乒乓球儿桌上飞！
    大娘满身力，
    老汉不服气，
    砍根藤条来拔河，
    老两口儿把武比！
    多少大娘来帮忙，
    多少老汉来助威，
    田边溪水响淙淙，
    枝头鸟儿叫叽叽！
    川中田野春风起，
    春风轻柔暖心底，
    人民公社无限好，
    农民生活在幸福里。
