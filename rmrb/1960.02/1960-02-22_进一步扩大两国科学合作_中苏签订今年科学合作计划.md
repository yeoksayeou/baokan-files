Title: Further Expanding Scientific Cooperation Between the Two Countries: China and the Soviet Union Sign This Year's Scientific Cooperation Plan
Author:
Date: 1960-02-22
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, February 20th: The delegation of the Chinese Academy of Sciences and the delegation of the Soviet Academy of Sciences held talks in Moscow from the 9th to the 20th on the issue of scientific cooperation between the two academies for 1960. As a result of the talks, the two sides signed the 1960 Scientific Cooperation Plan between the two academies. The plan stipulates that in 1960, the contacts and cooperation between China and the Soviet Union in various aspects of science will be further expanded.

Pei Lisheng, Secretary-General of the Chinese Academy of Sciences, and Fedorov, Acting Academic Secretary-General of the Soviet Academy of Sciences, signed the 1960 Scientific Cooperation Plan on behalf of the two academies. Liu Xiao, Chinese Ambassador to the Soviet Union, and Topchiev, Vice President of the Soviet Academy of Sciences, attended the signing ceremony.

During this meeting, the delegations of the Chinese and Soviet Academies of Sciences both believed that the contacts between the two Academies of Sciences of China and the Soviet Union had achieved smooth development on the basis of mutual benefit, equality and friendship. Both parties expressed satisfaction with the implementation of the 1959 Scientific Cooperation Plan between the two Academies, and the delegation of the Chinese Academy of Sciences expressed its gratitude to the Soviet Academy of Sciences for its assistance to the Chinese Academy of Sciences.

### 进一步扩大两国科学合作  中苏签订今年科学合作计划

1960-02-22
第5版()
专栏：

    进一步扩大两国科学合作
    中苏签订今年科学合作计划
    新华社莫斯科20日电　中国科学院代表团和苏联科学院代表团，于9日到20日在莫斯科就两国科学院1960年度科学合作问题举行了会谈，会谈结果双方签订了1960年度两国科学院科学合作计划。计划规定，在1960年将进一步扩大中苏两国在科学各方面的联系和合作。
    代表两国科学院在1960年度科学合作计划上签字的，是中国科学院秘书长裴丽生和苏联科学院代理学术秘书长费奥多罗夫。中国驻苏联大使刘晓和苏联科学院副院长托普切夫参加了签字仪式。
    在这次会谈期间，中苏两国科学院代表团都认为，中苏两国科学院之间的联系在互利、平等和友好的基础上获得了顺利的发展，双方对于1959年度两国科学院科学合作计划的执行情况表示满意，中国科学院代表团对于苏联科学院给予中国科学院的帮助表示感谢。
