Title: Sino-Soviet Alliance Inspires the People of Both Countries to Move Forward. Workers, Cadres, and Students in the Capital Hold Friendship Soiree with the Soviet-Chinese Friendship Association Delegation
Author:
Date: 1960-02-21
Page: 3

Xinhua News Agency, February 20th. Over seven hundred workers, cadres, and students from Beijing held a "Sino-Soviet Friendship Soiree" tonight with the Soviet-Chinese Friendship Association delegation led by Ye Liujin at the Sino-Soviet Friendship Hall.

    Yang Shu, Vice President of the Beijing Sino-Soviet Friendship Association, spoke at the meeting. He said that in the years to come, the great Sino-Soviet friendship alliance will play an inestimable role in promoting the noble cause of world peace, socialism, and communism.

    Head of the delegation, Ye Liujin, said at the meeting that the Sino-Soviet Treaty of Friendship, Alliance, and Mutual Assistance will continue to inspire the people of the Soviet Union and China to move forward on the road of socialism and communism. Soviet-Chinese friendship is unbreakable, and imperialists and revisionists cannot destroy it. Delegation member Tikhvinskiy spoke in Chinese at the meeting. After describing the great achievements of the Chinese people in building socialism, he said that the Soviet people are very happy with the great achievements of the Chinese people and wish them even greater achievements in the future.

    The films "Celebrating the Tenth Anniversary of the Sino-Soviet Friendship Alliance" and "Hundred Birds Paying Homage to the Phoenix" were shown at the soiree.

    Also present at the Sino-Soviet Friendship Soiree were Ge Baoquan, Deputy Secretary-General of the Sino-Soviet Friendship Association, and Wang Jiong and Zhu Futang, Vice Presidents of the Beijing Sino-Soviet Friendship Association.

    Wei Lixiajin, Counselor of the Soviet Embassy in China, and Sang Feilou, First Secretary, also attended the soiree.

    The Soviet-Chinese Friendship Association delegation returned to Beijing by plane today from Shanghai, after visiting Jinan, Shanghai, Hangzhou, and other cities since leaving Beijing on the 12th.

    Xinhua News Agency, Shanghai, February 20th. The Soviet-Chinese Friendship Association delegation, led by Ye Liujin, Vice Chairman of the Council of the Soviet-Chinese Friendship Association and Minister of Higher and Secondary Specialized Education of the Soviet Union, left Shanghai by plane this morning to return to Beijing after visiting Shanghai and Hangzhou.

    Those who saw them off at the airport included Shi Ximin, Alternate Secretary of the Shanghai Municipal Committee of the Communist Party of China and President of the Shanghai Sino-Soviet Friendship Association; Liu Shuzhou, Alternate Secretary of the Shanghai Municipal Committee of the Communist Party of China and Vice Mayor of Shanghai; Jin Zhonghua and Ba Jin, Vice Presidents of the Shanghai Sino-Soviet Friendship Association; and Bai Yan, Secretary-General. Yelisavijin, Soviet Consul General in Shanghai, also saw them off at the airport.

    The Soviet-Chinese Friendship Association delegation arrived in Shanghai from Jinan on the morning of the 17th, accompanied by Ge Baoquan, Deputy Secretary-General of the Sino-Soviet Friendship Association, and Sang Feilou, First Secretary of the Soviet Embassy in China. They were warmly welcomed by people from all walks of life in Shanghai. On the evening of the 17th, Ke Qingshi, First Secretary of the Shanghai Municipal Committee of the Communist Party of China and Mayor of Shanghai, had a cordial meeting with all the distinguished guests of the delegation. Shi Ximin, President of the Shanghai Sino-Soviet Friendship Association, hosted a banquet for the distinguished guests. After the banquet, a literary and artistic soiree was held.

    During their stay in Shanghai, the delegation visited the Shanghai Machine Tool Plant, Jiangnan Shipyard, Steam Turbine Plant, Petroleum Machinery Parts Plant, Printing and Dyeing Plant No. 1, Huafu Gold Pen Factory, Changzheng People's Commune, Fudan University, Jiaotong University, East China Normal University, and Shanghai High School. They were warmly welcomed by workers, farmers, and students, and mass rallies were held at each unit. Ye Liujin, the head of the delegation, enthusiastically praised the profound friendship and great unity between the people of China and the Soviet Union over the past ten years at the two welcome rallies held at Jiaotong University and the Changzheng People's Commune. He said that no force in the world can weaken or shake the friendship between our two peoples, which is consolidated under the victorious banner of Marxism-Leninism, and that this friendship is conducive to world peace and the cause of human progress.

    The Soviet guests also visited the site of the First National Congress of the Communist Party of China - the Shanghai Revolutionary History Memorial Hall, the former residence of Sun Yat-sen, and laid wreaths at the tomb of Lu Xun.

    Gukrovsky, a member of the delegation and Secretary of the Leningrad Regional Committee of the Communist Party of the Soviet Union, visited the Shanghai Municipal Committee of the Communist Party of China. Ke Qingshi, First Secretary of the Shanghai Municipal Committee of the Communist Party of China, and other responsible persons of the Municipal Committee met with the distinguished guests and had cordial and friendly talks.

### 中苏同盟鼓舞两国人民向前迈进  首都工人干部学生同苏中友协代表团举行友谊晚会

1960-02-21
第3版()
专栏：

    中苏同盟鼓舞两国人民向前迈进
    首都工人干部学生同苏中友协代表团举行友谊晚会
    新华社20日讯　北京市工人、干部、学生七百多人，今晚同以叶留金为首的苏中友好协会代表团一起，在中苏友好馆举行了“中苏友谊晚会”。
    北京市中苏友好协会副会长杨述在会上讲话。他说，在未来的年代中，伟大的中苏友好同盟，一定能够在促进世界和平和社会主义、共产主义的崇高事业中，发挥出不可估量的作用。
    叶留金团长在会上讲话说，苏中友好同盟互助条约将不断鼓舞苏中两国人民，在社会主义、共产主义的道路上向前迈进。苏中友谊是牢不可破的，帝国主义、修正主义者都是破坏不了的。代表团团员齐赫文斯基在会上用汉语讲话。他在描述了中国人民在建设社会主义中取得的伟大成就后说，中国人民取得这么大的成就，苏联人民感到非常高兴，并祝中国人民今后取得更大的成就。
    晚会上放映了电影“欢庆中苏友好同盟十周年”和“百凤朝阳”。
    出席中苏友谊晚会的，还有中苏友好协会总会副秘书长戈宝权，北京市中苏友好协会副会长王炯、诸福棠。
    苏联驻华大使馆参赞魏立夏金、一等秘书桑飞楼，也参加了晚会。
    苏中友好协会代表团是在12日离北京去济南、上海、杭州等城市参观访问后，于今日由上海乘飞机返回北京的。
    新华社上海20日电　由苏中友好协会理事会副主席、苏联高等教育和中等专业教育部部长叶留金率领的苏中友好协会代表团，在上海和杭州参观访问以后，今天上午乘飞机离上海返回北京。
    到机场欢送的，有中共上海市委书记处候补书记、上海市中苏友好协会会长石西民，中共上海市委书记处候补书记、上海市副市长刘述周，上海市中苏友好协会副会长金仲华、巴金，秘书长白彦等。苏联驻上海总领事叶里沙维金也到机场送行。
    苏中友好协会代表团，是在17日上午在中苏友好协会总会副秘书长戈宝权、苏联驻华大使馆一等秘书桑飞楼的陪同下，由济南到达上海的。受到上海各界人士的热烈欢迎。17日晚间，中共上海市委第一书记、上海市市长柯庆施和代表团全体贵宾作了亲切的会见。上海市中苏友好协会会长石西民设宴招待贵宾。宴会之后，还举行了文艺晚会。
    代表团在沪期间，参观访问了上海机床厂、江南造船厂、汽轮机厂、石油机械配件厂、印染一厂、华孚金笔厂、长征人民公社、复旦大学、交通大学、华东师大和上海中学等单位。受到工人、农民、学生们的热烈欢迎，每个单位都举行了群众欢迎集会。代表团团长叶留金在交通大学和长征人民公社的两个欢迎集会上，热情地歌颂了十年来中苏两国人民的深厚友谊和伟大团结。他说，世界上没有任何力量能够削弱或者动摇我们两国人民在马克思列宁主义百战百胜的旗帜下巩固起来的友谊，这种友谊有利于世界和平和人类进步事业。
    苏联贵宾们还参观了中共第一次全国代表大会会址——上海革命历史纪念馆、孙中山故居，并到鲁迅墓献了花圈。
    代表团团员、苏共列宁格勒州委员会书记古克罗夫斯基访问了中共上海市委员会。中共上海市委第一书记柯庆施和市委书记处其他负责人会见了贵宾，并作了亲切友好的交谈。
