Title: Suslov and Others Receive Delegation of the Sino-Soviet Friendship Association; Popova Hosts Farewell Banquet for the Delegation
Author:
Date: 1960-02-25
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, February 24th: Suslov, member of the Presidium of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of the Soviet Union (CPSU) and Secretary of the Central Committee of the CPSU, and Kozlov, member of the Presidium of the Central Committee of the CPSU and First Deputy Chairman of the Council of Ministers of the Soviet Union, received Liu Changsheng, head of the delegation of the Sino-Soviet Friendship Association, member of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of China (CCP), and member of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress, and all members of the delegation in the Kremlin today.

Liu Xiao, Chinese Ambassador to the Soviet Union, was also present at the reception.

The Soviet side present at the reception included: Popova, Chairman of the Union of Soviet Societies for Friendship and Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries, and Zueva, Vice Chairman; Shebakov, responsible staff member of the Central Committee of the CPSU, and others.

Suslov and Kozlov held cordial and comradely talks with the Chinese guests.

Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, February 23rd: Andreyev, Chairman of the Soviet-Chinese Friendship Association, received all members of the Sino-Soviet Friendship Association delegation headed by Liu Changsheng in the Kremlin this afternoon.

Liu Xiao, Chinese Ambassador to the Soviet Union, and Zhang Yingwu, Cultural Counselor, were also present at the reception.

Andreyev and the delegation members had a friendly and cordial conversation.

Head of the delegation Liu Changsheng presented gifts to Chairman Andreyev.

Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, February 23rd: Popova, Chairman of the Union of Soviet Societies for Friendship and Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries, hosted a banquet on the afternoon of February 23rd to bid farewell to all members of the Sino-Soviet Friendship Association delegation headed by Liu Changsheng.

Among those present at the banquet were Zueva and Gorshkov, Vice Chairmen of the Union of Soviet Societies for Friendship and Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries; Osanyan, member of the Council of the Soviet-Chinese Friendship Association; Shebakov, responsible staff member of the Central Committee of the CPSU; Guttikov, Acting Director of the Far Eastern Department of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs, and others.

Liu Xiao, Chinese Ambassador to the Soviet Union, and Zhang Yingwu, Cultural Counselor, also attended the banquet.

Both the host and the guests delivered warm and friendly speeches at the banquet.

In her speech, Popova pointed out that the unbreakable friendship and unity between China and the Soviet Union are growing stronger day by day, and the close cooperation between the two countries in all fields over the past decade has achieved brilliant successes. She particularly emphasized the great contributions made by the Soviet-Chinese Friendship Association and the Sino-Soviet Friendship Association in strengthening the friendship between the peoples of the two countries.

In his speech, delegation head Liu Changsheng excitedly spoke of the deep and unforgettable impressions made on the delegation by its participation in the activities commemorating the tenth anniversary of the signing of the Sino-Soviet Treaty of Friendship, Alliance and Mutual Assistance and its visits to various parts of the Soviet Union. He expressed his heartfelt joy at the great achievements made by the Soviet Union in communist construction. He especially thanked the Soviet people for their fraternal assistance to the Chinese people, and said that the delegation would definitely convey the Soviet people's deep friendship to the Chinese people.

Ambassador Liu Xiao also spoke at the banquet.

The banquet was held in a very cordial, friendly, and warm atmosphere, with the hosts and guests frequently raising their glasses to toast the unbreakable fraternal friendship between the peoples of China and the Soviet Union.

That afternoon, Popova, Chairman of the Union of Soviet Societies for Friendship and Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries, received all members of the Sino-Soviet Friendship Association delegation headed by Liu Changsheng. Popova and the delegation members had a friendly and cordial conversation. During the reception, Popova and Liu Changsheng exchanged gifts.

Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, February 23rd: The delegation of the Sino-Soviet Friendship Association, which was invited to participate in celebrations marking the tenth anniversary of the signing of the Sino-Soviet Treaty of Friendship, Alliance and Mutual Assistance, is about to conclude its visit to the Soviet Union after spending a pleasant and friendly fourteen days.

During its stay in the Soviet Union, the delegation participated in celebrations in Moscow, Baku, Sumgait and other places, visited factories and collective farms, and met with leading comrades from various sectors of the Soviet Party, government, and people's organizations.

### 苏斯洛夫等接见中苏友协代表团  波波娃设宴为代表团饯行

1960-02-25
第5版()
专栏：

    苏斯洛夫等接见中苏友协代表团
    波波娃设宴为代表团饯行
    新华社莫斯科24日电　苏共中央主席团委员、苏共中央书记处书记苏斯洛夫和苏共中央主席团委员、苏联部长会议第一副主席科兹洛夫，今天在克里姆林宫接见了中苏友好协会代表团团长、中共中央委员、全国人民代表大会常务委员会委员刘长胜和代表团全体成员。
    接见时中国驻苏联大使刘晓也在座。
    接见时苏联方面在座的有：苏联对外友好和文化协会联合会主席波波娃和副主席朱耶娃，苏共中央负责工作人员谢巴科夫等。
    苏斯洛夫和科兹洛夫同中国客人进行了亲切的同志式的谈话。
    新华社莫斯科23日电　苏中友好协会主席安德烈耶夫今天下午在克里姆林宫接见了以刘长胜为首的中苏友好协会代表团全体团员。
    接见时，中国驻苏联大使刘晓和文化参赞张映吾也在座。
    安德烈耶夫和代表团团员们进行了友好亲切的谈话。
    刘长胜团长向安德烈耶夫主席赠送了礼品。
    新华社莫斯科23日电　苏联对外友好和文化协会联合会主席波波娃23日下午设宴欢送以刘长胜为首的中苏友好协会代表团全体团员。
    出席宴会的有苏联对外友好和文化协会联合会副主席朱耶娃和戈尔什科夫、苏中友好协会理事鄂山荫、苏共中央负责工作人员谢巴科夫和外交部远东司代理司长顾悌科夫等人。
    中国驻苏联大使刘晓和文化参赞张映吾也出席了宴会。
    宴会上宾主双方都作了热情友好的讲话。
    波波娃在讲话中指出，中苏两国牢不可破的友谊和团结正在日益增强，两国间十年来在各方面的密切合作已取得了辉煌的成就。她特别强调了苏中友好协会和中苏友好协会在加强两国人民友谊中所作的巨大贡献。
    刘长胜团长在讲话中兴奋地谈到了代表团到苏联参加中苏友好同盟互助条约签订十周年的活动和到苏联各地参观访问的深刻难忘的印象。他对苏联在共产主义建设中所作出的伟大成就表示衷心的高兴。他特别感谢苏联人民对中国人民的兄弟般的帮助，他说，代表团一定要把苏联人民对中国人民的深情厚谊转达给中国人民。
    刘晓大使在宴会上也讲了话。
    宴会在十分亲切友好热烈的气氛中进行，宾主间频频举杯为中苏两国人民牢不可破的兄弟友谊干杯。
    当天下午，苏联对外友好和文化协会联合会主席波波娃接见了以刘长胜为首的中苏友协代表团全体团员。波波娃和代表团团员们进行了友好亲切的谈话。接见时波波娃和刘长胜互相赠送了礼品。
    据新华社莫斯科23日电　应邀前来这里参加中苏友好同盟互助条约签订十周年庆祝活动的中苏友好协会代表团在苏联度过了愉快和充满友谊的十四天后，即将结束在苏联的访问。
    代表团在苏联期间，曾在莫斯科、巴库、苏姆盖特等地参加了庆祝活动，参观访问了工厂和农庄，会见了苏联党、政府和人民团体各方面的领导同志。
