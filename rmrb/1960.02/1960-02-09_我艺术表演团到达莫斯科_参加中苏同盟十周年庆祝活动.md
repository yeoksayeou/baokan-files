Title: Our Art Troupe Arrives in Moscow to Participate in Celebrations of the Tenth Anniversary of the Sino-Soviet Alliance
Author:
Date: 1960-02-09
Page: 5
    Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, 8th. A 22-member Chinese art troupe, led by Deputy Head Xiao Meng, arrived in Moscow by plane on the 7th.
    The performance troupe will participate in the celebrations of the tenth anniversary of the signing of Sino-Soviet Treaty of Friendship, Alliance and Mutual Assistance in Moscow, and will also visit and perform in more than ten cities in the Soviet Union in accordance with the 1960 implementation plan of the Sino-Soviet Cultural Cooperation Agreement.
    Representatives from the Soviet Ministry of Culture and Zhang Yingwu, Counselor of the Chinese Embassy in the Soviet Union, were at the airport to greet them.

### 我艺术表演团到达莫斯科  参加中苏同盟十周年庆祝活动

1960-02-09
第5版()
专栏：

    我艺术表演团到达莫斯科
    参加中苏同盟十周年庆祝活动
    新华社莫斯科8日电　中国艺术表演团一行二十二人，在副团长萧孟的率领下，7日乘飞机到达莫斯科。
    表演团将在莫斯科参加中苏友好同盟互助条约签订十周年纪念活动，并且将根据中苏文化合作协定1960年执行计划在苏联十多个城市进行访问演出。
    苏联文化部代表、我国驻苏联大使馆参赞张映吾都到机场迎接。
