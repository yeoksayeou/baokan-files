Title: Mass Rally in Leiqi Area on Sino-Burmese Border Welcomes Sino-Burmese Boundary Agreement
Author:
Date: 1960-02-18
Page: 5
　 Xinhua News Agency, Rangoon, February 16th: A mass rally was held on February 11th in the Leiqi area on the Sino-Burmese border to welcome the recent agreement on the Sino-Burmese boundary issue signed by Burmese Prime Minister Ne Win and Chinese Premier Zhou Enlai. Approximately 30,000 people from twenty-four villages in this area, including Kachin and Shan people, participated in the rally.
　　The attendees unanimously passed a resolution supporting the agreement on the Sino-Burmese boundary issue.

### 中缅边境上雷基地区群众集会  欢迎中缅边界协定

1960-02-18
第5版()
专栏：
 　 中缅边境上雷基地区群众集会
    欢迎中缅边界协定
    新华社仰光16日电　中缅边界上的雷基地区的群众在2月11日举行了群众大会，欢迎缅甸总理奈温和中国总理周恩来最近签订的关于中缅边界问题的协定。这个地区二十四个村的大约三万人，其中包括克钦族人和掸族人参加了大会。
    到会者一致通过了支持关于中缅边界问题的协定的决议。
