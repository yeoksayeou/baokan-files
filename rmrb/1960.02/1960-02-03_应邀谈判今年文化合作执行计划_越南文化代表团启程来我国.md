Title: Vietnamese Cultural Delegation Departs for Our Country to Negotiate This Year's Cultural Cooperation Implementation Plan
Author:
Date: 1960-02-03
Page: 5

    Invited to negotiate this year's cultural cooperation implementation plan
    Vietnamese Cultural Delegation Departs for Our Country
    Xinhua News Agency, Hanoi, February 1st: The cultural delegation of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam, led by Le Liem, Deputy Minister of Culture, departed by train at noon today for Beijing, China, to negotiate the 1960 cultural cooperation implementation plan between China and Vietnam.
    Huang Ming Giam, Minister of Culture of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam, Deputy Minister Qu Huy Can, and heads of relevant cultural departments went to the station to see them off.
    He Wei, the Chinese Ambassador to Vietnam, and Ai Wen, the Cultural Counselor, also went to the station to see them off.

### 应邀谈判今年文化合作执行计划  越南文化代表团启程来我国

1960-02-03
第5版()
专栏：

    应邀谈判今年文化合作执行计划
    越南文化代表团启程来我国
    新华社河内1日电　应邀前往中国谈判中越两国1960年文化合作执行计划的越南民主共和国文化代表团，在文化部副部长黎廉率领下，今天中午乘火车启程前往北京。
    前往车站送行的有越南民主共和国文化部部长黄明鉴，副部长瞿辉近以及有关文化部门的负责人。
    中国驻越南大使何伟和文化参赞艾汶也前往车站送行。
