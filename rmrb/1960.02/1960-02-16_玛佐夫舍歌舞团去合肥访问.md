Title: Mazowsze Song and Dance Ensemble to Visit Hefei
Author:
Date: 1960-02-16
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, February 15th: The Polish Mazowsze Song and Dance Ensemble, led by Bogusław Płasa, representative of the Polish Ministry of Culture and Art, and Mira Zimińska-Sygietyńska, head of the ensemble, left Beijing by special train this morning for a visit and performance in Hefei.

Zhang Zhixiang, Deputy Director of the Commission for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries, Wang Yi, Secretary-General of the Sino-Polish Friendship Association, and people from the capital's art circles went to the station today to see off the Polish artists.

### 玛佐夫舍歌舞团去合肥访问

1960-02-16
第4版()
专栏：

    玛佐夫舍歌舞团去合肥访问
    据新华社15日讯　波兰玛佐夫舍歌舞团在波兰文化艺术部代表鲍古斯瓦夫·普瓦萨和歌舞团团长米拉·齐明斯卡·塞格廷斯卡率领下，今天上午乘专车离开北京，前往合肥访问演出。
    对外文委副主任张致祥，中波友好协会秘书长王仪和首都艺术界人士，今天到车站欢送波兰艺术家。
