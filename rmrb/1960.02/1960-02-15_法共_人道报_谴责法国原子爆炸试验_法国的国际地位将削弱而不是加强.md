Title: French Communist Party "L'Humanité" Condemns French Atomic Bomb Test: France's International Status Will Be Weakened, Not Strengthened
Author:
Date: 1960-02-15
Page: 6
Xinhua News Agency, Paris, February 13th: "L'Humanité" published a commentary today condemning France's atomic bomb test in the Sahara. The newspaper said: The atomic test conducted by France in the Sahara is guaranteed not to achieve the results expected by de Gaulle and his government—to allow France to enter the "atomic club" and strengthen France's international prestige.

The newspaper then pointed out: Obviously, this test cannot change the balance of power between the three major powers and France. People will not see any benefit from this action; on the contrary, they can immediately see the danger.

"L'Humanité" emphasized that this action will erect a new obstacle on the road to banning nuclear tests, and of course, also a new obstacle to disarmament and international detente. It will increase the amount of radioactive particles in the atmosphere. It will weaken France's international position. The French government is actually violating a resolution of the United Nations General Assembly. This action will particularly worsen France's relations with African countries.

### 法共“人道报”谴责法国原子爆炸试验  法国的国际地位将削弱而不是加强

1960-02-15
第6版()
专栏：

    法共“人道报”谴责法国原子爆炸试验
    法国的国际地位将削弱而不是加强
    据新华社巴黎13日电　“人道报”今天发表评论谴责法国在撒哈拉进行原子爆炸试验。报纸说：法国在撒哈拉进行这样的原子试验，保证不会得到戴高乐和他的政府所预期的成果——使得法国进入“原子俱乐部”并且加强法国的国际威望。
    报纸接着指出：很明显，这种试验不能改变三大国同法国之间的力量对比。人们不会从这个行动中看到什么好处，相反地他们从这个行动中一眼就可以看到危险。
    “人道报”强调指出，这个行动将给通向禁止核试验的道路上竖立一个新障碍，当然这同时也就是裁军、国际缓和的新障碍。它将增加大气中的放射性微粒。它将削弱法国的国际地位。法国政府实际上违反联合国大会的一项决议。这个行动特别将恶化法国同非洲各国的关系。
