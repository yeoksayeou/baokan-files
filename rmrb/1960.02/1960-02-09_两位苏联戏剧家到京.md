Title: Two Soviet Playwrights Arrive in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1960-02-09
Page: 4
    
    Two Soviet Playwrights Arrive in Beijing
    Xinhua News Agency, February 8th. The Vice-Chairman of the Soviet Russian Theatrical Association, Shvidov, and Professor Markov of the Soviet National Lunacharsky Institute of Theatre Arts, arrived in Beijing by plane this afternoon. They are visiting China according to the 1960 implementation plan of the Sino-Soviet Cultural Cooperation Agreement.
    Among those who went to the airport to welcome them were Ouyang Yuqian, Vice-Chairman of the Chinese Dramatists Association, and playwrights Cao Yu and Ouyang Shanzun.

### 两位苏联戏剧家到京

1960-02-09
第4版()
专栏：

    两位苏联戏剧家到京
    新华社8日讯　苏联俄罗斯戏剧家协会副主席什维多夫和苏联国立鲁纳恰尔斯基戏剧学院教授马尔柯夫博士，今天下午乘飞机到达北京。他们是根据中苏文化合作协定1960年执行计划前来我国访问的。
    前往机场欢迎的有中国戏剧家协会副主席欧阳予倩和剧作家曹禺、欧阳山尊等。
