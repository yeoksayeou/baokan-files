Title: Strengthening the Unity of Students from All Countries in the Struggle for Peace and National Independence: Opening of the IUS Executive Committee Meeting
Author:
Date: 1960-02-25
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, Tunis, February 23rd: The Executive Committee Meeting of the International Union of Students (IUS) opened this morning in the Tunis Labor Union Conference Hall in Tunis. Eighty representatives and observers from student groups from various countries participated in the opening meeting.

This meeting is the first IUS meeting held in Africa at the invitation of the Tunisian Student Union. The meeting will discuss the tasks of the IUS and student organizations in promoting cooperation and solidarity in the struggle for peace, national independence, and democratization of education, as well as strengthening solidarity with Algerian students fighting for national independence and students in other African countries.

Mongi Kooli, Secretary-General of the Tunisian Student Union, delivered a speech welcoming all the representatives and observers to the meeting. He hoped that the Executive Committee meeting would be successful. He expressed his support and solidarity with the Algerian students' struggle for national independence and condemned the colonialist war of aggression launched by the French government in Algeria.

A representative of the Tunisian Minister of Education spoke at the meeting.

IUS President Belyakov delivered a report on the work of the IUS at the meeting.

### 加强各国学生在争取和平与民族独立中的团结  国际学联执委会会议开幕

1960-02-25
第5版()
专栏：

    加强各国学生在争取和平与民族独立中的团结
    国际学联执委会会议开幕
    据新华社突尼斯23日电　国际学生联合会执行委员会会议今天上午在突尼斯的突尼斯工会会议厅开幕。各国学生团体的八十名代表和观察员参加了开幕会议。
    这次会议是国际学联应突尼斯学生联合会的邀请第一次在非洲举行的会议。会议将讨论国际学生联合会和学生组织在争取和平、民族独立、教育民主化的斗争中促进合作和团结的任务，以及加强对争取民族独立的阿尔及利亚和非洲其他各国学生的声援等问题。
    突尼斯学生联合会总书记蒙吉·科利致词欢迎全体代表和观察员来参加这次会议。他希望执行委员会会议成功。他表示支持和声援阿尔及利亚学生争取民族独立的斗争，并且谴责了法国政府在阿尔及利亚发动的殖民主义侵略战争。
    突尼斯教育部长的代表在会上讲了话。
    国际学生联合会主席贝利康在会上作了关于国际学生联合会的工作报告。
