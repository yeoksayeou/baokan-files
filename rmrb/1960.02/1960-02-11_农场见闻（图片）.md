Title: Farm Scenes (Pictures)
Author: Fu Luo
Date: 1960-02-11
Page: 8
　
　　Farm Scenes
　　Fu Luo
　　Fields are a sea of green,
　　Vegetables are transported, never ceasing.
　　Abundant vegetables supply the city,
　　Workers eat and smile with glee.

　　Starting from scratch to build a factory,
　　Without equipment, we do it ourselves!
　　Lathes, drills, milling machines, planers we have,
　　The self-made machine tools are so grand!

　　Silver light flashes,
　　Fish fall into the net.
　　Catch more fish and shrimp,
　　To supply the market.

　　Developing production according to local conditions,
　　Seizing sideline occupations based on local materials,
　　We cut it ourselves and press it ourselves,
　　Diligent study and hard practice weaving reed mats.

　　One cannot judge a person by appearance,
　　The sea cannot be measured with a dipper;
　　Although the sugar factory is simple and crude,
　　It can make soy sauce and produce sugar!

　　Blacksmiths are working happily,
　　Carpenters are busy repairing.
　　Tools and implements need not be bought,
　　Saving funds for further production.

　　A pond full of ducks,
　　A pond full of green grass,
　　Well-fed,
　　Well-managed,
　　The ducks are growing and quacking loudly!

### 农场见闻（图片）
傅洛
1960-02-11
第8版()
专栏：

    农场见闻
    傅洛
    田里一片青，
    运菜忙不停。
    多产蔬菜供城市，
    工人吃了笑盈盈。
    白手起家办工厂，
    没有设备自己干！
    车钻铣刨咱都有，
    自制的机床多漂亮！
    银光闪闪，
    鱼儿落网。
    多捕鱼虾，
    供应市场。
    因人制宜搞生产，
    就地取材抓副业，
    自己割来自己压，
    勤学苦练编芦席。
    人不可貌相，
    海水不可斗量；
    糖厂虽为简陋，
    能制酱油能出糖！
    打铁干的欢，
    木匠修的忙。
    工具用具不用买，
    节约资金再生产。
    满塘鸭子，
    满塘青草，
    吃的饱，
    放的好，
    鸭子长的呱呱叫！
