Members of the October Production Team of the West Lake Commune in Hangzhou, Zhejiang Province, are actively learning to use manual tea picking machines to welcome a bumper harvest of spring tea. Using a manual tea picking machine can harvest twelve catties of tea per hour, increasing efficiency three times compared to hand picking.
　　　　　　　　　　Xinhua News Agency reporter Zhang Gengxin photo
