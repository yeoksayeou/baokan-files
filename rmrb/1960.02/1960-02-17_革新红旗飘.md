Title: Red Banner of Innovation Flutters
Author: Gao Jiansheng
Date: 1960-02-17
Page: 2

    The red banner of technical innovation flutters,
    The workers' zeal is higher than the sky;
    Heavy carrying poles are put down from the shoulders,
    And large iron shovels are thrown away from the hands.
    Worker of Yongyu Salt Company
              Gao Jiansheng
