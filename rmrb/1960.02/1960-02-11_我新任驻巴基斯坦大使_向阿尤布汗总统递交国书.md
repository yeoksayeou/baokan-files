Title: My New Ambassador to Pakistan Presents Credentials to President Ayub Khan
Author: 
Date: 1960-02-11
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, Karachi, February 10th: The newly appointed Ambassador Extraordinary and Plenipotentiary of the People's Republic of China to Pakistan, Ding Guoyu, presented his credentials to Pakistani President Ayub Khan in Karachi this morning.

    Pakistani Foreign Minister Qadir was present at the presentation of credentials.

    After presenting the credentials, President Ayub Khan had a friendly conversation with Ambassador Ding Guoyu.

### 我新任驻巴基斯坦大使  向阿尤布汗总统递交国书

1960-02-11
第5版()
专栏：

    我新任驻巴基斯坦大使
    向阿尤布汗总统递交国书
    新华社卡拉奇10日电　新任中华人民共和国驻巴基斯坦特命全权大使丁国钰今天上午在卡拉奇向巴基斯坦总统阿尤布汗递交国书。
    在递交国书时，巴基斯坦外交部长卡迪尔在场。
    在递交国书以后，阿尤布汗总统同丁国钰大使进行了友好的谈话。
