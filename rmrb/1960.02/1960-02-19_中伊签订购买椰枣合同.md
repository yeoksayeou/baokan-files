Title: China and Iraq Sign Contract for Purchase of Dates
Author:
Date: 1960-02-19
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, Baghdad, February 17th: A special contract for China to purchase 10,000 tons of dates from Iraq was signed here today. These dates will be used as industrial raw materials.

### 中伊签订购买椰枣合同

1960-02-19
第5版()
专栏：

    中伊签订购买椰枣合同
    新华社巴格达17日电　中国向伊拉克购买一万吨椰枣的特别合同今天在这里签字。这批椰枣将用作工业原料。
