Title: Czechoslovak Parliament Delegation Arrives in Hanoi; Chairman Ho Chi Minh Receives the Delegation
Author:
Date: 1960-02-04
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, Hanoi, February 3rd: The Czechoslovak Republic National Assembly delegation, headed by National Assembly Chairman Fierlinger, arrived in Hanoi by special plane this morning. The delegation is visiting Vietnam for a week at the invitation of the Standing Committee of the National Assembly of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam.

Welcoming the distinguished guests at Gia Lam Airport were Ton Duc Thang, Chairman of the Standing Committee of the National Assembly of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam, Vice-Chairmen Hoang Van Hoan and Ton Quang Phiet, Deputy Prime Ministers Phan Ke Toai and Pham Hung, Deputy Foreign Minister Ung Van Khiem, and representatives of various circles of the people, numbering over 500. Diplomatic envoys from various countries to Vietnam also went to the airport to welcome the delegation.

Chairman Ton Duc Thang delivered a speech at the airport, warmly welcoming the delegation. He said that in the arduous war waged by the Vietnamese people for the liberation of their motherland, the Czechoslovak people were once the close comrades-in-arms of the Vietnamese people; thereafter, in the process of the Vietnamese people healing the wounds of war and carrying out peaceful construction, the government and people of the Czechoslovak Republic, together with other countries of the socialist camp headed by the Soviet Union, provided Vietnam with selfless assistance in all aspects. Ton Duc Thang expressed his gratitude for the profound friendship of the Czechoslovak people.

Ton Duc Thang said that this visit by the delegation will contribute to further strengthening the fraternal relations between the two countries.

Chairman Fierlinger, in his reply, thanked the National Assembly and the people of Vietnam for their warm reception of the delegation. He conveyed the respect of the Czechoslovak National Assembly to the Vietnamese people. He said that the Czechoslovak people, like the people of the whole world, admire the heroic struggle of the Vietnamese people.

Fierlinger said that this visit by the delegation will further consolidate the friendship between the Czechoslovak and Vietnamese peoples.

Chairman Ho Chi Minh of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam received the delegation this morning.

The Czechoslovak National Assembly delegation arrived in Hanoi after ending an eight-day visit to Cambodia, leaving Phnom Penh on February 1 and arriving via Rangoon and Kunming.

### 捷议会代表团到达河内  胡志明主席接见代表团

1960-02-04
第5版()
专栏：

    捷议会代表团到达河内
    胡志明主席接见代表团
    新华社河内3日电　以国民议会主席费林格为首的捷克斯洛伐克共和国国民议会代表团今天上午乘专机到达河内。代表团应越南民主共和国国会常务委员会的邀请，将在越南进行为期一周的访问。
    到嘉林机场欢迎贵宾的，有越南民主共和国国会常务委员会委员长孙德胜，副委员长黄文欢、孙光阀，副总理潘继遂、范雄，外交部副部长雍文谦和各界人民的代表五百多人。各国驻越南的外交使节也到机场欢迎代表团。
    孙德胜委员长在机场致词，热烈欢迎代表团。他说，在越南人民为解放祖国而进行的艰苦战争中，捷克斯洛伐克人民曾经是越南人民的亲密战友；其后在越南人民医治战争创伤和进行和平建设的过程中，捷克斯洛伐克共和国政府和人民同以苏联为首的社会主义阵营其它各国一起，在各方面给予越南以无私的援助。孙德胜对捷克斯洛伐克人民的深厚情谊表示感谢。
    孙德胜说，代表团的这次访问将对进一步密切两国之间的兄弟般的关系作出贡献。
    费林格主席在致答词中，感谢越南国会和人民对代表团的热情接待。他向越南人民转致捷克斯洛伐克国民议会的敬意。他说，捷克斯洛伐克人民和全世界人民一样，敬佩越南人民的英勇斗争。
    费林格说，代表团的这次访问将进一步巩固捷克斯洛伐克和越南人民之间的友谊。
    越南民主共和国主席胡志明在今天上午接见了代表团。
    捷克斯洛伐克国民议会代表团是结束了在柬埔寨历时八天的访问，在1日离开金边经仰光和昆明到达河内的。
