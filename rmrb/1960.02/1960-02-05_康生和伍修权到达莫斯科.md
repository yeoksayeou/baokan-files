Title: Kang Sheng and Wu Xiuquan Arrive in Moscow
Author:
Date: 1960-02-05
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, February 3rd: Kang Sheng, alternate member of the Political Bureau of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of China, and Wu Xiuquan, member of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of China, arrived in Moscow by plane from Beijing on February 2nd to attend the high-level meeting of the Political Consultative Committee of the Warsaw Treaty Organization as observers of the People's Republic of China.

Those who went to the airport to welcome them included Pospelov, alternate member of the Presidium of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of the Soviet Union and secretary of the Secretariat of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of the Soviet Union, Ustinov, member of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of the Soviet Union and Vice-Chairman of the Council of Ministers, and responsible personnel from the Central Committee of the Communist Party of the Soviet Union and the Soviet Ministry of Foreign Affairs.

Liu Xiao, the Chinese Ambassador to the Soviet Union, who is also an observer at this meeting, also went to the airport to greet them.

### 康生和伍修权到达莫斯科

1960-02-05
第1版()
专栏：

    康生和伍修权到达莫斯科
    新华社莫斯科3日电　以中华人民共和国观察员身分出席华沙条约政治协商委员会高级会议的中共中央政治局候补委员康生和中共中央委员伍修权2月2日乘飞机从北京到达莫斯科。
    前往机场欢迎的有苏共中央主席团候补委员、苏共中央书记处书记波斯别洛夫，苏共中央委员、部长会议副主席乌斯季诺夫和苏共中央与苏联外交部负责工作人员。
    中国参加这次会议的另一位观察员、驻苏大使刘晓也到机场迎接。
