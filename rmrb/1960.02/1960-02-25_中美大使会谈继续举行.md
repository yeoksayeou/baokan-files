Title: Sino-U.S. Ambassadorial Talks Continue

Author:
Date: 1960-02-25
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, Warsaw, February 23rd: The ninety-sixth meeting of the Sino-U.S. ambassadorial talks was held in Warsaw today at 3:00 p.m. The meeting lasted two hours and ten minutes. Afterwards, both sides announced that the next meeting will be held at 2:00 p.m. on March 22nd.

### 中美大使会谈继续举行

1960-02-25
第5版()
专栏：

    中美大使会谈继续举行
    新华社华沙23日电　中美两国大使级会谈第九十六次会议，今天下午三时在华沙举行。会议历时二小时十分。会后双方宣布，下次会议将在3月22日下午二时举行。
