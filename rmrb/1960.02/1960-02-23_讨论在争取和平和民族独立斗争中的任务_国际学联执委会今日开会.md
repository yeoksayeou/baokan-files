Title: ISU Executive Committee Meets Today to Discuss Tasks in the Struggle for Peace and National Independence
Author:
Date: 1960-02-23
Page: 6
Xinhua News Agency, Tunis, February 21st - Representatives attending the Executive Committee of the International Student Union (ISU) have begun arriving in Tunis to attend the ISU Executive Committee meeting to be held in Tunis on February 23. This meeting is the first time the ISU has held a meeting in Africa, at the invitation of the Tunisian Student Union.

ISU President Bělík, Chinese representative Qian Liren, and representatives and observers from the Soviet Union, Czechoslovakia, Bulgaria, Poland, Hungary, the German Democratic Republic, Japan, Sudan, and other countries arrived in Tunis by special plane on the afternoon of the 21st. Prior to their arrival, representatives from Algeria, Cuba, Iraq, Brazil, and the West African Students' Union had already arrived.

According to sources here, approximately seventy representatives and observers from student organizations in forty countries will attend the meeting.

The agenda will include: the tasks of the International Student Union and student organizations in promoting cooperation and solidarity in the struggle for peace, national independence, and democratization of education; and strengthening support for Algerian and other African students who are fighting for national independence alongside their people. The meeting will also prepare for the convening of the Sixth Congress of the International Student Union. The 1960 activity plan organized or initiated by the International Student Union will be included in the agenda of the meeting.

### 讨论在争取和平和民族独立斗争中的任务  国际学联执委会今日开会

1960-02-23
第6版()
专栏：

    讨论在争取和平和民族独立斗争中的任务
    国际学联执委会今日开会
    新华社突尼斯21日电　出席国际学生联合会执行委员会的代表已经开始到达突尼斯，他们将出席国际学联执行委员会2月23日在突尼斯举行的会议。这次会议是国际学联应突尼斯学生联合会的邀请第一次在非洲举行的会议。
    国际学联主席贝利康、中国代表钱李仁和苏联、捷克斯洛伐克、保加利亚、波兰、匈牙利、德意志民主共和国、日本、苏丹等国的代表和观察员，已经在21日下午乘专机到达突尼斯。在他们到达以前，阿尔及利亚、古巴、伊拉克、巴西和西非学生总联合会的代表已经先到。
    据这里消息说，大约有四十个国家学生组织的七十名左右代表和观察员将出席这次会议。
    据委员会说，这次议程将包括：国际学生联合会和学生组织在争取和平、民族独立、教育民主化的斗争中促进合作和团结的任务；和加强对正在同自己的人民一起争取民族独立的阿尔及利亚和非洲其它各国学生的声援。会议还将为国际学生联合会第六届代表大会的召开作好准备。国际学生联合会组织或倡议的1960年活动计划将列入这次会议议程。
