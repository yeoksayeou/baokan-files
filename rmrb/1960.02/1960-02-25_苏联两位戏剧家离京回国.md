Title: Two Soviet Playwrights Leave Beijing for Home

Author: 
Date: 1960-02-25
Page: 5

　　Xinhua News Agency, February 23rd: Shvetov, Vice Chairman of the Soviet Russian Theatrical Society, and Dr. Markov, Professor of the Soviet State Lunacharsky Institute of Theatrical Arts, who came to China for a visit under the 1960 executive plan of the Sino-Soviet Cultural Cooperation Agreement, left Beijing by plane this morning to return home.

　　They arrived in China on February 8th and visited Beijing, Shanghai and Guangzhou during their stay.

　　Among those who went to the airport to see them off were Ouyang Yuqian, Vice Chairman of the Chinese Dramatists Association, and others.

### 苏联两位戏剧家离京回国

1960-02-25
第5版()
专栏：

    苏联两位戏剧家离京回国
    新华社23日讯　根据中苏文化合作协定1960年执行计划前来我国访问的、苏联俄罗斯戏剧家协会副主席什维多夫和苏联国立鲁纳恰尔斯基戏剧学院教授马尔柯夫博士，于今晨乘飞机离北京回国。
    他们是2月8日来到中国的，在华期间曾访问北京、上海、广州。
    前往机场送行的，有中国戏剧家协会副主席欧阳予倩等。
