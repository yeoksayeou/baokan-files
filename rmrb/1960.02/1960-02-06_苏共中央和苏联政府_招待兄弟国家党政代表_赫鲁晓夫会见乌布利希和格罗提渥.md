Title: CPC Central Committee and Soviet Government Host Representatives of Fraternal Countries' Party and Government / Khrushchev Meets with Ulbricht and Grotewohl
Author:
Date: 1960-02-06
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, February 5th – TASS reports: The Central Committee of the Communist Party of the Soviet Union and the Council of Ministers of the Soviet Union held a reception on the evening of February 4th to host participants of the conference of representatives from Communist and Workers' Parties of European socialist countries on exchanging experiences in developing agriculture, and the meeting of the Political Consultative Committee of the Warsaw Treaty member states.

Those attending the reception included: all participants of the two meetings, leaders of the Communist Party of the Soviet Union and the Soviet government, Soviet ambassadors to People's Democratic countries, ambassadors of socialist countries to the Soviet Union, prominent agricultural scientists and practical agricultural workers, and representatives from all walks of life.

The reception was held in the newly built guesthouse of the Soviet government on Lenin Hills.

Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, February 5th – TASS reports: First Secretary of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of the Soviet Union and Chairman of the Council of Ministers of the Soviet Union, Khrushchev, met today with First Secretary of the Central Committee of the Socialist Unity Party of Germany, Ulbricht, and Prime Minister of the German Democratic Republic, Grotewohl.

The meeting took place in a friendly and sincere atmosphere. In view of the upcoming summit meeting, the two sides exchanged views on the German question. Khrushchev, Ulbricht, and Grotewohl discussed further efforts by the Soviet Union and the German Democratic Republic to strive for the early conclusion of a peace treaty with Germany and the elimination of the abnormal situation in West Berlin. They reached complete agreement on all the issues discussed.

The two sides decided to continue to exchange views on issues of mutual interest that may arise in the process of preparing for the summit conference of heads of government to be held in May 1960.

### 苏共中央和苏联政府  招待兄弟国家党政代表  赫鲁晓夫会见乌布利希和格罗提渥

1960-02-06
第5版()
专栏：

    苏共中央和苏联政府
    招待兄弟国家党政代表
    赫鲁晓夫会见乌布利希和格罗提渥
    新华社5日讯　据塔斯社莫斯科讯：苏联共产党中央委员会和苏联部长会议4日晚举行招待会，招待欧洲社会主义国家共产党和工人党交流发展农业经验代表会议和华沙条约缔约国政治协商委员会会议的参加者。
    出席招待会的有：两个会议的全体参加者，苏联共产党和苏联政府的领导人，苏联驻人民民主国家的大使以及社会主义国家驻苏联的大使，著名的农业科学家和农业实际工作者，各界代表。
    招待会是在列宁山上苏联政府新建的招待所中举行的。
    新华社5日讯　塔斯社莫斯科讯：苏联共产党中央委员会第一书记、苏联部长会议主席赫鲁晓夫今天同德国统一社会党中央委员会第一书记乌布利希和德意志民主共和国总理格罗提渥举行了会晤。
    会晤是在友好真挚的气氛中进行的。由于将要举行最高级会议，双方就德国问题交换了意见。赫鲁晓夫、乌布利希和格罗提渥讨论了关于苏联和德意志民主共和国进一步努力争取尽速缔结对德和约和消除西柏林的不正常局面的问题。他们对所有讨论的问题意见完全一致。
    双方决定对于在准备1960年5月举行的政府首脑会议的过程中可能产生的双方感到兴趣的问题继续交换意见。
