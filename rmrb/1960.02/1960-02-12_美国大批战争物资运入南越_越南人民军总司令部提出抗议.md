Title: Large Quantities of American War Materials Transported to South Vietnam; General Headquarters of the Vietnam People's Army Lodges Protest
Author:
Date: 1960-02-12
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, Hanoi, February 11th: The Liaison Delegation of the General Headquarters of the Vietnam People's Army wrote to the International Commission of Vietnam on February 4th, protesting that American imperialism had illegally transported a large quantity of war materials into South Vietnam on nine ships between October 23rd and 27th of last year, and demanded that the International Commission investigate the aforementioned illegal incidents and draw appropriate conclusions.

The letter pointed out that from October 23rd to 25th, American ships such as the "Washington" unloaded more than 500 tons of war materials in Saigon. Two landing craft and another ship of the U.S. Pacific Fleet's Seventh Fleet also unloaded boxes of war materials at the Hoi An port during this period. On October 26th, the "American Southern" ship unloaded two hundred boxes of war materials in Saigon. On October 27th, an American ship and two landing craft unloaded a large quantity of war materials onto barges of the South Vietnamese army about 500 meters from the port of Vung Tau.

### 美国大批战争物资运入南越  越南人民军总司令部提出抗议

1960-02-12
第6版()
专栏：

    美国大批战争物资运入南越
    越南人民军总司令部提出抗议
    据新华社河内11日电　越南人民军总司令部联络代表团2月4日写信给越南国际委员会，抗议美帝国主义在去年10月23日到27日曾经派遣九艘轮船把大量战争物资非法运入南越，并且要求国际委员会就上述违法事件进行调查，作出应有的结论。
    信中指出，去年10月23日到25日，美国“华盛顿号”等两艘轮船在西贡卸下五百多吨战争物资。美国太平洋海军第七舰队的两艘登陆艇和另外一艘轮船也在这一时期在会安港口卸下多箱战争物资。10月26日“美南号”轮船在西贡卸下二百箱战争物资。10月27日，美国一艘轮船和两艘登陆艇向离头顿港口约五百米的南越军队的驳船卸下大批战争物资。
