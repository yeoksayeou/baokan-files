Title: Chairman Liu Approves the Sino-German Treaty of Commerce and Navigation
Author:
Date: 1960-02-11
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, February 10th. Liu Shaoqi, Chairman of the People's Republic of China, in accordance with the decision of the Twelfth Session of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress, approved on February 9th the Treaty of Commerce and Navigation between the People's Republic of China and the German Democratic Republic, signed in Beijing on January 18th of this year.

### 刘主席批准中德通商航海条约

1960-02-11
第1版()
专栏：

    刘主席批准中德通商航海条约
    新华社10日讯　中华人民共和国主席刘少奇根据全国人民代表大会常务委员会第十二次会议的决定，于2月9日批准了今年1月18日在北京签订的中华人民共和国和德意志民主共和国通商航海条约。
