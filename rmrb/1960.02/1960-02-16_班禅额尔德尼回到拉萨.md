Title: Panchen Erdini Returns to Lhasa
Author:
Date: 1960-02-16
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency, Lhasa, February 15th: Panchen Erdini Qoigyijabu, Vice Chairman of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress and Acting Director of the Preparatory Committee for the Tibet Autonomous Region, returned to Lhasa today from Xining by special plane.

Also arriving on the same plane were Pagbalha Geleg Namgyal, Vice Chairman of the National Committee of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference and Vice Chairman of the Preparatory Committee for the Tibet Autonomous Region, and Zhou Renshan, Deputy Secretary of the Tibet Work Committee of the Communist Party of China.

Panchen Erdini and Pagbalha were warmly welcomed by more than 1,500 people at the square in front of Drepung Monastery in the western suburbs of Lhasa.

Welcoming them there were Zhang Jingwu, Representative of the Central People's Government in Tibet and Secretary of the Tibet Work Committee of the Communist Party of China, Guo Xilan, Deputy Secretary of the Tibet Work Committee of the Communist Party of China, Zhan Huayu, Acting Political Commissar of the Tibet Military Region of the People's Liberation Army, Sangpo Tenzin Dhondup, Deputy Commander, Trijang Lozang Yeshe, member of the Preparatory Committee for the Tibet Autonomous Region, leading cadres of various organs, troops, and people's organizations in Lhasa, as well as representatives from the education and religious circles. Many Lhasa citizens and suburban farmers also came to welcome them. The lamas of Drepung Monastery, holding incense and prayer flags, lined up in the square according to religious rituals to greet them.

At 5 p.m., when the cars carrying Panchen Erdini and Pagbalha drove into the western suburban square, warm applause and cheers immediately erupted on the square.

Amidst welcoming music, Young Pioneers from Lhasa Middle School presented flowers to Panchen Erdini and Pagbalha.

Panchen Erdini and Pagbalha, accompanied by Zhang Jingwu, entered a tent to rest. Zhang Jingwu, Guo Xilan, Zhan Huayu, Sangpo Tenzin Dhondup, and others presented hadas to Panchen Erdini and Pagbalha in the rest tent, and Panchen Erdini and Pagbalha returned hadas. Zhang Jingwu and Panchen Erdini and Pagbalha also had a cordial and pleasant conversation in the rest tent. Subsequently, Panchen Erdini went by car to his residence in the Norbulingka for rest.

In the vast fields of the Lhasa suburbs, red flags were fluttering everywhere, and people were working hard to transport fertilizer, repair canals, and level the land, preparing for spring plowing ahead of schedule. Panchen Erdini expressed great joy when he saw the soaring production enthusiasm of the emancipated working people of Tibet on the road.

### 班禅额尔德尼回到拉萨

1960-02-16
第4版()
专栏：

    班禅额尔德尼回到拉萨
    新华社拉萨15日电　全国人民代表大会常务委员会副委员长、西藏自治区筹备委员会代理主任委员班禅额尔德尼·却吉坚赞今天从西宁乘专机回到拉萨。
    政协全国委员会副主席、西藏自治区筹备委员会副主任委员帕巴拉·卓列朗杰和中共西藏工委副书记周仁山也同机到达。
    班禅额尔德尼和帕巴拉在拉萨市西郊哲蚌寺前面的广场上受到了一千五百多人的热烈欢迎。
    在这里欢迎他们的有中央人民政府驻西藏代表、中共西藏工委书记张经武，中共西藏工委副书记郭锡兰，人民解放军西藏军区代政治委员詹化雨、副司令员桑颇·才旺仁增，西藏自治区筹备委员会委员崔科·登珠泽仁以及拉萨市各机关、部队、各人民团体的负责干部以及教育界、宗教界的代表人士。许多拉萨市民和郊区农民也前来欢迎。哲蚌寺的喇嘛们举着佛香和经幡，按照宗教仪式排列在广场上迎接。
    下午五时，当班禅额尔德尼和帕巴拉乘坐的汽车驶抵西郊广场时，广场上立即响起了一片热烈的掌声和欢呼声。
    在欢迎的乐曲声中，拉萨中学的少先队员向班禅额尔德尼和帕巴拉献了花。
    班禅额尔德尼和帕巴拉在张经武的陪同下进入帐篷休息。张经武、郭锡兰、詹化雨、桑颇·才旺仁增等在休息帐篷里向班禅额尔德尼和帕巴拉献了哈达，班禅额尔德尼和帕巴拉回敬了哈达。张经武和班禅额尔德尼、帕巴拉还在休息帐篷里进行了亲切愉快的交谈。随后，班禅额尔德尼即乘车前往雪策林卡中的住所休息。
    拉萨市郊区广阔的田野上到处红旗招展，人们正在紧张地运肥、修渠和平整土地，提前准备春耕。班禅额尔德尼在路上看到西藏翻身劳动人民奋发高涨的生产热情表示很高兴。
