Title: Grand Rallies Held Throughout the Soviet Union to Celebrate the Tenth Anniversary of the Sino-Soviet Treaty of Friendship, Alliance and Mutual Assistance
Author:
Date: 1960-02-16
Page: 5
Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, February 13th: TASS reported from Moscow on the 13th that grand rallies have been held throughout the Soviet Union in recent days to celebrate the tenth anniversary of the signing of the Sino-Soviet Treaty of Frienship, Alliance and Mutual Assistance.
    A celebration meeting was held in Leningrad on the 13th. The participants of the meeting unanimously passed a congratulatory telegram to the working people of Shanghai. This city on the Neva River has close traditional ties with Shanghai. On the eve of this significant anniversary, the "Electrical Equipment Factory" in Leningrad manufactured a turbine generator with a generating capacity of 100,000 kilowatts for the People's Republic of China.
    At the celebration meeting held in Kiev, the capital of the Ukrainian Republic, the Ukrainian Foreign Minister, Palamarchuk, delivered a report. He pointed out that the glorious treaty has proved its strong vitality. In the past ten years, the brotherly cooperation between the Soviet Union and the People's Republic of China has been strengthened.
    At the meeting held in Riga, the capital of the Latvian Republic, the Riga City Branch of the Soviet-Chinese Friendship Association was established.
    The Deputy Minister of Culture of the Estonian Republic, Usman, delivered a report in Tallinn entitled "On the Increasing Ties Between People from All Walks of Life in Estonia and the Chinese People." The participants of the evening also watched the play "Thunderstorm" by the Chinese playwright Cao Yu.
    A celebration evening was also held in Alma-Ata, the capital of the Kazakh Republic. The meeting was opened by Satpayev, the President of the Academy of Sciences of the Republic and Chairman of the Council of the Kazakh Branch of the Soviet-Chinese Friendship Association.
    Umarov, the President of the Academy of Sciences of the Tajik Republic, delivered a report at the grand celebration meeting held in Stalinabad. He emphasized that the friendship between the peoples of the two countries has a long history, and it is expanding and consolidating year by year.
    In order to welcome this glorious anniversary, the workers of Aktyubinsk in Kazakhstan manufactured a batch of mobile X-ray instruments for China two months ahead of schedule.
    On the 13th, grand rallies were also held in Tashkent, Frunze, Ashgabat, Kishinev, Vilnius, Irkutsk, Khabarovsk, Vladivostok, and other Soviet cities to celebrate this anniversary.
    In recent days, exhibitions celebrating the tenth anniversary of the signing of the Sino-Soviet treaty have also been held in many cities in the Soviet Union. The Moscow Library held an exhibition of Chinese books, and the "Friendship House of All Peoples" held an exhibition of modern Chinese prints. The Georgian National Publishing House also published selected poems and novels of Guo Moruo translated into Georgian. Chinese films were also screened in cinemas in Tbilisi. A Chinese photography exhibition was held in Tashkent, the capital of the Uzbek Republic.

### 庆祝中苏友好同盟来约签订十周年  苏联各地举行盛大集会

1960-02-16
第5版()
专栏：

    庆祝中苏友好同盟来约签订十周年
    苏联各地举行盛大集会
    新华社14日讯　据塔斯社莫斯科13日讯：近几天来，苏联各地都在举行盛大集会，庆祝中苏友好同盟互助条约签订十周年。
    列宁格勒13日举行了庆祝会议。会议的参加者一致通过了给上海劳动人民的贺电。?瓦河上的这个城市同上海有着紧密的传统的联系。在这个具有重大意义的节日前夕，列宁格勒“电力电工器材工厂”又为中华人民共和国制出了一台发电能力为十万瓩的透平发电机。
    在乌克兰共和国首都基辅举行的庆祝会上，乌克兰外交部长帕拉马尔楚克作了报告。他指出，光荣的条约证明了它强大的生命力。在过去的十年中，苏联和中华人民共和国之间的兄弟般的合作加强了。
    在拉脱维亚共和国首都里加举行的会议上，成立了苏中友好协会里加市分会。
    爱沙尼亚共和国文化部副部长乌斯曼在塔林举行的晚会上作了“关于爱沙尼亚各阶层人民和中国人民之间的联系日益加强”的报告。晚会的参加者还观看了中国剧作家曹禺的话剧“雷雨”。
    哈萨克共和国首都阿拉木图也举行了庆祝晚会。这个会议是由共和国科学院院长、苏中友好协会哈萨克分会理事会主席萨特帕耶夫主持开幕的。
    塔吉克共和国科学院院长乌马罗夫在斯大林纳巴德举行的隆重的庆祝会上作了报告。他强调指出，两国人民之间的友谊有着悠久的历史，它正在一年年地扩大和巩固。
    为了迎接这个光荣的纪念日，哈萨克的阿纠宾斯克的工人们比预定期限提前两个月为中国制造出了一批移动式的Ｘ光仪器。
    13日，在塔什干、伏龙芝、阿什哈巴德、基什尼奥夫、维尔纽斯、伊尔库次克、伯力、海参崴以及其他苏联城市都举行了隆重的集会，庆祝这个纪念日。
    这几天，在苏联许多城市还举办了庆祝中苏条约签订十周年的展览会。莫斯科图书馆举办了中国书籍展览会，“各国人民友谊之家”举办了中国现代版画展览会。格鲁吉亚国家出版局还出版了译成格鲁吉亚文的郭沫若诗歌、小说选集。第比利斯的电影院还上映了中国影片。乌兹别克共和国首都塔什干举办了中国摄影展览会。
