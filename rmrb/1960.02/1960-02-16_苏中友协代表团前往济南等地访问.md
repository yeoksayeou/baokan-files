Title: Soviet-Chinese Friendship Association Delegation Visits Jinan and Other Places
Author:
Date: 1960-02-16
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, February 15th. Yelyutin, Vice Chairman of the Council of the Soviet-Chinese Friendship Association and Minister of Higher and Secondary Specialized Education of the Soviet Union, and the delegation of the Soviet-Chinese Friendship Association led by him, left Beijing by train this evening to visit Jinan, Shanghai, Hangzhou and other cities.

　　The delegation participated in various activities in Beijing celebrating the tenth anniversary of the signing of the Sino-Soviet Treaty of Friendship, Alliance and Mutual Assistance treaty. Members of the delegation also went to some factories and universities in Beijing to celebrate the tenth anniversary of the Sino-Soviet Friendship Alliance with workers and students. They were warmly welcomed everywhere.

### 苏中友协代表团前往济南等地访问

1960-02-16
第4版()
专栏：

    苏中友协代表团前往济南等地访问
    新华社15日讯　苏中友好协会理事会副主席、苏联高等教育和中等专业教育部部长叶留金和由他率领的苏中友好协会代表团，今天晚上乘火车离开北京，前往济南和上海、杭州等城市访问。
    代表团在北京参加了我国庆祝中苏友好同盟互助条约签订十周年的各项活动。代表团的成员还分别到北京的一些工厂和大学，同职工和学生们一起，欢庆中苏友好同盟十周年。他们到处受到了热烈的欢迎。
