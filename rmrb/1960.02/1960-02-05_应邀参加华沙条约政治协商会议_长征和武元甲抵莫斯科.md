Title: Chang Zheng and Vo Nguyen Giap Arrive in Moscow to Attend Warsaw Pact Political Consultative Conference
Author:
Date: 1960-02-05
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, 4th: Chang Zheng, member of the Political Bureau of the Central Committee of the Vietnamese Labor Party and Vice Premier of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam, and Vo Nguyen Giap, member of the Political Bureau of the Central Committee and Vice Premier and Minister of National Defense, arrived in Moscow by plane on the 2nd. They were invited to attend the high-level meeting of the Political Consultative Committee of the Warsaw Treaty Organization.

Those who greeted them at the airport were: P.N. Pospelov, Alternate Member of the Presidium of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of the Soviet Union and Secretary of the Central Committee of the CPSU, Ustinov, Member of the Central Committee of the CPSU and Deputy Chairman of the Council of Ministers of the Soviet Union, as well as responsible personnel from the Central Committee of the Communist Party of the Soviet Union and the Soviet Ministry of Foreign Affairs.

Also present to welcome them was Nguyen Song Tung, Charge d'Affaires ad interim of the Vietnamese Embassy in the Soviet Union.

Chang Zheng and Vo Nguyen Giap passed through Beijing on their way to Moscow. When they left Beijing on the 2nd, those who saw them off at the airport were: Chen Yi and Tan Zhenlin, members of the Political Bureau of the Central Committee of the CPC and Vice Premiers of the State Council, and Liu Ningyi, member of the Central Committee of the CPC. Tran Tu Binh, the Vietnamese Ambassador to China, and Antonov, Charge d'Affaires ad interim of the Soviet Embassy in China, also saw them off at the airport.

### 应邀参加华沙条约政治协商会议  长征和武元甲抵莫斯科

1960-02-05
第6版()
专栏：

    应邀参加华沙条约政治协商会议
    长征和武元甲抵莫斯科
    新华社4日讯　莫斯科消息：越南劳动党中央政治局委员、越南民主共和国副总理长征，中央政治局委员、副总理兼国防部长武元甲，2日乘飞机到达莫斯科。他们是应邀前往参加华沙条约政治协商委员会高级会议的。
    到机场迎接的有：苏联共产党中央委员会主席团候补委员、苏共中央书记处书记波斯别洛夫，苏共中央委员、苏联部长会议副主席乌斯季诺夫，以及苏联共产党中央委员会和苏联外交部的负责工作人员。
    前往欢迎的还有越南驻苏联临时代办阮双松。
    长征和武元甲在前往莫斯科途中曾经路过北京。他们在2日离开北京时，到机场送行的有：中共中央政治局委员、国务院副总理陈毅、谭震林，中共中央委员刘宁一。越南驻中国大使陈子平、苏联驻中国大使馆临时代办安东诺夫也到机场送行。
