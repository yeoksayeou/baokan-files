Title: Chairman Zhu De Meets Chairman Fierlinger; Banquet Held Same Evening to Welcome Distinguished Guests
Author:
Date: 1960-02-12
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, February 11th: Chairman Zhu De of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress met this afternoon with Chairman Fierlinger of the Czechoslovak National Assembly and all members of the Czechoslovak National Assembly delegation led by him. The host and guests had a cordial and friendly conversation.
    Present at the meeting were Vice Chairmen Guo Moruo and Lin Feng, Vice Premier Chen Yi, members of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress Wang Weizhou, Wu Xinyu, Zhang Su, and Vice Minister of Foreign Affairs Zeng Yongquan.
    Also present at the meeting was Czechoslovak Ambassador to China, Bušiňák.
    Xinhua News Agency, February 11th: Chairman Zhu De of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress held a banquet this evening to welcome the Czechoslovak National Assembly delegation led by Chairman Fierlinger of the National Assembly.
    At the banquet, which was filled with a friendly atmosphere between China and Czechoslovakia, Chairman Zhu De and Chairman Fierlinger spoke successively. They wished the unbreakable friendship between the people of China and Czechoslovakia would develop forever, wished the socialist construction of China and Czechoslovakia would continuously achieve brilliant victories, and wished the unity and cooperation of the socialist camp headed by the Soviet Union would be strengthened day by day.
    Czechoslovak Ambassador to China, Bušiňák, was invited to attend the banquet.
    Present at the banquet were Vice Chairmen Guo Moruo and Lin Feng, Vice Premier Chen Yi, some members of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress, Vice Minister of Foreign Affairs Zeng Yongquan, and others. After the banquet, Fierlinger and all members of the delegation attended a Peking Opera performance held in their honor.

### 朱德委员长会见费林格主席  同日晚间举行宴会欢迎贵宾

1960-02-12
第1版()
专栏：

    朱德委员长会见费林格主席
    同日晚间举行宴会欢迎贵宾
    新华社11日讯　全国人民代表大会常务委员会委员长朱德，今天下午会见了捷克斯洛伐克国民议会主席费林格和由他率领的捷克斯洛伐克国民议会代表团全体人员，宾主进行了亲切友好的交谈。
    会见时在座的，有副委员长郭沫若、林枫，副总理陈毅，全国人民代表大会常委会委员王维舟、武新宇、张苏，外交部副部长曾涌泉等。
    会见时，捷克斯洛伐克驻中国大使布希尼亚克也在座。
    新华社11日讯　全国人民代表大会常务委员会委员长朱德今天晚间举行宴会，欢迎由国民议会主席费林格率领的捷克斯洛伐克国民议会代表团。
    在洋溢着中捷友好气氛的宴会上，朱德委员长和费林格主席先后讲话，他们祝中捷两国人民牢不可破的友谊永远发展，祝中捷两国的社会主义建设不断获得辉煌的胜利，祝以苏联为首的社会主义阵营的团结和合作日益加强。
    捷克斯洛伐克驻中国大使布希尼亚克应邀出席了宴会。
    出席宴会作陪的，有副委员长郭沫若、林枫，副总理陈毅，全国人民代表大会常务委员会的部分委员，外交部副部长曾涌泉等。宴会以后，费林格和代表团的全体成员出席了为欢迎他们而举行的京剧晚会。
