Title: Designed "Hands of Friendship" for Xu Xuehui, Lomanov Awarded Certificate of Merit
Author:
Date: 1960-02-07
Page: 3
Xinhua News Agency, February 5th. TASS, Moscow, February 4th: A. Lomanov, director of the Chita Orthopedic Equipment Factory in the Soviet Union, who designed the artificial hand for Xu Xuehui, was honorably awarded a certificate of merit by the Chita City Labor People's Representatives Soviet and the Chita City Committee of the Communist Party of the Soviet Union.

### 为徐学惠设计友谊之手  洛玛诺夫荣获奖状

1960-02-07
第3版()
专栏：

    为徐学惠设计友谊之手
    洛玛诺夫荣获奖状
    新华社5日讯　据塔斯社莫斯科4日报道：为徐学惠设计假手的苏联赤塔整形器材厂厂长阿·洛玛诺夫，光荣地获得了赤塔市劳动人民代表苏维埃和苏联共产党赤塔市委员会的奖状。
