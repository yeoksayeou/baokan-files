Title: Japanese "Zenshinza" Theater Troupe Visits and Performs in Xi'an
Author:
Date: 1960-02-29
Page: 7
    Xinhua News Agency, 28th. The Japanese "Zenshinza" Theater Troupe, led by the troupe leader Cho-juro Kawarasaki and deputy leaders Kan'emon Nakamura and Gasei Miyakawa, left Beijing by special train this morning for a visit and performance in Xi'an. After visiting Xi'an, the "Zenshinza" Theater Troupe will also go to Wuhan, Nanjing, Shanghai, Guangzhou and other cities for performances.
    Yang Hansheng, Vice President of the Chinese People's Association for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries, council member You Fengqi, and well-known figures from the capital's literary and art circles such as Mei Lanfang, Ouyang Yuqian, Lü Ji, Ma Shaobo, Wu Xue, Ouyang Shanzun, Ren Hong, Li Ni, Jin Ziguang, Yan Yong, Li Shaochun, Yuan Shihai, and more than one hundred others, went to the station to see them off. Actors from the capital presented flowers to the guests.
    Hwang Bong-koo, a Korean peace activist in Beijing, also went to the station to see them off.
    The "Zenshinza" Theater Troupe performed a total of five shows in Beijing. The excellent traditional Japanese plays they performed were warmly welcomed by audiences in the capital.

### 日本“前进座”剧团赴西安访问演出

1960-02-29
第7版()
专栏：

    日本“前进座”剧团赴西安访问演出
    新华社28日讯　日本“前进座”剧团在团长河原崎长十郎，副团长中村翫右卫门、宫川雅青率领下，于今天上午乘专车离开北京，前往西安访问演出。“前进座”剧团访问西安后，还将去武汉、南京、上海、广州等城市演出。
    到车站欢送的，有中国人民对外文化协会副会长阳翰笙、理事犹凤岐，以及首都文艺界著名人士梅兰芳、欧阳予倩、吕骥、马少波、吴雪、欧阳山尊、任虹、李尼、金紫光、宴甬、李少春、袁世海等一百多人。首都的演员们，向客人们献了鲜花。
    到车站欢送的，还有在京朝鲜和平人士黄凤九。
    “前进座”剧团在北京共演出了五场。他们演出的日本优秀传统剧目，受到首都观众的热烈欢迎。
