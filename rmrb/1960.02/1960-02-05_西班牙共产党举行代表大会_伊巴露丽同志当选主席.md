Title: Communist Party of Spain Holds Congress; Comrade Ibárruri Elected Chairman
Author:
Date: 1960-02-05
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, Paris, February 3rd: According to a report in the French newspaper "l'Humanité," the Communist Party of Spain has published a communiqué regarding its Sixth Congress.

The communiqué states that the Communist Party of Spain held its Sixth Congress from January 28th to 31st. Delegates from party organizations within Spain and party organizations residing abroad attended the congress.

The agenda of the congress included: a report on the work of the Central Committee (Santiago Carrillo); a report on the revision of the Party Program (Fernando Claudín), revision of the Party Constitution; a report on the fortieth anniversary of the founding of the Communist Party of Spain (Dolores Ibárruri); and the election of the Central Committee.

The communiqué states that, after comprehensive and in-depth discussions, the congress unanimously approved the Central Committee's summary report, the report on the revision of Party Program and Constitution, and the report on the fortieth anniversary of the founding of the Communist Party.

The congress unanimously elected new members of the Party Central Committee and adopted a political resolution, an appeal to anti-Franco forces in Spain, a resolution on defending peace, and a letter of condolence to political prisoners. The letter of condolence called on the Spanish people and international democratic public to launch a movement to strive for amnesty for Spanish political prisoners and exiles.

The communiqué states that the Communist Party of the Soviet Union, the Communist Party of China, and fraternal parties from many other countries sent congratulatory telegrams to the congress.

The communiqué says that the Sixth Congress was a vivid demonstration of the strong unity of the Communist Party of Spain. The work of the congress demonstrated the increased political and ideological level of party cadres and showed the growth of party organizations and the party's influence throughout Spain.

At its first plenary session, the Central Committee elected the Executive Committee and unanimously elected Comrade Dolores Ibárruri as Chairman of the Communist Party of Spain and Comrade Santiago Carrillo as General Secretary of the Party.

The Central Committee elected a Secretariat composed of the following personnel: Santiago Carrillo, Fernando Claudín, Ignacio Gallego, Antonio Mije, Eduardo García.

### 西班牙共产党举行代表大会  伊巴露丽同志当选主席

1960-02-05
第5版()
专栏：

    西班牙共产党举行代表大会
    伊巴露丽同志当选主席
    据新华社巴黎3日电　据法国“人道报”报道，西班牙共产党发表了关于党的第六次代表大会的公报。
    公报说，西班牙共产党从1月28日到31日举行了第六次代表大会。参加大会的有来自西班牙国内的党组织的代表和侨居国外的党组织的代表。
    代表大会的议程包括：中央委员会工作的报告（圣地亚哥·卡里略）；关于修改党纲的报告（费尔南多·克劳丁）；修改党章；关于西班牙共产党成立四十周年的报告（多洛雷斯·伊巴露丽）；选举中央委员会。
    公报说，经过全面而深入的讨论，代表大会一致同意中央委员会的总结报告、修改党纲和党章的报告以及关于西班牙共产党成立四十周年的报告。
    代表大会一致选举了党中央委员会新的成员，通过了政治决议、致西班牙反佛朗哥力量的呼吁书、保卫和平的决议以及致政治犯的慰问信。慰问信中呼吁西班牙人和国际民主公众展开争取大赦西班牙政治犯和流亡者的运动。
    公报说，苏联共产党、中国共产党和其他许多国家的兄弟党致电代表大会表示祝贺。
    公报说，第六次代表大会是西班牙共产党坚强团结的生动表现。代表大会的工作表明，党干部的政治思想水平提高了，并显示了党组织的壮大和党在西班牙全国的影响。
    中央委员会在第一次全体会议上选出了执行委员会，并且一致选举多洛雷斯·伊巴露丽同志为西班牙共产党主席，圣地亚哥·卡里略同志为党的总书记。
    中央委员会选举了由下列人员组成的书记处：圣地亚哥·卡里略、费尔南多·克劳丁、伊格纳西奥·加列果、安东尼奥·米赫、爱德华多·加西亚。
