Title: Chilean Painter Escamez Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1960-02-17
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, February 15th. Julio Escamez, a Chilean painter who was invited to China by the Chinese People's Association for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries, arrived in Beijing on the 15th.

Hua Junwu, Secretary-General of the Chinese Artists Association, and others welcomed him at the station.

Before arriving in Beijing, Escamez visited Kunming, Chengdu, Chongqing, Wuhan, Shanghai and other cities.

### 智利画家爱斯卡麦兹抵京

1960-02-17
第4版()
专栏：

    智利画家爱斯卡麦兹抵京
    新华社15日讯　应中国人民对外文化协会邀请来中国访问的智利画家胡利奥·爱斯卡麦兹在15日到达北京。
    中国美术家协会秘书长华君武等到车站欢迎。
    爱斯卡麦兹在来京以前，曾访问了昆明、成都、重庆、武汉、上海等城市。
