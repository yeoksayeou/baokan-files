Title: Bulgaria and Hungary Expand Economic Cooperation
Author:
Date: 1960-02-26
Page: 5
Xinhua News Agency, Sofia, February 25th: According to reports in the newspapers here today, the economic delegations of the Bulgarian and Hungarian governments held talks in Sofia from the 16th to the 24th, discussing the further expansion of specialization and cooperation in the machine manufacturing industries of the two countries, as well as the increase in the volume of trade between the two countries from 1961 to 1965.

After the talks, the two sides signed relevant agreements and decided to establish a Bulgarian-Hungarian Economic Cooperation Committee.

### 保匈扩大经济合作

1960-02-26
第5版()
专栏：

    保匈扩大经济合作
    新华社索非亚25日电　据这里的报纸今天报道，保加利亚和匈牙利两国政府的经济代表团于16日到24日在索非亚举行会谈，讨论了关于进一步扩大两国机器制造业的专业化和协作问题，以及两国在1961—1965年间增加换货量的问题。
    经过会谈，双方签订了有关协议，并且决定成立保匈经济合作委员会。
