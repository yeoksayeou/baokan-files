Title: NPC Standing Committee Holds Enlarged Meeting; Lü Dong Reports on the Work of the Metallurgical Industry in 1959
Author:
Date: 1960-02-24
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, February 23rd - The Standing Committee of the National People's Congress held its 21st meeting in an enlarged session this afternoon, hearing a report by Lü Dong, Vice Minister of the Metallurgical Industry, on the work of the Metallurgical Industry in 1959 and the tasks for 1960. Members of the Standing Committee of the National Committee of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference attended the meeting as observers.

### 人大常委会举行扩大会议  吕东报告冶金工业1959年工作情况

1960-02-24
第1版()
专栏：

    人大常委会举行扩大会议
    吕东报告冶金工业1959年工作情况
    新华社23日讯　全国人民代表大会常务委员会在23日下午举行第二十一次会议扩大会议，听取了冶金工业部副部长吕东所作的关于冶金工业1959年工作情况和1960年工作任务的报告。政协全国委员会的常务委员列席了这次会议。
