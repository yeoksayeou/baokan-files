Title: Taiyuan Iron and Steel Company Manufactures Automatic Combined Unloading Machine
Author:
Date: 1960-02-03
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, Taiyuan, January 31st. Workers at the Transportation Department of Taiyuan Iron and Steel Company recently successfully trial-produced an automatic combined unloading machine. This unloading machine can unload small-particle raw materials such as coal and ore, and can unload 200 tons of coal per hour, increasing efficiency by 57 times compared to manual coal unloading. From now on, the coal unloading workers of Taiyuan Iron and Steel Company have been freed from heavy physical labor, put down their shovels and carrying baskets, and realized mechanized coal unloading.

This unloading machine is composed of five parts: walking, lifting, hoisting, conveyor belt, and ejector, weighing 25 tons and has more than 2,000 parts. It was successfully trial-produced by the leading cadres, workers, and technicians of the Transportation Department using the "three-in-one" method. During the trial production process, it also received support from sixteen fraternal units, which helped to overcome many difficulties.

### 太钢制成自动联合卸车机

1960-02-03
第1版()
专栏：

    太钢制成自动联合卸车机
    新华社太原31日电　太原钢铁公司运输部职工最近试制成功一部自动联合卸车机。这台卸车机可以卸煤、卸矿石等小颗粒原材料，每小时能卸煤二百吨，比人工卸煤提高效率五十七倍。从此，太原钢铁公司的卸煤工人摆脱了笨重的体力劳动，放下了铁锹、抬筐，实现了卸煤机械化。
    这台卸车机是由走行、升降、卷扬、输送带、抛物器等五个部分组成的，重二十五吨，共有二千多个零件。它是运输部的领导干部、工人和技术人员，采用“三结合”的办法试制成功的。在试制过程中，还得到十六个兄弟单位的支援，帮助克服了许多困难。
