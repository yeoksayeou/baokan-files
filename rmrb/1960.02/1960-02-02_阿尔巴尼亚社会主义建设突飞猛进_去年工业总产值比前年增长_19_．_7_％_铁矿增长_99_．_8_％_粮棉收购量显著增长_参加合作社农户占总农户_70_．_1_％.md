Title: Albania's Socialist Construction Leaps Forward: Last Year's Total Industrial Output Value Increased by 19.7% Compared to the Previous Year; Iron Ore Increased by 99.8%; Significant Increase in Grain and Cotton Procurement; Farmer Households Participating in Cooperatives Account for 70.1% of Total Farmer Households
Author:
Date: 1960-02-02
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, Tirana, January 31st: The Albanian State Planning Commission Statistics Bureau released a communique on January 31st regarding the implementation of the 1959 national economic plan.

The communique pointed out that the total industrial output value in 1959 increased by 19.7% compared to 1958, which is about 2% higher than the 1960 level determined by the Third Congress of the Labor Party held in 1956.

The communique said that the industrial sector completed 102.1% of the total output value plan in 1959, and overproduced products worth 640 million lek above the plan.

Compared with 1958, the growth figures of major industrial products are as follows: electricity - 18%, crude oil - 18.9%, gasoline - 52.3%, coal - 12.6%, copper ore - 20.4%, chromium ore - 23.1%, iron ore - 99.8%. Cotton fabrics increased by 7.3%.

In terms of labor productivity, labor productivity has been improved in all sectors of the national economy. The petroleum industry increased by 17.8%, and the chromium ore and asphalt industries increased by 27.3% and 15.4% respectively.

The communique pointed out that great achievements were also made in agriculture in 1959. The agricultural collectivization movement has been further developed. The proportion of land cultivated by cooperatives in the country's cultivated land area has increased from 76.2% in 1958 to 83.2%. At present, farmer households participating in cooperatives account for 70.1% of the total number of farmer households in the country.

The area of ​​newly reclaimed land in 1959 increased by about one-half compared with 1958. In 1959, the state purchased 7.7% more grain than in 1958, 11.2% more cotton, 90.1% more sugar beets, and 40.5% more tobacco.

Agricultural mechanization has also been further developed. The amount of tillage by tractor stations increased by 30.7% compared to 1958.

The communique pointed out that the transportation plan for 1959 was also completed. The amount of construction completed 102% of the plan, an increase of 27% compared to 1958.

The communique said that the number of employees nationwide in 1959 increased by 15% compared to 1958. The turnover of socialist commerce increased by 13.1% compared to 1958.

### 阿尔巴尼亚社会主义建设突飞猛进  去年工业总产值比前年增长19．7％　铁矿增长99．8％  粮棉收购量显著增长　参加合作社农户占总农户70．1％

1960-02-02
第6版()
专栏：

    阿尔巴尼亚社会主义建设突飞猛进
    去年工业总产值比前年增长19．7％　铁矿增长99．8％
    粮棉收购量显著增长　参加合作社农户占总农户70．1％
    新华社地拉那31日电　阿尔巴尼亚国家计划委员会统计局在1月31日发表了关于1959年国民经济计划执行情况的公报。
    公报指出，1959年工业总产值比1958年增长了19．7％，比1956年召开的劳动党第三次代表大会所确定的1960年水平还高大约2％。
    公报说，1959年工业部门完成了总产值计划的102．1％，超计划生产了价值六亿四千万里克的产品。
    与1958年相比，各项主要工业产品增长数字如下：电力——18％，原油——18．9％，汽油——52．3％，煤——12．6％，铜矿——20．4％，铬矿——23．1％，铁矿——99．8％。棉织品增长了7．3％。
    在劳动生产率方面，所有国民经济各部门的劳动生产率都有了提高。石油工业部门提高了17．8％，铬矿和沥青工业部门分别提高了27．3％和15．4％。
    公报指出，1959年在农业方面也取得了巨大的成绩。农业合作化运动有了进一步的发展。合作社耕种的土地面积在全国耕地面积中所占的比重，已由1958年的76．2％增加到了83．2％，目前参加合作社的农户占全国总农户的70．1％。
    1959年所开垦的新地面积比1958年增加了二分之一左右。1959年国家所收购的粮食比1958年多7．7％，棉花多11．2％，甜菜多90．1％，烟草多40．5％。
    农业机械化也有了进一步的发展。拖拉机站的耕作量比1958年增加了30．7％。
    公报指出，1959年的运输计划也完成了。建筑量完成了计划的102％，比1958年增长了27％。
    公报说，1959年全国职工人数比1958年增长了15％。社会主义商业的流转量比1958年增长了13．1％。
