Title: The Joy of Laborers

Author:

Date: 1960-02-09
Page: 2

    The Joy of Laborers
    (I)
    Steel cables strung across the sky,
    Pulley blocks race like the wind.
    Thousand-year-old carrying poles sink to the bottom of the sea,
    Shoulders are freed and no longer ache.
    (II)
    Trains run across the land,
    Today they climb the slope onto the rooftops.
    Bricks, tiles, sand, gravel, and mortar,
    All are carried by inclined transport.
    More loading, faster transport, higher output,
    The old methods of the past can't compare.

### 壮工的欢乐

1960-02-09
第2版()
专栏：

    壮工的欢乐
    （一）
    半空横拉钢丝绳，
    滑车走线快如风。
    千年扁担沉海底，
    肩膀解放不受疼。
    （二）
    天下列车驰地上，
    今日爬坡上了房。
    砖瓦砂石和灰浆，
    斜坡运输全承当。
    多装快运产量高，
    昔日旧法比不上。
