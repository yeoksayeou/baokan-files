Title: U.S. Warship Intrudes into Fujian Waters; I Lodge the 85th Serious Warning
Author:
Date: 1960-02-10
Page: 4

    U.S. Warship Intrudes into Fujian Waters
    I Lodge the 85th Serious Warning
    Xinhua News Agency, February 9th: On February 9th, from 5:32 AM to 9:45 AM, a U.S. warship intruded into the waters of the Haitan and Dongyin areas of Fujian Province. Regarding this military provocation by the U.S. warship, a spokesperson for our Ministry of Foreign Affairs was instructed to issue the 85th serious warning.

### 美军舰侵入我福建海域  我提出第85次严重警告

1960-02-10
第4版()
专栏：

    美军舰侵入我福建海域
    我提出第85次严重警告
    新华社9日讯　2月9日五时三十二分至九时四十五分，美国军舰一艘侵入我福建省海坛、东引地区海域。对于美国军舰这种军事挑衅，我外交部发言人奉命提出第八十五次严重警告。
