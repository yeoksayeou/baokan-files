Title: Commemorating the 150th Anniversary of the Birth of the Genius Musician Chopin: International Chopin Piano Competition Opens; Chinese Young Pianists Li Mingqiang and Others Participate in the Competition
Author:
Date: 1960-02-24
Page: 5

　　Xinhua News Agency, Warsaw, February 22nd: The Sixth International Chopin Piano Competition solemnly opened tonight at the Warsaw National Philharmonic Hall. Polish party and state leaders Gomulka, Zawadzki, and Cyrankiewicz, as well as prominent figures from the cultural and musical circles, attended the opening ceremony.

　　After the Polish national anthem was played, Drzewiecki, Chairman of the Jury of the Chopin Piano Competition, announced the opening of the competition. Immediately, the brilliantly lit hall, packed with over a thousand listeners, erupted in enthusiastic applause. Subsequently, the world-renowned pianist and Honorary Chairman of the Jury of this year's Chopin Piano Competition, Rubinstein, performed Chopin's Piano Concerto accompanied by the National Philharmonic Orchestra.

　　The formal competition will begin on the 23rd and will last for three weeks, with eighty-six pianists from thirty-one countries participating in the competition.

　　The First International Chopin Music Academic Conference, held in commemoration of the 150th anniversary of Chopin's birth, concluded on the 21st. Conference participants visited Żelazowa Wola, Chopin's birthplace near Warsaw. Zawadzki, Chairman of the Polish Council of State, received the musicians attending the conference and the judges of the Sixth Chopin Piano Competition on the evening of the 21st.

　　The Polish Chopin Society held an exhibition in Warsaw commemorating the 150th anniversary of Chopin's birth, displaying Chopin's manuscripts, letters, the piano used by the composer, and many other precious cultural relics. Many cities held musical evenings featuring performances and appreciation of Chopin's works, and the melodies of Chopin's music resounded throughout Poland.

　　Xinhua News Agency, Warsaw, February 23rd: The first round of the Sixth International Chopin Piano Competition began this morning at the Warsaw National Philharmonic Hall.

　　Eighty-six young pianists from thirty-one countries, including Poland and the Soviet Union, are participating in the first round of the competition. China's Li Mingqiang, Gu Shengying, and Lin Ling will participate in the competition.

　　The competition will be held in three rounds and will end on March 13th. In the first round, participants must play two Chopin piano etudes, as well as a Chopin polonaise, nocturne, and other piece of music before the judging committee.

　　The judging committee consists of thirty-four famous pianists, composers, and musicians from twenty-three countries. Chinese composer Ding Shande is participating in the judging committee.

　　Today, in the concert hall, which was decorated with the flags of thirty-one countries and a bronze statue of Chopin, more than a thousand music lovers and young members of the Polish Chopin Society were in attendance to listen.

### 纪念天才音乐家萧邦诞生150周年  国际萧邦钢琴演奏比赛开幕  我国青年钢琴家李名强等参加比赛

1960-02-24
第5版()
专栏：

    纪念天才音乐家萧邦诞生150周年
    国际萧邦钢琴演奏比赛开幕
    我国青年钢琴家李名强等参加比赛
    新华社华沙22日电　第六届国际萧邦钢琴演奏比赛今晚在华沙国家音乐厅隆重开幕。波兰的党和国家领导人哥穆尔卡、萨瓦茨基、西伦凯维兹以及文化、音乐界的著名人士出席了开幕式。
    在演奏了波兰国歌之后，萧邦钢琴比赛裁判委员会主席德尔泽维茨基宣布比赛会开幕。于是，灯火辉煌、挤满了一千多名听众的大厅，迸发出了热烈的掌声。接着，世界著名的钢琴家、本届萧邦钢琴比赛裁判委员会名誉主席鲁宾斯坦，在国家音乐厅交响乐队的伴奏下演奏了萧邦的钢琴协奏曲。
    正式比赛将从23日开始，比赛将延续三个星期，有三十一个国家的八十六名钢琴家参加竞赛。
    为纪念萧邦诞生一百五十周年而举行的第一届国际萧邦音乐学术会议已在21日结束。会议参加者访问了华沙近郊萧邦的诞生地热里亚左瓦—沃里亚。波兰国务委员会主席萨瓦茨基在21日晚接见了参加会议的音乐家和第六届萧邦钢琴比赛的裁判。
    波兰萧邦协会在华沙举办了萧邦诞生一百五十周年展览会，展出了萧邦的手稿、信件、作曲家使用过的钢琴等许多珍贵的文物。许多城市举办了演奏和欣赏萧邦作品的音乐晚会，萧邦音乐的旋律响彻在波兰全国各地。
    新华社华沙23日电　第六届国际萧邦钢琴演奏比赛今天上午在华沙国家音乐厅开始第一轮比赛。
    参加第一轮比赛的有波兰、苏联等三十一个国家的八十六位青年钢琴家。中国的李名强、顾圣婴、林玲将参加比赛。
    比赛将分三轮进行，到3月13日结束。比赛的参加者在第一轮比赛时要在评判委员会前弹奏萧邦的两个钢琴练习曲，以及萧邦的波兰舞曲、夜曲、乐曲各一。
    比赛评判委员会由二十三个国家的三十四位著名钢琴家、作曲家、音乐家组成，中国作曲家丁善德参加了评判委员会。
    今天在挂着三十一个国家国旗和摆着萧邦铜像的音乐厅中，有一千多名音乐爱好者和波兰萧邦协会的年轻的会员们在场旁听。
