Title: Second Secretary Niang of the African Independence Party Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1960-02-23
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, February 21st: Babacar Niang, the Second Secretary of the African Independence Party, a guest from Senegal, arrived in Beijing by plane this afternoon at the invitation of the Chinese Committee for Afro-Asian Solidarity.

Wang Mingyuan, Acting Secretary-General of the Chinese Committee for Afro-Asian Solidarity, and others welcomed him at the airport.

### 非洲独立党第二书记尼昂到京

1960-02-23
第4版()
专栏：

    非洲独立党第二书记尼昂到京
    新华社21日讯　应中国亚非团结委员会邀请来我国访问的塞内加尔客人、非洲独立党第二书记巴巴卡·尼昂，今日下午乘飞机到达北京。
    到机场欢迎的有中国亚非团结委员会代秘书长王明远等。
