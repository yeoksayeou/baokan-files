Title: DKP First Secretary Reimann Points Out: West Germany Must Immediately Abandon Nuclear Armament
Author:
Date: 1960-02-12
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, Berlin, 10th. According to TASS, Berlin, 9th: The German "Nine-O-Four Freedom Radio" broadcast a letter of appeal from Max Reimann, First Secretary of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of Germany, to members of the West German Federal Parliament, urging them to oppose Adenauer's policy of preparing for nuclear war.

Reimann emphasized that West Germany must immediately abandon plans to arm the new "Bundeswehr" with nuclear weapons and establish missile bases on West German territory.

Reimann asked members of the West German Federal Parliament to make a decision at their next meeting, requiring the West German government to:

One: Reach an agreement with the government of the German Democratic Republic on holding a referendum on the issue of banning nuclear weapons throughout Germany;

Two: Begin negotiations with the government of the German Democratic Republic on taking measures to ease international tensions and immediately cease the armament of the two Germanys;

Three: Begin negotiations with the government of the German Democratic Republic on establishing an all-German committee, the purpose of which is to formulate detailed proposals for concluding a German peace treaty;

Four: Send a delegation to the upcoming summit conference to support all proposals that can promote the easing of international tensions, disarmament, the prohibition of nuclear weapons, the conclusion of a German peace treaty, and the adjustment of the West Berlin issue.

### 德共第一书记雷曼指出  西德必须立即放弃原子武装

1960-02-12
第6版()
专栏：

    德共第一书记雷曼指出
    西德必须立即放弃原子武装
    新华社10日讯　据塔斯社柏林9日讯：德国九零四自由广播电台广播了德国共产党中央委员会第一书记马克斯·雷曼给西德联邦议院议员的一封呼吁书，敦促他们反对阿登纳的准备原子战争的政策。
    雷曼强调，西德必须立即放弃用原子武器武装新“国防军”和在西德领土上建立导弹基地的计划。
    雷曼要求西德联邦议院议员在下次会议上作出决定，要求西德政府：
    一、同德意志民主共和国政府就在整个德国举行一次关于禁止核武器问题的公民投票达成协议；
    二、同德意志民主共和国政府就采取措施以缓和国际紧张局势和立即停止两个德国的武装开始谈判；
    三、同德意志民主共和国政府就建立一个全德委员会开始谈判，这个委员会的目的是详细制订关于缔结一项对德和约的建议；
    四、派遣一个代表团到未来的首脑会议上去支持所有能促进缓和国际紧张局势、裁军、禁止核武器、缔结一项对德和约和调整西柏林问题的建议。
