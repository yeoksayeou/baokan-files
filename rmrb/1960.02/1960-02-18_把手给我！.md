Title: Give Me Your Hand!
Author: A. Qiaqi
Date: 1960-02-18
Page: 8

Give Me Your Hand!
Albania A. Qiaqi
    Give me your hand!
    Ah! Xu Xuehui,
    How strong your hand is!
    My sister, from where
    Do you get all this strength?
    You feel,
    You and I,
    What kind of friends we have!
    The enemy cut off your hands,
    But they cannot extinguish the flame in your heart!
    This flame burns fiercely, burns on...
    A friend installed a hand for you,
    More powerful
    Than a real hand.
    Everywhere I have been,
    I have heard people call out "Soviet Union."
    In every place in China,
    Everyone calls out "Soviet Union."
    Throughout the world,
    People are heard calling out "Soviet Union."
    Xu Xuehui, my sister,
    You should always remember
    Lomanov, with the hands he gave you,
    You, build the future!
    Because,
    You, maybe me,
    —Although my hair has begun to turn white,
    Will sit on a Soviet rocket,
    To the lunar palace to meet Chang'e.
    Xu Xuehui, my sister,
    You did so well!
    For Communism, you dedicated your hands.
    Ah! How I long to learn from you!
    You are great! My sister,
    You and I
    Are advancing on the same road.
    The enemy will see,
    Even with such hands
    We will build Communism!
        1960, 2, 10 Beijing [Translated by Li Ninglai, proofread by Ge Baoquan]

### 把手给我！
阿·恰奇
1960-02-18
第8版()
专栏：

    把手给我！
    阿尔巴尼亚　阿·恰奇
    把手给我！
    啊！徐学惠，
    你的手多么有力！
    我的姊妹，你从哪里
    获得这一切力量？
    你感受到，
    我和你，
    有着什么样的朋友！
    敌人把你的双手切断，
    可是他们不能把你内心的火焰熄灭！
    这火焰炽烈地燃烧着，燃烧着……
    一个朋友给你安装的手，
    比真手
    更加有力。
    在我到过的所有地方，
    我都听到人们呼唤着“苏联”。
    在中国的各个地方，
    大家都呼唤着“苏联”。
    在整个世界上，
    都听到人们在呼唤着“苏联”。
    徐学惠，我的姊妹，
    应该永远记着
    洛玛诺夫，用他给你的双手，
    你啊，建设起未来！
    因为，
    你，也许我，
    ——虽然我的头发已开始花白，
    将会坐上苏联的火箭，
    到月宫去和嫦娥会见。
    徐学惠，我的姊妹，
    你做的真好！
    为了共产主义，献出了自己的双手。
    啊！我多么渴望向你学习！
    你真行！我的姊妹，
    我和你
    在一条道路上前进。
    敌人会看到，
    就是用这样的双手
    我们也会建设起共产主义！
        1960，2，10　北京〔李宁来译，戈宝权校〕
