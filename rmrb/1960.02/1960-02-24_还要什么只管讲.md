Title: Just Tell Us What Else You Need
Author: Zhang Qiong
Date: 1960-02-24
Page: 2

Just Tell Us What Else You Need
　　Zhang Qiong
　　Dalian Machine Tool Plant
　　Sunday morning,
　　The factory's machines have stopped their clamor;
　　Only in the spare-time processing plant,
　　There is still a busy scene.
　　Little Wang chases after Old Zhang,
　　Teacher and apprentice compete to assemble the universal milling head,
　　Racing against time, neither yielding to the other.
　　Little Wang says:
　　"Today, let's sweat a little more,
　　In autumn, the grains will be fragrant everywhere."
　　Old Zhang says:
　　"If we put on one more screw,
　　The 'Iron Ox' has a healthy guarantee."
　　Piles of parts run through their hands,
　　Universal milling heads lined up in rows,
　　The oiler puts on new clothes for it.
　　The porters load it onto the truck,
　　The workers say to it:
　　"When you see the peasant brothers,
　　Brag about our spare-time processing plant.
　　Tell them,
　　Just tell us what else you need,
　　We are expanding the spare-time processing plant."
　　Note: The universal milling head can replace drilling machines, milling machines, grinding machines, and boring machines. People's communes can use it to repair all farm tools.
                Luda, February 11th

### 还要什么只管讲
张琼
1960-02-24
第2版()
专栏：

    还要什么只管讲
    大连机床厂　张琼
    星期天的早上，
    工厂里的机器停止了喧嚷；
    只有业余加工厂里，
    还是一片忙碌景象。
    小王追赶老张，
    师徒竞赛把万能机头装，
    争分夺秒互不相让。
    小王说：
    “今天多流一滴汗，
    秋天粮谷遍地香。”
    老张说：
    “我们多上一个螺丝钉，
    ‘铁牛’健康有保障。”
    大堆的零件手下跑，
    万能机头排成行，
    油工给它穿上新衣裳。
    搬运工人把它装到汽车上，
    工人们对它把话讲：
    “见了农民兄弟，
    夸夸我们的业余加工厂。
    告诉他们，
    还要什么只管讲，
    我们正在扩大业余加工厂。”
    注：万能机头能代替钻床、铣床、磨床、镗床使用。人民公社用它可以修理一切农具。
                旅大2月11日
