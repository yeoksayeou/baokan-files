Title: Fire in an Indonesian City Leaves Over Five Thousand Overseas Chinese Homeless

Author: 
Date: 1960-02-09
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, Jakarta, February 7th: According to news agency reports here, a fire occurred in Bangka, West Kalimantan, Indonesia, a trading center, on February 2nd.

It is reported that 90% of the city was burned down, with estimated losses of one billion Indonesian Rupiah. More than 80% of Bangka's residents are overseas Chinese. 70% of the shops and businesses destroyed by the fire belong to overseas Chinese. More than five thousand overseas Chinese have been left homeless, along with eight hundred Indonesian citizens of Chinese descent and one thousand two hundred Indonesian residents.

The cause of the fire is still unknown.

### 印度尼西亚一城市发生大火  五千多名华侨无家可归

1960-02-09
第6版()
专栏：

    印度尼西亚一城市发生大火
    五千多名华侨无家可归
    新华社雅加达7日电　据这里的通讯社报道，印度尼西亚西加里曼丹的贸易中心——邦戛在2月2日发生大火。
    据报道，这个城市有90％被烧毁，遭受的损失估计达十亿印度尼西亚盾。邦戛的居民有80％以上是华侨。被大火焚毁的商店和企业中有70％是属于华侨的。有五千多名华侨已经无家可归，有八百名印度尼西亚华裔公民和一千二百名印度尼西亚居民也已经无家可归。
    到目前为止，起火的原因还不知道。
