Title: Khrushchev Receives French Ambassador to the Soviet Union
Author:
Date: 1960-02-11
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, Feb. 10 -- According to TASS, Chairman of the Council of Ministers of the Soviet Union, Khrushchev, received the French Ambassador to the Soviet Union, Maurice Dejean, on the 9th and held talks with him, further defining the schedule for Khrushchev's visit to France.
    Soviet Foreign Minister Gromyko was also present.

### 赫鲁晓夫接见法驻苏大使

1960-02-11
第5版()
专栏：

    赫鲁晓夫接见法驻苏大使
    新华社10日讯　据塔斯社莫斯科讯：苏联部长会议主席赫鲁晓夫9日接见了法国驻苏联大使莫里斯·德让，并同他进行了交谈，进一步确定了赫鲁晓夫访问法国的日程。
    在座的还有苏联外交部长葛罗米柯。
