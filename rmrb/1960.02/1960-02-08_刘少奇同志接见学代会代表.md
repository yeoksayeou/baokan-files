Title: Comrade Liu Shaoqi Receives Representatives of Student Congress

Author:
Date: 1960-02-08
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, 7th February. Liu Shaoqi, Vice Chairman of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of China, and Chen Yi and Tan Zhenlin, members of the Political Bureau of the Central Committee, received all the representatives of the 17th Congress of the All-China Students' Federation this afternoon.

Also present at the reception were leading comrades such as Guo Moruo, Lin Feng, Li Siguang, Zhang Jichun, Zhou Yang, and Hu Yaobang.

During the reception, the student representatives gave long and enthusiastic applause, shouting "Long live Chairman Mao!"

### 刘少奇同志接见学代会代表

1960-02-08
第1版()
专栏：

    刘少奇同志接见学代会代表
    新华社7日讯　中共中央副主席刘少奇，中央政治局委员陈毅、谭震林今天下午接见了中华全国学生第十七届代表大会的全体代表。
    一起接见的，有郭沫若、林枫、李四光、张际春、周扬、胡耀邦等领导同志。
    接见的时候，学生代表们长久发出热烈的掌声，高呼毛主席万岁。
