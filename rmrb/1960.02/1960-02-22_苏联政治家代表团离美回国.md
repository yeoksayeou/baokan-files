Title: Soviet Political Delegation Leaves the US for Home

Author: 
Date: 1960-02-22
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, February 21st, New York Dispatch: According to TASS, the Soviet political delegation headed by Polansky left New York on the evening of the 20th by plane to return home.

The Soviet delegation, which visited the United States at the invitation of the American side, arrived in the United States on January 29. During their stay in the United States, the delegation visited some American cities.

Xinhua News Agency, February 19th, Washington Dispatch: According to TASS, U.S. President Eisenhower received members of the Soviet political delegation, which is visiting the United States, at the White House on the afternoon of the 18th and had a 50-minute conversation with them.

The head of the delegation, Polansky, presented the US President with a gift - a model of the Soviet "Lenin" nuclear-powered icebreaker made of amber.

U.S. Secretary of State Herter received Polansky on the 17th. Those present at the reception included: Soviet Ambassador to the United States Menshikov, U.S. Under Secretary of State Dillon, and Assistant Secretary of State for European Affairs Kohler.

On the 17th, the Soviet political delegation also met with members of the U.S. House of Representatives Foreign Affairs Committee in the U.S. Capitol and held a discussion.

Since arriving in the United States on January 29, the delegation has visited some places in the United States.

### 苏联政治家代表团离美回国

1960-02-22
第5版()
专栏：

    苏联政治家代表团离美回国
    新华社21日讯　据塔斯社纽约讯：以波利扬斯基为首的苏联政治家代表团20日晚间离开纽约乘飞机回国。
    应美国方面邀请前去访问的这个苏联代表团是在1月29日到达美国的。在美国逗留期间，代表团访问一些美国城市。
    据新华社19日讯　据塔斯社华盛顿18日讯：美国总统艾森豪威尔18日下午在白宫接见了正在美国访问的苏联政治家代表团成员，并同他们进行了五十分钟的交谈。
    代表团团长波利扬斯基送给美国总统一分礼物——用琥珀做的苏联“列宁号”原子能破冰船的模型。
    美国国务卿赫脱17日接见了波利扬斯基。参加接见的有：苏联驻美国大使缅希科夫、美国副国务卿狄龙和负责欧洲事务助理国务卿科勒。
    苏联政治家代表团17日还在美国国会大厦同美国国会众议院外交委员会委员们会见并举行了座谈。
    代表团1月29日到达美国以后，在美国一些地方进行了参观。
