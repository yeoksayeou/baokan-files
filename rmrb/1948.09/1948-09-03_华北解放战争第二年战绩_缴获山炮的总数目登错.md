Title: Incorrect Total Number of Mountain Guns Confiscated in the Second Year of the North China Liberation War
Author: Wen Qun
Date: 1948-09-03
Page: 2

Column: Correspondence between Readers and Editors

　　Achievements of the Second Year of the North China Liberation War
  　Incorrect Total Number of Mountain Guns Confiscated
To the Editor:
    The number of mountain guns confiscated in the article "North China Military Region Announces Achievements of the Second Year of the Liberation War" on page one of the People's Daily, Issue 59 (fifteen guns), is incorrect. According to fifty-five guns from the Jin-Ji-Lu-Yu Region and twenty guns from the Jin-Cha-Ji Region, the total should be seventy-five guns.
    I hope this will be corrected and attention paid to typesetting in the future!
    　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　Reader, Wen Qun
To Comrade Wen Qun:
    The error you pointed out was due to insufficient care in our editing and proofreading work. We fully accept your opinion and will pay attention to overcoming it in the future.
    　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　——The Editor

### 华北解放战争第二年战绩　缴获山炮的总数目登错
文群
1948-09-03
第2版()
专栏：编读往来

　　华北解放战争第二年战绩
  　缴获山炮的总数目登错编辑同志：
    人民日报第五十九期第一版之“华北军区公布解放战争第二年战绩”一文内两区总计中的缴获山炮数字（十五门）不确，按晋冀鲁豫区五十五门、晋察冀区二十门，共应为七十五门。
    希更正并在今后排版中注意是荷！
    　　　　　　　　　　　读者文群文群同志：
    你所指出的错误，是由于我们编辑工作和校对工作不够细心所致，你的意见我们完全接受，今后当注意克服。
    　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　——编者
