Title: British Civil Servants' Political Freedom Committee Launches "Protest Against Government's Anti-Communism" Movement
Author:
Date: 1948-09-26
Page: 2

Xinhua News Agency, Northern Shaanxi, 22nd: TASS, London, 18th: The British Civil Servants' Political Freedom Committee has called on all men and women who believe in political freedom to participate in the movement to protest the government's exclusion of the Communist Party. The Civil Servants' Political Freedom Committee was established in July of this year and includes the Association of Scientific Workers, the Civil Service Clerical Association, the Civil Service Union, and the Association of Professional Civil Servants.

### 英公务员政治自由委员会　发起“抗议政府反共”运动

1948-09-26
第2版()
专栏：

　　英公务员政治自由委员会
  　发起“抗议政府反共”运动
    【新华社陕北二十二日电】塔斯社伦敦十八日讯：英国公务员政治自由委员会号召一切信仰政治自由的男女参加抗议政府排除共产党的运动。按公务员政治自由委员会于本年七月成立，包括科学工作者协会、公务书记员协会、公务员联合会和专业公务员协会。
