Title: The Mouthpiece of Three Million People in Southern Shanxi: The Linfen People's Daily Begins Publication
Author:
Date: 1948-09-20
Page: 2

Xinhua News Agency, Jin Sui, 17th: The mouthpiece of three million people in southern Shanxi, the Linfen People's Daily, was officially launched on September 10th. The newspaper is tentatively set to be a four-page edition, published every other day. The distribution area includes 31 counties in southern Shanxi and the two cities of Linfen and Xinyuan.

### 晋南三百万人民的喉舌　临汾人民报发刊

1948-09-20
第2版()
专栏：

　　晋南三百万人民的喉舌
  　临汾人民报发刊
    【新华社晋绥十七日电】晋南三百万人民的喉舌——临汾人民报，已于九月十日正式发刊。该报暂定为四开版，隔日发行。发行地区包括晋南三十一县及临汾、新原两市。
