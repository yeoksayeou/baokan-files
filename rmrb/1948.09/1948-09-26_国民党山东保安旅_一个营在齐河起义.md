Title: Nationalist Party Shandong Security Brigade: A Battalion Uprising in Qihe
Author:
Date: 1948-09-26
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Jinan Front, 24th – The entire 530+ officers and soldiers of the First Battalion of the 23rd Regiment of the Eighth Security Brigade of the Nationalist Party's Shandong Province, led by Battalion Commander Xin Yonggong and Deputy Battalion Commander Li Jiwu, carrying all their weapons, revolted in Beidianzi, south of Qihe (west of Jinan) on September 20th, and joined the People's Liberation Army.

### 国民党山东保安旅　一个营在齐河起义

1948-09-26
第1版()
专栏：

　　国民党山东保安旅
  　一个营在齐河起义
    【新华社济南前线廿四日电】国民党山东省保安第八旅二十三团第一营全体官兵五百三十余人，携带全部武装，在正副营长辛永功、李继武率领下，于九月二十日在齐河（济南西）城南的北店子起义，参加人民解放军。
