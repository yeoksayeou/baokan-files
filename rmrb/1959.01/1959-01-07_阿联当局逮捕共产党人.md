Title: UAR Authorities Arrest Communists
Author: 
Date: 1959-01-07
Page: 5

    UAR Authorities Arrest Communists
    Xinhua News Agency, 6th - Cairo dispatch: Authorities in the United Arab Republic conducted a search and arrest operation against communists in both the Egyptian and Syrian regions during the New Year period.
    According to foreign news agency reports, the arrests began simultaneously in the Egyptian and Syrian regions on December 31st. In the Damascus area, hundreds of people have been arrested, and many others have been arrested in other parts of Syria. More than one hundred people have been arrested in the Egyptian region. Those arrested in the two regions include intellectuals, workers, farmers, and students. At the same time, UAR authorities also closed three publishing houses in Cairo.

### 阿联当局逮捕共产党人

1959-01-07
第5版()
专栏：

    阿联当局逮捕共产党人
    新华社6日讯　开罗消息：阿拉伯联合共和国当局在新年期间对埃及和叙利亚两个地区的共产党人进行了搜捕。
    据外国通讯社报道，逮捕是从12月31日开始在埃及和叙利亚两个地区同时进行的。在大马士革地区已有几百人被捕，在叙利亚其他地区也有许多人被捕。在埃及地区逮捕的有一百多人。在两地区被捕的人中间有知识分子、工人、农民和学生。同时，阿联当局还在开罗封闭了三家出版社。
