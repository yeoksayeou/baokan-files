Title: Ningxia Plain Transforms into a Land of Song and Dance
Author:
Date: 1959-01-11
Page: 5

Ningxia Plain transforms into a land of song and dance
Old men and women dance on the stage
Children form choirs
Cultural troupes are established everywhere
Ningxia Plain transforms into a land of song and dance
Singing makes the Yellow River flow backwards
Singing makes the Helan Mountains tremble
The Autonomous Region gallops on a fine steed
A single day's leap surpasses a thousand years

### 宁夏川变歌舞川
