Title: China and Mongolia Sign Cultural Cooperation Implementation Plan
Author: 
Date: 1959-01-06
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, Ulaanbaatar, January 5th. The Chinese cultural delegation and the Mongolian cultural delegation signed the 1959 Sino-Mongolian Cultural Cooperation Implementation Plan in Ulaanbaatar today.

The plan stipulates that the two countries will send art troupes to visit and perform, and exchange students and graduate students for study in 1959. China will exhibit folk crafts and pictures of China's ten-year construction achievements in Mongolia. Mongolia will exhibit modeling art in China, and the Chinese and Mongolian film studios will continue to complete a documentary film with the theme of friendship between the Chinese and Mongolian people.

The plan also stipulates that scientific and educational institutions of China and Mongolia will continue to maintain direct contact; the sports cooperation projects planned by the sports commissions of the two countries will be one of the components of the implementation plan.

### 中蒙签订文化合作执行计划

1959-01-06
第5版()
专栏：

    中蒙签订文化合作执行计划
    新华社乌兰巴托5日电　中国文化代表团和蒙古文化代表团今天在乌兰巴托签订了1959年两国文化合作执行计划。
    计划规定，在1959年内两国互派艺术团进行访问演出，互派留学生和研究生进行学习。中国将在蒙古展览民间工艺品和中国十年建设成就等图片。蒙古将在中国展览造型艺术，中蒙两国电影制片厂将继续完成一部以中蒙两国人民友谊为题材的纪录影片。
    计划还规定中蒙两国的科学教育等机构继续保持直接联系；两国体育运动委员会预定的体育合作项目，作为执行计划的组成部分之一。
