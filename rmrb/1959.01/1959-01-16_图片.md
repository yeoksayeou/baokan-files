Author: Xiaozhuang

    In Gaochun County, Jiangsu Province, more than 30,000 people were mobilized to vigorously manage wheat fields. This is a scene of members of the Dongba People's Commune leveling ditches and replenishing soil in the wheat fields to ensure the safe overwintering of wheat seedlings.
              Photographed by Xiaozhuang (supplied by Xinhua Daily)
