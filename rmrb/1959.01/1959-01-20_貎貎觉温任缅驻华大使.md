Title: Maung Maung Kyaw Win Appointed Burmese Ambassador to China
Author: 
Date: 1959-01-20
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, 19th — The Government of the Union of Burma has appointed Brigadier General Maung Maung Kyaw Win as Ambassador Extraordinary and Plenipotentiary to the People's Republic of China.

### 貎貎觉温任缅驻华大使

1959-01-20
