Title: U.S. Aircraft Intrudes into Airspace over Lu Feng and Other Areas of China; We Issue the 46th Serious Warning
Author:
Date: 1959-01-06
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, 5th. A U.S. Navy patrol plane intruded into the airspace over the Lu Feng area of Guangdong Province from 8:52 to 9:03 on January 5th. From 14:33 to 14:37 on the 5th, another U.S. Navy patrol plane intruded into the airspace over the Zhoushan area of our country. Regarding this military provocation by U.S. military aircraft, a spokesman for our Ministry of Foreign Affairs was instructed to issue the 46th serious warning.

### 美机侵入我陆丰等地领海上空  我提出第46次严重警告

1959-01-06
第1版()
专栏：

    美机侵入我陆丰等地领海上空
    我提出第46次严重警告
    新华社5日讯　1月5日八时五十二分至九时零三分，美国海军巡逻机一架，侵入我国广东省陆丰地区的领海上空。5日十四时三十三分至十四时三十七分，又一架美国海军巡逻机侵入我国舟山地区的领海上空。对于美国军用飞机的这种军事挑衅，我外交部发言人奉命提出第四十六次严重警告。
