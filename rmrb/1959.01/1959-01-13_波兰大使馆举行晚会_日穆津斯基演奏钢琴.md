Title: Polish Embassy Holds Reception, Szpilman Plays Piano
Author:
Date: 1959-01-13
Page: 5

    Polish Embassy Holds Reception
    Szpilman Plays Piano
    Xinhua News Agency, 12th - The Polish Embassy in China held a piano recital tonight at the Beijing Hotel for Władysław Szpilman, the renowned Polish pianist and prize winner at the Fourth International Chopin Piano Competition.
    Minister of Culture Shen Yanbing, Vice Minister of Foreign Affairs Zeng Yongquan, Deputy Director of the Commission for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries Luo Jun, and Vice Chairman of the Chinese Musicians Association Ma Sicong, stepped onto the stage after the performance to warmly shake hands with the Polish pianist, congratulating him on the success of his performance.
    Tonight was Szpilman's final performance in China. The pianist arrived in Beijing on December 14th of last year and has since visited Beijing, Xi'an, Chengdu, Kunming and other places, performing nine concerts, with over ten thousand listeners. Each performance was warmly welcomed by the audience.

### 波兰大使馆举行晚会  日穆津斯基演奏钢琴

1959-01-13
第5版()
专栏：

    波兰大使馆举行晚会
    日穆津斯基演奏钢琴
    新华社12日讯  波兰驻中国大使馆今晚在北京饭店为波兰著名钢琴家、第四届国际萧邦钢琴比赛会获奖者日穆津斯基举行了钢琴独奏晚会。
    文化部部长沈雁冰、外交部副部长曾涌泉、对外文化联络委员会副主任罗俊和中国音乐家协会副主席马思聪，在演出结束后步上舞台同波兰钢琴家热烈握手，祝贺他的演出成功。
    今晚是日穆津斯基在中国的最后一场演出。钢琴家在去年12月14日到达北京，曾先后在北京、西安、成都、昆明等地访问，演出九场，听众一万多人，每场演出都获得听众的热烈欢迎。
