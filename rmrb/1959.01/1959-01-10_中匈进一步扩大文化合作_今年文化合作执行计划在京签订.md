Title: China and Hungary Further Expand Cultural Cooperation; This Year's Cultural Cooperation Implementation Plan Signed in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1959-01-10
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, January 9th. The 1959 implementation plan for the cultural cooperation agreement between the People's Republic of China and the Hungarian People's Republic was signed in Beijing today. Zhang Zhixiang, Deputy Director of the Chinese People's Association for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries, and Nogradi, the Hungarian Ambassador to China, signed the implementation plan on behalf of their respective governments.

Cultural cooperation between China and Hungary in 1959 will be further expanded compared to 1958. According to the implementation plan signed today, Hungary will send a ballet troupe of fifteen to twenty people to China for a two-week performance tour this year, and send a cultural delegation of three to five people to China for a visit of about one month. China will also send a Guangdong music ensemble of fifteen to twenty people and a delegation of three to four journalists to Hungary for visits of about two weeks and one month respectively. On the occasion of the tenth anniversary of the founding of the People's Republic of China and the fortieth anniversary of the founding of the Hungarian Soviet Republic this year, the two countries will hold the "Exhibition of Achievements in China's Ten Years of Construction" and the "Exhibition of Hungarian Revolutionary Art" respectively.

### 中匈进一步扩大文化合作  今年文化合作执行计划在京签订

1959-01-10
第4版()
专栏：

    中匈进一步扩大文化合作
    今年文化合作执行计划在京签订
    新华社9日讯　中华人民共和国和匈牙利人民共和国文化合作协定1959年执行计划今天在北京签订。我国对外文化联络委员会副主任张致祥和匈牙利驻中国大使诺格拉第，分别代表两国政府在执行计划上签字。
    中匈文化合作在1959年将比1958年进一步扩大。根据今天签订的执行计划，匈牙利方面今年将派遣一个由十五人到二十人组成的芭蕾舞团到中国作为期二周的访问演出，派遣三到五人的文化代表团到中国访问一个月左右。中国方面也将派遣由十五人到二十人组成的广东音乐演奏小组和三到四人的新闻工作者代表团，到匈牙利分别作为期两周和一月左右的访问。在今年我国国庆十周年和匈牙利苏维埃共和国成立四十周年纪念日到来的时候，两国将互相举行“中华人民共和国十年建设成就图片展览会”和“匈牙利革命艺术展览会”。
