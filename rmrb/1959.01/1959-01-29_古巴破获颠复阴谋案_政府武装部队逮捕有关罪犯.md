Title: Cuba Uncovers Subversion Plot, Government Armed Forces Arrest Involved Criminals
Author:
Date: 1959-01-29
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, Havana, 28th: Cuban armed forces uncovered a plot to subvert the provisional government on the 27th. According to a report from Havana Radio, those participating in the conspiracy were a group of former Batista army personnel.

The news stated that the conspiracy was crushed from the outset. The Cuban government armed forces arrested ten former Batista reactionary government army personnel involved in the subversion plot and seized a large quantity of military supplies and weapons used for the subversion activities. Sources say that government authorities are arresting other individuals involved in the conspiracy in five towns in Pinar del Río province. Major Escalona of the armed forces stationed in San Cristóbal, Pinar del Río province, stated that the criminal conspirators arrested will be submitted to a military court for review.

### 古巴破获颠复阴谋案  政府武装部队逮捕有关罪犯

1959-01-29
第5版()
专栏：

    古巴破获颠复阴谋案
    政府武装部队逮捕有关罪犯
    新华社28日讯　哈瓦那消息：古巴武装部队27日破获了一起图谋颠复临时政府的阴谋案。据哈瓦那广播电台报道，参加这一阴谋活动的是一群巴蒂斯塔的前陆军人员。
    消息说，这个阴谋活动在一开始就被粉碎了。古巴政府武装部队逮捕了和这次颠复阴谋有关的十名前巴蒂斯塔反动政府的陆军人员，并且查获了用于颠复活动的大批军火和武器。有消息说，政府当局正在比那尔德里奥省的五个城镇逮捕和这次阴谋有关的其他人员。驻扎在比那尔德里奥省圣克里斯托巴尔的武装部队少校埃斯卡洛纳说，受到逮捕的阴谋罪犯将提交军事法进行审查。
