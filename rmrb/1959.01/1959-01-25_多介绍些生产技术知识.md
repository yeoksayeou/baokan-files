    Introduce More Production Technology Knowledge
    Introducing production technology in newspapers allows for fast dissemination and broad influence. Methods like the previously published steel frying method, ore crushing method, and rope-pulled plows, enabled people to quickly learn these techniques and improve production. Although newspaper space is limited and needs to cover a wide range of areas, it is meaningful to dedicate some space to production technologies that the broad masses urgently need to understand.
          Beijing Mining College　Zou Yong

### 多介绍些生产技术知识
邹用
1959-01-25
第8版()
专栏：读者·作者·编者

    多介绍些生产技术知识
    报纸上介绍生产技术，传播快而且影响广。像从前刊登过的炒钢法、碎矿法，绳索牵引犁等，使人们很快地学到了这种方法，提高了生产。报纸的篇幅有限，需要报道的方面很广，但对广大群众所迫切需要了解的生产技术，就是花一点篇幅也是有意义的。
          北京矿业学院　邹用
