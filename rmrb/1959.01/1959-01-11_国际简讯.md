International Briefs

Indian National Congress Annual Meeting Opens
Nagpur Dispatch: The 64th annual meeting of the Indian National Congress began in Nagpur on the evening of the 9th. More than 2,000 representatives from various regions attended the meeting, and 50,000 people were present as observers.
The resolution on the Five-Year Plan was passed at the open meeting. This plan calls for the rapid accumulation of capital and the strengthening of the public sector. This resolution was previously passed by the Congress Working Committee meeting yesterday.

Nasser Meets with Nkrumah
Cairo Dispatch: UAR President Nasser met with Ghanaian Prime Minister Nkrumah at the Presidential Palace on the evening of the 9th for two hours. Afterwards, Nasser hosted a banquet for Nkrumah.

Philippines Demands Higher Import Taxes on American Goods
According to a report in the "Manila Chronicle" on the 8th, Philippine President Garcia insisted on raising the special import tax rate on American imported goods to 17% during a meeting with American Ambassador Bohlen at the Presidential Palace on the 7th.

Tito Leaves Rangoon for India
Rangoon Dispatch: Yugoslav President Tito concluded his visit to Burma and left Rangoon for India on the morning of the 10th.
Tito arrived in Rangoon on the 8th from Indonesia.

Yugoslavia Accepts British Loan
London Dispatch: A British Foreign Office spokesman announced on the 9th that Yugoslavia had formally accepted a British loan of three million pounds, and the two countries are conducting talks in London on the "details" of the loan.
As early as the end of October last year, Yugoslav State Secretary for Foreign Affairs Popovic went to London to meet with British Foreign Secretary Lloyd, begging Britain for a loan of fifteen million pounds.

### 国际简讯

1959-01-11
第3版()
专栏：国际简讯

    国际简讯
    印度国大党年会开幕
    那格浦尔讯：印度国大党第六十四届年会9日傍晚在那格浦尔开始举行。参加这次会议的有来自各地的两千多名代表，并且有五万人旁听。
    关于五年计划的决议在公开会议上获得了通过。这一计划要求迅速筹集资本，加强公营部分。这一决议案曾经在昨天由国大党议题委员会会议通过。
    纳赛尔同恩克鲁玛会谈
    开罗讯：阿联总统纳赛尔同加纳总理恩克鲁玛9日傍晚在共和国宫晤谈两小时。会后，纳赛尔设宴招待恩克鲁玛。
    菲律宾要求提高美国货进口税率
    据“马尼拉纪事报”8日报道，菲律宾总统加西亚7日在总统府同美国大使波伦举行会谈时，坚持要把对美国进口商品征收的特别进口税的税率提高到17％。
    铁托离仰光去印度
    仰光讯：南斯拉夫总统铁托结束了他对缅甸的访问，10日上午离开仰光前往印度。
    铁托是在8日从印度尼西亚来到仰光的。
    南斯拉夫接受英国贷款
    伦敦讯：英国外交部发言人9日宣布，南斯拉夫正式接受英国三百万英镑的贷款，两国并且正在伦敦就贷款“细节”进行会谈。
    南斯拉夫外交国务秘书长波波维奇早在去年10月底即曾到伦敦去同英国外交大臣劳埃德会谈，向英国乞求一千五百万英镑的贷款。
