Title: Old Farm Work Needs New Methods
Author:
Date: 1959-01-19
Page: 3

Editorial Note:

    Old Farm Work Needs New Methods

    Managing overwintering crops is an old farming task, but how to manage densely planted overwintering crops on deeply plowed land is a new problem. This requires all regions to adapt to new circumstances and create new methods of field management.

    In the past, various regions accumulated valuable experience in managing overwintering crops such as wheat and rapeseed, but this experience was mostly based on managing ordinary fields. Last year, many regions explored some experience in managing "satellite fields," but these were mostly created on small plots of land. Therefore, these management methods are often not fully applicable to the requirements of large-area, high-yield fields.

    Today's news about many places in Sichuan Province adopting new methods to manage spring crops is a new approach to adapt to this new situation. It not only introduces some new management techniques to us, but more importantly, it promptly raises this noteworthy issue for us.

### 老农活需要新做法

1959-01-19
第3版()
专栏：编后

    老农活需要新做法
    管理越冬作物是一件老农活，怎样才能把种在深耕土地上的密植的越冬作物管理好却是新问题。这就要求各地适应新情况，创造新的田间管理方法。
    过去，各地在管理小麦、油菜等越冬作物方面有不少可贵的经验，但是这些经验多数是在管理一般田的基础上产生的；去年许多地区摸索到一些管理“卫星田”的经验，但是它们又多是在小块土地上创造出来的；因此这些管理方法往往不能完全适用于大面积高产田的要求。
    今天本报发表的四川省许多地方采用新方法管理小春作物的消息，正是适应这种新情况的新作法。它不仅向我们介绍了一些新的管理技术，更重要的还在于它及时地向我们提出了这个值得注意的问题。
