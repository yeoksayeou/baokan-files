Brief News

　　Building Hydropower Stations on a Large Scale
　　Guangdong Province, relying on the favorable conditions of abundant water resources in the province, built 1,950 small hydropower stations last year, with a total capacity of over 14,000 kilowatts. Among them, 194 have already started generating electricity.

　　The construction of these hydropower stations has fully mobilized the masses, adopted a combination of indigenous and foreign methods, and overcome difficulties in technology, funds, and equipment.
　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　 (According to Xinhua News Agency)
　　“Attacking穗 Fertilizer”
　　The agricultural department of Nanchong Prefecture, Sichuan, uses microscopes to observe wheat ear primordia, understand the growth of wheat ears, and apply different fertilizers accordingly, to ensure that wheat receives corresponding nutrients during each growth and development period. Now, the Nanchong Prefectural Committee of the Chinese Communist Party is organizing all communes in the prefecture to launch a concentrated campaign to separately apply "穗 fertilizer" (mainly potash fertilizer) and "grain fertilizer" (mainly nitrogen fertilizer).
　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　(According to Sichuan Daily)
　　Forcing the fattening of pigs
　　In order to increase the supply of meat to cities, more than 2 million pigs have been force-fattened in various parts of Sichuan Province in the past month. The pigs being force-fattened generally weigh more than 130 catties, with thick fat and fleshy meat.
　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　(According to Xinhua News Agency)
　　Winter Hunting
　　Tens of thousands of people of Mongolian, Han, Daur, Oroqen, and Evenk ethnicities are engaging in winter hunting activities in the grasslands and mountains of Inner Mongolia. In the Xilingol grasslands alone, Mongolian herdsmen hunted more than 10,000 gazelles and 1,700 wolves in one month.

　　The Inner Mongolia Autonomous Region has recently established artificial breeding farms, preparing to protect and breed the rare and valuable animals hunted. (According to Xinhua News Agency)

### 简明新闻

第3版()
专栏：简明新闻

    简明新闻
    大办水电站
    广东省依靠本省水力资源丰富的有利条件，在去年一年中，兴建小型水力发电站一千九百五十座，总容量可达一万四千多瓩。其中，已开始发电的有一百九十四座。
    兴建这些水电站，充分地发动了群众，采取土洋结合，克服了技术、资金和器材等方面的困难。
                  （据新华社讯）
    “攻穗肥”
    四川南充专区农业部门用显微镜观察麦穗原始体，了解麦穗生长情况，分别追施不同的肥料，以保证小麦在各个生长发育期间得到相应的养料。现在，中共南充地委正组织全专区各公社，开展一个分别追施“攻穗肥”（以钾肥为主）、“攻粒肥”（以氮肥为主）的突击运动。
              （据四川日报报道）
    催育肥猪
    为增加城市肉食供应，四川省各地在最近一个月来已催育肥猪二百多万头。受催育的猪一般都重一百三十斤以上，膘厚肉肥。
                （据新华社讯）
    冬季打猎
    上万的蒙、汉、达斡尔、鄂伦春、鄂温克等民族人民，正在内蒙古草原和山林中展开冬季打猎活动。仅锡林郭勒草原的蒙古族牧民在一个月中，打获黄羊一万多只，狼一千七百多只。
    内蒙古自治区最近设立人工饲养场，准备保护和繁殖猎取的稀有名贵动物。（据新华社讯）
