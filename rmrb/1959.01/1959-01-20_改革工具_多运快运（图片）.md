Title: Reform Tools: More Transportation, Faster Transportation (Pictures)
Author: 
Date: 1959-01-20
Page: 2

    Hubei Province's Lanxi Port manufactured a "plank bridge," enabling cargo trucks to directly deliver goods onto ships, eliminating the need for stevedores and greatly increasing loading efficiency.
    Photo by Huang Shouxiang (Xinhua News Agency draft)
    Going uphill and downhill is the most strenuous labor for stevedores. Hubei Province's Lanxi Port manufactured a wooden-frame chain conveyor, powered by an automobile engine, which can transport various goods down to the river and up to the shore.
    Photo by Huang Shouxiang (Xinhua News Agency draft)
    The Inner Mongolia Department of Transportation manufactured a camel-drawn four-wheeled rubber-tire cart. Two camels can pull 7,000 jin of goods, increasing efficiency tenfold.
    Xinhua News Agency Reporter Altai Photo
    The Maliandao Warehouse of the Beijing Storage and Transportation Company manufactured a parallel conveyor, freeing workers from heavy physical labor and increasing efficiency by 1 to 2 times.
    Xinhua News Agency Reporter An Kang Photo

### 改革工具　多运快运（图片）

1959-01-20
第2版()
专栏：

    改革工具　多运快运
    湖北省兰溪港制造了一种“板桥”，使运货小车能直接将货物送上船，省去了搬运工人搬运，大大提高了装船效率。
    黄守祥摄（新华社稿）
    上坡、下坡是搬运工人最吃力的劳动，湖北省兰溪港制造了一种木架链板机，用汽车引擎带动，就可将各种货物送下河、起上岸。
    黄守祥摄（新华社稿）
    内蒙古交通厅制成一种骆驼牵引的四轮胶皮车，两峰骆驼可拉动七千斤货物，效率提高十倍。
    新华社记者  阿尔泰摄
    北京储运公司马连道仓库制造成平行运输机，解除了工人的繁重体力劳动，提高效1至2倍。
    新华社记者　安康摄
