Title: Celebrating the 15th Anniversary of the Founding of Albania, the Soviet Union Decides to Build a Cultural Palace for Albania
Author:
Date: 1959-01-20
Page: 4

    Celebrating the 15th Anniversary of the Founding of Albania
    The Soviet Union Decides to Build a Cultural Palace for Albania
    Xinhua News Agency, Tirana, January 17th: To celebrate the 15th anniversary of the founding of Albania, the Central Committee of the Communist Party of the Soviet Union has decided to build a cultural palace in Tirana as a gift.
    This decision was conveyed by Khrushchev on behalf of the Central Committee of the CPSU in a letter to Hoxha, First Secretary of the Central Committee of the Albanian Labor Party.
    The cultural palace will cover an area of 8,000 square meters, with a total volume of 55,000 cubic meters. The cultural palace will include a theater with a capacity of 800 spectators, a library with a collection of 500,000 books, an exhibition hall displaying handicrafts, and activity rooms for various cultural groups.

### 庆祝阿尔巴尼亚建国十五周年  苏联决定为阿建文化宫

1959-01-20
第4版()
专栏：

    庆祝阿尔巴尼亚建国十五周年
    苏联决定为阿建文化宫
    新华社地拉那17日电　苏联共产党中央委员会决定在地拉那建立一座文化宫，作为苏联庆祝阿尔巴尼亚建国十五周年的礼品。
    这个决定是由赫鲁晓夫代表苏共中央写给阿尔巴尼亚劳动党中央委员会第一书记霍查的一封信中转告的。
    这个文化宫将占地八千平方公尺，整个体积达五万五千立方公尺。文化宫将包括一座可容纳八百观众的剧场，一个藏书五十万册的图书馆和一个陈列工艺品的展览室以及各种文化小组的活动室等。
