Window of the Great Leap Forward

    Automatic Enlarger
    The Tianjin Innovation Factory recently produced a "Satellite Brand" fully automatic enlarger. Its focal length is automatically controlled, it has an automatic control scale, and it can automatically raise and lower. Therefore, it greatly improves the efficiency of printing work, estimated to be three to five times more efficient than a typical old-fashioned enlarger.
    This enlarger does not include a lens, but a standard 1:7.5 or other general-purpose lens can be installed. Negatives of 24×36 mm or 6×6 cm can be used. The materials used are carefully selected, and each part is both strong and lightweight.

### 自动放大机

1959-01-23
第3版()
专栏：跃进橱窗

    自动放大机
    天津市革新工厂最近制成一种“卫星牌”全自动放大机。它的焦距是自动控制的，有自动控制尺，并且能自动升降。因此，大大提高了对光的工作效率，估计比一般老式放大机可提高效率三至五倍。
    这种放大机不带镜头，但一般1比7.5或其他通用镜头都能安装。底片可用24×36糎或6×6公分底片。它的用料都经过精选，各个部件既坚固又轻巧。
