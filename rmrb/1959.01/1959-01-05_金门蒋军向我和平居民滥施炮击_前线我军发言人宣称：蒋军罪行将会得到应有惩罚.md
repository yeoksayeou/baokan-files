Title: Kinmen Chiang Troops Indiscriminately Shell Peaceful Residents; Frontline PLA Spokesman Declares: Chiang Troops' Crimes Will Receive Due Punishment

Author:

Date: 1959-01-05
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Xiamen, 4th. The spokesman for the People's Liberation Army on the Fujian Front declared: On the morning of January 3rd, the Chiang Kai-shek troops on Kinmen shelled the Greater and Lesser Deng Islands, Weitou, Lianhe, and Xiamen areas over three thousand rounds. Among them, thirty rounds were fired at Shantou Village on Greater Deng Island, causing significant damage to the People's Commune's nursery, resulting in the injury and death of forty-eight innocent residents, including twenty-three children. These heinous acts by the Chiang Kai-shek troops will receive the punishment they deserve.

### 金门蒋军向我和平居民滥施炮击  前线我军发言人宣称：蒋军罪行将会得到应有惩罚

1959-01-05
第1版()
专栏：

    金门蒋军向我和平居民滥施炮击
    前线我军发言人宣称：蒋军罪行将会得到应有惩罚
    新华社厦门4日电　福建前线人民解放军发言人宣称：金门蒋军3日上午向我大、小嶝岛、围头、莲河、厦门地区炮击三千多发。其中向我大嶝岛山头村炮击三十发，该村人民公社托儿所遭受重大损失，共伤亡无辜居民四十八人，内有儿童二十三名。蒋军这种罪恶行为将会得到应有的惩罚。
