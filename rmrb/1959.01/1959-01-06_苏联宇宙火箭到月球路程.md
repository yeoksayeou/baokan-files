Title: The Soviet Union's Cosmic Rocket's Journey to the Moon
Author:
Date: 1959-01-06
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, January 5th: The Soviet Union's cosmic rocket launched from Earth on January 2nd, and on January 4th at 5:59 AM Moscow time, it reached its closest point to the moon in its orbit, traveling 370,000 kilometers from Earth to the Moon in thirty-four hours. The following is the time it would take for a person and various means of transportation to complete this journey (assuming constant speed and a straight-line path):
	Walking	5 kilometers per hour requires more than eight years and one hundred and sixty-three days
	Bicycle	30 kilometers per hour requires approximately one year and one hundred and forty-nine days
	Automobile	100 kilometers per hour requires more than one hundred and fifty-four days
	Passenger plane	800 kilometers per hour requires more than nineteen days

### 苏联宇宙火箭到月球路程
