Title: New Faces Added to the Ranks of Master Athletes
Author:
Date: 1959-01-16
Page: 6
Xinhua News Agency, January 15th: The State Physical Culture and Sports Commission has approved five more master athletes. They include Chang Guanqun, a 46-year-old weightlifter who is currently the oldest master athlete in China, Sun Hongxia, China's first female speed skating master athlete, Wang Jinyu, a 17-year-old male speed skater, Bai Fengchi, a male gymnast from the People's Liberation Army, and Liu Shengcai, a Chinese-style wrestler from Liaoning Province.

### 运动健将榜上增新人

1959-01-16
第6版()
专栏：

    运动健将榜上增新人
    据新华社15日讯　国家体育运动委员会又批准了五名运动健将。包括目前中国年纪最大的运动健将、四十六岁的举重选手常冠群和中国第一个女子速度滑冰运动健将孙洪霞、十七岁的男子速度滑冰选手王金玉和解放军的男子体操选手白凤池，以及辽宁省的中国式摔跤选手刘生才。
