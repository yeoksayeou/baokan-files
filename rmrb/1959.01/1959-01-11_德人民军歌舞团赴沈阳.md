Title: German People's Army Song and Dance Troupe to Shenyang
Author:
Date: 1959-01-11
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency, Tianjin, January 8th. The Erich Weinert Song and Dance Troupe of the German Democratic Republic's People's Army, after a five-day performance and visit in Tianjin, left Tianjin by special train tonight for Shenyang, accompanied by Major General Wei Chuantong, Deputy Minister of the Propaganda Department of the General Political Department of the Chinese People's Liberation Army.

　　The song and dance troupe arrived in Tianjin on the 4th. Their performances were warmly welcomed by the people of Tianjin. The original plan was to perform three shows, but an additional show was added to meet the audience's demands. They also temporarily arranged and performed a dance celebrating the successful launch of the Soviet Union's space rocket.

　　While in Tianjin, the guests also visited Tianjin University, Tianjin Knitting Factory, Tianjin Carpet Factory, Tianjin New Port, and the Haihe River Sluice Construction Site, and held a get-together with the troops stationed in Tianjin. Before leaving, the guests attended a banquet held by the political department of the Tianjin Garrison District of the People's Liberation Army and the Tianjin Municipal Bureau of Culture.

### 德人民军歌舞团赴沈阳

1959-01-11
第4版()
专栏：

    德人民军歌舞团赴沈阳
    新华社天津8日电　德意志民主共和国人民军埃里希·魏纳特歌舞团在天津演出访问五天后，今晚由中国人民解放军总政治部宣传部副部长魏传统少将陪同，乘专车离天津去沈阳。
    歌舞团是在4日到达天津的。他们的演出受到天津人民的热烈欢迎，原订演出三场，为了满足观众的要求又加演了一场。他们还临时编排演了一个庆祝苏联发射宇宙火箭成功的舞蹈。
    客人们在天津还参观访问了天津大学、天津市针织厂、天津地毯厂、天津新港和海河建闸工地，并且和驻津部队进行了联欢。临行前，客人们出席了解放军天津警备区政治部和天津市文化局举行的宴会。
