Title: Vice Premier Chen Yi Receives Prince Halsa; Burhan Holds Banquet to Warmly Welcome Omani Guests
Author:
Date: 1959-01-31
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, January 30th – Vice Premier Chen Yi received Prince Halsa, the Deputy Minister of Religious Affairs of Oman, and his entourage this afternoon. Present at the meeting were Burhan, Chairman of the China Islamic Association, and Vice Chairmen Da Pongsheng and Zhang Jie, as well as Ke Hua, Director of the West Asia and Africa Department of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs.

Xinhua News Agency, January 30th – Burhan, Chairman of the China Islamic Association, held a banquet at the Beijing Hotel this evening to warmly welcome Prince Halsa, the Deputy Minister of Religious Affairs of Oman, who is representing the Omani people in their anti-colonialist struggle.

In his speech at the banquet, Prince Halsa conveyed the friendly sentiments of the Omani people towards the Chinese people. He said, "I believe that this visit is a good beginning for strengthening the friendly relations between our two countries." "Now we have found many friends, and we are very happy."

Prince Halsa reaffirmed the unwavering will of the Omani people to carry out the anti-colonialist struggle. He said, "The Omani people are engaged in the most arduous and difficult struggle with imperialism. They will never lay down their weapons until they have won the final victory."

Prince Halsa said, "Oman is a part of Asia, and our struggle relies on the support of the people of Asia and Africa, and on the support of all peace-loving people." The Prince said with confidence: "After the victory of the struggle, the Omani people will establish a democratic society, so that the country is full of justice and prosperity."

After Prince Halsa's speech, he warmly shook hands and embraced Burhan.

Burhan delivered a welcoming speech at the beginning of the banquet. He said, "The struggle of the Omani people against British imperialism, led by the Imam of Oman, is an important component of the Arab people's movement to win national independence and oppose colonialism." He pointed out that the heroic and tenacious struggle waged by the Omani people has won the sympathy and admiration of all peace-loving countries and people in the world.

In his speech, Burhan condemned the crimes of the imperialists in invading Oman. He also pointed out that in the era when the East wind prevails over the West wind, it is impossible for US and British imperialism to stay in Oman and the Arab region. Burhan declared: "The Chinese people have always deeply sympathized with and cared for, and resolutely supported the just struggle of the Omani people." He said, "We firmly believe that the anti-colonialist struggle of the Omani people, with the firm support of Arab countries, Asian and African countries, and all peace-loving countries and people in the world, as long as they persevere in the struggle to the end, and are sufficiently vigilant against all the conspiracies and tricks of US and British imperialism, will eventually win the final victory."

Kabbani, Chargé d'Affaires ad interim of the United Arab Republic in China, was invited to attend the banquet.

Also present at the banquet were Da Pongsheng and Zhang Jie, Vice Chairmen of the China Islamic Association, He Chengxiang, Director of the Religious Affairs Bureau of the State Council, and Ke Hua, Director of the West Asia and Africa Department of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs. Also attending the banquet were heads of units such as the Chinese Asian-African Solidarity Committee, as well as people from the Islamic community in Beijing.

### 陈毅副总理接见哈尔塞亲王  包尔汉举行宴会热烈欢迎阿曼贵宾

1959-01-31
第4版()
专栏：

    陈毅副总理接见哈尔塞亲王
    包尔汉举行宴会热烈欢迎阿曼贵宾
    新华社30日讯　陈毅副总理今天下午接见了阿曼副教长哈尔塞亲王和他的随行人员。接见时在座的，有中国伊斯兰教协会主任包尔汉，副主任达浦生、张杰和外交部西亚非洲司司长柯华。新华社30日讯　中国伊斯兰教协会主任包尔汉今天晚间在北京饭店举行宴会，热烈欢迎正在进行反殖民主义斗争的阿曼人民的代表——阿曼副教长哈尔塞亲王。
    哈尔塞亲王在宴会上的讲话，带来了阿曼人民对中国人民的友好情谊。他说：“我相信，我们这次访问是加强我们两国友好关系的良好开端。”“现在我们找到了许多朋友，我们非常高兴。”
    哈尔塞亲王重申了阿曼人民进行反殖民主义斗争的不可动摇的意志。他说：“阿曼人民同帝国主义正在进行着最艰苦最困难的斗争。这个斗争在未获得最后胜利之前，他们决不放下自己的武器。”
    哈尔塞亲王说：“阿曼是亚洲的一部分，我们的斗争依靠亚非人民的支持，依靠一切爱好和平的人民的支持。”这位亲王满怀信心地说：“阿曼人民在斗争胜利以后，他们要建立一个民主的社会，使这个国家里充满了正义和繁荣。”
    哈尔塞亲王讲话以后，他和包尔汉热烈握手和拥抱。
    包尔汉在宴会开始时致欢迎词。他说：“阿曼教长所领导的阿曼人民反英帝国主义的斗争，是阿拉伯人民争取民族独立、反对殖民主义运动的一个重要组成部分。”他指出，阿曼人民进行的英勇顽强的斗争，赢得了全世界一切爱好和平的国家和人民的同情和钦敬。
    包尔汉在讲话中谴责了帝国主义者侵略阿曼的罪行。他并且指出，在东风压倒西风的时代，美英帝国主义要赖在阿曼和阿拉伯地区不走是不行的。包尔汉宣称：“中国人民一向深切地同情和关怀并且坚决地支持阿曼人民的正义斗争。”他说：“我们深信，阿曼人民的反殖民主义斗争在阿拉伯国家、亚非国家和全世界一切爱好和平的国家和人民的坚决支持下，只要坚持斗争到底，并对美英帝国主义的一切阴谋诡计给予足够警惕，终将获得最后胜利。”
    阿拉伯联合共和国驻华临时代办卡巴尼应邀出席了宴会。
    出席宴会作陪的，有中国伊斯兰教协会副主任达浦生、张杰，国务院宗教事务局局长何成湘，外交部西亚非洲司司长柯华。出席宴会的还有中国亚非团结委员会等单位的负责人以及北京伊斯兰教界人士等。
