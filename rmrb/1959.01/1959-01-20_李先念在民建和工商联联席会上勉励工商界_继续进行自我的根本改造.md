Title: Li Xiannian Encourages Industrial and Commercial Circles at Joint Meeting of the China Democratic National Construction Association and the All-China Federation of Industry and Commerce to Continue Fundamental Self-Remolding
Author:
Date: 1959-01-20
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, 19th — Li Xiannian, member of the Political Bureau of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of China, gave an important report this morning to the representatives attending the Fourth Plenum of the First Central Committee of the China Democratic National Construction Association and the Third Plenum of the Second Executive Committee of the All-China Federation of Industry and Commerce (enlarged) meeting. In his report, Comrade Li Xiannian encouraged industrial and commercial circles to continue fundamental self-remolding. He also analyzed and explained the achievements of the Great Leap Forward in 1958 and some issues in the people's commune movement.

Today's report meeting was organized by the United Front Work Department of the CPC Central Committee. More than 1,000 people attended the report meeting, including the heads of various democratic parties and non-party democratic personages.

### 李先念在民建和工商联联席会上勉励工商界  继续进行自我的根本改造

1959-01-20
第6版()
专栏：

    李先念在民建和工商联联席会上勉励工商界
    继续进行自我的根本改造
    新华社19日讯　中共中央政治局委员李先念今天上午向出席中国民主建国会第一届中央委员会第四次全体会议以及中华全国工商业联合会第二届执行委员会第三次会议联席（扩大）会议的代表，作了重要的报告。李先念同志在报告中勉励工商界继续进行自我的根本改造，他还对1958年大跃进的成就以及人民公社运动中的一些问题作了分析和说明。
    今天的报告会是由中共中央统一战线工作部组织的。参加报告会的共有一千多人，各民主党派的负责人和无党派民主人士也参加了这次报告会。
