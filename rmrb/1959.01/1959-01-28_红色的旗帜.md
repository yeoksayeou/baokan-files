Title: The Red Flag
Author: Xie Fengsong
Date: 1959-01-28
Page: 8

    The Red Flag
    Xie Fengsong

    The Soviet flag, the red flag.
    The Soviet flag, the revolutionary flag.
    The flag is embroidered with a hammer, sickle, and a five-pointed golden star. Workers and peasants, illuminated by the five-pointed golden star, rise up in revolution; workers and peasants, guided by the red flag, bravely advance.

    The revolutionary flag beckons, the Soviet people build socialism; the revolutionary flag beckons, the Soviet people march towards a communist future!

    The Soviet flag, the flag of the proletarian revolution.
    In the capital of the first homeland of the proletariat, it rises high, waving in the wind.

    The Polish people look at it, fighting valiantly to reclaim their destiny from the fascists; the Hungarian people look at it, fighting valiantly to cleanse the bloodstains beneath the counter-revolutionary knives; the Chinese people look at it, fighting valiantly to overthrow the three great mountains weighing upon them, awakening the slumbering East.

    All the oppressed people of the world, looking at it, are resisting and fighting; all the enslaved people of the world, looking at it, are filled with hope and confidence!

    The Soviet flag, the flag of world peace.
    It flies at the Geneva conference, bringing peace to the Far East. It flies in the halls of the United Nations, where the voices of disarmament and the banning of nuclear weapons resound.

    Wherever there is a just struggle against imperialism, there will be its righteous support. In the Suez Canal, in the Taiwan Strait, in Beirut, in West Berlin, the imperialist schemes will never succeed!

    Wherever its red light shines, it brings the gospel of peace. On the banks of the Ganges, on the shores of the Yangtze, everywhere there is selfless assistance from the Soviet people, everywhere there is the great friendship of the Soviet people!

    The Soviet flag, the flag of cosmic peace.
    It rides on artificial satellites, singing loudly in the skies above the Earth; now, it rides on cosmic rockets, bringing the wisdom and friendship of mankind to the sun, moon, and stars. It shines with the stars, illuminating alongside the sun and moon!

    The red flag rises higher and higher, waving in the wind. The whole world, the whole universe, will be bathed in its light!

    The Soviet flag, the red flag.
    Long live the red flag! (Attached picture)

### 红色的旗帜
谢逢松
1959-01-28
第8版()
专栏：

    红色的旗帜
    谢逢松
    苏联的旗帜，红色的旗帜。
    苏联的旗帜，革命的旗帜。
    旗帜上绣着斧头、镰刀和五角金星。工人、农民，在五角金星的映照下，起来革命；工人，农民，在红色旗帜的招引下，奋勇前进。
    革命的旗帜在招引，苏联人民把社会主义建成；革命的旗帜在招引，苏联人民奔向共产主义前程！
    苏联的旗帜，无产阶级革命的旗帜。
    在无产阶级第一个祖国的首都，它高高升起，迎风招展。
    波兰人望着它，英勇斗争，从法西斯手里夺回了自己的命运；匈牙利人民望着它，英勇斗争，洗净了反革命屠刀之下的血腥；中国人望着它，英勇斗争，推翻了压顶的三座大山，把沉睡的东方唤醒。
    全世界被压迫的人们，望着它，都在反抗和斗争；全世界被奴役的人们，望着它，充满希望和信心！
    苏联的旗帜，世界和平的旗帜。
    它飘扬在日内瓦的会场上，给远东带来和平。它飘扬在联合国的大厅里，那里就响彻了裁军和禁止核武器的声音。
    哪里有反帝国主义的正义斗争，哪里就能得到它的正义的支援。在苏伊士运河，在台湾海峡，在贝鲁特，在西柏林，帝国主义的阴谋都无法得逞！
    它的红光映到哪里，就给哪里带来和平的福音。在恒河畔，在长江之滨，处处都有苏联人民的无私援助，处处都有苏联人民的伟大友情！
    苏联的旗帜，宇宙和平的旗帜。
    它乘上人造卫星，在地球上空高歌飞行；如今，它又乘上宇宙火箭，把人类的智慧和友情，带给了日月星辰。它与星辰同亮，与日月齐辉！
    红色的旗帜越升越高，迎风飘扬。全世界，全宇宙，都要映遍它的光芒！
    苏联的旗帜，红色的旗帜。
    红色的旗帜万岁！（附图片）
