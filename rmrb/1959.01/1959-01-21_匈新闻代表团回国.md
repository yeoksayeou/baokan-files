Title: Hungarian News Delegation Returns Home
Author:
Date: 1959-01-21
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, January 20th: The Hungarian journalists' delegation, led by Némes Dezső, editor-in-chief of "People's Freedom" and alternate member of the Political Bureau of the Hungarian Socialist Workers' Party Central Committee, left Beijing this afternoon to return home after a month-long visit to various parts of China.

Those who went to the airport to see them off included Zhang Ziyi, Deputy Director of the Propaganda Department of the CPC Central Committee, Wu Lengxi, Vice President of the All-China Journalists Association and Editor-in-Chief of "People's Daily," and Xu Huang, Deputy Director of the Information Department of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs.

Szigeti and Bojnok, counselors of the Hungarian Embassy in China, also went to the airport to see them off.

### 匈新闻代表团回国

1959-01-21
第4版()
专栏：

    匈新闻代表团回国
    新华社20日讯　以匈牙利“人民自由报”总编辑、匈牙利社会主义工人党中央政治局候补委员内梅什·德热为首的匈牙利新闻工作者代表团，在我国各地进行了一个多月的访问以后，今天下午离开北京回国。
    到机场欢送的，有中共中央宣传部副部长张子意，中华全国新闻工作者协会副会长、“人民日报”总编辑吴冷西，外交部新闻司副司长徐晃等人。
    匈牙利驻中国大使馆参赞西格蒂和波依诺克也到机场送行。
