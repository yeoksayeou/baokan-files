Title: Grotewohl to Lead Government Delegation to Beijing Today
Author:
Date: 1959-01-22
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, January 21st. The government delegation of the German Democratic Republic, led by Prime Minister Otto Grotewohl, will arrive in Beijing on the 22nd for a visit to our country at the invitation of our government.

### 格罗提渥总理率政府代表团今天到京

1959-01-22
第1版()
专栏：

    格罗提渥总理率政府代表团今天到京
    新华社21日讯　应我国政府的邀请前来我国访问的德意志民主共和国政府代表团，由奥托·格罗提渥总理率领，将于22日到达北京。
