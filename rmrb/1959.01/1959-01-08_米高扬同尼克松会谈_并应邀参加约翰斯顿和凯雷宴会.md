Title: Mikoyan Meets with Nixon and Accepts Invitations to Johnston and Clay's Banquets
Author:
Date: 1959-01-08
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency, Washington, 7th: According to TASS, Washington: The Chairman of the Motion Picture Association of America, Johnston, hosted a banquet on the evening of the 5th to entertain the First Deputy Chairman of the Council of Ministers of the USSR, Mikoyan.

    American attendees at the banquet included: Senate Democratic Leader Johnson, Speaker of the House Rayburn, Senators Fulbright, Cooper, Symington, etc., Chief of Naval Operations Burke, Deputy Assistant Secretary of State Murphy, and representatives from American business and the press.

Xinhua News Agency, Washington, 6th: According to TASS, Washington: The First Deputy Chairman of the Council of Ministers of the USSR, Mikoyan, who is here on an unofficial visit, and his entourage visited a newly opened large self-service food store in the suburbs of Washington this morning. Mikoyan spoke with workers and customers in the meat packing department.

    In the morning, Mikoyan attended a banquet at the invitation of Clay, the Vice President of the AFL-CIO and President of the International Brotherhood of Electrical Workers.
    Then, Mikoyan went to the United States Capitol at the invitation of U.S. Vice President Nixon.
    After meeting with the U.S. Vice President for nearly two and a half hours, Mikoyan told reporters that during the meeting, there was a beneficial and frank exchange of views on international issues, including the Berlin issue, and on Soviet-American relations.

### 米高扬同尼克松会谈  并应邀参加约翰斯顿和凯雷宴会

1959-01-08
第4版()
专栏：

    米高扬同尼克松会谈
    并应邀参加约翰斯顿和凯雷宴会
    新华社7日讯　据塔斯社华盛顿讯：美国全国电影业协会主席约翰斯顿5日晚上设宴招待苏联部长会议第一副主席米高扬。
    美国方面出席宴会的有：美国参议院民主党领袖约翰逊，国会众议院议长雷朋，参议员富布赖特、库柏、薛明顿等，美国海军作战参谋长伯克，美国副国务卿帮办墨菲，美国实业界和新闻界的代表。
    新华社7日讯　据塔斯社华盛顿6日讯：正在这里进行非正式访问的苏联部长会议第一副主席米高扬和他的随行人员今天上午访问了华盛顿近郊一个新开业的巨大的自动服务食品商店。米高扬同肉类包装部的工人和顾客谈了话。
    上午米高扬应美国劳联—产联副主席、电气和无线电工人工会主席凯雷的邀请参加宴会。
    然后米高扬应美国副总统尼克松的邀请前往美国国会大厦。
    米高扬在同美国副总统会谈了将近两个半小时以后向记者们说，在会谈过程中，对包括柏林问题在内的国际问题和苏美关系等问题有益地和坦率地交换了意见。
