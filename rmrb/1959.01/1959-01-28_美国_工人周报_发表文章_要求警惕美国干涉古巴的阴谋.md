Title: U.S. "Worker's Weekly" Publishes Article Calling for Vigilance Against U.S. Conspiracy to Interfere in Cuba
Author: 
Date: 1959-01-28
Page: 7
Xinhua News Agency, 27th: New York dispatch: The January 25th edition of the U.S. "Worker's Weekly" published an article by Jesús Colón from Havana, Cuba. In the article titled "As I See It From Here," the author called on world peace forces to be vigilant against the U.S. imperialist conspiracy to openly interfere in Cuba. The article stated that the danger of open interference in Cuba is not false. As democratic forces consolidate in Cuba, the Wall Street guys will shout even louder for intervention.

The article pointed out that if the forces for democracy and freedom within the United States do not become more vigilant and mobilize, and if the forces around the world supporting the Cuban people do not prove capable of countering the actions that U.S. imperialism may take under the false slogan of "saving Cuba from falling into communism," then intervention will come.

The article revealed that Wall Street and their mouthpieces in Washington will intervene not to save Cuba from "communism," but to prohibit the necessary and profound agricultural, industrial, economic, and social reforms that far-sighted people in Cuban parties and organizations believe are needed.

The article stated that for those who believe intervention is impossible, we can point out that the United States has intervened not only in Cuba but also in many other parts of Latin America and other parts of the world before.

The article then listed three historical instances of U.S. imperialist aggression against Cuba.

The article pointed out that without going back far into the past century—the annexation of Texas in 1835, the annexation of half of the remaining Mexican territory after the end of the Mexican War, and Walker's invasion of Central America—many examples can be cited of U.S. imperialism openly and without the legal basis of the Platt Amendment, carrying out military intervention in many Latin American countries in this century.

As a result of the Spanish-American War, U.S. imperialism seized Puerto Rico, the Philippines, and other dependencies. Then it stole the Canal Zone from the Republic of Panama, which the United States had forcibly separated from the Republic of Colombia. This act of robbery was so blatant that the United States paid Colombia $25 million.

In 1907, the United States intervened in the Dominican Republic.

In 1909, the United States sent Marines to Nicaragua.

From 1915 to 1934, for a long 19 years, the U.S. Marines occupied Haiti.

In 1916, General Pershing and U.S. troops invaded Mexico.

From 1916 to 1924, Wall Street forced the Washington authorities to occupy the Dominican Republic. U.S. military forces only left when they were convinced that a Marine-trained Sergeant Trujillo would adequately safeguard Wall Street's investments.

From 1926 to 1933, the U.S. Marines occupied Nicaragua. In 1934, the Nicaraguan people's leader Sandino was murdered.

After listing the above history of U.S. imperialist aggression against Latin America, the article emphasized that we should abandon the illusion that the United States "cannot intervene." The United States has intervened in Cuba in the past and has recently intervened in Cuba again. U.S. Marines also still occupy Cuban territory near the Guantanamo base, which is also part of Cuba.

### 美国“工人周报”发表文章  要求警惕美国干涉古巴的阴谋

1959-01-28
第7版()
专栏：

    美国“工人周报”发表文章
    要求警惕美国干涉古巴的阴谋
    新华社27日讯　纽约消息：1月25日的美国“工人周报”刊载了赫苏斯·科朗自古巴哈瓦那寄来的一篇文章。作者在这篇题为“照我从这里看到的”的文章里，要求世界和平力量警惕美帝国主义公开干涉古巴的阴谋。文章说，公开干涉古巴的危险并不是虚假的。当民主力量在古巴得到巩固时，华尔街的家伙们将甚至更高声地叫嚷干涉。
    文章指出，如果美国国内争取民主和自由的力量不提高警惕和动员起来的话，如果全世界声援古巴人民的力量，证明对付不了美帝国主义在所谓“拯救古巴不使陷入共产主义”的虚假口号下可能采取的行动的话，那么干涉就将到来。
    文章揭露说，华尔街和它们在华盛顿的喉舌将进行干涉，并不是为了所谓拯救古巴不使陷入“共产主义”，而是为了禁止必要的和深入的农业、工业、经济和社会改革，而古巴各党派和组织中的有远见的人士认为这种改革是需要的。
    文章说，对于那些认为不可能进行干涉的人们，我们可以指出，美国以前不仅在古巴而且在拉丁美洲的其他许多地区和世界其他地区都进行过干涉。
    文章接着列举了美帝国主义三次侵略古巴的历史。
    文章指出，不用远溯到过去一个世纪——在1835年并吞得克萨斯州，在墨西哥战争结束以后并吞剩下的墨西哥领土中的一半，以及沃克侵入中美洲——都可以举出许多例子，说明美帝国主义在本世纪中公开地和在没有普莱特修正案的法律依据的情况下，对许多拉丁美洲国家进行军事干涉。
    西班牙—美国战争的结果，美帝国主义攫取了波多黎各、菲律宾和其他属地。接着从巴拿马共和国抢走了运河地区，巴拿马是由美国硬从哥伦比亚共和国分出来的。这种强盗行为实在太露骨了，所以美国给了哥伦比亚两千五百万美元的代价。
    在1907年，美国对多米尼加共和国进行干涉。
    在1909年，美国派遣海军陆战队前往尼加拉瓜。
    在1915年到1934年这长长的十九年中，美国海军陆战队占领了海地。
    1916年，潘兴将军和美国军队侵入了墨西哥。
    从1916年到1924年，华尔街迫使华盛顿当局占领多米尼加共和国。美国军事部队只是在他们深信一个海军陆战队训练出来的军士特鲁希略会适当地保障华尔街的投资的时候才离开的。
    从1926年到1933年，美国海军陆战队占领了尼加拉瓜。1934年，尼加拉瓜人民领袖桑地诺被谋害了。
    文章在列举了以上美帝国主义对拉丁美洲的侵略史以后强调指出，我们应该抛弃这种认为美国“不可能进行干涉”的幻想。美国过去对古巴进行了干涉，最近又在古巴进行干涉。美国海军陆战队还占领了关塔那摩基地附近的古巴领土，关塔那摩基地也是古巴的一部分。
