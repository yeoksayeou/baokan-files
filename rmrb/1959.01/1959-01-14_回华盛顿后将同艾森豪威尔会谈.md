Title: To Meet with Eisenhower After Returning to Washington
Author: 
Date: 1959-01-14
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency, Washington, January 13th -- TASS, Washington, January 12th: White House Press Secretary Hagerty announced that U.S. President Eisenhower would receive Soviet First Deputy Premier Mikoyan at the White House on January 17. Hagerty also announced that U.S. Secretary of State Dulles would participate in the talks between Mikoyan and Eisenhower.

### 回华盛顿后将同艾森豪威尔会谈

1959-01-14
第4版()
专栏：

    回华盛顿后将同艾森豪威尔会谈
    新华社13日讯　据塔斯社华盛顿12日讯：白宫新闻秘书哈格蒂宣布，美国总统艾森豪威尔将于1月17日在白宫接见苏联部长会议第一副主席米高扬。哈格蒂同时宣布，美国国务卿杜勒斯将参加米高扬和艾森豪威尔的会谈。
