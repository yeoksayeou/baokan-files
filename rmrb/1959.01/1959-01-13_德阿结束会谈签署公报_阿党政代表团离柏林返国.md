Title: German-Albanian Talks Conclude with Signing of Communiqué; Albanian Party and Government Delegation Leaves Berlin for Home
Author:
Date: 1959-01-13
Page: 5
Xinhua News Agency, Berlin, January 11th. Following the conclusion of talks between the delegations of the German Democratic Republic and the Albanian People's Republic, a joint communiqué was signed here today.

	Signing the communiqué on behalf of the German Democratic Republic was Walter Ulbricht, the First Secretary of the Central Committee of the Socialist Unity Party of Germany and First Deputy Prime Minister. Signing on behalf of Albania were Enver Hoxha, First Secretary of the Central Committee of the Albanian Labor Party, and Mehmet Shehu, Chairman of the Council of Ministers.

	Deputy Minister of Foreign Affairs of the German Democratic Republic, Wenzel, and the Minister of Foreign Affairs of Albania, Shtulla, also signed a consular treaty between the two countries and a treaty on mutual judicial assistance in civil and criminal cases tonight.

Xinhua News Agency, Berlin, January 11th. The Albanian Party and Government Delegation left here tonight. First Secretary of the Central Committee of the Socialist Unity Party of Germany, Walter Ulbricht, and other leaders of the German Democratic Republic's Party and Government went to the station to see them off.

### 德阿结束会谈签署公报  阿党政代表团离柏林返国

1959-01-13
第5版()
专栏：

    德阿结束会谈签署公报
    阿党政代表团离柏林返国
    新华社柏林11日电　德意志民主共和国和阿尔巴尼亚人民共和国两国党和政府代表团会谈结束后，今天在这里签署了一项联合公报。
    代表德意志民主共和国方面在公报上签字的是德国统一社会党中央委员会第一书记、政府第一副总理乌布利希，代表阿尔巴尼亚方面签字的是阿尔巴尼亚劳动党中央委员会第一书记霍查和部长会议主席谢胡。
    德意志民主共和国外交部副部长文策尔和阿尔巴尼亚外交部部长什图拉今晚还签订了两国之间的一项领事条约和一项关于在民事案件和刑事案件方面互相提供司法协助的条约。
    新华社柏林11日电　阿尔巴尼亚党和政府代表团已于今晚离开这里。德国统一社会党中央委员会第一书记乌布利希和德意志民主共和国党和政府的其他领导人曾经到车站送行。
