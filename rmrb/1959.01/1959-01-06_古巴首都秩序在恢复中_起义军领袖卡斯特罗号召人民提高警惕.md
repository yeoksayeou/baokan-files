Title: Order Restored in Cuban Capital; Rebel Leader Castro Calls on People to Be Vigilant
Author:
Date: 1959-01-06
Page: 5

    Havana dispatch from Xinhua News Agency, January 5th: Normal social order is being restored in Havana, the capital of Cuba.

    It is reported that Cuban rebel leader Castro ordered a halt to the general strike last night through Havana Radio. He asked workers across the country to return to work, requested shops to reopen, and also demanded the resumption of national transportation. It is reported that Castro called for an end to the strike because the victory of the people's forces is now guaranteed. Castro also called for increased vigilance. He said that there are still individuals within the country who are maintaining the old system, and they are resisting and will continue to resist in the future.

    According to reports from Havana, shops in Havana have begun to open today, newspapers have begun to publish, and most people have returned to work. A transportation company has been ordered to resume automobile transportation throughout Cuba, and air services to foreign countries have also begun.

    It is reported that about six thousand Cuban rebel troops have entered Havana, where they were warmly welcomed by the residents of the capital. The rebel troops who entered Havana took over most government buildings, including the Presidential Palace, and continued to search for the remnants of the Batista dictatorship. Residents of Havana are tidying up the city, eagerly awaiting the arrival of Cuban rebel leader Castro and Cuban Provisional President Urrutia. (Attached picture)
    Rebel leader Castro (left) is listening to the latest news of victory, with one of the first women to join the rebel army on the right. Xinhua News Agency draft (faxed photo)

### 古巴首都秩序在恢复中  起义军领袖卡斯特罗号召人民提高警惕

1959-01-06
第5版()
专栏：

    古巴首都秩序在恢复中
    起义军领袖卡斯特罗号召人民提高警惕
    新华社5日讯　哈瓦那消息：古巴首都哈瓦那的正常的社会秩序正在恢复中。
    据报道，古巴起义军领袖卡斯特罗已经在昨天晚上通过哈瓦那广播电台下令停止总罢工。他要求全国的工人恢复工作，要求各商店恢复营业，并且还要求恢复全国的交通运输工作。据报道，卡斯特罗号召停止罢工是因为现在人民力量的胜利已经有了保证。卡斯特罗同时号召要提高警惕。他说，国内仍然有个别维护旧制度的人，他们正在反抗并且今后还会反抗。
    据来自哈瓦那的消息说，今天哈瓦那的商店已经开始营业，报纸已经开始出版，大部分人恢复了工作。一家运输公司已经奉命开始恢复横贯古巴全境的汽车运输，同外国的航空线的通航工作也已开始。
    据报道，约有六千名古巴起义军部队进入了哈瓦那，他们受到首都居民的热烈欢迎。进入哈瓦那的起义军部队接管了包括总统府在内的大部分政府建筑物，并且继续在搜捕巴蒂斯塔独裁政权的残余分子。哈瓦那的居民正在整顿市容，他们以热切的心情在等待着古巴起义军领袖卡斯特罗和古巴临时总统乌鲁蒂亚的到来。（附图片）
    起义军领袖卡斯特罗（左）正在听最新的胜利消息，右边是首批参加起义军的妇女之一。　新华社稿（传真照片）
