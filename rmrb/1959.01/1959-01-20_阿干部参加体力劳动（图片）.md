Title: Albanian Cadres Participate in Physical Labor (Photos)
Author:
Date: 1959-01-20
Page: 4

Albanian cadres participate in physical labor.
    Albanian national government cadres are actively participating in physical labor in response to the decisions of the Labor Party and the People's Assembly.
    According to regulations, all staff members of Albanian state organs, intellectuals, cadres of Party organs and mass organizations, including leading comrades, must participate in physical labor for at least one month each year.
    The picture shows Albanian party and government leaders and government cadres setting off to participate in physical labor. First row from right to left: Chairman of the State Supervisory Commission Shefqet Peçi, Chairman of the Council of Ministers Mehmet Shehu, Minister of Construction Josif Pashko, First Vice-Chairman of the Council of Ministers Spiro Koleka, Minister of Trade Kiço Ngjela.
                            Albanian Telegraph Agency (Xinhua News Agency)
    The picture shows the First Vice-Chairman of the Council of Ministers and Minister of Defense Beqir Balluku (center) participating in physical labor.
                              Albanian Telegraph Agency

### 阿干部参加体力劳动（图片）

1959-01-20
第4版()
专栏：

    阿干部参加体力劳动
    阿尔巴尼亚全国机关干部响应劳动党和人民议会的决定，积极参加体力劳动。
    按照规定，所有阿尔巴尼亚国家机关的工作人员、知识分子、党的机关和群众团体的干部，包括领导同志，每人每年必须至少参加体力劳动一个月。
    图为阿尔巴尼亚的党政领导人和机关干部出发去参加体力劳动。第一排从右到左：国家监察委员会主席谢·佩奇、部长会议主席穆·谢胡、建筑部部长约·帕希科、部长会议第一副主席斯·科列加、贸易部部长基·恩杰拉。
                            阿尔巴尼亚通讯社稿（新华社发）
    图为阿尔巴尼亚部长会议第一副主席兼国防部部长贝·巴卢库（中）在参加体力劳动。
                              阿尔巴尼亚通讯社稿（新华社发）
