Title: Cuban "Revolución" Reveals: U.S. Embassy Personnel Served as Spies for the Batista Regime

Author: 
Date: 1959-01-19
Page: 5

    Cuban "Revolución" Reveals
    U.S. Embassy Personnel Served as Spies for the Batista Regime
    Xinhua News Agency, Havana, January 18th: The Cuban "Revolución" revealed yesterday that U.S. embassy personnel had served as spies for the Batista regime.
    According to reports, the newspaper published on the 17th the names of five U.S. embassy personnel who were once "honorary" members of the Batista spy agency, and also published photos of the cards these people received from the Batista dictatorship. "Revolución" pointed out that one of the five was a member of the U.S. Federal Bureau of Investigation.

### 古巴“革命报”揭露  美使馆人员曾为巴蒂斯塔政权当间谍

1959-01-19
第5版()
专栏：

    古巴“革命报”揭露
    美使馆人员曾为巴蒂斯塔政权当间谍
    新华社18日讯　哈瓦那消息：古巴“革命报”昨天揭露说，美国的使馆人员曾经为巴蒂斯塔政权充当间谍。
    据报道，这家报纸17日公布了曾经在巴蒂斯塔间谍机关中当“名誉”成员的五个美国使馆人员的名字，同时还刊登了这些人从巴蒂斯塔独裁政府那里领到的卡片的照片。“革命报”指出，这五人中有一人是美国联邦调查局的人员。
