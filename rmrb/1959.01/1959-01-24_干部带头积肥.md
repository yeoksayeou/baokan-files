Title: Cadres Take the Lead in Accumulating Fertilizer
Author:
Date: 1959-01-24
Page: 4

Brief News

    Under the leadership of some commune party committee members, production brigade and village party branch secretaries, more than 2,700 cadres in Zunhua County, Hebei Province, joined the masses in collecting manure, sweeping up dirt, and turning over enclosure soil for fertilizer accumulation activities.
    The cadres' hands-on participation in accumulating and producing fertilizer greatly stimulated the enthusiasm of the commune members for accumulating fertilizer. The county accumulated more than 5.4 billion jin of fertilizer in half a month.
            (According to Xinhua News Agency)

### 干部带头积肥

1959-01-24
第4版()
专栏：简明新闻

    干部带头积肥
    在一些公社党委委员、生产大队和村的党支部书记的带动下，河北遵化县有二千七百多名干部，同群众一起参加拾粪、扫脏土、起垫圈土等积肥活动。
    干部亲自动手积肥造肥，大大激发了社员积肥的积极性，全县在半个月内就积肥五十四亿多斤。
            （据新华社讯）
