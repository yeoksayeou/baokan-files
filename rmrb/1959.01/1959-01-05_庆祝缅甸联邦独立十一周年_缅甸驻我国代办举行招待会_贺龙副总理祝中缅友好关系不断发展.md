Title: Celebrating the 11th Anniversary of the Independence of the Union of Burma, Burmese Chargé d'Affaires in Our Country Holds Reception, Vice Premier He Long Wishes Continuous Development of Sino-Burmese Friendly Relations
Author: 
Date: 1959-01-05
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, January 4th. Burmese Chargé d'Affaires to China, U Ba Maung, held a reception tonight to celebrate the 11th anniversary of the independence of the Union of Burma.

Vice Chairman Zhu De, Vice Premier He Long, Vice Chairman Chen Shutong, Vice Minister of Foreign Affairs Zhang Hanfu, and responsible persons from various departments of the Chinese government, people's organizations, and democratic parties were invited to attend the reception.

Vice Premier He Long spoke at the reception, extending warm congratulations on the Burmese National Day on behalf of the Chinese people and government. He said, "In the past year, the people of Asian and African countries have achieved great success in the struggle against colonialism, safeguarding national independence, and easing world tensions. We are pleased to see that Burma, as one of the initiators of the Five Principles of Peaceful Coexistence, has made contributions to the cause of maintaining Asian, African, and world peace."

Vice Premier He Long emphasized, "China and Burma are countries of relatives and close neighbors. In the past year, the friendly and cooperative relations between China and Burma have further developed on the basis of the Five Principles of Peaceful Coexistence." "Through friendly exchanges between the governments and peoples of the two countries, the people of the two countries have gained a better understanding of each other. There has also been progress in trade and technical cooperation between the two countries. We believe that with the joint efforts of our two countries, the friendly relations between China and Burma will surely be continuously consolidated and developed."

Chargé d'Affaires U Ba Maung first delivered a speech at the beginning of the reception. He said that the experience and progress made by the Union of Burma since its independence are sufficient to make them "face the future with confidence," and he also pointed out that Burma will strive to expand friendly relations with other countries on the basis of the Five Principles of Peaceful Coexistence.

Speaking of the relations between China and Burma, U Ba Maung said: "The relationship between our two countries is developing and strengthening day by day, just as it should be between two close neighbors. We have successfully implemented many of the measures envisioned in the joint statement issued by the Prime Ministers of our two countries in 1954." He also said: "It is the responsibility of every Burmese and Chinese person to build a lasting edifice of friendship," and "at a celebration like today, we reaffirm our commitment to the construction of this edifice."

At the reception, the guests repeatedly toasted to the close friendship between the people of China and Burma and to world peace.

Diplomatic envoys from various countries to China also attended tonight's reception.

### 庆祝缅甸联邦独立十一周年  缅甸驻我国代办举行招待会  贺龙副总理祝中缅友好关系不断发展

1959-01-05
第4版()
专栏：

    庆祝缅甸联邦独立十一周年
    缅甸驻我国代办举行招待会
    贺龙副总理祝中缅友好关系不断发展
    新华社4日讯　缅甸驻中国临时代办吴巴茂今晚举行招待会，庆祝缅甸联邦独立十一周年。
    朱德副主席，贺龙副总理，陈叔通副委员长，外交部章汉夫副部长，我国政府各部门、各人民团体和民主党派的负责人，应邀出席了招待会。
    贺龙副总理在招待会上讲话，代表中国人民和政府热烈祝贺缅甸国庆。他说：“过去的一年，亚非各国人民在反对殖民主义、维护民族独立和缓和世界紧张局势的斗争中获得了很大的成就，我们高兴地看到作为和平共处五项原则倡议国之一的缅甸，在维护亚非和世界和平的事业中作出了贡献。”
    贺龙副总理强调说：“中缅两国是亲戚的国家，也是亲密的邻邦。一年来，中缅两国的友好合作关系在和平共处五项原则的基础上，有了进一步的发展。”“通过两国政府和民间的友好往来，两国人民之间有了更多的了解。两国的贸易和技术合作关系也有了进展。我们相信，在我们两国的共同努力下，中缅之间的友好关系一定能够得到不断的巩固和发展。”
    吴巴茂临时代办在招待会开始时首先致词。他说，缅甸联邦独立后所取得的经验和进展已足以使他们“满怀信心地面对将来”，他并且指出缅甸将努力同其他国家在和平共处五项原则的基础上扩大友好关系。
    吴巴茂在谈到中缅两国关系时说：“我们两国之间的关系正如同在两个亲密邻邦之间应有的那样一天天地发展和加强。我们已经成功地实现了我们两国总理在1954年发表的共同声明里所设想的许多措施”。他还说：“建造一所持久的友谊大厦是每一个缅甸人和中国人的责任”，“在今天这样一个庆祝会上，我们重申我们将致力于这所大厦的建造”。
    招待会上，宾主不断地为中缅两国人民的亲密友谊和世界和平而干杯。
    各国驻华使节也出席了今晚的招待会。
