Title: Ode to the Bases

Author: 
Date: 1959-01-24
Page: 3

　　　　　Ode to the Bases
    Today we published four photos, introducing a corner of the four most important industrial bases in the country: Liaoning, Heilongjiang, Shanghai, and Tianjin. Of course, from these four small photos, there is no way to fully reflect the millions of kinds of products and millions of tons of materials shipped from these four bases to all parts of the country. However, from the mountains of steel, the huge and precise generator components, the stacks of product drawings on the drawing tables, and the winches ready to be shipped, it can already inspire people's imagination and give a glimpse of the grand scale and strong strength of these four major bases.
    Let us praise our old bases. They inspire the whole country spiritually and support the whole country materially. Starting from these old bases, we are witnessing new industrial bases being conceived, born, and growing stronger.
    Salute to the worker comrades of the old bases who wholeheartedly support the whole country! May you produce more and better things in this new year to support the whole country. We also salute the worker comrades of the future new industrial bases who receive these precious gifts. May you cherish every piece of steel and every machine that comes from afar, and let them contribute to the Great Leap Forward of the entire country faster and better!

### 颂基地

1959-01-24
第3版()
专栏：编后

    颂基地
    今天我们发表了四张照片，介绍辽宁、黑龙江、上海、天津这四个全国最主要的工业基地的一角。当然，从这四张小小的照片里，没有办法充分反映从这四个基地输送到全国各地去的千万种产品，千万吨物资。然而，从那堆积如山的钢材，巨大而又精密的发电机部件、绘图桌上一叠叠的产品图纸、整装待发的卷扬车，也已经可以启发人的想像，稍稍窥见这四大基地的宏伟规模和雄厚的力量了。
    让我们赞颂我们的老基地。它在精神上鼓舞全国，又在物资上支持全国。从这些老基地出发，我们眼看着一个个新的工业基地在孕育、在诞生、在成长壮大起来。
    向全心全意支援全国的老基地的职工同志们致敬！愿你们在这新的一年里，拿出更多更好的东西来支援全国。也向承受这些宝贵礼物的未来的新工业基地的职工同志们致敬，愿你们珍惜这远道而来的每一根钢材，每一台机器，让它们更快更好地为整个国家的大跃进出力！
