Title: At the Invitation of the China Islamic Association, to Visit Our Country, Omani Deputy Grand Mufti Prince Halsei Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1959-01-30
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, 29th. At the invitation of the China Islamic Association, Omani Deputy Grand Mufti Prince Halsei and his entourage of four arrived in Beijing by plane from Kunming this afternoon for a friendly visit to our country.

Prince Halsei was warmly welcomed at the East Suburb Airport in the capital by Burhan, Director of the China Islamic Association, Deputy Directors Da Puson and Zhang Jie, Director of the West Asia and Africa Department of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs, Ke Hua, and others. Also present at the airport to welcome them were responsible persons from the Chinese Committee for Afro-Asian Solidarity, the Chinese People's Committee for World Peace, the Chinese People's Institute of Foreign Affairs, the Chinese People's Association for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries, Imam An Shiwei of the Niujie Mosque in Beijing, Imam Ma Shutian of the Dongsi Mosque, and people from the Islamic community in Beijing.

The Chargé d'affaires ad interim of the United Arab Republic in China, Kabani, was at the airport to welcome them.

Prince Halsei arrived in Kunming from Rangoon on the 28th. (Attached picture)

Director Burhan of the China Islamic Association warmly welcomes Omani Deputy Grand Mufti Prince Halsei (left) at the airport. (Xinhua News Agency reporter Ta Jigu Le photo)

### 应中国伊斯兰教协会邀请访问我国  阿曼副教长哈尔塞亲王到京

1959-01-30
第1版()
专栏：

    应中国伊斯兰教协会邀请访问我国
    阿曼副教长哈尔塞亲王到京
    新华社29日讯　应中国伊斯兰教协会邀请前来我国进行友好访问的阿曼副教长哈尔塞亲王和他的随行人员一行四人，今天下午乘飞机从昆明到达北京。
    哈尔塞亲王在首都东郊机场受到中国伊斯兰教协会主任包尔汉，副主任达浦生、张杰，外交部西亚非洲司司长柯华等人的热烈欢迎。到机场欢迎的，还有中国亚非团结委员会、中国人民保卫世界和平委员会、中国人民外交学会、中国人民对外文化协会的负责人，北京牛街清真寺阿訇安士伟和东四清真寺阿訇马书田，以及北京伊斯兰教界人士等。
    阿拉伯联合共和国驻华临时代办卡巴尼到机场欢迎。
    哈尔塞亲王是在28日由仰光到达昆明的。（附图片）
