International Briefs

    Sihanouk Participates in Voluntary Labor
    Phnom Penh dispatch: Cambodian Prime Minister Prince Sihanouk, together with the Chinese Ambassador to Cambodia Wang Youping, Soviet Ambassador Anyikin, and Czechoslovak Minister Hradec, once again participated in physical labor on January 7 in Chi Isah, Takeo Province, where a dam is being built.

    Singapore's Exports Sharply Decline
    Singapore dispatch: Singapore's Minister of Commerce and Industry, Juma'at, told newspaper reporters on January 5 that due to the impact of the capitalist economic crisis and the decline in rubber and tin prices, Singapore's total export trade value in the first ten months of last year decreased by approximately 245 million Malayan dollars compared to the same period of the previous year.

    U.S. Lends Money to the UAR
    Washington dispatch: The U.S. Export-Import Bank announced on January 5 that it would grant the United Arab Republic a loan of 6.2 million Egyptian pounds.
    On December 24 of last year, the United Arab Republic and the United States signed an agreement for the UAR to purchase wheat and flour worth $24.9 million from the United States. This loan represents 25% of the price stipulated in this agreement. According to the agreement, the UAR will pay for the purchase of surplus U.S. agricultural products in Egyptian pounds.

    Austria's Unemployment Rises Sharply
    Vienna dispatch: The unemployment situation in Austria is serious. It is reported that in December of last year alone, the number of unemployed increased by 70,000. At the end of 1958, more than 175,000 people were registered in the Austrian labor market seeking work.

### 国际简讯

1959-01-09
第5版()
专栏：国际简讯

    国际简讯
    西哈努克参加义务劳动
    金边消息：柬埔寨首相西哈努克亲王7日再次同驻柬埔寨的中国大使王幼平、苏联大使安尼金和捷克斯洛伐克公使赫拉捷茨一起，在茶胶省芝依沙参加体力劳动，那里正在修建一座堤坝。
    新加坡出口锐减
    新加坡消息：新加坡工商部长朱马波5日对报纸记者说，由于受资本主义经济危机的影响，橡胶和锡的价格下跌，新加坡去年头十个月的出口贸易总值比前年同期约减少了马来亚币二亿四千五百万元。
    美国借给阿联一笔钱
    华盛顿消息：美国进出口银行5日宣布，它将给阿拉伯联合共和国一笔六百二十万埃镑的贷款。
    去年12月24日阿拉伯联合共和国和美国签订了阿联向美国购买价值二千四百九十万美元的小麦和面粉协定。这笔贷款就是这个协定所规定的价款的25％。根据这个协定，阿联购买美国的剩余农产品将用埃镑来支付。
    奥地利失业人数大增
    维也纳消息：奥地利失业情况严重，据报道，仅在去年12月一个月内，失业人数又增加了七万人。1958年年底在奥地利劳动市场登记要求工作的已超过十七万五千人。
