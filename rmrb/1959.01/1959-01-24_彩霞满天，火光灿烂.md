Title: Rosy Clouds Filling the Sky, Splendid Flames
Author: Sha Ou
Date: 1959-01-24
Page: 8

    Rosy Clouds Filling the Sky, Splendid Flames
    Sha Ou
    Dawn, with its bright hand,
    Opens the window of nineteen fifty-nine,
    I see a blaze of splendid fire,
    Like rosy clouds welcoming the sun,
    Rising vigorously from the West Indies and Africa.
    The sea stirs up rose-colored waves.
    Cuba and Congo,
    My brothers,
    Thrust their gleaming long swords
    Into the hearts of the colonialists,
    That pitch-black heart,
    Flowing with filthy blood.
    How can I describe my joy?
    Nineteen fifty-nine begins like this!
    I stretch out my arms,
    I use songs and poems,
    To join the ranks of my brothers,
    To fight together.
    At this moment, on the Atlantic coast,
    Beside the Caribbean Sea,
    On that emerald-like land,
    Came the sound of clenched fists
    Gritting.
    The streets of Havana,
    Are being shaken by a million-person demonstration.
    What a majestic procession!
    Like a force twelve typhoon,
    Rolling with anger,
    Roaring, whirling,
    Sweeping towards the White House—
    No interference in internal affairs!
    This sweetest of countries,
    Has shed the most bitter tears.
    The tyrant's torture and slaughter,
    Only planted hatred.
    The Batista regime has finally fallen,
    Like a rotten egg under a hammer;
    The people's enemies fled into the pockets of America,
    And America, sweating profusely,
    Still wants to reach out and crush
    The independence Cuba won with blood.
    This is just a dream!
    Six million people's arms are a wall,
    Six million people's hearts are soaring flames,
    Six million people's footsteps are a滔滔 flood,
    Unbreakable! Unquenchable! Unstoppable!
    Whirlwinds echo whirlwinds,
    Flames illuminate flames,
    Across the vast ocean,
    The Congo River is rushing angrily,
    Diamonds are scratching
    The soles of the colonialists' feet.
    That "oasis of stability"
    Has long been battered by wind and rain.
    Eighty years of pain and humiliation,
    Have written a thick ledger.
    The volcano erupts with white-hot magma,
    Cities and jungles
    The guns of revenge are aimed at the enemy,
    To smash the shackles of slavery,
    To destroy colonial rule!
    The Belgian dynasty is panic-stricken,
    Bloody suppression
    And empty "independence" checks,
    Are all useless!
    The bright red flag,
    The flag of national liberation,
    Is raised! Is raised!
    From Cuba, from Congo,
    From Latin America, from Africa,
    Raised high!
    Raised amidst the laughter of the East!
    Like clouds covering the sky,
    Like raging flames,
    Crush imperialism!
    Burn imperialism to death!
    Rosy clouds fill the sky,
    Flames are splendid,
    The sound of chains breaking,
    Has become magnificent and beautiful music.
    Rosy clouds,
    Please carry my song,
    Flames,
    Please illuminate my poem,
    My song and poem,
    Fly quickly! Fly quickly!
    Fly to Cuba caressed by the waves,
    Fly to the mythical Congo,
    Tell my fighting brothers:
    We
    Are supporting
          You!

### 彩霞满天，火光灿烂
沙鸥
1959-01-24
第8版()
专栏：

    彩霞满天，火光灿烂
    沙鸥
    黎明，用光亮的手，
    拉开一九五九年的窗户，
    我看见一片灿烂的火光，
    像迎接太阳的彩霞，
    从西印度群岛和非洲
    熊熊地升腾而起。
    大海掀起玫瑰的波涛。
    古巴和刚果，
    我的兄弟，
    用雪亮的长剑
    刺在殖民主义者的心上，
    那黑透了的心房，
    流着污秽的血水。
    用什么来描绘我的喜悦？
    一九五九年是这样开始！
    我伸出双臂，
    我用歌声和诗句，
    进入我兄弟的行列，
    一块战斗。
    这时，在大西洋岸边，
    加勒比海旁，
    那像翡翠一样的国土上，
    传来了握紧拳头的
    格吱吱的响声。
    哈瓦那的街道，
    正被百万人的示威震动。
    多么威风的队伍！
    像十二级台风，
    卷着愤怒，
    呼啸着，飞旋着，
    向白宫扫去——
    不准干涉内政！
    这最甜的国家，
    流过最酸的眼泪。
    暴君的苦刑与屠杀，
    只埋下仇恨。
    巴蒂斯塔政权终于败亡了，
    像铁锤下腐烂的鸡蛋；
    人民的敌人逃到美国的口袋中，
    而美国，满头大汗，
    还想伸手捏碎
    古巴用鲜血夺来的独立。
    这只是梦想！
    六百万人的臂膀是一道墙，
    六百万人的心是冲天的烈火，
    六百万人的脚步是滔滔洪流，
    碰不垮！浇不熄！挡不住！
    旋风呼应着旋风，
    火光辉映着火光，
    越过茫茫的大洋，
    刚果河正在激怒地奔流，
    金刚石正在划破
    殖民主义者的脚掌。
    那“安定的绿洲”
    早就风雨满楼了。
    八十年的痛苦与屈辱，
    写了厚厚一本账簿。
    火山口喷出白热的岩浆，
    城市与丛林
    复仇的枪口瞄准敌人，
    要砸碎奴役的枷锁，
    要摧毁殖民统治！
    比利时王朝失魂落魄，
    血腥镇压
    与空头的“独立”支票，
    全不管用了！
    鲜红的旗帜，
    民族解放的旗帜，
    举起来了！举起来了！
    从古巴，从刚果，
    从拉丁美洲，从非洲，
    高高地举起来了！
    在东方的欢笑中举起来了！
    像遮天盖地的云，
    像烈焰喧腾的火，
    压死帝国主义！
    烧死帝国主义！
    彩霞满天，
    火光灿烂，
    砍断铁链的声音，
    成了宏伟的美妙的音乐。
    彩霞，
    请托着我的歌声，
    火光，
    请照亮我的诗句，
    我的歌声和诗句，
    快飞吧！快飞吧！
    飞向海浪抚爱着的古巴，
    飞向神话般的刚果，
    告诉我正在战斗的兄弟：
    我们
    在支援
          你们！
