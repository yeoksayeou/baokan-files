Title: Chairman Mao Meets General Cárdenas. Premier Zhou Meets with Distinguished Mexican Guests and Lunches Together

Author:

Date: 1959-01-22
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, January 21st: Chairman Mao Zedong met this morning with General Lázaro Cárdenas, former President of Mexico and Vice-Chairman of the World Peace Council, and his entourage: Alejandro Carrillo, Professor at the University of Mexico; Cuauhtémoc Cárdenas, General Cárdenas’ son and a civil engineer; and César Buenrostro, a civil engineer. Present at the meeting were Guo Moruo, Chairman of the Chinese People's Committee for World Peace, and Liao Chengzhi, Vice-Chairman.

Xinhua News Agency, January 21st: Premier Zhou Enlai met at noon today with General Lázaro Cárdenas, former President of Mexico and Vice-Chairman of the World Peace Council, and his entourage: Alejandro Carrillo, Professor at the University of Mexico; Cuauhtémoc Cárdenas, General Cárdenas’ son and a civil engineer; and César Buenrostro, a civil engineer.

Present at the meeting were Vice Premier Chen Yi, Guo Moruo, Chairman of the Chinese People's Committee for World Peace, and Liao Chengzhi, Vice-Chairman.

After the meeting, Premier Zhou Enlai and others had lunch with the guests. (Attached pictures)

Bottom Left: Chairman Mao Zedong meets with General Cárdenas and his entourage. The third person from the left is General Cárdenas.

Bottom Right: Premier Zhou Enlai meets with General Cárdenas. Xinhua News Agency Reporter Liu Changzhong.

### 毛主席接见卡德纳斯将军  周总理会见墨西哥贵宾并共进午餐

1959-01-22
第1版()
专栏：

    毛主席接见卡德纳斯将军
    周总理会见墨西哥贵宾并共进午餐
    新华社21日讯　毛泽东主席今天上午接见了墨西哥前总统、世界和平理事会副主席拉萨罗·卡德纳斯将军和他的随行人员：墨西哥大学教授亚历山德罗·卡里略，卡德纳斯将军的儿子、土木工程师库奥特莫克·卡德纳斯，土木工程师塞萨尔·布恩罗斯特罗。接见时在座的，有中国人民保卫世界和平委员会主席郭沫若、副主席廖承志。
    新华社21日讯　周恩来总理今天中午会见了墨西哥前总统、世界和平理事会副主席拉萨罗·卡德纳斯将军和他的随行人员：墨西哥大学教授亚历山德罗·卡里略，卡德纳斯将军的儿子、土木工程师库奥特莫克·卡德纳斯，土木工程师塞萨尔·布恩罗斯特罗。
    会见时在座的，有陈毅副总理，中国人民保卫世界和平委员会主席郭沫若、副主席廖承志。
    会见以后，周恩来总理等同客人们共进午餐。（附图片）
    下左：毛泽东主席接见卡德纳斯将军和他的随行人员。左第三人为卡德纳斯将军。
    下右：周恩来总理会见卡德纳斯将军。新华社记者　刘长忠摄
