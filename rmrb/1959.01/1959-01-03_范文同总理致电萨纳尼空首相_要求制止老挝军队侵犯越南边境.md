Title: Premier Pham Van Dong Sends Telegram to Prime Minister Phoui Sananikone Demanding a Halt to Lao Troops Invading the Vietnamese Border
Author:
Date: 1959-01-03
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, Hanoi, January 1st: According to a report from the Vietnam News Agency, the Prime Minister of the Government of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam, Pham Van Dong, sent a telegram to the Prime Minister of the Government of the Kingdom of Laos, Phoui Sananikone, on December 28, 1958, stating that the Government of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam has repeatedly requested the Government of the Kingdom of Laos to pay attention to the problem of Lao troops invading the Vietnamese border, pointing out that this action endangers the normal life of the people in the border area and is detrimental to the friendship and neighborly relations between the two countries.

The telegram described some instances of Lao troops invading the Vietnamese border since October 13, 1958.

Pham Van Dong said in the telegram that, based on the desire to improve the friendship and good-neighborly relations between the two countries, he hopes that the Government of the Kingdom of Laos will immediately take measures to stop this very regrettable behavior.

Currently, incidents of Lao troops invading the Vietnamese border are still occurring. According to the Vietnam News Agency, a unit of the Kingdom of Laos' troops invaded Da Ro village in Xiangli commune, Vietnamese territory, at 5:00 a.m. on December 31, 1958, and opened fire on the Vietnamese sentries stationed in the village.

Xinhua News Agency, Hanoi, January 2nd: It was announced here: On January 1, the Prime Minister of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam, Pham Van Dong, sent a telegram to the Prime Minister of the Government of the Kingdom of Laos, Phoui Sananikone, regarding the invasion of the Vietnamese border by the armed forces of the Kingdom of Laos.

The telegram stated that at 5:00 a.m. on December 31, 1958, a unit of the armed forces of the Kingdom of Laos invaded Da Ro village in Xiangli commune, Vietnamese territory, in the border area of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam, and opened fire on the local sentries. In addition, the activities of the armed forces of the Kingdom of Laos in the aforementioned area have also been intensified. The telegram said that such actions are bound to cause regrettable consequences. "In the spirit of friendship and consultation, we still believe that the Government of the Kingdom of Laos will take the necessary measures on the above-mentioned incidents at an early date."

### 范文同总理致电萨纳尼空首相  要求制止老挝军队侵犯越南边境

1959-01-03
第6版()
专栏：

    范文同总理致电萨纳尼空首相
    要求制止老挝军队侵犯越南边境
    新华社河内1日电　据越南通讯社报道，越南民主共和国政府总理范文同曾经在1958年12月28日打电报给老挝王国政府首相培·萨纳尼空，讲到越南民主共和国政府曾经多次要求老挝王国政府注意老挝军队侵犯越南边境的问题，指出这种行动危害了边界地区人民的正常生活，有害于两国之间的友谊和邻国关系。
    电报叙述了1958年10月13日以来老挝军队侵犯越南边境的一些情况。
    范文同在电报中说，根据改善两国的友谊和邻邦关系的愿望，他希望老挝王国政府立即采取措施，制止这种非常令人遗憾的行为。
    目前，老挝军队侵犯越南边境的事件还在发生。据越南通讯社报道，老挝王国的一支部队在1958年12月31日清晨五时侵入越南领土向立乡达若村，并且向驻在村里的越南哨兵开枪射击。
    新华社河内2日电　此间宣布：越南民主共和国总理范文同1月1日就老挝王国武装力量侵犯越南边境事件，打电报给老挝王国政府首相培·萨纳尼空。
    电报说，1958年12月31日早晨五点钟，老挝王国军队的一个单位侵入了越南民主共和国边境地区越南领土向立乡的达若村，向当地放哨的人开枪。此外，老挝王国武装力量在上述一带地区的活动也有所加强。电报说，这类行动势必引起令人遗憾的后果。“本着友好和协商的精神，我们仍然相信，老挝王国政府会早日就上述事件采取必要的措施。”
