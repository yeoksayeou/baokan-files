Title: Albanian Guests Visit Agricultural Exhibition

Author:
Date: 1959-01-18
Page: 3

Xinhua News Agency, January 17th: Albanian People's Republic Deputy Chairman of the Council of Ministers, Abdyl Këllezi, and the Albanian government trade delegation he led, visited the National Agricultural Exhibition this morning. After the visit, the Deputy Chairman wrote in the exhibition's guestbook: "I wish the Chinese people even greater achievements in agricultural production, and we regard these achievements as our own."

The Albanian guests were warmly welcomed by Wei Zhenwu, the director of the exhibition's main hall, and other responsible persons when they arrived at the exhibition. The guests spent two hours here, viewing various exhibits in the comprehensive, crop, animal husbandry, aquatic products, rural industry, tool, and plant protection halls. Deputy Chairman Abdyl Këllezi also had a cordial conversation with Yuan Mingdi, the captain of the second district team of the Sanguo Commune in Qingchuan County, Sichuan Province, who came to Beijing to visit the exhibition.

### 阿贵宾参观农业展览会

1959-01-18
第3版()
专栏：

    阿贵宾参观农业展览会
    新华社17日讯　阿尔巴尼亚人民共和国部长会议副主席阿卜杜勒·凯列齐和由他率领的阿尔巴尼亚政府贸易代表团，今日上午参观了全国农业展览会。这位副主席在参观后给展览会的题词中说：“祝中国人民在农业生产上获得更大的成就，我们把这些成就也看做是自己的成就”。
    阿尔巴尼亚的贵宾们来到展览会时，受到展览会总馆馆长魏震五等负责人的热烈欢迎。贵宾们在这里共用了两个小时，观看了综合、农作物、畜牧、水产、农村工业、工具、植物保护等馆中的各种展品。阿卜杜勒·凯列齐副主席还同来北京参观展览会的四川省青川县三锅公社第二区队队长袁明弟等作了亲切的谈话。
