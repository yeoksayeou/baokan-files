Title: Mikoyan Visits Cleveland
Author:
Date: 1959-01-09
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Cleveland, 8th dispatch: According to TASS, Soviet First Deputy Chairman of the Council of Ministers, Mikoyan, who is currently on an unofficial visit to the United States, arrived in Cleveland, Ohio yesterday from Washington.

The famous American industrialist Cyrus Eaton and his wife were at the airport to welcome him.

### 米高扬到克利夫兰访问

1959-01-09
第4版()
专栏：

    米高扬到克利夫兰访问
    新华社8日讯　据塔斯社克利夫兰讯：正在美国作非正式访问的苏联部长会议第一副主席米高扬昨天从华盛顿到达克利夫兰市（俄亥俄州）。
    到飞机场欢迎的有著名的美国工业家勒赛斯·伊顿和他的夫人。
