Title: One Step to the Ninth Heaven

Author:

Date: 1959-01-11
Page: 5

The Ningxia Hui Autonomous Region
Two million heroic people
With soaring spirit and great leap forward
Surpassing twenty years in a single day
Countless stars in the sky
More joyous events this year than last
Last year's construction soared to the clouds
This year, one step to the ninth heaven
Shizuishan becomes a new coal capital
Qingtongxia builds a hydropower station
Factories stand like forests, smoke like the sea
The "Eleventh" train reaches Yinchuan
