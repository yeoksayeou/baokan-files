International Briefs

    United Arab Republic to Allow British Nationals to Enter
    Cairo dispatch: The "Al-Ahram" reported on the 18th that British nationals will be allowed to enter the United Arab Republic following the signing of a financial agreement between Britain and the United Arab Republic. Following the signing of this agreement, the British Overseas Airways Corporation, which was seized by Egypt, will be immediately returned to Britain, and thus the air route between Cairo and London will return to normal.

    Tito Leaves India for Ceylon
    New Delhi dispatch: Yugoslav President Tito left India by boat on the 9th for Ceylon.
    Tito arrived in India from Burma on January 13. He visited India for seven days and held talks with Indian Prime Minister Nehru. The two sides issued a joint communiqué on the 15th.

### 国际简讯

1959-01-20
第5版()
专栏：国际简讯

    国际简讯
    阿联将允许英国侨民入境
    开罗讯：“金字塔报”18日报道，在英国和阿拉伯联合共和国的财政协定签订以后，英国侨民将被允许进入阿联。在这一协定签订以后，将立即把被埃及查封的英国海外航空公司发还英国，因而开罗和伦敦之间的航线将恢复正常。
    铁托离印度前往锡兰
    新德里讯：南斯拉夫总统铁托9日乘船离开印度前往锡兰。
    铁托是在1月13日从缅甸到印度来的。他在印度访问了七天，同印度总理尼赫鲁举行了会谈。双方曾在15日发表了一项会谈公报。
