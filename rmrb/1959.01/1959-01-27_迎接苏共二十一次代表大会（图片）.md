Title: Welcoming the 21st Congress of the CPSU (Pictures)
Author: Yi Dining
Date: 1959-01-27
Page: 4

    Welcoming the 21st Congress of the CPSU
    In order to welcome the 21st Congress of the Communist Party of the Soviet Union, the coal miners of Donbass launched a vigorous labor competition and achieved great success.
    The members of the famous Ma Mai working team of Donbass coal mine completed the competition plan for welcoming the 21st Congress of the CPSU ahead of schedule. (TASS manuscript)
    Workers at the Korsakov Commercial Port on Sakhalin Island in the Soviet Union decided to complete the cargo handling plan ahead of schedule in order to welcome the Party Congress. This is the steamer "Volokhovstroy" unloading cargo.
    To welcome the convening of the 21st Congress of the Communist Party of the Soviet Union, Soviet agricultural laborers have great enthusiasm. Agricultural laborers from many state farms and collective farms launched competitions to become communist labor collectives. These are the feeders of the advanced Communist Youth League production team of the "New North" State Farm in the Yaroslavl Region. They are determined to achieve better results in the development of poultry farming and learn relevant poultry farming techniques.
      Photo by Yi Dining TASS manuscript (Xinhua News Agency)

### 迎接苏共二十一次代表大会（图片）
伊·迪宁
1959-01-27
第4版()
专栏：

    迎接苏共二十一次代表大会
    为了迎接苏联共产党第二十一次代表大会，顿巴斯的煤矿工人展开了热火朝天的劳动竞赛，并且获得了巨大的成就。
    顿巴斯著名的煤矿工作队——马码依工作队的队员们提前完成了为迎接苏共第二十一次代表大会的竞赛计划。　（塔斯社稿）
    苏联萨哈林岛科尔萨科夫商港的工人，为了迎接党代会决定提前完成货运计划。这是汽船“沃勒霍夫斯托罗伊”号在卸货。
    为迎接苏联共产党第二十一次代表大会的召开，苏联农业劳动者的干劲很大。许多国营农场和集体农庄的农业劳动者展开了竞赛，争取成为共产主义劳动集体。这是雅罗斯拉夫尔州“新北方”国营农场先进的共青团青年生产队的饲养员们。她们决心在发展养禽业中取得更好的成绩并且学习有关养禽业的技术。
      伊·迪宁摄　塔斯社稿（新华社发）
