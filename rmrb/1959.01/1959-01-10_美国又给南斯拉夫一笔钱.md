Title: The United States Gives Yugoslavia Another Sum of Money
Author: 
Date: 1959-01-10
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, 9th dispatch from Washington: The United States and Yugoslavia signed another agreement in Washington on the 8th, in which the United States gave Yugoslavia a "loan" of $22.5 million. It is said that this "loan" is to be used to build a fertilizer plant in Yugoslavia.

### 美国又给南斯拉夫一笔钱

1959-01-10
第5版()
专栏：

    美国又给南斯拉夫一笔钱
    新华社9日讯　华盛顿消息：美国和南斯拉夫8日在华盛顿又签订了一项美国给南斯拉夫二千二百五十万美元的“贷款”协定。据说，这笔“贷款”是用来修建南斯拉夫的一座肥料厂的。
