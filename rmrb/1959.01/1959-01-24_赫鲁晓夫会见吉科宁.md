Title: Khrushchev Meets with Kekkonen
Author:
Date: 1959-01-24
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, 23rd - TASS reports: Soviet Chairman of the Council of Ministers Khrushchev met with Finnish President Kekkonen, who is currently visiting Leningrad, on the 22nd. During the meeting, they exchanged views on issues relating to the relations between the Soviet Union and Finland, as well as on certain international situations. Present were Soviet Foreign Minister Gromyko and Minister of Foreign Trade Patolichev.

President Kekkonen arrived in Leningrad with his wife on the evening of the 21st for an informal visit at the invitation of Smirnov, Chairman of the Leningrad City Soviet Executive Committee. On the 22nd, Smirnov hosted a breakfast banquet in honor of President Kekkonen and his wife. Khrushchev, Gromyko, Patolichev, and Finnish Ambassador to the Soviet Union, Wori, attended the banquet. At the banquet, Khrushchev, Kekkonen, and Smirnov delivered speeches respectively.

### 赫鲁晓夫会见吉科宁

1959-01-24
第5版()
专栏：

    赫鲁晓夫会见吉科宁
    新华社23日讯  据塔斯社莫斯科讯：苏联部长会议主席赫鲁晓夫22日会见了正在列宁格勒访问的芬兰总统吉科宁。会见时，他们就苏联和芬兰两国的关系问题和某些国际局势问题交换了意见。在座的有苏联外交部长葛罗米柯和对外贸易部长帕托利切夫。
    吉科宁总统是应列宁格勒市苏维埃执行委员会主席斯米尔诺夫的邀请，于21日晚间偕夫人到列宁格勒作非正式访问的。22日，斯米尔诺夫设早宴，招待吉科宁总统和他的夫人。赫鲁晓夫、葛罗米柯、帕托利切夫和芬兰驻苏联大使沃里参加了宴会。在宴会上，赫鲁晓夫、吉科宁和斯米尔诺夫先后讲了话。
