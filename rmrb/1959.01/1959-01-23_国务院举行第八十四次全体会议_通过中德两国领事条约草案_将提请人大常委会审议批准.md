Title: The State Council Held Its Eighty-Fourth Plenary Meeting and Adopted the Draft Consular Treaty between China and Germany, Which Will Be Submitted to the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress for Review and Approval.
Author:
Date: 1959-01-23
Page: 2

Xinhua News Agency, 22nd - The State Council held its eighty-fourth plenary meeting today. After hearing the explanation by Vice Minister of Foreign Affairs Ji Pengfei, the meeting adopted the draft consular treaty between the People's Republic of China and the German Democratic Republic. This draft will be submitted to the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress for review and approval.

The meeting approved the changes in the administrative divisions of some counties in Qinghai Province and adopted appointments and removals.

### 国务院举行第八十四次全体会议  通过中德两国领事条约草案  将提请人大常委会审议批准

1959-01-23
第2版()
专栏：

    国务院举行第八十四次全体会议
    通过中德两国领事条约草案
    将提请人大常委会审议批准
    新华社22日讯　国务院今天举行第八十四次全体会议。会议在听了外交部副部长姬鹏飞的说明以后，通过了中华人民共和国和德意志民主共和国领事条约草案，这个草案将提交全国人民代表大会常务委员会审议批准。
    会议批准了青海省部分县行政区划的变更，通过了任免事项。
