Title: Delegations from Various Countries Visit Lenin and Stalin Mausoleums
Author:
Date: 1959-01-28
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, January 27th - TASS reports: Some communist and workers' party delegations that came to Moscow to participate in the 21st Congress of the Communist Party of the Soviet Union visited the Lenin and Stalin Mausoleums on the 26th and laid wreaths.

### 各国代表团拜谒列宁和斯大林陵墓

1959-01-28
第6版()
专栏：

    各国代表团拜谒列宁和斯大林陵墓
    新华社27日讯　据塔斯社莫斯科讯：到莫斯科参加苏联共产党第二十一次代表大会的一些共产党和工人党代表团26日拜谒了列宁和斯大林陵墓，并献了花圈。
