Title: The People of the Congo Continue Their Struggle: Anti-Belgian Demonstrations Erupt Again in Leopoldville
Author:
Date: 1959-01-18
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, January 17th: Leopoldville News: Another anti-Belgian colonial rule demonstration erupted in the N’djili-Ndjili suburb of Leopoldville, the capital of the Belgian Congo, on the 16th. The police of the Belgian colonial authorities opened fire to suppress the demonstration. On the same day, the Belgian colonial authorities implemented large-scale identity card checks in Leopoldville and arrested two hundred and eighty people during the checks.

The situation in Leopoldville remains tense, and martial law is still in effect. Colonial authorities' police patrols, helicopters, and "pickets" are on alert, ready to brutally suppress the people.

### 刚果人民斗争再接再厉  利奥波德维尔又爆发反比示威

1959-01-18
第4版()
专栏：

    刚果人民斗争再接再厉
    利奥波德维尔又爆发反比示威
    据新华社17日讯　利奥波德维尔消息：比属刚果首府利奥波德维尔的恩吉里—恩吉里郊区在16日又爆发了反对比利时殖民统治的示威。比利时殖民当局的警察曾经开枪弹压。同一天，比利时殖民当局在利奥波德维尔实行大规模的身份证检查，并且在检查时逮捕了二百八十人。
    利奥波德维尔的情况仍然紧张，还在继续戒严。殖民当局的警察巡逻队、直升飞机和“纠察兵”都处于警备状态，随时准备残酷镇压。
