Title: Grotewohl Visits Iraq
Author:
Date: 1959-01-12
Page: 5
Xinhua News Agency, Baghdad, January 10th: G. Gipner, Plenipotentiary Representative of the Government of the German Democratic Republic to Arab countries, held a cocktail party in Baghdad on the evening of the 10th to welcome Prime Minister Grotewohl and the government delegation of the German Democratic Republic, who are visiting Iraq.

Prime Minister Qasim and senior Iraqi officials, as well as the diplomatic missions of various countries stationed in Iraq, attended the welcome reception.

At noon on the 9th, Prime Minister Grotewohl visited Prime Minister Qasim at the Iraqi Ministry of Defense. Subsequently, Qasim invited Grotewohl to dinner.

Prime Minister Grotewohl and the delegation visited Babylon, a famous historical site in Iraq, on the morning of the 10th.

The delegation received a warm welcome from the local people while touring Baghdad. People surrounded the delegation and shouted "Long live the friendship between the people of Iraq and the German Democratic Republic!"

### 格罗提渥在伊拉克访问

1959-01-12
第5版()
专栏：

    格罗提渥在伊拉克访问
    新华社巴格达10日电　德意志民主共和国政府驻阿拉伯国家全权代表纪普纳10日晚在巴格达举行鸡尾酒会，欢迎来伊拉克进行访问的格罗提渥总理和德意志民主共和国政府代表团。
    卡塞姆总理和伊拉克的高级官员以及驻伊拉克的各国外交使团都出席了欢迎酒会。
    9日中午，格罗提渥总理到伊拉克国防部拜访了卡塞姆总理，随后，卡塞姆邀请格罗提渥共进晚餐。
    格罗提渥总理和代表团10日早晨访问了伊拉克的有名的历史故迹巴比伦。
    代表团在巴格达游览时，受到了当地人民的热烈欢迎。人们围着代表团，并且高呼伊拉克和德意志民主共和国人民之间的友谊万岁。
