Title: Soong Ching Ling, Qian Junrui, Guo Moruo, Li Siguang Congratulate on the Successful Launch of the Soviet Union's Cosmic Rocket
Author:
Date: 1959-01-05
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, January 4th — Soong Ching Ling, President of the Sino-Soviet Friendship Association, and Qian Junrui, Secretary-General, sent a telegram on January 4th to Popova, Chairman of the Presidium of the Union of Soviet Societies for Friendship and Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries, and Andreyev, Chairman of the Council of the Soviet-Chinese Friendship Association, congratulating them on the successful launch of the Soviet Union's giant cosmic rocket.
    The telegram stated: We are very excited to learn of the victorious success of the Soviet Union's launch of a giant cosmic rocket heading towards the moon. The entire Chinese people cheer for this great achievement made by the Soviet people under the leadership of the Communist Party of the Soviet Union! On behalf of all members of the Sino-Soviet Friendship Association, we extend our warmest congratulations to you, and through you, to the great Soviet people!
    The victorious success of the Soviet Union's launch of a giant cosmic rocket heading towards the moon is another incomparably brilliant victory following the launch of three artificial earth satellites. It marks a new stage in mankind's advance into outer space. This new achievement of Soviet science, which has great historical significance, is not only the pride of the Soviet people, but also the pride of the entire socialist camp, and the pride of the entire progressive humanity!
    The victorious success of the Soviet Union's launch of a giant cosmic rocket heading towards the moon is another vivid proof that the East Wind prevails over the West Wind. People all over the world are rejoicing and encouraged by the Soviet Seven-Year Plan, this magnificent program for building communism. The victorious success of the Soviet Union's launch of a giant cosmic rocket makes them even more convinced that socialism will surely triumph over capitalism. All people in the world who are fighting for peace and human progress will greatly increase their confidence and courage from this.
    The Chinese people, under the leadership of the Chinese Communist Party, will always unite closely with the great Soviet people and strive unremittingly for our common ideals and for the bright future of mankind!
    Xinhua News Agency, January 4th — Guo Moruo, President of the Chinese Academy of Sciences, sent a telegram on January 4th to Academician Nesmeyanov, President of the Soviet Academy of Sciences, congratulating him on the successful launch of the Soviet Union's giant cosmic rocket.
    The telegram stated: On the second day of the just-arrived 1959, Soviet scientists presented the upcoming 21st Congress of the Communist Party of the Soviet Union with the successful launch of a giant rocket heading towards the moon. All mankind is cheering for you. On behalf of Chinese scientists, I extend my warm congratulations to you, Soviet scientists, engineers, and all the Soviet people.
    The successful launch of the world's first giant cosmic rocket will not only bring mankind many valuable materials about outer space that were previously impossible to know, and promote the possibility of achieving interstellar travel; it will also provide further guarantees for the noble cause of safeguarding world peace and promoting human progress.
    We are greatly encouraged and must learn well from you, learn your bold creativity and your style of combining with being practical and realistic, and constantly strive for the construction of socialism and communism.
    Xinhua News Agency, January 4th — Li Siguang, Chairman of the Scientific and Technical Association of the People's Republic of China, sent a telegram on January 4th to Siluyanov, Chairman of the All-Union Scientific and Technical Association, Mitin, Chairman of the Soviet Society for the Dissemination of Political and Scientific Knowledge, and I. Grifkov, Chairman of the Trade Union of Workers in Higher Education Institutions and Scientific Organs of the Soviet Union, congratulating them on the successful launch of the Soviet Union's giant cosmic rocket.
    The telegram stated: Just as the people of our country are vigorously carrying out the technological revolution and the cultural revolution, news has come that the Soviet Union has successfully launched a cosmic rocket to the lunar region, completely fulfilling the planned scientific tasks, and will become the first artificial planet in the solar system. Chinese scientific and technical workers feel extremely excited about this.
    The fact that the Soviet Union successfully launched a cosmic rocket to the moon is another inspiring great achievement following the successful launch of several artificial satellites by the Soviet Union. It provides a practical possibility for mankind's ideal of flying to the moon and making interplanetary flights; and, once again, powerfully proves the superiority of socialism and the brilliant achievements of Soviet science and technology. On behalf of the Scientific and Technical Association of the People's Republic of China and Chinese scientific and technical workers, I extend my lofty respect and heartfelt congratulations to you and, through you, to all Soviet scientific and technical workers.

### 宋庆龄钱俊瑞郭沫若李四光  贺苏联宇宙火箭发射成功

1959-01-05
第1版()
专栏：

    宋庆龄钱俊瑞郭沫若李四光
    贺苏联宇宙火箭发射成功
    新华社4日讯　中苏友好协会总会会长宋庆龄，秘书长钱俊瑞4日致电苏联对外友好和文化协会联合会主席团主席波波娃，苏中友好协会理事会主席安德烈耶夫，祝贺苏联发射巨型宇宙火箭成功。
    电文说，我们非常兴奋地获悉苏联发射飞向月球方向的巨型宇宙火箭胜利成功的消息。全中国人民都为在苏联共产党领导下的苏联人民所取得的这一伟大成就而欢呼！我们谨代表中苏友好协会全体会员，向你们，并通过你们向伟大的苏联人民，表示最热烈的祝贺！
    苏联发射飞向月球方向的巨型宇宙火箭的胜利成功，是继三个人造地球卫星上天以后的又一个无比辉煌的胜利。它标志着人类向宇宙空间进军跨入了新的阶段。苏联科学的这个具有伟大历史意义的新成就，不仅是苏联人民的骄傲，而且是整个社会主义阵营的骄傲，是整个进步人类的骄傲！
    苏联发射飞向月球方向的巨型宇宙火箭的胜利成功，是东风压倒西风的又一个鲜明的证明。全世界人民正在为苏联七年计划这个宏伟的建设共产主义的纲领而欢欣鼓舞。苏联发射巨型宇宙火箭的胜利成功，使他们更加确信，社会主义必将胜过资本主义。全世界一切为和平和人类进步事业而奋斗的人们，必将由此大大增强自己的信心和勇气。
    在中国共产党领导下的中国人民，一定永远和伟大的苏联人民紧密地团结一致，为我们共同的理想，为人类的美好未来而不懈地奋斗！
    新华社4日讯　中国科学院院长郭沫若4日致电苏联科学院院长涅斯米扬诺夫院士，祝贺苏联发射巨型宇宙火箭成功。
    电文说：在1959年刚刚来临的第二天，苏联科学家就以成功地发射飞向月球方向的巨型火箭，向即将召开的苏联第二十一次党代表大会献礼。全人类都在向你们欢呼。我代表中国科学家，向您和苏联科学家、工程师和全体苏联人民致以热烈的庆贺。
    世界第一个巨型宇宙火箭的发射成功，不仅将为人类带来过去所不可能知道的有关宇宙空间的许多宝贵材料，促进了实现星际航行的可能性；并使维护世界和平、增进人类进步的崇高事业获得了更进一步的保障。
    我们受到很大的鼓舞，一定要很好地向您们学，学习您们的大胆创造和与实事求是相结合的作风，为建设社会主义和共产主义而不断地奋斗。
    新华社4日讯　中华人民共和国科学技术协会主席李四光4日致电全苏科学技术协会主席西鲁雅诺夫、苏联政治和科学知识普及协会主席米丁及苏联高等学校和科学机关工作者工会主席依·格里夫哥夫，祝贺苏联发射巨型宇宙火箭成功。
    电文说，正当我国人民大闹技术革命，文化革命的时候，传来了苏联成功地发射了宇宙火箭到达了月球区域，全部完成了预定的科学任务，并将成为太阳系第一颗人造行星的消息。中国科学技术工作者对此感到无比的兴奋。
    苏联向月球发射宇宙火箭成功的事实，是苏联几次发射人造卫星成功之后又一次振奋人心的伟大创举。它为人类飞向月球并作行星间飞行的理想提供了实际的可能；并且，再一次有力地证明了社会主义制度的优越性和苏联科学技术发展的光辉成就。我谨代表中华人民共和国科学技术协会和中国科学技术工作者，向您并通过您向全苏科学技术工作者表示崇高的敬意和衷心的祝贺。
