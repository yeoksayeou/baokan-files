Title: PLA Holds All-Army Political Work Conference; Chairman Mao Zedong Receives Comrades Attending the Meeting
Author:
Date: 1959-01-22
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, January 21st: The All-Army Political Work Conference of the Chinese People's Liberation Army, which began on December 26, 1958, successfully concluded on January 12 of this year. In accordance with the spirit of the Resolution of the Sixth Plenary Session of the Eighth Central Committee of the Party, the conference, based on summarizing the achievements and experiences of the Great Leap Forward in 1958, discussed how to deeply study the Resolution on Several Issues Concerning People's Communes, popularize secondary and higher education among cadres, the army's involvement in industrial and agricultural production, the army's participation in socialist construction, militia work, carrying out the movement of being proficient in one major and multiple skills and technological innovation, reform of teaching in colleges and universities, and improving work style. It also made arrangements for the work in 1959.

During the conference, Chairman Mao Zedong received the comrades attending the meeting and had cordial talks with some of the responsible cadres.

The comrades attending the conference conscientiously studied the Resolution of the Sixth Plenary Session of the Eighth Central Committee of the Party and conducted full discussions on the above eight issues. They unanimously believed that this conference would play an active role in the future leap forward of the army's work and expressed their agreement with and resolute implementation of the various instructions, outlines, and plans formulated at the conference.

During the discussion on how to deeply study the Resolution on Several Issues Concerning People's Communes, the conference unanimously believed that all comrades in the army must deeply study this resolution and, in combination with reality, read relevant Marxist-Leninist works to ensure that after studying, cadres have a clear concept of many fundamental issues in the current and future development stages, improve the theoretical and policy level of cadres, and further understand the correctness of the Party's General Line. The conference believed that supporting the people's communes should be the main content of this year's Spring Festival campaign to support the government and cherish the people.

The conference called on the entire army to participate in socialist construction in a more planned and organized manner, and to continue to strive together with the people of the whole country to gradually realize the grand plan of national industrialization, communal industrialization, and agricultural mechanization and electrification. The conference believed that, in order to train the troops and make officers and soldiers revolutionary soldiers with both combat skills and production skills, the troops should, in addition to helping the people with production, make use of existing land, technical equipment, and other favorable conditions to carry out regular agricultural and sideline production and industrial production, provided that it does not hinder the completion of regular basic tasks.

This conference was held under the auspices of the General Political Department. Participants included General Tan Zheng, Director of the General Political Department; General Xiao Hua, Deputy Director; General Gan Siqi; Lieutenant General Liu Zhijian; political commissars or directors of political departments of various military branches, arms of service, and colleges and universities; as well as political work cadres from the army, division, and regiment levels; and responsible cadres from some military and technical departments.

### 解放军召开全军政治工作会议  毛泽东主席接见了到会同志

1959-01-22
第1版()
专栏：

    解放军召开全军政治工作会议
    毛泽东主席接见了到会同志
    新华社21日讯　中国人民解放军全军政治工作会议自1958年12月26日开始，至今年1月12日胜利结束。会议根据党的八届六中全会决议精神，在总结1958年大跃进的成绩和经验的基础上，讨论了如何深入学习关于人民公社若干问题的决议、在干部中普及中等和高等教育、军队进行工农业生产、军队参加社会主义建设、民兵工作、开展一专多能和技术革新运动、院校教学改革、改进工作作风等问题，并部署了1959年的工作。
    会议期间，毛泽东主席接见了到会同志，并且同一些负责干部作了亲切的谈话。
    参加会议的同志认真学习了党的八届六中全会决议，对上述八项问题，进行了充分的讨论。一致认为，这次会议对今后军队工作的跃进将起到积极的作用，对会议中所订的各项指示、纲要与方案表示同意，并坚决贯彻执行。
    会议在讨论如何深入学习关于人民公社若干问题的决议时，一致认为，全军同志必须深入地学习这个决议，并结合实际，阅读有关的马克思列宁主义著作，务使干部经过学习之后，对当前和未来发展阶段中的许多带根本性质的问题有个明确的概念，使干部理论政策水平获得提高，进一步理解党的总路线的正确性。会议认为，应该把拥护人民公社作为今年春节拥政爱民活动的主要内容。
    会议要求全军更加有计划有组织地参加社会主义建设，同全国人民一道，为逐步地实现国家工业化、公社工业化和农业机械化、电气化的宏伟计划而继续奋斗。会议认为，为了锻炼部队，使官兵成为既有战斗技能又有生产技能的革命军人，部队除帮助人民生产外，还应该利用现有的土地、技术设备等有利条件，在不妨碍完成经常的基本任务的前提下，进行经常性的农副业和工业生产。
    这次会议是在总政治部主持下举行的。参加会议的有总政治部主任谭政大将、副主任萧华上将、甘泗淇上将、刘志坚中将和各军种、各兵种、各院校的政治委员或政治部主任，以及部分军、师、团各级政治工作干部，部分军事、技术部门的负责干部。
