Title: Vice Premier Kim Il Holds Banquet to Welcome Agricultural Delegations from Fraternal Countries
Author:
Date: 1959-01-11
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Pyongyang, January 10th. Vice Premier Kim Il of the Korean Cabinet held a banquet in Pyongyang tonight to welcome the agricultural delegations from fraternal countries attending the Korean National Agricultural Cooperative Congress.

The entire members of the Soviet agricultural delegation headed by Deputy Minister of Agriculture Petrov, the Chinese agricultural delegation headed by Minister of Agriculture Liao Luyan, as well as the agricultural delegations from Bulgaria, Vietnam, Mongolia, Czechoslovakia, Romania, and the German Democratic Republic were invited to the banquet. Diplomatic envoys from fraternal countries stationed in Korea also attended the banquet.

Park Jung-ae, Vice-Chairman of the Central Committee of the Korean Workers' Party, ministers of the Cabinet, and ministers of various departments of the Central Committee of the Workers' Party attended as companions.

In his speech at the banquet, Kim Il first expressed his gratitude to the agricultural delegations from fraternal countries attending the congress. He said that the Korean National Agricultural Cooperative Congress is an epoch-making turning point in further consolidating the victory of socialism in the Korean countryside and further developing agricultural production in the future. The magnificent fighting tasks put forward by Comrade Kim Il Sung at the congress inspire Korean farmers to strive for greater victories. Korean farmers are determined to realize the technological revolution and cultural revolution in the countryside in the shortest possible time and build the Korean countryside into a prosperous and cultured socialist countryside.

Kim Il said that the achievements made by the Korean people in socialist construction are inseparable from the selfless assistance and valuable experience of the people of fraternal countries headed by the Soviet Union.

The hosts and guests frequently toasted the friendship and unity of the socialist camp at the banquet.

### 金一副首相举行宴会  欢迎兄弟国家农业代表团

1959-01-11
第4版()
专栏：

    金一副首相举行宴会
    欢迎兄弟国家农业代表团
    新华社平壤10日电　朝鲜内阁副首相金一今天晚上在平壤举行宴会，欢迎参加朝鲜全国农业合作社大会的各兄弟国家的农业代表团。
    以农业部副部长彼得罗夫为首的苏联农业代表团、以农业部长廖鲁言为首的中国农业代表团以及保加利亚、越南、蒙古、捷克斯洛伐克、罗马尼亚、德意志民主共和国的农业代表团的全体成员应邀参加了宴会。各兄弟国家驻朝鲜的外交使节也参加了宴会。
    朝鲜劳动党中央委员会副委员长朴正爱，内阁各相、劳动党中央各部部长等出席作陪。
    金一在宴会上致词时首先向参加大会的各兄弟国家农业代表团表示感谢。他说，朝鲜全国农业合作社大会在进一步巩固朝鲜农村中社会主义的胜利和今后进一步发展农业生产上，是一个划时代的转折点，金日成同志在大会上提出的宏伟的斗争任务，鼓舞着朝鲜农民为争取更大的胜利而奋斗。朝鲜农民决心要在最短的时间内，实现农村的技术革命和文化革命，把朝鲜农村建设成为富裕而有文化的社会主义农村。
    金一说，朝鲜人民在社会主义建设中取得的成就，是同以苏联为首的各兄弟国家人民的无私援助和宝贵经验分不开的。
    宾主在宴会上频频为社会主义阵营的友谊和团结而干杯。
