Title: Chinese and German Comrades Gather Together to Celebrate New Year's Eve

Author: 
Date: 1959-01-03
Page: 6

    Xinhua News Agency, January 1st. Tonight, all members of the "Erich Weinert" Song and Dance Ensemble of the People's Army of the German Democratic Republic and members of the Chinese People's Liberation Army's art troupes in Beijing gathered to celebrate the New Year's Day of 1959.

    In the festive atmosphere of the Beijing Hotel hall, Chinese and German comrades held a grand joint dance party, and performed many wonderful song and dance programs in between dances.

    Also participating in this New Year's Eve party were General Peng Shaohui, Deputy Minister of National Defense, and commanders and officers of various services and arms of the Chinese People's Liberation Army; as well as Lieutenant Colonel Van Inhedde, Military Attaché of the German Democratic Republic to China.

    Musicians from the Central Experimental Opera House and the Central Philharmonic Orchestra also participated in the evening's performances.

    The "Erich Weinert" Song and Dance Ensemble of the People's Army of the German Democratic Republic returned to Beijing on December 30th of last year after visiting and performing in Nanjing, Shanghai, Hangzhou, Guangzhou, Wuhan and other places.

### 中德两国战友聚集一堂  共同欢度新年之夜

1959-01-03
第6版()
专栏：

    中德两国战友聚集一堂
    共同欢度新年之夜
    新华社1日讯  今晚，德意志民主共和国人民军埃里希·魏纳特歌舞团全体人员和中国人民解放军在北京的各文艺工作团的团员们欢聚一堂，度过了1959年的元旦之夜。
    在充满节日景象的北京饭店大厅里，中德两国的战友们举行了盛大的联欢舞会，并在舞会中间演出了许多精彩的歌舞节目。
    参加这个新年联欢晚会的，还有国防部副部长彭绍辉上将和中国人民解放军各军种各兵种部队的首长及军官们；还有德意志民主共和国驻华武官范因赫德中校。
    中央实验歌剧院和中央乐团的音乐家们，也参加了晚会的表演。
    德意志民主共和国人民军埃里希·魏纳特歌舞团，是在去年12月30日，从南京、上海、杭州、广州、武汉等地访问演出后回到北京的。
