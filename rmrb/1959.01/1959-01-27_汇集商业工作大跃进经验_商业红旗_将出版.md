Title: Gathering the Great Leap Forward Experiences in Commercial Work: “Commercial Red Flag” to be Published
Author:
Date: 1959-01-27
Page: 6

    Gathering the Great Leap Forward Experiences in Commercial Work
    “Commercial Red Flag” to be Published

    (Our newspaper's dispatch) "Commercial Red Flag" – a book compiling the experiences of the vast number of commercial workers in the Great Leap Forward in commercial work, has been compiled and selected, and is about to be published by the People's Publishing House.

    In 1958, on the basis of the victory of the rectification campaign and the anti-Rightist struggle, a situation of the Great Leap Forward centered on industrial and agricultural production appeared throughout the country, and a high tide of the Great Leap Forward was also launched on the commercial front, establishing many red flags. In order to enrich the experience of socialist commercial work, the Ministry of Commerce compiled these experiences of the red flag units into a book for promotion throughout the country. This will greatly promote the comprehensive development of the red flag movement on the commercial front and improve the quality of work.

    From this book, one can see typical experiences in all aspects of commercial work. This book is actually a summary of advanced experiences in the Great Leap Forward in commercial work over the past year.

    The compilation and selection of this book adopted the editing method of bottom-up and top-down, and after repeated revisions, removing the dross and selecting the essence. Therefore, the materials selected in this book are generally concise and easy to understand.

    Vice Chairman Zhu De wrote an inscription for "Commercial Red Flag," encouraging commercial workers to "Under the leadership of the Party, adhere to putting politics in command, follow the mass line, further improve commercial work, and strive to build our country into a great socialist country with highly developed modern industry, modern agriculture, and a modern science and culture!" Vice Premier Li Xiannian wrote a preface for "Commercial Red Flag."

### 汇集商业工作大跃进经验  “商业红旗”将出版

1959-01-27
第6版()
专栏：

    汇集商业工作大跃进经验
    “商业红旗”将出版
    本报讯　“商业红旗”——一部汇集广大商业职工商业工作大跃进经验的书籍，业已编选完成，即将由人民出版社出版。
    1958年在整风运动和反右派斗争胜利的基础上，全国出现了以工农业生产为中心的大跃进的局面，在商业战线上也掀起了大跃进的高潮，树立了许许多多红旗。为了丰富社会主义商业工作的经验，商业部把这些红旗单位的经验汇集成册，在全国范围内加以推广。这对全面展开商业战线上的红旗运动，提高工作质量，将会起很大的推动作用。
    从这部书里可以看到商业工作各个方面的典型经验。这一部书，实际上是一年来商业工作大跃进的先进经验总汇。
    对这部书的编选工作，采取了自下而上、自上而下的编辑方法，经过反复修订，去粗取精，因而这部书里所选辑的材料，一般都简明扼要，文字通俗易懂。
    朱德副主席为“商业红旗”题了词，他勉励商业工作者“在党的领导下，坚持政治挂帅，走群众路线，进一步做好商业工作，为把我国建设成为一个具有高度发展的现代工业、现代农业、现代科学文化的伟大的社会主义国家而奋斗！”李先念副总理为“商业红旗”写了序言。
