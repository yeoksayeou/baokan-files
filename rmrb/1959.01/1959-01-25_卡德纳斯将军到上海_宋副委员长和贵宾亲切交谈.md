Title: General Cárdenas Arrives in Shanghai, Vice Chairman Soong and Distinguished Guests Have Cordial Conversation
Author:
Date: 1959-01-25
Page: 2

    Xinhua News Agency, Shanghai, January 24th: Soong Ching-ling, Vice Chairman of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress, met with Lázaro Cárdenas, former President of Mexico and Vice Chairman of the World Peace Council, and his entourage in Shanghai this afternoon.
    Vice Chairman Soong Ching-ling and General Cárdenas had a cordial conversation and took a photo together before parting.
    Present at the meeting were Guo Moruo, Chairman of the China Peace Committee, and Jin Zhonghua, Chairman of the Shanghai Branch of the China Peace Committee.
    Xinhua News Agency, January 24th: Lázaro Cárdenas, former President of Mexico and Vice Chairman of the World Peace Council, and his entourage, accompanied by Guo Moruo, Chairman of the China Peace Committee, flew to Shanghai and other places by special plane today for a tour and visit.
    Those who went to the airport to welcome and see them off included Liao Chengzhi, Vice Chairman of the China Peace Committee, and well-known figures from all walks of life, including Li Dequan, Chu Tunan, Zhang Zhizhang, and Wu Han.
    Latin American peace activists in Beijing also went to the airport to see them off.
    Xinhua News Agency, Shanghai, January 24th: Lázaro Cárdenas, former President of Mexico and Vice Chairman of the World Peace Council, and his entourage arrived in Shanghai by special plane from Beijing this morning, accompanied by Guo Moruo, Chairman of the China Peace Committee.
    Greeting the distinguished guests at the airport were Jin Zhonghua, Chairman of the Shanghai Branch of the China Peace Committee, Rong Yiren, Vice Chairman, Ba Jin, Chairman of the Shanghai Branch of the Writers Association, and other well-known figures from all walks of life.
    In the afternoon, General Cárdenas and the Mexican guests who came with him, accompanied by Jin Zhonghua, Chairman of the Shanghai Branch of the China Peace Committee, toured the Shanghai urban area, workers' new village, Zhabei Industrial Zone, and the Pengpu Industrial Zone under construction. They also visited the state-owned Shanghai No. 1 Printing and Dyeing Factory and the Sun Yat-sen Residence. In the evening, Jin Zhonghua held a banquet to welcome General Cárdenas and the Mexican guests who came with him. After the banquet, the distinguished guests enjoyed excellent programs such as Yue Opera and Kunqu Opera performed by Shanghai artists for the distinguished guests.

### 卡德纳斯将军到上海  宋副委员长和贵宾亲切交谈

1959-01-25
第2版()
专栏：

    卡德纳斯将军到上海
    宋副委员长和贵宾亲切交谈
    新华社上海24日电　全国人民代表大会常务委员会副委员长宋庆龄今天下午在上海会见了墨西哥前总统、世界和平理事会副主席拉萨罗·卡德纳斯将军和他的随行人员。
    宋庆龄副委员长和卡德纳斯将军作了亲切的谈话，临别时还一起照了像。
    会见时在座的有：中国人民保卫世界和平委员会主席郭沫若、中国人民保卫世界和平委员会上海市分会主席金仲华。
    新华社24日讯　墨西哥前总统、世界和平理事会副主席拉萨罗·卡德纳斯将军和他的随行人员，今天由中国人民保卫世界和平委员会主席郭沫若陪同，乘专机飞上海等地参观访问。
    前往机场欢送的有中国人民保卫世界和平委员会副主席廖承志以及社会各界著名人士李德全、楚图南、张志让、吴晗等人。
    在北京的拉丁美洲和平人士也到机场欢送。
    新华社上海24日电　墨西哥前总统、世界和平理事会副主席拉萨罗·卡德纳斯将军和随行人员，今天上午在中国人民保卫世界和平委员会主席郭沫若陪同下，从北京乘专机到达上海。
    在机场欢迎贵宾的，有中国人民保卫世界和平委员会上海市分会主席金仲华、副主席荣毅仁，作协上海分会主席巴金等社会各界著名人士。
    卡德纳斯将军和随他同来的墨西哥贵宾们下午在中国人民保卫世界和平委员会上海市分会主席金仲华的陪同下，游览了上海市区、工人新村、闸北工业区和兴建中的彭浦工业区，参观了国营上海第一印染厂和中山故居。晚上，金仲华举行宴会，欢迎卡德纳斯将军和随他同来墨西哥贵宾。宴会后，贵宾们欣赏了上海艺术家为贵宾表演的越剧、昆曲等优秀节目。
