Title: State Sports Commission Announces National Records for Various Sports
Author:
Date: 1959-01-19
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, 18th — The Physical Culture and Sports Commission of the People's Republic of China announced on the 18th the 183 national records as of December 31, 1958, for weightlifting, swimming, track and field, speed skating, cycling, archery, shooting, radio, parachuting, gliding, and naval model sports. Among them, the national records for weightlifting in the lightest weight class snatch of 140.5 kg, the lightweight class snatch of 158 kg, and the light heavyweight class snatch of 177.5 kg, as well as the men's 100-meter breaststroke of 1 minute 11.4 seconds, are currently the highest world records.

Of these 183 national records, 64 national records for shooting, radio, parachuting, gliding, and naval model sports are being announced for the first time in our country. Some of them have already reached world level.

Many of the national records announced this time were created by newcomers to the sports arena. For example, He Yongxian, a 19-year-old male shot putter from the People's Liberation Army, improved Zheng Renqiang's 1956 national shot put record of 14 meters 18 centimeters to 15 meters 26 centimeters, becoming the first athlete in our country to push a 7.257 kg shot put beyond 15 meters. Han Laixiang, a heavyweight weightlifting newcomer, was also included in the ranks of national record holders for the first time. His 1958 national record of 122.5 kg for the heavyweight snatch was nearly 20 kg more than the 1957 national record of 103 kg set by heavyweight weightlifting veteran Chang Guanqun.

Of the 183 national records announced this time, nearly four-fifths (142) were created in 1958. Twenty-three were created in 1957, fifteen in 1956, and two in 1955. The only record that has lasted the longest is the national record of 1 minute 6.4 seconds for the men's 100-meter backstroke, created in 1954 by the late swimmer Wu Chuanyu.

### 国家体委公布  各项运动全国纪录

1959-01-19
第6版()
专栏：

    国家体委公布
    各项运动全国纪录
    新华社18日讯　中华人民共和国体育运动委员会18日公布了截至1958年12月31日为止的一百八十三项举重、游泳、田径、速度滑冰、自行车、射箭、射击、无线电、跳伞、滑翔和航海模型运动的全国纪录。其中举重方面的最轻量级双手挺举一百四十点五公斤，轻量级双手挺举一百五十八公斤和轻重量级双手挺举一百七十七点五公斤，以及男子一百公尺蛙泳一分十一秒四的全国纪录，都是目前的世界最高纪录。
    在这一百八十三项全国纪录中，有六十四项射击、无线电、跳伞、滑翔、航海模型运动的全国纪录，在我国还是第一次公布。其中有的也已经达到世界水平。
    在这次公布的全国纪录中，有许多是由运动场上的新手创造的。例如解放军十九岁的男子铅球选手贺永宪把郑仁强在1956年创造的十四公尺十八公分的铅球全国纪录，提高到了十五公尺二十六公分，成为我国第一个能把七点二五七公斤重的铅球推到十五公尺以外的运动员。重量级举重新手韩来祥也第一次被列入了全国纪录保持者的行列。他在1958年创造的重量级双手抓举一百二十二点五公斤的全国纪录，比重量级举重老将常冠群在1957年创造的一百零三公斤的全国纪录多了近二十公斤。
    在这次公布的一百八十三项全国纪录中，有近五分之四（一百四十二项）是在1958年创造的。在1957年创造的有二十三项，1956年的有十五项，1955年的有两项。唯一保持最久的一项是：已故的游泳选手吴传玉在1954年创造的男子一百公尺仰泳一分六秒四的全国纪录。
