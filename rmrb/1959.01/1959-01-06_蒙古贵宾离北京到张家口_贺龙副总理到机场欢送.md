Title: Mongolian Guests Leave Beijing for Zhangjiakou; Vice Premier He Long Greets and Sees Them Off at the Airport
Author:
Date: 1959-01-06
Page: 5
　　
　　Xinhua News Agency, Zhangjiakou, 5th -- The Mongolian government delegation, headed by Vice Chairman of the Council of Ministers of the Mongolian People's Republic, Dan. Molomzhamts, arrived in Zhangjiakou from Beijing by special plane at 10:00 a.m. today. Accompanying the delegation to Zhangjiakou were Chang Yanqing, Director of the Complete Plant Equipment Bureau of the Ministry of Foreign Trade, Zhang Shijie, Deputy Director of the Protocol Department of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs, and others.

　　The delegation was warmly welcomed at the airport by Xie Feng, Secretary of the Zhangjiakou Prefectural Committee of the Chinese Communist Party, Ma Zhesheng, Mayor of Zhangjiakou, and others. After a short rest, the distinguished guests, accompanied by Mayor Ma Zhesheng, visited the Mining Machinery Plant, the Prospecting Machinery Plant, the Machine Tool Plant, the Tannery, and the Wool Textile Factory. The distinguished guests were warmly welcomed by the workers at these locations. In the evening, Mayor Ma Zhesheng held a welcome banquet and a Jin opera performance for the distinguished guests.

　　Xinhua News Agency, 5th -- The government delegation of the Mongolian People's Republic, headed by Vice Chairman Dan. Molomzhamts, left Beijing by plane this morning to visit Zhangjiakou and Zhangbei. The delegation will return home after visiting Zhangjiakou and other places.

　　Vice Premier He Long went to the airport this morning to see off the distinguished guests. Also present at the airport to see them off were Vice Minister of Foreign Trade Li Qiang, Vice Chairman of the State Planning Commission Liu Mingfu, Vice Minister of Foreign Affairs Ji Pengfei, and heads of relevant departments.

　　Staff members of the Mongolian Embassy in China were also at the airport to see them off.

　　The Mongolian guests visited the Inner Mongolia Autonomous Region Million Folk Song Singing Exhibition in Beijing yesterday.

### 蒙古贵宾离北京到张家口  贺龙副总理到机场欢送

1959-01-06
第5版()
专栏：

    蒙古贵宾离北京到张家口
    贺龙副总理到机场欢送
    新华社张家口5日电　以蒙古人民共和国部长会议副主席丹·莫洛姆扎木茨为首的蒙古政府代表团，今天上午十时从北京乘专机到达张家口。陪同代表团一起到张家口的有对外贸易部成套设备局局长常彦卿、外交部礼宾司副司长张世杰等。
    代表团在飞机场上受到了中共张家口地委书记解峰、张家口市市长马哲生等人的热烈欢迎。贵宾们到张家口后休息片刻，在马哲生市长的陪同下参观了矿山机械厂、探矿机械厂、机床厂、制革厂、毛纺织厂等。贵宾们在这些地方受到了工人们的热烈欢迎。晚间，马哲生市长为贵宾们举行了欢迎宴会和晋剧晚会。
    新华社5日讯　以丹·莫洛姆扎木茨副主席为首的蒙古人民共和国政府代表团，今天上午乘飞机离开北京，前往张家口、张北参观访问。代表团将在访问过张家口等地后回国。
    贺龙副总理今天上午到机场欢送贵宾。到机场欢送的还有对外贸易部副部长李强，国家计划委员会副主任刘明夫，外交部副部长姬鹏飞，以及各有关部门的负责人。
    到机场送行的还有蒙古驻中国大使馆的工作人员。
    蒙古贵宾昨天曾在北京参观了内蒙古自治区百万民歌歌唱展览会。
