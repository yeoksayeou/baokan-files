Title: The Boiler Engine Spits Black Smoke
Author: Su Su
Date: 1959-01-18
Page: 8

    The boiler engine spits black smoke, chugging its way to the sky.
    The clouds in the sky are getting angry, and won't give you rain to dry you out!
    The boiler engine smiles happily, not afraid of a year without rain,
    For the rain is in my belly!
  (Hebei Changli, Su Su)

### 锅驼机冒黑烟
苏俗
1959-01-18
第8版()
专栏：

    锅驼机冒黑烟锅驼机，冒黑烟，咕嘟咕嘟上了天。天上彩云生了气，不给你下雨干死你！锅驼机，笑嘻嘻，不怕你一年不下雨，雨水在我肚子里！
  （河北昌黎　苏俗）
