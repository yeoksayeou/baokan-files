Title: Mikoyan Departs the U.S. for Home

Author: 
Date: 1959-01-22
Page: 4

    Xinhua News Agency, New York, January 21st: TASS, New York, January 21st—First Deputy Chairman of the Soviet Council of Ministers, Mikoyan, and his entourage departed from New York for home today aboard a Scandinavian Airlines plane.
    Before boarding the plane, Mikoyan answered questions from reporters, saying that he was very satisfied with this visit.
    When asked if he thought international relations would improve after this informal visit to the United States, Mikoyan replied: I dare not assert that. But my meetings and talks with people here are intended to achieve this goal.

### 米高扬离美回国

1959-01-22
第4版()
专栏：

    米高扬离美回国
    新华社21日讯　据塔斯社纽约21日讯：苏联部长会议第一副主席米高扬和他的随从人员今天乘斯堪的纳维亚航空公司的飞机从纽约回国。
    米高扬在飞机起飞前回答了记者的问题，他说，他对这次访问非常满意。
    有人问他，在这次非正式的访问美国以后，他是否以为国际关系会有所改善，米高扬回答说：我不敢断言。但是我在这里同人们的会见和谈话是想达到这个目的的。
