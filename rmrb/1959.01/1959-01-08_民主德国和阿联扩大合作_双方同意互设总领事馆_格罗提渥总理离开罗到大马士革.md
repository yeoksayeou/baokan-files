Title: German Democratic Republic and Arab League Expand Cooperation, Agree to Establish Consulates General Mutually, Prime Minister Grotewohl Leaves Cairo for Damascus
Author:
Date: 1959-01-08
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency, Cairo, January 7th. German Democratic Republic Prime Minister Grotewohl issued a statement this morning saying that during his talks with Arab League President Nasser here, they discussed the issue of developing friendly relations between the two countries. Both sides agreed to establish Consulates General mutually to strengthen the friendly relations between the two countries.

In a statement issued before he left for Damascus from here, he said that this agreement is an important step on the road to expanding cooperation between the two countries and can be considered a contribution to maintaining peace. He said that the people of the German Democratic Republic will heartily welcome this step. They have repeatedly demonstrated their friendly feelings towards the Arab people through their actions.

Grotewohl also said that he was deeply satisfied with the talks he held with President Nasser, during which the two sides discussed many important issues. He said that the talks were conducted in a truly harmonious and friendly atmosphere and were a valuable opportunity to establish personal contact. He also said that the talks confirmed that the two sides' views on important international issues were completely consistent.

Grotewohl thanked Nasser for the reception given to him and his entourage and wished the Arab League further achievements in national construction.

Xinhua News Agency, Cairo, January 6th. German Democratic Republic Prime Minister Grotewohl, who is visiting here, held a second meeting with President Nasser today.

Ali Sabri, the Arab League's Minister of State in charge of Presidential Affairs, also attended the meeting.

Prime Minister Grotewohl told reporters after the meeting that the meeting was successful and would lead to a rapid promotion of relations between the two countries.

Xinhua News Agency, Cairo, January 7th. German Democratic Republic Prime Minister Grotewohl and the government delegation he led left Cairo by plane this morning for Damascus after a three-day friendly visit to Cairo.

Foreign Minister Fawzi and other senior officials of the Arab League were at the airport to see them off. Soviet Ambassador to the Arab League, Kiselyov, Chinese Ambassador Chen Jiakang, and other foreign envoys were also at the airport to see them off.

Before leaving, Prime Minister Grotewohl asked Fawzi to convey his regards to the Arab League government and President Nasser and thanked them for their hospitality.

Xinhua News Agency, Damascus, January 7th. German Democratic Republic Prime Minister Grotewohl and the government delegation he led arrived in Damascus by special plane from Cairo this morning.

Those who welcomed them at the airport included: Kahali, Chairman of the Executive Committee of the Syrian Region of the Arab League, ministers, the Commander and Deputy Commander of the First Army of the Arab League, senior military officers, consuls stationed in Damascus, including Shi Gu, the Chinese Consul General, and Gasili, the Director of the Algerian Office in Damascus.

The band played the national anthems of the German Democratic Republic and the Arab League. Prime Minister Grotewohl reviewed the guard of honor at the airport.

Prime Minister Grotewohl will visit Damascus for one day. He is scheduled to go to Baghdad, the capital of the Republic of Iraq, tomorrow morning.

### 民主德国和阿联扩大合作  双方同意互设总领事馆  格罗提渥总理离开罗到大马士革

1959-01-08
第4版()
专栏：

    民主德国和阿联扩大合作
    双方同意互设总领事馆
    格罗提渥总理离开罗到大马士革
    新华社开罗7日电　德意志民主共和国总理格罗提渥今天上午发表声明说，他在这里同阿拉伯联合共和国总统纳赛尔举行会谈期间，讨论了发展两国的友好关系的问题，双方同意互设总领事馆，以加强两国的友好关系。
    他在从这里动身前往大马士革前所发表的声明说，这项协议是扩大两国之间的合作的道路上的一个重要步骤，并且可以被认为是对维护和平的贡献。他说，德意志民主共和国人民将衷心欢迎这个步骤。他们曾经再三用行动来证明他们对阿拉伯人民的友好感情。
    格罗提渥又说，他对他同纳赛尔总统举行的会谈深感满意，在这次会谈期间，双方讨论了许多重要的问题。他说，这次会谈是在真正和谐与友好的气氛中进行的，而且是建立个人接触的宝贵机会。他又说，这次会谈证实了双方对重要的国际问题的看法是完全一致的。
    格罗提渥感谢纳赛尔给予他和他的随行人员的接待，并且预祝阿联在国家建设中取得进一步的成就。
    新华社开罗6日电　正在这里访问的德意志民主共和国总理格罗提渥今天同纳赛尔总统举行了第二次会谈。
    阿联负责总统事务的国务部长阿里·萨布里也参加了会谈。
    格罗提渥总理在会谈后对报界说，这次会谈是成功的，它将导致迅速促进两国之间的关系。
    新华社开罗7日电　德意志民主共和国总理格罗提渥和他率领的政府代表团在开罗进行了三天的友好访问后，今天上午乘飞机离开开罗前往大马士革。
    到机场送行的有阿拉伯联合共和国外交部长法齐和其他高级官员。苏联驻阿联大使基谢廖夫和中国大使陈家康等各国外交使节也到机场送行。
    格罗提渥总理在行前要求法齐代他向阿联政府和纳赛尔总统致意，感谢对他的款待。
    新华社大马士革7日电　德意志民主共和国总理格罗提渥和他所率领的政府代表团在今天上午从开罗乘专机到达大马士革。
    在机场欢迎的有：阿联叙利亚地区行政委员会主席卡哈利和部长们，阿联第一军军长、副军长和高级军官们，驻大马士革的各国领事其中包括中国总领事施谷，阿尔及利亚驻大马士革办事处主任加西里。
    乐队奏了德意志民主共和国、阿拉伯联合共和国的国歌。格罗提渥总理在机场上检阅了仪仗队。
    格罗提渥总理将在大马士革访问一天。他预定在明天上午前往伊拉克共和国首都巴格达。
