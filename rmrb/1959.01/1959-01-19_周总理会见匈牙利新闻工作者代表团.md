Title: Premier Zhou Meets with Hungarian Journalists Delegation
Author:
Date: 1959-01-19
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, January 18th – Premier Zhou Enlai met this morning with the Hungarian Journalists Delegation, headed by Némes Dezső, Chief Editor of the Hungarian "People's Freedom" newspaper and alternate member of the Political Bureau of the Hungarian Socialist Workers' Party Central Committee, and all members of the delegation.

During the meeting, Hungarian Ambassador to China, Nográdi, was also present.

Also present at the meeting were Wu Lengxi, Vice President of the All-China Journalists Association and Chief Editor of "People's Daily," Xu Huang, Deputy Director of the Information Department of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs, and Hu Jiwei, Deputy Chief Editor of "People's Daily." (Attached picture)

Premier Zhou Enlai meets with the entire Hungarian Journalists Delegation. Second from the right is the delegation leader, Némes Dezső, and first from the right is Ambassador Nográdi.

### 周总理会见匈牙利新闻工作者代表团

1959-01-19
第1版()
专栏：

    周总理会见匈牙利新闻工作者代表团
    新华社18日讯　周恩来总理今天上午会见了匈牙利新闻工作者代表团团长、匈牙利“人民自由报”总编辑、匈牙利社会主义工人党中央政治局候补委员内梅什·德热和代表团全体团员。
    会见的时候，匈牙利驻中国大使诺格拉第也在座。
    会见时在座的，有中华全国新闻工作者协会副会长、“人民日报”总编辑吴冷西，外交部新闻司副司长徐晃，“人民日报”副总编辑胡绩伟。（附图片）
    周恩来总理会见匈牙利新闻工作者代表团全体人员，右第二人是内梅什·德热团长，右第一人是诺格拉第大使。
  　新华社记者　唐理奎摄
