Title: Albanian Government Trade Delegation Arrives in Kunming
Author:
Date: 1959-01-13
Page: 5
Xinhua News Agency, Guangzhou, January 12th: The Vice-Chairman of the Council of Ministers of the People's Republic of Albania, Abdyl Këllezi, and the Albanian Government Trade Delegation of six members he led, concluded their visit to Guangzhou this morning and flew to Kunming on a special plane. Luo Shigao, China's Ambassador to Albania, and Bai Ren, Assistant Minister of Foreign Trade, accompanied them.

Those who greeted them at the airport included Zhu Guang, Vice Governor of Guangdong Province and Mayor of Guangzhou, Liang Xiang, Vice Mayor of Guangzhou, and Sun Leyi, among others.

The Albanian Government Trade Delegation arrived in Guangzhou from Shanghai on the 10th. During their stay in Guangzhou, the distinguished guests visited the Fanshun County People's Commune, and toured the commune's high-yield sweet potato experimental fields, fertilizer plant, public canteen, nursery, kindergarten, and home for the elderly.

During their stay in Guangzhou, the distinguished guests also visited the site of the Peasant Movement Training Institute, the Guangzhou Uprising Martyrs Cemetery, Yuexiu Mountain Park, and the China Export Commodities Exhibition Hall.

Xinhua News Agency, Kunming, January 12th: The Albanian Government Trade Delegation, led by Abdyl Këllezi, Vice-Chairman of the Council of Ministers of Albania, arrived in Kunming by plane this afternoon.

Those who went to the airport to welcome them included Liu Zhuofu, Vice Governor of Yunnan Province, and Pan Shuoduan, Mayor of Kunming, among others.

In the afternoon, the guests visited the Daguan Pavilion, a famous scenic spot in Kunming, and toured Kunming city.

In the evening, Vice Governor Liu Zhuofu held a reception and welcoming banquet for the guests.

### 阿政府贸易代表团到昆明

1959-01-13
第5版()
专栏：

    阿政府贸易代表团到昆明
    新华社广州12日电　阿尔巴尼亚人民共和国部长会议副主席阿卜杜勒·凯列齐和他率领的阿尔巴尼亚政府贸易代表团一行六人，今天上午结束了在广州的参观访问，乘专机前往昆明。我国驻阿尔巴尼亚大使罗士高和对外贸易部部长助理白认陪同前往。
    到机场欢送的有广东省副省长、广州市市长朱光，广州市副市长梁湘、孙乐宜等人。
    阿尔巴尼亚政府贸易代表团是在10日由上海来到广州的。贵宾们在广州期间，曾访问了番顺县人民公社，参观了公社的甘薯丰产试验田、肥料厂、公共食堂、托儿所、幼儿园和敬老院等。
    贵宾们在广州期间，还到农民运动讲习所旧址、广州起义烈士陵园、越秀山公园和中国出口商品陈列馆等处进行了参观和游览。
    新华社昆明12日电　阿尔巴尼亚政府贸易代表团在阿尔巴尼亚部长会议副主席阿卜杜勒·凯列齐率领下，今天下午乘飞机到达昆明。
    前往机场欢迎的有云南省副省长刘卓甫，昆明市市长潘朔端等人。
    下午，客人们游览了昆明名胜大观楼和昆明市容。
    晚上，副省长刘卓甫为客人们举行了晚会和欢迎宴会。
