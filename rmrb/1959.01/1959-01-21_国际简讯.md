International Briefs

    Indonesian Parliament Meets
    Jakarta News: The Indonesian Parliament began its first session of the year on the 19th. Parliament Speaker Sartono said in his opening speech that the bills to be discussed and decided upon at this meeting include: the bill to abolish mining concession rights, the mining bill, the oil bill, the basic land law bill, the agricultural product distribution bill, the marriage bill, and the higher education bill.

    Afghan King Inspects Highway Project
    Kabul News: Afghan King Mohammed Zahir Shah inspected the highway project passing through the Salang Pass of the Hindu Kush Mountains on the 18th. This highway is being built with Soviet assistance.
    Afghan newspapers say that the King is very satisfied with the achievements made by the road builders.

    French Police Persecute Algerians
    Paris News: According to "Liberation," French police conducted a search of Algerians in the city of Grenoble on the evening of the 18th. Two hundred and fifty people were detained.

### 国际简讯

1959-01-21
第5版()
专栏：国际简讯

    国际简讯
    印度尼西亚国会开会
    雅加达讯：　印度尼西亚国会19日开始举行今年度第一次会议，国会议长沙多诺在开幕词中说，这次会议将讨论和决定的法案包括：废除开矿租借权法案、开矿法案、石油法案、土地基本法案、农产品分配法案、婚姻法案和高等学校法案。
    阿富汗国王巡视公路工程
    喀布尔讯：阿富汗国王穆罕默德·查希尔·沙阿18日巡视了通过兴都库什山脉萨兰格隘口的公路工程，这条公路是在苏联帮助下修筑的。
    阿富汗报纸说，国王对筑路者所取得的成绩非常满意。
    法国警察迫害阿尔及利亚人
    巴黎讯：据“解放报”报道，法国警察18日晚在格累诺布尔城对阿尔及利亚人进行了搜查。有二百五十人被扣留。
