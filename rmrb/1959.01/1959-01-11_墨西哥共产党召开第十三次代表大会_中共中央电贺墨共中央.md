Title: Mexican Communist Party Holds 13th National Congress; CPC Central Committee Sends Congratulatory Message to Mexican Communist Party Central Committee
Author:
Date: 1959-01-11
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, 10th. The Central Committee of the Communist Party of China sent a telegram to the Central Committee of the Mexican Communist Party on the 7th, congratulating the Mexican Communist Party on the 13th National Congress to be held from the 11th to the 17th. The telegram is as follows:
To the Central Committee of the Mexican Communist Party and to all comrades of the 13th National Congress of the Mexican Communist Party:

	On the occasion of the 13th National Congress of the Mexican Communist Party, the Central Committee of the Communist Party of China extends to you warm and fraternal congratulations from afar, congratulating you on your persistent struggle to safeguard Mexico's national independence, and strive for the Mexican people's democratic freedoms and improved lives, and have achieved outstanding achievements in the struggle.

	The current development of the international situation is entirely favorable to the people of all countries striving for peace, democracy, national independence and social progress, and unfavorable to the imperialist bloc. Imperialism is inevitably heading towards its demise, but its death throes will inevitably become more frantic. The US imperialists are carrying out military threats against China and the Middle East, and at the same time intensifying their political interference and economic plunder of Mexico and other Latin American countries, thereby seriously threatening world peace and the security of the people of all countries. Therefore, the people of all countries in the world must maintain a high degree of vigilance against the imperialist threat of war. We believe that your congress, held at such a time, will play a major role in strengthening the common struggle of the people of the world against US imperialism.

	We congratulate your congress on defending world peace, safeguarding Mexico's national independence and democratic freedoms, striving for the unity, solidarity and cooperation of the people of Latin American countries on the basis of opposing US imperialism, and strengthening the unity within the Mexican Party and strengthening the unity of the Mexican working class and all patriotic and democratic forces. Make new and significant contributions in the struggle. Congratulations on the success of the conference!

		The Central Committee of the Communist Party of China
			January 7, 1959

### 墨西哥共产党召开第十三次代表大会  中共中央电贺墨共中央

1959-01-11
第1版()
专栏：

    墨西哥共产党召开第十三次代表大会
    中共中央电贺墨共中央
    新华社10日讯　中国共产党中央委员会7日打电报给墨西哥共产党中央委员会，祝贺墨西哥共产党将于11日至17日召开的第十三次代表大会。电文如下：墨西哥共产党中央委员会并转墨西哥共产
  党第十三次代表大会全体同志：
    兹值墨西哥共产党召开第十三次代表大会之际，中国共产党中央委员会谨向你们遥致热烈的兄弟般的祝贺，祝贺你们一贯地为维护墨西哥的民族独立、争取墨西哥人民的民主自由和生活改善而进行了顽强的斗争，并且在斗争中取得了卓越的成就。
    目前国际形势的发展完全有利于争取和平、民主、民族独立和社会进步的各国人民，而不利于帝国主义集团。帝国主义不可避免地日益走上日暮途穷的境地，但他们在垂死前的挣扎也就必然更加疯狂。美帝国主义者正在对中国和中东进行武力威胁，同时也加紧对墨西哥和拉丁美洲其他国家实行政治干预和经济掠夺，从而严重地威胁着世界的和平和各国人民的安全。因此，世界各国人民对帝国主义的战争威胁必须保持高度警惕。我们相信，你们的代表大会在这样的时候召开，对于加强全世界人民反对美帝国主义的共同斗争一定会起重大的作用。
    我们祝贺你们的代表大会在捍卫世界和平、保卫墨西哥民族独立和民主自由的斗争中，在反对美帝国主义的基础上争取拉丁美洲各国人民的联合、团结和合作的斗争中，在增强墨西哥党内的团结、加强墨西哥工人阶级和一切爱国民主力量的团结的斗争中，作出新的重大的贡献。祝贺大会成功！
              中国共产党中央委员会
                    一九五九年一月七日
