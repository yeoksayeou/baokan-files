Title: Soviet Team Defeats U.S. Team in World Basketball Championship
Author:
Date: 1959-01-30
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Santiago, January 29th: The Soviet men's basketball team overwhelmingly defeated the world basketball champion - the United States team - by a score of 62 to 37 in Santiago (Chile) on the evening of the 28th. The Soviet basketball team defeated the U.S. team in the final stage of the Third World Basketball Championship. Since basketball was included as an Olympic sport in 1936, the United States has won the championship every time.

### 在世界篮球锦标赛中苏联队击败美国队

1959-01-30
第4版()
专栏：

    在世界篮球锦标赛中苏联队击败美国队
    据新华社29日讯　圣地亚哥消息：苏联男子篮球队28日晚上在圣地亚哥（智利）以六十二分比三十七分的压倒优势击败世界篮球冠军——美国队。苏联篮球队是在第三届世界篮球锦标赛的决赛阶段击败美国队的。自从1936年篮球运动列为奥运会比赛项目以来，每届美国都获冠军。
