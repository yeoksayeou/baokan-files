Title: Albanian Government Trade Delegation Leaves for Guangzhou
Author:
Date: 1959-01-12
Page: 5
Xinhua News Agency, Shanghai, January 11th: The Vice-Chairman of the Council of Ministers of the People's Republic of Albania, Abdyl Këllezi, and the Albanian government trade delegation he led, concluded their visit to Shanghai on the 10th and flew to Guangzhou by special plane. Xu Jianguo, Secretary of the Secretariat of the Shanghai Municipal Committee of the Communist Party of China and Deputy Mayor of Shanghai, and others went to the airport to see them off. The distinguished guests visited a shipyard, a machine factory, a printing and dyeing factory, a silk factory, and a children's palace in Shanghai on the 8th.

### 阿政府贸易代表团去广州

1959-01-12
第5版()
专栏：

    阿政府贸易代表团去广州
    新华社上海11日电　阿尔巴尼亚人民共和国部长会议副主席阿卜杜勒·凯列齐和他率领的阿尔巴尼亚政府贸易代表团，10日结束了在上海的访问，乘专机飞往广州。中共上海市委书记处书记、上海市副市长许建国等前往机场欢送。贵宾们8日在上海曾参观了造船厂、机器厂、印染厂、丝绸厂和少年宫等。
