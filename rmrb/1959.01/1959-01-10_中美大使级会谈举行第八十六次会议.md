Title: Sino-US Ambassadorial Talks Hold Eighty-Sixth Meeting
Author: 
Date: 1959-01-10
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Warsaw, January 9th. The eighty-sixth meeting of the Sino-US ambassadorial talks was held in Warsaw at 2:00 PM today.

    The meeting lasted two hours and twenty-five minutes. After the meeting, both sides announced that the next meeting would continue at 2:00 PM on February 13th (Friday).

### 中美大使级会谈举行第八十六次会议

1959-01-10
第1版()
专栏：

    中美大使级会谈举行第八十六次会议
    新华社华沙9日电　中美两国大使级会谈第八十六次会议今天下午二时在华沙举行。
    本次会议历时两小时二十五分。会后双方宣布，下次会议将在2月13日（星期五）下午二时继续举行。
