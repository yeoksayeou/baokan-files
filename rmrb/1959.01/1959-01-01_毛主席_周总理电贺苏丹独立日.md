Title: Chairman Mao and Premier Zhou Send Telegrams to Congratulate Sudan's Independence Day
Author:
Date: 1959-01-01
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, December 31st—Chairman Mao Zedong sent a telegram on December 29th to General Ibrahim Abboud, Chairman of the Supreme Council of the Armed Forces of the Republic of Sudan, to congratulate Sudan on its National Independence Day. The telegram reads as follows:

To His Excellency General Ibrahim Abboud, Chairman of the Supreme Council of the Armed Forces of the Republic of Sudan, Khartoum:

On the occasion of the National Independence Day of the Republic of Sudan, I would like to extend my warm congratulations to Your Excellency and the people of Sudan on behalf of the state and the entire Chinese people, and in my own name. I wish Sudan prosperity and its people happiness.

                                    Chairman of the People's Republic of China Mao Zedong, December 29, 1958

Xinhua News Agency, December 31st—Premier Zhou Enlai sent a telegram on December 29th to General Ibrahim Abboud, Chairman of the Supreme Council of the Armed Forces of the Republic of Sudan, to congratulate Sudan on its National Independence Day. The telegram reads as follows:

To His Excellency General Ibrahim Abboud, Chairman of the Supreme Council of the Armed Forces of the Republic of Sudan, Khartoum:

On the occasion of the National Independence Day of the Republic of Sudan, I would like to extend my sincere congratulations to your government and to Your Excellency on behalf of the government of the People's Republic of China, and in my own name. I wish the Sudanese government and people success in the cause of safeguarding national independence, and I wish the friendly relations between China and Sudan will develop day by day.

                                    Premier of the State Council of the People's Republic of China Zhou Enlai, December 29, 1958

### 毛主席　周总理电贺苏丹独立日

1959-01-01
第5版()
专栏：

    毛主席　周总理电贺苏丹独立日
    新华社31日讯　毛泽东主席29日致电苏丹共和国武装部队最高委员会主席易卜拉欣·阿布德将军，祝贺苏丹共和国国家独立纪念日。电文如下：喀土穆苏丹共和国武装部队最高委员会主席易卜拉欣·阿布德将军阁下：
    欣逢苏丹共和国国家独立纪念日，我谨代表国家和全体中国人民，并且以我个人的名义向阁下和苏丹人民表示热烈的祝贺。祝苏丹国家繁荣，人民幸福。
                中华人民共和国主席　毛泽东　1958年12月29日
    新华社31日讯　周恩来总理29日致电苏丹共和国武装部队最高委员会主席易卜拉欣·阿布德将军，祝贺苏丹共和国国家独立纪念日。电文如下：喀土穆苏丹共和国武装部队最高委员会主席易卜拉欣·阿布德将军阁下：
    值此苏丹共和国国家独立纪念日之际，我谨代表中华人民共和国政府，并且以我个人的名义向贵国政府和阁下致以热诚的祝贺。祝苏丹政府和人民在维护民族独立的事业中获得成就，祝中国和苏丹之间的友好关系日益发展。
            中华人民共和国国务院总理　周恩来　1958年12月29日
