Title: Motor Trains Like Dragons
Author: Zhou Guopin
Date: 1959-01-11
Page: 6
Motor trains like dragons,
Swallowing and spitting pig iron with huge capacity;
Winding day and night on the highway,
Making the blast furnace beam with a flushed face.

### 汽车列车似蛟龙
周国聘
1959-01-11
第6版()
专栏：

    汽车列车似蛟龙
    周国聘
  汽车列车似蛟龙，
  吞吐生铁肚量宏；
  日夜蜿蜒公路上，
  笑得高炉通脸红。
