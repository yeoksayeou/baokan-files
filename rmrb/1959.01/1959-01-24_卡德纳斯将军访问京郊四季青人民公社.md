Title: General Cárdenas Visits the Evergreen People's Commune in the Suburbs of Beijing
Author:
Date: 1959-01-24
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, January 23rd. Former Mexican President General Cárdenas and his entourage, accompanied by Guo Moruo, Chairman of the Chinese People's Committee for World Peace, visited the Evergreen People's Commune in the suburbs of Beijing today.

　　General Cárdenas showed great interest in all aspects of the commune's work during his visit today. From agricultural production to industrial production, from sideline production to welfare, General Cárdenas raised a series of questions, and Yang Ming, the Party Branch Secretary of the commune, and Wang Fuhai, the Deputy Director, gave the distinguished guests satisfactory answers. General Cárdenas said to Party Branch Secretary Yang Ming: "The experience China has put forward in agriculture is valuable."

　　Guided by the commune's leaders, General Cárdenas entered rows of greenhouses and visited the cucumbers, tomatoes, and other vegetables grown by the commune members. Upon saying goodbye, General Cárdenas wished the commune continued development, continued progress, and even greater achievements in the future.

　　After visiting the commune, General Cárdenas and the Mexican guests also visited the Ming Tombs Reservoir and toured the Changling Tomb.

　　On the afternoon of the 22nd, the Mexican guests visited Tsinghua University and toured the Summer Palace. (Attached picture)

### 卡德纳斯将军访问京郊四季青人民公社

1959-01-24
第5版()
专栏：

    卡德纳斯将军访问京郊四季青人民公社
    新华社23日讯　墨西哥前总统卡德纳斯将军和他的随行人员今天由中国人民保卫世界和平委员会主席郭沫若陪同，访问了北京郊区四季青人民公社。
    卡德纳斯将军今天参观这个公社的时候，对公社的各方面工作很有兴趣。从农业生产到工业生产，从副业生产到生活福利，卡德纳斯将军提出了一系列的问题，公社的党支部书记杨明和副社长王福海分别给了贵宾们以满意的回答。卡德纳斯将军对党支部书记杨明说：“中国在农业方面提出的经验是宝贵的。”
    卡德纳斯将军由公社的负责人引导，走进了一排排温室，参观了社员们种植的黄瓜、西红柿和其他各种蔬菜。在告别的时候，卡德纳斯将军祝这个公社继续发展，继续进步，今后取得更大的成就。
    在这个公社访问以后，卡德纳斯将军和墨西哥的贵宾们又去参观了十三陵水库，游览了长陵。
    22日下午，墨西哥贵宾们曾访问了清华大学，游览了颐和园。（附图片）
