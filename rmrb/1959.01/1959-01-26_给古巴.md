Title: To Cuba
Author: Li Ying
Date: 1959-01-26
Page: 8

    To Cuba
    Li Ying
    Cuba, you are a ship, for centuries
    Yet unable to set sail,
    America's anchor is locking you, ah,
    In the sunset, wanting to float yet sinking...
    But this morning,
    A patch of colorful clouds blew in with the east wind.
    From the dense forests, valleys, and harbors,
    From the Maestra Mountains,
    All at once rang out
    The crisp gunshots of departure;
    Thus, Havana
    Set sail from the port of 1959;
    How many comrades are cheering you,
    How many comrades are about to advance as well.
    The hands of clocks around the world
    Are recording this historical moment!
    Thus I see you,
    Treading on the morning glow, holding flowers,
    Heads held high, advancing on the road home!
    Tears streaming from your smiles,
    How much pride in those tears!
    Go home—
    Let our Cuban sisters,
    Use their own sugarcane to brew sweet wine;
    Let our Cuban brothers,
    Use their own soil to fire water bottles and earthenware pots;
    Let the children open the first Cuban history book,
    Let the first Cuban sun kiss them.
    There, ore is calling for pickaxes,
    There, sugarcane is inviting blades;
    The river water no longer laps at others' land,
    Let the big trees build nests for their own little birds,
    And provide shade for their own benches...
    Go home—
    My six million Cuban brothers and sisters, ah,
    Because you have a wicked American neighbor,
    And pirate ships are constantly prowling,
    You must put bullets and hearts together,
    Tightly into the magazine,
    And shout the name of Martí,
    Sharpen your knives, guard the gate!
    Salute! Your Cuban ship sailing afar,
    Let me from this side of the Earth,
    Offer you a branch of
    Chinese forsythia!

### 给古巴
李瑛
1959-01-26
第8版()
专栏：

    给古巴
    李瑛
    古巴，你是一只船，千百年
    却不能启航扬帆，
    美国的铁锚在锁着你呵，
    夕阳里，欲浮还沉……
    可今天早晨，
    东风吹来了一片彩云。
    从密林、河谷和港湾，
    从马埃斯特腊山野，
    一齐响起了
    清脆的启航的枪声；
    于是，哈瓦那
    从1959年的港口开航了；
    多少伙伴在欢呼你，
    多少伙伴也即将前进。
    全世界时钟的指针
    都记载着这历史的时辰！
    于是我看见你，
    踏着朝霞，捧着鲜花，
    在回家的路上，昂头前进！
    笑容里淌着泪滴，
    泪滴里多么骄矜！
    回家去——
    要我们古巴姐妹们，
    用自己的甘蔗酿甜酒吧；
    要我们古巴兄弟，
    用自己的泥土烧水瓶、瓦盆；
    让孩子们打开第一册古巴历史，
    让第一个古巴的太阳亲亲他们。
    那儿，矿石在招唤铁镐，
    那儿，甘蔗在邀请刀刃；
    那河水拍着的再不是别人的土地了，
    让大树给自己的小鸟营巢，
    给自己的长椅遮荫。……
    回家去——
    我的六百万古巴的兄弟姐妹们呵，
    因为你有一个万恶的美国邻居，
    还有一艘艘海盗船不住梭巡，
    你就要把子弹和心一起，
    紧紧的压进弹夹里，
    还要喊着马蒂的名字，
    磨亮刀，守住大门！
    敬礼！你古巴远航的船，
    让我从地球的这面，
    献给你一枝
    中国的迎春！
