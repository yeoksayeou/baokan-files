Title: Chinese Communist Party Delegation Visits Lenin-Stalin Mausoleum
Author: 
Date: 1959-01-28
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, 27th -- The head of the Chinese Communist Party delegation, Zhou Enlai, who is here to attend the 21st Congress of the Communist Party of the Soviet Union, paid respects at the Lenin-Stalin Mausoleum at 3:00 p.m. today and laid two wreaths. Accompanying Zhou Enlai were delegation members Kang Sheng, Li Xuefeng, Liu Ningyi, Liu Xiao, and accompanying staff.

### 中国共产党代表团谒列宁斯大林陵墓

1959-01-28
第1版()
专栏：

    中国共产党代表团谒列宁斯大林陵墓
    新华社莫斯科27日电　应邀前来参加苏共第二十一次代表大会的中国共产党代表团团长周恩来，今天下午三时拜谒了列宁斯大林陵墓，并献了两个花圈。随同周恩来前往谒陵的有：代表团团员康生、李雪峰、刘宁一、刘晓及随行人员。
