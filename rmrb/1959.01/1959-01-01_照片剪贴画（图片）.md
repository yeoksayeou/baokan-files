Title: Photo Clipping (Picture)
Author: He Jun
Date: 1959-01-01
Page: 5

    Photo Clipping
     He Jun, with accompanying poem

    Three sworn brothers in trouble,
    Spending New Year's at the White House together,
    Forcing a group photo,
    Worries make it hard to smile.
    Equally alarmed and at a loss,
    Three sets of distressed faces,
    The old year is unbearable to look back on,
    The prospects for the New Year are bleak!
    (Xinhua News Agency draft)

### 照片剪贴画（图片）
禾君
1959-01-01
第5版()
专栏：

    照片剪贴画
     禾君　配诗
    难兄难弟三个，
    白宫共度新年，
    勉强拍张合照，
    心忧难作笑颜。
    一样惊慌失措，
    三副愁眉苦脸，
    旧岁不堪回首，
    新年前景茫然！
    （新华社稿）
