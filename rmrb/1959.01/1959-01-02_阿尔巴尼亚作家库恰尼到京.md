Title: Albanian Writer Kuqani Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1959-01-02
Page: 3

Xinhua News Agency, December 31st. Anton Kuqani, an Albanian writer who is visiting China according to the 1958 implementation plan of the China-Albania Cultural Cooperation Agreement, arrived in Beijing by plane today.

### 阿尔巴尼亚作家库恰尼到京

1959-01-02
第3版()
专栏：

    阿尔巴尼亚作家库恰尼到京
    新华社12月31日讯　根据中国阿尔巴尼亚文化合作协定1958年执行计划来我国访问的阿尔巴尼亚作家安东·库恰尼，已经在今天乘飞机到达北京。
