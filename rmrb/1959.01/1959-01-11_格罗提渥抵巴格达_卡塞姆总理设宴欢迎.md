Title: Grotewohl Arrives in Baghdad; Prime Minister Qassem Hosts Banquet
Author:
Date: 1959-01-11
Page: 4

    Xinhua News Agency, Baghdad, January 8th: After visiting the United Arab Republic, the Prime Minister of the German Democratic Republic, Otto Grotewohl, and his entourage arrived here by special plane from Damascus at noon today. Prime Minister Grotewohl and his party will pay a three-day visit to Iraq.

    At the airport to welcome the distinguished German guests were Iraqi Republic's Minister of Interior Ahmed Mohammed Yahya, Deputy Minister of Foreign Affairs Ahmed Zakaria, and other government officials.

    The Chairman of the Supreme Council of Sovereignty of the Iraqi Republic, Najib al-Rubai, received Prime Minister Grotewohl and his party shortly after their arrival here.

    Xinhua News Agency, Baghdad, January 9th: The Prime Minister of the Iraqi Republic, Qassem, hosted a banquet on the evening of January 8th to welcome the Prime Minister of the German Democratic Republic, Otto Grotewohl, and his entourage.

### 格罗提渥抵巴格达  卡塞姆总理设宴欢迎

1959-01-11
第4版()
专栏：

    格罗提渥抵巴格达
    卡塞姆总理设宴欢迎
    新华社巴格达8日电　德意志民主共和国总理格罗提渥和他的随行人员在访问了阿拉伯联合共和国之后，今天中午从大马士革乘专机到达这里。格罗提渥总理一行将在伊拉克进行为时三天的访问。
    在机场欢迎德国贵宾的有伊拉克共和国内政部长艾哈迈德·穆罕默德·叶海亚、外交部副部长艾哈迈德·扎卡里亚和其他政府官员。
    伊拉克共和国最高权力委员会主席纳吉布·鲁巴伊在格罗提渥总理一行到达这里以后不久接见了他们。
    新华社巴格达9日电　伊拉克共和国总理卡塞姆8日晚间举行宴会，欢迎德意志民主共和国总理格罗提渥和他的随行人员。
