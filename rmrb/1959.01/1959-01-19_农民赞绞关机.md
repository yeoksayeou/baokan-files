Farmers Praise the Treadmill Machine

　　The treadmill machine is a treasure,
　　It can handle many tasks:
　　It can thresh grain, it can chop straw,
　　Pulling the plow across the fields,
　　Saving labor, animals, and materials,
　　Milling rice and pumping water with high efficiency.
　　To realize agricultural mechanization and electrification,
　　It will lead the charge as the vanguard!
　　(Heilongjiang Peasant Newspaper) (Attached Image)

### 农民赞绞关机

1959-01-19
第3版()
专栏：

    农民赞绞关机
    绞关机，是个宝，
    好多活计都能搞：
    能脱谷，能铡草，
    拉着犁杖满地跑，
    省工省畜又省料，
    制米抽水效率高。
    实现农业机电化，
    它要挂帅打头炮！
    （黑龙江农民报）（附图片）
