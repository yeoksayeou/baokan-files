Title: German Government Delegation Arrives in Moscow
Author:
Date: 1959-01-30
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, January 29th - The government delegation of the German Democratic Republic, headed by Prime Minister Grotewohl, arrived in Moscow this afternoon from Beijing on a "Tu-104" special plane.

Those who went to the airport to welcome the delegation included: Member of the Presidium of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of the Soviet Union and First Deputy Chairman of the Council of Ministers of the Soviet Union, Mikoyan; Alternate Member of the Presidium of the Central Committee of the CPSU and Soviet Ambassador to the German Democratic Republic, Pervukhin; Chinese Ambassador to the Soviet Union, Liu Xiao; German Democratic Republic Ambassador to the Soviet Union, Koenig; as well as diplomatic envoys and officials from the United Arab Republic, India, and Vietnam to the Soviet Union. Grotewohl will attend the 21st Congress of the Communist Party of the Soviet Union.

### 德政府代表团抵莫斯科

1959-01-30
第1版()
专栏：

    德政府代表团抵莫斯科
    新华社莫斯科29日电　以格罗提渥总理为首的德意志民主共和国政府代表团，今天下午乘“图—一○四”专机从北京抵达莫斯科。
    前往机场欢迎的有：苏联共产党中央委员会主席团委员、苏联部长会议第一副主席米高扬、苏共中央主席团候补委员、苏联驻德意志民主共和国大使别尔乌辛，中国驻苏联大使刘晓，德意志民主共和国驻苏联大使柯尼希，以及阿拉伯联合共和国、印度、越南驻苏联的外交使节和官员。格罗提渥将参加苏联共产党第二十一次代表大会。
