Title: Sino-German Government Delegations Hold Talks: Complete Agreement on International Situation, Unity of the Socialist Camp, and Bilateral Relations

Author:

Date: 1959-01-24
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, January 23rd: The government delegation of the People's Republic of China, headed by Premier Zhou Enlai, held talks with the government delegation of the German Democratic Republic, headed by Premier Otto Grotewohl, on January 23rd.

    The Chinese side participating in the talks included: Vice Premier and Minister of Foreign Affairs Chen Yi, Minister of Foreign Trade Ye Jizhuang, Vice Minister of Foreign Affairs Zeng Yongquan, Vice Minister of Foreign Trade Lin Haiyun, Director of the Institute of Atomic Energy of the Chinese Academy of Sciences Qian Sanqiang, Ambassador of the People's Republic of China to the German Democratic Republic Wang Guoquan, and Director of the Soviet Union and Eastern Europe Department of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs Wang Yutian.

    The German Democratic Republic side participating in the talks included: Deputy Prime Minister and Minister of Foreign Affairs Dr. Lothar Bolz, Deputy Minister of Foreign Affairs with the rank of Ambassador Sepp Schwab, Deputy Minister of Foreign Trade and Intra-German Trade Gerhard Weiss, Member of the Government Commission for the Peaceful Uses of Atomic Energy and Member of the Scientific Council for Research and Development in Natural Sciences and Technology Manfred von Ardenne, Honorary Doctor, Ambassador of the German Democratic Republic to the People's Republic of China Paul Wandel, and Director of the Asia, Africa, America, and Australia Department of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs Wolfgang Kiesewetter.

    During the several hours of talks, the two sides discussed issues of mutual concern regarding the international situation, strengthening the unity of the socialist camp, and further developing the fraternal relations between the two countries.

    The two Premiers noted with satisfaction that they had reached complete agreement on the issues discussed. The two Premiers also entrusted the two Foreign Ministers to draft a joint statement on the results of the talks for submission to both delegations for adoption.

    The Government of the People's Republic of China accepted the invitation from Premier Otto Grotewohl to send a government delegation of the People's Republic of China to participate in the celebration of the tenth anniversary of the founding of the German Democratic Republic.

    The talks were conducted in a fraternal and cordial atmosphere. (Photo attached)

### 中德两国政府代表团举行会谈  对国际局势、社会主义阵营团结和两国关系等的意见完全一致

1959-01-24
第1版()
专栏：

    中德两国政府代表团举行会谈
    对国际局势、社会主义阵营团结和两国关系等的意见完全一致
    新华社23日讯　以周恩来总理为首的中华人民共和国政府代表团同以奥托·格罗提渥总理为首的德意志民主共和国政府代表团于1月23日举行了会谈。
    中国方面参加会谈的有：副总理兼外交部长陈毅，对外贸易部部长叶季壮，外交部副部长曾涌泉，对外贸易部副部长林海云，中国科学院原子能研究所所长钱三强，中华人民共和国驻德意志民主共和国大使王国权，外交部苏联东欧司司长王雨田。
    德意志民主共和国方面参加会谈的有：副总理兼外交部长洛塔·博尔茨博士，大使衔外交部副部长塞普·施瓦布，对外和东西德贸易部副部长格哈德·魏斯，德意志民主共和国政府和平利用原子能科学委员会委员、德意志民主共和国自然科学技术研究和发展谘议委员会委员曼弗雷德·冯·阿尔登内荣誉博士，德意志民主共和国驻中华人民共和国大使保罗·汪戴尔博士，外交部亚非美澳司司长伏尔夫冈·基塞维特。
    在几小时的会谈中，双方谈及了双方在有关国际局势、加强社会主义阵营的团结以及进一步发展两国之间的兄弟关系方面所关心的问题。
    两国总理满意地指出，双方在所谈及的问题中取得了完全一致的意见。两国总理并委托两国外交部长就会谈结果起草共同声明，提交双方代表团进行通过。
    中华人民共和国政府接受了奥托·格罗提渥总理提出的中华人民共和国派遣政府代表团参加德意志民主共和国成立十周年纪念日的邀请。
    会谈是在兄弟般的亲切的气氛中进行的。（附图片）
