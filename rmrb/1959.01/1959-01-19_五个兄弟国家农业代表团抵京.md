Title: Agricultural Delegations from Five Brotherly Countries Arrive in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1959-01-19
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, January 18th: Agricultural delegations from five brotherly countries arrived in Beijing by train this morning at the invitation of the Chinese Ministry of Agriculture. These delegations are: the agricultural delegation of the Mongolian People's Republic, headed by Sandagsüren Baargan, First Deputy Minister of Agriculture and Animal Husbandry of Mongolia; the agricultural delegation of the People's Republic of Bulgaria, headed by Marko Petrov Dakov, Deputy Minister of Forestry and Agriculture of Bulgaria; the agricultural delegation of the Romanian People's Republic, headed by Alexei Yujin, Deputy Minister of Agriculture and Forestry of Romania; the agricultural delegation of the German Democratic Republic, headed by Erich Lübenzam, Deputy Minister of Agriculture and Forestry of the German Democratic Republic; and the agricultural delegation of the Czechoslovak Republic, headed by Ilší Frkin, Deputy Director of the Czech Academy of Agricultural Sciences.

During their stay in China, the delegations will visit the National Agricultural Exhibition and people's communes.

Those who greeted them at the station included Liu Ruilong and Yang Xiandong, Vice Ministers of Agriculture; Zhang Kexia, Vice Minister of Forestry; Ding Ying, President of the Chinese Academy of Agricultural Sciences; and Chen Shuliang, Director of the Second Asian Department of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs.

Li Yonghao, the Ambassador of North Korea to China, and personnel from the embassies of Mongolia, Bulgaria, Romania, Germany, and Czechoslovakia in China also greeted them at the station.

### 五个兄弟国家农业代表团抵京

1959-01-19
第4版()
专栏：

    五个兄弟国家农业代表团抵京
    新华社18日讯　五个兄弟国家的农业代表团应我国农业部邀请，在今天上午乘火车到达北京。这些代表团是：以蒙古农牧部第一副部长桑达苏伦根·巴尔岗为首的蒙古人民共和国农业代表团，以保加利亚农林部副部长马科·彼得洛夫·达科夫为首的保加利亚人民共和国农业代表团，以罗马尼亚农林部副部长阿莱克塞·尤金为首的罗马尼亚人民共和国农业代表团，以德意志民主共和国农林部副部长埃里希·吕本萨姆为首的德意志民主共和国农业代表团，以捷克农业科学院副院长伊尔什·弗尔金为首的捷克斯洛伐克共和国农业代表团。
    各国代表团在我国期间，将参观全国农业展览会和人民公社。
    到车站迎接的有农业部副部长刘瑞龙、杨显东，林业部副部长张克侠，中国农业科学院院长丁颖，外交部第二亚洲司司长陈叔亮等。
    朝鲜驻华大使李永镐和蒙、保、罗、德、捷等国驻华使馆人员也到车站迎接。
