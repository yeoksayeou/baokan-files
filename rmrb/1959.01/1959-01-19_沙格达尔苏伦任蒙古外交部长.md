Title: Shagdarssuren Appointed Mongolian Minister of Foreign Affairs
Author:
Date: 1959-01-19
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Ulan Bator, January 18th: The Mongolian "Truth Daily" reported today that the Presidium of the Great People's Hural of Mongolia has appointed P. Shagdarssuren as the Minister of Foreign Affairs of Mongolia.

### 沙格达尔苏伦任蒙古外交部长

1959-01-19
第4版()
专栏：

    沙格达尔苏伦任蒙古外交部长
    新华社乌兰巴托18日电　蒙古“真理报”今天报道，蒙古大人民呼拉尔主席团任命彭·沙格达尔苏伦为蒙古外交部长。
