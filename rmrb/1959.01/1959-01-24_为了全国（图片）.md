Title: For the Whole Country (Pictures)
Author: Xiao Ye, Tong Deyin, Tian Ming, Chen Juanmei
Date: 1959-01-24
Page: 3

    For the Whole Country
    Liaoning Province, Heilongjiang Province, Shanghai City, and Tianjin City, our country's important industrial bases, are supporting national construction with complete sets of large-scale, new equipment and large quantities of industrial raw materials.
    ? Anshan Iron and Steel is producing large quantities of steel.
    ? Harbin Electric Machinery Factory is manufacturing 50,000-kilowatt generators.
    ? The Tianjin Hoisting Machinery Factory's fifty-ton bridge crane winch, urgently made for Baotou Steel, is ready for transport.
    ? Shanghai not only supports the whole country with a large number of products, but also supplies product design drawings to various regions in large quantities.
    Our reporters Xiao Ye, Tong Deyin, Tian Ming, and Chen Juanmei photographed.
