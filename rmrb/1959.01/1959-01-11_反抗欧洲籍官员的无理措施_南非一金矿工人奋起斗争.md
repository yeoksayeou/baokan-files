Title: South African Gold Miner Resists Unreasonable Measures by European Officials and Fights Bravely
Author:
Date: 1959-01-11
Page: 3

Xinhua News Agency, Johannesburg, 9th: Six hundred African miners at the Virginia gold mine (in the Orange Free State) took action on the evening of the 7th to resist European officials and mine police in the area.

They took this action to protest changes to the night shift working hours.

Police used tear gas against the workers, and many were injured. One hundred and seventy-six miners were arrested.

On the 8th, approximately four hundred miners did not report to work. A large number of police are still ready to mobilize at any time to suppress the workers.

### 反抗欧洲籍官员的无理措施  南非一金矿工人奋起斗争

1959-01-11
第3版()
专栏：

    反抗欧洲籍官员的无理措施
    南非一金矿工人奋起斗争
    新华社9日讯　约翰内斯堡消息：南非的弗吉尼亚金矿（在奥兰治自治邦）的六百名非洲籍矿工7日晚间采取行动，反抗这个矿区的欧洲籍官员和矿警。
    他们是为了抗议夜班工作时间的改变而采取反抗行动的。
    警察向工人们施放催泪瓦斯，许多人受伤。有一百七十六名矿工被捕。
    8日，约有四百名矿工没有上工。目前大批警察仍然准备随时出动镇压工人。
