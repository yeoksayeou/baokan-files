Title: The Light Bulb Shines Again
Author: Bai Xiliang
Date: 1959-01-21
Page: 2

The employees of the Qianmen Street Electric Appliance Store in Beijing have created a tungsten wire welder to weld tungsten filaments, enabling non-illuminating light bulbs to shine again.

This tungsten wire welder resembles a small wooden box containing two rectifiers. The outside of the wooden box has a tool lamp, a lamp door, and a plug socket. The welder can increase the voltage from low to high, welding the tungsten filament inside a discarded light bulb together in two minutes. This welder can also repair fluorescent light tubes. The store has now begun repairing discarded light bulbs for customers, charging only 3 to 5 fen each.
  　(Bai Xiliang) (Attached picture)

### 灯泡又亮了
白习良
1959-01-21
第2版()
专栏：

    灯泡又亮了
    北京市前门大街电器商店的职工制成了一种钨丝焊接器，用来焊接钨丝，使不能发光的灯泡能重新发光。
    这个钨丝焊接器像个小木箱，里面装有两个整流器，木箱外面有工具灯、灯门和插销座等。焊接器能使电压由低变高，在两分钟内，就把废灯泡里的钨丝焊在一块。这个焊接器还能修理日光灯管。现在这家商店已开始给顾客修理废灯泡，每个只收费三至五分。
