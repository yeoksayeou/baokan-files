Title: Andreyev Sends Telegram to Soong Ching Ling, Extending New Year Greetings to the People of Our Country
Author:
Date: 1959-01-02
Page: 3

Xinhua News Agency, January 1st: The Sino-Soviet Friendship Association Headquarters received a New Year's telegram from A.A. Andreyev, Chairman of the Council of the Soviet-Chinese Friendship Association, to President Soong Ching Ling.

In the telegram, A.A. Andreyev extended New Year greetings to all members of the Sino-Soviet Friendship Association and President Soong Ching Ling. The telegram stated: "The Soviet people note with satisfaction that in the past year, our two peoples have achieved brilliant successes in economic and cultural construction, in further developing all-round cooperation and friendship between the Soviet Union and China, and in the closer unity of all socialist countries and progressive humanity in defending peace and opposing war provocateurs.

"Please allow us to express our heartfelt congratulations to you, our dear friends, and through you, to all the Chinese people - loyal brothers, friends, and allies - and wish you new achievements in socialist construction and the struggle to defend world peace."

### 安德烈耶夫致电宋庆龄  向我国人民祝贺新年

1959-01-02
第3版()
专栏：

    安德烈耶夫致电宋庆龄
    向我国人民祝贺新年
    新华社1日讯　中苏友好协会总会收到了苏中友好协会理事会主席安·安德烈耶夫给宋庆龄会长的新年贺电。
    安·安德烈耶夫在电报中向中苏友好协会全体会员和宋庆龄会长祝贺新年。电报说：“苏联人民怀着满意的心情指出，在过去一年中，我们两国人民在经济和文化建设，在进一步发展苏中两国的全面合作与友好，以及在所有社会主义国家和进步人类保卫和平、反对战争挑拨者的斗争中更加亲密团结等方面获得了辉煌的成就。
    “请允许我们向你们，亲爱的朋友们，并通过你们，向全体中国人民——忠实的兄弟、朋友和盟友——表示衷心的祝贺，祝你们在社会主义建设和保卫世界和平的斗争中获得新的成就。”
