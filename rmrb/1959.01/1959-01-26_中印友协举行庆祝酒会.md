Title: Sino-Indian Friendship Association Holds Reception

Author: 
Date: 1959-01-26
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, January 25th: The Sino-Indian Friendship Association held a reception this evening at the Xinqiao Hotel to celebrate the ninth anniversary of the founding of the Republic of India, which falls on January 26th.

Indian Ambassador to China, Parthasarathi, along with staff from the embassy, Indian experts in the capital, Indian friends, and Indian students were invited to attend the reception.

Vice Minister of Foreign Affairs Zhang Hanfu, Vice President of the Sino-Indian Friendship Association Chen Hansheng, and people from all walks of life in the capital attended the reception.

President of the Sino-Indian Friendship Association, Ding Xilin, and Ambassador Parthasarathi spoke successively, warmly congratulating world peace, congratulating the increasing prosperity and strength of China and India, and congratulating the continuous development of the traditional friendship between the two peoples.

### 中印友协举行庆祝酒会

1959-01-26
第1版()
专栏：

    中印友协举行庆祝酒会
    新华社25日讯　1月26日是印度共和国成立九周年的日子，中国印度友好协会今天晚间在新侨饭店举行了庆祝酒会。
    印度驻中国大使帕塔萨拉蒂和大使馆的工作人员，在首都的印度专家、印度朋友、印度留学生应邀出席了酒会。
    外交部副部长章汉夫和中国印度友好协会副会长陈翰笙以及首都各界人士等出席了酒会。
    中印友好协会会长丁西林和帕塔萨拉蒂大使先后讲话，热烈祝贺世界和平，祝贺中印两国日益繁荣富强，祝贺两国人民的传统友谊不断发展。
