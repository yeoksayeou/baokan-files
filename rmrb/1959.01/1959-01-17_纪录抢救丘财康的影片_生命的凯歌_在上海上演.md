Title: Documentary Film on the Rescue of Qiu Cai Kang, "Ode to Life's Triumph," Premieres in Shanghai
Author:
Date: 1959-01-17
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, Shanghai, January 15th: The moving story of Shanghai Guangci Hospital's rescue of steel warrior Qiu Cai Kang has been made into a large-scale documentary film, "Ode to Life's Triumph," by the Shanghai Science and Education Film Studio. It has been welcomed by the masses in Shanghai, with over 270,000 viewers in the first nine days of its release.

### 纪录抢救丘财康的影片  “生命的凯歌”在上海上演

1959-01-17
第6版()
专栏：

    纪录抢救丘财康的影片
    “生命的凯歌”在上海上演
    新华社上海15日电　上海广慈医院抢救钢铁战士丘财康的动人事迹，已经由上海科学教育电影制片厂摄制成了大型纪录片“生命的凯歌”。它在上海放映时，受到广大人民的欢迎，开始放映的九天中观众已达二十七万多人。
