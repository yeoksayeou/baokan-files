Title: Secretary-General of the General Council of Trade Unions of Japan, Akira Iwai, and Others Arrive in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1959-01-30
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, January 29th: At the invitation of the All-China Federation of Trade Unions, Akira Iwai, Secretary-General of the General Council of Trade Unions of Japan, and Ryoji Oyama, Director of the Political Department, arrived in Beijing by plane from Guangzhou on the afternoon of the 29th to visit our country. Liu Changsheng, Vice-Chairman of the All-China Federation of Trade Unions, Kang Yonghe, Secretary of the Secretariat, Chen Yu, Director of the International Liaison Department, Xiao Gongqi, Vice-Chairman of the National Committee of the Chinese Railway Trade Union, and others welcomed them at the airport.

### 日总评事务局长岩井章等到北京

1959-01-30
第4版()
专栏：

    日总评事务局长岩井章等到北京
    新华社29日讯　应中华全国总工会的邀请来我国访问的日本工会总评议会事务局长岩井章、政治部长小山良治，于29日下午乘飞机从广州到达北京。中华全国总工会副主席刘长胜、书记处书记康永和、国际联络部长陈宇、中国铁路工会全国委员会副主席萧供啓等都到机场欢迎。
