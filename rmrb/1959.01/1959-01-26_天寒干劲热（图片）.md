Title: The Cold Weather Intensifies Enthusiasm (Pictures)
Author: Li Fude, Feng Qi, Du Haizhen, Hao Changgeng
Date: 1959-01-26
Page: 5

    The Cold Weather Intensifies Enthusiasm
    The Dalian Mining and Industrial Vehicle Factory aims to increase the production of mining and industrial vehicles this year. Workers in the forging workshop created a double-layered octagonal furnace, enabling one furnace to do the work of two. Xinhua News Agency reporter Li Fude photo.
    In the severe cold of thirty degrees below zero, the installation work at the Harbin Thermal Power Plant is still accelerating. This is a photo of workers installing the heart of the thermal power plant - the steam turbine.
                                       Xinhua News Agency reporter Feng Qi photo.
    Employees of the No. 4 branch of the North China Radio Equipment United Factory are vigorously renovating equipment. In January, they have already renovated dozens of types of used and discarded molds, such as cold stamping dies, die-casting dies, and porcelain molds, thereby increasing production and saving steel. This is a photo of workers renovating the molds.
　　　　　　　　　　　　　 Xinhua News Agency reporter Du Haizhen photo.
    The Lanzhou Comprehensive Machine Tool Factory is speeding up construction in the severe cold. The builders are striving to complete the factory this year. This is a photo of workers hoisting prefabricated components for the factory buildings.
                                  Xinhua News Agency reporter Hao Changgeng photo.

### 天寒干劲热（图片）
李福德  冯琦  杜海振  郝常耕
1959-01-26
第5版()
专栏：

    天寒干劲热
    大连工矿车辆厂今年要增产工矿车辆。锻冶车间的工人创造了一种双层八角炉，使一个炉子能顶两个用。新华社记者　李福德摄
    在零下三十度严寒天气里，哈尔滨热电站安装工作仍在加速进行。这是工人们在安装热电站的心脏——汽轮机。
                                       新华社记者　冯琦摄
    华北无线电器材联合厂四分厂的职工正在大搞设备翻新。1月份已翻新了冷冲模、压铸模、瓷模等几十种废旧模具，提高了产量，又节约了钢材。这是工人们在翻新模具。　　　　　　　　　　　　　
　　新华社记者　杜海振摄
    兰州综合机床厂在严寒中加紧施工，建设者争取在今年建成这个工厂。这是工人在吊装厂房预制构件。
                                  新华社记者　郝常耕摄
