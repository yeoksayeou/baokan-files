Title: Bulgarian Communist Party Central Committee Holds Enlarged Meeting, Unanimously Approves Zhivkov's Report Outline
Author:
Date: 1959-01-19
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency, Sofia, January 17th. The Bulgarian Communist Party held an enlarged meeting of the Central Committee in Sofia from January 15th to 17th. Members of the Bulgarian Communist Party Central Committee's Inspection Commission, chairmen of the People's Councils of various states, and the first secretaries of county committees attended the meeting.

The plenary session discussed the report outline by Todor Zhivkov, First Secretary of the Central Committee of the Bulgarian Communist Party, on "Accelerating the Development of the National Economy, Improving the Material and Cultural Standards of the Working People, and Reorganizing State and Economic Leadership."

After extensive discussion, the Central Committee plenary session unanimously approved Zhivkov's report outline and passed a corresponding resolution.

The resolution stated that, in view of the great significance of this issue to the country, the Central Committee of the Bulgarian Communist Party believes it is necessary to submit it to the upcoming session of the Bulgarian National Assembly for discussion, and decided that Zhivkov would be the rapporteur.

The Central Committee also decided to publish Zhivkov's report outline in the January 20th newspapers to initiate a nationwide discussion.

The Central Committee of the Bulgarian Communist Party called on party organizations at all levels, the Bulgarian Agrarian People's Union, the Fatherland Front, and economic, trade union, and Communist Youth League organizations to widely discuss this report outline in cities, villages, enterprises, agricultural cooperatives, institutions, and machine tractor stations.

### 保共中央举行扩大会议  一致同意日夫科夫的报告提纲

1959-01-19
第4版()
专栏：

    保共中央举行扩大会议
    一致同意日夫科夫的报告提纲
    新华社索非亚17日电　保加利亚共产党1月15日到17日在索非亚举行了中央委员会扩大会议。保共中央检查委员会委员、各州人民会议的主席和县委第一书记参加了会议。
    全会讨论了保加利亚共产党中央委员会第一书记日夫科夫的“关于加速发展国民经济、提高劳动人民的物质和文化水平和关于改组国家和经济领导”的报告提纲。
    中央全会在经过广泛讨论后，一致同意日夫科夫的报告提纲，并通过了相应的决议。
    决议说，鉴于这个问题对国家有重大的意义，保加利亚共产党中央委员会认为有必要把它提交即将召开的保加利亚国民议会会议讨论，并决定日夫科夫为报告人。
    中央委员会还决定在1月20日的报纸上发表日夫科夫的报告提纲，以便在全民中展开讨论。
    保加利亚共产党中央委员会号召各级党组织、保加利亚农民联盟、祖国阵线和经济、工会、共青团的组织在城市、乡村、企业、农业合作社、机关、机器拖拉机站等，广泛讨论这个报告提纲。
