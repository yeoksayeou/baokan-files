From the Editor

    The Crucial First Step
    Today we publish a collection of photographs reflecting the activities of the "logistical forces" in mining, coal washing, and coking. In order to ensure that the steel marshal is "well-fed and well-nourished," these "logistical soldiers" are launching a high-quality, high-yield campaign. This is a movement of great significance. Because abundant, high-grade ore and ample, low-ash coke have a pivotal impact on the output and quality of pig iron. Therefore, grasping the ore, coal, and coke links is the first step in ensuring the completion of the 18 million tons of steel. We hope that more "logistical soldiers" will participate in this movement and wish the logistical forces victory in advance!

### 重要的一头

1959-01-21
第2版()
专栏：编后

    重要的一头
    今天我们发表了一组照片，反映了采矿、洗煤、炼焦等“后勤部队”的活动。为了保证钢铁元帅“吃得饱、吃得好”，这些“后勤兵”展开了高质量、高产量的运动。这是一个有重大意义的运动。因为，矿石足、品位高、焦炭多、灰分少，对生铁的产量和质量有举足轻重的影响。因此，抓好矿、煤、焦这一关，是保证完成一千八百万吨钢的第一个环节。我们希望更多的“后勤兵”参加这个运动，并且预祝后勤部队的胜利！
