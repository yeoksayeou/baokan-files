Title: Mongolian Government Delegation Returns to Beijing After Visiting Shanghai and Hangzhou
Author:
Date: 1959-01-04
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency, January 3rd - The government delegation of the Mongolian People's Republic, headed by Vice-Chairman D. Molomzhamts, returned to Beijing by special plane this afternoon from Hangzhou, after visiting Shanghai and Hangzhou.
    Those who greeted them at the airport included Li Qiang, Vice Minister of Foreign Trade, and Zhang Shijie, Deputy Director of the Protocol Department of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs.
    Xinhua News Agency, Hangzhou, January 3rd - The government delegation of the Mongolian People's Republic, headed by Deputy Chairman of the Council of Ministers D. Molomzhamts, left Hangzhou for Beijing by special plane this morning after spending the New Year happily with the people of Hangzhou. When Comrade D. Molomzhamts said goodbye to Wu Xian, Vice Governor of Zhejiang Province and Mayor of Hangzhou, and others who came to see them off at the airport, he said that they had spent a happy New Year in Hangzhou.
    The distinguished guests visited scenic spots such as Lingyin Temple, Yuquan, and Hupao in Hangzhou, and also visited the Jianqiao People's Commune in Hangzhou. In this commune, the distinguished guests viewed high-yield experimental fields of wheat and rapeseed, as well as various factories run by the commune.

### 在上海和杭州参观访问后  蒙古政府代表团返京

1959-01-04
第4版()
专栏：

    在上海和杭州参观访问后
    蒙古政府代表团返京
    新华社3日讯　以丹·莫洛姆扎木茨副主席为首的蒙古人民共和国政府代表团，在访问了上海、杭州以后，今天下午乘专机由杭州回到北京。
    到飞机场迎接的有对外贸易部副部长李强，外交部礼宾司副司长张世杰等人。
    新华社杭州3日电　以部长会议副主席丹·莫洛姆扎木茨为首的蒙古人民共和国政府代表团，同杭州市人民一道欢度新年后，今天上午乘专机离开杭州前往北京。丹·莫洛姆扎木茨团长在机场上向前来送行的浙江省副省长、杭州市市长吴宪等道别时说，他们在杭州度过了一个愉快的新年。
    贵宾们在杭州曾游览了灵隐、玉泉、虎跑等名胜，并且参观了杭州市笕桥人民公社。在这个公社里，贵宾们观看了小麦、油菜等高产试验田和公社办的各种工厂。
