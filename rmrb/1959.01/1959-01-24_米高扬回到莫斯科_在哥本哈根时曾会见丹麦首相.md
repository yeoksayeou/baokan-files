Title: Mikoyan Returns to Moscow; Met with Danish Prime Minister in Copenhagen
Author:
Date: 1959-01-24
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, January 23rd - TASS reports from Moscow: After visiting the United States, First Deputy Chairman of the Council of Ministers of the USSR, Mikoyan, returned to Moscow today.

Xinhua News Agency, Copenhagen, January 23rd - TASS reports from Copenhagen on January 22nd: First Deputy Chairman of the Council of Ministers of the USSR, Mikoyan, who was passing through Copenhagen on his way back to Moscow from the United States, attended a reception at the invitation of Danish Prime Minister H.C. Hansen and held talks with him.

Also present at the banquet were Danish Foreign Minister Krag, Secretary-General of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs Svenningsen, and Soviet Chargé d'Affaires to Denmark Mikhailov.

Xinhua News Agency, Copenhagen, January 23rd - TASS reports from Copenhagen on January 23rd: First Deputy Chairman of the Council of Ministers of the USSR, Mikoyan, stopped in Copenhagen for a day on January 22nd while returning home from his visit to the United States.

He attended a banquet held in his honor by the Danish-Soviet Cooperation Association. Among those present at the banquet were Danish Foreign Minister Krag and other senior government officials, as well as members of the Association's Council headed by the Chairman of the Danish-Soviet Cooperation Association and Professor at the University of Copenhagen, Jorgen Jorgensen, and Soviet Chargé d'Affaires to Denmark Mikhailov. Professor Jorgensen, Mikoyan, and Krag delivered speeches at the banquet successively. The banquet was held in a warm and friendly atmosphere.

After the banquet, Mikoyan visited the shipbuilding yard of "Burmeister & Wain" and was warmly welcomed by the company's general manager, all the employees, and representatives of the trade union. Mikoyan also spoke with workers while visiting the internal combustion engine factory at the shipyard.

The workers asked Mikoyan to convey their greetings to the Soviet working people and expressed their hope that world peace could be maintained.

The general manager of the shipyard, Munk, expressed his gratitude for Mikoyan's visit to the factory in his speech.

### 米高扬回到莫斯科  在哥本哈根时曾会见丹麦首相

1959-01-24
第5版()
专栏：

    米高扬回到莫斯科
    在哥本哈根时曾会见丹麦首相
    新华社23日讯　据塔斯社莫斯科讯：苏联部长会议第一副主席米高扬，在访问美国以后，于今天回到莫斯科。
    新华社23日讯　塔斯社哥本哈根22日讯：由美国回莫斯科路过哥本哈根的苏联部长会议第一副主席米高扬应丹麦首相汉森的邀请出席了招待会，并且同他进行了谈话。
    出席宴会的还有丹麦外交大臣克拉格、外交部秘书长斯温宁森以及苏联驻丹麦临时代办米哈伊洛夫。
    据新华社23日讯　据塔斯社哥本哈根23日讯：苏联部长会议第一副主席米高扬在访美归国途中22日在哥本哈根停留了一天。
    他应邀出席了丹苏合作协会为他举行的宴会。出席宴会的有丹麦外交大臣克拉格和其他政府高级官员，以丹苏合作协会主席、哥本哈根大学教授约根·约根森为首的协会理事会成员以及苏联驻丹麦临时代办米哈伊洛夫。约根森教授、米高扬和克拉格在宴会上相继致词。宴会是在热烈友好的气氛中进行的。
    宴会结束后，米高扬参观了“布迈斯特—瓦因”造船公司的造船厂，受到了该厂总经理以及全厂职工和工会代表的热烈欢迎。米高扬在参观造船厂的内燃机工厂时还同工人谈了话。
    工人请求米高扬代向苏联劳动人民致意，并且表示希望世界和平能够得到维护。
    造船厂总经理孟克在致词中对米高扬来厂访问表示感谢。
