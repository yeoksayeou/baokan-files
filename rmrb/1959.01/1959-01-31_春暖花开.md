Title: Spring Warmth and Flowers Bloom
Author: Guo Xiaochuan
Date: 1959-01-31
Page: 8

    Spring Warmth and Flowers Bloom
    Guo Xiaochuan
    I
    Spring has arrived,
    China is full of vitality.
    The north is snowing,
    Wheat roots stretch under the snow.
    Flowers bloom in the south,
    Cuckoos sing among the blossoms.
    The East Sea surges,
    White waves reflect the green on the shore.
    The west flies sand,
    Wind and sand roll and dance with new branches.
    Good spring,
    Making the world happy.
    Spring has arrived,
    Everywhere there is the feeling of spring.
    Small insects awaken,
    Stretch their waists and spread their wings.
    Wild geese soar high,
    Calling colorful clouds to roam the sky.
    Willow branches beckon,
    Inviting travelers to the fields.
    The wind dances gently,
    Secretly conveying affection.
    Good spring,
    Making the world a poem.
    Spring has arrived,
    All things raise their eyebrows and exhale.
    The river thaws,
    Noisily rushing towards the reservoir.
    The grass sprouts,
    Stubbornly breaking through the ground.
    The earth smells fragrant,
    Enticing iron oxen to plow.
    High mountains raise their heads,
    Yearning for their masters to plant red flags.
    Good spring,
    Truly a festival of struggle!
    Who is the master of spring?
    Quickly seize the spring.
    Spring is in the heart,
    No need to broadcast the news.
    Spring is everywhere,
    No need for postal or telegraphic notice.
    Where people are,
    Spring is there,
    Why go far to seek it?
    How high the people's hearts are,
    How good the spring is,
    Spring follows the will of the people.
    II
    Good friends,
    Good comrades!
    Come to spring,
    Don't hesitate.
    Spring warmth and flowers bloom,
    Now is the time for heroes to show their prowess.
    This great land,
    Is the place to summon wind and rain.
    The philosophy of lazybones,
    Must be discarded!
    A warrior's heart,
    Allows for no trace of delicacy!
    Good sisters,
    Good brothers!
    Come to spring,
    Don't waver!
    The Long March,
    Is only the beginning,
    The future is far,
    It is not yet time for leisure.
    Difficulties abound,
    How can they scare heroic sons and daughters!
    A thousand hardships, ten thousand toils,
    What do we fear?
    Good friends,
    Good comrades!
    Come to spring,
    Don't be lost!
    The great motherland,
    Is still a blank sheet of paper.
    Endless treasures,
    Are still locked deep underground.
    The leap forward pace,
    How can it stop halfway?!
    Skilled craftsmen,
    Have not yet displayed their superb skills?!
    Good sisters,
    Good brothers!
    Come to spring,
    Don't be careless!
    Imperialists,
    Are gnashing their teeth at us.
    Can peace-loving people not be vigilant?
    Magnificent China,
    Should be invincible in the world.
    The cause of construction,
    Must be more magnificent day by day.
    III
    Yes,
    We will not fail the beauty of spring.
    On this land,
    Live full of heroes.
    Young people are like Zhao Yun,
    The elderly are better than Huang Zhong,
    Children are like Nezha,
    Teenagers are like Luo Cheng,
    Grandmothers are like She Taijun,
    Women are like Mu Guiying.
    Good tradition,
    Eternal and evergreen.
    Yes,
    Everyone's heart is full of spring.
    On this land,
    Wealth is endless.
    Young men are brave,
    Old warriors are loyal,
    Women have many virtues,
    Adults are energetic,
    Children laugh,
    Girls are affectionate.
    Beautiful appearance,
    Hides a beautiful soul.
    When spring comes to the world,
    Flowers dance in the spring city,
    We are full of:
    Abundant knowledge,
    Heartfelt songs,
    Facing:
    The morning chill,
    The evening yellow wind.
    In the high sky,
    Release a rainbow of will,
    On the vast land,
    Spread heat and vigor.
    When spring fills the garden,
    The river thaws,
    We are full of:
    Fighting determination,
    Leap forward passion;
    Joyfully welcoming:
    The dawn clouds,
    The twilight bright stars.
    In the fields,
    Chasing the trace of youth,
    On the hills,
    Raising our hands to shake the sky.
    IV
    1958 has flown away,
    Invisible.
    Its colorful pen,
    Has already dyed our hearts red.
    Unforgettable:
    That vigorous atmosphere,
    Touching scene.
    Endless thoughts:
    Those heroic stories,
    Beautiful songs.
    Good times,
    Why not stop for a moment?
    1959 has flown in,
    The body is just as light.
    Its footsteps,
    Have already awakened us.
    Looking up at:
    That white morning light,
    Golden future;
    Can't help but:
    Dance and stamp our feet,
    High spirit and great ambition.
    Good year,
    Create another good year!
    Chinese sons and daughters,
    Broad-minded,
    Pure in heart.
    What is said,
    Can be done,
    Words must be followed by deeds.
    Many affairs,
    Heavy responsibility,
    We are willing to shoulder it.
    A little bit of exaggeration,
    A little bit of impetuosity,
    Let it all turn into a gentle breeze.
    Chinese sons and daughters,
    Ambitious,
    Capable.
    Thousands of mountains and rivers,
    Sudden rain and violent wind,
    Long familiar.
    Legs are sore,
    Muscles and bones ache,
    Can't beat a hard heart.
    Half a point of arrogance,
    Half a point of laziness,
    We will never let it survive the winter.
    V
    Friends, comrades,
    How good you are!
    Your determination,
    Is higher than the sky.
    Quickly stride forward like tigers,
    Straighten your backs like bears!
    Go to the fields,
    Go to the mountain valleys.
    Demand wealth from the earth,
    Demand treasures from the high mountains!
    The spring breeze moves,
    Opening the way for us with gongs and drums.
    1050 billion catties of grain,
    Is an ironclad indicator;
    100 million dan of cotton,
    Not even half a dan can be missing.
    The great plan of the year,
    Lies in this morning.
    Spring has come to the world,
    Quickly race against spring.
    Bring out great ambition and heroic spirit,
    Make a great disturbance in the fields.
    Let all difficulties,
    Kneel before us on both knees.
    Brothers, sisters,
    You are truly a generation of pride.
    Your drive,
    Surges to the ninth heaven.
    Quickly raise the iron hammer,
    Pick up the drill!
    Go to the furnace side,
    Enter the pit road.
    Release molten steel like rivers,
    Unlock the mysteries of nature.
    Bright spring sunshine,
    Is shining on our heads.
    18 million tons of steel,
    Must be solid.
    380 million tons of coal,
    Must be reached.
    Whose achievements,
    Will be created?
    Brave fighters,
    Do not refuse labor!
    Layers of checkpoints,
    Cannot block the way.
    Is it hard?
    Not worth a smile!
    VI
    Ah, spring has arrived!
    The masters of spring,
    Do not fail the good spring light.
    Diligent labor,
    Brave struggle,
    Only then will spring repay us.
    New spring colors,
    People are invigorated,
    People's hearts and spring colors flutter together.
    Spring has spirit,
    People also leap with joy.
    Ah, spring has arrived!
    The masters of spring,
    Make spring even more beautiful.
    Red flags waving,
    Crowds surging,
    Only then is spring so charming!
    Spring is in people,
    People are in spring,
    People and spring are融in one place.
    People are young,
    Spring is not old either.
    Spring has arrived,
    How much happiness does the world know!
    Intimate people,
    Don't misunderstand my frivolity.
    We are for today,
    And for tomorrow.
    Plant immortal peaches,
    Among the ten thousand mountains.
    Explore oil seedlings,
    In the vast Gobi.
    A lifetime of hard work, a lifetime of joy,
    The magnificent future is difficult to describe!
    Spring has arrived,
    Human joy is ten thousand feet high.
    Intimate people,
    Don't misunderstand my floating!
    We are not only for ourselves,
    Nor are we only for our families.
    A heart of hot blood,
    Burns like fire;
    Full of energy,
    Like a sword out of its sheath.
    Pledge to open the Yangguan Avenue,
    For communism!

### 春暖花开
郭小川
1959-01-31
第8版()
专栏：

    春暖花开
    郭小川
    一
    春天来了，
    中国布满生机。
    北方飘雪，
    麦根儿在雪下伸腰肢。
    南国花开，
    布谷鸟在花间啼。
    东海扬波，
    白浪映得岸上绿。
    西部飞砂，
    风砂滚滚舞新枝。
    好春天，
    惹得世界欢欢喜喜。
    春天来了，
    处处有春意。
    小虫睡醒，
    伸伸懒腰展开翅。
    雁儿高啭，
    唤来采云游天际。
    柳条招手，
    为田野招徕行旅。
    风儿漫舞，
    暗将情思传递。
    好春天，
    使天下皆诗。
    春天来了，
    万物扬眉吐气。
    河水解冻，
    吵吵嚷嚷奔向水库去。
    小草发芽，
    硬着头皮顶破地。
    大地喷香，
    逗引铁牛儿来下犁。
    高山抬头，
    渴望主人插红旗。
    好春天，
    真是斗争节日！
    谁是春天主人？
    快把春天占据。
    春在人心，
    毋须广播消息。
    春遍人间，
    不用邮电通知。
    人在哪里，
    春在哪里，
    何必远去寻觅。
    人心有多高，
    春天有多好，
    春从人意。
    二
    好朋友呀，
    好同志！
    到春天来吧，
    莫迟疑。
    春暖花开，
    正是英雄用武之时。
    大好江山，
    正是呼风唤雨之地。
    懒汉哲学，
    要丢弃！
    战士胸襟，
    不容一丝儿娇气！
    好姊妹呀，
    好兄弟！
    到春天来吧，
    莫游移！
    万里长征，
    仅仅是个开始，
    前途正远，
    还不是休闲时日。
    困难重重，
    岂能吓住英雄儿女！
    千辛万苦，
    咱们又何所畏惧？
    好朋友呀，
    好同志！
    到春天来吧，
    莫沉迷！
    伟大祖国，
    还是一张白纸。
    无尽宝藏，
    还关在深深地底。
    跃进步伐，
    岂能半路停止？！
    能工巧匠，
    还不施展高超技艺？！
    好姊妹呀，
    好兄弟！
    到春天来吧，
    莫粗心大意！
    帝国主义者，
    正对着我们咬牙切齿。
    和平人民，
    能不警惕？
    堂堂中国，
    理应天下无敌。
    建设事业，
    必须一天比一天壮丽。
    三
    是的，
    咱们不会辜负春天美景。
    这片国土上，
    住满英雄。
    青年像赵云，
    老年赛黄忠，
    儿童似哪吒，
    少年像罗成，
    婆婆好似佘太君，
    妇女如同穆桂英。
    好传统，
    万古长青。
    是的，
    人人心上春意浓。
    这片国土上，
    财富无穷。
    小伙子勇敢，
    老战士忠诚，
    女性多美德，
    壮年精力旺盛，
    孩子欢笑，
    姑娘多情。
    俊美外表，
    藏着俊美心灵。
    当春到人间，
    花舞春城，
    我们满载着：
    满腹文章，
    满腔歌声，
    面对着：
    早晨寒流，
    傍晚黄风。
    在高高天上，
    放出意志如虹，
    在广阔大地，
    散发着热气腾腾。
    当春色满园，
    河水解冻，
    我们满怀着：
    战斗决心，
    跃进激情；
    笑迎着：
    拂晓云霞，
    黄昏亮星。
    在田野里，
    追逐那青春踪影，
    在山岗上，
    举手掀动天空。
    四
    1958年飞去了，
    看不见迹踪。
    它那彩笔，
    却已把我们的心儿染红。
    忘不了：
    那蓬勃气象，
    动人场景。
    想不尽：
    那英雄故事，
    优美歌声。
    好时光，
    为什么不停一停？
    1959年飞来了，
    身子一样儿轻。
    它的脚步，
    已把我们唤醒。
    仰望着：
    那白色晨光，
    金色前程；
    禁不住：
    手舞足蹈，
    心高气盛。
    好年头，
    再创造个好年成！
    中国的儿女呀，
    胸襟开阔，
    心地光明。
    说得到，
    办得到，
    言必顾行。
    事务多，
    责任重，
    我们甘愿担承。
    半点虚夸，
    半点浮躁，
    让它一起化作清风。
    中国的儿女呀，
    有志气，
    有本领。
    千山万水，
    骤雨狂风，
    早不陌生。
    腿儿酸，
    筋骨痛，
    敌不过心肠硬。
    半分骄气，
    半分懒惰，
    决不让它度过寒冬。
    五
    朋友、同志呀，
    你们多么好！
    你们的决心，
    比天还高。
    快快迈开虎步，
    挺起熊腰！
    走向田野，
    走向山坳。
    向大地索取财富，
    向高山索取珍宝！
    春风动，
    正为我们鸣锣开道。
    一万零五百亿斤粮食，
    是铁的指标；
    一亿担棉花，
    半担也不可少。
    一年大计，
    在今朝。
    春到人间，
    快和春天赛跑。
    拿出壮志雄心，
    把田间大闹。
    让一切困难，
    在我们面前双膝跪倒。
    兄弟、姊妹呀，
    你们真是一代天骄。
    你们的干劲，
    冲九霄。
    快快举起铁锤，
    拿起钻镐！
    走向炉边，
    走进坑道。
    放出钢水如江河，
    打开大自然奥妙。
    春光明媚，
    正在我们头上照耀。
    一千八百万吨钢，
    一定要牢靠。
    三亿八千万吨煤，
    必须达到。
    丰功伟绩，
    谁创造？
    英勇斗士，
    不辞劳！
    层层关口，
    难挡道。
    辛苦吗？
    不值一笑！
    六
    呵，春天来了！
    春天的主人，
    不负春光好。
    辛勤劳动，
    勇敢斗争，
    春天才为我们报效。
    春色新，
    人儿振奋，
    人心与春色齐飘。
    春有精神，
    人也雀跃。
    呵，春天来了！
    春天的主人，
    把春天打扮得更美妙。
    红旗漫卷，
    人群鼎沸，
    春天才如此多娇！
    春在人里，
    人在春里，
    人和春天融在一道。
    人儿年青，
    春也不老。
    春天来了，
    人间幸福知多少！
    知心的人儿，
    莫错怪我轻佻。
    我们是一为今日，
    二为明朝。
    万山丛中，
    种仙桃。
    大戈壁里，
    探油苗。
    一生辛苦一生乐，
    壮丽的前程难画描！
    春天来了，
    人间喜气万丈高。
    知心的人儿，
    莫错怪我浮飘！
    我们不是仅为个人，
    也不是仅为家小。
    一腔热血，
    似火烧；
    满付精力，
    如刀出鞘。
    誓为共产主义，
    打开阳关大道！
