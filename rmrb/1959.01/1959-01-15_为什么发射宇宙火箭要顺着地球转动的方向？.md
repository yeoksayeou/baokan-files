Title: Why are cosmic rockets launched in the direction of the Earth's rotation?
Author:
Date: 1959-01-15
Page: 7

Why are cosmic rockets launched in the direction of the Earth's rotation?

    The Earth rotates on its axis, and this rotation gives any point (place) on the Earth's surface a certain speed. This speed varies with latitude. In the Northern Hemisphere, points at higher latitudes have a lower speed due to the Earth's rotation, while points at lower latitudes have a higher speed due to the Earth's rotation. A point on the Earth's equator has the highest speed, namely 465 meters per second. Therefore, launching a rocket in the direction of the Earth's rotation allows the rocket to gain more speed; conversely, launching a rocket against the direction of the Earth's rotation will cause the rocket to lose speed. For example, if we are on a ship that is moving east along the riverbank at 10 meters per second, and we are also walking east on the ship at 1 meter per second, then to someone on the shore, we are walking at 11 meters per second; if we are walking west, then to someone on the shore, we are walking at 9 meters per second. As another example, if an airplane is flying east at 300 meters per second, and a shell with an initial velocity of 600 meters per second is fired from the airplane in the direction of the airplane's motion, then the initial velocity of the shell will be 900 meters per second; conversely, if the shell is fired against the airplane's motion, then the initial velocity of the shell will be reduced to 300 meters per second.

### 为什么发射宇宙火箭要顺着地球转动的方向？

1959-01-15
第7版()
专栏：答读者

    为什么发射宇宙火箭要顺着地球转动的方向？
    地球是绕着它的轴自转的，这种转动使得地球面上任何一点（地方）都具有一定的速度。这个速度是随着纬度而变化的。就北半球而言，纬度较大的点，因地球自转而具有的速度较小；在纬度较小的点，因地球自转而具有的速度较大。在地球赤道上的点，就具有最大的速度，即465公尺／秒的速度。因此，顺着地球转动方向发射火箭，可使火箭多得速度；相反，如果逆着地球转动方向来发射火箭，则将使火箭失去速度。举例来说，如果我们在一艘轮船上，轮船正以每秒十公尺沿河岸向东移动，而我们也在船上以每秒一公尺的速度向东行走，那末对岸上来说，我们就是以每秒十一公尺的速度行走；如果我们的行走方向是向西，那末对岸上来说，我们是以每秒九公尺的速度行走的。又例如，飞机以每秒三百公尺的速度向东飞行，如果这时在飞机上顺着飞机前进方向发射初速为每秒六百公尺的炮弹，那末炮弹的初速将为每秒九百公尺；反之，如果炮弹逆着飞机前进方向发射，那末炮弹的初速将减少为每秒三百公尺。
