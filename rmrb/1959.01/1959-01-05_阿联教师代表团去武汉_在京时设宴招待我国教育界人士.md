Title: UAE Teachers Delegation to Wuhan; Held Banquet in Beijing to Entertain Chinese Education Figures
Author:
Date: 1959-01-05
Page: 4

    Xinhua News Agency, 4th. The United Arab Republic Teachers Union delegation, headed by Ahmed Zaki Muhammad, consisting of three people, left Beijing for Wuhan today. The delegation will visit Wuhan, Shanghai and other places before returning home.
    UAE Ambassador to China, Ragab, and Embassy Counselor Badran traveled with the delegation to various parts of China.
    Since arriving in Beijing on December 26th, the delegation has visited the Exhibition on the Integration of Education and Productive Labor and the National Industrial and Transportation Exhibition in Beijing. They also visited universities, middle schools, primary schools, the No. 2 State Cotton Factory, and the Children's Hospital. They held symposiums with the Ministry of Education and the China Islamic Association and participated in a get-together for Beijing teachers.
    Xinhua News Agency, 3rd. The United Arab Republic Teachers Union delegation held a banquet tonight to entertain Chinese education figures.
    At the banquet, the head of the delegation, Ahmed Zaki Muhammad, and the Vice Minister of Education, Wei Que, delivered speeches successively, warmly congratulating the friendly cooperation between the educational circles of the two countries and the friendship of the people. Da Punsheng and Zhang Jie, vice presidents of the Chinese-Arab Friendship Association, Zhou Peiyuan, vice president of Peking University, and Fang Ming, vice chairman of the Chinese Education Union, and other relevant department heads were invited to attend the banquet. Badran, Counselor of the UAE Embassy in China, also attended the banquet.

### 阿联教师代表团去武汉  在京时设宴招待我国教育界人士

1959-01-05
第4版()
专栏：

    阿联教师代表团去武汉
    在京时设宴招待我国教育界人士
    新华社4日讯　以艾哈迈德·扎基·穆罕默德团长为首的阿拉伯联合共和国教师工会代表团一行三人，今天离开北京前往武汉。代表团将在武汉、上海等地参观访问后回国。
    阿联驻华大使拉加卜和大使馆参赞巴德朗同代表团一起到我国各地旅行。
    代表团在去年12月26日到达北京以后，曾经在北京参观了教育与生产劳动相结合展览会和全国工业交通展览会，访问了大学、中学、小学、国棉二厂和儿童医院，同我国教育部和中国伊斯兰教协会举行了座谈会，参加了北京市教师的联欢晚会。
    新华社3日讯　阿拉伯联合共和国教师工会代表团今晚举行宴会，招待我国教育界人士。
    在宴会上，代表团团长艾哈迈德·扎基·穆罕默德和我国教育部副部长韦悫等先后致词，热烈祝贺两国教育界的友好合作和人民的友谊。中国阿联友好协会副会长达浦生、张杰，北京大学副校长周培源和中国教育工会副主席方明等有关部门的负责人应邀出席了宴会。阿联驻中国大使馆参赞巴德朗也出席了宴会。
