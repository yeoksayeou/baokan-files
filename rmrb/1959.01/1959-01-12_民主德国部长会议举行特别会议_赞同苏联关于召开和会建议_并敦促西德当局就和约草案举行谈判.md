Title: GDR Council of Ministers Holds Special Meeting, Endorses Soviet Proposal for a Peace Conference, and Urges West German Authorities to Negotiate on Draft Treaty
Author:
Date: 1959-01-12
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency, Berlin, January 10th: According to a report by the German News Agency, the Council of Ministers of the German Democratic Republic stated at a special meeting held here today that it agrees in principle with the draft of the Soviet proposal for a German peace treaty.

The Council of Ministers also announced its endorsement of the Soviet proposal to hold a peace conference in Warsaw or Prague within two months in order to formulate a mutually agreed-upon treaty.

The Council of Ministers calls on the people of both Germanies to express their opinions on the Soviet draft. Furthermore, it urges the West German authorities to begin preliminary negotiations on the draft treaty.

The government of the German Democratic Republic also announced that it is willing to discuss the draft German peace treaty with representatives of West German political parties, organizations, and social figures, and it requests that other relevant countries agree to conclude a German peace treaty as soon as possible.

### 民主德国部长会议举行特别会议  赞同苏联关于召开和会建议  并敦促西德当局就和约草案举行谈判

1959-01-12
第4版()
专栏：

    民主德国部长会议举行特别会议
    赞同苏联关于召开和会建议
    并敦促西德当局就和约草案举行谈判
    新华社柏林10日电　据德意志通讯社报道：德意志民主共和国部长会议今天在这里举行的特别会议上表示，它原则上同意苏联提出的对德和约草案。
    部长会议还宣布它赞同苏联关于在两个月内在华沙或布拉格举行和平会议，以便制订出一致同意的条约。
    部长会议号召两个德国的人民表示他们对苏联草案的意见。此外，它还敦促西德当局开始就和约草案举行预备性的谈判。
    德意志民主共和国政府并且宣布，它愿意同西德的政党、团体和社会人士的代表们讨论对德和约草案，它要求其他有关国家同意尽快签订对德和约。
