Author:
Date: 1959-01-13

Furnace-Side Joint Chant
Workers of Baodai Iron Plant, Zhuolu, Hebei

Ten thousand earthen furnaces of startling height,
Golden dragons dance in Baodai village tonight.
Heroic sons and daughters sweat with might,
To achieve "1800," a glorious feat to write!

Wang Chongbao: The smelting furnace, running well,
　　　　　Furnace workers, joy does swell.
　　　　　Poems by the mouth a thousand fly,
　　　　　Poetic waves in the smelting furnace lie.
Wang Shisheng: Not afraid of rain, not afraid of wind,
　　　　　Not afraid of burns, pain without end.
Dong Zhensheng: Not afraid of hardship, not afraid of woe,
　　　　　Regardless of heat or cold, before the furnace we go.
Tang Youcheng: If you want the molten iron refined well,
　　　　　Politics must come first, the foremost spell.
　　　　　First plant the red flag in your heart’s core,
　　　　　Then unleash a barrage on technology to explore.
　　　　　(As the chanting gets into full swing, the timing girl arrives.)
Dong Zhensheng: Braids in pigtails tied up high,
　　　　　A small floral jacket on her body does lie.
　　　　　Paired with black and blue trousers below,
　　　　　Square-mouthed shoes protect her feet, you know.
Dong Fuyi: The girl is active and capable,
　　　　　She announces when the iron is ladle-able.
Na Guixiang: Furnace workers, are heroes true,
　　　　　Removing their coats, before the furnace they hew.
　　　　　As energetic as Zhang Fei in full flight,
　　　　　Holding a ten-foot iron bar with all their might.
Dong Zhensheng: Speaking of heroes, heroes they are,
　　　　　They pick up the iron bar, without a scar,
　　　　　Three hammer blows open the tapping gate,
　　　　　And molten iron flows, in amounts so great.
Wang Shisheng: Iron waves three thousand miles long, the Great Wall they meet,
　　　　　Liberating Taiwan, an effortless feat.
Tang Youcheng: Mass steelmaking, a mass military exercise,
　　　　　Which enemy isn’t frightened, otherwise?
Wang Chongbao: The more molten iron, the more poetry flows,
　　　　　Let's sing a few closing lines, as everyone knows.
　　　　　The flames on the furnace burn red and bright,
　　　　　The joy in our hearts, beyond all sight.
Na Guixiang: Time is up! Comrades,
　　　　　Prepare to tap the iron!

### 炉前联唱
河北涿鹿保岱铁厂工人
1959-01-13
第8版()
专栏：

    炉前联唱
    河北涿鹿保岱铁厂工人万座土炉惊天柱，保岱村里舞金龙。英雄儿女挥汗水，要为“1800”立奇功！王崇宝：炼铁炉，很正常，
        炉前工，喜洋洋。
          信口联出诗千行，
          炼铁炉里滚诗浪。王世升：不怕雨，不怕风，
        不怕火烧不怕痛。董振声：不怕苦，不怕难，
        不分寒暑在炉前。唐有成：要想铁水炼得好，
        政治挂帅头一条。
          先把红旗插心坎，
          再向技术开排炮。（唱诗唱得正起劲，计时的姑娘来到了。）董振声：头上梳的大双辫，
        身上穿的小花袄。
        下身配上黑青裤，
        方口鞋儿护着脚。董富义：姑娘积极又能干，
        啥时出铁她发言。那桂香：炉前工，是好汉，
          脱掉棉袄炉前站。
        生龙活虎赛张飞，
          手举丈八大铁钎。董振声：说好汉，是好汉，
          拿起铁钎不怠慢，
        三锤打开出铁口，
          流出铁水千千万。王世升：铁浪三千漫长城，
          解放台湾不费难。唐有成：大炼钢铁大练兵，
        哪个敌人不害怕？王崇宝：铁水越多诗越多，
          再唱几句收尾歌。
          炉上的火苗红耀眼，
        心里的欢喜说不完。那桂香：时间到了。同志们，
          准备出铁！
