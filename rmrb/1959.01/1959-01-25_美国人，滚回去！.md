Title: Americans, Go Home!
Author: Nikolas Guillen
Date: 1959-01-25
Page: 8

    Americans, Go Home!
    By Nicolas Guillén
    Editor's note: Nicolás Guillén is a famous modern Cuban poet. His poems are full of fighting spirit, appealing and protesting for the oppressed people of Latin America, and calling on them to fight against the American ruling class. Most of his works can be sung by the masses with a six-string guitar, and are folk-style poems.
    The poem "Americans, Go Home!" was written by the author in 1952 when he attended the World Peace Council meeting in Vienna.
    I know there is a piece of land in Cuba (note)!
    It's not our flag that's hanging there,
    But the American flag,
    There is coal, tears, and blood.
    There, the American masters wield the whip.
    Cuban people! Learn this English phrase,
    To shout:
    Go home, Americans!
    I know those painful farms,
    Where your cries are so sad,
    Your heart is clenching tightly,
    Suffocating your song.
    There the American masters wield the whip.
    Cuban people! Learn this English phrase,
    To shout:
    Go home, Americans!
    I know bullets are shot into chests,
    I know how brutal the officers are,
    I know the endless torment in prisons!
    In the workers' huts, the flames of anger are burning.
    There, the American masters wield the whip.
    Cuban people! Learn this English phrase,
    To shout!
    Go home, Americans!
    With a red face, stepping off the plane,
    Smith—an ape,
    He's newly dispatched from the forests of Washington,
    In his embassy, cocktails are being prepared for him.
    There, the American masters wield the whip.
    Cuban people! Learn this English phrase,
    To shout:
    Go home, Americans!
    Note: This place refers to the port of Caimanera in Cuba, which was occupied by American imperialists as a naval base. (Attached picture)

### 美国人，滚回去！
尼古拉·纪廉
1959-01-25
第8版()
专栏：

    美国人，滚回去！
    古巴　尼古拉·纪廉
    编者按：尼古拉·纪廉是古巴现代著名的诗人。他的诗充满了战斗的气息，为受压迫的拉丁美洲人民呼吁、抗议，号召他们向美国统治阶级进行斗争。他的作品大半可以供群众伴着六弦琴歌唱，是一种民谣风的诗。
    “美国人，滚回去！”一诗是作者1952年在维也纳出席世界和平理事会会议时所作。
    我知道古巴有一块土地（注）！
    那儿挂的不是我们的国旗，
    却是美国的国旗，
    那儿有的是煤、眼泪、鲜血。
    在那儿，美国主子挥着皮鞭。
    古巴人民！学会这句英文，
    为了高呼：
    滚回去，美国人！
    我知道那些苦痛的农场，
    在那儿，你的呼号是多么悲伤，
    你的心绞得紧紧，
    窒息了你的歌声。
    在那儿美国主子挥着皮鞭。
    古巴人民！学会这句英文，
    为了高呼：
    滚回去，美国人！
    我知道枪弹射进胸口，
    我知道军官多么残暴，
    我知道监狱里是无穷的煎熬！
    工人的小屋里，愤怒的火焰在燃烧。
    在那儿，美国主子挥着皮鞭。
    古巴人民！学会这句英文，
    为了高呼！
    滚回去，美国人！
    脸孔红红的，从飞机上走下来，
    史密斯——一头人猿，
    他新从华盛顿森林里派来，
    他的使馆里，鸡尾酒正为他准备。
    在那儿，美国主子挥着皮鞭。
    古巴人民！学会这句英文，
    为了高呼：
    滚回去，美国人！
    注：这个地方指古巴的卡码尼拉港口，该地被美帝国主义者占领作为海军基地。（附图片）
