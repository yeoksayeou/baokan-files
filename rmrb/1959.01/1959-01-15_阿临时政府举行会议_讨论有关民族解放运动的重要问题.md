Title: Algerian Provisional Government Holds Meeting to Discuss Important Issues Concerning the National Liberation Movement
Author:
Date: 1959-01-15
Page: 5

　　Xinhua News Agency, Cairo, January 13th - The Algerian Provisional Government, chaired by Premier Abbas, held a meeting today. This is one of a series of meetings in recent days to discuss important issues related to the Algerian national liberation movement.

　　Algerian officials said that the government meeting is discussing some extremely important issues, including the military situation and the financial situation of the Algerian National Liberation Army. The official also said that the meeting is also discussing the results of the Algerian Provisional Government delegation's visit to China and the situation of the United Nations General Assembly's discussion of the Algerian issue.

　　According to the official, the Algerian Provisional Government will make new contacts with countries that have promised to provide assistance to Algeria.

### 阿临时政府举行会议  讨论有关民族解放运动的重要问题

1959-01-15
第5版()
专栏：

    阿临时政府举行会议
    讨论有关民族解放运动的重要问题
    新华社开罗13日电　阿尔及利亚临时政府今天在阿巴斯总理主持下举行了会议。这是几天来讨论有关阿尔及利亚民族解放运动的重要问题的一系列会议中的一次会议。
    阿尔及利亚官方人士说，政府会议正在讨论一些极为重要的问题，包括军事形势和阿尔及利亚民族解放军的财政状况。这位官方人士又说，会议还在讨论阿尔及利亚临时政府代表团访问中国的结果以及联合国大会讨论阿尔及利亚问题的情况。
    据这位官方人士说，阿尔及利亚临时政府将同答应向阿尔及利亚提供援助的国家取得新的接触。
