Title: Welcoming the Spring Fishing Season's Great Production
Author:
Date: 1959-01-27
Page: 3

Brief News

    Welcoming the Spring Fishing Season's Great Production
    Fishermen in Penglai County, Shandong Province, have broken with convention and have been going out to sea to pursue fish schools since winter. In December of last year alone, the aquatic product output reached eight million catties, more than three times the amount in the same period of 1957.
    In order to welcome the spring fishing season's great production, the county's fishermen are actively repairing ships and fishing nets, and the necessary supplies have also been fully prepared. (According to Xinhua News Agency)

### 迎接春汛大生产

1959-01-27
第3版()
专栏：简明新闻

    迎接春汛大生产
    山东蓬莱县渔民打破常规，入冬以来即下海追逐鱼群。仅去年12月，水产量达到八百万斤，比1957年同期增加了三倍多。
    为了迎接春汛大生产，这个县渔民正在积极修补船只和鱼网，所需物资也已准备齐全。（据新华社讯）
