Title: Urban Residents Participate in Manure Collection
Author:
Date: 1959-01-27
Page: 3

Xinhua News Agency report:
    Urban Residents Participate in Manure Collection
    In Lishu County, Jilin Province, 20,000 urban residents, teachers, students, and cadres are participating in manure collection in their spare time.

### 城镇人民参加积肥

1959-01-27

    城镇人民参加积肥
