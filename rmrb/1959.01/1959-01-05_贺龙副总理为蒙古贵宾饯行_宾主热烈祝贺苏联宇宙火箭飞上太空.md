Title: Vice Premier He Long Hosts Farewell Banquet for Mongolian Guests, Hosts and Guests Warmly Congratulate Soviet Cosmic Rocket's Flight into Space
Author:
Date: 1959-01-05
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, 4th. Vice Premier He Long hosted a banquet this evening to bid farewell to the Government Delegation of the Mongolian People's Republic, headed by Vice Chairman Dan. Molomjamts.

At the banquet, Vice Premier He Long, Vice Chairman Dan. Molomjamts, and all Chinese and Mongolian comrades present warmly congratulated the Soviet cosmic rocket on its flight into space. Vice Premier He Long raised his glass and said, "The Soviet cosmic rocket has left the earth and flown into the lunar region. This is a major event, we should have a drink!" At this time, the people attending the banquet raised their glasses and drank to the bottom.

The Mongolian guests will leave Beijing tomorrow to visit Zhangjiakou and other places. At this farewell banquet, the hosts and guests repeatedly raised their glasses to wish the fraternal friendship between the Chinese and Mongolian people would last forever, and to wish the economic cooperation between the two countries would develop day by day.

Vice Premier He Long said in his speech: "The visit of the Mongolian Government Delegation is a strong encouragement to our country's socialist construction. At the same time, it has enhanced the friendship and unity between our two peoples, and enabled new development in the economic cooperation between our two countries." Vice Premier He Long continued: "This fraternal economic cooperation will greatly accelerate the socialist construction of our two countries, further consolidate the fraternal friendship between the Chinese and Mongolian peoples, and will also inevitably promote the increasing prosperity of the socialist camp headed by the Soviet Union."

Vice Chairman Dan. Molomjamts spoke enthusiastically about his impressions of his visit to China. He said: "Wherever we went, the fraternal Chinese people gave our delegation a warm reception, which clearly shows that the fraternal ties between the Mongolian and Chinese people have been strengthened again, and it has also deeply inspired us."

In his speech, Vice Chairman Dan. Molomjamts highly praised our country's construction achievements and the development of people's communes. He said: "The great victory achieved by the fraternal Chinese people is a huge contribution to strengthening the strength of the socialist camp headed by the Soviet Union, and is also a major blow to imperialism. The Mongolian people and all progressive mankind are proud and delighted by the historic victory achieved by the great Chinese people."

Among those present at the banquet were Vice Minister of Foreign Trade Li Qiang, Deputy Director of the State Planning Commission Liu Mingfu, Vice Minister of Foreign Affairs Ji Pengfei, President of the China-Mongolia Friendship Association Zhang Zhixiang, and heads of relevant departments.

Staff members of the Mongolian Embassy in China were invited to attend the banquet.

### 贺龙副总理为蒙古贵宾饯行  宾主热烈祝贺苏联宇宙火箭飞上太空

1959-01-05
第4版()
专栏：

    贺龙副总理为蒙古贵宾饯行
    宾主热烈祝贺苏联宇宙火箭飞上太空
    新华社4日讯　贺龙副总理今天晚间设宴，欢送以丹·莫洛姆扎木茨副主席为首的蒙古人民共和国政府代表团。
    贺龙副总理和丹·莫洛姆扎木茨副主席以及出席宴会的中蒙两国的全体同志，在宴会上热烈祝贺苏联宇宙火箭飞上太空。贺龙副总理举杯说：“苏联宇宙火箭离开地球，飞进月球区域，这是一件大事，我们应该干一杯！”这时出席宴会的人们举起酒杯，一饮而尽。
    蒙古贵宾们将在明天离开北京，前往张家口等地参观访问。在这个临别前夕的宴会上，宾主们一再举杯，祝贺中蒙两国人民的兄弟友谊万古长青，祝贺两国的经济合作日益发展。
    贺龙副总理致词说：“蒙古政府代表团的访问，对我国的社会主义建设是一个有力的鼓舞，同时增进了我们两国人民的友谊与团结，使我们两国的经济合作有了新的发展。”贺龙副总理继续说：“这种兄弟般的经济合作将大大加速我们两国的社会主义建设，进一步巩固中蒙两国人民兄弟般的友谊。同时也必将促进以苏联为首的社会主义阵营经济的日益繁荣。”
    丹·莫洛姆扎木茨副主席在讲话中畅谈访华观感。他说：“在我们所到之处，兄弟的中国人民给予了我们代表团亲切的接待，这清楚地表明了蒙、中两国人民间兄弟般的联系再度加强，同时也深深地鼓舞了我们。”
    丹·莫洛姆扎木茨副主席在讲话中盛赞我国的建设成就和人民公社的发展。他说：“兄弟的中国人民取得的伟大胜利，是对加强以苏联为首的社会主义阵营力量的一项巨大贡献，也是对帝国主义的重大打击。蒙古人民和全世界进步人类都因伟大中国人民所取得的历史性胜利而感到骄傲和欢欣。”
    出席宴会作陪的有对外贸易部副部长李强，国家计划委员会副主任刘明夫，外交部副部长姬鹏飞，中国蒙古友好协会会长张致祥，以及有关部门的负责人等。
    蒙古驻中国大使馆的工作人员应邀出席了宴会。
