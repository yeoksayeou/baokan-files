Title: Little Steamboat Toot-Toots

Author:

Date: 1959-01-22
Page: 2

Little steamboat, toot-toots,
Running everywhere in Wolong Lake;
Commune members go to the fields by boat,
Ripe rice floats in piles,
It can transport rice to the grain station,
It can transport pesticides and fertilizers;
If we hadn't realized network of rivers,
How could there be such leisure today.
	(Suixi Wall Poem)

### 小汽船嘟嘟叫

1959-01-22
第2版()
专栏：

    小汽船嘟嘟叫
    小汽船，嘟嘟叫，
    卧龙湖里到处跑；
    社员下田都坐船，
    稻子熟了成垛飘，
    能运大米到粮点，
    能运农药和肥料；
    要不实现河网化，
    那有今朝这逍遥。
          （濉溪墙头诗）
