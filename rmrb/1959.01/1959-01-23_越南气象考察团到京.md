Title: Vietnamese Meteorological Delegation Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1959-01-23
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, January 22nd: The Vietnamese Meteorological Delegation, consisting of six members and led by Nguyen Tran, Minister of Social Relief and Director of the Central Hydrometeorological Bureau of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam, arrived in Beijing by train today at the invitation of the Central Meteorological Bureau of China. Zhang Naizhao, Deputy Director of the Central Meteorological Bureau, and others were at the station to welcome them.

### 越南气象考察团到京

1959-01-23
第5版()
专栏：

    越南气象考察团到京
    新华社22日讯　应中央气象局邀请前来我国访问的越南气象考察团一行六人，在越南民主共和国社会救济部长、中央水文气象局长阮阐率领下，今天乘火车到达北京。到车站欢迎的有中央气象局副局长张乃召等人。
