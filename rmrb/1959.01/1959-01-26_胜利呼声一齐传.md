Title: Victory Calls Resound Together
Author: Zang Kejia
Date: 1959-01-26
Page: 8

South wind, west wind,
Victory news blows into my ears.
Soaring three thousand feet high,
A storm rises over the African desert.

National liberation surges like a tide,
Ancient wells stir with waves,
The Congo must belong to the people,
The "Belgian" label must be removed!

Empty promises are rejected,
Independence and freedom are today!
Giant hands stir the ocean,
Washing away all the shameful marks!

Applause resounds continuously,
Cuba and the Congo echo each other,
The Batista regime is overthrown,
New heroes stand up in South America.

Torches are held high,
Indentures must be burned!
The people of the "Sweet Country" spit out their bitterness,
See where you, the tyrant, can escape!

Millions of people demonstrate,
America, you must open your eyes,
Millions of people with one voice,
The executioners must be judged!

America, you must open your eyes,
Don't treat South America as your backyard,
"Morality, kindness," fine words,
Unused yesterday but used today!

Victory news accompanies the New Year,
The end of winter and the light of spring are before us,
Cuba and the Congo are thousands of miles apart,
Victory calls resound together.

We listen with joy,
The calls are like a symphony,
Asia, Africa, South America,
Let us hold hands tightly.

January 24

### 胜利呼声一齐传
臧克家
1959-01-26
第8版()
专栏：

    胜利呼声一齐传
    臧克家
    南来的风，西来的风，
    胜利消息吹到耳中。
    直上高空三千丈，
    非洲大漠上起暴风。
    民族解放涌怒潮，
    千年古井起波涛，
    刚果定要属人民，
    “比属”的帽子要摘掉！
    空头支票决不要，
    独立自由在今朝！
    巨手掀起大海水，
    斑斑耻辱全洗掉！
    鼓掌声连声，
    古巴刚果相互应，
    巴蒂斯塔政权被推倒，
    南美洲站起来新英雄。
    火把举得高又高，
    卖身契约要焚烧！
    “甜国”人民吐尽苦水，
    看你暴君哪里逍遥！
    百万人民大示威，
    美国你要睁开眼，
    百万人民一个声，
    刽子手一定要审判！
    美国你要睁开眼，
    莫把南美当后院，
    “道义，善心”好字眼，
    不用在昨天却用在今天！
    胜利消息伴新年，
    冬尽春光在眼前，
    古巴刚果千万里，
    胜利呼声一齐传。
    我们听了心喜悦，
    呼声就像交响乐，
    亚洲，非洲，南美洲，
    让我们紧紧拉起手。
                    1月24日
