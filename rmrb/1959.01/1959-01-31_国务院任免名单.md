Title: State Council Appointments and Removals

Author:
Date: 1959-01-31
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, January 29th. The State Council appointed and removed a number of personnel from state organs, embassies abroad, and institutions of higher learning on November 21st and December 20th, 1958, respectively. The list is as follows:

    Appointed Zhu Yi as Deputy Director of the Counselor's Office of the State Council;
    Han Nianlong as Assistant Minister of Foreign Affairs;
    Huang Yiran as Counselor of the Embassy of the People's Republic of China in the Union of Soviet Socialist Republics;
    Wang Luming as Counselor of the Embassy of the People's Republic of China in the Romanian People's Republic;
    Zhang Yue as Counselor of the Legation of the People's Republic of China in the Mutawakkilite Kingdom of Yemen;
    Han Guang, Liu Xiyao, Zhang Youxuan, Fan Changjiang, and Wu Heng as Vice Chairmen of the Science and Technology Commission;
    Jin Zengyi as Vice President of the China University of Science and Technology;
    Wang Weiqi as Vice President of Northwestern Polytechnical University;
    Chen Xinren as President of the Institute of International Relations;
    Li Ming as Vice President of Taiyuan Mining Institute.

    Removed Zhang Xiuyan from the post of Assistant Minister of the Ministry of Supervision; Gan Yetao from the post of Counselor of the Embassy of the People's Republic of China in the Republic of Indonesia; Zhang Yue from the post of Counselor of the Embassy in the United Arab Republic; Chen Chu from the post of Counselor of the Embassy in the Union of Soviet Socialist Republics; Fan Ziwen from the post of Vice Minister of Foreign Trade; Huang Yiran from the post of Vice President of Harbin Institute of Technology.

    Appointed Zhang Xichou as President of Beijing Foreign Languages Institute; Liu Zhongrong and Yang Huafei as Vice Presidents.

    Removed Zhang Xichou from the post of President of Beijing Russian Language Institute, Yang Huafei from the post of Vice President; Liu Zhongrong from the post of President of Beijing Foreign Languages Institute; Zhao Lide, Chai Baozhong, Feng Yujiu, Yu Mei, and Ge Chen from the posts of Assistant Minister of the Ministry of Communications; Li Yukui, Ye Yunzhang, Li Yincang, Cheng Anyu, and Kuang Quanjie from the posts of Assistant Minister of the Ministry of Posts and Telecommunications.

### 国务院任免名单

1959-01-31
第6版()
专栏：

    国务院任免名单
    新华社29日讯　国务院在1958年11月21日、12月20日分别任免了一批国家机关、驻外使馆和高等学校工作人员。名单如下：
    任命朱毅为国务院参事室副主任；
    韩念龙为外交部部长助理；
    黄一然为中华人民共和国驻苏维埃社会主义共和国联盟大使馆参赞；
    王鲁明为中华人民共和国驻罗马尼亚人民共和国大使馆参赞；
    张越为中华人民共和国驻也门穆塔瓦基利亚王国公使馆参赞；
    韩光、刘西尧、张有萱、范长江、武衡为科学技术委员会副主任；
    晋曾毅为中国科学技术大学副校长；
    王维祺为西北工业大学副校长；
    陈辛仁为国际关系学院院长：
    李明为太原矿业学院副院长。
    免去张秀岩的监察部部长助理职务：甘野陶的中华人民共和国驻印度尼西亚共和国大使馆参赞职务；张越的驻阿拉伯联合共和国大使馆参赞职务；陈楚的驻苏维埃社会主义共和国联盟大使馆参赞职务；范子文的对外贸易部副部长职务；黄一然的哈尔滨工业大学副校长职务。
    任命张锡俦为北京外国语学院院长；刘仲容、杨化飞为副院长。
    免去张锡俦的北京俄语学院院长职务，杨化飞的副院长职务；刘仲容的北京外国语学院院长职务；赵立德、柴保中、冯于九、于眉、葛琛的交通部部长助理职务；李玉奎、叶云章、李荫苍、成安玉、旷泉吉的邮电部部长助理职务。
