Title: U.S. Warship Intrudes into Waters off My Matsu and Tungyin Islands; 47th Serious Warning Lodged

Author:

Date: 1959-01-11
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, January 10th: A U.S. warship intruded into the territorial waters off the Matsu and Tungyin areas of Fujian Province from 9:00 AM to 10:00 AM on January 10th. Regarding the military provocation of U.S. imperialism, a spokesperson of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs was instructed to issue the 47th serious warning.

### 美舰侵犯我马祖东引地区领海  我第47次提出严重警告

1959-01-11
第1版()
专栏：

    美舰侵犯我马祖东引地区领海
    我第47次提出严重警告
    新华社10日讯　1月10日九时至十时，美国军舰一艘侵入我国福建省的马祖、东引地区领海。对于美国帝国主义的军事挑衅行为，我外交部发言人奉命提出第四十七次严重警告。
