Title: Czechoslovakia Recognizes Cuban Provisional Government
Author:
Date: 1959-01-15
Page: 5

    Xinhua News Agency, Prague, January 14th: Czechoslovak Premier Široký sent a telegram to Cuban Provisional President Urrutia on the 13th, announcing that the Czechoslovak government recognizes the Cuban Provisional Government.

### 捷承认古巴临时政府

1959-01-15
第5版()
专栏：

    捷承认古巴临时政府
    新华社布拉格14日电　捷克斯洛伐克政府总理西罗基13日致电古巴临时总统乌鲁蒂亚，宣布捷克斯洛伐克政府承认古巴临时政府。
