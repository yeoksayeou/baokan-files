Title: Brotherly Party Delegations from Mongolia, Bulgaria, and Other Countries Set Off to Attend the 21st Congress of the CPSU
Author:
Date: 1959-01-25
Page: 4

Comprehensive report from our newspaper: According to Xinhua News Agency dispatches: Brotherly party delegations from various countries, invited to attend the 21st Congress of the Communist Party of the Soviet Union, have set off for Moscow one after another.

The delegation of the Mongolian People's Revolutionary Party, invited to attend the 21st Congress of the Communist Party of the Soviet Union, was led by Yumjaagiin Tsedenbal, First Secretary of the Central Committee, and left Ulaanbaatar by plane for Moscow on the afternoon of the 23rd. The delegation members were: N. Luvsanravdan, Alternate Member of the Political Bureau of the Central Committee of the Mongolian People's Revolutionary Party; and Damdin, Member of the Central Committee and First Secretary of the Bulgan Provincial Party Committee.

The Bulgarian Communist Party delegation, led by Todor Zhivkov, First Secretary of the Central Committee, left Sofia by special train on the evening of the 23rd for Moscow. The delegation will attend the 21st Congress of the Communist Party of the Soviet Union. The delegation is composed of five people, including Political Bureau members of the Bulgarian Communist Party, Yugov, Bulgaranov, Zankov, and Nikolov, member of the Bulgarian Communist Party Central Committee and First Secretary of the Kolorovgrad Oblast Party Committee.

A delegation from the Central Committee of the Communist Party of Czechoslovakia to attend the 21st Congress of the Communist Party of the Soviet Union, is scheduled to depart for Moscow on the 24th. The delegation consists of Antonín Novotný, First Secretary of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of Czechoslovakia, and Political Bureau members of the Communist Party of Czechoslovakia, Bacílek, Duras and Hendrych.

The delegation of the Italian Communist Party attending the 21st Congress of the Communist Party of the Soviet Union, led by Palmiro Togliatti, General Secretary of the Italian Communist Party, left Rome by train on the evening of the 22nd for Moscow. The delegation members include Pajetta, Lempredi, and Macaluso, members of the Central Committee and secretaries of the Secretariat of the Italian Communist Party.

The Austrian Communist Party will send a delegation to attend the 21st Congress of the Communist Party of the Soviet Union. The delegation will be composed of the following personnel: Johann Koplenig, Chairman of the Austrian Communist Party; Friedl Fürnberg, First Secretary of the Secretariat of the Austrian Communist Party; Theodor Malzpel; Josef Wodraschka.

At the invitation of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of the Soviet Union, a delegation of the Japanese Communist Party, led by Kenji Miyamoto, General Secretary of the Japanese Communist Party, departed from Tokyo on the 23rd to attend the 21st Congress of the Communist Party of the Soviet Union in Moscow. The delegation member is Shoichi Kasuga, member of the Presidium of the Central Committee of the Japanese Communist Party. Accompanying them was Tomio Nishizawa, Deputy Director of the Investigation Department of the Central Committee of the Japanese Communist Party.

### 应邀参加苏共二十一次代表大会  蒙保等国兄弟党代表团启程

1959-01-25
第4版()
专栏：

    应邀参加苏共二十一次代表大会
    蒙保等国兄弟党代表团启程
    本报综合报道　据新华社讯：应邀参加苏联共产党第二十一次代表大会的各国兄弟党代表团纷纷启程前往莫斯科。
    应邀参加苏联共产党第二十一次代表大会的蒙古人民革命党代表团，由中央委员会第一书记泽登巴尔率领，23日下午从乌兰巴托乘飞机去莫斯科。代表团的团员是：蒙古人民革命党中央政治局候补委员热格瓦拉尔、中央委员、布拉干省委第一书记达姆丁。
    由中央委员会第一书记日夫科夫率领的保加利亚共产党代表团，23日晚乘专车离开索非亚前往莫斯科。代表团将参加苏联共产党第二十一次代表大会。代表团由五人组成，其中包括保共中央政治局委员于哥夫，布尔加拉诺夫，赞科夫，保共中央委员、科拉罗夫格勒州党委第一书记尼科洛夫。
    捷克斯洛伐克共产党中央委员会参加苏联共产党第二十一次代表大会的代表团，订于24日启程前往莫斯科。代表团由捷共中央第一书记诺沃提尼和捷共中央政治局委员巴契列克、杜兰斯基和亨德里赫组成。
    意大利共产党出席苏联共产党第二十一次代表大会的代表团在意大利共产党总书记陶里亚蒂率领下在22日晚间乘火车离开罗马前往莫斯科。代表团团员有意共中央委员、书记处书记巴叶塔，朗普雷迪和马卡路索等。
    奥地利共产党将派代表团参加苏联共产党第二十一次代表大会。代表团由以下人员组成：奥地利共产党主席约翰·柯普勒尼希，奥共中央书记处第一书记弗里德尔·费恩伯，特奥多尔·马耳佩尔，约瑟夫·沃德腊兹卡。
    应苏联共产党中央委员会的邀请，由日本共产党总书记宫本显治率领的日本共产党代表团在23日从东京启程，前往莫斯科参加苏联共产党第二十一次代表大会。代表团团员是日本共产党中央委员会干部会委员春日正一。随行的还有代表团随员、日本共产党中央委员会调查部副部长西泽富夫。
