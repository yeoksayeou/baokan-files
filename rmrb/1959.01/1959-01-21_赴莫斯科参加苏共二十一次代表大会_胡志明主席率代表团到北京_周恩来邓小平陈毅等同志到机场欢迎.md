Title: To Moscow to Attend the 21st Congress of the CPSU: President Ho Chi Minh Leads Delegation to Beijing; Comrades Zhou Enlai, Deng Xiaoping, and Chen Yi Welcome at the Airport
Author:
Date: 1959-01-21
Page: 1

    Xinhua News Agency, 20th - At the invitation of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of the Soviet Union, President Ho Chi Minh of the Vietnam Workers' Party led a delegation of the Vietnam Workers' Party to Moscow to attend the 21st Congress of the Communist Party of the Soviet Union, which will begin on January 27th. The delegation left Hanoi by special plane this morning and arrived in Beijing this afternoon. Members of the delegation include: Hoang Van Hoan, member of the Political Bureau of the Central Committee of the Vietnam Workers' Party, and Nguyen Van Kinh, alternate member of the Central Committee and Vietnamese Ambassador to the Soviet Union.

    Welcoming them at the Beijing airport were Zhou Enlai, Vice Chairman of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of China, Deng Xiaoping, General Secretary, Chen Yi, member of the Political Bureau, Yang Shangkun, alternate member of the Secretariat of the Central Committee, Wu Xiuquan, member of the Central Committee, and Luo Guibo, alternate member of the Central Committee. Also present were Balili, Albanian Ambassador to China, Lubsan, Mongolian Ambassador to China, Antonov, Charge d'Affaires ad interim of the Soviet Union to China, Sigeti, Counselor of the Hungarian Embassy in China, and personnel from the Vietnamese Embassy in China.

    President Ho Chi Minh was accompanied to Beijing by Nguyen Khang, Vietnamese Ambassador to China.

    When the delegation left Hanoi, those who saw them off at Gia Lam Airport were Le Duan, Truong Chinh, Pham Van Dong, Vo Nguyen Giap, Le Duc Tho, Nguyen Duy Trinh, and Pham Hung, members of the Political Bureau of the Central Committee of the Vietnam Workers' Party, some members of the Central Committee of the Vietnam Workers' Party, heads of various departments of the Government of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam, leaders of the Vietnam Fatherland Front, Sokolov, Soviet Ambassador to the Democratic Republic of Vietnam, and diplomatic envoys from various countries. Sep Schwab, member of the government delegation of the German Democratic Republic visiting Vietnam and Deputy Minister of Foreign Affairs of the German Democratic Republic, also came to the airport to see them off. ### 赴莫斯科参加苏共二十一次代表大会  胡志明主席率代表团到北京  周恩来邓小平陈毅等同志到机场欢迎

1959-01-21
第1版()
专栏：

    赴莫斯科参加苏共二十一次代表大会
    胡志明主席率代表团到北京
    周恩来邓小平陈毅等同志到机场欢迎
    据新华社20日讯　应苏联共产党中央委员会的邀请，越南劳动党主席胡志明率领越南劳动党代表团去莫斯科参加将在1月27日开始举行的苏联共产党第二十一次代表大会。代表团今天上午乘专机离开河内，下午到达北京。代表团团员有：越南劳动党中央政治局委员黄文欢，中央候补委员、越南驻苏联大使阮文镜。
    到北京机场欢迎的，有中共中央副主席周恩来，总书记邓小平，政治局委员陈毅，中央书记处候补书记杨尚昆，中央委员伍修权，中央候补委员罗贵波。还有阿尔巴尼亚驻中国大使巴利里，蒙古驻中国大使鲁布桑，苏联驻中国临时代办安东诺夫，匈牙利驻中国大使馆参赞西格蒂，以及越南驻中国大使馆人员。
    胡志明主席是由越南驻中国大使阮康陪同到北京的。
    代表团离开河内时，到嘉林机场送行的有，越南劳动党中央政治局委员黎笋、长征、范文同、武元甲、黎德寿、阮维祯、范雄，越南劳动党中央委员会的部分委员，越南民主共和国政府各部门的负责人，越南祖国战线领导人，苏联驻越南民主共和国大使索科洛夫和各国的外交使节。正在越南访问的德意志民主共和国政府代表团团员、德意志民主共和国外交部副部长塞普·施瓦布也到机场欢送。（附图片）
