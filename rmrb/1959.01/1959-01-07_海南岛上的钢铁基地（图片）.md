Title: Steel Base on Hainan Island (Picture)
Author: Yang Qinglang
Date: 1959-01-07
Page: 2

    Hainan Island's steel base is taking shape.
    The Shilu Iron Mine in Guangdong has excellent quality iron ore. Japanese imperialism once carried out predatory exploitation here. During the Kuomintang rule, even some of the mining equipment pieced together by the Japanese devils was stolen and sold by the bureaucrats. In 1955, the Shilu Iron Mine began reconstruction. In August 1958, using indigenous methods, blast furnaces, converters, and coking ovens were built for the first time. Now this steel base is continuing to develop. Photo by Yang Qinglang (Xinhua News Agency)
    A group of 28 cubic meter blast furnaces that are already in production.
    Indigenous coking workshop next to the blast furnace at the Shilu Steel Base.
    No. 2 Red Flag Blast Furnace at the Shilu Steel Base is tapping iron.
    Smelting equipment, firebricks, coal, etc., are continuously transported from the railway lines.

### 海南岛上的钢铁基地（图片）
杨清朗
1959-01-07
第2版()
专栏：

    海南岛上的钢铁基地
    广东海南石碌钢铁基地正在形成。
    石碌铁矿区铁矿质地很好，日本帝国主义曾在这里进行过掠夺性的开采。国民党统治时期，连日本鬼子拼凑起来的一部分开采设备，也被官僚们盗卖了。1955年，石碌铁矿开始重建。1958年8月，土法上马，这里第一次建起了高炉、转炉、炼焦炉等。现在这个钢铁基地正在继续发展。杨清朗摄（新华社稿）
    已经投入生产的部分二十八立方公尺的高炉群。
    石碌钢铁基地高炉旁的土法炼焦车间。
    石碌钢铁基地红旗二号高炉在出铁。
    冶炼设备、耐火砖和煤炭等从铁路线上源源运来。
