Title: The Overthrow of the Cuban Dictatorial Regime: People of Latin America Celebrate in Unison
Author:
Date: 1959-01-04
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, 3rd: With the collapse of the Cuban dictatorial regime, all of Latin America is immersed in revelry.

In Caracas and Maracaibo, the capitals of Venezuela, and in Bogota and Barranquilla, the capitals of Colombia, mass demonstrations and parades were held.

Colombian students, the Legal Committee, and the Committee for the Freedom of Cuba demanded that the Colombian government recognize Dr. Urrutia's revolutionary government and sever diplomatic relations with the dictators Trujillo of Dominica and Stroessner of Paraguay.

In Argentina, many residents of Buenos Aires took to the streets in groups on the evening of the 1st to cheer for the victory of the Cuban patriots. Some of the masses held demonstrations in the city center to support the Cuban people.

Argentine groups, parties, social and political activists have all expressed their support for the Cuban people who have gained freedom and independence in a tenacious and bloody struggle. The Argentine Writers Association, the Democratic Party, and the Argentine Committee for Aid to Cuba have all issued statements supporting the struggle of the Cuban people. Articles published in "La Prensa" and "Democracia" today congratulated the Cuban patriots on their victory. "El Tiempo's" commentary opposed American interference in Cuba's internal affairs, advocating that the Cuban people decide their own destiny and establish a peaceful and democratic government.

In Uruguay, Cuban exiles and Uruguayan university students held a meeting in Montevideo on the afternoon of the 2nd to celebrate the victory of the Cuban people's heroic struggle.

### 古巴独裁政权垮台  拉丁美洲人民同声称庆

1959-01-04
第4版()
专栏：

    古巴独裁政权垮台
    拉丁美洲人民同声称庆
    新华社3日讯　由于古巴独裁政权的垮台，整个拉丁美洲都浸沉在狂欢里。
    在委内瑞拉的首都加拉加斯和马拉开，在哥伦比亚的首都波哥大和巴兰基雅都举行了群众的示威游行。
    哥伦比亚的学生、法律委员会和维护古巴自由委员会要求哥伦比亚政府承认乌鲁蒂亚博士的革命政府，并且断绝同多米尼加特鲁希略和巴拉圭斯特罗斯纳独裁者的外交关系。
    在阿根廷，布宜诺斯艾利斯的许多居民在1日晚上成群结队走到街上，为古巴爱国者的胜利而欢呼，还有一部分群众在城市中心举行了声援古巴人民的示威。
    阿根廷各团体、党派以及社会和政治活动家们，都表示声援在顽强的流血斗争中获得自由和独立的古巴人民。阿根廷作家协会、民主党和阿根廷援助古巴委员会等都发表了支持古巴人民斗争的声明。“新闻报”和“民主报”今天发表的文章对古巴爱国者的胜利表示祝贺。“时报”的评论反对美国干涉古巴内政，主张由古巴人民自己决定自己的命运，建立和平与民主的政府。
    在乌拉圭的古巴流亡者和乌拉圭的大学生们2日下午在蒙得维的亚举行集会，庆祝古巴人民英勇斗争的胜利。
