Title: The 21st Congress of the CPSU Opens; Comrade Khrushchev Delivers Report on the Control Figures for the Development of the Soviet National Economy
Author:
Date: 1959-01-28
Page: 1

    Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, January 27th: The 21st Extraordinary Congress of the Communist Party of the Soviet Union opened at 10:00 AM (Moscow time) today in the Grand Kremlin Palace.

    The conference hall was filled with delegates to the Congress. Participating in the Congress were also many guests—representatives of seventy fraternal communist and workers' parties.

    At 10:00 AM sharp, the leaders of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of the Soviet Union, headed by Comrade Khrushchev, appeared on the rostrum, at which point all those present stood up and cheered. When the heads of the delegations of the communist and workers' parties of various countries appeared on the rostrum, all those present once again stood up and cheered.

    The Congress was opened by N.S. Khrushchev, First Secretary of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of the Soviet Union. Khrushchev announced that 1,269 delegates with voting rights and 106 delegates with speaking rights were attending the Congress.

    At Khrushchev's suggestion, all those present stood in silent tribute to the outstanding activists of the international workers' movement and the international communist movement, Marcel Cachin, Bolesław Bierut, Antonín Zápotocký, Giuseppe Vittorio, Georgi Damyanov, and the outstanding peace fighter Frédéric Joliot-Curie, who passed away after the 20th Congress of the Communist Party of the Soviet Union.

    Khrushchev extended a welcome to the delegations and heads of delegations of the seventy fraternal communist and workers' parties who came to participate in the Congress.

    Subsequently, the Congress elected the Presidium of the Congress, the Secretariat, the Drafting Committee, and the Credentials Committee.

    Kirichenko, who presided over today's meeting, invited N.S. Khrushchev, First Secretary of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of the Soviet Union and Chairman of the Council of Ministers of the Soviet Union, to deliver the report on "Control Figures for the Development of the Soviet National Economy in 1959-1965."

    Khrushchev began his report at 10:30 AM, took two breaks in between, and finished at 7:15 PM, for a total duration of six hours.

    The attendees listened to Khrushchev's report with great attention, and the report was frequently interrupted by warm applause and cheers.

    After finishing his report, Comrade Khrushchev warmly shook hands with the heads of the delegations of the communist and workers' parties of various countries. At this point, the entire audience stood up and applauded for a long time, and the atmosphere at the venue was exceptionally warm. (Attached photo)

### 苏共第二十一次代表大会开幕  赫鲁晓夫同志作发展苏联国民经济控制数字的报告

1959-01-28
第1版()
专栏：

    苏共第二十一次代表大会开幕
    赫鲁晓夫同志作发展苏联国民经济控制数字的报告
    据新华社莫斯科27日电　苏联共产党第二十一次非常代表大会今天上午十时（莫斯科时间）在大克里姆林宫开幕。
    会议大厅中坐满了代表大会的代表。参加大会的还有许多来宾——七十个兄弟共产党和工人党的代表。
    十时整，以赫鲁晓夫同志为首的苏联共产党中央委员会领导人出现在主席台上，这时，全体到会者起立欢呼。当各国共产党和工人党代表团团长出现在主席台上时，全体到会者再度起立欢呼。
    代表大会由苏联共产党中央委员会第一书记尼·谢·赫鲁晓夫主持开幕。赫鲁晓夫宣布，出席这次代表大会的有一千二百六十九名有表决权的代表和一百零六名有发言权的代表。
    在赫鲁晓夫的建议下，全体到会者起立为在苏联共产党第二十次代表大会以后逝世的国际工人运动和国际共产主义运动的杰出活动家马赛尔·加香、波莱斯瓦夫·贝鲁特、安托宁·萨波托斯基、朱塞佩·维多里奥、格奥尔基·达米扬诺夫和杰出的和平战士弗雷德里克·约里奥—居里默哀。
    赫鲁晓夫对前来参加代表大会的七十个兄弟共产党和工人党的代表团和代表团团长表示欢迎。
    随后，代表大会选出了大会主席团、秘书处、编纂委员会和资格审查委员会。
    担任今天大会主席的基里钦科请苏联共产党中央委员会第一书记、苏联部长会议主席尼·谢·赫鲁晓夫作“1959—1965年发展苏联国民经济的控制数字”的报告。
    赫鲁晓夫在十时三十分开始报告，中间曾休息了两次，在下午七时十五分结束，他的报告历时六个小时。
    到会者以极大的注意听取了赫鲁晓夫的报告，报告不时为热烈的掌声和欢呼声所打断。
    赫鲁晓夫同志在结束他的报告后，和各国共产党和工人党代表团团长热烈握手。这时，全场起立，长时间地鼓掌，会场气氛异常热烈。（附图片）
