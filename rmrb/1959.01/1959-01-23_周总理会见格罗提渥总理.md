Title: Premier Zhou Meets with Premier Grotewohl
Author:
Date: 1959-01-23
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, January 22nd: Premier Zhou Enlai met this evening with Premier Otto Grotewohl and his wife, as well as members of the German Democratic Republic government delegation: Deputy Premier and Minister of Foreign Affairs, Chairman of the National Democratic Party Dr. Lothar Bolz, Deputy Minister of Foreign Affairs Sepp Schwab, Deputy Minister of Foreign and Intra-German Trade Gerhard Weiss, Member of the German Democratic Republic Government's Scientific Committee for Peaceful Uses of Atomic Energy, Member of the German Democratic Republic Advisory Committee for Natural Science and Technology Research and Development Professor Manfred von Ardenne, and the German Democratic Republic Ambassador to China Dr. Paul Wandel. Premier Zhou Enlai and Premier Grotewohl and other distinguished guests had a cordial conversation. Present at the meeting were Vice Premier Chen Yi, Bo Yibo, Vice Minister of Foreign Affairs Zeng Yongquan, China's Ambassador to the German Democratic Republic Wang Guoquan, and others. After the meeting, Premier Zhou Enlai hosted a luncheon in honor of the distinguished guests. (Attached picture)

### 周总理会见格罗提渥总理

1959-01-23
第1版()
专栏：

    周总理会见格罗提渥总理
    新华社22日讯　周恩来总理今天晚间会见了奥托·格罗提渥总理和他的夫人及德意志民主共和国政府代表团团员：副总理兼外交部长、国家民主党主席洛塔·博尔茨博士、外交部副部长塞普·施瓦布、对外和东西德贸易部副部长格哈德·魏斯、德意志民主共和国政府和平利用原子能科学委员会委员、德意志民主共和国自然科学技术研究和发展谘议委员会委员曼弗雷德·冯·阿尔登内教授、德意志民主共和国驻中国大使保罗·汪戴尔博士。周恩来总理和格罗提渥总理等贵宾进行了亲切的交谈。会见时在座的，有副总理陈毅、薄一波，外交部副部长曾涌泉，我国驻德意志民主共和国大使王国权等。会见后，周恩来总理设便宴招待贵宾们。（附图片）
