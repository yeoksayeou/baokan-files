Title: Guizhou Province Publishes Over 600,000 Books in Miao and Buyi Languages
Author:
Date: 1959-01-10
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency Report: Guizhou Province published over 600,000 books in the Miao and Buyi languages in the past year, enabling the 2.7 million Miao and Buyi people of Guizhou to read, for the first time in history, textbooks, folk tales, songs, popular science and technology readers on agriculture, and more than ten other kinds of books printed in their own languages.

Since the creation of the Miao and Buyi languages in November 1956, Guizhou Province has started compiling Miao and Buyi textbooks, and in early 1958, it established a national publishing house and began printing books in the two languages.

### 贵州省出版六十多万册苗文、布依文书籍

1959-01-10
第6版()
专栏：

    贵州省出版六十多万册苗文、布依文书籍
    据新华社讯　贵州省在过去一年间用苗文、布依文出版了六十多万册书籍，使贵州二百七十多万苗族、布依族人民在历史上第一次读到用本民族文字印刷的识字课本、民间故事、歌谣、农业科学技术读物等十多种书籍。
    自从1956年11月苗文、布依文诞生后，贵州省就着手编写苗文、布依文课本，并在1958年初成立了民族出版社，开始用两种文字印刷书籍。
