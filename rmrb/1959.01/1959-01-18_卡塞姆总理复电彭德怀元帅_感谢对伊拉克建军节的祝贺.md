Title: Premier Kassem Cables Marshal Peng Dehuai Expressing Gratitude for Congratulations on the Iraqi Army Day
Author:
Date: 1959-01-18
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Baghdad, January 16th - Iraq today published Premier Kassem's reply to the congratulatory telegram from Chinese Defense Minister Marshal Peng Dehuai to General Kassem on the occasion of the Iraqi Republic Armed Forces Day.

In his reply, Kassem expressed his gratitude to Marshal Peng Dehuai on behalf of the Iraqi Armed Forces. He extended his best wishes to the Chinese Defense Minister and wished the Chinese People's Liberation Army and the great Chinese people progress and happiness.

The telegram also stated, "I sincerely hope that all the strength of the people of all countries will be used to stabilize peace in the Middle East and the world. I am very confident that the close friendship between the people of our two countries will grow day by day."

In his congratulatory telegram to Kassem, the Minister of Defense and Commander-in-Chief of the Armed Forces of the Republic of Iraq, on January 5th, Marshal Peng Dehuai said, "The Chinese people and the Chinese People's Liberation Army respectfully wish the Iraqi people and armed forces even more brilliant victories in the struggle against imperialism and in defense of independence, and make important contributions to the cause of defending peace in the Middle East and the world. May the friendship between the people and armed forces of China and Iraq be increasingly consolidated and developed."

### 卡塞姆总理复电彭德怀元帅  感谢对伊拉克建军节的祝贺

1959-01-18
第1版()
专栏：

    卡塞姆总理复电彭德怀元帅
    感谢对伊拉克建军节的祝贺
    新华社巴格达16日电　伊拉克今天发表了卡塞姆总理对中国国防部长彭德怀元帅在伊拉克共和国武装部队建军节给卡塞姆将军的贺电的复电。
    卡塞姆在复电中代表伊拉克武装部队对彭德怀元帅表示感谢。他对中国国防部长表示最好的祝愿，并且祝中国人民解放军和伟大的中国人民进步和幸福。
    电报还说，“我衷心地希望各国人民的全部力量用于稳定中东和全世界的和平。我非常确信，我们两国人民的亲密友谊将会与日俱增。”
    彭德怀部长在1月5日给伊拉克国防部长兼武装部队总司令卡塞姆的祝贺伊拉克共和国武装部队建军节的贺电中说，“中国人民和中国人民解放军敬祝伊拉克人民和武装力量在反对帝国主义和维护独立的斗争中，取得更辉煌的胜利，并对保卫中东和世界和平事业作出重要的贡献。祝中、伊两国人民和武装部队之间的友谊日益巩固和发展。”
