Title: US Steel Production Plunged Last Year: Lowest Record in Nine Years
Author:
Date: 1959-01-18
Page: 4

    US Steel Production Plunged Last Year
    Lowest Record in Nine Years
    Xinhua News Agency, New York, 16th: According to materials released by the American Iron and Steel Institute, US steel production last year was 85,251,363 tons, the lowest record in American steel production since 1949.
    In 1957, US steel production was 112,714,996 tons.
    The highest annual US steel production was in 1955, at 117,036,085 tons.

### 美去年钢产量大降  为九年来最低记录

1959-01-18
第4版()
专栏：

    美去年钢产量大降
    为九年来最低记录
    新华社16日讯　纽约消息：据美国钢铁研究所公布的材料，去年美国钢产量为八千五百二十五万一千三百六十三吨，是1949年以来美国钢生产的最低记录。
    1957年美国钢产量为一亿一千二百七十一万四千九百九十六吨。
    美国钢最高年产量是1955年，为一亿一千七百零三万六千零八十五吨。
