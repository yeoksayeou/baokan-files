Title: Mikoyan Arrives in Washington
Author:
Date: 1959-01-06
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, Washington, January 5th – According to TASS, First Deputy Chairman of the Council of Ministers of the USSR, Mikoyan, arrived in Washington by car on the 4th from New York.

　　On his way to Washington, Mikoyan had breakfast at a Howard Johnson's roadside cafe. He learned about the cafe's work organization, toured the shop's fixtures and equipment, and talked with the service staff.

　　After arriving in Washington, Mikoyan told the many reporters gathered in front of the Soviet Embassy: "I am very happy to be in Washington." He said, "I would like to sincerely wish the residents of Washington and all the American people happiness on behalf of the Soviet people."

　　Mikoyan gave a brief talk at the request of reporters upon arriving in New York.

　　Mikoyan said, "I am very pleased to be visiting the United States once again. I visited the United States more than twenty years ago. Now, seeing the changes that have taken place in your country's life in the last twenty years, meeting old friends, and making new friends will be very interesting to me."

　　Mikoyan said that Khrushchev asked him to convey the best wishes of peace, happiness, and a peaceful life to the American people.

　　Someone asked Mikoyan for some comments on the successful launch of the Soviet cosmic rocket. Mikoyan said, "This once again clearly demonstrates the high achievements of Soviet science and technology and their contribution to the knowledge of all mankind. We are doing everything we can to make these achievements serve peace, not war."

　　In answering questions about the purpose and plans of his visit to the United States, Mikoyan said that he came to visit the Soviet Ambassador Menshikov and to spend part of his vacation here. Mikoyan added that he had no definite plans, and as he had already said, he wanted to meet old friends and make new friends.

　　A reporter asked Mikoyan whether he planned to meet with U.S. President Eisenhower, Mikoyan said, "I would be very happy to meet with him, if the President has time."

### 米高扬到达华盛顿

1959-01-06
第5版()
专栏：

    米高扬到达华盛顿
    新华社5日讯　据塔斯社华盛顿讯：苏联部长会议第一副主席米高扬4日从纽约乘汽车到华盛顿。
    米高扬去华盛顿途中，在霍华德—约翰逊公司的一家路旁咖啡馆里用了早餐。他了解了这家咖啡馆工作的组织方式，参观了店里的装置和设备，并同服务人员谈了话。
    米高扬到达华盛顿后，对聚集在苏联大使馆门前的许多记者说：到了华盛顿，我感到很高兴。他说，我愿意代表苏联人民真诚祝贺华盛顿居民和全体美国人民幸福。
    米高扬在抵达纽约时曾经应记者的请求发表了简短的谈话。
    米高扬说，能够再一次地访问美国，使我感到非常高兴。我是二十多年前到过美国的。现在看到最近二十年来贵国生活中所发生的变化，同老朋友会面，交新朋友，这对我说来将很感兴趣。
    米高扬说，赫鲁晓夫要我向美国人转致平安、幸福和和平生活的最好祝愿。
    有人请米高扬对苏联发射宇宙火箭成功发表一些看法。米高扬说，这再一次明显地证明了苏联科学技术的高度成就和它们对全人类知识的贡献。我们在尽一切努力使这些成就为和平服务，而不是为战争服务。
    在回答关于他这次访美的目的和计划时，米高扬说，他这次是到苏联大使缅希科夫这里来作客的，是来度他的一部分假期的。米高扬补充说，他没有一定的计划，正如他已经说过的，他想会会老朋友和交新的朋友。
    记者问米高扬是否打算同美国总统艾森豪威尔会见，米高扬说，我很高兴同他会见，如果总统有空的话。
