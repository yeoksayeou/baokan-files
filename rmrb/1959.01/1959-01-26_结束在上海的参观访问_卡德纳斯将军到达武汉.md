Title: General Cárdenas Arrives in Wuhan After Concluding Visit to Shanghai
Author:
Date: 1959-01-26
Page: 4

    Xinhua News Agency, Wuhan, January 25th. General Lázaro Cárdenas, former President of Mexico and Vice-Chairman of the World Peace Council, and his entourage, accompanied by Guo Moruo, Chairman of the Chinese People's Committee for World Peace, arrived in Wuhan by special plane at noon today for a visit. Wei Tinghuai, Deputy Mayor of Wuhan, and Li Bogang, Vice-Chairman of the Hubei Provincial and Wuhan Municipal Branches of the Chinese People's Committee for World Peace, welcomed the distinguished guests at the airport.
    In the afternoon, General Cárdenas and his party visited the Wuhan Yangtze River Bridge and the Wuhan Iron and Steel Corporation.
    In the evening, the distinguished guests toured the city and attended a luncheon hosted by Meng Futang, Deputy Governor of Hubei Province.
    Xinhua News Agency, Shanghai, January 25th. General Lázaro Cárdenas, former President of Mexico and Vice-Chairman of the World Peace Council, and his entourage, accompanied by Guo Moruo, Chairman of the Chinese People's Committee for World Peace, left Shanghai by special plane for Wuhan this morning. Jin Zhonghua, Chairman of the Shanghai Branch of the Chinese People's Committee for World Peace, Shu Xincheng, Vice-Chairman of the Shanghai Municipal Committee of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference, and prominent figures from all walks of life, including Ba Jin, Rong Yiren, Zhang Chunqiao, Shen Kefei, Qi Weili, and Wang Yuxian, were at the airport to see them off.

### 结束在上海的参观访问  卡德纳斯将军到达武汉

1959-01-26
第4版()
专栏：

    结束在上海的参观访问
    卡德纳斯将军到达武汉
    新华社武汉25日电　墨西哥前总统、世界和平理事会副主席卡德纳斯将军和他的随行人员，由中国人民保卫世界和平委员会主席郭沫若陪同，今日中午由上海乘专机到武汉访问。武汉市副市长魏廷槐、中国人民保卫世界和平委员会湖北省及武汉市分会副主席李伯刚等到机场欢迎贵宾。
    下午，卡德纳斯将军等一行参观了武汉长江大桥和武汉钢铁公司。
    晚间，贵宾游览了市容，并出席了湖北省副省长孟夫唐设的便宴。
    新华社上海25日电　墨西哥前总统、世界和平理事会副主席拉萨罗·卡德纳斯将军和他的随行人员，今天上午由中国人民保卫世界和平委员会主席郭沫若陪同，乘专机离上海飞往武汉。中国人民保卫世界和平委员会上海市分会主席金仲华、政协上海市委员会副主席舒新城及各界著名人士巴金、荣毅仁、张春桥、沈克非、齐维礼、王聿先等都到机场欢送。
