Title: Soviet Komsomol Members Help National Construction: Obligatory Construction of Over a Hundred Large Enterprises in the Next Seven Years
Author:
Date: 1959-01-26
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, 24th - TASS: In the seven years from 1959 to 1965, the Soviet Lenin Communist Youth League will voluntarily help the country build more than a hundred large enterprises in the metallurgical, chemical, and other industrial sectors, help lay more than 14,000 kilometers of railways and more than 20,000 kilometers of electrified railways. Several cement plants and coal mines will also be built.

This was written by S.P. Pavlov, Secretary of the Central Committee of the Soviet Komsomol, in an article published in "Pravda" on the 23rd.

Pavlov pointed out that the Komsomol organization in Moscow will voluntarily undertake the automation and comprehensive mechanization of twenty-seven enterprises from 1959 to 1965. The youth of Moscow will participate in the construction of 1,060 automatic production lines and 139 automatic workshops and automatic sections. Komsomol members from Ukraine, Kazakhstan and other republics will voluntarily undertake the construction of the largest enterprises.

Pavlov emphasized that Komsomol organizations in various regions will also voluntarily undertake the organization of ten to fifteen experimental demonstration enterprises, comprehensive mechanized enterprises, and automated enterprises in various regions of the Soviet Union.

### 苏联共青团员帮助国家建设  未来七年义务修建一百多大型企业

1959-01-26
第4版()
专栏：

    苏联共青团员帮助国家建设
    未来七年义务修建一百多大型企业
    新华社24日讯　塔斯社莫斯科讯：在1959年—1965年的七年中，苏联列宁共产主义青年团将义务帮助国家建设一百多个大型的冶金、化学和其它工业部门的企业，将帮助铺设一万四千多公里的铁路和二万多公里的电气化铁路。还要建设几个水泥厂和煤矿。
    这是苏联共青团中央委员会书记谢米恰斯内伊23日在“真理报”上发表的一篇文章中写的。
    谢米恰斯内伊指出，莫斯科的共青团组织，在1959—1965年中，将义务承担二十七个企业的自动化和综合机械化的工作。莫斯科的青年将参加建设一千零六十条自动作业线和一百三十九个自动车间和自动工段。乌克兰、哈萨克和其他加盟共和国的共青团员将义务承担最大的企业的建筑工程。
    谢米恰斯内伊强调指出，各地共青团组织还将义务承担在苏联各个地区组织十个到十五个试验—示范企业、综合机械化企业和自动化企业。
