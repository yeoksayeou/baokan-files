Title: Uranium in the Belgian Congo
Author:
Date: 1959-01-27
Page: 5

    Uranium in the Belgian Congo
    The Shinkolobwe uranium mine in Katanga Province of the Belgian Congo is the largest uranium mining area in the capitalist world. The first uranium mining area in the Congo, the Luiswishi mining area, was discovered as early as the beginning of 1913; the Shinkolobwe mining area was discovered in April 1915; and uranium mining began at the end of 1921.
    During World War II and the early post-war period, the Congo's uranium production accounted for about 90% of the total production in the capitalist world. According to a 1952 estimate, the mining volume reached 60% to 80% of the world level. In 1953, uranium production was 4,450 tons. In 1954, the mining volume of uranium ore from the Shinkolobwe mining area alone was more than the total output of 600 small mines in the United States. In recent years, the United States, Canada, South Africa and other places have increased uranium mining, and the proportion of Congo's uranium production has declined to a certain extent.
    The mining of uranium in the Congo is concentrated in the hands of Union Minière du Haut-Katanga, whose shares belong to Belgian, British, and American monopoly capital.
    During World War II, Belgium was occupied by fascist Germany, and most of the Congo's uranium ore was transported to the United States. The first atomic bombs in the United States were made with uranium from the Congo. American, British, and Belgian capitalists launched fierce struggles to seize uranium mines. In 1942, the United States, Britain, and Belgium signed an agreement to distribute the uranium mines mined in the Congo, and the United States took the opportunity to obtain the privilege of purchasing all the uranium ore produced in the Congo. Due to the United States' stepped-up production of atomic weapons, the Congo's uranium ore production increased about thirteen times from 1940 to 1950. In 1950, 1955, and 1956, the United States, Britain, and Belgium continued to wage fierce struggles to plunder the Congo's uranium mines. But the United States has always been the monopolist of uranium in the Belgian Congo. At present, the United States still has the priority to purchase 90% of the Congo's uranium.
                                (Xinhua News Agency)

### 比属刚果的铀

1959-01-27
第5版()
专栏：小资料

    比属刚果的铀
    比属刚果的上卡当加省的申科洛伯维铀矿，是资本主义世界中最大的铀矿区。早在1913年初，在刚果发现了第一个铀矿区路易斯维什矿区；1915年4月又发现了申科洛伯维矿区；1921年底，开始了铀矿的开采工作。
    刚果铀矿产量在第二次世界大战期间和战后初期，曾占资本主义世界总产量的90％左右，据1952年估计，开采量达世界水平的60％至80％。1953年铀产量为四千四百五十吨。1954年时，单只一个申科洛伯维矿区的铀矿砂的开采量就比美国六百个小矿场的总产量还多。近几年来，美国、加拿大和南非等地增加了铀矿的开采量，刚果铀矿产量所占比重有了一定的下降。
    刚果铀矿的开采集中在上卡当加矿业联合公司手中，这家公司的股票分属于比、英、美垄断资本。
    第二次世界大战期间，比利时被法西斯德国占领，刚果绝大部分的铀矿砂便被运往美国。美国第一批原子弹就是用刚果的铀制造的。美、英、比的资本家为了争夺铀矿，展开了激烈的斗争。1942年美、英、比签订分配刚果开采的铀矿的协定，美国趁势取得收购刚果出产的全部铀矿砂的特权。由于美国加紧生产原子武器，从1940年到1950年的十年中，刚果铀矿砂产量增加了十三倍左右。在1950年、1955年和1956年，美、英、比为了掠夺刚果的铀矿继续展开了尖锐的斗争。但是美国始终是比属刚果铀的垄断者。目前，美国仍获有购买刚果铀90％的优先权。
                                （新华社）
