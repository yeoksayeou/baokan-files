Title: Celebrating the New Year of 1959
Author: Wang Zimu
Date: 1959-01-02
Page: 4

    Celebrating the New Year of 1959
    Wang Zimu

    Six hundred million heroic hearts, the universe wide,
    Nine years of construction, defying all hardships.
    Revolution's success entirely relies on the Party,
    A spirit surpassing mountains and rivers, completely removing officials.
    Workers and peasants rise up urgently, leaping forward,
    Fiercely attacking science, emphasizing cutting-edge technology,
    Lofty red aspirations urge on specialized talents,
    Grand spectacles bloom, joy for all the people.

### 庆祝一九五九年元旦
王梓木
1959-01-02
第4版()
专栏：

    庆祝一九五九年元旦
    王梓木六亿雄心宇宙宽，九年缔造藐艰难。功高革命全凭党，气盖山河尽除官。急起工农飞跃进，猛攻科学重尖端，擎天红意催专蕊，大放奇观万众欢。
