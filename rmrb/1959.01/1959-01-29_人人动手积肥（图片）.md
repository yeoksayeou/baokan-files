Title: Everyone Mobilizes to Accumulate Fertilizer (Photo)
Author: Chen Fubei
Date: 1959-01-29
Page: 1

    Everyone Mobilizes to Accumulate Fertilizer
    The students of the Central Primary School of Daliang Commune, Fanshun County, Guangdong Province, also devote themselves to the fertilizer accumulation movement during their spare time. Most of the students in the school actively participate in the work of accumulating and transporting fertilizer. Xinhua News Agency reporter Chen Fubei.
