Title: Ho Chi Minh Receives Grotewohl
Author:
Date: 1959-01-19
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Hanoi, January 18th: Ho Chi Minh, President of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam, received the government delegation of the German Democratic Republic headed by Prime Minister Grotewohl on the afternoon of the 17th.

Present at the reception were Pham Van Dong, Prime Minister of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam; Le Duan, member of the Political Bureau of the Central Committee of the Vietnam Workers' Party; Ton Duc Thang, Chairman of the Standing Committee of the National Assembly of Vietnam; Deputy Prime Ministers Truong Chinh, Vo Nguyen Giap, and Pham Hung; Nguyen Duy Trinh, Director of the State Planning Commission; ministers and deputy ministers of various government departments; Bui Ky and Xuan Thuy, representatives of the Central Committee of the Vietnam Fatherland Front; and Pham Ngoc Thuan, Ambassador of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam to the German Democratic Republic.

The reception took place in a warm and friendly atmosphere.

### 胡志明接见格罗提渥

1959-01-19
第4版()
专栏：

    胡志明接见格罗提渥
    新华社河内18日电　越南民主共和国主席胡志明17日下午接见了以格罗提渥总理为首的德意志民主共和国政府代表团。
    接见时在座的有越南民主共和国总理范文同，越南劳动党中央政治局委员黎笋，越南国会常务委员会委员长孙德胜，副总理长征、武元甲、范雄，国家计划委员会主任阮维祯，政府各部部长和副部长，越南祖国战线中央委员会代表裴杞和春水以及越南民主共和国驻德意志民主共和国大使范玉纯等。
    接见是在热烈、友好的气氛中进行的。
