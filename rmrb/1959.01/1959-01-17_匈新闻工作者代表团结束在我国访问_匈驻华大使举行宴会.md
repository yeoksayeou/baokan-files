Title: Hungarian Journalists' Delegation Concludes Visit to Our Country; Hungarian Ambassador to China Hosts Banquet
Author:
Date: 1959-01-17
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, January 16th. Hungarian Ambassador to China, Nogradi, hosted a banquet on the evening of the 16th to mark the conclusion of the Hungarian journalists' delegation's visit to our country. Among those attending the banquet were Zeng Yongquan, Vice Minister of Foreign Affairs of our country; Zhang Ziyi, Deputy Director of the Propaganda Department of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of China; Zhang Zhixiang, Deputy Director of the Commission for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries; Wu Lengxi, Vice President of the All-China Journalists Association; and members of the press from the capital.

The Hungarian journalists' delegation was invited by the All-China Journalists Association and the Information Department of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs and arrived in our country on December 14th of last year for a visit.

During their visits to various places, the delegation gained an in-depth understanding of our country's industrial and agricultural construction and the situation of the people's communes, and they had extensive contact with people from all walks of life. They successively met with Ke Qingshi, First Secretary of the Shanghai Municipal Committee of the Communist Party of China; Lin Hujia, Secretary of the Zhejiang Provincial Committee; Tao Zhu, First Secretary of the Guangdong Provincial Committee; Xu Daoqi, Secretary of the Hubei Provincial Committee; Wu De, First Secretary of the Jilin Provincial Committee; and Guo Shushen, First Secretary of the Lüda Municipal Committee, and held cordial conversations.

### 匈新闻工作者代表团结束在我国访问  匈驻华大使举行宴会

1959-01-17
第4版()
专栏：

    匈新闻工作者代表团结束在我国访问
    匈驻华大使举行宴会
    新华社16日讯  匈牙利驻华大使诺格拉第16日晚为匈牙利访华新闻工作者代表团结束在我国的访问举行了宴会。出席宴会的有我国外交部副部长曾涌泉、中共中央宣传部副部长张子意、对外文化联络委员会副主任张致祥、中华全国新闻工作者协会副会长吴冷西和首都新闻界人士。
    匈牙利新闻工作者代表团是应中华全国新闻工作者协会和外交部新闻司联合邀请，于去年12月14日来我国访问的。
    代表团在各地参观访问期间，曾深入地了解我国的工农业建设和人民公社的情况，广泛地接触了各界人士。先后会见了中共上海市委第一书记柯庆施、浙江省委书记林乎加、广东省委第一书记陶铸、湖北省委书记许道琦、吉林省委第一书记吴德、旅大市委第一书记郭述申，并进行了亲切的谈话。
