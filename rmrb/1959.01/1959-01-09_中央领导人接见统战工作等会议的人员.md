Title: Central Leaders Receive Personnel from United Front Work and Other Meetings
Author:
Date: 1959-01-09
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, 8th: The United Front Work Department of the CPC Central Committee convened a National United Front Work Conference from December 18, 1958, to January 8, 1959, to discuss issues related to ethnic and religious affairs. Concurrently, the Nationalities Affairs Commission of the People's Republic of China convened a conference of directors of nationalities affairs commissions from various provinces, municipalities, and autonomous regions, and the Religious Affairs Bureau of the State Council convened a conference of directors (heads) of religious affairs bureaus (sections) from various provinces, municipalities, and autonomous regions.
On the afternoon of the 8th, central leaders of the CPC, including Liu Shaoqi, Zhu De, Chen Yun, Deng Xiaoping, Peng Zhen, Tan Zhenlin, Lu Dingyi, Kang Sheng, and other comrades, received all the personnel attending these three meetings.

### 中央领导人接见统战工作等会议的人员

1959-01-09
第1版()
专栏：

    中央领导人接见统战工作等会议的人员
    新华社8日讯　中共中央统一战线工作部于1958年12月18日到1959年1月8日召开了全国统一战线工作会议，讨论了民族工作和宗教工作问题。与此同时，中华人民共和国民族事务委员会召开了各省、市、自治区民族事务委员会主任会议，国务院宗教事务局召开了各省、市、自治区宗教事务局（处）局（处）长会议。
    8日下午，中共中央领导人刘少奇、朱德、陈云、邓小平、彭真、谭震林、陆定一、康生等同志接见了出席这三个会议的全体人员。
