Title: Chinese Agricultural Delegation Arrives in Pyongyang to Attend Korean National Agricultural Cooperative Congress
Author:
Date: 1959-01-06
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, Pyongyang, January 4th: The Chinese agricultural delegation, invited to attend the Korean Agricultural Producers Cooperative Congress and headed by Liao Luyan, Minister of Agriculture, arrived in Pyongyang by special train this afternoon.

The agricultural delegations from Bulgaria, Romania, and Vietnam also arrived in Pyongyang on the same train as the Chinese agricultural delegation.

Korean Minister of Agriculture Han Deon-jong, Vice Minister of Foreign Affairs Li Dong-geon, and the ambassadors of China, Bulgaria, Romania, and Vietnam to Korea were at the station to greet them.

### 参加朝鲜全国农业合作社大会  中国农业代表团到平壤

1959-01-06
第5版()
专栏：

    参加朝鲜全国农业合作社大会
    中国农业代表团到平壤
    新华社平壤4日电　应邀前来参加朝鲜农业生产合作社大会的中国农业代表团，由农业部部长廖鲁言率领，今天下午乘专车到达平壤。
    同中国农业代表团同车到达平壤的还有保加利亚、罗马尼亚、越南三国的农业代表团。
    朝鲜农业相韩典钟、外务省副相李东健和中国、保加利亚、罗马尼亚、越南四国驻朝鲜大使曾到车站迎接。
