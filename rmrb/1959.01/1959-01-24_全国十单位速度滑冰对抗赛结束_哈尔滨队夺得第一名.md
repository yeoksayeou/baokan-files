Title: National Ten-Unit Speed Skating Competition Concludes; Harbin Team Takes First Place
Author:
Date: 1959-01-24
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, Jilin, 22nd. The 1959 National Ten-Unit Speed Skating Competition concluded on the 21st after four days. The Harbin team won first place in both the men's and women's team total scores.

In this competition, the top four finishers in the women's speed skating all-around were all athletes from Harbin. Their rankings are: Jin Fushun, Xu Mingshu, Li Caiying, and Chen Meiyin. Fifth place went to Jin Yingzi from Jilin, and sixth place went to Piao Yujin from Qiqihar.

In the men's four-event speed skating competition held on the 20th and 21st, Piao Dahua from Qiqihar won first place in the all-around. Following him were Zhang Xingcai from Harbin, Li Chenglv from Qiqihar, Zhang Pengrui and Jin Hanshu from Harbin, and Yang Jingqing from Qiqihar.

During this competition, the results of one hundred and ninety-nine male and female athletes reached the standard for graded athletes. Six athletes from the Beijing skating team and figure skaters from Jilin Province, who are currently visiting Jilin, gave performances at the meeting. (Attached pictures)

### 全国十单位速度滑冰对抗赛结束  哈尔滨队夺得第一名

1959-01-24
第6版()
专栏：

    全国十单位速度滑冰对抗赛结束
    哈尔滨队夺得第一名
    新华社吉林22日电　1959年全国十单位速度滑冰对抗赛进行了四天，在21日结束。男子和女子团体总分第一名都被哈尔滨队夺得。
    这次比赛，获得女子速度滑冰全能前四名的都是哈尔滨选手，她们的名次是：金福顺、许明淑、李彩英和陈美银。第五名是吉林金英子，第六名是齐齐哈尔朴玉金。
    20日和21日举行的男子四项速度滑冰比赛，获得全能第一名的是齐齐哈尔的朴达华。在他以后的是哈尔滨的张兴才，齐齐哈尔的李成律，哈尔滨的张鹏瑞、金汉洙和齐齐哈尔的杨景清。
    在这次比赛中，有一百九十九名男女运动员的成绩达到了等级运动员的标准。正在吉林访问的北京滑冰队的六名运动员和吉林省的花样滑冰运动员，都在会上做了表演。（附图片）
