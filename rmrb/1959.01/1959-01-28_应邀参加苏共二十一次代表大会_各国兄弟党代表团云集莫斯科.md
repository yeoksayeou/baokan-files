Title: Fraternal Parties' Delegations Gather in Moscow in Response to Invitation to Attend the 21st Congress of the CPSU
Author:
Date: 1959-01-28
Page: 6
Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, 26th. Fraternal parties' delegations are arriving in Moscow, invited to attend the 21st Congress of the Communist Party of the Soviet Union by the Central Committee of the Communist Party of the Soviet Union.

In addition to the previously reported arrival of the Chinese Communist Party delegation and the Vietnam Workers' Party delegation in Moscow, the following have arrived as of now:

The Korean Workers' Party delegation, led by Kim Il-sung, Chairman of the Central Committee of the Korean Workers' Party and Premier of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea;

The Indonesian Communist Party delegation, led by D.N. Aidit, General Secretary of the Central Committee of the Indonesian Communist Party;

The Communist Party of India delegation, led by Ajoy Ghosh, General Secretary of the National Council of the Communist Party of India;

The Albanian Party of Labor delegation, led by Enver Hoxha, First Secretary of the Central Committee of the Albanian Party of Labor;

The Bulgarian Communist Party delegation, headed by Todor Zhivkov, First Secretary of the Central Committee of the Bulgarian Communist Party;

The Romanian Workers' Party delegation, headed by Gheorghe Gheorghiu-Dej, First Secretary of the Central Committee of the Romanian Workers' Party;

The Communist Party of Great Britain delegation, led by Harry Pollitt, Chairman of the Executive Committee of the Communist Party of Great Britain;

The Labor-Progressive Party of Canada delegation, led by Tim Buck, General Secretary of the Labor-Progressive Party of Canada;

The Czechoslovak Communist Party delegation, led by Antonín Novotný First Secretary of the Central Committe of the Czechoslovak Communist Party and President of the Czechoslovak Republic;

The Mongolian People's Revolutionary Party delegation, led by Yumjaagiin Tsedenbal, First Secretary of the Central Committee of the Mongolian People's Revolutionary Party and Chairman of the Council of Ministers of the Mongolian People's Republic;

The Netherlands Communist Party delegation, consisting of Wolf and de Vos, members of the Central Committee of the Netherlands Communist Party;

The Communist Party of the United States delegation, consisting of James Jackson and Hyman Lumer, members of the Executive Committee of the National Committee of the Communist Party of the United States;

The Communist Party of Israel delegation, led by Samuel Mikunis, General Secretary of the Communist Party of Israel;

The Communist Party of Australia delegation, led by E.F. Hill, member of the Central Political Committee and Secretary of the Secretariat of the Communist Party of Australia;

The Communist Party of Argentina delegation, led by Victorio Codovilla, General Secretary of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of Argentina;

The Communist Party of Uruguay delegation, led by Rodney Arismendi, First Secretary of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of Uruguay;

The Communist Party of Chile delegation, led by Luis Corvalán, General Secretary of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of Chile;

The Communist Party of Ecuador delegation, led by Pedro Saad-Niaim, General Secretary of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of Ecuador;

The Communist Party of Colombia delegation, led by Gilberto Vieira, General Secretary of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of Colombia;

The Italian Communist Party delegation, led by Palmiro Togliatti, General Secretary of the Italian Communist Party;

### 应邀参加苏共二十一次代表大会  各国兄弟党代表团云集莫斯科

1959-01-28
第6版()
专栏：

    应邀参加苏共二十一次代表大会
    各国兄弟党代表团云集莫斯科
    新华社莫斯科26日电　应苏联共产党中央委员会的邀请参加苏联共产党第二十一次代表大会的各国兄弟党代表团正在陆续到达莫斯科。
    除前已报道中国共产党代表团和越南劳动党代表团到达莫斯科外，截至目前到达的还有：
    朝鲜劳动党中央委员会委员长、朝鲜民主主义人民共和国内阁首相金日成率领的朝鲜劳动党代表团；
    印度尼西亚共产党中央委员会总书记艾地率领的印度尼西亚共产党代表团；
    印度共产党全国委员会总书记高士率领的印度共产党代表团；
    阿尔巴尼亚劳动党中央委员会第一书记霍查率领的阿尔巴尼亚劳动党代表团；
    以保加利亚共产党中央委员会第一书记日夫科夫为首的保加利亚共产党代表团；
