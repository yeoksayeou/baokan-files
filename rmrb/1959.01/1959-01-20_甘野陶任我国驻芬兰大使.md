Title: Gan Yetao Appointed as China's Ambassador to Finland
Author:
Date: 1959-01-20
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, January 19th: Chairman of the People's Republic of China, Mao Zedong, has appointed Gan Yetao as the Ambassador Extraordinary and Plenipotentiary of the People's Republic of China to the Republic of Finland.

Chairman of the People's Republic of China, Mao Zedong, has removed Chen Xinren from his position as Ambassador Extraordinary and Plenipotentiary of the People's Republic of China to the Republic of Finland.

### 甘野陶任我国驻芬兰大使

1959-01-20
第1版()
专栏：

    甘野陶任我国驻芬兰大使
    新华社19日讯　中华人民共和国主席毛泽东任命甘野陶为中华人民共和国驻芬兰共和国特命全权大使。
    中华人民共和国主席毛泽东免去陈辛仁的中华人民共和国驻芬兰共和国特命全权大使的职务。
