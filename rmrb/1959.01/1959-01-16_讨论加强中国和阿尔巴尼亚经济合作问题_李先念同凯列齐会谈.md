Title: Discussion on Strengthening Economic Cooperation Between China and Albania: Li Xiannian Holds Talks with Kellezi
Author:
Date: 1959-01-16
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, 15th: Vice Premier Li Xiannian and the head of the Albanian People's Republic government trade delegation, Vice Chairman Abdyl Këllezi, held talks this evening on issues such as further strengthening economic cooperation between China and Albania. The talks were conducted in a friendly atmosphere.

    On the Albanian side, those participating in the talks included the deputy head of the Albanian government trade delegation and Albanian Ambassador to China, Balili, delegation members Doçi, Nishku, and Bugjiri.

    On the Chinese side, those participating in the talks included Vice Minister of Foreign Trade Lin Haiyun, Deputy Director of the State Planning Commission Liu Mingtian, Vice Minister of Foreign Affairs Zeng Yongquan, Chinese Ambassador to Albania Luo Shigao, and other relevant responsible persons.

    After the talks, Vice Premier Li Xiannian hosted a banquet to entertain the distinguished guests.

    Xinhua News Agency, 15th: The Vice Chairman of the Council of Ministers of the Albanian People's Republic, Abdyl Këllezi, and the Albanian government trade delegation he led, returned to Beijing from Wuhan by special plane this afternoon. The distinguished Albanian guests left Beijing on the 8th, and, accompanied by Chinese Ambassador to Albania Luo Shigao and Assistant Minister of Foreign Trade Bai Ren, visited Shanghai, Guangzhou, Kunming, and Wuhan.

### 讨论加强中国和阿尔巴尼亚经济合作问题  李先念同凯列齐会谈

1959-01-16
第1版()
专栏：

    讨论加强中国和阿尔巴尼亚经济合作问题
    李先念同凯列齐会谈
    新华社15日讯　李先念副总理和阿尔巴尼亚人民共和国政府贸易代表团团长阿卜杜勒·凯列齐副主席，今天晚间就进一步加强中国和阿尔巴尼亚两国经济合作等问题进行了会谈。会谈是在友好的气氛中进行的。
    阿尔巴尼亚方面参加会谈的，有阿尔巴尼亚政府贸易代表团副团长、阿尔巴尼亚驻中国大使巴利里，代表团团员道齐、尼施库和布吉里。
    中国方面参加会谈的，有对外贸易部副部长林海云，国家计划委员会副主任刘明天，外交部副部长曾涌泉，我国驻阿尔巴尼亚大使罗士高和其它方面的有关负责人。
    会谈后，李先念副总理设宴招待贵宾们。
    据新华社15日讯　阿尔巴尼亚人民共和国部长会议副主席阿卜杜勒·凯列齐和由他率领的阿尔巴尼亚政府贸易代表团，今天下午乘专机从武汉回到北京。阿尔巴尼亚贵宾们于8日离开北京，他们在我国驻阿尔巴尼亚大使罗士高和对外贸易部部长助理白认陪同下，曾先后参观访问了上海、广州、昆明和武汉等城市。
