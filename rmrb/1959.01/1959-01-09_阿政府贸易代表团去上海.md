Title: Albanian Government Trade Delegation to Shanghai
Author:
Date: 1959-01-09
Page: 4
    Xinhua News Agency, 8th. The Vice-Chairman of the Council of Ministers of the People's Republic of Albania, Abdyl Këllezi, and the Albanian Government Trade Delegation he led, accompanied by the Chinese Ambassador to Albania, Luo Shigao, and the Assistant Minister of Foreign Trade, Bai Ren, left Beijing by special plane this morning to visit Shanghai. After visiting Shanghai, the delegation will also visit Guangzhou, Kunming, and Wuhan.
    Before today, the distinguished Albanian guests had visited cotton textile factories, wool and linen textile factories, canned food factories, hardware and iron factories, paper mills, and cigarette factories in Beijing and Tianjin.
    The delegation was warmly welcomed by the heads and workers of these units during their visits.
    During their stay in Beijing, the distinguished guests also held discussions with the Ministry of Metallurgical Industry and the Ministry of Geology on technical issues related to indigenous iron smelting and mobilizing the masses to explore for minerals.

### 阿政府贸易代表团去上海

1959-01-09
第4版()
专栏：

    阿政府贸易代表团去上海
    新华社8日讯　阿尔巴尼亚人民共和国部长会议副主席阿卜杜勒·凯列齐和由他率领的阿尔巴尼亚政府贸易代表团，在我国驻阿尔巴尼亚大使罗士高和对外贸易部部长助理白认陪同下，今天上午乘专机离开北京，前往上海参观访问。代表团在访问上海以后，还将访问广州、昆明和武汉等城市。
    阿尔巴尼亚贵宾们在今天以前，曾经在北京和天津两地参观访问了棉纺织厂、毛麻纺织厂、罐头食品厂、五金铁工厂、造纸厂和卷烟厂等。
    代表团在访问中受到这些单位的负责人和职工的热烈欢迎。
    贵宾们在北京期间，还同冶金工业部、地质部就关于土法炼铁的技术问题和发动群众探矿等问题，进行了座谈。
