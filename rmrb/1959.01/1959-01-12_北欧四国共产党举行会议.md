Title: Nordic Four Communist Parties Hold Meeting
Author:
Date: 1959-01-12
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, Stockholm, January 11th. TASS reported from Stockholm on the 10th that the Swedish "New Daily" reported that the Communist Parties of Denmark, Finland, Norway, and Sweden held a representative meeting in Stockholm from January 8th to 9th. The meeting discussed the political situation in Europe, strengthening the Nordic people's struggle for peace, the establishment of a Nordic common market plan, and workers' struggles to safeguard their vital interests.

### 北欧四国共产党举行会议

1959-01-12
第5版()
专栏：

    北欧四国共产党举行会议
    新华社11日讯　塔斯社斯德哥尔摩讯：瑞典“新日报”10日报道，丹麦、芬兰、挪威和瑞典的共产党1月8日到9日在斯德哥尔摩举行了代表会议。会议讨论了欧洲政治局势，加强北欧人民争取和平的斗争，建立北欧联合市场计划和工人为保障切身利益进行斗争等问题。
