Title: Get Prepared for Spring Plowing

Author:

Date: 1959-01-26
Page: 6

Editor's Note:

　　If we compare an agricultural production cycle to a battle, then the spring plowing stage is the first battle to lay the foundation for overall victory.

　　To win a complete victory in battle, one must be fully prepared. The same is true on the agricultural front. To achieve an even greater harvest throughout the year, thorough preparation is essential.

　　Now, this battle is about to begin. Agricultural front "commanders," please carefully check how your preparations are going. We should use sufficient "supplies" to welcome the arrival of spring plowing.

### 做好准备工作迎春耕

1959-01-26
第6版()
专栏：编后

    做好准备工作迎春耕
    如果把农业生产的一个周期比做一个战役，春耕阶段的生产，就是奠定全局胜利的第一仗。
    打仗要取得全胜，必须有充分的准备。在农业战线上也是这样，要取得全年更大丰收，没有周密的准备是不可能的。
    现在，这个战役就要开始了，农业战线上的“指战员”们，请你们切实地检查一下，准备工作做得怎么样？我们应该用充足的“粮草”来迎接春耕的到来。
