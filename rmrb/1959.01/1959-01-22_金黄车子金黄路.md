Title: Golden Car, Golden Road
Author:
Date: 1959-01-22
Page: 2

The sun comes out, golden yellow,
A small car runs on the mountain ridge,
Golden car, golden road,
The Yao people's life has greatly changed.

The convoy passes through the mountains, long and long,
Like a long dragon running on the mountains,
Sending away thousands of years of bitterness,
Bringing back thousands of pounds of grain.
        (Guangxi Yao Folk Song)

### 金黄车子金黄路

1959-01-22
第2版()
专栏：

    金黄车子金黄路
    太阳出来金黄黄，
    小车奔跑在山岗，
    金黄车子金黄路，
    瑶家生活大变样。
    车队过山长又长，
    好比长龙跑山上，
    送走千年万代苦，
    载回千斤万担粮。
        （广西瑶族民歌）
