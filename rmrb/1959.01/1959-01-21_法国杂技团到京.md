Title: French Acrobatic Troupe Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1959-01-21
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency, 20th. The 35-member French Paris Acrobatic Troupe, led by Georges Soria, president of the Paris Literary and Art Society, arrived in Beijing by plane from Moscow at noon today. This folk acrobatic troupe, composed of many famous French acrobats, will perform in Beijing, Guangzhou, Shanghai, Tianjin and other places in China for a month and a half.
    The guests were welcomed at the Beijing airport by A Liang, head of the China Acrobatic Troupe, and others.

### 法国杂技团到京

1959-01-21
第4版()
专栏：

    法国杂技团到京
    新华社20日讯　法国巴黎杂技团一行三十五人，由巴黎文学艺术社社长乔治·索利亚率领，在今天中午乘飞机由莫斯科到达北京。这个由法国不少著名杂技演员组成的民间杂技团，将在我国北京、广州、上海、天津等地演出一个半月。
    客人们在北京机场受到了中国杂技团团长阿良等人的欢迎。
