Title: Mongolian Guests Spend New Year in Hangzhou
Author:
Date: 1959-01-02
Page: 3
Xinhua News Agency, Hangzhou, January 1st: The government delegation of the Mongolian People's Republic, headed by Deputy Chairman of the Council of Ministers D. Molomzhamts, arrived in Hangzhou today from Shanghai by special plane to celebrate the New Year with the people of Hangzhou.

The delegation was warmly welcomed at the airport by Wu Xian, Vice Governor of Zhejiang Province and Mayor of Hangzhou, and others.

After a short rest upon arrival in Hangzhou, the distinguished guests took a boat trip to visit the scenic spots of West Lake. In the evening, the Zhejiang Provincial and Hangzhou Municipal People's Committees held a welcoming banquet for the guests.

Xinhua News Agency, Shanghai, December 31st: The Mongolian government delegation, headed by D. Molomzhamts, Deputy Chairman of the Council of Ministers of the Mongolian People's Republic, arrived in Shanghai by special plane from Beijing on the afternoon of the 30th.

Cao Diqiu, Deputy Mayor of Shanghai, and others went to the airport to welcome them.

After arriving in Shanghai, the distinguished guests, accompanied by Deputy Mayor Cao Diqiu, toured the city, visiting the Shanghai Export Commodities Exhibition Hall, the No. 5 loading and unloading area of the Port Authority, and the Revolutionary History Memorial Hall.

That evening, Shanghai Mayor Ke Qingshi held a banquet to welcome the distinguished guests.

### 蒙古贵宾在杭州欢度新年

1959-01-02
第3版()
专栏：

    蒙古贵宾在杭州欢度新年
    新华社杭州1日电　以部长会议副主席丹·莫洛姆扎木茨为首的蒙古人民共和国政府代表团，今天自上海乘专机到达杭州，同杭州市人民一起欢度新年。
    代表团在机场受到浙工省副省长、杭州市市长吴宪等人的热烈欢迎。
    贵宾们到杭州后稍作休息，就乘艇游览西湖名胜。晚间，浙江省和杭州市人民委员会为贵宾们举行了欢迎宴会。
    新华社上海12月31日电　以蒙古人民共和国部长会议副主席丹·莫洛姆扎木茨为首的蒙古政府代表团，30日下午由北京乘专机到达上海。
    前往机场欢迎的有上海市副市长曹荻秋等人。
    贵宾们到达上海后，在曹荻秋副市长陪同下游览了市区，参观了上海出口商品陈列厅、港务局第五装卸区和革命历史纪念馆等处。
    当天晚上，上海市市长柯庆施举行宴会，欢迎贵宾。
