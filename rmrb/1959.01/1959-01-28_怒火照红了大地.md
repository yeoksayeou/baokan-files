Title: Anger Lights Up the Earth
Author: Gu Gong
Date: 1959-01-28
Page: 8

    Anger Lights Up the Earth
    Gu Gong
    We cannot forget,
    The Cuban poet José Martí,
    We love "Versos Sencillos" (note),
    Like the most precious gold and jade.
    These verses from a hundred years ago,
    Are still deeply imprinted in our hearts;
    It curses the ambitious and greedy gaze,
    It praises the land of the Caribbean coast…
    For the honor of the motherland,
    He took up arms with the blacks,
    To fight a decisive battle with the colonialists,
    He dripped his blood into the red soil.
    Today, Cuba is ablaze again with torches,
    José Martí rises again in excitement,
    He stands in the midst of millions of marchers,
    He too is listening to Castro's words.
    The accusation of the peasant woman from Las Villas,
    Moved the poet to tears;
    Her husband had his hands and feet chopped off by the enemy,
    And stopped breathing in the burning gasoline…
    No, we cannot let the claws of the United States run rampant again,
    No, we cannot let Batista breathe again;
    The raging anger against imperialism,
    Lights up the poems,
    And also lights up the sky and the earth…
    Note: "Versos Sencillos" is a collection of poems published by José Martí in 1891.

### 怒火照红了大地
顾工
1959-01-28
第8版()
专栏：

    怒火照红了大地
    顾工
    我们不能忘记，
    古巴的诗人何塞·马蒂，
    我们喜爱“普通的诗篇”（注），
    像喜爱最珍贵的金玉。
    这一百年前的诗句，
    还深深地印进我们心底；
    它诅咒野心家贪婪的视线，
    它赞美加勒比海岸的土地……
    为了祖国的荣誉，
    他和黑人一起拿起了武器，
    和殖民主义者进行决战，
    他把鲜血滴进红色的土壤里。
    今天，古巴又燃起火炬，
    何塞·马蒂又激奋起立，
    他排在百万游行群众当中，
    他也在倾听卡斯特罗的话语。
    拉瓦萨农妇的控诉，
    感动得诗人流下泪滴；
    她的丈夫被敌人砍掉了手脚，
    又在汽油的燃烧中停止呼吸……
    不，不能再叫美国的爪牙猖狂，
    不，不能再叫巴蒂斯塔喘气；
    反对帝国主义的熊熊怒火，
    照红了诗篇，
    也照红了天空和大地……
    注：“普通的诗篇”是何塞·马蒂在一八九一年出版的诗集。
