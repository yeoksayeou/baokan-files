Title: Space Rocket Demonstrates Humanity's Unlimited Capabilities, Socialist Camp Advances From Victory to Victory, Vice Chairman Soong Ching Ling Issues Statement Congratulating the Glorious Achievements of the Soviet Union
Author:
Date: 1959-01-07
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Shanghai, January 6th: Vice Chairman Soong Ching Ling issued a written statement congratulating the Soviet Union on the successful launch of a space rocket, the full text of the statement is as follows:

    The Soviet Union's announcement of the launch of a space rocket that flew past the moon and entered orbit around the sun is an outstanding gift to science and the people of all countries as we enter 1959.

    This is a seemingly incredible achievement, accomplished by Soviet scientists under the support and encouragement of the great Communist Party of the Soviet Union and the entire Soviet people. This once again vividly proves the superiority of socialist society. Socialist society is the only society built by the broad masses of working people and entirely for their welfare and interests.

    The Soviet space rocket, as well as the previous three artificial satellites, are unmatched by the capitalist world, despite all their propaganda. The Soviet space rocket will remain in space as a symbol of humanity's limitless ability to solve problems related to conquering nature and establishing a socialist system throughout the earth. The socialist system shows all countries and all progressive people the prospect of lasting peace, happiness, and prosperity.

    The Chinese people are very happy to hear this historic news and extend our warmest congratulations to our Soviet brothers and sisters. For us, the space rocket is not only the first artificial planet to orbit the sun, but also clear evidence that the Soviet Union's outstanding Seven-Year Plan will definitely be completed and overfulfilled, and that the great socialist camp headed by the Soviet Union will surely grow stronger and stronger and advance from victory to victory. This is certainly encouraging news for all mankind.

### 宇宙火箭显示了人类无限能力  社会主义阵营从胜利走向胜利  宋庆龄副委员长发表谈话祝贺苏联光辉成就

1959-01-07
第1版()
专栏：

    宇宙火箭显示了人类无限能力
    社会主义阵营从胜利走向胜利
    宋庆龄副委员长发表谈话祝贺苏联光辉成就
    新华社上海6日电　宋庆龄副委员长发表书面谈话，祝贺苏联成功地发射宇宙火箭，谈话全文如下：
    苏联宣布发射宇宙火箭飞过月球、进入围绕太阳轨道的消息，是在我们进入1959年时对科学和全世界各国人民的出色礼物。
    这是一个看起来好像不可思议的成就，这个成就是苏联科学家在伟大的苏联共产党和苏联全体人民的支持和鼓舞下取得的。这再一次生动地证明了社会主义社会的优越性。社会主义社会是由广大的劳动人民所缔造，并且完全是为了他们的福利和利益服务的唯一的社会。
    苏联宇宙火箭以及以前的三个人造卫星，都是资本主义世界无法匹敌的，尽管它们进行了种种宣传，苏联宇宙火箭将一直留在宇宙中，作为显示人类解决有关征服自然的问题并且在整个地球上建立社会主义制度具有无限能力的象征。社会主义制度向一切国家和一切进步人士展示了持久和平、幸福和繁荣的前景。
  中国人民非常高兴地听到这个具有历史意义的消息，并且对我们的苏联兄弟姊妹们表示最热烈的祝贺。对我们来说，宇宙火箭不仅是第一个围绕太阳运行的人造行星，它还是苏联的卓越的七年计划一定会完成而且超额完成、以苏联为首的伟大的社会主义阵营一定会日益壮大和从胜利走向胜利的明证。这肯定是全人类欢欣鼓舞的消息。
