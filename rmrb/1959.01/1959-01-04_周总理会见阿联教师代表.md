Title: Premier Zhou Meets with Teachers' Representatives from the United Arab Republic

Author: 
Date: 1959-01-04
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, January 3rd: Premier Zhou Enlai met this afternoon with the delegation of the Teachers' Union of the United Arab Republic, headed by Ahmed Zaki Muhammad, and delegation members Abdul Saeed and Ahmed Hosseini. Premier Zhou had a cordial conversation with the guests for an hour.

Present at the meeting were Ragab, Ambassador of the United Arab Republic to China, and Badran, Counselor of the Embassy.

Also present at the meeting were Ding Xilin, Director of the Commission for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries, Liu Aifeng, Vice Minister of Education, and Fang Ming, Vice Chairman of the National Committee of the National Education Union of China.

### 周总理会见阿联教师代表

1959-01-04
第4版()
专栏：

    周总理会见阿联教师代表
    新华社3日讯　周恩来总理今天下午会见了阿拉伯联合共和国教师工会代表团团长艾哈迈德·扎基·穆罕默德和代表团团员：阿卜杜尔·赛义德，阿赫默·侯赛尼。周总理同客人们亲切地交谈了一个小时。
    会见的时候，阿拉伯联合共和国驻中国大使拉加卜和大使馆参赞巴德朗也在座。
    会见时在座的，还有对外文化联络委员会主任丁西林，教育部副部长刘皑风，中国教育工会全国委员会副主席方明。
