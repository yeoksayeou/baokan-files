Title: Mikoyan Talks with Dulles; Agreement Reached for Meeting with U.S. President
Author:
Date: 1959-01-07
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, Washington, January 5th: TASS reports from Washington on the 5th that First Deputy Chairman of the Council of Ministers of the Soviet Union, Mikoyan, held a lengthy discussion with U.S. Secretary of State Dulles at the U.S. Department of State on the afternoon of the 5th. Soviet Ambassador to the United States, Menshikov, was present at the talks. On the American side, Assistant Secretary of State for European Affairs, Merchant, Head of the State Department's Eastern European Section, Freers, and U.S. Ambassador to the Soviet Union, Thompson, were present. The talks lasted for one and a half hours.

After the talks, Mikoyan, in response to questions from reporters, said: "We spoke generally and preliminarily about certain major issues of common concern to both countries, including the Berlin issue, the German issue, disarmament, and trade. We have agreed to hold a more detailed discussion after my travels around the United States and return to Washington."

In response to a question about whether he would meet with Eisenhower, Mikoyan said that it had been agreed that he would meet with the U.S. President after his travels around the United States and return to Washington.

### 米高扬同杜勒斯谈话  已商定将同美国总统会晤

1959-01-07
第5版()
专栏：

    米高扬同杜勒斯谈话
    已商定将同美国总统会晤
    新华社6日讯　据塔斯社华盛顿5日讯：苏联部长会议第一副主席米高扬5日下午在美国国务院同美国国务卿杜勒斯进行了长时间的谈话。谈话时在座的有苏联驻美国大使缅希科夫。美国方面在座的有欧洲事务助理国务卿麦钱特、国务院东欧科科长弗里尔斯和美国驻苏联大使汤普森。谈话进行了一个半小时。
    谈话以后，米高扬在回答记者们的问题时说：“我们泛泛地、初步地谈到了两国共同关心的某些主要问题，包括柏林问题、德国问题、裁军问题和贸易问题。我们已经商定，在我游历美国各地返回华盛顿后，再举行一次更详细的谈话”。
    米高扬在回答他是否将同艾森豪威尔会晤的问题时说，已经商定，他将在游历美国各地返回华盛顿后同美国总统会晤。
