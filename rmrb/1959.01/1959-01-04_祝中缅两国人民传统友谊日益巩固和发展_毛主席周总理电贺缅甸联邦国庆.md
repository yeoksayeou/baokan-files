Title: May the Traditional Friendship Between the Chinese and Burmese Peoples Be Further Consolidated and Developed: Chairman Mao and Premier Zhou Send Telegrams to Congratulate Burma's National Day
Author:
Date: 1959-01-04
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, 3rd. Chairman Mao Zedong today sent a telegram to U Win Maung, President of the Union of Burma, to congratulate Burma's National Day. The telegram reads as follows:
His Excellency U Win Maung, President of the Union of Burma:
	On the occasion of your country's National Day, I would like to extend my warm congratulations to Your Excellency and the people of Burma on behalf of the state and the entire Chinese people, and in my own name. I wish the Union of Burma prosperity and strength, and may the traditional friendship between the Chinese and Burmese peoples be further consolidated and developed. I wish Your Excellency good health.
			Chairman of the People's Republic of China, Mao Zedong
						January 3, 1959

Xinhua News Agency, 3rd.
	Premier Zhou Enlai today sent a telegram to General Ne Win, Prime Minister of the Union of Burma, to congratulate Burma's National Day. The telegram reads as follows:
His Excellency General Ne Win, Prime Minister of the Union of Burma:
	On the happy occasion of the eleventh anniversary of the independence of the Union of Burma, please accept my sincere congratulations.
	I sincerely hope that the friendship between the Chinese and Burmese peoples based on the Five Principles of Peaceful Coexistence will be further consolidated and developed, and that China and Burma will jointly make new contributions to maintaining peace in Asia and the world.
						Premier of the State Council of the People's Republic of China, Zhou Enlai
								Beijing, January 3, 1959

### 祝中缅两国人民传统友谊日益巩固和发展  毛主席周总理电贺缅甸联邦国庆

1959-01-04
第1版()
专栏：

    祝中缅两国人民传统友谊日益巩固和发展
    毛主席周总理电贺缅甸联邦国庆
    新华社3日讯  毛泽东主席今天致电缅甸联邦总统吴温貌，祝贺缅甸联邦国庆。电文如下：缅甸联邦总统吴温貌阁下：
    在贵国国庆的时候，我谨代表国家和全体中国人民，并且以我个人的名义向阁下和缅甸人民表示热烈的祝贺。祝缅甸联邦繁荣富强，祝中缅两国人民的传统友谊日益巩固和发展，祝阁下身体健康。
                              中华人民共和国主席　毛泽东
                                            1959年1月3日
    新华社3日讯
  周恩来总理今天致电缅甸联邦总理奈温将军，祝贺缅甸联邦国庆。电文如下：缅甸联邦总理奈温将军阁下：
    欣逢缅甸联邦独立十一周年，请接受我热诚的祝贺。
    我衷心地希望，中缅两国人民以和平共处五项原则为基础的友谊日益巩固和发展，中缅两国对维护亚洲和世界的和平共同作出新的贡献。　中华人民共和国国务院总理　周恩来
                                    1959年1月3日于北京
