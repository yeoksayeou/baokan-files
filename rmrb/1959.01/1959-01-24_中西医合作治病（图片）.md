Date: 1959-01-24
Page: 6

Integrated Traditional Chinese and Western Medicine for Treatment

    (1) Dai Hongbo (right), a professor of Traditional Chinese Medicine at the Lanzhou General Hospital of the PLA, and Ge Baofeng (left), a Western medicine doctor, are studying the combined treatment of appendicitis and war-related bone fractures using both Traditional Chinese and Western medicine. Xinhua News Agency reporter Shi Keqin, Zhang Peiyuan.
    (2) Cao Jixun (right), a Traditional Chinese Medicine doctor, and Luo Yutian (middle), a Western medicine doctor, from the Affiliated Hospital of Chengdu College of Traditional Chinese Medicine, are diagnosing and treating a patient with esophageal stricture.
                      Xinhua News Agency reporter Chen Jie, Sun Zhongjing.
    (3) Doctor Yu Dongchuan, who has practiced Traditional Chinese Medicine for more than 50 years, is sharing his experience with Western medicine doctors. Xinhua News Agency reporter Wang Xudong.
    (4) The Fourth Hospital of Xi'an has achieved initial success in using electroacupuncture to replace drug anesthesia based on the principles of acupuncture. The picture shows a tooth extraction operation being performed in the Department of Stomatology using acupuncture instead of drug anesthesia. Xinhua News Agency reporter He Aiqun.
    (5) The Department of Physiology at Shanghai First Medical College is using medical instruments to study the physiological changes in breathing and skin activity during Qigong practice. This is an electroencephalogram experiment. Xinhua News Agency reporter Chen Juanmei.

### 中西医合作治病（图片）

1959-01-24

专栏：

    中西医合作治病
    （1）兰州陆军总医院的中医教授戴洪博（右）和西医葛宝丰（左）在一起研究阑尾炎和战伤骨折的中西医联合治疗问题。　新华社记者　史克勤、张培元摄
    （2）成都中医学院附属医院中医曹吉勋（右），西医罗禹田（中）正在给食道狭窄患者诊治。
                      新华社记者　陈捷、孙忠靖摄
    （3）从事五十多年中医的于东川大夫向西医介绍经验。　新华社记者　王旭东摄
    （4）西安市第四医院根据针灸原理初步研究成功用电针炙代替药物麻醉。图为口腔科用针灸代替药物麻醉后进行拨牙手术。　新华社记者　何爱群摄
    （5）上海第一医学院生理教研组正在用医学仪器来研究练气功时的呼吸和皮肤活动的生理变化。这是在做脑电波试验。　新华社记者　陈娟美摄
