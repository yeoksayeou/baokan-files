Title: Kassem Receives Chinese Trade Delegation
Author:
Date: 1959-01-07
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Baghdad, January 5th: Iraqi Republic Prime Minister Kassem received the Chinese trade delegation led by Lu Xuzhang, Deputy Minister of Foreign Trade, this morning. Guo Dakai, chargé d'affaires ad interim of the Chinese Embassy, was present at the meeting.

In his conversation, Prime Minister Kassem asked Lu Xuzhang to convey his best wishes and respect to the Chinese government and people. He said that the relationship between Iraq and China has a long history of thousands of years. The Iraqi people deeply love China. He expressed that the Iraqi people are grateful for China's help. He said: "Liberated people do need the help of liberated people."

Prime Minister Kassem said that Iraq had been under imperialist rule for hundreds of years, "but now we are awakened. We believe this is the last time we have driven out the imperialists, and they can never come back."

Lu Xuzhang, on behalf of the Chinese delegation, thanked the Iraqi government and people for their warm hospitality to the delegation. He said that the Chinese people are very happy to see Iraq's victory. Lu Xuzhang said: "In the struggle against the imperialists, the Iraqi people and the Chinese people are advancing hand in hand. We believe that we will overcome all difficulties, and we will achieve victory."

The meeting lasted for three quarters of an hour and took place in a friendly and sincere atmosphere.

### 卡塞姆接见我贸易代表团

1959-01-07
第4版()
专栏：

    卡塞姆接见我贸易代表团
    新华社巴格达5日电　伊拉克共和国总理卡塞姆今天上午接见了由对外贸易部副部长卢绪章率领的中国贸易代表团。接见时在座的有中国大使馆临时代办郭达凯。
    卡塞姆总理在谈话中请卢绪章向中国政府和人民转达他的最好的祝愿和敬意。他说，伊拉克和中国的关系有着几千年的悠久历史。伊拉克人非常热爱中国。他表示伊拉克人民感谢中国的帮助。他说：“解放了的人民的确需要解放了的人民的帮助。”
    卡塞姆总理说，伊拉克几百年来长期处于帝国主义的统治之下，“但是现在我们觉醒了。我们相信这是最后一次驱逐帝国主义者了，他们再也不能回来了。”
    卢绪章代表中国代表团感谢伊拉克政府和人民对代表团的殷勤招待。他说，中国人民非常高兴看到伊拉克的胜利。卢绪章说：“在反对帝国主义者的斗争中，伊拉克人民和中国人民携手并进。我们相信我们将克服一切困难，我们将取得胜利。”
    这次接见历时三刻钟，是在友好和诚挚的气氛中进行的。
