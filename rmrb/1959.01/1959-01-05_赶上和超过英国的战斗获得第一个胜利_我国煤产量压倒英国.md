Title: Winning the First Victory in the Battle to Catch Up With and Surpass Britain: China's Coal Production Overwhelms Britain

Author: 
Date: 1959-01-05
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, 4th: In 1958, China's coal production had already far surpassed that of Britain.

According to statistics from the Ministry of Coal Industry as of 24:00 on December 31, 1958, China produced a total of 270.2 million tons of raw coal this year, an increase of 1.07 times compared to 1957. Britain's raw coal production in 1957 was over 223 million tons. The British Ministry of Power announced that its production in 1958 for 52 weeks (the whole year has 52 weeks and one day) totaled 215.78 million tons, a decrease of 3.5% compared to the same period in 1957. Now, the capitalist country with coal production just ahead of China is the United States. According to UPI reports, as of December 20, the US coal production in 1958 was 387 million tons. According to the targets put forward by the Sixth Plenary Session of the Eighth Central Committee of the Communist Party of China, China's coal production will reach 380 million tons in 1959.

From the founding of New China to the end of the first Five-Year Plan, coal production increased from 30.98 million tons in 1949 to 130.5 million tons in 1957, jumping from the tenth position in the world to the fifth, with an average annual growth rate of 19%. This was already a rapid speed, but in 1958, it more than doubled. Compared with some capitalist countries, it took Britain thirty-seven years to increase from 129 million tons in 1873 to 268.6 million tons in 1910 (Britain's highest annual output reached 292 million tons, and began to decline after 1913); even the faster-developing United States took fourteen years to increase from 134.9 million tons in 1888 to 273.6 million tons in 1902. ### 赶上和超过英国的战斗获得第一个胜利  我国煤产量压倒英国

1959-01-05
第1版()
专栏：

    赶上和超过英国的战斗获得第一个胜利
    我国煤产量压倒英国
    据新华社4日讯　1958年，我国煤炭产量已经远远超过英国。
    据煤炭工业部截至1958年12月31日二十四时的统计，这一年我国共计生产了二亿七千零二十万吨原煤，比1957年增长一点零七倍。英国1957年的原煤产量是二亿二千三百多万吨，1958年的产量，据英国动力部宣布，五十二周（全年共五十二周又一天）共二亿一千五百七十八万吨，比1957年同期减产3.5％。现在，煤产量紧挨在我国前面的资本主义国家就是美国了。据合众国际社报道，到12月20日止，美国1958年的煤产量是三亿八千七百万吨。根据中共八届六中全会提出的指标，我国1959年煤产量将达三亿八千万吨。
    新中国从成立以后到第一个五年计划结束，煤炭产量从1949年的三千零九十八万吨上升到1957年的一亿三千零五十万吨，从原来世界第十位跃居到第五位，每年平均增长速度为19％。这已经是很快的速度了，但1958年却增长了一倍多。如果与一些资本主义国家比较，英国从1873年的一亿二千九百万吨提高到1910年的二亿六千八百六十万吨共计用了三十七年（英国最高年产量曾达二亿九千二百万吨，1913年后开始下降）；即使发展速度较快的美国，从1888年的一亿三千四百九十万吨上升到1902年的二亿七千三百六十万吨，也整整走了十四年。（详细报道见今日第五版）
