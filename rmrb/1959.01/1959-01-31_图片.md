    Guangxi Zhuang Autonomous Region, Binyang County, combined fertilizer accumulation with the prevention and treatment of schistosomiasis. People of all ethnic groups in Binyang County uprooted weeds from wasteland and eliminated the intermediate host of schistosomiasis - Oncomelania hupensis snails - by burning and burying them. Xinhua News Agency reporter He Guozheng photo
