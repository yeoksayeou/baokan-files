Title: Standing With You
Author: Liu Lanshan
Date: 1959-01-29
Page: 8

Standing With You
Liu Lanshan

On the surging Congo River,
The angry Congolese are as vast as the river,
Relentlessly冲ing towards the Belgian colonial barracks!
You have just emerged from the darkest night,
Dawn has already climbed the mountain ahead;
You are waving the banner of national independence,
Holding knives and guns,
To drive the colonial bandits far away overseas!
You hear from the north,
The good news of the Algerian National Liberation Army comes from the Atlas Mountains;
You hear from the south,
People from the Orient are urgently blowing anti-imperialist bugles in the streets of Havana!
African diamonds,
Enthusiastic Congolese brothers,
In this country leaping towards paradise,
We are angrily denouncing the Belgian colonialists,
Listening for the good news that final victory belongs to you!
Sparks on the equator,
Congolese brothers,
Our six hundred million people stand with you;
Every time you march, attack, and charge,
We stand firmly in your combat ranks! (Attached picture)

### 和你们站在一起
刘岚山
1959-01-29
第8版()
专栏：

    和你们站在一起
    刘岚山
    在汹涌奔腾的刚果河上，
    愤怒的刚果人像河水一样浩浩荡荡，
    滚滚不尽地冲向比利时殖民军营房！
    你们刚从最黑的夜里走来，
    黎明已经爬上前面的山崖；
    你们打着民族独立大旗，
    拿着刀枪，
    要把殖民强盗远远撵出海外！
    你们听见北方，
    阿特拉斯山传来阿尔及利亚民族解放军的捷报；
    你们听见南方，
    从奥连特来的人们正在哈瓦那街头急吹反帝的军号！
    非洲的钻石，
    热情的刚果弟兄，
    在这向乐园跃进着的国度中，
    我们正在怒斥比利时殖民者，
    倾听最后胜利属于你们的喜讯！
    赤道上的火花，
    刚果兄弟，
    我们六亿人民和你们站在一起；
    你们每一次游行、出击、冲锋，
    我们都紧紧站在你们的战斗队里！（附图片）
