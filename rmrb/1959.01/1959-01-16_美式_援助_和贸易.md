Title: American-style "Aid" and Trade
Author: Chi Bei'ou
Date: 1959-01-16
Page: 5

This is a Colombian cartoon. On the left, "American imperialism robs Colombia of coffee, oil, and platinum," and on the right, "American imperialism sells coffee, oil, and platinum back to Colombia." The cartoon exposes the plunder of Latin American countries by the United States. (Xinhua News Agency稿) Hello, friend, I truly sympathize with your poverty and backwardness. Please accept my sincere aid to help you grow coffee, dig for platinum, and extract oil... What is yours is also mine, and what is mine I can naturally take away. Hello, friend, I have everything you need. American oil is rich, platinum is pure, and American-brand coffee is the most delicious... The price is a bit high, but it's not expensive, and our trade is the fairest.

		Chi Bei'ou Poem:

### 美式“援助”和贸易
池北偶
1959-01-16
第5版()
专栏：

    美式“援助”和贸易
    这是一幅哥伦比亚的漫画。左边是“美帝国主义从哥伦比亚抢走了咖啡、石油和白金”，右边是“美帝国主义又将咖啡、石油和白金卖给哥伦比亚”。漫画揭露了美国对拉丁美洲国家的掠夺。　（新华社稿）哈罗，朋友，我真同情你的贫穷和落后。请接受我真诚的援助，帮你种咖啡，掘白金，采石
  油……你的也就是我的，我的我自然可以拿走。哈罗，朋友，你需要什么我这里全有。美国的石油浓郁白金纯，美国牌子的咖啡最可口……价钱高点不算贵，咱们的买卖最公道。
      池北偶配诗
