Title: German People's Army Song and Dance Troupe Visits Anshan; Performances in Shenyang Receive Warm Welcome
Author:
Date: 1959-01-16
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Shenyang, January 15th: The German Democratic Republic's People's Army Erich Weinert Song and Dance Troupe concluded its visit and performances in Shenyang and departed for Anshan and Lüda this morning.

The song and dance troupe arrived in Shenyang on the 9th. Their performances were warmly welcomed by the Shenyang audience. Every brilliant performance repeatedly garnered enthusiastic applause.

During their stay in Shenyang, Colonel Brandel, the head of the song and dance troupe, handed over a letter from the Bergmann-Borsig Turbine Factory in Berlin to the representatives of the Liaoning Power Plant, which is currently under construction. The letter warmly wished the Liaoning Power Plant a smooth construction process. The Liaoning Power Plant had ordered two turbines from this factory. In a return letter entrusted to the guests for the factory, the Liaoning Power Plant expressed heartfelt gratitude to the worker comrades on the front lines of peace in the West, and presented gifts such as jet carvings.

During their stay in Shenyang, the song and dance troupe also visited the Northeast Industrial Exhibition Hall, the Transformer Factory, and the Cable Factory, toured the city of Shenyang and its historical sites. On the eve of their departure, they also held a get-together with the Shenyang garrison troops and literary and artistic workers.

### 德人民军歌舞团赴鞍山  在沈阳演出受到热烈欢迎

1959-01-16
第4版()
专栏：

    德人民军歌舞团赴鞍山
    在沈阳演出受到热烈欢迎
    新华社沈阳15日电　德意志民主共和国人民军埃里希·魏纳特歌舞团结束了在沈阳的访问演出，今天上午前往鞍山、旅大。
    歌舞团是9日到达沈阳的。他们的演出，受到了沈阳市观众热情欢迎。每一场精采演出都一再地博得热烈的掌声。
    在沈阳期间，歌舞团团长布兰德尔上校曾将柏林市贝尔格曼·波尔西格汽轮机制造厂写给正在兴建中的辽宁发电厂的信，转给了辽宁发电厂的代表。信中热情地预祝辽宁发电厂顺利建成。辽宁发电厂曾向这个工厂订制两台汽轮机。辽宁发电厂托客人带给这个工厂的回信中，对处在西方和平前哨的工人弟兄的深情厚谊表示衷心的感谢，并赠送了煤精雕刻等礼物。
    歌舞团在沈阳期间还参观了东北工业陈列馆、变压器厂、电缆厂，游览了沈阳市容和名胜古迹；临行前夕，并与沈阳驻军部队和文艺工作者进行了联欢。
