Title: Heroes are Many on the Gobi Desert
Author: Jiang Hui
Date: 1959-01-04
Page: 6

Heroes are many on the Gobi Desert,
Developing treasures and exploring oil rivers;
The song shakes Kunlun and sings of Qinghai,
Green shade covers the vast desert.

### 戈壁滩上英雄多
姜辉

    戈壁滩上英雄多
