Title: German Guests Visit Our Agricultural Exhibition

Author: 
Date: 1959-01-27
Page: 4

    Xinhua News Agency, January 26th. Guests from the Government Delegation of the German Democratic Republic, accompanied by Vice Premier Peng Dehuai and Minister of Agriculture Liao Luyan, visited the National Agricultural Exhibition with great interest this afternoon.
    When the distinguished guests arrived at the Agricultural Exhibition, they were warmly welcomed by Wei Zhenwu, the director of the exhibition's main hall, and the staff. During the two-hour visit, the distinguished guests viewed various exhibits in the comprehensive, crop, animal husbandry, aquatic products, and increased production measures pavilions.
    After the visit, the distinguished guests wrote inscriptions for the exhibition, wishing China's agriculture further achievements in the cause of socialist construction.
    Vice Minister Weiss and Professor Aldenne, members of the Government Delegation of the German Democratic Republic, visited the Beijing No. 1 Machine Tool Plant this morning and were warmly welcomed by the workers.

### 德贵宾参观我农业展览会

1959-01-27
第4版()
专栏：

    德贵宾参观我农业展览会
    新华社26日讯　德意志民主共和国政府代表团的贵宾们，今天下午在彭德怀副总理和农业部部长廖鲁言陪同下，以极大的兴趣参观了全国农业展览会。
    贵宾们到达农业展览会的时候，受到展览会总馆馆长魏震五和工作人员的热烈欢迎。贵宾们在两个多小时的参观中，观看了综合、农作物、畜牧、水产、增产措施等分馆的各种展品。
    贵宾们参观后为展览会题词，祝中国农业在社会主义建设事业中取得进一步的成就。
    德意志民主共和国政府代表团团员魏斯副部长和阿尔登内教授，今天上午参观了北京第一机床厂，受到职工们的热烈欢迎。
