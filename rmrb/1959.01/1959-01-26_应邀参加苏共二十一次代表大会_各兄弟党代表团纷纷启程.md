Title: In Response to Invitation to Attend the 21st Congress of the CPSU, Delegations from Various Brother Parties Set Off
Author:
Date: 1959-01-26
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency Report: In response to invitations to attend the 21st Congress of the Communist Party of the Soviet Union, delegations from various brother parties have set off for Moscow.

    The delegation of the Hungarian Socialist Workers' Party, headed by First Secretary of the Central Committee János Kádár, departed for the Soviet Union by train on the 24th.

    Members of the delegation included György Marosán, Member of the Political Bureau of the Central Committee and First Deputy Premier of the Revolutionary Worker-Peasant Government, and Jenő Fock, Member of the Political Bureau of the Central Committee and Secretary of the Central Committee.

    The Romanian Workers' Party delegation left for Moscow by special train on the afternoon of the 24th. The delegation consisted of Gheorghe Gheorghiu-Dej, Chivu Stoica, Gheorghe Apostol, Nicolae Ceauşescu, and Leontin Răutu. Soviet Ambassador to Romania Alexei Yepishev accompanied the delegation to Moscow.

    The delegation of the Central Committee of the Socialist Unity Party of Germany, led by First Secretary of the Central Committee Walter Ulbricht, left Berlin by train on the evening of the 24th for Moscow. Members of the delegation included Otto Grotewohl, Member of the Political Bureau of the Central Committee, who is currently visiting China, as well as Heinrich Rau and A. Neumann, both Members of the Political Bureau of the Central Committee.

    The delegation of the Central Committee of the Polish United Workers' Party was scheduled to depart for Moscow on the 25th. Members of the delegation included Władysław Gomułka, First Secretary of the Central Committee of the Polish United Workers' Party, Political Bureau members Ignacy Loga-Sowiński, Marian Rybicki, Jerzy Zambrowski, Secretariat Secretary Jerzy Klishko, and Central Committee member and Polish Ambassador to the Soviet Union Tadeusz Gede.

### 应邀参加苏共二十一次代表大会  各兄弟党代表团纷纷启程

1959-01-26
第4版()
专栏：

    应邀参加苏共二十一次代表大会
    各兄弟党代表团纷纷启程
    本报综合报道　据新华社讯：应邀参加苏联共产党第二十一次代表大会的各国兄弟党代表团纷纷启程前往莫斯科。
    以中央委员会第一书记卡达尔为首的匈牙利社会主义工人党代表团，在24日乘火车赴苏联。
    代表团团员有：中央政治局委员、工农革命政府第一副总理奥普罗·安托尔，中央政治局委员、中央委员会书记福克·耶诺。
    罗马尼亚工人党代表团在24日下午乘专车赴莫斯科。代表团由乔治乌—德治、基伏·斯托伊卡、格奥尔基·阿波斯托尔、尼古拉·齐奥塞斯库、勒昂特·拉乌图组成。苏联驻罗马亚尼大使阿列克谢·叶皮谢夫陪同代表团前往莫斯科。
    德国统一社会党中央委员会代表团，在中央委员会第一书记乌布利希率领下，于24日晚间乘火车离开柏林前往莫斯科。代表团的团员包括正在中国访问的中央政治局委员格罗提渥，以及中央政治局委员海因里希·劳和阿·诺伊曼。
    波兰统一工人党中央委员会代表团定25日动身前往莫斯科。代表团成员有：波兰统一工人党中央委员会第一书记哥穆尔卡，中央政治局委员英德里霍夫斯基、腊帕茨基、萨姆布罗夫斯基，中央书记处书记克利什科和中央委员、波兰驻苏联大使盖德。
