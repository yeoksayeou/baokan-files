    Nie Dachun, the First Secretary of the Party Committee of the Taiping People's Commune in Pixian County, Sichuan Province, has taken on the teaching of political classes in various spare-time schools. This is a picture of Nie Dachun, the First Secretary of the Commune Party Committee, giving a lesson to students in the spare-time school, explaining the resolution on several issues concerning the People's Commune.
