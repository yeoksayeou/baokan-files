Title: Two Poems on Atomic Energy
Author: Fang Ji
Date: 1959-01-27
Page: 8

    Cyclotron
    Cyclotron,
    Rotating ceaselessly,
    From right to left, to the left,
    Hundreds of millions of times per second.
    Accelerating and rotating,
    Is to increase heat;
    Rotating, oh, rotating,
    To increase the heat of matter.
    Let matter increase heat,
    Liberate the power of matter.
    Like ancient people drilling wood for fire,
    And using fire to cast hoes and plows.
    One-thousandth of the energy liberated by the atom,
    One-hundredth of the energy liberated by hydrogen,
    If two hydrogen atoms collide,
    One-tenth of the energy is liberated.
    But oh, in the realm of energy,
    The concept of decimal places does not apply;
    Mathematics is formal logic,
    The calculation of energy requires astronomical figures.
    Heat becomes energy,
    Energy becomes power,
    Motion is the source of life,
    Is the law of the universe.
    Is the form of existence of all matter.
    Matter, immortal and indestructible;
    Ability, infinite and inexhaustible;
    Movement, never ceasing.
    Cyclotron,
    Rotating ceaselessly,
    From right to left, to the left,
    Hundreds of millions of times per second.
    Atomic Reactor
    Like a granary,
    Like a bunker,
    Containing infinite energy,
    Concealing fierce firepower.
    Uranium rods, just like a bunch of chopsticks,
    Deeply hidden in a cylindrical concrete body;
    And through those experimental channels,
    Miraculous wonders are happening:
    Isotopes are sent to medical institutions,
    Nuclear reactions are uncovering the secrets of matter;
    Researchers in white coats,
    Are as gentle as kind nurses;
    But they are not guarding patients,
    But a mighty Hercules!
    It can move mountains and overturn seas, ascend to heaven and enter the earth;
    Now obediently obeying human control.
    Let it benefit mankind,
    Serve the cause of peace,
    Explore all the resources of the universe,
    To build socialism.
    Atomic reactor,
    Our atomic reactor!
    Our achievement and pride,
    Our glory and victory.
    Come, step into the atomic age,
    Donkeys have served us for too long,
    Let science put on the wings of fantasy,
    And fly high on the east wind.
    Like a granary,
    Like a bunker,
    Containing infinite energy,
    Concealing fierce firepower.
    (January 19th, Beijing)

### 原子能两首
方纪
1959-01-27
第8版()
专栏：

    原子能两首
    方纪
    回旋加速器
    回旋加速器，
    不停地旋转，
    从右向左，向左，
    每秒钟百千万次。
    加速回旋，
    是为了增加热力；
    回旋呵回旋，
    让物质增加热力。
    让物质增加热力，
    解放物质的能力。
    像古代人钻木取火，
    又用火铸成锄犁。
    原子解放能的千分之一，
    氢解放能的百分之一，
    假如两个氢相撞，
    解放能的十分之一。
    但是呵，在能的范畴里，
    十进位的概念不适用；
    数学是形式逻辑，
    计算能力要用天文数字。
    热成为能，
    能成为动力，
    动是生命的源泉，
    是宇宙的规律。
    是一切物质存在的形式。
    物质，不朽不灭；
    能力，无穷无尽；
    运动，不休不止。
    回旋加速器，
    不停地旋转，
    从右向左，向左，
    每秒钟百千万次。
    原子反应堆
    像一座谷仓，
    像一座碉堡，
    蕴藏着无限的能量，
    掩蔽着猛烈的火力。
    铀棒，只像是一束筷子，
    深藏在圆柱形的水泥体中；
    而透过那一座座试验孔道，
    正发生出神妙的奇迹：
    同位素被送往医疗机构，
    核反应在揭开物质秘密；
    穿白衣的研究人员，
    温静得像善心的护士一样；
    但他们守护的不是病人，
    是一个力大无穷的大力士！
    它能翻山倒海，升天入地；
    如今乖乖地听从人的支配。
    要它为人类造福，
    为和平事业服务，
    发掘宇宙间一切资源，
    来建设社会主义。
    原子反应堆，
    我们的原子反应堆！
    我们的成就和骄傲，
    我们的光荣和胜利。
    来，跨进原子时代，
    毛驴为我们服务得太久了，
    让科学插上幻想的翅膀，
    乘东风高高飞去。
    像一座谷仓，
    像一座碉堡，
    蕴藏着无限的能量，
    掩蔽着猛烈的火力。
    （一月十九日·北京）
