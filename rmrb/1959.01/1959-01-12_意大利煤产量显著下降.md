Title: Significant Decline in Italian Coal Production
Author:
Date: 1959-01-12
Page: 5

    Xinhua News Agency report: The Italian financial and economic newspaper "Il Globo" anxiously reported that Italy's coal production had fallen by 30% in 1958 alone. The newspaper wrote that the reason for the decline was "competition from other countries, especially the United States."

### 意大利煤产量显著下降

1959-01-12
第5版()
专栏：

    意大利煤产量显著下降
    据新华社讯　意大利财政经济界机关报“寰球报”不安地报道了意大利采煤量仅在1958年一年就下降了30％的消息。报纸写道，下降的原因是“其他国家的竞争，特别是美国的竞争。”
