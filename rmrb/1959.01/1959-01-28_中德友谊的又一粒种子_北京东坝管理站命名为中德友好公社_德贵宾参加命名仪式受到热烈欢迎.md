Title: Another Seed of Sino-German Friendship: Beijing's Dongba Management Station Renamed Sino-German Friendship Commune; German Guests Attend Naming Ceremony and Receive Warm Welcome
Author:
Date: 1959-01-28
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, January 27th. To further develop the fraternal friendship between the Chinese and German peoples, the Dongba Management Station in Chaoyang District, Beijing, was renamed the Sino-German Friendship People's Commune this afternoon. Premier Grotewohl, his wife, and other distinguished guests from the government delegation of the German Democratic Republic, accompanied by Vice Premier Chen Yi and other officials, joined the male and female members of the Dongba Management Station in a grand and warm naming ceremony.

At the naming ceremony, Premier Grotewohl delivered a speech full of friendly passion. In his speech, he conveyed the German people's best wishes to the Chinese people, congratulated them on world peace, and wished the friendship between the German and Chinese people eternal development. He said: We are in the West of the world, and you are in the East; although our two countries are far apart, our hearts are linked together, and our friendship is like brothers. This friendship will last forever.

Premier Grotewohl said: Today, we are only planting a seed here. We must find ways to make this seed grow into a large tree symbolizing our friendship, so that we can live and work proudly under the shade of this socialist tree.

Vice Mayor of Beijing, Feng Jiping, said in his speech at the beginning of the naming ceremony: "Premier Grotewohl's visit and the naming of the Sino-German Friendship People's Commune have greatly encouraged us. We should cherish this honor and contribute to the socialist construction of the motherland and to enhancing the friendship between the Chinese and German people." Also speaking at the ceremony was Fu Zongkun, Secretary of the Party Committee of the Dongba Management Station. He assured Premier Grotewohl that they would definitely improve the commune's production to repay Premier Grotewohl's expectations and care.

At the naming ceremony, the distinguished guests and the heads of the Dongba Management Station exchanged gifts. Among the gifts from the distinguished guests was the national flag of the German Democratic Republic.

This morning, the Dongba Management Station was decorated as if it were a festival. Colorful towers were erected in the streets, and slogans welcoming the German guests were plastered on the walls. The couplet pasted on the door of the Dongba Management Station office read: "Welcoming distinguished guests from afar, distance cannot keep our hearts apart; Working together on construction, let's move forward hand in hand," with the horizontal scroll reading "Welcome, Welcome." More than two thousand male and female members had already lined up on both sides of the village road, joyfully waving colorful flags, welcoming the esteemed Premier Grotewohl to visit them.

At twelve noon, when Premier Grotewohl, his wife, and delegation members: Vice Premier and Minister of Foreign Affairs Bolz, Vice Minister Weiss, Professor Ardenne, Ambassador Wang Dandel, and other distinguished guests, accompanied by Vice Premier Chen Yi, Minister of Agriculture Liao Luyan, Vice Minister of Foreign Affairs Zeng Yongquan, our country's ambassador to the German Democratic Republic Wang Guoquan, Vice Mayor of Beijing Feng Jiping, and others, arrived at the Dongba Management Station, the members beat drums and gongs, played the Sheng flute, set off firecrackers, and shouted "Long Live Sino-German Friendship!" warmly welcoming the arrival of the distinguished guests.

After Premier Grotewohl and others met with station chief Han Xiaojiang and Party Committee Secretary Fu Zongkun, they visited the commune's kindergarten, lacquer carving workshop, agricultural machinery repair and manufacturing plant, hydroelectric power station, and large-scale high-yield wheat fields, and watched the members accumulate fertilizer in the river滩. Wherever Premier Grotewohl and others went in this Dongba Management Station with more than 6,100 households, applause and welcoming slogans resounded. (Attached photo)

The members of the Sino-German Friendship People's Commune joyfully lined the streets to welcome the distinguished guests.
                                 Xinhua News Agency Reporter Liu Qingrui

### 中德友谊的又一粒种子  北京东坝管理站命名为中德友好公社  德贵宾参加命名仪式受到热烈欢迎

1959-01-28
第4版()
专栏：

    中德友谊的又一粒种子
    北京东坝管理站命名为中德友好公社
    德贵宾参加命名仪式受到热烈欢迎
    新华社27日讯　为进一步发展中德两国人民的兄弟友谊，北京市朝阳区东坝管理站今天下午命名为中德友好人民公社。格罗提渥总理和他的夫人及德意志民主共和国政府代表团的其他贵宾们，由陈毅副总理等负责人陪同，同东坝管理站的男女社员们一起参加了隆重热烈的命名仪式。
    格罗提渥总理在命名仪式上作了充满友谊激情的讲话，他在讲话中转达了德国人民对中国人民的良好祝愿，祝贺世界和平，祝贺德中两国人民的友谊永远发展。他说：我们在世界的西方，你们在世界的东方；我们两国虽然相距很远，但是我们的心却连在一起，我们的友谊像兄弟一样，这种友谊将永远保持下去。
    格罗提渥总理说：今天我们在这里只是种下了种子，我们要想办法让这个种子节节长成象征我们友谊的大树，让我们在这棵社会主义的树荫下，骄傲地生活和劳动。
    北京市副市长冯基平在命名仪式开始时的讲话中说：“格罗提渥总理的来访和中德友好人民公社的命名，使我们受到很大的鼓舞。我们应当十分珍视这种荣誉，为祖国的社会主义建设，为增进中德两国人民的友谊作出贡献。”在仪式上讲话的还有东坝管理站党委书记傅宗昆，他向格罗提渥总理保证，他们一定要更好地搞好公社的生产，以这来回答格罗提渥总理的期望和关怀。
    在命名仪式上贵宾们和东坝管理站负责人互相赠送了礼物，贵宾们的赠礼中，有一件是德意志民主共和国的国旗。
    今天一清早，东坝管理站就打扮得如同过节一样，街头搭起彩塔，墙壁上贴满了欢迎德国贵宾的标语。东坝管理站办公室大门上贴的对联是：“万里迎贵宾地远心近，共同搞建设携手前进”，横披是“欢迎欢迎”。两千多名男女社员早就排列在村道两旁，他们手执彩旗，喜气洋洋，迎候敬爱的格罗提渥总理等到他们这里来作客。
    中午十二时，当格罗提渥总理和他的夫人及代表团团员：博尔茨副总理兼外交部长、魏斯副部长、阿尔登内教授、汪戴尔大使等贵宾们，由副总理陈毅，农业部部长廖鲁言，外交部副部长曾涌泉，我国驻德意志民主共和国大使王国权，北京市副市长冯基平等陪同，来到东坝管理站的时候，社员们敲锣打鼓，奏起笙笛，燃放鞭炮，并且高呼“中德友好万岁！”隆重热烈地欢迎贵宾们的光临。
    格罗提渥总理等会见了站长韩晓江和党委书记傅宗昆等以后，便参观了公社的幼儿园、雕漆车间、农机综合修造厂、水电站和大面积丰产麦田，并观看了社员们在河滩里积肥的情景。在这个有六千一百多户的东坝管理站，格罗提渥总理等走到哪里，哪里便响起了鼓掌声和欢迎的口号声。（附图片）
