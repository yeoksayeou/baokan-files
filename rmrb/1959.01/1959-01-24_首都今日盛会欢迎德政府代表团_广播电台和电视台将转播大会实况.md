Title: Capital to Host Grand Gathering Today to Welcome German Government Delegation; Radio and Television Stations to Broadcast the Assembly Live

Author:

Date: 1959-01-24
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, 23rd: People from all walks of life in the capital will hold a grand rally on the afternoon of the 24th to welcome the government delegation of the German Democratic Republic, headed by Prime Minister Otto Grotewohl. The Central People's Broadcasting Station will broadcast the live proceedings of the welcoming rally from 15:20 on the 24th, using the first program. Beijing People's Broadcasting Station and Beijing Television will broadcast simultaneously.

### 首都今日盛会欢迎德政府代表团  广播电台和电视台将转播大会实况

1959-01-24
第1版()
专栏：

    首都今日盛会欢迎德政府代表团
    广播电台和电视台将转播大会实况
    新华社23日讯　首都各界人民将在24日下午举行盛大集会，欢迎以奥托·格罗提渥总理为首的德意志民主共和国政府代表团。中央人民广播电台将从24日十五时二十分开始，用第一种节目转播欢迎大会的实况。北京人民广播电台和北京电视台将同时转播。
