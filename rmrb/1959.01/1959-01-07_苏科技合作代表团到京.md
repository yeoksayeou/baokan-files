Title: Soviet Science and Technology Cooperation Delegation Arrives in Beijing
Author: 
Date: 1959-01-07
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, 6th — The Soviet science and technology cooperation delegation, headed by N.M. Siruyanov, Chairman of the Soviet Group of the Sino-Soviet Science and Technology Cooperation Committee and Deputy Representative of the Soviet Union to the Council for Mutual Economic Assistance, arrived in Beijing by plane today. The other two representatives of the delegation arrived in Beijing on December 31 last year, and another representative will arrive in Beijing on the 7th. The delegation is here to attend the eighth meeting of the Sino-Soviet Science and Technology Cooperation Committee.

### 苏科技合作代表团到京

1959-01-07
第4版()
专栏：

    苏科技合作代表团到京
    新华社6日讯　以中苏科学技术合作委员会苏联组主席、八国经济互助委员会苏联副代表尼·米·西鲁扬诺夫为首的苏联科学技术合作代表团一行四人，今天乘飞机到达北京。代表团的另外两位代表已经在去年12月31日到达北京，另外一位代表将于7日到达北京。代表团是来参加中苏科学技术合作委员会第八届例会的。
