Title: Steel Piled High Like Mountains

Author: 
Date: 1959-01-07
Page: 2

Steel piled high like mountains,
Thousands of mountains, tens of thousands of mountains,
White clouds surround the mountains.
Not Kunlun moved here,
But steel piled high like mountains.
(Chongqing Steel Rolling Workers)

### 钢材堆成山

1959-01-07
第2版()
专栏：

    钢材堆成山千重山，万重山，白云绕在山中间。不是昆仑移在此，而是钢材堆成山。
    （重庆轧钢工人）
