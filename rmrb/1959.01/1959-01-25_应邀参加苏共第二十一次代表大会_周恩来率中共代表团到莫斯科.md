Title: Zhou Enlai Leads CCP Delegation to Moscow to Attend the 21st Congress of the CPSU
Author:
Date: 1959-01-25
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, January 24th: At the invitation of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of the Soviet Union to participate in the 21st Congress of the Communist Party of the Soviet Union, the Chinese Communist Party delegation, led by Zhou Enlai, Vice Chairman of the CCP Central Committee, left Beijing by plane this morning for Moscow.

The members of the Chinese Communist Party delegation are: Kang Sheng, Alternate Member of the Political Bureau of the CCP Central Committee; Li Xuefeng, Secretary of the Secretariat of the Central Committee; Liu Ningyi, Member of the Central Committee; and Liu Xiao, Member of the Central Committee and Chinese Ambassador to the Soviet Union.

Deng Xiaoping, General Secretary of the CCP Central Committee, Peng Zhen, Member of the Political Bureau, Liu Lantao and Yang Shangkun, Alternate Secretaries of the Secretariat of the Central Committee, Liao Chengzhi, Wu Xiuquan, and Xi Zhongxun, Members of the Central Committee, and Luo Guibo, Alternate Member of the Central Committee, were at the airport to see them off.

The ambassadors and chargés d'affaires ad interim of the Soviet Union and other socialist countries in China were also at the airport to see them off.

Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, January 24th: The Chinese Communist Party delegation, led by Zhou Enlai, Vice Chairman of the CCP Central Committee, arrived in Moscow this afternoon by "Tu-104" jet plane from Beijing. The delegation, at the invitation of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of the Soviet Union, will participate in the 21st Congress of the Communist Party of the Soviet Union.

The delegation was greeted at the airport by: Aristov, Member of the Presidium of the CPSU Central Committee and Secretary of the CPSU Central Committee; Kirichenko, Member of the Presidium of the CPSU Central Committee and Secretary of the CPSU Central Committee; Kozlov, Member of the Presidium of the CPSU Central Committee and First Deputy Chairman of the Council of Ministers of the Soviet Union; Suslov, Member of the Presidium of the CPSU Central Committee and Secretary of the CPSU Central Committee; Polyansky, Alternate Member of the Presidium of the CPSU Central Committee and Chairman of the Council of Ministers of the Russian Federation; Andreyev, Kuz’min, Ponomarev, and Shelepin, Members of the CPSU Central Committee, Satiukov, Editor-in-Chief of "Pravda," and responsible staff members of the organs of the CPSU Central Committee.

Liu Xiao, member of the delegation, member of the CCP Central Committee and Chinese Ambassador to the Soviet Union, Nguyen Song Tung, Charge d'Affaires ad interim of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam to the Soviet Union, and personnel from the Chinese and Vietnamese embassies also greeted the delegation at the airport.

### 应邀参加苏共第二十一次代表大会  周恩来率中共代表团到莫斯科

1959-01-25
第1版()
专栏：

    应邀参加苏共第二十一次代表大会
    周恩来率中共代表团到莫斯科
    新华社24日讯　由中共中央副主席周恩来率领的中国共产党代表团，应苏联共产党中央委员会的邀请参加苏联共产党第二十一次代表大会，已经在今天上午乘飞机离开北京前往莫斯科。
    中国共产党代表团的成员有：中共中央政治局候补委员康生，中央书记处书记李雪峰，中央委员刘宁一，中央委员、中国驻苏联大使刘晓。
    到机场欢送的，有中共中央总书记邓小平，政治局委员彭真，中央书记处候补书记刘澜涛、杨尚昆，中央委员廖承志、伍修权、习仲勋，中央候补委员罗贵波。
    苏联和社会主义各国驻中国的大使和临时代办也到机场欢送。
    新华社莫斯科24日电　由中共中央副主席周恩来率领的中国共产党代表团，今天下午乘“图—一○四”喷气飞机由北京抵达莫斯科。代表团应苏联共产党中央委员会的邀请，将参加苏联共产党第二十一次代表大会。
    到机场迎接代表团的有：苏共中央主席团委员、苏共中央书记阿里斯托夫，苏共中央主席团委员、苏共中央书记基里钦科，苏共中央主席团委员、苏联部长会议第一副主席科兹洛夫，苏共中央主席团委员、苏共中央书记苏斯洛夫，苏共中央主席团候补委员、俄罗斯联邦部长会议主席波利扬斯基，苏共中央委员安德烈耶夫、库兹?佐夫、波诺马烈夫、谢列平，“真理报”总编辑萨丘柯夫和苏共中央机关的负责工作人员。
    代表团团员、中共中央委员、中国驻苏联大使刘晓，越南民主共和国驻苏联临时代办阮双松以及中、越两国大使馆人员也到机场迎接。
