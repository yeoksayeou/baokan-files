Title: Governor Cid Sampaio of Pernambuco, Brazil Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1959-01-10
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, January 9th: Chu Tunan, President of the Chinese People's Association for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries, hosted a banquet today to welcome the Governor of Pernambuco, Brazil, Cid Sampaio, and his wife, along with his assistant, Jorge Albano, and his wife. In their speeches at the banquet, both hosts and guests expressed their willingness to work to promote the friendship between the people of China and Brazil.
　　
Cid Sampaio arrived in Beijing today at the invitation of the Chinese People's Association for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries and will be making a friendly visit to our country.

### 巴西伯南布哥州长希德·桑巴约到京

1959-01-10
第4版()
专栏：

    巴西伯南布哥州长希德·桑巴约到京
    据新华社9日讯　中国人民对外文化协会会长楚图南今天举行宴会，欢迎巴西伯南布哥州州长希德·桑巴约和夫人，以及他的助理乔治·阿尔巴诺和夫人。宾主双方在宴会上的讲话中，都表示愿为促进中巴两国人民的友谊而努力。
    希德·桑巴约是应中国人民对外文化协会邀请，在今天到达北京，将在我国进行友好访问。
