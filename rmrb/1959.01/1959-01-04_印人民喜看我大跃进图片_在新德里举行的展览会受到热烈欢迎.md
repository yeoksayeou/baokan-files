Title: Indian People Gladly View Pictures of Our Great Leap Forward: Exhibition in New Delhi Receives Warm Welcome
Author:
Date: 1959-01-04
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, New Delhi, January 2nd: The Chinese Great Leap Forward photo exhibition closed today. The exhibition has been warmly welcomed by Indian audiences in the past seven days.

　　People carefully viewed various pictures showing the speed of China's development in all aspects. They were particularly interested in pictures of steelmaking in small furnaces and various inventions in agriculture, industry, and science and technology.

　　Audiences wrote many enthusiastic opinions and comments. Sundarlal, chairman of the India-China Friendship Association, wrote: "Very happy to see the exhibition showing China's amazing progress in all aspects. This is positive proof that in a peaceful competition, the capitalist economy cannot stand in front of the communist economy."

　　An Indian economics lecturer wrote: "A moving exhibition of a great developing country." An engineer wrote: "Amazing achievements, there is much to learn in the field of electrical engineering." One visitor wrote: "This is a beautiful depiction of the rapid socialist construction of our great neighbor, China."

### 印人民喜看我大跃进图片  在新德里举行的展览会受到热烈欢迎

1959-01-04
第4版()
专栏：

    印人民喜看我大跃进图片
    在新德里举行的展览会受到热烈欢迎
    新华社新德里2日电　中国大跃进图片展览会今天闭幕，这个展览会在过去七天中受到了印度观众的热烈欢迎。
    人们仔细地观看表明中国在各方面发展速度的各种图片。他们特别感兴趣的是小平炉炼钢的图片和农业、工业和科学技术的种种发明。
    观众写下了许多热情的意见和评论。印中友好协会主席森德拉尔写道：非常高兴看到显示中国在各个方面的惊人进步的展览会。这积极地证明了，在和平竞赛中，资本主义经济在共产主义经济面前是站不住脚的。”
    一位印度经济学讲师写道：“正在发展的伟大国家的动人的展览会。”一位工程师写道：“惊人的成就，在电力工程方面有许多可以学习的东西。”有一位参观者写道：“这是我们伟大的邻国——中国的迅速的社会主义建设的美好描绘。”
