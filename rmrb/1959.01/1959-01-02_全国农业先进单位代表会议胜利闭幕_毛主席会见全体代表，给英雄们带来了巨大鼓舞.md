Title: National Agricultural Advanced Units Representative Conference Triumphantly Concludes; Chairman Mao Meets with All Representatives, Bringing Tremendous Inspiration to the Heroes

Author:
Date: 1959-01-02
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, January 1st: The National Agricultural Socialist Construction Advanced Units Representative Conference triumphantly concluded today amidst the jubilant sounds of welcoming a bigger, better, and more comprehensive leap forward in 1959.

Chairman Mao Zedong arrived at the venue during the closing ceremony and met with representatives of 500 million heroic farmers, bringing immense excitement and encouragement to the representatives.

The closing ceremony began at 4:00 PM today. All representatives unanimously adopted the ten proposals put forward by the National Agricultural Socialist Construction Advanced Units Representative Conference for achieving a bigger, better, and more comprehensive leap forward in 1959.

The conference also unanimously passed a congratulatory telegram to the Party Central Committee and Chairman Mao, and a congratulatory telegram to the Chinese People's Liberation Army and the troops on the Fujian Front.

At 4:35 PM, the great leader of the Chinese people, Chairman Mao Zedong, and other Party and state leaders, including Zhou Enlai, Zhu De, Chen Yun, Deng Xiaoping, Peng Zhen, He Long, Li Xiannian, Tan Zhenlin, Lu Dingyi, Kang Sheng, Bo Yibo, Li Xuefeng, Yang Shangkun, Hu Qiaomu, Deng Zihui, Guo Moruo, and Chen Shutong, arrived at the venue. At this moment, the "The East is Red" music began to play, and the mercury lamps in the hall shone brightly. The six thousand representatives in the gymnasium all stood up and applauded.

Chairman Mao Zedong and the central leaders, amidst cheers, circled the venue, meeting with all the representatives. Chairman Mao Zedong continuously waved to the representatives. Wherever he went, a wave of cheers erupted. Enthusiastic applause and cheers echoed through the venue for a long time. Then, Chairman Mao Zedong and the central leaders participated in the closing ceremony.

The closing ceremony continued. The conference awarded certificates from the State Council of the People's Republic of China to all advanced units in agricultural socialist construction and gave souvenirs to all representatives attending the conference.

Tan Zhenlin, Secretary of the Secretariat of the CPC Central Committee, delivered the closing address. Comrade Tan Zhenlin said: This conference has been very enthusiastic and successful. Thanks to the direct guidance of the Party Central Committee and the State Council, the kind attention of Chairman Mao, and the joint efforts of all comrades, our conference has now successfully completed its tasks. He said: Our conference is a conference full of revolutionary enthusiasm and a conference that adheres to the scientific spirit of seeking truth from facts. We must fully promote the spirit of the conference and bring it to all parts of the country and implement it in all our work. He finally called on all representatives to continue to play a leading and backbone role in future work, and to move forward more uniformly towards the goals indicated by the Party.

The closing ceremony concluded with the high-spirited singing of "Socialism is Good".

Xinhua News Agency, January 1st: The six thousand representatives attending the National Agricultural Socialist Construction Advanced Units Representative Conference celebrated the New Year of 1959 in the capital.

At the New Year's Eve party, Mei Lanfang and all the famous Peking Opera actors in the capital held a grand performance for the representatives to celebrate the great achievements on the agricultural front in 1958. Zhang Junqiu and Li Siguang performed "Nv Qi Jie" (Woman Escorted), Shang Xiaoyun and Xun Huisheng performed "Fan Jiang Guan," and Mei Lanfang, Tan Fuying, Ma Lianliang, Qiu Shengrong, Jiang Miaoxiang, Ma Fulu, Li Duokui, Li Hezeng, Liu Lianrong, and Huang Yuanqing performed "Long Feng Cheng Xiang" (Dragon and Phoenix Bring Auspiciousness). The evening also featured Qingchang performances by Pingju opera actress Xiao Bai Yushuang, Hebei Bangzi opera actress Li Guiyun, Peking Opera actress Zhao Yanxia, and crosstalk performances by crosstalk actors Hou Baolin and Guo Qiru. ### 全国农业先进单位代表会议胜利闭幕  毛主席会见全体代表，给英雄们带来了巨大鼓舞

1959-01-02
第1版()
专栏：

    全国农业先进单位代表会议胜利闭幕
    毛主席会见全体代表，给英雄们带来了巨大鼓舞
    新华社1日讯　全国农业社会主义建设先进单位代表会议，今天在迎接1959年更大更好更全面跃进的欢腾声中闭幕。
    毛泽东主席在闭幕大会进行中来到会场，会见了五亿英雄农民的代表，给代表们带来了莫大的兴奋和鼓舞。
    今天闭幕式在下午四时开始，全体代表一致通过了全国农业社会主义建设先进单位代表会议为在1959年实现更大更好更全面的跃进所提出的十大倡议。
    会议还先后一致通过了向党中央和毛主席的致敬电和向中国人民解放军和福建前线部队的致敬电。
    四时三十五分，中国人民的伟大领袖毛泽东主席和党和国家的其他领导人周恩来、朱德、陈云、邓小平、彭真、贺龙、李先念、谭震林、陆定一、康生、薄一波、李雪峰、杨尚昆、胡乔木、邓子恢、郭沫若、陈叔通等来到了会场。这时，乐队奏起了“东方红”的乐曲，会场上水银灯一齐大放光明。体育馆六千名代表全体起立，鼓掌欢呼。
    毛泽东主席和中央的首长们在欢呼声中，绕会场巡行一周，会见全体代表。毛泽东主席不住地向代表们招手致意。他走到哪里，哪里就激起一阵欢腾的声浪。热烈的掌声和欢呼声在会场上回旋起伏，久久不停。接着，毛泽东主席和中央的首长们参加了闭幕式。
    闭幕式继续进行，会议给全体农业社会主义建设先进单位颁发了中华人民共和国国务院的奖状，并给出席会议的全体代表发了纪念品。
    中共中央书记处书记谭震林致闭幕词。谭震林同志说：这次大会开得很热烈，很成功。这次大会，由于党中央和国务院的直接指导，由于毛主席的亲切关怀，由于全体同志的共同努力，现在已经顺利地完成了任务。他说：我们的大会，是一个充满了革命热情的大会，是一个坚持实事求是的科学精神的大会。我们必须把大会的精神充分地发扬起来，把它带到全国各地和贯彻到各项工作中去。他最后号召全体代表在今后工作中继续发挥带头作用和骨干作用，更加步伐整齐地向着党所指示的目标胜利前进。
    闭幕式最后在乐队高奏“社会主义好”歌声中结束。
    新华社1日讯　出席全国农业社会主义建设先进单位代表会议的六千代表，在首都欢度了1959年的新年。
    在元旦的晚会上，梅兰芳和所有首都的著名京剧演员们，为庆祝1958年农业战线上的丰功伟绩，给代表们举行了一次盛大的会演。张君秋和李四广演了女起解，尚小云和荀慧生演了樊江关，梅兰芳、谭富英、马连良、裘盛戎、姜妙香、马富禄、李多奎、李和曾、刘连荣、黄元庆等演了龙凤呈祥。晚会上，还有评剧名演员筱白玉霜、河北梆子戏名演员李桂云、京剧名演员赵燕侠的清唱，和相声名演员侯宝林、郭启儒的相声。（附图片）
