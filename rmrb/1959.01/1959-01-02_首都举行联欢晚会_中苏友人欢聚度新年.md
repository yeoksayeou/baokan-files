Title: A Gala Evening Held in the Capital: Chinese and Soviet Friends Gather to Celebrate the New Year
Author:
Date: 1959-01-02
Page: 3
Xinhua News Agency, January 1st – The Sino-Soviet Friendship Association Headquarters, the Beijing Sino-Soviet Friendship Association, and the Beijing Municipal Committee of the Communist Youth League held a gala evening tonight with staff from the Soviet Embassy in China, Soviet experts, and Soviet students to celebrate the New Year together. Li Dequan and Shao Lizi, vice presidents of the Sino-Soviet Friendship Association Headquarters, Qian Junrui, the secretary-general, and Ge Baoquan, the deputy secretary-general, also attended this gala evening.

Qian Junrui, Secretary-General of the Sino-Soviet Friendship Association Headquarters, and Sudarikov, Counselor of the Soviet Embassy in China, both spoke at the party, congratulating each other on the New Year. Qian Junrui said that the New Year is the first year of the great Soviet Seven-Year Plan and also the tenth anniversary of the founding of the People's Republic of China. The socialist camp, headed by the Soviet Union, will achieve even greater victories in this new year. Sudarikov warmly congratulated the Chinese people on their great achievements in his speech. He said that all Soviet people are preparing to devote all their efforts to implementing the Seven-Year Plan for the development of the Soviet national economy. He said that this plan, like the plans of all socialist countries, is the common cause of the powerful and invincible camp of peace and socialism.

The evening was filled with the unbreakable friendship between the Chinese and Soviet people. Members of the Sino-Soviet Friendship Association and Soviet friends danced, sang, participated in various games, and enjoyed song and dance performances by Chinese and Soviet artists together. The party lasted for more than three hours before it ended.

### 首都举行联欢晚会  中苏友人欢聚度新年

1959-01-02
第3版()
专栏：

    首都举行联欢晚会
    中苏友人欢聚度新年
    新华社1日讯　中苏友好协会总会、北京市中苏友协以及共青团北京市委会，今晚举行晚会和苏联驻华大使馆的工作人员、苏联专家以及苏联留学生们一起欢度新年。中苏友好协会总会副会长李德全、邵力子，秘书长钱俊瑞、副秘书长戈宝权等参加了这个联欢晚会。
    中苏友协总会秘书长钱俊瑞和苏联驻华大使馆参赞苏达利柯夫都在晚会上讲话，互相祝贺新年，钱俊瑞说，新的一年是苏联伟大七年计划的第一年，也是中华人民共和国建国十周年。以苏联为首的社会主义阵营在这新的一年中将取得更大的胜利。苏达利柯夫在讲话中热烈祝贺中国人民所取得的伟大成就。他说，全体苏联人民正在准备用全部精力来实现发展苏联国民经济的七年计划。他说，这个计划，同所有社会主义国家的计划一样，是强大的不可战胜的和平和社会主义阵营的共同事业。
    晚会上洋溢着中苏两国人民牢不可破的友谊。中苏友协的会员们和苏联友人们一起跳舞、唱歌，参加各种游戏，并且一起欣赏中苏艺术家们的歌舞表演。晚会进行了三个多小时才结束。
