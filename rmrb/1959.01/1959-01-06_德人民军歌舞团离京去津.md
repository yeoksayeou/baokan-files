Title: German People's Army Song and Dance Troupe Leaves Beijing for Tianjin
Author:
Date: 1959-01-06
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, 4th — The Erich Weinert Song and Dance Troupe of the German Democratic Republic's People's Army, accompanied by Major General Wei Chuantong, Deputy Director of the Propaganda Department of the Chinese People's Liberation Army General Political Department, left Beijing by train this morning for a visit and performance in Tianjin.

Those who went to the station to see them off included Senior Colonel Li Wei, Deputy Director of the Propaganda Department of the Chinese People's Liberation Army General Political Department, and Lieutenant Colonel Kautz, Deputy Military Attaché of the German Democratic Republic in China.

The song and dance troupe returned to Beijing from Wuhan at the end of last year. During their stay in Beijing, they gave two wonderful performances for the troops stationed in Beijing of the Chinese People's Liberation Army.

### 德人民军歌舞团离京去津

1959-01-06
第5版()
专栏：

    德人民军歌舞团离京去津
    新华社4日讯　德意志民主共和国人民军埃里希·魏纳特歌舞团今天上午由中国人民解放军总政治部宣传部副部长魏传统少将陪同，乘火车离北京，前往天津进行访问演出。
    到车站欢送的有中国人民解放军总政治部宣传部副部长李伟大校和德意志民主共和国驻华副武官考茨少校等。
    歌舞团是去年年底从武汉返回北京的。在京期间，曾为中国人民解放军驻京部队举行了两场精彩的演出。
