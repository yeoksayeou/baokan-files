Title: The majority of members of the German People's Army Song and Dance Troupe leave Beijing to return home

Author:
Date: 1959-01-28
Page: 4

    Xinhua News Agency, January 27th. Leading members and the majority of members of the German Democratic Republic People's Army Erich Weinert Song and Dance Troupe left Beijing by special plane this morning to return home.
    Those who went to the airport to see them off included Lieutenant General Yuan Ziqin, Air Force Lieutenant General Wang Huichiu, Navy Major General Duan Dezhang, Major General Wei Chuantong, Major General Zhang Zhengguang and other generals from various services of the Chinese People's Liberation Army, officers from the army, navy and air force stationed in Beijing, literary and art workers from the troops, Secretary-General of the Sino-German Democratic Republic Friendship Association Wang Yuanfang, and more than one thousand workers from the capital.
    Military Attaché of the German Democratic Republic to China Lieutenant Colonel Fan Yinhed, Deputy Military Attaché Major Kautz, and others also went to the airport to see them off.
    The remaining members of the song and dance troupe will return home by plane in the coming days.

### 德人民军歌舞团大部团员离京返国

1959-01-28
第4版()
专栏：

    德人民军歌舞团大部团员离京返国
    新华社27日讯　德意志民主共和国人民军埃里希·魏纳特歌舞团领导人员和大部分团员，今天上午乘专机离开北京返国。
    到机场欢送的有，袁子钦中将、王辉球空军中将、段德彰海军少将、魏传统少将、张正光少将等中国人民解放军各军种将领和驻京陆、海、空军军官，部队文艺工作者，中国德意志民主共和国友好协会秘书长王元方，以及首都工人等共一千多人。
    德意志民主共和国驻华武官范因赫德中校、副武官考茨少校等也到机场送行。
    歌舞团的另一部分团员将在日内乘飞机返国。
