Title: Comrade Liu Shaoqi Receives Comrade Nemes

Author:
Date: 1959-01-19
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, January 18th: Liu Shaoqi, Vice Chairman of the CPC Central Committee, received Nemes Dezső, Alternate Member of the Political Bureau of the Hungarian Socialist Workers' Party Central Committee and Editor-in-Chief of "People's Freedom," on the afternoon of January 18th and held a cordial conversation.

Present at the reception were Liu Ningyi, Member of the CPC Central Committee, and Wu Lengxi, Editor-in-Chief of the "People's Daily." (Attached picture)

### 刘少奇同志接见内梅什同志

1959-01-19
第1版()
专栏：

    刘少奇同志接见内梅什同志
