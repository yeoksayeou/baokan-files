Title: Sino-German Joint Statement Signed in Beijing; Consular Treaty Between the Two Countries Signed Simultaneously
Author:
Date: 1959-01-28
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, January 27th — The signing ceremony for the Joint Statement between the Government of the People's Republic of China and the Government of the German Democratic Republic was solemnly held in Beijing this afternoon. The Consular Treaty between the People's Republic of China and the German Democratic Republic was also signed simultaneously.

　　At 6:10 PM, Acting Premier Chen Yi and Premier Grotewohl signed the joint statement on behalf of the Chinese and German governments, respectively.

　　Following this, Foreign Minister Chen Yi and Foreign Minister Bolz signed the consular treaty on behalf of the Chinese and German governments, respectively.

　　The Chinese side participating in the signing ceremony included: Chairman Liu Shaoqi, Vice-Chairmen Shen Junru, Huang Yanpei, Peng Zhen, and Chen Shutong, Vice-Premiers Peng Dehuai and Bo Yibo, Vice Foreign Ministers Ji Pengfei and Zeng Yongquan, Vice Minister of Foreign Trade Lin Haiyun, Director of the Institute of Atomic Energy of the Chinese Academy of Sciences Qian Sanqiang, and the Chinese Ambassador to the German Democratic Republic, Wang Guoquan, among others.

　　The German Democratic Republic side participating in the signing ceremony included all members of the government delegation: Deputy Premier and Minister of Foreign Affairs Lothar Bolz, Deputy Minister of Foreign Affairs Sepp Schwab, Deputy Minister of Foreign and Intra-German Trade Gerhard Weiss, Member of the Scientific Advisory Committee on Peaceful Use of Atomic Energy under the Council of Ministers of the German Democratic Republic and Member of the Research Committee Professor Manfred von Ardenne, and the Ambassador of the German Democratic Republic to China, Paul Wandel.

　　Also participating in the signing ceremony was the head of the German Democratic Republic trade delegation currently visiting our country, State Secretary and First Deputy Minister of Foreign and Intra-German Trade, Hüttenrauch.

　　After the signing, the representatives of the two countries participating in the signing ceremony shook hands cordially and offered champagne in congratulation.

### 中德联合声明在京签字  两国领事条约同时签字

1959-01-28
第1版()
专栏：

    中德联合声明在京签字
    两国领事条约同时签字
    新华社27日讯　中华人民共和国政府和德意志民主共和国政府联合声明的签字仪式，今天下午在北京隆重举行。中华人民共和国和德意志民主共和国领事条约也同时签字。
    下午六时十分，陈毅代总理和格罗提渥总理分别代表中德两国政府在联合声明上签了字。
    接着陈毅外交部长和博尔茨外交部长分别代表中德两国政府在领事条约上签了字。
    中国方面参加签字仪式的有：刘少奇委员长，沈钧儒、黄炎培、彭真、陈叔通副委员长，彭德怀、薄一波副总理，外交部副部长姬鹏飞、曾涌泉，对外贸易部副部长林海云，中国科学院原子能研究所所长钱三强，中国驻德意志民主共和国大使王国权等人。
    德意志民主共和国方面参加签字仪式的有政府代表团的全体团员：副总理兼外交部部长洛塔·博尔茨，外交部副部长塞普·施瓦布，对外和东西德贸易部副部长格哈德·魏斯，德意志民主共和国部长会议和平利用原子能科学谘议委员会委员和研究委员会委员曼弗雷德·冯·阿尔登内教授，德意志民主共和国驻中国大使保罗·汪戴尔。
    正在我国访问的德意志民主共和国贸易代表团团长、对外和东西德贸易部国务秘书兼第一副部长许滕劳赫也参加了签字仪式。
    在签字以后，参加签字仪式的两国代表亲切握手，并进香槟酒表示祝贺。
