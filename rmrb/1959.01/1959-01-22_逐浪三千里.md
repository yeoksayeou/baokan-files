Title: Racing Three Thousand Li on the Waves
Author: Tang Shaoqiu
Date: 1959-01-22
Page: 2

Sailing Three Thousand Li

The crew's enthusiasm is extraordinary,
Afraid of neither hardship nor difficulty,
By day, racing three thousand li on the waves,
By night, crossing Ganxi's ninety-nine bends,
Flying straight through the winding Quchi Lake,
Breaking through shoal after shoal.
The moonlight sends me to dawn,
The sun greets me with "Good morning."
        (Crew member Tang Shaoqiu)

### 逐浪三千里
唐绍裘
1959-01-22
第2版()
专栏：

    逐浪三千里
    船员干劲非等闲，
    不怕辛苦不怕难，
    白天逐浪三千里，
    夜渡甘溪九九湾，
    曲尺湖中飞直跑，
    闯过一滩又一滩。
    月光送我到天亮，
    太阳迎我问早安。
        （船员　唐绍裘）
