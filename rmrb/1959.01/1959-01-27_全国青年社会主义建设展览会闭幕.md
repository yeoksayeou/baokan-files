Title: National Youth Socialist Construction Exhibition Closes
Author:
Date: 1959-01-27
Page: 6
Xinhua News Agency, January 26th: The National Youth Socialist Construction Exhibition, sponsored by the Central Committee of the Communist Youth League, closed today.

　　This exhibition, as part of the National Youth Socialist Construction Activists Conference, has been visited by a total of 240,000 people since its opening on November 26th last year. Among them were 200 visiting groups from all over the country and foreign guests from eleven countries including the Soviet Union.

　　The outstanding achievements and communist spirit demonstrated by the youth in the Great Leap Forward of 1958, as reflected in the exhibition, left a deep impression on the audience. Many young people and children regarded the exemplary deeds of Liao Shigang, Zhou Hanhua, Qiu Cai Kang and others exhibited at the exhibition as examples to learn from.

### 全国青年社会主义建设展览会闭幕

1959-01-27
第6版()
专栏：

    全国青年社会主义建设展览会闭幕
    新华社26日讯　共青团中央举办的全国青年社会主义建设展览会，已经在今天闭幕。
    这个展览会作为全国青年社会主义建设积极分子大会的一部分，自去年11月26日开幕以来，先后前来参观的共有二十四万人。其中包括来自全国各地的二百个参观团，和苏联等十一个国家的外宾。
    展览会反映出来的青年们的在1958年大跃进中表现的突出成就和共产主义精神面貌，给观众留下了深刻的印象。许多青、少年观众都把展览会展出的廖世刚、周汉华、丘财康等人的模范事迹当作学习的榜样。
