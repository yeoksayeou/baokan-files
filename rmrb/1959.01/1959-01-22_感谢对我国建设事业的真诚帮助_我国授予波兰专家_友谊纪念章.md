Title: In Gratitude for Sincere Assistance to Our Nation's Construction, China Awards "Friendship Medals" to Polish Experts

Author:

Date: 1959-01-22
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Warsaw, January 20th: Wang Bingnan, China's ambassador to Poland, today presented "Friendship Medals" on behalf of the State Council of China to Polish experts who had helped with China's construction at the Chinese Embassy in Poland.

A total of sixty-eight Polish scientists, professors, engineers, glider instructors, and various other experts were awarded the "Friendship Medal." They had all come to China between 1952 and 1956 to assist in our nation's construction.

### 感谢对我国建设事业的真诚帮助  我国授予波兰专家“友谊纪念章”

1959-01-22
第4版()
专栏：

    感谢对我国建设事业的真诚帮助
    我国授予波兰专家“友谊纪念章”
    新华社华沙20日电　我国驻波兰大使王炳南今天在我国驻波兰大使馆代表我国国务院向曾经帮助中国建设的波兰专家赠送“友谊纪念章”。
    被授予“友谊纪念章”的一共有六十八名波兰科学家、教授、工程师、滑翔机教练以及各种专家。他们都曾经在1952年到1956年期间，先后到中国帮助我国建设。
