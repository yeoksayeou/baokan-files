Title: To Promote the Normalization of Relations Between the Two Countries, Hungary Suggests Hungarian-U.S. Talks
Author:
Date: 1959-01-30
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency, Budapest, January 29th: The "People's Freedom Daily" published today a note from the Hungarian Ministry of Foreign Affairs to the U.S. government. The note pointed out that the facts of U.S. interference in the internal affairs of the Hungarian People's Republic and the anti-Hungarian activities of U.S. open and secret organizations are indisputable.

The note believes that the abnormal state of relations between the two countries caused by the United States should not continue. In order to normalize relations between the two countries, the Hungarian Ministry of Foreign Affairs suggests that the two sides hold diplomatic negotiations.

This note is related to the Hungarian note to the United States on September 20, 1958, and is a response to the U.S. note to Hungary on November 21, 1958.

### 促使两国关系正常化  匈牙利建议匈美举行谈判

1959-01-30
第4版()
专栏：

    促使两国关系正常化
    匈牙利建议匈美举行谈判
    新华社布达佩斯29日电　今天的“人民自由报”刊登了匈牙利外交部给美国政府的一份照会。照会指出，美国干涉匈牙利人民共和国内政的阴谋以及美国公开和秘密组织的反匈活动的事实是不容置辩的。
    照会认为由美国造成的两国关系不正常的局面，不应继续下去。为了使两国关系正常化，匈牙利外交部建议双方举行外交谈判。
    这个照会是与1958年9月20日匈牙利给美国的照会有关的，并且是对美国1958年11月21日给匈牙利的照会的答复。
