Title: List of Delegates of the Government of the German Democratic Republic
Author:
Date: 1959-01-23
Page: 2

Xinhua News Agency, January 22nd - The list of delegates of the Government of the German Democratic Republic:

    Otto Grotewohl, Prime Minister of the German Democratic Republic, Member of the Politburo of the Central Committee of the Socialist Unity Party of Germany, Head of the Delegation
    Mrs. Johanna Grotewohl, Wife of the Prime Minister of the German Democratic Republic
    Dr. Lothar Bolz, Deputy Prime Minister and Minister of Foreign Affairs, Chairman of the National Democratic Party, Member of the Delegation
    Sepp Schwab, Deputy Minister of Foreign Affairs, Member of the Delegation
    Gerhard Weiss, Deputy Minister of Foreign and Intra-German Trade, Member of the Delegation
    Professor Manfred von Ardenne, Member of the Scientific Committee for Peaceful Uses of Atomic Energy of the Government of the German Democratic Republic, Member of the Advisory Committee for Natural Science and Technology Research and Development of the German Democratic Republic, Member of the Delegation
    Dr Paul Wandel, Ambassador Extraordinary and Plenipotentiary of the German Democratic Republic to the People's of Republic of China, Member of the Delegation.
