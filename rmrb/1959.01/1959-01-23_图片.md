Title: He Guozheng
Author: He Guozheng
Date: 1959-01-23
Page: 3

    The various ethnic groups in the Yao Autonomous County of Dayao Mountain in Guangxi are vigorously engaged in indigenous copper smelting. In a short period, they have built 417 indigenous blast furnaces, which have already been put into production. Xinhua News Agency reporter He Guozheng photographed.
