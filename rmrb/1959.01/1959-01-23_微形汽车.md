	Leap Forward Showcase

    Micro Cars

    The Shanghai Micro Car Factory has recently begun production.

    The Feiyue (Leap Forward) brand micro car produced by this factory is intended to replace human-powered tricycles. This car is small, nimble, and easy to drive, only slightly wider than a tricycle, and can carry four people (including the driver). The entire car weighs only over 400 kilograms, with a maximum speed of 50 kilometers per hour. It can travel on city alleys and ordinary rural roads, consuming only 5 liters of fuel per 100 kilometers, making transportation costs cheaper than human power. (Attached picture)

### 微形汽车

1959-01-23
第3版()
专栏：跃进橱窗

    微形汽车
    上海微形汽车厂最近已投入生产。
    这个厂生产的飞跃牌微形汽车，是用来代替人力三轮车的。这种汽车小巧灵便，易驾驶，体积只比三轮车稍宽，可乘四人
  （连司机）。全车重量只四百多公斤，最高车速可达五十公里，能在城市小巷及乡村一般路上行驶，每百公里耗油仅五公升，车费比人力还便宜。（附图片）
    “飞跃”牌微形汽车    新华社记者　霍生联摄
