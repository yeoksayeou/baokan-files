Title: Cheers for the Launch of the Giant Cosmic Rocket
Author: Guo Moruo
Date: 1959-01-04
Page: 2

    Cheers for the Launch of the Giant Cosmic Rocket
    Guo Moruo
    The Soviet Union has launched a giant cosmic rocket which will reach the lunar region at noon Beijing time on January 4th, establishing another scientific base in the universe. This is the biggest New Year's gift for 1959. It is a resounding salute to the Soviet Union's commencement of the magnificent Seven-Year Plan to build communism.
    The era of humanity crawling on Earth has begun to end, and a new era of traveling into space and further conquering nature has arrived. This should broaden the minds of humanity to an infinite extent.
    All of humanity will cheer. All of humanity will hope that the champions who persist in the Cold War policy can also be liberated from the fate of reptiles and open their minds wider. That would be helpful for the happiness of mankind.
    I express my heartfelt congratulations to the great Soviet Party, the Soviet people, and Soviet scientists.
    I express my heartfelt congratulations for the happiness of all humanity.

### 欢呼巨型宇宙火箭上天
郭沫若
1959-01-04
第2版()
专栏：

    欢呼巨型宇宙火箭上天
    郭沫若
    苏联发射了巨型宇宙火箭，将于1月4日北京时间的正午到达月球区域，在宇宙中又建立了一个科学基地。这是献给1959年最大的新年礼品。是苏联开始执行雄伟的建设共产主义七年计划的响亮的礼炮。
    人类开始离开了在地球上爬行的时代，向太空中旅行、进一步征服自然的新时代到来了。这应该使人类的心胸开扩到无限广大。
    全人类都会欢呼。全人类都会希望坚持冷战政策的豪杰们能够也由爬虫类的命运解放出来，把理想放开朗一些。那对人类的幸福是会有所帮助的。
    向伟大的苏联的党、苏联人民和苏联科学家表示衷心的祝贺。
    为全人类的幸福表示衷心的祝贺。
