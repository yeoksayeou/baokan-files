Title: A Letter from a Romanian Reader - Addressed to the Amateur Performance Troupe of Revolutionary Disabled Soldiers of Sichuan Province, China
Author: Jī bó Yuē áng (基伯·约昂)
Date: 1959-01-11
Page: 8

A Letter from a Romanian Reader
    ——Addressed to the Amateur Performance Troupe of Revolutionary Disabled Soldiers of Sichuan Province, China
    Dear Comrades:
    First, allow me to introduce myself. My name is Jī bó Yuē áng, and I am a student in the History Department of the Bahon University in Bucharest, Romanian People's Republic.
    Some time ago, the Bucharest University Students' Cultural Palace organized a Chinese Film Evening. At this event, the film "The Strongest People," documenting your activities, was screened. It was through this film that I saw you and came to know you.
    I was deeply moved, not only by your strong will to overcome difficulties but also by your spirit of high revolutionary optimism. From you, I gained a deeper understanding of the Chinese people. You are outstanding children of our socialist family and a model for tens of thousands of us to learn from.
    Dear comrades, let us shake hands tightly, and allow me to wish you even greater achievements in your work!
            Most affectionately yours, Jī bó Yuē áng, Bucharest

### 一位罗马尼亚观众的来信——寄给中国四川省革命残废军人业余演出队
基伯·约昂　布加勒斯特
1959-01-11
第8版()
专栏：观众的信

    一位罗马尼亚观众的来信
    ——寄给中国四川省革命残废军人业余演出队亲爱的同志们：
    首先我想自我介绍一下。我的名字叫基伯·约昂，是罗马尼亚人民共和国布加勒斯特市巴宏大学历史系的学生。
    前些时候，布加勒斯特大学生文化宫组织了一个中国电影晚会。在这个晚会上，放映了纪录你们的活动的影片“最坚强的人”。就是从这部影片里面，我看到了你们，认识了你们。
    我深深地感动了，不仅因为你们所具有的那勇于克服困难的坚强的意志，也因为你们所具有的那种高度的革命乐观主义的精神。从你们身上，我进一步认识了中国人民。你们是我们的社会主义大家庭的优秀的儿女，是我们几万万人学习的榜样。
    亲爱的同志们，让我们紧紧地握手，让我预祝你们在工作中取得更大的成绩吧！
            最热爱你们的基伯·约昂　布加勒斯特
