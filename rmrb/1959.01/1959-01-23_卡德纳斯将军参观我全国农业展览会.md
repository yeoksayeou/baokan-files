Title: General Cárdenas Visits Our National Agricultural Exhibition
Author:
Date: 1959-01-23
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, 21st - General Lázaro Cárdenas, former President of Mexico and Vice-Chairman of the World Peace Council, and his entourage, accompanied by Guo Moruo, Chairman of the Chinese People's Committee for World Peace, and others, visited the National Agricultural Exhibition on the afternoon of the 21st.

The distinguished guests spent nearly two hours visiting the exhibition halls for comprehensive exhibits, as well as grain, cotton, hemp, oilseed specialties, animal husbandry and aquatic products, increased production measures, plant protection, and forestry. After the visit, General Cárdenas left an inscription for the exhibition, writing: "Having visited the agricultural exhibition today, we have seen the will, enthusiasm, and patriotism of the Chinese people, as well as the great efforts made in developing production and technology. This is a very good example for their people. We are glad to have this opportunity to personally understand the efforts they have made. We will take back their touching and sincere friendship as a reward."

When General Cárdenas shook hands with Wei Zhenwu, the chief curator of the exhibition, and others to say goodbye, he congratulated China on achieving greater success in developing agricultural production.

In the evening, the Chinese People's Committee for World Peace held a song and dance Peking opera evening to welcome all the Mexican friends headed by General Cárdenas.

### 卡德纳斯将军参观我全国农业展览会

1959-01-23
第5版()
专栏：

    卡德纳斯将军参观我全国农业展览会
    新华社21日讯　墨西哥前总统、世界和平理事会副主席卡德纳斯将军和他的随行人员，由中国人民保卫世界和平委员会主席郭沫若等陪同，21日下午参观了全国农业展览会。
    贵宾们用了将近两个小时的时间，参观了展览会的综合馆和粮食、棉麻、油料特产、畜牧水产、增产措施、植物保护、林业等展览馆。卡德纳斯将军在参观结束后给展览会留了题词，他写道：“在今天参观了农业展览会以后，我们看到了中国人民的意志、热情和爱国主义，以及在发展生产和技术方面所作的巨大努力。这是对于他们人民的很好的榜样。我们庆幸能有这个机会来亲自了解他们所作的努力。我们将把他们的动人的、诚恳的友谊，作为一种奖励带回去。”
    卡德纳斯将军在同展览会总馆长魏震五等握手告别的时候，他祝贺中国在发展农业生产方面获得更大的成就。
    晚间，中国人民保卫世界和平委员会举行歌舞京剧晚会，欢迎以卡德纳斯将军为首的全体墨西哥朋友。
