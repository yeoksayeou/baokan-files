Look, how robust our wheat is!
In YingShan County, Sichuan Province, all the people's communes are implementing a high-yield field record system. Detailed records are kept of the complete set of cultivation techniques and management measures from planting to harvesting, in order to systematically summarize the experience of high yields. This is a wheat team member from the Satellite People's Commune thinning and keeping seedlings in the wheat field. Photo by Zhang Qicai (Xinhua News Agency draft)

### 看，我们的小麦多茁壮！（图片）
张齐才
1959-01-23
第4版()
专栏：

    看，我们的小麦多茁壮！
    四川省营山县各人民公社在推行高产田档案制，从种到收全套耕种技术、管理措施都进行详细记载，以便系统地总结丰产经验。这是卫星公社小麦专业队队员在麦田里进行定苗留株。张齐才摄（新华社稿）
