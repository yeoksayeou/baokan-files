Title: American New Car Designs (Pictures)
Author: Jiang Yousheng, Yi Heyuan
Date: 1959-01-21
Page: 8

American New Car Designs
Jiang Yousheng (drawing), Yi Heyuan (poetry)

    Tiger Skin Style
    Tiger skin covers the car, design with painstaking effort,
    Riding around ostentatiously, relying entirely on appearance to scare people.

    Nixon Style
    Armor is extremely strong,
    "Gifts" can also be stored.
    Specifically for foreign travel,
    Safety is greatly assured.

    Dulles Style
    Black glass headlights,
    Specifically for carrying blind and foolish people,
    Everything does not exist,
    The world revolves only around me.

    Prosperity Style
    Equipped with a loud bell,
    Specifically for emergency use,
    Although on the verge of death,
    Still have to boast of prosperity!

    Water Tank Style
    Water tank trailer is really wonderful,
    Best for launching rockets,
    Guaranteeing safety first,
    Not afraid of rockets falling!

### 美国新式汽车设计（图片）
江有生  易和元
1959-01-21
第8版()
专栏：

    美国新式汽车设计
    江有生画　易和元　配诗
    虎皮式
    虎皮蒙上车身，设计煞费苦心，
    坐上招摇过市，全靠外表吓人。
    尼克松式
    装甲坚固非常，
    “礼品”也够存放。
    国外旅行专用，
    安全甚有保障。
    杜勒斯式
    黑色玻璃车灯，
    专载盲人蠢人，
    一切都不存在，
    天下唯我独尊。
    繁荣式
    备有强音大钟，
    专供急救之用，
    尽管奄奄一息，
    还得吹嘘繁荣！
    水箱式
    水箱拖车真妙，
    发射火箭最好，
    保证安全第一，
    不怕火箭下掉！
