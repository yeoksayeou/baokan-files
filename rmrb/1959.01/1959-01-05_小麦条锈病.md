    小麦条锈病
    Wheat Stripe Rust
    小麦条锈病又叫黄锈病，是为害小麦的三种锈病中的一种。条锈病是为害冬小麦最厉害的病害，由于它的蔓延，可使小麦严重减产甚至颗粒无收。一般防治条锈病的办法是：1、选择抗病品种；2、加强栽培管理，提高小麦抗病力；3、喷药防治；4、消灭地边自生麦和杂草。
    Wheat stripe rust, also known as yellow rust, is one of the three types of rust diseases that affect wheat. Stripe rust is the most serious disease affecting winter wheat, and its spread can lead to severe reductions in wheat production, even complete crop failure. The general methods for preventing and controlling stripe rust are: 1. Select disease-resistant varieties; 2. Strengthen cultivation management to improve wheat's disease resistance; 3. Spray with pesticides for prevention and control; 4. Eliminate self-seeded wheat and weeds along field edges.
