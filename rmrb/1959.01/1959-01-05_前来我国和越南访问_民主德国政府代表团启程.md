Title: East German Government Delegation Departs for Visits to Our Country and Vietnam
Author:
Date: 1959-01-05
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency, Hanoi, January 4th: The government delegation of the German Democratic Republic, headed by Prime Minister Grotewohl, left here this morning for visits to China and Vietnam. Grotewohl will first travel to Cairo as a guest of President Nasser.

First Secretary of the Central Committee of the German Socialist United Party and First Deputy Prime Minister of the German Democratic Republic, Ulbricht, Chairman of the Presidium of the People's Chamber, Dieckmann, and other government and democratic party leaders went to the airport to see off the delegation. Chargé d'affaires ad interim of the Chinese Embassy in the German Democratic Republic, Xu Ming, Ambassador of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam, Pham Ngoc Thuan, and other diplomatic personnel also went to the airport to see them off.

### 前来我国和越南访问  民主德国政府代表团启程

1959-01-05
第4版()
专栏：

    前来我国和越南访问
    民主德国政府代表团启程
    新华社河内4日电　以格罗提渥总理为首的德意志民主共和国政府代表团今天上午离开这里前往中国和越南访问。格罗提渥将作为纳赛尔总统的客人首先前往开罗。
    德国统一社会党中央委员会第一书记、德意志民主共和国第一副总理乌布利希、人民议院主席团主席狄克曼以及其他政府和民主党派领袖前往机场欢送代表团。中国驻德意志民主共和国大使馆临时代办徐明、越南民主共和国大使范玉纯以及其他外交界人士也到机场欢送。
