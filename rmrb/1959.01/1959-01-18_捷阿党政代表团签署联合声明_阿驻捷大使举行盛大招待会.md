Title: Czechoslovak-Albanian Party and Government Delegations Sign Joint Statement; Albanian Ambassador to Czechoslovakia Holds Grand Reception
Author:
Date: 1959-01-18
Page: 3
Xinhua News Agency, Prague, 16th - After the talks between the Czechoslovak and Albanian Party and Government delegations, a joint statement was signed here this afternoon.

Representing Czechoslovakia, the joint statement was signed by Antonín Novotný, First Secretary of the Central Committee of the Czechoslovak Communist Party, and Prime Minister Viliam Široký. Representing Albania, the statement was signed by Enver Hoxha, First Secretary of the Central Committee of the Albanian Labor Party, and Mehmet Shehu, Chairman of the Council of Ministers.

Xinhua News Agency, Prague, 16th - Albanian Ambassador to Czechoslovakia, Siri Carcani, held a grand reception here tonight for the Albanian Party and Government delegation headed by Hoxha's visit to Czechoslovakia.

Albanian Chairman of the Council of Ministers, Mehmet Shehu, emphasized in his speech at the reception that the unity of the socialist camp is the foundation for the victorious construction of socialist countries. He condemned the attempts by imperialism and the Yugoslav revisionists who serve imperialism to weaken this unity.

Czechoslovak Prime Minister Viliam Široký, in his speech, pointed out that the joint statement of the two countries' Party and Government delegations demonstrates that the two countries are faithful to Marxist doctrine and the socialist camp headed by the Soviet Union. He said that under the leadership of the Labor Party, which is loyal to the ideas and path of the October Revolution, Albania is a staunch member of the socialist camp.

Attending tonight's reception were Antonín Novotný, First Secretary of the Central Committee of the Czechoslovak Communist Party and President, other party and government leaders, as well as diplomatic envoys from various countries in Prague.

Xinhua News Agency, Prague, 17th - The Albanian Party and Government delegation left Prague by train this morning to return home. Those who went to the station to see off the delegation included: Antonín Novotný, First Secretary of the Central Committee of the Czechoslovak Communist Party and President of Czechoslovakia, Prime Minister Viliam Široký, as well as other government and party leaders.

In his farewell speech, Prime Minister Viliam Široký emphasized that the results of the talks between the two countries' Party and Government delegations are conducive to strengthening the friendship between the two countries and the unity of the socialist camp. He said that the two countries will make every effort to smash all the conspiracies of the imperialists and contribute to the realization of the Soviet Union's peace proposals.

In his reply, the head of the Albanian delegation, Hoxha, pointed out that the two countries are working together to enhance their brotherly relations, strengthen the unity of the international workers' movement, and oppose all attacks by imperialists and modern revisionists.

### 捷阿党政代表团签署联合声明  阿驻捷大使举行盛大招待会

1959-01-18
第3版()
专栏：

    捷阿党政代表团签署联合声明
    阿驻捷大使举行盛大招待会
    新华社布拉格16日电　捷克斯洛伐克和阿尔巴尼亚两国党政代表团会谈结束后，今天下午在这里签署了一项联合声明。
    代表捷克斯洛伐克方面在联合声明上签字的是捷克斯洛伐克共产党中央委员会第一书记诺沃提尼和政府总理西罗基；代表阿尔巴尼亚方面签字的是阿尔巴尼亚劳动党中央委员会第一书记霍查和政府部长会议主席谢胡。
    新华社布拉格16日电　阿尔巴尼亚驻捷克斯洛伐克大使西里·查尔查尼，为以霍查为首的阿尔巴尼亚党政代表团访问捷克斯洛伐克，今晚在这里举行了盛大的招待会。
    阿尔巴尼亚部长会议主席谢胡在会上致词时强调说，社会主义阵营的团结是社会主义国家建设胜利的基础。他谴责了帝国主义和服务于帝国主义的南斯拉夫修正主义者削弱这一团结的企图。
    捷克斯洛伐克总理西罗基在讲话中指出，两国党政代表团的联合声明，表明两国忠实于马克思主义学说和以苏联为首的社会主义阵营。他说，在忠于十月革命思想和道路的劳动党领导下，阿尔巴尼亚是社会主义阵营中的坚定的一员。
    出席今晚招待会的有捷克斯洛伐克共产党中央委员会第一书记、总统诺沃提尼和其他党政领导人，以及各国驻布拉格外交使节等。
    新华社布拉格17日电　阿尔巴尼亚党政代表团今天上午乘火车离开布拉格返国。前往车站欢送代表团的有：捷克斯洛伐克共产党中央委员会第一书记、捷克斯洛伐克总统诺沃提尼、西罗基总理以及政府和党的其他领导人。
    西罗基总理在致欢送词时强调指出，两国党政代表团会谈的结果有利于加强两国的友谊和社会主义阵营的团结。他说，两国将尽一切努力来粉碎帝国主义者的一切阴谋以及为实现苏联的和平建议作出贡献。
    阿尔巴尼亚代表团团长霍查在致答词时指出，两国正在共同努力来增进相互间的兄弟般的关系，加强国际工人运动的团结和反对帝国主义者和现代修正主义者的一切进攻。
