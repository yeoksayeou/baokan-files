Title: Mikoyan Vacations in the United States, Meets with Danish Prime Minister During Stopover in Copenhagen
Author:
Date: 1959-01-05
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, January 3rd -- TASS reported: Today, First Deputy Chairman of the Soviet Council of Ministers, Mikoyan, flew to the United States. In the United States, Mikoyan will spend part of his vacation as a guest of the Soviet Ambassador.

Xinhua News Agency, Copenhagen, January 4th -- TASS reported: First Deputy Chairman of the Soviet Council of Ministers, Mikoyan, passed through Copenhagen on January 3rd en route from Moscow to the United States.

At the Copenhagen airport, Mikoyan delivered a speech. He spoke of the long-standing friendship between the Soviet and Danish peoples, and congratulated the Danish people on their achievements and happiness in 1959. He said that the Danish people, like the Soviet people, are striving to maintain peace and ease international tensions. All peace-loving people yearn to use modern scientific and technological achievements to further enrich the material and spiritual lives of mankind. Our two countries—Denmark and the Soviet Union—can make significant contributions to achieving these lofty goals.

During his stay in Copenhagen, Mikoyan visited Danish Prime Minister Hansen and held talks with him.

Hansen hosted a banquet to welcome Mikoyan.

Xinhua News Agency, New York, January 4th -- TASS reported from New York: First Deputy Chairman of the Soviet Council of Ministers, Mikoyan, arrived here today en route to Washington. Welcoming Mikoyan at the New York airport were the Soviet Ambassador to the United States, Menshikov, the Soviet Permanent Representative to the United Nations, Sobolev, heads of various delegations, State Department officials, and many reporters from newspapers, magazines, radio stations, and television stations.

At the airport, Mikoyan delivered a brief speech to reporters from newspapers and radio stations.

### 米高扬到美国度假  途经哥本哈根时和丹麦首相会谈

1959-01-05
第4版()
专栏：

    米高扬到美国度假
    途经哥本哈根时和丹麦首相会谈
    新华社3日讯　据塔斯社莫斯科讯：今天苏联部长会议第一副主席米高扬乘飞机前往美国。在美国米高扬将作为苏联大使的客人度过自己的部分假期。
    新华社4日讯　据塔斯社哥本哈根讯：苏联部长会议第一副主席米高扬由莫斯科去美国，3日路过哥本哈根。
    在哥本哈根机场上，米高扬发表了谈话。他谈到苏联人民同丹麦人民悠久的友谊，并且祝贺丹麦人民在1959年获得成就和幸福。他说，丹麦人民和苏联人民一样，正在竭力维护和平和缓和国际紧张局势。一切爱好和平的人民都渴望用现代的科学技术成就来使人类的物质和精神生活进一步繁荣。我们两国——丹麦和苏联——可以为实现这些崇高的目的作出重大的贡献。
    在哥本哈根停留期间，米高扬拜访了丹麦首相汉森，并且和他进行了会谈。
    汉森设宴欢迎米高扬。
    新华社4日讯　塔斯社纽约4日讯：苏联部长会议第一副主席米高扬在去华盛顿途中今天到达这里。在纽约机场上欢迎米高扬的有苏联驻美国大使缅希科夫、苏联驻联合国常任代表索波列夫、各国代表团负责人、国务院的官员和报纸、杂志、广播电台和电视台的许多记者。
    米高扬在机场上向报纸和广播电台的记者发表了简短的谈话。
