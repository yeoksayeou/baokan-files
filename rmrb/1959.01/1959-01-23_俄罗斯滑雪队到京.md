Title: Russian Ski Team Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1959-01-23
Page: 5
    Xinhua News Agency, January 21st. The Soviet Russian Federation ski team, a group of five, arrived in Beijing by plane on the 21st. This is the first sports team from a fraternal country to visit China in 1959 at the invitation of China, and also the first ski team from a fraternal country to visit China.

    The Russian ski team will leave Beijing for Jilin on the 24th. They will participate in ski performances at the 1959 National Winter Games of China, which will begin on February 1st.

### 俄罗斯滑雪队到京

1959-01-23
第5版()
专栏：

    俄罗斯滑雪队到京
    据新华社21日讯　苏联俄罗斯联邦共和国滑雪队一行五人，21日乘飞机到达北京。这是1959年应邀来中国访问的第一个兄弟国家运动队，也是第一个来中国访问的兄弟国家滑雪队。
    俄罗斯滑雪队将在24日离开北京去吉林。他们将在2月1日开始举行的我国1959年全国冬季运动会上，参加滑雪表演。
