Title: Chairman Mao Zedong Receives Premier Grotewohl
Author:
Date: 1959-01-28
Page: 1

    Xinhua News Agency, January 27th – Chairman Mao Zedong received this morning the head of the government delegation of the German Democratic Republic, Premier Otto Grotewohl, and all members of the delegation: Deputy Premier and Minister of Foreign Affairs Lothar Bolz, Deputy Minister Sepp Schwab, Deputy Minister Gerhard Weiss, Professor Manfred von Ardenne, and Ambassador Paul Wandel.
    Present at the reception were: Chairman Liu Shaoqi, Vice-Chairman Peng Zhen, Vice-Premier and Minister of Foreign Affairs Chen Yi, Vice Minister of Foreign Affairs Zeng Yongquan, and Chinese Ambassador to the German Democratic Republic Wang Guoquan. (Attached picture)
    Chairman Mao Zedong receives the government delegation of the German Democratic Republic. Chairman Mao shakes hands with Premier Grotewohl.
  Xinhua News Agency reporter Hou Bo photographed

### 毛主席接见格罗提渥总理

1959-01-28
第1版()
专栏：

    毛主席接见格罗提渥总理
    新华社27日讯　毛泽东主席今天上午接见了德意志民主共和国政府代表团团长奥托·格罗提渥总理和代表团全体团员：洛塔·博尔茨副总理兼外交部长，塞普·施瓦布副部长，格哈德·魏斯副部长，曼弗雷德·冯·阿尔登内教授，保罗·汪戴尔大使。
    接见时在座的有：刘少奇委员长，彭真副委员长，陈毅副总理兼外交部长，外交部副部长曾涌泉，中国驻德意志民主共和国大使王国权。（附图片）
    毛泽东主席接见德意志民主共和国政府代表团。毛主席和格罗提渥总理握手。
