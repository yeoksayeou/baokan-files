Title: Chairman Mao and Premier Zhou Send Congratulations on Indian National Day
Author:
Date: 1959-01-26
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, January 25th: Chairman Mao Zedong today sent a telegram to the President of the Republic of India, Prasad, congratulating him on the National Day of the Republic of India. The telegram reads as follows: To His Excellency Rajendra Prasad, President of the Republic of India:

    On the occasion of your country's National Day, I would like to express my sincere congratulations to Your Excellency and the great Indian people on behalf of the Chinese people. May the close friendship between the people of China and India be further consolidated and developed. I wish the Indian people happiness and the country prosperity.
      Mao Zedong, Chairman of the People's Republic of China
      January 25, 1959

Xinhua News Agency, January 25th: Premier Zhou Enlai today sent a telegram to the Prime Minister of the Republic of India, Nehru, congratulating him on the National Day of the Republic of India. The telegram reads as follows: To His Excellency Jawaharlal Nehru, Prime Minister of the Republic of India:

    On the occasion of the ninth anniversary of the National Day of the Republic of India, I would like to extend my warm greetings to Your Excellency and your government on behalf of the government of the People's Republic of China and also in my own name.

    The Chinese people are pleased to see that the traditional friendship between the people of China and India has been continuously consolidated and developed on the basis of the Five Principles of Peaceful Coexistence. I firmly believe that the economic and cultural ties between China and India will continue to strengthen, and that the two countries will continue to cooperate closely in easing international tensions and promoting peace in Asia and the world. I wish the Republic of India prosperity and strength, and I wish Your Excellency new achievements in seeking the happiness of the Indian people and safeguarding world peace.
    Zhou Enlai, Premier of the State Council of the People's Republic of China
            January 25, 1959

### 毛主席　周总理电贺印度国庆

1959-01-26
第1版()
专栏：

    毛主席　周总理电贺印度国庆
    新华社25日讯　毛泽东主席今天致电印度共和国总统普拉沙德，祝贺印度共和国国庆。电文如下：印度共和国总统拉金德拉·普拉沙德阁下：
    欣逢贵国国庆，我谨代表中国人民向阁下和伟大的印度人民表示衷心的祝贺。祝中印两国人民之间的亲密友谊日益巩固和发展。并祝印度人民幸福，国家繁荣。
      中华人民共和国主席  毛泽东
      一九五九年一月二十五日
    新华社25日讯　周恩来总理今天致电印度共和国总理尼赫鲁，祝贺印度共和国国庆。电文如下：印度共和国总理贾瓦哈拉尔·尼赫鲁阁下：
    值此印度共和国国庆九周年之际，我谨代表中华人民共和国政府，并且以我个人的名义向阁下和贵国政府致热烈的祝贺。
    中国人民高兴地看到，中印两国人民间的传统友谊在和平共处五项原则的基础上不断巩固和发展。我坚决相信中印两国在经济、文化方面的联系将不断地加强，并且将在缓和国际紧张局势和促进亚洲及世界和平的事业中继续密切地合作。祝印度共和国繁荣富强，祝阁下在谋求印度人民幸福和维护世界和平的事业中获得新的成就。
    中华人民共和国国务院总理　周恩来
            一九五九年一月二十五日
