Title: Asian and Pacific Regions Peace Liaison Committee Sends Telegram to Cuban Government Condemning U.S. Interference, Supporting Cuba's Just Struggle
Author:
Date: 1959-01-27
Page: 5
Xinhua News Agency, 25th: The Asian and Pacific Regions Peace Liaison Committee today sent a telegram to the Cuban Provisional Government, congratulating the Cuban people on their great victory in their struggle for independence and freedom and the establishment of the Cuban Provisional Government, and supporting the Cuban people's struggle against U.S. interference. The text of the telegram is as follows:
    To: The Cuban Provisional Government, Havana, Cuba

    The Asian and Pacific Regions Peace Liaison Committee expresses its warm congratulations to the Cuban people on their great victory in the struggle for independence and freedom and the establishment of the Cuban Provisional Government.

    The victory of the Cuban people has dealt a heavy blow to the forces of war and aggression and is a major contribution to the cause of world peace.

    U.S. interventionists are violating the sacred rights of the Cuban people, threatening the peace and security of the Caribbean and Latin American regions. We condemn this aggressive act of creating tension.

    All peace-loving and freedom-loving people around the world stand with the Cuban people. The Asian and Pacific Regions Peace Liaison Committee deeply sympathizes with and supports the Cuban people's struggle against U.S. interference, to defend independence and peace, and deeply believes that the just cause of the Cuban people will achieve new victories.

        The Asian and Pacific Regions Peace Liaison Committee
                              January 25, 1959

### 亚洲及太平洋区域和平联络委员会致电古巴政府  谴责美国干涉行为　支持古巴正义斗争

1959-01-27
第5版()
专栏：

    亚洲及太平洋区域和平联络委员会致电古巴政府
    谴责美国干涉行为　支持古巴正义斗争
    新华社25日讯　亚洲及太平洋区域和平联络委员会今天致电古巴临时政府，祝贺古巴人民争取独立自由取得的伟大胜利和古巴临时政府的成立，并支持古巴人民反对美国干涉的斗争。电文如下：古巴　哈瓦那古巴临时政府
    亚洲及太平洋区域和平联络委员会对古巴人民争取独立自由的伟大胜利和古巴临时政府的成立，表示热烈庆贺。
    古巴人民的胜利，给战争侵略势力以沉重打击，是对世界和平事业的一个重大贡献。
    美国干涉者侵犯古巴人民的神圣权利，威胁着加勒比海和拉丁美洲地区的和平和安全。我们谴责这种制造紧张局势的侵略行为。
    全世界爱好和平与自由的人们都和古巴人民站在一起。亚洲及太平洋区域和平联络委员会深切同情和支持古巴人民反对美国干涉、保卫独立和和平的斗争，并深信古巴人民的正义事业，将获得新的胜利。
        亚洲及太平洋区域和平联络委员会
                              1959年1月25日
