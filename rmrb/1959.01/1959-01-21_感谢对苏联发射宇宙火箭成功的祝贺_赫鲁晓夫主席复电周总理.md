Title: Khrushchev Replies to Premier Zhou, Thanking Him for Congratulations on the Successful Launch of the Cosmic Rocket by the Soviet Union
Author:
Date: 1959-01-21
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, 20th. Premier Zhou Enlai received a telegram from Chairman Khrushchev of the Soviet Council of Ministers. In the telegram, Chairman Khrushchev expressed his gratitude to Premier Zhou Enlai for congratulating the Soviet Union on January 5th on behalf of the Chinese people and the Chinese government for the successful launch of the first cosmic rocket by mankind, and for giving extremely high evaluation to the achievements of Soviet science and technology. Chairman Khrushchev's telegram is as follows: To Comrade Zhou Enlai, Premier of the State Council of the People's Republic of China, Dear Comrade Zhou Enlai:

    I sincerely thank you for your warm congratulations on behalf of the people and government of the People's Republic of China to the Soviet people and the Soviet government and your high appraisal of the achievements of Soviet science and technology at the time of the Soviet Union's launch of the first cosmic rocket in human history. Dear Comrade Zhou Enlai, I fully agree with your view that the Soviet Union's launch of a cosmic rocket into interplanetary space will promote human progress and strengthen peace among the peoples of all countries.

                Chairman of the Soviet Council of Ministers, Khrushchev
                        January 19, 1959
                          At the Moscow Kremlin

### 感谢对苏联发射宇宙火箭成功的祝贺  赫鲁晓夫主席复电周总理

1959-01-21
第1版()
专栏：

    感谢对苏联发射宇宙火箭成功的祝贺
    赫鲁晓夫主席复电周总理
    新华社20日讯　周恩来总理收到了苏联部长会议主席赫鲁晓夫的一份电报。赫鲁晓夫主席在电报中对周恩来总理1月5日代表中国人民和中国政府祝贺苏联成功地发射出人类第一个宇宙火箭表示感谢。赫鲁晓夫主席的来电如下：北京中华人民共和国国务院总理周恩来同志，亲爱的周恩来同志：
    在苏联发射人类历史上第一个宇宙火箭的时候，承你代表中华人民共和国人民和政府向苏联人民和苏联政府表示热诚的祝贺并对苏联科学和技术的成就给予极高的评价，谨表示衷心的感谢。亲爱的周恩来同志，我完全同意你的看法：苏联向行星际空间发射宇宙火箭，将促进人类的进步和加强各国人民之间的和平。
                苏联部长会议主席　赫鲁晓夫
                        一九五九年一月十九日
                          于莫斯科克里姆林宫
