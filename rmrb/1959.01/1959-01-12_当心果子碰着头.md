Title: Watch Out for the Fruit Hitting Your Head
Author: Yang Yongqing
Date: 1959-01-12
Page: 8

Watch Out for the Fruit Hitting Your Head (Anhui Folk Song)

　　Yesterday the hillside was bare,
　　Today apricot, peach, and pomegranate are planted.
　　After a few more springs and autumns,
　　Trunks will be tall, branches green, and fruit will form balls.
　　When children come to play then,
　　Watch out for the fruit hitting your head.

　　Yang Yongqing Woodcut

### 当心果子碰着头
杨永青
1959-01-12
第8版()
专栏：

    当心果子碰着头（安徽民歌）
    昨天山边光溜溜，
  　今天栽起杏桃榴。
    再过几个春和秋， 　 干高枝绿果成球。
    那时来玩的小朋友， 　 当心果子碰着头。
    杨永青　木刻
