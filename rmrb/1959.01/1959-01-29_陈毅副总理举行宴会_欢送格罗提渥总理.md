Title: Vice Premier Chen Yi Holds Banquet to See Off Prime Minister Grotewohl
Author:
Date: 1959-01-29
Page: 1

    Xinhua News Agency, January 28th. Vice Premier Chen Yi hosted a banquet at noon today to bid farewell to Prime Minister Otto Grotewohl and his wife, as well as other distinguished guests from the government delegation of the German Democratic Republic.
    Among those present were Vice Premier Bo Yibo, Vice Minister of Foreign Affairs Zeng Yongquan, Chinese Ambassador to the German Democratic Republic Wang Guoquan, and President of the Sino-German Democratic Republic Friendship Association Zhu Kezhen.
    This morning, Prime Minister Grotewohl and his wife, along with other distinguished guests, toured the Ming Tombs scenic area in the outskirts of Beijing, accompanied by Vice Minister Zeng Yongquan, Ambassador Wang Guoquan, and others.
    Xinhua News Agency, January 28th. The All-China Journalists Association and the Information Department of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs jointly held a reception this evening to entertain the nine journalists who accompanied the government delegation of the German Democratic Republic on their visit to our country.
    At the reception, representatives from news organizations in the capital, along with their colleagues from the fraternal country, celebrated the further development of friendship between the two peoples and the close cooperation between journalists of the two countries.

### 陈毅副总理举行宴会  欢送格罗提渥总理

1959-01-29
第1版()
专栏：

    陈毅副总理举行宴会
    欢送格罗提渥总理
    新华社28日讯　陈毅副总理今天中午设宴欢送奥托·格罗提渥总理和他的夫人，以及德意志民主共和国政府代表团的其他贵宾们。
    出席作陪的，有薄一波副总理，外交部副部长曾涌泉，中国驻德意志民主共和国大使王国权，中国德意志民主共和国友好协会会长竺可桢。
    今天上午，格罗提渥总理和他的夫人与其他贵宾，由曾涌泉副部长和王国权大使等陪同，游览了京郊十三陵风景区。
    新华社28日讯　中华全国新闻工作者协会和外交部新闻司今晚联合举行酒会，招待随同德意志民主共和国政府代表团来我国访问的九位新闻工作者。
    酒会上，首都各新闻单位的代表同来自兄弟之邦的同业们一起，欢庆两国人民友谊的进一步发展和两国新闻工作者的亲密合作。
