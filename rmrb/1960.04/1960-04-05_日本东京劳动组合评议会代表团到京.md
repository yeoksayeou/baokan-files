Title: Japanese Tokyo Labor Union Council Delegation Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1960-04-05
Page: 6

    Xinhua News Agency, April 4th. The Japanese Tokyo Regional Labor Union Council delegation, led by Chairman Okamato U太郎, consisting of seven members, arrived in Beijing by plane today. The delegation is visiting China at the invitation of the Beijing Federation of Trade Unions. Wang Jiong, chairman of the Beijing Federation of Trade Unions, and others were at the airport to welcome them.

### 日本东京劳动组合评议会代表团到京

1960-04-05
第6版()
专栏：

    日本东京劳动组合评议会代表团到京
    新华社4日讯　由冈本丑太郎团长率领的日本东京地方劳动组合评议会访华代表团一行七人，今天乘飞机到达北京。代表团是应北京市总工会邀请来我国访问的。到机场欢迎的有北京市总工会主席王炯等人。
