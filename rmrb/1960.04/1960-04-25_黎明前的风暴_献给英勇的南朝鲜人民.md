Title: Storm Before Dawn - Dedicated to the Brave People of South Korea
Author: Tao Bingwei
Date: 1960-04-25
Page: 5

    Storm Before Dawn
    Dedicated to the Brave People of South Korea
    Tao Bingwei
    Good! South Korean brothers!
    The storm before dawn,
    Accompanying the raging waves of the South Sea①,
    It's here, finally here!
    A long night, fifteen years,
    A living hell, suffering torment,
    The country divided, light and dark,
    Wives and children separated, reunion difficult!
    In the North:
    Workers and peasants are masters of their own house,
    Racing against each other, Chollima displays its heroism;
    In the South:
    The American-Syngman Rhee gang plunders and slaughters,
    Demons and monsters run rampant, resentment high under bayonets.
    The anger can no longer be suppressed,
    Let it erupt fiercely—
    From Bukhansan, from Jirisan,
    From Daebaeksan, from Hallasan…
    Blow away the dark clouds overhead,
    Destroy the root of all evil hindering unification!
    Good! South Korean brothers!
    Darkness will pass and dawn will break.
    Look, azaleas are already red all over the fields,
    The three thousand li of rivers and mountains are full of spring.

        Note ① This uprising first originated in Masan, on the coast of the South Sea of Korea.

### 黎明前的风暴  献给英勇的南朝鲜人民
陶冰蔚
1960-04-25
第5版()
专栏：

    黎明前的风暴
    献给英勇的南朝鲜人民
    陶冰蔚
    好啊，南朝鲜兄弟们！
    黎明前的暴风雨，
    伴随南海怒涛①，
    来了，终于来了！
    漫漫长夜啊，十五年，
    人间地狱啊，受熬煎，
    国土分裂啊，有明暗，
    妻离子散啊，难团圆！
    北方是：
    工人农民当家做主，
    你追我赶，千里马上逞英豪；
    南方是：
    美李匪帮掠夺屠杀，
    鬼蜮横行，刺刀底下怨声高。
    怒火再也压不下，
    让它猛烈地爆发吧——
    从北汉山，从智异山，
    从大白山，从汉拿山……
    炸掉盖顶的乌云，
    炸毁阻挠统一的祸根！
    好啊，南朝鲜兄弟们！
    黑夜过去就是黎明。
    看，金达莱已经红遍田野，
    三千里江山春意正浓。
        注①这次起义首先发自朝鲜南海之滨的马山。
