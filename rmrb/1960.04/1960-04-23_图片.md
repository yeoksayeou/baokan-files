    Members of the Caocun Commune in Dezhou City, Shandong Province, drive tractors pulling rollers and seeders, simultaneously compacting the soil to preserve moisture and sowing seeds in the 10,000-mu high-yield cotton field, ensuring one sowing and full emergence of seedlings.
