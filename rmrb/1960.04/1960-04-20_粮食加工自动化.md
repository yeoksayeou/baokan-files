    Technical Revolution in Full Bloom

    Grain Processing Automation
    In the technical innovation and technical revolution movement, the grain processing industry in Jiaxing area, Zhejiang Province, has seen the emergence of twenty automated and semi-automated workshops. In these workshops, the entire production process, from paddy rice entering the factory to polished rice leaving the factory or being sent to the grain station, is automated. Only one worker is needed to manage the switches in the control room.
    The emergence of these automated and semi-automated workshops has increased the labor productivity of the grain processing industry in Jiaxing area by 70 percent, saving a lot of labor. (According to Xinhua News Agency)

### 粮食加工自动化

1960-04-20
第5版()
专栏：技术革命万紫千红

    粮食加工自动化
    浙江省嘉兴地区粮食加工工业在技术革新和技术革命运动中，出现了二十个自动化和半自动化车间。这些车间从稻谷进厂到大米出厂或送到粮站，整个生产过程是自动化的，只要一个工人在控制室里管理电门就行。
    这些自动化半自动化车间的出现，使嘉兴地区粮食加工工业的劳动生4产率提高了百分之七十，节约了很多劳动力。　（据新华社讯）
