Title: Mongolian Great People's Hural Delegation to Visit Our Country at the Invitation of Chairman Zhu De Arrives in Beijing Today
Author:
Date: 1960-04-08
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, 7th April. At the invitation of Zhu De, Chairman of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress, the delegation of the Great People's Hural of the Mongolian People's Republic, led by Bayanlayn Jargalsaikhan, Chairman of the Great People's Hural of Mongolia and member of the Central Committee of the Mongolian People's Revolutionary Party, will arrive in Beijing on April 8 for a friendly visit to our country.

### 应朱德委员长的邀请来我国访问  蒙古大人民呼拉尔代表团今日到京

1960-04-08
第1版()
专栏：

    应朱德委员长的邀请来我国访问
    蒙古大人民呼拉尔代表团今日到京
    新华社7日讯　由团长、蒙古大人民呼拉尔主席、蒙古人民革命党中央委员会委员巴扬勒·贾尔卡赛汗率领的蒙古人民共和国大人民呼拉尔代表团，应全国人民代表大会常务委员会朱德委员长的邀请，前来我国进行友好访问，将于4月8日到达北京。
