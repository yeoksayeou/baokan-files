Title: Deputy Prime Minister of the Provisional Government of the Republic of Algeria, Krim Belkacem
Author: 
Date: 1960-04-30
Page: 2

    Krim Belkacem, Deputy Prime Minister and Minister of Foreign Affairs of the Provisional Government of the Republic of Algeria, is 37 years old.

    In his youth, he was a fighter in the Algerian national movement and was wanted by the French colonial police for his involvement in the national movement.

    In 1947, Krim Belkacem entered the Algerian guerrilla zone. He was a key member of the "Secret Organization" established in 1947 to prepare for armed action. He was also one of the initiators of the "Committee of Unity and Revolutionary Action" established in 1954 to prepare for the launch of armed struggle, and one of the initiators of what was later renamed the Algerian National Liberation Front. He served as the Colonel Commander of the Third War Zone.

    Since August 20, 1956, Krim Belkacem has served as an executive member of the "Liaison and Execution Committee," the central executive body of the Algerian National Revolution Committee.

    Krim Belkacem previously served as Deputy Prime Minister and Minister of National Defense of the Provisional Government of the Republic of Algeria and was appointed to his current position in January 1960.
                            (Xinhua News Agency) (with picture)

### 阿尔及利亚共和国临时政府副总理  克里姆·贝勒卡塞姆

1960-04-30
第2版()
专栏：

    阿尔及利亚共和国临时政府副总理
    克里姆·贝勒卡塞姆
    阿尔及利亚共和国临时政府副总理兼外交部长克里姆·贝勒卡塞姆现年三十七岁。
    他在年青时就是阿尔及利亚民族运动的战士，曾因从事民族运动为法国殖民警察局通缉。
    1947年，克里姆·贝勒卡塞姆进入阿尔及利亚游击区。他是1947年成立的为准备武装行动的“秘密组织”的主要成员之一。也是1954年为准备发动武装斗争成立的“团结与行动革命委员会”的发起人之一和后来改称为阿尔及利亚民族解放阵线的发起人之一。他曾任第三战区上校司令。
    克里姆·贝勒卡塞姆自1956年8月20日起，任阿尔及利亚革命全国委员会中央执行机构“联络与执行委员会”执行委员。
    克里姆·贝勒卡塞姆曾任阿尔及利亚共和国临时政府副总理兼国防部长，1960年1月改任现职。
                            （新华社讯）（附图片）
