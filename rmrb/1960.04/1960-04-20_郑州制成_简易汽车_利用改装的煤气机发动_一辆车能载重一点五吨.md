Title: Zhengzhou Makes "Simple Automobiles" Using Modified Gas Generators for Power; One Vehicle Can Carry 1.5 Tons

Author:

Date: 1960-04-20
Page: 5

Technical Revolution: A Myriad of Colors

    Zhengzhou Makes "Simple Automobiles"
    Using Modified Gas Generators for Power; One Vehicle Can Carry 1.5 Tons
    Workers Li Guangyin and Li Yuzhou of the Zhengzhou Transportation Bureau's Carrying Station, after more than 50 research and experimentation efforts, recently successfully created a "simple automobile."
    This automobile has the advantages of a light body, low cost, high efficiency, and easy operation. Two ordinary handcart workers can learn to operate it in two days. One vehicle can pull three to five trailers, generally carrying 1.5 tons, traveling at a speed of 13 to 20 kilometers per hour, consuming 9.375 kilograms of fuel per 100 kilometers. The turning radius of the simple automobile is 4.5 meters, suitable for short-distance transportation in urban areas, suburbs, and rural construction sites. This simple automobile is powered by a 3.5 horsepower gas generator; after modifying the gas furnace part, it can use liquid fuel. Except for the tires, its components are all made of iron and wood.
    Currently, twenty of these simple automobiles have been produced. They plan to produce two hundred vehicles before this year's National Day, completely liberating the station's more than 1,800 handcart workers from heavy physical labor.
　　(Xinhua News Agency)

### 郑州制成“简易汽车”  利用改装的煤气机发动　一辆车能载重一点五吨

1960-04-20
第5版()
专栏：技术革命万紫千红

    郑州制成“简易汽车”
    利用改装的煤气机发动　一辆车能载重一点五吨
    郑州市交通局搬运站工人李广荫、李玉州等，经过五十多次的研究试验，最近创制成功一种“简易汽车”。
    这种汽车具有车身轻、成本低、工效高、易操作等优点，两个普通架子车工人学习两天就可操作。一辆车挂三至五个拖车，一般能载重一点五吨，行车时速十三到二十公里，每百公里耗油九点三七五公斤。简易汽车转向半径为四点五米，适用于城市、郊区和农村工地等短途运输。这种简易汽车是利用三点五马力的煤气机发动；把煤气炉部分加以改装之后，可以使用液体燃料。它的部件除轮胎外，都是铁木结构。
    目前，这种简易汽车已生产出二十辆。他们计划在今年国庆节前生产二百辆，使全站一千八百多名架子车工人彻底解脱重体力劳动。
　　（新华社）
