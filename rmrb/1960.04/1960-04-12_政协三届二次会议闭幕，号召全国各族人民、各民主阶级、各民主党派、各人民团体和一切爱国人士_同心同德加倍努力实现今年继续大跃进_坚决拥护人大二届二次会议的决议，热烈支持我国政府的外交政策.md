Title: CPPCC Third National Committee Second Session Closes, Calling on All Ethnic Groups, Democratic Classes, Democratic Parties, People's Organizations and All Patriotic Personages to Work Together and Redouble Efforts to Realize This Year's Continued Great Leap Forward; Firmly Support the Resolutions of the Second Session of the Second National People's Congress, and Warmly Support the Foreign Policy of Our Government
Author:
Date: 1960-04-12
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, 11th - The Second Session of the Third National Committee of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference closed this morning. The resolution passed at the meeting called on all ethnic groups, democratic classes, democratic parties, people's organizations and all patriotic personages across the country, under the leadership of the Chinese Communist Party and Chairman Mao Zedong, to work together, redouble their efforts to implement the resolutions of the Second Session of the Second National People's Congress, to complete and over fulfill the 1960 national economic plan, and to strive to achieve the National Agricultural Development Program ahead of schedule.

Presiding over today's meeting were: Zhou Enlai, Chairman of the CPPCC National Committee, and Vice-Chairmen Guo Moruo, Shen Junru, Huang Yanpei, Li Weihan, Li Siguang, Chen Shutong, Chen Yi, Pagbalha Geleg Namgyal, Ngapoi Ngawang Jigme and Secretary-General Xu Bing.

Eight hundred and four members attended today's meeting.

Chairman Zhou Enlai spoke before the closing.

The resolution on this meeting, unanimously adopted by the meeting, stated that the meeting heard the work report made by Vice Chairman Chen Shutong on behalf of the Standing Committee, and all members attended the Second Session of the Second National People's Congress, heard the reports of Vice Premier Li Fuchun "on the draft of the 1960 National Economic Plan," Vice Premier Li Xiannian "on the 1959 National Final Accounts and the 1960 National Budget Draft," and Vice Premier Tan Zhenlin "Strive to Achieve the National Agricultural Development Program Ahead of Schedule," and expressed their firm support; also heard Premier Zhou Enlai's speech on the international situation and China's foreign relations, and expressed their warm support for the government's foreign policy.

The resolution stated that the 1960 national economic plan is a great plan for continued leap forward, and the realization of this plan is of great significance for our future sustained leap forward, and to build our country into a strong socialist country with modern industry, modern agriculture, and modern science.

The resolution stated that the current situation in the country is very good: the people of the whole country are striving for a continued great leap forward in industrial and agricultural production with soaring enthusiasm, the socialist revolution on the political and ideological fronts is deepening, the mass movement of technical revolution and cultural revolution is developing vigorously, the rural people's communes are being further consolidated and developed, and people's communes are being established in large numbers in cities across the country.

The resolution stated that the CPPCC National Committee and local committees must adapt to the current excellent situation in the country, actively promote all ethnic groups, democratic classes, democratic parties and democratic figures from all walks of life to strengthen unity, strengthen work, strengthen learning under the leadership of the Chinese Communist Party, further play the role of the Chinese People's Democratic United Front, and with practical actions, implement the general line of socialist construction, adhere to the Great Leap Forward and the People's Communes, hold high the brilliant banner of Mao Zedong Thought, and forge ahead courageously.

Next, the meeting heard the report on the examination of proposals made by Committee Member Chen Zhengren, convener of the Proposals Examination Committee, on behalf of the Proposals Examination Committee, and unanimously adopted the resolution on the report on the examination of proposals.

The last item on the agenda of the meeting was the supplementary election of vice-chairmen of the National Committee. Based on the nomination of the Standing Committee, He Xiangning was unanimously elected as vice-chairman of the National Committee.

At 11:30, Chairman Zhou Enlai announced that all items on the agenda of this meeting had been completed and the meeting successfully concluded. All members stood up and applauded enthusiastically for a long time.

### 政协三届二次会议闭幕，号召全国各族人民、各民主阶级、各民主党派、各人民团体和一切爱国人士  同心同德加倍努力实现今年继续大跃进  坚决拥护人大二届二次会议的决议，热烈支持我国政府的外交政策

1960-04-12
第1版()
专栏：

    政协三届二次会议闭幕，号召全国各族人民、各民主阶级、各民主党派、各人民团体和一切爱国人士
    同心同德加倍努力实现今年继续大跃进
    坚决拥护人大二届二次会议的决议，热烈支持我国政府的外交政策
    新华社11日讯　中国人民政治协商会议第三届全国委员会第二次会议在今天上午闭幕。会议通过的决议号召全国各族人民、各民主阶级、各民主党派、各人民团体和一切爱国人士，在中国共产党和毛泽东主席的领导下，同心同德，加倍努力，为执行第二届全国人民代表大会第二次会议的决议、为完成和超额完成一九六○年的国民经济计划、为争取提前实现全国农业发展纲要而奋斗。
    主持今天大会的是：政协全国委员会主席周恩来，副主席郭沫若、沈钧儒、黄炎培、李维汉、李四光、陈叔通、陈毅、帕巴拉·卓列朗杰、阿沛·阿旺晋美和秘书长徐冰。
    出席今天大会的委员八百零四人。
    周恩来主席在闭幕之前讲了话。
    会议一致通过的关于本次会议的决议说，会议听取了陈叔通副主席代表常务委员会所作的工作报告，全体委员并列席了第二届全国人民代表大会第二次会议，听取了李富春副总理“关于一九六○年国民经济计划草案的报告”、李先念副总理“关于一九五九年国家决算和一九六○年国家预算草案的报告”、谭震林副总理“为提前实现全国农业发展纲要而奋斗”的报告，表示坚决拥护；还听取了周恩来总理关于国际形势和我国对外关系的讲话，表示热烈支持政府的外交政策。
    决议说，一九六○年的国民经济计划，是一个伟大的继续跃进的计划，实现这个计划，对于我们今后的持续跃进，把我国早日建成为一个拥有现代工业、现代农业和现代科学文化的强大的社会主义国家具有重大的意义。
    决议说，目前全国的形势很好：全国人民正在以冲天的干劲争取工农业生产的继续大跃进，政治战线、思想战线上的社会主义革命正在深入，技术革命和文化革命的群众运动正在轰轰烈烈地开展，农村人民公社正在进一步巩固和发展，全国城市正在大办人民公社。
    决议说，政协全国委员会和各地方委员会必须适应当前全国的大好形势，积极推动各民族、各民主阶级、各民主党派和各界民主人士在中国共产党的领导下，加强团结，加强工作，加强学习，进一步发挥中国人民民主统一战线的作用，以实际行动，贯彻社会主义建设总路线，坚持大跃进和人民公社，高举毛泽东思想的光辉旗帜，奋勇前进。
    接着，会议听取了提案审查委员会召集人陈正人委员代表提案审查委员会作的关于提案的审查报告，并且一致通过了关于提案审查报告的决议。
    会议最后的一项议程，是补选全国委员会的副主席，根据常务委员会的提名，一致选举何香凝为全国委员会副主席。
    十一时三十分，周恩来主席宣布本次会议的议程全部进行完毕，会议胜利闭幕。全体委员起立，长时间热烈地鼓掌。
