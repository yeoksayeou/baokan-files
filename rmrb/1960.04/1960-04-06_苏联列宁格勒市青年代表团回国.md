Title: Soviet Leningrad Youth Delegation Returns Home
Author:
Date: 1960-04-06
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, April 5th. The Soviet Leningrad Youth Delegation, led by K.M. Ivanov, First Secretary of the Leningrad City Committee of the All-Union Leninist Young Communist League (Komsomol), returned home by plane from Beijing on the 4th, after a nine day visit to Shanghai at the invitation of the Shanghai Municipal Committee of the Communist Youth League.

　　Ke Qingshi, member of the Political Bureau of the CPC Central Committee, First Secretary of the CPC Shanghai Municipal Committee, and Mayor of Shanghai, and Shi Ximin, alternate secretary of the CPC Shanghai Municipal Committee and President of the Shanghai Sino-Soviet Friendship Association, met with all members of the delegation on the 1st and had cordial talks with them. After the meeting, the CPC Shanghai Municipal Committee and the Municipal People's Committee hosted a banquet to welcome the delegation.

　　During their stay in Shanghai, the delegation visited factories such as the Shanghai Electric Machinery Factory and the Shanghai Machine Tool Factory, as well as the Changzheng People's Commune, Fudan University, Jiaotong University, Shanghai Middle School, the Youth Palace, the Children's Palace, and other units, and had extensive contact with Shanghai youth. The delegation also held discussions with leading cadres of the Shanghai Municipal Committee of the Communist Youth League on the experience of the Communist Youth League's work, and was invited to report to the Communist Youth League cadres of Shanghai on the work of the Leningrad Komsomol and the heroic labor of the Leningrad youth in completing the Seven-Year Plan ahead of schedule.

　　The delegation visited Hangzhou before leaving Shanghai.

### 苏联列宁格勒市青年代表团回国

1960-04-06
第5版()
专栏：

    苏联列宁格勒市青年代表团回国
    新华社5日讯　由团长、苏联列宁共产主义青年团列宁格勒市委员会第一书记基·米·伊凡诺夫率领的苏联列宁格勒市青年代表团一行四人，4日从北京乘飞机回国。
    代表团是应共青团上海市委邀请，在上海访问九天后，于2日到达北京的。中共中央政治局委员、中共上海市委第一书记、上海市市长柯庆施，中共上海市委书记处候补书记、上海市中苏友好协会会长石西民1日曾接见了代表团的全体成员，并且同他们进行了亲切的谈话。接见后，中共上海市委和市人民委员会设宴欢迎代表团。
    代表团在上海期间，参观访问了上海电机厂、上海机床厂等工厂和长征人民公社、复旦大学、交通大学、上海中学、青年宫、少年宫等单位，同上海青年进行了广泛的接触。代表团还和共青团上海市委的负责干部座谈了共青团工作的经验，并应邀向上海市的共青团干部报告了苏联列宁格勒共青团的工作和列宁格勒青年为提前完成七年计划而英勇劳动的情况。
    代表团在离开上海前曾去杭州参观访问。
