Title: Polish Pianist Berezovsky Returns Home
Author:
Date: 1960-04-18
Page: 6

    Xinhua News Agency, 17th. Polish pianist Jan Berezovsky, who visited China according to the 1960 execution plan of the Sino-Polish Cultural Cooperation Agreement, concluded his visit to China and left Beijing by plane today to return to his country. Among those who went to the airport to see him off were Wang Yi, Deputy Director of the Foreign Cultural Liaison Committee and Secretary-General of the Sino-Polish Friendship Association, Wang Yuanfang, Secretary of the Secretariat of the Chinese Musicians Association, and Huang Hui, Deputy Manager of the China Performing Arts Company.

### 波兰钢琴家贝莱仁斯基回国

1960-04-18
第6版()
专栏：

    波兰钢琴家贝莱仁斯基回国
    新华社17日讯　根据中波文化合作协定1960年执行计划来我国访问的波兰钢琴家扬·贝莱仁斯基，结束了在我国的访问，今天乘飞机离开北京回国。到机场欢送的有对外文化联络委员会副司长、中波友协秘书长王仪，中国音乐家协会书记处书记王元方，中国演出公司副经理黄回等。
