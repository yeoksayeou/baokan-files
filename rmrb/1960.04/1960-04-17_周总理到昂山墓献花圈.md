Title: Premier Zhou Enlai Lays Wreath at Aung San's Tomb
Author:
Date: 1960-04-17
Page: 4

　　Xinhua News Agency, Rangoon, April 16th: Premier Zhou Enlai, Vice Premier Chen Yi, and their entourage laid a wreath at the tomb of Burmese national hero General Aung San this morning.

　　Chief Justice of the Burmese Supreme Court U Myint Thein accompanied them.

　　Chinese Ambassador to Burma Li Yimang and Burmese Ambassador to China Kyaing Tun also accompanied them.

### 周总理到昂山墓献花圈

1960-04-17
第4版()
专栏：

    周总理到昂山墓献花圈
    新华社仰光16日电　周恩来总理、陈毅副总理和随行人员今天上午到缅甸民族英雄昂山将军的墓前献了花圈。
    陪同他们前去的有缅甸最高法院首席法官吴敏登。
    中国驻缅甸大使李一氓和缅甸驻中国大使叫温也陪同前往。
