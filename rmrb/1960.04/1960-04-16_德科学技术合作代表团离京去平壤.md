Title: GDR Science and Technology Cooperation Delegation Leaves Beijing for Pyongyang
Author: 
Date: 1960-04-16
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, April 15th: The Science and Technology Cooperation Delegation of the German Democratic Republic, led by Georg Henke, Chairman of the German Group of the Sino-German Standing Committee for Technical and Technical Scientific Cooperation, member of the State Planning Commission, and Director of the Foreign Trade Bureau, left Beijing by plane this morning for Pyongyang after participating in the Fifth Session of the Sino-German Standing Committee for Technical and Technical Scientific Cooperation in our country.

Those who went to the airport to see them off included: Wang Daohan, Chairman of the Chinese Group of the Committee and Vice Minister of the First Ministry of Machine Building, and Wang Dongnian, Director of the International Cooperation Bureau of the Scientific and Technological Commission. Mostetz, First Secretary of the Embassy of the German Democratic Republic in China, also went to the airport to see them off.

### 德科学技术合作代表团离京去平壤

1960-04-16
第4版()
专栏：

    德科学技术合作代表团离京去平壤
    新华社15日讯　来我国参加中德技术与技术科学合作常任委员会第五届会议的德意志民主共和国科学技术合作代表团，在委员会德国组主席、国家计划委员会委员兼对外贸易局局长盖奥格·亨凯率领下，今天上午乘飞机离开北京去平壤。
    前往机场欢送的有：委员会中国组主席、第一机械工业部副部长汪道涵，科学技术委员会国际合作局局长王东年等人。德意志民主共和国驻华大使馆一等秘书莫斯特茨也到机场欢送。
