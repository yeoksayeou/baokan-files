Title: Chongqing Iron and Steel Company Innovates Smelting Technology and Strengthens Enterprise Management to Strive to Overfulfill the Second Quarter Production Plan
Author:
Date: 1960-04-05
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Chongqing, April 4th. Chongqing Iron and Steel Company, on the basis of mobilizing the masses to conscientiously summarize the work of the first quarter, has adopted various measures to work hard in April and the second quarter, in order to overfulfill the state plan for the first half of the year.

In the first quarter of this year, the employees of Chongqing Iron and Steel Company carried out technical innovation and technical revolution movements, broke through many key production points, and achieved great results. The company overfulfilled the quarterly industrial output value plan, and the output of major products such as steel, steel products, pig iron, and refractory materials all increased significantly compared with the same period last year.

In order to ensure the overfulfillment of the more arduous second-quarter production plan, the factories and workshops of Chongqing Iron and Steel Company are continuing to vigorously carry out technical innovation and technical revolution movements, further expanding the furnace charge and shortening the smelting time; and strengthening enterprise management and technical management.

### 重钢革新冶炼技术加强企业管理  力争超额完成第二季度生产计划

1960-04-05
第1版()
专栏：

    重钢革新冶炼技术加强企业管理  力争超额完成第二季度生产计划
    据新华社重庆4日电　重庆钢铁公司在发动群众认真总结第一季度工作的基础上，又采取各种措施大干4月份和第二季度，以超额完成上半年的国家计划。
    在今年第一季度内，重庆钢铁公司职工开展技术革新和技术革命运动，突破了很多生产关键，取得了很大成绩。全公司超额完成了季度工业总产值计划，主要产品钢、钢材、生铁和耐火材料等产量都比去年同期大大增长。
    为了确保更加繁重的第二季度生产计划的超额完成，重庆钢铁公司各厂、各车间正在继续大力开展技术革新和技术革命运动，进一步扩大装炉量和缩短冶炼时间；并加强了企业管理和技术管理。
