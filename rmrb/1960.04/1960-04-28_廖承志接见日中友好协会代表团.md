Title: Liao Chengzhi Receives Delegation from the Japan-China Friendship Association
Author: 
Date: 1960-04-28
Page: 2
    Xinhua News Agency, April 27th: Liao Chengzhi, Chairman of the Chinese Committee for Asian-African Solidarity, received this afternoon the delegation from the Japan-China Friendship Association headed by Masuzo Akatsu, as well as three Japanese journalists including Takeo Ito. Zhao Anbo, Qu Tangliang, Zheng Senyu, Sun Pinghua, Yang Jin, and others were present at the reception.
    Japanese peace advocate Kinkazu Saionji and his wife were also present at the reception.
    A banquet was held after the reception. The banquet proceeded in a friendly and warm atmosphere.

### 廖承志接见日中友好协会代表团

1960-04-28
第2版()
专栏：

    廖承志接见日中友好协会代表团
    新华社27日讯　中国亚非团结委员会主席廖承志今天下午接见了以赤津益造为首的日中友好协会代表团和伊藤武雄等三位日本新闻工作者。接见时在座的有赵安博、区棠亮、郑森禹、孙平化、杨进等。
    接见时，日本和平人士西园寺公一和他的夫人也在座。
    接见后举行了宴会，宴会在友好热烈的气氛中进行。
