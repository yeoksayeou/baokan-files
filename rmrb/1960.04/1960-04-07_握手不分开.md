Title: Handshake Never Separates
Author: Fu Zhanyuan
Date: 1960-04-07
Page: 8

    Handshake Never Separates
    Fu Zhanyuan, Worker at Zhengzhou Thermal Power Plant
    It's truly worrying,
    It's truly making people anxious,
    The water pump for watering wheat,
    Just won't start!
    Don't worry,
    Don't be anxious,
    Look at the drought resistance team of workers,
    Coming in a mighty force...
    This old electrician,
    Immediately finds the problem,
    It turns out—
    The coil was wired incorrectly.
    The coil is wired correctly,
    And reinstalled,
    Then press the button,
    Ha! The water pump gushes water, making people truly happy!
    Ha! Truly happy,
    Ha! Truly happy,
    Eyes smiling at eyes,
    Worker and peasant handshake never separates...

### 握手不分开
傅占元
1960-04-07
第8版()
专栏：

    握手不分开
    郑州热电厂工人　傅占元
    真叫人心焦，
    真叫人急坏，
    浇麦的水泵，
    怎么也转不起来！
    不要心焦，
    不要急坏，
    你看工人抗旱大队，
    浩浩荡荡地走来……
    这个老电工，
    一下子就检查出毛病来，
    原来是——
    接错了线圈。
    接好线圈，
    又重新装起来，
    再把电钮按，
    哈！水泵哗哗地打水叫人真痛快！
    哈！真痛快，
    哈！真痛快，
    眼睛对着眼睛笑，
    工农握手不分开……
