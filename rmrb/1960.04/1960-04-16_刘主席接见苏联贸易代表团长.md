Title: Chairman Liu Receives Head of Soviet Trade Delegation
Author:
Date: 1960-04-16
Page: 1

    Xinhua News Agency, April 15th. Chairman Liu Shaoqi received Patolichev, the Minister of Foreign Trade of the Soviet Union and head of the Soviet government trade delegation, this evening, and had a cordial and friendly conversation.
    Present at the reception were Ye Jizhuang, Minister of Foreign Trade, and Vice Ministers Lei Renmin and Li Qiang, as well as Vice Minister of Foreign Affairs Zeng Yongquan.
    Also present at the reception was Soviet Ambassador to China Chervonenko.

### 刘主席接见苏联贸易代表团长

1960-04-16
第1版()
专栏：

    刘主席接见苏联贸易代表团长
    新华社15日讯　刘少奇主席今天晚上接见了苏联对外贸易部部长、苏联政府贸易代表团团长帕托利切夫，并进行了亲切友好的谈话。
    接见时在座的，有对外贸易部部长叶季壮，副部长雷任民、李强，外交部副部长曾涌泉等。
    接见时，苏联驻中国大使契尔沃年科也在座。
