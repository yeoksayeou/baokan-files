Title: Polish Military Delegation Leaves Zhengzhou to Visit Chongqing and Other Places
Author:
Date: 1960-04-11
Page: 7
Xinhua News Agency, Zhengzhou, April 9th: The Polish military delegation, headed by General Boziwowski, left Zhengzhou by special plane on the morning of the 9th to visit Chongqing and other places.

　　The Polish military delegation conducted a three-day visit in Henan. On the 6th and 7th, accompanied by Major General Huang Xinyou, Deputy Commander of the Henan Military Region, the delegation visited the Sanmenxia Dam and other projects in Sanmenxia and Luoyang, as well as the China's First Tractor Factory and a certain unit of the Chinese People's Liberation Army, and toured the historical sites in Luoyang. At the tractor factory, the delegates learned about the production process in detail, visited the main workshops, and stopped in the general assembly workshop for more than ten minutes until a tractor was assembled. General Boziwowski also sat on this tractor and drove it out of the workshop. The delegation returned to Zhengzhou on the morning of the 8th, toured the city, and visited the Henan Provincial Exhibition Hall and the Yellow River Exhibition Hall. The delegation was warmly welcomed by workers, cadres, and officers everywhere.

　　On the morning of the 9th, when the delegation left Zhengzhou, Lieutenant General Bi Zhanyun, Commander of the Henan Military Region, Wang Weiqun, Deputy Governor of Henan Province, and Li Ping, Deputy Mayor of Zhengzhou, saw them off at the airport.

### 波兰军事代表团  离郑州赴重庆等地访问

1960-04-11
第7版()
专栏：

    波兰军事代表团
    离郑州赴重庆等地访问
    新华社郑州9日电　以博齐沃夫斯基上将为首的波兰军事代表团，9日上午离开郑州，乘专机前往重庆等地参观访问。
    波兰军事代表团在河南进行了三天的参观访问。6日和7日，代表团在河南军区副司令员黄新友少将陪同下，到三门峡和洛阳等地，参观了三门峡大坝等工程和我国第一拖拉机制造厂以及中国人民解放军某部队，并游览了洛阳的名胜古迹。在拖拉机厂，代表们详细了解了生产过程，参观了主要车间，并且在总装配车间停了十几分钟，直到装好一部拖拉机。博齐沃夫斯基上将还坐在这部拖拉机的上面，开出车间。代表团在8日上午回到郑州，游览了市容，参观了河南省展览馆和黄河陈列馆。代表团到处受到工人、干部、军官们的热烈欢迎。
    9日上午，代表团离开郑州市，河南军区司令员毕占云中将、河南省副省长王维群、郑州市副市长李平等到机场送行。
