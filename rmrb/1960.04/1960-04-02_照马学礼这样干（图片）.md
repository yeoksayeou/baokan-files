Title: Do As Ma Xueli Does (Picture)
Author: Yu Chengjian
Date: 1960-04-02
Page: 2

    Do As Ma Xueli Does
    Ma Xueli strives to study Chairman Mao's writings, determined to always be an advanced worker who is both red and expert.
    Since the beginning of this year, Ma Xueli has proposed more than 80 innovation suggestions, of which more than 20 have been implemented.
    Ma Xueli enthusiastically helps others catch up with him, teaching others everything he knows.
    Ma Xueli came to Wuhan Automobile Manufacturing Factory to seek teachers, visit the wise and learn from experience.
                                 Xinhua News Agency reporter Yu Chengjian photographed
