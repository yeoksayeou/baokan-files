Title: Polish Cultural Delegation Leaves Beijing for Home

Author: 
Date: 1960-04-23
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, April 22nd: The Polish Cultural Delegation, led by Polish Minister of Culture and Art Tadeusz Galinski, left Beijing by plane this morning to return home.

Those who went to the airport to see them off included Minister of Culture Shen Yanbing, Vice Minister Xu Guangxiao, and Deputy Director of the Commission for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries Zhang Zhixiang. Polish Chargé d'affaires ad interim to China, Mr. Furato, also went to the airport to see them off.

### 波兰文化代表团离北京回国

1960-04-23
第5版()
专栏：

    波兰文化代表团离北京回国
    据新华社22日讯　以波兰文化艺术部部长塔德乌什·加林斯基为首的波兰文化代表团，今天早晨乘飞机离北京回国。
    到机场欢送的，有文化部部长沈雁冰、副部长徐光霄，对外文化联络委员会副主任张致祥。波兰驻华大使馆临时代办傅拉托也到机场送行。
