Title: Beijing Automobile Factory Holds Meeting to Celebrate the 15th Anniversary of the Liberation of Hungary; Hungarian Guests Wish the People of Hungary and China to Unite and Advance Shoulder to Shoulder
Author:
Date: 1960-04-05
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, April 4th: The Beijing Automobile Factory held a grand meeting today with over a thousand employees to celebrate the 15th anniversary of the liberation of Hungary. János Pataki, head of the Hungarian-Chinese Friendship Association delegation, and all members of the delegation, László Vadas, head of the Hungarian Communist Youth League Central Art Troupe, and all members of the troupe, as well as Counselor Bainok of the Hungarian Embassy in China, attended the celebration. Soviet expert Harlov, who works at the Beijing Automobile Factory, also attended the meeting.

At the meeting, factory director Chen Ziliang, on behalf of all the factory employees, extended festive greetings to the distinguished guests. He said that we are extremely pleased with the great achievements made by the Hungarian people under the leadership of the Hungarian Socialist Workers' Party and the Workers' and Peasants' Revolutionary Government. The people of China and Hungary must closely unite in the big family headed by the Soviet Union and make greater contributions to socialist construction and the defense of world peace.

Counselor Bainok of the Hungarian Embassy in China spoke at the meeting. After describing the achievements made in Hungary over the past fifteen years since liberation, he said that the Hungarian people are as happy about the achievements of the Chinese people as they are about their own. He said that the people of our two countries, together with the great Soviet people and the people of other socialist brother countries, are closely united and advancing shoulder to shoulder, jointly striving for the building of socialism and communism and for the peace of all mankind. He said that cherishing this friendship is our most important internationalist obligation.

János Martin, a member of the Hungarian-Chinese Friendship Association delegation and the veteran worker of Hungary's first socialist labor team, also congratulated the workers of Hungary and China on achieving greater success in production at the meeting. The meeting also read out a congratulatory letter from the employees of the Beijing Automobile Factory to their Hungarian worker brothers.

Before the meeting, the distinguished guests, accompanied by the factory's Party committee secretary and the factory director, visited the factory. After the meeting, the Beijing Automobile Factory's workers' amateur cultural troupe and the Hungarian Communist Youth League Central Art Troupe performed cultural programs.

### 北京汽车制造厂集会庆祝匈牙利解放十五周年  匈牙利贵宾祝匈中人民团结并肩前进

1960-04-05
第6版()
专栏：

    北京汽车制造厂集会庆祝匈牙利解放十五周年
    匈牙利贵宾祝匈中人民团结并肩前进
    新华社4日讯　北京汽车制造厂一千多名职工今天隆重庆祝匈牙利解放十五周年。匈中友协代表团团长帕塔基·亚诺什和全体团员，匈牙利共青团中央艺术团团长瓦达斯·拉斯洛和全体团员以及匈牙利驻华大使馆参赞白诺克参加了庆祝大会。在北京汽车制造厂工作的苏联专家哈尔洛夫也出席了会议。
    在大会上，厂长陈子良代表全厂职工向贵宾们致以节日的祝贺。他说，我们对匈牙利人民在匈牙利社会主义工人党和工农革命政府领导下取得的伟大成就感到万分的高兴。中匈两国人民要紧密地团结在以苏联为首的大家庭里，为社会主义建设事业和保卫世界和平作出更大的成绩。
    匈牙利驻华大使馆参赞白诺克在大会上讲话。他在叙述了匈牙利解放十五年来取得的成就以后说，匈牙利人民把中国人民取得的成就看成像自己的成就一样高兴。他说，我们两国人民与伟大的苏联人民以及其他社会主义兄弟国家的人民正紧密团结、并肩前进，为建成社会主义和共产主义、为全人类的和平而共同奋斗。他说，爱护这种友谊是我们最重大的国际主义义务。
    匈中友协代表团团员、匈牙利第一个社会主义劳动队队长老工人马尔丁·亚诺什，也在会上祝匈中两国工人在生产上取得更大的成就。大会还宣读了北京汽车制造厂职工给匈牙利工人兄弟的贺信。
    在开会前，贵宾们在工厂党委书记和厂长的陪同下参观了工厂。会后，北京汽车制造厂的工人业余文工团和匈牙利共青团中央艺术团演出了文艺节目。
