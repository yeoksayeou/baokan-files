Title: Indonesia Insists on a Twelve-Nautical-Mile Territorial Sea
Author:
Date: 1960-04-19
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, Jakarta, April 16th: An Indonesian Foreign Ministry spokesman said on April 16th that every country has the right to determine its own territorial sea limit, and Indonesia's declaration of a twelve-nautical-mile territorial sea is "very natural and undeniable."

He made these remarks when commenting on the recent International Conference on the Law of the Sea held in Geneva, which rejected the demands of Indonesia and other countries for a twelve-nautical-mile territorial sea limit.

The Foreign Ministry spokesman also stated that Indonesia's position has consistently been supported by Asian and African countries.

### 印度尼西亚坚持领海为十二浬

1960-04-19
第5版()
专栏：

    印度尼西亚坚持领海为十二浬
    新华社雅加达16日电　印度尼西亚外交部发言人16日说，每一个国家都有权利决定它自己的领海范围，印度尼西亚宣称它的领海为十二浬，是“很自然的和不能否认的”。
    他是在评论最近在日内瓦举行的国际海洋法会议拒绝印度尼西亚和其他国家要求领海的范围为十二浬时说这番话的。
    这位外交部发言人还说，印度尼西亚的主张一直得到亚非国家的支持。
