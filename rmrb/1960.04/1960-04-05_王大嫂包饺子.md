Title: Aunt Wang Makes Dumplings
Author: Zhang Xianbin
Date: 1960-04-05
Page: 8

　　It's a holiday for the commune members today,
　　Uncle Wang is arranging to go to the opera.
　　Auntie is left alone to manage the canteen,
　　Uncle Wang asks her what good food she’s preparing?
　　“It's a day off to eat well,
　　Meat dumplings, with thin skins.”
　　Uncle Wang shakes his head in disbelief,
　　He says Auntie is boasting.
　　“One person can make five thousand dumplings,
　　A mule can also produce twins.”
　　It's lively at the club,
　　Clappers, comic duets, Peking opera and Ping opera.
　　Too preoccupied to watch the wonderful performances,
　　Uncle Wang is hatching a plan.
　　“No love is greater than between husband and wife,
　　I must help her make dumplings.”
　　He steps into the kitchen,
　　Uncle Wang gets angry as soon as he looks.
　　Auntie is sitting cross-legged on the kang,
　　Reading a book leisurely!
　　Uncle Wang reproaches her, but she ignores him,
　　She calmly gets off the kang.
　　She fills the pot with clear spring water,
　　And turns on the blower.
　　The water boils and rolls in the pot,
　　Auntie moves out a wooden gadget.
　　Holding the wooden handle and shaking it three times,
　　This thing chops filling and rolls out skins.
　　The dumplings automatically jump into the pot,
　　Uncle Wang is fascinated.
　　While watching, he keeps praising:
　　"Women are really amazing."
　　Auntie glares at him,
　　"Peeking through the crack of the door, you underestimate us women.
　　"If you men can make innovations,
　　We women can also build machines."

### 王大嫂包饺子
张宪彬
1960-04-05
第8版()
专栏：

    王大嫂包饺子
    张宪彬
    社员今天度假日，
    王大哥张罗去看戏。
    大嫂一人留下管食堂，
    王大哥问她准备什么好吃的？
    “放假日子吃好的，
    肉馅饺子，薄薄的皮。”
    王大哥摇头不相信，
    他说大嫂吹牛皮。
    “一个人能包出五千饺子，
    骡子也能产双驹。”
    俱乐部里好热闹，
    快板，双簧，京评剧。
    精彩表演无心看，
    大哥心里打主意。
    “恩爱不过是夫妻，
    我得帮她包饺子。”
    一脚迈进厨房里，
    大哥一看来了气。
    大嫂盘腿炕上坐，
    消消停停看书呢！
    大哥数落她不理，
    从从容容下了地。
    锅里添满清泉水，
    回手打开吹风机。
    水花翻滚锅烧开，
    大嫂搬出一个木玩艺。
    手握木把摇三摇，
    这东西又剁馅子又擀皮。
    饺子自动往锅里跳，
    大哥看得入了迷。
    一边看来一边忙称赞：
    “妇女可真了不起。”
    大嫂瞪了他一眼，
    门缝瞧人，看扁俺妇女。
    “你们男子能闹革新，
    我们妇女也能造机器。”
