Title: Khrushchev Meets with De Gaulle, and Meets with Representatives of French Trade Unions
Author:
Date: 1960-04-03
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, April 2nd, TASS Paris, April 1st: Soviet Council of Ministers Chairman Khrushchev and French President De Gaulle held talks on the morning of April 1st at the presidential residence in Rambouillet, near Paris.

In the afternoon, talks continued after lunch. Later, Soviet Council of Ministers Deputy Chairman Kosygin and French Prime Minister Debré also arrived at Rambouillet to participate in the talks.

Xinhua News Agency, April 2nd, TASS Paris, April 1st: On March 31st, Soviet Council of Ministers Chairman Khrushchev held a friendly meeting with representatives of French trade unions who visited him at his residence. During the meeting, Khrushchev distributed a statement.

The statement reviewed the traditional friendly contacts between Soviet and French workers and pointed out the task of the people of the Soviet Union and France to fight for the maintenance of peace. He drew attention to the Soviet proposal for complete and thorough disarmament and the necessity of eliminating the remnants of World War II and concluding a peace treaty with Germany. The statement said that the establishment of lasting peace and the cessation of the arms race would greatly contribute to improving the situation of working people.

The statement said that the historical task of the working class is to abolish the system of human exploitation and free humanity from the scourge of war.

The statement expressed firm belief that the scope of contacts between the working classes of France and the Soviet Union will be further expanded and will promote understanding between the two countries.

During the meeting, Khrushchev held conversations with trade union workers and answered their questions.

### 赫鲁晓夫同戴高乐会谈  并和法国工会代表会见

1960-04-03
第4版()
专栏：

    赫鲁晓夫同戴高乐会谈
    并和法国工会代表会见
    新华社2日讯　塔斯社巴黎1日讯：苏联部长会议主席赫鲁晓夫和法国总统戴高乐1日上午在巴黎郊区的郎布依埃总统官邸举行了会谈。
    下午，在午餐以后继续进行了会谈。后来到郎布依埃参加会谈的，有苏联部长会议副主席柯西金和法国总理德勃雷。
    新华社2日讯　据塔斯社巴黎1日讯：3月31日，苏联部长会议主席赫鲁晓夫，同到他寓所去拜访他的法国工会代表们举行了友好的会见。赫鲁晓夫在会见时散发了一项声明。
    声明回顾了苏联和法国工人之间的传统的友好接触，并且指出苏联和法国两国人民为维护和平而斗争的任务。他提请大家注意苏联提出的全面彻底裁军建议和消除第二次世界大战的残余影响、同德国缔结和约的必要性。声明说，建立持久和平、制止军备竞赛将大大有助于改进劳动人民的处境。
    声明说，工人阶级的历史任务是废除人剥削人的制度和使人类免除战争的祸害。
    声明表示坚信，法国和苏联两国工人阶级的接触的范围将进一步扩大，并将促进两国之间的谅解。
    在会见时，赫鲁晓夫同工会工作者举行了交谈并且回答了他们的问题。
