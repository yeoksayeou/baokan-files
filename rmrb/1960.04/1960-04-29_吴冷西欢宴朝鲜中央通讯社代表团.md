Title: Wu Lengxi Hosts Banquet for Delegation from the Korean Central News Agency
Author:
Date: 1960-04-29
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, April 28th: Wu Lengxi, Director of the New China News Agency, hosted a banquet tonight in honor of the delegation from the Korean Central News Agency, headed by Ko Song-chun.

Present at the banquet was the Korean Ambassador to China, Li Yong-ho.

Also present were Shao Zonghan, Deputy Director of the Information Department of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs; Wang Mo, Deputy Director of the Newspaper Department of the Propaganda Department of the Central Committee; Hu Jiwei, Deputy Editor-in-Chief of the People's Daily; and heads of various departments of the Xinhua News Agency.

### 吴冷西欢宴朝鲜中央通讯社代表团

1960-04-29
第6版()
专栏：

    吴冷西欢宴朝鲜中央通讯社代表团
    新华社28日讯　新华通讯社社长吴冷西今晚设宴欢迎以高成淳为首的朝鲜中央通讯社代表团。
    出席宴会的有：朝鲜驻华大使李永镐。
    作陪的有：外交部新闻司副司长邵宗汉、中宣部报刊处副处长王谟，人民日报副总编辑胡绩伟及新华社各部门负责人等。
