Title: Sending Off the "Poetry"

Author: Guo Xiaochuan

Date: 1960-04-24

Page: 8

Supporting the Cuban People

    Sending Off the "Poetry"
    Guo Xiaochuan
    Not only with labor,
    But also with fervent hearts,
    Building a flying golden bridge;
    Not only with blood and sweat,
    But also with brotherly affection,
    Converging into a great winged river.
    The heroic golden bridge,
    Breaks through the hateful gaze of American pirates,
    Flying from every city to the streets of Havana.
    The bold great river,
    Crosses the black plumes of smoke above American warships,
    Flying from all over the world to the West Indies.
    This is our "Poetry,"
    The sun is your road sign,
    The people's friendship is your passport;
    This is our "Poetry,"
    The rosy clouds are your banner,
    The victorious east wind is your reliance.
    Hello, our friend Guillén,
    To defend your sweet country,
    We sincerely respond to your call;
    Hello, our friend Alberti,
    For the common struggle against imperialism,
    Let us embrace you tightly once again.
    Farewell, "Poetry,"
    We gaze at the distant southwest,
    Waiting for your red triumphant news;
    Farewell, "Poetry,"
    We listen intently to the roaring waves,
    Joyfully watching the mountains of Latin America roar.
    Good news is coming,
    "Poetry" shot the flag of the robber,
    American-made planes are burning beside the sugarcane fields;
    Good news is coming,
    "Poetry" hit the steering wheel of the enemy ship,
    Interventionists are swallowed in one gulp by the raging waves.
    Take off, golden bridge!
    For Cuba, for peace,
    Our people spare no sacrifice, fearing no hardship,
    Take off, great river,
    For our friends and brothers,
    Our poet-warriors have only a red heart.

### 为“诗歌号”送行
郭小川
1960-04-24
第8版()
专栏：支援古巴人民

    为“诗歌号”送行
    郭小川
    不仅用劳动，
    而且用热烈的心，
    架起一座飞行的金桥；
    不仅用血汗，
    而且用兄弟的情谊，
    汇成带翅膀的大江一条。
    英勇的金桥，
    冲开美国海盗的仇恨的目光，
    从各个城市飞向哈瓦那的街道。
    豪迈的大江，
    越过美国军舰上空的黑色烟柱，
    从世界各地飞向西印度群岛。
    这是我们的“诗歌号”，
    太阳是你的路标，
    人民的友爱是你的护照；
    这是我们的“诗歌号”，
    红霞是你的旗帜，
    胜利的东风是你的依靠。
    我们的朋友纪廉，你好，
    为了保卫你的甜美的国家，
    我们诚挚地响应你的号召；
    我们的朋友阿尔贝蒂，你好，
    为了共同的反对帝国主义斗争，
    让我们再一次紧紧把你拥抱。
    再会呵，“诗歌号”，
    我们凝望着遥远西南方，
    等候着你的红色的捷报；
    再会吧，“诗歌号”，
    我们谛听着海浪滔滔，
    喜看拉丁美洲的群山呼啸。
    佳音就要来了，
    “诗歌号”射中了强盗的旗子，
    美制的飞机在甘蔗田边燃烧；
    佳音就要来了，
    “诗歌号”击中了敌舰的方向盘，
    干涉者被惊涛骇浪一口吞掉。
    起飞吧，金桥！
    为了古巴，为了和平，
    我们的人民不惜牺牲不畏辛劳，
    起飞吧，大江，
    为了我们的朋友和兄弟，
    我们的诗人——战士只有赤心一条。
