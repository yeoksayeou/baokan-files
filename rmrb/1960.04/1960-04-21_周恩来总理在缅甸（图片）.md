Title: Premier Zhou Enlai in Burma (Pictures)
Author: Du Xiuxian
Date: 1960-04-21
Page: 5

Premier Zhou Enlai in Burma

    On April 18, Premier Zhou Enlai, Vice Premier Chen Yi, and other accompanying personnel visited the Thamaing State-owned Textile Mill in the northern suburbs of Yangon. This textile mill was built in 1958 with Chinese assistance. The picture shows Premier Zhou visiting the factory accompanied by Prime Minister U Nu (first person on the right in the front row).

    On April 18, Premier Zhou Enlai and Vice Premier Chen Yi met with 220 overseas Chinese representatives in Yangon. The picture shows Premier Zhou Enlai having a warm conversation with the overseas Chinese representatives.
                        Xinhua News Agency reporter Du Xiuxian

    A scene of Premier Zhou Enlai celebrating the Water Splashing Festival with Yangon residents during his visit to Burma.
                              (Xinhua News Agency draft)

### 周恩来总理在缅甸（图片）
杜修贤
1960-04-21
第5版()
专栏：

    周恩来总理在缅甸
    4月18日，周恩来总理、陈毅副总理和其他随行人员参观了仰光北郊达棉国营纺织厂。这家纺织厂是在中国的帮助下，在1958年建成的。图为周总理在吴努总理（右前第一人）陪同下参观工厂。
    周恩来总理、陈毅副总理于4月18日在仰光接见了二百二十名华侨代表。图为周恩来总理和华侨代表们进行热情谈话的情形。
                        新华社记者  杜修贤摄
    周恩来总理在缅甸访问时，与仰光居民欢度泼水节的情形
                              （新华社稿）
