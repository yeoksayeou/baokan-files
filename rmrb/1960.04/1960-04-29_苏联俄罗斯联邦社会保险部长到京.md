Title: Minister of Social Security of the Soviet Russian Federation Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1960-04-29
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, April 28th. At the invitation of Minister of Internal Affairs Qian Ying, Minister of Social Security of the Soviet Russian Federation, Muravyeva, and Director of the Bureau for Mothers with Large Families, Filimonova, arrived in Beijing by plane today for a friendly visit to our country. Those who greeted them at the airport included: Minister of Internal Affairs Qian Ying, Vice Ministers Chen Qiyuan, Wang Yifu, Guo Bingkun, and Zhang Yibai.

### 苏联俄罗斯联邦社会保险部长到京

1960-04-29
第6版()
专栏：

    苏联俄罗斯联邦社会保险部长到京
    新华社28日讯　应内务部部长钱瑛邀请前来我国进行友好访问的苏联俄罗斯联邦社会保险部部长穆拉维耶娃和多子女母亲管理局局长菲里莫诺娃，今天乘飞机到达北京。到机场迎接的有：内务部部长钱瑛，副部长陈其瑗、王一夫、郭炳坤、章夷白。
