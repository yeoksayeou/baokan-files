Title: Prominent Argentine Lawyer Alfaro Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1960-04-29
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, April 28th. Rodolfo Araoz Alfaro, a prominent Argentine lawyer, and his wife, Margarita Aguirre, a famous writer, arrived in Beijing by plane today at the invitation of the Chinese People's Association for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries.

### 阿根廷著名律师阿尔法罗到京

1960-04-29
第6版()
专栏：

    阿根廷著名律师阿尔法罗到京
    新华社28日讯　应中国人民对外文化协会邀请前来我国访问的阿根廷著名律师罗多尔夫·阿拉奥斯·阿尔法罗和他的夫人、著名作家玛格丽达·阿盖莱，今天乘飞机到达北京。
