Title: Sino-Hungarian Friendship Association Hosts Banquet for Delegation of Hungarian-Chinese Friendship Association
Author:
Date: 1960-04-01
Page: 3

Xinhua News Agency, 31st — The Chinese-Hungarian Friendship Association hosted a banquet tonight to welcome the delegation of the Hungarian-Chinese Friendship Association, led by Chairman Pátaki János, who were invited to China to participate in "Sino-Hungarian Friendship Week" and to pay a visit. Among those present at the banquet were Xiao San and He Luting, vice presidents of the Sino-Hungarian Friendship Association, Zhang Lianhua, Secretary-General, Yan Wenjing, director, Feng Shiyun, Minister of Propaganda of the All-China Federation of Trade Unions, Dang Ruxiang, Deputy Manager of Shijingshan Iron and Steel Company, and Chen Zibin, Secretary of the Party Committee of Huangtugang People's Commune.

Also present at the banquet were Luo Jun, Deputy Director of the Commission for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries, and Yu Zhan, Deputy Director of the Soviet Union and Eastern Europe Department of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs.

Szigeti, Chargé d'Affaires ad interim of the Hungarian Embassy in China, and Counselor Bai Nuoke were also invited to the banquet.

### 中匈友协设宴欢迎匈中友协代表团

1960-04-01
第3版()
专栏：

    中匈友协设宴欢迎匈中友协代表团
    新华社31日讯　中国匈牙利友好协会今晚设宴欢迎以帕塔基·亚诺什团长率领的应邀前来我国参加“中匈友好周”并进行访问的匈中友协代表团。出席宴会的有中匈友协副会长萧三、贺绿汀，秘书长张联华、理事严文井，中华全国总工会宣传部长冯诗云，石景山钢铁公司副经理党入庠，黄土岗人民公社党委书记陈子斌等。
    出席宴会的还有对外文委副主任罗俊，外交部苏联东欧司副司长余湛。
    匈牙利驻华大使馆临时代办西格蒂、参赞白诺克也应邀出席了宴会。
