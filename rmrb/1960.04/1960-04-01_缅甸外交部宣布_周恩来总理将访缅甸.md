Title: Burmese Foreign Ministry Announces Premier Zhou Enlai to Visit Burma
Author:
Date: 1960-04-01
Page: 5

    Burmese Foreign Ministry Announces
    Premier Zhou Enlai to Visit Burma
    Xinhua News Agency, Rangoon, March 31st. The Burmese Foreign Ministry announced here this afternoon that Premier Zhou Enlai and his entourage, at the invitation of the Burmese government, will pay an official visit to Burma from April 16th to 18th before going to India.

### 缅甸外交部宣布  周恩来总理将访缅甸

1960-04-01
第5版()
专栏：

    缅甸外交部宣布
    周恩来总理将访缅甸
    新华社仰光31日电　缅甸外交部今天下午在这里宣布，周恩来总理和他的随行人员应缅甸政府的邀请，将在去印度之前从4月16日到18日在缅甸进行正式访问。
