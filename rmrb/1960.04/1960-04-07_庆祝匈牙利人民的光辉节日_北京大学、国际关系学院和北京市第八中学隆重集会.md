Title: Celebrating the Glorious Festival of the Hungarian People: Peking University, the Institute of International Relations, and Beijing No. 8 Middle School Hold Grand Gatherings
Author:
Date: 1960-04-07
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, April 6th: Peking University faculty, staff, and students held a joint celebration tonight to commemorate the 15th anniversary of Hungary's liberation. János Pataki, head of the Hungarian-Chinese Friendship Association delegation, along with all members of the delegation, Barnok, Counselor of the Hungarian Embassy in China, some embassy staff, and Hungarian and international students studying at Peking University attended the celebration.

    Zhou Peiyuan, Vice President of Peking University, extended the warmest and most sincere festive greetings on behalf of the university's 20,000 faculty, staff, and students to the Hungarian Socialist Workers' Party, the Hungarian National Union of Students and Teachers, and the Hungarian students studying at Peking University.

    Gál András, a member of the Hungarian-Chinese Friendship Association delegation, introduced the development of Hungarian education over the past fifteen years and conveyed the congratulations of the students of Budapest University to the students of Peking University.

    Student representatives from Peking University and Hungarian student representatives exchanged congratulatory remarks at the meeting. A Soviet student representative also spoke at the meeting.

    Peking University students performed artistic programs at the celebration.

    Xinhua News Agency, April 5th: Beijing No. 8 Middle School and the Institute of International Relations held grand celebrations and get-togethers tonight to celebrate the 15th anniversary of Hungary's liberation.

    Attending the celebration at Beijing No. 8 Middle School were János Pataki, head of the Hungarian-Chinese Friendship Association delegation, and members István Simon, János Martin, and Baoduojin, an attache of the Hungarian Embassy in China, and others.

    Wang Shaoxun, Principal of Beijing No. 8 Middle School, warmly congratulated the guests and the teachers and students of Hungary on the festival on behalf of the entire faculty, staff, and students. Baoduojin, an attache of the Hungarian Embassy in China, introduced Hungary's achievements in socialist economic construction and cultural and educational undertakings over the past fifteen years. István Simon, a member of the Hungarian-Chinese Friendship Association delegation, poet, and winner of the Kossuth Prize, also spoke at the meeting.

    The faculty and students of the Institute of International Relations held a get-together tonight to celebrate the great festival of the Hungarian people together with Gál András, János Vass, members of the Hungarian-Chinese Friendship Association delegation, and Kádár and Jímbolás, attaches of the Hungarian Embassy in China. He Wushuang, Deputy Secretary of the Party Committee and Vice President of the Institute, and Gál András and Kádár spoke at the meeting. Students performed and recited dances and poems they had composed themselves praising Sino-Hungarian friendship. Hungarian students at the institute also performed Hungarian folk songs.

### 庆祝匈牙利人民的光辉节日  北京大学、国际关系学院和北京市第八中学隆重集会

1960-04-07
第6版()
专栏：

    庆祝匈牙利人民的光辉节日
    北京大学、国际关系学院和北京市第八中学隆重集会
    新华社6日讯　北京大学师生员工今晚举行庆祝匈牙利解放十五周年联欢晚会，匈中友协代表团团长帕塔基·亚诺什和代表团的全体团员，匈牙利驻华大使馆参赞白诺克和使馆部分工作人员，以及在北京大学学习的匈牙利和各国留学生都参加了联欢晚会。
    北京大学副校长周培源在联欢晚会上代表全校两万名师生员工，向匈牙利社会主义工人党、匈牙利全国大学生和教师以及在北京大学学习的匈牙利留学生表示最热烈最诚挚的节日祝贺。
    匈中友协代表团团员高劳·安德烈在晚会上向大家介绍了十五年来，匈牙利教育事业发展的情况，并转达了布达佩斯大学学生对北京大学学生的祝贺。
    北京大学学生代表和匈牙利留学生代表在会上互祝贺词，苏联留学生代表也在会上讲了话。
    北大学生在联欢会上演出了文艺节目。
    新华社5日讯　北京市第八中学和国际关系学院今晚隆重举行庆祝会和联欢会，庆祝匈牙利解放十五周年。
    到北京市第八中学参加庆祝会的有匈中友协代表团团长帕塔基·亚诺什和团员西蒙·依斯特万、马尔丁·亚诺什和匈牙利驻华大使馆随员包多金等。
    北京市第八中学校长王少勋代表全校师生员工，在庆祝会上向客人以及匈牙利的教师和学生热烈祝贺节日。匈牙利驻华大使馆随员包多金介绍了匈牙利十五年来社会主义经济建设和文化教育事业的成就。匈中友协代表团团员、诗人、科苏特奖金获得者西蒙·依斯特万也在会上讲了话。
    国际关系学院的师生们今晚举行联欢会，和匈中友协代表团团员高劳·安德烈、瓦什·亚诺什以及匈牙利驻华大使馆随员卡达尔、金波拉什一起欢庆匈牙利人民的伟大节日。学院的党委副书记、副院长何戊双和高劳·安德烈、卡达尔在会上讲了话。学生们在会上演出和朗诵了自己编的歌颂中匈友谊的舞蹈和诗歌，学院里的匈牙利留学生也演出了匈牙利的民间歌曲。
