Title: Fully Loaded with the Unity and Friendship of the Asian and African People, Our Delegation Leaves Guinea for Home
Author:
Date: 1960-04-20
Page: 7

Xinhua News Agency, Conakry, April 18th: The Chinese delegation attending the Second Asian-African People's Solidarity Conference, led by head of delegation Liao Chengzhi, left Conakry by plane this morning to return home.

Those who saw the delegation off at the airport included Guinean Minister of Public Works, Ismael Touré, and Minister of National Defence, Keita Fodeba, as well as the Chinese Ambassador to Guinea, Ke Hua, and personnel from the Chinese Embassy.

Guinean President Sékou Touré received the Chinese delegation on the evening of the 17th. Present at the reception were the Speaker of the Guinean National Assembly, Diallo Saifoulaye, the Minister of Economic Affairs, Louis Lansana Béavogui, the Minister of Public Works, Ismael Touré, and the Minister of National Defence, Keita Fodeba.

Also present was the Chinese Ambassador to Guinea, Ke Hua.

### 满载亚非人民团结与友谊而归  我代表团离几内亚回国

1960-04-20
第7版()
专栏：

    满载亚非人民团结与友谊而归
    我代表团离几内亚回国
    新华社科纳克里18日电　参加第二届亚非人民团结大会的中国代表团在团长廖承志率领下今天早晨乘飞机离开科纳克里回国。
    到机场欢送的有几内亚公共工程部长伊斯梅尔·杜尔和国防部长凯塔·福代巴，以及中国驻几内亚大使柯华和中国大使馆人员等。
    几内亚总统塞古·杜尔曾经在17日晚间接见了中国代表团。接见时在场的有几内亚国民议会议长迪阿洛·塞福拉耶、经济部长路易·兰萨纳·贝阿沃吉、公共工程部长伊斯梅尔·杜尔、国防部长凯塔·福代巴。
    在场的还有中国驻几内亚大使柯华。
