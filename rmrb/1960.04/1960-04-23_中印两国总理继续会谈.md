Title: Sino-Indian Prime Ministers Continue Talks
Author:
Date: 1960-04-23
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, New Delhi, April 22nd: Premier of the State Council of the People's Republic of China, Zhou Enlai, and Prime Minister of the Republic of India, Jawaharlal Nehru, continued their talks this morning.
