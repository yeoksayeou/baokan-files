Title: President U Win Maung Hosts Premier Zhou Enlai; Premiers of China and Burma Continue Talks and Decide to Issue Joint Communiqué; Premier Zhou Visits the Tatmadaw Cotton Mill in Rangoon and Receives a Warm Welcome; General Ne Win Hosts Luncheon for Premier Zhou Enlai and Vice Premier Chen Yi
Author:
Date: 1960-04-19
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, Rangoon, April 18th - Burmese President U Win Maung held a banquet tonight to express a warm welcome to Premier Zhou Enlai of the People's Republic of China, who is on a friendly visit to Burma.

Vice Premier and Foreign Minister Marshal Chen Yi, Vice Foreign Minister Zhang Hanfu, and other important members of the Chinese Premier's entourage also attended the banquet.

Also present at the banquet were Burmese Prime Minister U Nu, Burmese Chief of Staff General Ne Win, Chief Justice of the Supreme Court U Myint Thein, Speakers of both Houses of Parliament, Cabinet Ministers, senior officers of the armed forces, and prominent figures from Burmese society.

Chinese Ambassador to Burma Li Yimang, Burmese Ambassador to China Kyaing Wynn, and foreign diplomatic envoys stationed in Rangoon also attended the banquet.

Xinhua News Agency, Rangoon, April 18th - Premier Zhou Enlai of the People's Republic of China and Prime Minister U Nu of the Union of Burma continued their talks on the afternoon of April 18th.

During their talks this afternoon, Premier Zhou Enlai and Prime Minister U Nu decided to issue a joint communiqué, the contents of which will be released tomorrow.

Xinhua News Agency, Rangoon, April 18th - Premier Zhou Enlai, Vice Premier Chen Yi, and other members of their entourage received a warm welcome from the workers when they visited the state-owned Tatmadaw Cotton Mill in the northern suburbs of Rangoon this morning. Today was the first working day for the workers after the Burmese New Year.

This textile mill was built in 1958 with Chinese assistance. Many workers and technicians still remember the Chinese experts who helped them build the mill and learn how to operate the machinery. They were very happy to receive the visiting leaders of the Chinese government who had sent experts to help them. The national flags of China and Burma were hung side by side at the entrance of the factory and in front of the workshops to express their welcome to the distinguished Chinese guests. When Premier Zhou Enlai stepped out of the car, a group of young female workers in festive attire presented him with flowers.

Premier Zhou Enlai, accompanied by Prime Minister U Nu, visited the various workshops and carefully inspected each textile process. They examined the operation of textile machines manufactured in Zhengzhou, Shanghai, and Tianjin. Prime Minister U Nu told Premier Zhou Enlai that he had personally seen one of the factories that manufactured these machines during his last visit to China.

During his visit, Premier Zhou Enlai spoke with some of the workers and wished them success in their work.

This textile mill in the northern suburbs of Rangoon has 20,000 spindles and 196 looms, with good ventilation and air conditioning equipment. The factory uses cotton produced in Burma for its production.

Xinhua News Agency, Rangoon, April 18th - Burmese Chief of Staff General Ne Win and his wife hosted a luncheon here today for Premier Zhou Enlai and Vice Premier and Foreign Minister Marshal Chen Yi.

The Chinese side attending the luncheon included: Vice Foreign Minister Zhang Hanfu, Chinese Ambassador to Burma Li Yimang, and Chinese Military Attaché to Burma Jiang Keding.

The Burmese side included: Deputy Chief of Staff (Army) Brigadier Aung Gyi, Deputy Chief of Staff (Navy) Brigadier Than Pe, Deputy Chief of Staff (Air Force) Brigadier Clift, Brigadier Tin Pe, Colonel Maung Maung, Former Attorney General U Ba Sein, Former Foreign Minister U Khin Tun Aung, and Burmese Ambassador to China Kyaing Wynn.

### 吴温貌总统欢宴周总理  中缅两国总理继续会谈决定发表联合公报  周总理参观仰光达棉纺织厂受到热烈欢迎  奈温将军设午宴招待周恩来总理和陈毅副总理

1960-04-19
第1版()
专栏：

    吴温貌总统欢宴周总理
    中缅两国总理继续会谈决定发表联合公报
    周总理参观仰光达棉纺织厂受到热烈欢迎
    奈温将军设午宴招待周恩来总理和陈毅副总理
    新华社仰光18日电　缅甸联邦总统吴温貌今天晚上举行宴会，对到缅甸来进行友好访问的中华人民共和国国务院总理周恩来表示热烈的欢迎。
    副总理兼外交部长陈毅元帅、外交部副部长章汉夫和中国总理的其他重要随行人员也出席了宴会。
    出席宴会的还有缅甸联邦总理吴努，缅甸国防军总参谋长奈温将军，最高法院首席法官吴敏登，议会两院的议长，内阁部长们，武装部队的高级军官和缅甸社会著名人士。
    中国驻缅甸大使李一氓，缅甸驻中国大使叫温和外国驻仰光的外交使节也出席了宴会。
    新华社仰光18日电　中华人民共和国国务院总理周恩来和缅甸联邦总理吴努在4月18日下午继续举行会谈。
    周恩来总理和吴努总理在今天下午的会谈中决定发表一项联合公报，联合公报的内容将在明天发表。
    新华社仰光18日电　周恩来总理、陈毅副总理和其他随行人员今天上午在仰光北郊的达棉国营纺织厂参观时，受到工人们的热烈欢迎。今天，是工人们在缅甸新年后的第一个工作日。
    这家纺织厂是在中国的帮助下在1958年建成的。许多工人和技术人员仍然记得中国专家们帮助他们兴建这个纺织厂和帮助他们学习操作机器。他们非常高兴来接待曾经派遣专家帮助他们的前来访问的中国政府领导人。在工厂入口处和车间前面，并排挂起了中缅两国的国旗，以表示对中国贵宾们的欢迎。当周恩来总理步下汽车时，一批穿着节日服装的青年女工向他献了花。
    周恩来总理在吴努总理的陪同下，参观了各个车间，并仔细参观了每一个纺织工序。他们察看了在郑州、上海和天津制造的纺织机器的操作情况。吴努总理对周恩来总理说，他在上次访问中国时，亲自看到了制造这些机器的一家工厂。
    周恩来总理在参观时，同一些工人进行了谈话，并祝他们在工作中成功。
    在仰光北郊的这家纺织厂，有两万纱锭和一百九十六台织机，拥有良好的通风和冷气设备。这家工厂利用缅甸本国出产的棉花来进行生产。
    新华社仰光18日电　缅甸联邦国防军总参谋长奈温将军和夫人今天在这里举行午宴招待周恩来总理和副总理兼外交部长陈毅元帅。
    中国方面出席午宴的有：外交部副部长章汉夫，中国驻缅甸大使李一氓和中国驻缅甸大使馆武官蒋克定。
    缅甸方面有：副总参谋长（陆军）昂季准将，副总参谋长（海军）丹佩准将，副总参谋长（空军）克利夫特准将，丁佩准将，貌貌上校，前检察总长吴巴盛，前外交部长吴千吞昂，缅甸驻中国大使叫温。
