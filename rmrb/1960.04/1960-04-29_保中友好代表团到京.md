Title: Bulgarian-Chinese Friendship Delegation Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1960-04-29
Page: 6
Xinhua News Agency, April 28th. At the invitation of the Sino-Bulgarian Friendship Association, the Bulgarian-Chinese Friendship Delegation, consisting of five people and headed by Ivan Yotov, Secretary General of the Bulgarian Committee for Foreign Friendship and Cultural Relations, arrived in Beijing by plane today to participate in the May Day celebrations and pay a friendly visit to our country. Those who welcomed them at the airport included Nie Zhen, Vice President of the Sino-Bulgarian Friendship Association, and Xia Ting, Secretary-General, among others.

### 保中友好代表团到京

1960-04-29
第6版()
专栏：

    保中友好代表团到京
    新华社28日讯　应中保友好协会邀请前来我国参加五一节庆祝活动和进行友好访问的保中友好代表团一行五人，在团长、保加利亚对外友好和文化联络委员会秘书长伊万·约托夫率领下，今天乘飞机到达北京。到机场欢迎的有中保友好协会副会长聂真、秘书长夏霆等。
