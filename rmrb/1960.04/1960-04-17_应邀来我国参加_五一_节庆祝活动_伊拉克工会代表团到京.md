Title: Iraqi Trade Union Delegation Arrives in Beijing to Attend "May Day" Celebrations in China
Author:
Date: 1960-04-17
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, April 15th -- An Iraqi trade union delegation of ten members, led by Ali Shukr, Chairman of the Iraqi Trade Union Federation, and Hussein Alwan, Vice Chairman, arrived in Beijing by plane this afternoon.

They were invited by the All-China Federation of Trade Unions to come to China to participate in the "May Day" International Labor Day celebrations and for a visit.

### 应邀来我国参加“五一”节庆祝活动  伊拉克工会代表团到京

1960-04-17
第4版()
专栏：

    应邀来我国参加“五一”节庆祝活动
    伊拉克工会代表团到京
    新华社15日讯　伊拉克工会代表团一行十人，在伊拉克工会联合会主席阿里·舒克尔和副主席侯赛因·阿尔旺率领下，今天下午乘飞机到达北京。
    他们是应中华全国总工会的邀请，前来我国参加“五一”国际劳动节庆祝活动和访问的。
