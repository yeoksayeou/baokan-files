Title: Hungary and Czechoslovakia Sign Long-Term Trade Agreement
Author:
Date: 1960-04-01
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, Budapest, March 31st: According to a report in the Hungarian "People's Freedom," Hungary and Czechoslovakia signed a long-term trade agreement for 1961-1965 in Budapest on the 30th.

According to the agreement, the volume of trade between the two countries will increase by 60% in five years. Czechoslovakia will export agricultural machinery, cars and trucks, chemical and rubber industry equipment, coking coal, coke, wood, chemical raw materials and machine tools to Hungary. Hungary will export chain tractors, combine harvesters, gas generators, electric locomotives, aluminum, machine tools and construction machinery to Czechoslovakia.

### 匈捷签订长期贸易协定

1960-04-01
第5版()
专栏：

    匈捷签订长期贸易协定
    据新华社布达佩斯31日电　据匈牙利“人民自由报”报道，匈牙利与捷克斯洛伐克30日在布达佩斯签订了1961—1965年长期贸易协定。
    根据协定，五年内两国换货额将增加60％。捷克斯洛伐克将向匈牙利输出农业机器、小汽车和载重汽车、化学工业和橡胶工业的装备、炼焦煤、焦炭、木材、化学原料和机床等。匈牙利将向捷克斯洛伐克输出链子拖拉机、谷物收割机、煤气发电机、电气机车、铝、机床和建筑机器等。
