Title: Tong Yun, Member of the Central Committee of the Communist Youth League, Goes to Denmark
Author: 
Date: 1960-04-14
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency, April 12th: At the invitation of the Danish Communist Youth League, Tong Yun, member of the Central Committee of the Chinese Communist Youth League, and two others left Beijing today for Copenhagen to attend the 24th National Congress of the Danish Communist Youth League, which will be held on the 14th.

### 共青团中央委员仝云去丹麦

1960-04-14
第4版()
专栏：

    共青团中央委员仝云去丹麦
    新华社12日讯　应丹麦共产主义青年团的邀请，中国共产主义青年团中央委员会委员仝云等二人今天离开北京前往哥本哈根，参加将在14日举行的丹麦共产主义青年团第二十四次代表大会。
