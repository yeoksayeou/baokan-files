Title: National Chairman of the Youth League of the Congolese National Movement Party Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1960-04-21
Page: 4

    Xinhua News Agency, April 20th.  At the invitation of the All-China Youth Federation, Gebabu Dominique, National Chairman of the Youth League of the Congolese National Movement Party, arrived in Beijing by plane this afternoon for a visit to China.
    Wang Zhaohua, Standing Committee Member of All-China Youth Federation and Secretary of the Secretariat of the Central Committee of the Communist Youth League, and others welcomed him at the airport.

### 刚果民族运动党青年联盟全国主席到京

1960-04-21
第4版()
专栏：

    刚果民族运动党青年联盟全国主席到京
    新华社20日讯　应中华全国青年联合会邀请来我国访问的刚果民族运动党青年联盟全国主席格巴布·多米尼克，今天下午乘飞机到达北京。
    到机场欢迎的有中华全国青年联合会常委、共青团中央书记处书记王照华等人。
