Title: Opposing the Japan-U.S. Military Treaty and Nuclear Arms Race: Japanese People Begin 10,000-Li Peace March; Seven Armies Head to Tokyo, Calling on People to Struggle When Passing U.S. Military Bases
Author:
Date: 1960-04-26
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, Tokyo, April 25th: The 10,000-kilometer peace march organized by the Japanese people to oppose the new Japan-U.S. "Security Treaty," oppose Japan's nuclear armament, and demand the prohibition of nuclear weapons has officially begun.

The first team of the march set off from Yoron Island, the southernmost part of Japan, on the 21st. After crossing the sea by boat to Okinoerabu Island, they joined the delegation from Okinawa Island on the 22nd and marched together towards their destination, Tokyo.

The 10,000-kilometer peace march of the Japanese people is being conducted nationwide under the auspices of the Japan Council Against Atomic and Hydrogen Bombs, and the number of people expected to participate in the march successively will reach 30 million. The march from Yoron Island to Tokyo is part of the entire march. In addition, marching teams starting from Hokkaido and Kagoshima and six other places will depart successively from May 1st.

The seven marching teams will arrive in Tokyo on August 5th, when the Sixth World Conference Against Atomic and Hydrogen Bombs and for General Disarmament opens, in order to express to the world conference the firm will of the Japanese people to oppose the Japan-U.S. military alliance and demand peace. On the way, the marching teams will pass through 214 U.S. military bases and Japanese "Self-Defense Force" military bases in Japan, calling on the people in these places to fight against the ratification of the new "Security Treaty" and against nuclear weapons. According to the Japan Council Against Atomic and Hydrogen Bombs' announcement on April 16th, 30 million people will participate in this peace march.

### 反对日美军事条约和核军备竞赛  日本人民开始万里和平大游行  七路大军奔向东京　途经美军基地时将呼吁人民斗争

1960-04-26
第6版()
专栏：

    反对日美军事条约和核军备竞赛
    日本人民开始万里和平大游行
    七路大军奔向东京　途经美军基地时将呼吁人民斗争
    新华社25日讯　东京消息：日本人民为反对新日美“安全条约”、反对日本核武装、要求禁止核武器而组织的一万公里和平大游行，已经正式开始。
    游行的第一支队伍在21日从日本最南瑞的与论岛出发。他们乘船渡海到冲永良部岛以后，在22日同冲绳岛的代表团汇合，一起向目的地东京进发。
    日本人民一万公里和平大游行是由日本禁止原子弹和氢弹协议会主持在全国范围内举行的，预定先后参加游行的人数将达三千万。从与论岛到东京的游行是整个大游行的一部分。另外，分别由北海道和鹿儿岛等六个地方为起点的游行队伍将于5月1日起陆续出发。
    七路游行队伍将于8月5日第六届禁止原子弹氢弹和争取全面裁军世界大会开幕时到达东京，以便向世界大会表示日本人民反对日美军事同盟、要求和平的坚决意志。途中，游行队伍将经过日本境内的二百一十四个美军和日本“自卫队”的军事基地，呼吁这些地方的人民为反对批准新“安全条约”和反对核武器而斗争。据日本禁止原子弹和氢弹协议会4月16日宣布，参加这次和平大游行的将达三千万人。
