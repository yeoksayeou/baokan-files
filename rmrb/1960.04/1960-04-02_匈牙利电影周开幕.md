Title: Hungarian Film Week Opens
Author:
Date: 1960-04-02
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency, April 1st: The "Hungarian People's Republic Film Week," jointly organized by the Ministry of Culture, the Commission for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries, and the Sino-Hungarian Friendship Association, held its opening ceremony this afternoon at the Capital Theater.

    Xia Yan, Vice Minister of Culture, spoke at the opening ceremony. He said that in order to celebrate the 15th anniversary of Hungary's liberation and further enhance the friendship and cultural exchange between the people of our two countries, the "Hungarian People's Republic Film Week" will be held in various parts of our country from April 1st. The three films screened during this film week, "The Thirty-Ninth Brigade," "Yesterday," and "Saint Peter's Umbrella," reflect the past and present lives and struggles of the Hungarian people from different historical perspectives.

    Szigeti, Chargé d'Affaires ad interim of the Embassy of the Hungarian People's Republic in China, spoke at the opening ceremony. He said that the rich and colorful activities of the Sino-Hungarian Friendship Week demonstrate the close cooperation between socialist brotherly countries headed by the Soviet Union. He also talked about Hungary's work and achievements in various fields over the past fifteen years.

    The delegation from the Sino-Hungarian Friendship Association headed by Pátaki János, and the Central Art Troupe of the Hungarian Communist Youth League headed by Vadas László and Görecz Péter, who came to our country to participate in the Sino-Hungarian Friendship Week activities, attended today's opening ceremony.

    Vice Minister of Foreign Affairs Zeng Yongquan, Deputy Director of the Commission for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries Zhang Zhixiang, Vice President of the Sino-Hungarian Friendship Association Xiao San, Chairman of the China Film Workers Association Cai Chusheng, and others also attended the opening ceremony. "The Thirty-Ninth Brigade" was screened after the opening ceremony.

    The "Hungarian People's Republic Film Week" will be held around April 4th in eight cities including Beijing, Tianjin, Chengdu, Guangzhou, Wuhan, Xi'an, Changchun, and Jinan.

### 匈牙利电影周开幕

1960-04-02
第4版()
专栏：

    匈牙利电影周开幕
    据新华社1日讯　文化部、对外文委和中国匈牙利友好协会联合举办的“匈牙利人民共和国电影周”，今天下午在首都剧场举行开幕式。
    文化部副部长夏衍在开幕式上讲话。他说，为了庆祝匈牙利解放十五周年，进一步增强我们两国人民间的友谊和文化交流，从4月1日起，在我国各地举办“匈牙利人民共和国电影周”。这次电影周放映的三部影片“第三十九旅”、“昨天”、“圣彼得的伞”，从不同的历史侧面，反映了匈牙利人民过去和现在的生活和斗争。
    匈牙利人民共和国驻华大使馆临时代办西格蒂在开幕式上讲了话。他说，中匈友好周丰富多采的活动，显示了以苏联为首的社会主义兄弟国家间的紧密合作。他还谈到匈牙利十五年来在各方面的工作和成就。
    前来我国参加中匈友好周活动的以帕塔基·亚诺什为首的匈中友协代表团、以瓦达斯·拉斯洛和哥雷格·彼得为首的匈牙利共青团中央艺术团参加了今天的开幕式。
    外交部副部长曾涌泉、对外文化联络委员会副主任张致祥、中匈友好协会副会长萧三、中国电影工作者联谊会主席蔡楚生等也参加了开幕式。开幕式后放映了“第三十九旅”。
    “匈牙利人民共和国电影周”4日前后将在北京、天津、成都、广州、武汉、西安、长春、济南等八个城市举行。
