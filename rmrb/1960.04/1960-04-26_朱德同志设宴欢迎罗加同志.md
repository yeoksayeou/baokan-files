Title: Comrade Zhu De Hosts Banquet in Honor of Comrade Roca
Author:
Date: 1960-04-26
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, April 25th: Comrade Zhu De, Vice-Chairman of the CPC Central Committee, held a banquet tonight to welcome Blas Roca, General Secretary of the Cuban People's Socialist Party, and his wife, who are visiting China. He also had a warm and cordial conversation with them.

Those attending the banquet included: Deng Xiaoping, General Secretary of the CPC Central Committee; Peng Zhen, Li Fuchun, and He Long, members of the Political Bureau; Kang Sheng, alternate member of the Political Bureau; Liu Lantao, Yang Shangkun, and Hu Qiaomu, alternate members of the Secretariat; and Cai Chang, Liu Ningyi, Wu Xiuquan, and other members of the Central Committee. (Attached picture)

### 朱德同志设宴欢迎罗加同志

1960-04-26
第1版()
专栏：

    朱德同志设宴欢迎罗加同志
    新华社25日讯　中共中央副主席朱德今晚举行宴会，欢迎前来中国访问的古巴人民社会党总书记布拉斯·罗加和他的夫人，并且同他们进行了热情亲切的交谈。
    参加宴会的有：中共中央总书记邓小平，政治局委员彭真、李富春、贺龙，政治局候补委员康生，书记处候补书记刘澜涛、杨尚昆、胡乔木和中央委员蔡畅、刘宁一、伍修权等。（附图片）
    在宴会上，左起：邓小平、布拉斯·罗加、朱德
            　新华社记者　李长永摄
