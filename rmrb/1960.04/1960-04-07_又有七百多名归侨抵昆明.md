Title: Another 700+ Returned Overseas Chinese Arrive in Kunming
Author:
Date: 1960-04-07
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Kunming, 6th. Today, another 700+ returned overseas Chinese from Indonesia arrived in Kunming, and were warmly welcomed by more than 5,000 people from all ethnic groups in Kunming. These returned overseas Chinese will settle down in Yunnan Province.

　　The returned overseas Chinese received a warm reception from the people of Kunming as soon as they got off the train today. Cui Jiequan, Standing Committee Member of the CPC Kunming Municipal Committee and Deputy Mayor of Kunming, Wang Bo, Deputy Director of the Office of the Yunnan Provincial Reception and Resettlement Committee for Returned Overseas Chinese, and others stepped forward to shake hands with the returned overseas Chinese. More than 40 representatives of Indonesian returned overseas Chinese, including Liao Shilong, who arrived in Kunming on the 2nd, also came to welcome them today. At around 5:00 p.m., all sectors of Kunming held a welcome meeting for this group of returned overseas Chinese. Cui Jiequan, on behalf of the CPC Kunming Municipal Committee, the Kunming Municipal People's Committee, and the one million plus people of Kunming, expressed a warm welcome to the returned overseas Chinese. Gu Haiming and Zhang Guangrong, representatives of the returned overseas Chinese, also spoke at the meeting.

### 又有七百多名归侨抵昆明

1960-04-07
第4版()
专栏：

    又有七百多名归侨抵昆明
    新华社昆明6日电　今天又有七百多名从印度尼西亚归国的华侨到达昆明，受到了昆明市各族各界五千多人的热烈欢迎。这些归侨将在云南省安家落户。
    侨胞们今天一下车就受到了昆明市人民的亲切接待。中共昆明市委常委、昆明市副市长崔洁泉，云南省接待和安置归国华侨委员会办公室副主任王波等都上前同侨胞们握手，2日到达昆明的印度尼西亚归侨的代表廖世隆等四十多人，今天也前来欢迎。下午五点多钟，昆明市各界为这批侨胞举行了欢迎会。崔洁泉代表中共昆明市委、昆明市人民委员会和昆明市一百多万人民，向归侨表示热烈欢迎。归侨代表古海铭和张广荣也在会上讲了话。
