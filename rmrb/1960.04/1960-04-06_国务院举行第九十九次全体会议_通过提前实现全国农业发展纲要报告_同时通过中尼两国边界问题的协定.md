Title: State Council Holds 99th Plenary Meeting, Passes Report on Achieving National Agricultural Development Program Ahead of Schedule, and Passes Agreement on Sino-Nepalese Boundary Issue
Author: 
Date: 1960-04-06
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, 5th — The State Council held its 99th plenary meeting this morning to discuss and pass the report "Striving to Achieve the National Agricultural Development Program Ahead of Schedule" which will be delivered to the Second Session of the Second National People's Congress by Tan Zhenlin, Secretary of the Secretariat of the CPC Central Committee and Vice Premier of the State Council.
    Today's meeting also discussed and passed the "Agreement between the Government of the People's Republic of China and the Government of His Majesty the King of Nepal on the Boundary Question" and decided to submit this agreement to the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress for deliberation and approval.

### 国务院举行第九十九次全体会议  通过提前实现全国农业发展纲要报告  同时通过中尼两国边界问题的协定

1960-04-06
第1版()
专栏：

    国务院举行第九十九次全体会议
    通过提前实现全国农业发展纲要报告
    同时通过中尼两国边界问题的协定
    新华社5日讯　国务院在今天上午举行第九十九次全体会议，讨论和通过了中共中央书记处书记、国务院副总理谭震林将向第二届全国人民代表大会第二次会议作的“为提前实现全国农业发展纲要而奋斗”的报告。
    今天的会议还讨论和通过了“中华人民共和国政府和尼泊尔国王陛下政府关于两国边界问题的协定”，并且决定将这个协定提请全国人民代表大会常务委员会审议批准。
