Title: Algerian Youth Delegation Leader Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1960-04-17
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, April 16th: Chabi Hashimi, leader of the Algerian Youth Delegation and Deputy Director of the Youth and Sports Committee of the Algerian National Liberation Front, and Chaiguo Xie Delazi, a member of the Algerian Student Delegation, arrived in Beijing by plane this afternoon.

Those who welcomed them at the airport included: Li Menghua, Vice Chairman of the All-China Youth Federation; Hu Qili, Chairman of the All-China Students' Federation; and Qian Dawei, Deputy Director of the International Liaison Department of the All-China Youth Federation.

The Algerian Youth Delegation and Student Delegation are visiting China at the invitation of the All-China Youth Federation and the All-China Students' Federation.

The members of the Algerian Youth Delegation, the leader of the Algerian Student Delegation, and other members will arrive in Beijing in the coming days.

### 阿尔及利亚青年代表团团长抵京

1960-04-17
第4版()
专栏：

    阿尔及利亚青年代表团团长抵京
    新华社16日讯　阿尔及利亚青年代表团团长、阿尔及利亚民族解放阵线青年及体育委员会副主任夏比·哈息米，阿尔及利亚学生代表团团员柴果谢·德拉齐，今天下午乘飞机到北京。
    到机场欢迎的有：中华全国青年联合会副主席李梦华，中华全国学生联合会主席胡启立，全国青联国际联络部副部长钱大卫。
    阿尔及利亚青年代表团和学生代表团是应中华全国青年联合会和中华全国学生联合会的邀请来我国访问的。
    阿尔及利亚青年代表团的团员和阿尔及利亚学生代表团的团长和其他团员，将于日内到达北京。
