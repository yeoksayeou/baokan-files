Title: Finnish Communist Party Congress Concludes Victoriously
Author:
Date: 1960-04-22
Page: 5
Xinhua News Agency, Helsinki, 19th: The Twelfth Congress of the Finnish Communist Party concluded victoriously yesterday in Helsinki amidst the powerful strains of "The Internationale." In announcing the closing of the congress, Party Chairman Aaltonen stated that many members of our party are infinitely loyal to the principles of Marxism-Leninism. Our party will hold high the banner of proletarian international solidarity.

The congress elected the Party's Central Committee and the Supervisory Committee. Subsequently, the Central Committee held a plenary session, re-electing Pessi as General Secretary of the Party, Aaltonen as Chairman, and Murto as Vice Chairman. The Central Committee plenary session also elected the Politburo.

The congress adopted the Central Committee's report, as well as a resolution on the Party's tasks in the recent period. It also passed an appeal for strengthened worker cooperation, a statement celebrating International Labor Day ("May Day"), and an appeal celebrating the sixtieth anniversary of the Finnish women's movement.

The congress also unanimously demanded that West German authorities abolish the ban on the activities of the German Communist Party.

### 芬共代表大会胜利闭幕

1960-04-22
第5版()
专栏：

    芬共代表大会胜利闭幕
    据新华社19日讯　赫尔辛基消息：芬兰共产党第十二次代表大会昨天在“国际歌”的雄壮歌声中胜利闭幕。党的主席阿尔托宁在宣布大会闭幕时说，我们党的许多党员对马克思列宁主义原则无限忠诚。我们的党将高举无产阶级国际团结的旗帜。
    大会选出了党的中央委员会和监察委员会。然后中央委员会举行全体会议，再次选出培西为党的总书记、阿尔托宁为主席、穆尔托为副主席。中央委员会全体会议还选出了政治局。
    大会通过了中央委员会的报告以及党在最近一个时期的任务的决议，并且通过了加强工人合作的呼吁书、庆祝“五一”节的声明和庆祝芬兰妇女运动六十周年的呼吁书。
    代表大会还一致要求西德当局废除禁止德国共产党活动的禁令。
