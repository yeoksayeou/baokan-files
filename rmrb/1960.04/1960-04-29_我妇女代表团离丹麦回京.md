Title: Our Women's Delegation Leaves Denmark for Beijing
Author:
Date: 1960-04-29
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, Copenhagen, April 27th. The Chinese women's delegation attending the International Women's Congress celebrating the 50th anniversary of International Women's Day, led by its head Li Dequan, left here today to return to Beijing.

On the eve of the delegation's departure from Copenhagen, the Danish-Chinese Association held a reception for them.

### 我妇女代表团离丹麦回京

1960-04-29
第6版()
专栏：

    我妇女代表团离丹麦回京
    新华社哥本哈根27日电　中国妇女出席庆祝国际妇女节五十周年国际妇女大会的代表团，在团长李德全率领下今天离开这里回北京。
    在代表团离开哥本哈根的前夕，丹（麦）—中协会为她们举行了招待会。
