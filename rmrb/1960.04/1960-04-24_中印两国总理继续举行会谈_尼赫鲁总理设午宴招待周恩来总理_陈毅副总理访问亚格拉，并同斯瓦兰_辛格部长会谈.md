Title: Chinese and Indian Premiers Continue Talks; Premier Nehru Hosts Luncheon for Premier Zhou Enlai; Vice Premier Chen Yi Visits Agra and Meets with Minister Swaran Singh
Author:
Date: 1960-04-24
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, New Delhi, April 23rd: Premier of the State Council of the People's Republic of China, Zhou Enlai, and Prime Minister of the Republic of India, Jawaharlal Nehru, continued their talks this afternoon.

Xinhua News Agency, New Delhi, April 23rd: Indian Prime Minister Jawaharlal Nehru hosted a luncheon today for Premier Zhou Enlai.  Attending the luncheon were Zhang Yan, Deputy Director of the Foreign Affairs Office of the State Council of China, Assistant Minister of Foreign Affairs Qiao Guanhua, and Director of the First Asian Affairs Department Zhang Wenjin.

Also attending the luncheon were Indian Minister of Railways Jagjivan Ram, Minister of Commerce and Industry Lal Bahadur Shastri, Minister of Public Works, Housing and Supply Kishenbhai Chhangalal Raya Reddy, Minister of Defence Vengalil Krishnan Krishna Menon, and Minister of Transport P. Subbarayan.

Also attending the luncheon were Chinese Ambassador to India Pan Zili and Indian Ambassador to China G. Parthasarathi.

Xinhua News Agency, New Delhi, April 23rd:  Chinese Deputy Premier and Minister of Foreign Affairs Marshal Chen Yi and Vice Minister of Foreign Affairs Zhang Hanfu, accompanied by Indian Minister of Steel, Mines, and Fuel Swaran Singh, visited Agra today. This morning, accompanied by local Indian officials, they visited the famous Taj Mahal and Agra Fort.  In the afternoon, they held talks with Indian Minister of Steel, Mines, and Fuel Swaran Singh.

Xinhua News Agency, New Delhi, April 22nd: Chinese Deputy Premier and Minister of Foreign Affairs Marshal Chen Yi once again received Indian Minister of Steel, Mines, and Fuel Swaran Singh this morning.  Also present at the meeting was Vice Minister of Foreign Affairs Zhang Hanfu. In the afternoon, Director of the First Asian Affairs Department of the Chinese Ministry of Foreign Affairs, Zhang Wenjin, and two other Chinese officials visited the Indian Ministry of Foreign Affairs and discussed the Sino-Indian border issue with Indian Ministry of Foreign Affairs officials. The Chinese and Indian Premiers will meet again on the 23rd.

### 中印两国总理继续举行会谈  尼赫鲁总理设午宴招待周恩来总理  陈毅副总理访问亚格拉，并同斯瓦兰·辛格部长会谈

1960-04-24
第1版()
专栏：

    中印两国总理继续举行会谈
    尼赫鲁总理设午宴招待周恩来总理
    陈毅副总理访问亚格拉，并同斯瓦兰·辛格部长会谈
    新华社新德里23日电中华人民共和国国务院总理周恩来和印度共和国总理贾瓦哈拉尔·尼赫鲁今天下午继续举行了会谈。
    新华社新德里23日电印度总理贾瓦哈拉尔·尼赫鲁今天举行午宴招待周恩来总理。出席午宴的有中国国务院外事办公室副主任张彦，外交部部长助理乔冠华和第一亚洲司司长章文晋。
    出席午宴的还有印度铁道部长贾格吉凡·拉姆，工商部长拉尔·巴哈杜尔·夏斯特里，公共工程、住房和供应部长基桑巴利·钱加拉腊雅·雷迪，国防部长文加利尔·克里希南·克里希纳·梅农，交通运输部长普·苏巴拉延。
    出席午宴的还有：中国驻印度大使潘自力和印度驻中国大使高·帕塔萨拉蒂。
    新华社新德里23日电　中国副总理兼外交部长陈毅元帅和外交部副部长章汉夫今天在印度钢铁、矿业和燃料部长斯瓦兰·辛格陪同下前往亚格拉访问。他们在上午在印度当地官员的陪同下参观了著名的泰吉·玛哈尔陵和亚格拉堡。下午他们同印度钢铁、矿业和燃料部长斯瓦兰·辛格举行了会谈。
    新华社新德里22日电中国国务院副总理兼外交部长陈毅元帅今天上午再次接见了印度钢铁、矿业和燃料部长斯瓦兰·辛格。接见时在座的有外交部副部长章汉夫。下午，中国外交部第一亚洲司司长章文晋和其它两位中国官员拜访了印度外交部，并同印度外交部官员讨论了中印边界问题。中国和印度两国总理将在23日再次会谈。
