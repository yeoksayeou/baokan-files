Title: Yang Hansheng Hosts Banquet to Welcome Japan-China Friendship Association Delegation
Author:
Date: 1960-04-25
Page: 2

Xinhua News Agency, April 24th: Yang Hansheng, Vice President of the Chinese People's Association for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries, hosted a banquet tonight to welcome all members of the Japan-China Friendship Association delegation visiting China, as well as accompanying journalists.

Yang Hansheng, on behalf of the Association for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries, welcomed the Japan-China Friendship Association delegation with fraternal feelings. He said that the Japan-China Friendship Association has carried out active struggles in difficult circumstances against the new Japan-U.S. "Security Treaty" and to promote the normalization of Japan-China diplomatic relations. He expressed his belief that in this struggle, final victory will surely belong to the Japanese people.  Masuzo Akatsu, head of the delegation, said in his speech that they would continue to oppose the reactionary policies pursued by the Nobusuke Kishi government and its conspiracy to collude with U.S. imperialism, and would work hard for the friendship between the Chinese and Japanese people.

Among those attending the banquet were Cai Tingkai, Lü Ji, Yang Jin, Zhao Anbo, Lin Lin, Sun Pinghua, Jin Ziming, Xie Nanguang, Xiao Xiangqian, Ding Tuo, and other people from various circles.

### 阳翰笙设宴欢迎日中友协代表团

1960-04-25
第2版()
专栏：

    阳翰笙设宴欢迎日中友协代表团
    新华社24日讯　中国人民对外文化协会副会长阳翰笙今晚设宴欢迎日中友好协会访华代表团全体团员和同行的新闻界人士。
    阳翰笙代表对外文化协会以兄弟的心情来欢迎日中友协访华代表团，他说，日中友好协会在困难的环境下，为反对新日美“安全条约”以及促进日中邦交正常化进行了积极的斗争。他表示相信在这个斗争中，最后胜利一定属于日本人民。代表团团长赤津益造在讲话中表示要继续反对岸信介政府执行的反动政策以及它同美帝国主义勾结的阴谋，并为中日两国人民的友好而努力。
    参加宴会的有蔡廷锴、吕骥、杨进、赵安博、林林、孙平化、金子明、谢南光、萧向前、丁拓等各界人士。
