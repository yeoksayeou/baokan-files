Title: 600,000 Mu of Land Reclaimed in Hotan Prefecture

Author:
Date: 1960-04-05
Page: 5

Brief News

    600,000 Mu of Land Reclaimed in Hotan Prefecture
    The Hotan Prefecture of the Xinjiang Uygur Autonomous Region is making every effort to expand the area sown with grain. The entire prefecture has reclaimed 600,000 mu of land, exceeding this year's land reclamation task by 200,000 mu. The achievement of one year's reclamation is equivalent to more than 80% of the total reclaimed area in the entire prefecture in the ten years since liberation.

### 和田专区开荒造田60万亩

1960-04-05
第5版()
专栏：简要新闻

    和田专区开荒造田60万亩
    新疆维吾尔自治区和田专区千方百计扩大粮食播种面积。全专区已开荒造田六十万亩，超额二十万亩完成了今年的开荒造田任务。一年开荒的成绩等于全专区解放十年来开荒面积总和的80％多。
