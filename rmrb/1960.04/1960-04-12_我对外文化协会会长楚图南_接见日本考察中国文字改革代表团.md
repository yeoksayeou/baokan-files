Title: China's Association for Cultural Exchange with Foreign Countries President Chu Tunan Receives Japanese Delegation for Investigating Chinese Character Reform
Author:
Date: 1960-04-12
Page: 6

    Xinhua News Agency, April 11th: Chu Tunan, President of the Chinese People's Association for Cultural Exchange with Foreign Countries, today received all members of the Japanese academic delegation for investigating Chinese character reform, headed by the renowned Japanese linguist Toki Zemmaro, and held cordial talks with them. Present at the meeting were Zhou Erfu, Secretary-General of the Chinese People's Association for Cultural Exchange with Foreign Countries, and Deputy Secretaries-General Lin Lin and Sun Pinghua, as well as Zuo Gong, Deputy Director of the Beijing Library, and others. After the meeting, the Chinese People's Association for Cultural Exchange with Foreign Countries held a film soiree to entertain all members of the delegation.

### 我对外文化协会会长楚图南  接见日本考察中国文字改革代表团

1960-04-12
第6版()
专栏：

    我对外文化协会会长楚图南
    接见日本考察中国文字改革代表团
    新华社11日讯　中国人民对外文化协会会长楚图南今天接见了以日本著名语言学家土岐善麿为首的日本考察中国文字改革学术代表团全体团员，并进行了亲切的谈话。接见时，在座的有中国人民对外文化协会秘书长周而复，副秘书长林林、孙平化，北京图书馆副馆长左恭等。接见后，中国人民对外文化协会举行电影晚会，招待代表团全体团员。
