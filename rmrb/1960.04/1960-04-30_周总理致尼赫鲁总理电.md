Title: Premier Zhou's Telegram to Prime Minister Nehru
Author:
Date: 1960-04-30
Page: 2

Xinhua News Agency, April 29th: Premier Zhou Enlai's telegram to Indian Prime Minister Nehru.

To His Excellency Prime Minister Nehru of the Republic of India, New Delhi:

    As I fly over Indian territory on my return trip from Kathmandu, I would like to once again extend my greetings to Your Excellency and the people of India.

            Zhou Enlai
            Premier of the State Council of the People's Republic of China
            April 29, 1960

### 周总理致尼赫鲁总理电

1960-04-30
第2版()
专栏：

    周总理致尼赫鲁总理电
    新华社29日讯　周恩来总理给印度总理尼赫鲁的电文新德里印度共和国尼赫鲁总理阁下阁下：
    在我从加德满都返国途中飞越印度的国土的时候，我谨再次向阁下和印度人民致意。
            中华人民共和国国务院总理  周恩来
                        一九六○年四月二十九日
