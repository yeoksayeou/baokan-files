Title: Mongolian Economic Delegation Expert Group Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1960-04-05
Page: 6

    Xinhua News Agency, April 4th. An expert group of the Mongolian government economic delegation, headed by Tseven, Vice Chairman of the State Planning Commission of the Mongolian People's Republic, arrived in Beijing by train today.
    Those who went to the station to welcome them included Lu Xuzhang, Vice Minister of Foreign Trade, Liu Yumin, Vice Minister of Construction Engineering, Luo Shuzhang, Vice Minister of Light Industry, Li Qiangfen, Deputy Director of the Second Asian Department of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs, and Lu Ping, Deputy Director of the Foreign Economic Bureau of the State Planning Commission.
    Mongolian Ambassador to China, Sharab, and Economic and Commercial Counselor, Dorji, also greeted them at the station.

### 蒙古经济代表团专家组抵京

1960-04-05
第6版()
专栏：

    蒙古经济代表团专家组抵京
    新华社4日讯　以蒙古人民共和国国家计划委员会副主席策文为首的蒙古政府经济代表团专家组在今天乘火车到达北京。
    前往车站欢迎的有对外贸易部副部长卢绪章、建筑工程部副部长刘裕民、轻工业部副部长罗叔章、外交部第二亚洲司副司长李强奋、国家计划委员会对外经济局副局长鲁平等人。
    蒙古驻华大使沙拉布、经济及商务参赞道尔吉等也到车站迎接。
