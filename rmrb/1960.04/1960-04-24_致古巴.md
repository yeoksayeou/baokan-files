Title: To Cuba
Author: Tian Jian
Date: 1960-04-24
Page: 8

    To Cuba
    Tian Jian
    Latin America roars,
    The Caribbean Sea boils,
    The Sierra Maestra peaks,
    Echo with bursts of spring thunder,
    Thousands of miles of thunder, ten thousand miles of lightning,
    The chains of slaves have been shattered.
    The flag of freedom is raised,
    The sickle also joins the battle,
    We hear here,
    Your glorious declaration—
    Defend the revolutionary regime,
    Never retreat forward.
    Let the spring thunder sound louder,
    Never give way forward;
    The flag has already been raised,
    Never to be lowered again;
    Alberti said it well:
    "Those who kill will perish!"
    The morning sun has appeared at sea,
    The east wind sings for you;
    Freedom, democracy, independence,
    This is the life of the people;
    Sugarcane groves, palm trees,
    These are the tents of heroes.
    Let the spring thunder sound louder,
    We will speak for you;
    Whoever dares to ignite the flames of war,
    We will be your backing;
    We will lift the Kunlun Mountains high,
    As the iron fist of the people.
    Sugarcane is a sweet tree,
    Why is there bitterness?
    Could it not be the Americans,
    Who sprinkled poison and bitter water.
    Cuba will turn the sugarcane fields,
    Into a garden of happiness.
    Guillén says in the sugarcane fields,
    There is an arrow that is eternally launched.
    Let us hold up the ocean,
    To raise a glass for this sharp arrow!
    For all revolutionary fighters,
    Offer flowers and poems!

### 致古巴
田间
1960-04-24
第8版()
专栏：支援古巴人民

    致古巴
    田间
    拉丁美洲怒吼了，
    加勒比海沸腾了，
    马埃斯特腊山峰，
    响起一阵阵春雷，
    千里雷声万里闪，
    奴隶的锁链已被粉碎。
    自由的旗子升起，
    镰刀也参加作战，
    我们在这里听见，
    你的光荣的宣言——
    保卫革命政权，
    向前决不后退。
    春雷响吧更响些，
    向前决不退让；
    旗子已经升起了，
    再也不会下降；
    阿尔贝蒂说得好：
    “杀人者自取灭亡！”
    海上已出现朝阳，
    东风为你们歌唱；
    自由、民主、独立，
    这是人民的生命；
    甘蔗林子棕榈树，
    这是英雄的篷帐。
    春雷响吧更响些，
    我们要为你发言；
    谁敢来点起战火，
    我们做你的后盾；
    要把昆仑山高高举起，
    当作人民的铁拳。
    甘蔗本是甜蜜树，
    为何有了苦味？
    难道不是美国佬，
    撒下毒药和苦水。
    古巴要把甘蔗田，
    变成一座幸福园。
    纪廉说甘蔗田里，
    有一支永恒发射的箭。
    让我们捧起海洋，
    为这支利箭举杯！
    为一切革命战士，
    献上花朵和诗篇！
