Title: Foreign Cultural Relations Commission Hosts Cultural and Educational Representatives from Fraternal Countries
Author:
Date: 1960-04-30
Page: 7
Xinhua News Agency, April 29th: The Director of the Foreign Cultural Relations Commission, Zhang Xiruo, held a banquet at the Qianmen Hotel tonight to celebrate International Labor Day with representatives from cultural, artistic, educational, scientific, and health delegations from fraternal countries.

	During this gathering, symbolizing the unity and solid friendship of the socialist family, Zhang Xiruo raised a glass to toast the guests. He congratulated everyone on making greater contributions to the prosperity and development of socialist cultural undertakings and to the further development of cultural exchange and cooperation between socialist countries under the current excellent international situation.

	At the banquet, the hosts and guests repeatedly toasted the solidarity of the socialist camp headed by the Soviet Union and for world peace. The President of the Sino-Korean Friendship Association, Li Dequan, and the representative of the Korean Central News Agency delegation, Kim Young-ki, clinked glasses to congratulate the South Korean people on their victory in the struggle against the US-Lee fascist rule. At this moment, all the hosts and guests stood up and applauded warmly.

	Those present at the banquet included: Shen Yanbing, Li Dequan, Ji Pengfei, Qian Junrui, Xia Yan, Chu Tunan, Zhang Zhixiang, Ding Xilin, Lao She, Xiao San, Cui Yitian, Zhu Kezhen, Ge Baoquan, as well as people from cultural and artistic circles and other fields in the capital.

	A dance was held after the banquet.

### 对外文委招待兄弟国家文教代表

1960-04-30
第7版()
专栏：

    对外文委招待兄弟国家文教代表
    新华社29日讯　对外文化联络委员会主任张奚若今晚在前门饭店举行宴会，同兄弟国家文化艺术、教育、科学、卫生等方面代表团的代表们一起，共庆“五一”国际劳动节。
    在这次象征着社会主义大家庭的团结和牢固的友谊的欢聚中，张奚若举杯向客人祝酒。他祝贺大家在目前国际局势大好的形势下，为繁荣和发展社会主义文化事业，为进一步发展社会主义各国间的文化交流和合作作出更大的贡献。
    在宴会上，宾主们不断地为以苏联为首的社会主义阵营的团结，为世界和平而干杯。中朝友好协会会长李德全与朝鲜中央通讯社代表团代表金永起互相碰杯，祝贺南朝鲜人民在反对美李法西斯统治斗争中取得的胜利。这时，全体宾主起立热烈鼓掌。
    出席宴会的有：沈雁冰、李德全、姬鹏飞、钱俊瑞、夏衍、楚图南、张致祥、丁西林、老舍、萧三、崔义田、竺可桢、戈宝权以及首都文化艺术等各界人士。
    宴会后举行了舞会。
