Title: Three Vietnamese Cultural Delegations Arrive in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1960-04-29
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, April 28th: The Vietnamese Cultural Relics Delegation, the Vietnamese Arts and Crafts Delegation, and the Vietnamese Film Delegation, who are visiting our country according to the Sino-Vietnamese Cultural Cooperation Agreement, arrived in Beijing by train this morning, led by Nguyen Van Nghi, Deputy Director of the Cultural Relics Preservation Bureau of the Vietnamese Ministry of Culture, Nguyen Van Y, Principal of the Secondary Fine Arts School of the Vietnamese Ministry of Culture, and Nguyen Viet Thang, Director of the Film Machinery Factory of the Vietnamese Ministry of Culture.

　　On the same afternoon, all members of the three delegations attended the rally of people from all walks of life in the capital to support the patriotic and just struggle of the South Korean people.

　　In the evening, Xu Guangxiao, Vice Minister of Culture, held a welcome banquet for the three delegations.

### 越南三个文化代表团到京

1960-04-29
第6版()
专栏：

    越南三个文化代表团到京
    新华社28日讯　根据中越文化合作协定前来我国访问的越南文物代表团、越南工艺美术代表团、越南电影代表团，在越南文化部文物保藏司副司长阮文义、越南文化部中等美术学校校长阮文依、越南文化部电影机器厂厂长阮越详三位团长率领下，今天上午乘火车到达北京。
    当天下午，三个代表团的全体成员都参加了首都各界人民支援南朝鲜人民爱国正义斗争大会。
    晚间文化部副部长徐光霄为这三个代表团举行了欢迎宴会。
