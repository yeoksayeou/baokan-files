Title: NPC Budget Committee Approves Review Report on National Final Accounts and Budget Draft
Author:
Date: 1960-04-05
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, April 4th: The Budget Committee of the Second National People's Congress held a meeting on the morning of April 4th to discuss and approve the review report on the 1959 national final accounts and the 1960 national budget draft, which the Budget Committee will submit to the Second Session of the Second National People's Congress.

### 人大预算委员会通过国家决算和预算草案审查报告

1960-04-05
第1版()
专栏：

    人大预算委员会通过国家决算和预算草案审查报告
    新华社4日讯　第二届全国人民代表大会预算委员会在4日上午举行会议，讨论和通过了预算委员会将向第二届全国人民代表大会第二次会议提出的关于1959年国家决算和1960年国家预算草案的审查报告。
