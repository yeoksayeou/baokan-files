Title: Himalayan Prince Holds Tea Party to Host Premier Zhou Enlai; Ambassador Pan Zili Holds Reception for Premier Zhou's Visit to Nepal
Author:
Date: 1960-04-30
Page: 2
Xinhua News Agency, Kathmandu, April 28th. His Royal Highness Prince Himalaya Bir Bikram Shah Deva of Nepal held a tea party this afternoon to host Premier Zhou Enlai.

Also present at the tea party were His Royal Highness Prince Basundhara, Princesses, and other members of the Nepalese royal family. Vice Premier Chen Yi, all members of Premier Zhou Enlai's entourage, and Chinese Ambassador to Nepal Pan Zili attended the tea party. Nepalese Prime Minister Koirala, ministers, members of parliament, and prominent social figures also attended the tea party.

Premier Zhou Enlai and Vice Premier Chen Yi had friendly conversations with the Nepalese princes at this open-air tea party and were introduced to all the Nepalese friends.

Xinhua News Agency, Kathmandu, April 29th. Secretary-General to the King of Nepal, Sardar Hansa Man Singh, Private Secretary to the King of Nepal, Bhandari, and Joint Secretary to the King of Nepal, Dayaran B. Matma, paid a visit to Premier Zhou Enlai at the "Shitaniwas" State Guest House today.

Xinhua News Agency, Kathmandu, April 28th. Chinese Ambassador to Nepal, Pan Zili, held a grand reception this evening for Chinese Premier Zhou Enlai's visit. His Royal Highness Prince Himalaya Bir Bikram Shah Deva, His Royal Highness Prince Basundhara, other members of the royal family, and Prime Minister B.P. Koirala attended the reception. Among the four hundred guests attending the reception were cabinet ministers, members of parliament, party leaders, prominent social figures, and foreign envoys. Vice Premier Chen Yi and all members of the Chinese Premier's entourage attended the reception.

The reception was held in a very friendly atmosphere. The guests conversed with each other and toasted the growing friendship between the two countries.

### 喜马拉雅亲王举行茶会招待周总理  潘自力大使为周总理访尼举行招待会

1960-04-30
第2版()
专栏：

    喜马拉雅亲王举行茶会招待周总理  潘自力大使为周总理访尼举行招待会
    新华社加德满都28日电　尼泊尔喜马拉雅·比尔·比克拉姆·沙阿·德瓦亲王殿下今天下午举行茶会招待周恩来总理。
    出席茶会的还有巴森达兰亲王殿下、公主和尼泊尔皇室的其他成员。陈毅副总理和周恩来总理的全体随行人员以及中国驻尼泊尔大使潘自力都出席了茶会。出席茶会的还有尼泊尔首相柯伊拉腊和大臣们、议会议员们以及社会著名人士。
    周恩来总理和陈毅副总理在这个露天茶会上同尼泊尔的亲王们友好地交谈，并被介绍给所有的尼泊尔朋友。
    新华社加德满都29日电　尼泊尔国王秘书长萨达尔·汉萨·曼·辛格、尼泊尔国王私人秘书班达里和尼泊尔国王联合秘书达亚兰·布·马特马今天到这里的“希塔尼瓦斯”迎宾馆拜会了周恩来总理。
    新华社加德满都28日电　中国驻尼泊尔大使潘自力今天晚间为前来访问的中国总理周恩来举行盛大的招待会。喜马拉雅·比尔·比克拉姆·沙阿·德瓦亲王殿下、巴森达兰亲王殿下和王室的其他成员以及毕·普·柯伊拉腊首相出席了招待会。出席招待会的四百位客人中有内阁大臣、议会议员、政党领袖、社会著名人士和外国使节们。陈毅副总理和中国总理的全体随行人员出席了招待会。
    招待会是在十分友好的气氛中进行的。宾主们相互交谈，并为两国间日益增长的友谊干杯。
