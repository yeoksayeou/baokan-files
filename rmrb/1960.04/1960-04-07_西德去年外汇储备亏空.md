    West Germany's Foreign Exchange Reserves Deficit Last Year
    According to a report released by the West German Federal Bank on April 4th, West Germany suffered a deficit of 2.1 billion marks in foreign exchange reserves last year – the first foreign exchange reserve deficit since 1950.

### 西德去年外汇储备亏空

1960-04-07
第7版()
专栏：西风残叶

    西德去年外汇储备亏空
    据西德联邦银行于4月4日发表的一份报告说，西德去年在外汇储备方面亏空了二十一亿马克——这是从1950年以来第一次外汇储备亏空。
