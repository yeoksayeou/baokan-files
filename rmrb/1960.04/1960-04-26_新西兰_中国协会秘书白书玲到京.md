Title: New Zealand-China Society Secretary, Ruth Weiss, Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1960-04-26
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, April 24th – Ruth Weiss, Secretary of the New Zealand-China Society, arrived in Beijing by plane today for a visit to our country at the invitation of the Chinese People's Association for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries. Zhu Boshen and Jin Ziming, Deputy Secretaries-General of the Chinese People's Association for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries, were among those who welcomed her at the airport.

### 新西兰—中国协会秘书白书玲到京

1960-04-26
第4版()
专栏：

    新西兰—中国协会秘书白书玲到京
    新华社24日讯　应中国人民对外文化协会邀请前来我国访问的新西兰—中国协会秘书白书玲，今天乘飞机到达北京。到机场欢迎的有中国人民对外文化协会副秘书长朱伯深、金子明等。
