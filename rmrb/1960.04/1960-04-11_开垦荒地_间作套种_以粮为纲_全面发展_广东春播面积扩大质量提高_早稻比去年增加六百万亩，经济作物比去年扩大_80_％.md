Title: Reclaim Wasteland, Intercropping and Relay Planting, Take Grain as the Key Link, Comprehensive Development: Guangdong Expands Spring Planting Area and Improves Quality; Early Rice Increases by Six Million Mu Compared to Last Year, Economic Crops Expand by 80%
Author: Li Zuoji

Page: 5

    Reclaim Wasteland, Intercropping and Relay Planting, Take Grain as the Key Link, Comprehensive Development
    Guangdong Expands Spring Planting Area and Improves Quality
    Early Rice Increases by Six Million Mu Compared to Last Year, Economic Crops Expand by 80%
    (Our Dispatch) The policy of taking grain as the key link and promoting comprehensive development has been well implemented in Guangdong Province's spring plowing and production this year. Early rice, coarse grains, and economic crops have shown a great leap forward. The planting season is earlier and more timely than last year, the area is larger, and the quality is higher.
    By Qingming (April 5th), the province had transplanted over 28 million mu of early rice, completing 86% of the planned planting area, and basically completing rice transplanting more than 20 days earlier than last year. At the same time, over 23 million mu of spring-sown crops have been planted, completing about 85% of the planting plan, including over 10 million mu of coarse grains, over 2.2 million mu of sugarcane, over 3.1 million mu of peanuts, about 1 million mu of fruits, over 170,000 mu of jute, over 120,000 mu of tobacco, over 1.8 million mu of vegetables, and over 1.7 million mu of fodder. The planting area of all these crops has greatly increased compared to last year, and they have been sown earlier and more timely.
    In this year's spring plowing and production, Guangdong Province has proposed focusing on grain, with equal emphasis on grain crops and economic crops; within grain production, focusing on early rice, with equal emphasis on early rice and coarse grains; in production, combining multiple planting and harvesting with high-yield and multiple harvesting, as specific measures to implement the policy of taking grain as the key link and promoting comprehensive development. This year, the province has decided to sow over 60 million mu of early rice, coarse grains, and economic crops in spring, including over 32 million mu of early rice, an increase of over 6 million mu compared to last year, making it the year with the largest early rice planting area in history; over 28 million mu of coarse grains and economic crops, an expansion of about 80% compared to last year, is also unprecedented. Due to the large task and high quality requirements of this year's spring plowing and production, the labor force appears very tight. In order to solve this contradiction, various regions have vigorously engaged in tool reform on the one hand, and fully leveraged the superiority of the people's commune being "larger in size and having a higher degree of public ownership" on the other hand, combining the high concentration of labor for shock tasks with comprehensive and rational arrangements, and breaking with conventions to plan early, start early, seize time, and seize the initiative. As a result, the speed and efficiency of the entire production movement are unmatched by any previous year. By the end of February, although facing a severe drought of several months without rain, the province had taken the initiative in preparatory work, making it possible to quickly concentrate 4.5 million people to fight the drought. Under the slogan of "blocking the river wherever it is seen, and damming the river wherever it is seen," the province sealed and dammed more than 150,000 locations, ensuring the smooth progress of spring plowing and spring planting ahead of schedule.
    Guangdong's sugar production accounts for half of the country's total output, and it is also a famous hometown of fruits and a producing area of jute and peanuts. Vegetables can be grown year-round. In the past, Guangdong had less spring-sown coarse grains. This year, the large-scale development of pig raising also requires a large amount of coarse grains as feed. Therefore, this year's spring plowing and production requires that while early rice is sown early, planted more, and planted well, we must also focus on the planting of economic crops and coarse grain crops such as sugarcane, peanuts, fruits, jute, and vegetables. The main methods for expanding the planting area of various economic crops and coarse grains are to reclaim a large amount of wasteland and interplant and relay plant. Before the busy season of rice transplanting, the province mobilized more than 3 million people to reclaim wasteland and rush spring planting. In just over a month, more than 9.8 million mu of wasteland were reclaimed, exceeding the original plan by one time. Averaging the province's agricultural population, each person has increased by one-third of farmland. Many regions have also established production command organizations for sugarcane, peanuts, fruits, and vegetables, and organized professional teams to be responsible for planting and management. In this way, the land, labor force, and sowing time required for early rice, coarse grains, and economic crops have been rationally arranged, achieving simultaneous progress.
    The common characteristics of this year's planting of various major economic crops are: early, more, and better, while also emphasizing both planting and management, so they are growing well. Among the more than 2.2 million mu of sugarcane, the newly planted sugarcane has emerged from the soil by about one foot, and the stubble sugarcane is more than three feet tall. The people's communes in the sugarcane area are now implementing the "three guarantees" and enthusiastically carrying out sugarcane production competitions. The more than 3.1 million mu of peanuts have generally sprouted tender green buds, and those planted earlier have already stretched their branches and leaves. Fruit farmers have pruned and fertilized the province's more than 1 million mu of various fruit trees that will be harvested this year to promote flowering and fruit setting. They are now full of flowers. The first batch of fast-growing fruits such as watermelons, fruit sugarcane, papayas, and water chestnuts planted this year and harvested in the same year will be harvested around May. Jute and vegetables are also growing well.

### 开垦荒地　间作套种　以粮为纲　全面发展  广东春播面积扩大质量提高  早稻比去年增加六百万亩，经济作物比去年扩大80％
黎作骥
1960-04-11
第5版()
专栏：

    开垦荒地　间作套种　以粮为纲　全面发展
    广东春播面积扩大质量提高
    早稻比去年增加六百万亩，经济作物比去年扩大80％
    本报讯　以粮为纲全面发展的方针，在广东省今年春耕生产中贯彻执行得很好，早稻、杂粮和经济作物，出现了全面跃进的大好形势，播种季节都比去年适时偏早，面积都比去年多，质量也都比去年高。
    到“清明”（4月5日）这天，全省已栽插早稻二千八百多万亩，完成计划种植面积的86％，可比去年提早二十多天基本完成插秧。同时种下春种作物二千三百多万亩，完成种植计划85％左右，其中杂粮一千多万亩，甘蔗二百二十多万亩，花生三百一十多万亩，水果约一百万亩，黄麻十七万多亩，烟叶十二万多亩，蔬菜一百八十多万亩，饲料一百七十多万亩。所有这些作物，都比去年的种植面积大大增加，并且提早适时下种。
    广东省在今年春耕生产中，提出了以粮食为重点，粮食作物和经济作物并举；在粮食生产中又以早稻为重点，早稻和杂粮并举；在生产上，多种多收和高产多收并举，作为贯彻以粮为纲全面发展方针的具体措施。今年全省决定春季播种的早稻、杂粮和经济作物面积共六千多万亩，其中早稻三千二百多万亩，比去年增加六百多万亩，这是历来播种早稻面积最多的一年；杂粮和经济作物二千八百多万亩，比去年扩大约80％，也是前所未有的。由于今年春耕生产任务大、质量要求高，劳动力显得很紧张。各地为了解决这个矛盾，一方面大搞工具改革，一方面充分发挥公社一大二公的优越性，高度集中劳力突击和全面合理安排相结合，并打破常规，早筹谋，早动手，争时间，争主动。结果，整个生产运动行动之快，工效之高，过去任何一年都赶不上的。去年秋收一结束，就有五百多万人投入水利建设高潮；接着是三百七十多万人大闹深翻改土；深翻告一段落，四百七十多万人的积肥高潮又起，使水利、深翻改土、积肥的任务比往年提早一两个月超额完成，水、土、肥三大高潮后，全省又掀起了三百多万人的开荒和春种运动。到2月下旬，虽然遇上几个月不雨的苦旱，但全省备耕工作已取得主动，有可能迅速集中四百五十多万人抗旱，在“见江就堵，见河就拦”的口号下，全省封江堵河十五万多处，保证了春耕春种提早季节顺利进行。
    广东糖的产量占全国产量的一半，又是有名的水果之乡和黄麻、花生的产区，蔬菜更是常年可种；过去广东春种杂粮较少，今年养猪大发展也要求有大量杂粮作饲料。因此，今年春耕生产要求在早稻种早、种多、种好的同时，还要突出抓好甘蔗、花生、水果、黄麻、蔬菜等经济作物和杂粮作物的播种工作。扩大各种经济作物和杂粮播种面积的主要办法，是大量开垦荒地和间种套种。全省在插秧大忙以前，曾出动三百多万人开垦荒地、突击春种，短短的一个多月内开垦了九百八十多万亩荒地，超过原定计划的一倍。按全省农业人口平均，每人增加了三分耕地。许多地区还成立了甘蔗、花生、水果、蔬菜的生产指挥机构，组织专业队负责播种和管理工作。这样，早稻、杂粮、经济作物所需的土地、劳动力和播种时间都得到合理的安排，做到了齐头并进。
    今年各种主要经济作物播种的共同特点是：早、多、好，同时做到种、管并重，因而长得也好。二百二十多万亩甘蔗中，新植蔗已出土一尺左右，宿根蔗也三尺多高了。蔗区各人民公社现正实行“三包”，热烈开展甘蔗超产竞赛。三百一十多万亩花生普遍冒出嫩绿的新芽，种得早的已经伸枝发叶。果农们对全省今年有收获的一百多万亩各类果树进行了修枝施肥、催花攻果，现在都繁花满枝；新种下当年收获的西瓜、果蔗、木瓜、荸荠等速生水果，第一批西瓜大约5月间便可收获。黄麻、蔬菜也生长良好。
