Title: Symbolizing the Unbreakable Friendship Between the Chinese and Hungarian Peoples, Huangtugang Commune Renamed Sino-Hungarian Friendship Commune, Hungarian Guests Congratulate Members on Achieving Greater Success
Author:
Date: 1960-04-04
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, 3rd April. The Huangtugang People's Commune in Fengtai District, Beijing, held a grand meeting today to celebrate the 15th anniversary of Hungary's liberation and the naming of this commune as the Sino-Hungarian Friendship People's Commune.

　　The Sino-Hungarian Friendship People's Commune was filled with joyous atmosphere today. Two thousand commune members gathered on the edge of the village early in the morning, beating gongs and drums, playing Chinese and Hungarian folk music, and setting off firecrackers to welcome the Chargé d'Affaires ad interim of the Hungarian Embassy in China, Mr. Szirti, and his wife, the head of the Sino-Hungarian Friendship Association delegation, Pátaki János, and all members of the delegation, as well as all members of the Hungarian Communist Youth League Central Art Troupe, who came to participate in the celebration and naming ceremony.

　　Beijing Vice Mayor Feng Jiping spoke at the meeting. He said: The Chinese people are overjoyed and encouraged by the great achievements made by the fraternal Hungarian people in economic and cultural fields under the leadership of the Hungarian Socialist Workers' Party and the Workers' and Peasants' Revolutionary Government. Yin Weichen, director of the Sino-Hungarian Friendship People's Commune, extended holiday greetings to the distinguished guests on behalf of all commune members, and through the distinguished guests, to all the Hungarian people. He said that all of our commune members are extremely happy that the commune has been named the Sino-Hungarian Friendship People's Commune, and we will definitely cherish this friendship with practical actions of continuously improving production.

　　The Chargé d'Affaires ad interim of the Hungarian Embassy in China, Mr. Szirti, extended warm congratulations to all members of the Sino-Hungarian Friendship People's Commune in his speech. He said that the naming of the Sino-Hungarian Friendship People's Commune is a symbol of the unbreakable friendship between the people of China and Hungary. After describing the great achievements Hungary has made in the fifteen years since its liberation, he said that the people of our two countries will unite even more closely in the socialist camp headed by the Soviet Union and advance side by side, fighting together.

　　Pátaki János, head of the Sino-Hungarian Friendship Association delegation, also spoke at the meeting. He praised the great achievements made by this commune in production and wished the members greater success this year.

　　A holiday congratulatory letter from the Sino-Hungarian Friendship Commune to the Hungarian peasant brothers was read out at the meeting. The Hungarian Communist Youth League Central Art Troupe, the Chongqing Acrobatic Troupe, and the commune members of this commune performed literary and artistic programs after the meeting. The distinguished guests had lunch at the commune. The commune gave peanuts and other local products to the distinguished guests.

　　Also present today were Vice Minister of Agriculture Gu Dachuan, Deputy Director of the Soviet and Eastern European Affairs Department of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs Chen Boqing, and Secretary-General of the Sino-Hungarian Friendship Association Zhang Lianhua.

### 象征着中匈两国人民友谊牢不可破  黄土岗公社命名为中匈友好公社  匈牙利贵宾祝贺社员们取得更大成绩

1960-04-04
第5版()
专栏：

    象征着中匈两国人民友谊牢不可破
    黄土岗公社命名为中匈友好公社
    匈牙利贵宾祝贺社员们取得更大成绩
    新华社3日讯　北京市丰台区黄土岗人民公社今天隆重集会，庆祝匈牙利解放十五周年和这个公社被命名为中匈友好人民公社。
    中匈友好人民公社里今天充满了欢乐的气氛。两千名社员清早就聚集在村边，敲锣打鼓，吹奏着中匈民间音乐，放着鞭炮，迎接前来参加庆祝大会和命名大会的匈牙利驻华大使馆临时代办西格蒂和他的夫人，匈中友协代表团团长帕塔基·亚诺什和代表团的全体团员以及匈牙利共青团中央艺术团的全体团员。
    北京市副市长冯基平在大会上讲话。他说：中国人民对兄弟的匈牙利人民在匈牙利社会主义工人党和工农革命政府的领导下，在经济文化方面取得的伟大成就感到欢欣鼓舞。中匈友好人民公社主任殷维臣在大会上代表全体社员向贵宾们并通过贵宾向全体匈牙利人民致节日的祝贺。他说，我们全体社员对公社被命名为中匈友好人民公社感到万分高兴，我们一定以不断提高生产的实际行动来珍惜这种友情。
    匈牙利驻华大使馆临时代办西格蒂在讲话中向中匈友好人民公社的全体社员表示热烈祝贺。他说，中匈友好人民公社的命名是中匈两国人民之间牢不可破的友谊的象征。他在叙述了匈牙利解放十五年来所取得的伟大成就后说，我们两国人民今后将更加紧密地团结在以苏联为首的社会主义阵营内并肩前进，共同奋斗。
    匈中友协代表团团长帕塔基·亚诺什也在会上讲了话，他赞扬这个公社在生产上取得的伟大成就，并祝社员们在今年取得更大的成绩。
    大会上宣读了中匈友好公社给匈牙利农民兄弟的节日贺信。匈牙利共青团中央艺术团、重庆杂技团和这个公社的社员们在会后分别演出了文艺节目。贵宾们在公社进午餐。公社把花生等土特产赠给贵宾。
    今天到会的还有农业部副部长顾大川、外交部苏联东欧司副司长陈伯清、中匈友好协会秘书长张联华。
