Title: Premier Zhou Holds Banquet on Eve of Departure from India, Bids Farewell to President Prasad, Indian Home Minister Pant Holds Tea Party to Host Premier Zhou
Author:
Date: 1960-04-26
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, New Delhi, April 25th: Premier Zhou Enlai held a banquet at the Chinese Embassy in India tonight on the eve of his departure from India.

    The Indian dignitaries attending the banquet included: Vice President Sarvepalli Radhakrishnan, Prime Minister Jawaharlal Nehru, Mrs. Indira Gandhi, Speaker of the People's House M. A. Ayyangar, Finance Minister Morarji Desai, Minister of Steel, Mines and Fuel Swaran Singh, Defense Minister Krishna Menon, Minister of Public Works, Housing and Supply K. C. Reddy, Minister of Transport P. Subbarayan, Minister of Agriculture Panjabrao Deshmukh, Minister of Mines and Oil K. D. Malaviya,  Minister of Community development and Co-operation S. K. Dey and Education Minister K. L. Shrimali. Also attending the banquet were: Army Chief of Staff General K. S. Thimayya, Indian Ambassador to China G. Parthasarathi, President of the India-China Friendship Association Pandit Sundarlal, Chairman of the National Executive Committee of the India-China Friendship Association Mrs. Uma Nehru, Indian peace activist Dr. Saifuddin Kitchlew, senior Indian government officials and Indian parliamentarians.
    The Chinese side attending the banquet included: Vice Premier Chen Yi, Vice Minister of Foreign Affairs Zhang Hanfu and the main members of Premier Zhou's entourage. Chinese Ambassador to India Pan Zili also attended the banquet.
    The banquet was held in a friendly atmosphere.
    Xinhua News Agency, New Delhi, April 25th: Premier of the State Council of the People's Republic of China, Zhou Enlai, paid a farewell visit to the President of the Republic of India, Rajendra Prasad, at 5:30 p.m. local time.
    Marshal Chen Yi, Vice Premier of the State Council and Minister of Foreign Affairs, was present at the farewell visit.
    Xinhua News Agency, New Delhi, April 25th: Premier of the State Council of the People's Republic of China, Zhou Enlai, and Prime Minister of the Republic of India, Jawaharlal Nehru, continued their talks this morning. The talks began at 11:00 a.m. local time.
    Xinhua News Agency, New Delhi, April 25th:  The Home Minister of the Republic of India, Govind Ballabh Pant, held a tea party at 6:30 p.m. local time today to host Chinese Premier Zhou Enlai and Vice Premier and Foreign Minister Chen Yi.
    Among the more than 400 guests attending the tea party were Indian Prime Minister Jawaharlal Nehru, cabinet ministers, ministers of state, other senior officials of the Indian government, parliamentarians, senior military officers, prominent social figures, and diplomatic envoys from various countries to India.

### 周总理离印前夕举行宴会  并拜会普拉沙德总统向他辞行  印度内政部长潘特举行茶会招待周总理

1960-04-26
第1版()
专栏：

    周总理离印前夕举行宴会
    并拜会普拉沙德总统向他辞行
    印度内政部长潘特举行茶会招待周总理
    新华社新德里25日电　周恩来总理今天晚上在离开印度前夕在中国驻印度大使馆举行宴会。
    出席宴会的印度贵宾有：副总统萨·拉达克里希南，总理贾瓦哈拉尔·尼赫鲁，英迪拉·甘地夫人，人民院议长姆·阿·阿延加尔，财政部长莫拉尔吉·德赛，钢铁、矿业和燃料部长斯瓦兰·辛格，国防部长克里希纳·梅农，公共工程、住房和供应部长基·钱·雷迪，交通运输部长普·苏巴拉延，农业部长潘·德希穆克，矿业和石油部长克·德·马拉维亚，乡村发展和合作部长苏·库·戴伊和教育部长卡·拉·什里马利。出席宴会的还有：陆军参谋长克·斯·蒂迈雅将军，印度驻中国大使高·帕塔萨拉蒂，印中友好协会主席潘迪特·森德拉尔，印中友好协会全国执行委员会主席乌玛·尼赫鲁夫人，印度和平人士赛福丁·克其鲁博士，印度政府的高级官员们和印度议员们。
    中国方面出席宴会的有：副总理陈毅，外交部副部长章汉夫和中国总理的主要随行人员。中国驻印度大使潘自力也出席了宴会。
    宴会是在友好的气氛中举行的。
    新华社新德里25日电　中华人民共和国国务院总理周恩来在当地时间十七点三十分拜会印度共和国总统拉金德拉·普拉沙德，向他辞行。
    辞行时在场的有：国务院副总理兼外交部长陈毅元帅。
    新华社新德里25日电　中华人民共和国国务院总理周恩来和印度共和国总理贾瓦哈拉尔·尼赫鲁今天上午继续举行会谈。会谈是在当地时间十一点开始举行的。
    新华社新德里25日电　印度共和国内政部长戈·巴·潘特在今天当地时间十八点三十分举行茶会，招待中国总理周恩来和副总理兼外交部长陈毅元帅。
    在出席茶会的四百多位客人中，有印度总理贾瓦哈拉尔·尼赫鲁，内阁部长们，国务部长们，印度政府的其他高级官员，议员们，高级军官们，社会著名人士和各国驻印度的外交使节。
