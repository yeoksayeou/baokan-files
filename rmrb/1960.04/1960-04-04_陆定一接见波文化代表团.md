Title: Lu Dingyi Receives Polish Cultural Delegation
Author:
Date: 1960-04-04
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, 3rd April: Vice Premier of the State Council Lu Dingyi received this afternoon the head of the Polish cultural delegation currently visiting China, Tadeusz Galinski, the Polish Minister of Culture and Art, as well as delegation members Stanislaw Neumark, Director of the General Office of the Polish Ministry of Culture and Art, and journalist Mostowicz.

Present at the reception were Minister of Culture Shen Yanbing, Vice Ministers Xia Yan and Xu Guangxiao, and Deputy Director of the Commission for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries Zhang Zhixiang, among others. The delegation will leave Beijing tomorrow to visit Nanjing, Shanghai, Hangzhou, Wuhan, Guangzhou and other places.

### 陆定一接见波文化代表团

1960-04-04
第6版()
专栏：

    陆定一接见波文化代表团
    新华社3日讯　国务院副总理陆定一今天下午接见了正在我国访问的波兰文化代表团团长、波兰文化艺术部部长塔德乌什·加林斯基，团员、波兰文化艺术部办公厅主任斯塔尼斯瓦夫·奈乌马尔克和记者莫斯托维奇。
    接见时在座的，有文化部部长沈雁冰，副部长夏衍、徐光霄，对外文化联络委员会副主任张致祥等。代表团将于明天离开北京，去南京、上海、杭州、武汉、广州等地参观访问。
