Title: Shanxi Generally Adjusts Employee Dormitories
Author:
Date: 1960-04-04
Page: 2

Brief News

    Shanxi Generally Adjusts Employee Dormitories
    Industrial enterprises in Shanxi Province are actively promoting Yangquan City's experience of adjusting employee dormitories according to production units. In Taiyuan, Datong, Changzhi and other cities with a large proportion of industry, the municipal party committee secretaries are personally in charge, leading the work of adjusting dormitories. As of April 1, 400,000 employees in Taiyuan City have been living according to their production units.

### 山西普遍调整职工宿舍

1960-04-04
第2版()
专栏：简要新闻

    山西普遍调整职工宿舍
    山西省各工矿企业正在积极推广阳泉市按生产单位调整职工宿舍的经验。工业比重大的太原、大同、长治等市，都是市委书记亲自挂帅，领导调整宿舍的工作。到4月1日止，太原市四十万职工已经都按生产单位居住。
