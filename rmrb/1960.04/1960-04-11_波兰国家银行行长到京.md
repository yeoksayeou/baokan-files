Title: President of the National Bank of Poland Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1960-04-11
Page: 7

Xinhua News Agency, 10th — At the invitation of Cao Juru, President of the Head Office of the People's Bank of China, Drozniak, President of the National Bank of Poland, and Ciesielski, Director of the Foreign Bureau of the bank, arrived in Beijing by plane today for a visit to China.

Cao Juru, President of the Head Office of the People's Bank of China, and Vice Presidents Chen Xiyu, Huang Yaguang, and Qiao Peixin welcomed the guests at the airport. Szymanski, Commercial Counselor of the Polish Embassy in China, also welcomed them at the airport.

This afternoon, Drozniak and others were invited to attend the closing ceremony of the Second Session of the Second National People's Congress.

### 波兰国家银行行长到京

1960-04-11
第7版()
专栏：

    波兰国家银行行长到京
    新华社10日讯　波兰国家银行行长德罗日尼亚克和这个银行的国外局局长契斯尔应中国人民银行总行行长曹菊如的邀请来我国访问，在今天乘飞机到达北京。
    中国人民银行总行行长曹菊如，副行长陈希愈、黄亚光、乔培新到机场欢迎客人。波兰驻华大使馆商务参赞席曼斯基也到机场欢迎。
    今天下午，德罗日尼亚克等应邀参加了我全国人民代表大会第二届第二次会议的闭幕式。
