Title: Indonesian "Republic Daily" Welcomes the Achievements of China-Burma and China-Nepal Talks
Author:
Date: 1960-04-06
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, Jakarta - The Indonesian newspaper "Republic Daily" published an editorial on March 31, welcoming the talks between the government leaders of Burma and China, and between the government leaders of Nepal and China. The newspaper believes that the problems historically left over between Asian and African countries can undoubtedly be resolved through wise approaches that are conducive to consolidating the unity of Asian and African countries. It also pointed out that the border issue between India and China can also be resolved through negotiations.

The newspaper pointed out that the achievements of the talks between Burma and China, and Nepal and China, as well as the current world political situation, are also conducive to Indonesia resolving the issue of overseas Chinese with China on the premise of the great anti-imperialist unity of Asian and African countries.

### 印度尼西亚“共和国报”  欢迎中缅中尼会谈成就

1960-04-06
第5版()
专栏：

    印度尼西亚“共和国报”
    欢迎中缅中尼会谈成就
    据新华社雅加达电　印度尼西亚“共和国报”在3月31日发表社论，欢迎缅甸和中国的政府领导人以及尼泊尔和中国的政府领导人之间的会谈。这家报纸认为，亚非国家之间历史上遗留下来的问题，无疑是可以通过有利于巩固亚非国家团结的明智的途径来解决的。它还指出，印度和中国之间的边界问题也可以通过谈判来解决。
    这家报纸指出，缅甸和中国以及尼泊尔和中国之间会谈的成就，以及当前的世界政治局势，也有利于印度尼西亚同中国之间在亚非国家反帝大团结的前提条件下，解决华侨问题。
