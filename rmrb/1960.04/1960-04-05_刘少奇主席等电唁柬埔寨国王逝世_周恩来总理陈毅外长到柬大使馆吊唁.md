Title: Chairman Liu Shaoqi and Others Send Condolences on the Death of the Cambodian King; Premier Zhou Enlai and Foreign Minister Chen Yi Visit the Cambodian Embassy to Express Condolences
Author:
Date: 1960-04-05
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, April 4th. Chairman of the People's Republic of China, Liu Shaoqi, sent a telegram on the 3rd to Prince Norodom Sihanouk, Prime Minister of the Royal Government of Cambodia, expressing condolences on the death of King Suramarit of Cambodia. The telegram reads as follows:
His Royal Highness Prince Norodom Sihanouk, Prime Minister of the Royal Government of Cambodia, Phnom Penh:
Upon hearing the sad news of the unfortunate passing of His Majesty King Norodom Suramarit, I, with a heavy heart, extend my deepest condolences to the Cambodian people and to Your Highness on behalf of the Chinese people and in my personal capacity.
Liu Shaoqi
Chairman of the People's Republic of China
April 3, 1960

Xinhua News Agency, April 4th. Zhou Enlai, Premier of the State Council of the People's Republic of China, sent a telegram on the 3rd to Prince Norodom Sihanouk, Prime Minister of the Royal Government of Cambodia, expressing condolences on the death of King Suramarit of Cambodia. The telegram reads as follows:
His Royal Highness Prince Norodom Sihanouk, Prime Minister of the Royal Government of Cambodia, Phnom Penh:
Upon hearing the sad news of the unfortunate passing of His Majesty King Norodom Suramarit, I, on behalf of the Chinese government and in my personal capacity, extend my deepest condolences to Your Highness and the Royal Government of Cambodia, and through Your Highness, convey my sincerest condolences to Her Majesty the Queen.
The valuable contributions His Majesty the King made during his lifetime to promote the traditional friendship between the peoples of China and Cambodia will always remain in the memories of the Cambodian and Chinese peoples.
Zhou Enlai
Premier of the State Council of the People's Republic of China
April 3, 1960

Xinhua News Agency, April 4th. Chen Yi, Vice Premier of the State Council and Minister of Foreign Affairs of the People's Republic of China, sent a telegram on the 3rd to Song Sann, Deputy Prime Minister and Minister of Foreign Affairs of the Royal Government of Cambodia, expressing condolences on the death of King Suramarit of Cambodia. The telegram reads as follows:
His Excellency Song Sann, Deputy Prime Minister and Minister of Foreign Affairs of the Royal Government of Cambodia:
Upon hearing the sad news of the unfortunate passing of His Majesty King Norodom Suramarit, I extend my deepest condolences.
Chen Yi
Vice Premier of the State Council and Minister of Foreign Affairs of the People's Republic of China
April 3, 1960

Xinhua News Agency, April 4th. After learning the news of the unfortunate passing of King Norodom Suramarit of Cambodia on April 3rd, Premier Zhou Enlai and Vice Premier and Foreign Minister Chen Yi personally visited the Cambodian Embassy in China this morning to express their condolences.
In the hall of the embassy, a portrait of King Norodom Suramarit was hung, and wreaths presented by Chairman Mao Zedong, Chairman Liu Shaoqi, Vice Chairpersons Soong Ching Ling and Dong Biwu, Chairman Zhu De, Premier Zhou Enlai, and Vice Premier and Foreign Minister Chen Yi were placed in front of the portrait.
Also present at the Cambodian Embassy in China today to express condolences were Zhang Xiruo, Director of the Commission for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries; Ji Pengfei, Vice Minister of Foreign Affairs; Lieutenant General Liao Hansheng, Vice Minister of National Defense; Song Yangchu, Deputy Director of the State Planning Commission; Lei Renmin, Vice Minister of Foreign Trade; Wang Kunlun, Vice Mayor of Beijing; Zhao Puchu and Zhou Shujia, Vice Presidents of the Buddhist Association of China; and more than 300 people from all walks of life.
Today, envoys from various countries to China also visited the Cambodian Embassy in China to express their condolences.
Cambodian Ambassador to China, Leng Ngeth, received the mourners and thanked them.

### 刘少奇主席等电唁柬埔寨国王逝世  周恩来总理陈毅外长到柬大使馆吊唁

1960-04-05
第1版()
专栏：

    刘少奇主席等电唁柬埔寨国王逝世
    周恩来总理陈毅外长到柬大使馆吊唁
    新华社4日讯　中华人民共和国主席刘少奇3日打电报给柬埔寨王国政府首相西哈努克亲王，对柬埔寨国王苏拉玛里特逝世表示哀悼。电文如下：金边柬埔寨王国政府首相诺罗敦·西哈努克亲王殿下：
    惊悉诺罗敦·苏拉玛里特国王陛下不幸逝世，我以沉痛的心情代表中国人民并以我个人的名义向柬埔寨人民和殿下表示深切的哀悼。
    中华人民共和国主席　刘少奇
            一九六○年四月三日
    新华社4日讯　中华人民共和国国务院总理周恩来3日打电报给柬埔寨王国政府首相西哈努克亲王，对于柬埔寨国王苏拉玛里特逝世表示哀悼。电文如下：金边柬埔寨王国政府首相诺罗敦·西哈
  努克亲王殿下：
    惊闻诺罗敦·苏拉玛里特国王陛下不幸逝世，我谨代表中国政府并以我个人的名义向殿下和柬埔寨王国政府表示沉痛的哀悼，并通过殿下向王后陛下转致最诚挚的慰问。
    国王陛下生前对促进中柬两国人民的传统友谊所作的宝贵贡献，将一直留在柬埔寨人民和中国人民的记忆里。
    中华人民共和国国务院总理
                        周恩来
            一九六○年四月三日
    新华社4日讯　中华人民共和国国务院副总理兼外交部长陈毅3日打电报给柬埔寨王国政府副首相兼外交大臣松山，对柬埔寨国王苏拉玛里特逝世表示哀悼。电文如下：柬埔寨王国政府副首相兼外交大臣
  松山阁下：
    惊悉诺罗敦·苏拉玛里特国王陛下不幸逝世，谨致沉痛的哀悼。
    中华人民共和国国务院副总理兼外交部长　陈毅
            一九六○年四月三日
    新华社4日讯　周恩来总理和陈毅副总理兼外交部长在获知柬埔寨国王诺罗敦·苏拉玛里特不幸于4月3日逝世的消息后，今天上午亲自到柬埔寨驻中国大使馆吊唁。
    在大使馆的大厅里，悬挂着诺罗敦·苏拉玛里特国王的遗像，遗像前面放着毛泽东主席、刘少奇主席、宋庆龄和董必武副主席、朱德委员长、周恩来总理、陈毅副总理兼外交部长献的花圈。
    今天前往柬埔寨驻中国大使馆吊唁的，还有对外文化联络委员会主任张奚若，外交部副部长姬鹏飞，国防部副部长廖汉生中将，国家计划委员会副主任宋养初，对外贸易部副部长雷任民，北京市副市长王昆仑和中国佛教协会副会长赵朴初、周叔迦以及各界人士三百多人。
    今天，各国驻中国的使节也前往柬埔寨驻中国大使馆吊唁。
    柬埔寨驻华大使兰·涅特接待前往吊唁的人，并向他们致谢。
