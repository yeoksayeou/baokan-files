Title: Chairman Mao Receives Militia Representatives
Author:
Date: 1960-04-24
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, April 23rd. Chairman Mao Zedong, Vice-Chairmen Zhu De and Lin Biao, General Secretary Deng Xiaoping of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of China, and Vice-Chairmen Song Qingling and Dong Biwu of the People's Republic of China, and other party and state leaders, received all the representatives attending the National Militia Representatives Conference this afternoon.

Also present at the reception were Lin Boqu, Peng Zhen, Luo Ronghuan, and He Long, members of the Political Bureau of the CPC Central Committee; Chen Boda and Kang Sheng, alternate members; Tan Zheng, Secretary of the Secretariat; Hu Qiaomu, alternate secretary; Luo Ruiqing and Xi Zhongxun, Vice Premiers of the State Council; and Cheng Qian, Zhang Zhizhong, and Fu Zuoyi, Vice Chairmen of the National Defense Council. (Attached picture)

### 毛主席接见民兵代表

1960-04-24
第1版()
专栏：

    毛主席接见民兵代表
    新华社23日讯　中国共产党中央委员会主席毛泽东，副主席朱德、林彪，总书记邓小平，中华人民共和国副主席宋庆龄、董必武等党和国家领导人，今天下午接见了出席全国民兵代表会议的全体代表。
    接见时在座的还有，中共中央政治局委员林伯渠、彭真、罗荣桓、贺龙，候补委员陈伯达、康生，书记处书记谭政，候补书记胡乔木，国务院副总理罗瑞卿、习仲勋，国防委员会副主席程潜、张治中、傅作义等。（附图片）
