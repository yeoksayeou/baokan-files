Title: Polish Military Delegation Visits Urban People's Commune in Zhengzhou
Author:
Date: 1960-04-07
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, Zhengzhou, 6th April. The Polish military delegation, headed by General Bozzi Wowski, visited the urban people's commune in Zhengzhou.

The Polish military delegation arrived in Zhengzhou from Beijing by special plane yesterday morning. In the afternoon, accompanied by General Peng Shaohui, Deputy Chief of the General Staff of the Chinese People's Liberation Army, and Major General Huang Xinyou, Deputy Commander of the Henan Military Region, they visited the Hongqi People's Commune in the Guancheng District of Zhengzhou City. They toured the commune-run industries—the electric motor factory and the chemical plant—as well as the commune's newly built residential areas, public canteens, homes for the elderly, and kindergartens. General Bozzi Wowski held cordial talks with the commune cadres and learned with great interest about the establishment and development of the commune, as well as its organizational structure and management. After the visit, he said, "You are doing a great undertaking."

Subsequently, the Polish military delegation also visited the Zhengzhou Textile Factory and toured the commune-run industries of the factory's people's commune—the wood factory, the machine factory, and the clothing factory.

During the visit to the urban people's commune, General Bozzi Wowski and all members of the delegation were warmly welcomed and enthusiastically received by the commune members.

When the Polish military delegation arrived in Zhengzhou in the morning, Lieutenant General Bi Zhanyun, Commander of the Henan Military Region, Li Li, Secretary of the Henan Provincial Committee of the Communist Party of China, Wang Weiqun, Vice Governor, Liu Bi, Secretary of the Zhengzhou Municipal Committee of the Communist Party of China, and Li Ping, Vice Mayor, and other party, government, and military leaders, went to the airport to welcome them.

### 波兰军事代表团  在郑州参观城市人民公社

1960-04-07
第6版()
专栏：

    波兰军事代表团
    在郑州参观城市人民公社
    新华社郑州6日电　以博齐沃夫斯基上将为首的波兰军事代表团，在郑州参观访问了城市人民公社。
    波兰军事代表团昨天上午乘专机由北京到达郑州后，下午由中国人民解放军副总参谋长彭绍辉上将和河南军区副司令员黄新友少将陪同，在郑州市管城区红旗人民公社，参观了这个社社办工业——电机厂和化工厂，以及公社新建的居民点和公共食堂、敬老院、幼儿园等。博齐沃夫斯基上将与公社干部进行了亲切的交谈，带着极大的兴趣了解了公社建立过程和发展情况，以及公社的组织机构和经营管理。他参观后说，你们正在做的是伟大的事业。
    随后，波兰军事代表团代表又到郑州纺织厂，参观访问了这个厂人民公社的社办工业——木工厂、机械厂和被服厂。
    参观城市人民公社当中，博齐沃夫斯基上将和代表团全体团员，受到了公社社员的热烈欢迎和盛情招待。
    波兰军事代表团上午到达郑州时，河南军区司令员毕占云中将、中共河南省委书记李立、副省长王维群、中共郑州市委书记刘必、副市长李平等党政军负责人，曾到机场欢迎他们。
