Title: China Federation of Literary and Art Circles Hosts Reception Welcoming Japanese Delegation for the Study of Chinese Language Reform

Author:

Date: 1960-04-11
Page: 7

Xinhua News Agency, April 10th: The China Federation of Literary and Art Circles held a reception this evening to welcome the Japanese delegation for the study of Chinese language reform, led by Toki Zemmaro.

	Playwright Ouyang Yuqian delivered a speech at the reception, welcoming the Japanese delegation for the study of Chinese language reform to visit our country. The head of the delegation, Toki Zemmaro, also spoke at the reception. He expressed his warm gratitude for the sincere support and sympathy that the Chinese literary and art circles had given to the Japanese people and the Japanese literary and art circles in their struggle against the Japan-U.S. "Security Treaty". He sternly condemned Nobusuke Kishi's policy of hostility towards China. He said that deepening friendly relations with China, realizing the restoration of diplomatic relations between Japan and China as soon as possible, and safeguarding peace in Asia and the world are the primary and noble tasks of Japanese cultural workers.

	More than 50 people from Chinese cultural circles attended the reception this evening, including Hu Yuzhi, Wei Que, Tian Han, Mei Lanfang, Zhao Puchu, Lao She, Xu Guangping, Lü Ji, Xie Bingxin, Ye Shengtao, Xiao San, Zhou Erfu, and others.

### 我文学艺术界联合会举行酒会  欢迎日本考察中国文字改革代表团

1960-04-11
第7版()
专栏：

    我文学艺术界联合会举行酒会
    欢迎日本考察中国文字改革代表团
    新华社10日讯　中国文学艺术界联合会今晚举行酒会，欢迎由土岐善麿率领的日本考察中国文字改革学术代表团。
    戏剧家欧阳予倩在酒会上致词欢迎日本考察中国文字改革学术代表团来我国访问。代表团团长土岐善麿在酒会上讲了话。他对中国文艺界对于日本人民和日本文艺界在反对日美“安全条约”的斗争中所给予的真诚支持和同情，表示了热情的感谢。他严厉地谴责了岸信介敌视中国的政策。他说，加深同中国的友好关系、早日实现恢复日中两国邦交、保卫亚洲和世界和平，是日本文化工作者的首要的崇高事业。
    参加今晚酒会的有中国文化界人士胡愈之、韦悫、田汉、梅兰芳、赵朴初、老舍、许广平、吕骥、谢冰心、叶圣陶、萧三、周而复等五十多人。
