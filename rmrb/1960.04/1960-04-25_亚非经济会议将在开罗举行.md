Title: Asian-African Economic Conference to be Held in Cairo
Author: 
Date: 1960-04-25
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, Cairo, April 23rd: The chairman of the Asian-African Economic Conference, Muhammad Rushdi, announced the agenda for the Second Asian-African Economic Conference, to be held in Cairo from April 30th to May 3rd, at a press conference held here today.

According to the announcement, the agenda includes: the Secretary-General's report; approval of the charter and implementing regulations; election of the executive body; and the establishment of an Asian-African organization related to private investment and the provision of emergency assistance to Asian-African countries.

Rushdi said that thirty-six of the forty-two invited Asian-African countries have agreed to attend this conference.

He then said that the German Democratic Republic and some other European countries would attend the conference as observers.

The first Asian-African Economic Conference was held in Cairo in December 1958.

### 亚非经济会议将在开罗举行

1960-04-25
第5版()
专栏：

    亚非经济会议将在开罗举行
    新华社开罗23日电　亚非经济会议主席穆罕默德·鲁什迪今天在这里举行的记者招待会上宣布了将从4月30日到5月3日在开罗举行的第二届亚非经济会议的议程。
    据宣布，议程包括：秘书长的报告；批准章程和执行条例；选举执行机构；建立一个有关私人投资和给予亚非国家紧急援助的亚非组织。
    鲁什迪说，在四十二个被邀请的亚非国家中，已经有三十六个国家同意出席这一届会议。
    他接着说，德意志民主共和国和其他一些欧洲国家将以观察员身份出席会议。
    第一届亚非经济会议是1958年12月在开罗举行的。
