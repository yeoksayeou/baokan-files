Title: Automated Coal Briquette Workshop
Author:
Date: 1960-04-04
Page: 2
Brief News

    Automated Coal Briquette Workshop
    The No. 1 Mechanized Coal Briquette Factory in Hedong District, Tianjin, has pioneered an automated workshop for making coal briquettes. Workers manage various buttons and instruments in the control room, realizing the long-cherished dream of "doing dirty work in white clothes." After implementing automated operation, production efficiency has increased twelvefold; the number of workers has been reduced from thirty-nine in three shifts per day to three.

### 自动化煤球车间

1960-04-04
第2版()
专栏：简要新闻

    自动化煤球车间
    天津市河东区第一机制煤球厂首创打制煤球的自动化车间。工人在操作室里管理各种电钮和仪表，实现了多年梦寐以求的“干黑活穿白衣”的理想。实现自动化操作后，生产效率提高十二倍；工人由原来每天三班三十九人减少到三人。
