Title: Chairman Mao Meets General Secretary Roca, Comrades Zhu De and Dong Biwu Receive Polish Military Delegation
Author:
Date: 1960-04-30
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, Tianjin, April 29th: Chairman Mao Zedong of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of China met this afternoon in Tianjin with Blas Roca, General Secretary of the People's Socialist Party of Cuba, and his wife, and held a sincere conversation.

    Present at the meeting were Deng Xiaoping, General Secretary of the CPC Central Committee, Yang Shangkun, Alternate Secretary of the Secretariat, and Wu Xiuquan, Member of the Central Committee.

Xinhua News Agency, April 29th: Zhu De, Vice Chairman of the CPC Central Committee, and Dong Biwu, Vice Chairman of the People's Republic of China, received this afternoon the entire members of the Polish Military Delegation headed by General Bozyczkowski and held a cordial conversation.

    Present at the reception were General Luo Ruiqing, Senior General Peng Shaohui, Vice Minister of Foreign Affairs Luo Guibo, and Lieutenant Colonel Jiang Yizhen, Military Attaché of our Embassy in Poland.

    Also present at the reception were Furato, Chargé d'Affaires ad interim of the Polish Embassy in China, and Lieutenant Colonel Szymanski, Acting Military Attaché. (Photos attached)

    Comrade Zhu De shakes hands cordially with Head of Delegation Bozyczkowski. Comrade Dong Biwu is on the front right.

        Xinhua News Agency reporters Chen Juanmei and Liu Dong'ao

### 毛主席会见罗加总书记  朱德和董必武同志接见波兰军事代表团

1960-04-30
第1版()
专栏：

    毛主席会见罗加总书记
    朱德和董必武同志接见波兰军事代表团
    新华社天津29日电　中国共产党中央委员会主席毛泽东今天下午在天津会见了古巴人民社会党总书记布拉斯·罗加和他的夫人，并进行了恳切的谈话。
    会见时在座的有中共中央总书记邓小平，书记处候补书记杨尚昆，中央委员伍修权。
    新华社29日讯　中共中央副主席朱德、中华人民共和国副主席董必武，今天下午接见了以博齐沃夫斯基上将为首的波兰军事代表团全体成员，并且进行了亲切的交谈。
    接见时在座的有，罗瑞卿大将、彭绍辉上将，外交部副部长罗贵波，我国驻波兰大使馆武官姜一震中校等。
    接见时，波兰驻我国大使馆临时代办傅拉托和代理武官席曼斯基中校也在座。（附图片）
