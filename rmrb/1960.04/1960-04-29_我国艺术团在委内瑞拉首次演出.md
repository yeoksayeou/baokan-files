Title: China's Art Troupe Gives First Performance in Venezuela
Author:
Date: 1960-04-29
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, Caracas, 26th -- The Chinese Art Troupe gave its first performance at the City Theater here tonight. Venezuelan government officials, members of parliament, members of the Caracas City Council, leaders of the Venezuela-China Friendship Association, and well-known figures from all walks of life watched the performance.

### 我国艺术团在委内瑞拉首次演出

1960-04-29
第6版()
专栏：

    我国艺术团在委内瑞拉首次演出
    新华社加拉加斯26日电　中国艺术团今晚在这里的市剧院首次演出。委内瑞拉的政府官员、议员、加拉加斯市议员、委内瑞拉中国之友协会的领导人和各界知名人士观看了这次演出。
