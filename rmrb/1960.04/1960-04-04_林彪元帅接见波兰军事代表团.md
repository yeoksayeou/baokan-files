Title: Marshal Lin Biao Receives Polish Military Delegation
Author:
Date: 1960-04-04
Page: 1

    Xinhua News Agency, April 3rd - Marshal Lin Biao, Vice Premier of the State Council and Minister of National Defense, received the entire Polish Military Delegation, headed by General Bożywojewski, this morning. The hosts and guests engaged in cordial talks.
    Present at the reception were General Tan Zheng, Vice Minister of National Defense; General Zhang Aiping, Deputy Chief of the General Staff of the Chinese People's Liberation Army; as well as Lieutenant General Liu Zhijian, Lieutenant General Xiao Xiangrong, Lieutenant General Qiu Huizuo, Vice Admiral Liu Daosheng, Air Force Major General He Tingyi, and Colonel Jiang Yizhen, China's military attache to Poland.
    Also present at the reception were Mr. Furato, Chargé d'affaires ad interim of the Polish Embassy in China, and Lieutenant Colonel Szymański, Acting Military Attaché.
    After the reception, Marshal Lin Biao and the entire Polish Military Delegation took a photo together.

### 林彪元帅接见波兰军事代表团

1960-04-04
第1版()
专栏：

    林彪元帅接见波兰军事代表团
    新华社3日讯　国务院副总理、国防部长林彪元帅今天上午接见了以博齐沃夫斯基上将为首的波兰军事代表团的全体人员。宾主进行了亲切的交谈。
    接见时在座的有国防部副部长谭政大将，中国人民解放军副总参谋长张爱萍上将，以及刘志坚中将、萧向荣中将、丘会作中将、刘道生海军中将、何廷一空军少将和我国驻波兰武官姜一震中校等。
    接见时，波兰驻中国临时代办傅拉托和代理武官席曼斯基中校也在座。
    接见后，林彪元帅和波兰军事代表团的全体人员一起照了像。
