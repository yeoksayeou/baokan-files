Title: Liao Chengzhi Hosts a Banquet for Overseas Chinese and Hong Kong and Macao Compatriots
Author:
Date: 1960-04-30
Page: 7
Xinhua News Agency, April 29th: Liao Chengzhi, Director of the Overseas Chinese Affairs Commission, hosted a banquet tonight to entertain eight hundred overseas Chinese, Hong Kong and Macao compatriots, returned overseas Chinese, relatives of overseas Chinese, and returned overseas Chinese students who came to Beijing to participate in the "May Day" celebrations.

He Xiangning, Vice Chairman of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress and Vice Chairman of the National Committee of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference, attended today's banquet.

Also present at the banquet were Fang Fang and Zhuang Mingli, Deputy Directors of the Overseas Chinese Affairs Commission, and Cai Tingkai, Chen Qiyou, and Lian Guan, members of the commission, as well as You Yangzu, Wang Yuanxing, and Chen Qiyuan, Vice Chairmen of the All-China Federation of Returned Overseas Chinese.

After the banquet began, Liao Chengzhi proposed a toast amidst warm applause. On behalf of the State Council and the Overseas Chinese Affairs Commission, he extended a warm welcome to the relatives who had returned from abroad, the Hong Kong and Macao compatriots, and the returned overseas Chinese and their families from all parts of the motherland. The banquet was filled with a joyful and cordial atmosphere.

### 廖承志欢宴华侨和港澳同胞

1960-04-30
第7版()
专栏：

    廖承志欢宴华侨和港澳同胞
    新华社29日讯　华侨事务委员会主任廖承志今晚举行宴会，招待前来北京参加“五一”节庆祝活动的八百名国外华侨、港澳同胞和归侨、侨眷、归国华侨学生。
    全国人民代表大会常务委员会副委员长、中国人民政治协商会议全国委员会副主席何香凝出席了今天的宴会。
    出席宴会的，还有华侨事务委员会副主任方方、庄明理，委员蔡廷锴、陈其尤、连贯，全国侨联副主席尤扬祖、王源兴、陈其瑗等。
    宴会开始后，廖承志在热烈的掌声中祝酒。他代表国务院和华侨事务委员会对国外回来的亲人们、港澳同胞、和来自祖国各地的归侨、侨眷们表示热烈欢迎。宴会上充满着欢乐和亲切的气氛。
