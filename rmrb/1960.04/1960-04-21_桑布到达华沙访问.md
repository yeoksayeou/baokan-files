Title: Sambu Arrives in Warsaw for Visit
Author:
Date: 1960-04-21
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, Warsaw, April 20th: Sambu, Chairman of the Presidium of the Great People's Hural of the Mongolian People's Republic, and his entourage arrived in Warsaw by special plane today. Sambu is visiting Poland on a friendly visit at the invitation of Polish State Council Chairman Zawadzki.

Welcoming him at the airport were Polish state leaders Zawadzki and Cyrankiewicz, as well as diplomatic envoys from various countries to Poland and more than a thousand representatives from all walks of life in Warsaw.

Sambu and Zawadzki delivered speeches at the airport one after the other.

Accompanied by Polish leaders, Sambu traveled by car from the airport to his residence to rest, and was warmly welcomed by the citizens of Warsaw along the way.

Today's "People's Tribune" published an editorial welcoming Sambu.

### 桑布到达华沙访问

1960-04-21
第6版()
专栏：

    桑布到达华沙访问
    新华社华沙20日电　蒙古人民共和国大人民呼拉尔主席团主席桑布及其随行人员，今天乘专机抵达华沙。桑布是应波兰国务委员会主席萨瓦茨基的邀请前来波兰进行友好访问的。
    到机场欢迎的有波兰国家领导人萨瓦茨基、西伦凯维兹等，以及各国驻波兰外交使节和华沙各界群众代表共一千余人。
    桑布和萨瓦茨基在机场上先后讲了话。
    桑布由波兰领导人陪同乘车从机场到住所休息，沿途受到华沙市民的热烈欢迎。
    今天的“人民论坛报”发表了欢迎桑布的社论。
