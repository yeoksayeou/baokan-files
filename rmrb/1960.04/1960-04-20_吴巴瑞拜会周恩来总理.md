Title: U Ba Swe Meets with Premier Zhou Enlai
Author:
Date: 1960-04-20
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Rangoon, April 19th: U Ba Swe, Chairman of the Burmese Anti-Fascist People's Freedom League, met with Premier Zhou Enlai and Vice Premier and Foreign Minister Chen Yi here last night at 23:00 local time.

Li Yimang, Chinese Ambassador to Burma, was present at the meeting.

### 吴巴瑞拜会周恩来总理

1960-04-20
第1版()
专栏：

    吴巴瑞拜会周恩来总理
    新华社仰光19日电　缅甸巩固的反法西斯人民自由同盟主席吴巴瑞，昨晚于当地时间二十三点在这里拜会了周恩来总理和副总理兼外交部长陈毅元帅。
    拜会时在座的有中国驻缅甸大使李一氓。
