Title: State Council Holds its 100th Plenary Meeting
Author: 
Date: 1960-04-30
Page: 2

Xinhua News Agency, April 29th: The State Council held its 100th plenary meeting this morning.

The meeting resolved to appoint Cao Ying, Ambassador of the People's Republic of China to the Czechoslovak Republic, as the plenipotentiary representative for the signing of the "Consular Treaty between the People's Republic of China and the Czechoslovak Republic," and submitted it to the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress for decision.

After discussion, the meeting approved the "Cultural Cooperation Agreement between the Government of the People’s Republic of China and the Government of the Republic of Guinea."

The meeting also discussed and approved the Trademark Management Regulations and decided to submit them to the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress for deliberation and approval.

The meeting also approved changes in the administrative division of counties and cities, and personnel appointments and removals.

### 国务院举行第一百次全体会议

1960-04-30
第2版()
专栏：

    国务院举行第一百次全体会议
    新华社29日讯　国务院今天上午举行第一百次全体会议。
    会议决议派中华人民共和国驻捷克斯洛伐克共和国大使曹瑛为签订“中华人民共和国和捷克斯洛伐克共和国领事条约”的全权代表，提请全国人民代表大会常务委员会决定。
    会议经过讨论，核准了“中华人民共和国政府和几内亚共和国政府文化合作协定”。
    会议还讨论和通过了商标管理条例，并决定提请全国人民代表大会常务委员会审议批准。
    会议还通过了县、市行政区划变更事项和任免事项。
