Title: British-China Friendship Association Delegation Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1960-04-25
Page: 2

Xinhua News Agency, April 24th. The British-China Friendship Association delegation, consisting of four people led by Chairman Boggis, arrived in Beijing by plane today at the invitation of the Chinese People's Association for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries. Zhu Boshen and Jin Ziming, Deputy Secretaries-General of the Chinese People's Association for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries, were at the airport to welcome them.

### 英中友协代表团到京

1960-04-25
第2版()
专栏：

    英中友协代表团到京
    新华社24日讯　应中国人民对外文化协会邀请前来我国访问的英中友协代表团一行四人，在团长鲍给斯率领下，今天乘飞机到达北京。到机场欢迎的有中国人民对外文化协会副秘书长朱伯深、金子明等。
