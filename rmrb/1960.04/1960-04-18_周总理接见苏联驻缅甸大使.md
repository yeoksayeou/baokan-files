Title: Premier Zhou Enlai Receives the Soviet Ambassador to Burma
Author: 
Date: 1960-04-18
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Rangoon, April 17th: Premier Zhou Enlai received the Soviet Ambassador to Burma, A.M. Ledovsky, at the hotel at 3:30 this afternoon.

Present at the meeting were Vice Premier and Foreign Minister Chen Yi and Chinese Ambassador to Burma, Li Yimang.

### 周总理接见苏联驻缅甸大使

1960-04-18
第1版()
专栏：

    周总理接见苏联驻缅甸大使
    新华社仰光17日电　周恩来总理在今天下午三点三十分在宾馆接见了苏联驻缅甸大使安·麦·列多夫斯基。
    在场的有副总理兼外交部长陈毅和中国驻缅甸大使李一氓。
