Title: Vietnamese Ministry of Transport and Post Office Education Delegation Returns Home
Author:
Date: 1960-04-03
Page: 4

    Xinhua News Agency, April 2nd. The Vietnamese Democratic Republic Ministry of Transport and Post Office Education Delegation, led by Nguyen Nhu Que, Deputy Minister of the Vietnamese Democratic Republic Ministry of Transport and Post Office, returned to China after a month and a half of inspection and visits in Beijing, Shanghai, Dalian, Wuhan and other places. They left Beijing by train this morning to return home.
    Yu Guang-sheng, Vice Minister of Railways, and representatives from the Ministry of Transport and the Ministry of Posts and Telecommunications went to the station to see them off.
    Also seeing them off was Tran Trong Quat, Charge d'Affaires of the Embassy of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam in China, and others.

### 越南交通和邮电部教育考察团返国

1960-04-03
第4版()
专栏：

    越南交通和邮电部教育考察团返国
    新华社2日讯　越南民主共和国交通和邮电部副部长阮如晷率领的越南民主共和国交通和邮电部教育考察团，在我国北京、上海、大连、武汉等地经过一个半月考察参观后，今天早晨乘火车离京返国。
    前往车站欢送的，有铁道部副部长余光生和交通部、邮电部的代表等。
    同时送行的，还有越南民主共和国驻华大使馆代办陈仲括等。
