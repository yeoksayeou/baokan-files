Title: Delegation of the General Union of Algerian Workers Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1960-04-21
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, April 20th. At the invitation of the All-China Federation of Trade Unions, the delegation of the General Union of Algerian Workers, headed by Fares Ben Mehall, and member Fatima Ben Salem, arrived in Beijing by plane this afternoon to participate in the "May Day" International Labor Day celebrations and visit China.
    The other two members of the delegation are on their way to China.

### 阿尔及利亚工人总联合会代表团到京

1960-04-21
第4版()
专栏：

    阿尔及利亚工人总联合会代表团到京
    新华社20日讯　应中华全国总工会邀请，来我国参加“五一”国际劳动节庆祝活动和访问的阿尔及利亚工人总联合会代表团团长法莱斯·本·梅哈尔和团员法蒂马·本·萨莱姆，今天下午乘飞机到达北京。
    代表团的另外两名团员，正在来华途中。
