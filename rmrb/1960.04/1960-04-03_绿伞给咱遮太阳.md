Title: A Green Umbrella to Shade Us
Author: Han Li
Date: 1960-04-03
Page: 8
    Green Umbrella to Shade Us
    In the third month of spring, flowers bloom everywhere,
    A beautiful scene on the Xu-Huai Plain.
    The tree-planting army fills the mountains and fields,
    Red flags flutter, showing great momentum.
    Hoes flash, dancing in the air,
    Sweat drops to the ground, scenting the soil.
    The mountain ridges are planted with honey peaches,
    Willows are planted along the roadsides.
    Spring breeze and autumn rain for three to five years,
    Peach blossoms red, willows green, a long-lasting affection.
    In summer, walk on the tree-lined paths,
    A green umbrella to shade us from the sun.
    Combating Drought
    Along the canal, silhouettes bustle,
    On the Xu-Hai road, songs resonate,
    Cars like dragons, people form lines,
    Water buckets shatter the moon in the river,
    Carrying poles bend like bows under the weight.
    Loads of golden silver liquid,
    Irrigating ten thousand acres of fields,
    Bright lights shame the stars and moon,
    Like the Huaihai Campaign of old.
    Jiangsu Xuzhou Han Li

### 绿伞给咱遮太阳
韩莉
1960-04-03
第8版()
专栏：

    绿伞给咱遮太阳
    三春遍地花儿香，
    徐淮平原好风光。
    植树大军满山野，
    红旗飘飘气势壮。
    镐头闪光空中舞，
    汗水落地泥土香。
    山岭种满水蜜桃，
    杨柳栽在道路旁。
    春风秋雨三五载，
    桃红柳绿情意长。
    夏日走上林荫道，
    绿伞给咱遮太阳。
    抗旱
    运河沿岸人影乱，
    徐海路上歌声喧，
    车如龙，人成线，
    水桶击碎河中月，
    扁担压得弓样弯。
    担担金银浆，
    灌饱万顷田，
    灯火辉煌羞星月，
    好像当年淮海战。
    江苏徐州　韩莉
