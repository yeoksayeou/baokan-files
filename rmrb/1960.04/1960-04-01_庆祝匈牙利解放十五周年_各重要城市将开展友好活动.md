Title: Celebrating the 15th Anniversary of Hungary's Liberation, Major Cities Will Carry Out Friendly Activities
Author:
Date: 1960-04-01
Page: 3

Xinhua News Agency, 31st. April 4th is the great festival of the 15th anniversary of the liberation of Hungary. From April 1st to 7th, many important cities in our country will carry out "Sino-Hungarian Friendship Week" celebrations to warmly celebrate the festival of the liberation of the Hungarian people.

	During the "Sino-Hungarian Friendship Week," the working people of Beijing, Tianjin, Wuhan, Guangzhou, Chengdu, Xi'an, Changchun, Jinan and other cities will widely hold celebration meetings, film weeks, report meetings, and get-togethers. The "Celebration Meeting for the 15th Anniversary of the Liberation of Hungary" sponsored by the Committee for Foreign Cultural Relations, the Sino-Hungarian Friendship Association, the All-China Federation of Trade Unions, the All-China Youth Federation, the All-China Women's Federation, and the All-China Federation of Literary and Art Circles will be grandly held in the capital on April 3. The naming ceremony of the Beijing "Huangtugang Sino-Hungarian Friendship People's Commune" will be held in the commune on April 3. In Ziyang, Shandong Province, where the Sino-Hungarian Friendship Tractor Station and other Hungarian experts work, the workers and peasants there will also hold celebrations together with the Hungarian experts.

### 庆祝匈牙利解放十五周年  各重要城市将开展友好活动

1960-04-01
第3版()
专栏：

    庆祝匈牙利解放十五周年
    各重要城市将开展友好活动
    新华社31日讯　4月4日是匈牙利解放十五周年的伟大节日。从4月1日到7日，我国许多重要城市将开展“中匈友好周”的庆祝活动，热烈庆祝匈牙利人民解放的节日。
    在“中匈友好周”内，北京、天津、武汉、广州、成都、西安、长春、济南等城市的劳动人民将广泛举行庆祝会、电影周、报告会、联欢会等庆祝活动。由对外文化联络委员会、中匈友好协会、全国总工会、全国青联、全国妇联、全国文联主办的“匈牙利解放十五周年庆祝大会”将于4月3日在首都隆重举行。北京“黄土岗中匈友好人民公社”命名大会4月3日将在该社举行。山东省滋阳的中匈友谊拖拉机站和其他有匈牙利专家工作的地方，那里的工人和农民也将同匈牙利专家在一起举行庆祝活动。
