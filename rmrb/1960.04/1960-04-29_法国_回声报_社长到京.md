Title: President of the French "Les Echos" Arrives in Beijing
Author: 
Date: 1960-04-29
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, April 28th. Emile Servan-Schreiber, President of the French newspaper "Les Echos," and his wife arrived in Beijing by plane on the afternoon of the 27th, at the invitation of the All-China Journalists Association for a visit to our country.

### 法国“回声报”社长到京

1960-04-29
第6版()
专栏：

    法国“回声报”社长到京
    新华社28日讯　应中华全国新闻工作者协会邀请来我国访问的法国“回声报”社长埃米尔·塞文—希莱伯和他的夫人，27日下午乘飞机到达北京。
