Title: Removing the Stumbling Blocks on the Road Forward (Picture)
Author:
Date: 1960-04-06
Page: 5

The agricultural collectivization movement in the German Democratic Republic is making new progress. Boundary markers, as symbols of private ownership, have become stumbling blocks to the progress of the farmers. The picture shows members of the Bodenkow cooperative and tractor drivers in the Anklam district of Neubrandenburg removing the boundary marker.

　　German News Agency dispatch (Xinhua News Agency)

### 搬掉前进道路上的绊脚石（图片）

1960-04-06
第5版()
专栏：

    搬掉前进道路上的绊脚石
    德意志民主共和国农业合作化运动正在取得新的进展，作为私有制象征的界石已经成为农民们进步的绊脚石。这是新勃兰登堡安克拉姆区博德科夫的社员与拖拉机手们把界石搬掉的情形。
  　德意志通讯社稿（新华社发）
