Title: A Guest from Senegal Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1960-04-30
Page: 7
    Xinhua News Agency, April 29th. Mr. Tidiane Baidy Ly, a guest from Senegal, Secretary-General of the West African Asian-African Solidarity Committee, and member of the Central Committee of the African Independence Party, arrived in Beijing by plane today at the invitation of the Chinese Asian-African Solidarity Committee.

### 一位塞内加尔客人到京

1960-04-30
第7版()
专栏：

    一位塞内加尔客人到京
    新华社29日讯　应中国亚非团结委员会邀请前来我国访问的塞内加尔客人、西非亚非团结委员会秘书长和非洲独立党中央委员提迪安·巴依迪·黎，今天乘飞机到达北京。
