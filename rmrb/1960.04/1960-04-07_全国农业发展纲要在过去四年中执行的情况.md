The implementation of the National Agricultural Development Program in the Past Four Years

	Agricultural output value: In 1959, it reached 78.3 billion yuan (excluding community-run industries), with an average income of about 85 yuan per person, achieving the 1962 target ahead of schedule.
	Grain: In 1959, 504 counties reached or exceeded yields of 400, 500, and 800 jin per mu respectively, accounting for 28% of the country's counties.
	Cotton: In 1959, 204 counties had yields of 60, 80, or more than 100 jin of ginned cotton per mu, accounting for 20% of the country's cotton-growing counties.
	Pigs: In 1959, a total of 270 million pigs were raised throughout the year, with 180 million pigs in stock at the end of the year, reaching the 1962 standard ahead of schedule.

### 全国农业发展纲要在过去四年中执行的情况

1960-04-07
第1版()
专栏：

    全国农业发展纲要在过去四年中执行的情况
    农业产值　1959年达到783亿元（不包括社办工业），平均每人收入85元左右，提前达到1962年前后的目标
    粮食 1959年有504个县分别达到和超过亩产400斤、500斤、800斤，占全国县数的28％
    棉花　1959年有204个县亩产皮棉60斤、80斤或100斤以上，占全国植棉县的20％
    猪　1959年全年共养猪27000万头，年底存栏18000万头，提前达到1962年的标准
