Title: Lenin Will Live Forever in the Hearts of All Mankind; Fraternal Countries Launch Activities to Commemorate Lenin's Birthday
Author:
Date: 1960-04-22
Page: 5

    Lenin Will Live Forever in the Hearts of All Mankind
    Fraternal Countries Launch Activities to Commemorate Lenin's Birthday
    Xinhua News Agency, Hanoi, April 20th: More than 8,000 people from all walks of life in Vietnam gathered at the Hanoi People's Theater on the evening of the 20th to solemnly commemorate the 90th anniversary of Lenin's birth.
    Attending the meeting were party and government leaders such as Le Duan, Pham Van Dong, Truong Chinh, Nguyen Duy Trinh, representatives from all walks of life, and foreign envoys to Vietnam.
    Lenin Peace Prize winner, Chairman of the Presidium of the Central Committee of the Vietnam Fatherland Front, and President of the Vietnam-Soviet Friendship Association, Ton Duc Thang, delivered the opening speech, followed by a speech by Le Duan, member of the Political Bureau of the Vietnam Workers' Party.
    Le Duan warmly praised Lenin's great contribution to the treasure house of Marxism and Lenin's genius leadership. He said that under the banner of Leninism, millions of people are fighting for the victory of communism and peace.
    Le Duan also emphasized the influence of Leninism on the Vietnamese revolution. He said that the October Revolution awakened the people of the East and brought Leninism to the Vietnamese people.
    Soviet Ambassador to Vietnam Sokolov also spoke at the meeting.
    The meeting finally adopted a congratulatory message to the Central Committee of the CPSU and the Soviet government on the 90th anniversary of Lenin's birth.
    Xinhua News Agency, April 20th: Romania, the German Democratic Republic, Hungary, Czechoslovakia, Albania, and Mongolia have widely launched activities to commemorate the 90th anniversary of Lenin's birth.
    The Romanian Workers' Party Senior Party School, the Institute of Party History, the Institute of Social Sciences of the Academy of Sciences, and the Military Academy jointly held a scientific conference on April 14th and 15th to commemorate Lenin's birth. Twelve reports on Leninism theory were presented at the meeting. In addition, the Academy of Sciences also held three report meetings to commemorate Lenin's birth.
    In the past week, cultural centers and clubs in Romanian cities and villages have widely launched activities to commemorate Lenin. In more than 300 rural cultural centers in Oradea State alone, more than 160,000 people participated in activities commemorating Lenin's birth. Amateur art groups across the country are actively preparing literary and artistic programs to commemorate Lenin.
    The German Democratic Republic began preparations for commemorating Lenin's birth at the beginning of this month. The Socialist Unity Party organizations at all levels, as well as factories and rural areas, will hold commemorative activities from the 20th to the 23rd. In recent days, central and local newspapers have continuously published many articles introducing Lenin's life, excerpts from Lenin's works, and memoirs written by people who had met Lenin. The German-Soviet Friendship Association held an exhibition on Lenin's life, and screened films such as "Lenin in October," "Lenin in 1918," "The Ulyanov Family," and "The Story of Lenin" in many of the association's clubs.
    In recent days, tens of thousands of people have visited the Lenin Museum in Leipzig, where Lenin met with members of the Bolshevik faction of the State Duma in early 1912. People also visited the "Spark" Museum in Leipzig, where the first "Spark" newspaper was published sixty years ago.
    Factories, schools, and rural areas throughout Hungary will generally gather on the 21st to commemorate Lenin's birth. On the same day, a national commemorative meeting will be held in the sports hall of the Csepel factory in Budapest. The Academy of Sciences will also hold a scientific conference to discuss the great significance of Leninism.
    The nine agricultural production cooperatives in Szolnok State, Hungary, named after Lenin, have recently universally organized photo exhibitions on Lenin's life, and they have also invited veteran revolutionaries to talk about Lenin's revolutionary activities.
    In the cities and villages of Czechoslovakia, evenings, report meetings, and forums commemorating Lenin's birth have been held in recent days. Thousands of people have visited the Lenin Museum in Prague every day recently. The museum is preparing to hold a large-scale exhibition of "The Victory of Leninism," exhibiting a large number of documents reflecting the great victory of Leninism in the Soviet Union, in other socialist countries, and in the international workers' movement.
    In Tirana, the capital of Albania, the Lenin Stalin Museum and the National Publishing Bureau jointly held an exhibition of Lenin's works published in Albanian. Bookstores across the country have special sections recommending Lenin's works to the general public.
    The Lenin Life Exhibition held by the Central Committee of the Mongolian People's Revolutionary Party to commemorate the 90th anniversary of Lenin's birth opened at the Central Museum in Ulaanbaatar on the 20th. Mongolian party and government leaders Tsedenbal and others, and foreign envoys to Mongolia attended the opening ceremony.
    The exhibition displays more than 600 items, including pictures and manuscripts reflecting Lenin's life and revolutionary activities.

### 列宁永远活在全人类的心中  兄弟国家纷纷展开活动纪念列宁诞辰

1960-04-22
第5版()
专栏：

    列宁永远活在全人类的心中
    兄弟国家纷纷展开活动纪念列宁诞辰
    新华社河内20日电　越南各界人民代表八千多人20日晚上在河内人民剧场集会隆重纪念列宁诞辰九十周年。
    出席大会的有黎笋、范文同、长征、阮维祯等党政领导人、各界代表和各国驻越南使节。
    列宁和平奖金获得者、越南祖国战线中央委员会主席团主席、越苏友好协会会长孙德胜致开幕词后，越南劳动党政治局委员黎笋发表了讲话。
    黎笋热烈赞扬了列宁对马克思主义宝库的伟大贡献和列宁的天才领导。他说，在列宁主义的旗帜下，千百万人民正在为争取共产主义和和平的胜利而斗争。
    黎笋还着重说明了列宁主义对越南革命的影响。他说，十月革命唤醒了东方人民，并且把列宁主义带给了越南人民。
    苏联驻越南大使索科洛夫在大会上也讲了话。
    大会最后通过了就列宁诞辰九十周年纪念给苏共中央和苏联政府的贺电。
    新华社20日讯　罗马尼亚、德意志民主共和国、匈牙利、捷克斯洛伐克、阿尔巴尼亚和蒙古等国广泛展开纪念列宁诞生九十周年的活动。
    罗马尼亚工人党高级党校、党史研究所、科学院社会科学研究所和军事学院，在14日和15日联合举行了纪念列宁诞辰的科学会议。会上作了有关列宁主义理论的十二个报告。此外，科学院还举办了三次纪念列宁诞辰的报告会。
    近一周来，罗马尼亚城乡的文化馆、俱乐部广泛展开了纪念列宁的活动。单在俄拉第亚州的三百多个农村文化馆中，参加纪念列宁诞辰活动的人就达十六万以上。全国的业余艺术团体都在积极准备纪念列宁的文艺节目。
    德意志民主共和国从本月初就开始筹备纪念列宁诞辰。统一社会党各级组织以及工厂和农村将从20日到23日举行纪念活动。近日来，中央和地方报纸连续刊登了许多介绍列宁生平的文章、列宁著作的摘录，以及一些曾经同列宁会过面的人写的回忆录。德苏友好协会举办了有关列宁生平的展览会，在协会的许多俱乐部放映了“列宁在十月”、“列宁在一九一八”、“乌里扬诺夫一家”和“列宁的故事”等影片。
    这几天，成千上万的人参观了来比锡的列宁博物馆，1912年初，列宁曾经在那里会见国家杜马布尔什维克党团的成员。人们还参观了来比锡“火星”博物馆，六十年前，第一份“火星报”就是在那里出版的。
    匈牙利各地的工厂、学校和农村将在21日普遍集会纪念列宁诞辰。同一天，在布达佩斯切佩尔工厂体育馆内将举行全国性的纪念大会。科学院也将举行科学会议，讨论列宁主义学说的伟大意义。
    匈牙利索尔诺克州以列宁命名的九个农业生产合作社，最近已普遍组织了有关列宁生平的图片展览，它们还邀请了老革命战士讲述列宁的革命活动。
    在捷克斯洛伐克的城市和乡村，这些天来纷纷举行了纪念列宁诞辰的晚会、报告会和座谈会。布拉格的列宁博物馆最近以来每天都有上千的人前往参观。博物馆正在准备举行“列宁主义的胜利”的大型展览会，展出大量反映列宁主义在苏联、在其它社会主义国家和在国际工人运动中取得伟大胜利的文献。
    在阿尔巴尼亚首都地拉那，列宁斯大林博物馆和国家出版局联合举办了用阿文出版的列宁著作展览会。全国各地的书店都设有向广大读者推荐列宁著作的专部。
    蒙古人民革命党中央委员会为纪念列宁诞生九十周年而举办的列宁生平展览会，20日在乌兰巴托中央博物馆开幕。蒙古党政领导人泽登巴尔等和各国驻蒙古的外交使节出席了开幕式。
    展览会共展出了反映列宁生活和革命活动的图片、手稿等六百多件。
