Title: Chu Tunan Receives Delegation from the Japan-China Friendship Association

Author:
Date: 1960-04-27
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, April 26th. Chu Tunan, President of the Chinese People's Association for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries, received this afternoon all members of the delegation from the Japan-China Friendship Association, led by Masuzo Akatsu, head of the delegation, Managing Director of the Japan-China Friendship Association, and Vice-Chairman of the Committee for the Restoration of Diplomatic Relations of the Japan-China Friendship Association.

Present at the reception were Lin Lin and Jin Ziming, Deputy Secretaries-General of the Chinese People's Association for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries.

### 楚图南接见日中友协访华代表团

1960-04-27
第4版()
专栏：

    楚图南接见日中友协访华代表团
    新华社26日讯　中国人民对外文化协会会长楚图南今天下午接见了由团长、日中友好协会常务理事、日中友好协会恢复邦交委员会副委员长赤津益造率领的日中友好协会访华代表团全体团员。
    接见时在座的有中国人民对外文化协会副秘书长林林、金子明等。
