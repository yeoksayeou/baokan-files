Title: Chairman Jalkhasaikhan
Author:
Date: 1960-04-08
Page: 1

Chairman of the Great People's Hural of the Mongolian People's Republic, Bayanlygiin Jalkhasaikhan, was born in 1915 into a herdsman's family in the Choibalsan Province. He graduated from primary school, middle school, party school, and the Moscow Institute of Oriental Studies.

Comrade Jalkhasaikhan has worked in the Mongolian Revolutionary Youth League organization since 1934, serving as chairman of the league committee, a minister in the Central Committee of the League, and other positions. He has also served as the director of youth magazines, academic research magazines, literary magazines, and the Youth League newspaper, as well as president of the Academy of Sciences and chairman of the Writers Association.

Since 1941, Comrade Jalkhasaikhan transferred to work in diplomatic organs, serving as embassy secretary, ambassador, and director and minister of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs. In 1950, he was appointed as the first ambassador to China.

Since 1951, he has been elected as a representative to the Great People's Hural of Mongolia. In 1957, he was elected as chairman of the Foreign Affairs Committee of the Great People's Hural, and in 1959, he was elected as chairman of the Great People's Hural.

Since 1947, Comrade Jalkhasaikhan has been elected as a member of the Central Committee of the Mongolian People's Revolutionary Party. From 1956 to the present, he has served as the Minister of the International Liaison Department of the Central Committee of the Mongolian People's Revolutionary Party.

(Xinhua News Agency) (Attached picture)

### 贾尔卡赛汗主席

1960-04-08
第1版()
专栏：

    贾尔卡赛汗主席
    蒙古人民共和国大人民呼拉尔主席巴扬勒·贾尔卡赛汗1915年生于乔巴山省的一个牧民家庭，毕业于小学、中学、党校和莫斯科东方研究学院。
    贾尔卡赛汗同志自1934年起就在蒙古革命青年团组织里担任工作，曾任过团委员会主席、团中央的部长等职。他还先后担任过青年杂志社、学术研究杂志社、文艺杂志社和青年团报社的社长以及科学院院长和作家协会主席等职务。
    1941年起，贾尔卡赛汗同志转到外交机关工作，曾任大使馆秘书、大使和外交部司长、部长等职。1950年他曾被任为首任驻华大使。
    1951年起，他被选为蒙古大人民呼拉尔代表，1957年被选为大人民呼拉尔外事委员会主席，1959年被选为大人民呼拉尔主席。
    贾尔卡赛汗同志，自1947年起被选为蒙古人民革命党中央委员会委员，从1956年开始到现在，一直担任蒙古人民革命党中央委员会对外联络部部长。
