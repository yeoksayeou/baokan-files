Title: Lenin's Voice
Author: Lǐ Xué'áo
Date: 1960-04-22
Page: 8

    Lenin's Voice
    Li Xue'ao, Worker at the Beijing People's Printing Factory
    Lenin—the great teacher!
    In the busy printing workshop,
    We print your most glorious works day and night,
    Listening to your most resounding voice!
    I hear,
    You passionately lecturing to the workers of St. Petersburg,
    Every word deeply imprinted in people's hearts.
    I hear,
    You fiercely debating with revisionists,
    Using the sharp blade of language to clamp down on the traitors' lips!
    I hear,
    You and the Moscow workers doing voluntary labor,
    Using the communist spirit to build the railway!
    This is a "Great Beginning"!
    You loudly praise the sprouts of communism!
    I hear,
    You striding into China,
    Accompanied by the guns of the Aurora.
    Looking at the red flag of Jinggangshan,
    Looking at the cave dwellings of Yan'an,
    Looking at the blast furnaces of Anshan,
    Looking at the oil wells of Yumen,
    Looking at the newly born People's Commune,
    Looking at the red lights of Tiananmen Square...
    You happily smiled, smiling so sweetly!
    I hear,
    You loudly talking about communism!
    Talking about the world's class struggle!
    Talking about the great Sino-Soviet friendship!
    Talking about the solid socialist camp!…
    In the busy printing workshop,
    I listen to you day and night,
    The thunderous voice of truth!

### 列宁的声音
李学鳌
1960-04-22
第8版()
专栏：

    列宁的声音
    北京人民印刷厂工人　李学鳌
    列宁——伟大的导师！
    在繁忙的印书车间，
    我们日夜印着您最光辉的著作，
    听着您最洪亮的声音！
    我听见，
    您向彼得堡的工人热情讲演，
    每句话都深印在人们心中。
    我听见，
    您同修正主义者激烈辩论，
    用语言的利刃，钳烂了叛徒的嘴唇！
    我听见，
    您和莫斯科工人义务劳动，
    用共产主义精神把铁路修成！
    这是“伟大的创举”！
    您高声地把共产主义的萌芽赞颂！
    我听见，
    您迈着大步来到中国，
    伴着阿芙洛尔的炮声。
    望着井冈山的红旗，
    望着延安的窑洞，
    望着鞍钢的高炉，
    望着玉门的油井，
    望着新生的人民公社，
    望着天安门的红灯。……
    您高兴地笑了，笑得那么动听！
    我听见，
    您大声讲着共产主义！
    讲着世界的阶级斗争！
    讲着伟大的中苏友谊！
    讲着牢固的社会主义阵营！……
    在繁忙的印书车间，
    我日夜听着您，
    巨雷般的真理的声音！
