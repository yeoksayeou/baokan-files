Title: Premier Zhou Calls on Prime Minister Koirala
Author:
Date: 1960-04-27
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, Kathmandu, April 26th: Premier Zhou Enlai called on Nepalese Prime Minister B. P. Koirala at 13:30 local time today. Present at the meeting were Vice Premier and Foreign Minister Chen Yi, other principal members of Premier Zhou's entourage, and several Nepalese cabinet ministers. Also present were Pan Zili, Chinese Ambassador to Nepal, and Ban Zhantrui, Charge d'Affaires ad interim of the Nepalese Embassy in China. After a friendly conversation, the hosts and guests had lunch together.

### 周总理拜会柯伊拉腊首相

1960-04-27
第1版()
专栏：

    周总理拜会柯伊拉腊首相
    新华社加德满都26日电　周恩来总理在今天当地时间十三时三十分拜会尼泊尔首相毕·普·柯伊拉腊。拜会时在场的有副总理兼外交部长陈毅和周总理其他主要的随行人员和尼泊尔几位内阁大臣。中国驻尼泊尔大使潘自力和尼泊尔驻中国大使馆临时代办班特瑞也在场。在进行友好会谈后，宾主在一起共进午餐。
