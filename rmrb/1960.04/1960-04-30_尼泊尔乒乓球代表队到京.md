Title: Nepalese Table Tennis Team Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1960-04-30
Page: 7

Xinhua News Agency, April 29th: The Nepalese Table Tennis Team, consisting of eight members led by Team Leader Shanta Ram Bhandari, arrived in Beijing by train this evening at the invitation of the All-China Sports Federation. Among them, Mrs. Kamal Rana, the Deputy Speaker of the Nepalese Upper House of Parliament and the Nepalese Women's Table Tennis Singles Champion, also came to China for a visit at the invitation of the China-Nepal Friendship Association.
　　
When the guests arrived at the station, they were warmly welcomed by Zhang Lianhua, Secretary-General of the All-China Sports Federation, Zhang Yihua, Secretary-General of the China-Nepal Friendship Association, and Wang Chuanyao, Sun Meiying, and other famous Chinese male and female table tennis players.

The Nepalese Table Tennis Team arrived in China on April 16th. They have successively held friendly matches with local table tennis players in Kunming, Guangzhou, and Shanghai. The guest team will be invited to participate in the "May Day" International Labor Day celebrations in Beijing and are scheduled to hold friendly matches with Beijing table tennis players on May 2nd and 4th.

### 尼泊尔乒乓球代表队到京

1960-04-30
第7版()
专栏：

    尼泊尔乒乓球代表队到京
    新华社29日讯　应中华全国体育总会邀请来我国访问的尼泊尔乒乓球代表队一行八人，由领队山塔·拉姆·班达里率领，今晚乘火车到达北京。其中，尼泊尔国会上议院副议长、尼泊尔女子乒乓球单打冠军卡玛尔·拉纳夫人，还同时应中国尼泊尔友好协会邀请来我国访问。
  当客人们到达车站时，受到了中华全国体育总会秘书长张联华、中国尼泊尔友好协会总干事张亦桦，以及王传耀、孙梅英等我国著名男女乒乓球运动员们的热烈欢迎。
    尼泊尔乒乓球队是4月16日到达我国的。他们曾先后在昆明、广州、上海同当地乒乓球运动员进行了友谊比赛。客队将应邀在北京参加“五一”国际劳动节庆祝活动，并且预定5月2日和4日同北京市乒乓球运动员进行友谊比赛。
