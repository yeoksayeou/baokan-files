Title: Premier Zhou Enlai Departs for Foreign Visit

Author:

Date: 1960-04-14
Page: 1

At the invitation of the governments of Burma, India and Nepal
Premier Zhou Enlai Departs for Foreign Visit
Vice Premier of the State Council and Minister of Foreign Affairs Marshal Chen Yi and others accompany him
State leaders such as Soong Ching Ling, Dong Biwu, Zhu De, Chen Yun, Lin Biao, and Deng Xiaoping see him off at the airport
Xinhua News Agency, April 13th: Premier of the State Council Zhou Enlai and his entourage, at the invitation of the Government of the Union of Burma, the Government of the Republic of India, and the Government of His Majesty the King of Nepal, left Beijing by special plane this morning for friendly visits to these countries. Premier Zhou Enlai and his party will arrive in Rangoon on the 15th.

Accompanying him on the visit were Marshal Chen Yi, Vice Premier of the State Council and Minister of Foreign Affairs, Zhang Hanfu, Vice Minister of Foreign Affairs, Zhang Yan, Deputy Director of the Foreign Affairs Office of the State Council, Qiao Guanhua, Assistant Minister of Foreign Affairs, Luo Qingchang, Deputy Director of the Premier's Office of the State Council, Zhang Wenjin, Director of the First Asian Department of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs, Kang Maozhao, Deputy Director of the Information Department of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs, and other accompanying personnel.

State leaders such as Vice Chairpersons Soong Ching Ling and Dong Biwu, Chairman Zhu De, and Vice Premiers Chen Yun, Lin Biao, and Deng Xiaoping went to the airport to see him off.

Before boarding the plane, Premier Zhou Enlai and Vice Premier Chen Yi warmly shook hands with the state leaders and all those seeing him off at the airport. Everyone wished Premier Zhou Enlai a successful and safe trip abroad.

Also present at the airport to see him off were Vice Premiers of the State Council He Long, Li Fuchun, Li Xiannian, Nie Rongzhen, Bo Yibo, Tan Zhenlin, Lu Dingyi, and Xi Zhongxun; Vice Chairpersons of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress Lin Boqu, Luo Ronghuan, Shen Junru, Guo Moruo, Peng Zhen, Li Weihan, Chen Shutong, Saifudin, Cheng Qian, and Lin Feng; Vice Chairpersons of the National Committee of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference Li Siguang, Kang Sheng, Pagbalha Geleg Namgyal, and Ngapoi Ngawang Jigme; Vice Chairpersons of the National Defense Council Zhang Zhizhong and Fu Zuoyi; President of the Supreme People's Court Xie Juezai; Procurator-General of the Supreme People's Procuratorate Zhang Dingcheng; and Vice Ministers of Foreign Affairs Luo Guibo, Ji Pengfei, Zeng Yongquan, and Geng Biao.

Also present at the airport to see him off were the heads of various government departments, members of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress, members of the Standing Committee of the National Committee of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference, the heads of various democratic parties, people's organizations, and Beijing Municipality, the heads of the People's Committees and CPPCC committees of various provinces and autonomous regions in Beijing, as well as representatives and members of ethnic minorities attending the Second Session of the Second National People's Congress, and attending the Second Session of the Third National Committee of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference, and so on.

The foreign diplomatic officials who went to the airport to see him off included: Afghan Ambassador Samad, Swedish Ambassador Book, Ambassador of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea Li Yong-ho, Czechoslovak Ambassador Bushniak, Ambassador of the German Democratic Republic Wang Dael, Ceylonese Ambassador Goebbels-Rava, Bulgarian Ambassador Panchevsky, Cambodian Ambassador Lan-?Te, Pakistani Ambassador Malik, Romanian Ambassador Chahal Leccu, Norwegian Ambassador Dons, Swiss Ambassador Navi Yi, Finnish Ambassador War Wanni, Albanian Ambassador Palivti, Soviet Ambassador Chervonenko, British Charge d'Affaires Steward, Dutch Charge d'Affaires Kordavitch, Polish Embassy Charge d'Affaires Fulato, Vietnamese Embassy Charge d'Affaires Tran Trong Quat, Hungarian Embassy Charge d'Affaires Szigeti, Indonesian Embassy Charge d'Affaires Suleiman, United Arab Republic Embassy Charge d'Affaires Kailani, Indian Embassy Charge d'Affaires Kanan Pilai, Burmese Embassy Charge d'Affaires Wu Ong Qin, Yugoslav Embassy Charge d'Affaires Saukolaz, Mongolian Embassy Counselor Dangesuren, Danish Embassy First Secretary Cao Ersheng, and others. ### 周恩来总理启程出国访问

1960-04-14
第1版()
专栏：

    应缅甸、印度和尼泊尔政府的邀请
    周恩来总理启程出国访问
    国务院副总理兼外交部长陈毅元帅等随同前往
    宋庆龄、董必武、朱德、陈云、林彪、邓小平等国家领导人到机场送行
    新华社13日讯　国务院总理周恩来和他的随行人员，应缅甸联邦政府、印度共和国政府和尼泊尔国王陛下政府的邀请，今天上午乘专机离开北京，前往这些国家进行友好访问。周恩来总理一行将在15日到达仰光。
    随同前往访问的，有国务院副总理兼外交部长陈毅元帅，外交部副部长章汉夫、国务院外事办公室副主任张彦、外交部部长助理乔冠华、国务院总理办公室副主任罗青长、外交部第一亚洲司司长章文晋、外交部新闻司副司长康矛召和其他随行人员。
    宋庆龄、董必武副主席，朱德委员长，陈云、林彪、邓小平副总理等国家领导人，前往机场送行。
    周恩来总理和陈毅副总理等，在走上飞机之前，同到机场的国家领导人和全体送行的人们，一一亲切握手。大家祝周恩来总理出国访问成功，一路平安。
    到机场送行的，还有国务院副总理贺龙、李富春、李先念、聂荣臻、薄一波、谭震林、陆定一、习仲勋，全国人民代表大会常务委员会副委员长林伯渠、罗荣桓、沈钧儒、郭沫若、彭真、李维汉、陈叔通、赛福鼎、程潜、林枫，中国人民政治协商会议全国委员会副主席李四光、康生、帕巴拉·卓列朗杰、阿沛·阿旺晋美，国防委员会副主席张治中、傅作义，最高人民法院院长谢觉哉，最高人民检察院检察长张鼎丞，外交部副部长罗贵波、姬鹏飞、曾涌泉、耿飚。
    到机场送行的，还有政府各部门的负责人，全国人民代表大会常务委员会的委员，中国人民政治协商会议全国委员会常务委员会的委员，各民主党派、各人民团体和北京市的负责人，在北京的各省、自治区人民委员会和政协各省、自治区委员会的负责人，以及出席第二届全国人民代表大会第二次会议和出席中国人民政治协商会议第三届全国委员会第二次会议的少数民族代表和委员等。
