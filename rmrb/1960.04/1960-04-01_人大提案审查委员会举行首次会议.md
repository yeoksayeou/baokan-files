Title: NPC Proposal Examination Committee Holds First Meeting
Author:
Date: 1960-04-01
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, March 31st: The Proposal Examination Committee of the Second National People's Congress held its first meeting this morning.

The meeting was chaired by Zhang Youyu, the chairman of the committee, and adopted the work plan of the Proposal Examination Committee. The plan emphasized that each proposal must be carefully analyzed and studied specifically, and full attention must be paid to the opinions of the original proposal. Based on needs and possibilities, realistic examination opinions should be put forward.

The meeting elected Li Zhucen and Guan Wenwei as vice chairmen of the Proposal Examination Committee.

The meeting also determined that the Proposal Examination Committee would establish five professional review groups: industry, agriculture, finance and trade, culture and education, and comprehensive. The proposals of the congress will first be reviewed by each professional group, and preliminary review opinions will be put forward, and then the committee will discuss and decide. Today's meeting has divided these professional review groups and elected the conveners of each group.

### 人大提案审查委员会举行首次会议

1960-04-01
第1版()
专栏：

    人大提案审查委员会举行首次会议
    新华社31日讯　第二届全国人民代表大会第二次会议提案审查委员会，今天上午举行了第一次会议。
    会议由张友渔主任委员主持，通过了提案审查委员会工作方案。方案中特别强调地指出，对于审查每一件提案，都必须认真地进行具体分析和研究，充分重视原提案的意见，根据需要和可能，实事求是地提出审查意见。
    会议推定李烛尘、管文蔚为提案审查委员会副主任委员。
    会议还确定，提案审查委员会分设工业、农业、财贸、文教、综合五个专业审查组。大会提案先由各专业组审查，提出初步审查意见，再由委员会讨论决定。今天会议已经划分了这些专业审查组，并且推定了各组的召集人。
