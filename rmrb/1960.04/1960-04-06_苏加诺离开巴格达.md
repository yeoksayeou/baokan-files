Title: Sukarno Leaves Baghdad
Author: 
Date: 1960-04-06
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, Baghdad, April 5th: Indonesian President Sukarno left Baghdad this morning for Yugoslavia after concluding a three-day visit to Iraq.

The joint communique signed by President Sukarno and Prime Minister Kassim reaffirmed that the governments of both countries support the "flourishing Asian and African struggle against colonialism."

The communique strongly condemned French imperialism for continuing the war in Algeria, and condemned the South African Federal Government's policy of racial discrimination and repression of African residents.

### 苏加诺离开巴格达

1960-04-06
第6版()
专栏：

    苏加诺离开巴格达
    新华社巴格达5日电　印度尼西亚总统苏加诺在结束了对伊拉克的三天访问以后，今天上午离开巴格达前往南斯拉夫访问。
    苏加诺总统和卡塞姆总理签署的联合公报重申，两国政府支持“亚洲和非洲的蓬勃发展的反对殖民主义的斗争”。
    公报强烈谴责法帝国主义继续在阿尔及利亚进行战争，并且谴责南非联邦政府的种族歧视政策和对非洲居民的镇压。
