Title: U.S. Military Aircraft Intrudes into Our Airspace; I Issue the Ninety-Fourth Serious Warning
Author: 
Date: 1960-04-29
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, April 28th: On April 28th, from 12:03 to 12:13, a U.S. Navy patrol plane intruded into the airspace over Yongxing Island and Shidao Island in the Xisha Islands of Guangdong Province. Regarding this military provocation by U.S. military aircraft, a spokesperson for our Ministry of Foreign Affairs was instructed to issue the ninety-fourth serious warning.

### 美军用飞机侵入我领空  我提出第九十四次严重警告

1960-04-29
第6版()
专栏：

    美军用飞机侵入我领空
    我提出第九十四次严重警告
    新华社28日讯　4月28日十二时零三分到十二时十三分，美国海军巡逻机一架侵入我广东省西沙群岛的永兴岛、石岛上空。对于美国军用飞机的这种军事挑衅，我外交部发言人奉命提出第九十四次严重警告。
