Title: All-China Youth Federation and All-China Students' Federation Send Telegrams of Support for "Africa Day": The African People Are Fearless New Forces; Imperialism is a Paper Tiger, Strong in Appearance but Weak in Reality

Author:
Date: 1960-04-16
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, April 15th: The All-China Youth Federation and the All-China Students' Federation today sent a telegram to the Afro-Asian People's Solidarity Organization, extending heartfelt respect, on the occasion of the "Africa Day" held at the conference, to the African people and youth who are opposing imperialism and striving for national independence.

The telegram from the All-China Youth Federation and the All-China Students' Federation stated: "The African people's struggle against imperialism and colonialism has become a prairie fire, burning the imperialist colonial system to death in a raging inferno. Imperialism is a paper tiger, strong in appearance but weak in reality, while the awakened African people are fearless new forces. Chinese youth are elated and encouraged by the achievements of the African people and youth and will always support the just struggle of the African people and youth. We wish the united and persevering African people and youth new victories on the road from victory."

### 我青联学联致电支持“非洲日”  非洲人民是无畏的新生力量  帝国主义是外强中干的纸老虎

1960-04-16
第6版()
专栏：

    我青联学联致电支持“非洲日”
    非洲人民是无畏的新生力量
    帝国主义是外强中干的纸老虎
    新华社15日讯　中华全国青年联合会和中华全国学生联合会今天致电亚非人民团结大会，在大会举行“非洲日”的时候，代表中国青年向反对帝国主义、争取民族独立的非洲人民和青年致衷心的敬意。
    全国青联和全国学联的电报说：“非洲人民反对帝国主义和殖民主义的斗争已如烈火燎原，正把帝国主义的殖民制度在熊熊怒火中烧死。帝国主义是外强中干的纸老虎，而觉醒了的非洲人民则是无畏的新生力量。中国青年为非洲人民和青年的成就感到欢欣鼓舞，永远支持非洲人民和青年的正义斗争。祝团结一致、坚持斗争的非洲人民和青年从胜利走向新的胜利。”
