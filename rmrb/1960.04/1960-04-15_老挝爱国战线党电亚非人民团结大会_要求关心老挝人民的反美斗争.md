Title: Lao Patriotic Front Sends Telegram to Asian-African People's Solidarity Conference, Demanding Attention to the Lao People's Anti-American Struggle
Author:
Date: 1960-04-15
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, Hanoi, April 13th: The Central Committee of the Lao Patriotic Front recently sent a telegram to the Second Asian-African People's Solidarity Conference, congratulating the conference on its brilliant success.

    The telegram, published in the April 8th edition of "Lao Patriotic News," stated that the Lao people's anti-American struggle is part of the magnificent movement of Asian and African nations against imperialism. The Central Committee of the Lao Patriotic Front hopes that the conference will actively support the Lao Patriotic Front's sincere desire for peace and pay attention to the Lao people's arduous struggle against U.S. imperialism. The telegram said that the Geneva Conference held in 1954 brought peace to Laos. Six years have passed since then, but the Lao people still have to wage a protracted struggle against the U.S. imperialist conspiracy to turn Laos into its new colony and war base in Southeast Asia.

### 老挝爱国战线党电亚非人民团结大会  要求关心老挝人民的反美斗争

1960-04-15
第5版()
专栏：

    老挝爱国战线党电亚非人民团结大会
    要求关心老挝人民的反美斗争
    新华社河内13日电　老挝爱国战线党中央委员会最近打电报给第二届亚非人民团结大会，祝贺大会取得辉煌胜利。
    这份刊载在4月8日出版的“老挝爱国新闻”上的电报说，老挝人民的反美斗争，是亚非各民族反对帝国主义这一波澜壮阔的运动中的一部分。老挝爱国战线党中央委员会希望大会将积极地支持老挝爱国战线党的和平诚意，并且关心老挝人民反对美帝国主义的艰苦斗争。电报说，1954年举行的日内瓦会议给老挝带来了和平，从那时起已经六年了，但是老挝人民仍然不得不进行长期的斗争，来反对美帝国主义企图把老挝变成它在东南亚的新的殖民地和战争基地的阴谋。
