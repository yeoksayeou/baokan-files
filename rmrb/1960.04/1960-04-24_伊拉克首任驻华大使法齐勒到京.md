Title: First Iraqi Ambassador to China, Fazil, Arrives in Beijing
Author: 
Date: 1960-04-24
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, April 23rd: The first Ambassador Extraordinary and Plenipotentiary of the Republic of Iraq to China, Mr. Abdul-Haq Fazil, arrived in Beijing by train this afternoon.

Yu Peiwen, Director of the Protocol Department of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs, and Gong Dafei, Deputy Director of the West Asia and Africa Department, were at the station to welcome him.

### 伊拉克首任驻华大使法齐勒到京

1960-04-24
第1版()
专栏：

    伊拉克首任驻华大使法齐勒到京
    新华社23日讯　伊拉克共和国首任驻华特命全权大使阿卜杜勒·哈格·法齐勒先生，在今天下午乘火车到达北京。
    前往车站欢迎的有外交部礼宾司司长俞沛文，西亚非洲司副司长宫达非。
