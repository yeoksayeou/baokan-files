Title: The Presidium of the Burma National United Front Issues a Statement Warmly Welcoming Premier Zhou's Visit to Burma
Author:
Date: 1960-04-13
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, Yangon, April 12th: The Presidium of the Burma National United Front issued a statement on the 11th, warmly welcoming Premier Zhou Enlai's upcoming visit to Burma. The statement said that Premier Zhou Enlai's visit to Burma will strengthen Sino-Burmese friendship and help further strengthen world peace.
    The Burma National United Front also expressed its hope that the Sino-Indian border issue could be resolved on a friendly basis.

### 缅甸民族团结阵线主席团发表声明  热烈欢迎周总理访问缅甸

1960-04-13
第5版()
专栏：

    缅甸民族团结阵线主席团发表声明
    热烈欢迎周总理访问缅甸
    新华社仰光12日电　缅甸民族团结阵线主席团11日发表声明，对周恩来总理即将访问缅甸表示热烈欢迎。声明说，周恩来总理对缅甸的访问将加强中缅友谊，并有助于进一步加强世界和平。
    缅甸民族团结阵线还表示希望，中印边界问题有可能在友好的基础上得到解决。
