Author:
Date: 1960-04-02
Page: 5

    Sino-Soviet Friendship Association Sends Telegrams to Popova and Andreyev
    Expressing Respect to Four Soviet Heroes
    Xinhua News Agency, April 1st — The Sino-Soviet Friendship Association today telegraphed Popova, Chairman of the Council of the Union of Societies for Friendship and Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries of the Soviet Union, and Andreyev, Chairman of the Council of the Soviet-Chinese Friendship Association, expressing sincere respect to the four Soviet soldiers—Tsyganshin, Kryuchkovsky, Poplavsky, and Fedotov—who fought against the raging storms on the Pacific Ocean and tenaciously persisted for forty-nine days. The telegram stated: "The struggle of these four Soviet soldiers against nature under extremely difficult conditions vividly demonstrates the high revolutionary optimism and heroic and tenacious fighting will of the Soviet people. This rare heroic miracle in the world most powerfully proves the excellent qualities of the Soviet people cultivated and grown up under the leadership of the Communist Party of the Soviet Union and under the socialist system. When these four heroic Soviet soldiers victoriously return to Moscow, please convey our sincere respect on our behalf!"

### 中苏友协电波波娃和安德烈耶夫  向四位苏联英雄表示敬意

1960-04-02
第5版()
专栏：

    中苏友协电波波娃和安德烈耶夫
    向四位苏联英雄表示敬意
    新华社1日讯　中苏友好协会总会今天致电苏联对外友好和文化协会联合会理事会主席波波娃和苏中友好协会理事会主席安德烈耶夫，对在太平洋上与狂风暴浪搏斗，顽强坚持了四十九天的四位苏联士兵——齐甘申、克留奇科夫斯基、波普拉夫斯基和费多托夫，表示真诚的敬意。电报说：“这四位苏联士兵在极端艰苦的条件下同自然所进行的搏斗，生动地表现了苏联人的高度革命乐观主义精神和英勇顽强的战斗意志。这种世界上少有的英雄奇迹，最有力地证明了在苏联共产党领导下和在社会主义制度下培养和成长起来的苏联人的优良品质。当这四位英雄的苏联士兵胜利回到莫斯科的时候，请你们代为转达我们的真诚的敬意！”
