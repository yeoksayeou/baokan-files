Title: Polish Military Delegation Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1960-04-03
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, April 2nd. At the invitation of the Ministry of National Defense of our country, the military delegation of the People's Republic of Poland, led by Vice Minister of National Defense General Bozhivovsky, arrived in Beijing this afternoon for a friendly visit to our country.

The Polish military delegation headed by General Bozhivovsky consists of nine people. The members are Lieutenant General Korchinsky, Lieutenant General Valishak, Major General Jie Linsky, Major General Stuginsky, Major General Rachkovsky, Major General Funkiewicz, Colonel Shidlovsky, and Colonel Hoja. In addition, there are four entourage members.

At 3 p.m. today, the Tu-104 aircraft carrying the Polish military delegation landed at the Capital Airport. When General Bozhivovsky and others stepped off the plane, they were warmly welcomed.

Our Deputy Minister of National Defense General Tan Zheng, General Wang Shusheng, Deputy Chief of General Staff of the Chinese People's Liberation Army General Peng Shaohui, General Zhang Aiping, and others shook hands cordially with the delegation members. Members of the song and dance troupes of the Army, Navy and Air Force presented flowers to General Bozhivovsky and all members of the delegation.

Also present at the airport to welcome the delegation today were senior generals such as General Yang Yong, Lieutenant General Liu Zhijian, Lieutenant General Xiao Xiangrong, Lieutenant General Qiu Huizuo, Lieutenant General Liu Shaowen, Vice Admiral Zhao Qimin, Lieutenant General Cheng Shicai, Air Force Major General Li Jikai, and officers from the Army, Navy and Air Force stationed in Beijing.

Furato, Charge d'Affaires ad interim of the People's Republic of Poland in our country, personnel from the embassy, and military attaches of various countries in China also came to the airport to welcome the delegation.

### 波兰军事代表团到京

1960-04-03
第4版()
专栏：

    波兰军事代表团到京
    新华社2日讯　应我国国防部邀请来我国作友好访问的波兰人民共和国军事代表团，在国防部副部长博齐沃夫斯基上将率领下，于今天下午到达北京。
    以博齐沃夫斯基上将为首的波兰军事代表团，共由九人组成。团员有科尔钦斯基中将、瓦里沙克中将、节林斯基少将、斯图今斯基少将、拉奇科夫斯基少将、丰克维奇少将、希德沃夫斯基上校和霍哈上校。此外还有随员四人。
    今天下午三点，波兰军事代表团乘坐的图—104飞机在首都机场降落。当博齐沃夫斯基上将等走下飞机时，受到了热烈的欢迎。
    我国防部副部长谭政大将、王树声大将，中国人民解放军副总参谋长彭绍辉上将、张爱萍上将等与代表团人员一一亲切握手。陆海空军文工团的团员们，向博齐沃夫斯基上将及代表团全体人员献了花。
    今天到机场欢迎的还有杨勇上将、刘志坚中将、萧向荣中将、丘会作中将、刘少文中将、赵启民海军中将、程世才中将、李继开空军少将等高级将领，以及中国人民解放军驻京陆海空军军官。
    波兰人民共和国驻我国临时代办傅拉托和大使馆人员，以及各国驻华武官也到机场欢迎。
