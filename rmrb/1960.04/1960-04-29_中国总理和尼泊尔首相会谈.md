Title: Chinese Premier and Nepalese Prime Minister Hold Talks
Author:
Date: 1960-04-29
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Pokhara, April 27th: Premier of the State Council of the People's Republic of China, Zhou Enlai, and Prime Minister of the Kingdom of Nepal, B.P. Koirala, began formal talks at 16:20 today at Ba Palace in Pokhara. The palace is located on a beautiful lakeside, with the snow-capped Himalayas in the background, a very scenic location. At 18:20, Premier Zhou and Prime Minister Koirala concluded their first meeting. Afterwards, they boarded a yacht composed of two dugout canoes and toured the lake for about half an hour. Chinese and Nepalese national flags and a red flag with the word "Welcome" written on it were raised on the mast.

Xinhua News Agency, Kathmandu, April 28th: Premier Zhou Enlai, accompanied by Nepalese Prime Minister B.P. Koirala, returned to Kathmandu from Pokhara by plane this morning.

Premier Zhou Enlai and Prime Minister Koirala held a second meeting last night.

Xinhua News Agency, Kathmandu, April 28th: Nepalese Prime Minister B.P. Koirala hosted a luncheon today in honor of Premier Zhou Enlai. Chinese Vice Premier and Foreign Minister Chen Yi, Vice Foreign Minister Zhang Hanfu, all other members of the Chinese Premier's entourage, Chinese Ambassador to Nepal Pan Zili, and the head of the Chinese expert group attended the banquet. Among the four hundred people attending the banquet were Nepalese cabinet ministers, deputy ministers and other senior officials, the Chief Justice of the Supreme Court, the speakers and members of both houses of the Nepalese Parliament, the Commander-in-Chief of the Nepalese Army and other senior officers, the Chairman of the Kathmandu Municipal Committee, and diplomatic envoys from various countries stationed in Kathmandu.

The luncheon proceeded in a friendly atmosphere. During the luncheon, the band played a Chinese folk song praising Chairman Mao Zedong.

### 中国总理和尼泊尔首相会谈

1960-04-29
第1版()
专栏：

    中国总理和尼泊尔首相会谈
    新华社博克拉27日电　中华人民共和国国务院总理周恩来和尼泊尔王国首相柯伊拉腊今天十六时二十分在博克拉的宝宫开始正式会谈。这座宫殿座落在一个美丽的湖滨，背后是白雪复盖的喜马拉雅山，风景十分优美。十八点二十分，周总理和柯伊拉腊首相结束了他们第一次会谈。然后，他们乘坐一条由两只独木船组成的游艇，泛舟湖上，游览了约半小时。船桅上飘起中国和尼泊尔两国国旗和一面写着“欢迎”字样的红旗。
    新华社加德满都28日电　周恩来总理今天上午在尼泊尔首相毕·普·柯伊拉腊陪同下从博克拉乘飞机回到了加德满都。
    周恩来总理和柯伊拉腊首相在昨天晚上曾经举行了第二次会谈。
    新华社加德满都28日电　尼泊尔首相毕·普·柯伊拉腊今天举行午宴，招待周恩来总理。中国副总理兼外交部长陈毅，外交部副部长章汉夫，中国总理的所有其他随行人员，中国驻尼泊尔大使潘自力和中国专家组组长出席了宴会。在出席宴会的四百人中，有尼泊尔政府的内阁大臣们，副大臣们和其他高级官员们，最高法院首席法官，尼泊尔议会两院的议长和议员们，尼泊尔陆军总司令和其他的高级军官，加德满都市政委员会主席和各国驻加德满都的外交使节。
    午宴是在友好的气氛中进行的。在午宴进行时，乐队演奏了一曲赞美毛泽东主席的中国民歌。
