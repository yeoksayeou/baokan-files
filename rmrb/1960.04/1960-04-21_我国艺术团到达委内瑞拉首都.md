Title: Chinese Art Troupe Arrives in Venezuelan Capital
Author:
Date: 1960-04-21
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, Caracas, April 18th: The second group of the Chinese Art Troupe, headed by Chen Zhongjing, arrived in Caracas on the 17th.

Those who went to Maiquetía Airport to welcome them included members of the Executive Committee of the Venezuela-China Friendship Association and Dr. Roberto Gabaldón Márquez, a leading member of the National Agricultural Commission for implementing land reform, Dr. Octavio Cabello, a councilor of the Federal District, music professor Antonio Estévez, poet Carlos Augusto León, Congressman Héctor Rodríguez Bauza, and theater manager César Rengifo. The welcomers presented the female artists of the art troupe with red camellias.

The first group of the Chinese Art Troupe arrived in the Venezuelan capital on the 16th.

### 我国艺术团到达委内瑞拉首都

1960-04-21
第6版()
专栏：

    我国艺术团到达委内瑞拉首都
    据新华社加拉加斯18日电　在团长陈忠经率领下的中国艺术团第二批人员已在17日到达加拉加斯。
    前往迈凯蒂亚机场欢迎的有委内瑞拉中国之友协会执行委员和执行土地改革的全国农业委员会的领导成员罗伯托·加瓦耳东·马克斯博士，联邦区市议员奥克塔维奥·卡维略博士，音乐教授安东尼奥·埃斯特梅斯，诗人卡洛斯·奥古斯托·莱昂，众议员埃克托·罗德里格斯·包萨以及剧院经理塞萨·伦希福等人。欢迎者向艺术团的女艺术家献上了红山茶花。
    中国艺术团的第一批成员是在16日到达委内瑞拉首都的。
