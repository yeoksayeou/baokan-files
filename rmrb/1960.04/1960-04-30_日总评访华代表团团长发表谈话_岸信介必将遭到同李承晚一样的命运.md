Title: Head of the Japanese General Council of Trade Unions (Sohyo) Delegation to China Issues Statement: Kishi Nobusuke is Destined to Meet the Same Fate as Syngman Rhee
Author:
Date: 1960-04-30
Page: 7
Xinhua News Agency, April 29th. The head of the Japanese General Council of Trade Unions (Sohyo) delegation to China, Nobuji Hagiwara, stated in Beijing today that the patriotic and just struggle of the South Korean people has had a tremendous impact in Japan, and will further promote the Japanese people's struggle against the Japan-U.S. "Security Treaty," triggering an even greater climax.
　　At a press conference held by the delegation, Nobuji Hagiwara said that South Korean tyrant Syngman Rhee was overthrown by the heroic struggle of the South Korean people, "We are certain that in the near future, the Kishi Nobusuke cabinet will meet the very same fate." Deputy head of the delegation, Masao Komori said: "The struggle of the South Korean people has strengthened the courage of the Japanese workers to fight." The secretary-general of the delegation, Shunichi Yamazaki, said: The struggle of the South Korean people has given the Japanese people great encouragement, "We must resolutely smash the Japan-U.S. 'Security Treaty'."
　　In his speech, Nobuji Hagiwara introduced the situation of the Japanese workers and people's struggle against the Kishi Nobusuke group's attempt to have the Japanese Diet pass the Japan-U.S. "Security Treaty." Nobuji Hagiwara also said: "The abolition of the Japan-U.S. 'Security Treaty' and the restoration of diplomatic relations between Japan and China are inseparable." In his speech, he warmly wished for the further enhancement of the friendship between the people of Japan and China.
    Xinhua News Agency, April 29th. The Japanese General Council of Trade Unions (Sohyo) delegation to China, led by Nobuji Hagiwara, Executive Committee Chairman of the Tokyo Transportation Workers' Union, arrived in Beijing by plane today. They were invited by the All-China Federation of Trade Unions to participate in the "May Day" International Labor Day celebrations and to visit China.

### 日总评访华代表团团长发表谈话  岸信介必将遭到同李承晚一样的命运

1960-04-30
第7版()
专栏：

    日总评访华代表团团长发表谈话
    岸信介必将遭到同李承晚一样的命运
    新华社29日讯　日本工会总评议会访华代表团团长萩原信治今天在北京发表谈话说，南朝鲜人民的爱国正义斗争在日本发生了极大影响，它将进一步促进日本人民反对日美“安全条约”的斗争，掀起更大的高潮。
    萩原信治在代表团举行的记者招待会上说，南朝鲜暴君李承晚在南朝鲜人民的英勇斗争下被赶下了台，“我们确信，不久将来，岸信介内阁必将遭到如此同样的命运。”代表团副团长小森正夫说：“南朝鲜人民的斗争增强了日本工人的斗争勇气。”代表团事务局长山崎俊一说：南朝鲜人民的斗争给了日本人民很大的鼓励，“我们要坚决粉碎日美‘安全条约’”。
    萩原信治在谈话中介绍了日本工人和人民反对岸信介集团企图使日本国会通过日美“安全条约”的斗争情况。萩原信治还说：“废除日美‘安全条约’与恢复日中邦交是不可分割的。”他在谈话中热烈祝日中两国人民之间友谊的进一步增进。
    新华社29日讯　以东京交通工会执行委员长萩原信治为首的日本总评访华代表团一行十一人，在今天乘飞机到达北京。他们是应中华全国总工会邀请前来参加“五一”国际劳动节庆祝活动和访问的。
