Title: Khrushchev Arrives in Baku to Participate in the Glorious Festival of the Azerbaijani People
Author:
Date: 1960-04-26
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, April 25th: According to a TASS report from Baku, Nikita Khrushchev, First Secretary of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of the Soviet Union and Chairman of the Council of Ministers of the Soviet Union, arrived in Baku on the 24th to participate in the celebrations of two glorious festivals of the Azerbaijani people: the 40th anniversary of the establishment of Soviet power in Azerbaijan and the founding of the Communist Party.

Gafarov, Chairman of the Presidium of the Supreme Soviet of Azerbaijan, and others welcomed Khrushchev at the station. Akhundov, First Secretary of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of Azerbaijan, and Iskenderov, Chairman of the Council of Ministers of Azerbaijan, who greeted Khrushchev en route, arrived with Khrushchev in the same car.

### 参加阿塞拜疆人民的光荣节日  赫鲁晓夫到达巴库

1960-04-26
第5版()
专栏：

    参加阿塞拜疆人民的光荣节日
    赫鲁晓夫到达巴库
    新华社莫斯科25日电　据塔斯社巴库消息，苏联共产党中央委员会第一书记、苏联部长会议主席赫鲁晓夫24日抵达巴库，前来参加阿塞拜疆人民的两个光荣节日的庆典：阿塞拜疆苏维埃政权建立和共产党成立四十周年。
    到车站欢迎赫鲁晓夫的，有阿塞拜疆最高苏维埃主席团主席加法罗夫等人。前往途中迎接赫鲁晓夫的阿塞拜疆共产党中央第一书记阿洪多夫和阿塞拜疆部长会议主席伊斯肯杰罗夫与赫鲁晓夫同车到达。
