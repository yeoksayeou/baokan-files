Title: Cambodia Forms New Cabinet
Author: 
Date: 1960-04-20
Page: 7

Xinhua News Agency, Phnom Penh, April 18th: The Cambodian National Assembly approved the new Cambodian cabinet headed by Pho Proeung (formerly translated as Fu·Bolun) tonight, with 37 votes in favor, 1 vote against, and 9 abstentions.

Pho Proeung, the new cabinet's Prime Minister and Minister of Interior and Religious Affairs, said in the National Assembly that his cabinet would adhere to all policies and plans of the former Sihanouk cabinet.

### 柬埔寨组成新内阁

1960-04-20
第7版()
专栏：

    柬埔寨组成新内阁
    据新华社金边18日电　柬埔寨国民议会今晚通过了以福·波伦（前译伏波伦）为首的柬埔寨新内阁，票数是三十七票对一票，九票弃权。
    新内阁的首相兼内政和宗教大臣福·波伦在国民议会上说，他的内阁将奉行前西哈努克内阁的一切政策和计划。
