Title: Mongolian Great People's Hural Delegation Leaves Beijing for Wuhan for Visit

Author: 
Date: 1960-04-11
Page: 7

Xinhua News Agency, April 10th. The delegation of the Great People's Hural of the Mongolian People's Republic, headed by Bayanlegyin Jargalsaikhan, Chairman of the Great People's Hural, accompanied by Zhang Qilong, member of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress, and Lian Guan, Deputy Secretary-General, left Beijing by special plane this morning for a visit to Wuhan.

The Mongolian Ambassador to China, Sharav, accompanied the delegation.

### 蒙古大人民呼拉尔代表团  离京赴武汉参观访问

1960-04-11
第7版()
专栏：

    蒙古大人民呼拉尔代表团
    离京赴武汉参观访问
    新华社10日讯　以大人民呼拉尔主席巴扬勒·贾尔卡赛汗为首的蒙古人民共和国大人民呼拉尔代表团，由全国人民代表大会常务委员会委员张启龙、副秘书长连贯陪同，今天上午乘专机离开北京，前往武汉参观访问。
    蒙古驻中国大使沙拉布随同代表团前往。
