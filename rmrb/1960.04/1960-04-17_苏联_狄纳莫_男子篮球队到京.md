Title: Soviet "Dynamo" Men's Basketball Team Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1960-04-17
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, 16th April. The Soviet "Dynamo" Voluntary Sports Association men's basketball team, a group of fifteen, arrived in Beijing by plane today to participate in the first men's basketball competition of the socialist countries' Public Security Sports Organizations. Observers from the German Democratic Republic's "Dynamo" Sports Association, Mann Kirst and Wo Herschel, also arrived on the same flight. They were warmly welcomed at the airport by Ren Meibin, Vice Chairman of the National Committee of the Chinese "Pioneer" Sports Association, and members of the Chinese "Pioneer" Sports Association men's basketball team.

Hanzabu Pagde, the leader of the Mongolian People's Republic Public Security men's basketball team, who arrived in Beijing earlier, also went to the airport to greet them.

The Soviet "Dynamo" men's basketball team, which has two players over two meters tall, is composed of excellent players from the Moscow "Dynamo" men's basketball team, which has twice won third place in the All-Soviet Basketball Championship. The average height of the twelve players on the team is 1.90 meters; coach Kolpakov served as the coach of the Soviet National Youth Men's Basketball Team that visited our country last year. Thirty-two-year-old Meritorious Athlete Ozerov (number 9) was selected as a national player for ten consecutive years, participated in the Olympic basketball competition twice, and also visited our country in 1950 as a member of the Soviet National Men's Basketball Team. There are also six Master Athletes in the visiting team, including 1.98-meter-tall Kor-Yev (number 3), who participated in the World Men's Basketball Championship last year. The two players on the visiting team who are over two meters tall are: 2.09-meter-tall Petrov (number 13) and 2.02-meter-tall Balabanov (number 10).

### 苏联“狄纳莫”男子篮球队到京

1960-04-17
第4版()
专栏：

    苏联“狄纳莫”男子篮球队到京
    新华社16日讯　参加社会主义国家公安体育组织第一届男子篮球比赛的苏联“狄纳莫”志愿体育协会男子篮球队一行十五人，今天乘飞机到达北京。德意志民主共和国“狄纳莫”体育协会的观察员曼·基尔斯特和沃·赫歇尔，也乘同机到达。他们在飞机场受到中国“前卫”体育协会全国委员会副主任任梅彬和中国“前卫”体育协会男子篮球队队员们的热烈欢迎。
    先期到达北京的蒙古人民共和国公安男子篮球队领队汉扎布·帕格德也到机场迎接。
    拥有两名身高两米以上队员的苏联“狄纳莫”男子篮球队，是由曾经两次获得全苏篮球锦标赛第三名的莫斯科“狄纳莫”男子篮球队的优秀队员组成。全队十二名运动员的平均身高是一米九十；教练科尔帕科夫去年曾经担任过来我国访问的苏联国家青年男子篮球队的教练。三十二岁的功勋运动员奥泽洛夫（九号）曾经接连十年当选为国家选手、两次参加奥林匹克运动会的篮球比赛，1950年也曾经作为苏联国家男子篮球队的队员访问过我国。客队中还有六名运动健将，其中身高一米九十八的科尔?耶夫（三号）去年曾经参加过世界男子篮球锦标赛。客队两名身高超过两米的队员是：身高两米零九的彼得罗夫（十三号）和身高两米零二的巴拉巴诺夫（十号）。
