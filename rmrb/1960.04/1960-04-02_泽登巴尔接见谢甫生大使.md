Title: Tsedenbal Receives Ambassador Xie Fusheng
Author:
Date: 1960-04-02
Page: 5

    Xinhua News Agency, Ulaanbaatar, March 31st: Tsedenbal, First Secretary of the Central Committee of the Mongolian People's Revolutionary Party and Chairman of the Council of Ministers, received Xie Fusheng, the Chinese Ambassador to Mongolia, this afternoon.
    Present at the reception were Molomjamts, First Deputy Chairman of the Mongolian Council of Ministers, and Tseveen, Vice Chairman of the State Planning Committee.

### 泽登巴尔接见谢甫生大使

1960-04-02
第5版()
专栏：

    泽登巴尔接见谢甫生大使
    新华社乌兰巴托31日电　蒙古人民革命党中央委员会第一书记、部长会议主席泽登巴尔，今天下午接见了中国驻蒙古大使谢甫生。
    接见时在座的有蒙古部长会议第一副主席莫洛姆扎木茨，国家计划委员会副主席策文。
