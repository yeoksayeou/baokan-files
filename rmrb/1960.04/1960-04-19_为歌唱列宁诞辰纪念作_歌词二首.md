Compositions for Singing in Commemoration of Lenin's Birthday
——Two Songs by Tian Jian

I
The Volga's waves surge,
Lenin was born in the storm,
Like a petrel charging into the raging waves,
Like an eagle spreading its wings and soaring—
Lenin and his class brothers,
Opened the first dawn in the world.
The successor of Marx,
Is the great Lenin,
He used the seeds of revolution,
To awaken the oppressed people,
To shatter the chains of the old world,
And build a new world at an early date.

II
The universe is like a small boat,
Lenin is the helmsman,
The red flag is the sail on the boat,
Sailing through the four oceans and five continents,
The old world has collapsed,
The new world rests on our shoulders.
The Earth, the Moon, the planets,
Follow the working class,
Lenin and us together,
Ascend to the first port,
When he bid farewell to us he said:
Continue to move forward, don't stop!
The masters of the new world,
Are our working people.
The thrones of all tyrants,
Are collapsing one after another,
The doomsday of imperialism,
Will also arrive soon.
The radiance of Mao Zedong Thought,
Shines upon our people's communes,
Hold high the battle flag of Marxism-Leninism,
Workers of all countries, unite!

### 为歌唱列宁诞辰纪念作——歌词二首
田间
1960-04-19
第8版()
专栏：

    为歌唱列宁诞辰纪念作
    ——歌词二首	田间	一
    伏尔加波涛滚滚，
    列宁在风暴中诞生，
    像海燕冲进怒涛，
    像雄鹰展翅飞腾——
    列宁和阶级弟兄，
    打开世界第一个黎明。
    马克思的继承者，
    就是伟大的列宁，
    他用革命的火种，
    唤起被压迫的人们，
    要粉碎旧世界的锁链，
    把新世界早日建成。	二
    宇宙像一叶扁舟，
    列宁是一位舵手，
    红旗就是船上帆，
    穿过四洋五大洲，
    旧世界已经崩溃，
    新世界担在我们肩头。
    地球、月球、星球，
    跟着工人阶级走，
    列宁和我们一起，
    登上第一座港口，
    他和我们永别时说：
    要继续前进别停留！
    新世界的主人，
    是我们劳动人民。
    一切暴君的王座，
    正在纷纷崩溃，
    帝国主义的末日，
    不久也就要来临。
    毛泽东思想的光辉，
    照耀着我们人民公社，
    高举马列主义的战旗，
    全世界无产者联合起来！（附图片）
    列宁（木刻）黄永玉
