Title: Promote Asian-African Solidarity, Safeguard National Independence: Asia-Pacific Peace Conference Sends Telegram Congratulating the Second Asian-African People's Solidarity Conference
Author:
Date: 1960-04-12
Page: 6
Xinhua News Agency, April 11th: The Secretariat of the Asian and Pacific Regions Peace Liaison Committee today sent a telegram to the Second Asian-African People's Solidarity Conference held in Conakry, Guinea, stating that the convening of this conference marks the unprecedented upsurge of the current Asian-African national independence movement and the close unity of the Asian and African people in the common struggle against imperialism, colonialism, and in defense of world peace. The Asian and Pacific Regions Peace Liaison Committee expresses warm support for this conference.

The congratulatory telegram from the Asian and Pacific Regions Peace Liaison Committee said: "The heroic struggles of the Asian and African people to win and safeguard national independence have also powerfully supported the national democratic movements of the Latin American people and the struggles of the people in the Pacific and other parts of the world to defend peace. We are deeply convinced that this conference will make even more important contributions to further promoting the friendly unity of the Asian and African people, the struggle to win and safeguard national independence, and the common cause of defending world peace."

### 促进亚非团结  维护民族独立  亚太和会电贺第二届亚非人民团结大会

1960-04-12
第6版()
专栏：

    促进亚非团结  维护民族独立
    亚太和会电贺第二届亚非人民团结大会
    新华社11日讯　亚洲及太平洋区域和平联络委员会秘书处今天打电报给在几内亚科纳克里举行的第二届亚非人民团结大会说，这一次会议的召开，标志着当前亚非民族独立运动的空前高涨和亚非人民在反对帝国主义、殖民主义、保卫世界和平的共同斗争中的紧密团结。亚洲及太平洋区域和平联络委员会对这次会议表示热烈支持。
    亚洲及太平洋区域和平联络委员会的贺电说：“亚非各国人民争取和维护民族独立的英勇斗争也有力地支持了拉丁美洲人民的民族民主运动以及太平洋和世界其他地区人民保卫和平的斗争。我们深信，这届大会将对进一步促进亚洲、非洲人民的友好团结、争取和维护民族独立的斗争、以及保卫世界和平的共同事业作出更重要的贡献。”
