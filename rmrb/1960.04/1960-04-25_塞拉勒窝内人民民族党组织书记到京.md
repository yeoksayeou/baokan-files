Title: Organizing Secretary of the Sierra Leone People's National Party Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1960-04-25
Page: 2

Xinhua News Agency, April 24th: Mohammed Bash-Taqi, Organizing Secretary of the Sierra Leone People's National Party, arrived in Beijing by plane today at the invitation of the Chinese Committee for Afro-Asian Solidarity.

### 塞拉勒窝内人民民族党组织书记到京

1960-04-25
第2版()
专栏：

    塞拉勒窝内人民民族党组织书记到京
    新华社24日讯　塞拉勒窝内人民民族党组织书记穆罕默德·巴什·塔吉，应中国亚非团结委员会邀请，今天乘飞机到达北京。
