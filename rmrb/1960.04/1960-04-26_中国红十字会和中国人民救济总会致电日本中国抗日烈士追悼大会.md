Title: The Red Cross Society of China and the Red Relief Society of China Send a Telegram to the Memorial Service for Chinese Anti-Japanese Martyrs in Japan
Author:
Date: 1960-04-26
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, April 25th: The Red Cross Society of China and the Red Relief Society of China today jointly sent a telegram to all circles in Japan, regarding the memorial service for Chinese anti-Japanese martyrs ("National Grand Memorial Service for Chinese Prisoner-of-War Martyrs") to be held in Tokyo on the 26th, expressing their gratitude to the Japanese people.
    The telegram said: "In the struggle against the Japan-U.S. military treaty, the Japanese people have compiled a list of our anti-Japanese martyrs who sacrificed their lives in Japan and are holding a memorial service. This once again expresses the Japanese people's desire to oppose war and be friendly with the Chinese people. The Chinese people are willing to join the Japanese people in resolutely opposing the revival of Japanese militarism and defending peace, in order to comfort the Chinese martyrs."

### 中国红十字会和中国人民救济总会致电日本中国抗日烈士追悼大会

1960-04-26
第6版()
专栏：

    中国红十字会和中国人民救济总会致电日本中国抗日烈士追悼大会
    新华社25日讯　中国红十字会总会，中国人民救济总会今天联名打电报给日本各界，26日将在东京举行的中国抗日烈士追悼大会（“中国人俘虏殉难者国民大慰灵祭”），对日本各界人民表示感谢。
    电报说：“日本人民在反对日美军事条约的斗争中编制了在日本牺牲的我抗日烈士名单，并举行追悼会。这又一次表达了日本人民反对战争，愿和中国人民友好的愿望。中国人民愿和日本人民一起坚决反对日本军国主义的复活，保卫和平，以告慰中国烈士。”
