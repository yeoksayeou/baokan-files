Title: Ah, the Streets of Beijing
Author: Li Xue'ao
Date: 1960-04-12
Page: 8

    Ah, the streets of Beijing!
    I praise your rapid changes,
    Your brand new look!
    I praise you,
    Star-like factories—
    Shining on every street,
    Pouring out hundreds of products,
    Every single one so glorious.
    Adding a close younger brother to the big factories;
    Adding even more beautiful colors to the colorful shops!
    I praise you,
    The bright and clean canteens—
    The food is so well-made!
    Everyone eats with satisfaction,
    Increasing their energy a lot.
    Every face,
    Smiling all day long!
    I praise you,
    The little babies in the nurseries—
    Little birds in red cradles,
    Growing up in happiness,
    Jumping joyfully in the sunshine,
    Although just babbling,
    They can sing loudly:
    The People's Commune is good!
    The People's Commune is good!
    I praise you,
    The "Always Available" service stations—
    Serving the residents so thoughtfully,
    Washing clothes, sewing quilts, delivering messages, delivering newspapers,
    Taking care of the elderly,
    Boiling medicine for the sick…
    Who can count,
    On the newly painted walls,
    How many big-character posters praising you!
    I walk in Beijing city,
    I visited the big and small streets.
    I saw women dancing,
    I saw old people laughing,
    I saw the seven million people of the capital,
    Shouting slogans towards Zhongnanhai:
    Dear Chairman Mao,
    You have shown us another path to happiness!

### 好啊，北京的街道
李学鳌
1960-04-12
第8版()
专栏：

    好啊，北京的街道	李学鳌
    好啊，北京的街道！
    我赞美你神速的变化，
    崭新的面貌！
    我赞美你，
    群星似的工厂——
    发亮在所有街道，
    流出千百件产品，
    件件那么光耀。
    给大工厂添了至亲的弟弟；
    给多彩的商店加上更美的色调！
    我赞美你，
    明净的食堂——
    饭菜做得多好！
    人人吃得满意，
    增长干劲不少。
    张张脸呀，
    整天都是欢笑！
    我赞美你，
    托儿所中的小宝宝——
    红色摇篮中的小鸟，
    在快乐中成长，
    在阳光下欢跳，
    虽然刚呀呀学语，
    却会大声歌唱：
    人民公社好！
    人民公社好！
    我赞美你，
    “有求必应”的服务站——
    为居民服务得多周到，
    洗衣、缝被，传信、送报，
    照顾老人，
    给病人煎药，……
    谁能数清呀，
    在新刷未干的墙上，
    有多少表扬你们的大字报！
    我走在北京城里，
    我参观了大小街道。
    我看见妇女在跳，
    我看见老人在笑，
    我看见七百万首都人民，
    向着中南海高呼口号：
    亲爱的毛主席呀，
    你又给咱指出一条幸福的大道！
