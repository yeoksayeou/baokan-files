Title: Korean Central News Agency Delegation Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1960-04-28
Page: 2

Xinhua News Agency, April 27th. The delegation of the Korean Central News Agency, led by Vice President Ko Song-chun, arrived in Beijing by plane this afternoon at the invitation of the Xinhua News Agency for a visit to our country.

Those who welcomed them at the airport were Deng Gang, Vice President of the Xinhua News Agency, and Mu Qing, among others.

### 朝鲜中央通讯社代表团到北京

1960-04-28
第2版()
专栏：

    朝鲜中央通讯社代表团到北京
    新华社27日讯　应新华通讯社邀请来我国访问的朝鲜中央通讯社代表团，在副社长高成淳率领下，于今天下午乘飞机到达北京。
    到机场欢迎的有：新华通讯社副社长邓岗、穆青等。
