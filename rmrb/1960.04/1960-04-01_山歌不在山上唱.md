Title: Mountain Songs Are Not Sung on Mountains
Author: Li Mingcheng
Date: 1960-04-01
Page: 4

    Mountain songs are not sung on mountains,
    Mountain songs are sung next to the converter,
    Singing makes the converter's sparks glow,
    The song turns into high-quality steel!
        Shanghai Steel Plant No. 2 Li Mingcheng

### 山歌不在山上唱
李明成
1960-04-01
第4版()
专栏：

    山歌不在山上唱
    山歌不在山上唱，
    山歌唱在转炉旁，
    唱得转炉火花旺，
    歌声变成优质钢！
        上钢二厂　李明成
