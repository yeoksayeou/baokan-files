Title: Our Women's Delegation to Visit the United Kingdom
Author:
Date: 1960-04-18
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, April 17th: The Chinese Women's Delegation, led by head of the delegation Han Youtong, left Beijing by plane today for the United Kingdom to attend the Anglo-Chinese Friendship Association's annual meeting and to conduct visits at the invitation of the Anglo-Chinese Friendship Association. The members of the delegation are: Ni Feijun, Shi Ruzhang, Wu Lintao.

Those who saw them off at the airport included Pu Jiexiu, Zhang Shuyi, Chang Zheng, and Liang Siyi.

### 我妇女代表团去英国访问

1960-04-18
第6版()
专栏：

    我妇女代表团去英国访问
    新华社17日讯　应英中友协邀请前往英国参加英中友协年会并进行参观访问的中国妇女代表团，在团长韩幽桐率领下，今天乘飞机离开北京去英国。代表团团员有：倪斐君、施如璋、吴琳涛。
    到机场欢送的有浦洁修、张淑仪、常征、梁思懿等。
