Title: Iraqi First Ambassador to China Fazil Meets with Luo Guibo to Discuss Presenting Credentials
Author:
Date: 1960-04-26
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, April 25th. Mr. Abdul-Haq Fazil, the first Ambassador Extraordinary and Plenipotentiary of the Republic of Iraq to China, met with Luo Guibo, Vice Minister of Foreign Affairs, this morning to discuss matters related to the presentation of credentials. Present at the meeting were Yu Peiwen, Director of the Protocol Department of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs, and He Ying, Director of the West Asia and Africa Department.

### 伊拉克首任驻华大使法齐勒会见罗贵波商谈递交国书事宜

1960-04-26
第4版()
专栏：

    伊拉克首任驻华大使法齐勒会见罗贵波商谈递交国书事宜
    新华社25日讯　伊拉克共和国首任驻华特命全权大使阿卜杜勒·哈格·法齐勒先生今天上午会见我外交部副部长罗贵波，商谈递交国书事宜。会见时在座的有外交部礼宾司司长俞沛文、西亚非洲司司长何英。
