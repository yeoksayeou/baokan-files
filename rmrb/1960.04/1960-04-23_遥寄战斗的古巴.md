Title: Sending Greetings to the Fighting Cuba
Author: Wen Jie
Date: 1960-04-23
Page: 8
Support for the Cuban People

    Sending Greetings to the Fighting Cuba
    Wen Jie
    I truly wish to spur on a winged steed,
    To fly across the Pacific, to faraway Cuba;
    Brothers! I tightly grasp your gun-holding hands,
    Offering my song, offering a red camellia.
    My song is dedicated to the island standing tall amidst the storm,
    Dedicated to the nation that gave birth to José Martí,
    Dedicated to the Cuban warriors fighting for freedom,
    The power of the people is forever invincible!
    Brothers! You come from the Sierra Maestra,
    Expelling the reactionary Batista with arms,
    You bravely advanced through smoke and gunfire,
    Shattering the iron shackles of imperialism imprisoning Cuba.
    My flower is dedicated to the martyrs who sacrificed for peace,
    Dedicated to Havana, inspiring the people's fighting spirit,
    Dedicated to the national heroes leading the Cuban revolution,
    Fighting Cuba, revolutionary friends are everywhere!
    Brothers! You hold high the blazing torch,
    Illuminating the Americas shrouded in dark clouds,
    You have taken your destiny into your own hands,
    On the island stands the edifice of the people's democratic dictatorship.
    I truly wish to spur on a winged steed,
    To fly across the Pacific, to faraway Cuba;
    Brothers! I wish to stand shoulder to shoulder with you,
    Singing loudly as the flowers of victory bloom all over Cuba.

### 遥寄战斗的古巴
闻捷
1960-04-23
第8版()
专栏：支援古巴人民

    遥寄战斗的古巴
    闻捷
    我真想催动长着翅膀的追风马，
    飞过太平洋，飞到遥远的古巴；
    兄弟呵！我紧握你们持枪的手，
    献上我的歌，献上一枝红山茶。
    我的歌献给风浪中屹立的海岛，
    献给诞生了何塞·马蒂的国家，
    献给为自由而战斗的古巴勇士，
    人民的力量，永远无敌于天下！
    兄弟呵！你们来自马埃斯特腊，
    以武装驱逐了反动的巴蒂斯塔，
    你们冒着硝烟和战火奋勇前进，
    粉碎帝国主义囚禁古巴的铁枷。
    我的花献给为和平殉难的烈士，
    献给激扬着人们斗志的哈瓦那，
    献给领导古巴革命的民族英雄，
    战斗的古巴，革命朋友遍天下！
    兄弟呵！你们高举熊熊的火炬，
    照亮满天阴云笼罩的阿美利加，
    你们以双手掌握了自己的命运，
    海岛上矗立起人民专政的大厦。
    我真想催动长着翅膀的追风马，
    飞过太平洋，飞到遥远的古巴；
    兄弟呵！我愿和你们并肩而站，
    放声歌唱古巴开遍胜利的鲜花。
