Title: Chairman Liu to Visit Mongolia
Author:
Date: 1960-04-10
Page: 1
    Xinhua News Agency, 9th. Comrade Zh. Sambu, Chairman of the Presidium of the Great People's Hural of the Mongolian People's Republic, has invited Comrade Liu Shaoqi, Chairman of the People's Republic of China, to pay a friendly visit to the Mongolian People's Republic at a time he deems suitable. Chairman Liu Shaoqi has accepted this invitation.

### 刘主席将访问蒙古

1960-04-10
第1版()
专栏：

    刘主席将访问蒙古
    新华社9日讯　蒙古人民共和国大人民呼拉尔主席团主席扎·桑布同志邀请中华人民共和国主席刘少奇同志在他认为适合的时候到蒙古人民共和国进行友好访问。刘少奇主席已经接受了这项邀请。
