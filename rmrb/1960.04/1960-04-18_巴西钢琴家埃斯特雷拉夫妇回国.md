Title: Brazilian Pianist Couple Estrela Return Home
Author:
Date: 1960-04-18
Page: 6

　　Xinhua News Agency, 17th. Brazilian pianist Arnaldo Estrela and his wife, violinist Marifeia Iakovino, who were invited to visit our country by the Chinese People's Association for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries and the Chinese Musicians Association, left Beijing by plane this morning to return home.

### 巴西钢琴家埃斯特雷拉夫妇回国

1960-04-18
第6版()
专栏：

    巴西钢琴家埃斯特雷拉夫妇回国
    新华社17日讯　应中国人民对外文化协会和中国音乐家协会邀请来我国访问的巴西钢琴家阿纳耳多·埃斯特雷拉和他的夫人、小提琴家玛丽芙霞·亚科维诺，今天上午乘飞机离开北京回国。
