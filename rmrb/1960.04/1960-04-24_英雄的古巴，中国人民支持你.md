Title: Heroic Cuba, the Chinese People Support You
Author: Gao Changfu
Date: 1960-04-24
Page: 8
Column: Support for the Cuban People

    Heroic Cuba, the Chinese People Support You
    Worker, Beijing People's Printing Factory, Gao Changfu
    Heroic Cuba,
    You and China are separated by ten thousand miles,
    Though the journey is long,
    Peace unites us together.
    When you drove away the fierce jackals,
    When you recovered your own land,
    It's like we increased production by ten million tons of steel,
    The Chinese people cheered in unison for victory!
    Heroic Cuba,
    The Chinese people support you,
    Support you in suppressing the bad elements within the country,
    Support you in eliminating the American imperialists who dare to invade you!
    Support you in reclaiming your own plantations,
    Support you in reclaiming your own land.
    Heroic Cuba,
    Advance, fight on,
    Six hundred million Chinese people,
    Fully support you,
    If the imperialists dare to attack you,
    We will raise our iron fists,
    Beat them to death and throw them into the sea!

### 英雄的古巴，中国人民支持你
高长福
1960-04-24
第8版()
专栏：支援古巴人民

    英雄的古巴，中国人民支持你
    北京人民印刷厂工人　高长福
    英雄的古巴，
    你和中国相隔万里，
    万里路程虽远，
    和平把我们联在一起。
    当你赶跑了凶恶的豺狼，
    当你收复了自己的土地，
    就像我们增产了千万吨钢铁，
    中国人民齐声欢呼胜利！
    英雄的古巴，
    中国人民支持你，
    支持你镇压国内的坏分子，
    支持你消灭敢侵犯你的美帝国主义！
    支持你收回自己的庄园，
    支持你收回自己的土地。
    英雄的古巴，
    前进吧，战斗吧，
    六万万中国人民，
    全力支持你，
    帝国主义胆敢向你进犯，
    我们就举起铁拳，
    把它打死，投进海里！
