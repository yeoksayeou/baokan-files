Title: Sino-French Sign Cultural and Scientific-Technological Cooperation Agreement
Author:
Date: 1960-04-04
Page: 6
Xinhua News Agency, April 2nd dispatch from Paris: A Sino-French Cultural and Scientific-Technological Cooperation Protocol and Agreement were signed on the 2nd at the French Ministry of Foreign Affairs.

The protocol stipulates the exchange of information, personnel, and the implementation of scientific work plans that have been initially agreed upon. The protocol was signed by Zhukov, Chairman of the State Committee for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries of the Soviet Council of Ministers, and Seydoux, Director of Cultural and Technical Affairs of the French Ministry of Foreign Affairs.

The Agreement on Cooperation in the Peaceful Use of Atomic Energy was signed by Yemelyanov, Director of the General Administration for the Utilization of Atomic Energy of the Soviet Council of Ministers, and Couture, the Government's General Representative of the French Atomic Energy Commission.

Xinhua News Agency, April 3rd dispatch from Paris, April 2nd: The Soviet Union and France today signed a new payment agreement in Paris. The agreement stipulates that all payments between the Soviet Union and France will henceforth be made in French francs, and the French franc must be freely convertible into any other foreign currency. The agreement will come into effect on April 10 this year.

### 苏法签订文化和科学技术合作协定

1960-04-04
第6版()
专栏：

    苏法签订文化和科学技术合作协定
    新华社2日讯　据塔斯社巴黎讯：苏法文化和科学技术合作议定书和协定2日在法国外交部签字。
    议定书规定要交换情报、人员和实行将初步达成协议的科学工作计划。在这个议定书上签字的，是苏联部长会议国家对外文化委员会主席茹科夫和法国外交部文化和技术司司长赛杜。
    在关于和平利用原子能方面合作的协定上签字的，是苏联部长会议利用原子能总管理局局长叶麦利扬诺夫和法国原子能委员会政府总负责人库屠尔。
    新华社3日讯　塔斯社巴黎2日讯：苏联和法国今天在巴黎签订了一项新的支付协定。协定规定，苏联和法国之间今后的一切支付将使用法国法郎，而法国法郎必须能自由兑换成其他任何外国货币。协定将在今年4月10日生效。
