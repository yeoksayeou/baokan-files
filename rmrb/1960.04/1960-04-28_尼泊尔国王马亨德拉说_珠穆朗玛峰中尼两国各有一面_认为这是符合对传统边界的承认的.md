Title: Nepal's King Mahendra says Mount Everest has one side belonging to China and one belonging to Nepal, considering this to be in line with the recognition of traditional borders
Author:
Date: 1960-04-28
Page: 2

Xinhua News Agency, April 27th, Honolulu dispatch: Nepal's King Mahendra said that Nepal recognizes that one side of Mount Everest belongs to Nepal, and the other side belongs to Tibet, China. He said that this is in line with the recognition of traditional borders.

King Mahendra ended his visit to Japan and was en route to Washington, D.C. via Honolulu.

### 尼泊尔国王马亨德拉说  珠穆朗玛峰中尼两国各有一面  认为这是符合对传统边界的承认的

1960-04-28
第2版()
专栏：

    尼泊尔国王马亨德拉说
    珠穆朗玛峰中尼两国各有一面
    认为这是符合对传统边界的承认的
    新华社27日讯　檀香山消息：尼泊尔国王马亨德拉说，尼泊尔承认珠穆朗玛峰的一面是属于尼泊尔的，另一面是属于中国的西藏的。他说，这是符合对传统边界的承认的。
    马亨德拉国王结束了他在日本的访问，途经檀香山前往华盛顿。
