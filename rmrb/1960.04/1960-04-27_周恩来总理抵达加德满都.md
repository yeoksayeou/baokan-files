Title: Premier Zhou Enlai Arrives in Kathmandu
Author:
Date: 1960-04-27
Page: 1

    Promoting the Further Consolidation and Development of the Precious Friendship Between China and Nepal
    Premier Zhou Enlai Arrives in Kathmandu
    Prime Minister Koirala and Almost All National Leaders Greet Him at the Airport
    The Nepalese Capital Takes a Day Off, Citizens Gather in the Streets to Cheer Enthusiastically
    Xinhua News Agency, Kathmandu, April 26th: Premier Zhou Enlai and his entourage arrived in Kathmandu, the capital of Nepal, by special plane from New Delhi today. Premier Zhou Enlai was warmly welcomed by the Nepalese people upon his arrival.
    As Premier Zhou Enlai stepped off the plane, the band played the national anthems of both countries, and the welcoming crowd at the airport burst into enthusiastic cheers. When Premier Zhou Enlai reviewed the guard of honor, a 19-gun salute was fired in his honor. The welcomers at the airport warmly cheered the Chinese Premier and hailed the friendship between China and Nepal.
    In his welcoming speech, Nepalese Prime Minister Koirala said that Premier Zhou Enlai is an old friend of the Nepalese people, and his visit will promote the cause of traditional friendship between the two countries.
    In his reply, Premier Zhou Enlai said that he brought the profound friendship and cordial greetings of the 650 million Chinese people to the Nepalese people. He expressed the hope that his visit would further consolidate and develop the precious friendship between the two countries.
    The welcoming crowd at the airport responded to Premier Zhou's speech with cheers and applause. They enthusiastically waved the colorful flags and flowers in their hands.
    Today, Kathmandu Airport was decorated with colorful flags, flowers, and floral arches. Almost all of Nepal's national leaders went to the airport to welcome the distinguished Chinese guests.
    Those who went to the airport to welcome included: cabinet ministers, senior officials of the Royal Government of Nepal, the Speaker of the Lower House, the Chairman of the Upper House, generals of the Royal Army, the Chief Justice, members of the Nepalese Parliament , leaders from various political parties and leaders from the Nepal-China Friendship Association.
    Diplomatic envoys of various countries to Nepal also went to the airport to welcome him.
    When Premier Zhou Enlai and Vice Premier Chen Yi were introduced to the Nepalese leaders, the hosts presented the Chinese guests with one garland after another and bouquets of flowers.
    In the capital of this friendly country, a holiday was declared today so that the people could warmly welcome the Chinese Premier. Almost all the citizens gathered on the streets and cheered excitedly as Premier Zhou Enlai and Prime Minister Koirala passed by. On all the roads, many arches were decorated with welcome slogans and photos of the Chinese Premier, some of which were photos of Prime Minister Koirala and Premier Zhou Enlai shaking hands warmly.
    When the Chinese guests arrived at their residence, the band played the national anthems of both countries again.

### 周恩来总理抵达加德满都

1960-04-27
第1版()
专栏：

    促进中尼两国间的珍贵友谊更加巩固和发展
    周恩来总理抵达加德满都
    柯伊拉腊首相和几乎所有的国家领袖到机场欢迎
    尼泊尔首都放假一天市民聚集在街道上热情欢呼
    新华社加德满都26日电　周恩来总理和随行人员今天乘专机自新德里到达尼泊尔首都加德满都。周恩来总理到达时受到尼泊尔人民的热烈欢迎。
    当周恩来总理从飞机上下来以后，乐队演奏两国国歌，机场上的欢迎的人群发出了热烈的欢呼声。周恩来总理检阅仪仗队时，鸣礼炮十九响向他致敬。机场上的欢迎者向中国总理热烈欢呼，并且欢呼中尼两国的友谊。
    尼泊尔首相柯伊拉腊在致欢迎词时说，周恩来总理是尼泊尔人民的老朋友，他这次访问将促进两国之间的传统友谊事业。
    周恩来总理在致答词时说，他给尼泊尔人民带来了六亿五千万中国人民的深厚友谊和亲切的问候。他表示希望，他的这次访问能使两国间的珍贵友谊更加巩固和发展。
    机场上的欢迎人群对周总理的讲话报以欢呼和鼓掌。他们热情地挥舞着手中的彩旗和鲜花。
    今天加德满都机场用旗帜、鲜花和花朵扎成的牌楼装饰得五彩缤纷。几乎所有的尼泊尔国家领袖都前往机场欢迎中国贵宾们。
    前往机场欢迎的有：内阁大臣们，尼泊尔王国政府高级官员们，下议院议长，上议院议长，王国部队的将军们，首席法官，尼泊尔议会议员们，各政党领袖和尼中友好协会的领袖们。
    前往机场欢迎的还有各国驻尼泊尔外交使节。
    当周恩来总理和陈毅副总理被介绍给尼泊尔领袖们的时候，主人们把一个又一个的花环和一束又一束的鲜花送给中国客人。
    在这个友好国家的首都，今天宣布放假，以便人民能热烈欢迎中国总理。几乎全体市民都聚集在街道上，并在周恩来总理和柯伊拉腊首相经过的时候兴高彩烈地向他们欢呼。在所有的道路上，许多拱门上都装饰着欢迎的标语和中国总理的照片，其中有些是柯伊拉腊首相和周恩来总理热烈握手的照片。
    当中国客人们抵达他们下榻的地方时，乐队又奏起两国国歌。
