Title: Indian Vice President Hosts Luncheon for Premier Zhou; Ambassador Pan Zili's Reception Held in Friendly Atmosphere, Indian Friends Hopeful for Successful Talks
Author:
Date: 1960-04-23
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, New Delhi, April 22nd. Indian Vice President Dr. Radhakrishnan hosted a luncheon today in honor of Premier Zhou Enlai.

　　Vice Premier and Foreign Minister Chen Yi, Vice Foreign Minister Zhang Hanfu, Deputy Director of the State Council Foreign Affairs Office Zhang Yan, and Assistant Foreign Minister Qiao Guanhua attended the luncheon.

　　Indian Minister of Home Affairs Govind Ballabh Pant, Minister of Finance Morarji Ranchhodji Desai, Minister of Steel, Mines, and Fuel Sardar Swaran Singh, and Minister of Defence V. K. Krishna Menon also attended the luncheon.

　　Also present at the luncheon were: Chinese Ambassador to India Pan Zili and Indian Ambassador to China G. Parthasarathi.

Xinhua News Agency, New Delhi, April 21st. Our correspondent reports: Indian government officials, members of parliament, and people from all walks of life, when meeting Premier Zhou Enlai and Vice Premier Chen Yi at the reception hosted by Chinese Ambassador to India Pan Zili for Premier Zhou Enlai's visit to India, repeatedly expressed their heartfelt hope that the talks between the premiers of China and India would be successful.

　　Premier Zhou Enlai and Prime Minister Nehru attended the reception after their talks in the afternoon. They gathered with Indian Vice President S. Radhakrishnan, Chinese Vice Premier Chen Yi, Indian Minister of Steel, Mines, and Fuel Sardar Swaran Singh, Minister of Defence V. K. Krishna Menon, and other attendees. They engaged in pleasant conversation in a harmonious atmosphere.

　　At this grand reception, Indian friends continuously came to Premier Zhou Enlai to express their greetings. The renowned peace activist Dr. Kitchlew greeted Premier Zhou Enlai with hands clasped together, and Premier Zhou Enlai wished him good health. Kitchlew said: "You are doing the right thing. You and Prime Minister Nehru should have a good talk. China and India, these two great nations, should be friendly with each other. I wish the talks between the two premiers success!"

　　President of the India-China Friendship Association, Sundarlal, said to Premier Zhou Enlai: "I pray day and night for your success. I have carefully read your speeches at the airport and at the banquet. Your speeches are excellent, excellent! Your sincerity has deeply moved us!" Premier Zhou Enlai said: "We have indeed come with a sincere desire to solve the problem."

　　Many members of parliament walked up to Premier Zhou Enlai, and their first words were to wish him success. Many of them cheered "Hindi-Chini bhai-bhai! (Indians and Chinese are brothers!)" Congress Party member of Parliament Saigal presented Premier Zhou Enlai with a fine gift.

　　Vice Premier Chen Yi arrived at the reception in the same car as Indian Minister of Steel, Mines, and Fuel Sardar Swaran Singh. They had met in the afternoon. They shook hands and said to each other: "I've accompanied you here!" This elicited heartfelt laughter. Some famous writers and journalists, upon meeting Vice Premier Chen Yi, affectionately called him "Marshal Writer" and "Marshal Journalist." Vice Premier Chen Yi smiled and said: "The Marshal is about to become unemployed!"

　　Indian economist Gyan Chand, Congress Party member J. N. Sahai, Chairman of the Delhi Branch of the India-China Friendship Association Radha Raman, and Verma, who once taught Hindi at Peking University, sincerely wished the two premiers success in their talks when they met Vice Premier Chen Yi, and expressed their hope that the border issue would be resolved.

　　Premier Zhou Enlai and Vice Premier Chen Yi also spoke with the heads of diplomatic missions to India from various countries, who also wished the talks between the Chinese and Indian premiers success. The Chinese Premier said to some of them that the resolution of the dispute between China and India would not only benefit the friendship between the two countries, but also world peace.

　　In the garden illuminated by colored lights, people talked to each other in the fresh evening breeze. This grand reception was filled with a friendly and harmonious atmosphere. People repeatedly expressed their wishes for the success of the China-India premiers' talks. An Hindi writer said: "No matter what differences may exist between the two countries, we are always bound together by a thousand years of friendship and long-standing cultural ties. We must maintain and further develop this friendship and culture!"

### 印度副总统设午宴招待周总理  潘自力大使招待会上气氛友好，印度朋友希望会谈成功

1960-04-23
第1版()
专栏：

    印度副总统设午宴招待周总理
    潘自力大使招待会上气氛友好，印度朋友希望会谈成功
    新华社新德里22日电　印度副总统拉达克里希南博士今天设午宴招待周恩来总理。
    副总理兼外交部长陈毅元帅，外交部副部长章汉夫，国务院外事办公室副主任张彦，外交部部长助理乔冠华出席了午宴。
    印度内政部长戈·巴·潘特，财政部长莫·兰·德赛，钢铁、矿业和燃料部长萨·斯·辛格和国防部长文·克·克·梅农也出席了午宴。
    出席午宴的还有：中国驻印度大使潘自力和印度驻中国大使高·帕塔萨拉蒂。
    新华社新德里21日电　本社记者报道：印度的政府官员、议会议员和社会人士在中国驻印度大使潘自力为周恩来总理访问印度举行的招待会上会见周恩来总理和陈毅副总理时，一再表示衷心希望中印两国总理的会谈得到成功。
    周恩来总理和尼赫鲁总理在下午会谈结束后出席了招待会。他们同印度副总统萨·拉达克里希南，中国副总理陈毅，印度钢铁、矿业和燃料部长萨·辛格，国防部长文·克·克·梅农和其它出席招待会的人聚会在一起。他们在和谐的气氛中愉快地交谈。
    在这个盛大的招待会上，印度朋友们川流不息地来到周恩来总理面前向他致意。著名的和平战士克其鲁博士向周恩来总理合掌致意，周恩来总理祝他身体健康。克其鲁说：“您做得对。您和尼赫鲁总理应该好好会谈一下。中国和印度这两个大国应该彼此友好。祝两国总理的会谈成功！”
    印中友好协会主席森德拉尔对周恩来总理说：“我日夜祝祷您得到成功。我已经仔细地阅读了您在机场和宴会上的讲话。您的讲话好极了，好极了！您的真诚深深地感动了我们！”周恩来总理说：“我们的确是抱着解决问题的真诚愿望来的。”
    许多议会议员走到周恩来总理面前，第一句话就是祝他成功。他们许多人欢呼“印地秦尼巴依巴依（印中人民是兄弟）！”国大党议员赛加尔送给周恩来总理一件精美的礼物。
    陈毅副总理同印度钢铁、矿业和燃料部长萨·辛格同车前来参加招待会。他们曾在下午会晤。他们握着手互相说：“我陪您到这里来了！”这引起了人们衷心的欢笑。有些著名作家和记者在遇到陈毅副总理时，亲热地称他为“元帅作家”和“元帅记者”。陈毅副总理微笑着说：“元帅将要失业了！”
    印度经济学家吉安·昌德，国大党人乔·阿尔瓦，印中友好协会德里分会主席拉达·拉曼和曾经在北京大学教授印地语的梵尔玛在遇见陈毅副总理时诚挚地祝两国总理会谈成功，并表示希望边界问题得到解决。
    周恩来总理和陈毅副总理还同各国驻印度使节交谈，这些使节也祝中印两国总理的会谈成功。中国总理对他们中间的一些人说，中印之间的争端的解决不仅有利于两国的友谊，而且有利于世界和平。
    在各色灯光照耀着的花园里，人们在清新的晚风中彼此交谈。这次盛大的招待会洋溢着友好和和谐的气氛。人们一再表示祝中印总理会谈成功。一位印地文作家说：“不论两国之间可能存在些什么分歧，我们总是被有千年历史的友谊和悠久的文化联系在一起的。我们必须保持和进一步发展这种友谊和文化！”
