Title: Chinese and Indian Prime Ministers Continue Talks, Vice Premier Chen Yi Holds Talks with Indian Defense Minister Menon, Premier Zhou Enlai Receives Soviet Ambassador to India
Author:
Date: 1960-04-25
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, New Delhi, April 24th: Premier of the State Council of the People's Republic of China, Zhou Enlai, and Prime Minister of the Republic of India, Jawaharlal Nehru, held talks today from 10:30 AM to 1:45 PM local time.

The two Prime Ministers will continue their talks tomorrow morning.

Officials from both countries will hold talks this afternoon.

Xinhua News Agency, New Delhi, April 23rd: Vice Premier of the State Council and Minister of Foreign Affairs of the People's Republic of China, Marshal Chen Yi, and Vice Minister of Foreign Affairs Zhang Hanfu held talks with Indian Defense Minister V. K. Krishna Menon this evening.

Xinhua News Agency, New Delhi, April 24th: Vice Premier of the State Council and Minister of Foreign Affairs of the People's Republic of China, Marshal Chen Yi, continued his talks with Indian Defense Minister V. K. Krishna Menon today. The talks began at 11:40 AM local time. Vice Minister of Foreign Affairs Zhang Hanfu of China was present at the talks.

Xinhua News Agency, New Delhi, April 24th: Officials from the Chinese government and the Indian government held talks this afternoon.

The Chinese side participating in the talks included Assistant Minister of Foreign Affairs Qiao Guanhua, Director of the First Asian Department of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs Zhang Wenjin, and others.

The Indian side participating in the talks included Foreign Secretary S. Dutt, Director of the Historical Research Division S. Gopal, Director of the Northern Division J. S. Mehta, and other officials from the Ministry of Foreign Affairs, as well as Indian Ambassador to China G. Parthasarathi.

The talks began at 4:10 PM local time.

Xinhua News Agency, New Delhi, April 24th: Premier Zhou Enlai received Indian Minister of Home Affairs Govind Ballabh Pant at 6:35 PM local time this afternoon.

Vice Premier and Minister of Foreign Affairs of China, Marshal Chen Yi, was present at the reception.

Xinhua News Agency, New Delhi, April 24th: Premier Zhou Enlai received the Soviet Ambassador to India, Ivan Alexandrovich Benediktov, at 4:45 PM local time this afternoon.

Vice Premier and Minister of Foreign Affairs Chen Yi and Chinese Ambassador to India Pan Zili were present at the reception.

### 中印两国总理继续会谈  陈毅副总理同印度国防部长梅农会谈  周恩来总理接见苏联驻印度大使

1960-04-25
第1版()
专栏：

    中印两国总理继续会谈
    陈毅副总理同印度国防部长梅农会谈
    周恩来总理接见苏联驻印度大使
    新华社新德里24日电　中华人民共和国国务院总理周恩来和印度共和国总理贾瓦哈拉尔·尼赫鲁在今天当地时间十点三十分至十三点四十五分举行会谈。
    两位总理将在明天上午继续举行会谈。
    两国官员今天下午将举行会谈。
    新华社新德里23日电　中华人民共和国国务院副总理兼外交部长陈毅元帅和外交部副部长章汉夫今天晚上同印度国防部长文·克·克里希纳·梅农举行了会谈。
    新华社新德里24日电　中国国
  务院副总理兼外交部长陈毅元帅同印度国防部长文·克·克里希纳·梅农今天继续举行了会谈。会谈是在当地时间上午十一时四十分开始的。会谈时在座的有中国外交部副部长章汉夫。
    新华社新德里24日电　中国政府和印度政府的官员们今天下午举行了会谈。
    中国方面参加会谈的有外交部部长助理乔冠华，外交部第一亚洲司司长章文晋等。
    印度方面参加会谈的有外交秘书苏·杜特，历史研究司司长斯·戈帕尔，北方司司长杰·斯·梅达和外交部其他官员以及印度驻中国大使高·帕塔萨拉蒂。
    会谈是在当地时间十六点十分开始的。
    新华社新德里24日电　周恩来总理在今天下午当地时间十八点三十五分接见印度内政部长戈·巴·潘特。
    接见时在座的有中国副总理兼外交部长陈毅元帅。
    新华社新德里24日电　周恩来总理在今天下午当地时间十六点四十五分接见苏联驻印度大使伊凡·亚历山德罗维奇·别涅吉克托夫。
    接见时在座的有：副总理兼外交部长陈毅元帅和中国驻印度大使潘自力。
