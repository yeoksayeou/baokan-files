Title: Brazilian Trade Union Delegation Arrives in Beijing
Author: 
Date: 1960-04-14
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, April 13th: A six-member delegation from the Brazilian National Confederation of Civil Servants' Unions and the Brazilian National Federation of Bank Employees, headed by Carlos Telles da Cunha e Mello, Vice-Chairman of the Brazilian National Confederation of Civil Servants' Unions, arrived in Beijing by plane this afternoon. They are visiting China and participating in the "May Day" International Labor Day celebrations at the invitation of the All-China Federation of Trade Unions.

### 巴西工会代表团到京

1960-04-14
第4版()
专栏：

    巴西工会代表团到京
    新华社13日讯　以巴西全国公务员工会副主席卡洛斯·泰勒·达·库尼亚·埃·梅洛为首的巴西全国公务员工会和巴西全国银行职员联合会代表团一行六人，今天下午乘飞机到达北京。他们是应中华全国总工会的邀请，前来我国参加“五一”国际劳动节庆祝活动和访问的。
