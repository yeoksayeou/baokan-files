Title: Wu Yuzhang Receives Japanese Academic Delegation for Investigating Chinese Character Reform

Author: 
Date: 1960-04-07
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, April 6th: Wu Yuzhang, member of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress and Director of the Chinese Character Reform Committee, received Toki Zemmaro, head of the Japanese Academic Delegation for Investigating Chinese Character Reform, and all members of the delegation this morning and had a cordial conversation with them.

Present at the reception were Hu Yuzhi, Wei Que, and Ding Xilin, vice directors of the Chinese Character Reform Committee.

### 吴玉章接见日本考察中国文字改革学术代表团

1960-04-07
第6版()
专栏：

    吴玉章接见日本考察中国文字改革学术代表团
    新华社6日讯　全国人民代表大会常务委员会委员、中国文字改革委员会主任吴玉章，今天上午接见了日本考察中国文字改革学术代表团团长土岐善麿和全体团员，并进行了亲切的谈话。
    接见时在座的有中国文字改革委员会副主任胡愈之、韦慤、丁西林。
