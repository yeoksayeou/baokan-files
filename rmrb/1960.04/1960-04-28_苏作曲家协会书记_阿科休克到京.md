Title: Secretary of the Soviet Composers Association, Akekhyuk, Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1960-04-28
Page: 2

Xinhua News Agency, April 27th. Akekhyuk, Secretary of the Soviet Composers Association, arrived in Beijing by plane this afternoon for a visit to China according to the 1959 executive plan of the Sino-Soviet Cultural Cooperation Agreement.

Wang Yuanfang, Secretary of the Secretariat of the Chinese Musicians Association, and others welcomed him at the airport.

### 苏作曲家协会书记  阿科休克到京

1960-04-28
第2版()
专栏：

    苏作曲家协会书记
    阿科休克到京
    新华社27日讯　根据中苏文化合作协定1959年执行计划前来我国访问的苏联作曲家协会书记阿科休克，今天下午乘飞机到达北京。
    中国音乐家协会书记处书记王元方等到机场欢迎。
