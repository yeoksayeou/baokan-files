Title: Iraq National Peace Conference Closes; Baghdad Holds Peace Demonstration Parade
Author:
Date: 1960-04-05
Page: 7
Xinhua News Agency, Baghdad, April 3rd: A grand peace demonstration parade was held in Baghdad, the capital of Iraq, on the afternoon of the 3rd.
    Leading the march were members of the National Committee of Iraqi Peace Fighters, followed closely by representatives from various provinces who participated in the Third Iraqi National Peace Conference.
    People of all professions participated in the march, including workers, farmers, students, businessmen, intellectuals, housewives, and those engaged in religious activities. They held high banners and huge pictures expressing people's desire for peace and their determination to defend the republic.
    At 5:30 p.m., the procession began to pass by the Ministry of Defense.
    Prime Minister Kassem and Indonesian President Sukarno, who is currently visiting Iraq, reviewed the parade near the gate of the Ministry of Defense.
    The parade lasted until evening.
    The Iraqi National Peace Conference closed on the 2nd. The conference passed resolutions supporting the struggles of the Algerian people, the South African people, and others, and elected a new leadership structure for the National Committee of Iraqi Peace Fighters.

### 伊拉克全国和平大会闭幕  巴格达举行和平示威游行

1960-04-05
第7版()
专栏：

    伊拉克全国和平大会闭幕
    巴格达举行和平示威游行
    据新华社巴格达3日电　3日下午在伊拉克首都巴格达举行了盛大的和平示威游行。
    走在游行队伍前面的是伊拉克和平战士全国委员会委员们，紧跟着的是来自各省参加第三次伊拉克全国和平大会的代表。
    参加游行的有各种职业的人们，包括工人、农民、学生、商人、知识分子、家庭妇女和那些从事宗教活动的人。他们高举着表示人们的和平愿望和保卫共和国决心的标语牌和巨幅图片。
    下午五点三十分，游行队伍开始经过国防部。
    卡塞姆总理和现在正在伊拉克访问的印度尼西亚总统苏加诺在国防部大门附近检阅了游行队伍。
    游行一直到晚上才结束。
    伊拉克全国和平大会已在2日闭幕。会议曾通过了支持阿尔及利亚人民、南非人民的斗争等决议，并且选出了伊拉克和平战士全国委员会的新领导机构。
