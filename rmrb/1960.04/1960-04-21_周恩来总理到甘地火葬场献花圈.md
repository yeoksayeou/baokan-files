Title: Premier Zhou Enlai Lays Wreath at Gandhi Cremation Site
Author:
Date: 1960-04-21
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, New Delhi, April 20th: Premier Zhou Enlai, Vice Premier Chen Yi, and their entourage visited Rajghat this morning and laid a wreath at the Gandhi cremation site. Afterwards, the Chinese Premier and Vice Premier signed the guest book.

Accompanying them were Pan Zili, the Chinese Ambassador to India, and from the Indian side, the Indian Ambassador to China, Parthasarathi, and the Chief of Protocol of the Ministry of External Affairs, M.R.A. Baig.

### 周恩来总理到甘地火葬场献花圈

1960-04-21
第1版()
专栏：

    周恩来总理到甘地火葬场献花圈
    新华社新德里20日电　周恩来总理、陈毅副总理和随行人员今天上午访问了拉志加，并在甘地火葬场献了花圈。事后，中国总理和副总理在来宾簿上签名留念。
    陪同他们前去的，中国方面有中国驻印度大使潘自力，印度方面有印度驻中国大使帕塔萨拉蒂和外交部礼宾司司长米·拉·阿·贝格。
