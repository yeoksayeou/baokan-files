Title: Premier Nehru Hosts Banquet for Premier Zhou; Sino-Indian Premiers Begin Talks; Premier Zhou Pays Respects to President Prasad and Premier Nehru
Author:
Date: 1960-04-21
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, New Delhi, 20th -- Indian Prime Minister Jawaharlal Nehru hosted a banquet tonight in honor of Zhou Enlai, Premier of the State Council of the People's Republic of China.

Marshal Chen Yi, Vice Premier of the State Council and Minister of Foreign Affairs, Vice Minister of Foreign Affairs Zhang Hanfu, and other senior officials accompanying the Chinese Premier, as well as Chinese Ambassador to India Pan Zili, attended the banquet.

Those present at the banquet included: Indian Vice President Radhakrishnan, Mrs. Indira Gandhi, cabinet ministers, ministers of state, other senior officials of the Indian government, members of parliament, and senior officers of the armed forces.

Foreign diplomatic envoys also attended the banquet.

The banquet was held at the Presidential Palace.

Xinhua News Agency, New Delhi, 20th -- Premier Zhou Enlai of the People's Republic of China and Prime Minister Jawaharlal Nehru of the Republic of India began talks this morning and continued them in the afternoon.

Xinhua News Agency, New Delhi, 20th -- Premier Zhou Enlai, Vice Premier Chen Yi, and other senior members of the Premier's entourage paid a courtesy call on Indian President Rajendra Prasad at the Presidential Palace this morning.

Also present were Chinese Ambassador to India Pan Zili and Indian Ambassador to China Parthasarathi.

Xinhua News Agency, New Delhi, 20th -- Premier Zhou Enlai and Vice Premier Chen Yi visited Premier Nehru at Premier Nehru's residence at 11:00 a.m. local time.

Present during the visit on the Indian side were: Minister of Steel, Mines and Fuel Swaran Singh, Indian Ambassador to China Parthasarathi, and senior officials from the Indian Ministry of Foreign Affairs.

Present on the Chinese side were: Chinese Ambassador to India Pan Zili and senior officials from the Premier's entourage.

Xinhua News Agency, New Delhi, 20th -- Premier Zhou Enlai, accompanied by Vice Premier Marshal Chen Yi and other members of his entourage, went to the residence of Indian Prime Minister Nehru at 11:00 a.m. this morning to pay a courtesy call. Premier Zhou Enlai introduced his entourage to the Indian Prime Minister.

Present during the visit was the Indian Minister of Steel, Mines, and Fuel, Sardar Swaran Singh. Subsequently, Chinese Vice Premier Chen Yi, Sardar Swaran Singh, and other personnel withdrew.

The two premiers held talks for two hours.

In the afternoon, Indian Defense Minister Krishna Menon visited the Chinese Premier at the Presidential Palace and stayed with him for two hours.

The talks between the two premiers resumed at 5:00 p.m. at the Prime Minister's residence and continued until 7:00 p.m.

The talks will continue tomorrow.

### 尼赫鲁总理设宴招待周总理  中印两国总理开始举行会谈  周总理分别拜会普拉沙德总统和尼赫鲁总理

1960-04-21
第1版()
专栏：

    尼赫鲁总理设宴招待周总理  中印两国总理开始举行会谈
    周总理分别拜会普拉沙德总统和尼赫鲁总理
    新华社新德里20日电　印度共和国总理贾瓦哈拉尔·尼赫鲁今天晚上举行宴会招待中华人民共和国国务院总理周恩来。
    国务院副总理兼外交部长陈毅元帅、外交部副部长章汉夫和随同中国总理访问的其他高级官员和中国驻印度大使潘自力出席了宴会。
    出席宴会的有：印度副总统拉达克里希南，英迪拉·甘地夫人，内阁部长们，国务部长们和印度政府的其他高级官员，议员们和武装部队的高级军官。
    各国外交使节也出席了宴会。
    宴会是在总统府举行的。
    新华社新德里20日电　中华人民共和国总理周恩来和印度共和国总理贾瓦哈拉尔·尼赫鲁今天上午开始举行会谈，并且在下午继续举行会谈。
    新华社新德里20日电　周恩来总理、陈毅副总理和总理的其他高级随行人员今天上午在总统府拜会了印度总统拉金德拉·普拉沙德。
    在场的有中国驻印度大使潘自力和印度驻中国大使帕塔萨拉蒂。
    新华社新德里20日电　周恩来总理和陈毅副总理在当地时间十一点钟到尼赫鲁总理官邸拜会了尼赫鲁总理。
    拜会时在座的，印度方面有：钢铁、矿业和燃料部长斯瓦兰·辛格、印度驻中国大使帕塔萨拉蒂和印度外交部的高级官员。
    中国方面在座的有：中国驻印度大使潘自力和总理随行人员中的高级官员。
    新华社新德里20日电　周恩来总理在副总理陈毅元帅和其他随行人员的陪同下在今天上午十一时前往印度总理尼赫鲁的官邸拜会印度总理。周恩来总理把他的随行人员介绍给印度总理。
    拜会时在座的有印度钢铁、矿业和燃料部长萨达尔·斯瓦兰·辛格。随后，中国副总理陈毅以及萨达尔·斯瓦兰·辛格和其他人员退出。
    两国总理会谈达两小时。
    下午，印度国防部长克里希纳·梅农前往总统府拜访中国总理，同他在一起呆了两小时。
    两国总理的会谈在下午五时在总理官邸继续进行，一直进行到下午七时。
    会谈明天将继续进行。
