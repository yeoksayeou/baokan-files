Title: Foreign Trade Union Delegations and Representatives Arrive in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1960-04-25
Page: 2

Xinhua News Agency, April 24th. The Hungarian Trade Union Delegation headed by Valka György, Secretary of the Central Council of Hungarian Trade Unions; the Moroccan Trade Union Delegation headed by El Aloui Moulay Lyazid, Deputy Secretary-General of the Moroccan Federation of Public Works and Aviation Unions; Abdou Rahman Jama Hassan, Secretary-General of the Somaliland African Workers' Independent Trade Union Federation; and the Ecuadorian Workers' Confederation Delegation headed by Luis Armando Challdos arrived in Beijing by plane this afternoon. They were invited by the All-China Federation of Trade Unions to participate in the "May Day" celebrations and to visit our country.

### 一批外国工会代表团和工会代表到京

1960-04-25
第2版()
专栏：

    一批外国工会代表团和工会代表到京
    新华社24日讯　以匈牙利工会中央理事会书记瓦尔卡·捷尔吉为首的匈牙利工会代表团、以摩洛哥公共工程及航空工会联合会副总书记埃拉路易·穆拉依·利亚吉德为首的摩洛哥工会代表团、索马里兰非洲人独立工会联合会总书记阿卜杜·拉曼·扎玛·哈森、以路易斯·阿曼多·沙尔多斯为首的厄瓜多尔工人联合会代表团，今天下午乘飞机到达北京。他们是应中华全国总工会邀请，来我国参加“五一”庆祝活动和访问的。
