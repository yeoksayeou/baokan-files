Title: Nepalese Table Tennis Team Continues Matches in Shanghai
Author:
Date: 1960-04-29
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, Shanghai, April 28th. The Nepalese table tennis team held a friendly match with the Shanghai student team at the Shanghai Municipal Gymnasium on the 27th. In the ten matches played, including women's singles, men's singles, women's doubles, men's doubles, and mixed doubles, the Shanghai student team won all of them. More than three thousand spectators continuously applauded the wonderful performances of both sides, and the whole venue was filled with a friendly atmosphere.

The Nepalese table tennis team is scheduled to leave Shanghai for Beijing on the 28th. During their stay in Shanghai, the entire team visited factories, the Children's Palace, workers' residential areas, and other places. The athletes from the visiting team also practiced with Shanghai's table tennis players, learning from each other and exchanging experiences.

### 尼泊尔乒乓球代表队在上海继续比赛

1960-04-29
第6版()
专栏：

    尼泊尔乒乓球代表队在上海继续比赛
    新华社上海28日电　尼泊尔乒乓球代表队27日在上海市体育馆同上海市学生队举行友谊比赛。在进行的女子单打、男子单打、女子双打、
  男子双打和男、女混合双打等十场比赛中，都是上海市学生队获胜。三千多名观众对双方精彩的表演不断鼓掌，全场充满着友好的气氛。
    尼泊尔乒乓球代表队预定在28日离上海去北京。客队全体人员在上海期间，参观访问了工厂、少年宫、工人住宅区等处。客队运动员们还同上海的乒乓球运动员一起练球，相互学习，交流经验。
