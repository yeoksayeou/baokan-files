Title: Polish and Romanian Basketball Teams Hold Exhibition Games with Chinese Basketball Teams at Beijing Gymnasium
Author:
Date: 1960-04-27
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, 26th. The Polish "Guard" Men's Basketball Team and the Romanian "Dinamo" Men's Basketball Team, participating in the First Men's Basketball Competition of Socialist Countries' Public Security Sports Organizations, held exhibition games tonight at the Beijing Gymnasium competition hall with the Chinese People's Liberation Army "August 1st" Men's Basketball Team and the Beijing Youth Men's Basketball Team, respectively, to entertain the delegates attending the National Militia Representative Conference.

As a result of the games, the "August 1st" team and the Beijing Youth team won with scores of 76-65 and 71-61, respectively.

### 波、罗篮球队分别同我篮球队  在北京体育馆举行表演赛

1960-04-27
第4版()
专栏：

    波、罗篮球队分别同我篮球队
    在北京体育馆举行表演赛
    新华社26日讯　参加社会主义国家公安体育组织第一届男子篮球比赛大会的波兰“近卫军”男子篮球队、罗马尼亚“狄纳莫”男子篮球队，今晚在北京体育馆比赛馆分别同中国人民解放军“八一”男子篮球队、北京青年男子篮球队举行表演赛，招待参加全国民兵代表会议的代表们。
    比赛结果，“八一”队和北京青年队分别以七十六比六十五和七十一比六十一获胜。
