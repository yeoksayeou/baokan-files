Title: Polish News Delegation Returns Home
Author:
Date: 1960-04-11
Page: 7

Xinhua News Agency, 10th: The Polish news delegation, which came to China for a visit under the Sino-Polish Cultural Cooperation Agreement, left Beijing by plane this morning to return home, led by Mr. M. Rakowski, head of the delegation, Chairman of the Polish Journalists Association, and editor-in-chief of the Polish "Political Weekly."

Li Bingquan, Secretary of the Secretariat of the All-China Journalists Association, Wang Yi, Secretary-General of the Sino-Polish Friendship Association, and people from the capital's press circles went to the airport to see them off. Futor, Chargé d'Affaires ad interim of the Polish Embassy in China, also went to the airport to see them off.

### 波兰新闻代表团回国

1960-04-11
第7版()
专栏：

    波兰新闻代表团回国
    新华社10日讯　根据中波文化合作协定来我国访问的波兰新闻工作者代表团，在团长、波兰新闻工作者协会主席、波兰“政治周刊”主编米·拉科夫斯基率领下，今天早晨乘飞机离开北京回国。
    前往机场欢送的有中华全国新闻工作者协会书记处书记李炳泉，中波友协秘书长王仪和首都新闻界人士。波兰驻华大使馆临时代办傅拉托也到机场送行。
