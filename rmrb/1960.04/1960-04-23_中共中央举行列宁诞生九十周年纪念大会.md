Title: The CCP Central Committee Holds a Commemorative Meeting for the 90th Anniversary of Lenin's Birth
Author:
Date: 1960-04-23
Page: 1
    Commemorate the Great Teacher of the Proletarian Revolution; Highly Develop Lenin's Spirit of Thorough Revolution
    The CCP Central Committee Holds a Commemorative Meeting for the 90th Anniversary of Lenin's Birth
    Leaders such as Zhu De, Lin Biao, Deng Xiaoping, Song Qingling, and Dong Biwu, along with people from all walks of life, attended the meeting of 10,000 people
    Comrade Lu Dingyi Delivered a Report Entitled "Unite Under Lenin's Revolutionary Banner" at the Meeting
    Xinhua News Agency, April 22nd: The Central Committee of the Communist Party of China held a grand meeting at the Great Hall of the People today to solemnly commemorate the 90th anniversary of the birth of the great teacher of the proletarian revolution, Lenin.
    The commemorative meeting was held at 3:00 PM. Ten thousand people attended the meeting. Lu Dingyi, Alternate Member of the Political Bureau of the CCP Central Committee, delivered a lengthy report at the meeting entitled "Unite Under Lenin's Revolutionary Banner."
    Zhu De, Vice Chairman of the CCP Central Committee, Lin Biao, Vice Chairman of the CCP Central Committee, Deng Xiaoping, General Secretary of the CCP Central Committee, and Song Qingling, Vice Chairman of the People's Republic of China, attended today's meeting.
    Also attending the meeting were Lin Boqu, Dong Biwu, Peng Zhen, Luo Ronghuan, Li Fuchun, and He Long, Members of the Political Bureau of the CCP Central Committee, Zhang Wentian, Chen Boda, Kang Sheng, and Bo Yibo, Alternate Members of the Political Bureau, Tan Zheng, Secretary of the Secretariat of the CCP Central Committee, Liu Lantao, Yang Shangkun, and Hu Qiaomu, Alternate Secretaries, and Central Committee Members and Alternate Members in Beijing. Soviet Ambassador to China, Chervonenko, was invited to attend the meeting. As they walked onto the rostrum, the audience burst into warm and sustained applause.
    Today, the Great Hall of the People was decorated solemnly and plainly. A huge portrait of Lenin hung high on the rostrum. In front of the ten red flags on both sides of the portrait hung huge golden letters "1870-1960". The front of the podium was filled with blooming flowers.
    Peng Zhen, Member of the Political Bureau of the CCP Central Committee and Secretary of the Secretariat, announced the opening of the commemorative meeting for the 90th anniversary of the birth of the great teacher of the proletarian revolution, Lenin. At this moment, everyone stood up and the band played the Internationale.
    Comrade Lu Dingyi then delivered a report at the meeting entitled "Unite Under Lenin's Revolutionary Banner." His report repeatedly evoked enthusiastic applause from the audience. Finally, he raised his hand and shouted "Long live Marxism-Leninism!" At this moment, the whole audience stood up and a storm of prolonged and enthusiastic applause resounded through the hall.
    Also attending the commemorative meeting was the Vice Premier of the State Council, Vice Chairmen of the Standing Committee of the National People’s Congress, Vice Chairman of the National Committee of the Chinese People’s Political Consultative Conference, Vice Chairmen of the Defense Council, Deputies to the National People’s Congress and Members of the National Committee of the CPPCC in Beijing, heads of various departments of the government, democratic parties, and people’s organizations, senior generals of the Chinese People’s Liberation Army, heads of Party and government in Beijing, advanced producers and advanced workers in Beijing, and well-known figures from all aspects of China.
    In addition to the Soviet Union's envoys and diplomatic officials in China, the ambassadors and charge d'affaires ad interim of other fraternal countries to our country also attended the meeting: Li Yong-ho, Ambassador of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea, Bushniak, Ambassador of Czechoslovakia, Wandel, Ambassador of the German Democratic Republic, Panchevski, Ambassador of Bulgaria, Chahalascus, Ambassador of Romania, Sharab, Ambassador of Mongolia, Palivti, Ambassador of Albania, Fulahto, Charge d'Affaires ad interim of the Polish Embassy in China, Tran Trong Quat, Charge d'Affaires ad interim of the Embassy of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam in China, Sigeti, Charge d'Affaires ad interim of the Hungarian Embassy in China, and diplomatic officials of these countries, as well as experts from fraternal countries in Beijing, delegations from fraternal countries visiting our country, and experts from fraternal parties in Beijing, etc.
    A grand concert was held after the commemorative meeting. The joint symphony orchestra composed of musicians from the capital played "Spring Festival Overture" by Li Huanzhi and "Symphony No. 11 '1905'" by Shostakovich of the Soviet Union. The Eleventh Symphony is based on the Russian Revolution of 1905, expressing the awakening and great struggle of the Russian people under the rule of the Tsar at that time. (Attached picture)
    On the rostrum at the meeting. From the left: Hu Qiaomu, Yang Shangkun, Bo Yibo, Chen Boda, Zhang Wentian, He Long, Luo Ronghuan, Dong Biwu, Lin Biao, Chervonenko, Peng Zhen, Zhu De, Song Qingling, Deng Xiaoping, Lin Boqu, Li Fuchun, Lu Dingyi, Kang Sheng, Tan Zheng, Liu Lantao.

### 中共中央举行列宁诞生九十周年纪念大会

1960-04-23
第1版()
专栏：

    纪念伟大的无产阶级革命导师　高度发扬列宁的彻底革命精神
    中共中央举行列宁诞生九十周年纪念大会
    朱德、林彪、邓小平、宋庆龄、董必武等领导人和各界人士一万人出席了大会
    陆定一同志在会上作了题为“在列宁的革命旗帜下团结起来”的报告
    据新华社22日讯　中国共产党中央委员会今天在人民大会堂举行大会，隆重纪念伟大的无产阶级革命导师列宁诞生九十周年。
    纪念大会在下午三时举行。参加大会的有一万人。中共中央政治局候补委员陆定一在会上作了题为“在列宁的革命旗帜下团结起来”的长篇报告。
    中共中央副主席朱德、林彪，中共中央总书记邓小平和中华人民共和国副主席宋庆龄出席了今天的大会。
    出席大会的还有，中共中央政治局委员林伯渠、董必武、彭真、罗荣桓、李富春、贺龙，候补委员张闻天、陈伯达、康生、薄一波，中共中央书记处书记谭政，候补书记刘澜涛、杨尚昆、胡乔木和在北京的中央委员和候补委员等。苏联驻中国大使契尔沃年科应邀出席了大会。当他们走上主席台的时候，全场响起热烈的经久不息的掌声。
    今天，人民大会堂布置得庄严、朴素。主席台上高悬巨幅列宁画像，在画像两旁的十面红旗前面，挂着“1870—1960”的巨大金色字标。讲坛前布满盛开的鲜花。
    中共中央政治局委员、中央书记处书记彭真宣布伟大的无产阶级革命导师列宁诞生九十周年纪念大会开始。这时全体起立，乐队高奏国际歌。
    陆定一同志接着在会上作了题为“在列宁的革命旗帜下团结起来”的报告。他的报告不断引起全场的热烈掌声。最后，他举手高呼“马克思列宁主义万岁！”这时全场起立，万人大厅里响起了暴风雨般的长时间的热烈掌声。
    出席纪念大会的，还有国务院副总理，全国人民代表大会常务委员会副委员长，政协全国委员会副主席，国防委员会副主席，在北京的全国人民代表大会的代表和政协全国委员会的委员，政府各部门、各民主党派、人民团体的负责人，中国人民解放军高级将领，北京市党政负责人，在北京的先进生产者和先进工作者，以及中国各方面的著名人士。
    出席大会的除了苏联驻中国的使节和外交官员以外，还有其他兄弟国家驻我国的大使和临时代办：朝鲜民主主义人民共和国大使李永镐，捷克斯洛伐克大使布希尼亚克，德意志民主共和国大使汪戴尔，保加利亚大使潘切夫斯基，罗马尼亚大使查哈勒斯库，蒙古大使沙拉布，阿尔巴尼亚大使帕里夫蒂，波兰驻中国大使馆临时代办傅拉托，越南民主共和国驻中国大使馆临时代办陈仲括，匈牙利驻中国大使馆临时代办西格蒂和这些国家的外交官员，以及在北京的兄弟国家的专家，正在我国访问的兄弟国家的代表团，和在北京的兄弟党的专家等。
    纪念大会后举行了盛大的音乐会。首都音乐工作者组成的联合交响乐队演奏了李焕之作的“春节序曲”和苏联萧斯塔科维奇作的“第十一交响乐‘一九○五’”。第十一交响乐是以俄国1905年的革命为题材，表现了当时在沙皇统治下的俄罗斯人民的觉醒和巨大的斗争。（附图片）
