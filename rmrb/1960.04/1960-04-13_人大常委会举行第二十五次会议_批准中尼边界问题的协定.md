Title: NPC Standing Committee Holds 25th Session; Approves Agreement on Sino-Nepalese Boundary Question
Author:
Date: 1960-04-13
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, April 12th. The Standing Committee of the National People's Congress held its 25th session today. The session decided to approve the agreement between the Government of the People's Republic of China and the Government of His Majesty the King of Nepal on the boundary question between the two countries.

### 人大常委会举行第二十五次会议  批准中尼边界问题的协定

1960-04-13
第1版()
专栏：

    人大常委会举行第二十五次会议
    批准中尼边界问题的协定
    新华社12日讯　全国人民代表大会常务委员会今天举行第二十五次会议，会议决定批准中华人民共和国政府和尼泊尔国王陛下政府关于两国边界问题的协定。
