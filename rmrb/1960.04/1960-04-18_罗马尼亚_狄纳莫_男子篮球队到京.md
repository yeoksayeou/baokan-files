Title: Romanian "Dinamo" Men's Basketball Team Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1960-04-18
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, April 17th. The Romanian "Dinamo" Sports Club men's basketball team, a group of fourteen led by team leader Silviu Țițescu, arrived in Beijing by plane today. The team leader of the Polish "Guards" men's basketball team, Witold Sieńkiewicz, and the Hungarian international basketball referee Vincei György, who was invited to participate in the first men's basketball competition of the socialist countries' public security sports organizations, also arrived on the same flight.

Mei Bin, Deputy Director of the National Committee of the China "Qianwei" Sports Association, and members of the China "Qianwei" Sports Association men's basketball team, welcomed them at the airport.

The deputy team leader of the Polish "Guards" men's basketball team, Stefan Askanas, and the team members who arrived earlier also came to the airport to greet them.

### 罗马尼亚“狄纳莫”男子篮球队到京

1960-04-18
第6版()
专栏：

    罗马尼亚“狄纳莫”男子篮球队到京
    据新华社17日讯　罗马尼亚“狄纳莫”体育俱乐部男子篮球队一行十四人，今天在领队西耳维乌·季采斯库率领下乘飞机到达北京。波兰“近卫军”男子篮球队领队维托耳德·谢恩凯维奇，和应邀参加社会主义国家公安体育组织第一届男子篮球比赛大会的匈牙利篮球国际裁判文蔡·捷尔吉，也同机到达。
    中国“前卫”体育协会全国委员会副主任梅彬和中国“前卫”体育协会男子篮球队的队员们，都到机场欢迎他们。
    早些时候到达的波兰“近卫军”男子篮球队副领队斯特凡·阿斯卡纳斯和队员们也到机场迎接。
