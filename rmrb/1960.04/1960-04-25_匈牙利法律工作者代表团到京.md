Title: Hungarian Legal Workers Delegation Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1960-04-25
Page: 2
Xinhua News Agency, April 24th. The Hungarian legal workers delegation of three members, led by Andor Ferenc, Deputy Minister of Justice of the Hungarian People's Republic, arrived in Beijing by plane this afternoon at the invitation of the China Political Science and Law Association for a visit to our country. The delegation members include Markoje Imie, Chief of the Administrative Department of the Central Committee of the Hungarian Socialist Workers' Party, and Katona Zoltán, Deputy Chief Prosecutor of the Hungarian People's Republic. Welcoming them at the airport were Wu Defeng, Vice President and First Secretary of the Secretariat of the China Political Science and Law Association, Zhang Zhirang, Zhang Youyu, Vice Presidents, Li Shiying, Yao Zhongming, Secretaries of the Secretariat, and representatives of the Sino-Hungarian Friendship Association.

### 匈牙利法律工作者代表团到京

1960-04-25
第2版()
专栏：

    匈牙利法律工作者代表团到京
    新华社24日讯　应中国政治法律学会邀请来我国访问的匈牙利法律工作者代表团一行三人，在团长、匈牙利人民共和国司法部副部长安多·费伦茨率领下，今天下午乘飞机到达北京。代表团团员有匈牙利社会主义工人党中央委员会行政部处长马尔科耶·伊米埃和匈牙利人民共和国副总检察长卡托纳·佐耳坦。到机场欢迎的有中国政治法律学会副会长兼书记处第一书记吴德峰，副会长张志让、张友渔，书记处书记李士英、姚仲明和中匈友协的代表。
