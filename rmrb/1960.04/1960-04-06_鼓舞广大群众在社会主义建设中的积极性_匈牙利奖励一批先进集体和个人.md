Title: Encouraging the Enthusiasm of the Broad Masses in Socialist Construction: Hungary Rewards a Group of Advanced Collectives and Individuals
Author:
Date: 1960-04-06
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, Budapest Telegram: To celebrate the 15th anniversary of Hungary's liberation, the Central Committee of the Hungarian Socialist Workers' Party and the Presidium of the Hungarian People's Republic have rewarded a number of advanced production units and workers, peasants, cultural workers, and Party and government cadres who have made significant achievements in socialist construction, and called on the broad masses to continuously accelerate socialist construction in their respective work posts.

The Central Committee of the Hungarian Socialist Workers' Party decided on March 30 to award the "Party Congress Red Flag" to nine units, including the Budapest Red Star Tractor Factory and the Tiszaszentimre State Farm, which achieved excellent results in the labor emulation drive to greet the Party's Seventh Congress, and to award the "Party Congress Certificate of Merit" to eighteen production units, including the Danube River Coal Mine United Enterprise and the Polgárdi Agricultural Machinery Station.

Dobi, Chairman of the Presidium of the Hungarian People's Republic, presented government awards at the National Assembly Building on April 1 to outstanding workers in various work posts, of whom one hundred and eighty-seven were awarded the Order of Labor, the Medal of Socialist Labor, and the Medal of Labor, respectively. Among them were miners, workshop directors, and actors.

In addition, First Deputy Minister of Defense, Lieutenant General Usta Gyula, was awarded the Order of the Red Banner; Peter Gyorgy, Director of the Central Bureau of Statistics, and another person were awarded the Order of the Red Banner of Labor; Antal Bartos, Deputy Minister of the Interior, and seventeen others were awarded the Order of the Red Star. Pal Losonczi, Minister of Agriculture of Hungary, presented the Medal of Socialist Labor and the Medal of Labor to seventy-one outstanding agricultural workers on behalf of the Presidium of the Hungarian People's Republic on April 1.

Dobi, Chairman of the Presidium of the Hungarian People's Republic, also presented government awards at the National Assembly Building on April 2 to leading cadres and members of cooperatives who have made outstanding achievements in the agricultural collectivization movement over the past decade. Twelve people were awarded the Order of Labor, sixty-eight people were awarded the Medal of Socialist Labor, and thirty-three people were awarded the Medal of Labor.

### 鼓舞广大群众在社会主义建设中的积极性  匈牙利奖励一批先进集体和个人

1960-04-06
第5版()
专栏：

    鼓舞广大群众在社会主义建设中的积极性
    匈牙利奖励一批先进集体和个人
    新华社布达佩斯电　为庆祝匈牙利解放十五周年，匈牙利社会主义工人党中央和匈牙利人民共和国主席团奖励了一批先进的生产单位和在社会主义建设中有显著成就的工人、农民、文化工作者和党政工作干部，并号召广大群众在不同的工作岗位上不断地加速社会主义建设。
    匈牙利社会主义工人党中央委员会3月30日曾决定授予在迎接党第七次代表大会的劳动竞赛中，取得优良成绩的布达佩斯红星拖拉机工厂、蒂萨赛恩提姆雷国营农场等九个单位以“党代表大会红旗”，授予多瑙河流域煤矿联合企业和波耳加尔德农业机器站等十八个生产单位以“党代表大会奖状”。
    匈牙利人民共和国主席团主席道比4月1日在国民议会大厦把政府奖授予在不同工作岗位上的杰出工作者，其中有一百八十七人分别荣获劳动勋章、社会主义劳动奖章和劳动奖章。他们当中有矿工、车间主任和演员。
    此外，国防部第一副部长乌斯塔·久洛中将荣获红旗勋章；中央统计局局长彼得·捷尔吉等二人荣获劳动红旗勋章；内务部副部长巴托什·安托尔等十七人荣获红星勋章。匈牙利农业部部长洛绍契·帕尔代表匈牙利人民共和国主席团在4月1日把社会主义劳动奖章和劳动奖章分别授予七十一位杰出的农业劳动者。
    匈牙利人民共和国主席团主席道比4月2日在国民议会大厦又把政府奖授予十年来在农业合作化运动中有杰出成绩的合作社的领导干部和社员。有十二人荣获劳动勋章，六十八人荣获社会主义劳动奖章，三十三人荣获劳动奖章。
