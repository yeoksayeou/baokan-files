Title: Chairman Liu Receives Three Brazilian Journalists
Author:
Date: 1960-04-16
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, April 15th: Chairman Liu Shaoqi met this afternoon with Paulo da Silveira, President of the "Ultima Hora" of Brazil, Ladoico Guimarães, a reporter from "União de Jornais," and Octávio Malta, Editor-in-Chief of "Ultima Hora," who are currently visiting our country, and had a friendly conversation with them.

Present at the meeting were Zhu Muzhi, Vice-Chairman of the All-China Journalists Association, Li Bingquan, Secretary of the Secretariat, and Shen Jian, Director of the Department of American and Australian Affairs of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs.

The Brazilian guests are in China for a visit at the invitation of the All-China Journalists Association and the Chinese People's Association for Cultural Exchange with Foreign Countries.

### 刘主席接见三位巴西新闻界人士

1960-04-16
第1版()
专栏：

    刘主席接见三位巴西新闻界人士
    新华社15日讯　刘少奇主席今天下午接见了正在我国访问的巴西“最后一点钟报”社长保罗·达西尔维拉，“报刊联营”记者拉多依科·吉马赖恩斯和“最后一点钟报”总编辑奥克塔维奥·马尔塔，并同他们进行了友好的谈话。
    接见时在座的，有中华全国新闻工作者协会副主席朱穆之、书记处书记李炳泉，外交部美澳司司长申健。
    巴西来宾是应中华全国新闻工作者协会和中国人民对外文化协会邀请来我国访问的。
