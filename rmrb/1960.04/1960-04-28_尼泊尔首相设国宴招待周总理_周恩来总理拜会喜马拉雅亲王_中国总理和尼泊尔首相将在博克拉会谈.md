Title: Nepalese Prime Minister Hosts State Banquet for Premier Zhou; Premier Zhou Enlai Visits Prince Himalaya; Chinese Premier and Nepalese Prime Minister to Hold Talks in Pokhara
Author:
Date: 1960-04-28
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Kathmandu, 26th – Premier of the State Council of the People's Republic of China, Zhou Enlai, and Prime Minister of the Kingdom of Nepal, B.P. Koirala, both expressed tonight their hope that the Chinese Premier's current visit to Nepal and the talks between the two sides will further consolidate and develop the friendship between the two countries. They expressed this wish in speeches at the state banquet held by Prime Minister Koirala tonight for Premier Zhou Enlai.

They both pointed out that the friendly cooperation between the two countries has been and will continue to be conducive to Asian-African solidarity and world peace.

The state banquet began at 7:30 PM local time today.

Among the guests attending the banquet were Chinese Vice Premier Chen Yi and other senior members of the Chinese Premier's entourage, as well as Chinese Ambassador to Nepal, Pan Zili.

Also attending the banquet were Nepalese cabinet ministers and deputy ministers, speakers of both houses of parliament, senior military officers, leaders of various political parties, and foreign diplomatic envoys stationed in Nepal.

After the banquet, the guests watched a performance of Nepalese folk songs and dances.

Xinhua News Agency, Kathmandu, 26th – Premier of the State Council of the People's Republic of China, Zhou Enlai, paid a visit to His Royal Highness Prince Himalaya Bir Bikram Shah Deva at 4:30 PM local time today.

Present at the meeting were Vice Premier Chen Yi and other senior members of the Chinese Premier's entourage, Nepalese Prime Minister B.P. Koirala, and Chinese Ambassador to Nepal, Pan Zili.

Earlier this afternoon, Premier Zhou Enlai, accompanied by Nepalese Prime Minister B.P. Koirala, signed the guest book at the Royal Palace.

Xinhua News Agency, Kathmandu, 27th – Premier Zhou Enlai, accompanied by Nepalese Prime Minister B.P. Koirala, flew this morning to Pokhara, a scenic resort about ninety-six miles northwest of Kathmandu.

Also accompanying them were Vice Premier and Foreign Minister Chen Yi, other members of the Chinese Premier's entourage, and Chinese Ambassador to Nepal, Pan Zili.

They will be touring and sightseeing in Pokhara.

The two Prime Ministers will also hold talks there.

### 尼泊尔首相设国宴招待周总理  周恩来总理拜会喜马拉雅亲王  中国总理和尼泊尔首相将在博克拉会谈

1960-04-28
第1版()
专栏：

    尼泊尔首相设国宴招待周总理
    周恩来总理拜会喜马拉雅亲王
    中国总理和尼泊尔首相将在博克拉会谈
    新华社加德满都26日电　中华人民共和国国务院总理周恩来和尼泊尔王国首相柯伊拉腊今天晚上都表示，希望中国总理目前对尼泊尔的访问和双方之间的会谈将进一步巩固和发展两国之间的友谊。他们在柯伊拉腊首相今晚为周恩来总理举行的国宴上讲话时表明了这一愿望。
    他们都指出，两国之间的友好合作过去是而且将来也是有利于亚非团结和世界和平的。
    国宴是在今天当地时间十九时三十分开始的。
    参加宴会的宾客中有中国副总理陈毅和中国总理随行人员中的其他高级人员以及中国驻尼泊尔大使潘自力。
    出席宴会的还有尼泊尔内阁大臣和副大臣们、议会两院议长、军队的高级军官们、各政党的领袖们和驻尼泊尔的各国外交使节。
    宴会结束后，来宾们观看了尼泊尔的民间歌舞表演。
    新华社加德满都26日电　中华人民共和国国务院总理周恩来在今天当地时间十六点三十分拜会了喜马拉雅·比尔·比克拉姆·沙阿·德瓦亲王殿下。
    拜会时在座的有副总理陈毅和中国总理随行人员中的其他高级人员，尼泊尔首相毕·普·柯伊拉腊，以及中国驻尼泊尔大使潘自力。
    在今天下午早些时候，周恩来总理在尼泊尔首相毕·普·柯伊拉腊陪同下，在王宫的来宾簿上签名留念。
    新华社加德满都27日电　周恩来总理今天上午在尼泊尔首相毕·普·柯伊拉腊陪同下，乘飞机前往加德满都西北约九十六英里的风景优美的胜地博克拉。
    同行的有副总理兼外交部长陈毅和中国总理的其他随行人员以及中国驻尼泊尔大使潘自力。
    他们将在博克拉进行游览参观。
    两位总理还将在那里举行会谈。
