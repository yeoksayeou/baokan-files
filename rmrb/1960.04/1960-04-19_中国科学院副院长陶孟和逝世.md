Title: Vice President of the Chinese Academy of Sciences, Tao Menghe, Passes Away
Author:
Date: 1960-04-19
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Shanghai, April 18th: Tao Menghe, Vice President of the Chinese Academy of Sciences, delegate to the National People’s Congress and Standing Member of the National Committee of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference, passed away in Shanghai at 12:12 noon on April 17, 1960, due to acute posterior myocardial infarction, despite all rescue efforts.

Mr. Tao Menghe was a native of Tianjin, Hebei Province, and was 73 years old.

The funeral committee for Mr. Tao Menghe has been formed, and the list is as follows: Guo Moruo, Yin Zanshun, Lu Dingyi, Li Weihan, Li Siguang, Wu Youxun, Yan Jici, Du Runsheng, Chen Boda, Chen Yuan, Zhou Enlai, Zhou Peiyuan, Zhou Taixuan, Lin Tie, Zhu Kezhen, Fan Wenlan, Ke Qingshi, Xu Bing, Kang Sheng, Zhang Jichun, Zhang Su, Zhang Jingfu, Zhang Xiruo, Peng Zhen, Tong Dizhou, Tang Yongtong, Yang Shixian, Pei Lisheng, Pan Zinian, Qian Sanqiang, Qian Changzhao, Nie Rongzhen.

### 中国科学院副院长陶孟和逝世

1960-04-19
第4版()
专栏：

    中国科学院副院长陶孟和逝世
    新华社上海18日电　中国科学院副院长、全国人民代表大会代表、政协全国委员会常务委员陶孟和，患急性后心机梗死症，经抢救医治无效，于1960年4月17日中午十二时十二分在上海逝世。
    陶孟和先生是河北省天津人，享年七十三岁。
    陶孟和先生治丧委员会已经组成，名单如下：郭沫若、尹赞勋、陆定一、李维汉、李四光、吴有训、严济慈、杜润生、陈伯达、陈垣、周恩来、周培源、周太玄、林铁、竺可桢、范文澜、柯庆施、徐冰、康生、张际春、张苏、张劲夫、张奚若、彭真、童第周、汤用彤、杨石先、裴丽生、潘梓年、钱三强、钱昌照、聂荣臻。
