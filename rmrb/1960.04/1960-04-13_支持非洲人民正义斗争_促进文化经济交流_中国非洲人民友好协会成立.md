Title: Support the Just Struggle of the African People and Promote Cultural and Economic Exchange: China-Africa People's Friendship Association Established
Author:
Date: 1960-04-13
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, April 12th: The China-Africa People's Friendship Association was established in Beijing this afternoon. The purpose of the association is to support the just struggle of the African people against imperialism and colonialism, to enhance the friendly relations between the people of China and African countries, and to promote cultural and economic exchanges between the people of China and African countries. The President of the Association is Liu Changsheng, and the Vice-Presidents are Bao Erhan, Qu Wu, Nan Hanchen, Xu Guangping, Liu Zizai, Xu Pingyu, Liu Xiyuan, and Zhang Tiesheng.

The China-Africa People's Friendship Association was jointly initiated by seventeen people's organizations in China. These organizations are: All-China Federation of Trade Unions, All-China Youth Federation, All-China Women's Federation, Chinese People's Committee for World Peace, Chinese Committee for Afro-Asian Solidarity, Chinese People's Association for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries, China Federation of Literary and Art Circles, Chinese Writers Association, All-China Journalists Association, China Council for the Promotion of International Trade, All-China Sports Federation, Red Cross Society of China, China Political Science and Law Society, Chinese People's Institute of Foreign Affairs, China Association for Science and Technology, Chinese Medical Association, and China Islamic Association. This afternoon, the heads of the seventeen organizations held a preparatory meeting and unanimously agreed to establish the China-Africa People's Friendship Association, and elected 133 council members of the China-Africa People's Friendship Association, including representatives from all aspects of China.

The China-Africa People's Friendship Association held its first council meeting this afternoon. The meeting unanimously adopted the charter of the China-Africa People's Friendship Association, elected the President, Vice-Presidents, Standing Council Members, Secretary-General, and Deputy Secretaries-General, and discussed the work plan of the Friendship Association for this year. The meeting approved the group members of the China-Africa People's Friendship Association. In addition to the above-mentioned seventeen founding groups, the All-China Federation of Industry and Commerce, the All-China Students' Federation, and the China Film Workers Association were also approved as group members.

### 支持非洲人民正义斗争　促进文化经济交流  中国非洲人民友好协会成立

1960-04-13
第1版()
专栏：

    支持非洲人民正义斗争　促进文化经济交流
    中国非洲人民友好协会成立
    新华社12日讯　中国非洲人民友好协会今天下午在北京成立。协会的宗旨是：支持非洲人民反对帝国主义、反对殖民主义的正义斗争，增进中国和非洲各国人民之间的友好关系，促进中国和非洲各国人民之间的文化经济交流。协会的会长是刘长胜，副会长是包尔汉、屈武、南汉宸、许广平、刘子载、徐平羽、刘西元、张铁生。
    中国非洲人民友好协会是由中国十七个人民团体联合发起成立的。这些团体是：中华全国总工会、中华全国青年联合会、中华人民共和国全国妇女联合会、中国人民保卫世界和平委员会、中国亚非团结委员会、中国人民对外文化协会、中国文学艺术界联合会、中国作家协会、中华全国新闻工作者协会、中国国际贸易促进委员会、中华全国体育总会、中国红十字总会、中国政治法律学会、中国人民外交学会、中华人民共和国科学技术协会、中华医学会、中国伊斯兰教协会。今天下午，十七个团体的负责人举行筹备会议，一致同意成立中国非洲人民友好协会，推选出中国非洲人民友好协会理事一百三十三人，其中包括中国各方面的代表人士。
    中国非洲人民友好协会今天下午举行第一次理事会议。会上一致通过了中国非洲人民友好协会章程，推选了会长、副会长、常务理事和总干事、副总干事，讨论了友好协会今年的工作规划。会议通过了中国非洲人民友好协会的团体会员。除上述十七个发起团体外，还通过中华全国工商业联合会、中华全国学生联合会、中国电影工作者联谊会为团体会员。
