Title: Iraqi Cultural Delegation Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1960-04-24
Page: 5

    Xinhua News Agency, April 23rd: The Iraqi Cultural Delegation, consisting of eight members and led by Dr. Mahdi Mattsumi, arrived in Beijing by train this afternoon. The delegation is visiting China at the invitation of the Commission for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries to discuss the 1960 executive plan for the China-Iraq Cultural Cooperation Agreement.
    Welcoming them at the station were Luo Jun, Vice-Chairman of the Commission for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries; Liu Ziyu, Deputy Director of the General Office of the Ministry of Education; Li Wu, Deputy Director of the Broadcasting Affairs Bureau; Zhang Suhua, Director of the International Liaison Department of the State Physical Culture and Sports Commission; Wu Qing, Director of the Commission for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries; Zhang Jie, Vice-Chairman of the Islamic Association of China; and Fang Ming, Vice-Chairman of the China Education Trade Union, and others.

### 伊拉克文化代表团到京

1960-04-24
第5版()
专栏：

    伊拉克文化代表团到京
    新华社23日讯　应对外文化联络委员会邀请来我国商定中伊文化合作协定1960年执行计划的伊拉克文化代表团一行八人，在团长马赫迪·马特苏米博士率领下，今天下午乘火车到达北京。
    到车站欢迎的，有对外文化联络委员会副主任罗俊，教育部办公厅副主任刘子余，广播事业局副局长李伍，体育运动委员会国际联络司司长张苏华，对外文化联络委员会司长吴青，伊斯兰教协会副主任张杰和中国教育工会副主席方明等。
