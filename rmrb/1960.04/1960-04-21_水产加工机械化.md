Mechanization of Aquatic Product Processing

    Workers at the Dongshan County Aquatic Product Processing Factory in Fujian Province have created various processing machines such as a universal steaming and baking oven, a fish essence fermentation oven, and an automatic cuttlefish killing machine. These have increased work efficiency by twenty-four to forty-five times, basically achieving the mechanization of aquatic product processing.

### 水产加工机械化

1960-04-21
第2版()
专栏：

    水产加工机械化
    福建省东山县水产加工厂职工创造了万能蒸烘炉、鱼精发酵炉、自动杀目鱼机等多种加工机械，工效分别提高二十四倍至四十五倍，基本实现水产加工机械化。
