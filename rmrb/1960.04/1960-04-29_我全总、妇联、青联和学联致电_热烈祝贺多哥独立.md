Title: ACFTU, Women's Federation, All-China Youth Federation, and All-China Students' Federation Send Telegrams Warmly Congratulating Togo's Independence
Author:
Date: 1960-04-29
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, 28th. The All-China Federation of Trade Unions (ACFTU) sent a telegram to the National Confederation of Togolese Workers on the 25th, extending warm congratulations from the Chinese workers to the Togolese workers and people on Togo's independence.

The ACFTU's telegram said: "Chinese workers wish the Togolese workers and people's national independence movement further development and greater achievements, and wish the friendship between the workers and people of China and Togo to develop."

The All-China Women's Federation of the People's Republic of China also sent a telegram to the Togolese Women's Federation, extending warm congratulations to the Togolese women on Togo's independence.

Xinhua News Agency, 28th. The All-China Youth Federation and the All-China Students' Federation jointly sent a telegram today to the Togolese Rural Youth Committee, the Togolese Youth Party, the Togolese Youth Solidarity Action Organization, the Togolese Labor Youth League, and the Association of Togolese Students in France, representing the Chinese youth and students in warmly congratulating Togo's independence.

In their telegram, the All-China Youth Federation and the All-China Students' Federation wished the Togolese people new achievements in their struggle to safeguard national independence, develop the national economy, and defend world peace, and wished the friendship between the youth and students of China and Togo to be continuously consolidated and developed.

### 我全总、妇联、青联和学联致电  热烈祝贺多哥独立

1960-04-29
第6版()
专栏：

    我全总、妇联、青联和学联致电
    热烈祝贺多哥独立
    新华社28日讯　中华全国总工会25日打电报给多哥工人全国联合会，代表中国工人向多哥工人和人民热烈祝贺多哥独立。
    全总的电报说：“中国工人预祝多哥工人和人民的民族独立运动进一步发展并获得更大的成就，祝中、多两国工人和人民的友谊得到发展。”
    中华人民共和国全国妇女联合会也打电报给多哥妇女联合会，向多哥妇女热烈祝贺多哥独立。
    新华社28日讯　中华全国青年联合会、中华全国学生联合会今天联名致电多哥农村青年委员会、多哥青年党、多哥青年团结行动组织，多哥劳动青年联盟和多哥留法学生联合会，代表中国青年和学生热烈祝贺多哥独立。
    全国青联和全国学联在电报中祝多哥人民在维护民族独立、发展民族经济和保卫世界和平的斗争中取得新的成就，并祝中国和多哥两国青年和学生之间的友谊不断巩固和发展。
