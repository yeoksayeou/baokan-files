Title: State Council Appointment and Removal List
Author:
Date: 1960-04-17
Page: 3

Xinhua News Agency, 15th. The 98th plenary meeting of the State Council held on March 28th passed a list of appointments and removals of personnel from state organs, universities and colleges, and embassies and consulates abroad, as follows:

    Appointed Peng Tao as Vice Chairman of the State Planning Commission;
    Wu Xiufeng as Counselor of the State Council;
    Lu Ping as President of Peking University;
    Wang Tao as President of Tongji University;
    Zhang Jun as President of Beijing Institute of Architectural Engineering;
    Zhang Xiuji, Saifeng, and Su Huan as Vice Presidents;
    Guo Linjun as President of Harbin Institute of Civil Engineering and Architecture, and Li Guodong and Liu Keping as Vice Presidents;
    Qian Yinglin as President of Beijing Railway Institute;
    Zang Zhenwu as Vice President of Beijing Institute of Agricultural Mechanization;
    Xu Huang as Counselor of the Embassy of the People's Republic of China in the German Democratic Republic;
    Xian Yi as Counselor of the Embassy of the People's Republic of China in the Czechoslovak Republic;
    Wang Chongli as Consul General of the People's Republic of China in Damascus.
    Removed Ma Yinchu from the position of President of Peking University, and Lu Ping from the position of Vice President;
    Qian Yinglin from the position of President of Tangshan Railway Institute;
    Li Zewang from the position of Counselor of the Embassy of the People's Republic of China in the Soviet Union;
    Shi Gu from the position of Consul General of the People's Republic of China in Damascus.

### 国务院任免名单

1960-04-17
第3版()
专栏：

    国务院任免名单
    新华社15日讯　国务院3月28日举行的第九十八次全体会议通过任免一批国家机关、高等院校和驻外大使馆工作人员以及领事人员，名单如下：
    任命彭涛为国家计划委员会副主任；
    吴秀峰为国务院参事；
    陆平为北京大学校长；
    王涛为同济大学校长；
    张君为北京建筑工业学院院长；
    张修己、塞风、苏桓为副院长；
    郭林军为哈尔滨建筑工程学院院长，李国栋、刘克屏为副院长；
    钱应麟为北京铁道学院院长；
    臧珍五为北京农业机械化学院副院长；
    徐晃为中华人民共和国驻德意志民主共和国大使馆参赞；
    冼依为中华人民共和国驻捷克斯洛伐克共和国大使馆参赞；
    王崇理为中华人民共和国驻大马士革总领事。
    免去马寅初的北京大学校长职务，陆平的副校长职务；
    钱应麟的唐山铁道学院院长职务；
    李则望的中华人民共和国驻苏维埃社会主义共和国联盟大使馆参赞职务；
    施谷的中华人民共和国驻大马士革总领事职务。
