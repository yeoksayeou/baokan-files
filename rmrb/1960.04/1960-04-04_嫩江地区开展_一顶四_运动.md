Author:
Date: 1960-04-04
Page: 2

Nenjiang Area Launches "One Tops Four" Campaign

    In the Nenjiang area of Heilongjiang Province, workers are widely launching the "One Tops Four" campaign, aiming for one person to do the work of four, one machine to do the work of four, and one tool to do the work of four. According to incomplete statistics, one factory, thirty workshops, one hundred and fifty-two teams, and two thousand nine hundred and twenty-six workers have respectively achieved "One Tops Four" to "One Tops Thirteen." The production level of many factories in the Nenjiang area has significantly increased, and a large number of factories have completed their first quarter plans ahead of schedule.

### 嫩江地区开展“一顶四”运动

1960-04-04
第2版()
专栏：简要新闻

    嫩江地区开展“一顶四”运动
    黑龙江省嫩江地区的职工，广泛开展以一人顶四人、一机顶四机、一刀顶四刀等为目标的“一顶四”运动。据不完全统计，现在已有一个工厂、三十个车间、一百五十二个小组、两千九百二十六名工人分别达到了“一顶四”到“一顶十三”。嫩江地区许多工厂的生产水平都显著上升，一大批工厂提前完成了首季计划。
