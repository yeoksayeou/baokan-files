Title: Soong Ching Ling Cables Popova and Andreyev to Congratulate International Labor Day

Author:

Date: 1960-04-30
Page: 2

Xinhua News Agency, 29th. Soong Ching Ling, President of the Sino-Soviet Friendship Association, cabled N.V. Popova, Chairman of the Presidium of the Union of Soviet Societies for Friendship and Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries, and Andreyev, Chairman of the Council of the Soviet-Chinese Friendship Association, on the 28th to congratulate them on International Labor Day. The text of the cable is as follows: Comrade Popova, Chairman of the Presidium of the Union of Soviet Societies for Friendship and Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries, Comrade Andreyev, Chairman of the Council of the Soviet-Chinese Friendship Association, Dear Comrade Popova! Dear Comrade Andreyev!

	On the occasion of the International Labor Day, on behalf of all members of the Sino-Soviet Friendship Association, I would like to extend my warmest congratulations to you and, through you, to the fraternal great Soviet people!

	Long live the eternal and unbreakable great friendship between the Chinese and Soviet peoples!

		Soong Ching Ling, President of the Sino-Soviet Friendship Association
					April 28, 1960

### 宋庆龄电波波娃和安德烈耶夫  祝贺“五一”国际劳动节

1960-04-30
第2版()
专栏：

    宋庆龄电波波娃和安德烈耶夫
    祝贺“五一”国际劳动节
    新华社29日讯　中苏友好协会总会会长宋庆龄28日致电苏联对外友好和文化协会联合会主席团主席波波娃、苏中友好协会理事会主席安德烈耶夫，祝贺“五一”国际劳动节。电文如下：苏联对外友好和文化协会联合会主席团主席波波娃同志苏中友好协会理事会主席安德烈耶夫同志亲爱的波波娃同志！亲爱的安德烈耶夫同志！
    值此五一国际劳动节之际，谨代表中苏友好协会全体会员，向你们，并通过你们，向兄弟般的伟大苏联人民致以最热烈的祝贺！
    中苏两国人民永恒的牢不可破的伟大友谊万岁！
                中苏友好协会总会会长　宋庆龄
                          一九六○年四月二十八日
