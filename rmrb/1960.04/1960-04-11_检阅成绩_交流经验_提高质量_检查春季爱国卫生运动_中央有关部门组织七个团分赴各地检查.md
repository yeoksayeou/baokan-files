Title: Review Achievements, Exchange Experiences, Improve Quality, Inspect the Spring Patriotic Health Campaign: Relevant Central Departments Organize Seven Groups to Inspect Various Regions
Author:
Date: 1960-04-11
Page: 5

    Review Achievements Exchange Experiences Improve Quality
    Inspect the Spring Patriotic Health Campaign
    Relevant Central Departments Organize Seven Groups to Inspect Various Regions

    Xinhua News Agency, 10th - The upsurge of the spring patriotic health campaign has formed nationwide.
    In order to review achievements, exchange experiences, and further push the movement to a higher stage, the Central Patriotic Health Campaign Committee, the Ministry of Health, together with relevant departments, organizations, and the office of the CCP Central Committee's Schistosomiasis Prevention Nine-Person Group, organized a nationwide general inspection of the spring patriotic health campaign. The inspection is divided into seven groups, which will successively begin in various regions from the 10th.
    More than 120 responsible comrades and cadres from eighteen units, including the All-China Federation of Trade Unions, the Central Committee of the Communist Youth League, the All-China Women's Federation, the Ministry of Agriculture, the Ministry of Commerce, the Ministry of Labor, the Ministry of Construction Engineering, the Ministry of Education, the Ministry of Culture, the Ministry of Food, the State Physical Culture and Sports Commission, and the Health Department of the General Logistics Department of the People's Liberation Army, are participating in this inspection work, and a portion of representatives from the National People's Congress and members of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference have been invited to participate.
    Shao Lizi, member of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress, Liu Qingyang, representative of the National People's Congress, Xi Zhanyuan, Secretary of the Secretariat of the All-China Federation of Trade Unions, Tian Xiujuan, Secretary of the Secretariat of the All-China Women's Federation, Xu Yunbei, He Biao, and Qian Xinzhong, Vice Ministers of the Ministry of Health, will lead the various inspection groups to Central South, Beijing-Shanghai-Tianjin, Northeast, Northwest, East China, North China, and Southwest regions to conduct inspections.
    The Central Patriotic Health Campaign Committee invited the inspection participants to a symposium on April 6th. The meeting pointed out that the general inspection is a great promotion, a crucial approach to learning, summarizing, and promoting advanced experiences, resolving problems existing in the movement, and improving the quality of the movement. The inspection must be carried out in a planned and focused manner, according to the "Notice on Organizing a General Inspection of the Spring Patriotic Health Campaign" issued by the Central Patriotic Health Campaign Committee and the Ministry of Health.
    Various regions have also organized health inspection groups at all levels, led by responsible comrades from the Party and government, to conduct a general inspection of the spring patriotic health campaign.
    This general inspection will conclude at the end of April.

### 检阅成绩  交流经验  提高质量  检查春季爱国卫生运动  中央有关部门组织七个团分赴各地检查

1960-04-11
第5版()
专栏：

    检阅成绩  交流经验  提高质量
    检查春季爱国卫生运动
    中央有关部门组织七个团分赴各地检查
    新华社10日讯　春季爱国卫生运动高潮已经在全国范围内形成。
    为了检阅成绩，交流经验，进一步把运动推向更高的阶段，中央爱国卫生运动委员会、卫生部会同有关部门、团体和中共中央防治血吸虫病九人小组办公室，组织了全国春季爱国卫生运动大检查。检查分七个团，从10日起，在各地陆续开始进行。
    全国总工会、共青团中央、全国妇联、农业部、商业部、劳动部、建筑工程部、教育部、文化部、粮食部、国家体育运动委员会、人民解放军总后勤部卫生部等十八个单位的一百二十余名负责同志和干部参加这次检查工作，并邀请了全国人民代表大会的一部分代表和政协委员参加。
    全国人民代表大会常务委员会委员邵力子，全国人民代表大会代表刘清扬，全国总工会书记处书记郗占元，全国妇联书记处书记田秀涓，卫生部副部长徐运北、贺彪、钱信忠等，将分别率领各个检查团前往中南、京沪津、东北、西北、华东、华北、西南等地区进行检查。
    中央爱国卫生运动委员会曾于4月6日邀请参加检查的人员举行了座谈会。会议指出，大检查就是大促进，就是学习、总结和推广先进经验，就是解决运动中存在的问题和提高运动质量的重要作法，必须根据中央爱国卫生运动委员会和卫生部日前发出的“关于组织春季爱国卫生运动大检查的通知”，有计划、有重点地进行检查。
    各地区也都组织了各级卫生检查团，由党、政府的负责同志领导，进行春季爱国卫生运动大检查。
    这次大检查将于4月底结束。
