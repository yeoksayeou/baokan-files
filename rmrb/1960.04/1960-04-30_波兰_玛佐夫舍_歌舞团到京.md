Title: Polish "Mazowsze" Song and Dance Ensemble Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1960-04-30
Page: 7

    Polish "Mazowsze" Song and Dance Ensemble Arrives in Beijing
    Xinhua News Agency, April 29th: The entire Polish "Mazowsze" Song and Dance Ensemble arrived in Beijing by train on the 28th. The song and dance ensemble is returning to our country after visiting Japan and will participate in activities in Beijing to celebrate International Labor Day on May 1st.

### 波兰“玛佐夫舍”歌舞团到京

1960-04-30
第7版()
专栏：

    波兰“玛佐夫舍”歌舞团到京
    新华社29日讯　波兰“玛佐夫舍”歌舞团全体团员在28日乘火车到达北京。歌舞团是在访问日本后重返我国的，他们将在北京参加庆祝“五一”国际劳动节的活动。
