Title: CPC Central Committee Holds Commemorative Meeting Today to Solemnly Commemorate the 90th Anniversary of Lenin's Birth
Author:
Date: 1960-04-22
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, April 21st: The Central Committee of the Communist Party of China will hold a commemorative meeting on the afternoon of the 22nd to solemnly commemorate the 90th anniversary of the birth of the great proletarian revolutionary teacher Lenin. The Central People's Broadcasting Station will broadcast the live proceedings of the commemorative meeting from 2:50 PM on the 22nd, using the frequency of its first program. Beijing Television will also broadcast simultaneously.

### 中共中央今日举行纪念大会  隆重纪念列宁诞生九十周年

1960-04-22
第1版()
专栏：

    中共中央今日举行纪念大会
    隆重纪念列宁诞生九十周年
    新华社21日讯　中国共产党中央委员会将在22日下午举行纪念大会，隆重纪念伟大的无产阶级革命导师列宁诞生九十周年。中央人民广播电台从22日下午十四点五十分开始，用第一种节目的频率转播纪念大会的实况。北京电视台也同时转播。
