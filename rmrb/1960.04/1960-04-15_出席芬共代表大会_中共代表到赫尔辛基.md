Title: To Attend the Finnish Communist Party Congress, Chinese Communist Party Representative Arrives in Helsinki
Author:
Date: 1960-04-15
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, Helsinki, April 14th: Wu Zhipu, the representative of the Chinese Communist Party attending the 12th Congress of the Finnish Communist Party, arrived in Helsinki today.

　　Finnish Communist Party Chairman Aimo Aaltonen and other leaders of the Finnish Communist Party were at the airport to welcome him.

### 出席芬共代表大会  中共代表到赫尔辛基

1960-04-15
第6版()
专栏：

    出席芬共代表大会
    中共代表到赫尔辛基
    新华社14日讯　赫尔辛基消息：出席芬兰共产党第十二届代表大会的中国共产党代表吴芝圃今天到达赫尔辛基。
    到机场欢迎的有芬兰共产党主席艾摩·阿尔托宁和芬共其它领导人。
