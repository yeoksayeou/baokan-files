Title: Picture
Author: Qing Yun
Date: 1960-04-27
Page: 2

    A pair of blast furnace brothers,
    Shoulder to shoulder, spurring on the warhorses.
    The elder brother reaches out to pull the younger,
    The younger brother whips to urge on the elder.
    Advancing together on the road to high yield,
    We will certainly seize the victory red banner.
                                By Qing Yun
