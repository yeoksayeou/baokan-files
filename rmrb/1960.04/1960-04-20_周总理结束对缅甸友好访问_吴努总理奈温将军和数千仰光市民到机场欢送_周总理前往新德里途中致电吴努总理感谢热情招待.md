Title: Premier Zhou Ends Friendly Visit to Burma; Prime Minister U Nu, General Ne Win, and Thousands of Rangoon Citizens See Him Off at the Airport; Premier Zhou Cables Prime Minister U Nu from Route to New Delhi, Thanking Him for Cordial Hospitality
Author:
Date: 1960-04-20
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Rangoon, April 19th: Premier of the State Council of the People's Republic of China, Zhou Enlai, Vice Premier and Minister of Foreign Affairs Marshal Chen Yi, and their entourage concluded their four-day friendly visit to Burma and left Rangoon by plane this morning for New Delhi.

Premier Zhou Enlai, accompanied by Burmese Prime Minister U Nu and Chief of Staff of the Defense Forces General Ne Win, arrived at the airport at 10:50 AM Rangoon time. The three to four thousand Rangoon citizens gathered at the airport waved to them and shouted, "Long Live Sino-Burmese Friendship!"

Premier Zhou Enlai reviewed the guard of honor. He shook hands and bid farewell to Burmese government officials, prominent figures from all walks of life, diplomats from the Chinese Embassy in Burma, and diplomatic envoys from various countries stationed in Rangoon.

Also present at the airport to see Premier Zhou Enlai off were: Chief Justice of the Supreme Court U Myint Thein, Minister of Finance and Revenue Thakin Tin, Minister of Transportation Bo Min Gaung, Minister of Justice U Aye Maung, Deputy Chief of Staff of the Defense Forces (Army) Brigadier Aung Gyi, Burmese Ambassador to China Kyaw Winn, Chairman of the Anti-Fascist People's Freedom League U Ba Swe, Vice Chairman of the Anti-Fascist People's Freedom League U Kyaw Yein, and other senior government officials and prominent social figures.

Burmese Prime Minister U Nu, General Ne Win, and other senior government officials accompanied Premier Zhou Enlai to the waiting special plane. Burmese girls stepped forward to present flowers to Premier Zhou Enlai and Vice Premier Chen Yi.

Burmese Prime Minister U Nu and General Ne Win repeatedly shook hands with Premier Zhou Enlai and wished him a pleasant journey.

Xinhua News Agency, Rangoon, April 19th: Premier Zhou Enlai sent a telegram to Burmese Prime Minister U Nu from his special plane en route from Rangoon to New Delhi today, expressing his gratitude. The full text of the telegram is as follows:

To His Excellency Prime Minister U Nu of the Union of Burma, Rangoon:

Dear Prime Minister U Nu,

After a four-day friendly visit in your beautiful country, we are now about to leave your land. Please allow me, in my own name and on behalf of Vice Premier Chen Yi, to express our heartfelt gratitude to Your Excellency and the Burmese government and people for your kind invitation and warm hospitality. We are very pleased that through this visit, the friendly relations between China and Burma have been further developed. I wish Burma prosperity, the Burmese people happiness, and Your Excellency and Madam Nu good health.

Zhou Enlai
Premier of the State Council of the People's Republic of China

April 19, 1960, en route from Rangoon to New Delhi

### 周总理结束对缅甸友好访问  吴努总理奈温将军和数千仰光市民到机场欢送  周总理前往新德里途中致电吴努总理感谢热情招待

1960-04-20
第1版()
专栏：

    周总理结束对缅甸友好访问
    吴努总理奈温将军和数千仰光市民到机场欢送
    周总理前往新德里途中致电吴努总理感谢热情招待
    新华社仰光19日电　中华人民共和国国务院总理周恩来、副总理兼外交部长陈毅元帅和他们的随行人员结束了历时四天对缅甸的友好访问，在今天上午乘飞机离开仰光前往新德里。
    周恩来总理在缅甸总理吴努和国防军总参谋长奈温将军的陪同下，在仰光时间十时五十分到达机场。聚集在机场上的三、四千仰光市民向他们招手，并高呼“中缅友谊万岁！”
    周恩来总理检阅了仪仗队。他同缅甸政府官员、各界著名人士和中国驻缅甸大使馆外交官和各国驻仰光外交使节握手道别。
  前往机场为周恩来总理送行的还有：最高法院首席法官吴敏登，财政和税务部长德钦丁，运输部长波木昂，司法部长吴埃貌，国防军副总参谋长（陆军）昂季准将，缅甸驻中国大使叫温，缅甸巩固的反法西斯人民自由同盟主席吴巴瑞，缅甸巩固的反法西斯人民自由同盟副主席吴觉迎以及政府其他高级官员和社会著名人士。
    缅甸总理吴努、奈温将军和政府其他高级官员陪同周恩来总理前往等候着的专机。缅甸姑娘们上前向周恩来总理和陈毅副总理献花。
    缅甸总理吴努和奈温将军一再同周恩来总理握手，并祝他一路顺风。
    新华社仰光19日电　周恩来总理今天乘专机离开仰光前往新德里途中打电报给缅甸总理吴努表示感谢。电报全文如下：仰光缅甸联邦吴努总理亲爱的吴努总理阁下，
    我们在你们的美好的国家进行了四天的友好访问以后，现在就要离开你们的国土了。请允许我，以我自己和陈毅副总理的名义，向阁下和缅甸政府和人民表示衷心的感谢，感谢你们的殷勤的邀请和热情的招待。我们感到很高兴，通过这次访问，中缅两国之间的友好关系有了进一步的发展。祝缅甸繁荣，缅甸人民幸福，阁下和努夫人身体健康。
      中华人民共和国国务院总理　周恩来
    1960年4月19日在从仰光前往新德里途中
