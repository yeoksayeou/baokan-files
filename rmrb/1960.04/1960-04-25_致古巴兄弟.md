Title: To Our Cuban Brothers
Author: Cao Hongsen
Date: 1960-04-25
Page: 8

A column: Supporting the Cuban People

    To Our Cuban Brothers
    Cao Hongsen, Shijingshan Iron and Steel Plant

    Fight on, Latin America!
    Fight on, Cuban brothers!
    The six hundred and fifty million Chinese people are with you,
    Resolutely defend national independence.

    Fight on, Latin America!
    Fight on, Cuban brothers!
    Peace-loving people all over the world,
    Have built an impregnable wall with blood.
    If warmongers dare to invade you,
    We will defend justice with our blood.

    Fight on, Latin America!
    Fight on, Cuban brothers!
    The revolutionary fire has already been ignited,
    We will never allow war maniacs to extinguish it.
    The beautiful sugarcane forest has grown tall,
    We will never allow bombs to destroy it again.

    Fight on, Latin America!
    Fight on, Cuban brothers!
    We will use the fighting pen,
    To defend your victory.
    We warmly respond to the initiative of Rafael Alberti and Guillén,
    And donate money to buy the Poetry Airplane.

    Fight on, Latin America!
    Fight on, Cuban brothers!
    We will use the Poetry Airplane,
    To defend every inch of your land.
    We will use the Poetry Airplane,
    To defend your peace, happiness, and justice!

### 致古巴兄弟
曹洪森
1960-04-25
第8版()
专栏：支援古巴人民

    致古巴兄弟
    石景山钢铁厂　曹洪森
    战斗吧，拉丁美洲！
    战斗吧，古巴兄弟！
    六亿五千万中国人民和你在一起，
    坚决保卫民族独立。
    战斗吧，拉丁美洲！
    战斗吧，古巴兄弟！
    全世界爱好和平的人民，
    用鲜血筑起了铜墙铁壁。
    战争贩子胆敢侵犯你，
    我们就用鲜血来保卫正义。
    战斗吧，拉丁美洲！
    战斗吧，古巴兄弟！
    革命的烈火已经熊熊燃起，
    决不允许战争狂人把它熄。
    美丽的蔗林已经高高长起，
    决不允许炸弹再把它摧毁。
    战斗吧，拉丁美洲！
    战斗吧，古巴兄弟！
    我们要用战斗的笔，
    来保卫你们的胜利。
    我们热烈响应拉斐尔·阿尔贝蒂和纪廉的倡议，
    捐款购买诗歌号飞机。
    战斗吧，拉丁美洲！
    战斗吧，古巴兄弟！
    我们要用诗歌号飞机，
    保卫你每一寸土地。
    我们要用诗歌号飞机，
    保卫你的和平幸福和正义！
