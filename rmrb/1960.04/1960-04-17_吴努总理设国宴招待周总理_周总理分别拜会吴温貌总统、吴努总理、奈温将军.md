Title: Prime Minister U Nu Hosts State Banquet in Honor of Premier Zhou; Premier Zhou Pays Respectful Visits to President U Win Maung, Prime Minister U Nu, and General Ne Win
Author:
Date: 1960-04-17
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Rangoon, April 16th: Burmese Prime Minister U Nu and his wife hosted a grand state banquet at 7:00 PM today in honor of Premier Zhou Enlai. The grand state banquet proceeded in a warm and friendly atmosphere.

    Prime Minister U Nu and Premier Zhou Enlai both delivered speeches at the state banquet (full text on page 3).

    Among those attending the state banquet were Vice Premier Chen Yi, Vice Minister of Foreign Affairs Zhang Hanfu, all other accompanying personnel, and Chinese Ambassador to Burma Li Yimang.

    On the Burmese side, those attending the state banquet included: former Prime Minister and Chief of Staff of the Defense Forces General Ne Win, Chief Justice of the Supreme Court U Myint Thein, Speaker of the Chamber of Nationalities Sao Khun Kyi, Speaker of the Chamber of Deputies Mann Ba Swe, Cabinet Ministers Sao Khun Cho, Thakin Tin, Bo Min Gaung, U Aye Maung, U Tun, Deputy Chief of Staff of the Defense Forces (Army) Brigadier Aung Gyi, Deputy Chief of Staff of the Defense Forces (Navy) Commodore Than Pe, as well as other senior government officials and senior military officers, leaders of various Burmese political organizations, and prominent figures from all walks of life.

    The Burmese Ambassador to China, Kyao Win, also attended the state banquet.

    Diplomatic envoys from various countries stationed in Rangoon also attended the state banquet.

    Xinhua News Agency, Rangoon, April 16th: Premier Zhou Enlai and Vice Premier Chen Yi paid a visit to Burmese President U Win Maung this morning. Present were Chinese Ambassador to Burma Li Yimang and Burmese Ambassador to China Kyao Win.

    Xinhua News Agency, Rangoon, April 16th: Premier Zhou Enlai and Vice Premier Chen Yi paid a visit to Burmese Prime Minister U Nu this morning.

    Present during the visit were Burmese Minister of Foreign Affairs Sao Khun Cho, Minister of Finance and Revenue, National Planning Thakin Tin, Minister of Transport, Posts and Telegraphs, Maritime Shipping and Civil Aviation, Public Works, Housing and Construction Bo Min Gaung.

    Also present were Chinese Ambassador to Burma Li Yimang and Burmese Ambassador to China Kyao Win.

    Xinhua News Agency, Rangoon, April 16th: Premier Zhou Enlai, Vice Premier Chen Yi, and Vice Minister of Foreign Affairs Zhang Hanfu paid a visit to former Burmese Prime Minister and Chief of Staff of the Burmese Defense Forces General Ne Win this afternoon.

    Present during the visit were Chinese Ambassador to Burma Li Yimang and Burmese Ambassador to China Kyao Win.

    Xinhua News Agency, Rangoon, April 16th: The Chief of Staff of the Burmese Defense Forces General Ne Win and his wife had dinner with Premier Zhou Enlai, Vice Premier Chen Yi, and Vice Minister of Foreign Affairs Zhang Hanfu at the hotel last night.

    Also present were: Former Burmese Deputy Prime Minister U Lun Pe, U Khin Tun Aung, who accompanied General Ne Win on his visit to China, U Chit Thoung, the representative of the Burmese government who attended the 10th anniversary celebrations of the founding of the People's Republic of China, and others.

    Also present were: Chinese Ambassador to Burma Li Yimang and Burmese Ambassador to China Kyao Win. (Attached picture)
    Right picture: Premier Zhou Enlai received a warm welcome from Burmese Prime Minister U Nu and people from all walks of life in Burma upon his arrival in Rangoon. The picture shows Prime Minister U Nu (right) shaking hands warmly with Premier Zhou. Xinhua News Agency reporter Du Xiuxian (faxed photograph)

### 吴努总理设国宴招待周总理  周总理分别拜会吴温貌总统、吴努总理、奈温将军

1960-04-17
第1版()
专栏：

    吴努总理设国宴招待周总理
    周总理分别拜会吴温貌总统、吴努总理、奈温将军
    新华社仰光16日电　缅甸联邦总理吴努和夫人于今天晚间七时举行盛大的国宴招待周恩来总理。这个盛大的国宴是在热烈和友好的气氛中进行的。
    吴努总理和周恩来总理都在国宴上讲了话（全文见第三版）。
    出席国宴的有副总理陈毅、外交部副部长章汉夫和所有其他随行人员以及中国驻缅甸大使李一氓。
    缅甸方面出席国宴的有：前总理、国防军总参谋长奈温将军，最高法院首席法官吴敏登，民族院议长藻昆基，代表院议长曼巴赛，内阁部长藻昆卓、德钦丁、波木昂、吴埃貌、吴敦，国防军副总参谋长（陆军）昂季准将，国防军副总参谋长（海军）丹佩准将以及政府的其他高级官员和高级军官，缅甸各政治组织的领袖和各界著名人士。
    缅甸驻中国大使叫温也出席了国宴。
    出席国宴的还有各国驻仰光的外交使节。
    新华社仰光16日电　周恩来总理和陈毅副总理今天早晨拜会了缅甸总统吴温貌。在场的有中国驻缅甸大使李一氓和缅甸驻中国大使叫温。
    新华社仰光16日电　周恩来总理和陈毅副总理今天上午拜会了缅甸总理吴努。
    拜会时在场的有缅甸外交部长藻昆卓，财政和税务、国家计划部长德钦丁，运输、邮电、海运和民用航空、公共工程、房屋和建设部长波木昂。
    在场的还有中国驻缅甸大使李一氓和缅甸驻中国大使叫温。
    新华社仰光16日电　周恩来总理、陈毅副总理和外交部副部长章汉夫今天下午拜会前缅甸总理、缅甸联邦国防军总参谋长奈温将军。
    拜会时在座的有中国驻缅甸大使李一氓和缅甸驻中国大使叫温。
    新华社仰光16日电　缅甸联邦国防军总参谋长奈温将军和他的夫人昨天晚间同周恩来总理、陈毅副总理和外交部副部长章汉夫在宾馆共进晚餐。
    在座的有：前缅甸联邦副总理吴伦博和陪同奈温将军访问中国的吴千吞昂，还有作为缅甸政府代表参加中华人民共和国建国十周年庆典的代表吴漆东以及其他的人。
    在座的还有：中国驻缅甸大使李一氓和缅甸驻中国大使叫温。（附图片）
