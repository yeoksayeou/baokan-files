Title: Mongolian Great People's Hural Scheduled for June Elections
Author:
Date: 1960-04-26
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, Ulaanbaatar, The Presidium of the Great People's Hural of the Mongolian People's Republic issued a directive, setting the date for the election of the Fourth Great People's Hural on June 19 this year. The term of office of the current Great People's Hural will expire on June 16.

### 蒙古大人民呼拉尔定于六月举行选举

1960-04-26
第5版()
专栏：

    蒙古大人民呼拉尔定于六月举行选举
    新华社乌兰巴托讯　蒙古人民共和国大人民呼拉尔主席团颁布指示，定于今年6月19日进行第四届大人民呼拉尔选举，本届大人民呼拉尔的任期到6月16日届满。
