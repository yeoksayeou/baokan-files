Title: Soviet Komsomol University Workers Delegation Returns Home
Author:
Date: 1960-04-15
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, April 13th – The Soviet Komsomol University Workers Delegation and the Young Pioneers Workers Delegation, after paying a friendly visit to our country for more than twenty days at the invitation of the Central Committee of the Chinese Communist Youth League, left Beijing by plane today to return home.

Wang Zhaohua, Secretary of the Secretariat of the Central Committee of the Communist Youth League, and others were at the airport to see them off.

### 苏联共青团大学工作者代表团回国

1960-04-15
第4版()
专栏：

    苏联共青团大学工作者代表团回国
    新华社13日讯　苏联共青团大学工作者代表团和少年工作者代表团应中国共产主义青年团中央委员会邀请在我国进行了二十多天的友好访问后，今天乘飞机离开北京回国。
    到机场欢送的有共青团中央书记处书记王照华等。
