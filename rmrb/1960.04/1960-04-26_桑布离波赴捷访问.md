Title: Sambu Leaves for Czechoslovakia for a Visit
Author:
Date: 1960-04-26
Page: 5
Xinhua News Agency, Warsaw, April 25th: Sambu, Chairman of the Presidium of the Great People's Hural of Mongolia, and his entourage, left Warsaw by special plane this morning for a visit to Czechoslovakia.
  Polish Party and state leaders Gomulka, Cyrankiewicz, Jędrychowski, Kliszko, Ochab, Rapacki, Spychalski, Zambrowski, Zawadzki, as well as representatives from all walks of life in Warsaw, totaling over a thousand people, went to the airport to see them off.
  Sambu and his party arrived in Poland on the 20th for a visit. During their stay in Poland, they visited Warsaw, Silesia and Gdansk. The Chairman of the Polish Council of State, Zawadzki, held a banquet on the 20th to entertain Sambu. The Mongolian Ambassador to Poland, Battaochir, held a reception on the 24th for Sambu's visit to Poland, which was attended by many Polish Party and state leaders.
  Xinhua News Agency, Prague, April 25th: Sambu, Chairman of the Presidium of the Great People's Hural of Mongolia, and his entourage, arrived in Prague by plane on the morning of the 25th. Sambu is paying a friendly visit at the invitation of the President of the Czechoslovak Republic, Novotny.
  Czech Party and government leaders Novotny, Fierlinger, Hendrych, David and others were at the airport to welcome them.
  Novotny and Sambu spoke at the airport one after the other.
  President Novotny received Sambu on the same day.

### 桑布离波赴捷访问

1960-04-26
第5版()
专栏：

    桑布离波赴捷访问
    新华社华沙25日电　蒙古大人民呼拉尔主席团主席桑布及其随行人员今天上午乘专机离华沙前往捷克斯洛伐克访问。
  波兰党和国家领导人哥穆尔卡、西伦凯维兹、英德里霍夫斯基、克利什科、奥哈布、腊帕茨基、斯彼恰尔斯基、萨姆布罗夫斯基、萨瓦茨基以及一些国家驻波兰外交使节，华沙各界人民代表共千余人前往机场欢送。
    桑布等一行是在20日到波兰访问的。在波兰期间，他们访问了华沙、西里西亚和革但斯克等地。波兰国务委员会主席萨瓦茨基曾于20日举行宴会，招待桑布。蒙古驻波兰大使巴特奥奇尔在24日为桑布访问波兰举行了招待会，许多波兰党和国家领导人出席了招待会。
    新华社布拉格25日电　蒙古大人民呼拉尔主席团主席桑布和他的随行人员，25日上午乘飞机抵达布拉格，桑布是应捷克斯洛伐克共和国总统诺沃提尼的邀请，前来进行友好访问的。
    到机场欢迎的有捷克斯洛伐克党政领导人诺沃提尼、费林格、亨德里赫、戴维等人。
    诺沃提尼和桑布先后在机场上讲了话。
    诺沃提尼总统在当天接见了桑布。
