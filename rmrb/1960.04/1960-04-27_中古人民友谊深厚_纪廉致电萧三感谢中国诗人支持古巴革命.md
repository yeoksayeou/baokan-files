Title: Profound Friendship Between the Chinese and Cuban People: Guillén Sends Telegram to Xiao San, Thanking Chinese Poets for Supporting the Cuban Revolution
Author:
Date: 1960-04-27
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, April 26th: The poet Xiao San received a telegram from the famous Cuban poet Guillén on the 25th. Guillén's telegram said: "We express our deep gratitude to the Chinese artists, poets, and intellectuals for their generous help in purchasing the 'Poetry Aircraft'. This is an outstanding contribution to defending the Cuban liberation revolution."

### 中古人民友谊深厚  纪廉致电萧三感谢中国诗人支持古巴革命

1960-04-27
第6版()
专栏：

    中古人民友谊深厚
    纪廉致电萧三感谢中国诗人支持古巴革命
    据新华社26日讯　诗人萧三25日收到了古巴著名诗人纪廉发来的一份电报。纪廉的电报说：“我们对中国艺术家、诗人和知识分子慷慨地帮助购买‘诗歌号飞机’表示深深的感谢。这是对保卫古巴解放革命的卓越贡献。”
