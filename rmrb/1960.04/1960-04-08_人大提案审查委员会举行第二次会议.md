Title: NPC Proposal Examination Committee Holds Second Meeting
Author:
Date: 1960-04-08
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, 7th — The Proposal Examination Committee of the Second National People's Congress held its second meeting this morning.
　　The meeting examined the 46 proposals received, one by one, and put forward examination opinions.
　　The meeting also discussed and approved the Proposal Examination Committee’s report on the examination of the proposals, which is to be submitted to the Congress for deliberation.

### 人大提案审查委员会举行第二次会议

1960-04-08
第1版()
专栏：

    人大提案审查委员会举行第二次会议
    新华社7日讯　第二届全国人民代表大会第二次会议提案审查委员会，今天上午举行了第二次会议。
    会议对收到的四十六件提案逐案进行了审查，并提出了审查意见。
    会议还讨论通过了准备提请大会审议的提案审查委员会关于提案的审查报告。
