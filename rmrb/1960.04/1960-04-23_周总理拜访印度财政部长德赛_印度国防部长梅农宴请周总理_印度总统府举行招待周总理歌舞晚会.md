Title: Premier Zhou Visits Indian Finance Minister Desai; Indian Defense Minister Menon Hosts a Banquet for Premier Zhou; Indian Presidential Palace Holds Song and Dance Soiree in Honor of Premier Zhou
Author:
Date: 1960-04-23
Page: 3

Xinhua News Agency, New Delhi, April 22nd: Premier Zhou Enlai visited Indian Finance Minister Morarji Desai this afternoon.

    Present at the visit were Chinese Vice Premier and Foreign Minister Chen Yi, and Vice Foreign Minister Zhang Hanfu.

    Indian Ambassador to China G. Parthasarathi was also present.

Xinhua News Agency, New Delhi, April 22nd: Indian Defense Minister Krishna Menon hosted a banquet at his residence today to entertain Chinese Premier Zhou Enlai and Vice Premier Chen Yi.

    Attending the banquet were Chinese Vice Foreign Minister Zhang Hanfu, Assistant Foreign Minister Qiao Guanhua, and Chinese Ambassador to India Pan Zili.

Xinhua News Agency, New Delhi, April 21st: Premier Zhou Enlai received former Indian Ambassador to China R.K. Nehru and his wife at 10:30 pm tonight.

    Vice Premier Chen Yi and Vice Foreign Minister Zhang Hanfu were present at the reception.

Xinhua News Agency, New Delhi, April 22nd: A song and dance soiree was held at the Indian Presidential Palace tonight to entertain Chinese Premier Zhou Enlai.

    Indian President Rajendra Prasad, Vice President Sarvepalli Radhakrishnan, and Prime Minister Nehru attended the song and dance soiree.

    The Chinese side attending the soiree included Vice Premier Chen Yi, Vice Foreign Minister Zhang Hanfu, and members of the Chinese Premier's entourage. Chinese Ambassador to India Pan Zili and officials from the Chinese Embassy also attended the soiree.

    The Indian side attending the soiree included cabinet ministers, ministers of state, other senior officials of the Indian government, the Chief Justice of the Indian Supreme Court, Indian Ambassador to China G. Parthasarathi, members of parliament, and prominent figures from society.

    Diplomatic envoys from various countries stationed in India also attended the soiree.

    Indian artists performed wonderful classical dances and folk dances and music for the distinguished guests. (Photo attached)

    On April 21st, Chinese Ambassador to India Pan Zili held a reception for Premier Zhou Enlai's visit to India. Indian Vice President Radhakrishnan and Prime Minister Nehru attended the reception, with eight hundred guests in attendance. ### 周总理拜访印度财政部长德赛  印度国防部长梅农宴请周总理  印度总统府举行招待周总理歌舞晚会

1960-04-23
第3版()
专栏：

    周总理拜访印度财政部长德赛  印度国防部长梅农宴请周总理
    印度总统府举行招待周总理歌舞晚会
    新华社新德里22日电　周恩来总理今天下午拜访了印度财政部长莫·兰·德赛。
    拜访时在座的有：中国副总理兼外交部长陈毅和外交部副部长章汉夫。
    印度驻中国大使高·帕塔萨拉蒂也在座。
    新华社新德里22日电　印度国防部长克里希纳·梅农今天在他的寓所举行宴会，招待中国总理周恩来和副总理陈毅。
    出席宴会的有：中国外交部副部长章汉夫，外交部部长助理乔冠华和中国驻印度大使潘自力。
    新华社新德里21日电　周恩来总理在今天晚间十时三十分接见了前印度驻中国大使拉·库·尼赫鲁和他的夫人。
    接见时在座的有陈毅副总理和外交部副部长章汉夫。
    新华社新德里22日电　今天晚上在印度总统府举行了歌舞晚会，招待中国总理周恩来。
    印度总统拉·普拉沙德、副总统萨·拉达克里希南和尼赫鲁总理出席了歌舞晚会。
    中国方面出席歌舞晚会的有：副总理陈毅，外交部副部长章汉夫和中国总理的随行人员。中国驻印度大使潘自力和中国大使馆的官员也出席了歌舞晚会。
    印度方面出席歌舞晚会的有：内阁部长，国务部长和印度政府的其他高级官员，印度最高法院首席法官，印度驻中国大使高·帕塔萨拉蒂，议员和社会著名人士。
    各国驻印度外交使节也出席了歌舞晚会。
    印度艺术家为贵宾表演了精彩的古典舞蹈和民间舞蹈和音乐。（附图片）
    四月二十一日，中国驻印度大使潘自力为周恩来总理访问印度举行招待会。印度副总统拉达克里希南和总理尼赫鲁出席了招待会，有八百名客人出席了招待会。图为周总理、陈毅副总理和尼赫鲁总理在招待会上。          新华社记者　杜修贤摄（传真照片）
