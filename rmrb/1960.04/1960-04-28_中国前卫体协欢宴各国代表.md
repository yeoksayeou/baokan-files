Title: China Avant-Garde Sports Association Hosts Banquet for Foreign Representatives
Author:
Date: 1960-04-28
Page: 2

Xinhua News Agency, April 27th: The National Committee of the China "Avant-Garde" Sports Association hosted a banquet tonight for the representatives, observers, and specially invited referees from various countries participating in the first Socialist countries' Public Security Sports Organization Men's Basketball Tournament.

Also present at the banquet tonight were Yang Qiqing, Deputy Minister of Public Security; Cai Tingkai and Huang Zhong, Deputy Directors of the State Physical Culture and Sports Commission; Zhang Qingji, Director of the Beijing Municipal Sports Commission, and others.

Members of embassies in China from relevant countries were also invited to the banquet.

All the guests participating in the first Socialist countries' Public Security Sports Organization Men's Basketball Tournament will visit other cities in China for friendly matches tomorrow.

### 中国前卫体协欢宴各国代表

1960-04-28
第2版()
专栏：

    中国前卫体协欢宴各国代表
    据新华社27日讯　中国“前卫”体育协会全国委员会，今晚欢宴参加社会主义国家公安体育组织第一届男子篮球比赛大会的各国代表队、观察员和特邀裁判员。
    今晚出席宴会的，还有公安部副部长杨奇清，国家体委副主任蔡廷锴、黄中，北京市体委主任张青季等人。
    各有关国家驻我国大使馆人员，也应邀出席了宴会。
    参加社会主义国家公安体育组织第一届男子篮球比赛大会的全体客人们，将在明天分头到我国其他城市进行访问比赛。
