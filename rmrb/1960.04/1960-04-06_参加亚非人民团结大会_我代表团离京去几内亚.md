Title: Attending the Asian-African People's Solidarity Conference: Our Delegation Departs Beijing for Guinea
Author:
Date: 1960-04-06
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, 5th. The Chinese delegation attending the Second Asian-African People's Solidarity Conference, led by Liao Chengzhi and Liu Ningyi, departed from Beijing by plane today for Conakry, Guinea.

The delegation members departing today also included: Ji Chao Ding, Meng Yongqian, Tang Mingzhao, Hu Naiqiu, Wu Xueqian, Yang Shuo, Wang Chuanbin, Chen Jun, and Cai Yingping. Other members of the delegation, Bao Erhan and Jin Zhonghua, will travel from abroad to attend the conference, and Zhu Ziqi has already arrived in Conakry.

Those who went to the airport to see them off included: Mao Dun, Li Dequan, Liu Changsheng, Zhang Zhixiang, Huang Changshui, Zhuang Mingli, Qu Tangliang, Cao Mengjun, Li Menghua, Wu Maosun, Wang Mingyuan, Zheng Senyu, Chen Yu, Wang Yafan, Song Yatian, and other people from all walks of life.

### 参加亚非人民团结大会  我代表团离京去几内亚

1960-04-06
第5版()
专栏：

    参加亚非人民团结大会
    我代表团离京去几内亚
    新华社5日讯　出席第二届亚非人民团结大会的中国代表团在廖承志、刘宁一率领下，今天乘飞机从北京启程前往几内亚科纳克里。
    今天一同启程的代表团成员有：冀朝鼎、孟用潜、唐明照、胡耐秋、吴学谦、杨朔、王传斌、陈浚、蔡英平。代表团的其他成员包尔汉、金仲华将从国外前往参加大会，朱子奇已经到达科纳克里。
    前往机场送行的有：茅盾、李德全、刘长胜、张致祥、黄长水、庄明理、区棠亮、曹孟君、李梦华、吴茂荪、王明远、郑森禹、陈宇、王亚凡、宋亚田等各界人士。
