Title: Our Youth Delegation Leaves for Havana
Author:
Date: 1960-04-02
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, 31st -- At the invitation of the Cuban Socialist Youth League, a three-member Chinese youth delegation has left Beijing for Havana to participate in the Fourth National Congress of the Cuban Socialist Youth League, which will be held from April 4th to 10th. The head of the delegation, Qian Liren, Standing Committee Member of the Central Committee of the Chinese Communist Youth League and Secretary-General of the All-China Youth Federation, has already gone abroad. The delegation members, Zhang Xueshu, member of the Central Committee of the Chinese Communist Youth League, and two others, flew to Havana today.

### 我青年代表团赴哈瓦那

1960-04-02
第5版()
专栏：

    我青年代表团赴哈瓦那
    新华社31日讯　应古巴社会主义青年联盟的邀请，中国青年代表团一行三人已离京前往哈瓦那参加将在4月4日至10日举行的古巴社会主义青年联盟第四次全国代表大会。代表团团长、中国共产主义青年团中央委员会常务委员、中华全国青年联合会秘书长钱李仁已先去国外，代表团团员、中国共产主义青年团中央委员会委员张学书等二人在今天乘飞机前往哈瓦那。
