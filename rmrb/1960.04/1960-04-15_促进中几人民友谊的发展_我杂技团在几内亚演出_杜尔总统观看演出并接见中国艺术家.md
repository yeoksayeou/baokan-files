Title: Promoting the Development of Sino-Guinean Friendship: Our Acrobatic Troupe Performs in Guinea; President Touré Watches the Performance and Receives Chinese Artists
Author:
Date: 1960-04-15
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, Conakry, April 13th: The Chinese Acrobatic Troupe gave its premiere performance here on the evening of the 12th.

Among those present at the performance were: President of the Republic of Guinea, Sékou Touré, President of the National Assembly, Diallo Saifoulaye, government ministers, and diplomatic envoys of various countries to Guinea. All the delegates attending the Second Asian-African People's Solidarity Conference were also invited to watch the performance.

After the performance, Sékou Touré and his wife, accompanied by Chinese Ambassador Ke Hua and the head of the Acrobatic Troupe, Qu Wu, went on stage to meet and shake hands with the Chinese artists, congratulating them on the success of their performance. Finally, they also took a group photo with them.

Before the start of the performance, Bankoura Kanfory, the leader in charge of cultural and artistic affairs of the Guinean African Democratic Youth League, delivered a welcoming speech. He said that the visit of the Chinese Acrobatic Troupe to Guinea prominently embodies the friendly relations between the people of China and Guinea. He expressed his firm belief that this visit and performance will strengthen the sincere friendship between the two countries.

Qu Wu, the head of the Chinese Acrobatic Troupe, delivered a reply speech. He said that the Chinese Acrobatic Troupe not only brought the Chinese acrobatic art with a history of two thousand years to the Guinean people, but also brought the best wishes of the 650 million Chinese people to the Guinean people. He expressed his firm belief that through this cultural exchange, the mutual understanding and friendly relations between the people of China and Guinea will be further developed.

Qu Wu also congratulated the Second Asian-African People's Solidarity Conference on its great achievements. The audience expressed great appreciation for the performances of the Chinese artists, and joyful laughter constantly erupted from the venue from the beginning of the program to the end of the entire performance. Each program was repeated several times at the request of the audience, accompanied by prolonged applause and cheers.

### 促进中几人民友谊的发展  我杂技团在几内亚演出  杜尔总统观看演出并接见中国艺术家

1960-04-15
第5版()
专栏：

    促进中几人民友谊的发展
    我杂技团在几内亚演出
    杜尔总统观看演出并接见中国艺术家
    新华社科纳克里13日电　中国杂技艺术团12日晚间在这里举行首次演出。
    出席观看演出的有：几内亚共和国总统塞古·杜尔、国民议会议长迪阿洛·塞福拉耶、政府部长们以及各国驻几内亚外交使节。出席第二届亚非人民团结大会的全体代表也都应邀观看了演出。
    表演结束后，塞古·杜尔和夫人在中国大使柯华、杂技艺术团团长屈武的陪同下，登上舞台同中国艺术家们见面握手，祝贺他们演出的成功。最后还同他们一起合影留念。
    演出开始前，几内亚非洲民主青年联盟负责文化艺术事务的领导人班库拉·坎福里致欢迎词。他说，中国杂技艺术团的访问几内亚，突出地体现了中、几两国人民之间的友好关系。他表示深信，这次访问演出将加强两国之间的真诚友谊。
    中国杂技艺术团团长屈武致答词。他说，中国杂技艺术团不仅给几内亚人民带来了具有两千年历史的中国杂技艺术，而且给几内亚人民带来了六亿五千万中国人民的最好的祝愿。他表示深信，通过这种文化交流，中国同几内亚两国人民之间的相互了解和友好关系都将进一步发展。
    屈武还祝贺第二届亚非人民团结大会取得巨大成就。观众们对中国艺术家的表演表示十分欣赏，从节目开始到全部表演结束时场内不断发出欢乐的笑声。每个节目都在观众的长时间鼓掌和欢呼声的要求下重演数次。
