Title: Vietnam, Mongolia, Romania Recognize Togolese Republic; Cyrankiewicz and Louis Saillant Warmly Congratulate Togo on Independence
Author: 
Date: 1960-04-29
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency dispatch: Following Togo's declaration of independence, Pham Van Dong, Prime Minister and Minister of Foreign Affairs of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam, Yumjaagiin Tsedenbal, Chairman of the Council of Ministers of Mongolia, and Chivu Stoica, Chairman of the Council of Ministers of Romania, each sent telegrams to Togolese Prime Minister Olympio, recognizing the Togolese Republic and congratulating Togo on its independence.

Józef Cyrankiewicz, Chairman of the Council of Ministers of Poland, and Louis Saillant, Secretary-General of the World Federation of Trade Unions, sent telegrams to Togolese Prime Minister Olympio and the Togolese Trade Union Confederation, respectively, warmly congratulating Togo on its independence.

### 越蒙罗三国承认多哥共和国  西伦凯维兹和路易·赛扬热烈祝贺多哥独立

1960-04-29
第6版()
专栏：

    越蒙罗三国承认多哥共和国
    西伦凯维兹和路易·赛扬热烈祝贺多哥独立
    本报综合报道　据新华社讯：多哥宣告独立后，越南民主共和国总理兼外交部长范文同、蒙古部长会议主席泽登巴尔、罗马尼亚部长会议主席基伏·斯托伊卡分别致电多哥共和国总理奥林匹欧承认多哥共和国并祝贺多哥独立。
    波兰部长会议主席西伦凯维兹和世界工会联合会总书记路易·赛扬分别致电多哥共和国总理奥林匹欧和多哥工会联合会，热烈祝贺多哥独立。
