Title: The Second Session of the Second National People's Congress Successfully Concludes, Issuing a Solemn Call to the People of the Whole Country: Strive to Overfulfill This Year's National Economic Plan, Strive to Realize the National Agricultural Development Program Ahead of Schedule

Author:

Date: 1960-04-11
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, April 10th: The Second Session of the Second National People's Congress of the People's Republic of China concluded victoriously this afternoon.

    In the resolution passed by the congress, it solemnly called on the people of the whole country to unite under the leadership of the Chinese Communist Party and Chairman Mao Zedong, hold high the three red banners of the General Line, the Great Leap Forward, and the People's Commune, and strive to complete and overfulfill the 1960 national economic plan and state budget, strive to achieve a continued Great Leap Forward in 1960, and strive to realize the great task of achieving the National Agricultural Development Program two or three years ahead of schedule.

    Chairman Mao Zedong, Chairman Liu Shaoqi, Vice-Chairpersons Soong Ching Ling and Dong Biwu, Chairman Zhu De, Premier Zhou Enlai, Vice-Premiers Chen Yun, Lin Biao, and Deng Xiaoping, as well as other Party and state leaders, attended the meeting. When they stepped onto the rostrum, the entire audience erupted in thunderous and enthusiastic applause.

    Zeng Shan, Chairman of the Budget Committee, made a report on the examination of the 1959 state final accounts and the 1960 state budget draft.

    The congress passed resolutions on the 1960 national economic plan, the 1959 state final accounts, and the 1960 state budget. The resolution approved the reports made to the congress by Vice-Premiers Li Fuchun and Li Xiannian. The resolution emphasized that: China's national economy has achieved a Great Leap Forward for two consecutive years, completing the main targets of the Second Five-Year Plan three years ahead of schedule, which is a great victory. This victory proves that the general line for building socialism and a set of "simultaneous development" policies formulated by the Party Central Committee and Chairman Mao Zedong are the only correct path for China to rapidly achieve national prosperity and increase people's happiness. It proves that Mao Zedong Thought has the great power to be ever-victorious.

    The resolution stated: The 1960 national economic plan is a plan for continued leap forward, and the 1960 fiscal budget is in line with the development of the national economy. The congress called on all nationalities and democratic parties across the country to unite under the leadership of the Chinese Communist Party and Chairman Mao Zedong, better implement the general line of socialist construction, adhere to the Great Leap Forward, consolidate and develop the People's Commune, carry out the technical innovation and technical revolution movement more widely and deeply, continue to implement the policy of building the country diligently and thriftily, and running all undertakings diligently and thriftily, and strive to complete and overfulfill the 1960 national economic plan and state budget, and strive to achieve a continued Great Leap Forward this year.

    The congress passed a resolution on striving to realize the National Agricultural Development Program ahead of schedule. The resolution approved the report made to the congress by Vice-Premier Tan Zhenlin. The resolution pointed out: The National Agricultural Development Program from 1956 to 1967 formulated by the Central Committee of the Communist Party of China is a great program for the high-speed development of China's socialist agriculture and the construction of new socialist rural areas. Achieving the National Agricultural Development Program ahead of schedule is of extremely great significance to the continued leap forward of China's socialist construction.

    The resolution stated: The congress, in passing the National Agricultural Development Program, only revised Article 27 of the draft revision regarding the elimination of the four pests to: "Starting from 1956, within twelve years, basically eliminate rats, bedbugs, flies, and mosquitoes in all possible places." In addition, no modifications were made to any of the articles.

    The resolution called on: All nationalities across the country, and all departments of industry, agriculture, transportation, finance, banking, trade, science, education, culture, health, and sports to work together and strive to achieve the great task of realizing the National Agricultural Development Program two or three years ahead of schedule.

    When the above two resolutions were passed, the entire audience burst into thunderous applause that lasted for a long time.

    The congress passed a resolution approving the work report of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress.

    Zhang Youyu, Chairman of the Proposals Examination Committee, made a report on the examination of proposals, and the congress passed the opinions on the examination of proposals.

    The congress began at 3:00 p.m. After the congress discussions were concluded, the above resolutions were passed, and the closing ceremony was held. A total of 1,034 delegates attended the congress today. Members attending the Second Session of the Third National Committee of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference, heads of various government departments, and others attended today's meeting as observers. Foreign envoys to China and some foreign guests in Beijing were invited to attend the meeting.

    The executive chairmen this afternoon were: Zhu De, Lin Boqu, Luo Ronghuan, Shen Junru, Guo Moruo, Huang Yanpei, Peng Zhen, Li Weihan, Chen Shutong, Saifudin, Cheng Qian, He Xiangning, Liu Bocheng, and Lin Feng.

    At 6:00 p.m., Vice-Chairman Peng Zhen announced the successful conclusion of the congress. At this time, everyone stood up and applauded warmly for a long time. The band played the national anthem. (Photo attached)

    Right picture: Chairman Mao Zedong and Chairman Liu Shaoqi stood up and applauded enthusiastically with all the delegates when the Second Session of the Second National People's Congress announced its successful conclusion. Our reporter Lü Xiangyou.

### 全国人民代表大会二届二次会议胜利闭幕，向全国人民发出庄严的号召：为超额完成今年国民经济计划而奋斗  为提前实现全国农业发展纲要而奋斗

1960-04-11
第1版()
专栏：

    全国人民代表大会二届二次会议胜利闭幕，向全国人民发出庄严的号召：
    为超额完成今年国民经济计划而奋斗  为提前实现全国农业发展纲要而奋斗
    新华社10日讯　中华人民共和国第二届全国人民代表大会第二次会议，今天下午胜利闭幕。
    大会在通过的决议中，庄严地号召全国人民在中国共产党和毛泽东主席的领导下，团结一致，高举总路线、大跃进、人民公社三面红旗，为争取完成和超额完成一九六○年国民经济计划和国家预算而奋斗，为争取实现一九六○年继续大跃进而奋斗，为提前两年或者三年实现全国农业发展纲要这个伟大的任务而奋斗。
    毛泽东主席，刘少奇主席，宋庆龄、董必武副主席，朱德委员长，周恩来总理，陈云、林彪、邓小平副总理以及其他党和国家的领导人，出席了大会。当他们走上主席台的时候，全场响起暴风雨般的热烈掌声。
    预算委员会主任委员曾山作了关于一九五九年国家决算和一九六○年国家预算草案的审查报告。
    大会通过了关于一九六○年国民经济计划、一九五九年国家决算和一九六○年国家预算的决议。决议批准了李富春、李先念两位副总理向大会作的报告。决议中强调指出：我国国民经济连续两年实现了大跃进，提前三年完成了第二个五年计划的主要指标，这是一个伟大的胜利。这个胜利，证明了党中央和毛泽东主席所制定的建设社会主义的总路线和一套“同时并举”的方针，是我国迅速实现国家富强和增进人民幸福的唯一正确的道路。证明了毛泽东思想具有无往不胜的伟大力量。
    决议中说：一九六○年的国民经济计划是继续跃进的计划，一九六○年的财政预算是同国民经济的发展相适应的。大会号召全国各族人民、各民主党派，在中国共产党和毛泽东主席的领导下，团结一致，更好地贯彻执行社会主义建设的总路线，坚持大跃进，巩固和发展人民公社，更加广泛更加深入地开展技术革新和技术革命运动，继续贯彻执行勤俭建国、勤俭办一切事业的方针，为争取完成和超额完成一九六○年国民经济计划和国家预算而奋斗，为争取实现今年继续大跃进而奋斗。
    大会通过了关于为提前实现全国农业发展纲要而奋斗的决议，决议批准了谭震林副总理向大会作的报告。决议指出：中共中央制订的一九五六年到一九六七年全国农业发展纲要，是高速度发展我国社会主义农业和建设社会主义新农村的伟大纲领。提前实现全国农业发展纲要，对于我国社会主义建设的继续跃进具有极其重大的意义。
    决议说：大会通过全国农业发展纲要，只将修正草案第二十七条关于除四害的条文修改为：“从一九五六年起，在十二年内，在一切可能的地方，基本上消灭老鼠、臭虫、苍蝇和蚊子。”此外，各条都不作修改。
    决议号召：全国各族人民，工业、农业、交通、财政、金融、贸易、科学、教育、文化、卫生、体育等一切部门共同努力，为提前两年或者三年实现全国农业发展纲要这个伟大的任务而奋斗。
    在通过上述两个决议的时候，全场掌声雷动，经久不息。
    大会通过决议，批准了全国人民代表大会常务委员会的工作报告。
    提案审查委员会主任委员张友渔作了关于提案的审查报告，大会通过了提案审查意见。
    大会在下午三时开始。在结束了大会讨论之后，通过了上述各项决议，举行了闭幕式。今天出席大会的代表共一千零三十四人。出席政协第三届第二次会议的委员们，政府各部门的负责人，列席了今天的会议。各国驻中国的使节和在北京的一部分外宾，应邀参加了会议。
    今天下午的执行主席是：朱德、林伯渠、罗荣桓、沈钧儒、郭沫若、黄炎培、彭真、李维汉、陈叔通、赛福鼎、程潜、何香凝、刘伯承、林枫。
    下午六时，彭真副委员长宣布大会胜利闭幕。这时，全体起立，热烈地长时间地鼓掌。乐队高奏国歌。（附图片）
