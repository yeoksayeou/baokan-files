Title: Cypriot Trade Union Delegation Arrives in Beijing
Author: 
Date: 1960-04-23
Page: 5

    Xinhua News Agency, April 21st -- A three-member Cypriot trade union delegation, headed by Pantelis Varnavas, arrived in Beijing by plane on the afternoon of the 21st.
    Zanzibar trade union representative and organizing secretary of the Zanzibar Federation of Labour, Ali Sultan Issa, and Algerian General Workers' Union representative Bendris Brahim also arrived on the same flight.
    They were invited by the All-China Federation of Trade Unions to come to our country to participate in the "May Day" International Labor Day celebrations and for a visit.

### 塞浦路斯工会代表团到北京

1960-04-23
第5版()
专栏：

    塞浦路斯工会代表团到北京
    新华社21日讯　以本台里斯·凡纳佛斯为首的塞浦路斯工会代表团一行三人，21日下午乘飞机到达北京。
    桑给巴尔工会代表、桑给巴尔劳工联合会组织书记阿里·苏尔丹·依萨和阿尔及利亚工人总联合会代表本德里斯·勃拉希姆也同机到达。
    他们是应中华全国总工会邀请，来我国参加“五一”国际劳动节庆祝活动和访问的。
