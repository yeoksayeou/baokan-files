Title: Communiqué of the Sino-German Standing Committee for Technical and Scientific Cooperation
Author:
Date: 1960-04-16
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, April 15th: Communiqué of the Fifth Session of the Sino-German Standing Committee for Technical and Scientific Cooperation.

    The Fifth Session of the Standing Committee for Technical and Scientific Cooperation between the People's Republic of China and the German Democratic Republic was held in Beijing from April 2nd to 13th, 1960. The meeting was conducted in an atmosphere of sincere friendship and mutual understanding. During the meeting, both sides made resolutions on further strengthening scientific and technological cooperation between the two countries.

    The protocol stipulates that the People's Republic of China will provide the German Democratic Republic with technical data related to the metallurgical, chemical, mechanical manufacturing, and other industries, and will receive experts from the German Democratic Republic to conduct inspections in China on the production of tungsten-molybdenum wire, precision optical instruments, fish meal, and cotton linter; the German Democratic Republic will provide the People's Republic of China with technical data related to the chemical, agricultural, metallurgical, and mechanical manufacturing industries, dispatch experts to China to provide technical assistance, and receive Chinese experts to conduct inspections in the German Democratic Republic on metallurgy, coal, water conservancy and electric power, and mechanical manufacturing. In addition, the two sides exchanged views and made resolutions on the establishment of direct contacts between corresponding scientific research institutions of the two countries.

    The protocol was signed on April 13th. The signatories to the protocol were: Wang Daohan, Chairman of the Chinese Group of the Committee and Vice Minister of the First Ministry of Machine Building, and Georg Handke, Chairman of the German Group of the Committee, Member of the State Planning Commission, and Director of the Foreign Trade Bureau.

    Also participating in the signing ceremony on the Chinese side were Wu Heng, Vice Chairman of the Scientific and Technological Commission of the People's Republic of China, and Chen Boqing, Deputy Director of the Soviet Union and Eastern European Department of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs, and others. Also participating in the signing ceremony on the German side were Wieland, Ambassador of the German Democratic Republic to China, and others.

### 中德技术与技术科学合作常任委员会公报

1960-04-16
第4版()
专栏：

    中德技术与技术科学合作常任委员会公报
    新华社15日讯　中德技术与技术科学合作常任委员会第五届会议公报。
    中华人民共和国和德意志民主共和国技术与技术科学合作常任委员会第五届会议自1960年4月2日到13日在北京举行。会议是在真诚友好和互相谅解的气氛中进行的。会议期间，双方就进一步加强两国科学技术合作的问题作出了决议。
    议定书规定，中华人民共和国供给德意志民主共和国有关冶金、化学、机械制造等工业方面的技术资料，接待德意志民主共和国专家来华在钨钼丝生产、精密光学仪器、鱼粉、棉绒生产等方面进行考察；德意志民主共和国供给中华人民共和国有关化学、农业、冶金、机械制造等方面的技术资料，派遣专家来华提供技术援助，接待中国专家赴德意志民主共和国在冶金、煤炭、水利电力、机械制造等方面进行考察。此外，双方就两国相应科学研究机构之间建立直接联系的问题交换了意见，并作出了决议。
    4月13日签订了议定书，在议定书上签字的是：委员会中国组主席、第一机械工业部副部长汪道涵，委员会德国组主席、国家计划委员会委员兼对外贸易局局长盖奥格·亨凯。
    中国方面参加签字仪式的还有中华人民共和国科学技术委员会副主任武衡，外交部苏联东欧司副司长陈伯清等。德国方面参加签字仪式的还有德意志民主共和国驻华大使汪戴尔等。
