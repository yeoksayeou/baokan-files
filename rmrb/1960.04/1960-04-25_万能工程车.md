All-Purpose Engineering Vehicle

The construction of telephone lines includes carrying wooden poles, removing wooden poles, digging pole holes, erecting wooden poles, transporting cable steel strands, stringing cable steel strands, wrapping cables, and inflating cables. It is a very heavy physical labor. Now, this heavy physical labor can be replaced by automated all-purpose engineering vehicles. This all-purpose engineering vehicle can automatically complete twenty-four operations in telephone line construction projects such as digging pole holes, erecting poles, tightening steel strands, and inflating cables. With this engineering vehicle, people will no longer see line workers covered in mud and sweating profusely.

The creator of this engineering vehicle is neither an engineer nor a technician, but Liu Baoshan, a line worker from the Tianjin City Telephone Bureau.

### 万能工程车

1960-04-25
第3版()
专栏：

    万能工程车
    电话线路的架设工程包括有扛木杆、撤木杆、打杆洞、立木杆、搬运电缆钢绞线、架设电缆钢绞线、缠绕电缆，电缆充气等部分，是一项很笨重的体力劳动。现在，这项笨重的体力劳动可以用自动化的万能工程车来代替了，这种万能工程车可以自动地完成打杆洞，立杆，紧钢绞线，电缆充气等电话线路架设工程中的二十四种操作。使用这种工程车，人们再也看不到线路工人满身污泥，遍体流汗了。
    创造这辆工程车的，既不是工程师，也不是技术员，而是天津市内电话局线路工人刘宝山。
