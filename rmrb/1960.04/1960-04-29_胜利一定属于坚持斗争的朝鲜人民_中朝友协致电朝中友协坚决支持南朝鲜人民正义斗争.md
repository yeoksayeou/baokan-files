Title: Victory Surely Belongs to the Korean People Who Persist in Struggle: China-Korea Friendship Association Sends Telegram to Korea-China Friendship Association Firmly Supporting the Just Struggle of the South Korean People
Author:
Date: 1960-04-29
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, April 28th: The China-Korea Friendship Association today sent a telegram to the Korea-China Friendship Association, firmly supporting the patriotic and just struggle of the South Korean people against American imperialist aggression and the fascist rule of the Syngman Rhee group.

The China-Korea Friendship Association's telegram stated: "The Chinese people deeply sympathize with the profound suffering of the South Korean people and resolutely support the South Korean people's struggle against American imperialist aggression and the fascist rule of the Syngman Rhee group. We fully support the suggestions and propositions put forward by the Central Committee of the Korean Workers' Party in its 'Letter to the South Korean People'. We firmly believe that one day, the American aggressors and their puppet regime in South Korea will be burned to ashes in the raging flames of the Korean people's struggle. Final victory surely belongs to the united Korean people who persist in struggle."

In its telegram, the China-Korea Friendship Association also expressed its respect to the South Korean people who persist in heroic struggle and expressed its boundless anger and solemn condemnation of the towering crimes of American imperialism and the Syngman Rhee reactionary clique in their bloody suppression of the South Korean people.

### 胜利一定属于坚持斗争的朝鲜人民  中朝友协致电朝中友协坚决支持南朝鲜人民正义斗争

1960-04-29
第4版()
专栏：

    胜利一定属于坚持斗争的朝鲜人民
    中朝友协致电朝中友协坚决支持南朝鲜人民正义斗争
    新华社28日讯　中国朝鲜友好协会今天打电报给朝鲜中国友好协会，坚决支持南朝鲜人民反对美帝国主义侵略、反对李承晚集团法西斯统治的爱国正义斗争。
    中朝友协的电报说：“中国人民深切同情南朝鲜人民受到的深重灾难，坚决支持南朝鲜人民反对美帝国主义侵略、反对李承晚集团法西斯统治的斗争。我们完全支持朝鲜劳动党中央委员会在‘告南朝鲜人民书’中所提出的建议和主张。我们坚信，总有一天，美国侵略者和它在南朝鲜的傀儡政权必将在朝鲜人民的斗争烈火中烧得一干二净。最后胜利一定属于团结起来、坚持斗争的朝鲜人民。”
    中朝友协在电报中还向坚持英勇斗争的南朝鲜人民表示敬意，并且对美帝国主义和李承晚反动集团血腥镇压南朝鲜人民的滔天罪行表示无比的愤怒和严正的谴责。
