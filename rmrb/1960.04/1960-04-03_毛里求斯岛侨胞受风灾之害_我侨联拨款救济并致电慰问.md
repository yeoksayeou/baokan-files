Title: Overseas Chinese in Mauritius Suffer from Typhoon Disaster: All-China Federation of Returned Overseas Chinese Allocates Funds for Relief and Sends Condolences
Author:
Date: 1960-04-03
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, 2nd. The All-China Federation of Returned Overseas Chinese allocated RMB 10,000 yuan on March 28th to provide relief to overseas Chinese in Mauritius who suffered from the typhoon disaster and sent a telegram of condolences.

It is reported that Mauritius suffered from typhoon disasters on January 18th and 19th, and February 27th. It is understood that tens of thousands of people have been made homeless due to the typhoon disaster, and a large number of houses have been destroyed, among which overseas Chinese have also suffered serious losses. The relief funds from the All-China Federation of Returned Overseas Chinese have been telegraphed to the Mauritius Renhe Association for transfer to the Overseas Chinese Relief Committee for relief.

### 毛里求斯岛侨胞受风灾之害  我侨联拨款救济并致电慰问

1960-04-03
第4版()
专栏：

    毛里求斯岛侨胞受风灾之害
    我侨联拨款救济并致电慰问
    新华社2日讯　中华全国归国华侨联合会于3月28日拨款人民币一万元救济毛里求斯岛受风灾的侨胞，并致电进行慰问。
    毛里求斯岛于1月18、19日和2月27日先今遭受风灾。据悉，因风灾受害而无家可归的有数万人，大量房屋被毁，其中华侨也受到严重损失。全国归国华侨联合会的救济款已电汇毛里求斯仁和会馆转华侨救济委员会进行救济。
