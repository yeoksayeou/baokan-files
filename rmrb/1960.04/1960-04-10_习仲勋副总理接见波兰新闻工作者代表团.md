Title: Vice Premier Xi Zhongxun Receives Polish Journalists Delegation
Author:
Date: 1960-04-10
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, April 9th: Vice Premier of the State Council, Xi Zhongxun, received the Polish journalists delegation this morning, headed by Mieczysław Rakowski, Chairman of the Polish Journalists Association and Editor-in-Chief of the Polish "Polityka" weekly.

Present at the meeting were Zhu Muzhi, Vice Chairman of the All-China Journalists Association, Li Bingquan, Secretary of the Secretariat, Shao Zonghan, Deputy Director of the Information Department of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs, Yu Zhan, Deputy Director of the Department of Soviet and Eastern European Affairs, Shi Mai, Deputy Editor-in-Chief of the Workers' Daily, and others.

Also present was Franciszek Frątczak, Chargé d'Affaires ad interim of the Polish Embassy in China.

### 习仲勋副总理接见波兰新闻工作者代表团

1960-04-10
第6版()
专栏：

    习仲勋副总理接见波兰新闻工作者代表团
    新华社9日讯　国务院副总理习仲勋今天上午接见了以波兰新闻工作者协会主席、波兰“政治周刊”主编米·拉科夫斯基为首的波兰新闻工作者代表团。
    接见时，在座的有中国新闻工作者协会副主席朱穆之、书记处书记李炳泉，外交部新闻司副司长邵宗汉、苏欧司副司长余湛和工人日报副总编辑史迈等。
    接见时，在座的还有波兰驻中国大使馆临时代办傅拉托。
