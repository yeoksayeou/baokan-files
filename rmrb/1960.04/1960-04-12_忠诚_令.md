Title: "Loyalty" Order
Author: Chi Bei'ou, Hua Junwu
Date: 1960-04-12
Page: 6

    "Loyalty" Order 	Chi Bei'ou (poem), Hua Junwu (drawing)
    News from the US Information Service: Eisenhower issued an order on April 8th, designating May 1st as "Loyalty Day," requiring the American people to "reaffirm their loyalty to the United States" in churches and other venues, and to "piously express that our nation firmly grips our hearts."
    The president has a mental illness,
    Orders "May Day" to be renamed,
    Cease mentioning the sanctity of labor,
    Only professions of loyalty are allowed.
    Despite unemployment, poverty, and hunger,
    Do not utter complaints or roars;
    Despite the lack of freedom and democracy,
    The "nation must firmly grip our hearts."
    No strikes or gatherings,
    And certainly no demonstrations or marches.
    We should pray to the divine,
    And sincerely swear allegiance:
    Loyal to the America of bosses and tycoons,
    To serve the American bosses and tycoons.

### “忠诚”令
池北偶  华君武
1960-04-12
第6版()
专栏：

    “忠诚”令	池北偶诗  华君武画
    美新处消息：艾森豪威尔4月8日下令以5月1日为“忠诚日”，要美国人民在教堂和其他场合“重新肯定他们对美国的忠诚”、“虔诚地表示我们的国家牢牢抓住我们的心灵”。
    总统心里有病，
    下令“五一”改名，
    休提劳动神圣，
    只可表白忠诚。
    尽管失业、贫困、饥饿，
    也别发出怨声、吼声；
    尽管没有自由、民主，
    须让“国家牢牢抓住心灵”。
    不要罢工集会，
    更勿示威游行。
    应当祷告神明，
    诚心宣誓坚贞：
    忠于老板、大亨的美国，
    替美国的老板大亨效命。
