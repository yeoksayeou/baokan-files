Title: Commemorating Lenin's Birth Anniversary and Strengthening Sino-Soviet Friendship: Sino-Soviet Friendship Association and the Soviet Academy of Sciences Institute of Chinese Studies Jointly Hold Evening Party
Author:
Date: 1960-04-23
Page: 3
Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, April 21st: The Council of the Sino-Soviet Friendship Association, the Institute of Chinese Studies of the Soviet Academy of Sciences, and the Sino-Soviet Friendship Association Branch of the Moscow Power Institute jointly held an evening party on the 20th to commemorate the 90th anniversary of Lenin's birth.

Present at the evening party were: Andreyev, Chairman of the Council of the Sino-Soviet Friendship Association; Satyukov, Vice Chairman of the Council of the Sino-Soviet Friendship Association and Editor-in-Chief of "Pravda"; Rogov, Editor-in-Chief of the magazine "Sino-Soviet Friendship"; Tsikhvinsky, Director of the Institute of Chinese Studies of the Soviet Academy of Sciences; and some Old Bolsheviks, etc.

Zhang Weilie, Chargé d'affaires ad interim of the Chinese Embassy in the Soviet Union, embassy staff, and Chinese students also attended the evening party.

Andreyev said at the evening party that Lenin dedicated his entire life to the cause of striving for the victory of socialism. Lenin and the party he cultivated not only theoretically, but also practically, pointed out the road to socialism and communism for mankind. Lenin's brilliant image, his life full of struggles for human liberation, and his immortal thought will forever exist in people's hearts, encouraging all mankind to fight for the final victory of peace and socialism.

Zhang Weilie said at the meeting that the victory of the Chinese socialist revolution and socialist construction is a great victory of Leninism. On the common foundation of Leninism, the people of China and the Soviet Union have established the greatest friendship and unity in the world. The great Sino-Soviet friendship will continue to develop and strengthen forever, which no force can break.

Tsikhvinsky delivered a report at the meeting entitled "Lenin and China." He elaborated in detail on Lenin's infinite concern for the Chinese people's revolutionary struggle and the influence of Leninism on the Chinese revolution.

Speaking at tonight's party were also three Old Bolsheviks who fought and lived with Lenin. A documentary film about Lenin's revolutionary activities was shown at the party, and entertainment programs were performed.

### 纪念列宁诞辰　加强中苏友谊  苏中友协和苏联科学院中国问题研究所联合举行晚会

1960-04-23
第3版()
专栏：

    纪念列宁诞辰　加强中苏友谊
    苏中友协和苏联科学院中国问题研究所联合举行晚会
    新华社莫斯科21日电　苏中友好协会理事会、苏联科学院中国问题研究所和莫斯科动力学院苏中友好协会支会，在20日晚间联合举行晚会，纪念列宁诞生九十周年。
    出席晚会的有：苏中友好协会理事会主席安德烈耶夫，苏中友好协会理事会副主席、“真理报”总编辑萨丘科夫，“苏中友好”杂志总编辑罗果夫，苏联科学院中国问题研究所所长齐赫文斯基以及一些老布尔什维克等。
    中国驻苏联大使馆临时代办张伟烈、使馆工作人员和中国留学生也出席了晚会。
    安德烈耶夫在晚会上讲话说，列宁把自己的全部生命献给了争取社会主义胜利的事业。列宁和他培育起来的党不仅在理论上，而且在实践上，为人类指出了走向社会主义和共产主义的道路。列宁的光辉形象，他的充满争取人类解放斗争的一生和他的不朽思想将永远存在于人们的心中，鼓舞全人类为争取和平和社会主义的最后胜利而斗争。
    张伟烈在会上讲话说，中国社会主义革命和社会主义建设的胜利是列宁主义的伟大胜利。在列宁主义的共同基础上，中苏两国人民建立了世界上最伟大的友谊和团结。伟大的中苏友谊将永远不断地发展和加强，这是任何力量都攻不破的。
    齐赫文斯基在会上作了题为“列宁和中国的报告”。他详细地阐述了列宁对中国人民革命斗争的无限关怀和列宁主义学说对中国革命的影响。
    在今天的晚会上讲话的，还有同列宁一起斗争和生活过的三位老布尔什维克。晚会上放映了有关列宁革命活动的纪录影片，并演出了文娱节目。
