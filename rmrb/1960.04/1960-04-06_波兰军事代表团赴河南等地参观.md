Title: Polish Military Delegation Visits Henan and Other Places
Author:
Date: 1960-04-06
Page: 5

    Xinhua News Agency, 5th. The Polish military delegation headed by General Bozhi Wofu Siqi departed Beijing by special plane this morning to visit Henan and other places. Accompanying them were General Peng Shaohui, Deputy Chief of the General Staff of the Chinese People's Liberation Army, Senior Colonel Zhu Kaiyin, Director of the Foreign Affairs Bureau of the Ministry of National Defense, and Lieutenant Colonel Jiang Yizhen, our military attache to Poland. During its stay in Beijing, the Polish military delegation visited the Great Hall of the People and the Palace Museum, and visited the Political Academy of the Chinese People's Liberation Army.

### 波兰军事代表团赴河南等地参观

1960-04-06
第5版()
专栏：

    波兰军事代表团赴河南等地参观
    新华社5日讯　以博齐沃夫斯基上将为首的波兰军事代表团，今天上午由北京乘专机去河南及各地参观访问。陪同的是中国人民解放军副总参谋长彭绍辉上将、国防部外事处处长朱开印大校和我国驻波兰武官姜一震中校。波兰军事代表团在北京期间曾参观了人民大会堂和故宫博物院，访问了中国人民解放军政治学院。
