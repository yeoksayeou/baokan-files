Title: All-China Federation of Trade Unions and Others Host Soiree for Hungarian Communist Youth League Central Art Troupe
Author:
Date: 1960-04-02
Page: 4

    Xinhua News Agency, April 1st. The All-China Federation of Trade Unions for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries, the Sino-Hungarian Friendship Association, and the Central Committee of the Chinese Communist Youth League jointly hosted a banquet tonight in honor of all members of the Hungarian Communist Youth League Central Art Troupe, led by Vadas Laszlo, head of the troupe, and Greg Peter, artistic director.
    Luo Jun, Vice-Chairman of the All-China Federation of Trade Unions for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries, Xu Guangxiao, Vice-Minister of the Ministry of Culture, Xiao San, Vice-President of the Sino-Hungarian Friendship Association, and Zeng Delin, Alternate Secretary of the Secretariat of the Central Committee of the Communist Youth League, attended the banquet.
    Sun Shen, Secretary of the Secretariat of the Chinese Musicians Association, and Sheng Jie, Secretary-General of the Chinese Dance Art Research Association, also attended the banquet.
    Szigeti, Chargé d'Affaires ad interim of the Hungarian Embassy in China, and his wife were also invited to attend the banquet.

### 对外文委等欢宴匈共青团中央艺术团

1960-04-02
第4版()
专栏：

    对外文委等欢宴匈共青团中央艺术团
    新华社1日讯　对外文化联络委员会、中国匈牙利友好协会、中国共产主义青年团中央委员会今晚联合设宴招待由团长瓦达斯·拉斯洛、艺术指导哥雷格·彼得率领的匈牙利共产主义青年团中央艺术团全体人员。
    对外文委副主任罗俊、文化部副部长徐光霄、中匈友协副会长萧三、共青团中央书记处候补书记曾德林等出席了宴会。
    出席宴会的还有中国音乐家协会书记处书记孙慎、中国舞蹈艺术研究会秘书长盛婕等。
    匈牙利驻华大使馆临时代办西格蒂和夫人也应邀出席了宴会。
