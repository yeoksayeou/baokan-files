Title: In Pursuit of Genuine Human Treatment: American Artist Requests to Become a German Citizen
Author:
Date: 1960-04-10
Page: 7

Xinhua News Agency, Berlin, April 9th. According to a Deutsche-Nachrichten-Agentur Berlin dispatch, the American artist Tronda and his wife submitted a request on the 8th, hoping to become citizens of the German Democratic Republic.

Tronda said: "In the West, artists have no rights." "In the German Democratic Republic, the working conditions are much better. Here, one can obtain truly human treatment."

Tronda is an actor from a circus in Hollywood and Los Angeles, USA.

### 为求得真正的人的待遇  美一艺术家请求成为德国公民

1960-04-10
第7版()
专栏：

    为求得真正的人的待遇
    美一艺术家请求成为德国公民
    新华社柏林9日电　据德意志通讯社柏林讯，美国艺术家特朗达和他的妻子8日提出请求，希望成为德意志民主共和国公民。
    特朗达说：“在西方，艺术家没有什么权利。”“在德意志民主共和国，工作条件好得多。这里，才能获得真正一个人的待遇。”
    特朗达是美国好莱坞和洛杉矶一个马戏团的演员。
