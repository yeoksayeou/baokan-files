Title: The Algerian National Liberation Army Achieves Brilliant Victories: 8,600 French Troops Killed or Wounded in March
Author:
Date: 1960-04-06
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, Cairo, April 4th. The headquarters of the Algerian National Liberation Army announced here today a communique regarding military operations from March 1st to 31st.

The communique stated that during this period, the Algerian National Liberation Army ambushed French military convoys 185 times in various parts of Algeria, engaged in 115 encounters with enemy forces, and carried out 579 acts of sabotage.

During this period, the Algerian National Liberation Army killed 5,495 French soldiers, wounded 3,178, shot down 51 aircraft, destroyed 10 trains, destroyed 374 armored vehicles, and captured 3,217 weapons. A total of 174 French soldiers surrendered to the Algerian National Liberation Army.

### 阿尔及利亚民族解放军战果辉煌  三月份毙伤法军八千六百多人

1960-04-06
第6版()
专栏：

    阿尔及利亚民族解放军战果辉煌
    三月份毙伤法军八千六百多人
    新华社开罗4日电　阿尔及利亚民族解放军总部今天在这里发表了关于3月1日到31日的军事行动的公报。
    公报说，在这期间，阿尔及利亚民族解放军在阿尔及利亚各地伏击法军运输队一百八十五次，同敌军进行了一百一十五次遭遇战，进行了五百七十九次破坏行动。
    在这期间，阿尔及利亚民族解放军打死法军五千四百九十五名，打伤三千一百七十八名，击落飞机五十一架，炸毁十列火车，击毁三百七十四辆装甲车辆，缴获三千二百一十七件武器。共有一百七十四名法军向阿尔及利亚民族解放军投诚。
