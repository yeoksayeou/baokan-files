Title: South African Prime Minister Wounded in Assassination Attempt
Author:
Date: 1960-04-11
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, Johannesburg, 10th Dispatch: South African Prime Minister Verwoerd was attacked in Johannesburg on the 9th and seriously injured.

When the Prime Minister, notorious for implementing a policy of racial discrimination, was attending an exhibition, someone shot at him, hitting him in the face. A white suspect was arrested on the spot.

### 南非总理遇刺受伤

1960-04-11
第6版()
专栏：

    南非总理遇刺受伤
    据新华社10日讯　约翰内斯堡消息：南非联邦总理维沃尔德9日在约翰内斯堡遇刺，受了重伤。
    当这个以执行种族歧视政策而臭名远扬的总理在出席一个展览会时，有人向他开枪，打中了他的脸部。有一个白人嫌疑犯当场被捕。
