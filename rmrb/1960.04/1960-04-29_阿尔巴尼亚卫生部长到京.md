Title: Albanian Minister of Health Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1960-04-29
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, April 28th. The Minister of Health of the People's Republic of Albania, Taqi Skëndi, arrived in Beijing by plane this afternoon. He is visiting China at the invitation of Li Dequan, Minister of Health of our country. Accompanying him were Nikola Shurbani, Deputy Director of the Medical Department of the University of Tirana, and Filip Pajma, Physician of the Health Department of the Ministry of Health. Zhang Kai, Vice Minister of Health, Ji Zhongpu, Director of the Medical Education Department, Zhang Zhiqiang, Vice President of the Academy of Medical Sciences, and others went to the airport to welcome them.

### 阿尔巴尼亚卫生部长到京

1960-04-29
第6版()
专栏：

    阿尔巴尼亚卫生部长到京
    新华社28日讯　阿尔巴尼亚人民共和国卫生部部长塔齐·斯坎迪，在今天下午乘飞机到达北京。他是应我国卫生部部长李德全邀请来我国访问的。随同前来的有地拉那大学医学系副主任尼古拉·舒勒班尼和卫生部卫生科医师菲利蒲·派玛。前往机场欢迎的有卫生部副部长张凯、医学教育司司长季钟朴，医学科学院副院长张之强等。
