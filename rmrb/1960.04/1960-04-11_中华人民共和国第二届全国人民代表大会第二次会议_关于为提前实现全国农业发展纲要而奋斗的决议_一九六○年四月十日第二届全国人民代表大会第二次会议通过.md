Title: Resolution of the Second Session of the Second National People's Congress of the People's Republic of China on Striving to Realize the National Agricultural Development Program Ahead of Schedule
Author:
Date: 1960-04-11
Page: 2

The Second Session of the Second National People's Congress of the People's Republic of China approved Vice Premier Tan Zhenlin's report on striving to realize the National Agricultural Development Program ahead of schedule.

The National Agricultural Development Program for 1956 to 1967, formulated by the Central Committee of the Communist Party of China, is a great program for developing China's socialist agriculture at high speed and building a new socialist countryside. Since this program was published in draft form in 1956, it has mobilized the enthusiasm of the broadest masses, promoted the rapid development of China's agricultural production and various economic and cultural undertakings in rural areas, and promoted the rapid development of the entire national economy. In particular, due to the continuous Great Leap Forward in 1958 and 1959 and the communization of rural people in China, some contents of the National Agricultural Development Program have been surpassed by actual life, and some requirements have been basically realized, mostly realized, or realized ahead of schedule in some areas. This makes it entirely possible for us to realize the National Agricultural Development Program two or three years ahead of schedule after a period of hard work.

The Congress passed the National Agricultural Development Program, only amending Article 27 of the revised draft on eliminating the four pests to: "Starting from 1956, within twelve years, basically eliminate rats, bedbugs, flies, and mosquitoes in all possible places." In addition, no amendments were made to any other articles.

Agriculture is the foundation of the national economy, and realizing the National Agricultural Development Program ahead of schedule is of extremely great significance for the continued leap forward of China's socialist construction. The Congress calls on: the people of all ethnic groups throughout the country, and all departments of industry, agriculture, transportation, finance, banking, trade, science, education, culture, health, and sports to work together to strive to realize the great task of realizing the National Agricultural Development Program two or three years ahead of schedule!

### 中华人民共和国第二届全国人民代表大会第二次会议  关于为提前实现全国农业发展纲要而奋斗的决议  一九六○年四月十日第二届全国人民代表大会第二次会议通过

1960-04-11
第2版()
专栏：

    中华人民共和国第二届全国人民代表大会第二次会议
    关于为提前实现全国农业发展纲要而奋斗的决议
    一九六○年四月十日第二届全国人民代表大会第二次会议通过
    第二届全国人民代表大会第二次会议批准谭震林副总理关于为提前实现全国农业发展纲要而奋斗的报告。
    中共中央制订的1956年到1967年全国农业发展纲要是高速度发展我国社会主义农业和建设社会主义新农村的伟大纲领。这个纲要自从1956年以草案形式公布以来，调动了最广大群众的积极性，促进了我国的农业生产和农村各项经济文化事业的迅速发展，并且推动了整个国民经济的迅速发展。特别是由于1958年和1959年两年的连续大跃进和我国农村人民公社化，全国农业发展纲要的某些内容已经被实际生活所超过，有些要求已经基本实现、大部实现，或者在部分地区提前实现了。这就使我们完全有可能再经过一个时期的艰苦努力提前两年或者三年实现全国农业发展纲要。
    大会通过全国农业发展纲要，只将修正草案第二十七条关于除四害的条文修改为：“从1956年起，在十二年内，在一切可能的地方，基本上消灭老鼠、臭虫、苍蝇和蚊子。”此外，各条都不作修改。
    农业是国民经济的基础，提前实现全国农业发展纲要，对于我国社会主义建设的继续跃进具有极其重大的意义。大会号召：全国各族人民，工业、农业、交通、财政、金融、贸易、科学、教育、文化、卫生、体育等一切部门共同努力，为提前两年或者三年实现全国农业发展纲要这个伟大的任务而奋斗！
