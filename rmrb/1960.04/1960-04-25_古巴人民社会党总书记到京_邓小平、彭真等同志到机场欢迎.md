Title: General Secretary of the Cuban People's Socialist Party Arrives in Beijing; Comrades Deng Xiaoping, Peng Zhen, and Others Welcome Him at the Airport

Author:

Date: 1960-04-25
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, April 24th. Blas Roca, General Secretary of the Cuban People's Socialist Party, and his wife, arrived in Beijing by plane this afternoon at the invitation of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of China for a visit to our country.

Those who welcomed them at the airport included: Deng Xiaoping, General Secretary of the CPC Central Committee; Peng Zhen, member of the Political Bureau; Yang Shangkun, alternate member of the Secretariat; Cai Chang and Wu Xiuquan, members of the Central Committee; and Li Qixin and other responsible personnel from the organs of the CPC Central Committee. (Photo attached)

### 古巴人民社会党总书记到京  邓小平、彭真等同志到机场欢迎

1960-04-25
第1版()
专栏：

    古巴人民社会党总书记到京
    邓小平、彭真等同志到机场欢迎
    新华社24日讯　应中国共产党中央委员会邀请，来我国访问的古巴人民社会党总书记布拉斯·罗加和他的夫人，今天下午坐飞机到达北京。
    到飞机场迎接的有：中共中央总书记邓小平，政治局委员彭真，书记处候补书记杨尚昆，中央委员蔡畅、伍修权，中共中央机关负责工作人员李启新等。（附图片）
    应中国共产党中央委员会邀请，来我国访问的古巴人民社会党总书记布拉斯·罗加和他的夫人4月24日乘飞机到达北京。图为中共中央总书记邓小平在机场上和布拉斯·罗加亲切握手。
          新华社记者　李长永摄
