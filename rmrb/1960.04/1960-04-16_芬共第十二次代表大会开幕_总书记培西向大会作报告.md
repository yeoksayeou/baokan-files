Title: Finnish Communist Party 12th Congress Opens; General Secretary Pessi Delivers Report to the Congress
Author: 
Date: 1960-04-16
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, Helsinki, April 15th: According to TASS, the 12th Congress of the Finnish Communist Party opened in Helsinki on the 15th.

    Two hundred and seventy-eight delegates and more than five hundred guests attended the congress. The guests included delegations from the Communist and Workers' Parties of the Soviet Union, China, France, Italy, Poland, Czechoslovakia, Romania, Hungary, Sweden, Denmark, Norway, and other countries.

    The congress will hear and discuss reports on the activities of the Party Central Committee, elect the Party's leading bodies, and make decisions on the Party's tasks and policies for the future.

    Finnish Communist Party General Secretary Pessi delivered a report to the congress.

### 芬共第十二次代表大会开幕  总书记培西向大会作报告

1960-04-16
第5版()
专栏：

    芬共第十二次代表大会开幕 
    总书记培西向大会作报告
    新华社15日讯　据塔斯社赫尔辛基讯：芬兰共产党第十二次代表大会15日在赫尔辛基开幕。
    参加大会的有二百七十八名代表和五百多位来宾，来宾中包括苏联、中国、法国、意大利、波兰、捷克斯洛伐克、罗马尼亚、匈牙利、瑞典、丹麦、挪威和其他国家的共产党和工人党的代表团。
    大会将听取和讨论党中央委员会活动的报告，选举党的领导机构和对党今后时期的任务和政策作出决定。
    芬兰共产党总书记培西向大会作了报告。
