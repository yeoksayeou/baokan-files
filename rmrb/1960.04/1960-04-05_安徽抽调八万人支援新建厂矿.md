Title: Anhui Draws 80,000 People to Support New Construction and Mining Enterprises
Author: Yu Feng
Date: 1960-04-05
Page: 5

Brief News

    Anhui Draws 80,000 People to Support New Construction and Mining Enterprises
    In the technological innovation and technological revolution movement, the industrial and transportation front of Anhui Province actively rectified labor organizations, adjusted layouts, and formulated new quota schemes, saving a large number of labor forces. In the first quarter, the province saved a total of more than 110,000 laborers, and more than 80,000 of these have now been assigned to support new and expanded factories and mines. In the first quarter, the labor productivity of Anhui Province increased by 25% compared with the fourth quarter of last year, and major products such as steel, iron, and coal all completed their plans ahead of schedule. (Yu Feng)

### 安徽抽调八万人支援新建厂矿
于风
1960-04-05
第5版()
专栏：简要新闻

    安徽抽调八万人支援新建厂矿
    安徽省工业交通战线在技术革新和技术革命运动中，积极整顿劳动组织、调整布局、制订新的定员定额方案，节约了大批的劳动力。第一季度内全省共节余劳力十一万多人，现已安排了八万多名，这些抽出的劳动力都及时支援了新建扩建厂矿。第一季度，安徽全省劳动生产率比去年第四季度提高25％，钢、铁、煤等主要产品都提前完成了计划。　（于风）
