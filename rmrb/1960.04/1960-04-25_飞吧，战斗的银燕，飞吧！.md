Title: Fly, Battle Silver Swallow, Fly!
Author: Bing Xin
Date: 1960-04-25
Page: 8

    Fly, Battle Silver Swallow, Fly!
    Bing Xin
    Fly, battle silver swallow, fly,
    Fly to the heroic and beautiful Cuba,
    Who says China and Cuba are separated by vast oceans,
    Cuba, it is not a distant country.
    Ocean waves push wave upon wave,
    The people of China and Cuba are heart to heart!
    We are close neighbors, we are blood relatives,
    We have common aspirations,
    We have a common enemy.
    The American imperialists are truly a joke,
    Wanting to buy a solemn country with stinking dollars!
    They madly drop bombs of destruction
    But only hasten the blooming of more revolutionary flowers!
    Fly, battle silver swallow, fly,
    Fully loaded with passionately overflowing poems—
    The warm east wind in the sky blows tighter and tighter,
    On the ground, the giant wheel of history rumbles forward!
    The united cries of the people of the world converge from all around,
    The imperialists are collapsing and fleeing,
    Let us raise high the battle flag,
    And sing them a funeral dirge!

### 飞吧，战斗的银燕，飞吧！
冰心
1960-04-25
第8版()
专栏：支援古巴人民

    飞吧，战斗的银燕，飞吧！
    冰心
    飞吧，战斗的银燕，飞吧，
    飞向英雄美丽的古巴，
    谁说中国和古巴重洋远隔，
    古巴，它不是一个遥远的国家。
    重洋洋水浪推浪，
    中巴人民心连心！
    我们是紧邻我们是血亲，
    我们有共同的愿望，
    我们有共同的敌人。
    美帝国主义者真是笑话，
    想用腥臭的美元买一个庄严的国家！
    他们疯狂地丢下毁灭的炸弹
    也只催开了更多的革命的鲜花！
    飞吧，战斗的银燕，飞吧，
    满载着热情洋溢诗篇——
    天上的和煦煦东风越吹越紧，
    地上历史的巨轮隆隆地前进！
    全世界人民团结的呼声四周合拢，
    帝国主义者正在溃散奔逃，
    让我们高举起战斗的旗帜，
    给他们唱一首送葬的挽歌！
