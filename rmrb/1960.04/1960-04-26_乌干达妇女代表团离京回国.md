Title: Ugandan Women's Delegation Leaves Beijing for Home

Author:

Date: 1960-04-26
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, April 25th: The Ugandan women's delegation, led by Ms. Eseza Rosalind Juko, which visited China at the invitation of the All-China Women's Federation, left Beijing by plane this morning for home after visiting Shanghai, Hangzhou, Luoyang and other places.

### 乌干达妇女代表团离京回国

1960-04-26
第4版()
专栏：

    乌干达妇女代表团离京回国
    新华社25日讯　应全国妇联邀请来我国参观访问的乌干达妇女代表团，在团长埃塞丽·罗萨林德·朱科率领下，在上海、杭州、洛阳等地参观访问后，今天上午乘飞机离开北京回国。
