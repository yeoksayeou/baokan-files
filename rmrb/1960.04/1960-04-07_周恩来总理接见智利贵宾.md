Title: Premier Zhou Enlai Receives Distinguished Guests from Chile
Author:
Date: 1960-04-07
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, 6th April. Premier Zhou Enlai received this afternoon the entire Chilean education delegation headed by Juan Gómez Millas, President of the University of Chile and President of the Latin American University Union, and the Chilean painter Julio Escámez.

Present at the reception were: Chu Tunan, President of The Chinese people's Association for Friendship with Foreign Countries and President of the China-Latin America Friendship Association, Zhang Zhixiang, Deputy Director of the Commission for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries, Jiang Nanxiang, Vice Minister of Education, Zhou Peiyuan, Vice President of Peking University, and Zhou Erfu, Vice President of the China-Latin America Friendship Association.

### 周恩来总理接见智利贵宾

1960-04-07
第6版()
专栏：

    周恩来总理接见智利贵宾
    新华社6日讯　周恩来总理今天下午接见了以智利大学校长、拉丁美洲大学联合会主席胡安·戈麦斯·米利亚斯为首的智利教育代表团全体团员和智利画家胡利奥·爱斯卡麦兹。
    接见时在座的有：中国人民对外文化协会会长、中国拉丁美洲友好协会会长楚图南，对外文化联络委员会副主任张致祥，教育部副部长蒋南翔，北京大学副校长周培源和中国拉丁美洲友好协会副会长周而复。
