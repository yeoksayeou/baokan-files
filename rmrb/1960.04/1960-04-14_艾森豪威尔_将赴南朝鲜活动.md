Title: Eisenhower to Visit South Korea for Activities
Author:
Date: 1960-04-14
Page: 6

　　Augusta dispatch: President Eisenhower's vacation headquarters announced on the 12th that Eisenhower will visit South Korea for one day in June.

　　It was announced that Eisenhower's visit to South Korea will take place during his visit to Japan. He is scheduled to stay in Japan from June 19th to 23rd. The visit to South Korea will take place on the 22nd.

　　Eisenhower announced his visit to South Korea at a time when the United States is violating the Korean Armistice Agreement, stepping up the transport of missiles to South Korea, and South Korea has recently experienced an uprising against the fascist rule of the Syngman Rhee clique.

### 艾森豪威尔  将赴南朝鲜活动

1960-04-14
第6版()
专栏：

    艾森豪威尔
    将赴南朝鲜活动
    本报讯　奥古斯大消息：美国总统艾森豪威尔休假总部12日宣布，艾森豪威尔将在6月到南朝鲜进行一天的访问。
    据宣布，艾森豪威尔对南朝鲜的访问将在访问日本期间进行。他预订6月19日到23日在日本逗留。对南朝鲜的访问将在22日进行。
    艾森豪威尔是在美国违返朝鲜停战协定，正加紧向南朝鲜运进导弹，和南朝鲜最近爆发了反对李承晚集团法西斯统治的起义的情况下，宣布访问南朝鲜的。
