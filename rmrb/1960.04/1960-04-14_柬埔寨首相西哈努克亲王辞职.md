Title: Cambodian Prime Minister Prince Sihanouk Resigns
Author:
Date: 1960-04-14
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, Phnom Penh, April 13th: According to an emergency announcement released by the Cambodian Ministry of Information on the 12th, Cambodian Prime Minister Prince Sihanouk submitted his resignation to the Regency Council on the 11th. The Cambodian News Agency today quoted a letter written by Sihanouk to the Regency Council on the 12th stating that Prince Sihanouk declined to form a new Cambodian cabinet. Sihanouk said that due to many matters requiring his personal attention, he could not accept this task for the time being. In his capacity as Chairman of the People's Socialist Community, he proposed that the Regency Council invite one of five important figures to form the new cabinet. These five individuals are Prince Norodom Pennouth, Nhiek Tioulong, Son Sann, Ung Hong Sath and Lon Nol.

### 柬埔寨首相西哈努克亲王辞职

1960-04-14
第6版()
专栏：

    柬埔寨首相西哈努克亲王辞职
    新华社金边13日电　据柬埔寨宣传部12日发表的紧急通告，柬埔寨首相西哈努克亲王11日向摄政委员会提出了辞呈。柬埔寨新闻社今天援引西哈努克12日写给摄政委员会的信说，西哈努克亲王拒绝组织柬埔寨新内阁。西哈努克说，由于有许多需要他亲自照顾的事情，所以他暂时不能接受这项任务。他以人民社会同盟主席的身份提议摄政委员会请五位重要人物之一出来组织新内阁。这五个人是宾努亲王，桑云，松山，?刁隆和朗诺。
