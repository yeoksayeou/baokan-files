Title: Chinese Delegation to Casablanca Fair Hosts Journalists, Introduces China's Construction Achievements and the Chinese Pavilion

Author: 
Date: 1960-04-29
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, Rabat, 27th—The Chinese delegation participating in the 16th Casablanca International Fair held a press conference at the Chinese Pavilion on the afternoon of the 26th. In addition to the journalists, more than 40 people attended the press conference, including the Chairman of the Fair, the Director of the Moroccan National Tea Monopoly Bureau, and the Director of the Casablanca Commercial Bureau.

    Zhang Tingyu, head of the Chinese delegation, introduced the great achievements of China's economic construction and the situation of the Chinese Pavilion this year at the press conference. He said, "There is no doubt that this exhibition will help strengthen and develop the traditional friendship and economic and trade relations between the peoples of China and Morocco."

    Dutton, Chairman of the Casablanca International Fair, then spoke. He said that this year, the Chinese Pavilion is the most important pavilion in the International Fair. People can see the achievements of the hard work of the Chinese people and government. He hopes that the friendship and trade relations between China and Morocco will further develop.

    After the press conference, the head of the Chinese delegation accompanied the guests to visit the exhibits.

    The 16th Casablanca International Fair will open on the 28th. This is the fourth time that China has participated in this fair. This year, the Chinese Pavilion covers an area of 3,690 square meters and has more than 2,000 exhibits, as well as a kiosk and a cinema.

### 参加卡萨布兰卡博览会中国代表团招待记者  介绍我国建设成就和中国馆情况

1960-04-29
第6版()
专栏：

    参加卡萨布兰卡博览会中国代表团招待记者
    介绍我国建设成就和中国馆情况
    新华社拉巴特27日电　参加第十六届卡。布兰卡国际博览会的中国代表团26日下午在中国馆举行了记者招待会。参加记者招待会的除了记者们以外，还有博览会主席、摩洛哥全国茶叶专卖局局长和卡萨布兰卡商业局局长等四十多人。
    中国代表团团长张廷禹在记者招待会上介绍了中国经济建设的巨大成就，以及今年中国馆的情况。他说，“毫无疑问，这次展览将有助于加强并发展中国同摩洛哥两国人民之间的传统友谊以及经济和贸易关系。”
    卡萨布兰卡国际博览会主席达顿接着讲话。他说，在今年，中国展览馆是国际博览会最重要的展览馆。人们可以看到中国人民和政府辛勤建设的成就。他希望中国同摩洛哥之间的友谊和贸易关系进一步发展。
    记者招待会后中国代表团团长陪同客人参观了展览品。
    第十六届卡萨布兰卡国际博览会将在28日开幕，中国参加这个博览会这次已是第四次。今年中国馆的面积达三千六百九十平方米，一共有二千多件展览品，此外还有售货亭和电影院。
