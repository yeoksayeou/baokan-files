Title: Nigerian Representative Kassim Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1960-04-18
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, April 17th: Nurudeen Kassim, the Nigerian representative invited by the Chinese People's Institute of Foreign Affairs and the All-China Youth Federation to participate in the "Capital's People Commemorate the Fifth Anniversary of the Bandung Conference and Celebrate the Inaugural Meeting of the Chinese-African People's Friendship Association" and to visit our country, arrived in Beijing by plane this afternoon.

Those who welcomed him at the airport included Wu Xiaoda, Deputy Secretary-General of the Chinese People's Institute of Foreign Affairs and Director of the Chinese-African People's Friendship Association, and representatives from the All-China Youth Federation and the Chinese Committee for Asian-African Solidarity.

### 尼日利亚代表凯桑巴到京

1960-04-18
第6版()
专栏：

    尼日利亚代表凯桑巴到京
    新华社17日讯　应中国人民外交学会和中华全国青年联合会邀请，来我国参加“首都各界人民纪念万隆会议五周年及庆祝中国非洲人民友好协会成立大会”和访问的尼日利亚代表努鲁丁·凯桑巴，今天下午乘飞机到达北京。
    到机场欢迎的有中国人民外交学会副秘书长、中国非洲人民友好协会理事吴晓达和中华全国青年联合会、中国亚非团结委员会的代表等。
