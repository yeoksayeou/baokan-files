Title: Soviet Aeromodelling Team Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1960-04-08
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, April 7th: The Soviet Aeromodelling Team, a delegation of five, arrived in Beijing by plane on the 7th at our invitation. They will perform radio-controlled model aircraft, helicopter model aircraft, and jet model aircraft flight demonstrations in Beijing on the 10th.

    The Aeromodelling Team is led by N. Bolotov, Vice Chairman of the Soviet Central Chkalov Aviation Club. The coach is Sherbak. The other three are aeromodelling athletes, including Master of Sport Ivannikov, who holds the world record for the flight speed of jet model aircraft (301 kilometers per hour); Master of Sport Aierlier, thirty-eight years old, has been involved in aeromodelling activities for twenty-five years; and the young Slepkov specializes in the research of helicopter model aircraft.

    The Aeromodelling Team will also visit and perform in Shanghai, Hangzhou, Nanjing, and other places later.

### 苏联航空模型队到京

1960-04-08
第5版()
专栏：

    苏联航空模型队到京
    新华社7日讯　应邀来我国访问的苏联航空模型队一行五人，7日乘飞机到达北京。他们将于10日在北京举行无线电操纵模型飞机、直升模型飞机和喷气式模型飞机的飞行表演。
    航空模型队由苏联中央契卡洛夫航空俱乐部副主席尼·博洛托夫率领。教练是谢尔巴克。另外三名是航空模型选手，其中运动健将伊万尼科夫是喷气式模型飞机飞行速度世界纪录（每小时三百零一公里）保持者；运动健将、三十八岁的艾尔列尔参加航空模型活动已有二十五年的历史；年轻的斯列普科夫在研究直升模型飞机方面有专长。
    航空模型队以后还将到上海、杭州、南京等地访问表演。
