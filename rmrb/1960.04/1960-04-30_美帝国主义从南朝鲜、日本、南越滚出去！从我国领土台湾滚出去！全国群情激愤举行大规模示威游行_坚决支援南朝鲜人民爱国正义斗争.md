Xinhua News Agency

Yankee Imperialists, Get Out of South Korea, Japan, and South Vietnam! Get Out of Taiwan, Our Territory!
The Whole Country, Filled with Indignation, Holds Large-Scale Demonstrations and Parades, Resolutely Supporting the Patriotic and Just Struggle of the South Korean People

Resolutely Support the South Korean People
Shenyang

Xinhua News Agency, Shenyang, April 29th: Two hundred thousand people from all walks of life in Shenyang held a massive rally this afternoon to support the patriotic and just struggle of the South Korean people.

Huang Huoqing, First Secretary of the Liaoning Provincial Committee of the Communist Party of China, Jiao Ruoyu, First Secretary of the Shenyang Municipal Committee, and other party and government leaders, as well as heads of people's organizations and democratic parties in Liaoning Province and Shenyang City, attended today's meeting. Also attending the meeting were Soviet experts in Shenyang and Korean students and interns.

Jiao Ruoyu, Standing Committee Member of the Liaoning Provincial Committee of the Communist Party of China and First Secretary of the Shenyang Municipal Committee, spoke at the meeting first. He said, "We, the people of all walks of life in Shenyang, along with the people of the whole country, express our greatest indignation at the bloody atrocities of the American imperialists and the Syngman Rhee clique against the South Korean people. We express our deep sympathy and resolute support for the heroic patriotic and just struggle of the South Korean people against the fascist rule of the American-Rhee group." He also expressed his firm support for the just and patriotic struggle of the Japanese people against American imperialism and the Nobusuke Kishi clique.

Also speaking at the meeting were responsible cadres from the trade union, the Communist Youth League, and the Women's Federation, as well as representatives from various democratic parties, workers, members of urban people's communes, farmers, and students.

The meeting unanimously adopted a telegram supporting the patriotic and just struggle of the South Korean people, followed by a massive demonstration.

In the parade, there were returned overseas Chinese who had lived in Korea for many years and volunteer soldiers and laborers who had participated in the great War to Resist U.S. Aggression and Aid Korea. While shouting slogans, they excitedly expressed to the surrounding masses their sympathy for the South Korean people and their infinite indignation towards the American-Rhee group.

Drive the American Aggressors Out of South Korea
Yanbian

Xinhua News Agency, Yanji, April 29th: More than 30,000 people from all ethnic groups and all walks of life in Yanji City, the capital of the Yanbian Korean Autonomous Prefecture in Jilin Province, solemnly gathered this afternoon to resolutely support the patriotic and just struggle of the South Korean people.

The people from all ethnic groups and all walks of life who participated in the conference included workers, farmers, students, members of urban communes, and well-organized militia teams. Responsible persons of the Yanbian Korean Autonomous Prefectural Committee of the Communist Party of China, people's organizations of the Autonomous Prefecture, Yanji Municipal Committee of the Communist Party of China, and people's organizations of Yanji City all attended the conference. All members of the Hamgyongbuk-do Local Industry Internship Delegation of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea, which is visiting Yanbian Korean Autonomous Prefecture, also attended the conference.

The conference began at 2:00 pm. Zhu Dehai, First Secretary of the Yanbian Korean Autonomous Prefectural Committee of the Communist Party of China, spoke at the conference. He sternly condemned the bloody massacre of the South Korean people by the American-Rhee gang, saying that the South Korean people, with the support of the people of the world, insisted on the struggle, and would certainly be able to drive the American aggressors out of South Korea and realize the peaceful reunification of the motherland.

Next, Choi Eung-won, head of the Hamgyongbuk-do Local Industry Internship Delegation of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea, spoke at the conference. He said that the Chinese people's active support and solidarity with the patriotic and just struggle of the South Korean people would surely encourage the people of the southern half of Korea who are fighting for national liberation, democratic freedom, and the right to survival to move from victory to victory. He finally said that the Korean people's struggle for the peaceful reunification of the motherland will surely be victorious.

Also speaking at the conference were the heads of various mass organizations in the Yanbian Korean Autonomous Prefecture and Yanji City, as well as representatives of workers, farmers, and students. They all expressed their firm support for the patriotic and just struggle of the South Korean people.

Insisting on the Struggle Will Surely Lead to Victory
Lhasa

Xinhua News Agency, Lhasa, April 29th: Twenty thousand people from all walks of life in Lhasa City held a mass rally today at the newly built Lhasa People's Stadium to resolutely support the patriotic and just struggle of the South Korean people.

Leading cadres from the Tibet Work Committee of the Communist Party of China, the Lhasa Municipal Committee of the Communist Party of China, the Preparatory Committee of the Tibet Autonomous Region, and the Tibet Military Region, as well as the heads of people's organizations in Lhasa City, all participated in today's mass rally.

Fang Chixin, Minister of the Propaganda Department of the Tibet Work Committee of the Communist Party of China, spoke at the conference. He said that the people of Tibet, like the people of the whole country, expressed immense anger at the atrocities of the American imperialists and the Syngman Rhee reactionary clique in suppressing and massacring the patriotic South Korean people, and expressed great sympathy and support for the South Korean people who are fighting for national liberation, democratic freedom, and the right to survival. We believe that the South Korean people, with the support of the people of the world who love peace and uphold justice, strengthen unity, insist on struggle, will certainly be able to completely defeat the fascist rule of the Syngman Rhee criminal group, and will certainly be able to drive the American aggressors out of South Korea, and realize the desire for the peaceful reunification of the motherland. Representatives of workers, youth, students, women, the Tibet Committee of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference, and the Buddhist community in Lhasa City all spoke at the mass rally.

After the conference, the emotionally charged masses held a demonstration in Lhasa City.

### 美帝国主义从南朝鲜、日本、南越滚出去！从我国领土台湾滚出去！全国群情激愤举行大规模示威游行  坚决支援南朝鲜人民爱国正义斗争

1960-04-30
第3版()
专栏：

    美帝国主义从南朝鲜、日本、南越滚出去！从我国领土台湾滚出去！
    全国群情激愤举行大规模示威游行  坚决支援南朝鲜人民爱国正义斗争
    坚决支持南朝鲜人民
    沈阳
    据新华社沈阳29日电　沈阳市各界人民二十万人今天下午举行了声势浩大的支援南朝鲜人民爱国正义斗争大会。
    中共辽宁省委第一书记黄火青、沈阳市委第一书记焦若愚等党政领导人和辽宁省、沈阳市各人民团体、各民主党派负责人参加了今天的大会；参加大会的还有在沈阳的苏联专家和朝鲜留学生和实习生。
    中共辽宁省委常委、中共沈阳市委第一书记焦若愚首先在大会上讲话，他说：“我们沈阳市各界人民，和全国人民一道，对美帝国主义和李承晚集团对南朝鲜人民的血腥暴行表示最大的愤慨。对南朝鲜人民反对美李集团法西斯统治的英勇的爱国正义斗争，表示深切的同情和坚决的支持。”同时他还表示坚决支持日本人民反对美帝国主义和岸信介集团的正义爱国斗争。
    在大会上讲话的还有：工会、共青团、妇联的负责干部以及各民主党派代表、工人、城市人民公社社员、农民、学生等。
    大会一致通过了支援南朝鲜人民爱国正义斗争的通电，接着举行了声势浩大的示威游行。
    在游行的队伍里，有旅居朝鲜多年的归国华侨和曾经参加伟大抗美援朝战争的志愿军战士、民工，他们一面高呼口号，一面激动地向周围群众表示自己对南朝鲜人民的同情和对美李集团的无限愤慨。
    把美国侵略军赶出南朝鲜
    延边
    据新华社延吉29日电　吉林省延边朝鲜族自治州首府延吉市各族各界三万多人，今天下午隆重集会，坚决支援南朝鲜人民的爱国正义斗争。
    参加大会的各族各界人民，包括工人、农民、学生、城市公社社员和严整的民兵队伍。中共延边朝鲜族自治州委、自治州各人民团体和中共延吉市委、延吉市各人民团体负责人，都出席了大会。正在延边朝鲜族自治州访问的朝鲜民主主义人民共和国咸镜北道地方工业实习团全体成员也出席了大会。
    大会在下午二时开始，中共延边朝鲜族自治州委第一书记朱德海在大会上讲了话。他严厉谴责美李匪帮血腥屠杀南朝鲜人民的暴行，他说，南朝鲜人民在全世界人民支持下，坚持斗争，一定能够把美国侵略军赶出南朝鲜，实现祖国的和平统一。
    接着，朝鲜民主主义人民共和国咸镜北道地方工业实习团团长崔应元在大会上讲话。他说，中国人民积极支持和声援南朝鲜人民的爱国正义斗争，一定会鼓舞为争取民族解放、民主自由和生存权利而斗争的朝鲜南半部人民从胜利走向胜利。他最后说，朝鲜人民争取和平统一祖国的斗争一定会取得胜利。
    在大会上讲话的，还有延边朝鲜族自治州和延吉市各群众团体的负责人，以及工人、农民、学生的代表。他们一致表示坚决支援南朝鲜人民的爱国正义斗争。
    坚持斗争一定能胜利
