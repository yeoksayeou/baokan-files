Title: Liu Changsheng Sends a Telegram to the Prime Minister of Togo, Warmly Congratulating Togo on its Independence
Author:
Date: 1960-04-28
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, April 27th. Liu Changsheng, President of the Chinese-African People's Friendship Association, sent a telegram today to the Prime Minister of Togo, Sylvanus Olympio, warmly congratulating Togo on its independence.

Liu Changsheng's telegram said:

"On the occasion of Togo's declaration of independence, I would like to extend, on behalf of the Chinese-African People's Friendship Association, the warmest congratulations to you and, through you, to the heroic people of Togo who have opposed imperialism and colonialism and striven for national independence. I wish Togo national prosperity and the people happiness!

"May the friendship between the Chinese and Togolese people grow stronger every day!"

### 刘长胜致电多哥总理  热烈祝贺多哥独立

1960-04-28
第6版()
专栏：

    刘长胜致电多哥总理
    热烈祝贺多哥独立
    新华社27日讯　中国非洲人民友好协会会长刘长胜今天打电报给多哥总理西凡虞·奥林匹欧，热烈祝贺多哥独立。
    刘长胜的电报说：
    “值此多哥宣布独立之际，我谨代表中国非洲人民友好协会，向您并通过您向英勇地反对帝国主义和殖民主义，争取民族独立的多哥人民致以最热烈的祝贺。并祝多哥国家繁荣、人民幸福！
    “愿中国人民和多哥人民之间的友谊日益发展！”
