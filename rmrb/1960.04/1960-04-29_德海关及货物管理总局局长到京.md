Title: Director of the German Customs and Goods Management Administration Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1960-04-29
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, April 28th: At the invitation of our country, Anton Ruh, Director of the Customs and Goods Management Administration of the Ministry of Foreign and Intra-German Trade of the German Democratic Republic, and his party of three arrived in Beijing by plane this afternoon for a visit. Welcoming them at the airport were Lin Haiyun, Vice Minister of Foreign Trade, Yin Zhiyue, Deputy Director of the General Administration of Customs of the Ministry of Foreign Trade, and others.

### 德海关及货物管理总局局长到京

1960-04-29
第6版()
专栏：

    德海关及货物管理总局局长到京
    新华社28日讯　应邀来我国参观访问的德意志民主共和国对外及东西德贸易部海关及货物管理总局局长安东·鲁等一行三人，今天下午乘飞机到达北京。到机场欢迎的有，对外贸易部副部长林海云、对外贸易部海关总署副署长殷之钺等。
