Title: Hungary Holds Grand National Day Banquet to Celebrate the 15th Anniversary of Liberation
Author:
Date: 1960-04-06
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, Budapest, April 4th: The Presidium of the Hungarian People's Republic held a grand banquet at the Parliament Building on the evening of April 4th to celebrate the 15th anniversary of the liberation of Hungary.

Hungarian Socialist Workers' Party and government leaders Kádár, Dobi, and Münnich, ministers of the government, and representatives from all walks of life attended the banquet.

The Soviet delegation headed by Mazurov, the Chinese and other fraternal countries' press delegations, and diplomatic envoys from various countries to Hungary were also invited to attend the banquet.

President Dobi of the Presidium of the Hungarian People's Republic delivered a speech at the banquet, congratulating everyone on the festival and extending a warm welcome to the foreign guests.

At the meeting, everyone frequently toasted the great achievements Hungary has made under the leadership of the Party in the past 15 years, and the great, brotherly unity of the socialist camp.

### 庆祝解放十五周年  匈牙利举行盛大国庆宴会

1960-04-06
第5版()
专栏：

    庆祝解放十五周年
    匈牙利举行盛大国庆宴会
    新华社布达佩斯4日电　匈牙利人民共和国主席团4日晚间在国会大厦举行盛大宴会，庆祝匈牙利解放十五周年。
    匈牙利社会主义工人党和政府领导人卡达尔、道比、明尼赫，政府各部部长，各界代表都出席了宴会。
    以马祖罗夫为首的苏联代表团、中国和其他一些兄弟国家的新闻工作代表团和各国驻匈牙利的外交使节也应邀出席了宴会。
    匈牙利人民共和国主席团主席道比在宴会上致了词，向大家祝贺节日，并且对外国来宾们表示热烈欢迎。
    会上，大家频频为匈牙利十五年来在党的领导下取得的伟大成就、为社会主义阵营各国兄弟般的伟大团结而干杯。
