Title: Hungarian Communist Youth League Central Art Troupe Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1960-04-01
Page: 3
Xinhua News Agency, March 31st. The Hungarian Communist Youth League Central Art Troupe, a group of twenty-nine people led by the head of the troupe, Vadas Laszlo, and artistic director, Greiger Peter, arrived in Beijing by train this afternoon after visiting the Democratic People's Republic of Korea. They are visiting China for performances according to the 1960 executive plan of the Sino-Hungarian Cultural Cooperation Agreement.

Among those who went to the station to welcome them were Luo Jun, Deputy Director of the Commission for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries, Yang Haibo, Secretary of the Secretariat of the Central Committee of the Communist Youth League, and Zhong Ling, Director of the Sino-Hungarian Friendship Association.

The Hungarian Communist Youth League Central Art Troupe has participated in the World Youth and Students for Peace and Friendship Festival many times and has won six awards. The art troupe has also visited the Soviet Union, Czechoslovakia, the German Democratic Republic, France, Iraq, the Democratic People's Republic of Korea and other countries for performances, and has been widely welcomed by audiences. The art troupe will hold public performances in Beijing from April 1st and will participate in the activities of the Sino-Hungarian Friendship Week.

### 匈牙利共青团中央艺术团到京

1960-04-01
第3版()
专栏：

    匈牙利共青团中央艺术团到京
    新华社31日讯　匈牙利共产主义青年团中央艺术团一行二十九人，由团长瓦达斯·拉斯洛和艺术指导哥雷格·彼得率领，在访问朝鲜民主主义人民共和国后，今天下午乘火车到达北京。他们是根据中匈文化合作协定1960年执行计划来我国进行访问演出的。
    前往车站欢迎的有对外文化联络委员会副主任罗俊，共青团中央书记处书记杨海波，中匈友协理事钟灵等。
    匈牙利共青团中央艺术团曾多次参加世界青年与学生和平友谊联欢节，并曾六次获奖。艺术团还曾到苏联、捷克斯洛伐克、德意志民主共和国、法国、伊拉克、朝鲜民主主义人民共和国等国家进行访问演出，受到观众的广泛欢迎。艺术团4月1日起将在北京举行公演，并将参加中匈友好周的活动。
