    Mongolian Great People's Khural

    The Mongolian Great People's Khural means the All-Mongolian People's Congress. It is the highest organ of state power in the Mongolian People's Republic. It has the powers to exercise the state's legislative power, approve and amend the constitution, formulate the basic principles and measures of domestic and foreign policies, and elect the Presidium of the Great People's Khural. Its local organization is the Khural of Workers' Deputies.

    The representatives of the Great People's Khural are elected by citizens over the age of eighteen according to the principles of universal, equal, and direct suffrage by secret ballot. It is re-elected every three years. The Great People's Khural holds an annual regular meeting. It has one chairman and two vice-chairmen. The current chairman is Bayanle Jargalsaikhan.

    During the adjournment of the Great People's Khural, its functions are exercised by the Presidium of the Great People's Khural. The current chairman of the Presidium is Zhamsrangiin Sambuu.
                      (Xinhua News Agency)

### 蒙古大人民呼拉尔

1960-04-08
第1版()
专栏：

    蒙古大人民呼拉尔
    蒙古大人民呼拉尔的意思就是全蒙古人民代表大会。它是蒙古人民共和国的最高权力机关，它具有行使国家的立法权、批准和修改宪法、制订内外政策的基本原则和措施，以及选举大人民呼拉尔主席团等职权。它的地方组织是劳动人民代表呼拉尔。
    大人民呼拉尔的代表是由年满十八岁以上的公民按普遍、平等、直接的原则用秘密投票的办法产生的，每三年改选一次。大人民呼拉尔每年举行一次常会。它有主席一人和副主席二人，现任主席是巴扬勒·贾尔卡赛汗。
    在大人民呼拉尔闭会期间，由大人民呼拉尔主席团代行其职权，现任主席团主席是扎木斯朗金·桑布。
                      （新华社）
