Title: Nguyễn Duy Trinh Discusses This Year's National Plan at the Vietnamese National Assembly; Continue to Build Socialism Rapidly; Hoang Anh Reports on Last Year's National Budget Implementation and This Year's National Budget

Author:

Date: 1960-04-13
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, Hanoi, April 11th. Nguyen Duy Trinh, Director of the Vietnamese State Planning Commission, delivered a report on "The Plan for Transforming and Developing the Economy and Developing Culture of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam in 1960" at the twelfth session of the Vietnamese National Assembly, which opened this afternoon.

He said that the general direction of the 1960 National Plan of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam is: to continue to move forward rapidly, to immediately and steadily advance the socialist transformation and socialist construction in the North, to ensure the overfulfillment and early completion of the plan, and to enable the people to enter the Five-Year Plan with confidence and excitement.

Nguyen Duy Trinh said that in 1960: the initial cooperativization of agriculture and handicrafts and the joint state-private operation of private capitalist industry and commerce should be basically completed, agricultural and industrial production should be vigorously developed, and the focus should be on promoting the development of agriculture, mainly grain, in accordance with the principle of comprehensiveness, speed, and stability. While vigorously developing capital construction in industry, agriculture, and other economic sectors, attention should be paid to the development of culture, education, public services, and housing construction.

When talking about the main indicators of the 1960 National Plan, Nguyen Duy Trinh said that the total value of industrial and agricultural output in 1960 will reach 4,335 million yuan, an increase of 14.3% compared with 1959. Among them, the total output value of agriculture and sideline occupations will be 2,687 million yuan, an increase of 11.5% compared with 1959. The total output value of industry and handicrafts will reach 1,648 million yuan, an increase of 19.2% compared with 1959. Among them, state-owned industry will be 732 million yuan, an increase of 26.4% compared with 1959.

In terms of culture and education, Nguyen Duy Trinh said that in 1960, 4,700 new university students will be enrolled, an increase of 4.6% compared with 1959. The planned number of new students enrolled in secondary vocational schools and primary schools will increase by 49.6% and 7% respectively compared with 1959; medical units will increase by 35.7% compared with 1959.

Nguyen Duy Trinh pointed out that in order to complete the 1960 task, it is necessary to strengthen leadership in ideology, policy, and organization. Special attention must be paid to promoting socialist ideological education, so that everyone attaches importance to the interests of the state and the collective, and is determined to strive to complete tasks and complete plans. At the same time, it is necessary to be vigilant and promptly stop and smash all the enemy's sabotage conspiracies and actions.

The afternoon meeting also heard a report on the draft Compulsory Military Service Law by Nguyen Van Vinh, Deputy Minister of National Defense of Vietnam.

At the meeting held tonight, Vietnamese Minister of Finance Hoang Anh made a report on the implementation of the 1959 National Budget and the 1960 National Budget of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam.

Hoang Anh said that the total revenue and total expenditure in the 1960 National Budget are 1,445.8 million yuan, an increase of 20.4% and 27.9% respectively compared with 1959.

Hoang Anh pointed out that there are many favorable conditions for realizing and overfulfilling the 1960 National Budget, especially the favorable conditions in the political field, which must be brought into play. He also pointed out that practicing economy is a very important financial and economic policy of socialism.

Hoang Anh expressed his belief that under the leadership of the Vietnamese Labor Party and President Ho Chi Minh, the 1960 draft National Budget will be well implemented.

At the beginning of the report, Hoang Anh talked about the implementation of the 1959 National Budget. He said that the actual total revenue of the 1959 National Budget was 1,200.4 million yuan, equivalent to 103.3% of the revenue budget, an increase of 31.7% compared with the actual revenue in 1958. The actual total expenditure of the 1959 National Budget was 1,130.2 million yuan, an increase of 26.4% compared with the actual expenditure in 1958.

Hoang Anh said that the results of the implementation of the 1959 National Budget show the superiority of the socialist system in North Vietnam. In 1959, industrial and agricultural production increased by nearly 16% compared with 1958, of which state-owned industry increased by more than 50%. The people's lives are getting better and better, and the people's purchasing power is getting higher and higher. This is the solid foundation of our finance. (Attached picture)

### 阮维祯在越南国会谈今年国家计划  继续快步猛进建设社会主义  黄英作关于去年国家预算执行情况和今年国家预算的报告

1960-04-13
第5版()
专栏：

    阮维祯在越南国会谈今年国家计划
    继续快步猛进建设社会主义
    黄英作关于去年国家预算执行情况和今年国家预算的报告
    新华社河内11日电　越南国家计划委员会主任阮维祯在今天下午开幕的越南国会第十二次会议上作了关于“越南民主共和国1960年改造和发展经济、发展文化的计划”的报告。
    他说，越南民主共和国1960年国家计划的总的方向是：继续快步猛进，立即使北方的社会主义改造和社会主义建设稳步前进，保证超额和提前完成计划，使人民满怀信心和兴奋地进入五年计划。
    阮维祯说，1960年要：基本完成农业和手工业的初级合作化和私营资本主义工商业的公私合营工作，大力发展农业和工业生产，重点是根据全面、快速、稳当的方针，促进以粮食为主的农业的发展。在大力发展工农业和其他经济部门的基本建设的同时，注意发展文化、教育、公共事业和住宅建设。
    阮维祯在谈到1960年国家计划的主要指标时说，1960年工农业总产值将达到四十三亿三千五百万元，比1959年增长14.3％。其中农业和副业总产值为二十六亿八千七百万元，比1959年增长11.5％。工业和手工业总产值达到十六亿四千八百万元，比1959年增长19.2％。其中国营工业为七亿三千二百万元，比1959年增长26.4％。
    在文化教育方面，阮维祯说，1960年新招大学生四千七百人，比1959年增长4.6％，中等专业学校和小学新招学生人数计划分别比1959年增长49.6％和7％；医疗单位比1959年增加35.7％。
    阮维祯指出，为了完成1960年任务，必须加强思想、政策和组织方面的领导。必须特别注意促进社会主义的思想教育，使得大家都重视国家和集体利益，决心为完成任务、完成计划而奋斗，同时必须提高警惕，及时制止和粉碎敌人的一切破坏阴谋和行动。
    下午的会议还听取了越南国防部副部长阮文荣所作的关于义务兵役法草案的报告。
    在今天晚上举行的会议上，越南财政部长黄英作了关于越南民主共和国1959年国家预算的执行情况和1960年国家预算的报告。
    黄英说，1960年国家预算中的总收入和总支出均为十四亿四千五百八十万元，分别比1959年增长20.4％和27.9％。
    黄英指出，实现和超额实现1960年国家预算具备有很多有利条件，特别是政治方面的有利条件，必须加以发挥。他还指出，厉行节约是社会主义的非常重要的财政经济政策。
    黄英表示相信，在越南劳动党和胡志明主席的领导下，1960年国家预算草案将会得到良好的执行。
    在报告开始时，黄英谈到1959年国家预算的执行情况，他说，1959年国家预算的实际收入总数是十二亿零四十万元，等于收入预算的103.3％，比1958年的实际收入增长31.7％。1959年国家预算的实际支出总数是十一亿三千零二十万元，比1958年的实际支出增长26.4％。
    黄英说，1959年国家预算的执行结果说明了北越社会主义制度的优越性，1959年工农业生产比1958年增长近16％，其中国营工业增长50％以上。人民的生活越来越改善，人民的购买力越来越提高，这是我们的财政的稳固基础。（附图片）
