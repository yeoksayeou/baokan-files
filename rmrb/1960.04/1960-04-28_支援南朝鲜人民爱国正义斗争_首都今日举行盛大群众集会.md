Title: Support the South Korean People's Patriotic and Just Struggle - Grand Mass Rally Held in the Capital Today
Author:
Date: 1960-04-28
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, 27th. The people of all circles in the capital will hold a grand mass rally on the afternoon of the 28th to support the South Korean people's patriotic and just struggle. The Central People's Broadcasting Station will broadcast the live proceedings of the conference starting at 2:50 pm on the 28th, using the frequency of the first program. Beijing Television will also broadcast it simultaneously.

### 支援南朝鲜人民爱国正义斗争  首都今日举行盛大群众集会

1960-04-28
第1版()
专栏：

    支援南朝鲜人民爱国正义斗争
    首都今日举行盛大群众集会
    新华社27日讯　首都各界人民将在28日下午举行盛大群众集会，支援南朝鲜人民爱国正义斗争。中央人民广播电台将从28日下午两时五十分开始，用第一种节目的频率转播大会的实况。北京电视台也将同时转播。
