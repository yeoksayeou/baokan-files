Title: Moscow Circles Celebrate "Africa Day"; Galmashev Says African People Will Eventually Achieve Liberation
Author:
Date: 1960-04-16
Page: 6
Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, April 15th: According to TASS, Moscow, on the 14th, people from all walks of life in Moscow held an evening party at the Moscow "House of Friendship with the Peoples of All Countries" to celebrate "Africa Day." Staff members of the embassies of the United Arab Republic, Ethiopia, Ghana, and Sudan in the Soviet Union, as well as African students studying in Moscow, were invited to attend the party.
    Galmashev, Vice Chairman of the Council of the Soviet Association for Friendship with the Peoples of Africa, spoke at the meeting. He said that the Soviet people are happy for the independence achieved by many African countries. He said that we believe that the forces of peace and progress will eventually defeat the forces of the Cold War and oppression, and the people of African countries will be forever liberated from the oppression of colonialism and imperialism.
    Bochkarev, a member of the Council of the Soviet Association for Friendship with the Peoples of Africa, said in his speech that the national liberation movement in Africa is an inevitable movement of our time. Independent African countries are playing an increasingly active role in the struggle for peace and international security.
    The meeting sent a telegram to the Second Asian-African People's Solidarity Conference being held in Conakry, congratulating it on its opening.

### 莫斯科各界庆祝“非洲日”  加尔马歇夫说非洲人民终将获得解放

1960-04-16
第6版()
专栏：

    莫斯科各界庆祝“非洲日”
    加尔马歇夫说非洲人民终将获得解放
    新华社15日讯　据塔斯社莫斯科讯：莫斯科各界人士14日在莫斯科“各国人民友谊之家”举行晚会，庆祝“非洲日”。阿拉伯联合共和国、埃塞俄比亚、加纳和苏丹驻苏联大使馆的工作人员以及在莫斯科学习的非洲各国留学生应邀参加了晚会。
    苏联非洲人民友好协会理事会副主席加尔马歇夫在会上讲了话。他说，苏联人民为许多非洲国家取得了独立而高兴。他说，我们相信，和平和进步的力量最终将战胜冷战和压迫势力，非洲各国人民将从殖民主义和帝国主义的压迫下永远解放出来。
    苏联非洲人民友好协会理事会委员鲍奇卡列夫在讲话中说，非洲的民族解放运动是当代不可避免的运动。在争取和平和国际安全的斗争中，取得独立的非洲国家正起着愈来愈积极的作用。
    会议打电报给正在科纳克里举行的第二届亚非人民团结大会，祝贺大会的召开。
