Title: Cyprus United Democratic Youth Organization Delegation Returns Home
Author:
Date: 1960-04-20
Page: 6

    Xinhua News Agency News: The Cyprus United Democratic Youth Organization delegation of five people, who were invited by the All-China Youth Federation to visit our country, returned to their country by plane on the morning of the 17th after visiting Beijing, Anshan, Dalian, Nanjing, Shanghai, Hangzhou and other places for more than a month.
    Wang Zhaohua, Standing Committee Member, All-China Youth Federation, Secretary of the Secretariat of the Central Committee of the Communist Youth League, and Qian Dawei, Deputy Director of the International Liaison Department of the Central Committee of the Communist Youth League, were among those who saw them off at the airport.

### 塞浦路斯统一民主青年组织代表团回国

1960-04-20
第6版()
专栏：

    塞浦路斯统一民主青年组织代表团回国
    新华社讯　应全国青年联合会邀请来我国访问的塞浦路斯统一民主青年组织代表团一行五人，在北京、鞍山、大连、南京、上海、杭州等地进行了一个多月的访问以后，17日早晨乘飞机离开北京回国。
    到机场送行的有全国青联常务委员、共青团中央书记处书记王照华、共青团中央国际联络部副部长钱大卫等。
