Title: Our Women's Delegation Leaves Beijing for Denmark
Author:
Date: 1960-04-19
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency, April 17th. The Chinese women's delegation attending the International Women's Congress celebrating the 50th anniversary of International Women's Day, led by head of delegation Li Dequan, departed from Beijing by plane on the 17th for Copenhagen, Denmark.

The delegation members are: Gong Pusheng, Cao Mengjun, Guo Jian, Apai Cai Danzhuoga, Yan Renying, Huang Ganying, Zou Dexin, Wu Qixiu, Liu Jin, Li Yunhui, Zhao Qing, Wang Yunling, and Xu Xuehai. Another member of the delegation, Qi Yun, is already abroad. Those who saw them off at the airport included: Shi Liang, Cui Yitian, Sa Kongliao, Pu Jiexiu, Zhang Shuyi, and Qi Guihua.

### 我妇女代表团离京去丹麦

1960-04-19
第4版()
专栏：

    我妇女代表团离京去丹麦
    新华社17日讯　中国妇女出席庆祝国际妇女节五十周年国际妇女大会的代表团，在团长李德全率领下，17日乘飞机从北京启程前往丹麦哥本哈根。
    代表团成员有：龚普生、曹孟君、郭建、阿沛·才丹卓噶、严仁英、黄甘英、邹得心、吴启秀、刘金、李蕴辉、赵青、王允玲、徐学海。代表团的另一成员戚云已在国外。到机场送行的有：史良、崔义田、萨空了、浦洁修、张淑仪、戚桂华等。
