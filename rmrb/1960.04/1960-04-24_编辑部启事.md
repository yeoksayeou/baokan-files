    Editor's Note
    In the People's Daily editorial of April 22, "Advance Along the Road of the Great Lenin," the following two points should be added to the summary of the U.S. government's and Eisenhower's anti-peace words and deeds:
    On April 20, U.S. Under Secretary of State Dillon delivered a speech attacking the Soviet Union's foreign policy, falsely accusing the Soviet Union of having "expansionist ambitions," claiming that "'coexistence' is actually both terrible and abominable," and clamoring to "maintain and strengthen" America's military strength and aggressive military bloc system.
    On the same day, U.S.-backed rebels launched an armed rebellion in an attempt to overthrow the Venezuelan government.

### 编辑部启事

1960-04-24
第1版()
专栏：

    编辑部启事
    4月22日人民日报编辑部文章“沿着伟大列宁的道路前进”中，美国政府和艾森豪威尔反和平言行摘要尚需补充以下两条：
    4月20日，美国副国务卿狄龙发表演说，攻击苏联的对外政策，诬蔑苏联有“扩张野心”，认为“‘共处’这个字眼实际上是既可怕又可恶的”，叫嚷“保持和加强”美国的军事力量和侵略性的军事集团体系。
    同日，美国支持的叛乱分子发动武装叛乱，企图推翻委内瑞拉政府。
