Title: My New Consul General to Damascus, Wang Chongli, Arrives

Author:
Date: 1960-04-17
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Damascus, April 14th: Wang Chongli, the new Consul General of the People's Republic of China to Damascus, Syria, of the United Arab Republic, arrived here by plane from Cairo today.

### 我驻大马士革新任总领事王崇理到任

1960-04-17
第4版()
专栏：

    我驻大马士革新任总领事王崇理到任
    新华社大马士革14日电　中华人民共和国驻阿拉伯联合共和国叙利亚地区大马士革新任总领事王崇理今天从开罗乘飞机到达这里。
