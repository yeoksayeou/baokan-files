Title: Premier Zhou Enlai in Nepal (Photos)
Author: Du Xiuxian
Date: 1960-04-30
Page: 4

Premier Zhou Enlai in Nepal

April 26, Premier Zhou Enlai and his entourage arrived in Kathmandu, the capital of Nepal, from New Delhi by special plane, and were greeted by crowds along the way.
		Xinhua News Agency reporter Du Xiuxian (Fax photo)

April 26, Prime Minister Koirala welcomes Premier Zhou at the airport
		Xinhua News Agency reporter Du Xiuxian

April 26, Premier Zhou visits Prince Himalaya Xinhua News Agency reporter Du Xiuxian

April 28, the Nepal-China Friendship Association held a reception to welcome Premier Zhou. The picture shows Premier Zhou, Prime Minister Koirala, and Purna Bahadur (left), president of the Nepal-China Friendship Association, having a cordial conversation. Xinhua News Agency reporter Du Xiuxian

April 28, Premier Zhou Enlai (left) and Prime Minister Koirala (right) sign the Treaty of Peace and Friendship between the People's Republic of China and the Kingdom of Nepal in Kathmandu.
	Xinhua News Agency reporter Du Xiuxian (Fax photo)

April 26, citizens of Kathmandu, the capital of Nepal, held a grand welcoming rally for Premier Zhou Enlai's visit to Nepal. At the meeting, the masses presented many garlands to Premier Zhou Enlai and Vice Premier Chen Yi. The second person from the right is Nepalese Prime Minister Koirala.
	Xinhua News Agency reporter Du Xiuxian (Fax photo)

    周恩来总理在尼泊尔
    四月二十六日，周恩来总理和随行人员乘专机自新德里到达尼泊尔首都加德满都，受到群众夹道欢迎。
                新华社记者  杜修贤摄（传真照片）
    四月二十六日，柯伊拉腊首相在机场欢迎周总理
                          新华社记者  杜修贤摄

    4月28日，尼中友好协会举行招待会，欢迎周总理。图为周总理、柯伊拉腊首相和尼中友好协会会长普尔纳·巴哈杜尔（左）亲切交谈                              新华社记者　杜修贤摄
