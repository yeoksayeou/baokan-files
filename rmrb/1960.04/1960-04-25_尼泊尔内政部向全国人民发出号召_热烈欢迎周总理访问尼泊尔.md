Title: The Nepalese Ministry of Home Affairs Issues a Call to the People Nationwide: Warmly Welcome Premier Zhou's Visit to Nepal

Author:

Date: 1960-04-25
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, New Delhi, April 23rd: According to the Indian Press Trust from Kathmandu: The Nepalese government's Ministry of Home Affairs issued a public statement on the 23rd, calling on the Nepalese people to warmly welcome Premier Zhou Enlai's visit to Nepal. The statement said that giving the distinguished guests of the government of the People's Republic of China, who are scheduled to visit Nepal on April 26th, such a welcome would be in line with Nepal's traditional spirit of hospitality.

### 尼泊尔内政部向全国人民发出号召  热烈欢迎周总理访问尼泊尔

1960-04-25
第1版()
专栏：

    尼泊尔内政部向全国人民发出号召
    热烈欢迎周总理访问尼泊尔
    新华社新德里23日电　据印度报业托辣斯自加德满都报道：尼泊尔政府内政部23日发表一项公报，号召尼泊尔人民热烈欢迎周恩来总理访问尼泊尔。公报说，给予定于4月26日前来尼泊尔访问的中华人民共和国政府的贵宾们这样的欢迎，将是符合尼泊尔传统的好客精神的。
