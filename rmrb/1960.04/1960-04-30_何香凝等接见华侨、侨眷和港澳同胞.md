Title: He Xiangning and Others Receive Overseas Chinese, Relatives of Overseas Chinese, and Hong Kong and Macao Compatriots
Author:
Date: 1960-04-30
Page: 7

Xinhua News Agency, April 29th: He Xiangning, Vice Chairman of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress and Vice Chairman of the National Committee of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference; Liao Chengzhi, Director of the Overseas Chinese Affairs Commission; and Deputy Directors Fang Fang and Zhuang Mingli, today received some overseas Chinese, Hong Kong and Macao compatriots, and returned overseas Chinese relatives who came to Beijing to participate in the "May 1st" celebration.

Also present at the reception were Cai Tingkai and Chen Qiyou, members of the Overseas Chinese Affairs Commission, and Chen Qiyuan, Vice Chairman of the All-China Federation of Returned Overseas Chinese.

### 何香凝等接见华侨、侨眷和港澳同胞

1960-04-30
第7版()
专栏：

    何香凝等接见华侨、侨眷和港澳同胞
  新华社29日讯　全国人民代表大会常务委员会副委员长、中国人民政治协商会议全国委员会副主席何香凝，华侨事务委员会主任廖承志，副主任方方、庄明理，今天接见了前来北京参加“五一”节庆祝活动的部分国外华侨、港澳同胞和归侨侨眷。
    接见时在座的还有，华侨事务委员会委员蔡廷锴、陈其尤，中华全国归国华侨联合会副主席陈其瑗等。
