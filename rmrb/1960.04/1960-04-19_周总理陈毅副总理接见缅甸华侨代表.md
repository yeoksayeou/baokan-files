Title: Premier Zhou and Vice Premier Chen Yi Receive Representatives of Overseas Chinese in Burma
Author:
Date: 1960-04-19
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Rangoon, April 18th: Premier Zhou Enlai and Vice Premier and Foreign Minister Marshal Chen Yi received two hundred and twenty representatives of Overseas Chinese in Burma here this afternoon. Premier Zhou Enlai had a warm conversation with them. Premier Zhou Enlai and Vice Premier Chen Yi also took a photo with all the Overseas Chinese representatives.

### 周总理陈毅副总理接见缅甸华侨代表

1960-04-19
第1版()
专栏：

    周总理陈毅副总理接见缅甸华侨代表
    新华社仰光18日电　周恩来总理、副总理兼外交部长陈毅元帅今天下午在这里接见了两百二十名缅甸华侨代表。周恩来总理同他们进行了热情的谈话。周恩来总理和陈毅副总理还同全体华侨代表在一起拍照。
