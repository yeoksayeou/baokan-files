Title: Mongolian Ambassador to the Soviet Union Hosts Reception for Sambu's Visit to the Soviet Union
Author:
Date: 1960-04-16
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, April 14th: The Mongolian Ambassador to the Soviet Union, Lubsan, held a grand reception here on the 13th for Sambu, Chairman of the Presidium of the Great People's Hural of Mongolia, to visit the Soviet Union.

Soviet attendees at the reception included Voroshilov, Ignatov, Kozlov, Mukhitdinov, Shvernik, Kosygin, and Polyansky. At the reception, Sambu and Kozlov delivered speeches to each other.

Sambu arrived in the Soviet Union on the 12th for a friendly visit at the invitation of Voroshilov, Chairman of the Presidium of the Supreme Soviet of the Soviet Union. On the same day, Sambu paid a visit to Voroshilov and Kozlov at the Kremlin. In the evening, the Presidium of the Supreme Soviet of the Soviet Union hosted a banquet in honor of Sambu.

On the 13th, Sambu hosted an early banquet at the Mongolian Embassy in the Soviet Union to entertain Soviet party and government leaders such as Voroshilov.

### 为桑布访问苏联  蒙古驻苏大使举行招待会

1960-04-16
第5版()
专栏：

    为桑布访问苏联
    蒙古驻苏大使举行招待会
    新华社莫斯科14日电　蒙古人民共和国驻苏联大使鲁布桑13日为蒙古大人民呼拉尔主席团主席桑布访问苏联，在这里举行了盛大招待会。
    苏联方面出席招待会的有伏洛希洛夫、伊格纳托夫、科兹洛夫、穆希金诺夫、什维尔尼克、柯西金、波利扬斯基等人。在招待会上，桑布和科兹洛夫互相致词。
    桑布是应苏联最高苏维埃主席团主席伏罗希洛夫的邀请，在12日到达苏联进行友好访问的。当天，桑布到克里姆林宫拜会了伏罗希洛夫和科兹洛夫。同日晚间，苏联最高苏维埃主席团设宴招待桑布。
    13日，桑布在蒙古驻苏联大使馆设早宴招待苏联党政领导人伏罗希洛夫等人。
