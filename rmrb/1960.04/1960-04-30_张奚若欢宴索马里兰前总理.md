Title: Zhang Xiruo Hosts Banquet for Former Prime Minister of Somaliland

Author: 
Date: 1960-04-30
Page: 7

Zhang Xiruo Hosts Banquet for Former Prime Minister of Somaliland

(Our newspaper dispatch) Zhang Xiruo, President of the Chinese People's Institute of Foreign Affairs, hosted a banquet tonight to welcome Mohamed Harbi, former Prime Minister of French Somaliland and Chairman of the Democratic Union, and Ali Abdillahi, Secretary-General, who are visiting our country.

At the beginning of the banquet, President Zhang Xiruo first expressed his admiration for the heroic struggle of the people of Somaliland for independence and unification. He also expressed his belief that this struggle will surely achieve victory soon. Mohamed Harbi said at the banquet that their visit to China this time brought the friendship and sincere feelings of the people of Somaliland. Referring to the struggle of the South Korean people against the Syngman Rhee puppet regime, Harbi congratulated the South Korean people on the victory of their struggle.

Among those attending the banquet were Hu Yuzhi, Vice President of the Chinese People's Institute of Foreign Affairs, Burhan, Vice Chairman of the Asian-African Solidarity Committee, and Zhang Tiesheng, Vice President of the Chinese-African People's Friendship Association.

### 张奚若欢宴索马里兰前总理

1960-04-30
第7版()
专栏：

    张奚若欢宴索马里兰前总理
    本报讯　中国人民外交学会会长张奚若今晚设宴欢迎前来我国访问的法属索马里兰前总理、民主联盟主席穆罕默德·哈比和总书记阿里·阿卜迪拉西。
    张奚若会长在宴会开始时，首先对索马里兰人民为争取独立和统一而进行的英勇的斗争表示钦佩。并且相信这一斗争一定能够很快地取得胜利。穆罕默德·哈比在宴会上讲话说，他们这次来到中国访问，带来了索马里兰人民的友谊和真诚的感情。在谈到南朝鲜人民反对李承晚傀儡政权的斗争时，哈比祝贺南朝鲜人民斗争的胜利。
    参加这次宴会的有中国人民外交学会副会长胡愈之，亚非团结委员会副主席包尔汉，中国非洲人民友好协会副会长张铁生等。
