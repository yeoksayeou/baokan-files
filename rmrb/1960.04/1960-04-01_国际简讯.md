International Briefs

    Nasser Arrives in India for Visit
    New Delhi dispatch: President Nasser of the United Arab Republic arrived in New Delhi on the evening of the 29th to begin his official visit to India.
    Welcoming him at the airport were Indian President Prasad, Vice President Radhakrishnan, Prime Minister Nehru, and foreign diplomatic envoys.
    The Indian President delivered a speech at the airport, welcoming Nasser. Nasser gave a reply.
    Nasser will visit India for twelve days.

    Agadir, Morocco is Recovering
    Rabat dispatch: In Agadir, the city in southwestern Morocco that suffered from the earthquake disaster, some major factories essential to the local economy resumed work and production at the end of last month. The port of Agadir has resumed operations last week. Nearly five thousand tons of oranges have been shipped from this port in the past week.
    The port railway and other facilities damaged by the earthquake are being repaired.

### 国际简讯

专栏：国际简讯

    国际简讯
    纳赛尔到达印度访问
    新德里讯　阿拉伯联合共和国总统纳赛尔29日晚间抵达新德里，开始对印度的正式访问。
    在机场上欢迎他的有印度总统普拉沙德、副总统拉达克里希南、总理尼赫鲁以及各国外交使节等。
    印度总统在机场上致词，向纳赛尔表示欢迎。纳赛尔致了答词。
    纳赛尔将在印度访问十二天。
    摩洛哥阿加迪市在恢复中
    拉巴特讯　在摩洛哥西南部遭受地震灾害的阿加迪市，当地经济所必需的一些主要工厂在上月底重新开工恢复生产。阿加迪港已在上星期恢复活动。过去一周中已经从这个港口运出将近五千吨桔子。
    遭到地震破坏的港口铁路和其他设施正在修复中。
