Title: Canadian International Payments Deficit Hits Record High
Author:
Date: 1960-04-07
Page: 7
West Wind Remnants

    Canadian International Payments Deficit Hits Record High
    According to a report in the "Canadian Tribune" on March 21, Canada's international payments deficit last year was 1.46 billion (Canadian) dollars. This is 36 million dollars more than Canada's previous highest deficit in 1957.

### 加拿大国际支付赤字创新纪录

1960-04-07
第7版()
专栏：西风残叶

    加拿大国际支付赤字创新纪录
    据“加拿大论坛报”3月21日报道：加拿大去年国际支付赤字为十四亿六千万（加）元（下同）。这比加拿大1957年的前一次最高赤字多三千六百万元。
