Title: On the Eve of Leaving Nepal, Premier Zhou Held a Press Conference
Author:
Date: 1960-04-30
Page: 2

Xinhua News Agency, Kathmandu, April 28th: On the eve of his departure from Nepal, Premier Zhou Enlai held a press conference here today at 22:30 local time. He answered all questions raised by Nepalese and other foreign journalists.

Vice Premier Chen Yi and Vice Foreign Minister Zhang Hanfu also attended the press conference.

The Prime Minister of the Kingdom of Nepal, B. P. Koirala, was also present at the press conference.

### 离开尼泊尔前夕  周总理举行记者招待会

1960-04-30
第2版()
专栏：

    离开尼泊尔前夕
    周总理举行记者招待会
    新华社加德满都28日电　周恩来总理离开尼泊尔前夕在今天当地时间二十二时三十分在这里举行记者招待会。他回答了尼泊尔记者和其他外国记者向他提出的所有问题。
    副总理陈毅和外交部副部长章汉夫也出席了记者招待会。
    出席记者招待会的还有尼泊尔王国首相毕·普·柯伊拉腊。
