Title: Promote a Greater Leap Forward in Socialist Literature and Art: National Congress of Literary and Art Workers to be Held in May
Author:
Date: 1960-04-12
Page: 3

Xinhua News Agency, 11th - The China Federation of Literary and Art Circles held its Fourth National Committee Enlarged Meeting today. The meeting passed a resolution deciding to convene the Third National Congress of Literary and Art Workers in May this year. Today's meeting was presided over by Guo Moruo, Chairman of the National Federation of Literary and Art Circles. Mao Dun, Zhou Yang, Vice-Chairmen of the Federation, and members of the Presidium, Xia Yan, Ba Jin, Lao She, Tian Han, Ke Zhongping, Ouyang Yuqian, Mei Lanfang, Yang Hansheng, Cai Chusheng, and Lü Ji, were present at the meeting. More than 100 members of the National Committee of the Federation of Literary and Art Circles and representatives from the literary and art circles who participated in the recent National People's Congress and the National Committee of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference attended the meeting.

The resolution passed today pointed out that the purpose of convening the Third National Congress of Literary and Art Workers is to further implement the Party's general line for building socialism and the Party's literary and art policies in all fields of literature and art, to mobilize literary and art workers across the country to unite more closely under the banner of serving workers, peasants, and soldiers, to improve their ideology and skills, to exert full efforts, to carry out socialist competition among literary and art workers, to set high aspirations, to strive for a greater leap forward in socialist literature and art. This Congress will summarize the great achievements and rich experiences that China's literature and art have made under the leadership of the Party and Comrade Mao Zedong since the Second National Congress of Literary and Art Workers in 1953, especially since the Great Leap Forward in 1958, and further affirm the correct path of development of China's socialist literature and art. Based on the current excellent situation, the congress will put forward the tasks of literary and artistic innovation and creation in the future. The conference will exchange experiences of literary and art workers in going deep into the lives of workers, peasants and soldiers, participating in labor training and practical work, and promote the further popularization and improvement of literature and art. The conference will also discuss how to carry out the struggle against modern revisionism in the literary and art circles in depth, and critically re-evaluate the literary and artistic heritage.

This Congress will revise the charter of the China Federation of Literary and Art Circles and re-elect the leadership.

The representatives participating in this Congress will be very broad, including literary artists, organizers of literary and artistic work from all parts of the country and all ethnic groups, and amateur literary and artistic workers from workers, peasants, and soldiers, totaling more than 2,000 people.

At the same time as the Third National Congress of Literary and Art Workers is held, the Chinese Dramatists Association, the Chinese Musicians Association, the Chinese Artists Association, the China Film Workers Association, the China Dance Research Association, and the China Photographic Society will hold membership representative conferences. The Chinese Writers Association, the Chinese Ballad Singers Association, and the Chinese Folk Literature and Art Research Association will hold expanded council meetings. Each association and research society will also hold small-scale exhibitions or performances of art, drama, music, film, dance, ballad singing, and photography during the conference.

### 促使社会主义文学艺术事业更大跃进  全国文代大会将于五月召开

1960-04-12
第3版()
专栏：

    促使社会主义文学艺术事业更大跃进
    全国文代大会将于五月召开
    新华社11日讯　中国文学艺术界联合会今天举行了第四次全国委员会扩大会议。会议通过决议，决定今年5月间召开全国第三次文学艺术工作者代表大会。今天的会议由全国文联主席郭沫若主持。文联副主席茅盾、周扬和主席团委员夏衍、巴金、老舍、田汉、柯仲平、欧阳予倩、梅兰芳、阳翰笙、蔡楚生、吕骥等出席了会议。参加会议的有文联全国委员会委员和参加最近举行的全国人代会会议、政协全国委员会会议的文艺界代表一百多人。
    今天通过的决议指出，召开第三次全国文代大会的目的，是要在文学艺术的各个领域里进一步贯彻党的建设社会主义总路线和党的文艺方针，动员全国文艺工作者在工农兵方向的旗帜下，更紧密地团结起来，提高思想和技巧，鼓足干劲，开展文艺工作者之间的社会主义竞赛，树雄心、立大志，为争取社会主义文学艺术的更大跃进而努力。这次文代大会将总结1953年第二次文代大会以来、特别是1958年大跃进以来，我国文学艺术在党和毛泽东同志领导下所取得的巨大成绩和丰富经验，进一步肯定我国社会主义文学艺术发展的正确道路。并根据目前的大好形势，提出今后文学艺术革新和创造的任务。会上将交流文艺工作者深入工农兵、参加劳动锻炼和实际工作的经验，促进文学艺术更进一步的普及和提高。这次大会还将讨论如何在文艺界深入开展对现代修正主义斗争的问题，并对文艺遗产进行批判和重新估价。
    这次文代大会将修改中国文学艺术界联合会会章，并改选领导机构。
    参加这次文代大会的代表极为广泛，将有全国各地、各民族的文学艺术家、文艺工作的组织者和工农兵群众业余文艺工作者，共二千多人。
    在全国第三次文代大会召开的同时，中国戏剧家协会、中国音乐家协会、中国美术家协会、中国电影工作者联谊会、中国舞蹈艺术研究会、中国摄影学会将召开会员代表大会。中国作家协会、中国曲艺工作者协会、中国民间文艺研究会将召开扩大理事会。各协会、研究会还将在会议期间分别举办美术、戏剧音乐、电影、舞蹈、曲艺、摄影等小型展览或演出。
