Title: Gomulka Speaks at Polish Youth League Congress, Emphasizing Socialist Education of Youth; Heads of Soviet and Chinese Delegations Extend Greetings to the Congress
Author:
Date: 1960-04-27
Page: 6
Xinhua News Agency, Warsaw, April 25th: While speaking at the Second National Congress of the Polish Socialist Youth League, Władysław Gomułka, First Secretary of the Central Committee of the Polish United Workers' Party, emphasized the need to educate youth in the socialist spirit. He stated that the main task of the Polish Socialist Youth League is to educate its members and the broad masses of young people in the spirit of socialism.

The congress opened at 2:00 PM on the 25th at the Palace of Culture and Science in Warsaw. The congress was presided over by Marian Renke, First Secretary of the Central Committee of the Polish Socialist Youth League.

Józef Cyrankiewicz, Aleksander Zawadzki, and other party and government leaders attended the congress.

Gomułka pointed out at the congress, attended by more than 1,200 delegates and delegations from 30 foreign youth organizations, that the Youth League should educate young people to fight for the victory of the socialist system over the capitalist system, and to fight for new things and for socialism.

Gomułka emphasized: World reactionary forces and imperialism, as well as reactionary forces operating in various forms within our country and carrying out anti-socialist propaganda, will not let go of the youth, and even primarily expect to influence the youth. Furthermore, jumping from one social form to another—from the capitalist system to the socialist system—does not automatically cause a leap in the ideological viewpoints of people left over from the old ruling class and long-standing traditions. He pointed out that the remnants of capitalism will exist for a long time in the economic aspects of the new socialist system, and especially in people's thoughts, habits, and morals. Until the remnants of the old system are eradicated, they will contaminate the younger generation growing up under the new socialist social conditions. Eliminating the old under new conditions is a long-term educational process.

Gomułka pointed out that to educate youth in the socialist spirit means to transform their social consciousness, to make them understand the essence of capitalism, to understand the history of their country, to understand the workers' revolutionary movement, to understand Marxism-Leninism, and to cultivate their Marxist worldview and correct way of thinking.

After Gomułka's speech, Marian Renke, First Secretary of the Central Committee of the Polish Socialist Youth League, then delivered a report on the work of the Youth League over the past three years on behalf of the Central Committee of the Polish Socialist Youth League at the congress.

He pointed out that the Socialist Youth League already has nearly 500,000 members. It is an important task to ideologically and politically educate young people.

Afterwards, Ešar, Secretary-General of the World Federation of Democratic Youth, representatives of the International Union of Students, the head of the Soviet delegation of the Leninist Young Communist League and Secretary of the Central Committee of the Soviet Leninist Young Communist League, Lyeshetov, and the head of the delegation of the Chinese Communist Youth League and alternate secretary of the Central Committee of the Chinese Communist Youth League, Lu Jindong, delivered congratulatory speeches to the congress. Their congratulatory messages were warmly welcomed.

The congress will continue for five days.

### 哥穆尔卡在波兰青年联盟代表大会上讲话  强调以社会主义精神教育青年  苏中两国代表团长向代表大会致祝词

1960-04-27
第6版()
专栏：

    哥穆尔卡在波兰青年联盟代表大会上讲话
    强调以社会主义精神教育青年
    苏中两国代表团长向代表大会致祝词
    新华社华沙25日电　波兰统一工人党中央委员会第一书记哥穆尔卡在波兰社会主义青年联盟第二次全国代表大会上讲话时，强调要以社会主义精神教育青年。他说，以社会主义精神教育自己的成员和广大青年群众，是波兰社会主义青年联盟的主要任务。
    这一届代表大会是在25日下午二时在华沙科学文化宫开幕的。大会由波兰社会主义青年联盟中央委员会第一书记马里安·伦克主持。
    西伦凯维兹、萨瓦茨基和党政其他领导人出席了大会。
    哥穆尔卡在这个共有一千二百多名代表和三十个外国青年组织的代表团出席的大会上指出，青年联盟应该教育青年为社会主义体系战胜资本主义体系而斗争，为新事物、为社会主义而斗争。
    哥穆尔卡强调指出：世界反动势力和帝国主义，以及以各种形式在我们国家内活动着的反动势力所进行的敌对社会主义的宣传，不会放过青年，甚至首先是指望着影响青年。此外，从一个社会形式跳跃到另一个社会形式——从资本主义制度跃向社会主义制度，并不跟着引起旧的统治阶级和长期的传统所遗留下的人们思想观点的自动的飞跃。他指出，资本主义的残余将长期存在于新的社会主义制度内的经济方面，特别是存在于人的思想、习惯和道德方面。当旧制度的残余没有被根除以前，它就将会熏染在新的社会主义的社会条件下成长起来的年轻的一代。在新的条件下消灭旧的是一个长期的教育过程。
    哥穆尔卡指出，以社会主义精神教育青年，就是改造他们的社会意识，使他们了解资本主义的实质，了解本国的历史，了解工人革命运动，了解马克思列宁主义，培养他们的马克思主义的世界观和正确的思想方法。
    哥穆尔卡讲话后，波兰社会主义青年联盟中央委员会第一书记伦克接着代表波兰社会主义青年联盟中央委员会在大会上作了关于青年联盟三年来的工作报告。
    他指出，社会主义青年联盟已拥有将近五十万盟员。从思想上和政治上教育青年是一项重要的任务。
    之后，世界民主青年联盟总书记埃夏尔，国际学生联合会代表、苏联列宁共产主义青年团代表团团长、苏联列宁共产主义青年团中央委员会书记列谢托夫，中国共产主义青年团代表团团长、中国共产主义青年团中央委员会候补书记路金栋等人分别向大会致祝词。他们的祝词受到热烈的欢迎。
    大会将继续进行五天。
