Title: Sambu Arrives in Moscow
Author:
Date: 1960-04-13
Page: 5

    Sambu Arrives in Moscow
    Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, April 12th: Sambu, Chairman of the Presidium of the Great People's Hural of the Mongolian People's Republic, arrived in Moscow by plane today for a friendly visit at the invitation of Voroshilov, Chairman of the Presidium of the Supreme Soviet of the Soviet Union.
    Welcoming him at the airport were Soviet leaders Voroshilov, Ignatov, Kozlov, Mukhitdinov, and others.
    Voroshilov and Sambu exchanged words at the airport.
    Xinhua News Agency, Ulaanbaatar, April 12th: Sambu, Chairman of the Presidium of the Great People's Hural of Mongolia, left by plane this morning for friendly visits to the Soviet Union, Poland, Czechoslovakia, and the German Democratic Republic.
    Seeing him off at the airport were Mongolian party and government leaders Tsedenbal, Baljinnyam, Dugersuren, Molomjamts, Jagvaral, as well as diplomatic envoys from various countries to Mongolia.

### 桑布到达莫斯科

1960-04-13
第5版()
专栏：

    桑布到达莫斯科
    新华社莫斯科12日电　蒙古人民共和国大人民呼拉尔主席团主席桑布，应苏联最高苏维埃主席团主席伏罗希洛夫的邀请，今天乘飞机抵达莫斯科作友好访问。
    到机场欢迎的有：苏联领导人伏罗希洛夫、伊格纳托夫、科兹洛夫、穆希金诺夫等人。
    伏罗希洛夫和桑布在机场上相互讲了话。
    新华社乌兰巴托12日电　蒙古大人民呼拉尔主席团主席桑布今天上午乘飞机前往苏联、波兰、捷克斯洛伐克和德意志民主共和国做友好访问。
    到机场送行的，有蒙古党政领导人泽登巴尔、巴尔吉尼雅姆、杜格苏伦、莫洛姆扎木茨、热格瓦拉尔以及各国驻蒙古外交使节。
