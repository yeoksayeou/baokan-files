Title: Khrushchev Returns to Moscow
Author:
Date: 1960-04-04
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, April 3rd: Soviet Minister Council Chairman Khrushchev, after concluding his visit to France, returned to Moscow by plane from Paris this afternoon.

### 赫鲁晓夫返抵莫斯科

1960-04-04
第6版()
专栏：

    赫鲁晓夫返抵莫斯科
    据新华社莫斯科3日电　苏联部长会议主席赫鲁晓夫结束了在法国的访问后，在今天下午乘飞机从巴黎返抵莫斯科。
