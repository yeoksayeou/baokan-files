Title: Celebrating the 15th Anniversary of Hungary's Liberation: Chinese and Hungarian Children Hold Joint Celebration
Author:
Date: 1960-04-03
Page: 4

    To celebrate the 15th Anniversary of Hungary's Liberation, more than 500 Chinese and Hungarian children from Beijing held a get-together at the Children's Palace tonight. Hungarian Chargé d'Affaires ad interim to China, Mr. Sigeti and his wife, as well as the head of the Hungarian-Chinese Friendship Association delegation, Mr. Pataki János, and members Simon István, Martin János, and Vas János attended the gathering.

    At the gathering, Liang Liming, a Chinese elementary school student, extended holiday greetings to Hungarian children on behalf of the children of Beijing, saying that Chinese and Hungarian children should unite more closely and contribute to the socialist construction of their countries. Subsequently, Xu Hongyi, on behalf of the children of Beijing, read out a congratulatory letter to the Hungarian children.

    Pataki János, the head of the Hungarian-Chinese Friendship Association delegation, encouraged Hungarian and Chinese children to study hard for the socialist construction of their countries.

    At the gathering, Chinese children presented the "Beijing Children's Palace Activity Photo Album" to the Hungarian children and performed cultural programs such as the Hungarian folk tale "The Gooseherd Boy, Matyi."

### 庆祝匈牙利解放十五周年  中匈儿童举行联欢

1960-04-03
第4版()
专栏：

    庆祝匈牙利解放十五周年
    中匈儿童举行联欢
    新华社2日讯　北京市中匈少年儿童五百多人今晚在少年宫举行联欢会，庆祝匈牙利解放十五周年。匈牙利驻华大使馆临时代办西格蒂及其夫人和匈中友好协会代表团团长帕塔基·亚诺什和团员西蒙·依斯特万、马尔丁·亚诺什、瓦什·亚诺什参加了联欢会。
    在联欢会上，中国小学生梁立明代表北京市少年儿童向匈牙利小朋友致以节日的祝贺，她说，中匈两国小朋友要更紧密地团结在一起，为祖国的社会主义建设事业贡献力量。接着，徐宏懿代表北京市少年儿童宣读了给匈牙利小朋友的祝贺信。
    匈中友好协会代表团团长帕塔基·亚诺什在会上勉励匈牙利和中国的少年儿童们为了祖国的社会主义建设事业好好学习。
    会上，中国小朋友把“北京市少年宫活动相册”赠送给匈牙利小朋友，并演出了匈牙利民间故事“牧鹅少年马季”等文艺节目。
