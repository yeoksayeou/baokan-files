Title: Cuba Publishes "Guerrilla Warfare" to Help People Learn from the Experience of Fighting the Enemy
Author:
Date: 1960-04-09
Page: 7
Xinhua News Agency, Havana, April 6th: "Guerrilla Warfare," a book written by Che Guevara, President of the National Bank of Cuba and Training Officer of the Cuban Rebel Army, has been released today.

The book collects all the experiences from the war to overthrow the Batista tyranny. The book's contents include the general principles of guerrilla warfare, the elements of conducting such warfare, its strategy and tactics, combat in favorable and unfavorable areas, and suburban combat.

In addition to the Rebel Army learning this book, the militia will also learn it.

### 帮助人民学习对敌斗争的经验  古巴出版“游击战”一书

1960-04-09
第7版()
专栏：

    帮助人民学习对敌斗争的经验
    古巴出版“游击战”一书
    新华社哈瓦那6日电　古巴国家银行行长、古巴起义军训练长官格瓦拉所著的“游击战”一书已经在今天开始发行。
    这本书收集了推翻巴蒂斯塔暴政的战争中的一切经验。书的内容包括游击战的一般原则、进行这种战争的要素、它的战略和战术、在有利和不利地域内的战斗以及城郊战斗等。
    除了起义军要学习这本书之外，民兵也要学习这本书。
