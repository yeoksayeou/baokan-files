Title: Tsedenbal Discusses Mongolia's Current Tasks; Mongolia's New Five-Year Plan Will Double Production; Socialist Material and Technical Base Will Be Roughly Established Within Ten Years
Author:
Date: 1960-04-26
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, Ulaanbaatar, 23rd – According to a report in the Mongolian “Truth” newspaper: Tsedenbal, First Secretary of the Central Committee of the Mongolian People's Revolutionary Party and Chairman of the Council of Ministers, said at the Third Plenary Session of the Mongolian Revolutionary Youth League recently that Mongolia's current task is to establish a socialist material and technical base so that the country's economic development can rapidly reach the level of productive forces of other socialist countries.

He said that it would take approximately two five-year plans to roughly establish the socialist material and technical base in Mongolia. He also said that equipping the national economic sectors with modern production tools, improving production tools, and further realizing mechanization are the foundations of the work to establish the socialist material and technical base.

Tsedenbal said: Mongolia is preparing to replace old machinery and equipment with modern and advanced technology within ten to fifteen years. Establishing a socialist material and technical base in agriculture and animal husbandry requires the mechanization of the basic parts of agricultural production and certain parts of animal husbandry, providing all livestock with warm sheds, solving the water problem, establishing feed bases, systematically settling herdsmen, and electrifying all residential points and cooperative centers.

Tsedenbal said: At the same time, it is required to further raise the ideological awareness of the working people, extensively cultivate cadres, and improve the technical and cultural level of staff in economic and cultural departments.

He said that we should bear in mind the great Lenin's thesis that there is no wall separating socialism and communism, and pay attention to the important significance of the assertion that socialist countries will roughly enter communism at the same time.

Tsedenbal pointed out that Mongolia's new Five-Year Plan (1961-1965) is of decisive significance for establishing the socialist material and technical base. During the Five-Year Plan period, a large-scale and comprehensive construction work will be carried out with the assistance of the Soviet Union, China and other socialist countries, the foundation of the metallurgical industry will be laid, and the requirements for the development of non-ferrous metal industry and chemical industry will be put forward; the building materials industry will develop forward to meet the requirements of basic construction. In these five years, the total industrial output value will double, agricultural output will increase many times, the output of animal husbandry products will also increase greatly, and cultural construction will also be accelerated.

Tsedenbal said that a magnificent prospect of national electrification is unfolding before us. If hydropower stations are built on the Selenga River and other rivers, the national economy can be electrified in vast areas.

### 泽登巴尔谈蒙古当前任务  蒙古新五年计划将使生产成倍增长  十年内将大致建成社会主义物质技术基础

1960-04-26
第5版()
专栏：

    泽登巴尔谈蒙古当前任务
    蒙古新五年计划将使生产成倍增长
    十年内将大致建成社会主义物质技术基础
    新华社乌兰巴托23日电　据蒙古“真理报”报道：蒙古人民革命党中央委员会第一书记、部长会议主席泽登巴尔最近在蒙古革命青年团三中全会上讲话说，蒙古当前的任务是建立社会主义的物质技术基础，使国家的经济发展迅速达到其他社会主义国家生产力发展的水平。
    他说，在蒙古大致建成社会主义物质技术基础，需要大约两个五年计划的时间。他又说，以现代化生产工具装备国民经济部门、改进生产工具、进一步实现机械化，都是建立社会主义物质技术基础工作的基础。
    泽登巴尔说：蒙古正在准备在十年到十五年内用现代化的进步的技术装备来代替旧机器设备的条件。在农牧业方面建立社会主义物质技术基础，就是要求农业生产的基本部分和牧业生产的某些部分实行机械化，使全部牲畜有暖棚，解决水的问题，建立饲料基地，有计划地使牧民定居，在全部居民点和合作社中心实现电气化。
    泽登巴尔说：与此同时，要求进一步提高劳动人民的思想觉悟，广泛地培养干部，提高经济和文化部门工作人员的技术和文化水平。
    他说，应该想到伟大列宁指出的社会主义和共产主义之间没有隔着一道墙的论点，并注意到关于社会主义国家大致同时进入共产主义的论断的重要意义。
    泽登巴尔指出，蒙古新的五年计划（1961—1965）对建立社会主义物质技术基础有决定性的意义。五年计划期间，将在苏联、中国和其他社会主义国家的援助下开展一个大规模的全面的建设工作，将奠定冶金工业的基础，同时提出发展有色金属工业和化学工业的要求；建筑材料工业将适应基本建设的要求向前发展。在这五年中，工业总产值将增长到两倍，农业产量将增长很多倍，畜牧业产品产量也将大大增加，文化建设也要加快。
    泽登巴尔说，在我们面前展现一幅全国电气化的宏伟前景。如果在色楞格河和其它河流修建水电站，就可以在广大地区实现国民经济的电气化。
