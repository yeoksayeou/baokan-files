Title: Zhang Xiruo Hosts Banquet to Welcome Iraqi Cultural Delegation
Author:
Date: 1960-04-26
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, April 25th: Zhang Xiruo, Director of the Commission for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries, hosted a banquet at the National Cultural Palace this evening to warmly welcome all members of the Iraqi Cultural Delegation headed by Dr. Mahdi al-Matsumi.

　　In a welcoming speech at the banquet filled with a friendly atmosphere, Zhang Xiruo proposed a toast to the growing friendship and cultural exchange between the people of China and Iraq. Dr. Mahdi al-Matsumi delivered a speech thanking the host for the warm hospitality, and he proposed a toast to the eternal friendship between the people of Iraq and China.

　　Among those present at the banquet were Luo Jun, Liu Ziyu, Li Wu, Zhang Jie, Zhang Lianhua, Gong Dafei, Wu Qing, Xu Hongjiu, Fang Ming, as well as people from educational and cultural circles in the capital.

　　This morning, Zhang Xiruo met with Dr. Mahdi al-Matsumi and all members of the Iraqi Cultural Delegation, and the hosts and guests held friendly talks.

### 张奚若设宴欢迎伊拉克文化代表团

1960-04-26
第4版()
专栏：

    张奚若设宴欢迎伊拉克文化代表团
    新华社25日讯　对外文化联络委员会主任张奚若今晚在民族文化宫设宴，热烈欢迎以马赫迪·马特苏米博士为首的伊拉克文化代表团全体成员。
    张奚若在充满友好气氛的宴会上致欢迎词，他提议为中伊两国人民之间的友谊和文化交流的日益发展，干杯。马赫迪·马特苏米博士致词感谢主人的盛情款待，他提议为伊中两国人民永恒的友谊，干杯。
    出席宴会作陪的，有罗俊、刘子余、李伍、张杰、张联华、宫达非、吴青、徐宏九、方明，以及首都教育、文化界人士等。
    今天上午，张奚若接见了马赫迪·马特苏米博士和伊拉克文化代表团的全体成员，宾主进行了友好的谈话。
