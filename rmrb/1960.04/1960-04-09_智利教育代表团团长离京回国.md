Title: Head of Chilean Education Delegation Leaves Beijing for Home

Author:
Date: 1960-04-09
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, April 8th: Juan Gómez Millas, head of the Chilean Education Delegation, left Beijing by plane this morning to return home. Among those who went to see him off were Zhu Boshen, Deputy Secretary-General of the Chinese People's Association for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries, Zhou Peiyuan, Vice President of Peking University, and Zhou Zhenzhong, Director of the China-Latin America Friendship Association.

### 智利教育代表团团长离京回国

1960-04-09
第6版()
专栏：

    智利教育代表团团长离京回国
    新华社8日讯　智利教育代表团团长胡安·戈麦斯·米利亚斯，在今天上午乘飞机离开北京回国。前往送行的有中国人民对外文化协会副秘书长朱伯深、北京大学副校长周培源、中国拉丁美洲友好协会理事周震中等。
