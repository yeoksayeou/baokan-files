Title: Our Delegation Leaves for Cairo to Attend the Asian-African Economic Conference
Author:
Date: 1960-04-23
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, April 22nd: The Chinese delegation to the Second Asian-African Economic Conference, led by its head Nan Hanchen, departed from Beijing by plane today for Cairo. The deputy head of the delegation, Ji Chao Ding, will travel from abroad to attend the conference.

### 我代表团去开罗参加亚非经济会议

1960-04-23
第5版()
专栏：

    我代表团去开罗参加亚非经济会议
    据新华社22日讯　出席第二次亚非经济会议的中国代表团，在团长南汉宸率领下，今天乘飞机从北京启程前往开罗。代表团副团长冀朝鼎将从国外前往参加大会。
