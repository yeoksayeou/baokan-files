Title: Shen Yanbing Hosts Banquet for Polish Cultural Delegation
Author:
Date: 1960-04-21
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency, 20th.
　　Minister of Culture Shen Yanbing hosted a banquet tonight for the Polish Cultural Delegation headed by Tadeusz Galinski, Minister of Culture and Art of Poland. The delegation returned to Beijing by plane on the afternoon of the 18th after visiting Nanjing, Hangzhou, Shanghai, Guangzhou and other places.
　　Among those present at the banquet tonight were Wang Weizhou, member of the Council of the Sino-Polish Friendship Association, Vice Minister of Foreign Affairs Zeng Yongquan, Deputy Director of the Commission for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries Zhang Zhixiang, Vice Ministers of Culture Qian Junrui and Xu Guangxiao, Deputy Director of the Broadcasting Bureau Li Wu, Secretary-General of the National Federation of Literary and Art Circles Yang Hansheng, and figures from the capital's literary and art circles such as Mei Lanfang, Lü Ji, Zhao Feng, and Hua Junwu. Polish Chargé d'Affaires to China, Mr. and Mrs. Furato, were also invited to attend the banquet.
　　At noon today, Polish Chargé d'Affaires to China, Mr. Furato, held a banquet for the Polish Cultural Delegation's visit to China. Wang Weizhou, Shen Yanbing, Zeng Yongquan, Cai Chusheng, Zhang Zhixiang, Xu Guangxiao and others were invited to attend the banquet.
　　Last night, the Sino-Polish Friendship Association also held a reception to entertain the Polish guests.

### 沈雁冰欢宴波兰文化代表团

1960-04-21
第4版()
专栏：

    沈雁冰欢宴波兰文化代表团
    新华社20日讯　文化部部长沈雁冰今晚设宴招待以波兰文化艺术部部长塔德乌什·加林斯基为首的波兰文化代表团。这个代表团在南京、杭州、上海、广州等地参观访问后，在18日下午乘飞机回到北京。
    出席今晚宴会作陪的，有中波友好协会理事王维舟，外交部副部长曾涌泉，对外文化联络委员会副主任张致祥，文化部副部长钱俊瑞，徐光霄，广播事业局副局长李伍，全国文联秘书长阳翰笙及首都文艺界人士梅兰芳、吕骥、赵沨、华君武等人。波兰驻我国临时代办傅拉托夫妇也应邀出席了宴会。
    今天中午，波兰驻我国临时代办傅拉托为波兰文化代表团访问我国举行宴会。应邀出席宴会的有王维舟、沈雁冰、曾涌泉、蔡楚生、张致祥、徐光霄等人。
    昨晚，中波友好协会也曾经举行酒会招待波兰客人。
