Title: China Decides to Recognize the Republic of Togo; Premier Zhou Enlai and Foreign Minister Chen Yi Send Congratulatory Telegrams
Author:
Date: 1960-04-28
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, April 27th – Premier Zhou Enlai of the State Council of the People's Republic of China sent a telegram on the 26th to Mr. Sylvanus Olympio, Prime Minister of the Republic of Togo, congratulating the Republic of Togo on its declaration of independence. The telegram reads as follows:
    To His Excellency Mr. Sylvanus Olympio, Prime Minister of the Republic of Togo, Lome, Togo:

    On the occasion of the declaration of independence of the Republic of Togo, I would like to extend my heartfelt congratulations to Your Excellency, and through Your Excellency, to the Government and people of the Republic of Togo, on behalf of the Government and people of the People's Republic of China. I wish Your Excellency and the people of Togo new achievements in the cause of safeguarding national independence, and I wish your country prosperity and your people happiness.

                          Zhou Enlai
                          Premier of the State Council of the People's Republic of China
                                      April 26, 1960

Xinhua News Agency, April 27th – Foreign Minister Chen Yi of the People's Republic of China sent a telegram on the 26th to Mr. Sylvanus Olympio, Prime Minister of the Republic of Togo, informing him that the People's Republic of China had decided to recognize the Republic of Togo. The telegram reads as follows:
    To His Excellency Mr. Sylvanus Olympio, Prime Minister of the Republic of Togo, Lome, Togo:

    I am pleased to learn of Togo's declaration of independence, and I would like to extend my sincere congratulations to the Government of Togo on behalf of the Government of the People's Republic of China.. I am honored to inform Your Excellency that the Government of the People's Republic of China. has already decided to recognize the Republic of Togo. I hope that our government's recognition of your country will lead to the further development of friendly relations between China and Togo.

                        Chen Yi
                        Minister of Foreign Affairs of the People's Republic of China
                                    April 26, 1960

### 我国决定承认多哥共和国  周恩来总理、陈毅外交部长致电祝贺

1960-04-28
第1版()
专栏：

    我国决定承认多哥共和国
    周恩来总理、陈毅外交部长致电祝贺
    新华社27日讯　中华人民共和国国务院总理周恩来26日致电多哥共和国总理西凡虞·奥林匹欧，祝贺多哥共和国宣告独立。电文如下：多哥　洛美多哥共和国总理西凡虞·奥林匹欧先生阁下：
    在多哥共和国宣告独立之际，我谨代表中华人民共和国政府和人民向阁下，并通过阁下向多哥共和国政府和人民致以衷心的祝贺。祝阁下和多哥人民在维护民族独立的事业中取得新的成就，并祝贵国国家繁荣，人民幸福。
                          中华人民共和国国务院总理　周恩来
                                      一九六○年四月二十六日
    新华社27日讯　中华人民共和国外交部长陈毅26日致电多哥共和国总理西凡虞·奥林匹欧，通知他中华人民共和国已经决定承认多哥共和国。电文如下：多哥　洛美多哥共和国总理西凡虞·奥林匹欧先生阁下：
    欣闻多哥宣告独立，我谨代表中华人民共和国政府向多哥政府致以诚挚的祝贺。我荣幸地通知阁下，中华人民共和国政府已经决定承认多哥共和国。我希望，我国政府对贵国的承认将导致中、多两国友好关系的进一步发展。
                        　中华人民共和国外交部长　陈毅
                                    一九六○年四月二十六日
