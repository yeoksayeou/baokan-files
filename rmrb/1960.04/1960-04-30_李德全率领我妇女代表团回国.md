Title: Li Dequan Leads Chinese Women's Delegation Back Home
Author:
Date: 1960-04-30
Page: 7

　　Xinhua News Agency, April 29th -- The Chinese women's delegation of fourteen people, led by head of delegation Li Dequan, which went to Copenhagen to attend the International Women's Conference celebrating the 50th anniversary of International Women's Day, returned to Beijing by plane on the afternoon of the 28th.

### 李德全率领我妇女代表团回国

1960-04-30
第7版()
专栏：

    李德全率领我妇女代表团回国
    新华社29日讯　去哥本哈根出席庆祝国际妇女节五十周年国际妇女大会的中国妇女代表团一行十四人，在团长李德全率领下，28日下午乘飞机回到北京。
