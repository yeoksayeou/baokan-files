Title: Delegation to the Asian-African People's Solidarity Conference Returns to China
Author:
Date: 1960-04-26
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency, April 24th—The Chinese delegation to the Second Asian-African People's Solidarity Conference, led by Liao Chengzhi, Liu Ningyi, and Bao Erhan, returned to Beijing by plane today.

Welcoming them at the airport were people from all walks of life including Liu Changsheng, Chu Tunan, Zhang Zhixiang, Zhuang Mingli, Qu Tangliang, He Ying, Wu Maosun, Wang Mingyuan, and Zheng Senyu.

### 我出席亚非人民团结大会代表团回国

1960-04-26
第4版()
专栏：

    我出席亚非人民团结大会代表团回国
    新华社24日讯　出席第二届亚非人民团结大会的中国代表团在廖承志、刘宁一、包尔汉率领下，今天乘飞机回到北京。
    到机场欢迎的有刘长胜、楚图南、张致祥、庄明理、区棠亮、何英、吴茂荪、王明远、郑森禹等各界人士。
