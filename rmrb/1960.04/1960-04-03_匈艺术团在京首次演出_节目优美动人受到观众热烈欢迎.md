Title: Hungarian Art Troupe's First Performance in Beijing: Beautiful and Moving Programs Warmly Welcomed by Audience
Author:
Date: 1960-04-03
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency, 2nd. The Central Art Troupe of the Hungarian Communist Youth League gave its first performance at the Capital People's Theater tonight.

Vice Premier Lu Dingyi watched the performance and went on stage to shake hands with the performers one by one to congratulate them on the success of their performance. Also watching tonight's performance were Vice Minister of Foreign Affairs Zeng Yongquan, Deputy Directors of the Commission for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries Zhang Zhixiang, Ding Xilin, and Luo Jun, Vice Minister of Culture Xu Guangxiao, Vice President of the Sino-Hungarian Friendship Association Xiao San, President of the Foreign Cultural Association Chu Tunan, and famous figures from the capital's literary and art circles. Also watching the performance were diplomatic envoys and diplomatic officials from various countries stationed in China. The delegation of the Hungarian-Chinese Friendship Association and Hungarian Chargé d'Affaires ad interim to China, Sigeti, also attended.

The nineteen dance and music programs performed tonight were beautiful and moving, filled with the enthusiasm of the Hungarian working people in building socialism and the atmosphere of a happy and joyful life, and were warmly welcomed by 1,500 spectators. The folk dances such as "Ribbon Dance," "Bottle Dance," and "Water Splashing Dance" were simple and beautiful. The dances reflecting real life, "At the Well," "Before the Exam," and "Dear Enemy... Girls," humorously reflected the happy life of the Hungarian people; the "Poster Dance" showed the struggle against the reactionary rulers before liberation. "Sword Dance" and "Segei Dance" have ancient national colors. The orchestra gave outstanding performances of "Hungarian Fantasy," "Hungarian Rhapsody No. 14," "Gypsy Song," and "Folk Song Suite," etc. After the performance, the actors received flowers and congratulations from representatives from all walks of life in the capital.

The Central Art Troupe of the Hungarian Communist Youth League came to China for a performance tour under the 1960 executive plan of the Sino-Hungarian Cultural Cooperation Agreement. Before coming to China, they visited and performed in the Democratic People's Republic of Korea, and received extremely high praise. This art troupe has made great achievements in inheriting national traditions and reflecting real life over the past ten years, and they have won six awards at previous World Youth Festivals. After participating in the celebrations of the 15th anniversary of the liberation of Hungary held in the capital and giving public performances, they will go to Jinan, Shanghai, Nanjing, and Tianjin for visit performances.

### 匈艺术团在京首次演出  节目优美动人受到观众热烈欢迎

1960-04-03
第4版()
专栏：

    匈艺术团在京首次演出
    节目优美动人受到观众热烈欢迎
    新华社2日讯　匈牙利共产主义青年团中央艺术团，今晚在首都人民剧场举行了首次演出。　　陆定一副总理观看了演出并上台与演员们一一握手祝贺他们演出的成功。观看今晚演出的还有外交部副部长曾涌泉，对外文化联络委员会副主任张致祥、丁西林、罗俊，文化部副部长徐光霄，中匈友好协会副会长萧三，对外文化协会会长楚图南及首都文艺界著名人士。观看演出的还有各国驻华使节和外交官员。前来我国访问的匈中友协代表团和匈牙利驻华大使馆临时代办西格蒂也到场。
    今晚演出的十九个舞蹈、音乐节目，优美动人，洋溢着匈牙利劳动人民建设社会主义的热情和欢乐幸福的生活气氛，受到一千五百名观众的热烈欢迎。来自民间的“丝带舞”、“瓶舞”、“泼水舞”等朴素而优美。表现现实生活的舞蹈“在井边”、“考试前”、“亲爱的敌人……姑娘们”，幽默风趣地反映了匈牙利人民的幸福生活；“贴标语舞”则表现了解放前与反动统治者的斗争。“剑舞”、“塞盖伊舞”具有古老的民族色采。管弦乐队出色地演奏了“匈牙利幻想曲”、“匈牙利第十四狂想曲”、“吉普赛之歌”、“民歌组曲”等。演出结束后，演员们接受了首都各界代表的献花和祝贺。
    匈牙利共产主义青年团中央艺术团是根据中匈文化合作协定1960年执行计划前来我国进行访问演出的。在来我国之前曾赴朝鲜民主主义人民共和国进行了访问演出，得到极高的评价。这个艺术团十年来在继承民族传统和反映现实生活方面取得了很大成绩，他们在历届世界青年联欢节曾六次获奖。在参加首都举行的匈牙利解放十五周年庆祝活动和进行公演后，将前往济南、上海、南京、天津进行访问演出。
