Title: Party and State Leaders Receive Hungarian Guests
Author:
Date: 1960-04-04
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, 3rd -- Party and state leaders Zhu De, Dong Biwu, Chen Yi, Shen Junru, and Guo Moruo received this evening all members of the Hungarian-Chinese Friendship Association delegation headed by Pataki János, as well as the head of the Hungarian Communist Youth League Central Art Troupe, Vadas László, and the deputy head and artistic director, Golerg Péter, and had cordial talks with them.

Present at the reception were Zhang Xiruo, Shen Yanbing, Zeng Yongquan, Xiao San and others.

Szigeti, Charge d'Affaires ad interim of the Hungarian Embassy in China, was also present at the reception.

### 党和国家领导人接见匈牙利贵宾

1960-04-04
第1版()
专栏：

    党和国家领导人接见匈牙利贵宾
    新华社3日讯　党和国家领导人朱德、董必武、陈毅、沈钧儒、郭沫若今天晚间接见了以团长帕塔基·亚诺什为首的匈牙利中国友好协会代表团全体成员，和匈牙利共青团中央艺术团团长瓦达斯·拉斯洛、副团长兼艺术指导哥雷格·彼得，同他们进行了亲切的谈话。
    接见时在座的，有张奚若、沈雁冰、曾涌泉、萧三等。
    接见时，匈牙利驻中国大使馆临时代办西格蒂也在座。
