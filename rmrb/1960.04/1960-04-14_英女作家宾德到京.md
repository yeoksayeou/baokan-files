Title: British Woman Writer Binder Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1960-04-14
Page: 4

    Xinhua News Agency, April 12th: British woman writer Binder arrived in Beijing by plane today at the invitation of the Chinese People's Association for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries.

### 英女作家宾德到京

1960-04-14
第4版()
专栏：

    英女作家宾德到京
    新华社12日讯　应中国人民对外文化协会邀请来我国访问的英国女作家宾德，今天乘飞机到达北京。
