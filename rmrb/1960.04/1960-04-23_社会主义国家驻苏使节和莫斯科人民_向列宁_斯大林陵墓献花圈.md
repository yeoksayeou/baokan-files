Title: Envoys from Socialist Countries to the Soviet Union and People of Moscow Lay Wreaths at the Lenin-Stalin Mausoleum
Author:
Date: 1960-04-23
Page: 3
Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, April 22nd: To commemorate the 90th anniversary of the birth of the great Lenin, Liu Xiao, the Chinese Ambassador to the Soviet Union, and embassy staff members laid a wreath at the Lenin-Stalin Mausoleum this morning. They were accompanied by Molochkov, Director of the Protocol Department of the Soviet Ministry of Foreign Affairs, and Gutikov, Acting Director of the Far East Department.
    Ambassadors and embassy staff members from other socialist countries also laid wreaths at the Mausoleum. From early morning today, workers from Moscow's factories, old Bolsheviks, youth, and students flocked to pay their respects at the Lenin-Stalin Mausoleum. The area around the tomb was filled with flowers and wreaths.

### 社会主义国家驻苏使节和莫斯科人民  向列宁—斯大林陵墓献花圈

1960-04-23
第3版()
专栏：

    社会主义国家驻苏使节和莫斯科人民
    向列宁—斯大林陵墓献花圈
    新华社莫斯科22日电　为了纪念伟大列宁诞生九十周年，中国驻苏联大使刘晓和使馆人员今天上午到列宁、斯大林陵墓前献了花圈。陪同他们献花圈的有苏联外交部礼宾司司长莫洛奇科夫、远东司代理司长顾悌科夫。
    其他社会主义国家大使和使馆人员都向陵墓献了花圈。今天从清晨起，莫斯科各工厂的工人、老布尔什维克、青年和学生们纷纷前往拜谒列宁——斯大林陵墓。墓旁布满了鲜花和花圈。
