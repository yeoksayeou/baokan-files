Title: Warsaw Military Court Sentences American Spy to Imprisonment
Author:
Date: 1960-04-13
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, 12th - According to the Polish Press Agency's Warsaw dispatch on the 11th: The Warsaw Military Court tried the case of Shaum Bowerski, a spy for American detective agencies, and sentenced him to ten years of imprisonment in accordance with the law.

### 华沙专区军事法庭  判处一名美国间谍徒刑

1960-04-13
第5版()
专栏：

    华沙专区军事法庭
    判处一名美国间谍徒刑
    新华社12日讯　据波兰通讯社华沙11日讯：波兰华沙专区军事法庭审讯了美国侦探机关间谍沙姆鲍尔斯基案件，并且依法判处他十年徒刑。
