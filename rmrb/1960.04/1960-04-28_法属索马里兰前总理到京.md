Title: Former Premier of French Somaliland Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1960-04-28
Page: 2

    Xinhua News Agency, April 27th – Former Premier of French Somaliland and Chairman of the French Somaliland Democratic Union, Mohamed Harbi, and Secretary-General of the French Somaliland Democratic Union, Ali Abdillahi, arrived in Beijing by plane today. They were invited by the Chinese People's Institute of Foreign Affairs and the Chinese Committee for Afro-Asian Solidarity to visit China and participate in the "May 1st" International Labor Day celebrations.
    Those who went to the airport to welcome them included Hu Yuzhi, Vice President of the Chinese People's Institute of Foreign Affairs; Zhu Ziqi, Secretary-General of the Chinese Committee for Afro-Asian Solidarity; and Wu Maosun, Secretary-General of the Chinese People's Institute of Foreign Affairs and Executive Director of the Chinese-African People's Friendship Association.

### 法属索马里兰前总理到京

1960-04-28
第2版()
专栏：

    法属索马里兰前总理到京
    新华社27日讯　法属索马里兰前总理、法属索马里兰民主联盟主席穆罕默德·哈比和法属索马里兰民主联盟总书记阿里·阿卜迪拉西，今天乘飞机到达北京。他们是应中国人民外交学会和中国亚非团结委员会邀请来我国访问并参加“五一”国际劳动节庆祝活动的。
    前往机场欢迎的有中国人民外交学会副会长胡愈之，中国亚非团结委员会秘书长朱子奇，中国人民外交学会秘书长、中国非洲人民友好协会常务理事吴茂荪。
