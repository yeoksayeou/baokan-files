Title: President of Brazil's "Ultima Hora" Leaves Beijing for Home
Author:
Date: 1960-04-19
Page: 5

    Column:

    President of Brazil's "Ultima Hora" Leaves Beijing for Home
    Xinhua News Agency, April 18th. Paulo Silveira, president of the Brazilian newspaper "Ultima Hora," and Octavio Malta, editor-in-chief, who were invited to China for a visit by the All-China Journalists Association and the Chinese People's Association for Friendship with Foreign Countries, left Beijing by plane today to return home. After arriving in Beijing on March 21st, they visited Luoyang, Wuhan, Guangzhou, Hangzhou, Shanghai, and Hefei.
    On the 15th, Zhu Muzhi, vice chairman of the All-China Journalists Association, held a farewell banquet for them. Many people from Beijing's journalistic circles attended the banquet.

### 巴西“最后一点钟报”  社长离京回国

1960-04-19
第5版()
专栏：

    巴西“最后一点钟报”
    社长离京回国
    新华社18日讯　应中华全国新闻工作者协会和中国人民对外文化协会邀请来华访问的巴西“最后一点钟报”社长保罗·西尔维拉和总编辑奥克塔维奥·马尔塔今天乘飞机离开北京回国。他们在3月21日抵京后，曾访问了洛阳、武汉、广州、杭州、上海和合肥等地。
    15日，中华全国新闻工作者协会副主席朱穆之为他们举行了送别宴会。北京新闻界人士多人出席了宴会。
