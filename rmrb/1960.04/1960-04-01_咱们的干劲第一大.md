Title: Our Enthusiasm is the Greatest!
Author: 
Date: 1960-04-01
Page: 4

    Our Enthusiasm is the Greatest!
    The sky is not high,
    Our ambition is the highest;
    The earth is not big,
    Our enthusiasm is the greatest.
    With the General Line,
    No matter how big the difficulties,
    Treat them like a tiny sesame seed!
        　　　　　Shanggang Workers
