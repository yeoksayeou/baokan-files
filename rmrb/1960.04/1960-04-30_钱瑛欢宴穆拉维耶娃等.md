Title: Qian Ying Hosts Banquet for Muravyeva and Others
Author:
Date: 1960-04-30
Page: 7

Xinhua News Agency, April 29th: Qian Ying, Minister of Internal Affairs, hosted a banquet tonight to welcome Muravyeva, Minister of Social Security of the Russian Soviet Federative Socialist Republic, and Filimonova, Director of the Bureau for Mothers with Many Children, who are visiting China for a friendly visit.

Those attending the banquet included Shuai Mengqi, Deputy Director of the Organization Department of the CPC Central Committee; Wu Defeng, Deputy Director of the State Council Political and Legal Office; Li Jiebo, Vice Chairman of the All-China Federation of Trade Unions; Hu Naiqiu, Secretary of the Secretariat of the All-China Women's Federation; and representatives from the Sino-Soviet Friendship Association and the Ministry of Health.

Also attending the banquet were Sudarikov, Counselor of the Soviet Embassy in China, and others.

### 钱瑛欢宴穆拉维耶娃等

1960-04-30
第7版()
专栏：

    钱瑛欢宴穆拉维耶娃等
    新华社29日讯　内务部部长钱瑛今晚设宴欢迎前来我国进行友好访问的俄罗斯苏维埃联邦共和国社会保障部长穆拉维耶娃及多子女母亲管理局局长菲里莫诺娃。
    参加宴会的有中共中央组织部副部长帅孟奇，国务院政法办公室副主任吴德峰，中华全国总工会副主席李颉伯，全国妇联书记处书记胡耐秋和中苏友好协会、卫生部的代表。
    参加宴会的还有苏联驻华大使馆参赞苏达利柯夫等。
