Title: At the Invitation of Chairman Liu Shaoqi, President of the Republic of Guinea Will Visit Our Country
Author:
Date: 1960-04-15
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, April 14th: At the invitation of Liu Shaoqi, Chairman of the People's Republic of China, Sékou Touré, President of the Republic of Guinea, will pay a friendly visit to the People's Republic of China at a time he deems suitable. President Touré has accepted Chairman Liu Shaoqi's invitation.

### 应刘少奇主席的邀请  几内亚共和国总统将访问我国

1960-04-15
第1版()
专栏：

    应刘少奇主席的邀请
    几内亚共和国总统将访问我国
    新华社14日讯　中华人民共和国主席刘少奇邀请几内亚共和国总统塞古·杜尔在他认为适合的时候来中华人民共和国进行友好访问。杜尔总统已接受了刘少奇主席的这项邀请。
