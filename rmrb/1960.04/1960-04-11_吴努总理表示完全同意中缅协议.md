Title: Premier U Nu Expresses Complete Agreement with Sino-Burmese Agreement
Author:
Date: 1960-04-11
Page: 7

Xinhua News Agency, Rangoon, April 10th: Burmese Prime Minister U Nu said at a press conference held on the 9th, his first since returning to office, that he completely agreed with the agreements recently signed between former Burmese Prime Minister Ne Win and Chinese Premier Zhou Enlai between the two countries of China and Burma.

U Nu also said that during Premier Zhou Enlai's visit to Burma, he would discuss with Premier Zhou Enlai the organization of a joint committee to discuss and resolve various specific issues related to the Sino-Burmese border.

### 吴努总理表示完全同意中缅协议

1960-04-11
第7版()
专栏：

    吴努总理表示完全同意中缅协议
    新华社仰光10日电　缅甸总理吴努9日在他重新执政后第一次举行的记者招待会上说，他完全同意前缅甸总理奈温同周恩来总理最近签署的中缅两国之间的协议。
    吴努又说，在周恩来总理访问缅甸时，他将同周恩来总理讨论关于组织一个商谈解决中缅边界各项具体问题的联合委员会。
