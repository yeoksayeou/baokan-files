Title: Union Delegations and Representatives from Seventeen Countries Come to China to Participate in "May Day"

Author:
Date: 1960-04-29
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, April 28th:

    Welcoming Honored Guests on the Eve of May Day
    Union Delegations and Representatives from Seventeen Countries Come to China to Participate in "May Day"
    The Korean Trade Union Delegation headed by Kim Hee-joon, Chairman of the Hamgyongnam-do Committee of the Korean Federation of Trade Unions; the Romanian Trade Union Delegation headed by Sandu Ioan, Chairman of the Central Committee of the Romanian Medical Workers' Union; the Bulgarian Trade Union Delegation headed by Atanas Naidenov, Chairman of the Central Committee of the Bulgarian Miners' Union; the Indonesian Workers' Delegation headed by Sudejo, Chairman of the Trade Union of the Indonesian Ministry of Information; the Ceylon Trade Union Federation Delegation headed by Mandis, Chairman of the Ceylon Trade Union Federation; the Guinean Trade Union Federation Delegation headed by Keita Masmale, Secretary-General of the Guinean Veterinary Trade Union; the Malian Federation of Trade Unions Delegation headed by N'do Amadou Koumba, Administrative and Economic Secretary of the National Union of Malian Federation; the Zanzibar Federation of Labor Delegation headed by Moyu Hassan Nasso, Secretary-General of the Pemba Labor Union; the Italian General Confederation of Labor Delegation headed by Pigna Bruno, Member of the Executive Committee of the Italian General Confederation of Labor; and the Australian Council of Trade Unions Delegation headed by Robert Wilson, Federal Secretary of the Australian Automobile Manufacturing Union, arrived in Beijing today by train and plane respectively. They are invited by the All-China Federation of Trade Unions to participate in the "May Day" International Labor Day celebrations and to visit China.
    Liu Changsheng and Zhu Xuefan, Vice-Chairmen of the All-China Federation of Trade Unions, Zhang Xiuzhu, Secretary of the Secretariat of the All-China Federation of Trade Unions, and others welcomed them at the railway station and airport.
    Xinhua News Agency, April 28th: The Czechoslovak Trade Union Delegation of four, headed by Josef Obaufsky, Secretary of the Slovak Trade Union Council, arrived in Beijing by plane on the afternoon of the 27th. They were invited by the All-China Federation of Trade Unions to participate in the "May Day" International Labor Day celebrations in China. After "May Day" International Labor Day, they will visit China as members of the Czechoslovak Friendship Delegation at the invitation of the Sino-Czech Friendship Association. Xu Zhizhen, Vice-Chairman of the All-China Federation of Trade Unions, and Xia Yan, President of the Sino-Czech Friendship Association, welcomed the guests at the airport today.
    Xinhua News Agency, April 28th: The German Trade Union Delegation headed by Lukas Verner, Member of the Presidium of the Confederation of Free German Trade Unions; the Polish Trade Union Delegation headed by Franciszek Lech, Member of the Presidium of the Central Council of Polish Trade Unions; the Congolese National Workers' Union Delegation headed by Edouard Valentin Moudombe, First Secretary-General of the Congolese National Workers' Union; the Costa Rican Trade Union Delegation headed by Alvaro Montero Vega, Secretary of the Costa Rican General Confederation of Workers; the French General Confederation of Labor Delegation headed by André Betelu, Secretary of the Secretariat of the French General Confederation of Labor; and the Singapore Trade Union Congress Delegation headed by Devan Nair, Advisor to the Singapore Amalgamated Union of Public Employees, arrived in Beijing by plane on the afternoon of the 27th. They were invited by the All-China Federation of Trade Unions to participate in the "May Day" celebrations and to visit China.
    Zhu Xuefan and Xu Zhizhen, Vice-Chairmen of the All-China Federation of Trade Unions, and others welcomed them at the airport.

### 十七个国家的工会代表团和代表来我国参加“五一”节

1960-04-29
第6版()
专栏：

    五一前夕迎佳宾
    十七个国家的工会代表团和代表来我国参加“五一”节
    新华社28日讯　以朝鲜职业总同盟咸镜南道委员会委员长金熙俊为首的朝鲜工会代表团、以罗马尼亚医务工会中央委员会主席桑杜·依昂恩为首的罗马尼亚工会代表团、以保加利亚矿工工会中央委员会主席阿塔纳斯·纳依德诺夫为首的保加利亚工会代表团、以印度尼西亚情报部工会主席苏德佐为首的印度尼西亚工人代表团、以锡兰工会联合会主席曼迪斯为首的锡兰工会联合会代表团、以几内亚兽医工会总书记凯塔·马斯玛勒为首的几内亚工人工会联合会代表团、以马里联邦全国工会联合会行政、经济书记恩多·阿玛杜·库姆巴为首的马里联邦工会联合会代表团、以奔巴劳工联合会总书记摩郁·哈萨姆·纳索为首的桑给巴尔劳工联合会代表团、以意大利总工会执行委员会委员彼尼亚·布鲁诺为首的意大利总工会代表团、以澳大利亚汽车制造工会联邦书记罗伯特·威尔逊为首的澳大利亚工会理事会代表团，今天分别乘火车、飞机到达北京。他们是应中华全国总工会邀请，来我国参加“五一”国际劳动节庆祝活动和访问的。
    到车站、机场欢迎的有中华全国总工会副主席刘长胜、朱学范，中华全国总工会书记处书记张修竹等。
    新华社28日讯　以斯洛伐克工会理事会书记约瑟夫·奥巴夫斯基为首的捷克斯洛伐克工会代表团一行四人，27日下午乘飞机到达北京。他们是应中华全国总工会的邀请来我国参加“五一”国际劳动节庆祝活动的。在“五一”国际劳动节以后，他们将作为捷克斯洛伐克友好代表团的成员，应中捷友好协会的邀请在我国进行友好访问。中华全国总工会副主席许之桢、中捷友好协会会长夏衍今天到机场欢迎客人。
    新华社28日讯　以自由德国工会联合会主席团委员鲁卡斯·维尔纳为首的德国工会代表团、以波兰工会中央理事会主席团委员弗朗齐舍克·列赫为首的波兰工会代表团、以刚果全国工人联合会第一总书记爱德华·瓦朗丁·穆东伯为首的刚果全国工人联合会代表团、以哥斯达黎加工人总工会书记阿耳瓦罗·蒙得罗·维加为首的哥斯达黎加工会代表团、以法国总工会书记处书记安德烈·贝特卢特为首的法国总工会代表团、以新加坡陆路交通工会联合会顾问德万·奈尔为首的新加坡职工联合总会代表团，27日下午乘飞机到达北京。他们是应中华全国总工会邀请，来我国参加“五一”庆祝活动和访问的。
    到机场欢迎的有中华全国总工会副主席朱学范、许之桢等。
