Title: CPPCC Meeting Elects He Xiangning as Vice-Chairman of the National Committee
Author:
Date: 1960-04-12
Page: 3

Xinhua News Agency, 11th — The Second Session of the Third National Committee of the Chinese People’s Political Consultative Conference, at its plenary meeting today, upon the proposal of the Standing Committee, elected He Xiangning as Vice-Chairman of the Third National Committee.

### 政协会议补选何香凝为全国委员会副主席

1960-04-12
第3版()
专栏：

    政协会议补选何香凝为全国委员会副主席
    新华社11日讯　中国人民政治协商会议第三届全国委员会第二次会议，在今天举行的全体会议上，根据常务委员会的提议，补选何香凝为第三届全国委员会副主席。
