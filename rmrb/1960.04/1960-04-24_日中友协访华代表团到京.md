Title: Japan-China Friendship Association Delegation Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1960-04-24
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, April 23rd. The delegation of the Japan-China Friendship Association, invited by the Chinese People's Association for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries to visit China, arrived in Beijing this morning by train from Guangzhou. The delegation of twenty people was led by Akatsu Masuzo, Managing Director of the Japan-China Friendship Association. Also arriving in Beijing with the delegation were Japanese journalists Takeo Ito and two others. Welcoming them at the station were Yang Hansheng, Vice President of the Chinese People's Association for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries, Vice Secretaries-General Lin Lin, Sun Pinghua, Jin Ziming, and Council Members Yang Chunsong, Xie Nanguang, Zhao Anbo, Deputy Secretary-General of the Chinese People's Institute of Foreign Affairs Xiao Xiangqian, and journalists Ding Tuo, etc.

### 日中友协访华代表团到京

1960-04-24
第5版()
专栏：

    日中友协访华代表团到京
    新华社23日讯　应中国人民对外文化协会邀请来我国访问的日中友好协会访华代表团一行二十人，在团长、日中友好协会常务理事赤津益造率领下，今天上午乘火车从广州到达北京。和代表团一同到达北京的还有日本新闻界人士伊藤武雄等三人。到车站欢迎的有中国人民对外文化协会副会长阳翰笙，副秘书长林林、孙平化、金子明和理事杨春松、谢南光、赵安博，外交学会副秘书长肖向前，新闻界人士丁拓等。
