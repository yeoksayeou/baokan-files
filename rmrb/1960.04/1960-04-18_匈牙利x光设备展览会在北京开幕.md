Title: Hungarian X-ray Equipment Exhibition Opens in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1960-04-18
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, April 15th: The Hungarian People's Republic X-ray Equipment Exhibition opened this morning at the Hungarian Industrial Sample Exhibition Hall in Beijing.

This exhibition is sponsored by the Commercial Counselor's Office of the Hungarian Embassy in China. The exquisite exhibits on display demonstrate the high level of Hungarian X-ray medical device manufacturing and the remarkable achievements in the application of X-rays for diagnosis. Mr. Szigeti, Chargé d'Affaires ad interim of the Hungarian Embassy in China, spoke at the opening ceremony. He pointed out that this exhibition not only demonstrates the effective labor of the Hungarian working people, but also embodies the fraternal relationship between the Chinese and Hungarian people, which is constantly developing in all areas of life. Xiao Fangzhou, Deputy Secretary-General of the China Council for the Promotion of International Trade, also spoke at the opening ceremony. He said that this exhibition provides us with a good learning opportunity. On behalf of the China Council for the Promotion of International Trade, I wish the exhibition a successful display and the further development of economic and technological cooperation between China and Hungary.

Representatives from Chinese health departments and medical equipment manufacturing units, experts from the medical community, professors, and other people also attended today's opening ceremony.

Hungary has also sent Professor Szebők Zoltán, a famous Doctor of Medicine and winner of the Kossuth Prize, to give special lectures during the exhibition.

### 匈牙利X光设备展览会在北京开幕

1960-04-18
第6版()
专栏：

    匈牙利X光设备展览会在北京开幕
    据新华社15日讯　匈牙利人民共和国X光设备展览会今天上午在北京匈牙利工业样品展览馆开幕。
    这个展览会是由匈牙利驻华大使馆商务参赞处举办的。展览会上陈列的精湛的展品，显示出匈牙利X光医疗器械制造的高度水平，和在运用X光进行诊断方面取得的卓越成就。匈牙利驻华大使馆临时代办西格蒂在开幕式上讲话。他指出，这个展览会不仅显示了匈牙利劳动人民有效的劳动，而且体现了匈中两国人民兄弟般的关系正在生活的一切领域中不断向前发展。中国国际贸易促进委员会副秘书长萧方洲也在开幕式讲了话。他说，这个展览会为我们提供了良好的学习机会，我代表中国国际贸易促进委员会预祝展览会展出成功和中匈两国经济和技术合作关系进一步发展。
    中国卫生部门和医疗设备制造单位的代表、医学界的专家、教授等人士也参加了今天的开幕式。
    匈牙利还派来了著名医学博士、“科苏特”奖金获得者谢布克·查尔丹教授，在展出期间作专题讲演。
