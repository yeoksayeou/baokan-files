Title: Socialist Countries' Public Security Sports Organizations Men's Basketball Tournament Closes
Author:
Date: 1960-04-26
Page: 4

    Xinhua News Agency, April 25th. The First Men's Basketball Tournament of the Public Security Sports Organizations of Socialist Countries solemnly closed this evening at the Beijing Gymnasium.
    At approximately 7 PM, the men's basketball teams from the five participating countries—the Soviet Union's "Dynamo" team, the Mongolian People's Republic Public Security Team, the Polish "Guard" team, the Romanian "Dynamo" team, and China's "Pioneer" team—marched into the arena. The band played marches, and the audience responded with enthusiastic applause.
    The Hungarian "Dosza" Sports Association observers Horvath and Goller, and Hungarian international basketball referee Vincze, as well as the German Democratic Republic "Dynamo" Sports Association observers Kirst and Herschel, were also invited to attend the closing ceremony.
    Wang Zhao, Director of the Tournament Organizing Committee and Director of the Chinese "Pioneer" Sports Association, delivered the closing address. He expressed heartfelt thanks and sincere respect to the brotherly delegations and observers for their assistance and support in all aspects of the tournament, and to the referees of all countries for their conscientious and impartial spirit in their contributions to the tournament. He said that this competition left an extremely good impression on the Chinese people and played a great role in encouraging and promoting China's sports undertakings, especially the sports movement of China's public security system.
    Wang Zhao said that this competition has been permeated with the spirit of proletarian internationalism from beginning to end, demonstrating the close friendship and unity between the socialist camp countries headed by the Soviet Union. Athletes from all countries have shown the revolutionary heroism and collectivist spirit of forging ahead bravely, cooperating closely, and striving for the top; they have demonstrated the excellent qualities and communist style of unity, friendship, mutual learning, and being neither arrogant in victory nor discouraged in defeat.
    Wang Zhao said that the brilliance of this competition proves that the sports undertakings of socialist countries are full of youthful vitality, flourishing, and thriving. In this competition, no matter which country wins, it is a victory for the sports undertakings of the entire socialist camp. Let us work together to strive to raise the level of our sports to the highest peak in the world.
    Wang Zhao then presented awards to the top three teams in the competition. China's "Pioneer" team won the championship. The Polish "Guard" team won second place. The Soviet Union's "Dynamo" team took third place.
    After the closing ceremony, two exciting exhibition matches were held: the Soviet Union's "Dynamo" team versus the Beijing Men's Basketball Team, and the Romanian "Dynamo" team versus the Beijing "Pioneer" Men's Basketball Team. As a result, the Soviet Union's "Dynamo" team and the Beijing "Pioneer" team won with scores of 82 to 76 and 71 to 56 respectively.

### 社会主义国家公安体育组织  男子篮球比赛大会闭幕

1960-04-26
第4版()
专栏：

    社会主义国家公安体育组织
    男子篮球比赛大会闭幕
    新华社25日讯　社会主义国家公安体育组织第一届男子篮球比赛大会，今晚在北京体育馆比赛馆隆重闭幕。
    七时许，参加这次大会的五个国家的男子篮球队——苏联“狄纳莫”队、蒙古人民共和国公安队、波兰“近卫军”队、罗马尼亚“狄纳莫”队、中国“前卫”队列队入场。这时，乐队高奏进行曲，观众中响起了热烈的掌声。
    匈牙利“杜沙”体育协会观察员霍瓦特、戈勒希和匈牙利篮球国际裁判文蔡，以及德意志民主共和国“狄纳莫”体育协会观察员基尔斯特、赫歇尔等人，都应邀出席了闭幕式。
    大会组织委员会主任、中国“前卫”体育协会主任王昭致闭幕词。他对各兄弟代表队和观察员对这次大会的各项工作给予的帮助和支持，以及各国裁判员以认真、公正的精神对大会所作出的贡献，表示衷心的感谢和诚挚的敬意。他说，这次比赛给我国人民留下了极为良好的印象，对我国的体育事业，特别是对我国公安系统的体育运动起到了很大的鼓舞和推动作用。
    王昭说，这次比赛大会自始至终贯串着无产阶级国际主义的精神，显示了以苏联为首的社会主义阵营各国之间的亲密友谊和团结。各国运动员都表现了勇往直前、密切协作，力争上游的革命英雄主义和集体主义精神；表现了团结友爱、相互学习，胜不骄、败不馁的优良品德和共产主义风格。
    王昭说，这次比赛如此的精彩，证明了社会主义国家的体育事业充满了青春的活力，蓬蓬勃勃，蒸蒸日上。在这次比赛中，无论那个国家获得胜利，都是整个社会主义阵营体育事业的胜利。让我们携手并进，为把我们的各项体育运动的水平提高到世界水平的最高峰而奋斗。
    王昭随后向获得这次比赛前三名的球队发了奖。中国“前卫”队获得了这次比赛的冠军。波兰“近卫军”队获得亚军。苏联“狄纳莫”队得第三名。
    闭幕式后，由苏联“狄纳莫”队对北京男子篮球队；罗马尼亚“狄纳莫”队对北京“前卫”男子篮球队举行了两场精彩的表演赛。结果，苏联“狄纳莫”队和北京“前卫”队分别以八十二比七十六和七十一比五十六获胜。
