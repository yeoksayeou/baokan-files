Title: The United States Again Engages in Provocative Activities in Korea; Representatives from the Korean and Chinese Sides Lodge Strong Protests
Author:
Date: 1960-04-05
Page: 7
Xinhua News Agency, Pyongyang, April 4th: According to the Korean Central News Agency in Kaesong: At today's meeting of the secretaries of both sides of the Korean Military Armistice Commission, Colonel Lin Qiji, the secretary of the Korean and Chinese side, lodged a strong protest with the US side regarding the recent serious violations of the armistice agreement by the US side.

	The secretary of the Korean and Chinese side pointed out at the meeting that on March 27th and 28th, US military aircraft intruded into the airspace over the Korean and Chinese side's area on three occasions. On March 29th, US military personnel used heavy machine guns and other weapons to fire at the Korean and Chinese side's area for up to four hours, firing over a thousand bullets. These bullets landed in the Korean and Chinese side's area, threatening the lives of personnel on the Korean and Chinese side and hindering farmers' agricultural production.

	The secretary of the Korean and Chinese side emphasized that the US side's serious provocative behavior has damaged and trampled upon the most important ceasefire arrangements in the armistice agreement.

	The secretary of the Korean and Chinese side strongly demanded that the US side be responsible for investigating the above-mentioned incidents, severely punish the personnel involved, and take strict measures to ensure that similar hostile acts do not occur in the future.

### 美国又在朝鲜进行挑衅活动  朝中方面代表提出强硬抗议

1960-04-05
第7版()
专栏：

    美国又在朝鲜进行挑衅活动
    朝中方面代表提出强硬抗议
    新华社平壤4日电　据朝鲜中央通讯社开城讯：在今天举行的朝鲜军事停战委员会双方秘书长会议上，朝中方面秘书长林箕济上校就最近美方严重违反停战协定事件向美方提出强硬抗议。
    朝中方面秘书长在会上指出，3月27日和28日两天内，有美方军用飞机三架次侵入朝中方面地区上空；3月29日，美方军事人员用重机枪等武器向朝中方面地区射击前后达四小时，发射了一千多发子弹，这些子弹落在朝中方面地区内，对朝中方面人员的生命造成了威胁，并且妨碍了农民的农业生产。
    朝中方面秘书长强调指出，美方的这种严重挑衅行为破坏和蹂躏了停战协定中最重要的关于停火的安排。
    朝中方面秘书长强烈要求美方负责调查上述事件，严厉惩处有关人员，并采取严格的措施保证今后不再发生类似敌对行为。
