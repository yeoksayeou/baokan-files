Title: Algerian Cabinet Discusses Military Situation, Decides to Broadly Accept Foreign Volunteers; National Liberation Army Annihilates Over a Thousand Enemy Troops in Ten Days
Author:
Date: 1960-04-15
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, Cairo, April 11th. A statement issued here today by the Provisional Government of the Republic of Algeria announced that the Algerian Provisional Government held a cabinet meeting in Tripoli, the capital of Libya, from April 4th to 6th, and made decisions on strengthening the struggle of the Algerian people and their National Liberation Army.

The statement said that at the meeting, the Algerian Provisional Government discussed the political, diplomatic, and military situation in Algeria, as well as the implementation of the decisions of the Algerian National Revolutionary Committee.

The statement said, "The Algerian Provisional Government has decided to accept foreign volunteer soldiers regardless of their origin. Priority will be given to accepting technical personnel needed for the revolution."

The statement said: "The Algerian Provisional Government has taken measures to deal with the French conspiracy to hold so-called regional elections in May."

The statement said, "The Algerian Provisional Government accepted the invitations extended by brotherly and friendly countries and has drafted the action dates for government delegations."

Xinhua News Agency, Cairo, April 13th. The headquarters of the Algerian National Liberation Army announced today in a military communiqué that from April 3rd to 12th, the Algerian National Liberation Army engaged the enemy in twenty-two locations, launched twenty-five attacks on French military strongholds and fortresses, ambushed enemy transport convoys thirty-nine times, and carried out fifty-six sabotage operations throughout Algeria.

The communiqué said that the French army lost 1,166 soldiers during this period, of whom 761 were killed.

During this period, the Algerian National Liberation Army captured 628 weapons, destroyed 56 armored vehicles, and shot down 16 enemy aircraft. Fifty-four French soldiers defected to the Algerian National Liberation Army with all their weapons.

### 阿尔及利亚内阁讨论军事情况  决定广泛地接受外国志愿军  民族解放军在十天内歼敌千余人

1960-04-15
第5版()
专栏：

    阿尔及利亚内阁讨论军事情况
    决定广泛地接受外国志愿军
    民族解放军在十天内歼敌千余人
    新华社开罗11日电　阿尔及利亚共和国临时政府今天在这里发表的一项声明中宣布，阿尔及利亚临时政府在4月4日到6日在利比亚首都的黎波里举行内阁会议，作出了关于加强阿尔及利亚人民及其民族解放军斗争的决定。
    声明说，在会议上，阿尔及利亚临时政府讨论了阿尔及利亚的政治、外交和军事情况以及阿尔及利亚全国革命委员会的决定的执行情况。
    声明说，“阿尔及利亚临时政府决定不分来源地接受外国志愿军。对革命所需要的技术人员将优先予以接受。”
    声明说：“阿尔及利亚临时政府已经采取措施来对付法国打算在5月举行所谓地区选举的阴谋。”
    声明说，“阿尔及利亚临时政府接受了兄弟友好国家提出的邀请，并且拟订了政府代表团的行动日期。”
    新华社开罗13日电　阿尔及利亚民族解放军总部今天发表军事公报说，从4月3日到12日，阿尔及利亚民族解放军在二十二个地方同敌军进行了遭遇战，对法军据点和堡垒展开了二十五次进攻，伏击敌军运输队三十九次，在阿尔及利亚全境进行了五十六次破坏行动。
    公报说，法军在这段时期里损失了一千一百六十六名士兵，其中有七百六十一名被打死。
    阿尔及利亚民族解放军在这期间缴获六百二十八件武器，击毁五十六辆装甲车，击落敌机十六架。有五十四名法国士兵携带全副武装向阿尔及利亚民族解放军投诚。
