Title: Indian President Hosts Luncheon for Premier Zhou; The Two Premiers Continue Talks; Premier Zhou Pays Courtesy Call on Vice President Radhakrishnan; Chinese Ambassador to India Pan Zili Holds Grand Reception
Author:
Date: 1960-04-22
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, New Delhi, April 21st: Indian President Rajendra Prasad hosted a luncheon today in honor of Premier Zhou Enlai. Vice Premier and Foreign Minister Chen Yi, Vice Foreign Minister Zhang Hanfu, and other members of the Chinese Premier's delegation, including Zhang Yan, Qiao Guanhua, Luo Qingchang, Zhang Wenjin, and Kang Maozhao, attended the luncheon. On the Indian side, those present included: Vice President S. Radhakrishnan, Prime Minister and Foreign Minister Jawaharlal Nehru, Home Minister Govind Ballabh Pant, Minister of Steel, Mines and Fuel Sardar Swaran Singh, Defense Minister V. K. Krishna Menon, Deputy Minister of External Affairs Lakshmi N. Menon, other senior officials of the Ministry of External Affairs, other officials of the Indian government, senior military officers, and members of parliament. Also attending the luncheon were: Chinese Ambassador to India Pan Zili and Indian Ambassador to China G. Parthasarathi.

Xinhua News Agency, New Delhi, April 21st: Premier of the State Council of the People's Republic of China Zhou Enlai and Prime Minister of the Republic of India Jawaharlal Nehru continued their talks this afternoon, discussing all aspects of the boundary question. The discussion lasted for two and a half hours. They will meet again tomorrow morning.

Xinhua News Agency, New Delhi, April 21st: Premier Zhou Enlai, Vice Premier Chen Yi, and other senior members of the Premier's delegation paid a courtesy call on Dr. Sarvepalli Radhakrishnan, Vice President of the Republic of India, this morning. They had a general discussion lasting approximately ninety minutes. Also present during the visit were Chinese Ambassador to India Pan Zili and Indian Ambassador to China G. Parthasarathi.

Xinhua News Agency, New Delhi, April 21st: Premier Zhou Enlai, Vice Premier and Foreign Minister Chen Yi, and other senior members of the Premier's delegation paid a visit this morning to Govind Ballabh Pant, Home Minister of India and member of the Working Committee of the All-India Congress Committee, and held talks with him for approximately one hour and forty-five minutes. Present at the meeting were Minister of Steel, Mines and Fuel Sardar Swaran Singh and Indian Ambassador to China G. Parthasarathi.

Xinhua News Agency, New Delhi, April 21st: Chinese Vice Premier and Foreign Minister Chen Yi received Indian Minister of Steel, Mines and Fuel Swaran Singh this afternoon. Also present at the reception was Chinese Vice Foreign Minister Zhang Hanfu.

Xinhua News Agency, New Delhi, April 21st: Chinese Ambassador to India Pan Zili held a grand reception this evening in honor of Premier Zhou Enlai's visit to India. Indian Vice President Radhakrishnan and Prime Minister Nehru attended the reception. Chinese Vice Premier and Foreign Minister Chen Yi, Vice Foreign Minister Zhang Hanfu, and other members of the Chinese Premier's delegation also attended the reception. Also attending the reception were: Indian cabinet ministers, ministers of state, members of parliament, senior military officers of the armed forces, prominent figures from society, foreign diplomatic envoys, and representatives of the overseas Chinese community here.

The reception was held in the garden of the Chinese Embassy. The guests and hosts engaged in pleasant conversations.

Xinhua News Agency, New Delhi, April 21st: Premier Zhou Enlai, when answering questions raised by a group of Indian reporters at the reception held here today by Chinese Ambassador to India Pan Zili, said that he was very pleased to meet with Prime Minister Nehru in Delhi and also very pleased to have contact with many responsible individuals. He said that this enhanced mutual understanding, and we came here to seek ways to resolve issues related to both countries. He added that this is our sincere wish.

An Indian reporter asked about the progress of the talks between the Chinese and Indian Premiers. The Chinese Premier, in answering this question, said that naturally there would be progress.

### 印度总统设午宴招待周总理  两国总理继续会谈，周总理拜会拉达克里希南副总统  我驻印度大使潘自力举行盛大的招待会

1960-04-22
第1版()
专栏：

    印度总统设午宴招待周总理
    两国总理继续会谈，周总理拜会拉达克里希南副总统
    我驻印度大使潘自力举行盛大的招待会
    新华社新德里21日电　印度共和国总统拉金德拉·普拉沙德今天举行午宴招待周恩来总理。副总理兼外交部长陈毅元帅、外交部副部长章汉夫和中国总理的其他随行人员张彦、乔冠华、罗青长、章文晋、康矛召等出席了午宴。印度方面出席午宴的有：副总统萨·拉达克里希南，总理兼外交部长贾·尼赫鲁，内政部长戈·巴·潘特，钢铁、矿业和燃料部长萨·斯·辛格，国防部长文·克·克·梅农，外交部副部长拉·梅农夫人和外交部的其他高级官员，印度政府的其他官员，高级军官和议员们。出席午宴的还有：中国驻印度大使潘自力和印度驻中国大使高·帕塔萨拉蒂。
    新华社新德里21日电　中华人民共和国国务院总理周恩来和印度共和国总理贾瓦哈拉尔·尼赫鲁今天下午继续举行了会谈，讨论边界问题的各个方面。讨论进行了两个半小时。他们明天上午将再次会晤。
    新华社新德里21日电　周恩来总理、陈毅副总理和总理随行人员中的其他高级人员今天上午拜会了印度共和国副总统萨瓦帕利·拉达克里希南博士。他们进行了大约九十分钟的一般性讨论。拜会时在座的还有中国驻印度大使潘自力和印度驻中国大使高·帕塔萨拉蒂。
    新华社新德里21日电　周恩来总理、副总理兼外交部长陈毅和总理的其他高级随行人员今天上午拜访了印度内政部长、国大党全国委员会工作委员会委员戈·巴·潘特，同他进行了约一小时四十五分钟的会谈。会见时在座的有钢铁、矿业和燃料部长萨·斯·辛格和印度驻中国大使高·帕塔萨拉蒂。
    新华社新德里21日电　中国副总理兼外交部长陈毅元帅今天下午接见了印度钢铁、矿业和燃料部长斯瓦兰·辛格。接见时在座的有中国外交部副部长章汉夫。
    新华社新德里21日电　中国驻印度大使潘自力今天晚上为周恩来总理访问印度举行盛大的招待会。印度副总统拉达克里希南和总理尼赫鲁出席了招待会。中国国务院副总理兼外交部长陈毅元帅、外交部副部长章汉夫和中国总理的其他随行人员也出席了招待会。出席招待会的还有：印度内阁部长们，国务部长们，议员们，武装部队的高级军官，社会著名人士，各国外交使节和这里的华侨代表。
    招待会是在中国大使馆的花园中举行的。宾主进行了愉快的交谈。
    新华社新德里21日电　周恩来总理在中国驻印度大使潘自力今天在这里举行的招待会上回答一批印度记者提出的问题时说，他很高兴在德里同尼赫鲁总理会晤，也很高兴同许多负责人士接触。他说，这增进了相互了解，我们到这里来是为了寻求解决同两国有关的问题的途径的。他又说，这是我们的真诚愿望。
    一位印度记者问到中印两国总理会谈的进展情况。中国总理在回答这个问题时说，自然是会有进展的。
