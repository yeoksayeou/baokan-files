Title: Chervonenko Holds Film Evening to Commemorate the 90th Anniversary of Lenin's Birth
Author:
Date: 1960-04-16
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, April 15th: Soviet Ambassador to China, Chervonenko, held a film evening tonight to commemorate the 90th anniversary of Lenin's birth, screening the Soviet documentaries "Immortal Lenin" and "The Places Where Lenin Lived."

Deng Xiaoping, General Secretary of the CPC Central Committee; Peng Zhen, member of the Political Bureau of the CPC Central Committee; and Liu Lantao and Yang Shangkun, alternate secretaries of the Secretariat of the CPC Central Committee, attended the film evening.

Also attending the evening were Xi Zhongxun, Vice Premier of the State Council; Lin Feng, Vice Chairman of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress; Xie Juezai, President of the Supreme People's Court; Luo Guibo and Zeng Yongquan, Vice Ministers of Foreign Affairs; Wu Yuzhang, Li Dequan, Shen Yanbing, and Li Zhuchen, Vice Presidents of the Sino-Soviet Friendship Association; and diplomatic envoys from various countries to China. Also present were heads of various departments of the Chinese government, the Vice President of the Beijing Sino-Soviet Friendship Association, advanced producers from the Shijingshan Iron and Steel Company, members of the China-Soviet Friendship People's Commune, and people from all walks of life in the capital.

### 纪念列宁诞辰九十周年  契尔沃年科举行电影晚会

1960-04-16
第4版()
专栏：

    纪念列宁诞辰九十周年
    契尔沃年科举行电影晚会
    新华社15日讯　苏联驻中国大使契尔沃年科今晚举行纪念列宁诞辰九十周年的电影晚会，放映苏联纪录片“永生的列宁”和“列宁住过的地方”。
    中共中央总书记邓小平，中共中央政治局委员彭真，中共中央书记处候补书记刘澜涛、杨尚昆出席了电影晚会。
    出席晚会的还有国务院副总理习仲勋，人民代表大会常务委员会副委员长林枫，最高人民法院院长谢觉哉，外交部副部长罗贵波、曾涌泉，中苏友好协会总会副会长吴玉章、李德全、沈雁冰、李烛尘，以及各国驻华使节。出席晚会的还有我国政府各部门负责人，北京市中苏友好协会副会长，石景山钢铁公司先进生产者，中苏友好人民公社社员和首都各界人士等。
