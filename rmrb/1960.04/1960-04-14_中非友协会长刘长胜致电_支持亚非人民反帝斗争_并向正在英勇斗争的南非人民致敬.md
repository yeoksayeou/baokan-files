Title: President of the Chinese-African Peoples' Friendship Association Liu Changsheng Sends a Telegram Supporting the Anti-Imperialist Struggle of Asian and African Peoples and Saluting the Valiant Struggle of the People of South Africa
Author:
Date: 1960-04-14
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, April 13th: Liu Changsheng, President of the Chinese-African Peoples' Friendship Association, sent a telegram today to the Second Asian-African Peoples' Solidarity Conference, expressing firm support for the conference.

Liu Changsheng's telegram stated: "Since the First Asian-African Peoples' Solidarity Conference, under the brilliant illumination of the Bandung spirit, the unity and friendly relations between the peoples of Asian and African countries have been continuously consolidated and developed. The heroic struggles of the peoples of Asian and African countries, especially the African people, to win and safeguard national independence against imperialism and colonialism are developing vigorously. This struggle has received the sympathy and support of the Chinese people and progressive people throughout the world.

"We are deeply convinced that, on the basis of continuing to promote the Bandung spirit, the Second Asian-African Peoples' Solidarity Conference will make even greater contributions to the friendly unity of the Asian and African peoples, as well as to the common struggle to safeguard and strive for Asian-African national independence, oppose imperialism, oppose colonialism, and defend world peace."

Xinhua News Agency, April 13th: Liu Changsheng, President of the Chinese-African Peoples' Friendship Association, sent a telegram today to the African National Congress of South Africa, saying that the 650 million Chinese people will always firmly support the just struggle of the South African people against racial discrimination. He pointed out that this struggle is a part of the entire Asian-African people's struggle against imperialism and colonialism, and for the maintenance and striving for national independence. "We firmly believe that as long as the South African people unite as one and carry this great struggle, which is supported by people all over the world, through to the end, they will surely achieve final victory."

In the telegram, Liu Changsheng expressed his highest respect to the heroic and unyielding people of South Africa, and expressed great anger and strong protest against the South African Federal authorities who are frantically suppressing the South African people.

### 中非友协会长刘长胜致电  支持亚非人民反帝斗争  并向正在英勇斗争的南非人民致敬

1960-04-14
第5版()
专栏：

    中非友协会长刘长胜致电
    支持亚非人民反帝斗争
    并向正在英勇斗争的南非人民致敬
    新华社13日讯　中国非洲人民友好协会会长刘长胜今天打电报给第二届亚非人民团结大会，对这次大会表示坚决支持。
    刘长胜的电报说：“自从第一届亚非人民团结大会以来，在万隆会议精神的光辉照耀下，亚非各国人民的团结友好关系得到了不断的巩固和发展。亚非各国人民，特别是非洲人民为了争取和维护民族独立而进行的反对帝国主义和殖民主义的英勇斗争，正在波澜壮阔地向前发展。这一斗争受到中国人民和世界进步人类的同情和支持。
    “我们深信，在继续发扬万隆会议精神的基础上，第二届亚非人民团结大会必将对亚非人民的友好团结以及维护和争取亚非民族独立、反对帝国主义、反对殖民主义和保卫世界和平的共同的斗争作出更大的贡献。”
    新华社13日讯　中国非洲人民友好协会会长刘长胜今天打电报给南非非洲人国民大会说，六亿五千万中国人民将永远坚决支持南非人民反对种族歧视的正义斗争。他指出，这个斗争是整个亚非人民反对帝国主义和殖民主义、维护与争取民族独立斗争的一部分，“我们坚信，南非人民只要团结一致，把这个受到全世界人民支持的伟大斗争进行到底，就一定能够取得最后的胜利。”
    刘长胜在电报中向英勇不屈的南非人民表示崇高的敬意，并且对正在疯狂镇压南非人民的南非联邦当局表示极大的愤怒和强烈的抗议。
