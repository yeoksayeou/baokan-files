Title: The Gera District and Suburban Areas of Democratic Berlin in Germany Have All Achieved Agricultural Cooperativization
Author:
Date: 1960-04-09
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, Berlin, 8th. The Gera District of the German Democratic Republic and the suburban areas of Democratic Berlin have announced in recent days that agriculture has been fully cooperativized. Thus far, with the exception of Democratic Berlin, ten of the fourteen districts in the Republic have achieved full agricultural cooperativization.

The Gera District and the ninth fully cooperativized district, the Erfurt District, are both adjacent to West Germany. These two districts have a total of more than 691,000 hectares of arable agricultural land. The suburban areas of Democratic Berlin have more than 14,000 hectares of arable agricultural land.

### 德国格拉专区和柏林民主区郊区全部实现农业合作化

1960-04-09
第6版()
专栏：

    德国格拉专区和柏林民主区郊区全部实现农业合作化
    新华社柏林8日电　德意志民主共和国格拉专区和柏林民主区郊区近几天先后宣布农业已全部实现合作化。至此，除柏林民主区外，在共和国十四个专区中已有十个专区实现了农业的全部合作化。
    格拉专区和第九个全部合作化专区——艾尔福特专区都紧邻西德。这两个专区共有农业可耕地六十九万一千多公顷。柏林民主区郊区的农业可耕地有一万四千多公顷。
