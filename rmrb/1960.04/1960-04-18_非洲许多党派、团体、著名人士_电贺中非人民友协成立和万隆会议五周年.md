Title: Many African Parties, Groups, and Prominent Figures Send Congratulations on the Establishment of the Chinese-African People's Friendship Association and the Fifth Anniversary of the Bandung Conference
Author:
Date: 1960-04-18
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency, April 17th. Many parties, social groups, and prominent figures from African countries and regions have sent congratulatory messages to celebrate the establishment of the Chinese-African People's Friendship Association and the fifth anniversary of the Bandung Conference.

    The Secretary-General of the Arab United Republic China Friendship Association, Omar Mari, said in his congratulatory message: "I wish the new association great progress, and may the friendship between the Chinese and our people last forever." The Afro-Asian Committee of Black Africa said in its congratulatory message that we believe that cooperation and friendship between the Chinese and African people will contribute to the promotion of peace and happiness for mankind.

    Felix Moumié, Chairman of the Cameroon People's Union, sent a congratulatory message in the name of the Cameroonian people, who are engaged in a desperate struggle against all forms of colonial interference. In his congratulatory message, he said: "For the suffering peoples of Africa who are subjected to various forms of oppression and are increasingly vigilant against imperialism, the victory of the Chinese revolution is both a valuable encouragement and an inexhaustible source of lessons." The Algerian Red Crescent Society said in its congratulatory message: "We are confident that the friendly relations between our people will be further strengthened." The Central Committee of the African Independence Party said in its congratulatory message: "The Central Committee of the African Independence Party is convinced that the friendship between the Chinese and African people will defeat reactionaries and all imperialists." Da Cruz and Lara of the Angola People's Liberation Alliance said in their congratulatory message: "Long live cooperation for the complete eradication of colonialism and imperialism!"

    The Secretary-General of the Zanzibar Nationalist Party, Abdu Rahman Muhammad, said in his congratulatory message that the African people welcome the establishment of the Chinese-African People's Friendship Association. The African and Chinese people will understand each other through the Friendship Association and enhance the friendship between the Asian and African people. Muhammad Bashi Taji, the National Organizing Secretary of the Sierra Leone People's National Party, said in his congratulatory message: May the Chinese-African People's Friendship Association "have a successful future in promoting the unity of the Chinese and African people and Asian-African solidarity."

    Also sending congratulatory messages were: Zeze Li, Chairman of the Tunisian International Exposition; Rifat, Secretary-General of the Asian-African Economic Cooperation Organization; the Madagascar Democratic Youth Association; the Madagascar Native Students Association; Amani Tani, leader of the Zanzibar Youth's Own League; the Ghana Red Cross Society; Oshiabo Okello, Chairman of the Cairo Office of the Kenyan Political Organization Foreign Representative Office; Fusu Fa Langua, Secretary-General of the Cameroon Democratic Youth League; Babaka Niang, Second Secretary of the African Independence Party; Majumon Diop, First Secretary of the Mali Federation of the African Independence Party; the Liberian Red Cross Society; the Cameroon Agricultural and Forestry Trade Union; and the Federation of Black African Students in France.

### 非洲许多党派、团体、著名人士  电贺中非人民友协成立和万隆会议五周年

1960-04-18
第4版()
专栏：

    非洲许多党派、团体、著名人士
    电贺中非人民友协成立和万隆会议五周年
    新华社17日讯　非洲国家和地区的许多党派、社会团体和著名人士发来贺电，祝贺中国非洲人民友好协会的成立和万隆会议五周年。
    阿拉伯联合共和国中国友好协会秘书长欧默尔·马里在贺电中说：“谨祝新协会获得巨大进展，并愿中国人民和我国人民之间的友谊万古长存。”黑非洲亚非委员会在贺电中说，我们认为中国和非洲人民之间的合作和友谊将为促进人类的和平和幸福，作出贡献。
    喀麦隆人民联盟主席费利克斯·穆米埃以正在同殖民主义各种形式的干涉进行决死斗争的喀麦隆人民的名义，来电祝贺。他在贺电中说：“对于受到各种形式的压迫和对帝国主义愈来愈警觉的非洲受难的各国人民来说，中国革命的胜利既是一个宝贵的鼓励，又是一个汲取教益的永不枯竭的源泉。”阿尔及利亚红新月会在贺电中说：“我们确信我们人民之间的友好关系将会进一步地加强。”非洲独立党中央委员会在贺电中说：“非洲独立党中央委员会确信中国和非洲人民的友谊，将击败反动分子和一切帝国主义分子。”安哥拉人民解放联盟达·克路兹和拉腊在贺电中说：“为彻底铲除殖民主义和帝国主义而进行的合作万岁！”
    桑给巴尔民族主义党总书记阿布都·拉赫曼·穆罕默德在贺电中说，非洲人民欢迎中国非洲人民友好协会的成立。非洲和中国人民将通过友协彼此进行了解，并增强亚非人民之间的友谊。塞拉勒窝内人民国民党全国组织书记穆罕默德·巴希·塔吉在贺电中说：愿中国非洲人民友好协会“在促进中非人民的团结及亚非团结方面有一个成功的未来。”
    发来贺电的还有：突尼斯国际博览会主席泽泽利，亚非经济合作组织秘书长利法特，马达加斯加民主青年协会，马达加斯加原籍学生协会，桑给巴尔青年人自己的联盟领导人阿马尼·坦尼，加纳红十字会，怯尼亚政治组织国外代表机构驻开罗办事处主席欧希阿波·奥克罗，喀麦隆民主青年联盟总书记福素法朗素瓦，非洲独立党第二书记巴巴卡·尼昂，非洲独立党马里联邦第一书记马杰蒙·迪奥普，利比里亚红十字会，喀麦隆农林工会，黑非留法学生联合会。
