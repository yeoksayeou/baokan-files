Title: Premier Zhou Enlai Receives Indian Defense Minister Menon
Author:
Date: 1960-04-21
Page: 1

    Xinhua News Agency, New Delhi, April 20th: Premier Zhou Enlai received Indian Defense Minister V. K. Krishna Menon at the Presidential Palace today at 15:00 local time. Present at the meeting were Chinese Vice Premier and Foreign Minister Marshal Chen Yi and Vice Foreign Minister Zhang Hanfu.

### 周恩来总理接见印国防部长梅农

1960-04-21
第1版()
专栏：

    周恩来总理接见印国防部长梅农
    新华社新德里20日电　周恩来总理今天在当地时间十五时在总统府接见印度国防部长文·克·克·梅农。接见时在座的有中国副总理兼外交部长陈毅元帅和外交部副部长章汉夫。
