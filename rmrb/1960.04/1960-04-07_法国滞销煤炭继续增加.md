Title: French Unsalable Coal Continues to Increase
Author:
Date: 1960-04-07
Page: 7

Western News Scraps

    French Unsalable Coal Continues to Increase

    According to a report by the Swiss "Neue Zürcher Zeitung" on March 26: The total amount of unsold coal in the entire French coal mining industry had reached 11.39 million tons by the end of February (11.03 million tons at the end of 1959, and 8.08 million tons at the end of February last year).

### 法国滞销煤炭继续增加

1960-04-07
第7版()
专栏：西风残叶

    法国滞销煤炭继续增加
    据瑞士“新苏黎世报”3月26日报道：法国整个采煤业滞销的存煤在2月底已经达到一千一百三十九万吨（1959年底是一千一百零三万吨，去年2月底是八百零八万吨）。
