Title: To Our South Korean Brothers
Author: Gao Changfu
Date: 1960-04-29
Page: 8

To Our South Korean Brothers
Beijing People's Printing Factory Worker Gao Changfu

    You harvest the grain but have no food to eat;
    You gather the cotton but have no clothes to wear;
    You cannot see the sun, cannot see the sky;
    You suffer torment in a sea of knives and fire...
    Whenever I think of these things,
    My heart aches as if carved with a knife.
    Who cleaved the beautiful land in two?
    Who tore apart flesh and blood, brothers and sisters?
    Who turned South Korea into an iron cage?
    Who put the chains on you?
    Whenever I think of these things,
    A flame of anger ignites in my heart.
    The vicious American wolves!
    The cruel Syngman Rhee!
    Colluding to harm the people,
    Countless debts of blood are owed.
    Deep-seated blood feuds in our hearts,
    Blood debts must be repaid with blood!
    Thunder rolls over Seoul,
    Storms sweep across South Korea;
    The final moment has arrived,
    Friends, bravely charge forward!
    From the deep mountains and dense forests,
    From the rivers and lakes,
    From the city streets,
    From the rural fields,
    Raise the torches from everywhere,
    Drive the American wolves out of Korea!
    Pick up your hoes, pick up your shovels,
    Pick up your axes, pick up your guns,
    Take up all weapons,
    Smash Syngman Rhee's lair!
    South Korean brothers,
    Advance victoriously,
    Break through the darkness to reach the dawn,
    The light of day is before you!

### 致南朝鲜兄弟们
高长福
1960-04-29
第8版()
专栏：

    致南朝鲜兄弟们
    北京人民印刷厂工人　高长福
    你们打下粮食吃不上饭；
    你们收了棉花没衣穿；
    你们见不到太阳见不到天；
    你们刀山火海受熬煎……。
    每当我想起了这些啊，
    我的心难受的就像用刀挖。
    是谁把大好河山一刀斩断？
    是谁把南北同胞骨肉拆散？
    是谁把南朝鲜变为铁牢笼？
    是谁给你们套上了锁链？
    每当我想起了这些啊，
    我心里就燃起了愤怒的火焰。
    凶恶的美国狼！
    残暴的李承晚！
    狼狈为奸害人民，
    累累血债数不完。
    血海深仇记心里，
    血债定要血来还！
    汉城上空霹雷滚，
    暴风漫卷南朝鲜；
    最后的时刻来到了，
    朋友们哪，勇猛冲向前！
    从深山密林，
    从江河湖边，
    从城市的街头，
    从乡村的田园，
    从一切地方举起火把，
    把美帝野狼赶出朝鲜！
    拿起镐头，拿起钢锨，
    拿起斧锤，拿起枪杆，
    拿起所有的武器，
    把李承晚的巢穴砸烂！
    南朝鲜的兄弟们，
    胜利的前进吧，
    冲破黑暗就是黎明，
    曙光就在你们面前！
