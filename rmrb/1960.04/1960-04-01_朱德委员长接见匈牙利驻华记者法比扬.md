Title: Chairman Zhu De Receives Hungarian Correspondent Fabjan

Author:
Date: 1960-04-01
Page: 3

Xinhua News Agency, 31st — Chairman Zhu De received Ferenc Fabjan, correspondent in China for the Hungarian "People's Freedom Daily," today. Present at the reception were Kang Maozhao, Deputy Director of the Information Department of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs, and Chen Boqing, Deputy Director of the Soviet and European Affairs Department.

　　During the meeting, Chairman Zhu De extended warm congratulations on behalf of the Chinese people to the people of the Hungarian People's Republic on the upcoming 15th anniversary of the liberation of the Hungarian people.

### 朱德委员长接见匈牙利驻华记者法比扬

1960-04-01
第3版()
专栏：

    朱德委员长接见匈牙利驻华记者法比扬
    新华社31日讯　朱德委员长今天接见了匈牙利“人民自由报”驻华记者费伦茨·法比扬。陪同接见的有外交部新闻司副司长康矛召，苏欧司副司长陈伯清。
    朱德委员长在接见中，对即将到来的匈牙利人民解放十五周年的节日，代表中国人民向匈牙利人民共和国人民表示了热烈的祝贺。
