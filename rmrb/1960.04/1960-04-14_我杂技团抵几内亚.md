Title: Our Acrobatic Troupe Arrives in Guinea
Author:
Date: 1960-04-14
Page: 5
Xinhua News Agency, Conakry, April 11th: The second group of members of the Chinese Acrobatic Arts Troupe, led by Qu Wu, Deputy Director of the Commission for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries, arrived in Conakry on the morning of the 11th. The first group of members arrived here on April 3rd.

The Chinese Acrobatic Arts Troupe is in Guinea for a performance tour according to the cultural agreement signed between China and Guinea in 1959.

The garrison commander of the Conakry Administrative Region, Donkala Jean Falke, and member of the Political Bureau of the Guinean Democratic Party, Loufou, welcomed them at the airport today. Also at the airport to welcome them were: Ke Hua, the Chinese Ambassador to Guinea, and other officials from the Chinese Embassy.

### 我杂技团抵几内亚

1960-04-14
第5版()
专栏：

    我杂技团抵几内亚
    新华社科纳克里11日电　由对外文化联络委员会副主任屈武率领的中国杂技艺术团第二批团员于11日上午抵达科纳克里。第一批团员已在4月3日到达这里。
    中国杂技艺术团是根据1959年中国和几内亚之间签订的文化协定前来几内亚作访问演出的。
    科纳克里行政区卫戍司令顿卡拉·让·法尔克和几内亚民主党政治局委员卢福今天到机场欢迎。到机场欢迎他们的还有：中国驻几内亚大使柯华和中国大使馆其它官员。
