Title: The U.S. Intensifies Tensions in Southeast Asia; Southeast Asia Treaty Organization Holds Military Exercises
Author:
Date: 1960-04-30
Page: 6
Xinhua News Agency, April 28th, Bangkok Dispatch: The long-planned large-scale maritime military exercise of the Southeast Asia Treaty Organization (SEATO) aggressive bloc, led by the United States, began today simultaneously in the waters off Bangkok and Manila.

Participating in this exercise, called "Sea Lion," are more than sixty ships, including aircraft carriers, and more than one hundred aircraft (including giant bombers) from the eight member countries of SEATO, as well as approximately 20,000 soldiers.

It is announced that the exercise is presided over by Vice Admiral Begg of the British Far East Fleet.

The Southeast Asia Treaty aggressive bloc is conducting this exercise under the circumstances of the United States intensifying the creation of tensions in the Southeast Asian region.

### 美国加紧在东南亚地区制造紧张局势  东南亚条约组织举行军事演习

1960-04-30
第6版()
专栏：

    美国加紧在东南亚地区制造紧张局势
    东南亚条约组织举行军事演习
    新华社28日讯　曼谷消息：以美国为首的东南亚条约组织侵略集团策划已久的又一次大规模海上军事演习今天同时在曼谷和马尼拉的海面开始。
    参加这次被叫做“海狮”演习的有东南亚条约组织八个成员国的六十多艘包括航空母舰在内的舰只和一百多架飞机（包括巨型轰炸机），还有约两万名士兵。
    据宣布，这次演习由英国远东海军副司令贝格主持。
    东南亚条约侵略集团是在美国加紧在东南亚地区制造紧张局势的情况下进行这次演习的。
