Title: Mongolia and Guinea Decide to Establish Diplomatic Relations
Author:
Date: 1960-04-25
Page: 6

    Mongolia and Guinea Decide to Establish Diplomatic Relations
    Xinhua News Agency, Ulan Bator, April 21st. It was announced here today that the governments of Mongolia and Guinea have reached an agreement to establish diplomatic relations at the ambassadorial level between the two countries.

### 蒙古和几内亚决定建交

1960-04-25
第6版()
专栏：

    蒙古和几内亚决定建交
    新华社乌兰巴托21日电　今天这里宣布，蒙古和几内亚两国政府达成协议，决定在两国间建立大使级外交关系。
