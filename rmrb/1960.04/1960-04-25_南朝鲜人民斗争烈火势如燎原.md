Title: The Flames of Struggle of the South Korean People Are Raging Like a Prairie Fire
Author:
Date: 1960-04-25
Page: 5

The heroic struggle of the South Korean people against the fascist rule of the US-Syngman Rhee clique has seen continuous demonstrations and marches in more than ten cities from April 11th to 23rd, a total of thirteen days. According to incomplete statistics, the number of participants reached more than 200,000. (Attached picture)

### 南朝鲜人民斗争烈火势如燎原

1960-04-25
第5版()
专栏：

    南朝鲜人民斗争烈火势如燎原
    英勇的南朝鲜人民反对美李集团法西斯统治的斗争，从4月11日到23日的十三天中，在十多个城市连续举行示威游行，仅据不完全统计参加人数就达二十余万人。（附图片）
