Title: Belgian Communist Party Congress Concludes Successfully
Author: 
Date: 1960-04-22
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, Brussels, April 19th: The 13th Congress of the Belgian Communist Party concluded victoriously in Liège today after three days of discussion.

    Bernal delivered the closing speech to the congress. He said that all delegates unanimously agreed with the party's political line. This line will become the strong foundation and guideline for the actions of the entire party. This congress marks a very important stage in the struggle of the Belgian people for peace, progress, and against monopoly capital. The congress elected the new leading bodies of the Belgian Communist Party on the 18th. These include a new Central Committee composed of sixty-seven members, a Political Supervisory Committee composed of twelve members, and a Financial Supervisory Committee composed of fourteen members. The new Central Committee elected the party's Politburo and elected Bernal, Béren, Blum, Degoninke, and Vandenbranden as secretaries of the Central Committee.

### 比共代表大会胜利闭幕

1960-04-22
第5版()
专栏：

    比共代表大会胜利闭幕
    据新华社布鲁塞尔19日电　比利时共产党第十三次代表大会经过了三天讨论今天在列日胜利闭幕。
    伯内尔向大会致闭幕词。他说，大会全体代表一致同意党的政治路线。这个路线将成为全党行动的坚强基础与准则。这次大会标志着比利时人民力量争取和平、进步与反对垄断资本的斗争进入了一个非常重要的阶段。大会在18日选出了比利时共产党新的领导机构。其中包括由六十七名委员组成的新的中央委员会，由十二名委员组成的政治监察委员会和由十四名委员组成的财政监察委员会。新的中央委员会选出党的政治局，并选出伯内尔、贝伦、布吕姆、德贡宁克与范登布兰敦为中央委员会书记。
