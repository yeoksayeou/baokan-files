Title: Ecuadorian Professor Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1960-04-11
Page: 7

Xinhua News Agency, April 9th -- Manuel Medina, a professor at the University of Guayaquil, Ecuador, arrived in Beijing by plane this afternoon at the invitation of the Chinese Association for Friendship with Latin America.

Zhu Boshen, Standing Director of the Chinese Association for Friendship with Latin America and Deputy Secretary-General of the Chinese People’s Association for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries, welcomed the guest at the airport.

### 厄瓜多尔一位教授到京

1960-04-11
第7版()
专栏：

    厄瓜多尔一位教授到京
    新华社9日讯　应中国拉丁美洲友好协会邀请前来我国访问的厄瓜多尔瓜亚基尔大学教授马奴埃尔·梅第纳，今天下午乘飞机到达北京。
    中拉友协常务理事、中国人民对外文化协会副秘书长朱伯深到机场欢迎客人。
