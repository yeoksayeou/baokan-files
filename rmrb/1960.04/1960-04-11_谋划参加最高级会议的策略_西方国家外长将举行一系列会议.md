Title: Plotting Strategies for Attending the Highest-Level Meeting Western Foreign Ministers to Hold Series of Meetings
Author:
Date: 1960-04-11
Page: 6

    Xinhua News Agency, Washington, April 10th: The U.S. State Department announced yesterday that the foreign ministers of Western countries will hold a series of meetings in Washington next week to discuss the German and Berlin issues, disarmament issues, and East-West relations issues, in order to strategize for the East-West summit meeting to be held in Paris in May.

### 谋划参加最高级会议的策略  西方国家外长将举行一系列会议

1960-04-11
第6版()
专栏：

    谋划参加最高级会议的策略
    西方国家外长将举行一系列会议
    据新华社10日讯　华盛顿消息：美国国务院昨天宣布，西方国家的外交部长将于下周在华盛顿举行一系列会议，讨论德国问题和柏林问题、裁军问题和东西方关系问题，为5月间将在巴黎举行的东西方最高级会议进行谋划。
