Title: Delegation of the France-China Friendship Association Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1960-04-28
Page: 2
Xinhua News Agency, April 27th. The delegation of the France-China Friendship Association, a group of six people who are visiting our country at the invitation of the Chinese People's Association for Friendship with Foreign Countries, arrived in Beijing by plane this afternoon.

### 法中友协代表团到京

1960-04-28
第2版()
专栏：

    法中友协代表团到京
    新华社27日讯　应中国人民对外文化协会邀请来我国访问的法中友好协会代表团一行六人，今天下午乘飞机到达北京。
