Title: Strengthen Militia Building, Develop Production, Consolidate National Defense
Author:
Date: 1960-04-28
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, April 27th: The National Militia Representative Conference successfully concluded this afternoon. This was a gathering of heroes to promote militia divisions on a large scale, and also a mobilization meeting to accelerate China's socialist construction. After ten days of meetings, all the representatives further clarified the great significance of running militia divisions on a large scale for accelerating China's economic and national defense construction. They established great ambitions and lofty goals, determined to hold even higher the general line of socialist construction and the red banner of Mao Zedong Thought, and to make greater contributions to rapidly building China into a great socialist power with modern industry, modern agriculture, modern science and culture, and modern national defense.

Vice Chairmen of the CPC Central Committee Zhu De and Lin Biao, General Secretary Deng Xiaoping, Vice Chairmen of the People's Republic of China Soong Ching Ling and Dong Biwu, and other Party and state leaders attended today's closing ceremony. When they ascended the rostrum, the audience stood up and applauded thunderously. Also on the rostrum with them were members of the Political Bureau of the CPC Central Committee Lin Boqu, Peng Zhen, Luo Ronghuan, He Long, Alternate Secretary of the Secretariat Liu Lantao, Vice Premiers of the State Council Luo Ruiqing and Xi Zhongxun, Vice Chairmen of the National Defense Council Cheng Qian, Zhang Zhizhong, and Fu Zuoyi, heads of relevant departments of the CPC Central Committee and the State Council, heads of various people's organizations, senior generals of the Chinese People's Liberation Army Chen Geng, Zhang Yunyi, Wang Shusheng, Li Kenong, Zhang Aiping, Yang Yong, Fu Zhong, Xiao Hua, Gan Siqi, Li Juikui, Liu Yalou, Su Zhenhua, Chen Shiju, Fu Qiutao, Li Da, and others, as well as all members of the presidium of the conference.

Vice Chairman of the CPC Central Committee, Vice Premier of the State Council and Minister of National Defense, Marshal Lin Biao delivered the closing speech at the meeting.

Amidst prolonged applause, all the representatives unanimously adopted the congratulatory telegram to the Party Central Committee and Chairman Mao, the congratulatory telegram to the Chinese People's Liberation Army, and the proposal to the national militia. The proposal issued ten grand initiatives to the national militia: actively participate in socialist construction; actively participate in the technical innovation and technical revolution movement; actively participate in the patriotic health movement; actively cooperate with the People's Liberation Army to consolidate national defense; actively maintain social order and protect production; actively learn military technology; actively participate in physical exercise and national defense sports activities; actively learn the glorious traditions of the People's Liberation Army; actively study cultural and scientific knowledge; and actively study Chairman Mao's works. The proposal called on the national militia, together with the people of the whole country, under the wise leadership of the Party Central Committee and Chairman Mao, to exert greater revolutionary efforts, carry out revolutionary competitions, and move from victory to greater victory in the great cause of building and defending socialism.

After the conference passed the proposal to the national militia, one hundred Young Pioneers, boys and girls, ascended the rostrum to the sound of music, representing 560,000 Young Pioneers in Beijing to present poems and flowers to the male and female militia heroes from all fronts of the motherland. They recited: "You respond to Chairman Mao's call to run militia divisions on a large scale, everyone takes up guns, everyone is armed, while studying culture and practicing martial arts, while actively producing, the will of 600 million people, the momentum is surging, making the people magnificent, and making the enemy tremble!" After each section of the Young Pioneers' recitation, thunderous applause erupted in the venue. Then, eight Young Pioneers, carrying two large flower baskets filled with flowers, walked onto the rostrum to present flowers to the heroes.

In the majestic military music, General Luo Ruiqing, Vice Premier of the State Council and Chief of the General Staff of the Chinese People's Liberation Army, the host of today's conference, announced the successful conclusion of the conference.

This morning, Deputy Chief of the General Staff of the Chinese People's Liberation Army, General Zhang Aiping and Deputy Director of the General Political Department, General Gan Siqi, spoke to all the representatives. In the evening, Marshal Lin Biao held a grand banquet to entertain all the representatives. After the banquet, a Beijing opera and song and dance evening was held to celebrate the successful conclusion of the conference.

### 加强民兵建设发展生产巩固国防

1960-04-28
第1版()
专栏：

    全国民兵代表会议向全国亿万民兵发出豪迈号召
    加强民兵建设发展生产巩固国防
    大会胜利闭幕，通过了向党中央和毛主席致敬电、向解放军致敬电和致全国民兵倡议书
    据新华社27日讯　全国民兵代表会议今天下午胜利闭幕。这是一次大办民兵师的群英大会，也是一次加速我国社会主义建设的誓师大会。全体代表经过十天的会议，进一步明确了大办民兵师对加速我国经济建设和国防建设的伟大意义，树雄心，立大志，决心更高地举起社会主义建设总路线和毛泽东思想红旗，为迅速把我国建设成为具有现代工业、现代农业、现代科学文化和现代国防的伟大社会主义强国，作出更大的贡献。
    中共中央副主席朱德、林彪，总书记邓小平，中华人民共和国副主席宋庆龄、董必武等党和国家领导人，出席了今天的闭幕式。当他们登上主席台时，全场起立，掌声雷动。和他们一起登上主席台的，还有中共中央政治局委员林伯渠、彭真、罗荣桓、贺龙，书记处候补书记刘澜涛，国务院副总理罗瑞卿、习仲勋，国防委员会副主席程潜、张治中、傅作义，中共中央和国务院有关部门的负责人，各人民团体的负责人，中国人民解放军高级将领陈赓、张云逸、王树声、李克农、张爱萍、杨勇、傅钟、萧华、甘泗淇、李聚奎、刘亚楼、苏振华、陈士榘、傅秋涛、李达等，以及大会主席团的全体成员。
    中共中央副主席、国务院副总理兼国防部长林彪元帅在会上致闭幕词。
    在经久不息的掌声中，全体代表一致通过向党中央和毛主席的致敬电、向中国人民解放军致敬电和致全国民兵的倡议书。倡议书中向全国民兵发出豪迈的十大倡议：积极参加社会主义建设；积极参加技术革新和技术革命运动；积极参加爱国卫生运动；积极配合人民解放军巩固国防；积极维护社会治安，保护生产；积极学习军事技术；积极参加体育锻炼和国防体育活动；积极学习人民解放军的光荣传统；积极学习文化科学知识；积极学习毛主席的著作。倡议书号召全国民兵，和全国人民一道，在党中央和毛主席的英明领导下，鼓足更大的革命干劲，开展革命竞赛，在建设和保卫社会主义的伟大事业中，从胜利走向更大的胜利。
    大会在通过致全国民兵的倡议书后，一百个男女少先队员在乐声中登上主席台，代表北京市五十六万少先队员向来自祖国各个战线上的男女民兵英雄献词、献花。他们朗诵道：“你们响应毛主席大办民兵师的号召，人人拿起枪支，个个武装起来，一面学文练武，一面积极生产，六亿人民的意志，气势汹涌澎湃，让人民气壮山河，让敌人心惊胆战！”少先队的献词每朗诵一段，会场上就爆发出春雷般的掌声。接着，八个少先队员，抬着两个盛满鲜花的大花篮，走上主席台向英雄们献花。
    在雄壮的军乐声中，今天大会主持人国务院副总理、中国人民解放军总参谋长罗瑞卿大将宣布大会胜利闭幕。
    今天上午，中国人民解放军副总参谋长张爱萍上将和总政治部副主任甘泗淇上将，向全体代表讲了话。晚间，林彪元帅举行盛大宴会，招待全体代表。宴会结束后，举行了京剧歌舞晚会，欢庆大会胜利闭幕。
