Tongwen Printing Factory Announcement
    Our factory undertakes the printing of all letterpress-printed books, magazines, textbooks, lithographic books, forms, cigarette packaging, stocks, medals, finely crafted color maps, etc., and the engraving of seals. Our printing is exquisite, and our printing costs are low. We also produce copper plates. If any government agencies, schools, factories, or stores wish to have printing done, please contact Sihexing Wood Factory in Yetao, Wu'an, or Huafengxiang Store in Wu'an City. All are welcome.

### 同文印刷厂启事

1947-09-27
第1版()
专栏：

    同文印刷厂启事
    本厂承印一切铅印书籍、杂志、课本，石印图书、表格、纸烟包装、股票、奖章，精制套色地图等，刻图章，印刷精美，印费从廉，并代制铜版，如有各机关、学校、工厂、商店愿意付印者。请到武安冶陶四合兴木工厂或武安城华丰祥商店接洽，无任欢迎。
