Title: General Feng Yuxiang Reveals Lack of Freedom in Chiang-controlled Areas
Author:
Date: 1947-09-11
Page: 1

　　General Feng Yuxiang Reveals
  　Lack of Freedom in Chiang-controlled Areas
    Xinhua News Agency, Northern Shaanxi, September 9th: According to an Associated Press report from San Francisco: General Feng Yuxiang stated definitively at a YMCA gathering on the 7th, "Under the current rule of the Kuomintang government, there is no freedom in China. If you speak the truth, you risk imprisonment." Feng stated that Chiang Kai-shek sent him to "inspect" water conservancy in the United States for a year, but he has already received orders to remain in the United States for another year when the term expires.

### 冯玉祥将军揭露　蒋区无自由

1947-09-11
第1版()
专栏：

　　冯玉祥将军揭露
  　蒋区无自由
    【新华社陕北九日电】据美联社旧金山讯：冯玉祥将军七日于一青年会集会上肯定地说：“在现在国民党政府统治下，中国已没有了自由。你一讲出真理，就有入狱危险。”冯氏说：蒋介石派他来“考察”美国的水利，时间是一年，但是他已接到命令，当期满的时候再在美国停留一年。
