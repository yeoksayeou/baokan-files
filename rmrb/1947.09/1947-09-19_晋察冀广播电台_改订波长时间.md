Jin-Cha-Ji Border Region Radio Station
 Revised Broadcast Schedule

　　[Xinhua News Agency, Jin-Cha-Ji, 16th] This Xinhua Radio Station will change its wavelength starting today, and revise its schedule and programs. The new wavelength is 45.7 meters, 6570 kilocycles. Broadcasting will begin daily at 17:45 Shanghai time. From 17:45 to 18:00, there will be local news. From 18:00 to 21:00, programs from the Shaan-Gan-Ning Border Region Xinhua Radio Station will be rebroadcast. From 21:00 to 21:15, there will be local comprehensive news dispatches or editorials. From 21:15 to 21:30, broadcasts will be directed towards the KMT troops in North China. From 21:30 to 22:00, there will be recurring programs. The recurring program schedule is as follows: Mondays - "Voices of the People," Tuesdays - "Introduction to the Liberated Areas," Wednesdays - "Lectures on Philosophy by the Renowned Philosopher Ai Siqi," Thursdays and Fridays - "Introduction to the Chiang Kai-shek Bandit Gang," Saturdays - "Essays on Literature and Art," Sundays - "Weekly Review Report." The station's call sign remains "XGNC," and the prelude music is "The Blacksmith of Dawn."

### 晋察冀广播电台　改订波长时间

1947-09-19
第1版()
专栏：

　　晋察冀广播电台
  　改订波长时间
    【新华社晋察冀十六日电】此间新华广播电台，今日起改换波长，并重订时间和节目。新波长为四五点七公尺六五七零千周。每天上海时间十七点四十五分开始播送。四十五分至十八点为本区新闻，十八点至二十一点转播陕北新华广播电台节目，二十一点至二十一点十五分本区综合新闻通讯或专论，二十一点十五分至三十分播给华北蒋军，三十分至廿二点为轮回节目。轮回节目星期一为人民呼声，星期二介绍解放区，星期三名哲学家艾思奇哲学讲座，星期四、五蒋匪帮介绍，星期六文艺丛谈，星期日每周评论汇报。该台呼号仍为“ＸＧＮＣ”，前奏曲为“黎明铁匠。”
