The First Machine Factory of the Border Region Industry and Mining Department Announcement

Our factory undertakes the manufacture of spare parts and complete machines for factories and mines in the Liberated Areas, and has an electrical department specializing in the installation of various motors. We have specially hired skilled knife sharpeners from other areas, who are responsible for sharpening various knives. We strive to keep prices low and guarantee completion on schedule.

Recently, our factory has produced new-style drilling vehicles and a large number of machine parts, including bench vises, large and small taps, dies, etc., and has newly repaired several large and small horsepower machines. All goods are of good quality, durable, and low in cost. They are being sold at reduced prices. If you are interested, please visit the cooperative in Xiazhuang Village, Ninth District of Wu'an to negotiate.

### 边区工矿处第一机器厂启事

1947-09-22
第1版()
专栏：

    边区工矿处
    第一机器厂启事
    敝厂承造解放区各工厂、矿山零整机件，且附设电气部专门承装各种电机。特由其他区聘来刀错名师，承剁各种错刀，定货力求低廉，保证按期完成。
    厂近来造出新式钻车和大批机件，有老虎钳、大小丝锥、板牙等等，并新修成大小马力机数部，各货均质地良好，坚固耐用，成本低廉，减价出售，如蒙惠顾，请驾临武安九区夏庄村合作社接洽。
