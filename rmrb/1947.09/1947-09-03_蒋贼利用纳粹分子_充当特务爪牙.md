Title: Traitor Chiang Uses Nazi Elements as Special Agent Henchmen
Author:
Date: 1947-09-03
Page: 1

　　Chiang Kai-shek's Clique Utilizes Nazi Elements
  　as Special Agent Henchmen
    (Xinhua News Agency, Northern Shaanxi, 1st) The Chiang government recently clamored about "repatriating German nationals," but continues to retain Nazi elements to participate in its special agent organs. According to a TASS Shanghai dispatch on the 28th, citing a Beiping newspaper's report from authoritative sources, the former head of the North China special agent organization, a German named Sulzer, suddenly "disappeared" on August 26th. He had worked in the secret special agent organization of the Chinese (Chiang government), and was regarded as one of the most dangerous Nazi elements who has not yet been repatriated. This Hitlerite has been sent to the interior, and the official has not explained his whereabouts.

### 蒋贼利用纳粹分子　充当特务爪牙

1947-09-03
第1版()
专栏：

　　蒋贼利用纳粹分子
  　充当特务爪牙
    【新华社陕北一日电】蒋政府最近扬言“遣送德侨”，但仍继续留用纳粹分子参加他的特务机关。据塔斯社沪二十八日电引北平报纸载权威人士消息称：前华北特务组织首领德人苏尔兹，于八月二十六日突告“失踪”，他曾在中国（蒋政府）的秘密特务组织工作，被目为迄今仍未遣送出境的最危险的纳粹分子之一。此一希特勒分子已被送往内地，其行踪官方未予说明。
