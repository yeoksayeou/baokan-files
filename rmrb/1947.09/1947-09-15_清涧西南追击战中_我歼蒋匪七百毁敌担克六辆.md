Title: In the Pursuit Battle Southwest of Qingjian, We Annihilated 700 Chiang Kai-shek Bandits and Destroyed 6 Enemy Tanks
Author:
Date: 1947-09-15
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Northwest, 12th: The Northwest People's Liberation Army, following up and fiercely attacking the enemy fleeing south in disarray from the Suide area, destroyed six tanks of Hu Zongnan's tank company (reportedly the company had a total of nine tanks), the only one on the Northwest battlefield, and 25 American-made trucks in the southwest of Qingjian on the 9th. More than 300 Hu troops were killed or wounded, and more than 200 enemies were captured, including important officials whose names are yet to be determined. Another unit of the People's Liberation Army captured more than 140 Hu troops alive and killed or wounded dozens of them at Jiuli Mountain, south of Suide, on the 1st. The main spoils of the two battles included one 60mm mortar and 37 machine guns. In other news: The local armed forces of the Guanzhong Sub-district of the People's Liberation Army routed the 3rd Battalion of the 5th Regiment of the Kuomintang Army's Provisional 3rd Brigade, which had invaded from Yi County, on the 7th, capturing Battalion Commander Yang Yongren and 80 others alive, and killing or wounding 70 others including the company commander. Three 60mm mortars, one mortar, and 16 light and heavy machine guns were captured.

Xinhua News Agency, Northeast, 13th: Delayed news: A unit of the Hebei-Rehe-Chahar People's Liberation Army fiercely attacked a battalion of the 10th Brigade of the Provisional 3rd Army of the Kuomintang Army that invaded from Yongning, east of Yanqing, Chahar Province, near Yongning on August 26th. After two hours of fierce fighting, more than 180 enemy soldiers, including the battalion commander, were killed or wounded, and a number of weapons were captured. In addition, the local armed forces of eastern Hebei captured the Chiang Kai-shek bandit stronghold of Chenjialinzhuang, south of Tianjin, on the 22nd of last month. At 4 p.m. on the same day, another unit attacked more than 300 Chiang Kai-shek puppet troops of the 14th Division and reactionary landlord armed forces in Zhuangzizhen, killing or wounding more than a dozen enemies and capturing 16 puppet police officers and others. Furthermore, a unit of the local armed forces of Liaobei crossed the Liao River eastward, penetrated the western side of the northern section of the Siping-Shenyang Railway, and protected the masses in carrying out land reform. On the 15th of last month, they attacked a unit of the Chiang Kai-shek puppet cavalry 1st Division near Jintun, west of Changtu, killing or wounding more than 30 enemies, capturing 13, and removing the strongholds of Xishaoguo and Litezhong around Jintun.

### 清涧西南追击战中　我歼蒋匪七百毁敌担克六辆

1947-09-15
第1版()
专栏：

　　清涧西南追击战中
  　我歼蒋匪七百毁敌担克六辆
    【新华社西北十二日电】西北人民解放军跟踪痛击由绥德一带狼狈南逃之敌，九日在清涧西南，击毁胡宗南在西北战场上仅有之坦克连的坦克六辆（据悉该连共有坦克九辆），及美造大卡车二十五辆，毙伤胡军三百余，俘敌二百余，其中有重要官员，姓名待查。另部解放军于一日在绥德南之九里山，生俘胡军一百四十余人，并毙伤其数十人。两役主要胜利品有六○炮一门，机枪三十七挺。另息：关中分区解放军地方武装，于七日击溃由邑出犯之蒋军暂三旅第五团之第三营，生俘营长杨勇仁以下八十余人，另毙伤其连长以下七十余人，缴获六○炮三门，迫击炮一门，轻重机枪十六挺。
    【新华社东北十三日电】迟到消息：冀热察解放军一部，八月廿六日在察省延庆以东永宁附近，痛击自永宁出犯之蒋军暂三军十旅之一个营，激战二小时，毙伤敌营长以下一百八十余名，缴获武器一部。另冀东地方武装上月廿二日攻克天津以南之陈家林庄蒋匪据点，同日下午四时另部袭击桩子镇之蒋伪军一四师一个连及反动地主武装等三百余名，毙伤敌十余名，俘伪警官以下十六名。又辽北地方武装某部，东渡辽河，深入四（平）沈（阳）路北段西侧，掩护群众进行土地改革，上月十五日袭击驻昌图以西金家屯附近之蒋伪骑兵第一师一部，毙伤敌三十余名，俘十三名，并拔除金家屯周围之西烧锅、利特中两据点。
