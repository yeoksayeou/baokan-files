Title: Jilu Yu Local Armed Forces Launch Full-Scale Offensive, Coordinating Counter-Offensive to Annihilate 4,000 Enemies
Author:
Date: 1947-09-14
Page: 2

　　Xinhua News Agency, Jinji Luyu, 12th. The local armed forces in the entire Jilu Yu region have launched a full-scale offensive, bravely and flexibly engaging in blocking and intercepting the enemy in the areas of Yuncheng, Dingtao, Jinxiang, and Juye, successfully completing the glorious task of containing the enemy and coordinating with the Liu-Deng-Xu-Li army's southward advance. In the forty days since July, a total of 4,043 Chiang Kai-shek troops have been annihilated, including 3,601 captured and 442 killed or wounded (in addition to the more than 4,000 bandits and landlords captured by cooperating with the militia and the masses in mopping-up and interception operations, which are not included in the statistics). The number of weapons captured: 11 mortars, 21 heavy machine guns, 78 light machine guns, 17 small cannons, 1,552 rifles and pistols, more than 268,000 rounds of ammunition, more than 7,300 rounds of various types of shells, and 11 destroyed vehicles. We only suffered 107 wounded and 24 killed.

### 冀鲁豫地方武装全面出击　配合反攻歼敌四千

1947-09-14
第2版()
专栏：

　　冀鲁豫地方武装全面出击
  　配合反攻歼敌四千
    【新华社晋冀鲁豫十二日电】冀鲁豫全区地方武装全面出击，在郓城、定陶、金乡、钜野地区灵活勇敢的向敌人展开堵击、截击战，胜利的完成牵制敌人配合刘邓徐李大军南下的光荣任务。自七月起四十天中，共歼蒋军四千零四十三名，内俘三千六百零一名，毙伤四百四十二名（另协同民兵群众清剿截击俘虏还乡团四千余未统计在内），缴迫击炮十一门、重机枪二十一挺、轻机枪七十八挺、小炮十七门、长短枪一千五百五十二支、子弹二十六万八千余发、各种炮弹七千三百余发、打毁汽车十一辆，我只伤一百零七名，牺牲二十四名。
