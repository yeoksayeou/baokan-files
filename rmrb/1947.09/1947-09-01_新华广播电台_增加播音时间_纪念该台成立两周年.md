Title: Xinhua Broadcasting Station Increases Broadcasting Time to Commemorate the Second Anniversary of its Founding
Author:
Date: 1947-09-01
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Northern Shaanxi, 30th: The Xinhua Broadcasting Station of Northern Shaanxi will increase broadcasting time by one hour from September 5th, the second anniversary of its founding. The new program schedule is as follows:

　　18:00 - 18:30: Introductions and letters from KMT officers who have laid down their arms.
　　18:30 - 19:00: News
　　19:00 - 19:20: Commentary (including weekly military reviews and international reviews)
　　19:20 - 19:45: Correspondences, stories, comprehensive reports, etc. (Every Sunday will feature a special "Sunday Arts and Literature" program, introducing works, folk songs, and artistic activities from the liberated areas.)
　　19:45 - 20:00: Concise News
　　20:00 - 20:40: Newsreel
　　20:40 - 21:00: English News

　　The station's call sign is XNCR, wavelength 40 meters, 7500 kilocycles; simultaneously rebroadcast by Handan Broadcasting Station, wavelength 49.2 meters, 6096 kilocycles; and rebroadcast by Jin-Cha-Ji Broadcasting Station, wavelength 35 meters, 8660 kilocycles.

### 新华广播电台　增加播音时间　纪念该台成立两周年

1947-09-01
第1版()
专栏：

　　新华广播电台
  　增加播音时间
  　纪念该台成立两周年
    【新华社陕北三十日电】陕北新华广播电台从九月五日该台成立两周年纪念日起，增加播音时间一小时。新节目如下：
    一八·○○——一八·三○放下武器蒋军军官介绍及书信等
    一八·三○——一九·○○新闻
    一九·○○——一九·二○评论（包括每周军事述评及国际述评）
    一九·二○——一九·四五通讯、故事、综合报导等（每星期日特设“星期文艺”，介绍解放区作品、歌谣及文艺活动等）
    一九·四五——二○·○○简明新闻
    二○·○○——二○·四○记录新闻
    二○·四○——二一·○○英语新闻
    该台呼号ＸＮＣＲ，波长四○公尺，七五○○千周；同时由邯郸广播电台转播，波长四九·二公尺，六○九六千周；并由晋察冀广播电台转播，波长三五公尺，八六六○千周。
