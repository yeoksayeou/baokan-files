	Tongwen Printing Factory Announcement
	Our factory undertakes the printing of all letterpress books, magazines, textbooks, lithographic books, forms, cigarette packaging, stocks, medals, and the fine production of color maps, engraving seals. The printing is exquisite, and the printing costs are low. We also produce copper plates. If any government agencies, schools, factories, or stores wish to have printing done, please contact Sihe Xing Wood Factory in Yetao, Wu'an or Huafengxiang Store in Wu'an City. All are welcome.

### 同文印刷厂启事

1947-09-22
第1版()
专栏：

    同文印刷厂启事
    本厂承印一切铅印书籍、杂志、课本，石印图书、表格、纸烟包装、股票、奖章，精制套色地图等，刻图章，印刷精美，印费从廉，并代制铜版，如有各机关、学校、工厂、商店愿意付印者。请到武安冶陶四合兴木工厂或武安城华丰祥商店接洽，无任欢迎。
