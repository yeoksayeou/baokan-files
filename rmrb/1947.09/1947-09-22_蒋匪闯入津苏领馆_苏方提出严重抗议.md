Title: Chiang Kai-shek Bandits Break Into Tianjin Soviet Consulate; Soviet Side Issues Strong Protest
Author:
Date: 1947-09-22
Page: 1

    Xinhua News Agency, Northern Shaanxi, 15th. Chiang Kai-shek's armed soldiers, numbering about one hundred, broke into the Soviet Consulate in Tianjin, destroyed the flowers and plants in the building, and attempted to search it. The Soviet side has issued a strong protest. According to Reuters and Associated Press dispatches from Beijing and Tianjin: Approximately one hundred Chinese (Chiang) armed soldiers broke into the Soviet Consulate courtyard on the 13th, aimed pistols at the gatekeeper, coerced him to hand over the gate keys, and attempted to beat him. After the Soviet Consul-General came forward and formally refused, the soldiers destroyed the flowers and plants in the courtyard and fled. Most of the soldiers did not wear insignia and did not explain the reason for the search. The Soviet Consul-General, Kutonkov, has lodged a strong protest with the Tianjin office of the Chinese (Chiang) Ministry of Foreign Affairs.

### 蒋匪闯入津苏领馆  苏方提出严重抗议

1947-09-22
第1版()
专栏：

    蒋匪闯入津苏领馆
    苏方提出严重抗议
    【新华社陕北十五日电】天津苏联领事馆被蒋家武装士兵约百名闯入，捣毁馆中花草并企图加以搜查，苏方已提出严重抗议。据路透、合众社平津电讯报导称：中国（蒋）武装士兵约百人，十三日闯入苏联领事馆院内，以手枪瞄准守门人，胁迫交出大门钥匙，并企图加以殴击，当苏联总领事出面正式拒绝后，士兵即捣毁院中花草遁去。士兵大部未佩有徽章，对搜查原因亦未加以说明。苏联总领事库顿科夫对此，已向中国（蒋）外交部天津办事处提出严重抗议。
