　　Important Announcement from Northern University

　　The following supplementary notice is hereby issued regarding matters that new students should pay attention to concerning application and admission:

　　I. Required qualifications for applicants:

　　1. Graduates from junior high school or above, or university graduates.

　　2. Those with outstanding self-taught academic abilities or excellent on-the-job learning performance, equivalent to junior high school graduation or above.

　　3. Those who have worked for two years or more in specialized business departments with the same nature as the college they are applying to.

　　II. All those sent by schools and institutions from the Liberated Areas to study at our university must bring money or send detailed materials such as appraisal letters and autobiographies by registered mail in order to understand their education.

　　III. All those whose families are in the Liberated Areas or who have studied and worked in schools and institutions in the Liberated Areas must bring bedding and clothing, and it is hoped that the institutions and schools will issue supply certificates; otherwise, no supplements will be provided.

　　Please be informed to avoid unnecessary travel.

　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　Principal Fan Wenlan

### 北方大学重要启事

1947-09-07
第1版()
专栏：

　　北方大学重要启事
    兹将有关新生投考及入学应行注意事项补充通告如下：
    一、投考学生必备条件：
    １、初中以上学校毕业或大学毕业者。
    ２、自修学力优异或在职学习成绩优良，相当初中毕业以上程度者。
    ３、在与所考学院性质相同之专门业务部门，曾经工作二年以上者。
    二、凡自解放区学校、机关送来本校学习者，均须携钱或挂号寄来鉴定书、自传等详细材料，以便了解教育。
    三、凡家在解放区或曾在解放区之学校、机关学习工作者均须携带衣被，并希机关学校发予供给证，否则概不补充。
    希即知照，以免徒劳往返。
    　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　校长范文澜
