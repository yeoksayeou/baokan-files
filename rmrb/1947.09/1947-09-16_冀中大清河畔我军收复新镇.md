Title: Our Army Recovers Xin Town on the Banks of the Daqing River in Central Hebei
Author:
Date: 1947-09-16
Page: 1

　　Xinhua News Agency, Jin-Cha-Ji, 14th: A unit of the Central Hebei People's Liberation Army recovered Xin Town county seat on the banks of the Daqing River and its surrounding Jiang bandit strongholds on the 9th. The two regiments of the 94th Division of the Jiang bandit 16th Army, which invaded and occupied the county in August, attacked from the county seat and Mawutou, 40 li southwest of the city, towards the south bank of the Daqing River. They were met with a head-on blow by a unit of the People's Liberation Army. After a fierce battle until 4 pm, the bandits were unable to withstand the attack and collapsed. The unit took advantage of the victory to pursue the bandits, approaching the county seat and Mawutou. That night, the bandits fled in panic. The unit then recovered the county seat and the surrounding bandit strongholds of Mawutou, Kaikou, Hongcheng, and Mao'er at dawn on the 9th. In this battle, more than 300 Jiang bandits were killed or wounded, and more than 100 bandit corpses were left behind. Xin Town was captured by the Jiang bandits during their large-scale invasion of the north of the Daqing River on August 7th.

### 冀中大清河畔我军收复新镇

1947-09-16
第1版()
专栏：

　　冀中大清河畔我军收复新镇
    【新华社晋察冀十四日电】冀中解放军一部，九日收复大清河畔之新镇县城及其外围蒋匪各据点。八月侵踞该县之蒋匪十六军九四师之两个团，自县城及城西南四十里之马务头两地向大清河南岸出犯，当遭解放军某部迎头痛击，激战至下午四时，匪不支溃退，该部乘胜追击，进迫县城及马务头等地。是夜匪就狠狈逃窜，该部遂于九日拂晓收复县城及其外围之马务头、开口、洪城、毛儿等匪据点。是役共毙伤蒋匪三百余，匪遗尸达百余具。按新镇系于八月七日蒋匪大举窜犯大清河北时所侵陷。
