Title: Military Region Announces Battle Results for July and August: 22,000 Enemies Eliminated
Author:
Date: 1947-09-15
Page: 1

　　Military Region Announces Battle Results for July and August
  　Eliminated 22,000 Enemies
    [Special Dispatch from This Newspaper] The Jin-Ji-Lu-Yu Military Region Headquarters of the People's Liberation Army recently issued its Eighth Operational Communiqué, summarizing the battle results of our military region's corps and various field columns in July and August:
    (1) Conducted 642 battles of various sizes.
    (2) Captured and once recovered seven county towns: Bo'ai, Mengxian, Qinyang, Xiuwu, Wuzhi (i.e., Mulandian), Xiangling, and Jiexian.
    (3) Eliminated a total of 22,089 Chiang Kai-shek's regular army and miscellaneous stubborn local armed forces (including 1,623 Chiang Kai-shek's regular army killed or wounded, 5,038 Chiang Kai-shek's regular army captured, 6,087 miscellaneous stubborn local armed forces killed or wounded, and 9,298 miscellaneous stubborn local armed forces captured).
    (4) Captured 7,234 various rifles and carbines, and 2,369 various pistols.
    (5) Captured 277 light machine guns, 46 heavy machine guns, 56 American-made carbine submachine guns, 127 Thompson submachine guns, 63 general submachine guns, and 59 assault rifles.
    (6) Captured 2 field guns, 6 anti-tank guns, 26 mortars, 4 rocket launchers, 21 60mm mortars, 32 small cannons, and 66 grenade launchers.
    (7) Captured 19 radio stations, 83 telephone sets, and over 10,000 catties of various electrical wires.
    (8) Captured 10 automobiles and 492 head of livestock.
    (9) Captured 16.38 million Chiang currency and over 40,000 catties of salt.
    (10) Shot down one American-made Chiang Kai-shek aircraft.
    (11) Many other supplies were captured.

### 军区公布七八两月战果　歼敌两万二千

1947-09-15
第1版()
专栏：

　　军区公布七八两月战果
  　歼敌两万二千
    【本报特讯】人民解放军晋冀鲁豫军区司令部顷发表第八号作战公报，综合七、八两月我军区兵团及各野战纵队之战果：
    （一）进行大小战斗六百四十二次。
    （二）攻克与一度收复县城七座——博爱、孟县、沁阳、修武、武陟（即木栾店）、襄陵、解县。
    （三）共歼蒋正规军与杂顽地方兵团两万两千零八十九人（内毙伤蒋正规军一千六百二十三人，俘蒋正规军五千零卅八人，毙伤杂顽地方兵团六千零八十七人，俘杂顽地方兵团九千二百九十八人）。
    （四）缴获各种步马枪七千二百三十四支，各种短枪二千三百六十九支。
    （五）缴获轻机关枪二百七十七挺，重机关枪四十六挺，美式卡宾手提机枪五十六架，汤姆斯手提机枪一百二十七架，一般手提机枪六十三架，冲锋机枪五十九架。
    （六）缴获野炮两门，战防炮六门，迫击炮二十六门，火箭筒四个，六零小炮二十一门，小门三十二门，掷弹筒六十六个。
    （七）缴获电台十九部，电话机八十三付，各种电线万余斤。
    （八）缴获汽车十辆，牲口四百九十二头。
    （九）缴获蒋币一千六百三十八万元，食盐四万余斤。
    （一○）击落美制蒋机一架。
    （一一）其他物资甚多。
