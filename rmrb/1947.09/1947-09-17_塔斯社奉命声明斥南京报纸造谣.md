Title: TASS is Instructed to Issue a Statement Denouncing Nanjing Newspaper for Spreading Rumors
Author:
Date: 1947-09-17
Page: 1

　　Xinhua News Agency, Northern Shaanxi, 14th. A dispatch from TASS, Moscow, 13th, stated: On September 9th, the Nanjing newspaper "China Tribune" published a piece of news claiming that in 1946, Soviet representatives and CCP representatives held a meeting in Harbin, which resulted in the conclusion of an agreement on Soviet aid to the CCP. TASS is now instructed to declare that this piece of news in the "China Tribune" is a complete fabrication.

### 塔斯社奉命声明斥南京报纸造谣

1947-09-17
第1版()
专栏：

　　塔斯社奉命声明斥南京报纸造谣
    【新华社陕北十四日电】据塔斯社莫斯科十三日电称：九月九日南京报纸“中国评论日报”发表一项消息，谓一九四六年苏联代表与中共代表曾在哈尔滨举行会议，结果缔结苏联援助中共之协定。塔斯社兹奉命声明中国评论日报之此项消息是彻头彻尾的捏造。
