Title: Victory in Recapturing Zhengding: Our Army Annihilates 2,000 Enemies
Author:
Date: 1947-09-06
Page: 1

　　Victory in Recapturing Zhengding
  　Our Army Annihilates 2,000 Enemies
    [Xinhua News Agency, Jin-Cha-Ji, 4th] The Jin-Cha-Ji People's Liberation Army's victory in recapturing Zhengding on August 24th has now been verified. The results include 1,877 enemies killed, wounded, or captured, including the live capture of 1,331 people including puppet battalion commander Zhao Ziyun and Chiang Kai-shek's Zhengding County Chief Wang Haidong. Two mortars, 24 light machine guns, and 944 rifles and pistols were seized.

### 收复正定之役　我军歼敌二千

1947-09-06
第1版()
专栏：

　　收复正定之役
  　我军歼敌二千
    【新华社晋察冀四日电】晋察冀解放军八月二十四日收复正定之役，战果兹已查明，计毙伤俘一千八百七十七名，内生俘伪大队长赵子云、蒋记正定县长王海东以下一千三百三十一名。缴迫击炮二门，轻机枪二十四挺，长短枪九百四十四支。
