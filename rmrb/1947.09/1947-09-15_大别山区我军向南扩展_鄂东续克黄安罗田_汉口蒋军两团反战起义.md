Title: Dabie Mountain Area: Our Army Expands Southward; Eastern Hubei Continues to Capture Huang'an and Luotian; Two Regiments of Chiang's Army in Hankou Stage Anti-War Uprising
Author:
Date: 1947-09-15
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, Central Plains Front, 10th (delayed dispatch) - The Liu-Deng-Xu-Li army continued to capture the two cities of Huang'an and Luotian in eastern Hubei. On the 4th, a certain unit of the People's Liberation Army captured Huang'an County, and the defending enemy fled in panic. They captured a part of the county's security team and their weapons, and captured a deputy battalion commander of a certain regiment of the Chiang army's 40th Division. A repair shop was seized, along with more than a thousand rifles and other military supplies. Another unit of the People's Liberation Army captured Luotian County on the 6th, and the results of the battle are being tallied.
Xinhua News Agency, Northern Shaanxi, 13th - According to a report from Nanjing by the French News Agency on the 12th, citing people from Hankou arriving in Nanjing: When the vanguard of the People's Liberation Army's Liu-Deng army reached 40 miles near Hankou, two regiments of a certain unit of Chiang's army in Hankou had already staged an uprising and joined the People's Liberation Army.

### 大别山区我军向南扩展　鄂东续克黄安罗田　汉口蒋军两团反战起义

1947-09-15
第1版()
专栏：

　　大别山区我军向南扩展
  　鄂东续克黄安罗田
  　汉口蒋军两团反战起义
    【新华社中原前线十日电】（迟到）刘邓徐李大军续克鄂东之黄安、罗田两城。四日，解放军某部攻克黄安县城，守敌闻风逃窜，生俘该县保安队人枪一部，并俘蒋军四十师某营副营长一名。缴获修械所一座，步马枪千余支，其他军用品甚多。解放军另一部，于六日攻克罗田县城，战果清查中。
    【新华社陕北十三日电】据十二日法国新闻社南京电引汉口到南京人士谈话透露：当人民解放军刘邓大军先遣部队抵达汉口附近四十英里处时，汉口蒋军某部两个团已举行起义，投入解放军。
