Title: Our Army Withdraws from Yancheng
Author:
Date: 1947-09-15
Page: 1

　　Our Army Withdraws from Yancheng
    [Xinhua News Agency, Central China, 13th] Delayed news: The Central China People's Liberation Army, which captured Yeting City (Yancheng) on the 12th of last month, actively withdrew from the city on the 21st after completing the task of annihilating the enemy and clearing all Chiang Kai-shek puppet organizations from the city.

### 我军撤离盐城

1947-09-15
第1版()
专栏：

　　我军撤离盐城
    【新华社华中十三日电】迟到消息：上月十二日攻克叶挺城（盐城）之华中人民解放军于完成歼敌任务并扫除该城一切蒋伪组织后，于二十一日主动撤离该城。
