　　Important Announcement from Northern University

　　The following supplementary notice is hereby given regarding the application and enrollment procedures for new students:

　　I. Essential qualifications for applicants:

　　1. Graduates from junior high school or above, or students who have not completed university studies.
　　2. Those with outstanding self-study abilities or excellent on-the-job learning performance, equivalent to or above junior high school graduation level.
　　3. Those who have worked for two years or more in specialized business departments with the same nature as the college they are applying to.

　　II. Those sent by schools or organizations in the Liberated Areas to study at our university must bring or send by registered mail detailed materials such as appraisal letters and autobiographies in order to understand their education.

　　III. Those whose families are in the Liberated Areas or who have studied and worked in schools or organizations in the Liberated Areas must bring clothing and bedding, and it is hoped that the organizations or schools will issue supply certificates, otherwise no supplements will be provided.

　　Please be informed so as to avoid futile trips.

　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　President Fan Wenlan

### 北方大学重要启事
范文澜
1947-09-13
第1版()
专栏：

　　北方大学重要启事
    兹将有关新生投考及入学应行注意事项补充通告如下：
    一、投考学生必备条件：
    １、初中以上学校毕业或大学肄业者。
    ２、自修学力优异或在职学习成绩优良，相当初中毕业以上程度者。
    ３、在与所考学院性质相同之专门业务部门，曾经工作二年以上者。
    二、凡自解放区学校、机关送来本校学习者，均须携带或挂号寄来鉴定书、自传等详细材料，以便了解教育。
    三、凡家在解放区或曾在解放区之学校、机关学习工作者均须携带衣被，并希机关学校发予供给证，否则概不补充。
    希即知照，以免徒劳往返。
    　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　校长范文澜
