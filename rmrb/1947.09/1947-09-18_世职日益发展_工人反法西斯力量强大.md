Title: World Federation of Trade Unions Grows Daily; Workers' Anti-Fascist Forces Strengthen

Author:

Date: 1947-09-18
Page: 3

　　World Federation of Trade Unions Grows Daily
  　Workers' Anti-Fascist Forces Strengthen
    【Xinhua News Agency, Northern Shaanxi, 14th】 Moscow dispatch: Saillant, Secretary-General of the World Federation of Trade Unions, who recently visited the Soviet Union, told TASS reporters on the 10th that since its establishment two years ago, the Federation has been growing daily and now has 70 million members. In addition, the German Trade Union with 500,000 members has also decided to join. The current focus of work is to strengthen the anti-fascist struggle of trade unions in various countries, demanding that the Greek government restore freedom, democracy, and trade union rights in Greece, calling for the establishment of a democratic system in Spain, and proposing that UN Secretary-General Lie arbitrate the war between the Netherlands and Indonesia.

### 世职日益发展　工人反法西斯力量强大

1947-09-18
第3版()
专栏：

　　世职日益发展
  　工人反法西斯力量强大
    【新华社陕北十四日电】莫斯科讯：最近赴苏之世界职工联盟总书记赛南，十日告塔斯社记者称：职工联盟成立两年以来日益发展，现已拥有七千万盟员，还有德国职工会五十万会员，亦已决定入盟。目前工作的中心，在加强各国职工会的反法西斯斗争，要求希腊政府恢复希腊的自由民主及职工会的权利，呼吁在西班牙建立民主制度，建议由联合国秘书长莱氏仲裁荷兰与印尼的战争。
