Title: Communication Exchange No. 27
Author:
Date: 1947-09-18
Page: 1

　　Communication Exchange
  　No. 27
    To all organs, factories, school committees and correspondent comrades directly under the Central Committee, the Border Region, the Military, and the Finance Office!
    The Three Check-ups Movement has been widely launched in all organs. Please seize the opportunity to reflect the situation and experience of this movement. In addition to reflecting your learning progress and leadership experience in each period, please also gather typical reflection materials, thoughts of various types of people, and different views on the land reform movement, and write to us.
　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　The Editorial Liaison Section of this Newspaper

### 通讯往来　第二十七号

1947-09-18
第1版()
专栏：

　　通讯往来
  　第二十七号
    中直、边直、军直及财办各机关各工厂学委会及通讯员同志！
    三查运动已在各机关普遍展开，请你们抓紧反映这一运动的情形与经验。除了反映你们每个时期学习进度与领导经验外，并希将典型反省材料，各种类型人物的思想以及对土改运动不同立场的各种看法等汇集起来，写给我们。
　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　本报通联科
