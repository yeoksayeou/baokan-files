Title: Loss Notices
Author: Nie Xin Xian, Fan Dafang
Date: 1947-09-26
Page: 1

Loss Notices
(Lost in Xingtai on September 7th)

    I, Nie Xinxian, carelessly lost the 28th Active Duty Honor Certificate issued by the Administrative Office. In addition to applying to the health authority for a replacement, I hereby declare that anyone who obtains this certificate should consider it void.                 Nie Xinxian

    I, Fan Dafang, carelessly lost a leather bag in Shexian City on August 26th. It contained a letter from Bao Yishan of the Border Region Government and six blank slips from the Public Security Bureau Third Branch; six blank slips from Deji Goods Store, and over 20,000 Yuan of Jicha banknotes. I hereby publish this notice to declare the letters and blank slips invalid.  
          Deji Goods Store, Changzhi City   Fan Dafang

### 遗失声明
聂新现  范达芳
1947-09-26
第1版()
专栏：

    遗失声明
    （于九月七号邢台失掉）
    本人一时不慎，将行署发给第二十八号现役荣誉证失掉，除呈请卫生机关补发外，任何人得去此证作废，特此声明。            聂新现
    本人因一时不慎，于八月二十六日在涉县城内，遗失皮的包一个，内装边府包一山同志信一封与公安三分局空白条子六张；德记货栈空白条子六张，冀钞二万余元，特登报声明信件和空白条子作废。  
          长治市德记货栈   范达芳
