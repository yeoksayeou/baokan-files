Title: Soviet Union Controls Dalian According to Treaty, Rejects US Demand to "Open" the Port
Author:
Date: 1947-09-06
Page: 1

　　Soviet Union Controls Dalian According to Treaty
  　Rejects US Demand to "Open" the Port
    【Xinhua News Agency, Northern Shaanxi, 4th】Washington Dispatch: The Soviet Union has rejected the United States' demand of August 14th to "open" Dalian. According to a statement released by the US State Department on the 2nd, the Soviet Union stated in its reply to the US on August 26th: "According to the Sino-Soviet Treaty of August 14, 1945, Dalian should be governed by the political institutions established in the naval base of Lushun (Port Arthur) while the state of war with Japan exists. Currently, the state of war with Japan has not ended, and the peace treaty with Japan is yet to be signed. Therefore, the political institutions of Lushun can continue to govern Dalian."

### 苏联依约管制大连　拒绝美国“开放”该港要求

1947-09-06
第1版()
专栏：

　　苏联依约管制大连
  　拒绝美国“开放”该港要求
    【新华社陕北四日电】华盛顿讯：苏联已拒绝美国上月十四日“开放”大连的要求，据二日美国务院发表苏联于上月二十六日致美国复文中称：“按一九四五年八月十四日中苏条约规定，对日战争状态存在时，大连应受海军基地旅顺港所设政治机构的管制，目前对日战争状态尚未终止，对日和约尚待签订，故旅顺之政治机构自可继续管制大连。”
