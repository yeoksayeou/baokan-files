Title: Jinan Third Administrative Office Orders Cessation of Labor Service During Autumn Harvest
Author:
Date: 1947-09-18
Page: 2

　　Jinan Third Administrative Office Orders
  　Cessation of Labor Service During Autumn Harvest
    [Report from this newspaper's Taiyue correspondent, 16th] The Third Administrative District of the Jinan New Liberated Area and the Military Sub-district Command, in order to take care of the masses' autumn plowing and sowing, have promulgated a joint order stipulating that from September 10th to October 10th, labor service will be suspended for one month, so that the masses will have sufficient time to complete the autumn harvest and sowing.

### 晋南三专署下令　秋收停止支差

1947-09-18
第2版()
专栏：

　　晋南三专署下令
  　秋收停止支差
    【本报太岳十六日电】晋南新区第三专区与分区司令部，为了照顾群众秋耕秋种，特颁布联合命令，规定从九月十日起至十月十日止，停止支差一月，以便群众有充分时间完成秋收秋种。
