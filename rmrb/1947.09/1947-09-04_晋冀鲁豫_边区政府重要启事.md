Date: 1947-09-04

Jin-Ji-Lu-Yu
  Border Region Government Important Announcement

　　Recently, some individuals have been impersonating our government and forging official seals and passes, engaging in unscrupulous activities in × place, clearly intending to undermine the government's authority. In addition to being prosecuted according to the law, from now on, regardless of who it is, carrying what kind of documents, if there is any unclear behavior or suspicious conduct, they should be arrested and interrogated. It is hoped that military and political organs, militia sentry posts in all areas will pay close attention!

### 晋冀鲁豫　边区政府重要启事

1947-09-04
第1版()
专栏：

　　晋冀鲁豫
  　边区政府重要启事
    近有假冒本府，伪造本府长戳路条之分子，在×地胡作非为，显系故意破坏政府威信。除法办外，今后不论何人，带有何种证件，如有不清行为，或行迹可疑者，即应逮捕审讯。尚希各地军政机关民兵岗哨严密注意为荷！
    　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　九月一日
