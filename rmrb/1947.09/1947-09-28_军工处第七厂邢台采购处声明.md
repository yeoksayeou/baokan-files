Title: Military Industry Department No. 7 Factory, Xingtai Procurement Office Statement
Author:
Date: 1947-09-28
Page: 1

    Military Industry Department No. 7 Factory, Xingtai Procurement Office
    Statement

    This office, acting on orders to reduce its size, has decided to abolish the Handan and Zuoquan County Tongyu Town procurement stations. All official seals and stamps of the Handan and Tongyu procurement stations are void from this date.

    Drug traffickers and corrupt elements, Wang Zichun (from Shilidian Village, Wu'an), have been expelled and are now handed over to the government for investigation. Those with relevant procedures are requested to come for settlement in September; we will not be responsible for overdue matters. This is hereby declared.
    Address: No. 7, Dongyangshi Road, Nanguan, Xingtai. Manager: Zhao Yuzhai, Supervisor: Feng Chunwang

### 军工处第七厂邢台采购处声明

1947-09-28
第1版()
专栏：

    军工处第七厂邢台采购处
    声明
    本处奉命缩小机构，特决定取消邯郸及左权县桐峪镇采购站。邯郸、桐峪采购站的一切公章图记从即日作废。
    毒贩及贪污分子王子春（武安十里店村人）已被开除，现送交政府查办，并希有关手续者，在九月份前来清理，过期概不负责。特此声明。
    地址——邢台南关东羊市道门牌七号  经理赵玉斋、监委冯春旺
