Title: Central Bureau Sends Condolences to Xu Fanting's Family
Author:
Date: 1947-09-23
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Jinji-Luyu, 21st. The CCP Jinji-Luyu Central Bureau today sent a telegram of condolence to Comrade Xu Fanting's family. The original telegram is as follows:

To the family of Comrade Xu Fanting, please forward via the CCP Central Committee Jin Sui Branch:

At a time when the People's Liberation Army's victorious counter-offensive is inspiring all, we are shocked and grieved to hear of Comrade Fanting's passing. Fanting devoted himself to the cause of the Chinese national democratic revolution, fought bravely against traitors like Chiang Kai-shek and warlords like Yan Xishan, and spared no sacrifice for truth and the people. He feared no tyranny, was incorruptible and unyielding, and displayed a spirit of unwavering righteousness. His loyalty to the country is truly inspiring. Our tens of millions of Shanxi people will surely inherit Comrade Fanting's legacy, unite and advance, overthrow Chiang Kai-shek, and strive for the complete victory of the patriotic self-defense war. A special telegram is sent to express our condolences, and we hope you will take care of yourselves in your grief.

Xinhua News Agency, Jinsui, 21st. After the death of Comrade Xu Fanting, Director of the Jinsui Border Region Administrative Office, the military and civilians in the liberated areas deeply mourned. Telegrams of condolence were sent to his family from various places. Lin Boqu, He Long, Xi Zhongxun, and others sent special telegrams of condolence. The original telegram is as follows: To the Jinsui Border Region Administrative Office, forwarded to Madame Xu: We were shocked and saddened to learn of the death of Director Xu Fanting on the 12th of this month. Comrade Fanting was a pioneer of the revolution in our Northwest, leading the people of Jinsui to defeat the Japanese invaders and establish a democratic government. In recent years, he resolutely opposed Chiang's traitorous and dictatorial policies, displaying great righteousness and upholding the patriotic self-defense war, making outstanding contributions. At a time when the cause of liberation of the people in the Northwest is developing day by day, it is a great loss to our Northwest people that Comrade Fanting has unfortunately passed away. However, the spirit of Comrade Fanting will be immortal with the victory of the people's liberation cause! A special telegram is sent to express condolences and we hope you will take care of yourselves in your grief. Furthermore, the CCP Central Committee Jinsui Branch's telegram of condolence stated: "Our entire party in Jinsui is determined to inherit Comrade Fanting's legacy, better unite with the people of Jinsui, strengthen frontline combat, thoroughly implement land reform, develop and consolidate the Jinsui Liberated Area, and strive for the complete victory of the patriotic self-defense war."

### 中央局电唁续范亭家属

1947-09-23
第1版()
专栏：

    中央局电唁续范亭家属
    【新华社晋冀鲁豫二十一日电】中共晋冀鲁豫中央局今日电唁续范亭同志家属原电如下：
    中共中央晋绥分局请转范亭同志家属：
    正当人民解放军反攻捷报鼓舞全体之际；惊闻范亭同志病逝噩耗，曷胜悲悼。范亭一身致力于中国民族民主革命事业，与汉奸蒋介石、阎锡山等匪首奋勇战斗，为真理为人民不惜牺牲，不畏强暴，廉介不阿，百折不挠正义浩气，忠贞为国，可歌可泣，我数千万三晋人民子弟，必能继承范亭同志遗志，团结前进，打倒蒋介石，争取爱国自卫战争的彻底胜利，专电致唁，诸祈节哀。
    【新华社晋绥廿一日电】晋绥边区行政公署主任续范亭同志逝世后，解放区军民深为痛悼，各地纷纷致电家属吊唁，林柏渠、贺龙、习仲勋等特致电悼唁，原电如下：晋绥边区行署转续夫人礼鉴：噩耗传来，惊悉续主任范亭同志于本月十二日逝世，不胜哀悼！范亭同志为我西北革命先进，领导晋绥人民战胜日寇，建立民主政权，几年来坚决反对蒋贼卖国独裁政策，正义磅礴，坚持爱国自卫战，卓著勋劳。当此西北人民解放事业，日益发展之际，范亭同志竟不幸逝世，实为我西北人民之重大损失，但范亭同志的精神必随人民解放事业之胜利永垂不朽！特电唁问尚希节哀。又中共中央晋绥分局唁电称：“我晋绥全党决承继范亭同志遗志，更好的与晋绥人民团结，加强前线作战，彻底实行土地改革，发展与巩固晋绥解放区，为争取爱国自卫战争的彻底胜利而奋斗”。
