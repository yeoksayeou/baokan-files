Title: Handan Xinhua Radio Station Adds Local News Broadcasts
Author: 
Date: 1947-09-03
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Jinji-Luyu, 1st: The Handan Radio Station, starting from its first anniversary on September 1st, has innovated its programming and strengthened its local focus by adding fifty minutes of reporting on the liberated areas of this region. Starting September 5th, it will follow the Yan'an Xinhua Broadcasting Station by extending its broadcast time by one hour, shifting the broadcast time later by one hour. The new programs after "September 1st" are as follows:

　　7:00 to 8:30 AM – Exclusively broadcasting documentary news to the frontline troops of the People's Liberation Army;
　　5:00 to 5:30 PM – Reporting program, important news;
　　5:30 to 6:00 PM – Commentary, comprehensive reports, introductions of PLA officers, family letters and works;
　　6:00 to 8:00 PM – Rebroadcasting various programs from the Yan'an Xinhua Broadcasting Station (Starting September 5th, rebroadcasting until 9:00 PM);
　　8:00 to 8:30 PM – (Starting September 5th, 9:00 to 9:30 PM) Jinji-Luyu Liberated Area News;
　　8:30 to 8:50 PM – (Starting September 5th, 9:30 to 9:50 PM) Jinji-Luyu Liberated Area Reports;
　　8:50 to 9:00 PM – (Starting September 5th, 9:50 to 10:00 PM) Concise News.

　　The call sign is XGHT, wavelength is 49.2 meters, 60966 cycles.

### 邯郸新华广播电台　增加本区新闻广播

1947-09-03
第1版()
专栏：

　　邯郸新华广播电台
  　增加本区新闻广播
    【新华社晋冀鲁豫一日电】邯郸广播电台从九月一日该台一周年纪念日起，革新节目，加强地方性，增加五十分钟本解放区的报导。并从九月五日起，跟着陕北新华广播电台延长时间一小时，而移后播音时间一小时。“九一”以后的新节目如下：
    七点到八点半——专给人民解放军前线部队广播纪录新闻；
    十七点到十七点半——报告节目，重要新闻；
    十七点半到十八点——评论、综合报导、解放军官的介绍、家信和作品；
    十八点到二十点——转播陕北新华广播电台的各种节目（九月五日起，转播到二十一点为止）；
    二十点到二十点半——（九月五日起，二十一点到二十一点三十分）晋冀鲁豫解放区新闻；
    二十点半到二十点五十分——（九月五日起，二十一点半到二十一点五十分）晋冀鲁豫解放区报导；
    二十点五十分到二十一点——（九月五日起，二十一点五十分到二十二点）简明新闻。
    该号呼ＸＧＨＴ，波长四十九点二公尺，六○九六六周。
