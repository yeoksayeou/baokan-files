Title: Our Army Voluntarily Withdraws from Zhucheng
Author:
Date: 1947-09-16
Page: 1

　　Our Army Voluntarily Withdraws from Zhucheng
    [Xinhua News Agency, Shandong, 14th] Delayed news: The People's Liberation Army voluntarily withdrew from Zhucheng on the 8th. On the 9th, the Chiang Kai-shek troops, who had invaded the Binbei area from the eastern section of the Jiaoji Railway, occupied the city.

### 我军主动撤出诸城

1947-09-16
第1版()
专栏：

　　我军主动撤出诸城
    【新华社山东十四日电】迟到消息：解放军八日主动撤出诸城，九日胶济东段南犯滨北之蒋军侵占该城。
