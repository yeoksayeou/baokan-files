Title: Bohai Establishes Coastal Defense Headquarters to Launch Counteroffensive from the Sea

Author:

Date: 1947-09-19
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Shandong, 17th—As the People's Liberation Army launches a major counteroffensive, the Bohai Military Region Coastal Defense Headquarters of the People's Liberation Army in Shandong Province was established on the 5th. A grand celebration was held on the coast with ten thousand people to celebrate the founding of the navy. Commander Xiao addressed the assembly: "We must resolutely eliminate the Chiang bandit forces at sea, fight to defend the 700-li coastline of Bohai, and launch a counteroffensive from the sea."

### 渤海成立海防总队将从海面进行反攻

1947-09-19
第1版()
专栏：

　　渤海成立海防总队将从海面进行反攻
    【新华社山东十七日电】正当解放军大举反攻之际，山东渤海区人民解放军海防总队于五日宣告成立。并于海滨举行万人庆贺海军建军大典。肖司令员向全体号召：“我们要坚决消灭海上蒋匪，为保卫渤海七百里海岸线而战斗，并从海面上进行反攻。”
