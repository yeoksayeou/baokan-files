Title: Qingdao Factories Shut Down, 70,000 Workers Unemployed
Author:
Date: 1947-09-04
Page: 1

　　Qingdao Factories Shut Down
　　70,000 Workers Unemployed
　　【Xinhua News Agency, Northern Shaanxi, 2nd】 Tianjin Daily reports: Qingdao, known for its favorable industrial conditions, is facing an increasingly dire situation in various industries. The trend of factory shutdowns and closures is intensifying. On August 1st alone, 38 dyeing and weaving factories closed down. Even those that can barely stay afloat are mostly operating partially or intermittently. According to official statistics announced by the Social Affairs Bureau on August 24th, the number of unemployed workers in Qingdao has reached over 70,000.

### 青岛工厂停工歇业　失业工人已达七万

1947-09-04
第1版()
专栏：

　　青岛工厂停工歇业
  　失业工人已达七万
    【新华社陕北二日电】津报讯：素称具有良好工业条件之青岛，各种工业已日进绝境。工厂停工倒闭之风日烈，仅上月一日即有三十八家染织业工厂歇业，即能勉强支持者也多数是局部开工，或时开时停。据八月二十四日的记社会局宣布之官方统计：青市失业工人已达七万余人。
