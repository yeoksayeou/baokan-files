Title: Jin-Cha-Ji Military Attacks Tongpu Northern Section, Annihilates 1,500 Chiang-Yan Bandits; South of Yan'an, We Capture大小劳山 (Da Xiao Lao Shan)
Author:
Date: 1947-09-30
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Jin-Cha-Ji, 28th: A unit of the Jin-Cha-Ji People's Liberation Army actively launched an attack on the area north of Taiyuan and around Xin County and Dingxiang on the northern section of the Tongpu Railway from the 16th to the 18th of this month, successively capturing more than ten important villages and towns, including Zhicun, Nanhua, Yijing, Dongxiloucun, etc. According to preliminary statistics, a part of Yan's 39th Provisional Division and the entire pseudo-Dingxiang Police Corps, totaling more than 1,500 men, were annihilated. Among them, the acting pseudo-Dingxiang County Chief Liu Qingshan, the head of the National Soldiers Corps Wang Tongzhang, and 1,045 others were captured alive. The captured included 18 various artillery pieces, 44 light and heavy machine guns, and 498 rifles and carbines.

Xinhua News Agency, Northwest, 28th: The Northwest People's Liberation Army, in pursuit of the defeated remnants of Hu’s army fleeing south, captured the strategically important military location of大小劳山 (Da Xiao Lao Shan) and Sujiahe, more than 30 kilometers south of Yan'an on the Xian-Yan'an Highway, on the 22nd, annihilating the entire 2nd Company Headquarters of the Engineering Battalion of the enemy's 17th Division and seizing a truck full of flour.

### 晋察冀我军出击同蒲北段  歼灭蒋阎匪千五百  延安南我克大小劳山

1947-09-30
第1版()
专栏：

    晋察冀我军出击同蒲北段
    歼灭蒋阎匪千五百
    延安南我克大小劳山
    【新华社晋察冀二十八日电】晋察冀人民解放军一部，于本月十六日至十八日，主动向太原以北、同蒲线北段之忻县，定襄周围出击，先后攻克智村、南化、义井、东西楼村等重要村镇十余处。据初步统计，歼阎军暂三十九师一部及伪定襄保警大队全部共一千五百余人。内生俘伪定襄代理县长刘清山、国民兵团大队长王同章以下一千零四十五人。缴获各种炮十八门，轻重机枪四十四挺，步马枪四百九十八枝。
    【新华社西北二十八日电】西北人民解放军跟踪追击南逃之胡军残败部队，二十二日一举攻克延安南三十余公里咸（阳）延（安）公路上之军事要地大小劳山及苏家河等地，歼灭敌十七师工兵营第二连部全部，缴获满载面粉之汽车一辆。
