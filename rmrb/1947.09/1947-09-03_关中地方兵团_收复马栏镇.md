Title: Guanzhong Local Army Corps Recovers Malan Town
Author:
Date: 1947-09-03
Page: 1

　　Guanzhong Local Army Corps

  　Recovers Malan Town
    Xinhua News Agency, Northwest, 30th. The Guanzhong Sub-district Liberation Army local corps recovered Malan Town, the former location of the democratic government's special commissioner's office, on the 24th. Currently, there are no enemy traces within the borders of Xinzheng and Xinning counties. Malan was occupied by Hu's army on February 20th of this year. Furthermore, on the 21st, over 200 of Hu's troops attempting to retreat south from the southeast area of Mizhi were intercepted by the Border Region Clothing Bureau's worker guerrilla team, resulting in 32 enemy killed, wounded, or captured, and the seizure of three Czech-style light machine guns and eleven rifles.

### 关中地方兵团　收复马栏镇

1947-09-03
第1版()
专栏：

　　关中地方兵团
  　收复马栏镇
    【新华社西北三十日电】关中分区解放军地方兵团二十四日收复民主政府前专署所在地之马栏镇。现新正、新宁两县境内已无敌踪。马栏系于本年二月二十日被胡军侵占。又二十一日企图由米脂东南地区向南溃退之胡军二百余人，遭我边区被服局工人游击队截击，计毙伤俘敌三十二名，缴获捷克式轻机枪三挺，步枪十一支。
