Title: Tibetan Visiting Group Leaves Beijing to Return to Tibet
Author:
Date: 1955-03-29
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, March 28th:

The entire Tibetan visiting group, led by Deputy Head Lha-gyari Namgyal Gyatso, left Beijing by train on the evening of the 28th to return to Tibet.

Those who went to the station to see them off included: Sa Kongliao, Vice Chairman of the Nationalities Affairs Commission of the People's Republic of China; Zhang Jingwu, Representative of the Central People's Government in Tibet; Tan Guansan, Political Commissar of the Tibet Military Region of the Chinese People's Liberation Army; officials from the Dalai Lama's Office in Beijing, officials from the Panchen Lama's Office in Beijing, and students from the Central Institute for Nationalities, totaling more than 60 people.

The Tibetan visiting group arrived in Beijing on September 29, 1954. After participating in the National Day celebrations of the Fifth National Day and visiting scenic spots and historical sites in the capital, they left Beijing on October 28 to visit various parts of the motherland. Over the past four months, they visited factories, schools, and scenic spots and historical sites in Shenyang, Anshan, Tianjin, Shanghai, Guangzhou, Wuhan, Xi'an, Lanzhou, and the Yanbian Korean Autonomous Prefecture in Jilin Province, and paid homage to monasteries such as Tar Monastery in Qinghai Province.

The great achievements of the motherland's various constructions and the enthusiastic welcome from the people of various regions deeply moved the members of the visiting group. Before their departure, they all expressed their determination to work hard to build Tibet after returning.

### 西藏参观团离京回藏

1955-03-29
第1版()
专栏：

    西藏参观团离京回藏
    【新华社二十八日讯】西藏参观团全体团员在副团长拉加利·朗吉嘉措率领下，二十八日晚乘车离北京返西藏。
    前往车站欢送的有：中华人民共和国民族事务委员会副主任萨空了，中央人民政府驻西藏代表张经武，中国人民解放军西藏军区政治委员谭冠三，达赖驻京办事处、班禅驻京办事处官员和中央民族学院学生等共六十多人。
    西藏参观团一九五四年九月二十九日到达北京。他们参加了第五届国庆节观礼和参观了首都名胜古迹后，在十月二十八日离京赴祖国各地参观。四个多月中，他们先后参观了沈阳、鞍山、天津、上海、广州、武汉、西安、兰州和吉林省延边朝鲜族自治区等地的工厂、学校和名胜古迹，朝拜了青海省塔尔寺等寺院。
    祖国各项建设的巨大成就和各地人民的热情欢迎，使参观团的团员非常感动。行前他们都表示回去后，要努力建设西藏。
