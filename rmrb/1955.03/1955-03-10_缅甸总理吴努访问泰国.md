Title: Burmese Prime Minister U Nu Visits Thailand
Author:
Date: 1955-03-10
Page: 4

    Xinhua News Agency, Bangkok, March 9th: Burmese Prime Minister U Nu arrived in Bangkok on the 7th to begin a week-long visit to Thailand. The accompanying officials included four ministers and four representatives of the Burmese armed forces.
    Prime Minister U Nu said at a press conference in Bangkok on the 8th that Burma would not participate in the Manila Treaty Organization, nor would it be possible to participate in the economic part of the Manila Treaty Organization. U Nu also expressed hope that the Asian-African Conference would be a successful conference.

### 缅甸总理吴努访问泰国

1955-03-10
第4版()
专栏：

    缅甸总理吴努访问泰国
    【新华社九日讯】曼谷消息：缅甸联邦总理吴努在七日到达曼谷，准备在泰国进行一星期的访问。随行的官员包括四个部长和四个缅甸武装部队的代表。
    吴努总理八日在曼谷记者招待会上说，缅甸不参加马尼拉条约组织，也不可能参加马尼拉条约组织的经济部分。吴努并表示对于亚非会议将是一个成功的会议抱有希望。
