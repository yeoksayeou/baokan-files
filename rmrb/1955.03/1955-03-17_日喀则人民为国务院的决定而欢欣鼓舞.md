Title: The People of Xigazê Cheer for the State Council's Decision
Author:
Date: 1955-03-17
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, Lhasa, 16th. The news of the State Council's decisions regarding the establishment of the Preparatory Committee for the Tibet Autonomous Region has spread throughout Xigazê. People from all walks of life in Xigazê are rejoicing at this happy news. Lhamin Yixi Chuchen, Director of the Panchen Kanpo Lhentsog Committee, said excitedly upon hearing the news: "On behalf of all the officials of the Kanpo Lhentsog Committee, I express my heartfelt gratitude to the Communist Party and Chairman Mao. The establishment of the Preparatory Committee for the Tibet Autonomous Region is the ardent desire of the Tibetan people. In the future, we will definitely work hard to build a new Tibet under the leadership of the State Council and the Preparatory Committee for the Autonomous Region." Upon hearing the news, Wang Lejie, a Grand Lama of Tashilhunpo Monastery, immediately notified the monastery's major Khenpos to prepare for a grand religious ceremony to celebrate. Nilon, Deputy Director of the Xigazê Patriotic Youth Cultural Association, said after listening to the Tibetan broadcast of the State Council's various decisions regarding Tibet on the radio: "These decisions are the happiness of the Tibetan people." When the primary school students heard that the school buildings were to be expanded, they immediately spread the word, saying: Our school will get better day by day. The merchants of Xigazê are particularly interested in the State Council's decision on transportation issues in Tibet. The manager of Luoyun Store said: "This will bring prosperity to Tibetan commerce."

### 日喀则人民为国务院的决定而欢欣鼓舞

1955-03-17
第1版()
专栏：

    日喀则人民为国务院的决定而欢欣鼓舞
    【新华社拉萨十六日电】　国务院关于成立西藏自治区筹备委员会等决定的消息传遍了日喀则；日喀则各界人民正为这些喜讯欢欣鼓舞。班禅堪布会议厅委员会主任委员拉敏·益喜楚臣听到消息后兴奋地说：“我代表堪布会议厅委员会全体官员，向共产党和毛主席表示最衷心的感谢。成立西藏自治区筹备委员会是藏族人民的殷切愿望，今后我们一定在国务院和自治区筹备委员会的领导下努力建设新西藏。”扎什伦布寺大喇嘛王乐阶听到消息后，马上通知寺内各大堪布准备举行隆重宗教仪式庆祝。日喀则市爱国青年文化联谊会副主任委员尼隆从收音机里听到国务院对西藏各项决定的藏语广播以后说：“这些决定就是藏族人民的幸福。”小学生们听说要扩建校舍，立即相互传告说：我们的学校要一天比一天好起来了。日喀则的商人对国务院关于有关西藏交通运输问题的决定特别感兴趣，罗云商店的经理说：“这将给西藏商业带来繁荣。”
