Title: The Fourth Session of the Vietnamese National Assembly Continues: Hearing Reports on the Implementation of the Geneva Ceasefire Agreement and Land Reform, etc.
Author:
Date: 1955-03-22
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency, Hanoi, March 21st: According to the Vietnam News Agency, the fourth session of the National Assembly of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam, which opened on the morning of the 20th, continued in the afternoon.

Minister of National Defense General Vo Nguyen Giap, on behalf of the government, reported to the National Assembly on the implementation of the Geneva Ceasefire Agreement over the past eight months. After pointing out the actions of the US imperialists, the group of French colonialists and the Ngo Dinh Diem clique who violated the agreement, as well as the conspiracies to undermine peace and obstruct the reunification of Vietnam, General Vo Nguyen Giap emphasized the determination of the government, army, and people of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam to faithfully implement the Geneva Accords. General Vo Nguyen Giap said: We are fighting for a just cause, and we are determined to fight until we achieve final victory, and we will surely succeed.

Subsequently, Deputy Minister of Agriculture and Forestry and member of the Central Land Reform Committee Ho Viet Thang gave a report on land reform. He described the significant achievements made in land reform and put forward suggestions on revising the Land Reform Law.

Deputy Minister of the Interior Le Van Luong reported to the National Assembly on the government's policy on ethnic autonomy for ethnic minorities in the country. He described some of the key points of the policy and reviewed the implementation of this policy over the past eight years. Following this, he read out a draft on the establishment of the Thai ethnic minority autonomous region.

Minister of Justice Vu Dinh Hoe explained the policy of the government of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam on freedom of religious belief. He read out a bill on guaranteeing freedom of religious belief.

Minister of Finance Le Van Hien, on behalf of the government, made a report on tax policy.

After listening to these reports, the delegates formed subcommittees to discuss economic and financial issues, diplomatic activities and the implementation of the ceasefire agreement, land reform issues, religious issues, ethnic autonomy issues, cultural, social and educational activities issues, and national defense issues.

The subcommittees will discuss the reports over three days from March 21st to 23rd.

### 越南国民大会第四次会议继续举行  听取日内瓦停战协定执行情况和土地改革等问题的报告

1955-03-22
第4版()
专栏：

    越南国民大会第四次会议继续举行
    听取日内瓦停战协定执行情况和土地改革等问题的报告
    【新华社河内二十一日电】据越南通讯社报道：二十日上午开幕的越南民主共和国国民大会第四次会议在下午继续进行。
    国防部长武元甲将军代表政府向国民大会报告了过去八个月中日内瓦停战协定的执行情况。武元甲将军在指出了美帝国主义者、破坏停战的法国殖民分子集团和吴庭艳集团违反协定的行为以及破坏和平、阻挠越南统一的阴谋以后，着重指出了越南民主共和国政府、军队和人民忠实履行日内瓦协定的决心。武元甲将军说：我们正在为正义的事业而进行斗争，我们决心斗争到获得最后的胜利，而且必将得到成功。
    接着，农林部副部长、中央土地改革委员会委员胡曰胜作了关于土地改革的报告。他叙述了在土地改革中取得的重大成就，并且提出了关于修改土地改革法的建议。
    内务部副部长黎文良向国民大会报告了政府关于国内各民族自治的政策。他叙述了政策的一些要点，回顾了过去八年中执行这一政策的情况。接着，他宣读了关于成立泰族少数民族自治区的草案。
    司法部长武廷槐说明了越南民主共和国政府关于宗教信仰自由的政策。他宣读了关于保障宗教信仰自由的法案。
    财政部长黎文献代表政府作了关于税收政策的报告。
    在听取这些报告后，代表们组成小组委员会，讨论经济和财政问题，外交活动和履行停战协定问题，土地改革问题，宗教问题，民族自治问题，文化、社会和教育活动问题和国防问题。
    小组委员会将在三月二十一日到二十三日三天中讨论各报告。
