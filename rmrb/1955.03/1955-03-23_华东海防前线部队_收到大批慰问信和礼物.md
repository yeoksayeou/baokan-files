Title: Frontline Troops on the Southeast Coast Receive Large Numbers of Letters and Gifts of Appreciation
Author:
Date: 1955-03-23
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, Nanjing, 22nd. Since October 1954, the Political Department of the East China Military Region of the Chinese People's Liberation Army has received more than 200,000 letters of appreciation and over 630 gifts from people all over the country addressed to the People's Liberation Army land, sea, and air force troops fighting on the southeastern coastal defense line of the motherland. The Political Department of the Military Region has promptly forwarded these letters and gifts to the officers and soldiers of the frontline troops.

These letters and gifts came from all over the country, from Changbai Mountain in the north to Hainan Island in the south. Among the gifts were red scarves sent by Young Pioneers, needlework sent by rural women, flashlights for the coastal defense troops to use on night patrols provided by workers, "fur ears" to shield island sentries from the wind provided by employees of livestock companies, raisins sent by traffic workers in Xinjiang Province, and peanuts and sunflower seeds grown by farmers in Laiyang County, Shandong Province. There were also large quantities of books, New Year pictures, fountain pens, and notebooks.

### 华东海防前线部队  收到大批慰问信和礼物

1955-03-23
第1版()
专栏：

    华东海防前线部队
    收到大批慰问信和礼物
    【新华社南京二十二日电】中国人民解放军华东军区政治部从一九五四年十月以来，共收到全国各地人民写给战斗在祖国东南海防线上的人民解放军陆海空军部队的慰问信二十多万封，礼品六百三十多件。军区政治部已把这些信件和礼品及时地转给了前线各部队的指战员们。
    这些慰问信和礼物来自北起长白山、南至海南岛的全国各地。礼物中，有少年先锋队员送的红领巾、农村妇女送的针线，有工人给海防部队夜间巡逻用的手电筒，有畜产公司职工赠给海岛哨兵遮风用的“毛耳朵”，有新疆省交通工人赠送的葡萄干以及山东省莱阳县农民自己种植的落花生和葵花子。另外还有大批书籍、年画、钢笔和学习本等。
