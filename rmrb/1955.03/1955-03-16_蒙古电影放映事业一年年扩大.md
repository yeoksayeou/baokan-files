Title: Mongolian Film Screening Undertaking Expanding Year by Year
Author:
Date: 1955-03-16
Page: 4

    Mongolian film screening undertaking expanding year by year
    The work of screening films for herdsmen and livestock raisers in the Mongolian People's Republic is improving every year.
    Last year, there were ninety mobile film projection teams nationwide, and currently there are one hundred and twenty-four.
    New cinemas have been opened in East Gobi, Zavkhan, Central, Choibalsan and other provinces. (According to Xinhua News Agency)

### 蒙古电影放映事业一年年扩大

1955-03-16
第4版()
专栏：

    蒙古电影放映事业一年年扩大
    蒙古人民共和国向牧民和养畜人的放映电影工作每年都在改善。
    去年全国有九十个电影流动放映队，目前已有一百二十四个。
    在东戈壁、扎布汗、中央、乔巴山和其他省已开设了新的电影院。　（据新华社讯）
