Title: All-China Women's Federation Holds Reception on "March 8th" to Entertain Female Experts and Experts' Wives from Brotherly Countries in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1955-03-09
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, March 8th: The All-China Democratic Women's Federation held a reception on International Women's Day "March 8th" to entertain female experts and experts' wives from the Soviet Union and various people's democratic countries in Beijing. Women representatives from all walks of life in the capital attended as guests. More than 1,300 women from China, the Soviet Union, and other countries attended this grand reception.

At the beginning of the reception, Zhang Yun, Vice-Chairman of the All-China Democratic Women's Federation, delivered a speech, saying: As we joyfully celebrate International Women's Day "March 8th," Chinese women especially think of the Soviet male and female experts and experts' wives entrusted by the Soviet government and people, who have come from afar to help us with our construction, and we deeply appreciate their warm assistance. She talked about the great role played by the unity and cooperation between the people and women of China, the Soviet Union, and various people's democratic countries in the cause of defending world peace. She said that Chinese women will make unremitting efforts to enhance and consolidate this unity and friendly cooperation. Next, Soviet female expert Shpova spoke. She said: The struggle for peace is the fundamental task of the present. In the struggle for peace, working women play a particularly important role. The tears shed by women in the last war are unforgettable. Only peace can bring peace and happiness to women. She said: In order to strive for peace, we must strengthen the power of the peaceful, democratic, and socialist camp with positive actions and selfless labor. In order to strive for peace, we must make greater efforts to consolidate the friendship between the Soviet Union and the People's Republic of China and various people's democratic countries.

After the speeches, in the brightly lit hall, the guests and hosts began cordial conversations, and laughter filled every corner of the hall. The enthusiastic guests and hosts frequently rose to toast the friendship between women of China, the Soviet Union, and various people's democratic countries, for world peace, and for the happiness and health of mothers and children!

During the reception, wonderful performances began on stage. Soviet women performed solo singing and dancing, and famous Chinese actors and singers Xin Fengxia, Zhang Quan, and others performed wonderful programs for the guests. Finally, Chinese children performed many moving and beautiful songs and dances, and enthusiastic applause rang out in the hall.

In a happy and cordial gathering, the guests and hosts happily spent their own festival.

### 全国妇联在“三八”节举行酒会  招待在京的兄弟国家的女专家和专家夫人

1955-03-09
第1版()
专栏：

    全国妇联在“三八”节举行酒会
    招待在京的兄弟国家的女专家和专家夫人
    【新华社八日讯】中华全国民主妇女联合会在“三八”国际妇女节举行酒会，招待在京的苏联和各人民民主国家的女专家和专家夫人。首都各界妇女代表出席作陪。中苏等国妇女共一千三百多人出席了这个盛大的酒会。
    酒会开始时，中华全国民主妇女联合会副主席章蕴首先致词，她说：在我们欢欣鼓舞地度过“三八”国际妇女节的时候，我们中国妇女特别想到受苏联政府和苏联人民委托、远离祖国来帮助我们建设的苏联男女专家和专家夫人们，我们深深感激他们热情的帮助。她谈到了中国和苏联及各人民民主国家的人民和妇女之间的团结合作，在保卫世界和平的事业中发挥的巨大作用，她说，中国妇女将为增强和巩固这种团结友好的合作而努力不懈。接着，苏联女专家什波娃讲话。她说：为和平而斗争，是当前的基本任务。在争取和平的斗争中，劳动妇女起着特别重要的作用。在上一次战争中，妇女们所流的眼泪是难以忘记的。只有和平，才能使妇女们获得安宁和幸福。她说：为了争取和平，我们要以积极的行动和忘我的劳动来增强和平民主社会主义阵营的力量，为了争取和平，我们要更加努力巩固苏联和中华人民共和国及各人民民主国家之间的友谊。
    讲话完毕，在灯火辉煌的大厅里，宾主们开始了亲切的交谈，欢声和笑语充溢在大厅的每个角落。热情的客人和主人们频频起立，为中国、苏联和各人民民主国家妇女之间的友谊，为世界和平，为母亲和孩子们的幸福和健康而干杯！
    酒会进行时，台上开始了精采的表演。苏联妇女表演了独唱和舞蹈，中国的名演员和歌唱家新凤霞、张权等为客人演出了精采的节目。最后，由中国儿童表演了许多动人优美的歌唱和舞蹈，大厅中不断响起热烈的掌声。
    在愉快而亲切的聚会中，宾主们欢乐地度过了她们自己的佳节。
