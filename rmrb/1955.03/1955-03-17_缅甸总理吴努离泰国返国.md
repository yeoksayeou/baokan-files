Title: Burmese Prime Minister U Nu Leaves Thailand to Return Home
Author:
Date: 1955-03-17
Page: 4

　　Xinhua News Agency, Bangkok, 16th: Burmese Prime Minister U Nu left Bangkok on the 14th to return to Burma after a seven-day visit to Thailand.

### 缅甸总理吴努离泰国返国

1955-03-17
第4版()
专栏：

    缅甸总理吴努离泰国返国
    【新华社十六日讯】曼谷消息：缅甸总理吴努在泰国进行了七天访问以后，已在十四日离开曼谷返回缅甸。
