Title: Elections Held for Supreme Soviets and Local Soviets in Eleven Soviet Republics Including the Russian Soviet Federated Socialist Republic
Author:
Date: 1955-03-01
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, 28th: TASS reports: On the 27th, elections for the Supreme Soviets and local Soviets of the Russian Soviet Federated Socialist Republic, the Ukrainian Soviet Socialist Republic, the Byelorussian Soviet Socialist Republic, the Uzbek Soviet Socialist Republic, the Georgian Soviet Socialist Republic, the Lithuanian Soviet Socialist Republic, the Moldavian Soviet Socialist Republic, the Latvian Soviet Socialist Republic, the Tajik Soviet Socialist Republic, the Turkmen Soviet Socialist Republic, and the Estonian Soviet Socialist Republic were held.

　　While the Saturday (26th) evening performances were still underway in Moscow's theaters, elections had already begun in Kamchatka, Sakhalin, Vladivostok, Khabarovsk, and other areas of the Russian Federation's Far East. Before the polls opened at six in the morning, groups of workers, young people, fishermen of all nationalities, and hunters from the great forests were rushing to polling stations to vote. Within the first hour of the polling stations opening, thousands of voters in Khabarovsk alone had cast their ballots.

　　In Moscow, the streets and squares seemed particularly lively on this Sunday morning. As the Kremlin clock struck six, the polling stations became bustling. Voters from various enterprises, government offices, schools, and families flocked to the polling stations to cast their ballots. In the electoral districts where leaders of the Soviet Communist Party and government, such as Bulganin, Malenkov, Molotov, and Khrushchev, were running for election as deputies to the Supreme Soviet of the Russian Federation, the vast majority of voters had cast their ballots before noon. By 4:00 PM, more than 99% of the voters in all 1,795 electoral districts in Moscow had voted.

　　On the 27th, a festive atmosphere prevailed throughout the urban and rural areas of the Ukrainian Republic. Many voters in Kiev, the capital of the republic, enthusiastically cast their ballots from the start of the voting. The situation in the October electoral district was particularly enthusiastic, where voters unanimously elected Khrushchev, First Secretary of the Central Committee of the Soviet Communist Party, as a deputy to the Supreme Soviet of the Ukrainian Republic. Voting in various parts of the republic was almost completed in the first half of election day.

　　The working people of Byelorussia, Uzbekistan, Lithuania, and other republics participated in the election with soaring political enthusiasm. The vast majority of voters in urban and rural areas of the Byelorussian Republic had voted by the first half of the day. In Uzbekistan, by noon, the number of voters who had already voted accounted for more than 90% of the total number of voters. In the Tajik Republic, by 2:00 PM, 98.2% of voters had fulfilled their civic duty.

　　Along with the people of the whole country, thousands of Soviet citizens traveling by train and ship on the 27th also participated in the election while en route. The Moscow-Beijing express train was en route to the capital of the People's Republic of China on the 27th. At the start of the election, residents of Moscow, Leningrad, Kiev, Kazan, Irkutsk, Chita, and other cities traveling on this train cast their ballots in a specially prepared car. Among the first voters were the famous Soviet writers Wanda Wasilewska and Alexander Korneichuk, who were invited to Beijing by Chinese public organizations. Personnel of the "Glory" whaling fleet sailing in the Southern Hemisphere, personnel of the North Atlantic Expedition, as well as personnel on other ships sailing on the high seas and scientists working at the Arctic ice floe station participated in the election.

### 苏联俄罗斯联邦等十一个加盟共和国  举行最高苏维埃和地方苏维埃的选举

1955-03-01
第4版()
专栏：

    苏联俄罗斯联邦等十一个加盟共和国
    举行最高苏维埃和地方苏维埃的选举
    【新华社二十八日讯】据塔斯社莫斯科讯：苏联俄罗斯联邦、乌克兰、白俄罗斯、乌兹别克、格鲁吉亚、立陶宛、摩尔达维亚、拉脱维亚、塔吉克、土库曼和爱沙尼亚等十一个加盟共和国，在二十七日举行了各该加盟共和国最高苏维埃和地方苏维埃的选举。
    当莫斯科各剧院的星期六（二十六日）晚间节目还正在上演的时候，堪察加、库页岛、海参威、伯力和俄罗斯联邦远东其他地区的选举已经开始了。在清晨六时投票开始前，就有一群一群工人、青年、各民族的渔夫和从大森林里来的猎人赶往各地的选举站投票。在选举站开放后头一小时内，单是伯力城就有几千选民投了票。
    在莫斯科，各街道和广场上，这一个星期日的清晨显得特别活跃。当克里姆林宫的钟声刚刚打了六下的时候，各选举站就热闹起来。来自各企业、机关、学校和家庭的选民络绎不绝地到各选举站去投票。在选举苏联共产党和政府的领导人布尔加宁、马林科夫、莫洛托夫、赫鲁晓夫为俄罗斯联邦最高苏维埃代表的各选区，绝大多数选民在上午十二时以前都投完了票。截至下午四时，莫斯科所有一千七百九十五个选区已有百分之九十九以上的选民投了票。
    乌克兰共和国城乡各地二十七日到处都是一片节日气象。共和国首都基辅的许多选民，一开始投票时就踊跃投了票。十月选区中情况特别热烈，这个选区的选民们一致选举苏联共产党中央委员会第一书记赫鲁晓夫为乌克兰共和国最高苏维埃代表。共和国各地的投票在选举日上半天就差不多结束了。
    白俄罗斯、乌兹别克、立陶宛和其他加盟共和国各地的劳动人民，以高涨的政治热情参加了这次选举。白俄罗斯共和国城乡各地绝大多数选民在上半天就投了票。在乌兹别克，到中午时已经投票的选民已占选民总数百分之九十以上。在塔吉克共和国，到下午二时为止，已经有百分之九十八点二的选民履行了自己的公民职责。
    和全国人民一起，二十七日乘火车和轮船旅行的成千上万的苏联公民也在旅途中参加了选举。莫斯科—北京快车二十七日正在开往中华人民共和国的首都途中。选举开始时，乘这个列车旅行的莫斯科、列宁格勒、基辅、喀山、伊尔库次克、赤塔和其他城市的居民都在一个特殊预备的车厢内投了票。最先投票的选民中有著名的苏联作家瓦·华西列夫斯卡娅和亚·考湼楚克，他们都是应中国公众团体的邀请到北京去的。正在南半球航行的“光荣”捕鲸船队的人员、北大西洋探险队的人员、以及其他正在远洋航行的轮船上的人员和在北极浮冰站上工作的科学工作者都参加了选举。
