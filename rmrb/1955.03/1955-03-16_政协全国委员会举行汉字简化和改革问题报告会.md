Title: CPPCC National Committee Holds Report Meeting on the Simplification and Reform of Chinese Characters
Author:
Date: 1955-03-16
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, March 15th: The National Committee of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference (CPPCC) held a report meeting on the 15th, inviting Wu Yuzhang, Director of the Chinese Language Reform Committee, and committee member Hu Qiaomu to give reports on the simplification of Chinese characters and the reform of the Chinese language. More than 1,300 people attended the report meeting, including members of the CPPCC National Committee in Beijing, staff members of other relevant state organs and central organs of various democratic parties, as well as people from cultural and educational circles in the capital.

The National Committee of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference is also preparing to organize a forum for members in Beijing in the near future to discuss the draft plan for the simplification of Chinese characters recently announced by the Chinese Language Reform Committee.

### 政协全国委员会举行汉字简化和改革问题报告会

1955-03-16
第1版()
专栏：

    政协全国委员会举行汉字简化和改革问题报告会
    【新华社十五日讯】中国人民政治协商会议全国委员会在十五日举行报告会，邀请中国文字改革委员会主任吴玉章和委员胡乔木就汉字简化和汉字改革问题作了报告。出席报告会的有政协全国委员会在京委员以及其他有关国家机关和各民主党派中央机关的工作人员以及首都文化、教育界人士共一千三百多人。
    中国人民政治协商会议全国委员会并准备在最近组织在京委员座谈中国文字改革委员会不久前公布的汉字简化方案草案。
