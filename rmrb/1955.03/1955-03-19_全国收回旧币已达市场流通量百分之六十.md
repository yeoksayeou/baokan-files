Title: National Recall of Old Currency Reaches Sixty Percent of Market Circulation

Author: 
Date: 1955-03-19
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, 18th: According to incomplete statistics, within the first ten days of issuing the new currency, approximately sixty percent or more of the old currency in the entire country's urban and rural markets had been recovered. In eight major cities, including Beijing, Tianjin, Shanghai, Xi'an, and Chongqing, the People's Bank has recovered nearly eighty percent of the old currency in circulation; in cities such as Beijing, Tianjin, Chongqing, and Xi'an, the new Renminbi has basically replaced the old Renminbi in market circulation. In rural areas, according to statistics from eleven provinces including Zhejiang, Anhui, Jiangsu, Guangdong, Guangxi, Jiangxi, Sichuan, Gansu, Shaanxi, Xinjiang, and Liaoning, the amount of old currency recovered accounts for approximately fifty-five percent of the market circulation.

### 全国收回旧币已达市场流通量百分之六十

1955-03-19
第1版()
专栏：

    全国收回旧币已达市场流通量百分之六十
    【新华社十八日讯】　据不完全统计，发行新币的头十天内，全国城乡市场上已收回了大约占整个货币流通量百分之六十以上的旧币。在北京、天津、上海、西安、重庆等八大城市，人民银行收回的旧币将近占市场流通量的百分之八十；北京、天津、重庆、西安等市，新人民币基本上代替了旧人民币在市场流通。在农村，据浙江、安徽、江苏、广东、广西、江西、四川、甘肃、陕西、新疆、辽宁等十一省统计，旧币收回量占市场流通量的百分之五十五左右。
