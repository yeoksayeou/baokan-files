Title: Korean Cabinet Thanks Democratic Germany for Assisting in Korean Reconstruction Work
Author:
Date: 1955-03-17
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency, Pyongyang, March 15th: According to the Korean Central News Agency on the 13th, the Cabinet of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea passed a resolution on March 7th, expressing heartfelt gratitude to the government of the German Democratic Republic for its resolution on assisting Korea's reconstruction work.

According to the German Democratic Republic government's resolution of February 17, the German Democratic Republic will provide material assistance to Korea worth 79 million rubles in 1955, 67 million rubles in 1956, and 71 million rubles in 1957. This assistance will be used to build a diesel engine factory and a printing combine in Korea, construct the Hwanghae Iron Works, restore Hungnam City, and supply Korea with various goods.

The government of the German Democratic Republic also decided to allocate 35 million rubles annually from 1958 to 1964 to restore Hungnam City in Korea. Therefore, calculating from 1952, the total amount of assistance provided by the German Democratic Republic to Korea will reach 545.4 million rubles.

The government of the German Democratic Republic also decided to establish a central leading body headed by Deputy Prime Minister and Minister of Mechanical Engineering Hans Heinrich Rau to implement unified guidance in the assistance work for the restoration of Hungnam City.

The Cabinet of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea has also decided to establish the Hungnam City Restoration Committee to ensure the smooth progress of the restoration work in Hungnam City with the assistance of the German Democratic Republic and to guide all work related to this matter.

### 朝鲜内阁感谢民主德国协助朝鲜恢复工作

1955-03-17
第4版()
专栏：

    朝鲜内阁感谢民主德国协助朝鲜恢复工作
    【新华社平壤十五日电】据朝鲜中央通讯社十三日讯：朝鲜民主主义人民共和国内阁三月七日通过决议，对德意志民主共和国政府关于援助朝鲜恢复重建工作的决议表示由衷的感谢。
    根据德意志民主共和国政府二月十七日的决议，德意志民主共和国将在一九五五年对朝鲜提供价值七千九百万卢布的物质援助，在一九五六年提供六千七百万卢布的物质援助，在一九五七年提供七千一百万卢布的物质援助。这些援助将用来在朝鲜建立一个柴油引擎工厂、一个印刷联合工厂，修建黄海制铁所，恢复咸兴市和供给朝鲜以各种货物。
    德意志民主共和国政府还决定从一九五八年到一九六四年每年拨出三千五百万卢布来恢复朝鲜咸兴市。因此，从一九五二年计算起来，德意志民主共和国给朝鲜的援助总额将达五亿四千五百四十万卢布。
    德意志民主共和国政府还决定设立一个以副总理兼机械工业部部长汉斯·亨利希·劳为首的中央领导机构，以便在恢复咸兴市的援助工作方面实行统一的指导。
    朝鲜民主主义人民共和国内阁也已决定设立咸兴市恢复委员会，以保证咸兴市的恢复工作在德意志民主共和国援助下顺利进行并指导同此事有关的总的工作。
