Title: Representatives of Civil Aviation Enterprises from Bulgaria, Czechoslovakia, Romania, Hungary, Poland and other countries sign protocol, stipulating that the five countries strengthen cooperation in organizing air transportation

Author:
Date: 1955-03-29
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Warsaw, March 23rd: According to a report from the Polish Press Agency, representatives of civil aviation enterprises from Bulgaria, Czechoslovakia, Romania, Hungary, and Poland recently held a meeting in Warsaw.

At the meeting, the Romanian TAROM Civil Aviation Company declared its participation in the agreement signed a year ago by Bulgaria, Czechoslovakia, Hungary, and Poland on the joint use of certain air routes.

The representatives attending the meeting also signed a protocol. The protocol stipulates that the above-mentioned five countries' civil aviation enterprises will conduct further cooperation in organizing air transportation.

Representatives from the German Democratic Republic also attended the meeting.

### 保、捷、罗、匈、波等国民用航空企业代表签订议定书  规定五国在组织航空运输方面加强合作

1955-03-29
第4版()
专栏：

    保、捷、罗、匈、波等国民用航空企业代表签订议定书
    规定五国在组织航空运输方面加强合作
   【新华社讯】据塔斯社华沙二十三日讯：据波兰通讯社报道：保加利亚、捷克斯洛伐克、罗马尼亚、匈牙利和波兰民用航空企业代表，最近在华沙举行了会议。
    会上，罗马尼亚塔罗姆民用航空公司声明参加保、捷、匈、波四国在一年前签订的关于共同使用某些航空线的协定。
    参加会议的代表还签订了一项议定书。议定书规定上述五国民用航空企业在组织航空运输方面进行进一步的合作。
    德意志民主共和国的代表也参加了会议。
