Title: Strive to Cultivate Various Agricultural Technical Talents (Picture)
Author: Gao Liang
Date: 1955-03-11
Page: 2

    Strive to Cultivate Various Agricultural Technical Talents
    With the socialist transformation of agriculture and the development of agricultural production in our country, the vast countryside also needs more agricultural technical talents.
    Shijiazhuang Agricultural Mechanization School in Hebei Province is a school specializing in training agricultural technicians and tractor drivers. Graduates from this school are successively assigned to agricultural machinery stations in various places, which will further strengthen the agricultural machinery stations in various places and help farmers master agricultural technology.
    Our reporter Gao Liang Photo
    The school is equipped with many agricultural machines for teaching purposes. This is a teacher explaining the performance of a tractor made in Czechoslovakia to the students.
    The teacher is explaining the precautions for using the 24-row seeder. The students are studying the principle of the centrifugal waterwheel with the teacher.
    Students must not only understand the principles of machine construction, but also learn mechanical repairs. The students have already learned to drive tractors.
    This group of forty graduates went to Zhangbei area with joy. The principal and students came to see them off.

### 努力培养各种农业技术人材（图片）
高粮
1955-03-11
第2版()
专栏：

    努力培养各种农业技术人材
    随着我国农业社会主义改造和农业生产的发展，广大农村也就需要更多的农业技术人材。
    河北省石家庄农业机械化学校就是一座专门培养农业技术员和拖拉机手的学校。从这个学校毕业的学生陆续分配到各地的农业机器站去，将进一步加强各地的农业机器站，帮助农民掌握农业技术。
    本报记者  高粮摄
    学校里设有许多教学用的农业机器。这是教员正在给学生讲解捷克斯洛伐克出品的拖拉机的性能。
    教员正在给学生讲解二十四行播种机使用时应注意的事项。学生们同教员一起研究离心力水车的原理。
    学生们不仅要懂得机器构造的原理。而且还必须学会机械的修理。学生们已经学会了驾驶拖拉机。
    这一批毕业的四十个学生带着愉快的心情到张北地区去。校长和同学们都来欢送他们。
