Title: The Dalai Lama and Panchen Erdeni Depart Beijing
Author:
Date: 1955-03-13
Page: 1

(Xinhua News Agency, March 12th) The Dalai Lama Danzeng Gyaco and his entourage and family members left Beijing by train at 4:25 PM on the 12th, accompanied by Liu Geping, Deputy Minister of the United Front Work Department of the CPC Central Committee, to visit the Northwest and Southwest regions before returning to Tibet. The Panchen Erdeni Qoiji Gyaltsen and his entourage and family members left Beijing by train at 4:50 PM on the same afternoon, accompanied by Liu Chun, Deputy Director of the Nationalities Affairs Commission, to visit the East China and South China regions before returning to Tibet via the Northwest.

Those who went to the station to see them off included: Vice Premiers of the State Council Peng Dehuai, Deng Xiaoping, Chen Yi, and Ulanhu, Secretary-General Xi Zhongxun, ministers and vice ministers of various ministries, directors and deputy directors of various commissions, Deputy Directors of the Nationalities Affairs Commission Wang Feng, Sa Kongliao, and Yang Jingren, Representative of the Central People's Government in Tibet Zhang Jingwu, and Vice Mayors of Beijing Zhang Youyu, Wu Han, and Wang Kunlun.

Also present at the station to see them off were: Vice Chairmen of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress Li Jishen, Huang Yanpei, Li Weihan, Chen Shutong, and members in Beijing, and Deputy Directors of the Nationalities Committee Zhang Zhiyi.

Also present at the station to see them off were: President of the Supreme People's Court Dong Biwu, Vice Chairmen of the National Defense Council Zhang Zhizhong, Fu Zuoyi, Long Yun, Deputy Director of the General Political Department of the Chinese People's Liberation Army Gan Siqi, Political Commissar of the Tibet Military Region Tan Guansan, and Deputy Political Commissar Fan Ming.

Also present at the station to see them off was: Vice Chairman of the National Committee of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference Zhang Bojun and standing committee members in Beijing.

Also present at the station to see them off were: Acting President of the Chinese Buddhist Association Xirao Jiacuo, Vice Presidents Zhao Puchu, Gara Cang, and other leaders of people's organizations and religious groups, heads of the Dalai Lama's Office in Beijing and the Panchen Erdeni's Office in Beijing, and teachers and students of the Central Institute for Nationalities, etc.

More than 800 people went to the station to see them off.

When the Dalai Lama and the Panchen Erdeni walked onto the platform, the band played farewell music, and warm applause rang out from the farewell crowd. The Dalai Lama and the Panchen Erdeni presented Hada scarves to Vice Premiers Peng Dehuai, Deng Xiaoping, and Chen Yi as farewell gifts. The Dalai Lama and the Panchen Erdeni also exchanged Hada scarves and touched heads in a religious ritual to bid farewell to each other. Young Pioneers in the capital then enthusiastically presented them with flowers, and the Dalai Lama and the Panchen Erdeni shook hands with the well-wishers to say goodbye. The train slowly pulled out of the station.

Before leaving Beijing, the Dalai Lama and the Panchen Erdeni had bid farewell to the Chairman of the People's Republic of China Mao Zedong, Vice Chairman Zhu De, Chairman of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress Liu Shaoqi, and Premier of the State Council Zhou Enlai.

### 达赖喇嘛和班禅额尔德尼离京

1955-03-13
第1版()
专栏：

    达赖喇嘛和班禅额尔德尼离京
    【新华社十二日讯】达赖喇嘛·丹增嘉措和随行人员及家属在十二日下午四时二十五分乘火车离开北京，由中共中央统一战线工作部副部长刘格平陪同去西北、西南等地区参观后返回西藏，班禅额尔德尼·却吉坚赞和随行人员及家属在同日下午四时五十分乘火车离开北京，由民族事务委员会副主任刘春陪同去华东、华南等地区参观后经西北返回西藏。
    前往车站欢送的有：国务院副总理彭德怀、邓小平、陈毅、乌兰夫，秘书长习仲勋，各部部长、副部长，各委员会主任、副主任，民族事务委员会副主任汪锋、萨空了、杨静仁，中央人民政府驻西藏代表张经武，北京市副市长张友渔、吴晗、王昆仑。
    前往车站欢送的还有：全国人民代表大会常务委员会副委员长李济深、黄炎培、李维汉、陈叔通和在京委员，民族委员会副主任委员张执一。
    前往车站欢送的还有：最高人民法院院长董必武，国防委员会副主席张治中、傅作义、龙云，中国人民解放军总政治部副主任甘泗淇，西藏军区政治委员谭冠三、副政治委员范明。
    前往车站欢送的还有：中国人民政治协商会议全国委员会副主席章伯钧和在京常务委员。
    前往车站欢送的还有：中国佛教协会代理会长喜饶嘉错，副会长赵朴初、噶喇藏和其他人民团体及宗教团体负责人，达赖喇嘛驻京办事处和班禅额尔德尼驻京办事处负责人以及中央民族学院师生等。
    前往车站欢送的共有八百多人。
    当达赖喇嘛、班禅额尔德尼走进月台时，乐队奏欢送曲，欢送的行列里响起了热烈的掌声。达赖喇嘛和班禅额尔德尼分别向彭德怀、邓小平、陈毅副总理献哈达辞别。达赖喇嘛和班禅额尔德尼也互换哈达，并按宗教仪式互相碰头告别。首都少年先锋队队员接着向他们热情地献花，达赖喇嘛和班禅额尔德尼分别与欢送者握手告别。列车徐徐开出车站。
    达赖喇嘛和班禅额尔德尼离开北京前，曾向中华人民共和国主席毛泽东、副主席朱德，全国人民代表大会常务委员会委员长刘少奇，国务院总理周恩来辞行。
