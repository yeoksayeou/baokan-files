Title: The Tibet Engineering Survey Team Formed According to the State Council's Decision Departs
Author:
Date: 1955-03-13
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, March 12th: The Tibet Engineering Survey Team departed from Beijing for Lhasa on March 12th.

    The Tibet Engineering Survey Team was formed by the Ministry of Fuel Industry, the Ministry of Water Resources, the Ministry of Local Industry, and the Ministry of Geology, according to the State Council's decision to assist the local government of Tibet in construction matters. The team consists of 41 people, including engineers, engineering technicians, and technical workers. After surveying the various projects to be constructed in Tibet in the future, the survey team will provide the necessary data for design.

    Before the departure of the Tibet Engineering Survey Team, the Nationalities Affairs Commission hosted a farewell banquet. Zhang Jingwu, representative of the Central People's Government, Tan Guansan, Political Commissar of the Tibet Military Region, and Fan Ming, Deputy Political Commissar, introduced the situation in Tibet to all team members at the banquet, and encouraged them to work hard to help the Tibetan people in production and construction.

### 根据国务院决定组成的西藏工程勘察队出发

1955-03-13
第1版()
专栏：

    根据国务院决定组成的西藏工程勘察队出发
    【新华社十二日讯】　西藏工程勘察队，在本月十二日从北京出发前往拉萨。
    西藏工程勘察队是根据国务院关于帮助西藏地方进行建设事项的决定，由燃料工业部、水利部、地方工业部和地质部派人组成。全队共有工程师、工程技术员以及技术工人等四十一人。勘察队对将来在西藏建设的各项工程，经过勘察后，要提供在设计时所需要的各种资料。
    西藏工程勘察队在出发前，民族事务委员会曾设宴欢送。中央人民政府代表张经武、西藏军区政治委员谭冠三，副政治委员范明等在宴会上向全体队员介绍了西藏地区情况，并勉励他们为帮助西藏人民生产建设而努力工作。
