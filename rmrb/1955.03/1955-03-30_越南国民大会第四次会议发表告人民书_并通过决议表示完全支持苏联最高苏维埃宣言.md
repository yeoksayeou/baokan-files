Title: Vietnam National Assembly Fourth Session Issues Letter to the People and Passes Resolution Expressing Full Support for the Soviet Supreme Soviet Declaration
Author:
Date: 1955-03-30
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Hanoi, March 29th: According to Vietnam News Agency on March 28th, the Fourth Session of the National Assembly of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam, held in Hanoi from March 20th to 26th, issued a letter to the Vietnamese people.

The letter to the people stated: The National Assembly has clearly expressed the will of the people, concentrated the aspirations of the people, and resolved pressing issues related to the welfare of the country and the people. The National Assembly unanimously approved the government's policies in the current stage and expressed complete confidence in the government headed by President Ho Chi Minh.

The letter to the people pointed out: The common tasks of the entire Vietnamese people at present are:

	1. Continue to struggle for the strict implementation of the Geneva Armistice Agreement in order to consolidate peace and complete national reunification through free general elections;

	2. Accelerate and complete land reform to consolidate North Vietnam, strive to restore and develop the economy and culture, strengthen national defense, consolidate the people's regime, establish the Thai and Miao ethnic minority autonomous region in Northwest Vietnam, and promote diplomatic activities;

	3. Expand and consolidate national unity, and win the approval and support of the people of the world.

The letter to the people stated: The National Assembly calls on all the people to unite more closely and extensively around President Ho Chi Minh, the government, and the National Assembly, and to fully implement the policies adopted by the National Assembly; calls on the Vietnamese People's Army to strive to improve its political and technical level, enhance its fighting spirit, further heighten vigilance, support the people's regime, and love the people; calls on the people of South Vietnam to unite with other people and fight against the schemes of the US imperialists, the French colonialists who are sabotaging the armistice agreement, and the Ngo Dinh Diem clique who are attempting to split the country, undermine national unity, and disrupt peace.

Xinhua News Agency, Hanoi, March 29th: According to Vietnam News Agency on March 28th, the Fourth Session of the National Assembly of Vietnam passed a resolution on March 26th, expressing support for the Soviet Supreme Soviet's declaration on establishing direct contacts between parliaments of various countries to maintain peace.

The resolution condemned the activities and conspiracies of the US war-mongers, and pointed out that countries with different political systems can coexist peacefully.

The resolution said: The current session of the National Assembly of Vietnam believes it is its responsibility to once again express its full support for the declaration of the Soviet Supreme Soviet, is prepared to expand direct relations with the parliaments of other countries, and welcomes parliamentary delegations from all countries willing to visit the Democratic Republic of Vietnam in order to establish and develop fraternal relations between the Vietnamese people and the people of other countries in the world.

### 越南国民大会第四次会议发表告人民书  并通过决议表示完全支持苏联最高苏维埃宣言

1955-03-30
第4版()
专栏：

    越南国民大会第四次会议发表告人民书
    并通过决议表示完全支持苏联最高苏维埃宣言
    【新华社河内二十九日电】据越南通讯社二十八日讯：从三月二十日到二十六日在河内召开的越南民主共和国国民大会第四次会议发表了一个告越南人民书。
    告人民书说：国民大会已清楚地表达了人民的意志，集中了人民的愿望，并且解决了关系国家和人民福利的迫切问题。国民大会全体一致地通过了政府在目前阶段中的政策并对以胡志明主席为首的政府表示完全的信任。
    告人民书指出：目前越南全体人民的共同任务是：
    一、继续为日内瓦停战协定的严格履行而斗争，以便巩固和平和通过自由普选完成全国统一；
    二、加快并且完成土地改革来巩固北越，努力恢复和发展经济和文化，加强国防，巩固人民政权，在越南西北部建立泰族、苗族少数民族自治区，推进外交活动；
    三、扩大和巩固全国的团结，争取世界人民的赞同和支持。
    告人民书说：国民大会号召全体人民更紧密地和更广泛地团结在胡志明主席、政府和国民大会的周围，全力执行国民大会所通过的各项政策；号召越南人民军努力提高政治和技术水平，提高它的战斗精神，进一步提高警惕，支持人民政权和热爱人民；号召南越人民同其他人民团结一致，向美帝国主义者、破坏停战协定的法国殖民分子和吴庭艳集团企图分裂这个国家、破坏民族统一和破坏和平的阴谋进行斗争。
   【新华社河内二十九日讯】据越南通讯社二十八日讯：越南国民大会第四次会议在二十六日会议上通过决议，表示支持苏联最高苏维埃关于各国议会间建立直接接触以维护和平的宣言。
    决议中谴责美国战争贩子的活动和阴谋，并且指出不同政治制度的国家可以和平共处。
    决议说：越南国民大会本次会议认为有责任再一次表示，完全支持苏联最高苏维埃的宣言，准备扩展同其他国家议会之间的直接关系，并且欢迎愿意访问越南民主共和国的一切国家的议会代表团，以便建立和发展越南人民同世界上其他国家人民之间的兄弟关系。
