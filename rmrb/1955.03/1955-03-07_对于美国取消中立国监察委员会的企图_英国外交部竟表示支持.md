Title: British Foreign Office Expresses Support for U.S. Attempt to Abolish the Neutral Nations Supervisory Commission
Author:
Date: 1955-03-07
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, London, 6th - According to a Reuters report, a British Foreign Office spokesperson said on the 4th that Britain "fully supports" the memorandum from the U.S. State Department to the governments of Sweden and Switzerland, in which the U.S. advocates for the abolition of the Korean Neutral Nations Supervisory Commission.

According to reports, London democrats, upon hearing this news, pointed out that the British government's hasty expression of support for the U.S. note is just another demonstration that Britain is subservient to the U.S. aggressive bloc on the Korean issue.

### 对于美国取消中立国监察委员会的企图  英国外交部竟表示支持

1955-03-07
第4版()
专栏：

    对于美国取消中立国监察委员会的企图
    英国外交部竟表示支持
    【新华社六日讯】伦敦消息：据路透社报道，英国外交部发言人四日说，英国“完全支持”美国国务院给瑞典和瑞士两国政府的备忘录，美国在这个备忘录中主张取消朝鲜中立国监察委员会。
    据消息报道，伦敦民主人士听到这一消息后指出，英国政府急急忙忙地表示支持美国照会，只不过又一次证明英国在朝鲜问题上是听命于美国侵略集团的。
