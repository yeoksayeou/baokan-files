Title: Soviet Minister of Health Kovrigina and Others Return Home
Author:
Date: 1955-03-19
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, March 18th. Soviet Minister of Health Kovrigina, and the Minister of Health of the Uzbek Soviet Socialist Republic, Sagatov, who accompanied Minister Kovrigina on her visit to China, concluded their visit to China at the invitation of the Ministry of Health of the People's Republic of China, and left Beijing on the 18th to return home.

Those who went to the airport to see them off included: Li Dequan, Minister of Health of the People's Republic of China, Vice Ministers He Cheng, He Biao, and Cui Yitian, and Yan Jingqing, Director of the Beijing Public Health Bureau, and many others.

Charge d'Affaires ad interim of the Soviet Embassy in China, Luo Maijin, and others also went to the airport to see them off.

During their stay in China, Kovrigina and Sagatov visited the Shanghai First Medical College and the East China Branch of the Nanjing Central Institute of Hygiene in Shanghai, Nanjing, and other places. After returning to Beijing, they also visited the Beijing Soviet Red Cross Hospital. On the evening of the 16th, they also attended a farewell banquet hosted by our Minister of Health, Li Dequan.

### 苏联卫生部长科夫里金娜等人回国

1955-03-19
第1版()
专栏：

    苏联卫生部长科夫里金娜等人回国
    【新华社十八日讯】　应中华人民共和国卫生部邀请来我国访问的苏联卫生部长科夫里金娜和随同科夫里金娜部长访问我国的乌兹别克苏维埃社会主义共和国卫生部长萨加托夫结束了在我国的访问，已在十八日离开北京回国。
   前往机场欢送的有：中华人民共和国卫生部部长李德全，副部长贺诚、贺彪、崔义田，北京市公共卫生局局长严镜清等多人。
    苏联驻华大使馆临时代办罗迈进等也到机场欢送。
    科夫里金娜和萨加托夫在我国期间，曾去上海、南京等地参观了上海第一医学院和南京中央卫生研究院华东分院等处。回北京后，又参观了北京苏联红十字医院。十六日晚间，他们还出席了我卫生部长李德全的欢送宴会。
