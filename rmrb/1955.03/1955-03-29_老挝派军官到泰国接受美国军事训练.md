Title: Laos Sends Officers to Thailand for U.S. Military Training
Author:
Date: 1955-03-29
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Hanoi, 26th. A dispatch from Vientiane states: The Royal Lao Government has sent some officers to Thailand to receive military training under U.S. administration. The training period will be six months.

### 老挝派军官到泰国接受美国军事训练

1955-03-29
第4版()
专栏：

    老挝派军官到泰国接受美国军事训练
   【新华社河内二十六日电】万象来的消息说：老挝王国政府已经派了一些军官到泰国去，接受美国管理下的军事训练。训练时间为六个月。
