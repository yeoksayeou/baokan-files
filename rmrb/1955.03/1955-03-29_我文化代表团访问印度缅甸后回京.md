Title: My Cultural Delegation Returns to Beijing After Visiting India and Burma
Author: 
Date: 1955-03-29
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, March 27th—The People's Republic of China Cultural Delegation, which went to India and Burma in December 1954 at the invitation of the governments of the Republic of India and the Union of Burma for a visit and performance tour, returned to Beijing on March 26.

The People's Republic of China Cultural Delegation stayed in India and Burma for a total of 76 days, visiting the cities of Delhi, Calcutta, Bombay, and Madras in India, and Rangoon and Mandalay in Burma; it was warmly welcomed by the governments and people of India and Burma. Indian President Prasad, Prime Minister Nehru, Burmese President Ba U, and Prime Minister U Nu all received the delegation and watched its performances. The delegation gave a total of 26 performances, with approximately 300,000 spectators; the delegation also held discussions with people from cultural and artistic circles in India and Burma to exchange experiences and learn from each other in dance and singing. In addition, the delegation visited famous cultural sites in India and Burma, such as the Taj Mahal, Ajanta, the Shwedagon Pagoda, and Mandalay Hill.

### 我文化代表团访问印度缅甸后回京

1955-03-29
第1版()
专栏：

    我文化代表团访问印度缅甸后回京
    【新华社二十七日讯】应印度共和国和缅甸联邦共和国政府的邀请，在一九五四年十二月间去印度和缅甸访问演出的中华人民共和国文化代表团已经在三月二十六日回到北京。
    中华人民共和国文化代表团在印度和缅甸共停留了七十六天，访问了印度的德里、加尔各答、孟买和马德拉斯和缅甸的仰光、曼德勒等城市；受到印度、缅甸政府和广大人民的热烈欢迎。印度普拉沙德总统、尼赫鲁总理，缅甸巴宇总统、吴努总理都曾接见了代表团，并看了代表团的演出。代表团共演了二十六次，观众约三十万人；代表团还同印度和缅甸的文化艺术界人士进行座谈，交流经验，并相互学习了舞蹈和歌唱。此外代表团还参观了印度和缅甸的著名文化古迹泰吉·玛哈尔陵、阿旃陀、大金塔、曼德勒山等。
