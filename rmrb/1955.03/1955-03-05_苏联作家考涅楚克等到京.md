Title: Soviet Writers Korneichuk and Others Arrive in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1955-03-05
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, March 4th:
    At the invitation of the Chinese People's Committee for World Peace and the Chinese People's Association for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries, Korneichuk, Presidium member of the Soviet Peace Committee and famous writer, and Vasilevskaya, member of the Soviet Peace Committee and famous writer, arrived in Beijing by train on the afternoon of the fourth.
    Greeting them at the station were: Guo Moruo, Chairman of the Chinese People's Committee for World Peace, Chu Tunan, President of the Chinese People's Association for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries, Mao Dun, Chairman of the Chinese Writers Association, and others.

### 苏联作家考涅楚克等到京

1955-03-05
第1版()
专栏：

    苏联作家考涅楚克等到京
    【新华社四日讯】　应中国人民保卫世界和平委员会和中国人民对外文化协会的邀请，苏联拥护和平委员会主席团委员、名作家考涅楚克和苏联拥护和平委员会委员、名作家华西列夫斯卡娅乘火车在四日下午到达北京。
    到车站欢迎的有：中国人民保卫世界和平委员会主席郭沫若，中国人民对外文化协会会长楚图南，中国作家协会主席茅盾等人。
