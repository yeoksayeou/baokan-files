Title: The State Council Appoints Staff to National Agencies and Universities

Author:

Date: 1955-03-10
Page: 3

The State Council Appoints Staff to National Agencies and Universities

On March 3rd, the State Council of the People's Republic of China issued an order appointing staff to several national agencies and universities. The list is as follows:

    Liu Chengyun, Zhou Xinwu, Jin Zhao, and Li Wu are appointed as Deputy Directors of the Broadcasting Affairs Bureau;
    Zhu Dixing is appointed as Vice Minister of the Second Ministry of Machine Building;
    Lu Weite is appointed as Vice President of Xiamen University;
    Kang Di and Chen Wuyu are appointed as Vice Presidents of Northwest Agricultural College;
    Feng Zhongyun is appointed as President of East China Institute of Water Conservancy; Liu Mingfan is appointed as President of Fujian Normal College.

### 国务院任命一批国家机关和高等学校的工作人员

1955-03-10
第3版()
专栏：

    国务院任命一批国家机关和高等学校的工作人员
    中华人民共和国国务院三月三日发布命令，任命了一批国家机关和高等学校的工作人员，名单如下：
    刘程云、周新武、金照、李伍为广播事业局副局长；
    朱涤新为第二机械工业部副部长；
    陆维特为厦门大学副校长；
    康迪、陈吾愚为西北农学院副院长；
    冯仲云为华东水利学院院长；刘明凡为福建师范学院院长。
