Title: India-China Friendship Association National Conference to be Held Next Month in Calcutta
Author:
Date: 1955-03-04
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, New Delhi, March 3rd: The second national conference of the India-China Friendship Association will be held in Calcutta from April 21st to 24th.

    The chairman of the National Conference Reception Committee and Speaker of the West Bengal Legislative Assembly, Mr. Mukherjee, said at a press conference in Calcutta on February 26th that the purpose of the meeting is to further enhance the deep friendship of the Indian people towards China. He said that the Five Principles of Peaceful Coexistence put forward in the joint statement of Premier Zhou Enlai and Prime Minister Nehru have laid a new foundation not only for the friendly relations between India and China but also for the friendly relations between all countries in the world.

    Mukherjee said that it is expected that about eight hundred representatives from various provinces (states) of India will participate in the conference.

### 印中友协全国会议下月在加尔各答举行

1955-03-04
第4版()
专栏：

    印中友协全国会议下月在加尔各答举行
    【新华社新德里三日电】印中友好协会第二次全国会议将在四月二十一日到二十四日在加尔各答举行。
    全国会议招待委员会主席、西孟加拉省立法会议议长慕克吉二月二十六日在加尔各答举行的记者招待会上说：会议的目的是进一步增进印度人民对中国的深切友谊。他说：周恩来总理和尼赫鲁总理联合声明中提出的和平共处的五项原则不但给印中的友好关系而且给世界各国之间的友好关系奠定了新的基础。
    慕克吉说，预料将约有印度各省（邦）的八百个代表参加会议。
