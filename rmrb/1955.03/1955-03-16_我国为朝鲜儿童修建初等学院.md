Title: China Builds Elementary School for Korean Children
Author:
Date: 1955-03-16
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Shenyang, March 15th: In Xiong Yuecheng, Liaoning Province, a scenic area, the Chinese government has built an elementary school for Korean children receiving treatment in China. The opening ceremony was held on the 13th.

More than 300 Korean orphans, aged six to eighteen, who lost their parents in the Korean War and suffer from chronic illnesses, have come to this school for recuperation. This elementary school is large in scale and fully equipped, and the children will receive good rest and treatment here.

### 我国为朝鲜儿童修建初等学院

1955-03-16
第1版()
专栏：

    我国为朝鲜儿童修建初等学院
    【新华社沈阳十五日电】在辽宁省风景幽美的熊岳城，我国政府为来我国疗养的朝鲜儿童修建了一所初等学院。并在十三日举行了开院典礼。
    来到这个学院疗养的都是因为在朝鲜战争中失去了父、母，身体患有慢性疾病的六岁到十八岁的朝鲜孤儿，共有三百多人。这个初等学院规模较大，设备齐全，儿童们将在这里得到很好的休息和疗养。
