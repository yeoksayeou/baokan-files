Title: Dulles Prepares for Activities in the Middle East
Author:
Date: 1955-03-26
Page: 4

    Xinhua News Agency, Cairo, March 25th—TASS dispatch from Cairo, March 25th: According to the "Al-Akhbar" newspaper's Washington correspondent, citing sources in American political circles, Dulles is preparing to visit the Near and Middle Eastern countries in the near future "in order to understand the new situation in this region."
    The press here believes that Dulles' planned trip is related to the sharpening of the situation in the Near and Middle East caused by the signing of the Turkish-Iraqi treaty, and believes that his trip is another American attempt to drag other Arab countries into this treaty. The reporter also reported that the United States is in discussions with Britain, wanting to "persuade Eden to visit the Near and Middle Eastern countries at the same time as Dulles."

### 杜勒斯准备去中东活动

1955-03-26
第4版()
专栏：

    杜勒斯准备去中东活动
    【新华社二十五日讯】塔斯社开罗二十五日讯：据埃及“消息报”驻华盛顿记者自美国政界人士方面得到的消息说，杜勒斯准备在最近访问中近东国家，“以便了解这一地区的新局势”。
    这里的新闻界人士认为杜勒斯所计划的旅行同土耳其—伊拉克条约的签订而引起的中近东局势尖锐化有关，并且认为他的旅行是美国又一次企图把其他阿拉伯国家拖进这个条约。记者还报道，美国正在和英国进行商谈，想“说服艾登同杜勒斯同时去访问中近东国家”。
