Title: Establishment of Our Commercial Agency in Kalimpong
Author:
Date: 1955-03-29
Page: 1

    Establishment of Our Commercial Agency in Kalimpong
    【Xinhua News Agency, New Delhi, 27th】 In accordance with the provisions of the Sino-Indian Agreement on Trade and Communications between the Tibet Region of China and India in 1954, the Commercial Agency of the People's Republic of China in Kalimpong was formally established on March 25.

### 我驻噶伦堡商务代理处成立

1955-03-29
第1版()
专栏：

    我驻噶伦堡商务代理处成立
    【新华社新德里二十七日电】根据一九五四年中印两国关于中国西藏地方和印度之间的通商和交通协定的规定，中华人民共和国驻噶伦堡商务代理处已经在三月二十五日正式成立。
