Our Great Motherland
Of the 297 first graduates of the Marxist-Leninist Evening University attached to the People's University of China, over 88 percent of the students achieved scores of five and four in all their exams. These graduates are excitedly viewing their newly received graduation certificates.

### 我们伟大的祖国（图片）

1955-03-31
第1版()
专栏：

    我们伟大的祖国
    中国人民大学附设马克思列宁主义夜大学的二百九十七名第一届毕业生，有百分之八十八以上学员的各课考试成绩获得了五分和四分。这几位毕业生正以兴奋的心情相互观看刚领到的毕业证书。
