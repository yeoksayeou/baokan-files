Title: Nam Il's Statement on Japan Relations Evokes Widespread Response in Japan
Author:
Date: 1955-03-02
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Tokyo, 28th—Nam Il, Minister of Foreign Affairs of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea, issued a statement on February 25th suggesting the normalization of relations between the Democratic People's Republic of Korea and Japan, which has provoked a widespread response in Japan. Tokyo Radio rebroadcast a recording and Japanese translation of the statement one hour after Pyongyang Radio broadcast it. Most Japanese newspapers published the full text of Nam Il's statement in their evening editions on the same day.

According to reports, on the afternoon of the day Nam Il made the statement, Japanese Prime Minister Ichiro Hatoyama said, "Our government is willing to respond to the desires expressed on the other side." Hatoyama also said, "My policy of conducting cultural and economic exchanges with all countries has not changed in the slightest." Japanese Foreign Minister Mamoru Shigemitsu said, "There are no objections to the Nam Il statement itself."

Tadao Kudo, Director of the Foreign Affairs Department of the Japanese Red Cross, said that establishing economic and cultural relations with the Democratic People's Republic of Korea would be of great benefit to Japan.

Lee Ho-ran, Chairman of the Democratic United Front of Korean Residents in Japan, representing the 500,000 Korean residents in Japan, warmly welcomed Foreign Minister Nam Il's statement. He said this was "proof of a genuine desire to ease the current international tensions."

### 南日的对日关系声明在日本引起广泛反应

1955-03-02
第4版()
专栏：

    南日的对日关系声明在日本引起广泛反应
    【新华社二十八日讯】东京消息：朝鲜民主主义人民共和国外务相南日在二月二十五日发表的建议使朝鲜民主主义人民共和国和日本的关系正常化的声明，在日本引起了广泛的反应。东京电台在平壤电台广播这个声明一小时以后重播了这个声明的录音和日语译文。大多数日本报纸都在当天晚刊上刊载了南日声明的全文。
    据消息报道，在南日发表声明的当天下午，日本首相鸠山一郎说：“我国政府愿意答复另一方面表示的愿望。”鸠山又说：“我所主张的同所有国家进行文化和经济交流的政策丝毫未变。”日本外务相重光葵说：“对于南日声明本身，并没有什么异议”。
    日本红十字会外事部长工藤忠夫说：同朝鲜民主主义人民共和国建立经济和文化关系，对日本将有很大好处。
    旅日朝鲜侨民民主统一战线主席李浩然代表住在日本的五十万朝鲜侨民，热烈欢迎南日外务相的声明。他说，这是“证明希望缓和目前国际紧张局势的真正证据”。
