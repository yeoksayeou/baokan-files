Title: China Presents Six Hundred Handicrafts to Germany
Author:
Date: 1955-03-26
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, Berlin, March 25th: Wang Yutian, Chargé d'Affaires ad interim of the Embassy of the People's Republic of China in the German Democratic Republic, presented approximately six hundred Chinese handicrafts to the German Association for Foreign Cultural Relations in Berlin on the 23rd. These handicrafts were previously displayed at the Chinese Handicraft Exhibition in Berlin and included ivory carvings, pottery, lacquerware, and woven goods.

Professor Robert Alt, representing the German Association for Foreign Cultural Relations, expressed his gratitude for China's gift and stated that the association will continue to organize exhibitions to introduce Chinese handicrafts to the German people in order to deepen the friendship between the Chinese and German peoples.

### 我国赠给德国六百件工艺品

1955-03-26
第1版()
专栏：

    我国赠给德国六百件工艺品
    【新华社柏林二十五日讯】中华人民共和国驻德意志民主共和国大使馆临时代办王雨田二十三日在柏林把约六百件中国工艺品赠送给德国对外文化联络协会。这些工艺品曾在柏林举行的中国工艺品展览会上展出过，其中包括象牙雕刻、陶器、漆器和编织品等。
    罗伯特·阿尔特教授代表德国对外文化联络协会感谢中国赠送这些礼品，并表示协会将继续组织展览会，向德国人民介绍中国的工艺品，以加深中德两国人民之间的友谊。
