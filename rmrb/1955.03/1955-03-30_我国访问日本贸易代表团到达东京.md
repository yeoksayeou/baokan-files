Title: Chinese Trade Delegation Arrives in Tokyo

Author:

Date: 1955-03-30
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Tokyo, March 29th: The Chinese Trade Delegation to Japan, led by delegation head Lei Renmin, arrived in Tokyo on the afternoon of the 29th, invited by the Japan International Trade Promotion Association and the Diet Members' League for Promoting Japan-China Trade.

Those who welcomed the delegation at the airport included: Murata Shozo, President of the Japan International Trade Promotion Association; Ikeda Masanosuke, Standing Director of the Diet Members' League for Promoting Japan-China Trade; as well as Tajima Masao, Vice President of the Japan International Trade Promotion Association, and other leaders such as Yamamoto Kumaichi, Suzuki Kazuo, and Uda Koichi.

Also welcoming were: Sugiyama Motojiro, Vice Speaker of the House of Representatives; Sasaki Kozo, Nakamura Koichi, Hozumi Shichiro, and Hoashi Kei, members of the House of Representatives; Kinoshita Gengo, Takara Tomi, Sudo Goro, and Hori Makoto, members of the House of Councillors; and Makida Yogichi, Speaker of the Hokkaido Prefectural Assembly.

Also welcoming were: Matsumoto Jiichiro, President of the Japan-China Friendship Association; Uchiyama Kanzo, Vice President; Shimazu Tadanari, President of the Japanese Red Cross Society; and Hirano Yoshitaro, Secretary-General of the Japan Peace Committee.

Domnitsky, head of the Soviet delegation in Tokyo, also came to the airport to welcome the delegation. Representatives from Japanese business circles, trade unions, farmers' organizations, as well as overseas Chinese and Korean residents in Japan, also came to the airport to welcome the delegation. In total, more than 350 people came to welcome the delegation.

After shaking hands with the welcomers and receiving bouquets at the airport, delegation head Lei Renmin made a radio address, saying: "The Chinese Trade Delegation to Japan is very pleased to come to your country at the invitation of the Japan International Trade Promotion Association and the Diet Members' League for Promoting Japan-China Trade. The purpose of our visit is to promote the further development of economic exchange between China and Japan and to enhance mutual understanding and friendship between the peoples of China and Japan. Here, I would like to express my warm greetings to Mr. Murata Shozo, President of the Japan International Trade Promotion Association, Mr. Ikeda Masanosuke, Standing Director of the Diet Members' League for Promoting Japan-China Trade, and friends from all walks of life who are enthusiastic about promoting China-Japan trade, as well as the citizens of Tokyo and the people of Japan."

### 我国访问日本贸易代表团到达东京

1955-03-30
第1版()
专栏：

    我国访问日本贸易代表团到达东京
   【新华社东京二十九日电】中国访问日本贸易代表团应日本国际贸易促进协会和日本国会议员促进日中贸易联盟的邀请，在团长雷任民率领下，在二十九日下午到达东京。
    到机场欢迎的有：日本国际贸易促进协会会长村田省藏、日本国会议员促进日中贸易联盟常任理事池田正之辅以及日本国际贸易促进协会副会长田岛正雄和其他负责人山本熊一、铃木一雄、宇田耕一等人。
    欢迎的还有：日本众议院副议长杉山元治郎，众议院议员佐佐木更三、中村高一、穗积七郎、帆足计，参议院议员木下源吾、高良富、须藤五郎、堀真琴，北海道议会议长莳田余吉等人。
    欢迎的还有：日中友好协会会长松本治一郎，副会长内山完造，日本红十字会会长岛津忠承，日本拥护和平委员会总书记平野义太郎等人。
    苏联驻东京代表团团长多穆尼茨基亦到机场欢迎。到机场欢迎的还有：日本企业界、工会组织、农民组织以及旅日华侨和朝鲜侨民的代表等人。到机场欢迎的共有三百五十多人。
    雷团长在机场和欢迎者一一握手并接受献花后，发表广播谈话说：中国访问日本贸易代表团应日本国际贸易促进协会和日本国会议员促进日中贸易联盟的邀请，来到贵国，感到非常愉快。我们这次访问的目的是为促进中、日两国间经济交流的进一步发展，并为增进中、日两国人民的相互了解和友谊。在此我特向日本国际贸易促进协会会长村田省藏先生、国会议员促进日中贸易联盟常任理事池田正之辅先生，和热心于促进中、日贸易的各界朋友们，东京的市民和全日本人民热烈地致意。
