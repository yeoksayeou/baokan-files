Title: Restructuring of the Japanese Communist Party Central Guidance Department Meeting
Author:
Date: 1955-03-17
Page: 4
    Xinhua News Agency, Tokyo, 16th. The Central Guidance Department of the Japanese Communist Party announced in a press conference in Tokyo on the 15th that the Central Guidance Department had been reorganized based on the decision of its meeting on the 14th.
    The statement said: In order to adapt to the new situation after the general election and strengthen the leading organization and various specialized agencies, the Central Guidance Department decided to reduce its eight members to four, and the other four original members will be responsible for leading the newly established specialized agencies.
    The new Central Guidance Department will continue to be chaired by Masami Kasuga, and the members are Yoshio Shiga, Kenji Miyamoto, and Toru Yonehara.
    The statement stated that the Japanese Communist Party will work to strengthen the unity of democratic forces in the future in accordance with its election platform.

### 日本共产党中央指导部举行会议进行改组

1955-03-17
第4版()
专栏：

    日本共产党中央指导部举行会议进行改组
    【新华社十六日讯】东京消息：日本共产党中央指导部十五日在东京举行的记者招待会上发表声明宣布，中央指导部根据它在十四日举行的会议的决定，进行了改组。
    声明说：为了适应大选后的新形势，加强领导组织和各专门机构，中央指导部决定把它的八个委员减少到四个，其他原任的四个委员将负责领导新设立的专门机构。
    新的中央指导部继续由春日正一担任主席，委员是志贺义雄、宫本显治和米原昶。
    声明表示：日本共产党今后将根据竞选纲领，为加强团结民主力量而努力。
