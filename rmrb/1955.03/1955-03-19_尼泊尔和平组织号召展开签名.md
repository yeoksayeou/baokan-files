Title: Nepalese Peace Organization Calls for Signature Drive
Author:
Date: 1955-03-19
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, New Delhi, March 17th: A dispatch from Kathmandu: The All-Nepal Peace Committee recently issued a statement appealing to the Nepalese people and all parties to actively participate in the signature campaign against the use of atomic weapons.

The statement pointed out that warmongers are eager to realize their desire to launch a world war, and they are accelerating the manufacture of atomic weapons. The statement said that the Nepalese people have consistently supported the peace movement, and the Nepalese people will do a good job in the signature campaign against the use of atomic weapons.

### 尼泊尔和平组织号召展开签名

1955-03-19
第4版()
专栏：

    尼泊尔和平组织号召展开签名
    【新华社新德里十七日电】　加德满都消息：全尼泊尔和平委员会最近发表声明，吁请尼泊尔人民和各党派踊跃参加反对使用原子武器的签名运动。
    声明指出，战争贩子们正急于实现他们的发动世界大战的愿望，他们正在加速制造原子武器。声明说，尼泊尔人民一贯是支持和平运动的，尼泊尔人民将把反对使用原子武器的签名运动做好。
