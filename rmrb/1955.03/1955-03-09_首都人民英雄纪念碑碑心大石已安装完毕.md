Title: The Core Stone of the People's Heroes Monument in the Capital Has Been Installed
Author:
Date: 1955-03-09
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, March 8th: The core stone of the People's Heroes Monument in the capital was installed on the 6th and 7th. This core stone, which is 14.4 meters high, will have the eight characters "人民英雄永垂不朽" (Eternal Glory to the People's Heroes) inscribed on it, as written by Chairman Mao.

Prior to the installation, workers and technicians spent nearly a year processing the stone, designing the installation plan, testing equipment, and studying techniques. Soviet experts in Beijing and Chinese experts provided great assistance to this installation, ensuring the safe completion of the installation task.

In mid-March, the overall installation of the monument's stone materials will begin. All workers, designers, and engineers have expressed their determination to strive for the early completion of the monument's construction.

### 首都人民英雄纪念碑碑心大石已安装完毕

1955-03-09
第1版()
专栏：

    首都人民英雄纪念碑碑心大石已安装完毕
    【新华社八日讯】首都人民英雄纪念碑碑心大石在六、七两日安装完毕。这块高达十四点四公尺的碑心石上，将雕刻着毛主席题的：“人民英雄永垂不朽”八个大字。
    在安装前，工人和技术人员们进行了将近一年的石料加工、安装方案设计、设备试验和技术学习，在京苏联专家和中国的专家对这次安装给了大力的帮助，保证了安装任务的安全完成。
    三月中旬，首都人民英雄纪念碑将全面开始安装碑身石料，全体工人、设计师和工程师们都表示要争取早日完成建碑工作。
