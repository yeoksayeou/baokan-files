Title: Film "Silver-Gray Powder" to be Shown
Author:
Date: 1955-03-18
Page: 3

    Film "Silver-Gray Powder" to be Shown
    The Soviet color feature film "Silver-Gray Powder" will be released in ten cities including Beijing, Tianjin, Shanghai, Shenyang, Wuhan, Chongqing, and Xi'an starting in late March, and will be shown successively throughout the country starting in April.
    The film depicts an American chemistry Ph.D. who develops a murderous weapon with a strong radioactive toxin - "silver-gray powder," with which American warmongers fantasize about using it as a trump card to conquer the world. However, due to contradictions between American consortia and the opposition of peace-loving American people, the "silver-gray powder" and its inventor are destroyed, and the warmongers' wishful thinking is also shattered. (According to Xinhua News Agency report)

### 影片“银灰色的粉末”将放映

1955-03-18
第3版()
专栏：

    影片“银灰色的粉末”将放映
    苏联彩色故事影片“银灰色的粉末”三月下旬起将在北京、天津、上海、沈阳、武汉、重庆、西安等十个城市上映，四月份起将陆续在全国各地放映。
    影片描写美国一个化学博士，研究出一种具有强烈放射性毒素的杀人武器——“银灰色的粉末”，美国的战争贩子妄想以此作为征服世界的王牌。但是由于美国财团之间的矛盾和爱好和平的美国人民的反对，“银灰色的粉末”和它的发明者都毁灭了，战争贩子的妄想也破灭了。（据新华社讯）
