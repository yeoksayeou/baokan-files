Title: The Second Session of the Second Great People's Hural of Mongolia Closes: Endorsing the Declaration of the Supreme Soviet of the Soviet Union and Adopting this Year's National Budget
Author:
Date: 1955-03-10
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Ulaanbaatar, March 9th: The Second Session of the Second Great People's Hural of the Mongolian People's Republic closed on March 4th. The meeting unanimously adopted the resolution endorsing the declaration of the Supreme Soviet of the Soviet Union and the 1955 Mongolian National Budget Bill.

The 1955 Mongolian National Budget stipulates that the total annual revenue and total expenditure are each 507 million tugriks (Mongolian currency). The total revenue of the 1955 National Budget increased by 13.2 percent compared to the actual revenue of 1954, and the total expenditure increased by 9.7 percent compared to the actual expenditure of 1954.

In the 1955 National Budget, the income from socialist enterprises and institutions accounts for 82 percent of the total budget revenue, an increase of 40.6 million tugriks compared to 1954. The amount of revenue from taxation in the budget remains the same as in 1954, but its proportion in the total budget revenue this year has been reduced to 16.7 percent.

In the 1955 National Budget, investment in the national economy accounts for 33 percent of the total budget expenditure, an increase of 35 million tugriks compared to last year. Among them, 43.2 million tugriks are used for the development of agriculture and animal husbandry, 39.8 million tugriks are used for the development of industry, and 57.7 million tugriks are used for the development of transportation and telecommunications (of which 40 million tugriks is the Mongolian side's investment in the construction of the Ulaanbaatar-Zamyn-Üüd railway). The appropriation for social and cultural facilities in the 1955 National Budget accounts for 35.4 percent of the total budget expenditure, an increase of 20.6 million tugriks compared to last year. In order to meet the needs of large-scale economic construction plans, the 1955 National Budget stipulates an appropriation of 114.5 million tugriks as investment in various capital construction projects, an increase of 29.1 million tugriks compared to last year.

### 蒙古第二届大人民呼拉尔第二次会议闭幕  拥护苏联最高苏维埃宣言并通过今年国家预算

1955-03-10
第4版()
专栏：

    蒙古第二届大人民呼拉尔第二次会议闭幕
    拥护苏联最高苏维埃宣言并通过今年国家预算
    【新华社九日讯】乌兰巴托消息：蒙古人民共和国第二届大人民呼拉尔第二次会议在三月四日闭幕。会议一致通过了拥护苏联最高苏维埃的宣言的决议和一九五五年度蒙古国家预算法案。
    蒙古一九五五年度国家预算规定全年的总收入和总支出各为五亿零七百万图格里克（蒙币）。一九五五年度国家预算的总收入比一九五四年度的实际收入增加百分之十三点二，总支出比一九五四年度实际支出增加百分之九点七。
    一九五五年度国家预算规定的收入来自社会主义成分企业机构的占全部预算收入的百分之八十二，比一九五四年度增加四千零六十万图格里克。预算收入中来自税收部分的金额仍和一九五四年度一样，但在今年度全部预算收入中所占的比重已减为百分之十六点七。
    在一九五五年度国家预算中，用于国民经济的投资占预算总支出的百分之三十三，比去年度增加了三千五百万图格里克。其中用于发展农牧业的为四千三百二十万图格里克，用于发展工业的为三千九百八十万图格里克，用于发展交通电讯业的为五千七百七十万图格里克（其中四千万图格里克是建设乌兰巴托—扎木乌得铁路的蒙古方面的投资）。一九五五年度国家预算在社会文化设施方面的拨款占预算总支出的百分之三十五点四，比去年度增加二千零六十万图格里克。为了适应大规模经济建设计划的需要，一九五五年度国家预算规定拨款一亿一千四百五十万图格里克作为各项基本建设工程的投资，比去年增加二千九百一十万图格里克。
