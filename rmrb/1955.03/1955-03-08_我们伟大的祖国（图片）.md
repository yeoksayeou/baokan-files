    Our Great Motherland
    New Chinese women are working hard to learn technology and constantly improve their cultural level, to make more contributions to the socialist industrialization construction of the motherland. This is a second-year female student majoring in metal cutting and processing at the metal cutting machine tool department of Beijing Machine Manufacturing School, studying homework in the practice factory.

### 我们伟大的祖国（图片）

1955-03-08
第1版()
专栏：

    我们伟大的祖国
    新中国妇女正在努力学习技术和不断地提高文化水平，要为祖国社会主义工业化建设作更多的贡献。这是北京机器制造学校金属切削机床科金属切削加工专业二年级女生正在实习工厂研究作业。
