Date: 1955-03-15

    Our Great Motherland
    Deng Fangzhi, the mother of Huang Jiguang, the special-class hero of the Chinese People's Volunteer Army, sent her youngest son, Ji Shu, to enlist in the People's Liberation Army. When Huang Ji Shu joined the army, his mother, Huang, encouraged him to be a good soldier of the people, to learn from his brother Ji Guang, and to strive for meritorious service and become a hero.

### 我们伟大的祖国（图片）

1955-03-15
第1版()
专栏：

    我们伟大的祖国
    中国人民志愿军特级英雄黄继光烈士的母亲邓芳芝，送她最小的儿子继恕应征参加了人民解放军。在黄继恕入伍时，黄妈妈勉励他，要他做个人民的好战士，向继光哥哥学习，争取立功当英雄。
