Title: Chairman of the Indonesian Nationalist Party Says Asian-African Conference Will Help Reduce the Danger of War, Iran Decides to Attend Asian-African Conference
Author:
Date: 1955-03-23
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Jakarta, March 22nd: According to a report in the Indonesian newspaper "Sin Po," the chairman of the Indonesian Nationalist Party, Zoeyosukarto, said on the 18th that the upcoming Asian-African Conference would help reduce the danger of war. Asian and African countries support world peace and renounce war. He said that anyone who wants to launch a war must first consider the Asian and African people's desire for peace.

Zoeyosukarto also said that the Indonesian Nationalist Party will support every effort to strive for the destruction of atomic weapons and other weapons of mass destruction. He expressed satisfaction with the progress made by the worldwide movement against preparing for atomic war. He said that atomic energy can and must be used for the benefit of mankind.

Xinhua News Agency, Jakarta, March 21st: The Joint Secretariat of the Asian-African Conference announced that it has received a formal reply from the Iranian government, stating that Iran has decided to participate in the Asian-African Conference.

### 印度尼西亚国民党主席说  亚非会议有助于减少战争危险  伊朗决定参加亚非会议

1955-03-23
第1版()
专栏：

    印度尼西亚国民党主席说
    亚非会议有助于减少战争危险
    伊朗决定参加亚非会议
    【新华社雅加达二十二日电】据印度尼西亚“新报”报道，印度尼西亚国民党主席佐约苏卡托十八日说，行将召开的亚非会议将有助于减少战争危险。亚非国家是支持世界和平和弃绝战争的。他说，谁要发动战争，谁就必须先考虑亚洲和非洲人民爱好和平的愿望。
    佐约苏卡托又说，印度尼西亚国民党将支持争取销毁原子武器和其他大规模毁灭性武器的每一个努力。他对全世界反对准备原子战争的运动所获得的进展表示满意。他说，原子能能够而且必须用于造福人类。
    【新华社雅加达二十一日电】亚非会议联合秘书处宣布，已经收到伊朗政府的正式答复，表示伊朗已决定参加亚非会议。
