Title: North Korean Delegation to Asian Countries Conference Leaves for India
Author: 
Date: 1955-03-16
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Pyongyang, March 15th: The delegation of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea, headed by Park Jung-ae, to the Asian Countries Conference, left Pyongyang by special train at 11:00 am on the 15th for Beijing en route to India, to attend the Asian Countries Conference to be held in New Delhi on April 6th.

Those who went to the station to see them off included the heads of the Korean National Peace Committee and various political parties and social organizations.

### 朝鲜出席亚洲国家会议代表团去印度

1955-03-16
第1版()
专栏：

    朝鲜出席亚洲国家会议代表团去印度
    【新华社平壤十五日电】出席亚洲国家会议的朝鲜民主主义人民共和国代表团在团长朴正爱率领下，于十五日上午十一时乘专车去北京转印度，参加即将于四月六日在新德里举行的亚洲国家会议。
    前往车站欢送的有朝鲜拥护和平全国民族委员会和各政党以及社会团体的负责人等。
