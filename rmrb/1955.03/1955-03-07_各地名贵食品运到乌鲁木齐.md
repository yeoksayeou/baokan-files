Title: Precious Foods from Various Regions Shipped to Urumqi
Author:
Date: 1955-03-07
Page: 2

In the food departments of the state-owned trading companies and supply and marketing cooperatives in Urumqi, Xinjiang Province, many customers of all ethnic groups now come every day to buy their favorite Longkou vermicelli, Guangzhou tangerines, dried lychees, Shaanxi persimmon cakes, Tianjin candied lotus root slices, dried prawns, Changsha cured meat, Sichuan pickled mustard tuber, dried longan, Fujian seaweed, and other famous local and special products from all over the country. Customers are very satisfied that state-owned commercial departments have brought in these precious foods that were rarely available or never seen before the liberation.

Recently, sixty to seventy tons of cured meat, persimmon cakes, and other foods are about to arrive in Urumqi. (Xinhua News Agency)

### 各地名贵食品运到乌鲁木齐

1955-03-07
第2版()
专栏：

    各地名贵食品运到乌鲁木齐
    在新疆省乌鲁木齐市国营贸易公司、供销合作社食品门市部里，现在每天都有许多各族男女顾客，购买他们喜爱的龙口粉丝，广州柑子、荔枝干，陕西柿饼，天津糖藕片、干对虾，长沙腊肉，四川榨菜、桂元肉，福建紫菜等全国驰名的土产品和特产品。顾客们都非常满意国营商业部门运来这些在解放前很少能够买到或从来没有看到过的珍贵食品。
    最近，六、七十吨腊肉、柿饼等食品将要运达乌鲁木齐。　（新华社）
