Title: Chinese Academy of Sciences Undertakes Soil Research

Author: 
Date: 1955-03-18
Page: 3

    The Forestry and Soil Research Institute of the Chinese Academy of Sciences, in coordination with the Ministry of Agriculture's task of surveying 100 million mu of wasteland within three years, has decided to begin surveying and researching wasteland soil and other natural conditions in Heilongjiang Province this year. The goal is to accurately assess the soil productivity of wasteland in Heilongjiang Province and provide a scientific basis for the utilization and development of wasteland in Heilongjiang Province.
    The Forestry and Soil Research Institute plans to complete the study of approximately 2 million hectares of wasteland soil in eastern and central Heilongjiang Province this year, produce a 1:500,000 soil sketch map, determine the soil types and agricultural utilization value in this area, and complete research on the basic properties of some soil types.

### 中国科学院进行土壤研究工作

1955-03-18
第3版()
专栏：

    中国科学院进行土壤研究工作
    中国科学院林业土壤研究所，为配合农业部提出的三年内勘测荒地一亿亩的任务，决定从今年开始在黑龙江省进行荒地土壤和其他自然条件的调查研究工作，以正确估计黑龙江省荒地的土壤生产力，提出黑龙江省荒地利用开发的科学根据。
    林业土壤研究所计划在今年将可完成黑龙江省东部和中部约二百万公顷荒地土壤的研究，并制成五十万分一土壤草图，确定这个区域中土壤类型及农业利用价值，并完成一部分各土种基本性质的研究。
