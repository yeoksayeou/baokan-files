Title: The Second Session of the Second Great People's Hural of Mongolia Opens

Author:
Date: 1955-03-05
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Ulan Bator: According to a TASS dispatch from Ulan Bator, the second session of the Second Great People's Hural of the Mongolian People's Republic opened in Ulan Bator on the 3rd.

The meeting adopted the following agenda: (1) Discussion of the declaration of the Supreme Soviet of the Soviet Union. (2) Adoption of the national budget of the Mongolian People's Republic for 1955. (3) Approval of the decrees of the Presidium of the Great People's Hural.

Representative Zedib made a report on the first item on the agenda. The representatives engaged in discussions, all expressing their warm support for the Soviet Union's peaceful policies and condemning the aggressive plans of the American imperialists. The meeting unanimously endorsed the declaration of the Supreme Soviet of the Soviet Union and passed a corresponding resolution.

Next, the representatives began to discuss the second item on the agenda. Molomzhamts, Minister of Finance of the Mongolian People's Republic, delivered a report on the national budget of the Mongolian People's Republic.

### 蒙古第二届大人民呼拉尔第二次会议开幕

1955-03-05
第4版()
专栏：

    蒙古第二届大人民呼拉尔第二次会议开幕
    【新华社四日讯】　据塔斯社乌兰巴托讯：蒙古人民共和国第二届大人民呼拉尔第二次会议三日在乌兰巴托开幕。
    会议通过了下列议程：（一）讨论苏联最高苏维埃的宣言。（二）通过蒙古人民共和国一九五五年的国家预算。（三）批准大人民呼拉尔主席团的各项命令。
    泽狄布代表就第一项议程作了报告。代表们展开讨论，都表示热烈拥护苏联的和平政策，并且谴责了美帝国主义者的侵略计划。会议一致赞同苏联最高苏维埃的宣言，并且通过了相应的决议。
    接着，代表们开始讨论第二项议程。蒙古人民共和国财政部长莫洛姆然茨代表作了蒙古人民共和国国家预算的报告。
