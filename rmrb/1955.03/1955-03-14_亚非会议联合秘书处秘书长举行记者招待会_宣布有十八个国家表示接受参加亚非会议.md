Title: Secretary-General of the Joint Secretariat of the Asian-African Conference Holds Press Conference, Announcing That Eighteen Countries Have Indicated Acceptance of Invitation to the Asian-African Conference
Author:
Date: 1955-03-14
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, Jakarta, March 13th. The Secretary-General of the Joint Secretariat of the Asian-African Conference, Ruslan Abdulgani, announced at a press conference held in Jakarta on the 12th that eighteen of the twenty-five countries invited to the Asian-African Conference have formally accepted the invitation. These countries are: Thailand, Libya, Liberia, the People's Republic of China, Afghanistan, Ethiopia, the Democratic Republic of Vietnam, Iraq, Egypt, Sudan, Syria, Saudi Arabia, Yemen, Nepal, Japan, Laos, Cambodia, and South Vietnam.

Abdulgani said that the Prime Minister of the Central African Federation, Huggins, had written to the Prime Minister of Indonesia, Sastroamidjojo, stating that the Central African Federation could not attend the Asian-African Conference due to "circumstances."

Abdulgani said that formal replies had not yet been received from the Gold Coast, Iran, Jordan, the Philippines, Turkey, and Lebanon, but some of these countries had already stated that they would accept the invitation to attend the Asian-African Conference.

### 亚非会议联合秘书处秘书长举行记者招待会  宣布有十八个国家表示接受参加亚非会议

1955-03-14
第1版()
专栏：

    亚非会议联合秘书处秘书长举行记者招待会
    宣布有十八个国家表示接受参加亚非会议
    【新华社雅加达十三日电】亚非会议联合秘书处秘书长鲁斯兰·阿卜杜加尼十二日在雅加达举行的记者招待会上宣布，在被邀请参加亚非会议的二十五个国家中已经有十八个国家正式表示接受邀请，这些国家是：泰国、利比亚、利比里亚、中华人民共和国、阿富汗、埃塞俄比亚、越南民主共和国、伊拉克、埃及、苏丹、叙利亚、沙特阿拉伯、也门、尼泊尔、日本、老挝、柬埔寨和南越。
    阿卜杜加尼说，中非联邦总理赫金斯已经写信给印度尼西亚总理沙斯特罗阿米佐约，表示中非联邦由于“环境”的缘故，不能够参加亚非会议。
    阿卜杜加尼说，还没有收到黄金海岸、伊朗、约旦、菲律宾、土耳其和黎巴嫩的正式答复，但是其中某些国家已经声明表示它们将接受参加亚非会议的邀请。
