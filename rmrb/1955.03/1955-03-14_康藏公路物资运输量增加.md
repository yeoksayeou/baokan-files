Title: Increased Freight Volume on the Kangding-Tibet Highway
Author:
Date: 1955-03-14
Page: 2

Xinhua News Agency

The freight volume on the Kangding-Tibet Highway has been increasing since the highway was fully opened to traffic more than two months ago. According to the vehicle registration records of the Kangding Transportation Management Station in the Tibetan Autonomous Prefecture of Xikang Province, the total amount of rice, tea, general merchandise, and construction materials transported from the interior to the Tibetan Plateau via the highway from January to February this year increased by 20% compared to the same period last year; the amount of native and special products such as wool, cowhide, and medicinal materials transported to the interior via this highway increased by 136%; and the number of vehicles traveling directly from the interior to Lhasa in February increased by more than 150% compared to January.

### 康藏公路物资运输量增加

1955-03-14
第2版()
专栏：

    康藏公路物资运输量增加
    康藏公路全线通车以后两个多月来，物资运输量一天天增加。据西康省藏族自治州康定交通管理站车辆登记册上的记载表明：今年一月到二月由内地运到康藏高原上的大米、茶叶、百货和建筑器材等物资总量，比去年同时期增加了百分之二十；经这条公路运往内地的羊毛、牛皮和药材等土产、特产品增加了百分之一百三十六；由内地直驶拉萨的车辆次数，二月份比一月份增加了一倍半还多。（新华社）
