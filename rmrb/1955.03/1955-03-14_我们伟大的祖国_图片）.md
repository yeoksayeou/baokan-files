    Our Great Motherland
    Women in New China are actively participating in the socialist industrial construction of the motherland. On the construction site of the First Automobile Manufacturing Plant, thirty-six young female drivers emerged in just one year. Now they are able to master various operations such as mechanical excavation and mechanical hoisting.

### 我们伟大的祖国(图片）

1955-03-14
第1版()
专栏：

    我们伟大的祖国
    新中国妇女积极参加祖国社会主义工业建设。在第一汽车制造厂的工地上，一年时间内就涌现出三十六名青年女司机。现在她们已能掌握机械挖土和机械吊装等各种不同的作业。
