Title: State Council Appoints Personnel to the Central Institute for Nationalities
Author:
Date: 1955-03-18
Page: 3

    State Council Appoints Personnel to the Central Institute for Nationalities
    The State Council of the People's Republic of China issued an order on March 9, appointing personnel to the Central Institute for Nationalities. The list is as follows:
    Liu Geping as President of the Central Institute for Nationalities, Su Keqin and Xiong Shouqi as Vice Presidents of the Central Institute for Nationalities.

### 国务院任命中央民族学院的工作人员

1955-03-18
第3版()
专栏：

    国务院任命中央民族学院的工作人员
    中华人民共和国国务院三月九日发布命令，任命了中央民族学院的工作人员，名单如下：
    刘格平为中央民族学院院长，苏克勤、熊寿祺为中央民族学院副院长。
