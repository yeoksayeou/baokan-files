Title: French National Assembly Honorary President Herriot Advocates Establishing Connections Between National Parliaments

Author:
Date: 1955-03-30
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, March 28th: The latest issue of "Spark" magazine published a letter from Édouard Herriot, Honorary President of the French National Assembly, to its editor-in-chief. "Spark" magazine had requested several Western European parliamentarians to express their opinions on the Soviet Supreme Soviet's declaration of February 9, 1955, regarding the establishment of direct contacts between national parliaments and the exchange of parliamentary delegations. In his letter, Herriot expressed his support for this proposal.

### 法国国民议会名誉议长赫里欧  赞成各国议会间建立联系

1955-03-30
第4版()
专栏：

    法国国民议会名誉议长赫里欧
    赞成各国议会间建立联系
    【新华社二十九日讯】据塔斯社莫斯科二十八日讯：最近一期的“星火”杂志发表了法国国民议会名誉议长爱德华·赫里欧给它的总编辑的信。“星火”杂志曾要求几位西欧的议会议员就苏联最高苏维埃一九五五年二月九日的宣言中所提出的关于建立各国议会之间的直接联系和互派议会代表团的问题发表他们的意见。赫里欧在他的信里表示赞成这个建议。
