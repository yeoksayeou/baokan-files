Title: Cui Yongjian Hosts Banquet to See Off Representatives of the Returning Chinese People's Volunteer Army

Author:
Date: 1955-03-31
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Pyongyang, March 30th. Cui Yongjian, Vice Premier of the Cabinet and Minister of National Defense of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea, held a grand banquet in Pyongyang on the evening of the 29th to bid farewell to the representatives of the returning Chinese People's Volunteer Army.

On the Korean side, those present at the banquet included Kim Tu-bong, Chairman of the Standing Committee of the Supreme People's Assembly; Park Kim-chul, Director of the Organizational and Guidance Department of the Central Committee of the Korean Workers' Party; Ha Yang-cheon, Director of the Social Science Department; Chairmen of the Presidium of the Central Committee of the Democratic Front for the Reunification of the Fatherland; heads of various democratic political parties and social organizations in Korea; senior generals of the Korean People's Army; Heroes of the Republic; and figures from the Korean cultural, artistic, and journalistic circles.

Commanders and combatants representing the returning Chinese People's Volunteer Army attended the banquet. General Wang Yunrui, Deputy Chief of Staff of the Chinese People's Volunteer Army, and Li Shi, Charge d'Affaires ad interim of the Chinese Embassy in Korea, were also invited to attend.

Also invited to the banquet were Soviet Ambassador to Korea, Mr. Suzdalev, and diplomatic envoys from various countries.

Vice-Marshal Cui Yongjian delivered a speech at the beginning of the banquet, praising the immortal achievements of the officers and soldiers of the returning Chinese People's Volunteer Army in Korea with their high internationalist spirit, and wishing them greater success in the struggle to peacefully build their own country.

Chen Yizhen, the representative of the returning Chinese People's Volunteer Army, raised his glass and said that on behalf of all commanders and combatants of the returning troops, he expressed his heartfelt gratitude to the Korean people for their constant love and support, and wished the Korean people more brilliant victories in the struggle for the restoration and construction of the national economy and the peaceful reunification of the fatherland.

The banquet participants frequently raised their glasses to the unbreakable friendship between the peoples of Korea and China, to the friendly unity of the peace, democracy, and socialist camp headed by the Soviet Union, and to the victory of the great cause of peace.

The banquet proceeded throughout in an atmosphere full of friendship and solidarity.

### 崔庸健设宴为我志愿军归国部队代表饯行

1955-03-31
第1版()
专栏：

    崔庸健设宴为我志愿军归国部队代表饯行
    新华社平壤三十日电　朝鲜民主主义人民共和国内阁副首相兼民族保卫相崔庸健次帅二十九日晚在平壤市举行盛大宴会，为中国人民志愿军归国部队代表饯行。
    朝鲜方面出席宴会的有最高人民会议常任委员会委员长金枓奉，朝鲜劳动党中央委员会组织指导部长朴金喆、社会科学部长河仰天，朝鲜祖国统一民主主义战线中央委员会议长团议长们，朝鲜各民主政党、社会团体负责人，朝鲜人民军高级将领和共和国英雄和朝鲜文化、艺术、新闻界人士等。
    中国人民志愿军归国部队指挥员、战斗员代表们出席了宴会，中国人民志愿军副参谋长王蕴瑞将军和中国驻朝鲜大使馆临时代办李石也应邀出席。
    应邀出席宴会的还有苏联驻朝鲜大使苏兹达列夫和各国外交使节。
    崔庸健次帅在宴会开始时致词，他赞扬了中国人民志愿军归国部队将士们以高度国际主义精神在朝鲜所建立的不朽功勋，并预祝他们在和平建设自己祖国的斗争中取得更大的成就。
    中国人民志愿军归国部队代表陈一震举杯说，他代表归国部队全体指挥员、战斗员向朝鲜人民经常给予的热爱和支援表示由衷的感谢，并祝朝鲜人民在为国民经济的恢复建设和祖国的和平统一的斗争中获得更光辉的胜利。
    宴会参加者频频为朝中两国人民牢不可破的友谊和以苏联为首的和平、民主、社会主义阵营的友好团结和伟大的和平事业的胜利而举杯。
    宴会始终在充满友好、团结的空气中进行。
