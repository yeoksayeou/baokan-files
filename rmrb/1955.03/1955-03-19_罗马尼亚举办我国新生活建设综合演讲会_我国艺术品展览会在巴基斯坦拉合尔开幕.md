Title: Romania Holds Comprehensive Speech Meeting on China's New Life Construction; Chinese Art Exhibition Opens in Lahore, Pakistan
Author:
Date: 1955-03-19
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, Prague, 18th. Bucharest dispatch: The Theoretical Education Department of the Stalin District Committee of the Romanian Workers' Party in Bucharest organized a comprehensive speech meeting on "The Construction of New Life in the People's Republic of China" on March 14th in Bucharest. The audience included party activists, workers, soldiers, and students.

The speakers at the meeting were people who had visited China. Among them, engineer Balan spoke on "The Development of China's Economy," university professor Vitner spoke on "The People of New China," writer Camilla spoke on "How China Solves the Nationalities Problem," and "Spark Daily" editor Fintinaru Ion spoke on "The Role of the Chinese Communist Party in Winning the Victory of the Chinese People's Revolution, in the Great National Construction Undertaking, and in Cultivating New Talents." Their speeches were warmly welcomed.

Xinhua News Agency, 17th. Karachi dispatch: The Chinese Art Exhibition opened on the 15th of this month in Lahore, the capital of Punjab Province, the cultural center of Pakistan. More than 200 items are on display, including traditional Chinese paintings, woodcuts, pictures (of the Huai River harnessing project and the life of the Hui people), ivory carvings, sculptures, porcelain, Hunan embroidery, and silk fabrics.

The exhibition is sponsored by the "Pakistan Arts Council." More than 500 people attended the opening ceremony, including Munir, Chief Justice of the Federal Court of Pakistan, provincial government officials, members of the provincial assembly, university professors, and people from the cultural and artistic circles. Munir cut the ribbon for the exhibition. Rahman, Chairman of the Pakistan Arts Council and Judge of the Punjab High Court, praised the great achievements of Chinese culture and art in his opening remarks. He expressed his hope to strengthen cultural and artistic exchanges between China and Pakistan.

### 罗马尼亚举办我国新生活建设综合演讲会  我国艺术品展览会在巴基斯坦拉合尔开幕

1955-03-19
第1版()
专栏：

    罗马尼亚举办我国新生活建设综合演讲会
    我国艺术品展览会在巴基斯坦拉合尔开幕
    【新华社布拉格十八日电】　布加勒斯特消息：罗马尼亚工人党布加勒斯特市斯大林区委员会理论教育处，三月十四日在布加勒斯特组织了“中华人民共和国新生活建设”综合演讲会。听讲的人包括党的积极分子、工人、军人和学生等。
    演讲会由曾经访问过中国的人主讲。其中工程师巴兰主讲“中国经济的发展情况”，大学教授维特纳主讲“新中国的人民”，作家卡米拉主讲“中国如何解决民族问题”，“火花报”编辑芬蒂纳鲁·伊昂主讲“中国共产党在争取中国人民革命的胜利、在伟大的国家建设事业和培养新人材的工作中的作用”。他们的演讲受到了热烈的欢迎。
    【新华社十七日讯】　喀喇蚩消息：中国艺术品展览会已于本月十五日在巴基斯坦文化中心旁遮普省省会拉合尔开幕，展出国画、木刻、图片（治淮及回民生活）、象牙、雕刻、瓷器、湘绣、丝织等二百多件。
    展览会由“巴基斯坦艺术协会”主办。参加开幕式的有巴基斯坦联邦法院大法官穆尼尔，省政府官员、省议会议员、大学教授及文化艺术界人士五百多人。展览会由穆尼尔剪彩。巴基斯坦艺术协会主席旁遮普省高等法院法官拉曼在开幕词中称赞中国文化艺术的巨大成就，他希望在中巴两国间加强文化艺术交流。
