Title: Air Force Hero, Model Worker, and Meritorious Representative Congress Opens
Author:
Date: 1955-03-22
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, March 21st: The Chinese People's Liberation Army Air Force's first Hero, Model Worker, and Meritorious Representative Congress grandly opened in Beijing on the 21st.

The opening ceremony began at 9:00 am. On both sides of the rostrum were hung inscriptions from Chairman Mao Zedong and Vice Chairman Zhu De for the congress. Chairman Mao's inscription read: "Build a strong people's air force to defend the motherland and prepare to defeat the invaders." Vice Chairman Zhu's inscription read: "Promote revolutionary heroism, consolidate national defense, and defend against imperialist aggression."

More than 20 representatives from the General Political Department, General Cadre Department, General Logistics Department of the Chinese People's Liberation Army, various military services, special forces, North China Military Region, Military Academy, Military Engineering Academy, as well as various agencies and organizations attended the opening ceremony.

Air Force Deputy Commander Wang Bingzhang delivered the opening address. He first expressed his respect and warm congratulations to the heroes, model workers, and meritorious representatives attending the congress, and observed a moment of silence for the martyrs who gloriously sacrificed themselves in the struggle to defend the motherland. In his opening address, he said: Since its establishment, the Chinese People's Liberation Army Air Force, under the wise and correct leadership and cordial care of the Chinese Communist Party, Chairman Mao, and the General Headquarters of the Chinese People's Liberation Army, and with the active support of the people of the whole country and fraternal military services, has achieved brilliant successes in performing combat, training, and various construction tasks. For more than five years, the Chinese People's Liberation Army Air Force has vigilantly defended the airspace of the motherland, closely cooperated with the army and navy in the struggle to liberate Taiwan and the enemy-occupied islands along the coast, repeatedly and victoriously struck at the Chiang bandit planes that harassed the mainland of the motherland, bombed and destroyed the Chiang bandit military positions and Chiang bandit warships on the islands along the coast of Zhejiang, and directly coordinated with the army and navy in liberating Yijiangshan Island. According to incomplete statistics, the Air Force has produced 30 heroes and models, more than 60,000 meritorious people, and 1,820 collective merit-achieving units in performing various tasks. He pointed out that the task of this congress is to fully exchange combat, learning, and work experience, summarize the meritorious service movement, commend the model figures and deeds of various departments of the Air Force in the past five years, select new heroes and models, and establish various examples to inspire the entire army's sense of honor in rendering meritorious service to the people, and to make a hundredfold effort to build a strong, modern, and regularized people's air force, defend the motherland, liberate Taiwan, and safeguard Asian and world peace.

Xiao Hua, Deputy Director of the General Political Department of the Chinese People's Liberation Army, gave instructions to the congress. He represented the General Political Department of the Chinese People's Liberation Army in expressing his cordial greetings and gratitude to all the representatives attending the meeting and, through them, to all the personnel of the Air Force. He also called on the heroes, model workers, meritorious people, and all commanders and fighters of the Air Force to accelerate the modernization and regularization of the people's air force, be ready at all times to deal with any provocations by the imperialists, and to fight for the liberation of the enemy-occupied islands and Taiwan along the coast and the complete annihilation of the Chiang bandit army.

Representatives from various military services, academies, and agencies also spoke at the congress, warmly congratulating the People's Air Force on the brilliant achievements it has made in performing combat, defending the motherland, and various tasks, and wished the congress a success.

A total of 396 representatives of heroes, model workers, meritorious people, and collective merit-achieving units, and 129 nonvoting delegates participated in this congress. Among these heroes, model workers, meritorious people, and merit-achieving units are Wang Hai, Zhang Jihui, and Zhao Baotong (the above three were absent due to reasons), first-class combat heroes of the Air Force who had volunteered to resist US aggression and aid Korea and had made outstanding achievements; Zheng Changhua, second-class combat hero and representative of the First National People's Congress; Jiao Jingwen, excellent wingman pilot and second-class combat hero; Wang Tianbao, Li Han, and Zou Yan, second-class combat heroes; Zhang Weiliang, Song Zongzhou, and Liu Jianhan, who have repeatedly made military achievements in the struggle to liberate the coastal islands of Zhejiang; and a certain detachment that won the collective second-class merit. There are also Song Zhongwen, an outstanding pilot who diligently studied and mastered advanced course flight technology and shot down and wounded three Chiang bandit planes; Shao Changyuan, who won the title of second-class model flight instructor; Wang Hongzhi, a second-class model pilot who broke through the "air forbidden zone" and opened up the air route to the Kangzang Plateau; Wu Zhudi, a representative of the first group of female aviation personnel in New China; Song Xiwu, a second-class model of maintenance work; Ma Yumin, a navigation staff officer who created the "cloud penetration flight slide rule"; Lu Shenglin, a oil storage keeper who created the "oil scraper" and saved a lot of wealth for the motherland; and a certain maintenance team that has won the collective merit five times.

### 空军英雄模范功臣代表大会开幕

1955-03-22
第1版()
专栏：

    空军英雄模范功臣代表大会开幕
    【新华社二十一日讯】中国人民解放军空军首届英雄模范功臣代表大会，二十一日在北京隆重开幕。
    大会开幕式在上午九时开始。大会主席台两旁悬着毛泽东主席、朱德副主席给大会的题词。毛主席的题词是“建立一支强大的人民空军，保卫祖国，准备战胜侵略者。”朱副主席的题词是“发扬革命英雄主义，巩固国防，防御帝国主义的侵略。”
    参加开幕式的来宾有中国人民解放军总政治部、总干部部、总后勤部、各军种、各特种兵、华北军区、军事学院、军事工程学院以及各机关、团体代表共二十多人。
    空军副司令员王秉璋致开幕词。他首先向出席大会的英雄、模范、功臣们表示敬意和热烈祝贺，向在保卫祖国的斗争中光荣牺牲的烈士们默哀。他在开幕词中说：中国人民解放军空军自建军以来，在中国共产党、毛主席和中国人民解放军总部的英明而正确的领导和亲切的关怀下，在全国人民和兄弟军种部队的积极支援下，在执行作战、训练和各项建设任务中都获得了光辉的成就。五年多来，中国人民解放军空军部队警惕地保卫了祖国的领空，密切配合陆、海军部队在解放台湾和沿海敌占岛屿的斗争中，多次胜利地痛击了窜扰祖国大陆上空的蒋贼飞机，轰炸和摧毁了浙江沿海岛屿的蒋贼军阵地和蒋贼军舰，并直接协同陆、海军部队解放了一江山岛。据不完全统计，空军部队在执行各项任务中共涌现出三十名英雄、模范，六万多名人民功臣和一千八百二十个集体立功单位。他指出这次大会的任务是充分地交流战斗、学习和工作经验，总结立功运动，表扬五年来空军各部门的模范人物和事迹，评选新的英雄和模范，树立各种榜样，以激励全军人员为人民立功的荣誉感，为建设强大的现代化正规化的人民空军，保卫祖国、解放台湾，维护亚洲及世界和平而百倍努力。
    中国人民解放军总政治部副主任萧华向大会作了指示。他代表中国人民解放军总政治部向到会的全体代表并通过他们向空军全体人员表示亲切的慰问和感谢。并号召英雄、模范、功臣们和空军全体指战员加速人民空军现代化、正规化建设，时刻做好应付帝国主义者任何挑衅的战斗准备，为解放沿海敌占岛屿和台湾、彻底消灭蒋贼军而斗争。
    各军种、学院、机关的代表也在大会上讲了话，热烈祝贺人民空军在执行作战、保卫祖国和各项任务中所取得的光辉成就，并预祝大会的成功。
    参加这次大会的英雄、模范、功臣代表和立集体功单位代表共三百九十六人，列席代表一百二十九人。在这些英雄、模范、功臣和立功单位中，有曾经志愿抗美援朝、功绩卓著的空军一级战斗英雄王海、张积慧、赵宝桐（以上三人因故未出席）、刘玉堤、鲁珉；二级战斗英雄、第一届全国人民代表大会代表郑长华，出色的僚机飞行员、二级战斗英雄焦景文，二级战斗英雄王天保、李汉、邹炎；有在解放浙江沿海岛屿的斗争中屡建战功的张伟良、宋宗周、刘建汉和荣立集体二等功的某支队；还有刻苦钻研、掌握了高级课目飞行技术并击落击伤蒋贼飞机三架的优秀飞行员宋中文，荣获二级模范飞行教员称号的邵长源，突破“空中禁区”开辟康藏高原航线的二级模范飞行员王洪智，新中国第一批女航空人员代表女飞行员伍竹迪，二级机务工作模范宋锡文，创造“穿云飞行计算尺”的领航参谋马于民，创造“刮油器”为祖国节省了大量财富的油料保管员卢胜林，以及先后立过五次集体功的某机务小组等。
