Title: The Chinese People's Volunteers Allocate Saved Grain to Aid Disaster-Stricken People in Korea; Korean Delegations Visit the Garrison of Returning CPV Troops to Express Gratitude

Author: 
Date: 1955-03-29
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Pyongyang, March 28th: The leadership of the Chinese People's Volunteers (CPV) recently decided to allocate the grain saved by the entire army in 1954 to the people in the areas of Korea that suffered from natural disasters in 1954, to help them solve the difficulties in their lives and production. Currently, the leading organs of many CPV units are assisting local Korean governments in quickly distributing the grain to the Korean people.

Xinhua News Agency, Pyongyang, March 28th: The first delegation of the Korean people from all walks of life, forming a welcoming and seeing-off delegation for the returning Chinese People's Volunteers, and the accompanying art troupe, departed from Pyongyang on March 28th to visit and bid farewell to a certain unit of the returning CPV troops stationed there. The first delegation is led by Lee Song-un, Prosecutor General of the Supreme Procuratorate of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea. They are also carrying gifts such as silk banners, commemorative medals, and letters of gratitude from people of all walks of life. Other delegations will depart successively.

### 志愿军拨出节约的粮食赠送朝鲜受灾人民  朝鲜各界代表团前往我志愿军归国部队驻地进行慰问

1955-03-29
第1版()
专栏：

     志愿军拨出节约的粮食赠送朝鲜受灾人民
     朝鲜各界代表团前往我志愿军归国部队驻地进行慰问
    【新华社平壤二十八日电】中国人民志愿军领导机关最近决定拨出全军一九五四年度节约的粮食，赠给朝鲜一九五四年遭受自然灾害地区的人民，帮助他们解决生活和生产中的困难。目前，志愿军许多部队的领导机关正在协助朝鲜当地政府很快地把粮食分发给朝鲜人民。
    【新华社平壤二十八日电】朝鲜各界人民组成的欢送中国人民志愿军归国部队代表团第一团和随行的艺术队已于二十八日从平壤出发，前往中国人民志愿军归国部队某部驻地进行慰问和送别。第一团由朝鲜民主主义人民共和国最高检察所检察总长李松云率领。他们并携带有锦旗、纪念章和各界人民的感谢信等礼物。其他各团也将陆续出发。
