Title: China and Albania Sign This Year's Cultural Cooperation Agreement Implementation Plan
Author:
Date: 1955-03-06
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, Tirana, March 5th: The 1955 Implementation Plan for the Cultural Cooperation Agreement between the People's Republic of China and the People's Republic of Albania was signed in Tirana on March 2nd.

According to the plan, China and Albania will expand cultural relations in 1955, exchange cultural delegations, and exchange experiences and achievements in science, education, and culture.

Signing the plan for Albania was Spiro Koleka, Minister of Education and Culture of the People's Republic of Albania, and for China, Xu Yixin, Ambassador Extraordinary and Plenipotentiary of the People's Republic of China to Albania.

Attending the signing ceremony were Albanian Minister of Foreign Affairs Behar Shtylla, Chairman of the Committee for Foreign Cultural Relations Ramiz Alia, President of the Academy of Sciences Aleks Buda, Deputy Minister of Foreign Affairs Natai Nathanaili, Director of the Protocol Department Kroy, and First Secretary of the Chinese Embassy in Albania Wang Dong, and others.

### 中阿签订今年文化合作协定执行计划

1955-03-06
第1版()
专栏：

    中阿签订今年文化合作协定执行计划
    【新华社五日讯】地拉那消息：中华人民共和国和阿尔巴尼亚人民共和国之间的文化合作协定一九五五年执行计划，已经于三月二日在地拉那签订。
    根据计划规定，中阿两国一九五五年将扩大文化关系，互派文化代表团，并且交换科学、教育和文化等方面的经验和成就。
    在计划上签字的，阿尔巴尼亚方面是阿尔巴尼亚人民共和国教育和文化部部长斯巴修，中国方面是中华人民共和国驻阿尔巴尼亚特命全权大使徐以新。
    参加签字仪式的有阿尔巴尼亚外交部长什图拉、对外文化联络委员会主席帕奇拉米、科学院院长帕帕里斯托，外交部副部长纳塔奈利、交际司司长克罗伊，和中华人民共和国驻阿尔巴尼亚大使馆一等秘书王栋等。
