Title: Two British Merchant Ships Attacked by Chiang Kai-shek Bandit Gunboats
Author:
Date: 1955-03-09
Page: 1

　　Xinhua News Agency, Guangzhou, March 8th: Hong Kong news: According to an announcement by the British naval authorities in Hong Kong on the 7th, on the morning of the 7th, the British merchant ship "Wai Shi Wei" of Hong Kong's Hua Xia Shipping Company was bombarded by Chiang Kai-shek bandit military gunboats. This British merchant ship was attacked in the sea area north of Xiamen at 119 degrees 10 minutes east longitude and 24 degrees 50 minutes north latitude.
    The British naval authorities also announced that another British merchant ship, the "Da Shun Kang," was also bombarded by Chiang Kai-shek bandit warships in the Taiwan Strait.

### 两艘英商轮遭蒋贼炮艇轰击

1955-03-09
第1版()
专栏：

    两艘英商轮遭蒋贼炮艇轰击
    【新华社广州八日电】香港消息：据香港英国海军当局七日宣布，七日早晨，香港华厦船务公司的英国商船“卫士卫号”受到蒋贼军炮艇的轰击。这艘英国商船是在厦门以北东经一百十九度十分、北纬二十四度五十分的海面遭到轰击的。
    英国海军当局又宣布，另外一艘英国商船“大顺康号”也在台湾海峡的海面遭到蒋贼军炮舰的轰击。
