Title: Comrade He Kaifeng's Memorial Service Held in Beijing
Author: 
Date: 1955-03-28
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, March 27th:

    The memorial service for Comrade He Kaifeng, former Deputy Director of the Propaganda Department of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of China, was held in Beijing at 10:00 a.m. on March 27th.

    The memorial service was presided over by Deng Xiaoping, Secretary-General of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of China. Lu Dingyi, Xu Teli, Chen Boda, Yang Shangkun, Zhang Jichun, Li Zhuoran, Yang Xianzhen, Zhou Yang, and Zhang Panshi were present as pallbearers.

    Comrade He Kaifeng's remains were interred at the Babaoshan Revolutionary Cemetery at 11:00 a.m. on the same day.

### 何凯丰同志追悼会在京举行

1955-03-28
第1版()
专栏：

    何凯丰同志追悼会在京举行
    【新华社二十七日讯】前中国共产党中央委员会宣传部副部长何凯丰同志追悼大会二十七日上午十时在北京举行。
    追悼大会由中国共产党中央委员会秘书长邓小平主祭。陪祭的有陆定一、徐特立、陈伯达、杨尚昆、张际春、李卓然、杨献珍、周扬、张磐石。
    何凯丰同志遗体已在当日上午十一时在八宝山烈士公墓安葬。
