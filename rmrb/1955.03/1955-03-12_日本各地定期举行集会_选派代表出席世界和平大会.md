Title: Regular Rallies Held Across Japan; Representatives Selected to Attend World Peace Conference
Author:
Date: 1955-03-12
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Tokyo, 10th: The Japanese Preparatory Committee for participation in the World Peace Conference to be held in Helsinki in May held its first meeting in Tokyo on the 9th, discussing the guiding principles for the preparatory work.

The meeting decided that before April 20th, rallies should be held by various groups across the country to clarify the purpose of the World Peace Conference to the masses; before early May, representatives selected from all over the country to attend the World Peace Conference should be determined.

### 日本各地定期举行集会  选派代表出席世界和平大会

1955-03-12
第4版()
专栏：

    日本各地定期举行集会
    选派代表出席世界和平大会
    【新华社十日讯】东京消息：筹备参加五月在赫尔辛基举行的世界和平大会的日本筹备会，九日在东京举行第一次会议，讨论了筹备工作的方针。
    会议决定：在四月二十日以前，在全国各地由各团体举行集会，使人民群众明了世界和平大会的目的；在五月上旬以前，确定由全国各地选派出来的参加世界和平大会的代表。
