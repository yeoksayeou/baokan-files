Title: Documentary “Liberation of Dachen Island” Premieres in the Capital
Author:
Date: 1955-03-03
Page: 1

    “Liberation of Dachen Island” Documentary Premieres in the Capital
    [Xinhua News Agency, 2nd] The short documentary “Liberation of Dachen Island,” produced by the Central Newsreel and Documentary Film Studio, premiered in the capital on the 2nd and will soon be shown across the country. The film records the crimes of the U.S. fleet shielding the Chiang bandit troops' retreat, and the Chiang bandit troops' cruel destructive acts on Dachen Island, such as abducting residents, burning houses, and looting residents' property, as evidence of their crimes. The film also records the landing of our People's Liberation Army on Dachen Island and the transport of large quantities of materials by the people of the motherland to Dachen Island to support the People's Liberation Army.

### “解放大陈岛”纪录片在首都上映

1955-03-03
第1版()
专栏：

    “解放大陈岛”纪录片在首都上映
    【新华社二日讯】中央新闻纪录电影制片厂摄制的短纪录片“解放大陈岛”二日已在首都上映，并即将在全国各地上映。影片纪录了美国舰队掩护蒋贼军撤逃的罪行和蒋贼军在大陈岛劫运居民、焚毁房屋、抢夺居民财物等等残酷破坏行为的罪证。影片还纪录了我人民解放军在大陈岛登陆及祖国人民将大批物资运往大陈岛支援人民解放军的情形。
