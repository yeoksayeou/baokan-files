Title: Premier Zhou Enlai Holds Grand Banquet to Bid Farewell to the Dalai Lama and the Panchen Erdeni
Author:
Date: 1955-03-11
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, March 10th: Premier Zhou Enlai of the State Council held a grand banquet at Zhongnanhai in Beijing on the afternoon of the 10th to bid farewell to the Dalai Lama Tenzin Gyatso and the Panchen Erdeni Chökyi Gyaltsen, and their entourage, who are about to leave Beijing to return to Tibet.

The Dalai Lama, the Panchen Erdeni, and their entourage, including Living Buddha Lincang, Living Buddha Jiayang, Sokang Wangqing Gelei, Ngapoi Ngawang Jigme, Ji Jinmei, the Dalai Lama's and Panchen Erdeni's family members, and secular and religious officials from the Tibetan local government in Beijing, all attended the banquet.

Those present at the banquet included: Vice Premiers of the State Council Peng Dehuai, Deng Xiaoping, and Chen Yi; Secretary-General Xi Zhongxun; ministers and vice-ministers of various ministries; directors and deputy directors of various commissions; Vice Directors of the Nationalities Affairs Commission Wang Feng, Liu Chun, Sa Kongliao, and Yang Jingren; Representative of the Central People's Government in Tibet Zhang Jingwu; and Vice Mayors of Beijing Wu Han and Wang Kunlun.

Also present at the banquet were: Vice Chairmen of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress Li Jishen, Huang Yanpei, Peng Zhen, Li Weihan, Chen Shutong, and members of the Standing Committee in Beijing; Chairman of the Nationalities Committee Liu Geping, Vice Chairman Zhang Zhiyi, and members of the committee in Beijing.

Also present at the banquet were: President of the Supreme People's Court Dong Biwu, Procurator-General of the Supreme People's Procuratorate Zhang Dingcheng, Vice Chairmen of the National Defense Council Zhang Zhizhong and Fu Zuoyi, Deputy Director of the General Political Department of the Chinese People's Liberation Army Gan Siqi, Political Commissar of the Tibet Military Region Tan Guansan, and Deputy Political Commissar Fan Ming.

Also present at the banquet was: Vice Chairman of the National Committee of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference Zhang Bojun and members of the Standing Committee in Beijing.

Also present at the banquet were: Acting President of the Buddhist Association of China Xirao Jiacuo, Vice Presidents Zhao Puchu and Gara Gacang, and other heads of people's organizations and religious organizations, heads of the Central Institute for Nationalities, and heads of the Dalai Lama's Office in Beijing and the Panchen Erdeni's Office in Beijing.

At the banquet, Premier Zhou Enlai first delivered a farewell speech (full text to be issued separately). Subsequently, the Dalai Lama and the Panchen Erdeni delivered speeches in reply (full text to be issued separately).

The banquet proceeded in an atmosphere of unity and friendship.

After the banquet, a Peking Opera performance was held. (Picture attached)

### 周恩来总理举行盛大宴会  欢送达赖喇嘛和班禅额尔德尼

1955-03-11
第1版()
专栏：

    周恩来总理举行盛大宴会
    欢送达赖喇嘛和班禅额尔德尼
    【新华社十日讯】国务院总理周恩来十日下午在北京中南海举行盛大宴会，欢送行将离京返藏的达赖喇嘛·丹增嘉措和班禅额尔德尼·却吉坚赞，及随行官员。
    达赖喇嘛、班禅额尔德尼和他们的随行人员林仓活佛、嘉样活佛、索康·旺清格来、阿沛·阿旺晋美、计晋美，达赖喇嘛和班禅额尔德尼的家属，以及西藏地方在京僧俗官员等都出席了宴会。
    出席宴会的有：国务院副总理彭德怀、邓小平、陈毅，秘书长习仲勋，各部部长、副部长，各委员会主任、副主任，民族事务委员会副主任汪锋、刘春、萨空了、杨静仁，中央人民政府驻西藏代表张经武，北京市副市长吴晗、王昆仑。
    出席宴会的还有：全国人民代表大会常务委员会副委员长李济深、黄炎培、彭真、李维汉、陈叔通和在京委员，民族委员会主任委员刘格平、副主任委员张执一和在京委员。
    出席宴会的还有：最高人民法院院长董必武，最高人民检察院检察长张鼎丞，国防委员会副主席张治中、傅作义，中国人民解放军总政治部副主任甘泗淇，西藏军区政治委员谭冠三、副政治委员范明。
    出席宴会的还有：中国人民政治协商会议全国委员会副主席章伯钧和在京常务委员。
    出席宴会的还有：中国佛教协会代理会长喜饶嘉错，副会长赵朴初、噶喇藏和其他人民团体及宗教团体负责人，中央民族学院负责人，达赖喇嘛驻京办事处和班禅额尔德尼驻京办事处负责人。
