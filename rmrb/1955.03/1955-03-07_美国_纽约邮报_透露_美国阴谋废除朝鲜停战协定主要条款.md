Title: US "New York Post" Reveals US Plot to Abolish Key Provisions of the Korean Armistice Agreement
Author:
Date: 1955-03-07
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, New York, March 5th (TASS dispatch): The "New York Post" published a report from Washington by Robert Allen confirming that the United States is seriously considering the question of abolishing the main clauses of the Korean Armistice Agreement. According to Allen's report, the U.S. Joint Chiefs of Staff recommended the following measures: to remove all restrictions on supplying South Korean troops with new military equipment, and to completely abolish the Neutral Nations Supervisory Commission. The reporter pointed out that Admiral Radford strongly advocated the adoption of this plan.

According to a United Press reporter from Washington, U.S. officials said the United States would increase its military strength in Korea regardless of the restrictions of the armistice agreement.

### 美国“纽约邮报”透露  美国阴谋废除朝鲜停战协定主要条款

1955-03-07
第4版()
专栏：

    美国“纽约邮报”透露
    美国阴谋废除朝鲜停战协定主要条款
    【新华社六日讯】塔斯社纽约五日讯：“纽约邮报”刊载罗伯特·艾伦从华盛顿发出的报道证实，美国正在认真地考虑关于废除朝鲜停战协定的主要条款的问题。据艾伦报道，美国参谋长联席会议建议采取下列措施：取消对于供给南朝鲜军队新式军事装备的一切限制，并且完全取消中立国监察委员会。记者指出，雷德福海军上将坚决主张通过这个计划。
    据合众社记者自华盛顿报道，美国官员们说，美国将不管停战协定的限制而增加它在朝鲜的军事力量。
