Title: Gaiping County Expands Apple Export Work
Author: Yang Shaoqin Ren Enfu
Date: 1955-03-23
Page: 2

    Gaiping County Expands Apple Export Work
    The state-owned food company in Gaiping County, Liaoning Province, an important apple-producing area, has begun this year's spring apple export work. On March 9th, the first batch of trains had already transported 280 tons. Gaiping County plans to export more than 2,000 tons of apples this spring, and it is expected that this phase of work will be completed in late April. As of March 9th, Gaiping County has also shipped more than 120 tons of apples to domestic cities such as Shanghai, Shenyang, and Anshan.
                          (Yang Shaoqin Ren Enfu)

### 盖平县展开苹果出口工作
杨绍勤  任恩富
1955-03-23
第2版()
专栏：

    盖平县展开苹果出口工作
    辽宁省重要苹果产地盖平县的国营食品公司，开始进行今年春季的苹果出口工作。三月九日，第一批列车已经运出二百八十吨。盖平县今春计划出口苹果二千多吨，预计四月下旬结束这一阶段的工作。到三月九日止，盖平县还运往上海、沈阳、鞍山等国内城市一百二十多吨苹果。
                          （杨绍勤　任恩富）
