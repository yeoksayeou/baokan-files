Title: Austrian Cabinet Holds Meeting, Decides to Accept Invitation from Soviet Government
Author: 
Date: 1955-03-31
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Vienna, March 29th (TASS dispatch): The Austrian News Agency broadcast the official announcement of the Austrian Cabinet meeting.

The announcement said: Under the chairmanship of Chancellor Raab, the Cabinet on the 29th discussed in detail the Soviet response of March 24 to the Austrian Federal Government's statement of March 14. The Cabinet decided to accept the formal invitation extended by Soviet Foreign Minister Molotov in the Soviet reply note for Federal Chancellor Raab and other members of the Austrian government to visit Moscow. Federal Chancellor Raab, Vice-Chancellor Schärf, Federal Foreign Minister Figl, and Deputy Foreign Minister Kreisky are scheduled to travel to Moscow on April 11. The attendants accompanying the four government representatives will be decided in the near future.

### 奥地利内阁举行会议  决定接受苏联政府的邀请

1955-03-31
第4版()
专栏：

    奥地利内阁举行会议
    决定接受苏联政府的邀请
    新华社三十日讯　据塔斯社维也纳二十九日讯：奥地利通讯社播发了官方发表的奥地利内阁会议的公告。
    公告说：在拉布总理主持下，内阁二十九日详细讨论了三月二十四日苏联对奥地利联邦政府三月十四日声明的答复。内阁决定接受苏联外交部长莫洛托夫在苏联复照中对联邦总理拉布和奥地利政府其他人员访莫斯科的正式邀请。联邦总理拉布、副总理谢尔夫、联邦外交部长费格尔和外交部副部长克赖斯基定四月十一日赴莫斯科。四位政府代表的随员将在最近决定。
