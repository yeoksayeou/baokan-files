Title: The National Assembly of Vietnam Passes Resolution on Subcommittee's Proposal to Study the Armistice Agreement, Calling on the Vietnamese People to Fight for the Consolidation of Peace and the Realization of Unification
Author:
Date: 1955-03-26
Page: 4

    Xinhua News Agency, Hanoi, March 25th: Vietnam News Agency reported that the National Assembly of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam, in a public communique issued on the evening of the 24th, announced that at its meeting on the 24th, it unanimously adopted the conclusions and recommendations put forward by the subcommittee for the study of the Armistice Agreement.
    The recommendations proposed by the aforementioned subcommittee and adopted by the National Assembly included four key points: Expressing confidence in the wise leadership of President Ho Chi Minh, the Vietnam Workers' Party, and the Government of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam; Expressing the determination of all Vietnamese people to fulfill the Armistice Agreement and demanding that the other side also correctly fulfill this agreement; Expressing indignation at the conspiracies of the American imperialists, the group of French colonialists and Ngo Dinh Diem clique who are sabotaging the Armistice Agreement and exposing this conspiracy to the world; Calling on all people to unite widely and closely under the leadership of President Ho Chi Minh and the Government of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam in order to fight for peace, unification, and national independence and democracy.
    Fan Wenbai, representing the South Vietnamese representatives, expressed "the deep gratitude and unshakable trust of the South Vietnamese people in President Ho Chi Minh, the Central Committee of the Vietnam Workers' Party, and the Government of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam." Fan Wenbai pointed out: "South Vietnam is our flesh and blood. It is a part of our beloved motherland. The perfidious conspiracy to separate South Vietnam from the rest of the country has failed in the past and will certainly fail in the future."
    General Vo Nguyen Giap, representing the Government of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam, gave a report on the political line of the Government of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam. He particularly emphasized the righteousness of the Government of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam in respecting the Geneva Armistice Agreement. General Vo Nguyen Giap said: "This righteous path will undoubtedly lead us to glorious victory."
    After this, all the representatives unanimously adopted the conclusions of the subcommittee for the study of the Armistice Agreement, expressing complete agreement with and trust in the actions of the Government of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam in signing the Armistice Agreement for the realization of peace, and expressing their firm determination to strive for the consolidation of peace and the realization of unification.

### 越南国民大会就研究停战协定小组委员会的建议通过决议  号召越南人民为巩固和平实现统一而斗争

1955-03-26
第4版()
专栏：

    越南国民大会就研究停战协定小组委员会的建议通过决议
    号召越南人民为巩固和平实现统一而斗争
    【新华社河内二十五日电】越南通讯社讯：越南民主共和国国民大会在二十四日晚上发表的公报宣布，国民大会在二十四日的会议上一致通过了研究停战协定小组委员会所提出的结论和建议。
    前述小组委员会所提出的由国民大会通过的建议包括四个要点：对于胡志明主席、越南劳动党和越南民主共和国政府的英明领导表示信任；表示全体越南人民履行停战协定的决心，并且要求对方也正确地履行这个协定；对于美帝国主义者、破坏停战协定的法国殖民分子集团和吴庭艳集团破坏日内瓦协定的阴谋表示愤慨，并且向全世界揭发这种阴谋；号召全体人民广泛地和紧密地团结在胡志明主席和越南民主共和国政府领导下，以便为争取和平、统一和全国独立和民主而进行斗争。
    范文白代表南越代表，表示“南越人民对胡志明主席、越南劳动党中央委员会和越南民主共和国政府的深切感谢和不可动摇的信任”。范文白指出：“南越是我们的血肉。它是我们可爱的祖国的一部分。企图把南越同这个国家的其他部分割裂的背信弃义的阴谋，在过去遭到了失败，将来也一定会遭到失败。”
    武元甲将军代表越南民主共和国政府，就越南民主共和国政府的政治路线作了报告。他特别强调越南民主共和国政府尊重日内瓦停战协定的正义性。武元甲将军说：“这条正义的道路毫无疑问将引导我们走向辉煌的胜利。”
    在这以后，全体代表一致通过了研究停战协定小组委员会的结论，表示完全赞同并信任越南民主共和国政府为实现和平而签署停战协定的行动，并且表示他们坚决为巩固和平和实现统一而奋斗。
