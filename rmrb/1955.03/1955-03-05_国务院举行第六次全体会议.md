Title: The State Council Holds its Sixth Plenary Meeting
Author:
Date: 1955-03-05
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, March 4th. The Sixth Plenary Meeting of the State Council of the People's Republic of China was held on the afternoon of the 3rd. The meeting heard and approved the report by Liao Luyan, Minister of Agriculture of the People's Republic of China, on the basic situation of agricultural production in 1954 and current measures for increasing agricultural production, and adopted the State Council's resolution on spring plowing production. The meeting heard and approved the report by Xie Juezai, Minister of Internal Affairs of the People's Republic of China, on the Third National Civil Affairs Conference and the National Civil Affairs Work Plan for 1955. The meeting adopted the State Council's decision to approve the establishment of the Mengcun and Dachang Hui Autonomous Counties in Hebei Province. In addition, the meeting also approved various appointment and removal proposals.

### 国务院举行第六次全体会议

1955-03-05
第1版()
专栏：

    国务院举行第六次全体会议
    【新华社四日讯】　中华人民共和国国务院全体会议第六次会议在三日下午举行。会议听了和批准了中华人民共和国农业部部长廖鲁言所作的关于一九五四年农业生产基本情况和当前农业增产措施的报告，通过了国务院关于春耕生产的决议。会议听了和批准了中华人民共和国内务部部长谢觉哉所作的关于第三次全国民政会议的报告和一九五五年全国民政工作计划。会议通过了国务院关于批准河北省建立孟村、大厂两回族自治县的决定。此外，会议还通过了各项任免案。
