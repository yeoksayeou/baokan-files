    Our Great Motherland
    Yanbian University is the highest institution of learning in the Yanbian Korean Autonomous Prefecture of Jilin Province. There are 1,048 students studying in the university's medical school, teachers college, and agricultural school. More than 780 graduates have now been assigned to the autonomous region and other parts of the country to participate in the socialist construction of the motherland. This is a picture of fourth-year students from the agricultural school helping the Liming Collective Farm measure land.

### 我们伟大的祖国（图片）

1955-03-18
第1版()
专栏：

    我们伟大的祖国
    延边大学是吉林省延边朝鲜族自治区最高学府。在这个大学医学院、师范学院和农业专科学习的学生有一千零四十八人。现在已有七百八十多个毕业生分配到自治区和全国其他地区参加祖国的社会主义建设。这是农业专科四年级学生正帮助黎明集体农庄测量土地。
