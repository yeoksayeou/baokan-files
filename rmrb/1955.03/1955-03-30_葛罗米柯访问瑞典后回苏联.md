Title: Gromyko Returns to Soviet Union After Visiting Sweden
Author:
Date: 1955-03-30
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Stockholm, March 29th: TASS reports that Soviet First Deputy Minister of Foreign Affairs Gromyko left Stockholm by plane on the 28th to return to Moscow.

Gromyko made a statement to reporters at the airport, saying: I had a very pleasant meeting with the King of Sweden in Stockholm. I also had fruitful talks with some Swedish politicians, especially with Prime Minister Erlander and Foreign Minister Undén. In these talks, we naturally discussed some political issues, and also discussed the relations between the Soviet Union and Sweden.

### 葛罗米柯访问瑞典后回苏联

1955-03-30
第4版()
专栏：

    葛罗米柯访问瑞典后回苏联
    【新华社二十九日讯】塔斯社斯德哥尔摩讯：苏联外交部第一副部长葛罗米柯二十八日乘飞机离斯德哥尔摩回莫斯科。
    葛罗米柯在机场上向记者发表声明说：我在斯德哥尔摩同瑞典国王进行了一次很愉快的会谈。我还同某些瑞典政治家、特别是同首相埃兰德先生和外交大臣恩顿先生进行了有成果的会谈。在这些会谈中，我们自然谈到了某些政治问题，而且也讨论到苏联和瑞典的关系。
