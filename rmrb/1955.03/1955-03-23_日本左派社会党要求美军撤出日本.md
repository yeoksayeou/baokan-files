Title: Japanese Leftist Socialist Party Demands U.S. Troops Withdraw from Japan
Author:
Date: 1955-03-23
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Tokyo, 22nd. The Japanese Leftist Socialist Party's Foreign Affairs Bureau issued a statement on the 19th demanding the withdrawal of U.S. troops from Japan. The statement protested against the U.S. military authorities' plan to expand military bases at the foot of Mount Fuji. The statement pointed out that this plan threatens the lives of farmers in these areas. The statement also protested the behavior of the U.S. military throwing money to Japanese elementary school students to lure them to grab the money for amusement, and condemned this behavior of the U.S. military as an insult to the Japanese people.

### 日本左派社会党要求美军撤出日本

1955-03-23
第4版()
专栏：

    日本左派社会党要求美军撤出日本
    【新华社二十二日讯】东京消息：日本左派社会党外交局十九日发表声明，要求美军撤出日本。声明抗议美军当局企图在富士山麓扩大军事基地的计划。声明指出，这种计划威胁着这些地区的农民的生活。声明还抗议美国军队扔钱给日本小学生引诱他们来抢钱以取乐的行为，并谴责美军这种行为是对日本人民的侮辱。
