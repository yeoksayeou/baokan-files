Title: Sino-Czech Cultural Cooperation Agreement This Year's Implementation Plan Exchanged in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1955-03-08
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, March 7th: The 1955 Sino-Czech Cultural Cooperation Implementation Plan, formulated according to the Sino-Czech Cultural Cooperation Agreement, was exchanged on March 1st by Zhang Hanfu, Vice Minister of Foreign Affairs of the People's Republic of China, and Mr. Kousal, Ambassador Extraordinary and Plenipotentiary of the Czechoslovak Republic to China, representing their respective governments after consultation and agreement between the two sides.

### 中捷文化合作协定今年执行计划在京换文

1955-03-08
第1版()
专栏：

    中捷文化合作协定今年执行计划在京换文
    【新华社七日讯】　根据中捷文化合作协定制订的中捷一九五五年文化合作执行计划，经双方商谈协议后，已在三月一日由中华人民共和国外交部副部长章汉夫和捷克斯洛伐克共和国驻华特命全权大使康萨拉分别代表双方政府换文。
