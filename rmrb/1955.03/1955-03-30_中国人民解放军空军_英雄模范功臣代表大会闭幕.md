Title: Closing of the Chinese People's Liberation Army Air Force Hero, Model, and Meritorious Personnel Representative Conference
Author:
Date: 1955-03-30
Page: 1
　
Xinhua News Agency, 29th. The first Chinese People's Liberation Army Air Force Hero, Model, and Meritorious Personnel Representative Conference successfully concluded on the 29th.

　　On the morning of the 29th, Gan Siqi, Deputy Director of the General Political Department of the Chinese People's Liberation Army, attended the meeting and delivered a report. He first explained in detail the necessity of accelerating the construction of a strong people's air force. He encouraged all heroes, models, and meritorious personnel to continue their efforts with a fighting spirit, be modest and prudent, guard against arrogance and rashness, consolidate existing achievements, strive for greater achievements, and play a backbone role among the masses, leading them to establish more merits together.

　　The closing ceremony was held at 3:30 PM. At the meeting, the order of the leaders of the Chinese People's Liberation Army Air Force was first announced. The order decided to award Zhang Weiliang, Song Zongzhou, and Liu Jianhan, who had repeatedly made meritorious achievements in the struggle to liberate the coastal islands of Zhejiang, the title of "Second-Class Combat Hero" and to promote each of them one grade ahead of schedule; to award Song Zhongwen and 18 others the title of Second-Class Model, of which six were promoted one grade ahead of schedule, and eleven took photos in front of the military flag.

　　Accompanied by military music, Air Force leaders presented prizes to all the heroes, models, meritorious personnel, and representatives of collective meritorious units attending the conference.

　　Sixty Young Pioneer representatives from the capital presented gifts to the conference on behalf of all the Young Pioneers in Beijing. They tied red scarves for all the heroes and models present. Young Pioneer Wang Jingyu also delivered a congratulatory message to the conference on behalf of all the Young Pioneers in the capital. Song Xiwu, a Second-Class Model Worker in Air Force Maintenance, presented a model airplane to the Young Pioneers in the capital on behalf of the conference.

　　Zhang Weiliang, a Second-Class Combat Hero of the Air Force, delivered a reply on behalf of all the heroes, models, and meritorious personnel. He assured the conference: We will continue to carry forward revolutionary heroism, never be arrogant or complacent, strive to improve our tactics, techniques, and socialist consciousness, vigilantly defend the motherland's airspace, coordinate with the army and navy, be ready to defeat the aggressors at any time, and resolutely complete the combat tasks of liberating the islands occupied by the Chiang bandit troops along the coast and liberating Taiwan and eliminating the Chiang Kai-shek traitorous clique!

　　The conference also unanimously passed a congratulatory telegram to Chairman Mao and a telegram to all commanders and fighters of the Air Force.

　　Finally, Wang Huaiqiu, Director of the Political Department of the Air Force, delivered the closing speech. He called on all heroes, models, and meritorious personnel to continue to play their leading, backbone, and bridge roles, learn with humility, unite the masses, and strive to complete various tasks such as combat and training! Fight for the liberation of Taiwan and the elimination of the Chiang Kai-shek traitorous clique!

### 中国人民解放军空军  英雄模范功臣代表大会闭幕

1955-03-30
第1版()
专栏：

    中国人民解放军空军
    英雄模范功臣代表大会闭幕
   【新华社二十九日讯】中国人民解放军空军首届英雄模范功臣代表大会，在二十九日胜利闭幕。
    二十九日上午，中国人民解放军总政治部副主任甘泗淇到会作了报告。他首先详尽地说明了加速建设强大的人民空军的必要。他勉励全体英雄、模范、功臣们要以战斗的精神继续努力，谦虚谨慎，戒骄戒躁，巩固已有的成就，争取更大的成绩，并在群众中发挥骨干作用，带动群众一起建立更多的功勋。
    大会在下午三时半举行闭幕式。会上首先宣读了中国人民解放军空军首长的命令。命令决定授予在解放浙江沿海岛屿的斗争中屡建战功的张伟良、宋宗周、刘健汉等三人以“二级战斗英雄”称号，并各提前晋升一级；授予宋中文等十八人以二级模范的称号，其中有六人提前晋升一级，十一人分别在军旗前照像。
    空军首长在军乐伴奏声中向参加大会的全体英雄、模范、功臣和集体立功单位的代表分别发给了奖品。
    首都六十名少年先锋队员代表北京市全体少年先锋队员向大会献礼，他们给到会的全体英雄、模范系带了红领巾。少年先锋队员汪静愉并代表首都全体少年先锋队员向大会致贺词。空军二级机务工作模范宋锡文代表大会把一架飞机模型赠给首都少年先锋队员们。
    空军二级战斗英雄张伟良代表全体英雄、模范、功臣致答词。他向大会保证：一定继续发扬革命英雄主义，永远不骄傲不自满，努力提高战术、技术和社会主义觉悟，警惕地保卫祖国领空，协同陆、海军部队，随时准备战胜侵略者，坚决完成解放沿海蒋贼军盘踞的岛屿和解放台湾、消灭蒋介石卖国集团的战斗任务！
    大会还一致通过了给毛主席的致敬电和致空军全体指挥员战斗员的电文。
    大会最后由空军政治部主任王辉球致闭幕词。他号召全体英雄、模范、功臣继续发挥带头作用、骨干作用和桥梁作用，虚心学习，团结群众，为完成作战、训练等各项任务而努力！为解放台湾、消灭蒋介石卖国集团而斗争！
