Title: Czechoslovak and Albanian Public Opinion Fully Supports the Consultations of the Eight Countries Participating in the Moscow Conference
Author:
Date: 1955-03-25
Page: 4

Czechoslovak and Albanian Public Opinion
Fully Supports the Consultations of the Eight Countries Participating in the Moscow Conference

Xinhua News Agency, Prague, March 24th: Czechoslovak Radio commented on the consultations of the eight countries at the Moscow Conference on the conclusion of a treaty of friendship, cooperation, and mutual assistance on the 22nd. It said: All the Czechoslovak people fully agree with this measure to guarantee the integrity of their borders. The commentary emphasized: The development of the situation in West Germany cannot be ignored, "We are prepared to more firmly join our allies to build the dikes necessary for our security."

Albanian newspapers published editorials on the consultations of the eight countries at the Moscow Conference on the 23rd. "People's Voice" pointed out: This consultation demonstrates the unity, cooperation, and invincible determination of the eight countries at the Moscow Conference. "People's Voice" said: The principles of the Moscow Conference regarding the conclusion of a treaty of friendship, cooperation, and mutual assistance among the eight countries, as well as the decision to establish a joint command after the ratification of the Paris Accords, reflect the wishes of the Albanian people. The Albanian people wholeheartedly welcome this action to defend our national independence and peaceful labor, and to maintain world peace.

### 捷克斯洛伐克和阿尔巴尼亚舆论  完全拥护参加莫斯科会议八国的磋商

1955-03-25
第4版()
专栏：

    捷克斯洛伐克和阿尔巴尼亚舆论
    完全拥护参加莫斯科会议八国的磋商
   【新华社布拉格二十四日电】捷克斯洛伐克广播电台二十二日就莫斯科会议八国磋商缔结友好合作互助条约事发表评论。它说：全体捷克斯洛伐克人民完全同意这种保障他们的边境完整的措施。评论强调：不能坐视西德局势的发展，“我们准备更坚定地同我们的盟国一起，来建立我们安全所必需的堤防。”
    阿尔巴尼亚报纸二十三日就莫斯科会议八国的磋商发表社论。“人民之声报”指出：这次磋商表明了莫斯科会议八国的团结、合作和不能被战胜的决心。“人民之声报”说：莫斯科会议关于八国缔结友好合作互助条约的原则以及在巴黎协定批准后成立联合司令部的决定，反映了阿尔巴尼亚人民的愿望。阿尔巴尼亚人民衷心欢迎这种保卫我国独立和和平劳动以及维持世界和平的行动。
