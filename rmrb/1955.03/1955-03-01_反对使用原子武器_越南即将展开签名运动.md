Title: Against the Use of Atomic Weapons - Vietnam to Launch Signature Campaign
Author:
Date: 1955-03-01
Page: 4

Against the Use of Atomic Weapons
Xinhua News Agency: Vietnam to Launch Signature Campaign
According to Xinhua News Agency: The Vietnam Committee for the Defense of World Peace held a meeting on February 26th. The meeting decided that the signature campaign against the use of atomic weapons, which will soon be launched nationwide, should achieve the following objectives:
    I. To demonstrate the determination of the Vietnamese people to defend peace, and to contribute a share to the common struggle of the people of the world to stop the clutches of the American imperialists who are currently plotting to provoke an atomic war;
    II. To thoroughly expose the conspiracies of the American warmongers and advance the movement against American interference in Vietnam;
    III. To oppose the atomic weapons propaganda being conducted by American imperialists in Vietnam.
    Signature campaign committees will be established at both the central and local levels. The signature campaign will continue until the end of April this year.

### 反对使用原子武器  越南即将展开签名运动

1955-03-01
第4版()
专栏：

    反对使用原子武器
    越南即将展开签名运动
    据新华社讯：越南保卫世界和平委员会二月二十六日召开了一个会议。会议决定，通过即将在全国展开的反对使用原子武器的签名运动要达到下列目的：
    一、显示越南人民决心保卫和平，并且对世界人民的共同斗争贡献出一分力量，来制止目前正在阴谋挑起原子战争的美帝国主义者的魔掌；
    二、彻底揭露美国战争贩子的阴谋，推进反对美国干涉越南的运动；
  　三、反对美帝国主义者在越南进行的原子武器宣传。
    中央和地方都将成立签名运动委员会。签名运动将一直进行到今年四月底。
