Title: Presidium of the Supreme Soviet of the USSR Appoints Mikhailov as Minister of Culture
Author:
Date: 1955-03-25
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, 21st TASS: The Presidium of the Supreme Soviet of the USSR, upon the proposal of N.A. Bulganin, Chairman of the Council of Ministers of the USSR, decided to relieve G.F. Alexandrov of his duties as Minister of Culture of the USSR due to his failure to ensure the leadership of the Ministry of Culture.

    The Presidium appointed N.A. Mikhailov as Minister of Culture of the USSR, relieving him of his post as Ambassador of the USSR to the Polish People's Republic.

### 苏联最高苏维埃主席团  任命米哈伊洛夫为文化部长

1955-03-25
第4版()
专栏：

    苏联最高苏维埃主席团
    任命米哈伊洛夫为文化部长
   【新华社讯】塔斯社莫斯科二十一日电：苏联最高苏维埃主席团根据苏联部长会议主席尼·亚·布尔加宁的提议，由于格·费·亚历山德罗夫没有使文化部的领导得到保证，决定解除他的苏联文化部部长职务。
    主席团任命尼·亚·米哈伊洛夫为苏联文化部部长，解除他所担任的苏联驻波兰人民共和国大使的职务。
