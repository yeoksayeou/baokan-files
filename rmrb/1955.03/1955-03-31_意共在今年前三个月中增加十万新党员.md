Title: Italian Communist Party Adds 100,000 New Members in First Three Months of This Year
Author:
Date: 1955-03-31
Page: 4

　　Xinhua News Agency, Rome, March 29th (TASS): At a conference in Venice, Giorgio Amendola, a secretary of the Secretariat of the Italian Communist Party, said that in the first three months of 1955, 100,000 new members joined the Italian Communist Party.

### 意共在今年前三个月中增加十万新党员

1955-03-31
第4版()
专栏：

    意共在今年前三个月中增加十万新党员
    新华社三十日讯　塔斯社罗马二十九日讯：意大利共产党书记处的书记乔治·阿门多拉在威尼斯的大会上讲话时说，一九五五年的前三个月中参加意大利共产党的有十万新党员。
