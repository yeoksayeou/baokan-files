Title: Marshal Govorov of the Soviet Union Buried in Red Square; Chinese Defense Minister Peng Dehuai Sends Condolences on Govorov's Death
Author:
Date: 1955-03-24
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, March 23rd: TASS, Moscow reports: The funeral of Soviet Marshal L.A. Govorov was held on the 22nd in Red Square.

Prior to the funeral, working people from Moscow and Leningrad, Soviet soldiers, and students from military schools continuously went to the Hall of Columns of the House of Trade Unions to pay their respects to Govorov. For two days, Govorov's remains were surrounded by wreaths presented by the working people.

At 2:30 PM, the Hall of Columns was closed to the public. Soviet Marshals carefully carried Govorov's urn out of the Hall of Columns and headed towards Red Square. The Moscow garrison was lined up in Red Square, and many representatives of the Moscow working people gathered.

At 4:00 PM, the memorial service began in Red Square. Leaders of the Soviet Communist Party and the government, as well as Soviet Marshals, ascended the platform of the Lenin-Stalin Mausoleum. Soviet Minister of Defense, Marshal Zhukov, spoke at the memorial service on behalf of the Central Committee of the Soviet Communist Party, the Soviet Council of Ministers, and the Soviet Ministry of Defense. Representatives of the working people of Moscow and Leningrad also spoke at the memorial service.

After the memorial service, the urn containing the ashes of Soviet Marshal Govorov was placed in the Kremlin Wall to the sound of mournful music.

Xinhua News Agency, March 23rd: Peng Dehuai, Minister of National Defense of the People's Republic of China, sent a telegram to Soviet Minister of Defense, Marshal Zhukov, to express condolences on the death of Leonid Alexandrovich Govorov, Deputy Minister of Defense of the Soviet Union. The telegram is as follows:

To Marshal Zhukov, Minister of Defense of the Union of Soviet Socialist Republics:

I am deeply saddened to learn of the passing of Comrade Leonid Alexandrovich Govorov, Deputy Minister of Defense of the Soviet Union, an outstanding general of the Soviet armed forces, a famous hero of the Great Patriotic War, and Marshal of the Soviet Union. I extend to you my deepest condolences.

Peng Dehuai
Minister of National Defense of the People's Republic of China
March 22, 1955, in Beijing

### 苏联元帅戈沃罗夫的葬礼在红场举行  我国防部长彭德怀电唁戈沃罗夫逝世

1955-03-24
第4版()
专栏：

    苏联元帅戈沃罗夫的葬礼在红场举行
    我国防部长彭德怀电唁戈沃罗夫逝世
    【新华社二十三日讯】据塔斯社莫斯科讯：苏联元帅列·亚·戈沃罗夫的葬礼于二十二日在红场举行。
    在葬礼举行以前，莫斯科和列宁格勒的劳动人民、苏联军人和军事学校的学生，络绎不绝地前往工会大厦圆柱大厅向戈沃罗夫告别。两天来，戈沃罗夫的灵前堆满了劳动人民敬献的花圈。
    下午二时三十分，圆柱大厅停止开放。苏联元帅们小心地抬起戈沃罗夫的骨灰罐走出圆柱大厅，走向红场。红场上排列着莫斯科卫戍部队，聚集了许多莫斯科劳动人民代表。
    下午四时，红场上开始举行追悼大会。苏联共产党和政府的领导人以及苏联元帅们走上了列宁斯大林陵墓的陵台。苏联国防部部长、苏联元帅朱可夫代表苏联共产党中央委员会、苏联部长会议和苏联国防部在追悼会上讲了话。在追悼会上讲话的还有莫斯科和列宁格勒劳动人民的代表。
    追悼大会结束后，苏联元帅戈沃罗夫的骨灰罐在哀乐声中被安放到克里姆林宫墙里。
    【新华社二十三日讯】中华人民共和国国防部长彭德怀致电苏联国防部长朱可夫元帅，吊唁苏联国防部副部长列昂尼德·亚历山德罗维奇·戈沃罗夫逝世。电文如下：苏维埃社会主义共和国联盟国防部长朱可夫元帅
    痛悉苏联国防部副部长、苏联武装部队杰出的将领、伟大卫国战争中的著名英雄、苏联元帅列昂尼德·亚历山德罗维奇·戈沃罗夫同志逝世噩耗，我谨向您表示最沉痛的哀悼。
            中华人民共和国国防部长　彭德怀
                一九五五年三月二十二日于北京
