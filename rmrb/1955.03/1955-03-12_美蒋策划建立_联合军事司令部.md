Title: US-Chiang Clique Plotting to Establish "Joint Military Command"
Author:
Date: 1955-03-12
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, New York, March 10th: A US-Chiang Kai-shek joint military command is currently being planned for establishment in Taiwan. The Associated Press quoted sources from the Kuomintang in a dispatch from Taipei as saying that American flag officers would be appointed as "deputy commanders" of Chiang Kai-shek's air force and navy. They will "assist in directing operations and ensuring coordinated action between US and Chinese troops."

### 美蒋策划建立“联合军事司令部”

1955-03-12
第4版()
专栏：

    美蒋策划建立“联合军事司令部”
    【新华社十一日讯】塔斯社纽约十日讯：目前正打算在台湾建立一个美国—蒋介石联合军事司令部。合众社记者在发自台北的电报中引用国民党方面的消息说，美国的将级军官将被任命为蒋介石的空军和海军的“副司令”。他们将在“指挥作战方面和保证中美两国部队的协同动作方面予以协助”。
