	Our Great Motherland
	The Qianjiadian Agricultural Machine Station in Inner Mongolia has signed a 1955 production contract with the Red Star, Double Star, New Hero, and New Victory agricultural production cooperatives in Tongliao County. This famous Mongolian female tractor driver, Sa Ren, is inspecting the seed drill, preparing to carry out spring sowing for the agricultural production cooperatives.

### 我们伟大的祖国（图片）

1955-03-24
第1版()
专栏：

    我们伟大的祖国
    内蒙古钱家店农业机器站同通辽县红星、双星、新英雄和新胜利四个农业生产合作社，签订了一九五五年生产合同。这位著名的蒙族女拖拉机手萨仁，正检查播种机，准备为农业生产合作社进行春播。
