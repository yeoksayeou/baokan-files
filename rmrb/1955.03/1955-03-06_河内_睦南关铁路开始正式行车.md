Title: Hanoi-Friendship Gate Railway Begins Formal Operation
Author:
Date: 1955-03-06
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, March 5th. According to Vietnam News Agency: The first train carrying passengers and goods on the Hanoi-Friendship Gate railway departed from Hanoi on the morning of March 3rd. Among the passengers were not only business people, but also many Hanoi citizens visiting relatives in Bac Giang and Lang Son provinces.

　　The train that ran at the Hanoi-Friendship Gate railway opening ceremony on February 28th returned to Hanoi at 6:40 p.m. on March 2nd. Tens of thousands of people welcomed the train back at the station. Tran Dang Khoa, Minister of Transportation and Public Works of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam, reported at a welcome meeting held at the station that the people along the railway warmly welcomed the opening of the railway. Chemist Du Rang, representing the Hanoi area representatives who rode the train, spoke about his feelings regarding the people's great creativity and the close friendship between the Vietnamese and Chinese people.

### 河内—睦南关铁路开始正式行车

1955-03-06
第1版()
专栏：

    河内—睦南关铁路开始正式行车
    【新华社五日讯】据越南通讯社讯：河内—睦南关铁路上第一列装运旅客和货物的火车，在三月三日上午从河内开出。旅客中除了经商的人以外，还有许多到北江省和谅山省探望亲属的河内市民。
    在二月二十八日河内—睦南关铁路举行通车典礼时行驶的列车，已经在三月二日下午六时四十分回到河内。成千上万的人民在车站上欢迎列车回来。越南民主共和国交通公政部长陈登科在车站举行的欢迎会上报告了铁路沿线的人民热情地欢迎通车的情况。化学家杜让代表乘坐这次列车的河内地区的代表们讲话，谈到他对于人民的巨大创造力和越中人民亲密的友谊的感受。
