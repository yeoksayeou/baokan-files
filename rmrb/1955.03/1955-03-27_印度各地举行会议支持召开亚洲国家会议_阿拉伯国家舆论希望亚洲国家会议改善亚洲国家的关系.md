Title: Meetings Held in Various Parts of India to Support the Convening of the Asian Countries Conference / Arab Countries' Public Opinion Hopes that the Asian Countries Conference Will Improve Relations Between Asian Countries
Author:
Date: 1955-03-27
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, New Delhi, March 23rd: Meetings have been held in various parts of India to support the Asian Countries Conference to be held in Delhi in early April.

    A meeting was held on the 20th in Amritsar, an important city in East Punjab Province. Anup Singh, a member of the Indian Parliament, explained the purpose of the Asian Countries Conference at the meeting. Zheng Senyu, a Chinese writer and representative of China working for the International Preparatory Secretariat of the Asian Countries Conference, said in his speech that the Chinese people wholeheartedly welcome the upcoming Asian Countries Conference and have selected a delegation to attend the conference and formed a preparatory committee to carry out preparatory work. Japan's representative from the International Preparatory Secretariat, Hatanaka Masaharu, called for a ban on the use of atomic weapons in his speech.
    More than two hundred representatives from various parties from Patiala and the Patiala and East Punjab States Union held a meeting in Patiala on the 20th. The meeting called for the destruction of all existing atomic weapons and supported the Five Principles of Peaceful Coexistence. Sofronov, the Soviet representative of the International Preparatory Secretariat of the Asian Countries Conference, said at the meeting that the upcoming Asian Countries Conference in Delhi would be a symbol of the growing unity and friendship of the people of Asian countries.

    Xinhua News Agency, New Delhi, March 26th: Syria's "Al-Chalk," Lebanon's "Al-Hayat" and "Al-Khabar" newspapers expressed their support for the aims of the Asian Countries Conference to be held in Delhi in early April. In editorials, these newspapers expressed the hope that the conference would express the deep aspirations of the Arab and Asian peoples and improve relations between Asian countries. Lebanon's "Al-Shark" published an article stating that it hoped that the Arab League in Cairo would attach importance to the Asian Countries Conference in Delhi and recognize the historical significance of this conference.

### 印度各地举行会议支持召开亚洲国家会议  阿拉伯国家舆论希望亚洲国家会议改善亚洲国家的关系

1955-03-27
第1版()
专栏：

    印度各地举行会议支持召开亚洲国家会议
    阿拉伯国家舆论希望亚洲国家会议改善亚洲国家的关系
    【新华社新德里二十三日电】印度各地举行会议支持将于四月初在德里举行的亚洲国家会议。
    东旁遮普省的重要城市阿姆利则二十日举行会议。印度国会议员阿努普·辛格在会上说明了亚洲国家会议的目的。中国作家、参加亚洲国家会议国际筹备秘书处工作的中国代表郑森禹在会上发言说，中国人民衷心地欢迎即将召开的亚洲国家会议，并且已经选出了出席这个会议的代表团，组成了筹备委员会进行筹备工作。国际筹备秘书处的日本代表畑中政春在发言中要求禁止使用原子武器。
    从巴地阿拉及东旁遮普邦联盟各地来的各党派的两百多名代表二十日在巴地阿拉市举行会议。会议要求销毁一切现存的原子武器，并支持和平共处的五项原则。亚洲国家会议国际筹备秘书处的苏联代表索弗罗诺夫在会上说，即将在德里举行的亚洲国家会议将是亚洲各国人民日益增长着的团结和友谊的象征。
    【新华社新德里二十六日电】叙利亚的“创造报”，黎巴嫩的“生活报”和“消息报”等表示支持四月初在德里举行的亚洲国家会议的宗旨。这些报纸在社评中表示希望会议将表达阿拉伯人民和亚洲人民的深切的愿望，并改善亚洲国家之间的关系。黎巴嫩的“东方报”发表文章：表示希望在开罗的阿拉伯联盟重视在德里举行的亚洲国家会议，并认识这个会议的历史意义。
