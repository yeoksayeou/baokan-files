Title: Attempted Assassination of Indian Prime Minister Nehru
Author:
Date: 1955-03-14
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, New Delhi, March 13th: According to the Press Trust of India, Nagpur, Indian Prime Minister Nehru was attacked in Nagpur on the 12th, but was not injured. On the morning of that day, when Prime Minister Nehru was traveling from Sonegaon Airport to the residence of Shukla, the Chief Minister of Central Province, a rickshaw puller named Babu Rao jumped into Nehru's open-top car with a knife in an attempt to assassinate him. The Provincial Governor's aide-de-camp and some police officers immediately seized his knife and arrested him.

### 印度总理尼赫鲁遇刺

1955-03-14
第4版()
专栏：

    印度总理尼赫鲁遇刺
    【新华社新德里十三日电】据印度报业托辣斯那格浦尔消息：印度总理尼赫鲁十二日在那格浦尔遇刺，未曾受到伤害。这一天早晨，尼赫鲁总理从索奈加昂机场到中央省首都部长舒克拉官邸去的时候，一个名叫巴布·拉奥的人力车夫带着刀子跳上尼赫鲁乘坐的敞篷汽车企图行刺。省长的侍从武官和一些警官立刻夺下了他的刀子，并且把他逮捕了起来。
