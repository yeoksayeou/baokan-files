Title: French National Peace Council Publishes Open Letter to the French People, Calling on Them to Oppose Atomic War and the Threat of German Militarism
Author:
Date: 1955-03-07
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Paris, March 5th: TASS reports that the Executive Committee of the French National Peace Council has published an open letter to the French people.

The open letter points out that the French government is doing its utmost to ensure the Senate of the Republic rapidly approves the Paris Accords. At the same time, foreign countries are once again exerting pressure and making threats against France.

The open letter emphasizes that the people of both France and Germany oppose the London and Paris Accords, and calls on all French people to express that "the French people abhor policies that pose a double threat to France: the threat of atomic war and the threat from German militarism."

The Executive Committee of the French National Peace Council announces that a national peace conference will be held in Paris on April 2nd and 3rd. It points out that this conference will determine the path to strengthen the French people's struggle against the revival of German militarism, for the prohibition of atomic weapons, and for the holding of international negotiations.

### 法国全国和平理事会发表告法国人民书  号召法国人民反对原子战争和德国军国主义威胁

1955-03-07
第4版()
专栏：

    法国全国和平理事会发表告法国人民书
    号召法国人民反对原子战争和德国军国主义威胁
    【新华社六日讯】塔斯社巴黎五日讯：法国全国和平理事会执行委员会发表了告法国人民书。
    告法国人民书指出：法国政府竭力使共和国参议院迅速批准巴黎协定。同时，外国又重新对法国施行压力和进行威胁。
    告法国人民书强调法国和德国的人民都反对伦敦和巴黎协定，并且号召全体法国人表示“法国人民痛恨给法国造成两重威胁的政策。这两重威胁是：原子战争的威胁和德国军国主义方面的威胁”。
    法国全国和平理事会执行委员会宣布要在四月二日和三日在巴黎举行全国和平大会。它指出：这次大会将确定加强法国人民反对复活德国军国主义、争取禁止原子武器和争取举行国际谈判的斗争的道路。
