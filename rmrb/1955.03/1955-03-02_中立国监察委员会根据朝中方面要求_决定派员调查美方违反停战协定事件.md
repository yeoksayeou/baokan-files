Title: Neutral Nations Supervisory Commission, at the Request of the Korean-Chinese Side, Decides to Send Personnel to Investigate U.S. Violations of the Armistice Agreement

Author: 
Date: 1955-03-02
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Pyongyang, February 28th: According to the Korean Central News Agency, Kaesong, February 27th: The Neutral Nations Supervisory Commission has accepted the request made by the Korean-Chinese side on February 23rd and has decided to authorize the Mobile Neutral Nations Inspection Teams to investigate the incidents proposed by the Korean-Chinese side, such as the U.S.'s illegal import of war materials to arm the South Korean army, which violate the Armistice Agreement.

The Mobile Neutral Nations Inspection Teams, composed of commissioners from Poland, Czechoslovakia, Sweden, and Switzerland, will immediately depart for Chinhae, Pohang, Osan, Gwangju, Daejeon, and Sacheon.

### 中立国监察委员会根据朝中方面要求  决定派员调查美方违反停战协定事件

1955-03-02
第4版()
专栏：

    中立国监察委员会根据朝中方面要求
    决定派员调查美方违反停战协定事件
    【新华社平壤二十八日电】据朝鲜中央通讯社开城二十七日讯：中立国监察委员会已接受了朝中方面在二月二十三日提出的要求，决定授权机动中立国视察小组调查朝中方面提出的美方非法运入作战物资以武装南朝鲜军队等违反停战协定的事件。
    由波兰、捷克斯洛伐克、瑞典和瑞士四国委员组成的机动中立国视察小组将立刻动身到镇海、浦项、乌山、光州、大田和泗川去。
