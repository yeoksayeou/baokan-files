Title: Yemen Notifies Egypt of Agreement to Join New Arab League
Author:
Date: 1955-03-29
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, March 27th: According to Reuters, Yemen formally notified Egypt on the 26th that it agreed to join the new Arab military and economic alliance being established by Egypt, Syria, and Saudi Arabia to counter the Turkish-Iraqi pact. Yemen also notified Egypt that it would participate in the upcoming meeting of representatives of Arab countries in Cairo to discuss the establishment of this new Arab alliance.

### 也门通知埃及同意参加新阿拉伯联盟

1955-03-29
第4版()
专栏：

    也门通知埃及同意参加新阿拉伯联盟
   【新华社二十七日讯】据路透社报道，也门已经在二十六日正式通知埃及说，它同意加入埃及、叙利亚和沙特阿拉伯商定要建立的同土耳其—伊拉克条约抗衡的新阿拉伯军事和经济联盟。也门还通知埃及说，它将参加预定不久在开罗举行的阿拉伯国家代表的会议，来讨论建立这个新阿拉伯联盟的问题。
