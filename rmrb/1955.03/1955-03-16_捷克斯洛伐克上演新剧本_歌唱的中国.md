Title: Czechoslovakia Stages New Play "Singing China"
Author:
Date: 1955-03-16
Page: 4

(Xinhua News Agency, Prague, March 14) The Municipal Theater of Kladno, a famous mining city in Czechoslovakia, premiered the new play "Singing China" on the evening of March 12. The script was adapted by Moravik from the reportage work of the same name by Czechoslovak People's Artist Majerová after her visit to China. The play is divided into eight scenes, vividly depicting the deep suffering of the Chinese people in the past, their heroic struggles, and their happy lives after liberation.

Those who attended the premiere included Czechoslovak President Zápotocký, Prime Minister Široký, government ministers, and Majerová, the original author of "Singing China." Cao Ying, the Chinese Ambassador to Czechoslovakia, also attended the performance. The play was warmly welcomed by the audience.

After the performance, a tea party was held at the theater. At the meeting, President Zápotocký and Ambassador Cao Ying delivered speeches successively, mutually wishing for the eternal development of the friendship between the Chinese and Czechoslovak peoples.

### 捷克斯洛伐克上演新剧本“歌唱的中国”

1955-03-16
第4版()
专栏：

    捷克斯洛伐克上演新剧本“歌唱的中国”
    【新华社布拉格十四日电】捷克斯洛伐克著名矿工城克拉德诺的市立剧院，在三月十二日晚首次上演新剧“歌唱的中国”。剧本是莫拉维克根据捷克斯洛伐克人民艺术家玛耶洛娃访问中国后所著的同名的报告文学作品改编的。全剧共分八场，生动地描述了中国人民过去的深重苦难，他们进行的英勇斗争，以及他们解放后的幸福生活。
    出席观看首次演出的有捷克斯洛伐克共和国总统萨波托斯基、总理西罗基、政府各部部长和“歌唱的中国”的原作者玛耶洛娃。中国驻捷克斯洛伐克大使曹瑛也出席观看。这个剧受到了观众热烈的欢迎。
    演出结束后，在剧院举行了茶会。会上，萨波托斯基总统和曹瑛大使先后致词，互祝中捷两国人民友谊的永恒发展。
