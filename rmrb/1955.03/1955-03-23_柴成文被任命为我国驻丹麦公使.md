Title: Chai Chengwen Appointed as China's Envoy to Denmark
Author:
Date: 1955-03-23
Page: 1

    Xinhua News Agency, 21st.
    Chai Chengwen has been appointed by Mao Zedong, Chairman of the People's Republic of China, as the Ambassador Extraordinary and Plenipotentiary of the People's Republic of China to the Kingdom of Denmark.

### 柴成文被任命为我国驻丹麦公使

1955-03-23
第1版()
专栏：

    柴成文被任命为我国驻丹麦公使
    【新华社二十一日讯】中华人民共和国主席毛泽东任命柴成文为中华人民共和国驻丹麦王国特命全权公使。
