Title: Former Mexican President Cárdenas Says China Has the Right to Liberate Its Islands
Author:
Date: 1955-03-22
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, New York, March 20th: According to TASS, former Mexican President Cárdenas said in an interview with "Impact" magazine that the People's Republic of China "has the right to liberate its islands and obtain sovereignty over its territory. We also wish to enjoy such rights."

Cárdenas also formally expressed his opposition to the use of atomic bombs.

### 前墨西哥总统卡德纳斯说  中国有权解放自己的岛屿

1955-03-22
第1版()
专栏：

    前墨西哥总统卡德纳斯说
    中国有权解放自己的岛屿
   【新华社二十一日讯】据塔斯社纽约二十日讯：前墨西哥总统卡德纳斯在接见“冲击”杂志记者时说，中华人民共和国“有权解放它的岛屿和对它的领土取得主权。这样的权利也是我们所愿意享受的”。
  卡德纳斯还正式表示反对使用原子弹。
