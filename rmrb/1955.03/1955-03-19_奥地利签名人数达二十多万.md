Title: Over Two Hundred Thousand Signatures in Austria
Author:
Date: 1955-03-19
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Vienna, March 15th (TASS): The signature collection campaign in Austria for the World Peace Council Standing Committee's "Appeal to the People of the World" is proceeding victoriously. To date, more than 200,000 signatures have been collected.

### 奥地利签名人数达二十多万

1955-03-19
第4版()
专栏：

    奥地利签名人数达二十多万
    【新华社十六日讯】　据塔斯社维也纳十五日讯：在奥地利，为世界和平理事会常务委员会的“告全世界人民书”征集签名的运动正在胜利地进行。到现在为止，签名的已有二十多万人。
