Title: Stop the Enemies of Peace from Using Atomic Weapons
Author: 
Date: 1955-03-14
Page: 4

    The Democratic Republic of Vietnam Begins Signing
    On the evening of the 10th, representatives from all walks of life in Hanoi, the capital of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam, solemnly held a ceremony to begin the signature campaign against atomic weapons. More than 300 representatives from various democratic parties, people's organizations, and religious organizations signed the signature book against atomic weapons.
    Le Dinh Tham, Chairman of the Vietnamese Committee for the Signature Campaign Against Atomic Weapons, said at the meeting that the Vietnamese people, together with the people of the world, are responding to the call against atomic weapons. He said that if those who sign contribute their strength against atomic weapons, there will be sufficient strength to stop the enemies of peace from using atomic weapons, and they will be forced to destroy atomic weapons according to the will of the people.
    From the 11th, signature campaigns against atomic weapons have been widely launched in Hanoi's agencies, schools, and factories.
    More Than 8 Million Signatures in Romania
    The Romanian people's signature campaign against the use of atomic weapons has achieved great success. As of March 9th, 8,770,000 people across the country have signed the World Peace Council Standing Committee's appeal to the people of the world.
    The signature campaign is continuing to deepen.
    Mass Rallies Held Throughout Bulgaria
    Mass rallies against the preparation of atomic warfare have been held throughout Bulgaria. The masses attending the meetings unanimously signed the World Peace Council Standing Committee's appeal to the people of the world.
    As of now, more than 500,000 signatures have been collected in the three regions of Sofia, Plovdiv, and Tirnovo.
    The signature campaign is continuing.
    Signature Campaign Continues in Indonesia
    According to a report in the Indonesian "People's Daily" on the 9th, the East Java Branch of the All-Indonesia Central Trade Union Federation has decided to collect 300,000 signatures within one month. The branch now has 1,239 members who have signed. The newspaper said that the Central Java Branch of the Indonesian People's Youth League has issued a statement supporting the signature campaign against the preparation of atomic warfare.
    On the 8th, signature campaigns also began in Palembang from all walks of life. Within two days, more than 1,000 people in the area had signed.
    Dr. Rustamaji, Chairman of the Palembang Local Legislative Council, was the first person to sign in Palembang.
    People from All Walks of Life in Argentina Support the Signature Campaign
    The campaign to collect signatures for the World Peace Council Standing Committee's appeal to the people of the world has begun in Argentina. According to preliminary statistics released by the Argentine Peace Committee, in the first few days, 6,000 people in the city of Buenos Aires, as well as in the provinces of Buenos Aires and Santa Fe, have signed. The signature campaign has received support from the broadest strata of people, with workers, intellectuals, and some members of the National Assembly signing against atomic warfare. National Assembly members Sanchi and Allende are preparing to submit a proposal to the House of Representatives requesting a ban on nuclear weapons.

### 制止和平的敌人使用原子武器

1955-03-14
第4版()
专栏：

    制止和平的敌人使用原子武器
    越南民主共和国开始签名
    十日晚，越南民主共和国首都河内各界代表隆重举行了开始反对原子武器签名运动的仪式。到会的三百多名各民主党派、人民团体和宗教组织等的代表，都在反对原子武器的签名书上签了名。
    越南反对原子武器签名运动委员会主任委员黎廷探在会上致词说，越南人民和世界人民一起响应反对原子武器的号召。他说，如果签名的人都为反对原子武器贡献自己的力量，就有充分的力量制止和平的敌人使用原子武器，就将迫使他们按照人民的意愿销毁原子武器。
    十一日起，河内各机关、学校、工厂普遍展开了反对原子武器的签名运动。
    罗马尼亚签名者已有八百多万人
    罗马尼亚人民反对使用原子武器的签名运动已经取得了巨大成就。到三月九日为止，全国已经有八百七十七万人在世界和平理事会常务委员会告全世界人民书上签了名。
    签名运动仍在继续深入开展中。
    保加利亚各地举行群众集会
    保加利亚各地纷纷举行反对准备原子战争的群众集会。到会群众一致在世界和平理事会常务委员会告全世界人民书上签名。
    截至目前为止，索非亚、普罗夫迪夫和第尔诺夫三个地区已经征集了五十多万个签名。
    签名运动在继续进行中。
    印度尼西亚继续开展签名运动
    据印度尼西亚“人民日报”九日报道，印度尼西亚总工会东爪哇分会已决定在一个月之内征集三十万人的签名。该分会现在已经有一千二百三十九名会员参加签名。这家报纸说，印度尼西亚人民青年团中爪哇分会已经发表声明，支持反对准备原子战争的签名运动。
    八日，巨港各阶层也开始了签名运动。在两天以内，该地参加签名的已经有一千多人。
    三宝垅地方立法议会主席鲁斯塔曼杰博士是第一个在三宝垅签名的人。
    阿根廷各阶层人士支持签名运动
    为世界和平理事会常务委员会告全世界人民书征集签名的运动已经在阿根廷开始。根据阿根廷和平委员会所发表的初步统计数字，一开始的几天中，在布宜诺斯艾利斯市以及在布宜诺斯艾利斯省和圣菲省已经有六千人签了名。签名运动得到最广泛阶层的人们的支持，工人、知识分子和一些国民议会议员都签名反对原子战争。国民议会议员桑齐和阿伦德准备在众议院提出一项要求禁止核子武器的提案。
