Title: Our Great Motherland (Picture)
Author:
Date: 1955-03-03
Page: 1

    Our Great Motherland
    After the liberation of all the coastal islands of Zhejiang Province, shipping between Shanghai and Wenzhou has been unimpeded. These are the army and navy soldiers guarding the frontline of Zhejiang's coastal defense. They are vigilantly defending the motherland's seas and skies, preventing enemy harassment, and ensuring the safety of navigation.

### 我们伟大的祖国（图片）

1955-03-03
第1版()
专栏：

    我们伟大的祖国
    浙江省沿海所有岛屿解放后，上海和温州之间的航运已畅通无阻。这是守卫在浙江海防前线的陆军和海军战士。他们正警惕地保卫着祖国的海洋和天空，防止敌人骚扰，保证航行的安全。
