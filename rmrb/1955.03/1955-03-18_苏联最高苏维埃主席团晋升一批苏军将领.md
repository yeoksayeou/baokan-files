Title: The Presidium of the Supreme Soviet of the USSR Promotes a Group of Soviet Army Generals
Author:
Date: 1955-03-18
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Moscow: According to TASS, the Presidium of the Supreme Soviet of the USSR issued an order on March 11 conferring the rank of Marshal of the Soviet Union on Army Generals I. Kh. Bagramyan, S. S. Biryuzov, A. A. Grechko, A. I. Yeremenko, K. S. Moskalenko, and V. I. Chuikov. Air Marshal P. F. Zhigarev was awarded the rank of Chief Marshal of Aviation.

Air Force Generals S. I. Rudenko and V. A. Sudets were awarded the rank of Air Marshal.

At the same time, Artillery Generals S. S. Varentsov and V. I. Kazakov were awarded the rank of Marshal of Artillery.

On March 12, the Council of Ministers of the USSR decided to promote Army General P. Batov to the rank of Army General.

### 苏联最高苏维埃主席团晋升一批苏军将领

1955-03-18
第4版()
专栏：

    苏联最高苏维埃主席团晋升一批苏军将领
    【新华社讯】据塔斯社莫斯科讯：苏联最高苏维埃主席团在三月十一日发布命令，授予伊·赫·巴格拉米扬、谢·谢·比留佐夫、安·安·格列奇科、安·伊·叶雷曼科、基·谢·莫斯卡伦科和瓦·伊·崔可夫等陆军大将以苏联元帅军衔。授予空军元帅帕·费·日加列夫以空军主帅军衔。
    授予空军上将谢·伊·鲁金科和弗·亚·苏吉茨以空军元帅军衔。
    同时，还授予炮兵上将谢·谢·瓦连佐夫和瓦·伊·卡扎科夫以炮兵元帅军衔。
    三月十二日，苏联部长会议决定将陆军上将帕·巴托夫晋升为陆军大将。
