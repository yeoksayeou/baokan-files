Title: French Peace Supporters Delegation Arrives in Hanoi
Author:
Date: 1955-03-13
Page: 4

    Xinhua News Agency, Hanoi, March 12th: A delegation of French peace supporters, led by Madame Yves Farge, arrived in Hanoi on the afternoon of the 11th for a visit.
    Greeting them at the station were Le Dinh Tham, Chairman of the Vietnam Committee for the Defense of World Peace, Ho Dac Di, President of the Vietnam-France Friendship Association, and more than 10,000 representatives of workers, peasants, and religious organizations.
    Le Dinh Tham delivered a welcome speech at the station. Madame Yves Farge then spoke, pointing out that this was the first delegation of the French people to arrive in Vietnam, and expressed the hope that it would pave the way for friendly exchanges between the two peoples in the future. She said: We are determined to work hard to ensure that the Geneva Accords and France's signature are respected, because we firmly believe that an atmosphere of peace and friendship will allow various issues between the two peoples to be resolved on an equal footing.

### 法和平拥护者代表团到河内

1955-03-13
第4版()
专栏：

    法和平拥护者代表团到河内
    【新华社河内十二日电】　法国和平拥护者代表团由伊夫·法奇夫人率领在十一日下午到达河内访问。
    在车站欢迎的有越南保卫世界和平委员会主席黎廷探、越法友好协会会长胡得怡和工人、农民、宗教团体的代表一万多人。
    黎廷探在车站致欢迎词。伊夫·法奇夫人接着讲话，她指出这是第一个到达越南的法国人民代表团，希望它将为未来的两国人民的友好交往铺平道路。她说：我们决心努力使日内瓦协定和法国的签字得到尊重，因为我们坚信和平友好的气氛将使两国人民之间的各种问题在平等的基础上得到解决。
