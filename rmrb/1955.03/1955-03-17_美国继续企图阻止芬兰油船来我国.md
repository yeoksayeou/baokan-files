Title: The United States Continues Attempts to Prevent Finnish Oil Tanker from Coming to Our Country
Author:
Date: 1955-03-17
Page: 1

    Xinhua News Agency, New York, March 15th (TASS): According to a Washington radio broadcast, McClellan, chairman of the U.S. Senate Government Operations Committee, said that the United States will take measures to prevent the Finnish oil tanker "Aruba" from reaching China.
    Xinhua News Agency, London, March 15th (TASS): According to a London radio broadcast, a British Foreign Office spokesman said on the 15th that the British representative in Helsinki is "maintaining contact" with the Finnish Foreign Ministry regarding the "Aruba" oil tanker issue. The Foreign Office spokesman said that various British ports in the Far East will refuse to refuel this oil tanker.

### 美国继续企图阻止芬兰油船来我国

1955-03-17
第1版()
专栏：

    美国继续企图阻止芬兰油船来我国
    【新华社讯】塔斯社纽约十五日电：据华盛顿电台广播，美国参议员政府工作委员会主席麦克利兰说：美国将采取措施，不使装载燃料的芬兰油船“阿鲁巴号”到达中国。
    【新华社讯】塔斯社伦敦十五日电：据伦敦电台广播，英国外交部发言人十五日说，英国在赫尔辛基的代表正就“阿鲁巴号”油船问题同芬兰外交部“保持联系”。外交部发言人说，英国在远东的各个港口将拒绝为这艘油船添加燃料。
