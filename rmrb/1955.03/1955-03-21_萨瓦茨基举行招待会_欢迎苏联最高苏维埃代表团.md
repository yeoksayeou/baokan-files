Title: Zawadzki Hosts Reception Welcoming Soviet Supreme Soviet Delegation
Author:
Date: 1955-03-21
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Warsaw, March 19th: According to TASS, the Chairman of the State Council of the Polish People's Republic, Zawadzki, held a reception on the 18th to welcome the Soviet Supreme Soviet delegation headed by Volkov.

Among those attending the reception were Polish United Workers' Party and government leaders Bierut and Cyrankiewicz, Speaker of the Polish Parliament Dąbrowski, and representatives from all sectors of Polish society.

Soviet Ambassador to Poland Mikhailov and many foreign diplomatic envoys to Poland also attended the reception.

### 萨瓦茨基举行招待会  欢迎苏联最高苏维埃代表团

1955-03-21
第4版()
专栏：

    萨瓦茨基举行招待会
    欢迎苏联最高苏维埃代表团
   【新华社二十日讯】据塔斯社华沙十九日讯：波兰人民共和国国务委员会主席萨瓦茨基在十八日举行招待会，欢迎以沃尔科夫为首的苏联最高苏维埃代表团。
    出席招待会的有：波兰统一工人党和政府的领导人贝鲁特和西伦凯维兹等，波兰议会议长邓博夫斯基以及波兰社会各界的代表。
    苏联驻波兰大使米哈伊洛夫和许多国家驻波兰外交使节也出席了招待会。
