Title: Asian Countries Conference to be Held in Delhi Starting April 6th; Indian Preparatory Committee Chairman, Mrs. Rala Nehru, Writes About the Conference's Purpose
Author:
Date: 1955-03-30
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Delhi, March 28th: According to TASS, an Asian Countries Conference will be held in Delhi from April 6th to 10th to discuss easing international tensions. Public delegations from thirteen countries will participate, including the Soviet Union, the People's Republic of China, India, the Democratic People's Republic of Korea, the Democratic Republic of Vietnam, Japan, and Burma.

    The "Delhi Times" weekly wrote that the purpose of the conference is to exchange opinions on issues related to Asian countries. The central agenda will be the Five Principles of Peaceful Coexistence proposed by Zhou Enlai and Nehru in their joint statement.

    Indian newspapers have pointed out the great importance of this conference for the cause of Asian peace. For example, the "New Century" weekly said that this historically significant conference will make a major contribution to strengthening peace and promoting understanding among Asian countries. This conference has already aroused great interest among various political and social groups in these countries. The "New Century" weekly emphasized that the people of Asia place great hopes on the Delhi conference and are convinced that this conference will help ease tensions in this region of the world.

    Xinhua News Agency, New Delhi, March 29th: Mrs. Rameshwari Nehru, Chairman of the Indian Preparatory Committee for the Asian Countries Conference, published an article in the "Hindustan Standard" on the 27th, saying that the purpose of the Asian Countries Conference will be to provide an opportunity to exchange views on common issues related to Asia. Such an exchange of views will help the people of Asian countries achieve greater common understanding and closer ties in all aspects, thereby promoting cooperation and solidarity among the people of Asian countries. It will also help expand the peace zone and help increase the peaceful atmosphere we all desire. She said that there is no doubt that the conference will make a great contribution to strengthening ties between Asian countries and establishing peace in Asia and the world.

### 亚洲国家会议四月六日起将在德里举行  印度筹委会主席拉·尼赫鲁夫人撰文谈会议的目的

1955-03-30
第1版()
专栏：

    亚洲国家会议四月六日起将在德里举行
    印度筹委会主席拉·尼赫鲁夫人撰文谈会议的目的
   【新华社二十九日讯】据塔斯社德里二十八日讯：亚洲国家会议将在四月六日到十日在德里举行，来讨论缓和国际紧张局势问题。参加会议的将有十三个国家的公众代表团，其中包括苏联、中华人民共和国、印度、朝鲜民主主义人民共和国、越南民主共和国、日本和缅甸。
    “德里时代”周刊写道，会议的目的是要就同亚洲国家有关的问题交换意见。中心议程将是周恩来和尼赫鲁在他们的联合声明中所提出的和平共处五项原则。
    印度报纸指出这次会议对亚洲和平事业的巨大重要性。例如，“新世纪”周报说，这个具有历史意义的会议将在加强和平和促进亚洲国家间的了解方面作出重大贡献。这个会议已经在这些国家的各种政治和社会团体中引起了很大的兴趣。“新世纪”周报强调说，亚洲人民对德里会议寄以很大希望，而且确信这个会议将有助于缓和世界这一地区的紧张局势。
    【新华社新德里二十九日讯】亚洲国家会议印度筹备委员会主席拉米希瓦里·尼赫鲁夫人二十七日在“印度斯坦旗报”发表文章说，亚洲国家会议的目的将是对与亚洲有关的共同问题提供交换意见的机会。这样的交换意见将有助于亚洲各国人民在各个方面取得更大的共同谅解和紧密的联系，从而促进亚洲国家人民之间的合作和团结。它还将有助于扩大和平地区，有助于增加我们大家都渴望的和平气氛。她说，毫无疑问，会议在加强亚洲国家的联系、建立亚洲和世界和平方面，将会作出巨大的贡献。
