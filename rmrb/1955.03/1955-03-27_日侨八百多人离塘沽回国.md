Title: More than 800 Japanese Nationals Leave Tanggu to Return Home
Author:
Date: 1955-03-27
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Tianjin, March 25th: Eight hundred and sixteen Japanese nationals who wished to return to their country left Tanggu New Port on the Japanese ship "Kouan Maru" on the 25th to return to Japan.

    At the farewell meeting held at the pier, Li Yunke, president of the Tianjin branch of the Chinese Red Cross, delivered a farewell speech to all the Japanese nationals. He wished the returning Japanese nationals a safe journey and hoped that they would bring the friendship of the Chinese people to the millions of Japanese friends across the ocean.

    Abe Qu, the representative of the returning Japanese nationals, expressed his gratitude for the deep friendship of the Chinese people in his reply.

### 日侨八百多人离塘沽回国

1955-03-27
第1版()
专栏：

    日侨八百多人离塘沽回国
    【新华社天津二十五日电】愿意回国的日侨八百一十六人在二十五日乘日轮“兴安丸”离开塘沽新港返回日本。
    在码头举行的送别会上，中国红十字会天津市分会会长李允恪向全体日侨致送别词。他预祝回国日侨一路平安，并希望把中国人民的友谊带给海洋彼岸千千万万的日本朋友。
    回国日侨代表阿部渠在致答词时对中国人民的深厚友情表示感谢。
