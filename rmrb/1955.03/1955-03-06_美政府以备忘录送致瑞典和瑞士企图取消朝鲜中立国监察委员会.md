Title: U.S. Government Sends Memorandum to Sweden and Switzerland Seeking to Abolish the Neutral Nations Supervisory Commission in Korea
Author:
Date: 1955-03-06
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, March 5th—The U.S. government released on March 3rd a memorandum it sent separately to the governments of Sweden and Switzerland, stating that "it is doubtful whether the Neutral Nations Supervisory Commission continues to be of any use" and expressing that "the Neutral Nations Supervisory Commission should be abolished." While expressing doubts about the role of the Neutral Nations Supervisory Commission, the memorandum is very active in calling for its abolition, "hoping that the Swedish (Swiss) government will continue to conduct urgent consultations on this issue and await the outcome of these consultations with concern." In the memorandum, the U.S. government also attributed the U.S.'s consistent obstructionist activities against the Neutral Nations Supervisory Commission to the Korean-Chinese side, attempting to evade responsibility for its past consistent obstruction and sabotage, as well as its current formal advocacy for abolishing the Neutral Nations Supervisory Commission, through slander.

### 美政府以备忘录送致瑞典和瑞士企图取消朝鲜中立国监察委员会

1955-03-06
第1版()
专栏：

    美政府以备忘录送致瑞典和瑞士企图取消朝鲜中立国监察委员会
    【新华社五日讯】美国政府在三月三日公布了它分别送致瑞典和瑞士政府的一份备忘录，借口“中立国监察委员会继续下去，是否有什么用处，是可以怀疑的”，表示“中立国监察委员会应予撤销”。备忘录一方面对于中立国监察委员会的作用表示怀疑，另一方面对于取消中立国监察委员会则表示得很积极，“希望瑞典（瑞士）政府继续就这个问题进行紧急性质的磋商，并且关心地等待着这种磋商的结果”。美国政府还在备忘录里将美方对于中立国监察委员会的一贯的阻挠活动说成是来自朝中方面，企图用诬蔑办法来逃避其过去一贯阻挠和破坏以及现在竟正式主张取消中立国监察委员会的责任。
