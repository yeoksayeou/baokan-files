Title: Vietnamese Trade Delegation Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1955-03-11
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, March 10th: The trade delegation of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam, led by Nguyen Van Tran, head of the delegation and Deputy Minister of Transport and Public Works of Vietnam, arrived in Beijing by train on the afternoon of the 10th to conduct trade negotiations between China and Vietnam for the year 1955.

Those who went to the station to welcome the delegation included Kong Yuan, Deputy Minister of Foreign Trade of China, Jiang Ming, Director of the First Bureau, Yang Mian, Director of the Protocol Department of the Ministry of Foreign Trade, and Ye Jinghao, Commercial Counselor of the Chinese Embassy in the Democratic Republic of Vietnam.

Zhou Liang, Charge d'Affaires ad interim of the Embassy of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam in China, and Fan Ping, Second Secretary, also went to welcome them.

### 越南贸易代表团到京

1955-03-11
第4版()
专栏：

    越南贸易代表团到京
    【新华社十日讯】前来我国进行一九五五年度中越贸易谈判的越南民主共和国贸易代表团，由团长、越南交通公政部副部长阮文珍率领，在十日下午乘车到达北京。
    前往车站迎接的有我国对外贸易部副部长孔原、第一局局长江明、交际处处长杨勉和我国驻越南民主共和国大使馆商务参赞叶景灏等。
    越南民主共和国驻华大使馆临时代办周亮、二等秘书范平等也前往迎接。
