Title: Broadcast of Lectures on Materialism to Begin
Author:
Date: 1955-03-19
Page: 3

    Broadcast of Lectures on Materialism to Begin
    To promote materialistic thought and criticize bourgeois idealist thought, the Central People's Broadcasting Station will host lectures on materialism.
    Starting next week, recordings of lectures organized by the Beijing Municipal Committee of the Chinese Communist Party, the All-China Federation of Literary and Art Circles, and other organizations on this topic will be broadcast every Monday, Wednesday, and Friday at 2:00 PM. Broadcasts of the lecture manuscripts will be replayed by announcers every Tuesday, Thursday, and Saturday from 7:00 AM to 8:00 AM.
    Starting on March 21st, a report by Yang Xianzhen, Vice President of the Marx-Lenin Institute, on "The Struggle Between the Communist Worldview and the Subjective Idealist Worldview" will be broadcast continuously. Comrade Yang Xianzhen's report elucidates some basic issues of the dialectical materialistic worldview.

### 唯物主义讲座的广播将开始

1955-03-19
第3版()
专栏：

    唯物主义讲座的广播将开始
    中央人民广播电台为了宣传唯物主义思想和批判资产阶级唯心主义思想，将举办唯物主义讲座。
    从下星期起，在每星期一、三、五的十四点钟，播送中共北京市委、全国文联等单位所组织的关于这方面的演讲录音，并在每星期二、四、六的早晨七点钟到八点钟，由播音员重播演讲稿。
    从三月二十一日起，将连续广播马克思列宁学院副院长杨献珍关于“共产主义世界观与主观唯心主义世界观的斗争”的报告。杨献珍同志的报告，阐明了辩证唯物主义世界观的一些基本问题。
