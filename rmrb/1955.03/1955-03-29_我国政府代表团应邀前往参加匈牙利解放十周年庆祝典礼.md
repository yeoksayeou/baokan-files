Title: Chinese Government Delegation Invited to Attend the 10th Anniversary Celebration of Hungary's Liberation
Author: 
Date: 1955-03-29
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, March 28th: At the invitation of the Government of the Hungarian People's Republic to attend the celebrations for the 10th anniversary of the liberation of Hungary, the Government Delegation of the People's Republic of China, led by Deng Zihui, Vice Premier of the State Council, left Beijing for Hungary by plane on March 28.
    Those who saw them off at the airport included: Chang Lifu, Deputy Secretary-General of the State Council; Dong Yueqian, Director of the General Office of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs; Chen Chu, Director of the Soviet and East European Affairs Department of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs, and others.
    Siklós Skótán, Ambassador of the Hungarian People's Republic to China, and embassy staff also saw them off at the airport.
    The list of the Government Delegation of the People's Republic of China is as follows:
    Head of Delegation: Deng Zihui, Vice Premier of the State Council
    Members: Yang Xiandong, Vice Minister of Agriculture
    Hao Deqing, Ambassador Extraordinary and Plenipotentiary of the People's Republic of China to the Hungarian People's Republic (already in Hungary)

### 我国政府代表团应邀前往参加匈牙利解放十周年庆祝典礼

1955-03-29
第1版()
专栏：

    我国政府代表团应邀前往参加匈牙利解放十周年庆祝典礼
    【新华社二十八日讯】应匈牙利人民共和国政府的邀请前往参加匈牙利解放十周年庆祝典礼的中华人民共和国政府代表团，由国务院副总理邓子恢率领，在三月二十八日乘飞机离京去匈牙利。
    到机场送行的有：国务院副秘书长常黎夫、外交部办公厅主任董越千、外交部苏联东欧司司长陈楚等。
    匈牙利人民共和国驻华大使斯克拉丹和使馆人员也到机场送行。
    中华人民共和国政府代表团名单如下：
    团长  邓子恢国务院副总理
    团员  杨显东农业部副部长
    郝德青中华人民共和国驻匈牙利人民共和国特命全权大使（已在匈牙利）
