Title: U.S. Increases "Aid" to the Chiang Kai-shek Traitorous Clique
Author:
Date: 1955-03-15
Page: 1

    Xinhua News Agency, Washington, March 14th: The U.S. Foreign Operations Administration announced on the 13th that it is giving the Chiang Kai-shek traitorous clique another $48 million to help "defend" Taiwan.

    According to the Associated Press, with this new aid allocation, the total amount of military, economic, and technical assistance provided by the United States to the Chiang Kai-shek traitorous clique this year has increased to $138 million, an increase of $27 million over last year's total aid.

    The continuous increase in U.S. "aid" to the Chiang Kai-shek traitorous clique signifies the increasing U.S. armed intervention in China. However, the historical fact of Chiang Kai-shek's defeat on the Chinese mainland shows that even billions of dollars cannot save him from his fate of destruction.

### 美国对蒋介石卖国集团增加“援助”

1955-03-15
第1版()
专栏：

    美国对蒋介石卖国集团增加“援助”
    【新华社十四日讯】华盛顿消息：美国援外事务管理署十三日宣布，它正在再给予蒋介石卖国集团四千八百万美元，以帮助“保卫”台湾。
    据合众社说，加上这笔新的援助拨款，今年到目前为止美国供应蒋介石卖国集团的军事、经济和技术援助的总数就增加到一亿三千八百万美元，比去年的援助总数增加了二千七百万美元。
    美国对蒋介石卖国集团的“援助”的不断增加意味着美国对中国的武装干涉行为的日益加甚。但是，蒋介石在中国大陆溃败的历史事实说明，即使是成十亿的美元也挽救不了他的覆没的命运。
