Title: Turkey Decides to Attend Asian-African Conference
Author:
Date: 1955-03-20
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, Jakarta, March 19th. The Joint Secretariat of the Asian-African Conference announced on the 19th that the Turkish government has replied to the Indonesian government, stating that Turkey has decided to attend the Asian-African Conference.

### 土耳其决定参加亚非会议

1955-03-20
第1版()
专栏：

    土耳其决定参加亚非会议
    【新华社雅加达十九日电】亚非会议联合秘书处十九日宣布，土耳其政府已经复电印度尼西亚政府，表示土耳其决定参加亚非会议。
