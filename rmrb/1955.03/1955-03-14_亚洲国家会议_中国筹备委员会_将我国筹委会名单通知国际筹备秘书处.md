Title: "Asian Countries Conference" China Preparatory Committee Notifies International Preparatory Secretariat of China's Preparatory Committee List
Author:
Date: 1955-03-14
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, March 13th: The China Preparatory Committee for the "Asian Countries Conference" notified Mrs. Rameshwari Nehru, Chairman of the International Preparatory Secretariat for the "Asian Countries Conference" in New Delhi, on the 12th of the members of this preparatory committee. The preparatory committee was formally established on the 11th.

The Chairman of the China Preparatory Committee for the "Asian Countries Conference" is: Guo Moruo.

The members are: Saifuddin, Li Siguang, Mao Dun, Li Dequan, Hou Debang, Ding Xilin, Ding Ling, Gao Chongmin, Liao Chengzhi, Liu Ningyi, Chu Tunan, Wu Yaozong, Chen Hansheng, Hua Luogeng, Jin Zhonghua, Chen Jingyu, Liu Guanyi, Meng Yongqian, Mei Gongbin, Ba Jin.

### “亚洲国家会议”中国筹备委员会  将我国筹委会名单通知国际筹备秘书处

1955-03-14
第1版()
专栏：

    “亚洲国家会议”中国筹备委员会
    将我国筹委会名单通知国际筹备秘书处
    【新华社十三日讯】“亚洲国家会议”中国筹备委员会十二日将这个筹备委员会的组成人员通知新德里“亚洲国家会议”国际筹备秘书处主席拉米希瓦里·尼赫鲁夫人。筹备委员会已在十一日正式成立。
