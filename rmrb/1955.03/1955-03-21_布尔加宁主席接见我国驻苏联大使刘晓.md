Title: Chairman Bulganin Receives Chinese Ambassador to the Soviet Union Liu Xiao
Author:
Date: 1955-03-21
Page: 1

    Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, March 20th: TASS, Moscow, March 19th: Chairman of the Council of Ministers of the Soviet Union, N.A. Bulganin, received the Ambassador Extraordinary and Plenipotentiary of the People's Republic of China to the Soviet Union, Liu Xiao, on the 19th. Present at the reception was the First Vice-Chairman of the Council of Ministers of the Soviet Union, A.I. Mikoyan.

### 布尔加宁主席接见我国驻苏联大使刘晓

1955-03-21
第1版()
专栏：

    布尔加宁主席接见我国驻苏联大使刘晓
    【新华社二十日讯】塔斯社莫斯科十九日讯：苏联部长会议主席尼·亚·布尔加宁十九日接见了中华人民共和国驻苏联特命全权大使刘晓。接见时在座的有苏联部长会议第一副主席阿·伊·米高扬。
