Title: Syrian Prime Minister al-Asali States Determination to Safeguard Syria's Sovereignty and Independence
Author:
Date: 1955-03-27
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency, Beirut, March 25th: Damascus dispatch, al-Asali, the Syrian Prime Minister, stated on the 24th when receiving a delegation of students from the University of Syria that his government strictly adheres to the government's declaration on foreign policy issues. Al-Asali said that the government pays close attention to safeguarding Syria's sovereignty and independence and will continue to implement this policy direction.

The Prime Minister, when discussing Syria’s attitude towards Turkey, said that this attitude has already been stated in the official gazette of March 22nd. The Syrian government stated in that gazette its opposition to the attitude of the Turkish government, and expressed that it will implement a policy aimed at ensuring Syria's independence and security, regardless of all crude methods and pressures.

### 叙利亚总理阿萨利表示  决维护叙利亚的主权和独立

1955-03-27
第4版()
专栏：

    叙利亚总理阿萨利表示
    决维护叙利亚的主权和独立
    【新华社二十六日讯】塔斯社贝鲁特二十五日讯：大马士革消息，叙利亚总理阿萨利二十四日在接见叙利亚大学学生代表团时表示，他的政府在外交政策问题上是严格遵守政府的声明的。阿萨利说，政府深切注意维护叙利亚的主权和独立，并将继续执行这一政策方针。
    总理谈到叙利亚对土耳其的态度时说，这种态度已经在三月二十二日的官方公报中表明了。叙利亚政府在那个公报中表示反对土耳其政府的态度，并且表示，不顾一切粗暴的手段和压力，它都要执行目的在于保障叙利亚的独立和安全的政策。
