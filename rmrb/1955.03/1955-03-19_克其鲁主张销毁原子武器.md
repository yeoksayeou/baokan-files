Title: Kitchlew Advocates for the Destruction of Atomic Weapons
Author:
Date: 1955-03-19
Page: 4

    Xinhua News Agency, New Delhi, March 17th. The Meerut City Peace Committee of Uttar Pradesh, India, held a public gathering in Meerut on the 15th regarding the launch of a signature campaign to ban atomic weapons.

    Dr. Kitchlew, Chairman of the All-India Peace Council, delivered a speech at the meeting. He said: All destructive weapons like atomic bombs should be destroyed, and all plans aimed at depriving any country of its freedom should be abandoned so that people of all countries can live in a peaceful environment.

    Dr. Kitchlew said that the American rulers are trying to ignite the flames of war by using Asians to fight Asians. They intend to undermine peace in Asia through their new war plans. However, we will not allow their conspiracies to be realized.

### 克其鲁主张销毁原子武器

1955-03-19
第4版()
专栏：

    克其鲁主张销毁原子武器
    【新华社新德里十七日电】　印度北方省密拉特市和平委员会十五日在密拉特召开了关于开展禁止原子武器签名运动的公众集会。
    全印和平理事会主席克其鲁博士在会上发表演说。他说：一切像原子弹这样的毁灭性武器都应该予以销毁，一切目的在于剥夺任何国家的自由的计划都应该予以放弃，以便世界各国人民都能在和平的环境里生活。
    克其鲁博士说，美国统治者企图用亚洲人打亚洲人的办法来燃起战火。他们打算通过他们的新的战争计划来破坏亚洲的和平。但是，我们不会让他们的阴谋得到实现。
