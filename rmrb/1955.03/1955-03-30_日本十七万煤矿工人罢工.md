Title: 170,000 Japanese Coal Miners Strike
Author:
Date: 1955-03-30
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Tokyo, March 29th: Approximately 170,000 coal miners in 98 coal mines in Hokkaido, the Tohoku region of Honshu, and Kyushu staged a 24-hour strike on the 28th, demanding a monthly wage increase of 3,000 yen.

Union leaders stated that if the management does not agree to the workers' demands, the miners will hold a larger-scale strike.

This coal miners' strike and the private railway workers' strike on the 27th are both part of the spring wage increase campaign decided by the General Council of Trade Unions of Japan.

At the same time, workers in the chemical industry and other sectors have also carried out strikes or demonstrations demanding wage increases.

### 日本十七万煤矿工人罢工

1955-03-30
第4版()
专栏：

    日本十七万煤矿工人罢工
    【新华社二十九日讯】东京消息：约十七万煤矿工人二十八日在北海道、本州东北地区和九州的共九十八个煤矿，举行了二十四小时罢工，要求把每月工资增加三千日元。
    工会的负责人表示：如果资方不答应工人的要求，矿工们将要举行规模更大的罢工。
    这次的煤矿工人罢工和二十七日的私营铁道工人的罢工，都是日本工会总评议会决定展开的春季要求提高工资运动的一部分。
    同时，化学工业等部门的工人也进行了要求提高工资的罢工或示威游行。
