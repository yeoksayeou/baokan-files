Title: Soviet Union Widely Uses Tracer Atoms to Improve Production
Author:
Date: 1955-03-28
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, March 27th: Soviet scientists are not only using tracer atoms to determine the condition of molten iron and slag in blast furnaces, but also using tracer atoms to determine the gas flow within blast furnaces, in order to improve the blast furnace production process.

According to the Soviet "Literary Gazette," scientists, when using tracer atoms to determine gas flow in blast furnaces, place small glass bottles containing radioactive material—the inert gas hydrogen—inside a metal box containing gunpowder (the small box resembles a artillery shell). The metal box is then placed on one of the tuyeres used to send hot air into the furnace. The circuit is connected, causing an explosion. In the explosion, the radioactive hydrogen diffuses into the gas within the blast furnace. At this point, a special detector, relying on the tracer atoms, continuously displays the gas flow situation inside the blast furnace every one-fiftieth of a second, and automatically records this situation on film. Based on the recorded material, it becomes possible to understand the ironmaking process, reduce coke consumption, and increase blast furnace productivity.

Among the instruments used to measure gas flow, a particularly precise electrolytic cell with an intensifier is used, which can determine gas flow based on the ionization caused by radioactive substances. The intensifier of the electrolytic cell increases the strength of the electric field formed by electrons by 600,000 times, thus allowing the recording of each individual particle.
Xinhua News Agency: According to the Soviet "Literary Gazette," Soviet scientists have greatly increased fish production and reduced the labor involved in fish farming by using tracer atom methods to study fish farming.

Soviet scientists have designed a "Type Б device" to study the use of organic matter and mineral fertilizers in fish ponds. They put radioactive phosphorus into these substances and then placed them in the water. Using the "Type Б device," they study the absorption of nutrients by recording the impulses of the radioactive phosphorus. According to experiments, nutrients are absorbed fastest by bacteria and aquatic plants in the water, followed by small invertebrates, and finally by fish. At the same time, the speed and quantity of nutrient absorption by fish can also be determined. Based on this information, scientists have formulated the best feeding regime for raising valuable carp and herring, increasing fish production from 80 to 100 kilograms per hectare of water surface to 400 to 500 kilograms.

Soviet scientists also use tracer atom methods to calculate the number of fish in fish ponds. This calculation is very precise and particularly convenient.

### 苏联广泛利用示踪原子提高生产

1955-03-28
第4版()
专栏：

    苏联广泛利用示踪原子提高生产
   【新华社莫斯科二十七日电】苏联科学家不仅利用示踪原子测定高炉中的铁水和炉渣情况，并且还利用示踪原子测定高炉中气体运行的情况，来改进高炉生产过程。
    据苏联“文学报”报道：科学家在利用示踪原子测定高炉中气体运行时，把装有放射性物质——惰性气体氢的小玻璃瓶放在装有火药的金属匣内（小匣类似一个炮弹壳），再把金属匣放在往炉膛送热风用的一个送风口上，接通电流，引起爆炸，在爆炸中，放射性氢就弥漫在高炉的气体中。这时，特别装置的一种测定器就依靠示踪原子，不断地显示气体在高炉内每五十分之一秒钟的运行的情况，并且自动地把这种情况记录在胶片上。根据记录材料，就有可能了解炼铁进程，达到减少焦炭消耗和提高高炉生产率的目的。
    在测定气体运行情况的仪器中，有一种带有强化器的电解箱特别精密，它可以根据放射性物质所引起的电离作用来测定气体运行情况。电解箱的强化器使由电子形成的电场的强度提高为六十万倍，因此，可以记录每一个别的微粒。
   【新华社讯】据苏联“文学报”报道：苏联科学家利用示踪原子法研究养鱼，大大提高了鱼产量并减轻了养鱼劳动。
    苏联科学家设计了一种“Б式装置”，研究在养鱼池中使用有机物质和矿质肥料的办法。他们把放射性磷放进这些东西里面，然后放入水中，利用“Б式装置”通过记录放射性磷的冲量来研究其中的营养物质被吸收的情况。根据试验，营养物质被水中的细菌和水草吸收最快，其次被小的无脊椎动物吸收，最后才被鱼所吸收。同时也可以知道，鱼吸收营养物质的速度和数量。根据这些材料，科学家制定了饲养珍贵的鲤鱼和鲱鱼的最好的饲养制度，使每一公顷水面的鱼产量从八十至一百公斤增加到四百至五百公斤。
    苏联科学家还利用示踪原子法来计算养鱼池中鱼的数量，这种计算十分精确，而且特别省事。
