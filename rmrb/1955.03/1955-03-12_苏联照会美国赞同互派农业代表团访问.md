Title: Soviet Note to the United States Agrees to Exchange Agricultural Delegations
Author:
Date: 1955-03-12
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, March 11th: TASS Moscow, March 11th: On the 10th, the Soviet Ministry of Foreign Affairs sent a note to the US Embassy in the Soviet Union. The note stated: "The suggestion made by the Iowa 'Des Moines Register' regarding the exchange of agricultural delegations between the United States of America and the Soviet Union has recently received widespread response from American and Soviet newspapers. The Soviet government also believes that the exchange of agricultural delegations between the United States of America and the Soviet Union would be beneficial. Soviet agricultural institutions are willing to receive the American agricultural delegation visiting the Soviet Union and are willing to send a Soviet agricultural delegation to visit the United States. In view of the above, the Soviet government is willing to know the attitude of the United States government on the issue of exchanging agricultural delegations between the United States and the Soviet Union."

### 苏联照会美国赞同互派农业代表团访问

1955-03-12
第4版()
专栏：

    苏联照会美国赞同互派农业代表团访问
    【新华社十一日讯】塔斯社莫斯科十一日讯：十日，苏联外交部致美国驻苏联大使馆照会一件，照会说：“美国衣阿华州‘得梅因纪事报’所提出的关于美利坚合众国和苏联两国互派农业代表团访问的建议，最近得到美国和苏联报纸的广泛响应。苏联政府同样认为，美利坚合众国和苏联互派农业代表团是会有益的。苏联农业机构愿接待访问苏联的美国农业代表团，并愿派遣苏联农业代表团访问美国。鉴于上述情况，苏联政府愿意知道美国政府对美国和苏联互派农业代表团访问的问题的态度。”
