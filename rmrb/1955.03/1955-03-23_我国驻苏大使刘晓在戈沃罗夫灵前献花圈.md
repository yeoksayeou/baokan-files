Title: Chinese Ambassador to the Soviet Union Liu Xiao Lays Wreath Before Govorov's Casket
Author:
Date: 1955-03-23
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, March 22nd: Liu Xiao, Ambassador of the People's Republic of China to the Soviet Union, and embassy staff, went to the Hall of Columns of the House of Trade Unions on the afternoon of the 21st to lay a wreath before the casket of Soviet Marshal Govorov.

### 我国驻苏大使刘晓在戈沃罗夫灵前献花圈

1955-03-23
第4版()
专栏：

    我国驻苏大使刘晓在戈沃罗夫灵前献花圈
  【新华社莫斯科二十二日电】中华人民共和国驻苏联大使刘晓和使馆人员，二十一日下午前往工会大厦圆柱大厅，在苏联元帅戈沃罗夫灵前献花圈。
