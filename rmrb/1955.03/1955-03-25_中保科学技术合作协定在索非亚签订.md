Title: Sino-Bulgarian Scientific and Technological Cooperation Agreement Signed in Sofia
Author:
Date: 1955-03-25
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, Sofia, March 24th: After friendly negotiations, representatives of the People's Republic of China and the People's Republic of Bulgaria signed the Sino-Bulgarian Scientific and Technological Cooperation Agreement in Sofia on March 23rd.

    The agreement stipulates that China and Bulgaria will exchange experiences in various sectors of the national economy, exchange technical documents, and send experts to assist each other in science and technology.

    The agreement was signed by Monov, Chairman of the Bulgarian Committee for Scientific and Technological Cooperation, on behalf of Bulgaria, and Zhou Zhu'an, Ambassador Extraordinary and Plenipotentiary of the People's Republic of China to Bulgaria, on behalf of China.

    After the signing ceremony, Monov and Zhou Zhu'an delivered congratulatory speeches successively.

### 中保科学技术合作协定在索非亚签订

1955-03-25
第1版()
专栏：

    中保科学技术合作协定在索非亚签订
    【新华社二十四日讯】索非亚消息：中华人民共和国和保加利亚人民共和国的代表经过友好的谈判后，于三月二十三日在索非亚签订了中保科学技术合作协定。
    协定规定，中保两国将交换国民经济各部门的经验，交换技术文件，并互派专家在科学技术方面互相帮助。
    在协定上签字的，保加利亚方面是保加利亚科学技术合作委员会主席莫诺夫，中国方面是中华人民共和国驻保加利亚特命全权大使周竹安。
    签字仪式举行后，莫诺夫和周竹安曾相继致词祝贺。
