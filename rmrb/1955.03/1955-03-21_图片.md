Date: 1955-03-21

    The newly built public-private joint venture Yellow River Cotton Textile Factory in Xi'an has commenced production. This year, the factory will produce more than 200,000 printed bed sheets and other cotton textiles of various colors to supply the needs of people in the Northwest region. This female worker and Youth League member, He Qiaoling, has increased her daily production of towels from 210 to 278.
                      Xinhua News Agency reporter Sheng Jirun Photo

    西安新建的公私合营黄河棉织厂已开工生产。这个厂今年将生产二十多万条各色印花床单和其他棉织品，供应西北各地人民的需要。这个女工、青年团员何巧玲，织毛巾的日产量已由二百一十条提高到二百七十八条。
