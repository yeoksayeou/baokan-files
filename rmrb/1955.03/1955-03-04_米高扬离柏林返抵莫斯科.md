Title: Mikoyan Departs Berlin, Returns to Moscow
Author:
Date: 1955-03-04
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, March 3rd: According to TASS, the head of the Soviet government delegation visiting the Leipzig Fair, First Deputy Chairman of the Council of Ministers of the USSR, A.I. Mikoyan, left Berlin on March 2nd and returned to Moscow.

### 米高扬离柏林返抵莫斯科

1955-03-04
第4版()
专栏：

    米高扬离柏林返抵莫斯科
    【新华社三日讯】据塔斯社讯：参观来比锡博览会的苏联政府代表团团长、苏联部长会议第一副主席阿·伊·米高扬三月二日离开柏林回到莫斯科。
