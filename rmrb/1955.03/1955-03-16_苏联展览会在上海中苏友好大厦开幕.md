Title: Soviet Exhibition Opens at the Sino-Soviet Friendship Building in Shanghai
Author:
Date: 1955-03-16
Page: 1

    Soviet Exhibition Opens at the Sino-Soviet Friendship Building in Shanghai
    [This newspaper Shanghai, March 15th dispatch] The Soviet Economic and Cultural Construction Achievements Exhibition officially opened at the Sino-Soviet Friendship Building in Shanghai on the morning of March 15th. A grand opening ceremony was held. Those attending the opening ceremony included Academician Yudin, the Soviet Union's Ambassador Extraordinary and Plenipotentiary to China, and his wife; Danishenko, the Soviet Deputy Trade Representative to China; Borisinko, the Vice Chairman of the Soviet Chamber of Commerce and Director of the Soviet Economic and Cultural Construction Achievements Exhibition; Soviet experts and staff working at the exhibition; Soviet experts Andreyev and Gokhman, who personally guided and assisted in the construction of the Sino-Soviet Friendship Building; and Gutikov, the Soviet Consul General in Shanghai, and his wife.

    Consuls of various countries stationed in Shanghai and personnel handling overseas Chinese affairs were also invited to attend the opening ceremony.

    The Chinese side was represented by Pan Hannian, Vice Mayor of Shanghai; Fan Ziwen, Deputy Minister of Foreign Trade of the People's Republic of China; and Xiao Fangzhou, Deputy Secretary-General of the China Council for the Promotion of International Trade.

    Also present were Ma Zishui, Deputy Secretary of the Shanghai Municipal Committee of the Communist Party of China; leaders of the Shanghai Municipal Consultative Committee and local organizations of various democratic parties in Shanghai; leaders of various services of the Chinese People's Liberation Army stationed in Shanghai; responsible persons of Shanghai people's organizations; combat heroes; model workers in industry and agriculture; people from all walks of life in Shanghai; and representatives from Shanghai's urban and rural districts, totaling more than 6,000 people.

    At the opening ceremony, Academician Yudin, the Soviet Union's Ambassador Extraordinary and Plenipotentiary to the People's Republic of China, Pan Hannian, Vice Mayor of Shanghai, Fan Ziwen, Deputy Minister of Foreign Trade of the People's Republic of China, and Borisinko, Vice Chairman of the Soviet Chamber of Commerce and Director of the Soviet Economic and Cultural Construction Achievements Exhibition, delivered speeches. Ambassador Yudin said: The exhibition of Soviet economic and cultural construction achievements in Shanghai once again vividly demonstrates the increasingly consolidated, eternal, and unbreakable friendly ties between our two great peoples, which are based on mutual respect for national interests, mutual extensive effective assistance and support. It helps to promote the prosperity of our two peoples and is a solid guarantee for peace in the Far East and the world.

    Vice Mayor Pan Hannian first extended warm congratulations on the opening of the exhibition on behalf of the Shanghai Municipal People's Committee and the people of Shanghai. He then said: Chairman Mao has repeatedly taught us to work hard to learn from the Soviet Union and take the Soviet Union as an example. The Soviet Economic and Cultural Construction Achievements Exhibition fully and vividly introduces the brilliant achievements of the Soviet Union in economic and cultural construction over the past thirty-seven years; we can see the rapid development of Soviet socialist industry; we can see the highly developed production of Soviet agriculture; we can see the brilliant achievements of Soviet culture and art and the happy lives of the Soviet people. This will be a great encouragement and education to the people of Shanghai who are fighting for the socialist construction and transformation of the motherland. The people of Shanghai are determined to respond to Chairman Mao's call with practical actions, to further consolidate the great friendship between China and the Soviet Union, to fight for the liberation of Taiwan by the People's Liberation Army, to defend world peace, and to oppose American aggression, and to strive to build our motherland into a great socialist country. After speeches by Fan Ziwen and Borisinko, Vice Mayor of Shanghai Pan Hannian cut the ribbon for the opening of the exhibition. Then the guests entered the grand exhibition for a visit. On the first day of the exhibition, more than 30,000 people visited. (Picture attached)

### 苏联展览会在上海中苏友好大厦开幕

1955-03-16
第1版()
专栏：

    苏联展览会在上海中苏友好大厦开幕
    【本报上海十五日电】苏联经济及文化建设成就展览会三月十五日上午在上海中苏友好大厦正式开幕，隆重举行了展览会开幕典礼，参加开幕典礼的，有苏联驻我国特命全权大使尤金院士及其夫人，苏联驻华商务副代表丹尼辛柯，苏联商会副主席、苏联经济及文化建设成就展览会主任鲍里辛科，在展览会中工作的苏联专家及工作人员，亲自指导和帮助建筑中苏友好大厦的苏联专家安得烈也夫、郭赫曼和苏联驻上海总领事顾悌克夫及其夫人等。
    各国驻上海领事及办理侨务人员等，也应邀参加了开幕典礼。
    参加开幕典礼的中国方面有上海市副市长潘汉年、中华人民共和国对外贸易部副部长范子文、中国国际贸易促进委员会副秘书长萧方洲等。
    中国共产党上海市委员会副书记马子水、上海市协商委员会和各民主党派上海市地方组织的负责人、中国人民解放军驻上海部队各兵种首长以及上海各人民团体负责人、战斗英雄、工农业劳动模范和各界人士、上海市区两级代表六千余人。
    在开幕典礼会上，苏联驻中华人民共和国特命全权大使尤金院士，上海市副市长潘汉年，中华人民共和国对外贸易部副部长范子文和苏联商会副主席、苏联经济及文化建设成就展览会主任鲍里辛科都讲了话。尤金大使说：苏联经济及文化建设成就展览会在上海展出，又一次鲜明地表现了我们两国伟大人民间日益巩固的、永恒的和牢不可破的友好联系，是建立在相互尊重民族利益、相互广泛的有效帮助和支持的基础上。它有助于促进我们两国人民的繁荣，是远东和世界和平的坚固的保障。
    潘汉年副市长首先代表上海市人民委员会和上海人民，对展览会的开幕表示热烈的祝贺。接着他说：毛主席一再教导我们，要努力学习苏联，以苏联为榜样。苏联经济及文化建设成就展览会，正是全面地、生动地介绍了苏联三十七年来在经济及文化建设事业上的辉煌成就；可以看到苏联社会主义工业发展的突飞猛进；可以看到苏联农业的生产高度发展；可以看到苏联文化艺术的光辉成就和苏联人民生活的幸福。这对正在为祖国的社会主义建设和改造事业而奋斗的上海人民，将是莫大的鼓舞和教育。上海人民决以实际行动响应毛主席的号召，来进一步巩固中苏两国的伟大友谊，为支援人民解放军解放台湾、保卫世界和平、反对美国侵略而奋斗，为把我们的祖国建设成为一个伟大的社会主义国家而奋斗。范子文和鲍里辛科相继讲话后，展览会由上海市副市长潘汉年剪彩。接着来宾们即进入规模宏大的展览会参观。展览会的第一天，有三万余人前往参观。（附图片）
