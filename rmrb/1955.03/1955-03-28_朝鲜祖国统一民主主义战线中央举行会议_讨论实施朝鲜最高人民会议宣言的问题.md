Title: Korean Fatherland Unification Democratic Front Central Committee Holds Meeting to Discuss Implementation of DPRK Supreme People's Assembly Declaration
Author:
Date: 1955-03-28
Page: 4

    Korean Fatherland Unification Democratic Front Central Committee Holds Meeting
    To Discuss Implementation of DPRK Supreme People's Assembly Declaration
   [Xinhua News Agency, Pyongyang, March 27] According to the Korean Central News Agency, the Central Committee of the Korean Fatherland Unification Democratic Front held a meeting on the 26th to discuss the implementation of the declaration of the Ninth Session of the Supreme People's Assembly of the DPRK.

    Li Young, Chairman of the Presidium of the Central Committee, pointed out in his speech at the meeting that this declaration demonstrates our Republic's consistent peaceful policy and illustrates the indomitable spirit and unshakable position of the Korean people in their struggle for the peaceful reunification of the fatherland and the consolidation of world peace. It has received full support from all political parties and people's organizations of the Korean Fatherland Unification Democratic Front.

    Representatives of many political parties and people's organizations spoke at the meeting in support of the declaration and expressed their determination to wage a resolute struggle for the realization of the declaration.

    The meeting adopted a resolution supporting the measures put forward by the Ninth Session of the Supreme People's Assembly of the DPRK for the peaceful reunification of Korea. The resolution called for all efforts to be made to withdraw foreign troops from Korea, to reduce the number of troops in North and South Korea to 100,000 each, to send parliamentary delegations from North and South Korea to each other, to establish contact between North and South Korea to discuss and negotiate issues related to the peaceful reunification of Korea, and to convene an international conference of countries concerned with the peaceful settlement of the Korean issue as soon as possible.

    The resolution finally called for opposing the imperialists' policy of war provocation in order to consolidate world peace, and for further resolute struggle to ban atomic and hydrogen weapons and for universal disarmament.

### 朝鲜祖国统一民主主义战线中央举行会议  讨论实施朝鲜最高人民会议宣言的问题

1955-03-28
第4版()
专栏：

    朝鲜祖国统一民主主义战线中央举行会议
    讨论实施朝鲜最高人民会议宣言的问题
   【新华社平壤二十七日电】据朝鲜中央通讯社讯：朝鲜祖国统一民主主义战线中央委员会在二十六日举行会议，讨论实施朝鲜最高人民会议第九届会议宣言的问题。
    中央委员会议长团议长李英在会上发言指出：这个宣言表现了我们共和国始终不渝的和平政策，说明了朝鲜人民为祖国的和平统一和巩固世界和平而不屈不挠地斗争的精神和不可动摇的立场。它受到朝鲜祖国统一民主主义战线的一切政党和人民团体的充分支持。
    许多政党和人民团体的代表都在会上发言支持这个宣言，并表示决心为实现这个宣言而进行坚决的斗争。
    会议通过了一项决议，支持朝鲜最高人民会议第九届会议宣言为和平统一朝鲜而提出的各项措施。决议号召尽一切努力使外国军队撤出朝鲜，把北朝鲜和南朝鲜的军队数目各自减少到十万人，北朝鲜和南朝鲜互派议会代表团，建立北朝鲜和南朝鲜之间的接触以讨论和协商有关和平统一朝鲜的问题，以及早日召开与和平解决朝鲜问题有关各国的国际会议。
    决议最后号召反对帝国主义者的战争挑衅政策以巩固世界和平，为禁用原子武器、氢武器和普遍裁减军备作进一步坚决的斗争。
