    Our Great Motherland
    On the first day of the issuance of the new RMB, the people of the capital excitedly exchanged their old currency for the new at the banks. These two citizens carefully examine the new RMB, finding it both attractive and easy to recognize, as well as convenient to count.

### 我们伟大的祖国（图片）

1955-03-02
第1版()
专栏：

    我们伟大的祖国
    新人民币发行的第一天，首都人民都兴奋地向银行兑换新币。这两位市民仔细地观看新人民币，她们觉得新人民币又好看，又好认，数起来也方便。
