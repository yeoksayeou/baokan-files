Title: Gheorghe Gheorghiu-Dej Receives Ke Bonian
Author:
Date: 1955-03-19
Page: 1

    Xinhua News Agency, Prague, March 18th: Bucharest dispatch: Gheorghe Gheorghiu-Dej, Chairman of the Council of Ministers of the Romanian People's Republic, received Ke Bonian, Ambassador Extraordinary and Plenipotentiary of the People's Republic of China to Romania, on the 17th.
    Present at the meeting was Simion Bughici, Minister of Foreign Affairs of the Romanian People's Republic.

### 乔治乌—德治接见柯柏年

1955-03-19
第1版()
专栏：

    乔治乌—德治接见柯柏年
    【新华社布拉格十八日电】　布加勒斯特消息：罗马尼亚人民共和国部长会议主席乔治乌—德治，在十七日接见了中华人民共和国驻罗马尼亚特命全权大使柯柏年。
    接见时在座的有罗马尼亚人民共和国外交部长西·布吉奇。
