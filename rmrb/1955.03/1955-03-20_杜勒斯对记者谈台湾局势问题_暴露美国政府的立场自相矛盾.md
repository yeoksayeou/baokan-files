Title: Dulles' Remarks to Reporters on the Taiwan Situation Expose the Self-Contradictory Position of the U.S. Government
Author:
Date: 1955-03-20
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, March 19th: U.S. Secretary of State Dulles, in a press conference in Ottawa on the 18th, spoke about the Taiwan situation and thoroughly exposed how the U.S. government's insistence on aggression against Taiwan has led its position into a completely contradictory and chaotic state.

According to the U.S. Information Service, Dulles said at the press conference: "The United States recognizes that Communist China can say Taiwan is its own, but it does not have the right to take it by force." Here, Dulles had to publicly admit that Taiwan is Chinese territory, yet he could not produce a single reason to deny the Chinese people's right to recover their own territory. Dulles didn't dare mention a single word about why, while he insisted that the Chinese people "have no right to take" their own Taiwan by force, the U.S. ruling clique has the right to forcibly occupy Taiwan, which does not belong to it.

Dulles's remarks further demonstrate the moral bankruptcy of the U.S. ruling clique.

### 杜勒斯对记者谈台湾局势问题  暴露美国政府的立场自相矛盾

1955-03-20
第1版()
专栏：

    杜勒斯对记者谈台湾局势问题
    暴露美国政府的立场自相矛盾
    【新华社十九日讯】美国国务卿杜勒斯十八日在渥太华的一次记者招待会上谈到台湾局势问题时，彻底暴露了美国政府坚持侵略台湾的结果已经使自己的立场陷于完全矛盾错乱的境地。
    据美国新闻处报道，杜勒斯在这次记者招待会上说：“美国承认共产党中国可以说台湾是它的，但是并没有权利用武力来取得它。”在这里，杜勒斯不得不公开承认台湾是中国的领土，然而他一个字的理由也举不出来就居然想否认中国人民收复自己的领土的权利。杜勒斯也一个字都不敢提到，为什么在他硬说中国人民“没有权利用武力取得”属于自己的台湾的同时，美国统治集团却能有权利用武力霸占不属于他的台湾。
    杜勒斯的谈话进一步证明了美国统治集团在道义上的破产。
