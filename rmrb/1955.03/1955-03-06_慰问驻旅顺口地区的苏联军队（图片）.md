Visiting and Comforting the Soviet Army Stationed in the Lushunkou Area

On the afternoon of February 22nd, the delegation of the People's Republic of China for visiting and comforting the Soviet Army stationed in the Lushunkou area solemnly held a grand rally in Lushun-Dalian to celebrate the 37th anniversary of the founding of the Soviet Army and to visit and comfort the Soviet Army stationed in the Lushunkou area. The picture shows Peng Dehuai, head of the delegation of the People's Republic of China for visiting and comforting the Soviet Army stationed in the Lushunkou area, Vice Premier of the State Council and Minister of National Defense of the People's Republic of China, delivering a congratulatory speech at the rally.
The pictures in this issue of the pictorial are taken by our reporter Gao Liang, except for those with signatures.

    Soldiers of the Soviet naval forces stationed in the Lushunkou area are wearing the Sino-Soviet Friendship Badge presented by the delegation.

    Head of Delegation Peng Dehuai presents a flag to the representatives of the Soviet Army stationed in the Lushunkou area on behalf of Chairman Mao Zedong.

    Head of Delegation Peng Dehuai held a grand banquet to entertain representatives of the Soviet Army stationed in the Lushunkou area. The picture shows Head of Delegation Peng Dehuai and Soviet Army representatives raising their glasses to celebrate the great friendship between the people and armies of China and the Soviet Union.

    Xinhua News Agency photo: The entire delegation presents wreaths to the Soviet Red Army Martyrs Monument.

    The delegation visits the artillery unit of the Soviet Army stationed in the Lushunkou area.

    The delegation visits the naval unit of the Soviet Army stationed in the Lushunkou area.

    Head of Delegation Peng Dehuai receives a warship model presented by the naval unit of the Soviet Army stationed in the Lushunkou area.

    The delegation visits the Lushun Military History Museum.

    The song and dance troupe of the Chinese People's Liberation Army, affiliated with the delegation's literary and art troupe, gives a performance to comfort the Soviet Army stationed in the Lushunkou area.

    A grand ceremony was held on the square in front of the Lushun Museum to lay the foundation stone for the Sino-Soviet Friendship Tower. The picture shows Guo Moruo, deputy head of the delegation, addressing the meeting.

    Xinhua News Agency photo: Head of Delegation Peng Dehuai and Deputy Head of Delegation Song Qingling hold shovels to lay the foundation stone for the Sino-Soviet Friendship Tower.
