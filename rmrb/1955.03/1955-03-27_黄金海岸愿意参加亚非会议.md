Title: Gold Coast Willing to Participate in the Asian-African Conference
Author:
Date: 1955-03-27
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, Jakarta, March 26th. The Joint Secretariat of the Asian-African Conference announced on the 23rd that the Prime Minister of the Gold Coast, Kwame Nkrumah, telegraphed the Joint Secretariat of the Asian-African Conference, stating that the Gold Coast is willing to participate in the Asian-African Conference. The Joint Secretariat also said that some issues have not yet been clarified, and the Joint Secretariat is waiting for further clarification from the Gold Coast.

### 黄金海岸愿意参加亚非会议

1955-03-27
第1版()
专栏：

    黄金海岸愿意参加亚非会议
    【新华社雅加达二十六日电】亚非会议联合秘书处二十三日宣布：黄金海岸总理恩克鲁玛打电报给亚非会议联合秘书处，表示黄金海岸愿意参加亚非会议。联合秘书处又说，但是有些问题还没有弄清楚，联合秘书处正在等待黄金海岸作进一步的解释。
