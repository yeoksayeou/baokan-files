Title: US-Chiang Military Conference Concludes
Author:
Date: 1955-03-07
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, 6th. The US-Chiang military conference, which began on the 3rd in Taipei, concluded on the 5th. According to reports, the meeting discussed the implementation of the US-Chiang treaty, deployed measures to obstruct the Chinese People's Liberation Army from liberating the coastal islands, expanded American naval and air force bases in Taiwan, accelerated and increased American aid to the Chiang Kai-shek traitorous clique, and strengthened communication between the American military delegation stationed in Taiwan and the Chiang bandit military headquarters. According to the US Chief of Naval Operations, Carney, who participated in the meeting, the US military aid advisory group stationed in Taiwan will be expanded; at the same time, he will expedite the delivery of US military aid materials to Taiwan.

### 美蒋军事会议结束

1955-03-07
第4版()
专栏：

    美蒋军事会议结束
    【新华社六日讯】从三日开始在台北举行的美国和蒋贼的军事会议已经在五日结束。据消息透露，会议讨论了实施美蒋条约的问题，部署了阻挠中国人民解放沿海岛屿，发展美国在台湾的海空军基地，加速和增加美国对蒋介石卖国集团的援助，加强美国驻台湾的军事代表团和蒋贼军司令部之间的联络问题。据参加会议的美国海军作战部长卡涅在会后说，美国驻台湾军事援助顾问团将扩大；同时，他将赶快把美国军事援助物资运到台湾。
