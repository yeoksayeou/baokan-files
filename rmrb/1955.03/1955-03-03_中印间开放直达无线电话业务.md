Title: China and India Open Direct Wireless Telephone Service
Author: 
Date: 1955-03-03
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, March 2nd:

    Following consultations, the postal and telecommunications authorities of the People's Republic of China and the Republic of India have agreed to open direct wireless telephone service between China and India starting March 2nd. After the opening, direct calls will be possible between cities such as Beijing and Shanghai in China and cities such as New Delhi and Mumbai in India. On March 2nd, Zhu Xuefan, Minister of Posts and Telecommunications of China, and Ram, Minister of Transportation of India, exchanged congratulatory messages in their first call.

### 中印间开放直达无线电话业务

1955-03-03
第1版()
专栏：

    中印间开放直达无线电话业务
   【新华社二日讯】中华人民共和国和印度共和国双方邮电领导机构，经洽商同意，从三月二日起开放中印间直达无线电话业务。开放后，我国北京、上海等地和印度新德里、孟买等地间，都能够直接通话。三月二日，我国邮电部部长朱学范和印度交通部部长拉姆在第一次通话中互致贺词。
