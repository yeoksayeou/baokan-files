Title: The Soviet Steel Factory Has Already Applied Atomic Energy in Steelmaking

Author:
Date: 1955-03-12
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency dispatch: The Chelyabinsk Steel Factory in the Soviet Union has already applied atomic energy in steelmaking. The engineers and steelworkers of this factory are using special instruments to record the changes of "tracer atoms" in the smelting process in order to improve the operation of machinery and improve the quality of steel. A group of engineers from the factory, together with scientific staff from the Chelyabinsk Institute of Technology, are also using radioactive isotopes under the leadership of Professor Morozov to study the desulfurization process of steel in electric furnaces. Metallurgical workers have used "tracer atoms" to improve the steel production process, reducing the average smelting time by fifteen to twenty minutes. The factory also used radioactive isotopes to study the crystallization process of steel ingots. This makes it possible to improve the pouring process of steel ingots and increase steel rolling output. Industrial enterprises such as the Chelyabinsk Steel Pipe Factory and the Zlatoust Metallurgical Factory are also using radioactive isotopes.

### 苏联钢铁工厂已经在炼钢方面运用原子能飞机场

1955-03-12
第4版()
专栏：

    苏联钢铁工厂已经在炼钢方面运用原子能
    据新华社讯：苏联车里雅宾斯克钢铁工厂已经在炼钢方面运用原子能。这个工厂的工程师和炼钢工人们正在利用专门仪器，记录出“示踪原子”在熔炼过程中的变动情况，以改善机械的操纵，改进钢的质量。工厂的一群工程师还和车里雅宾斯克技术学院的科学工作人员们一道，在莫罗佐夫教授的领导下用放射性同位素来研究钢铁在电炉中的去硫过程。冶金工人已经运用“示踪原子”改进了钢的生产过程，平均把冶炼时间缩短了十五到二十分钟。工厂还用放射性同位素研究了钢锭的结晶过程。这样便可以改进钢锭的灌注过程并提高轧钢产量。车里雅宾斯克钢管工厂、兹拉陶斯特冶金工厂等工业企业也在利用放射性同位素。
