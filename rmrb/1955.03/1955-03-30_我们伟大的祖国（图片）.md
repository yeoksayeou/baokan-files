Author:
Date: 1955-03-30

    Our Great Motherland
    Liu Jianhan, a Second-Class Combat Hero who participated in the first Air Force Hero, Model Worker Representative Conference, repeatedly performed meritorious deeds in the struggle to liberate the coastal islands of Zhejiang. He is signing autographs for the Young Pioneers of the capital as a souvenir.

### 我们伟大的祖国（图片）

1955-03-30
第1版()
专栏：

    我们伟大的祖国
    参加首届空军英雄模范功臣代表大会的二级战斗英雄刘健汉，在解放浙江沿海岛屿斗争中曾屡建战功。他正为首都少年先锋队员们签名留念。
