    Our Great Motherland
    The workers' segment of the mass amateur music and dance festival held in the capital has concluded. In this performance, the folk song and dance Yunnan Flower Lantern "Ten Sisters" performed by the Chongqing workers' delegation portrayed the different personalities of tea-picking girls.

### 我们伟大的祖国（图片）

1955-03-07
第1版()
专栏：

    我们伟大的祖国
    在首都举行的群众业余音乐舞蹈观摩演出会工人部分的演出已结束。在这次演出中，重庆市工人代表队演出的民间歌舞云南花灯“十大姐”，表现了采茶姑娘的不同性格。
