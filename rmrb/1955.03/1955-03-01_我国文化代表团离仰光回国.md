Title: China Cultural Delegation Leaves Rangoon for Home
Author:
Date: 1955-03-01
Page: 4

    Xinhua News Agency, Rangoon: Part of the Chinese Cultural Delegation, led by Head of Delegation Zheng Zhenduo and Deputy Head of Delegation Zhou Erfu, left Rangoon by ship on the 20th to return home. The other members of the delegation had already left Rangoon for home on the 18th.
    Those who came to the dock to see them off included Burmese Union Minister of Culture U Win, Supreme Court Justice U Min Thein and other government officials, as well as Rangoon University Registrar Tin Aung, Burma-China Friendship Association Honorary Chairman Thakin Kodaw Hmaing, Chairman Htin Paw, Burmese cultural and artistic figures, and representatives of overseas Chinese.
    Also at the dock to see them off were Soviet Ambassador to Burma, Sipalin, and many diplomatic and trade officials from various countries stationed in Burma.
    Chinese Ambassador to Burma Yao Zhongming and embassy staff also went to the dock to see them off.
    Before departing, representatives of Burmese literary and art groups and overseas Chinese student representatives presented flowers to Head of Delegation Zheng Zhenduo, Deputy Head of Delegation Zhou Erfu, and the delegation members.

### 我国文化代表团离仰光回国

1955-03-01
第4版()
专栏：

    我国文化代表团离仰光回国
    【新华社讯】仰光消息：中国文化代表团一部分团员二十日在团长郑振铎和副团长周而复率领下乘轮船离仰光回国。另外一部分团员已经在十八日先行离仰光回国。
    到码头欢送的有缅甸联邦文化部长吴温、最高法院法官吴敏登等政府官员，以及仰光大学教务长廷昂、缅中友好协会名誉主席德钦哥都迈、主席信波、缅甸文化艺术界人士、华侨代表等。
    到码头欢送的还有苏联驻缅甸大使希鲍林以及很多国家驻缅甸的外交和贸易官员。
    中国驻缅甸大使姚仲明和使馆人员也到码头欢送。
    在离别前，缅甸文艺团体代表和华侨学生代表向团长郑振铎、副团长周而复和团员们献了花。
