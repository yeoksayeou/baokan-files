Title: Nehru Disapproves of Dissolving the Neutral Nations Supervisory Commission in Korea
Author:
Date: 1955-03-23
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, New Delhi, March 22nd: According to the Press Trust of India, Prime Minister Nehru of India stated in the Indian Parliament on the 21st that, although the Indian government is not directly involved with the Neutral Nations Supervisory Commission in Korea, the Indian government believes that dissolving this only neutral organization currently in Korea would not be helpful to the current situation in Korea. Nehru pointed out that dissolving the Neutral Nations Supervisory Commission would require the consent of both sides' commands.

### 尼赫鲁不赞成解散朝鲜中立国监察委员会

1955-03-23
第1版()
专栏：

    尼赫鲁不赞成解散朝鲜中立国监察委员会
    【新华社二十二日讯】据印度新闻处新德里消息：印度总理尼赫鲁二十一日在印度国会说，虽然印度政府并不是与朝鲜中立国监察委员会直接有关的，但是，印度政府认为：要是解散这个现在是在朝鲜的唯一的中立机构的话，那将不会有助于目前朝鲜的局势。尼赫鲁指出：解散中立国监察委员会将需要得到双方司令部的同意。
