Author:
Date: 1955-03-29

Hangzhou Circles Hold Public Memorial for Traditional Chinese Painter Huang Binhong

Mr. Huang Binhong, a traditional Chinese painter, passed away in Hangzhou on March 25th. People from all walks of life in Hangzhou held a public memorial service on the 27th.

Mr. Huang Binhong was a renowned traditional Chinese painter, Vice Chairman of the Shanghai Branch of the Chinese Artists Association, member of the Second National Committee of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference, Director of the Chinese Artists Association, Director of the Institute of National Fine Arts of the Central Academy of Fine Arts, and Professor of the East China Branch of the Central Academy of Fine Arts.

The public memorial service began at 9:00 AM, presided over by Sha Wenhan of Zhejiang Province and others. Representatives from the Zhejiang Provincial People's Committee, the Zhejiang Provincial Committee of the Communist Party of China, the Zhejiang Provincial Federation of Literary and Art Circles, all teachers and students of the East China Branch of the Central Academy of Fine Arts in Hangzhou, and friends of Mr. Huang Binhong attended the service, totaling more than 300 people. Huang Yongming, the eldest son of Mr. Huang Binhong, announced at the memorial service that, in accordance with Mr. Huang Binhong's wishes, he decided to donate all of Mr. Huang Binhong's paintings, manuscripts, and collection of calligraphy, paintings, books, porcelain, bronze, jade artifacts, and cultural relics from past dynasties to the country.

Mr. Huang Binhong was originally from Anhui Province and was ninety-two years old this year. His landscape paintings had an original style. From the age of ten when he began learning to paint, until just before his death, his creative labor never ceased. He made great contributions to the collection of historical materials on fine arts and also researched ancient characters and ancient artistic relics. Since liberation, he consistently supported the Party and the People's Government and actively participated in political and artistic activities. On his ninetieth birthday, the former East China Administrative Committee's Cultural Department awarded him a certificate of merit.

The coffin of Mr. Huang Binhong was buried in the Nanshan Cemetery in the suburbs of Hangzhou at 12:00 noon on the day of the public memorial service.

### 杭州各界公祭国画家黄宾虹

1955-03-29
第3版()
专栏：

    杭州各界公祭国画家黄宾虹
    国画家黄宾虹先生于三月二十五日在杭州逝世。杭州市各界于二十七日举行公祭。
    黄宾虹先生是我国著名的国画家、中国美术家协会上海分会副主席、中国人民政治协商会议第二届全国委员会委员、中国美术家协会理事、中央美术学院民族美术研究所所长、中央美术学院华东分院教授。
    公祭于上午九时开始，由浙江省长沙文汉等主祭，参加公祭的有浙江省人民委员会、中共浙江省委员会、浙江省文联的代表和中央美术学院华东分院全体在杭教师、学生，以及黄先生生前友好等共三百多人。黄宾虹先生长子黄用明在公祭时宣布：决定遵照黄先生遗志，把黄宾虹先生生前的绘画作品、手稿和收藏的历代字画、书籍、瓷铜玉器文物，全部献给国家。
    黄宾虹先生原籍安徽，今年九十二岁。他所作的山水画，有独创风格。从十岁开始学画起，一直到临终之前，他的创作劳动，从未间断。他在美术史料的搜集工作上，有很大的劳绩，对于古文字和古代艺术文物，也有研究。解放以来，他一贯拥护党和人民政府，积极参加政治活动和艺术活动。在他九十寿辰的时候，前华东行政委员会文化部曾颁给奖状。
    黄宾虹先生的灵柩，已于公祭的当天中午十二时在杭州市郊南山公墓安葬。
