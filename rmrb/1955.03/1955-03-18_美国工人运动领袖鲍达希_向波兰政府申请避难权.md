Title: American Labor Leader Bodahe Asks Polish Government for Asylum

Author:

Date: 1955-03-18
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Warsaw, March 16th: Owen Bodahe, a well-known leader of the American labor movement, who has been persecuted by the American authorities for his progressive activities, arrived in Warsaw on March 15th.

Bodahe has applied to the Polish government for asylum. The Polish government has granted his request.

### 美国工人运动领袖鲍达希  向波兰政府申请避难权

1955-03-18
第4版()
专栏：

    美国工人运动领袖鲍达希
    向波兰政府申请避难权
    【新华社十七日讯】塔斯社华沙十六日讯：由于进步活动受到美国当局迫害的、美国工人运动著名领袖欧文·鲍达希，在三月十五日到达华沙。
    鲍达希向波兰政府申请避难权。波兰政府已经答应了他的请求。
