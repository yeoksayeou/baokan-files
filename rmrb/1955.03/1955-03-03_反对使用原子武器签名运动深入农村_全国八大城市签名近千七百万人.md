Title: Anti-Atomic Weapon Signature Campaign Penetrates Rural Areas; Nearly 17 Million Signatures Collected in Eight Major Cities Nationwide
Author:
Date: 1955-03-03
Page: 1

    Anti-Atomic Weapon Signature Campaign Penetrates Rural Areas
    Nearly 17 Million Signatures Collected in Eight Major Cities Nationwide
    [Our Newspaper] The signature campaign against the use of atomic weapons by the people of our country has basically concluded in major cities and some medium-sized cities. The number of signatures in eight major cities, including Beijing, Tianjin, Shanghai, Shenyang, Wuhan, Guangzhou, Chongqing, and Xi'an, totals more than 16,930,000. Now, the signature campaign is being carried out in depth in small towns and rural areas of various provinces.
    As of February 28, Shandong Province had more than 4,734,000 signatures, of which the number of signatures in Jinan and Qingdao, where the signature campaign has ended, totaled more than 1,370,000, accounting for about 85 percent of the total population of each city. In the Tai'an area around the old revolutionary base of Mount Culai, more than 740,000 people have signed.
    As of February 28, Heilongjiang Province had 4,520,043 signatures. The number of people participating in the signature campaign in the three cities of Harbin, Qiqihar, and Hegang each accounted for more than 85 percent of the total population of these cities. In Jiamusi and Mudanjiang, the number of people who signed accounted for more than 90 percent of the total number of residents. In the Heihe area on the right bank of the Heilong River in the northernmost part of our country, the signature campaign is also being widely carried out. In the forests of the Lesser Khingan Mountains, more than 42,000 lumberjacks and farmers participating in timber transportation have signed.
    As of February 28, Hubei Province had 1,659,338 signatures. The signature campaign has been launched in the rural areas of many counties. In the Enshi area, Badong, Jianshi, Xianfeng, and other five counties have begun to collect signatures in streets and villages. In Wangcheng Township in Xishui County, Dengfan Township in Dawu County, and Qili Township in Hong'an County, an old revolutionary base area during the Second Domestic Revolutionary War, the number of signatures reached more than 70 percent of the total population of the township within a few days.
    The people in the old revolutionary base areas of Jiangxi Province are enthusiastically signing against the use of atomic weapons. By the end of February, in the Jinggang Mountain area and parts of Ruijin, Xingguo, Ningdu, and other counties alone, more than 500,000 people had signed. The signature campaign has basically ended in the twenty-seven natural villages on Jinggang Mountain and the Donggu Mountain revolutionary base area in Ji'an County, with the vast majority of people in these villages having signed. Many townships and villages in the old revolutionary base areas have also held signature rallies and demonstrations against the use of atomic weapons.
    As of February 27, Zhejiang Province had 1,049,957 signatures. When signing, the broad masses of farmers have put forward practical actions to do a good job in spring plowing and production to oppose the use of atomic weapons. After more than 400 members of the Xukou Agricultural Production Cooperative in Jinhe Township, Xindeng County, signed, they actively participated in spring plowing. After an 88-year-old woman in the cooperative signed, she immediately took care of her daughter-in-law's children so that her daughter-in-law could participate in water conservancy construction, accumulate soil fertilizer, and other work.
    As of the end of February, according to incomplete statistics from various places in the Inner Mongolia Autonomous Region, more than 479,000 people of Mongolian, Han, and Hui ethnic groups had signed the "Letter to the People of the World." More than 85,000 people have signed in Hohhot, and more than 90 percent of the residents in Baotou have signed. More than 101,000 people have signed in Hailar, accounting for 80 percent of the total number of residents. In pastoral areas, herdsmen are also enthusiastically signing.
    The signature campaign in Urumqi, Xinjiang Province, has basically ended, with more than 95,000 people signing in the city. Almost 100 percent of the employees, students, teachers, and staff of various agencies, organizations, factories, and schools in Xinjiang Province and Urumqi have signed. Religious professionals and business people from the city's sixty-three Islamic mosques have also enthusiastically signed the "Letter to the People of the World." In Kashi in southern Xinjiang, Yining in the Ili Kazakh Autonomous Prefecture, the Bayingolin Mongol Autonomous Prefecture, and the Changji Hui Autonomous Prefecture, agencies, organizations, and schools have held signature rallies. Nine hundred and forty rural and pastoral area propagandists in the Urumqi area are carrying out propaganda activities against the use of atomic weapons in the vast rural and pastoral areas.
    The Lhasa Patriotic Women's Federation and the Patriotic Youth Cultural Federation jointly held a signature rally against the use of atomic weapons on February 26. All 252 members present signed. The wife and son of Kalon Doegar Pengtso Rabgye of the Tibetan local government also came to sign. When signing, the women and young people released peace pigeons. Before the meeting, the members of the Patriotic Women's Federation and the Patriotic Youth Cultural Federation also held a joint meeting. At the meeting, signature books were distributed, and it was decided that members and group leaders would collect signatures from the broad masses of women and young people, and it was decided to jointly organize a propaganda team to carry out propaganda in urban and suburban areas. On the afternoon of the 26th, the staff of various agencies in Lhasa also held a signature rally against the use of atomic weapons, and more than 800 people signed the "Letter to the People of the World."

### 反对使用原子武器签名运动深入农村  全国八大城市签名近千七百万人

1955-03-03
第1版()
专栏：

    反对使用原子武器签名运动深入农村
    全国八大城市签名近千七百万人
    【本报讯】我国人民反对使用原子武器签名运动，在大城市和若干中等城市已基本结束。北京、天津、上海、沈阳、武汉、广州、重庆、西安等八大城市的签名人数共达一千六百九十三万多人。现在签名运动正在各省小城镇和农村中深入开展。
    山东省到二月二十八日止，已有四百七十三万四千多人签名，其中已结束签名运动的济南、青岛两市签名人数共达一百三十七万多人，占各该市总人口的百分之八十五左右。革命老根据地徂徕山周围的泰安专区也已有七十四万多人签名。
    黑龙江省截至二月二十八日止，已有四百五十二万零四十三人签名。哈尔滨、齐齐哈尔和鹤岗三个城市参加签名的人各占这些城市总人口的百分之八十五以上。佳木斯、牡丹江两市，签名的人各占居民总数的百分之九十以上。我国最北部黑龙江右岸的黑河地区，签名运动也正广泛展开。在小兴安岭的森林里，伐木工人和参加拖运木材的农民，已有四万二千多人签了名。
    湖北省到二月二十八日止，已有一百六十五万九千三百三十八人签名。签名运动已在许多县的农村中展开。恩施专区的巴东、建始、咸丰等五个县已开始在街道和村庄中征集签名。浠水县望城乡、大悟县邓畈乡和第二次国内革命战争时期老根据地红安县七里乡的签名人数，在几天内就达到全乡总人数的百分之七十以上。
    江西省革命老根据地人民踊跃签名反对使用原子武器。到二月底止，仅井冈山地区和瑞金、兴国、宁都等县一部分地区，签名的就有五十多万人。井冈山上的二十七个自然村和吉安县东固山革命老根据地，签名运动已基本结束，这些村的绝大部分人都签了名。革命老根据地的许多乡、村，还举行了反对使用原子武器的签名集会和示威游行。
    浙江省到二月二十七日止，已有一百零四万九千九百五十七人签名。广大农民在签名时纷纷提出以搞好春耕生产的实际行动来反对使用原子武器。新登县金河乡胥口农业生产合作社四百多社员签名后，积极投入春耕，该社一个八十八岁的老太婆签名后，马上给媳妇看孩子，让媳妇参加修水利，积土肥等工作。
    内蒙古自治区截至二月底，据各地不完全统计，已有蒙、汉、回各族人民四十七万九千多人在“告全世界人民书”上签了名。呼和浩特市签名的已有八万五千多人，包头市有百分之九十以上的居民签了名。海拉尔市也有十万一千多人签名，占居民总数百分之八十。在牧区，牧民们也踊跃签名。
    新疆省乌鲁木齐市签名运动已基本结束，全市有九万五千多人签了名。新疆省和乌鲁木齐市的各机关、团体、工厂、学校的职工、学生、教师和工作人员，几乎百分之百地签了名。全市六十三个伊斯兰寺坊的宗教职业者和工商界人士也都踊跃地在“告全世界人民书”上签了名。在南疆的喀什市、伊犁哈萨克族自治州的伊宁市、博尔塔拉蒙古族自治州和昌吉回族自治州所在地的各机关、团体、学校纷纷举行签名集会。乌鲁木齐专区有九百四十名农村和牧区的宣传员正在广大的农村和牧区展开反对使用原子武器的宣传活动。
    拉萨市爱国妇女联谊会和爱国青年文化联谊会在二月二十六日联合举行反对使用原子武器签名大会。到会的二百五十二名会员都签了名。西藏地方政府噶伦朵噶·彭错饶杰的夫人和她的儿子，也一齐来签名。签名时，妇女、青年们放出了和平鸽子。大会前，爱国妇女联谊会和爱国青年文化联谊会的委员们还举行了联席会议。会上分发了签名簿，决定由委员和组长在广大妇女、青年中征集签名，并决定联合组织宣传队，在市区和郊区展开宣传。二十六日下午，拉萨市各机关工作人员也举行了反对使用原子武器签名大会，有八百多人在“告全世界人民书”上签了名。
