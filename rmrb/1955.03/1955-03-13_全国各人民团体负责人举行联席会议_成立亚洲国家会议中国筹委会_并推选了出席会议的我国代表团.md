Title: National People's Organizations Leaders Hold Joint Meeting to Establish Chinese Preparatory Committee for the Asian Countries Conference; Delegation to Attend the Conference Selected
Author:
Date: 1955-03-13
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, March 11th: Leaders of national people's organizations held a joint meeting in the capital on the 11th, where the "Asian Countries Conference" Chinese Preparatory Committee was established, and the Chinese delegation to attend the "Asian Countries Conference" was selected.

The following attended this joint meeting: representatives from the China Peace Committee, All-China Federation of Trade Unions, All-China Democratic Women's Federation, All-China Federation of Democratic Youth, the China Federation Of Literary and Art Circles, Chinese People's Association for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries, the All-China Federation of Natural Science Societies, the China Association for Science and Technology Popularization, the Red Cross Society of China, the China Council for the Promotion of International Trade, the All-China Federation of Industry and Commerce, the China Association of Journalists, the China Islamic Association, the Buddhist Association of China, and other organizations.

The meeting was chaired by Guo Moruo, one of the initiators of the "Asian Countries Conference." Guo Moruo reported on the initiation and preparation of the "Asian Countries Conference," pointing out that the conference was proposed by the Indian delegation attending the International Conference for Relaxation of Tension held in Stockholm last June and unanimously agreed upon by the Asian representatives present. Its purpose is to gather peace-loving people from Asian countries to jointly study and discuss issues related to safeguarding Asian peace and easing the Asian situation. The initial preparatory work for the conference was the responsibility of peace-loving Indian individuals, and a preparatory meeting was held in New Delhi in early February, establishing an international preparatory secretariat chaired by Mrs. Rameshwari Nehru. The conference is scheduled to be held in New Delhi from April 6th to 10th. Social figures from Asian countries have enthusiastically responded to and supported the "Asian Countries Conference," and have established broadly representative preparatory committees and selected delegates to attend the conference. The aims of the "Asian Countries Conference" are in line with the demands of the Chinese people, and the Chinese people attach great importance to the convening of the conference. Therefore, this joint meeting of leaders of national people's organizations was held to discuss the establishment of a Chinese preparatory committee and the selection of a Chinese delegation to attend the conference.

Speaking at the meeting were Li Zhucheng, Vice Chairman of the All-China Federation of Industry and Commerce and one of the initiators of the "Asian Countries Conference;" Zhu Xuefan, Vice Chairman of the All-China Federation of Trade Unions; Peng Zeming, Vice President of the Red Cross Society of China; Xu Guangping, Vice Chairman of the All-China Democratic Women's Federation; Wu Han, Vice Chairman of the All-China Federation of Democratic Youth; Da Pongsheng, Deputy Director of the China Islamic Association; Xi Raojiacuo, Acting President of the Buddhist Association of China; Hou Debang, Vice Chairman of the All-China Federation of Natural Science Societies; and Wang Yunsheng, Vice Chairman of the China Association of Journalists. In their speeches, they expressed the ardent desire of the Chinese people for peace and warmly welcomed the convening of the conference. They unanimously pointed out that the people of Asian countries are peace-loving, and the successful convening of this conference will surely promote the friendly unity of the people of Asian countries and promote peaceful coexistence among Asian countries. This is not only conducive to safeguarding Asian peace but also helps to safeguard world peace.

The meeting unanimously elected Guo Moruo as Chairman of the "Asian Countries Conference" Chinese Preparatory Committee, and 20 leaders of various people's organizations as members of the preparatory committee. The Chinese delegation to the "Asian Countries Conference" selected by the meeting is headed by Guo Moruo, and the delegation consists of 38 representatives, including leaders of various people's organizations and people from all walks of life.

### 全国各人民团体负责人举行联席会议  成立亚洲国家会议中国筹委会  并推选了出席会议的我国代表团

1955-03-13
第1版()
专栏：

    全国各人民团体负责人举行联席会议
    成立亚洲国家会议中国筹委会
    并推选了出席会议的我国代表团
    【新华社十一日讯】　全国各人民团体负责人十一日在首都举行联席会议，在会上成立了“亚洲国家会议”中国筹备委员会；并推选出出席
  “亚洲国家会议”中国代表团。
    出席这次联席会议的有：中国人民保卫世界和平委员会、中华全国总工会、中华全国民主妇女联合会、中华全国民主青年联合会、中国文学艺术界联合会、中国人民对外文化协会、中华全国自然科学专门学会联合会、中华全国科学技术普及协会、中国红十字会总会、中国国际贸易促进委员会、中华全国工商业联合会、中国新闻工作者联谊会、中国伊斯兰教协会、中国佛教协会等团体的负责人。
    会议由“亚洲国家会议”发起人之一郭沫若主持。郭沫若在会上报告了“亚洲国家会议”的发起及筹备经过，他指出这一会议是出席去年六月在斯德哥尔摩召开的缓和局势国际会议印度代表团提出并经与会的亚洲各国代表一致赞同发起的，其目的在集合亚洲各国爱好和平的社会人士，共同研究和讨论有关保卫亚洲和平、缓和亚洲局势的问题。会议的初步筹备工作由爱好和平的印度人士负责，已于二月初旬在新德里召开了筹备会议，成立由拉米希瓦里·尼赫鲁夫人任主席的国际筹备秘书处，并定于四月六日至十日在新德里召开大会。亚洲各国社会人士对于“亚洲国家会议”都热烈响应和支持，已纷纷成立了有广泛代表性的筹备委员会，并推选出席会议的代表。“亚洲国家会议”的宗旨是和中国人民的要求相符合的，中国人民极为重视会议的召开，因此举行这次各全国性人民团体负责人的联席会议，讨论成立中国筹备委员会和推选出席会议的中国代表团。
    在会上发言的有“亚洲国家会议”发起人之一，中华全国工商业联合会副主席李烛尘，中华全国总工会副主席朱学范，中国红十字会总会副会长彭泽民，中华全国民主妇女联合会副主席许广平，中华全国民主青年联合会副主席吴晗，中国伊斯兰教协会副主任达浦生，中国佛教协会代理会长喜饶嘉错，中华全国自然科学专门学会联合会副主席侯德榜，中国新闻工作者联谊会副主席王芸生。他们在发言中表达了中国人民爱好和平的热切愿望，热烈欢迎会议的召开。他们一致指出亚洲各国人民是爱好和平的，这次会议的胜利召开必将促进亚洲各国人民的友好团结，促进亚洲各国之间的和平共处。这不仅有利于保卫亚洲和平，而且也有助于保卫世界和平。
    会上一致推选郭沫若为“亚洲国家会议”中国筹备委员会主席，各人民团体负责人二十人为筹备委员会的委员。会议推选的出席“亚洲国家会议”中国代表团由郭沫若担任团长，代表有各人民团体负责人和各界人士三十八人。
