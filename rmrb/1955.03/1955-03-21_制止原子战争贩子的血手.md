Title: Stop the Bloody Hands of the Atomic Warmongers
Author:
Date: 1955-03-21
Page: 4

    Stop the Bloody Hands of the Atomic Warmongers
    Czechoslovakia: Seven Million Signatures Reached
   [Xinhua News Agency, Prague, March 20] According to the Czechoslovak News Agency, as of March 18, more than 7,012,000 people in the Czechoslovak Republic had signed the World Peace Council Standing Committee's "Appeal to the People of the World." While signing, the working people also actively put forward production guarantees. In Slovakia alone, the working people put forward more than 157,100 production guarantees.
  Bulgaria: 1.3 Million Signatures Reached
   [Xinhua News Agency, Prague, March 20] Sofia News: As of March 18, more than 1,335,000 people in Bulgaria had signed the World Peace Council Standing Committee's "Appeal to the People of the World."
  Argentina: Over 18,000 Signatures Received
   [Xinhua News Agency, March 20] According to TASS, Buenos Aires, March 19: The campaign to collect signatures for the World Peace Council's Appeal to the People of the World is progressing smoothly in Argentina, with over 18,000 signatures already collected.
  Chilean Peace Organization Calls on Chilean People to Sign
   [Xinhua News Agency, March 19] According to TASS, Buenos Aires, March 18: The Chilean "Siglo" newspaper reported that the Chilean Peace Committee issued a statement calling on the Chilean people to participate in the movement to ban nuclear weapons and to wholeheartedly support the World Peace Council Standing Committee's Appeal to the People of the World against preparing for atomic war.
    The statement called on people of all nationalities, people of all faiths, people of all classes and circles, and people of all parties and groups across the country to sign the Appeal to the People of the World. The statement said that every citizen, every Chilean, should regard this movement as his own affair.
  Hanoi Launches Signature Campaign Against Atomic Weapons
   [Xinhua News Agency, Hanoi, March 20] According to Vietnam News Agency: The signature campaign against atomic weapons is underway across various regions. From March 13 to 16, Hanoi has collected over 10,000 signatures in four days.
    In addition, the Vietnam Committee for the Signature Campaign Against Atomic Weapons has also received many petitions from the Hanoi Railway Bureau, the National Congress of Patriotic and Peace-Loving Catholics of Vietnam, various units of the People's Army, and various public institutions and factories.

### 制止原子战争贩子的血手

1955-03-21
第4版()
专栏：

    制止原子战争贩子的血手
    捷克斯洛伐克签名人数达七百万
   【新华社布拉格二十日电】据捷克斯洛伐克通讯社报道，截至三月十八日为止，捷克斯洛伐克共和国已经有七百零一万二千多人在世界和平理事会常务委员会“告全世界人民书”上签了名。劳动人民在签名的同时，还纷纷提出生产保证。单是在斯洛伐克，劳动人民就提出了十五万七千一百多项生产保证。
  保加利亚签名人数达一百三十万
   【新华社布拉格二十日电】索非亚消息：截至三月十八日为止，保加利亚已经有一百三十三万五千多人在世界和平理事会常务委员会“告全世界人民书”上签了名。
  阿根廷已有一万八千多人签名
   【新华社二十日讯】据塔斯社布宜诺斯艾利斯十九日讯：为世界和平理事会告全世界人民书征集签名的运动正在阿根廷顺利开展，已经有一万八千多人签了名。
  智利和平组织号召智利人民签名
   【新华社十九日讯】据塔斯社布宜诺斯艾利斯十八日讯：智利“世纪报”报道，智利和平委员会发表了一项声明，号召智利人民参加禁止核子武器的运动和毫无保留地支持世界和平理事会常务委员会反对准备原子战争的告全世界人民书。
    这个声明号召全国人民、各种信仰的人们、各阶级和各界人士、各党派和各团体的人们都在这个告全世界人民书上签名。声明说，每一个公民、每一个智利人都应该把这种运动当作他自己的事情。
  河内展开反对原子武器签名运动
   【新华社河内二十日讯】据越南通讯社讯：反对原子武器签名运动正在各地展开。从三月十三日到十六日的四天中，河内已收集到一万多个签名。
    此外，越南反对原子武器签名运动委员会还收到了河内的铁路局、越南爱国爱和平天主教徒全国大会、人民军各个单位和各公共机关和工厂的许多请愿书。
