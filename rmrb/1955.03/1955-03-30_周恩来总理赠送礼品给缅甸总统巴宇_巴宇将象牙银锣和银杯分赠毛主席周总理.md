Title: Premier Zhou Enlai Presents Gifts to Burmese President Ba U; Ba U Gifts Ivory Silver Gong and Silver Cup to Chairman Mao and Premier Zhou

Author:
Date: 1955-03-30
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Rangoon, March 29th: Chinese Ambassador to Burma Yao Zhongming, on behalf of Premier Zhou Enlai of the People's Republic of China, presented a carpet to Burmese President Ba U on the 28th.

President Ba U said after accepting the carpet, "I am very grateful and happy to receive the carpet from Premier Zhou. This gift represents how close the governments and peoples of the two countries are. The beauty of Chinese carpets is world-renowned. I would like to express my respect to Chairman Mao and Premier Zhou on behalf of the Burmese government and people, and present an ivory silver gong to Chairman Mao and a silver cup to Premier Zhou."

After his speech, President Ba U personally handed the ivory silver gong and silver cup to Ambassador Yao Zhongming. Ambassador Yao Zhongming expressed his gratitude on behalf of Chairman Mao and Premier Zhou.

Those who attended the gift presentation ceremony included Burmese Foreign Minister Sao Khun Hkio, Permanent Secretary of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs U Tun Shein, and Director of Protocol U Khin Maung Gyi.

### 周恩来总理赠送礼品给缅甸总统巴宇  巴宇将象牙银锣和银杯分赠毛主席周总理

1955-03-30
第1版()
专栏：

    周恩来总理赠送礼品给缅甸总统巴宇
    巴宇将象牙银锣和银杯分赠毛主席周总理
    【新华社二十九日讯】仰光消息：中国驻缅甸大使姚仲明二十八日代表中华人民共和国总理周恩来向缅甸联邦总统巴宇赠送地毯一幅。
    巴宇总统在接受地毯后致词说，“周总理送我地毯，我很感谢和高兴。这一礼物的赠送表示着两国政府及人民是如何亲切。中国地毯的优美是世界闻名的。我谨代表缅甸政府和人民向毛主席和周总理表示敬意，并将象牙银锣赠送毛主席，银杯赠送周总理。”
    巴宇总统致词后将象牙银锣和银杯亲自交给姚仲明大使。姚仲明大使代表毛主席和周总理表示谢意。
  参加这一赠送礼物仪式的有：缅甸外交部长藻昆卓，外交部常任秘书吴顿辛和交际司长吴阵貌支等。
