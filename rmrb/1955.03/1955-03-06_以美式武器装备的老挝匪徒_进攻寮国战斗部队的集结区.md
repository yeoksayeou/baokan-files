Title: Lao bandits equipped with US weapons attack the assembly area of the Lao fighting forces
Author:
Date: 1955-03-06
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Hanoi, March 5th: It is reported that in Laos, a group of armed bandits numbering over one hundred and equipped with American mortars and machine guns attacked the Lao fighting forces stationed in Muang Bang, forty kilometers southwest of Sam Neua, on March 1st. This area is within the assembly area of the Lao fighting forces.

On March 1st, these armed bandits launched attacks all day and night in an attempt to occupy three mountains six hundred meters away from Muang Bang. The bandits also shot at and burned a village inhabited by local people. This is a serious violation of the Lao armistice agreement.

### 以美式武器装备的老挝匪徒  进攻寮国战斗部队的集结区

1955-03-06
第4版()
专栏：

    以美式武器装备的老挝匪徒
    进攻寮国战斗部队的集结区
    【新华社五日讯】越南通讯社河内四日讯：据悉，在老挝，一批数目超过一百人并且装备着美国迫击炮和机关枪的武装匪徒，在三月一日进攻驻扎在桑怒西南四十公里芒磅地方的寮国战斗部队，这个地方是在寮国战斗部队的集结区以内。
    这批武装匪徒在三月一日整日整夜地进行攻击要占领距芒磅六百公尺的三座山。匪徒们还射击和焚烧当地人民居住的一个村庄。这是一个严重违反老挝停战协定的行为。
