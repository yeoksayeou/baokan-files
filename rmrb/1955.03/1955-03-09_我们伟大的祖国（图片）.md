Title: Our Great Motherland (Picture)
Author:
Date: 1955-03-09
Page: 1

    Our Great Motherland
    The first group of female Jianglun (Yangtze River Steamer) drivers in New China has officially started driving Jianglun on the Yangtze River. Lin Youhua was the first female driver to formally participate in the voyage of the new "People No. 1" freighter in January this year, and now she can accurately steer the vessel forward. This is a scene of Lin Youhua assisting the captain, Mo Jiarui, a special-class labor model in the Yangtze River area, in studying navigation orders.

### 我们伟大的祖国（图片）

1955-03-09
第1版()
专栏：

    我们伟大的祖国
    新中国第一批女江轮驾驶员已正式在长江上驾驶江轮。林幼华是今年一月第一个正式参加“人民一号”新型货轮航行的女驾驶员，现在她已能准确地驾驶船只前进。这是林幼华正协助船长、长江区特等劳动模范莫家瑞研究航行命令的情形。
