Title: Zhukov Cables Peng Dehuai to Thank Him for Congratulations on Soviet Army Day
Author:
Date: 1955-03-01
Page: 1

    Xinhua News Agency, February 28th: Soviet Defense Minister Marshal Zhukov cabled a reply to Peng Dehuai, Minister of National Defense of the People's Republic of China, thanking him for his congratulations on the 37th anniversary of the founding of the Soviet Army. The cable reads as follows: Comrade Peng Dehuai, Minister of National Defense of the People's Republic of China, Beijing:
    I extend my heartfelt gratitude to you, esteemed Minister Comrade, for your congratulations and good wishes on the occasion of the 37th anniversary of the founding of the Soviet Army and Navy.
    Minister of Defense of the Union of Soviet Socialist Republics, Marshal of the Soviet Union, Zhukov
    February 24, 1955

### 朱可夫电谢彭德怀对苏军建军节的祝贺

1955-03-01
第1版()
专栏：

    朱可夫电谢彭德怀对苏军建军节的祝贺
    【新华社二十八日讯】苏联国防部长朱可夫元帅电复中华人民共和国国防部长彭德怀，感谢他对苏军建军三十七周年的祝贺。电文如下：北京中华人民共和国国防部长彭德怀同志：
    当苏联陆海军建军三十七周年之际，承致祝贺和良好的愿望，我谨向您，敬爱的部长同志，致以衷心的谢意。
    苏维埃社会主义共和国联盟国防部长苏联元帅  朱可夫
    一九五五年二月二十四日
