Title: Western European People Firmly Oppose the Paris Agreements
Author:
Date: 1955-03-27
Page: 4
    Western European People Firmly Oppose the Paris Agreements
    French people hold up signs in front of the National Assembly, opposing the Paris Agreements. (The signs read: Don't forget Dobrian, Auschwitz, Oradour!)
    British people go to the House of Commons, demanding that members take firm action to oppose the arming of West Germany and the Paris Agreements.
    West Berlin youth hold demonstrations, opposing the Paris Agreements and conscription.
    Austrian youth hold demonstrations in Vienna, opposing the revival of the German Wehrmacht and demanding Austrian independence. (Xinhua News Agency Special)

### 西欧各国人民坚决反对巴黎协定（图片）

1955-03-27
第4版()
专栏：

    西欧各国人民坚决反对巴黎协定
    法国人民在国民议会前高举标语，反对巴黎协定。（标语上写的是：不要忘记多布里昂、奥斯威辛、奥拉多尔！）
    英国人民到下议院去，要求议员们采取坚决行动，反对武装西德，反对巴黎协定。
    西柏林青年举行示威游行，反对巴黎协定，反对征兵。
    奥地利青年在维也纳举行示威游行，反对复活德国国防军，要求奥地利独立。（新华社特稿）
