Title: Living Buddha Losang Tubten and Others Leave Beijing to Return to Xikang
Author:
Date: 1955-03-07
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency:

Living Buddha Losang Tubten·Jiangyang Kezhu, of the Litang Great Lamasery in Litang County, Xikang Province, and Ngawang Dingji·Acheng·Losai, along with eight others, left Beijing on the morning of the 3rd to return to Xikang.

Those who came to the station to see them off included the Deputy Director and Deputy Director of the General Office of the Nationalities Affairs Commission of the People's Republic of China, and many others.

Living Buddha Losang Tubten and his party arrived in Beijing on January 12th. They were received by Chairman Mao Zedong, Chairman Liu Shaoqi, and Premier Zhou Enlai, and also separately paid respects to the Dalai Lama Danzeng Gyatso and Bainqen Erdini Qoiji Gyaincain. Losang Tubten and others also visited factories, scenic spots, and temples in Beijing and Tianjin.

### 罗桑土登活佛等离京回西康

1955-03-07
第1版()
专栏：

    罗桑土登活佛等离京回西康
    【新华社讯】西康省理塘县理塘大喇嘛寺活佛罗桑土登·降央克珠和昂旺定吉·阿称·罗赛等九人，在三日上午离京回西康。
    到车站欢送的，有中华人民共和国民族事务委员会副主任和办公厅副主任等多人。
    罗桑土登活佛等一行在一月十二日来京。他们曾受到毛泽东主席、刘少奇委员长和周恩来总理的接见，并曾分别拜见了达赖喇嘛·丹增嘉措和班禅额尔德尼·却吉坚赞。罗桑土登等还在北京、天津两地参观了工厂、名胜和寺院等多处。
