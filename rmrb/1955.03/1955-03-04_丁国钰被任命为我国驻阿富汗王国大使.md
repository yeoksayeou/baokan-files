Title: Ding Guoyu Appointed as Our Country's Ambassador to the Kingdom of Afghanistan
Author:
Date: 1955-03-04
Page: 1

    Ding Guoyu Appointed as Our Country's Ambassador to the Kingdom of Afghanistan
    [Xinhua News Agency, March 3rd] Mao Zedong, Chairman of the People's Republic of China, has appointed Ding Guoyu as the Ambassador Extraordinary and Plenipotentiary of the People's Republic of China to the Kingdom of Afghanistan.

### 丁国钰被任命为我国驻阿富汗王国大使

1955-03-04
第1版()
专栏：

    丁国钰被任命为我国驻阿富汗王国大使
    【新华社三日讯】中华人民共和国主席毛泽东任命丁国钰为中华人民共和国驻阿富汗王国特命全权大使。
