Title: Danish Minister Holds Reception Celebrating Danish National Day
Author:
Date: 1955-03-12
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, March 11th—Danish Minister to China, Mr. Gregers Tycho, held a reception in Beijing on the 11th to celebrate Danish National Day.

More than one hundred people were invited to attend the reception. Li Xiannian, Vice Premier of the State Council of the People's Republic of China; Dalai Lama Danzeng Gyatso, Vice Chairman of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress; Zhu Xuefan, Minister of Posts and Telecommunications of the People's Republic of China; Zhang Hanfu, Vice Minister of Foreign Affairs; Ding Xilin, Vice Minister of Culture; Zeng Zhaolun, Vice Minister of Higher Education; and Wu Han, Deputy Mayor of Beijing, were invited to attend. Ambassadors and diplomatic officials from various countries to China were also invited to attend.

The reception proceeded in a friendly atmosphere.

### 丹麦公使举行庆祝丹麦国庆日招待会

1955-03-12
第1版()
专栏：

    丹麦公使举行庆祝丹麦国庆日招待会
    【新华社十一日讯】丹麦驻华公使格瑞杰生十一日在北京举行招待会，庆祝丹麦国庆日。
    应邀出席招待会的有一百多人。中华人民共和国国务院副总理李先念，全国人民代表大会常务委员会副委员长达赖喇嘛·丹增嘉措，中华人民共和国邮电部部长朱学范，外交部副部长章汉夫，文化部副部长丁西林、高等教育部副部长曾昭抡，北京市副市长吴晗等人应邀出席。应邀出席的还有各国驻华使节和外交官员。
    招待会在友好空气中进行。
