Title: Presidium of the Bulgarian National Assembly Issues Order Ending State of War Between Bulgaria and Germany
Author:
Date: 1955-03-03
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Sofia: The Presidium of the Bulgarian National Assembly issued an order on March 1 concerning the end of the state of war between the People's Republic of Bulgaria and Germany, according to the Bulgarian Telegraph Agency.

The order stated that, for the benefit of the Bulgarian and German peoples, it is necessary to end the state of war between the two countries and establish peaceful and normal relations between Bulgaria and the entire German people.

The order stated that, based on the proposal of the Government of the People's Republic of Bulgaria and Article 35, Paragraph 5 of the Constitution of the People's Republic of Bulgaria, the Presidium of the Bulgarian National Assembly has decided to: end the state of war between the People's Republic of Bulgaria and Germany, and establish peaceful relations between the two countries, while retaining the rights and obligations they enjoy and bear under international agreements on eliminating the consequences of the war against Germany.

The People's Republic of Bulgaria will continue to cooperate with all peace-loving countries to jointly defend and consolidate peace, strive for a peaceful solution to the German question, and safeguard the security of the People's Republic of Bulgaria and the peoples of European countries.

### 保加利亚国民议会主席团  发布结束保德之间战争状态的命令

1955-03-03
第4版()
专栏：

    保加利亚国民议会主席团
    发布结束保德之间战争状态的命令
    【新华社二日讯】据保加利亚通讯社索非亚讯：保加利亚国民议会主席团在三月一日发布了关于结束保加利亚人民共和国和德国之间的战争状态的命令。
    命令说，为了保加利亚和德国人民的利益，必须结束两国之间的战争状态，在保加利亚和全体德国人民之间建立和平的、正常的关系。
    命令说，保加利亚国民议会主席团根据保加利亚人民共和国政府的建议和保加利亚人民共和国宪法第三十五条五款，决定：结束保加利亚人民共和国和德国之间的战争状态，建立两国之间的和平关系，同时保留它们根据关于消除对德战争的后果的国际协定而享有的权利和承担的义务。
    保加利亚人民共和国将继续同一切爱好和平的国家合作，共同为保卫和巩固和平、争取和平解决德国问题、保障保加利亚人民共和国和欧洲各国人民的安全而斗争。
