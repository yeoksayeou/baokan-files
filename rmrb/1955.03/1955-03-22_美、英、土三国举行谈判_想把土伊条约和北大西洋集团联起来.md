Title: US, Britain, and Turkey Hold Negotiations to Link the Turco-Iraqi Pact with the North Atlantic Group
Author:
Date: 1955-03-22
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Cairo, March 19th: The Cairo weekly "Today's News" reported that the United States, Britain, and Turkey are holding negotiations to discuss the formal linking of the Turco-Iraqi Pact with the North Atlantic Group. Quoting official sources, it wrote: "The Turco-Iraqi Pact—which the United States, Britain, and Pakistan will soon join—will be formally linked to the North Atlantic Alliance."

It further stated that the Turco-Iraqi Pact and the Turco-Pakistani Pact will be linked to the North Atlantic Group through a special agreement, which is currently being drafted by the governments of Britain, the United States, and Turkey.

### 美、英、土三国举行谈判  想把土伊条约和北大西洋集团联起来

1955-03-22
第4版()
专栏：

    美、英、土三国举行谈判
    想把土伊条约和北大西洋集团联起来
    【新华社二十日讯】塔斯社开罗十九日讯：埃及“今天消息”周报的驻伦敦记者报道，美国、英国和土耳其正在举行谈判，讨论把土伊条约和北大西洋集团正式联结起来的问题。他引用官方人士的话写道：“土伊条约——不久美国、英国和巴基斯坦就要参加这个条约——将正式同北大西洋联盟联结起来。”
    他又说，土伊条约和土巴条约将经由一个特别协定同北大西洋集团联结起来，这个协定现在正由英国、美国和土耳其的政府拟订中。
