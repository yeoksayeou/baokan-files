Title: Iran Says It Will Attend the Asian-African Conference
Author:
Date: 1955-03-12
Page: 4
    
    Xinhua News Agency, Tehran, March 11th: According to reports from Tehran, Iranian Foreign Minister Abdullah Entezam stated at a press conference in Tehran on the 10th that Iran will participate in the Asian-African Conference scheduled to be held in Indonesia.
    
    Furthermore, South Vietnam has formally expressed its acceptance of the invitation to attend the Asian-African Conference.

### 伊朗表示将参加亚非会议

1955-03-12
第4版()
专栏：

    伊朗表示将参加亚非会议
    【新华社十一日讯】据德黑兰消息：伊朗外交大臣阿卜杜拉·安迪让十日在德黑兰举行的记者招待会上说，伊朗将参加准备在印度尼西亚召开的亚非会议。
    又讯：不久以前，南越已经正式表示接受参加亚非会议的邀请。
