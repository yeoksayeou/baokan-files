Title: Representatives of Communist and Workers' Parties from Many Countries Lay Wreaths at Marx's Tomb
Author:
Date: 1955-03-17
Page: 1

(Xinhua News Agency, London, 16th) According to TASS, March 14th marked the 72nd anniversary of Karl Marx's death. Representatives of the Communist Party of Great Britain and the Communist and Workers' Parties of the Soviet Union, the United States, Romania, Hungary, Poland, and the People's Republic of China laid wreaths at Marx's tomb in Highgate Cemetery in London on this day.

### 许多国家的共产党工人党代表  在马克思墓上献花圈

1955-03-17
第1版()
专栏：

    许多国家的共产党工人党代表
    在马克思墓上献花圈
    【新华社十六日讯】据塔斯社伦敦讯：三月十四日是卡尔·马克思逝世七十二周年纪念日。英国共产党和苏联、美国、罗马尼亚、匈牙利、波兰、中华人民共和国的共产党和工人党的代表在这一天赴伦敦海格特公墓在马克思墓上献了花圈。
