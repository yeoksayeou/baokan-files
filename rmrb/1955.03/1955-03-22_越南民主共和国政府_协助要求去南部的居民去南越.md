Title: Government of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam Assists Residents Wishing to Go South to South Vietnam
Author:
Date: 1955-03-22
Page: 4

    Government of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam
    Assists Residents Wishing to Go South to South Vietnam
    Xinhua News Agency report: On March 16, the government of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam assisted more than 1,600 residents wishing to go south to South Vietnam. This is another proof of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam government's faithful implementation of the Geneva Accords.
    On the 14th, the government of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam transported this group of residents to岑山 by automobile. On the evening of the 16th, this group of residents left岑山 for South Vietnam on the Polish ship "Krynica". The mobile group of the International Supervisory and Control Commission went to the scene to inspect the departure of this group of residents.

### 越南民主共和国政府  协助要求去南部的居民去南越

1955-03-22
第4版()
专栏：

    越南民主共和国政府
    协助要求去南部的居民去南越
    据新华社讯：越南民主共和国政府在三月十六日协助一千六百多名要求去南部的居民到南越去。这是越南民主共和国政府忠实履行日内瓦协议的又一证明。
    十四日，越南民主共和国政府用汽车把这批居民送到岑山。十六日晚，这批居民乘波兰轮船“克林斯基号”离开岑山去南越。国际监察和监督委员会的机动小组曾到场视察这批居民的离去。
