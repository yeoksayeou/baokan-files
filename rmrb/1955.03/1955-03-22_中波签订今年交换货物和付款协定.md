Title: China and Poland Sign Agreement on Exchange of Goods and Payments for This Year
Author:
Date: 1955-03-22
Page: 1
    
    Xinhua News Agency, March 21st: The governments of the People's Republic of China and the People's Republic of Poland, in order to further develop trade relations between the two countries, strengthen friendly cooperation, and seek common economic upsurge, have signed an agreement on the exchange of goods and payments between China and Poland for 1955 in Beijing on March 21st, after friendly and harmonious negotiations between the representatives of both sides. According to this agreement, China will supply Poland with mineral products, livestock products, food products, tea products, and other commodities necessary for the national economy. Poland will supply China with complete sets of equipment, hardware and steel, machinery, vehicles, chemical raw materials, and other commodities necessary for the national economy.
    Xu Xuehan, Vice Minister of Foreign Trade of the People's Republic of China, signed the agreement on behalf of the Government of the People's Republic of China; Bolesław Gałaj, head of the trade delegation of the People's Republic of Poland and Vice Minister of Foreign Trade of Poland, signed the agreement on behalf of the Government of the People's Republic of Poland.
    Attending the signing ceremony on the Chinese side were: Ye Jizhuang, Minister of Foreign Trade of the People's Republic of China, He Wei, Assistant Minister of Foreign Affairs, and directors and deputy directors of relevant bureaus of the Ministry of Foreign Trade. On the Polish side were: Kirylok, Ambassador of the People's Republic of Poland to China, Gołdgrober, Commercial Counselor of the Polish Embassy in China, and all members of the trade delegation of the People's Republic of Poland.

### 中波签订今年交换货物和付款协定

1955-03-22
第1版()
专栏：

    中波签订今年交换货物和付款协定
    【新华社二十一日讯】中华人民共和国政府和波兰人民共和国政府，为了进一步发展两国间的贸易关系，加强两国间的友好合作，以求得共同经济高涨，双方代表经过友好融洽的谈判，在一九五五年三月二十一日在北京签订了一九五五年中波交换货物和付款协定。根据这个协定，中国将供应波兰矿产类、畜产类、食品类、茶叶类等国民经济所必需的商品。波兰将供应中国成套设备、五金钢材、机械、车辆、化工原料等国民经济所必需的商品。
    代表中华人民共和国政府在这个协定上签字的是中华人民共和国对外贸易部副部长徐雪寒；代表波兰人民共和国政府在这个协定上签字的是波兰人民共和国贸易代表团团长、波兰对外贸易部副部长包列斯瓦夫·高伦斯基。
    参加签字典礼的，中国方面有：中华人民共和国对外贸易部部长叶季壮、外交部部长助理何伟和对外贸易部有关的局长、副局长。波兰方面有：波兰人民共和国驻华大使基里洛克，波兰驻华大使馆商务参赞高尔德格若伯和波兰人民共和国贸易代表团全体团员。
