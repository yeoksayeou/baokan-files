Title: Women Participating in the Socialist Construction of the Motherland (Photos)
Author:
Date: 1955-03-08
Page: 3

Women Participating in the Socialist Construction of the Motherland

　　In the nation's economic construction, many women hold leadership positions in enterprise departments. This is Zhang Chen (center), the director of the Shenyang Pneumatic Tool Factory, discussing the assembly of a new product – a pneumatic drill – with the chief production dispatcher.　Xinhua News Agency reporter Yu Zhao photo

　　Many female tractor drivers have been transferred to the state-owned Youyi Farm. This is Li Ailin (center), who received a model award in production and learning, Dong Yanlan (left), a graduate of the Agricultural Mechanization School affiliated with the Northeast Agricultural College, and Bai Guizhi, transferred from the state-owned Tongbei Farm, talking about their work.　Xinhua News Agency reporter Tan Zhiqiang photo

　　Jin Ailian (second from right), a female engineer at the Fushun Power Plant, is discussing technical issues in production with workers.
Xinhua News Agency reporter Zhang Shenming photo

　　Many women have become the backbone of promoting the mutual aid and cooperation movement. This is Shen Jilan (first from left), the vice director of the Xigou Township Agricultural, Forestry and Animal Husbandry Production Cooperative in Pingshun County, Shanxi Province, shelling corn with cooperative members.　Xinhua News Agency draft

　　Zhang Lianfang (center), a physician at the Shanghai Fourth People's Hospital, selflessly treated and prevented diseases for road construction workers in support of the construction of the Kangzang Highway, and was twice named a model worker. This is her working actively in the hospital after returning to Shanghai.
Xinhua News Agency reporter Yang Putao photo

　　In the Ili Kazakh Autonomous Prefecture of Xinjiang Province, Bao Dai (third from left), a female deputy township head who is enthusiastic about serving the people, was elected in the general election. She is soliciting opinions from women. Xinhua News Agency reporter Wang Ping photo

### 参加祖国社会主义建设的妇女们（图片）

1955-03-08
第3版()
专栏：

    参加祖国社会主义建设的妇女们
    在国家经济建设中，许多妇女担任了企业部门的领导工作。这是沈阳风动工具厂厂长张忱（中）同生产总调度员研究新产品——风钻的装配问题的情形。　新华社记者　于肇摄
    许多女拖拉机手调到了国营友谊农场。这是在生产和学习中获得模范奖的李爱林（中）、东北农学院附属农业机械化学校毕业的董延兰（左）同国营通北农场调来的白桂芝在一起交谈工作的情形。　新华社记者　谭志强摄
    抚顺发电厂女工程师金爱濂（右起第二人）正在同工人们研究生产中的技术问题。
                      新华社记者　张申明摄
    许多妇女已经成为推动互助合作运动的骨干。这是山西省平顺县西沟乡农林牧生产合作社副社长申纪兰（左第一人）同社员们一起剥玉米的情形。　新华社稿
    上海第四人民医院医师张莲芳（中）在支援康藏公路的修建中，忘我地为筑路工人防治疾病，曾二次被评为模范工作者。这是她回到上海后在医院中积极工作的情形。
                      新华社记者　杨溥涛摄
    新疆省伊犁哈萨克族自治州在普选中选出了一位热心为人民办事的女副乡长包代（左起第三人），她正向妇女们征求意见。新华社记者　王平摄
