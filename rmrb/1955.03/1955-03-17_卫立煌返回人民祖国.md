Title: Wei Lihuang Returns to the People's Motherland
Author:
Date: 1955-03-17
Page: 1

    Xinhua News Agency, Guangzhou, 16th. Former Kuomintang senior general Wei Lihuang has returned to the people's motherland on the 15th.
    When Wei Lihuang and his wife, Ms. Han Quanhua, arrived in Guangzhou from Hong Kong, Lin Liming and Rao Zhangfeng, vice chairmen of the Guangdong Provincial Committee of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference, and others welcomed them. Mr. Wei immediately issued a "Letter to Comrades and Friends in Taiwan."
    Before liberation, Wei Lihuang successively held the positions of Deputy Commander-in-Chief of the Second War Zone of the Kuomintang Army, Commander-in-Chief of the First War Zone, Commander of the Chinese Expeditionary Force, Deputy Commander-in-Chief of the Chinese Army, Commander-in-Chief of the Northeast "Bandit Suppression," and member of the Kuomintang Central Executive Committee. He went to Hong Kong before the liberation of Nanjing in early 1949 and resided there until his return to Guangzhou.

### 卫立煌返回人民祖国

1955-03-17
第1版()
专栏：

    卫立煌返回人民祖国
    【新华社广州十六日电】前国民党高级将领卫立煌已在十五日返回人民祖国。
    卫立煌和他的夫人韩权华女士自香港抵达广州时，中国人民政治协商会议广东省委员会副主席林李明、饶彰风等前往欢迎。卫氏当即发表了“告台湾袍泽朋友书”。
    卫立煌在解放前，曾历任国民党军第二战区副司令长官、第一战区司令长官、中国远征军司令、中国陆军副总司令、东北“剿匪”总司令和国民党中央执行委员等职，一九四九年初南京解放前夕前往香港，一直居住到回广州时止。
