Title: Balkan Pact Nations Meeting Ends
Author:
Date: 1955-03-05
Page: 4

    Xinhua News Agency, Ankara, March 3rd: TASS reports that the Balkan Pact nations held a meeting in Ankara from February 28th to March 2nd. The participants in the meeting were: Turkish Foreign Minister Köprülü, Greek Foreign Minister Stephanopoulos, and Yugoslav Secretary of State for Foreign Affairs Popović.
    A communiqué issued after the meeting stated that the participants discussed the international situation in light of developments since the Balkan Pact nations meeting in Bled last August.
    The meeting adopted a decision to establish a Balkan Consultative Assembly composed of sixty parliamentarians, with twenty members from each of the three countries. The meeting also adopted a decision on further cooperation among the Balkan Pact nations in technical, economic, and cultural fields.
    It should be noted that two of the three member states of the Balkan Pact (Turkey and Greece) are members of the North Atlantic Treaty Organization.

### 巴尔干条约国家会议结束

1955-03-05
第4版()
专栏：

    巴尔干条约国家会议结束
    【新华社四日讯】　塔斯社安卡拉三日讯：巴尔干条约国家从二月二十八日到三月二日在安卡拉召开了会议。参加会议的有：土耳其外交部长柯普路鲁、希腊外交大臣斯特凡诺普洛斯和南斯拉夫外交秘书长波波维奇。
    会议结束后发表的公报说，会议参加者根据去年八月在布列德举行巴尔干条约国家会议以来的形势发展讨论了国际情况。
    会议通过一项决定：成立一个由三国各派二十名议员共六十名议员组成的巴尔干咨询议会。会议还通过巴尔干条约国家在技术、经济和文化方面进一步合作的决定。
    应当提起注意的是：巴尔干条约三个成员国中有两个（土耳其和希腊）是北大西洋公约成员国。
