Title: China National Import and Export Corporation Delegation Arrives in France
Author: 
Date: 1955-03-29
Page: 1

(Xinhua News Agency, Berlin, March 26) Paris News: At the invitation of the Schneider Group’s Le Creusot steelworks and the Compagnie Générale de Télégraphie Sans Fil (CSF) group of France, the technical delegation sent by the China National Import and Export Corporation arrived in France on the 24th to visit the factories and products of these two groups. The China National Import and Export Corporation technical delegation includes experts and staff in the fields of motor, telecommunications, and mechanical equipment, totaling eight people, headed by Zhang Xiancheng, Deputy Manager of the China National Import and Export Corporation.

### 我进出口公司参观团到法国

1955-03-29
第1版()
专栏：

    我进出口公司参观团到法国
    【新华社柏林二十六日电】巴黎消息：应法国施奈德集团克勒佐钢铁工厂和无线电讯总公司集团的邀请，由中国进出口公司派遣的技术参观团已在二十四日到达法国，参观这两个集团所属各工厂和产品。中国进出口公司技术参观团包括电机、电讯、机械设备等方面的专家和工作人员共八人，团长是中国进出口公司副经理张先成。
