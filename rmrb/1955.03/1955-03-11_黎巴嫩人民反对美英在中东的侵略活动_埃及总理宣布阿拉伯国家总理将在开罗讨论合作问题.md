Title: Lebanese People Oppose US-UK Aggression in the Middle East; Egyptian Prime Minister Announces Arab Prime Ministers to Discuss Cooperation in Cairo
Author:
Date: 1955-03-11
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Beirut, March 9th: TASS reports that "Al- تلغراف" (Al- تلغراف) received a letter from residents of Beirut sharply criticizing the Turkish-Iraqi military alliance and calling on the people of all Arab countries to rise up against this sinister treaty.

    A large group of Lebanese youth visited the newspaper's editorial office, condemning the Turkish-Iraqi alliance. They said that American and British imperialism are attempting to use this alliance to drag Lebanon into another war of aggression.

    The pressure exerted by the United States and Britain on Lebanon to join the Turkish-Iraqi alliance has aroused the indignation of nationally minded people in the country, who firmly oppose foreign interference in Lebanese affairs.

    "Al- تلغراف" (Al- تلغراف) reported that some prominent politicians visited Prime Minister Sami el-Solh and told him that the Lebanese people are very dissatisfied with the government's "uncertain attitude" towards the Turkish-Iraqi military alliance.

Xinhua News Agency, March 10th: Cairo dispatch: Egyptian Prime Minister Nasser announced on the 8th that the Prime Ministers of Arab countries will hold a meeting in Cairo in the second half of March to discuss political, economic and military cooperation among Arab countries based on the agreements between Egypt, Syria, and Saudi Arabia.

    Prior to this, Egyptian Minister of National Guidance, Salah Salem, visited Lebanon, Syria, Jordan, and Saudi Arabia, and discussed with the leaders of these countries the issue of strengthening cooperation among Arab countries based on the principle of rejecting any military blocs with non-Arab countries.

    Egyptian newspapers welcomed the establishment of a military and economic cooperation organization among Arab countries. "Al-Qahira" (Cairo Newspaper) emphasized that "to safeguard the interests of Arab countries, it is only through maintaining unity, and not through participating in the Turkish-Iraqi treaty." "Al-Gomhuria" (The Republic) wrote that Western countries and their agents are creating obstacles to hinder the unity of Arab countries, because "this unity will shatter their conspiracy to undermine the independence of Arab countries and impose foreign military treaties and agreements on Arab countries."

### 黎巴嫩人民反对美英在中东的侵略活动  埃及总理宣布阿拉伯国家总理将在开罗讨论合作问题

1955-03-11
第4版()
专栏：

    黎巴嫩人民反对美英在中东的侵略活动
    埃及总理宣布阿拉伯国家总理将在开罗讨论合作问题
    【新华社十日讯】塔斯社贝鲁特九日讯：“电讯报”报道，它收到一封贝鲁特一些居民寄来的信，这封信尖锐斥责土耳其—伊拉克军事联盟，并要求所有阿拉伯国家的人民起来反对这个罪恶的条约。
    一大批黎巴嫩青年访问了这家报纸的编辑部，他们斥责土耳其—伊拉克联盟。他们说，美国和英国帝国主义企图利用这个联盟把黎巴嫩拖入另一次侵略战争。
    美国和英国对黎巴嫩施加压力来使它参加土耳其—伊拉克联盟这件事，已引起国内具有民族思想的人士的愤慨，他们坚决反对外国干涉黎巴嫩的事务。
     “电讯报”报道，一些著名的政治家曾经访问总理索勒哈，并告诉他说，黎巴嫩人民非常不满意政府对于土耳其—伊拉克军事联盟的“不确定的态度”。
    【新华社十日讯】开罗消息：埃及总理纳赛尔八日宣布，阿拉伯国家的总理将在三月下半月在开罗举行会议，讨论以埃及、叙利亚和沙特阿拉伯的协定为基础的阿拉伯国家政治、经济和军事合作问题。
    在这以前，埃及国家指导部长赛拉·萨勒姆访问了黎巴嫩、叙利亚、约旦和沙特阿拉伯，并曾和这些国家的领袖讨论过根据拒绝和非阿拉伯国家组织任何军事集团的原则，来巩固阿拉伯国家之间的合作问题。
    埃及报纸欢迎在阿拉伯国家之间建立一个军事和经济合作的组织。“开罗报”强调说：“要保障阿拉伯国家的利益，只有通过保持团结，而不是通过参加土耳其—伊拉克条约。”“共和国报”写道，西方国家和它们的代理人正在制造障碍来阻挠阿拉伯国家的团结，因为“这种团结将粉碎它们想破坏阿拉伯国家的独立，并把外国军事条约和协定强加在阿拉伯国家身上的阴谋”。
