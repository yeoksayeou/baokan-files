Title: International Chopin Piano Competition Held in Warsaw; Chinese Pianist to Participate in Second Round

Author: 
Date: 1955-03-07
Page: 4

    International Chopin Piano Competition Held in Warsaw
    Chinese Pianist to Participate in Second Round

    [Xinhua News Agency, Warsaw, 6th] The Fifth International Chopin Piano Competition, which began in Warsaw on February 22nd, is continuing. Seventy-four pianists from twenty-four countries, including the Soviet Union, the People's Republic of China, France, Britain, and Germany, are participating in the competition.

    During the competition, the audience gave high praise to the performance of Soviet young pianist Vladimir Ashkenazy. Chinese young pianist Fu Cong has been selected to participate in the second round of the competition in the first round. A total of forty-one people were selected to enter the second round of the competition. Those with excellent results in the second round will then proceed to the third round to determine the final results of the competition.

### 国际萧邦钢琴比赛在华沙举行  我钢琴家将参加第二轮比赛

1955-03-07
第4版()
专栏：

    国际萧邦钢琴比赛在华沙举行
    我钢琴家将参加第二轮比赛
    【新华社华沙六日电】二月二十二日在华沙开始举行的第五届国际萧邦钢琴比赛大会正在继续进行。参加这次比赛的有苏联、中华人民共和国、法国、英国和德国等二十四个国家的七十四位钢琴家。
    在比赛中，大会听众对苏联青年钢琴家弗拉吉米尔·阿希肯纳齐的表演给予很高的评价。中国青年钢琴家傅聪在第一轮比赛中已被录取参加第二轮比赛。录入第二轮比赛的共有四十一人，第二轮比赛中的成绩优良者将再进行第三轮比赛，决定比赛的最后结果。
