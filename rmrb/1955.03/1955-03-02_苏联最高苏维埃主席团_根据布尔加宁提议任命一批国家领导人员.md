Title: Presidium of the Supreme Soviet of the USSR Appoints a Number of National Leaders Based on Bulganin's Proposal
Author:
Date: 1955-03-02
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, February 28th: TASS reports: The Presidium of the Supreme Soviet of the USSR, based on the proposal of the Chairman of the Council of Ministers of the USSR, N.A. Bulganin, passed the following resolutions:

    I. In addition to L.M. Kaganovich and V.M. Molotov serving as First Deputy Chairmen of the Council of Ministers of the USSR, appoint A.I. Mikoyan, M.G. Pervukhin, and M.Z. Saburov as First Deputy Chairmen of the Council of Ministers of the USSR.

    II. Appoint A.P. Zavenyagin, V.A. Kucherenko, P.P. Lobanov, and M.V. Khrunichev as Deputy Chairmen of the Council of Ministers of the USSR.

    III. In view of V.A. Malyshev's leadership of a number of machine-building ministries within the Council of Ministers of the USSR, relieve him of his post as Minister of Medium Machine Building of the USSR.

    IV. Appoint A.P. Zavenyagin, Deputy Chairman of the Council of Ministers of the USSR, as Minister of Medium Machine Building of the USSR.

### 苏联最高苏维埃主席团  根据布尔加宁提议任命一批国家领导人员

1955-03-02
第1版()
专栏：

    苏联最高苏维埃主席团
    根据布尔加宁提议任命一批国家领导人员
    【新华社一日讯】塔斯社莫斯科二月二十八日讯：苏联最高苏维埃主席团根据苏联部长会议主席尼·亚·布尔加宁的提议，通过决议如下：
    一、除拉·莫·卡冈诺维奇和维·米·莫洛托夫担任苏联部长会议第一副主席以外，任命阿·伊·米高扬、米·格·别尔乌辛和马·扎·萨布罗夫为苏联部长会议第一副主席。
    二、任命阿·普·查文尼亚金、维·阿·库切连柯、帕·帕·洛巴诺夫和麦·维·赫鲁尼切夫为苏联部长会议副主席。
    三、鉴于维·亚·马利歇夫在苏联部长会议中担任领导一批机器制造部的职务，解除他的苏联中型机器制造部部长的职务。
    四、任命苏联部长会议副主席阿·普·查文尼亚金为苏联中型机器制造部部长。
