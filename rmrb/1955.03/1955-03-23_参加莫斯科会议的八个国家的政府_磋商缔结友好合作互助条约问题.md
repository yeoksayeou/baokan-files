Title: Governments of Eight Countries Participating in Moscow Conference Consult on Treaty of Friendship, Cooperation, and Mutual Assistance
Author:
Date: 1955-03-23
Page: 1

    Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, March 21st (TASS): Regarding the consultations held at the Soviet Ministry of Foreign Affairs concerning the resolutions of the Moscow Conference.
    Over the recent period, the governments of the Soviet Union, the People's Republic of Poland, the Czechoslovak Republic, the German Democractic Republic, the People's Republic of Hungary, the Romanian People's Republic, the People's Republic of Bulgaria, and the People's Republic of Albania, in accordance with the resolutions of the Moscow Conference of European countries on safeguarding peace and security in Europe, have conducted consultations on the issue of concluding a treaty of friendship, cooperation, and mutual assistance among the eight countries participating in the Moscow Conference.
    The consultations have demonstrated that the aforementioned eight governments hold completely consistent views on the principles of this treaty and on the organization of a joint command of the signatory states, which will be established once the Paris Agreements are ratified, to guarantee the security of these countries and to maintain peace in Europe.
    The Government of the People's Republic of China also participated in the consultations of the eight governments and expressed its complete agreement with the proposed measures.

### 参加莫斯科会议的八个国家的政府  磋商缔结友好合作互助条约问题

1955-03-23
第1版()
专栏：

    参加莫斯科会议的八个国家的政府
    磋商缔结友好合作互助条约问题
    【新华社二十二日讯】塔斯社莫斯科二十一日电：关于就莫斯科会议决议在苏联外交部举行磋商的情形。
    最近一个时期，苏联、波兰人民共和国、捷克斯洛伐克共和国、德意志民主共和国、匈牙利人民共和国、罗马尼亚人民共和国、保加利亚人民共和国和阿尔巴尼亚人民共和国政府，根据欧洲国家关于保障欧洲和平和安全的莫斯科会议的决议，就缔结参加莫斯科会议的八国的友好合作互助条约的问题进行了磋商。
    磋商过程表明，上述八国政府对这个条约的原则和组织缔约国联合司令部的问题，抱有完全一致的看法，这个司令部将在巴黎协定一旦批准以后建立，以保证这些国家的安全和维护欧洲和平。
    中华人民共和国政府也参加了八国政府的磋商，它表示完全同意所拟定的措施。
