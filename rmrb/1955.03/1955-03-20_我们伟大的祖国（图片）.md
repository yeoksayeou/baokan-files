    Our Great Motherland
    The desolate mountainous areas along the Kangzang Highway are gradually becoming prosperous. In the pine forest on the shore of the glacial lake at the eastern foot of Que'er Mountain, a sanatorium has been built, and post offices and banks have also set up offices. Now people call this beautiful place Xinluhai.

### 我们伟大的祖国（图片）

1955-03-20
第1版()
专栏：

    我们伟大的祖国
    康藏公路沿线的荒僻山地，正逐渐繁荣起来。在这雀儿山东麓的冰川湖滨的松林里，已经修建了一座疗养所，邮局和银行也都设立了办公机构。现在人们把这个风景秀丽的地方叫做新路海。
