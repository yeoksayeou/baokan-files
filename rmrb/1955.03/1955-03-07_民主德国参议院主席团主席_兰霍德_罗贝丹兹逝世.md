Title: Chairman of the Presidium of the People's Chamber of the German Democratic Republic, Lothar Bolz, Passes Away
Author:
Date: 1955-03-07
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Berlin, March 6th. TASS, Berlin, 5th: According to a report by the German News Agency, Lothar Bolz, Chairman of the Presidium of the People's Chamber of the German Democratic Republic and member of the Presidium of the Central Executive Committee of the Christian Democratic Union of Germany, passed away on March 5th.

Bolz was one of the founders of the Christian Democratic Union of Germany. From the founding of the German Democratic Republic, he had been the Chairman of the Presidium of the People's Chamber of the German Democratic Republic.

### 民主德国参议院主席团主席  兰霍德·罗贝丹兹逝世

1955-03-07
第4版()
专栏：

    民主德国参议院主席团主席
    兰霍德·罗贝丹兹逝世
    【新华社六日讯】塔斯社柏林五日讯：据德意志通讯社报道，德意志民主共和国参议院主席团主席、德国基督教民主联盟中央常务委员会主席团委员兰霍德·罗贝丹兹于三月五日逝世。
    罗贝丹兹是德国基督教民主联盟的创始人之一。从德意志民主共和国成立的时候起，他就一直是德意志民主共和国参议院主席团的主席。
