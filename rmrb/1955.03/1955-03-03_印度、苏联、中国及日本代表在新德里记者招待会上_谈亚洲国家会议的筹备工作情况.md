Title: Indian, Soviet, Chinese, and Japanese Representatives Hold Press Conference in New Delhi to Discuss Preparations for the Asian Countries Conference
Author:
Date: 1955-03-03
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, New Delhi, March 2nd. Mrs. Rameshwari Nehru, Chairman of the Indian Preparatory Committee for the Asian Countries Conference, and representatives from the Soviet Union, China, and Japan who are working in the Secretariat of the Asian Countries Conference, gave speeches at a press conference in New Delhi on March 1st, outlining the preparations for the upcoming Asian Countries Conference.

Mrs. Nehru said: It is expected that the conference will produce some concrete steps to enable the entire Asian region to have a clear understanding of the Five Principles of Peaceful Coexistence outlined in the joint statement of the Prime Ministers of India and China. The conference will help to galvanize Asian public opinion to demand solutions to pressing Asian issues. The conference will lay the foundation for future consultations and cooperation among Asian countries, and will help Asia to have a common view on international issues.

Mrs. Nehru said that the Asian Countries Conference will discuss the threat of nuclear weapons and study the possibility of using atomic energy for peaceful purposes and constructive development. She said that the sooner the stockpiles of nuclear weapons are destroyed and the sooner their manufacture is banned, the sooner people can live in peace of mind and devote themselves to constructive work.

Mrs. Nehru said that the agenda of the Asian Countries Conference has been divided into five categories: political, economic, scientific, cultural, and social. The conference will be divided into five committees or working groups to prepare proposals for these five areas respectively.

Soviet representative A.V. Sofronov, Chinese representative Zheng Senyu, and Japanese representative Hatanaka Masaharu also reported on the preparations being made in their respective countries at the press conference.

Soviet representative A.V. Sofronov said that the Soviet Union has established a preparatory committee headed by Tikhonov. The Soviet people warmly support this conference, and the Soviet delegation will participate in it.

Chinese representative Zheng Senyu said that the purpose of the Asian Countries Conference is in line with the demands of the Chinese people. The Chinese people widely support this conference. A delegation representing all sections of the Chinese people will attend the conference.

Japanese representative Hatanaka Masaharu explained in his speech the importance that the Japanese people attach to the Asian Countries Conference. He said that the Japanese people demand the establishment of diplomatic relations with the Soviet Union and the People's Republic of China. He said that the Japanese delegation will participate in this conference, and the Japanese people hope that this conference will be a complete success.

### 印度、苏联、中国及日本代表在新德里记者招待会上  谈亚洲国家会议的筹备工作情况

1955-03-03
第4版()
专栏：

    印度、苏联、中国及日本代表在新德里记者招待会上
    谈亚洲国家会议的筹备工作情况
    【新华社新德里二日电】亚洲国家会议印度筹备委员会主席拉米希瓦里·尼赫鲁夫人和参加亚洲国家会议秘书处工作的苏联、中国及日本代表三月一日在新德里的记者招待会上发表谈话，叙述即将召开的亚洲国家会议的筹备情况。
    尼赫鲁夫人说：预料会议的结果将会产生一些具体步骤，以使整个亚洲对印中两国总理的联合声明中的五项和平原则有明智的了解。会议将有助于鼓舞亚洲舆论要求解决那些急待解决的亚洲问题。会议将为亚洲国家之间今后的磋商和合作打下基础，并将帮助使亚洲对国际问题有共同的看法。
    尼赫鲁夫人说，亚洲国家会议将讨论核子武器的威胁和研究把原子能用于和平目的和建设性发展的可能性，她说，越早把核子武器的储藏销毁，和越早禁止制造它们，人们就能越早地安心生活，并且致力于建设性的工作。
    尼赫鲁夫人说，亚洲国家会议的议程已分为政治、经济、科学、文化和社会五项。会议将分为五个委员会或工作小组，以便分别为这五个方面准备提案。
    苏联代表安·维·索弗罗诺夫，中国代表郑森禹和日本代表畑中政春也在记者招待会上报告了在他们各自国家内正在进行的筹备工作情况。
    苏联代表安·维·索弗罗诺夫说，苏联已经成立了以吉洪诺夫为首的筹备委员会。苏联人民热烈支持这个会议，苏联代表团将参加这个会议。
    中国代表郑森禹说，亚洲国家会议的宗旨是和中国人民的要求相符合的。中国人民广泛地支持这个会议。一个代表中国各阶层人民的代表团将要出席这个会议。
    日本代表畑中政春在讲话中说明了日本人民对亚洲国家会议的重视。他说，日本人民要求同苏联和中华人民共和国建立外交关系。他说，日本代表团将参加这个会议，日本人民希望这个会议圆满成功。
