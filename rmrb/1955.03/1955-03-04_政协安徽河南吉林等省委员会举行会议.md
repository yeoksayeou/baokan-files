Title: CPPCC Provincial Committees of Anhui, Henan, Jilin, and Other Provinces Hold Meetings
Author:
Date: 1955-03-04
Page: 3

Xinhua News Agency dispatch:

    The First Session of the First Anhui Provincial Committee of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference (CPPCC) was held in Hefei from February 3rd to 9th. The members of this committee were all produced through democratic consultation, and the number of members is almost double that of the members of the Anhui Provincial People's Representatives Conference Consultative Committee.
    More than thirty committee members spoke at the meeting. They all expressed their support for the Declaration, the Constitution of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference, and related resolutions of the First Session of the Second National Committee of the CPPCC. They also expressed their determination to continue to unite the people of the entire province more widely under the leadership of the Chinese Communist Party, assist the government in implementing policies and laws, and do a good job in various tasks. First and foremost, they would focus on the current urgent task of damming breaches and reinforcing embankments, as well as spring plowing and production, and support the liberation of Taiwan and the construction of socialist industry with practical actions. The members also made many suggestions regarding the government's various tasks in their speeches.
    At the end of the meeting, Zeng Xisheng was elected as the Chairman of the First Anhui Provincial Committee of the CPPCC by secret ballot, and Li Yunhe, Yu Yanong, Chen Yinnan, Cheng Shifan, Yao Ke, and Fang Zhiwu were elected as Vice Chairmen. (According to Xinhua News Agency)

    The First Session of the First Henan Provincial Committee of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference was held in Zhengzhou from February 7th to 9th. The number of committee members was more than double that of the former Henan Provincial People's Representatives Conference Consultative Committee, and the units they represented were also more extensive than in the past.
    In their speeches, the members unanimously expressed their determination to abide by the Constitution of the CPPCC and support the Declaration of the First Session of the Second National Committee of the CPPCC. They also angrily denounced the crime of U.S. imperialism in invading our country's Taiwan, and expressed that they would unite even more closely around the Chinese Communist Party and Chairman Mao in the future, mobilize and unite the people of the entire province more widely, strengthen their work, overcome difficulties, and strive together with the people of the whole country for the liberation of Taiwan and for building our country into a great socialist nation.
    On the last day of the meeting, Pan Fusheng was elected as the Chairman of the First Henan Provincial Committee of the CPPCC by secret ballot, and Yang Weiping, Wang Guohua, Hou Lianying, Wang Yizhai, Liu Hongwen, Gao Zhenwu, Liu Jixue, and Ren Zhiming were elected as Vice Chairmen. In addition, the Standing Committee members and the Secretary-General were also elected. (According to Xinhua News Agency)

    The First Session of the First Jilin Provincial Committee of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference was held in Changchun from February 10th to 11th. A total of sixty-three members attended this meeting.
    Twenty members spoke at the meeting. They believed that the number of members participating in this meeting had doubled compared to the past, proving that the people's democratic united front was more extensive and consolidated. The members expressed that they would further unite around the Chinese Communist Party in the future, earnestly implement the Constitution of the CPPCC, more widely unite the people of all strata in the province to promote various tasks, strengthen the study of Marxism-Leninism, carry out self-transformation, and complete the various construction tasks of the country in 1955. In their speeches, the members also angrily denounced the war provocations of U.S. imperialism and expressed their determination to continue to support the struggle to liberate Taiwan.
    The meeting also proposed responding to the call of the World Peace Council and quickly launching a widespread and in-depth signature campaign against the use of atomic weapons throughout the province.
    Li Diping was elected as the Chairman of the First Jilin Provincial Committee of the CPPCC by secret ballot; Xu Shouxuan, Zhang Dexin, Song Renyuan, and Cheng Shengsan were elected as Vice Chairmen.
    (According to Xinhua News Agency)

### 政协安徽河南吉林等省委员会举行会议

1955-03-04
第3版()
专栏：

    政协安徽河南吉林等省委员会举行会议
    中国人民政治协商会议第一届安徽省委员会，二月三日到九日在合肥市举行第一次全体会议。这一届委员会的委员都是经过民主协商产生的，委员名额比安徽省各界人民代表会议协商委员会的委员名额扩大了将近一倍。
    在会议上发言的委员有三十多人，他们都表示拥护中国人民政治协商会议第二届全国委员会第一次全体会议宣言、中国人民政治协商会议章程和有关决议；并表示要继续在中国共产党领导下，更广泛地团结全省人民，协助政府推行政策法令，作好各项工作，首先作好当前紧张的堵口整堤和春耕生产工作，以实际行动支援解放台湾，支援社会主义工业建设。委员们在发言中还对政府各项工作提出了许多建议。
    会议最后用无记名投票方式选举了曾希圣为中国人民政治协商会议第一届安徽省委员会主席，李云鹤、余亚农、陈荫南、程士范、姚克、房秩五为副主席。　（据新华社讯）
    中国人民政治协商会议第一届河南省委员会第一次全体会议，二月七日到九日在郑州市举行。委员会委员的名额比前河南省各界人民代表会议协商委员会的委员名额增加了一倍多，他们所代表的单位也比过去更为广泛。
    委员们在发言中一致表示要遵守中国人民政治协商会议章程，拥护中国人民政治协商会议第二届全国委员会第一次全体会议宣言。他们还愤怒地斥责了美帝国主义侵略我国台湾的罪行，并表示今后要更加紧密地团结在中国共产党和毛主席的周围，更广泛地动员和团结全省人民，加强工作，克服困难，和全国人民一起为解放台湾、为把我国建设成为一个伟大的社会主义国家而奋斗。
    会议在最后一天以无记名投票方式选举了潘复生为中国人民政治协商会议第一届河南省委员会主席，杨蔚屏、王国华、侯连瀛、王毅斋、刘鸿文、高镇五、刘积学、任芝铭为副主席，另外还选举了常务委员和秘书长。　（据新华社讯）
    中国人民政治协商会议第一届吉林省委员会二月十日到十一日在长春市举行第一次全体会议。出席这次会议的委员共六十三人。
    在会议上发言的有二十名委员，他们认为参加这次会议的委员比过去增加一倍，证明了人民民主统一战线更加广泛和巩固。委员们表示今后要进一步团结在中国共产党的周围，认真执行中国人民政治协商会议章程，更广泛地团结全省各阶层人民推动各项工作，并加强学习马克思列宁主义，进行自我改造，完成一九五五年国家的各项建设任务。委员们在发言中还愤怒地斥责了美帝国主义的战争挑衅行为，表示要继续支援解放台湾的斗争。
    会议还提议响应世界和平理事会号召，迅速在全省范围内普遍深入地展开反对使用原子武器的签名运动。
    会议以无记名投票方式选举了李砥平为中国人民政治协商会议第一届吉林省委员会主席；徐寿轩、张德馨、宋任远、成盛三为副主席。
　（据新华社讯）
