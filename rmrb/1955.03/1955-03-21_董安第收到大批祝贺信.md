Title: Dong Andi Receives a Large Number of Congratulatory Letters
Author:
Date: 1955-03-21
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, 20th. Patriotic youth Dong Andi recently received a large number of congratulatory letters from people in Beijing, Shanghai, Wuhan, Dalian, Tangshan, Xi'an, Suzhou, Liaoyang and other places. The letter writers include soldiers of the Chinese People's Liberation Army, students, medical workers and staff of government offices.

　　These congratulatory letters unanimously praised Dong Andi's patriotic ideology and actions, and many people also expressed their desire to learn from him in different positions. Tang Weihai, a medical worker who is interning at Xi'an People's Hospital in Shaanxi Province, said in his letter: "I am a young medical worker, and after completing my internship, I will go to work in Qinghai. I want to learn from your patriotic spirit and serve the health of all ethnic groups faithfully." Liang Zhuqi, a middle school student in Shanghai, said in his letter: "I will study hard and improve my physical fitness, and be ready to respond to the call of the motherland at any time and enlist in the army to defend the motherland."

### 董安第收到大批祝贺信

1955-03-21
第1版()
专栏：

    董安第收到大批祝贺信
     【新华社二十日讯】爱国青年董安第最近接到北京、上海、武汉、大连、唐山、西安、苏州、辽阳等各地人民写给他的大批祝贺信。写信的人中有中国人民解放军战士、学生、医务工作者和机关工作人员等。
    这些祝贺信中都一致赞扬了董安第的爱国主义思想和行动，许多人并且表示要在不同的岗位上向他学习。正在陕西省西安市人民医院实习的医务工作者唐维海在信中说：“我是一个青年医务工作者，在实习期满后，将到青海去工作。我要学习你的爱国精神，忠实地为各族人民的健康服务。”上海市的一个中学生梁竹淇在信中说：“我一定努力学习，把身体锻炼好，准备随时响应祖国的召唤，应征入伍保卫祖国。”
