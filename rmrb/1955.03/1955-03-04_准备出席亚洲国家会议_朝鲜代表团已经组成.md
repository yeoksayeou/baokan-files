Title: Preparing to Attend the Asian Countries Conference: Korean Delegation Formed
Author:
Date: 1955-03-04
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Pyongyang, March 3rd: The delegation of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea to attend the Asian Countries Conference has been formed. The head of the delegation is Pak Chong-ae, and the members are Han Sorya, Kang Ryang-uk, and Do Yoo-ho.

This delegation was selected at a joint meeting held on March 2nd by the Central Committee of the Democratic Front for the Reunification of the Fatherland and the Korean National Committee for the Defense of Peace.

### 准备出席亚洲国家会议  朝鲜代表团已经组成

1955-03-04
第4版()
专栏：

    准备出席亚洲国家会议
    朝鲜代表团已经组成
    【新华社平壤三日电】出席亚洲国家会议的朝鲜民主主义人民共和国代表团已经组成。团长是朴正爱，团员是韩雪野、康良煜、都宥浩。
    这个代表团是在朝鲜祖国统一民主主义战线中央委员会和朝鲜拥护和平全国民族委员会三月二日举行的联席会议上选出的。
