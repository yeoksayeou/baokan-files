Title: Greetings from Guo Moruo, Chairman of the Chinese People's Committee for the Defense of World Peace

Author: 
Date: 1955-03-23
Page: 1

    To the Burmese Committee for the Defense of World Peace:
    The National Enlarged Representative Conference of the Burmese Committee for the Defense of World Peace, held in Rangoon from March 18th to 20th, is a major event in the current Burmese peace movement. On behalf of the Chinese people, I extend warm congratulations to the conference. I sincerely wish the conference the anticipated success and victory.
    Guo Moruo, Chairman of the Chinese People's Committee for the Defense of World Peace
                    March 17, 1955

### 我国保卫和平委员会主席郭沫若的贺电

1955-03-23
第1版()
专栏：

    我国保卫和平委员会主席郭沫若的贺电缅甸保卫世界和平委员会：
    三月十八日至二十日在仰光举行的缅甸保卫世界和平委员会全国扩大代表大会，是当前缅甸和平运动中的一件大事，我谨代表中国人民向会议致以热烈的祝贺。谨祝大会获得预期的成功和胜利。
    中国人民保卫世界和平委员会主席　郭沫若
                    一九五五年三月十七日
