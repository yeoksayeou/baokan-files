Title: Our Country's Famous Labor Models, Combat Heroes, and People from All Fields Report to the駐旅順口 Soviet Army on the Progress of Construction in Our Country Over the Past Five Years
Author:
Date: 1955-03-26
Page: 1

    Xinhua News Agency, Lüda, March 25th: The Lüda Sino-Soviet Friendship Association recently invited more than 30 of our country's famous labor models, combat heroes, and people from all fields to report to the Soviet troops stationed in the Lüshunkou area, who are about to return home. The reporting activities began on the 23rd. The reports focus on introducing the economic, cultural, and other aspects of construction in our country over the past five years, as well as the people's living conditions, to the Soviet troops. The speakers also held a get-together with the Soviet troops.
    Those participating in the reports include famous industrial labor models Wang Chonglun, Li Chuanjiang, Yi Shijuan (female), Zhu Shunyu, Li Shouyong, agricultural labor models Li Shunda, Han En, Wuhan flood prevention special-class hero Yang Mingcong, railway labor model Guo Shude, postal and telecommunications labor model Guo Xiuyun (female), model medical worker Hu Yamei (female); also participating in the reports are famous air force combat hero Wang Hai, Volunteer Army special-class hero Zhao Maochen, first-class hero Zhao Renhu, the commander of a certain PLA unit that liberated Yijiangshan Island, combat hero Mao Zhangmiao, the PLA Navy unit representative Zhu Hongxi who sank the Chiang bandit army's "Taiping" warship, and the PLA representative Hong Liu who constructed the Kangzang Highway; in addition, there are also Liao Jingtian, Deputy Secretary-General of the Sino-Soviet Friendship Association headquarters, Xi Zhanyuan, Minister of the Labor Insurance Department of the All-China Federation of Trade Unions, and Liu Jialin, Deputy Director of the Propaganda Department of the All-China Democratic Women's Federation.
    On the 23rd, Wang Chonglun, Li Shunda, Zhu Hongxi, and others gave six reports, each of which was warmly welcomed by Soviet officers, soldiers, and their families. The Soviet officers and soldiers were all delighted by the great achievements of the Chinese people in various fields and expressed that they would work harder in the future to further develop and consolidate Sino-Soviet friendship. After the reports, the Soviet army also showed movies and held tea parties to entertain the reporters.

### 我国著名劳动模范、战斗英雄和各方面人士  向驻旅顺口苏军报告我国五年来建设情况

1955-03-26
第1版()
专栏：

    我国著名劳动模范、战斗英雄和各方面人士
    向驻旅顺口苏军报告我国五年来建设情况
    【新华社旅大二十五日电】旅大市中苏友好协会最近邀请了我国著名的劳动模范、战斗英雄和各方面人士三十多人向即将回国的驻旅顺口地区的苏军作报告。报告活动已从二十三日开始。报告的内容是着重向苏军介绍我国五年来经济、文化等方面的建设情况和人民生活的情况。报告的人还和苏军举行联欢。
    参加报告的有著名的工业劳动模范王崇伦、李川江、裔式娟（女）、朱顺馀、李寿永，农业劳动模范李顺达、韩恩，武汉防汛特等功臣杨明聪，铁路劳动模范郭树德，邮电劳动模范郭秀云（女），模范医务工作者胡亚美（女）；参加报告的还有著名的空军战斗英雄王海，志愿军特等功臣赵毛臣、一等功臣赵仁虎，解放一江山岛的解放军某部指挥员、战斗英雄毛张苗，击沉蒋贼军“太平号”军舰的解放军海军某舰艇部队代表朱鸿禧和修筑康藏公路的解放军代表洪流；另外还有中苏友好协会总会副总干事廖经天、中华全国总工会劳动保险部部长郗占元、中华全国民主妇女联合会宣传部副部长刘加林等。
    在二十三日这一天，有王崇伦、李顺达、朱鸿禧等人作了六场报告，每场都受到苏军军官、士兵和家属们的热烈欢迎。苏军军官、战士们都为中国人民的各种巨大成就而欢欣，并表示今后要为中苏友谊的进一步发展和巩固而更加努力。报告会后，苏军还放映电影、举行茶会等招待报告员。
