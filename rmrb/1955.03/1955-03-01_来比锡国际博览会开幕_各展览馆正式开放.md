Title: Leipzig International Fair Opens, All Exhibition Halls Officially Open
Author:
Date: 1955-03-01
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Leipzig, February 28th: The 1955 Spring Leipzig International Fair's exhibition halls officially opened on the 27th. The sixteen consumer goods exhibition halls in the center of Leipzig were crowded with a constant stream of people from 8:00 in the morning. The technical exhibition area halls also opened at 1:00 PM.

Leaders of the German Democratic Republic government, government delegations from countries participating in the fair, and envoys from various countries to the German Democratic Republic visited the exhibition halls of various countries in batches. At 1:00 PM, German Democratic Republic Prime Minister Grotewohl, Deputy Prime Minister Ulbricht, and Soviet government delegation leader Mikoyan, accompanied by the head of the Chinese government delegation Jiang Guangnai, the head of the Chinese exhibition delegation Gao Shirong, and the Chargé d'affaires of the Chinese Embassy Wang Yutian, visited the Chinese pavilion. The guests unanimously praised the new achievements of China's socialist industrialization construction shown in this exhibition.

At 2:00 PM, the Chinese pavilion opened to the public. The Chinese pavilion attracted great interest from the audience with its various machine tools and world-renowned silk, carpets, handicrafts, native products, and specialties. The achievements China has made in the two years since the implementation of the Five-Year Plan have left a deep impression on visitors from all over the world.

### 来比锡国际博览会开幕  各展览馆正式开放

1955-03-01
第4版()
专栏：

    来比锡国际博览会开幕
    各展览馆正式开放
    【新华社来比锡二十八日电】一九五五年春季来比锡国际博览会各展览馆二十七日正式开放。位于来比锡市中心的十六个日用品展览馆从早晨八时起就拥挤着川流不息的人群。技术展览区各馆也在下午一时开放。
    德意志民主共和国政府领袖、各国参加博览会的政府代表团以及各国驻德意志民主共和国的使节等分批参观了各国的展览馆。下午一时，德意志民主共和国总理格罗提渥、副总理乌布利希、苏联政府代表团团长米高扬等由中国政府代表团团长蒋光鼐、中国展览团团长高士融和中国大使馆临时代办王雨田等陪同到中国馆参观。来宾们对于这次展览中所显示的中国社会主义工业化建设的新成就一致表示赞扬。
    下午二时，中国馆向观众开放。中国馆以各式机床和享有世界盛名的丝绸、地毯、手工艺品、土产和特产等引起了观众的巨大兴趣。中国实行五年计划两年来所取得的成就给来自世界各国的参观者留下了深刻的印象。
