Title: All-China Youth Federation Calls on Young People to Welcome the World Youth Festival
Author:
Date: 1955-03-19
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, March 18th. The Standing Committee of the Second National Committee of the All-China Youth Federation held its seventh meeting on the evening of the 18th. The meeting focused on discussing and adopting the resolution of the All-China Youth Federation on preparing to participate in the Fifth World Youth and Student Festival for Peace and Friendship, calling on young people across the country to greet this festival with greater achievements in their work and study.

The meeting also discussed the main points of the All-China Youth Federation's work plan for 1955, deciding that this year the All-China Youth Federation should focus on further resolving the problems of social youth and ethnic minority youth work under the general goal of socialist construction and socialist transformation.

The current Secretary-General, Yu Beichen, has been transferred. The meeting decided that Du Qian will serve as the Secretary-General of the All-China Youth Federation, and Wang Chuanbin will serve as the Deputy Secretary-General.

### 全国青联号召广大青年迎接世界青年联欢节

1955-03-19
第1版()
专栏：

    全国青联号召广大青年迎接世界青年联欢节
    【新华社十八日讯】　中华全国民主青年联合会第二届全国委员会常务委员会，十八日晚举行第七次会议。会议着重讨论和通过了中华全国民主青年联合会关于筹备参加第五届世界青年与学生和平友谊联欢节的决议，号召全国青年以自己在劳动和学习中的更大成就来迎接本届联欢节。
    会议还讨论了全国青联一九五五年工作计划要点，决定今年青联应在社会主义建设和社会主义改造的总目标下，以进一步解决社会青年和民族青年工作问题为中心。
    现任秘书长于北辰已调动工作。会议决定由杜前担任全国青联秘书长，王传斌担任副秘书长。
