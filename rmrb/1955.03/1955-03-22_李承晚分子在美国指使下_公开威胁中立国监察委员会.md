Title: Syngman Rhee Clique, Under US Direction, Openly Threatens Neutral Nations Supervisory Commission

Author: 
Date: 1955-03-22
Page: 4

    Xinhua News Agency, Seoul, March 20th: Syngman Rhee group's "Foreign Minister" Pyun Yung-tai issued a statement on the 19th, openly clamoring that the Syngman Rhee group will take concrete measures to force the departure of the Polish and Czechoslovak members of the Neutral Nations Supervisory Commission from South Korea. He even hinted that these measures include the use of force.
    This clamor of the Syngman Rhee group openly threatening the Neutral Nations Supervisory Commission is in execution of its American master's plan to unilaterally dissolve the Neutral Nations Supervisory Commission. In his talk, Pyun Yung-tai also openly advocated declaring the armistice invalid. This exposes that the purpose of the United States and the Syngman Rhee group in actively sabotaging the Neutral Nations Supervisory Commission is to undermine the entire armistice agreement and pave the way for plans to prepare for a new war.

### 李承晚分子在美国指使下  公开威胁中立国监察委员会

1955-03-22
第4版()
专栏：

    李承晚分子在美国指使下
    公开威胁中立国监察委员会
    【新华社二十日讯】汉城消息：李承晚集团“外务部长官”卞荣泰十九日发表声明，公然叫嚣李承晚集团将采取具体的措施强迫中立国监察委员会的波兰委员和捷克斯洛伐克委员离开南朝鲜。他甚至暗示，这些措施包括使用武力。
    李承晚集团这种公开威胁中立国监察委员会的叫嚣，是在执行它的美国主子片面解散中立国监察委员会的计划。卞荣泰在他的谈话中还公开主张宣布停战无效。这就暴露出，美国和李承晚集团积极破坏中立国监察委员会的目的，是要破坏整个停战协定，为准备新战争的计划铺平道路。
