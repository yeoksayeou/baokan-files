Title: Sino-Romanian Cultural Cooperation Agreement this year's Implementation Plan Signed
Author:
Date: 1955-03-14
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, Bucharest: After friendly and cooperative discussions between representatives of the People's Republic of China and the Romanian People's Republic, the 1955 Implementation Plan for the Sino-Romanian Cultural Cooperation Agreement was signed in Bucharest on March 10th.

Representing the Chinese side was Ke Bonian, head of the Chinese delegation and Ambassador Extraordinary and Plenipotentiary of the People's Republic of China to Romania. Representing the Romanian side was Mi Rosianu, head of the Romanian delegation and Chairman of the Romanian Association for Foreign Culture.

In addition to members of the delegations from both sides, those attending the signing ceremony included Ni Dinulescu, First Deputy Minister of Education of Romania, Con Prisnea, First Deputy Minister of Culture, Ni Ciuoroiu, Deputy Minister of Foreign Affairs, as well as figures from Romanian science, culture, art, and journalism.

Personnel from the Embassy of the People's Republic of China in Romania also attended the signing ceremony.

### 中罗文化合作协定今年执行计划签字

1955-03-14
第1版()
专栏：

    中罗文化合作协定今年执行计划签字
    【新华社讯】布加勒斯特消息：中罗文化合作协定一九五五年执行计划经过中华人民共和国和罗马尼亚人民共和国双方代表在友好合作气氛中进行商谈后，已于三月十日在布加勒斯特签字。
    代表中国方面签字的是中国代表团团长、中华人民共和国驻罗马尼亚特命全权大使柯柏年；代表罗马尼亚方面签字的是罗马尼亚代表团团长、罗马尼亚对外文化协会主席米·罗山亚努。
    参加签字仪式的除双方代表团人员外，还有罗马尼亚教育部第一副部长尼·迪努列斯库，文化部第一副部长康·普里斯内亚，外交部副部长尼·丘罗以及罗马尼亚科学、文化、艺术和新闻界人士。
    参加签字仪式的还有中华人民共和国驻罗马尼亚大使馆人员。
