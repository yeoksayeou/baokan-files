Title: Bulganin Receives Burmese Ambassador to the Soviet Union
Author:
Date: 1955-03-11
Page: 4

    Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, March 9th: TASS, Moscow, March 9th—The Chairman of the Council of Ministers of the Soviet Union, N.A. Bulganin, received U Ohn, Ambassador Extraordinary and Plenipotentiary of the Union of Burma to the Soviet Union, on March 9.

### 布尔加宁接见缅甸驻苏大使

1955-03-11
第4版()
专栏：

    布尔加宁接见缅甸驻苏大使
    【新华社十日讯】塔斯社莫斯科九日讯：苏联部长会议主席尼·亚·布尔加宁，在三月九日接见了缅甸联邦驻苏联特命全权大使吴旺。
