Title: The United States and Syngman Rhee Intensify Efforts to Sabotage the Armistice Agreement; South Korean National Assembly Publicly Resolves to Abolish the Armistice Agreement
Author:
Date: 1955-03-25
Page: 4

    Xinhua News Agency, March 24th: Seoul dispatch: Under the manipulation of the Syngman Rhee clique, the South Korean National Assembly passed a resolution on the 23rd, actually suggesting that the Syngman Rhee government "declare the Korean Armistice Agreement invalid" and demanding that it "take immediate action" to force the Polish and Czechoslovakian members of the Neutral Nations Supervisory Commission to leave South Korea. This is another concrete step by the Syngman Rhee clique in implementing the plan of its American masters to sabotage the Korean Armistice Agreement. Prior to this, Syngman Rhee's "Minister of Foreign Affairs" Pyun Yung-tae clamored on the 19th that the Syngman Rhee clique would take concrete measures to force the Polish and Czechoslovakian members of the Neutral Nations Supervisory Commission to leave South Korea. This new provocative act by the Syngman Rhee clique shows that the United States and the Syngman Rhee clique have intensified their steps in preparing for war in Korea.

### 美国和李承晚加紧破坏停战协定  南朝鲜国会公然决议要废除停战协定

1955-03-25
第4版()
专栏：

    美国和李承晚加紧破坏停战协定
    南朝鲜国会公然决议要废除停战协定
   【新华社二十四日讯】汉城消息：南朝鲜国会在李承晚集团操纵下二十三日通过决议，竟然建议李承晚政府“宣布朝鲜停战协定无效”，并且要求它“立刻采取行动”，强迫中立国监察委员会的波兰委员和捷克斯洛伐克委员离开南朝鲜。这是李承晚集团执行美国主子破坏朝鲜停战协定的计划的又一个具体步骤。在这以前，李承晚的“外务部长官”卞荣泰曾在十九日叫嚣李承晚集团将采取具体措施，强迫中立国监察委员会的波兰委员和捷克斯洛伐克委员离开南朝鲜。李承晚集团这一新的挑衅行为，表明美国和李承晚集团已加紧了在朝鲜准备战争的步骤。
