Title: Albanian People Eagerly Subscribe to Government Bonds; First Day of Subscription Exceeds Quota
Author:
Date: 1955-03-18
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency, Tirana, March 17th: The Albanian people exceeded the quota on the first day of subscribing to the fourth issue of government bonds. According to a communique issued by the Albanian Ministry of Finance, as of 10:00 PM on March 15th, the total amount of government bonds subscribed nationwide had reached 408,322,000 leks (Albanian currency), while the originally planned issuance amount was 400 million leks.

### 阿尔巴尼亚人民踊跃认购公债  第一天认购就突破定额

1955-03-18
第4版()
专栏：

    阿尔巴尼亚人民踊跃认购公债
    第一天认购就突破定额
    【新华社十七日讯】据阿尔巴尼亚通讯社地拉那讯：阿尔巴尼亚人民开始认购第四期公债的第一天就已经突破了定额。据阿尔巴尼亚财政部发表的公报说，截至三月十五日下午十时止，全国认购公债的总额已经达到四亿零八百三十二万二千里克（阿币名），而原订发行数额是四亿里克。
