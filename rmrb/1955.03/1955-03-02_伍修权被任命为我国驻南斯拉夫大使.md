Title: Wu Xiuquan Appointed as China's Ambassador to Yugoslavia
Author:
Date: 1955-03-02
Page: 1

    Xinhua News Agency, 1st. -- Chairman Mao Zedong of the People's Republic of China has appointed Wu Xiuquan as the Ambassador Extraordinary and Plenipotentary of the People's Republic of China to the Federal People's Republic of Yugoslavia.
    The State Council of the People's Republic of China has relieved Wu Xiuquan of his post as Vice Minister of Foreign Affairs of the People's Republic of China.

### 伍修权被任命为我国驻南斯拉夫大使

1955-03-02
第1版()
专栏：

    伍修权被任命为我国驻南斯拉夫大使
    【新华社一日讯】中华人民共和国主席毛泽东任命伍修权为中华人民共和国驻南斯拉夫联邦人民共和国特命全权大使。
    中华人民共和国国务院免去伍修权的中华人民共和国外交部副部长的职务。
