Title: Beijing Holds Amateur Workers' Art Exhibition
Author: Wang Shucun
Date: 1955-03-18
Page: 3

    The "Beijing Amateur Workers' Art Exhibition," jointly sponsored by the Propaganda Department of the Beijing Federation of Trade Unions and the Beijing Federation of Literary and Art Workers, opened on March 15th at the Working People's Cultural Palace. The exhibition serves two purposes: firstly, to review the art activities of factories, mines, and enterprises in Beijing and to summarize the experience of this work, in order to further promote amateur art creation by workers in the city and better serve political propaganda and production construction; and secondly, to select some outstanding works from this exhibition to participate in the "First Workers' Amateur Art Creation Exhibition," jointly organized by the All-China Federation of Trade Unions and the Chinese Artists Association, scheduled for this year's International Labor Day on May 1st. The exhibition features over ninety works including sketches, quick sketches, and cartoons. It is scheduled to run for ten days.

### 北京举办职工业余美术展览会
王树村
1955-03-18
第3版()
专栏：

    北京举办职工业余美术展览会
    北京市总工会宣传部、北京市文学艺术工作者联合会联合举办的“北京市职工业余美术展览会”，三月十五日在劳动人民文化宫开幕。这次展出，一方面是为了检阅北京市各厂、矿、企业的美术活动情况，并总结这一工作的经验，以进一步推动本市职工业余美术创作，更好地为政治宣传、为生产建设服务；另一方面，准备从这次展览会中挑选一部分优秀的作品，参加中华全国总工会和中国美术家协会决定于今年“五一”国际劳动节联合举行的
  “第一届工人业余美术创作展览会”。作品有素描、速写、漫画等共九十多件。预定展出十天。  （王树村）
