Title: Women in Major Cities Across the Country Celebrate International Women's Day

Author: 
Date: 1955-03-09
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, March 8th: Women in the capital joyfully celebrated International Women's Day on March 8th. Today, the Summer Palace, Beihai Park, the Temple of Heaven, and other places were open to women free of charge. In Beijing's Cultural Palace of the Working People, the melting snow revealed a constant stream of people visiting. The "Roll of Honor" in the ancient cypress grove displayed large photographs of five female advanced producers today. On the Cultural Palace's stadium, six women's basketball teams performed exhibition matches. In the Labor Theater, more than 3,500 women enjoyed Peking Opera, Ping Opera, Quyi Opera, and Qinshu performances. Famous Peking Opera actress Wu Suqiu and Ping Opera actress Li Yilan performed wonderful programs for the women. On this day, eleven cinemas across the city simultaneously screened the film "A Disturbance." Many district cultural centers performed short plays and slide shows for housewives.

Many factories, government offices, schools, and suburban villages held commemorative meetings, report meetings, forums, and literary and artistic evenings. Yang Shu, Director of the Propaganda Department of the Beijing Municipal Committee of the Communist Party of China, gave a report to more than 1,300 cadres from industrial and mining enterprises and government offices on strengthening communist moral education and establishing correct views on marriage. The Beijing Democratic Women's Federation held a reception to entertain representatives of families of revolutionary martyrs and soldiers, female model workers from all walks of life, female advanced workers, and women's workers. More than 1,200 people attended the reception. At a commemorative meeting attended by more than 500 women in the Dongdan District, Bai Shulan, a female labor model in Beijing and a skilled worker in the drawnwork production cooperative, said: "Today we can celebrate our own festival, but the women in Taiwan are still suffering. We must work hard in production and support the People's Liberation Army in liberating our sisters in Taiwan as soon as possible!" More than 300 female members of the production brigades of the Hongxing Collective Farm in the suburbs held commemorative meetings and forums. The female members all expressed their determination to actively participate in the spring plowing production competition and strive for increased production this year.

According to Xinhua News Agency, March 8th: Women in major cities such as Tianjin, Shanghai, and Shenyang held various gatherings to commemorate International Women's Day on March 8th.

More than 2,000 women from all walks of life in Tianjin held a commemorative meeting for International Women's Day on March 8th. Women from the Soviet Union and people's democratic countries in Tianjin also participated in the commemorative meeting. Luo Yun, Director of the Tianjin Democratic Women's Federation, delivered a speech at the meeting. Bai Jian, Deputy Secretary of the Tianjin Municipal Committee of the Communist Party of China and Deputy Mayor of Tianjin, gave a report on the current international situation and the achievements of Tianjin women in participating in national economic construction.

Women from all walks of life in Shanghai held a celebration meeting for International Women's Day on the evening of the 7th. More than 1,600 women from all walks of life participated in the meeting. International female friends in Shanghai were also invited to attend the meeting. Zhao Xian, Director of the Shanghai Democratic Women's Federation, spoke at the meeting about the contributions of Shanghai women in various fields such as industrial and agricultural production and cultural education since liberation. Chen Pixian, Second Secretary of the Shanghai Municipal Committee of the Communist Party of China, and Jin Zhonghua, Deputy Mayor of Shanghai, both spoke at the meeting, extending warm congratulations to the women of Shanghai.

On the morning of the 8th, women from all walks of life in Wuhan held a commemorative meeting. Xiao Huina, Deputy Director of the Wuhan Democratic Women's Federation, called on women from all walks of life in the city to actively participate in and support the country's industrial construction, raise their socialist consciousness, and defend world peace and oppose wars of aggression with practical actions. Song Kanfu, Second Secretary of the Wuhan Municipal Committee of the Communist Party of China and Mayor of Wuhan, Soviet friend Andreyeva, and women representatives Wang Ergu and Xia Wangying spoke at the meeting.

Female cadres from the directly subordinate organs of Liaoning Province gathered in Shenyang on the 7th to commemorate International Women's Day. Li Huang, Deputy Secretary of the Liaoning Provincial Committee of the Communist Party of China, explained the current international situation and the main tasks of Liaoning Province in 1955 at the meeting. He also analyzed how female cadres should correctly treat their work, handle marriage and family issues, and children's education issues.

More than 800 women representatives from all walks of life in Chongqing held a commemorative meeting. Hua Yi, Director of the Chongqing Democratic Women's Federation, gave a report at the meeting. Industrial labor model He Xiuying and agricultural labor model Zhang Lirong spoke at the meeting.

More than 1,000 women from all walks of life in Xi'an held a commemorative meeting. Fang Zhongru, Second Secretary of the Xi'an Municipal Committee of the Communist Party of China, called on women in Xi'an to work with the people of the city to strive to complete and overfulfill the industrial construction tasks of Xi'an.

At the commemorative meeting held by women from all walks of life in Guangdong Province and Guangzhou City, Qu Mengjue, Director of the Guangdong Democratic Women's Federation, reported on the achievements of women in Guangdong Province and Guangzhou City in participating in the construction of the motherland. She called on women throughout the province to work hard, strengthen unity, continue to implement the Marriage Law, establish new moral views on marriage, and support the liberation of Taiwan with practical actions.

### 全国各大城市妇女欢度“三八”国际妇女节

1955-03-09
第1版()
专栏：

    全国各大城市妇女欢度“三八”国际妇女节
    【新华社八日讯】首都妇女欢乐地度过“三八”国际妇女节。今天，颐和园、北海公园、天坛等地都为妇女免费开放。北京市劳动人民文化宫里积雪初融，川流不息的人群在这里游览。古柏林中的“光荣榜”今天挂上了五个女先进生产者的大幅照片。在文化宫的体育场上，有六队女子篮球队作表演赛。在劳动剧场里，三千五百多名妇女欣赏了京剧、评剧、曲剧和琴书。著名京剧女演员吴素秋、评剧女演员李忆兰为妇女们演出了精采节目。这一天，全市十一所电影院同时放映了影片“一场风波”。许多区的文化馆为家庭妇女们演出了短剧和幻灯等文艺节目。
    很多工厂、机关、学校和郊区农村的妇女举行了纪念会、报告会、座谈会和文艺晚会。中共北京市委员会宣传部部长杨述向全市工矿企业、机关的一千三百多名干部作了关于加强共产主义道德教育树立正确的婚姻观点的报告。北京市民主妇女联合会举行了招待会，招待烈属、军属代表、各界妇女模范、女先进工作者和妇女工作者，出席招待会的有一千二百多人。在东单区五百多名妇女参加的纪念会上，北京市女劳动模范、挑花生产合作社的生产能手白淑兰说：“今天我们能欢度自己的节日，但台湾的妇女还受着苦难。我们要努力生产，支援解放军早日解放台湾的妇女同胞！”郊区红星集体农庄各生产大队的三百多名女庄员分别举行了纪念会、座谈会，女庄员们都表示要积极参加春耕生产竞赛，争取今年的增产。
    据新华社八日讯：天津、上海、沈阳等各大城市的妇女，举行各种集会纪念“三八”国际妇女节。
    天津市各界妇女两千多人在八日举行了“三八”国际妇女节纪念大会。现在天津的苏联和人民民主国家的妇女也参加了纪念大会。天津市民主妇女联合会主任罗云在会上致词后，中共天津市委副书记、天津市副市长白坚就目前的国际形势和天津市妇女参加国家经济建设方面的成就作了报告。
    上海市各界妇女在七日晚举行庆祝“三八”国际妇女节大会。参加大会的各界妇女一千六百多人。在上海的国际妇女友人也应邀出席了大会。上海市民主妇女联合会主任赵先在会上谈到了解放后上海妇女在工农业生产和文化教育等各方面的贡献。中共上海市委员会第二书记陈丕显和上海市副市长金仲华都在会上讲了话，向上海妇女致热烈的祝贺。
    八日上午，武汉市各界妇女举行了纪念大会。武汉市民主妇女联合会副主任萧慧纳在会上号召全市各界妇女积极参加和支援国家工业建设，提高社会主义觉悟，以实际行动来保卫世界和平反对侵略战争。中国共产党武汉市委员会第二书记、武汉市市长宋侃夫，苏联友人安德列叶娃，各界妇女代表王二姑、夏望英等都在会上讲了话。
    辽宁省直属机关的妇女干部，七日在沈阳集会纪念“三八”国际妇女节。中共辽宁省委员会副书记李荒在会上讲解了目前的国际形势和一九五五年辽宁省的几项主要工作，并对妇女干部应该如何正确地对待工作，处理婚姻和家庭问题、子女教育问题等作了分析。
    重庆市各界妇女代表八百多人举行了纪念大会。会上，重庆市民主妇女联合会主任华逸作了报告。工业劳动模范何秀英、农业劳动模范张莉蓉等都在会上讲了话。
    西安市各界妇女一千多人举行了纪念大会。中共西安市委员会第二书记方仲如在会上号召西安市妇女要和全市人民一道为完成和超额完成西安市工业建设任务而奋斗。
    在广东省、广州市各界妇女举行的纪念会上，广东省民主妇女联合会主任区梦觉报告了广东省、广州市的妇女在参加祖国建设事业中，所获得的成绩。号召全省妇女要努力工作、加强团结、继续贯彻婚姻法和树立新的婚姻道德观点，用实际行动支援解放台湾。
