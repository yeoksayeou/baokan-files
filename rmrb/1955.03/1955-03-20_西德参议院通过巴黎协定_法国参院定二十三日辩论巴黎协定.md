Title: West German Senate Passes Paris Agreements; French Senate to Debate Paris Agreements on the 23rd
Author:
Date: 1955-03-20
Page: 4

    West German Senate Passes Paris Agreements
    French Senate to Debate Paris Agreements on the 23rd

    Xinhua News Agency, Bonn, March 19th: The West German Senate approved the Paris Agreements, which stipulate West German rearmament, on the 18th. The Senate passed the agreement regarding the relationship between Western countries and the Bonn government by a vote of 29 to 9; representatives from Hesse and Lower Saxony voted against it. The agreements regarding the stationing of American, British, and French troops in West Germany and West Germany's accession to the Western European Union and the North Atlantic Treaty Organization were considered passed without a formal vote.

    The Hesse representative opposed the passage of the law approving the Saar Agreement, suggesting that the Saar Agreement be submitted to a parliamentary liaison committee composed of representatives from the Senate and the Federal Parliament for study. This proposal was rejected by a vote of 21 to 17.

    TASS, Paris, March 18th: The chairmen of the parliamentary groups of various parties in the French Republic's Senate made a decision at a joint meeting held on the 17th: The Republic's Senate is scheduled to debate the Paris Agreements from March 23rd to 25th.

### 西德参议院通过巴黎协定  法国参院定二十三日辩论巴黎协定

1955-03-20
第4版()
专栏：

    西德参议院通过巴黎协定
    法国参院定二十三日辩论巴黎协定
    【新华社十九日讯】波恩消息：西德参议院已在十八日批准了规定西德重新武装的巴黎协定。参议院以二十九票对九票通过了关于西方国家同波恩政府关系的协定，黑森州和下萨克森州的代表投票反对。关于美、英、法军队留驻西德的协定和西德加入西欧联盟和北大西洋集团的协定未经正式表决即作为通过。
    黑森州代表反对通过批准萨尔协定法案，建议把萨尔协定提交由参议院和联邦议院代表组成的议会联络委员会研究。这个提案以二十一票对十七票被否决。
    【新华社十九日讯】塔斯社巴黎十八日讯：法国共和国参议院各党派议会党团主席在十七日举行的联席会议上作出决定：共和国参议院定在三月二十三日到二十五日就巴黎协定进行辩论。
