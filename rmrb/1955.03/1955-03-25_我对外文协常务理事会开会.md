Title: The Chinese People's Association for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries Holds Meeting of Standing Council

Author:

Date: 1955-03-25
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency: The Standing Council of the Chinese People's Association for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries held its second meeting on the 23rd. The meeting heard and discussed Chairman Chu Tunan's report on the work of the association since its establishment, decided to establish branches of the Chinese People's Association for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries in major cities such as Shanghai and Tianjin, and approved the China Welfare Institute as a member of the Chinese People's Association for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries. The meeting also elected Zhao Yimin as Vice President and Standing Council Member, and Wang Renshu, Li Yimang, and Chen Kangbai as Council Members.

### 我对外文协常务理事会开会

1955-03-25
第1版()
专栏：

    我对外文协常务理事会开会
    据新华社讯：中国人民对外文化协会常务理事会在二十三日举行第二次会议。会议听了和讨论了楚图南会长关于该会成立以来的会务工作的报告，并决定在上海、天津等主要城市成立中国人民对外文化协会分会，通过中国福利会为中国人民对外文化协会的会员。会议加推赵毅敏为该会副会长并任常务理事，加推王任叔、李一氓、陈康白为理事。
