Title: Capital Women's Gathering Commemorates "March 8th" Women's Day; Soviet Experts in Beijing Invite Capital Women Representatives to Attend Celebration

Author:
Date: 1955-03-08
Page: 1

    Capital Women's Gathering Commemorates "March 8th" Women's Day
    Soviet Experts in Beijing Invite Capital Women Representatives to Attend Celebration
    [By Our Staff Reporter] On the eve of "March 8th," the capital's women from all walks of life held a grand meeting to commemorate "March 8th" International Women's Day. Among those attending the meeting were representatives of female workers from various industrial and mining enterprises in the capital, representatives of female farmers from suburban areas, family members of revolutionary martyrs and revolutionary soldiers, as well as female representatives from various fields such as government organs, schools, medical and health services, science and art, culture and education, various democratic parties, various people's organizations, various ethnic minorities, and religious circles, totaling more than 1,700 people.
    Wives of foreign envoys and embassy personnel stationed in China and other international female friends were also invited to attend the meeting.
    The meeting was presided over by Lin Qiaozhi, Vice-Chairman of the Beijing Democratic Women's Federation. Zhang Xiaomei, Chairman of the Beijing Democratic Women's Federation, delivered the opening speech to the meeting. Zhang Yun, Vice-Chairman of the All-China Democratic Women's Federation, delivered a speech at the meeting (the full text of which is on page two of today's newspaper).
    Xu Guangping, head of the delegation attending the International Democratic Women's Federation Council meeting and Vice-Chairman of the All-China Democratic Women's Federation, conveyed at the meeting the proceedings of the International Democratic Women's Federation Council meeting in Geneva and the resolution on convening the World Mothers' Congress. Also speaking at the meeting were: Liu Shuying, industrial labor model of Beijing and female worker of Beijing Pharmaceutical Factory; Zhao Shuzhen, agricultural labor model of Beijing and director of the Women's Committee of Red Star Collective Farm; and Li Lichen, model member of Beijing No. 6 Bookbinding Production Cooperative and family member of a soldier.
    The meeting adopted a congratulatory telegram to Chairman Mao, a congratulatory telegram to the International Democratic Women's Federation, a congratulatory telegram to the Chinese People's Liberation Army, and a congratulatory telegram to the Chinese People's Volunteers. In their congratulatory telegram to Chairman Mao Zedong, women from all walks of life in the capital pledged: "Under the wise leadership of the Chinese Communist Party and yourself, we will unite with all the people and continue to work hard in various positions, to oppose the aggression of US imperialism, liberate our territory Taiwan, defend peace in the Far East and the world, and strive to build our country into a great socialist country!" In their congratulatory telegram to the International Democratic Women's Federation, women from all walks of life in the capital expressed their firm will to defend peace and the rights and interests of women and children, and expressed their warm support for the decision of the International Democratic Women's Federation Council to convene the World Mothers' Congress.
    The meeting concluded with a wonderful artistic performance.
    [Xinhua News Agency, 7th] On the eve of "March 8th" International Women's Day, Soviet experts in Beijing invited representatives of women from all walks of life in the capital to participate in the celebration meeting they held to commemorate "March 8th."
    In the hall decorated with colorful balloons and flowers, more than a thousand Chinese and Soviet women were dressed in festive attire, happily gathering together in an atmosphere of close friendship, celebrating their own holiday evening.
    After the celebration began, Soviet expert Bankradova and Xu Guangping, Vice-Chairman of the All-China Democratic Women's Federation, spoke at the meeting one after another. They congratulated each other on the great achievements made by women in China and the Soviet Union in the cause of defending peace and building their motherlands, and congratulated each other on the further consolidation and development of the friendship between the people and women of China and the Soviet Union. Xu Guangping also thanked the Soviet people on behalf of the Chinese women for their selfless assistance to our country's construction.
    After the speeches, the Young Pioneers of the Soviet Middle School in Beijing marched into the venue in formation. The children gave a warm Young Pioneer salute to the mothers and presented flowers to the Chinese women attending the celebration.
    Then, Soviet women in Beijing performed wonderful and moving musical and dance programs; Chinese women also gave wonderful performances. Their performances received warm applause.
    A dance was held at the end of the celebration.

### 首都妇女集会纪念“三八”妇女节  在京的苏联专家邀请首都妇女代表参加庆祝会

1955-03-08
第1版()
专栏：

    首都妇女集会纪念“三八”妇女节
    在京的苏联专家邀请首都妇女代表参加庆祝会
    【本报讯】　“三八”前夕，首都各界妇女举行纪念“三八”国际妇女节大会。参加大会的有首都各工矿企业女工代表，郊区女农民代表，革命烈士家属和革命军人家属以及机关、学校、医务卫生、科学艺术、文化教育、各民主党派、各人民团体、各少数民族和宗教界等各方面的妇女代表共一千七百多人。
    在京的各国驻华使节和使馆人员的夫人和其他国际妇女友人也应邀参加了大会。
    大会由北京市民主妇女联合会副主席林巧稚主持，北京市民主妇女联合会主席张晓梅向大会致开幕词。中华全国民主妇女联合会副主席章蕴在大会上讲话（全文见今日本报第二版）。
    出席国际民主妇联理事会代表团团长、中华全国民主妇女联合会副主席许广平在大会上传达了关于国际民主妇联理事会在日内瓦开会的经过和关于召开世界母亲代表大会的决议。在大会上讲话的还有：北京市工业劳动模范、北京市制药厂女工刘淑英，北京市农业劳动模范、红星集体农庄的妇女委员会主任赵淑贞，北京市第六装订生产合作社模范社员、军属李藜晨等人。
    大会通过了向毛主席致敬电、向国际民主妇女联合会致敬电、向中国人民解放军致敬电、向中国人民志愿军致敬电。首都各界妇女在给毛泽东主席的致敬电中提出保证说：“我们要在中国共产党和您的英明领导下，与全体人民团结一致，在各种不同的岗位上继续努力，为反对美帝国主义侵略，解放我国领土台湾，保卫远东和世界和平，为把我国建设成一个伟大的社会主义国家而奋斗到底！”在给国际民主妇女联合会的致敬电中，首都各界妇女表达了保卫和平、保卫妇女与儿童的权益的坚决意志，并表示热烈地拥护国际民主妇联理事会关于召开世界母亲代表大会的决定。
    大会最后演出了精彩的文艺节目。
    【新华社七日讯】“三八”国际妇女节前夕，在京的苏联专家邀请首都各界妇女代表参加了他们为纪念“三八”节举行的庆祝大会。
    在用彩球和鲜花装饰的大厅里，一千多位中国和苏联的妇女都穿着节日的盛装，她们兴高采烈地欢聚一堂，在亲密友好的空气里，欢度着她们自己的节日前的夜晚。
    庆祝会开始后，苏联专家班克拉多娃和中华全国民主妇女联合会副主席许广平先后在会上讲话。她们互祝中、苏两国妇女在保卫和平和建设祖国的事业中所获得的伟大成就，并互祝中、苏两国人民和妇女的友谊更加巩固和发展。许广平并代表中国妇女感谢苏联人民对我国建设的无私的援助。
    讲话后，在北京的苏联中学的少年先锋队员们整队走进会场，孩子们向母亲们致热烈的少年先锋队队礼，并向参加庆祝会的中国妇女献花。
    接着，在京的苏联妇女演出了精采而动人的音乐舞蹈等节目；中国妇女也作了精采表演。她们的演出都获得热烈的掌声。
    庆祝会的最后举行了舞会。
