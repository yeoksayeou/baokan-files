Title: Van Fleet Maneuvers to Become Chiang Kai-shek's Chief of Staff
Author:
Date: 1955-03-25
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, New York, March 23rd (TASS dispatch): The "Daily Mirror" commentator Drew Pearson reported that the U.S. Joint Chiefs of Staff recommended that the Nationalist-American Joint Command should command all armed forces in Taiwan.

Pearson wrote that this might require the appointment of an American to be Chiang Kai-shek's chief of staff. He added that Van Fleet had been trying to get this position.

### 范佛里特活动当蒋贼参谋长

1955-03-25
第4版()
专栏：

    范佛里特活动当蒋贼参谋长
   【新华社二十四日讯】塔斯社纽约二十三日讯：“每日镜报”评论员德鲁·皮尔逊报道，美国参谋长联席会议建议由国民党—美国联合司令部来指挥在台湾的所有的武装部队。
    皮尔逊写道，这就可能需要任命一个美国人来做蒋介石的参谋长，他又说，范佛里特一直在设法要得到这个职位。
