Title: Closing of the Fourth Session of the National Assembly of Czechoslovakia

Author:
Date: 1955-03-25
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Prague, March 24th: The Fourth Session of the National Assembly of the Czechoslovak Republic concluded in Prague on March 23rd.

　　After thorough discussion, the meeting unanimously approved the 1955 National Economic Development Plan and the 1955 National Budget. The meeting also approved the execution results of the 1953 National Budget and passed several bills.

　　Czechoslovak Communist Party and state leaders Zápotocký, Široký, Novotný, and others attended the closing meeting.

### 捷克斯洛伐克国民议会第四次会议闭幕

1955-03-25
第4版()
专栏：

    捷克斯洛伐克国民议会第四次会议闭幕
    【新华社布拉格二十四日电】捷克斯洛伐克共和国国民议会第四次会议，于三月二十三日在布拉格闭幕。
    会议经过充分讨论后，一致批准了一九五五年发展国民经济国家计划和一九五五年国家预算。会议还批准了一九五三年国家预算的执行结果，通过了一些法案。
    捷克斯洛伐克共产党和国家领导人萨波托斯基、西罗基和诺沃提尼等参加了闭幕会议。
