Title: Japanese Woman Musician Seki Akiko Arrives in Beijing
Author: 
Date: 1955-03-20
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, March 19th: Japanese woman musician Seki Akiko arrived in Beijing by plane on March 19th. She is in China for a visit at the invitation of the All-China Democratic Women's Federation, after attending the World Federation of Democratic Youth Executive Committee meeting.

### 日本女音乐家关鉴子到京

1955-03-20
第1版()
专栏：

    日本女音乐家关鉴子到京
    【新华社十九日讯】日本女音乐家关鉴子三月十九日乘机到达北京。她是在参加世界民主青年联盟执行委员会会议以后应中华全国民主妇女联合会的邀请来中国访问的。
