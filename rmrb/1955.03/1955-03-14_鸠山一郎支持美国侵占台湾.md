Title: Hatoyama Ichiro Supports U.S. Occupation of Taiwan
Author:
Date: 1955-03-14
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, March 12th: According to a report by United Press International correspondent Potts in Tokyo, Japanese Prime Minister Ichiro Hatoyama made a statement on the 10th regarding the situation in the Taiwan area and other issues. Disregarding the fact that Taiwan is Chinese territory, Ichiro Hatoyama actually said that "two Chinas" is the "best way" to resolve the tense situation in the Taiwan area. He also stated: "If a hot war really breaks out, we cannot legally refuse the U.S. military from using (Japan's) bases."

These claims by Ichiro Hatoyama, which support the U.S.'s policy of aggression against China, contradict his repeated expressions during the recent election campaign expressing hope for the normalization of relations between Japan and China, and contain the danger of dragging Japan into the U.S.'s war of aggression.

### 鸠山一郎支持美国侵占台湾

1955-03-14
第4版()
专栏：

    鸠山一郎支持美国侵占台湾
    【新华社十二日讯】据合众社驻东京记者波茨报道，日本首相鸠山一郎十日对他就台湾地区局势等问题发表了谈话。鸠山一郎不顾台湾是中国的领土的事实，竟然说“两个中国”是解决台湾地区的紧张局势的“最好办法”。他还表示：“如果真正发生热战的话，我们在法律上不能拒绝美国军队使用（日本的）基地”。
    鸠山一郎的这些支持美国对中国的侵略政策的主张，是和鸠山一郎在不久前的竞选运动中一再作出的希望日本同中国恢复正常关系的表示相违背的，而且包藏着把日本拖入美国侵略战争的危险。
