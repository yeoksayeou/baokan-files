Title: Hanoi People Rally to Welcome French Peace Advocates Delegation
Author:
Date: 1955-03-17
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Hanoi, 16th -- According to Vietnam News Agency, over ten thousand people in Hanoi held a rally on the evening of the 15th to welcome the French Peace Advocates Delegation.

The delegation arrived in Hanoi on March 11th. Ho Chi Minh, President of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam, received the delegation on the 14th.

At the Hanoi people's welcoming rally on the 15th, Madame Farge, the head of the delegation, delivered a speech, saying that they were extremely pleased with their visit to the Democratic Republic of Vietnam. She extended warm congratulations to the people of Hanoi.

Jacques Mitterrand, a member of the delegation and a member of the French Federal Assembly, addressed the meeting, saying: For many years, the Vietnamese and French peoples have fought together against the war that destroyed the homeland of the Vietnamese people, stained the land of Vietnam with blood, and spread sorrow in France and Vietnam.

He pointed out that France signed the Geneva Accords, and it must respect its signature and not abandon this signature under any foreign pressure.

Mitterrand said: We French men and women who participate in the peace movement solemnly guarantee that, for our honor and your rights, we demand respect for the Geneva Accords.

Le Dinh Tham, Chairman of the Vietnam Committee for the Defense of World Peace, delivered the closing speech. He wished for the further consolidation of the friendship between the Vietnamese and French peoples and the restoration and development of economic and cultural relations between the two countries.

### 河内人民集会欢迎法国和平拥护者代表团

1955-03-17
第4版()
专栏：

    河内人民集会欢迎法国和平拥护者代表团
    【新华社河内十六日电】据越南通讯社报道：河内人民一万多人在十五日晚间举行大会，欢迎法国和平拥护者代表团。
    代表团是在三月十一日到达河内的。越南民主共和国主席胡志明在十四日曾接见了代表团。
    在十五日河内人民的欢迎大会上，代表团团长法奇夫人致词，说他们对于访问越南民主共和国极感愉快。她向河内人民致热诚的祝贺。
    代表团团员、法兰西联邦议会议员雅克·密特朗在会上致词说：多年来，越南人民和法国人民曾一起斗争，反对这个破坏越南人民的祖国、血染越南的土地、在法国和越南散布哀伤的战争。
    他指出：法国签订了日内瓦协议，它必须尊重它的签字并且在任何外国压力下都不背弃这个签字。
    密特朗说：我们参加和平运动的法国男女庄严地保证，为了我们的荣誉和你们的权利，要求尊重日内瓦协议。
    越南保卫世界和平委员会主席黎廷探最后致闭会词。他祝越南人民同法国人民之间的友谊进一步巩固，两国的经济和文化关系恢复和发展。
