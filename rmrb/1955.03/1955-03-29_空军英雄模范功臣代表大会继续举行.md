Title: Air Force Hero, Model, and Meritorious Representative Conference Continues
Author:
Date: 1955-03-29
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, 28th: The first Conference of Heroes, Models, and Meritorious Personnel of the Chinese People's Liberation Army Air Force held its eighth session (28th) in the morning. Zhu De, Vice Chairman of the People's Republic of China, met with all the hero, model, and meritorious representatives at the conference. Su Yu, Chief of the General Staff of the Chinese People's Liberation Army, attended the meeting and gave important instructions. He congratulated the conference on its successful achievements on behalf of the General Staff of the Chinese People's Liberation Army, and wished the heroes, models, and meritorious personnel greater achievements in their future work and battles. In the afternoon, all the representatives present at the meeting elected the heroes and models of the Air Force by secret ballot to the accompaniment of military music.

Since the opening of the conference on the 21st, Xiao Hua, Deputy Director of the General Political Department of the Chinese People's Liberation Army, and Liu Yalou, Commander of the Chinese People's Liberation Army Air Force, and Wu Faxian, Deputy Political Commissar, have attended the conference and made reports. In his report, Air Force Commander Liu Yalou pointed out the achievements made by the Chinese People's Liberation Army Air Force in carrying out various tasks in the past five years and more, as well as the various tasks for the Air Force's construction in the future. He called on all heroes, models, and meritorious personnel to guard against arrogance and impetuosity, maintain close ties with the masses, continue to play a leading, backbone, and bridging role in future tasks, and work with the army and navy to be ready to defeat aggressors at any time, and resolutely complete the combat tasks of liberating the coastal islands occupied by the Chiang bandit army and liberating Taiwan.

Wu Faxian, Deputy Political Commissar of the Air Force, delivered a report to the conference entitled "Basic Summary of the Air Force's Merit-Earning Movement." He pointed out that the Air Force's merit-earning movement has gradually become closely integrated with the work of the troops, thus inspiring the revolutionary heroism of the broad masses of commanders and fighters, enhancing class consciousness, and encouraging fighting will, learning enthusiasm, and work initiative and creativity, which has played a positive role in the completion of various tasks. He put forward some specific opinions on further developing the merit-earning movement in the future. Finally, he called on all heroes, models, and meritorious personnel to maintain their honor, carry forward their honor, lead the masses, and carry out the Air Force's merit-earning movement more widely and deeply, so as to accelerate the modernization and regularization of the Air Force, to liberate Taiwan and eliminate the Chiang Kai-shek traitorous clique, and to resolutely implement Chairman Mao's instructions to "build a strong people's air force to defend the motherland and prepare to defeat aggressors!"

During the meeting, Zheng Changhua, second-class combat hero of the Air Force and representative of the First National People's Congress, Zhang Weiliang, an air commander who repeatedly made meritorious contributions in the struggle to liberate the coastal islands of Zhejiang, and Liu Dong, representative of a certain detachment who was awarded the collective second-class merit, and 19 others, respectively made typical reports on the deeds of collective merit-earning units and personal merit-earning deeds at the conference. In their reports, they introduced in detail their respective meritorious deeds and valuable experiences, and unanimously expressed that they would continue to work hard, move forward forever, and resolutely complete the glorious tasks entrusted to them by the Party and the people of the motherland.

### 空军英雄模范功臣代表大会继续举行

1955-03-29
第1版()
专栏：

    空军英雄模范功臣代表大会继续举行
    【新华社二十八日讯】中国人民解放军空军首届英雄模范功臣代表大会的第八天（二十八日）上午，中华人民共和国副主席朱德到会会见了全体英雄、模范、功臣代表。中国人民解放军总参谋长粟裕到会作了重要指示，并代表中国人民解放军总参谋部祝贺大会取得胜利的成就，预祝英雄、模范、功臣们在今后工作和战斗中取得更大的成就。下午，到会的全体代表在军乐伴奏声中，以无记名投票方式选举了空军的英雄和模范。
    大会自二十一日开幕以来，中国人民解放军总政治部副主任萧华，中国人民解放军空军司令员刘亚楼、副政治委员吴法宪先后到会作了报告。空军司令员刘亚楼在报告中指出了中国人民解放军空军建设五年多以来在执行各项任务中所取得的成就和今后空军建设的各项任务。他号召全体英雄、模范、功臣们戒骄戒躁，密切联系群众，在今后各项任务中继续发挥带头、骨干和桥梁作用，协同陆、海军部队，随时准备战胜侵略者，坚决完成解放沿海蒋贼军盘踞的岛屿和解放台湾的战斗任务。
    空军副政治委员吴法宪向大会作了“空军立功运动基本总结”的报告。他指出：空军立功运动已与部队工作逐步紧密结合起来，因而激发了广大指战员的革命英雄主义，提高了阶级觉悟，鼓舞了战斗意志、学习热忱和工作积极性与创造性，对各项任务的完成都起了积极作用。他对今后进一步开展立功运动提出了若干具体意见。他最后号召全体英雄、模范、功臣们要保持荣誉，发扬荣誉，带领群众，把空军的立功运动更广泛、更深入地开展起来，以加速空军现代化、正规化建设，为解放台湾、消灭蒋介石卖国集团，为坚决执行毛主席指示“建立一支强大的人民空军，保卫祖国，准备战胜侵略者”而奋斗！
    会议期间，空军二级战斗英雄、第一届全国人民代表大会代表郑长华，在解放浙江沿海岛屿的斗争中屡建战功的空中指挥员张伟良，荣立集体二等功的某支队代表刘栋等二十二人分别在大会上作了关于集体立功单位的事迹和个人立功事迹的典型报告。他们在报告中详细介绍了各自的立功事迹和宝贵经验，并一致表示要继续努力、永远前进、坚决完成党和祖国人民交给自己的光荣任务。
