Title: Executive Committee of the International Union of Students Passes Resolution Acknowledging the Contribution of the All-China Students' Federation to the Construction of the Asian Students' Sanatorium
Author:
Date: 1955-03-11
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Prague, March 10th: The Executive Committee of the International Union of Students, at a recent meeting, passed a resolution thanking the All-China Students’ Federation for its valuable contribution to the construction of the International Union of Students’ Asian Students’ Sanatorium in Beijing. The Executive Committee also expressed its gratitude to all the students and student organizations of various countries who have helped to realize this Asian Student Sanatorium project in different ways. The resolution stated that the current task of the International Union of Students and Asian national student organizations is to make full use of this huge sanatorium to serve the health of Asian students.

### 国际学生联合会执委会通过决议  感谢我学联对建设亚洲学生疗养院的贡献

1955-03-11
第1版()
专栏：

    国际学生联合会执委会通过决议
    感谢我学联对建设亚洲学生疗养院的贡献
    【新华社布拉格十日电】国际学生联合会执行委员会在最近举行的一次会议上通过决议，感谢中华全国学生联合会对设在北京的国际学生联合会亚洲学生疗养院的建成所作的有价值的贡献。执行委员会还向所有那些曾以不同的方式来帮助这个亚洲学生疗养院计划实现的各国学生和学生组织表示感谢。决议说，国际学生联合会和亚洲国家学生组织目前的任务是充分利用这个巨大的疗养院来为亚洲学生的健康服务。
