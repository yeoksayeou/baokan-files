Title: Nehru to Lead Indian Delegation to Asian-African Conference; To Visit Soviet Union in Early June This Year
Author:
Date: 1955-03-29
Page: 1

    Nehru to Lead Indian Delegation to Asian-African Conference
    To Visit Soviet Union in Early June This Year
    Xinhua News Agency, New Delhi, March 28th: According to Indian press sources, Prime Minister Nehru will lead the Indian delegation to the Asian-African Conference scheduled to be held in Indonesia on April 18th. Members of the Indian delegation will include Indian Minister of External Affairs, Mahmud, and the head of the Indian delegation to the UN General Assembly, V.K. Krishna Menon, among nine others.
    Xinhua News Agency, March 28th: TASS, Moscow, March 27th: The Soviet Foreign Ministry issued the following statement:
    Some time ago, Soviet newspapers reported that Indian Prime Minister Nehru would visit the Soviet Union this summer at the invitation of the Soviet government.
    The Indian government and the Soviet government exchanged views a few days ago regarding the date of Mr. Nehru's visit to the Soviet Union.
    It has been agreed that Mr. Nehru will visit the Soviet Union in early June this year.

### 尼赫鲁将率领印度代表团出席亚非会议  并将在今年六月初到苏联访问

1955-03-29
第1版()
专栏：

    尼赫鲁将率领印度代表团出席亚非会议
    并将在今年六月初到苏联访问
    【新华社新德里二十八日电】据印度新闻处消息：尼赫鲁总理将率领印度代表团出席定于四月十八日在印度尼西亚举行的亚非会议。印度代表团团员将包括印度外交部部长马茂德、印度出席联合国大会代表团团长维·克·克里希纳·梅农等九人。
    【新华社二十八日讯】塔斯社莫斯科二十七日讯：苏联外交部发表声明如下：
    前些时候苏联报纸报道，印度总理尼赫鲁先生应苏联政府的邀请将在今年夏天访问苏联。
    印度政府和苏联政府在几天前就尼赫鲁先生访问苏联的日期交换了意见。
    已经商定，尼赫鲁先生将在今年六月初到苏联访问。
