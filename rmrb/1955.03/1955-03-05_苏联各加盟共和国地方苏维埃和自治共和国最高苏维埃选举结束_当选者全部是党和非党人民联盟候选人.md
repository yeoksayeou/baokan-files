Title: Elections for Local Soviets of Soviet Union Republics and Supreme Soviets of Autonomous Republics Concluded: All Elected Are Candidates of the Party and Non-Party People's Alliance
Author:
Date: 1955-03-05
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, March 4th: According to a TASS dispatch from Moscow on March 3rd: The results of the elections held on February 27, 1955, for the local Soviets of eleven Soviet Union Republics and the Supreme Soviets of various autonomous republics have been announced.

In the elections for local Soviets in the Russian Federation, 99.64% to 99.97% of registered voters in various levels (krais, oblasts, districts, cities, villages, towns) participated in the elections for local Soviets. Voters who voted for candidates of the Communist Party and the Non-Party People's Alliance accounted for 99.32% to 99.78% of the voters. A total of 833,376 representatives were elected, of which women representatives accounted for 36.4%.

The results of the elections for local Soviets at all levels in the republics of Ukraine, Belarus, Uzbekistan, Georgia, Lithuania, Moldova, Latvia, Tajikistan, Turkmenistan, and Estonia have proven that the elections in all locations were conducted with high political enthusiasm from the working people. All elected representatives are candidates of the Communist Party and the Non-Party People's Alliance.

Elections for the Supreme Soviets were held in the autonomous republics of Abkhazia, Ajaria, Bashkir, Buryat-Mongol, Dagestan, Kabarda, Kara-Kalpak, Komi, Mari, Mordovian, North Ossetian, Tatar, Udmurt, Chuvash, and Yakut. The number of voters participating in the election accounted for 99.87% to 100% of the total number of voters. Voters who voted for candidates of the Communist Party and the Non-Party People's Alliance accounted for 99.28% to 99.95% of the total number of voters. All elected representatives are candidates nominated by the Communist Party and the Non-Party People's Alliance.

### 苏联各加盟共和国地方苏维埃和自治共和国最高苏维埃选举结束  当选者全部是党和非党人民联盟候选人

1955-03-05
第4版()
专栏：

    苏联各加盟共和国地方苏维埃和自治共和国最高苏维埃选举结束
    当选者全部是党和非党人民联盟候选人
    【新华社四日讯】　据塔斯社莫斯科三日讯：一九五五年二月二十七日举行的苏联十一个加盟共和国地方苏维埃和各自治共和国最高苏维埃的选举结果已经公布。
    在地方苏维埃选举中，俄罗斯联邦各地有百分之九十九点六四到百分之九十九点九七登记选民参加了各级（边疆区、州、区、市、村、镇）地方苏维埃的选举。投票选举共产党和非党人民联盟的候选人的选民占投票选民的百分之九十九·三二到百分之九十九·七八。总共选出了八十三万三千三百七十六名代表，其中妇女代表占百分之三十六点四。
    乌克兰、白俄罗斯、乌兹别克、格鲁吉亚、立陶宛、摩尔达维亚、拉脱维亚、塔吉克、土库曼和爱沙尼亚等加盟共和国各级地方苏维埃选举的结果，证明了各地的选举都是在劳动人民高涨的政治热情中进行的。所有当选的代表都是共产党和非党人民联盟的候选人。
    阿布哈兹、阿扎尔、巴什基尔、布利亚特—蒙古、达吉斯坦、卡巴尔达、卡拉—卡尔巴克、科米、马里、摩尔多夫、北奥塞金、鞑靼、乌德摩尔特、楚瓦什和雅库特等自治共和国都进行了最高苏维埃的选举。参加选举的选民占选民总数百分之九十九点八七到百分之百。投票选举共产党和非党人民联盟的候选人的选民，占投票选民总数的百分之九十九点二八到九十九点九五。所有当选的代表都是共产党和非党人民联盟提出的候选人。
