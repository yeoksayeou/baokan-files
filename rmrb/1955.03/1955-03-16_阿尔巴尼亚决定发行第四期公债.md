Author:
Date: 1955-03-16
Page: 4

Albania Decides to Issue Fourth Series of National Bonds

The Presidium of the People's Assembly of the People's Republic of Albania has promulgated a decree on the issuance of the fourth series of national bonds for developing the economy and culture. The total issuance of the bonds is 400 million lekë (Albanian currency), to be repaid in twenty years.

### 阿尔巴尼亚决定发行第四期公债

1955-03-16
第4版()
专栏：

    阿尔巴尼亚决定发行第四期公债
    阿尔巴尼亚人民共和国人民议会主席团颁布了关于发行第四期发展经济和文化公债的法令。公债总发行额为四亿里克（阿币名），分二十年偿还。
