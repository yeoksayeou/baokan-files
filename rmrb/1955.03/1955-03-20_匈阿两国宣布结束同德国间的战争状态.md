Title: Hungary and Albania Announce End to War Status with Germany

Author:

Date: 1955-03-20
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Budapest, March 19th: According to TASS, the Presidium of the Hungarian People's Republic issued an order on the 18th, announcing that the state of war between the Hungarian People's Republic and Germany had ended and that peaceful relations between the two countries had been established.

The order stated that the end of the state of war does not affect Germany's international obligations, nor does it affect the rights and responsibilities of the Hungarian People's Republic due to existing four-power international agreements concerning Germany.

Xinhua News Agency, Tirana, March 19th: According to the Albanian Telegraph Agency, the Presidium of the People's Assembly of the People's Republic of Albania issued an order, announcing that the state of war between Albania and Germany has ended and that peaceful relations between the two countries have been established.

The order pointed out that the end of the state of war with Germany does not change Germany's international obligations, nor does it affect Albania's rights and obligations under international agreements concerning Germany in which Albania participates.

### 匈阿两国宣布结束同德国间的战争状态

1955-03-20
第4版()
专栏：

    匈阿两国宣布结束同德国间的战争状态
    【新华社十九日讯】据塔斯社布达佩斯讯：匈牙利人民共和国主席团十八日发布命令，宣布匈牙利人民共和国同德国间的战争状态已告结束，两国间的和平关系已告建立。
    命令说，战争状态的结束并不影响德国的国际义务，也不影响匈牙利人民共和国因现有的关于德国的四国国际协定而具有的权利和责任。
    【新华社十九日讯】据阿尔巴尼亚通讯社地拉那十八日讯：阿尔巴尼亚人民共和国人民议会主席团发布命令，宣布阿尔巴尼亚同德国间的战争状态已经结束，两国间的和平关系已经建立。
    命令指出，结束同德国的战争状态并不改变德国的国际义务，也不影响阿尔巴尼亚在有阿尔巴尼亚参加的关于德国的国际协定中所具有的权利和义务。
