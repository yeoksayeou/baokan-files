Title: Japanese "Sohyo" Decides to Launch a Movement Demanding that Hatoyama Fulfill His Promises
Author:
Date: 1955-03-06
Page: 4

    Xinhua News Agency, Tokyo, March 4th: The General Council of Trade Unions of Japan (Sohyo), which has over three million members, held a meeting of its executive committee on the 1st and decided to launch a mass movement throughout the country to demand that the Hatoyama Cabinet fulfill its five promises to the people. These promises include: normalizing relations with China and the Soviet Union, building housing, reducing taxes, and eliminating unemployment.
    With the assistance of the left and right-wing Socialist Parties and the Labor-Farmer Party, the General Council of Trade Unions of Japan plans to officially launch this movement nationwide from late March to early April.

### 日“总评”决展开要求鸠山实现诺言的运动

1955-03-06
第4版()
专栏：

    日“总评”决展开要求鸠山实现诺言的运动
    【新华社四日讯】东京消息：拥有三百多万会员的日本工会总评议会在一日举行干事会会议，决定要在全国范围内展开一个群众运动，要求鸠山内阁实现它对人民所作的五项诺言，这些诺言包括：同中苏两国关系正常化，建设住宅，减税，消除失业等。
    日本工会总评议会在左、右两派社会党和劳农党的协助下，计划在三月下旬到四月上旬正式在全国展开这一运动。
