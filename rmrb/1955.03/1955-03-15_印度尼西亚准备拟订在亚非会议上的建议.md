Title: Indonesia Prepares to Draft Proposals for the Asian-African Conference
Author:
Date: 1955-03-15
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Jakarta, March 14th: The Joint Secretariat of the Asian-African Conference announced on the 12th that Indonesian Prime Minister Ali Sastroamidjojo has established an inter-ministerial political committee headed by Foreign Minister Sunarjo to draft Indonesia's proposals for the Asian-African Conference. The committee members include senior officials from various Indonesian government ministries.

### 印度尼西亚准备拟订在亚非会议上的建议

1955-03-15
第1版()
专栏：

    印度尼西亚准备拟订在亚非会议上的建议
    【新华社雅加达十四日电】亚非会议联合秘书处十二日宣布，印度尼西亚总理沙斯特罗阿米佐约已经成立了一个以外交部长苏纳约为首的政治部际委员会，以拟订印度尼西亚在亚非会议上提出的建议。这个委员会的委员包括印度尼西亚政府各部的高级官员。
