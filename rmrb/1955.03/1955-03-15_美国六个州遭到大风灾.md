Title: Six U.S. States Hit by Severe Windstorm
Author:
Date: 1955-03-15
Page: 4

　　Xinhua News Agency, New York, March 13th (TASS dispatch): United Press reports that on March 11th, six U.S. states – Kansas, Missouri, Arkansas, Texas, Colorado, and Wyoming – were hit by a severe dust storm. The dust raised by the storm reached a height of six kilometers. In Colorado, the storm destroyed approximately 600,000 hectares of crops, wiping out half of the state's total winter wheat. Crops in other states also suffered significant damage.

　　In the eastern United States, heavy rains caused the Ohio River to flood. There have been casualties in the flooded areas.

　　According to United Press, the disaster has caused hundreds of millions of dollars in damage in the United States, making it one of the largest natural disasters in U.S. history.

### 美国六个州遭到大风灾

1955-03-15
第4版()
专栏：

    美国六个州遭到大风灾
    【新华社十四日讯】塔斯社纽约十三日讯：合众社报道，在三月十一日，美国堪萨斯、密苏里、阿肯色、得克萨斯、科罗拉多和怀俄明六个州遭到了大风沙的袭击。暴风刮起的尘土高达六公里。在科罗拉多州，暴风摧毁了约六十万公顷的庄稼，把这个州全部田地里的冬麦毁了一半。其他各州的庄稼也遭到巨大的损害。
    在美国东部地区，暴雨使俄亥俄河泛滥。遭到水灾的地区有生命损失。
    据合众社报道，这次天灾在美国造成的损失达数亿美元，是美国有史以来最大的天灾之一。
