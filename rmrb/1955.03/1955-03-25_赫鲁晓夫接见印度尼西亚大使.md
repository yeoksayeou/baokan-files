Title: Khrushchev Receives Indonesian Ambassador
Author:
Date: 1955-03-25
Page: 4

　　Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, March 22nd: TASS Moscow, March 22nd: N.S. Khrushchev received Mr. Subandrio, Ambassador Extraordinary and Plenipotentiary of the Republic of Indonesia to the Soviet Union, on the 22nd.

### 赫鲁晓夫接见印度尼西亚大使

1955-03-25
第4版()
专栏：

    赫鲁晓夫接见印度尼西亚大使
    【新华社二十三日讯】塔斯社莫斯科二十  二日讯：尼·谢·赫鲁晓夫在二十二日接见了  印度尼西亚共和国驻苏联特命全权大使苏班德里奥先生。
