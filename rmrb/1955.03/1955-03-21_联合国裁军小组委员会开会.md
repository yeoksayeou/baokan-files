Title: UN Disarmament Subcommittee Meets
Author: 
Date: 1955-03-21
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, London, March 19th: TASS reports: According to an official communiqué, the United Nations Disarmament Commission's subcommittee held a meeting on March 18th, presided over by the French representative, Jules Moch. The next meeting is scheduled for March 22nd.

### 联合国裁军小组委员会开会

1955-03-21
第4版()
专栏：

    联合国裁军小组委员会开会
   【新华社二十日讯】塔斯社伦敦十九日讯：根据正式公报，联合国裁军委员会小组委员会三月十八日在法国代表儒勒·莫克主持下举行会议。下次会议定三月二十二日举行。
