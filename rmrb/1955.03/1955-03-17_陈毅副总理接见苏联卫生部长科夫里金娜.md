Title: Vice Premier Chen Yi Receives Soviet Minister of Health Kovrigina
Author:
Date: 1955-03-17
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, March 16th: Vice Premier Chen Yi of the State Council of the People's Republic of China received Soviet Minister of Health Kovrigina and the Minister of Health of the Uzbek Soviet Socialist Republic, Sagatov, who came to China for a visit at the invitation of the Chinese Ministry of Health, on the morning of the 16th.

Present at the reception were Li Dequan, Minister of Health of the People's Republic of China, He Cheng, Vice Minister, and Fan Changjiang, Deputy Director of the Second Office of the State Council.

### 陈毅副总理接见苏联卫生部长科夫里金娜

1955-03-17
第1版()
专栏：

    陈毅副总理接见苏联卫生部长科夫里金娜
    【新华社十六日讯】十六日上午，中华人民共和国国务院副总理陈毅接见了应我国卫生部邀请来我国访问的苏联卫生部长科夫里金娜和随同她前来访问的乌兹别克苏维埃社会主义共和国卫生部长萨加托夫。
    接见时在座的有：中华人民共和国卫生部长李德全、副部长贺诚，国务院第二办公室副主任范长江。
