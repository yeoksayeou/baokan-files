Title: Soviet Representative Sobolev Writes to UN Secretary-General Refuting the UN's "Report" Slandering China for Practicing "Slave Labor"
Author:
Date: 1955-03-28
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, New York, March 26th: TASS, New York, March 25th—Recently, the Soviet delegation to the United Nations received a report slandering the People's Republic of China, which was written by Hans Engen and published and distributed to all members of the Economic and Social Council as an official document under the instructions of the UN Secretary-General.

On March 23rd, the Permanent Representative of the Soviet Union to the United Nations, Sobolev, sent a letter to the UN Secretary-General, pointing out that the author of the aforementioned "report" attempted to create the impression that the People's Republic of China was practicing "slave labor." This is an intentional fabrication and a malicious slander against the great Chinese people.

Sobolev's letter stated: "Because the representatives of the Chinese People's Government have not been able, and cannot, obtain their legitimate seat in the United Nations and the Economic and Social Council due to American obstruction to defend China's interests in person at the United Nations, the Soviet delegation requests you, Mr. Secretary-General, to distribute this letter and the enclosed extracts from the Constitution of the People's Republic of China, adopted at the meeting of the First National People's Congress of China on September 20, 1954, as a United Nations document for the discussion of items 7 and 8 on the agenda of the nineteenth session of the Economic and Social Council. The fundamental law of the Chinese people, especially Articles 1, 2, 9, 16, 85, 86, 87, 89, 91, 92, 93, 94, and 96, which are the main legislative provisions concerning political and economic rights and guarantees for the free and creative labor of the Chinese people, are sufficient to refute the slanders included in the aforementioned Engen report."

### 苏联代表索波列夫写信给联合国秘书长  驳联合国诬蔑我实行“奴隶劳动”的“报告”

1955-03-28
第1版()
专栏：

    苏联代表索波列夫写信给联合国秘书长
    驳联合国诬蔑我实行“奴隶劳动”的“报告”
   【新华社二十六日讯】塔斯社纽约二十五日讯：不久以前，苏联驻联合国代表团接到一个诬蔑中华人民共和国的报告，这个报告是由汉斯·恩根编写的，并根据联合国秘书长的指示，作为官方文件发表并分发给经济及社会理事会的所有理事国。
    三月二十三日，苏联常驻联合国代表索波列夫给联合国秘书长一封信，信上指出，上述“报告”的作者企图造成这样一种印象，即中华人民共和国在实行“奴隶劳动”。这是有意造谣和对伟大的中国人民的恶毒诽谤。
    索波列夫的信里说：“因为中国人民政府的代表由于美国的阻挠而没有并且也不能在联合国和经济及社会理事会取得他们的合法席位来在联合国亲自维护中国的利益，苏联代表团要求你，秘书长先生，为了讨论经济及社会理事会第十九届会议议程上的第七项和第八项，把这封信和所附的一九五四年九月二十日中国第一届全国人民代表大会会议通过的中华人民共和国宪法的摘录作为联合国的文件分发。中国人民的根本法，特别是其中第一条、第二条、第九条、第十六条、第八十五条、第八十六条、第八十七条、第八十九条、第九十一条、第九十二条、第九十三条、第九十四条和第九十六条都是有关政治和经济权利的主要立法条款和对于中国人民的自由的创造性的劳动的保障，这就足以驳斥上述恩根的报告中所包括的诬蔑。”
