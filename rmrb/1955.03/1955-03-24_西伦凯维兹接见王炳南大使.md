Title: Cyrankiewicz Receives Ambassador Wang Bingnan
Author:
Date: 1955-03-24
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, Warsaw, March 23rd: Polish Chairman of the Council of Ministers, Cyrankiewicz, received Wang Bingnan, the newly appointed Chinese Ambassador Extraordinary and Plenipotentiary to Poland, on the 21st.

Xinhua News Agency, Warsaw, March 23rd: Wang Bingnan, the newly appointed Chinese Ambassador Extraordinary and Plenipotentiary to Poland, laid a wreath at the Tomb of the Unknown Soldier in Warsaw on the 21st.

### 西伦凯维兹接见王炳南大使

1955-03-24
第1版()
专栏：

    西伦凯维兹接见王炳南大使
    【新华社华沙二十三日电】波兰部长会议主席西伦凯维兹在二十一日接见我国新任驻波兰特命全权大使王炳南。
    【新华社华沙二十三日讯】我国新任驻波兰特命全权大使王炳南，在二十一日到华沙无名战士墓前献了花圈。
