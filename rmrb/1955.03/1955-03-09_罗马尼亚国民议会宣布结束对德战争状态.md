Title: Romanian National Assembly Announces End of State of War with Germany
Author:
Date: 1955-03-09
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Bucharest, March 8th: The Presidium of the National Assembly of the Romanian People's Republic, at its meeting on March 7th, discussed the proposal submitted by the Council of Ministers of the Romanian People's Republic to end the state of war between Romania and Germany. Groza, Chairman of the Presidium of the National Assembly, delivered a report on the issue at the meeting. The meeting passed the following decree:

	The state of war declared by Romania against Germany on August 24, 1944, is hereby terminated;

	The cessation of the state of war with Germany does not affect the mutual rights and obligations assumed by Romania and Germany under existing international agreements.

### 罗马尼亚国民议会宣布结束对德战争状态

1955-03-09
第4版()
专栏：

    罗马尼亚国民议会宣布结束对德战争状态
    【新华社八日讯】据罗马尼亚通讯社布加勒斯特讯：罗马尼亚人民共和国国民议会主席团在三月七日的会议上，讨论了罗马尼亚人民共和国部长会议提出的结束罗马尼亚同德国之间战争状态的建议。国民议会主席团主席格罗查就这个问题在会上作了报告，会议通过下列命令：
    结束罗马尼亚在一九四四年八月二十四日对德国宣布的战争状态；
    停止同德国的战争状态并不影响由于现行的国际协定而使罗马尼亚和德国承担的相互权利和义务。
