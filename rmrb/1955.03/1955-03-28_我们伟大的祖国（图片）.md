Title: Our Great Motherland (Picture)
Author:
Date: 1955-03-28
Page: 1

    Our Great Motherland
    With the development of animal husbandry production, the cultural and educational undertakings in the Kizilsu Kirghiz Autonomous Region of Xinjiang Province have also developed accordingly. Eighty-eight primary schools and one middle school have been established in the entire region. This is a scene of students from the Subashi Township Primary School in the Autonomous Region reviewing their lessons on the grassland.

### 我们伟大的祖国（图片）

1955-03-28
第1版()
专栏：

    我们伟大的祖国
    新疆省克孜勒苏柯尔克孜族自治区随着畜牧业生产的发展，文化教育事业也有了相应的发展。全区已建立了八十八所小学和一所中学。这是自治区苏巴什乡小学校的学生们正在草地上温课的情景。
