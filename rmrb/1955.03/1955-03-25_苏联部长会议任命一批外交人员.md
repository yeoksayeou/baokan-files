Title: Soviet Council of Ministers Appoints a Group of Diplomats
Author: 
Date: 1955-03-25
Page: 4

    (Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, March 22nd) TASS Moscow, March 22nd: The Soviet Council of Ministers has appointed V.V. Kuznetsov as First Deputy Minister of the Soviet Foreign Ministry.
    The Soviet Council of Ministers has appointed V.S. Semyonov and Fedrin as Deputy Ministers of the Soviet Foreign Ministry.

### 苏联部长会议任命一批外交人员

1955-03-25
第4版()
专栏：

    苏联部长会议任命一批外交人员
   【新华社二十四日讯】塔斯社莫斯科二十二日讯：苏联部长会议任命瓦·瓦·库兹涅佐夫为苏联外交部第一副部长。
    苏联部长会议任命弗·斯·谢苗诺夫和费德林为苏联外交部副部长。
