Author:
Date: 1955-03-17
Page: 1

Our Great Motherland
    The scale of construction on the northern section of the Baoji-Chengdu Railway this year is very large, and the workload for the whole year will be four times greater than last year. At present, the equipment needed for large-scale mechanized construction has been transported to the construction site, creating good conditions for comprehensive construction. This is a picture of workers testing the operation of the excavators and cranes that have just been transported to the construction site.

### 我们伟大的祖国(图片）

1955-03-17
第1版()
专栏：

    我们伟大的祖国
    宝成铁路北段今年施工的规模很大，全年的工作量要比去年大四倍。现在为大规模机械化施工所需要的器材都已运到了工地，给全面施工创造了良好的条件。这是工人们正在试验刚运到工地的挖掘机和吊车的运转情况。
