Title: China and the Soviet Union Express Concern for the Indonesian People Affected by Floods
Author:
Date: 1955-03-04
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Jakarta, March 3rd: Huang Zhen, the Chinese Ambassador to Indonesia, visited Indonesian Foreign Minister Sunario on the 2nd to express sympathy for the Indonesian people affected by the floods. He informed Sunario that the staff of the Chinese Embassy and Consulates in Indonesia had donated 40,000 rupiah to the Indonesian people affected by the floods.

Xinhua News Agency Dispatch: According to TASS, Jakarta: The Alliance of Red Cross and Red Crescent Societies of the Soviet Union has donated 400,000 Indonesian rupiah to relieve the victims of the recent floods in Indonesia. This was announced by D.A. Zhukov, the Soviet Ambassador to Indonesia, during a meeting with Indonesian Prime Minister Sastroamidjojo on February 23rd. Zhukov said that this amount would be handed over to the Indonesian National Bank. Zhukov also expressed the sympathy of the Soviet government to Sastroamidjojo for the losses suffered by certain regions of the Republic of Indonesia due to the floods.

Prime Minister Sastroamidjojo, on behalf of the Indonesian people and government, warmly thanked the Soviet people and government for their assistance to the flood victims.

Recently, Sumatra, Java, and Kalimantan in Indonesia have all experienced floods, causing significant losses to the people. In central Java, floods destroyed more than 11,000 houses, submerged hundreds of hectares of rice fields, and losses are estimated at 10 million rupiah. In West Java, widespread flooding has also occurred, leaving thousands of residents homeless. The floods in Sumatra are particularly severe. Floods have submerged seven cities and vast rice fields, with the Jambi region suffering the greatest losses. Out of 400,000 residents, 300,000 have been affected by the floods.

### 中苏两国关怀遭受水灾的印度尼西亚人民

1955-03-04
第4版()
专栏：

    中苏两国关怀遭受水灾的印度尼西亚人民
    【新华社雅加达三日电】中国驻印度尼西亚大使黄镇二日访问了印度尼西亚外交部长苏纳约，对遭受水灾的印度尼西亚人民表示同情。他通知苏纳约说，中国驻印度尼西亚大使馆和总领事馆的工作人员已经捐赠四万盾给遭受水灾的印度尼西亚人民。
    【新华社讯】据塔斯社雅加达讯：苏联红十字会和红新月会联合会捐助四十万盾印度尼西亚币，救济印度尼西亚最近遭受水灾的灾民。这是苏联驻印度尼西亚大使德·阿·茹科夫在二月二十三日拜会印度尼西亚总理沙斯特罗阿米佐约时宣布的。茹科夫说，这个款项将交给印度尼西亚国家银行。茹科夫也向沙斯特罗阿米佐约表达了苏联政府对于印度尼西亚共和国的若干地区发生水灾而受到损失的同情。
    沙斯特罗阿米佐约总理代表印度尼西亚人民和政府，热烈感谢苏联人民和政府对于水灾灾民的援助。
    印度尼西亚的苏门答腊、爪哇和加里曼丹最近都发生水灾，人民遭到了重大的损失。在爪哇岛中部，大水冲毁了一万一千所以上住房，淹没了几百公顷的稻田，损失估计有一千万盾。在西爪哇，广大地区也发生水灾，使数以千计的居民无家可归。苏门答腊的水灾特别严重。大水淹没了七个城市和大片稻田，占碑地区损失最大，四十万居民中，有三十万人遭到水灾。
