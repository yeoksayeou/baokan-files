Title: US Seventh Fleet Commander Pride Arrives in Taiwan, Steps Up Plans to Establish US-Chiang Joint Operations Agency
Author:
Date: 1955-03-24
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, March 23rd: The United States is accelerating its collaboration with the Chiang Kai-shek traitorous clique to plan the establishment of a joint operations agency, to strengthen the US military occupation of Taiwan and prepare for a new war.

US Seventh Fleet Commander Pride arrived in Taiwan on the 23rd. Reports say that Pride will discuss a plan to establish a joint operations center with the Chiang Kai-shek traitorous clique, and will continue to study the military needs of the Chiang Kai-shek traitorous clique. This discussion will be a continuation of the US-Chiang talks held in early March. After US Secretary of State Dulles, Chief of Naval Operations Carney, Pacific Fleet Commander Stump, and Pride participated in the US-Chiang talks in early March, negotiations on US-Chiang "joint defense" moved to the staff level to continue. According to reports, this negotiation is in the stage of discussing specific military details. Joint military negotiations related to the Navy will conclude with Pride's participation.

### 美国第七舰队司令普赖德到台湾  加紧筹划建立美蒋联合作战机构

1955-03-24
第1版()
专栏：

    美国第七舰队司令普赖德到台湾
    加紧筹划建立美蒋联合作战机构
    【新华社二十三日讯】美国正在加紧和蒋介石卖国集团筹划建立联合作战机构，来加强美国对台湾的军事占领和准备新战争。
    美国第七舰队司令普赖德二十三日到达台湾。消息说，普赖德将和蒋介石卖国集团讨论成立一个联合作战中心的计划，并且将继续研究蒋介石卖国集团的军事需要。这次讨论将是三月初举行的美蒋会谈的继续。三月初美国国务卿杜勒斯、海军作战部长卡湼、太平洋舰队司令斯图普和普赖德参加了美蒋会谈以后，有关美蒋“联防”的谈判移至参谋一级继续举行。据消息报道，这项谈判正处在讨论具体军事细节的阶段。同海军有关的联合军事谈判，将在普赖德的参加之下结束。
