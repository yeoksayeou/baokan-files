Title: Ba U Receives Chinese Agricultural Delegation
Author:
Date: 1955-03-28
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, Rangoon, March 27th: President Ba U of the Union of Burma received Liu Ruilong, head of the Chinese agricultural delegation visiting Burma at the invitation of the Burmese government, and all members of the delegation on March 25th.
    Yao Zhongming, Chinese Ambassador to Burma, was present at the reception.
    Xinhua News Agency, Rangoon, March 27th: The Chinese agricultural delegation, which is visiting Burma at the invitation of the Burmese government, has visited Mandalay, Taunggyi, Meiktila, Sagaing, Shwebo, and other places in the past two weeks, inspecting agriculture, water conservancy, and forest management. During the visit, the Chinese agricultural delegation received a warm welcome from local government agencies, agricultural and forestry departments, farmers' organizations, people from all walks of life, and overseas Chinese.

### 巴宇接见我国农业访问团

1955-03-28
第1版()
专栏：

    巴宇接见我国农业访问团
    【新华社二十七日讯】仰光消息：缅甸联邦总统巴宇在三月二十五日接见了应缅甸联邦政府邀请访问缅甸的中国农业访问团团长刘瑞龙和全体团员。
    陪同受接见的有中国驻缅甸大使姚仲明。
   【新华社二十七日讯】仰光消息：应缅甸联邦政府邀请访问缅甸的中国农业访问团两周来先后访问了曼德勒、东枝、密铁捞、实皆、梢埠等地，考察了各地的农业、水利及森林管理等。中国农业访问团在访问中，受到了各地政府机关、农林部门、农民团体和各界人士以及华侨的热烈欢迎。
