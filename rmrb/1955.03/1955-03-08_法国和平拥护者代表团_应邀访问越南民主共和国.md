Title: French Peace Advocate Delegation Invited to Visit Democratic Republic of Vietnam
Author:
Date: 1955-03-08
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency, Paris, March 5th: According to the "l'Humanité" newspaper, a delegation of French peace advocates has left for the Democratic Republic of Vietnam at the invitation of the Vietnamese Committee for the Defense of World Peace. The members of the delegation are Mrs. Yves Farge, member of the Presidium of the French National Peace Council, Jacques Mitterrand, member of the Council of the French Union, Mrs. Perrot, editor of "French Women" magazine, Bélugie, Socialist member of the Dordogne Provincial Council, and Leclère, a Catholic, among others. The delegation intends to learn about issues concerning the development of economic and cultural ties between France and the Democratic Republic of Vietnam.

### 法国和平拥护者代表团  应邀访问越南民主共和国

1955-03-08
第4版()
专栏：

    法国和平拥护者代表团
    应邀访问越南民主共和国
    【新华社六日讯】　塔斯社巴黎五日讯：据“人道报”消息，法国和平拥护者代表团应越南保卫世界和平委员会的邀请已经前往越南民主共和国。代表团的成员是法国全国和平理事会主席团委员伊夫·法奇夫人，法兰西联邦议会议员雅克·密特朗，“法国妇女”杂志编辑佩罗夫人，多顿涅省省议会社会党议员贝律吉和天主教徒勒克等人。代表团打算了解关于发展法国和越南民主共和国之间的经济和文化联系的问题。
