Title: A Batch of Overseas Chinese in Japan Return to China
Author:
Date: 1955-03-23
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, Tianjin, March 21st. The Japanese ship "Kouan Maru," which came to China to transport Japanese expatriates back to their country, arrived at Tanggu New Port on the 21st. Seventy-two overseas Chinese from Japan returned to their motherland aboard the "Kouan Maru."

Kudo Tadao, the Foreign Affairs Director of the Japanese Red Cross Society, who accompanied the overseas Chinese back to China, as well as Tsukahara Masashige, Hasegawa Toshizo, and Terakado Hiroshi, representatives from the Japanese Red Cross Society, the Japan-China Friendship Association, and the Japan Peace Liaison Committee, who came to China to receive Japanese expatriates, and eleven Japanese journalists accompanying them, arrived in Tianjin on the same day.

Li Yunke, President of the Tianjin Branch of the Chinese Red Cross Society, held a banquet that evening to welcome Kudo Tadao and others. Ni Feijun, Deputy Secretary-General of the Chinese Red Cross Society Headquarters, and Ji Feng, Director of the International Liaison Department, who came to Tianjin to welcome Kudo Tadao and others, attended the banquet.

### 我国旅日侨胞一批回国

1955-03-23
第1版()
专栏：

    我国旅日侨胞一批回国
    【新华社天津二十一日电】前来中国接运日本侨民回国的日本轮船“兴安丸”二十一日抵达塘沽新港。我国旅日侨胞七十二人乘“兴安丸”返回祖国。
    陪送我国侨民回国的日本红十字会外事部长工藤忠夫，来中国接日侨回国的日本红十字会、日中友好协会、日本和平联络会三团体的代表塚原政繁、长谷川敏三、寺门博以及随行的十一名日本新闻记者当天到达天津。
    中国红十字会天津市分会会长李允恪当晚举行宴会，欢迎工藤忠夫等人。前来天津欢迎工藤忠夫等人的中国红十字会总会副秘书长倪斐君、国际联络部部长纪锋都出席了宴会。
