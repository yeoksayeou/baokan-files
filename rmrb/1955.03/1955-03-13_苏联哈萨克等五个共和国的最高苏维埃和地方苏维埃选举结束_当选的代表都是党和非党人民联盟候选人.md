Title: The Supreme Soviets and Local Soviets Elections of the Soviet Union's Five Republics, including Kazakhstan, Have Concluded; All Elected Representatives are Candidates of the League of Party and Non-Party People

Author:
Date: 1955-03-13
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, March 11th: TASS reported from Moscow on March 10th that the results of the Supreme Soviet elections held on the 6th in the Soviet Union's Kazakh, Azerbaijani, Kyrgyz, Armenian, and Karelo-Finnish republics, as well as the Nakhichevan Autonomous Republic, have been announced.

    The elections in all regions were conducted in an atmosphere of high political enthusiasm among the working people. During the elections, 99.99% of the total number of voters in Kazakhstan, Azerbaijan, Kyrgyzstan, and Armenia participated. The proportions of voters in each republic who voted for candidates of the League of Communists and Non-Party People were 99.85%, 99.86%, 99.77%, and 99.86% respectively. In the Karelo-Finnish Republic, 99.97% of the total number of voters participated in the elections, and 99.44% of the participating voters voted for candidates of the League of Communists and Non-Party People. In the Nakhichevan Autonomous Republic, all voters participated in the elections, and 99.99% of the voters voted for candidates of the League of Communists and Non-Party People.

    Currently, the Central Election Commissions of each union republic and the Nakhichevan Autonomous Republic have registered the representatives elected from each constituency. All elected representatives are candidates of the League of Communists and Non-Party People.

Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, March 11th: TASS reported from Moscow on March 11th that the results of the elections for local Soviets at all levels, held on the 6th in the Soviet Union's five union republics—Kazakhstan, Azerbaijan, Kyrgyzstan, Armenia, and Karelo-Finnish—have been announced.

    The elections in all regions were conducted in an atmosphere of high political enthusiasm among the working people. During the elections, 99.96% to 99.99% of the total number of voters participated. Of the participating voters, 99.13% to 99.96% voted for candidates of the League of Communists and Non-Party People.

    All elected representatives are candidates of the League of Communists and Non-Party People.

### 苏联哈萨克等五个共和国的最高苏维埃和地方苏维埃选举结束  当选的代表都是党和非党人民联盟候选人

1955-03-13
第4版()
专栏：

    苏联哈萨克等五个共和国的最高苏维埃和地方苏维埃选举结束
    当选的代表都是党和非党人民联盟候选人
    【新华社十一日讯】　据塔斯社莫斯科十日讯：苏联的哈萨克、阿塞拜疆、吉尔吉斯、亚美尼亚和卡累利阿—芬兰等加盟共和国以及纳希切凡自治共和国在六日举行的最高苏维埃选举的结果已经公布。
    各地选举都是在劳动人民政治热情高涨的气氛中进行的。在选举中，哈萨克、阿塞拜疆、吉尔吉斯、亚美尼亚占选民总数百分之九十九点九九的选民都参加了选举，各共和国内投票选举共产党和非党人民联盟的候选人的选民在参加选举的选民中所占的比例分别为百分之九十九点八五、九十九点八六、九十九点七七和九十九点八六。在卡累利阿—芬兰共和国，占选民总数百分之九十九点九七的选民参加了选举，参加选举的选民中，百分之九十九点四四投票选举共产党和非党人民联盟的候选人。在纳希切凡自治共和国，全体选民都参加了选举，其中，百分之九十九点九九的选民投了共产党和非党人民联盟的候选人的票。
    现在，各加盟共和国和纳希切凡自治共和国的中央选举委员会已经登记了各选区所选出的代表。全体当选的代表都是共产党和非党人民联盟的候选人。
    【新华社十二日讯】　据塔斯社莫斯科十一日讯：苏联的哈萨克、阿塞拜疆、吉尔吉斯、亚美尼亚和卡累利阿—芬兰五个加盟共和国在六日举行的各级地方苏维埃选举的结果已经公布。
    各地的选举都是在劳动人民政治热情高涨的情况下举行的。在选举中，占选民总数百分之九十九点九六到九十九点九九的选民都参加了选举。参加选举的选民中，百分之九十九点一三到九十九点九六的选民都投票选举共产党和非党人民联盟的候选人。
    所有当选的代表都是共产党和非党人民联盟的候选人。
