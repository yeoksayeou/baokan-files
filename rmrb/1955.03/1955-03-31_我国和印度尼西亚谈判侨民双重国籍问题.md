Title: China and Indonesia Negotiate on the Issue of Dual Nationality for Overseas Chinese
Author: 
Date: 1955-03-31
Page: 1

    Xinhua News Agency, Jakarta, March 30th. Negotiations between the People's Republic of China and the Republic of Indonesia on the issue of dual nationality for overseas Chinese began in Jakarta on the 29th.
    The Indonesian delegation participating in the negotiations consisted of Su Wirjopranoto (Head of Delegation), Director of the Asia-Pacific Department of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs of the Republic of Indonesia, Suharjo, and Taib Napis.
    The Chinese delegation consisted of Huang Zhen (Head of Delegation), Ambassador of China to the Republic of Indonesia, Zhao Zhongshi, Consul General of China in Jakarta, and Lin Zhaonan.

### 我国和印度尼西亚谈判侨民双重国籍问题

1955-03-31
第1版()
专栏：

    我国和印度尼西亚谈判侨民双重国籍问题
    新华社雅加达三十日电　中华人民共和国和印度尼西亚共和国双方关于侨民双重国籍问题的谈判，二十九日在雅加达开始举行。
    参加谈判的印度尼西亚代表团由印度尼西亚共和国外交部亚洲—太平洋司司长苏·维约普拉诺托（团长）、苏哈佐和塔伊布·纳皮斯组成。
    中国代表团由中国驻印度尼西亚共和国大使黄镇（团长）、中国驻雅加达总领事赵仲时和林兆南组成。
