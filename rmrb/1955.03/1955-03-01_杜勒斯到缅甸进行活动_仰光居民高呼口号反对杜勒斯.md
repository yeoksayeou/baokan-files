Title: Dulles Conducts Activities in Burma; Rangoon Residents Shout Slogans Against Dulles
Author:
Date: 1955-03-01
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, 28th. Rangoon News: After the Bangkok Conference, U.S. Secretary of State Dulles arrived in Rangoon, the capital of Burma, on February 26th for a one-day visit.

In Rangoon, Dulles held talks with Burmese Prime Minister U Nu. A joint statement issued after the talks said that Dulles and U Nu "discussed a number of issues of common concern." They "exchanged views" on major international affairs concerning the Asian and Pacific regions.

When Dulles arrived in Rangoon on the 26th, Rangoon residents shouted slogans against Dulles in the streets. They shouted, "Don't try to drag us into the Southeast Asia Treaty Organization!"

### 杜勒斯到缅甸进行活动  仰光居民高呼口号反对杜勒斯

1955-03-01
第4版()
专栏：

    杜勒斯到缅甸进行活动
    仰光居民高呼口号反对杜勒斯
    【新华社二十八日讯】仰光消息：美国国务卿杜勒斯在曼谷会议结束以后，于二月二十六日到达缅甸首都仰光，进行了一天访问。
    杜勒斯在仰光，同缅甸总理吴努举行了会谈。会谈后发表的联合声明说，杜勒斯和吴努“讨论了若干共同有关的问题”。他们就有关亚洲和太平洋地区的重大国际事务“交换了意见”。
    在杜勒斯二十六日到达仰光时，仰光居民在大街上高呼反对杜勒斯的口号。他们高呼：“别打算拉我们参加东南亚条约组织！”
