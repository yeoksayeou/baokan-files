Title: Cambodian General Election Postponed to September
Author:
Date: 1955-03-18
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Hanoi, March 17th: A Phnom Penh dispatch reports that the Cambodian Royal Government announced on the 15th that the Cambodian general election, originally scheduled for April 17th, will be postponed to September 11th.

On the same day that the Cambodian Royal Government announced the postponement of the general election, the U.S. special envoy to South Vietnam, Collins, arrived in Phnom Penh.

According to Agence France-Presse, the content of the talks concerned the issue of the United States sending a military delegation to Cambodia.

### 柬埔寨普选推迟到九月举行

1955-03-18
第4版()
专栏：

    柬埔寨普选推迟到九月举行
    【新华社河内十七日电】据金边消息，柬埔寨王国政府在十五日宣布，原定在四月十七日举行的柬埔寨普选，将推延到九月十一日举行。
    就在柬埔寨王国政府宣布推迟普选的同一天，美国驻南越的特使柯林斯到达了金边。
    据法国新闻社报道，会谈的内容是关于美国派遣军事代表团到柬埔寨的问题。
