Title: Provincial Committees of the CPPCC in Guizhou, Zhejiang, Xinjiang and Other Provinces Hold Meetings
Author:
Date: 1955-03-07
Page: 3

Xinhua News Agency Report:

    The first plenary session of the First Guizhou Provincial Committee of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference (CPPCC), lasting five days, concluded on February 20th. The meeting heard and discussed the work report of the Guizhou Provincial People's Representative Conference Consultative Committee, the report on the transmission of the First Plenary Session of the Second National Committee of the CPPCC, and the report on carrying out the signature campaign against the use of atomic weapons. The attendees expressed their unanimous support for the declaration and resolutions of the First Plenary Session of the Second National Committee of the CPPCC and their adherence to the CPPCC Charter in their speeches at the meeting. The meeting passed a resolution expressing its determination to support the liberation of Taiwan and eliminate the Chiang Kai-shek traitorous clique with practical actions to do a good job in current work. All ninety members and over eighty non-voting delegates signed the "Letter to the People of the World."
    The meeting elected Shen Yunpu as Chairman of the First Guizhou Provincial Committee of the CPPCC, and Xie Xinhe, Tian Junliang, Chen Tie, Wang Jialie, and Shuang Qing as Vice-Chairmen.

    The first plenary session of the First Zhejiang Provincial Committee of the CPPCC, which opened in Hangzhou on February 22nd, concluded on the 26th.
    After hearing relevant reports, the meeting discussed the work of the Zhejiang Provincial People's Representative Conference Consultative Committee in the past and the current situation. Thirty-seven members spoke at the meeting, and twenty-six members submitted written speeches. The members unanimously agreed that the Zhejiang Provincial People's Representative Conference Consultative Committee had made achievements in its various tasks under the leadership of the Chinese Communist Party and Chairman Mao in the past five years; in the future, it should unite more closely around the Chinese Communist Party and continue to work hard to successfully complete its tasks. On the morning of February 25th, all the members attending the meeting signed the "Letter to the People of the World."
    At the end of the meeting, Jiang Hua was elected as Chairman of the First Zhejiang Provincial Committee of the CPPCC, and Yang Si Yi, He Xiehou, Lin Feng, Song Yunbin, Tang Yuanbing, and Yu Ji Yi were elected as Vice-Chairmen.

    The first plenary session of the First Xinjiang Provincial Committee of the CPPCC was held in Urumqi from February 22nd to 26th. The meeting elected Saifuddin as Chairman of the First Xinjiang Provincial Committee of the CPPCC, and Lü Jianren (Han), Jiaheda (Kazak), Muhammatjan Mahsum (Uyghur), Dashap (Mongolian), Yu Zhanlin (Hui), and Mamet Aisha (Kyrgyz) as Vice-Chairmen, and also elected standing committee members and a secretary-general.
    During the meeting, Wang Enmao, First Secretary of the Xinjiang Sub-Bureau of the CPC Central Committee, delivered a speech. Burhan, Vice-Chairman of the Second National Committee of the CPPCC, gave a report on the transmission of the First Plenary Session of the Second National Committee of the CPPCC. All members also heard a work report on the Xinjiang Provincial People's Consultative Committee of All Ethnic Groups in the past. In the discussions, the members unanimously condemned the crimes of U.S. imperialism in occupying our territory Taiwan, creating world tensions, and clamoring for atomic warfare, and firmly stated that they would further mobilize and unite the people of all ethnic groups and all circles in Xinjiang, strengthen socialist construction and socialist transformation, and fight for the liberation of our territory Taiwan and the defense of peace in the Far East and the world.
    At the meeting, all members unanimously expressed their support for the state's measures to issue new Renminbi and withdraw the current old Renminbi, and called on the people of all ethnic groups and all circles to assist the government in doing this work well.

### 政协贵州浙江新疆等省委员会举行会议

1955-03-07
第3版()
专栏：

    政协贵州浙江新疆等省委员会举行会议
    中国人民政治协商会议第一届贵州省委员会第一次全体会议，历时五天已在二月二十日闭幕。会议听了和讨论了关于贵州省各族各界人民代表会议协商委员会的工作报告，中国人民政治协商会议第二届全国委员会第一次全体会议的传达报告，和开展反对使用原子武器签名运动的报告。到会人员在大会发言中，一致表示拥护中国人民政治协商会议第二届全国委员会第一次全体会议的宣言、决议，遵守中国人民政治协商会议章程。会议通过一项决议，表示要以做好当前各项工作的实际行动来支援解放台湾，消灭蒋介石卖国集团。到会的九十名委员和八十多名列席代表都在“告全世界人民书”上签了名。
    会议选举了申云浦为中国人民政治协商会议第一届贵州省委员会主席，谢鑫鹤、田君亮、陈铁、王家烈、双清为副主席。　（据新华社讯）
    二月二十二日在杭州市召开的中国人民政治协商会议第一届浙江省委员会第一次全体会议，到二十六日结束。
    会议听了有关各项报告以后，就过去浙江省各界人民代表会议协商委员会的工作和目前形势进行了讨论，有三十七位委员在大会上发言，二十六位委员书面发言。委员们一致认为：浙江省各界人民代表会议协商委员会过去五年来在中国共产党和毛主席的领导下，进行的各项工作是有成绩的；今后更要团结在中国共产党周围，为胜利完成自己的任务而继续努力。二十五日上午，出席会议的全体委员在“告全世界人民书”上签了名。
    会议最后选举了江华为中国人民政治协商会议第一届浙江省委员会主席，杨思一、何燮侯、林枫、宋云彬、汤元炳、余纪一为副主席。　（据新华社讯）
    中国人民政治协商会议第一届新疆省委员会第一次全体会议，二月二十二日到二十六日在乌鲁木齐市举行。会议选出了赛福鼎为中国人民政治协商会议第一届新疆省委员会主席，吕剑人（汉族）、贾和达（哈萨克族）、木汗买提江·马合苏木（维吾尔族）、达夏甫（蒙古族）、禹占林（回族）、买买提艾沙（柯尔克兹族）等为副主席，并选出了常务委员和秘书长。
    会议期间，中共中央新疆分局第一书记王恩茂曾到会讲话；中国人民政治协商会议第二届全国委员会副主席鲍尔汉，在会上作了关于中国人民政治协商会议第二届全国委员会第一次全体会议的传达报告；全体委员还听了关于过去新疆省各族各界人民协商委员会的工作报告。委员们在会议讨论中，一致斥责了美帝国主义侵占我国领土台湾、制造世界紧张局势和叫嚣原子战争的罪行，坚决表示要进一步动员和团结全新疆各族各界人民，加强社会主义建设和社会主义改造工作，为解放我国领土台湾和保卫远东及世界和平而斗争。
    会上，全体委员对国家发行新人民币和收回现行旧人民币的措施一致表示拥护，并号召各族各界人民协助政府做好这一工作。　（据新华社讯）
