Title: Beirut Receives Ambassador Wang Bingnan
Author:
Date: 1955-03-25
Page: 1
    Beirut Receives Ambassador Wang Bingnan
    Wang Bingnan Visits Polish Minister of Foreign Trade and Others
    Xinhua News Agency, Warsaw, March 24th: The First Secretary of the Central Committee of the Polish United Workers' Party, Bolesław Bierut, received the newly appointed Ambassador Extraordinary and Plenipotentiary of the People's Republic of China to Poland, Wang Bingnan, on the 23rd.
    Xinhua News Agency, Warsaw, March 24th: The newly appointed Ambassador Extraordinary and Plenipotentiary of the People's Republic of China to Poland, Wang Bingnan, visited the Minister of Foreign Trade of Poland, Konstanty Dąbrowski, on the 22nd.
    Xinhua News Agency, Warsaw, March 24th: The newly appointed Ambassador Extraordinary and Plenipotentiary of the People's Republic of China to Poland, Wang Bingnan, visited the Deputy Minister of Foreign Affairs of Poland, Marian Naszkowski, on the 22nd.

### 贝鲁特接见王炳南大使

1955-03-25
第1版()
专栏：

    贝鲁特接见王炳南大使
    王炳南拜访波兰对外贸易部长等人
    【新华社华沙二十四日电】波兰统一工人党中央委员会第一书记波·贝鲁特，在二十三日接见了中华人民共和国新任驻波兰特命全权大使王炳南。
    【新华社华沙二十四日讯】中华人民共和国新任驻波兰特命全权大使王炳南，在二十二日拜访了波兰对外贸易部部长康·董布罗夫斯基。
   【新华社华沙二十四日电】中华人民共和国新任驻波兰特命全权大使王炳南，在二十二日拜访了波兰外交部副部长马·纳斯科夫斯基。
