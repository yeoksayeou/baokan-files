Title: Czechoslovak Ambassador to China, Mr. Kang Shala, Recalled Home

Author:

Date: 1955-03-07
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, March 6th: Czechoslovak Ambassador Extraordinary and Plenipotentiary to China, Mr. Kang Shala, was recalled and left Beijing for home on the 6th. Assistant Minister of Foreign Affairs He Wei and Director of the Protocol Department Ke Hua went to the station to see him off.

Diplomatic envoys and embassy staff from various countries to China, as well as all members of the Czechoslovak Embassy in China, also went to see him off.

### 捷驻华大使康萨拉奉调回国

1955-03-07
第1版()
专栏：

    捷驻华大使康萨拉奉调回国
    【新华社六日讯】捷克斯洛伐克共和国驻华特命全权大使康萨拉奉调在六日离北京回国。前往车站送行的有我外交部部长助理何伟、礼宾司司长柯华。
    各国驻华使节、使馆人员和捷克斯洛伐克驻华大使馆全体馆员也前往送行。
