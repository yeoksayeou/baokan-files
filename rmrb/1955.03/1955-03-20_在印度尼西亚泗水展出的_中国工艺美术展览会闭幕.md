Title: Chinese Arts and Crafts Exhibition in Surabaya, Indonesia Closes

Author:
Date: 1955-03-20
Page: 1

    Xinhua News Agency, Jakarta, 19th. The Chinese Arts and Crafts Exhibition in Surabaya, Indonesia, closed on the 13th. The exhibition will continue in Medan soon.
    The exhibition aroused great interest from the public in Surabaya. During the ten days of the exhibition, a total of 130,000 people visited.

### 在印度尼西亚泗水展出的  中国工艺美术展览会闭幕

1955-03-20
第1版()
专栏：

    在印度尼西亚泗水展出的
    中国工艺美术展览会闭幕
    【新华社雅加达十九日讯】在印度尼西亚泗水展出的中国工艺美术展览会已经在十三日闭幕。展览会即将在棉兰继续展出。
    展览会引起了泗水公众很大的兴趣。在展出的十天期间，一共有十三万人前往参观。
