Title: Death of Marshal Govorov, Deputy Minister of Defense of the Soviet Union
Author:
Date: 1955-03-21
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, March 20th: The Central Committee of the Communist Party of the Soviet Union and the Council of Ministers of the Soviet Union issued an announcement on the 19th, stating: The Central Committee of the Communist Party of the Soviet Union and the Council of Ministers of the Soviet Union announce with deep sorrow that Comrade Leonid Alexandrovich Govorov , Alternate Member of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of the Soviet Union, Deputy of the Supreme Soviet of the Soviet Union, Deputy Minister of Defense of the Soviet Union, one of the outstanding generals and builders of the Soviet Armed Forces, a famous hero of the Great Patriotic War, and Marshal of the Soviet Union, passed away on March 19th after a long and serious illness. Govorov's funeral will be held in Red Square.
    The Council of Ministers of the Soviet Union has established a funeral committee for Govorov, chaired by Marshal V.D. Sokolovsky of the Soviet Union.
    According to the announcement of the Govorov Funeral Committee, Govorov's coffin will be placed in the Hall of Columns of the House of Trade Unions. In order to allow the public to bid farewell to Govorov, the Hall of Columns of the House of Trade Unions will be open from 11:00 am on March 21st.

### 苏联国防部副部长戈沃罗夫元帅逝世

1955-03-21
第4版()
专栏：

    苏联国防部副部长戈沃罗夫元帅逝世
   【新华社二十日讯】据塔斯社莫斯科讯：苏联共产党中央委员会和苏联部长会议十九日发表公告说：苏联共产党中央委员会和苏联部长会议沉痛地宣告：苏联共产党中央委员会候补委员、苏联最高苏维埃代表、苏联国防部副部长、苏联武装部队杰出的将领和建设者之一、伟大卫国战争中的著名英雄、苏联元帅列昂尼德·亚历山德罗维奇·戈沃罗夫，经过长期重病后，于三月十九日逝世。戈沃罗夫的葬礼将在红场举行。
    苏联部长会议已经成立了戈沃罗夫治丧委员会，由苏联元帅瓦·德·索科洛夫斯基担任主席。
    据戈沃罗夫治丧委员会的公告，戈沃罗夫的灵柩将停放在工会大厦圆柱大厅。为了让公众向戈沃罗夫告别，工会大厦圆柱大厅将在三月二十一日上午十一时起开放。
