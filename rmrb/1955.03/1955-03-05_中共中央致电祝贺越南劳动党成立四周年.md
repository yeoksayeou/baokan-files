Title: CPC Central Committee Sends Telegram of Congratulations on the Fourth Anniversary of the Founding of the Vietnam Workers' Party
Author:
Date: 1955-03-05
Page: 1

The CPC Central Committee Sends Telegram of Congratulations on the Fourth Anniversary of the Founding of the Vietnam Workers' Party

[Report from this newspaper] The Central Committee of the Communist Party of China sent a telegram to the Central Committee of the Vietnam Workers' Party, congratulating the Vietnam Workers' Party on its fourth anniversary. The telegram reads as follows:
To the Central Committee of the Vietnam Workers' Party:

    On the occasion of the fourth anniversary of the Vietnam Workers' Party, the Central Committee of the Communist Party of China extends warm congratulations, congratulating the Vietnam Workers' Party for the great victories it has achieved in leading the Vietnamese people in the struggle for Vietnam's independence and peace, and wishing the Vietnam Workers' Party further achievements in consolidating peace in Vietnam, striving for Vietnam's reunification, and striving for the restoration and construction of the national economy.

    The Central Committee of the Communist Party of China
    March 3, 1955

### 中共中央致电祝贺越南劳动党成立四周年

1955-03-05
第1版()
专栏：

    中共中央致电祝贺越南劳动党成立四周年
    【本报讯】　中国共产党中央委员会致电越南劳动党中央委员会，祝贺越南劳动党成立四周年。电文如下：越南劳动党中央委员会：
    值此越南劳动党四周年纪念日，中国共产党中央委员会谨致以热烈祝贺，祝贺越南劳动党领导越南人民在为越南独立与和平的斗争中所已取得的伟大的胜利，并祝越南劳动党在巩固越南和平、争取越南统一、争取国家经济的恢复与建设中获得进一步的成就。
    中国共产党中央委员会
    一九五五年三月三日
