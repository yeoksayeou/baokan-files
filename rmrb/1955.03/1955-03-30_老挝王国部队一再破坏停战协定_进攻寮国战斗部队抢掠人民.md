Title: Lao Kingdom Forces Repeatedly Violate Ceasefire Agreement, Attacking Lao Fighting Units and Looting the People
Author:
Date: 1955-03-30
Page: 4

    Xinhua News Agency, Hanoi, March 29th: According to Lao sources, more than 200 soldiers of the Lao Kingdom government army again launched an attack on the area south of Na Xing, southeast of Muong Bun, on the 24th. They attacked Lao fighting units and looted the people's property. The Lao fighting units were forced to defend themselves. The invaders did not achieve their aims and were forced to retreat to Ban Kang. On the same day at noon, the Kingdom government army once again attacked the Lao fighting units, but after being defeated, they were again forced to retreat. According to reports, on the same day, an airplane also airdropped fourteen parachutes in Muong Bun.

### 老挝王国部队一再破坏停战协定  进攻寮国战斗部队抢掠人民

1955-03-30
第4版()
专栏：

    老挝王国部队一再破坏停战协定
    进攻寮国战斗部队抢掠人民
    【新华社河内二十九日电】据老挝消息说，老挝王国政府指挥下的军队两百多人，又在二十四日从芒奔向芒奔东南的那馨以南地区进攻，他们攻击寮国战斗部队和抢掠人民的财产。寮国战斗部队被迫自卫，进犯者未达到他们的目的，被迫撤回本康。同日中午，王国政府军队再次向寮国战斗部队进犯，但在遭到失败后又被迫撤退了。据消息说，在同一天，又有飞机一架在芒奔空投了十四个降落伞。
