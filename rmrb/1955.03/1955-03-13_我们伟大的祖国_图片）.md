Our Great Motherland
The broad masses of young people across the country actively responded to the state's decision to recruit supplementary soldiers and enthusiastically signed up for enlistment. These young men who have enlisted in the army have already put on their military uniforms and pinned on their badges; they deeply feel the honor and pride of becoming a soldier of the National Defense Force.

### 我们伟大的祖国(图片）

1955-03-13
第1版()
专栏：

    我们伟大的祖国
    全国各地的广大青年积极响应了国家征集补充兵员的决定，踊跃报名应征。这几位应征入伍的青年已经穿上军服，缀上胸章；他们深深感觉到成为一个国防军战士的荣誉和骄傲。
