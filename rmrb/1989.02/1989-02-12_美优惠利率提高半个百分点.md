Title: U.S. Prime Rate Rises by Half a Percentage Point
Author:
Date: 1989-02-12
Edition: 6

Several major U.S. banks raised their prime lending rate from 10.5 percent to 11 percent on the 10th, reaching the highest level since the second half of 1984.

This is the first time this year that the U.S. has raised its prime lending rate. On November 28 last year, major U.S. commercial banks had raised the prime lending rate from 10 percent to 10.5 percent.

The consecutive increases in the prime lending rate by U.S. banks are a result of the Federal Reserve's tight monetary policy aimed at curbing inflation.

### 美优惠利率提高半个百分点

1989-02-12
第6版(国际)
专栏：国际新闻简报

　　美优惠利率提高半个百分点
    美国几家大银行十日将优惠利率从百分之十点五提高到百分之十一，为一九八四年下半年以来的最高水平。
    这是今年以来美国第一次提高优惠利率。去年十一月二十八日，美国各大商业银行曾将优惠利率由百分之十提到百分之十点五。
    美国银行接连提高优惠利率，是美国联邦储备委员会为防通货膨胀而奉行紧缩货币政策所致。　　　　
　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　（新华社）
