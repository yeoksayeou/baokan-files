Title: Japan Develops a 2.5 Mach Large Passenger Aircraft
Author:
Date: 1989-02-22
Edition: 3

Japan's aerospace industry association proposed a development plan for the next generation of supersonic passenger aircraft on February 15 in Nagoya. The plan suggests that by the early 21st century, it will be possible to produce a supersonic aircraft with a cruising speed of 2.5 Mach (2,700 kilometers per hour), capable of carrying 300 passengers, and with a range of 10,000 to 14,000 kilometers. The speed of this aircraft is three times that of Boeing aircraft. The fuselage is approximately 100 meters long, the wing area is twice that of Boeing aircraft, and the weight is roughly the same as Boeing aircraft, at about 330 tons.

The plan suggests that due to the enormous development costs, international cooperation will be necessary.

### 日本研制二点五马赫大型客机  计划在二十一世纪初完成

1989-02-22
第3版(国际新闻)
专栏：

　　日本研制二点五马赫大型客机
    计划在二十一世纪初完成
    本报讯  日本航空宇宙工业会二月十五日在名古屋提出一项下一代超音速客机的开发计划。这一计划认为，在二十一世纪初，可以具备制造巡航速度二点五马赫（时速二千七百公里）、载员三百人、续航距离为一万至一点四万公里的超音速客机的技术。这种客机的速度是波音飞机的三倍，机体长约一百米，机翼面积是波音飞机的二倍，机重与波音飞机大体相同，约为三百三十吨。
    该计划认为，由于开发用费巨大，必须实行国际共同开发。
