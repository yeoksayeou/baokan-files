Title: French President Arrives in India for a Visit
Author:
Date: 1989-02-02
Edition: 6

French President Mitterrand arrived in New Delhi on February 1, beginning a four-day state visit to India.

Mitterrand told reporters that he would hold talks with Indian leaders on issues of peace and development.

According to an Indian Foreign Ministry spokesman, it is expected that Mitterrand will discuss current international issues with Prime Minister Rajiv Gandhi, including the latest situation in Cambodia, disarmament, the Middle East, and North-South dialogue. They will also discuss other international political and economic issues.

### 法国总统抵达印度访问

1989-02-02
第6版(国际)
专栏：国际新闻简报

　　法国总统抵达印度访问
    法国总统密特朗２月１日抵达新德里，开始对印度进行为期四天的国事访问。
    密特朗对记者说，他将同印度领导人就和平与发展问题举行会谈。
    据印度外交部发言人说，预计，密特朗将同拉·甘地总理讨论当前的一些国际问题，其中包括柬埔寨最新局势、裁军、西亚和南北对话等问题，还将讨论其他国际政治和经济问题。
