Title: Sociologists and Economists Issue "Warnings to Society"
Author: Feng Yuan
Date: 1989-02-16
Edition: 3

Some sociologists and economists, after reflecting on the 40 years of New China and the 10 years of reform, have each issued "warnings to society." The sister books "Sociologists' Warnings to Society" and "Economists' Warnings to Society" will soon be published by China Women's Publishing House and Academia Publishing House, respectively.

"Sociologists' Warnings to Society" explores various social issues related to economic development and social management—political system reform, media supervision, and coordinated economic and social development, among others.

"Economists' Warnings to Society" describes China's current economic situation and reform trends from different perspectives, such as industrial structure and economic system.

### 社会学和经济学学者发出“对社会的警告”  两套反思改革的丛书即将出版
冯媛
1989-02-16
第3版(科教·文化·体育)
专栏：

　　社会学和经济学学者发出“对社会的警告”    
    两套反思改革的丛书即将出版
    本报讯  一些社会学者和经济学者在反思新中国４０年和改革１０年以后，各自提出了“对社会的警告”。《社会学者对社会的警告》和《经济学者对社会的警告》作为姐妹丛书，最近将分别由中国妇女出版社和学苑出版社推出。
    《社会学者对社会的警告》探讨了与经济发展和社会管理有关的各种社会问题——政治体制改革、新闻舆论监督、经济社会协调发展等。
    《经济学者对社会的警告》则从产业结构、经济体制等不同层面描述了中国当前的经济状况和改革态势。
　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　（冯媛）
