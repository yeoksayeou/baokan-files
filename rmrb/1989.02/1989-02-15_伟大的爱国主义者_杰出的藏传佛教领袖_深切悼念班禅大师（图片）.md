Edition: 4

Great Patriot and Outstanding Leader of Tibetan Buddhism

— Deeply Mourn the Passing of the Panchen Erdeni

1. In 1952, the Panchen Erdeni (right) sat on the throne and chanted scriptures at the Tashilhunpo Monastery in Shigatse, Tibet.
2. On April 22, 1959, Panchen Erdeni Qoi Gyainzain spoke at the Second Session of the Second National People's Congress.
3. In 1982, the Panchen Erdeni visited Tibet and offered a khata to the statue of Songtsen Gampo at the Jokhang Temple in Lhasa.
4. During the consecration ceremony of the Panchen Eastern Tomb in Xiazhenan Jie, the Panchen Erdeni had a cordial conversation with Hu Jintao, the newly appointed Secretary of the Tibet Autonomous Region Party Committee.
5. In October 1988, Panchen Erdeni Qoi Gyainzain, Vice Chairman, led the Chinese NPC delegation to visit Bolivia. Local residents presented the Panchen Erdeni with national characteristic shawls and woolen hats.
6. In 1985, Vice Chairman Panchen propagated the Party's ethnic and religious policies to the masses in Shigatse, Tibet.

### 伟大的爱国主义者　杰出的藏传佛教领袖——深切悼念班禅大师（图片）

1989-02-15
第4版(政治·法律·社会)
专栏：

　　伟大的爱国主义者　杰出的藏传佛教领袖    
    ——深切悼念班禅大师
    ⒈１９５２年，班禅大师（右）在西藏日喀则市扎什伦布寺坐床诵经。
    ⒉１９５９年４月２２日，班禅额尔德尼·确吉坚赞在全国人大二届一次会议上发言。
    ⒊１９８２年，班禅大师在西藏视察时，在拉萨大昭寺向松赞干布的塑像献哈达。
    ⒋班禅大师主持班禅东陵扎什南捷开光典礼期间与新任中共西藏自治区区委书记胡锦涛亲切交谈。
    ⒌１９８８年１０月，班禅额尔德尼·确吉坚赞副委员长率中国人大代表团访问玻利维亚。当地居民向班禅赠送了具有民族特色的披肩、绒线帽等。
    ⒍１９８５年，班禅副委员长在西藏日喀则市向群众宣传党的民族政策和宗教政策。
