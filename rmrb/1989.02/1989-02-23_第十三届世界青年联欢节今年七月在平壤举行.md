Title: The 13th World Youth Festival to be Held in Pyongyang This July
Author: Xú Bǎokāng
Date: 1989-02-23
Edition: 3

The 13th World Youth Festival will be held in Pyongyang this July. Chairman Choe Ryong-hae of the Korean International Youth Festival Preparatory Committee said in his speech at the opening ceremony of the committee today that this festival will be a good stage for the youth of the world to strengthen solidarity, cooperation, and to seek understanding and trust. He expressed his expectation to meet with youth representatives from all over the world and South Korean students in Pyongyang.

Choe Ryong-hae said that holding the 13th World Youth Festival in Pyongyang is the greatest joy for the youth of Korea. The Korean youth will enthusiastically welcome young people from all over the world and provide them with all necessary conveniences.

It is reported that this festival will have youth organizations and representatives from 140 countries and regions participating. Under the banner of "anti-imperialism, peace, and friendship," various meetings and discussion sessions will be held to study and discuss a series of problems that youth face in the current international situation. Various exhibitions and rich and colorful art performances and sports activities will also be held.

The Standing Committee of the International Preparatory Committee consists of representatives from 8 international youth organizations and 32 countries. They will specifically divide the work and formulate the implementation plan for the festival.

### 第十三届世界青年联欢节今年七月在平壤举行
徐宝康
1989-02-23
第3版(国际新闻)
专栏：

　　第十三届世界青年联欢节
    今年七月在平壤举行
    本报平壤２月２０日电  记者徐宝康报道：第１３届世界青年联欢节将于今年７月在平壤举行。朝鲜国际青年节筹委会委员长崔龙海今天在筹委会开馆仪式上致词说，这届联欢节将成为世界青年加强团结、合作、谋求了解和信赖的良好舞台，并期待同世界所有青年和南朝鲜青年学生代表在平壤相逢。
    崔龙海说，第１３届世界青年联欢节在平壤举行，这是朝鲜青年的最大喜事。朝鲜青年将热情迎接世界各国青年，并为他们提供一切方便条件。
    据透露，这届联欢节，世界将有１４０个国家和地区的青年组织和代表参加，联欢节在“反帝、和平、友谊”的旗帜下，将举办各种集会、讨论会，研究讨论目前国际形势下，青年所面临的一系列问题，并举办各种展览会和丰富多采的文艺演出及体育活动。
    国际筹委会常委会由世界８个国际青年组织和３２个国家的青年代表组成，他们将具体分工，并制订联欢节实施计划。
