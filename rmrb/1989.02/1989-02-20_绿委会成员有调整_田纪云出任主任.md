Title: Adjustments Made to the Members of the National Greening Committee; Dian Jiyun Appointed as Director
Author:
Date: 1989-02-20
Edition: 1

Recently, the State Council, in the course of its institutional reforms, decided to retain the National Greening Committee. Due to personnel changes resulting from the institutional reforms, adjustments have been made to the composition of the National Greening Committee. After the adjustments, Vice Premier Dian Jiyun will serve as the Director.

The five Deputy Directors are: Gao Dezhan, Luogan, Zhao Nanqi, Lin Hanxiong, and Yong Wentao.

### 绿委会成员有调整  田纪云出任主任

1989-02-20
第1版(要闻)
专栏：

　　绿委会成员有调整
    田纪云出任主任
    新华社北京２月１９日电  最近，国务院在机构改革中，决定保留全国绿化委员会，由于机构改革后人事变动，对全国绿化委员会组成人员作了调整，调整后由国务院副总理田纪云任主任。
    五位副主任是，高德占、罗干、赵南起、林汉雄、雍文涛。
