Title: 33 People Donate Blood and Skin for a Female Worker
Author:
Date: 1989-02-27
Edition: 2

According to Xinhua News Agency in Shenyang, a rubberizing machine caught fire on January 24 at the Fourth Factory of the Qingyang Chemical Plant in Liaoyang City. A female worker was severely burned and required urgent blood transfusions and skin grafts for her rescue. Based on medical examinations and the patient's needs, four young workers donated blood, and 29 young workers underwent skin graft surgeries. The severely burned female worker was eventually saved.

### ３３人为一女工献血献皮

1989-02-27
第2版(国内新闻)
专栏：四面八方

　　３３人为一女工献血献皮
    据新华社沈阳电  辽阳市庆阳化工厂四分厂一台胶化机１月２４日着火，一女工被烧成重伤，抢救时急需输血、植皮。根据体检情况和伤者需要，有４名青年职工输血，２９名青年职工做了取皮手术。这名被严重烧伤的女工终于得救。
