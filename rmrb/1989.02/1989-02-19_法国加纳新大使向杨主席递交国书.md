Title: New Ambassadors from France and Ghana Present Credentials to Chairman Yang
Author:
Date: 1989-02-19
Edition: 4

The new ambassadors from France and Ghana presented their credentials to Chairman Yang Shangkun today.

According to Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, February 18: The new French Ambassador to China, Marc, and the new Ghanaian Ambassador to China, Jonas Awuku Afari, presented their credentials to Chairman Yang Shangkun at the Great Hall of the People this morning.

### 法国加纳新大使向杨主席递交国书

1989-02-19
第4版(政治·法律·社会)
专栏：

　　法国加纳新大使向杨主席递交国书
    新华社北京２月１８日电  法国新任驻华大使马乐、加纳新任驻华大使乔纳斯·阿武库·阿法里今天上午在人民大会堂分别向国家主席杨尚昆递交了国书。
