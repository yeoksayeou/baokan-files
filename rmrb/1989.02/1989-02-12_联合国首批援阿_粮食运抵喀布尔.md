Title: The First Batch of UN Aid to Afghanistan Arrives in Kabul
Author: Mă Guāng Gě Xiāng Wén
Date: 1989-02-12
Edition: 6

The first batch of grain aid provided by the United Nations humanitarian organization to Afghanistan arrived in Kabul this evening.

The first batch of aid, consisting of 29.5 tons of wheat, was transported by a Boeing 707 aircraft operated by Ethiopian Airlines.

### 联合国首批援阿  粮食运抵喀布尔
马光　葛相文
1989-02-12
第6版(国际)
专栏：

　　联合国首批援阿
    粮食运抵喀布尔
    据新华社喀布尔２月１０日电  （记者马光、葛相文）由联合国人道主义组织向阿富汗提供的第一批粮食今天晚上运抵喀布尔。
    首批援助的２９．５吨小麦是由埃塞俄比亚航空公司一架波音７０７飞机运到这里的。
