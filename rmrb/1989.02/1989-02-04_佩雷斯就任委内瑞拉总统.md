Title: Carlos Andres Perez Assumes Presidency of Venezuela
Author: ZHU Manting
Date: 1989-02-04
Edition: 6

Carlos Andres Perez today took the oath of office as the President of Venezuela, beginning his leadership of the 7th democratically elected government in this South American country. Perez was elected in December last year.

Heads of state and government leaders from 26 countries in Latin America and the Caribbean, as well as Spain and Portugal, were invited to attend. Among them was Fidel Castro, the Chairman of Cuba, who made his first visit in over 30 years. The United States sent a delegation led by Vice President Quayle. The representative of the Chinese government, Yang Taifang, Minister of Posts and Telecommunications, attended the inauguration ceremony. After taking the oath, Perez delivered a lengthy speech. In discussing the international situation, he said that humanity is now living in an era of great change. He called on the people of Latin America to jointly face the challenges of resolving the Central American problem. Regarding U.S.-Latin American relations, Perez urged the new U.S. administration to treat Latin American countries as friends who can support each other, hoping to develop cooperation between Venezuela and the United States. He called for joint efforts to thoroughly resolve the debt issue, peacefully resolve the Central American problem, restore the democratic process in Panama, and normalize Latin American relations.

In discussing domestic issues, Perez pointed out that 1989 will be a year of reform. The government will reduce economic intervention and take measures to correct imbalances in fiscal, financial, and tax areas. He said that there will inevitably be a difficult period, but he believes that with cooperation from all sides, the difficulties will be overcome.

### 佩雷斯就任委内瑞拉总统
朱满庭
1989-02-04
第6版(国际)
专栏：

　　佩雷斯就任委内瑞拉总统
    本报加拉加斯２月２日电  记者朱满庭报道：卡洛斯·安德烈斯·佩雷斯今天宣誓就任委内瑞拉总统，开始领导这个南美洲国家的第７任民选政府。佩雷斯是去年１２月当选的。
    拉美和加勒比地区以及西班牙、葡萄牙等２６个国家的元首和政府首脑应邀出席，其中包括３０多年首次来访的古巴主席菲德尔·卡斯特罗。美国派出了以副总统奎尔为首的代表团。我国政府代表、邮电部部长杨泰芳出席了就职仪式。佩雷斯在宣誓就职后发表了长篇讲话。他在谈到国际局势时说，现在人类生活在一个巨大变化的时代。他呼吁拉丁美洲人民为解决中美洲问题共同迎接挑战。在美国和拉美的关系上，佩雷斯呼吁美国新政府把拉美国家当作可以相互支持的朋友，希望发展委内瑞拉和美国之间的合作关系，共同为彻底解决债务问题，为和平解决中美洲问题，为恢复巴拿马的民主进程以及实现拉美关系的正常化而共同努力。
    在谈到国内问题时，佩雷斯指出，１９８９年将是改革的一年。政府将减少经济干预，采取措施纠正财政、金融、税收等方面的不平衡。他说，不可避免要有一个困难的时期，但他相信在各方面的合作下，困难将被克服。
