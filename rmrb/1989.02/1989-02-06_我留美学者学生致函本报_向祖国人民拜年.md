Title: Chinese Scholars and Students in the U.S. Send a Letter to Our Newspaper to Greet the People of the Motherland for the New Year
Author:
Date: 1989-02-06
Edition: 3

On the eve of the Year of the Snake, the Federation of Chinese Students and Scholars in the U.S. sent a letter to our newspaper to greet the people of the motherland. The full text of the letter is as follows:

On the occasion of the Spring Festival, we, on behalf of 36,000 Chinese scholars and students in the U.S., extend our greetings to the people of the motherland. We sincerely hope that in the new year, the political, economic, and institutional reforms of our motherland will proceed smoothly, and that science, technology, culture, education, and agricultural and industrial production will continue to advance. We wish the people of the motherland a happy and prosperous life, and we wish our country to become even more prosperous and strong.

Federation of Chinese Students and Scholars in the U.S.
February 4, 1989

### 我留美学者学生致函本报  向祖国人民拜年

1989-02-06
第3版(国际)
专栏：

　　我留美学者学生致函本报
　  向祖国人民拜年
    本报华盛顿２月４日电  蛇年春节前夕，我留美学者学生联谊会联合会致函本报，向祖国人民拜年。信件全文如下：
    欣逢新春佳节，谨代表３．６万名留美学者学生向祖国人民拜年。衷心期望在新的一年里祖国的政治、经济体制改革顺利进行，科学技术文化教育和工农业生产向前发展。祝愿祖国人民生活美满幸福，祝愿我们的国家更加繁荣富强。
    中华人民共和国留美学者学生联谊会联合会
　　　　　１９８９年２月４日
