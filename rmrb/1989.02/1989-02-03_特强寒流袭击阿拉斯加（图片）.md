Title: A Powerful Cold Front Hits Alaska
Author:
Date: 1989-02-03
Edition: 7

A powerful cold front that has been striking Alaska in the United States recently began to move southward. On January 31, the highest temperature in Anchorage, a port city in the state, rose to minus 18 degrees Celsius. However, to resist the invasion of the cold wind, the city's residents still had to dress warmly.

### 特强寒流袭击阿拉斯加（图片）

1989-02-03
第7版(国际)
专栏：

　　特强寒流袭击阿拉斯加
    袭击美国阿拉斯加州的强劲寒流近日开始出现南下势头。一月三十一日，该州港口城市安克雷奇最高气温虽已回升到零下十八度，但该市的市民为抵御寒风的侵袭仍不得不穿戴得严严实实。              新华社发（传真照片）
