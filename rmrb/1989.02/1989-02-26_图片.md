Title: Workers of Changzhou Cotton Mill No. 4 Develop New Non-Woven Products
Author: Geng Jianguo
Date: 1989-02-26
Edition: 5

Workers of Changzhou Cotton Mill No. 4 are vigorously developing new non-woven products while maintaining the production of traditional yarn and fabric.

The image shows workers inspecting the new non-woven thick felt sheets that are about to be shipped.
