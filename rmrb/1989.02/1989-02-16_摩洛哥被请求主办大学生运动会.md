Title: Morocco Requested to Host the University Sports Festival
Author:
Date: 1989-02-16
Edition: 3

The International University Sports Federation on the 14th requested that the Kingdom of Morocco replace Brazil in hosting this year's World University Sports Festival. Morocco's Minister of Youth and Sports, Semlali, stated that he will consider the request from the International University Sports Federation and make arrangements to organize the university sports festival. This year's university sports festival was originally scheduled to be held from August 19th to 29th in Brazil, but Brazil withdrew from hosting due to financial reasons.

### 摩洛哥被请求主办大学生运动会

1989-02-16
第3版(科教·文化·体育)
专栏：体育

　　摩洛哥被请求主办大学生运动会
    国际大学生体育联合会１４日请求摩洛哥王国代替巴西举办今年的世界大学生运动会。摩洛哥青年和体育部长塞姆拉利表示，将考虑国际大学生体联的请求并设法安排组织大学生运动会。今年的大学生运动会原定８月１９日至２９日在巴西举行，巴西由于财政原因而放弃主办。
　　　　　　　　　（据新华社）
