Title: Angola Protests South African Invasion; Dos Santos Ends Visit to Congo
Author:
Date: 1989-02-12
Edition: 6

Angola protests South African invasion

Dos Santos ends visit to Congo

	ROANDA, Feb. 10 (Xinhua) -- The Angolan government today sent a letter to the South African government protesting the invasion of South African troops into southern Angola, attacking Angolan armed forces.

	According to the Angolan National Radio, Angolan President Jose Eduardo dos Santos announced this during a press conference held during his visit to Congo. Dos Santos said that copies of the Angolan government's protest letter were also handed over to Cuba, the United States, and the Soviet Union, which are related to the New York Agreement. He also announced that due to the invasion of South African troops into the southern province of Cunene in Angola, the Angolan-South African Joint Commission responsible for supervising the ceasefire needs to hold a meeting to create an atmosphere for negotiations.

	BRAZZAVILLE, Feb. 10 (Xinhua) -- Angola and Congo today issued a joint statement condemning South Africa's invasion of southern Angola.

	The statement was issued after Angolan President Jose Eduardo dos Santos concluded his brief visit to Congo. The statement said that Congolese President Denis Sassou Nguesso held talks with Angolan President Jose Eduardo dos Santos on developing friendly cooperation between the two countries and common concerns about peace in southwestern Africa.

### 安哥拉抗议南非入侵  多斯桑托斯结束对刚果的访问

1989-02-12
第6版(国际)
专栏：

　　安哥拉抗议南非入侵
    多斯桑托斯结束对刚果的访问
    新华社罗安达二月十日电  安哥拉政府今天致函南非政府，抗议南非军队入侵安南部地区，袭击安武装部队。
    据安哥拉国家电台报道，安哥拉总统多斯桑托斯今天在访问刚果时举行的记者招待会上作了上述宣布。多斯桑托斯总统说，安哥拉政府抗议信的副本同时交给与纽约协议有关的方面古巴、美国以及苏联。他还宣布，由于南非军队侵入安哥拉南部宽多库邦戈省境内四十公里的地区，负责监督停火的安哥拉和南非联合委员会需要举行会议，以创造和谈气氛。
    新华社布拉柴维尔二月十日电　安哥拉和刚果今天在一项新闻公报中谴责南非入侵安哥拉南部。
    新闻公报是在安哥拉总统多斯桑托斯结束对刚果短暂访问后发表的。公报说，刚果总统萨苏同安哥拉总统多斯桑托斯就发展两国的友好合作关系和共同关心的西南部非洲和平问题进行了会谈。
