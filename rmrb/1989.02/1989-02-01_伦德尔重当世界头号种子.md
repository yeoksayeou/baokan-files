Title: Lendl Becomes World Number One Seed Again
Author:
Date: 1989-02-01
Edition: 3

Czechoslovakian tennis star Ivan Lendl today won the Australian Open men's singles championship in 40-degree Celsius heat, and thus regained his position as the world's top-ranked men's tennis player.

Lendl today defeated another Czechoslovakian player, Miloslav Mecir, with a score of 6-2, 6-2, 6-2. During the match, Lendl wore a baseball cap backward to protect his neck from the scorching sun.

The entire match lasted 115 minutes. In the first set, Lendl easily secured the victory. In the second set, he led 5-1 but took nearly an hour to win the final point.

### 伦德尔重当世界头号种子

1989-02-01
第3版(科技·文化·体育)
专栏：体育

　　伦德尔重当世界头号种子
    据新华社北京１月２９日电  据外电报道，捷克斯洛伐克网球名将伊万·伦德尔今天在４０摄氏度高温下赢得澳大利亚网球公开赛男单冠军，并缘此而重新成为世界男子网球头号选手。
    伦德尔今天以３个６∶２战胜另一名捷克斯洛伐克选手米·梅恰。比赛时，伦德尔将一顶棒球帽反戴在头上，以保护脖颈免受似火骄阳的烤灼。
    全场比赛一共用了１１５分钟。第一局，伦德尔轻松拿下。第二局，他以５∶１领先后，却花了近一小时才打下最后一分。（附图片）
