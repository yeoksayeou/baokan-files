Title: The Orphan School is Well-Managed
Author: Han Shushan
Date: 1989-02-11
Edition: 3

The orphan school located in Chaoyang District of Liaoning Province has set up various activity rooms for orphans, including calligraphy and painting, table tennis, chess, television, and library. A total of 151 orphans from various parts of the province are receiving good education here. Over the years, the school has been consecutively rated as a civilized unit at both provincial and municipal levels. The picture shows children applauding and congratulating the students who have been selected as "Three Good Students."

The photograph was taken by Han Shushan.

### 孤儿学校办得好（图片）
韩树山
1989-02-11
第3版(科教·文化·体育)
专栏：

    孤儿学校办得好
    位于朝阳的辽宁省孤儿学校为孤儿们开设了书画、乒乓球、棋类、电视、图书等丰富多彩的活动室。来自省内各地的151名孤儿，在这里受到了良好的教育。多年来，该校连续被评为省、市文明单位。图为孩子们在向当选的“三好学生”鼓掌祝贺。
            韩树山摄
