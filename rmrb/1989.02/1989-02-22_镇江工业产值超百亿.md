Title: Zhenjiang's Industrial Output Exceeds 10 Billion Yuan
Author: Jiang Guo Xing Li Guo Zhong
Date: 1989-02-22
Edition: 2

Zhenjiang, the city with the smallest population and land area in Jiangsu Province, achieved an industrial output value of 10.3 billion yuan in 1988, entering the ranks of cities nationwide with an industrial output value exceeding 10 billion yuan. At the same time, sales revenue and taxes also saw synchronized and comprehensive growth.

In 1988, the city's 143 industrial production units achieved a 91.6% stability rate for their 150 quality indicators, an increase of 6.04% compared to the previous year. The output value of high-quality products grew by 62.37% compared to the previous year.

### 镇江工业产值超百亿
蒋国星　李国忠
1989-02-22
第2版(国内新闻)
专栏：四面八方

　　镇江工业产值超百亿
    本报讯  江苏省人口最少、面积最小的镇江市，１９８８年工业总产值达到１０３亿元，进入全国实现工业产值百亿元城市行列。同时，销售收入、利税也同步全面增长。
    １９８８年，该市考核的１４３个工业生产的１５０项质量指标稳定提高率达９１．６％，比上年提高６．０４％，优质品产值比上年增长６２．３７％。
　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　（蒋国星　李国忠）
