Title: Chinese in London Celebrate the Spring Festival with Lion Dance
Author: Xǔ Rúyuán
Date: 1989-02-14
Edition: 7

On February 12th, Chinese residents in London gathered in Chinatown to perform a lion dance, celebrating the Lunar New Year. Tens of thousands of Londoners participated in the festivities of that day.
