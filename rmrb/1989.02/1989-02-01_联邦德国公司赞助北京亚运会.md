Title: West German Companies Sponsor Beijing Asian Games
Author: Liu Dong
Date: 1989-02-01
Edition: 3

West German companies sponsor Beijing Asian Games

Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, January 30th — (Reporter Liu Dong) Lufthansa Airlines of West Germany, along with the construction companies Philip Holzmann and Bilfinger, donated 10 million marks to the Eleventh Asian Games Foundation today.

### 联邦德国公司赞助北京亚运会
刘东
1989-02-01
第3版(科技·文化·体育)
专栏：体育

　　联邦德国公司赞助北京亚运会
    新华社北京１月３０日电  （记者刘东）联邦德国的汉莎航空公司以及菲利普·霍尔兹曼和贝尔芬格两家建筑公司今天在这里向第十一届亚运会基金会捐资１００万马克。
