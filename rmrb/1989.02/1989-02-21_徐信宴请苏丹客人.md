Title: Xu Xin Hosts Dinner for Sudanese Guests
Author:
Date: 1989-02-21
Edition: 2

	Xu Xin Hosts Dinner for Sudanese Guests

	BEIJING, Feb. 20 (Xinhua) -- Vice Chairman of the Chinese People's Liberation Army General Staff Xu Xin hosted a dinner at the Great Hall of the People this evening to welcome the Sudanese delegation led by Interior Minister Mubarak Fadl al-Mahdi.

	Xu Xin and Mubarak toasted at the dinner, expressing satisfaction with the continuous development of friendly cooperation between the two countries and their armies. They believed that the delegation's visit would promote cooperation in economic, trade, and military aspects between the two countries.

	The Sudanese guests arrived in Beijing this morning at the invitation of the Chinese Ministry of National Defense.

### 徐信宴请苏丹客人

1989-02-21
第2版(国内新闻)
专栏：

　　徐信宴请苏丹客人
    新华社北京二月二十日电  中国人民解放军副总参谋长徐信上将今晚在人民大会堂举行宴会，欢迎以内政部长穆巴拉克·法德尔·马赫迪为团长的苏丹代表团。
    徐信和穆巴拉克在宴会上祝酒时对两国和两国军队之间友好合作关系不断发展表示满意。他们相信代表团此行将促进两国的经济、贸易和军事方面的合作。
    苏丹客人是应中国国防部的邀请于今天清晨到达北京的。
