Title: Soviet Foreign Minister and PLO Chairman Hold Press Conference After Talks
Author: Zhang Jun
Date: 1989-02-24
Edition: 3

    On February 22, Soviet Foreign Minister Eduard Shevardnadze and PLO Chairman Yasser Arafat held a press conference after their talks in Cairo. Arafat stated that the PLO is prepared to engage in negotiations with Israel under the auspices of the five permanent members of the United Nations Security Council at the Middle East peace conference.
