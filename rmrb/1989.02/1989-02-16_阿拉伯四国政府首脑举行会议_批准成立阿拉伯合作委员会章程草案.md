Title: Four Arab Countries' Leaders Hold Meeting to Approve Draft Charter for Arab Cooperation Council
Author:
Date: 1989-02-16
Edition: 7

The prime ministers (or premiers) of Jordan, Egypt, Iraq, and the Arab Republic of Yemen held their first working meeting today in Amman to finalize the organizational structure of the Arab Cooperation Council, which is scheduled to be established in Baghdad on the 18th.

Jordan's Prime Minister Zaid Rifai emphasized in his opening remarks that the purpose of establishing the Arab Cooperation Council is to promote solidarity, cooperation, and mutual learning among the four countries.

Jordan's Minister of Information, Hani Hassouna, said at the meeting that the discussions were conducted in a serious, cooperative, and understanding atmosphere. He stated that the establishment of the Arab Cooperation Council is assured because this organization is the result of thorough consultation and unity among the four countries.

The prime ministers of Jordan, Egypt, Iraq, and the Arab Republic of Yemen approved the draft charter for the "Arab Cooperation Council" at an earlier meeting held today.

Jordan's Prime Minister Rifai, in his closing remarks at the one-day meeting, said that the establishment of the "Arab Cooperation Council" is not only for the benefit of these four countries but also for the benefit of the entire Arab world.

Jordan's Minister of Information, Hani Hassouna, told reporters after the meeting that the Cooperation Council is a regional organization with special institutions and regulations, and it will be open to all Arab countries.

### 阿拉伯四国政府首脑举行会议  批准成立阿拉伯合作委员会章程草案

1989-02-16
第7版(国际)
专栏：

　　阿拉伯四国政府首脑举行会议   
    批准成立阿拉伯合作委员会章程草案
    新华社安曼２月１４日电  约旦、埃及、伊拉克和阿拉伯也门４国总理（首相）今天在这里举行第一次工作会议，为１８日计划在巴格达成立的阿拉伯合作委员会最后确定组织机构。
    约旦首相扎伊德·里法伊在开幕式上强调，成立阿拉伯合作委员会的宗旨，是为了４国间的团结、合作和取长补短。
    约旦新闻大臣哈尼·哈苏纳在会上说，这次会议是在认真、合作和谅解的气氛中进行的。他说，阿拉伯合作委员会的成立是有保障的。因为这个组织是４国充分协商和团结的结果。
    新华社安曼２月１５日电　约旦、埃及、伊拉克和阿拉伯也门四国政府首脑在今天早些时候举行的会议，批准了成立“阿拉伯合作委员会”章程草案。
    约旦首相里法伊在为期一天的会议的闭幕式上说，成立“阿拉伯合作委员会”不仅是为了这四国的利益，也有利于阿拉伯世界。
    约旦新闻大臣哈尼·哈苏纳会后对记者说，合作委员会是一个有特别制度和特别规定的地区性组织，将向所有阿拉伯国家开放。
