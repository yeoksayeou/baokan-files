Title: Reflections on a Light Drink
Author: Zhang Mengfei
Date: 1989-02-07
Edition: 4

	Where in the south can one find the incense stick?
	Meeting a thousand miles away to discuss poetry and literature.
	Rain and snow add to the interest,
	The waves have not subsided, but the warmth remains.
	In the year of the dragon, at the dragon gate, recognizing the dragon's offspring,
	In the year of the snake, at the snake's mouth, meeting the snake's spirit.
	The high tower wine is unforgettable,
	Every drop is the essence of the sea.

### 小酌偶感——陪袁庚会饮诸文友，借得半联凑成八句
张梦飞
1989-02-07
第4版(副刊)
专栏：

　　小酌偶感
　　——陪袁庚会饮诸文友，借得半联凑成八句
　　张梦飞
    一柱天南何处寻，
  　相逢万里论诗文。
  　雨雪绸缪添意兴， 
　　风波未了盛余温。
  　龙岁龙门认龙种，
  　蛇年蛇口会蛇神。  
　　难忘高天楼上酒，
  　点滴都是海之魂。
