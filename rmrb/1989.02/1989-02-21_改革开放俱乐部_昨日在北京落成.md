Title: The Reform and Opening-Up Club Officially Opens in Beijing
Author: Wang Zhiguang
Date: 1989-02-21
Edition: 2

The Reform and Opening-Up Club officially opened in Beijing yesterday. Our reporter Wang Zhiguang reports: This afternoon, the China Reform and Opening-Up Club, sponsored by the China Reform and Opening-Up Foundation and the China International Cultural Exchange Center and organized by the Beijing Overseas Alumni Association of Universities, was officially opened in Beijing.

The China Reform and Opening-Up Club is located in a two-story building on Nan Xiaojie Street in Dongcheng District, Beijing. It serves intellectuals and people from all walks of life who are committed to the cause of reform and opening-up.

### 改革开放俱乐部  昨日在北京落成
王志光
1989-02-21
第2版(国内新闻)
专栏：四面八方

　　改革开放俱乐部  昨日在北京落成
    本报北京２月２０日讯  记者王志光报道：今天下午，由中国改革与开放基金会、中国国际文化交流中心资助，高等院校校友会北京海外联谊会承办的中国改革与开放俱乐部在北京正式落成。
    中国改革与开放俱乐部是座落在北京东城南小街的一幢二层建筑。它以知识分子和有志于改革开放事业的社会各界人士为服务对象。
