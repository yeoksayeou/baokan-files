Title: Soviet Cargo Ship Docks with Orbital Station
Author:
Date: 1989-02-14
Edition: 7

The Soviet automatic cargo ship "Progress-40" successfully docked with the "Peace" orbital station today at 13:30 Moscow time, according to a report by TASS.

The "Progress-40" cargo ship, launched on February 11, completed its mutual search, approach, docking, and docking with the orbital station entirely through its onboard automatic control systems, under the supervision of the ground command—measurement and control complex and the three cosmonauts on the orbital station.

The "Progress-40" cargo ship docked with the "Kvant" laboratory module of the orbital station. The cargo ship delivered fuel, food, water, equipment, instruments, and mail to the orbital station.

The report stated that all systems on the orbital station are functioning normally, and the cosmonauts Volkov, Krikalev, and Polyakov are in good health.

### 苏货运飞船同轨道站实现对接

1989-02-14
第7版(国际)
专栏：

　　苏货运飞船同轨道站实现对接
    新华社莫斯科２月１２日电  据塔斯社报道，苏联自动货运飞船“进步—４０”号于今天莫斯科时间１３时３０分，同“和平”号轨道联合体实现了对接。
    １１日发射的“进步—４０”号货运飞船，同轨道联合体的相互寻找、接近、停靠和对接，都是由飞船上的自动控制设备完成的，并受地面指挥——测量综合装置及轨道站上的３名宇航员的监督。
    “进步—４０”号货运飞船同轨道联合体的“量子”实验室的对接口对接。货运飞船为轨道站运去了燃料、食品、水、设备、仪表及邮件。
    报道说，轨道站上的各种系统工作正常，宇航员沃尔科夫、克里卡廖夫和波利亚科夫自我感觉良好。
