Title: Significant Changes in Romania's Foreign Trade System: Foreign Trade Companies Belong to Enterprises
Author:
Date: 1989-02-20
Edition: 3

	Romania's foreign trade system has undergone significant changes. Starting from this year, the over 60 existing foreign trade companies will no longer be under the jurisdiction of the Ministry of Foreign Trade and International Economic Cooperation. Instead, they will be affiliated with various industrial centers and large-scale factory enterprises.

### 罗外贸体制重要变动  外贸公司归企业

1989-02-20
第3版(国际新闻)
专栏：

　　罗外贸体制重要变动
    外贸公司归企业
    罗马尼亚外贸体制发生重要变动。从今年１月起原有的６０多家外贸公司不再由外贸和国际经济合作部主管，而改为归属各工业中心和大型工厂企业。
