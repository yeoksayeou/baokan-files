Title: Another Upset in the World Youth Football Tournament: Host Nation Faces Elimination
Author: Wú Yìhóng
Date: 1989-02-21
Edition: 4

World Youth Football Tournament Sees Another Upset: Host Nation Faces Elimination

According to Xinhua News Agency in Riyadh on February 19, the Czechoslovakia youth football team defeated the Saudi Arabian team 1-0 tonight.

Today's results in the other three groups of the World Youth Football Championship were as follows: Group B's Soviet Union team defeated Costa Rica 1-0, Group C's Brazil team defeated Mali 5-0, and Group D's Argentina team defeated Norway 2-0. After today's matches, both Saudi Arabia in Group A and Norway in Group D have lost both of their games, eliminating any hope of advancing to the next round.

### 世青足球赛又爆冷门  东道主出线无望
吴毅宏
1989-02-21
第4版(文化生活·体育)
专栏：体育

　　世青足球赛又爆冷门
    东道主出线无望
    据新华社利雅得２月１９日电  （记者吴毅宏）捷克斯洛伐克青年足球队今晚在此间以１∶０击败沙特阿拉伯队。
    世界青年足球锦标赛今天另三个赛区的比赛结果是：Ｂ组的苏联队以１∶０胜哥斯达黎加队，Ｃ组巴西队以５∶０大胜马里队，Ｄ组的阿根廷队以２∶０力克挪威队。经过今天的比赛，Ａ组的沙特队和Ｄ组的挪威队均二战皆负，失去了进入复赛的可能。
