Education, Science, and Culture News Flashes

    ● Henan Province implements a child immunization insurance system, with the number of children insured ranking among the top in the nation. By the end of 1988, the province had insured over 6.27 million children, accounting for more than 85% of the children who should be insured.

    ● The Young Scientists and Technologists Legal Research Association was established in Beijing on February 1, aiming to enhance cooperation and exchange between young scientists and technologists and legal professionals, and to promote the development of science and technology law. Kong Ping serves as the president.

    ● On the afternoon of February 4, Beijing held a concert to commend outstanding educators in primary, secondary, and kindergarten schools. Artists and representatives from primary and secondary schools performed various programs.

### 教科文零讯

1989-02-05
第3版(科教·文化·体育)
专栏：教科文零讯

　　教科文零讯
    ●河南省实行儿童计划免疫保险制度儿童入保数居全国前列。截至１９８８年底，全省儿童入保数达６２７万多名，占应入保儿童的８５％以上。
　　　　　　　　　　　（据新华社）
    ●青年科技法研究会２月１日在北京成立，旨在增进青年科技工作者和法律工作者的合作、交流，促进科技法事业的发展。孔平任会长。
　　　　　　　　　　　（文任）
    ●２月４日下午，北京市举行音乐会，慰问中、小、幼先进教育工作者。艺术家及中小学生代表表演了节目。　　　　　　（魏玉凤）
