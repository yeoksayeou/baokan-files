Title: Chinese Acrobats Gain Fame in Paris
Author: Dǒng Sūhuá
Date: 1989-02-07
Edition: 3

The Chinese Guangxi Acrobatic Troupe's performance of the act "Double Swinging Bars" won the gold medal at the 13th International Circus Festival of Tomorrow in Paris.
