Title: The International Volleyball Federation Introduces a Series of New Competitions
Author: Xiao Chen
Date: 1989-02-26
Edition: 4

The vice president of the International Volleyball Federation and the executive director of the Japanese Volleyball Association, Matsuhira Yasutaka, recently revealed in Osaka that the International Volleyball Federation will host the first Grand Prix. The "World Circuit Tournament" involving eight countries will commence next May, with a prize fund of one billion yen (approximately $800,000).

Additionally, in December of this year, the first World Club Men's Volleyball Championship and the Under-17 World Youth Cup Volleyball Championship will be held for the first time in Italy. The men's competition will take place in the United States, and the women's competition will be held in Brazil, from the end of this year to the beginning of next year.

### 国际排联新设一系列比赛
肖晨
1989-02-26
第4版(社会生活·体育)
专栏：体育

　　国际排联新设一系列比赛
    本报讯  国际排联副主席、日本排协专务理事松平康隆日前在大阪透露，国际排联将举办首届大奖赛。由八个国家参加的“世界循环赛”将于明年五月开始举行，这次比赛的奖金为一亿日元（约合八十万美元）。
    此外，今年十二月还将首次在意大利举行世界俱乐部男子排球锦标赛和十七岁以下的世界青年杯排球锦标赛，比赛时间为今年底至明年初，男子赛址在美国，女子在巴西。　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　
　　　　（肖晨）
