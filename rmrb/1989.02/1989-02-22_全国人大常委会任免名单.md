Title: List of Appointments and Dismissals by the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress
Author:
Date: 1989-02-22
Edition: 2

The Standing Committee of the National People's Congress Announces Appointments and Dismissals
Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, February 21st, 1989

The Sixth Meeting of the Seventh National People's Congress Standing Committee, held on February 21st, 1989, approved the following appointments and dismissals:

1. Appoint Peng Ruisheng and Huang Kunyi as advisors to the Education, Science, Culture, and Health Committee of the National People's Congress.

2. Appoint Yang Kedi as the deputy director of the Second Criminal Tribunal of the Supreme People's Court.

3. Dismiss Fei Zongyi from the position of deputy director of the Economic Tribunal of the Supreme People's Court.

4. Dismiss Xu Kuiqiu from the position of member of the Procuratorial Committee of the Supreme People's Procuratorate.
