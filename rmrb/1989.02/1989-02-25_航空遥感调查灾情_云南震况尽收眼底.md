Title: Aerial Remote Sensing Surveys Disaster Situations in Yunnan
Author: Wang Xiyuan
Date: 1989-02-25
Edition: 2

Aerial remote sensing surveys disaster situations in Yunnan, earthquake conditions fully in view

Beijing, February 24 (Xinhua) — Reporter Wang Xiyuan reports: The State Seismological Bureau and the National Remote Sensing Center's Aviation Department, in collaboration, recently used color-infrared aerial remote sensing technology to photograph a 5,185-square-kilometer area of the earthquake-stricken regions of Lincang and Gengma in Yunnan Province. Preliminary indoor interpretation of the 258 color-infrared reversal films obtained shows the damage to topography, buildings, and other features in the disaster area clearly.

The use of this advanced technology for earthquake situation surveys is a first in China and has not been reported elsewhere in the world.

This survey demonstrates that using aerial remote sensing technology for earthquake disaster surveys is an efficient and cost-effective advanced method. On the photographs, industrial, residential, and service facilities, as well as different types of buildings and severely damaged structures such as collapsed or partially collapsed houses, can all be distinguished.

### 航空遥感调查灾情  云南震况尽收眼底
王溪元
1989-02-25
第2版(国内新闻)
专栏：

　　航空遥感调查灾情  云南震况尽收眼底
    本报北京二月二十四日讯  记者王溪元报道：国家地震局和国家遥感中心航空一部等单位相配合，日前运用彩红外航空遥感技术对云南省澜沧、耿马地震灾区五千一百八十五平方公里地面作了空中摄影，对所获二百五十八幅彩红外反转片进行室内初步判读，灾区地貌、建筑等受损情况一目了然。
    运用这种先进技术进行震情调查在我国是首次，在世界范围也还没见先例报道。
    这次调查证明，用航空遥感技术进行地震灾情调查，是一种效率高、成本低的先进手段。在照片上，对工业、生活、服务设施、不同结构类型的房屋，以及房屋倒塌、部分倒塌等严重破坏情况均可区分。
