Title: Glass Fiber Mongolian Yurt Introduced
Author: Ào Tēng
Date: 1989-02-27
Edition: 2

The traditional felt Mongolian yurts that have been the homes of generations of nomads on the grasslands will soon be replaced by glass fiber yurts. The Inner Mongolia Autonomous Region has recently developed a glass fiber yurt, which features elegant interior decoration, good lighting and ventilation, making it feel spacious, bright, and comfortable. It has excellent insulation, moisture resistance, fire resistance, and waterproofing properties, and is also convenient to assemble and disassemble.

### 玻璃钢蒙古包问世
傲腾
1989-02-27
第2版(国内新闻)
专栏：四面八方

　　玻璃钢蒙古包问世
    本报呼和浩特２月２６日电  记者傲腾报道：草原牧民祖祖辈辈居住的毡制蒙古包将由玻璃钢制蒙古包所代替。内蒙古自治区最近研制出的玻璃钢蒙古包，有素雅的内装修，采光通风好，使人感到宽敞、明亮、舒适。它具有保暖、防潮、防火、防水性能，拆装也很方便。
