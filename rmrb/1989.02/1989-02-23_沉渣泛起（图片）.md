Title: Dregs Resurface
Author: Tan Zhisheng
Date: 1989-02-23
Edition: 2

Currently, the number of people coming from other provinces to Beijing to "read palms" is increasing. Look, this person is even soliciting customers on the street. For one yuan, he reads palms once, and in just about ten minutes, he has read the palms of 13 people, nine of whom are teenagers.

	Tan Zhisheng, photographed at the Fengtai Commercial Street

### 沉渣泛起（图片）
谭志生
1989-02-23
第2版(国内新闻)
专栏：

    沉渣泛起
    目前，外地来京“看手相”的人日渐增多。瞧，此人竟在大街上招徕看客，一元钱看一次手相，十来分钟便看了13人次，其中9人是青少年。
                      谭志生摄于丰台商业街
