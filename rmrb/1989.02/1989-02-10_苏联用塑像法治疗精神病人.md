Title: The Soviet Union Uses Sculpture Therapy to Treat Mental Patients
Author:
Date: 1989-02-10
Edition: 7

The All-Union Moscow Center for the Study of Mental Disorders has discovered a method of using sculpture to treat mental illness. After clinical trials, nearly 300 patients have recovered their health.

### 苏联用塑像法治疗精神病人

1989-02-10
第7版(国际)
专栏：

　　苏联用塑像法治疗精神病人
    新华社莫斯科２月８日电  全苏莫斯科精神病分泌学中心的科研人员发现了用塑像来治疗精神病的方法，经临床使用，已有近３００名患者恢复了健康。
