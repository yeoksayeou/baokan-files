Title: Fujian Province Exceeds Plan for Textbook Paper Production
Author: Fei Weiwei
Date: 1989-02-03
Edition: 2

Fujian Province's paper industry overcame severe shortages in raw materials, water, and electricity, as well as a comprehensive price increase in necessary production materials. Despite the low selling prices of their products and the resulting enterprise losses, they prioritized the bigger picture and, in 1988, produced and supplied 11,700 tons of paper for primary and secondary school textbooks. This exceeded the annual plan by 95%, achieving the highest level in the province's history. In the national evaluation of textbook paper supply for 1988, Fujian Province ranked first.

### 福建超额完成课本用纸计划
费伟伟
1989-02-03
第2版(经济)
专栏：

　　福建超额完成课本用纸计划
    本报讯  福建省造纸工业公司克服原材料、水、电供应严重不足和生产所需各种原辅材料全面涨价等困难，在产品售价偏低、企业发生亏损的情况下，顾大局，识大体，去年生产、供应中小学生课本用纸１１７００吨，超额９５％完成全年计划，创历年来最高水平，在去年全国课本用纸供应评比中名列榜首。
