Title: Friendly News Briefs
Author:
Date: 1989-02-27
Edition: 3

	The Chairman of the Council of State of Vietnam, Vo Chi Cong, received the outgoing Chinese Ambassador to Vietnam, Li Shichun, on February 25 in Hanoi.

	The new Speaker of the Finnish Parliament, Kalevi Sorsa, received the Chinese Ambassador to Finland, Yu Lizhen, on February 9 at the Parliament.

	The General Secretary of the Central Committee of the Bulgarian Communist Party and Chairman of the State Council of Bulgaria, Todor Zhivkov, received the outgoing Chinese Ambassador to Bulgaria, Teng Shaozhi, on February 9 at the State Council Building.

	More than 40 prominent figures from various sectors in Burundi held a meeting in Bujumbura on February 11, announcing the establishment of the Burundi-China Friendship Association.

	The 1989 China-Poland Trade and Payment Agreement was signed in Warsaw on February 1. According to the agreement, the trade volume between the two countries this year will be 1.281 billion Swiss francs, an increase of 5.3 percent over last year.

	The President of the International Fund for Agricultural Development (IFAD), Idris Jazairy, and the Chinese Ambassador to Italy, Du Gong, signed the Sichuan Province Livestock Development Project Agreement on behalf of IFAD and the Chinese government, respectively, on February 13 in Rome. According to the agreement, IFAD will provide a concessional loan of $17.3 million for the aforementioned project to China.

### 友好简讯

1989-02-27
第3版(国际新闻)
专栏：

　　友好简讯
    ●越南国务委员会主席武志公２５日在河内会见了即将离任的中国驻越南大使李世淳。
    ●芬兰新任议长卡莱维·索尔萨９日在议会接见了中国驻芬兰大使于立暄。
    ●保加利亚共产党中央总书记、保加利亚国务委员会主席日夫科夫９日在国务委员会大厦接见了即将离任的中国驻保加利亚特命全权大使滕绍志。
    ●４０多名布隆迪各界知名人士１１日在布琼布拉举行会议，宣告布隆迪—中国友好协会成立。
    ●中国波兰１９８９年换货和付款议定书２月１日在华沙签字。
    根据议定书，今年两国贸易额将为１２．８１亿瑞士法郎，比去年增加５．３％。　　　　　　　　　　
　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　（据新华社）
    ●国际农业发展基金会（简称农发基金）总裁伊德里斯·雅泽里和中国驻意大利大使杜攻１３日在罗马分别代表农发基金和中国政府签署了四川省畜牧发展项目协议。根据这一协议，农发基金将为上述项目向中国提供１７３０万美元的优惠贷款。　　　　　　　　　　　　　　
　　　　　　　　　（本报专电）
