Title: American Scientists Discover Nickel Oxide Superconductor
Author:
Date: 1989-02-14
Edition: 7

American scientists have discovered a new type of nickel oxide superconductor.

According to the latest issue of the journal *Science*, researchers at Purdue University have confirmed that a lanthanum-strontium-nickel oxide becomes superconducting at 70 K. This is the first material that does not contain copper and has a critical temperature above 30 K.

The research team conducted resistivity and magnetic property tests on the material samples, confirming that it exhibits superconductivity in the temperature range from 0 K to 70 K. Furthermore, after losing its superconductivity, the material can regain it through reheating.

### 美科学家发现镍氧化物超导体

1989-02-14
第7版(国际)
专栏：

　　美科学家发现镍氧化物超导体
    新华社华盛顿２月１２日电  美国普度大学的研究人员新发现了一种镍氧化物超导体。
    据最新一期《科学》杂志报道，研究人员已经证实这种镧—锶—镍氧化物在７０Ｋ时成为超导体。这是第一种不含铜而且临界温度高于３０Ｋ的超导材料。
    研究小组对材料样品进行了阻抗和磁性能试验，证实它在零Ｋ—７０Ｋ温度范围内呈现超导性，而且这种超导性失去后，通过再次加热处理可以恢复。
