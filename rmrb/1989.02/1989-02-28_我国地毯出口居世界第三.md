Title: China's Carpet Exports Rank Third in the World
Author: Moxinyuan
Date: 1989-02-28
Edition: 2

China's carpet exports reached $350 million in 1988, a 20% increase over the previous year, placing it third in the world, behind Iran and India. The 15th China International Carpet Fair opened today in Beijing, attracting more than 200 guests from over 20 countries and regions.

The carpet industry is labor-intensive, with the advantages of low investment and quick returns. Currently, there are 500,000 people nationwide engaged in this industry.

### 我国地毯出口居世界第三
莫新元
1989-02-28
第2版(国内新闻)
专栏：

　　我国地毯出口居世界第三
    本报北京２月２７日讯  记者莫新元报道：去年，中国地毯出口额达到３．５亿美元，比上年增长２０％，仅次于伊朗和印度，跃居世界第三位。今天在北京开幕的第１５届中国地毯出口交易会，吸引了２０多个国家和地区的２００多位来宾。
    地毯行业属于劳动密集型产业，具有投资少、见效快的特点。目前，全国从事这一行业生产的人员多达５０万。
