Title: Beijing Chaoyang District Busts Masked Knife-Wielding Robbery Gang
Author: Yang Hongbao
Date: 1989-02-18
Edition: 4

Recently, the Chaoyang District Public Security Bureau of Beijing, in collaboration with relevant departments, successfully apprehended a masked knife-wielding home invasion robbery gang in just four days.

On the night of February 9th from 11 PM to the early morning of the 10th at 2 AM, four consecutive masked knife-wielding home invasion robberies occurred in the Guan Zhuang Township of Beijing's Chaoyang District and adjacent areas of Tongxian County. The robbers stole over 5,000 yuan in cash along with gold rings, cameras, watches, and many other items. Following the incidents, the public security authorities swiftly launched an investigation. By noon on February 14th, all three members of the robbery gang were captured, and all stolen money and items were recovered. The case is still under further investigation.

### 北京朝阳区破获蒙面持刀抢劫团伙
杨鸿宝
1989-02-18
第4版(政治·法律·社会)
专栏：

　　北京朝阳区破获蒙面持刀抢劫团伙
    本报讯  最近，北京市公安局朝阳分局在有关部门的通力合作下，仅用４天时间就查获了一蒙面持刀入室抢劫的犯罪团伙。
    ２月９日晚１１时至次日晨２时，在北京市朝阳区管庄乡及与通县毗邻地区，连续发生４起蒙面人持刀入室抢劫大案。抢劫人民币５０００余元及金戒指、照相机、手表等许多物品。案发后，公安部门迅速展开侦破，于２月１４日中午将抢劫团伙３名罪犯全部捉拿归案，并收缴全部赃款赃物。现此案仍在进一步审理中。　
　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　（杨鸿宝）
