Title: 1992 Olympics Equestrian Event May Be Relocated
Author:
Date: 1989-02-23
Edition: 4

According to Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, February 22nd, Reuters reported that due to an uncontrolled disease spreading among horses used in competitions in Spain, the equestrian events of the 1992 Olympics may not be held in Spain.

The International Equestrian Federation has not yet commented on this matter. However, the President of the Portuguese Olympic Committee, Fe Belo, has stated that if Spain cannot host the 1992 Olympics equestrian events, Portugal would be happy to take over the hosting responsibilities.

### １９９２年奥运会马术赛可能易地

1989-02-23
第4版(社会生活·体育)
专栏：体育

　　１９９２年奥运会马术赛可能易地
    据新华社北京２月２２日电  据路透社报道，由于在西班牙比赛用的马中流行的一种疾病尚未得到控制，１９９２年奥运会的马术比赛有可能不在西班牙举行。
    国际马术联合会尚未就此事表态。但是，葡萄牙奥委会主席费·贝洛已经表示，如果西班牙不能举办１９９２年奥运会马术比赛的话，葡萄牙乐于接手承办。
