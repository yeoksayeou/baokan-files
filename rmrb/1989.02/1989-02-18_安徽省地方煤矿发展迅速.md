Title: Anhui's Local Coal Mines Develop Rapidly
Author:
Date: 1989-02-18
Edition: 2

The rapidly developing local coal mines in Anhui Province have formed a strong presence alongside the state-owned coal mines in Huainan and Huabei. According to statistics, in 1988, Anhui's local coal mines produced over 700 million tons of coal, an increase of 10.2% compared to the previous year, which is higher than the national coal production growth rate.

### 安徽省地方煤矿发展迅速

1989-02-18
第2版(经济)
专栏：

    安徽省地方煤矿发展迅速
  　迅速发展起来的安徽省地方煤矿，已与淮南和淮北国家统配煤矿形成鼎足之势。据统计，１９８８年安徽省地方煤矿产煤超过了７００万吨，比上一年增长１０．２％，高于全国煤炭产量增长的水平。
    　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　（据新华社）
