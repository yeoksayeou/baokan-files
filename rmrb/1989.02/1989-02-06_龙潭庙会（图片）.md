Title: The Dragon Pool Temple Fair
Author: Wang Dong
Date: 1989-02-06
Edition: 1

The annual Beijing Dragon Pool Temple Fair began on February 5. Folk performance troupes from Shanghai, Shandong, Hebei, Liaoning, and other places gathered at Dragon Pool, each presenting their outstanding performances to give Beijing residents a visual feast.

    龙潭庙会
