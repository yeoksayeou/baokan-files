Title: Botha Resigns as Chairman of South Africa's Ruling Party
Author: Lin Jiaoming
Date: 1989-02-03
Edition: 6

Botha Resigns as Chairman of South Africa's Ruling Party

	BEIRUT, Feb. 2 (Xinhua) -- South African President P.W. Botha has decided to resign as chairman of the ruling National Party due to health reasons, according to a radio broadcast here today.

	Botha made the announcement in a letter to the National Party's annual conference, which is being held in Pretoria. The 73-year-old president was hospitalized in Cape Town last month after suffering a "mild stroke." Deputy President F.W. de Klerk has been acting as president since then.

### 博塔辞去南非执政党主席职务
林皎明
1989-02-03
第6版(国际)
专栏：本报最后消息

　　博塔辞去南非执政党主席职务
    本报开罗２月２日电  记者林皎明报道：据此间电台广播，南非总统博塔因健康原因今天决定辞去执政的南非国民党主席职务。他是在今天写给正在比勒陀利亚召开的南非国民党年度大会的一封信中宣布这一决定的。
    上月１８日，７３岁的博塔因患“轻度中风”，住进开普敦一家医院，霍尼斯代理南非总统职务。
