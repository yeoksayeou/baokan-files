Title: Hijacker Sentenced to Life Imprisonment
Author:
Date: 1989-02-26
Edition: 3

The Lebanese hijacker Ali was sentenced to life imprisonment by the criminal court of the Federal Court of Lausanne, Switzerland, on February 24.

This is the first time the Federal Court of Switzerland has applied the new provisions of the criminal code on hostage-taking.

### 劫机犯被判处终身监禁

1989-02-26
第3版(国际新闻)
专栏：

　　劫机犯被判处终身监禁
    黎巴嫩劫机犯阿里理２４日被洛桑瑞士联邦法院刑事法庭判处终身监禁。
    这是瑞士联邦法院第一次使用关于绑架人质刑事法典的新条款。
　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　（据新华社）
