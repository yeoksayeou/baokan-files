Title: U.S. Inspects Boeing Aircraft Safety Systems
Author:
Date: 1989-02-02
Edition: 7

The Federal Aviation Administration of the United States ordered on January 31 that all American airlines inspect the engines and cargo hold safety systems of 741 Boeing aircraft produced in the past eight years within 25 days.

Following the crash of a British Midland Airways Boeing 737 on January 8, subsequent inspections revealed wiring issues in four Boeing aircraft.

### 美检查波音机安全系统

1989-02-02
第7版(国际)
专栏：国际经济动态

　　美检查波音机安全系统
    美国联邦航空局一月三十一日指令美国各家航空公司在二十五天内检查过去八年中出厂的七百四十一架波音飞机的发动机和货舱安全系统。
    今年一月八日英国米德兰航空公司一架波音７３７飞机坠毁之后，随后的检查中发现四架波音飞机存在线路串线问题。  
　　（据新华社）
