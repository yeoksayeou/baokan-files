Title: Western Ginseng Takes Root in China
Author: He Huangbiao
Date: 1989-02-24
Edition: 2

Western ginseng, renowned as a precious health supplement, can it take root and be promoted in China? The Institute of Special Products of the Chinese Academy of Agricultural Sciences has, after five years of research, completed the comprehensive cultivation technology for Western ginseng and established a high-standard seed source base, thus solving this problem.

This important achievement was approved the day before yesterday at a technical appraisal held by the Chinese Academy of Agricultural Sciences.

### 西洋参落户中华
何黄彪
1989-02-24
第2版(国内新闻)
专栏：四面八方

　　西洋参落户中华
    本报北京二月二十三日讯  记者何黄彪报道：被誉为珍贵保健补品的西洋参能不能在我国安家落户、扩大推广？中国农科院特产研究所经过五年攻关，先后完成了西洋参综合栽培技术研究，建成了高标准的种源基地，从而解决了这个问题。
    这一重要成果前天通过中国农科院主持的技术鉴定。
