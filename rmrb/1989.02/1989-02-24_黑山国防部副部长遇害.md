Title: Deputy Minister of Defense of Montenegro Assassinated
Author:
Date: 1989-02-24
Edition: 3

Deputy Minister of Defense of Montenegro Assassinated

Xinhua News Agency, Belgrade, February 23rd - According to today's report from the Yugoslavian "News Evening Post," the Deputy Minister of Defense of the Socialist Federal Republic of Montenegro, Colonel V. Shchepanovich, was shot and killed on the 21st at his workplace.

The assailant was a security personnel of the republic's defense department, Z. Daikovich. He has been detained on the spot. The motive for the murder is currently under investigation.

### 黑山国防部副部长遇害

1989-02-24
第3版(国际新闻)
专栏：

　　黑山国防部副部长遇害
    新华社贝尔格莱德２月２３日电  据南斯拉夫《新闻晚报》今天报道，南斯拉夫黑山共和国国防部副部长沃·什切潘诺维奇上校本月２１日晚在工作地点被人开枪打死。
    凶手是该共和国国防部警卫人员佐·达伊科维奇。他已被当场拘留。谋杀原因目前正在调查中。
