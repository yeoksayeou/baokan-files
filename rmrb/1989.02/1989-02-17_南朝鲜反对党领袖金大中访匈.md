Title: South Korean Opposition Leader Kim Dae-jung Visits Hungary
Author:
Date: 1989-02-17
Edition: 6

South Korean opposition leader Kim Dae-jung arrived in Hungary on the 13th for a visit.

Kim Dae-jung is the first important South Korean political figure to visit Hungary since the formal establishment of diplomatic relations between Hungary and South Korea on January 31. During his visit, Kim Dae-jung was received by the Chairman of the Hungarian Presidential Council, Straub, and the Speaker of the National Assembly, Szűrös György.

### 南朝鲜反对党领袖金大中访匈

1989-02-17
第6版(国际)
专栏：国际新闻简报

　　南朝鲜反对党领袖金大中访匈
    南朝鲜反对党领袖金大中十三日抵达匈牙利访问。
    金大中是匈牙利同南朝鲜自一月三十一日正式建交后第一个访匈的南朝鲜重要政界人物。在访问中，金大中受到匈共和国主席团主席什特劳乌布和国民议会议长什陶丁盖尔的接见。　　　　　　　　　　　　
　　　
　　　（据新华社）
