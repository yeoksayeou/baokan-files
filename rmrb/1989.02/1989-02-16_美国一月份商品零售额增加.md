Title: U.S. January Retail Sales Increase
Author:
Date: 1989-02-16
Edition: 7

According to figures released by the U.S. Department of Commerce, U.S. retail sales in January increased by 0.6 percent compared to the previous month, while December's retail sales decreased by 0.1 percent.

January's total retail sales in the U.S. reached $138.9 billion.

Retail sales account for approximately one-third of the U.S. gross national product, making them an important indicator of the U.S. economic situation.

### 美国一月份商品零售额增加

1989-02-16
第7版(国际)
专栏：

　　美国一月份商品零售额增加
    据美国商务部公布的数字，今年一月份美国商品零售额比上个月增加百分之零点六，而去年十二月份的零售额下降了百分之零点一。
    一月份美国的商品零售总额达一千三百八十九亿美元。
    商品零售额约相当于美国国民生产总值的三分之一，因此被认为是观察美国经济形势的晴雨表。　　　　
　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　（据新华社）
