Title: One-Sentence News
Author:
Date: 1989-02-01
Edition: 6

	One-Sentence News

	△ Soviet leader Gorbachev will officially visit Britain in early April this year.

	△ Hungarian Prime Minister and Hungarian Socialist Workers' Party Politburo member Nemeth announced in Budapest on January 30 that Hungary will unilaterally reduce its military by 9,300 personnel.

	△ On January 30, the coalition government of the Austrian Social Democratic Party and the Austrian People's Party underwent a partial reshuffle. The current Federal Chancellor's Office Minister Franz Leschnak (Social Democratic Party) will take over as Federal Interior Minister, and the chairman of the Austrian Textile, Clothing, and Leather Industry Union, Harald Ettl (Social Democratic Party), will assume the position of Federal Chancellor's Office Minister.

	△ The 45th session of the United Nations Human Rights Commission opened in Geneva on January 30, with 43 member countries' commissioners attending the meeting.
