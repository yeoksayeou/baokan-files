Title: Salvador Guerrillas Kill and Wound Hundreds of Government Soldiers
Author:
Date: 1989-02-08
Edition: 3

Salvadoran guerrillas recently launched a series of fierce attacks on government forces over the past week, killing and wounding 109 soldiers. According to a battle report broadcast by the guerrillas' "We Will Win Radio" on February 6, the Salvadoran guerrillas conducted 64 attacks in the northern province of Chalatenango. They seized two villages, destroyed a helicopter, and blew up a military camp.

### 萨游击队打死打伤政府军百人

1989-02-08
第3版(国际)
专栏：国际要闻简报

　　萨游击队打死打伤政府军百人
    萨尔瓦多游击队最近一周再次向政府军发动猛烈进攻，共打死打伤政府军官兵一百零九人。据游击队《我们必胜电台》六日广播的战报透露，萨尔瓦多游击队最近在北部的查拉特南戈省共向政府军发动了六十四次进攻，攻占了两个村庄，还炸毁了一架直升机和一个军营。
