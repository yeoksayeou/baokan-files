Title: Iran to Pardon 2,600 Political Prisoners
Author:
Date: 1989-02-16
Edition: 6

Iran will pardon 2,600 political prisoners. Iranian intelligence chief Mohammad Reza Rezaie announced on the 14th that before the Iranian New Year on March 21, 2,600 members of anti-government parties will be pardoned and released.

### 伊朗将大赦二千六百政治犯

1989-02-16
第6版(国际)
专栏：国际新闻简报

　　伊朗将大赦二千六百政治犯
    伊朗情报部长穆罕默迪·雷沙里十四日宣布，在三月二十一日伊朗新年之前，将有二千六百名反政府政党的成员被大赦获释。　　　　　
　　（据新华社）
