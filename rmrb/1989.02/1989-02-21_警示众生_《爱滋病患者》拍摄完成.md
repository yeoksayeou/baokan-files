Title: Warning the Masses: The Filming of "AIDS Patient" Completed
Author: Biān
Date: 1989-02-21
Edition: 4

The first domestic film about AIDS, "AIDS Patient," has recently been completed by the Beijing Youth Film Studio.

The film tells the story of national security department detectives, with the assistance of local public security authorities, tracking and investigating three female AIDS carriers who had relations with a foreign teacher. The film is fast-paced, humorous, and interspersed with serious moral education, serving as a reminder to society: "One must take social responsibility for one's actions."

### 警示众生  《爱滋病患者》拍摄完成
边
1989-02-21
第4版(文化生活·体育)
专栏：

　　警示众生
    《爱滋病患者》拍摄完成
    本报讯  我国第一部以爱滋病为题材的故事片《爱滋病患者》，最近由北京青年电影制片厂摄制完成。
    影片述说国家安全部刑警，在当地公安部门协助下，跟踪追查与外籍教师有关系的三个女性爱滋病带毒者的过程。节奏快捷，幽默调侃，而又不乏严峻的道德教化，借以提醒世人：“必须对自己的行为负社会责任。”　　　　（边）
