Title: China Hopes to Quickly Settle the "Satanic Verses" Incident
Author:
Date: 1989-02-24
Edition: 1

China hopes that the incident surrounding the book "The Satanic Verses" can be quickly resolved.

New China News Agency, Beijing, February 23rd: Foreign Ministry spokesman Li Zhaoxing said that China hopes the incident surrounding the book "The Satanic Verses" can be quickly resolved.

At the afternoon press conference, a foreign journalist asked about China's stance on Ayatollah Khomeini's order to execute the author of "The Satanic Verses," Salman Rushdie. Li Zhaoxing said, "We have always advocated that the religious beliefs and customs of all peoples should be respected. We have also noted that the author of the book has issued a public apology. We hope this incident can be quickly resolved."

### 中国希望能够尽快平息《撒旦的诗篇》一书事件

1989-02-24
第1版(要闻)
专栏：

　　中国希望能够尽快平息《撒旦的诗篇》一书事件
    新华社北京２月２３日电  外交部发言人李肇星说，中国希望关于《撒旦的诗篇》一书的事件能够尽快平息。
    在下午的新闻发布会上，有外国记者问，中国对霍梅尼下令处死《撒旦的诗篇》作者拉什迪一事有何评论。李肇星说，“我们一贯主张各国人民的宗教信仰和习俗应该得到尊重。我们也注意到，那本书的作者已经作出公开道歉。我们希望这个事件能够尽快平息下去。”
