Title: European Investment Bank Increases Loans
Author:
Date: 1989-02-04
Edition: 7

The European Investment Bank, an institution of the European Community, announced today in Brussels that its loan disbursements in 1988 exceeded 10 billion European Currency Units (ECU) for the first time, reaching 101.75 billion ECU, an increase of 30% compared to 1987.

Of these loans, nearly 95 billion ECU were disbursed within the Community, primarily to support regional development, infrastructure, industrial development, environmental protection, and other projects within the Community. The remaining 7 billion ECU were provided to Mediterranean countries and African, Caribbean, and Pacific nations that are signatories to the Lomé Convention. According to the bank, it raised nearly 77 billion ECU in the financial markets in 1988, an increase of approximately 37% compared to the 56 billion ECU raised in 1987.

The bank's leadership stated that in the coming years, the bank will continue to strive towards the goal of achieving a unified internal market within the Community by 1992.

### 欧洲投资银行放款增加  去年突破１００亿欧洲货币单位

1989-02-04
第7版(国际)
专栏：

　　欧洲投资银行放款增加
　  去年突破１００亿欧洲货币单位
    新华社布鲁塞尔２月２日电  欧洲共同体所属的欧洲投资银行今天在这里宣布，该行发放的贷款额，１９８８年首次突破１００亿欧洲货币单位（１欧洲货币单位现约合１．１１４美元），达到１０１．７５亿欧洲货币单位，比１９８７年增加３０％。
    在这些贷款中，近９５亿欧洲货币单位是在共同体内发放的，主要用于资助共同体的地区开发、基本建设、工业发展、环境保护等方面的一些项目。另外的７亿多欧洲货币单位是发放给地中海地区国家及参加《洛美协定》的非洲、加勒比和太平洋国家的。另据这家银行提供的数字，该行１９８８年在金融市场筹款近７７亿欧洲货币单位，比１９８７年的５６亿欧洲货币单位增加了约３７％。
    欧洲投资银行负责人表示，在今后几年中，该行将继续为１９９２年实现共同体内部统一大市场的目标作出努力。
