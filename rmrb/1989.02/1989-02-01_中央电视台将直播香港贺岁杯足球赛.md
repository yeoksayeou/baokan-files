Title: CCTV to Broadcast the Hong Kong New Year Cup Football Match
Author: Cen Chuanli
Date: 1989-02-01
Edition: 3

CCTV will broadcast live the Hong Kong New Year Cup football match. The match will kick off on the first day of the Lunar New Year in the afternoon. Two European teams and two Asian teams will participate, with the following four teams: the Chinese team, the Danish Odense team, the Swedish Malmo team, and the Hong Kong team.

This tournament is the first international football match that the Chinese team will participate in this year, and it is also a warm-up match before the Chinese team's participation in the 14th World Cup football qualifiers. In addition to facing the Hong Kong team again, the Chinese team will also compete against the Danish Odense team and the Swedish Malmo team for the first time. CCTV will broadcast live the three matches in which the Chinese team will participate. The schedule is as follows:

February 6, 16:30, China vs. Sweden Malmo
February 9, 18:30, China vs. Denmark Odense
February 12, 15:00, China vs. Hong Kong

### 中央电视台将直播香港贺岁杯足球赛
岑传理
1989-02-01
第3版(科技·文化·体育)
专栏：体育

　　中央电视台将直播香港贺岁杯足球赛
    本报讯  １９８９年香港贺岁杯足球精英赛农历正月初一下午鸣笛开战。两支欧洲球队和两支亚洲球队将出场献技，四支队伍是：中国队、丹麦欧登塞队、瑞典马尔摩队和香港队。
    这次比赛是中国队今年参加的第一次国际足球赛，也是中国队参加第１４届世界杯足球预选赛前的一次热身赛。中国队除与香港队再次交锋外，还将第一次与丹麦欧登塞队和瑞典马尔摩队交锋。中央电视台将现场直播中国队参加的三场比赛，时间如下：
    ２月６日１６点３０分，中国队—瑞典马尔摩队；
    ２月９日１８点３０分，中国队—丹麦欧登塞队；
　　２月１２日１５点，中国队—香港队。
　　　　　　　　　　（岑传理）
