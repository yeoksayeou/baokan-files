Title: Summit of Five Central American Countries Opens to Discuss Peace Process
Author: Hu Tairan, Song Xinde
Date: 1989-02-15
Edition: 6

The summit of five Central American countries opened on the morning of February 13 in the Sun Coast Hotel, located 65 kilometers outside the capital of El Salvador, San Salvador. The summit discussed the peace process in Central America. This is the fourth meeting of the five countries' leaders since 1986.

The presidents of El Salvador, Duarte, Costa Rica, Arias, Honduras, Azcona, Nicaragua, Ortega, and Guatemala, Cerezo attended the meeting. Duarte said in his opening speech that the purpose of this summit is to "seek solutions that are conducive to peace and progress in Central America."

After Duarte's speech, the meeting entered a secret discussion phase. It is reported that the main topics of this two-day meeting are to review the implementation of the Central American Peace Agreement reached by the five countries' leaders in Guatemala on August 5, 1987, and some new specific proposals put forward by the participating countries.

According to El Salvador's foreign minister, the five presidents reached a preliminary agreement on the establishment of a Central American Security Council during the morning meeting on the 13th. The meeting will conclude on the 14th.

### 中美洲五国首脑会议开幕  讨论中美洲和平进程问题
胡泰然　宋心德
1989-02-15
第6版(国际)
专栏：

　　中美洲五国首脑会议开幕
    讨论中美洲和平进程问题
    新华社太阳海岸（萨尔瓦多）２月１３日电（记者胡泰然、宋心德）中美洲５国首脑会议１３日上午在萨尔瓦多首都圣萨尔瓦多郊外６５公里处的太阳海岸旅馆开幕，讨论中美洲和平进程问题。这是１９８６年以来５国首脑举行的第４次会晤。
    萨尔瓦多总统杜阿尔特、哥斯达黎加总统阿里亚斯、洪都拉斯总统阿斯科纳、尼加拉瓜总统奥尔特加和危地马拉总统塞雷索出席了会议。杜阿尔特在开幕式上说，这次首脑会议的目的是“寻求有利于中美洲和平与进步的解决办法”。
    杜阿尔特致辞后，会议转入秘密讨论阶段。据透露，这次为期２天的会议主要议题是检查１９８７年８月５国首脑在危地马拉达成的中美洲和平协议的执行情况和与会国提出的一些新的具体建议。
    据萨尔瓦多外长１３日下午在记者招待会上透露，５国首脑在上午的会议上已就建立中美洲安全委员会问题初步达成协议。会议将于１４日结束。
