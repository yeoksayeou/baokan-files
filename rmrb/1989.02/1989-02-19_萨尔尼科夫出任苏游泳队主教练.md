Title: Sarnikov Appointed as Head Coach of the Soviet Swimming Team
Author:
Date: 1989-02-19
Edition: 3

According to a report from TASS, the renowned Soviet swimmer Vladimir Salnikov has recently been appointed by the Soviet State Sports Committee as the head coach of the Soviet national swimming team. Salnikov will turn 29 in May. He is the most famous swimmer in the Soviet Union. In the 1980 Olympics, he won gold medals in the men's 400-meter freestyle, 1500-meter freestyle, and the 4x200-meter freestyle relay. In the 1988 Seoul Olympics, he again won the gold medal in the men's 1500-meter freestyle. He was named the Soviet Union's best athlete of 1988.

### 萨尔尼科夫出任苏游泳队主教练

1989-02-19
第3版(科教·文化·体育)
专栏：体育

　　萨尔尼科夫出任苏游泳队主教练
    据塔斯社报道，苏联著名游泳运动员萨尔尼科夫最近被苏联国家体委任命为苏联国家游泳队主教练。萨尔尼科夫今年５月将满２９岁，是苏联泳坛最负盛名的选手。他在１９８０年奥运会上夺得男子４００米自由泳、１５００米自由泳和４×２００米自由泳接力３块金牌，在去年的汉城奥运会上他又夺得男子１５００米自由泳金牌。他被评选为１９８８年苏联最佳运动员。　　（据新华社）
