Title: UN Secretary-General Discusses Monitoring the Iran-Iraq Ceasefire
Author:
Date: 1989-02-05
Edition: 6

The United Nations Secretary-General, Javier Pérez de Cuéllar, on the 3rd proposed extending the activities of the UN Iran-Iraq Military Observer Group to monitor the ceasefire between Iran and Iraq until September 30th of this year.

This proposal is included in a report submitted by the Secretary-General to the Security Council regarding the activities of the Military Observer Group. The Military Observer Group was dispatched to Iran and Iraq to monitor the ceasefire on August 9th of last year and has been in place until February 2nd of this year.

In the report, Pérez de Cuéllar pointed out that the continued presence of the Military Observer Group in Iran and Iraq is an important condition for ensuring the full implementation of United Nations Resolution 598 on the settlement of the Iran-Iraq conflict.

### 联合国秘书长谈监督两伊停火

1989-02-05
第6版(国际)
专栏：国际新闻分析

　　联合国秘书长谈监督两伊停火
    联合国秘书长佩雷斯·德奎利亚尔三日建议把联合国两伊军事观察团监督两伊停火的活动延长至今年九月三十日。
    这项建议包括在秘书长提交给安理会的一份关于军事观察团活动的报告中。军事观察团于去年八月九日被派往两伊监督停火，直到今年二月二日为止。
    德奎利亚尔在报告中指出，军事观察团继续留在伊朗和伊拉克是保证彻底贯彻联合国关于解决两伊冲突的五百九十八号决议的重要条件。
