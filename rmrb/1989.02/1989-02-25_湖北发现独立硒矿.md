Title: Independent Selenium Mine Discovered in Hubei
Author: Han Yacong
Date: 1989-02-25
Edition: 2

The Hubei Provincial Bureau of Geology and Mineral Resources' Second Geological Brigade has, after more than a year of field geological surveys, recently discovered an independent selenium mine covering an area of more than 60 square kilometers in the Shuanghe Mountain area of western Hubei. According to records, the discovery of an independent selenium mine is unprecedented worldwide.

Selenium is a metalloid, similar in properties to sulfur, with a melting point of 220°C and a boiling point of 685°C. It is the most ideal semiconductor material. Its conductivity increases by thousands of times under light compared to in the dark. Therefore, it has extremely broad applications in industry and defense.

### 湖北发现独立硒矿
韩亚卿
1989-02-25
第2版(国内新闻)
专栏：四面八方

　　湖北发现独立硒矿
    本报武汉２月２４日电  湖北省地矿局第二地质大队通过一年多的野外地质勘查，最近在鄂西双河山区发现一处方圆６０多平方公里的独立硒矿矿床。据资料记载，独立的硒矿矿床在世界上尚属首次发现。
    硒是半金属类，性质与硫相似，熔点２２０℃，沸点为６８５℃，是最理想的半导体材料。它在光照下的导电性比在黑暗中成千倍地增加，因此，在工业、国防中有极其广泛的用途。（韩亚卿）
