Title: Returning to Business After Leaving Politics
Author:
Date: 1989-02-18
Edition: 6

Former U.S. Secretary of State George Shultz has returned to the Bechtel Group, headquartered in California, where he will serve as a company consultant.

Shultz served as a senior manager at Bechtel from 1974 to 1982. When he took over as Secretary of State in the Reagan administration in 1982, he was the president of Bechtel.

Shultz is currently also a member of the board of directors of General Motors and a member of the board of directors of Chevron.

### 离开官场　重操实业  美前国务卿舒尔茨履新职

1989-02-18
第6版(国际)
专栏：

　　离开官场　重操实业
    美前国务卿舒尔茨履新职
    新华社纽约２月１６日电  美国前国务卿舒尔茨又回到了总部设在加利福尼亚州的比奇特尔集团公司，兼任公司顾问。
    舒尔茨在１９７４—１９８２年期间是该公司的高级经理。当他１９８２年出任里根政府国务卿时，是比奇特尔公司总裁。
    舒尔茨目前还是通用汽车公司董事会成员，以及雪佛龙公司董事会成员。
