Title: The European Community and Japan Sign a Scientific Research Cooperation Agreement
Author:
Date: 1989-02-22
Edition: 3

The European Community Commission announced that the Community and Japan signed an agreement today in this city to cooperate in the field of controlled nuclear fusion research.

This is the first formal scientific research cooperation agreement signed between the two sides. According to this agreement, the Community and Japan will carry out scholar exchanges, information sharing, and material exchanges in the field of controlled nuclear fusion research.

### 欧共体和日本签订科研合作协定

1989-02-22
第3版(国际新闻)
专栏：

　　欧共体和日本签订科研合作协定
    新华社布鲁塞尔２月２０日电  欧洲共同体委员会宣布，共同体与日本今天在这里就双方在可控核聚变研究领域进行合作签订一项协定。
    这是欧日双方签订的第一个正式的科研合作协定。根据这项协定，共同体和日本将在可控核聚变研究方面开展学者互访、情报交流和材料交换。
