Title: Premier Li Peng Meets with Australian Minister for Foreign Affairs and Trade
Author:
Date: 1989-02-01
Edition: 1

Premier Li Peng Meets with Australian Minister for Foreign Affairs and Trade
	Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, January 31st - Premier Li Peng met with the visiting Australian Minister for Foreign Affairs and Trade, Gareth Evans, and his delegation this afternoon in the Zhongnanhai's Ziguangge.

	Li Peng told the guests that China, like Australia, has the desire to further develop comprehensive cooperation between the two countries. He hoped that both sides would make joint efforts for this purpose.

	Evans expressed that the two countries should continue to expand the scope of economic cooperation. He hoped that the Australia-China Ministerial Economic Joint Committee, scheduled to be held in April this year, could conduct constructive discussions on bilateral economic cooperation.

	During the meeting, Evans conveyed the warm greetings from Australian Prime Minister Bob Hawke to Li Peng. Li Peng also asked Evans to convey his greetings to Hawke.

	Li Peng and Evans also exchanged views on major international issues and regional situations.

	At the request of the guests, Li Peng introduced the current situation of China's efforts to rectify the economic environment, rectify economic order, and deepen comprehensive reforms.

	Foreign Minister Qian Qichen was present during the meeting.

	It is understood that the Evans delegation will leave Beijing tomorrow afternoon to conclude their visit to China.

### 李鹏会见澳外交贸易部长

1989-02-01
第1版(要闻)
专栏：

　　李鹏会见澳外交贸易部长
    新华社北京１月３１日电  国务院总理李鹏今天下午在中南海紫光阁会见了正在这里访问的澳大利亚外交贸易部长加里思·埃文斯一行。
    李鹏总理对客人说，中国和澳大利亚一样，有进一步发展中澳全面合作的愿望。他希望双方为此共同做出努力。
    埃文斯表示，中澳两国应继续扩大经济合作领域。他希望，将于今年４月召开的澳中部长级经济联合委员会能就双边经济合作进行建设性的讨论。
    会见时，埃文斯转达了澳大利亚总理罗伯特·霍克对李鹏的亲切问候，李鹏也请埃文斯转达他对霍克的问候。
    李鹏和埃文斯还就当前重大国际问题和地区局势交换了意见。
    应客人的要求，李鹏介绍了中国目前治理经济环境、整顿经济秩序、全面深化改革的情况。
    会见时，中国外交部长钱其琛在座。
    据悉，埃文斯一行将于明天下午离京结束访华。
