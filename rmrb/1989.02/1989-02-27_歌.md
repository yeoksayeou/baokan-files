Title: Song
Author: Yu Yao
Date: 1989-02-27
Edition: 8

	Song
	Yu Yao

	Fragments of blue sky drift across the lake's surface
	How can I praise this life's perfection
	New buds mingled with frost-kissed withered leaves
	How can I seek this harmony of nature
	The closed heart has opened wide
	Soul, do not linger outside
	Why so insistently sing the praises of life
	Life's meaning is half owned by maggots
	Why so ardently love the earth
	The earth quietly nurtures a batch of poisons
	The scales of ideals have gone awry
	Balance must be adjusted from the soul
	I store happiness in my memories
	But it has frozen, turned into a bone-chilling ice
	I bury pain deep in my heart
	But it sprouts, grows into a dream of joy
	The soul can wrap around eternity
	In hand, only a second
	With fearless passion, I chase the sun
	In its dazzling light, I lose my way
	Countless times I give myself to the future
	In dreams, it kisses me; awake, it casts me off to the present
	The structure of life shifts slowly
	How can the soul's song be clear

　　歌
    于尧
    湖面上飘洒着蓝天的碎片
    我怎样礼赞这生活的美满
    新芽中夹杂着经霜的枯叶
    我如何寻找这自然的和谐
    紧闭的心扉已经洞开
    灵魂　不要在外徘徊
    为什么那么执著地歌颂生命
    生命的意义有一半属于蛆虫
    为什么那么热烈地爱恋泥土
    泥土悄悄地养育着一批毒物
    理想的天平已经失灵
    平衡　该从灵魂调整
    我把幸福放在记忆中贮存
    却凝固了　变成一块砭骨的冰
    我把痛苦埋在心灵深处
    却发芽了　长出一支欢乐的梦
    灵魂可以裹住永恒
    手里　只握一秒钟
    以无畏的热情　我追逐太阳
    在耀眼的光芒里　我迷失了方向
    千次百次我把自己交给未来
    梦中它吻我　醒时抛给现在
    人生的结构缓缓错动
    灵魂的歌声怎能透明
