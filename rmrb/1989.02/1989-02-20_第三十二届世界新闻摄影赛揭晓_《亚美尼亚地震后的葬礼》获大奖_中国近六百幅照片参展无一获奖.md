Title: The 32nd World Press Photo Contest Announced: "Funeral After the Armenian Earthquake" Wins Top Prize; Nearly 600 Chinese Photos Exhibited, None Awarded
Author:
Date: 1989-02-20
Edition: 3

The 32nd World Press Photo Contest concluded today in Amsterdam. David Turnley, a photographer from the American newspaper "Detroit Free Press," won the top prize with his photograph "Funeral After the Armenian Earthquake."

Nearly 1,300 photographers from 62 countries participated in this year's contest.

The contest featured nine categories: Spot News, General News, People in the News, Sports, Daily Life, Contemporary Issues, Nature, Portrait, and Spot News Stories. The winners in these categories came from Belgium, Czechoslovakia, France, West Germany, Ireland, the Netherlands, Switzerland, the United Kingdom, the United States, and the Soviet Union.

China sent nearly 600 photographs from 137 photographers, ranking second in terms of quantity, but none of the entries won an award.

### 第三十二届世界新闻摄影赛揭晓  《亚美尼亚地震后的葬礼》获大奖  中国近六百幅照片参展无一获奖

1989-02-20
第3版(国际新闻)
专栏：

　　第三十二届世界新闻摄影赛揭晓    
    《亚美尼亚地震后的葬礼》获大奖    
    中国近六百幅照片参展无一获奖
    据新华社阿姆斯特丹２月１７日电  第３２届世界新闻摄影比赛今天在这里结束。美国《底特律自由新闻报》摄影记者大卫·特恩利拍摄的《亚美尼亚地震后的葬礼》获得本届比赛的大奖。
    ６２个国家的近１３００名摄影记者的作品参加了本届摄影比赛。
    本届比赛的突发新闻、新闻特写、新闻人物、体育、幽默、艺术、科技、自然和日常生活等９个单项奖分别被比利时、捷克斯洛伐克、法国、联邦德国、爱尔兰、荷兰、瑞士、英国、美国和苏联的摄影记者获得。
    中国送展１３７名摄影记者的近６００幅照片，数量居第二，但无一中奖。
