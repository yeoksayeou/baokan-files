Title: Benazir Bhutto Leaves Shanghai to Return Home
Author: ZHU Yunlong CHEN Maodi
Date: 1989-02-14
Edition: 1

Benazir Bhutto Leaves Shanghai to Return Home

    Benazir Bhutto concluded her official friendly visit to China and departed from Shanghai tonight to return home. The Chinese government's accompanying delegation, led by the Minister of the Textile Industry, Wu Wenying, and the Mayor of Shanghai, Zhu Rongji, saw her off at the airport.

    Today at noon, the Shanghai Municipal Government held a banquet to warmly welcome Benazir Bhutto and her delegation. This afternoon, Benazir Bhutto visited the Shanghai First Silk Dyeing Factory and watched a fashion show by the factory's fashion performance team.

### 贝·布托离沪回国
朱云龙　陈毛弟
1989-02-14
第1版(要闻)
专栏：

　　贝·布托离沪回国
    新华社上海二月十三日电  （记者朱云龙、陈毛弟）巴基斯坦总理贝·布托圆满结束对中国的正式友好访问，今天晚上乘专机离开上海回国。中国政府陪同团团长、纺织工业部部长吴文英，上海市市长朱熔基到机场送行。
    今天中午，上海市人民政府举行宴会，热烈欢迎贝·布托总理一行。贝·布托今天下午参观了上海第一丝绸印染厂，观看了这家厂时装表演队的表演。
