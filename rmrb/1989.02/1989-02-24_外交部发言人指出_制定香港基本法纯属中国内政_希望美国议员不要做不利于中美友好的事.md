Title: Spokesperson of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs: Drafting of the Hong Kong Basic Law is Purely China's Internal Affair
Author:
Date: 1989-02-24
Edition: 1

	Foreign Ministry Spokesperson: Drafting of the Hong Kong Basic Law is Purely China's Internal Affair
	Hopes U.S. Congressmen Do Not Do Things Harmful to Sino-U.S. Friendship

	New China News Agency Beijing, February 23 — Foreign Ministry Spokesperson Li Zhaoxing said today that drafting the Hong Kong Special Administrative Region Basic Law is purely China's internal affair, and no country has the right to interfere.

	At today's afternoon press conference, Li Zhaoxing made the above comments regarding a report by a Hong Kong newspaper that some U.S. Congressmen are planning to propose a resolution criticizing the draft of the Hong Kong Special Administrative Region Basic Law.

	He said, it is well known that Hong Kong will become a Special Administrative Region of the People's Republic of China with a high degree of autonomy starting from July 1, 1997. The policies of the Chinese government toward the Hong Kong Special Administrative Region will be stipulated in legal form through the drafting of the Hong Kong Special Administrative Region Basic Law. The current draft of the Basic Law has been formed through a very democratic procedure, widely soliciting opinions. The Basic Law will provide legal guarantees for the democratic development of Hong Kong after 1997.

	He hopes that the U.S. Congressmen will not do anything harmful to Sino-U.S. friendship.

### 外交部发言人指出  制定香港基本法纯属中国内政  希望美国议员不要做不利于中美友好的事

1989-02-24
第1版(要闻)
专栏：

　　外交部发言人指出
    制定香港基本法纯属中国内政       
    希望美国议员不要做不利于中美友好的事
    新华社北京２月２３日电  外交部发言人李肇星今天说，制定香港特别行政区基本法纯属中国的内政，任何国家无权干预。
    在今天下午的新闻发布会上，李肇星就香港一家报纸报道美国一些国会议员正酝酿提出一项抨击正在草拟中的香港特别行政区基本法的决议案一事发表了上述评论。
    他说，众所周知，香港自１９９７年７月１日起将成为中华人民共和国的一个具有高度自治权的特别行政区。中国政府对香港特别行政区的方针政策将通过香港特别行政区基本法的制定，以法律形式规定下来。目前的基本法草案是经过一个十分民主的程序，广泛征询意见而形成的。基本法将对１９９７年后香港的民主发展提供法律保障。
    他希望美国的议员先生们不要做出任何不利于中美友好的事情来。
