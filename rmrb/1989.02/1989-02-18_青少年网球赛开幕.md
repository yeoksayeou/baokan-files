Title: Youth Tennis Tournament Opens
Author:
Date: 1989-02-18
Edition: 3

The "March Forward Cup" National Youth Tennis Tournament opened today in Kunming. Due to issues with funding and transportation, only 70 out of the originally planned 260 athletes were able to participate.

### 青少年网球赛开幕

1989-02-18
第3版(科教·文化·体育)
专栏：体育

　　青少年网球赛开幕
    据新华社昆明２月１７日电  全国“进军杯”青少年网球赛今天在昆明开幕。由于经费、交通等问题的影响，使得原定的２６０名选手只有７０人前来参赛。
