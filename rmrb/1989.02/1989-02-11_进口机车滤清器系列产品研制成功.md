Title: Imported Locomotive Filter Series Products Successfully Developed
Author: Lin Mu
Date: 1989-02-11
Edition: 2

To meet the demand for parts for imported locomotives, the Anping County Machinery Parts Factory in Hebei Province recently successfully developed the "Imported Locomotive Filter Series Products" and has begun mass production. After inspection by relevant departments, the product's various indicators have all reached the standards of similar foreign products from the 1980s.

### 进口机车滤清器系列产品研制成功
林木
1989-02-11
第2版(经济)
专栏：

    进口机车滤清器系列产品研制成功
  　为满足进口机车的配件需求，河北省安平县机械配件厂近日研制成功“进口机车滤清器系列产品”，并开始批量生产。经有关部门检验，产品各项指标均达到国外８０年代同类产品的标准。
    　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　（林木）
