Title: The 3rd National Excellent Sports News Works (Foshan Chemical Fiber Cup) Selection Begins
Author:
Date: 1989-02-26
Edition: 4

The 3rd National Excellent Sports News Works (Foshan Chemical Fiber Cup) selection has begun. The Chinese Sports Journalists Association recently decided that, in addition to local sports journalists associations and capital news organizations recommending excellent news works according to regulations, individuals engaged in sports reporting and writing can also recommend their own works that meet the selection criteria to the committee. The recommended works should be from July 1987 to December 1988, including written, broadcast, and television works.

### 全国体育好新闻评选开始

1989-02-26
第4版(社会生活·体育)
专栏：体育

　　全国体育好新闻评选开始
    本报讯  第三届全国优秀体育新闻作品（佛山化纤杯）评选工作已经开始。中国体育记者协会日前作出决定，除各地体育记协和首都各新闻单位按规定数目推荐优秀新闻作品外，从事体育报道、写作的个人，也可自我向评委会推荐符合评选规定的新闻作品。推荐的应是一九八七年七月到一九八八年十二月这期间的文字、广播和电视作品。
