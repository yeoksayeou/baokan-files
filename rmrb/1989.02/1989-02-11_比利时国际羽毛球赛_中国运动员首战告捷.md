Title: Chinese Athletes Win First Match in Belgium International Badminton Tournament
Author: Liú Yǒngshèng
Date: 1989-02-11
Edition: 3

The Chinese team won their first match in the Belgium International Badminton Tournament held in Antwerp, Belgium. Liu Zhiheng defeated Danish player Niels Skjold in the men's singles, with a score of 15-9, 3-15, and 15-11. In the evening's mixed doubles, Zhang Qiang and Shi Wen easily defeated the Dutch pair of D. Maike and F. Estelle with a score of 15-8 and 15-1.

The Belgium International Badminton Tournament officially began yesterday. Yesterday's competition included a day of preliminaries. As a strong team, China's team can directly participate in the official competition. The Belgium International Badminton Tournament is held annually in Antwerp, and this year marks the 10th anniversary. This is the third time the Chinese team has participated in this tournament.

### 比利时国际羽毛球赛  中国运动员首战告捷
刘永胜
1989-02-11
第3版(科教·文化·体育)
专栏：体育

　　比利时国际羽毛球赛
    中国运动员首战告捷
    新华社安特卫普２月９日电 （记者刘永胜）在比利时安特卫普市举行的国际羽毛球大奖赛中，中国队今天首战告捷。刘志衡在男子单打中战胜了丹麦选手尼尔斯·斯凯比，比分为１５∶９、３∶１５和１５∶１１。在晚间的混合双打中，张强和施文以１５∶８和１５∶１的比分轻取荷兰的代·马艾凯和菲·埃斯泰尔。
    比利时国际羽毛球大奖赛是昨天正式开始的。昨天进行了一天预赛，中国队因属强队，可直接参加正式比赛。比利时国际羽毛球大奖赛每年在安特卫普市举行，今年是第１０届。中国队今年是第三次参加这项大奖赛的。
