Title: Foreign Affairs Brief
Author:
Date: 1989-02-28
Edition: 2

	Central Military Commission member and Chief of the General Staff of the People's Liberation Army of China, General Deng Huaodian, departed Beijing on the afternoon of February 27th to visit Pakistan and Bangladesh at the invitation of the respective countries.

	Peng Chong, Vice Chairman of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress, met with the Minister of Health of the United Arab Emirates, Maitefu, and his delegation on February 27th.

	Rei Jieqiong, Vice Chairman of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress, met and hosted a banquet for the delegation of the Guatemalan Parliament led by Roberto Alejos Cambara, Chairman of the Special Affairs Committee of the Guatemalan Parliament, on the evening of February 25th at the Great Hall of the People.

	Rei Jieqiong stated that although China and Guatemala have not yet established diplomatic relations, this does not hinder exchanges between the parliaments. Both sides expressed their willingness to increase contact and mutual understanding.

	The Guatemalan guests arrived in Beijing on February 24th at the invitation of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress for a friendly visit.

### 外事简讯

1989-02-28
第2版(国内新闻)
专栏：

　　外事简讯
    ▲中央军委委员、中国人民解放军总参谋长迟浩田上将２月２７日下午离京应邀前往巴基斯坦、孟加拉国进行友好访问。
    ▲全国人大常委会副委员长彭冲２月２７日会见了阿拉伯联合酋长国卫生部长迈特法一行。
    ▲全国人大常委会副委员长雷洁琼２５日晚在人民大会堂会见并宴请了以危地马拉议会特别事务委员会主席罗伯托·阿莱霍斯·坎巴拉为团长的危地马拉议会代表团。
    雷洁琼说，中危两国目前还没有建交，但这不妨碍议会之间的来往。双方表示今后要增加接触和相互了解。
    危地马拉客人应人大常委会邀请来华进行友好访问，于２４日抵达北京。
　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　（据新华社）
