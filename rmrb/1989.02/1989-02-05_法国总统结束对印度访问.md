Title: French President Ends Visit to India
Author:
Date: 1989-02-05
Edition: 6

French President François Mitterrand concluded his four-day state visit to India today and departed from the western coastal city of Mumbai to return to his home country.

Yesterday, Mitterrand held a press conference where he stated that his discussions with Indian leaders had resulted in enhanced bilateral understanding and the identification of areas for cooperation. Both sides agreed to collaborate in the fields of nuclear power plants, space, aviation, communications, vaccines, and biotechnology.

On February 1, India and France signed a five-year biotechnology cooperation agreement.

### 法国总统结束对印度访问

1989-02-05
第6版(国际)
专栏：

　　法国总统结束对印度访问
    新华社新德里２月４日电  法国总统密特朗结束了对印度为期４天的国事访问后，今天离开印度西海岸城市孟买回国。
    昨天，密特朗在记者招待会上说，他同印度领导人会谈的成果是加强了双边谅解和确定了合作领域。双方将在核电站、空间、航空、通讯、疫苗和生物等方面进行合作。
    ２月１日，印度和法国签署了一项为期５年的生物科技合作协定。
