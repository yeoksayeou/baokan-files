Title: Provincial and Military Leaders Send Condolences for the Passing of Panchen Vice Chairman
Author:
Date: 1989-02-02
Edition: 4

The Standing Committee of the National People's Congress and the Panchen Vice Chairman's Funeral Committee received condolence telegrams from the party and government leadership of Qinghai, Sichuan, and Yunnan provinces, expressing deep sorrow over the passing of Panchen Erdeni Qojue Gyantsen, Vice Chairman.

The Communist Party of China Qinghai Provincial Committee, the Standing Committee of the Qinghai Provincial People's Congress, the Qinghai Provincial People's Government, and the Qinghai Provincial Committee of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference sent a telegram to the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress and the Tibetan Autonomous Region leadership, stating, "We were shocked to learn that Vice Chairman Panchen Rinpoche passed away due to a sudden heart attack and despite efforts to save him, he unfortunately passed away. We, the party and government leadership of Qinghai Province and all the people of Qinghai, express our deep sorrow and grief."

The telegram said, "Qinghai is the hometown of Vice Chairman Panchen. He visited Qinghai many times to inspect and guide the work, paying great attention to the various undertakings of our province, especially the development of the national economy, strengthening national unity, cultivating and using minority cadres, and implementing the party's policies on nationality and religion. The people of Qinghai have deep feelings for Panchen Rinpoche. We mourn Vice Chairman Panchen by following the leadership of the Party Central Committee and the State Council, earnestly implementing the line, principles, and policies of the Thirteenth National Congress of the Communist Party of China, striving to develop the economy, maintain the unity of all nationalities, safeguard national unity, and wholeheartedly serve the people of all nationalities to build a prosperous, wealthy, democratic, and civilized Qinghai!"

The Communist Party of China Sichuan Provincial Committee, the Standing Committee of the Sichuan Provincial People's Congress, the Sichuan Provincial People's Government, and the Sichuan Provincial Committee of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference sent a telegram saying, "With deep sorrow, we mourn the passing of Vice Chairman Panchen Erdeni Qojue Gyantsen. Panchen Rinpoche was a great patriot, a renowned statesman, and an outstanding religious leader of the Tibetan people. His passing is a great loss to our country and to all the people of the nation. We feel deeply saddened. Please convey our sincere condolences to the family of Vice Chairman Panchen."

The Communist Party of China Yunnan Provincial Committee, the Standing Committee of the Yunnan Provincial People's Congress, the Yunnan Provincial People's Government, and the Yunnan Provincial Committee of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference sent a telegram saying, "We were shocked to learn of the passing of Vice Chairman Panchen Erdeni Qojue Gyantsen, Honorary President of the Buddhist Association of China. We express our deep sorrow. Panchen Rinpoche was a great patriot and a renowned statesman of our country. His passing is a great loss to the people of all nationalities in China. The people of Yunnan will carry forward the spirit of Vice Chairman Panchen, who loved the Communist Party of China and the socialist motherland, and with decisive actions to safeguard national unity and ethnic unity, and to promote socialist modernization, we will mourn Panchen Rinpoche."

Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, February 1: The General Staff Department, the General Political Department, and the General Logistics Department of the People's Liberation Army today sent a telegram to the Panchen Vice Chairman's Funeral Committee, expressing deep sorrow over the passing of Vice Chairman Panchen. The full text of the telegram is as follows:

Panchen Erdeni Qojue Gyantsen Vice Chairman's Funeral Committee:

We were shocked to learn of the sudden passing of Vice Chairman Panchen. Panchen Rinpoche's life was dedicated to his country, and his name will be remembered in history. We express our deep sorrow over his passing and extend our sincere condolences to his family.

The General Staff Department, the General Political Department, and the General Logistics Department of the People's Liberation Army
February 1, 1989

### 青川滇等省党政领导机关致电全国人大常委会  沉痛哀悼班禅副委员长逝世  总参总政总后沉痛致唁电

1989-02-02
第4版(政治·法律·社会)
专栏：

　　青川滇等省党政领导机关致电全国人大常委会
    沉痛哀悼班禅副委员长逝世
    总参总政总后沉痛致唁电
    新华社北京２月１日电  全国人大常委会和班禅副委员长治丧委员会分别收到青海、四川、云南等省党政领导机关的唁电，对班禅额尔德尼·确吉坚赞副委员长逝世表示沉痛哀悼。
    中共青海省委、青海省人大常委会、青海省人民政府、青海省政协在致全国人大常委会和西藏自治区领导机关的唁电中说，惊悉全国人大常委会副委员长班禅大师因心脏病突发，抢救无效，不幸逝世，我们青海省党政领导机关和全省各族人民表示十分沉痛和哀悼。
    唁电说，青海是班禅副委员长的故乡，他生前曾多次来青海视察指导工作，对我省的各项事业特别是发展民族经济，加强民族团结，培养和使用少数民族干部，贯彻党的民族政策和宗教政策等方面的工作给予了极大的关注。青海各族人民对班禅大师怀有深厚的感情。我们悼念班禅副委员长，就是要在党中央、国务院的领导下，认真贯彻执行党的十三大的路线、方针、政策，努力发展经济，维护各族人民的团结，维护祖国统一，全心全意地为各族人民服务，为建设繁荣、富裕、民主、文明的青海而奋斗！
    中共四川省委、四川省人大常委会、四川省人民政府、政协四川省委员会的唁电说，我们怀着极其沉痛的心情，深切悼念班禅额尔德尼·确吉坚赞副委员长不幸逝世。班禅大师是伟大的爱国主义者、著名的国务活动家和藏族人民杰出的爱国宗教领袖。他的逝世，是我们国家的一个重大损失，是全国各族人民的一个重大损失。我们感到十分悲痛。请向班禅副委员长的亲属转致我们的诚挚的慰问。
    中共云南省委、云南省人大常委会、云南省人民政府、云南省政协的唁电说，惊悉全国人大常委会副委员长、中国佛教协会名誉会长班禅大师病逝，对此我们表示十分沉痛的哀悼。班禅大师是我国伟大的爱国主义者、著名的国务活动家，他的逝世，是我国各族人民的一个重大损失。云南各族人民，将发扬班禅副委员长热爱中国共产党、热爱社会主义祖国的精神，以坚决维护祖国统一和民族团结，搞好社会主义现代化建设的实际行动来悼念班禅大师。
    新华社北京２月１日电　人民解放军总参谋部、总政治部、总后勤部今天致电班禅副委员长治丧委员会，对班禅副委员长逝世，表示沉痛哀悼。唁电全文如下：班禅额尔德尼·确吉坚赞副委员长治丧委员会：
    惊悉班禅副委员长猝然病逝，不胜痛惜。班禅大师一生爱国，名垂青史。对他的逝世谨表示沉痛的哀悼，并向其亲属表示诚挚的慰问。
      　　中国人民解放军总参谋部、总政治部、总后勤部
        　　１９８９年２月１日
