Title: Egypt and Israel Sign Agreement to Resolve the Taba Issue
Author:
Date: 1989-02-28
Edition: 3

Egypt and Israel Sign Agreement to Resolve the Taba Issue
March 15 Set as Taba Return Date
Israeli Troops to Withdraw Completely from Taba by Then

New China News Agency, Cairo, February 26 - Egypt and Israel signed three agreements here tonight to finally resolve the Taba issue.

According to the agreements, Israel will withdraw completely from Taba by March 15 (the Taba return date), Israeli citizens will no longer need visas to enter Taba in the future, and Egypt will compensate the owners of hotels and resorts in Taba, which belong to Israel, with $38.1 million.

During the 1967 Middle East War, Israel occupied the Egyptian Sinai Peninsula, including Taba. In 1982, Israel withdrew from the Sinai Peninsula according to the 1979 Egypt-Israel Peace Treaty, but refused to return this one-square-kilometer beach area to Egypt. In September 1988, the International Arbitration Commission ruled that Israel should return the Taba area to Egypt, but Israel has been slow to implement the international arbitration. (Photo attached)

On February 26, Egypt and Israel signed three agreements in Taba, with Israel agreeing to return Taba to Egypt by March 15, thus ending the seven-year border dispute between the two countries. This is the Egyptian (left) and Israeli (middle) negotiators shaking hands in celebration.

### 埃以签署协议塔巴问题解决  定三月十五日为塔巴归还日  届时以色列军全部撤出塔巴

1989-02-28
第3版(国际新闻)
专栏：

　　埃以签署协议塔巴问题解决
    定三月十五日为塔巴归还日 
    届时以色列军全部撤出塔巴
    新华社开罗２月２６日电  埃及和以色列今天晚上在这里签署了关于最终解决塔巴问题的３项协议。
    根据协议，以色列在３月１５日（塔巴归还日）前全部撤出塔巴，以色列公民今后不用签证可以进入塔巴，埃及将向以色列在塔巴的旅馆和度假村的拥有者支付３８１０万美元的赔偿。
    １９６７年中东战争中，以色列占领了包括塔巴在内的埃及西奈半岛。１９８２年以色列根据１９７９年埃以和平条约撤出了西奈半岛，但拒绝将这块仅有一平方公里的海滩地带归还埃及。１９８８年９月国际仲裁委员会裁定，以色列应该把塔巴地区归还埃及，但以色列对国际仲裁迟迟未能执行。 （附图片）
     2月26日，埃及和以色列在塔巴签署3项协议，以色列将于3月15日前把塔巴归还埃及，从而结束两国长达7年之久的边界争端。这是埃及（左）和以色列（中）谈判代表握手祝贺。
                             新华社发（传真照片）
