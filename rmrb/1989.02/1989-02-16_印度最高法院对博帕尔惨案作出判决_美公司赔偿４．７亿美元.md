Title: Indian Supreme Court Rules on Bhopal Disaster, US Company to Pay $470 Million in Compensation
Author:
Date: 1989-02-16
Edition: 1

The Indian Supreme Court today ruled that the American Union Carbide Company should compensate $470 million for the damages caused by the gas leak incident at its subsidiary in Bhopal in December 1984. The court ordered the American company to pay the compensation by March 31 as the final settlement of the incident.

The spokesperson for the American company has already expressed acceptance of the Indian court's decision, describing it as "fair and reasonable."

On December 3, 1984, the pesticide factory of the company in Bhopal leaked methyl isocyanate gas, killing over 3,000 people and affecting tens of thousands.

### 印度最高法院对博帕尔惨案作出判决  美公司赔偿４．７亿美元

1989-02-16
第1版(要闻)
专栏：

　　印度最高法院对博帕尔惨案作出判决
    美公司赔偿４．７亿美元
    据新华社新德里２月１４日电  印度最高法院今天要求美国联合碳化物公司为其在印度博帕尔市的子公司于１９８４年１２月发生毒气渗漏事故赔偿４．７亿美元的损失，作为事故的最后解决办法。法庭要求美国公司于３月３１日前付清这笔赔款。
    据悉，美国这家公司的发言人已表示接受印度法院的决定，并认为决定是“公正和合理的”。
    １９８４年１２月３日，位于印度博帕尔市的这家公司的农药厂渗漏出异氰酸甲酯毒气，使３０００多人死亡，数十万人受到影响。
