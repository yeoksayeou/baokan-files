Title: Chinese People's Association for Friendship with Foreign Countries Holds a Reception to Celebrate the 10th Anniversary of the Establishment of Diplomatic Relations Between China and Portugal
Author:
Date: 1989-02-04
Edition: 4

The Chinese People's Association for Friendship with Foreign Countries held a reception at the Beijing Hotel tonight to celebrate the 10th anniversary of the establishment of diplomatic relations between China and Portugal.

Vice Chairman of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress, Liao Hansheng, attended the reception.

President of the Chinese People's Association for Friendship with Foreign Countries, Zhang Wenjin, and Portuguese Ambassador to China, Nitu Valerio, spoke at the reception. They noted that in the 10 years since the establishment of diplomatic relations, official relations and people-to-people exchanges between China and Portugal have made satisfactory progress. They expressed the belief that with the satisfactory resolution of the Macao issue between China and Portugal, friendly relations between the two countries will enter a new phase in an atmosphere of international harmony.

Relevant officials from the Chinese government and civil organizations, as well as Portuguese friends in Beijing, attended the reception.

### 对外友协举行招待会  庆祝中葡建交１０周年

1989-02-04
第4版(政治·法律·社会)
专栏：

　　对外友协举行招待会
　  庆祝中葡建交１０周年
    新华社北京２月３日电  中国人民对外友好协会今晚在北京饭店举行招待会，庆祝中国和葡萄牙建交１０周年。
    全国人大常委会副委员长廖汉生出席了招待会。
    对外友协会长章文晋和葡萄牙驻中国大使内图·瓦莱里奥在招待会上讲话时指出，建交１０年来，中葡两国的官方关系和民间往来都取得了令人满意的进展。他们表示相信，随着中葡两国圆满地解决澳门问题，两国的友好关系将在国际和睦的气氛中进入未来。
    中国政府和民间的有关单位负责人以及在北京的葡萄牙朋友出席了招待会。
