Title: A War That Should Never Have Happened Finally Ends
Author: Wang Xianju
Date: 1989-02-16
Edition: 6

A war that should never have happened finally ends

    The Soviet troops have completely withdrawn from Afghanistan

    Xinhua News Agency, Termez (Soviet Union), February 15 - (Reporter Wang Xianju) The commander of the Soviet troops in Afghanistan, General Gromov, returned to Termez in the Uzbek Soviet Socialist Republic at 9:55 Moscow time this morning from the Afghan border city of Hairatan. Thus, the Soviet Union has fulfilled its commitment to withdraw all its troops from Afghanistan by the deadline, as stipulated in the Geneva Accords signed last April, ending its nine-year and one-month military presence in Afghanistan.

    Gromov has been appointed as the commander of the Kiev Military District.

### 一场不该发生的战争终于结束  苏军全部撤出阿富汗
王宪举
1989-02-16
第6版(国际)
专栏：

　　一场不该发生的战争终于结束    
    苏军全部撤出阿富汗
    新华社铁尔梅兹（苏联）２月１５日电  （记者王宪举）驻阿富汗苏军司令格罗莫夫中将今天上午莫斯科时间９点５５分从阿富汗边境城市海拉顿回到苏联乌兹别克共和国的铁尔梅兹镇。至此，苏联按期履行了去年４月签署的关于政治解决阿富汗问题的日内瓦协议，全部撤走了在阿富汗的军队，从而结束了它在阿富汗的长达９年零１个多月的军事存在。
    格罗莫夫已被任命为基辅军区司令。
