Title: World Youth Football Championship Approaching: Brazilian Young Stars Aim for Third Consecutive Victory
Author:
Date: 1989-02-11
Edition: 3

The World Youth Football Championship is approaching. The Brazilian youth football team will depart from here today to Saudi Arabia to participate in the championship, which will be held from February 16 to March 3. The Brazilian team has won the championship twice in previous World Youth Championships and aims to win the title for the third time. The team is filled with stars, including Franca, Anderson, Leonardo, and Rogerio, who all play in top professional clubs.

Brazilian coach Simons believes that the strongest opponents for the Brazilian team in the World Youth Championship will be the Soviet Union and Argentina.

### 世界青年足球锦标赛在即  巴西小将志在第三次夺魁

1989-02-11
第3版(科教·文化·体育)
专栏：体育

　　世界青年足球锦标赛在即   
    巴西小将志在第三次夺魁
    新华社巴西利亚二月九日电  巴西青年足球队今天离开这里前往沙特阿拉伯，参加二月十六日至三月三日举行的世界青年足球锦标赛。巴西队在以往的世青赛中曾经两次获得冠军，此番意在第三次夺冠。巴西队中名将云集，弗兰萨、安德森、莱昂纳多、罗热里奥等都在一流的职业俱乐部中踢球。
    巴西队教练西蒙斯认为，巴西队在世青赛中最强硬的对手将是苏联队和阿根廷队。
