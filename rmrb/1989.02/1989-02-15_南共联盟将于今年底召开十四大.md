Title: League of Communists of Yugoslavia to Hold 14th Congress by Year-End
Author:
Date: 1989-02-15
Edition: 6

The 14th Congress of the League of Communists of Yugoslavia will be held from December 15 to 20 this year.

According to a report by Tanjug, this was revealed by the secretary of the Central Committee of the League of Communists of Yugoslavia, Stipe Šuvak, on the 13th during a discussion with the secretaries of the Central Committees of the republics, autonomous provinces, and the People's Army.

The 13th Congress of the League of Communists of Yugoslavia was held in June 1986.

### 南共联盟将于今年底召开十四大

1989-02-15
第6版(国际)
专栏：国际新闻简报

　　南共联盟将于今年底召开十四大
    南共联盟第十四次代表大会将在今年十二月十五日至二十日举行。
    据南通社报道，这是南共联盟中央主席团书记科罗舍茨十三日同各共和国、自治省和人民军共盟组织的主席团书记谈话时透露的。
    南共联盟十三大是一九八六年六月举行的。
