Title: Memorial Service for Huang Qingqu Held in Guangzhou
Author: Wang Chuanzhen
Date: 1989-02-05
Edition: 4

	Memorial Service for Huang Qingqu Held in Guangzhou

	Xinhua News Agency, Guangzhou, February 3rd - (Reporter Wang Chuanzhen) The memorial service for Huang Qingqu, Vice Chairman of the Central Committee of the China Zhi Gong Party and Chairman of the Guangdong Provincial Committee, and Vice Chairman of the Guangdong Provincial Political Consultative Conference, was held this morning at the Guangzhou Funeral Parlor. Huang Qingqu passed away on January 23rd due to acute myocardial infarction while on duty, despite efforts to save him. He was 59 years old.

	Leaders such as Yan Mingfu, Zhou Gucheng, Rong Yiren, Ye Fei, Song Jian, and Xu Jiatun sent wreaths or telegrams of condolence.

	Guangdong Provincial Governor Ye Xuanping presided over the memorial service. Provincial Political Consultative Conference Chairman Wu Nansheng delivered the eulogy. He said, Huang Qingqu was from Longyan, Fujian Province. Before 1949, he studied and worked in Indonesia, Singapore, and other places. In 1949, he returned to China via Hong Kong. He had served as a lecturer, associate professor, and associate researcher, among other positions. From 1984 to the end of 1987, he served as the Vice Governor of Guangdong Province, overseeing science and technology and overseas Chinese affairs. He made significant contributions to promoting the reform of the science and technology system in the entire province, driving the economic development of the coastal regions of Guangdong, and safeguarding the legitimate rights and interests of overseas Chinese, returned overseas Chinese, and relatives of overseas Chinese.

	Leaders from the Guangdong Provincial Committee of the Communist Party of China, the Guangdong Provincial People's Government, the Standing Committee of the Guangdong Provincial People's Congress, responsible persons from various democratic parties in Guangdong Province, and people from all walks of life, totaling more than 800, attended the memorial service.

### 黄清渠追悼会在广州举行
王传真
1989-02-05
第4版(政治·法律·社会)
专栏：

　　黄清渠追悼会在广州举行
    新华社广州２月３日电  （记者王传真）中国致公党中央委员会副主席兼广东省委员会主任委员、广东省政协副主席黄清渠追悼会今天上午在广州殡仪馆举行。黄清渠是１月２３日在工作岗位上因急性心肌梗塞，经抢救无效不幸逝世的，终年５９岁。阎明复、周谷城、荣毅仁、叶飞、宋健、许家屯等领导同志送了花圈或发来了唁电。
    广东省省长叶选平主持了追悼会。省政协主席吴南生致悼词。他说，黄清渠同志是福建省龙岩市人，１９４９年之前先后在印度尼西亚、新加坡等地读书、做工，１９４９年经香港回国，曾任讲师、副教授、副研究员等职，１９８４年至１９８７年底期间担任广东省副省长，主管科技和侨务工作，为促进全省科技体制改革，推动广东沿海地区的经济发展，维护华侨、归侨、侨眷的合法利益作出了较大贡献。
    中共广东省委、广东省人民政府、广东省人大常委会的领导干部、各民主党派广东省组织的负责人，以及各界人士等共８００多人出席了追悼会。
