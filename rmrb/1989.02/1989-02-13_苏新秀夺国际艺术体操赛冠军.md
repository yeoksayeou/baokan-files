Title: Su Xinxiu Wins International Rhythmic Gymnastics Championship
Author: Li Zongyang
Date: 1989-02-13
Edition: 3

16-year-old Soviet newcomer Larisa Medvedeva made her debut today at the "Daily Mirror" International Rhythmic Gymnastics Championships held here, securing the all-around championship with a total score of 38.25 points.

Two Bulgarian girls competed fiercely with Medvedeva. Ultimately, 19-year-old Boryana Gineva and 15-year-old Mila Marinova took the second and third places, respectively.

Athletes from Britain, Bulgaria, China, Czechoslovakia, West Germany, France, Japan, the Soviet Union, Spain, the United States, and Yugoslavia participated in the competition. China's Wu Yuyu ranked 12th with a total score of 36.35 points.

### 苏新秀夺国际艺术体操赛冠军
李宗扬
1989-02-13
第3版(科教·文化·体育)
专栏：体育

　　苏新秀夺国际艺术体操赛冠军
    新华社伦敦２月１１日电  （记者李宗扬）１６岁的苏联新秀拉里莎·梅德韦杰娃今天下午在这里的“每日镜报”国际艺术体操赛上崭露头角，以３８．２５分的总成绩夺取了这一赛事的全能冠军。
    两名保加利亚姑娘与梅德韦杰娃进行了激烈的争夺。结果，１９岁的博里亚娜·吉涅娃和１５岁的米拉·玛里诺娃分居第２、３位。
    来自英国、保加利亚、中国、捷克斯洛伐克、西德、法国、日本、苏联、西班牙、美国和南斯拉夫的１３名选手参加了这一比赛。中国的吴苑宇名列第１２，总分为３６．３５分。（附图片）
    拉里莎在绳操比赛中。新华社记者  许如元摄
