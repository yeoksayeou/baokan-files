Title: Argentine Police Recover Stolen Masterpiece
Author:
Date: 1989-02-28
Edition: 3

Argentine police on February 26 recovered a masterpiece stolen last year, valued at eight million dollars, titled "Portrait of Philip II."

The painting, created by the 16th-century Spanish artist Alonso Sánchez Coello, was originally housed in the art museum in Rosario. In March 1987, the painting, along with four other masterpieces, was stolen.

### 阿警方查获失盗名画

1989-02-28
第3版(国际新闻)
专栏：

　　阿警方查获失盗名画
    阿根廷警方二十六日在布宜诺斯艾利斯城查获去年被盗、价值达八百万美元的名画《费莉佩二世肖像》。
    这幅画出自十六世纪西班牙画家阿隆索·桑切斯·科埃略之手，原收藏在罗萨里奥城艺术博物馆。一九八七年三月，该画与其它四幅名画一起被人盗走。
　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　（据新华社）
