Title: Conjoined Twin Girls Live Three Days Before Passing Away
Author:
Date: 1989-02-25
Edition: 2

A pair of conjoined twin girls born in Urumqi lived for only three days before passing away on February 19 at the hospital. The cause of their early death was that both girls had cleft lips and palates, making it difficult for them to suckle and unable to feed.

### 连体女婴活三天夭折

1989-02-25
第2版(国内新闻)
专栏：四面八方

　　连体女婴活三天夭折
    本报讯  在乌鲁木齐出生的一连体女婴，只度过三天寿命后，２月１９日在医院夭折。连体女婴早逝原因，系两女婴均有唇裂腭裂，吸吮困难，无法吸奶。
