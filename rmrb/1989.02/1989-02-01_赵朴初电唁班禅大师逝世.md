Zhao Pu Chu Cables Condolences on the Passing of the Panchen Lama

New China News Agency Hong Kong, January 31st: Vice Chairman of the National Committee of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference Zhao Pu Chu, who is currently in Hong Kong, sent a telegram to the Chinese Buddhist Association and forwarded it to the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress today, expressing deep sorrow over the passing of the Panchen Lama. The full text of the telegram is as follows:

Chinese Buddhist Association, forwarded to the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress:

I have just returned from Australia to the country and am in Hong Kong to inspect the Tian Tan Buddha project. I learned of the passing of the Panchen Lama. The Lama devoted his life to the country and the religion, and his passing is a great loss. Please convey my condolences to the family of the Lama.

Zhao Pu Chu
Hong Kong
January 31st, 1989

### 赵朴初电唁班禅大师逝世

1989-02-01
第4版(政治·法律·社会)
专栏：

　　赵朴初电唁班禅大师逝世
    新华社香港１月３１日电  正在香港的全国政协副主席赵朴初今天致电中国佛教协会并转全国人大常委会，对班禅大师逝世表示沉痛哀悼。唁电全文如下：中国佛教协会转全国人大常委会：
    顷自澳洲回国过港视察天坛大佛工程，始悉班禅大师示寂。大师为国为教，尽瘁一生，日坠中天，曷胜震悼。请代向经师遗属致唁。
    　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　赵朴初于香港
　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　１９８９年１月３１日
