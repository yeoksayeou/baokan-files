Title: Lech Walesa Says Solidarity Union Will Not Be a Political Organization After Legalization
Author:
Date: 1989-02-25
Edition: 3

Lech Walesa, the former chairman of the Solidarity Union, said today that the Solidarity Union will "not be a political organization" after it is legalized.

Walesa made this assurance today at a press conference in Gdansk to government officials and people from all walks of life. The Polish Press Agency reported his remarks.

He also said that the future Solidarity Union will "act rationally, effectively, and creatively" to benefit the working class and the broad masses of laborers.

Walesa believes that the current situation in Poland is quite difficult, and it is necessary for all Poles to understand the country's predicament.

### 瓦文萨说  团结工会合法后将不是政治组织

1989-02-25
第3版(国际新闻)
专栏：

　　瓦文萨说
    团结工会合法后将不是政治组织
    新华社华沙２月２３日电  波兰前团结工会主席瓦文萨今天说，合法后的团结工会“将不是政治组织”。
    瓦文萨是今天在格但斯克举行的记者招待会上向当局和社会各界人士作上述保证的。波通社报道了他的这一讲话。
    他还说，今后的团结工会将“理智地、有效地和创造性地进行活动，”为工人阶级和广大劳动人民谋福利。
    瓦文萨认为，目前波兰国内形势是相当困难的，需要全体波兰人理解国家的困境。
