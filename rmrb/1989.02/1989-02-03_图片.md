Title: Chemical Explosion in Budapest Factory
Author:
Date: 1989-02-03
Edition: 6

On February 1, a chemical explosion occurred in a factory in the Hungarian capital of Budapest, resulting in the deaths of two people and injuries to 15 others. The blast also destroyed 800 square meters of the factory building. The image shows rescue workers at the scene of the explosion. (Photo provided by Xinhua News Agency)
