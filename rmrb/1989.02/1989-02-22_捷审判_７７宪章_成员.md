Title: Czechoslovakia Trials Members of "Charter 77"
Author:
Date: 1989-02-22
Edition: 3

The District 3 Court in Prague, the capital of Czechoslovakia, held a public trial on the morning of the 21st for playwright and member of the "Charter 77" movement, Václav Havel. Havel was arrested during a demonstration held on January 16th in Prague to commemorate the 20th anniversary of the self-immolation of student Jan Palach, who had protested against the Soviet invasion of Czechoslovakia in 1968.

### 捷审判“７７宪章”成员

1989-02-22
第3版(国际新闻)
专栏：

　　捷审判“７７宪章”成员
    捷克斯洛伐克首都布拉格３区法院于２１日上午开庭公审剧作家、“７７宪章运动”成员哈维尔。哈维尔是于今年１月１６日在布拉格举行的纪念大学生帕拉赫为抗议苏联１９６８年出兵捷克斯洛伐克而自焚２０周年的示威活动中被捕的。    　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　
　　　　　　　　　　　　　　（新华社）
