Title: 1988 National Light Industry "Can-Durable Cup" Good News Awards Announced
Author: Qing Wen
Date: 1989-02-02
Edition: 2

This year's awards featured 39 winning entries. These works reported on various aspects of the new achievements, current status, and some new problems facing China's light industry, timely conveying important information about light industry production. Among them, "Over 100 Light Industry Enterprises Become Bonded Factories," "The Dilemma and Way Out for Salt," "The Idle Equipment Problem in China's Battery Industry Needs Attention," and "Increasing Effective Supply in the Light Industry Market" won first prizes.

### １９８８年全国轻工业“可耐杯”好新闻评选揭晓
庆闻
1989-02-02
第2版(经济)
专栏：

  　▲１９８８年全国轻工业“可耐杯”好新闻评选揭晓　这次评选共有３９篇作品获奖。这些作品从不同角度报道了我国轻工业发展的新成就、状况及面临的一些新问题，及时传递了轻工业生产的重要信息。其中，《百余家轻工企业成为保税厂》、《食盐的困惑与出路》、《我国电池行业设备闲置问题需引起注意》、《增加轻工市场的有效供给》等获一等奖。　
  　　　（庆闻）
