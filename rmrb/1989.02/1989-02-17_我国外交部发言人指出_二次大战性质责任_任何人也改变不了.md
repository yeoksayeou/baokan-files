Title: China's Foreign Ministry Spokesperson: The Nature and Responsibility of World War II Cannot Be Altered
Author:
Date: 1989-02-17
Edition: 1

China's Foreign Ministry spokesperson Li Zhaoxing today stated that no one can change the nature and responsibility of the Second World War.

At a press conference held this afternoon, he said: "The nature of that war of aggression, which brought immense disaster to the Chinese and Asian peoples, and the responsibility for the war are historical facts that no one can alter."

According to reports, on February 14, Japanese Cabinet Legislation Bureau Director Ajimuji Haruo stated in a parliamentary response that, whether from domestic law or international law, Emperor Hirohito bears no war responsibility. On the same day, Japanese Prime Minister Takeshita Noboru said: "Whether the last war was an act of aggression should be evaluated by historians of the future."

### 我国外交部发言人指出  二次大战性质责任  任何人也改变不了

1989-02-17
第1版(要闻)
专栏：

　　我国外交部发言人指出    
    二次大战性质责任    任何人也改变不了
    新华社北京２月１６日电  中国外交部发言人李肇星今天在这里指出，谁也改变不了第二次世界大战的性质和战争责任。
    在今天下午举行的新闻发布会上，他说：“那场给中国和亚洲人民带来深重灾难的侵略战争和战争的责任是历史事实，谁也不可能改变。”
    据报道，日本内阁法制局长官味村治２月１４日在国会答询时说，不论是从国内法，还是从国际法看，裕仁天皇都没有战争责任。同一天，日本首相竹下登说：“上次大战是不是侵略，应由后世历史学家做评价。”
