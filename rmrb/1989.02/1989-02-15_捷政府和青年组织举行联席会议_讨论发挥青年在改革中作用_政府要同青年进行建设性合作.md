Title: Czechoslovak Government and Youth Organizations Hold Joint Meeting
Author:
Date: 1989-02-15
Edition: 7

The Czechoslovak government and the Socialist Youth Union held a joint meeting in Prague today to discuss how to leverage the role of young people in economic reforms and other issues.

This is the first time the new government, formed on October 12 last year, has held a meeting with youth organizations to study youth issues.

At the meeting, the chairman of the Socialist Youth Union Central Committee, Mohorita, reported on the contributions made by young people in scientific and technological activities and environmental management. He also requested that the government address issues related to housing, education, employment, and training for young people.

Czechoslovak Prime Minister Adamets spoke at the meeting. He expressed his hope that young people would actively participate in social and economic reforms and stated that the government would treat young people as equal partners, engaging in constructive cooperation with them.

At the end of the meeting, the Czechoslovak government and the Socialist Youth Union Central Committee adopted a resolution on urgent issues facing the younger generation in social reforms. The resolution states that to accelerate social reforms, the Czechoslovak government and the Socialist Youth Union Central Committee will create conditions to improve the living and working conditions of the younger generation, and actively promote their role in the political, socio-economic, and cultural development of the country.

### 捷政府和青年组织举行联席会议  讨论发挥青年在改革中作用  政府要同青年进行建设性合作

1989-02-15
第7版(国际)
专栏：

　　捷政府和青年组织举行联席会议    
    讨论发挥青年在改革中作用    
    政府要同青年进行建设性合作
    新华社布拉格二月十三日电  捷政府和社会主义青年联盟中央书记处今天在布拉格举行联席会议，讨论如何发挥青年人在经济改革中的作用等问题。
    这是新政府自去年十月十二日组成以来，第一次同青年组织召开研究青年问题的会议。
    在这次会议上，社会主义青年联盟中央主席莫霍里塔介绍了青年在参加科技活动、治理环境等方面所作的贡献，并要求政府解决青年人的住房、学习、就业、培训等方面存在的问题。
    捷政府总理阿达麦茨在会上发表了讲话。他希望青年人能积极参加社会经济改革，并表示政府要把青年人看作是关系平等的伙伴，同他们进行富有建设性合作。
    会议结束时，通过了捷政府和社会主义青年联盟中央书记处关于青年一代在社会改革中的迫切问题的决议。决议说，为了加速社会改革，捷政府和社会主义青年联盟中央将创造条件，改善青年一代的生活和工作条件，积极发挥他们在国家政治、社会经济和文化发展方面的作用。
