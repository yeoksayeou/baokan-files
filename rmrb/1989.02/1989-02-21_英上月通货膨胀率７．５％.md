Title: UK Inflation Rate at 7.5% for Last Month
Author:
Date: 1989-02-21
Edition: 3

	According to official figures released by the UK, the annual inflation rate for January has risen from 6.8% in December last year to 7.5%. This is the highest level in six and a half years.

### 英上月通货膨胀率７．５％

1989-02-21
第3版(国际新闻)
专栏：

　　英上月通货膨胀率７．５％
    据英国官方公布，英国１月份的通货膨胀年率已从去年１２月的６．８％上升到７．５％。这是六年半来的最高水平。　（本报专电）
