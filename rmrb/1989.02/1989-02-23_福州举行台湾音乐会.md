Fuzhou Hosts Taiwan Music Concert

According to Xinhua News Agency, Fuzhou, February 22 (Reporter Jing Yan) The first Taiwan composer's works concert, organized by the Fujian Provincial Cultural Bureau, was held last night at the Taiwan Grand Theater in Fuzhou. Renowned Taiwanese female pianist Chen Shuzhen and mainland famous conductor Han Zhongjie were invited to perform as the piano soloist and conductor, respectively.

The concert featured six pieces, most of which are beloved by the Taiwanese people and rich in national characteristics, including the orchestral piece "Chang'e Flies to the Moon" and the piano ensemble "Three Melodies of Homesickness."

The image shows the internationally renowned Taiwanese pianist Chen Shuzhen performing "Love Sickness" and four other pieces.

### 福州举行台湾音乐会
景延  黄兴泉
1989-02-23
第2版(国内新闻)
专栏：四面八方

　　福州举行台湾音乐会
    据新华社福州２月２２日电  （记者景延）由福建省文化厅举办的首次台湾作曲家作品音乐会昨晚在福州市台湾大戏院举行。著名台湾女钢琴家陈淑贞，大陆著名指挥家韩中杰应邀为音乐会分别担任钢琴主奏和指挥。
    这场音乐会共演奏６首作品，大都是深受台湾人民喜爱的富有民族特色的乐曲，其中有管弦乐曲《嫦娥奔月》、钢琴重奏《乡愁三调》等。（附图片）
