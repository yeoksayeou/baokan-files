Title: Calgary Awarded the Olympic Cup
Author:
Date: 1989-02-17
Edition: 3

Calgary Awarded the Olympic Cup

The city of Calgary in Canada was awarded the Olympic Cup by the International Olympic Committee for its outstanding hosting of the 15th Winter Olympic Games. On the 13th, IOC President Juan Antonio Samaranch presided over the award ceremony. The IOC, in its news release, described the Calgary Winter Olympics as one of the most successful in the history of the Winter Games.

		(According to Xinhua News Agency)

### 卡尔加里市获奥林匹克奖杯

1989-02-17
第3版(科教·文化·体育)
专栏：体育

　　卡尔加里市获奥林匹克奖杯
    加拿大卡尔加里市因出色地主办了第１５届冬季奥运会而荣获国际奥委会颁发的奥林匹克奖杯。１３日，国际奥委会主席萨马兰奇主持了颁奖仪式。国际奥委会在其新闻公报中称卡尔加里冬季奥运会为冬季奥运会史上极其成功的一届。
    　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　（据新华社）
