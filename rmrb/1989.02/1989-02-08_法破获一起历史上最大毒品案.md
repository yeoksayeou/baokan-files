Title: France Busts Largest Drug Case in History
Author:
Date: 1989-02-08
Edition: 3

France’s police on the 6th uncovered a major drug case in Nevers, seizing 471 kilograms of cocaine. This is the largest drug bust in French history.

The drugs were discovered on a ship, which was towed by a specialized vehicle to a parking lot along the Loire River. Three individuals suspected of transporting the drugs have been arrested by the police.

### 法破获一起历史上最大毒品案

1989-02-08
第3版(国际)
专栏：国际要闻简报

　　法破获一起历史上最大毒品案
    法国警方六日在涅夫勒破获一起毒品案，共查获四百七十一公斤可卡因。这是法国历史上最大的一起毒品案。
    这批毒品是在一艘船上发现的。当时一辆专用车将这艘船拖运到卢瓦尔河畔的一个停车场上。涉嫌运送毒品的三个人已被警方逮捕。
　　　　　　　　　（据新华社）
