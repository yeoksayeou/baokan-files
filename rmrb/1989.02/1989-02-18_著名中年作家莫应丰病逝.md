Title: Renowned Middle-Aged Writer Mo Yingfeng Passes Away
Author:
Date: 1989-02-18
Edition: 3

Renowned middle-aged writer Mo Yingfeng passes away
    The People's Daily reports: Member of the Chinese Writers Association, Vice Chairman of the Chinese Writers Association Hunan Branch, Member of the Hunan Provincial Federation of Literary and Art Circles Party Group, First Teahouse Literary Award winner, and renowned writer Mo Yingfeng passed away in Changsha on February 17 due to illness, aged 51.

    During his illness, Mo Yingfeng received warm care and support from the Hunan Provincial Committee of the Communist Party of China, the Hunan Provincial Government, the Chinese Writers Association, and the China Literature Foundation, among other relevant parties.

    Mo Yingfeng's major works include the novel "General's Song" and others.

### 著名中年作家莫应丰病逝

1989-02-18
第3版(科教·文化·体育)
专栏：

　　著名中年作家莫应丰病逝
    本报讯  中国作家协会理事、中国作协湖南分会副主席、湖南省文联党组成员、首届茅盾文学奖获得者、著名作家莫应丰同志，因病医治无效，于２月１７日在长沙逝世，终年５１岁。
    莫应丰患病期间，得到中共湖南省委、湖南省政府和中国作协、中华文学基金会等有关方面的亲切关怀和照顾。
    莫应丰的主要作品有长篇小说《将军吟》等。
