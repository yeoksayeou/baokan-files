Title: Famous Folk Dancer Zhou Guobao Passes Away
Author:
Date: 1989-02-27
Edition: 2

    Famous folk dancer Zhou Guobao, a member of the Chinese Dancers Association and a renowned Yange (a traditional folk dance) artist from Changli County, Hebei Province, passed away on February 17 due to illness. He was 85 years old.

    Zhou Guobao was known as a famous Yange artist in his youth, earning the nickname "Running Donkey Zhou." He co-created and performed the Yange dance "Running Donkey," which was awarded an excellent performance at the first National Folk Music and Dance Festival. The performance was also filmed and screened nationwide. Additionally, it won a silver medal at the Fourth World Youth Festival.

### 秧歌艺人周国宝逝世

1989-02-27
第2版(国内新闻)
专栏：

　　秧歌艺人周国宝逝世
    新华社石家庄２月２６日电  我国著名民间舞蹈家、中国舞蹈家协会会员、河北省昌黎县著名秧歌艺人周国宝先生，于２月１７日因病逝世，享年８５岁。
    周国宝先生年轻时就成了闻名遐迩的地秧歌艺人，人称“跑驴周”。他与人合作创作演出的地秧歌《跑驴》，在全国第一届民间音乐、舞蹈汇演大会上被评为优秀节目，并被拍成艺术片在全国放映，还在第四届世界青年联欢节演出中获银质奖章。
