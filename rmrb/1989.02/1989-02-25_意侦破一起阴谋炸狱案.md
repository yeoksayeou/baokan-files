Title: Italian Authorities Foil a Plot to Bomb a Prison
Author:
Date: 1989-02-25
Edition: 3

Italian carabinieri recently uncovered a plot to bomb a prison near the Roma Termini railway station, and three suspects have been arrested.

According to the investigation, the target prison was the Rebibbia prison located in the western suburbs of Rome. The aim of the bombing was to facilitate the escape of accomplices held there, including some notorious terrorists.

### 意侦破一起阴谋炸狱案

1989-02-25
第3版(国际新闻)
专栏：

　　意侦破一起阴谋炸狱案
    意大利宪兵近日在罗马火车站附近侦破一起企图炸狱案，３名案犯被抓获。
    警方根据调查结果认为，他们企图要炸的监狱是位于罗马西郊的雷比比亚监狱。他们炸监的目的是使关押在那里的同伙（其中包括某些恐怖分子要犯）得以越狱逃跑。　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　
　　　（据新华社）
