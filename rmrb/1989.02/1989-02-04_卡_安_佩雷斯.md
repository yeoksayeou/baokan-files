Title: Carlos Andres Perez
Author:
Date: 1989-02-04
Edition: 6

Carlos Andres Perez, the presidential candidate of the Democratic Action Party of Venezuela who won the elections on December 4 last year, took the oath of office on February 2, becoming the seventh democratically elected president of Venezuela since 1958.

Perez was born on October 27, 1922, in Rubio City, Tachira State. He studied law at the Central University of Venezuela from 1944 to 1947. After participating in the successful overthrow of the Medina dictatorship in October 1945, he served as the personal secretary and cabinet meeting secretary for the chairman of the revolutionary government council, Betancourt. From 1947 to 1948, he was elected as a member of the House of Representatives. He served as the Minister of the Interior from 1962 to 1963. From 1968 to 1972, he served as the general secretary of the Democratic Action Party. He was the president of Venezuela from March 1974 to March 1979. In August 1980, he was elected as the chairman of the executive committee of the Latin American Human Rights Association. He is currently the secretary of the Latin American region of the Socialist International.

### 卡·安·佩雷斯

1989-02-04
第6版(国际)
专栏：人物介绍

　　卡·安·佩雷斯
    在去年１２月４日大选中获胜的委内瑞拉民主行动党总统候选人、前总统卡洛斯·安德烈斯·佩雷斯２月２日宣誓就职，成为委内瑞拉自１９５８年以来的第七任民选总统。
    佩雷斯１９２２年１０月２７日生于塔奇拉州鲁比奥城。１９４４年—１９４７年就读于委内瑞拉中央大学法律系。１９４５年１０月参加推翻梅迪纳独裁政权成功后，任革命执政委员会主席贝坦科尔特的私人秘书和内阁会议秘书。１９４７—１９４８年当选为众议员。１９６２—１９６３年任内政部长。１９６８—１９７２年任民主行动党总书记。１９７４年３月—１９７９年３月任委内瑞拉总统。１９８０年８月当选为拉美人权协会执行委员会主席。现任社会党国际拉美地区书记。
　　　　   （据新华社）
