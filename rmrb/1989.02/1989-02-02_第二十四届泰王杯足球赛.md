Title: The 24th King's Cup Football Tournament
Author:
Date: 1989-02-02
Edition: 3

In the 24th King's Cup Football Tournament, South Korea and the Thai national team defeated Switzerland and Indonesia 2-0 and 3-0 respectively on the 30th. On the 31st of January, two strong contenders for the championship, the Danish team and the Soviet Union team, played to a 0-0 draw. The Democratic People's Republic of Korea team defeated the Thai second team 2-0.

### 第二十四届泰王杯足球赛
