Title: Radio Free Khmer Reveals Vietnam's Encroachment on Cambodian Territory
Author:
Date: 1989-02-03
Edition: 6

Radio Free Khmer reported that Vietnam has recently illegally moved border markers along the Cambodian-Vietnamese border into Cambodian territory, encroaching on Cambodian land.

The message broadcasted by Radio Free Khmer today stated that in the northeastern part of Cambodia's Mondulkiri Province, in the O'Reang district's Daddam area, the Vietnamese side moved the border markers 10 to 15 kilometers into Cambodian territory, reaching the Cambodian side of Highway 14. They aim to turn this Cambodian highway into a new border between the two countries. This indicates that Vietnam is using every means to annex Cambodian territory.

### 民柬电台揭露  越南正蚕食柬领土

1989-02-03
第6版(国际)
专栏：

　　民柬电台揭露
    越南正蚕食柬领土
    新华社北京２月２日电  据民柬国民军及当地群众报告，越南最近非法将柬越边界界碑朝柬埔寨一侧移动，蚕食柬领土。
    民柬电台今天广播的这则消息说，在柬埔寨东北部蒙多基里省奥拉英县达达姆地段，越方把界碑向柬领土移动了１０到１５公里，移至柬埔寨的１４号公路沿线，企图把这条柬境内的公路变成两国的新界。这表明越南正在千方百计侵吞柬埔寨领土。
