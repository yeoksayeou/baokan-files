Title: Our Winter Sports Athletes to Participate in the World University Winter Games
Author:
Date: 1989-02-16
Edition: 3

The 14th World University Winter Games will be held from March 2 to 12 this year in Sofia, the capital of Bulgaria. China will send athletes to compete in short-track speed skating, figure skating, cross-country skiing, and biathlon. It is reported that athletes from 33 countries and regions have registered to participate.

(According to Xinhua News Agency)

### 我冰雪健儿将参加世界大学生冬运会

1989-02-16
第3版(科教·文化·体育)
专栏：体育

　　我冰雪健儿将参加世界大学生冬运会
    第十四届世界大学生冬季运动会将于今年３月２日至１２日在保加利亚首都索非亚举行。我国将派运动员参加短跑道速滑、花样滑冰、越野滑雪和冬季两项４个项目的比赛。据悉，已有３３个国家和地区的选手报名参赛。
    　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　（据新华社）
