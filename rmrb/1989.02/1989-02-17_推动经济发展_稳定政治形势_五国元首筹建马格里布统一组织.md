Title: Promoting Economic Development and Stabilizing Political Situation: Five Heads of State Plan to Establish the Maghreb Unity Organization
Author: Lin Jiaoming
Date: 1989-02-17
Edition: 7

After drinking a bowl of fresh milk and eating two dates, the heads of state of the five Maghreb countries—Hassan II, Qaddafi, Chadli, Zine El Abidine Ben Ali, and Taya—entered the municipal hall of Marrakech at 2 p.m. today and sat around a round table to begin preparations for the second Maghreb Summit, aimed at establishing the Arab Maghreb Union.

Marrakech, a city with a 1,300-year history, was bustling today. Locals dressed in vibrant, contrasting traditional attire filled the streets, singing and dancing to welcome the heads of state of Algeria, Tunisia, Libya, and Mauritania.

The summit is scheduled to sign the establishment of the Arab Maghreb Union tomorrow, uniting the five countries of Northwest Africa in a confederation to promote economic development and political stability in the region, and to meet the challenges of the 1992 European Single Market.

At today's opening ceremony, King Hassan II delivered a welcome speech. He emphasized that the establishment of the Great Maghreb Organization is a major historical event for the region and will have a significant impact on the entire Arab nation. Qaddafi also spoke at the meeting.

### 推动经济发展　稳定政治形势  五国元首筹建马格里布统一组织
林皎明
1989-02-17
第7版(国际)
专栏：

　　推动经济发展　稳定政治形势   
    五国元首筹建马格里布统一组织
    本报马拉喀什２月１６日电  记者林皎明报道：今天下午２点，在喝过一碗鲜奶、吃两颗蜜糖椰枣后，马格里布五国元首哈桑二世、卡扎菲、沙德利、塔亚和本·阿里走进马拉喀什市政厅，坐在一张圆桌前，开始筹备成立阿拉伯马格里布统一组织的第２届马格里布首脑会议。
    拥有１３０年古都历史的马拉喀什今天沸腾了。身穿色彩鲜艳、对比强烈民族服装的当地居民倾城出动，丛拥在街道两旁，载歌载舞，欢迎阿尔及利亚、突尼斯、利比亚和毛里塔尼亚国家元首。
    本届首脑会议预定在明天签署成立阿拉伯马格里布统一组织，将西北非五国以邦联形式结合在一起，促进整个地区的经济发展和局势稳定，迎接１９９２年欧洲统一大市场的挑战。
    在今天的开幕式上，哈桑二世国王首先致欢迎词。他强调指出，大马格里布组织的建立，是本地区历史的重大事件，其意义将对整个阿拉伯民族产生影响。卡扎菲也在会上发了言。
