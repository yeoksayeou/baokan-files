Title: Soviet Player Wins International Women's Chess Championship
Author:
Date: 1989-02-15
Edition: 3

International grandmaster K. Alakamiya of the Soviet Union won the championship on February 12 in the international women's chess tournament held in Le Havre, France. In the final round, she narrowly defeated her compatriot and the previous champion, N. Gaprindashvili. Gaprindashvili finished second. This tournament was part of the international chess festival held in Le Havre.

(According to Xinhua News Agency)

### 苏选手获国际女子象棋冠军

1989-02-15
第3版(要闻)
专栏：体育

　　苏选手获国际女子象棋冠军
    国际特级大师、苏联的卡·阿拉卡米娅十二日在法国勒阿弗尔举行的国际女子象棋赛中夺得冠军。她在最后一轮比赛中，以微弱优势击败她的同胞、上届冠军诺·加普里达维莉。加普里达维莉名列第二。这次比赛是在勒阿弗尔举行的国际象棋节的一部分。
       　　　　　　　　                 （据新华社）
