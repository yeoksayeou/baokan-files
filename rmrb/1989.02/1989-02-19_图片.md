Title: Afghan Mujahideen Celebrate Soviet Withdrawal with Horse Race
Author:
Date: 1989-02-19
Edition: 6

On February 17, Afghan Mujahideen cavalry soldiers held a horse race in Peshawar, Pakistan, to celebrate the complete withdrawal of Soviet troops from Afghanistan. (Xinhua News Agency)
