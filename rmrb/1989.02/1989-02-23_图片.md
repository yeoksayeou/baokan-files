Title: Tangshan Automobile Factory Produces First Domestic Concrete Mixer Truck
Author: Yang Wendao
Date: 1989-02-23
Edition: 5

Tangshan Special Automobile Manufacturing Plant has utilized foreign advanced technology to develop and produce China's first-generation TSCZJ-6-ton concrete mixer truck, filling a gap in the domestic market. Over the past year since its production, the truck has been used by construction units and has met all technical standards comparable to international counterparts.

		Reporter Yang Wendao
