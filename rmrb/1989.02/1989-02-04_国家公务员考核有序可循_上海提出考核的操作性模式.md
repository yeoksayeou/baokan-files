Title: National Civil Servant Evaluations Follow a Clear Process: Shanghai Proposes an Operational Model for Evaluations
Author: Xiao Guan Gen
Date: 1989-02-04
Edition: 4

How to evaluate and assess national civil servants is a necessary condition for implementing the civil servant system. For this purpose, Shanghai Jiaotong University's School of Management has led a research project that provides a relatively complete evaluation model. After being simulated and tested by the Shanghai Municipal Finance Bureau and the Shanghai Chemical Industry Bureau, and Shanghai County, it has proven to be successful and effective.

The research, titled "Research on the Index System, Technical Standards, and Methods for Evaluating National Civil Servants," consists of a comprehensive report, seven sub-reports, and a set of reference training materials. It proposes a set of evaluation indicators, technical standards, and specific operational procedures that are basically suitable for the characteristics of administrative agencies, with a focus on work performance.

### 国家公务员考核有序可循  上海提出考核的操作性模式
萧关根
1989-02-04
第4版(政治·法律·社会)
专栏：

　　国家公务员考核有序可循
　  上海提出考核的操作性模式
    本报上海电  记者萧关根报道：如何测评、考察国家机关工作人员是推行公务员制的必备条件。为此，上海交通大学管理学院主持研究的成果提供了一套比较完整的考核模式，最近经过上海市财政局模拟试点和上海市化工局、上海县试点后证明是成功的、有效的。
    这项题为《国家机关工作人员测评的指标体系、技术标准和方法手段的研究》的成果，由一个总体报告、七个分报告和一套参考培训教材组成。它提出了一套基本上能够适应行政机关特点的以工作实绩为主的全面考核指标体系、技术标准和具体操作程序。
