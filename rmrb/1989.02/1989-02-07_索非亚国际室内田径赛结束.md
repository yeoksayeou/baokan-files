Title: The 20th Sofia International Indoor Athletics Meet Concludes
Author: Wáng Jīnbó
Date: 1989-02-07
Edition: 2

The 20th Sofia International Indoor Athletics Meet concluded here today after a day of intense competition. In the 21 events contested, athletes from Cuba and Bulgaria each won six gold medals.

A total of 254 athletes from the Soviet Union, Cuba, Austria, Poland, Yugoslavia, Hungary, Japan, Belgium, Bulgaria, and 11 other countries participated in the competition. Cuban athlete Jan Soto Mayor set a new meet record in the men's high jump with a height of 2.36 meters, surpassing the previous record of 2.31 meters. Bulgarian women's high jump newcomer Di Borysova cleared a height of 1.91 meters. Experts believe she has the potential to challenge the world women's high jump record. Bulgarian newcomer De Lessev set a new meet record in the pole vault with a height of 5.60 meters, surpassing the previous record of 5.55 meters.

### 索非亚国际室内田径赛结束
王金柏
1989-02-07
第2版(要闻)
专栏：体育

　　索非亚国际室内田径赛结束
    据新华社索非亚２月５日电（记者王金柏）在这里举行的第二十届大学生国际室内田径邀请赛经过一天的紧张比赛后于今天晚间结束。在总共２１个项目的角逐中，古巴和保加利亚选手分别摘走六项桂冠。
    苏联、古巴、奥地利、波兰、南斯拉夫、匈牙利、日本、比利时、保加利亚等１１个国家的２５４名选手参加了比赛。古巴的汉·索托马约尔以２米３６的成绩刷新了男子室内跳高的赛会纪录（２米３１）。保加利亚的女子跳高新秀迪·鲍里索娃跃过了１米９１的高度。专家们认为，她是有希望向世界女子跳高纪录挑战的强手。保加利亚新秀德·列赛夫以５米６０的成绩刷新了撑竿跳赛会纪录（５米５５）。
