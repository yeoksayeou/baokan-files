Title: Talent Fair
Author: Gu Zuowei
Date: 1989-02-22
Edition: 2

The 1989 session of Wuxi City's fair for college and university graduates seeking employment and enterprises seeking talent began on February 12. Over 15,000 people visited the "talent fair," and 230 enterprises in the city area posted notices seeking talented individuals. Within two days, 380 soon-to-be graduates found their "match."

The image shows a corner of Wuxi City's "talent fair."

    人才庙会
