Title: Mandela Receives European Parliament Human Rights Award
Author:
Date: 1989-02-21
Edition: 3

Nelson Mandela, the leader of the African National Congress in South Africa who is imprisoned by the South African authorities, was awarded the Sakharov Prize for Freedom of Thought by the European Parliament last Wednesday.

Mandela's 14-year-old grandson, Mandla Mandela, accepted the award on his behalf in Europe. The prize money was approximately $5,870.

### 曼德拉获欧洲议会人权奖

1989-02-21
第3版(国际新闻)
专栏：

　　曼德拉获欧洲议会人权奖
    被南非当局监禁的南非非洲人国民大会领导人纳尔逊·曼德拉上星期三被欧洲议会授予萨哈罗夫人权奖。
    曼德拉的１４岁孙子曼拉·曼德拉代表他前往欧洲领取了约５８７０美元的奖金。　　　　　　　　　　
　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　（新华社）
