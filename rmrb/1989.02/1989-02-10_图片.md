Title: Finnish Doctor Invents Medical Air Cushion
Author:
Date: 1989-02-10
Edition: 7

Finnish doctor Ilkka Toivio recently invented a type of medical air cushion. It can evenly support the patient's body, thereby preventing various patients who are bedridden for long periods from developing bedsores.
