Title: The Central Committee of the Communist Youth League and the Forestry Department of the National Afforestation Committee Decide to Launch the Excellent Youth Afforestation Project
Author: XIA DeLin
Date: 1989-02-20
Edition: 2

The Central Committee of the Communist Youth League, the National Afforestation Committee, and the Forestry Department have decided to launch the Excellent Youth Afforestation Project nationwide starting this year.

The Excellent Youth Afforestation Project will be carried out in afforestation projects at the county level and above. The conditions for an excellent youth afforestation project are as follows: (1) The project must have a comprehensive plan and design, with complete diagrams and text; (2) The project area at the provincial, municipal (city), and county levels should respectively reach 10,000 mu, 5,000 mu, and 1,000 mu, and should be relatively concentrated and contiguous; (3) After the project is completed, it must undergo inspection and acceptance by relevant business departments, with the quality meeting the standards, the survival rate of afforestation reaching 90% or above, and the retention rate being 85% or above.

### 团中央绿委会林业部决定  开展青年绿化创优活动
夏德林
1989-02-20
第2版(国内新闻)
专栏：

　　团中央绿委会林业部决定
    开展青年绿化创优活动
    本报讯  共青团中央、全国绿化委员会、林业部决定：今年起，在全国开展创建优秀青年绿化工程活动。
    青年绿化工程创优活动在县级以上绿化工程中开展。优秀青年绿化工程的条件是：（１）工程有规划设计，图表文字齐全；（２）省、地（市）、县级工程面积应分别达到１万亩、５０００亩和１０００亩以上，并相对集中连片；（３）工程竣工后必须经过有关业务部门检查验收，质量符合标准，造林成活率达到９０％以上，保存率在８５％以上。
　　　　　　　　　　　　　（夏德林）
