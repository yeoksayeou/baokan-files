Title: The "Taiwan, Hong Kong, and Macau Handbook" Will Be Published
Author:
Date: 1989-02-17
Edition: 3

A large reference book, the "Taiwan, Hong Kong, and Macau Handbook," will be published by Hua Yi Publishing House.

The book is divided into seven sections: an overview of the Taiwan, Hong Kong, and Macau regions; politics and society; economy; culture; history; notable figures; and important policies of the central government regarding Taiwan, Hong Kong, and Macau. It contains over 3,000 entries and more than 1 million words.

### 《台港澳手册》将出版

1989-02-17
第3版(科教·文化·体育)
专栏：

　　《台港澳手册》将出版
    据新华社北京电  一部大型工具书《台港澳手册》将由华艺出版社出版。
    全书分台港澳地区的概况、政治社会、经济、文化、历史、著名人物及中央对台、港、澳的重要政策等７方面的内容，共３０００多条目，１００余万字。
