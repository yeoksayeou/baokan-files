Title: Two Cuban Fishing Boats Collide and Sink
Author:
Date: 1989-02-14
Edition: 6

Cuba's Ministry of Fisheries issued a statement announcing that the Cuban fishing vessel "Rio Sasá" collided with a Taiwanese fishing boat in the southwestern waters of the Atlantic Ocean on the 11th, resulting in the sinking of the Cuban vessel. All 86 crew members on board were rescued.

The Cuban Ministry of Fisheries did not disclose whether the Taiwanese fishing boat was damaged.

According to another report, the Cuban merchant ship "Capitán San Luis" collided with a U.S. cruiser near Cuba's Holguín Province on the 10th, causing the Cuban ship to sink. The captain and two mechanics are missing, and 12 crew members were injured.

### 古两渔船与他船相撞沉没

1989-02-14
第6版(国际)
专栏：国际新闻简报

　　古两渔船与他船相撞沉没
    古巴渔业部发表公报宣布，古巴“里奥萨萨”号渔船十一日与中国台湾一艘渔船在大西洋西南部水域相撞沉没，船上全部八十六名船员获救。
    古巴渔业部没有透露台湾渔船是否受损。
    另据报道，古巴“圣路易斯船长”商船十日在古巴奥尔金省附近与一艘美国巡洋舰相撞沉没，船长和两位机械师失踪，十二名船员受伤。
    　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　（据新华社）
