Two boxing champions meet in Las Vegas to vie for supremacy: Tyson vs. Bruno draws attention

According to Xinhua News Agency in Washington on February 24 (reporter Lu Wenyue), the boxing match between world champion Tyson and British champion Bruno will take place at 10 PM on the 25th (11 AM on the 26th, Beijing time) at the Hilton Hotel in Las Vegas. Since this is Tyson's first fight in 8 months and Bruno's first challenge in the United States, the match has attracted considerable attention.

The famous "Home Box Office" cable television network (HBO) in the United States has paid $3.8 million for the live broadcast rights. The company's public relations director, Fox, told the press that this broadcast could bring in $20 million to $25 million for the company. The main source of income is the cable viewing fee, with each household paying $20 to watch the match. There are 17 million households in the United States that receive the company's cable programs, and Fox estimates that at least a million households will watch the match tonight.

As for the prospects of victory and defeat, boxing experts here generally believe that the 27-year-old Bruno cannot withstand Tyson's multiple blows, and the entire match will probably end in a few minutes. However, Bruno himself is full of energy. On the 23rd, he declared to the news media in New York that now is the best time to defeat Tyson.

### 两个拳王相会赌城一争高低  泰森同布鲁诺决战引人瞩目
陆文岳
1989-02-26
第4版(社会生活·体育)
专栏：体育

　　两个拳王相会赌城一争高低  泰森同布鲁诺决战引人瞩目
    据新华社华盛顿２月２４日电  （记者陆文岳）世界拳王泰森与英国拳王布鲁诺的一场拳击大较量定于２５日晚上１０时（北京时间２６日中午１１时）在美国赌城拉斯维加斯希尔顿大饭店举行。由于这是泰森８个月来首次亮拳，也是布鲁诺首次来美国挑战，所以格外引人瞩目。
    美国有名的“家庭票房公司”有线电视台（ＨＢＯ）已出价３８０万美元买下实况转播权。该公司公共负责人福克斯对报界说，这次转播将能使公司获取２０００万至２５００万美元。收入来源主要是有线收视费，每个家庭如收看这场比赛须付２０美元。美国共有１７００万个家庭接收该公司有线节目，福克斯估计收看明晚比赛的家庭少说也在百万以上。
    至于胜负前景，此间拳击界人士大多认为２７岁的布鲁诺经不住２２岁的泰森多少次猛击，整个比赛大概在十几分钟内便可结束。但布鲁诺本人劲头十足，２３日他在纽约向新闻界宣称，现在是斗倒泰森的最佳时机。
