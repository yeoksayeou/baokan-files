Title: Kim Il-sung: The Cause of Korean Unification Will Definitely Be Realized
Author:
Date: 1989-02-27
Edition: 3

Kim Il-sung: The Cause of Korean Unification Will Definitely Be Realized

	New China News Agency, Pyongyang, February 25th – On the 25th, the leader of the Korean Workers' Party and the state, Kim Il-sung, said at a banquet in Pyongyang welcoming Burundi President Pierre Buyoya that the overall situation in Korea is developing in a direction favorable to national reunification, and the cause of Korean unification will definitely be realized.

	Kim Il-sung said that due to the U.S. military occupation of South Korea and the activities of separatists to create "two Koreas," there are still obstacles and difficulties on the road to Korean reunification. It is necessary to use the strength of the entire nation to smash separatist activities and actively promote the situation, which is developing well, to complete the historic cause of national reunification.

	President Buyoya arrived in Pyongyang on the 25th.

### 金日成：朝鲜统一事业一定会实现

1989-02-27
第3版(国际新闻)
专栏：

　　金日成：朝鲜统一事业一定会实现
    新华社平壤２月２５日电  朝鲜党和国家领导人金日成２５日在平壤欢迎布隆迪总统皮埃尔·布约亚的宴会上说，朝鲜的全面形势正在向着有利于祖国统一的方向发展，朝鲜的统一事业一定要实现。
    金日成说，由于美国军队霸占南朝鲜和分裂主义者制造“两个朝鲜”的活动，在实现朝鲜统一的道路上还有障碍和困难，应该以全民族团结的力量粉碎分裂主义活动，积极推动正在得到良好发展的形势，以完成祖国统一的历史性事业。
    布约亚总统是２５日抵达平壤的。
