Title: Hab Zhumu Xun Writes to Mourn the Passing of the Panchen Lama
Author:
Date: 1989-02-20
Edition: 1

Vice Chairman of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress Hab Zhumu Xun has written an article titled "Deeply Missing the Faithful Friend of the Chinese Communist Party, the Panchen Lama," highly praising the life of the Panchen Lama and expressing deep sorrow over his unfortunate passing. The article is published on the sixth page of today's newspaper.

### 习仲勋撰文悼念班禅大师

1989-02-20
第1版(要闻)
专栏：

　　习仲勋撰文悼念班禅大师
    本报讯  全国人民代表大会常务委员会副委员长习仲勋撰写题为《深切怀念中国共产党的忠实朋友班禅大师》的悼念文章，对班禅大师的一生给予高度的评价，并对他的不幸逝世表示深切的哀悼。该文刊登在今日本报第六版上。
