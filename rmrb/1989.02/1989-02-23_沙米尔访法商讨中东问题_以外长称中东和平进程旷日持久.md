Title: Shamir Visits France to Discuss Middle East Issues
Author:
Date: 1989-02-23
Edition: 3

	Shamir Visits France to Discuss Middle East Issues
	Israeli Foreign Minister Says Middle East Peace Process is Prolonged

	Xinhua News Agency Cairo February 21st: Israeli Prime Minister Shamir today left Tel Aviv for Paris for a four-day visit to France to discuss Middle East issues with French leaders.

	Before departing, Shamir told reporters at Tel Aviv Airport that he hoped to reach a consensus with President Mitterrand and other French leaders on the issue of peacefully resolving the Arab-Israeli conflict.

	Xinhua News Agency Cairo February 21st: Israeli Foreign Minister Arens said on the 21st in Cairo to the Middle East News Agency that both Israel and Egypt believe that the Middle East peace process must be accelerated. Arens made the remarks after talks with Egyptian Foreign Minister Abdel Majid.

	He added that the Middle East peace process will be very difficult and prolonged.

### 沙米尔访法商讨中东问题  以外长称中东和平进程旷日持久

1989-02-23
第3版(国际新闻)
专栏：

　　沙米尔访法商讨中东问题
    以外长称中东和平进程旷日持久
    新华社开罗２月２１日电  以色列总理沙米尔今天离开特拉维夫前往巴黎对法国进行４天访问，以便同法国领导人商讨中东问题。
    临行前，沙米尔在特拉维夫机场对记者说，他希望同密特朗总统和其他法国领导人就和平解决阿拉伯—以色列冲突问题达成一致意见。
    新华社开罗２月２１日电　以色列外长阿伦斯２１日在开罗对中东通讯社发表谈话说，以色列和埃及都认为，必须加快中东和平进程。阿伦斯是在同埃及外长阿卜杜勒·马吉德会谈后发表上述谈话的。
    他又说，中东和平进程将是十分艰难的和旷日持久的。
