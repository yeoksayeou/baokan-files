Title: The Final of the Ying Cup is Scheduled to be Held in Zhejiang
Author:
Date: 1989-02-21
Edition: 4

The final of the "Ying Cup" Go tournament is scheduled to be held from April 15 to 28 in Ningbo and Hangzhou, the hometown of the initiator of the "Ying Cup," Mr. Ying Changqing. The closing ceremony and award presentation will be held in Ningbo.

This final will be a best-of-five series, with China's Nie Weiping and South Korea's Cho Hunhyun competing for the championship.

### 应氏杯决赛拟在浙江举行

1989-02-21
第4版(文化生活·体育)
专栏：体育

　　应氏杯决赛拟在浙江举行
    “应氏杯”围棋总决赛拟于４月１５日至２８日在“应氏杯”发起人应昌期先生的故乡宁波和杭州举行，并在宁波举行颁奖闭幕式。
    这次总决赛采用五局三胜制，由中国的聂卫平和南朝鲜的曹熏铉争夺桂冠。
    　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　（新华社）
