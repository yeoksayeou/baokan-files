Title: Greek Workers and Employees Stage Mass Strike
Author:
Date: 1989-02-04
Edition: 6

More than a million Greek workers and employees went on a 24-hour strike on February 2nd to protest the government's income policy and demand wage increases. This strike was initiated by 40 major unions and 24 trade federations in Greece. Teachers, postal workers, civil aviation and road transport service personnel, taxi drivers, doctors, as well as employees of banks, telecommunications, and other public facilities widely responded to the call for the strike.

### 希腊工人职员举行大罢工

1989-02-04
第6版(国际)
专栏：=国际新闻简报

　　希腊工人职员举行大罢工
    希腊一百多万名工人和职员二日举行二十四小时大罢工，抗议政府的收入政策，并要求增加工资。
    这次罢工是由希腊四十个主要工会和二十四个贸易联合会共同发起的。罢工得到了教师、邮政工人、民航及公路交通服务人员、出租汽车司机、医生以及银行、电信机构和其它公共设施职员的广泛响应。
　　　　　　　　　　（据新华社）
