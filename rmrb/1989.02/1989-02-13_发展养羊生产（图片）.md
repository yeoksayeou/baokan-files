Title: Developing Sheep Farming
Author: Zhao Bingyao
Date: 1989-02-13
Edition: 2

	The Taolin Township in Shanxi Province's Pu County has implemented preferential policies to encourage villagers to develop sheep farming. The township has increased its sheep population from 400 to 3,860.

	The image shows villagers feeding small-tailed Han sheep.
