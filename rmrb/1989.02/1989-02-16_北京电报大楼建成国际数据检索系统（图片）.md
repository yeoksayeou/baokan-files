Title: The Beijing Telegraph Building Establishes an International Data Retrieval System
Author: Xue Tiejun
Date: 1989-02-16
Edition: 3

The international data retrieval system was recently established and put into use in the Beijing Telegraph Building. It has now opened data retrieval systems to Italy, West Germany, Austria, the United Kingdom, and other countries. Users can operate the keyboard at their office terminals, and within a few minutes, they can retrieve various data and information from hundreds of domestic and international databases. The image shows the control room of the data retrieval system center.

### 北京电报大楼建成国际数据检索系统（图片）
薛铁军
1989-02-16
第3版(科教·文化·体育)
专栏：

    北京电报大楼建成国际数据检索系统
    国际数据检索设备最近在北京电报大楼建成并投入使用。现已开通北京至意大利、联邦德国、奥地利、英国等国的数据检索系统。用户在办公室里终端机前操作键盘，几分钟就可以从国内外几百个数据库中，查出所需要的各种数据和情报。图为数据检索系统中心控制室。
    新华社记者  薛铁军摄
