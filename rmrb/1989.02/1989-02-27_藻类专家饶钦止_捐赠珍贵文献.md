Title: Algae Expert Rao Qinzhi Donates Valuable Literature
Author:
Date: 1989-02-27
Edition: 2

Algae expert Rao Qinzhi donates valuable literature

    NEW YORK, Feb. 26 (Xinhua) -- According to a report by the Changjiang Daily, Rao Qinzhi, one of the pioneers and founders of algae research in China, recently donated a collection of valuable algae literature to the Institute of Hydrobiology, Chinese Academy of Sciences.

    The donated literature consists of 74 volumes and 1,648 articles. Some of the publications date back 150 years and are now out of print. Among the most valuable is a compilation of algae literature written by a French author, which was first published in 1889 with only 80 copies.

### 藻类专家饶钦止  捐赠珍贵文献

1989-02-27
第2版(国内新闻)
专栏：四面八方

　　藻类专家饶钦止
    捐赠珍贵文献
    新华社武汉２月２６日电  据《长江日报》报道，我国藻类学研究的开拓者与奠基人之一饶钦止教授，近日将他收集珍藏的一批藻类学文献，捐赠给中国科学院水生生物研究所。
    他捐献的这批文献资料共７４集１６４８篇。其中有的出版时间距今已有１５０年，早已绝版；由法国人写的一部藻类学类编资料最为珍贵，它在１８８９年头版时仅有８０部。
