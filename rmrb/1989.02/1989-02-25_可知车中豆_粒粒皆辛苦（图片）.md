Title: Seeing the Beans in the Truck, Each Grain is the Result of Hard Work
Author: Qi Yingxiang
Date: 1989-02-25
Edition: 2

Look, there are yellow beans scattered along the roadside, piled up to a thickness of 20 centimeters! This is the result of some migrant workers from Anqing City, Anhui Province, recklessly unloading their trucks. It is suggested that relevant departments strengthen management to prevent similar incidents from happening again.
