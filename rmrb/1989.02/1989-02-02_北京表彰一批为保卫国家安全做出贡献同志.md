Title: Beijing Honors Contributors to National Security
Author: Yǔ Shūfēn, Huáng Zhìmǐn
Date: 1989-02-02
Edition: 4

Beijing honors a group of individuals who have contributed to national security
Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, February 1st - (Correspondent Yu Shufen, Reporter Huang Zhiming) Today, the Beijing Municipal State Security Bureau held a grand ceremony to award certificates and medals to over 1,000 veterans who have long been engaged in national security work and have made significant contributions to safeguarding national security. The Beijing Municipal State Security Bureau also honored several advanced collectives and individuals who have made outstanding contributions in recent years to the work of solving cases.

### 北京表彰一批为保卫国家安全做出贡献同志
于淑芬　黄智敏
1989-02-02
第4版(政治·法律·社会)
专栏：

　　北京表彰一批为保卫国家安全做出贡献同志
    新华社北京２月１日电  （通讯员于淑芬、记者黄智敏）北京市国家安全局今天举行隆重仪式，向１０００多名长期从事国家安全工作并为保卫国家安全做出贡献的老同志颁发了荣誉证章、证书。北京市国家安全局今天还表彰了一批近年来在侦破案件工作中做出突出贡献的先进集体和个人。
