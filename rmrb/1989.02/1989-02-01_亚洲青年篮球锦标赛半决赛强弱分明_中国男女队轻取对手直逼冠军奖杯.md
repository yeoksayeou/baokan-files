Title: Clear Strength Differences in the Semifinals of the Asian Youth Basketball Championship
Author:
Date: 1989-02-01
Edition: 3

The semifinals of the 10th Asian Youth Basketball Championship were marked by clear differences in strength. The Chinese men's and women's youth basketball teams easily defeated their opponents, advancing to the finals.

The New China News Agency reported from Manila on January 31 that the Chinese men's and women's youth basketball teams won their respective semifinals today, securing their spots in the finals.

In the men's semifinal, the defending champion Chinese youth team defeated the host Philippines team 80-57. In the other semifinal, Chinese Taipei defeated Japan 96-76.

The Chinese team will face Chinese Taipei in the men's final on the 2nd.

The results of the women's semifinals were as follows: the Chinese team defeated Chinese Taipei 96-76, and South Korea defeated Japan 99-76.

The women's final will also be held on the 2nd, with the Chinese team facing South Korea.

### 亚洲青年篮球锦标赛半决赛强弱分明  中国男女队轻取对手直逼冠军奖杯

1989-02-01
第3版(科技·文化·体育)
专栏：体育

　　亚洲青年篮球锦标赛半决赛强弱分明
    中国男女队轻取对手直逼冠军奖杯
    新华社马尼拉１月３１日电  中国青年男、女篮球队今天在这里进行的第１０届亚洲青年篮球锦标赛半决赛中，分别战胜菲律宾队和中国台北队，获得决赛权。
    在男子半决赛中，卫冕的中国青年队以８０∶５７击败东道主菲律宾队。在另一场半决赛中，中国台北队以９６∶７６战胜日本队。
    中国队将于后天与中国台北队进行决赛。
    两场女子半决赛的结果为：中国队以９６∶７６战胜中国台北队，南朝鲜队以９９∶７６击败日本队。
    女子决赛也将于后天进行，中国队与南朝鲜队交锋。
