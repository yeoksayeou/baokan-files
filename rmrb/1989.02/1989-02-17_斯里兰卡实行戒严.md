Title: Sri Lanka Implements Martial Law
Author:
Date: 1989-02-17
Edition: 6

Sri Lanka Implements Martial Law

A statement from the Sri Lankan government announced that martial law will be in effect from 9 AM today until 6 AM tomorrow to prevent violent incidents following the parliamentary elections.

The parliamentary elections in Sri Lanka were held yesterday. On that day, violent incidents across the country resulted in 13 deaths and 18 injuries, with two polling stations being attacked.

### 斯里兰卡实行戒严

1989-02-17
第6版(国际)
专栏：

　　斯里兰卡实行戒严
    新华社科伦坡２月１６日电  据斯里兰卡政府的一份公报宣布，从今天上午９时至明天上午６时斯里兰卡实行戒严，以防止在议会大选后发生暴力事件。
    斯里兰卡议会大选于昨天举行。当天，在斯里兰卡各地的暴力事件中，有１３人被杀，１８人受伤，两个投票站遭袭击。
