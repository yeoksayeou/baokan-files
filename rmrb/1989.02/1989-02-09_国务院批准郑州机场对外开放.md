Title: State Council Approves Opening of Zhengzhou Airport to International Flights
Author: Li Erliang
Date: 1989-02-09
Edition: 2

The State Council has approved the opening of Zhengzhou Airport to international flights. The airport officially began international operations on February 5. This marks the establishment of an international aviation hub in Henan, an inland province.

The newly expanded Zhengzhou Airport can accommodate large and medium-sized aircraft such as the Boeing 737.

Currently, Zhengzhou Airport has flights to 13 provinces and cities, including Beijing, Shanghai, Guangzhou, and Xi'an, with a total of 74 flights per week.

### 国务院批准郑州机场对外开放
李而亮
1989-02-09
第2版(经济)
专栏：

    国务院批准郑州机场对外开放　经国务院批准，郑州机场于２月５日正式对外开放。自此，河南这个内陆省份有了一个国际航空港。新扩建的郑州机场，可以起落波音７３７等大中型客机。
    郑州机场目前开通了至北京、上海、广州、西安等１３个省市的国内航线，每周７４个航班。　  
　　　　（李而亮）
