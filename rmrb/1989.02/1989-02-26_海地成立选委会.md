Title: Haiti Establishes Election Commission
Author:
Date: 1989-02-26
Edition: 3

Haiti Establishes Election Commission

	According to Xinhua News Agency, Havana, February 24: Port-au-Prince News: Haitian President Avril issued an order on February 24 to establish a temporary election commission to be responsible for organizing and supervising future elections.

	The order stipulates that the temporary election commission is independent, and its members will be appointed by the government, the church, trade unions, and human rights organizations.

### 海地成立选委会

1989-02-26
第3版(国际新闻)
专栏：

　　海地成立选委会
    据新华社哈瓦那２月２４日电  太子港消息：海地总统阿夫里尔２４日颁布一项法令，决定成立临时选举委员会，以负责组织和监督未来的大选工作。
    法令规定，临时选举委员会具有独立性质，其成员的任命要由政府、教会、工会及人权组织等来决定。
