Title: U.S. Air Force Launches New Generation Navigation Satellite
Author:
Date: 1989-02-16
Edition: 7

The U.S. Air Force successfully launched a new generation navigation satellite on February 14 using a new Delta 2 rocket from Cape Canaveral.

The navigation satellite, valued at $65 million, is the first of a new generation of Global Positioning System satellites. It can accurately determine the position and altitude of U.S. and NATO land, sea, and air forces at any location in the world, with an error margin of only 15 meters.

### 美国空军发射新一代导航卫星

1989-02-16
第7版(国际)
专栏：

　　美国空军发射新一代导航卫星
    新华社华盛顿２月１４日电  美国空军２月１４日在卡纳维拉尔角，用新型的德尔塔２型火箭成功地发射了一颗新一代导航卫星。
    这颗价值６５００万美元的导航卫星，是第一颗新一代全球定位系统卫星，它能准确地确定美国和北约陆、海、空军在世界任何地点的位置和高度，其误差仅在１５米范围内。
