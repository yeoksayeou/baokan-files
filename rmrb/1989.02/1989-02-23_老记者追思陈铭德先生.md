Title: Veteran Journalist Remembers Mr. Chen Mingde
Author: Qian Jiang
Date: 1989-02-23
Edition: 2

	Veteran Journalist Remembers Mr. Chen Mingde
	Our reporter Qian Jiang reports from Beijing: This morning, more than 30 veteran journalists gathered at the China National Democratic Construction Association to commemorate the recently deceased seventh-term National Committee member of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference, renowned patriot and democrat, veteran news industry figure, and founder of the *Xinmin Evening News*, Mr. Chen Mingde. They paid tribute to his lifelong pursuit of progress and dedication to the development of the news industry.

### 老记者追思陈铭德先生
钱江
1989-02-23
第2版(国内新闻)
专栏：

　　老记者追思陈铭德先生
    本报北京２月２２日讯  记者钱江报道：今天上午，３０多位老新闻工作者来到中国国民党革命委员会，聚会追思刚刚去世的七届全国政协常委、著名爱国民主人士、老一辈新闻实业家、《新民报》创始人陈铭德先生，缅怀他追求进步、致力于新闻事业发展的一生。
