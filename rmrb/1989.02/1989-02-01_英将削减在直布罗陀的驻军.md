Title: UK to Reduce Troops in Gibraltar
Author:
Date: 1989-02-01
Edition: 6

The British government confirmed on January 30 that it intends to "at an appropriate time" withdraw "all or most" of the infantry battalion stationed in the British overseas territory of Gibraltar.

The Sunday Times reported on January 29 that the British government had decided to withdraw more than a thousand personnel from Gibraltar and close a large number of military facilities there.

The total British military presence in Gibraltar includes: 781 soldiers and support personnel, 529 Royal Navy and Royal Marines personnel, and 387 Royal Air Force personnel.

### 英将削减在直布罗陀的驻军

1989-02-01
第6版(国际)
专栏：国际新闻简报

　　英将削减在直布罗陀的驻军
    英国政府一月三十日证实，它打算“在适当的时候”将驻扎在英属海外领地直布罗陀的步兵营“全部或大部”撤回。
    《星期日泰晤士报》二十九日报道说，英国政府决定从直布罗陀撤走一千多人并关闭那里的大批军事设施。
    英国在直布罗陀的全部驻军包括：七百八十一名士兵及支援部队，五百二十九名皇家海军和海军陆战队人员和三百八十七名皇家空军人员。
　（据新华社）
