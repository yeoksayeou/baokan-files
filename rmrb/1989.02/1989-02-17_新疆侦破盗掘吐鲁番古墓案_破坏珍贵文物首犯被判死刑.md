Title: Xinjiang Solves Case of Tomb Robbery at Turpan Ancient Tombs
Author: Ma Hongjian
Date: 1989-02-17
Edition: 3

    Xinjiang Solves Case of Tomb Robbery at Turpan Ancient Tombs: Main Culprit Sentenced to Death for Destroying Precious Cultural Relics

    According to Xinhua News Agency, Urumqi, February 16 - (Correspondent Ma Hongjian) The main culprit, Yang Xionglin, in the case of robbing the ancient tombs of Turpan and destroying precious cultural relics, was recently sentenced to death with a two-year reprieve and deprivation of political rights for life by the Xinjiang Uygur Autonomous Region High Court. The principal accomplice, Abudureximu·Yahufu, was sentenced to life imprisonment and deprivation of political rights for life.

    In 1987, individual merchants Yang Xionglin and Abudureximu·Yahufu went to the Turpan region four times, using deception to incite local farmers to rob 86 ancient tombs from the Spring and Autumn and Warring States periods, stealing 384 precious cultural relics. Yang Xionglin deceived and obtained 90 cultural relics for only 430 yuan, while Abudureximu·Yahufu deceived and obtained 39 cultural relics. Among them, there were 7 first-class national relics and 22 second-class national relics.

### 新疆侦破盗掘吐鲁番古墓案  破坏珍贵文物首犯被判死刑
马洪健
1989-02-17
第3版(科教·文化·体育)
专栏：

　　新疆侦破盗掘吐鲁番古墓案    破坏珍贵文物首犯被判死刑
    据新华社乌鲁木齐２月１６日电  （通讯员马洪健）盗掘吐鲁番古墓珍贵文物首犯杨雄林最近被新疆维吾尔自治区高级法院依法判处死刑，缓期２年执行，剥夺政治权利终身；同案主犯阿不都热衣木·牙合甫被依法判处无期徒刑，剥夺政治权利终身。
    １９８７年，个体商贩杨雄林和阿不都热衣木·牙合甫先后四次窜到吐鲁番地区，以欺骗手段煽动当地农民盗掘春秋战国时期古墓８６座，盗出珍贵文物３８４件。杨雄林仅以４３０元人民币就骗得文物９０件，阿不都热衣木·牙合甫骗得文物３９件。其中国家一级文物７件，二级文物２２件。
