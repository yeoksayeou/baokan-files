Title: Shijiazhuang Locomotive Depot's Jingguang Passenger Train Team Remembers Passenger Safety, Hauls 129,000 Trains Without Incident
Author: Su Guangsheng
Date: 1989-02-02
Edition: 2

The Shijiazhuang Railway Bureau's Shijiazhuang Locomotive Depot's Jingguang Passenger Train Team always keeps passenger safety in mind. By January 25, this locomotive team had achieved 1,970 consecutive days of safe operation. They had safely hauled 129,000 passenger trains, equivalent to each locomotive safely traveling around the Earth 36 times.

### 石家庄机务段京广客车队牢记旅客安全  牵引１２．９万列客车无事故
苏广生
1989-02-02
第2版(经济)
专栏：

　　石家庄机务段京广客车队牢记旅客安全
    牵引１２．９万列客车无事故
    本报讯  石家庄铁路分局石家庄机务段京广客车队，教育职工时刻把旅客生命财产的安全记在心间，截至元月２５日，这个机车队已连续安全运行１９７０天，安全牵引旅客列车１２．９万多列，相当于每台机车绕地球安全行走了３６圈。    　
  　　（苏广生）
