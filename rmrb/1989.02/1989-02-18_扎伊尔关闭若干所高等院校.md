Title: Zaire Closes Several Higher Education Institutions

Author:

Date: 1989-02-18

Edition: 6

The National Executive Committee (government) of Zaire issued a statement announcing the decision to close the University of Kinshasa, the Higher Institute of Medical Technology, the National Institute of Education, the School of Public Health, and the Higher Institute of Applied Technology starting from the 15th.

It is reported that since the public transportation fares increased by seventy-five percent on the 7th of this month, students from the University of Kinshasa and other higher education institutions in the capital have held demonstrations and protests against this measure.

### 扎伊尔关闭若干所高等院校

1989-02-18
第6版(国际)
专栏：国际新闻简报

　　扎伊尔关闭若干所高等院校
    扎伊尔全国执行委员会（政府）发表公报宣布，决定从十五日起关闭金沙萨大学、高等医学技术学院、全国教育学院、公共卫生学校和高等应用技术学院。
    据悉，自从本月七日公共交通费用上涨百分之七十五以来，金沙萨大学和首都一些高等院校的学生举行了示威游行，抗议这一措施。
    　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　（据新华社）
