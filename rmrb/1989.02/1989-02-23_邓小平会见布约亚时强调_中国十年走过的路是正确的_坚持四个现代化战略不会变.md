Title: Deng Xiaoping Emphasizes During Meeting with Buyoya: China's Path Over the Past Decade is Correct; Adherence to the Four Modernizations Strategy Will Not Change
Author: Dèng Xiǎopíng
Date: 1989-02-23
Edition: 1

Central Military Commission Chairman Deng Xiaoping said today, "It can be said with certainty that the path China has taken over the past 10 years is correct, and we must firmly continue on this path. The strategy of the Four Modernizations will not change."

He said that to achieve this strategic goal, the main policies, especially the policies of reform and opening up, will not change.

Chairman Deng met with Lieutenant Colonel Pierre Buyoya, Chairman of the National Salvation Military Committee and President of the Republic of Burundi, this morning at the Great Hall of the People.

Deng Xiaoping said that China has made some progress in the past 10 years, but there are also many problems. The problems that exist are problems of progress. By overcoming the existing problems and gaining a better environment and conditions, China can continue to move forward.

Buyoya expressed admiration for the great progress China has made.

He said that Burundi is also a developing country. Burundi must make great efforts to meet the needs of the Burundian people. In this regard, Burundi also faces many difficulties.

He said that his visit to China is to further strengthen the friendly cooperation between the two countries.

Deng Xiaoping said, "The relationship between our two countries should continue to develop." He wished Burundi success in its development endeavors.

Date: 1989-02-22
Edition: 1

CPC Central Committee General Secretary Zhao Ziyang said today that the correct domestic and foreign policies of a ruling party and its leaders are the key to changing the backward state of developing countries.

Zhao Ziyang met with Lieutenant Colonel Pierre Buyoya, Chairman of the National Salvation Military Committee and President of the Republic of Burundi, this afternoon in Zhongnanhai. He said, "We are all developing countries. To change our backward state, we must do many things. But the most crucial thing is for the ruling party and its leaders to formulate correct domestic and foreign policies."

He said, "This policy should be conducive to maintaining domestic stability and unity, and to mobilizing all positive factors to promote the development of productive forces."

He said that developing countries should learn from each other and exchange experiences, which is also part of South-South cooperation.

During the meeting, both hosts and guests expressed satisfaction with the good relations between the two countries and the two parties. Both sides also expressed their willingness to further develop the relationship between the two parties.

### 邓小平会见布约亚时强调  中国十年走过的路是正确的  坚持四个现代化战略不会变
杨子迪　王士龙
1989-02-23
第1版(要闻)
专栏：

　　邓小平会见布约亚时强调
    中国十年走过的路是正确的  坚持四个现代化战略不会变
    赵紫阳同日会见布隆迪总统
    新华社北京２月２２日电　（记者杨子迪　王士龙）中央军委主席邓小平今天说，“可以肯定说，中国１０年来所走过的路是正确的，要坚定地走下去。坚持四个现代化的战略也不会改变。”
    他说，为了实现这个战略目标，各项主要的政策，特别是改革开放的政策，也不会改变。
    邓小平主席今天上午在人民大会堂会见了布隆迪救国军事委员会主席、共和国总统皮埃尔·布约亚少校。
    邓小平说，中国在１０年内有点进步，但是问题也不少。存在的问题是前进中的问题。克服了存在的问题，获得了一个较好的环境和条件，中国就可以继续前进。
    布约亚对中国所取得的巨大进步表示赞赏。
    他说，布隆迪也是发展中国家。布隆迪要尽很大努力，以满足布隆迪人民的需要。在这方面，布隆迪也有很多困难。
    他说，他这次访华是为了进一步加强两国的友好合作关系。
    邓小平说：“我们两国关系要继续发展。”他祝愿布隆迪的发展事业取得新的成就。
    新华社北京２月２２日电　（记者杨子迪）中共中央总书记赵紫阳今天说，执政党和领导人的正确的内外政策是发展中国家改变落后状态的关键。
    赵紫阳今天下午在中南海会见布隆迪救国军事委员会主席、共和国总统皮埃尔·布约亚少校时说：“我们都是发展中国家。为改变落后状态，我们要做许多事情。但最关键的是执政党和领导人应制定一个正确的内外政策。”
    他说：“这个政策要有利于保持国内安定团结，有利于调动各方面的积极因素，以促进生产力的发展。”
    他说，发展中国家之间应该互相借鉴，交流经验，这也是南南合作的一个部分。
    会见时，宾主双方对中布两国两党的良好关系表示满意。双方还表示愿意进一步发展两党的关系。
