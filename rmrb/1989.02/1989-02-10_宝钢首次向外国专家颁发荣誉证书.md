Title: Baosteel Awards Honor Certificates to Foreign Experts for the First Time
Author:
Date: 1989-02-10
Edition: 2

	54 foreign experts who made significant contributions to the construction of the second phase of Baosteel recently received "Honor Certificates" awarded by the Baosteel Engineering Command and Baosteel General Plant. Since the commencement of the second phase of Baosteel, over 500 foreign experts have participated in this massive engineering project. They approached each project of the engineering work with a rigorous scientific attitude, delved into the construction sites, and proposed over 600 rationalization suggestions, which played an active role in the smooth progress of the project. The 54 experts came from the Federal Republic of Germany, Japan, the United States, and France.

### 宝钢首次向外国专家颁发荣誉证书

1989-02-10
第2版(经济)
专栏：

    宝钢首次向外国专家颁发荣誉证书　５４位在宝钢二期工程建设中作出显著贡献的外国专家，最近获得了宝钢工程指挥部、宝钢总厂颁发的“荣誉证书”。宝钢二期工程建设以来，先后有５００多名外国专家参与了这项规模巨大的工程建设。他们以严谨的科学态度对待工程每一个项目，深入施工现场，先后提出合理化建议６００多项，对工程顺利进展起了积极作用。５４位专家分别来自联邦德国、日本、美国和法国。
