Title: One-Sentence News
Author:
Date: 1989-02-10
Edition: 6

	One-Sentence News
	△ Sri Lankan President Premadasa said on the 8th that he had informed the Indian government that the mission of the Indian Peacekeeping Force had ended, and they could return home.
	△ The Japanese government decided on the 8th to implement amnesty on the day of the Emperor's funeral on the 24th of this month, including general amnesty, special amnesty, and commutation of sentences, involving up to 11 million people, of whom 28,000 will receive general amnesty.
	△ The Mexican Finance Minister stated that the government is seeking to limit Mexico's annual debt repayment to within 2.5% of the gross domestic product, thereby reducing the annual debt repayment by $5 billion. (Reported by this newspaper)

### 一句话新闻

1989-02-10
第6版(国际)
专栏：一句话新闻

　　一句话新闻
    △斯里兰卡总统普雷马达萨８日说，他已告诉印度政府，印度维持和平部队的使命已结束，他们可以回国了。
    △日本政府８日决定在本月２４日天皇葬礼这天实行恩赦，包括大赦、特赦、免刑等，涉及对象多达１１００万人，其中大赦２８０００人。
    △墨西哥财政部长表示政府正寻求将墨年偿债额限制在国内生产总值２．５％以内，这样每年可少还债５０亿美元。（本报讯）
