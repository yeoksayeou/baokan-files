Title: London Gold Price Drops to Its Lowest Point
Author:
Date: 1989-02-20
Edition: 3

London gold prices have dropped to their lowest point, reaching $378.15 per ounce. This decline was influenced by Australia's sale of gold in Asia, causing the price to fall to its lowest level in two and a half years on February 17.

In January 1980, gold prices peaked at $850 per ounce. Throughout 1988, gold prices fluctuated above $400 per ounce.

### 伦敦金价跌到最低点  ３７８．１５美元一盎司

1989-02-20
第3版(国际新闻)
专栏：

　　伦敦金价跌到最低点
    ３７８．１５美元一盎司
    由于澳大利亚在亚洲抛售黄金的影响，伦敦黄金市场上的黄金价，２月１７日跌到３７８．１５美元一盎司，为２年半来的最低价。
    １９８０年１月份，黄金价格曾高达８５０美元一盎司。１９８８年全年黄金价格都在４００美元一盎司以上徘徊。
