Title: The World Tourism Expo Opens in Paris
Author: Zhang Qihua
Date: 1989-02-17
Edition: 7

The 14th World Tourism Expo opened today in Paris at the Porte de Versailles Exhibition Park. The Chinese tourism delegation, led by Zhang Xinsheng, Vice Minister of the China National Tourism Administration, attended the opening ceremony.

In the exhibition hall, the Chinese pavilion, rich in national characteristics, attracted great interest. On the 100-square-meter pavilion, a central red-lacquered four-pillar pavilion was set up, with surrounding displays arranged in a radial pattern. Exhibits included the China International Travel Service, Xinjiang, Guizhou Provincial Tourism Bureau, Beijing Xiyuan Hotel, and the International Hotel. The French Minister of Tourism, Stein, enthusiastically viewed the Chinese pavilion.

### 世界旅游博览会在巴黎开幕  我五单位设台参展受欢迎
张启华
1989-02-17
第7版(国际)
专栏：

　　世界旅游博览会在巴黎开幕    
    我五单位设台参展受欢迎
    本报巴黎２月１６日电  记者张启华报道：今天上午，第１４届世界旅游博览会在巴黎凡尔赛门的展览公园举行，中国旅游代表团团长、中国国家旅游局副局长章新胜率团参加了开幕仪式。
    在展览大厅中，饱具民族风格的中国展台引起了人们极大的兴趣。在１００平方米的展台上，中央设一红漆四柱亭台，周围呈放射状，陈设着中国国际旅行社、新疆、贵州省旅游局、北京西苑饭店和国际饭店展台。法国旅游部长斯蒂恩热情观看了中国展台。
