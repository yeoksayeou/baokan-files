Title: Foreign Ministers of Five Central American Countries to Meet in New York
Author:
Date: 1989-02-08
Edition: 3

Foreign ministers of five Central American countries will convene in New York starting February 8 for a two-day meeting to prepare for the upcoming summit of Central American heads of state in El Salvador next week.

Miguel d'Escoto, the foreign minister of Nicaragua, stated upon his arrival in New York on February 6 that the foreign ministers will also discuss the implementation of the Central American peace agreement verification plan with United Nations Secretary-General Javier Pérez de Cuéllar.

### 中美洲五国外长将去纽约会晤

1989-02-08
第3版(国际)
专栏：国际要闻简报

　　中美洲五国外长将去纽约会晤
    中美洲五国外长将从本月八日起在纽约举行两天会议，为下周在萨尔瓦多举行的中美洲国家首脑会议进行准备。
    尼加拉瓜外长米格尔·德斯科托六日在抵达纽约时说，外长们还将同联合国秘书长佩雷斯·德奎利亚尔讨论中美洲和平协议核查计划的执行情况。
