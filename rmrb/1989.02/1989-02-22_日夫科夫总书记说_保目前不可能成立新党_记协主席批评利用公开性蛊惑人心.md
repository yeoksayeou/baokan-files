Title: Zhivkov Says New Party Formation Currently Impossible in Bulgaria
Author:
Date: 1989-02-22
Edition: 3

	Zhivkov Says New Party Formation Currently Impossible in Bulgaria
	Chairman of Journalists' Union Criticizes Use of Openness to Mislead

	SOFIA, Feb. 20 (Xinhua) -- Todor Zhivkov, General Secretary of the Central Committee of the Bulgarian Communist Party, said today that Bulgaria does not currently have the social conditions for the formation of multiple political centers, and thus there is no question of establishing new parties.

	Zhivkov emphasized that the Bulgarian Communist Party is a vibrant political force capable of applying Marxism-Leninism to Bulgaria's specific conditions and providing accurate answers to various mature social issues.

	He said that Bulgaria currently lacks the social basis for the formation of several political centers. If in the future there is a need to establish new parties, then that issue can be discussed specifically at that time.

	SOFIA, Feb. 20 (Xinhua) -- Trayko Traykov, Chairman of the Bulgarian Journalists' Union and President of the Bulgarian News Agency, criticized in a speech today those who fantasize about using openness for misleading propaganda, even harboring anti-socialist intentions.

	The Bulgarian news leader made these remarks during a meeting between the Political Bureau of the Central Committee of the Bulgarian Communist Party and representatives of intellectuals from various fields.

### 日夫科夫总书记说  保目前不可能成立新党  记协主席批评利用公开性蛊惑人心

1989-02-22
第3版(国际新闻)
专栏：

　　日夫科夫总书记说
    保目前不可能成立新党
    记协主席批评利用公开性蛊惑人心
    新华社索非亚２月２０日电  保共中央总书记日夫科夫２０日在会见知识界代表时表示，保加利亚没有成立多元政治中心的社会土壤，所以不存在成立新的党派的问题。
    日夫科夫强调，保加利亚共产党是生机勃勃的政治力量，它有能力把马列主义运用于保加利亚的具体条件，也有能力对各种已经成熟的社会问题作出各种准确的解答。
    他说，保加利亚现在还没有成立几个政治中心的社会基础，如果将来有一天需要成立新的党派，到时再进行具体讨论。
    新华社索非亚２月２０日电　保加利亚记协主席、保通社社长特莱科夫２０日在一次会议发言中批评某些人幻想利用公开性进行蛊惑性宣传，甚至怀有反社会主义的图谋。
    这位保加利亚新闻界领导人是在保共中央政治局同各界知识分子代表举行会见时讲这番话的。
