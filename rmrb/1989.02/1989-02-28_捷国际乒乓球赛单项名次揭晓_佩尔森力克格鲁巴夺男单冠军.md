Title: The 39th Czechoslovakian International Table Tennis Championship Results Announced
Author:
Date: 1989-02-28
Edition: 4

The 39th Czechoslovakian International Table Tennis Championship concluded in Bratislava on February 26. Sweden's Persson and Romania's Badescu won the men's and women's singles titles, respectively. Sweden's W. Scholler and Carlsson, and Czechoslovakia's Hrachova and Kasalova won the men's and women's doubles titles.

In the men's singles final, Sweden's Persson and Poland's Gruba both played exceptionally well, each pushing the other to the limit. After tying at 2-2, in the crucial fifth set, Gruba led twice with scores of 15-10 and 18-15. However, he ultimately could not withstand Persson's offensive, losing 2-3.

In the women's singles final, Romania's Badescu defeated West Germany's Nemesova 3-0 in a fierce attacking match.

### 捷国际乒乓球赛单项名次揭晓  佩尔森力克格鲁巴夺男单冠军

1989-02-28
第4版(文化生活·体育)
专栏：体育

　　捷国际乒乓球赛单项名次揭晓
    佩尔森力克格鲁巴夺男单冠军
    新华社布拉格２月２６日电  捷克斯洛伐克第３９届国际乒乓球锦标赛２６日在布拉迪斯拉发落幕。瑞典的佩尔森和罗马尼亚选手伯德斯科娃分获男女单打冠军。瑞典的文·谢勒和卡尔松，捷克斯洛伐克的赫拉霍娃和卡萨洛娃分别获得男、女双打冠军。
    男子单打决赛，瑞典的佩尔森和波兰的格鲁巴在场上发挥都很好，你追我赶，互不相让，打成２平后，在关键的第五局，格鲁巴虽两度以１５∶１０和１８∶１５领先，但终因未能抵住佩尔森的进攻，以２∶３败北。
    女子单打决赛，罗马尼亚的伯德斯科娃在一场对攻战中，以３∶０战胜了联邦德国的内梅索瓦。
