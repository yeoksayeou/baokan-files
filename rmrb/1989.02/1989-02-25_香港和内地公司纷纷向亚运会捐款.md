Title: Hong Kong and Mainland Companies Donate to the Asian Games
Author:
Date: 1989-02-25
Edition: 4

Hong Kong and mainland companies have been actively donating to the Asian Games. On the 22nd, Mr. Wong Kam-tung, chairman of the Hong Kong Kam Shing Embroidery Tablecloth Company, donated 200,000 Hong Kong dollars to the Beijing Asian Games. On the 23rd, the Coal Trading Department of the Beijing Xinghai Industrial Company donated 100,000 yuan to the Asian Games.

### 香港和内地公司纷纷向亚运会捐款

1989-02-25
第4版(文化生活·体育)
专栏：体育

　　香港和内地公司纷纷向亚运会捐款
    本报讯  ２２日，香港锦兴绣花台布公司董事长翁锦通先生向北京亚运会捐款２０万元港币。２３日，北京市兴海实业公司煤炭经营部向亚运会捐赠了１０万人民币。
