Title: Former Political Commissar of Chengdu Military Region Air Force, Feng Yingshan, Passes Away
Author:
Date: 1989-02-12
Edition: 4

The former Political Commissar of the Chengdu Military Region Air Force, Feng Yingshan, passed away due to illness on January 28 in Chengdu, aged 64. The farewell ceremony for Feng Yingshan's remains was held on February 2 at the Chengdu Funeral Parlor.

Yang Ruda and others attended the farewell ceremony and sent wreaths. The Air Force Party Committee, the Chengdu Military Region Party Committee, the Chengdu Military Region Air Force Party Committee, as well as the Sichuan and Shanxi provincial and municipal party and government organs, sent wreaths.

Feng Yingshan was a native of Xiangfen County, Shanxi Province. He enlisted in 1938 and joined the Communist Party of China in 1940. He held various positions, including soldier, instructor, political director, section chief, regiment political commissar, political commissar of the Second Aviation School, political commissar of the Second Anti-Aircraft Artillery Academy, political commissar of the Air Force Engineering Academy, and political commissar of the Fujian Military Region Air Force.

Throughout his long revolutionary career, Feng Yingshan was always loyal to the Party and the people. During the War of Resistance Against Japan and the War of Liberation, he participated in numerous battles in the Luixi South, Houguan, and Baita, as well as the Huaihai Campaign, fighting bravely and making significant contributions to the liberation of the Chinese people. After the national liberation, he long served in leadership positions in military academies, dedicating himself to training air force personnel and improving the military and political quality of the troops. After becoming the political commissar of the military region air force, he worked diligently, practiced self-discipline, maintained unity, and set a good example. During the ten years of turmoil, he fought resolutely against the counter-revolutionary cliques of Lin Biao and Jiang Qing. He wholeheartedly supported the line, principles, and policies of the Party's Third Plenary Session of the Eleventh Central Committee and consciously maintained a high degree of unity with the Central Committee of the Party.

Feng Yingshan was an outstanding Party member, an excellent military commander, and a political worker. He commanded the troops strictly, adhered to Party discipline, sought truth from facts, and linked theory with practice; he upheld principles, was honest, humble, cautious, cared for his comrades, and was approachable; he was meticulous in his work, took responsibility bravely, and set an example with his actions; he lived frugally, governed cleanly, was strict with himself, and demanded much from his children, maintaining the excellent qualities of a Communist Party member. His life was a revolutionary life, a life of struggle, and a life dedicated to serving the people wholeheartedly.

### 成都军区空军原政委冯应山逝世

1989-02-12
第4版(政治·法律·社会)
专栏：

　　成都军区空军原政委冯应山逝世
    新华社成都２月１１日电  成都军区空军原政治委员冯应山因病于１月２８日在成都逝世，终年６４岁。向冯应山同志遗体告别仪式２月２日在成都殡仪馆悼念厅举行。
    杨汝岱等参加了向冯应山同志遗体告别的仪式并送了花圈。空军党委、成都军区党委、成都军区空军党委，以及四川省、山西省党政机关等送了花圈。
    冯应山是山西省襄汾县人，１９３８年入伍，１９４０年加入中国共产党，历任战士、指导员、教导员、科长、团政治委员、空军第二航校政治委员、空军第二高炮学院政治委员、空军工程学院政治委员、福州军区空军政治委员等职。
    冯应山在长期的革命生涯中，一贯忠于党、忠于人民。抗日战争和解放战争中，他参加了鲁西南侯关、白塔等多次战斗和淮海战役，作战勇敢，为中国人民的解放事业作出了贡献。全国解放后，他长期担负院校领导工作，为培养空军飞行和其他专业人才，提高部队军政素质，做了大量工作。他担任军区空军政治委员后，勤勤恳恳，克己奉公，维护团结，率先垂范。在十年动乱中，他同林彪、江青反革命集团进行了坚决的斗争。他衷心拥护党的十一届三中全会以来的路线、方针、政策，自觉同党中央保持高度一致。
    冯应山是中国共产党的优秀党员，我军的优秀指挥员和政治工作者。他治军严格，遵守党纪，实事求是，理论联系实际；他坚持原则，为人正直，谦虚谨慎，关心同志，平易近人；他对工作一丝不苟，勇于负责，以身作则；他生活俭朴，为政清廉，严于律己，对子女要求严格，保持了共产党员的优秀品质。他的一生是革命的一生，战斗的一生，全心全意为人民服务的一生。
