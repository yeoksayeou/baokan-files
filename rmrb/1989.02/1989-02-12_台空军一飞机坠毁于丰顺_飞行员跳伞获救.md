Title: Taiwanese Air Force Plane Crashes in Fengshun; Pilot Parachutes to Safety
Author:
Date: 1989-02-12
Edition: 1

Taiwanese Air Force Plane Crashes in Fengshun; Pilot Parachutes to Safety

Today at around 10 a.m., a Taiwanese Air Force F-5E fighter jet crashed in Fengshun County, Guangdong Province, due to fuel shortage. The pilot, Colonel Lin Xianshun, safely parachuted to the ground and was rescued.

		The image shows Taiwanese pilot Lin Xianshun after safely landing following his parachute jump, receiving warm care and attention from our side.

		Photographed by Xinhua News Agency reporter Chen Xuesi (faxed photograph)

### 台空军一飞机坠毁于丰顺  飞行员跳伞获救

1989-02-12
第1版(要闻)
专栏：

　　台空军一飞机坠毁于丰顺
    飞行员跳伞获救
    新华社北京２月１１日电  今天上午１０时许，台湾空军一架Ｆ—５Ｅ飞机因油料不足，在广东省丰顺县境内坠毁，飞行员林贤顺中校跳伞获救。
    （附图片）
    图为台湾飞行员林贤顺跳伞安全着落后，受到我方人员亲切关怀和照料。
