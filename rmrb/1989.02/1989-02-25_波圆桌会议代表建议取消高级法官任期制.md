Title: Representatives of the Polish Round Table Conference Propose Abolishing the Term Limit for Senior Judges
Author:
Date: 1989-02-25
Edition: 3

Representatives of the Polish "Round Table Conference" have suggested to the Polish authorities that the five-year term limit for judges of the Supreme Court be abolished. This would be beneficial for judges to uphold justice impartially and serve the public with integrity.

According to a report by the Polish News Agency, this proposal was made by members of the legal and judicial reform subgroup of the "Round Table Conference" at their meeting on the 22nd. (Xinhua News Agency)

### 波圆桌会议代表建议取消高级法官任期制

1989-02-25
第3版(国际新闻)
专栏：

　　波圆桌会议代表建议取消高级法官任期制
    波兰“圆桌会议”的各方代表建议波兰当局，取消最高法院法官５年任期制。这将有利于法官秉公执法，廉洁奉公。
    据波通社报道，这项建议是“圆桌会议”法律和法院改革问题分组成员在２２日的会议上提出的。（新华社）
