Title: Brief News on Education, Science, and Culture
Author:
Date: 1989-02-11
Edition: 3

A type of petroleum gas-saving and pollution-reducing device suitable for household use has been put into mass production by the Beijing Shuangqing Engineering Machinery Repair Factory. It uses special magnetic materials as catalysts and cracking agents, allowing liquefied gas to burn more completely. Installing this device can generally save 15% to 25% of gas, while significantly reducing the amount of carbon monoxide in the exhaust.

The Sichuan Provincial Committee of the Communist Party of China and the editorial department of "Study and Construction" magazine have begun publishing the "Series on the Construction of Grassroots Party Organizations." The first batch includes five books: "How to Be a Good Party Branch Secretary" (revised edition) and "Thirty Topics on Enterprise Party Organization Work."

The Old Mountain Frontline held the opening ceremony for the Old Mountain Cup Photography Exhibition on February 3. The 170 works on display were selected from nearly a thousand photographs. The authors are all soldiers who have undergone the trials of blood and fire in this local war.

### 教科文零讯

1989-02-11
第3版(科教·文化·体育)
专栏：教科文零讯

　　教科文零讯
    ●一种适合家庭用的石油气节气减污装置，已由北京双清工程机械修配厂投入批量生产。它以特殊磁性材料为催化、裂化剂，使液化气能够得到充分燃烧。安装这种装置一般可节气１５％—２５％，同时明显减少了烟气中的一氧化碳。
    　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　（柯纪）
    ●四川省委组织部和《学习与建设》杂志社编辑出版的《党的基层组织建设系列丛书》开始出版。第一批有《怎样当好党支部书记》（修订本）、《企业党组织工作三十题》等五本。　　　　　　　　　　　
　　　
　　　（苏青阳）
    ●老山前线举办的老山杯摄影作品展览２月３日开幕。展出的１７０幅作品，是从近千幅照片中筛选出来的。作者均是在这场局部战争中经受过血与火洗礼的军人。　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　
　　　
　　　（王正伟）
