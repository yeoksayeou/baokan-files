Title: New Discoveries in Oil Exploration in the Tarim Basin
Author:
Date: 1989-02-17
Edition: 2

The 60146 drilling team of the Southern Xinjiang Petroleum Exploration Company recently achieved significant oil and gas indicators in the Yingmaili No. 1 well, located 40 kilometers west of Shaya in the northern part of the Tarim Basin. The well tested a daily production of 211 cubic meters of crude oil and 1,000 cubic meters of natural gas.

### 塔里木盆地石油勘探又获新成果

1989-02-17
第2版(经济)
专栏：

    塔里木盆地石油勘探又获新成果
  　新疆南疆石油勘探公司六零一四六钻井队近日在塔里木盆地北部地区沙雅西四十公里英买力一号井获得高产油气显示，经测试日产原油二百一十一立方米、天然气一千立方米。
