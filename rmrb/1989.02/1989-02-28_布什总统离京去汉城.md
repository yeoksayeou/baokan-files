Title: Bush President Leaves Beijing for Seoul
Author: Xīn Huáishí
Date: 1989-02-28
Edition: 2

Bush President Leaves Beijing for Seoul

New China News Agency, Beijing, February 27th: (Reporter Xīn Huáishí) U.S. President George Bush, after completing his working visit to China, left Beijing this morning by special plane for Seoul.

Bush's wife, Barbara, and Secretary of State Baker also departed with him.

Vice Premier Wu Xueqian and others saw them off at the airport.

### 布什总统离京去汉城
辛怀时
1989-02-28
第2版(国内新闻)
专栏：

　　布什总统离京去汉城
    新华社北京二月二十七日电  （记者辛怀时）美国总统乔治·布什，结束对中国的工作访问，今天早晨乘专机离开北京去汉城。
    布什总统的夫人巴巴拉和国务卿贝克也同机离开这里。
    吴学谦副总理等到机场送行。
