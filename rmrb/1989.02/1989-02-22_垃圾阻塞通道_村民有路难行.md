Date: 1989-02-22
Edition: 6

Garbage Blocks Pathway, Villagers Struggle to Pass

A major road in the north of our village leads to the No. 2 Gate Oil Mine in the Henan Oilfield. This road is the only route for our villagers to go to the market. In recent years, the No. 2 Gate Oil Mine has been dumping garbage on this road every day. Now, the garbage piles on the road are like small mountains. The garbage contains a large amount of broken glass, nails, and iron filings, which frequently puncture tires and pierce the soles of shoes.

	Bai Pan Village, Gaodian Township, Mian Yang County, Henan Province	Wáng Míngzhī, Wáng Ēncháng (spoken)
	Mian Yang County People's Radio Station	Liú Jiāfú (written)

### 垃圾阻塞通道　村民有路难行
王明芝　王恩长　刘家福
1989-02-22
第6版(读者来信)
专栏：监督哨

　　垃圾阻塞通道　村民有路难行
    我村村北一条通往河南油田下二门油矿的大道，是我村村民赶集的唯一通道。近几年来，下二门油矿每天把垃圾倒在这条大道上。现在，大道上的垃圾堆已似小山。垃圾里有大量的碎玻璃、废钉、铁屑等，扎坏车胎、刺穿鞋底的事时有发生。
