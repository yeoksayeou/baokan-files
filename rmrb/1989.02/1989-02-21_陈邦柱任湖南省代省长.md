Title: Chen Bangzhu Appointed Acting Governor of Hunan
Author: Cao Guanghui
Date: 1989-02-21
Edition: 1

Chen Bangzhu Appointed Acting Governor of Hunan

The seventh session of the seventh Standing Committee of the Hunan Provincial People's Congress decided yesterday to accept the resignation of Provincial Committee Secretary and Governor Xiong Qingquan from his post as governor. The 54-year-old Vice Governor Chen Bangzhu will act as governor.

### 陈邦柱任湖南省代省长
曹光晖
1989-02-21
第1版(要闻)
专栏：

　　陈邦柱任湖南省代省长
    新华社长沙２月２０日电  （记者曹光晖）湖南省七届人大常委会第七次会议昨天作出决定，接受省委书记、省长熊清泉辞去省长职务，由５４岁的副省长陈邦柱任代理省长。
