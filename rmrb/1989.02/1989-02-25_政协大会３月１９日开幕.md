Title: The Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference to Open on March 19
Author:
Date: 1989-02-25
Edition: 1

The spokesperson for the National Committee of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference, Lu Zhichao, announced today: The second session of the Seventh National Committee of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference will open on March 19 in Beijing, with a duration of approximately 10 days.

### 政协大会３月１９日开幕

1989-02-25
第1版(要闻)
专栏：

　　政协大会３月１９日开幕
    新华社北京２月２４日电  全国政协新闻发言人卢之超今天宣布：全国政协七届二次会议定于３月１９日在北京开幕，会期约１０天。
