Title: Setting Sun
Author: Zhang Xuefeng Liu Li
Date: 1989-02-24
Edition: 8

	Setting Sun
	Zhang Xuefeng (Painting)
	Liu Li (Poem)

	Many years
	Clouds gather and disperse, that ancient wooden boat
	Floats through one season after another
	Passes through one town after another, one islet after another
	One child after another with silver locks grows up
	One sturdy man after another
	The elderly
	So many years have passed, so many stories
	Drift away
	You, what do you want to tell us?
