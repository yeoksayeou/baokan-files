Title: Chinese Ambassador Meets with U.S. Secretary of State
Author: Zhang Qixun
Date: 1989-02-08
Edition: 3

	Chinese Ambassador Meets with U.S. Secretary of State

	Xinhua News Agency, Washington, February 6 - Reporter Zhang Qixun reports: Chinese Ambassador to the United States, Han Xu, met with U.S. Secretary of State James Baker this afternoon at the U.S. Department of State. The two sides engaged in a wide-ranging and friendly conversation.

	Secretary of State Baker will accompany U.S. President Bush on a visit to China on February 25.

### 我大使会见美国国务卿
张启昕
1989-02-08
第3版(国际)
专栏：

　　我大使会见美国国务卿
    本报华盛顿２月６日电  记者张启昕报道：中国驻美国大使韩叙今天下午在美国国务院会见了美国国务卿詹姆斯·贝克，双方进行了内容广泛的友好谈话。
    贝克国务卿将随同美国总统布什于２月２５日访问中国。
