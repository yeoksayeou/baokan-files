Title: U.S. Information Technology Products See Increased Production
Author:
Date: 1989-02-21
Edition: 3

According to materials released by the American Computer and Business Equipment Manufacturers Association on the 18th, the output value of information technology industry products in the United States in 1988 reached $67.705 billion, an increase of over 10 percent from $61.215 billion in 1987.

(Xinhua News Agency)

### 美信息技术产品增产

1989-02-21
第3版(国际新闻)
专栏：

　　美信息技术产品增产
    据美国计算机和商业设备制造商协会１８日公布的材料，１９８８年美国国内的信息技术工业产品产值达６７７．０５亿美元，比１９８７年的６１２．１５亿美元增加了１０％以上。　　　　　　　　　　　　　
　　（新华社）
