Title: Ghanaian Leaders Receive Our Newspaper's Reporting Team
Author:
Date: 1989-02-18
Edition: 6

Ghanaian leaders received the reporting team from the People's Daily today.

The New China News Agency reported from Accra on February 15: Member of the Ghanaian National Provisional Defense Council, Annan, said on February 14 that Ghana treasures its friendly relations with China.

Annan made these remarks when he received the People's Daily reporting team led by Luo Erzhuang, the director of the International Department of the People's Daily. He said that the friendly relations between Ghana and China have withstood the test of time, and Ghana hopes to strengthen these relations.

### 加纳领导人接见本报记者组

1989-02-18
第6版(国际)
专栏：

　　加纳领导人接见本报记者组
    新华社阿克拉２月１５日电  加纳全国临时保卫委员会委员安南１４日在这里说，加纳珍惜同中国的友好关系。
    安南是在以《人民日报》国际部主任罗尔庄率领的《人民日报》记者组拜会他时说这番话的。他说，加纳同中国的友好关系经受了时间的考验，加纳希望加强这种关系。
