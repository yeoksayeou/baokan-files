Title: Soviet and Israeli Foreign Ministers Meet
Author:
Date: 1989-02-24
Edition: 3

Soviet and Israeli foreign ministers held a two-and-a-half-hour meeting at the Soviet Embassy in Cairo on February 22. This was the first time the two foreign ministers had met outside the United Nations.

After the meeting, Shevardnadze told reporters that resolving the Middle East problem requires Israel to show goodwill.

Regarding the issue of convening an international peace conference on the Middle East, Shevardnadze said that differences still exist.

Arens said he believed that the talks with Shevardnadze were "very important." An official accompanying Arens on his visit said that holding an international peace conference on the Middle East under the auspices of the United Nations is still unacceptable to Israel.

### 苏以外长会晤  苏外长说分歧仍然存在  以外长说会谈非常重要

1989-02-24
第3版(国际新闻)
专栏：

　　苏以外长会晤
    苏外长说分歧仍然存在  以外长说会谈非常重要
    新华社开罗２月２２日电  苏联外长谢瓦尔德纳泽和以色列外长阿伦斯，２２日在苏联驻开罗大使馆举行了两个半小时的会谈。这是两国外长第一次在联合国以外举行会晤。
    会谈以后，谢瓦尔德纳泽对记者说，解决中东问题需要以色列表示出良好的意愿。
    关于召开中东和平国际会议问题，谢瓦尔德纳泽说，分歧仍然存在。
    阿伦斯说，他认为，同谢瓦尔德纳泽的会谈是“非常重要的”。一位陪同阿伦斯进行访问的官员说，召开在联合国主持下的中东和平国际会议对以色列来说，仍然是不可接受的。
