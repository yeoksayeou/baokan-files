Title: Wang Gang Company of the United States Donates to the Asian Games
Author:
Date: 1989-02-19
Edition: 3

Wang Wen Gang, who hails from Shandong Province, China, and later settled in the United States, is currently the chairman of the Wang Gang International Import and Export Company. He expressed his joy upon hearing about Beijing hosting the Asian Games and declared his support by donating $100,000 to the event. The donation ceremony was held on February 18 in Beijing.

### 美国王刚公司向亚运会捐款

1989-02-19
第3版(科教·文化·体育)
专栏：体育

　　美国王刚公司向亚运会捐款
    本报讯  祖籍中国山东省、后到美国定居的王文刚先生，现任美国王刚国际进出口贸易公司董事长。他听说北京举办亚运会很高兴，表示支持，向亚运会捐赠１０万美元。捐赠仪式１８日在京举行。
