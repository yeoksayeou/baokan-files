Title: Yugoslav President Visits Cyprus
Author:
Date: 1989-02-04
Edition: 6

Yugoslav President Visits Cyprus

The President of the Socialist Federal Republic of Yugoslavia, Dize Dardarevic, who is visiting Cyprus, expressed support on the 2nd for all efforts to resolve the Cyprus problem.

Dize Dardarevic, in his speech to the Cypriot Parliament, praised the proposal by Cypriot President Vasiliou to demilitarize Cyprus. He said that the Non-Aligned Movement would continue to contribute to the resolution of the Cyprus problem as it has always done.

Yugoslavia is one of the founding countries of the Non-Aligned Movement.

He pointed out that Cyprus should not be divided and should enjoy complete independence, sovereignty, territorial integrity, and unity.

### 南斯拉夫主席访塞浦路斯

1989-02-04
第6版(国际)
专栏：=国际新闻简报

　　南斯拉夫主席访塞浦路斯
    正在塞浦路斯访问的南斯拉夫社会主义联邦共和国主席团主席迪兹达雷维奇二日表示支持所有解决塞浦路斯问题的努力。
    迪兹达雷维奇在向塞浦路斯议会发表演讲时，赞扬塞浦路斯总统瓦西利乌提出的使塞浦路斯非军事化的建议。他说，不结盟运动将一如既往地为解决塞浦路斯问题作出贡献。
    南斯拉夫是不结盟运动的创始国之一。
    他指出，塞浦路斯不应当分裂，应该享有完全的独立、主权、领土完整和统一。
