Title: Macau Hosts Chinese Film Week
Author:
Date: 1989-02-14
Edition: 3

Macau Hosts Chinese Film Week

Xinhua News Agency, Macau, February 13 - The Chinese Film Week, organized by the Macau Cultural Association, began today in Macau.

The five-day film week will screen five films, including "Well," "King of the Children," "The Great Dictator," "Sunrise," and "Youth Sacrifice." All films shown during the film week will be free for the public to enjoy.

### 澳门举办中国电影周

1989-02-14
第3版(科教·文化·体育)
专栏：

　　澳门举办中国电影周
    新华社澳门２月１３日电  由澳门文化学会主办的中国电影周今天起在澳门举行。
    为期５天的电影周，将放映《老井》、《孩子王》、《非常大总统》、《日出》、《青春祭》等５部影片。电影周期间放映的影片，全部免费供市民欣赏。
