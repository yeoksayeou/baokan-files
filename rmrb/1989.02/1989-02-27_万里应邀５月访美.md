Title: Wan Li to Visit the U.S. in May
Author:
Date: 1989-02-27
Edition: 1

Wan Li to Visit the U.S. in May

    According to a report from our newspaper, during his visit to China, President Bush conveyed an invitation from Vice President Quayle for Chairman Wan Li to make an official visit to the United States in May of this year.

    President Bush said he looks forward to meeting Chairman Wan Li in Washington in May and playing tennis together.

### 万里应邀５月访美

1989-02-27
第1版(要闻)
专栏：

　　万里应邀５月访美
    本报讯  布什总统在访华期间，转达了奎尔副总统对万里委员长于今年５月正式访美的邀请。
    布什总统说，他期待着５月份在华盛顿与万里委员长相会，并一起打网球。
