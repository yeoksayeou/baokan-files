Title: Yan Zhen Completes Epic Poetic Novel "The Mountain Oath"
Author: Song Xia
Date: 1989-02-20
Edition: 4

Poet Yan Zhen has completed the epic poetic novel "The Mountain Oath" after more than twenty years of work. This is a monumental work spanning an entire historical period, consisting of over twenty thousand lines. The book features grand scenes and a multitude of characters; it includes both heroic and tragic war scenes and heart-wrenching tales of love and separation.

### 严阵完成长篇诗体小说《山盟》
松下
1989-02-20
第4版(文化生活·体育)
专栏：文苑剪影

　　严阵完成长篇诗体小说《山盟》
    诗人严阵用二十多年时间完成了诗体长篇小说《山盟》。这是一部概括了整整一个历史时期的长达两万行的著作。全书场面壮阔，人物繁多；既有可歌可泣的战争场面，又有生离死别的爱情故事。　　　　　
　　　
　　　　（松下）
