Title: Neither Pro-Western Nor Pro-Eastern: Saudi Arabia Emphasizes Diplomacy Should Serve National Interests
Author: Wú Yìhóng
Date: 1989-02-20
Edition: 3

The chairman of the organizing committee for the 5th World Youth Football Finals and the chairman of the Saudi Arabian Youth Welfare Committee, Prince Faisal bin Fahd, emphasized today that Saudi Arabia's foreign policy is neither pro-Western nor pro-Eastern, but rather serves the interests of Saudi Arabia itself.

Prince Faisal made these remarks during a meeting with foreign journalists and local reporters who are covering the 5th World Youth Football Finals. Prince Faisal is the eldest son of the Saudi King.

Prince Faisal said, "We advocate maintaining friendly relations with all countries in the world." He pointed out that although Saudi Arabia has not established diplomatic relations with some countries, this does not hinder Saudi Arabia from engaging in trade, economic, cultural, and sports exchanges with these countries. He expressed that Saudi Arabia will continue to develop these relationships.

When discussing Saudi Arabia's relations with Iran, Prince Faisal reiterated, "Saudi Arabia hopes to maintain friendly relations with all Islamic countries, first and foremost Iran, because it is an important country in the Gulf and a major Islamic nation."

### 既不亲西方　也不亲东方  沙特强调外交要符合本国利益
吴毅宏
1989-02-20
第3版(国际新闻)
专栏：

　　既不亲西方　也不亲东方    
    沙特强调外交要符合本国利益
    新华社利雅得２月１８日电  （记者吴毅宏）第５届世界杯青年足球决赛组织委员会主席、沙特阿拉伯青年福利委员会主席费萨尔·本·法赫德亲王今天在这里强调说，沙特的对外政策是既不亲西方，也不亲东方，而是为沙特本国利益服务。
    费萨尔亲王是在今天下午会见正在这里报道第５届世界杯青年足球决赛的外国记者和当地记者时说这番话的。费萨尔亲王是沙特国王的长子。
    费萨尔亲王说，“我们主张同世界上所有国家保持友好关系”。他指出，尽管沙特同一些国家还没有建立外交关系，但这不妨碍沙特同这些国家开展贸易、经济、文化、体育方面的交往。他表示，沙特将继续同这些国家发展这种关系。
    在谈到沙特同伊朗的关系时，费萨尔亲王重申：“沙特希望同所有伊斯兰国家都保持友好关系，首先是伊朗，因为它是海湾的一个重要国家，也是信奉伊斯兰教的一个大国”。
