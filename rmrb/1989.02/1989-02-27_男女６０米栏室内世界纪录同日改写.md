Title: Men's and Women's 60m Hurdles Indoor World Records Broken on the Same Day
Author:
Date: 1989-02-27
Edition: 4

On February 25, Soviet hurdler Igor Kazanoff set a new world record in the men's 60-meter hurdles with a time of 7.42 seconds at the Soviet Indoor Athletics Championships held in Moscow. On the same day, East German athlete Cornelia Oschkenat broke the women's 60-meter hurdles indoor world record with a time of 7.73 seconds at the three-nation indoor athletics meet in Vienna.

### 男女６０米栏室内世界纪录同日改写

1989-02-27
第4版(文化生活·体育)
专栏：体育

　　男女６０米栏室内世界纪录同日改写
    苏联跨栏运动员伊戈尔·卡扎诺夫２５日在莫斯科举行的苏联室内田径锦标赛中，以７秒４２的成绩刷新了男子６０米栏世界纪录。民主德国选手奥施克纳特２５日在维也纳举行的三国室内田径赛上，以７秒７３的成绩打破女子６０米栏世界室内纪录。　　　　　　　　　　　
　　　　　　　　　（新华社）
