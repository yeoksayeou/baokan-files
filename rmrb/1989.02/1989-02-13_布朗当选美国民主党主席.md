Title: Brown Elected Chairman of the U.S. Democratic Party
Author:
Date: 1989-02-13
Edition: 6

Ronald Brown was elected chairman of the Democratic National Committee of the United States on February 10, becoming the first African American to hold this position in the party's history.

The 47-year-old Democrat is a partner in a law firm in Washington. He has been involved in national politics since the 1980s, serving as the vice chairman of the Democratic National Committee from 1982 to 1985. He also served as an assistant to civil rights leader Jesse Jackson.

### 布朗当选美国民主党主席

1989-02-13
第6版(国际)
专栏：国际要闻简报

　　布朗当选美国民主党主席
    罗纳德·布朗十日当选为美国民主党全国委员会主席，成为该党有史以来的第一位黑人主席。
    这位四十七岁的民主党人是华盛顿一家法律事务所的合伙人，自八十年代开始便投身于国家政治事务，从一九八二年至一九八五年担任民主党全国委员会副主席。他还担任过民权运动领袖杰西·杰克逊的助手。
