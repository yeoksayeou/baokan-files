Title: Spring
Author: Yu Shuheng
Date: 1989-02-07
Edition: 4

	Spring
	Yu Shuheng

	Ice floes still bear the traces of the New Year,
	The willow flags have yet to sway,
	Spring, with a fiery temper,
	Wakes up after a short nap.

	Spring sets up camp in the snow,
	In the vegetable gardens of rural homes,
	Rows of plastic greenhouses stand,
	Inside, young green songs are sung,
	Attracting snowflakes from the sky,
	Crawling outside the greenhouses to eavesdrop.

	The poultry farms of contract holders
	Are hatching a springtime scene,
	On the warm beds, iron beaks
	Peck through round dreams,
	Clusters of yellow flowers bloom loudly.

	Children tug at the sky with their hands,
	Ropes tie them to beautiful shadows;
	Duck girls borrow the village's small river,
	A bamboo pole drives away the dawn;
	Seed experts want to partner with spring,
	He rides a motorcycle to check the soil moisture.

### 春天
于书恒
1989-02-07
第4版(副刊)
专栏：

　　春天
　　于书恒
    冰凌上还挂着正月，
    柳旗还未摆动，
    春，是个火脾气，
    打个盹儿就醒。
    春天到雪地里扎营，
    在农家的菜园里，
    搭起一排排塑料大棚，
    棚里正唱着青嫩的歌，
    招来满天的雪花儿，
    爬在棚外偷听。
    承包大户的鸡舍，
    正孵化着一个春日小景，
    温床上，一只只铁嘴，
    啄破了圆圆的梦，
    丛丛黄花开得闹哄哄。
    娃们手扯着高空，
    绳儿系着丽影；
    鸭姑娘借用村边小河，
    一根竹竿赶来了黎明；
    种子专家要与春天联营，
    他骑着摩托来察看墒情。
