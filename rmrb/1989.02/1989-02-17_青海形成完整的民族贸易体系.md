Title: Qinghai Establishes a Complete Ethnic Trade System
Author:
Date: 1989-02-17
Edition: 2

Since the Third Plenary Session of the Eleventh Central Committee, the vast ethnic minority areas of Qinghai Province have been undergoing a historic transformation from a natural economy to a commodity economy. A complete ethnic trade system composed of state-owned, collective, and individual economic components has gradually taken shape. Currently, the number of commercial outlets in the autonomous regions of the entire province has reached more than 15,000, which is more than sixteen times the number in 1978.

### 青海形成完整的民族贸易体系

1989-02-17
第2版(经济)
专栏：

    青海形成完整的民族贸易体系
  　党的十一届三中全会以来，青海省广大少数民族地区正在实现着由自然经济向商品经济转化的历史性变革，一个由国营、集体、个体等多种经济成分组成的完整的民族贸易体系逐步形成。目前，全省民族自治地区的社会商业网点已达一万五千多个，比一九七八年增长十六倍以上。
    　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　（据新华社）
