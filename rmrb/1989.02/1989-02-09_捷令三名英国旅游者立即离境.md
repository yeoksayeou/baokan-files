Title: Czechoslovakia Orders Three British Tourists to Leave Immediately
Author:
Date: 1989-02-09
Edition: 6

Czechoslovakia has ordered three British tourists to leave the country immediately. According to a report by the Czech News Agency on the 7th, these three British individuals distributed leaflets in Prague and Brno promoting a Czech opposition group.

### 捷令三名英国旅游者立即离境

1989-02-09
第6版(国际)
专栏：国际要闻简报

　  捷令三名英国旅游者立即离境
    捷克斯洛伐克已勒令三名英国旅游者立即离开捷克斯洛伐克。据捷通社七日报道，这三名英国人在布拉格和布尔诺散发了宣传捷克斯洛伐克一反对派组织的传单。
