Title: U.S. President Bush
Author:
Date: 1989-02-25
Edition: 1

George Herbert Walker Bush, born on June 12, 1924, in Milton, Massachusetts. In 1942, he joined the Navy. In 1948, he graduated from Yale University with a bachelor's degree in economics.

In 1966, Bush was elected as a member of the House of Representatives, and in 1968, he was re-elected. From 1971 to 1972, he served as the permanent representative of the United States to the United Nations. From 1973 to 1974, he served as the chairman of the Republican National Committee. From 1974 to 1975, he served as the chief of the United States Liaison Office in the People's Republic of China. From 1976 to 1977, he served as the director of the Central Intelligence Agency. From 1980 to 1988, he served as the Vice President.

In November 1988, Bush was elected as the 41st President of the United States. Today, January 20, he was sworn in.

### 美国总统布什

1989-02-25
第1版(要闻)
专栏：人物介绍

　　美国总统布什    
    乔治·赫伯特·沃尔克·布什，１９２４年６月１２日生于马萨诸塞州米尔顿。１９４２年加入海军服役。１９４８年毕业于耶鲁大学，获经济学学士学位。
    １９６６年当选为国会众议员，１９６８年连任。１９７１年至１９７２年任美国常任联合国代表。１９７３年至１９７４年任美国共和党全国委员会主席。１９７４年至１９７５年任美国驻中华人民共和国联络处主任。１９７６年至１９７７年任美国中央情报局局长。１９８０年至１９８８年任副总统。
    １９８８年１１月，布什当选为美国第４１任总统。今天１月２０日宣誓就职。　　　　　　　　　　　
　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　（据新华社）
