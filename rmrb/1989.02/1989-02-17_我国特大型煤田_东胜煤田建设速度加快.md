Title: Accelerated Construction of China's Major Coalfield - Dongsheng Coalfield
Author: Zheng Yanyong
Date: 1989-02-17
Edition: 2

Last year, the mine completed construction investments totaling 223.2 million yuan, which is 1.26 times the total of the previous three years. It has now formed a production capacity of over 100 million tons.

The Dongsheng Coalfield is located in the central and southern parts of the Ordos Plateau in Inner Mongolia. The proven reserves amount to 72.752 billion tons, accounting for one-tenth of the nation's proven coal reserves. The estimated reserves are 669 billion tons.

### 我国特大型煤田——东胜煤田建设速度加快
郑彦永
1989-02-17
第2版(经济)
专栏：

    我国特大型煤田——东胜煤田建设速度加快
  　去年，这个矿完成矿建投资二千二百三十二万元，相当于前三年总和的一点二六倍，已形成一百万吨以上生产能力。
    东胜煤田位于内蒙古鄂尔多斯高原的中、南部，探明储量七百二十七点五二亿吨，相当于全国探明煤炭储量的十分之一；预测储量六千六百九十亿吨。
    　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　（郑彦永）
