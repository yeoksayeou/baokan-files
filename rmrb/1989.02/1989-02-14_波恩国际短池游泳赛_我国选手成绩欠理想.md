Title: The Seventh Bonn International Short Course Swimming Meet
Author: Weng Zhenbao
Date: 1989-02-14
Edition: 3

The seventh Bonn International Short Course Swimming Meet concluded today in Bonn. The 21-year-old East German athlete Susanne Bonpierre set a new world best time of 2 minutes 22.92 seconds in the women's 200-meter breaststroke. Additionally, two athletes set two European best times.

A total of 300 athletes from 29 countries participated in the three-day competition.

Today, East German athlete Niels Rudoph shortened the men's 50-meter butterfly best time to 24.31 seconds. British athlete Nick Gillingham improved the European best time for the men's 200-meter breaststroke to 2 minutes 9.81 seconds. Due to insufficient training after the Olympics, Chinese athletes' performances were subpar and none of them advanced to the finals.

### 波恩国际短池游泳赛  我国选手成绩欠理想
翁振葆
1989-02-14
第3版(科教·文化·体育)
专栏：体育

　　波恩国际短池游泳赛    
    我国选手成绩欠理想
    新华社波恩２月１２日电  （记者翁振葆）第七届波恩游泳节短池游泳赛今天在这里结束，２１岁的民主德国选手苏姗·博尼克以２分２２秒９２的成绩创造了女子２００米蛙泳世界最好成绩。此外还有２人２次创造２项欧洲最好成绩。
    来自２９个国家的３００名运动员参加了这次历时３天的比赛。
    民主德国选手尼尔斯·鲁多夫今天把男子５０米蝶泳最好成绩缩短到２４秒３１。英国选手尼克·吉林厄姆今天在男子２００米蛙泳赛中把该项欧洲最好成绩提高到２分０９秒８１。中国选手因奥运会后训练不足，成绩欠佳，均未能进入决赛。
