Title: Soviet Foreign Minister Returns to Moscow
Author: Shen Faliang
Date: 1989-02-08
Edition: 3

Soviet Foreign Minister Returns to Moscow

Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, February 6 - (Reporter Shen Faliang) After concluding his official visit to China and his working visit to Pakistan, Soviet Foreign Minister Eduard Shevardnadze returned to Moscow this afternoon.

At the airport, Shevardnadze answered questions from Chinese journalists. He said that this visit to China was very successful and fruitful. The most memorable part was his meetings with Deng Xiaoping and Li Peng, and he also had very interesting discussions with Qian Qichen. He expressed that the Soviet Union has high hopes for the highest-level meeting between the Soviet Union and China, and now a lot of preparatory work should be done for this, including preparing various documents related to the new principles of Sino-Soviet relations.

The Soviet Foreign Minister visited China from February 2 to 4.

### 苏联外长回到莫斯科
沈法良
1989-02-08
第3版(国际)
专栏：

　　苏联外长回到莫斯科
    新华社莫斯科２月６日电  （记者沈法良）苏联外交部长谢瓦尔德纳泽结束对中国的正式访问和对巴基斯坦的工作访问之后，今天下午返回莫斯科。
    谢瓦尔德纳泽在机场回答了中国记者的提问。他说，这次访华非常成功，非常富有成果，印象最深的是同邓小平和李鹏的会晤，同钱其琛也进行了很有兴趣的会谈。他表示，苏联对苏中最高级会晤寄予厚望，现在应该为此作大量的准备工作，准备有关苏中关系新原则的各种文件。
    苏联外长是２月２日至４日对中国进行正式访问的。
