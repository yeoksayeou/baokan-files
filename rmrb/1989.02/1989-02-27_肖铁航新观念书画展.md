Title: XIAO Tiehang's New Concepts Calligraphy and Painting Exhibition
Author:
Date: 1989-02-27
Edition: 4

The exhibition "XIAO Tiehang's New Concepts Calligraphy and Painting" will open on February 21st at the China Art Gallery. The artist graduated from the Guangzhou Academy of Fine Arts in 1962 and is currently the general manager of the Shenzhen Public Art Company. In his spare time, he continues to engage in artistic creation.

### 肖铁航新观念书画展

1989-02-27
第4版(文化生活·体育)
专栏：

　　肖铁航新观念书画展
