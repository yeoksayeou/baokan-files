Title: The National Committee of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference Has Established Ties with Nearly 30 Countries
Author:
Date: 1989-02-17
Edition: 4

The National Committee of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference (CPCCC) has established ties with nearly 30 countries. Yesterday, a tea party was held to entertain some foreign envoys stationed in China.

Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, February 16 - The Foreign Affairs Committee of the National Committee of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference held a spring tea party in the CPCCC Hall this afternoon to entertain some foreign envoys stationed in China.

Qian Qichen, Vice Chairman of the National Committee of the CPCCC, said in his speech at the tea party that in the past year, under the leadership of the Communist Party of China, the CPCCC has played an important role in China's democratic political construction, economic construction, and various aspects of social life, as well as in the struggle to maintain world peace.

He said that after the first session of the Seventh National Committee, the CPCCC established the Foreign Affairs Committee. Most members of the Foreign Affairs Committee are experienced former ambassadors who have returned to the country. In the past year, they have done a lot of work to promote the people's diplomacy of the CPCCC. Now, the CPCCC has established friendly relations with nearly 30 countries.

He said, "In the foreign affairs activities of the CPCCC, we have received the enthusiastic support and help of the ambassadors and friends from various embassies. On behalf of the CPCCC, I would like to express my heartfelt thanks to you all."

He said, "A new year has begun. The CPCCC is willing to continuously expand exchanges with friendly organizations and individuals from various countries around the world, deepen our understanding and friendship. We believe that in the future friendly exchanges with foreign countries, we will continue to receive your enthusiastic help and support."

Vice Chairpersons of the National Committee of the CPCCC Ma Wenrui, Qian Zhengying, and Sima Yimaiti attended the tea party.

Artists from the capital performed solo songs, ensemble performances, and other art programs at the tea party.

### 全国政协已与近三十个国家建立联系  昨举行茶话会招待部分国家驻华使节

1989-02-17
第4版(政治·法律·社会)
专栏：

　　全国政协已与近三十个国家建立联系    
    昨举行茶话会招待部分国家驻华使节
    新华社北京２月１６日电  全国政协外事委员会今天下午在政协礼堂举行迎春茶话会，招待部分国家驻华使节。
    全国政协副主席程思远在茶话会上讲话时说，在过去的一年里，人民政协在中国共产党领导下，在我国的民主政治建设、经济建设和社会生活的各个方面，在维护世界和平的斗争中发挥了重要作用。
    他说，七届一次会议以后，全国政协建立了外事委员会。外委会的大部分成员是离任回国的富有经验的老大使。在过去的一年里，他们为了开展政协的人民外交活动作了大量工作。现在全国政协已与近３０个国家建立了友好往来。
    他说，“在政协的外事活动中得到各位大使先生和各个使馆的朋友们的热情支持和帮助，在此，我谨代表全国政协向你们表示衷心的感谢。”
    他说，“新的一年已经开始了，人民政协愿意不断扩大同世界各国友好组织、友好人士之间的交往，增进我们之间的了解和友谊。我们相信，在今后我会的对外友好交往中，我们将会继续得到你们的热情帮助和支持。”
    全国政协副主席马文瑞、钱正英、司马义·艾买提等出席了茶话会。
    首都文艺工作者在茶话会上表演了独唱、民乐合奏等文艺节目。
