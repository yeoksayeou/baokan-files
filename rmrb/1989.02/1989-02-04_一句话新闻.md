One-Sentence News

	* Spanish Foreign Minister Ordóñez made a brief visit to Italy on the 2nd, exchanging views with Italian Foreign Minister Andreotti on the construction of the European Community, relations between the Community and non-Community countries, and the situation in the Middle East.
	* The People's Revolutionary Movement of Zaire (ruling party) proposed to the party chairman and state president Mobutu to suspend or sever diplomatic relations with Belgium.

1989-02-04
第6版(国际)
专栏：一句话新闻

    △澳大利亚总理霍克３日在曼谷强调指出，西哈努克亲王是在解决柬埔寨问题中起重要作用的人物。
