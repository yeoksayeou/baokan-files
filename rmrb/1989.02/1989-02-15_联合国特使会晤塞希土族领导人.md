Title: UN Envoy Meets with Cypriot Turkish Leaders
Author:
Date: 1989-02-15
Edition: 6

UN envoy Oscar Camill met separately with Cyprus President and Greek Cypriot leader Vasiliou and Cypriot Turkish leader Denktash in Nicosia on the morning of the 13th. He briefed them on his discussions with the UN Secretary-General regarding the Cyprus issue.

Camill conveyed letters from the UN Secretary-General to both Vasiliou and Denktash. These letters addressed procedural matters related to the peace talks between the Greek and Turkish Cypriot leaders.

Camill returned to Cyprus on the evening of the 12th after reporting to the UN Secretary-General on the latest developments in the peace talks between the two sides under UN auspices.

### 联合国特使会晤塞希土族领导人

1989-02-15
第6版(国际)
专栏：国际新闻简报

　　联合国特使会晤塞希土族领导人
    联合国驻塞浦路斯特使奥斯卡·卡米利昂十三日上午在尼科西亚分别同塞浦路斯总统、希族领导人瓦西利乌和塞浦路斯土族领导人登克塔什举行了会谈，向他们介绍了他同联合国秘书长磋商塞浦路斯问题的情况。
    卡米利昂向瓦西利乌和登克塔什转交了联合国秘书长给他们的信。这封信涉及希腊族和土耳其族领导人和平谈判的程序问题。
    卡米利昂是在向联合国秘书长汇报在联合国主持下塞浦路斯两派和平谈判的最新发展后，于十二日晚上返回塞浦路斯的。
