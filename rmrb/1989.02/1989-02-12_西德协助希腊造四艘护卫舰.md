Title: West Germany Assists Greece in Building Four Frigates
Author:
Date: 1989-02-12
Edition: 6

West Germany Assists Greece in Building Four Frigates

The Greek Ministry of Defense announced on the 10th that Greece and West Germany signed an agreement on the 10th for West Germany to assist in building four frigates for the Greek Navy.

According to the agreement, the first frigate will be built in West Germany, while the remaining three will be constructed in Greek shipyards using materials and services provided by West Germany.

The Greek Ministry of Defense stated that this agreement is part of West Germany's military aid to Greece.

### 西德协助希腊造四艘护卫舰

1989-02-12
第6版(国际)
专栏：国际新闻简报

　　西德协助希腊造四艘护卫舰
    希腊国防部十日宣布，希腊和西德十日签署了一项由西德协助，为希腊海军建造四艘护卫舰的协定。
    根据这项协定，第一艘护卫舰将在西德建造，另外三艘将使用西德提供的材料和服务在希腊的造船厂建造。
    希腊国防部说，这一协定是西德向希腊提供军事援助中的一部分。
    　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　（据新华社）
