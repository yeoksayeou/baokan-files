Title: A French Expert Who Has Worked in China for 35 Years Celebrates Her 80th Birthday with Chinese and Foreign Friends
Author: Yang Guoqiang
Date: 1989-02-24
Edition: 2

	Chinese and foreign friends in Beijing gathered tonight at the Beijing Hotel to celebrate the 35th anniversary of Denise's work in China and her 80th birthday. Members of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference (CPPCC) Huang Hua, Rong Jiyuan, and Kang Keqing sent their congratulations to Denise for her good health and longevity. Liu Deyou, Deputy Minister of the Ministry of Culture, delivered a speech in which he praised Denise as a "true friend and sincere friend of the Chinese people." He said, "Denise loves China and the Chinese people. She regards the revolutionary and construction causes of the Chinese people as her own. She has made valuable contributions to China's foreign publicity and publishing work."

	Denise was born in Paris in 1909 and married the Chinese painter Li Fengbai, who was studying in France, in 1936. After coming to China with her husband in 1953, she has been working at the Foreign Languages Press. Denise's memoir, "Love That Never Fades," written in an essay style, was also officially published and distributed in Beijing today.

### 自法来华辛勤工作３５年  中外人士为戴妮丝祝寿
杨国强
1989-02-24
第2版(国内新闻)
专栏：

　　自法来华辛勤工作３５年
    中外人士为戴妮丝祝寿
    据新华社北京２月２３日电  （记者杨国强）首都８０多名中外人士今晚在北京饭店集会，庆祝外文出版社法国专家戴妮丝来华工作３５周年暨８０寿辰。中顾委常委黄华、全国人大常委会副委员长雷洁琼、全国政协副主席康克清祝戴妮丝健康长寿。文化部副部长刘德有致辞称戴妮丝是“中国人民的挚友、诤友”。他说，“戴妮丝热爱中国和中国人民，把中国人民的革命和建设事业当作自己的事业，为中国的对外报道及出版工作做出了宝贵的贡献。”
    戴妮丝１９０９年生于巴黎，１９３６年与赴法勤工俭学的中国画家李风白结婚。１９５３年随丈夫来华后，一直在外文出版社工作。戴妮丝以散文笔调撰写的回忆录《爱是不会凋谢的》也于今天在京正式出版发行。
