Title: Commemorating the 90th Anniversary of the Birth of the People's Artist Lao She
Author: Zhang Shiying
Date: 1989-02-04
Edition: 3

On the evening of February 2, the newly opened "Lao She Teahouse" in Beijing was bustling with activity, with red lanterns hanging and a steady stream of visitors. Over 160 cultural figures from the capital gathered here to commemorate the 90th anniversary of the birth of the People's Artist Lao She.

At the event, Li Ximing, Secretary of the Beijing Municipal Committee of the Communist Party of China, and prominent cultural figures such as Wu Zuguang, Yin Shoushi, Deng Youmei, Yu Zhi, and Yin Shengxi, the General Manager of the Big Bowl Tea Trade Group Company, spoke, expressing their admiration for Mr. Lao She. Pu Jie attended the event and inscribed a calligraphy piece. Renowned writers Ba Jin and Bing Xin sent telegrams and letters to pay their respects. Lao She's wife, Hu Qiongqing, attended the memorial with her children.

Several famous cultural workers performed excerpts from Lao She's plays, folk arts, and children's songs that he created during his lifetime.

### 纪念人民艺术家老舍９０诞辰　“老舍茶馆”里演老舍剧作
张世英
1989-02-04
第3版(科教·文化·体育)
专栏：=文化天地

　　纪念人民艺术家老舍９０诞辰
　　“老舍茶馆”里演老舍剧作
    本报讯  记者张世英报道：２月２日晚，北京新开张的“老舍茶馆”门前红灯高悬，车水马龙，比往日更加热闹。首都１６０多位文艺界人士会聚这里，纪念人民艺术家老舍先生９０诞辰。
    会上，中共北京市委书记李锡铭，文艺界知名人士吴祖光、尹瘦石、邓友梅、于是之和大碗茶商贸集团公司总经理尹盛喜等发言，表达了对老舍先生的崇敬之情。溥杰到会并题字。著名作家巴金、冰心来电、来信，以示缅怀。老舍夫人胡絜青携子女出席了纪念会。
    一些著名文艺工作者在会上表演了老舍生前创作的话剧、曲艺、儿歌等作品的片断。
