Title: Dissonant Notes in the Process of Politically Resolving the Cambodian Issue
Author:
Date: 1989-02-16
Edition: 6

Political resolution of the Cambodian issue encounters dissonant notes

Vietnamese Foreign Minister Nguyen Co Thach has openly threatened that if the second informal meeting on the Cambodian issue in Jakarta fails to reach an agreement, Vietnam will not honor its commitment to withdraw all troops from Cambodia by September this year.

Nguyen Co Thach made these remarks today in Hanoi during an interview with Agence France-Presse. He said, "If next week's informal meeting in Jakarta fails to reach an agreement among the Cambodian parties, Hanoi, and ASEAN countries, we will no longer be bound by the timetable, and then it will be up to us to decide when to withdraw from Cambodia."

Nguyen Co Thach further stated that if the Jakarta meeting fails, "the issue of Vietnam's withdrawal from Cambodia will no longer be part of the plan for a political solution to the Cambodian issue."

### 政治解决柬埔寨问题进程中的不和谐音  阮基石说越可能不履行九月撤军保证

1989-02-16
第6版(国际)
专栏：

　　政治解决柬埔寨问题进程中的不和谐音   
    阮基石说越可能不履行九月撤军保证
    新华社北京２月１５日电  越南外长阮基石今天公然扬言，如果第二次关于柬埔寨问题的雅加达会议不能达成协议的话，越南将不履行其今年９月从柬埔寨全部撤军的保证。
    阮基石是今天在河内接受法新社记者的采访时讲这番话的。他说，“如果下星期雅加达非正式会议时，柬埔寨各方、河内与东盟国家之间不能达成协议，我们将不再受时间表的约束，那时将由我们来决定”何时从柬埔寨撤军了。
    阮基石还说，如果雅加达会议失败，“越南撤军问题将不再作为政治解决柬埔寨问题的方案的一部分来处理了”。
