Title: Advanced Methods for Early Fetal Detection at Peking Union Medical College Hospital
Author: Hǎo Qìng
Date: 1989-02-27
Edition: 2

	Peking Union Medical College Hospital's obstetrics department uses advanced methods to detect early fetal conditions. It can extract fetal villi or maternal amniotic fluid when the fetus is only two months old for testing. This allows for a diagnosis of whether the fetus's chromosomes and genes are normal, as well as the presence of any enzyme deficiencies.

### 胎儿早期检测  协和手段先进
郝庆
1989-02-27
第2版(国内新闻)
专栏：

　　胎儿早期检测  协和手段先进
    据新华社北京２月２６日电  （记者郝庆）北京协和医院妇科用先进手段检测早期胎儿情况，可在胎儿仅有两个月时，取出胎儿绒毛或母体羊水加以检测，对胎儿的染色体、基因是否正常，以及有无酶的缺欠做出诊断。
