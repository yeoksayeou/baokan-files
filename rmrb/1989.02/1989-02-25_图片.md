Title: Art Exhibitions
Author: Wei Qimei, Wang Zunong, Wu Xinghong
Date: 1989-02-25
Edition: 5

	上：枷锁
	This is a depiction of the shackles that bind people, symbolizing oppression and the struggle for freedom.

	左：“这回孩子们就平安了！”
	This artwork captures a moment of relief and joy, portraying a parent's heartfelt wish for the safety and well-being of their children.

	右：×县“文艺会演”
	This piece showcases the vibrant cultural activities in X County, highlighting the community's artistic talents and the spirit of unity through performances.
