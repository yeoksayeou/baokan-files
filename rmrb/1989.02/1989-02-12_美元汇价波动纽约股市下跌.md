Title: Dollar Exchange Rate Fluctuates, New York Stock Market Falls
Author:
Date: 1989-02-12
Edition: 6

On the 10th, the dollar exchange rate fluctuated; the New York stock market fell.

On February 10, at the close of the Tokyo foreign exchange market, the dollar-to-yen exchange rate was 1:127.65, down 1.74 yen from the previous day's closing price. In the Frankfurt foreign exchange market, the dollar-to-West German mark exchange rate dropped from 1:1.8598 the day before to 1:1.8410. However, when the New York foreign exchange market closed, the dollar-to-West German mark exchange rate rose from 1:1.8503 the day before to 1:1.8705, and the dollar-to-yen exchange rate rose from 1:128.03 to 1:128.68.

(According to Xinhua News Agency)

### 美元汇价波动纽约股市下跌

1989-02-12
第6版(国际)
专栏：国际新闻简报

　　美元汇价波动纽约股市下跌
    十日，美元汇价出现波动；纽约股市下跌。
    二月十日东京外汇市场收盘时，美元对日元的比价为一比一百二十七点六五，比九日的收盘价下跌一点七四日元；在法兰克福外汇市场，美元对西德马克的比价却由前天的一比一点八五九八降到一比一点八四一零。但是，当纽约外汇市场收盘时，美元对西德马克的比价由前天的一比一点八五零三上升到一比一点八七零五，对日元的比价由一比一百二十八点零三上升到一比一百二十八点六八。
    　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　（据新华社）
