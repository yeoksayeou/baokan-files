Title: Sirimavo Bandaranaike Injured in Bomb Attack
Author:
Date: 1989-02-07
Edition: 3

	Sirimavo Bandaranaike, former Prime Minister of Sri Lanka, was injured in a bomb attack during an election campaign on February 5. She sustained minor injuries to her leg.

	During an election rally in Hingurakgoda in eastern Sri Lanka, as the 72-year-old leader of the Freedom Party, Sirimavo Bandaranaike, was shaking hands with supporters from the stage, three bombs exploded nearby. Two security personnel, one police officer, and her driver, among 38 others, were injured. Bandaranaike was injured by shrapnel to her leg.

### 班达拉奈克夫人遭袭击受轻伤

1989-02-07
第3版(国际)
专栏：国际新闻简报

　　班达拉奈克夫人遭袭击受轻伤
    斯里兰卡前总理班达拉奈克夫人五日在议会竞选活动中遭到炸弹袭击，她的腿部受轻伤。
    在斯里兰卡东部的欣古拉克戈德举行的竞选集会上，正当七十二岁的自由党领导人班达拉奈克夫人从讲台上与支持者们握手之时，三枚炸弹在她附近爆炸。两名负责安全的人员、一名警察和她的司机等三十八人受伤，班达拉奈克夫人被飞来的弹片擦伤了腿部。
                                    （据新华社）
