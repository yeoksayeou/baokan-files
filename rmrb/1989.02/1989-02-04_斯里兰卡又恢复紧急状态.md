Title: Sri Lanka Reimposes Emergency Rule
Author:
Date: 1989-02-04
Edition: 6

Sri Lanka has reimposed emergency rule. According to a report from Colombo newspapers on the third, due to the escalating violence in Sri Lanka, the Sri Lankan military has decided to reinstate emergency rule, strengthen security measures, and maintain social order.

Some anti-government armed groups in Sri Lanka have been engaged in sabotage activities. Approximately six hundred people died in violent incidents in January. The nationwide parliamentary elections on February 15 also became a target for armed elements. Recent victims are mostly members or supporters of the ruling party.

### 斯里兰卡又恢复紧急状态

1989-02-04
第6版(国际)
专栏：=国际新闻简报

　　斯里兰卡又恢复紧急状态
    据科伦坡报纸三日报道，鉴于斯里兰卡暴力活动逐步升级，斯里兰卡军方决定恢复紧急状态，加强戒备，维持社会治安。
    斯里兰卡一些反政府武装组织一直在搞破坏活动。今年一月份约有六百人死于暴力事件。二月十五日的全国议会大选又成了武装分子的破坏目标。最近的一些受害者多数为执政党成员或支持者。
