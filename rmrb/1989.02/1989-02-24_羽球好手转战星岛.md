Title: Badminton Stars Compete in Singapore
Author:
Date: 1989-02-24
Edition: 4

The Konica Cup badminton tournament in Singapore began on the 22nd. In the first day's men's and women's singles matches, four Chinese players won, while three lost.

### 羽球好手转战星岛
