Title: Volunteering to Write Spring Couplets
Author: Wang Tian Ding
Date: 1989-02-04
Edition: 1

Before the Spring Festival, the Writers' Association of Wuzhi County, Henan Province, organized more than 20 new and old calligraphy enthusiasts to go to the streets and volunteer to write Spring Festival couplets for the farmers.
