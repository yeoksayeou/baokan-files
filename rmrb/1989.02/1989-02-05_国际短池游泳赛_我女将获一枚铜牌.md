Title: Chinese Women Swimmers Win a Bronze Medal at the International Short Course Swimming Competition
Author:
Date: 1989-02-05
Edition: 3

The fourth Coca-Cola Cup International Short Course Swimming Championship opened on February 3 in Paris. Chinese athletes won a bronze medal in the women's 4x100-meter freestyle relay final with a time of 3 minutes and 52.07 seconds. Huang Xiaomin, who won the silver medal in the women's 200-meter breaststroke at the Seoul Olympics, has been suffering from a fever for several days. Although she participated in the competition, she did not advance to the finals.

### 国际短池游泳赛  我女将获一枚铜牌

1989-02-05
第3版(科教·文化·体育)
专栏：体育

　　国际短池游泳赛
　  我女将获一枚铜牌
    第四届可口可乐杯国际短池游泳赛３日在巴黎开幕。中国选手在女子４×１００米自由泳接力决赛中以３分５２秒０７的成绩夺得铜牌。汉城奥运会女子２００米蛙泳银牌获得者、中国选手黄晓敏数日来一直发烧，虽参加了比赛，但未能进入决赛。　　　　　　　
　　　　（据新华社）
