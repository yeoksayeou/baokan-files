Title: Premier Li Peng Meets with Outgoing Cameroonian Ambassador
Author:
Date: 1989-01-31
Edition: 4

Premier Li Peng met with the outgoing Ambassador of the Republic of Cameroon to China, Jean Ketchia, this afternoon at Zhongnanhai.

Jean Ketchia assumed his post as the Cameroonian Ambassador to China in August 1985.

### 李鹏会见喀麦隆离任大使

1989-02-01
第4版(政治·法律·社会)
专栏：

　　李鹏会见喀麦隆离任大使
    新华社北京１月３１日电  国务院总理李鹏今天下午在中南海会见了即将离任的喀麦隆共和国驻中国大使让·克恰。
    让·克恰是于１９８５年８月出任喀麦隆驻华大使的。
