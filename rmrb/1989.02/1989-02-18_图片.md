Title: A Dedicated Bus Ticket Seller
Author: Tao Junfeng
Date: 1989-02-18
Edition: 4

Zhao Li, a female ticket seller from the Jinan City Public Bus Company, is committed to ensuring that students are not late for class. Every month, she takes the initiative to prepare the bus monthly tickets for the students of Nanxin Village Primary School in Jinan City and personally delivers them to each student.
