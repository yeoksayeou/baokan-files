Title: A Good Textbook for Situational Education: "Challenges and Countermeasures"
Author:
Date: 1989-02-10
Edition: 2

A good textbook for situational education, "Challenges and Countermeasures – 100 Questions on Securing an Agricultural Bumper Harvest," compiled by the Propaganda Bureau of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of China, will soon be published by the China University of Political Science and Law Press. The book systematically propagandizes and explains the various policies and guidelines of the Central Committee and the State Council on securing an agricultural bumper harvest in 1989, and answers some questions of general concern to a broad range of rural cadres and the masses. It can be used as a reference for various localities in conducting situational and task education in rural areas.

					(According to Xinhua News Agency)

### 形势教育的好教材《挑战与对策》一书于近日内出版发行

1989-02-10
第2版(经济)
专栏：

    形势教育的好教材《挑战与对策》一书于近日内出版发行　为适应农村进行形势教育的需要，由中共中央宣传部宣传局组织编写的《挑战与对策——夺取农业丰收百题》一书，将于近日内由中国政法大学出版社出版发行。该书比较系统地宣传解释了中央、国务院关于夺取１９８９年农业丰收决定的各项方针政策，解答了广大农村干部和群众普遍关心的一些问题，可供各地农村在进行形势与任务教育时参考。　　　
　　　　　　　　　　　　　（据新华社）
