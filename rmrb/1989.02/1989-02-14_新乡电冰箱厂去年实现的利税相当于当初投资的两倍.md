Title: Xinxiang Refrigerator Factory's Profits and Taxes Double Initial Investment
Author:
Date: 1989-02-14
Edition: 2

By the end of 1984, the leadership of Xinxiang Refrigerator Factory understood market trends and decided to adopt the method of external cooperation and internal linkage to undertake the refrigerator production project. The factory introduced the complete set of refrigerator production technology and equipment from Philips Company. It is reported that from the project's initiation to the mass production of refrigerators, it took only 16 months, making it a high-speed, high-efficiency introduction project recognized by Henan Province. In the year of formal production, 1987, the total industrial output value reached 53.12 million yuan, achieving a tax and profit of 10.89 million yuan, almost equivalent to the total output value and profit of the factory's 30 years of operation.

### 新乡电冰箱厂去年实现的利税相当于当初投资的两倍

1989-02-14
第2版(经济)
专栏：

　　新乡电冰箱厂去年实现的利税相当于当初投资的两倍
  　１９８４年底，该厂领导了解市场动态，决定采取外引内联的方法，上冰箱生产项目。这个厂从飞利浦公司引进全套冰箱生产技术和设备。据介绍，这个厂从立项到冰箱投入批量生产，只用了１６个月的时间，被河南省评为高速高效引进项目。在正式投产的１９８７年里，工业总产值就达５３１２万元，实现税利１０８９万元，几乎是建厂３０年产值和利润的总和。　　　
　　　　　　　　　　　　　（据新华社）
