Title: Images of Mars' Moon Phobos Sent Back by Soviet Mars Probe
Author:
Date: 1989-02-25
Edition: 3

The Soviet interplanetary probe "Phobos 1," which is currently orbiting Mars, recently transmitted the first image of Mars' moon Phobos back to Earth. This image was taken by the probe's automatic television camera system when "Phobos 1" was between 860 kilometers and 1,130 kilometers away from Phobos on February 21st.

(Xinhua News Agency, fax photo)

### 苏联火星探测器发回的火星卫星图像（图片）

1989-02-25
第3版(国际新闻)
专栏：

    苏联火星探测器发回的火星卫星图像
    正绕火星飞行的苏联星际探测器“火卫一”号近日向地面传回的火星卫星火卫一的第一张图像。这张图像是当“火卫一”号探测器2月21日距离火卫一860公里至1130公里时，其自动电视摄影系统进行拍摄并传回地面的。
                            新华社发（传真照片）
