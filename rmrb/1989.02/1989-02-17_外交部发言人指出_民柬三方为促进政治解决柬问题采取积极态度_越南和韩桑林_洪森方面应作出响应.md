Title: Spokesperson of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs: The Three Factions of the Democratic Kampuchea Show a Positive Attitude Towards Promoting a Political Solution to the Cambodian Issue; Vietnam and the Heng Samrin-Hun Sen Side Should Respond
Author:
Date: 1989-02-17
Edition: 4

The spokesperson of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs, Li Zhaoxing, said today in Beijing that the three factions of the Democratic Kampuchea recently held a special meeting in Beijing. They proposed the implementation methods for Prince Sihanouk's five-point peace plan for the political solution to the Cambodian issue, once again demonstrating the positive attitude of the three factions of the Democratic Kampuchea in promoting a fair and reasonable political solution to the Cambodian issue. Vietnam and the Heng Samrin-Hun Sen side should respond positively.

The spokesperson made these remarks at a press conference this afternoon in response to a reporter's question.

Li Zhaoxing also said, "We believe that Prince Sihanouk's five-point peace plan for the political solution to the Cambodian issue is reasonable."

### 外交部发言人指出  民柬三方为促进政治解决柬问题采取积极态度  越南和韩桑林—洪森方面应作出响应

1989-02-17
第4版(政治·法律·社会)
专栏：

　　外交部发言人指出     
    民柬三方为促进政治解决柬问题采取积极态度    
    越南和韩桑林—洪森方面应作出响应
    新华社北京２月１６日电  外交部发言人李肇星今天在这里说，民柬三方最近在北京召开特别会议，提出了关于西哈努克亲王的政治解决柬埔寨问题的五点和平计划的实施办法，再次体现了民柬三方为促进公正合理地政治解决柬埔寨问题所采取的积极态度。越南和韩桑林—洪森方面应该作出积极响应。
    这位发言人是在今天下午的新闻发布会上回答记者提问时说这番话的。
    李肇星还说，“我们认为西哈努克亲王提出的政治解决柬埔寨问题的五点和平计划是合情合理的。”
