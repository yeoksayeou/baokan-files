Title: The Taba Issue
Author: Wú Wénfāng
Date: 1989-02-28
Edition: 3

On February 26, 1989, Egypt and Israel signed three agreements to finally resolve the Taba issue, thereby ending years of dispute between the two countries.

Taba is Egyptian territory, located in the northeastern part of the Sinai Peninsula, at the tip of the Gulf of Aqaba. It is a coastal area of about 1 square kilometer, adjacent to the Israeli port of Eilat.

During the 1967 Middle East War, Israel occupied the Sinai Peninsula, including Taba. In March 1979, the Egypt-Israel Camp David Accords were signed. According to this agreement, although Israel withdrew from most of the Sinai Peninsula in 1982, it still occupied Taba and refused to return it to Egypt. In 1986, Egypt and Israel agreed to resolve the Taba issue through international arbitration. In January 1987, a 10-person multinational peacekeeping force entered the Taba area. In September 1988, the International Arbitration Commission ruled that Egypt has sovereignty over Taba. On November 29 of the same year, the two countries signed the Taba Dispute International Arbitration Agreement and established the Egypt-Israel Liaison Group. Due to Israel's demand for compensation for the construction of the Sonesta Hotel in Taba and the right for Israeli residents to enter and exit Taba without a visa, negotiations between the two sides had reached a stalemate.

### 塔巴问题
吴文芳
1989-02-28
第3版(国际新闻)
专栏：小资料

　　塔巴问题
    １９８９年２月２６日，埃及和以色列签署了关于最终解决塔巴问题的３项协议，从而结束了两国间多年的争端。
    塔巴是埃及的领土，位于西奈半岛东北部，是亚喀巴湾顶端的一块海滨，面积约１平方公里，它紧靠以色列的港口——埃拉特。
    １９６７年中东战争中，以色列占领了包括塔巴在内的埃及西奈半岛。１９７９年３月签署了埃—以戴维营协议。根据这项协议，以色列虽然于１９８２年撤出了西奈半岛大部分地区，但它仍占据塔巴，拒不归还给埃及。１９８６年埃以同意将塔巴问题通过国际仲裁解决。１９８７年１月，由１０人组成的多国维持和平部队进入塔巴地区。１９８８年９月国际仲裁委员会作出了埃及拥有塔巴主权的裁决。同年１１月２９日，两国签署实施塔巴争端国际仲裁协定，并成立埃以联络小组。由于以色列要求赔偿以色列在塔巴建造的索尼斯旅馆的费用和维护以色列居民进出塔巴不需要签证的权利，曾使双方谈判陷于僵局。
　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　（吴文芳）
