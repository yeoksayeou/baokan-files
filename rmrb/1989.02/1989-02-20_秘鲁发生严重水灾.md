Title: Peru Hit by Severe Flooding
Author:
Date: 1989-02-20
Edition: 3

Peru's Moyobamba region has experienced continuous heavy rain since the 17th, causing several rivers to overflow. Seven villages have been struck by mudslides, resulting in 31 deaths and 51 missing persons.

### 秘鲁发生严重水灾

1989-02-20
第3版(国际新闻)
专栏：

　　秘鲁发生严重水灾
    秘鲁莫约班巴地区１７日以来连降暴雨，数条河流决堤。７个村镇遭到泥石流袭击，已有３１人丧生，５１人失踪。　　　　　　　　　
　　（据新华社）
