Title: Funeral for Comrade Fan Yingwu Held
Author:
Date: 1989-02-24
Edition: 2

The funeral for Comrade Fan Yingwu, an outstanding son of the Party and the people, and an old Red Army soldier, was held today at the Babaoshan Revolutionary Cemetery. Wreaths were sent by Yang Chengwu, Xiao Jingguang, and other comrades.

Fan Yingwu was born in Wuchang, Hubei, in 1906. He participated in the Northern Expedition in 1925, joined the Chinese Workers' and Peasants' Red Army in 1931, and joined the Communist Party of China in 1935. He served as the director of the health departments of the Luxi Military Region, the Jilu Military Region, the Rehe Military Region, and the Fourth Field Army's Jirechazhi Military Region. After the founding of the nation, he held positions such as the director of the Jiangxi Provincial Health Department, the director of the Health Office of the Central Directly Affiliated Units, and the deputy director of the Fuwai Hospital of the Chinese Academy of Medical Sciences. He retired in 1982.

Fan Yingwu dedicated over 60 years of his life to the struggle for the liberation and construction of the motherland. During the revolutionary war period, he used his expertise in medical and health care, as well as his deep affection for the Party and his comrades, to save comrades in life-and-death situations. After the founding of New China, he worked tirelessly day and night to establish and develop the country's health and sanitation cause. He passed away in Beijing on January 25, 1989, at the age of 83 due to illness.

### 范英武遗体告别仪式举行

1989-02-24
第2版(国内新闻)
专栏：

　　范英武遗体告别仪式举行
    新华社北京２月２３日电  向党和人民的优秀儿子、老红军范英武同志遗体告别仪式今天在八宝山革命公墓举行。杨成武、肖劲光等同志送了花圈。
    范英武１９０６年生于湖北武昌，１９２５年参加北伐战争，１９３１年加入中国工农红军，１９３５年加入中国共产党。他曾任鲁西军区、冀鲁豫军区、热河军区、四野冀热察军区卫生部部长等职务，建国后历任江西省卫生厅厅长、中央直属机关卫生处处长、中国医科院阜外医院副院长等职务，１９８２年离休。
    范英武为祖国的解放事业和建设事业艰苦奋斗６０多年。在革命战争时期，他凭借自己在医疗卫生方面的专长和对党对同志的深厚感情，出生入死救战友；新中国成立后，他为创建和发展我国卫生保健事业日夜操劳。他因病于１９８９年１月２５日在京逝世，终年８３岁。
