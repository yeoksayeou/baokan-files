Title: The Soviet Union Conducts Its Third Underground Nuclear Test This Year
Author:
Date: 1989-02-19
Edition: 6

The Soviet Union conducted its third underground nuclear test this year. At 7:01 AM Moscow time on the 17th, the Soviet Union carried out its third underground nuclear explosion of the year at the Semipalatinsk test site, with a yield of 20 kilotons.

According to TASS, the purpose of this nuclear explosion was to test the results of research in nuclear explosion physics. The radiation situation in the test area and its surroundings is normal.

### 苏联进行今年第三次地下核试验

1989-02-19
第6版(国际)
专栏：国际新闻简报

　　苏联进行今年第三次地下核试验
    莫斯科时间十七日早晨七点零一分，苏联在塞米巴拉金斯克试验场进行了今年以来的第三次地下核爆炸，当量为二万吨。
    据塔斯社报道，此次核爆炸的目的是检验核爆炸物理学方面的研究成果。试验地区及其周围放射性情况正常。　　　　　　　　　　　　
　　（据新华社）
