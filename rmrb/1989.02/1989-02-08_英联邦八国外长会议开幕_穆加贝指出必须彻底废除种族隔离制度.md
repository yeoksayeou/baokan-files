Title: Commonwealth Eight Foreign Ministers' Meeting Opens; Mugabe Calls for Complete Abolition of Apartheid
Author:
Date: 1989-02-08
Edition: 3

The Commonwealth Eight Foreign Ministers' Meeting opened today. Zimbabwean President Robert Mugabe stated that the Commonwealth should more forcefully call for comprehensive sanctions against South Africa and clearly inform South Africa's friends and sympathizers that the Commonwealth will not allow anyone to suppress this call until South Africa is ready to abolish the apartheid system.

Mugabe made these remarks at the opening ceremony of the Commonwealth Eight Foreign Ministers' Meeting on Southern African issues.

Commonwealth Secretary-General Ramphal, at the opening ceremony, called for increased financial and trade pressure on South Africa. He believed that without new foreign loans, South Africa's economy would not achieve a satisfactory growth rate.

This is the third meeting held by the committee since its establishment in 1987. The committee is composed of the foreign ministers of Australia, Canada, Guyana, India, Nigeria, Tanzania, Zambia, and Zimbabwe.

### 英联邦八国外长会议开幕　穆加贝指出必须彻底废除种族隔离制度

1989-02-08
第3版(国际)
专栏：

　　英联邦八国外长会议开幕
　　穆加贝指出必须彻底废除种族隔离制度
    新华社哈拉雷２月６日电  津巴布韦总统穆加贝今天在这里说，英联邦应当更加强有力地呼吁对南非实行全面制裁，并明确告知南非的朋友和同情者，在南非准备废除种族隔离制度之前，英联邦决不允许任何人禁止这种呼声。
    穆加贝是在英联邦关于南部非洲问题８国外长委员会会议的开幕式上讲这番话。
    英联邦秘书长兰法尔在开幕式上呼吁对南非加强财政和贸易压力。他认为，失去了新的外国贷款，南非经济就不会取得令人满意的增长率。
    这是该委员会１９８７年成立以来召开的第三次会议，它是由澳大利亚、加拿大、圭亚那、印度、尼日利亚、坦桑尼亚、赞比亚和津巴布韦外长组成。
