Title: Italian Prime Minister De Mita Believes U.S. Military Presence in Europe Should Continue
Author:
Date: 1989-02-02
Edition: 6

Italian Prime Minister De Mita today said at a press conference held by the Foreign Press Association that the presence of U.S. military forces in Europe should continue. This is not only for defense purposes but also to maintain the unity between Western Europe and the United States.

De Mita pointed out that the European Single Market, which will be formed by 1992, should not be seen as a "Fortress Europe."

He said that Italy's foreign policy is consistent with the general policy of the European Community, especially in Middle East policy and cooperation with the Soviet Union.

When discussing the Soviet Union, he said that the Soviet Union faces the challenge of modernizing its economic structure. This can strengthen its cooperation with developed countries, including Europe and Italy. However, this does not mean providing financial aid but rather creating economic conditions for cooperation.

In discussing the Middle East, De Mita said that Italy's consistent goal is to ensure the autonomy of the Palestinian people and the achievement of an independent status. He said that more and more countries in the international community support the legitimate demands of the Palestinian people, and the Soviet Union is ready to promote the resolution of regional issues through political mediation.

### 意大利总理德米塔认为  美驻欧军事力量应继续存在

1989-02-02
第6版(国际)
专栏：

　　意大利总理德米塔认为
    美驻欧军事力量应继续存在
    新华社罗马１月３１日电  意大利总理德米塔今天在外国记协举行的记者招待会上说，美国军事力量在欧洲的存在应继续下去，这不仅仅是出于防务原因，而且是为了保持西欧与美国的团结。
    德米塔指出，将于１９９２年形成的欧洲统一大市场不应被看成是“欧洲堡垒”。
    他说，意大利的外交政策与欧洲共同体的总方针是一致的，在中东政策和对苏合作问题上尤其是这样。
    在谈到苏联问题时他说，苏联面临使其经济结构现代化的问题，这样做可以使苏联加强同发达国家、同欧洲包括意大利的合作。但这指的不是提供财政援助，而是为合作创造经济条件。
    在谈到中东问题时，德米塔说，意大利一贯的目标是保证巴勒斯坦人的自主和拥有一种独立的形式。他说，国际上有越来越多的国家支持巴勒斯坦人的正当要求和苏联准备通过政治调节促进这个地区问题的解决。
