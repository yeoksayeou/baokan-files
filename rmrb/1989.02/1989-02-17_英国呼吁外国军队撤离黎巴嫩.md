Title: Britain Calls for Foreign Troops to Withdraw from Lebanon
Author:
Date: 1989-02-17
Edition: 6

British Minister Urges Withdrawal of Foreign Troops from Lebanon

British Foreign and Commonwealth Affairs Minister William Waldegrave, who is visiting Kuwait, on the 15th called for all foreign troops to withdraw from Lebanon, so that the Lebanese people can determine their own fate.

Waldegrave told reporters after meeting with Kuwaiti Deputy Prime Minister and Foreign Minister Sabah Ahmed that Britain supports the Arab League's mediation committee, headed by Sabah Ahmed, which was established to resolve the current political crisis in Lebanon.

### 英国呼吁外国军队撤离黎巴嫩

1989-02-17
第6版(国际)
专栏：国际新闻简报

　　英国呼吁外国军队撤离黎巴嫩
    正在科威特访问的英国外交和联邦事务国务大臣威廉·沃尔德格雷夫十五日呼吁所有外国军队撤离黎巴嫩，以便让黎巴嫩人民决定他们自己的命运。
    沃尔德格雷夫在会见科威特副首相兼外交大臣萨巴赫·艾哈迈德后对记者说，英国支持阿拉伯联盟为解决黎巴嫩目前的政治危机而成立的以萨巴赫·艾哈迈德为首的阿拉伯调解委员会。
