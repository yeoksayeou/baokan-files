Title: Offering Preferential Conditions to Encourage Talents to Shine
Author: Lei Chengda, Huang Zhimin
Date: 1989-02-03
Edition: 3

Beijing has decided to reward 210 outstanding scientific, technological, and management experts who have made significant contributions. These experts will be promoted one to three levels in their current professional and technical position salary grades.

This information was obtained by reporters today at the conference held by the Beijing Municipal Committee of the Communist Party of China and the Beijing Municipal People's Government to commend experts who have made outstanding contributions in science, technology, and management.

It is understood that Beijing will also arrange annual physical examinations and vacations for these experts and will prioritize solving their housing and other living difficulties.

These awarded experts are recipients of national or ministerial/municipal science and technology awards, invention awards, and progress awards.

Members of the Political Bureau of the CPC Central Committee, Secretary of the Beijing Municipal Committee Li Xixin, State Councilor and Mayor of Beijing Chen Xitong, and leaders from the Organization Department of the CPC Central Committee, the Ministry of Personnel, the Beijing Municipal Committee, and the municipal government attended the ceremony. They presented certificates of honor to the experts and extended their warm congratulations.

It is reported that Beijing will continue to take effective measures to encourage outstanding talents and will continue to reward those who have made significant contributions in various fields of intellectual work.

### 提供优惠条件　鼓励人才冒尖  北京重奖２１０名贡献突出专家
雷呈达　黄智敏
1989-02-03
第3版(科教·文化·体育)
专栏：

　　提供优惠条件　鼓励人才冒尖
    北京重奖２１０名贡献突出专家
    新华社北京２月１日电  （通讯员雷呈达、记者黄智敏）北京市决定给２１０名有突出贡献的科学、技术和管理专家，在现有专业技术职务工资档次的基础上，分别晋升一级到三级工资。
    这是记者今天从中共北京市委和市人民政府举行的表彰有突出贡献的科学、技术、管理专家的大会上获悉的。
    据了解，北京市每年还将给这些专家进行体格检查、安排休养，并优先解决他们在住房和其它生活方面的困难。
    这些受奖专家都是国家或部、市的科学技术奖、科学发明奖、科学进步奖的获奖者。
    中共中央政治局委员、中共北京市委书记李锡铭，国务委员、北京市市长陈希同，以及中央组织部、人事部、北京市委和市政府等有关方面的领导同志，向专家们颁发了荣誉证书，并向他们表示热烈祝贺。
    据介绍，北京市将进一步采取有效措施，鼓励人才冒尖，对做出突出贡献的各类知识分子都要继续给予重奖。
