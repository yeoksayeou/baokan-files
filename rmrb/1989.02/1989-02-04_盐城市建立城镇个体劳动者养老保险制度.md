Title: Yancheng City Establishes Pension Insurance System for Urban Individual Laborers
Author:
Date: 1989-02-04
Edition: 2

Over the past year, more than a hundred individual householders and private enterprise employees have participated in this insurance program, accounting for one-tenth of the total number of individual laborers in the city area.

The implementation of this system has been warmly welcomed by individual business operators throughout the city, with many eager to apply for insurance.

### 盐城市建立城镇个体劳动者养老保险制度

1989-02-04
第2版(经济)
专栏：

    ▲盐城市建立城镇个体劳动者养老保险制度　近一年来，已有百余名个体户和私营企业职工参加了此项保险，占市区个体劳动者总数的十分之一。
    这一制度的实行，受到全市个体经营者的欢迎，申请投保者踊跃。
