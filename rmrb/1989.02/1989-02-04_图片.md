Title: Magpies Sing on the Plum Blossoms, Another Spring
Author: Dong Wen
Date: 1989-02-04
Edition: 8

Magpies sing on the plum blossoms, another spring has arrived,
The boundless scenery is radiantly new.
The tide of reform and opening up surges forward,
Everywhere, there are people creating a new era.
