Title: The World's Top Ten Publishing Companies
Author:
Date: 1989-02-05
Edition: 8

The Bertelsmann company of West Germany is the world's largest publishing company. Its revenue is 31 billion francs, with 32,000 employees worldwide. It owns book and record distribution companies, publishing houses (over 30, in four languages), printing plants, and newspaper and magazine publishers.

Next is the Dai Nippon Printing Company. Its revenue in 1986 was 30 billion francs. This company is on par with the Time Magazine Company of the United States.

Following them are the Newhouse newspaper group (with a revenue of 27 billion francs) and the Times-Mirror Publishing Company (with a revenue of 26 billion francs).

Ranking sixth is the Toppan Printing Company of Japan (with a revenue of 25 billion francs). Following it are the Rupert Murdoch Company of the United States (with a revenue of 21 billion francs) and the Gannett Company (with a revenue of 20 billion francs).

The ninth and tenth largest companies are the Fuji Sangyo Company (with a revenue of 19.5 billion francs) and the International Publishing Company of the United Kingdom (with a revenue of 19 billion francs).

### 世界十大出版公司

1989-02-05
第8版(每周文摘)
专栏：

　　世界十大出版公司
    西德的贝特尔斯曼公司是世界上第一大出版公司。它的营业额为３１０亿法郎，在世界各地的职工达３．２万，拥有图书和唱片发行公司、出版社（３０多家、４种文字）、印刷厂以及报刊杂志出版社。
    其次是大日本印刷公司。它的营业额在１９８６年为３００亿法郎。该公司与美国的时代杂志公司并驾齐驱。
    居于它们之后的是纽豪斯报系（营业额为２７０亿法郎）和《时报——镜报》出版公司（营业额为２６０亿法郎）。
    居于第六位的是日本凸版印刷公司（营业额为２５０亿法郎），跟在它后面的是美国鲁珀特·默多克公司（营业额为２１０亿法郎）和甘内特报业公司（营业额为２００亿法郎）。
    第九家和第十家大公司是富士产经公司（营业额为１９５亿法郎）和英国里德国际出版公司（营业额为１９０亿法郎）。
　　　（１９８８年１１月２２日《吉林日报》）
