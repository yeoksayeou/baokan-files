Title: Kabul Regime Announces Afghanistan in State of Emergency
Author:
Date: 1989-02-20
Edition: 1

Kabul Regime Announces
Afghanistan Nationwide in State of Emergency

New China News Agency, Beijing, February 19: Kabul reports that on the 18th, the leader of the Kabul regime in Afghanistan, Najibullah, announced that Afghanistan is in a state of emergency.

According to Western news agencies, the Kabul regime implemented the state of emergency three days after the complete withdrawal of Soviet troops from Afghanistan and within 48 hours of continuous rocket attacks by Afghan guerrilla forces on Kabul and other cities.

After the state of emergency was declared, tanks, armored personnel carriers, and soldiers increased on the streets of Kabul.

The report cited a broadcast from Kabul Radio stating that on the 17th, Afghan guerrilla forces fired rockets at cities such as Kandahar, Herat, and Jalalabad, resulting in some casualties.

### 喀布尔政权宣布  阿富汗全国处于紧急状态

1989-02-20
第1版(要闻)
专栏：

　　喀布尔政权宣布
    阿富汗全国处于紧急状态
    新华社北京２月１９日电  喀布尔消息：阿富汗喀布尔政权领导人纳吉布拉１８日宣布，阿富汗全国处于紧急状态。
    据西方通讯社报道，喀布尔政权是在苏联军队全部撤出阿富汗３天之后，以及阿富汗游击队在过去４８小时内不断用火箭袭击喀布尔和其他城市的情况下实施紧急状态的。
    在实施紧急状态后，喀布尔街头增加了坦克、装甲运兵车和士兵。
    报道援引喀布尔电台的广播说，阿富汗游击队在１７日向坎大哈、赫拉特、贾拉拉巴德等城市发射了火箭，造成一些人的伤亡。
