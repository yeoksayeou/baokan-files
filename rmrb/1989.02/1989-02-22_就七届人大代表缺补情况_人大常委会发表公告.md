Title: On the Situation of Supplementary Elections for the Seventh National People's Congress Representatives
Author:
Date: 1989-02-22
Edition: 2

The Standing Committee of the Seventh National People's Congress issued a notice regarding the situation of supplementary elections for the Seventh National People's Congress representatives.

New China News Agency, Beijing, February 21st: Notice of the Standing Committee of the Seventh National People's Congress

The Seventh National People's Congress should have 2,978 representatives. By the time of the Fifth Session of the Standing Committee of the Seventh National People's Congress, there were 2,962 representatives, with 16 representatives still to be elected by the relevant election units. Currently, Henan Province has elected 1 representative, and Sichuan Province has elected 3 representatives. The Standing Committee of the Seventh National People's Congress agrees with the report of the Representative Qualification Examination Committee on the supplementary election of representatives, confirming that the qualifications of the supplementary elected representatives of the Seventh National People's Congress, Lin Xiao, Tudengniema (Tibetan), Wu Weixin, and Zhong Shuliang, are valid. This is hereby announced.

After the Fifth Session of the Seventh National People's Congress Standing Committee, Panchen Erdeni Qojya Gyainzain, a representative of the Seventh National People's Congress elected by the Tibet Autonomous Region, passed away. The Seventh National People's Congress currently has 2,965 representatives, with 13 representatives still to be elected by the relevant election units.

Standing Committee of the Seventh National People's Congress
February 21, 1989

### 就七届人大代表缺补情况  人大常委会发表公告

1989-02-22
第2版(国内新闻)
专栏：

　　就七届人大代表缺补情况
    人大常委会发表公告
    新华社北京２月２１日电  全国人民代表大会常务委员会公告
    第七届全国人民代表大会代表应为２９７８人，到全国人大常委会第五次会议时实有代表２９６２人，还缺１６名代表尚待有关选举单位补选。现已由河南省补选出七届全国人大代表１人。四川省补选出代表３人。全国人民代表大会常务委员会同意代表资格审查委员会关于补选代表的代表资格的审查报告，确认补选的第七届全国人民代表大会代表林晓、土登尼玛（藏族）、吴味辛、钟树梁的代表资格有效。特此公布。
    第七届全国人大常委会第五次会议以后，西藏自治区选出的全国人大代表班禅额尔德尼·确吉坚赞逝世。第七届全国人民代表大会现共有代表２９６５人，还缺１３名代表尚待有关选举单位补选。
　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　全国人民代表大会常务委员会
　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　１９８９年２月２１日
