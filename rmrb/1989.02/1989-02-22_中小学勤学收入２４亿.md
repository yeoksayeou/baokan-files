Title: Primary and Secondary Schools Earn 2.4 Billion Yuan from Work-Study Programs
Author:
Date: 1989-02-22
Edition: 2

    The People's Daily reports that according to incomplete statistics from the State Education Commission, the total income from work-study programs in primary and secondary schools across the country in 1988 was approximately 2.4 billion yuan, an increase of over 500 million yuan from the 1.9 billion yuan in 1987. Provinces and municipalities with work-study program incomes exceeding 1 billion yuan include Liaoning, Jilin, Heilongjiang, Hebei, Jiangsu, Shandong, Shanghai, Zhejiang, Henan, Hubei, Guangdong, and Sichuan.

### 中小学勤学收入２４亿

1989-02-22
第2版(国内新闻)
专栏：四面八方

　　中小学勤学收入２４亿
    本报讯  记者从国家教委获悉：据不完全统计，１９８８年全国中小学勤工俭学纯收入共计２４亿元左右，较１９８７年的１９亿余元增加了５亿多元。勤工俭学纯收入超过１亿元的省市有辽宁、吉林、黑龙江、河北、江苏、山东、上海、浙江、河南、湖北、广东、四川。
