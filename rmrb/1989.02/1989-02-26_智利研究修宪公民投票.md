Title: Chile Studies Constitutional Amendment Referendum
Author:
Date: 1989-02-26
Edition: 3

Chile's Interior Minister Casseres confirmed on the 24th that President Pinochet is studying the possibility of holding a referendum on amending the constitution before July. The interior minister said that the issue of amending the constitution must be studied and decided by the government before it is put to a public vote.

### 智利研究修宪公民投票

1989-02-26
第3版(国际新闻)
专栏：

　　智利研究修宪公民投票
    智利内政部长卡塞雷斯２４日发表谈话证实，皮诺切特总统正研究在７月之前就修改宪法问题举行公民投票的可能性。这位内政部长说，修改宪法问题在交由公民表决之前，必须经过政府研究和做出决定。　　　　
　　（据新华社）
