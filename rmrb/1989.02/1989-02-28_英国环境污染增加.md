Title: Increase in Environmental Pollution in the UK
Author:
Date: 1989-02-28
Edition: 3

The UK Department for the Environment admitted on the 25th that due to the increase in vehicle emissions, air and water pollution in the UK have increased in recent years.

In 1987, the emission of nitrogen dioxide increased to 230,000 tons. The emission of carbon monoxide increased to 526,000 tons.

With the increase in gasoline consumption, the lead content in the air increased to 3,000 tons in 1987.

In 1987, water pollution incidents increased by 9%, of which 37% were industrial pollution incidents, 19% were agricultural pollution incidents, and 20% were sewage pollution incidents.

### 英国环境污染增加

1989-02-28
第3版(国际新闻)
专栏：

　　英国环境污染增加
    英国环境部二十五日发表的官方数字承认，由于车辆排出的废气增多，近几年来英国的空气和水污染增加。
    一九八七年，二氧化氮的排放量增加到二百三十万吨。一氧化碳的排放量增加到五百二十六万吨。
    随着汽油消费量的增加，大气中的铅含量一九八七年增加到三千吨。
    一九八七年水污染事故增加了百分之九，其中工业污染事故占百分之三十七，农业污染事故占百分之十九，污水污染事故占百分之二十。
　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　（据新华社）
