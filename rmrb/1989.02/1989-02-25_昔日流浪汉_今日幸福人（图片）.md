Title: From Vagrant to Happy Man
Author: Ma Jingqiu
Date: 1989-02-25
Edition: 1

The Tibetan New Year in February, staff from the Lhasa Welfare Institute carried "Cutema," symbolizing "auspiciousness and prosperity," to the dormitory of 74-year-old Anwangqunpei (center) to celebrate the New Year.
