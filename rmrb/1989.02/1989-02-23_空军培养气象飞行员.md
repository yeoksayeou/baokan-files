Title: Air Force Trains Meteorological Pilots
Author: Sun Maoqing
Date: 1989-02-23
Edition: 2

The Air Force has trained the first batch of university-level undergraduate meteorological pilots for bomber, transport, and three types of aircraft. According to relevant departments, the training quality of this batch of graduates is the best in history. Their performance in high-difficulty subjects such as instrument flying, takeoff and landing, aerobatics, night flying, low-altitude takeoff, and medium-altitude bombing has all achieved excellent results.

### 空军培养气象飞行员
孙茂庆
1989-02-23
第2版(国内新闻)
专栏：四面八方

　　空军培养气象飞行员
    据新华社北京二月二十一日电  （记者孙茂庆）据空军有关部门介绍，我国空军院校培养出首批大学本科轰炸机、运输机三种气象飞行员。有关部门提供的资料表明，这批毕业学员的训练质量为历年最好，其仪表、起落、特技、夜航、低空出航、中空轰炸等高难度课目均达到优秀成绩。
