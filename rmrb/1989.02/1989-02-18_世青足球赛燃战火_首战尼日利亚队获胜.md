Title: The Youth World Cup Football Tournament Ignites
Author: Wú Yìhóng
Date: 1989-02-18
Edition: 3

The Youth World Cup Football Tournament Ignites

    The African football powerhouse Nigeria defeated the host team Saudi Arabia 2-1 in the opening match of the 1989 World Youth Football Championship at King Fahd Stadium in Riyadh on the evening of February 16.

    A total of 16 teams from Asia, Africa, the Americas, and Europe are participating in this tournament. They will be divided into four groups and compete for 16 days. The opening ceremony of the football tournament was held in the afternoon of February 16. Saudi King Fahd and FIFA President João Havelange attended the opening ceremony.

### 世青足球赛燃战火  首战尼日利亚队获胜
吴毅宏
1989-02-18
第3版(科教·文化·体育)
专栏：体育

　　世青足球赛燃战火    
    首战尼日利亚队获胜
    新华社利雅得２月１６日电  （记者吴毅宏）非洲足坛劲旅尼日利亚队１６日晚在这里的法赫德国王体育场以２∶１击败了东道主沙特阿拉伯队，从而揭开了１９８９年世界青年足球锦标赛的序幕。
    参加本届比赛的有来自亚洲、非洲、美洲和欧洲１６支球队。它们将分成４个组进行为期１６天的角逐。１６日下午举行了本届足球赛的开幕式。沙特国王法赫德和国际足联主席阿维兰热出席了开幕式。
