Title: The Soviet Mars Probe Sends Back Images
Author:
Date: 1989-02-24
Edition: 3

The Soviet interplanetary probe "Phobos 1," which is currently orbiting Mars, has transmitted the first batch of image data of Mars' moon Phobos back to Earth.

According to TASS, on February 21st from 15:35 to 16:25 Moscow time, when the interplanetary probe "Phobos 1" was 860 to 1,130 kilometers away from Phobos, the probe's automatic television camera system photographed Phobos and transmitted the image data back to Earth.

The Soviet interplanetary probe "Phobos 1" was launched on July 12th of last year. Its primary mission is to investigate Mars' moon Phobos and Mars itself, providing necessary data for future human missions to Mars.

### 苏火星探测器发回图像

1989-02-24
第3版(国际新闻)
专栏：

　　苏火星探测器发回图像
    新华社北京２月２３日电  莫斯科消息：正绕火星飞行的苏联星际探测器“火卫一”号，向地面传回了火星的卫星火卫一的首批图像资料。
    据塔斯社报道，２月２１日莫斯科时间１５时３５分至１６时２５分，当星际探测器“火卫一”号距离火卫一８６０公里至１１３０公里时，探测器的自动电视摄影系统对火卫一进行了拍摄并将其图像资料传回了地面。
    苏联星际探测器“火卫一”号是去年７月１２日发射的，它的主要使命是对火星的卫星火卫一及其火星进行考察，为未来的人类飞向火星提供必要的资料。
