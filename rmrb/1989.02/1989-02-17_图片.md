Title: Four Cars Set New World Record in Sweden
Author:
Date: 1989-02-17
Edition: 7

    Recently, four cars in Sweden's Mantorp Park drove over 100 kilometers on two wheels. This achievement broke the previous Guinness World Record of 55 kilometers and is expected to be included in the "Guinness World Records" book. These four cars are prototype models produced by the manufacturer after updating certain components and materials. Xinhua News Agency
