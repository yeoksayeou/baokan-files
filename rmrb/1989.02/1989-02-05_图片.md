Title: Paraguay Military Coup
Author:
Date: 1989-02-05
Edition: 6

On February 2, General Rodriguez of the Paraguayan Army staged a coup, overthrowing the Stroessner regime that had ruled Paraguay for 35 years. The image shows the modern national telephone building in Asunción, the capital of Paraguay, which was damaged by artillery fire from both sides during the coup. (Xinhua News Agency, fax photo)
