Title: Various Levels of Government Strive for Spring Transportation Safety
Author:
Date: 1989-02-10
Edition: 1

	Various levels of government have been vigorously promoting spring transportation safety.
	January saw a decrease in road traffic accidents.

	New China News Agency, Beijing, February 9: This January, a total of 21,201 road traffic accidents occurred nationwide, a decrease of 10.1% compared to the same period last year; 3,821 people died, a decrease of 14.68%; 11,700 people were injured, a decrease of 15.64%; and the financial loss amounted to 27.32 million yuan, an increase of 14.69% compared to last year.

	Various levels of government have been vigorously promoting spring transportation safety work. From January 15 to 30, the Ministry of Public Security organized a nationwide safety inspection campaign focusing on the enforcement of the "Provisions of the People's Republic of China on Road Traffic Management" and the implementation of spring transportation safety work. Local public security organs have actively taken preventive measures, and units with vehicles have strengthened safety work and safety education for drivers, achieving good results.

	The Ministry of Public Security requires that local public security organs continue to work hard and do a good job in ensuring safety during the spring transportation period.

### 各级政府狠抓春运安全  元月道路交通事故下降

1989-02-10
第1版(要闻)
专栏：

　　各级政府狠抓春运安全
　  元月道路交通事故下降
    新华社北京２月９日电  今年１月份全国共发生道路交通事故２１２０１起，比去年同期下降１０．１％；死亡３８２１人，下降１４．６８％；伤１１７００人，下降１５．６４％；损失折款２７３２万元，比去年上升１４．６９％。
    各级政府狠抓了春运交通安全工作。１月１５日至３０日，公安部组织了以检查执行《中华人民共和国道路交通管理条例》情况和落实春运道路交通安全工作情况为主要内容的全国省际间的安全大检查活动。各地公安机关积极采取预防措施，各有车单位加强了安全工作和对驾驶人员的安全思想教育工作，取得了良好的效果。
    公安部要求各地公安机关再接再厉，继续努力，做好春运期间安全保卫工作。
