Title: Public Security Ministry Reports Decrease in Traffic Accidents Last Year
Author: Chen Yan
Date: 1989-02-24
Edition: 1

Public Security Ministry reports indicate that the trend of sharply increasing road traffic accidents over the past few years has been curbed to some extent, with 12 provinces, autonomous regions, and municipalities directly under the Central Government seeing a reduction in the number of accidents and the number of deaths and injuries.

The 12 provinces, autonomous regions, and municipalities are Beijing, Hebei, Shanxi, Inner Mongolia, Liaoning, Shanghai, Jiangsu, Henan, Hunan, Sichuan, Guizhou, and Shaanxi.

According to Zhang Zhengchang, Director of the Traffic Management Bureau of the Public Security Ministry, there were 276,071 road traffic accidents nationwide in 1988, resulting in 54,814 deaths, 170,598 injuries, and direct property losses amounting to 308,613,669 yuan. Compared to 1987, the number of accidents decreased by 7.4%, the number of deaths increased by 2.57%, the number of injuries decreased by 8.97%, and the amount of property losses increased by 10.46%.

Zhang Zhengchang noted that the serious situation of traffic accidents on rural roads has improved somewhat. In 1988, there were 186,058 traffic accidents on public roads, resulting in 42,890 deaths and 119,211 injuries.

### 公安部发布的情况表明  去年交通事故有下降
陈雁
1989-02-24
第1版(要闻)
专栏：

　　公安部发布的情况表明
    去年交通事故有下降
    新华社北京２月２３日电  （记者陈雁）记者从公安部今天在京召开的１９８８年道路交通安全情况新闻发布会上获悉，近几年来道路交通事故大幅度上升的势头在去年初步得到缓解，全国已有１２个省、自治区、直辖市的交通事故次数和死亡、受伤人数全面下降。
    这１２个省、自治区、直辖市是北京、河北、山西、内蒙古、辽宁、上海、江苏、河南、湖南、四川、贵州、陕西。
    据公安部交通管理局局长张正常介绍，１９８８年全国共发生道路交通事故２７６０７１起，死亡５４８１４人，伤１７０５９８人，车物直接损失折款３０８６１３６６９元；与１９８７年相比，事故起数减少７．４％，死亡人数增加２．５７％，受伤人数减少８．９７％，损失折款增加１０．４６％。
    张正常说，值得提出的是，多年来农村公路交通事故严重的情况有所改变，１９８８年公路上共发生交通事故１８６０５８起，死亡４２８９０人，伤１１９２１１人。
