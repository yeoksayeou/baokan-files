Title: Rising Labor Costs in Western Countries
Author:
Date: 1989-02-14
Edition: 7

    Rising Labor Costs in Western Countries
    Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, February 13th — According to the February 11th to 17th issue of the British magazine *The Economist*, the private sector unit labor costs in the 24 member countries of the Organization for Economic Co-operation and Development (OECD) increased by an average of 3.25% in 1988 compared to the previous year. It is projected that this figure will rise to 3.75% in 1989.

    According to the magazine, Japan's unit labor costs decreased by 0.75% in 1988 compared to the previous year. It is projected that this figure will increase by 0.5% in 1989.

### 西方国家劳动成本上升

1989-02-14
第7版(国际)
专栏：

　　西方国家劳动成本上升
    新华社北京２月１３日电  据英国《经济学家》杂志今年２月１１日至１７日一期报道，去年经济合作与发展组织２４个成员国私营部门的单位劳动成本平均比上一年上升了３．２５％，预计今年将上升３．７５％。据这家杂志报道，去年日本单位劳动成本比上年下降了０．７５％，预计今年将上升０．５％。
