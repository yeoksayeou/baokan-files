Title: Shandong's Industrial and Commercial Tax Revenue Increased by 1.289 Billion Yuan in 1988
Author:
Date: 1989-02-10
Edition: 2

Shandong's industrial and commercial tax revenue increased by 1.289 billion yuan in 1988. By promoting production and plugging loopholes, and through comprehensive tax administration, Shandong Province saw a significant increase in its industrial and commercial tax revenue. Last year, Shandong Province collected a total of 9.84 billion yuan in industrial and commercial taxes, an increase of 1.289 billion yuan over the previous year, exceeding the state's planned target.

### 山东去年净增工商税收１２．８９亿元

1989-02-10
第2版(经济)
专栏：

    山东去年净增工商税收１２．８９亿元　　促产、堵漏，综合治税，使山东省工商税收有了大幅度增长。去年，山东省共完成工商税收９８．４亿元，比上年净增收１２．８９亿元，超额完成了国家计划。
