Title: Saving Venice and the Great Wall
Author: Wú Bǎozhāng
Date: 1989-02-13
Edition: 7

Nearly 70 pieces of art created by 50 contemporary famous painters from the United States, France, and China will be part of the second charity auction to save Venice and the Great Wall, scheduled for May 6.

The second charity performance to save Venice and the Great Wall will be held on the evening of May 5 at the Great Hall of the People in Beijing. More than ten famous singers from Western Europe will perform excerpts from operas by Wagner, Ponchielli, Khachaturian, and Ravel.

### 拯救威尼斯和长城  第二次义卖将在京举行
吴葆璋
1989-02-13
第7版(国际)
专栏：

　　拯救威尼斯和长城    
    第二次义卖将在京举行
    据新华社巴黎２月９日电  （记者吴葆璋）５０位美国、法国和中国的当代著名画家创作的近７０件美术作品将参加预计于５月６日举行的拯救威尼斯和长城第二次义卖活动。
    拯救威尼斯和长城第二次义演活动将于５月５日晚在北京人民大会堂举行。西欧十多位著名的歌唱家将演唱瓦格纳、彭切利、哈恰图良和拉维尔的歌剧片断。
