Title: Soviet Foreign Minister to Visit Five Middle Eastern Countries to Mediate Conflicts
Author: Sheng Shiliang
Date: 1989-02-14
Edition: 6

Soviet Foreign Minister to Visit Five Middle Eastern Countries to Mediate Conflicts

	NEW DELHI, Feb. 13 (Xinhua) — Soviet Foreign Ministry spokesman Gennady Gerasimov announced today that Soviet Foreign Minister Eduard Shevardnadze will visit a series of Middle Eastern countries from February 17 to 27 to mediate conflicts in the region.

	The countries to be visited are Syria, Jordan, Egypt, Iraq, and Iran.

	Gerasimov said the Soviet Union is eager to make significant progress in resolving this long-standing regional conflict and ensuring the legitimate rights of the Palestinian Arab people. During his visit, the Soviet Foreign Minister will also discuss the comprehensive resolution of the Iran-Iraq conflict, the situation in Afghanistan, and bilateral relations.

	Observers here noted that this is the first time Soviet Foreign Minister Eduard Shevardnadze will consecutively visit several Middle Eastern countries. Against the backdrop of the gradual resolution of conflicts around the world, the Soviet Union will use its influence in the Middle East to participate in the mediation of conflicts.

### 为调解中东冲突  苏联外长将访问中东五国
盛世良
1989-02-14
第6版(国际)
专栏：

　　为调解中东冲突
    苏联外长将访问中东五国
    新华社莫斯科２月１３日电  （记者盛世良）苏联外交部发言人佩尔菲利耶夫今天宣布，苏联外长谢瓦尔德纳泽将于本月１７日至２７日访问一系列中东国家，以调解中东冲突。
    这些国家是叙利亚、约旦、埃及、伊拉克和伊朗。
    佩尔菲利耶夫说，苏联力求在解决这一最旷日持久的地区性冲突和保证巴勒斯坦阿拉伯人民合法权利方面取得显著进展。苏联外长访问期间还将讨论彻底解决两伊冲突、阿富汗局势以及双边关系问题。
    此间人士注意到，这是苏联外长谢瓦尔德纳泽第一次接连访问若干个中东国家。在世界各地地区冲突逐一开始解决的背景下，苏联将利用它在中东地区的影响参与冲突的调解。
