Title: Egypt and the United States Hold Talks on the Taba Issue
Author:
Date: 1989-02-01
Edition: 6

Egypt and the United States held talks in Cairo on January 30 to implement the decision of the International Arbitration Tribunal on the Taba issue.

The head of the Egyptian delegation to the Taba negotiations, Arabi, and the U.S. Department of State's Assistant Legal Adviser, Norsen, participated in the talks.

According to the decision of the International Arbitration Tribunal, Taba should be returned to Egypt. However, Israel has so far refused to implement this decision, citing the need for Egypt to compensate Israel for the costs of building hotels in Taba and to grant Israeli citizens the right to enter Taba without a visa.

In recent weeks, negotiations between Egypt and Israel on these differences have reached a stalemate. The U.S. government is currently mediating to resolve these differences.

### 埃及和美国就塔巴问题会谈

1989-02-01
第6版(国际)
专栏：国际新闻简报

　　埃及和美国就塔巴问题会谈
    埃及和美国一月三十日在开罗举行会谈，实施国际仲裁大陪审团作出关于塔巴问题的裁决。
    埃及出席塔巴谈判代表团团长阿拉比和美国务院助理法律顾问诺森参加了会谈。
    根据国际仲裁大陪审团的裁决，塔巴应归还埃及。但是，以色列至今拒绝实施这项裁决，理由是要求埃及赔偿以色列在塔巴建造旅馆的费用和给以色列公民进入塔巴不需要签证的权利。
    几个星期来，埃及和以色列在这些分歧上的谈判陷于僵局。美国政府正在为消除这些分歧进行调解。
