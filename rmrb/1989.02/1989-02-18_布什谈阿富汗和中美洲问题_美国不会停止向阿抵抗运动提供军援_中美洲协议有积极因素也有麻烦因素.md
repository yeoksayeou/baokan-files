Title: Bush Discusses Afghanistan and Central America Issues
Author: Cang Lide
Date: 1989-02-18
Edition: 6

U.S. Will Not Stop Military Aid to Afghan Resistance
Central America Agreement Has Positive and Troublesome Elements

	BEIJING, Feb. 16 (Xinhua) -- U.S. President Bush said today that the United States will continue to support the Afghan resistance forces and rejected the Soviet Union's call for an immediate ceasefire and simultaneous halt to arms shipments by all countries.

	Bush told reporters at the White House that although Soviet troops have withdrawn from Afghanistan, they left behind "a large number of lethal weapons" for the Kabul regime. If aid to the resistance forces is stopped, Afghanistan may face an "imbalance unacceptable to the United States." Therefore, "as long as the struggle for self-determination by the resistance continues, U.S. aid will continue." Bush also said that the United States supports a peaceful settlement of the Afghan issue and encourages reconciliation.

	Regarding the peace agreement reached by the five Central American heads of state on Tuesday, Bush said there are "some positive elements" and "some troublesome elements." The positive element is that the Nicaraguans seem to be taking steps towards democratization according to the Esquipulas agreement, while the troublesome element is that "promises made are not kept."

	Bush said that despite the new peace plan proposed by the five Central American heads of state a few days ago, which calls for democratic elections and the disbanding of anti-government armed forces in Nicaragua, he will still seek new non-military aid for the Nicaraguan anti-government forces.

### 布什谈阿富汗和中美洲问题  美国不会停止向阿抵抗运动提供军援  中美洲协议有积极因素也有麻烦因素
仓立德
1989-02-18
第6版(国际)
专栏：

　　布什谈阿富汗和中美洲问题    
    美国不会停止向阿抵抗运动提供军援    
    中美洲协议有积极因素也有麻烦因素
    本报华盛顿２月１６日电  记者仓立德报道：美国总统布什今天表示，美国将继续支持阿富汗抵抗力量，并拒绝苏联关于阿富汗双方立即停火、所有国家同时停止运送武器的呼吁。
    布什在白宫会见记者时说，苏军虽已撤离阿富汗，但为喀布尔政权留下“大量致命武器”，如果停止援助抵抗力量，阿富汗将可能出现美国“无法接受的不平衡”。因此，“只要争取自决的抵抗斗争继续下去，美国的援助也将继续下去”。布什还说，美国支持阿富汗问题的和平解决，“鼓励和解”。
    在谈到中美洲五国首脑星期二达成的和平协议时，布什说有“一些积极因素”，也有“一些麻烦因素”。积极因素是尼加拉瓜人看来将根据埃斯基普拉斯协议正就尼加拉瓜的民主化采取一些步骤，麻烦因素是因为“做了许诺不兑现”。
    布什表示，尽管数日前中美洲五国首脑会议提出一项新的和平计划，要求尼加拉瓜实行民主选举、解散反政府武装，他仍将为尼加拉瓜反政府武装寻求新的非武器援助。
