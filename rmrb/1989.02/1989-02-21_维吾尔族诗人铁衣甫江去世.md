Title: Renowned Uyghur Poet Tieyifujiang Passes Away
Author:
Date: 1989-02-21
Edition: 2

Famous Uyghur poet Tieyifujiang Ailiyev passed away due to illness in the early hours of today (Beijing time) in Urumqi.

Tieyifujiang was born in 1930 and joined the Communist Party of China in 1953. Over several decades of literary creation, he published more than a thousand poems totaling over 25,000 lines and published 11 poetry collections, including "Selected Poems of Tieyifujiang" and "Inspiration of Spring."

This poet, who has influence both domestically and internationally, currently serves as the Vice Chairman of the Chinese Writers Association, the Director of the Ethnic Minority Literature Committee of the Chinese Writers Association, and a Standing Committee Member of the Political Consultative Conference of the Xinjiang Uyghur Autonomous Region.

### 维吾尔族诗人铁衣甫江去世

1989-02-21
第2版(国内新闻)
专栏：

　　维吾尔族诗人铁衣甫江去世
    新华社北京２月２０日电  著名维吾尔族诗人铁衣甫江·艾力耶夫因病于今日凌晨（北京时间）在乌鲁木齐去世。
    铁衣甫江生于１９３０年，１９５３年加入中国共产党。在从事文学创作的几十年间发表诗作千余首、总２５０００多行，并出版了《铁衣甫江诗选》、《春的灵感》等１１本诗集。
    这位在国内外有影响的诗人现任中国作家协会副主席、中国作家协会少数民族文学委员会主任，并担任新疆维吾尔自治区政治协商会议常务委员。
