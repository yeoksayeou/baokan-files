Alpu Sends Condolences on the Passing of Panchen Erdeni

Xinhua News Agency, Lhasa, February 1: The parents of Panchen Vice Chairman received a telegram of condolence from Alpu Awang Jinmei, Vice Chairman of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress, and his wife, expressing their sorrow over the passing of Panchen Erdeni. The full text of the telegram is as follows:

To the respected Yoshi Gungdain and Yoshi Solang Qima and their relatives, and all the staff who worked with Panchen Erdeni:

I was deeply saddened to hear of the sudden passing of Panchen Erdeni, Vice Chairman of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress and a dear friend whom I have always respected. My family and I are deeply grieved and extend our heartfelt condolences and deepest sympathies to you. We hope that you will take care of your health and find solace in this difficult time.

	Alpu Awang Jinmei
	Alpu Cairen Qigia and family
	February 31, 1989, Beijing

### 阿沛电唁班禅大师逝世

1989-02-02
第4版(政治·法律·社会)
专栏：

　　阿沛电唁班禅大师逝世
    新华社拉萨２月１日电  班禅副委员长的父母今天收到了全国人大常委会副委员长阿沛·阿旺晋美及夫人为班禅大师逝世发来的唁电。唁电全文如下：尊敬的尧西·古公才旦，尧西·索朗卓玛及其亲属和班禅大师身边的全体工作人员：
    惊悉全国人大常委会副委员长、我一向非常敬重的亲密朋友班禅大师不幸突然病逝，我和全家感到万分痛惜，并向你们表示最亲切的慰问和最沉痛的哀悼。希望您们节哀和保重身体。　　
        阿沛·阿旺晋美
      　　　阿沛·才旦卓嘎及全家
    　　　　　　　１９８９年１月３１日于北京
