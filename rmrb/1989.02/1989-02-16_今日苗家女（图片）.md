Title: Today's Miao Woman
Author: Long Qiyun
Date: 1989-02-16
Edition: 1

	Today's Miao woman
	In Hunan Province's Huayuan County, Pailou Township, Miao girls used to embroider only for their own wedding dresses. Now, their embroidered products are sold both domestically and internationally. The picture shows Ma Laoqian (left) embroidering Miao brocade.

### 今日苗家女（图片）

1989-02-16

专栏：

    今日苗家女
    湖南省花垣县排吾乡苗族姑娘，过去挑花刺绣只是为自己绣嫁衣，如今刺绣品已销往国内外市场。图为麻老前（左）在绣苗锦。
