Title: Buying New Year's Goods
Author: Wú Zōngqí
Date: 1989-02-03
Edition: 2

The Communist Party's policy of enriching the people has brought about a thriving and prosperous new scene in the rural areas of Yiwu City, Zhejiang Province. In recent years, the agricultural markets here have been exceptionally lively, and the lives of farmers have been improving steadily.

Look! This elderly man who has come to the market to buy New Year's goods has taken a fancy to those few sheep...
