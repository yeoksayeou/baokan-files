Title: Bright Lanterns and Joyous Lantern Festival
Author: Meng Renquan
Date: 1989-02-19
Edition: 1

The week-long 1989 Beijing Municipal Workers' Lantern Festival began on February 16 at the Workers' Cultural Palace. More than 500 lanterns of various shapes and designs attracted tens of thousands of men, women, children, and elderly people to celebrate the Lantern Festival together. The image shows enthusiastic visitors enjoying the spectacle of fire trees and silver flowers.

    花灯灿　闹元宵
    为期一周的1989年北京市职工元宵灯会2月16日在劳动人民文化宫开始举行，500多盏千姿百态的花灯吸引成千上万的男女老少，共庆元宵佳节。图为游人兴致勃勃地观赏火树银花。
