Title: Expressing Gratitude to the Capital's Various Circles for Their Care for the Disabled
Author: Luo Tongsong
Date: 1989-02-01
Edition: 4

To express gratitude to the capital's various circles for their care for the disabled, the China Disabled Persons' Welfare Foundation held a get-together today at the Beijing Sanlu Factory.

More than 200 officials from central government departments, the People's Liberation Army, business circles, the arts, religious circles, and the media, along with some disabled persons from the capital, gathered together to discuss the excellent situation of China's reform and opening up, as well as the flourishing development of the cause of the disabled.

The chairman of the China Disabled Persons' Federation, Deng Pufang, who is also the president of the China Disabled Persons' Welfare Foundation, presented certificates of donation to some donating units and individuals and expressed his thanks to them.

The painter Li Teng from Wuhan specially came to Beijing and donated 100 of his works, which he had spent 10 years creating, to the China Disabled Persons' Welfare Foundation.

### 感谢首都各界对残疾人的关怀  中国残疾人福利基金会举行联谊活动
罗同松
1989-02-01
第4版(政治·法律·社会)
专栏：

　　感谢首都各界对残疾人的关怀
    中国残疾人福利基金会举行联谊活动
    本报北京１月３１日讯  记者罗同松报道：为感谢首都各界对残疾人的关怀，中国残疾人福利基金会今天在北京三露厂举行联谊活动。
    中央一些部委的负责人、人民解放军、工商界、文艺界、宗教界、新闻界的人士２００多人，和首都一些残疾人济济一堂，畅谈我国改革、开放的大好形势以及残疾人事业的兴旺发达。
    中国残疾人联合会主席团主席、中国残疾人福利基金会理事长邓朴方向一些捐赠单位和个人颁发了捐赠证书，并且向他们表示感谢。
    武汉画家李腾专程赶来北京，在今天的会上把他花费１０年心血创作的１００幅作品，捐赠给了中国残疾人福利基金会。
