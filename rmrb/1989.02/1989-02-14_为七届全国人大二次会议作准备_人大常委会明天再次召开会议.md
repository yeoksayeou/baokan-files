Title: Preparations for the Second Session of the Seventh National People's Congress
Author: Su Ning
Date: 1989-02-14
Edition: 1

The sixth session of the Standing Committee of the Seventh National People's Congress will be held in Beijing on February 15. The session is expected to last approximately one week. This was announced today by Zhang Hugeng, spokesperson for the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress, at a press conference.

The main task of this session is to prepare for the second session of the Seventh National People's Congress, which will be held in Beijing on March 20. According to Zhang Hugeng, the agenda proposed by the Presidium of the Standing Committee includes the following items:

	* Review the draft decision of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress on convening the second session of the Seventh National People's Congress;
	* Continue to review the draft of the Law of the People's Republic of China on the Inspection of Import and Export Commodities and the draft of the Law of the People's Republic of China on the Prevention and Treatment of Infectious Diseases;
	* Continue to review the draft of the Administrative Litigation Law of the People's Republic of China and the draft of the Rules of Procedure of the National People's Congress, and submit them to the second session of the Seventh National People's Congress for review;
	* Review and decide on the publication of the draft of the Basic Law of the Hong Kong Special Administrative Region of the People's Republic of China and related documents for comments;
	* Review the draft decision of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress on the composition of the delegation of Hainan Province to the Seventh National People's Congress.

In addition to the above agenda items, this session will also review the report of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress on the qualifications of the newly elected representatives of the Seventh National People's Congress, the report on the visit of the delegation of the National People's Congress to the Philippines, personnel appointments and dismissals, and other matters.

### 为七届全国人大二次会议作准备  人大常委会明天再次召开会议
苏宁
1989-02-14
第1版(要闻)
专栏：

　　为七届全国人大二次会议作准备    
    人大常委会明天再次召开会议
    本报北京２月１３日讯  记者苏宁报道：第七届全国人民代表大会常务委员会第六次会议，将于２月１５日在北京举行。会期大约一周。这是全国人大常委会新闻发言人张虎生今天上午在新闻发布会上宣布的。
    本次常委会的主要任务，是为将于３月２０日在北京召开的七届全国人大第二次会议作好准备。据张虎生介绍，委员长会议提出的本次常委会会议议程的建议，主要有以下几项内容：
    审议《全国人民代表大会常务委员会关于召开第七届全国人民代表大会第二次会议的决定（草案）》；继续审议《中华人民共和国进出口商品检验法（草案）》和《中华人民共和国传染病防治法（草案）》；继续审议《中华人民共和国行政诉讼法（草案）》和《全国人民代表大会议事规则（草案）》，并提请七届全国人大二次会议审议；审议决定公布《中华人民共和国香港特别行政区基本法（草案）》及有关文件征求意见；审议《全国人民代表大会常务委员会关于海南省出席第七届全国人民代表大会代表团组成的决定（草案）》。
    除上述议程外，本次常委会会议还将审议代表资格审查委员会关于补选的七届全国人大代表的代表资格的审查报告，全国人大代表团访问菲律宾的情况报告，任免事项及其他事项。
