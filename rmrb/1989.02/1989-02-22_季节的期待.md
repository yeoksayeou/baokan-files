Title: The Anticipation of the Seasons
Author: Zhao Qing Tang
Date: 1989-02-22
Edition: 8

Every life form stretches out
Soft veins
Every leaf grows
A parched throat
Countless nerves in a state of exhaustion
Fervently
Anticipate the rain
And so, under the sunlight, all things stand still
Releasing colors
Swaying with the grace of a season
The scorching sun blazes like fire
Desire trembles, sprouting
Vibrant layers of life
Page after page
Uniformly filled
Summer emerges
Thunderstorms emerge
Monsoons and heat vortices emerge
Dark clouds hang in the sky, reporting
Rain or no rain
The arid heat and the breakthrough
Cover every page
Of the calendar filled with life

### 季节的期待
赵庆堂
1989-02-22
第8版(副刊)
专栏：

　　季节的期待
    赵庆堂
    每一棵生命都伸展
    柔软的经络
    每一片叶面都长出
    干渴的喉舌
    无数条神经以焦竭状
    痴情地
    期待雨汛
    于是阳光下万物静然
    宣泄色彩
    摇曳一个季节之神韵
    毒阳炽烈如火
    欲望颤栗着拔节
    长出生机勃勃的层次
    整块整块版面
    齐刷刷地跃满
    夏季生成
    雷雨生成
    季风和热旋流生成
    乌云悬空报道
    有雨抑或无雨
    燥热和突破对每一页
    涂满生机的日历
    都不例外
