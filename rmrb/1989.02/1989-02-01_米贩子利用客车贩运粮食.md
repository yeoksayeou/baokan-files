Title: Grain Traders Use Passenger Trains to Transport Grain
Author: He Linsen, Zou Yan Yu, Xiao Gong Hua
Date: 1989-02-01
Edition: 5

In mid-December last year, staff of the Fengshiyan Grain Storage and Transportation Inspection Station in our county repeatedly discovered grain traders transporting grain on the 405 (Hengyang to Nanning) and 411 (Hengyang to Zhanjiang) direct passenger trains. Over a period of 10 days, they seized 12,785 kilograms of grain. On the evening of December 11 at 10 PM, when the 405 train arrived at Fengshiyan Station, inspectors found sacks of rice in some of the carriages. They confiscated 2,000 kilograms on the spot and apprehended one grain trader.

### 米贩子利用客车贩运粮食
何林森　邹雁瑜　肖功华
1989-02-01
第5版(读者来信)
专栏：

　　米贩子利用客车贩运粮食
    去年１２月上旬，我县风石堰粮食储运检查站的工作人员，多次在４０５次（衡阳至南宁）、４１１次（衡阳至湛江）直通客车上，发现一些米贩子外运粮食，１０天时间里，查缴粮食１２７８５公斤。１２月１１日晚１０时，４０５次列车进入风石堰车站时，检查人员发现列车的一些车厢里有用麻袋装的大米，当场收缴了２０００公斤，并抓获一名米贩子。　　湖南祁东县广播局　何林森
    　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　祁东县粮食局　邹雁瑜　肖功华
