Title: Tianjin Television Station Presents the Serial Drama "Middle School Dream Melody"
Author:
Date: 1989-02-21
Edition: 4

Tianjin Television Station has recently completed the production of the five-episode serial drama "Middle School Dream Melody," adapted from Xiao Fuxing's novel of the same name. The drama depicts the ordinary lives of a group of high school students nearing graduation, sketching a colorful and diverse spiritual landscape of contemporary youth. The drama handles the sensitive topic of early romance among young boys and girls in a natural and refreshing manner, attempting to understand and recognize it from the students' own experiences. The scriptwriter is Gao Chunli, and the director is Dong Jintan.

### 天津电视台推出连续剧  《中学生梦幻曲》
文一
1989-02-21
第4版(文化生活·体育)
专栏：

　　天津电视台推出连续剧《中学生梦幻曲》
    根据肖复兴同名小说改编的五集电视连续剧《中学生梦幻曲》最近由天津电视台摄制完毕。该片通过一群临近毕业的高中学生的平凡生活，勾勒了当代青年多彩多姿的精神风貌。对少男少女早恋这一敏感话题，剧作处理得也较为自然清新，力图从中学生自身的体验中予以把握和认识。该剧编剧为高春丽，导演董金棠。　　
　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　（文一）
