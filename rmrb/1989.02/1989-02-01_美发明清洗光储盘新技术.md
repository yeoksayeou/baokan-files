Title: U.S. Invents New Technology for Cleaning and Reusing Optical Storage Disks
Author:
Date: 1989-02-01
Edition: 7

U.S. scientists have invented a new technology that can clean optical data storage disks and make them reusable.

According to a report by the magazine "High-Tech Business," this new technology was developed by researchers at the Battelle Pacific Northwest Laboratory. The technology uses a laser beam to create and freeze "impact blocks" on the storage disk to record information. The data is then read out and displayed on a computer screen. To erase the data, the laser beam heats the storage disk, causing the "impact blocks" to disappear, thus creating reusable data space.

Optical data storage disks have a storage capacity one thousand times greater than magnetic disks and offer the advantages of fast processing and long-term storage.

### 美发明清洗光储盘新技术

1989-02-01
第7版(国际)
专栏：

　　美发明清洗光储盘新技术
    新华社华盛顿一月三十日电  美国科学家发明了一种能清洗光数据存储盘，并使之反复使用的新技术。
    据《高技术商业》杂志报道，这种新技术是由巴特尔太平洋西北实验室研究人员研制出的。激光束通过在存储盘上产生撞击并冻结成“撞击块”来记录信息，光束读出显示在计算机屏幕上的数据。如果要清洗数据，激光束给存储盘加热，使“撞击块”消失，便形成可再使用的数据空间。
    光学数据存储盘的存储能力比磁盘存储能力高一千倍，并有处理快、保存期长的优点。
