Title: Wang Zhongyu Appointed Acting Governor of Jilin
Author: 陈美凤 (Chen Meifeng)
Date: 1989-02-01
Edition: 1

Wang Zhongyu Appointed Acting Governor of Jilin

A recent meeting of the Seventh Session of the Standing Committee of the Seventh People's Congress of Jilin Province passed a resolution: Wang Zhongyu, deputy secretary of the Jilin Provincial Committee of the Communist Party of China, is appointed acting governor of Jilin Province. The request of He Bukan to resign from the position of governor was accepted.

### 王忠禹任吉林代省长
陈美凤
1989-02-01
第1版(要闻)
专栏：

　　王忠禹任吉林代省长
    新华社长春一月二十九日电  （记者陈美凤）刚刚结束的吉林省七届人大常委会第七次会议通过决定：中共吉林省委副书记王忠禹任吉林省代理省长，接受何竹康辞去省长职务的请求。
