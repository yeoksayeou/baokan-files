Title: SWAPO Holds Special Meeting
Author:
Date: 1989-02-15
Edition: 6

The Central Committee of the South West Africa People's Organization (SWAPO) recently held a special meeting in Luanda to discuss the organization's strategy in the election process in Namibia.

The meeting noted that although Angola, Cuba, and South Africa signed a tripartite agreement on peace in southwestern Africa, the situation in Namibia has not changed. The South African authorities continue to intimidate the people of Namibia and have increased the number of occupying troops in Namibia. The recent armed invasion of Angola by South African forces indicates that the South African authorities have no intention of adhering to international agreements.

### 西南非洲人民组织举行特别会议

1989-02-15
第6版(国际)
专栏：国际新闻简报

　　西南非洲人民组织举行特别会议
    西南非洲人民组织中央委员会最近在罗安达举行特别会议，讨论了该组织在纳米比亚选举进程中采取的战略。
    会议指出，虽然安哥拉、古巴和南非签署了关于西南部非洲和平的三方协议，但是纳米比亚国内的局势没有变化。南非当局继续进行恐吓纳米比亚人民的行动，增加在纳米比亚的占领军。南非军队最近武装入侵安哥拉，表明南非当局对遵守国际协议没有诚意。
