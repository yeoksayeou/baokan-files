Title: The Soviet Union Commemorates the 90th Anniversary of Lao She's Birth
Author:
Date: 1989-02-24
Edition: 3

The Soviet Union's Association for Friendship and Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries and the Soviet-China Friendship Association jointly held a commemorative meeting for the 90th anniversary of the birth of the renowned Chinese writer Lao She at the House of Friendship of All Peoples in Moscow today.

Soviet-China Friendship Association member Solokin presided over the commemorative activities. The deputy director of the Soviet Far East Institute, Zhelakhovtsev, gave a report titled "Lao She is One of the Most Popular Chinese Writers in the Soviet Union." The report reviewed Lao She's creative career and introduced the publication of Lao She's works in the Soviet Union. After the commemorative meeting, the film "Moon Crescent," based on Lao She's work of the same name, was screened.

The chairman of the Soviet-China Friendship Association, Zikhmensky, attended the commemorative meeting.

### 苏联纪念老舍诞辰九十周年

1989-02-24
第3版(国际新闻)
专栏：

　　苏联纪念老舍诞辰九十周年
    新华社莫斯科２月２２日电  苏联对外友好和文化协会联合会与苏中友好协会今天在莫斯科各国人民友谊之家联合举行中国著名作家老舍诞辰９０周年纪念会。
    苏中友协理事索洛金主持了纪念活动。苏联远东问题研究所语言学副博士热拉霍夫采夫作了题为《老舍是苏联最受欢迎的中国作家之一》的报告。报告回顾了老舍的创作生涯，介绍了老舍作品在苏联的出版情况。纪念会结束后放映了根据老舍同名作品拍摄的中国故事片《月牙儿》。
    苏中友协理事会主席齐赫文斯基出席了纪念会。
