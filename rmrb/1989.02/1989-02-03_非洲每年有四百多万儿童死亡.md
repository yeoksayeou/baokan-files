Title: Over Four Million Children Die in Africa Each Year
Author:
Date: 1989-02-03
Edition: 7

	Over four million children die in Africa each year.
	According to a report by the Pan African News Agency today, citing a report by the United Nations Children's Fund on the situation of children in 1989, Africa sees the death of 4.5 million children annually.

	The UNICEF report reveals that among the 20 countries with the highest child mortality rates, 16 are in Africa. Many children succumb to measles, tetanus, whooping cough, diphtheria, tuberculosis, and polio, all of which can be prevented through vaccination.

### 非洲每年有四百多万儿童死亡

1989-02-03
第7版(国际)
专栏：

　　非洲每年有四百多万儿童死亡
    新华社达喀尔二月一日电  据泛非通讯社今天援引联合国儿童基金会关于一九八九年世界儿童情况的一项报告报道，非洲每年有四百五十万儿童死亡。
    据联合国儿童基金会的报告透露，在儿童死亡率最高的二十个国家中，有十六个是非洲国家；许多儿童是死于麻疹、破伤风、百日咳、白喉、结核和脊髓灰质炎这六种疾病，而这些疾病是可以依靠疫苗接种来预防的。
