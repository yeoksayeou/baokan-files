Title: Brief News on Education, Science, and Culture
Author:
Date: 1989-02-17
Edition: 3

Those who have not had the chance to visit a space launch site may wish to see the splendor of a scientific holy land, and citizens who have not been to the South China Sea may want to witness the marvel of building an ocean observation station on Yongshu Reef. The August First Film Studio has produced and will soon release the documentaries "China Space City" and "180 Days and Nights," allowing viewers to feast their eyes.

	Shanghai's over 500 renowned doctors have provided free consultations to more than 10,000 patients over the past two days, demonstrating their noble medical ethics.

	Taiwan's Tai Sheng Publishing House will publish a series of novels by the famous Taiwanese female writer Guo Lianghui, totaling more than ten works, including "Lonesome Holiday" and "My Heart, My Heart."

	"The Practical Study of Broadcast Language" has been published by the China Radio and Television Publishing House. This book reveals the characteristics and laws of Chinese broadcast language.

### 教科文零讯

1989-02-17
第3版(科教·文化·体育)
专栏：教科文零讯

　　教科文零讯
    ●没机会到航天发射场的人大概想领略科学圣地的风采，未能亲临南沙的公民也许愿一睹永暑礁建设海洋观测站的奇观。八一电影制片厂摄制并即将上映的纪录片《中国航天城》和《１８０个日日夜夜》，可使观众一饱眼福。
    　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　（据新华社）
    ●上海市５００多位名医两天来为一万多名患者进行了义务咨询，表现了崇高的医德。
    ●台声出版社将陆续出版台湾著名女作家郭良蕙女士的小说系列，共十余部，有《寂寞的假期》、《我心·我心》等。
    ●《实用广播语体学》由中国广播电视出版社出版。该书揭示了汉语广播语体的特点、规律。　　　　　
　　　　　　　　　　　　（钟广）
