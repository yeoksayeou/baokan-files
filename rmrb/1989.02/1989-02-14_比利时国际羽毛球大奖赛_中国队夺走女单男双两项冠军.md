The Belgian International Badminton Grand Prix
China Team Wins Two Titles in Women's Singles and Men's Doubles

XINHUA NEWS AGENCY, ANTWERP, February 12 (Reporter Liu Yongsheng) In the Belgian International Badminton Grand Prix, which concluded today, Chinese athletes won the women's singles and men's doubles titles. Tang Jiuhong defeated her teammate Huang Hua 2-0 to win the women's singles title. Zhang Qiang and Zhou Jincan defeated Denmark's S. Frødeberg and J. Knudsen 2-0 to win the men's doubles title.

In today's women's doubles final, Sweden's M. Bengtsson and K. Magnusson defeated England's J. Clark and S. Sanki 2-1 (15-4, 13-15, 15-4) to win the championship, with the English athletes securing the runner-up position. China's Huang Hua and Tang Jiuhong finished in third place.

In the mixed doubles final, Danish athlete G. Poulsen and English athlete J. Gowls collaborated to defeat Swedish athletes Jan-Erik Antonsson and M. Bengtsson 2-0 (18-15, 15-12), winning the championship. The Swedish athletes secured the runner-up position, while China's Zhang Qiang and Shi Wen finished in third place.

Danish athlete Palle-Erik Hoyer defeated his teammate M. Kjeldsen 2-0 (15-10, 15-6) to win the men's singles title, with Kjeldsen securing the runner-up position. China's Liu Zhiheng finished in fifth place.

The Belgian International Badminton Grand Prix is held annually in Antwerp, Belgium. This year marked the 10th edition. The competition featured over 160 athletes from 13 countries and regions.

### 比利时国际羽毛球大奖赛  中国队夺走女单男双两项冠军
刘永胜
1989-02-14
第3版(科教·文化·体育)
专栏：体育

　　比利时国际羽毛球大奖赛    
    中国队夺走女单男双两项冠军
    新华社安特卫普２月１２日电  （记者刘永胜）在今天结束的比利时国际羽毛球大奖赛中，中国选手获得女子单打和男子双打两项冠军。唐九红以２∶０战胜队友黄华获女单冠军。张强和周金灿以２∶０击败丹麦的斯·弗拉德伯格和耶·克努森获得男双冠军。
    在今天进行的女子双打决赛中，瑞典的玛·本格森和克·马格努松以２∶１（１５∶４、１３∶１５、１５∶４）战胜英格兰的吉·克拉克和萨·桑基获得冠军，英格兰选手获得亚军；中国的黄华和唐九红获第三名。
    在混合双打决赛中，丹麦选手吉·帕乌尔森和英格兰选手吉·高尔斯合作，以２∶０（１８∶１５、１５∶１２）打败了瑞典选手扬一埃里克·安通松和玛·本格森获得冠军，瑞典选手获得亚军；第三名是中国的张强和施文。
    丹麦选手帕尔一埃里克·霍耶以２∶０（１５∶１０、１５∶６）战胜队友米·克耶尔森获得男子单打冠军，克耶尔森获得亚军；中国的刘志衡名列第五。
    比利时国际羽毛球大奖赛每年在比利时安特卫普市举行，今年为第十届。参加本届大奖赛的有１３个国家和地区的１６０余名运动员。
