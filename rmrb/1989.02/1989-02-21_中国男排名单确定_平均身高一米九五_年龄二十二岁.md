Title: China Men's Volleyball Team Roster Confirmed
Author:
Date: 1989-02-21
Edition: 4

After more than 40 days of training and selection, the Chinese men's volleyball team was officially formed in Beijing on February 20. Head coach Yu Youwei and coach Yang Zhiguang will lead the 14 selected players to begin a new round of training.

The 14 players come from 9 provinces, municipalities, and the PLA team. They are:

- Wong Yik-ching (21 years old, 2.04 meters, Shanghai)
- Xu Feng (22 years old, 2.01 meters, Beijing)
- Li Haiyun (19 years old, 2.02 meters, Liaoning)
- Xu Guorong (23 years old, 2.00 meters, Fujian)
- Wang Qi (17 years old, 1.98 meters, Henan)
- Cao Maowen (24 years old, 1.96 meters, Hubei)
- Jiang Jie (24 years old, 1.95 meters, PLA)
- Zhang Jianwei (20 years old, 1.93 meters, Nanjing Military Region)
- Miao Zhihong (22 years old, 1.93 meters, Shanghai)
- Zhao Zhen (25 years old, 1.93 meters, PLA)
- Wu Wei (21 years old, 1.92 meters, Zhejiang)
- Wang Chaogun (24 years old, 1.90 meters, Shandong)
- Yu Yiqing (23 years old, 1.90 meters, Liaoning)
- Wang Baquan (27 years old, 1.86 meters, Tianjin)

The newly formed Chinese men's volleyball team is a mix of experienced and young players. Five have previously served in the national team, seven come from the national youth team, and two are new selections. The team's average age is 22 years old, and the average height is 1.95 meters. Overall, the players have good physical conditions, but their techniques are still rough and they lack competitive experience.

The Chinese men's volleyball team will train in Beijing for two months and will participate in the Japanese Sports Festival volleyball competition in Tokyo in late April. In September, the team will participate in the Asian Men's Volleyball Championship held in Seoul.

### 中国男排名单确定  平均身高一米九五　年龄二十二岁

1989-02-21
第4版(文化生活·体育)
专栏：体育

　　中国男排名单确定
    平均身高一米九五　年龄二十二岁
    本报讯  经过４０多天训练的筛选，中国男排２０日在京正式组成。主教练余有为，教练杨志光将带领入选的１４名队员开始新的一轮集训。
    １４名队员分别来自９个省市和解放军队。他们是翁亦青（２１岁、２．０４米、上海）、许峰（２２岁、２．０１米、北京）、李海运（１９岁、２．０２米、辽宁）、许国荣（２３岁、２米、福建）、王琦（１７岁、１．９８米、河南）、曹茂稳（２４岁、１．９６米、湖北）、蒋杰（２４岁、１．９５米、解放军）、张坚卫（２０岁、１．９３米、南京部队）、缪志红（２２岁、１．９３米、上海）、赵铎（２５岁、１．９３米、解放军）、吴卫（２１岁、１．９２米、浙江）、王朝群（２４岁、１．９０米、山东）、于一清（２３岁、１．９０米、辽宁）、王宝泉（２７岁、１．８６米、天津）。新组建的中国男排是一支新老结合的队伍，有５人曾在上届国家队服役，７人来自国家青年队，２人是第一次入选。全队平均年龄２２岁，平均身高１．９５米。总的看来，队员身体条件不错，但技术还嫌粗糙，缺乏比赛经验。
    中国男排将在北京集训两个月，４月下旬赴东京参加日本体育节排球赛。９月份中国男排将参加在汉城举行的亚洲男排锦标赛。
