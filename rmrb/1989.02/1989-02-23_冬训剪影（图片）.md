Title: Winter Training Silhouettes
Author: Wang Jianmin
Date: 1989-02-23
Edition: 1

The Shenyang Military Region's certain division extensively conducted winter training. Soldiers and officers ate, lived, moved, and fought in the icy and snowy environment, enhancing the unit's capabilities in wild survival, rapid response, and actual combat. The image shows light cavalry galloping across the vast snowfield.

	New China News Agency reporter Wang Jianmin
