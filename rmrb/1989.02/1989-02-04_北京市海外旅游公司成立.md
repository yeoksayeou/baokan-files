Title: Beijing Overseas Tourism Company Established
Author: Guan Jianwen
Date: 1989-02-04
Edition: 2

Beijing Overseas Tourism Company was officially established on February 2. This is the first travel agency in Beijing since the founding of the nation that has the right to organize outbound tours, classified as a "Class A" travel agency. Beijing Overseas Tourism Company is the second such company established after Guangzhou. Other provinces, municipalities, and autonomous regions will follow suit in establishing their own overseas tourism companies.

### 北京市海外旅游公司成立
官建文
1989-02-04
第2版(经济)
专栏：

    ▲北京市海外旅游公司成立　北京市海外旅游公司于二月二日宣告成立。这是北京市建国以来第一家拥有出国组团权的“一类”旅行社。北京市海外旅游公司是继广州之后成立的第二家海外旅游公司。各省、直辖市、自治区将陆续成立海外旅游公司。　　　　　
　　　　（官建文）
