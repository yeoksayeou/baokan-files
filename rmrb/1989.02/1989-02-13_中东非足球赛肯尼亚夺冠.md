Title: Kenya Wins Middle East and Africa Football Tournament
Author: Huang Jin
Date: 1989-02-13
Edition: 3

Kenya's Breweries team today defeated Tanzania's Coastal Union team 3-0 in Nairobi, securing the championship of the Middle East and Africa Club Football Tournament. The Coastal Union team took the runner-up position.

### 中东非足球赛肯尼亚夺冠
黄瑾
1989-02-13
第3版(科教·文化·体育)
专栏：体育

　　中东非足球赛肯尼亚夺冠
    新华社内罗毕２月１１日电  （记者黄瑾）肯尼亚酿造队今天在这里以３∶０击败坦桑尼亚海岸联盟队，从而夺得中东非俱乐部足球赛的冠军。海岸联盟队获亚军。
