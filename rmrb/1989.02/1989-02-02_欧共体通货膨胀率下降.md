Title: Inflation Rate in the European Community Declines
Author:
Date: 1989-02-02
Edition: 7

According to data released by the European Community Statistical Office on January 31, the average inflation rate for the twelve member states of the Community in 1988 was 3.225 percent, slightly lower than the 3.5 percent level in 1987.

However, the inflation rate for the Community in December 1988, calculated on an annual basis, reached 4.4 percent, significantly higher than the 2.6 percent for the same month in 1987.

### 欧共体通货膨胀率下降

1989-02-02
第7版(国际)
专栏：国际经济动态

　　欧共体通货膨胀率下降
    据欧洲共同体统计局一月三十一日公布的数字，一九八八年，共同体十二国的平均通货膨胀率为百分之三点二二五，略低于一九八七年百分之三点五的水平。
    但是，去年十二月份共同体的通货膨胀按年率计算达到百分之四点四，大大高于一九八七年同月的百分之二点六。
