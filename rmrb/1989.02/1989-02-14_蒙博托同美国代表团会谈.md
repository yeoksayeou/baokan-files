Title: Mobutu Meets with U.S. Delegation
Author:
Date: 1989-02-14
Edition: 6

Zairean President Mobutu held talks with a U.S. congressional delegation on February 12 in Badolite, Equateur Province, Zaire, regarding the development of the situation in southern Africa.

The delegation, led by Representative Dan Burton, chairman of the House Subcommittee on Southern Africa, stated after the talks that the U.S. congressional delegation would proceed to southern Africa and Namibia to verify on-site the implementation of the peace agreement signed last December in New York by Cuba, Angola, South Africa, and the United States.

### 蒙博托同美国代表团会谈

1989-02-14
第6版(国际)
专栏：国际新闻简报

　　蒙博托同美国代表团会谈
    扎伊尔总统蒙博托十二日在扎伊尔赤道省巴多利特就南部非洲局势的发展同由众议员、众议院南部非洲小组委员会主席丹·伯顿率领的美国国会代表团举行了会谈。
    伯顿在会谈结束后向报界发表谈话说，美国国会代表团将前往南部非洲和纳米比亚，以便就地核查古巴、安哥拉、南非和美国去年十二月在纽约签订的和平协议的执行情况。
