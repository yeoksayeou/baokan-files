Title: Shuvars Emphasizes the Need to Stabilize the Domestic Situation
Author:
Date: 1989-02-09
Edition: 6

South Kosovo Albanians Stage Strikes and Protests

	South Slav Communist League Central Committee Chairman Shuvars today emphasized, "We must stabilize the domestic situation, carry out economic reforms and party reforms, in order to overcome the social crisis facing Yugoslavia."

	In discussing the internal reforms of the South Slav Communist League, he said that the South Slav Communist League must undergo the greatest possible reforms, with the most important being the issue of democratic governance within the party.

	New York Times Belgrade, February 7 (Reporter Xu Kunming) In recent days, Albanian workers in the southern Yugoslav province of Kosovo have staged strikes and protests, expressing dissatisfaction with the removal of former Kosovo leader Vlas from the Central Committee of the South Slav Communist League.

	On February 3 and 4, over 1,000 Albanian workers in the Titov Mitrovica region were the first to go on strike and hold protests. Subsequently, factories in Suva Reka, Prizren, and other locations also held protest gatherings.

	On February 6 and 7, the wave of strikes and protests spread to more than 10 cities, including the provincial capital Pristina. The situation is still developing.

### 舒瓦尔强调必须稳定国内局势  南科索沃阿尔巴尼亚族人举行罢工和集会

1989-02-09
第6版(国际)
专栏：

　　舒瓦尔强调必须稳定国内局势
    南科索沃阿尔巴尼亚族人举行罢工和集会
    新华社贝尔格莱德2月4日电　南斯拉夫共产主义者联盟中央主席团主席舒瓦尔今天强调指出，“我们必须稳定国内局势，进行经济改革和党内改革，以便克服南斯拉夫面临的社会危机。”
    他在谈到南共联盟的内部改革时说，对南共联盟必须进行最大程度的改革，其中最重要的是实行党内民主问题。
    新华社贝尔格莱德2月7 日电　（记者徐坤明）南斯拉夫南部科索沃自治省的阿尔巴尼亚族工人最近几天举行罢工和集会，对科索沃前主要领导人弗拉西被解除南共联盟中央委员职务表示不满。
    2月3日和4日，铁托米特罗维察区1000多名阿族工人首先举行罢工和抗议集会，接着，苏瓦雷卡、普里兹伦等地的工厂也举行了抗议集会。
    2月6日和7日，罢工和抗议集会的浪潮已波及自治省首府普里什蒂纳等10多个城镇。目前，事态还在发展。
