Title: China Supports Regional Cooperation Among Arab States
Author:
Date: 1989-02-24
Edition: 2

China supports the efforts of Arab states to develop regional cooperation and hopes that such cooperation will play a positive role in promoting economic prosperity in these regions.

Li Zhaoxing, the spokesperson for the Ministry of Foreign Affairs, made these remarks at a press conference today in response to a reporter's request for comments on the establishment of the Arab Cooperation Council by Egypt, Iraq, Jordan, and North Yemen, as well as the formation of the Arab Maghreb Union by Morocco, Algeria, Tunisia, Libya, and Mauritania.

### 中国支持阿拉伯国家区域合作

1989-02-24
第2版(国内新闻)
专栏：

　　中国支持阿拉伯国家区域合作
    新华社北京２月２３日电  外交部发言人李肇星今天在这里说，中国支持阿拉伯国家为发展区域合作所作的努力，希望这种合作能为促进这些地区的经济繁荣发挥积极作用。
    李肇星是在今天下午的新闻发布会上应记者要求对埃及、伊拉克、约旦和北也门成立阿拉伯合作委员会及摩洛哥、阿尔及利亚、突尼斯、利比亚和毛里塔尼亚成立阿拉伯马格里布联盟作出评论时说这番话的。
