Title: Soviet Cyclist Achieves New Record
Author:
Date: 1989-02-10
Edition: 3

According to a report from TASS, the renowned Soviet athlete Yekimov on February 3 set a new world record in the men's 20-kilometer indoor cycling event during the Aeroflot Cup International Indoor Cycling Competition held in Moscow. His time was 23 minutes 14.553 seconds, breaking the previous best time of 23 minutes 52.098 seconds, which was also set by Yekimov himself in Moscow in October 1986.

### 苏自行车选手创佳绩

1989-02-10
第3版(科教·文化·体育)
专栏：体育

　　苏自行车选手创佳绩
    据塔斯社报道，苏联著名选手叶基莫夫２月３日在莫斯科举行的民航杯国际室内自行车赛上，以２３分１４秒５５３的成绩刷新室内自行车男子２０公里世界最好成绩。原最好成绩为２３分５２秒０９８，是叶基莫夫本人１９８６年１０月在莫斯科创造的。（新华社）
