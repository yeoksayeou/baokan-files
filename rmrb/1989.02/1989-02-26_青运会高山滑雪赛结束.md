Title: The Second National Youth Games Winter Alpine Skiing Competition Concludes
Author: Wáng Jìfù Huǎ Hào
Date: 1989-02-26
Edition: 4

The Second National Youth Games (Winter) Alpine Skiing Competition concluded today after seven days of intense competition. In the eight final events, the PLA team secured three gold medals, while the Tonghua team won two gold medals. The Jilin team, Harbin team, and Jiamusi team each claimed one gold medal.

In the men's slalom final held this morning, Li Changsong of the Harbin team achieved the top spot with a time of 1 minute and 37.12 seconds.

In the afternoon, the women's slalom final took place. Chen Yingmei of the Jilin team won the championship with a time of 1 minute and 34.50 seconds. Sun Jiahai of the PLA team and Han Yang of the Jilin team secured the second and third places, respectively. After the competition, the organizing committee awarded the Sportsmanship Award to the Jilin Alpine Skiing Team and the Xinjiang Altay Region Ski Jumping Team.

### 青运会高山滑雪赛结束
王继富　胡浩
1989-02-26
第4版(社会生活·体育)
专栏：体育

　　青运会高山滑雪赛结束
    据新华社吉林二月二十四日电  （通讯员王继富、记者胡浩）历时七天的第二届全国青运会（冬季）高山滑雪赛今天在这里结束。在总共八个项目的决赛中，解放军队获得三枚金牌，通化队获得两枚金牌，吉林队、哈尔滨队、佳木斯队各获一块金牌。
    在今天上午进行的男子回转决赛中，哈尔滨队的李昌松以一分三十七秒一二的成绩取得了第一名。
    下午进行了女子回转决赛，冠军被吉林队的陈英梅以一分三十四秒五○的成绩夺走。解放军队的孙家海和吉林队的韩阳取得了第二名和第三名。赛后，组委会还向吉林高山滑雪队和新疆阿勒泰地区跳台滑雪队颁发了体育道德风尚奖。
