Title: "Rain Man" Nominated for Oscar Awards
Author: Lù Wényuè
Date: 1989-02-17
Edition: 7

The film "Rain Man," which has been highly successful at the box office in the United States, received eight nominations today for the 61st Academy Awards, including Best Picture.

Two other American films, "Dangerous Liaisons" and "Mississippi Burning," each received seven nominations. The other two American films nominated for Best Picture are "Working Girl" and "The Accidental Tourist." American film star Dustin Hoffman received a nomination for Best Actor for his role in "Rain Man." The film's director, Barry Levinson, also received a nomination.

### 《雨中人》获奥斯卡奖提名
陆文岳
1989-02-17
第7版(国际)
专栏：

　　《雨中人》获奥斯卡奖提名
    新华社华盛顿２月１５日电  （记者陆文岳）美国卖座率很高的影片《雨中人》今天获得第６１届奥斯卡最佳影片等８项奖的提名。
    获７项奖提名的美国影片有两部：《危险关系》和《密西西比河在燃烧》。另外两部得到最佳影片奖提名的美国影片是《邂逅》和《职业姑娘》。美国大影星达斯汀·霍夫曼获《雨中人》最佳男主角奖提名。此片导演巴里·莱文森也获得提名。
