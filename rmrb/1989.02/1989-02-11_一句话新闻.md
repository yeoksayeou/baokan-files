Author:
Date: 1989-02-11
Edition: 6

	One-Sentence News
		△	Kuwait's Minister of Oil said on the 9th that due to the guarantee of maritime safety in the Gulf waters, Kuwaiti tankers will soon stop registering in the United States, the Soviet Union, and the United Kingdom.
		△	On the 9th, Palestinian residents clashed with Israeli troops in the West Bank, resulting in the deaths of 5 Palestinian youths and 1 child.
		△	The United Nations Security Council will hold a formal meeting on the 10th to discuss the situation in the occupied Palestinian territories.
		△	On the 9th, the General Secretary of the Czechoslovak Communist Party, Jakes, accused some political groups and newspapers in the West of launching a campaign against Czechoslovakia, undermining its sovereignty.
		△	On the morning of the 9th, Panama's Channel 2 television station was bombed, damaging some facilities, but there were no casualties.

### 一句话新闻

1989-02-11
第6版(国际)
专栏：一句话新闻

　　一句话新闻
    △科威特石油大臣９日说，由于海湾水域的航运安全得到了保障，科威特油轮将很快停止在美国、苏联和英国注册。
    △巴勒斯坦居民９日同以色列军队在约旦河西岸发生激烈的冲突，致使５名巴勒斯坦青年和１名儿童被打死。
    △联合国安理会１０日将举行正式会议讨论巴勒斯坦被占领土的形势问题。
    △捷共中央总书记雅克什９日指责西方一些政治集团和报刊掀起反对捷克斯洛伐克的运动，破坏捷的主权。
    △巴拿马国家电视二台９日凌晨３点，遭到炸弹袭击，部分设施被毁坏，但没有人员伤亡。
    　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　（本报讯）
