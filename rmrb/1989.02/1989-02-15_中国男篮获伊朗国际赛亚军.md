Title: China Men's Basketball Team Wins Silver at Iran International Tournament
Author:
Date: 1989-02-15
Edition: 3

The international men's basketball tournament held to celebrate the Iranian Revolution concluded on the afternoon of the 11th in the eastern Iranian city of Mashhad. In the final, the Chinese team lost to the Soviet team with a score of 60 to 91, securing the silver medal. The Soviet team won the championship, while the Iranian team finished third.

### 中国男篮获伊朗国际赛亚军

1989-02-15
第3版(要闻)
专栏：体育

　　中国男篮获伊朗国际赛亚军
    为庆祝伊朗革命节而举行的国际男篮赛十一日下午在伊朗东部城市马什哈德结束。决赛中，中国队以六十比九十一负于苏联队，获得亚军，苏联队获得冠军。伊朗队名列第三。
