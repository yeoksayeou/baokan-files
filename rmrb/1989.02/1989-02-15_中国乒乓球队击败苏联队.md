Title: The Chinese Ping Pong Team Defeats the Soviet Team
Author:
Date: 1989-02-15
Edition: 3

The international ping pong invitational held to celebrate Iran's Revolution Day concluded on the 11th in the southern Iranian city of Shiraz. The Chinese team defeated the Soviet team 5-0 to win the team championship. Athletes from ten countries participated in this competition.

### 中国乒乓球队击败苏联队

1989-02-15
第3版(要闻)
专栏：体育

　　中国乒乓球队击败苏联队
    为庆祝伊朗革命节而举行的国际乒乓球邀请赛十一日在伊朗南部城市设拉子结束，中国队以五比零击败苏联队夺得团体冠军。十个国家的选手参加了这次比赛。
