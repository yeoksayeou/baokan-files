Title: The Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference Holds a Tea Party for the Widows of Deceased Comrades
Author: Feng Yuan
Date: 1989-02-01
Edition: 1

More than 100 widows of deceased members of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference and prominent figures attended a spring festival tea party held by the National Committee of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference today at the National Committee's auditorium.

Vice Chairpersons of the National Committee of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference, Wang Renhe, Gu Mu, Yang Jingren, Kang Keqing, Zhou Peiyuan, Qu Wu, Qian Weichang, Sun Xiaocun, Cheng Siyuan, Lu Jiashi, and Qian Zhengying, greeted everyone. Wang Renhe also conveyed greetings from Chairman Li Xiannian, wishing everyone a happy Spring Festival, good health, and happiness for their families.

Wang Renhe said in his speech that in the process of establishing and consolidating the people's democratic regime, carrying out socialist revolution and construction, the sisters present, like the deceased old comrades and friends, have devoted their efforts, made contributions, and paid their dues. Wang Renhe hoped that the widows would offer suggestions and criticisms to improve the work of the National Committee of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference.

The following widows attended the tea party: Ma Zhenzhen (wife of Ma Jian), Wang Lifeng (wife of Wu Youxun), Wang Guangmei (wife of Liu Shaoqi), Wang Zhongzhen (wife of Ma Yincui), Wang Dingguo (wife of Xie Jueyuan), Wang Xinlan (wife of Xiao Hua), Feng Huide (wife of Qi Yanming), Zhu Yushu (wife of Chen Mingxun), Zhu Duanshu (wife of Xiong Jinting), Yan Meihe (wife of Yang Yunzhi), Wu Xiaoyuan (wife of Hua Luogeng), Song Xiaojuan (wife of He Jicheng), Zhang Suyi (daughter of Zhou Jibin and Zhang Zhong), Chen Lan (wife of Deng Zicong), Zheng Xiaozhen (wife of Sa Kongyi), Zhao Shuzhi (wife of Chen Shouxian), Hu Xieqing (wife of Shu Sheyu), Zhong Fuguang (wife of Shi Fulang), Wong Huixiao (wife of Peng Zemin), Guo Xiuyi (wife of Huang Qixiang), Guo Yiqing (wife of Cheng Qian), Pu Jiexiu (wife of Du Chunyan), Huang Wei (wife of Ou Qinqin), Sheng Cijun (wife of Chen Cisheng), Liang Xiuyan (wife of Li Jishen), Hui Guofang (wife of Zhang Chong), Fu Xuewen (wife of Shao Lizi), Liao Jingwen (wife of Xu Beihong), Yan Mingyi (wife of Li Pingheng), Xue Wenshu (wife of Li Shucheng).

The Secretary-General of the National Committee of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference, Zhou Shaozhen, presided over the tea party.

### 政协举办已故老同志夫人茶话会  王任重代表李先念祝大家春节快乐
冯媛
1989-02-01
第1版(要闻)
专栏：

　　政协举办已故老同志夫人茶话会
    王任重代表李先念祝大家春节快乐
    本报北京１月３１日讯  记者冯媛报道：１００多名已故政协委员和知名人士的夫人们，今天来到全国政协礼堂，出席全国政协为她们举办的春节茶话会。
    全国政协副主席王任重、谷牧、杨静仁、康克清、周培源、屈武、钱伟长、孙晓村、程思远、卢嘉锡、钱正英向大家拜了年。王任重还代表李先念主席祝大家春节快乐，身体健康，阖家幸福。
    王任重在讲话中说，在人民政协完成建立和巩固人民民主政权、在进行社会主义革命和社会主义建设中，在座的各位姐妹同已故的老同志、老朋友一样，花费了心血，付出了劳动，作出了贡献。王任重希望各位夫人对改进全国政协工作提出建议和批评。
    马存真（马坚夫人）、王立芬（吴有训夫人）、王光美（刘少奇夫人）、王仲贞（马寅初夫人）、王定国（谢觉哉夫人）、王新兰（肖华夫人）、冯慧德（齐燕铭夫人）、朱玉淑（陈铭枢夫人）、朱端绶（熊瑾汀夫人）、严梅和（杨允植夫人）、吴筱元（华罗庚夫人）、宋晓菡（何基沣夫人）、张素我（周嘉彬夫人、张治中女儿）、陈兰（邓子恢夫人）、郑小箴（萨空了夫人）、赵恕之（陈劭先夫人）、胡絜青（舒舍予夫人）、钟复光（施复亮夫人）、翁会巧（彭泽民夫人）、郭秀仪（黄琪翔夫人）、郭翼青（程潜夫人）、浦洁修（杜春晏夫人）、黄葳（欧阳钦夫人）、盛此君（陈此生夫人）、梁秀莲（李济深夫人）、惠国芳（张冲夫人）、傅学文（邵力子夫人）、廖静文（徐悲鸿夫人）、颜明宜（李平衡夫人）、薛文淑（李书城夫人）等出席了今天的茶话会。
    全国政协秘书长周绍铮主持了今天的茶话会。
