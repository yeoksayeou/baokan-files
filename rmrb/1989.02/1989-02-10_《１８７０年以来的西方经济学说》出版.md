Title: Publication of "Western Economic Theories Since 1870"
Author: Hou Huiling
Date: 1989-02-10
Edition: 5

The economic theory monograph "Western Economic Theories Since 1870" written by Hu Jizhuang has been published by the Economic Science Press. This work systematically analyzes the historical development of Western economic theories over the past century. It avoids the simplistic tendency to outrightly reject Western economic theories and strives to provide an objective examination and evaluation of both the correct and incorrect aspects of Western economic theories.
