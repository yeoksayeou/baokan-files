Title: Celebrating the 40th Anniversary of the Founding of New China: National Calligraphy and Painting Exhibition to be Held in Beijing This Summer
Author: Yì Jiǎnrú
Date: 1989-02-03
Edition: 3

To celebrate the 40th anniversary of the founding of the People's Republic of China, the Beijing Qingyun Poetry, Calligraphy, and Painting Association and others will host a national calligraphy and painting exhibition.

The exhibition, jointly organized by the Qingyun Poetry, Calligraphy, and Painting Association, the Overseas Edition of the People's Daily, the Cultural Department of the Economic Daily, the Huasheng Daily, and the China Senior Citizen's Daily, will feature works from renowned calligraphers and painters, as well as a competition open to the public. The exhibition aims to collect and display approximately 600 selected works from across the nation, including Hong Kong, Macau, Taiwan, and overseas Chinese communities.

The exhibition is scheduled to take place in July and August of this year at the Chinese History Museum in Beijing.

### 庆祝新中国成立四十周年  全国书画大联展今夏在京举行
易俭如
1989-02-03
第3版(科教·文化·体育)
专栏：

　　庆祝新中国成立四十周年
    全国书画大联展今夏在京举行
    据新华社北京２月２日电  （记者易俭如）为庆祝中华人民共和国成立４０周年，北京卿云诗书画联谊社等将举办全国书画大联展。
    由卿云诗书画联谊社与人民日报社海外版、经济日报文艺部、华声报、中国老年报共同发起筹办的这次大联展，除向书画名家特约一部分作品外，主要采取竞赛方式，向全国包括港澳、台湾及海外同胞广泛征稿，预计可遴选出６００幅参展作品。
    联展将于今年七八月间在中国历史博物馆举行。
