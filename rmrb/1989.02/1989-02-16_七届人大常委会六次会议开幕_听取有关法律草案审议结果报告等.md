Title: The Sixth Session of the Seventh National People's Congress Standing Committee Opens
Author:
Date: 1989-02-16
Edition: 1

The sixth session of the Seventh National People's Congress Standing Committee opened today at the Great Hall of the People.

Wan Li, the Chairman, presided over the meeting.

At the beginning of the meeting, Peng Chong, Vice Chairman, explained the draft agenda for this session. The committee members approved this agenda.

According to the agenda, the committee members today heard reports on the following:

* The results of the deliberations on the draft of the Law on the Inspection of Import and Export Commodities by Song Ruwei and Xiang Chunyi, both Vice Chairmen of the National People's Congress Law Committee.
* The results of the deliberations on the draft of the Law on the Prevention and Treatment of Infectious Diseases by Song Ruwei and Xiang Chunyi.
* The explanation of the draft of the Administrative Litigation Law and the draft of the Rules of Procedure of the National People's Congress by Wang Hanbin, Vice Chairman of the Standing Committee and Director of the Legislative Affairs Commission.
* The report on the draft of the Basic Law of the Hong Kong Special Administrative Region of the People's Republic of China and related documents by Ji Pengfei, Chairman of the Drafting Committee of the Basic Law of the Hong Kong Special Administrative Region.
* The report on the examination of the qualifications of the supplementary representatives by Feng Zhijun, Vice Chairman of the National People's Congress Standing Committee's Qualification Examination Committee for Representatives.

Today's meeting also distributed the draft decision on the composition of the delegation of Hainan Province to the Seventh National People's Congress and the written report on the visit of the delegation of the National People's Congress to the Philippines submitted by Ye Fei, Vice Chairman.

Vice Chairmen Hu Zongxun, Zhu Lifan, Apu Aqingjinmei, Saifuding Aizisi, Zhou Guocheng, Yan Jizi, Rong Yiren, Ye Fei, Ni Zhifu, Chen Muhua, Fei Xiaotong, Lei Jieqiong, and Wang Hanbin attended the meeting.

State Councilor Chen Junsheng, President of the Supreme People's Court Ren Jianxin, and Procurator-General of the Supreme People's Procuratorate Liu Fuzhi attended the meeting as non-voting participants.

### 七届人大常委会六次会议开幕  听取有关法律草案审议结果报告等

1989-02-16
第1版(要闻)
专栏：

　　七届人大常委会六次会议开幕    
    听取有关法律草案审议结果报告等
    新华社北京２月１５日电  七届全国人大常委会第六次会议今天在人民大会堂开幕。
    万里委员长主持会议。
    会议开始时，彭冲副委员长就这次会议的议程草案作了说明。委员们通过了这个议程。
    根据议程，委员们今天听取了全国人大法律委员会副主任委员宋汝棼、项淳一分别作的对进出口商品检验法草案和传染病防治法草案审议结果的报告，听取了全国人大常委会副委员长、法制工作委员会主任王汉斌作的关于修改行政诉讼法草案和受委员长会议委托作的关于全国人民代表大会议事规则草案的说明，听取了香港特别行政区基本法起草委员会主任委员姬鹏飞作的关于提请全国人大常委会审议中华人民共和国香港特别行政区基本法草案及有关文件的报告，听取了全国人大常委会代表资格审查委员会副主任委员冯之浚作的代表资格审查委员会关于补选代表的代表资格的审查报告。
    今天的会议还印发了全国人大常委会关于海南省出席第七届全国人民代表大会代表团组成的决定草案和叶飞副委员长提交的全国人大代表团访问菲律宾的情况的书面报告。
    副委员长习仲勋、朱学范、阿沛·阿旺晋美、赛福鼎·艾则孜、周谷城、严济慈、荣毅仁、叶飞、倪志福、陈慕华、费孝通、雷洁琼、王汉斌，出席了会议。
    国务委员陈俊生、最高人民法院院长任建新、最高人民检察院检察长刘复之列席了会议。
