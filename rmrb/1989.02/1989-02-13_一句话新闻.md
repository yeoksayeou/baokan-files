One-Sentence News

    △ Philippine President Corazon Aquino on the 10th reiterated that she does not seek re-election as president in 1992.
    △ Polish United Workers' Party Central Committee Politburo member Jan Rejewski on the 10th suggested at the "Round Table" political reform subcommittee meeting to amend the constitution to establish a presidential system.
    △ Kuwaiti Prime Minister Sheikh Saad Abdul Salam Sabah returned home on the 11th after concluding his visit to Iraq.
    △ West German Vice President of the Bundestag Annemarie Renninger on the 10th concluded her three-day visit to Poland and left, during which she met with Polish President Mieczysław Rakowski and Prime Minister Tadeusz Mazowiecki, among others, and discussed issues such as expanding economic cooperation between the two countries.
    △ Czechoslovakian Minister of Foreign Trade Jan Šejba on the 10th led a Czechoslovakian government economic delegation on a one-day visit to Albania and signed the 1989 barter and payment agreement between the two countries.

### 一句话新闻

1989-02-13
第6版(国际)
专栏：一句话新闻

　　一句话新闻
    △菲律宾总统科拉松·阿基诺１０日重申，她不谋求在１９９２年连任总统。
    △波兰统一工人党中央政治局委员扬·雷伊科夫斯基１０日在“圆桌会议”政治改革问题小组会上建议修改宪法，设立总统制。
    △科威特首相谢赫萨阿德·阿卜杜拉·萨利姆·萨巴赫１１日结束了对伊拉克的访问后回国。
    △联邦德国联邦议院副议长安·伦格尔１０日结束了在波兰为期３天的访问，离开波兰，访问期间，他同波兰议长马利诺夫斯基和总理拉科夫斯基等举行了会见，双方探讨了两国扩大经济合作等问题。
    △捷克斯洛伐克外贸部长扬·什杰尔巴１０日率捷政府经济代表团对阿尔巴尼亚进行了为期一天的访问，并签署了两国１９８９年换货和付款议定书。
    　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　（本报讯）
