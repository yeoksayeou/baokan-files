Title: Tyson Defeats Bruno
Author:
Date: 1989-02-27
Edition: 1

World heavyweight boxing champion Mike Tyson of the United States (center) on the evening of February 25, in the fifth round, struck British boxer Frank Bruno (right) with a barrage of about a dozen heavy punches, leaving him dazed and forced him to concede defeat.

This match was broadcast by the U.S. "Home Box Office" cable television network, which paid Tyson $3.8 million. Despite his defeat, Bruno still earned $2 million.

### 泰森击败布鲁诺（图片）

1989-02-27
第1版(要闻)
专栏：

    泰森击败布鲁诺
    世界重量级拳击冠军美国的迈克·泰森（中）2月25日晚上在第五个回合中，以急雨似的十几个重拳把英国拳王弗兰克·布鲁诺（右）打得晕头转向，迫使他俯首称臣。
    这场大赛是美国“家庭票房公司”有线电视台转播的，它向泰森付了380万美元。布鲁诺尽管败北，也可净得200万美元。
                                         新华社发（传真照片）
