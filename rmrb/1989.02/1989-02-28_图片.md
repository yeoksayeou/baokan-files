Title: Dalian New Port's Commitment to Environmental Protection
Author: Xing Yongchen
Date: 1989-02-28
Edition: 2

Dalian New Port's harbor management company has been diligent in pursuing enterprise efficiency without neglecting environmental construction. In recent years, it has achieved remarkable results in environmental protection work and has been recognized as a national model for environmental excellence. The image depicts a corner of the harbor area.
