Title: UN Security Council Emergency Meeting Condemns Israeli Atrocities, PLO Demands Early Convening of Middle East Conference
Author: Liú Kāichén Yán Shìyuán Mǎ Sōnglín
Date: 1989-02-12
Edition: 6

The United Nations Security Council held an emergency meeting today to discuss the situation in the Palestinian-occupied territories. Representatives from five Arab and African countries condemned the escalating Israeli repression of Palestinians in the West Bank and Gaza Strip.

The meeting was convened at the request of Ahmed Gessar, the Tunisian representative to the United Nations and current chairman of the Arab Group.

Over the past two months, Israeli authorities have intensified their suppression of Palestinian demonstrators in the West Bank and Gaza Strip, killing 55 Palestinians and injuring at least 500 others.

Abdullah Salah, the Jordanian representative, cited Israeli military actions such as the destruction of Palestinian homes and olive groves, as well as the arrest and massacre of large numbers of Palestinians. He called on the Security Council to take measures to protect the safety of Palestinians in Israeli-occupied areas.

Egyptian representative Abdel Badiwi stated that the deteriorating situation in the West Bank and Gaza Strip is rooted in Israel's illegal occupation and violent repression. He emphasized that before a comprehensive solution to the Middle East problem is found, Israel should adhere to customary international law, particularly the Fourth Geneva Convention applicable to occupied territories. Israel should also recognize the legitimate rights of the Palestinians, especially their right to self-determination.

Senegalese representative Abou Sall Diop pointed out that Israel's policy of massacre in the occupied territories is contrary to the historical trend and violates human rights. He urged the Security Council to unanimously adopt a resolution condemning Israel's human rights violations.

The meeting will continue on the 13th.

Xinhua News Agency, Tunis, February 10 (Reporters Yan Shiyuan and Ma Songlin) Farouk Kaddoumi, the head of the Political Department of the Palestine Liberation Organization, said today that an international conference on Middle East peace should be convened as soon as possible to seek a just and comprehensive solution to the Middle East problem.

Kaddoumi made the remarks before his visit to China.

Kaddoumi criticized the Israeli authorities' negative attitude towards convening this conference and called on the member states of the United Nations Security Council to exert pressure on Israel.

Regarding the Palestinian people's struggle against violence, Kaddoumi said that in the face of the escalating repression by Israeli authorities in the occupied territories, the United Nations Security Council should adopt a resolution to protect the safety of the Palestinian people, thereby creating favorable conditions for convening an international conference on Middle East peace.

Regarding the U.S.-Palestine meeting, Kaddoumi believed that although the first U.S.-Palestine meeting in December last year did not yield satisfactory results, it is necessary for the U.S. and Palestine to continue holding meetings.

He reiterated the Palestinian people's determination to continue their struggle until all occupied Arab territories are recovered.

Kaddoumi praised the friendly relations between the Chinese and Palestinian peoples.

Kaddoumi will visit China on the 13th at the invitation of Foreign Minister Qian Qichen.

### 安理会紧急会议谴责以色列暴行  巴解要求尽早召开中东和会
刘开宸　阎世缘　马松林
1989-02-12
第6版(国际)
专栏：

　　安理会紧急会议谴责以色列暴行    
    巴解要求尽早召开中东和会
    本报联合国２月１０日电  记者刘开宸报道：联合国安全理事会今天举行紧急会议，审议“巴勒斯坦被占领领土局势”问题。５个阿拉伯和非洲国家代表在发言中谴责以色列当局在约旦河西岸地区和加沙地带镇压巴勒斯坦人的暴行升级。
    这次会议是应阿拉伯集团本月主席、突尼斯常驻联合国代表艾哈迈德·格萨尔的要求而召开的。
    近两个月来，以色列当局在约旦河西岸和加沙地带进一步镇压巴勒斯坦示威群众，杀害５５名巴勒斯坦人，被打伤的巴勒斯坦人至少有５００名。
    约旦代表阿卜杜拉·萨拉赫列举以色列军队摧毁巴勒斯坦居民住宅、橄榄树园以及逮捕和屠杀大批巴勒斯坦人的暴行，要求安理会采取措施，保护以色列占领区内巴勒斯坦人的安全。
    埃及代表阿卜杜勒·巴达威在发言中指出，约旦河西岸和加沙地带的形势恶化，其根源是以色列的非法占领和暴力镇压，在中东问题全面解决前，以色列应遵守习惯法，特别是适用于占领区的《第四日内瓦公约》。以色列应承认巴勒斯坦人的合法权利，特别是自决权。
    塞内加尔代表阿布萨·迪亚洛指出，以色列在占领区执行的屠杀政策是违反历史潮流的，也是违反人权的行为。他要求安理会一致通过谴责以色列违反人权的决议草案。
    会议将于１３日继续举行。
    新华社突尼斯２月１０日电　（记者阎世缘、马松林）巴勒斯坦解放组织政治部主任法鲁克·卡杜米今天在这里说，必须尽早召开关于中东和平国际会议，以寻求中东问题的公正和全面解决。
    卡杜米是在访华前夕向本社记者发表上述谈话的。
    卡杜米批评了以色列当局对召开这次会议所持的消极态度，并呼吁联合国安理会成员国对以色列施加压力。
    在谈到巴勒斯坦人民的抗暴斗争时，卡杜米说，面对以色列当局在被占领土镇压行动的升级，联合国安理会应通过一项保护巴勒斯坦人民安全的决议，以便为召开中东和平国际会议创造有利的条件。
    关于美巴会晤，卡杜米认为，去年１２月美、巴首次会晤虽然未能取得满意的成果，但美、巴继续举行会晤是必要的。
    他还重申了巴勒斯坦人民决心继续进行斗争，直到收复全部被占的阿拉伯领土。
    卡杜米赞扬中国人民和巴勒斯坦人民的友好关系。
    卡杜米应外交部长钱其琛的邀请，将于１３日对中国进行友好访问。
