Title: Bank of Communications Shanghai Branch Raises Funds to Support Enterprises
Author:
Date: 1989-02-17
Edition: 2

The Bank of Communications Shanghai Branch has fully leveraged the advantages of being a comprehensive bank, actively raising funds to provide "blood and oxygen" for Shanghai enterprises. Last year, it issued loans totaling 11.2 billion yuan to more than 2,300 enterprises, marking a 163% increase over the previous year.

### 交通银行上海分行筹资为企业“输血供氧”

1989-02-17
第2版(经济)
专栏：

    交通银行上海分行筹资为企业“输血供氧”
  　交通银行上海分行充分发挥综合性银行的优势，积极筹集资金为上海企业“输血供氧”，去年向二千三百多家企业发放贷款一百一十二亿元，比上年增长百分之一百六十三。
