Title: The 1989 Beijing International Logistics Machinery Exhibition to be Held in Beijing
Author: Zhao Hua
Date: 1989-02-15
Edition: 2

The 1989 Beijing International Logistics Machinery Exhibition will be held in Beijing from April 22 to 28 this year.

This exhibition will showcase a variety of transportation and loading/unloading equipment suitable for the circulation, storage, and transportation of materials; automated three-dimensional warehouses, packaging machinery, and packaging materials, totaling 15 categories and 260 varieties.

The exhibition is organized by the China Materials Economy Society and the China Council for the Promotion of International Trade.

### １９８９年北京国际物流机械展览会将在京举办
赵华
1989-02-15
第2版(经济)
专栏：

    １９８９年北京国际物流机械展览会将在京举办
    今年４月２２日至２８日将在北京举办１９８９年国际物流机械展览会。
    本届展览会将展出适用于物资流通储运环节的各种运输、装卸设备；自动化立体仓库、包装机械及包装材料等１５大类２６０个品种。
    展览会是由中国物资经济学会、中国国际贸易促进委员会主办的。
    （赵华）
