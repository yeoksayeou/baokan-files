Title: An International Drug Syndicate is Captured in the U.S.
Author: Huang Yong
Date: 1989-02-23
Edition: 3

An international drug syndicate was captured in the U.S.
Seized were drugs worth $10 billion

	NEW YORK, Feb. 21 (Xinhua) -- U.S. police and FBI detectives today raided a location in Queens, New York City, and busted an international drug trafficking ring. They seized 800 pounds of heroin worth $10 billion and $3 million in cash, and arrested 17 people on the spot.

	Prior to this, the International Criminal Police Organization had arrested 45 drug trafficking suspects in New York, Buffalo, Los Angeles, San Francisco, Detroit, Toronto, Vancouver, Hong Kong, and Singapore on Monday night and Tuesday morning.

	The FBI had been investigating for a year and a half for today's operation, code-named "White Nightmare."

	Law enforcement officials said the 800 pounds of heroin seized were smuggled from the Golden Triangle in Southeast Asia to Hong Kong and then hidden in the tires of handcarts exported to the United States. These drugs are enough for 100,000 "junkies" to use for a year.

	Statistics show that 23 million Americans used drugs last year, and 70% of the drugs sold on the streets of New York come from the Golden Triangle.

### 一国际贩毒集团在美落网  缴获价值１０亿美元毒品
黄泳
1989-02-23
第3版(国际新闻)
专栏：

　　一国际贩毒集团在美落网
    缴获价值１０亿美元毒品
    新华社华盛顿２月２１日电  （记者黄泳）美国警方和联邦调查局侦探今天在纽约市皇后区的一次突袭搜捕中，破获了一个国际毒品走私集团，搜出８００磅价值１０亿美元的海洛因和３００万美元现金，并当场逮捕１７人。
    在此之前，国际警察组织已于星期一晚上和星期二上午分别在纽约、布法罗、洛杉矶、旧金山、底特律、多伦多、温哥华、香港和新加坡抓获４５名毒品走私嫌疑犯。
    联邦调查局为今天的行动已进行了一年半侦查工作，其秘密代号为“白色恶梦”。
    警方人士说，被查获的８００磅海洛因是从东南亚的金三角运往香港后，装入出口美国的手推车轮胎中走私进入美国的。这些毒品足够１０万“隐君子”享用一年。
    据统计，美国去年有２３００万人吸毒，而在纽约街头出售的毒品有７０％来自金三角。
