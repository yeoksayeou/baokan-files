Title: China Electronics Information Industry Group Company Established
Author:
Date: 1989-02-24
Edition: 2

China Electronics Information Industry Group Company, composed of several directly affiliated electronic enterprises and institutions under the Ministry of Mechanical and Electronic Industry, was recently established in Beijing.

### 中电集团公司成立

1989-02-24
第2版(国内新闻)
专栏：四面八方

　　中电集团公司成立
    本报北京２月２３日讯  由机械电子工业部部分直属电子企事业单位组成的中国电子信息产业集团公司，近日在北京成立。
