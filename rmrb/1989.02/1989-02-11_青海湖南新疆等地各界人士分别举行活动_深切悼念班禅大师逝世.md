Title: People from Qinghai, Hunan, Xinjiang and other places hold activities to mourn the passing of Panchen Erdeni
Author:
Date: 1989-02-11
Edition: 4

People from Qinghai, Hunan, Xinjiang and other places hold activities to mourn the passing of Panchen Erdeni

According to Xinhua News Agency, Beijing: Party and government leaders, as well as monks and laypeople from Qinghai, Hunan, and Xinjiang, have recently held activities to mourn the passing of Panchen Erdeni.

In Qinghai Province, people from the five Tibetan Autonomous Prefectures of Hainan, Haibei, Huangnan, Yushu, and Guoluo, as well as the Haixi Mongol and Tibetan Autonomous Prefecture, held memorial meetings to recall the achievements of Vice Chairman Panchen Erdeni. In Hainan Tibetan Autonomous Prefecture, where Vice Chairman Panchen Erdeni had visited multiple times, people from various ethnic groups presented khadas (ceremonial scarves) in front of his portrait. Secretary Dongzhu Jia of the Prefecture Committee spoke at the memorial meeting about the care and support Vice Chairman Panchen Erdeni had given to Hainan Prefecture in terms of ethnic unity, economy, and cultural education. In the hometown of Panchen Erdeni, Wendu Tibetan Village, Madi Village, Xunhua Salar Autonomous County, more than 7,000 people from various sectors and religious groups have come to pay their respects at his former residence and participate in the memorial activities.

Since February 1, the Buddhist Association of Hunan Province and the Buddhist Associations of Changsha, Hengyang, Nanling, Shaoyang, Xiangtan, and Yiyang have held memorial services in local temples according to Han Buddhist rituals to commemorate the merits of Panchen Erdeni. Party and government leaders from Hunan Province and Changsha City attended the memorial service at Kaifu Temple in Changsha on the afternoon of February 3 and presented khadas to the portrait of Panchen Erdeni.

The Buddhist Association of the Xinjiang Uygur Autonomous Region and the Buddhist Association of Urumqi held a memorial prayer service for the 10th Panchen Erdeni on February 3. Party Secretary Song Hanliang and Chairman Tiemuer Dawameti of the Autonomous Region presented khadas to the portrait of Panchen Erdeni. Living Buddha Xia Liruan, President of the Autonomous Region Buddhist Association, read the prayer text at the service.

On the morning of February 4, more than 180 Tibetan teachers and students from Beijing Tibet Middle School held a memorial meeting for Panchen Erdeni in the newly constructed campus. The teachers and students presented khadas to the portrait of Panchen Erdeni. Vice Mayor He Luli of Beijing also attended the memorial meeting.

### 青海湖南新疆等地各界人士分别举行活动  深切悼念班禅大师逝世

1989-02-11
第4版(政治·法律·社会)
专栏：

　　青海湖南新疆等地各界人士分别举行活动   
    深切悼念班禅大师逝世
    据新华社北京电  青海、湖南、新疆等地的党政领导和僧俗各界最近分别举行活动悼念班禅大师。
    青海省的海南、海北、黄南、玉树、果洛五个藏族自治州和海西蒙古族藏族自治州的各界人士分别举行座谈会缅怀班禅副委员长的业绩。在班禅副委员长生前多次视察过的海南藏族自治州，各族干部群众把一条条哈达献到班禅副委员长的遗像前。州委书记东主加（藏族）在座谈会上讲述了班禅副委员长对海南州的民族团结、经济和文化教育事业给予的关怀和支持。在班禅大师的故乡循化撒拉族自治县文都藏族乡麻日村，已有７０００多各界人士和信教群众前来瞻仰大师故居，参加悼念活动。
    ２月１日以来，湖南省佛教协会及长沙、衡阳、南岳、邵阳、湘潭、益阳等地的佛教协会，分别在当地寺庙按汉传佛教仪式举行回向法会，纪念班禅大师的功德。湖南省和长沙市的党政领导干部３日下午到长沙市开福寺的班禅大师示寂回向法会上向班禅大师遗像献了哈达。
    新疆维吾尔自治区佛教协会和乌鲁木齐市佛教协会３日举行十世班禅大师圆寂祈祷法会。自治区党委书记宋汉良、自治区主席铁木尔·达瓦买提等前往向班禅大师遗像献了哈达。自治区佛教协会会长夏立宛活佛在法会上宣读祈祷文。
    北京西藏中学的１８０多名藏族师生，２月４日上午在新建的校园内举行了班禅大师悼念大会。师生们向班禅大师遗像献了哈达。北京市副市长何鲁丽也参加了悼念大会。
