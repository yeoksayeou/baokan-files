Title: China to Form Seven Major Regional Economic Groups
Author:
Date: 1989-02-26
Edition: 8

China's economic management model will transition from the traditional system of direct national control over provinces, municipalities, and autonomous regions to a new model where the state controls approximately three to four hundred economic center cities through seven major economic regions. The future seven major economic regions will be as follows:

1. The Northeast Iron and Steel, Petroleum, Heavy Machinery, Automobile, and Aviation Group, covering the regions of Liaoning, Heilongjiang, Jilin, and the eastern part of Inner Mongolia.

2. The North China Coal, Petroleum, Electronics, Chemical, and Textile Group, covering the regions of Beijing, Tianjin, Hebei, Shanxi, and the central part of Inner Mongolia.

3. The East China Machinery, Textile, Electronics, Chemical, and Light Industry Group, covering the regions of Shanghai, Jiangsu, Zhejiang, Shandong, and Anhui.

4. The Central China Iron and Steel, Machinery, Non-ferrous Metals, and Automobile Group, covering the regions of Hubei, Hunan, Jiangxi, and Henan.

5. The South China Electronics, Light Industry, Food, and Petroleum Group, covering the regions of Guangdong, Guangxi, Fujian, and Taiwan.

6. The Southwest Defense Industry, Machinery, Non-ferrous Metals, and Automobile Group, covering the regions of Sichuan, Yunnan, Guizhou, and Tibet.

7. The Northwest Petroleum, Chemical, Non-ferrous Metals, and Textile Group, covering the regions of Shaanxi, Gansu, Ningxia, Qinghai, Xinjiang, and the western part of Inner Mongolia.

### 我国将形成七大区域性经济集团

1989-02-26
第8版(每周文摘)
专栏：

　　我国将形成七大区域性经济集团
    我国经济管理模式将由国家直接控制省、市、自治区的传统模式，逐步转变为国家通过７大经济区控制三四百个经济中心城市的新模式。未来的７大经济性区域集团是：１、东北钢铁、石油、重型机械、汽车、航空集团，其地域范围是：辽宁、黑龙江、吉林和内蒙古东部。２、华北煤炭、石油、电子、化工、纺织集团，其地域范围是：北京、天津、河北、山西和内蒙古中部。３、华东机械、纺织、电子、化工、轻工集团，其地域范围是：上海、江苏、浙江、山东、安徽５省市。４、华中钢铁、机械、有色金属、汽车集团，其地域范围是：湖北、湖南、江西、河南４省。５、华南电子、轻工、食品、石油集团，其地域范围是：广东、广西、福建、台湾４省区。６、西南军工、机械、有色金属、汽车集团，其地域范围是：四川、云南、贵州、西藏４省区。７、西北石油、化工、有色金属、纺织集团，其地域范围是：陕西、甘肃、宁夏、青海、新疆５省区和内蒙古西部。
　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　（１月３０日《中国企业报》）
