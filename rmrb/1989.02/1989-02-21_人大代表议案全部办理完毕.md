Title: All Proposals from NPC Deputies Have Been Processed
Author:
Date: 1989-02-21
Edition: 1

The Standing Committee of the National People's Congress Office reported that all 394 proposals submitted by deputies during the first session of the Seventh National People's Congress have been processed, and relevant departments have provided written responses.

It is understood that out of these 394 proposals, 48 have had their issues resolved or basically resolved, accounting for 12.2%; 168 are being gradually resolved or are under study for resolution, accounting for 42.6%; 121 are complex or lack the necessary conditions and will be addressed later, accounting for 30.7%; and 57 have been explained by the handling units or are being kept for reference, accounting for 14.5%.

### 人大代表议案全部办理完毕

1989-02-21
第1版(要闻)
专栏：

　　人大代表议案全部办理完毕
    新华社北京２月２０日电  据全国人大常委会办公厅介绍，七届全国人大一次会议期间，代表提出的３９４件议案，有关部门已全部提出了处理意见并作了书面答复。
    据悉，在这３９４件议案中，所提问题已经解决或基本解决的４８件，占１２．２％；正在逐步得到解决或正在研究解决的１６８件，占４２．６％；因较复杂或条件暂不具备而需留待以后解决的１２１件，占３０．７％；承办单位作了解释或留作参考的５７件，占１４．５％。
