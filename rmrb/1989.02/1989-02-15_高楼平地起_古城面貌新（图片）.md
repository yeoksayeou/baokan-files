Title: Skyscrapers Rise from the Ground, Ancient City Transformed
Author: Tudeng
Date: 1989-02-15
Edition: 1

In recent years, Lhasa's old city has seen the construction of a number of Tibetan-style buildings, bringing the city's per capita living space to 6.33 square meters. The newly built residences are equipped with water supply pipes and underground drainage systems in front of and behind the houses, significantly improving the convenience of residents' lives.

### 高楼平地起  古城面貌新（图片）
土登
1989-02-15
第1版(要闻)
专栏：

    高楼平地起  古城面貌新
    拉萨古城近几年来新建了一批藏式楼房，使全市人均住房面积达到6.33平方米。新建住宅的房前屋后普遍安装了自来水管道和地下排水管道，大大方便了居民生活。
                        新华社记者　土登摄
