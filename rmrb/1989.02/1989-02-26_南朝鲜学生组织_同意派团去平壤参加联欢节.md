Title: South Korean Student Organization Agrees to Send Delegation to Pyongyang for Youth Festival
Author: Xú Bǎokāng
Date: 1989-02-26
Edition: 3

South Korean Student Organization
Agrees to Send Delegation to Pyongyang for Youth Festival

	Pyeongyang, February 24 (Xinhua) — Seoul, February 23 — The National University Student Representative Council of South Korea announced yesterday that it has formally agreed to send a delegation to participate in the 13th World Youth Festival to be held in Pyongyang this July. The council also proposed holding a student working-level talk in Panmunjom on March 16 to discuss specific issues related to the participation in the festival.

	The council said that during the student working-level talks, both sides will discuss the manner and scale of participation in the festival, as well as the possibility of jointly organizing academic, cultural, and artistic activities. According to reports, the South Korean authorities have expressed a positive attitude towards this decision by the students.

### 南朝鲜学生组织　同意派团去平壤参加联欢节
徐宝康
1989-02-26
第3版(国际新闻)
专栏：

　　南朝鲜学生组织
    同意派团去平壤参加联欢节
    本报平壤２月２４日电  记者徐宝康报道：汉城消息，南朝鲜全国大学生代表协议会２３日正式同意派代表团，参加将于今年７月在平壤举行的第１３届世界青年联欢节，并建议３月１６日在板门店举行南北学生工作会谈，商讨参加联欢节具体问题。
    南朝鲜全国大学生代表协议会说，在南北学生工作会谈中，双方将商讨参加联欢节的方式、规模以及共同举办学术、文化、艺术活动等问题。据报道，南朝鲜当局对南方学生的这一决定持肯定态度。
