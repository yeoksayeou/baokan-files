Title: Five-Star Brewery Leads the Industry in Profits for Last Year
Author: Lǐ Lónghuá
Date: 1989-02-01
Edition: 2

The Beijing Double Prosperity Five-Star Brewery, with a history of over seventy years, actively addressed factors that reduced profits and sought to increase both production and efficiency. In 1988, the brewery achieved a profit of 11.08 million yuan, a 58% increase over the previous year, setting a historical record. The profit per ton of beer reached 353 yuan, ranking first in the nation's industry. The total amount of profits and taxes paid was 10.47 million yuan, with each employee contributing 25,887 yuan in profits and taxes.

### 五星啤酒厂去年效益居同行业之首
李珑华
1989-02-01
第2版(经济)
专栏：

    ▲五星啤酒厂去年效益居同行业之首  具有七十多年历史的北京双合盛五星啤酒厂积极消化减利因素，向双增双节要效益，一九八八年实现利润一千一百零八万元，比上年增长百分之五十八，创历史最好水平，吨酒利润达三百五十三元，名列全国同行业之首。上交利税共一千零四十七万元，人均创利税二万五千八百八十七元。　　　　　　　　　　
　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　（李珑华）
