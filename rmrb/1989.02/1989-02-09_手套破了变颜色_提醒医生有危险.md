Title: Gloves Change Color When Torn to Warn Doctors of Danger
Author:
Date: 1989-02-09
Edition: 7

A British engineer from Wales has developed a new type of surgical glove that changes color when torn, alerting doctors to the risk of infection.

This glove is made of two layers of different colored materials. The outer layer is a lighter color, while the inner layer is darker. The two layers are vacuum-sealed together, making them adhere closely. When the glove is torn, air enters between the two layers, causing them to separate and revealing the darker inner layer.

### 手套破了变颜色　提醒医生有危险

1989-02-09
第7版(国际)
专栏：

　　手套破了变颜色
　　提醒医生有危险
    新华社伦敦2月6日电　据报道，英国威尔士一位工程师发现了一种做手术时用的新型手套，这种手套破了以后会变颜色，提醒医生有感染的危险。
    这种手套是用两层不同颜色的材料制成，颜色浅的一层在外，颜色深的一层在内。两层间抽成真空后，这两层便紧密贴在一起。手套破了后，空气进入两层之间，使两层分离，深颜色的这一层就显露出来。
