Title: Six Units Jointly Hold Symposium on Chinese Democratic Theory
Author:
Date: 1989-02-27
Edition: 6

The China Democratic Theory Research Group, the Democracy Series Editorial Committee, the China Natural Dialectics Research Society, the Four Communications Society Development Research Institute, the New Observation Magazine Editorial Office, and the China Legal System and Social Development Research Institute of Shougang jointly held a symposium on Chinese democratic theory on February 23. Speakers discussed their views on China's democratic political construction, new authoritarianism, and other issues.

### 六单位联合召开中国民主理论问题研讨会

1989-02-27
第6版(理论研究)
专栏：理论简讯

　　六单位联合召开中国民主理论问题研讨会
    本报讯  ２月２３日，中国民主理论研究课题组、民主丛书编委会、中国自然辩证法研究会、四通社会发展研究所、新观察杂志社、首钢研究与开发公司中国法制与社会发展研究所联合召开中国民主理论问题研讨会，发言者就中国民主政治建设、新权威主义等问题谈了各自的看法。
