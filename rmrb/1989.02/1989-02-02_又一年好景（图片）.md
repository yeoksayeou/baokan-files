Title: Another Year of Good Scenery
Author: Ruán Xiángyí Liu Lí
Date: 1989-02-02
Edition: 8

    Another Year of Good Scenery		Photography by Ruán Xiángyí		Poetry by Liu Lí

    The sunlight shines on your land,
    Your harvest and your busy days.
    Joy, like the fruits, is full and ripe.
    You seem content and intoxicated,
    Smiles from the heart to the eyebrows and eyes.
    Counting and counting, how can it ever be complete?
