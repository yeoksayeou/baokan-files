Title: Superior Product Quality and Attentive Customer Service: Wujin Plastic Machinery Factory Named Star Enterprise
Author: Liu Xieyang
Date: 1989-02-19
Edition: 2

	Superior Product Quality and Attentive Customer Service: Wujin Plastic Machinery Factory Named Star Enterprise

	By reporter Liu Xieyang: Jiangsu Wujin Plastic Machinery Factory has won the trust of its users with its superior product quality and attentive service, and was named a star enterprise by Changzhou City last year.

	Originally a small, unassuming factory, Wujin Plastic Machinery Factory now employs over 600 workers, with an annual output value of over 10 million yuan and profits of 1.35 million yuan. The factory produces plastic machinery, electronic industry machinery, and textile machinery. In 1986, a relevant department in Beijing accepted an urgent foreign aid project. An African country urgently needed a batch of film machines that were cheap, of high quality, easy to operate, and had a simple structure, with a guarantee of no maintenance for two years. Many units shook their heads upon hearing this. However, Wujin Plastic Machinery Factory boldly accepted the task. After more than a month of hard work, brand new film machines were finally produced, meeting all the requirements in terms of quality, specifications, and technology. Since then, the small Wujin Plastic Machinery Factory has gained a great reputation.

	As its fame grew, the enterprise became more meticulous in its service to customers. They handle not only production and research but also installation, debugging, and training, overseeing the entire process from start to finish.

### 产品质量过硬　待客服务周到   武进塑料机械厂被评为明星企业
刘燮阳
1989-02-19
第2版(经济)
专栏：

　　产品质量过硬　待客服务周到    
    武进塑料机械厂被评为明星企业
    本报讯  记者刘燮阳报道：江苏武进塑料机械厂以过硬的产品质量和周到的服务精神，赢得了用户的信任，去年被常州市评为明星企业。
    这个厂原先是个不起眼的小厂，现在，已拥有职工６００多人，年产值１０００多万元，利润１３５万元，能生产塑料机械、电子工业机械和纺织机械。１９８６年，北京有关部门接受了一项紧急的援外项目。有一个非洲国家急需一批薄膜机，价格要便宜，质量要好，操作要简便，结构要简单，还要保证两年内不维修。许多单位一听就摇头。而武进塑料机械厂却大胆接受了这项任务。经过一个多月的艰苦劳动，一台台崭新的薄膜机终于制作成功了，质量、规格、工艺等，完全合乎要求。从此，小小的武进塑料机械厂名声大振。
    知名度提高后，这家企业为顾客服务的工作越作越细致。他们既管生产和科研，又管安装、调试、培训，从头到尾，一抓到底。
