Title: Li Xiannian Meets Danish Prince Henrik
Author: Gù Yùqíng
Date: 1989-02-01
Edition: 1

Li Xiannian Meets Danish Prince Henrik

People's Daily, Beijing, January 31 - Correspondent Gu Yuqing reports: Chairman of the National Committee of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference Li Xiannian met with Prince Henrik, husband of Queen Margrethe II of Denmark, this evening at the Great Hall of the People.

Li Xiannian said that Denmark and other Nordic countries possess advanced technology, and China is willing to further develop friendly cooperative relations with these countries.

Prince Henrik said that the European Community countries have strong economic strength, and Denmark, being a member of the European Community, has many advantages for cooperation with China. He hopes that the cooperative relationship can be further developed.

Prince Henrik is the chairman of the Danish branch of the World Wildlife Fund. Chairman Li praised the prince for doing a lot of beneficial work in protecting wildlife.

State Councilor Chen Junsheng met with Prince Henrik this afternoon.

### 李先念会见丹麦亨里克亲王
顾玉清
1989-02-01
第1版(要闻)
专栏：

　　李先念会见丹麦亨里克亲王
    本报北京１月３１日讯  记者顾玉清报道：全国政协主席李先念今晚在人民大会堂会见丹麦女王玛格丽特二世的丈夫亨里克亲王。
    李先念说，丹麦和其他北欧国家拥有先进的技术，中国愿意同这些国家进一步发展友好合作关系。
    亨里克亲王说，欧共体国家有很强的经济实力，丹麦又是欧共体成员国之一，具备了与中国合作的不少优越条件，希望合作关系能进一步发展。
    亨里克亲王是世界野生生物基金会丹麦分会主席。李主席称赞亲王为保护野生动物做了不少有益的工作。
    国务委员陈俊生今天下午会见了亨里克亲王。
