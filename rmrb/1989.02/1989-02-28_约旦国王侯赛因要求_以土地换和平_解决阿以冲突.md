Title: King Hussein of Jordan Calls for "Land for Peace" to Resolve Arab-Israeli Conflict
Author:
Date: 1989-02-28
Edition: 3

King Hussein of Jordan believes that the appropriate way to resolve the Arab-Israeli conflict is to implement United Nations Security Council Resolution 242 and "land for peace."

Hussein, in Tokyo, told a reporter from an American television network that the "PLO-U.S. dialogue should address the core issues."

He said that the clear statements made by PLO Executive Committee Chairman Yasser Arafat in Geneva regarding the recognition of Israel's existence and the condemnation of terrorism are clear. He said that it should not just be a dialogue, but that the PLO should also be invited to participate in the international peace conference on the Middle East.

Regarding the role of the Soviet Union in the Middle East peace process, he said that the Soviet Union should be a participant, as it is a major power interested in the region.

Hussein believes that Israel's opposition to holding an international peace conference on the Middle East under the auspices of the United Nations is "short-sighted." He said, "We should not let the world be manipulated by these short-sighted hardliners."

### 约旦国王侯赛因要求 “以土地换和平”解决阿以冲突

1989-02-28
第3版(国际新闻)
专栏：

　　约旦国王侯赛因要求
    “以土地换和平”解决阿以冲突
    新华社安曼２月２６日电  据约旦报纸今天报道，约旦国王侯赛因认为，解决阿拉伯—以色列冲突的合适方式在于执行联合国安理会２４２号决议和以“土地换和平”。
    侯赛因在东京接见美国电视网记者时指出，“巴解组织—美国对话应该涉及问题的本质。”
    他说，巴解组织执委会主席阿拉法特在日内瓦发表的关于承认以色列的存在和对恐怖主义的谴责是明确的。他说，不应该只是对话，而且必须邀请巴解组织参加中东和平国际会议。
    他在谈到苏联在中东和平进程中的作用时说，苏联应该是一个参加者，因为这是一个对本地区感兴趣的大国。
    侯赛因认为，以色列反对在联合国主持下召开中东和平国际会议是“目光短浅”。他说：“我们不应该让世界受这些目光短浅的强硬路线派的摆布。”
