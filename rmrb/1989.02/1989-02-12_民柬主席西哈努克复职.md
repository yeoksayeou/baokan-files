Title: Prince Norodom Sihanouk Resumes Position as Chairman of the Democratic Kampuchea
Author:
Date: 1989-02-12
Edition: 1

Prince Norodom Sihanouk today morning received Thai journalists and said that he will resume his position as the Chairman of Democratic Kampuchea starting from today.

### 民柬主席西哈努克复职

1989-02-12
第1版(要闻)
专栏：

　　民柬主席西哈努克复职
    新华社北京２月１１日电  诺罗敦·西哈努克亲王今天上午在这里接见泰国记者时说，他从今天起恢复民主柬埔寨主席的职务。
