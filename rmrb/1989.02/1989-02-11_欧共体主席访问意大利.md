Title: Chairman of the European Community Visits Italy
Author: Luo Jinbiao
Date: 1989-02-11
Edition: 7

The Chairman of the European Community Commission, Delors, held a lengthy discussion with Italian Prime Minister De Mita in Rome on the 7th, exchanging views on the main issues to be discussed at the Madrid Summit of the European Community in June.

During the talks, Chairman Delors and Prime Minister De Mita focused on the European monetary policy. The issue of monetary unification will be the top agenda item at the Madrid Summit, and Delors believes it is "the key to the future of the European Community."

### 欧共体主席访问意大利
罗晋标
1989-02-11
第7版(国际)
专栏：

　　欧共体主席访问意大利
    本报罗马电  记者罗晋标报道：欧共体委员会主席德洛尔７日在罗马同意大利总理德米塔举行长时间会谈，就今年６月马德里欧共体首脑会议将讨论的主要问题交换了意见。
    会谈中，德洛尔主席和德米塔总理着重讨论了欧洲货币政策问题。货币统一问题将是马德里首脑会议的首要议题，德洛尔认为这是“决定欧共体未来的关键所在”。
