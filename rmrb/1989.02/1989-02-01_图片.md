Title: More American Women Participate in Self-Defense Training
Author:
Date: 1989-02-01
Edition: 7

Currently, an increasing number of women in the United States are participating in specialized self-defense training. The image shows a woman receiving training and using the learned techniques to attack an opponent. This type of self-defense training is highly popular among American women.
