Title: The State Administration for Quality Supervision Publishes List of Substandard Products for the First Time
Author:
Date: 1989-02-04
Edition: 2

	Hubei Xianning Wine Factory	Gan Shui Brand Osmanthus Wine	Excessive fusel oil

	Chongqing China Chuan Yu Grape Wine Factory	Premium Red Grape Wine (no brand)	No grape juice, zero dry extract
	Jilin Tonghua Grape Wine Factory	Tian Chi Brand Tonghua White Grape Wine	Severe turbidity in the wine
	Shaanxi Baoji Bed Factory	Bao Chuang Brand Electric Blanket	Poor pressure resistance
	Guangdong Dapu County Household Appliance Factory	Eiderdown Brand Electric Blanket	Internal wiring, heating elements, rated value markings not up to standard
