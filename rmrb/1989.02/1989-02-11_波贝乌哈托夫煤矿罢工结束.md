Title: Strike at the Bełchatów Coal Mine Ends
Author:
Date: 1989-02-11
Edition: 6

More than 7,000 workers at the Bełchatów Coal Mine in central Poland ended their three-day occupation strike on the 9th.

According to the Polish News Agency, the mine manager reached a temporary agreement with the strike committee representatives on the 9th, deciding to meet some of the strikers' demands by increasing their monthly wages by 21,000 zlotys. The striking workers had originally demanded an increase of 30,000 zlotys per month.

The strike began on the evening of February 6th.

### 波贝乌哈托夫煤矿罢工结束

1989-02-11
第6版(国际)
专栏：国际要闻简报

　　波贝乌哈托夫煤矿罢工结束
    波兰中部的贝乌哈托夫煤矿七千多名工人九日结束了历时三天的占领性罢工。
    据波通社报道，该煤矿经理九日与罢工委员会代表达成一项暂时性协议，决定满足罢工者的部分要求，每月增加工资二点一万兹罗提。罢工工人曾要求每月增加三万兹罗提。
    这次罢工是二月六日晚开始的。
    　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　（新华社）
