Author:
Date: 1989-02-18
Edition: 1

Li Peng Meets with Mitsubishi Corporation Delegation

	New China News Agency, Beijing, February 17 — Premier Li Peng of the State Council met with a delegation from the Mitsubishi Corporation of Japan, led by President Shosuke Morita, this afternoon in the Zhongnanhai Purple Light Pavilion. Both sides engaged in friendly discussions on further strengthening economic and technological cooperation between China and Japan.

	Li Peng welcomed Mitsubishi Corporation and other Japanese enterprises to invest in China, particularly in coastal regions, and hoped they would contribute to the development of China's export-oriented enterprises.

	Shosuke Morita expressed a willingness to further expand cooperation with China.

	Mitsubishi Corporation is a comprehensive trading group. So far, it has conducted extensive economic and trade exchanges and technological cooperation with China in the fields of energy, transportation, industrial raw materials, and agriculture. In 1984, the State Science and Technology Commission's China Science and Technology Exchange Center signed an agreement on scientific and technological exchanges and cooperation with Mitsubishi Corporation, and the implementation has been smooth.

### 李鹏会见三菱商事代表团

1989-02-18
第1版(要闻)
专栏：

　　李鹏会见三菱商事代表团
    新华社北京２月１７日电  国务院总理李鹏今天下午在中南海紫光阁会见了由社长诸桥晋六率领的日本三菱商事株式会社代表团，双方就进一步加强中日经济技术合作问题进行了友好的交谈。
    李鹏欢迎三菱商事等日本厂商来中国特别是沿海地区投资，并希望他们在帮助中国发展外向型企业方面作出努力。
    诸桥晋六表示愿意进一步扩大同中国的合作。
    三菱商事株式会社是一个综合性贸易集团。到目前为止，它与中国在能源、交通运输、工业原材料及农业等领域进行了比较广泛的经济贸易交流与技术合作。１９８４年，国家科委中国科技交流中心同三菱商事签订了关于科技交流与合作的协议，执行情况良好。
