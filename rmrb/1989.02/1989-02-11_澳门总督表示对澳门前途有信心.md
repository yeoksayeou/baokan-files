Title: Macau Governor Expresses Confidence in Macau's Future
Author: Lu Zhaoming
Date: 1989-02-11
Edition: 4

Macau Governor Expresses Confidence in Macau's Future

Xinhua News Agency, Macau, February 10 - (Reporter Lu Zhaoming) Macau Governor Carlos D'Abreu today, at a luncheon held to welcome Macau's media leaders, told our reporter that he has confidence in the friendly relations between China and Portugal and in Macau's future.

Carlos D'Abreu said that this year will see a good start for Macau, such as the official establishment of the Sino-Portuguese Joint Liaison Group in Macau on February 15, which marks the beginning of this good start.

According to a report by Macau Radio, Carlos D'Abreu also said on the 10th anniversary of the establishment of diplomatic relations between China and Portugal that the establishment of diplomatic relations between the two countries and the signing of the Sino-Portuguese Joint Declaration on the Macau issue reflect the will of both countries, ensuring the prosperity and development of Macau.

### 澳门总督表示对澳门前途有信心
罗照明
1989-02-11
第4版(政治·法律·社会)
专栏：

　　澳门总督表示对澳门前途有信心
    新华社澳门２月１０日电  （记者罗照明）澳门总督文礼治今天在举行午餐会招待澳门新闻界负责人时，向本社记者表示，对中葡两国友好关系和澳门前途要有信心。
    文礼治说，今年澳门的形势将会有一个好的开始，如：中葡联合联络小组从２月１５日起正式进驻澳门，便是这好的开始的标志。
    另据澳门电台报道，文礼治在中葡建交十周年时表示，两国建立外交关系以及中葡关于澳门问题的联合声明的签署，体现了两国的意愿，保证了澳门的繁荣和发展。
