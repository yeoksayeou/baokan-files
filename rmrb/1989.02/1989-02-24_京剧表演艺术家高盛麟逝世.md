Title: Renowned Peking Opera Artist Gao Shenglin Passes Away
Author:
Date: 1989-02-24
Edition: 4

Peking Opera artist Gao Shenglin passed away. According to a report from our newspaper, Peking Opera artist Gao Shenglin passed away due to illness and failed treatment on the morning of February 13 in Beijing, at the age of 74.

Gao Shenglin was dedicated to inheriting traditional performance techniques and was innovative in his approach. He excelled in creating vivid character portrayals, each with a unique charm, and was highly regarded by a wide audience.

### 京剧表演艺术家高盛麟逝世

1989-02-24
第4版(文化生活·体育)
专栏：

　　京剧表演艺术家高盛麟逝世
    本报讯  京剧表演艺术家高盛麟同志，因病医治无效，于２月１３日晨在北京逝世，终年７４岁。
    高盛麟在表演艺术上，认真继承传统，刻意革新、创进。塑造人物形象，形神并妙，各具风韵，在广大观众中享有盛誉。
