Title: The US and Soviet Union Conduct Nuclear Tests
Author:
Date: 1989-02-13
Edition: 6

The United States conducted its first underground nuclear test of the year on February 10 at the Nevada Test Site.

Jim Boyer, a spokesperson for the U.S. Department of Energy, said today that the explosive power of this nuclear weapon was equivalent to 20 to 150 tons of TNT.

This is the 688th nuclear test announced by the United States since January 1981.

The Soviet Union conducted an underground nuclear explosion at 7:15 AM Moscow time today at the Semipalatinsk Test Site in the Kazakh Soviet Socialist Republic. This is the second nuclear test conducted by the Soviet Union this year.

According to TASS, the yield of this underground nuclear explosion was between 20,000 and 150,000 tons.

### 美苏分别进行核试验

1989-02-13
第6版(国际)
专栏：

　　美苏分别进行核试验
    新华社华盛顿２月１１日电  美国１０日在内华达试验场进行了今年首次地下核试验。
    美国能源部发言人吉姆·博耶今天说，这枚核武器的爆炸力相当于２０到１５０吨梯恩梯。
    自１９８１年１月以来，这是美国宣布的第６８８次核试验。
    新华社莫斯科２月１２日电　苏联于莫斯科时间今天早晨７时１５分，在哈萨克共和国的塞米巴拉金斯克试验场进行了一次地下核爆炸，这是苏联今年进行的第二次核试验。
    据塔斯社报道，这次地下核爆炸的当量为２至１５万吨。
