Title: Yang Shangkun Joins Nanjing Military and Civilians in Celebration
Author: Yang Xiangduan, Yin Xuecheng
Date: 1989-02-04
Edition: 1

    Yang Shangkun, the Chairman of the People's Republic of China, attended a Spring Festival celebration for military and civilian personnel in Nanjing today. The event was jointly organized by the Jiangsu Provincial People's Government and the Political Department of the Nanjing Military Region.

    Before the celebration began, Yang Shangkun, accompanied by Han Peixin, Secretary of the Jiangsu Provincial Party Committee, Gu Xiu Lian, Governor of Jiangsu Province, Xiang Shouzhi, Commander of the Nanjing Military Region, and Fu Kuiqing, Political Commissar of the Nanjing Military Region, met with party, government, and military leaders in Nanjing, as well as some veteran comrades. They exchanged New Year greetings.

    During the celebration, artists performed a variety of entertaining programs.

    Yang Shangkun arrived in Jiangsu on January 30 to conduct a work inspection. From January 31 to February 2, he visited the bustling Yingshun Comprehensive Market, Xiaohestreet Vegetable Market, and agricultural product market in Changzhou City, learning about the market supply situation before the festival. He hoped that local officials and commercial staff would do a good job in market supply, work hard to stabilize prices, and ensure that the people have a happy Spring Festival.

### 杨尚昆与南京军民联欢
张行端　殷学成
1989-02-04
第1版(要闻)
专栏：

　　杨尚昆与南京军民联欢
    新华社南京２月３日电  （记者张行端、殷学成）国家主席杨尚昆今天下午在南京市出席了由江苏省人民政府、南京军区政治部等联合举办的春节军民联欢会。
    联欢会开始前，杨尚昆由中共江苏省委书记韩培信、江苏省省长顾秀莲，南京军区司令员向守志、政委傅奎清陪同，会见了在南京的党、政、军领导同志和部分老同志，大家互致节日问候。
    文艺工作者在联欢会上演出了精彩的文艺节目。
    杨尚昆主席是１月３０日到江苏考察工作的。从１月３１日至２月２日，他在常州市访问了繁华的迎春综合市场、小河沿菜场和农贸集市，了解节日前的市场供应情况。他希望当地干部和商业职工认真做好市场供应工作，努力平抑物价，让人民群众过一个愉快的春节。
