Title: Five Teams of Young Sailors Compete in Xiamen
Author:
Date: 1989-02-17
Edition: 3

The "Flying Seagull Cup" National Youth Optimist Dinghy Invitational Race began on February 13 in Xiamen's coastal area. Teams from Liaoning, Shandong, Zhejiang, Fujian, and the national training team are participating in the competition. The race is divided into two categories: wooden boats and fiberglass boats, and includes two events: triangular buoy racing and long-distance island circumnavigation. The Optimist Dinghy has been included as an official event in next year's Beijing Asian Games.

### 五队小选手厦门竞帆

1989-02-17
第3版(科教·文化·体育)
专栏：体育

　　五队小选手厦门竞帆
    新华社北京２月１４日电  “飞鹭杯”全国少年乐观级帆船邀请赛于１３日开始在厦门市海滨举行。参加比赛的有来自辽宁、山东、浙江、福建和国家集训队的选手。比赛分木制船和玻璃钢船两个组别，进行三角绕标和环岛长距离赛两个项目的角逐。乐观级帆船已列入明年的北京亚运会正式比赛项目。
