Title: China-Africa Friendship Association and China-Sudan Friendship Association Hold a Reception to Celebrate the 30th Anniversary of China-Sudan Diplomatic Relations
Author:
Date: 1989-02-03
Edition: 4

The China People's Association for Friendship with Foreign Countries and the China-Africa Friendship Association held a reception on the evening of February 2 in Beijing to celebrate the 30th anniversary of the establishment of diplomatic relations between China and Sudan.

Vice Chairman of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress Chen Muxia attended the event to offer congratulations.

Vice Chairman of the China People's Association for Friendship with Foreign Countries Liu Gengyin and Sudanese Ambassador to China Muhammad Matar both spoke, recalling the traditional friendship between the peoples of the two countries in various fields and expressing the hope that this friendly relationship would continue to develop.

### 对外友协中非友协举行招待会  庆祝中国苏丹建交３０周年

1989-02-03
第4版(政治·法律·社会)
专栏：

　　对外友协中非友协举行招待会
    庆祝中国苏丹建交３０周年
    新华社北京２月２日电  中国人民对外友好协会和中国非洲人民友好协会２日晚在北京联合举行招待会，庆祝中国和苏丹建交３０周年。
    全国人大常委会副委员长陈慕华到会祝贺。
    对外友协副会长刘庚寅和苏丹驻华大使穆罕默德·马塔尔分别在致辞中回顾了两国人民在各个领域的传统友谊，表示希望这一友好关系能继续发展。
