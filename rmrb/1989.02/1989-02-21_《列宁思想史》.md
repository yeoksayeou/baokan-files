Title: The History of Lenin's Thought
Author:
Date: 1989-02-21
Edition: 6

	The History of Lenin's Thought
		Compiled by the Institute of the History of Marxism-Leninism Development, Renmin University of China
		Published by Shanghai People's Publishing House

	This book elucidates the development of Lenin's thought, demonstrating that Lenin became an outstanding Marxist not only by inheriting and defending Marxism but also by liberating his mind, daring to explore, and creatively applying the basic principles of Marxism to analyze and solve a series of new issues that arose in the Russian revolution and socialist construction under imperialist conditions. He advanced Marxism to a new stage, namely the stage of Leninism.

### 《列宁思想史》
中国人民大学马列主义发展史研究所编
1989-02-21
第6版(理论研究)
专栏：

　　《列宁思想史》
     中国人民大学马列主义发展史研究所编
     上海人民出版社出版
    本书阐述了列宁思想的发展过程，表明列宁之所以成为杰出的马克思主义者，不仅在于他继承和捍卫了马克思主义，而且在于他解放思想，大胆探索，运用马克思主义的基本原理创造性地分析和解决了帝国主义条件下俄国革命及社会主义建设中提出的一系列新课题，把马克思主义推进到一个新的阶段，即列宁主义阶段。
