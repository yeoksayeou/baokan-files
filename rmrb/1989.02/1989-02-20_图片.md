Title: Photo
Author: Zhang Ya Xin
Date: 1989-02-20
Edition: 5

	The image depicts Lu Wanhai (center), the political commissar of the Xinjiang Ili detachment of the Chinese People's Armed Police, visiting a border defense outpost. He is seen warmly conversing with local ethnic minority residents and discussing matters related to strengthening border defense construction. The photo was taken by People's Daily reporter Zhang Ya Xin.
