Title: Large Ceramic White Snake Story
Author:
Date: 1989-02-21
Edition: 4

A large ceramic version of the "White Snake Story" recently debuted in Zhenjiang City, Jiangsu Province. The piece is 13.7 meters long and 2 meters high, exuding an ancient and elegant charm. The creator is Zhang Guichang, deputy manager of the Zhenjiang Film Company. It was fired by the Jingdezhen Art Porcelain Factory.
