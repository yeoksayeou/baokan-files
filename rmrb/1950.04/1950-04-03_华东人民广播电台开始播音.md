Title: East China People's Broadcasting Station Begins Broadcasting
Author:
Date: 1950-04-03
Page: 3

　　Xinhua News Agency, Shanghai, April 2nd: The East China People's Broadcasting Station was officially established in Shanghai on the 1st and began broadcasting on the same day. The station's shortwave wavelength is 50.12 meters, and the frequency is 5,985 kilocycles; the medium wave wavelength is 375 meters, and the frequency is 800 kilocycles. The station's broadcasting times are: the first time is from 6:00 to 10:00, the second time is from 12:00 to 16:40, and the third time is from 17:00 to 19:00. No broadcasts on Sundays.

### 华东人民广播电台开始播音

1950-04-03
第3版()
专栏：

　　华东人民广播电台开始播音
    【新华社上海二日电】华东人民广播电台已于一日在上海正式成立，并即日开始播音。该台短波波长为五○·一二公尺，周率为五、九八五千周；中波波长为三七五公尺，周率为八○○千周。该台播音时间：第一次六时至十时，第二次是十二时至十六时四十分，第三次是十七时至十九时。星期日停播。
