Author:
Date: 1950-04-26

Central People's Broadcasting Station today has a social science lecture, Ai Siqi speaks on "What is Revolution?". 20:00 People's Forum: A program sponsored by the Ministry of Education of the Central People's Government: Deputy Minister Qian Junrui speaks on "The Direction of Education Serving Workers and Peasants." 18:45 Natural Science Lecture: Huang Shuze speaks on "Vomiting." 21:45 Character Stories: Introducing the deeds of the Four Martyrs of Guanjia Fort, Wenshui County, Shanxi. 12:45 Book Review: The communication and literary publication "Shuo Shuo Chang Chang" edited by the Beijing Mass Literature and Art Creation Research Association. 18:00 Folk Art: Gu Rongfu and Yin Fulai perform the row play "Red Flowers and Green Leaves Help Each Other." 20:30 Art and Literature Program: Soviet Songs. 22:00 Art and Literature Program: Tong Xinkui broadcasts the storytelling "Who Harmed It."
  Beijing Municipal
    People's Broadcasting Station at 7:30 today has new songs. 16:30 Youth Program: (1) School News, (2) Cultural Entertainment: "Introducing the Youth Service Department." 17:00 Time signal, entertainment program: (1) Soldiers' entertainment: The Jian Guo Bu 12th Detachment performs a clapper talk: "Introduction to the Cultural Life of the Troops." (2) Broadcasting instrumental music (1) Northwest folk music medley, (2) Agricultural dance music. 17:30 Children's Program: Mr. Sun Jingxiu continues to tell "The Story of Timur." 18:00 Workers' Program: Continuing to teach the song "Commemorating May Day." 18:30 Voice of the Municipality: A recording of a speech on "Regarding the Marriage Law" by Wang Feiran, President of the Beijing Municipal People's Court. 20:00 Russian Language Lecture. 21:30 Listener Service: (1) Answering Mr. Yang Jiming's questions regarding the Beijing urban real estate exchange; (2) Answering Mr. Song Kai, Mr. Zhang Fengqi, and Mr. Tian Mingqi's questions regarding radio receivers; (3) Broadcasting the story of the Xinhua Bookstore North China General Branch raising books and magazines for the troops; (4) A brief introduction to the Soviet film "Poet Lennis" to be screened tomorrow.

　　广播节目
