Title: Memorial Meetings and Exhibitions are being Organized Throughout the Soviet Union to Commemorate the 80th Anniversary of Lenin's Birth; The Lenin District of Moscow will Send out 10,000 Propagandists to Report on Lenin's Life and Work
Author:
Date: 1950-04-22
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, 21st dispatch via TASS: The people of the entire Soviet Union are enthusiastically preparing to commemorate the 80th anniversary of Lenin's birth. All regions are preparing various kinds of commemorative meetings. The Lenin District of Moscow will send out 10,000 propagandists to report on Lenin's life and work. Enterprises are organizing visits to the Lenin Central Museum and various memorial sites where Lenin carried out activities.

Exhibitions have been held throughout the Soviet Union. The Academy of Sciences of the Soviet Union is exhibiting Lenin's works published in dozens of languages of various Soviet nationalities and the world, as well as speeches by leaders of foreign communist parties. These include collections of speeches by Thorez, Marty, and Cachin commemorating Lenin; Foster's book "Fragments of a Worker's Life," which contains several memories of meetings with Lenin; Togliatti's reference to the Italian political leader Gramsci's books about remembering Lenin; and papers about Lenin published in foreign magazines and newspapers.

### 苏联各地筹办纪念会和展览会  纪念列宁诞辰八十周年  莫斯科列宁区将出动一万宣传员报告列宁生平和工作

1950-04-22
第4版()
专栏：

　　苏联各地筹办纪念会和展览会
    纪念列宁诞辰八十周年
    莫斯科列宁区将出动一万宣传员报告列宁生平和工作
    【新华社二十一日讯】据塔斯社莫斯科讯：全苏人民热烈筹备纪念列宁诞辰八十周年。各地都在准备各种各样的纪念会。莫斯科列宁区将出动一万宣传员，报告列宁的生平及工作，各企业都在组织参观列宁中央博物馆，及各个列宁进行过活动的纪念地。
    全苏各地都举办了展览会。在苏联科学院中，展览着以苏联各民族及全世界数十种语言出版的列宁的著作，及外国共产党领袖的言论。其中包括多列士、马尔梯和加香追念列宁的演说集；福斯特的《工人生活断片》一书，内有若干会晤列宁的回忆；托里亚蒂援引意大利著名政治领袖格拉姆希关于追忆列宁的书籍；和外国杂志报章所载关于列宁的论文。
