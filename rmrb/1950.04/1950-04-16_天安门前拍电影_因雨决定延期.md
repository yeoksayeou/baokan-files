Title: Filming in Front of Tiananmen Square Postponed Due to Rain
Author:
Date: 1950-04-16
Page: 4

　　Filming in Front of Tiananmen Square
    Postponed Due to Rain
    [News from Beijing News Agency] The Beijing Film Studio originally planned to shoot a film of mass activities in front of Tiananmen Square today (the 16th). It has now been decided to postpone it by one day due to rain. If the rain does not stop, it will be postponed further.

### 天安门前拍电影  因雨决定延期

1950-04-16
第4版()
专栏：

　　天安门前拍电影
    因雨决定延期
    【京市新闻处讯】北京电影制片厂原定今（十六）日在天安门前拍摄群众活动影片，现决定因雨延期一天，如雨不停仍顺延。
