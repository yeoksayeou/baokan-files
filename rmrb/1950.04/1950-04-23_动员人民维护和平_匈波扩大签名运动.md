Title: Mobilize the People to Maintain Peace: Hungary and Poland Expand Signature Campaign

Author:
Date: 1950-04-23
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, April 20th: Warsaw, Polish News Agency, April 18th: In order to mobilize millions of Polish peace supporters to sign the Great Peace Council's appeal, and to mobilize the broadest masses to maintain peace, the Polish National Peace Committee has made the following decisions: (1) Attract all people who do not want war, regardless of their worldview and opinions, into the peace movement. This is the main task facing the Polish peace movement. (2) Expand the organizational network of the peace support committees and strengthen their activities. To this end, it calls on all villages, towns, factories, schools, institutions, and state-owned farms to widely establish peace support committees. (3) All peace support committees must use all possible means to publicize the decisions of the Stockholm Conference of the Great Peace Council and struggle against attempts to spread imperialist propaganda.

Xinhua News Agency, April 20th: TASS, Budapest, April 19th: Representatives from all walks of life in the Hungarian Republic held a mass meeting in the Budapest Music Hall on April 18th to discuss the struggle for peace. Fifteen hundred representatives attended, including Sandor Ronai, Chairman of the Presidential Council of the People's Republic, Prime Minister Dobi, and leaders of the Hungarian Workers' Party. Professor Endres, who attended the Great Peace Council meeting, reported on the achievements of the Stockholm Conference to the assembly. She called on the Hungarian people to unanimously sign the peace appeal to demonstrate their strength and unity in striving for peace. The assembly finally passed a resolution emphasizing that the Hungarian people should stand united and without fear in the peace camp led by the great Soviet Union.

### 动员人民维护和平  匈波扩大签名运动

1950-04-23
第4版()
专栏：

　　动员人民维护和平
    匈波扩大签名运动
    【新华社二十日讯】波兰通讯社华沙十八日讯：波兰全国和平委员会为了发动波兰的千百万和平拥护者在和大常委会的呼吁书上签名，以动员最广大群众维护和平，已作出如下的决定：（一）把一切不愿意战争的人们，不论他们的世界观和见解都吸引到和平运动中。这是摆在波兰和平运动面前的主要任务。（二）扩大拥护和平委员会的组织网，并加强其活动。为此，号召各乡村、城镇工厂、学校、机关和国营农场，广泛设立拥护和平委员会。（三）一切拥护和平委员会须用一切可能的方法宣传和大常委会斯德哥尔摩会议的决定；并与散布帝国主义宣传等企图，进行斗争。
    【新华社二十日讯】塔斯社布达佩斯十九日讯：匈牙利共和国各界代表于四月十八日在布达佩斯音乐厅举行了讨论争取和平的斗争的群众性会议。出席代表一千五百人，其中包括人民共和国主席团主席斯查卡西兹，总理道比，匈牙利工人党领袖等。出席和大常委会会议的安第斯教授，向大会报告了斯德哥尔摩会议的成就。她号召匈牙利人民应该一致在和平呼吁书上签名，来表明他们争取和平的力量及团结。大会最后通过决议，强调匈牙利人民应举国一致，毫无畏惧地站在以伟大的苏联为首的和平阵营中。
