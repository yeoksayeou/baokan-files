Title: Zhangjiakou All-Circles Representatives Conference Concludes; Exercises the Functions and Powers of the People's Congress; Elects the Mayor and Members of the Municipal Government

Author:
Date: 1950-04-26
Page: 3

　　Zhangjiakou All-Circles Representatives Conference Concludes
　　Exercises the Functions and Powers of the People's Congress; Elects the Mayor and Members of the Municipal Government
　　[This dispatch] According to the Chahar Daily: The Third Zhangjiakou Municipal All-Circles People's Representatives Conference opened on the 12th and victoriously concluded on the 15th, lasting four days. This conference, acting in place of the People's Congress, unanimously passed four resolutions on the 14th, including the "Municipal Work Guidelines and Tasks for the Next Nine Months". After full deliberation and consultation, a general election was held on the 16th, electing Sun Jingwen as the Mayor of the Municipal People's Government, Quan Zhemin and Li Guanying as Deputy Mayors, and Jiang Shizhi and sixteen others as government members. These appointments have been submitted to the Provincial People's Government for forwarding to the Central People's Government for approval. The conference also elected Sun Jingwen as Chairman of the Zhangjiakou Municipal All-Circles People's Representatives Conference Consultative Committee, Wang Man, Yu Dehai, Ji Yaoting, and Hu Dongqiao as Vice-Chairmen, and Zhang Heming and twenty-four others as members.

### 张垣各界代表会闭幕  代行人民代表大会职权  选出市长及市政府委员

1950-04-26
第3版()
专栏：

　　张垣各界代表会闭幕
    代行人民代表大会职权  选出市长及市政府委员
    【本报讯】据察哈尔日报消息：张市第三届各界人民代表会议，十二日开幕，历时四天，十五日胜利闭幕。本届会议代行人民代表大会的职权，于十四日一致通过“今后九个月市政工作方针任务”等四项决议后，经过充分酝酿和协商，于十六日进行大会选举，选出孙敬文为市人民政府市长，权哲民、李贯英为副市长，江石之等十六人为政府委员，一并报请省人民政府转呈中央人民政府批准。会议又选出孙敬文为张市各界人民代表会议协商委员会主席，王漫、于德海、冀耀庭、胡东樵为副主席，张何明等二十四人为委员。
