Title: National Natural Science Workers Representative Congress Preparatory Committee Telegraph Endorsing the Banning of Atomic Weapons
Author:
Date: 1950-04-06
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, 5th:

　　National Natural Science Workers Representative Congress Preparatory Committee
    Telegraph Endorsing the Banning of Atomic Weapons

　　The Preparatory Committee of the First National Congress of Natural Science Workers of China, in response to the World Peace Congress Standing Committee's appeal to ban the use of atomic weapons, issued a telegram stating:

    The Third Plenary Session of the Standing Committee of the World Peace Congress passed an important appeal, demanding the unconditional prohibition of atomic weapons as weapons of aggression and mass destruction of people, and declaring that the first government to use atomic weapons against any country would be considered a war criminal. This is a powerful rebuff to the warmongers who constantly advocate atomic warfare, and it fully represents the wishes of peace-loving people around the world.

    As a scientific worker, as long as he has a bit of conscience, he must not tolerate the results of scientific research being misused as weapons to destroy the people, nor can he allow the bloody hands of warmongers to defile the dignity of science. Science is a sacred cause for the happiness of millions of people, and every scientific worker must fight for the peace and democracy of millions of people. Here, we represent all Chinese natural science workers in warmly supporting the resolution of the Standing Committee of the World Peace Congress, and call on scientists around the world to unite and fight the warmongers to the end, and smash all their conspiracies to incite anti-people wars.

### 全国自然科学工作者代表大会筹委会  通电拥护禁用原子武器

1950-04-06
第1版()
专栏：

　　全国自然科学工作者代表大会筹委会
    通电拥护禁用原子武器
    【新华社五日讯】中华全国第一次自然科学工作者代表大会筹备委员会为响应世界拥护和平大会常设委员会关于禁止使用原子武器的呼吁，特发表通电称：
    世界拥护和平大会常设委员会第三次全体会议通过了一项重要的呼吁书，要求无条件禁止把原子武器作为侵略与大规模毁灭人民的武器，并宣布第一个使用原子武器来对付任何国家的政府将被认为战争罪犯。这是对成天鼓吹原子战争的战争贩子们一个有力的回击，它完全代表了全世界爱好和平的人民的愿望。
    作为一个科学工作者，只要他还有一点良心，绝不能容忍科学研究的结果被盗用作毁灭人民的武器，也绝不能容许战争贩子们的血手来沾污科学的尊严。科学是为千百万人民谋幸福生活的神圣事业，每一个科学工作者必须为千百万人民的和平、民主而斗争。在这里我们代表全中国自然科学工作者热烈拥护和大常务委员会的决议，并号召全世界科学工作者团结起来和战争贩子们斗争到底，粉碎他们一切煽动反人民战争的阴谋。
