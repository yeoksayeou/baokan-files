Title: New Knowledge Forum, Today's Speaker is Qian Jiaju

Author:

Date: 1950-04-14
Page: 4

　　New Knowledge Forum
　　Today's Speaker is Qian Jiaju
    [Our Newspaper's News] The New Knowledge Forum of the Beijing Branch of the China Democratic National Construction Association is scheduled to hold its 65th meeting at the Xiehe Auditorium at 7:30 PM on the 14th. Deputy Director Qian Jiaju of the Private Enterprise Administration will give a lecture on "The Future of Private Enterprises." Members are admitted by notification. Others who wish to attend the lecture can obtain admission tickets from the China Democratic National Construction Association with a letter of introduction from a member or organization.

### 新知识座谈会  今请千家驹主讲

1950-04-14
第4版()
专栏：

　　新知识座谈会
　　今请千家驹主讲
    【本报讯】民主建国会北京市分会的新知识座谈会，定于十四日下午七时半在协和礼堂举行第六十五次会，请私营企业管理局千家驹副局长主讲《私营企业的前途》。会友凭通知入场。其他愿往听讲者，可事先由会友或机关团体具函介绍到民建会洽领入场券。
