　　Unite Against the Vietnamese Colonial War
    Vietnam General Confederation of Labor Sends Respects to French Workers.
    The Internationalist Spirit of the French People Greatly Encourages the Vietnamese People's Anti-Imperialist Struggle.
    [Xinhua News Agency, 5th] The Vietnam General Confederation of Labor recently sent a telegram to the French General Confederation of Labor, thanking the French working class for their internationalist spirit in opposing the Vietnamese colonial war, and expressing the unity of the working classes of the two countries. According to a Vietnam News Agency North Vietnam 2nd dispatch, the text of the Vietnam General Confederation of Labor's telegram is as follows:
    On behalf of the Vietnamese workers, we extend warm greetings to the working class and people of France, who are bravely fighting to restore peace in Vietnam and to guarantee the happiness and freedom of the French people. The internationalist spirit of the French people has greatly encouraged the Vietnamese people's struggle against the French colonialists and will help strengthen the struggle of all peoples in the world. We are deeply convinced that the current sacrifices of the French people and the selfless struggle of the Vietnamese people will surely eliminate the French colonialists from this part of the world and restore the freedom and happiness of the French and Vietnamese peoples. Our current struggle will also break down all the obstacles that the colonialists have placed in the way of mutual understanding and friendship between our two peoples. We pledge to support the French working people who are currently fighting to defend world peace and democracy.

1950-04-06
第4版()
专栏：

　　团结一致反对越南殖民战争
    越总工会电法工人致敬 
    法国人民的国际主义精神，大大鼓舞了越南人民反帝斗争。
    【新华社五日讯】越南总工会最近致电法国总工会，感谢法国工人阶级反对越南殖民战争的国际主义精神，并表示两国工人阶级团结一致。据越南通讯社北越二日电，越南总工会的电文如下：
    我们代表越南工人，向法兰西的工人阶级与人民致热烈的敬礼，他们正在勇敢地斗争，以恢复越南的和平，并保证法国人民的幸福与自由。法国人民的国际主义的精神，大大鼓舞了越南人民反抗法国殖民者的斗争，并将有助于加强世界所有人民的斗争。我们深信，法国人民目前的牺牲以及越南人民忘我的斗争，定能把法国殖民者从这一部分世界上消除，并恢复法国和越南人民的自由与幸福。我们目前的斗争也将打破殖民者放在我们两国人民间相互了解与友谊的道路上的一切障碍。我们保证支持目前正为保卫世界和平与民主而斗争的法国劳动人民。
