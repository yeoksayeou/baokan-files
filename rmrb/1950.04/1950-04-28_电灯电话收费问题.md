Title: Electricity and Telephone Charge Issues
Author:
Date: 1950-04-28
Page: 6

To the Editor:

　　Electricity prices, what determines them? What regulations govern their fluctuations?
　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　Wang Rizeng

Wang Rizeng:

　　Your question has been answered by the North China Electric Power Administration Bureau of Beijing Electric Power Bureau as follows:

　　The electricity price of Beijing Electric Power Bureau is calculated based on the wage and millet price in Beijing, Tianjin, and Tangshan, with reference to the wholesale metal index of Nankai University of North China and the special-grade final coal price for public utilities delivered in Beijing and Tianjin announced by Kailuan. If the price fluctuation affects the increase or decrease of the electricity price within 20% of the original electricity price, the electricity price will remain unchanged.
　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　Editor

To the Editor:

　　Why are the intra-city telephone charges in Beijing calculated based on the People's Victory Discounted Public Bonds? Is the current telephone charge too high?
　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　Wu Jieping

Wu Jieping:

　　Your question has been answered by the Beijing Telecommunications Bureau as follows:

　　Now the telecommunications undertakings in all parts of the country have been unified. Calculating the telecommunications price based on the People's Victory Discounted Public Bonds not only unifies the national telecommunications price, but also is relatively reasonable in maintaining the costs of machine line equipment (such as fuel, electricity, transportation, depreciation, etc.) and taking care of the minimum living standards of employees.

　　According to the current situation, the telephone charge can only maintain the cost. The construction and development of the telecommunications business still rely on government investment. The current telephone price is not high, less than one-third of the telephone price before the War of Resistance.
　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　Editor

### 电灯电话收费问题

1950-04-28
第6版()
专栏：

　　电灯电话收费问题编辑同志：
    电价是由什么决定的？按什么规定涨落？
                                                 王日增王日增君：
    你的问题，已由华北电业管理总局北京电业局答复如下：
    北京电业局电价，是照京津唐三地工资小米价，并参照南开大学华北金属批发指数及开滦公布的京津交货公用事业特号末煤价格，合算的结果。倘物价涨落影响到电价的增减在不超出原电价的百分之二十时，电价仍不变动。
                                                     编者编辑同志：
    北京市市内电话收费为何要按人民胜利折实公债计算？现在电话收费是否太高？
                                                        吴阶平吴阶平君：
    你的问题，由北京电信局答复如下：
    现在全国各地的电信事业已经统一，电信价目按人民胜利折实公债计算不仅使全国电信价目统一，而且在维护机线器材等费用（如油料、电力、运输、折旧等），和照顾员工最低生活等方面都是比较合理的。
    根据目前情况，电话收费仅能维持成本，电信事业的建设与发展，还得依靠政府投资。目前电话价并不高，尚不及抗战前电话价的三分之一。
                                                        编者
