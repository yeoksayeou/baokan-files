Title: Foreign Minister Zhou Sends Another Telegram to Ernst: China Has Appointed Li Qiang, Zong Zhifa, and Lin Dingxu as Representatives to the International Broadcasting Conference
Author:
Date: 1950-04-02
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, 1st - Foreign Minister Zhou Enlai sent a telegram to Ernst, Secretary-General of the International Telecommunication Union, regarding the appointment of Li Qiang as China's representative to the International Telecommunication Union. Today, Minister Zhou sent another telegram notifying Mr. Ernst that the Central People's Government of China has appointed Li Qiang, Zong Zhifa, and Lin Dingxu as representatives to the International Broadcasting Conference to be held in Florence, Italy, on April 1st, and requested him to forward this information to relevant countries of the International Telecommunication Union.

### 周外长再电艾奈斯特  我国已任命李强宗之发林定勖为参加国际广播会议代表

1950-04-02
第1版()
专栏：

　　周外长再电艾奈斯特
    我国已任命李强宗之发林定勖为参加国际广播会议代表
    【新华社一日讯】为任命李强为我国参加国际电讯联盟的代表一事，中央人民政府外交部周恩来部长日前曾致电国际电讯联盟秘书长艾奈斯特。今日周部长再致电通知艾氏，我中央人民政府已任命李强、宗之发、林定勖三人为参加四月一日在意大利佛罗棱萨召开之国际广播会议的代表，并请其转知与国际电讯联盟有关各国。
