Title: Democratic Germany's Youth Strengthen Peace Struggle
Author:
Date: 1950-04-02
Page: 4

　　Xinhua News Agency, Brandenburg, March 30th (via Telegraph) – At a recent conference, the youth members of various trade unions in the German Democratic Republic passed a resolution calling on the Free German Trade Union Federation and the Free German Youth League to cooperate closely in the movement to strive for peace and to fight for a unified Germany and the construction of a new German economy. All the young people who participated in the conference decided to join the Free German Youth League and called on other trade union youth members to take concerted action. The Secretariat of the Free German Youth League has agreed to this action and requested that the trade unions fully support their young members in achieving this goal.

### 民主德国青年加强和平斗争

1950-04-02
第4版()
专栏：

　　民主德国青年加强和平斗争
    【新华社一日讯】电通社勃兰登堡三月三十日讯：德意志民主共和国各工会的青年会员们，在最近召开的一次大会中通过决议，号召各自由德国工会和自由德国青年联盟相互紧密合作，进行争取和平的运动，并为统一德国和建设德国新经济而斗争。参加大会的全体青年，都决定加入自由德国青年联盟，并且号召其他工会青年会员们采取一致行动。自由德国青年联盟书记处已经同意这一行动，并要求各工会充分支援青年会员们以达到这个目标。
