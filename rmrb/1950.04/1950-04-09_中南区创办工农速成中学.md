Title: Central South Region Establishes Workers' and Peasants' Rapid Middle School
Author:
Date: 1950-04-09
Page: 3

Xinhua News Agency, Hankou, 7th: The Central South Military and Administrative Committee issued a decision on the establishment of Workers' and Peasants' Rapid Middle Schools in the Central South Region on the 1st. The decision stipulates that students participating in Workers' and Peasants' Rapid Middle Schools should be systematically transferred and selected by various agencies, factories, and schools. The qualifications are: male and female worker-peasant cadres on the original supply system who have participated in revolutionary struggles for more than three years, possess a level equivalent to that of a senior elementary school graduate, are between the ages of sixteen and thirty, are ideologically progressive, physically healthy, and actively working. The length of study is tentatively set at three years. According to the specific conditions of the Central South Region, the decision stipulates that in 1950, efforts will be concentrated on establishing four Workers' and Peasants' Rapid Middle Schools in Wuhan, Guangzhou, Kaifeng and other places to create experience and lay the foundation for the large-scale establishment of Workers' and Peasants' Rapid Middle Schools in the future. The decision points out that establishing Workers' and Peasants' Middle Schools is a major political task and must be taken seriously by all localities. The decision instructs subordinate government agencies and education departments at all levels to transfer the best cadres and teachers, allocate appropriate equipment and school buildings, and transfer and select qualified and sufficient students to study at the Workers' and Peasants' Rapid Middle Schools.

### 中南区创办工农速成中学

1950-04-09
第3版()
专栏：

　　中南区创办工农速成中学
    【新华社汉口七日电】中南军政委员会于一日发布关于中南地区创办工农速成中学的决定。决定规定，参加工农速成中学学习的学生，应由各机关、工厂、学校有计划地抽调和选送；其条件为参加革命斗争三年以上、具有相当于高小毕业的程度、年龄在十六至三十岁、思想进步、身体健康、工作积极的原供给制的男女工农干部；修业年限暂定三年。根据中南区的具体条件，决定规定在一九五零年内，集中力量先在武汉、广州、开封等地开办四所工农速成中学，以创造经验，为今后大量开办工农速成中学奠定基础。决定指出，开办工农中学是一个重大政治任务，必须引起各地重视。决定责成所属各级政府机关和教育部门，抽调最好的干部和教师，配置适当的设备、校舍，调选合格的足够的学员到工农速成中学去学习。
