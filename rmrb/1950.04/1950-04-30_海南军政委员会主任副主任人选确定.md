Title: Hainan Military and Political Committee Chairman and Vice Chairman Candidates Determined
Author:
Date: 1950-04-30
Page: 3

Xinhua News Agency, 28th: The 30th Administrative Council Meeting of the Central People's Government approved the following list of appointments to be submitted to the Central People's Government Committee for approval:

　　I. List of Chairman and Vice Chairmen of the Hainan Military and Political Committee:
　　Chairman: Deng Hua Currently Commander of the Corps of the Fourth Field Army of the People's Liberation Army,
　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　Member of the Guangdong Provincial People's Government.
　　Vice Chairman: Feng Baiju Currently Commander of the Qiongya Column of the People's Liberation Army, Member of the Guangdong
　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　Provincial People's Government.
　　II. List of Presidents of Xiamen University:
　　President: Wang Yanan Currently Professor at Tsinghua University.
