Title: Songjiang and Heilongjiang Provinces Elect Chairmen and Vice-Chairmen
Author:
Date: 1950-04-10
Page: 3

　　Xinhua News Agency, Shenyang, April 8th: The provinces of Songjiang and Heilongjiang in Northeast China successively held their first sessions of the People's Representative Conference in the latter part of last month. Both provincial conferences acted on behalf of the Provincial People's Congress, electing provincial chairmen, vice-chairmen, and members, and forming the People's Government Committee. The first session of the Songjiang Provincial People's Representative Conference was held from the 25th to the 29th of last month. The conference listened to, discussed, and approved Chairman Feng Zhongyun of the Provincial People's Government's report on "The Work of the Songjiang Provincial People's Government in the Past Three and a Half Years" and Secretary Rao Bin of the Songjiang Provincial Committee of the Communist Party of China's report on "The Work Tasks of Songjiang Province in 1950." After full deliberation and consultation, the conference elected the chairman, vice-chairmen, and members of the Provincial People's Government, and also elected members of the Consultative Committee of the Songjiang Provincial People's Representative Conference.
    The first session of the Heilongjiang Provincial People's Representative Conference was held from the 25th to the 31st of last month. The conference listened to, discussed, and approved Chairman Yu Yifu of the Provincial People's Government's "Summary of the Work of the Provincial People's Government in 1949 and Work Tasks for 1950" and Secretary Zhao Dezun of the Heilongjiang Provincial Committee of the Communist Party of China's report "Taking the Economy of Heilongjiang a Step Forward." The conference received a total of 727 proposals, which were reviewed by the Proposal Review Committee and merged into 322 items. The resolutions passed by the conference included seven items such as "Establishing Mutual Aid Committees at All Levels in Urban and Rural Areas to Strengthen Urban-Rural Mutual Aid," "Properly Resettling Immigrants for Land Reclamation and Production," and "Protecting Maternal and Child Welfare." The rest were respectively handed over to the relevant departments of the People's Government for implementation and transferred to the higher-level government for processing. Finally, the conference elected the chairman, vice-chairmen, and members of the Provincial People's Government; and also elected Yu Yifu as the chairman, Zhang Ruilin and Wang Qingzheng as the vice-chairmen, and Zhao Dezun and other 27 people as members of the Consultative Committee of the Provincial People's Representative Conference.

### 松江黑龙江两省选出主席副主席

1950-04-10
第3版()
专栏：

　　松江黑龙江两省选出主席副主席
    【本报讯】据新华社沈阳八日电：东北松江、黑龙江两省，相继于上月下旬举行首届各界人民代表会议。两省会议均代行省人民代表大会职权，选举了省主席、副主席及委员，组成了人民政府委员会。松江省首届各界人民代表会议于上月二十五日至二十九日举行。会议听取、讨论并通过了省人民政府冯仲云主席关于“松江省人民政府三年半以来的工作”和中共松江省委书记饶斌关于“松江省一九五○年工作任务”两报告。会议经过充分酝酿和协商后，选举了省人民政府主席、副主席、委员，也选出省各界人民代表会议协商委员会委员。
    黑龙江省首届各界人民代表会议于上月二十五日至三十一日举行。会议听取、讨论和通过了省人民政府于毅夫主席“关于省人民政府一九四九年工作总结与一九五○年工作任务”和中共黑龙江省委书记赵德尊“把黑龙江经济提高一步”的两个报告。会议共收到各种提案计七二七件，由提案审查委员会审查后，合并为三二二件。经大会讨论通过的有“成立各级城乡互助委员会加强城乡互助”、“妥善安置移民开荒生产”、“保护妇婴福利”等七项决议案。其余分别交人民政府有关部门实行和转请上级政府处理。会议最后选举了省人民政府主席、副主席、委员；并选出该省各界人民代表会议协商委员会主席于毅夫、副主席张瑞林、王清正及委员赵德尊等二十七人。
