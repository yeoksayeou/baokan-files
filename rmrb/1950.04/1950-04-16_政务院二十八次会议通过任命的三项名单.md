Title: The 28th Meeting of the Government Administration Council Approved Three Lists of Appointments
Author:
Date: 1950-04-16
Page: 3

Xinhua News Agency, April 15th - The 28th Government Administration Council meeting of the Central People's Government approved the following three lists of appointments:

    I. Supplementary List of Councillors of the Government Administration Council of the Central People's Government
        Councillor: Pan Huaishu, formerly Dean of the Law School of Anhui University.
                  Liu Zhiyan, currently head of the Rural Group of the Policy Research Office of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of China.

    II. List of Staff Members of the People's Government of the Inner Mongolia Autonomous Region
        Secretary-General: Liang Yiming, formerly League Leader of the Jerim League People's Government.
        Director of the Financial and Economic Committee: Wang Yilun, currently a member of the Inner Mongolia Sub-bureau of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of China.
        Deputy Director: Wang Duo (concurrently)
        Minister of Civil Affairs: Kui Bi (concurrently)
        Deputy Minister: Wulitu, formerly Deputy Minister of Civil Affairs of the People's Government of the Inner Mongolia Autonomous Region.
        Minister of Public Security: Wang Zaitian (concurrently)
        Deputy Minister: Wu Tong, formerly Deputy Minister of Public Security of the People's Government of the Inner Mongolia Autonomous Region.
        Minister of Finance: Temurbagen (concurrently)
        Deputy Minister: Hu Zishou, formerly Second Minister of Finance of the People's Government of the Inner Mongolia Autonomous Region.
        Minister of Industry and Commerce: Zhao Yunshi, formerly Minister of Industry and Commerce of the People's Government of the Inner Mongolia Autonomous Region.
        Deputy Minister: Liu Jingping, formerly Deputy Minister of Industry and Commerce of the People's Government of the Inner Mongolia Autonomous Region.
        Minister of Transportation: Wulejiao Xier (concurrently)
        Minister of Agriculture and Animal Husbandry: Gao Boze Bu (concurrently)
        Deputy Minister: Kang Ren, formerly Deputy Minister of Agriculture and Animal Husbandry of the People's Government of the Inner Mongolia Autonomous Region.
        Minister of Culture and Education: Hafeng A (concurrently)
        Deputy Minister: Bao Yan (concurrently)
        Minister of Health: Li Benzhou, formerly Minister of Health of the People's Government of the Inner Mongolia Autonomous Region.
        Deputy Minister: Huerqin Bilige (concurrently)

    III. List of League Leaders and Deputy League Leaders of the People's Governments of Various Leagues in the Inner Mongolia Autonomous Region
        People's Government of the Xing'an League
        Deputy League Leader: Qi Junshan, formerly Director of the Xilin Gol League Branch of the Inner Mongolia Autonomous Movement Federation.
        People's Government of the Hulunbuir Naven Morin League
        League Leader: Erqin Batu, formerly League Leader of the Hulunbuir Naven Morin League People's Government of Inner Mongolia.
        Deputy League Leader: Wuruxi Yele Tu, formerly Deputy League Leader of the Hulunbuir Naven Morin League People's Government of Inner Mongolia.
                      Chen Bingyu, formerly Deputy League Leader of the Hulunbuir Naven Morin League People's Government of Inner Mongolia.
        People's Government of the Jerim League
        League Leader: Wang Xiaotian, formerly League Leader of the Jerim League People's Government of Inner Mongolia.
        Deputy League Leader: Bao Zhong'ai, formerly Deputy League Leader of the Jerim League People's Government of Inner Mongolia.
        People's Government of the Zhaowuda League
        League Leader: Gun Ga Dan Rubu, formerly Deputy Director of the Organization Department of the Zhaowuda League Committee of the Communist Party of China.
        Deputy League Leader: Gao Huamin, formerly Deputy League Leader of the Zhaowuda League People's Government of Inner Mongolia.
        People's Government of the Xilin Gol League
        League Leader: Chao Luomeng, formerly League Leader of the Xilin Gol League People's Government of Inner Mongolia.
        Deputy League Leader: Wang Qin, formerly Deputy League Leader of the Xilin Gol League People's Government of Inner Mongolia.
        People's Government of the Chahar League
        Deputy League Leader: Se Yin E Li Bu, formerly Deputy League Leader of the Chahar League People's Government of Inner Mongolia.
