Title: Receiving Donations

Author:

Date: 1950-04-25
Page: 6

　　Receiving Donations
　　This newspaper has received the following donations for the relief of unemployed workers in Shanghai, which have been handed over to the Workers' Daily for forwarding:
　　　　All staff of the Xinhua Bookstore General Administration 1,035,600 yuan
　　　　Twenty-eight employees of the All-China Women's Federation New China Children's Toy Factory 242,100 yuan
　　　　Xu Shiyuan, Langfang Outpost, Fengtai Inspection Section 40,000 yuan
　　　　Li Ji, Third Bureau of the Military Commission 12,000 yuan
　　　　Wang Ying, Publishing Department, Research Department, Renmin University of China 6,250 yuan
　　　　All staff of the International Bookstore 668,100 yuan
