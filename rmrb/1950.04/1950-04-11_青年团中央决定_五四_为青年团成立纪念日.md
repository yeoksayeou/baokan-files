Title: Central Committee of the Youth League Decides: "May Fourth" to be the Anniversary of the Founding of the Youth League
Author:
Date: 1950-04-11
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, April 10th: The Standing Committee of the Central Committee of the China New Democratic Youth League has recently decided to designate May 4th, Chinese Youth Day, as the anniversary of the founding of the Youth League. The "May Fourth" Movement was the beginning of the Chinese New Democratic Revolution and also the Chinese revolutionary youth movement. The Central Committee of the Youth League designating "May Fourth" as the anniversary of the founding of the Youth League has the significance of enabling the Youth League to inherit and carry forward the glorious revolutionary tradition of Chinese youth since the "May Fourth" Movement.

### 青年团中央决定  “五四”为青年团成立纪念日

1950-04-11
第1版()
专栏：

　　青年团中央决定
    “五四”为青年团成立纪念日
    【新华社十日讯】中国新民主主义青年团中央委员会常委会，顷决定以五月四日中国青年节同时做为青年团成立纪念日。按“五四”运动是中国新民主主义革命也是中国革命青年运动的开端，青年团中央将“五四”定为青年团成立纪念日，具有使青年团承继和发扬“五四”以来中国青年光荣革命传统的意义。
