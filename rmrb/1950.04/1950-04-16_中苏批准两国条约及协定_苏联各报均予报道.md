Title: China and the Soviet Union Approve Treaties and Agreements Between the Two Countries; All Soviet Newspapers Report
Author:
Date: 1950-04-16
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, 14th: TASS Moscow reported: All newspapers on the 13th reported the news that the Presidium of the Supreme Soviet of the Soviet Union and the Central People's Government Committee of China had approved the Treaty of Friendship, Alliance and Mutual Assistance and agreements between the Soviet Union and the People's Republic of China.

### 中苏批准两国条约及协定  苏联各报均予报道

1950-04-16
第1版()
专栏：

　　中苏批准两国条约及协定  苏联各报均予报道
    【新华社十四日讯】塔斯社莫斯科讯：一切报纸都在十三日报道苏联最高苏维埃主席团和中国中央人民政府委员会已批准苏联和中华人民共和国的友好同盟互助条约及协定的消息。
