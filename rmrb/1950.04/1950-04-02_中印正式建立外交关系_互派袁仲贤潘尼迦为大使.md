Title: China and India Officially Establish Diplomatic Relations; Yuan Zhongxian and Panikkar Appointed as Ambassadors

Author:

Date: 1950-04-02
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, 1st of April:
　　China and India Officially Establish Diplomatic Relations
    Mutual Appointment of Yuan Zhongxian and Panikkar as Ambassadors

    The Indian government announced on December 31st of last year its willingness to enter into diplomatic relations with the People's Republic of China. Our Minister of Foreign Affairs, Zhou Enlai, replied to Minister of External Affairs of the Indian government, Nehru, on January 4th of this year, expressing the same desire and requesting the Indian government to send representatives to Beijing to negotiate the preliminary and procedural matters of establishing diplomatic relations between China and India. The Indian government's representative, Chargé d'affaires ad interim Y. K. Yong sent arrived in Beijing on February 13th and immediately began these negotiations. The negotiations have now successfully concluded. The Chinese and Indian governments have agreed that the Indian government will appoint the former Indian Ambassador to the Nationalist government, Panikkar, as the Ambassador to the People's Republic of China, and the Central People's Government of the People's Republic of China will appoint General Yuan Zhongxian as the Ambassador of the People's Republic of China to India.

### 中印正式建立外交关系  互派袁仲贤潘尼迦为大使

1950-04-02
第1版()
专栏：

　　中印正式建立外交关系
    互派袁仲贤潘尼迦为大使
    【新华社一日讯】印度政府于去年十二月三十一日宣布愿与中华人民共和国进入外交关系。我外交部周恩来部长当于本年一月四日电复印度政府外交部部长尼赫鲁，表示同样愿望，并请印度政府派遣代表前来北京，就中印两国建立外交关系的初步与程序问题进行谈判。印度政府派遣的谈判代表雁谒森临时代办，于二月十三日到达北京，随即开始进行此项谈判。现谈判已顺利结束。中印两国政府并已同意：印度政府派遣前印度驻国民党政府大使潘尼迦为驻中华人民共和国大使，中华人民共和国中央人民政府派遣袁仲贤将军为中华人民共和国驻印度大使。
