Title: Progressive American Public Figures Initiate Peace Conference, Calling for US-Soviet Negotiations to Ban Atomic Bombs

Author: 
Date: 1950-04-09
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, New York, 7th TASS - Two hundred and fifty prominent American educators, scientists, labor union workers, writers, pastors, and public figures have issued a declaration calling for a "Mid-Century Peace Conference" to be held in Chicago on May 29th. The declaration states: "We reject the philosophy of those who proclaim war inevitable, and we affirm that peace is possible. We must seek peaceful alternatives to the 'Cold War.' We firmly believe that the social systems of the United States and the Soviet Union can coexist peacefully. Therefore, we urge the President and the United States Congress to negotiate with the Soviet government to find ways to ban the atomic bomb, as the first step towards disarmament and peaceful resolution of all outstanding differences and the eventual restoration of commerce."

Reverend Thompson, Vice-Rector of Rockefeller Chapel at the University of Chicago, said that the conference would be initiated by the "Committee of Advocates for a Path to Peace," whose honorary chairmen include Balch, winner of the 1946 Nobel Peace Prize, writer Thomas Mann, and the prominent Black pastor, Woolsey.

### 美国进步社会名流发起召开和平大会  呼吁美苏谈判，设法禁用原子弹

1950-04-09
第4版()
专栏：

　　美国进步社会名流发起召开和平大会
    呼吁美苏谈判，设法禁用原子弹
    【新华社八日讯】塔斯社纽约七日讯，美国著名的教育家、科学家、工会工作者、作家、牧师和社会名流二百五十人发表宣言，要求于五月二十九日在芝加哥召开“世纪中叶和平大会”。宣言称：“我们摈斥那些宣扬战争不可避免的人的哲学，我们确认和平是可能的。我们必须觅致代替“冷战”的和平途径。我们坚信美国的社会制度和苏联的社会制度是能和平共存的。因此我们吁请总统和美国国会与苏联政府进行谈判，以设法禁用原子弹，作为走向裁军和和平解决一切未决的分歧与最后恢复通商的第一步。”
    “芝加哥大学洛克菲勒教堂”副主教汤普逊神父说，这次大会将由“和平道路拥护者委员会”发起，该委员会的名誉主席有一九四六年诺贝尔和平奖金获得者巴尔区，作家托玛斯·曼和著名的黑人神父伍尔什。
