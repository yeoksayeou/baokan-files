Title: Scottish Trades Union Congress Resolution Demands Ban on Atomic Weapons
Author:
Date: 1950-04-25
Page: 4

　　Xinhua News Agency, Scotland, 24th: According to a Reuters Scottish dispatch: Nearly 400 delegates, representing 726,420 trade union members in Scotland, unanimously demanded on the 19th that the British government should strive to prohibit the manufacture of hydrogen bombs and all atomic weapons. Delegates attending the 53rd annual meeting of the Scottish Trades Union Congress passed a resolution urging the British government to appeal to the United Nations to reach an international agreement banning hydrogen bombs and atomic weapons, as well as for international control of atomic energy resources.

### 苏格兰职工大会决议  要求禁止原子武器

1950-04-25
第4版()
专栏：

　　苏格兰职工大会决议
    要求禁止原子武器
    【新华社二十四日讯】据路透社苏格兰消息：代表苏格兰七十二万六千四百二十个工会会员的将近四百名代表，于十九日一致要求：英国政府应为禁止氢弹及所有原子武器的制造而努力。参加苏格兰职工大会五十三届年会的代表们通过决议，敦促英国政府吁请联合国获致禁止氢弹与原子武器以及国际管制原子能资源的国际协定。
