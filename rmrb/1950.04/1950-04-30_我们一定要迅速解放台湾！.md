Title: We Must Swiftly Liberate Taiwan!
Author: Guo Xixiang
Date: 1950-04-30
Page: 6

　　We Must Swiftly Liberate Taiwan!
　　Reader Guo Xixiang wrote to the Fourth Field Army saying that the liberation of Hainan Island corrected his mistaken belief that our army was incapable of cross-sea operations.
　　　　Dear Fourth Field Army of the People's Liberation Army:
　　I was extremely happy to read the good news in the April 21st Daily about our army's successful landing on Hainan Island. Your heroic action of crossing a hundred-mile sea and successfully landing on Hainan Island is a heavy blow to the American imperialists and the remnant Kuomintang bandits, shattering the Kuomintang bandits' illusion of relying on the ocean to catch their breath and stage a comeback. In the battle, you quickly landed on the islands amidst the enemy's so-called "three-dimensional sea, land, and air defense" and annihilated the enemy forces. This proves the bankruptcy of all the Kuomintang bandit army's deceptive propaganda!
　　Your cross-sea landing has a great impact internationally and domestically, and it has also given me a great ideological education.
　　In the past, I had a wrong idea in my mind, thinking that our army had very little experience in naval and air combat during the twenty-odd years of revolutionary war, while the Kuomintang bandits occupied Taiwan and Hainan Island overseas, and had airplanes, cannons, and warships given to them by American imperialism. Therefore, I often had wrong worries in my mind about our army's task of liberating Taiwan and liberating all of China.
　　Now, our beloved People's Liberation Army, under the wise leadership of Chairman Mao, has not only comprehensively liberated the mainland of the country, but also quickly crossed the ocean and successfully landed on Hainan Island. This corrected my wrong thinking, proving that the sea water cannot stop our army, and the Kuomintang's airplanes, warships, and cannons cannot stop us from liberating Hainan Island and Taiwan. We must swiftly liberate Taiwan and liberate all of China under the banner of Chairman Mao.
　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　Guo Xixiang

### 我们一定要迅速解放台湾！
郭喜祥
1950-04-30
第6版()
专栏：

　　我们一定要迅速解放台湾！
    读者郭喜祥写信给第四野战军说：海南岛的解放，纠正了他认为我军不能渡海作战的错误认识。
            亲爱的人民解放军第四野战军：
    看了四月二十一号日报上我军胜利登陆海南岛的好消息非常高兴。你们横跨百里海面，胜利登陆海南、这一空前而英勇的行动，对美帝国主义和残余的国民党匪帮们是一个沉重的打击，粉碎了国民党匪帮们企图依靠海洋从事喘息，卷土重来的幻想。在战斗中你们在敌军所谓“海陆空立体防御”的层层火网中，迅速的登陆各岛，歼灭敌军，这证明了国民党匪军的一切欺骗宣传的破产！
    你们的跨海登陆，在国际与国内有着很大的影响，对我个人的思想也给了一个很大的教育。
    过去，我思想里存在一个错误的想法，认为我军在二十余年的革命战争中，很少海空战斗的经验，而国民党匪帮盘踞海外台湾、海南岛，并有美帝国主义给他的飞机、大炮、军舰，因此，对我军解放台湾、解放全中国的任务，在思想上经常有错误的担忧。
    现在，我们亲爱的人民解放军，在英明领袖毛主席的领导下，不但全面的解放了全国大陆，而又迅速的渡过了海洋，胜利的登陆海南岛。这纠正了我的错误思想，证明了海水是阻挡不住我军的，国民党的飞机、军舰、大炮是不能够阻挡住我们解放海南岛及台湾的。我们一定要在毛主席的旗帜下迅速的解放台湾、解放全中国。
                郭喜祥
