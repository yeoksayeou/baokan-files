Title: Central Committee of the Communist Youth League Issues Slogans for Commemorating the May Fourth Youth Day
Author:
Date: 1950-04-20
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, April 19th. The Central Committee of the Chinese New Democratic Youth League has issued slogans for commemorating the "May Fourth" Youth Day. The full text is as follows:

　　Slogans for Commemorating the "May Fourth" Youth Day

　　One, Commemorate the 31st Anniversary of the "May Fourth" Movement.
　　Two, Celebrate Chinese Youth Day.
　　Three, Celebrate the First Anniversary of the Founding of the Chinese New Democratic Youth League.
　　Four, Pay high tribute to Chairman Mao Zedong, the great leader of the Chinese people and the great teacher of Chinese youth.
　　Five, Pay high tribute to Marshal Stalin, the teacher of the working people of the world.
　　Six, Support the People's Republic of China. Support the Central People's Government.
　　Seven, Pay tribute to the Communist Party of China.
　　Eight, Pay tribute to the Chinese People's Liberation Army.
　　Nine, Pay tribute to all democratic parties in the country.
　　Ten, Support the Sino-Soviet Alliance.
　　Eleven, Support lasting world peace.
　　Twelve, Liberate Tibet.
　　Thirteen, Liberate Taiwan.
　　Fourteen, Liberate Hainan Island.
　　Fifteen, Unite all young male and female workers, young peasants, and young intellectuals, support the people's democratic dictatorship, and strive to realize the Common Program of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference.
　　Sixteen, Young male and female workers across the country, cherish state-owned industries, actively engage in production, and launch labor emulation campaigns.
　　Seventeen, Young male and female workers across the country, intensify technical learning, and launch respect-teachers-love-apprentices movement.
　　Eighteen, Young male and female workers in private factories across the country, correctly implement the principle of both labor and capital benefiting, strive to improve production, and learn culture and technology.
　　Nineteen, Young male and female peasants across the country, support land reform, improve agricultural production, and grow more grain and more cotton.
　　Twenty, Young male and female peasants across the country should stand at the forefront of the cooperative movement.
　　Twenty-one, Young male and female peasants across the country, strive to launch literacy campaigns, eliminate illiteracy, and improve culture.
　　Twenty-two, Young commanders and fighters of the Chinese People's Liberation Army, fight to eliminate the remnant bandits and consolidate national defense.
　　Twenty-three, Young male and female students across the country, support the New Democratic education, strive to learn skills well, and build a new China.
　　Twenty-four, Pay tribute to the revolutionary cultural workers and progressive teachers.
　　Twenty-five, Young male and female staff members across the country, support the decrees of the Central People's Government, and strive to master their own business.
　　Twenty-six, Young male and female scientific workers across the country, strive to research, learn from the experience and new inventions of socialist countries, and fight to improve the scientific and technological level of our country.
　　Twenty-seven, Young male and female instructors of the National Young Pioneers, in order to cultivate the entire younger generation, better guide and help the work of the Young Pioneers.
　　Twenty-eight, Outstanding young men and women of all nationalities across the country, unite to consolidate the freedom and independence of our motherland and to establish a large family of friendship and cooperation among our nationalities.
　　Twenty-nine, Learn from the Soviet Union's Lenin Communist Youth League.
　　Thirty, Support the World Federation of Democratic Youth.
　　Thirty-one, Extend warm greetings to the democratic youth of all countries in the world.
　　Thirty-two, Unite the fighting youth of the Eastern nations, oppose imperialism, and fight for national independence and freedom.
　　Thirty-three, Outstanding young men and women across the country, strive to study Marxism-Leninism, study Mao Zedong Thought, and join the New Democratic Youth League.
　　Thirty-four, Outstanding young men and women across the country, hold high the banner of Mao Zedong, promote patriotism and internationalism, and fight for the victory of the New Democratic social construction.

### 青年团中央发出纪念五四青年节口号

1950-04-20
第1版()
专栏：

　　青年团中央发出纪念五四青年节口号
    【新华社十九日讯】中国新民主主义青年团中央委员会发出纪念“五四”青年节的口号，全文如下：
    纪念“五四”青年节口号
    一、纪念“五四”运动三十一周年。
    二、庆祝中国青年节。
    三、庆祝中国新民主主义青年团成立一周年。
    四、向中国人民的伟大领袖，中国青年的伟大导师毛泽东主席致崇高敬礼。
    五、向全世界劳动人民的导师斯大林大元帅致崇高敬礼。
    六、拥护中华人民共和国。拥护中央人民政府。
    七、向中国共产党致敬。
    八、向中国人民解放军致敬。
    九、向全国各民主党派致敬。
    一○、拥护中苏同盟。
    一一、拥护世界持久和平。
    一二、解放西藏。
    一三、解放台湾。
    一四、解放海南岛。
    一五、全国男女青年工人、青年农民、青年知识分子团结起来，拥护人民民主专政，为实现人民政协共同纲领而奋斗。
    一六、全国男女青年工人，爱护国营工业，积极生产，展开劳动竞赛。
    一七、全国男女青年工人，加紧学习技术，展开尊师爱徒运动。
    一八、全国私营工厂男女青年工人，正确实行劳资两利原则，努力提高生产，学习文化和技术。
    一九、全国男女青年农民，拥护土地改革，提高农业生产，多种粮食，多种棉花。
    二○、全国男女青年农民要站在合作运动的最前线。
    二一、全国男女青年农民，努力展开识字运动，扫除文盲，提高文化。
    二二、中国人民解放军青年指战员，为消灭残匪、巩固国防而斗争。
    二三、全国男女青年学生，拥护新民主主义教育，努力学好本领，建设新中国。
    二四、向革命的文化工作者和进步教师们致敬。
    二五、全国男女青年职员，拥护中央人民政府法令，努力精通自己的业务。
    二六、全国男女青年科学工作者，努力研究，学习社会主义国家的经验与新发明，为提高我们祖国的科学与技术水准而斗争。
    二七、全国少年儿童队男女青年辅导员同志们，为了培养整个青年一代，更好地指导和帮助少年儿童队的工作。
    二八、全国各民族的优秀男女青年团结起来，为巩固我们祖国的自由与独立，为建立我们各民族友爱合作的大家庭而斗争。
    二九、向苏联列宁共产主义青年团学习。
    三○、拥护世界民主青年联盟。
    三一、向世界各国民主青年致热烈敬礼。
    三二、东方各民族战斗青年团结起来，反对帝国主义，为争取民族独立与自由而斗争。
    三三、全国优秀男女青年，努力学习马列主义，学习毛泽东思想，参加新民主主义青年团。
    三四、全国优秀男女青年，高举毛泽东的旗帜，发扬爱国主义与国际主义精神，为新民主主义社会建设的胜利而斗争。
