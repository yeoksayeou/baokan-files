Title: French and British Workers' Delegations Arrive in the Soviet Union to Attend May Day Celebrations
Author:
Date: 1950-04-29
Page: 4

　　Invited to Attend May Day Celebrations
    French and British Workers' Delegations Arrive in the Soviet Union
    [Xinhua News Agency, 28th] According to TASS news: A delegation from the French General Confederation of Labor, at the invitation of the All-Union Central Council of Trade Unions, has arrived in Moscow to participate in the May Day celebrations. The delegation is headed by Ducha, Secretary of the French General Confederation of Labor.
    The British-Soviet Friendship Society announced that they have formed a British workers' delegation to go to the Soviet Union to participate in the May Day celebrations. The representatives represent the most important sectors of British industry. The British-Soviet Friendship Society issued a statement saying that once the British delegation returns, "British-Soviet Friendship Month" will begin, during which special gatherings, film screenings and exhibitions will be arranged, and signatures will be collected for friendship letters to be sent to various Soviet towns.

### 应邀参加五一节庆祝会  法英工人代表团抵苏联

1950-04-29
第4版()
专栏：

　　应邀参加五一节庆祝会
    法英工人代表团抵苏联
    【新华社二十八日讯】据塔斯社消息：法国总工会代表团，应全苏工会中央理事会之邀，已抵达莫斯科，参加五一节的庆祝会。代表团以法国总工会的书记杜沙为首。
    英苏友好协会宣布：他们已组成英国工人代表团到苏联去参加庆祝五一劳动节。代表们代表英国工业最重要的各部门。英苏友好协会发表声明说：一当英国代表团回来后，“英苏友好月”就将开始，在那时期，将布置一些特别集会、电影放映和展览会，并将收集致送苏联各城镇的友谊信的签名。
