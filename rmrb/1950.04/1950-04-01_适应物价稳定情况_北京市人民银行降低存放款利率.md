Title: Adapting to Stable Prices: Beijing People's Bank Lowers Deposit and Loan Interest Rates
Author:
Date: 1950-04-01
Page: 4

　　Adapting to Stable Prices
    Beijing People's Bank Lowers Deposit and Loan Interest Rates
    [Beijing News Agency Dispatch] Recently in Beijing, due to stable prices and a stable market, the amount of bank deposits has significantly increased. In order to ensure that deposit and loan interest rates do not exceed normal industrial and commercial profits, and to facilitate the development of production, the Beijing Branch of the People's Bank of China has decided to lower the bank's deposit and loan interest rates starting today (April 1st). It is stipulated that the monthly interest rate for current deposits will be reduced from 3.6 to 4.2 per mille to 0.5 to 0.3 per mille; for fixed deposits, the monthly interest rate for half a month to six months will be reduced from 6 to 15 per mille to 0.27 to 0.6 per mille; it is stipulated that the monthly interest rate for loans will be reduced from 10.5 to 18 per mille to 0.72 to 1.05 per mille.

### 适应物价稳定情况  北京市人民银行降低存放款利率

1950-04-01
第4版()
专栏：

　　适应物价稳定情况
    北京市人民银行降低存放款利率
    【京市新闻处讯】京市近来由于物价平疲，市场稳定，银行存款数量显著增加。中国人民银行北京分行为使存放款利率不超过正常工商业利润，以利于发展生产，特决定自今（四月一日）日起降低该行存放款利率。规定活期存款月息由三分六厘至四分二厘降低为由五厘至三分；定期存款，半月到六月月息由六分至十五分降低为由二分七厘至六分；规定放款月息由十分五厘至十八分降低为由七分二厘至十分五厘。
