Title: Beijing News Office Correction
Author: 
Date: 1950-04-27
Page: 4

　　Beijing News Office Correction
    In the news item released by this office on the 25th regarding suburban farmers catching and killing pests (published on page 4 of this newspaper on the 26th), the statement that the 15th district established a pest control headquarters was a mistake. It should have been the 19th district.
