Title: To Facilitate Public Enterprise Cash Deposits, Municipal People's Bank Extends Office Hours
Author:
Date: 1950-04-24
Page: 4

　　[Beijing News Agency Dispatch] To realize cash management and ensure that the Central Treasury and the treasury funds of various public enterprises are deposited in a timely manner, the Beijing branch of the People's Bank of China has extended its office hours. Effective immediately, the bank and its various departments and offices (excluding sub-offices) will be open daily from 7:30 AM to 10:00 PM (including Sundays) to handle the payment of various treasury funds and deposits for various government agencies and public enterprises. At the same time, to save time, a mutual credit packaging method will be implemented for the acceptance of large amounts of money paid by various agencies and enterprises. That is, the bulk amount will be accepted first based on the seal, and the detailed amount will be counted later. Discrepancies will be handled according to the method of refunding overpayments and supplementing underpayments.

### 为便利公营企业现金入库  市人民银行延长办公

1950-04-24
第4版()
专栏：

　　为便利公营企业现金入库
    市人民银行延长办公
    【京市新闻处讯】中国人民银行北京分行为实现现金管理，及使中央金库与各公营企业库款及时入库，顷延长办公时间。自即日起，该行及所属各部各办事处（分理处暂不在内）每日自上午七时半至下午十时（星期日在内），办理各机关、公营企业缴纳各种金库款及存款；同时为节省时间，对各机关、企业缴纳巨额款项之点收，实行相互信用封包办法，即凭封签先行点收大数，以后清点细数，不符时按长退短补办法办理。
