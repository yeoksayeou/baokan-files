Title: Commemoration of Mayakovsky: Memorial Meeting to Be Held in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1950-04-15
Page: 3

　　Commemoration of Mayakovsky
　　Memorial Meeting to Be Held in Beijing
　　[Report from this newspaper] To commemorate the 20th anniversary of the death of the brilliant Soviet poet Mayakovsky, the All-China Federation of Literary and Art Circles, the Beijing Sino-Soviet Friendship Association, and the Beijing Branch of the Soviet Society for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries will jointly hold a memorial meeting at 7:00 PM on Sunday, the 16th of this month, at No. 19 Nanheyan Street, Beijing, the location of the Beijing Branch of the Soviet Society for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries. The meeting will include reports, poetry recitations, films, and other programs. Those holding admission tickets to the memorial meeting are welcome to attend.
　　　　　　　　　　　　　　 (Beijing Sino-Soviet Friendship Association)

### 纪念马雅柯夫斯基  京将举行纪念会

1950-04-15
第3版()
专栏：

　　纪念马雅柯夫斯基
    京将举行纪念会
    【本报讯】中华全国文学艺术界联合会、北京苏联对外文化协会、北京市中苏友好协会为纪念苏联天才诗人马雅柯夫斯基逝世二十周年，定于十六日（本星期日）下午七时在南河沿十九号北京苏联对外文化协会联合举行纪念会。会中有报告、诗朗诵、电影等节目，凡持有纪念会入场券者可迳往参加。
                              （北京市中苏友好协会）
