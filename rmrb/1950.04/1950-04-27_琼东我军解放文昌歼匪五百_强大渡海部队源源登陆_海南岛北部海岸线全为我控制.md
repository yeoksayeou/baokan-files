Title: Qiong Eastern Our Army Liberates Wenchang, Annihilates 500 Bandits; Powerful Cross-Sea Troops Land Continuously; Northern Coastline of Hainan Island Entirely Under Our Control
Author:
Date: 1950-04-27
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Hainan Front, 26th – After liberating Haikou, the People's Liberation Army on the Hainan Front pursued the enemy fleeing south from the city, and occupied Wenchang City in Qiong Eastern at 5:00 PM on the 24th, annihilating more than 500 bandits. On the same day, the fourth wave of powerful PLA cross-sea troops landed continuously at the Tianwei Port, Rongshan Factory, and Huachang Port line west of Haikou. Thus, the entire northern coastline of Hainan Island, stretching over 400 li from east to west, is now under our control.

### 琼东我军解放文昌歼匪五百  强大渡海部队源源登陆  海南岛北部海岸线全为我控制

1950-04-27
第1版()
专栏：

　　琼东我军解放文昌歼匪五百
    强大渡海部队源源登陆
    海南岛北部海岸线全为我控制
    【新华社海南前线二十六日电】海南前线人民解放军在解放海口后，尾追由该市南逃之敌，于二十四日下午五时进占琼东的文昌城，歼匪五百余。同日，解放军的第四次强大渡海部队在海口以西的天尾港、荣山厂、花场港一线源源登陆。至此，海南岛北部东西长达四百余里之海岸线已全部为我控制。
