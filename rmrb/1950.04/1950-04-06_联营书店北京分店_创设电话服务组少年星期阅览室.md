Title: Joint Bookstore Beijing Branch Establishes Telephone Service Group and Children's Sunday Reading Room
Author:
Date: 1950-04-06
Page: 6

　　The Beijing branch of the Joint Bookstore Service Department has established a telephone service group and a Sunday children's reading room starting on the 1st. The purpose of the telephone service group is to facilitate readers' book purchases and answer various questions regarding book purchases. If factories, schools, government offices, and organizations in this city need to purchase books, please call the Second Bureau, 0904, and someone will be sent immediately to deliver the required books. The children's reading room is located at No. 1, Old Xingbu Street, and is open on Saturdays from 1:00 PM to 5:30 PM, and on Sundays from 9:30 AM to 11:30 AM and from 1:00 PM to 5:30 PM. Children are welcome to read there.

### 联营书店北京分店  创设电话服务组少年星期阅览室

1950-04-06
第6版()
专栏：

　　联营书店北京分店
    创设电话服务组  少年星期阅览室
    京市联营书店服务部于一日起，增设电话服务组及星期少年阅览室。电话服务组目的在便利读者购书及解答购书方面的各项问题。本市各工厂、学校、机关、团体如要购书请打电话二局○九○四，当即派人将所需之书送上。少年阅览室地点在旧刑部街一号，星期六下午一时至五时半、星期日上午九时半至十一时半、下午一时至五时半开放，欢迎少年儿童前往阅览。
