Title: List of Appointments by the Government Administration Council

Author:

Date: 1950-04-08
Page: 3

Xinhua News Agency, 7th: The 27th Government Affairs Conference of the Central People's Government Administration Council passed two lists of appointments as follows:

        I. Supplementary List of Staff of the Northwest Military and Political Committee

    Deputy Secretary-General of the Culture and Education Committee: Wang Juren (incumbent member of the Culture and Education Committee of the Northwest Military and Political Committee).

    Director of the Office: Wang Juren (incumbent member of the Culture and Education Committee of the Northwest Military and Political Committee).

    Secretary-General of the People's Supervision Committee: Tan Shengbin (incumbent member of the People's Supervision Committee of the Northwest Military and Political Committee).

        II. List of the Mayor of Xuzhou Municipal People's Government, Shandong Province

    Mayor: Zhang Guangzhong (incumbent Commander of the Xuzhou Garrison of Shandong Province).

### 政务院任命名单

1950-04-08
第3版()
专栏：

　　政务院任命名单
    【新华社七日讯】中央人民政府政务院第二十七次政务会议通过任命案两项名单如下：
            一、西北军政委员会工作人员补充名单
    文化教育委员会副秘书长  王菊人  现任西北军政委员会文化教育委员会委员。
    办公室主任  王菊人  现任西北军政委员会文化教育委员会委员。
    人民监察委员会秘书长  谭生彬  现任西北军政委员会人民监察委员会委员。
            二、山东省徐州市人民政府市长名单
    市长  张光中  现任山东省徐州市警备司令。
