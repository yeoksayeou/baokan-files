Title: People Across the Country Warmly Commemorate Lenin's Birthday
Author:
Date: 1950-04-24
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, April 23rd: People across the country warmly commemorated the 80th anniversary of the birth of the great revolutionary teacher Lenin yesterday in various ways. Shenyang launched a city-wide campaign to study Lenin, and the Northeast China-Soviet Friendship Association exhibited photographs of Lenin's life and deeds. Branches of the association held commemorative meetings or symposiums. The Lushun-Dalian China-Soviet Friendship Association held a thousand-person meeting on the evening of the 22nd, and invited Lieutenant Colonel Gozlov of the Soviet garrison to report on Lenin's life and career. Branches of the China-Soviet Friendship Association in various cities and counties held commemorative meetings and photo exhibitions of Lenin's life. Thirteen cinemas in the Lushun-Dalian area simultaneously screened films about Lenin's career. The library of the Dalian China-Soviet Friendship Association Club also organized a Lenin study group, absorbing more than 30 members from various branches, preparing them to return to their respective branches to conduct widespread dissemination. Members of the Jinzhou China-Soviet Friendship Association are studying Stalin's article "On Lenin" from the 22nd to the 27th. The Jinzhou China-Soviet Friendship Association also broadcast Lenin's life on the radio. The Shanghai China-Soviet Friendship Association held a grand exhibition of Lenin's portraits and photographs of Soviet women's lives and work. Commemorative meetings were also held in Baoding, Nanjing, Qingdao, Wuhu, Fuzhou, Changsha, Nanchang, Kaifeng, Xi'an, Lanzhou and other places. Newspapers and magazines published special issues or articles, cinemas screened films such as "Lenin's Life", and bookstores uniformly sold Lenin's works and various books and albums about Lenin at low prices.

### 全国各地人民热烈纪念列宁诞辰

1950-04-24
第1版()
专栏：

　　全国各地人民
    热烈纪念列宁诞辰
    【新华社二十三日讯】全国各地人民昨日以各种方式热烈纪念伟大革命导师列宁八十诞辰。沈阳全市展开了学习列宁的活动，东北中苏友好协会展览了列宁生平事迹照片，该会分支会纷纷举行了纪念会或座谈会。旅大中苏友好协会于二十二日晚间举行了千人的大会，敦请苏联驻军高兹罗夫中校报告列宁生平事业。各市县的中苏友好协会支会分别召开了纪念会，举办了列宁生平的照片展览。旅大区十三个电影院同时放映了有关列宁事业的影片。大连中苏友好协会俱乐部图书馆还组织了一个学习列宁小组，吸收各支分会的三十多名会员参加，准备由他们回到各支分会进行广泛的传达。锦州市中苏友协会员们从二十二日起到二十七日，学习斯大林著的《论列宁》一文。该市中苏友好协会并在广播台播讲列宁的一生。上海中苏友好协会举行了盛大的列宁画像及苏联妇女生活与工作照片展览会。保定、南京、青岛、芜湖、福州、长沙、南昌、开封、西安、兰州等地都举行了纪念会，报纸、杂志刊出了专刊或专文，电影院放映《列宁生平》等影片，书店一律廉价出售列宁所著和有关列宁的各种书籍、画册。
