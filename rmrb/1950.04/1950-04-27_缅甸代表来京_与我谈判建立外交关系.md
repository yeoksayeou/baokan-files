Title: Burmese Representative Arrives in Beijing to Negotiate Establishment of Diplomatic Relations with Us
Author:
Date: 1950-04-27
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, 26th: The Burmese government has dispatched U Pe Kin, designated as Chargé d'affaires ad interim, as a negotiating representative to negotiate with the Central People's Government of our country on the establishment of diplomatic relations. U Pe Kin arrived in Beijing at 7:55 this morning. Han Xu, section chief of the Protocol Department of our Ministry of Foreign Affairs, went to the station to meet him.

### 缅甸代表来京  与我谈判建立外交关系

1950-04-27
第1版()
专栏：

　　缅甸代表来京
    与我谈判建立外交关系
    【新华社二十六日讯】为与我国中央人民政府就建立外交关系问题进行谈判，缅甸政府已派遣被指定为临时代办的吴辟为谈判代表。吴辟业于今晨七时五十五分抵京，我外交部派交际处科长韩叙赴站迎接。
