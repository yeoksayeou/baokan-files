Title: "How Should Industry and Commerce Operate in the New Situation?" Zhang Naiqi to Broadcast on Central People's Broadcasting Station Tonight

Author:

Date: 1950-04-28
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, April 27th:  Zhang Naiqi, Political Commissar of the Government Administration Council and economist, has been invited to give a broadcast speech on the Central People's Broadcasting Station at 8:00 PM on April 28th. The title of the speech is "How Should Industry and Commerce Operate in the New Situation?". The program originally scheduled for 8:00 PM on the station will be broadcast at a later date.

### “新形势下工商业怎样办？”  章乃器今晚将在中央人民广播电台播讲

1950-04-28
第4版()
专栏：

　　“新形势下工商业怎样办？”
    章乃器今晚将在中央人民广播电台播讲
    【新华社二十七日讯】政务院政务委员、经济学家章乃器应邀于二十八日下午八时在中央人民广播电台广播演讲，讲题是“在新形势下工商业应该怎样办？”该台原定八时播送的节目改期播送。
