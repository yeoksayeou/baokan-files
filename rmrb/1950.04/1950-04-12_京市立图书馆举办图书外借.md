Title: Beijing Municipal Library Offers Book Lending Service
Author:
Date: 1950-04-12
Page: 4

　　Beijing Municipal Library Offers Book Lending Service
    [This Newspaper's Dispatch] To broadly meet the reading needs of citizens, the Beijing Municipal Library, in addition to establishing mobile library stations on the Beijing-Shanghai train and in fourteen trade unions in the city, has recently started a book lending service. The library has prepared over 700 kinds of new cultural books in social sciences and literature. Any citizen of the city with a reliable guarantor can borrow books from the reading department of the library at 22 Toufahu Tong, Xuanwumen Inner Street.

### 京市立图书馆举办图书外借

1950-04-12
第4版()
专栏：

　　京市立图书馆举办图书外借
    【本报讯】北京市市立图书馆为广泛供应市民阅览的需要，除已于京沪车及本市十四个职工会设立图书巡回站外，最近又举办图书外借。该馆已准备社会科学与文学等类新文化书籍七百多种，凡本市市民有可靠保证者，均可向宣武门内头发胡同二十二号该馆阅览部索借书籍。
