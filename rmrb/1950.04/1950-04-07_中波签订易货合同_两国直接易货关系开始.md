Title: China and Poland Sign Barter Contracts, Direct Barter Relationship Begins Between the Two Countries
Author:
Date: 1950-04-07
Page: 1

　　China and Poland Sign Barter Contracts
    Direct Barter Relationship Begins Between the Two Countries
    [Xinhua News Agency, 6th] In January of this year, the Ministry of Foreign Trade of the Republic of Poland dispatched a trade delegation headed by Mr. Strożan to China to conduct negotiations with our Ministry of Trade on establishing trade relations. After two months of consultations, both parties signed various barter contracts last month. In addition, the Ministries of Trade of China and Poland signed a barter agreement between the two countries pending the conclusion of a trade agreement, signed by Lin Haiyun, Director of the Foreign Trade Department of the Ministry of Trade of the Central People's Government, and Mr. Strożan, head of the Polish Foreign Trade Delegation. This marks the beginning of a direct barter relationship between China and Poland. Mr. Strożan, head of the Polish trade delegation, and others have left China and returned home after completing their mission.

### 中波签订易货合同  两国直接易货关系开始

1950-04-07
第1版()
专栏：

　　中波签订易货合同
    两国直接易货关系开始
    【新华社六日讯】本年一月间，波兰共和国对外贸易部曾派遣以斯特罗赞先生为首的贸易代表团来华，与我贸易部进行建立贸易关系的谈判。经过两个月的磋商，双方已于上月签订了各项易货合同。此外，中波两国贸易部间并已签订了一项两国在缔结贸易协定前的易货协议书，由我中央人民政府贸易部国外贸易司司长林海云及波兰对外贸易部代表团团长斯特罗赞先生代表签字。由此中波两国间的直接易货关系已告开始。波兰贸易代表团团长斯特罗赞先生等，在完成任务之后已离华返国。
