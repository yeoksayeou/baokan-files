Title: Lenin Central Museum Collects Priceless Documents and Materials to Help the Broad Masses of Working People Grasp the Great Thought of Leninism.
Author:
Date: 1950-04-25
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, 19th (TASS dispatch):  Neznanov, Director of the Lenin Central Museum, wrote in the *Pravda* that the Soviet people and all progressive humanity are commemorating the 80th anniversary of Lenin's birth in an atmosphere of continuous victories for the socialist and democratic camp.  Inspired by Leninism, hundreds of millions of people around the world are participating in the struggle for peace, democracy, and socialism.

　　The Lenin Central Museum, which has collected the most precious documents and materials, helps the broad masses of working people grasp the great thought of Leninism. The museum exhibits more than 7,000 items, including rare manuscripts of Lenin's works, Lenin's personal belongings, rare photographs, and the most precious oil paintings, sculptures, and paintings depicting his solemn remains.

　　The exhibits explain how Lenin and Stalin overcame all difficulties and obstacles, creating a new type of party—the Leninist party—which led the Soviet people to the epoch-making victory of socialism, and set an example for the fraternal Communist parties and the parties of the working class.

　　The Lenin Central Museum is extremely popular with the people.

　　No other museum in the world attracts so many visitors as this museum. Since its opening on May 15, 1936, up to April 1, 1950, the number of visitors to the museum has reached 9,675,523, with over 800,000 visitors in 1949.

　　Many inscriptions in the visitors' opinion book reveal lofty emotions. People of all ages and from all walks of life, before leaving the museum, pledge to follow Lenin's example in everything and to prove their love and loyalty to the socialist motherland with their labor achievements.

　　Lenin's great deeds and thoughts have become the banner for all people who are fighting for lasting peace, national independence, people's democracy and socialism, and against imperialism. Foreign delegations visiting Moscow usually come to visit the Lenin Central Museum. In 1948-1949, nearly 15,000 foreign guests visited the museum. Many of our foreign friends inscribed in the opinion book, expressing their deep respect and love for Lenin.

### 列宁中央博物馆  搜集了最珍贵的文献和材料，帮助广大劳动人民掌握列宁主义伟大思想。

1950-04-25
第4版()
专栏：

　　列宁中央博物馆
    搜集了最珍贵的文献和材料，帮助广大劳动人民掌握列宁主义伟大思想。
    【新华社讯】塔斯社莫斯科十九日电：列宁中央博物馆馆长涅兹纳诺夫在真理报上著文称：苏联人民以及全世界进步人类，都在社会主义和民主阵营连续获得胜利的气氛中纪念列宁八十周年诞辰。全世界亿万人民受着列宁主义思想的鼓舞，参加了为和平、民主和社会主义而进行的斗争。
    收集了最珍贵的文献和材料的列宁中央博物馆，帮助广大的劳动群众掌握列宁主义的伟大思想。博物馆展览了七千多件陈列品，其中包括列宁著述的手稿珍本，列宁的私人遗物，稀有的照片，描绘他庄严遗容的最珍贵的油画，雕刻和绘画。
    展览品说明列宁和斯大林怎样战胜了一切困难和障碍，创造了新型的党——列宁主义的党，它曾将苏联人民带向社会主义划时代的胜利，它给各兄弟共产党和工人阶级的政党树立了榜样。
    列宁中央博物馆受到人民极大的欢迎。
    世界上再没有任何博物馆像这个博物馆那样吸引着这么多的观众。从该馆一九三六年五月十五日开幕以来，到一九五○年四月一日，参观该馆的人数竟达九百六十七万五千五百二十三人，一九四九年参观人数超过八十万。
    参观者意见簿上的许多题词，都流露着崇高的感情。各种年龄、各行各业的人，都在离开博物馆以前，保证事事效法列宁的榜样，并且保证以劳动业绩来证明他们对社会主义祖国的热爱和忠诚。
    列宁伟大的行为和思想，已经成了一切为持久和平、为民族独立、为人民民主和社会主义、为反对帝国主义而斗争的人民的旗帜。访问莫斯科的外国代表团，照例都来列宁中央博物馆参观。一九四八——一九四九年，外国来宾到该馆参观的将及一万五千人。我们许多外国友人在意见簿上题字，表示他们对列宁深挚的崇敬和爱戴。
