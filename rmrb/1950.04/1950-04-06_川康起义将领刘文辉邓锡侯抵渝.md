Title:  Generals Liu Wenhui and Deng Xihou, Leaders of the Sichuan-Kang Rebellion, Arrive in Chongqing
Author: 
Date: 1950-04-06
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Chongqing, 5th. On the evening of the 3rd of this month, the Southwest Military and Political Committee, the Southwest Bureau of the CPC Central Committee, the Southwest Military Region of the People's Liberation Army, the Chongqing Military Control Commission, and over a thousand representatives from various democratic parties and all sectors of society held a grand evening party to welcome Generals Liu Wenhui and Deng Xihou, leaders of the Sichuan-Kang Rebellion, who arrived in Chongqing from Chengdu on the 31st of last month. After Chairman Liu Bocheng delivered a brief welcoming speech, Generals Liu Wenhui and Deng Xihou spoke successively, expressing their heartfelt support for the various policies of the People's Government and the leadership of the Chinese Communist Party.

### 川康起义将领刘文辉邓锡侯抵渝

1950-04-06
第1版()
专栏：

　　川康起义将领刘文辉邓锡侯抵渝
    【新华社重庆五日电】本月三日晚，西南军政委员会、中共中央西南局、人民解放军西南军区、重庆市军管会及各民主党派、各界代表千余人，举行盛大晚会欢迎上月三十一日由成都来重庆的川康起义将领刘文辉、邓锡侯两将军。在刘伯承主席致简短欢迎词后，刘文辉、邓锡侯两将军相继讲话，表示衷心拥护人民政府的各项政策和中国共产党的领导。
