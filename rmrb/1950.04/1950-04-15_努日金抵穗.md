Title: Nuzhdin Arrives in Guangzhou

Author:
Date: 1950-04-15
Page: 3

　　Xinhua News Agency, Guangzhou, April 13th: Professor Nuzhdin, Doctor of Biology from the Soviet Union, arrived here on the 12th. Professor Nuzhdin was invited to Guangzhou to give lectures by the Guangzhou Branch of the Sino-Soviet Friendship Association, Zhongshan University, Southern University, and the South China People's Literature and Art Institute. Li Zhangda, Vice Chairman of the Guangdong Provincial People's Government and Vice President of the Guangzhou Branch of the Sino-Soviet Friendship Association, Xiao Xiangrong, Minister of the Propaganda Department of the South China Bureau of the CPC Central Committee, Deputy Minister Li Fanfu, Zhu Guang, Deputy Mayor of the Guangzhou Municipal People's Government, Du Guoxiang, Director of the Guangdong Provincial Department of Culture and Education and Vice President of the Guangzhou Branch of the Sino-Soviet Friendship Association, Ouyang Shan, Director-General of the Guangzhou Branch of the Sino-Soviet Friendship Association and Director of the Preparatory Committee of the South China Federation of Literary and Art Circles, Chen Weishi and Luo Ming, Vice Presidents of Southern University, Liu Qu and Ding Ying, Professors of Zhongshan University, Wang Li and Li Peiwen, Professors of Lingnan University, and representatives from various agencies and organizations all went to the station to welcome him.

### 努日金抵穗

1950-04-15
第3版()
专栏：

　　努日金抵穗
    【新华社广州十三日电】苏联生物学博士努日金教授已于十二日抵达此间。努日金教授系应中苏友好协会广州分会、中山大学、南方大学、华南人民文学艺术学院的联合邀请来广州讲学。广东省人民政府副主席兼中苏友好协会广州分会副会长李章达，中共中央华南分局宣传部长萧向荣、副部长李凡夫，广州市人民政府副市长朱光，广东省文教厅厅长兼中苏友好协会广州分会副会长杜国庠，中苏友好协会广州分会总干事兼华南文联筹委会主任欧阳山，南方大学副校长陈唯实、罗明，中山大学教授刘渠、丁颖，岭南大学教授王力、李佩文，以及各机关、团体代表均至车站欢迎。
