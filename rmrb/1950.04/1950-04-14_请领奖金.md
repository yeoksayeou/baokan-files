Title: Please Claim Your Reward

Author:

Date: 1950-04-14
Page: 6

　　Please Claim Your Reward
　　On March 3rd, our bureau received a phone call from Comrade Zhang of Pig Market Street, which led to the investigation and handling of a violation case. According to regulations, Comrade Zhang is entitled to a secret report reward of over 30,000 yuan. Comrade Zhang is requested to bring their name seal to the First Tax Bureau before April 15th to collect the reward.

### 请领奖金

1950-04-14
第6版()
专栏：

　　请领奖金
    我局于三月三日接到猪市大街张同志的电话报告，因此查获了一个违章案件，业经处理完毕，依法应给张同志密报奖金三万余元，请张同志持具名章于四月十五日前来税务第一分局领取为荷。
