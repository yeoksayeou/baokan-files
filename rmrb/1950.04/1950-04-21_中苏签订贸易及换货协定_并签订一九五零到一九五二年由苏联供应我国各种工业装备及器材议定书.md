Title: China and the Soviet Union Sign Trade and Exchange of Goods Agreement; Protocol Signed on the Soviet Union Supplying China with Various Industrial Equipment and Materials from 1950 to 1952
Author:
Date: 1950-04-21
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, 20th:
As a result of negotiations between the Trade Delegation of the People's Republic of China and the Ministry of Foreign Trade of the Soviet Union, conducted in a friendly and mutually understanding atmosphere, a trade agreement and an agreement on the exchange of goods for 1950 were signed in Moscow this April. According to the agreement on the exchange of goods, the Soviet Union will export various types of industrial equipment, and China will export various raw materials. At the same time, a protocol was also signed on the Soviet Union supplying the People's Republic of China with various industrial equipment and materials from 1950 to 1952. This equipment and materials will be calculated from the loan stipulated in the agreement of February 14, 1950. The signatories to the agreement and protocol were Ye Jizhuang, Minister of Trade of the Central People's Government, for the Chinese side, and M.A. Menshikov, Minister of Foreign Trade, for the Soviet side.

### 中苏签订贸易及换货协定  并签订一九五零到一九五二年由苏联供应我国各种工业装备及器材议定书

1950-04-21
第1版()
专栏：

　　中苏签订贸易及换货协定
    并签订一九五零到一九五二年由苏联供应我国各种工业装备及器材议定书
    【新华社二十日讯】中华人民共和国贸易代表团与苏联对外贸易部之间在友好的相互了解的气氛中进行谈判的结果，已于本年四月间在莫斯科签订一九五○年的贸易协定及交换货物的协定。根据交换货物的协定，苏联将输出各种工业装备，中国将输出各种原料。同时并签订了一九五○年到一九五二年由苏联供应中华人民共和国各种工业装备及器材之议定书。此项装备及器材将由一九五○年二月十四日协定的贷款中计算之。签订协定及议定书者，中国方面为中央人民政府贸易部长叶季壮，苏联方面为对外贸易部长墨·安·孟希科夫。
