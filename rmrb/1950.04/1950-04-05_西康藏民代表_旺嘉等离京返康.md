Title: Xikang Tibetan Representatives Wang Jia and Others Leave Beijing to Return to Xikang
Author:
Date: 1950-04-05
Page: 3

　　Xikang Tibetan Representatives
　　Wang Jia and Others Leave Beijing to Return to Xikang
    [Xinhua News Agency, 3rd] Xikang Tibetan representatives Wang Jia, Bo Zhi, Cai Liang and others, who came to Beijing to pay their respects to the leaders of the Central People's Government, have left Beijing to return to Xikang after meeting with the central leaders. Before their departure, the three representatives made a statement, unanimously stating that they were deeply grateful for the warm reception and care they received from various departments of the Central People's Government during their forty-plus days in the capital. The three representatives also stated that after returning to Xikang, they would universally inform the people of Xikang about the Central People's Government's ethnic policies and its concern for minority ethnic groups.

### 西康藏民代表  旺嘉等离京返康

1950-04-05
第3版()
专栏：

　　西康藏民代表
    旺嘉等离京返康
    【新华社三日讯】来京向中央人民政府首长致敬的西康藏民代表旺嘉、伯志、蔡良等三人，于晋谒中央首长后，已离京返康。三代表在临行前发表谈话，一致认为：在首都四十余天中，受到中央人民政府各机关的热烈招待与关怀，深为感激。三代表并表示：回康后一定把中央人民政府的民族政策及对少数民族的关切，普遍地告诉西康人民。
