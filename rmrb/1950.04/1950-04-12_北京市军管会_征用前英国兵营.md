Title: Beijing Military Control Commission Requisitions Former British Barracks
Author: 
Date: 1950-04-12
Page: 1

　　Beijing Military Control Commission
    Requisitions Former British Barracks
    (Xinhua News Agency, 11th) The Beijing Military Control Commission of the Chinese People's Liberation Army issued a proclamation on January 6th, requisitioning foreign barracks. According to this proclamation, the former American, French, and Dutch barracks have been requisitioned. On the 4th of this month, the Military Control Commission issued an order to the former British consul, requisitioning the former British barracks, and instructed him to appoint someone to hand over the former British barracks at No. 1 Xingguo Road, Dongjiaomin Lane, within one week. This morning at 9:30 AM, the Military Control Commission sent personnel to take over, and the takeover was completed by 1:00 PM. The former British barracks occupies approximately 60 mu of land, with approximately 800 rooms.

### 北京市军管会  征用前英国兵营

1950-04-12
第1版()
专栏：

　　北京市军管会
    征用前英国兵营
    【新华社十一日讯】中国人民解放军北京市军事管制委员会，曾于一月六日颁发布告，征用外国兵营。根据该项布告，前美、法、荷兵营已予以征用。本月四日，军管会向前英国领事发出命令，征用前英国兵营，并着其于一星期内指派专人将东交民巷兴国路一号前英国兵营交出。今日上午九时半，军管会派员前往接收，至下午一时接收完毕。按前英国兵营占地约六十亩，房屋约八百间。
