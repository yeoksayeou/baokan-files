	Brief Financial News
    In order to organize floating capital for investment in production, nine private banks and financial institutions in Nanchang City, under the leadership of the Jiangxi Provincial Branch of the People's Bank of China, established the Nanchang City United Banking and Financial Lending Office on the 20th of last month. It has now begun processing loan applications. The initial loan amount has been set at 4.4 billion yuan, targeting the textile industry.
    The Qinghai Branch of the People's Bank of China, acting on the instruction of the Northwest Regional Bank, is launching a "real value" deposit system for government agencies, in accordance with the State Council's decision on unifying national financial and economic work. It stipulates that funds from military and government agencies and enterprises can be deposited in the bank according to the periodic "real value" deposit method. The minimum deposit period is ten days.
    In order to effectively complete the collection of the first phase of industrial and commercial business tax for this year, the Xinjiang Provincial Tax Bureau has specially dispatched a group of cadres and trainees from the tax personnel training class to various districts and counties in Northern Xinjiang to assist in the collection work.

### 财政金融简讯

1950-04-13
第2版()
专栏：

　　财政金融简讯
    为组织游资投入生产事业，南昌市九家私营行庄在中国人民银行江西省分行领导下，于上月二十日成立南昌市银钱业联合放款处，现已开始办理贷款申请手续，首批贷款数额确定为四十四亿元，对象为纺织业。
    中国人民银行青海分行奉西北区行电令，依照政务院关于统一国家财经工作的决定，举办机关折实存款，规定各军政机关之经费与各企业单位之资金，可按折实存款定期办法存入银行，存款期限最短不得少于十天。
    新疆省税务局为切实完成本年第一期工商业营业税之征收，特抽调一部分干部及税务人员训练班学员，分赴北疆各区、县协助征收工作。
