Title: Ministry of Railways Decides to Reduce Southern Railway Ticket Prices
Author:
Date: 1950-04-29
Page: 1

　　Ministry of Railways Decides
    Reducing Southern Railway Ticket Prices
    [Xinhua News Agency, 28th] The Ministry of Railways of the Central People's Government has decided to reduce passenger ticket prices on Southern Railways starting May 1st, to bring them in line with passenger ticket prices on Northern Railways. The original basic fare for third-class passenger tickets on Southern Railways was 135 yuan per person per kilometer, which is now reduced to 115 yuan (first and second class fares are multiples of the third-class fare). After the adjustment, the third-class ticket price between Shanghai and Guangzhou will be reduced from 152,600 yuan to 130,000 yuan; the price between Beijing and Shanghai will be reduced from 145,100 yuan to 138,500 yuan; and the price between Shanghai and Nanjing will be reduced from 40,400 yuan to 34,400 yuan.

### 铁道部决定减低南方铁路票价

1950-04-29
第1版()
专栏：

　　铁道部决定
    减低南方铁路票价
    【新华社二十八日讯】中央人民政府铁道部决定自五月一日起减低南方铁路客票票价，使与北方铁路客票价取得一致。南方铁路原定三等客票每人每公里基本运价为一百三十五元，现改为一百十五元（头、二等以三等递次加倍）。调整后，上海、广州间三等票价由十五万二千六百元减为十三万元；北京、上海间由十四万五千一百元减为十三万八千五百元；上海、南京间由四万零四百元减为三万四千四百元。
