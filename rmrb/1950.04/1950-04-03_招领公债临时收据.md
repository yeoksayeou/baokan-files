Title: Notice Regarding Lost Government Bond Temporary Receipt

Author: 
Date: 1950-04-03
Page: 6

To the Editor of Lost Government Bond Temporary Receipt:

　　Xu Dihua, a resident of Xianliang Temple in our district, found a government bond temporary receipt (Bu Dong No. 768) on Wangfujing Street and voluntarily handed it over to our office. We are now sending you the receipt and request that you publish a notice to find the owner.

　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　Sincerely,
　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　Civil Affairs Section, First District People's Government of Beijing Municipality

Editor's Note: Mr. Xu Dihua's behavior is very good. The receipt is now kept in our newspaper's "People's Garden" section. We hope the loser will come and claim it.

### 招领公债临时收据

1950-04-03
第6版()
专栏：

　　招领公债临时收据编辑同志：
    兹有本区贤良寺内居民徐棣华君在王府井大街拾得公债临时收据（补东字七六八号）一纸，自动交给本所。现将该收据送给你们，请你们登报招领。
                                        此致敬礼
                  北京市人民政府第一区区公所民政科         
    编者按：徐棣华君这一种行为是很好的。现该收据存在本报“人民园地”组，希失者即来领取。
