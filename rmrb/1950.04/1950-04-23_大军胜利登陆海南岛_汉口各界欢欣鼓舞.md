Title: The Army Triumphantly Lands on Hainan Island; All Circles in Hankou Cheer Enthusiastically
Author:
Date: 1950-04-23
Page: 3
Xinhua News Agency, Hankou, April 21st: After the news that the People's Liberation Army's Fourth Field Army's sea-crossing troops had triumphantly landed on Hainan Island arrived, all circles here were jubilant and celebrated this great victory in the final battle to liberate the entire Central South region. The Yangtze River Daily, Dagang Daily, Central China Worker, and Hubei Peasant News all published this victory news in large space today, and published editorials to pay tribute to the sea-crossing troops. Deng Zihui, Vice Chairman of the Central South Military and Political Committee, also delivered a broadcast speech at 8 pm on the 20th to celebrate the victory, calling on the people of the entire region to step up spring plowing and production, and fully support the front line, so as to quickly and completely liberate Hainan Island.

### 大军胜利登陆海南岛  汉口各界欢欣鼓舞

1950-04-23
第3版()
专栏：

　　大军胜利登陆海南岛
    汉口各界欢欣鼓舞
    【新华社汉口二十一日电】人民解放军第四野战军渡海部队，在海南岛胜利登陆的消息传来后，此间各界莫不欢欣鼓舞，庆祝这一解放中南全境最后一战的伟大胜利。长江日报、大刚报、华中工人、湖北农民报等，今日均以巨大篇幅，刊载这一胜利消息，并发表社论，向渡海部队致敬。中南军政委员会副主席邓子恢，亦于二十日下午八时发表广播词，庆祝胜利，号召全区人民加紧春耕生产，全力支援前线，以迅速全部解放海南岛。
