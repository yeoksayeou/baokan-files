Title: The 27th Meeting of the Government Administration Council Passes the Decision on Cash Management of State Organs
Author:
Date: 1950-04-08
Page: 1

　　Xinhua News Agency, 7th. The 27th Government Administration Council meeting of the Central People's Government was held today at 3:00 PM. The meeting heard a report from Nan Hanchen, President of the People's Bank of China, on the current situation of cash revenue and expenditure, and discussed and approved the decision on implementing cash management for state organs. The meeting then discussed the issue of rewarding production technology and decided to organize a special group to study the issue. In addition, the meeting also approved several important appointment cases and appointment cases to be submitted to the Central People's Government Committee for approval.

### 政务院二十七次会议通过关于国家机关现金管理的决定

1950-04-08
第1版()
专栏：

　　政务院二十七次会议通过关于国家机关现金管理的决定
    【新华社七日讯】中央人民政府政务院第二十七次政务会议，于今日下午三时举行。会议听取了中国人民银行行长南汉宸关于目前现金收支情况的报告，讨论和通过了关于实行国家机关现金管理的决定。会议继讨论了关于奖励生产技术的问题，并决定组织专门小组，对该问题进行研究。此外，会议并通过重要任命案及提请中央人民政府委员会批准任命案多起。
