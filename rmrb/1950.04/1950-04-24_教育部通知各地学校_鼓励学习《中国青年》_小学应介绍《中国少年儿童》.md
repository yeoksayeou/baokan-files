Title: Ministry of Education Notifies Schools Nationwide to Encourage Studying "China Youth"; Elementary Schools Should Introduce "China Young Pioneers"
Author:
Date: 1950-04-24
Page: 3

Xinhua News Agency, April 22nd: The Ministry of Education of the Central People's Government issued a notice on April 18th to schools in all regions, encouraging students to study "China Youth." The notice stated: "China Youth" provides great inspiration and assistance to the vast number of young people in studying Marxism-Leninism and Mao Zedong Thought, and in establishing a worldview of serving the people. The publication has already been valued and loved by teachers and students in several large and middle schools, and extracurricular study groups have been organized. Education departments in all regions should immediately inform their affiliated schools to encourage students to read "China Youth" in their spare time, with necessary guidance provided by teachers. In elementary schools, "China Young Pioneers" can be introduced for reading.

### 教育部通知各地学校  鼓励学习《中国青年》  小学应介绍《中国少年儿童》

1950-04-24
第3版()
专栏：

　　教育部通知各地学校
    鼓励学习《中国青年》
    小学应介绍《中国少年儿童》
    【新华社二十二日讯】中央人民政府教育部于四月十八日通知各地学校鼓励学生学习《中国青年》。通知略称：《中国青年》对广大青年学习马列主义、毛泽东思想，和建立为人民服务的人生观，有很大的启发作用与帮助。该刊在若干大中学中，已为教师学生所重视与爱好，并组织了课外学习。各地教育部门应即转知所属各校，鼓励学生在课外阅读《中国青年》，并由教师予以必要的指导。在小学中，可介绍阅读《中国少年儿童》。
