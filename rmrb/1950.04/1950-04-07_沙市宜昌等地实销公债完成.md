Title: Public Bond Sales Completed in Shashi, Yichang and Other Places
Author:
Date: 1950-04-07
Page: 2
　　
　　Xinhua News Agency, Hankou, 5th: The cities of Shashi and Yichang, Hubei Province, and Xindi Township (a city directly under the jurisdiction of the Mianyang Special Prefecture), exceeded their public bond sales targets by the end of March. Shashi's allocated quota was 200,000 shares, with all sectors subscribing to 209,426 shares and actually selling 208,438 shares; Yichang City's allocated quota was 120,000 shares, with actual sales of 125,315 shares; Xindi Township's allocated quota was 5,000 shares, with actual sales of 11,744 shares, exceeding the allocated quota by 130 percent.

### 沙市宜昌等地实销公债完成

1950-04-07
第2版()
专栏：

　　沙市宜昌等地实销公债完成
    【新华社汉口五日电】湖北省沙市、宜昌两市及新堤镇市（沔阳专署直属市），已于三月底超额完成公债推销任务。沙市分配任务为二十万分，各界认购二十万九千四百二十六分，实销二十万八千四百三十八分；宜昌市分配任务为十二万分，实销十二万五千三百十五分；新堤镇分配任务为五千分，实销一万一千七百四十四分，超过分配任务百分之一百三十。
