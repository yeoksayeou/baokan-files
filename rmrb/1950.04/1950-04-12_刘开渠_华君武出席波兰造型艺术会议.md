Title: Liu Kaiqu and Hua Junwu Attend Polish Conference on Visual Arts
Author:
Date: 1950-04-12
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, 10th: Liu Kaiqu, a sculptor, and Hua Junwu, a cartoonist, were sent by the All-China Federation of Literary and Art Circles to attend the Polish Visual Arts Conference to be held in Warsaw on the 13th of this month. The two of them have already departed for abroad. The All-China Association of Fine Arts Workers entrusted them to take a batch of fine arts books, periodicals, and photographs to the Polish fine arts organizations. The All-China Association of Fine Arts Workers also had them bring a congratulatory letter to the Polish Visual Arts Conference. The congratulatory letter reads: "To the Chairman of the Polish Visual Arts Conference and all comrades: We extend our congratulations to the opening and success of the conference with infinite joy. Our Chinese and Polish art workers will surely be able to work hand in hand and exchange experiences, fighting side by side under the premise of defending peace and democracy. With sincere respect, and wishing the friendship between China and Poland lasts forever."

### 刘开渠·华君武出席波兰造型艺术会议

1950-04-12
第1版()
专栏：

　　刘开渠·华君武出席波兰造型艺术会议
    【新华社十日讯】中华全国文学艺术界联合会派雕刻家刘开渠、漫画家华君武出席将于本月十三日在华沙举行的波兰造型艺术会议。他们两人已先后动身出国。中华全国美术工作者协会曾托他们带了一批美术书刊和照片送给波兰美术团体。中华全国美术工作者协会致波兰造型艺术会议的贺函一件也已由他们带去。贺函称：“波兰造型艺术会议主席并转全体同志：我们怀着无限愉快的心情祝贺大会的开幕和成就。我们中波两国艺术工作者一定能亲密的携手，交流经验。在保卫和平和民主的前提下并肩作战。谨致诚挚的敬礼，并祝中波友谊万岁。”
