Title: Our Trade Delegation Leaves the Soviet Union; Gromyko and Others See Them Off at the Station
Author:
Date: 1950-04-22
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, April 21st: The trade delegation of the People's Republic of China left Moscow on April 19th. The delegation included Mr. Li Fuchun, Vice Chairman of the Northeast People's Government of the People's Republic of China, Mr. Ye Jizhuang, Minister of Trade of the People's Republic of China, and Mr. Wu Xiuquan, Director of the Department of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs of the People's Republic of China.

　　Those who went to the station to see off the delegation included: Gromyko, Deputy Minister of Foreign Affairs of the Soviet Union, Yelyumin, Deputy Minister of Foreign Trade of the Soviet Union, Slatkovsky, Director of the Oriental Department of the Ministry of Foreign Trade of the Soviet Union, Migunov, Trade Representative of the Soviet Union to the People's Republic of China, Dong Jin, Acting Director of the First Far Eastern Department of the Soviet Ministry of Foreign Affairs, Molochkov, Director of the Protocol Department of the Soviet Ministry of Foreign Affairs, and Kuzminsky, Director of the Protocol Department of the Soviet Ministry of Foreign Trade.

　　Mr. Wang Jiaxiang, Ambassador Extraordinary and Plenipotentiary of the People's Republic of China to the Soviet Union, and embassy staff also went to the station to see them off.

### 我贸易代表团离苏  葛罗米柯等赴站欢送

1950-04-22
第1版()
专栏：

　　我贸易代表团离苏
    葛罗米柯等赴站欢送
    【新华社二十一日讯】塔斯社莫斯科二十一日讯：中华人民共和国贸易代表团，已于四月十九日离开莫斯科。代表团有中华人民共和国东北人民政府副主席李富春先生、中华人民共和国贸易部部长叶季壮先生、中华人民共和国外交部司长伍修权先生等。
    赴车站欢送代表团者，有：苏联外交部副部长葛罗米柯，苏联对外贸易部副部长叶列明，苏联对外贸易部东方司司长斯拉特科夫斯基，苏联驻中华人民共和国贸易代表米古诺夫，苏联外交部第一远东司代理司长董金、苏联外交部交际司司长莫洛赤科夫、苏联对外贸易部交际司司长库兹敏斯基。
    中华人民共和国驻苏联特命全权大使王稼祥先生和大使馆人员，亦赴车站送行。
