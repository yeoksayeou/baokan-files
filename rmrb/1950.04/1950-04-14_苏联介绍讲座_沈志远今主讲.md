Title: Lecture on the Soviet Union: Shen Zhiyuan to Lecture Today
Author:
Date: 1950-04-14
Page: 4

　　Lecture on the Soviet Union
　　Shen Zhiyuan to Lecture Today
    [Our Newspaper] The seventh lecture in the "Introduction to the Soviet Union" lecture series, sponsored by the Beijing Sino-Soviet Friendship Association, is scheduled for 6:30 PM on April 14 (Friday). Mr. Shen Zhiyuan will lecture on "The Superiority of the Soviet Socialist Economic System." The location is the Beijing Soviet Foreign Cultural Association at 19 Nanheyan. All those holding long-term or temporary lecture passes from the association are welcome to attend.
                                        (Beijing Sino-Soviet Friendship Association)

### 苏联介绍讲座  沈志远今主讲

1950-04-14
第4版()
专栏：

　　苏联介绍讲座
　　沈志远今主讲
    【本报讯】北京市中苏友好协会主办的“苏联介绍”讲座第七讲定于四月十四日（星期五）下午六时半举行，请沈志远主讲《苏联社会主义经济制度的优越性》。地点在南河沿十九号北京苏联对外文化协会。凡持有该会长期或临时听讲证者皆可前往听讲。
                                        （北京市中苏友好协会）
