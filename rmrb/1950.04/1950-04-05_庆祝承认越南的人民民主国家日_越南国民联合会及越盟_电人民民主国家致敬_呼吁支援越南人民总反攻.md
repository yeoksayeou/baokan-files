Title: “Celebrating the Day of Recognition of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam” Vietnamese National United Front and Viet Minh Send Respects to People's Democratic Countries, Calling for Support for the Vietnamese People's General Counter-Offensive
Author:
Date: 1950-04-05
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, April 4th: Comprehensive report from Vietnam News Agency: Following the celebration of "Vietnam-Soviet-China Friendship Day" on February 18th, the Vietnamese people have scheduled a nationwide "Celebrating the Day of Recognition of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam" on April 4th. The Central Executive Committee of the Vietnamese National United Front and the Standing Committee of the Vietnam Independence League (the two major people's organizations that form the Vietnam National United Front) sent a telegram on the 1st to the organizations and people of Hungary, Romania, Czechoslovakia, Bulgaria, Poland, Albania, North Korea, and the German Democratic Republic to pay their respects. The telegram reads as follows:

    The Vietnam National United Front and the Vietnamese people have always eagerly looked forward to establishing friendly relations with the organizations and people of your countries. Today, the Democratic Republic of Vietnam clearly knows that from now on, it will stand on the side of all democratic and progressive countries in the world. The entire Vietnamese people celebrate the establishment of diplomatic relations with your countries with great enthusiasm. The establishment of these diplomatic relations will surely strengthen the friendship between our peoples. We guarantee to learn from your heroic struggle to smash the French colonialists and international reactionaries in order to liberate our people, thereby consolidating the democratic front, safeguarding world peace, and fulfilling our task as the vanguard of the world democratic front in Southeast Asia.

    We would like to express our sincerest gratitude for your support of our cause. At the same time, we hope that you will give us greater support in the upcoming general counter-offensive against the French colonialists.

        Bui Bang Doan (signed), Chairman of the Central Committee of the Vietnamese National United Front
        Hoang Quoc Viet (signed), Secretary-General of the Standing Committee of the Vietnam Independence League

### “庆祝承认越南的人民民主国家日”  越南国民联合会及越盟  电人民民主国家致敬  呼吁支援越南人民总反攻

1950-04-05
第4版()
专栏：

　　“庆祝承认越南的人民民主国家日”
    越南国民联合会及越盟
    电人民民主国家致敬
    呼吁支援越南人民总反攻
    【新华社四日讯】综合越南通讯社消息：继二月十八日庆祝“越、苏、中友谊日”之后，越南人民又定于四月四日在全国各地广泛举行“庆祝承认越南的人民民主国家日”。越南国民联合会中央执委会和越南独立同盟常委会（组成越南民族统一战线的两大人民团体），为此特于一日致电匈、罗、捷、保、波、阿尔巴尼亚、朝鲜和德意志民主共和国的团体和人民致敬，电文如下：
    越南民族统一战线与越南人民始终热望着与你们国家的团体和人民建立友好关系。今天越南民主共和国十分清楚地知道，从今以后，它将站在世界上所有民主和进步国家的一边。全体越南人民以极大的热情，欢庆与你们国家外交关系的建立，这种外交关系的建立，定将加强我们人民间的友谊。我们保证向你们英勇的斗争学习，粉碎法国殖民主义者与国际反动派，以便解放我们的人民，从而巩固民主阵线，保卫世界和平，并且履行我们作为世界民主阵线在东南亚的前卫部队的任务。
    我们谨向你们致以最诚挚的谢意，感谢你们对我们事业的支持。同时，希望你们在我们即将到来的向法国殖民主义者发动的总反攻中，给我们更大的支持。
        越南国民联合会中央委员会主席裴朋团（签字）
        越南独立同盟常委会总书记黄国越（签字）
