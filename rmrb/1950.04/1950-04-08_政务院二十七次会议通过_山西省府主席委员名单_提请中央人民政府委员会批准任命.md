Title: The 27th Meeting of the Government Administration Council Approved the List of Chairman and Members of Shanxi Provincial Government, Submitted to the Central People's Government Committee for Approval and Appointment
Author:
Date: 1950-04-08
Page: 3

Xinhua News Agency, 7th: The 27th Government Administration Council meeting of the Central People's Government approved the list of Chairman, Vice-Chairmen, and Members (totaling 31) of the Shanxi Provincial People's Government, which was submitted to the Central People's Government Committee for approval and appointment:

Chairman: Cheng Zihua, formerly Chairman of the Shanxi Provincial People's Government.

Vice-Chairmen:
　　Pei Lisheng, formerly Vice-Chairman of the Shanxi Provincial People's Government.
　　Wang Shiying, formerly Vice-Chairman of the Shanxi Provincial People's Government.
　　Deng Chumin, current Central Committee Member of the China Democratic League and President of Shanxi University.

Members: 27 people (in order of stroke count of surnames):
　　Wang Ziru (female), current member of the Shanxi Provincial Democratic Women's Federation.
　　Zhi Yinglin, veteran member of the Tongmenghui.
　　Chi Zhuang, formerly Director of Affairs at Shanxi University.
　　Du Renzhi, current Alternate Central Committee Member of the China Democratic League.
　　He Mu, formerly Director of the Health Bureau of the Jin-Ji-Lu-Yu Border Region Government.
　　Song Zichun, current Chairman of the Preparatory Committee of the Taiyuan City Federation of Industry and Commerce in Shanxi Province.
　　Zhou Huamin, current Director of the Department of Commerce of the Shanxi Provincial People's Government.
　　Zhou Yizhong, current Manager of the Shanxi Branch of the People's Bank of China.
　　Tao Lujia, current Minister of the Propaganda Department of the Shanxi Provincial Committee of the Communist Party of China.
　　Ma Lin, current President of the Shanxi Provincial People's Court.
　　Ma Peixun, formerly Deputy Commander of the Luliang Military Region of the People's Liberation Army.
　　Zhang Junxuan, current Director of the Department of Civil Affairs of the Shanxi Provincial People's Government.
　　Kang Yonghe, current Chairman of the Shanxi Provincial Federation of Trade Unions.
　　Huang Wei (female), current Second Deputy Secretary of the Women's Work Committee of the Shanxi Provincial Committee of the Communist Party of China.
　　Cao Ruizhi, current Water Conservancy Advisor to the Shanxi Provincial People's Government.
　　Cheng Guliang, current Director of the Public Security Department of the Shanxi Provincial People's Government.
　　Jiao Guoding, current Director of the Finance Department of the Shanxi Provincial People's Government.
　　Xie Xuegong, current Minister of the Organization Department of the Shanxi Provincial Committee of the Communist Party of China.
　　Zhen Rongdian, formerly a member of the Jin-Ji-Lu-Yu Border Region Council.
　　Yang Zixiu, formerly Vice-Chairman of the Taiyuan City People's Representative Conference in Shanxi Province.
　　Zhao Zongfu, current Director of the Department of Education of the Shanxi Provincial People's Government.
　　Liu Shaobai, formerly a member of the Shanxi Provincial People's Government.
　　Lai Ruoyu, current Deputy Secretary of the Shanxi Provincial Committee of the Communist Party of China.
　　Ji Gongquan, current Special Commissioner of the Legislative Affairs Commission of the Central People's Government.
　　Bo Shaozong, formerly Chief Engineer of the Gansu Mining Company.
　　Han Chunde, current Mayor of the Taiyuan Municipal People's Government in Shanxi Province.
　　Xiao Wenjiu, current Deputy Commander of the Shanxi Military Region of the People's Liberation Army.
