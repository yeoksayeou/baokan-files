Title: National Warehouse Material Inventory and Allocation Committee Dispatches Three Working Groups to Assist in Work Across Various Regions
Author:
Date: 1950-04-10
Page: 2

　　Xinhua News Agency, April 8th—The National Warehouse Material Inventory and Allocation Committee has dispatched three working groups to East China, Central and South China, and Southwest China to assist in the work of inventorying warehouse materials. The East China working group is led by Shi Lin and will be based in Shanghai; the Central and South China working group is led by Lai Jifa and will be based in Guilin; and the Southwest China working group is led by Wang Fengyuan and will be based in Chongqing. The committee also issued a notice a few days ago, stipulating several issues regarding the inventory and allocation of warehouse materials.

### 全国仓库物资清理调配委会  已派出三个工作团分赴各地协助工作

1950-04-10
第2版()
专栏：

　　全国仓库物资清理调配委会
    已派出三个工作团分赴各地协助工作
    【新华社八日讯】全国仓库物资清理调配委员会已派出三个工作团分赴华东、中南、西南各地协助进行清理仓库物资工作。华东工作团由石林率领，工作地点是上海；华中南工作团由赖际发率领，工作地点是桂林；华西南工作团由王逢原率领，工作地点是重庆。该委员会并已于日前发出通知，对于清理调配仓库物资的几个问题有所规定。
