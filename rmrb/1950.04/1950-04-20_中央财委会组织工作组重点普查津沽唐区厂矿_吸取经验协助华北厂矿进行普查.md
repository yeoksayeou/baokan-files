Title: Central Financial and Economic Committee Organizes Work Group to Focus on Census of Factories and Mines in the Tianjin-Qinhuangdao-Tangshan Area; Drawing on Experience to Assist in Census of Factories and Mines in North China
Author:
Date: 1950-04-20
Page: 2

(Xinhua News Agency, 18th) In order to assist in the work of surveying factories and mines in the North China region, the Financial and Economic Committee of the Government Administration Council of the Central People's Government has specially organized a work group for the focused census of factories and mines in the Tianjin-Qinhuangdao-Tangshan area in conjunction with relevant central ministries. The group will select nine factories in the Tianjin, Qinhuangdao, and Tangshan areas for a focused census in order to gain experience. The group's staff members departed for Tianjin on the 17th to begin work.

### 中央财委会组织工作组重点普查津沽唐区厂矿  吸取经验协助华北厂矿进行普查

1950-04-20
第2版()
专栏：

　　中央财委会组织工作组重点普查津沽唐区厂矿
    吸取经验协助华北厂矿进行普查
    【新华社十八日讯】中央人民政府政务院财政经济委员会为协助华北区厂矿普查工作，特会同中央有关各部组织津沽唐区重点厂矿普查工作组，在津、沽、唐地区选定九个工厂进行重点普查，以求取得经验。该组工作人员已于十七日赴津工作。
