Title: Soviet Youth Delegation to Lecture the Education Sector This Afternoon at 4 PM
Author:
Date: 1950-04-30
Page: 4

　　Soviet Youth Delegation to Lecture the Education Sector This Afternoon at 4 PM

　　[Reported by this newspaper] The Soviet Youth Delegation will give a lecture to Beijing education workers at the Municipal No. 8 Middle School today (30th). Originally scheduled to begin at 2 PM, it has been changed to 4 PM, still at the same location.

### 苏青年代表团今日下午四时对教育界讲演

1950-04-30
第4版()
专栏：

　　苏青年代表团今日下午四时对教育界讲演
    【本报讯】苏联青年代表团本日（三十日）在市立八中对京市教育工作者讲演，原定于下午二时开始，现改于下午四时仍在原地举行。
