Title: Popular Science Lecture Today: "Sun, Moon, and Stars"
Author:
Date: 1950-04-08
Page: 4

　　Popular Science Lecture
    Today's Lecture: "Sun, Moon, and Stars"
    [Our Newspaper's Report] The popular science lecture organized by the Youth Service Department will, from now on, be jointly organized by the Youth Service Department and the Science Popularization Bureau of the Ministry of Culture of the Central People's Government. The second lecture will be held today (Saturday) at 2:00 PM at the Sino-Soviet Friendship Association (19 South River Bank Street). Mr. Tao Hong will speak on "Sun, Moon, and Stars." All are welcome to attend. Tickets can be obtained from the Youth Service Department.
                                   (China Youth Press)

### 大众科学讲座  今讲“太阳、月亮、星星”

1950-04-08
第4版()
专栏：

　　大众科学讲座
    今讲“太阳、月亮、星星”
    【本报讯】青年服务部举办的大众科学讲座，自即日起改由青年服务部和中央人民政府文化部科学普及局合办。订今日（星期六）下午二时在苏联对外文化协会（南河沿十九号）举行第二讲，请陶宏先生讲“太阳、月亮、星星”，欢迎各界前往听讲。入场券可到青年服务部领取。
                                   （中国青年社）
