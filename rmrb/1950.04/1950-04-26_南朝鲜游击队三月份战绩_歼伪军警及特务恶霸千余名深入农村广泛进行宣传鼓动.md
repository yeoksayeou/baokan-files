Title: South Korean Guerrillas' March Achievements: Annihilating Over a Thousand Puppet Troops, Police, and Secret Agents/Local Tyrants; Deepening Propaganda and Mobilization in Rural Areas
Author:
Date: 1950-04-26
Page: 4
　　
    Xinhua News Agency, Pyongyang, 25th. The South Korean Labor News published the following summary of the South Korean People's Guerrilla's March achievements: In March, the South Korean People's Guerrillas fought 1,038 battles against puppet troops and police, killing and wounding 570 puppet troops and police, and seizing a considerable amount of weapons.
    In March, the people's guerrillas in various regions went deep into the countryside and held 924 mass meetings, extensively carrying out propaganda and mobilization work. In close contact with the peasants, the guerrillas eliminated a total of 459 armed secret agents and local tyrants from the puppet "National Defense Forces," "Great Korean Youth Corps," and "Civil Defense Corps," and returned a large amount of confiscated grain, clothing, and other items to the peasants.

### 南朝鲜游击队三月份战绩  歼伪军警及特务恶霸千余名深入农村广泛进行宣传鼓动

1950-04-26
第4版()
专栏：

　　南朝鲜游击队三月份战绩
    歼伪军警及特务恶霸千余名深入农村广泛进行宣传鼓动
    【新华社平壤二十五日电】南朝鲜劳力者报发表南朝鲜人民游击队三月份综合战果如下：南朝鲜人民游击队在三月中，与伪军警作战一千零三十八次，毙伤伪军警五百七十名，缴获武器颇多。
    在三月中，各地区的人民游击队深入农村，召开了九百二十四次群众大会，广泛地进行了宣传鼓动工作。游击队在与农民的紧密联系下，肃清了伪“护国军”、“大韩青年团”、“民保团”等武装特务及恶霸分子共四百五十九名，并将没收的大批粮谷、被服等交还给农民。
