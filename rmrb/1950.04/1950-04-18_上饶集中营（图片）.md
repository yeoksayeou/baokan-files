    Shangrao Concentration Camp
    Shao Yu
    "Three Points on a Line"
    This was the most common form of torture in the concentration camp. The head, buttocks, and heels were against the wall in a line, with two bricks placed under the bend of the knees, forcing one to stand like this for several hours.

### 上饶集中营（图片）
邵宇
1950-04-18
第6版()
专栏：

    上饶集中营
    邵宇
    “三点一线”
    这是集中营里最平常的一种苦刑。头、臂部、脚跟靠到墙上成一线，脚弯处压住两块砖头，这样连站几个钟头。
    吊打
