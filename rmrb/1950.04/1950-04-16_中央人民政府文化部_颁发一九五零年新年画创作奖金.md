Title: The Central People's Government Ministry of Culture Awards 1950 New Year Picture Creation Bonuses
Author:
Date: 1950-04-16
Page: 4

Following the Central People's Government Ministry of Culture's issuance of instructions last November to develop New Year pictures, cultural and educational authorities, art groups, and art workers across the country enthusiastically responded to the call, actively organizing the creation and publication of New Year pictures. According to incomplete statistics, this year New Year pictures were printed in 26 locations, including Beijing, Tianjin, Shijiazhuang, Shenyang, Harbin, Inner Mongolia, Chahar, Shanxi, Shandong, Pingyuan, Dalian, Kaifeng, Luoyang, Shanghai, Hangzhou, Wuxi, Taizhou, Nanjing, Wuhan, Yan'an, Xi'an, Gansu, Qinghai, Dunhuang, Guangzhou, and Guilin, totaling 412 types and a total distribution of over 7 million copies. Approximately 200 painters participated in the painting work, with many traditional Chinese painters and calendar poster artists participating, expanding the New Year picture creation lineup, and improving New Year pictures both in quantity and quality.

This year's New Year picture content generally reflected the victory of the People's Liberation War and the founding of the People's Republic of China, the development of industrial and agricultural production and the people's democratic life, the great unity of the people and the people's love for Chairman Mao, and the friendly mutual assistance between China and the Soviet Union. These works depicted the face of New China, conveyed the feelings of the Chinese people, and were universally loved by the people. This year's New Year picture exhibitions in various places were generally praised by the audience, and many excellent New Year pictures were sold out after publication, which is a clear example of the combination of New Year picture art and the broad masses of the people.

In order to encourage and commend the work of New Year pictures, the Central People's Government Ministry of Culture specially collected New Year picture works from various places, invited experts and considered mass opinions to evaluate them. After final review by Guo Moruo, Director of the Culture and Education Committee, and Shen Yanbing, Minister of Culture, the 1950 New Year Picture Creation Bonus was awarded. ### 中央人民政府文化部  颁发一九五零年新年画创作奖金

1950-04-16
第4版()
专栏：

　　中央人民政府文化部
    颁发一九五零年新年画创作奖金
    中央人民政府文化部自去年十一月颁布开展新年画工作指示后，全国各地文教主管机关、美术团体及美术工作者均热烈响应号召，积极组织新年画创作及出版工作。根据不完全的统计，今年印制新年画的地区计有北京、天津、石家庄、沈阳、哈尔滨、内蒙、察哈尔、山西、山东、平原、大连、开封、洛阳、上海、杭州、无锡、泰州、南京、武汉、延安、西安、甘肃、青海、敦煌、广州及桂林等二十六处，共印制新年画四百十二种，发行总数为七百余万份。参加绘制工作的画家约二百余人，并有不少国画家和月份牌画家参与工作，扩大了新年画的创作阵容，使新年画在数量质量上均提高了一步。
    今年新年画内容普遍地反映了人民解放战争的胜利和中华人民共和国的成立，反映了发展工农业生产和人民民主生活，反映了人民的大团结及人民对于毛主席的热爱，反映了中苏友好互助。这些作品都描绘了新中国的面貌，传达了中国人民的感情，获得了人民的普遍喜爱。今年各地举行的新年画展览大都为观众所赞许，有很多优秀的年画在出版后销售一空，这就是新年画艺术与广大人民相结合的明显的例证。
    中央人民政府文化部为鼓励与表扬新年画工作起见，特征集各地新年画作品，邀请专家及根据群众意见加以评选，经文教委员会郭沫若主任、文化部部长沈雁冰等负责首长最后审定，颁发一九五○年新年画创作奖金。兹将评奖结果附列于后：
