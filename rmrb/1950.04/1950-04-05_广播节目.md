Radio Programs

    [This newspaper reports] The Central People's Broadcasting Station today (the 5th) at 20:20 International Current Affairs. 20:30 Correspondence: (1) Yao compatriots liberated from arduous struggle; (2) Miao compatriots love the People's Liberation Army. 17:30 Youth Time. 18:30 Natural Science Lecture: "Why Things Burn". 18:00 First Literary and Art Program: Gu Rongfu and Yin Fulai perform the "New Five Saints Paying Homage to Heaven" brand-tune opera. 21:00 Second Literary and Art Program: Shenyang Literary Association Ping Opera Group performs Ping Opera "New Dog Beating Advising Husband". 18:45 New Songs.
    [Another News] Beijing People's Broadcasting Station 12:30 Workers' Program: (1) Entertainment: The Workers' Troupe of the Silver Money Industry Trade Union performs the radio drama "Everyone Run a Trade Union", (2) Correspondence Speech: Wang Baoyu from Beijing Telecommunications Section introduces the situation of the section signing apprenticeship contracts. 13:00 Youth Program: (1) School News, (2) Li Yang recites the poem "Zoya". 16:30 Voice of the Municipality: (1) Beijing Municipal People's Government promulgates the Provisional Measures for Handling Ownerless Houses and Land in Urban Areas, (2) Beijing Municipal Public Health Bureau publishes slogans for citizens' spring hygiene and epidemic prevention. 18:45 Listener Service: (1) Answering the question "Why is it said that 92 rice and 81 flour are more nutritious than refined rice and refined flour?"; (2) Answering the question "How much electricity does a four-tube radio consume, equivalent to how many candlepower light bulbs?"; (3) Introducing the new Soviet film "The Rebirth of Stalingrad" showing in the city. 19:20 Russian Language Lecture.

### 广播节目

1950-04-05
第4版()
专栏：广播节目

　　广播节目
    【本报讯】中央人民广播电台今天（五日）二十点二十分国际时事。二十点三十分通讯：（一）从艰苦的斗争中获得解放的猺族同胞；（二）苗族同胞热爱人民解放军。十七点三十分青年时间。十八点三十分自然科学讲座：“东西为什么会燃烧”。十八点第一次文艺节目：顾荣甫、尹福来演唱牌子戏“新五圣朝天”。二十一点第二次文艺节目：沈阳文协评戏组演唱评戏“新打狗劝夫”。十八点四十五分新歌曲。
    【又讯】北京市人民广播电台十二点三十分工人节目：（一）文娱：银钱业工会工人剧团演广播剧《大家办工会》，（二）通讯讲话：北京电务段王宝玉介绍该段订师徒合同的情形。十三点青年节目：（一）学校新闻，（二）黎阳朗诵诗《索亚》。十六点三十分市政之声：（一）北京市人民政府颁布的处理城区无主房屋土地暂行办法，（二）北京市公共卫生局发布的市民春季卫生防疫的标语。十八点四十五分听众服务：（一）回答“为什说九二大米八一面比精米精面有营养”的问题；（二）回答“四灯的收音机所用的电量等于多少烛电灯”的问题；（三）介绍本市上映的新苏联影片《斯大林格勒复兴》。十九点二十分俄语讲座。
