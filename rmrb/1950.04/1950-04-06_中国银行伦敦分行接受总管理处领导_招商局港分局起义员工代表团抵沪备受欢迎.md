Title: Bank of China London Branch Accepts Leadership of Head Office; Representatives of Employees of China Merchants Steam Navigation Company Hong Kong Branch, Who Revolted, Arrive in Shanghai and Are Warmly Received
Author:
Date: 1950-04-06
Page: 1

　　Bank of China London Branch Accepts Leadership of Head Office
    Representatives of Employees of China Merchants Steam Navigation Company Hong Kong Branch, Who Revolted, Arrive in Shanghai and Are Warmly Received
    [Xinhua News Agency, 5th] The London branch of the Bank of China has formally accepted the leadership of the Bank of China's head office and has established normal relations with the head office. The London branch is the tenth branch of the Bank of China's overseas branches to accept the leadership of the head office. The other nine are Hong Kong, Singapore, Penang, Kuala Lumpur, Rangoon, Calcutta, Karachi, Chittagong, and Bombay. The London branch telegraphed its willingness to accept leadership as early as January 7 this year, and on February 11, it jointly telegraphed, resolutely accepting the leadership of the head office. In addition to sending a telegram of commendation, the Bank of China head office instructed branches at home and abroad to establish contact with the London branch and unfreeze its deposits.
    [Xinhua News Agency, Shanghai, 5th] The employees of the thirteen ships of the Hong Kong branch of China Merchants Steam Navigation Company, who revolted in Hong Kong in January, are eager to participate in the people's shipping industry as soon as possible, and specially organized a delegation to visit Shanghai. The delegation of ninety people arrived in Shanghai on the 30th of last month and was warmly welcomed by Shanghai sailors and employees of China Merchants Steam Navigation Company. On the 1st of this month, the China Merchants Steam Navigation Company Trade Union and the Shanghai Seamen's Union held a forum with the visiting group, and a grand get-together was held that evening.

### 中国银行伦敦分行接受总管理处领导  招商局港分局起义员工代表团抵沪备受欢迎

1950-04-06
第1版()
专栏：

　　中国银行伦敦分行接受总管理处领导
    招商局港分局起义员工代表团抵沪备受欢迎
    【新华社五日讯】中国银行伦敦分行已正式接受中国银行总管理处领导，并已与该总管理处建立正常关系。伦敦分行是中国银行国外行处接受总管理处领导的第十个行处，其他九个是香港、新加坡、槟榔屿、吉隆坡、仰光、加尔各答、喀喇蚩、吉大港和孟买。伦敦分行于今年一月七日即来电表示愿意接受领导，于二月十一日又联名来电，坚决接受总管理处领导。中行总管理处除去电嘉勉外，并电嘱国内外行处与伦敦分行建立联系，并解冻其存款。
    【新华社上海五日电】一月间在香港起义的招商局香港分局十三艘轮船员工，热望早日参加人民航运事业，特组织代表团来沪观光。该团一行九十人，于上月三十日抵沪后，备受上海海员和招商局员工的欢迎。本月一日招商局工会、上海海员工会与观光团举行座谈会，当晚并举行盛大的联欢晚会。
