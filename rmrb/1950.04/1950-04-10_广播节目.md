　　Radio Programs
　　[Our Dispatch] The Central People's Broadcasting Station will change its radio programs starting today (the 10th). The newly established International Broadcasting Station will also begin broadcasting from 16:30 today. The station's important programs for today are as follows: 19:00 Social Science Lecture: Ai Siqi will speak on "The Purpose and Method of Studying the History of Social Development." 20:00 People's Forum: Hua Luogeng, a professor at Tsinghua University who recently returned from the United States, will speak on "The United States Government and Scientists." 18:45 Natural Science Lecture: Zhang Jimin of the Science Popularization Bureau of the Ministry of Culture of the Central People's Government will speak on "The Nutritional Value of Brown Rice." 21:45 Character Story: The story of the heroine Liu Hulan. 12:45 Book Introduction: Introduction to "Literary Gazette." 13:00 Storytelling: The station's broadcasting art troupe will broadcast "New Children's Heroes." 18:00 Folk Art: Cai Liangui sings the Xihe Dagu "Shi Bulan Drives the Cart." 20:00 Literary Program: Radio play recording: The China Youth Art Theater presents "The Patriot." 22:00 Literary Program: The Beijing Opera Research Institute of the Ministry of Culture's Opera Improvement Bureau broadcasts the Beijing Opera "Shi Xiu Explores the Village."

### 广播节目

1950-04-10
第4版()
专栏：广播节目

　　广播节目
    【本报讯】中央人民广播电台从今天（十日）起改变广播节目，增设的国际广播电台也从今天十六点三十分起开始播音。该台今天重要节目如下：十九点社会科学讲座：艾思奇讲“学习社会发展史的目的和方法”。二十点人民讲坛：最近由美国回国的清华大学教授华罗庚讲“美国政府与科学家”。十八点四十五分自然科学讲座：中央人民政府文化部科学普及局张姞民讲“糙米的营养价值”。二十一点四十五分人物故事：女英雄刘胡兰的故事。十二点四十五分书刊介绍：介绍文艺报。十三点故事讲述：该台广播文工团播讲“新儿女英雄传”。十八点曲艺：蔡连贵演唱西河大鼓“石不烂赶车”。二十点文艺节目：话剧录音：中国青年艺术剧院演出《爱国者》。二十二点文艺节目：文化部戏曲改进局京剧研究院演播京剧《石秀探庄》。
