Title: Japanese Communist Party Central Committee Issues May Day Slogans: Demands Treaty Based on Potsdam Declaration

Author:

Date: 1950-04-29
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Tokyo, April 28th: According to United Press, the Central Committee of the Japanese Communist Party published slogans for the celebration of May Day on the Akahata newspaper on the 27th. There are nineteen slogans in total, one of which is: Demand the immediate withdrawal of the occupying forces after the conclusion of a comprehensive peace treaty based on the Potsdam Declaration.

Simultaneous with the release of the slogans, the Central Committee of the Japanese Communist Party issued a statement calling on the Japanese working masses to rise up and resist attempts to turn Japan into a colony and a military base. The statement pointed out: Activities aimed at turning Japan into a military base will subject the Japanese people to political, economic, and cultural enslavement. The statement also condemned the war preparations against the Soviet Union, New China, and other Asian countries.

### 日共中央发布五一口号  要求根据波茨坦宣言缔结和约

1950-04-29
第4版()
专栏：

　　日共中央发布五一口号
    要求根据波茨坦宣言缔结和约
    【新华社二十八日讯】据合众社东京讯：日本共产党中央委员会于二十七日在赤旗报上发布庆祝五一劳动节的口号。口号共十九条，其中之一是：要求根据波茨坦宣言缔结全面和约后立即撤退占领军。
    与发布口号的同时，日共中央并发表一项声明，号召日本劳动大众起来抵抗使日本沦为殖民地地位及把日本变为军事基地的企图。声明指出：力图将日本变为军事基地的活动，将使日本人民在政治上、经济上和文化上遭受奴役。声明并谴责对苏联、新中国以及其他亚洲国家的备战活动。
