Title: North China Revolutionary University Plants 7,000 Trees
Author:
Date: 1950-04-25
Page: 3

　　North China Revolutionary University Plants 7,000 Trees
　　[Reported by this newspaper] The North China People's Revolutionary University responded to the government's call for afforestation. On the 21st of last month, it mobilized all faculty, staff, and students to begin planting trees. As of the end of the month, a total of 7,309 saplings of pine, cypress, poplar, locust, peach, apricot, plum, persimmon, walnut, grape, and apple trees were planted along roadsides and in the farm, etc., within the university. All the above saplings were supplied by the North China Agricultural Science Research Institute. The university's farm has also opened up more than one mu of nursery to cultivate seedlings to ensure self-sufficiency in tree planting in the future. More than ten kinds of seedlings have now been cultivated, and more than a thousand cuttings have been planted.
　　　　　　　　(Agricultural Research Group, Office of the University Affairs Department of Revolutionary University)

### 华北革大植树七千株

1950-04-25
第3版()
专栏：

　　华北革大植树七千株
    【本报讯】华北人民革命大学响应政府植树造林号召，于上月二十一日动员全校学工人员开始植树工作，截至月底止，共在校内路侧隙地和农场等处栽植松、柏、杨、槐、桃、杏、李、柿、核桃、葡萄、苹果等苗木七千三百零九株。以上苗木均由华北农业科学研究所供给。该校农场并已开辟苗圃一亩余，以培育苗木，保证今后植树自给自足。现已育苗十余种，插条千余株。
               （革大校务处办公室农业研究组）
