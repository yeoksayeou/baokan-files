Title: Poland and Mongolia Establish Diplomatic Relations
Author:
Date: 1950-04-17
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Warsaw, 16th: According to the Polish Press Agency, the Polish Ambassador to the Soviet Union, Naszkowski, delivered a note to the Ambassador of the Mongolian People's Republic to the Soviet Union on the 14th, stating: The Polish government is very pleased to accept the proposal of the government of the Mongolian People's Republic to establish diplomatic relations between the two countries and requests the Mongolian Ambassador to the Soviet Union to convey to the government of the Mongolian People's Republic that the Polish government firmly believes that the friendship between Poland and Mongolia is a contribution to strengthening the camp of peace and socialism.

### 波蒙建立邦交

1950-04-17
第4版()
专栏：

　　波蒙建立邦交
    【新华社十六日讯】据波兰通讯社华沙讯：波兰驻苏大使纳兹科夫斯基，于十四日以照会一件送交蒙古人民共和国驻苏大使，内称：波兰政府非常满意地接受了蒙古人民共和国政府关于建立两国外交关系的建议，并请蒙古驻苏大使转达蒙古人民共和国政府：波兰政府深信波蒙两国的友谊，对加强和平与社会主义阵营是一种贡献。
