Title: Saifuddin and Others Return to Dihua
Author: 
Date: 1950-04-25
Page: 1

　　Xinhua News Agency, Dihua, April 24th: Saifuddin, Vice Chairman of the Xinjiang Provincial People's Government, and his party have returned to Dihua on the 22nd, after completing their mission to the Soviet Union to participate in the Sino-Soviet negotiations.

### 赛福鼎等返抵迪化

1950-04-25
第1版()
专栏：

　　赛福鼎等返抵迪化
    【新华社迪化二十四日电】新疆省人民政府副主席赛福鼎等一行赴苏参加中苏谈判公毕，已于二十二日返抵迪化。
