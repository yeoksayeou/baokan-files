Title: Communist Party Central Committee Issues May Day Slogans
Author:
Date: 1950-04-28
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, April 25th: The Central Committee of the Communist Party of the Soviet Union has issued this year's May Day slogans, excerpts of which are as follows:

    Long live May Day—the festival of unity of the working people of the world, the festival of solidarity of workers of all countries!
    Fraternal greetings to the people of all countries fighting for peace, democracy, and socialism!
    Fraternal greetings to the people's democratic countries following the path of socialist construction!
    Long live the great Chinese people who have won an epoch-making victory in the struggle against imperialism and its Kuomintang lackeys!
    Long live the Communist Party of China, which has opened the road to socialism for the Chinese people!
    Greetings to the people of Yugoslavia of all nationalities who are carrying out the struggle for liberation, opposing the fascist regime of the Tito clique, and opposing the servants of imperialism!
    May a unified, independent, democratic, and peace-loving Germany exist and prosper forever!
    Working people of all countries! Expose the criminal plans of the warmongers! Expand and consolidate the powerful front in defense of peace! Defending peace is the cause of all people of the world!
    Long live the friendship of the people of Great Britain, the United States, and the Soviet Union in the struggle for world peace!
    Fraternal greetings to the people of colonial and dependent countries fighting for freedom and independence!
    Glory to the Soviet Armed Forces, who guard the peace and security of our motherland! Glory to the best sons and daughters of our motherland—the Heroes of the Soviet Union and Heroes of Socialist Labor!
    Workers, peasants, and intellectuals of the Soviet Union! Further expand the socialist competition to complete the 1950 national economic plan ahead of schedule! Working people of the Soviet Union! Fight for further advances in technology in industry, transportation, and agriculture! Workers, male and female, engineers, and technicians! Fight to maximize the saving of raw materials, fuel, and electricity! Reduce production costs and improve product quality!
    Soviet scientists! Enrich the science and technology of our motherland with new research, discoveries, and inventions! Strengthen the connection between science and industry! Workers in literature, art, and film! Raise the level of artistic activity and create new works of art with lofty ideological content, worthy of the great Soviet people!
    Long live the Soviet trade unions—the school of communism!
    Long live the Soviet women—active builders of communism!
    Long live the Lenin-Stalin Communist Youth League! Young men and women! Tirelessly master the most advanced technology, science, and culture! Be strong and brave, ready to overcome any difficulties!
    Communist Party members and Communist Youth League members! Stand at the forefront of the battle, fight for a new upsurge in economy and culture, and for further consolidation of the power of the Soviet state!
    Long live our great Soviet motherland!
    Long live the great Bolshevik Party, our inspirer and organizer of victory!
    Under the banner of Lenin, under the leadership of Stalin, advance towards the complete victory of communism!

### 联共中央发布五一节口号

1950-04-28
第1版()
专栏：

　　联共中央发布五一节口号
    【新华社二十七日讯】据塔斯社莫斯科二十五日讯：苏联共产党中央委员会顷发布今年五一节的口号，节略如下：
    五一节——全世界劳动人民团结的节日，一切国家工人兄弟团结的节日——万岁！
    向为和平、民主、社会主义而斗争的各国人民致兄弟的敬礼！
    向依循着社会主义建设的道路的各人民民主国家致兄弟的敬礼！
    在反对帝国主义及其国民党奴仆的斗争中，赢得了划时代胜利的伟大的中国人民万岁！
    为中国人民打开了走向社会主义的道路的中国共产党万岁！
    向进行着解放斗争、反对铁托集团法西斯政权、反对帝国主义从仆的南斯拉夫各族人民致敬！
    祝统一、独立、民主、爱好和平的德国长存与繁荣！
    一切国家的劳动人民！揭露战争贩子的罪恶计划！扩大与巩固强大的拥护和平阵线！保卫和平是全世界一切人民的事业！
    英、美、苏三国人民在争取全世界和平斗争中的友谊万岁！
    向为自由、独立而斗争的殖民地与附属国家的人民致兄弟的敬礼！
    光荣归于警卫和平及我们祖国安全的苏联武装部队！光荣归于我们祖国最优秀的儿女——苏联英雄及社会主义劳动英雄！
    苏联的工人、农民和知识分子们！进一步扩大争取先期完成一九五○年国民经济计划的社会主义竞赛！苏联的劳动人民！为工业、运输业、农业中技术的进一步进展而斗争！男女工人、工程师和技师们！为最大限度地节省原料、燃料及电力而斗争！减低生产成本，改进产品质量！
    苏联科学工作者们！以新的研究、发现与发明来丰富我们祖国的科学与技术！巩固科学与工业的联系！文学、艺术与电影工作者们！提高艺术活动的水平，创造有崇高思想内容、无愧于伟大苏维埃人民的新艺术作品！
    苏联工会——共产主义的学校万岁！
    苏联妇女——共产主义积极的缔造者万岁！
    列宁、斯大林共产主义青年团万岁！青年男女们！不倦地精通最先进的技术、科学与文化！要坚强而勇敢，准备克服任何困难！
    共产党员和共产主义青年团员们！站在战斗的最前列，为经济与文化的新高潮，为进一步巩固苏维埃国家的威力而斗争！
    我们伟大的苏维埃祖国万岁！
    伟大的布尔什维克党，我们胜利的鼓舞者与组织者万岁！
    在列宁的旗帜下，在斯大林的领导下，向共产主义的完全胜利前进！
