Title: Soviet Ambassador to Hungary Holds Banquet to Host Soviet Government Delegation; Hungarian Government Leaders and Delegations from Various Countries Attend

Author:

Date: 1950-04-08
Page: 4

　　招待 Soviet Government Delegation
    Soviet Ambassador to Hungary Holds Banquet
    Hungarian Government Leaders and Delegations from Various Countries Attend
    [Xinhua News Agency, 7th] (TAS) Budapest dispatch: Soviet Ambassador to Hungary, Kiselyov, hosted a banquet on the 5th to welcome the Soviet Government Delegation which had come to Hungary to participate in the National Day celebrations. The entire delegation, headed by Voroshilov and Suslov, attended. Those invited to the banquet included Hungarian government leaders Rakosi, Szakasits, and Dobi, representatives from Hungarian science and culture, Stakhanovite workers, advanced farmers, etc.; and delegations from the People's Republic of China, the German Democratic Republic, Poland, Czechoslovakia, Romania, Bulgaria, Korea, etc., who had come to Hungary to participate in the celebrations, diplomatic envoys from various People's Democratic countries, and representatives from the Communist Parties of France, Italy, the United Kingdom, West Germany, Austria, Denmark, Finland, Canada, Greece, and Spain. The reception took place in an extremely warm and friendly atmosphere.

### 招待苏联政府代表团  苏驻匈大使举行宴会  匈政府领袖各国代表团均出席

1950-04-08
第4版()
专栏：

　　招待苏联政府代表团
    苏驻匈大使举行宴会         
    匈政府领袖各国代表团均出席
    【新华社七日讯】塔斯社布达佩斯讯：苏联驻匈牙利大使基塞列夫五日设宴招待来匈参加国庆庆典的苏联政府代表团。出席以伏罗希洛夫、苏斯洛夫为首的代表团全体。应邀赴宴者有匈政府领袖拉科西、斯查卡西兹和道比，匈牙利科学与文化界代表，斯塔哈诺夫式工人，先进农民等；和来匈参加盛典的中华人民共和国、德意志民主共和国、波、捷、罗、保、朝鲜等国的代表团，各人民民主国家外交使节，及法、意、英、西德、奥地利、丹麦、芬兰、加拿大、希腊和西班牙等国的共产党代表。招待会在极度热情、友好的气氛中进行。
