Title: In Stark Contrast to the U.S. Imperialists Sending Arms to Italian Reactionaries, The Soviet Union Gifts Tractors to Italian Cooperatives: People of Emilia Hold Grand Gathering to Praise Soviet Friendship
Author:
Date: 1950-04-28
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency, Rome, 25th: According to TASS, Soviet cooperative organizations have gifted twenty tractors to Italian farmers' agricultural cooperatives. The presentation ceremony was held a few days ago in Reggio nell'Emilia. Stimofeev, Chairman of the Central Auditing Commission of All-Union Cooperatives, led a Soviet cooperative delegation to Italy to participate in the ceremony hosted by the Italian National Cooperative League.
　　The entire population of the city and thousands of nearby farmers participated in this grand gathering. The walls of the venue were hung with numerous large banners about the friendship between the Soviet and Italian peoples. The banners emphasized that the United States brought arms to Italy, while the Soviet Union sent tools for peaceful labor.
　　The tractors gifted by the Soviet cooperatives will become the core of Italian machine and tractor stations. The National Cooperative League will establish machine and tractor stations in the provinces of Salerno, Potenza, Catanzaro, Messina, and Palermo.

### 与美帝送给意反动派军火成显明对比  苏联赠意合作社拖拉机  艾米利亚人民举行盛会赞扬苏联友谊

1950-04-28
第4版()
专栏：

　　与美帝送给意反动派军火成显明对比
    苏联赠意合作社拖拉机
    艾米利亚人民举行盛会赞扬苏联友谊
    【新华社二十七日讯】据塔斯社罗马二十五日讯：苏联的合作社组织送给意大利的农民农业合作社二十部拖拉机，并于数天之前在勒佐·尼尔·艾米利亚举行赠送仪式。全苏合作社中央监察委员会主席斯提莫费耶夫，特率领苏联合作社代表团来意大利，参加意大利全国合作社联盟主持的典礼。
    全市的居民和数千附近的农民都参与了这次盛会。会场的四壁悬挂着许多关于苏联人民和意大利人民的友谊的大幅标语。标语着重指出美国给意大利带来军火，而苏联则给意大利送来了和平劳动的工具。
    苏联合作社赠送的拖拉机，将成为意大利机器和拖拉机站的核心。全国合作社联盟将在萨勒诺、波坦查、加坦萨罗、麦西拿、巴勒摩等省，设立机器和拖拉机站。
