　　"Soviet Union Introduction" Lecture Rescheduled for Today
    Baudlev Lectures on "Soviet Finance"
    [Our Newspaper] The sixth lecture in the "Soviet Union Introduction" lecture series, organized by the Beijing Sino-Soviet Friendship Association, has been rescheduled to be held at 7:00 PM on April 7th (this Friday). Professor Baudlev, a Soviet financial expert who is in China to lecture, will be specially invited to speak on "Soviet Finance." The location remains the Sino-Soviet Cultural Association at 19 Nanheyan Street, Beijing. All those holding long-term or temporary listening permits from the association are welcome to attend. (Publicity and Education Group, Service Department of the Beijing Sino-Soviet Friendship Association)

### “苏联介绍”讲座改于今日举行  包德列夫讲“苏联的财政”

1950-04-07
第4版()
专栏：

　　“苏联介绍”讲座改于今日举行
    包德列夫讲“苏联的财政”
    【本报讯】北京市中苏友好协会主办的“苏联介绍”讲座第六讲，现改期于四月七日（本星期五）下午七时举行。届时特请此次来中国讲学的苏联财政学专家包德列夫教授主讲“苏联的财政”。地点仍在南河沿十九号北京苏联对外文化协会。凡持有该会长期或临时听讲证者皆可前往听讲。（北京市中苏友好协会服务部宣教组）
