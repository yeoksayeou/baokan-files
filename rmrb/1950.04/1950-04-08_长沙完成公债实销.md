Title: Changsha Completes Actual Sale of Public Bonds
Author:
Date: 1950-04-08
Page: 1

　　Xinhua News Agency, Changsha, 7th. As of the 5th, the actual sales figure for public bonds in Changsha City had reached 1,102,074 shares, exceeding the assigned task by 2,074 shares. People from all walks of life in the city are still continuing to make payments, and the oversubscribed amount of more than 50,000 shares of public bonds will soon be fully paid.

### 长沙完成公债实销

1950-04-08
第1版()
专栏：

　　长沙完成公债实销
    【新华社长沙七日电】长沙市实销公债数字，至五日止已达一百一十万二千零七十四分，超过分配任务二千零七十四分。现该市各界仍在继续缴款，不日将可将超额认购的五万余分的公债款缴足。
