Title: Japanese Trade Unions Resolve to Jointly Celebrate May Day
Author:
Date: 1950-04-22
Page: 4
　
　　Xinhua News Agency, Tokyo, 21st: According to the Associated Press and Kyodo News Agency, Tokyo: Japanese trade unions resolved at a preparatory meeting for May Day on the 19th to jointly hold a May Day celebration rally. The celebration slogans passed at the preparatory meeting included: "Defend peace and independence" and "Promote overall peace." According to the spokesman of the All-Japan Federation of Industrial Unions (Sanbetsu), which will lead the celebration, it is estimated that 400,000 workers will hold a celebration ceremony in the large plaza in front of the Imperial Palace on May 1.

### 日本工会决议联合庆祝五一

1950-04-22
第4版()
专栏：

　　日本工会决议联合庆祝五一
    【新华社二十一日讯】据美联社、共同社东京讯：日本各工会十九日在“五一”节筹备会上决议联合举行“五一”节庆祝大会。筹备会上通过的庆祝口号有：“保卫和平与独立”，“促进全面媾和”。据将领导这次庆祝大会的全日产业别工会联合会（产联）的发言人估计，五月一日将有四十万工人在皇宫前大广场举行庆祝仪式。
