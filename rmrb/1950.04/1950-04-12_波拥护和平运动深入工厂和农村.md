Title: Polish Peace Movement Penetrates Factories and Rural Areas
Author:
Date: 1950-04-12
Page: 4

　　Xinhua News Agency, Warsaw, 8th. TASS: Broad masses of people in all walks of life in Poland are actively participating in the struggle for peace, and peace committees are being established everywhere. In Mielec County, Rzeszów Province, nine district peace committees and 103 village peace committees have been established. In the city of Jarosław in the same province, 28 factory peace committees have also been established.

### 波拥护和平运动深入工厂和农村

1950-04-12
第4版()
专栏：

　　波拥护和平运动深入工厂和农村
    【新华社十日讯】塔斯社华沙八日讯：波兰各界广大人士正积极参加争取和平的斗争，各地正纷纷建立拥护和平委员会。在勒佐省麦列特克县内，就成立了九个区的拥护和平委员会和一百另三个村的拥护和平委员会。该省的雅罗斯拉夫城，也成立了二十八个工厂拥护和平委员会。
