    Responding to Chairman Mao's Call: North China Liberation Army Engages in Large-Scale Production
    Photos by: Zhang Yeyu, Zhong Sheng, Zhen Chuan, Ying Chun, Zhuo Zhi
    Troops organize their own mules and horses to cultivate the land, preparing for planting.
    A unit of the People's Liberation Army heads to the production front.
    Checking farming tools. Photo by Kong Fangen
    Transforming a desolate wilderness into fertile paddy fields.
    Establishing field broadcasting to exchange production experiences, commend production models, and encourage production enthusiasm.
    During production, catering comrades deliver meals and water, ensuring comrades are neither thirsty nor hungry, to complete the large-scale production task.
    Conducting cultural and recreational activities to enliven production enthusiasm.
