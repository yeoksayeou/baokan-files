Title: International Democratic Women's Federation Executive Committee Unanimously Passes Appeal to the Women of the World, Calling for Intensified Struggle to Defend Peace
Author:
Date: 1950-04-22
Page: 4

　　International Democratic Women's Federation Executive Committee
    Unanimously Passes Appeal to the Women of the World
    Calling for Intensified Struggle to Defend Peace
    [Xinhua News Agency, Helsinki, 20th] TASS, Helsinki, 20th—The Executive Committee of the International Democratic Women's Federation unanimously passed an appeal to the women of the world at its meeting on the 19th, calling on women worldwide to intensify the struggle to defend peace. The appeal to the women of the world, after pointing out the existence of the threat of war, declared: The International Democratic Women's Federation, representing hundreds of millions of women worldwide, announces its support for the appeal of the Standing Committee of the World Congress of Supporters of Peace, calling on women worldwide to sign this appeal and actively participate in the work of establishing peace committees, support all measures aimed at stopping the arms race, oppose the shipment of American arms to other countries, oppose the transportation of troops and weapons to colonies and dependent countries, and fully support the dockworkers and railway workers who are striking to refuse the unloading of arms.
    The appeal to the women of the world loudly called out: "Women, wives and mothers, women workers and peasant women, women scientists and women students, women from all walks of life, all social strata, and all nationalities around the world, more boldly raise angry protests against the instigators of a new war! Let hundreds of millions of men and women issue their powerful voices on May Day, and let this voice resonate across every continent, becoming a warning to the warmongers that cannot be ignored!"

### 国际民主妇联执委会  一致通过告世界妇女书  号召加紧斗争保卫和平

1950-04-22
第4版()
专栏：

　　国际民主妇联执委会
    一致通过告世界妇女书
    号召加紧斗争保卫和平
    【新华社二十一日讯】塔斯社赫尔辛基二十日讯：国际民主妇联执行委员会在十九日会议上一致通过告世界妇女书，号召全世界妇女加紧进行保卫和平的斗争。告世界妇女书在指出战争威胁之存在后宣称：国际民主妇联代表全世界亿万妇女，宣布支持世界拥护和平大会常设委员会的呼吁书，号召全世界妇女在这一呼吁书上签名，并积极参加建立和平委员会的工作，支持一切旨在阻止军备竞赛的措施；反对美国军火运往其他国家；反对运送军队和武器到殖民地与附属国家去；全力支持拒绝卸运军火而举行罢工的码头工人和铁路工人。
    告世界妇女书大声呼吁：“妇女们，妻子与母亲们，女工与农妇们，女科学家和女学生们，各行各业各阶层和全世界各民族的妇女们，更大胆地对新战争挑拨者提出愤怒的抗议吧！让亿万的男女在五一节发出他们强有力的呼声，并且让这个呼声响彻各个大陆，成为对战争贩子一种不容轻视的警告吧！”
