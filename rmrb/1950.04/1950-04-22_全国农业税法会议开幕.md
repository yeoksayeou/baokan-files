Title: National Agricultural Tax Law Conference Opens
Author:
Date: 1950-04-22
Page: 2

Xinhua News Agency, April 20th. The first National Agricultural Tax Law Conference convened by the Ministry of Finance of the Central People's Government officially opened in Beijing on the 19th. Attendees included those in charge of agricultural tax policy from East China, Central South, Northwest, Inner Mongolia, and other regions and provinces, as well as those in charge of finance and grain work. At the opening ceremony, Rong Zihe and Wang Shao'ao, both Vice Ministers of the Ministry of Finance of the Central People's Government, delivered reports. In addition to pointing out the achievements and shortcomings of last year's collection work, Vice Minister Rong Zihe emphasized that the main task of this conference is to formulate a relatively unified draft agricultural tax law suitable for the newly liberated areas based on the state's tax policy, and to discuss the implementation of agricultural tax and the issue of surveying land and assessing output.

### 全国农业税法会议开幕

1950-04-22
第2版()
专栏：

　　全国农业税法会议开幕
    【新华社二十日讯】中央人民政府财政部所召开的首届全国农业税法会议，已于十九日在京正式开幕，出席会议者有：华东、中南、西北、内蒙等区省农业税税政主管人员及财政、粮食工作的负责人。在开幕式上，中央人民政府财政部戎子和、王绍鏊两副部长都作了报告。戎子和副部长除指出去年征收工作的成绩及缺点外，着重提到这次会议上主要的是根据国家的税收政策制定一个比较统一的适合于新解放区的农业税税法草案，并讨论农业税的执行和调查土地评订产量问题。
