Title: Cultural Organizations in Various Parts of the Soviet Union Serve School Children, "Children's Book Week" Ends
Author:
Date: 1950-04-05
Page: 4
　　
　　Cultural Organizations in Various Parts of the Soviet Union Serve School Children
    "Children's Book Week" Ends
    [Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, April 4th] TASS Moscow dispatch: Soviet Children's Book Week ended on April 2nd. Children's Book Week was held in one hundred and thirty Soviet towns and many villages. Cultural Palaces, workers' clubs and three thousand five hundred children's libraries all served school children. Millions of young people and writers held gatherings, participated in recitation competitions, attended literary appreciation meetings, film screenings and visited book exhibitions.

### 苏各地文化组织都为学童们服务  “儿童读物周”结束

1950-04-05
第4版()
专栏：

　　苏各地文化组织都为学童们服务
    “儿童读物周”结束
    【新华社四日讯】塔斯社莫斯科讯：苏联儿童读物周已于四月二日结束。一百三十个苏联城镇及许多乡村都举行了儿童读物周。各文化宫、工人俱乐部和三千五百所儿童图书馆都为学童们服务。千百万少年和作家们曾举行集会，参加朗诵比赛，出席文艺欣赏会、电影放映会并参观图书展览。
