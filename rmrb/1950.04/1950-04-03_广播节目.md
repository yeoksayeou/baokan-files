Broadcast Program

　　[Our Dispatch] The Central People's Broadcasting Station today (the 3rd) will broadcast international current events at 20:20. At 20:30: (1) Newsletter "Henan's Liberated Peasants Love Chairman Mao"; (2) "My Experiences in Land Reform Work." Women's hour at 17:30. Lecture on natural sciences at 18:30: "New Achievements in Soviet Science and Inventions." First literary program at 18:00: Ma Xiaorong sings the Henan Zhui Zi ballad "New Journey to the Capital." Second literary program at 21:00: Soviet songs. New songs at 18:45: "Little Song of the Beijing-Hankou Railway," "Division of Labor and Cooperation."

### 广播节目

1950-04-03
第4版()
专栏：广播节目

　　广播节目
    【本报讯】中央人民广播电台今天（三日）二十点二十分国际时事。二十点三十分：（一）通讯《河南翻身农民热爱毛主席》；（二）《我在土改工作中的体验》。十七点三十分妇女时间。十八点三十分自然科学讲座；《苏联科学与发明的新成就》。十八点第一次文艺节目：马小荣演唱坠子《新上京》。二十一点第二次文艺节目：苏联歌曲。十八点四十五分新歌曲：《京汉路小唱》，《分工合作》。
