Title: Mass Activity Film to be Shot in Tiananmen Square; Various Performing Arts Troupes to Participate in Performance
Author:
Date: 1950-04-12
Page: 4

　　[Beijing News Agency] The Beijing Film Studio is scheduled to shoot a film of mass activities in Tiananmen Square at 8:00 am on the 16th of this month. All municipal performing arts troupes and theater troupes will participate in the performance, with an estimated tens of thousands of people attending. This morning (the 12th), the performing arts troupes will hold a rehearsal in Tiananmen Square.

### 天安门前将拍群众活动影片  各文工团参加表演

1950-04-12
第4版()
专栏：

　　天安门前将拍群众活动影片  各文工团参加表演
    【京市新闻处讯】北京电影制片厂定于本月十六日上午八时在天安门拍摄群众活动影片，全市各文艺工作团、剧团均参加表演，与会群众预计有数万人。今（十二）日上午各文工团将在天安门举行预演。
