Title: 29th Meeting of the Government Administration Council
Author:
Date: 1950-04-22
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, April 21st - The 29th meeting of the Central People's Government Administration Council was held at 3:30 PM today. The meeting heard a comprehensive report from Li Lisan, Minister of the Central People's Government Ministry of Labor, on the National Labor Bureau Directors' Conference, and a report from Hu Qiaomu, Director of the News Publication Administration, on the News Work Conference. The meeting discussed and approved the draft Trade Union Law of the People's Republic of China and decided to submit it to the Central People's Government Committee for approval. The meeting also approved the "Instruction on Launching Spare-Time Education for Workers and Staff," approved the "Instruction of the Central People's Government Ministry of Labor on Establishing Labor-Capital Consultation Meetings in Private Enterprises," the "Decision of the Central People's Government News Publication Administration on Improving Newspaper Work," and the "Decision on Establishing a Radio Broadcasting Network." The meeting approved the Sino-Soviet Agreement on Establishing the Sino-Soviet Petroleum Joint-Stock Company in Xinjiang, the Sino-Soviet Agreement on Establishing the Sino-Soviet Non-Ferrous and Rare Metals Joint-Stock Company in Xinjiang, and the Sino-Soviet Agreement on Establishing the Sino-Soviet Civil Aviation Joint-Stock Company. In addition, the meeting approved two appointment cases.

### 政务院廿九次会议

1950-04-22
第1版()
专栏：

　　政务院廿九次会议
    【新华社二十一日讯】中央人民政府政务院第二十九次政务会议于今日下午三时半举行。会议听取了中央人民政府劳动部李立三部长关于全国劳动局长会议的综合报告和新闻总署胡乔木署长关于新闻工作会议的报告。会议讨论和通过了中华人民共和国工会法草案，决定提请中央人民政府委员会批准。会议并通过了关于展开职工业余教育的指示，批准了中央人民政府劳动部关于在私营企业中设立劳资协商会议的指示、中央人民政府新闻总署关于改进报纸工作的决定及关于建立广播收音网的决定。会议批准了中苏关于在新疆创办中苏石油股份公司协定、中苏关于在新疆创办中苏有色及稀有金属股份公司协定、中苏关于建立中苏民用航空股份公司的协定。此外，会议通过了任命案两件。
