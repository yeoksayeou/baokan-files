Title: ACFTU Immediately Convened an Emergency Standing Committee to Study Relief for Unemployed Workers in Shanghai
Author:
Date: 1950-04-18
Page: 1

　　ACFTU Immediately Convened an Emergency Standing Committee to Study Relief for Unemployed Workers in Shanghai

    [Xinhua News Agency, 17th] After the All-China Federation of Trade Unions received a special letter from the Shanghai Federation of Trade Unions on the 16th of this month, appealing to the workers across the country to provide mutual assistance to relieve unemployed workers in Shanghai, it was decided that an emergency standing committee meeting would be held on the 20th of this month to specifically study how to mobilize the broad masses of workers across the country to display a high degree of class love and spirit of solidarity and mutual assistance, actively respond to the call of the Shanghai Federation of Trade Unions, and carry out concrete measures for the relief of unemployed workers movement, in order to effectively assist unemployed workers in Shanghai to overcome their current difficulties and strive for the early arrival of economic improvement.

### 全总即开紧急常委会  研究救济沪失业工人

1950-04-18
第1版()
专栏：

　　全总即开紧急常委会  研究救济沪失业工人
    【新华社十七日讯】本月十六日中华全国总工会接到了上海市总工会请向全国工人弟兄呼吁以互助办法救济上海失业工人的专函之后，即决定于本月二十日召开紧急常委会议，专门研究如何发动全国广大工人群众高度的阶级友爱和团结互助精神，积极响应上海总工会的呼吁，举行救济失业工人运动的具体办法，以期有效地援助上海失业工人渡过目前难关，争取经济情况好转的早日到来。
