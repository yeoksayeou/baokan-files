Title: Central People's Broadcasting Station Broadcast - Zhao Guilan's Speech Recording
Author:
Date: 1950-04-16
Page: 4

　　Central People's Broadcasting Station Broadcast - Zhao Guilan's Speech Recording
　　[This Newspaper's News] The Central People's Broadcasting Station will broadcast today (the 16th) at 20:00 People's Forum: Dalian Jianxin Factory female worker and Communist Party member Zhao Guilan will speak on "I Vow to Serve the People to the End" (recording). At 12:45, book introductions: Four books published by Xinhua Bookstore, "A Preliminary Draft of the History of the New Democratic Revolution in China," "Stalin on the Chinese Revolution," "The Birth of New China," and "The Embryo of Industrial China." From 18:00 to 20:00, Sunday Art and Literature Program: Music Evening: Ma Sicong's violin solo "Rondo"; Guan Yuyi's solo "Little Oriole"; Jiang Fengzhi's Erhu solo "Empty Mountain Birds"; Li Huanzhi's flute solo "Shepherd's Short Flute"; Zhang Lu's solo "Save Production Small Song." At 20:30, Art and Literature Program: Radio Play: The Radio Art Troupe will perform "Pan Xiuzhi." At 22:00, Art and Literature Program: The Amateur National Music Research Society will perform "Cantonese Music" and "Silk and Bamboo Music."
　　[Another News Item] Beijing People's Broadcasting Station, 7:30, Workers' Program: (1) Broadcast of the Yangko Opera: "The Whole Family is Busy," (2) New Songs. 16:30, Youth Program: Broadcasting Western music. 17:00, Entertainment Program: (1) Soldiers' Entertainment: Singing by the Artillery Political Department Entertainment Training Team; (2) Western music. 20:00, Sino-Soviet Friendship Sound: Zeng Ping, Deputy Secretary-General of the Beijing Sino-Soviet Friendship Association, will speak on "On Sino-Soviet Friendship and the Sino-Soviet Friendship Association." 21:30, Listener Services: (1) Answering questions on how to select and soak cotton seeds; (2) Introducing the Indian film "Son of the Soil"; (3) Recruitment advertisement for statistics students by the Northeast People's Government Statistics Bureau.

　　中央人民电台广播  赵桂兰讲话录音
