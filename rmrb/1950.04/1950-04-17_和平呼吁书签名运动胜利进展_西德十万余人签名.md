Title: Signature Campaign for Peace Appeal Achieves Victory Progress: Over 100,000 Signatures in West Germany
Author:
Date: 1950-04-17
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency, Berlin, April 15th: The signature campaign for the Peace Appeal in West Germany has made victorious progress. In the days since the campaign began, more than 100,000 people have signed the World Peace Congress Standing Committee's appeal. In Fildstadt, 7,500 people signed in two days. In many enterprises, almost all workers have signed. Near Stuttgart, one peace supporter collected 132 signatures in two hours. On the 13th, 2,000 employees of Daimler-Benz in Mannheim, 400 tram workers in the city, and 1,000 miners from the Neumueller mine in Duisburg all signed the Peace Appeal.

### 和平呼吁书签名运动胜利进展  西德十万余人签名

1950-04-17
第4版()
专栏：

　　和平呼吁书签名运动胜利进展
    西德十万余人签名
    【新华社十六日讯】塔斯社柏林十五日讯：西德征集和平呼吁书的签名运动已获胜利进展。运动开始后数日来，已有十万多人在世界拥护和平大会常委会的呼吁书上签名。在菲尔兹城，两天内已有七千五百人签了名。在许多企业中，几乎全体工人都签了名。在斯图加特附近，有一个和平拥护者在两小时内就征集了一百三十二个人签名。十三日，曼海姆的达姆勒——本兹公司的两千职工，该市四百名电车工人及杜伊斯堡新缪勒矿场的一千名矿工，都在和平呼吁书上签了名。
