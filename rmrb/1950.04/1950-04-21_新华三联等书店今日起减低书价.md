Title: Xinhua, Sanlian and Other Bookstores to Reduce Book Prices Starting Today
Author:
Date: 1950-04-21
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, April 20th: The books and periodicals published by the National Xinhua Bookstore and Sanlian Bookstore, as well as Soviet edition books distributed by the International Bookstore, were originally sold at 1200 times the base price. Recently, due to the decline in prices and reduced costs, in order to alleviate the burden on readers, it has been decided to reduce the price to 1000 times the base price starting from the 21st.

### 新华三联等书店今日起减低书价

1950-04-21
第1版()
专栏：

　　新华三联等书店今日起减低书价
    【新华社二十日讯】全国新华书店、三联书店出版的书刊和国际书店经销的苏联版书刊，原按基本定价一千二百倍发售，最近因物价降落，成本减低，为减轻读者负担，决定自二十一日起改按基本定价一千倍发售。
