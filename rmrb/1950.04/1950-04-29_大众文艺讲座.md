Title: Popular Literature and Art Lecture
Author:
Date: 1950-04-29
Page: 4

　　[News from the Beijing News Agency] The Popular Literature and Art Creation Research Association and the Youth Service Department are jointly sponsoring the weekly literature and art lecture series. This week, Huang Yaomian will be the speaker, and the topic will be "On the Appearance of Characters in Novels." After the lecture, Guan Xuezeng and Wu Changbao will perform the Qinshu "Don't Be Superstitious." The venue is the Central Academy of Fine Arts in Xiaowei Camp, and the time is Sunday (April 30th) at 9:30 AM. Long-term tickets or temporary tickets are accepted for admission. Temporary tickets can be obtained from the Youth Service Department from Saturday.

### 大众文艺讲座

1950-04-29
第4版()
专栏：

　　大众文艺讲座
    【京市新闻处讯】大众文艺创作研究会与青年服务部联合主办的星期文艺讲座本周由黄药眠主讲，讲题为“论小说中人物的登场”。会后由关学增、吴长宝演唱琴书“别迷信”。会址在校尉营中央美术学院，时间为星期日（三十日）上午九时半，凭长期券或临时券均可入场。临时券可于星期六至青年服务部领取。
