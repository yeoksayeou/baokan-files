Title: Youth Service Department Holds Table Tennis Competition
Author:
Date: 1950-04-24
Page: 4

　　Youth Service Department Holds Table Tennis Competition
    [This newspaper reported] The Youth Service Department, in order to develop and popularize healthy recreational activities, is holding a table tennis competition. It is divided into two groups: ordinary (workers, government employees, staff, troops, citizens) and students. Each team consists of five to seven people, formed by participants freely. Registration is open to young men and women from all walks of life from today until the 25th. (China Youth Society)

### 青年服务部举办乒乓赛

1950-04-24
第4版()
专栏：

　　青年服务部举办乒乓赛
    【本报讯】青年服务部为开展和普及正当文娱活动，顷举办乒乓球赛。分普通（工人、机关、工作人员、部队、市民）和学生两组，以五人至七人为一队，由参加者自由组合。自即日起至二十五日止。各界青年男女均可报名。（中国青年社）
