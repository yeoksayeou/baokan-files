Title: Disaster Relief Donation
Author:
Date: 1950-04-03
Page: 6

　　Disaster Relief Donation
    This newspaper yesterday received, on behalf of the North China United Publishing House, a disaster relief donation of 154,000 yuan, which has been forwarded to the Disaster Relief Committee.

### 救灾捐款

1950-04-03
第6版()
专栏：

　　救灾捐款
    本报昨日代收华北联合出版社救灾捐款拾伍万肆千元正，已转交救灾委员会。
