Title: Ministry of Labor to Hold Meeting Tomorrow to Discuss Measures to Relieve Unemployed Workers; Beijing Relief Committee Established
Author:
Date: 1950-04-26
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency: The Ministry of Labor of the Central People's Government, in order to study methods for relieving unemployed workers in various parts of the country, is scheduled to convene a meeting of the Directors of Labor Bureaus from Shanghai, Nanjing, Wuhan, Shandong, Tianjin and other places with more serious unemployment situations in Beijing from April 27th to 29th. The main topics of discussion will include the scope of relief for unemployed workers, relief agencies, the source and distribution of relief funds, and the placement of unemployed workers.

This Newspaper Reports: In order to support unemployed workers in Shanghai and other places in an organized and led manner, the Beijing Municipal Federation of Trade Unions specially invited the Municipal Government, the Beijing Municipal Committee of the Communist Party of China, the Political Department of the North China Military Region, the Municipal Labor Bureau, the Municipal Public Security Bureau, the Municipal Committee of the Communist Youth League, the Municipal Women's Federation, and the Preparatory Committee of the Municipal Education Workers' Union to a meeting yesterday morning (the 25th) to establish the Beijing Relief Committee for Unemployed Workers. The meeting decided, based on the principle of conscious voluntarism, to mobilize troops and staff of government organizations to donate two catties of rice or one day's salary per person (the donation campaign for workers has already begun). It was also decided that donations from all units must be submitted before May 15th. The location for submitting donations will be announced separately in the newspaper after it has been determined.

The meeting elected Xiao Ming (Vice Chairman of the Municipal Federation of Trade Unions), Wu Han (Deputy Mayor of Beijing), Cheng Hongyi (Director of the Municipal Bureau of Commerce), Zhao Fan (Director of the Municipal Party Committee Office), Zhang Zhixiang (Director of the Political Department of the North China Military Region), Wan Yi (Deputy Director of the Municipal Labor Bureau), Zhang Minghe (Deputy Director of the Municipal Public Security Bureau), Zhang Xiaomei (Director of the Municipal Women's Federation), Yang Bozheng (Deputy Secretary of the Municipal Committee of the Communist Youth League), and Qian Duansheng (Deputy Director of the Preparatory Committee of the Municipal Education Workers' Union), a total of ten people, to form the Beijing Relief Committee for Unemployed Workers. Xiao Ming was elected as the Chairman of the Committee.

### 劳动部定明日举行会议  讨论救济失业工人办法  京市救济委员会成立

1950-04-26
第4版()
专栏：

　　劳动部定明日举行会议
    讨论救济失业工人办法
    京市救济委员会成立
    【新华社讯】中央人民政府劳动部为研讨全国各地失业工人救济办法，定于四月二十七日至二十九日召集失业情况较为严重的上海、南京、武汉、山东、天津等地之劳动局长在北京举行会议。主要讨论内容将为失业工人之救济范围、救济机构、救济金之来源及发放、失业工人之安置等项问题。
    【本报讯】为着有组织有领导地支援上海等地的失业工人，北京市总工会昨日（二十五）上午特邀请市政府、中共北京市委会、华北军区政治部、市劳动局、市公安局、团市工委、市妇联、市教育工作者工会筹委会等单位开会，成立京市救济失业工人委员会。会议决定在自觉自愿的原则下，发动部队、机关人员每人捐助二斤米或一天的薪资（工人的捐献运动已展开）。并决定各单位捐款务须于五月十五日以前缴到，缴款地点决定后，另行在报上发表。
    会议推选萧明（市总工会副主席）、吴晗（京市副市长）、程宏毅（市商业局局长）、赵凡（市委办公室主任）、张致祥（华北军区政治部主任）、万一（市劳动局副局长）、张明河（市公安局副局长）、张晓梅（市妇联主任）、杨伯箴（团市工委副书记）、钱端升（市教育工作者工会筹委会副主任）等十人组成京市救济失业工人委员会；萧明为主任委员。
