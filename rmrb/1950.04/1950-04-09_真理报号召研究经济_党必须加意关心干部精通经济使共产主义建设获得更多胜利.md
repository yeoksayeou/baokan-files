Title: *Pravda* Calls for Economic Study: The Party Must Pay More Attention to Cadres Mastering Economics to Achieve Greater Victories in Communist Construction
Author:
Date: 1950-04-09
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, April 6th (TASS): *Pravda* published an editorial calling for the study of economics, stating: Party organizations must pay more attention to ensuring that Soviet cadres master economics. Profound knowledge of technology, economics, and finance will enable Soviet workers to achieve greater victories in carrying out the practical tasks of communist construction and in increasing the prosperity of the Soviet motherland. The success of economic studies depends primarily on local Party and economic organizations, which must provide appropriate guidance regularly. The more successful these studies are, the more fully production potential can be utilized, and the more closely they can be integrated with the specific practical work of enterprises and the most urgent economic tasks of a given region.

### 真理报号召研究经济  党必须加意关心干部精通经济使共产主义建设获得更多胜利

1950-04-09
第4版()
专栏：

　　真理报号召研究经济
    党必须加意关心干部精通经济使共产主义建设获得更多胜利
    【新华社八日讯】塔斯社莫斯科六日讯：真理报发表社论，号召研究经济称：党的组织必须加意关心使苏维埃干部精通经济。对技术、经济和财政的渊博知识，将使苏维埃工作者在执行共产主义建设中的实践的任务上，在增加苏维埃祖国的繁荣上，获得更多的胜利。经济研究的能否成功，首先取决于地方党组织与经济组织，它们必须经常予以得当的指导。这些研究愈成功，就愈能尽量利用生产潜力，也就愈能与企业的具体实际工作、与一定地区的最急迫的经济任务配合得更加密切。
