Title: Cadres Attending the National United Front Work Conference Warmly Received by Democratic Parties
Author:
Date: 1950-04-06
Page: 1

　　Xinhua News Agency, 5th: The various democratic parties of China jointly hosted a reception at the Beijing Hotel on the 2nd for the cadres in charge of United Front Work from various major administrative regions, provinces, and cities who came to Beijing to attend the National United Front Work Conference convened by the United Front Work Department of the CPC Central Committee. Li Weihan, Minister of the United Front Work Department of the CPC Central Committee, Xing Xiping, Deputy Minister, responsible cadres of the United Front Work from various major administrative regions, provinces, and cities, Li Jishen of the Revolutionary Committee of the Chinese Kuomintang, Zhang Lan and Zhang Bojun of the China Democratic League, Huang Yanpei of the China Democratic National Construction Association, Ma Xulun of the China Association for Promoting Democracy, Peng Zemin of the Chinese Peasants' and Workers' Democratic Party, Yan Xichun of the China Zhi Gong Party, Xu Deheng of the Jiusan Society, Lin Kengsheng of the Taiwan Democratic Self-Government League, and non-party democrats Chen Shutong and Shen Yanbing attended, totaling more than 170 guests and hosts. Li Jishen, Chairman of the Revolutionary Committee of the Chinese Kuomintang, presided over the reception, and Zhang Lan, Chairman of the China Democratic League, delivered a welcome speech. Zhang Zhiyi, Minister of the United Front Work Department of the CPC Central China Bureau, delivered a reply speech at the end. The reception was conducted in an atmosphere full of unity and friendship.

### 出席全国统一战线会议的干部受到民主党派热烈招待

1950-04-06
第1版()
专栏：

　　出席全国统一战线会议的干部受到民主党派热烈招待
    【新华社五日讯】中国各民主党派于二日在首都北京饭店联合招待来京出席中共中央统一战线部召开的全国统一战线工作会议的各大行政区及各省、市统一战线部工作干部。到中共中央统一战线部部长李维汉、副部长邢西屏及各大行政区各省、市统一战线工作负责干部，中国国民党革命委员会李济深、中国民主同盟张澜、章伯钧、中国民主建国会黄炎培、中国民主促进会马叙伦、中国农工民主党彭泽民、中国致公党严希纯、九三学社许德珩、台湾民主自治同盟林铿生以及无党派民主人士陈叔通、沈雁冰等宾主共计一百七十余人。招待会由中国国民党革命委员会主席李济深主席，由中国民主同盟主席张澜致欢迎词。中共中南区统一战线部部长张执一最后致答词。招待会在充满团结友爱的气氛中进行。
