Title: Kangding Celebrates the Liberation of the Entire Province of Xikang with a Grand Gathering; Southwest Military and Administrative Committee Hosts a Banquet for Wangjia and Others
Author:
Date: 1950-04-18
Page: 1

　　Kangding Celebrates the Liberation of the Entire Province of Xikang with a Grand Gathering
    Southwest Military and Administrative Committee Hosts a Banquet for Wangjia and Others
    Xinhua News Agency, Chongqing, 17th: Kangding News: On the 6th of this month, people from all walks of life in Kangding enthusiastically held a grand celebration for the liberation of the entire province of Xikang. Attendees included Han, Yi, Tibetan, Fan, and Hui compatriots, as well as representatives from various organizations, groups, and military units, totaling over 5,000 people. The grand meeting commenced amidst the sound of artillery salutes and music. Fan Zhizhong, deputy division commander of a certain unit of the People's Liberation Army and chairman of the meeting, reported on the significance of the gathering. Subsequently, Zhang Weijiong, vice chairman of the Xikang Provincial People's Government, and representatives from various ethnic groups and sectors delivered speeches. They unanimously expressed their warm congratulations on the liberation of the entire province of Xikang and emphasized the need, under the leadership of the Central People's Government, to strengthen the fraternal unity and mutual assistance among all ethnic groups to build a new Xikang. The meeting concluded at 3:00 PM, followed by a grand parade. When the fully armed and heroic ranks of a certain unit of the People's Liberation Army marched through the streets, people of all ethnic groups were extremely excited, with firecrackers and cheers erupting one after another. Soldiers and civilians jointly performed recreational programs, and compatriots of all ethnic groups happily performed their own ethnic dances, creating an unprecedented spectacle.
    Xinhua News Agency, Chongqing, 17th: The Southwest Military and Administrative Committee hosted a banquet on the 15th to welcome Wangjia, Bozhi, Cai Liang, and other Tibetan representatives from Xikang, who had returned from Beijing. Chairman Liu Bocheng delivered a welcome speech, saying: "The Tibetan people have long suffered from the oppression of imperialism and the Chiang Kai-shek bandit gang. The People's Liberation Army will march into Tibet. We must unite with the Tibetan people to completely eliminate the remaining forces of imperialism and the reactionary rule of the Kuomintang and liberate the Tibetan people." That same evening, the Nationalities Affairs Commission of the Southwest Military and Administrative Committee also held a welcome reception.

### 康定各界举行盛会  庆祝西康全省解放  西南军政委会欢宴旺嘉等

1950-04-18
第1版()
专栏：

　　康定各界举行盛会
    庆祝西康全省解放
    西南军政委会欢宴旺嘉等
    【新华社重庆十七日电】康定讯：康定各界人民于本月六日热烈举行庆祝西康全省解放大会，到会者有汉、彝、藏、番、回各族同胞及各机关、团体、部队达五千余人。盛会在礼炮、乐声中揭幕，大会主席人民解放军某师副师长樊执中报告大会意义后，即由西康省人民政府副主席张为炯及各族、各界代表相继讲话，他们对于西康全省解放，一致表示热烈的庆祝；并强调指出要在中央人民政府领导下，加强各民族之间的兄弟团结互助，建设新西康。大会在下午三时结束后，举行盛大游行。当人民解放军某部全副武装、英姿奕奕的雄壮行列从各街道走过时，各族人民极为兴奋，鞭炮和欢呼声此落彼起。军民共同表演游艺节目，各族同胞快乐地演出自己的民族舞蹈，盛况空前。
    【新华社重庆十七日电】西南军政委员会于十五日设宴欢迎由北京返康抵此的西康藏族代表旺嘉、伯志、蔡良等三人。刘伯承主席致欢迎词说：“藏族人民久受帝国主义和蒋介石匪帮的压迫。人民解放军将进军西藏。我们一定要和藏族人民团结一致，彻底消灭帝国主义和国民党反动统治的残余势力，解放西藏人民。”同日晚，西南军政委员会民族事务委员会并举行欢迎晚会。
