Title: Supervisory Committee and Ministry of Railways Dispatch Investigative Team to Thoroughly Investigate Zhengzhou Water Tower Embezzlement Case
Author:
Date: 1950-04-02
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Kaifeng, 1st. The People's Supervisory Committee of the Government Administration Council's supervisory team and the Ministry of Railways inspection team, totaling twenty-one people, investigating the Zhengzhou Railway Bureau's water tower project embezzlement case, arrived in Zhengzhou on the 23rd and 24th of last month. On the 24th, 26th, and 27th, the supervisory team, together with the Zhengzhou Railway Bureau, respectively convened bureau affairs meetings, engineer symposiums, and a mobilization meeting for the bureau's employees; and has formed a Zhengzhou Bureau Water Tower Incident Investigation Committee to begin work. In addition, all cadres of the Zhengzhou Railway Bureau, from March 25th to 31st, discussed the lessons of the water tower embezzlement incident during the originally scheduled study time each day, and from the 1st, began studying Stalin's "On Self-Criticism" and other documents, preparing to carry out self-criticism from top to bottom, in order to thoroughly investigate water tower project embezzlement and improve the management work of the Zhengzhou Railway Bureau.

### 监察委员会及铁道部派出小组  彻查郑州水塔贪污案

1950-04-02
第1版()
专栏：

　　监察委员会及铁道部派出小组
    彻查郑州水塔贪污案
    【新华社开封一日电】调查郑州铁路局水塔工程贪污案件的政务院人民监察委员会监察小组及铁道部检查组共二十一人已于上月二十三、二十四两日先后到达郑州。在二十四、二十六、二十七三日中监察小组会同郑州路局分别召开了局务会议、工程师座谈会与路局员工动员大会；并已组成了郑局水塔事件调查委员会开始工作。此外，郑州路局全体干部亦自三月二十五起至三十一日止，在每天原定学习时间内，讨论水塔贪污事件的教训，并于一日起学习斯大林《论自我批评》等文件，准备自上而下进行自我批评，以便彻底检查水塔工程贪污，使郑州路局管理工作提高一步。
