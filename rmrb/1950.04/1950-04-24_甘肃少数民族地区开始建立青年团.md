Title: Youth League Construction Begins in Gansu Minority Areas
Author:
Date: 1950-04-24
Page: 3

　　Xinhua News Agency, Lanzhou, April 22nd: New Democratic Youth League construction has begun among various ethnic minorities in parts of Gansu Province. According to statistics from the end of January, a total of 32 Tibetan members and 21 Hui members have been developed in the eight districts of Tianshui, Qingyang, Wudu, Dingxi, Wuwei, Zhangye, Jiuquan, and Linxia. According to statistics from the end of February, in the Tibetan areas of Xiahe County in Linxia District alone, 31 Tibetan members and 1 Hui member have been developed. New Democratic Youth League organizations have also been established in factories, schools, and suburban areas of Lanzhou City. Gansu Provincial Middle School alone has more than 30 ethnic minority members. The Lanzhou Municipal Working Committee of the Youth League and the Lanzhou Municipal Youth Federation have also organized seven people's night schools, with 265 ethnic minority youths and women participating in studies. The organization of the Chinese Young Pioneers is also beginning to be established in ethnic minority primary schools in Lanzhou City.

### 甘肃少数民族地区开始建立青年团

1950-04-24
第3版()
专栏：

　　甘肃少数民族地区开始建立青年团
    【新华社兰州二十二日电】甘肃省部分地区已开始在各少数民族中建立新民主主义青年团。据一月底统计，天水、庆阳、武都、定西、武威、张掖、酒泉、临夏等八个分区共发展藏族团员三十二名，回族团员二十一名。据二月底统计，仅临夏分区夏河县藏族地区，已发展了藏族团员三十一名，回族团员一名。兰州市工厂、学校和郊区亦已建立新民主主义青年团。仅甘肃省立中学就有少数民族团员三十余名。青年团兰州市工作委员会和兰州市青年联合会，并组织了七个民众夜校，有二百六十五名少数民族青年、妇女参加学习。中国少年儿童队的组织也在兰州市各少数民族小学中开始建立。
