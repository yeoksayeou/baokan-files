Title: Four U.S. Military Officers Arrive in Hainan to Help Bandits Struggle

Author:

Date: 1950-04-06
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, 5th – A United Press Hong Kong dispatch on the 4th revealed: Four U.S. Army, Navy, and Air Force officers arrived in Haikou, Hainan Island from Taiwan on the 2nd to assist the bandits Xue Yue and Chen Jitang in their death throes. United Press reported that the four U.S. military officers have already met with Xue Yue and Chen Jitang. The dispatch also revealed that the U.S. "Army Attaché" and "Naval Attaché" had previously traveled from Hong Kong and Taiwan to Hainan Island "in order to assess the possibility of repelling the attack."

### 美国军官四名抵琼助匪挣扎

1950-04-06
第1版()
专栏：

　　美国军官四名抵琼助匪挣扎
    【新华社五日讯】合众社香港四日电透露：美国陆、海、空军军官四人，于二日由台湾抵达海南岛的海口，协助薛岳、陈济棠两匪作垂死挣扎。合众社称：美国四军官已与薛岳、陈济棠会商。该电并透露：美国“陆军参赞”和“海军参赞”，过去曾由香港与台湾前往海南岛，“以便估计击退进攻的可能性”。
