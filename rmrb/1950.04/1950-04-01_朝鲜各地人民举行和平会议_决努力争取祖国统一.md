Title: People in Various Parts of Korea Hold Peace Conferences, Resolve to Strive for Peaceful Reunification of the Fatherland
Author:
Date: 1950-04-01
Page: 4

　　Xinhua News Agency, Pyongyang, 27th (TASS): Workers, farmers, fishermen, staff, students, intellectuals, small merchants, and manufacturers from various parts of Korea are now holding peace conferences one after another to discuss the tasks of fighting for peace and the establishment of local peace committees. The participants unanimously expressed that they would resolutely exert their utmost efforts to strive for the peaceful reunification of the fatherland. They exposed the perfidious tricks of the war instigators headed by the US imperialists.

### 朝鲜各地人民举行和平会议  决努力争取祖国统一

1950-04-01
第4版()
专栏：

　　朝鲜各地人民举行和平会议
    决努力争取祖国统一
    【新华社三十一日讯】塔斯社平壤二十七日讯：朝鲜各地的工人、农民、渔夫、职员、学生、知识分子、小商人及制造家等，现正纷纷举行和平会议，讨论为和平而斗争的任务和建立地方和平委员会的问题。与会者一致表示，他们要坚决地尽最大的努力，以争取祖国的和平统一。他们揭露了以美帝国主义者为首的战争挑拨者的背信弃义的诡计。
