Title: Suiyuan Province Exceeds First Quarter Tax Revenue Target

Author: 
Date: 1950-04-28
Page: 2

　　Xinhua News Agency, 26th: Suiyuan Province has exceeded its tax revenue target for the first quarter. The assigned task for the province's first quarter accounted for 27 percent of the annual task, but the actual completed amount exceeded the original assigned task by 49.6 percent. Among this, commodity tax accounted for 32.8 percent of the completed amount, and other taxes accounted for 66.2 percent.

### 绥远省超额完成首季税收

1950-04-28
第2版()
专栏：

　　绥远省超额完成首季税收
    【新华社二十六日讯】绥远省第一季度税收任务已超额完成。该省第一季度分配的任务数占全年任务百分之二十七，实际完成数超过原分配任务的百分之四十九点六，其中货物税占完成数的百分之三十二点八，其他各项税收占百分之六十六点二。
