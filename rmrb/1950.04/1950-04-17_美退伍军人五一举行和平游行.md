Title: US Veterans to Hold May Day Peace Parade
Author:
Date: 1950-04-17
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency, New York, 14th - Telecommunications: American veterans will wear uniforms to participate in the New York City "May Day" parade to show their solidarity with the people in the struggle for peace. The headquarters of the Labor and People's United Celebration "May Day" Committee announced that veterans of the First World War, soldiers of the International Brigade who fought with the Spanish against Franco fascists, and veterans of the Second World War will participate in this peace parade. At the same time, the "May Day" Committee is actively conducting a campaign to solicit 100,000 signatures on the peace petition passed at the All-American Labor Union's Peace Conference.

### 美退伍军人五一举行和平游行

1950-04-17
第4版()
专栏：

　　美退伍军人五一举行和平游行
    【新华社十六日讯】电通社纽约十四日讯：美国退伍军人将穿着制服参加纽约市“五一”大游行，以表示他们在争取和平的斗争中和人民团结一致。劳工与人民联合庆祝“五一”委员会总部发表消息称：第一次世界大战的退伍军人，曾在西班牙与佛朗哥法西斯分子作战的国际纵队军人，和第二次世界大战的退伍军人，均将参加此项争取和平的游行。与此同时，“五一”委员会正积极进行一项运动，征求十万人在全美工会拥护和平大会上通过的和平请愿书上签名。
