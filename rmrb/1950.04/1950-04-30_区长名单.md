Title: List of District Heads

Author:

Date: 1950-04-30
Page: 4

[News from the Beijing Municipal News Agency] The fifth meeting of the Beijing Municipal People's Government Committee approved the list of district heads and deputy district heads for each district after the adjustment of district boundaries in the urban area as follows:

　　District Head of the First District: Su Jie. District Head of the Second District: Ji Ke, Deputy District Head: Qin An. District Head of the Third District: Fan Zhigang. District Head of the Fourth District: Wei Bin, Deputy District Head: Yang Runwu. District Head of the Fifth District: Zhai Lindong. District Head of the Sixth District: Gao Yunchao, Deputy District Head: Wang Yu. District Head of the Seventh District: Han Linguang. Deputy District Head: Hu Wenrui. District Head of the Eighth District: Jin Yaru, Deputy District Head: Yang Yuzhen. District Head of the Ninth District: Wu Yihang, Deputy District Head: Jia Jingzhong.

### 区长名单

1950-04-30
第4版()
专栏：

　　区长名单
    【京市新闻处讯】北京市人民政府委员会第五次会议通过任命城区调整区划后各区区长、副区长名单如下：
    第一区区长苏捷。第二区区长冀克，副区长秦安。第三区区长樊志刚。第四区区长魏彬，副区长杨润吾。第五区区长翟林栋。第六区区长高云超，副区区长王宇。第七区区长韩林光。副区长胡文瑞。第八区区长金雅如，副区长杨愚震。第九区区长吴一行，副区长贾静中。
