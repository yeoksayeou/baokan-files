Title: Central People's Broadcasting Station Important Program Preview for Next Week
Author:
Date: 1950-04-15
Page: 3
Xinhua News Agency, 13th: The following are the important evening programs of the Central People's Broadcasting Station from April 16th to 22nd:

　　Quyi (Folk Art Forms): Broadcast every evening from 18:00 to 18:20
　　April 16th: Wu Yueqiu sings Tiepian Dagu: "Production and Employment"
　　April 17th: Ma Juquan sings Danxian: "Wu Song Fights the Tiger"
　　April 18th: Guan Xuezeng sings Qinshu: "Good Husband and Wife"
　　April 19th: Li Xinghai sings Leting Dagu: "Don't Be Superstitious"
　　April 20th: Ma Xiaorong sings Zhui Zi: "Three Heroes Push the Boat Across the River"
　　April 21st: Wei Xikui sings Fengtiao Dagu: "Li Dacheng Saves the Fire"
　　April 22nd: Yang Jirui sings Xihe Dagu: "Catching Demons and Evil Spirits"

　　Natural Science Lectures – Broadcast every evening from 18:45 to 19:00:
　　April 17th & 18th: Huang Shuze, Director of the Education Department of the Health Department of the Revolutionary Military Commission, speaks on: "Cough"
　　April 19th: Zhang Jimin of the Science Popularization Bureau of the Ministry of Culture speaks on: "White Rice, White Flour, and Beriberi"
　　April 20th: Lecture Section of this station speaks on: "Soviet Agricultural Machinery"
　　April 21st: Lecture Section of this station speaks on: "Tractors Made by the Lushun-Dalian Labor Reform Vocational Factory"
　　April 22nd: Lecture Section of this station speaks on: "From the Successful Trial Production of Clostridium Perfringens Vaccine to the Prevention and Control of Rinderpest"

　　Social Science Lectures – Broadcast every evening from 19:00 to 20:00:
　　April 17th: Ai Siqi speaks on: "The Laboring Masses Create History" (History of Social Development, Chapter 2, Lecture 1)
　　April 18th: Ai Siqi speaks on: "Productive Forces and Relations of Production" (History of Social Development, Chapter 2, Lecture 2)
　　April 19th: Ai Siqi speaks on: "Five Modes of Production (Part 1)" (History of Social Development, Chapter 2, Lecture 3)
　　April 20th: Yu Guangyuan speaks on: "Value" (Political Economy, Lecture 3)
　　April 21st: Yu Guangyuan speaks on: "The Law of Value" (Political Economy, Lecture 4)
　　April 22nd: Soviet Economics Master Associate Professor Baudlev speaks on: "The Financial System of the Soviet State"

　　People's Forum: Every evening from 20:00 to 20:30
　　April 16th: Zhao Guilan, a female worker and Communist Party member from Dalian Jianxin Factory, speaks on: "I Vow to Serve the People to the End." (Recording)
　　April 17th: Yang Enfu, Researcher at the Central Institute of Hygiene Experiments, speaks on: "Recent Overview of Food Processing in Various Countries."
　　April 18th: (1) Broadcast of the article "Overseas Chinese in America Suffer Grievous Discrimination" by He Zhuofeng, an overseas Chinese from the United States;
　　(2) Chen Xuan, Preparatory Committee Member of the Beijing Returned Overseas Chinese Association and former reporter for the Philippine Vanguard, speaks on: "The Persecution of Overseas Chinese in the Philippines."
　　April 19th: (1) Introduction to the Shenyang Factory Youth League in the New Record Creation Movement;
　　(2) Introduction to the Liulangzhuang Rural Youth League in the Production Movement, a suburb of Beijing.
　　April 20th: (1) Broadcast of the article "Indonesia and Indonesian Overseas Chinese" by Fei Zhendong, Director of the Cultural and Educational Propaganda Department of the Overseas Chinese Affairs Commission;
　　(2) Xiao Gang, former Director of the Myanmar Branch of the China Democratic League and Editor-in-Chief of the Yangon People's Daily, speaks on: "The Situation of Overseas Chinese in Myanmar."
　　April 21st: (1) Broadcast of the article "Overseas Chinese in Vietnam Under the Trampling of French Imperialism" by Chen Feng, an overseas Chinese who recently returned from Vietnam;
　　(2) A listener's opinion on implementing "Work Standardization."
　　April 22nd: A listener's letter exposing the broadcasts of the remnant bandits.

　　Art and Literature Program: Broadcast every evening from 20:30 to 21:00
　　April 16th: Radio drama. Broadcast Art Troupe performance: "Pan Xiuzhi" (Today's Sunday Art Program is added, 18:45–20:00 Broadcasting a recording of the Central Conservatory of Music's music evening, including Ma Sicong violin solo, Guan Yu Xuanxuan solo, Jiang Fengzhi erhu solo, Li Huanzhi flute solo, Zhang Lu solo).
　　April 17th: Music Program. Central Academy of Drama Orchestra performance:
　　(1) Dance Music: "Children's Play" (2) Orchestral Music: "Shaanxi Suite, Second Movement."
　　April 18th: Soviet Songs.
　　April 19th: Film Introduction Recording. Northeast Film Studio Feature Film: "Boundless Light."
　　April 20th: Music Program. Broadcast Art Troupe sings: New Songs, Folk Songs; plays instrumental music: Folk Ensemble.
　　April 21st: Western Music. Introduction to Bach and his works: (1) D Major "Toccata" and "Fugue"; (2) Air on the G String.
　　April 22nd: Literary Works Recitation. Writer Lao She recites a section from the novel "Rickshaw Boy": Xiangzi in the Tea House.

　　Fifth News Broadcast: Every evening from 21:00 to 21:15
　　Commentary: Every evening from 21:15 to 21:30
　　Sixth News Broadcast – International News: Every evening from 21:30 to 21:45
　　Local News and Character Stories: Every evening from 21:45 to 22:00
　　April 17th, 19th, 21st, 22nd: Broadcast of the story of the female hero Liu Hulan.
　　April 16th, 18th, 20th: Broadcast of local news.

　　Art and Literature Program: Broadcast every evening from 22:00 to 22:30
　　April 16th: Amateur National Music Research Society plays Cantonese music and silk and bamboo music.
　　April 17th to 20th: Zhao Yingpo tells the Ping Shu story: "Jin Baoniang"
　　April 21st and 22nd: The Beijing Opera Experimental School of the Ministry of Culture's Opera Improvement Bureau performs the new Beijing Opera: "Da Cao Gu (Seizing Grain in the Fields)"

　　Seventh News Broadcast: Every evening from 22:30 to 22:45
　　Music - Closing Music: Every evening from 22:45 to 23:00
　　Recorded News: Every evening from 23:00 to 0:30
