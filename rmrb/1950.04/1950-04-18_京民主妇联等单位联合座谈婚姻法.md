Title: Beijing Democratic Women's Federation and other units jointly hold forum on Marriage Law
Author:
Date: 1950-04-18
Page: 4

　　Beijing Democratic Women's Federation and other units jointly hold forum on Marriage Law
    [From Our Newspaper] After the promulgation of the Marriage Law of the People's Republic of China, the Beijing Democratic Women's Federation, the Beijing Federation of Trade Unions, the Beijing Working Committee of the New Democratic Youth League, and the Preparatory Committee of the Beijing Democratic Youth Federation are scheduled to jointly invite responsible persons from various people's organizations in the city to hold a forum at 9:00 AM today (the 18th), and have invited relevant heads of agencies and experts to attend.
    [Another Dispatch] In order to do a good job in publicizing the Marriage Law, the Beijing Democratic Women's Federation invited Meng Qingshu, a member of the Legislative Affairs Committee of the Central People's Government, to talk about the basic spirit of the Marriage Law at 4:00 PM yesterday (the 17th).
                                            (City Women's Federation Communication)

### 京民主妇联等单位联合座谈婚姻法

1950-04-18
第4版()
专栏：

　　京民主妇联等单位联合座谈婚姻法
    【本报讯】中华人民共和国婚姻法公布后、北京市民主妇女联合会、北京市总工会、新民主主义青年团北京市工作委员会和北京市民主青年联合会筹委会等单位定于今（十八）日上午九时联合邀请本市各人民团体负责人举行座谈会，并邀请了有关机关首长及专家出席。
    【又讯】北京市民主妇女联合会为了作好关于婚姻法的宣传工作，昨（十七）日下午四时邀请中央人民政府法制委员会委员孟庆澍讲述婚姻法的基本精神。
                                            （市妇联通讯）
