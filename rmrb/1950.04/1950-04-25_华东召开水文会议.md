Title: East China Holds Hydrology Conference
Author:
Date: 1950-04-25
Page: 2
Xinhua News Agency, Shanghai, 19th. The East China Military and Political Committee's Water Conservancy Department convened the East China Hydrology Conference in Nanjing from April 5th to 13th. Li Baohua and Zhang Hanying, both Deputy Ministers of the Central Water Conservancy Department, were present to provide guidance. The conference decided to establish 167 hydrological stations throughout the East China region and plans to establish experimental stations in Nanjing, Haining, Jinshanwei, and other six locations in succession to facilitate the construction of water conservancy projects.

### 华东召开水文会议

1950-04-25
第2版()
专栏：

　　华东召开水文会议
    【新华社上海十九日电】华东军政委员会水利部于四月五日至十三日在宁召开华东水文会议。中央水利部李葆华、张含英两副部长亲临指导。会议决定全华东区设立一百六十七个水文站，并拟在南京、海宁、金山嘴等六地陆续设立实验站，以利水利工程的建设。
