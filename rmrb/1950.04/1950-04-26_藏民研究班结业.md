Title: Tibetan Cadre Training Class Graduates
Author:
Date: 1950-04-26
Page: 3

Xinhua News Agency, April 24th: The Tibetan Cadre Training Class, organized by the Ethnic Affairs Commission of the Central People's Government, held its graduation ceremony on the 23rd of this month. Lin Boqu, Secretary-General of the Central People's Government, and Ulanhu and Liu Geping, Deputy Chairmen of the Ethnic Affairs Commission, all attended and spoke, unanimously encouraging all students to be unafraid of difficulties, abide by discipline, and constantly improve themselves in their work after graduation. The class began on February 1st of this year, and its main task was to cultivate and educate Tibetan cadres.

### 藏民研究班结业

1950-04-26
第3版()
专栏：

　　藏民研究班结业
    【新华社二十四日讯】中央人民政府民族事务委员会举办的藏民研究班，已于本月二十三日举行毕业典礼。中央人民政府秘书长林伯渠、民族事务委员会副主任委员乌兰夫、刘格平等均出席讲话，一致勉励全体学员毕业后在工作中要不怕困难，遵守纪律，不断提高自己。按该班系于今年二月一日开学，主要任务为培养和教育藏族人民干部。
