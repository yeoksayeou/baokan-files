Title: First Meeting of the National Committee of the China Postal and Telecommunications Workers' Union Elects Chairman and Standing Committee Members
Author:
Date: 1950-04-02
Page: 2

Xinhua News Agency:

    China Postal and Telecommunications Workers' Union
    First Meeting of the National Committee Elects Chairman and Standing Committee Members

    [Xinhua News Agency] The China Postal and Telecommunications Workers' Union held its first meeting of the National Committee on March 30th, and elected Liu Yin as Chairman, and Li Jinghan, Lu Xiangxian, and Xu Xin (female) as Vice-Chairmen. The Standing Committee members are Li Jinghan, Wang Xixian, Song Fengxiang, Li Guangyu, Zhou Ying, Liu Yin, Wang Zhonggang, Wang Junfa, Xu Xin, Xiang Minghua, Hu Jiacong, Meng Jinsheng, Kong Xianpu, Lu Xiangxian, Yang Daoxin, Zhang Enquan, Qiu Guanjin, Xu Yishan, and Gao Huantu, totaling nineteen people. On the same day, the Funding Review Committee held its first meeting and elected Zhang Guangdai and Yang Zaoxin as Chairman and Vice-Chairman, respectively. The above lists have been approved by the 27th enlarged meeting of the Standing Committee of the All-China Federation of Trade Unions. The list of the National Committee and the Funding Review Committee of the China Postal and Telecommunications Workers' Union is as follows:
