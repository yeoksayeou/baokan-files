Title: Bandits Frenziedly Persecute the People, Arresting 5,000 Taiwanese Compatriots
Author:
Date: 1950-04-12
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, 8th. The Kuomintang remnant bandits, with their backs to the wall, are frenziedly persecuting the people of Taiwan. According to a United Press report from Hong Kong on the 6th, citing the Overseas Chinese Daily in Taipei, the pseudo-Ministry of National Defense Confidential Bureau (i.e., the Military Command) has already arrested 5,000 so-called "spies and suspects."

### 匪疯狂迫害人民  逮捕台胞五千人

1950-04-12
第1版()
专栏：

　　匪疯狂迫害人民  逮捕台胞五千人
    【新华社八日讯】日暮途穷的国民党残匪正疯狂迫害台湾人民。据美联社香港六日电引华侨日报台北消息，伪国防部保密局（即军统）已经逮捕了五千名所谓“间谍和嫌疑犯”。
