Title: Chinese Delegation Leaves Beijing Yesterday to Attend "May Day" Celebrations in Soviet Union and Poland
Author:
Date: 1950-04-25
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, April 24th:
　　The Chinese delegation, invited to go to the Soviet Union and Poland to participate in the "May Day" celebration, left Beijing this morning to begin its journey abroad. The representatives going to the Soviet Union to participate in the "May Day" celebrations are: Liu Ningyi, Wang Yiqing (All-China Federation of Trade Unions), Zhang Qi (Shanghai Federation of Trade Unions), Jin Ziming (Nanjing Federation of Trade Unions), Xu Xin (female, China Postal and Telecommunications Workers' Union), Hang Huilan (female cotton mill worker), Zhao Guoyou (Labor Hero), Wang Wenbin (Deputy Director of Beijing Electric Power Plant), and Zhang Fang (Engineer). The representative going to Poland is Qiu Jin (Tianjin Federation of Trade Unions).

### 赴苏波参加“五一”庆祝会  我国代表团昨离京

1950-04-25
第1版()
专栏：

　　赴苏波参加“五一”庆祝会
    我国代表团昨离京
    【新华社二十四日讯】应邀前往苏联与波兰参加“五一”庆祝大会的我国代表团，已于今晨离京首途出国。前往苏联参加“五一”节庆祝会的代表为：刘宁一、王亦清（中华全国总工会）、张祺（上海总工会）、金子明（南京总工会），徐欣（女，中国邮电工会）、杭惠兰（纱厂女工）、赵国有（劳动英雄）、王文斌（北京电业厂副厂长）、张方（工程师）。前往波兰之代表为丘金（天津总工会）。
