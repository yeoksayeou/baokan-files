Title: Which Countries Have Established Diplomatic Relations with Our Country?
Author: Bai Zhenwu
Date: 1950-04-24
Page: 6

To the Editor:

　　Which Countries Have Established Diplomatic Relations with Our Country?
　　Dear Editor:
　　1. Which countries have established diplomatic relations with our country, and when were they established? Which other countries are willing to establish diplomatic relations with our country?
　　2. Who are the diplomatic envoys of various countries stationed in our country? Who are the envoys of our country stationed abroad?
　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　Bai Zhenwu

Comrade Bai Zhenwu:
