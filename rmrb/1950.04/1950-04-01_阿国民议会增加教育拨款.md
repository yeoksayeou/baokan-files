Title: Albanian National Assembly Increases Education Funding
Author:
Date: 1950-04-01
Page: 4

　　Tirana, TASS dispatch, March 31st: The Albanian National Assembly passed a new budget for public education in the middle of this month. The allocation for education funding increased by 142 percent compared to last year. The number of primary schools nationwide will increase from 2,130 to 2,190. Last year, the total number of primary school students was 186,328, and this year it will increase to 200,217. Last year, the total number of middle school students was 5,186, and this year it will be 6,687. Last year, there were a total of 2,180 boarding schools, and this year there will be 3,227.

### 阿国民议会增加教育拨款

1950-04-01
第4版()
专栏：

　　阿国民议会增加教育拨款
    【新华社三十一日讯】塔斯社地拉那讯：本月中旬，阿尔巴尼亚国民议会通过公共教育新预算，教育经费的拨款较去年增加了百分之一百四十二。全国小学校的数目，将自二千一百三十所增加到二千一百九十所。去年，小学生的总数是十八万六千三百二十八人，本年将增加到二十万零二千二百十七人。去年，中学生的总数是五千一百八十六人，本年将为六千六百八十七人。去年共有二千一百八十所寄宿学校，本年将增为三千二百二十七所。
