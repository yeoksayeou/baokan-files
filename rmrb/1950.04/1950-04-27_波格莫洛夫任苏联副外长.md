Title: Bogomolov Appointed Soviet Deputy Foreign Minister
Author:
Date: 1950-04-27
Page: 4

　　Bogomolov Appointed Soviet Deputy Foreign Minister

    Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, April 26th: TASS, Moscow, April 24th: The Soviet Council of Ministers has recently appointed Alexander Bogomolov, former ambassador to France, as Deputy Minister of Foreign Affairs of the Soviet Union.
    The Presidium of the Supreme Soviet of the Soviet Union appointed Alexei Pavlov, former ambassador to Belgium and minister to Luxembourg, as Ambassador Extraordinary and Plenipotentiary to France.

### 波格莫洛夫任苏联副外长

1950-04-27
第4版()
专栏：

　　波格莫洛夫任苏联副外长
    【新华社二十六日讯】塔斯社莫斯科二十四日讯：苏联部长会议顷任命前驻法大使亚力山大·波格莫洛夫为苏联外交部副部长。
    苏联最高苏维埃主席团任命前驻比利时大使兼驻卢森堡公使阿列克赛·巴夫洛夫为驻法国特命全权大使。
