Title: Twelve Trade Union Internationals Scheduled to Meet in Hungary Next Month
Author:
Date: 1950-04-14
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency, Budapest, 13th: TASS, Budapest, 13th—The leaders of twelve Trade Union Internationals (industrial departments of the World Federation of Trade Unions) will hold a meeting in Budapest from May 10th to 24th. The Executive Bureau of the World Federation of Trade Unions will also hold a meeting, followed by a meeting of the Executive Committee of the World Federation of Trade Unions.

Trade union representatives from the Soviet Union, China, France, Italy, Poland, Czechoslovakia, Hungary, Romania, Bulgaria, Albania, the German Democratic Republic, and other countries will participate in the work of the Executive Committee meeting of the World Federation of Trade Unions.

### 十二个工会国际定下月在匈开会

1950-04-14
第4版()
专栏：

　　十二个工会国际定下月在匈开会
    【新华社十三日讯】塔斯社布达佩斯十三日讯：十二个工会国际（世界工联各产业部）的领导者，将于五月十日至二十四日在布达佩斯举行会议。世界工联执行局亦将举行会议，随后又将召开世界工联执委会会议。
    苏、中、法、意、波、捷、匈、罗、保、阿尔巴尼亚、德意志民主共和国及其他国家的工会代表，将参加世界工联执委会会议的工作。
