Title: US Aircraft Invades Swedish Neutrality, Swedish Public Extremely Indignant, Newspapers Prominently Publish Soviet Protest
Author:
Date: 1950-04-14
Page: 4

　　Xinhua News Agency, Stockholm, April 13th: TASS, Stockholm, April 13th: All newspapers published the full text of the Soviet government's protest against the US aircraft invading the Soviet border on the front page with headline titles.

　　New Era newspaper specially carried the Soviet government's protest under the headline "US Navy Maneuvers in the Baltic Sea - Deliberate Provocation."

　　Stockholm News' headline was: "Lost US Aircraft Attacked by Soviet Fighters."

　　New Era commented on the Soviet government's protest in an editorial, saying: The United States can now believe that "the Soviet border is safely guarded."

　　Social Democratic and bourgeois newspapers mostly published reports from American and Western European news agencies about Washington's "response" to the Soviet government's protest.

　　Xinhua News Agency, Oslo, April 13th: TASS, Oslo, April 13th: Local newspapers published the Soviet government's note of protest to the US government regarding the US military aircraft's invasion of Soviet airspace in large headlines and in prominent positions. The Morgenpost editorial stated: "If either side or both sides deliberately hold their ground, this matter will have serious consequences. However, it can be expected that if the US government finds that its aircraft did fly over Soviet airspace as confirmed, then it will apologize." The newspaper also pointed out: Not only communists, but also many others, believe that the United States is using this opportunity to conduct a thorough study of the Baltic Sea region and the Soviet naval strength in the region. The Swedish public expressed indignation at the US military aircraft violating Swedish neutrality and flying over the Swedish Karlskrona naval base.

### 美机侵犯瑞典中立  瑞典公众极为激愤  各报显著刊载苏对美抗议

1950-04-14
第4版()
专栏：

　　美机侵犯瑞典中立  瑞典公众极为激愤
    各报显著刊载苏对美抗议
    【新华社十三日讯】塔斯社斯德哥尔摩十三日讯：所有报纸均以头条标题在第一版上刊登苏联政府关于美国飞机侵犯苏联边境的抗议的全文。
    新时代报在“美国海军在波罗的海操演——蓄意的挑衅”的头条标题下特载了苏联政府的抗议。
    斯德哥尔摩新闻的标题是：“迷失方向的美国飞机遭到苏联战斗机的攻击”。
    新时代报于社论中评论苏联政府的抗议称：美国现在可以相信“苏联的边境是安全地警卫着”了。
    社会民主党和资产阶级的报纸，大部分刊载美国和西欧诸通讯社关于华盛顿对苏联政府抗议的“反响”的报道。
    【新华社十三日讯】塔斯社奥斯陆十三日讯：此间各报以大字标题，在极显著地位刊登苏联政府就美国军用飞机侵犯苏联领空事向美国政府提出的抗议照会。《晨邮报》社论称：“假如任何一方或双方都蓄意相持不下，这个事体就会有严重的后果。但是，可以预期，美国政府如果发现其飞机确如所证实的那样，飞临苏联领空，那末，它将会道歉。”报纸同时指出：不仅是共产党，就是其他许多人，都以为美国在利用这个机会，对波罗的海区域和该区的苏联海军实力，作一番彻底的研究。瑞典公众对于美国军用飞机侵犯瑞典的中立，而飞越瑞典的卡尔斯克伦纳海军基地，表示激愤。
