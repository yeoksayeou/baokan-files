Title: Celebrating the Fifth Anniversary of the Liberation of Hungary: Photo Exhibition Held in Zhongshan Park
Author:
Date: 1950-04-16
Page: 4

　　Celebrating the Fifth Anniversary of the Liberation of Hungary
　　Zhongshan Park Holds Photo Exhibition
　　[Our Newspaper] To celebrate the fifth anniversary of the liberation of Hungary, the Hungarian Embassy in China held the opening ceremony of the Hungarian Photo Exhibition at the Waterside Pavilion in Zhongshan Park at 3:00 pm yesterday (the 15th). Chinese government leaders, foreign envoys, and people from all walks of life attended. The photo exhibition will be officially held from today (the 16th) to the 21st. The location is the Waterside Pavilion in Zhongshan Park. The hours are from 9:00 am to 5:00 pm daily. The content includes Hungary's construction situation in the past five years, the friendship of the Soviet Union, the Three-Year Plan to the Five-Year Plan, and more. All are welcome to visit.
　　(Foreign Cultural Liaison Bureau of the Ministry of Culture of the Central People's Government)

### 庆祝匈牙利解放五周年  中山公园举行图片展览

1950-04-16
第4版()
专栏：

　　庆祝匈牙利解放五周年
    中山公园举行图片展览
    【本报讯】为庆祝匈牙利解放五周年，匈牙利驻我国公使馆在昨（十五）日下午三时假中山公园水榭举行匈牙利图片展览揭幕式，有我国政府首长、各国使节及各界人士参加。该项图片展览将由今（十六）日起至二十一日止正式举行。地点在中山公园水榭。时间，每日自上午九时至下午五时。内容包括匈牙利五年来的建设情形、苏联的友谊、三年计划到五年计划等多种。欢迎各界前往参观。
                  （中央人民政府文化部对外文化联络事务局）
