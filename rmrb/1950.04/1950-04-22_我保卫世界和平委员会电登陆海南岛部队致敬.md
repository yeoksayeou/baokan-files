Title: China Peace Committee Telegraphs Congratulations to Troops Landing on Hainan Island
Author:
Date: 1950-04-22
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, April 20th: The Second Meeting of the China Committee for the Defence of World Peace telegraphed its congratulations to all commanders and fighters of the People's Liberation Army Hainan Front Troops who victoriously landed on Hainan Island. The telegram reads as follows:
    Comrades Commanders and Fighters of the Hainan Island Front:
    We are gathered in Beijing on April 20th to commemorate the first anniversary of the World Peace Congress, and we are thrilled to hear the news of your victorious landing on Hainan Island. This great success not only expands the victory of the Chinese People's Revolution, but also consolidates the foundation for defending world peace. We extend our highest respects to you, and wish you a swift, thorough, and complete annihilation of all Kuomintang remnant bandits who refuse to surrender, and the completion of the glorious task of liberating Hainan Island.

          China Committee for the Defence of World Peace
                April 20th, 1950

### 我保卫世界和平委员会电登陆海南岛部队致敬

1950-04-22
第4版()
专栏：

　　我保卫世界和平委员会
    电登陆海南岛部队致敬
    【新华社二十日讯】中国保卫世界和平大会委员会第二次会议，通电向胜利登陆海南岛的人民解放军海南前线部队全体指战员致敬。电文如下：海南岛前线全体指战员同志们：
    我们于四月二十日在北京集会纪念世界拥护和平大会一周年，欣闻你们胜利登陆海南岛的消息，全场振奋。这一伟大成功，不仅扩大了中国人民革命的胜利，并巩固了保卫世界和平的基础。谨向你们致崇高的敬礼，并预祝你们迅速、彻底、全部歼灭一切不投降的国民党残余匪军，完成解放海南岛的光荣任务。
          中国保卫世界和平大会委员会
                一九五○年四月二十日
