Title: French General Confederation of Labor Issues Resolution, Calls on French Workers to Strive for Peace and Fight for "Declaring the Atomic Bomb Illegal"
Author:
Date: 1950-04-02
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, April 1st, TASS, Paris, March 31st: The Administrative Committee of the French General Confederation of Labor (CGT) has issued a resolution on the struggle of French workers for peace. The resolution first congratulates the French dockworkers for their heroic and persistent actions in defending peace by opposing the shipment of war materials that prolong the Vietnam War and opposing the unloading of American munitions intended for aggressive war. The resolution also calls on the French working people to strengthen the struggle against the production of war materials and to strive for the production of civilian goods.

The Administrative Committee of the CGT fully endorses the resolutions of the Standing Committee of the World Congress of Supporters of Peace and urges activists among the union members to make every effort to mobilize the peace committees in various enterprises to fight for "declaring the atomic bomb illegal."

### 法总工会发布决定  号召法工人争取和平  为“宣布原子弹非法”而斗争

1950-04-02
第4版()
专栏：

　　法总工会发布决定
    号召法工人争取和平
    为“宣布原子弹非法”而斗争
    【新华社北京一日电】塔斯社巴黎三月三十一日讯：法国总工会行政委员会发布关于法国工人争取和平斗争的决定。决定首先向法国码头工人反对装运延续越南战争的战争物资，反对卸运准备侵略战争所用的美国军火的英勇不懈的保卫和平的行为，表示祝贺。决定并号召法国劳动人民加强反对生产战争物资和争取生产民用货品的斗争。
    总工会行政委员会完全赞成世界拥护和平大会常设委员会的决议，并敦促各工会会员中的积极分子，尽力动员各企业中的和平委员会，为“宣布原子弹非法”而斗争。
