Title: Lecture Announcement: Xiao San Speaks on Defending Peace
Author:
Date: 1950-04-22
Page: 4

　　Lecture Announcement
    Xiao San Speaks on Defending Peace
    [Beijing News Agency Dispatch] The Beijing Sino-Soviet Friendship Association is scheduled to hold a lecture on "Defending Peace" at the Beijing Soviet Foreign Cultural Association, 19 Nanheyan Street, at 7:00 PM on Sunday, April 23rd. Xiao San, a member of the Standing Committee of the World Peace Congress, will speak on "The New Stage of Defending World Peace". All those holding admission tickets or "Soviet Introduction" lecture series long-term listening passes are welcome to attend.
              (Beijing Sino-Soviet Friendship Association)

### 讲演预告  萧三讲保卫和平

1950-04-22
第4版()
专栏：

　　讲演预告
    萧三讲保卫和平
    【京市新闻处讯】北京市中苏友好协会定于四月二十三日（星期日）下午七时在南河沿十九号北京苏联对外文化协会举办“保卫和平”讲演会，请世界保卫和平大会常委会委员萧三讲“保卫世界和平的新阶段”。凡持有入场券或“苏联介绍”讲座长期听讲证者，皆可前往参加。
              （北京市中苏友好协会）
