Title: Polish Minister of Internal Affairs Issues Order to Cancel Retail Trade Restrictions
Author:
Date: 1950-04-11
Page: 4

　　Xinhua News Agency, Warsaw, 10th: TASS, Warsaw, 9th: The Polish Minister of Internal Affairs recently issued an order: to cancel all restrictions on retail trade and to implement a free sales system for all goods in state-owned stores and cooperative stores.

### 波兰内政部长颁令取消零售贸易限制

1950-04-11
第4版()
专栏：

　　波兰内政部长颁令取消零售贸易限制
    【新华社十日讯】塔斯社华沙九日讯：波兰内政部长顷颁发一项命令：取消零售贸易的一切限制，并在国营商店与合作社商店中实行自由出售一切货物的制度。
