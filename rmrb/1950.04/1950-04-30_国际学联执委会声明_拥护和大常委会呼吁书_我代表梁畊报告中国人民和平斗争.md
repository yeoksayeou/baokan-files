Title: Executive Committee Statement of the International Union of Students Endorses the Appeal of the Standing Committee of the World Peace Congress. I Represent Liang Geng's Report on the Chinese People's Struggle for Peace

Author: 
Date: 1950-04-30
Page: 4

(Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, 28th) A summary of TASS Moscow dispatches: The Executive Committee meeting of the International Union of Students, which opened in Moscow on the 24th, issued a statement on the 26th, announcing its full endorsement of the appeal of the Standing Committee of the World Peace Congress. All the representatives attending the meeting signed the appeal at that time.

The agenda of the Executive Committee on the 25th and the morning of the 26th was to discuss the report by International Union of Students President Grohman on the tasks of the International Union of Students and student organizations of various countries in the struggle for peace. Speakers at the meeting included U.S. representative Hull, Bulgarian representative Georgieva, Chinese representative Liang Geng, Soviet representative Shelepin, British representative Rust, Australian representative Abels, and World Federation of Democratic Youth Secretary Williams, who was specially invited to attend the meeting.

In his speech, Liang Geng detailed the great victory of the Chinese people and pointed out that the struggle for peace is closely linked to the construction of the new China. Therefore, Chinese students will spare no effort to strengthen their People's Republic.

On the morning of the 26th, Grohman made a concluding speech. He pointed out that the members of the Executive Committee bear a great responsibility to the student organizations in their respective countries. The International Union of Students has a responsibility to constantly support all upright students who express support for the peace camp. The Executive Committee of the International Union of Students must now focus its attention on the preparations for the Second Congress of the International Union of Students, scheduled to be held in Prague in August this year. This congress should be held under the banner of the struggle for world peace.

### 国际学联执委会声明  拥护和大常委会呼吁书  我代表梁畊报告中国人民和平斗争

1950-04-30
第4版()
专栏：

　　国际学联执委会声明
    拥护和大常委会呼吁书
    我代表梁畊报告中国人民和平斗争
    【新华社二十八日讯】综合塔斯社莫斯科消息：二十四日在莫斯科开幕的国际学生联合会执行委员会会议，于二十六日发表声明，宣布完全拥护世界拥护和平大会常设委员会的呼吁书。出席会议的全体代表，均于当时在呼吁书上签名。
    执委会二十五日与二十六日上午的议程，是讨论国际学联主席格罗曼关于国际学联与各国学生组织在争取和平斗争中之任务的报告。在会上发言的有美国代表胡尔、保加利亚代表格奥尔卡娃、中国代表梁畊、苏联代表谢利平、英国代表鲁斯特、澳大利亚代表阿贝尔斯与为会议特别邀请参加的世界民主青联书记威廉士等。
    梁畊在发言中详细叙述了中国人民的伟大胜利，并指出争取和平的斗争是和新中国的建设紧密联系在一起的。因此，中国学生将不遗余力地来加强他们的人民共和国。
    二十六日上午，格罗曼作总结发言。他指出：执委会委员对他们各该国家的学生组织负有巨大责任。国际学联有责任经常支援一切表示支持和平阵营的正直学生。国际学联执委会现在必须集中注意定于今年八月在布拉格举行的国际学联第二届代表大会的筹备工作。这次大会应该在为世界和平而斗争的旗帜下举行。
