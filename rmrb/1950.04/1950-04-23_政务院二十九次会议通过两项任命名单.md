Title: The 29th Meeting of the Government Administration Council Passed Two Lists of Appointments
Author:
Date: 1950-04-23
Page: 3

Xinhua News Agency, 21st: The 29th Government Administration Council meeting of the Central People's Government passed the following two lists of appointments:

　　I. List of Deputy Directors of the Chemical Industry Bureau of the Ministry of Heavy Industry of the Central People's Government
　　Deputy Director: Li Su (formerly Director of the Preparatory Office of the North China Chemical Industry Company of the Ministry of Heavy Industry of the Central People's Government).

　　II. List of Mayor, Deputy Mayor, and Committee Members of the Datong Municipal People's Government of Chahar Province
　　Mayor: Zhao Han (formerly Mayor of the Datong Municipal People's Government of Chahar Province).
　　Deputy Mayor: Li Tiesheng (formerly Deputy Mayor of the Datong Municipal People's Government of Chahar Province).
　　Committee Members: Thirteen people (in order of the number of strokes in their surnames)
　　　　Wang Yiming: Currently Director of the Public Security Bureau of the Datong Municipal People's Government of Chahar Province.
　　　　Wang Renshan: Currently Secretary-General of the Datong Municipal People's Government of Chahar Province.
　　　　An Maohua: Currently Chief of the Security Section of the Datong Mining Bureau of Chahar Province.
　　　　Wu Xiaoshan: Educator.
　　　　Wu Huanlin: Currently Captain of the Pingwang Power Plant Engineering Team of Datong City, Chahar Province.
　　　　Song Xinbo: Currently Chairman of the Datong Municipal Federation of Trade Unions of Chahar Province.
　　　　Song Baoliang: Coal miner.
　　　　Wu Dejiu: Currently Commander of the Yanbei Military Sub-district of the Chahar Military Region of the People's Liberation Army.
　　　　Qi Letian: Formerly Principal of the Fifth Women's Normal School of Datong City, Shanxi Province.
　　　　Xu Zhiyuan: Currently Director of the Kouquan Mining Area Office of the Datong Municipal People's Government of Chahar Province.
　　　　Dong Shangyun: Currently Manager of the Tongchang Iron Factory of Datong City, Chahar Province.
　　　　Liu Chun: Currently Director of the Industry and Commerce Bureau of the Datong Municipal People's Government of Chahar Province.
　　　　Lan Xiaotang: Gentry.
