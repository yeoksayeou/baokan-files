Title: Central Committee of the All-Union Communist Party (Bolsheviks) Slogans for the May Day Celebration
Author: 
Date: 1950-04-29
Page: 4

　　Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, 25th (TASS dispatch): The Central Committee of the Communist Party of the Soviet Union (Bolsheviks) has issued the following slogans for the May Day celebration of 1950:

　　1. Long live May Day – the festival of the unity of working people all over the world, the festival of the fraternal solidarity of workers of all countries!

　　2. Fraternal greetings to the peoples of all countries fighting for peace, democracy, and socialism!

　　3. Fraternal greetings to the People's Democracies, which are following the road of socialist construction!

　　4. Long live the great Chinese people, who have won a momentous victory in the struggle against imperialism and its Kuomintang lackeys!

　　5. Long live the Communist Party of China, which has opened the road to socialism for the Chinese people!

　　6. Greetings to the peoples of Yugoslavia, who are waging a liberation struggle against the fascist Tito clique and the henchmen of imperialism!

　　7. May a united, independent, democratic, and peace-loving Germany live long and prosper!

　　8. Working people of all countries! Expose the sinister schemes of the warmongers! Expand and strengthen the mighty front of peace supporters! The defense of peace is the cause of all people throughout the world!

　　9. Long live the friendship of the peoples of Great Britain, the United States, and the Soviet Union in the struggle for world peace!

　　10. Fraternal greetings to the peoples of the colonies and dependent countries fighting for freedom and independence!

　　11. Glory to the Soviet Armed Forces, guardians of peace and the security of our Motherland!

　　12. Long live the Soviet border guards – the vigilant guardians of the sacred frontiers of our Motherland!

　　13. Glory to the finest sons and daughters of our Motherland – the Heroes of the Soviet Union and the Heroes of Socialist Labor!

　　14. Workers, peasants, and intellectuals of the Soviet Union! Further expand the socialist competition to complete the 1950 national economic plan ahead of schedule!

　　15. Working people of the Soviet Union! Fight for the further advancement of technology in industry, transportation, and agriculture! Adopt new techniques and perfect production technology! Constantly increase labor productivity, which is the basis for further reducing the prices of consumer goods!

　　16. Male and female workers, engineers, and technicians! Strive for better utilization of fixed and working capital! Fight to maximize the saving of raw materials and other materials, fuel, and electricity! Reduce production costs and improve product quality!

　　17. Male and female workers, engineers, and technicians of the coal mining industry! Work for the complete mechanization of the entire coal mining process! Accelerate the construction of new mines! Produce more coal for our national economy!

　　18. Male and female workers, engineers, and technicians of the petroleum industry! Increase the speed of extraction and refining! Drill new oil wells and develop new oilfields at a faster pace! Produce more oil and petroleum products for the socialist economy!

　　19. Male and female workers, engineers, and technicians of the iron and steel industry! Ensure a new upsurge in metal production! Maximize the utilization of the production capacity of machines and machinery! Master the experience of advanced steelworkers! Produce more pig iron, steel, rolled metal, and non-ferrous metals for the country!

　　20. Male and female workers, engineers, and technicians of power stations! Increase the output of electricity! Open new power stations at a faster pace, ensuring a continuous supply of electricity to the national economy!

　　21. Male and female workers, engineers, and technicians of machine-building enterprises! Equip our national economy with advanced technology! Establish new production units at a faster pace! Supply more machinery to industry, transportation, and agriculture!

　　22. Male and female workers, engineers, and technicians of the automobile and tractor industry! Make every effort to increase the production of automobiles and tractors!

　　23. Male and female workers, engineers, and technicians of the chemical industry! Fight for the adoption of new and progressive technologies! Increase the production of mineral fertilizers! Supply the national economy with more chemical products!

　　24. Male and female workers, engineers, and technicians of the shipbuilding industry! Master new shipbuilding technologies! Build new ships faster! Establish a strong fleet for the Soviet state!

　　25. Male and female workers, engineers, and technicians engaged in construction work! Rapidly build new enterprises, homes, and cultural institutions! Expand the mechanization of construction work! Reduce costs and improve the quality of work!

　　26. Male and female workers, engineers, and technicians of the building materials industry! Establish and master new production units as quickly as possible! Increase the production of cement, bricks, glass, roofing, and other materials!

　　27. Male and female workers, engineers, and technicians of the timber and paper industry! Implement mechanization on a larger scale in timber production! Maximize the production and transportation of timber! Produce more paper for the country!

　　28. Male and female workers, engineers, and technicians of the light industry! Supply the people with more textiles, clothing, knitwear, boots, shoes, and other items! Strive to save raw materials, improve quality, and increase the variety of consumer goods!

　　29. Workers in the food, meat, dairy, and fish industries! Increase the production of food and improve its quality! Supply the people with more sugar, butter, meat, cheese, fish, and other foods!

　　30. Workers in local industries and production cooperatives! Produce more goods using local raw materials! Improve the quality of consumer goods! Ensure better service to the working people!

　　31. Male and female workers, engineers, and technicians engaged in public utilities and housing construction! Accelerate the construction of houses and cultural institutions and improve their quality! Strive to improve cities and workers' residential areas!

　　32. Railway workers! Increase freight volume, accelerate truck turnaround rates, and reduce transportation costs! Promote advanced labor methods! Increase the speed of railway construction and improve its quality! Improve service to passengers!

　　33. Workers on marine and inland river vessels! Increase freight volume and accelerate shipping turnover rates! Increase the speed of freight transport for the national economy! Strive for a model navigation season in 1950!

　　34. Agricultural workers! Greatly increase the achievements of socialist agriculture and animal husbandry! Strive to carry out spring sowing and other agricultural work smoothly at the best time required by agricultural technology! Produce sufficient grain for the people and sufficient raw materials for light industry!

　　35. Male and female collective farmers, workers of machine and tractor stations and state farms, and agricultural experts! Strive for a bumper harvest in all sown areas of collective farms and state farms! Widely promote the achievements of advanced agricultural science! Expand the ranks of advanced agricultural and animal husbandry workers!

　　36. Male and female representatives of collective farms! Increase the collective wealth of collective farms and strengthen labor discipline! Strictly abide by the agricultural cooperative charter – the basic law of collective farm life!

　　37. Tractor drivers, combine drivers, mechanics of machine and tractor stations and state farms! Do your best to achieve high productivity of tractors and combines! Improve the quality of farmland work and strive for a bumper harvest!

　　38. Male and female collective farmers, workers of state farms, animal husbandry experts, and veterinarians! Make every effort to develop the public breeding of livestock! Increase the number of livestock while increasing the productivity of livestock products! Establish a solid feed base for livestock breeding!

　　39. Workers in agriculture and forestry! Strive to complete the plan for planting protective forest belts in the grassland and forest-grassland zone ahead of schedule! Adopt crop-grass rotation systems at the fastest speed, and develop the excavation of ponds and reservoirs on a larger scale! Guarantee bumper harvests every year!

　　40. Workers in Soviet trade organizations, cooperatives, and public supply agencies! Widely develop effective Soviet trade in urban and rural areas! Improve service to Soviet consumers!

　　41. Staff members of state organs! Improve the work of Soviet administrative institutions, strengthen state discipline, and pay attention to the demands and needs of the working people!

　　42. Soviet scientists! Enrich the science and technology of our Motherland with new research, discoveries, and inventions! Consolidate the connection between science and industry!

　　43. Workers in literature, art, and film! Raise the level of your artistic activities and create new artistic works with noble ideological content, worthy of the great Soviet people!

　　44. Male and female teachers, workers in public education! Arm students with solid knowledge based on science! Educate our youth in the spirit of Soviet patriotism! Cultivate knowledgeable and cultured citizens of socialist society, and train active fighters for communism!

　　45. Medical workers! Improve medical services for the people! Adopt the achievements of Soviet medical science in daily practice! Strive for exemplary work in public health organs!

　　46. The whole country sends its regards to the disabled veterans of the Patriotic War and to the families of the heroic Soviet fighters who gave their lives for the freedom and independence of our Motherland!

　　47. Soviet trade unions! More widely develop socialist competition and strive for the early completion of the 1950 national economic plan! Promote the experience of production reformers! Persistently care about improving the material and cultural level of staff members and workers! Long live the Soviet trade unions – the school of communism!

　　48. Soviet women! Strive for the even greater prosperity of our Motherland's national economy and culture! Long live Soviet women – active builders of communism!

　　49. Long live the Lenin-Stalin Communist Youth League – the advanced contingent of young builders of communism, the reliable assistant and reserve force of the Bolshevik Party!

　　50. Young men and women of the Soviet Union! Tirelessly master the most advanced technology, science, and culture! Be strong and brave, ready to overcome any difficulties! Work selflessly for the benefit of our country!

　　51. Young Pioneers and schoolchildren! Master knowledge and prepare to become strong fighters for the cause of Lenin and Stalin!

　　52. Communist Party members and Communist Youth League members! Stand at the forefront of the battle, move forward, and fight for a new high tide in economic and cultural development, and for the further consolidation of the power of the Soviet state!

　　53. Long live our great Soviet Motherland – the glorious bastion of friendship among all the peoples of our country!

　　54. Long live the great Bolshevik Party, the Party of Lenin and Stalin – the battle-tested vanguard of the Soviet people, the inspirer and organizer of our victories!

　　55. Under the banner of Lenin, under the leadership of Stalin, advance towards the complete victory of communism!

　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　Central Committee of the All-Union Communist Party (Bolsheviks) (Signed)

### 联共中央纪念五一节口号

1950-04-29
第4版()
专栏：

　　联共中央纪念五一节口号
    【新华社讯】塔斯社莫斯科二十五日电：苏联共产党（布）中央委员会发表一九五○年五一节口号如下：
    一、五一节——全世界劳动人民团结的节日，一切国家工人兄弟团结的节日——万岁！
    二、向为和平、民主、社会主义而斗争的各国人民致兄弟的敬礼！
    三、向依循着社会主义建设的道路的各人民民主国家致兄弟的敬礼！
    四、在反对帝国主义及其国民党奴仆的斗争中，赢得了划时代胜利的伟大的中国人民万岁！
    五、为中国人民打开了走向社会主义的道路的中国共产党万岁！
    六、向进行着解放斗争、反对铁托集团法西斯政权、反对帝国主义从仆的南斯拉夫各族人民致敬！
    七、祝统一、独立、民主、爱好和平的德国长存与繁荣！
    八、一切国家的劳动人民！揭露战争贩子的罪恶计划！扩大与巩固强大的拥护和平阵线！保卫和平是全世界一切人民的事业！
    九、英、美、苏三国人民在争取全世界和平斗争中的友谊万岁！
    十、向为自由、独立而斗争的殖民地与附属国家的人民致兄弟的敬礼！
    十一、光荣归于警卫和平及我们祖国的安全的苏联武装部队！
    十二、苏联边防军——我们祖国神圣边疆的警觉的卫士万岁！
    十三、光荣归于我们祖国最优秀的儿女——苏联英雄及社会主义劳动英雄！
    十四、苏联的工人、农民和知识分子们！进一步扩大争取先期完成一九五○年国民经济计划的社会主义竞赛！
    十五、苏联的劳动人民！为工业、运输业、农业中技术的进一步进展而斗争！采行新的技术，使生产技术臻于完善！不断提高劳动生产率，它是进一步减低消费品价格的基础！
    十六、男女工人、工程师和技师们！为更好地利用基本和流动资金而努力！为最大限度地节省原料及其他材料、燃料及电力而斗争！减低生产成本，改进产品质量！
    十七、煤矿工业的男女工人、工程师和技师们！为采煤全部过程的完全机械化而工作！加速建造新矿场！为我国国民经济生产更多的煤！
    十八、石油工业的男女工人、工程师和技师们！提高开采与炼油的速度！以更高的速度开凿新油井与开发新油田！为社会主义经济生产更多的石油与石油产品！
    十九、钢铁工业的男女工人、工程师和技师们！保证五金生产上的新高涨！最大限度地利用机器与机械的生产能力！精通先进的钢铁工人的经验！为国家生产更多的铣铁、钢、辗金属与非铁金属！
    二十、动力站的男女工人、工程师和技师们！增加电力的产量！以更高的速度开办新的动力站，保证以源源不断的电力供应国民经济！
    二十一、机器制造企业的男女工人、工程师和技师们！以进步的技术装备我国国民经济！以更高的速度建立新的生产单位！以更多的机器供给工业、运输业与农业！
    二十二、汽车与拖拉机工业的男女工人、工程师和技师们！尽力增加汽车及拖拉机的产量！
    二十三、化学工业的男女工人、工程师和技师们！为采行新的进步的技术而斗争！增加矿物肥料的生产！以更多的化学产品供应国民经济！
    二十四、造船工业的男女工人、工程师和技师们！掌握造船的新技术！更快地建造新的船只！建立苏维埃国家的强大舰队！
    二十五、从事于建筑工作的男女工人、工程师和技师们！迅速建造新企业、家庭与文化机关！扩大建筑工作的机械化！减低成本和提高工作的质量！
    二十六、营造材料工业的男女工人、工程师和技师们！尽速建立与掌握新的生产单位！增加水泥、砖、玻璃、屋顶及其他材料的生产！
    二十七、木材与造纸工业的男女工人、工程师和技师们！在木材生产方面，在更广大的规模上实行机械化！最大限度地增加木材的生产与运输！为国家生产更多的纸张！
    二十八、轻工业的男女工人、工程师和技师们！以更多的纺织物、衣服、编织品、靴鞋及其他物品供应人民！为节省原料、提高质量和增加消费品的种类而努力！
    二十九、食品、肉类、乳酪品与鱼类工业的工人们！增加食品的生产并改进其质量！以更多的食糖、牛油、肉类、乳酪品、鱼类及其他食品供应人民！
    三十、地方工业及生产合作社的工人们！用本地的原料生产更多的货物！改进消费品质量！保证更好地为劳动人民服务！
    三十一、从事于公共事业与房屋建造的男女工人、工程师和技师们！加速房屋与文化机关的建设并改进其质量！为改进城市与工人住宅区而努力！
    三十二、铁路工人们！增加载货量，加速卡车运转率，减低运输成本！推广先进的劳动方法！增加建筑铁路的速度并改进其质量！改进对乘客的服务！
    三十三、海洋与内河轮船的工人们！增加货运量，加速航行周转率！为国民经济增加货运速度！为争取一九五○年航海季节的模范而努力！
    三十四、农业工人们！大大增加社会主义农业与牧畜业的成绩！努力争取在农业技术所要求的最好时机内，顺利进行春季播种与其他的农业工作！为人民生产充足的粮食，并为轻工业生产充足的原料！
    三十五、集体农场的男女农民、机器与拖拉机站和国营农场的工人、农业专家们！为集体农场与国家农场的全部播种地区的丰收而努力！广泛地推行先进农业科学的成就！扩大先进的农业与牧畜业工人的队伍！
    三十六、集体农场的男女代表们！增加集体农场的公有财富，加强劳动纪律！严格遵守农业合作社章程——集体农场生活的基本法！
    三十七、拖拉机驾驶员们、联合机驾驶员们、机器与拖拉机站和国营农场的机械师们！为拖拉机与联合机的高度生产率尽你们最大的努力！改善农田工作的质量和争取丰收！
    三十八、集体农场的男女农民、国营农场的工人、畜牧专家和兽医们！尽力发展牲畜的公共饲养！增加牲畜头数同时提高牲畜产物生产率！为牲畜饲养建立稳固的饲料基地！
