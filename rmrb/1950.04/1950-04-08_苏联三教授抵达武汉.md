Title: Three Soviet Professors Arrive in Wuhan
Author:
Date: 1950-04-08
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, Hankou, 7th. Three Soviet professors, Dr. Nurzhin, Dr. Giselev, and Ms. Makarova, who were invited to China to lecture by the All-China Association for Friendship with the Soviet Union, arrived in Wuhan this afternoon, accompanied by Pan Defeng, Deputy Director of the Service Department of the All-China Association for Friendship with the Soviet Union. Those who went to the station and wharf to welcome them included Zhao Yimin, Director of the Propaganda Department of the CCP Central Committee's Central and Southern Bureau and Secretary-General of the Wuhan Branch of the Sino-Soviet Friendship Association, Pan Nian, Minister of Education of the Central and Southern Military and Political Committee, Li Xiannian, Chairman of the Hubei Provincial People's Government, and representatives of Wuhan's cultural and academic groups and the Soviet Overseas Chinese Association. The three professors will begin lecturing in the city tomorrow.

### 苏联三教授抵达武汉

1950-04-08
第1版()
专栏：

　　苏联三教授抵达武汉
    【新华社汉口七日电】应中苏友好协会总会邀请来华讲学的苏联三教授：努日金博士、吉谢列夫博士和马卡洛娃硕士，偕同中苏友协总会服务部副主任潘德枫于今日下午抵达武汉。前往车站及码头欢迎者有中共中央中南局宣传部长、中苏友好协会武汉分会总干事赵毅敏，中南军政委员会教育部潘年部长，湖北省人民政府李先念主席及武汉市各文化学术团体及苏侨协会代表。三教授自明日起将分别在本市讲学。
