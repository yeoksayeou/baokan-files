Title: Guo Moruo to Give Lecture Today: "China's Slave Society" at Peking University
Author: 
Date: 1950-04-26
Page: 4

　　Guo Moruo to Give Lecture Today
    Giving a Lecture at Peking University on "China's Slave Society"
    [Our Newspaper] At the invitation of the History Department of Peking University, Guo Moruo is scheduled to give a lecture on "China's Slave Society" at 2:00 PM today (April 26th) in the auditorium of the Science College of Peking University.
          (Peking University Correspondent Group)

### 郭沫若今日讲演  在北大讲“中国奴隶社会”

1950-04-26
第4版()
专栏：

　　郭沫若今日讲演
    在北大讲“中国奴隶社会”
    【本报讯】郭沫若应北大史学系系会之请，定于今日（二十六日）下午二时来北大理学院大礼堂讲演“中国奴隶社会”。
