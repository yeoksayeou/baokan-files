Title: All-China Women's Federation Holds 17th Standing Committee Meeting, Decides to Vigorously Promote Marriage Law
Author:
Date: 1950-04-22
Page: 3

　　Xinhua News Agency, 20th: The All-China Democratic Women's Federation held its 17th Standing Committee meeting at 2:00 PM on the 17th, presided over by Vice-Chairman Li Dequan. At the meeting, the committee listened to the preliminary measures for publicizing the Marriage Law of the People's Republic of China, drafted by relevant departments of the Federation. The meeting decided to hold a meeting jointly with the heads of the All-China Federation of Trade Unions, the Central Committee of the China New Democratic Youth League, and others, to issue a joint notice, calling on cadres of people's organizations at all levels to conscientiously study, publicize, and assist the People's Government in implementing the Marriage Law. The meeting decided that in the near future, it would organize reports and broadcasts on the Marriage Law, publish booklets suitable for cadres and the masses to read, and the "New Chinese Women" monthly magazine would publish a special issue, etc. At the meeting, the committee also listened to reports from Executive Committee members Yang Yunyu and Tian Xiujuan, and Deputy Secretary-General Luo Qiong on the first women's congresses and women's work in Henan and Hebei provinces and Shenyang City, and from Minister of Children's Welfare Kang Keqing on her inspection of women's and children's work in Tianjin. After listening to the above reports, the meeting decided that each reporter would be responsible for collecting materials on the experiences and situations of women's work in their respective regions, compile them into materials, and distribute them to all parts of the country to exchange experiences.

### 全国妇联举行十七次常委会  决定大力宣传婚姻法

1950-04-22
第3版()
专栏：

　　全国妇联举行十七次常委会
    决定大力宣传婚姻法
    【新华社二十日讯】中华全国民主妇女联合会十七日下午二时举行第十七次常委会，由副主席李德全主持。会上听取了本会有关部门所拟的关于宣传中华人民共和国婚姻法的初步办法。会议决定联合中华全国总工会、中国新民主主义青年团中央委员会等负责人举行会议，发布联合通知，号召各地各级人民团体干部认真学习宣传并协助人民政府实施婚姻法。会议决定在近期内，组织有关婚姻法的报告、广播，出版适合于干部及群众阅读的小册子，《新中国妇女》月刊出特辑等。会上并听取了执委杨蕴玉、田秀涓，副秘书长罗琼关于出席河南、河北省及沈阳市三地首届妇女代表大会及妇女工作情况的汇报，儿童福利部长康克清考察天津市妇女儿童工作的汇报。在听取了上列报告后，会议决定由各报告人负责汇集各该地妇女工作经验、情况等材料，编成资料，分发全国各地，交流经验。
