Title: Millions of Ordinary People Guarding Peace: World Peace Movement Sweeps the Globe; Soviet Newspapers Publish Articles Commemorating the First Anniversary of the World Peace Congress
Author:
Date: 1950-04-22
Page: 4
　　Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, April 21st: TASS, Moscow, April 20th: Soviet newspapers are commemorating the first anniversary of the World Peace Congress, held simultaneously in Paris and Prague, with extensive coverage. Pravda, Izvestia, Trud, and Komsomolskaya Pravda have all published editorials and articles commemorating this anniversary. The newspapers have also published other articles discussing the memorable anniversary of the first establishment in human history of an organized front for the struggle for peace. Kudryavtsev and Mikhailov published an article in Izvestia entitled "Strive for Peace!" Pravda reprinted an article from the organ of the Standing Committee of the World Peace Congress entitled "We Must Achieve the Prohibition of Atomic Weapons." Trud published an article written by Gagarina, a delegate to the First World Peace Congress and participant in the Stockholm meeting of the World Peace Congress Standing Committee.
    Pravda's editorial pointed out: The First World Peace Congress, which opened on April 20, 1949, played a significant role in developing a powerful and organized peace movement that swept across all countries and continents.
    This congress clearly confirmed the profound truth contained in Stalin's historically significant statement: Peace is guarded by millions of ordinary people, who have their own views, their own policies, and they will certainly be able to safeguard their interests.

### 千百万普通人民警卫着和平  世界和平运动席卷全球  苏联各报著论纪念世界和大一周年

1950-04-22
第4版()
专栏：

　　千百万普通人民警卫着和平
    世界和平运动席卷全球
    苏联各报著论纪念世界和大一周年
    【新华社二十一日讯】塔斯社莫斯科二十日讯：苏联报纸以巨大篇幅纪念在巴黎与布拉格两地同时举行的世界拥护和平大会一周年。真理报、消息报、劳动报及共青真理报均发表社论，并刊登专文，纪念这一节日。各报并刊登了其他专文，论述有组织的和平斗争阵线在人类历史上首次建立起来的可纪念的周年纪念日。库德里亚夫切夫及米哈依洛夫以《争取和平！》为题在消息报上发表文章。真理报转载了世界拥护和平大会常设委员会机关刊所发表的题为《我们要做到禁止原子武器》一文。劳动报刊载了出席第一届世界拥护和平大会和参加和大常委会斯德哥尔摩会议的代表嘉嘉里娜所撰的专文。
    真理报社论指出：一九四九年四月二十日开幕的第一届世界拥护和平大会，在发展席卷所有国家和一切大陆的、强大而有组织的和平运动上，起了重大的作用。
    这一大会明白地证实了斯大林的有历史意义的言论中所包括的深刻真理；他说：和平是被千百万普通人民警卫着的，他们有自己的观点、自己的政策，而且他们也一定能够维护他们的利益。
