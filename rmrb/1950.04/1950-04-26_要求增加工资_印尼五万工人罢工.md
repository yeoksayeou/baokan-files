Title: 50,000 Indonesian Workers Strike Demanding Wage Increase
Author:
Date: 1950-04-26
Page: 4

　　Demanding Wage Increase
    50,000 Indonesian Workers Strike
    [Xinhua News Agency, The Hague, 21st] TASS from Jakarta: The strike movement in Indonesia is growing daily. To the east of Jakarta, more than 50,000 Indonesian workers employed by Dutch and British colonial enterprises have gone on strike. They are demanding a 40% wage increase and a ban on child labor.

### 要求增加工资  印尼五万工人罢工

1950-04-26
第4版()
专栏：

　　要求增加工资
    印尼五万工人罢工
    【新华社二十五日讯】塔斯社海牙二十一日讯：据雅加达消息：印尼罢工运动正在日益增长。在雅加达以东，受雇于荷兰与英国殖民者企业中的五万余印尼工人已经罢工。他们要求增加工资百分之四十，并禁止使用童工。
