Title: North China Maritime Union Representative Meeting Concludes
Author:
Date: 1950-04-16
Page: 2

Xinhua News Agency, Tianjin, April 15th. The North China Maritime Union Member Representative Meeting was held in Tianjin from the 10th to the 12th of this month. The meeting elected 33 preparatory committee members to form the North China Regional Preparatory Committee of the Chinese Maritime Union; and elected Zeng Shoulong as the director of the preparatory committee, and Ren Rongke and Zuo Zhongxia as deputy directors. In accordance with the spirit of the resolutions of the National Maritime Union Representative Meeting, the meeting passed six resolutions, including assisting the government in developing Beiyang shipping, concluding collective contracts, and strengthening seafarer education.

### 华北海员工会代表会议闭幕

1950-04-16
第2版()
专栏：

　　华北海员工会代表会议闭幕
    【新华社天津十五日电】华北区海员工会会员代表会议于本月十日至十二日在天津举行。会上选出筹备委员三十三人，组成中国海员工会华北区筹委会；并推选曾寿隆为筹委会主任、任荣科和左中侠为副主任。会议根据全国海员工会代表会决议的精神，通过了协助政府发展北洋航运、订立集体合同、加强海员教育等六项决议。
