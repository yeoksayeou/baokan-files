Title: Two Inspection Teams of the Ministry of Water Resources Depart for East and Central South China for Inspection
Author:
Date: 1950-04-09
Page: 2

　　Xinhua News Agency, 5th. To gain an in-depth understanding of the spring repair projects for water conservancy in various regions and to strengthen leadership in this work, the Ministry of Water Resources of the Central People's Government has organized two spring repair project inspection teams for East China and Central South China, which will go to the respective regions for inspection. The inspection teams will focus on inspecting the spring repair projects of the Yangtze River and the Huai River. The East China inspection team, led by Li Baohua and Zhang Hanying, both Vice Ministers of the Ministry of Water Resources, departed from Beijing on the 1st. The Central South China inspection team also departed on the 3rd.

### 水利部两检查小组分赴华东中南视察

1950-04-09
第2版()
专栏：

　　水利部两检查小组分赴华东中南视察
    【新华社五日讯】中央人民政府水利部为深入了解各地水利春修工程情况，及加强对这一工作的领导，已组织华东、中南两春修工程检查组，分别到各该地视察。检查组将以检查长江、淮河春修工程为重点。华东区检查小组由水利部李葆华、张含英两副部长率领，已于一日自京出发。中南区检查小组也已于三日启程。
