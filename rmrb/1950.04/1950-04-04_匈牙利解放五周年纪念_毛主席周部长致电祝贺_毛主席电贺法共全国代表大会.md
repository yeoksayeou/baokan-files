Title: Fifth Anniversary of Hungary's Liberation: Chairman Mao and Minister Zhou Send Congratulatory Telegrams; Chairman Mao Sends Congratulatory Telegram to French Communist Party National Congress
Author:
Date: 1950-04-04
Page: 1

　　Xinhua News Agency, 3rd: April 4th marks the fifth anniversary of the liberation of Hungary. Chairman Mao Zedong and Minister Zhou Enlai sent telegrams on April 1st to Chairman Szakasits of the Presidium of the Hungarian People's Republic and Minister Kállai of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs, respectively, to express their congratulations. The telegrams are as follows:
    (I) Chairman Mao's Telegram to Chairman Szakasits
    To His Excellency Chairman Szakasits: On the occasion of the fifth anniversary of the liberation of Hungary, I, on behalf of the Central People's Government of the People's Republic of China and its people, extend my heartfelt congratulations to Your Excellency and wish the Hungarian people happiness and progress!
                     Chairman Mao Zedong of the Central People's Government of the People's Republic of China
                          April 1, 1950, Beijing
    (II) Minister Zhou's Telegram to Minister Kállai
    To His Excellency Minister Kállai: On the happy occasion of the fifth anniversary of the liberation of Hungary, I extend warm congratulations to your government and people.
        Minister of Foreign Affairs Zhou Enlai of the Central People's Government of the People's Republic of China
                            April 1, 1950, Beijing
    Xinhua News Agency, 3rd: Chairman Mao Zedong of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of China sent a telegram on March 31st to congratulate the opening of the 12th National Congress of the French Communist Party. The telegram is as follows:
    To the Central Committee of the French Communist Party,
    To the 12th National Congress of the French Communist Party:
    We warmly congratulate the success of the 12th National Congress of the French Communist Party, and wish the French Communist Party increasing strength in leading the French people in their revolutionary struggle.
                  Chairman Mao Zedong of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of China

### 匈牙利解放五周年纪念  毛主席周部长致电祝贺  毛主席电贺法共全国代表大会

1950-04-04
第1版()
专栏：

　　匈牙利解放五周年纪念
    毛主席周部长致电祝贺
    毛主席电贺法共全国代表大会
    【新华社三日讯】四月四日为匈牙利解放五周年纪念日。毛泽东主席与周恩来部长于四月一日分别致电匈牙利人民共和国国会主席团斯查卡西兹主席及外交部卡莱部长，表示祝贺。电文如下：
    （一）毛主席致斯查卡西兹主席电斯查卡西兹主席阁下：谨代表中华人民共和国中央人民政府及中国人民，当此匈牙利解放五周年之际，谨向阁下致祝贺之忱，并祝匈牙利人民幸福和进步！
                     中华人民共和国中央人民政府主席毛泽东
                          一九五○年四月一日于北京
    （二）周部长致卡莱部长电
    卡莱部长阁下：欣逢匈牙利解放五周年之际，谨向贵国政府及人民致热烈的祝贺。
        中华人民共和国中央人民政府外交部长周恩来
                            一九五○年四月一日于北京
    【新华社三日讯】中国共产党中央委员会主席毛泽东于三月三十一日致电祝贺法国共产党第十二次全国代表大会的召开。电文如下：
    法国共产党中央委员会转
    法国共产党第十二次全国代表大会：
    我们热烈祝贺法国共产党第十二次全国代表大会的成功，并祝法国共产党在领导法国人民进行革命斗争中日益强大。
                  中国共产党中央委员会主席毛泽东
