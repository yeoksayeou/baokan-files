Title: Public Affairs Lecture: Xiao Feng to Lecture Today
Author:
Date: 1950-04-15
Page: 4

　　Public Affairs Lecture
　　Xiao Feng to Lecture Today
　　[Our newspaper's report] The fourth lecture of the Youth Service Department's Public Affairs Lecture series will be held at the Soviet Foreign Culture Association (19 South River Bank) at 2:00 PM on Saturday, April 15th. We have invited Comrade Xiao Feng, editor of this newspaper, to lecture on "Sino-Soviet Economic Cooperation." Young people and organizations from all walks of life are welcome to attend the lecture. Please bring a letter of introduction to collect your ticket.
                                              (China Youth Society)

### 大众时事讲座  萧风今日主讲

1950-04-15
第4版()
专栏：

　　大众时事讲座
　　萧风今日主讲
    【本报讯】青年服务部大众时事讲座第四讲，四月十五日（星期六）下午二时于苏联对外文化协会（南河沿十九号）举行，请本报编委萧风同志主讲《中苏经济合作》。欢迎各界青年和机关团体听讲，凭介绍信领票。
                                              （中国青年社）
