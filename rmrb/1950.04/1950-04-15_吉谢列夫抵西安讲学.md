Title: Kiselyov Arrives in Xi'an to Lecture

Author: 
Date: 1950-04-15
Page: 3

Xinhua News Agency, Xi'an, April 14th: Professor Kiselyov, a Soviet Doctor of History who was invited to lecture in China, arrived in Xi'an on the morning of the 13th from Hankou. That evening, at the invitation of the Sino-Soviet Friendship Association of Xi'an, he gave a lecture at the Mass Hall entitled "Lenin and Stalin's Contributions to Historical Science," which was attended by more than 1,000 people.

### 吉谢列夫抵西安讲学

1950-04-15
第3版()
专栏：

　　吉谢列夫抵西安讲学
    【新华社西安十四日电】应邀来我国讲学的苏联历史学博士吉谢列夫教授于十三日晨由汉口抵西安，当晚应中苏友协西安分会的邀请，在群众堂讲演《列宁、斯大林对历史科学的贡献》，前往听讲的一千余人。
