Title: All-China Women's Federation Women and Children Welfare Department Holds Childcare Symposium
Author:
Date: 1950-04-22
Page: 4

　　The Women and Children Welfare Department of the All-China Democratic Women's Federation held its sixth childcare symposium at 2:00 PM on the 20th of this month in the conference room of the Federation. More than 80 people attended, including heads and childcare staff from 55 public and private nurseries. The topic of the symposium was how the unit of "air defense" cultivates good qualities in children.

### 妇联妇女儿童福利部  举行保育座谈会

1950-04-22
第4版()
专栏：

　　妇联妇女儿童福利部
    举行保育座谈会
    北京市民主妇女联合会妇女儿童福利部，于二十日下午二时在该会会议室召开第六次保育座谈会。出席的有公私立托儿所五十五个单位的负责人、保教人员等共八十余人。座谈会的题目是通过了“防空”这个单元怎样培养了儿童的优良品质。
