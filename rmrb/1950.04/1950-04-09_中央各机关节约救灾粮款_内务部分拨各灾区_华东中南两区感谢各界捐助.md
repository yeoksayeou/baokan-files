Title: Central Government Organs Save Relief Grain and Funds; Ministry of Interior Distributes to Disaster Areas; East China and Central South Regions Thank All Sectors for Donations
Author:
Date: 1950-04-09
Page: 1

　　Xinhua News Agency, April 7th: The Ministry of Interior of the Central People's Government received a total of 463.14 million yuan in relief funds saved by all staff members of the central government organs, 84,577 catties of relief grain, and 353 pieces of relief clothing in March. These have been allocated to East China, Central South, Northwest, North China, Northeast, Beijing, and other places according to the severity of the disasters in each area.
　　Rao Shushi, Chairman of the East China Military and Political Committee, after receiving two batches of disaster relief donations totaling 100 million yuan from central government organs and people from all walks of life in Beijing, wrote a letter to the Ministry of Interior to express his gratitude to the central government organs and the citizens of Beijing. The Central South Military and Political Committee also unanimously passed a resolution at the administrative meeting on the 29th of last month to send a telegram to the Ministry of Interior of the Central Government, expressing sincere gratitude to the central government organs on behalf of the disaster victims in the entire region.

### 中央各机关节约救灾粮款  内务部分拨各灾区  华东中南两区感谢各界捐助

1950-04-09
第1版()
专栏：

　　中央各机关节约救灾粮款
    内务部分拨各灾区
    华东中南两区感谢各界捐助
    【新华社七日讯】中央人民政府内务部于三月份内共收到中央人民政府各机关全体工作人员节约救灾款四亿六千三百十四万余元、救灾粮八万四千五百七十七斤、救灾衣物三百五十三件，并已按各地灾情轻重分别拨发给华东、中南、西北、华北、东北、北京市等地。
    华东军政委员会饶漱石主席收到中央机关及北京市各界人民救灾捐款两批共一亿元后，已写信给内务部转中央各机关和北京市民表示感谢。中南军政委员会亦于上月二十九日行政例会上，一致通过致电中央内务部，代表全区灾民向中央各机关表示衷诚的感激。
