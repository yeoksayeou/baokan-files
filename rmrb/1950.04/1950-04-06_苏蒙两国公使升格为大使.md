Title: Soviet-Mongolian Ministers to be Upgraded to Ambassadors
Author:
Date: 1950-04-06
Page: 4

　　Soviet-Mongolian Ministers to be Upgraded to Ambassadors

　　[Xinhua News Agency, 5th] TASS Moscow, 4th—The Soviet government and the government of the Mongolian People's Republic have recently reached a decision to change the Soviet Legation in Ulaanbaatar and the Mongolian People's Republic Legation in Moscow to embassies, and to upgrade the ministers of the two countries stationed in each other's country to ambassadors.

　　To this end, the Presidium of the Supreme Soviet of the Soviet Union has appointed Mr. Prikhodov, the Soviet Minister to the Mongolian People's Republic, as the Soviet Ambassador Extraordinary and Plenipotentiary to the Mongolian People's Republic.

　　The Presidium of the Small Hural of the Mongolian People's Republic (referring to the highest organ of power during the recess of the Great People's Assembly) has appointed Mr. Idan-jab, the Minister of the Mongolian People's Republic to the Soviet Union, as the Mongolian People's Republic Ambassador Extraordinary and Plenipotentiary to the Soviet Union.

### 苏蒙两国公使升格为大使

1950-04-06
第4版()
专栏：

　　苏蒙两国公使升格为大使
    【新华社五日讯】塔斯社莫斯科四日电：苏联政府与蒙古人民共和国政府顷通过一项决定，将苏联驻乌兰巴托尔公使馆和蒙古人民共和国驻莫斯科公使馆分别改为大使馆，并将两国驻对方公使升格到大使。
    为此，苏联最高苏维埃主席团已任命苏联驻蒙古人民共和国公使普里霍多夫为苏联驻蒙古人民共和国特命全权大使。
    蒙古人民共和国小呼拉尔（按为最高人民会议休会期间的最高权力机关）主席团已任命蒙古人民共和国驻苏联公使伊丹甲布为蒙古人民共和国驻苏联特命全权大使。
