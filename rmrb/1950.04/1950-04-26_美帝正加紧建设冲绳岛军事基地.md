Title: US Imperialism Intensifies Construction of Military Bases on Okinawa

Author:

Date: 1950-04-26
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, April 25th: The United States is accelerating the establishment of military bases on Okinawa. According to a TASS dispatch from Tokyo on the 24th, citing the Japan Times, bids for the construction project of the Okinawa military base were opened in Tokyo on April 8th, with more than 40 construction companies from the United States, Japan, and the Philippines participating. On the 18th, MacArthur Headquarters announced that the first batch of construction contracts under the new military base construction plan on Okinawa had been signed, undertaken by the American Morrison-Knudsen Company and the Japanese Shimizu Corporation, which will employ Japanese labor and use Japanese construction materials. The report also revealed that more such contracts will be concluded soon. The report also stated that Lieutenant General Stratemeyer, commander of the US Far East Air Force, said recently that the construction of permanent aircraft facilities has begun on Okinawa, and the United States intends to transfer a large number of air force troops from Guam.

### 美帝正加紧建设冲绳岛军事基地

1950-04-26
第4版()
专栏：

　　美帝正加紧建设冲绳岛军事基地
    【新华社二十五日讯】美国正加紧在冲绳岛建立军事基地。据塔斯社二十四日电引东京日本时报消息：冲绳岛军事基地建设工程的合同，已于四月八日在东京开标，参加投标的有四十多个美国、日本和菲律宾的建设公司。十八日，麦克阿瑟总部宣布：冲绳岛新军事基地建设计划下的第一批建设工程合同业已签订，由美国穆利逊——甘德森公司和日本清水公司包办，并将雇用日本劳动力和使用日本建设物资。该报并透露：不久将缔结更多的这种合同。该报又称：美国远东空军司令斯特拉特梅耶中将不久前曾说：永久性飞机设备的建设，已在冲绳岛开始，美国并打算从关岛调来大量空军部队。
