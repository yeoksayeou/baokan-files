Title: Major Products of State-Owned Industries in Northeast China Overfulfilled in March
Author:
Date: 1950-04-11
Page: 2

Xinhua News Agency, Shenyang, April 10th: Major products of state-owned industries in Northeast China mostly overfulfilled their targets in March. According to statistics from the Planning Department of the Industry Department of the Northeast People's Government: among the 34 major products of factories and mines under the department, 26 have completed and exceeded their targets. Among them, the largest excesses are: electric wire exceeding by 81%, V-belts by 60%, cement by 48.6%, concentrated sulfuric acid by 42%, electrolytic copper by 41%, and electric furnace steel ingots by 36%. Among the major light and heavy industrial units, all major products of the Light Industry Bureau exceeded their targets. Except for open-hearth steel ingots (97% completion) and power generation (80% completion) which did not complete their tasks, all other products of Benxi Coal and Iron Company completed and exceeded their targets.

### 东北国营工业主要产品  三月份大半超额完成

1950-04-11
第2版()
专栏：

　　东北国营工业主要产品
    三月份大半超额完成
    【新华社沈阳十日电】东北国营工业的主要产品，在三月份内有四分之三完成并超过任务。根据东北人民政府工业部计划处统计：该部所属各厂矿的三十四种主要产品中，已有二十六种完成并超过任务。其中超过最多者如电线超过百分之八十一，三角带百分之六十，水泥百分之四十八点六，浓硫酸百分之四十二，电铜百分之四十一，电炉钢锭百分之三十六。在各主要轻重工业单位中，轻工业管理局各种主要产品全部超过任务。本溪煤铁公司除平炉钢锭完成百分之九七，发电完成百分之八零未完成任务以外，其余均完成并超过。
