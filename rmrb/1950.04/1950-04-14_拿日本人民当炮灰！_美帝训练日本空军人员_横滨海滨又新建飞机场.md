Title: Using the Japanese People as Cannon Fodder! The American Imperialists Train Japanese Air Force Personnel; New Airfield Built on Yokohama Beach

Author:

Date: 1950-04-14
Page: 4

　　Using the Japanese People as Cannon Fodder!
　　The American Imperialists Train Japanese Air Force Personnel
　　New Airfield Built on Yokohama Beach
　　[Xinhua News Agency, 13th Dispatch] According to a TASS dispatch on the 12th, citing the Japan Times: According to a statement by McKeant, Assistant Director of Personnel for the U.S. Far East Air Force: The training of Japanese people serving in the U.S. Air Force began on April 10th. This is being carried out in accordance with the U.S. Air Force personnel training plan. The first group of trainees includes thirty Japanese people selected from all regions of Japan.
　　According to Kyodo News Agency, Tokyo, 4th Dispatch: The Eighth U.S. Army in Japan announced: The U.S. military recently built a new airfield in the Negishi beach area of Isogo Ward, Yokohama City, and began using it on April 3rd.

### 拿日本人民当炮灰！  美帝训练日本空军人员  横滨海滨又新建飞机场

1950-04-14
第4版()
专栏：

　　拿日本人民当炮灰！
　　美帝训练日本空军人员
　　横滨海滨又新建飞机场
    【新华社十三日讯】据塔斯社十二日电引日本时报消息：根据美国驻远东空军人事处助理处长麦谦特的声明说：对在美国空军中服务的日本人的训练，已于四月十日开始。这是按照美国空军人员训练计划进行的。第一批受训者，包括自日本所有各区选出的三十名日本人。
    另据共同社东京四日讯：美国驻日占领军第八军宣布：美军最近在横滨市矶子区根岸海滨地方建筑了一个新飞机场，并已自四月三日起开始使用。
