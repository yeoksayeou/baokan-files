Title: *Izvestia* Publishes International Commentary: Condemning US Imperialism for Sabotaging the United Nations. The US Refuses to Expel Kuomintang Swindlers from UN Agencies, Paralyzing the UN
Author:
Date: 1950-04-03
Page: 4

　　Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, 2nd: TASS Moscow reported: *Izvestia* published an international commentary on March 31, detailing the new pressure exerted by the United States on Marshall Plan nations, and pointed out that American monopoly capitalists have decided to apply Acheson's recently announced "total diplomacy" to Western European countries. The United States emphasizes through all its actions that it does not recognize the independence of the member states of the North Atlantic Alliance and regards this alliance as a tool to completely control these countries not only in military matters but also in economic and political aspects. *Izvestia* further pointed out that the North Atlantic Alliance is now openly confronting the United Nations. The United States wants to replace the United Nations with this tool for dominating the world (which gives the United States complete freedom of action); the United States has long refused to expel the Kuomintang swindlers from the various agencies of this international organization, refused to recognize the legitimate rights of the Central People's Government of the People's Republic of China, and deliberately paralyzed the activities of the United Nations.

### 消息报发表国际述评  斥美帝破坏联合国  美国拒绝把国民党骗子逐出联合国各机构，使联合国陷于瘫痪

1950-04-03
第4版()
专栏：

　　消息报发表国际述评
    斥美帝破坏联合国
    美国拒绝把国民党骗子逐出联合国各机构，使联合国陷于瘫痪
    【新华社二日讯】塔斯社莫斯科讯：消息报在三月三十一日登载的国际述评中，详论美国对马歇尔化国家所施的新压力，并指出：美国垄断资本家们已决定把艾奇逊最近宣布的“总体外交”施用于西欧国家。美国以它的一切行动强调说明：它不承认北大西洋联盟的会员国的独立，并把这个联盟看成是不仅在军事问题上而且在经济和政治方面完全控制这些国家的工具。消息报进一步指出：北大西洋联盟现在公开地与联合国相抗衡。美国想用这个独霸世界的工具（这个工具给予美国完全的行动自由）来代替联合国；美国很久以来就拒绝把国民党骗子们逐出这一国际组织的各个机构，拒绝承认中华人民共和国中央人民政府的合法权利，故意使联合国的活动陷于瘫痪。
