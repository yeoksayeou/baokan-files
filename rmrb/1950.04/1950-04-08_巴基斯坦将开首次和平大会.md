Title: Pakistan to Hold First Peace Conference
Author:
Date: 1950-04-08
Page: 4
　　Xinhua News Agency, Karachi, 6th -- Tass reports: Pakistani progressive writers have announced that the first Pakistan Peace Supporters Conference will be held in Akala Town (in West Punjab) from April 13th to 14th. Progressive organizations of workers, peasants, and students will participate in the meeting.

### 巴基斯坦将开首次和平大会

1950-04-08
第4版()
专栏：

　　巴基斯坦将开首次和平大会
    【新华社七日讯】塔斯社喀喇蚩六日讯：巴基斯坦进步作家已发出通知，定四月十三日到十四日在阿卡拉镇（在西旁遮普）举行巴基斯坦和平拥护者第一次大会。工人、农民和学生的进步组织将参加会议。
