Title: Makarova Returns to Beijing
Author:
Date: 1950-04-15
Page: 3

　　Makarova Returns to Beijing
    [Xinhua News Agency, 14th] Associate Professor Makarova, a Master of Economics who was invited to China to lecture, returned to Beijing from Hankou on the 13th.

### 马卡洛娃返京

1950-04-15
