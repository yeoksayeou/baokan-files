Title: Welcoming the "May Day" Festival with New Labor Achievements: Soviet Union Publishes First Quarter Production Communiqué
Author:
Date: 1950-04-29
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, April 27th: The Central Statistical Bureau of the Council of Ministers of the Soviet Union has published a communiqué on the results of the implementation of the plan for the development of the national economy in the first quarter of 1950. The communiqué points out the development of industry, agriculture, and transportation in the first quarter of this year, the expansion of basic construction projects and trade volume, and the improvement of people's living standards and cultural levels.

	People's Living Standards and Cultural Levels Further Improved
	(1) Industry: In the first quarter of this year, the total industrial output of the Soviet Union increased by 22% compared with the first quarter of last year. The percentages of fulfillment of the production plans for total industrial output by various ministries are as follows: Ministry of Metallurgical Industry 99, Ministry of Coal Industry 99, Ministry of Petroleum Industry 94, Ministry of Power Stations 103, Ministry of Chemical Industry 103, Ministry of Electrical Industry 105, Ministry of Heavy Machinery Manufacturing 105, Ministry of Automobile and Tractor Industry 101, Ministry of Machine Tool Manufacturing Industry 101, Ministry of Machine and Tool Manufacturing Industry 102, Ministry of Construction and Road Construction Machinery Manufacturing Industry 101, Ministry of Transportation Machinery Manufacturing Industry 98, Ministry of Light Industry 101, Ministry of Food Industry 96. In the first quarter of this year, a large amount of electricity, rolled metal, automatic loaders, and steam turbines were produced in excess of the plan. Although the Ministry of Coal Industry did not complete the total output plan in some affiliated departments, it exceeded the coal production plan.
	(2) Agriculture: This year's spring farming work was equipped with more machinery than last year, for example, the number of agricultural tractors increased by 19% and the number of seed drills increased by 24%. At the beginning of this spring's farming work, 50% more mineral fertilizers were transported than last year. As of April 15, the sown area of spring crops increased by 5.4 million hectares compared with the same day last year. As of April 1, 144 new farmland shelterbelt afforestation stations have been established. The number of livestock on collective farms and state farms has also increased.
	(3) Transportation: In the first quarter of this year, the average daily loading volume of railways increased by 13% compared with the same period last year, and the volume of maritime cargo increased by 13%.
	(4) Basic Construction Projects: In the first quarter of this year, it increased by 22% compared with the first quarter of last year.
	(5) Trade: In the first quarter of this year, the goods sold to residents increased by 25% compared with the same period last year (calculated at comparable prices), the sales volume of food increased by 19%, and the sales volume of manufactured products increased by 35%.
	(6) Increase in the Number of Employees and Improvement of Labor Productivity: In the first quarter of this year, the total number of employees increased by 2 million compared with the first quarter of last year. The number of people working in industry, agriculture and forestry, construction, and transportation increased by 1.6 million, and the number of people working in education, science, research, and medical institutions increased by nearly 300,000.
	In the first quarter of this year, the labor productivity of industrial workers increased by 13% compared with the first quarter of last year, of which the mechanical manufacturing sector increased by 17%, the metallurgical sector increased by 10%, and the coal mining industry increased by 12%.
	(7) Education, Science, and Health: In the first quarter of this year, the number of students in higher education institutions (including correspondence students) was 1,132,000, an increase of 100,000 compared with the same period last year. The number of students in primary schools, seven-year schools, middle schools, technical schools, and other specialized education institutions reached 36.4 million, an increase of nearly 2 million compared with the first quarter of last year.
	This spring, the government awarded Stalin Prizes to 1,610 scientists, engineers, agriculturalists, artists, writers, workers, and advanced agricultural workers.
	In the first quarter of this year, the Soviet Union had more than 300,000 libraries and reading rooms with more than 600 million books. In factories, institutions, educational institutions, state farms, and collective farms, there are more than 127,000 cultural palaces, cultural centers, clubs, and rural reading rooms, as well as more than 100,000 recreation rooms. In the first quarter of this year, the number of cinemas, film venues, and portable projectors increased by 7,300 compared with the first quarter of last year. In the first quarter of this year, the number of people participating in sports organizations increased by 43% compared with the first quarter of last year, more than double the number in 1940. The number of hospitals, maternity hospitals, and clinics has also further increased.

### 以新的劳动成就迎接“五一”节  苏发表第一季生产公报

1950-04-29
第4版()
专栏：

　　以新的劳动成就迎接“五一”节
    苏发表第一季生产公报
    工业产量较去年同期增加百分之二十二农业运输业及建筑工程等均获显著发展
    【新华社二十八日讯】据塔斯社莫斯科二十七日讯：苏联部长会议中央统计局，顷发表一九五○年第一季发展国民经济计划执行结果的公报。公报指出了今年第一季在工业、农业与运输业的发展，基本建设工程和贸易额的扩大，以及人民生活与文化水平方面的提高。
  人民生活文化水平更加提高
    （一）工业方面：今年第一季，苏联全部工业总产量较去年第一季增加了百分之二十二。各部执行工业总产量生产计划的百分比如下：冶金工业部九九，煤业部九九，石油工业部九四，动力站部一○三，化学工业部一○三，电气工业部一○五，重型机器制造部一○五，汽车与拖拉机工业部一○一，工作母机制造工业部一○一，机器与工具制造工业部一○二，建筑与筑路机器制造工业部一○一，运输机器制造工业部九八，轻工业部一○一，食品工业部九六。今年第一季超计划生产了大量电力、辗金属、自动装载机和蒸汽透平等等。煤业部虽然在一些附属部门中没有完成总产量计划，但却超过了产煤计划。
    （二）农业方面：今年春季农田工作比去年获得更多的机器装备，例如农业拖拉机增加了百分之十九，条播机增加了百分之二十四。今春农田工作开始时，比去年多运出了百分之五十的矿物肥料。到四月十五日止，春季作物的播种面积比去年同日增加了五百四十万公顷。到四月一日，已成立了一百四十四个新的农田防护林植造站。集体农场和国家农场的牲畜头数也增加了。
    （三）运输方面：今年第一季，铁路每日平均装载量较去年同期增加了百分之十三，海上运货量增加了百分之十三。
    （四）基本建筑工程方面：今年第一季较去年第一季增加了百分之二十二。
    （五）贸易方面：今年第一季售给居民的货物，较去年同期增加了百分之二十五（以比较价格计算），食品销售量增加了百分之十九，制成品增加了百分之三十五。
    （六）职工数目的增加与劳动生产率的提高：今年第一季的职工总人数较去年第一季增加了二百万。在工业、农业与林业、建设与运输业中工作的人数，增加了一百六十万，在教育、科学、研究与医药等机关工作的人数，增加了将近三十万。
    今年第一季，产业工人的劳动生产率较去年第一季增加了百分之十三，其中，机械制造方面增加百分之十七，冶金方面百分之十，采煤工业方面百分之十二。
    （七）教育、科学、卫生方面：今年第一季，高等教育机关学生（包括函授学生）为一百十三万二千人，较去年同期增加了十万人。小学、七年制学校、中学、技术学校及其他专科教育机关的学生人数达三千六百四十万，较去年第一季增加了将近二百万。
    今春，政府曾对一千六百一十位科学家、工程师、农业学家、艺术家、文学家、工人和先进的农业工作者颁发了斯大林奖金。
    今年第一季，苏联共有三十多万个拥有六亿余册书籍的图书馆和阅览室。在工厂、机关、教育机关、国家农场和集体农场里，有十二万七千多个文化宫、文化之家、俱乐部和乡间阅览室，以及十万多个游艺室。今年第一季，电影院、电影场和可携带的放映机的数目，较去年第一季增加了七千三百个。今年第一季参加运动组织的人数，较去年第一季增加了百分之四十三。较一九四○年增加了一倍多。医院、产科医院、诊疗所的数目，也有进一步的增加。
