Title: Commemorating the "April 12th" Incident: Fu Jen University to Hold Photo Exhibition Starting Today
Author: Liu Fuhui
Date: 1950-04-12
Page: 4

　　Commemorating "April 12th"
    Fu Jen University to Hold Photo Exhibition Starting Today
    [Our Newspaper] To commemorate the "April 12th" Incident and raise awareness of air defense, the Student Union's Department of Academic Affairs and the Security Department of Fu Jen University will hold the "Air Defense and Anti-Espionage and Photo Exhibition of the Chongqing Massacre by American Imperialists and Chiang Kai-shek Bandits" on the 12th and 13th of this month at the university.
                                              (Liu Fuhui)

### 纪念“四、一二”  辅大今起举行照片展览
刘福惠
1950-04-12
第4版()
专栏：

　　纪念“四、一二”
    辅大今起举行照片展览
    【本报讯】辅仁大学学生会学艺部、保卫部为纪念“四、一二”、提高防空警惕，特定本月十二、十三两日在该校举行“防空反特及美帝蒋匪重庆大屠杀照片展览会。”
