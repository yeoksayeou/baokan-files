Title: Delegation to USSR for May Day Celebrations Arrives in Moscow; Women Representatives from Soviet Union, China, and Other Countries Return to Soviet Capital from Finland
Author:
Date: 1950-04-28
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, dated Moscow, 27th: According to TASS, Moscow, 27th: The Chinese trade union delegation coming to participate in the May Day celebrations arrived here on April 26th.

Xinhua News Agency, dated Moscow, 27th: TASS, Moscow, 27th: The Soviet delegation led by Popova, Chairman of the Soviet Women's Anti-Fascist Committee, which participated in the Executive Committee meeting of the Women's International Democratic Federation, returned to Moscow from Finland on April 26th. Accompanying the delegation to Moscow were representatives from China, Mongolia, and North Korea who participated in the meeting and are returning to their countries via Moscow. At the invitation of the Soviet Women's Anti-Fascist Committee, they will stay in Moscow for several days and participate in the May Day celebrations.

### 赴苏参加五一庆典  我代表团抵莫斯科  苏、中等国妇女代表自芬返苏京

1950-04-28
第1版()
专栏：

　　赴苏参加五一庆典
    我代表团抵莫斯科
    苏、中等国妇女代表自芬返苏京
    【新华社二十七日讯】据塔斯社莫斯科二十七日电：前来参加五一节庆典的中国工会代表团，已于四月二十六日抵达此间。
    【新华社二十七日讯】塔斯社莫斯科二十七日电：苏联妇女反法西斯委员会主席波波娃所率领的参加国际民主妇联执委会会议的苏联代表团，已于四月二十六日自芬兰返抵莫斯科。和代表团一起来莫斯科的有参加会议的，正取道莫斯科返国的中国、蒙古和北朝鲜的代表。她们应苏联妇女反法西斯委员会之请，将留莫斯科数日，并将参加五一庆典。
