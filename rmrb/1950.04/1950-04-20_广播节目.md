Radio Programs
    Central People's Broadcasting Station today (20th), 19:00 Social Science Lecture: Yu Guangyuan speaks on "Value" (Political Economy, Lecture 3). 20:00 People's Forum: (1) Fei Zhendong, Director of the Cultural and Propaganda Department of the Overseas Chinese Affairs Commission, article "Indonesia and Overseas Chinese in Indonesia"; (2) Xiao Gang, former Chairman of the Burmese Branch of the China Democratic League and Editor-in-Chief of the Yangon People's Daily, speaks on "The Situation of Overseas Chinese in Burma." 18:45 Natural Science Lecture: "Soviet Agricultural Machinery." 12:45 Book Review: Issue 36 of "China Youth." 21:45 News: Liberated Peasants Welcome New Films. 20:30 Literary Programs:
    Beijing People's Broadcasting Station's youth lecture today, given by Ding Ling: "How to Read Literary Books?" Time is from 16:30 to 17:30.
    Beijing
    Beijing People's Broadcasting Station program today: 7:30 Workers' Lecture: Liu Laifu, Vice Chairman of the Municipal Federation of Trade Unions, speaks on "All Workers in the City Urgently Provide Relief to Unemployed Workers in Shanghai." 16:30 Youth Program. 17:30 Children's Program. 18:00 Workers' Program: Workers' Lecture: Liu Laifu, Vice Chairman of the Municipal Federation of Trade Unions, speaks on "How All Workers in the City Can Urgently Provide Relief to Unemployed Workers in Shanghai." 18:30 Voice of the Municipality: The Handling of Financial Cases by the Beijing Municipal People's Court in the Past Year. 20:00 Russian Language Lecture. 21:30 Listener Services: (1) Answering Mr. Xing Shuyuan's question: "Why doesn't the Sino-Soviet joint venture petroleum company borrow from the Soviet Union for us to exploit it ourselves?"; (2) Introducing the new Soviet film poet "Lenins"; (3) Broadcasting three telegrams found by the Beijing Telecommunications Bureau.

### 广播节目

1950-04-20
第4版()
专栏：

　　广播节目
    中央人民广播电台今天（二十日）十九点社会科学讲座：于光远讲“价值”（政治经济学第三讲）。二十点人民讲坛：（一）华侨事务委员会文教宣传处处长费振东的文章“印度尼西亚和印尼华侨”；（二）前中国民主同盟缅甸支部主任委员、仰光人民报总编辑萧岗讲“缅甸华侨情况”。十八点四十五分自然科学讲座：“苏联的农业机械”。十二点四十五分书刊介绍：三十六期“中国青年”。二十一点四十五分通讯：翻身农民欢迎新电影。二十点三十分文艺节目：
    北京市人民广播电台今天的青年讲座，由丁玲主讲：“怎样读文艺书？”时间是十六时三十分到十七时三十分。
    北京市
    人民广播电台今天节目：七点三十分工人讲座：市总工会副主席刘莱夫讲“全市工人同志紧急救济上海失业工人弟兄”。十六点三十分青年节目。十七点三十分儿童节目。十八点工人节目：工人讲座：市总工会副主席刘莱夫讲“全市工人同志怎样紧急救济上海失业工人弟兄”。十八点三十分市政之声：北京市人民法院一年来金融案件的处理情况。二十点俄语讲座。二十一点三十分听众服务（１）回答邢书元先生所问：“中苏合办的石油公司，为什么不向苏联贷款由我们自己开采呢？”；（２）介绍苏联新影片诗人《莱尼斯》；（３）播送北京电信局三件招领的电报。
