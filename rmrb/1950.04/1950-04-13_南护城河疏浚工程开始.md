Title: South Moat Dredging Project Begins
Author:
Date: 1950-04-13
Page: 4

　　South Moat Dredging Project Begins
    [Beijing News Agency] The dredging project of the South Moat in Beijing began yesterday (12th). This project stretches from Xibianmen to Dongbianmen, a total length of 15,841 meters. Participants include the masses from Districts 3, 10, and 9, the labor brigade of the court, and soldiers from the Public Security Forces. It is estimated that the entire project will be completed in twenty days.

### 南护城河疏浚工程开始

1950-04-13
第4版()
专栏：

　　南护城河疏浚工程开始
    【京市新闻处讯】京市南护城河的疏浚工程已于昨（十二）日开始。此项工程系由西便门到东便门，全长一万五千八百四十一公尺，参加工作的计有三区、十区、九区的群众、法院劳动大队及公安部队的战士。预计二十天可全部完工。
