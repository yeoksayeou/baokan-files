Title: *Izvestia* Editorial Calls For: Further Development of Labor Emulation; Rebuilding Cities and Villages and Workers' Housing

Author:

Date: 1950-04-16
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, 15th: TASS reports: *Izvestia* recently published an editorial discussing the restoration of towns and villages, stating: After the barbaric invasion of the fascist bandits, 25 million Soviet people were displaced. However, in the first four years of Stalin's post-war Five-Year Plan, state enterprises, institutions, local Soviets, and residents, with the help of state loans, have built and repaired over 72 million square meters of living space in various towns and workers' settlements. At the same time, 2.3 million houses have been built or repaired in the countryside. The editorial emphasized that the restoration of towns and villages is of great importance to the country. The editorial also urged local Soviet governments, ministries, and other organizations to further develop socialist emulation for the reconstruction of towns, workers' settlements, and villages.

### 消息报社论号召：进一步发展劳动竞赛  重建城乡和工人住区

1950-04-16
第4版()
专栏：

　　消息报社论号召：
    进一步发展劳动竞赛  重建城乡和工人住区
    【新华社十五日讯】塔斯社莫斯科讯：消息报顷发表社论，论述城镇与乡村的恢复工作称：在法西斯匪帮野蛮入侵后，二千五百万苏联人民曾因此流离失所。但是，在斯大林战后五年计划的头四年中，国家的企业、机关、地方苏维埃以及居民，在国家贷款帮助之下，已在各城镇与工人住区中建造和修缮了七千二百万平方公尺以上的居住面积。同时，乡间也建造或修缮了二百三十万幢房屋。社论强调指出：城镇与乡村的恢复工作，对国家有极大的重要性。社论并力促各地方苏维埃政府各部及其他组织，为重建城镇、工人住区和乡村而进一步发展社会主义竞赛。
