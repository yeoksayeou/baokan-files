Title: People of All Countries Prepare to Celebrate May Day; Polish and Vietnamese Workers Step Up Production Competition; North Korea to Launch Study Campaign Among Workers
Author:
Date: 1950-04-28
Page: 4

　　People of All Countries Prepare to Celebrate May Day
　　Polish and Vietnamese Workers Step Up Production Competition North Korea to Launch Study Campaign Among Workers
　　[Xinhua News Agency, 27th] Comprehensive reports from the Polish Press Agency: The Polish people are actively preparing to celebrate International Labor Day, "May 1st."
　　Two hundred and eight construction teams across Poland are participating in the competition for "National Best Construction Team."
　　Workers at the Andniżov Textile Factory will produce 400,000 meters of fabric over their planned quota. Employees of the Słupsk Agricultural Machinery Factory will produce 3.7 million złoty (Polish currency) worth of surplus finished goods. Agricultural workers from agricultural production cooperatives across the country have announced that they will fulfill their May Day production pledges.
　　The Presidium of the Central Council of Trade Unions of Poland passed a resolution on preparations for celebrating May Day. The resolution states: This year, the Polish working class celebrates May Day with new achievements. The Miners' Law, the Enterprise Fund Law, the Extended Paid Leave Law, and the Social Inspection Work Law, which guarantee workers' welfare, have all been implemented. The Polish working class has universally become aware of its position as master of the country, giving a new socialist meaning to work discipline in the struggle to complete the economic plan, and waging a resolute struggle against class enemies who attempt to obstruct the improvement of the welfare of the working class.
　　The Central Council calls on all trade union organizations to strengthen ideological and educational work, to commend and select those who demonstrate exemplary work discipline and outstandingly complete their tasks, and to combat any phenomena that undermine work discipline and a conciliatory attitude that tolerates absenteeism.
　　[Xinhua News Agency, Pyongyang, 27th] Under the unified leadership of the General Federation of Trade Unions of North Korea, factories, mines, and enterprises throughout North Korea will hold grand commemorative meetings and sports events in various places to commemorate May Day. Various production units will hold joint performances and street performances, and will widely launch study campaigns among workers, organizing the study of the historical significance of International Labor Day and the struggle of working people around the world for lasting peace. The Fatherland Unification Democratic Front of Korea has formed a joint preparatory committee for all sectors to commemorate May Day, with 19 people including Minister of Culture and Propaganda of the Republic Government Heo Jeong-suk, Director of the Propaganda and Agitation Department of the Labor Party Central Committee Park Chang-ok, and Chairman of the General Federation of Trade Unions of North Korea Choi Kyung-deok as preparatory committee members.
　　[Xinhua News Agency, 27th] Vietnam News Agency, North Vietnam, 26th: To celebrate May Day, workers throughout Vietnam have almost completed a month-long production campaign. Since the end of March, Vietnamese workers have been working an extra hour each day or one day each week to save extra wages to support the strikes of French and North African workers, or to help Vietnamese farmers' groups purchase agricultural tools, etc. Workers in rural areas have also decided to cooperate with farmers and other sectors to engage in various services, such as building roads, bridges, or engaging in other work related to rural construction.

### 各国人民筹备庆祝五一节  波兰与越南工人正加紧生产竞赛  北朝鲜将在工人中开展学习运动

1950-04-28
第4版()
专栏：

　　各国人民筹备庆祝五一节
    波兰与越南工人正加紧生产竞赛  北朝鲜将在工人中开展学习运动
    【新华社二十七日讯】综合波兰通讯社消息：波兰人民正积极筹备庆祝“五一”国际劳动节。
    波兰全国有二百零八个建筑工作队，正在参加“全国最优建筑工作队”的竞赛。
    安德尼佐夫纺织厂的职工，将超计划多纺四十万公尺的织物。斯鲁布斯克农业机器制造厂的职工，将出产价值三百七十万兹罗提（波币）的超额成品。全国各农业生产合作社的农业工人，宣布即将完成他们的五一生产保证。
    波兰工会中央理事会主席团通过筹备庆祝五一节的决定。决定指出：今年，波兰工人阶级在新的成就下庆祝五一节。保障工人福利的矿工法，企业基金法，延长付资假期法和社会检查工作法，均已付诸实施。波兰工人阶级已普遍自觉到居于国家主人翁的地位，在为完成经济计划的斗争中，给予工作纪律以新的社会主义意义，并向企图阻挠提高工人阶级福利的阶级敌人进行着坚决的斗争。
    中央理事会号召一切工会团体加强思想与教育工作，表扬选拔那些表现模范的工作纪律和出色地完成任务的人们，并与任何破坏工作纪律的现象及容忍旷工的调和态度作斗争。
    【新华社平壤二十七日电】北朝鲜各工厂、矿山与企业，在北朝鲜职业总同盟的统一领导下，将于各地举行盛大的纪念会和体育会，以纪念五一节。各生产机关将举办联欢公演和街头公演，并将在工人中广泛展开学习运动，组织学习“五一”国际劳动节的历史意义以及当前世界各国劳动人民争取持久和平的斗争情况。朝鲜祖国统一民主主义战线已组成各界纪念五一节联合筹备委员会，由共和国政府文化宣传部长许贞淑、劳动党中央宣传鼓动部长朴昌玉、北朝鲜职业总同盟委员长崔璟德等十九人任筹备委员。
    【新华社二十七日讯】越南通讯社北越二十六日讯：为庆祝五一劳动节，全越工人几已完成为期一月的生产运动。自从三月底以来，越南工人都已每日加工一小时或每周加工一日，以便节储额外的工资，用以支持法国与北非工人的罢工，或帮助越南农民团体购买农具等。乡村地区的工人，也决定与农民及其他各界人士合作，从事各项服务，例如修筑公路、桥梁或从事与乡村建设有关的其它工作。
