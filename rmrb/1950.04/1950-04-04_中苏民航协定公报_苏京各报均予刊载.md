Title: China-Soviet Civil Aviation Agreement Communiqué Published in Soviet Capital Newspapers
Author: 
Date: 1950-04-04
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, 3rd. TASS, Moscow, 2nd dispatch: All newspapers have published the communiqué on the signing of the agreement between the Soviet Union and the People's Republic of China on the establishment of the Sino-Soviet Civil Aviation Joint Stock Company.

### 中苏民航协定公报  苏京各报均予刊载

1950-04-04
第1版()
专栏：

　　中苏民航协定公报
    苏京各报均予刊载
    【新华社三日讯】塔斯社莫斯科二日电：所有报纸都刊登了苏联与中华人民共和国签订关于创办苏中民用航空股份公司协定的公报。
