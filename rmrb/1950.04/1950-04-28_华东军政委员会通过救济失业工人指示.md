Title: East China Military and Political Committee Passes Instructions on Relieving Unemployed Workers
Author:
Date: 1950-04-28
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, Shanghai, 27th—The tenth administrative meeting of the East China Military and Political Committee was held on the 25th. Chairman Rao Shushi, Vice Chairman Ma Yinchu, Secretary-General Wu Kejian, and heads of various committees, ministries, institutes, departments, bureaus, and banks attended. After full discussion, the meeting adopted the East China Military and Political Committee's instructions on launching a donation campaign to step up relief for unemployed workers.

### 华东军政委员会通过救济失业工人指示

1950-04-28
第1版()
专栏：

　　华东军政委员会
    通过救济失业工人指示
    【新华社上海二十七日电】华东军政委员会第十次行政例会于二十五日举行。出席饶漱石主席，马寅初副主席，吴克坚秘书长及各委、部、院、署、局、行各单位负责人。会议经充分讨论后，通过了华东军政委员会关于展开捐款运动加紧救济失业工人的指示。
