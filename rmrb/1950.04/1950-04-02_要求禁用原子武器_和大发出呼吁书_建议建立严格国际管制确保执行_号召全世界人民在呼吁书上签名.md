Title: Demand the Banning of Atomic Weapons and Issue a Grand Appeal; Suggest the Establishment of Strict International Controls to Ensure Implementation; Call on People Worldwide to Sign the Appeal

Author:

Date: 1950-04-02
Page: 4

　　[Xinhua News Agency, Dispatch from Warsaw, March 31st, via the Polish Press Agency:] The Standing Committee of the World Peace Congress, according to the resolution of the third plenary session held in Stockholm in mid-March, issued the following appeal: "We demand: the unconditional prohibition of the use of atomic weapons as a tool for the mass destruction of people, and the establishment of strict international control to ensure the implementation of this decision. We consider: Any government that is the first to use atomic weapons against any other country is a war criminal. We call on people worldwide to sign this appeal."
    Standing Committee Chairman Joliot-Curie, Vice-Chairmen Fadeyev (USSR), Saye (France), Bernal (UK), Nenni (Italy), Roche (Africa), D’Arboussier (Africa), Standing Committee members Ehrenburg (USSR), Xiao San (China), Hodinova-Spurna (Czechoslovakia), Wen Youzhang (Canada) and many other committee members have signed the appeal.

### 要求禁用原子武器  和大发出呼吁书  建议建立严格国际管制确保执行  号召全世界人民在呼吁书上签名

1950-04-02
第4版()
专栏：

　　要求禁用原子武器
    和大发出呼吁书
    建议建立严格国际管制确保执行
    号召全世界人民在呼吁书上签名
    【新华社一日讯】波兰通讯社华沙三月三十一日电引巴黎消息：世界拥护和平大会常设委员会，根据三月中旬在斯德哥尔摩举行的第三次全体会议的决议，发表下列呼吁书：“我们要求：无条件地禁止把原子武器用作大规模毁灭人民的工具，并建立严格的国际管制，以确保这一决定的执行。我们认为：任何第一个使用原子武器来对付任何其他国家的政府，就是战争罪犯。我们号召全世界人民在这个呼吁书上签名。”
    和大常委会主席约里奥——居里，副主席法捷耶夫（苏）、赛扬（法）、贝尔纳（英），南尼（意），罗奇（美）、达波赛（非），常委爱伦堡（苏）、萧三（中）、霍丁诺瓦——斯普尔纳（捷）、文幼章（加）及其他委员多人，已首先在呼吁书上签名。
