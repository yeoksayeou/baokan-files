Title: French Communist Party Congress Approves Thorez Report, Calling on the Entire Party to Carry Out Extensive Propaganda to the Entire Country
Author:
Date: 1950-04-10
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Paris, April 8th: TASS reports: The 12th Congress of the French Communist Party passed a resolution on Thorez's report, stating: This Congress approves the report made by General Secretary Thorez on behalf of the Central Committee regarding the struggle for national independence and peace. The Congress specifically stipulates that party organizations and members at all levels should follow the guidance given in the report in their activities and should make every effort for the following: (1) Actively participate in the signature campaign soliciting support for the Stockholm Appeal of the Standing Committee of the World Peace Congress to ban the atomic bomb; (2) Launch a movement against the storage, transportation, and manufacture of war materials; (3) Widely publicize the eleven-point program proposed in the Thorez report to the entire country.

### 法共代表大会批准多列士报告号召全党向全国进行广泛宣传

1950-04-10
第4版()
专栏：

　　法共代表大会批准多列士报告号召全党向全国进行广泛宣传
    【新华社九日讯】塔斯社巴黎八日讯：法国共产党第十二届代表大会就多列士报告通过决议称：本届代表大会批准总书记多列士代表中央委员会所作的关于为国家独立及和平而斗争的报告。代表大会特规定各级党组织和党员在其活动中应遵行该报告中所给予的提示，并应为下述各节尽一切努力：（一）积极参加征求对世界和平大会常委会斯德哥尔摩会议禁止原子弹呼吁书的签名运动；（二）发动反对储存、运输及制造战争物资的运动；（三）向全国广泛宣传多列士报告中提出的十一点纲领。
