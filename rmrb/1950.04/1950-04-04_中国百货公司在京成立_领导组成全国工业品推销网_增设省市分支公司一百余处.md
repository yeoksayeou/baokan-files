Title: China General Merchandise Company Established in Beijing, Leads the Formation of National Industrial Products Sales Network, Establishing Over 100 Branch Companies in Provinces and Cities
Author: Wang Xiaotian, Liu Zhixin
Date: 1950-04-04
Page: 4

　　China General Merchandise Company Established in Beijing
    Leads the Formation of National Industrial Products Sales Network
    Establishing Over 100 Branch Companies in Provinces and Cities
    [By Our Newspaper] To form a national industrial products sales network and unify and strengthen the organization of state-owned general merchandise companies, the China General Merchandise Company was officially established in Beijing on the 1st of this month. The Ministry of Trade of the Central People's Government convened a meeting in Beijing on February 25th to establish the China General Merchandise Company, which concluded on March 26th. Based on the principle of supplying daily necessities of industrial products to the broad masses of farmers and urban residents, combining with cooperatives and retail companies to form a rural and urban industrial products sales network, and adhering to the business policy of small profits and quick turnover, strengthening retail, distribution, ordering and guiding production, the meeting formulated the 1950 national business and financial plan and adopted various systems and regulations. In addition to the existing North China, East China, Central South, and Southwest regional companies and their 37 affiliated branch companies, one Northwest regional company will be added; and thirty-eight provincial and municipal companies will be added in Suiyuan, Chahar, Hebei, Shanxi, Pingyuan, Tianjin, Beijing, Shandong, Southern Jiangsu, Northern Jiangsu, Northern Anhui, Southern Anhui, Zhejiang, Fujian, Shanghai, Nanjing, Qingdao, Hubei, Hunan, Jiangxi, Guangdong, Guangxi, Henan, Wuhan City, Eastern Sichuan, Southern Sichuan, Western Sichuan, Northern Sichuan, Yunnan, Guizhou, Xikang, Chongqing, Shaanxi, Qinghai, Ningxia, Gansu, Xinjiang, and Xi'an; sixty-seven branch companies; fifty-six sub-branch companies; eighty-four outlets; four wholesale departments; and forty-eight retail stores. The China General Merchandise Company was officially established on April 1st at Shilaoniang Hutong, Xisi North, Beijing, and began operations. (Wang Xiaotian, Liu Zhixin)

### 中国百货公司在京成立  领导组成全国工业品推销网  增设省市分支公司一百余处
王筱天　刘之信
1950-04-04
第4版()
专栏：

　　中国百货公司在京成立
    领导组成全国工业品推销网 
    增设省市分支公司一百余处
    【本报讯】为组成全国工业品推销网，统一、加强国营百货公司机构，中国百货公司已于本月一日在京正式成立。中央人民政府贸易部于二月二十五日在京召开成立中国百货公司会议，于三月二十六日结束。会议根据向广大农民、城市市民供给日用必需工业品，结合合作社、零售公司构成农村与城市工业品销售网，根据薄利多销、加强零售、通过经销、定货指导生产的经营方针，制订了一九五○年全国业务与财务计划：并通过了各种制度条例。除原有华北、华东、中南、西南区公司及所属三十七个分支公司外，增设西北区公司一个；绥远、察哈尔、河北、山西、平原、天津、北京、山东、苏南、苏北、皖北、皖南、浙江、福建、上海、南京、青岛、湖北、湖南、江西、广东、广西、河南、武汉市、川东、川南、川西、川北、云南、贵州、西康、重庆、陕西、青海、宁夏、甘肃、新疆、西安等省市公司三十八个；分公司六十七个；支公司五十六个；门市部八十四个；批发部四个；零售店四十八个。中国百货公司四月一日在北京西四北石老娘胡同正式成立，开始办公。（王筱天、刘之信）
