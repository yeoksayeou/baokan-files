Title: Welcoming the "May 1st" Labor Day: Workers in Wuhan and Other Places Show High Enthusiasm for Production
Author:
Date: 1950-04-18
Page: 1

　　Welcoming the "May 1st" Labor Day
    Workers in Wuhan and Other Places Show High Enthusiasm for Production
    [Xinhua News Agency, Hankou, 17th] Workers in Wuhan and other places are stepping up production to greet the "May 1st" International Labor Day. After overfulfilling the production task for the first quarter of 1950, the workers of the Jiang'an Locomotive Factory of the Hankou Branch of the Beijing-Hankou Railway are continuing to work hard to complete the production task for April ahead of schedule. Workers Yue Xizhang and others from the mechanical workshop and the sand casting factory of the factory are preparing to complete the research on the manufacturing methods of the chopping machine and the brake shoe machine before "May 1st." Workers and staff of the Hubei Mechanical Reclamation Office are striving to achieve self-sufficiency from April onwards and to overfulfill the task of manufacturing "agricultural tools for loans" to increase the supply of agricultural tools to disaster areas. The farm of the Reclamation Office also plans to reclaim 4,000 mu of wasteland, apply 96,000 jin of fertilizer (oil cake), sort out 24,000 jin of cotton seeds, and sow cotton on 4,000 mu of land within one month. Wuhan Match Factory, Wuchang Maintenance Factory of Hubei Highway Bureau, and others have also formulated production plans before "May 1st." The preparatory committee of the Jiangxi Provincial Federation of Trade Unions and the Jiangxi Provincial Working Committee of the New Democratic Youth League have issued a joint notice to launch a production campaign to welcome "Red May." Members of the Youth League of the Kaifeng Locomotive Section of the Longhai Railway in Henan Province formulated a spare-time processing and production plan in mid-March.

### 迎接“五一”劳动节  武汉等地工人生产情绪高涨

1950-04-18
第1版()
专栏：

　　迎接“五一”劳动节
    武汉等地工人生产情绪高涨
    【新华社汉口十七日电】武汉等地工人，正以加紧生产的行动来迎接“五一”国际劳动节。京汉铁路汉口分局江岸机厂的工人，在超额完成一九五○年第一季的生产任务后，继续为提前完成四月份的生产任务而努力。该厂机械工场和翻沙厂的工人岳锡章等，准备在“五一”前完成剁剉机、闸瓦机制造方法的研究。湖北机械农垦处的工人和职员，争取从四月份起开始做到自给自足，和超额完成制造“贷放农具”的工作，以增加灾区农具的供应。农垦处农场亦计划在一个月中开垦四千亩荒地，施肥（油饼）九万六千斤。整理棉种二万四千斤，播种棉花四千亩。武汉火柴厂、湖北公路局武昌保养厂等也纷纷制订了“五一”前的生产计划。江西省总工会筹委会和新民主主义青年团江西省工作委员会已发出了迎接“红五月”展开生产运动的联合通知。河南陇海路开封机务段的青年团团员，在三月中旬即订出业余加工生产计划。
