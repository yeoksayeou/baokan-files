　　Erratum
    Regarding the lyrics and musical score of the National Anthem published on page four of the People's Daily on March 5th, the "愿" (yuàn, meaning "willing" or "desire") character in "起来！不愿做奴隶的人们！" (Qǐlái! Bú yuàn zuò núlì de rénmen!, meaning "Arise! Ye who refuse to be slaves!") should have been a dotted eighth note in the musical notation. Due to a clerical error during transcription, the flag of the note was omitted, resulting in a dotted quarter note. We hereby issue this correction.
      Ministry of Culture of the Central People's Government
            All-China Musicians Association

### 来函更正
中央人民政府文化部　中华全国音乐工作者协会
1950-04-27
第4版()
专栏：

　　来函更正
    三月五日人民日报第四版刊登的国歌词谱，其中“起来！不愿做奴隶的人们！”的“愿”字，五线谱上应为附点八分音符，抄写时误将符尾脱落，致成附点四分音符，特此更正。
      中央人民政府文化部
            中华全国音乐工作者协会
