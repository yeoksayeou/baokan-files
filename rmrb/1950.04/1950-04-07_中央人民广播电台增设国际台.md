Title: Central People's Broadcasting Station Adds International Station
Author:
Date: 1950-04-07
Page: 3

Xinhua News Agency, April 6th: The Central People's Broadcasting Station will add an international broadcasting station from April 10th, call sign "Radio Peking." Wavelength: 428.57 meters, 700 kilocycles; 29.24 meters, 10260 kilocycles; 19.92 meters, 15060 kilocycles. The station's broadcast program is tentatively scheduled once a day, including news and communications in English, Japanese, Indonesian, Vietnamese, Thai, and Burmese; news and communications in Xiamen dialect, Chaozhou dialect, Hakka dialect, and Cantonese for overseas Chinese; Mandarin news, commentaries, and cultural programs; and recorded news for use by overseas Chinese newspapers. In May, the station will add Korean language programs and Mongolian and Tibetan language programs for Mongolian and Tibetan compatriots, and increase the number of broadcasts. The station's tentative program schedule is as follows: Beijing time 16:30, recorded news for overseas Chinese; 18:00, Japanese program; 18:30, Indonesian program; 19:00, Xiamen dialect program; 19:30, Hakka dialect program; 20:00, Vietnamese program; 20:30, Cantonese program; 21:00, Mandarin news, commentary; 21:30, English program; 22:00, Thai program; 22:30, Chaozhou dialect program; 23:00, Burmese program.

### 中央人民广播电台增设国际台

1950-04-07
第3版()
专栏：

　　中央人民广播电台增设国际台
    【新华社六日讯】中央人民广播电台从四月十号起增设国际广播电台，呼号为“北京广播电台”。波长：四二八·五七公尺，七○○千周；二九·二四公尺，一○二六○千周；一九·九二公尺，一五○六○千周。该台播音节目暂定每天一次，包括英语、日语、印尼语、越南语、暹罗语、缅甸语的新闻、通讯，对海外侨胞的厦门话、潮州话、客家话、广州话的新闻、通讯，普通话新闻、评论、文艺节目，以及供华侨报纸采用的记录新闻。五月份该台将增设朝鲜语和为蒙藏同胞而设的蒙语、藏语节目，并增加播音次数。该台暂定节目时间表如下：北京时间十六时三十分，对海外侨胞记录新闻；十八时，日语节目；十八时三十分，印尼语节目；十九时，厦门话节目；十九时三十分，客家话节目；二十时，越南语节目；二十时三十分，广州话节目；二十一时，普通话新闻，评论；二十一时三十分，英语节目；二十二时，暹罗语节目；二十二时三十分，潮州话节目；二十三时，缅甸语节目。
