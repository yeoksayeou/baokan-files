Title: Executive Committee of the International Democratic Women's Federation Meets; Representatives Unanimously Endorse Appeal; My Representative Bai Yun Reports on the Great Significance of the Asian Women's Conference
Author:
Date: 1950-04-22
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency, April 21st: A comprehensive TASS dispatch: The Executive Committee of the International Democratic Women's Federation held its ninth meeting on the 18th in Helsinki, the capital of Finland. Forty-six representatives from nineteen countries, including the Soviet Union, China, Czechoslovakia, Germany, Mongolia, France, Italy, the United Kingdom, the United States, Finland, and India, attended the meeting. The meeting was conducted under the banner of "Unite in the struggle for peace and for survival."

　　At the first day's meeting, the representatives reported on the activities of women's organizations in their respective countries. The Finnish representative, Kuusinen, pointed out in her report: Finnish democratic women are resolutely fighting against the reactionary plot to turn Finland into an anti-Soviet springboard. The German representative, Dauneman, said: German women are playing a major role in the German people's struggle against imperialist aggression plans and for the unification and democracy of Germany. The French representative, Tournade, said: "We will never let our sons fight against the Soviets—this statement has won the approval of millions of French women." Representatives from Eastern European people's democracies described the brilliant and splendid scene of victorious socialist construction and the growing peace movement in their countries. Other speakers included women representatives from Sweden, the Netherlands, Mongolia, and the United States. The speakers unanimously endorsed the appeal of the Standing Committee of the World Peace Congress calling for the unconditional prohibition of atomic bombs and the designation of first users as war criminals.

　　On the 19th, after unanimously passing a letter to the women of the world, the meeting proceeded with a report by the Chinese representative, Bai Yun, on the work of the Asian Women's Representative Conference held in December of last year in Beijing, the capital of China. She emphasized the great importance of this representative conference in the struggle for the liberation of women in colonial and semi-colonial countries. She said: The representative conference determined the direction and tasks of the women's movement in Asian countries, raised the political awareness of the representatives, educated them in the spirit of internationalism, made their ranks more united, and more firmly linked the women of Asian countries with the women of all countries in the world. After the Chinese representative's report, the meeting discussed the report. The Indian and British representatives both emphasized the historical importance of the Asian Women's Representative Conference to the international women's movement.

### 国际民主妇联执委会开会  各代表一致拥护和大呼吁书  我代表白芸报告亚洲妇代会重大意义

1950-04-22
第4版()
专栏：

　　国际民主妇联执委会开会
    各代表一致拥护和大呼吁书
    我代表白芸报告亚洲妇代会重大意义
    【新华社二十一日讯】综合塔斯社消息：国际民主妇联执委会于十八日在芬兰首都赫尔辛基举行第九次会议。出席会议的有苏、中、捷、德、蒙、法、意、英、美、芬、印等十九个国家的四十六名代表。会议是在“在争取和平与争取生存的斗争中团结起来”的旗帜下进行的。
    在第一日会议上，与会代表分别报告各国妇女组织的活动情况。芬兰代表库西宁在报告中指出：芬兰民主妇女正在坚决地和反动派变芬兰为反苏跳板的阴谋进行斗争。德国代表道纳曼说：德国妇女在德国人民反对帝国主义侵略计划与争取德意志的统一和民主的斗争中，起着重大的作用。法国代表图尔纳德说：“我们决不让我们的儿子们对苏作战——这句话已赢得了千百万法国妇女的赞成。”东欧人民民主国家的代表，在会议上描述了她们国家中胜利的社会主义建设与日益增长的拥护和平运动的光辉灿烂的情景。其他发言的尚有瑞典、荷兰、蒙古与美国的妇女代表。发言者一致拥护世界和平大会常设委员会要求无条件禁止原子弹并将首先使用者定为战犯的呼吁书。
    十九日，会议在一致通过告世界妇女书后，继由中国代表白芸报告去年十二月在中国首都北京举行的亚洲妇女代表会议的工作。她着重指出这次代表会议在殖民地与半殖民地国家妇女解放斗争中的巨大重要性。她说：代表会议确定了亚洲各国妇女运动的方向与任务，提高了代表们的政治觉悟，以国际主义的精神教育了她们，使她们的队伍团结得更紧密，使亚洲各国妇女和全世界各国妇女更坚实地连结在一起。中国代表报告毕，会议进行了对于这个报告的讨论，印度代表和英国代表均着重指出亚洲妇女代表会议对于国际妇女运动的历史重要性。
