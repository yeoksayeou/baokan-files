Title: Spring Rain Generally Falls on the Chahar Plain
Author:
Date: 1950-04-07
Page: 2

Xinhua News Agency, Zhangjiakou, 4th—Spring rain fell generally throughout Chahar Province from 1:00 AM to 6:00 AM on the 3rd, and the amount of rainfall has connected with the damp soil underground. The Chahar Provincial People's Government has already telegraphed instructions to the special districts and county people's governments to step up the organization of the masses, complete the cultivation of the uncultivated land within a set time, carry out crash afforestation, and further promote seed soaking and seed mixing work.

Xinhua News Agency, Xinxiang, 6th—According to reports received here: rain fell generally in various parts of Pingyuan Province on the 25th and 26th of March. In the Heze, Liaocheng, Puyang, and Xinxiang special districts, it was about four finger-widths; the Heze special district was completely soaked through, and parts of the Liaocheng and Puyang special districts were soaked. On the evening of April 2nd, there was another light rain in the Anyang and Xinxiang special districts. These several spring rains have guaranteed spring plowing and sowing, and will also have a certain effect on increasing wheat production. However, due to continuous cloudy and rainy weather, wheat seedlings in individual areas have developed jaundice.

### 察哈尔平原普降春雨

1950-04-07
第2版()
专栏：

　　察哈尔平原普降春雨
    【新华社张家口四日电】察哈尔省各地于三日一时至六时普落春雨，雨量均已与地下湿土衔接。察省人民政府，当已电令各专区及县人民政府加紧组织群众，限期耕完白茬地，突击植树，并进一步推广浸种拌种工作。
    【新华社新乡六日电】据此间所收到的报告：平原省各地已于三月二十五、六两日普遍落雨，菏泽、聊城、濮阳、新乡四专区约有四指左右；下透地者有菏泽专区全部，聊城、濮阳专区各一部。四月二日晚安阳、新乡专区又落一次小雨。这几次春雨保证了春耕播种，对麦子增产亦将有一定作用。但由于连续阴雨，个别地区麦苗已发生黄疸病。
