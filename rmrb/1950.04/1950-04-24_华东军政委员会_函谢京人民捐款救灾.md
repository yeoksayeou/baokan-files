Title: East China Military and Political Committee Expresses Gratitude to Beijing People for Donating Funds for Disaster Relief
Author:
Date: 1950-04-24
Page: 4

　　East China Military and Political Committee
    Sends Letter of Thanks to Beijing People for Donating Funds for Disaster Relief
    [Beijing News Agency Dispatch] Part of the donations raised by all circles in Beijing in last year's disaster relief campaign, along with part of the disaster relief donations saved by central government agencies, totaling 230 million yuan, was transferred to the East China region through the Ministry of Internal Affairs. The East China Military and Political Committee has further allocated these funds to severely affected areas in northern Jiangsu and Shandong to provide relief to disaster victims. Recently, the Beijing Municipal People's Government received a letter of thanks from the East China Military and Political Committee, forwarded by the Ministry of Internal Affairs of the Central People's Government, expressing gratitude to central government agencies and people from all walks of life in Beijing for their active assistance to disaster-stricken compatriots in East China. The letter stated that the concern and assistance from central government agencies and the people of Beijing have greatly encouraged disaster victims in East China in their efforts towards production and self-help.

### 华东军政委员会  函谢京人民捐款救灾

1950-04-24
第4版()
专栏：

　　华东军政委员会
    函谢京人民捐款救灾
    【京市新闻处讯】京市各界在去年救灾运动中所募集的部分捐款与中央各机关部分节约救灾捐款共计二亿三千万元，经内务部转汇华东区后，已由华东军政委员会转拨苏北山东等地，救济重灾区灾民。日前，京市人民政府收到中央人民政府内务部转来华东军政委员会致谢函一件，对中央各机关及京市各界人民积极援助华东灾胞一事，表示谢意，并称：由于中央各机关及京市人民的关怀与援助，使华东区灾胞在生产自救上，得到极大的鼓励。
