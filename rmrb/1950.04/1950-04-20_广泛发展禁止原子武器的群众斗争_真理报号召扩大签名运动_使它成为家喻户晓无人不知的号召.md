Title: Broadly Develop the Mass Struggle to Ban Atomic Weapons. *Pravda* Calls for Expanding the Signature Campaign, Making it a Household Slogan Known to All.
Author:
Date: 1950-04-20
Page: 4

　　Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, 19th: Vasiliev, in the first section of *Pravda*'s international commentary on the 16th, emphasized that in the struggle for peace, it is very important to develop the broadest mass movement to ban atomic weapons.

　　The author continued: The number of people signing the World Peace Congress Standing Committee's appeal is increasing. The Secretariat of the World Federation of Democratic Youth, representing 60 million young men and women of different political views and religions, announced its participation in the appeal of the World Peace Congress Standing Committee. The Standing Committee of the French Peace Movement is mobilizing democratic organizations in the country to make the call to ban atomic weapons a household slogan known to all. Participants in the peace movement are collecting signatures from thousands and hundreds of people on the streets of London. All parts of Italy are holding provincial peace conferences, and all cities are launching a campaign to vote in favor of proposals to defend peace. In Germany, Canada, Poland, Korea, Romania, Cuba, Bulgaria, and Lebanon, the signature campaign is expanding.

　　The article pointed out that the masses of people in the Marshall Plan countries are actively opposing imperialist aggressors by signing the appeal to ban atomic weapons. The vital interests of freedom-loving people around the world firmly demand that the scale of the signature campaign be further expanded and made more organized.

### 广泛发展禁止原子武器的群众斗争  真理报号召扩大签名运动  使它成为家喻户晓无人不知的号召

1950-04-20
第4版()
专栏：

　　广泛发展禁止原子武器的群众斗争
    真理报号召扩大签名运动
    使它成为家喻户晓无人不知的号召
    【新华社十九日讯】塔斯社莫斯科讯：瓦西里耶夫十六日在真理报国际评论的第一部分，着重指出在争取和平的斗争中，发展禁止原子武器这一最广泛的群众运动，是很重要的。
    作者继称：在世界拥护和平大会常设委员会的呼吁书上签名的人数，正在增加。世界民主青联书记处，代表六千万政见与宗教信仰不同的青年男女，宣布参加和大常委会这一呼吁。法国的和平运动常设委员会正在动员该国民主组织，使禁止原子武器的呼吁，成为家喻户晓，无人不知的号召。和平运动的参加者在伦敦街头征集成千成百的人签名。意大利全国各地正纷纷召开各省和平大会，一切城市正在展开投票拥护保卫和平建议的运动。在德国、加拿大、波兰、朝鲜、罗马尼亚、古巴、保加利亚和黎巴嫩，征集签名的运动正在扩大中。
    该文指出各马歇尔化国家的人民群众正在禁止原子武器的呼吁书上签名，来积极反对帝国主义侵略者。全世界爱好自由的各国人民的切身利益，坚定不移地要求把征集签名运动的规模，更加扩大，并使它更有组织性。
