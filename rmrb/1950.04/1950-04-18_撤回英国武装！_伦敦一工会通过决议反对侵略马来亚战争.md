Title: Withdraw British Armed Forces! London Union Passes Resolution Against War of Aggression in Malaya
Author:
Date: 1950-04-18
Page: 4
　　
　　Withdraw British Armed Forces!
    London Union Passes Resolution Against War of Aggression in Malaya
    [Xinhua News Agency, 17th] TASS, London, 14th: The *Daily Worker* reports that the Stoke Newington (in North London) branch of the Amalgamated Engineering Union has passed a resolution strongly opposing the British war against the people of Malaya, demanding the immediate withdrawal of British armed forces from this avaricious war, and the sending home of the Gurkhas and the various barbaric and bloodthirsty tribal people.

### 撤回英国武装！  伦敦一工会通过决议反对侵略马来亚战争

1950-04-18
第4版()
专栏：

　　撤回英国武装！
    伦敦一工会通过决议反对侵略马来亚战争
    【新华社十七日讯】塔斯社伦敦十四日电引《工人日报》称：英国混合机器工会斯托克·纽文顿（在伦敦市北区）分会通过决议，强烈地反对英国对马来亚人民的战争，要求立即从这个贪利的战争中把英国的武装部队撤回，并把廓尔喀和各野蛮嗜教的部落人送回家去。
