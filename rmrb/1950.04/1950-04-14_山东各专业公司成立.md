Title: Shandong Establishes Various Specialized Companies
Author:
Date: 1950-04-14
Page: 2

　　Xinhua News Agency, Jinan, 12th: Shandong Province has formally established various specialized companies. According to the decision of the Government Administration Council of the Central People's Government on unifying national financial and economic work and the decision on implementing unified national state-owned trade, the Shandong Provincial Trade General Company ceased operations on April 8th. On the same day, the China Grain Company Shandong Provincial Company, the China Cotton Yarn & Cloth Company Jinan Branch, the China Native Produce Company Shandong Provincial Company, the China General Merchandise Company Shandong Provincial Company, the China Salt Industry Company Shandong Provincial Company, the China Coal Industry Company Shandong Provincial Company, and the China Egg Products Company Jinan Branch were established. Furthermore, eleven provincial-level specialized trade organizations, including those dealing with oils, bristles, petroleum, and imports, have been established in Qingdao. Each specialized company will also establish branches and sub-branches in major cities and towns before the 15th of this month, as needed. In addition, to control the retail market and stabilize prices, the Provincial Department of Industry and Commerce has decided to establish retail companies and stores in Jinan, Qingdao, and Xuzhou to directly supply the needs of the people.

### 山东各专业公司成立

1950-04-14
第2版()
专栏：

　　山东各专业公司成立
    【新华社济南十二日电】山东省各专业公司业已正式成立。根据中央人民政府政务院关于统一国家财政经济工作的决定及统一全国国营贸易实施办法的决定，山东省贸易总公司已于四月八日停止办公，同日建立了中国粮食公司山东省公司、中国花纱布公司济南分公司、中国土产公司山东省公司、中国百货公司山东省公司、中国盐业公司山东省公司、中国煤业公司山东省公司、中国蛋品公司济南分公司，并在青岛建立油脂、猪鬃、石油、进口等省一级专业贸易机构十一处。各专业公司并将根据需要，于本月十五日前在各主要城市和市镇创设分、支机构。此外，省工商部为掌握零售市场，稳定物价，更决定在济南、青岛、徐州三市设零售公司与商店，以便直接供给人民群众需要。
