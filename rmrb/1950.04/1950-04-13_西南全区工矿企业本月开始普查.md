Title: Southwest Region-Wide Survey of Industrial and Mining Enterprises to Begin This Month
Author:
Date: 1950-04-13
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, Chongqing, 12th. The Southwest Financial and Economic Committee, the Warehouse Material Inventory and Allocation Committee, the Chongqing Enterprise Bureau of the Ministry of Industry, and the Bank of Communications have jointly formed the Southwest Industrial Survey Committee. In accordance with the decision of the Central Government Administration Council, they will conduct a comprehensive survey of industrial and mining enterprises throughout the Southwest region. The survey will begin on April 20th, and a comprehensive statistical report will be submitted to the Central Government by the end of August.

### 西南全区工矿企业本月开始普查

1950-04-13
第1版()
专栏：

　　西南全区工矿企业本月开始普查
    【新华社重庆十二日电】西南财经委员会与仓库物资清理调配委员会、工业部重庆市企业局及交通银行等单位已合组西南工业普查委员会，遵照中央政务院决定，进行全西南地区内的工矿企业普查。普查将自四月二十日开始，八月底作综合统计报告送达中央。
