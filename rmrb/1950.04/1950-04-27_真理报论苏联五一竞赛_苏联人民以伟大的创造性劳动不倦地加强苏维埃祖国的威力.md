Title: Pravda on the Soviet May Day Competition: The Soviet People Tirelessly Strengthen the Power of the Soviet Motherland with Great Creative Labor
Author:
Date: 1950-04-27
Page: 4
　　
Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, 26th: Pravda published an editorial on the 23rd, discussing the ever-growing socialist competition to celebrate May Day, stating: Millions of workers, collective farmers, and intellectuals have already joined the May Day competition. Cheerful news reporting on the achievements of the competition is coming from all over the Soviet Union. The Soviet people are tirelessly strengthening the power of the Soviet motherland and implementing Stalin's grand plan for building a communist society with great creative labor that determines the course of historical development.

### 真理报论苏联五一竞赛  苏联人民以伟大的创造性劳动不倦地加强苏维埃祖国的威力

1950-04-27
第4版()
专栏：

　　真理报论苏联五一竞赛
    苏联人民以伟大的创造性劳动不倦地加强苏维埃祖国的威力
    【新华社二十六日讯】塔斯社莫斯科讯：真理报于二十三日发表社论，论述规模越来越大的庆祝五一节的社会主义竞赛称：千百万的工人、集体农民与知识分子，业已参加了庆祝五一节的竞赛。报道竞赛成就的愉快消息，正自苏联各地传来。苏联人民以决定历史发展进程的伟大创造性劳动，来不倦地加强苏维埃祖国的威力，和实行斯大林建设共产主义社会的宏大计划。
