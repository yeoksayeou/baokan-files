Title: Central South Region Heavy Industry Department Convenes Industrial Conference
Author:
Date: 1950-04-16
Page: 2
Xinhua News Agency, April 12th: The first industrial conference of the Central South Military and Political Committee's Heavy Industry Department opened in Hankou on the 3rd of this month. In addition to determining this year's production guidelines and tasks, the conference will focus on discussing factory and mine safety, economic accounting, and wage labor insurance issues. Attendees include 180 administrative heads of directly subordinate and provincial/municipal state-owned factories and mines under the Central South Heavy Industry Department, trade union cadres, and employee representatives. The conference is scheduled to last for fifteen days.

### 中南区重工业部召开工业会议

1950-04-16
第2版()
专栏：

　　中南区重工业部召开工业会议
    【新华社十二日讯】中南区军政委员会重工业部第一届工业会议已于本月三日在汉口开幕。会议除确定今年生产方针和任务外，将着重讨论厂矿安全、经济核算以及工资劳动保险等问题。出席会议的有中南重工业部直属的和省市营的厂矿行政负责人、工会干部、职工代表共一百八十人。会期预定为十五天。
