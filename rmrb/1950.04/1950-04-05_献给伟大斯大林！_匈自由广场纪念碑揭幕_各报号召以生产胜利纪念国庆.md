Title: "Dedicated to the Great Stalin!" Unveiling of the Monument in Liberty Square, Hungary. Newspapers Call for Production Victories to Commemorate National Day.
Author: 
Date: 1950-04-05
Page: 4

　　Xinhua News Agency, Budapest, April 3rd: The Hungarian people began a carnival on the 2nd to celebrate the fifth anniversary of the Soviet army's liberation of Hungary. The festival began with the grand unveiling ceremony of the monument in Liberty Square, with the inscription on the base of the monument reading: "Dedicated to the Great Stalin. Respectfully presented by the grateful Hungarian people." Hungarian government and Workers' Party leaders, representatives of the working people, and diplomatic representatives from the Soviet Union and various people's democratic countries attended the unveiling ceremony. On the same day, the Hungarian people laid wreaths at the Soviet Army Monument in Budapest.

　　Delegations from various countries attending the grand ceremony have arrived here one after another, including the Soviet Union, the People's Republic of China, Poland, Czechoslovakia, Bulgaria, Albania, and North Korea; delegations from the German Democratic Republic and Romania will also arrive to participate.

　　Hungarian newspapers have published commemorative articles, pointing out that the Hungarian people should offer new victories in production as gifts for the festival.

### “献给伟大斯大林！”  匈自由广场纪念碑揭幕  各报号召以生产胜利纪念国庆

1950-04-05
第4版()
专栏：

　　“献给伟大斯大林！” 
    匈自由广场纪念碑揭幕
    各报号召以生产胜利纪念国庆
    【新华社四日讯】综合塔斯社布达佩斯三日讯：匈牙利人民于二日开始了庆祝苏军解放匈牙利五周年的狂欢节。节日从自由广场纪念碑的隆重揭幕典礼开始，纪念碑座上刻着：“献给伟大斯大林。感激您的匈牙利人民敬献”。匈牙利政府与工人党领袖，劳动人民代表，和苏联与各人民民主国家的外交代表均参加揭幕礼。同日，匈牙利人民向达布佩斯苏军纪念碑敬献花圈。
    赴匈参加盛典的各国代表团已先后抵此，包括苏联、中华人民共和国、波、捷、保、阿尔巴尼亚和朝鲜等国；德意志民主共和国和罗马尼亚的代表团亦将赶来参加。
    匈牙利报纸均刊登纪念专文，指出匈牙利人民要以生产的新胜利作为节日的献礼。
