Title: Fishing Prohibited on the Banks of the Imperial Moat River

Author:

Date: 1950-04-24
Page: 4

　　Fishing Prohibited on the Banks of the Imperial Moat River
    [This Newspaper News] According to the Beijing News Agency: In order to maintain the architecture of the river walls on both sides of the Tiananmen Imperial Moat River and to maintain the safety of traffic order, the Beijing Public Security Bureau issued a notice prohibiting fishing on both sides of the river.

### 御带河畔禁止钓鱼

1950-04-24
第4版()
专栏：

　　御带河畔禁止钓鱼
    【本报讯】据京市新闻处讯：京市公安局为维护天安门御带河两岸河墙之建筑及维持交通秩序之安全，发出布告禁止在该河两岸钓鱼。
