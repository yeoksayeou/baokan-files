Title: US Imperialism Ships Batch of Aviation Gasoline to Taiwan to Help Chiang Kai-shek Remnants Bomb China
Author:
Date: 1950-04-02
Page: 1

　　Xinhua News Agency, 1st. American imperialism is shipping a batch of aviation gasoline to Taiwan to aid the Chiang Kai-shek bandit remnants in bombing the Chinese people. According to a March 29th dispatch from the Associated Press in San Francisco, the American shipping company vessel "Oregon" loaded 708 auxiliary gasoline tanks for military aircraft and set sail from the United States on the 29th for Keelung, Taiwan.

### 美帝为助残匪轰炸飞机汽油一批运台

1950-04-02
第1版()
专栏：

　　美帝为助残匪轰炸飞机汽油一批运台
    【新华社一日讯】美国援助台湾国民党残匪用以进行轰炸中国人民的飞机汽油一批正在运台途中。据美联社旧金山三月二十九日电，美国轮船公司“俄勒冈”号货轮装载军用机所用辅助汽油箱七百零八个，于二十九日由美启碇赴台湾基隆。
