Title: Beijing Flower & Cloth Branch Company Sells Calico at Low Prices
Author:
Date: 1950-04-24
Page: 4

　　Beijing Flower & Cloth Branch Company
    Sells Calico at Low Prices
    [This newspaper's dispatch] To supply citizens with spring and summer clothing, the Beijing Flower & Cloth Branch Company has newly shipped dozens of types of seasonal calico from Shanghai and is promoting them at low prices; it will also sell a large amount of accumulated cloth remnants at a 30% discount.
    In order to strengthen the exchange of goods between urban and rural areas, the company is promoting sales to the countryside. It has established sales locations in each branch company and affiliated flower collection team in Nan苑, Dingxing, Zhuoxian, and Tongzhou to directly supply farmers and exchange for raw materials. (Communications Group)

### 北京花纱布分公司  廉价出售花布

1950-04-24
第4版()
专栏：

　　北京花纱布分公司
    廉价出售花布
    【本报讯】北京花纱布分公司为供应市民春夏服装，新由上海运来应时花布数十种，低价推销；并将积存的大量布头，七扣出售。
    该公司为加强城乡物资交流，面向农村推销，已于南苑、定兴、涿县、通州各支公司及所属收花小组，普设售货地点，直接供应农民，并换回原料。（通讯组）
