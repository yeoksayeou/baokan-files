Title: Vice Chairman Soong Arrives in Beijing Yesterday; Zhang Lan, Lin Boqu, and Others Greet Her at the Station
Author:
Date: 1950-04-04
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, April 3rd:

　　Vice Chairman Soong Ching Ling of the Central People's Government arrived in Beijing from Shanghai at 7:30 this morning. Those who went to the station to welcome her included Vice Chairman Zhang Lan of the Central People's Government, Secretary-General Lin Boqu, Premier Zhou Enlai of the Government Administration Council, Vice Premiers Guo Moruo, Huang Yanpei, and Vice President Deng Yingchao of the All-China Democratic Women's Federation.

### 宋副主席昨抵京  张澜林伯渠等赴站欢迎

1950-04-04
第1版()
专栏：

　　宋副主席昨抵京
    张澜林伯渠等赴站欢迎
    【新华社三日讯】中央人民政府宋庆龄副主席于今日上午七时半由沪抵京。前往车站欢迎的有中央人民政府副主席张澜，秘书长林伯渠，政务院总理周恩来，副总理郭沫若、黄炎培及中华全国民主妇女联合会副主席邓颖超等。
