Date: 1950-04-02

　　Cultural and Educational Briefs
    The Cadre School of the Ministry of Heavy Industry of the Central People's Government opened in Tong County on the 25th of this month. The school has more than 700 students. He Changgong, Deputy Minister of the Ministry of Heavy Industry, personally attended the opening ceremony.
                                  (Xu Bingzhong, Xu Shuguang, Li Jingtao)
    The "? Ji Zibai Pan" received by the Cultural Relics Bureau of the Ministry of Culture of the Central People's Government has recently been transferred to the Palace Museum for safekeeping. The museum is scheduled to move the pan to the newly collected cultural relics exhibition hall in the Qin'an Hall on the central axis of the Forbidden City for public display starting on the 1st. Afterwards, it will be added to the bronze ware exhibition hall for long-term display. (General Affairs Office of the Palace Museum)

### 文化教育简讯
徐炳忠　徐书光　李经桃
1950-04-02
第3版()
专栏：文化教育简讯

　　文化教育简讯
    中央人民政府重工业部干部学校，于本月二十五日在通县开学。该校有学员七百余人。重工业部何长工副部长曾亲往参加开学典礼。
                                  （徐炳忠、徐书光、李经桃）
    中央人民政府文化部文物局收到的“?季子白盘”，最近已拨交故宫博物院保管。该院定于一日起将该盘移至故宫中路钦安殿新收文物陈列室公开展览。嗣后将加入铜器专门陈列室长期陈列。（故宫博物院总务处）
