Title: All-China Federation of Trade Unions Holds 27th Standing Committee Enlarged Meeting to Discuss Celebrations for "May Day" / All-China Federation of Trade Unions Approves the Establishment of the China Coal Miners' Union Preparatory Committee
Author:
Date: 1950-04-01
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, 31st - The All-China Federation of Trade Unions held its 27th Standing Committee Enlarged Meeting on March 29th, presided over by Vice Chairman Liu Ningyi. The meeting discussed the methods for celebrating this year's "May Day" Labor Day and the issue of staffing for local trade unions that are not involved in production. It approved the list of representatives to be sent to Lahore for the second annual meeting of the Pakistan Federation of Trade Unions from April 21st to 23rd and to Poland for the "May Day" demonstration. It also approved the list of members, standing committee members, chairman, vice-chairmen, and the finance review committee of the National Committee of the China Postal and Telecommunications Workers' Union, the list of standing committee members of the Beijing Federation of Trade Unions, and the "Regulations on the Organization and Work of Safety and Health Committees of Coal Mine Trade Unions" proposed by the China Coal Miners' Union Preparatory Committee.

Xinhua News Agency - The All-China Federation of Trade Unions Standing Committee Enlarged Meeting has approved the decision of the National Coal Mine Trade Union Representatives Meeting on February 1st to establish the China Coal Miners' Union Preparatory Committee and the list of members of the preparatory committee. Before the official establishment of the National Committee of the China Coal Miners' Union, the preparatory committee will serve as the unified highest leading body for coal mine trade unions throughout the country. Its main tasks are to determine the date of the first National Congress of China Coal Miners' Union Members, the number of delegates from various regions, and the election methods, to formulate a draft constitution for the coal miners' union, to review the implementation of the resolutions of the previous National Coal Mine Trade Union Representatives Meeting, and to summarize the experience of collective contract signing and rationalization proposals in various coal mines. ### 全总第二十七次常委扩大会议  讨论庆祝“五一”节办法  全总批准成立中国煤矿工会筹委会

1950-04-01
第1版()
专栏：

　　全总第二十七次常委扩大会议
    讨论庆祝“五一”节办法
    全总批准成立中国煤矿工会筹委会
    【新华社三十一日讯】中华全国总工会于三月二十九日举行第二十七次常委扩大会议，由刘宁一副主席主持。会议讨论了庆祝今年“五一”劳动节的办法和关于地方工会脱离生产人员的编制问题，通过了我国派赴巴基斯坦参加四月二十一日至二十三日在拉霍尔城召开的巴基斯坦总工会第二届年会和派赴波兰参加“五一”示威运动的代表名单，批准了中国邮电工会全国委员会委员、常委正副主席及经费审查委员会名单、北京市总工会常委会名单、以及中国煤矿工会筹备委员会提出的“关于煤矿工会安全卫生委员会的组织及工作条例”。
    【新华社讯】中华全国总工会常委会扩大会议，已批准全国煤矿工会代表会议二月一日通过的关于成立中国煤矿工会筹备委员会的决定和筹备委员会委员名单。在中国煤矿工会全国委员会未正式成立前，该筹备委员会即为全国各地煤矿工会统一的最高领导机关，其主要任务为：规定第一次中国煤矿工会全国会员代表大会会期和各地出席代表名额及其选举办法，制定煤矿工会组织章程草案，检查上次全国煤矿工会代表会议决议的执行情况和总结各地煤矿签订集体合同与实行合理化建议的经验。该会筹委会委员及筹委会常务委员会委员名单如下：
