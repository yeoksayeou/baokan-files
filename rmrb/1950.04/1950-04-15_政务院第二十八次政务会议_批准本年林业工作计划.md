Title: The 28th Administrative Council Meeting of the Government Administration Council Approves This Year's Forestry Work Plan
Author:
Date: 1950-04-15
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, April 14th—The 28th Administrative Council meeting of the Central People's Government's Government Administration Council was held at 3:30 PM today. After hearing the report on forestry work by Liang Xi, Minister of the Ministry of Forestry and Land Reclamation of the Central People's Government, the meeting approved the department's current guidelines, tasks, and 1950 work plan for forestry work. The meeting heard a report on the work situation in Suiyuan Province by Dong Qiwu, Chairman of the Suiyuan Provincial People's Government, and a supplementary speech by Fu Zuoyi, Chairman of the Suiyuan Military and Political Committee, and expressed satisfaction with the province's work since the establishment of the Military and Political Committee and the Provincial People's Government. The meeting then heard a summary report on the first session of the People's Representative Conference of All Circles of Shanxi Province by Cheng Zihua, Chairman of the Shanxi Provincial People's Government. In addition, the meeting approved three appointment proposals.

### 政务院第二十八次政务会议  批准本年林业工作计划

1950-04-15
第1版()
专栏：

　　政务院第二十八次政务会议
    批准本年林业工作计划
    【新华社十四日讯】中央人民政府政务院第二十八次政务会议于今日下午三时半举行。会议在听取了中央人民政府林垦部梁希部长关于林业工作的报告后，批准了该部目前关于林业工作的方针、任务及一九五零年的工作计划。会议听取了绥远省人民政府董其武主席关于绥远工作情况的报告及绥远军政委员会傅作义主席的补充发言，并对该省自军政委员会及省人民政府成立以来的工作表示满意。会议随后又听取了山西省人民政府程子华主席关于山西省第一届各界人民代表会议的总结报告。此外，会议通过了三项任命案。
