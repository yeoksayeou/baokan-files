Title: German Democratic Women's League to Hold Third Congress
Author:
Date: 1950-04-02
Page: 4

　　German Democratic Women's League to Hold Third Congress
    [Xinhua News Agency, 1st dispatch] Deutsche Nachrichten Agentur reports: The Third Congress of the German Democratic Women's League will be held in Berlin from April 21st to 24th. Two thousand delegates from the states of the German Democratic Republic, Berlin, and West German states will participate in the conference, and foreign guests will also be invited to attend. The conference will proceed under the theme of strengthening the National Front of Democratic Germany.
    According to a Telegraph Agency Berlin dispatch on March 29th: Mrs. Elli Schmidt, Chairman of the German Democratic Women's League, announced that a preparatory committee of the "German Democratic Women's League" has been established in West Germany. This committee will organize branches of the "German Democratic Women's League" in various occupied areas of West Germany, and this work will begin in a very short period of time. The League's program is the democratization and unification of Germany, and the struggle for peace.

### 德国民主妇联将开三届大会

1950-04-02
第4版()
专栏：

　　德国民主妇联将开三届大会
    【新华社一日讯】德意志通讯社报道：德国民主妇女联盟第三届大会将于四月二十一日至二十四日在柏林举行。大会将有德意志民主共和国各州、柏林与西德各州的二千代表参加，外国来宾亦将应邀赴会。大会将在加强民主德国全国阵线的主旨下进行。
    另据电通社柏林三月二十九日消息：德国民主妇女联盟主席艾利·施密特夫人宣布，西德已成立了“德国民主妇女联盟”筹备委员会，该委员会将在西德各占领区组织“德国民主妇女联盟”支会，这个工作在极短期间即将开始。联盟的纲领是德国民主化和统一，并为争取和平而斗争。
