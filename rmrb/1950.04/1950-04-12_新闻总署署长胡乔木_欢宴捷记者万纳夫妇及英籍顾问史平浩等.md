Title: Director of the News Administration Bureau, Hu Qiaomu, Hosts a Banquet for Czech Journalist Vána and Wife, and British Advisor S.H.Smith and Others

Author: 
Date: 1950-04-12
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, April 11th: Director of the Central People's Government News Administration Bureau, Hu Qiaomu, hosted a banquet tonight to welcome Mr. Vána, a journalist from the Czechoslovak News Agency and Red Right Newspaper, and his wife, who recently arrived in Beijing from Czechoslovakia. Also welcomed were Mr. S.H. Smith, a British advisor hired by the International News Bureau of the News Administration Bureau, and his wife, Alan Winnington, and Xia Binuo. Those invited to accompany the guests included heads of units such as the People's Daily, Guangming Daily, Xinhua News Agency, Broadcasting Affairs Bureau, International News Bureau, and other journalists in Beijing. Rogov, the director of the TASS China Branch, and Kim Sung-yong, the director of the Korean Central News Agency China Branch, were also invited to attend.

### 新闻总署署长胡乔木  欢宴捷记者万纳夫妇及英籍顾问史平浩等

1950-04-12
第1版()
专栏：

　　新闻总署署长胡乔木
    欢宴捷记者万纳夫妇及英籍顾问史平浩等
    【新华社十一日讯】中央人民政府新闻总署署长胡乔木今晚设宴欢迎最近自捷克斯洛伐克抵京的捷克斯洛伐克新闻通讯社和红色权利报记者万纳及其夫人，和新闻总署国际新闻局聘请的英籍顾问史平浩及其夫人、阿兰·魏宁顿、夏庇诺。应邀作陪者有人民日报、光明日报、新华通讯社、广播事业局、国际新闻局等单位负责人和其他在京新闻界人士。塔斯社驻华总分社社长罗果夫、朝鲜中央通讯社驻华总分社社长金圣容亦应邀出席。
