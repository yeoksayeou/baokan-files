Title: Beijing May Day Preparatory Committee Moves Office to Tiananmen

Author: Jing Shi Xin Wen Chu

Date: 1950-04-29
Page: 4

　　Beijing May Day Preparatory Committee Moves Office to Tiananmen

    [Beijing Municipal News Agency Report] The capital's "May Day" Labor Day parade preparatory committee has been established and is actively carrying out its work. To facilitate the arrangement of the venue and communication with all parties, the preparatory committee's office has been moved from the Municipal Government to the West Wing within Tiananmen. If any units have matters to discuss, they may go directly to the committee's office or call Bureau 5, number 0468 to contact them.

### 京五一筹备会迁天安门办公
京市新闻处
1950-04-29
第4版()
专栏：

　　京五一筹备会迁天安门办公
    【京市新闻处讯】首都“五一”劳动节大游行筹备会成立后，正积极展开工作。为了布置会场及与各方联络方便起见，筹备会办公地点已由市府迁至天安门内西朝房。各单位如有联络事项，可径往该会办公地点或拨用电话五局○四六八接洽。
