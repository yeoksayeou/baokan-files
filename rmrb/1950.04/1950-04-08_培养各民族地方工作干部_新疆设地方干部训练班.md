Title: Training Local Ethnic Minority Cadres: Xinjiang Establishes Local Cadre Training Class
Author:
Date: 1950-04-08
Page: 3

　　Cultivating Local Cadres from All Ethnic Groups
    Xinjiang Establishes Local Cadre Training Class
    [Xinhua News Agency, Dihua, 6th] In order to cultivate local cadres from all ethnic groups in Xinjiang, the Xinjiang Bureau of the Central Committee of the Chinese Communist Party has established a local cadre training class, which officially opened on the 1st of this month. The class has recruited 306 outstanding sons and daughters (including 24 female students) of the people from twelve ethnic groups including Uyghur, Han, Hui, Kazakh, Mongolian, Manchu, Xibe, Solon, Russian, Tatar, Taranqi, and Uzbek. Uyghur students account for about half of the students. Generally, the students understand both Uyghur and Han languages. In addition to their native language, most of them can also speak two or three other ethnic languages. Wang Zhen, Secretary of the Xinjiang Bureau of the Central Committee of the Chinese Communist Party, attended and spoke at the opening ceremony. Burhan Shahidi, Chairman of the Xinjiang Provincial People's Government, also addressed the meeting. The students all firmly expressed their acceptance of the instructions of the leaders, and their willingness to redouble their efforts in study, overcome shortcomings, and serve the people wholeheartedly.

### 培养各民族地方工作干部  新疆设地方干部训练班

1950-04-08
第3版()
专栏：

　　培养各民族地方工作干部
    新疆设地方干部训练班
    【新华社迪化六日电】中共中央新疆分局为了培养新疆各民族的地方工作干部而设立的地方干部训练班，已于本月一日正式开学。该班已招收维、汉、回、哈、蒙、满、锡伯、索伦、俄罗斯、塔塔尔、塔兰其、乌兹别克等十二个民族人民的优秀子女三百零六名为学员（内女学员二十四名），其中维族学员约占一半。一般学员均了解维汉两种文字，除本民族语言外，大部并会讲两三种别的民族的语言。在开学典礼上，中共中央新疆分局书记王震曾出席讲话，新疆省人民政府主席鲍尔汉亦在会上致词。学员们均坚决表示接受各位首长指示，愿加倍努力学习，克服缺点，全心全意为人民服务。
