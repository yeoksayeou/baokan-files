Title: National Warehouse Material Clearing and Allocation Committee: Chen Yun and Yang Lisan Appointed as Chairman and Vice-Chairman

Author: 
Date: 1950-04-01
Page: 1

　　National Warehouse Material Clearing and Allocation Committee
    Chen Yun and Yang Lisan Appointed as Chairman and Vice-Chairman
    [Xinhua News Agency, 31st] The State Council of the Central People's Government has appointed Chen Yun as Chairman of the National Warehouse Material Clearing and Allocation Committee, Yang Lisan as Vice-Chairman, and Song Shaowen, Wang Fengyuan, Sun Yueqi, Rong Zihe, and Zhang Guojian as members, with Song Shaowen as Secretary-General and Wang Fengyuan as Deputy Secretary-General.

### 全国仓库物资清理调配委员会  陈云杨立三任正副主任

1950-04-01
第1版()
专栏：

　　全国仓库物资清理调配委员会
    陈云杨立三任正副主任
    【新华社三十一日讯】中央人民政府政务院任命陈云为全国仓库物资清理调配委员会主任，杨立三为副主任，宋劭文、王逢原、孙越崎、戎子和、张国坚为委员，宋劭文为秘书长，王逢原为副秘书长。
