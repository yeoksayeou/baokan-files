Title: From Leningrad to Siberian Villages, Soviet People Prepare Various Activities to Commemorate the 80th Anniversary of Lenin's Birth
Author:
Date: 1950-04-21
Page: 4

　　Xinhua News Agency, 18th: According to a TASS dispatch: The Soviet people are enthusiastically preparing to commemorate the 80th anniversary of Lenin's birth (April 22nd). Throughout the country, factories, institutions, higher education institutions, collective farms, and Soviet military units are holding lectures and discussions on Lenin's life and deeds. Clubs and cultural palaces are holding exhibitions about Lenin. Feature films and documentaries about Lenin are being shown everywhere.

　　In Leningrad, people are constantly visiting the Leningrad Central Lenin Museum branch. Twenty years ago, on Stalin's suggestion, the branch was opened in the Marble Palace on the Neva embankment. Now, six thousand historical and Party documents, including valuable personal manuscripts of Lenin, are on display in twenty-two halls. The residence on Ilyich Street where Lenin lived in 1894 has now been converted into a museum.

　　In a hall at Leningrad University, a monument stands with the inscription: "The young Vladimir Ulyanov took his exams here." In the large square in front of the Finland Station, a bronze statue of Lenin speaking from an armored car commemorates the scene of Lenin's return from exile in April 1917. These places are crowded with visitors. The Smolny Institute, which served as the headquarters of the October Revolution, and the famous conference hall in the institute where Lenin announced the birth of the Soviet regime, are the places that most inspire awe and excitement among visitors.

　　In the Siberian village of Yermakovskoye, a plaque on the wall of a farmhouse reads: "In 1899, Lenin convened a meeting of seventeen Marxist political exiles here." The villagers carefully protect this historically significant building and have arranged an exhibition about Lenin there.

### 从列宁格勒直到西伯利亚乡村  苏联人民筹备各种活动  纪念列宁诞辰八十周年

1950-04-21
第4版()
专栏：

　　从列宁格勒直到西伯利亚乡村
    苏联人民筹备各种活动
    纪念列宁诞辰八十周年
    【新华社十八日讯】综合塔斯社消息：苏联人民热烈筹备纪念列宁诞辰八十周年（四月二十二日）。全国各企业、机关、高等教育机关、集体农场及苏军部队，都在举行关于列宁生平及事迹的演讲和座谈会。各俱乐部及文化宫正举行关于列宁的展览会。各地都在放映关于列宁的故事片和记录片。
    在列宁格勒，人民正络绎不绝地往访列宁格勒中央列宁博物馆分馆。二十年前，该分馆在斯大林的建议下，开设在涅瓦坝上的大理石宫中。现在二十二个厅堂内，陈列着六千件历史的和党的文献，其中包括珍贵的列宁私人文稿。一八九四年列宁住过的伊里奇街上的住宅，现已改为博物馆。
    在列宁格勒大学的一个礼堂里，竖着一块纪念碑，上面写着：年青的乌拉第米尔·乌里扬诺夫曾在此考试。在芬兰火车站前的大广场上，竖立着列宁在装甲车上演说的铜像，纪念一九一七年四月列宁流亡归国时的情景。这些地方都拥塞着参观的人流。曾为十月革命司令部的斯摩尔尼学院，和该学院中列宁宣布苏维埃政权诞生的著名会议厅，是最引起参观者肃然兴奋的地方。
    在西伯利亚叶尔马科夫斯村，一个农家的墙上，有一块碑文写着：“一八九九年列宁曾在此召集十七名马克斯主义政治流亡者的会议”。村民小心护卫着这所具有历史意义的建筑物。并在那里布置了关于列宁的展览会。
