Title: London Trades Council Resolution Opposes the Rearmament of West Germany
Author:
Date: 1950-04-17
Page: 4

　　London Trades Council Resolution
    Opposes the Rearmament of West Germany
    [Xinhua News Agency, London, 16th dispatch] TASS, London: The London Trades Council, with over 600,000 members, unanimously passed a resolution on the 13th, denouncing Churchill's proposal to rearm West Germany. The resolution stated that arming West Germany "would pose a new threat to world peace." The well-known progressive journalist, Gordon Schaffer, representing the executive committee, moved the motion saying: "Today, in West Germany, those in power are the same figures from the Nazi era, the same class, supported by German industrialists, many of whom are the old ones."

### 伦敦工会委员会决议  反对重行武装西德

1950-04-17
第4版()
专栏：

　　伦敦工会委员会决议
    反对重行武装西德
    【新华社十六日讯】塔斯社伦敦讯：拥有六十多万会员的伦敦工会委员会于十三日一致通过决议，痛斥邱吉尔的重行武装西德的建议。决议称，给西德以武器，“就会给世界和平以新的威胁”。著名的进步记者高登·斯卡夫代表执行委员会提出动议说：“今天，在西德，当权的人都是德国工业家支持下的纳粹时代同一的人物，同一的阶级，有许多就是旧人。”
