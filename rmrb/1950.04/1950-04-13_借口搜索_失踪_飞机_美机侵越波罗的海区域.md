Title: Under Pretext of Searching for "Missing" Plane, US Aircraft Invade Baltic Sea Area
Author:
Date: 1950-04-13
Page: 4

　　Xinhua News Agency, Stockholm, April 12th: All newspapers here published the following news: On April 8th, a US four-engine bomber went missing in the Baltic Sea, "at a location between Gotland Island and the Lithuanian coast." The plane carried ten people, four of whom were officers. Newspapers pointed out that this plane was flying to the Estonian coast on a special mission.
　　Xinhua News Agency, London, April 12th: A Daily Mail correspondent in Copenhagen reported that twenty-three American aircraft are searching the Baltic Sea area for the missing American bomber. The correspondent said that Danish military figures believe the scale of the search is "unusually large."
　　Xinhua News Agency, Copenhagen, April 12th: The Danish public is gradually realizing that reports about the US plane crash are a pretext for US Air Force exercises. The purpose of this exercise is to enable American pilots to become familiar with the use of the Danish Kastrup Airport and with the Baltic Sea area.

### 借口搜索“失踪”飞机  美机侵越波罗的海区域

1950-04-13
第4版()
专栏：

　　借口搜索“失踪”飞机
    美机侵越波罗的海区域
    【新华社十二日讯】塔斯社斯德哥尔摩十二日讯：此间一切报纸均刊登下列消息：在四月八日，一架美国的四引擎轰炸机在波罗的海失踪，“地点在哥特兰岛和立陶宛海岸间”。飞机上载有十人，其中四名是军官。报纸指出，这架飞机是负有特殊使命飞往爱沙尼亚海岸的。
    【新华社十二日讯】塔斯社伦敦十二日讯：每日邮报驻哥本哈根记者报道称，二十三架美国飞机正在波罗的海区域进行搜索失踪的美国轰炸机。该记者称，丹麦军事方面人士认为，这次搜索的规模，“异常宏大”。
    【新华社十二日讯】塔斯社哥本哈根十二日讯：丹麦公众渐渐认识清楚，关于美国飞机失事的报道，乃是美国空军演习的借口。这次演习的目的，在使美国驾驶员可以熟悉利用丹麦加斯杜普机场并熟悉波罗的海地区。
