Title: Soviet Workers Enthusiastically Greet May Day - Large-Scale Labor Emulation Launched - Tula Completes First Quarter Coal Production Plan Ahead of Schedule - Leningrad Factories Intensifying Efforts to Increase Production
Author:
Date: 1950-04-05
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, 3rd dispatch: A socialist emulation campaign launched by Soviet workers to celebrate May Day is unfolding on a large scale in various enterprises across the country. Tula miners have completed their first quarter coal production plan two days ahead of schedule. Compared with the first quarter of 1949, the average daily output of the Tula Coal Mine Trust has increased by 19.3 percent. The young furnace operators of the No. 3 blast furnace at the Azov Steel Plant in Zhdanov have successfully completed accelerated smelting and set a new record of producing 257 tons of pig iron above the daily quota. Steelworkers, machine-tool workers, textile workers, and workers in shoe factories in Leningrad are struggling to exceed industrial targets and to save raw materials and produce first-class goods.

　　Accelerating the production of every automobile and tractor, reducing costs, and saving raw materials are the main pledges of the workers of the Soviet automobile and tractor factories to commemorate May Day. Workers and engineers at the Gorky Automobile Plant have decided to adopt a number of technical innovations this year and to make large-scale use of the latest achievements in Soviet machine tool manufacturing. According to preliminary estimates, after the complete modernization of the workshops, the Gorky Automobile Plant will accelerate automobile production by 15 to 20 percent.

### 苏联工人热烈迎接五一节  大规模展开劳动竞赛  图拉第一季产煤计划提前完成  列宁格勒各厂正加紧提高生产

1950-04-05
第4版()
专栏：

　　苏联工人热烈迎接五一节
    大规模展开劳动竞赛     
    图拉第一季产煤计划提前完成  列宁格勒各厂正加紧提高生产
    【新华社三日讯】综合塔斯社莫斯科消息：苏联工人为庆祝五一节而举行的社会主义竞赛，正在国内各企业大规模展开。图拉矿工已提早两日完成第一季产煤计划。和一九四九年第一季相较，图拉煤矿托拉斯每日平均产量增加了百分之十九点三。日丹诺夫城“亚速炼钢厂”第三号鼓风炉的年青司炉们，已胜利完成加速熔炼，并创造了超过每日定额生产二百五十七吨铣铁的新纪录。列宁格勒各制造厂与工厂的炼钢工人、机器制造工人、纺织工人和制鞋企业的工人，正在为超过工业目标并为节省原料和生产头等货品而斗争。
    加速每一辆汽车与拖拉机的生产，减低成本与节省原料，这是苏维埃汽车与拖拉机工厂的工人们纪念“五一”的主要保证。高尔基城汽车工厂的工人和工程师们，业已决定在今年采用若干技术方面的革新，并大规模利用苏维埃工作母机制造方面的最新成就。根据初步估计，当各工作间完全现代化之后，高尔基汽车工厂将加速汽车生产百分之十五到百分之二十。
