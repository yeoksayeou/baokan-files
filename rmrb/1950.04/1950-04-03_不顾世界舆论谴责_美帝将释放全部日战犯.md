Title: Defying World Public Opinion, US Imperialism Will Release All Japanese War Criminals
Author:
Date: 1950-04-03
Page: 4

　　Xinhua News Agency, Washington, 2nd. TASS, Washington, 1st: State Department representative White recently "explained" to newspaper representatives MacArthur's order regarding the possibility of granting parole to Japanese war criminals. White explicitly declared that, according to this order, all major Japanese war criminals sentenced by the International Military Tribunal can be released before the expiration of their sentences.

### 不顾世界舆论谴责  美帝将释放全部日战犯

1950-04-03
第4版()
专栏：

　　不顾世界舆论谴责
    美帝将释放全部日战犯
    【新华社二日讯】塔斯社华盛顿一日讯：美国国务院代表怀特顷向各报代表“解释”麦克阿瑟关于给予日本战犯以具结释放之可能性的命令，怀特明白宣称：根据这一命令，国际军事法庭所判决的所有日本重要战犯，都能够在徒刑期满前获释。
