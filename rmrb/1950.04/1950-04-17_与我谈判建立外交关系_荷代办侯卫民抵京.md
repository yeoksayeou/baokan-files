Title: Dutch Chargé d'Affaires Mr. H.M. Hirschfeld Arrives in Beijing to Negotiate Establishment of Diplomatic Relations With Us
Author:
Date: 1950-04-17
Page: 1

　　Xinhua News Agency, 16th: Mr. H.M. Hirschfeld, designated by the Dutch government as Chargé d'Affaires ad interim to negotiate with the Central People's Government of our country on the establishment of diplomatic relations, arrived in Beijing at 7:45 this morning. Han Xu, Section Chief of the Protocol Department of our Ministry of Foreign Affairs, was dispatched to the station to greet him.

### 与我谈判建立外交关系  荷代办侯卫民抵京

1950-04-17
第1版()
专栏：

　　与我谈判建立外交关系  荷代办侯卫民抵京
    【新华社十六日讯】为与我国中央人民政府就建立外交关系问题进行谈判，荷兰政府所指定为临时代办的侯卫民先生于今晨七时四十五分抵达北京，我外交部派交际处科长韩叙赴站迎接。
