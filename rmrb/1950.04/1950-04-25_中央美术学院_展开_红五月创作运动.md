Title: Central Academy of Fine Arts Launches "Red May Creation Movement"
Author: Zhongyang Meishu Xueyuan Tongxun Zu
Date: 1950-04-25
Page: 3

　　Central Academy of Fine Arts
    Launches "Red May Creation Movement"

    [This News] Students at the Central Academy of Fine Arts in Beijing are launching the "Red May Creation Movement." This movement began on the 19th. Submissions are due on May 24th, with an exhibition and evaluation to be held starting May 26th. Awards will be given out and a summary report will be made on June 1st. The content of the creations is divided into two main parts: (1) Historical Subjects: Including historical facts such as "May 1st," "May 4th," "May 20th," "May 30th," and other anti-imperialist, anti-feudal, and anti-bureaucratic-capitalist revolutionary struggles and the War of Liberation. (2) Real life after the nationwide revolutionary victory: Including production and construction, land reform, cultural education, and other real-life subjects. (Central Academy of Fine Arts Correspondence Group)

### 中央美术学院  展开“红五月创作运动”
中央美术学院通讯组
1950-04-25
第3版()
专栏：

　　中央美术学院
    展开“红五月创作运动”
    【本报讯】北京中央美术学院同学正展开“红五月创作运动”。此一运动自十九日起开始。订五月二十四日收稿，五月二十六日起举行展览并进行评议，最后于六月一日发奖并作总结报告。创作内容分两大部分：（一）历史题材：包括“五一”、“五四”、“五二○”、“五卅”及其他反帝反封建反官僚资本的革命斗争及解放战争等史实。（二）全国革命胜利后的现实生活：包括生产建设、土地改革、文化教育及其他现实生活题材。（中央美术学院通讯组）
