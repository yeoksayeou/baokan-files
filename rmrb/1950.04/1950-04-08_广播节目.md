　　Broadcast Programs
    [This Newspaper] Central People's Broadcasting Station today (8th) at 20:20: Professor Baudrev, Soviet financial economics expert lecturing in China, will speak on: "The Soviet Financial and Economic System." Youth Time at 17:30: "Introducing the situation of Dalian youth commemorating World Youth Week." First literary program at 18:00: Guan Chengyan sings the Xihe Dagu "Examining the Witch." Second literary program at 21:00: Music recording of the "Building the Motherland Yangko" performed by the Central Academy of Drama. Soviet songs at 18:30. New songs at 18:45.

### 广播节目

专栏：广播节目

　　广播节目
    【本报讯】中央人民广播电台今天（八日）二十点二十分：来中国讲学的苏联财政学专家包德列夫教授讲：《苏联的财政经济制度》。十七点三十分青年时间：《介绍大连青年纪念世界青年周的情形》。十八点第一次文艺节目：关呈艳演唱西河大鼓《考神婆》，二十一点第二次文艺节目：中央戏剧学院演出的《建设祖国大秧歌》的音乐录音。十八点三十分苏联歌曲。十八点四十五分新歌曲。
