Title: Municipal Trade Union Council and Other Units Confer on Preparations for "May Day"

Author:

Date: 1950-04-22
Page: 4

　　Municipal Trade Union Council and Other Units
    Confer on Preparations for "May Day"
    [Report from this newspaper] The Municipal Trade Union Council invited representatives from the Municipal Government, the Propaganda Department of the Municipal Party Committee, the Sino-Soviet Friendship Association, the Women's Federation, the Youth League Committee, and other units at 2:00 PM the day before yesterday to discuss preparations for the "May Day" celebration. The meeting agreed that the main content of this year's commemorative event would be to implement production policies and provide relief to unemployed brothers and sisters. A "May Day" Preparatory Committee was established, with Comrade Xiao Ming, Vice Chairman of the Municipal Trade Union Council, serving as Chairman. It was also decided that the Preparatory Committee would set up a Secretariat, as well as three departments for command, publicity, and arrangements.

### 市总工会等单位  商筹纪念“五一”节

1950-04-22
第4版()
专栏：

　　市总工会等单位
    商筹纪念“五一”节
    【本报讯】市总工会于前天下午二时，邀请市政府、市委宣传部、中苏友好协会、妇联、青委等单位、商讨纪念“五一”筹备工作。会上通过这一次纪念会以贯彻生产政策和救济失业弟兄为主要内容。同时成立纪念“五一”筹备会，由市总工会副主席萧明同志任主席。并决定筹备会下设秘书处、和指挥、宣传布置等三部。
