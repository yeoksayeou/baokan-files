Title: The Broad Masses of People Worldwide Support the Great Appeal and Resolution
Author:
Date: 1950-04-12
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency, Bern, April 10th: According to the "Worker's Voice" newspaper, the Swiss Peace Committee submitted a statement to the government stating that, in accordance with the decision of the Standing Committee of the World Congress of Supporters of Peace regarding submitting peace proposals to the parliaments of all countries, the Swiss Peace Committee deems it necessary to submit these proposals to the Swiss government. The statement quoted the appeal of the Stockholm meeting of the Standing Committee of the World Congress of Supporters of Peace and hoped that the Swiss government would support this appeal, thereby contributing to the establishment of necessary coordination among countries for world peace.

Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, April 10th: TASS, Moscow: Archbishop Alexius of All Russia and Moscow presided over the Easter service at the Epiphany Cathedral in Moscow on the 8th, with many believers participating. Easter services were held in all Russian Orthodox churches, and the Archbishop's message calling on Orthodox believers to support peace was read.

Previously, the bishops of the Russian Orthodox Church issued a statement saying: "In the name of the bishops, priests, and believers of all dioceses of the Russian Orthodox Church within our great motherland, we express our complete agreement with the resolutions of the Stockholm meeting of the Standing Committee of the World Congress of Supporters of Peace. We are convinced that the implementation of these resolutions will help to further consolidate world peace. Therefore, we call on all independent fraternal Orthodox churches to support the aforementioned resolutions and to make every effort to carry out the work of consolidating and safeguarding peace among believers." The announcement was signed by Archbishop Alexius of All Russia and Moscow, Archbishop of All Ukraine, and many other diocesan bishops.

Xinhua News Agency, Prague, April 10th: TASS, Prague: The Easter service hosted by the Czechoslovak Red Cross and the Czechoslovak Committee for the Defense of Peace passed a peace appeal. The Easter service was held in Prague on the 8th, attended by Jan, Chairman of the National Assembly, government officials, the Soviet Ambassador to Czechoslovakia, Sirin, envoys from various People's Democratic countries to Czechoslovakia, workers, and representatives from all walks of life.

The appeal declared: "We support the appeal of the Stockholm meeting of the Standing Committee of the World Congress of Supporters of Peace. Let us intensify the struggle for peace. Let us work with greater determination for the happiness of our country. Let us unite even more closely in the peace camp, around the Soviet Union."

### 世界广大人民拥护和大决议

1950-04-12
第4版()
专栏：

　　世界广大人民拥护和大决议
    瑞士和平委会建议政府接受和大文告
    【新华社十一日讯】塔斯社伯尔尼十日讯：据《工人呼声》报消息，瑞士和平委员会向政府提交一项声明称：按照世界拥护和平大会常设委员会关于向一切国家的议会提出和平建议的决定，瑞士和平委员会认为有必要把这些建议提交瑞士政府。声明引述和大常委会斯德哥尔摩会议的号召，并希望瑞士政府支持这个号召，从而对于为了全世界的和平而在各国间建立必要的协调有所贡献。
    全俄及乌克兰正教教徒文告
    【新华社十日讯】塔斯社莫斯科讯：全俄及莫斯科总主教阿利修斯于八日在莫斯科主显节大礼拜堂中主持了复活节礼拜，有信徒多人参加。在所有俄罗斯正教的教堂中，都举行了复活节礼拜，并宣读了总主教的呼吁正教徒拥护和平的文告。
    前此，俄罗斯正教教会主教们曾发表公告称：“以我们伟大祖国境内俄罗斯正教会各教区的主教、牧师与信徒的名义，我们表示完全同意世界拥护和平大会常设委员会斯德哥尔摩会议的决议。我们确信：实行这些决议，将有助于进一步巩固世界和平，因此，我们号召一切独立的兄弟正教教会拥护上述决议，并竭尽最大努力在教徒中进行巩固保卫和平事业的工作。”在这个公告上签名的有全俄及莫斯科总主教阿利修斯、全乌克兰总主教及其他教区主教多人。
    捷红十字会及和平委会通过呼吁书
    【新华社十日讯】据塔斯社布拉格讯：捷克斯洛伐克红十字会及捷克斯洛伐克拥护和平委员会主持的复活节礼拜，通过了和平呼吁书。复活节礼拜于八日在布拉格举行，参加的有国民议会主席伊昂及政府要员，苏驻捷大使西林，各人民民主国家驻捷使节，工人和各界代表等。
    呼吁书宣称：“我们拥护和大常设委员会斯德哥尔摩会议的呼吁书。让我们加紧争取和平的斗争。让我们以更大的坚毅为我国的幸福而工作。让我们在和平阵营中，在苏联的周围更加紧密地团结起来。”
