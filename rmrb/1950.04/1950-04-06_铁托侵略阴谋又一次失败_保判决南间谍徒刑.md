Title: Tito's Aggression Conspiracy Fails Again; Bulgaria Sentences Yugoslav Spies to Prison
Author:
Date: 1950-04-06
Page: 4

　　Xinhua News Agency, Sofia, 5th: A comprehensive TASS report: The Sofia District Court of Bulgaria tried twenty-six Yugoslav spies, including Zverlach, on March 29th and 30th. The defendants confessed in court. On March 31st, the court summoned witnesses and heard expert opinions. On April 1st, Bulgarian Prosecutor General Petlinsky made a concluding statement in court, pointing out the Tito clique's conspiracy of aggression against the People's Republic of Bulgaria and demanding that the defendants be severely punished for willingly engaging in espionage activities. On the 3rd, the court announced the verdicts in the case, sentencing defendants Zverlach and Stoykova to life imprisonment, a fine of one million leva, and deprivation of political rights for life. Milosevic, Spasova, Goranov, and Petrov were sentenced to fifteen years in prison, a fine of five hundred thousand leva, and deprivation of political rights for eighteen years. The remaining defendants were sentenced to prison terms ranging from two to ten years and varying terms of deprivation of political rights and fines of different amounts.

### 铁托侵略阴谋又一次失败  保判决南间谍徒刑

1950-04-06
第4版()
专栏：

　　铁托侵略阴谋又一次失败
    保判决南间谍徒刑
    【新华社五日讯】综合塔斯社索非亚消息：保加利亚索非亚地方法庭于三月二十九、三十日审讯了南斯拉夫间谍兹维尔拉奇等二十六人，被告等均已在庭上服罪。三十一日，法庭传讯证人，并听取了专家判断。四月一日，保检察长彼特林斯基在法庭上作总结发言，指出铁托集团对保加利亚人民共和国的侵略阴谋，并要求对被告等甘心被利用从事间谍罪恶活动予以严正的惩处。法庭于三日宣布了对该案的判决，判处被告兹维尔拉奇和斯托伊科娃无期徒刑，罚款一百万利瓦，褫夺公权终身。米洛塞维奇、斯巴索娃、哥拉诺夫和彼特罗夫十五年徒刑，罚款五十万利瓦，褫夺公权十八年。其余的被告分别被判处两年到十年的有期徒刑和不同年限、不同数目的褫夺公权与罚款。
