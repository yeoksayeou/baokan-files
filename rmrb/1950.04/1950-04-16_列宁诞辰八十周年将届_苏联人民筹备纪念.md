Title: Approaching the Eightieth Anniversary of Lenin's Birth: Soviet People Prepare to Commemorate

Author:

Date: 1950-04-16
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, April 15th: (TASS dispatch) The Soviet people are widely preparing to commemorate the eightieth anniversary of Lenin's birth on April 22nd. In Ulyanovsk, Lenin's birthplace, staff at the Lenin Museum have delivered a series of speeches about Lenin. In Kazan (where Lenin studied at university and began his revolutionary activities), professors and students are preparing to hold scientific conferences and grand exhibitions. In Baku, the Azerbaijan Bolshevik Party History Museum is also holding an exhibition. The documents displayed here illustrate Lenin's life and revolutionary activities, and how Lenin's legacy has been realized in Soviet life under Stalin's leadership. The exhibition at the Shushenskoye Museum (where Lenin spent three years in exile) includes more than 300 books on politics, literature, and novels from the 1890s that Lenin read.

In Leningrad, Kiev, Minsk, and other places, working people are preparing to commemorate Lenin's birthday.

### 列宁诞辰八十周年将届  苏联人民筹备纪念

1950-04-16
第4版()
专栏：

　　列宁诞辰八十周年将届
    苏联人民筹备纪念
    【新华社十五日讯】综合塔斯社莫斯科讯：四月二十二日是列宁第八十周年诞辰，苏联人民正在广泛筹备纪念。在列宁故乡乌里扬诺夫斯克，列宁博物馆的人员发表了一系列关于列宁的演讲。在喀山（列宁读大学及开始革命活动的地方），教授和学生们正在筹备举行科学会议和盛大展览会。在巴库，阿塞拜疆布尔什维克党组织历史博物馆也举办了展览会。这里陈列的文献，说明列宁的生平事迹、革命活动，以及列宁的遗训怎样在斯大林的领导下在苏联的生活中实现。在寿山斯克（列宁曾在这里过了三年放逐生活）博物馆的展览会中，收藏了列宁读过的三百余册关于九十年代的政治、文学与小说的书籍。
    在列宁格勒、基辅、明斯克等地，劳动人民都在筹备纪念列宁的诞辰。
