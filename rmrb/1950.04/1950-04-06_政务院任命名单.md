Title: List of Appointments by the Government Administration Council

Author:

Date: 1950-04-06
Page: 3

Xinhua News Agency, April 4th—The Government Administration Council of the Central People's Government recently appointed Zhou Gengsheng (Professor) and Mei Ru'ao (former judge of the Tokyo Far East International Military Tribunal) as advisors to the Ministry of Foreign Affairs; Liu Zerong (former Counselor of the Kuomintang Government's Embassy in the Soviet Union) and Mei Ru'ao (concurrently) as members of the Treaty Committee of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs; He Sijing (former member of the Legal Committee of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of China), Zhang Jiongbo (current Standing Director of the China Democratic National Construction Association), Ye Jingshen (former specialist of the Resources Committee of the Kuomintang Government), and Lu Diandong (former full-time legal advisor to the Central Bank) as specialized members; and Xiao Bingjun (former Deputy Director of the Highway Bureau of the Northeast People's Government) as a counselor to the Ministry of Communications.

### 政务院任命名单

1950-04-06
第3版()
专栏：

　　政务院任命名单
    【新华社四日讯】中央人民政府政务院顷任命周鲠生（教授）、梅汝璈（曾任东京远东国际军事法庭法官）为外交部顾问，刘泽荣（前国民党政府驻苏联大使馆参事）、梅汝璈（兼）为外交部条约委员会委员，何思敬（曾任中国共产党中央法律委员会委员）、张絅伯（现任民主建国会常务理事）、叶景莘（前国民党政府资源委员会专门委员）、陆殿栋（前中央银行专任法律顾问）为专门委员；萧秉钧（曾任东北人民政府公路总局副局长）为交通部参事。
