Title: Saifuddin and Others Leave Moscow for Home

Author: 
Date: 1950-04-07
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, 6th—Saifuddin, Vice Chairman of the Xinjiang Provincial People's Government, and other representatives of the Xinjiang Provincial People's Government, who were representatives of our delegation participating in negotiations with the Soviet Union, left Moscow on the 5th to return home.

### 赛福鼎等离苏京返国

1950-04-07
第1版()
专栏：

　　赛福鼎等离苏京返国
    【新华社莫斯科六日电】参加与苏联谈判的我国代表团代表，新疆省人民政府副主席赛福鼎及其他新疆省人民政府代表，已于五日离莫斯科返国。
