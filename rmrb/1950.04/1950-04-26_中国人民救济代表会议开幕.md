Title: Opening of the China People's Relief Representative Conference
Author:
Date: 1950-04-26
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, April 24th: The China People's Relief Representative Conference opened in Beijing today. One hundred and forty-six representatives attended the meeting, including representatives from relevant departments of the Central People's Government, relevant people's organizations, overseas Chinese, major administrative regions, the China Liberated Areas Relief Association, the China Welfare Fund, and those receiving relief. Xiong Jin Ding, a representative of the preparatory committee, reported on the preparatory process at today's meeting. The meeting also approved a presidium consisting of thirty-one people, including Soong Ching Ling, Dong Biwu, Wu Yaozong, Li Dequan, Xie Juezai, Yan Baohang, Lei Jieqiong, Kang Keqing, Zhuang Xiquan, Su Jingguan, Xie Bangding, Li Xian, Shu Sheyu, Liu Qingyang, Zhao Puchu, Kui Yanfang, Wu Zhiyuan, Bian Jieqing, Pu Jiexu, Hu Lansheng, Zhang Yuanshan, Ni Feijun, Yang Sulan, Deng Zhiyu, Xiong Jin Ding, Wu Yunfu, Pan Qi, Li Xiaoting, Pang Wenhua, Li Xueliang, and Cui Jianpei.

### 中国人民救济代表会议开幕

1950-04-26
第1版()
专栏：

　　中国人民救济代表会议开幕
    【新华社二十四日讯】中国人民救济代表会议今日在京开幕。出席会议的有中央人民政府有关部门、有关人民团体、海外华侨、各大行政区、中国解放区救济总会、中国福利基金会及被救济者的代表等一百四十六人。筹备会代表熊瑾玎在今天的会上报告筹备经过。会议并通过了宋庆龄、董必武、吴耀宗、李德全、谢觉哉、阎宝航、雷洁琼、康克清、庄希泉、苏井观、谢邦定、李仙、舒舍予、刘清扬、赵朴初、蒉延芳、吴志渊、边洁清、浦洁修、胡兰生、章元善、倪斐君、杨素兰、邓志裕、熊瑾玎、伍云甫、潘琪、李筱亭、庞文华、李学良、崔建培等三十一人为主席团。
