Title: International Student Union Calls on Affiliated Organizations to Support and Launch Large-Scale Signature Drive
Author:
Date: 1950-04-25
Page: 4

　　Xinhua News Agency, Prague, April 24th: The Secretariat of the International Student Union (ISU) issued a statement on the 20th, calling on student organizations in its 60 member countries to support the signature drive for the appeal of the Standing Committee of the World Congress of Supporters of Peace.
　　The statement from the ISU Secretariat said: The ISU welcomes the resolutions of the Stockholm Conference, especially the appeal to ban atomic weapons and convene the Second World Congress of Supporters of Peace. This resolution represents the interests of all people, and also the interests of all students. Therefore, the ISU calls on all upright students to sign this appeal.
　　The statement concluded: The ISU believes that mobilizing students to actively fight for peace is the most important task for all democratic students, and also a very important part of the preparatory work for the Second World Student Congress (scheduled for August).
　　All the staff of the ISU Secretariat have signed the appeal of the Standing Committee.

### 国际学联号召所属组织支持和大征集签名运动

1950-04-25
第4版()
专栏：

　　国际学联号召所属组织支持和大征集签名运动
    【新华社二十四日讯】电通社布拉格讯：国际学联书记处于二十日发出公告，号召所属六十个会员国的学生组织，支持为世界拥护和平大会常委会的呼吁书征集签名的运动。
    国际学联书记处的文告称：国际学联欢迎斯德哥尔摩会议的决议，尤其欢迎关于禁止原子武器以及召开第二届世界拥护和平大会的呼吁。该决议代表一切人民的利益，也代表一切学生的利益。因此，国际学联号召一切正直的学生，都在这一呼吁书上签名。
    文告末称：国际学联认为，动员学生为和平而积极斗争，是一切民主学生最重要的任务，也是第二次世界学生代表大会（按大会定于八月召开）筹备工作中很重要的一部分工作。
    国际学联书记处的全体人员，已在和大常委会的呼吁书上签名。
