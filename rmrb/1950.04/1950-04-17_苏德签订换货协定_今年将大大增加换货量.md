Title: Soviet-German Sign Barter Agreement: Substantially Increased Barter Volume This Year
Author:
Date: 1950-04-17
Page: 4

　　Soviet-German Sign Barter Agreement
    Substantially Increased Barter Volume This Year
    Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, 15th: TASS, Moscow: As a result of negotiations between the Soviet Ministry of Foreign Trade and the trade delegation of the German Democratic Republic, conducted in a friendly and mutually understanding atmosphere, the Soviet Union and the German Democratic Republic signed a barter and payment agreement in Moscow on April 12th.

    Based on the desire to promote the economic development of both countries and to expand and deepen trade relations between the two sides, thereby creating closer and more consolidated friendly relations between the two peoples, both parties agreed to substantially increase the volume of barter in 1950.

    The Soviet Union will supply the German Democratic Republic with grains, fats, ferrous and non-ferrous metals, trucks, concentrated apatite, manganese ore, petroleum products, coking coal, cotton, and other goods needed by the GDR for the development of its national economy.

    The German Democratic Republic will supply the Soviet Union with Industrial plant and machinery, cement, potash chemicals, coal briquettes, and other commodities.

    Soviet Minister of Foreign Trade Menshikov and Minister of Domestic and Foreign Trade and Material Supply of the German Democratic Republic, Handke, signed the agreement on behalf of their respective parties.

### 苏德签订换货协定  今年将大大增加换货量

1950-04-17
第4版()
专栏：

　　苏德签订换货协定
    今年将大大增加换货量
    【新华社十五日讯】塔斯社莫斯科讯：由于苏联对外贸易部与德意志民主共和国贸易代表团在友好与互相谅解的气氛中进行谈判的结果，苏联与德意志民主共和国于四月十二日在莫斯科签订了换货与付款协定。
    本着增进两国经济发展及扩大并加深双方贸易关系，从而使两国人民产生更为密切及巩固的友好关系的意愿，双方同意于一九五○年大大增加换货量。
    苏联将供给德意志民主共和国以谷类、脂肪、铁金属及非铁金属、卡车、浓缩磷灰石、锰矿砂、石油产品、焦煤、棉以及德意志民主共和国为发展其国民经济所需要的其他物品。
    德意志民主共和国将以工业设备与机器、水泥、钾化学制品、煤砖及其他商品供给苏联。
    苏联对外贸易部部长孟希科夫，德意志民主共和国国内外贸易及物资供应部部长韩克，代表双方在协定上签字。
