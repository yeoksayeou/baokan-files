Title: Northwest Financial and Economic Committee Organizes Financial and Economic Inspection Teams to Go to Gansu, Qinghai, Ningxia, and Xinjiang for In-depth Inspection

Author:
Date: 1950-04-26
Page: 2

Xinhua News Agency, Xi'an, April 24th. The Northwest Financial and Economic Committee, in order to implement the "Decision on Unifying National Financial and Economic Work" of the Central Government Administration Council and the specific tasks decided by the Northwest Financial Conference, recently organized financial and economic inspection teams to go to Gansu, Qinghai, Ningxia, Xinjiang and other provinces to conduct in-depth inspections of financial and economic work. The inspection team is headed by Jia Tuofu, director of the Northwest Financial and Economic Committee, and has three groups: agriculture, industry and mining, and finance.

### 西北财政经济委员会  组织财政经济检查组  分赴甘青宁新深入检查工作

1950-04-26
第2版()
专栏：

　　西北财政经济委员会
    组织财政经济检查组
    分赴甘青宁新深入检查工作
    【新华社西安二十四日电】西北区财政经济委员会为贯彻中央政务院“关于统一国家财政经济工作的决定”及西北财政会议所决定的具体任务，特于日前组成了财政经济检查组，分赴甘肃、青海、宁夏、新疆等省，深入检查财经工作。该检查组由西北财经委员会贾拓夫主任任组长，下设农业、工矿、财政三小组。
