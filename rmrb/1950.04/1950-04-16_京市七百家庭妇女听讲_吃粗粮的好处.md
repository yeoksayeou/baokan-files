Title: Seven Hundred Housewives in Beijing Hear Lecture on "The Benefits of Eating Coarse Grains"
Author:
Date: 1950-04-16
Page: 4

　　Seven Hundred Housewives in Beijing Hear Lecture on "The Benefits of Eating Coarse Grains"
　　[This newspaper dispatch] In order to promote standard rice and flour among the broad masses of housewives, the Science Popularization Bureau of the Ministry of Culture of the Central People's Government and the Beijing Democratic Women's Federation held two lecture meetings on the 12th and 14th, inviting Zhang Jimin of the Science Popularization Bureau and Zhou Xuan, director of the Dietetics Department of Peking Union Medical College Hospital, to speak on "Why Change the Standard of Grain Processing" and "The Benefits of Eating Coarse Grains." More than 700 people attended.
　　In their speeches, everyone unanimously supported the government's method of changing the standard of rice and flour processing, and expressed that they would not only respond with practical actions, but also publicize it to relatives, friends, and neighbors.
　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　(City Women's Federation Communication Group)

### 京市七百家庭妇女听讲“吃粗粮的好处”

1950-04-16
第4版()
专栏：

　　京市七百家庭妇女听讲“吃粗粮的好处”
    【本报讯】中央人民政府文化部科学普及局和北京市民主妇联为了在广大的家庭妇女中展开标准米面的宣传，于十二、十四两日举行了两次讲演会，邀请科学普及局的张姞民和协和医院饮食部主任周旋讲“为什么改变粮食加工的标准”及“吃粗粮的好处”。到会七百余人。
    大家发言中一致拥护政府改变米面加工标准的办法，并表示不但自己用实际行动来响应，还要向亲友、邻居宣传。
                                          （市妇联通讯组）
