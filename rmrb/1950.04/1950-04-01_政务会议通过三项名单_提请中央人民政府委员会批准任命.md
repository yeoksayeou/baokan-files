Title: Government Affairs Council Approves Three Lists, Submitting to the Central People's Government Committee for Approval of Appointments
Author:
Date: 1950-04-01
Page: 3

　　Xinhua News Agency, March 31st: The 26th Government Affairs Council of the Central People's Government Administration Council approved three lists to be submitted to the Central People's Government Committee for approval of appointments, as follows:

　　I. Supplementary List of Deputy Directors of the North Sichuan People's Administrative Office
　　Deputy Director: Liu Jukui, formerly served as Political Commissar of a division of the People's Liberation Army.

　　II. List of Director of the North Jiangsu People's Administrative Office
　　Director: Hui Yuyu, formerly served as Director of the Political Department of an army of the People's Liberation Army.

　　III. List of Director of the North Anhui People's Administrative Office
　　Director: Huang Yan, formerly served as Deputy Political Commissar of the North Anhui Military Region of the People's Liberation Army.

### 政务会议通过三项名单  提请中央人民政府委员会批准任命

1950-04-01
第3版()
专栏：

　　政务会议通过三项名单
    提请中央人民政府委员会批准任命
    【新华社三十一日讯】中央人民政府政务院第二十六次政务会议通过提请中央人民政府委员会批准任命的三项名单如下：
    一、川北人民行政公署副主任补充名单
    副主任  刘聚魁  曾任人民解放军师政治委员。
    二、苏北人民行政公署主任名单
    主任  惠浴宇  曾任人民解放军军政治部主任。
    三、皖北人民行政公署主任名单
    主任  黄岩  曾任人民解放军皖北军区副政治委员。
