Title: Government Administration Council Holds 26th Meeting: Approves Provisional Regulations on Deed Tax; Decides to Abolish Feudal Monopoly System in the Transportation Industry
Author:
Date: 1950-04-01
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, March 31st—The 26th meeting of the Central People's Government Administration Council was held at 3:00 PM today. The meeting heard and approved the report of Wang Zheng, Deputy Minister of the Ministry of Posts and Telecommunications of the Central People's Government, on the National Telecommunications Conference. The meeting also discussed and approved the Provisional Regulations on Deed Tax and the Government Administration Council's decision to approve the proposal of the Transportation Workers' Union to abolish the feudal monopoly system in the transportation industry in various regions. In addition, the meeting also approved several important appointments and submitted several appointments to the Central People's Government Committee for approval.

### 政务院举行廿六次政务会议  通过契税暂行条例  决定废除搬运业中封建把持制度

1950-04-01
第1版()
专栏：

　　政务院举行廿六次政务会议
    通过契税暂行条例     
    决定废除搬运业中封建把持制度
    【新华社三十一日讯】中央人民政府政务院第二十六次政务会议于今日下午三时举行。会议听取和批准了中央人民政府邮电部王铮副部长关于全国电信会议的报告。会议并讨论和通过了契税暂行条例及政务院关于批准搬运工会废除各地搬运事业中封建把持制度之建议的决定。此外，会议并通过重要任命案及提请中央人民政府委员会批准任命案多起。
