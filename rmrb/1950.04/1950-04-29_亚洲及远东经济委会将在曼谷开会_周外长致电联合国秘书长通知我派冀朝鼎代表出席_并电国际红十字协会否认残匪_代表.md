Title: ECAFE to Meet in Bangkok; Foreign Minister Zhou Cables UN Secretary-General Notifying of Ji Chao-ding’s Attendance; Cables International Red Cross Denying Remnant Bandit “Representatives”
Author:
Date: 1950-04-29
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, April 28th—Foreign Minister Zhou Enlai of the Central People's Government recently sent a telegram to UN Secretary-General Trygve Lie regarding the participation of our delegation in the plenary session of the United Nations Economic and Social Council’s Economic Commission for Asia and the Far East to be held in Bangkok. The telegram reads as follows:
    Mr. Trygve Lie, Secretary-General of the United Nations, Lake Success, New York, and please forward to the member states of the Economic and Social Council and the Economic Commission for Asia and the Far East:
    The Economic Commission for Asia and the Far East, under the United Nations Economic and Social Council, will convene a plenary session in Bangkok on May 16th of this year. I hereby notify you that the Central People's Government of the People's Republic of China has appointed Ji Chao-ding as the representative to attend the meeting of the Commission. The illegal representatives of the remnant group of the Chinese Kuomintang reactionaries are not qualified to participate in the meeting of the Commission and must be expelled from the Commission. Please forward this to all relevant parties and notify the Thai government to grant entry visas to our representatives, and promptly send the agenda and related meeting notices.
            Zhou Enlai, Minister of Foreign Affairs of the Central People's Government of the People's Republic of China
      April 28, 1950, Beijing

Xinhua News Agency, April 28th—On April 28th, Foreign Minister Zhou Enlai of our Ministry of Foreign Affairs sent a telegram to Mr. Ruegger, Secretary of the International Red Cross, denying the so-called "representatives" of the remnant group of the Kuomintang reactionaries. The telegram reads as follows:
International Red Cross
    Mr. Ruegger, Secretary:
    The Central People's Government of the People's Republic of China is the only legitimate government representing the Chinese people. I am now notifying you on behalf of the Central People's Government of the People's Republic of China that we formally deny the so-called "representatives" of the remnant group of the Chinese Kuomintang reactionaries, who are completely unqualified to participate in the International Red Cross Association and attend its various meetings. Please expel them from the International Red Cross Association immediately and inform all institutions of the International Red Cross Association and the relevant national Red Cross societies accordingly.
            Zhou Enlai, Minister of Foreign Affairs of the Central People's Government of the People's Republic of China
          April 28, 1950, Beijing

On the same day, Foreign Minister Zhou Enlai of our Ministry of Foreign Affairs also sent a telegram with the same content to Mr. Dusechsa, Secretary-General of the International Committee of the Red Cross.

Xinhua News Agency, April 28th—Hu Lansheng, Secretary-General of the Red Cross Society of China, telegraphed Mr. Ruegger, Secretary of the International Red Cross Association, and Mr. Dusechsa, Secretary-General of the International Committee of the Red Cross, on April 28th, informing them that the institutions of the Red Cross Society of China are currently being reorganized. Once the reorganization is complete, representatives will be sent to participate in the above two organizations and attend the various meetings of the two organizations. At the same time, please inform the various institutions of the two organizations and the relevant national Red Cross societies.

### 亚洲及远东经济委会将在曼谷开会  周外长致电联合国秘书长通知我派冀朝鼎代表出席  并电国际红十字协会否认残匪“代表”

1950-04-29
第1版()
专栏：

　　亚洲及远东经济委会将在曼谷开会
    周外长致电联合国秘书长通知我派冀朝鼎代表出席
    并电国际红十字协会否认残匪“代表”
    【新华社二十八日讯】中央人民政府外交部周恩来部长，顷就我国代表参加联合国经济暨社会理事会所属亚洲及远东经济委员会将在曼谷召开的全体会议事，致电联合国秘书长赖伊，电文如下：纽约成功湖联合国秘书长赖伊先生并请转达经济暨社会理事会与亚洲及远东经济委员会各会员国：
    联合国经济暨社会理事会所属亚洲及远东经济委员会将于今年五月十六日在曼谷召开全体委员会会议，我特通知阁下：中华人民共和国中央人民政府业已任命冀朝鼎为出席该委员会会议的代表。中国国民党反动派残余集团的非法代表没有参加该委员会会议的资格，必须从该委员会驱逐出去。请即转达有关各方，并通知泰国政府对我代表给予入境签证，另将会议议程及有关会议之通知迅予寄来为盼。
            中华人民共和国中央人民政府外交部部长周恩来
      一九五○年四月二十八日于北京
    【新华社二十八日讯】四月二十八日，我外交部周恩来部长致电国际红十字协会秘书鲁希，否认国民党反动派残余集团的所谓“代表”。电文如下：国际红十字协会
    秘书鲁希先生：
    中华人民共和国中央人民政府是代表中国人民的唯一合法政府。我现在代表中华人民共和国中央人民政府通知阁下：对于完全没有资格参加国际红十字协会和出席其各种会议的中国国民党反动派残余集团的所谓“代表”，正式加以否认。请即将其从国际红十字协会开除出去，并转知国际红十字协会各机构及有关各国红十字会为荷。
            中华人民共和国中央人民政府外交部部长周恩来
          一九五○年四月二十八日于北京
    同日，我外交部周恩来部长亦以同样内容，致电红十字会国际委员会秘书长杜旭沙。
    【新华社二十八日讯】中国红十字会秘书长胡兰生，于四月二十八日致电国际红十字协会秘书鲁希，及红十字会国际委员会秘书长杜旭沙告以中国红十字会机构正在改组，一俟改组完毕，即将派遣代表参加以上两组织，并出席该两组织的各项会议；同时请他们转知该两组织的各机构及有关各国红十字会。
