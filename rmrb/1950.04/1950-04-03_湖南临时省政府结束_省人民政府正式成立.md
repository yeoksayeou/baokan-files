Title: End of the Hunan Provisional Provincial Government; Establishment of the Hunan Provincial People's Government

Author:

Date: 1950-04-03
Page: 3

Xinhua News Agency, Changsha, 1st—The Hunan Provincial People's Government was formally established today. The Hunan Provisional Provincial Government, organized after the peaceful liberation in August of last year, has completed its work tasks after more than half a year and has ended at the same time.

### 湖南临时省政府结束  省人民政府正式成立

1950-04-03
第3版()
专栏：

　　湖南临时省政府结束
    省人民政府正式成立
    【新华社长沙一日电】湖南省人民政府今日正式成立。在去年八月间和平解放后组织的湖南临时省政府，半年多以来业已完成其工作任务已于同时结束。
