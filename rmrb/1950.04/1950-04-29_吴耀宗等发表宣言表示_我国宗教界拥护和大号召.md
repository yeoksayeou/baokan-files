Title: Wu Yaozong and Others Issue Declaration Stating Religious Circles in Our Country Endorse and Answer the Call
Author: 
Date: 1950-04-29
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, April 28th: Religious figures in our country, including Wu Yaozong, have recently issued a declaration endorsing the call of the World Congress of Supporters of Peace. The full text of the declaration is as follows:

　　We enthusiastically endorse the call issued by the Standing Committee of the World Congress of Supporters of Peace: demanding the unconditional ban of atomic weapons and declaring the government that first uses atomic weapons to be war criminals.

　　Only two paths lie before the people of the world: the path of war and the path of peace. Except for a small number of warmongers headed by American imperialists, the people of the whole world have decided to take the path of peace. This decision cannot be shaken by any incitement or rumors by the imperialists.

　　In the surging movement of the whole world opposing imperialist war, our China occupies an extremely important position. China suffered more than a hundred years of imperialist aggression; in the eight-year War of Resistance against Japan and the four-year War of Liberation, the disasters inflicted on the Chinese people by imperialism cannot be calculated in numbers. The rapid success of the Chinese revolution greatly strengthened the forces of peace and changed the face of the world. This made the imperialists panic and become at a loss. In this state of panic and disappointment, they are frantically carrying out various war preparations, attempting to make a final struggle. At this time, our efforts to strive for peace cannot be relaxed for a moment. We should unite the broad masses, make them recognize the true face of imperialism, and make them a strong pillar of the world peace camp.

　　If we want to strive for lasting world peace, we must unite with the Soviet Union, the world's fortress of peace, and all people's democratic countries. This peace camp represents more than one-third of the world's population. In addition, the broad masses of people in various imperialist countries and oppressed nations are equally determined to oppose war and support peace. This force supporting peace is incomparably powerful.

　　We must not only oppose the use of atomic weapons, we must also oppose bacteriological warfare, oppose the imperialists' war propaganda, oppose their release of German and Japanese war criminals, oppose their oppression of the colonial people's struggle for national independence and democratic peace movements, oppose their brutal acts against democratic and just people in their own countries, and oppose all fascist measures in imperialist countries.

　　As religious believers, we must be especially vigilant against the imperialists' all-pervasive provocations and sowing of discord, and their conspiracies to cultivate reactionary forces within the revolutionary camp. We must relentlessly expose and forcefully strike at them, so that they cannot use the name of religion and relief work to carry out their criminal acts.

　　Standing on the position of religious faith and humanitarianism, we call on all Christians, Buddhists, Muslims, and followers of other religions in China to unite closely with all peace-loving and democratic people in the world, using all practical and effective words and actions, to work hard to defend world peace, strive for national independence, and realize people's democracy.

　　Wu Yaozong—Director of the Editorial Department of the National Association of the YMCA of China (Member of the National Committee of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference)
　　Liu Liangmo—Secretary of the YMCA of China
　　Zhao Zichen—Dean of the School of Religion, Yenching University

　　Juzan—Director of the Zhejiang Buddhist Association, President of the Wulin Buddhist Academy
　　Ma Jian—Muslim, Professor of the Department of Oriental Languages, Peking University (Member of the National Committee of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference)
　　Deng Yuzhi—General Secretary of the National Association of the YWCA of China
　　Tu Yuqing—General Secretary of the YMCA of China

　　Jiang Changchuan—Bishop of the North China District of the Methodist Church
　　Wang Zizhong—General Secretary of the North China Joint Association of the Church of Christ in China
　　Zhou Shujia—Director of the Beijing Buddhist Layman's Forest

### 吴耀宗等发表宣言表示  我国宗教界拥护和大号召

1950-04-29
第1版()
专栏：

　　吴耀宗等发表宣言表示
    我国宗教界拥护和大号召
    【新华社二十八日讯】我国宗教界人士吴耀宗等，顷发表宣言，拥护世界拥护和平大会的号召，宣言全文如下：
    我们热烈地拥护世界拥护和平大会常设委员会所发出的号召：要求无条件地禁用原子武器，并宣布首先使用原子武器的政府为战争罪犯。
    摆在世界人民面前的只有两条道路：一条是战争的道路，一条是和平的道路。除了以美国帝国主义者为首的少数战争贩子以外，全世界的人民已经决定走和平的大道，这个决定是帝国主义者任何煽动和谣言所不能动摇的。
    在全世界反对帝国主义战争的澎湃运动中，我们中国占了一个极其重要的位置。中国受了一百多年的帝国主义侵略；在八年抗日战争、四年解放战争中，帝国主义所加给中国人民的灾难，更是不能以数字来计算的。中国革命迅速的成功，大大地加强了和平的力量，改变了世界的面貌。这就使帝国主义者惊惶失措，无所适从。在这个慌乱失望的情绪中，他们更疯狂地进行着准备战争的种种工作，企图作最后的挣扎。在这时候，我们争取和平的努力是不能一刻松懈的。我们应当团结广大的群众，使他们认识帝国主义的真面目，使他们成为世界和平阵营有力的支柱。
    我们若要争取世界持久和平，就必须与世界和平堡垒苏联和一切人民民主国家团结在一起。这个和平的阵营代表全世界三分之一以上的人口。此外，在各个帝国主义国家和被压迫民族里面的广大人民，也一样坚决地反对战争，拥护和平。这个拥护和平的力量是无比强大的。
    我们不只要反对使用原子武器，我们也要反对细菌战争，反对帝国主义者煽动战争的宣传，反对他们将德日战犯释放，反对他们对殖民地人民争取民族独立与民主和平运动的压迫，反对他们对他们国内民主正义人士的残暴行为，反对帝国主义国家内一切法西斯化的措施。
    作为宗教信徒的我们，尤其要对帝国主义者无孔不入的挑拨离间，和他们在革命阵营里面培养反动力量的阴谋加以高度的警惕，并予以无情的暴露和有力的打击，务使他们无法假借宗教及救济事业的名义，去进行他们的罪恶行为。
    我们站在宗教信仰和人道主义的立场，号召全中国的基督徒、佛教徒、回教徒以及其他宗教信仰者，用一切实际有效的言论与行动，和全世界爱好和平民主的人民紧紧地团结在一起，为保卫世界和平、争取民族独立、实现人民民主而努力。
    吴耀宗—中华基督教青年会全国协会编辑部主任（中国人民政治协商会议全国委员会委员）
