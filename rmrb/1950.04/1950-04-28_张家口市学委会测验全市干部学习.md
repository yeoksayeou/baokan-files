Title: Zhangjiakou City Learning Committee Tests Learning of Cadres Citywide
Author:
Date: 1950-04-28
Page: 3

　　Zhangjiakou City Learning Committee
    Tests Learning of Cadres Citywide
    [Our Newspaper] In order to deepen the learning of cadres and promote their review of the previous stage of study, the Zhangjiakou City Learning Committee has decided to conduct a citywide test of cadres' cultural and theoretical learning. The cultural learning test will be organized by the Municipal Education Bureau; the theoretical learning (political economy) test will be set by the Municipal Committee of the Chinese Communist Party, and will be held collectively in each unit on the 24th and 25th, with papers submitted on the spot. From the 26th to the 28th, each unit will hold group democratic grading, and learning models will be selected based on the prescribed conditions. Before the end of the month, each unit must also make a summary and report to the Municipal Learning Committee.

### 张家口市学委会测验全市干部学习

1950-04-28
第3版()
专栏：

　　张家口市学委会
    测验全市干部学习
    【本报讯】张家口市学委会为使干部学习更深入一步，促进干部复习前一阶段的学习，决定举行全市干部的文化、理论学习测验。文化学习测验由市教育局负责主持；理论学习（政治经济学）测验由中共市委会出题，定二十四、二十五两日在各单位集体考试，当场交卷。二十六日至二十八日，各单位将举行小组民主评卷，并根据规定条件，评选学习模范。月底前各单位并须作出总结，报告市学委会。
