Title: U.S. Plots Long-Term Occupation of Japan, Uses it as a Major Base for Pacific Aggression Line; Military Construction Occupies One-Third of Ryukyu's Cultivated Land

Author:

Date: 1950-04-06
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, April 5th: The United States is plotting a long-term occupation of Japan and the Ryukyu Islands, including Okinawa, as a major base in its Pacific aggression line. According to a Washington dispatch from the Associated Press, the House Appropriations Subcommittee announced on the 2nd the testimony given by Under Secretary of the Army Voorhees, responsible for occupied territories affairs, and other officials to the committee in early March. The testimony showed that U.S. Army officials believe that "the United States must retain a 'friendly' Japan as its greatest asset in the 'Cold War' in Asia." Voorhees bluntly declared: "It is quite clear that our (referring to the U.S.) troops will have to be stationed in Japan for a very long period." He also said that the U.S. military has decided to "station troops in the Ryukyu Islands indefinitely and begin large-scale military construction plans." According to Voorhees, U.S. military construction occupies one-third of Ryukyu's cultivated land. Furthermore, according to a Washington dispatch from the Associated Press on March 28th, U.S. "State Department officials proposed a plan months ago to maintain troops in Japan even after the occupation ends, and this plan can be accomplished by concluding a special military base treaty."

### 美力图长期占领日本  作为太平洋侵略线的主要基地  军事建筑占琉球耕地三分之一

1950-04-06
第4版()
专栏：

　　美力图长期占领日本
    作为太平洋侵略线的主要基地 
    军事建筑占琉球耕地三分之一
    【新华社五日讯】美国力图长期占领日本和包括冲绳岛在内的琉球群岛，作为其太平洋侵略线中的主要基地。据合众社华盛顿消息，美国众议院拨款小组委员会于二日公布陆军部负责占领区事务的副部长伍里斯及其他官员三月初在该委员会所作的证言。这些证言显示：美国陆军部官员认为“美国必须保留‘友好’的日本，作为在亚洲‘冷战’中最大的一笔资产”。伍里斯率直地宣称：“十分明显，我们的（按指美国的）军队将必须在日本驻很长一个时期。”他又说：美军已决定“无限期在琉球群岛驻扎，并开始实行大规模军事建设计划”。据伍里斯透露，美国军事建筑占据了琉球三分之一的耕地。另据美联社华盛顿三月二十八日电，美“国务院官员数月前提出一个甚至在占领结束后也要在日本保持军队的计划，这个计划可以用缔结一个特殊军事基地的条约来完成”。
