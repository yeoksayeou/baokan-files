Title: Pakistani Civil Servants Hold Strike Demonstrations Demanding Wage Increase
Author:
Date: 1950-04-02
Page: 4

　　Pakistani Civil Servants Hold Strike Demonstrations
    Demanding Wage Increase
    [Xinhua News Agency, Karachi, 30th] TASS, Karachi, 29th: Nine hundred civil servants in Karachi held a strike and demonstration on the 28th and gathered in front of Pakistan's Constitutional Assembly, demanding the release of Amit Muhammad, chairman of the Pakistan Civil Servants Union, who was recently detained by the authorities, and demanding the re-employment of Shafiq Gulisi, the union's current chairman. Civil servants also demanded increased wages and cost of living allowances.

### 巴基斯坦公务员举行罢工示威  要求增加工资

1950-04-02
第4版()
专栏：

　　巴基斯坦公务员举行罢工示威
    要求增加工资
    【新华社三十日讯】塔斯社喀喇蚩二十九日讯：喀喇蚩九百名公务员于二十八日举行罢工和示威游行，并在巴基斯坦的立宪会议前集会，要求释放最近被当局所拘禁的巴基斯坦公务员联合会主席阿密特·穆汗麦德，要求重新雇用该联合会的现任主席沙非克·古里西。公务员们并要求增加工资及生活费用津贴。
