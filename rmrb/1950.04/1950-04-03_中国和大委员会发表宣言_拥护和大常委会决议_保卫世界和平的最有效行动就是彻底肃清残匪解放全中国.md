Title: China Peace Committee Issues Declaration, Endorsing the Standing Committee of the World Peace Congress Resolution: The Most Effective Action to Defend World Peace is to Thoroughly Eradicate the Remnant Bandits and Liberate All of China
Author:
Date: 1950-04-03
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, April 2nd: The China Committee for the Defense of World Peace issued a declaration today, warmly endorsing the resolutions and appeals of the Standing Committee of the World Peace Congress. The full text of the declaration is as follows:

We, representing the peace-loving Chinese people, warmly endorse all the resolutions and appeals of the Third Plenary Session of the Standing Committee of the World Peace Congress. An important resolution passed by the plenary session demands the unconditional prohibition of the use of atomic weapons as instruments of aggression and mass destruction of people, and declares that any government that first uses atomic weapons will be judged as war criminals. This solemn resolution reflects the wishes and calls of the majority of the people of the world and serves as a serious warning to the adventurers who are preparing for atomic warfare.

In response to the appeal of the Third Plenary Session of the Standing Committee of the World Peace Congress to further develop the movement to defend world peace, we have decided to first establish branches of the China Committee for the Defense of World Peace in major cities across the country, including Shanghai, Nanjing, Tianjin, Guangzhou, Hankou, Chongqing, Xi'an, Shenyang, Kunming, Dihua, and Dalian, to more deeply mobilize and organize the people, resolutely oppose the imperialists' new war plans, and strive for lasting world peace.

We call on the people of the whole country to make a final effort to support the front lines, thoroughly eradicate the remaining forces of the US imperialism and its running dog, the Kuomintang bandit gang, and complete the liberation of all of China, as this is the most urgent need and most practical and effective action for the Chinese people's cause of defending world peace at present.

At the same time, we call on the people of the whole country to unite closely around the peace and democracy camp headed by the Soviet Union, and unite around the Standing Committee of the World Peace Congress, to fight to shatter the new war plans of the imperialists.

### 中国和大委员会发表宣言  拥护和大常委会决议  保卫世界和平的最有效行动就是彻底肃清残匪解放全中国

1950-04-03
第1版()
专栏：

　　中国和大委员会发表宣言
    拥护和大常委会决议
    保卫世界和平的最有效行动就是彻底肃清残匪解放全中国
    【新华社二日讯】中国保卫世界和平大会委员会于本日发表宣言，热烈拥护世界拥护和平大会常设委员会的决议和号召。宣言全文如下：
    我们代表爱好和平的中国人民，热烈拥护世界拥护和平大会常设委员会第三次全会的一切决议和号召。全会所通过的一项重要决议，要求无条件地禁止把原子武器作为侵略与大规模毁灭人民的武器，并宣布任何第一个使用原子武器的政府，将被判为战争罪犯。这个庄严的决议，反映了世界大多数人民的愿望和呼声，给了正在准备原子战争的冒险家们以严重警告。
    为了响应和大常委会第三次全会关于进一步发展保卫世界和平运动的号召，我们决定首先在上海、南京、天津、广州、汉口、重庆、西安、沈阳、昆明、迪化、大连等全国各大城市，立即成立中国保卫世界和平委员会分会，更深入地动员和组织人民，坚决反对帝国主义新战争计划，竭力争取世界的持久和平。
    我们号召全国人民最后努一把力支援前线，彻底肃清美帝国主义及其走狗国民党匪帮的残余势力，完成全中国的解放，因为这正是目前中国人民保卫世界和平事业的一个最迫切需要和最实际有效的行动。
    我们同时号召全国人民要紧紧地团结在以苏联为首的和平民主阵营周围，团结在世界拥护和平大会常设委员会周围，为粉碎帝国主义的新战争计划而斗争。
