Title: Beijing Radio Station Adds Program "Sino-Soviet Friendship Voice"
Author:
Date: 1950-04-16
Page: 4

　　Beijing Radio Station Adds Program "Sino-Soviet Friendship Voice"
    [This newspaper dispatch] The Beijing Sino-Soviet Friendship Association will add a "Sino-Soviet Friendship Voice" program every Sunday afternoon from 8:00 PM to 8:30 PM on the Beijing People's Broadcasting Station starting from April 16th, mainly to introduce the Soviet Union. Tonight, Comrade Zeng Ping, Deputy Secretary-General of the association, will speak on "The Nature and Work of the Sino-Soviet Friendship Association."

### 京市广播电台增设节目“中苏友声”

1950-04-16
第4版()
专栏：

　　京市广播电台增设节目“中苏友声”
    【本报讯】北京市中苏友好协会自四月十六日起每星期日下午八时至八时半在北京市人民广播电台增设“中苏友声”节目，以介绍苏联为主。今晚由该会副总干事曾平同志讲“中苏友好协会的性质及工作。”
