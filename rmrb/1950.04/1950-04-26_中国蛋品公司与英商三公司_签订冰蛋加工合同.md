Title: China Egg Products Company Signs Frozen Egg Processing Contract with Three British Companies
Author:
Date: 1950-04-26
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, April 24th:

　　The China Egg Products Company signed a frozen egg processing contract with three British companies: Jardine Matheson & Co., Ltd., Butterfield & Swire (China) Ltd., and Mao Chang Co., Ltd. earlier this month. This contract has been approved by the Ministry of Trade of the Central People's Government. The contract stipulates that the China Egg Products Company will supply fresh egg raw materials to the three British companies, including Jardine Matheson, etc., this year. The three British companies, including Jardine Matheson, etc., will produce 20,000 tons of frozen eggs (2,240 pounds per ton) and be responsible for export. The period for completion is from March to December this year. The contract contains specific provisions for the supply of raw materials, the production and export of products, product prices, shipping, and payment methods.

### 中国蛋品公司与英商三公司  签订冰蛋加工合同

1950-04-26
第1版()
专栏：

　　中国蛋品公司与英商三公司
    签订冰蛋加工合同
    【新华社二十四日讯】中国蛋品公司于本月初与英商和记有限公司、英商怡和洋行以及茂昌股份有限公司签订了冰蛋加工合同。该项合同业经中央人民政府贸易部批准。合同规定本年由中国蛋品公司供给英商和记等三公司鲜蛋原料，由英商和记等三公司生产冰蛋两万吨（每吨二、二四○磅），并负责出口。完成的时期为自本年三月至十二月。合同对原料供给、产品之生产和出口、产品价格、装运及付款等项办法，均有具体规定。
