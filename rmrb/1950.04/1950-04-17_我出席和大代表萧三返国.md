Title: My Representative Xiao San Returns Home After Attending the World Peace Congress
Author:
Date: 1950-04-17
Page: 1

　　Xinhua News Agency, 16th. Xiao San, the representative of our country attending the third plenary meeting of the Standing Committee of the World Congress of Supporters of Peace held in Sweden, returned to Beijing this morning.

### 我出席和大代表萧三返国

1950-04-17
第1版()
专栏：

　　我出席和大代表萧三返国
    【新华社十六日讯】出席在瑞典召开的世界拥护和平大会常委会第三次全体大会的我国代表萧三，已于今晨返抵北京。
