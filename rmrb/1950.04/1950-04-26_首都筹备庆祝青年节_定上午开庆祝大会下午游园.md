Title: Capital Prepares to Celebrate Youth Day: Celebration Meeting in the Morning, Garden Party in the Afternoon
Author:
Date: 1950-04-26
Page: 4

　　Capital Prepares to Celebrate Youth Day
    Celebration Meeting in the Morning, Garden Party in the Afternoon
    [Xinhua News Agency] The preparatory committee for the capital's celebration of Youth Day has been formed, with participating units including the Ministry of Education of the Central People's Government, the Bureau of Cultural Relics of the Ministry of Culture of the Central People's Government, the General Political Department of the Chinese People's Revolutionary Military Commission, the Political Department of the North China Military Region, the All-China Federation of Trade Unions, the National Committee of the Chinese Railway Trade Union, the Central Committee of the Chinese New Democratic Youth League, the All-China Youth Federation, the All-China Students' Federation, the All-China Democratic Women's Federation, the All-China Federation of Literary and Art Circles, the Beijing Working Committee of the Youth League, the Beijing Students' Federation, the Beijing Democratic Youth Federation, and the Beijing Federation of Literary and Art Circles. The preparatory committee has decided to hold a Chinese Youth Day celebration meeting in the Taihe Hall on the morning of May 4th, and a Capital Garden Party for Chinese Youth Day in Zhongshan Park in the afternoon. Various entertainment programs will be organized by the capital's cultural troupes, artistic, folk art, technical, and sports groups, as well as relevant school groups. These will include: large-scale collective singing and dancing throughout the park, individual game competitions, group game competitions, song and dance entertainment parties, new folk art performances, and sports demonstrations, which will be held in various locations in the park. In addition, the Exhibition of Historical Materials on the Chinese Youth Movement, prepared by relevant units, will also be inaugurated in the Zhongshan Hall of Zhongshan Park on May 4th.

### 首都筹备庆祝青年节  定上午开庆祝大会下午游园

1950-04-26
第4版()
专栏：

　　首都筹备庆祝青年节
    定上午开庆祝大会下午游园
    【新华社讯】中国青年节首都庆祝会筹备会已经组成，参加单位有中央人民政府教育部、中央人民政府文化部文物局、中国人民革命军事委员会总政治部、华北军区政治部、中华全国总工会、中国铁路工会全国委员会、中国新民主主义青年团中央委员会、中华全国民主青年联合总会、中华全国学生联合会、中华全国民主妇女联合会、中华全国文学艺术界联合会、青年团北京市工作委员会、北京市学生联合会、北京市民主青年联合会、北京市文学艺术界联合会等。筹备会已决定在五月四日上午在太和殿举行中国青年节庆祝大会，下午在中山公园并举行中国青年节首都游园会，由首都各文工团、文艺、曲艺、技艺、体育等团体及各学校有关团体组织各种游艺节目，其中有：全园大集体歌舞、个人游戏竞赛、团体游戏竞赛、歌舞游艺会、新曲艺演出会、体育表演等，在公园各处分别举行。另外由有关各单位筹备的中国青年运动史料展览会，也将在五月四日在中山公园中山堂揭幕。
