Title: Hungarian Agricultural Production Reaches Pre-War Levels; Rationing of High-Quality Flour Abolished
Author:
Date: 1950-04-04
Page: 4

　　Hungarian Agricultural Production Reaches Pre-War Levels
    Rationing of High-Quality Flour Abolished
    [Xinhua News Agency, 3rd dispatch] TASS, Budapest, 1st dispatch: Budapest Radio reported that the Hungarian Council of Ministers has decided to abolish the rationing of high-quality flour and implement free sale nationwide. Hungary had already abolished bread rationing on September 1st of last year. Since the Three-Year Plan was completed at the end of last year, agricultural production has reached pre-war levels, thus providing the basis for further abolishing the rationing of high-quality flour.

### 匈农业生产达战前水平  上等面粉配给制取消

1950-04-04
第4版()
专栏：

　　匈农业生产达战前水平
    上等面粉配给制取消
    【新华社三日讯】塔斯社布达佩斯一日讯：据布达佩斯电台广播称：匈牙利部长会议业已决定在全国各地取消上等面粉的配给并实行自由售卖。按匈牙利已于去年九月一日取消面包的配给制。由于三年计划在去年年底完成以后，农业生产已达战前水平，因此提供了进一步取消上等面粉配给制的基础。
