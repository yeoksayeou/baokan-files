Title: Ministry of Agriculture Sends Technical Cadres to Assist in Pest and Disease Control

Author:
Date: 1950-04-06
Page: 2

Xinhua News Agency, 3rd. The Ministry of Agriculture of the Central People's Government, in order to strengthen pest and disease control, recently dispatched seventeen experts and technical cadres to assist the pest and disease control stations in Xingtai, Yangliuqing, and Changli in Hebei Province; Xinxiang, Shanxian, and Liaocheng in Pingyuan Province; Huimin and Xuzhou in Shandong Province; and Zhangjiakou, Taiyuan, and Kaifeng. The tasks of the pest and disease control stations are to guide the masses to improve pest and disease control techniques, prepare pest and disease control medicines and machinery, study and improve the masses' native methods of pest and disease control, and organize the masses to capture and kill pests and diseases when they occur. The pest and disease control stations are long-term professional institutions, and the cadres sent by the Central Ministry of Agriculture to assist the work of each station are scheduled to work for five to seven months.

### 农业部派出技术干部协助病虫害防治工作

1950-04-06
第2版()
专栏：

　　农业部派出技术干部协助病虫害防治工作
    【新华社三日讯】中央人民政府农业部为加强病虫害防治工作，日前派出专家和技术干部十七人，分赴河北省邢台、杨柳青、昌黎，平原省新乡、单县、聊城，山东省惠民、徐州，及张家口、太原、开封等地的病虫防治站协助工作。病虫防治站的任务是指导群众改进病虫防治技术，准备防治病虫药械，研究并改进群众防治病虫的土办法，病虫发生时组织群众进行捕打。防治站是长期性的专业机构，中央农业部派出协助各站工作的干部，预定工作五至七个月。
