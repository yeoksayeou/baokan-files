Title: The Central People's Government Committee Appoints Li Fuchun as Government Council Member, Deputy Director of the Financial and Economic Committee, and Minister of Heavy Industry
Author:
Date: 1950-04-14
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, 13th: The sixth meeting of the Central People's Government Committee approved the following four lists of appointments:
    1. Supplementary list of members of the Government Administration Council of the Central People's Government
        Government Council Member: Li Fuchun, current Vice Chairman of the Northeast People's Government.
    2. Supplementary list of members of the Political and Legal Committee of the Government Administration Council of the Central People's Government
        Member: Liu Lantao, current Third Secretary of the North China Bureau of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of China.
    3. Supplementary list of Deputy Directors of the Financial and Economic Committee of the Government Administration Council of the Central People's Government
        Deputy Director: Li Fuchun (concurrent)
    4. List of Ministers of the Ministry of Heavy Industry of the Central People's Government
        Minister: Li Fuchun (concurrent)
        (Vice Premier Chen Yun has resigned from his concurrent position as Minister of Heavy Industry)

### 中央人民政府委员会  任命李富春为政务委员兼财经委员会副主任及重工业部长

1950-04-14
第1版()
专栏：

　　中央人民政府委员会
    任命李富春为政务委员兼财经委员会副主任及重工业部长
    【新华社十三日讯】中央人民政府委员会第六次会议通过任命四项名单如下：
    一、中央人民政府政务院政务委员补充名单政务委员  李富春  现任东北人民政府副主席。
    二、中央人民政府政务院政治法律委员会委员补充名单委员  刘澜涛  现任中国共产党中央华北局第三书记。
    三、中央人民政府政务院财政经济委员会副主任补充名单副主任  李富春  （兼）
    四、中央人民政府重工业部部长名单部长  李富春  （兼）
    （陈云副总理已辞去所兼重工业部部长职）
