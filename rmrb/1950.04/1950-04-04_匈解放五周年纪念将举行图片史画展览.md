Title: Photo and Historical Painting Exhibition to Be Held to Commemorate the Fifth Anniversary of the Liberation of Hungary
Author:
Date: 1950-04-04
Page: 3

[Report from this newspaper] To commemorate the fifth anniversary of the liberation of Hungary, the Foreign Cultural Liaison Bureau of the Ministry of Culture of the Central People's Government will hold an exhibition of Hungarian photos and artworks at the waterside pavilion in Zhongshan Park, starting on the 12th of this month. The exhibition will include photos of Hungarian-Soviet friendship, the tasks of the Communist Party, Hungary's Three-Year Plan to Five-Year Plan, and Hungary's agriculture and social welfare; and historical paintings of "Dózsa" carved by famous Hungarian printmakers ("Dózsa" was a hero of peasant revolution in Hungarian history).

### 匈解放五周年纪念将举行图片史画展览

1950-04-04
第3版()
专栏：

　　匈解放五周年纪念将举行图片史画展览
    【本报讯】为纪念匈牙利解放五周年，中央人民政府文化部对外文化联络事务局，将自本月十二日起在中山公园内水榭，举行匈牙利图片、美术品展览。其中有匈牙利与苏联的友谊、共产党的任务、匈牙利三年计划到五年计划以及匈牙利的农业、社会福利等照片；有匈牙利名版画家所刻之“陶夏”史画（“陶夏”是匈牙利历史上的农民革命英雄）。
