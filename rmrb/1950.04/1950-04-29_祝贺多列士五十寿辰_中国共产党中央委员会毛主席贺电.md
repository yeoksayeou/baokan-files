Title: Congratulations to Thorez on his Fiftieth Birthday – Telegram from Chairman Mao of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of China
Author:
Date: 1950-04-29
Page: 1
    
    Xinhua News Agency, April 28th: The Central Committee of the Communist Party of China and Chairman Mao Zedong sent a telegram to congratulate Thorez on his fiftieth birthday. The text of the telegram is as follows:
    Dear Comrade Thorez:
    On the occasion of your fiftieth birthday, I wish you good health and longevity, and express my respect to you and the French Communist Party.
               Chairman Mao Zedong of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of China

### 祝贺多列士五十寿辰  中国共产党中央委员会毛主席贺电

1950-04-29
第1版()
专栏：

    祝贺多列士五十寿辰 　 中国共产党中央委员会毛主席贺电
    【新华社二十八日讯】中国共产党中央委员会毛泽东主席电贺多列士五十寿辰，电文如下：亲爱的多列士同志：
    当你的五十寿辰之际，谨祝你的健康和长寿，并向你和法国共产党表示敬意。
              中国共产党中央委员会主席  毛泽东
