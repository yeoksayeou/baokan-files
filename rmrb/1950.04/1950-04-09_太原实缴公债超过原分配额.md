Title: Taiyuan's Actual Public Bond Subscription Exceeds Original Allocation

Author:
Date: 1950-04-09
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Taiyuan, 8th—All sectors of Taiyuan City have exceeded the target for actual payment of the People's Victory Inflation-Adjusted Public Bonds. The city-wide allocation of public bonds was 350,000 shares. As of March 31, the number of shares subscribed by all sectors totaled 411,037.5. The actual amount paid during the same period reached 350,434 shares, accounting for more than 85% of the city's total subscription, exceeding the city's allocation of 350,000 shares by 0.12%. All sectors of the city are continuing to clear outstanding payments.

### 太原实缴公债超过原分配额

1950-04-09
第1版()
专栏：

　　太原实缴公债超过原分配额
    【新华社太原八日电】太原市各界已超额完成人民胜利折实公债实缴任务。该市全市配售公债数字为三十五万分，截至三月三十一日，各界认购数字共为四十一万一千零三十七分半，同期实缴数字已达三十五万零四百三十四分，占全市认购数字的百分之八十五强，超过了全市配销数字三十五万分的百分之零点一二。现全市各界正在继续清缴所欠尾数。
