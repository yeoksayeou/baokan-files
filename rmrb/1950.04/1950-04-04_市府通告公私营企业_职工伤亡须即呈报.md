Title: Municipal Government Notice to Public and Private Enterprises: Employee Injuries and Deaths Must Be Reported Immediately
Author:
Date: 1950-04-04
Page: 4

[Beijing News Agency Dispatch] The Beijing Municipal People's Government, in order to understand the safety situation of employees in all public and private enterprises and factories in the city in a timely manner, and to strengthen labor protection work, stipulates that from the 1st of this month, all public and private enterprises and factories in this city must report to the Municipal Labor Bureau in accordance with the following regulations when employees are injured or die: (1) Employee injuries and deaths must be reported immediately, and in special circumstances, the report must not exceed two days; (2) When reporting, a detailed form (which can be obtained from the Labor Bureau, the Trade Union, or the Trade Association) must be filled out and submitted in writing, and a telephone report may be made first if necessary; (3) Those who intentionally fail to report within the time limit will be punished as illegal once discovered.

### 市府通告公私营企业  职工伤亡须即呈报

1950-04-04
第4版()
专栏：

　　市府通告公私营企业
    职工伤亡须即呈报
    【京市新闻处讯】京市人民政府为及时了解全市各公私营企业工厂职工的安全情况，以加强劳动保护工作，规定自本月一日起，凡属本市各公私营企业工厂如遇职工有伤亡情形时，须按照下列规定向市劳动局呈报：（一）职工发生伤亡后，须立即呈报，如有特殊情况者，亦不得超过两天；（二）呈报时，须依照规定表格（可向劳动局、工会或同业公会领取），详细填写，备文呈报，必要时可先用电话报告；（三）故意逾期不报者，一经查出，即以违法论处。
