Title: Beijing People's Broadcasting Station Changes Program Schedule Starting Today
Author:
Date: 1950-04-10
Page: 4

[Report from this newspaper] The Beijing People's Broadcasting Station, based on listeners' requests, is changing its program schedule starting from the tenth of this month. The number of broadcasts per day will be changed from five to two. The first broadcast will be from 6:30 AM to 9:10 AM; the second broadcast will be from 4:20 PM to 11:15 PM. The city's market prices will be reported daily at 6:45 AM and 10:45 PM. The first local news broadcast will be changed to 7:15 AM to 7:30 AM. The workers' program will be changed to 7:30 AM to 8:00 AM. The youth program will be changed to 4:30 PM to 5:00 PM. The children's program will be changed to 5:30 PM to 5:45 PM. The Voice of the Municipality will be changed to 6:30 PM to 6:45 PM. The Russian language lecture will be changed to 8:00 PM to 9:00 PM. On Sundays, 8:00 PM to 8:30 PM will be "China-Soviet Friendship Sounds," discussing some issues and speeches related to Sino-Soviet friendly relations, and 8:30 PM to 9:00 PM will rebroadcast the Central People's Broadcasting Station's arts and literature program. The recorded Russian language lecture will be broadcast every Tuesday, Thursday, and Saturday from 6:00 AM to 7:00 AM. Listener services will be provided every Tuesday, Wednesday, Thursday, Friday, and Sunday from 9:30 PM to 9:45 PM. The women's program will be on every Monday and Saturday from 9:30 PM to 9:45 PM. The second local news broadcast will be from 9:45 PM to 10:00 PM. The Beijing People's Broadcasting Station will also rebroadcast the Central People's Broadcasting Station's first news broadcast, folk art programs, social science lectures, natural science lectures, the fifth news and commentary, arts and literature programs, and the seventh news broadcast on time.

### 京市人民广播电台从今天起改变节目

1950-04-10
第4版()
专栏：

　　京市人民广播电台从今天起改变节目
    【本报讯】北京市人民广播电台根据听众要求，从本月十日起改变节目。每天播音次数由五次改成两次。第一次播音时间自六时半至九时十分；第二次播音时间自十六时二十分至二十三时十五分。每天六时四十五分和二十二时四十五分报告本市行情。第一次本市新闻改在七时十五分至七时三十分。工人节目改在七时三十分至八时。青年节目改在十六时三十分至十七时。儿童节目改在十七时三十分至十七时四十五分。市政之声改在十八时三十分至十八时四十五分。俄语讲座改在二十时至二十一时，星期日二十时至二十时三十分是中苏友声，关于中苏友好关系中的一些问题和讲演，二十时三十分至二十一时转播中央人民电台文艺节目。每星期二、四、六之六时至七时播送俄语讲座录音。听众服务在每星期二、三、四、五、日之二十一时三十分至二十一时四十五分。妇女节目在每星期一、六之二十一时三十分至二十一时四十五分。第二次本市新闻在二十一时四十五分至二十二时。中央人民广播电台之第一次新闻，曲艺节目、社会科学讲座、自然科学讲座、第五次新闻和评论、文艺节目和第七次新闻，北京市人民广播电台亦准时转播。
