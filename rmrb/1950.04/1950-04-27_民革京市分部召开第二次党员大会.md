Title: Beijing Municipal Branch of the Revolutionary Committee of the Chinese Kuomintang Convenes Second Party Congress
Author:
Date: 1950-04-27
Page: 4

　　Beijing Municipal Branch of the Revolutionary Committee of the Chinese Kuomintang
    Convenes Second Party Congress
    [Beijing News] The Beijing Municipal Branch of the Revolutionary Committee of the Chinese Kuomintang, in order to review past work, determine future directions, and elect members of the Second Committee, will hold its Second Municipal Party Congress at Kongde School on Donghuamen Street, starting on April 28th, lasting for three days. The branch has sent invitations to the leaders and representatives of the Municipal People's Government, the Municipal Committee of the Chinese Communist Party, and the municipal-level organizations of various democratic parties, as well as Chairman Li Jishen and many members of the Central Committee of the headquarters of the association to attend and speak at the meeting.

### 民革京市分部召开第二次党员大会

1950-04-27
第4版()
专栏：

　　民革京市分部
    召开第二次党员大会
    【京市讯】中国国民党革命委员会北京市分部为检讨以往工作，确定今后方针，并选举第二届委员会委员，定于四月二十八日起，假东华门大街孔德学校，召开全市第二次党员大会，会期三日。该部现已柬请市人民政府、中共市委会、各民主党派市级机构各首长及代表，以及该会总部李主席济深与中委多人届时莅会讲话。
