Title: Sofia Holds Meeting of Shock Workers to Guarantee Early Completion of Work Plan
Author:
Date: 1950-04-19
Page: 4

Telegraph Agency Sofia dispatch: A conference of national shock workers, rationalizers, and activists of Bulgaria was held in Sofia on the 11th. Vulko Chervenkov, Chairman of the Council of Ministers of Bulgaria, members of the Politburo of the Communist Party, and ministers of various ministries attended the meeting. Minister of Industry Yugov reported on the achievements made since the implementation of the 1950 industrial plan and severely criticized the shortcomings of certain enterprise managers in their work.

Shock workers and rationalizers from all over Bulgaria reported on their achievements at the meeting and guaranteed to complete their work plans ahead of schedule.

### 索非亚开突击工人会议  保证提前完成工作计划

1950-04-19
第4版()
专栏：

　　索非亚开突击工人会议  保证提前完成工作计划
    【新华社十五日讯】电通社索非亚讯：保加利亚全国突击工人、合理化运动者及积极分子会议，于十一日在索非亚举行。保加利亚部长会议主席契尔文科夫，共产党政治局委员和各部部长均到会。工业部长于哥夫在会上报告了一九五○年工业计划执行以来所获得的成绩；并严厉地批评了某些企业经理在工作上的缺点。
    来自保加利亚全国各地的突击工人和合理化运动者们，在会上报告了他们的成绩，并且保证先期完成工作计划。
