Title: Soviet Zhdanov University Compiled Korean-Russian Dictionary
Author:
Date: 1950-04-14
Page: 4

　　Soviet Zhdanov University Compiled Korean-Russian Dictionary
    [This newspaper dispatch] (Xinhua News Agency) reported: Scholars of the Oriental Department of Leningrad State Zhdanov University, under the leadership of Professor Kholodovich, have compiled a large Korean-Russian dictionary.
    Another dispatch reported: The Soviet Industrial Literature Publishing Bureau has published approximately four thousand books on metalworking and various machinery manufacturing in the past ten years, with a total circulation of over twenty million copies. This year, seven hundred books will be published, with five million copies printed.

### 苏联日丹诺夫大学编成朝俄辞典

1950-04-14
第4版()
专栏：

　　苏联日丹诺夫大学编成朝俄辞典
    【本报讯】据新华社讯：列宁格勒国立日丹诺夫大学东方部的学者们，在霍洛多维奇教授领导下，已编成一部朝俄大辞典。
    又讯：苏联工艺读物出版局在过去十年内出版了约四千种关于金属工艺及各种机械制造的书籍，发行总数共二千余万册。本年将出版七百种书籍，印行五百万册。
