Title: People's Bank of China Lowers Exchange Rates; Revised Exchange Rates for All Regions of the Country Effective Today
Author:
Date: 1950-04-01
Page: 2

　　People's Bank of China Lowers Exchange Rates
　　Revised Exchange Rates for All Regions of the Country Effective Today
　　[This Newspaper's Report] In order to facilitate the transfer of public and private funds within the country, invigorate the financial market, and promote the exchange of materials, the Head Office of the People's Bank of China has decided to lower exchange rates and vigorously develop domestic exchange business. Effective April 1st, the Head Office and the Beijing Branch will revise the exchange rates for all regions of the country as follows:

　　(North China Region) Tianjin: 1/1000; Hebei Province: 1/1000 - 2/1000; Shanxi Province: 2/1000 - 3/1000; Pingyuan Province: 2/1000 - 3/1000; Chahar Province: 2/1000 - 3/1000; Suiyuan Province: 3/1000 - 4/1000.

　　(East China Region) Shanghai: 4/1000; Nanjing: 4/1000; Jiangsu Province: 4/1000 - 6/1000; Zhejiang Province: 5/1000 - 6/1000; Anhui Province: 4/1000 - 6/1000; Shandong Province: 3/1000 - 5/1000; Fujian Province: 20/1000.

　　(Central South China Region) Wuhan: 4/1000; Guangzhou: 7/1000; Hubei Province: 4/1000 - 6/1000; Hunan Province: 5/1000 - 7/1000; Henan Province: 3/1000 - 5/1000; Jiangxi Province: 6/1000 - 8/1000; Guangdong Province: 7/1000 - 9/1000; Guangxi Province: 7/1000 - 9/1000.

　　(Southwest Region) Chongqing: 10/1000; Sichuan Province: 10/1000; Xikang Province: 10/1000; Yunnan Province: 10/1000; Guizhou Province: 8/1000 - 10/1000.

　　(Northwest Region) Shaanxi Province: 4/1000 - 6/1000; Gansu Province: 5/1000 - 9/1000; Ningxia Province: 7/1000; Qinghai Province: 9/1000; Xinjiang Province: 10/1000.

　　(For each province with two exchange rates, the lower rate applies to locations within the province accessible by railway, and the higher rate applies to locations within the province not accessible by railway.)

　　The above exchange rates will be reduced differentially based on telegraphic transfer, draft, and mail transfer. For government and military units, public enterprises, cooperatives, private cultural, educational, and health organizations, as well as private enterprises with deposit relationships with the Bank, discounts and preferential treatment will be given based on the exchange fees collected. Currently, the Beijing Branch has 870 exchange points, covering the entire country. Places where individual branches have been established but are not yet connected for exchange can be transferred through neighboring branches.

### 中国人民银行降低汇率  自今日起改订对全国各地汇率

1950-04-01
第2版()
专栏：

　　中国人民银行降低汇率
    自今日起改订对全国各地汇率
    【本报讯】中国人民银行总行为了便利国内公私资金调拨，活泼金融市场，促进物资交流，决定降低汇率，大力开展内汇业务，自四月一日起，总行及北京分行对全国各地汇率改订如下：
    （华北区）天津：千分之一；河北省：千分之一——千分之二；山西省：千分之二——千分之三；平原省：千分之二——千分之三；察哈尔省：千分之二——千分之三；绥远省：千分之三——千分之四。
    （华东区）上海：千分之四；南京：千分之四；江苏省：千分之四——千分之六；浙江省：千分之五——千分之六；安徽省：千分之四——千分之六；山东省：千分之三——千分之五；福建省：千分之二十。
    （华中南区）武汉：千分之四；广州：千分之七；湖北省：千分之四——千分之六；湖南省：千分之五——千分之七；河南省：千分之三——千分之五；江西省：千分之六——千分之八；广东省：千分之七——千分之九；广西省：千分之七——千分之九。
    （西南区）重庆：千分之十；四川省：千分之十；西康省：千分之十；云南省：千分之十；贵州省：千分之八——千分之十。
    （西北区）陕西省：千分之四——千分之六；甘肃省：千分之五——千分之九；宁夏省：千分之七；青海省：千分之九；新疆省：千分之十。
    （以上各省订有两个汇率的，其低汇率适用于省内通铁路地点，高汇率适用于省内不通铁路地点。）
    上项汇率并按电、票、信汇分别予以差别减低。对于军政，公营企业、合作社、私人文教卫生团体，以及与该行有存款往来关系的私营企业汇兑，还可照应收汇费予以折扣优待。目前北京分行通汇点共达八百七十个，已遍及全国，其个别已设行而未通汇的地方，亦可由邻近行处转汇。
