Title: Soviet Newspapers Publish International Commentary

Author:
Date: 1950-04-14
Page: 4

　　TASS, Moscow, 12th: All newspapers today published various foreign commentaries on the Soviet government's protest to the US government regarding the intrusion of a US military aircraft into Soviet territory.

### 苏联各报刊载各国评论

1950-04-14
第4版()
专栏：

　　苏联各报刊载各国评论
    【新华社十三日讯】塔斯社莫斯科十二日讯：所有报纸今日都刊登外国对苏联政府就美国军用机侵入苏境一事而向美国政府提出抗议的各种评论。
