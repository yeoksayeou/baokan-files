Title: Ministry of Culture, Bureau of Art, Archives Seeks Literary Books and Periodicals
Author:
Date: 1950-04-18
Page: 6

The Archives of the Bureau of Art of the Ministry of Culture Seeks Literary Books and Periodicals

For work needs, this office is seeking the following books and materials:

　　I. Jiang Guangci: "Shorts Party."
　　II. Hu Yepin: "Light is Before Us."
　　III. Ying Xiuren: "The Golden Pagoda and the Silver Pagoda."
　　IV. Regarding left-wing writers such as Yin Fu, Bai Mang, Feng Keng, Rou Shi, Ying Xiuren, Qiu Dongping, and Hong Lingfei: (1) posthumous works, (2) biographies, (3) related life materials, catalogs of works, etc.

Readers who have these books, periodicals, or materials and are willing to sell, donate, or lend them, please contact the Archives of the Bureau of Art of the Ministry of Culture at No. 5, Dongsitou Tiao, Beijing.

Central People's Government Ministry of Culture Bureau of Art Archives

### 文化部艺术局资料室征求文艺书刊资料

1950-04-18
第6版()
专栏：

　　文化部艺术局资料室征求文艺书刊资料
    本室因工作需要，征求下列书籍及资料：
    一、蒋光慈著：《短裤党》。
    二、胡也频著：《光明在我们面前》。
    三、应修人著：《金宝塔和银宝塔》。
    四、关于殷夫、白莽、冯铿、柔石、应修人、丘东平、洪灵菲等左翼作家之（一）遗著，（二）传记，（三）有关生活材料作品目录等。
    读者如有此项书籍、刊物或资料愿出让、赠送或出借者，请与北京东四头条五号文化部艺术局资料室接洽为荷。
                             中央人民政府文化部艺术局资料室
