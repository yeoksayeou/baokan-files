Title: China Peace Committee Announces May Day Launch of Peace Signature Campaign in Response to World Peace Congress Standing Committee Call
Author:
Date: 1950-04-29
Page: 1

　　China Peace Committee Announcement
    May Day Launch of Peace Signature Campaign
    Responding to the Call of the World Peace Congress Standing Committee
    [Xinhua News Agency, April 28th] The China Peace Committee today issued the following announcement:
    Fellow compatriots who love peace throughout the country:
    The Standing Committee of the World Peace Congress has called on peace-loving people around the world to sign a petition calling for the prohibition of atomic warfare. We hope that the people of the whole country will enthusiastically respond to this call, launch the broadest possible signature campaign demanding the prohibition of atomic warfare in commemoration of this year's May Day Labor Day, and establish branches of this committee in major central cities to lead various activities to defend world peace.
    The appeal of the World Peace Congress is as follows: "We demand: the unconditional prohibition of the use of atomic weapons as a tool for mass destruction of people, and the establishment of strict international control to ensure the implementation of this decision. We believe: any government that is the first to use atomic weapons against any other country is a war criminal. We call on the people of the world to sign this appeal."
          China Peace Committee
          April 28th

### 中国保卫世界和平大会委员会通告  五一展开和平签名运动  响应世界拥护和平大会常委会号召

1950-04-29
第1版()
专栏：

　　中国保卫世界和平大会委员会通告
    五一展开和平签名运动
    响应世界拥护和平大会常委会号召
    【新华社二十八日讯】中国保卫世界和平大会委员会，本日发出通告如下：
    全国爱好和平的同胞们：
    世界拥护和平大会常设委员会号召全世界爱好和平的人民签名在要求禁止原子战争呼吁书上。我们希望全国人民热烈地响应这个号召，在纪念今年五一劳动节的时候，展开最广泛的要求禁止原子战争的签名运动，并在各中心城市成立本会分会，来领导各项保卫世界和平的活动。
    世界拥护和平大会的呼吁书如下：“我们要求：无条件地禁止把原子武器用作大规模毁灭人民的工具，并建立严格的国际管制，以确保这一决定的执行。我们认为：任何第一个使用原子武器来对付任何其他国家的政府，就是战争罪犯。我们号召全世界人民在这个呼吁书上签名。”
          中国保卫世界和平大会委员会
          四月二十八日
