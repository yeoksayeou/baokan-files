Title: Central Committee of the Communist Youth League: Notice on Commemorating the May Fourth Youth Day
Author:
Date: 1950-04-20
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, 19th: The Central Committee of the China New Democratic Youth League issued a notice on commemorating the "May Fourth" Youth Day. The full text is as follows:

　　This year's "May Fourth" Youth Day is the first Chinese Youth Day after the founding of the People's Republic of China, and it is also the anniversary of the China New Democratic Youth League. This is a festival that is most worthy of grand commemoration by young people across the country. New Democratic Youth League organizations and other youth groups in various regions should hold commemorations. On the "May Fourth" Commemoration Day, youth mass rallies should be held as much as possible, celebration parades should be held, and forums, lectures, exhibitions or parties can also be held. According to the League's slogan for commemorating the "May Fourth" Youth Day and the "China Youth" editorial, discussions should be conducted in conjunction with the actual situation of local youth's thoughts and lives.
                                        April 18, 1950

### 青年团中央委员会  关于纪念五四青年节的通知

1950-04-20
第1版()
专栏：

　　青年团中央委员会
    关于纪念五四青年节的通知
    【新华社十九日讯】中国新民主主义青年团中央委员会发出关于纪念“五四”青年节的通知，全文如下：
    今年的“五四”青年节，是中华人民共和国成立后的第一次中国青年节，又是中国新民主主义青年团的周年纪念日，这是最值得全国青年隆重纪念的节日。各地新民主主义青年团和其他青年团体，应举行纪念。在“五四”纪念日应尽可能的召开青年群众大会，举行庆祝游行，并可举行座谈会、讲演、展览会或联欢会等。根据本会的纪念“五四”青年节的口号，“中国青年”社论，结合当地青年思想、生活的实际情况，进行讨论。
                                        一九五○年四月十八日
