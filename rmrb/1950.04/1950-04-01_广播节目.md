　　Radio Programs
    [Our Newspaper] The Central People's Broadcasting Station will broadcast international current affairs today (the 1st) at 20:20. 20:30: (1) A summary broadcast of the article "This Spring's Afforestation" by Liang Xi, Minister of Forestry and Land Reclamation of the Central People's Government; (2) Communication: "Farmers in Western Hebei Province Enthusiastically Launch Afforestation Movement." 17:30 Youth Time: "Political and Ideological Activities of Harbin Students." 18:00 First Literary Program: The second half of the Jingyun Dagu "Daughter Hero Wang Guiying". 21:00 Second Literary Program: The Amateur National Music Research Society will broadcast national music. 18:30 Soviet Songs. 18:45 New Songs.
    [Furthermore] The Beijing People's Broadcasting Station at 12:30 Workers and Youth Joint Program: (1) Teaching to sing "People of the World Sing Together"; (2) School News. 16:30 Voice of the Municipality: An article jointly written by the Beijing Municipal People's Government Sanitation Engineering Bureau and the Public Health Bureau, "Talking to Citizens About How to Maintain Cleanliness". 17:00 Children's Program (1) "Why Children's Day Was Changed to June 1st", (2) "Talking About Lunar Eclipses". 18:45 Women's Program: Dr. An Xiaolan of Peking University Medical College talks about "Several Diseases That Newborn Babies Are Prone to Suffer From". 19:15 Ding Haochuan's recorded lecture "Talking to Young Friends About Family Issues".

### 广播节目

1950-04-01
第4版()
专栏：广播节目

　　广播节目
    【本报讯】中央人民广播电台今天（一日）二十点二十分国际时事。二十点三十分：（一）摘要播送中央人民政府林垦部部长梁希的文章《这一次的春季造林》；（二）通讯：《河北省西部农民热烈展开造林运动》。十七点三十分青年时间：“哈尔滨学生的政治思想活动”。十八点第一次文艺节目：京韵大鼓“女儿英雄王桂英”后半段。二十一点第二次文艺节目：业余国乐研究社演播国乐。十八点三十分苏联歌曲。十八点四十五分新歌曲。
    【又讯】北京市人民广播电台十二点三十分工人青年联合节目：（一）教唱歌“全世界人民齐欢唱”；（二）学校新闻。十六点三十分市政之声：北京市人民政府卫生工程局和公共卫生局合写的一篇文章“和市民们谈谈怎样保持清洁”。十七点儿童节目（一）“儿童节为什么改在六月一日”，（二）“谈谈月蚀”。十八点四十五分妇女节目：北大医学院安笑兰大夫讲“新生婴儿容易害的几种病”。十九点十五分丁浩川讲演录音“和青年朋友谈谈家庭问题”。
