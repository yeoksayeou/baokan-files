Title: Beijing Establishes Public Enterprise Company to Unify Leadership of Municipal Public Factories
Author:
Date: 1950-04-14
Page: 4

　　[Beijing News Agency Dispatch] The Beijing Municipal People's Government established the Public Enterprise Company on the 13th of this month to strengthen the leadership of municipal public factories. Zhao Pengfei, former Deputy Director of the Construction Bureau, and Yu Diqing, Deputy Director of the Industry Bureau, were appointed as General Manager and Deputy General Manager respectively. More than ten units, including the Qinghe Woolen Mill, Yanjing Paper Mill, XinJian Glass Factory, and XinJian Flour Mill, are under the leadership of the Public Enterprise Company. The original municipal enterprise companies will be incorporated into the Public Enterprise Company. The scope of the Industry Bureau's current leadership is the tram company, water company, and bus company – three public utilities – and private industry.

### 京市成立公营企业公司  统一领导市属公营工厂

1950-04-14
第4版()
专栏：

　　京市成立公营企业公司
　　统一领导市属公营工厂
    【京市新闻处讯】京市人民政府为加强市属公营工厂的领导，于本月十三日成立公营企业公司。原建设局副局长赵鹏飞、工业局副局长余涤清分任正副经理。划归公营企业公司领导的工厂有清河制呢厂、燕京造纸厂、新建玻璃厂、新建面粉厂等十余单位，原有的市属企业公司则将并入公营企业公司。工业局现在所领导的范围是电车公司、自来水公司、公共汽车公司三个公用事业和私营工业。
