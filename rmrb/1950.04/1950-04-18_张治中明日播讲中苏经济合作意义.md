Title: Zhang Zhizhong to Broadcast Tomorrow on the Significance of Sino-Soviet Economic Cooperation
Author:
Date: 1950-04-18
Page: 1

　　Zhang Zhizhong to Broadcast Tomorrow on the Significance of Sino-Soviet Economic Cooperation
　　[This newspaper] Central People's Government Committee member and Vice Chairman of the Northwest Military and Political Committee, Zhang Zhizhong, has been invited to deliver a radio address on the Central People's Broadcasting Station at 20:00 on the 19th, entitled "From the Sino-Soviet Treaty to the Significance of Sino-Soviet Economic Cooperation in Xinjiang". The program originally scheduled for broadcast at that time will be postponed.

### 张治中明日播讲中苏经济合作意义

1950-04-18
第1版()
专栏：

　　张治中明日播讲中苏经济合作意义
    【本报讯】中央人民政府委员、西北军政委员会副主席张治中应邀于十九日二十点正，在中央人民广播电台发表广播演讲，题目是“从中苏盟约说到中苏在新疆经济合作的意义”。原定该时间广播的节目改期播送。
