Title: Presidium of the Supreme Soviet Approves Sino-Soviet Treaty and Agreements
Author:
Date: 1950-04-14
Page: 1
　
　　Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, 13th: TASS, Moscow, 13th: The Presidium of the Supreme Soviet of the Soviet Union approved on April 11, 1950, the Treaty of Friendship, Alliance and Mutual Assistance between the Union of Soviet Socialist Republics and the People's Republic of China, the Agreement on the Chinese Changchun Railway, Port Arthur, and Dalian, and the Agreement on a loan to the People's Republic of China, all signed in Moscow on February 14, 1950.

### 苏最高苏维埃主席团批准中苏条约及协定

1950-04-14
第1版()
专栏：

　　苏最高苏维埃主席团批准中苏条约及协定
    【新华社十三日讯】塔斯社莫斯科十三日讯：苏联最高苏维埃主席团于一九五○年四月十一日批准了一九五○年二月十四日在莫斯科签订的苏维埃社会主义共和国联盟与中华人民共和国友好同盟互助条约，关于中国长春铁路、旅顺口及大连的协定，和关于贷款给中华人民共和国的协定。
