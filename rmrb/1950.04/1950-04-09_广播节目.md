　　Radio Programs
    [Our Dispatch] The Central People's Broadcasting Station today (9th) at 17:30, Workers' Time: Commentary: "No Infringement of Human Rights Allowed." 18:00, First Literary Program: Lianhualuo (a type of ballad) "Yang Fa-gui Smashes His Son." 21:00, Second Literary Program: China Arts Theatre broadcasts orchestral music: "Responding to Flowers," "Frontier Dance," "New Life in the Mountains," etc. 20:30, Weekly Literary Program: North China University Second Performing Arts Troupe sings Shaanxi-Gansu-Ningxia revolutionary story songs: "Singing of Liu Zhidan." 18:30, Broadcast of Soviet Songs.
    [Another Dispatch] The Beijing People's Broadcasting Station at 12:30, Workers' Program: Workers' news and songs. 13:00, Youth Program: New songs, Western music. 13:30, Local Operatic Music: Northeast Drum Song "Anti-Japanese Hero Li Zhaolin" and "Small Song of Sino-Soviet Friendship." 16:30, Peking Opera. 17:00, Children's Program: Sun Jingxiu tells "The Story of Timur."

### 广播节目

1950-04-09
第3版()
专栏：广播节目

　　广播节目
    【本报讯】中央人民广播电台今天（九日）十七点三十分职工时间：评论：《不容许侵犯人权》。十八点第一次文艺节目：莲花落《杨发贵摔子》。二十一点第二次文艺节目：人民艺术剧院演播管弦乐：《对花》、《塞外舞曲》、《山中新生》等。八点三十分星期文艺节目：华北大学第二文工团演唱陕北革命故事歌曲：《歌唱刘志丹》。十八点三十分播送苏联歌曲。
    【又讯】北京市人民广播电台十二点三十分工人节目：工人动态和歌曲。十三点青年节目：新歌曲，西乐。十三点三十分地方杂曲：东北大鼓《抗日英雄李兆麟》和《中苏友谊小唱》。十六点三十分京剧。十七点儿童节目：孙敬修讲《铁木儿的故事》。
