Title: Yan'an Commemorates "April 8th" Martyrs' Cemetery; Xinhua News Agency Headquarters Holds Memorial Meeting for Qin Bangxian
Author:
Date: 1950-04-09
Page: 1

　　Xinhua News Agency, Xi'an, April 8th: People from all walks of life in Yan'an solemnly commemorated the "April 8th" Martyrs' Cemetery on Qingming Festival. Before the memorial ceremony began, Li Jingying, Secretary of the Shaanbei District Committee of the Chinese Communist Party, reported on the accident in Black Tea Mountain on April 8, 1946, when Wang Ruofei, Qin Bangxian, Ye Ting, Deng Fa, and Huang Qisheng and other martyrs were flying from Chongqing to Yan'an. In the afternoon, the heads of various agencies also went to Qingliang Mountain and other places to sweep the tombs of Yang Song, the former editor-in-chief of the Liberation Daily, and Zhang Hao, the former secretary of the Central Workers Committee of the Chinese Communist Party.
　　Xinhua News Agency, April 8th: All staff members of the Xinhua News Agency headquarters gathered this afternoon to mourn the fourth anniversary of the death of Qin Bangxian (Bo Gu), the former director of the Yan'an Liberation Daily and Xinhua News Agency, and to mourn other revolutionary martyrs who died on April 8, 1946, and to commemorate Yang Song, the former editor-in-chief of the Yan'an Liberation Daily. Qin Bangxian's wife, Zhang Yuexia, also attended.
　　Xinhua News Agency, April 7th: We continue to receive news from all over the country about the commemoration of revolutionary martyrs by the people on Qingming Festival. More than ten units, including the Nanjing Municipal Committee of the Communist Party of China and the Municipal People's Government, sent representatives to the Yuhuatai Martyrs' Cemetery in the city to pay their respects in the rain on Qingming Festival. More than 10,000 teachers and students from the Education Department of the Hubei Provincial People's Government and secondary schools and above in Wuchang went to the tomb of Shi Yang, the "February 7th" martyr, to sweep the tomb. Wreaths presented by various schools piled up in front of the martyr's tomb, showing the people's infinite respect for the revolutionary martyrs. All the staff of the Logistics Department of the Hubei Military Region held a memorial meeting for the dead martyrs on the 5th, with a total of more than 700 people. People from all walks of life in Xi'an solemnly held a public memorial meeting for the martyrs in the city's Revolutionary Park on the 6th. Representatives of the people's governments of various districts and citizens of Shenyang went to the martyrs' cemeteries in Wanquan Park, Nanhu Park, and Xiaoheyan in the city on the 5th to pay tribute to the martyrs who gloriously sacrificed for the liberation of Northeast China, add soil to their graves, and rebuild new monuments. The people's governments and people from all walks of life in Harbin, Qiqihar, Chengde and other cities and Longhua, Rehe, held various ceremonies to commemorate the martyrs on the 5th.

### 延安祭“四八”烈士墓  新华总社集会悼念秦邦宪

1950-04-09
第1版()
专栏：

　　延安祭“四八”烈士墓
    新华总社集会悼念秦邦宪
    【新华社西安八日电】延安各界于清明节隆重祭“四、八”烈士墓。祭仪开始前，由中共陕北区区委会书记李景膺报告王若飞、秦邦宪、叶挺、邓发、黄齐生诸烈士一九四六年四月八日由重庆飞延安时在黑茶山遇难的经过。下午，各机关首长并往清凉山等地扫祭前解放日报总编辑杨松及前中共中央职工委员会书记张浩二烈士墓。
    【新华社八日讯】新华通讯社总社全体工作人员今日下午集会悼念前延安解放日报社兼新华通讯社社长秦邦宪（博古）遇难四周年，并悼念一九四六年四月八日遇难的其他革命烈士，同时纪念前延安解放日报总编辑杨松。秦邦宪夫人张月霞亦出席。
    【新华社七日讯】此间继续收到各地人民于清明节纪念革命烈士消息。中共南京市委员会、市人民政府等十余单位均派出代表，于清明节日纷纷冒雨前往该市雨花台烈士陵墓祭奠。湖北省人民政府教育厅及武昌中等以上学校师生共一万余人，赴“二七”烈士施洋地墓祭扫，各学校致敬的花圈堆满烈士墓前，表现出人民对革命先烈的无限崇敬。湖北军区后勤部全体工作人员共七百多人在五日举行了祭奠死难烈士大会。西安市各界人民于六日在该市革命公园隆重举行公祭先烈大会。沈阳市各区人民政府代表及市民于五日分赴该市万泉公园、南湖公园、小河沿等处的烈士墓地，向为解放东北而光荣牺牲的烈士致敬，并添土扫墓，重建新碑。哈尔滨、齐齐哈尔、承德等市及热河隆化等地人民政府和各界人民都于五日举行了各种纪念先烈的仪式。
