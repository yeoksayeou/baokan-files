Title: All-China Federation of Trade Unions Cadre School Establishes Stevedore Union Cadre Training Class: First Session Opened in Tianjin on the 5th

Author:

Date: 1950-04-09
Page: 3

All-China Federation of Trade Unions Cadre School
　　Establishes Stevedore Union Cadre Training Class
　　First Session Opened in Tianjin on the 5th
　　[This newspaper dispatch] In order to implement the resolutions of the First Congress of the China Stevedore Union, abolish the feudal monopoly system in stevedoring across various regions, establish stevedoring companies, and strengthen the work of the stevedore union, the All-China Federation of Trade Unions has decided to establish a short-term training class for stevedore union cadres at the All-China Federation of Trade Unions Cadre School. Over two hundred stevedore union work cadres from across the country have been transferred to the school to study. The various resolutions of the First Congress of the All-China Stevedore Union will be the main courses of study. The study period is tentatively set for one month. The first group of students have all arrived at the school in Tianjin and classes began on the 5th of this month. (All-China Federation of Trade Unions Cadre School Communications Group)

### 全总干部学校  设搬运工会干训班  第一期已于五日在津开学

1950-04-09
第3版()
专栏：

　　全总干部学校
    设搬运工会干训班
    第一期已于五日在津开学
    【本报讯】中华全国总工会为了贯彻中国搬运工会第一届代表大会的决议，废除各地搬运事业中的封建把持制度，建立搬运公司，加强搬运工会工作，特决定在全国总工会干部学校开办搬运工会干部短期训练班。抽调全国各地搬运工会工作干部二百余人来校学习。以全国搬运工会第一届代表大会各种决议案为主要学习课程。学习时间暂定为一个月。第一期学员现已全部到达天津该校，已于本月五日开课。（全总干校通讯组）
