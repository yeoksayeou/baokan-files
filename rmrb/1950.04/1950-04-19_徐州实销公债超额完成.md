Title: Xuzhou Successfully Completes Public Bond Sales, Exceeding Target

Author:

Date: 1950-04-19
Page: 2

Xinhua News Agency, Jinan, 16th. As of the 14th, the actual sales of public bonds in Xuzhou City reached 400,404 shares, exceeding the assigned task by 404 shares. People from all walks of life in the city are continuing to make payments, and the funds for the more than 10,000 shares of bonds purchased in excess will be paid in full soon.

### 徐州实销公债超额完成

1950-04-19
第2版()
专栏：

　　徐州实销公债超额完成
    【新华社济南十六日电】徐州市实销公债数字，至十四日止，已达四十万零四百零四分，超过分配任务四百零四分。现该市各界仍继续缴款，不日可将超额认购的一万余分公债款缴齐。
