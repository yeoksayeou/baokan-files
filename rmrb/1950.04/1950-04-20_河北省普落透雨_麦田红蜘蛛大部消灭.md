Title: Hebei Province Experiences General Moderate Rain, Most Wheat Field Red Spider Mites Eliminated
Author:
Date: 1950-04-20
Page: 2

(Via this newspaper)
    Hebei Province Experiences General Moderate Rain
    Most Wheat Field Red Spider Mites Eliminated

    [This Newspaper] According to the Ministry of Agriculture of the Central People's Government by telephone: On the 15th and 16th of this month, Hebei Province experienced general moderate rain, and most of the wheat field red spider mites and other insect pests were eliminated. At present, Party and government cadres in various parts of the province are leading the masses to continue to carry out insect extermination campaigns.

### 河北省普落透雨  麦田红蜘蛛大部消灭

1950-04-20
第2版()
专栏：

　　河北省普落透雨
    麦田红蜘蛛大部消灭
    【本报讯】据中央人民政府农业部电话：本月十五、十六两日，河北省普落透雨，麦田红蜘蛛等虫害大部消灭。现全省各地党、政干部，正领导群众继续开展除虫运动。
