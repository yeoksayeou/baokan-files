Title: Eight New Newspapers and Periodicals Approved by the Beijing Military Control Commission
Author:
Date: 1950-04-04
Page: 4

[Beijing News Agency Report] After investigation and research by the News Department of the Beijing Municipal People's Government, the Beijing Military Control Commission approved the registration of eight newspapers and periodicals that applied for registration in the second half of February and March, and issued temporary registration certificates: (1) One bi-monthly: *Observer*; (2) Six monthlies: *Work Bulletin*, *Tax Work Research*, *Science Popularization Bulletin*, *People's Customs*, *Drama Bulletin*, *Beijing Municipal Gazette*; (3) One irregular publication: *China Workers Pictorial*. From March of last year to March of this year, Beijing has approved the registration of 110 newspaper, periodical, and news agency units.

### 京市军管会新批准报纸期刊八种

1950-04-04
第4版()
专栏：

　　京市军管会新批准报纸期刊八种
    【京市新闻处讯】京市申请登记的报纸期刊经市人民政府新闻处调查研究，二月下半月及三月份呈奉军管会批准，准予登记并已发给临时登记证的计有八种：（一）半月刊一种：观察；（二）月刊六种：工作通讯、税工研究、科学普及通讯、人民海关、戏剧通讯、北京市政报；（三）不定期刊一种：中国工人画刊。自去年三月至今年三月，京市批准登记的报刊通讯社已有一百一十个单位。
