Title: Lebanese Government Persecutes Peace Fighters; Youth Peace Committee Protests
Author:
Date: 1950-04-20
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency, Damascus, April 17th: The Lebanese Youth for Peace Committee issued a statement protesting the Lebanese government's persecution of peace fighters and the banning of pro-peace rallies. The statement declared: On April 10th, police dispersed a peace rally in the Amin center area of northern Lebanon, arresting and imprisoning eleven participants. The statement emphasized: "This incident occurred at a time when imperialists are trying to turn our country into a strategic bridgehead and drive our people to become cannon fodder. However, like all people in the world, the Lebanese people have decided to fight for peace. They believe that they will be able to defend their freedom and independence."

### 黎政府迫害和平斗士  青年和委会提出抗议

1950-04-20
第4版()
专栏：

　　黎政府迫害和平斗士  青年和委会提出抗议
    【新华社十九日讯】塔斯社大马士革十七日讯：黎巴嫩青年拥护和平委员会发表声明，抗议黎巴嫩政府迫害和平斗士，并禁止举行争取和平的集会。声明宣称：在四月十日，警察曾冲散了黎巴嫩北部阿米恩中心区的和平集会，并逮捕和囚禁了十一名与会者。声明着重指出：“该事件发生在帝国主义者力图把我国变成战略桥头堡垒，并驱使我国人民充当炮灰的时候。然而，像全世界一切人民一样，黎巴嫩人民已决定为和平而斗争。他们相信：他们将能保卫他们的自由与独立。”
