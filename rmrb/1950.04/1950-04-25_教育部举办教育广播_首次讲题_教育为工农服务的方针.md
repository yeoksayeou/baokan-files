Title: Ministry of Education to Launch Educational Broadcasts; First Lecture: "The Direction of Education Serving Workers and Peasants"
Author:
Date: 1950-04-25
Page: 3

　　Ministry of Education to Launch Educational Broadcasts
    First Lecture: "The Direction of Education Serving Workers and Peasants"
    [Xinhua News Agency, April 24th] The Ministry of Education of the Central People's Government, with the approval of the Central People's Broadcasting Station, has decided to launch weekly educational broadcasts to explain educational policies, report on the educational situation, and exchange educational experiences. The speakers will be heads of units under the Ministry and education experts. The first broadcast is scheduled for April 26th (Wednesday) from 8:00 PM to 8:30 PM (Beijing time), with Qian Junrui, Deputy Minister of Education of the Central People's Government, speaking on: "The Direction of Education Serving Workers and Peasants." This educational broadcast, except for postponements or rescheduling due to special circumstances, is scheduled to be held every Wednesday from 8:00 PM to 8:30 PM (Beijing time).

### 教育部举办教育广播  首次讲题“教育为工农服务的方针”

1950-04-25
第3版()
专栏：

　　教育部举办教育广播
    首次讲题“教育为工农服务的方针”
    【新华社二十四日讯】中央人民政府教育部征得中央人民广播电台的同意，决定举办每周教育广播，以解释教育政策，报道教育情况，交流教育经验。主讲人将由该部所属各单位的负责人及教育专家担任。首次广播定于本月二十六日（星期三）下午八时至八时半（北京时间）举行，由中央人民政府教育部钱俊瑞副部长讲：“教育为工农服务的方针”。此项教育广播除因特殊情形展期或改期外，定于每星期三下午八时至八时半（北京时间）按期举行。
