Date: 1950-04-22
Page: 4

　　Broadcast Programs
　　Central People's Broadcasting Station today (April 22nd) at 19:00 Social Science Lecture: Soviet economics master, Associate Professor Bao Deliev, lectures on "The Financial System of the Soviet State." 20:00 People's Forum: A listener of the station exposes the conspiracy of remnant bandit broadcasts. 18:45 Natural Science Lecture: The station's lecture department talks about "From the successful trial production of Clostridium chauvoei vaccine to the prevention and treatment of rinderpest." 12:45 Film Introduction: Soviet film "Lenin's Life." 21:45 Character Story: The story of the heroine Liu Hulan. 18:00 Folk Art: Yang Jirui sings the Western River Drum "Catching Demons and Exorcising Evil." 20:30 Literary Program: Writer Lao She reads "Xiangzi in the Teahouse." 22:00 Literary Program: The Beijing Opera Experimental School of the Ministry of Culture's Opera Improvement Bureau performs the Beijing Opera "Da Cao Gu".
　　Beijing People's Broadcasting Station today at 16:30 Youth Program: (1) School News, (2) Western Music. 17:00 Time Signal, Entertainment Program: Soviet music and songs. 17:30 Children's Program: (1) Telling several stories of Lenin, (2) Storytelling and singing by children from the Central National Elementary School in the First District. 18:00 Workers' Program: Teaching to sing the song "Commemorating May Day". 18:30 Voice of the Municipality: Introducing the road excavation rules formulated by the Beijing Municipal Construction Bureau. 20:00 Russian Language Lecture. 21:30 Women's Program: Dr. An Xiaolan of Peking University Medical College speaks on "What to do if a child doesn't have milk to eat?"

### 广播节目

1950-04-22
第4版()
专栏：

　　广播节目
    中央人民广播电台今天（二十二日）十九点社会科学讲座：苏联经济学硕士包德列夫副教授讲《苏维埃国家的财政制度》。二十点人民讲坛：该台一个听者揭露残匪广播的阴谋。十八点四十五分自然科学讲座：该台讲座科讲《从气肿疽菌苗试制成功谈到牛瘟的防治》。十二点四十五分电影介绍：苏联影片《列宁生平》。二十一点四十五分人物故事：女英雄刘胡兰的故事。十八点曲艺：杨吉瑞演唱西河大鼓《捉妖拿邪》。二十点三十分文艺节目：作家老舍朗诵《祥子在茶馆里》。二十二点文艺节目：文化部戏曲改进局京戏实验学校演唱京剧《打草谷》。
    北京市人民广播电台今天十六点三十分青年节目：（１）学校新闻，（２）西乐，十七点对时，文娱节目：苏联音乐和歌曲。十七点三十分儿童节目：（一）讲几个列宁的故事，（二）第一区中心国民小学小朋友们讲故事唱歌。十八点工人节目：教唱《纪念五一节》歌。十八点三十分市政之声：介绍北京市建设局拟定的掘路规则。二十点俄语讲座。二十一点三十分妇女节目：北大医学院安笑兰大夫讲《小孩没奶吃怎么办？》
