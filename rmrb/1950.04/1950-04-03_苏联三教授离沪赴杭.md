Title: Three Soviet Professors Leave Shanghai for Hangzhou
Author:
Date: 1950-04-03
Page: 3

Xinhua News Agency, Shanghai, April 2nd: Three Soviet professors, Dr. Nurzhin (Biology), Dr. Giselev (History), and Ms. Makarova (Economics), who were invited to China to lecture, have completed their lectures in Shanghai and left for Hangzhou by train on the afternoon of the 1st. Those who went to the station to see them off included Chen Wangdao, Li Zhaohuan, and Xia Yan, Vice Presidents and Secretary-General of the Sino-Soviet Friendship Association Shanghai Branch, Wu Youxun, President of Jiaotong University, Professors Lu Yudao and Zhou Gucheng of Fudan University, Shen Tilan, representatives of Shanghai People's Organizations and CPPCC, students from Fudan University, and representatives from Shanghai's cultural, scientific, and economic circles, totaling more than 80 people. Professor Nurzhin and the other two professors arrived in Shanghai from Nanjing on the evening of March 27th and gave ten special lectures at Jiaotong University, Fudan University, the Chinese Academy of Sciences, and the Great Light Cinema, and participated in three symposiums. The audience included professors, students, and government cadres, totaling 10,000 people.

　　Also, Hangzhou News: Professors Nurzhin, Giselev, and Makarova arrived in Hangzhou from Shanghai at 8:41 PM on the 1st. Those who went to the station to welcome them included Zhang Jingfu, Director of the Finance and Economics Committee of the Zhejiang Provincial People's Government, Lin Hujia, Minister of Propaganda of the Zhejiang Provincial Committee of the Chinese Communist Party, Yu Zhongwu, Deputy Director of the Education Department of the Zhejiang Provincial People's Government, Chen Xuezhao, Secretary-General of the Zhejiang Branch of the Sino-Soviet Friendship Association, Bei Shizhang, Dean of the School of Science of Zhejiang University, Yan Rengeng, Dean of Academic Affairs, and representatives of the Hangzhou Branch of the China Association of Scientific Workers, totaling more than 20 people. Professor Nurzhin and the other two professors will give academic lectures in Hangzhou.

### 苏联三教授离沪赴杭

1950-04-03
第3版()
专栏：

　　苏联三教授离沪赴杭
    【新华社上海二日电】应邀来我国讲学的苏联教授生物学博士努日金、历史学博士吉谢列夫、经济学硕士马卡洛娃，已在沪讲学完毕，于一日下午乘车离沪赴杭。赴车站欢送者有中苏友协上海分会副会长陈望道、黎照寰、总干事夏衍，交通大学校长吴有训，复旦大学教授卢于道、周谷城，上海各人民团体政协代表沈体兰，复旦大学学生以及上海文化、科学、经济各界人士等共八十余人。努日金等三教授系于三月二十七日晚自宁抵沪，先后在交通大学、复旦大学、中国科学院、大光明电影院等处作专题演讲十次，参加座谈会三次，听众有教授、学生、机关干部等达一万人。
    又杭州讯：努日金、吉谢列夫、马卡洛娃等三教授已于一日晚八时四十一分由沪抵杭。赴车站欢迎者有浙江省人民政府财政经济委员会主任张劲夫、中共浙江省委宣传部长林乎加、浙省人民政府教育厅副厅长俞仲武、中苏友好协会浙江分会总干事陈学昭及浙江大学理学院院长贝时璋、教务长严仁赓与中国科学工作者协会杭州分会代表等二十余人。努日金等三教授在杭将分别作学术讲演。
