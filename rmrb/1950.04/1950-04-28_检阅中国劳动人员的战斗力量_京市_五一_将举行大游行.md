Title: Reviewing the Fighting Strength of Chinese Laborers: Beijing to Hold Grand Parade on "May Day"
Author:
Date: 1950-04-28
Page: 1

　　Reviewing the Fighting Strength of Chinese Laborers
    Beijing to Hold Grand Parade on "May Day"
    [Beijing News Agency Dispatch] On May 1st, the capital will hold a grand "May Day" Labor Day parade to celebrate the first "May Day" Labor Day after the founding of the People's Republic of China, review, and mobilize the fighting strength of the Chinese working class and all working people to defend world peace and consolidate the victory of the Chinese people. Participants in the parade will include the army, navy, and air force units defending the capital, as well as workers, students, government staff, and citizens, expected to reach 300,000 people. The "May Day Labor Day Grand Parade Preparatory Committee," composed of government agencies at all levels, various parties, military units, and mass organizations invited by the Beijing Municipal People's Government, has now begun work within the Municipal Government.

### 检阅中国劳动人员的战斗力量  京市“五一”将举行大游行

1950-04-28
第1版()
专栏：

　　检阅中国劳动人员的战斗力量
    京市“五一”将举行大游行
    【京市新闻处讯】五月一日首都将举行“五一”劳动节大游行，庆祝中华人民共和国成立后第一个“五一”劳动节，检阅与动员中国工人阶级及一切劳动人民为保卫世界和平与巩固中国人民胜利果实的战斗力量。参加游行的将包括捍卫首都的陆、海、空军部队以及工人、学生、机关人员及市民，预计可达三十万人。京市人民政府邀集各级政府机关、各党派、部队、群众团体所组成的“五一劳动节大游行筹备会”现已在市政府内开始办公。
