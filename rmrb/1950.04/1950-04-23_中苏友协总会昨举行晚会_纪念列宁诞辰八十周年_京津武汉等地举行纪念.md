Title: Sino-Soviet Friendship Association Headquarters Holds Evening Party to Commemorate the 80th Anniversary of Lenin's Birth; Commemorations Held in Beijing, Tianjin, Wuhan and Other Places

Author:
Date: 1950-04-23
Page: 1
　
　　Xinhua News Agency, April 22nd: The Sino-Soviet Friendship Association Headquarters held a lecture and film commemorative evening party tonight to commemorate the 80th anniversary of Lenin's birth. More than 700 people from various organizations attended. Zhao Zhongchi, Deputy Secretary-General of the Association, spoke on "The Guidance and Influence of Several Issues in Leninism on the Chinese Revolution." After the speech, the film "The Life of Lenin" was screened.

　　Xinhua News Agency, April 22nd: Today, Beijing and Tianjin newspapers published special articles or photos with huge coverage to commemorate the 80th anniversary of Lenin's birth. The Beijing People's Daily also published an editorial entitled "Commemorate Lenin and Learn Internationalism." Some organizations and schools in Tianjin held commemorations. Major cinemas in both cities jointly screened the film "The Life of Lenin" today. Major bookstores will also jointly offer a 20% discount for three days starting today on related works by Lenin.

　　The Hankou and Wuchang (Hubei) branches of the Sino-Soviet Friendship Association held separate gatherings today to commemorate the 80th anniversary of Lenin's birth. Major bookstores in both cities also offered a three-day discount on Lenin's works.

### 中苏友协总会昨举行晚会  纪念列宁诞辰八十周年  京津武汉等地举行纪念

1950-04-23
第1版()
专栏：

　　中苏友协总会昨举行晚会
    纪念列宁诞辰八十周年
    京津武汉等地举行纪念
    【新华社二十二日讯】中苏友协总会今晚举行纪念列宁诞辰八十周年讲演电影纪念晚会，到会各机关团体七百余人，由该会副总干事赵仲池讲“列宁主义中若干问题对于中国革命的指导和影响”。演讲后并放映电影《列宁生平》。
    【新华社二十二日讯】北京、天津各报今日均以巨大篇幅刊载专文或照片纪念列宁诞生八十周年。北京人民日报并发表题为《纪念列宁、学习国际主义》的社论。天津市部分机关学校分别举行纪念。两地各大电影院今日均联合放映《列宁生平》影片。各大书店亦于今日起联合减价三天，有关列宁著作均八折出售。
    汉口和武昌（湖北）的中苏友好协会分会，今日分别集会纪念列宁八十诞辰。两地各大书店也减价三天出售列宁著作。
