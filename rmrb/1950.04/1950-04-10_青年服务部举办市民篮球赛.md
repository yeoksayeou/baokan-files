Title: Youth Service Department to Hold Citywide Basketball Tournament
Author:
Date: 1950-04-10
Page: 6

　　People's Garden

　　Youth Service Department to Hold Citywide Basketball Tournament
    In order to more broadly popularize national physical education and unite youth from all walks of life, the Youth Service Department has decided to hold a citywide basketball tournament this month. Shops, handicraft industries, unemployed and out-of-school youth, and adult night schools within the city's jurisdiction are all welcome to form teams and participate. Registration begins on the 8th. The registration location is the Youth Service Department on East Chang'an Street.

### 青年服务部举办市民篮球赛

1950-04-10
第6版()
专栏：人民园地

　　青年服务部举办市民篮球赛
    青年服务部为了更广泛地普及国民体育运动，团结各界青年，决定在本月内举办市民篮球比赛，凡市辖区内之商店、手工业行业、失学失业者、成人夜校都可组队参加。自八日起开始报名。报名地点在东长安街青年服务部。
