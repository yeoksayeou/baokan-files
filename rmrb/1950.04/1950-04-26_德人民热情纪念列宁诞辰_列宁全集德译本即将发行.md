Title: German People Warmly Commemorate Lenin's Birthday; German Translation of Lenin's Collected Works to be Published Soon
Author:
Date: 1950-04-26
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, April 24th, TASS: The Pravda correspondent in Berlin, Korolkov, reports that the working people of the German Democratic Republic are enthusiastically commemorating the 80th anniversary of Lenin's birth, stating: Factories, higher education institutions, and rural collectives have all organized special theoretical studies of "Lenin and the Struggle for a New Type of Party." Due to the increasing demand for the works of Lenin and Stalin, the Central Committee of the Socialist Unity Party of Germany has decided to publish the German translation of Lenin's Collected Works. A grand exhibition was held in the center of Berlin. Films commemorating Lenin are being screened throughout the country. The Greater Berlin Municipal Council has decided to rename Landesberger Allee as Lenin Avenue and Landesberger Platz as Lenin Square.

### 德人民热情纪念列宁诞辰  列宁全集德译本即将发行

1950-04-26
第4版()
专栏：

　　德人民热情纪念列宁诞辰
    列宁全集德译本即将发行
    【新华社二十五日讯】塔斯社莫斯科二十四日讯：真理报驻柏林记者科罗尔科夫报道德意志民主共和国劳动人民热情纪念列宁诞辰八十周年称：各工厂，各高等教育机关及农村团体，都组织了“列宁及为新型政党而斗争”的特别理论学习。由于对列宁、斯大林著作的需求日益增加，德国统一社会党中央委员会已决定发行德译列宁全集。柏林市中心举行了盛大展览会。纪念列宁的影片在各地放映。大柏林市市政委员会已决定将兰德斯伯格大道改名为列宁大道，将兰德斯伯格广场改名为列宁广场。
