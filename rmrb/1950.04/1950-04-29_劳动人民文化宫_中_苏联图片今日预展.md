Title: Soviet Photos to be Previewed Today at the "Laboring People's Cultural Palace"
Author:
Date: 1950-04-29
Page: 4

　　"Laboring People's Cultural Palace"
    Soviet Photos to be Previewed Today

    [Beijing News Agency] The Soviet Photo Exhibition, sponsored by the Beijing Sino-Soviet Friendship Association, is scheduled to officially open on April 30th at the Laboring People's Cultural Palace (formerly the Imperial Ancestral Temple). The exhibition includes nine parts: (1) "May Day" in the Soviet Union, (2) Heroic Figures, (3) Industrial Production, (4) Collective Farms, (5) Culture and Education, (6) Children and Youth Life, (7) Supreme Soviet Election Activities, (8) One of the Awakened People's Lands - the Uzbek Republic, (9) Sino-Soviet Friendship. The association will hold a preview today (the 29th) to entertain people from all walks of life and heads of various branches of the Sino-Soviet Friendship Association. The exhibition is scheduled for half a month (April 30th - May 15th). The exhibition time is set from 10:00 AM to 6:00 PM every day. People from all walks of life are requested to visit on time.
    [Another News] At the same time, the association will also hold small exhibitions at the Gulou No. 1 Cultural Center and the No. 4 Cultural Center (Liulichang Fire God Temple), also for half a month (May 1st - 15th).

### “劳动人民文化宫”中  苏联图片今日预展

1950-04-29
第4版()
专栏：

　　“劳动人民文化宫”中
    苏联图片今日预展
    【京市新闻处讯】北京市中苏友好协会主办的苏联图片展览会定于四月三十日在劳动人民文化宫（前太庙）正式揭幕。展览内容包括九个部分：（一）“五一”在苏联（二）英雄像（三）工业生产（四）集体农庄（五）文化教育（六）儿童青年生活（七）最高苏维埃选举活动（八）觉醒了的人民国土之一——乌兹别克共和国（九）中苏友好。该会于今日（二十九日）举行预展，招待各界人士及中苏友协各支会负责人。该展览会定期为半个月（四月三十日——五月十五日）展览时间定为每日上午十时至下午六时，希各界人民准时前往参观。
    【另讯】该会同时又于鼓楼第一文化馆和第四文化馆（琉璃厂火神庙）举行小型展览，时间亦为半月（五月一日——十五日）。
