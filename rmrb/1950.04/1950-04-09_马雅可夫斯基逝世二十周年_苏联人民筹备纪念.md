Title: Soviet People Prepare to Commemorate the Twentieth Anniversary of Mayakovsky's Death
Author:
Date: 1950-04-09
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, 6th: The Soviet people are preparing to commemorate the twentieth anniversary of the death of the great Soviet poet Mayakovsky on April 14th. Millions of people who love the poet's works know Stalin's words: "Mayakovsky is the best and most talented poet of our Soviet era." Mayakovsky used all his poems to teach the Soviet people to infinitely love their socialist motherland, to hate all its enemies, and to be vigilant and cautiously guard the fruits of the October Revolution.
　　
The Central Writers' Club in Moscow is holding a series of commemorative meetings, and the annual Mayakovsky poetry recitation competition has already begun at the Moscow State Literary Museum. Clubs, cultural palaces, and libraries are preparing lectures and exhibitions.

### 马雅可夫斯基逝世二十周年  苏联人民筹备纪念

1950-04-09
第4版()
专栏：

　　马雅可夫斯基逝世二十周年
    苏联人民筹备纪念
    【新华社八日讯】塔斯社莫斯科六日讯：苏联人民正在准备于四月十四日纪念苏联伟大诗人马雅可夫斯基逝世二十周年。千百万热爱诗人作品的人民都知道斯大林的话：“马雅可夫斯基是我们苏维埃时代最优秀的，最有才能的诗人”。马雅可夫斯基曾以他的全部诗作教导苏联人民要无限热爱社会主义祖国，要痛恨它的一切敌人，要警惕并谨慎警卫十月革命的果实。
    莫斯科作家中央俱乐部正在举行一连串的纪念会，一年一度的马雅可夫斯基作品朗诵比赛，已经在莫斯科国立文学博物馆中开始。各俱乐部、文化宫、图书馆正在筹备讲演会和展览会。
