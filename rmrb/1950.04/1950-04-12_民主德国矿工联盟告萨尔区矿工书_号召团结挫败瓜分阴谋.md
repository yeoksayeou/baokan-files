Title: Democratic German Miners' Union Writes to Saar Miners, Calling for Unity to Thwart Partition Conspiracy
Author:
Date: 1950-04-12
Page: 4

　　Democratic German Miners' Union Writes to Saar Miners
    Calling for Unity to Thwart Partition Conspiracy
    [Xinhua News Agency, Berlin, 11th] TASS Berlin, 8th: The Central Council of the Miners' Union of the German Democratic Republic has issued a letter to the miners of the Saar region, urging them to further unite their ranks to thwart the schemes of the imperialists and their German accomplices. The letter assures the working people of the Saar that the miners of the German Democratic Republic will resolutely oppose the treacherous transfer of the Saar to the imperialist countries, which want to use it as an important center for preparing new wars. The letter concludes: Do not succumb to the imperialist countries and their German accomplices! Organize united trade union actions as the most powerful weapon in the struggle against the enemies of the working class!

### 民主德国矿工联盟告萨尔区矿工书  号召团结挫败瓜分阴谋

1950-04-12
第4版()
专栏：

　　民主德国矿工联盟告萨尔区矿工书
    号召团结挫败瓜分阴谋
    【新华社十一日讯】塔斯社柏林八日讯：德意志民主共和国矿工联盟中央理事会发出告萨尔区矿工书，要他们更进一步团结他们的队伍，以挫败帝国主义者及其德国的共谋犯们的诡计。该书向萨尔劳动人民保证：德意志民主共和国的矿工们，将坚决反对背信弃义地把萨尔转交与帝国主义国家，那些国家想利用它作为准备新的战争的重要中心。该书最后称：不要向帝国主义国家和它们的德国共谋犯屈服！组织工会的团结行动，作为对工人阶级敌人斗争中的最强有力的武器！
