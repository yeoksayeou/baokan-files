　　Lecture Announcement
    [Beijing News Agency Dispatch] The ninth lecture of the "Introduction to the Soviet Union" series, sponsored by the Beijing Sino-Soviet Friendship Association, will be held at 7:00 PM on Sunday, April 30th, at the Soviet Foreign Cultural Association at 19 Nanheyan Street. Soviet legal expert Sudarikov will give a lecture on "May 1st is International Labor Day." A film will be shown after the lecture. All those holding long-term or temporary lecture passes from the association are welcome to attend.
            (Beijing Sino-Soviet Friendship Association)

### 讲座预告

1950-04-27
第4版()
专栏：

　　讲座预告
    【京市新闻处讯】北京市中苏友好协会主办的《苏联介绍》讲座第九讲，定于四月三十日（星期日）下午七时在南河沿十九号苏联对外文化协会举行。请苏联法学专家苏达立科夫主讲“五月一日是国际劳动节日。”会后并放映电影。凡持有该会长期或临时听讲证者，皆可前往听讲。
            （北京市中苏友好协会）
