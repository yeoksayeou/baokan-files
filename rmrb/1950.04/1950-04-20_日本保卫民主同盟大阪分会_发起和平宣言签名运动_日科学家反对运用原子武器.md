Title: Osaka Branch of the Japan Democratic Protection League Initiates Signature Campaign for Peace Declaration; Japanese Scientists Oppose the Use of Atomic Weapons
Author:
Date: 1950-04-20
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, 19th: TASS dispatch from Tokyo on the 16th quoting the Tokyo Akahata (Red Flag) newspaper: The Executive Committee of the Osaka Branch of the Japan Democratic Protection League has passed a resolution to initiate a signature campaign for a peace declaration. The peace movement has begun to develop in various rural areas. A residents' meeting held in a village in Fukushima Prefecture decided to support the Communist Party village group's proposal to develop the peace movement and conclude a comprehensive peace treaty with Japan. A meeting of the youth federation and various organizations in Ishikawa District, Fukushima Prefecture, also passed similar resolutions.

According to a United Press dispatch from Tokyo on the 17th: One hundred and twenty-one senior Japanese scientists and scholars, at a meeting held in Tokyo, resolved to oppose the use of atomic science for war. The resolution also stated that the results of scientific research must be used for peace.

### 日本保卫民主同盟大阪分会  发起和平宣言签名运动  日科学家反对运用原子武器

1950-04-20
第4版()
专栏：

　　日本保卫民主同盟大阪分会
    发起和平宣言签名运动
    日科学家反对运用原子武器
    【新华社十九日讯】据塔斯社十六日电引东京赤旗报消息：日本保卫民主同盟大阪分会执行委员会通过决定，发起和平宣言的签名运动。和平运动开始在各乡村地区发展起来。在福岛县某村举行的居民大会，决定支持共产党乡村小组发展和平运动和缔结全面对日和约运动的建议。福岛县石川郡的青年联合会和各组织的会议，也通过了类似的决定。
    另据合众社东京十七日讯：日本高级科学家与学者一百二十一名，顷在东京举行的会议中，决议反对把原子科学运用于战争。该决议并称：科学研究的结果，必须用于和平。
