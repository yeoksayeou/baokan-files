　　Broadcast Programs
    [Our Newspaper] The Central People's Broadcasting Station today (the 2nd) at 20:20, International Current Affairs. 20:30: (1) Encourage liberated peasants to raise funds to establish schools; (2) The story of Li Hailian's rationalization proposals. 17:30 Worker's Time. 18:30 Natural Science Lecture: "The Total Solar Eclipse on April 3rd." 20:30 Saturday Arts Program: Yangko Opera "Land Returns Home." 18:00 First Arts Program: Qinshu "Three Warriors Pushing a Boat Across the River." 21:00 Second Arts Program: Interview Recording "Folk Music of Lushun-Dalian." 18:45 New Songs and Instrumental Music.
    [Another News] Beijing People's Broadcasting Station at 12:30 Worker's Program: Worker Dynamics: Talking about the situation of several factories and enterprises running Factory Management Committees. 13:00 Youth Program: Western Music Appreciation. 13:30 Local Operas: Local Zhui Zi "Fiancée Persuades Husband to Join the Army." 18:45 "Talking about Lunar Eclipses."

专栏：广播节目

　　广播节目
    【本报讯】中央人民广播电台今天（二日）二十点二十分国际时事。二十点三十分：（一）鼓励翻身农民集资兴学；（二）李海连提合理化建议的故事。十七点三十分职工时间。十八点三十分自然科学讲座：“四月三日的全食”。八点三十分星期文艺节目：秧歌剧“土地返家”。十八点第一次文艺节目：琴书“三勇士推船渡江”。二十一点第二次文艺节目：采访录音“旅大民间音乐”。十八点四十五分新歌曲和器乐。
