Title: National Conference of Young Pioneers Workers Concludes
Author:
Date: 1950-04-28
Page: 3

Xinhua News Agency, April 27th: The National Conference of Young Pioneers Workers, which opened on the 23rd of this month and lasted for five days, concluded today. The conference heard the report on Young Pioneers work by He Li, Director of the Young Pioneers Department of the Central Committee of the Communist Youth League, as well as experience reports on the activities and guidance work of Young Pioneers teams in Beijing, Harbin, Dalian, Shijiazhuang, and other places. The attendees unanimously supported the team flag, team song, team salute, team member badge, and slogans promulgated by the Central Committee of the Communist Youth League. At the conclusion of the conference, Feng Wenbin, Secretary of the Central Committee of the Communist Youth League, gave a summary report.

### 全国少年儿童工作干部大会闭幕

1950-04-28
第3版()
专栏：

　　全国少年儿童工作干部大会闭幕
    【新华社二十七日讯】本月二十三日召开的全国少年儿童工作干部大会，历时五日，已于今日结束。大会听取了青年团中央少年儿童部部长何礼的少年儿童工作报告，及北京、哈尔滨、大连、石家庄等地少年儿童队的活动、辅导工作的经验报告。与会者一致拥护青年团中央颁布的队旗、队歌、队礼、队员标志和口号。大会结束时，青年团中央书记冯文彬作总结报告。
