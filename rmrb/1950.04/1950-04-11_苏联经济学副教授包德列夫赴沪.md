Title: Soviet Economics Associate Professor Baudlev Goes to Shanghai
Author:
Date: 1950-04-11
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, April 10th: Soviet economics master and associate professor Baudlev, who was invited by the Sino-Soviet Friendship Association to lecture in our country, completed his lectures in Beijing today and departed this afternoon to continue lecturing in Shanghai. Associate Professor Baudlev delivered a total of eight lectures in Beijing and participated in seven symposiums and tea parties, with a total audience of approximately 12,490 people. During his stay in Beijing, Associate Professor Baudlev visited the Beijing Library, National Peking University, and Tsinghua University.

### 苏联经济学副教授包德列夫赴沪

1950-04-11
第1版()
专栏：

　　苏联经济学副教授包德列夫赴沪
    【新华社十日讯】应中苏友好协会总会邀请来我国讲学的苏联经济学硕士包德列夫副教授，今日在京讲学完毕，已于下午启程赴沪继续讲学。包德列夫副教授总计在京讲演共八次，参加座谈会及茶话会七次，听众共约一万二千四百九十人。包德列夫副教授在留京期间，曾参观了北京图书馆、国立北京大学和清华大学。
