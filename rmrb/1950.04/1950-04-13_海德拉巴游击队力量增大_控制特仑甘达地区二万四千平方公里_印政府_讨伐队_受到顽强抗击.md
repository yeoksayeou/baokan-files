Title: Hyderabad Guerrilla Forces Grow Stronger, Control 24,000 Square Kilometers of Telangana Region, Indian Government "Suppression Force" Meets Fierce Resistance
Author:
Date: 1950-04-13
Page: 4

　　Xinhua News Agency, Delhi, 12th: The Hyderabad magazine reported on the 10th: Indian authorities have taken new repressive actions against the Hyderabad guerrillas. These actions are being carried out by motorized police units under the command of the special commissioner of Telangana-Nanchap. The commissioner has unlimited power. It is reported that Madras Province is also taking similar repressive actions against the guerrillas. According to the "Hindustan Times", a guerrilla force of 2,000 men has controlled 24,000 square kilometers of land in the Telangana region and is putting up a stubborn resistance against the suppression forces.

### 海德拉巴游击队力量增大  控制特仑甘达地区二万四千平方公里  印政府“讨伐队”受到顽强抗击

1950-04-13
第4版()
专栏：

　　海德拉巴游击队力量增大
    控制特仑甘达地区二万四千平方公里
    印政府“讨伐队”受到顽强抗击
    【新华社十二日讯】塔斯社德里讯：海德拉巴杂志十日报道：印度当局已对海德拉巴游击队采取新的镇压行动。这些行动是由特仑甘达·南查普的特别专员指挥下的警察摩托化部队进行的。该专员握有无限的权力。据悉，马德拉斯省也在对游击队采取同样的镇压行动。根据“印度斯坦时报”的消息。拥有两千人的游击部队，已控制了特仑甘达地区中的二万四千平方公里的土地，并对讨伐部队作顽强的抗击。
