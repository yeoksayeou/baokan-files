Title: Czech National Court Sentences Two American Spies to Prison
Author:
Date: 1950-04-18
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, 17th Dispatch: According to reports from TASS and CTK: The Czechoslovak National Court on the 13th tried two American spies, defendants Elsner and Kasellovskaia, who were employees hired by the American Embassy in Prague last summer. They were tried for espionage and conspiracy against the Czech Republic. Elsner was a Nazi official during the war, and Kasellovskaia was formerly the secretary of Lee Arns, a correspondent for the British London Times in the Czech Republic, who has been expelled for engaging in anti-Czech activities. The trial also revealed that Kolarek, the press attache of the American Embassy, engaged in espionage activities to subvert the Czech Republic. It fully confirmed that both defendants were Kolarek's loyal tools. They published and distributed propaganda materials inciting treason and stole state secrets. Both defendants have pleaded guilty.

The court sentenced Elsner to 18 years in prison and Kasellovskaia to 15 years in prison.

### 捷克国家法庭判处两美间谍徒刑

1950-04-18
第4版()
专栏：

　　捷克国家法庭判处两美间谍徒刑
    【新华社十七日讯】综合塔斯社电通社讯：捷克斯洛伐克国家法庭于十三日审讯两名美国间谍，被告埃尔斯纳与卡赛罗夫斯卡娅为美国驻布拉格大使馆去夏雇用的职员，因进行反捷克共和国的间谍与阴谋罪受审。埃尔斯纳在战争期间是纳粹的官员，卡赛罗夫斯卡娅曾任英国伦敦泰晤士报驻捷记者李阿斯的秘书，李阿斯已因进行反捷活动被逐出境。审讯并揭露了美国大使馆新闻参赞科拉列克从事阴谋颠覆捷克共和国的间谍活动，完全证实两被告都是科拉列克的忠实工具，他们曾出版和散发煽动叛国的宣传材料，并窃取国家机密。两被告均已服罪。
    法庭判处埃尔斯纳十八年有期徒刑，判处卡赛罗夫斯卡娅十五年有期徒刑。
