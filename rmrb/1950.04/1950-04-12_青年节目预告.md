Title: Youth Program Announcement
Author:
Date: 1950-04-12
Page: 4

　　Youth Program Announcement
    [This newspaper reported] The Beijing People's Broadcasting Station's youth program has been changed from 16:30 to 17:00 daily, starting from the 10th of this month. The program content from the 13th to the 16th is as follows:
    April 13th (Thursday) (1) School News. (2) Youth Lecture: Comrade Lü Zheng from the Ministry of Agriculture of the Central People's Government will speak on "Several Issues Concerning Spring Sowing Vegetable Cultivation."
    April 14th (Friday) (1) School News. (2) Youth Lecture: Professor Wang Juyuan from the Department of Horticulture of the Agricultural University will speak on "Several Issues to Pay Attention to When Growing Vegetables."
    April 15th (Saturday) (1) School News. (2) Music Appreciation.
    April 16th (Sunday) Cultural Entertainment
    [This newspaper reported] The Central People's Broadcasting Station will present a social science lecture at 19:00 today (the 12th): Ai Siqi will speak on "Labor Creates the World." At 20:00, the People's Forum: Roumanna, Deputy Director of the Preparatory Committee of the Northwest Women's Federation, Central Committee Member of the Xinjiang Peace and Democracy League, and representative of the Tatar women, will speak on "Xinjiang Women Before and After Liberation." At 18:45, the Natural Science Lecture: the station's lecture group will speak on "The Creation of New Wheat Varieties." At 21:45, a biographical story: "The Story of the Female Hero Liu Hulan." At 13:00, story telling: "New Children's Heroes." At 18:00, ballad singing: Cai Liangui will perform the Western River Drum ballad "Shi Bu Lan Drives the Cart." At 20:30, an art and literature program: The Broadcasting Work Group of the Central Academy of Drama Theater will recite literary works: (1) Ten Mile Salt Bay (narrative poem); (2) Xiao Fen and Xiao Wa (narrative poem); (3) Grits and the Landlord (Soviet folk tale). At 22:00, an art and literature program: Lian Kuoru will broadcast the storytelling "Zhou Detachment Makes a Big Scene in Pingchuan."
    [Another report] The Beijing People's Broadcasting Station will broadcast a workers' program at 7:30: (1) Music; (2) Correspondence speech: Jin Yutang from the Fengtai Locomotive Depot will speak, introducing Liu Yulin, a first-class meritorious worker of the depot. At 16:30, a youth program: School news; (2) Broadcasting the opera "Wang Xiuluan." At 18:30, Voice of the Municipality: Talking about the nutritional value of brown rice. At 21:30, listener service: (1) Answering the question "How to transplant trees"; (2) Answering the question "How to learn painting on your own"; (3) Recruitment notice for technicians from the North China Agricultural Machinery General Factory; (4) Brief reply to Mr. Liu Zihua.

### 青年节目预告

1950-04-12
第4版()
专栏：广播节目

　　青年节目预告
    【本报讯】北京市人民广播电台青年节目从本月十日起改在每天十六点三十分到十七点，十三日到十六日节目内容如下：
    十三日（星期四）（一）学校新闻。（二）青年讲座：由中央人民政府农业部吕正同志讲《关于春播蔬菜栽培的几个问题。》
    十四日（星期五）（一）学校新闻。（二）青年讲座：由农业大学园艺系汪菊渊教授讲：“种菜应注意的几个问题。”
    十五日（星期六）（一）学校新闻。（二）音乐欣赏。
    十六日（星期日）文化娱乐
    【本报讯】中央人民广播电台今天（十二日）十九点社会科学讲座：艾思奇讲“劳动创造世界”。二十点人民讲坛：西北妇女联合会筹委会副主任、新疆保卫和平民主同盟中央委员、塔塔尔族妇女代表柔曼娜讲“解放前后的新疆妇女”。十八点四十五分自然科学讲座：本台讲座科讲“新麦种的创造”。二十一点四十五分人物故事：“女英雄刘胡兰的故事”。十三点故事讲述：“新儿女英雄传”。十八点曲艺：蔡连贵演唱西河大鼓“石不烂赶车”。二十点三十分文艺节目：中央戏剧学院话剧团广播工作组朗诵文艺作品：（一）十里盐湾（叙事诗）；（二）小芬和小娃（叙事诗）；（三）葛里茨和地主（苏联民间故事）。二十二点文艺节目：连阔如播讲评书“周支队大闹平川”。
    【又讯】北京市人民广播电台七点三十分工人节目：（一）音乐；（二）通讯讲话：丰台机务段金玉堂讲话，介绍该段一等功臣刘玉林。十六点三十分青年节目：学校新闻；（二）播送歌剧《王秀鸾》。十八点三十分市政之声：谈谈糙米的营养价值。二十一点三十分听众服务：（一）回答“怎么接树挪树”的问题；（二）回答“怎样自修绘画”的问题；（三）华北农业机械总厂招考技工的启事；（四）简复刘子华先生。
