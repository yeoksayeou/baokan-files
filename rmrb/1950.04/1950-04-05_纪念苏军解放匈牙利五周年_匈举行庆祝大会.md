Title: Hungary Holds Celebration Rally to Commemorate the Fifth Anniversary of the Soviet Army's Liberation of Hungary
Author:
Date: 1950-04-05
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, April 4th. TASS, Budapest, April 4th: The Council of Ministers of the Hungarian People's Republic held a celebration rally at the State Opera House on April 3rd to commemorate the fifth anniversary of the heroic Soviet Army's liberation of Hungary. The rally was attended by Hungarian politicians, leaders of various political parties, social leaders, the most outstanding representatives of the Hungarian scientific and artistic circles, advanced workers, personnel of the Soviet Embassy in Hungary, personnel from embassies of People's Democratic countries and several other countries. Special seating was provided in the boxes for foreign delegations that had come to Hungary.

The presidium included: Rakosi, General Secretary of the Hungarian Workers' Party; Dobi, Chairman of the Council of Ministers; Szakasits, Chairman of the Presidential Council of the Hungarian People's Republic; other leaders of the Republic; Kiselyov, Soviet Ambassador to Hungary; the Soviet delegation led by Voroshilov, Deputy Chairman of the Soviet Council of Ministers and Marshal of the Soviet Union; and the heads of other foreign delegations participating in the commemoration.

The rally was declared open by Dobi, Chairman of the Council of Ministers, who welcomed the foreign guests in his speech, and extended a particularly warm welcome to the Soviet delegation.

Rakosi, General Secretary of the Hungarian Workers' Party and Deputy Chairman of the Council of Ministers, delivered a report on the commemoration of the fifth anniversary of the liberation of Hungary. His report was repeatedly met with thunderous applause and unanimous cheers from the audience. The entire audience paid tribute to the great Stalin, Marshal Voroshilov, and the Soviet people.

Marshal of the Soviet Union Voroshilov delivered a speech expressing congratulations. His speech was also repeatedly met with unanimous applause and cheers from the audience.

Subsequently, the heads of other foreign delegations delivered speeches, including Lieutenant General Bian Zhangwu, head of the government delegation of the People's Republic of China, who was warmly welcomed by the audience.

A grand musical performance followed the rally.

### 纪念苏军解放匈牙利五周年  匈举行庆祝大会

1950-04-05
第4版()
专栏：

　　纪念苏军解放匈牙利五周年
    匈举行庆祝大会
    【新华社四日讯】塔斯社布达佩斯四日讯：匈牙利人民共和国部长会议于四月三日在国家歌剧院举行庆祝大会，纪念英雄的苏军解放匈牙利第五周年。参加这次大会的，有匈牙利的政治家、各政党领袖、社会领袖、匈牙利科学界和艺术界最杰出的代表人物、先进工人、苏联驻匈牙利大使馆人员、人民民主国家及若干其他国家使馆人员。在包厢中特设有前来匈牙利的外国代表团的席位。
    主席团包括：匈牙利工人党总书记拉科西、部长会议主席道比、匈牙利人民共和国主席团主席斯查卡西兹，共和国其他领袖，苏联驻匈牙利大使基塞列夫，苏联部长会议副主席、苏联元帅伏罗希洛夫所率领的苏联代表团及参加这次纪念的其他外国代表团团长。
    大会由部长会议主席道比宣布开会，他在致词中欢迎外国来宾，并以特别的热忱欢迎苏联代表团。
    匈牙利工人党总书记、部长会议副主席拉科西在会上作了关于纪念匈牙利解放第五周年的报告。他的报告一再博得雷动的掌声和全场一致的欢呼。全场向伟大的斯大林、伏罗希洛夫元帅和苏联人民致敬。
    苏联元帅伏罗希洛夫发表了演说，表示贺忱。他的演说也是屡次博得全场一致的鼓掌和欢呼。
    接着由其他外国代表团团长发表演说，其中有中华人民共和国政府代表团团长边章五中将，他受到会众的热烈欢迎。
    会后接着举行盛大音乐演奏。
