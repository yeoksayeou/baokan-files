Title: American Weapons, Get Out! Representatives from the Belgian League for the Defense of Peace and Other Groups Protest the Unloading of American Weapons in Ostend
Author:
Date: 1950-04-17
Page: 4

　　American Weapons, Get Out!
    Representatives from the Belgian League for the Defense of Peace and Other Groups Protest the Unloading of American Weapons in Ostend
    [Xinhua News Agency, 16th] TASS, Brussels, April 14th: A delegation from the Belgian League for the Defense of Peace, the Belgian Women's League for the Defense of Peace, and the Belgian People's Youth protested to the mayor of Ostend against the possible unloading of American armaments at the port of Ostend. A member of the municipal council who received the delegation said: It is inappropriate to unload armaments in Ostend.

### 美国军火滚回去！比保卫和平联盟等团体代表抗议在俄斯坦得卸美国武器

1950-04-17
第4版()
专栏：

　　美国军火滚回去！
    比保卫和平联盟等团体代表抗议在俄斯坦得卸美国武器
    【新华社十六日讯】塔斯社布鲁塞尔十四日讯：比利时保卫和平联盟、比利时妇女保卫和平联盟和比利时人民青年的代表团，向俄斯坦得市长提出抗议，反对美国军备可能在俄斯坦得港卸运。接见代表团的市政委员会的委员说：在俄斯坦得卸运军备是不合宜的。
