Title: International Commission Makes Conclusion: US Military Illegally Entered South Vietnam
Author:
Date: 1959-08-18
Page: 5

　　International Commission Makes Conclusion
　　US Military Illegally Entered South Vietnam
　　Xinhua News Agency, Hanoi, 17th: According to a Vietnam News Agency report on the 16th: The International Commission for Vietnam, on August 3rd, notified the Liaison Delegation of the General Command of the Vietnam People's Army of its conclusions regarding ten instances of illegal entry and exit of American military officers and military delegations into South Vietnam.
    The International Commission's conclusions point out that these incidents violated Article 16 of the Geneva Accords concerning the cessation of hostilities in Vietnam. This provision prohibits the entry of new military personnel into Vietnam.

### 越国际委员会作出结论　美军违法进入南越

1959-08-18
第5版()
专栏：

　　越国际委员会作出结论
　　美军违法进入南越
　  据新华社河内17日电　据越南通讯社16日报道：越南国际委员会8月3日把它对美国军官和军事代表团非法出入南越的十次事件所作的结论通知了越南人民军总司令部联络代表团。
    国际委员会的结论指出，这些事件违反了日内瓦协议关于在越南停止敌对行动的协定的第十六条规定。这项规定禁止新增的军事人员进入越南。
