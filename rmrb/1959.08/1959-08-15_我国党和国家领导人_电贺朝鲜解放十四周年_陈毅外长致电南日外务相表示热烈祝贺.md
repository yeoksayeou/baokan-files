Title: Chinese Party and State Leaders Send Congratulations on the 14th Anniversary of the Liberation of Korea; Foreign Minister Chen Yi Sends a Telegram to Foreign Minister Nam Il Expressing Warm Congratulations
Author:
Date: 1959-08-15
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, August 14th

　　Chinese Party and State Leaders
　　Send Congratulations on the 14th Anniversary of the Liberation of Korea
　　Foreign Minister Chen Yi Sends a Telegram to Foreign Minister Nam Il Expressing Warm Congratulations
    Xinhua News Agency, August 14th - The leaders of the Chinese Party and State today sent a telegram to the leaders of the Workers' Party of Korea and the Democratic People's Republic of Korea, congratulating them on the 14th anniversary of the liberation of Korea. The telegram reads as follows:
    To Comrade Kim Il Sung, Chairman of the Central Committee of the Workers' Party of Korean and Premier of the Cabinet of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea;
    To Comrade Choi Yong-kun, Chairman of the Presidium of the Supreme People's Assembly, Democratic People's Republic of Korea:
    On the occasion of the 14th anniversary the liberation of Korea, we, on behalf of the Communist Party of China, the Government of the People' Republic of China, and the Chinese People, extend our warm congratulations to the Workers' Party of Korea, the Government of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea, and the Korean People.
    The people of Korea, under the correct leadership of the Workers' Party of Korea and the Government of the Democratic Republic of Korea, headed by Comrade Kim Il Sung, have been working selflessly and displaying infinite creativity, completing the industrial gross output target of the First Five-Year Plan victoriously and two and a half years ahead of schedule, at the rapid pace of Chollima. The Chinese people feel infinitely delighted and encouraged by the brilliant achievements of the brotherly Korean people.
    The Government and people of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea have consistently pursued a peaceful foreign policy, making unremitting efforts to ease international tensions in the Far East and safeguard world peace. The Korean people's widespread just struggle to demand the withdrawal of American invading troops from South Korea and the peaceful reunification of their motherland has received universal support from peace-loving people all over the world. The Chinese people firmly support the just struggle of the brotherly Korean people and firmly believe that American imperialism will certainly be driven out, and that the cause of the peaceful reunification of the Korean people will surely be victorious.
    Over the past year, the unbreakable fraternal friendship and close cooperation between the peoples of China and Korea have been further consolidated and developed in the cause of seeking common economic and cultural prosperity and the happiness of the people of both countries. This is of great importance for strengthening the unity of the socialist camp headed by the Soviet Union and safeguarding peace in Asia and the world.
    We wish the Korean people even greater achievements in socialist construction and in the struggle to achieve the reunification of their motherland.
    May the close friendship between the brotherly peoples of China and Korea last forever.

        Mao Zedong, Chairman of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of China
	Liu Shaoqi, Chairman of the People's Republic of China
        Zhu

### 我国党和国家领导人　电贺朝鲜解放十四周年　陈毅外长致电南日外务相表示热烈祝贺

1959-08-15
第1版()
专栏：

　　我国党和国家领导人
　　电贺朝鲜解放十四周年
　　陈毅外长致电南日外务相表示热烈祝贺
    新华社14日讯  中华人民共和国党和国家领导人今天致电朝鲜民主主义人民共和国党和国家领导人，祝贺朝鲜解放十四周年。电文如下：朝鲜劳动党中央委员会委员长、朝鲜民主主义人民共和国内阁首相金日成
  同志：朝鲜民主主义人民共和国最高人民会议常任委员会委员长崔庸健同志：
    在朝鲜解放十四周年的时候，我们谨代表中国共产党、中华人民共和国政府和中国人民向朝鲜劳动党、朝鲜民主主义人民共和国政府和朝鲜人民表示热烈的祝贺。
    朝鲜人民在以金日成同志为首的朝鲜劳动党和朝鲜民主主义人民共和国政府的正确领导下，进行了忘我的劳动，发挥了无穷的创造性，以千里马的快速步伐，胜利地提前两年半完成了第一个五年计划的工业总产值计划。中国人民对兄弟的朝鲜人民取得的辉煌成就，感到无限的欢欣和鼓舞。
    朝鲜民主主义人民共和国政府和人民一贯执行和平外交政策，为和缓远东国际紧张局势、维护世界和平作了坚持不懈的努力。朝鲜人民广泛展开的要求美国侵略军队撤出南朝鲜和实现祖国和平统一的正义斗争，得到全世界爱好和平人民的普遍支持。中国人民坚决支持兄弟的朝鲜人民的正义斗争并坚信美帝国主义一定会被赶走，朝鲜人民和平统一祖国的事业一定胜利。
    一年来，中朝两国人民牢不可破的兄弟友谊和亲密合作，在谋求两国经济文化的共同繁荣和人民幸福的事业中更加巩固和发展了。这对加强以苏联为首的社会主义阵营的团结和维护亚洲与世界和平的事业具有重大的作用。
    祝朝鲜人民在社会主义建设和争取自己祖国统一的斗争中取得更大的成就。
    祝中朝两国兄弟人民的亲密友谊万古长青。
        中国共产党中央委员会主席　毛泽东
        中华人民共和国主席刘少奇
        中华人民共和国全国人民代表大会常务委员会委员长朱德
        中华人民共和国国务院总理周恩来
                                        1959年8月14日
    新华社14日讯　中华人民共和国国务院副总理兼外交部长陈毅在8月14日致电朝鲜内阁副首相兼外务相南日，祝贺朝鲜解放十四周年。电文如下：朝鲜民主主义人民共和国内阁副首相兼外务相南日同志：
    值此朝鲜解放十四周年之际，谨向你致以热烈的祝贺。祝你在执行朝鲜民主主义人民共和国政府的和平外交政策和保卫亚洲与世界和平的事业中取得新的成就。祝中朝两国人民的兄弟友谊日益发展。
                  中华人民共和国国务院副总理兼外交部长陈毅
                                        1959年8月14日
