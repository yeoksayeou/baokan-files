Title: Thirty-Four Countries' Youth Representatives Invited to Visit the Soviet Union; Moscow Youth Warmly Welcome Guests

Author:

Date: 1959-08-09
Page: 3

Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, August 8th: Youth representatives from thirty-four countries, who participated in the Seventh World Youth and Student Festival, were invited to visit Moscow on the 7th. That evening, Moscow youth held a warm meeting with the guests in Gorky Central Culture and Rest Park.

These youth representatives from thirty-four countries were invited by Soviet youth to travel and visit the Soviet Union. They will visit many large cities such as Kiev, Leningrad, Kharkov, Baku, and Riga.

Fifteen thousand young people from all walks of life in Moscow warmly welcomed the guests at the meeting and expressed their heartfelt congratulations on the great achievements they had made at the festival.

Moscow youth worker Yevseyenko said at the meeting, "Although we did not go to Vienna, our hearts are with you."

Ali Bokar, a youth representative from West Africa, said, "The people of Africa have been waging a heroic struggle against the colonialists. Although the patriots there are suffering various tortures, no force can prevent the African people from moving towards freedom and national independence."

The guests and hosts held cordial conversations, and the meeting ended in song.

### 三十四国青年代表应邀访苏  莫斯科青年热情会佳宾

1959-08-09
第3版()
专栏：

    三十四国青年代表应邀访苏
    莫斯科青年热情会佳宾
    据新华社8日讯　据塔斯社莫斯科消息：参加第七届世界青年与学生联欢节的三十四个国家的青年代表，7日应邀到莫斯科访问。当晚，莫斯科青年在高尔基中央文化休息公园与客人们举行了热情的会见。
    这三十四国的青年代表是应苏联青年的邀请，到苏联作旅行访问的，他们将到基辅、列宁格勒、哈尔科夫、巴库、里加等很多大城市进行参观。
    一万五千名莫斯科的各界青年在会见会上向客人们表示了热情的欢迎，并且对他们在联欢节中取得的巨大成就表示了衷心的祝贺。
    莫斯科青年工人叶夫谢因科在会见时说，我们虽然没有去维也纳，但是我们的心是和你们在一起的。
    来自西非洲的青年代表阿里·博卡尔说，非洲的人民一直在同殖民主义者进行英勇的斗争，虽然，那里的爱国者经受着各种折磨，但是没有任何力量能阻止非洲人民走向自由和民族独立。
    宾主们进行了亲切的交谈，最后会见在歌声中结束。
