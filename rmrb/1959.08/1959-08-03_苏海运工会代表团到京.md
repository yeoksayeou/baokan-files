Title: Soviet Maritime Transport Union Delegation Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1959-08-03
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, August 1st. At the invitation of the National Committee of the Chinese Seamen's Union, the four-member delegation of the Soviet River Transport Workers' Union, led by But, a member of the Presidium of the Central Committee of the Soviet River Transport Workers' Union, arrived in Beijing by plane today.

Liu Dachao, Chairman of the National Committee of the Chinese Seamen's Union, welcomed the guests at the airport.

### 苏海运工会代表团到京

1959-08-03
第4版()
专栏：

    苏海运工会代表团到京
    新华社1日讯　应中国海员工会全国委员会邀请来中国访问的苏联海河运输工会代表团一行四人，在团长、苏联海河运输工会中央委员会主席团委员布特率领下，今天乘飞机到达北京。
    中国海员工会全国委员会主席刘达潮到机场欢迎客人。
