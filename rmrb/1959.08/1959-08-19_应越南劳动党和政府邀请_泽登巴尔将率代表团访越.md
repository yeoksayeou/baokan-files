Title:  Zedenbal to Lead Delegation to Visit Vietnam at the Invitation of the Vietnamese Labor Party and Government
Author: 
Date: 1959-08-19
Page: 4

　　Xinhua News Agency, Ulaanbaatar, August 18th: It was announced here today that a Mongolian Party and government delegation headed by Zedenbal, First Secretary of the Central Committee of the Mongolian People's Revolutionary Party and Chairman of the Council of Ministers, will pay a friendly visit to the Democratic Republic of Vietnam in the near future at the invitation of the Central Committee of the Vietnamese Labor Party and the Government of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam.

### 应越南劳动党和政府邀请　泽登巴尔将率代表团访越

1959-08-19
第4版()
专栏：

　　应越南劳动党和政府邀请
　　泽登巴尔将率代表团访越
    新华社乌兰巴托18日电　今天这里宣布，以蒙古人民革命党中央委员会第一书记、部长会议主席泽登巴尔为首的蒙古党政代表团，应越南劳动党中央委员会和越南民主共和国政府的邀请，将在最近前往越南民主共和国进行友好访问。
