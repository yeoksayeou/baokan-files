Title: Iraqi Teachers Delegation Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1959-08-16
Page: 3

    Iraqi Teachers Delegation Arrives in Beijing
    (Xinhua News Agency, August 15th) The Iraqi teachers delegation, consisting of ten members and led by the head of the delegation Mamdouh Hussein Alousi, arrived in Beijing by plane this afternoon.

### 伊拉克教师代表团到京

1959-08-16
第3版()
专栏：

    伊拉克教师代表团到京
    据新华社15日讯　伊拉克教师代表团一行十人，在团长马姆杜哈·侯赛因·阿卢西率领下，今天下午乘飞机到达北京。
