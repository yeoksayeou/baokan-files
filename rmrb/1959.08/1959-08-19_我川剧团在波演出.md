Title: Sichuan Opera Troupe Performs in Poland
Author:
Date: 1959-08-19
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency, Warsaw: The Chinese Sichuan Opera Troupe gave performances in Warsaw on the 15th and 17th. Present at the performances were Ochab, member of the Political Bureau of the Central Committee of the Polish United Workers' Party; Indryhovsky, member of the Political Bureau of the Central Committee of the Polish United Worker’s Party and Chairman of the Polish-Chinese Friendship Association; Zambrowski, member of the Political Bureau of the Central Committee of the Polish United Workers' Party; Podejdeworny, Vice-Chairman of the Polish State Council; Lusinek, Acting Minister of Culture and Art; and many well-known figures from cultural and artistic circles. The Sichuan Opera actors' performances were warmly welcomed by the audience.
　　On the morning of the 17th, Lusinek, Acting Minister of Culture and Art of Poland, received the leaders and main actors of the Sichuan Opera Troupe.
　　On the 18th, the Sichuan Opera Troupe left Warsaw to continue its tour performances in various parts of Poland.

### 我川剧团在波演出

1959-08-19
第4版()
专栏：

　　我川剧团在波演出
    据新华社华沙电　中国川剧团15日和17日在华沙举行访问演出。波兰统一工人党中央政治局委员奥哈布，统一工人党中央政治局委员、波中友协主席英德里霍夫斯基，统一工人党中央政治局委员萨姆布罗夫斯基，波兰国务委员会副主席波戴德沃尔尼，文化艺术部代理部长鲁西内克和文化艺术界知名人士等多人观看了演出。中国川剧演员们的演技受到观众们的热烈欢迎。
    17日上午，波文化艺术部代理部长鲁西内克接见了我川剧团领导人和主要演员。
    18日，川剧团离开华沙赴波兰各地继续访问演出。
