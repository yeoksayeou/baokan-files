Title: Democratic Republic of Vietnam Issues National Day Slogans, Calling on the Entire People to Unite and Achieve Peaceful Reunification of the Motherland
Author:
Date: 1959-08-15
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency, Hanoi, August 14th. The Democratic Republic of Vietnam today issued slogans celebrating the 14th anniversary of the "August 19th" August Revolution Day and the "September 2nd" National Day.
　　The slogans called on all Vietnamese people to unite and strive to build a peaceful, unified, independent, democratic, and prosperous Vietnam.
　　The slogans called on all Vietnamese people to further oppose the US-Ngo Dinh Diem clique's policies of persecution and massacre in South Vietnam, demand that US imperialism get out of South Vietnam, and demand the peaceful reunification of the motherland.
　　The slogans called on the people of all strata in North Vietnam to strive to complete the socialist construction and socialist transformation in North Vietnam.
　　The slogans called for: "Further consolidate national defense and always be vigilant against the provocative conspiracies of US imperialism and its running dogs in South Vietnam and Laos."
　　The slogans stated: "Resolutely oppose US imperialism's instigation of the (Laotian) Phoui Sananikone government to create tension in Laos, threatening peace in Indochina and Southeast Asia."
　　The slogans demanded that "the Phoui Sananikone government must respect the Geneva Accords, immediately stop the civil war in Laos, and restore the freedom of the leaders of the Lao Patriotic Front."
　　The slogans demanded that "the International Control Commission in Laos quickly resume its activities to ensure that the Geneva Accords concerning Laos are strictly implemented and that the flames of war in Laos are immediately extinguished."
　　The slogans warmly supported the Soviet Union's proposal to convene a summit conference.
　　The slogans expressed warm congratulations to the Soviet Union, China, and other socialist brotherly countries for their great achievements in the cause of building socialism and communism and in the cause of defending world peace. And expressed heartfelt gratitude for their support for Vietnam's socialist construction and the cause of national reunification.

### 越南民主共和国发表庆祝国庆节口号　号召全民团结实现祖国和平统一

1959-08-15
第4版()
专栏：

　　越南民主共和国发表庆祝国庆节口号
　　号召全民团结实现祖国和平统一
    新华社河内14日电　越南民主共和国今天发表庆祝“八一九”八月革命节和“九二”国庆节十四周年口号。
    口号号召越南全体人民团结起来，为建设一个和平、统一、独立、民主和富强的越南而奋斗。
    口号号召全体越南人民进一步反对美国—吴庭艳集团在南越的迫害屠杀政策，要求美帝国主义滚出南越，要求和平统一祖国。
    口号号召越南北方各阶层人民努力完成越南北方的社会主义建设和社会主义改造事业。
    口号号召：“进一步巩固国防，时刻警惕美帝国主义和它在南越和老挝的走狗的挑衅阴谋。”
    口号提出：“坚决反对美帝国主义唆使
  （老挝）培·萨纳尼空政府制造老挝紧张局势，威胁印度支那和东南亚的和平。”
    口号要求“培·萨纳尼空政府必须尊重日内瓦协议，立即停止老挝内战，恢复老挝爱国战线党领导人的自由”。
    口号要求“老挝国际委员会迅速恢复活动，以保证有关老挝的日内瓦协议得到严正履行，立即扑灭老挝的战火。”
    口号热烈支持苏联关于召开最高级会议的建议。
    口号表示热烈祝贺苏联、中国和各社会主义兄弟国家在建设社会主义和共产主义事业和在保卫世界和平事业中的伟大成就。并且对它们对越南社会主义建设和统一国家的事业的支援表示衷心的感谢。
