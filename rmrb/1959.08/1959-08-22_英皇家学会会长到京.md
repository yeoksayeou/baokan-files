Title: President of the British Royal Society Arrives in Beijing
Author: 
Date: 1959-08-22
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, 21st - Sir Cyril Norman Hinshelwood, President of the British Royal Society, arrived in Beijing by plane this afternoon for a visit and lectures in China. This British physical chemist is visiting China at the invitation of Guo Moruo, President of the Chinese Academy of Sciences. He was welcomed at the airport by Wu Youxun, Vice President of the Chinese Academy of Sciences, and Hou Debang, Chairman of the Chinese Chemical Society, and others.

### 英皇家学会会长到京

1959-08-22
第4版()
专栏：

    英皇家学会会长到京
    新华社21日讯　英国皇家学会会长欣歇耳伍德爵士今天下午乘飞机到达北京，来中国进行访问和讲学。这位英国物理化学家是应中国科学院院长郭沫若的邀请来中国访问的。他在机场受到中国科学院副院长吴有训，中国化学化工学会理事长侯德榜等人的欢迎。
