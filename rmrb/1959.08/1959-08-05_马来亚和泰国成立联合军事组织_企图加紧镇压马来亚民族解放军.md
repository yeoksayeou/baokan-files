Title: Malaya and Thailand Establish Joint Military Organization in Attempt to Intensify Suppression of the Malayan National Liberation Army
Author:
Date: 1959-08-05
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, 3rd, Kuala Lumpur News: Malaya and Thailand announced the establishment of two joint committees in an attempt to intensify the suppression of the Malayan National Liberation Army.

It is reported that a Thai delegation composed of three army officers and three police officers arrived in Kuala Lumpur on July 25 and held a series of talks with the Malayan authorities on the joint suppression of the Malayan National Liberation Army. During the talks, both sides planned countermeasures against the Malayan National Liberation Army, such as blockading the border.

The joint communique issued after the talks announced the establishment of these two joint committees. The first committee, called the "Senior Staff Committee," is composed of senior staff officers from Thailand and Malaya, and it will plan combat operations on the border; the second committee, called the "Border Operations Committee," is tasked with implementing the plans of the first committee.

The communique also declared that "agreement has been reached on various other aspects of cooperation in the border area," and that the delegations from both sides will report the results of the talks to their respective governments.

### 马来亚和泰国成立联合军事组织  企图加紧镇压马来亚民族解放军

1959-08-05
第4版()
专栏：

    马来亚和泰国成立联合军事组织
    企图加紧镇压马来亚民族解放军
    新华社3日讯  吉隆坡消息：马来亚和泰国宣布成立两个联合委员会，企图加紧镇压马来亚民族解放军。
    据报道，由陆军三名军官和三名警官组成的泰国代表团在7月25日到达吉隆坡以后，同马来亚当局就联合镇压马来亚民族解放军一事举行了一系列的会谈。双方在会谈中策划了封锁边境等对付马来亚民族解放军的对策。
    双方在会谈以后发表的联合公报宣布成立这两个联合委员会。第一个委员会称为“高级参谋委员会”，由泰国和马来亚的高级参谋军官组成，它将计划边境的战斗行动；第二个委员会称为“边境作战委员会”，它的任务是执行第一个委员会的计划。
    公报还宣称，“在边境地区合作的其他各个方面”也已经达成了协议，双方代表团将分别向本国政府报告会谈的结果。
