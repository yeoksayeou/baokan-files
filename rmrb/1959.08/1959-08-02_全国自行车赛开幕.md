Title: National Bicycle Race Opens
Author:
Date: 1959-08-02
Page: 2

    National Bicycle Race Opens
    (According to Beijing Evening News) The first National Track Cycling Championships are scheduled to open at 8:30 a.m. on the 2nd at the newly completed velodrome by Longtan Lake in Beijing. Sports enthusiasts in Beijing will be able to witness the spectacle of outstanding cyclists chasing each other on the banked, bowl-shaped track for the first time.
    Fifty-two male and female athletes from nine units, including Beijing, Yunnan, the People's Liberation Army, Guangdong, Shanxi, Heilongjiang, Shandong, Fujian, and Jiangxi, are participating in this competition.

### 全国自行车赛开幕

1959-08-02
第2版()
专栏：

    全国自行车赛开幕
    据北京晚报讯　第一届全国赛车场自行车锦标赛定2日上午8时半在北京龙潭湖畔新落成的赛车场上举行。北京的体育爱好者们将是第一次看到优秀自行车选手们在盆形的斜坡跑道上互相追逐的景况。
    参加这次比赛的有北京、云南、解放军、广东、山西、黑龙江、山东、福建、江西等九个单位的五十二名男女运动员。
