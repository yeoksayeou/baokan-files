Title: Chairman Ho Chi Minh Visits Beijing

Author:
Date: 1959-08-20
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, August 19th: Chairman Ho Chi Minh visited the newly completed Chinese People's Revolutionary Military Museum in Beijing this afternoon, and was warmly welcomed by the builders of the museum. After arriving in Beijing on the 13th, Chairman Ho Chi Minh also visited the Ming Tombs Reservoir, toured the Summer Palace and the Temple of Heaven, and received Vietnamese students studying in Beijing.

### 胡志明主席在京参观游览

1959-08-20
第1版()
专栏：

　　胡志明主席在京参观游览
    新华社19日讯　胡志明主席今天下午在北京参观了新建成的中国人民革命军事博物馆，受到了博物馆的建设者们的热烈欢迎。胡志明主席在13日到达北京以后，还参观了十三陵水库，游览了颐和园和天坛，并且接见了在北京学习的越南留学生。
