Title: Ceylon Teachers' Union Delegation Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1959-08-16
Page: 3

Xinhua News Agency, August 15th. At the invitation of the National Committee of the Chinese Educational Workers' Union, the four-member delegation of the Ceylon Teachers' Union, headed by Chairman Ranayake, arrived in Beijing by plane this afternoon.

### 锡教师工会代表团到京

1959-08-16
第3版()
专栏：

    锡教师工会代表团到京
    新华社15日讯　应中国教育工会全国委员会邀请来我国访问的锡兰教师工会代表团一行四人，在团长拉纳雅克率领下，今天下午乘飞机到达北京。
