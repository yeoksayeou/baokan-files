Title: Prominent Latin American Politicians Gather in Santiago to Strengthen National Democratic Movement and Unite in Struggle; Youth Representatives from Seven Countries Gather to Support Cuba and Venezuela Against U.S. Intervention
Author:
Date: 1959-08-18
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, Santiago, August 15th. According to a report in today's "El Siglo," prominent politicians from some Latin American countries held a gathering in Santiago to discuss the issue of strengthening the national democratic movement in Latin America. All speakers unanimously agreed that it is necessary to unite in the liberation struggle of Latin America.

Those who attended the meeting included Cuban Minister of Education Armando Hart; Carlos Rafael Rodríguez, director of the Cuban People's Socialist Party's newspaper "Hoy"; Rodolfo Ghioldi, Chairman of the Executive Committee of the Argentine Communist Party; as well as political leaders and prominent figures from Venezuela, Bolivia, Paraguay, the Dominican Republic, and Nicaragua.

Representing the Chilean People's Action Front were Chilean Socialist Party leaders Salvador Allende and Salomón Corbalán; Communist Party leaders Luis Corvalán and Orlando Millas; Mamelto Figueroa of the National Workers' Union; representatives of various parties participating in the People's Action Front; and senators and members of parliament.

Argentine Communist Party leader Rodolfo Ghioldi told "El Siglo" reporters that the Meeting of American Foreign Ministers "is a meeting of the United States against Cuba. The imperialists' talk of representative democratic government is intended to confuse the current situation in Latin America. The meeting will seek to intervene in Cuba, oppose revolutionary armed coups, and arbitrarily undermine the Cuban people's right to self-determination." He said, "The Argentine people fully support the Cuban revolution and believe that the revolution led by Castro fully represents their will. A strong movement in support of the Cuban revolution has emerged in Argentina. This was demonstrated when Castro visited our country. The Americas have awakened to oppose imperialist aggression, and standing with Cuba are the majority of democratic people in our Americas."

Xinhua News Agency, Santiago, August 16th. Youth representatives from seven Latin American countries gathered in Santiago to support Cuba and Venezuela and oppose U.S. intervention. According to a report in "El Siglo," the meeting issued a statement on the Meeting of American Foreign Ministers, condemning the U.S. State Department's conspiracy to use the Organization of American States to interfere in the Cuban revolution. The statement said: "We oppose the establishment of a special committee to investigate the situation in the Caribbean. Such a committee will become a cover for intervention in Cuba, while at the same time giving support to the dictatorships of Trujillo, Somoza, Stroessner, and Duvalier."

Participating in the gathering were youth representatives from Chile, Argentina, Cuba, Venezuela, the Dominican Republic, Nicaragua, and Paraguay.

### 拉丁美洲国家著名政界人士在圣地亚哥集会　加强民族民主运动　在斗争中联合起来　七国青年代表集会支持古巴和委内瑞拉反对美国干涉

1959-08-18
第5版()
专栏：

　　拉丁美洲国家著名政界人士在圣地亚哥集会
　　加强民族民主运动　在斗争中联合起来
　　七国青年代表集会支持古巴和委内瑞拉反对美国干涉
    新华社圣地亚哥15日电　据“世纪报”今天报道，拉丁美洲一些国家的著名政界人士在圣地亚哥举行了一次集会，讨论加强拉丁美洲的民族民主运动的问题。所有发言的人一致认为，必须在拉丁美洲的解放斗争中联合起来。
    参加这次会议的有古巴教育部长阿曼多·阿特，古巴人民社会党机关报“今日报”社长卡洛斯·拉斐尔·罗德里格斯，阿根廷共产党执行委员会主席罗道夫·基奥迪；以及委内瑞拉、玻利维亚、巴拉圭、多米尼加和尼加拉瓜的政界领袖和知名人士。
    代表智利人民行动阵线出席的有智利社会党领袖萨尔瓦多·阿伦德，萨洛蒙·科尔巴兰；共产党领袖路易斯·科尔巴兰和奥尔兰多·米利亚斯；全国工人联盟的马梅尔托·菲格罗亚，参加人民行动阵线的各党派代表和参议员与众议员。
    阿根廷共产党领导人罗道夫·基奥迪对“世纪报”记者发表谈话说，美洲外长会议“是美国反对古巴的会议。帝国主义谈代议制民主政府的目的是为了混淆拉丁美洲当前的局势。会议所追求的将是干涉古巴、反对革命的武装政变和任意破坏古巴人民的自决权”。他说，“阿根廷人民完全支持古巴的革命，并认为卡斯特罗所领导的革命完全代表了他们的意志。在阿根廷涌现了支持古巴革命的强大运动。当卡斯特罗访问我国的时候，这一点已经得到证明。美洲已经觉悟到要反对帝国主义侵略，站在古巴这方面的是我们美洲大多数的民主的人民”。
    新华社圣地亚哥16日电　拉丁美洲七国青年代表在圣地亚哥集会，支持古巴和委内瑞拉、反对美国干涉。据“世纪报”报道，会议就美洲外长会议发表声明，谴责美国国务院利用美洲国家组织干涉古巴革命的阴谋。声明说：“我们反对建立特别委员会来调查加勒比海局势。这种委员会将成为干涉古巴的幌子，而同时却给特鲁希略、索摩查、斯特罗斯纳和杜瓦利埃独裁政权以支持。”
    参加这次集会的有智利、阿根廷、古巴、委内瑞拉、多米尼加、尼加拉瓜和巴拉圭的青年代表。
