Title: Gaganova Receives Lofty Award
Author:
Date: 1959-08-01
Page: 5

    Gaganova Receives Lofty Award
    (Our report) On July 31, Kliment Voroshilov, Chairman of the Presidium of the Supreme Soviet of the Soviet Union, awarded the title of "Hero of Socialist Labor," the "Order of Lenin," and the "Hammer and Sickle" gold medal to Valya Gaganova, in recognition of her exemplary deeds in helping the backward catch up with the advanced.
    (Below is a photo of Voroshilov awarding Gaganova the medal and order. TASS fax photo, Xinhua News Agency draft) (Attached image)

### 加干诺娃接受崇高奖赏

1959-08-01
第5版()
专栏：

    加干诺娃接受崇高奖赏
    本报讯　7月31日，苏联最高苏维埃主席团主席伏罗希洛夫把“社会主义劳动英雄”称号、“列宁勋章”和“镰刀和锤子”金质奖章，授予瓦利亚·加干诺娃，以表扬她帮助落后赶先进的模范事迹。
    （下图是伏罗希洛夫授给加干诺娃勋章和奖章。塔斯社传真照片，新华社稿）（附图片）
