Title: Yanbian Folk Song
Author:
Date: 1959-08-02
Page: 5

　　Gully streams moisten fertile fields,
　　Rice paddies like a sea, waves of rice surge,
　　Green pines and cypresses cover the mountains,
　　Abundant fruits hang from the branches;
　　Chickens and ducks in flocks, pigs fill the pens,
　　Specks of yellow cattle cover the hills,
　　Mountain products and sideline occupations busy all year round,
　　Mineral resources hidden underground.
　　Thinking of the past,
　　Tears well up;
　　Thinking of the present,
　　Full of joy;
　　Thinking of the future,
　　Spirits are high.
　　Good days thanks to the Communist Party.

### 延边民歌

1959-08-02
第5版()
专栏：

    延边民歌
    沟沟溪水润肥田，
    稻田似海稻浪翻，
    苍山翠柏满山峦，
    丰硕果实垂枝端；
    鸡鸭成群猪满圈，
    点点黄牛遍山岗，
    山产副业四季忙，
    矿产资源地下藏。
    想起过去，
    眼泪汪汪；
    想起现在，
    喜气洋洋；
    想起未来，
    精神气爽。
    好日子多亏共产党。
