Title: Five Romanian Musicians Arrive in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1959-08-15
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency, August 14th—Five Romanian solo performers arrived in Beijing by plane this afternoon. They are violinist Mihai Constantin Niescu, soprano soloist Selma Haulingher, Meritorious Artist and soprano soloist Iolanda Mărgulescu, tenor soloist Titus Măraru, and piano accompanist Barnig Mariana.
　　They are visiting China according to the 1959 executive plan of the Sino-Romanian cultural cooperation agreement.

### 罗五位音乐家到京

1959-08-15
第4版()
专栏：

　　罗五位音乐家到京
    新华社14日讯　罗马尼亚的五位单独表演家今天下午乘飞机到达北京。他们是小提琴家米哈依·康斯坦丁·奈斯库，女高音独唱家谢尔玛·豪林盖尔，功勋艺术家、女高音独唱家约朗达·默尔古列斯特，男高音独唱家狄图斯·毛拉鲁和钢琴伴奏巴尔尼格·玛丽安娜。
    他们是根据中罗文化合作协定1959年执行计划来中国访问的。
