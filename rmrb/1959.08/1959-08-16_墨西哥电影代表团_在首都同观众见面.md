Title: Mexican Film Delegation Meets Audience in the Capital
Author: 
Date: 1959-08-16
Page: 3
Xinhua News Agency, August 15th – Today is the first day of the Mexican Film Week. Starting from 8:00 am, the Mexican feature films "Brute Force," "Rio Escondido," and "Los Olvidados" are being screened simultaneously in nine major cinemas in the capital.

Alejandro Galindo, acting head of the Mexican film delegation currently visiting the capital, along with delegation members Edmundo Báez and actress María Cruz Olivier, met with audiences at the Capital Cinema, the Beijing Exhibition Center Cinema, and were warmly welcomed by the audience.

### 墨西哥电影代表团  在首都同观众见面

1959-08-16
第3版()
专栏：

    墨西哥电影代表团
    在首都同观众见面
    新华社15日讯　今天是墨西哥电影周第一天，从上午八时开始在首都九家大电影院同时上映墨西哥故事片“偷渡的苦工”、“躲藏的激流”、“被遗弃的人”。
    正在首都访问的墨西哥电影代表团代理团长亚历杭德罗·加林多，团员埃德孟多·拜斯和女演员玛丽·克鲁斯·奥利维，先后在首都电影院、北京展览馆电影馆和观众见面，受到观众的热烈欢迎。
