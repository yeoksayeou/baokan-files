Title: Chairman Ho Chi Minh Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1959-08-14
Page: 1

　　Xinhua News Agency, 13th. After vacationing in the Soviet Union and visiting the Northwest region of our country on his way back home, Chairman Ho Chi Minh arrived in Beijing by special plane at noon today. Those who welcomed him at the airport included Vice Premier of the State Council Chen Yi, Alternate Secretary of the Secretariat of the CPC Central Committee Liu Lantao, Vice Minister of Foreign Affairs Ji Pengfei, and Chinese Ambassador to Vietnam He Wei. Phạm Bình, Chargé d'Affaires ad interim of the Vietnamese Embassy in China, also came to the airport to welcome him.

### 胡志明主席到达北京

1959-08-14
第1版()
专栏：

　　胡志明主席到达北京
　　新华社13日讯　胡志明主席在苏联休假后回国途中参观了我国西北地区以后，今天中午乘专机到达北京。到机场欢迎的，有国务院副总理陈毅，中共中央书记处候补书记刘澜涛，外交部副部长姬鹏飞，中国驻越南大使何伟。越南驻中国大使馆临时代办范平也到机场欢迎。
