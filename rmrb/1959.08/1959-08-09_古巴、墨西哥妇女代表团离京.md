Title: Cuban and Mexican Women's Delegations Depart Beijing
Author:
Date: 1959-08-09
Page: 3

Xinhua News Agency, August 8th: The Cuban and Mexican women's delegations concluded their visit to China and left Beijing by plane this morning to visit the Democratic People's Republic of Korea and the Soviet Union, respectively. The head of the Mexican women's delegation, Marianela Mondragón Núñez, and the Cuban woman delegate, Yolanda Catomeli Chacón, had already returned home.

These two women's delegations were invited to visit China by the All-China Women's Federation of the People's Republic of China. They visited Beijing, Shenyang, Tianjin, Anshan, Shanghai, Hangzhou and other places.

### 古巴、墨西哥妇女代表团离京

1959-08-09
第3版()
专栏：

    古巴、墨西哥妇女代表团离京
    新华社8日讯　古巴妇女代表团和墨西哥妇女代表团结束了在中国的访问，今天上午乘飞机离开北京，分别去朝鲜民主主义人民共和国和苏联访问。墨西哥妇女代表团团长玛里亚内拉·蒙德拉贡·努涅斯和古巴妇女代表约兰达·卡托梅利·查空已经先行回国。
    这两个妇女代表团是应中华人民共和国全国妇女联合会的邀请来中国访问的。她们曾访问了北京、沈阳、天津、鞍山、上海、杭州等地。
