Title: Korean Ambassador to China Holds Film Reception to Celebrate the 14th Anniversary of the Liberation of Korea
Author:
Date: 1959-08-14
Page: 4

　　Celebrating the 14th Anniversary of the Liberation of Korea
　　Korean Ambassador to China Holds Film Reception
    Xinhua News Agency, 13th - The Ambassador of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea to China, Li Yong-ho, held a grand film reception tonight to celebrate the 14th anniversary of the liberation of Korea.
    Li Dequan, President of the Sino-Korean Friendship Association, Ji Pengfei, Vice Minister of Foreign Affairs, Qu Wu, Deputy Director of the Commission for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries, and other responsible persons were invited to attend the reception. More than 2,000 people, including workers, peasants, students, People's Liberation Army soldiers, and state organ cadres from the capital, also attended the reception.
    Korean musical art films such as "Kumgangsan Girl" were screened at the reception.

### 庆祝朝鲜解放十四周年　朝鲜驻华大使举行电影招待会

1959-08-14
第4版()
专栏：

　　庆祝朝鲜解放十四周年
　　朝鲜驻华大使举行电影招待会
    新华社13日电　朝鲜民主主义人民共和国驻华大使李永镐今晚举行盛大电影招待会，庆祝朝鲜解放十四周年。
    中国朝鲜友好协会会长李德全，外交部副部长姬鹏飞，对外文化联络委员会副主任屈武等负责人应邀出席了招待会。出席招待会的还有首都工人、农民、学生、人民解放军战士和国家机关干部等二千多人。
    招待会上放映了朝鲜音乐艺术片“金刚山姑娘”等影片。
