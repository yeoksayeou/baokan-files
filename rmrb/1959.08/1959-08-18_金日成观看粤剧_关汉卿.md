Title: Kim Il-sung Watches Cantonese Opera "Guan Hanqing"
Author:
Date: 1959-08-18
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency, Pyongyang, August 17th. Premier of the Cabinet of North Korea, Kim Il-sung, watched the Cantonese opera "Guan Hanqing" performed by the Chinese Guangdong Cantonese Opera Troupe, which is currently visiting North Korea, tonight.
    Also attending the performance at the Pyongyang National Art Theater were North Korean Party and government leaders Choi Yong-kun, Hong Myung-hee, Park Jung-ae, Park Kim-chul, Kim Chang-man, Chairman of the Korea-China Friendship Association Ha Ang-cheon, Chargé d'Affaires ad interim of the Chinese Embassy in North Korea Cao Keqiang, and diplomatic envoys from various countries to North Korea.
    After the performance, Premier Kim Il-sung and others went on stage to meet the actors and actresses, and received the President of the Cantonese Opera Troupe Ma Shizeng, Vice President Hong Xiannu, and other leading actors and actresses.

### 金日成观看粤剧“关汉卿”

1959-08-18
第4版()
专栏：

　　金日成观看粤剧“关汉卿”
    新华社平壤17日电　朝鲜内阁首相金日成今天晚上观看了正在朝鲜访问的中国广东粤剧院演出的粤剧“关汉卿”。
    到平壤市国立艺术剧场观看演出的还有朝鲜党、政领导人崔庸健、洪命熹、朴正爱、朴金哲、金昌满，朝中友好协会委员长河仰天，中国驻朝鲜大使馆临时代办曹克强，各国驻朝鲜外交使节等。
    在演出后，金日成首相等还登台同演员们相见，并且接见了粤剧院院长马师曾、副院长红线女以及其他主要演员。
