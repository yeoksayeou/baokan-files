Title: Celebrating the Fifteenth Anniversary of Romania's Liberation: Workers from Three Factories in Beijing Hold Grand Meetings; Romanian Construction Achievement Photo Exhibition Opens in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1959-08-21
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, August 20th. To warmly celebrate the fifteenth anniversary of Romania's liberation, more than 500 workers from the Beijing Wire Factory, North China Radio Equipment Factory, and Beijing Electronic Tube Factory held a meeting tonight.

Romanian Ambassador to China, Zaharescu, and Romanian solo performers who are currently visiting Beijing were invited to attend the conference and were warmly welcomed by the workers. Ambassador Zaharescu delivered a passionate speech full of friendship at the meeting. In his speech, he introduced the history of the Romanian people's struggle and the great achievements made in socialist construction. He sincerely congratulated the unbreakable brotherly friendship between the Romanian and Chinese people on its growing development and consolidation. The conference handed a letter to Ambassador Zaharescu congratulating all Romanian workers on the holiday. Romanian solo performers gave a wonderful performance at the meeting.

More than 600 Young Pioneers in the capital also held a get-together at the Jingshan Park Young Pioneers Palace tonight to celebrate the Romanian people's national holiday with Romanian friends in Beijing.

Xinhua News Agency, August 20th. The photo exhibition introducing the achievements of socialist construction in the Romanian People's Republic opened this morning at Daoningzhai in Beihai Park, Beijing.

The exhibition was jointly organized by the Commission for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries and the Sino-Romanian Friendship Association. Those who went to see the exhibition today included Luo Jun, Deputy Director of the Commission for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries, Zeng Yongquan, Deputy Minister of Foreign Affairs, and Zhu Ming, Secretary-General of the Sino-Romanian Friendship Association. Romanian Ambassador to China, Zaharescu, as well as diplomatic envoys and cultural officials from various countries' embassies in China also attended.

The exhibits on display show that under the wise leadership of the Romanian Workers' Party, the Romanian people have made brilliant achievements in the construction of various sectors of the national economy. By the end of this year, the total value of national industrial products will be 4.5 times that of 1938. Oil is one of Romania's richest treasures, and oil production in 1958 had already reached 1.7 times that of 1938. In agriculture, organized farmers account for more than 70% of the total number of farmers in the country. After visiting the various exhibits, Luo Jun and Zeng Yongquan warmly congratulated Ambassador Zaharescu on the brilliant achievements made by the Romanian people in construction, and wished the brotherly friendship between the Chinese and Romanian people continued development.

### 庆祝罗马尼亚解放十五周年  北京三个工厂职工隆重集会  罗建设成就图片展览会在京开幕

1959-08-21
第4版()
专栏：

    庆祝罗马尼亚解放十五周年
    北京三个工厂职工隆重集会
    罗建设成就图片展览会在京开幕
    新华社20日讯　北京有线电厂，华北无线电器材厂和北京电子管厂的五百多名职工，今天晚间举行集会热烈庆祝罗马尼亚解放十五周年。
    罗马尼亚驻中国大使查哈勒斯库和正在北京访问的罗马尼亚单独表演家应邀出席了大会，受到职工们的热烈欢迎。查哈勒斯库大使在会上发表了充满友谊激情的讲话，他在讲话中介绍了罗马尼亚人民的斗争历史和在社会主义建设中取得的巨大成就，他衷心祝贺罗中两国人民牢不可破的兄弟友谊日益发展和巩固。大会把一封向罗马尼亚全体工人祝贺节日的信，交给了查哈勒斯库大使。罗马尼亚单独表演家在会上作了精彩表演。
    首都六百多名少年先锋队员今天晚上也在景山公园少先宫举行联欢会，同在北京的罗马尼亚的朋友们共庆罗马尼亚人民的全民节日。
    新华社20日电　介绍罗马尼亚人民共和国社会主义建设成就的图片展览会，今天上午在北京北海公园道宁斋开幕。
    展览会是对外文化联络委员会和中国罗马尼亚友好协会联合举办的。今天前往观看的有对外文委副主任罗俊，外交部副部长曾涌泉，中罗友好协会秘书长朱明等。前往观看的还有罗马尼亚驻华大使查哈勒斯库，以及各国驻华使节和大使馆的文化官员。
    展览会上陈列的展品表明，罗马尼亚人民在罗马尼亚工人党的英明领导下，国民经济各部门的建设都取得了辉煌的成就。到今年年底全国工业产品的总额将是1938年的四点五倍。石油是罗马尼亚最丰富的宝藏之一，1958年的石油开采量已经达到1938年的一点七倍。在农业方面，已经组织起来的农户占到全国农户总数的70％以上。罗俊和曾涌泉等参观了各种展品以后，向查哈勒斯库大使热烈祝贺罗马尼亚人民在建设中取得了辉煌的成就，并祝中罗两国人民之间的兄弟友谊不断发展。
