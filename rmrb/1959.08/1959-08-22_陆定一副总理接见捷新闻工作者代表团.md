Title: Vice Premier Lu Dingyi Receives Czechoslovak Journalists Delegation
Author:
Date: 1959-08-22
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, 21st — Vice Premier Lu Dingyi received the Czechoslovak journalists delegation led by Comrade Oldřich Švestka, editor-in-chief of the Czechoslovak newspaper "Red Right," this afternoon.

Present at the meeting were Xu Huang, Deputy Director of the Information Department of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs, and Lu Minggu, Deputy Secretary-General of the All-China Journalists Association.

### 陆定一副总理接见捷新闻工作者代表团

1959-08-22
第4版()
专栏：

　　陆定一副总理接见捷新闻工作者代表团
    新华社21日讯　陆定一副总理在今天下午接见了由捷克斯洛伐克“红色权利报”总编辑奥德里赫·什维斯特卡同志率领的捷克斯洛伐克新闻工作者代表团。
    接见时在座的有外交部新闻司副司长徐晃，中华全国新闻工作者协会副秘书长卢鸣谷。
