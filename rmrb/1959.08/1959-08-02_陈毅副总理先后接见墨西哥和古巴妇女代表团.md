Title: Vice Premier Chen Yi Receives Women's Delegations from Mexico and Cuba
Author:
Date: 1959-08-02
Page: 3
Xinhua News Agency, 1st August. Vice Premier Chen Yi received the Mexican Women's Delegation led by Marianela Mondragón Núñez this morning. Present at the reception were Cao Mengjun, member of the Presidium of the All-China Women's Federation, and Shen Jian, Director of the Department of American and Australian Affairs of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs.

Xinhua News Agency, 1st August. Vice Premier Chen Yi received the Cuban Women's Delegation led by Violeta Teresa Casal this afternoon.
Present at the reception were Cao Mengjun, member of the Presidium of the All-China Women's Federation, and Shen Jian, Director of the Department of American and Australian Affairs of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs.

### 陈毅副总理先后接见墨西哥和古巴妇女代表团

1959-08-02
第3版()
专栏：

    陈毅副总理先后接见墨西哥和古巴妇女代表团
    新华社1日讯　陈毅副总理今天上午接见了以玛里亚内拉·蒙德拉贡·努涅斯为首的墨西哥妇女代表团。接见时在座的，有全国妇联主席团委员曹孟君、外交部美洲澳洲司司长申健等。
    新华社1日讯　陈毅副总理今天下午接见了以比奥莱塔·特雷萨·卡萨耳为首的古巴妇女代表团。
    接见时在座的，有全国妇联主席团委员曹孟君，外交部美洲澳洲司司长申健等。
