Title: Lujiang Alum Mine's Great Leap Forward
Author:
Date: 1959-08-15
Page: 2

Xinhua News Agency, Hefei. The Lujiang Alum Mine in Anhui Province has launched a mass technical innovation campaign, which has rapidly increased both the output and quality of alum. From January to July this year, a total of 9,751 tons of alum were produced, an increase of 21 percent over the same period last year. The alum produced is in large, transparent blocks, with 80 percent meeting the first-class standard.

### 庐江矾矿大跃进

1959-08-15
第2版()
专栏：简讯

　  庐江矾矿大跃进
    据新华社合肥讯　安徽省庐江矾矿开展群众性的技术革新运动，使明矾的产量和质量都迅速提高。今年一月到七月，共生产明矾九千七百五十一吨，比去年同期增加百分之二十一，出厂的明矾透明块大，百分之八十达到规定的一级标准。
