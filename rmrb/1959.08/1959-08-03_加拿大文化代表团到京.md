Title: Canadian Cultural Delegation Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1959-08-03
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, August 1st. At the invitation of the Chinese People's Association for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries, the Canadian cultural delegation of five people, led by Margaret Fairley, arrived in Beijing by plane today.

### 加拿大文化代表团到京

1959-08-03
第4版()
专栏：

    加拿大文化代表团到京
    新华社1日讯　应中国人民对外文化协会邀请来中国访问的加拿大文化代表团一行五人，在团长玛格里特·费尔莱率领下，今天乘飞机到达北京。
