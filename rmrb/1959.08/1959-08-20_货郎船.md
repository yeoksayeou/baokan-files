Date: 1959-08-20

　　Peddler Boat
    Wujin County Shipping Company in Jiangsu Province recently developed a type of peddler boat. At temporary transport points such as Luoshu Bay, they utilize the boatmen's rest time to supply more than twenty kinds of commodities such as oil, grain, cigarettes, fish, and vermicelli, which is welcomed by the boatmen.

### 货郎船

1959-08-20
第2版()
专栏：

　　货郎船
    江苏武进县航运公司最近搞了一种货郎船，在罗墅湾等临时运输点，利用船民休息时间，供应油、粮、香烟、鱼、粉丝等二十多种商品，受到船工们的欢迎。
