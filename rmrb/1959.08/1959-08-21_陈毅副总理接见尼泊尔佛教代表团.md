Title: Vice Premier Chen Yi Receives Nepalese Buddhist Delegation
Author: 
Date: 1959-08-21
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, August 20th — Vice Premier Chen Yi received Kanlu Xi, Vice President of the World Fellowship of Buddhists and President of the Nepal Buddhist Revival Association, and all members of the Nepalese Buddhist Delegation he led this evening.

    Present at the reception were He Chengxiang, Director of the Religious Affairs Bureau of the State Council, Zhao Puchu, Vice President of the Buddhist Association of China, and Zhou Shujia, Vice President of the China-Nepal Friendship Association.

### 陈毅副总理接见尼泊尔佛教代表团

1959-08-21
第4版()
专栏：

    陈毅副总理接见尼泊尔佛教代表团
    新华社20日讯　陈毅副总理今天晚上接见了世界佛教徒联谊会副主席、尼泊尔佛教复兴会会长甘露喜和由他率领的尼泊尔佛教代表团全体团员。
    接见时在座的有国务院宗教事务局局长何成湘、中国佛教协会副会长赵朴初、中国尼泊尔友好协会副会长周叔迦。
