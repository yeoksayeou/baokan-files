Title: Sino-North Korean Friendship Association Holds Film Reception to Celebrate North Korean National Day
Author:
Date: 1959-08-13
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency, August 12th: The Sino-Korean Friendship Association held a film reception this evening to celebrate the 14th anniversary of the liberation of Korea.

    Korean Ambassador to China, Li Yong-ho, embassy staff, Korean guests in Beijing, and students studying in China were invited to attend the reception. Those present at the reception included Li Dequan, President of the Sino-Korean Friendship Association, Vice Presidents Lao She and Wei Chuantong, Vice Minister of Foreign Affairs Ji Pengfei, and Deputy Directors of the Commission for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries Qu Wu and Luo Jun. Korean and Chinese documentaries and feature films were screened at the reception.

### 中朝友协举行电影晚会庆祝朝鲜国庆

1959-08-13
第4版()
专栏：

　　中朝友协举行电影晚会庆祝朝鲜国庆
    新华社12日讯　中国朝鲜友好协会今天晚上举行电影晚会，庆祝朝鲜解放十四周年。
    朝鲜驻华大使李永镐和大使馆工作人员以及在北京的朝鲜客人和留学生应邀出席了晚会。出席晚会的有中朝友好协会会长李德全、副会长老舍、魏传统，外交部副部长姬鹏飞，对外文化联络委员会副主任屈武、罗俊等。晚会上放映了朝鲜和中国两国的纪录片和故事片。
