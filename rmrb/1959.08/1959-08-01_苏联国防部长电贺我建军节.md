Title: Soviet Defense Minister Sends Congratulations on Army Day
Author:
Date: 1959-08-01
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, July 31st. Marshal Peng Dehuai, Minister of National Defense of our country, received a telegram from Marshal Malinovsky, Minister of National Defense of the Soviet Union, congratulating the 32nd anniversary of the founding of the Chinese People's Liberation Army. The full text of the congratulatory telegram is as follows:
To Comrade Peng Dehuai, Minister of National Defense of the People's Republic of China, Marshal of the People's Republic of China:
    Dear Comrade Peng Dehuai:
    On behalf of all the servicemen of the Soviet Armed Forces, and in my own name, I extend to you, and through you, to all personnel of the fraternal Chinese People's Liberation Army, congratulations on the glorious 32nd anniversary of the founding of the Army.
    All personnel of the Soviet Armed Forces sincerely wish all servicemen of the Chinese People's Liberation Army further achievements in military training, political education, and strengthening the defense capabilities of the People's Republic of China.
    I wish the unbreakable friendship between the great peoples of the Soviet Union and China, and the combat cooperation between our two countries' armies and the armies of the socialist countries, further consolidation and development. The socialist countries are a reliable guarantee of peace and the security of our peoples, and a reliable guarantee of further achievements in completing the great tasks of socialist construction.
      Minister of Defense of the Union of Soviet Socialist Republics
                        Marshal of the Soviet Union
                        R. Ya. Malinovsky
                                July 31, 1959

### 苏联国防部长电贺我建军节

1959-08-01
第1版()
专栏：

    苏联国防部长电贺我建军节
    新华社31日讯　我国国防部部长彭德怀元帅接到了苏联国防部部长马利诺夫斯基元帅祝贺中国人民解放军建军三十二周年的电报。贺电全文如下：中华人民共和国国防部长中华人民共和国元帅彭德怀同志亲爱的彭德怀同志：
    我谨代表苏联武装力量全体军人，并以我个人名义，向您，并通过您，向兄弟的中国人民解放军全体人员祝贺光荣的建军三十二周年纪念日。
    苏联武装部队全体人员衷心地祝中国人民解放军全体军人在军事训练、政治教育及加强中华人民共和国国防威力中获得进一步成就。
    祝苏中两国伟大人民之间牢不可破的友谊，祝我们两国军队和社会主义各国军队之间的战斗合作进一步巩固和发展。社会主义各国是和平及我们各国人民的安全的可靠保证，是在完成社会主义建设的巨大任务中获得进一步成就的可靠保证。
      苏维埃社会主义共和国联盟国防部长
                        苏　联　元　帅
                        罗·雅·马利诺夫斯基
                                1959年7月31日
