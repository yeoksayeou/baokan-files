Title: Romanian Musicians Perform Again; Vice Premier Chen Yi Attends Performance and Meets with Performers
Author: 
Date: 1959-08-19
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, August 18th: The five Romanian solo performers who are currently visiting Beijing, held their second public performance tonight.

Vice Premier Chen Yi watched the performance and met with the Romanian musicians. Romanian Ambassador to China, Barbu Zaharescu, was also present at the meeting.

Acting Director of the Commission for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries, Zhang Zhixiang, Vice Minister of Culture, Xia Yan, and diplomatic envoys from various countries to China attended tonight's concert.

Tonight's program included opera selections, folk songs and dances by Romanian composers, as well as works by classical composers such as Schumann, Schubert, Rubinstein, Dvorak, and Verdi, and Chinese songs. Honored Artist Iolanda Mărculescu's soprano solo and young musician Mihai Constantin Nesu's violin solo received enthusiastic applause from the audience and several curtain calls.

After the performance, Vice Premier Chen Yi, Zhang Zhixiang, Xia Yan and others went on stage and warmly shook hands with the musicians, expressing their warm congratulations on the success of their performance.

The Romanian solo performers will participate in activities in Beijing to celebrate the 15th anniversary of Romania's National Day, and will visit and perform in Jinan, Hefei, Nanjing, Shanghai, Wuhan and other cities for a month. (Attached picture)

### 罗马尼亚音乐家再次公演　陈毅副总理观看演出并予以接见

1959-08-19
第4版()
专栏：

　　罗马尼亚音乐家再次公演
　　陈毅副总理观看演出并予以接见
    新华社18日讯　正在北京访问的五位罗马尼亚单独表演家，今晚举行第二场公演。
    陈毅副总理观看了演出，接见了罗马尼亚音乐家们。接见时，罗马尼亚驻华大使巴尔布·查哈勒斯库也在场。
    对外文化联络委员会代理主任张致祥、文化部副部长夏衍，各国驻华使节出席了今晚的音乐会。
    今晚的节目中，有罗马尼亚作曲家的歌剧选曲、民歌和舞曲，并有舒曼、舒伯特、卢宾什坦、德沃夏克、威尔第等古典作家的作品和中国歌曲。功勋艺术家约朗达·默尔古列斯特的女高音独唱，青年音乐家米哈伊·康斯坦丁·奈斯库的小提琴独奏等，都得到观众热烈鼓掌，几次谢幕。
    演出结束后，陈毅副总理和张致祥、夏衍等上台和音乐家们亲切握手，对他们演出的成功表示热烈祝贺。
    罗马尼亚单独表演家们将在北京参加庆祝罗马尼亚国庆十五周年的活动，并将去济南、合肥、南京、上海、武汉等城市访问演出一个月。（附图片）
