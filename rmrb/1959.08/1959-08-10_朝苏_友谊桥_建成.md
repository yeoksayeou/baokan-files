Title: 朝苏“友谊桥”建成 (Completion of the China-Soviet "Friendship Bridge")
Author:
Date: 1959-08-10
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Pyongyang, 7th. "Labor News" reported today that the "Friendship Bridge," built by Korean and Soviet workers in the lower reaches of the Tumen River, was completed today.

The completion of this bridge will help strengthen economic and cultural exchanges between Korea and the Soviet Union and further consolidate the friendly relations between the two countries.

### 朝苏“友谊桥”建成

1959-08-10
第4版()
专栏：

    朝苏“友谊桥”建成
    据新华社平壤7日电　据“劳动新闻”今天报道，朝鲜苏联两国工人在图门江下游兴建的“友谊桥”在今天竣工。
    这座桥的建成，将有助于加强朝苏两国经济、文化交流和进一步巩固两国的友谊关系。
