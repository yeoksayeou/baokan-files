Title: Chairman Kana Bogar of the African Youth Council Arrives in Beijing
Author: 
Date: 1959-08-19
Page: 4
　
　　Chairman of the African Youth Council
　　Kana Bogar Arrives in Beijing
    Xinhua News Agency, August 18th: Kana Ali Bogar, Chairman of the African Youth Council, arrived in Beijing by plane on the afternoon of the 17th. He is visiting China at the invitation of the All-China Youth Federation.
    Zhang Chao, Vice-Chairman of the All-China Youth Federation, hosted a banquet tonight to welcome the guest.

### 非洲青年理事会主席卡纳·波加尔到京

1959-08-19
第4版()
专栏：

　　非洲青年理事会主席
　  卡纳·波加尔到京
    新华社18日讯　非洲青年理事会主席卡纳·阿利·波加尔17日下午乘飞机到达北京。他是应中华全国青年联合会的邀请来中国访问的。
    中华全国青年联合会副主席张超今晚设宴欢迎客人。
