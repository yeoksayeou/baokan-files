Title: Head of Sino-Polish Friendship Association Delegation Wang Weizhou Returns to Beijing After Visit to Poland
Author:
Date: 1959-08-12
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, August 11th: Wang Weizhou, head of the Sino-Polish Friendship Association delegation that visited Poland, returned to Beijing by plane tonight. The members of the delegation will return to Beijing in the coming days.

The delegation stayed in Poland for a total of twenty-four days. In addition to participating in the celebrations for the 15th anniversary of Poland's National Day in Warsaw, they also visited Gdańsk, Poznań, Krakow, and other cities.

### 中波友协代表团团长王维舟访波后返京

1959-08-12
第4版()
专栏：

　　中波友协代表团团长王维舟访波后返京
    据新华社11日讯　去波兰访问的中波友好协会代表团团长王维舟，今天晚上乘飞机回到北京。代表团的团员将在日内回到北京。
    代表团在波兰共逗留了二十四天，除在华沙参加波兰国庆十五周年庆祝活动外，还访问了革但斯克、波兹南、克拉科夫以及其它城市。
