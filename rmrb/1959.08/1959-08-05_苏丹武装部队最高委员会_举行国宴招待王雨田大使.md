Title: Sudanese Armed Forces Supreme Council Holds State Banquet in Honor of Ambassador Wang Yutian
Author:
Date: 1959-08-05
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, Cairo, 2nd - The Sudanese Armed Forces Supreme Council held a state banquet at the Republican Palace in Khartoum on the evening of August 1st to honor Wang Yutian, the first Ambassador of the People's Republic of China to Sudan, and the diplomatic personnel of the Chinese Embassy.

On the Sudanese side, those attending the banquet included General Ibrahim Abboud, Chairman of the Armed Forces Supreme Council, Hassan Beshir Nasr, Member of the Council and Deputy Commander-in-Chief, Rida Farid, Member of the Council and Minister of Public Works and Mineral Resources, and Ahmed Magzoub Bahari, Member of the Council and Minister of Interior and Local Government. Foreign Minister Ahmed Kheir also attended the banquet.

Before the banquet, the Chinese Ambassador presented a gift from Liu Shaoqi, Chairman of the People's Republic of China, to General Abboud. After viewing the gift, the General praised the exquisite craftsmanship of Chinese artifacts.

During the banquet, the hosts and guests engaged in conversation in a friendly and harmonious atmosphere. General Abboud praised China's construction achievements. The national anthems of the two countries were played at the end of the banquet.

### 苏丹武装部队最高委员会  举行国宴招待王雨田大使

1959-08-05
第5版()
专栏：

    苏丹武装部队最高委员会
    举行国宴招待王雨田大使
    新华社开罗2日电　苏丹武装部队最高委员会1日晚上在喀土穆共和宫举行国宴，招待中华人民共和国驻苏丹第一任大使王雨田和中国大使馆的外交人员。
    苏丹方面参加宴会的有武装部队最高委员会主席易卜拉欣·阿布德将军、委员会委员兼副总司令哈桑·贝希尔·纳斯尔、委员会委员兼公共工程和矿产部部长里达·法里德、委员会委员兼内政和地方政府部部长艾哈迈德·迈吉祖卜·巴哈里。外交部长艾哈迈德·赫尔也出席了宴会。
    在宴会之前，中国大使代表中华人民共和国主席刘少奇向阿布德将军赠送礼物。将军观看了礼物以后称赞中国工艺品的精致。
    宴会上，宾主在友好和融洽的气氛中进行交谈。阿布德将军赞扬中国建设的成就。宴会结束时演奏了两国的国歌。
