Title: Henan to Build Newsprint Mill
Author:
Date: 1959-08-03
Page: 2

Henan to Build Newsprint Mill

(Our dispatch) Henan Province's first modern paper mill, the Henan Newsprint Mill, recently began construction in Zhongmu County. Upon completion of the first phase of the project at the end of the year, it will be able to produce more than 40 tons of newsprint daily.

### 河南兴建新闻纸厂

1959-08-03
第2版()
专栏：

    河南兴建新闻纸厂
    本报讯　河南省第一座现代化造纸厂——河南新闻纸厂最近在中牟县动工兴建。第一期工程在年底完成后，每日可产新闻纸四十多吨。
