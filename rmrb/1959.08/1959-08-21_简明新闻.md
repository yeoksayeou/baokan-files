	Ningxia Tan Sheep Achieve a Bumper Harvest
	The Ningxia Hui Autonomous Region has achieved a bumper harvest of Tan sheep.
	The autonomous region produced over one million goat and sheep lambs during the spring and summer seasons this year. The lambs are generally growing strong and healthy, with a survival rate of about 90%. Approximately 15% of the ewes produced two litters of lambs in one year, and many ewes even had three or four lambs in a single birth. Currently, the total number of goats and sheep in the region has increased by 42.2% compared to the end of last year, exceeding this year's production plan by 19.9%.

	Inner Mongolia Raises Precious Fur-Bearing Animals
	Animal farms in Hohhot, Hailar, Jining, and other areas of the Inner Mongolia Autonomous Region have been raising over four hundred silver foxes, arctic foxes, minks, coypu, muskrats, sables, and other precious fur-bearing animals since their establishment last year. They have also begun to experiment with raising otters and raccoon dogs captured within the region.

	Wuxi Honey Peaches Achieve a Bumper Harvest
	Wuxi honey peaches in Jiangsu Province have achieved an unprecedented bumper harvest this year. The average yield per mu is 1,000 to 1,200 jin, and the total output of the county has reached over 100,000 dan, an increase of about 100% compared to last year.
	Wuxi County's honey peaches are famous throughout the country for their large size, juiciness, and sweetness.

    宁夏滩羊丰收
    宁夏回族自治区滩羊大丰收。
    自治区今年春夏两季共产山羊、绵羊羔一百多万只，羔羊普遍生长健壮，成活率达90％左右。有15％左右的母羊一年产了两次羔，许多母羊并且一胎产三羔或四羔。现在，全区共有山羊、绵羊比去年年底增殖了42.2％，超额19.9％完成了今年的生产计划。
    内蒙古饲养珍贵皮毛兽
    内蒙古自治区呼和浩特、海拉尔、集宁等地的动物饲养场，从去年建立到现在，已经饲养了四百多只银黑狐、北极狐、水貂、海狸鼠、麝鼠、紫貂等珍贵的皮毛兽，并且开始试养区内捕获的水獭、貉子等。
    无锡水蜜桃丰收
    江苏省无锡水蜜桃今年空前丰收。每亩平均产桃一千斤至一千二百斤，全县总产量达到十万担以上，比去年增产一倍左右。
    无锡县的水蜜桃以果大、汁多、味甜闻名各地。
