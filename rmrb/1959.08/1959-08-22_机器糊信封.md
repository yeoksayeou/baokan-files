Title: Machine Gluing Envelopes
Author:
Date: 1959-08-22
Page: 2

    Machine Gluing Envelopes
    Workers at the Beijing No. 4 Printing Factory have switched the corner-cutting and embossing processes in envelope gluing to machine production, which has not only increased efficiency thirty-fold, but also greatly improved quality. Envelopes glued by hand in the past were inconsistent in size, but this problem does not exist with machine-embossed envelopes.

### 机器糊信封

1959-08-22
第2版()
专栏：

    机器糊信封
    北京市印刷四厂糊信封的工人把切角、轧印两道工序改为机器生产，不仅提高效率三十倍，而且质量大有提高。过去用手工糊的信封大小不一致，用机器轧出来的就没有这种毛病。
