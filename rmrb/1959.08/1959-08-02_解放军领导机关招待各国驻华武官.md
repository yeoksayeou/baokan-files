Title: PLA Leadership Organs Host Military Attaches from Various Countries

Author:

Date: 1959-08-02
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, 1st August. The General Staff Department and the General Political Department of the Chinese People's Liberation Army held a reception tonight to celebrate the 32nd anniversary of the "August 1st" Army Day.

Military attaches and acting military attaches of various countries stationed in China and their wives were invited to attend the reception tonight. Colonel Ismail Razouki, leader of the Iraqi Republic Army football team, who is visiting our country for the first time, was also invited to attend the reception.

Senior generals of the Chinese People's Liberation Army, including General Yang Chengwu, Deputy Chief of the General Staff, General Li Juikui, General Chen Shiju, Lieutenant General Liu Zhijian, Lieutenant General Xiao Xiangrong, Lieutenant General Zhang Lingbin, Lieutenant General Rao Zhengxi, Lieutenant General Zhang Chiming, Lieutenant General Liu Shaowen, Lieutenant General Cao Lihuai of the Air Force, Lieutenant General Zhou Biao of the Air Force, Rear Admiral Zhao Yiping of the Navy, and Major General Zhao Zhangcheng, attended the reception tonight.

Yu Peiwen, Director of the Protocol Department of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs, and others also attended the reception.

General Yang Chengwu delivered a speech at the beginning of the reception, saying that today is the 32nd anniversary of the founding of the Chinese People's Liberation Army. We feel very honored to be able to celebrate this festival together with representatives of the armed forces of various countries and the responsible military officers of the Iraqi Republic Army football team who are visiting our country for the first time. General Yang Chengwu proposed: To toast to the friendship and unity between the Chinese People's Liberation Army and the armies of all friendly countries, to the progress and development of the armies of all friendly countries, to the prosperity and happiness of the people of all friendly countries, and to peace in the Far East and the world.

The reception proceeded in a warm and friendly atmosphere. During the banquet, the hosts and guests repeatedly raised their glasses to toast the development of mutual friendship and solidarity.

### 解放军领导机关招待各国驻华武官

1959-08-02
第1版()
专栏：

    解放军领导机关招待各国驻华武官
    新华社1日讯　中国人民解放军总参谋部和总政治部今晚举行了招待会，庆祝“八一”建军节三十二周年。
    各国驻华武官、代理武官及他们的夫人应邀出席了今晚的招待会。应邀出席招待会的还有首次来我国访问的伊拉克共和国军队足球队的领队伊斯梅尔·拉佐基上校等。
    中国人民解放军副总参谋长杨成武上将和李聚奎上将、陈士榘上将、刘志坚中将、萧向荣中将、张令彬中将、饶正锡中将、张池明中将、刘少文中将、曹里怀空军中将、周彪空军中将、赵一萍海军少将、赵章成少将等高级将领，出席了今晚的招待会。
    出席招待会的还有外交部礼宾司司长俞沛文等。
    杨成武上将在招待会开始时致词说，今天是中国人民解放军建军三十二周年纪念日。我们能够在这里和各国武装部队的代表以及第一次来我国访问的伊拉克共和国军队足球队的负责军官一起，共同欢度这个节日，感到十分荣幸。杨成武上将提议：为中国人民解放军和各友好国家军队之间的友谊团结，为各友好国家军队的进步和发展，为各友好国家人民的繁荣和幸福，为远东和世界和平干杯。
    招待会在热烈友好的气氛中进行。席间，宾主一再为相互间的友谊和团结的发展而举杯祝酒。
