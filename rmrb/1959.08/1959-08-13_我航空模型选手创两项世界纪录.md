Title: Our Aviation Model Athletes Set Two World Records
Author:
Date: 1959-08-13
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, 12th — The two world records for the duration and straight-line distance of piston-engine model helicopters, created earlier this year by our country's aviation model athletes, have been reviewed and confirmed by the relevant departments in our country.

　　These two world records were both created by aviation model athletes of the Chinese People's Liberation Army. Among them, Wang Xun, 35, created a new world record of 22 minutes and 27 seconds for the duration of piston-engine model helicopters in a model aircraft record-breaking flight test held in Xi'an on April 6. The original world record was created by Soviet aviation model athlete S. Vorobyov on July 11, 1958, and his record was 18 minutes and 40 seconds.

　　The other world record was created by Liu Litian, 17, on April 26 in Liangxiang, Beijing, with a straight-line distance of 18.038 kilometers for piston-engine model helicopters. The original world record was also held by Soviet aviation model athlete S. Vorobyov, who created the world record of 11.92 kilometers on March 9, 1958.

### 我航空模型选手创两项世界纪录

1959-08-13
第6版()
专栏：

　　我航空模型选手创两项世界纪录
    新华社12日讯　我国航空模型选手今年早些时候创造的活塞式发动机模型直升飞机留空时间和飞行直线距离的两项世界新纪录，已经由我国有关部门审核肯定。
    这两项世界新纪录，都是由中国人民解放军的航空模型选手创造的。其中三十五岁的王?，4月6日在西安举行的一次模型飞机创纪录飞行测验中，创造了活塞式发动机模型直升飞机留空时间二十二分二十七秒的世界新纪录。原来这项世界纪录是苏联航空模型选手斯·沃罗比约夫在1958年7月11日创造的，他的纪录是十八分四十秒。
    另一项世界新纪录，是十七岁的刘立天4月26日在北京良乡创造的活塞式发动机模型直升飞机飞行直线距离十八点零三八公里。原来的世界纪录也是由苏联航空模型选手斯·沃罗比约夫保持的，他在1958年3月9日创造的世界纪录是十一点九二公里。
