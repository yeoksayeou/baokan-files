Title: Bao Erhan Hosts Banquet to Welcome Indonesian Art Troupe
Author:
Date: 1959-08-13
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency, August 12th: Bao Erhan, President of the China-Indonesia Friendship Association, hosted a banquet this evening to warmly welcome all members of the Indonesian Art Troupe.

President Bao Erhan, Art Troupe Leader Banda Harahap, and Indonesian Chargé d'Affaires to China Suleiman spoke at the banquet, congratulating the growing development of cultural exchange and friendship between the two countries. At the banquet, Leader Banda Harahap presented a gift to the China-Indonesia Friendship Association on behalf of the Indonesian China Friendship Association.

### 包尔汉设宴欢迎印度尼西亚艺术团

1959-08-13
第4版()
专栏：

　　包尔汉设宴欢迎印度尼西亚艺术团
    据新华社12日讯　中国印度尼西亚友好协会会长包尔汉今晚举行宴会，热烈欢迎印度尼西亚艺术团全体人员。
    包尔汉会长、艺术团团长班达·哈拉哈普和印度尼西亚驻中国大使馆临时代办苏雷曼在宴会上先后讲话，祝贺两国文化交流和人民友谊的日益发展。宴会上，班达·哈拉哈普团长代表印度尼西亚中国友好协会向中国印度尼西亚友好协会赠送了礼品。
