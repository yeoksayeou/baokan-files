Title: Indonesian Art Troupe Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1959-08-12
Page: 4

　　Indonesian Art Troupe Arrives in Beijing
    Xinhua News Agency, 11th—The Indonesian Art Troupe, led by its head, Banda Harahap, totaling forty-nine members, arrived in Beijing by train from Guangzhou this afternoon. The art troupe is visiting China at the invitation of the China-Indonesia Friendship Association.

### 印度尼西亚艺术团到京

1959-08-12
第4版()
专栏：

　　印度尼西亚艺术团到京
    据新华社11日讯　由团长班达·哈拉哈普率领的印度尼西亚艺术团一行四十九人，今天下午乘火车从广州到达北京。艺术团是应中国印度尼西亚友好协会邀请来中国访问的。
