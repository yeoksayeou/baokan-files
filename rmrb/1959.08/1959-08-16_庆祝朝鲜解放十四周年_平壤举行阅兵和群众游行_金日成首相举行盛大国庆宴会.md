Title: Celebrating the 14th Anniversary of Korea's Liberation, Pyongyang Holds Military Parade and Mass Parade; Premier Kim Il-sung Holds Grand National Day Banquet
Author:
Date: 1959-08-16
Page: 3

Xinhua News Agency, Pyongyang, August 15th: Pyongyang held a grand military parade and a mass parade of 300,000 people this morning to celebrate the 14th anniversary of Korea's liberation on "August 15th."

The Korean people, riding the Chollima (flying horse) spirit, are celebrating their national holiday this year with the glorious victory of completing the industrial output value plan of the First Five-Year Plan two and a half years ahead of schedule, at the end of June this year. After the rain, Pyongyang was sunny, with red flags flying, colorful arches erected, and the whole city was filled with an atmosphere of victory and joy.

At 8:00 a.m. sharp, Korean Premier Kim Il-sung and party and government leaders such as Choi Yong-kun, Kim Il, Hong Myung-hee, Park Jung-ae, Park Kim-chul, Kim Chang-man, Jung Il-ryong, and Nam Il ascended the rostrum on Kim Il-sung Square. Responsible cadres from Korean party and government organs and diplomatic envoys from various countries to Korea watched the ceremony on the stands. Then the military parade began. General Kim Kwang-hyup, Minister of National Defense of Korea, inspected the armed forces neatly lined up in Kim Il-sung Square and surrounding streets in an open-top car. Then General Kim Kwang-hyup delivered a speech congratulating the festival.

Subsequently, the march-past began. Students from the army, navy, and air force officer schools and mechanized units, artillery, and tank units passed in neat formations in front of the rostrum, demonstrating the powerful strength of the Korean People's Armed Forces defending the motherland and defending peace.

The colorful mass parade followed. Workers, farmers, students, women, and children, wearing festive costumes and carrying bouquets and colorful flags, cheered as they walked past the rostrum. They carried portraits of Kim Il-sung, Choi Yong-kun, and leaders of fraternal countries. They carried a huge Chollima model symbolizing the rapid development of Korea. The charts and propaganda paintings in the parade showed the brilliant achievements of Korea's economic construction in the fourteen years since liberation.

The cartoons carried by the parading masses exposed the crimes of the US military occupying South Korea. People shouted "US troops get out of South Korea immediately!" "Long live the peaceful reunification of the fatherland!"

Korean women and artists danced folk dances. Students sang the revolutionary songs of Kim Il-sung's anti-Japanese guerrillas as they passed the rostrum. In a huge lotus petal in the mass procession, there was a pair of children born in the year of Korea's liberation. They were wearing beautiful national costumes, waving and smiling at the rostrum. They symbolized the happy life of the liberated Korean people.

Xinhua News Agency, Pyongyang, August 15th: Korean Premier Kim Il-sung held a grand banquet tonight to celebrate the 14th anniversary of Korea's liberation on "August 15th."

Those attending the banquet included: Choi Yong-kun, Kim Il, Hong Myung-hee, Park Jung-ae, Park Kim-chul, Kim Chang-man, Jung Il-ryong, Nam Il and other leaders of the Korean Workers' Party and government, responsible persons of democratic political parties and social organizations, senior generals of the People's Army, prominent figures from all walks of life, representatives of the working people, diplomatic envoys of various countries to Korea, and foreign nationals in Korea.

In his speech at the banquet, Kim Il-sung expressed his warm congratulations to the Korean working people who completed the industrial output value plan of the First Five-Year Plan two and a half years ahead of schedule. He also expressed his gratitude to the Communist Party and Government of the Soviet Union. He proposed a toast to the prosperity and development of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea and the socialist camp, and to world peace.

The banquet was held in a warm and joyful atmosphere.

### 庆祝朝鲜解放十四周年  平壤举行阅兵和群众游行  金日成首相举行盛大国庆宴会

1959-08-16
第3版()
专栏：

    庆祝朝鲜解放十四周年
    平壤举行阅兵和群众游行
    金日成首相举行盛大国庆宴会
    据新华社平壤15日电　平壤市今天上午举行盛大的阅兵式和三十万人的群众游行，庆祝“八·一五”朝鲜解放十四周年。
    跨上千里马的朝鲜人民今年以提前两年半在今年6月底完成第一个五年计划的工业总产值计划的光辉胜利来庆祝自己的民族节日。雨后初晴的平壤，阳光灿烂，红旗招展，彩牌高搭，全城洋溢着胜利和欢乐的气氛。
    上午八时正，朝鲜内阁首相金日成和崔庸健、金一、洪命熹、朴正爱、朴金哲、金昌满、郑一龙、南日等党和政府的领导人登上金日成广场上的主席台。在观礼台上观礼的有朝鲜党政机关负责干部和各国驻朝鲜外交使节。接着开始了阅兵式。朝鲜民族保卫相金光侠大将乘敞篷汽车检阅了整齐地排列在金日成广场和周围街道上的武装部队。然后金光侠大将发表了祝贺节日的演说。
    随后分列式开始。陆海空三军军官学校的学生和机械化部队、炮兵、坦克部队，排着整齐的队列通过主席台前，显示了保卫祖国保卫和平的朝鲜人民武装部队的强大力量。
    丰富多彩的群众游行接着开始。工人、农民、学生、妇女、儿童穿着节日服装，拿着花束和彩旗，欢呼着走过主席台前。他们抬着金日成、崔庸健和兄弟国家领袖的肖像。他们抬着象征朝鲜正在一日千里地发展着的巨大的千里马模型。游行队伍中的图表和宣传画中表现了解放后十四年来朝鲜经济建设取得了辉煌成就。
    游行群众拿着的漫画揭露了强占着南朝鲜的美军的罪行。人们高呼“美军立即从南朝鲜滚出去！”“祖国和平统一万岁！”
    朝鲜的妇女和艺术工作者跳着民族舞蹈。学生们唱着金日成抗日游击队的革命歌曲走过主席台前。群众队伍中在一朵巨大的莲花瓣里，有一对在朝鲜解放那一年诞生的儿童，他们穿着美丽的民族服装，向主席台招手微笑。他们象征着解放了的朝鲜人民的幸福生活。
    新华社平壤15日电　朝鲜内阁首相金日成今天晚上举行盛大宴会，庆祝“八·一五”朝鲜解放十四周年。
    参加宴会的有：崔庸健、金一、洪命熹、朴正爱、朴金喆、金昌满、郑一龙、南日等朝鲜劳动党和政府的领导人，各民主政党、社会团体负责人，人民军高级将领，各界著名人士，劳动人民代表和各国驻朝外交使节以及在朝鲜的外国人士。
    金日成在宴会上致词时，向提前二年半完成第一个五年计划工业总产值计划的朝鲜劳动人民表示热烈的祝贺。他同时向苏联共产党和政府表示感谢。他提议为朝鲜民主主义人民共和国和社会主义阵营的繁荣发展、为世界和平干杯。
    宴会在热烈欢乐的气氛中进行。
