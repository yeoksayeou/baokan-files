Title: Thirty Million People Cannot Get Medical Treatment
Author:
Date: 1959-08-01
Page: 6

Thirty Million People Cannot Get Medical Treatment
    "New York Times" medical affairs commentator Rusk wrote, "Due to the lack of an organized national healthcare system, thirty million Americans cannot get medical treatment." Rusk said, "Our current plan for training medical personnel can only guarantee that we will obtain 6.4% of the number of personnel we need to train."

### 三千万人得不到医疗

1959-08-01
第6版()
专栏：

    三千万人得不到医疗
    “纽约时报”医疗问题评论员腊斯克写道，“由于缺乏有组织的国家保健机构，三千万美国人得不到医疗”。腊斯克说，“我们目前培养医务工作人员的计划只能保证我们得到需要培养的人员数目的6.4％。”
