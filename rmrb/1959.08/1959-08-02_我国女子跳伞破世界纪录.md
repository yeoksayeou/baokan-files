Title: China's Women Parachutists Break World Records
Author:
Date: 1959-08-02
Page: 2

Xinhua News Agency, 1st August. China's young female parachutists broke two more world records in aircraft parachuting in July.

On July 30th, during the Shanghai, Jiangsu, Hubei, Hebei, Shandong, and Chinese People's Liberation Army Parachuting Competition held in Xuzhou, female athletes He Jianhua, Li Shuhui, and Li Guizhen broke the world record of 8.77 meters set by Soviet female athletes in April this year, with an average distance of 2.21 meters from the target center in the women's daytime 1,000-meter team precision landing event. On that day, He Jianhua landed 1.80 meters from the target center after jumping from the plane. Li Shuhui landed slightly further away, at 1.95 meters. Li Guizhen landed 2.89 meters from the target center.

On July 3rd, female parachutists Lü Xuehui, Hua Shaolin, and Wei Xiuling from Beijing also broke the world record for women's daytime 1,000-meter team combined parachuting in Zhuoxian County, Hebei Province. They created a new record of an average distance of 6.80 meters from the target center, surpassing the original world record of 11.63 meters set by Soviet athletes in 1957. On that day, their individual distances from the target center were: Lü Xuehui 6.40 meters, Hua Shaolin 6.60 meters, and Wei Xiuling 7.40 meters.

### 我国女子跳伞破世界纪录

1959-08-02
第2版()
专栏：

    我国女子跳伞破世界纪录
    新华社1日讯　我国年轻的女子跳伞运动员7月间又打破了两项飞机跳伞的世界纪录。
    7月30日，在徐州举行上海、江苏、湖北、河北、山东和中国人民解放军跳伞对抗赛时，女运动员赫建华、李淑慧、李桂珍在女子日间一千米集体定点跳伞比赛中，以平均距靶心二点二一米的成绩，打破了苏联女运动员今年4月间创造的八点七七米的世界纪录。赫建华这一天跳离飞机后落到距靶心一点八零米的地方。李淑慧落下的地方稍远，是一点九五米。李桂珍落下的地方距靶心二点八九米。
    7月3日，北京的女子跳伞运动员吕学会、华绍林和魏秀玲三个人，也曾经在河北省涿县打破了女子日间一千米集体综合跳伞的世界纪录。他们创造的新成绩是平均距靶心六点八零米，原来由苏联运动员在1957年创造的世界纪录是十一点六三米。这一天，她们三个人个人距靶心的成绩分别是：吕学慧六点四零米，华绍林六点六零米，魏秀玲七点四零米。
