Title: Indonesian Art Troupe Holds First Performance in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1959-08-14
Page: 4

　　Indonesian Art Troupe
　　Holds First Performance in Beijing
    Xinhua News Agency, 13th – The Indonesian Art Troupe's artists captivated the Beijing audience with their moving songs and graceful dances during their first performance in Beijing tonight.

    Every performance was warmly welcomed by the 1,400 audience members. Vice Premier Chen Yi attended the performance tonight. Also in attendance were Burhan Shahidi, President of the Chinese-Indonesian Friendship Association; Ding Xilin, Vice President of the Chinese People's Association for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries; Zhang Zhixiang and Luo Jun, Deputy Directors of the Commission for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries; Zeng Yongquan, Vice Minister of Foreign Affairs; Huang Zhen, Chinese Ambassador to Indonesia; and Da Pusheng, Deputy Director of the China Islamic Association, among others.

    Suleiman, Chargé d'Affaires ad interim of the Indonesian Embassy in China, also attended the performance.

    After the performance, the Chinese-Indonesian Friendship Association presented the art troupe with flower baskets. Vice Premier Chen Yi, accompanied by Chargé d'Affaires ad interim Suleiman, went on stage to meet with the heads and artists of the art troupe, congratulating them on their successful performance.

### 印度尼西亚艺术团　在京举行首次演出

1959-08-14
第4版()
专栏：

　　印度尼西亚艺术团
　　在京举行首次演出
    据新华社13日讯　印度尼西亚艺术团的艺术家今晚在北京的第一次演出中，就以他们动人的歌声和优美的舞蹈吸引了北京观众。
    每一个节目都受到了一千四百名观众的热烈欢迎。今晚，陈毅副总理出席观看了演出。观看演出的还有中国印度尼西亚友好协会会长包尔汉，中国人民对外文化协会副会长丁西林，对外文化联络委员会副主任张致祥、罗俊，外交部副部长曾涌泉，中国驻印度尼西亚大使黄镇和中国伊斯兰教协会副主任达浦生等。
    印度尼西亚驻中国大使馆临时代办苏雷曼也观看了演出。
    在演出结束以后，中国印度尼西亚友好协会向艺术团赠送了花篮，陈毅副总理等由苏雷曼临时代办陪同登上舞台同艺术团的负责人和艺术家们会见，祝贺他们成功的演出。
