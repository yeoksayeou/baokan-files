Title: Jingbao Railway repaired and reopened

Author: 
Date: 1959-08-01
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Hohhot, July 31st: The section of the Jingbao Railway near Hohhot, which was washed out by floods on July 27th, was repaired and reopened to traffic at 6:00 PM on July 31st, thanks to the day and night efforts of more than 6,000 workers and staff members of the Hohhot Railway Bureau.

After the line between Sandaoying east of Hohhot and Taigemu west of Hohhot was washed out by floods on July 27th, the Hohhot Railway Bureau immediately established a repair command and organized a repair force of more than 6,000 people to carry out crash repairs in four groups. The section from the east of Hohhot to Sandaoying was repaired on the afternoon of July 27th.

### 京包铁路修复通车

1959-08-01
第1版()
专栏：

    京包铁路修复通车
    新华社呼和浩特31日电　7月27日被洪水冲断的京包铁路呼和浩特市附近一段，经过呼和浩特铁路局六千多名职工日夜抢修，已在31日下午六时修复通车。
    呼和浩特以东的三道营到呼和浩特以西的台阁牧之间的线路，27日被洪水冲断以后，呼和浩特铁路局立即成立了抢修指挥部，组织六千多人的抢修大军，分四路突击抢修。呼和浩特市东面到三道营一段，27日下午就已修复。
