Title: Sino-US Talks Postponed
Author:
Date: 1959-08-18
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Warsaw, August 17th: It was announced here this morning that the Chinese and American ambassadors have mutually agreed to postpone the ninety-second meeting of the Sino-US ambassadorial talks, originally scheduled for August 25th, to September 1st due to administrative reasons.

### 中美会谈延期举行

1959-08-18
第4版()
专栏：

　　中美会谈延期举行
    新华社华沙17日电　据今天上午此间宣布，中美两国大使一致协议，由于行政性的理由，原订在8月25日举行的中美两国大使级会谈第九十二次会议，推迟到9月1日举行。
