Author: Chen Fubei Li Changyong Lü Houmin Li Xilong
Date: 1959-08-09

Pearl of the South China Sea

    The salt fields of Yulin Saltworks in Yaxian County, Hainan Island.

    A car is passing through a highway on Wuzhi Mountain.

    The second large-scale iron and steel joint enterprise in Guangdong – Hainan Iron and Steel Plant, is under intense construction.

    The Songtao Reservoir in Hainan is under full-scale construction.

    The Basuo Port in Hainan Island has been expanded into one of Guangdong's modern ports.

    Female workers at the Xinglong Overseas Chinese Farm are happily picking ripe coffee beans.

    Members of the Xinglong People’s Commune in Yaxian County, Hainan Island, are rehearsing the Coconut Harvest Dance in their spare time.

    The above photos were taken by our reporters Chen Fubei, Li Changyong, Lü Houmin, and Li Xilong

### 南海上的明珠（图片）
陈福北  李长永  吕厚民  李喜龙
1959-08-09
第5版()
专栏：

    南海上的明珠
    海南岛崖县榆亚盐场的盐田。
    汽车正在通过五指山上的一条公路。
    广东第二个大型钢铁联合企业——海南钢铁厂，正在紧张建设中。
    海南松涛水库正在全面施工。
    海南岛的八所港已扩建成为广东现代化的港口之一。
    兴隆华侨农场的女工在愉快地采摘成熟的咖啡子。
    海南岛崖县兴隆人民公社的社员们在劳动之余排练椰子丰收舞。
