Title: Vice Premier Chen Yi Receives Belgian Trade Union Leaders
Author:
Date: 1959-08-11
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, 10th - Vice Premier Chen Yi received Andre Renard, Deputy Secretary-General of the Belgian General Federation of Labour, Raymond Larden, Secretary-General of the Belgian Metalworkers' Union, and Anthony Gulman, Executive Committee Member of the Belgian Metalworkers' Union, who are currently visiting China, this morning.

Present at the reception were Kang Yonghe, Secretary of the Secretariat of the All-China Federation of Trade Unions and Chairman of the First Machine Building Trade Union, and others.

### 陈毅副总理接见比利时工会领导人

1959-08-11
第4版()
专栏：

　　陈毅副总理接见比利时工会领导人
    新华社10日讯　陈毅副总理今日上午接见了正在我国访问的比利时总工会副总书记安德列·雷纳，比利时五金工会总书记雷蒙·拉丹和比利时五金工会执行委员安东尼·古尔曼。
    接见时在座的有中华全国总工会书记处书记、第一机械工会主席康永和等。
