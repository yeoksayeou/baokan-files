Title: Fraternal Countries Congratulate China on Army Day; Military Attaché of Our Embassy in the Soviet Union Holds Reception
Author:
Date: 1959-08-02
Page: 3
Xinhua News Agency, August 1st News: On the eve of the "August 1st" Army Day, Marshal Peng Dehuai, Minister of National Defense of our country, received telegrams from Deputy Chairman of the Council of Ministers and Minister of National Defense of the People's Republic of Bulgaria, General Mikhailov; Lieutenant General Leontin Salajan, Minister of the Armed Forces of the Romanian People's Republic; Lieutenant General Révész Géza, Minister of National Defense of the Hungarian People's Republic; General Kim Kwang-hyup, Minister of National Defense of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea; General Bohumír Lomský, Minister of National Defense of the Czechoslovak Republic; General Spychalski, Minister of National Defense of the Polish People's Republic; Lieutenant General Jamyangiin Lhagvasüren, Minister of People's Army Affairs and Commander of the People's Army of the Mongolian People's Republic; General Stoph, Minister of National Defense of the German Democratic Republic; General Beqir Balluku, Minister of National Defense of the People's Republic of Albania; and General Vo Nguyen Giap, Minister of National Defense of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam, congratulating the 32nd anniversary of the founding of the Chinese People's Liberation Army.

In their congratulatory telegrams, the leaders of the armed forces of the fraternal countries congratulated the Chinese People's Liberation Army on its new achievements under the leadership of the Chinese Communist Party in political education, improving combat effectiveness, and further consolidating the fighting friendship of the socialist camp's armies; and congratulated the Chinese People's Liberation Army on its glorious victories in safeguarding peace in the Far East and the world. They pointed out in their telegrams: The unbreakable friendship between the armed forces of the socialist camp and the Chinese People's Liberation Army is forever linked together, a friendship built on the common goal of building socialism and the common task of defending the socialist camp from imperialist aggression. The unity of the socialist camp's strength is the most powerful guarantee for safeguarding and consolidating world peace.

Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, July 31st: To celebrate the 32nd anniversary of the founding of the Chinese People's Liberation Army, Major General Pan Zhenwu, Military Attaché of the Chinese Embassy in the Soviet Union, held a reception here on July 31st.

Among those present at the reception were Marshal Sokolovsky, First Deputy Minister of Defense of the Soviet Union; Marshals Grechko and Bagramyan of the Soviet Union; as well as senior generals of the Soviet Air Force, Artillery, and Navy, and more than 100 military attachés from various countries stationed in the Soviet Union. Pushkin, Deputy Minister of Foreign Affairs of the Soviet Union, also attended the reception.

Major General Pan Zhenwu and Marshal Sokolovsky spoke at the reception.

In his speech, Major General Pan Zhenwu said that under the leadership of the Chinese Communist Party and the great leader of the Chinese people, Comrade Mao Zedong, and with the care and support of the broad masses of the people, and with the help of the Soviet Union, the Chinese People's Liberation Army has overcome countless difficulties, defeated all kinds of enemies at home and abroad, and has grown and become stronger in these struggles; today it has become a powerful force for defending the peaceful labor of the Chinese people and defending peace in the Far East and the world. He said that the Chinese People's Liberation Army has always adhered to the policy of learning from the Soviet Army and learning all the advanced experiences of the Soviet Army. The fighting friendship and unity between the Chinese People's Liberation Army and the Soviet Army is developing and consolidating day by day, which has greatly strengthened the great unity between the countries of the socialist camp.

In his speech, Marshal Sokolovsky said that we are pleased to see the achievements made by the Chinese People's Liberation Army in political education, military training, and organizational construction. We are deeply convinced that the Chinese People's Liberation Army and the armed forces of other socialist countries are reliable pillars for defending peace in the Far East and the world. The reception was held in a warm and friendly atmosphere.

### 各兄弟国家祝贺我建军节  我驻苏使馆武官举行庆祝酒会

1959-08-02
第3版()
专栏：

    各兄弟国家祝贺我建军节
    我驻苏使馆武官举行庆祝酒会
    新华社1日讯　我国国防部长彭德怀元帅“八一”建军节前夕接到了保加利亚人民共和国部长会议副主席兼国防部长米哈伊洛夫大将、罗马尼亚人民共和国武装部队部部长列昂廷·萨拉扬上将、匈牙利人民共和国国防部长雷维斯·格佐上将、朝鲜民主主义人民共和国民族保卫相金光侠大将、捷克斯洛伐克共和国国防部长波古米尔·洛姆斯基上将、波兰人民共和国国防部长斯彼哈尔斯基上将、蒙古人民共和国人民军事务部部长兼人民军司令员札木杨·勒哈格瓦苏伦中将、德意志民主共和国国防部部长斯多夫上将、阿尔巴尼亚人民共和国国防部长贝·巴卢库上将和越南民主共和国国防部长武元甲大将等，分别发来的祝贺中国人民解放军建军三十二周年的电报。
    各兄弟国家武装部队领导人员的贺电中，祝贺中国人民解放军在中国共产党的领导下，在政治教育、提高战斗力以及进一步巩固社会主义阵营军队的战斗友谊方面取得新的成就；祝贺中国人民解放军在维护远东及世界和平事业中所获得的光辉胜利。他们在贺电中指出：社会主义阵营各国武装部队和中国人民解放军之间的牢不可破的友谊，永远连结在一起，这种友谊是建立在建设社会主义的共同目标和保卫社会主义阵营不受帝国主义侵略的共同任务上的，社会主义阵营力量的团结是保卫和巩固世界和平的最有力的保证。
    新华社莫斯科31日电　为庆祝中国人民解放军建军三十二周年，中国驻苏联大使馆武官潘振武少将7月31日在此间举行招待酒会。
    出席招待会的有苏联国防部第一副部长、苏联元帅索科洛夫斯基，苏联元帅格列奇科、巴格拉米扬，以及苏联空军、炮兵和海军的高级将领和许多国家驻苏联武官等一百多人。苏联外交部副部长普希金也出席了招待会。
    潘振武少将和索科洛夫斯基元帅在招待会上讲了话。
    潘振武少将在讲话中说，中国人民解放军在中国共产党和中国人民的伟大领袖毛泽东同志的领导下，在广大人民的关怀和支援下，在苏联的帮助下，曾经克服了无数的困难，战胜了国内外各种各样的敌人，并且在这些斗争中发展和壮大起来了；今天她已经成为保卫中国人民的和平劳动和保卫远东与世界和平的一支强大的力量。他说，中国人民解放军始终坚持向苏联军队学习的方针，学习苏联军队的一切先进经验。中国人民解放军和苏联军队之间的战斗友谊和团结日益发展和巩固，这大大地加强了社会主义阵营各国之间的伟大团结。
    索科洛夫斯基元帅在讲话中说，我们高兴地看到中国人民解放军在政治教育、军事训练和组织建设方面所取得的成就。我们深信，中国人民解放军和其他社会主义国家武装力量是保卫远东和世界和平的可靠支柱。招待会是在热烈友好的气氛中进行的。
