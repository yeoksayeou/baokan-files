    Soda Powder
    The Heihe Alcohol Factory in Heilongjiang has produced a new product: soda powder. Soda powder is made from the ingredients used to make soda. When drinking, it is mixed with water in the correct proportions, and it fizzes and foams like soda, tastes the same as soda, but is cheaper than soda.

### 汽水粉

1959-08-22
第2版()
专栏：

    汽水粉
    黑龙江黑河酒精厂制成一种新产品——汽水粉。汽水粉是用制汽水的原料配制成的。饮用时按比例兑上水，就和汽水一样泛出很多汽沫，味道和汽水相同，价格却比汽水低。
