Title: Chilean Writer Yangas Returns Home
Author:
Date: 1959-08-21
Page: 4

    Xinhua News Agency, 20th - Chilean writer Lautaro Yangas and his wife concluded their visit to China and left Beijing by plane this morning to return home.

### 智利作家杨加斯回国

1959-08-21
第4版()
专栏：

    智利作家杨加斯回国
    新华社20日讯　智利作家拉乌达罗·杨加斯夫妇结束了在中国的访问，今天早晨乘飞机离开北京回国。
