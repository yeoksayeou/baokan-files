Title: Massive Scale, Assembled Champions: Valuyev Discusses the Second All-Union Games
Author:
Date: 1959-08-10
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, August 8th: According to TASS, Moscow, August 7th: The Second All-Union Games of the Peoples of the USSR opened in Moscow on August 8th. The chief referee of this games, Ye. Valuyev, introduced this largest national sports competition in the Soviet Union at the "Soviet Sports Newspaper" and a press conference on the 7th.

Valuyev said that 8,000 male and female athletes from 235 cities in the Soviet Union, representing fifteen union republics, are participating in this national competition.

In order to welcome this grand review, preparations began a year ago in various parts of the Soviet Union, and people showed great enthusiasm for this games. A total of 40 million people participated in the qualifying rounds, an increase of 22 million compared to the first games.

The preparations for the Second All-Union Games have made the Soviet sports movement more active, and it has unfolded in an even larger sports team.

In the three years since the First All-Union Games, the number of athletes in the Soviet Union has increased by 3.5 million, and more than 3,000 new sports clubs have been established in the Russian Federation alone.

A total of 22 events will be held in this sports meeting, including motorcycle, table tennis and chess.

Valuyev pointed out that this games has aroused the interest of sports enthusiasts from all over the world. Those who come to Moscow to visit this games include not only guests from all over the Soviet Union but also many foreign guests, heads of many international sports organizations and heads of sports organizations from many countries. These include guests from Albania, Bulgaria, Czechoslovakia, Poland, China, Afghanistan, the United Kingdom, Finland, Mexico, Sweden, Switzerland and other countries.

### 规模巨大　健将云集  瓦卢耶夫谈第二届全苏运动会

1959-08-10
第4版()
专栏：

    规模巨大　健将云集
    瓦卢耶夫谈第二届全苏运动会
    据新华社8日讯　据塔斯社莫斯科7日讯：第二届苏联各民族运动大会8日在莫斯科开幕，这次大会的总裁判长叶·瓦卢耶夫7日在
  “苏联体育报”和一次记者招待会上介绍了这个苏联最大一次的全国性体育比赛。
    瓦卢耶夫说，参加这次全国比赛的有苏联二百三十五个城市，代表着十五个加盟共和国的八千名男女健儿。
    为了迎接这次盛大的检阅，苏联各地在一年前就开始了准备工作，人们对这次运动会表现了极大的热情。一共有四千万人参加了选拔赛，比第一届时增加了二千二百万人。
    第二届全苏运动大会的准备工作使得苏联的体育运动生活更加活跃了，它在更加壮大的运动队伍中展开。
    自第一届全苏运动大会后三年来苏联的运动员增加了三百五十万人，仅俄罗斯联邦就新成立了三千多个体育俱乐部。
    在这次运动大会一共要进行二十二个项目的比赛，其中包括摩托车、乒乓球和象棋等。
    瓦卢耶夫指出，这次运动会引起了世界各国体育爱好者的兴趣。前来莫斯科参观这次运动会的不仅有苏联各地的客人而且有许多外宾、许多国际体育组织的负责人和许多国家体育组织的负责人。其中包括阿尔巴尼亚、保加利亚、捷克斯洛伐克、波兰、中国、阿富汗、英国、芬兰、墨西哥、瑞典、瑞士等国的嘉宾。
