Title: Celebrating the 15th Anniversary of Romania's Liberation: Film Reception Held in the Capital
Author: 
Date: 1959-08-22
Page: 4

　　Xinhua News Agency, August 21st: To celebrate the 15th anniversary of Romania's liberation, the Ministry of Culture, the Commission for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries, and the Sino-Romanian Friendship Association held a premiere reception in Beijing this afternoon for the Romanian film "Behind the Pine Forest."
　　Before the film screening, Qu Wu, Deputy Director of the Commission for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries, and Romanian Ambassador to China, Chivu Stoica, delivered speeches. Qu Wu said: The exchange of film art between China and Romania has become a bridge of friendship between the peoples of China and Romania, and an important way for the Chinese people to learn from the Romanian people. Ambassador Chivu Stoica introduced the development of Romanian cultural undertakings, especially the film industry, in his speech. He pointed out that cultural exchange occupies an important position in the fraternal cooperative relations between the Romanian and Chinese peoples. This fraternal cooperative relationship between Romania and China helps to consolidate the unity of socialist camp countries. Zhu Ming, Secretary-General of the Sino-Romanian Friendship Association, and diplomatic envoys from various countries to China attended today's film reception.
　　The story of the film "Behind the Pine Forest" vividly depicts the scene of the heroic Romanian people fighting against the German fascist army.

### 庆祝罗马尼亚解放十五周年  首都举行电影招待会

1959-08-22
第4版()
专栏：

　　庆祝罗马尼亚解放十五周年
    首都举行电影招待会
    新华社21日讯　为了庆祝罗马尼亚解放十五周年，文化部、对外文化联络委员会和中国罗马尼亚友好协会，今天下午在北京举行罗马尼亚影片“在松林的后面”首映招待会。
    在放映电影以前，对外文化联络委员会副主任屈武和罗马尼亚驻我国大使查哈勒斯库先后讲话。屈武说：中罗两国电影艺术的交流，已经成为中罗两国人民之间的友谊的桥梁，也成了中国人民向罗马尼亚人民学习的重要途径。查哈勒斯库大使在讲话中介绍了罗马尼亚文化事业特别是电影事业发展的情况，他指出，文化交流在罗中两国人民兄弟般的合作关系中占有重要地位。罗中两国这种兄弟般的合作关系，有助于巩固社会主义阵营各国的团结。出席今天电影招待会的，有中罗友好协会秘书长朱明和各国驻华使节等。
    “在松林的后面”这部影片的故事，生动地描述了英勇的罗马尼亚人民同德国法西斯军队进行斗争的情景。
