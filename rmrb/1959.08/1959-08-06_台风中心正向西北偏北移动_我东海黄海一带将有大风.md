Title: Typhoon Center Moving Northwest by North, Gale Force Winds Expected in the East China Sea and Yellow Sea Areas
Author:
Date: 1959-08-06
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, 5th: The Central Meteorological Science Research Institute issued a typhoon warning at 8 PM today:

    The typhoon in the Pacific Ocean was centered at 2 PM today approximately 100 kilometers north of Okinawa Island, near 27.5 degrees North latitude and 128.4 degrees East longitude. The typhoon center is moving northwest by north at a speed of 9 to 11 knots per hour. The maximum wind force near the center is force 12. It is expected that in the next 24 hours, wind force in the northern part of the East China Sea and the southern part of the Yellow Sea will increase to force 8 to 10, wind force in the central part of the Yellow Sea will increase to force 6 to 8, and wind force along the Jiangsu coast will be force 6 to 7. In the next 48 hours, there will be force 8 to 10 gales in the central and southern Yellow Sea, and force 6 to 8 gales in the northern Yellow Sea and along the Shandong Peninsula coast. There will also be heavy rain in eastern Shandong Peninsula.

### 台风中心正向西北偏北移动  我东海黄海一带将有大风

1959-08-06
第1版()
专栏：

    台风中心正向西北偏北移动
    我东海黄海一带将有大风
    新华社5日讯　今日下午八时，中央气象科学研究所发布台风警报：
    太平洋上的台风，今天下午二时中心位置在冲绳岛北方约一百公里的海面上，即北纬二十七·五度、东经一百二十八·四度附近。台风中心正以每小时九到十一浬的速度向西北偏北方向移动。中心附近最大风力有十二级。预计未来二十四小时内，东海北部、黄海南部风力将加大到八到十级，黄海中部的风力将加大到六到八级，江苏沿海风力有六到七级。未来四十八小时内，黄海中部、南部有八到十级的大风，黄海北部和山东半岛沿海有六到八级的大风。山东半岛东部并将有暴雨。
