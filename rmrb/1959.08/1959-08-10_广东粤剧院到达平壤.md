Title: Guangdong Cantonese Opera Troupe Arrives in Pyongyang
Author:
Date: 1959-08-10
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Pyongyang. According to the 1959 Sino-Korean cultural cooperation plan, the Chinese Guangdong Cantonese Opera Troupe, consisting of 58 members and led by Ma Shizeng, the director of the Cantonese Opera Troupe, and Hong Xiannv, the vice-director, arrived in Pyongyang by train on the 7th. They will visit and perform in various parts of Korea for more than a month.

### 广东粤剧院到达平壤

1959-08-10
第4版()
专栏：

    广东粤剧院到达平壤
    据新华社平壤电　根据1959年中朝文化合作计划，来朝鲜访问演出的中国广东粤剧院一行五十八人，由粤剧院院长马师曾和副院长红线女率领，7日下午乘车到达平壤。他们将在朝鲜各地访问演出一个多月。
