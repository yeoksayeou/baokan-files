Title: U.S. Instigates Chilean Authorities to Detain Cuban Plane Carrying Evidence of Anti-Cuban Conspiracy

Author: 
Date: 1959-08-19
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, Santiago, 17th -- A Cuban plane carrying evidence related to the Dominican dictatorship's involvement in anti-Cuban counter-revolutionary conspiracies, en route to Santiago, was detained today by the Chilean government at Los Cerillos Airport in Santiago. All passengers on board, including Cuban Minister of Transportation Camacho and several journalists, were also detained. It is reported that the United States exerted strong pressure on Chile in order to prevent the aforementioned Cuban plane from landing.

### 美国指使智利当局　扣留一架古巴飞机　机上载有反古巴阴谋的罪证

1959-08-19
第5版()
专栏：

　　美国指使智利当局
　　扣留一架古巴飞机
　　机上载有反古巴阴谋的罪证
    新华社圣地亚哥17日电　古巴当局的一架载运有关多米尼加独裁政权参与古巴反革命阴谋的证据前往圣地亚哥的飞机今天被智利政府扣留在圣地亚哥的洛斯卡里略斯机场。机上的全部乘客，包括古巴运输部长卡马乔和新闻记者多人也都被扣留。据悉，美国曾对智利施加强大的压力，以便阻止上述古巴飞机着陆。
