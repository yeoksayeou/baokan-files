Title: Vietnamese Ministry of Labor Inspection Group Returns Home
Author:
Date: 1959-08-10
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency: The cadre inspection group from the Ministry of Labor of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam concluded their inspection visit to China and returned home by train in Hengyang, Hunan Province on the 7th.

### 越南劳动部考察团回国

1959-08-10
第4版()
专栏：

    越南劳动部考察团回国
    据新华社讯　越南民主共和国劳动部干部考察团结束了在中国的考察访问，7日在湖南省衡阳乘火车回国。
