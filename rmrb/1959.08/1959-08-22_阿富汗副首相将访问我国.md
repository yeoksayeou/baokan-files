Title: Afghan Deputy Prime Minister to Visit Our Country
Author:
Date: 1959-08-22
Page: 1

    Xinhua News Agency, 20th: At the invitation of Premier Zhou Enlai of the People's Republic of China, the Deputy Prime Minister and Minister of Foreign Affairs of Afghanistan, Sardar Mohammed Naim, will pay a friendly visit to our country in early September.

### 阿富汗副首相将访问我国

1959-08-22
第1版()
专栏：

    阿富汗副首相将访问我国
    新华社20日讯　阿富汗副首相兼外交大臣萨达尔·穆罕默德·纳伊姆应中华人民共和国周恩来总理的邀请，将于9月上旬来我国作友好访问。
