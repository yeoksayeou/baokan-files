Title: Chairman Liu Shaoqi Meets President Ho Chi Minh
Author: 
Date: 1959-08-22
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, August 21st: Chairman Liu Shaoqi met and hosted a banquet for President Ho Chi Minh this evening.

Present were comrades Zhou Enlai, Peng Zhen, Chen Yi, Nie Rongzhen, Wang Jiaxiang, He Wei, and others. (Attached picture)
