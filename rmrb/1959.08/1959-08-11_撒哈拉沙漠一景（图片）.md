　　The Pulse of Africa

　　A Scene in the Sahara Desert
　　Originally published in Paris "Express"
　　The French government's decision to test atomic weapons in the Sahara Desert region has been universally opposed by the African people. The Secretariat of the Asian-African People's Solidarity Council has called on people in various parts of Africa to hold large-scale demonstrations and protests.

1959-08-11
第5版()
专栏：非洲的脉搏

　　撒哈拉沙漠一景
