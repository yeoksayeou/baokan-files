Title: Think More for the Passengers
Author: Wang Junqi
Date: 1959-08-21
Page: 2

    Think More for the Passengers
    Whenever I visit a new city, I always hope to check the bus or tram station to find the place I'm looking for, but I'm always disappointed. The signs at the stations only have the station name, with no clear indication of where the route starts, where it passes through. This reminds me of the signs at the bus stops in Beijing, which clearly show the route number, stops, passing stations, and the names of the starting and ending stations, making it easy to understand at a glance. I suggest that all regions learn and promote this method.
              Sichuan　Wang Junqi

### 多为旅客想
王俊旗
1959-08-21
第2版()
专栏：来信

    多为旅客想
    我每到过一些新城市，总希望去公共汽车或电车站查寻一下自己要找的地方，但是很失望，车站的牌子上只有一个站名，路线起自何地、经过哪里，完全没有写明白。这使我想起北京的公共汽车站的牌子，它有线路号数、停站、经过站、始发和终点站的名字，使人看了一目了然。建议各地学习和推广这一办法。
              四川　王俊旗
