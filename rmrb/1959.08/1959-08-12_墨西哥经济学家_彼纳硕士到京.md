Title: Mexican Economist Pena Arrives in Beijing

Author: 
Date: 1959-08-12
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, 11th - Mexican economist Moises de la Pena arrived in Beijing by plane today. He is visiting China at the invitation of the Chinese People's Association for Friendship with Foreign Countries.

### 墨西哥经济学家　彼纳硕士到京

1959-08-12
第4版()
专栏：

　　墨西哥经济学家
　　彼纳硕士到京
    新华社11日讯　墨西哥经济学家莫宇赛斯·德·拉·彼纳硕士，今天乘飞机到北京。他是应中国人民对外文化协会的邀请来中国访问的。
