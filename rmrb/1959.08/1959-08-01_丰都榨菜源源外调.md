Title: Fuling Mustard Pickles from Fengdu are Shipped Out Continuously
Author:
Date: 1959-08-01
Page: 2

Fengdu County in Sichuan is continuously shipping its Fuling mustard pickles to Shanghai, Tianjin, Jilin, Heilongjiang, Liaoning and other places. This year, the county produced 28,000 dan of mustard pickles. The fragrant and spicy taste of the mustard pickles is made with eight kinds of spices, including white pepper, sand peach, san nai, star anise, magnolia bark, and osmanthus, and the taste is very good.

### 丰都榨菜源源外调

1959-08-01
第2版()
专栏：

    丰都榨菜源源外调
    四川丰都县的榨菜，正源源调往上海、天津、吉林、黑龙江、辽宁等地。这个县今年生产了榨菜二万八千担，榨菜香辣味是用白胡椒、沙桃、三奈、八角、朴桂等八种香料做成的，味道很好。
