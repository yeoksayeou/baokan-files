Title: Korea Holds Student Sports Meet, Youth Perform Highly Ideological Rhythmic Gymnastics
Author:
Date: 1959-08-16
Page: 3

Xinhua News Agency, Pyongyang, August 15th: To celebrate the 14th anniversary of the liberation of Korea on "August 15th," the National Student Sports Meet of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea, with 25,000 young student athletes from all over the country, began in Pyongyang today.

This afternoon, the opening ceremony was held at the Moranbong Stadium, where 15,000 young students performed innovative and highly ideological rhythmic gymnastics. During the one-hour performance, the youth depicted the historical process of the Korean national liberation struggle.

Today, Korean Premier Kim Il Sung and other party and government leaders watched the performance.

### 朝鲜举行学生体育大会  青少年表演高度思想性的艺术体操

1959-08-16
第3版()
专栏：

    朝鲜举行学生体育大会
    青少年表演高度思想性的艺术体操
    据新华社平壤15日电　为庆祝“八·一五”朝鲜解放十四周年，朝鲜民主主义人民共和国各地的二万五千名青少年学生体育选手从今天开始在平壤举行全国学生体育大会。
    今天下午，大会在牡丹峰运动场举行开幕式，一万五千名青少年学生表演了新颖和具有高度思想性的艺术体操。青少年们在一小时的表演中表现了朝鲜民族解放斗争的历史过程。
    今天朝鲜内阁首相金日成等党和政府的领导人观看了表演。
