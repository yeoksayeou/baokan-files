Title: U.S. Aircraft Intrudes into Airspace over Our Xisha Islands for Provocation; We Issue the 61st Serious Warning
Author:
Date: 1959-08-01
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, July 31st: At 11:00 AM on July 31st, a U.S. Navy patrol plane intruded into the airspace over Yongxing Island in the Xisha Islands of Guangdong Province. Regarding this military provocation by U.S. military aircraft, the spokesperson of our Ministry of Foreign Affairs was instructed to issue the 61st serious warning.

### 美机侵入我西沙群岛上空挑衅  我提出第61次严重警告

1959-08-01
第1版()
专栏：

    美机侵入我西沙群岛上空挑衅
    我提出第61次严重警告
    新华社31日讯　7月31日十一时，美国海军巡逻机一架，侵入我广东省西沙群岛的永兴岛上空。对于美国军用飞机的这种军事挑衅，我外交部发言人奉命提出第六十一次严重警告。
