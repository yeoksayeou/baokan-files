Title: Bratsk Morning
Author: Aòsītèluōwōyī
Date: 1959-08-02
Page: 3

    Bratsk Morning
    Soviet Union, Aòsītèluōwōyī Little bugs flit up and down, bumping east and crashing west, in the misty morning in the riverbank depression… The little bugs seem to fly out of a bag, or like fine raindrops sprinkling in the air. The eaves have already towered up, on the Angara River, look, a survey team girl, with a veil tied on her head, stands on the rock overlooking the bottom. Just below, the concrete dam pier stands toweringly, fighting and wrestling with the river, the waves captured by the dam struggling violently and roaring. Just below, the three shifts of the overhead crane have already started working… The sun climbed onto the foundation pit of the dam, as if coming to a new world.
    (Translated by Fang Xuan from the Soviet “Literary Gazette” on July 16) (Attached picture) Construction site of Bratsk Hydropower Station Painted by Kupriyanov, Soviet Union

### 布拉次克的早晨
奥斯特罗沃依
1959-08-02
第3版()
专栏：

    布拉次克的早晨
    苏联  奥斯特罗沃依小虫儿上下翻飞，东撞西冲，在那河岸的洼地上晨雾蒙蒙……小虫儿好像从袋子里飞出来，又好像点点细雨飘洒在空中。房檐已高耸起来了，在安加拉河上，看啊，一位勘探队的姑娘，头上系着一面纱巾，站在山岩上俯视着下方。就在那下方，混凝土坝墩巍然屹立，同河水撕杀，搏斗，那被堤坝俘掳了的波涛，暴跳着挣扎，怒吼。就在那下方，高架起重机三班的工作已经开始……太阳爬上了堤坝的基坑，好像来到一个新的天地。
    （方萱译自7月16日苏联“文学报”）（附图片）布拉次克水电站工地　苏联　库普里扬诺夫绘
