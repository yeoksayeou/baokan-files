Title: India Celebrates Independence Day
Author:
Date: 1959-08-16
Page: 4

    Xinhua News Agency, New Delhi, August 15th. India celebrated its Independence Day throughout the country today. It was on this day twelve years ago that India gained independence from Britain.
    A mass rally was held at Red Fort Square in the Indian capital. Prime Minister Nehru spoke at the rally.
    On the eve of Independence Day, President Prasad congratulated the people of the country. Today, he reviewed a parade in Hyderabad, the capital of Andhra Pradesh.
    Vice President Radhakrishnan held a banquet this afternoon at the Presidential Palace to entertain officials, prominent figures in society, and foreign diplomatic envoys to India.
    Indian newspapers have all published special editions today to celebrate the festival.

### 印度庆祝独立日

1959-08-16
第4版()
专栏：

    印度庆祝独立日
    新华社新德里15日电　印度全国各地今天庆祝独立日。十二年前的这一天，印度从英国那里取得了独立。
    在印度首都红堡广场举行了群众集会。尼赫鲁总理在集会上讲了话。
    在独立日前夕，普拉沙德总统向全国人民祝贺。今天，他在安得拉邦首府海得拉巴检阅了游行队伍。
    拉达克里希南副总统今天下午在总统府举行宴会招待官员、社会著名人士和各国驻印度的外交使节。
    印度各报今天都发表特刊庆祝这个节日。
