Title: All-China Women's Federation Holds Banquet to Welcome Women Representatives from Algeria and Other Countries
Author:
Date: 1959-08-15
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, August 14th: The All-China Women's Federation held a banquet this evening to welcome women's delegations from Algeria, Tunisia, and Singapore who are visiting our country.

Li Dequan, Vice-Chairman of the All-China Women's Federation, extended a warm welcome to the women representatives from afar at the banquet. She enthusiastically raised her glass to wish the women sisters of the world unite and strive for a happy life for mankind.

Among those attending this evening's banquet were members of the Presidium of the All-China Women's Federation, Shen Ziji, Cao Mengjun, Vice-President of the Chinese People's Association for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries, Ding Xilin, and负责人负责人from the Beijing Women's Federation.

### 我全国妇联举行宴会　欢迎阿尔及利亚等国妇女代表

1959-08-15
第4版()
专栏：

　　我全国妇联举行宴会
　　欢迎阿尔及利亚等国妇女代表
    新华社14日讯　全国妇联今天晚间举行宴会，欢迎前来我国访问的阿尔及利亚、突尼斯和新加坡的妇女代表团。
    全国妇联副主席李德全在宴会上向远道而来的各国妇女代表表示热烈欢迎，她热情地举杯祝全世界妇女姊妹团结起来，为争取人类幸福的生活而努力。
    参加今晚宴会的有全国妇联主席团委员沈兹九、曹孟君，中国人民对外文化协会副会长丁西林，以及北京市妇联负责人等。
