Title: Scenes of Fighting Drought
Author: Cui Wanhe
Date: 1959-08-10
Page: 3

    Scenes of Fighting Drought
    Who waves the flag and cracks the whip,
    Commanding the Yellow Dragon to spout clear springs?
    Not gods, not immortals,
    Commune members climb the canal to crank the sluice gate.
           ＊
    Whose skillful hands weave long threads,
    Embroidering squares of green brocade with mandarin ducks?
    Young women, busily changing rows,
    A gentle breeze stirs their colorful clothing.
           ＊
    Who casts down ten thousand bolts of silk,
    Setting squares of green brocade with silver edges?
    Commune members, watering the autumn fields,
    Streams of water wind around the fields.
           ＊
    Who plucks down ten thousand stars,
    Making a thousand *li* of autumn fields shine bright?
    Water fills the canals, lamps line the dikes,
    The army fighting drought launches a night campaign.
      ——Cui Wanhe

### 抗旱小景
崔万和
1959-08-10
第3版()
专栏：

    抗旱小景
    是谁摇旗又挥鞭，
    喝令黄龙吐清泉？
    不是神，不是仙，
    社员登渠把闸扳。
           ＊
    是谁手巧针线长，
    方方绿锦绣鸳鸯？
    姑娘们，改畦忙，
    微风拂动花衣裳。
           ＊
    是谁撒下万匹练，
    块块绿锦镶银边？
    社员们，浇秋田，
    条条渠水绕田转。
           ＊
    是谁摘下万颗星，
    千里秋田一片明？
    水满渠，堤满灯，
    抗旱大军夜兴兵。
      ——崔万和
