Title: National People's Congress Delegate Zhang Yuanji Passes Away
Author:
Date: 1959-08-18
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, Shanghai, 16th — Mr. Zhang Yuanji, Delegate to the National People's Congress, Director of the Shanghai Research Institute of Culture and History, and Chairman of the Joint State-Private-Owned Commercial Press, passed away in Shanghai at 8:04 PM on the 14th due to illness. He was 93 years old. A funeral committee has been formed, consisting of Ke Qingshi, Zhu De, Shen Junru, Li Weihan, Chen Shutong, Chen Yi, Guo Moruo, Peng Zhen, Huang Yanpei, and others, and the encoffining ceremony is scheduled for the afternoon of the 17th.

### 全国人民代表大会代表张元济逝世

1959-08-18
第6版()
专栏：

　　全国人民代表大会代表张元济逝世
    新华社上海16日电　全国人民代表大会代表、上海市文史馆馆长、公私合营商务印书馆董事长张元济先生，14日下午八时四分在上海病逝。享年九十三岁。现在已经由柯庆施、朱德、沈钧儒、李维汉、陈叔通、陈毅、郭沫若、彭真、黄炎培等组成治丧委员会，定17日下午举行大殓。
