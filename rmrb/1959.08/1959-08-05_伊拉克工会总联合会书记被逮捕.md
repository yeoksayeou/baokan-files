Title: Iraqi Trade Union Federation Secretary Arrested
Author:
Date: 1959-08-05
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Baghdad, August 4th: According to Iraqi newspapers, Sadiq Fallahi, Secretary of the Iraqi Trade Union Federation, has been arrested by the authorities. The report also stated that the Iraqi Trade Union Federation and some of its branches in various parts of Iraq have been closed down by the authorities.

### 伊拉克工会总联合会书记被逮捕

1959-08-05
第4版()
专栏：

    伊拉克工会总联合会书记被逮捕
    新华社4日讯　巴格达消息：据伊拉克报纸报道，伊拉克工会总联合会书记萨迪克·费拉希已经被当局逮捕。消息还说，伊拉克工会总联合会和它在伊拉克各地的一些分会已经被当局封闭。
