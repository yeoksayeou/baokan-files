Title: Commander-in-Chief of the Cuban Armed Forces to Santiago
Author:
Date: 1959-08-18
Page: 5

　　Cuban Armed Forces Commander-in-Chief
　　Goes to Santiago
Xinhua News Agency, Santiago, August 16th: The Cuban delegation participating in the Meeting of Foreign Ministers of the Americas officially announced that the Commander-in-Chief of the Cuban Revolutionary Armed Forces, Raúl Castro, will arrive in Santiago on the 17th.
　　The plane carrying Raúl Castro has various documents, photographs, and other evidence proving that the Trujillo dictatorship in the Dominican Republic masterminded and participated in the most recent anti-Cuban conspiracy that was crushed.
　　Raúl arrived in the Venezuelan capital Caracas on the 16th and met with Venezuelan President Betancourt.

### 古巴武装部队总司令　去圣地亚哥

1959-08-18
第5版()
专栏：

　　古巴武装部队总司令
　　去圣地亚哥
    新华社圣地亚哥16日电　参加美洲外长会议的古巴代表团正式宣布，古巴革命武装部队总司令劳尔·卡斯特罗将在17日到达圣地亚哥。
    劳尔·卡斯特罗乘坐的飞机带有各种文件、照片和其他证据，证明多米尼加特鲁希略独裁政权策动和参与最近一次被粉碎的反古巴阴谋。
    劳尔已经在16日到达委内瑞拉首都加拉加斯，并且会见了委内瑞拉总统贝坦科尔特。
