Title: Major General Sok Leaves Kunming to Return Home
Author:
Date: 1959-08-19
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Kunming, August 18th: Major General Sok, member of the World Peace Council, Vice-Chairman of the All-India Peace Council, and renowned bacteriologist, concluded his visit to China and left Kunming by plane this morning, returning to his country via Rangoon.

Major General Sok came to China for a visit on July 4th at the invitation of Guo Moruo, Chairman of the Chinese People's Committee for the Defense of World Peace.

### 索克少将离昆明返国

1959-08-19
第4版()
专栏：

　　索克少将离昆明返国
    据新华社昆明18日电　世界和平理事会理事、全印和平理事会副主席、著名细菌学家索克少将结束了在中国的访问，今天早晨乘飞机离开昆明，取道仰光回国。
    索克少将是应中国人民保卫世界和平委员会主席郭沫若的邀请，在7月4日来中国访问的。
