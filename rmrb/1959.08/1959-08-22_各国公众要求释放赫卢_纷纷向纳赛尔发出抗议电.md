Title: Public from Various Countries Demand the Release of Helou, Sending Protests to Nasser

Author:

Date: 1959-08-22
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, Prague, August 18th - According to the Czechoslovak News Agency, the Secretariat of the World Federation of Democratic Youth sent a telegram to the President of the United Arab Republic, Nasser, protesting the arrest and imprisonment of Helou, the leader of the Lebanese National Liberation Movement, by the UAR authorities.

The telegram pointed out that Helou is not only an enthusiastic and loyal fighter for the interests of the Lebanese people, but also for the interests of all Arab peoples.

Xinhua News Agency, Beirut, August 20th - According to a report in today's Lebanese "Call of the People" newspaper, the movement demanding the release of Helou, the leader of the Lebanese Communist Party and a well-known journalist, who was arrested by the UAR authorities in Damascus in June this year, is continuing.

The newspaper said that in addition to protest telegrams from Lebanon, some foreign organizations have also telegraphed UAR President Nasser, protesting the arrest of Helou and demanding his release. According to the newspaper, protests to Nasser from abroad include the President of the All-India Youth Federation, the Canadian Socialist Youth League, the Brazilian Communist Youth League, the Finnish Democratic Youth League, and Arab expatriates living abroad.

Xinhua News Agency, Tirana, August 20th - The Albanian newspaper "People's Voice" today demanded in a commentary the release of Helou, a prominent Lebanese patriot who was recently arrested by the authorities of the United Arab Republic.

The commentary emphasized that the Albanian public, along with all those who strive for peace and defend democratic freedoms, demand an end to the persecution of Helou.

### 各国公众要求释放赫卢  纷纷向纳赛尔发出抗议电

1959-08-22
第5版()
专栏：

　　各国公众要求释放赫卢
    纷纷向纳赛尔发出抗议电
    新华社布拉格18日电　据捷克斯洛伐克通讯社报道，世界民主青年联盟秘书处致电阿拉伯联合共和国总统纳赛尔，抗议阿联当局逮捕并监禁黎巴嫩民族解放运动的领袖赫卢。
    电报指出，赫卢不仅是争取黎巴嫩人民的利益、而且也是争取全体阿拉伯人民的利益的热情而忠诚的战士。
    新华社贝鲁特20日电　据黎巴嫩“呼声报”今天报道，要求释放今年6月在大马士革被阿联当局逮捕的黎巴嫩共产党领袖、著名的新闻记者赫卢的运动还在继续进行中。
    这家报纸说，除了从黎巴嫩国内发出抗议电报以外，某些外国组织也打电报给阿联总统纳赛尔，抗议逮捕赫卢的行动，并且要求把他释放。据这家报纸说，从外国打电报给纳赛尔表示抗议的包括全印青年联合会主席，加拿大社会主义青年团，巴西共产主义青年联盟，芬兰民主青年联盟以及住在外国的阿拉伯侨民。
    新华社地拉那20日电　阿尔巴尼亚“人民之声报”今天在评论中要求释放不久前被阿拉伯联合共和国当局逮捕的黎巴嫩杰出的爱国者赫卢。
    评论强调说，阿尔巴尼亚公众和一切争取和平、保卫民主自由的人们一道，要求停止对赫卢的迫害。
