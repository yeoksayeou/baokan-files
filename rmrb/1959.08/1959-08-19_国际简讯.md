International Briefs

Asian-African Economic Conference to be held at the end of the year
    Colombo Dispatch: According to a report in the Ceylon "Daily News" on the 17th, Ceylon Prime Minister Bandaranaike announced on the 16th that the Asian-African Economic Conference will be held at the end of this year.
    Bandaranaike said that the Prime Ministers of the Colombo Conference countries (Ceylon, India, Pakistan, Indonesia) are currently handling certain details of the draft agenda for the conference.
　  Burmese Minister of Finance Presents 1959-1960 Fiscal Budget
    Yangon Dispatch: Burmese Minister of Finance U Kyaw Nyein presented the Burmese government's 1959-1960 fiscal budget to the House of Representatives on the afternoon of the 17th.
    According to this fiscal budget, total revenue is 1,312,731,000 kyats, and total expenditure is 1,319,451,000 kyats. The deficit is 6,720,000 kyats. The deficit for the 1958-1959 fiscal year was 47,639,000 kyats. According to this fiscal budget, expenditures for defense and police account for approximately 41% of total expenditures.

### 国际简讯

1959-08-19
第5版()
专栏：国际简讯

　　国际简讯
　  亚非经济会议将在年底召开
    科伦坡讯　据锡兰“每日新闻”17日报道，锡兰总理班达拉奈克16日宣布，亚非经济会议在今年年底召开。
    班达拉奈克说，目前科伦坡会议国家（锡兰、印度、巴基斯坦、印度尼西亚）的总理正在处理有关会议议程草案的某些细节问题。
　  缅财政部长提出1959—1960年度财政概算
    仰光讯　缅甸财政部长吴觉宁17日下午在议会代表院提出了缅甸政府1959—1960年度的财政概算。
    根据这个财政概算，总收入是十三亿一千二百七十三万一千缅元，总支出是十三亿一千九百四十五万一千缅元。赤字是六百七十二万缅元。1958年—1959年度的赤字是四千七百六十三万九千缅元。根据这个财政概算，用于防御和警察方面的开支占总开支的大约41％。
