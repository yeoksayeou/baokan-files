    Madoi County Abundant in Aquatic Products
    Preliminary surveys have shown that the Jialing Lake, Eling Lake, Black Sea, Yellow River along, Xinxing Sea, Chalang Sea, and other areas of Madoi County, Hainan Tibetan Autonomous Prefecture, Qinghai, are extremely rich in aquatic resources, not only in terms of large reserves but also in a variety of species.
    In Jialing Lake, large fish measuring two to three feet or even five to six feet long often appear in large schools of thousands of fish. There are also many fish schools along the Yellow River. A small net of twenty meters long can catch four to five thousand catties of fish in a morning with seven or eight people. In addition to the Huang fish, there are also bighead carp, cucumber fish, and a fish with three pairs of barbels.
    Heilongjiang Increases Medicinal Herb Planting
    Medicinal herb production in Heilongjiang Province occupies a very important position in the diversified economy of various regions this year. The area planted with medicinal herbs in the province reaches more than 52,000 mu, more than double that of last year. The variety of species is also increasing, including more than ten types of wild-to-domesticated species such as Astragalus membranaceus, Codonopsis pilosula, and Fritillaria thunbergii; and more than thirty types of species introduced from other provinces such as white peony, Atractylodes macrocephala, and Rehmannia glutinosa. The province also raises more than 700 deer. Now is the peak season for collecting various medicinal materials, and various regions are sending a large number of people to collect them.
    Re-collecting and Re-threshing Early Rice
    After basically completing the rush harvest of early rice, Liling County, Hunan Province, organized the masses to re-thresh and re-collect, recovering more than 500,000 catties of rice.

    简明新闻
    玛多县水产丰富
    据初步勘察证明：青海海南藏族自治州玛多县的加陵湖、鄂陵湖、黑海、黄河沿、新兴海、查郎海等地水产资源非常丰富，不仅储量大，而且品种多。
    加陵湖中二、三尺长甚至五、六尺长的大鱼，常常成群结队，一群就有几千尾。黄河沿的鱼群也很多，一个二十米长的小网，七、八个人一上午就可捕到四、五千斤鱼。除了湟鱼以外，还有大头鱼、黄瓜鱼以及一种带有三对触须的鱼。
    黑龙江增种药材
    黑龙江省今年的药材生产在各地多种经营中占很重要的地位。全省种植的药材面积达五万二千余亩，比去年增加一倍多，品种也在增加，其中，野生变家植的有黄茋、党参、贝母等十余种；引种外省的有白芍、白术、生地等三十余种。全省还饲养了七百多头鹿。现在正是各种药材采集的旺季，各地正派大批人力采集。
    复收复打早稻
    湖南省醴陵县基本结束早稻抢收工作后，组织群众复打复收，收回稻谷五十多万斤。
