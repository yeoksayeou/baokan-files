Title: Puerto Ricans in New York Demonstrate Against U.S. Rule of Puerto Rico
Author:
Date: 1959-08-05
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, San Juan, 4th - Puerto Rican political figures in New York are demonstrating against U.S. colonial rule over Puerto Rico.

    According to a report in the Puerto Rican "Justice News" on July 28th, the Puerto Rican Nationalist Party, the Puerto Rican National Movement, the Revolutionary Action Party, and the Free Puerto Rico Association held continuous demonstrations and rallies in New York on July 25th and 26th, opposing the United States' brutal rule and military intervention in Puerto Rico for sixty-one years.

### 在纽约的波多黎各人示威  抗议美国统治波多黎各

1959-08-05
第4版()
专栏：

    在纽约的波多黎各人示威
    抗议美国统治波多黎各
    据新华社4日讯　圣胡安消息：正在纽约的波多黎各政界人士举行示威游行抗议美国对波多黎各的殖民统治。
    据波多黎各“公正报”7月28日报道，波多黎各国民党、波多黎各民族运动、革命行动党以及自由波多黎各协会等政党7月25日和26日在纽约连续举行了示威游行和集会，反对美国对波多黎各六十一年的残暴统治和军事干涉。
