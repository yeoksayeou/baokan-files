Title: Vietnam and Thailand Sign Agreement: Overseas Vietnamese in Thailand to Return Home Regularly
Author: 
Date: 1959-08-16
Page: 3

Xinhua News Agency, Yangon, August 14th: Nguyen Van Thu, head of the delegation of the Red Cross Society of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam, and Phra Tit Ratana Sathi, head of the delegation of the Thai Red Cross Society, signed an agreement here today regarding the repatriation of Vietnamese nationals in Thailand. The work of repatriating Vietnamese nationals from Thailand will begin in January 1960 and will be completed within three years.

### 越泰两国签订协议  旅泰越侨定期返国

1959-08-16
第3版()
专栏：

    越泰两国签订协议
    旅泰越侨定期返国
    据新华社仰光14日电　越南民主共和国红十字会代表团团长阮文秋和泰国红十字会代表团团长拍提腊那沙今天在这里签订了关于旅泰越南侨民回国的协议。遣送旅泰越南侨民回国的工作将从1960年1月开始。遣送旅泰越侨回国的工作将在三年内完成。
