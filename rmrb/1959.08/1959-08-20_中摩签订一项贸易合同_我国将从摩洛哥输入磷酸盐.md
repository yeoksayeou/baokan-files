Title: China and Morocco Sign a Trade Agreement: China to Import Phosphates from Morocco
Author:
Date: 1959-08-20
Page: 5

　　Xinhua News Agency, Rabat, August 18th. The China National Minerals Corporation and the Moroccan Phosphate Office signed a contract in Rabat on the 17th, based on the trade agreement signed between China and Morocco last year. According to this one-year contract, China will import 500,000 tons of phosphates from Morocco, with a total value of 2.24 billion Moroccan francs.
　　Zhang Tingyu, Commercial Counselor of the Chinese Embassy in Morocco, and the representative of the North African Phosphate Mining Company signed the contract on behalf of the China National Minerals Corporation and the Moroccan Phosphate Office, respectively.

### 中摩签订一项贸易合同　我国将从摩洛哥输入磷酸盐

1959-08-20
第5版()
专栏：

　　中摩签订一项贸易合同
　　我国将从摩洛哥输入磷酸盐
    新华社拉巴特18日电　中国矿产公司和摩洛哥磷酸盐办公室根据中摩两国去年签订的贸易协定，17日在拉巴特签订了一项合同。根据这项为期一年的合同，中国将从摩洛哥输入总值二十二亿四千万摩洛哥法郎的五十万吨磷酸盐。
    中国驻摩洛哥大使馆商务参赞张廷禹和北非磷酸盐矿务公司的代表分别代表中国矿产公司和摩洛哥磷酸盐办公室签订了这项合同。
