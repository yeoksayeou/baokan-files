Title: National People's Congress Deputy Leng Yu Passes Away
Author:
Date: 1959-08-20
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Nanjing, August 19th: Leng Yu, Deputy to the National People's Congress, Vice Governor of Jiangsu Province, Vice Chairman of the Jiangsu Provincial Committee of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference, and Chairman of the Jiangsu Provincial Working Committee of the China Democratic National Construction Association, passed away in Nanjing at 20:03 on August 18th due to acute myocardial infarction, despite medical treatment.

Leng Yu was a native of Zhenjiang City, Jiangsu Province, and was 78 years old this year. People from all walks of life in Jiangsu Province have formed a funeral committee and will hold a public memorial service at 9:00 am on the 20th.

### 全国人民代表大会代表冷遹逝世

1959-08-20
第4版()
专栏：

　　全国人民代表大会代表冷遹逝世
    新华社南京19日电　全国人民代表大会代表、江苏省副省长、中国人民政治协商会议江苏省委员会副主席、中国民主建国会江苏省工作委员会主任委员冷遹，因患亚急性心肌梗死症，经医治无效，于8月18日二十时零三分在南京逝世。
    冷遹是江苏省镇江市人，今年七十八岁。江苏省各界人士已经组成了治丧委员会，将在20日上午九时举行公祭。
