Title: North Korean Exhibition Opens in Beijing Showcasing the Glorious Achievements of the "Chollima" Era
Author:
Date: 1959-08-06
Page: 4
    Exhibiting the Brilliant Achievements of the "Chollima" Era
    North Korean Exhibition Opens in Beijing

    Xinhua News Agency, August 5th: The "Chollima" Exhibition of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea, showcasing the brilliant achievements of the Korean people during the upsurge of socialist construction, grandly opened this morning at the Beijing Working People's Cultural Palace.

    Li Dequan, President of the Sino-Korean Friendship Association, and Li Yongho, the North Korean Ambassador to China, enthusiastically pointed out in their speeches at the opening ceremony that the North Korean "Chollima" Exhibition, held on the eve of the 14th anniversary of Korea's "August 15th" liberation, will further strengthen the traditional and profound friendship between the Chinese and Korean peoples and will make a great contribution to the future economic and cultural exchange between the two countries.

    This exhibition is jointly sponsored by the Commission for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries and the Sino-Korean Friendship Association. Qu Wu, Deputy Director of the Commission for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries, presided over today's opening ceremony. Among those who came to congratulate the opening of the exhibition today were Ji Pengfei, Vice Minister of Foreign Affairs; Ding Xilin, Deputy Director of the Commission for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries; Wei Chuantong, Vice President of the Sino-Korean Friendship Association, and other responsible persons from various fields. Envoys and diplomatic officials from socialist brother countries stationed in China also attended the opening ceremony.

### 展示“千里马”时代的辉煌成就  朝鲜展览会在京开幕

1959-08-06
第4版()
专栏：

    展示“千里马”时代的辉煌成就
    朝鲜展览会在京开幕
    据新华社5日讯　显示朝鲜人民在社会主义建设高潮中获得辉煌成就的朝鲜民主主义人民共和国“千里马”展览会，今天上午在北京劳动人民文化宫隆重开幕。
    中国朝鲜友好协会会长李德全和朝鲜驻中国大使李永镐在开幕式的讲话中兴奋地指出，朝鲜“千里马”展览会在朝鲜“八·一五”解放十四周年的前夕展出，它将进一步加强中朝两国人民传统的深厚友谊，并且对两国人民今后的经济和文化交流事业作出巨大贡献。
    这个展览会是由对外文化联络委员会和中国朝鲜友好协会联合举办的。对外文委副主任屈武主持今天的开幕式。今天前往祝贺展览会开幕的，有外交部副部长姬鹏飞，对外文化联络委员会副主任丁西林，中朝友好协会副会长魏传统等各方面的负责人。社会主义兄弟国家驻中国的使节和外交官员也参加了开幕式。
