Title: Strengthen Sino-Mexican Cultural Exchange: "Mexican Film Week" Opens in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1959-08-15
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, August 14th: The "Mexican Film Week," jointly organized by the Chinese People's Association for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries and the China Film Workers Association, opened this afternoon in the capital.

Vice Premier of the State Council Chen Yi and the Mexican film delegation attended the opening ceremony. Also present at today's opening ceremony were Zhang Zhixiang, Vice President of the Chinese People's Association for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries, Xia Yan, Vice Minister of Culture, Chen Bo, Secretary-General of the China Film Workers Association, and prominent figures from the capital's film and drama circles. Diplomatic envoys from various countries to China were also invited to attend the opening ceremony.

Zhang Zhixiang, Vice President of the Chinese People's Association for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries, congratulated the success of the "Mexican Film Week" at the opening ceremony and extended a warm welcome to the Mexican film delegation's visit to our country. In his speech, he said: "Through this Mexican Film Week held in our country and the visit of the Mexican film delegation to our country, the exchanges and friendship between Chinese and Mexican film workers will be strengthened, and it will pave the way for the further development of cultural exchanges between China and Mexico."

Alejandro Galindo, the acting head of the Mexican film delegation and a famous director, spoke at the meeting, saying: Our hearts have always dreamed of visiting this glorious country, and today this dream has come true. We are confident that through the silver screens of both of us, the people of China and Mexico will understand each other. Undoubtedly, this will bring the people of the two countries closer in spirit. After the meeting, the Mexican film "The Braceros" was screened.

### 加强中墨两国文化交流　“墨西哥电影周”在京开幕

1959-08-15
第4版()
专栏：

　　加强中墨两国文化交流
　　“墨西哥电影周”在京开幕
    据新华社14日讯　由中国人民对外文化协会和中国电影工作者联谊会联合举办的“墨西哥电影周”，今天下午在首都举行开幕式。
    国务院副总理陈毅和墨西哥电影代表团参加了开幕式。参加今天开幕式的还有中国人民对外文化协会副会长张致祥、文化部副部长夏衍、中国电影工作者联谊会秘书长陈播和首都电影、戏剧界著名人士。各国驻华使节也应邀出席了开幕式。
    中国人民对外文化协会副会长张致祥在开幕式上祝贺“墨西哥电影周”的成功，并对墨西哥电影代表团来我国访问表示热烈欢迎。他在致词中说：“通过这次在我国举行的墨西哥电影周和墨西哥电影代表团来我国访问，将加强中、墨两国电影工作者的往来和友谊，并将为中、墨两国之间文化交流的进一步发展开辟道路。”
    墨西哥电影代表团代理团长、著名导演亚历杭德罗·加林多在会上讲话，他说：我们的心曾一直梦想访问这个光荣的国家，而今天这个梦想已经实现了。我们确信通过我们双方的银幕，会使中、墨两国人民彼此了解。无疑的，这将使两国人民在精神上更加接近。会后，放映了墨西哥影片“偷渡的苦工”。
