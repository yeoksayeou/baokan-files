Title: Vice Premier Chen Yi Receives Chilean Writer
Author:
Date: 1959-08-19
Page: 4

　　Xinhua News Agency, 18th: Vice Premier Chen Yi received the Chilean writer Lautaro Yañez and his wife this morning. The host and guests had a cordial and friendly conversation.
    Present at the meeting were Zhu Boshen, Deputy Secretary-General of the Chinese People's Association for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries, and Wang Yafan, Deputy Secretary-General of the Chinese Writers Association.

### 陈毅副总理接见智利作家

1959-08-19
第4版()
专栏：

　　陈毅副总理接见智利作家
    新华社18日讯　陈毅副总理今天上午接见智利作家拉乌达罗·杨加斯和他的夫人。宾主进行了亲切友好的谈话。
    接见时在座的，有中国人民对外文化协会副秘书长朱伯深，中国作家协会副秘书长王亚凡。
