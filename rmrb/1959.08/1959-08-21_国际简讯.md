    International Briefs

    "Baghdad Pact" Group Changes its Signboard
    Ankara Dispatch: The "Baghdad Pact" group, an instrument of U.S. imperialism in the Middle East, formally announced its renaming as the "Central Treaty Organization" on the evening of the 19th.
    According to Western news agencies, the reason for using this new signboard is to indicate that this group is the central link of the three aggressive blocs controlled by U.S. imperialism, located between the North Atlantic Treaty Organization and the Southeast Asia Treaty Organization.

    Number of Japanese Cancer Patients Dying Daily Increases
    Tokyo Dispatch: The number of people who died from cancer last year reached 83,029, ranking third among the diseases causing the most deaths. Kyodo News Agency estimates that the number of people dying from cancer in Japan will increase this year and will rank first among the diseases causing the most deaths.

    Pakistan Initially Uses Domestic Newsprint to Print Newspapers
    Karachi Dispatch: According to the "Karachi Morning News" on the 19th, the English-language daily "Dacca Morning News" was printed on the 18th using newsprint produced in Pakistan. This is the first time that a Pakistani newspaper has been printed using domestically produced newsprint. The newsprint used by the "Dacca Morning News" was produced in a paper mill newly established in the Khulna area of East Pakistan, which began trial production at the beginning of this month and plans to officially produce newsprint by the end of September this year.

### 国际简讯

1959-08-21
第5版()
专栏：国际简讯

    国际简讯
    “巴格达条约”集团换招牌
    安卡拉讯　美帝国主义在中东的侵略工具“巴格达条约”集团，在19日晚间正式宣告改名为“中央条约组织”。
    据西方通讯社透露，改用这个新招牌是为了表明，这个集团是美帝国主义控制的三个侵略集团的中央环节，它位于北大西洋公约组织和东南亚条约组织的中间。
    日本患癌症病人死亡人数日增
    东京讯　去年因癌症而死亡的人数达八万三千零二十九人，在造成死亡最多的病种中占第三位。共同社估计，今年日本因患癌症而死亡的人数还要增加，将在造成死亡最多的病种中占第一位。
　　巴基斯坦初用国产白报纸印报
    卡拉奇讯　据“卡拉奇晨报”19日报道，英文日报“达卡晨报”18日用巴基斯坦生产的白报纸刊印。这是巴基斯坦报纸第一次用本国出产的白报纸刊印。“达卡晨报”使用的白报纸是在东巴基斯坦库尔纳地方新近建立的一家造纸厂在本月初开始试行生产的，它准备在今年9月底正式生产白报纸。
