Title: Monument to Heroes of the Mongolian People's Army Unveiled in Choibalsan City
Author:
Date: 1959-08-21
Page: 4

    Monument to Heroes of the Mongolian People's Army
    Unveiled in Choibalsan City
    Xinhua News Agency, Ulaanbaatar, August 20th: The Mongolian News Agency reports that the unveiling ceremony for the Monument to the Heroes of the Mongolian People's Army was held in Choibalsan City this morning.
    This monument was erected to commemorate the heroes of the People's Army who gloriously sacrificed themselves in the Battle of Khalkhin Gol in 1939, during the crushing of the Japanese imperialist invasion.
    The unveiling ceremony was attended by Mongolian Party and state leaders Sambu, Tseden, Molomjamts, Minister of People's Army Affairs and Commander of the People's Army, Lieutenant General Lkhagvasuren, as well as all members of the Soviet delegation who came to attend the unveiling ceremony of the monument, and diplomatic envoys from various countries to Mongolia.
    Tseden, Second Secretary of the Central Committee of the Mongolian People's Revolutionary Party, and General Filyuninsky, a member of the Soviet delegation who participated in the Battle of Khalkhin Gol, spoke at the meeting.
    Today, Lieutenant General Lkhagvasuren issued an order commemorating the event, and "Pravda" published relevant editorials and articles.

### 蒙古人民军英雄纪念碑  在乔巴山市举行揭幕式

1959-08-21
第4版()
专栏：

    蒙古人民军英雄纪念碑
    在乔巴山市举行揭幕式
    新华社乌兰巴托20日电　据蒙古通讯社报道：今天上午在乔巴山市举行了蒙古人民军英雄纪念碑的揭幕式。
    这个纪念碑是为了纪念在1939年粉碎日本帝国主义侵略的哈拉哈河战役中光荣牺牲的人民军英雄而建立的。
    参加揭幕式的有蒙古党和国家领导人桑布、曾德、莫洛姆扎木茨、人民军事务部部长兼人民军司令勒哈格瓦苏伦中将，以及前来参加纪念碑揭幕典礼的苏联代表团全体人员和各国驻蒙古的外交使节。
    蒙古人民革命党中央委员会第二书记曾德和曾参加过哈拉哈河战役的苏联代表团团员费柳宁斯基大将先后在会上讲了话。
    今天，勒哈格瓦苏伦中将发布了纪念这一事件的命令，“真理报”发表了有关的社论和文章。
