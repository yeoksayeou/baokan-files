Title: Chairman Ho Chi Minh Visits Xinjiang on His Way Home After Vacationing in the Soviet Union; Sends Telegram to Chairman Liu Shaoqi to Congratulate "August 1st" Army Day

Author:

Date: 1959-08-03
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Urumqi, 2nd. Chairman of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam and Chairman of the Central Committee of the Vietnam Workers' Party, Ho Chi Minh, passed through our country on his way home after vacationing in the Soviet Union, arriving in Urumqi by special plane at 10:15 AM on August 1st.

Those welcoming him at the airport were: Saifudin, Secretary of the Xinjiang Uygur Autonomous Region Committee of the Chinese Communist Party and Chairman of the Autonomous Region; Lü Jianren, Zeng Di, and Li Quan, Secretaries of the Autonomous Region Party Committee; Qi Guo and Lin Bomin, alternate secretaries; Yang Heting and Zakelov, Vice Chairmen of the Autonomous Region; Lieutenant General Guo Peng, Deputy Commander of the Xinjiang Military Region of the Chinese People's Liberation Army; Major General Zuo Qi, Deputy Political Commissar; General Tao Zhiyue, Commander of the Production and Construction Corps; Zhang Zhonghan, Deputy Political Commissar; Ren Gebai, First Secretary of the Urumqi Municipal Committee of the Chinese Communist Party; Yasheng Hudabai'erdi, Mayor of Urumqi, and others. Zhang Shijie, Deputy Director of the Protocol Department of our Ministry of Foreign Affairs, also welcomed him at the airport.

Dabashen, the Soviet Consul General in Urumqi, also came to the airport to welcome him.

After Chairman Ho Chi Minh alighted from the plane, he shook hands cordially with Saifudin and others and accepted flowers presented by the Young Pioneers of Urumqi. At 3:00 PM, Chairman Ho Chi Minh, accompanied by Saifudin, Qi Guo, and others, visited the October Tractor Factory, the August 1st Agricultural College, and the Geological Bureau.

In the evening, Chairman Ho Chi Minh, accompanied by Saifudin and others, watched a performance of ethnic song and dance. After the performance, Chairman Ho Chi Minh went on stage and shook hands cordially with the performers.

Chairman Ho Chi Minh will make a short visit to Xinjiang.

Xinhua News Agency, 2nd. Chairman Liu Shaoqi received a telegram from Chairman Ho Chi Minh of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam on the 1st, congratulating the 32nd anniversary of the founding of the Chinese People's Liberation Army. The text of the telegram is as follows:

Comrade Liu Shaoqi, Chairman of the People's Republic of China:

On this glorious day of the 32nd anniversary of the founding of the Chinese People's Liberation Army, as I return home from the Soviet Union and pass through Xinjiang, China, I would like to extend my heartfelt greetings to you, and through you, to all commanders and fighters of the Chinese People's Liberation Army and the Chinese people.

Ho Chi Minh
August 1, 1959, in Urumqi

Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, 1st. Chairman of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam and Chairman of the Central Committee of the Vietnam Workers' Party, Ho Chi Minh, left Moscow for home today. Chairman Ho Chi Minh came to the Soviet Union for vacation on July 2nd.

During his stay in the Soviet Union, Chairman Ho Chi Minh visited several union republics and Crimea, where he was warmly welcomed by the Soviet people. The Soviet people asked him to convey their friendly greetings to the Vietnamese people.

### 在苏联休假后返国途中路过我国  胡志明主席访问新疆  致电刘少奇主席祝贺“八一”建军节

1959-08-03
第1版()
专栏：

    在苏联休假后返国途中路过我国
    胡志明主席访问新疆
    致电刘少奇主席祝贺“八一”建军节
    新华社乌鲁木齐2日电　越南民主共和国主席、越南劳动党中央委员会主席胡志明在苏联休假后返国途中，路过我国，于8月1日上午十时十五分乘专机到达乌鲁木齐。
    到机场欢迎的有：中共新疆维吾尔自治区委员会书记、自治区主席赛福鼎，自治区党委书记吕剑人、曾涤、李铨，候补书记祁果、林渤民，自治区副主席杨和亭、扎克洛夫，中国人民解放军新疆军区副司令员郭鹏中将、副政治委员左齐少将，生产建设兵团司令员陶峙岳上将、副政治委员张仲瀚，中共乌鲁木齐市委员会第一书记任戈白，乌鲁木齐市长牙生·胡达拜尔的等。到机场欢迎的还有我外交部礼宾司副司长张世杰。
    苏联驻乌鲁木齐总领事达巴申也到机场欢迎。
    胡志明主席下机后，和赛福鼎等亲切握手，并接受了乌鲁木齐市少先队员的献花。下午三时，胡志明主席由赛福鼎、祁果等陪同，先后参观了十月拖拉机厂、八一农学院和地质局。
    当晚，胡志明主席由赛福鼎等陪同观看了民族歌舞表演。表演结束后，胡志明主席走上舞台，同演员们亲切握手。
    胡志明主席将在新疆作短期访问。
    新华社2日讯　刘少奇主席1日收到了越南民主共和国胡志明主席祝贺中国人民解放军建军三十二周年的电报。电文如下：
    中华人民共和国主席刘少奇同志：
    在这中国人民解放军建军三十二周年的光荣日子，我从苏联回国，路过中国新疆，我谨向你并通过你向中国人民解放军全体指战员和中国人民表示衷心的祝贺。
                  胡志明
      1959年8月1日于乌鲁木齐
    新华社莫斯科1日电　越南民主共和国主席、越南劳动党中央委员会主席胡志明，今天离莫斯科回国。胡志明主席是在7月2日来苏联休假的。
    在苏联期间，胡志明主席访问了几个加盟共和国和克里米亚等地，受到苏联人民的热烈欢迎。苏联人民请他向越南人民转达他们对越南人民的友好的问候。
