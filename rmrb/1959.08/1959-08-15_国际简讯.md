　　International Briefs

　　Soviet Union to Purchase Large Quantities of Sugar from Cuba
　　Havana dispatch: Cuban Minister of Trade, Raúl Cepero Bonilla, announced on the evening of the 12th that the Soviet Union has purchased 170,000 tons of Cuban sugar, to be delivered between August and November of this year.
　　Bonilla pointed out that this transaction is of highly constructive significance. He said that sugar prices have risen in recent days since the news of the Soviet Union and Cuba negotiating the purchase of Cuban sugar spread.

　　Rountree as US Ambassador to Pakistan
　　Karachi dispatch: The new US Ambassador to Pakistan, Rountree, arrived here on the 13th.
　　According to earlier news, the first important thing for Rountree after arriving in Pakistan is to sign a "Commerce, Navigation, and Friendship" treaty with Pakistan.

　　Severe Flooding in Austria
　　Vienna dispatch: Austria was hit by severe flooding on the 13th, unseen for decades. This was caused by more than forty consecutive hours of torrential rain.
　　The water level of the Danube and its tributaries has exceeded the highest mark, rising to twenty feet. According to incomplete statistics, the losses caused by the flood so far have exceeded one billion Austrian Schillings (equivalent to forty million US dollars).

### 国际简讯

1959-08-15
第5版()
专栏：国际简讯

　　国际简讯
　  苏联向古巴购买大批蔗糖
    哈瓦那讯　古巴贸易部长劳尔·塞佩罗·博尼利亚12日晚宣布，苏联已经购买古巴十七万吨糖，将在今年8月到11月间交货。
    博尼利亚指出，这笔交易具有高度的建设性意义。他说，苏联和古巴谈判购买古巴糖的消息传出后，最近几天糖价上涨了。
  　朗特里当美驻巴基斯坦大使
    卡拉奇讯　新任美国驻巴基斯坦大使朗特里13日到达这里。
    据早些时候的消息说，朗特里到达巴基斯坦以后的第一件重要的事情就是同巴基斯坦签订“通商、通航和友好”条约。
    奥地利特大水灾
    维也纳讯　奥地利13日遭到了数十年来未见的特大水灾。这是由于连续四十多小时的暴雨所引起的。
    多瑙河及其支流的水位已经超过最高标志，涨到了二十英尺。据不完全统计，这次水灾迄今为止造成的损失已经超过十亿奥地利先令（折合四千万美元）。
