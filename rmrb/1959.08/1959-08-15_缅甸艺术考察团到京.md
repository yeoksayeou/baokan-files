Title: Burmese Art Delegation Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1959-08-15
Page: 4

　　Xinhua News Agency, 14th. The Burmese Art Delegation, a group of five, led by head of the delegation U Yaw, arrived in Beijing by plane today.
　　The delegation is visiting our country at the invitation of the Chinese People's Association for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries.

### 缅甸艺术考察团到京

1959-08-15
第4版()
专栏：

　  缅甸艺术考察团到京
    新华社14日讯　缅甸艺术考察团一行五人，在团长吴耀率领下，今天乘飞机到达北京。
    考察团是应中国人民对外文化联络委员会的邀请来我国访问的。
