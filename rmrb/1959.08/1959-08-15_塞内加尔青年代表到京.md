Title: Senegalese Youth Representatives Arrive in Beijing
Author: 
Date: 1959-08-15
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, 14th. The five-member delegation of the Youth Movement of the Senegalese Progressive Union, led by Ndiaye Abdoumane, arrived in Beijing by plane today.

　　The guests are visiting China at the invitation of the All-China Youth Federation.

### 塞内加尔青年代表到京

1959-08-15
第4版()
专栏：

    塞内加尔青年代表到京
    新华社14日讯　塞内加尔进步联盟青年运动代表团一行五人，在团长恩迪耶·阿都曼率领下，今天乘飞机到达北京。
    客人们是应中华全国青年联合会的邀请来中国访问的。
