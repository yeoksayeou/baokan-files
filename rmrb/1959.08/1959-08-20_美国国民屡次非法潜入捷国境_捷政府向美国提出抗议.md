Title: U.S. Nationals Repeatedly Illegally Enter Czechoslovak Territory; Czechoslovak Government Lodges Protest with U.S.

Author:

Date: 1959-08-20
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, Prague, August 18th. The Czechoslovak Ministry of Foreign Affairs today issued a note to U.S. Ambassador to Prague, Allison, protesting the repeated illegal crossings of the Czechoslovak border by U.S. nationals.

    According to reports from the Czechoslovak authorities, a U.S. national, Robert Schwartz, was detained on August 10, 1959, when he illegally crossed the Czechoslovak border. The note points out that since July 15 of this year, there have been five incidents of U.S. nationals illegally crossing the Czechoslovak border.

    On behalf of the government, the Czechoslovak Ministry of Foreign Affairs lodges the strongest protest against the repeated, illegal, and conscious crossings of the Czechoslovak border by U.S. nationals. The note expresses serious concern that the U.S. authorities have not fulfilled their previous guarantee to take immediate and effective measures to prevent U.S. nationals from violating the Czechoslovak border.

    The note emphasizes that, henceforth, U.S. nationals must bear full responsibility and consequences arising from the illegal crossings of the Czechoslovak border.

### 美国国民屡次非法潜入捷国境　捷政府向美国提出抗议

1959-08-20
第5版()
专栏：

　　美国国民屡次非法潜入捷国境
　　捷政府向美国提出抗议
    新华社布拉格18日电　捷克斯洛伐克外交部今天向美国驻布拉格大使艾利逊发出一项照会，抗议美国国民一再非法潜越捷克斯洛伐克国境。
    据捷克斯洛伐克主管当局报告，一名美国国民罗伯特·施瓦茨在1959年8月10日非法潜越捷克斯洛伐克边境的时候被拘留。照会指出，今年7月15日以来，美国国民非法潜越捷克斯洛伐克边境的事件已有五起。
    捷克斯洛伐克外交部奉政府之命对美国国民屡次地、非法地、有意识地潜越捷克斯洛伐克边境的事件提出最强烈的抗议。美国当局没有实现他们以前作出的立即采取有效的措施以防止美国国民侵犯捷克斯洛伐克边境的保证，照会对此表示严重的不安。
    照会强调说，今后美国国民必须承担非法潜越捷克斯洛伐克边境所引起的全部责任和后果。
