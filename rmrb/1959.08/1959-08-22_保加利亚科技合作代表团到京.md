Title: Bulgarian Scientific and Technological Cooperation Delegation Arrives in Beijing
Author: 
Date: 1959-08-22
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, 21st: The Bulgarian delegation of two, led by Stoyan Dzhulemezov, Chairman of the Bulgarian Group of the China-Bulgaria Scientific and Technological Cooperation Committee and Vice Chairman of the National Planning Committee of Bulgaria, arrived in Beijing by plane today to attend the fourth session of the China-Bulgaria Scientific and Technological Cooperation Committee.

Wang Xinyuan, member of the Chinese group of the committee and Vice Minister of Light Industry, Wang Dongnian, member and Director of the International Cooperation Bureau of the State Science and Technology Commission, and others went to the airport to welcome them.

Ivan Balov, member of the Bulgarian group of the committee and Commercial Counselor of the Bulgarian Embassy in China, also welcomed them at the airport.

### 保加利亚科技合作代表团到京

1959-08-22
第4版()
专栏：

    保加利亚科技合作代表团到京
    新华社21日讯　前来我国参加中国保加利亚科学技术合作委员会第四届会议的保加利亚代表团一行二人，在委员会保加利亚组主席、保国家计划委员会副主席斯托扬·久列梅佐夫率领下，今天乘飞机到达北京。
    前往机场欢迎的有委员会中国组委员、轻工业部副部长王新元，委员、国家科学技术委员会国际合作局局长王东年等。
    到机场欢迎的还有委员会保加利亚组委员、保驻华商务参赞伊凡·巴洛夫。
