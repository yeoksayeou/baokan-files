Title: Chairman Liu Shaoqi Sends Congratulatory Telegram for Swiss National Day
Author:
Date: 1959-08-01
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, July 31st: Chairman of the People's Republic of China, Liu Shaoqi, today sent a telegram to the President of the Swiss Confederation, Paul Chaudet, congratulating him on Swiss National Day. The full text of the telegram is as follows:

To His Excellency Paul Chaudet, President of the Swiss Confederation:

On the occasion of Swiss National Day, I extend my sincere congratulations to Your Excellency and the Swiss people. I wish the friendly relations between China and Switzerland will develop and consolidate day by day. I wish the Swiss Confederation prosperity and its people flourishing.

                                          Chairman of the People's Republic of China
                            　Liu Shaoqi　July 31, 1959

### 刘少奇主席电贺瑞士国庆

1959-08-01
第1版()
专栏：

    刘少奇主席电贺瑞士国庆
    新华社31日讯　中华人民共和国主席刘少奇今天打电报给瑞士联邦主席保尔·舒特祝贺瑞士联邦国庆。电报全文如下：瑞士联邦主席保尔·舒特先生阁下：
    当瑞士联邦国庆的时候，我谨向阁下和瑞士人民表示热诚的祝贺。祝中国和瑞士之间的友好关系日益发展和巩固。祝瑞士联邦繁荣，人民兴旺。
                                          中华人民共和国主席
                            　刘少奇　一九五九年七月三十一日
