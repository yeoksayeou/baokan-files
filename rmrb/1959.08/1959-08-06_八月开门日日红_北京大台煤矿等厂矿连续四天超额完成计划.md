Title: Red Success Every Day in August: Beijing Datong Coal Mine and Other Factories and Mines Exceed Planned Targets for Four Consecutive Days
Author:
Date: 1959-08-06
Page: 1
    Xinhua News Agency, 5th. The broad masses of workers on the capital's industrial front have thrown themselves into the August production battle with even greater fervor.

    The workers of the Shijingshan Iron and Steel Company are full of fighting spirit. Company deputy managers and deputy party secretaries and other responsible cadres, go deep into the production site, to specifically direct production. After they tried every means to take measures to increase blast temperature, improve gas utilization, etc., the average daily output of pig iron from the 1st to the 4th increased by 170 tons compared with late July.

    After overfulfilling the July coal mining and tunneling plan, the Datong Coal Mine of the Jingxi Mining Bureau forged ahead victoriously, creating a red start to August production, overfulfilling the state coal mining plan by 6.99% in the first four days. As soon as August began, the mine's leading cadres and all the mine's workers threw themselves into an intense battle. On August 1, the mine's Party Secretary, mine manager, union chairman, and department cadres all put on work clothes and went down the well to produce with the workers, greatly encouraging the workers' production enthusiasm. On this day, the entire mine overfulfilled the daily plan by 25%.

    All the workers of the Liulihe Cement Plant fought against the incessant rain, overproducing 714 tons of cement for the country from the 1st to the 4th. In order to ensure the daily completion of the plan, this factory's factory-level personnel check production every day, the workshops check every shift, and the groups check every hour.

    The workers of the Beijing No. 1 Cotton Textile Mill launched a vigorous emulation campaign. The entire plant comprehensively overfulfilled the plan from the 1st to the 4th: cotton yarn overfulfilled by 2.37%, cotton cloth overfulfilled by 1.26%, the first-grade, first-class rate of cotton yarn reached 100%, and the first-grade product rate of cotton cloth entering the warehouse reached 99.89%.

### 八月开门日日红  北京大台煤矿等厂矿连续四天超额完成计划

1959-08-06
第1版()
专栏：

    八月开门日日红
    北京大台煤矿等厂矿连续四天超额完成计划
    新华社5日讯　首都工业战线上的广大职工，以更大的干劲投入了8月份的生产战斗。
    石景山钢铁公司的职工个个斗志昂扬。公司副经理和党委副书记等负责干部，深入生产现场，具体指挥生产。他们千方百计采取提高风温、提高煤气利用等措施以后，1至4日的生铁平均日产量比7月下旬提高了一百七十吨。
    京西矿务局大台煤矿超额完成7月份采煤和掘进计划之后，乘胜前进，创造了8月生产开门红，前四天超额6.99％完成了国家采煤计划。一进入8月，这个矿的领导干部和全矿职工就投入了紧张的战斗，8月1日，矿的党委书记、矿长、工会主席和科室干部，都穿上工作服，到井下同工人一起生产，大大鼓舞了工人的生产热情。这一天，全矿超额25％完成了日计划。
    琉璃河水泥厂的全体职工同霪雨展开搏斗，1至4日为国家超产水泥七百一十四吨。为了保证日日完成计划，这个工厂厂级每天检查生产，车间则每班进行检查，小组每小时检查一次。
    北京第一棉纺织厂职工开展了轰轰烈烈的评比竞赛，全厂1至4日全面超额完成了计划：棉纱超额2.37％，棉布超额1.26％，棉纱的一等一级品率达到100％，棉布入库一等品达到99.89％。
