Title: World Federation of Democratic Youth Holds Member Organization Conference to Discuss Strengthening Unity, Maintaining Peace, and Youth Rights
Author:
Date: 1959-08-12
Page: 4

　　Xinhua News Agency, Prague dispatch: The Fifth Member Organization Conference of the World Federation of Democratic Youth (WFDY) opened here on the morning of August 10. The conference will discuss cooperation among the various organizations affiliated with the WFDY, formulate the WFDY's tasks for the next stage, and discuss the struggle for peace, the liberation of colonial peoples, and the prohibition of weapons of mass destruction.

    The conference will continue until August 16.

    More than 450 delegates and observers from approximately 90 countries are participating in the conference.

    WFDY President Bruno Bernini delivered a report on behalf of the Executive Committee on the first item on the agenda, "The Question of Peace and Independence and the Responsibility of the Younger Generation."

    Bernini suggested that the conference consider organizing a large-scale European conference to discuss the struggle against militarism, especially German militarism; organizing an Asian youth conference to discuss turning the Pacific region into a nuclear-free zone; and organizing a Latin American youth conference. He called for support for the youth of Algeria and suggested establishing an international solidarity committee to support Algerian youth.

    On behalf of the WFDY, Bernini expressed serious concern about the persecution of democratic organizations in the United Arab Republic.

    He expressed pleasure at the mutual visits between Khrushchev and Eisenhower, emphasizing that people can and must reach an agreement that will forever stop the threat of new wars and guarantee a peaceful future for all mankind.

    He also suggested convening a "World Youth Discussion Meeting," inviting representatives from all international and national organizations to participate in this discussion, to discuss youth issues openly and without restriction, in order to find a basis for common action. He also suggested designating April 24 as "World Youth Day Against Colonialism and for Peaceful Coexistence."

    The plenary session continued on the afternoon of the 10th.

    The plenary session adopted telegrams to Khrushchev and Eisenhower. In the telegrams, the WFDY, on behalf of millions of young people around the world, welcomed the upcoming mutual visits of the two statesmen.

    At the afternoon meeting, WFDY General Secretary Esiade delivered a report entitled "Economic, Social, and Cultural Issues of Concern to Young People in Various Countries Today."

    The reporter emphasized that the WFDY supports the demands made by young people: to receive higher education, work, rest, and develop cultural, educational, and sports activities.

    He announced that the WFDY intends to convene the Third International Travel Conference in Warsaw in October this year, and intends to hold a "Summer of Youth Friendship" next summer.

    The reporter finally suggested considering the formulation of a basic program of common activities for the international youth movement, and considering the possibility of formulating a charter of economic, social, and cultural rights for youth.

    Then, the conference entered the discussion. Representatives from Finland, Cameroon, Ceylon, and Luxembourg spoke. They mainly talked about the issue of cooperation between young people from countries with different economic and social systems.

### 世界民主青联举行会员组织大会　讨论加强团结维护和平和青年权利问题

1959-08-12
第4版()
专栏：

　　世界民主青联举行会员组织大会
　　讨论加强团结维护和平和青年权利问题
    据新华社布拉格电　世界民主青年联盟第五届会员组织大会10日上午在这里开幕。大会将讨论世界民主青年联盟所属各组织之间的合作问题，拟订世界民主青年联盟下一个阶段的任务，讨论为争取和平、殖民地人民的解放和禁止大规模毁灭性武器而进行斗争的问题。
    这次会议将一直开到8月16日。
    来自大约九十个国家的四百五十多名代表和观察员参加了这次会议。
    世界民主青年联盟主席布伦诺·柏尼尼代表联盟执行委员会就第一项议程“和平和独立的问题以及青年一代的责任”作了报告。
    柏尼尼建议大会考虑组织一次大规模的欧洲会议来讨论反对军国主义、特别是反对德国军国主义的斗争；组织一次亚洲青年会议来讨论把太平洋区域变为无原子武器区的问题；组织一次拉丁美洲青年会议。他要求支持阿尔及利亚的青年，并且建议建立一个国际声援委员会来支持阿尔及利亚青年。
    柏尼尼代表世界民主青年联盟对阿拉伯联合共和国的民主组织遭到迫害表示严重关切。
    他对赫鲁晓夫和艾森豪威尔的相互访问表示高兴，他着重指出，人们能够、而且必须达成一项永远制止新战争威胁和保障全人类的和平前途的协议。
    他还建议召开一次“世界青年讨论会”，邀请一切国际性的和各国的组织派代表来参加这个讨论会，公开而不受限制地讨论青年的问题，以便找到共同行动的基础。他还建议把4月24日定为“反对殖民主义和争取和平共处世界青年
  日”。
    10日下午继续举行全体会议。
    全体会议通过了致赫鲁晓夫和艾森豪威尔的电报。世界民主青年联盟在电报里代表全世界千百万青年，欢迎这两位政治家即将进行的互相访问。
    在下午的会议上，世界民主青年联盟总书记埃夏德作了题为“今天世界各国青年所关心的经济、社会和文化问题”的报告。
    报告人强调说，世界民主青年联盟支持青年们提出的这些要求；接受更高的教育、工作、休息、以及发展文化、教育和体育方面的活动。
    他宣布，世界民主青年联盟打算于今年10月在华沙召开第三次国际旅行事业大会，并且打算在明年夏天举行“青年友谊之夏”。
    报告人最后建议考虑拟订一项国际青年运动共同活动的基本纲领，考虑制订青年的经济、社会、和文化权利宪章的可能性。
    接着，大会进入讨论。芬兰、喀麦隆、锡兰和卢森堡的代表都发了言。他们主要谈到了经济和社会制度不同的国家的青年进行合作的问题。
