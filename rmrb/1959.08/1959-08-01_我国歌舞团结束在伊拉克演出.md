Title: China's Song and Dance Troupe Concludes Performance in Iraq
Author:
Date: 1959-08-01
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, Baghdad, 30th - China's Song and Dance Troupe concluded its final performance in Iraq last night.

The Song and Dance Troupe was invited by the Iraqi government to participate in the anniversary celebration of the revolution. From July 23rd, the Chinese Song and Dance Troupe gave six public performances. Approximately 12,000 people saw the Chinese songs and dances. At the end of the final performance, representatives of the Iraq-China Friendship Association presented flowers to the Chinese Song and Dance Troupe.

### 我国歌舞团结束在伊拉克演出

1959-08-01
第5版()
专栏：

    我国歌舞团结束在伊拉克演出
    据新华社巴格达30日电　中国歌舞团昨天晚上结束了它在伊拉克的最后一场演出。
    歌舞团是应伊拉克政府的邀请来伊拉克参加革命一周年纪念的。从7月23日起，中国歌舞团公演了六次。大约有一万二千人看到了中国的歌舞。在最后一次演出结束时，伊拉克—中国友好协会的代表向中国歌舞团献花。
