Title: The Miner's Heart (and Two Other Poems)
Author: He Bo
Date: 1959-08-12
Page: 8

　　The Miner's Heart (and Two Other Poems)
　　He Bo
　　Underground lights, countless thousands upon thousands;
　　Illuminating the deep mine shafts, shining brilliantly like stars.
　　The glowing coal fire, emitting intense heat;
　　Coal, the underground sun, holding boundless passion.
　　But the bright lights, the blazing coal fires,
　　Their light and warmth, can't compare to the coal miners.
　　Because the people's beautiful life,
　　The motherland's great construction,
　　Humanity's lofty aspirations,
　　Are all entrusted to the miner's heart.

      The Coal-Hauling Girl
　　Legend says that in Jingxi's Nine Dragon Mountain,
　　Nine dragons lie hidden deep within;
　　Day and night they swallow clouds and spit mist,
　　Filling the mountains and fields with hazy rain.
　　The people living here, once went to explore the giant dragons;
　　Because they wanted to obtain the dragon's pearl,
　　To change their impoverished lives.
　　In fact, this group of giant dragons,
　　Has now transformed into coal-loading carriages;
　　Look at our coal-hauling girls,
　　Aren't they driving the giant dragons back and forth.

      Underground Tunnel
　　Seeming like a glorious fairyland,
　　A sea of light,
　　A field of stars on a summer night.
　　Above is a solid roof,
　　On both sides, a dense forest of pillars,
　　The tunnel leads to the distant core of the earth.
　　Endless treasures,
　　Precious black gold,
　　Millions of tons are transported out from here day and night,
　　The tunnel is also a road to happiness,
　　Connecting to the motherland's great future;
　　Ah, the path opened by the glorious miners...

### 矿工的心（外二首）
禾波
1959-08-12
第8版()
专栏：

　　矿工的心（外二首）
　　禾波地下明灯，千盏万盏数不清；把深深的矿井照亮，光辉灿烂像星群。通红的煤火，会发出强烈高温；煤，地下的太阳啊，它蕴藏着洋溢的热情。可是明亮的灯光，熊熊的煤火，它们的光明和温暖，都比不过煤矿工人。因为人民美好的生活，祖国伟大的建设，人类崇高的愿望，都寄托在矿工的心。
      运煤的姑娘传说这京西九龙山，有九条龙在山中深藏；它们日夜吞云吐雾，满山遍野才烟雨迷漫。这里居住的人民，曾经去把巨龙探访；因为想得到龙的宝珠，改变穷困生活的模样。其实这一群巨龙，如今已化作装煤的车厢；你看我们运煤的姑娘啊，不正是驾驶着巨龙来往。
      地下巷道仿佛辉煌的仙境，一片光的海洋，一片夏夜的繁星。头上有结实的顶板，两旁是支柱的密林，巷道啊通向遥远的地心。无尽的宝藏，珍贵的乌金，日夜从这里运出千万吨，巷道又是幸福的路，接连着祖国远大的未来；啊，光荣的矿工开辟的路径……
