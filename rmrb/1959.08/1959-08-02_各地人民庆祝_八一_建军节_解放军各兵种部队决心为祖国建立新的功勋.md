Title: People Across the Country Celebrate "August 1st" Army Day; PLA Units of All Branches are Determined to Establish New Merits for the Motherland
Author:
Date: 1959-08-02
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, August 1st: People across the country warmly celebrated "August 1st" Army Day with get-togethers and慰问 (comfort) activities.

　　In Shanghai, a joint military-civilian celebration was held last night. Cao Diqiu, Secretary of the Shanghai Municipal Committee of the Communist Party of China and Deputy Mayor of Shanghai, extended congratulations and regards on behalf of the people of the city to the troops stationed in Shanghai and the families of revolutionary martyrs, disabled veterans, and demobilized soldiers. In recent days, people from all walks of life in Shanghai have extensively carried out activities to comfort the People's Liberation Army. Many grassroots units also checked preferential treatment work and held forums for families of martyrs and disabled and demobilized soldiers.

　　The "August 1st"慰问 (comfort) delegation in Guangdong Province, headed by Vice Governor Lin Qiangyun, visited military hospitals and sanatoriums today to comfort wounded and sick soldiers of the People's Liberation Army, and will visit the garrison sites of the troops that made outstanding contributions in fighting floods and providing disaster relief within the next few days to express the gratitude of the people of the province.

　　On the Fujian front, naval troops reported the heroic deeds of the great communist fighter An Yemin to more than 1,000 workers and students in Xiamen last night. Labor models from industry and agriculture and combat heroes from the troops in Fuzhou, Quanzhou and other places met, had intimate talks, and encouraged each other.

　　At a joint military-civilian celebration held last night by Hebei Province and Tianjin, more than 8,000 people from all walks of life and officers and soldiers of the garrison cordially gathered together for celebrations and talks.

　　During the "August 1st" celebrations, the people of Liaoning, Jilin, and Heilongjiang Provinces deeply studied the glorious traditions of the People's Liberation Army and the Northeast Anti-Japanese Allied Army. Personnel from the Northeast Martyrs Memorial Hall recently gave more than 70 reports, and more than 100,000 people were deeply moved when they heard the stories of the struggles of anti-Japanese heroes such as Yang Jingyu, Li Zhaolin, and Zhao Yiman.

　　While celebrating "August 1st," people from all walks of life in Henan Province, led by Vice Governor Xing Zhaotang, Deputy Commander of the Henan Military Region Yu Pinxuan, and others, went to Luoyang, Kaifeng, Xuchang, Xinxiang, and other places to inspect the work of supporting the army and giving preferential treatment to families of soldiers. Military and civilians in Nanjing, Kunming, and other places also held celebrations today.

　　Xinhua News Agency, August 1st: Units of all branches of the Chinese People's Liberation Army extensively launched commemoration activities centered on revolutionary tradition education, warmly celebrating "August 1st" Army Day.

　　The "Red Army Regiment," which participated in the Pingjiang Uprising in Hunan, recently held a celebration meeting for the 31st anniversary of the founding of the regiment. General Tan Zheng, Director of the General Political Department, specially wrote an inscription to congratulate the event. General Zhang Aiping, General Peng Shaohui, He Changgong and other comrades also gave their photos and souvenirs from the Red Army and the Anti-Japanese War to the regiment in the name of "old soldiers." During the commemoration activities, the soldiers unanimously stated that they would maintain their honor, carry forward their honor, and add new glory to the glorious history of the regiment.

　　Ge Zhenlin, one of the "Five Warriors of Langya Mountain," recently visited his former company and introduced the heroic deeds of the warriors jumping off the cliff to the soldiers in detail. The troops in the Shenyang area also organized artillery hero Huang Jinhai, river-crossing hero Miao Peiyu, and volunteer army second-class combat hero Gao Yunhe to make巡回 (touring) reports to the troops, introducing the heroic deeds to the soldiers.

　　On the Fujian front and in troops in Beijing, Guangzhou, Kunming, and other regions, traditional education was also deeply carried out around "August 1st." At the same time, various troops also carried out various kinds of get-together activities with the local people.

　　Troops in the Tibet region held a celebration meeting last night. Major General Zhan Huayu called on all officers and soldiers at the meeting to carry forward the glorious traditions of our army and better fulfill the glorious tasks of combat teams, work teams, and production teams.

### 各地人民庆祝“八一”建军节  解放军各兵种部队决心为祖国建立新的功勋

1959-08-02
第1版()
专栏：

    各地人民庆祝“八一”建军节
    解放军各兵种部队决心为祖国建立新的功勋
    据新华社1日讯　各地人民热烈庆祝“八一”建军节，进行联欢、慰问活动。
    在上海，昨晚举行了军民联欢会。中共上海市委书记、上海市副市长曹荻秋代表全市人民向驻沪部队和烈军属、残废、复员军人表示祝贺和慰问。这几天，上海各界人民广泛开展了慰问解放军的活动。许多基层单位还检查了优抚工作，召开了烈、军属和残废、复员军人座谈会。
    广东省以副省长林锵云为首的“八一”慰问团，今天分别到部队医院、休养院，慰问解放军伤病员，并将在日内到抗洪救灾中有卓越贡献的部队驻地，表达全省人民的谢意。
    在福建前线，海军部队昨晚向厦门市一千多名工人、学生，报告了伟大的共产主义战士安业民的英雄事迹。福州、泉州等地的工农业劳动模范和部队的战斗英雄见了面，亲密谈心，互相鼓励。
    河北省、天津市昨晚联合举行的军民联欢大会上，各界人民和驻军官兵八千多人亲切地在一起联欢、谈心。
    辽宁、吉林、黑龙江省人民在庆祝“八一”活动中，深入学习人民解放军和东北抗日联军的光荣传统。东北烈士纪念馆的人员，最近做了七十多场报告，十多万人听到杨靖宇、李兆麟、赵一曼等抗日英雄的斗争故事时，都深受感动。
    河南省各界人民在庆祝“八一”的同时，副省长邢肇棠、河南军区副司令员余品轩等率领拥军优属检查团，分赴洛阳、开封、许昌、新乡等地检查拥军优属工作。南京、昆明等地军民，今天也都举行了庆祝活动。
    据新华社1日讯　中国人民解放军各兵种部队广泛展开以革命传统教育为中心的纪念活动，热烈庆祝“八一”建军节。
    曾参加湖南平江起义的“红军团”，最近举行了建团三十一周年纪念大会。总政治部主任谭政大将特题词祝贺。张爱萍上将、彭绍辉上将、何长工等同志还以“老战士”的名义把自己在红军和抗日战争期间的像片和纪念品，送给这个团。在纪念活动中，战士们一致表示，一定保持荣誉，发扬荣誉，在团的光荣历史上增添新的光荣。
    “狼牙山五壮士”之一葛振林，最近访问了他过去所在的连队，并向战士详细地介绍了壮士跳崖的英勇事迹。沈阳地区部队并且组织炮兵英雄黄金海、渡江英雄苗培玉、志愿军二级战斗英雄高云和等到部队作巡回报告，向战士们介绍英雄事迹。
    在福建前线和北京、广州、昆明等地区部队，也在“八一”前后深入展开了传统教育。同时，各部队还与驻地人民进行了多种多样的联欢活动。
    西藏地区部队昨晚举行了庆祝大会，詹化雨少将在会上号召全体官兵发扬我军的光荣传统，更好地完成战斗队、工作队和生产队的光荣任务。
