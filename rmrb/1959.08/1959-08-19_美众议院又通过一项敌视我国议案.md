Title: U.S. House of Representatives Passes Another Anti-China Bill
Author:
Date: 1959-08-19
Page: 5
Xinhua News Agency, Washington, 18th: The U.S. House of Representatives passed a resolution hostile to China on the 17th.

　　This resolution expresses the United States' continued opposition to the restoration of China's seat in the United Nations. The resolution was jointly proposed by Democrat Zablocki and Republican Judd. Some congressmen, in debate, advocated that the U.S. Congress should make it clear that "the United States does not intend to change its policy towards Communist China."

　　It is reported that the U.S. House of Representatives passed this resolution because it anticipated that the United States' attitude of opposing the restoration of China's legitimate seat in the United Nations would once again be criticized by many countries at the upcoming Fourteenth Session of the United Nations General Assembly, and therefore attempted to show these countries in advance that the United States would not change this stubborn attitude.

　　Democrat Meyer opposed the passage of this proposal. He said, "This futile action will neither win friends nor influence others."

### 美众议院又通过一项敌视我国议案

1959-08-19
第5版()
专栏：

　　美众议院又通过一项敌视我国议案
    新华社18日讯　华盛顿消息：美国众议院17日通过了一项敌视中国的决议案。
    这个决议案表示美国继续反对恢复中国在联合国中的席位。决议案是由民主党人扎布洛基和共和党人周以德联合提出的。一些众议员在辩论中鼓吹，美国国会应该表明“美国不打算改变对共产党中国的政策”。
    据报道，美国众议院所以通过这个决议案，是因为它预计到美国反对恢复中国在联合国的合法席位的态度在即将召开的联合国大会第十四届会议上将再一次遭到许多国家的抨击，因而企图事先向这些国家表明美国不会改变这种顽固态度。
    民主党人麦耶反对通过这种提案。他说，“这种徒然的行动既不能争取到朋友，也不能影响别人。”
