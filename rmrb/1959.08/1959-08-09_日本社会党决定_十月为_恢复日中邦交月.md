Title: Japanese Socialist Party Decides to Make October "Month for Restoring Japan-China Diplomatic Relations"
Author:
Date: 1959-08-09
Page: 3
Xinhua News Agency, Tokyo, August 8th: The Japanese Socialist Party held a meeting today of its chairman and bureau chiefs. The meeting decided to designate October as "Month for Restoring Japan-China Diplomatic Relations" and, centering on the 10th anniversary of the founding of the People's Republic of China on October 1st, to cooperate with relevant Japanese organizations to launch a national movement for the restoration of Japan-China diplomatic relations.

### 日本社会党决定  十月为“恢复日中邦交月”

1959-08-09
第3版()
专栏：

    日本社会党决定
    十月为“恢复日中邦交月”
    新华社8日讯　东京消息：日本社会党今天举行委员长、局长会议，会上确定10月为“恢复日中邦交月”，并且以10月1日中华人民共和国建国十周年的国庆节为中心，同日本有关各团体实行合作，展开一个恢复日中邦交的国民运动。
