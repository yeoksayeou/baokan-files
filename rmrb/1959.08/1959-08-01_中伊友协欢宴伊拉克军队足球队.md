Title: Sino-Iraqi Friendship Association Hosts Banquet for Iraqi Army Football Team
Author:
Date: 1959-08-01
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, July 31st: The China-Iraq Friendship Association held a reception in Beijing today to welcome the Iraqi Republic Army football team's visit to China.

The entire Iraqi Army football team, led by Colonel Ismail Razoqi, attended the reception. The twenty-five members of the Iraqi Army football team arrived in Beijing in two groups on the 27th and 30th. On the evening of the 30th, the All-China Sports Federation held a dinner to welcome them.

### 中伊友协欢宴伊拉克军队足球队

1959-08-01
第5版()
专栏：

    中伊友协欢宴伊拉克军队足球队
    据新华社31日讯　中国伊拉克友好协会今天在北京举行酒会，欢迎伊拉克共和国军队足球队来中国访问。
    由伊斯梅尔·拉佐基上校率领的伊拉克军队足球队全体人员应邀出席了酒会。伊拉克军队足球队全体人员二十五人是在27日和30日分批到达北京的。30日晚，中华全国体育总会曾举行便宴欢迎他们。
