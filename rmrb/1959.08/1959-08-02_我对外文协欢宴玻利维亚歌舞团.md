Title: China Foreign Cultural Association Hosts Banquet for Bolivian Song and Dance Troupe
Author:
Date: 1959-08-02
Page: 3

Xinhua News Agency, August 1st: The Chinese People's Association for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries hosted a luncheon today to welcome the Bolivian "Fundacion" song and dance troupe, which is currently visiting China. Ding Xilin, Vice President of the Chinese People's Association for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries, delivered a speech at the banquet, thanking the song and dance troupe for introducing the beautiful art of the Bolivian people to the Chinese people, and warmly congratulating the artists of the song and dance troupe for their successful performances in Beijing. Ascarrunz, the head of the "Fundacion" song and dance troupe, said in his speech that although Bolivia and China are far apart, we are so close, and art closely connects us.

### 我对外文协欢宴玻利维亚歌舞团

1959-08-02
第3版()
专栏：

    我对外文协欢宴玻利维亚歌舞团
    据新华社1日讯　中国人民对外文化协会今天中午设宴欢迎正在我国访问的玻利维亚“芳达西亚”歌舞团。中国人民对外文化协会副会长丁西林在宴会上致词，感谢歌舞团把玻利维亚人民优美的艺术介绍给中国人民，并且热烈祝贺歌舞团艺术家们在北京成功的演出。“芳达西亚”歌舞团团长阿斯卡伦斯在讲话中说，玻利维亚和中国虽然相隔很远，但是我们之间是如此亲密，艺术把我们紧紧联系在一起。
