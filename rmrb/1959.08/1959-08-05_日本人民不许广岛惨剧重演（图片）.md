Title: The Japanese People Will Not Allow the Hiroshima Tragedy to Repeat Itself (Picture)
Author:
Date: 1959-08-05
Page: 4

The Japanese people marching through Hiroshima Prefecture in a peace procession to ban atomic and hydrogen bombs, oppose nuclear armament, and prevent the revision of the U.S.-Japan "Security Treaty." Tokyo facsimile photo (New China News Agency)

Nagoya university students warmly welcome the Tokyo peace army marching towards Hiroshima. The placard reads: "Don't kill us with atomic and hydrogen bombs. We want to live!"

A girl in Hiroshima suffering from leukocytosis due to the atomic bomb explosion folds paper cranes, silently praying: "May people in the world no longer kill each other with atomic bombs. Birds, please carry my wishes to all parts of the world!"

### 日本人民不许广岛惨剧重演（图片）

1959-08-05
第4版()
专栏：

    日本人民不许广岛惨剧重演
    在广岛县境内行进的日本人民禁止原子弹和氢弹、反对核武装和阻止修改日美“安全条约”和平队伍。　东京传真照片（新华社发）
    名古屋大学生热烈欢迎向广岛进发的东京和平大军。标语牌上写着：“不许用原子弹和氢弹杀害我们。我们要活命！”
    广岛因受原子弹爆炸而患了白血球过多症的少女，她一面在折纸鸟，一面默祷：“愿世人不再用原子弹互相残杀。鸟儿呵，把我的愿望传到世界各地去吧！”
