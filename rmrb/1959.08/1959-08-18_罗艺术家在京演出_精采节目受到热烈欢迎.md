Title: Romanian Artists Perform in Beijing, Brilliant Program Receives Warm Welcome
Author:
Date: 1959-08-18
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency, 17th. Five Romanian solo performers gave their first performance in China tonight at the Beijing People's Theater.

The performers tonight included the Meritorious Artist Iolanda Mărgulescu, soprano solo; Mihai Constantin Naiscu, violin solo; Selma Haulingher, soprano solo; and Titus Mălaru, tenor solo. Accompanied by pianist Barnig Mariana, they performed thirty-four world-famous pieces, Romanian folk songs, and Chinese folk songs, each of which was warmly welcomed by more than 1,400 audience members.

Qu Wu, Deputy Director of the Commission for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries, Zhu Ming, Secretary-General of the Sino-Romanian Friendship Association, and Romanian Ambassador to China, Zaharescu, and others attended tonight's concert. After the performance, they went on stage to congratulate the performers on their success.

### 罗艺术家在京演出  精采节目受到热烈欢迎

1959-08-18
第4版()
专栏：

　　罗艺术家在京演出
　  精采节目受到热烈欢迎
    新华社17日讯　罗马尼亚五位单独表演家今晚在北京人民剧场举行访华首次演出。
    今晚演出的有功勋艺术家约朗达·默尔古列斯特的女高音独唱，米哈依·康斯坦丁·奈斯库的小提琴独奏，谢尔玛·豪林盖尔的女高音独唱，以及狄图斯·毛拉鲁的男高音独唱。他们在钢琴家巴尔尼格·玛丽安娜的伴奏下，演出了世界名曲、罗马尼亚民歌和中国民歌共三十四个节目，每个节目都受到一千四百多观众的热烈欢迎。
    对外文化联络委员会副主任屈武，中罗友好协会秘书长朱明，罗马尼亚驻我国大使查哈勒斯库等出席了今晚的音乐会。演出结束后，他们上台祝贺演出的成功。
