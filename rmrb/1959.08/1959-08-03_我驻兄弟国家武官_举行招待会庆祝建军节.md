Title: Military Attaches of Our Country Stationed in Brotherly Countries Hold Receptions to Celebrate Army Day
Author:
Date: 1959-08-03
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency, August 2nd -- To celebrate the 32nd anniversary of the founding of the Chinese People's Liberation Army, the military attaches of our embassies in Bulgaria, Romania, Hungary, North Korea, Czechoslovakia, Poland, the German Democratic Republic, and the Democratic Republic of Vietnam held receptions on the evenings of July 31st and August 1st, respectively.

    Among those present at the reception held by Wang Xiangsheng, acting military attache of our embassy in Bulgaria, were Bichvarov, First Deputy Minister of National Defense and Chief of General Staff of Bulgaria, and Angelov, Deputy Minister of Foreign Affairs.
    Among those present at the reception held by Mei Yongxi, military attache of our embassy in Romania, were General Salajan, Minister of the Armed Forces of Romania, General Draghici, Minister of the Interior, and Dumitrescu, Deputy Minister of Foreign Affairs.
    Xu Jianguo, the Chinese Ambassador to Romania, also attended the reception.
    Among those present at the reception held by Hu Binpu, military attache of our embassy in Hungary, were General Revész Géza, Minister of National Defense of Hungary, Lieutenant General Usta Gyula, First Deputy Minister, and senior generals of the Hungarian People's Army.
    Among those present at the reception held by Zhang Heting, military attache of the Chinese Embassy in the Democratic People's Republic of Korea, were Korean party and government leaders Kim Il, Hong Myung-hee, Park Geum-cheol, Jeong Il-ryong, and General Kim Kwang-hyeop, Minister of National Defense.
    Among those present at the reception held by Shen Shaoxing, military attache of our embassy in Czechoslovakia, were Lieutenant General Litryl, First Deputy Minister of National Defense and Chief of General Staff of the Czechoslovak People's Army, and Grigor, First Deputy Minister of Foreign Affairs.
    Cao Ying, the Chinese Ambassador to Czechoslovakia, also attended the reception.
    Among those present at the reception held by Jiang Yizhen, military attache of our embassy in Poland, were Sarazeki, Deputy Minister of National Defense of Poland, Czaplenski, Deputy Chief of General Staff, and senior generals. Wang Bingnan, the Chinese Ambassador to Poland, also attended the reception.
    Among those present at the reception held by Song Denghua, military attache of our embassy in the German Democratic Republic, were Kessler, Deputy Minister of National Defense of the German Democratic Republic, König, Deputy Minister of Foreign Affairs, and senior officers of the National People's Army.
    Among those present at the reception held by Xing Rongjie, military attache of our embassy in the Democratic Republic of Vietnam, were Nguyen Van Vinh and Ta Quang Buu, Deputy Ministers of National Defense of Vietnam, and senior generals of the People's Army.
    All the above receptions were held in an extremely warm and friendly atmosphere. At these receptions, the hosts and guests frequently toasted to the strengthening of the friendship between the armies and peoples of the two countries and to the defense of world peace.
    To celebrate the 32nd anniversary of the founding of the Chinese People's Liberation Army, the Tirana Garrison in Albania held a celebration gathering at the Central House of the People's Army on the evening of the 31st. Lieutenant General Bektashi, Deputy Minister of National Defense of Albania and Director of the Political Department of the People's Army, and other generals and senior officers attended the celebration.
    Wang Dong, First Secretary of our embassy in Albania, was invited to attend the celebration.
    Major General Shuti, Minister of Logistics of the Albanian People's Army, delivered a report on the history of the struggle of the Chinese People's Liberation Army at the meeting.

### 我驻兄弟国家武官  举行招待会庆祝建军节

1959-08-03
第4版()
专栏：

    我驻兄弟国家武官
    举行招待会庆祝建军节
    新华社2日讯　为庆祝中国人民解放军建军三十二周年，我国驻保加利亚、罗马尼亚、匈牙利、朝鲜、捷克斯洛伐克、波兰、德意志民主共和国、越南民主共和国的武官在7月31日和8月1日晚间分别举行了招待会。
    出席我国驻保加利亚大使馆代理武官王祥生中校举行的招待会的有保加利亚国防部第一副部长兼总参谋长比奇瓦罗夫、外交部副部长安格洛夫等。
    出席我国驻罗马尼亚大使馆武官梅永熙中校举行的招待会的有罗马尼亚武装部队部部长萨拉扬上将、内务部部长德勒吉奇上将，外交部副部长杜米特列斯库等。
    中国驻罗马尼亚大使许建国也出席了招待会。
    出席我国驻匈牙利大使馆武官胡彬甫上校的招待会的有匈牙利国防部长雷维斯·格佐上将，第一副部长乌斯塔·久洛中将以及匈人民军的高级将领。
    出席中国驻朝鲜民主主义人民共和国大使馆武官张禾亭上校举行的招待会的有朝鲜党和政府领导人金一、洪命熹、朴金哲、郑一龙以及民族保卫相金光侠大将等。
    出席我国驻捷克斯洛伐克大使馆沈少星上校举行的招待会的有捷克斯洛伐克国防部第一副部长兼人民军总参谋长里蒂尔中将、外交部第一副部长格里哥尔等。
    我国驻捷克斯洛伐克大使曹瑛也出席了招待会。
    出席我国驻波兰大使馆武官姜一震中校举行的酒会的有波兰国防部副部长萨热茨基、副总参谋长恰普伦斯基以及高级将领等。我国驻波兰大使王炳南也出席了酒会。
    出席我国驻德意志民主共和国大使馆武官宋登华上校举行的酒会的有德意志民主共和国国防部副部长凯斯勒、外交部副部长柯尼希以及国家人民军的高级军官。
    出席我国驻越南民主共和国大使馆武官邢荣杰大校举行的招待宴会的有越南国防部副部长阮文荣、谢光宝以及人民军高级将领。
    所有上述招待会都是在极其热烈友好的气氛中进行的。在这些招待会上，宾主双方频频为两国军队和人民之间的友谊的加强，为保卫世界和平而干杯。
    为了庆祝中国人民解放军建军三十二周年，阿尔巴尼亚首都地拉那卫戍部队31日晚在人民军中央之家举行了庆祝集会。阿尔巴尼亚国防部副部长、人民军政治部主任别克特希中将以及其他将领和高级军官参加了庆祝会。
    我驻阿尔巴尼亚大使馆一等秘书王栋应邀参加了庆祝会。
    阿尔巴尼亚人民军后勤部长舒蒂少将在会上作了介绍中国人民解放军斗争历史的报告。
