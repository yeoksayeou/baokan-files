Title: US President to Visit Western Europe

Author: 
Date: 1959-08-09
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, August 8th, TASS, London, August 7th: It has been officially announced here that US President Eisenhower will arrive in London on August 28th to meet with British Prime Minister Macmillan.

Xinhua News Agency, August 8th, TASS, Paris, August 7th: An official communique was issued here stating that US President Eisenhower will arrive in Paris on September 2nd.

### 美总统将赴西欧访问

1959-08-09
第4版()
专栏：

    美总统将赴西欧访问
    新华社8日讯　塔斯社伦敦7日讯：此间正式宣布，美国总统艾森豪威尔将在8月28日到达伦敦同英国首相麦克米伦会谈。
    新华社8日讯　塔斯社巴黎7日讯：此间发表正式公报说，美国总统艾森豪威尔将在9月2日到达巴黎。
