Title: Austrian Peace Delegation Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1959-08-14
Page: 4

　　Xinhua News Agency, August 13th: The Austrian Peace Delegation, invited by the Chinese People's Committee for World Peace to visit China, arrived in Beijing by plane today. The delegation was led by Professor Fritz Weber and consisted of ten people.

### 奥和平代表团到京

1959-08-14
第4版()
专栏：

　　奥和平代表团到京
    据新华社13日讯　应中国人民保卫世界和平委员会邀请来我国访问的奥地利和平代表团一行十人，在团长弗里茨·韦伯教授率领下，今天乘飞机到达北京。
