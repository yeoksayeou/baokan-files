Title: China-Iraq Football Teams Hold Friendly Match; "August 1st" Team Draws with Visiting Team
Author:
Date: 1959-08-03
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, August 2nd: The Chinese People's Liberation Army "August 1st" football team drew three to three with the Iraqi Republic Army football team in a friendly match today. The visiting team was leading three to one at the end of the first half.

After the game, Chen Yi, Vice Premier of the State Council, Ji Pengfei, Vice Minister of Foreign Affairs, Lieutenant General Liu Zhijian, Deputy Director of the General Political Department of the People's Liberation Army, Li Dequan, head of the Chinese women's delegation recently returned from Iraq, and Hou Debang, director of the China-Iraq Friendship Association, who watched the friendly match from the rostrum, went to the field to shake hands with the athletes of both sides and took photos with them.

The Iraqi Republic Army football team will soon leave Beijing to visit Shanghai and is scheduled to hold a friendly match with the Shanghai football team on the 7th.

### 中伊足球队举行友谊赛  “八一”队同客队踢成平局

1959-08-03
第4版()
专栏：

    中伊足球队举行友谊赛
    “八一”队同客队踢成平局
    据新华社2日讯　中国人民解放军“八一”足球队今天同伊拉克共和国军队足球队友谊比赛中，以三比三踢成平局。客队在上半场结束时曾以三比一领先。
    比赛结束后，在主席台上观看这场友谊比赛的国务院副总理陈毅、外交部副部长姬鹏飞、解放军总政治部副主任刘志坚中将、最近由伊拉克访问回国的中国妇女代表李德全和中国伊拉克友好协会理事侯德榜等人，都走到球场上同双方运动员握手，并同他们在一起合影。
    伊拉克共和国军队足球队即将离北京去上海访问，预定7日同上海足球队举行友谊比赛。
