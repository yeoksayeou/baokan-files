Title: Chinese Muslim Pilgrimage Delegation Leaves Morocco for Tunisia
Author:
Date: 1959-08-05
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, Rabat, 3rd: The Chinese Muslim Pilgrimage Delegation, led by Ma Zhuozhou, has concluded its visit to Morocco successfully and will leave here tonight for Tunisia.

The Chinese Ambassador to Morocco, Bai Ren, hosted a banquet for the delegation on August 1st. Moroccan religious figures, government officials, and diplomatic envoys attended the banquet.

The Chinese delegation visited twelve cities in Morocco, toured more than twenty mosques, and joined Moroccan Muslims in prayer.

### 我穆斯林朝觐代表团离摩洛哥去突尼斯

1959-08-05
第5版()
专栏：

    我穆斯林朝觐代表团离摩洛哥去突尼斯
    据新华社拉巴特3日电　由马卓洲率领的中国穆斯林朝觐代表团圆满地结束了在摩洛哥的访问，在今晚离开这里前往突尼斯。
    中国驻摩洛哥大使白认曾经在8月1日举行宴会招待代表团。摩洛哥宗教界人士、政府官员和外交使节参加了宴会。
    中国代表团在摩洛哥访问了十二个城市，参观了二十多个清真寺，同摩洛哥的穆斯林一起参加了祈祷。
