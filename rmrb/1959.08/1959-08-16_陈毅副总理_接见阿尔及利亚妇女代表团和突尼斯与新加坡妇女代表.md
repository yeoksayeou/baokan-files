Title: Vice Premier Chen Yi Receives Algerian Women's Delegation and Tunisian and Singaporean Women Representatives
Author:
Date: 1959-08-16
Page: 3

Xinhua News Agency, August 15th - Vice Premier Chen Yi received this afternoon the Algerian women's delegation headed by Mamiya Chenduf, which is currently visiting China, and four Tunisian women representatives: Safia Farhat, Zohra Ben Slimane, Gladys Ada, and Nayila Ben Amar.

Present at the reception were Cao Mengjun, member of the Presidium of the All-China Women's Federation, He Gongkai, Deputy Director of the West Asia and Africa Department of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs, and others.

Xinhua News Agency, August 15th - Vice Premier Chen Yi received Mrs. Lee George and Mrs. Sheelin Fodar, the Singaporean women's representatives, this evening. Present at the reception were Cao Mengjun, member of the Presidium of the All-China Women's Federation, and Shen Ping, Deputy Director of the First Asian Affairs Department of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs.

### 陈毅副总理　接见阿尔及利亚妇女代表团和突尼斯与新加坡妇女代表

1959-08-16
第3版()
专栏：

    陈毅副总理
    接见阿尔及利亚妇女代表团和突尼斯与新加坡妇女代表
    新华社15日讯　陈毅副总理今天下午接见了正在我国访问的以玛米亚·申杜芙团长为首的阿尔及利亚妇女代表团和突尼斯四位妇女代表：萨菲亚·法哈特、佐哈拉·本·斯里曼、格拉迪斯·阿达和奈依拉·本·阿玛尔。
    接见时在座的有：全国妇联主席团委员曹孟君、外交部西亚非洲司副司长何功楷等。
    新华社15日讯　陈毅副总理今晚接见了新加坡妇女代表李乔治夫人和希林·福达尔夫人。接见时在座的，有全国妇联主席团委员曹孟君和外交部第一亚洲司副司长沈平。
