Title: The U.S. Launches New Persecution of Communists Decides to Invoke the Notorious "Internal Security Act"
Author: 
Date: 1959-08-03
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, Washington, 31st dispatch: On July 30th, the U.S. Federal Court of Appeals, by a vote of two to one, passed a ruling to invoke the notorious so-called "Internal Security Act" to launch a new persecution against the American Communist Party. According to this reactionary law, the American Communist Party must register with the U.S. Department of Justice, hand over the list of all its members, and report the sources of its finances and the details of its publications. American communists are not allowed to participate in the work of government agencies, the law prohibits the issuance of passports to communists, and communists who are not included in the list will be subject to five years imprisonment and a fine of up to 10,000 U.S. dollars.

### 美国对共产党进行新迫害  决定引用臭名远扬的“国内安全法”

1959-08-03
第5版()
专栏：

    美国对共产党进行新迫害
    决定引用臭名远扬的“国内安全法”
    据新华社31日讯　华盛顿消息：7月30日美国联邦上诉法院以二比一的票数通过一项裁决，决定引用臭名远扬的所谓“国内安全法”对美国共产党进行新的迫害。根据这项反动的法律，美国共产党必须向美国司法部登记，交出全部成员的名单，报告财政来源和各种出版物的情况。美国共产党员无权参加政府机关的工作，法律禁止发给共产党员出国护照，没有列入名单的共产党员要受年五监禁和一万美元以内的罚款。
