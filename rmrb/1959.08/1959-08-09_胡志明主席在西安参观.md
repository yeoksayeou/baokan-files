Title: President Ho Chi Minh Visits Xi'an
Author:
Date: 1959-08-09
Page: 3
Xinhua News Agency, Xi'an, August 8th: President Ho Chi Minh, accompanied by Fang Zhongru, Secretary of the Secretariat of the Shaanxi Provincial Committee of the Chinese Communist Party, Li Qiming, Deputy Governor of Shaanxi Province, and Feng Zhi, Secretary of the Secretariat of the Xi'an Municipal Committee of the Chinese Communist Party, visited factories and historical sites in the Xi'an area today.

    In the morning, President Ho Chi Minh visited the Xi'an People's Enamel Factory, where he was warmly welcomed by the workers. The workers presented President Ho Chi Minh with carefully crafted enamel jars printed with Chairman Mao's "Qinyuan Spring" poem and a portrait of President Ho Chi Minh. President Ho Chi Minh also wrote an inscription for the workers as a souvenir. Afterward, President Ho Chi Minh toured Huaqing Pool in Lintong and ascended the more than fifty-zhang-high Mausoleum of Qin Shi Huang.

    In the afternoon, President Ho Chi Minh visited the Hūguó Xīngjiào Temple and the Du Gong Shrine in Chang'an County. Local people warmly welcomed President Ho Chi Minh. In the evening, President Ho Chi Minh watched a brilliant performance by the Xi'an Yisushe Opera Troupe and the Xi'an People's Song and Dance Troupe.

### 胡志明主席在西安参观

1959-08-09
第3版()
专栏：

    胡志明主席在西安参观
    新华社西安8日电　胡志明主席今天由中共陕西省委书记处书记方仲如、陕西省副省长李启明、中共西安市委书记处书记冯直等陪同，参观了西安地区工厂和名胜古迹。
    上午，胡志明主席参观了西安人民搪瓷厂，受到工人的热烈欢迎。工人们把精心制作的、上面印有毛主席沁园春词和胡志明主席像的搪瓷罐子送给胡志明主席，胡志明主席也为工人们题词留念。接着，胡志明主席游览了临潼华清池，并登上五十多丈高的秦始皇陵墓。
    下午，胡志明主席参观了长安县的护国兴教寺和杜公祠。当地群众热烈地欢迎了胡志明主席。晚上，胡志明主席观看了西安易俗社和西安人民歌舞团表演的精彩节目。
