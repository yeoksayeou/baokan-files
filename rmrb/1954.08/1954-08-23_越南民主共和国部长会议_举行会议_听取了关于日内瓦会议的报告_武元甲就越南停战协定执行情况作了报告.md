Title: The Council of Ministers of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam Holds a Meeting, Hears a Report on the Geneva Conference, and Vo Nguyen Giap Reports on the Implementation of the Vietnam Armistice Agreement
Author:
Date: 1954-08-23
Page: 4

(Xinhua News Agency, North Vietnam, August 22nd) According to Vietnam News Agency North Vietnam on August 21st: The Council of Ministers of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam held a meeting in mid-August 1954, presided over by President Ho Chi Minh. The Prime Minister's Office issued a communique on the meeting, the full text of which is as follows:

    I. The Council of Ministers heard a report from Deputy Prime Minister Pham Van Dong and members of the delegation of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam who participated in the Geneva Conference on the Geneva Conference. The Council of Ministers believes that the delegation has fully completed its task and won a great victory in the diplomatic struggle. The Council of Ministers congratulates the head of the delegation and all its members, and thanks the delegations of the fraternal countries.

    II. General Vo Nguyen Giap, Commander-in-Chief of the Vietnam People's Army, gave a report on the implementation of the Vietnam Armistice Agreement. The report stated that the Vietnam People's Army and the people have correctly implemented the ceasefire order and other provisions of the agreement.

    III. The Council of Ministers decided to establish a committee to receive the Vietnam People's Army and cadres withdrawing from South Vietnam. This committee will be led by Nguyen Van Tao, Minister of Labor.

    IV. The Council of Ministers decided to award medals to those who have made outstanding contributions in the War of Resistance, and special commemorative medals to government cadres and personnel who participated in the War of Resistance.

    V. The Council of Ministers decided to establish the Ministry of Information, with Hoang Minh Giam as Minister and To Huu as Deputy Minister.

    VI. The Council of Ministers decided that Deputy Prime Minister Pham Van Dong should continue to concurrently hold the post of Minister of Foreign Affairs.

    VII. The Council of Ministers appointed a delegation to visit the people and army in the newly liberated areas.

### 越南民主共和国部长会议 举行会议  听取了关于日内瓦会议的报告  武元甲就越南停战协定执行情况作了报告

1954-08-23
第4版()
专栏：

    越南民主共和国部长会议 举行会议
    听取了关于日内瓦会议的报告
    武元甲就越南停战协定执行情况作了报告
    【新华社二十二日讯】据越南通讯社北越二十一日讯：越南民主共和国部长会议于一九五四年八月月中在胡志明主席的主持下举行了一次会议。总理办公厅就这次会议发表了公报，全文如下：
    一、部长会议听取了范文同副总理以及参加日内瓦会议的越南民主共和国代表团团员们关于日内瓦会议的报告。部长会议认为，代表团已充分完成了它的任务，并在外交斗争中赢得了一个巨大的胜利。部长会议向代表团团长和全体团员表示祝贺，并向各兄弟国家的代表团致谢。
    二、越南人民军总司令武元甲将军就越南停战协定的执行情况作了一个报告。报告说，越南人民军和人民已正确地执行了停火命令以及协定中的其他规定。
    三、部长会议决定成立一个委员会来接待从南越撤出来的越南人民军和干部。这个委员会由劳动部部长阮文造来领导。
    四、部长会议决定以勋章授给那些在抗战中功勋卓著的人，并以特殊纪念章授给那些曾参加抗战的政府干部和人员。
    五、部长会议决定成立情报部，由黄明鉴任部长，素友任副部长。
    六、部长会议决定由副总理范文同继续兼任外交部部长之职。
    七、部长会议任命了一个代表团前往访问新解放区的人民和军队。
