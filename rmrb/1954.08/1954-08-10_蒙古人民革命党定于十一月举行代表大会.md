Title: Mongolian People's Revolutionary Party to Hold Congress in November
Author:
Date: 1954-08-10
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Ulaanbaatar, August 7th: TASS reported from Ulaanbaatar on the 7th that the Central Committee of the Mongolian People's Revolutionary Party recently held a plenary session. The plenary session decided to hold the 12th Congress of the Mongolian People's Revolutionary Party on November 18, 1954. The agenda is as follows:

    1. Summary report of the work of the Central Committee of the Mongolian People's Revolutionary Party, reported by Tsedenbal;
    2. Summary report of the work of the Central Auditing Commission of the Mongolian People's Revolutionary Party, reported by Buyamba;
    3. The 12th Party Congress's instructions on the Second Five-Year Plan of the Mongolian People's Republic for 1953-1957, reported by Shirindib;
    4. Revision of the Constitution of the Mongolian People's Revolutionary Party, reported by Damba;
    5. Election of the Party Central Organs.

### 蒙古人民革命党定于十一月举行代表大会

1954-08-10
第4版()
专栏：

    蒙古人民革命党定于十一月举行代表大会
    【新华社八日讯】塔斯社乌兰巴托七日讯：蒙古人民革命党中央委员会在最近举行了全体会议。全体会议决定在一九五四年十一月十八日举行蒙古人民革命党第十二次代表大会，议程如下：
    一、蒙古人民革命党中央委员会工作总结报告，报告人泽登巴尔；
    二、蒙古人民革命党中央检查委员会工作总结报告，报告人布雅姆巴；
    三、第十二次党代表大会关于一九五三—一九五七年蒙古人民共和国第二个五年计划的指示，报告人锡林迪布；
    四、修改蒙古人民革命党党章，报告人达姆巴；
    五、选举党中央机构。
