Title: British Labour Party Delegation Leaves Shanghai for Hangzhou
Author:
Date: 1954-08-30
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, Shanghai, August 29th. The British Labour Party delegation, headed by C.R. Attlee, left Shanghai by train for Hangzhou on the afternoon of the 29th, after a four-day visit to Shanghai.

Those who came to the station to see them off included: Jin Zhonghua and Shen Duanxian, directors of the Chinese People's Institute of Foreign Affairs in Shanghai; Pan Hannian, Deputy Mayor of Shanghai and First Deputy Secretary of the Shanghai Municipal Committee of the Chinese Communist Party; Xu Pingyu, Secretary-General of the Shanghai Municipal People's Government; Yu Peiwen, Acting Director of the Foreign Affairs Office; and responsible persons from the Shanghai Municipal Consultative Committee, the Shanghai local organizations of various democratic parties, Shanghai people's organizations, and people from the scientific, educational, cultural, artistic, and journalistic circles in Shanghai.

### 英工党代表团离上海去杭州

1954-08-30
第1版()
专栏：

    英工党代表团离上海去杭州
    【新华社上海二十九日电】以克·艾德礼为首的英国工党代表团在上海访问四天后，二十九日下午乘车离沪赴杭州。
    到车站欢送的有：中国人民外交学会在上海的理事金仲华、沈端先，上海市人民政府副市长、中国共产党上海市委员会第一副书记潘汉年以及上海市人民政府秘书长徐平羽、外事处代处长俞沛文、上海市协商委员会、各民主党派上海市地方组织、上海各人民团体的负责人和上海科学、教育、文化、艺术及新闻界人士等。
