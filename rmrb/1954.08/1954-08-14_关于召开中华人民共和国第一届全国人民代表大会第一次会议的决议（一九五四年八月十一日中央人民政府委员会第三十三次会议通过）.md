Title: Resolution on Convening the First Session of the First National People's Congress of the People's Republic of China (Adopted at the 33rd Meeting of the Central People's Government Committee on August 11, 1954)
Author:
Date: 1954-08-14
Page: 1

    Resolution on Convening the First Session of the First National People's Congress of the People's Republic of China
    (Adopted at the 33rd Meeting of the Central People's Government Committee on August 11, 1954)
    The 33rd Meeting of the Central People's Government Committee resolved: The First Session of the First National People's Congress of the People's Republic of China will be convened on September 15, 1954.

### 关于召开中华人民共和国第一届全国人民代表大会第一次会议的决议（一九五四年八月十一日中央人民政府委员会第三十三次会议通过）

1954-08-14
第1版()
专栏：

    关于召开中华人民共和国第一届全国人民代表大会第一次会议的决议
    （一九五四年八月十一日中央人民政府委员会第三十三次会议通过）
    中央人民政府委员会第三十三次会议决议：中华人民共和国第一届全国人民代表大会第一次会议，定于一九五四年九月十五日召开。
