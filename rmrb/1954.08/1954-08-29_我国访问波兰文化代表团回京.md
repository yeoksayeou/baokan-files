Title: Our Country's Cultural Delegation to Poland Returns to Beijing
Author:
Date: 1954-08-29
Page: 1

    Xinhua News Agency, August 24th—The Chinese cultural delegation headed by writer Yang Hansheng, which visited Poland according to the 1954 executive plan of the Sino-Polish Cultural Cooperation Agreement, returned to Beijing on the 24th.

### 我国访问波兰文化代表团回京

1954-08-29
第1版()
专栏：

    我国访问波兰文化代表团回京
    【新华社二十四日讯】根据中波文化合作协定一九五四年执行计划访问波兰的以作家阳翰笙为首的我国文化代表团已在二十四日返回北京。
