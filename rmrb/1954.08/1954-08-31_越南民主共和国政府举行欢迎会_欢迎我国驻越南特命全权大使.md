Title: Government of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam Holds Reception Welcoming China's Ambassador Extraordinary and Plenipotentiary to Vietnam
Author:
Date: 1954-08-31
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, North Vietnam, August 30th: The government of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam held a reception on the afternoon of the 28th in Thai Nguyen City, the capital of the Viet Bac Interzone, to welcome Luo Guibo, the People's Republic of China's Ambassador Extraordinary and Plenipotentiary to the Democratic Republic of Vietnam.

The reception was presided over by Pham Van Dong, Deputy Prime Minister and Minister of Foreign Affairs of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam.

The government representatives attending the reception included Minister of National Defense Vo Nguyen Giap, Deputy Minister of Finance Trinh Van Binh, Deputy Minister of Transportation Nguyen Van Tran, Deputy Minister of Industry and Commerce Dang Viet Chau, and Deputy Minister of Justice Tran Cong Tuong.

Also attending the reception were Ton Quang Phiet, member of the Standing Committee of the National Assembly of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam; Tran Cong and Pho Xuan Luan, members of the National Committee of the Vietnam National United Front; Chu Van Tan, Chairman of the Administrative Committee of the Viet Bac Interzone; and Vu Hung, Chairman of the Administrative Committee of Thai Nguyen Province.

Also attending the reception were Le Thanh Nghi, Central Committee member representing the Central Committee of the Vietnam Workers' Party, and representatives of various democratic parties and people's organizations such as Duong Duc Hien, Hoang Thi Ai, Le Xuan Dong, and Thi Son representing the Vietnam-China Friendship Association.

At the meeting, Deputy Prime Minister Pham Van Dong delivered a welcoming speech on behalf of the Vietnamese government and people. He said: I am pleased to extend my greetings to the great Chinese people and the People's Republic of China. The People's Republic of China is a major country in the world and a pillar of peace in Asia, and it has recently made great contributions to promoting the brilliant achievements of the Geneva Conference. The fact that the Central People's Government of the People's Republic of China has sent an ambassador to the Democratic Republic of Vietnam means that the friendly unity and relations between the Vietnamese and Chinese peoples will be strengthened, consolidated, and developed in order to safeguard peace in Southeast Asia and the world. Deputy Prime Minister Pham Van Dong concluded by wishing Ambassador Luo Guibo good health and success in completing his great mission.

Ambassador Luo Guibo delivered a reply. He first expressed his gratitude for the warm reception from the government and people of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam. He said: The peoples of China and Vietnam have always had fraternal and close friendly relations, and since the establishment of the People's Republic of China, this friendly relationship has greatly developed. I firmly believe that this profound relationship between our two countries will continue to develop and consolidate in the future, thereby helping to enhance the strength of the world peace forces headed by the Soviet Union and helping to maintain and consolidate peace in Asia and the world.

### 越南民主共和国政府举行欢迎会  欢迎我国驻越南特命全权大使

1954-08-31
第1版()
专栏：

    越南民主共和国政府举行欢迎会
    欢迎我国驻越南特命全权大使
    【新华社北越三十日电】越南民主共和国政府二十八日下午在越北联区首府太原市举行欢迎会，欢迎中华人民共和国驻越南民主共和国特命全权大使罗贵波。
  欢迎会由越南民主共和国政府副总理兼外交部部长范文同主持。
    出席欢迎会的有政府代表国防部部长武元甲、财政部副部长郑文炳、交通部副部长阮文真、工商部副部长邓曰洲和司法部副部长陈公祥。
    出席欢迎会的还有：越南民主共和国国民大会常务委员会委员孙光阀，越南国民联合战线全国委员会委员陈弓、傅春律，越北联区行政委员会主席朱文晋和太原省行政委员会主席武兴等。
  出席欢迎会的还有：越南劳动党中央委员会代表中央委员黎成仪，和各民主党派、人民团体的代表杨德贤、黄氏爱、黎春同及越中友好协会代表诗山等。
    会上，范文同副总理代表越南政府和人民致欢迎辞。他说：我高兴地向伟大的中国人民和中华人民共和国致意。中华人民共和国是世界上一个大国和亚洲的和平支柱，它最近在促使日内瓦会议获得辉煌成就方面曾作了巨大的贡献。中华人民共和国中央人民政府派遣大使驻在越南民主共和国，这个事实意味着：越南人民和中国人民之间的友好团结和关系将日益加强、日益巩固和发展，以便维护东南亚和全世界的和平。范文同副总理最后祝贺罗贵波大使身体健康并胜利地完成他的伟大任务。
    罗贵波大使致答辞。他首先对越南民主共和国政府和人民的热烈接待表示感谢。他说：中、越两国人民历来就具有兄弟般的、密切的友好关系，自从中华人民共和国成立以来，这种友好关系已有了很大的发展。我深信，我们两国之间这种深厚关系今后必将日益发展和巩固，从而有助于以苏联为首的世界和平力量的增强，有助于维护和巩固亚洲和世界的和平。
