Title: Viet Lien Front and Vietnam Peace Committee Hold Expanded Meeting, Calling on All Vietnamese People to Complete Tasks of the New Stage
Author:
Date: 1954-08-03
Page: 4

    Xinhua News Agency, North Vietnam, August 30th: The National Committee of the Vietnam National United Front and the Vietnam Committee for the Defense of World Peace held a joint expanded meeting on July 25 to study the new situation and new tasks, especially the Agreement on the Cessation of Hostilities in Indochina signed at the Geneva Conference.
    Sun Duc Thang, Chairman of the National Committee of the Vietnam National United Front, solemnly announced this great diplomatic victory in his opening remarks. He reiterated the Vietnam National United Front and the Vietnam Committee for the Defense of World Peace's consistent endorsement of the peace policy adopted by the Government of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam. He called on all the people to unite in thought and action to carry out the new tasks.
    Huang Quoc Viet, representative of the Central Executive Committee of the Vietnam Workers' Party, then spoke. He gave a detailed analysis of the new situation and pointed out the great significance of the new victory and the new tasks and work prescribed by President Ho Chi Minh, the Vietnam Workers' Party, and the Government of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam.
    Dr. Le Dinh Tham, Chairman of the Vietnam Committee for the Defense of World Peace, reported on the special meeting of the World Peace Council in Berlin and the international conference on easing the situation held in Stockholm.
    Congratulatory messages from friendly countries congratulating the Vietnamese people on their diplomatic victory, especially the congratulatory messages from the Presidium of the Supreme Soviet of the Soviet Union, the Council of Ministers of the Soviet Union, and Chairman Mao Zedong to President Ho Chi Minh, have clearly demonstrated the warm support of the people of the world for the Vietnamese people's struggle for peace, unification, independence, and democracy. These congratulatory messages were warmly welcomed, and the attendees responded with sustained applause.
    At the closing meeting, representatives from the Vietnam Democratic Party, the Vietnam Socialist Party, the Vietnam General Confederation of Labor, the Vietnam Farmers' Association, the Vietnam Youth League, the Vietnam Women's Federation, and other organizations expressed their approval of the Agreement on the Cessation of Hostilities in Indochina signed at the Geneva Conference.
    The joint expanded meeting of the National Committee of the Vietnam National United Front and the Vietnam Committee for the Defense of World Peace also wrote letters to President Ho Chi Minh, the Government of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam, the Central Executive Committee of the Vietnam Workers' Party, and the Chief Representative of the Vietnamese delegation to the Geneva Conference, Deputy Prime Minister and Acting Minister of Foreign Affairs Pham Van Dong, congratulating them on the signing of the ceasefire agreement and assuring them of their faithful adherence to these agreements.
    The meeting also passed a resolution containing the following points:
      (1) Fully endorse the Agreement on the Cessation of Hostilities in Indochina and the final declaration of the Geneva Conference on the Indochina issue;
    (2) Express warm congratulations to the delegation of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam to the Geneva Conference, headed by Deputy Prime Minister and Acting Minister of Foreign Affairs Pham Van Dong; congratulate the Soviet delegation and the delegation of the People's Republic of China; congratulate the French people, the people of Asian countries, and the peace-loving people of the world who have struggled for the success of the Geneva Conference;
      (3) Firmly trust the leadership of President Ho Chi Minh, the Vietnam Workers' Party, and the Government of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam;
      (4) Call on all the people to faithfully abide by the provisions of the ceasefire agreement and demand that the other party also strictly fulfill them; maintain a high degree of vigilance against the conspiracies of peace saboteurs;
      (5) Mobilize all the people to complete the tasks currently put forward due to the new situation.

### 越联战线和越南和平委员会举行扩大会议  号召全越人民完成新阶段的任务

1954-08-03
第4版()
专栏：

    越联战线和越南和平委员会举行扩大会议
    号召全越人民完成新阶段的任务
    【新华社三十一日讯】越南通讯社北越三十日讯：越南国民联合战线全国委员会和越南保卫世界和平委员会在七月二十五日举行联合扩大会议来研究新形势和新任务，特别是研究日内瓦会议上签订的印度支那停止敌对行动协定。
    越南国民联合战线全国委员会主席孙德胜在开幕词中庄严地宣布了这次外交上的巨大胜利。他重申了越南国民联合战线和越南保卫世界和平委员会对越南民主共和国政府所采取的和平政策一致表示赞同。他号召全体人民在思想上和行动上团结起来，以执行新任务。
    越南劳动党中央执行委员会代表黄国越接着发言。他对新的形势作了一次详细的分析，并指出了新胜利的伟大意义以及胡志明主席、越南劳动党和越南民主共和国政府所规定的新任务和新工作。
    越南保卫世界和平委员会主席黎廷探博士作了关于世界和平理事会柏林特别会议和在斯德哥尔摩举行的缓和局势国际会议的报告。
    从各友好国家拍来的祝贺越南人民的外交胜利的贺电，特别是苏联最高苏维埃主席团、苏联部长会议和毛泽东主席致胡志明主席的贺电，已清楚地表明了全世界人民对越南人民为争取和平、统一、独立和民主而进行的斗争的热烈支持。这些贺电受到了热烈欢迎，与会者报以经久不息的掌声。
    在闭幕会议上，越南民主党、越南社会党、越南总工会、越南农民联合会、越南青年联盟、越南妇女联合会以及其他组织的代表们都对日内瓦会议上签订的印度支那停战协定表示赞同。
    越南国民联合战线全国委员会和越南保卫世界和平委员会联合举行的扩大会议还写信给胡志明主席，越南民主共和国政府，越南劳动党中央执行委员会以及出席日内瓦会议的越南代表团首席代表、副总理兼代理外交部长范文同，祝贺停战协定的签订并保证忠实地履行这些协定。
    会议还通过一项决议，内容有以下几点：
      （一）完全赞同印度支那停止敌对行动协定和日内瓦会议关于印度支那问题的最后宣言；
    （二）向以副总理兼代理外交部部长范文同为首的出席日内瓦会议的越南民主共和国代表团表示热烈祝贺；向苏联代表团和中华人民共和国代表团表示祝贺；向曾为日内瓦会议的成功而斗争的法国人民、亚洲各国人民和世界爱好和平人民表示祝贺；
      （三）坚定地信赖胡志明主席、越南劳动党和越南民主共和国政府的领导；
      （四）号召全体人民忠实地遵守停战协定的规定，并要求对方也严格履行；对和平破坏者的阴谋保持高度警惕；
      （五）动员全体人民完成目前因新的形势而提出的任务。
