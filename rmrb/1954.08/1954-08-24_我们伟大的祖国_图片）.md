Author:
Date: 1954-08-24
Page: 1

    Our Great Motherland
    The close unity of the various ethnic groups of China has been further consolidated and developed with the establishment of the system of people's congresses. Among the 392 delegates to the First Session of the First People's Congress of Yunnan Province, there were 161 representatives representing 28 ethnic minorities. This is a scene of the enthusiastic entry of the representatives of the various ethnic groups.

### 我们伟大的祖国(图片）

1954-08-24
第1版()
专栏：

    我们伟大的祖国
    中国各民族人民亲密团结的关系，随着人民代表大会制度的建立，更加巩固和发展了。云南省第一届人民代表大会第一次会议的代表三百九十二人中，就有代表着二十八个少数民族的一百六十一名代表。这是各民族人民的代表们入场时的热烈情况。
