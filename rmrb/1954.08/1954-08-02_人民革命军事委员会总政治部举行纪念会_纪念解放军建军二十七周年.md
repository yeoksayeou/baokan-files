Title: General Political Department of the People's Revolutionary Military Commission Holds Meeting to Commemorate the 27th Anniversary of the Founding of the People's Liberation Army
Author:
Date: 1954-08-02
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, August 1st: The General Political Department of the People's Revolutionary Military Commission of the Central People's Government solemnly held a meeting in the capital on August 1st to celebrate the 27th anniversary of the founding of the Chinese People's Liberation Army ("August 1st").
    Those who attended the commemorative meeting were: Zhu De, Vice Chairman of the Central People's Government and Commander-in-Chief of the Chinese People's Liberation Army; Luo Ruiqing, member of the People's Revolutionary Military Commission of the Central People's Government; Zhang Zongxun, Deputy Chief of General Staff of the People's Revolutionary Military Commission; Fu Zhong, Xiao Hua, and Gan Siqi, Deputy Directors of the General Political Department; Xu Liqing, Deputy Minister of the General Cadre Department; Xiao Ke, Minister of the Military Training Department; Zhang Lingbin, Deputy Minister of the General Logistics Department; Huang Yongsheng, Deputy Commander of the Central South Military Region of the Chinese People's Liberation Army; Zhu Liangcai, Deputy Political Commissar and Director of the Political Department of the North China Military Region; Xiao Xiangrong, Director of the General Office of the Military Commission; and Chen Geng, Wang Zhen, Chen Xilian, Xu Guangda, Chen Shiju, Su Zhenhua, Wang Hongkun, Luo Shun, Wu Faxian, Zhang Zhen, Fu Qiutao, Mo Wenhao, Liu Zhijian, Qiu Chuancheng, Wang Bingzhang, Li Shouhuan, Fang Qiang, Gu Jingsheng, and Tang Tianji, heads of various departments and services of the People's Revolutionary Military Commission of the Central People's Government, as well as senior cadres, combat heroes, and model workers from various organs and units of the Chinese People's Liberation Army stationed in Beijing, totaling more than 1,500 people.
    Deng Hua, Deputy Commander of the Chinese People's Volunteers, was also invited to attend the meeting.
    The commemorative meeting began with the band playing the national anthem and the march of the Chinese People's Liberation Army.
    The commemorative meeting was presided over by Xiao Hua, Deputy Director of the General Political Department of the People's Revolutionary Military Commission of the Central People's Government.
    After the meeting began, representatives of the Young Pioneers of the capital first presented flowers to Commander-in-Chief Zhu De of the Chinese People's Liberation Army.
    Commander-in-Chief Zhu De of the Chinese People's Liberation Army delivered a speech at the meeting (full text to be published separately).
    After the meeting, the Central Song and Dance Troupe performed wonderful song and dance programs.

### 人民革命军事委员会总政治部举行纪念会  纪念解放军建军二十七周年

1954-08-02
第1版()
专栏：

    人民革命军事委员会总政治部举行纪念会
    纪念解放军建军二十七周年
    据新华社一日讯：中央人民政府人民革命军事委员会总政治部八月一日在首都隆重举行中国人民解放军“八一”建军二十七周年纪念大会。
    出席纪念大会的有：中央人民政府副主席、中国人民解放军总司令朱德，中央人民政府人民革命军事委员会委员罗瑞卿，人民革命军事委员会副总参谋长张宗逊，总政治部副主任傅钟、萧华、甘泗淇，总干部部副部长徐立清，军事训练部部长萧克，总后勤部副部长张令彬、中国人民解放军中南军区副司令员黄永胜，华北军区副政治委员兼政治部主任朱良才，军委办公厅主任萧向荣以及中央人民政府人民革命军事委员会各部门、各兵种首长陈赓、王震、陈锡联、许光达、陈士榘、苏振华、王宏坤、罗舜初、吴法宪、张震、傅秋涛、莫文骅、刘志坚、邱创成、王秉璋、李寿轩、方强、谷景生、唐天际和中国人民解放军驻京各机关、部队的高级干部、战斗英雄、工作模范等一千五百多人。
    中国人民志愿军副司令员邓华也应邀参加了大会。
    纪念大会在乐队高奏国歌和中国人民解放军进行曲声中开始。
    纪念大会由中央人民政府人民革命军事委员会总政治部副主任萧华主持。
    大会开始后，首先由首都少年先锋队代表向中国人民解放军朱总司令献花。
    中国人民解放军总司令朱德在大会上发表了讲话（全文另发）。
    大会结束后，由中央歌舞团演出了精彩的歌舞节目。
