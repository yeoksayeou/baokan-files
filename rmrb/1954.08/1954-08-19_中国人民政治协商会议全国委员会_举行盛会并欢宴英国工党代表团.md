Title: Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference National Committee Holds Grand Gathering and Fetes British Labour Party Delegation
Author:
Date: 1954-08-19
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, August 18th: The National Committee of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference (CPPCC) held a grand reception and banquet at 6 p.m. on August 18th to welcome the British Labour Party delegation led by Clement Attlee, who were invited to visit China.

The reception and banquet were presided over by Guo Moruo and Chen Shutong, Vice-Chairmen of the CPPCC National Committee.

Members of the British Labour Party delegation attending the reception and banquet were: Clement Attlee, Aneurin Bevan, E. Summerskill, W. Burke, Morgan Phillips, S. Watson, Harry Earnshaw, and the delegation's translators, Mr. and Mrs. Michael Lindsay.

The British Chargé d'Affaires in China, D.W. Gage, was also invited to attend.

Chinese attendees included: Li Jishen and Shen Junru, Vice-Chairmen of the CPPCC National Committee; Li Weihan, Secretary-General; Standing Committee members Jiang Guangnai, Zhang Bojun, Huang Yanpei, Zhang Naiqi, Yang Xiufeng, Shen Yanbing, Liang Xi, Shao Lizi, Luo Longji, Ma Yinchu, Xu Guangping, Huang Qixiang, Xu Deheng, Chen Qiyou, Jiang Nanxiang, Li Siguang, Sheng Pihua, Li Dequan, Wu Yaozong, and CPPCC members in Beijing.

Also in attendance were: Deng Xiaoping, Member and Secretary-General of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of China; Central Committee members He Long, Lu Dingyi, Liao Chengzhi, Wang Jiaxiang; Alternate Central Committee members Zhang Jichun, Ma Mingfang, Lü Zhengcao, Wang Congwu, Xi Zhongxun, Liu Lantao; Yu Zhenying, Li Shizhang, Chen Shaoxian, Chen Qiyuan, Mei Gongbin, Ning Wu, and Cai Tingkai, Standing Committee members of the Central Committee of the Revolutionary Committee of the Chinese Kuomintang; Zhou Xinmin, Pan Guangdan, Zhou Jingwen, Hu Yuzhi, Shen Zhiyuan, and Li Wenyi, Standing Committee members of the Central Committee of the China Democratic League; Li Chenggan, Zhou Shiguan, Sun Xiaocun, Sun Qimeng, Pu Jiexiu, Ling Qijun, Zhang Yuanshan, Zhang Jiongbo, Yang Weiyu, Yang Meizhen, Liu Yifeng, and Chen Weiji, Standing Committee members of the China Democratic National Construction Association; Ge Zhicheng, Yan Jingyao, and Lin Handa, Standing Standing Directors of the China Association for Promoting Democracy; Peng Zemin, Vice Chairman of the Chinese Peasants' and Workers' Democratic Party; Li Boqiu, Zhang Yunchuan, and Wang Renxuan, members of the Central Executive Bureau; Yan Xichun and Huang Dingchen, Standing Committee members of the China Zhi Gong Party; Yuan Hanqing, Lao Junzhan, Yang Zhaoxuan, Pan Shu, Xue Yu, Wei Jiangong, and Yan Jici, Standing Committee members of the Jiusan Society; Li Chunqing, Vice Chairman of the Taiwan Democratic Self-Government League; Hu Yaobang, Luo Yi, Ou Tangliang, and Zhang Ze, Secretaries of the Central Secretariat of the Chinese New Democratic Youth League.

Also present were: Zhang Xiruo, President of the Chinese People's Institute of Foreign Affairs; Zhou Gengsheng, Chen Hansheng, Qian Duansheng, and Qiao Guanhua, Vice Presidents; Lai Ruoyu, Chairman of the All-China Federation of Trade Unions; Zhu Xuefan, Vice Chairman; Zhang Yun, Vice-Chairman of the All-China Democratic Women's Federation; Wu Han, Vice-Chairman of the All-China Youth Federation; Guan Ruoluan, Vice-Chairman of the All-China Students' Federation; Zhou Yang, Vice-Chairman of the China Federation of Literary and Art Circles; Rong Yiren, Huang Changshui, and Hu Ziang, Deputy Directors of the All-China Federation of Industry and Commerce; Wu Youxun, Vice-Chairman of the All-China Federation of Natural Science Societies; Zhu Kezhen and Mao Yisheng, Vice-Chairmen of the All-China Association for Science and Technology Popularization; Chu Tunan, President of the Chinese People's Association for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries; Ding Xilin, Vice President; Zhang Qilong and Meng Yongqian, Deputy Directors of the All-China Federation of Supply and Marketing Cooperatives; Xiao Hua, Vice-Chairman of the All-China Sports Federation; Qian Junrui, Secretary-General of the Sino-Soviet Friendship Association; Wu Yunfu, Secretary-General of the China People's Relief Administration; Ma Songting, Deputy Director of the China Hui People's Cultural Advancement Association; Juezan, Deputy Secretary-General of the Buddhist Association of China; Da Pousheng, Deputy Director of the China Islamic Association; Chen Jianzhen and Chen Chonggui, Vice-Chairmen of the Three-Self Patriotic Movement Committee of Protestant Churches of China; Ji Chaoding, Secretary-General of the China Council for the Promotion of International Trade.

Also present were: Zhang Hanfu and Wu Xiuquan, Vice Ministers of Foreign Affairs of the Central People's Government; Ye Jizhuang, Minister of Foreign Trade; Lei Renmin, Vice Minister; Zheng Zhenduo, Vice Minister of Culture; Wei Que, Vice Minister of Education; He Cheng, Vice Minister of Health, as well as prominent figures from the scientific, educational, literary, and journalistic circles in Beijing and representatives of minority nationalities.

Foreign journalists in Beijing were also invited to attend the reception and banquet.

More than 600 people attended the reception and banquet.

The national flags of China and Britain were hung in the reception hall. At the beginning of the reception, Guo Moruo, Vice-Chairman of the CPPCC National Committee, delivered a speech of welcome (full text to be published separately). Following this, Mr. Clement Attlee and Mr. Aneurin Bevan of the British Labour Party delegation both spoke (full text to be published separately).

At the ensuing banquet, the hosts and guests repeatedly rose their glasses to toast the development of Sino-British friendship.

The reception and banquet proceeded in a friendly atmosphere.

### 中国人民政治协商会议全国委员会  举行盛会并欢宴英国工党代表团

1954-08-19
第1版()
专栏：

    中国人民政治协商会议全国委员会
    举行盛会并欢宴英国工党代表团
    【新华社十八日讯】中国人民政治协商会议全国委员会十八日下午六时举行盛大欢迎会和宴会，欢迎应邀来我国访问的以克·艾德礼为首的英国工党代表团。
    欢迎会和宴会由中国人民政治协商会议全国委员会副主席郭沫若、陈叔通分别主持。
    出席欢迎会和宴会的英国工党代表团人员有：克·艾德礼、安·比万、埃·萨末斯基尔、韦·伯克、摩·菲利普斯、塞·华生、哈·厄恩萧和代表团翻译林迈可夫妇。
