Title: U.S. Under Secretary of State Smith Resigns; Eisenhower Nominates Herbert Hoover Jr. as Successor
Author:
Date: 1954-08-20
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Washington, Eighteenth. Washington dispatch: U.S. authorities announced on the 17th that Walter Bedell Smith, U.S. Under Secretary of State who replaced U.S. Secretary of State Dulles as the chief representative of the U.S. delegation during the Geneva Conference, had submitted his resignation to U.S. President Eisenhower on the 17th. Eisenhower accepted Smith's resignation on the same day and immediately requested the Senate to approve the appointment of Herbert Hoover Jr., son of former President Herbert Clark Hoover, as the new Under Secretary of State.

### 美国副国务卿史密斯辞职  艾森豪威尔提名小胡佛继任

1954-08-20
第4版()
专栏：

    美国副国务卿史密斯辞职
    艾森豪威尔提名小胡佛继任
    【新华社十八日讯】华盛顿消息：美国当局十七日宣布在日内瓦会议期间接替美国国务卿杜勒斯担任美国代表团首席代表的美国副国务卿华尔特·贝德尔·史密斯于十七日已向美国总统艾森豪威尔提出辞职。艾森豪威尔已于当天接受史密斯的辞职，并随即提请参议院批准前总统赫伯特·克拉克·胡佛之子小赫伯特·胡佛继任副国务卿。
