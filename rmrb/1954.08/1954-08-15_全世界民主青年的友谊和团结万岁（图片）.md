Title: Long Live the Friendship and Solidarity of Democratic Youth Worldwide (Pictures)
Author:
Date: 1954-08-15
Page: 5

Long Live the Friendship and Solidarity of Democratic Youth Worldwide
The World Federation of Democratic Youth Council meeting in Beijing solemnly opened in Huairen Hall, Beijing on August 9th. The picture on the right shows the presidium of the meeting. In the center is Bruno Bernini, Chairman of the World Federation of Democratic Youth, giving the opening speech. Xinhua News Agency reporter Qi Guanshan.

Hu Yaobang, head of the Chinese youth delegation and Secretary of the Central Committee of the Chinese New Democratic Youth League, delivered a congratulatory speech to the meeting. Xinhua News Agency reporter Qi Guanshan.

The venue of the World Federation of Democratic Youth Council meeting in Beijing. Xinhua News Agency draft Jia Huamin. During a break in the meeting, the delegates chatted happily. Our reporter Gao Liang.

Youth representatives from all walks of life in Beijing warmly welcomed the delegates from various countries arriving in Beijing to attend the World Federation of Democratic Youth Council meeting in Beijing at the airport. Xinhua News Agency reporter Yu Huiru.

The youth of the Liu Yongchun Mutual Aid Group in Shizipo, East Suburb of Beijing, are selecting the largest tomatoes as gifts for the youth delegates from various countries attending the World Federation of Democratic Youth Council meeting in Beijing. China Youth Daily draft Li Jianhua.

Many shop windows on the streets of the capital display photos or propaganda posters about the activities of the World Federation of Democratic Youth to welcome the opening of the World Federation of Democratic Youth Council meeting in Beijing. This is the staff of the Xinhua Bookstore in Beijing decorating the windows. China Youth Daily draft Hong Ke.

The speeches of the delegates from various countries attending the World Federation of Democratic Youth Council meeting in Beijing were warmly welcomed by the audience. This is Hoang Truc, the representative of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam, speaking at the meeting. Our reporter Gao Liang.

After Hoang Truc, the representative of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam, spoke at the meeting, Leon Claude Rollandis, the representative of France, stepped forward and shook hands with him warmly. Xinhua News Agency reporter Qi Guanshan.

Marie Molvan (France, left) and Mamani Abdoul (Niger, right) in the lounge. Our reporter Gao Liang.

The All-China Federation of Democratic Youth held a grand banquet at the Xinqiao Hotel in Beijing at 9:00 PM on August 11 to entertain the delegates from various countries attending the World Federation of Democratic Youth Council meeting in Beijing. Our reporter Gao Liang.

During a break in the meeting, the delegates and Chinese young men and women chatted excitedly. Our reporter Gao Liang.

### 全世界民主青年的友谊和团结万岁（图片）

1954-08-15
第5版()
专栏：

    全世界民主青年的友谊和团结万岁
    世界民主青年联盟理事会北京会议八月九日在北京怀仁堂隆重开幕。右图是大会主席团。中立者为世界民主青年联盟主席布伦诺·柏尼尼在致开会词。                        新华社记者　齐观山摄
    中国青年代表团团长、中国新民主主义青年团中央委员会书记胡耀邦向大会致祝词。                      新华社记者　齐观山摄
    世界民主青年联盟理事会北京会议的会场。新华社稿　贾化民摄在会议休息时，代表们愉快地交谈着。        本报记者　高粮摄
    北京市各界青年代表在飞机场热烈欢迎陆续来北京参加世界民主青年联盟理事会北京会议的各国代表。      新华社记者　喻惠如摄
    北京市东郊十字坡刘永春互助组的青年们正在挑选最大的番茄，作为送给参加世界民主青年联盟理事会北京会议的各国青年代表的礼物。
    中国青年报稿　李建华摄
    首都街道上的许多橱窗都陈列着有关世界民主青年联盟活动的照片或宣传画，以迎接世界民主青年联盟理事会北京会议的召开。这是北京新华书店的工作人员在布置橱窗。　中国青年报稿　洪克摄
    出席世界民主青年联盟理事会北京会议的各国代表的发言，受到了全场的热烈欢迎。这是越南民主共和国代表胡竹在大会上发言。　本报记者　高粮摄
    越南民主共和国代表胡竹在会议上发言后，法国代表利昂·克劳德·罗兰奇上前和他热烈地握手。新华社记者　齐观山摄
    玛丽·莫尔凡（法国，左）和马曼尼·阿卜杜勒（尼日尔，右）在休息室里。本报记者　高粮摄
    中华全国民主青年联合会于八月十一日下午九时在北京新侨饭店举行盛大宴会，招待世界民主青年联盟理事会北京会议的各国代表。本报记者　高粮摄
    在会议休息时，代表们和中国男女青年们兴奋地交谈着。本报记者　高粮摄
