Title: British Labour Party Delegation Visits and Tours Beijing
Author: 
Date: 1954-08-18
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, August 17th: Members of the British Labour Party delegation spent the 17th in Beijing visiting, attending discussions, and touring Beijing's scenic spots and historical sites.

    In the morning, delegation members Mr. Aneurin Bevan, Mr. W. Burke, Mr. Morgan Phillips, and Mr. Harry Earnshaw visited the Ministry of Foreign Trade of the Central People's Government, meeting with Minister of Foreign Trade Ye Ji-zhuang, Vice Minister Lei Renmin, Director of the Third Bureau Lu Xuzhang, Deputy Director of the General Administration of Customs Ding Guitang, Deputy Manager of the China National Import and Export Corporation and head of the Chinese Trade Delegation to Britain Cao Zhongshu, and others. The two sides held discussions on Sino-British trade issues.

    In the morning, Mrs. Eirene White visited Minister of Health of the Central People's Government Li Dequan, Vice Minister He Cheng, and also visited the Beijing Municipal Maternity and Child Health Hospital.

    In the morning, Mr. Clement Attlee and delegation members Mr. S. Watson and Mr. Henry Franklin held discussions with Chinese Christian and Catholic figures. Those present included: Chairman of the Three-Self Patriotic Movement Committee of Protestant Churches in China Wu Yaozong, Bishop of the Anglican Church of China Chen Jianzhen, President of Chongqing Theological Seminary Chen Chonggui, President of the Anglican Church of China Zhao Zichen, Baptist Pastor Li Chuwen, Deputy Dean of Studies of Yenching Union Theological Seminary Zhao Fusan, Father of the Beijing Catholic Southern Cathedral Li Yin-chou, and Father of the Beijing Catholic Eastern Cathedral Ma Wenchun.

    In the afternoon, the entire delegation, headed by Mr. Clement Attlee, visited the Temple of Heaven, Beijing's beautiful ancient architecture. Afterwards, some members of the delegation also visited Beihai Park.

    Prior to this, on the morning of the 16th, the entire delegation, headed by Mr. Clement Attlee, visited the Forbidden City.

### 英国工党代表团人员在北京访问和游览

1954-08-18
第1版()
专栏：

    英国工党代表团人员在北京访问和游览
    【新华社十七日讯】英国工党代表团人员十七日在北京分别进行访问、座谈，并游览了北京的名胜风景区。
    上午，代表团团员安·比万先生、韦·伯克先生、摩·菲利普斯先生、哈·厄恩萧先生访问中央人民政府对外贸易部，会见了对外贸易部部长叶季壮、副部长雷任民、第三局局长卢绪章，海关总署副署长丁贵堂，中国进出口公司副经理、中国赴英贸易访问团团长曹中枢等，双方并就中英贸易问题进行座谈。
    代表团团员埃·萨末斯基尔夫人在上午会见了中央人民政府卫生部部长李德全、副部长贺诚，并参观了北京市妇幼保健院。
    克·艾德礼先生和代表团团员塞·华生先生、亨·弗兰克林先生在上午和我国基督教、天主教人士举行了座谈。在座的有：中国基督教三自爱国运动委员会主席吴耀宗，中华圣公会主教院主教陈见真，重庆神学院院长陈崇桂，中华圣公会会长赵紫宸，中华浸会牧师李储文，燕京协和神学院副教务长赵复三，北京天主教南堂神甫李荫挑，北京天主教东堂神甫马文纯。
    下午，以克·艾德礼先生为首的代表团全体人员游览了北京优美的古代建筑——天坛，随后部分团员又到北海公园游览。
    在此之前，以克·艾德礼先生为首的代表团全体人员曾在十六日上午游览了故宫。
