Title: Jiangsu, Yunnan, and Shanxi Provinces, and the Cities of Lüda and Harbin Held People's Congress Meetings, Respectively Electing Deputies to Attend the National People's Congress Meeting
Author:
Date: 1954-08-13
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Nanjing, August 11th: The first session of the First People's Congress of Jiangsu Province was held in Nanjing. A total of 449 delegates attended the meeting. The meeting opened on the 5th and closed on the 11th.

After discussion, all delegates unanimously agreed with the government's report on the work of the past year and a half and the province's current tasks, and the meeting adopted a resolution approving the report. The meeting also decided to unite the people of the province to continue raising the level of industrial production, carry out socialist transformation, continue to work hard in rural areas to overcome natural disasters, ensure the completion of this year's agricultural production plan, and consolidate and develop mutual aid and cooperation organizations. The meeting resolved to send a letter of appreciation to the people of the province and the Chinese People's Liberation Army troops stationed in the Jiangsu area who are persevering in the heroic struggle on the flood prevention and drainage front.

With excitement and pride, the delegates discussed the Draft Constitution of the People's Republic of China and adopted a resolution supporting the Draft Constitution.

Finally, the meeting elected Ding Xilin, Wang Xuewen, Wang Kunlun, Wang Shitai, Wang Shao'ao, Wang Xinzhang, Shi Liang (female), Wu Yunfu, Zhu Zhaoxue, Li Shihai, Li Mingyang, Li Chenggan, Li Weiguang, Leng Yu, Sha Qianli, Wu Yifang (female), Ji Fang, Jin Shanbao, Zhou Peiyuan, Shao Zonghan, Qiu Yihan (female), Hu Naiqiu (female), Hu Wenyao, Hu Qiaomu, Hou Debang, Ke Qingshi, Ke Zhongping, Mao Yisheng, Liu Yazi, Yu Huancheng, Xu Zhiyin (female), Xu Xiaobing, Xu Liqun, Xu Wentian, Lu Dingyi, Lu Yuanlei, Chen Yongkang, Chen Kehan, Chen Zhongjing, Zhang Wentian, Zhang Zhirang, Zhang Manjun (female), Zhang Jiongbo, Zhang Hanfu, Fei Xiaotong, Hua Junwu, Hui Yuyu, Si Xingjian, Huang Yanpei, Da Pousheng (Hui nationality), Yang Junsheng, Ye Shengtao, Guan Wenwei, Liao Luyan, Zheng Zhenduo, Zheng Bijang, Liu Daosheng, Liu Guojun, Pan Shu, Pan Zinian, Qian Sunqing, Qian Junrui, Qian Zhengying (female), Qian Weichang, Chu Anping, Xue Muqiao, Luo Qiong (female) and other sixty-seven people as representatives from Jiangsu Province (including the two provincial industrial cities of Nanjing and Wuxi with a population of more than 500,000) to attend the National People's Congress meeting.

[This newspaper, Kunming, 12th] The first session of the First People's Congress of Yunnan Province was held in Kunming from August 6th to 12th. 392 delegates attended the conference, including 161 representatives of 28 ethnic minorities. The meeting fully demonstrated the spirit of friendship and unity among all ethnic groups.

The delegates seriously discussed and reviewed the work report of Guo Yingqiu, Vice Chairman of the Yunnan Provincial People's Government, on the work of the Yunnan Provincial People's Government over the past four years. In his report, Guo Yingqiu specifically explained the achievements and progress made in various aspects of Yunnan Province in the four years since liberation. For example, state-owned industry developed to account for 46.77 percent of the total output value of large and small industries in the province by 1953; state-owned commerce accounted for 38.61 percent of the province's commerce; agricultural production exceeded the production increase plan last year, and mutual aid and cooperation organizations in the province have reached about 60 percent of the total number of farmers in areas where land reform has been implemented. The report also pointed out that all ethnic groups in the province have united in the big family of the motherland, and the politics, economy and culture of all ethnic groups are gradually developing. A total of 427 autonomous regions have been established successively throughout the province, and 11,342 ethnic minority cadres have been trained to actively help people of all ethnic groups manage their own ethnic affairs. With the development of ethnic regional autonomy, the material and cultural lives of people of all ethnic groups are gradually improving. Guo Yingqiu also explained the main tasks of Yunnan Province in the future in the report.

The delegates of all ethnic groups participating in the conference unanimously believed that the draft constitution fully conforms to the interests and common aspirations of all ethnic groups, and expressed their full support.

Finally, the meeting elected 45 representatives from Yunnan Province to attend the National People's Congress meeting. The elected representatives are: Zhu Jiabi, Li Neng, Li Guiying (Yi nationality), Zhou Baozhong (Bai nationality), He Wanbao (Naxi nationality), Xiong Kaiyou (Miao nationality), Nong Mingchuan (Nong nationality), Liu Rongxian (Yi nationality), Xie Fuzhi, Luo Yi, Wei Yanjing (Kawa nationality), Ou Gen (Bai nationality), Gao Shiqi, Zhang Chong (Yi nationality), Zhang Huiying, Zhang Zizhai (Bai nationality), Mei Yi, Huang Luofeng, Zeng Wenchang, Pu Zhao (Yi nationality), Dong Fusheng, Zheng Dun, Pan Suorui, Dao Youliang (Dai nationality), Li Guanghua (Lahu nationality), Li Guangrong (Yao nationality), Ma Jian (Hui nationality), Chu Tunan, Zhao Cunxin (Dai nationality), Li Hecai (Hani nationality), Yu Haiqing (Yi nationality), Hu Zhonghua (Kawa nationality), Dao Jingban (Dai nationality), Song Mou (Tibetan nationality), Xu Jiarui, Qin Renchang, Lei Chunguo (Jingpo nationality), Zhao Zhongqi (Hui nationality), Pei Aqian (Lisu nationality), Wang Shaoyan, Bai Xiaosong, Li Zhu'an, Gong Shou (Dai nationality), Lu Han (Yi nationality), Long Yun (Yi nationality).

Xinhua News Agency, Taiyuan, August 12th: The first session of the First People's Congress of Shanxi Province was held from August 3rd to 11th. A total of 315 delegates attended the meeting.

The delegates seriously and thoroughly discussed the Draft Constitution of the People's Republic of China and unanimously adopted a resolution supporting the Draft Constitution.

The meeting reviewed the work of the Shanxi Provincial People's Government in the past five years and the work tasks for the second half of this year, focusing on how Shanxi Province should specifically implement the general task of the state's transition period, ensure the completion of the state's industrial and agricultural production plan this year, and make up for the losses in agricultural production in disaster areas. The meeting resolved to immediately launch a widespread campaign to increase production and practice economy throughout the province. The meeting also unanimously passed two resolutions on carrying out soil and water conservation work in a planned way throughout the province and on implementing the policy of uniting Chinese medicine and giving greater play to the role of Chinese medicine.

The meeting elected Nan Hanchen, Zhang Jiafu, Wu Xinyu, Tao Lujia, Wang Shiying, Deng Chumin, Kang Yonghe, Xin Anting, Wang Guiying, Li Shunda, Wang Kaishan, Shen Jilan (female), Qu Yaoli, Li Hui (female), Wu Chun'an, Song Zichun, Hu Wenxiu (female), Ma Liuha, Guo Lanying (female), Guo Yu'en, Han Zhongren, Zhang Daozhong, Lu Jingyun, Cao Huanwen and other twenty-four people as representatives from Shanxi Province (including Taiyuan City) to attend the National People's Congress meeting.

Xinhua News Agency, Lüda, August 12th: The first session of the First People's Congress of Lüda City was held from August 7th to 9th. The 341 people's representatives attending the meeting listened to the "Work Report on Propaganda and Discussion of the Draft Constitution of the People's Republic of China among the People" made by Li Nan, Director of the Lüda City Constitution Draft Discussion Committee, and the "Report on the Transmission of the Central People's Government's Decision on Abolishing the First-Level Administrative Organs of the Great Regions and Merging Several Provinces and Municipalities" made by Qiao Chuanjue, Deputy Mayor of the Lüda City People's Government, and the "Report on Several Major Tasks in Lüda City at Present" made by Deputy Mayor Yu Guying. After repeated discussions, the meeting made corresponding resolutions.

The meeting elected Ouyang Qin, Zhang Tianyi, Liu Lifu, Tang Liyan, Wang Yuji, Li Feng (female), Deng Zhaoxiang, Qiao Chuanjue, Ding Guitang, Zhang Dayu, Fan Ximeng (female), Jiang Peilu and other twelve people as representatives from Lüda City to attend the National People's Congress meeting.

[This newspaper, Harbin, 12th] The first session of the First People's Congress of Harbin City was held from August 7th to 11th. A total of 340 representatives attended the meeting.

At the meeting, all delegates seriously discussed the Draft Constitution of the People's Republic of China, listened to and reviewed the report of Lü Qien, Mayor of the Harbin Municipal People's Government, on the work of the Harbin Municipal People's Government over the past few years and the current work. All delegates unanimously agreed with the report.

The meeting also made resolutions on supporting the Draft Constitution of the People's Republic of China and supporting the Central People's Government's decision to abolish the first-level administrative organs of the Great Regions and merge several provinces and municipalities.

Finally, all the delegates attending the meeting elected twelve representatives to attend the National People's Congress meeting by secret ballot. The elected representatives are: Yu Yifu, Guo Mingqiu (female), Che Xiangchen, Bai Xiqing, Ren Zhongyi, Li Chang, Chu Yinghuang, Su Changyou, Yang Maolin, Wang Sunci, Shi Zengrong, Liu Peizhi.

### 江苏、云南、山西三省和旅大、哈尔滨两市  召开人民代表大会会议  分别选出出席全国人民代表大会会议代表

1954-08-13
第1版()
专栏：

    江苏、云南、山西三省和旅大、哈尔滨两市
    召开人民代表大会会议
    分别选出出席全国人民代表大会会议代表
    据新华社南京十一日电：江苏省第一届人民代表大会第一次会议在南京市举行。出席会议的代表共四百四十九人。会议五日开幕，十一日结束。
    经过讨论，全体代表对政府一年半以来的工作及全省当前任务的报告一致表示同意，会议作出了批准这个报告的决议。会议并决定团结全省人民继续提高工业生产的水平，进行社会主义改造的工作，在农村中继续努力战胜自然灾害，保证今年农业生产计划的完成，巩固和发展互助合作组织。会议决议致信慰问在防汛排涝战线上坚持英勇斗争的全省人民和驻江苏地区的中国人民解放军部队。
    代表们怀着兴奋和自豪的心情讨论了中华人民共和国宪法草案，并作出了拥护宪法草案的决议。
    会议最后选出了丁西林、王学文、王昆仑、王世泰、王绍鏊、王新章、史良（女）、伍云甫、朱兆雪、李士海、李明扬、李承干、李维光、冷遹、沙千里、吴贻芳（女）、季方、金善宝、周培源、邵宗汉、邱一涵（女）、胡耐秋（女）、胡文耀、胡乔木、侯德榜、柯庆施、柯仲平、茅以升、柳亚子、俞寰澄、徐芝寅（女）、徐肖冰、许立群、许闻天、陆定一、陆渊雷、陈永康、陈克寒、陈忠经、张闻天、张志让、张曼筠（女）、张絅伯、章汉夫、费孝通、华君武、惠浴宇、斯行健、黄炎培、达浦生（回族）、杨俊生、叶圣陶、管文蔚、廖鲁言、郑振铎、郑辟疆、刘导生、刘国钧、潘菽、潘梓年、钱孙卿、钱俊瑞、钱正英（女）、钱伟长、储安平、薛暮桥、罗琼（女）等六十七人为江苏省（包括南京、无锡两个人口在五十万以上的省辖工业城市）出席全国人民代表大会会议的代表。
    【本报昆明十二日电】云南省第一届人民代表大会第一次会议，于八月六日到十二日在昆明举行。参加大会的代表三百九十二人，其中包括有二十八种少数民族代表一百六十一人。会议充分表现了各族人民友爱团结的精神。
    到会代表认真讨论并审查了云南省人民政府副主席郭影秋关于云南省人民政府四年多来的工作报告。郭影秋在报告中，具体说明了云南省解放四年多来在各方面的成绩和进步。如国营工业到一九五三年发展到占全省大小工业总产值的百分之四十六点七七；国营商业占全省商业的百分之三十八点六一；农业生产去年超额完成了增产计划，全省的互助合作组织，在已实行土地改革的地区已达总农户的百分之六十左右。报告中并指出：全省各民族已团结在祖国大家庭中，各民族的政治、经济和文化正在逐步发展。在全省范围内已先后建立了四百二十七个自治区，并培养了少数民族干部一万一千三百四十二人，积极帮助各族人民自己管理本民族的事务。随着民族区域自治的开展，各族人民的物质和文化生活正在逐步地提高。对今后云南省的主要工作，郭影秋在报告中也作了说明。
