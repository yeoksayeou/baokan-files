Title: Prominent Nepalese Figures Prepare to Establish Nepal-China Association
Author:
Date: 1954-08-24
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency, New Delhi, August 22nd: The "Indian Express" reported on the 21st that a number of prominent figures in Kathmandu, the capital of Nepal, held a meeting on the 18th and decided to prepare for the establishment of the Nepal-China Association. The meeting elected a five-member committee responsible for drafting the association's charter.

### 尼泊尔著名人士筹备成立尼中协会

1954-08-24
第4版()
专栏：

    尼泊尔著名人士筹备成立尼中协会
    【新华社新德里二十二日电】　“印度快报”二十一日报道，尼泊尔首都加德满都的著名人士多人十八日举行会议，决定筹备成立尼中协会。会议选出了一个由五人组成的委员会，负责起草协会的章程。
