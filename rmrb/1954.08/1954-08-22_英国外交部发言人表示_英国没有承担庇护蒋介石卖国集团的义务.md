Title: British Foreign Office Spokesman States Britain Has No Obligation to Shelter the Chiang Kai-shek Traitorous Clique
Author:
Date: 1954-08-22
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, London, August 21st: When asked at a press conference on the 18th about U.S. President Eisenhower's statement on the 17th concerning the use of force to protect the Chiang Kai-shek traitorous clique and prevent the Chinese people from liberating Taiwan, a British Foreign Office spokesman said that Britain "has not undertaken any obligation." When asked if Taiwan was included in the Southeast Asia military bloc, the spokesman said, "Southeast Asia and the Western Pacific do not include Taiwan."

According to a United Press report from the 18th, diplomatic circles in London said that "Britain strongly disapproves of the American approach to Taiwan." They believe that "Chiang Kai-shek is allowed to harass mainland China while being protected by the United States." They believe that this American policy "only increases the risk of war."

### 英国外交部发言人表示  英国没有承担庇护蒋介石卖国集团的义务

1954-08-22
第1版()
专栏：

    英国外交部发言人表示
    英国没有承担庇护蒋介石卖国集团的义务
    【新华社二十一日讯】伦敦消息：英国外交部发言人十八日在记者招待会上谈到美国总统艾森豪威尔十七日发表的关于要使用武力庇护蒋介石卖国集团，阻止中国人民解放台湾的谈话时表示，英国
  “没有承担任何义务”。发言人在谈到台湾是否包括在东南亚军事集团之内时说，“东南亚和西太平洋并不包括台湾在内。”
    据合众社十八日报道，伦敦方面的外交界人士表示，“英国很不赞同美国对待台湾的办法。”他们认为，“蒋介石被允许骚扰中国大陆，同时他又受到美国的保护。”他们认为美国的这种政策“只会增加战争的危险。”
