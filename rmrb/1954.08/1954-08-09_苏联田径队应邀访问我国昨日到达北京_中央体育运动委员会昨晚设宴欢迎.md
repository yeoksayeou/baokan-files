Title: Soviet Track and Field Team Arrives in Beijing Yesterday at Our Invitation; State Physical Culture and Sports Commission Hosted a Banquet Last Night to Welcome Them
Author:
Date: 1954-08-09
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, August 8th: The Soviet track and field team, consisting of 14 members led by team leader A. Gromadsky, arrived in Beijing by plane on the afternoon of August 8th at the invitation of the State Physical Culture and Sports Commission of our Central People's Government to visit our country and participate in friendly competitions.

Those who welcomed them at the airport included: Wei Que, member of the State Physical Culture and Sports Commission of the Central People's Government and Vice Minister of Education; Zhang Zhihuai, Deputy Director of the Cadre Training Department of the State Physical Culture and Sports Commission of the Central People's Government; Liao Jingtian, Deputy Secretary-General of the Sino-Soviet Friendship Association; Chai Zemin, Director of the Beijing Municipal People's Government Sports Commission, and more than 50 representatives of sports workers and athletes from Beijing.

Also welcoming them at the airport were: Pablichev, First Secretary of the Soviet Embassy in China; Kovalev, Second Secretary, and other embassy staff.

Romanin, representative of the Soviet Society for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries, also welcomed them at the airport.

The Soviet track and field team visiting our country this time includes six Honored Masters of Sports and six Masters of Sports. They will participate in friendly competitions at the 1954 Thirteen-City Track and Field and Gymnastics Tournament for students of secondary and higher education institutions in Lushun-Dalian, and will also visit and compete in friendly matches in Shenyang and other places.

The State Physical Culture and Sports Commission of the Central People's Government hosted a special banquet on the evening of August 8th to welcome the Soviet track and field team.

### 苏联田径队应邀访问我国昨日到达北京  中央体育运动委员会昨晚设宴欢迎

1954-08-09
第1版()
专栏：

    苏联田径队应邀访问我国昨日到达北京
    中央体育运动委员会昨晚设宴欢迎
   【新华社八日讯】应我中央人民政府体育运动委员会邀请来我国访问和作友谊比赛的苏联田径队共十四人，由领队爱·格罗马德斯基率领，在八日下午乘飞机到达北京。
    到机场欢迎的，有：中央人民政府体育运动委员会委员、教育部副部长韦悫，中央人民政府体育运动委员会干部训练司副司长张之槐，中苏友好协会副总干事廖经天，北京市人民政府体育运动委员会主任柴泽民和北京市体育工作者、运动员代表等五十多人。
    到机场欢迎的还有：苏联驻我国大使馆一等秘书柏布利雪夫，二等秘书柯瓦列夫和其他使馆人员。
    苏联对外文化协会代表罗满宁也到机场欢迎。
    这次来我国访问的苏联田径队有六个功勋运动员，六个运动健将。他们将参加在旅大市举行的一九五四年十三城市中等以上学校学生田径、体操运动大会作友谊比赛，并到沈阳等地访问和作友谊比赛。
    中央人民政府体育运动委员会在八日晚特设宴欢迎苏联田径队。
