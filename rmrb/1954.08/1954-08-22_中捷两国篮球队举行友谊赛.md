Title: China and Czechoslovakia Hold Basketball Friendly Matches
Author:
Date: 1954-08-22
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Prague, August 21st: The Chinese Youth Basketball Team and the Czechoslovak Basketball Team held two friendly matches, one for men and one for women, on the 19th, with approximately 5,000 spectators. The Czechoslovak Women's Basketball Team won second place in the European Women's Basketball Championship, and the Men's Basketball Team won fourth place in the European Men's Basketball Championship. The competition was lively and intense. The Czechoslovak basketball players displayed skillful techniques. The Chinese Youth Basketball Team also performed wonderfully and was warmly welcomed by the audience.

The results of the matches were: women's basketball, 53 points to 82 points, and men's basketball, 66 points to 85 points, with the Czechoslovak Basketball Team taking the lead in both.

### 中捷两国篮球队举行友谊赛

1954-08-22
第4版()
专栏：

    中捷两国篮球队举行友谊赛
    【新华社布拉格二十一日电】中国青年篮球队和捷克斯洛伐克篮球代表队十九日举行了男子和女子两场友谊比赛，观众约有五千人。捷女子篮球代表队曾在欧洲女子篮球锦标赛中荣获第二名，男子篮球代表队曾获欧洲男子篮球锦标赛的第四名。比赛情况热烈紧张。捷篮球队员表现了熟练的球技。中国青年篮球队表演得也很精采，受到观众的热烈欢迎。
    比赛结果，女子篮球以五十三分比八十二分，男子篮球六十六分比八十五分，均是捷克斯洛伐克篮球代表队占先。
