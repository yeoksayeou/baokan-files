Title: Medical Personnel from Guizhou and Other Places Arrive in Hubei Disaster Area to Assist

Author:
Date: 1954-08-26
Page: 3

Medical personnel from Guizhou and other places arrive in Hubei disaster area to assist

The Ministry of Health of the Central People's Government has transferred a large number of medical personnel from Guizhou, Xikang, Yunnan, Guangxi, Sichuan, Guangdong, Chongqing, Guangzhou and other provinces and cities to assist the Hubei Provincial Health Department in carrying out medical and health work in the disaster area. From August 14th to 20th, a total of 102 medical personnel arrived in Wuchang from various provinces and cities, including doctors, medical assistants, nurses, laboratory technicians, and midwives. Now, they have formed medical teams and gone to Yangxin, Xishui, Qichun, Honghu, Shishou and other counties to assist local health departments in carrying out their work.

(Our newspaper's Hubei reporter station)

### 贵州等地医务人员到湖北灾区协助工作

1954-08-26
第3版()
专栏：

    贵州等地医务人员到湖北灾区协助工作
    中央人民政府卫生部由贵州、西康、云南、广西、四川、广东、重庆、广州等省、市抽调大批医务人员，协助湖北省卫生部门开展灾区医疗卫生工作。从八月十四日到二十日，先后由各省、市到达武昌的医务人员，共有一百零二人，其中包括医师、医士、护士、化验员和助产士。现在，他们已分别组成医疗队，前往阳新、浠水、蕲春、洪湖、石首等县，协助当地卫生部门开展工作。
                        （本报湖北记者站）
