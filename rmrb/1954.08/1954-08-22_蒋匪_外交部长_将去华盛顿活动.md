Title: Chiang Kai-shek Bandit "Foreign Minister" to Conduct Activities in Washington
Author:
Date: 1954-08-22
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency, August 19th: Foreign news agencies report that Chiang Kai-shek bandit gang's "Foreign Minister," Yeh Kung-chao, will rush to Washington at the end of this month to negotiate with the US authorities, plotting to sign a so-called "common security treaty" between the US and Chiang, and increase military "aid" to the Chiang bandit gang. According to Chiang bandit gang members, the Chiang Kai-shek bandit gang hopes to accelerate the signing of this traitorous treaty with the United States, and hopes that the treaty will stipulate that the United States use force to protect the Chiang bandits when the Chinese People's Liberation Army liberates Taiwan, and provide the Chiang bandits with more military "aid."

### 蒋匪“外交部长”将去华盛顿活动

1954-08-22
第4版()
专栏：

    蒋匪“外交部长”将去华盛顿活动
    【新华社十九日讯】据外国通讯社报道，蒋介石匪帮“外交部长”叶公超将在本月底赶到华盛顿去和美国当局进行谈判，策划签订美蒋之间的所谓“共同安全条约”，和增加对蒋匪帮的军事“援助”。据蒋匪帮分子透露，蒋介石匪帮希望加速和美国签订这个卖国条约，并且希望在这个条约中规定美国在中国人民解放台湾时用武力庇护蒋匪，并给予蒋匪更多的军事“援助”。
