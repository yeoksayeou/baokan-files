Title: World Peace Council Secretariat Writes to the Japan Peace Supporters Committee to Express Respect to the Japanese People Fighting for the Prohibition of Atomic Weapons
Author:
Date: 1954-08-07
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Vienna, August 5th: TASS reported that the World Peace Council Secretariat wrote a letter to the Japan Peace Supporters Committee. The content of the letter is as follows:

    As the world pays attention to the ninth anniversary of the Hiroshima tragedy, the Secretariat of the World Peace Council expresses its deepest sympathy to the Japanese people—the first people to suffer from atomic bombing.
    The word "Hiroshima" will forever be a mark of the atomic war that horrifically threatens the world.
    The recent explosion of the hydrogen bomb on Bikini Island has brought new sacrifices to the Japanese people. These sacrifices are a serious warning to men and women all over the world.
    People all over the world are fighting for the unconditional prohibition of atomic and hydrogen weapons, as well as all other weapons of mass destruction. The Japanese people have played a glorious role in this sacred struggle.
    The current struggle of the Japanese people to ban atomic and hydrogen weapons is supported by people from all walks of life. This struggle has achieved great success. In a very short period of time, five million signatures have been collected.
    The Secretariat of the World Peace Council expresses its greatest respect to the Japan Peace Supporters Committee and all the Japanese people who are making persistent efforts in the struggle for peace and achieving new successes.

### 世界和平理事会书记处写信给日本拥护和平委员会  向争取禁止原子武器的日本人民表示敬意

1954-08-07
第4版()
专栏：

    世界和平理事会书记处写信给日本拥护和平委员会
    向争取禁止原子武器的日本人民表示敬意
    【新华社六日讯】塔斯社维也纳五日讯：世界和平理事会书记处写信给日本拥护和平委员会，信的内容如下：
    正当全世界各国都注目的广岛惨案的九周年之际，世界和平理事会书记处对日本人民——第一个遭到原子弹轰炸的人们——表示最深切的同情。
    “广岛”这个字眼将永远成为骇人的威胁世界的原子战争的一个标记。
    最近在比基尼岛爆炸的氢弹使日本人民遭到了新的牺牲。这些牺牲对全世界男女是一个严重的警告。
    全世界人民正在为无条件禁止原子武器和氢武器以及其他所有大规模毁灭性武器而斗争。日本人民在这个神圣的斗争中起了光荣的作用。
    日本人民现时进行的争取禁止原子武器和氢武器的斗争是得到各阶层人民的支持的。这个斗争已经取得巨大的成就。在一个很短的时期内，已经征集了五百万个签名。
    世界和平理事会书记处对在争取和平的斗争中再接再厉取得新的成就的日本拥护和平委员会和全体日本人民表示最大的敬意。
