Title: Attending the Romanian Consumer Cooperative Congress, Chinese Cooperative Delegation Leaves Beijing
Author:
Date: 1954-08-02
Page: 4

    Participating in the Romanian Consumer Cooperative Congress
    Chinese Cooperative Delegation Leaves Beijing
    [Xinhua News Agency, 1st] The Chinese cooperative delegation attending the Second Romanian Consumer Cooperative Congress, led by Deng Chenxi, Deputy Director of the All-China Federation of Supply and Marketing Cooperatives, left Beijing by plane on the morning of the 1st to go to Bucharest.

### 参加罗消费合作社代表大会  我国合作社代表团离京

1954-08-02
第4版()
专栏：

    参加罗消费合作社代表大会
    我国合作社代表团离京
    【新华社一日讯】参加罗马尼亚第二届消费合作社代表大会的中国合作社代表团，在中华全国供销合作总社副主任邓辰西率领下于一日晨乘飞机离京赴布加勒斯特。
