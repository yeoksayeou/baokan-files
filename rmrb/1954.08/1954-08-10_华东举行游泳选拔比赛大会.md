Title: East China Holds Swimming Trials

Author:
Date: 1954-08-10
Page: 3
  
The 1954 East China Swimming Trials were held at the Jiangwan Swimming Pool in Shanghai from August 7th to 8th. A total of 199 athletes from Shanghai, Shandong, Jiangsu, Zhejiang, Fujian, and six units directly under the East China region participated in the competition.

The results of the competition were outstanding. Six national records were broken in twenty-one events. These included: Lu Chuantang from the Shanghai team in the men's 1500m freestyle (22 minutes 58.9 seconds); Wu Yufang from the Shanghai team in the men's 200m backstroke (2 minutes 49 seconds); the Shanghai team in the men's 800m freestyle relay (10 minutes 31.9 seconds); the Shanghai team in the women's 400m freestyle relay (6 minutes 7 seconds); Zhao Qin from the Shanghai team in the women's 400m freestyle (6 minutes 38.8 seconds); and Pan Jiajin from the Shanghai team in the women's 100m butterfly (1 minute 37.4 seconds). Chen Jingzhong from the Shanghai team equaled the national record in the men's 100m butterfly (1 minute 18.1 seconds). In addition, eleven events, including the women's 100m breaststroke and the men's 200m breaststroke, broke the highest records of the 1952 East China Swimming Competition.

Before the end of the conference, swimming athletes from the Shanghai team and the Fujian team held a water polo exhibition match. (Xinhua News Agency)

### 华东举行游泳选拔比赛大会

1954-08-10
第3版()
专栏：

    华东举行游泳选拔比赛大会
    一九五四年华东区游泳选拔比赛大会，七日到八日在上海市江湾游泳池举行。参加比赛的有上海市和山东、江苏、浙江、福建等省以及华东一级机关六个单位的一百九十九名运动员。
    大会比赛结果，成绩非常优异。在二十一项比赛中，有六项打破了全国最高纪录。其中有男子一千五百公尺自由式上海队陆传棠（成绩二十二分五十八秒九）；男子二百公尺仰泳上海队吴于方（成绩二分四十九秒）；男子八百公尺自由式接力上海队（成绩十分三十一秒九）；女子四百公尺自由式接力上海队（成绩六分七秒）；女子四百公尺自由式上海队赵溱（成绩六分三十八秒八）和女子一百公尺蝶泳上海队潘家璡（成绩一分三十七秒四）。男子一百公尺蝶泳上海队陈景忠（成绩一分十八秒一）平全国最高纪录。此外，还有女子一百公尺蛙泳和男子二百公尺蛙泳等十一项都打破了一九五二年华东区游泳比赛大会的最高纪录。
    大会结束前，上海队和福建队的游泳运动员曾举行了水球表演比赛。　　　（新华社）
