Title: World Federation of Democratic Youth Secretary-General Denis and Others Arrive in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1954-08-03
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, August 2nd: The Secretary-General of the World Federation of Democratic Youth, Jacques Denis, who is here to attend the World Federation of Democratic Youth Council meeting in Beijing, arrived in Beijing by plane on the afternoon of the 2nd. Those who came on the same plane included World Federation of Democratic Youth Secretaries Biro (Hungary), Nixon (United Kingdom), Petrov (Bulgaria), as well as the Central Secretary of the Hungarian Working Youth League and the head of the Hungarian delegation to this meeting, Holos, and others.

Those who welcomed them at the airport included: Liu Daosheng and Qu Tangliang, Vice-Chairmen of the All-China Federation of Democratic Youth, the Secretary-General and Director of the Liaison Department, and Sun Fuling, Vice-Chairman of the Beijing Federation of Democratic Youth, and others.

Also present at the airport to welcome them were: the President of the World Federation of Democratic Youth, Bernini, and the Secretary of the World Federation of Democratic Youth, Nikitin (Soviet Union), and others.

More than 50 young students and Young Pioneers from Beijing also came to the airport to welcome them.

After Secretary-General Denis and others got off the plane, they received flowers from the Young Pioneers.

### 世界民主青年联盟总书记德尼等到达北京

1954-08-03
第4版()
专栏：

    世界民主青年联盟总书记德尼等到达北京
    【新华社二日讯】前来参加世界民主青年联盟理事会北京会议的世界民主青年联盟总书记雅克·德尼，已在二日下午乘飞机到达北京。随机前来的还有世界民主青年联盟书记比罗（匈牙利）、尼克松（英国）、彼得罗夫（保加利亚）以及匈牙利劳动青年联盟中央书记、匈牙利参加这次会议的代表团团长霍洛斯等。
    到机场欢迎的有：中华全国民主青年联合会副主席刘导生、区棠亮，秘书长和联络部部长以及北京市民主青年联合会副主席孙孚凌等。
    前往机场欢迎的还有：世界民主青年联盟主席柏尼尼和世界民主青年联盟书记尼基京（苏联）等人。
    北京市青年学生和少年先锋队队员五十余人也到机场欢迎。
    德尼总书记等下飞机后，接受了少年先锋队队员的献花。
