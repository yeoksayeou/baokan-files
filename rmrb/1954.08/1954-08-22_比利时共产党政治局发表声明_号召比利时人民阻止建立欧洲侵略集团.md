Title: Belgian Communist Party Politburo Issues Statement Calling on Belgian People to Prevent the Establishment of a European Aggression Bloc
Author:
Date: 1954-08-22
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Brussels:
    TASS, Brussels, August 21st:
  "The Red Flag" published a statement from the Politburo of the Belgian Communist Party on the 19th, exposing the aggressive nature of the "European Defence Community" and the conspiracies of the "European Defence Community" supporters.
    The statement said that the initiators of the Brussels meeting do not conceal their intentions. They succumbed to the continuous pressure from American leaders, seeking to rapidly restore the strength of the German army and hand over the leadership of Europe to it. Therefore, they placed their bets on the "European Defence Community" treaty.
    These advocates of German rearmament hope that by holding the Brussels meeting, they might more cleverly disguise the true nature of the "European Defence Community." They also hope to force the French and Italian governments to push the parliaments to approve this treaty, regardless of the opposition from public opinion in both countries.
    It is unnecessary to reiterate the enormous danger that the reconstruction of a strong German army on our border would pose to the security of our country and the world.
    Therefore, in these critical days, Belgians who are uneasy about the future of their families and their homeland are unanimously determined to use all of Belgium's influence to thwart efforts to arm West Germany and prevent the establishment of the "European Defence Community."
    The Belgian people unanimously hope that the Belgian government will abandon the "European Defence Community" policy pursued by the Catholic Social Party ministers.
    Our country yearns for peace and its consolidation, yearning for peaceful cooperation with the people of all countries.

### 比利时共产党政治局发表声明  号召比利时人民阻止建立欧洲侵略集团

1954-08-22
第4版()
专栏：

    比利时共产党政治局发表声明
    号召比利时人民阻止建立欧洲侵略集团
    【新华社二十一日讯】塔斯社布鲁塞尔讯：
  “红旗报”在十九日刊载了比利时共产党政治局的一篇声明，揭露“欧洲防务集团”的侵略性质和“欧洲防务集团”支持者的阴谋。
    声明说，布鲁塞尔会议的发起者并不掩饰他们的意图。他们向美国领导人物的不断压力屈服，要想迅速恢复德国陆军的力量，并把支配欧洲的领导权交付给它。因此，他们就把赌注压在“欧洲防务集团”条约上。
    这些拥护重新德国武装的人们希望由于举行布鲁塞尔会议，便可能更巧妙地掩饰“欧洲防务集团”的真正性质，他们还希望使得法国和意大利政府强迫议会不顾两国舆论的反对批准这个条约。
    在我国边境重建一支强大的德国国防军会给我国及世界安全造成多么巨大的危险，这一点已不必重提了。
    因此，在这紧要的日子里，为家庭和祖国的将来感到不安的比利时人，一致下定决心要使比利时利用它一切的影响来挫败武装西德的努力，阻止建立“欧洲防务集团”。
    比利时人一致希望比利时政府放弃天主教社会党的大臣们所奉行的关于“欧洲防务集团”的政策。
    我们国家热望和平及其巩固，热望与各国人民和平合作。
