Title: People from All Circles in the Capital Hold a Celebration Meeting to Celebrate the Tenth Anniversary of the Liberation of Romania
Author:
Date: 1954-08-23
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, August 22nd: People from all walks of life in the capital held a celebration meeting on the evening of the 22nd to warmly celebrate the tenth anniversary of the liberation of Romania. The celebration meeting was organized by the Ministry of Culture of the Central People's Government.

Those attending the meeting included: Liu Shaoqi, Vice Chairman of the Central People's Government; Zhou Enlai, Premier of the State Council; Guo Moruo, Huang Yanpei, and Deng Xiaoping, Vice Premiers; Zhang Zhirang and Zhang Su, Vice Presidents of the Supreme People's Court; Xi Zhongxun, Secretary-General of the State Council and Deputy Director of the Cultural and Educational Committee; Lu Dingyi, Deputy Director of the Cultural and Educational Committee; Shen Yanbing, Minister of Culture; Ding Xilin and Zheng Zhenduo, Vice Ministers; Zhang Hanfu and Wu Xiuquan, Vice Ministers of Foreign Affairs; Peng Zhen, Mayor of Beijing Municipality; Wu Han, Deputy Mayor, as well as the heads of various commissions, ministries, committees, academies, administrations, and banks under the State Council, responsible persons from various departments of the People's Revolutionary Military Commission, heads of various branches of the Chinese People's Liberation Army, representatives of various democratic parties, responsible persons of various people's organizations, and people from the scientific, educational, cultural, artistic, and journalistic circles of the capital, totaling over 500 people.

Foreign guests attending the meeting included Romanian Ambassador to China, Guo Zuowen, and embassy staff.

Diplomatic envoys from various countries stationed in China who attended the meeting included: Indonesian Ambassador Mononutu, Finnish Envoy Sun Shidun, Danish Envoy Gregerse, Soviet Union Charge d'Affaires ad interim Vasikov, Vietnamese Democratic Republic Charge d'Affaires ad interim Zhou Liang, Czechoslovak Charge d'Affaires ad interim Smits, Hungarian Charge d'Affaires ad interim Czator, Bulgarian Charge d'Affaires ad interim Grigorov, Swiss Charge d'Affaires ad interim Trullet, Mongolian Charge d'Affaires ad interim Lobsang Chultem, Polish Charge d'Affaires ad interim Gula, Democratic People's Republic of Korea Charge d'Affaires ad interim Xu Zhe, Dutch Negotiation Representative Smann, and embassy staff from various countries.

Other foreign guests attending the meeting included Romanian agricultural experts Mayer Ion and Ilca K. Florin, who are currently visiting China, Romanian microbiology expert Stefan Nicolau, and Romanian meritorious artist Ian Voicu, renowned director Sekfulid Wolfe Jeter, and the Romanian folk music team headed by Kerjan Constantin, who had just arrived in Beijing on the afternoon of the 22nd.

Hungarian exhibition experts and Mongolian journalists currently visiting China were also invited to attend the meeting.

Minister Shen Yanbing presided over the meeting.

As the meeting began, the orchestras played the national anthems of Romania and China respectively.

Amidst warm applause, Xi Zhongxun, Secretary-General of the State Council and Deputy Director of the Cultural and Educational Committee of the Central People's Government, took the stage to speak. (Full text to be published separately)

Amidst prolonged and enthusiastic applause from the audience, Romanian Ambassador Guo Zuowen then spoke. (Full text to be published separately)

At the celebration, Chinese literary and art workers performed wonderful programs, which were warmly welcomed.

### 首都各界人民举行庆祝大会  庆贺罗马尼亚解放十周年

1954-08-23
第4版()
专栏：

    首都各界人民举行庆祝大会
    庆贺罗马尼亚解放十周年
    据新华社二十二日讯：首都各界人民在二十二日晚举行庆祝大会，热烈庆贺罗马尼亚解放十周年。庆祝大会由中央人民政府文化部举办。
    出席大会的，有：中央人民政府副主席刘少奇，政务院总理周恩来，副总理郭沫若、黄炎培、邓小平，最高人民法院副院长张志让、张苏，政务院秘书长兼文化教育委员会副主任习仲勋，文化教育委员会副主任陆定一，文化部部长沈雁冰，副部长丁西林、郑振铎，外交部副部长章汉夫、伍修权，北京市人民政府市长彭真、副市长吴晗，以及政务院所属各委、部、会、院、署、行首长，人民革命军事委员会各部门负责人，中国人民解放军各兵种首长，各民主党派代表，各人民团体负责人，首都科学、教育、文化、艺术、新闻界人士等五百多人。
    出席大会的外宾有罗马尼亚驻华大使郭佐文和使馆人员。
    出席大会的各国驻华使节，有：印度尼西亚大使莫诺努图、芬兰公使孙士敦、丹麦公使格瑞杰生、苏联临时代办华司考、越南民主共和国临时代办周亮、捷克斯洛伐克临时代办石密茨、匈牙利临时代办察托尔道伊、保加利亚临时代办格利郭洛夫、瑞士临时代办崔礼、蒙古临时代办罗布桑楚勒图姆、波兰临时代办古拉、朝鲜民主主义人民共和国临时代办徐哲、荷兰谈判代表司曼以及各国使馆人员。
    出席大会的外宾，还有：正在我国访问的罗马尼亚农业专家马叶尔·伊昂、伊尔卡·克·弗洛林，罗马尼亚微生物学专家斯捷凡·尼古劳和在二十二日下午刚到达北京的罗马尼亚功勋艺术家扬·沃伊库，名导演色克夫利德·沃尔费捷尔以及以克尔然·康斯坦丁为首的罗马尼亚民间音乐队等。
    正在我国访问的匈牙利展览会专家和蒙古记者也应邀出席了大会。
    大会由沈雁冰部长主持。
    大会开始，乐队先后高奏罗、中两国国歌。
    中央人民政府政务院秘书长兼文化教育委员会副主任习仲勋在热烈掌声中上台讲话。（全文另发）
    在全场持久而热烈的掌声中，罗马尼亚大使郭佐文接着讲话。（全文另发）
    在庆祝会上，中国的文艺工作者演出了精采的节目，受到热烈欢迎。
