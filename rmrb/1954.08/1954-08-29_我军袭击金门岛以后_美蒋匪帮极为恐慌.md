Title: After Our Army's Attack on Kinmen Island, the American-Chiang Kai-shek Bandit Gangs Were Extremely Panicked
Author:
Date: 1954-08-29
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, August 28th – After the People's Liberation Army's maritime reconnaissance units on the Fujian front attacked Kinmen Island on the 23rd, the Chiang Kai-shek traitorous clique and the American aggressors were extremely panicked. According to the Associated Press, the Chiang Kai-shek traitorous clique hurriedly transported reinforcements to Kinmen on the 27th and increased patrols around the island. Britain's "Daily Express" revealed that American aircraft carriers and cruisers "escorted" the Chiang Kai-shek traitorous clique's reinforcements from Taiwan to Kinmen.

### 我军袭击金门岛以后  美蒋匪帮极为恐慌

1954-08-29
第1版()
专栏：

    我军袭击金门岛以后
    美蒋匪帮极为恐慌
    【新华社二十八日讯】在福建前线人民解放军海上侦察部队在二十三日袭击金门岛以后，蒋介石卖国集团与美国侵略者极为恐慌。据合众社报道，蒋介石卖国集团已在二十七日匆忙将增援部队运往金门，并增加了在该岛周围的巡逻力量。英国的“每日快报”透露说，在蒋介石卖国集团由台湾向金门增援的过程中，美国的航空母舰和巡洋舰曾予以“护送”。
