Title: Panchen Erdeni and Others Pass Through Xining on Their Way to Beijing
Author:
Date: 1954-08-31
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Xining, August 30th: Panchen Erdeni and other representatives from the Tibet region, who are going to Beijing to attend the National People's Congress meeting, arrived in Xining on August 22nd. Accompanying them was Ji Jinmei, a delegate to the National People's Congress and Director of the Panchen Kanpo Lija Committee.

General Fan Ming, a delegate to the National People's Congress and Deputy Political Commissar of the Tibet Military Region of the Chinese People's Liberation Army, accompanied Panchen Erdeni to Beijing.

Greeting them at the western suburbs of Xining were Xi Raojiacuo, Vice Chairman of the Qinghai Provincial People's Government, Li Shimao, Deputy Commander of the Qinghai Military Region, Zhou Renshan, Chairman of the Qinghai Provincial Nationalities Affairs Committee, and more than 300 representatives from various people's organizations, democratic parties, ethnic groups, and monasteries, as well as representatives from the Panchen Erdeni Office in Xining and Panchen Erdeni's relatives.

Gao Feng, Secretary of the Qinghai Provincial Committee of the Chinese Communist Party, also went to the western suburbs to welcome them.

After Panchen Erdeni and General Fan Ming and others got out of the car, they shook hands with the welcomers and accepted flowers presented by the Young Pioneers of Xining City.

On August 23rd, the Qinghai Provincial People's Government held a welcome banquet and evening party for Panchen Erdeni and General Fan Ming and others. At the meeting, Panchen Erdeni expressed his gratitude for the cordial care and reception from the Qinghai Provincial People's Government and people of all ethnic groups, and congratulated the achievements of the people of all ethnic groups in Qinghai. He said that Qinghai is the hometown of the Dalai Lama and himself, and the birthplace of Tsongkhapa, the founder of the Gelug school of Tibetan Buddhism. After the peaceful liberation of Tibet, he gloriously returned to Tibet from his beloved hometown. This time, he gloriously passed through Qinghai again to attend the National People's Congress meeting in the capital. Along the way and during his stay in Xining, he personally witnessed the happy lives of the people of all ethnic groups, who are united like brothers under the leadership of the Communist Party and Chairman Mao. The Tibetan, Mongolian, Hui, and other ethnic groups have established their own regional autonomous governments and are actively building their own homes. The freedom of religious belief of the people of all ethnic groups has been well protected. These facts prove the greatness of the Communist Party and Chairman Mao's national policy. He believed that in the big family of the motherland, with the wise leadership of the Chinese Communist Party and Chairman Mao, the Tibetan people will surely, like all brotherly ethnic groups, move towards prosperity and happiness with the development of the motherland's construction. He finally said: The upcoming National People's Congress meeting is a meeting of great unity of the people of all ethnic groups in the country. In this historically significant meeting, he is willing to strengthen national unity, consolidate national defense, and contribute his glorious responsibility to the cause of building a great and warm family of the motherland, fulfill the entrustment of the Tibetan people to the Dalai Lama and himself, and respond to the concern and hope of the people of all ethnic groups in Qinghai. On August 24th, Panchen Erdeni and his entourage paid homage to Kumbum Monastery, the birthplace of Tsongkhapa. On August 27th, Panchen Erdeni and General Fan Ming and his party left Xining for Lanzhou. Leading officials of provincial-level agencies of Qinghai Province, representatives from all ethnic groups and all walks of life, representatives from the Panchen Office in Xining, and Panchen Erdeni's relatives all went to the suburbs to see them off.

### 班禅额尔德尼等经西宁来京

1954-08-31
第1版()
专栏：

    班禅额尔德尼等经西宁来京
    【新华社西宁三十日电】赴北京出席全国人民代表大会会议的西藏地区代表班禅额尔德尼等，在八月二十二日抵达西宁。同行的有全国人民代表大会代表、班禅堪布会议厅委员会主任委员计晋美。
    偕同班禅额尔德尼赴京的有全国人民代表大会代表、中国人民解放军西藏军区副政治委员范明将军。
    前往西宁市西郊欢迎的有青海省人民政府副主席喜饶嘉措，青海军区副司令员李书茂，青海省民族事务委员会主任委员周仁山，和各人民团体、各民主党派、各民族、各寺院的代表共三百多人，还有西藏班禅驻西宁办事处的代表和班禅额尔德尼的亲属。
    中国共产党青海省委员会书记高峰也前往西郊欢迎。
    班禅额尔德尼和范明将军等下车后，和欢迎的人握手，接受了西宁市少年先锋队队员的献花。
    八月二十三日，青海省人民政府为班禅额尔德尼和范明将军等举行了欢迎宴会和晚会。会上班禅额尔德尼对青海省人民政府和各族人民的亲切关怀和招待表示感激，并祝贺青海各族人民的建设成就。他说，青海是达赖喇嘛和我的故乡，是黄教创始人宗喀巴的诞生地。西藏和平解放后，我光荣地从我亲爱的故乡回到了西藏。这次我又光荣地经过青海到首都出席全国人民代表大会会议。我在沿途和在西宁期间，亲眼看到了各族人民的幸福生活，他们在共产党和毛主席的领导下，团结得像亲兄弟一样。藏、蒙、回等族人民都建立了自己的区域自治政权，并积极地建设着自己的家乡。各族人民的宗教信仰自由得到了很好的保护。这些事实证明了共产党和毛主席民族政策的伟大。我相信在祖国大家庭内有中国共产党和毛主席的英明领导，我们西藏民族一定会和各兄弟民族一样，随着祖国建设事业的发展，日益走向繁荣和幸福。他最后说：即将召开的全国人民代表大会会议，是全国各族人民大团结的会议，在这个有历史意义的会议中，我愿意在加强民族团结，巩固祖国国防和建设伟大祖国温暖大家庭的事业中，尽到我光荣的责任，完成西藏人民给达赖喇嘛和我的委托，来回答青海各族人民的关心和希望。八月二十四日，班禅额尔德尼及随行官员朝拜了宗喀巴的诞生地——塔尔寺。八月二十七日，班禅额尔德尼和范明将军等一行，离开西宁赴兰州。青海省省级各机关首长、各族各界代表和班禅驻西宁办事处代表，班禅额尔德尼的亲属等都到市郊欢送。
