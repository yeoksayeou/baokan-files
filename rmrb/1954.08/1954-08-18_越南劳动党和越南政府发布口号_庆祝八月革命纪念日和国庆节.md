Title: The Vietnamese Labor Party and the Vietnamese Government Release Slogans to Celebrate the Anniversary of the August Revolution and National Day
Author:
Date: 1954-08-18
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, North Vietnam, August 16th: The Vietnamese Labor Party and the Government of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam have released slogans to celebrate the August 19th anniversary of the Vietnamese August Revolution and the September 2nd Vietnamese National Day.

    1. Warmly welcome the Indochina Armistice Agreement signed at the Geneva Conference.
    2. Resolutely fight for the implementation of the armistice agreement.
    3. The people of the whole country should further strengthen unity, consolidate peace, and achieve unification, complete national independence and democracy.
    4. Long live the heroic Vietnamese People's Army, the solid pillar for consolidating peace and national defense.
    5. Strive to advance land reform in the liberated areas.
    6. Work hard to realize the government's economic policy: taking both public and private interests into consideration; benefiting both labor and capital; restoring agricultural and industrial production.
    7. Unite intellectuals from all walks of life to realize a national, scientific, and mass culture and education.
    8. Equality, unity, and mutual assistance among all nationalities in the country.
    9. Respect the people's freedom of belief and religious freedom.
    10. Fight for the freedom and democratic rights of compatriots in South Vietnam.
    11. Fight for the realization of national unification through universal suffrage.
    12. Long live the close unity of the people of Vietnam, Laos, and Cambodia.
    13. Let us unite warmly with the people of Southeast Asian countries, the French people, and peace-loving people all over the world.
    14. Long live the great unity of Vietnam, the Soviet Union, and the People's Republic of China.
    15. Long live the Democratic Republic of Vietnam.
    16. Long live lasting peace in Asia and the world.
    17. Long live the Vietnamese Labor Party.
    18. Long live President Ho Chi Minh.
    19. Long live peace, unification, independence, and democracy in Vietnam.
    20. Warmly welcome the anniversary of the Vietnamese August Revolution and the 9th anniversary of National Day on September 2nd.
    21. Long live our consolidated people's regime.

### 越南劳动党和越南政府发布口号  庆祝八月革命纪念日和国庆节

1954-08-18
第4版()
专栏：

    越南劳动党和越南政府发布口号
    庆祝八月革命纪念日和国庆节
    【新华社十七日讯】据越南通讯社北越十六日讯：越南劳动党和越南民主共和国政府发布庆祝八月十九日越南八月革命纪念日和九月二日越南国庆节的口号。
    一、热烈欢迎在日内瓦会议上签订的印度支那停战协定。
    二、坚决为停战协定的实施而奋斗。
    三、全国人民进一步加强团结，巩固和平、实现统一、完成全国的独立和民主。
    四、巩固和平和国防的牢固支柱——英勇的越南人民军万岁。
    五、为推进解放区的土地改革而奋斗。
    六、努力实现政府的经济政策：公私兼顾；劳资两利；恢复农业和工业生产。
    七、团结各阶层知识分子，实现民族的、科学的、大众的文化教育。
    八、全国各民族平等、团结和互助。
    九、尊重人民的信仰自由和宗教自由。
    十、为实现越南南部同胞的自由和民主权利而斗争。
    十一、为通过普选实现全国的统一而斗争。
    十二、越南、老挝和柬埔寨的人民的紧密团结万岁。
    十三、让我们同东南亚各国人民、法国人民和全世界爱好和平的人民热诚地团结起来。
    十四、越南、苏联和中华人民共和国的伟大团结万岁。
    十五、越南民主共和国万岁。
    十六、亚洲和世界的巩固和平万岁。
    十七、越南劳动党万岁。
    十八、胡志明主席万岁。
    十九、越南的和平、统一、独立和民主万岁。
    二十、热烈迎接越南八月革命纪念日和九月二日国庆节九周年纪念。
    二十一、我们巩固的人民政权万岁。
