Title: The Vietnamese Embassy in Our Country Holds Reception for Deputy Prime Minister Pham Van Dong's Visit to Our Country; Vice Chairmen Zhu De, Liu Shaoqi, Li Jishen, and Premier Zhou Enlai Attend
Author:
Date: 1954-08-04
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, 3rd. The Chargé d'affaires ad interim of the Embassy of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam in our country, Zhou Liang, held a reception on the evening of the 3rd for the visit of Pham Van Dong, Deputy Prime Minister and Acting Minister of Foreign Affairs of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam, to China.

The Chinese attendees at the reception included: Vice Chairmen of the Central People's Government Zhu De, Liu Shaoqi, and Li Jishen, Secretary-General Lin Boqu, Premier of the State Council Zhou Enlai, Vice Premiers Dong Biwu, Guo Moruo, and Deng Xiaoping, Vice Chairmen of the National Committee of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference (CPPCC) Shen Junru and Chen Shutong, Members of the Central People's Government Committee Deng Zihui, Ma Yinchu, Shen Yanbing, Cai Tingkai, Peng Zemin, Zhang Bojun, Zhang Xiruo, Members of the State Council Zeng Shan, Teng Daiyuan, Chen Shaoxian, Wang Kunlun, Luo Longji, Zhang Naiqi, Shao Lizi, and Huang Shaohong, Standing Committee Members of the National Committee of the CPPCC Li Weihan, Liang Xi, Xu Guangping, Huang Qixiang, Xu Deheng, Li Siguang, and Li Dequan, Deputy Chief of the General Staff of the People's Revolutionary Military Council Zhang Zongxun, Deputy Directors of the General Political Department Fu Zhong, Xiao Hua, and Gan Siqi, Vice Presidents of the Supreme People's Court Zhang Zhirang and Zhang Su, Deputy Chief Procurator of the Supreme People's Procuratorate Gao Kelin, Vice Chairman of the State Planning Commission Jia Tuofu, Deputy Director of the Political and Legal Committee of the State Council Luo Ruiqing, Deputy Director of the Financial and Economic Committee Ye Jizhuang, Deputy Director of the Cultural and Educational Committee Xi Zhongxun, Deputy Director of the People's Supervisory Committee Pan Zhenya, Heads of various departments of the Central People's Government Wang Heshou, Zhao Erlu, Chu Tunan, and Hu Yuzhi, Vice Ministers of Foreign Affairs Wang Jiaxiang, Zhang Hanfu, and Wu Xiuquan, Heads of various departments of the People's Revolutionary Military Council and various services and arms of the Chinese People's Liberation Army Liu Fei, Xu Liqing, Xiao Ke, Xiao Xiangrong, Wang Zhen, Chen Xilian, Xu Guangda, Chen Shiju, Su Zhenhua, Wu Faxian, Zhang Zhen, Fu Qiutao, Cheng Jun, Tan Jiasu, Qiu Chuancheng, Li Tianhuan, Wang Bingzhang, Lu Zhengcao, Luo Shunchu, and Huang Zhiyong, Vice Mayors of the Beijing Municipal People's Government Zhang Youyu and Wu Han, as well as Deputy Ministers of various ministries of the Central People's Government, members of the National Committee of the CPPCC in Beijing, responsible persons of various units directly under the Central Committee of the CPC, various democratic parties, and people's organizations, non-party personages, representatives of various fraternal nationalities, and responsible persons of organs under the Central Government, North China, and Beijing Municipality, and well-known figures from the capital's scientific, educational, news, literary, artistic, and publishing circles, totaling more than 500 people.

The Vietnamese attendees included: Phan Anh, Minister of Industry and Commerce of the Government of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam, Tran Cong Tuong, Deputy Minister of Justice, the Vietnamese Women's Delegation headed by Ha Que, which will attend the Korean National Women's Congress, the Vietnamese Youth Delegation headed by Ho Truc, which will participate in the Beijing meeting of the World Federation of Democratic Youth Council, and Vietnamese students studying in our country.

The Lao Youth Delegation, which will also participate in the Beijing meeting of the World Federation of Democratic Youth Council, was also invited to attend the reception.

The diplomatic envoys of various countries to our country who attended the reception included: Burmese Ambassador U Hla Maung, Indian Ambassador R.K. Nehru, Romanian Ambassador Teodor Rudenco, Indonesian Ambassador Mononutu, Ambassador of the German Democratic Republic, Johannes König, Ambassador of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea, Choi Il, Soviet Chargé d'affaires ad interim V.V. Vaskov, Czechoslovak Chargé d'affaires ad interim Smíd, Hungarian Chargé d'affaires ad interim Czátor Dolhai, Bulgarian Chargé d'affaires ad interim Grigorov, Mongolian Chargé d'affaires ad interim Lubsanchultem, Polish Chargé d'affaires ad interim Gura, as well as diplomatic officials from various countries' embassies in our country.

At the meeting, Zhou Liang, Chargé d'affaires ad interim of the Embassy of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam in our country, first delivered a welcome speech. Subsequently, Deputy Prime Minister Pham Van Dong and Premier Zhou Enlai delivered speeches one after another (full texts will be published separately). Their speeches were warmly welcomed.

The reception proceeded in a warm and friendly atmosphere.

### 为范文同副总理访问我国  越南驻我国大使馆举行招待会  朱德刘少奇李济深副主席和周总理等出席

1954-08-04
第1版()
专栏：

    为范文同副总理访问我国
    越南驻我国大使馆举行招待会
    朱德刘少奇李济深副主席和周总理等出席
   【新华社三日讯】越南民主共和国驻我国大使馆临时代办周亮，为越南民主共和国副总理兼代理外交部部长范文同访问中国，在三日晚举行招待会。
    中国方面出席招待会的，有：中央人民政府副主席朱德、刘少奇、李济深，秘书长林伯渠，政务院总理周恩来、副总理董必武、郭沫若、邓小平，中国人民政治协商会议全国委员会副主席沈钧儒、陈叔通，中央人民政府委员会委员邓子恢、马寅初、沈雁冰、蔡廷锴、彭泽民、章伯钧、张奚若、政务院政务委员曾山、滕代远、陈劭先、王昆仑、罗隆基、章乃器、邵力子、黄绍竑，中国人民政治协商会议全国委员会常务委员李维汉、梁希、许广平、黄琪翔、许德珩、李四光、李德全，中央人民政府人民革命军事委员会副总参谋长张宗逊，总政治部副主任傅钟、萧华、甘泗淇，最高人民法院副院长张志让、张苏，最高人民检察署副检察长高克林，国家计划委员会副主席贾拓夫，政务院政治法律委员会副主任罗瑞卿，财政经济委员会副主任叶季壮，文化教育委员会副主任习仲勋，人民监察委员会副主任潘震亚，中央人民政府各部门首长王鹤寿、赵尔陆、楚图南、胡愈之，外交部副部长王稼祥、章汉夫、伍修权，人民革命军事委员会各部门、中国人民解放军各兵种首长刘斐、徐立清、萧克、萧向荣、王震、陈锡联、许光达、陈士榘、苏振华、吴法宪、张震、傅秋涛、成钧、谭家述、邱创成、李天焕、王秉璋、吕正操、罗舜初、黄志勇，北京市人民政府副市长张友渔、吴晗，以及中央人民政府各部副部长，人民政协全国委员会在京委员，中共中央直属各单位和各民主党派、各人民团体的负责人，无党派人士，各兄弟民族的代表，中央、华北和北京市所属机关的负责人，首都科学、教育、新闻、文学、艺术、出版界著名人士等共五百多人。
    越南方面出席的，有：越南民主共和国政府工商部部长潘英，司法部副部长陈公祥，将出席朝鲜全国妇女代表大会的以何桂为首的越南妇女代表团，将参加世界民主青年联盟理事会北京会议的以胡竹为首的越南青年代表团，以及越南在我国的留学生。
    将参加世界民主青年联盟理事会北京会议的寮国青年代表团也应邀出席了招待会。
    出席招待会的各国驻我国外交使节有：缅甸大使吴拉茂、印度大使赖嘉文、罗马尼亚大使郭佐文、印尼大使莫诺努图、德意志民主共和国大使柯尼希、朝鲜民主主义人民共和国大使崔一、苏联临时代办华司考、捷克斯洛伐克临时代办石密茨、匈牙利临时代办察托尔道伊、保加利亚临时代办格利郭洛夫、蒙古临时代办罗布桑楚勒图姆、波兰临时代办古拉，以及各国驻我国使馆的外交官员。
    会上，越南民主共和国驻我国大使馆临时代办周亮首先致欢迎词。接着，范文同副总理、周恩来总理先后讲话（全文另发）。他们的讲话受到了热烈欢迎。
    招待会在热烈友好的空气中进行。
