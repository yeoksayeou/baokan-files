Title: Our Great Motherland (Picture)
Author:
Date: 1954-08-28
Page: 1

    Our Great Motherland
    In the wheat-producing areas of Jilin Province (mainly the vast plains on both sides of the Songhua River), more than 90,000 hectares of spring wheat have yielded a bumper harvest, with an average yield of more than 1,812 catties per hectare, exceeding the national planned output by more than 10%. The picture shows members of the Xinghuo Agricultural Production Cooperative in Fuyu County, a wheat-producing area, loading harvested wheat onto large carts.

### 我们伟大的祖国（图片）

1954-08-28
第1版()
专栏：

    我们伟大的祖国
    吉林省产麦区（以松花江两岸大平原为主）的九万多垧少麦，获得丰收，平均每垧产量超过一千八百十二斤的国家计划产量一成以上。图为产麦区扶余县星火农业生产合作社社员把收割下来的小麦装上大车。
