Title: This is No Ordinary Star
Author: Lǐ Ruífū
Date: 1954-08-15
Page: 6

    This is No Ordinary Star
    Li Ruifu
    Along the northern edge of the military demarcation line,
    There is a string of bright stars,
    Regardless of day or night,
    Forever shining brightly.
    On stormy nights,
    When people are falling asleep,
    Only this string of stars,
    Illuminates the peaks of the front line.
    On snowy winter nights,
    When families are gathered around the stove chatting,
    Yet this string of stars,
    Moves through the wind and snow.
    These are not stars in the sky,
    These are "divine soldiers" on the ground,
    Patrolling the front line day and night,
    Watching the enemy's movements.
    These are not ordinary stars,
    These are peace sentinels,
    Ready to catch jackals crossing the border,
    Ready to eliminate the sparks of war.
    With this string of stars,
    People sleep peacefully;
    With this string of stars,
    The land remains ever green.
    Ah!
    You are not ordinary stars,
    You are heroes who defend peace,
    The people praise you in unison,
    With the most moving songs.
    July 1954
              Kaicheng

### 这不是普通的星星
李蕤夫
1954-08-15
第6版()
专栏：

    这不是普通的星星
    李蕤夫
    军事分界线北缘，
    有一串明亮的星星，
    不分白天黑夜，
    永远闪烁光明。
    在雷雨交加的夜晚，
    人们正沉入梦中，
    只有这串星星，
    照亮前沿的山峰。
    在漫天风雪的冬夜，
    亲人们正围炉谈心，
    可是这串星星，
    冒着风雪游动。
    这不是天上的星星，
    这是地上的“神兵”，
    日夜巡逻在前沿，
    注视敌人的动静。
    这不是普通的星星，
    这是和平的哨兵，
    要捕捉越界的豺狼，
    要消灭战争的火种。
    有了这串星星，
    人们就睡得宁静；
    有了这串星星，
    地上就草木常青。
    呵！
    你们不是普通的星星，
    你们是保卫和平的英雄，
    人民齐声赞颂你们，
    用最动人的歌声。
    一九五四年七月
              开城
