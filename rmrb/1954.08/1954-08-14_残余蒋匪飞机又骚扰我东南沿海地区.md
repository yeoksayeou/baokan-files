Title: Remnant Chiang Kai-shek Bandit Planes Harass Southeast Coastal Areas Again
Author:
Date: 1954-08-14
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, August 13th: Remnant Kuomintang air force planes from Taiwan are again harassing our southeast coastal areas.

　　At 10:00 AM on the 6th, two American-made Chiang Kai-shek F-47 fighter planes attacked the Quzhou area of Zhejiang Province. After being hit by our anti-aircraft artillery, they fled in a southeasterly direction.

　　On the 12th, two American-made Chiang Kai-shek F-47 fighter planes flew over Wuyi and Jinhua in Zhejiang Province for reconnaissance before fleeing. On the same day, one American-made Chiang Kai-shek plane harassed Pingtan and Gutian areas in Fujian Province; another harassed Nankang and Shangyou areas in Jiangxi Province.

### 残余蒋匪飞机又骚扰我东南沿海地区

1954-08-14
第4版()
专栏：

    残余蒋匪飞机又骚扰我东南沿海地区
    【新华社十三日讯】台湾国民党残余空军飞机又骚扰我东南沿海地区。
    六日十时，美造蒋匪F—四十七型战斗机两架袭击我浙江衢州地区，遭我高射炮打击后，向东南方向逃去。
    十二日，美造蒋匪F—四十七型战斗机二架，飞临我浙江武义、金华上空侦察，后逸去。同日，美造蒋匪飞机一架骚扰我福建省平潭和古田地区；另一架骚扰江西省南康和上犹等地区。
