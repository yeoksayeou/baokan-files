Title: British Labour Party Delegation Leaves Moscow to Visit China
Author:
Date: 1954-08-14
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, August 13th: The British Labour Party delegation, headed by Mr. Clement Attlee, consisting of eight members, who are visiting China at the invitation of the Chinese government, left Moscow for Beijing by plane on August 12th.

Those who saw the delegation off at the airport included: N. I. Bobrovnikov, Deputy Chairman of the Executive Committee of the Moscow City Soviet; L. N. Solovyov, Vice Chairman of the All-Union Central Council of Trade Unions; Ye. D. Kiselev, Director of the Protocol Department of the Soviet Ministry of Foreign Affairs; and L. Kislova, Member of the Council of the Soviet Society for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries.

Also at the airport to see them off were Wen Ning, Chargé d'Affaires ad interim of the Chinese Embassy in the Soviet Union, William Hayter, British Ambassador Extraordinary and Plenipotentiary to the Soviet Union, and U Onh Ghin, Ambassador Extraordinary and Plenipotentiary of the Union of Burma to the Soviet Union.

Xinhua News Agency, August 13th: According to TASS Moscow, August 12th, the British Labour Party delegation, headed by Mr. Clement Attlee, which passed through Moscow on its way to visit the People's Republic of China, visited Moscow State University, toured the Kremlin, the subway, medical facilities and the city of Moscow during its stay in Moscow. The delegation also paid respects at the Lenin and Stalin Mausoleums on the 12th.

### 英国工党代表团离莫斯科来我国访问

1954-08-14
第1版()
专栏：

    英国工党代表团离莫斯科来我国访问
     【新华社莫斯科十三日电】应邀访问中国的、以克·艾德礼先生为首的英国工党代表团一行八人已于八月十二日乘飞机离莫斯科赴北京。
    到机场送行的有：莫斯科市苏维埃执行委员会副主席尼·伊·波布罗夫尼科夫，全苏工会中央理事会副主席列·尼·索洛维耶夫，苏联外交部交际司司长叶·德·基谢列夫及苏联对外文化协会理事会理事丽·基斯洛娃。
    到机场送行的还有中华人民共和国驻苏联大使馆临时代办温宁，英国驻苏联特命全权大使威廉·海特尔和缅甸联邦驻苏联特命全权大使吴旺等。
    【新华社十三日讯】据塔斯社莫斯科十二日讯：以克·艾德礼先生为首的、路经莫斯科前往中华人民共和国访问的英国工党代表团在莫斯科逗留期间访问了国立莫斯科大学，参观了克里姆林宫、地下铁道、医疗机关及莫斯科市容。代表团并于十二日谒列宁、斯大林陵墓。
