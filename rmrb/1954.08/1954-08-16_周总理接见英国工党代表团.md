Title: Premier Zhou Enlai Receives British Labour Party Delegation
Author:
Date: 1954-08-16
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, August 15th: Premier Zhou Enlai of the State Council of the Central People's Government received the British Labour Party delegation, headed by Mr. Clement Attlee, which came to China at the invitation of the Chinese People's Institute of Foreign Affairs, on the morning of the 15th, and had lunch with them after the meeting.

Present at the meeting were members of the British Labour Party delegation: Mr. Aneurin Bevan, Mrs. E. Summerskill, Mr. W. Burke, Mr. Morgan Phillips, Mr. Stephen Watson, Mr. Harold Earnshaw, Mr. Henry Franklin, and Lord and Lady Lindsay, the delegation's translators.

Present on the Chinese side were: Vice Premiers of the State Council of the Central People's Government Huang Yanpei and Deng Xiaoping, Vice Minister of Foreign Affairs Zhang Hanfu, Minister of Foreign Trade Ye Jizhuang, President of the Chinese People's Institute of Foreign Affairs Zhang Xiruo, Vice Presidents Zhou Gengsheng and Qian Duansheng, and Director of the Department of European and African Affairs of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs Huan Xiang. ### 周总理接见英国工党代表团

1954-08-16
第1版()
专栏：

    周总理接见英国工党代表团
    【新华社十五日讯】我中央人民政府政务院周恩来总理在十五日上午接见应中国人民外交学会邀请来我国访问的、以克·艾德礼先生为首的英国工党代表团，并于接见后同进午餐。
    在座的，有：英国工党代表团团员安·比万先生、埃·萨末斯基尔夫人、韦·伯克先生、摩·菲利普斯先生、塞·华生先生、哈·厄恩萧先生、亨·弗兰克林先生和代表团翻译林迈可勋爵夫妇。
    中国方面在座的，有：中央人民政府政务院副总理黄炎培、邓小平，外交部副部长章汉夫，对外贸易部部长叶季壮，中国人民外交学会会长张奚若，副会长周鲠生、钱端升，外交部欧非司司长宦乡。（附图片）
    周恩来总理和英国工党代表团合影。前排左起：韦·伯克、安·比万、克·艾德礼、周总理、埃·萨末斯基尔、亨·弗兰克林；第二排左第二人起：摩·菲利普斯、塞·华生、哈·厄恩萧、中国人民外交学会会长张奚若。新华社记者　齐观山摄
