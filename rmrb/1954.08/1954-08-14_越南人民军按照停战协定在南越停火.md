Title: Vietnamese People's Army Ceases Fire in South Vietnam in Accordance with Armistice Agreement
Author:
Date: 1954-08-14
Page: 4

    Xinhua News Agency, 13th: Hanoi dispatch: According to the Vietnam Agreement on the Cessation of Hostilities, the Vietnamese People's Army ceased fire in South Vietnam on the 11th.
    Prior to this, North Vietnam and Central Vietnam had ceased fire on July 27th and August 1st respectively, and Laos and Cambodia had ceased fire on August 6th and 7th respectively. Thus, a ceasefire has been achieved throughout Indochina.

### 越南人民军按照停战协定在南越停火

1954-08-14
第4版()
专栏：

    越南人民军按照停战协定在南越停火
    【新华社十三日讯】北越消息：按照越南停止敌对行动协定，越南人民军十一日已在南越停火。
    在此以前，北越和中越已分别在七月二十七日和八月一日停火，老挝和柬埔寨已分别在八月六日和七日停火。至此，印度支那全境已实现停火。
