Title: Communist Parties of Britain, Denmark, Austria, and other countries voice support for the heroic struggle of the Communist Party of the United States against persecution
Author:
Date: 1954-08-24
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, London, August 19th: TASS reported from London on the 18th: The Executive Committee of the Communist Party of Great Britain sent a telegram to the Communist Party of the United States, expressing support for the Communist Party of the United States' struggle against persecution. The telegram said: "We protest against the reactionary forces in the United States declaring your party illegal, an act of destruction. We assure you that all democrats in Britain stand in solidarity with and support your struggle against these fascist attacks."

Xinhua News Agency, August 22nd: TASS reported from London on the 20th: The Executive Committee of the Communist Party of Great Britain sent a telegram to U.S. President Eisenhower, strongly protesting the U.S. Congress's deprivation of the democratic rights of communist organizations and trade unions, and the persecution of communist party members.

Xinhua News Agency, August 23rd: TASS reported from Copenhagen on the 19th: The Central Committee of the Communist Party of Denmark sent a telegram of condolences to the National Committee of the Communist Party of the United States. The telegram said: The persecution of the Communist Party of the United States has aroused indignation in all circles of Danish society. The democratic forces of Denmark believe that the attack on the Communist Party of the United States is an attack on all truly democratic forces in the United States.

Xinhua News Agency, August 23rd: TASS reported from Vienna on the 22nd: The Central Committee of the Communist Party of Austria sent a telegram to the National Committee of the Communist Party of the United States, the text of which is as follows: We have learned with great indignation that the reactionary forces in the United States are trying to crack down on the entire American workers' movement and all progressive forces in the United States by outlawing the Communist Party. We firmly protest this act of undermining basic democratic principles, we express our deep sympathy to you, and we express our full solidarity with your heroic struggle for the cause of the working class and for peace and democracy.

### 英国、丹麦、奥地利等国共产党  声援美国共产党反迫害的英勇斗争

1954-08-24
第4版()
专栏：

    英国、丹麦、奥地利等国共产党
    声援美国共产党反迫害的英勇斗争
    【新华社十九日讯】　据塔斯社伦敦十八日讯：英国共产党执行委员会致电美国共产党，声援美国共产党的反迫害斗争。电文说：“我们对美国反动派宣布你们的党为非法的这种摧残行为表示抗议。我们向你们保证，英国的一切民主人士都声援并支持你们对这些法西斯性攻击的斗争。”
    【新华社二十二日讯】　据塔斯社伦敦二十日讯，英国共产党执行委员会致电美国总统艾森豪威尔，强硬抗议美国国会剥夺共产党组织和工会的民主权利，并且迫害共产党党员。
    另据塔斯社哥本哈根十九日讯，丹麦共产党中央委员会打电报慰问美国共产党全国委员会。电报说：对美国共产党的迫害引起了丹麦社会各界的愤慨。丹麦的民主力量认为：对美国共产党的打击，就是对美国所有真正民主力量的打击。
    【新华社二十三日讯】　塔斯社维也纳二十二日讯：奥地利共产党中央委员会致电美国共产党全国委员会，电文如下：我们以极大的愤慨获悉美国反动派要用取缔共产党的办法来打击美国整个工人运动和美国所有的进步力量。我们坚决抗议这种破坏民主基本原则的行为，我们对你们表示深切的同情，并对你们为工人阶级事业、为和平和民主而进行的英勇斗争表示充分的声援。
