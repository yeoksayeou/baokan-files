Title: French Cabinet Holds Meeting; Passes French Premier's Proposal Regarding "European Army" Treaty; Three Ministers of the De Gaulle Faction Resign Due to Disagreement
Author:
Date: 1954-08-18
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency, Paris, 17th dispatch: According to newspaper reports, the French government cabinet held a meeting on the 13th, chaired by French President Coty. A very intense debate took place during the meeting. The meeting passed French Premier Mendès France's proposal regarding the "European Defense Community" issue. The cabinet authorized Mendès France to defend France's proposals for revising certain clauses in the "European Army" treaty draft at the upcoming meeting of the "European Defense Community" six nations in Brussels. General international political issues were also discussed at the meeting.

Several ministers from the Social Republican Party (De Gaulle faction) – Minister of National Defense and Armed Forces General Koenig, Minister of Housing and Construction Lemaire, and Minister of Public Works, Transportation and Tourism Chaban-Delmas – announced their disagreement with the cabinet's decision and immediately submitted their resignations.

Mendès France decided not to appoint new ministers to fill the vacancies until the "European Army" debate is over.

### 法国内阁举行会议  通过法总理关于“欧洲军”条约建议  戴高乐派的三个部长因不同意而辞职

1954-08-18
第4版()
专栏：

    法国内阁举行会议
    通过法总理关于“欧洲军”条约建议
    戴高乐派的三个部长因不同意而辞职
    【新华社十七日讯】据塔斯社巴黎讯：据报纸报道，法国政府内阁十三日举行会议，由法国总统戈蒂担任主席，会议中辩论十分激烈。会上通过了法国总理孟戴斯—弗朗斯关于“欧洲防务集团”问题的建议。内阁授权孟戴斯—弗朗斯在“欧洲防务集团”六国即将在布鲁塞尔举行的会议上，为法国关于修改“欧洲军”条约草案中若干条款的建议进行辩护。在会上还讨论了总的国际政治问题。
    社会共和党（戴高乐派）的几个部长——国防和武装部队部部长柯尼格将军、房屋与建设部部长勒梅尔和公共工程、运输与游览部部长沙邦—戴尔马宣布不同意内阁的决议，并且立刻提出辞职。
    孟戴斯—弗朗斯决定在“欧洲军”辩论结束以前不任命新部长来补缺。
