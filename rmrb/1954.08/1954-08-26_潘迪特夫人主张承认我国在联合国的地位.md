Title: Mrs. Pandit Advocates Recognition of China's Position in the United Nations
Author:
Date: 1954-08-26
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency, Jakarta, August 25th: Mrs. Pandit, President of the Eighth Session of the United Nations General Assembly, who is currently visiting various parts of Indonesia, said at a press conference in Surabaya on the 24th that the People's Republic of China must obtain its seat in the United Nations. She said that major issues such as the Indochina problem obviously cannot be resolved without the participation of the People's Republic of China.

Prior to this, on the 22nd, while in Yogyakarta discussing the People's Republic of China's entry into the United Nations, she pointed out that accepting the People's Republic of China into the United Nations and returning Taiwan to the People's Republic of China would help ease international tensions. In her remarks, she clarified that the People's Republic of China should have its seat in the United Nations, and that the island of Taiwan is part of the territory of the People's Republic of China.

### 潘迪特夫人主张承认我国在联合国的地位

1954-08-26
第4版()
专栏：

    潘迪特夫人主张承认我国在联合国的地位
   【新华社雅加达二十五日电】目前正在印度尼西亚各地访问的联合国第八届大会主席潘迪特夫人二十四日在泗水举行记者招待会时说，中华人民共和国必须取得它在联合国中的席位。她说，像印度支那问题之类的主要问题，如果没有中华人民共和国参加，显然不能得到解决。
    在这以前，二十二日她在日惹谈到中华人民共和国应该进入联合国时指出，接纳中华人民共和国进入联合国并把台湾归还给中华人民共和国，将有助于缓和国际紧张局势。她在谈话中说明了：中华人民共和国应该有它在联合国的席位，而台湾岛是中华人民共和国领土的一部分。
