Our Great Motherland
    Dalian Universal Machine Factory successfully trial-produced a scraper. The scraper is an important mechanized equipment for constructing building sites, railway and highway subgrades, dams and ditches. The test run of the first scraper was good. Soon, the scrapers produced by the factory will be continuously supplied to the needs of national key construction projects.

### 我们伟大的祖国（图片）

1954-08-29
第1版()
专栏：

    我们伟大的祖国
    大连通用机器厂试制刮土机成功。刮土机是修建建筑场地、铁路和公路的路基、堤坝和沟渠的重要机械化设备。第一台刮土机的试车情况良好，不久该厂生产的刮土机将源源供应国家重点建设工程的需要。
