Title: British Labour Party Delegation Arrives in Beijing at the Invitation of the Chinese People's Institute of Foreign Affairs
Author:
Date: 1954-08-15
Page: 1

    Xinhua News Agency, August 14th: The British Labour Party delegation, consisting of Clement Attlee, Aneurin Bevan, Edward Samuel, Wilfred Burke, Morgan Phillips, Stephen Watson, Harold Earnshaw, Henry Franklin, and others, arrived in Beijing by plane at 12:00 noon on August 14th, at the invitation of the Chinese People's Institute of Foreign Affairs.
    Lord Michael Lindsay and his wife, translators for the delegation, had arrived in Beijing a few days earlier.
    Among those welcoming them at the airport were Zhang Xiruo, President of the Chinese People's Institute of Foreign Affairs; Vice Presidents Zhou Gengsheng, Chen Hansheng, Hu Yuzhi, Qian Duansheng, and Qiao Guanhua; Secretary-General Wu Maosun; and Council members Luo Longji and Wang Tieya. Also present at the airport were Guo Moruo and Chen Shutong, Vice Chairmen of the National Committee of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference; Xing Xiping, Deputy Secretary-General; Liao Chengzhi, and representatives from the Chinese Communist Party, various democratic parties, and people's organizations, including Zhang Bojun, Chen Shaoxian, Sun Qimeng, Xu Guangping, Huang Qixiang, Chen Qiyou, Li Chunqing, Qu Tangliang, Li Dequan, Liu Ningyi, Chu Tunan, Wu Yaozong, Fu Lianzhang, Shu Sheyu, Wu Yunfu, Ji Chaoding, and Qian Weichang; Zhang Hanfu, Vice Minister of Foreign Affairs of the Central People's Government; Huan Xiang, Director of the Department of European and African Affairs; Wang Zhuoru, Director of the Protocol Department; Lei Renmin, Vice Minister of Foreign Trade; Ding Xilin, Vice Minister of Culture; and Cao Zhongshu, Head of the Chinese Trade Delegation to the United Kingdom. Peng Zhen, Mayor of the Beijing Municipal People's Government, was also present at the airport to welcome them. William Devey, the British Chargé d'Affaires in China, was also present at the airport. After disembarking from the plane, the delegation members received flowers presented by children of the capital and shook hands with the welcomers.

### 应中国人民外交学会邀请来我国访问  英国工党代表团到达北京

1954-08-15
第1版()
专栏：

    应中国人民外交学会邀请来我国访问
    英国工党代表团到达北京
    【新华社十四日讯】应中国人民外交学会邀请来我国访问的英国工党代表团克·艾德礼、安·比万、埃·萨末斯基尔、韦·伯克、摩·菲利普斯、塞·华生、哈·厄恩萧、亨·弗兰克林等一行八人在八月十四日上午十二时乘飞机到达北京。
    代表团翻译林迈可勋爵夫妇已于数日前先到北京。
    到飞机场欢迎的有中国人民外交学会会长张奚若，副会长周鲠生、陈翰笙、胡愈之、钱端升、乔冠华，秘书长吴茂荪，理事罗隆基、王铁崖等。到机场欢迎的还有中国人民政治协商会议全国委员会副主席郭沫若、陈叔通，副秘书长邢西萍，中国共产党和各民主党派、人民团体的代表廖承志、章伯钧、陈劭先、孙起孟、许广平、黄琪翔、陈其尤、李纯青、区棠亮、李德全、刘宁一、楚图南、吴耀宗、傅连暲、舒舍予、伍云甫、冀朝鼎、钱伟长，中央人民政府外交部副部长章汉夫、欧非司司长宦乡、交际处处长王倬如，对外贸易部副部长雷任民，文化部副部长丁西林，中国赴英贸易访问团团长曹中枢等。北京市人民政府市长彭真也到机场欢迎。到机场欢迎的还有英国驻华代办杜维廉。代表团人员下机后，接受了首都少年儿童的献花，并与欢迎者一一握手。
