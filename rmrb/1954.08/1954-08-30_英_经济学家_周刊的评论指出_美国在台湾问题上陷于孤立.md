Title: The British "Economist" Weekly's Commentary Points Out that the United States is Isolated on the Taiwan Issue
Author:
Date: 1954-08-30
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, August 27th:

A commentary published in the British weekly "The Economist" on August 21st stated that on the Taiwan issue, "the United States is clearly in a weak position internationally. The U.S. protection of Taiwan is a unilateral action; it has neither gained the support of Britain nor any endorsement from the United Nations." The commentary said that not only could Britain not participate in any guarantee of Taiwan because it recognized China, "but British public opinion is almost entirely sympathetic to Beijing's position on this issue." The commentary said, "Indeed, no issue that could plunge the United States into a war crisis receives less moral and political support from Britain than this one."

### 英“经济学家”周刊的评论指出  美国在台湾问题上陷于孤立

1954-08-30
第4版()
专栏：

    英“经济学家”周刊的评论指出
    美国在台湾问题上陷于孤立
      【新华社二十七日讯】英国“经济学家”周刊八月二十一日发表评论说，在台湾问题上，“美国在国际上显然处于软弱的地位。美国保护台湾是片面的行动；它既没有获得英国的支持，也没有得到联合国的任何赞同。”评论说，不仅英国由于承认了中国，因而在任何情况下，都不能参加对台湾的保证，“而且英国舆论在这个问题上几乎完全同情北京的立场。”评论说，“的确，没有一个可能使美国陷入战争危机中的问题比这个问题更得不到英国道义上和政治上的支持的了。”
