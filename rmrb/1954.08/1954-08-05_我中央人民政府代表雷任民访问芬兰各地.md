Title: My Central People's Government Representative Lei Renmin Visits Various Places in Finland
Author:
Date: 1954-08-05
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Prague, August 4th: Helsinki Telephone: Lei Renmin, representative of the Central People's Government of the People's Republic of China and Vice Minister of Foreign Trade, who is visiting Finland, left Helsinki on July 30th to visit Turku, the primary city in eastern Finland. In Turku, he visited the Wärtsilä shipyard. On the way to Turku, Lei Renmin visited the wife of Finnish Foreign Minister Kekkonen, who led the Finnish cultural delegation to China in 1953.

On July 31st, Lei Renmin visited Tampere, Finland's second largest city, and toured important factories and social welfare facilities in the city. During his visit to Tampere, Lei Renmin visited the city's mayor and attended a banquet hosted by the industrial community, which was also attended by the mayor.

On August 2nd, Lei Renmin visited a paper mill in Kumi and attended a welcome rally hosted by the Finnish-Chinese Association at 8 p.m. Among those present at the rally were Vice Chairman Tovi of the Finnish-Chinese Association, Finnish Envoy to China Sun Shidun, Chargé d'affaires Yao Dengshan of the Chinese Embassy in Finland, diplomatic envoys from the Soviet Union and other People's Democratic countries, members of the Finnish-Chinese Association, and people from all walks of life, totaling 500 people. At the meeting, Lei Renmin delivered a short speech. He described the close relationship between China and Finland in cultural exchange and trade, introduced the situation of China's economic construction in the past four years, and pointed out the prospects for the development of Sino-Finnish trade. After the meeting, "Liang Shanbo and Zhu Yingtai" and the Finnish film "Finland is Smiling" were screened.

### 我中央人民政府代表雷任民访问芬兰各地

1954-08-05
第4版()
专栏：

    我中央人民政府代表雷任民访问芬兰各地
     【新华社布拉格四日电】赫尔辛基电话：在芬兰访问的中华人民共和国中央人民政府代表、对外贸易部副部长雷任民于七月三十日离赫尔辛基前往芬兰东部首要城市土库参观。在土库，他参观了瓦兹拉造船厂。雷任民在赴土库途中拜访了曾于一九五三年率领芬兰文化代表团到中国访问的芬兰外交部长吉科宁的夫人。
    雷任民七月三十一日访问了芬兰第二大城市坦培累并参观了该市的重要工厂和社会福利事业。在访问坦培累市时，雷任民访问了该市市长，出席了工业界的宴会，该市市长也参加了这次宴会。
    八月二日，雷任民又赴库米参观造纸工厂，下午八时出席了由芬中协会主持的欢迎大会。出席大会的有芬中协会副主席托维、芬兰驻我国公使孙士敦、我国驻芬兰公使馆临时代办姚登山、苏联和各人民民主国家驻芬外交使节、芬中协会会员及各界人士等共五百人。会上，雷任民发表了简短演说。他叙述了中芬两国的文化交流和贸易往来的密切关系，介绍了四年来中国经济建设的情况，并指出了中芬贸易发展的前途。会后放映了“梁山伯与祝英台”和芬兰电影“芬兰在微笑”。
