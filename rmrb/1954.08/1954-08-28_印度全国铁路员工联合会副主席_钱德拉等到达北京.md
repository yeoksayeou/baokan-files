Title: Vice-Chairman Chandra of the All-India Railwaymen's Federation Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1954-08-28
Page: 1

    Xinhua News Agency, 26th. Vice-Chairman Chandra of the All-India Railwaymen's Federation, and Raju of the South Indian Railway Labour Union arrived in Beijing at noon on the 25th. Xiao Hongqi, Vice-Chairman of the National Committee of the Railway Workers' Union of China, and others were at the airport to greet them.

### 印度全国铁路员工联合会副主席  钱德拉等到达北京

1954-08-28
第1版()
专栏：

    印度全国铁路员工联合会副主席
    钱德拉等到达北京
   【新华社二十六日讯】印度全国铁路员工联合会副主席钱德拉，南印铁路工人工会拉久在二十五日中午到达北京。到机场迎接的有中国铁路工会全国委员会副主席萧洪启等。
