Title: Volunteer Army Units Warmly Celebrate PLA Founding Day
Author:
Date: 1954-08-04
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency:

    Volunteer Army Units Warmly Celebrate PLA Founding Day

    According to Xinhua News Agency: The leading organs and various units of the Chinese People's Volunteer Army warmly celebrated the 27th anniversary of the founding of the Chinese People's Liberation Army.

    The leading organ of the Chinese People's Volunteer Army held a celebration meeting on August 1st. The Korean People's Army delegation headed by General Park Song-bo and the Korean Ministry of Transportation Art Troupe attended the meeting.

    At the meeting, General Yang Yong, Deputy Commander of the Chinese People's Volunteer Army, delivered a speech. After briefly describing the glorious history of the Chinese People's Liberation Army, he called on all commanders and fighters of the Volunteer Army to better inherit and carry forward the fine traditions of the People's Liberation Army, conscientiously study the PLA's experience in tirelessly strengthening its regularization and modernization construction and fighting against the enemy in defending the socialist construction of the motherland, and improve the combat effectiveness of the troops, so as to successfully complete the glorious task of resisting U.S. aggression and aiding Korea. In his speech, General Yang Yong also particularly congratulated the signing of the armistice agreement in Indochina, and pointed out the fact that U.S. imperialism continues to create international tension. General Yang Yong said that in the face of this situation, the people of our country must not only continue to deepen the movement to resist U.S. aggression and aid Korea and strive for a peaceful settlement of the Korean issue, but also must continue to strive for the liberation of Taiwan, thoroughly smash the U.S. imperialist conspiracy to invade our country, and defend Asian and world peace. General Park Song-bo delivered a congratulatory speech at the commemoration meeting and presented a banner to the leading organ of the Volunteer Army. After the meeting, the Korean Ministry of Transportation Art Troupe performed wonderful song and dance programs. In the evening, the leading organ of the Volunteer Army also hosted a banquet to entertain the Korean People's Army delegation headed by General Park Song-bo and all members of the Korean Ministry of Transportation Art Troupe.

    The leading organs of various units of the Chinese People's Volunteer Army held commemorative meetings or evening parties on August 1st. Many units published wall newspapers and special issues to commemorate the "August 1st" Army Day and conducted sports competitions. The leading organ of a certain unit of the Volunteer Army stationed on the western front also exhibited pictures of the activities of the delegation of the people of the whole country to comfort the People's Liberation Army, and many representatives of the Volunteer Army who had participated in comforting the People's Liberation Army published their impressions of the consolation on the wall newspaper. The commemorative meeting held by the leading organ of a certain unit of the Volunteer Army stationed on the west coast was also attended by Kim Man-gyu, representative of the General Political Bureau of the Korean People's Army, Lieutenant General Jang Pyong-san, commander of a certain unit of the People's Army, and Li Il-kyung, chairman of the People's Committee of South Pyongan Province, who congratulated and presented banners and delivered speeches. In their speeches, they unanimously expressed their gratitude and congratulations to the Chinese People's Volunteer Army, which inherited and carried forward the glorious tradition of the Chinese People's Liberation Army. The soldiers of a certain unit of the Volunteer Army guarding the east coast put on new military uniforms, wore "Resist U.S. Aggression and Aid Korea" medals and "Long Live Peace" medals early in the morning to celebrate the festival. Wall newspapers commemorating "August 1st" appeared in many units' garrisons, and new couplets were posted on both sides of the barrack doors of some companies. The Volunteer Army units guarding the areas of Shangganling and Pyonggang also held commemorative meetings, forums, film evenings, cultural evenings, or ball games. Before "August 1st," the representatives of the Volunteer Army who had participated in the delegation of the people of the whole country to comfort the People's Liberation Army universally made reports to their respective units.

### 志愿军各部队热烈庆祝解放军建军节

1954-08-04
第1版()
专栏：

    志愿军各部队热烈庆祝解放军建军节
    据新华社讯：中国人民志愿军领导机关和各部队，热烈庆祝中国人民解放军建军二十七周年。
    中国人民志愿军领导机关在八月一日举行了庆祝大会。以朴松波将军为首的朝鲜人民军代表团和朝鲜交通省艺术团参加了大会。
    会上，中国人民志愿军副司令员杨勇将军发表了讲话。他在简述中国人民解放军的光荣历史后，号召志愿军全体指挥员、战斗员更好地继承和发扬人民解放军的优良传统，认真地学习解放军在保卫祖国社会主义建设事业中不倦地加强自己正规化现代化建设和对敌斗争的经验，提高部队的战斗力，从而圆满地完成抗美援朝斗争的光荣任务。杨勇将军在讲话中还特别祝贺印度支那停战协定的签订，并指出美帝国主义继续制造国际紧张局势的事实。杨勇将军说，面对着这一局势，我国人民不仅要继续深入加强抗美援朝运动，为争取朝鲜问题的和平解决而奋斗，并且一定要为解放台湾，彻底粉碎美帝国主义侵略我国的阴谋，为保卫亚洲和世界和平而继续奋斗。朴松波将军在纪念会上致祝词，并向志愿军领导机关献旗。会后，由朝鲜交通省艺术团演出精采的歌舞节目。当晚，志愿军领导机关并设宴招待以朴松波将军为首的朝鲜人民军代表团和朝鲜交通省艺术团全体人员。
    中国人民志愿军各部队领导机关都在八月一日分别举行了纪念大会或纪念晚会。许多单位出版了纪念“八一”建军节的墙报专刊和进行了体育比赛。驻在西线的志愿军某部领导机关还展览了全国人民慰问人民解放军代表团慰问活动的图片，许多曾参加慰问解放军的志愿军代表，都在墙报上发表了慰问感想。驻在西海岸的志愿军某部领导机关举行的纪念大会，还有朝鲜人民军总政治局代表金万奎、人民军某部司令官张平山中将和平安南道人民委员会委员长李一京等参加祝贺，并献旗致词。他们在讲话中一致向继承和发扬了中国人民解放军的光荣传统的中国人民志愿军表示感激和祝贺。守卫在东海岸的志愿军某部的战士们，清早起来，就穿上新军装、佩戴上“抗美援朝”纪念章和“和平万岁”纪念章开始欢度节日。许多部队的驻地上出现了纪念“八一”的墙报，有的连队的营房门两旁还贴上新对联。守卫在上甘岭、平康地区的志愿军各部队，也都举行了纪念会、座谈会、电影晚会、文娱晚会或球类比赛。“八一”前夕，曾参加全国人民慰问人民解放军代表团的志愿军代表，普遍地向所在部队作了传达报告。
