Title: Our Great Motherland (Picture)
Author:
Date: 1954-08-27
Page: 1

    Our Great Motherland
    Hegang, a famous coal-producing area in northeastern China, has become a beautiful new city. Now, new coal mine kindergartens, staff dormitories, miners' cinemas, miners' cultural centers, and staff sanatoriums have been built along both sides of the street. The picture shows children in a kindergarten in the mining area playing games.

### 我们伟大的祖国（图片）

1954-08-27
第1版()
专栏：

    我们伟大的祖国
    我国东北北部的著名产煤地——鹤岗，已经成为一座美丽的新城市了。现在街道的两旁新建了煤矿保育院、职工宿舍、矿工电影院、矿工文化馆和职工业余疗养所等。图为矿区幼儿园的孩子们正在作游戏。
