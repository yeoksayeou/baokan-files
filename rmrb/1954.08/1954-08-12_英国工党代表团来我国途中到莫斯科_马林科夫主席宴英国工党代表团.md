Title: British Labour Party Delegation Arrives in Moscow En Route to China; Chairman Malenkov Hosts Banquet for British Labour Party Delegation
Author:
Date: 1954-08-12
Page: 1

    Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, 11th: A British Labour Party delegation, headed by Labour Party leader Clement Attlee, arrived in Moscow by plane on the afternoon of August 10th on its way to visit China.
    The delegation was greeted at the airport by N.I. Bobrovnikov, Vice Chairman of the Moscow City Soviet Executive Committee, L.N. Solovyov, Vice Chairman of the All-Union Central Council of Trade Unions, and Ye.D. Kiselev, Director of the Protocol Department of the Soviet Ministry of Foreign Affairs, among others.
    Wen Ning, Chargé d'Affaires ad interim of the Embassy of the People's Republic of China in the Soviet Union, and Sir William Hayter, British Ambassador Extraordinary and Plenipotentiary to the Soviet Union, were also present at the airport to welcome the delegation.
   [Xinhua News Agency, 11th] TASS, Moscow, 10th: G.M. Malenkov, Chairman of the Council of Ministers of the Soviet Union, hosted a banquet on August 10th in honor of the British Labour Party delegation, headed by Mr. Clement Attlee, who were passing through on their way to the People's Republic of China.
    Members of the delegation attending the banquet included: Mr. Aneurin Bevan, Mrs. E. Summerskill, Mr. W. Burke, Mr. Morgan Phillips, Mr. S. Watson, Mr. H. Earnshaw, and Mr. H. Franklin.
    Mr. William Hayter, British Ambassador Extraordinary and Plenipotentiary to the Soviet Union, also attended the banquet.
    On the Soviet side, those present included A.I. Mikoyan, V.M. Molotov, N.S. Khrushchev, N.M. Shvernik, A.Y. Vyshinsky, M.A. Yasnov, Chairman of the Moscow City Soviet Executive Committee, N.V. Popova, and L. Kislova, a member of the Council of the Soviet Society for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries.
    The banquet was held in a friendly atmosphere.

### 英国工党代表团来我国途中到莫斯科  马林科夫主席宴英国工党代表团

1954-08-12
第1版()
专栏：

    英国工党代表团来我国途中到莫斯科
    马林科夫主席宴英国工党代表团
   【新华社莫斯科十一日电】以英国工党领袖克·艾德礼为首的英国工党代表团在赴中国访问途中于八月十日下午飞抵莫斯科。
    前往机场迎接代表团的有莫斯科市苏维埃执行委员会副主席尼·伊·波布罗夫尼科夫，全苏工会中央理事会副主席列·尼·索洛维耶夫和苏联外交部交际司司长叶·德·基谢列夫等人。
    到机场欢迎的还有中华人民共和国驻苏联大使馆临时代办温宁及英国驻苏联特命全权大使海特尔。
   【新华社十一日讯】塔斯社莫斯科十日讯：苏联部长会议主席格·马·马林科夫八月十日设宴招待以克·艾德礼先生为首的、路过这里前往中华人民共和国的英国工党代表团。
    出席宴会的有：代表团团员安·比万先生、埃·萨末斯基尔夫人、韦·伯克先生、摩·菲利普斯先生、塞·华生先生、哈·厄恩萧先生和亨·弗兰克林先生。
    出席宴会的还有英国驻苏联特命全权大使威廉·海特尔先生。
    苏联方面出席作陪的有阿·伊·米高扬、维·米·莫洛托夫、尼·谢·赫鲁晓夫、尼·米·什维尔尼克、安·扬·维辛斯基、莫斯科市苏维埃执行委员会主席米·阿·雅斯诺夫、尼·瓦·波波娃和苏联对外文化协会理事会理事丽·基斯洛娃。
    宴会在友好的空气中进行。
