Title: Commemorating the 9th Anniversary of the Soviet Army's Liberation of Korea; Premier Kim Il-sung Cables Chairman Malenkov to Express Gratitude; Kim Tu-bong and Nam Il Cable Voroshilov and Molotov Respectively
Author:
Date: 1954-08-16
Page: 4

    Xinhua News Agency, Pyongyang, August 15th: Kim Il-sung, Premier of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea, Kim Tu-bong, Chairman of the Standing Committee of the Supreme People's Assembly, and Nam Il, Minister of Foreign Affairs, sent telegrams to Chairman Malenkov of the Council of Ministers of the Soviet Union, Chairman Voroshilov of the Presidium of the Supreme Soviet of the Soviet Union, and Minister of Foreign Affairs Molotov on the 14th—the eve of the 9th anniversary of the Soviet Army's liberation of Korea—to express their gratitude.
    In his telegram to Chairman Malenkov, Premier Kim Il-sung first thanked the Soviet Union for liberating the Korean people from the shackles of Japanese imperialism.
    The telegram also stated that the selfless assistance provided by the Soviet people to the Korean people in the work of recovery and reconstruction will serve as a strong guarantee for the future prosperity, unification, and independence of Korea.
    The telegram stated: The great achievements of the Soviet people in communist construction inspire the Korean people to move towards victory.
    The telegram concluded: For the sake of lasting world peace and the progress and happiness of all mankind, I sincerely wish the Soviet people further new achievements in the great cause of communist construction.

### 纪念苏军解放朝鲜九周年  金日成首相电谢马林科夫主席  金枓奉南日分别电谢伏罗希洛夫莫洛托夫

1954-08-16
第4版()
专栏：

    纪念苏军解放朝鲜九周年
    金日成首相电谢马林科夫主席
    金枓奉南日分别电谢伏罗希洛夫莫洛托夫
     【新华社平壤十五日电】朝鲜民主主义人民共和国内阁首相金日成、最高人民会议常任委员会委员长金枓奉、外务相南日在十四日——苏联军队解放朝鲜九周年纪念日前夕，分别致电苏联部长会议主席马林科夫、苏联最高苏维埃主席团主席伏罗希洛夫、外交部部长莫洛托夫，表示感谢。
    金日成首相在给马林科夫主席的电报中首先感谢苏联使朝鲜人民从日本帝国主义羁绊下获得解放。
    电报又说，苏联人民在朝鲜恢复和重建工作中给予朝鲜人民的无私的援助，将成为朝鲜人民将来繁荣和统一、独立的坚强保证。
    电报说：苏联人民在共产主义建设中获得的巨大成就，鼓舞着朝鲜人民走向胜利。
    电报最后说：为了世界的持久和平和全人类的进步与幸福，谨祝苏联人民在伟大的共产主义建设中进一步地取得新的成就。
