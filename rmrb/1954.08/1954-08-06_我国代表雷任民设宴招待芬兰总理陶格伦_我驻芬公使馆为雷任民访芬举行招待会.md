Title: Chinese Representative Lei Renmin Hosts Banquet for Finnish Prime Minister Tuomioja; Chinese Embassy in Finland Holds Reception for Lei Renmin's Visit to Finland
Author: 
Date: 1954-08-06
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Prague, 5th: Helsinki telephone dispatch: Lei Renmin, representative of the Central People's Government of the People's Republic of China and Vice Minister of Foreign Trade, hosted a luncheon at the Chinese Embassy in Finland in Helsinki on the 4th to entertain Finnish Prime Minister Tuomioja. Also attending the luncheon were Minister of Foreign Affairs Kekkonen, Minister of Education Viriö, Minister of Trade and Industry Tervo, Acting Secretary-General of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs Wartiovaara, Director of the Commerce Department Palmroth, Finnish Ambassador to China Sundström, former Finnish Ambassador to China and current Finnish Ambassador to Switzerland Kleen, figures from industry and commerce, and the Vice Chairman of the Finnish-Chinese Association, Tovi.

From the Chinese side, those attending as escorts were Yao Dengshan, Chargé d'affaires ad interim of the Chinese Embassy in Finland, Commercial Counselor Zhang Yunxiao, Deputy Director of the Third Bureau of the Ministry of Foreign Trade Chen Ming, and Specialist of the Third Bureau Zou Siyi, etc.

The banquet proceeded in a friendly atmosphere.

Xinhua News Agency, Prague, 5th: Helsinki telephone dispatch: Yao Dengshan, Chargé d'affaires ad interim of the Chinese Embassy in Finland, held a reception at the Chinese Embassy in Finland in Helsinki on the 3rd for Lei Renmin, representative of the Central People's Government of the People's Republic of China and Vice Minister of Foreign Trade, for his visit to Finland.

Attending the reception were Finnish Minister of Foreign Affairs Kekkonen and his wife, Minister of Defense Skog, Minister of Finance V. J. Sukselainen, Minister of Education Viriö, Minister of Trade and Industry Tervo, Minister of Transportation Mitrunen, Acting Secretary-General of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs Wartiovaara, Director of the Commerce Department of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs Palmroth, Finnish Ambassador to China Sundström, former Finnish Ambassador to China Kleen, as well as over two hundred and fifty figures from industry and commerce and cultural circles.

Also attending the reception were Soviet Ambassador to Finland Lebedev and diplomatic envoys and commercial representatives of various People's Democratic countries stationed in Finland, representatives of Sweden and Israel stationed in Finland, and diplomatic officials from the British Embassy in Finland, etc.

At the reception, Lei Renmin and Kekkonen delivered speeches successively.

The reception proceeded in a friendly and warm atmosphere.

### 我国代表雷任民设宴招待芬兰总理陶格伦  我驻芬公使馆为雷任民访芬举行招待会

1954-08-06
第1版()
专栏：

    我国代表雷任民设宴招待芬兰总理陶格伦
    我驻芬公使馆为雷任民访芬举行招待会
    【新华社布拉格五日电】赫尔辛基电话：中华人民共和国中央人民政府代表、对外贸易部副部长雷任民四日在赫尔辛基中华人民共和国驻芬兰公使馆设午餐招待芬兰总理陶格伦。出席招待会的还有，外交部部长吉科宁，教育部长维洛莱宁，工商部长台尔伏，外交部代理秘书长华蒂奥瓦拉、商务司司长帕姆罗特，芬兰驻华公使孙士敦，前任芬兰驻华公使、现任芬兰驻瑞士公使克诺林，工商界人士及芬中协会副主席托维等人。
    中国方面出席作陪的有，中华人民共和国驻芬兰公使馆临时代办姚登山、商务专员张云啸，对外贸易部第三局副局长陈明，第三局专员邹斯颐等。
    宴会在友好气氛中进行。
    【新华社布拉格五日电】赫尔辛基电话：中华人民共和国驻芬兰公使馆临时代办姚登山为中华人民共和国中央人民政府代表、对外贸易部副部长雷任民访问芬兰，三日在赫尔辛基中华人民共和国驻芬兰公使馆举行了招待会。
    出席招待会的有芬兰外交部部长吉科宁夫妇，国防部长斯科格，财政部部长维·约·苏克西莱宁，教育部部长维洛莱宁，工商部部长台尔伏，交通部部长米图宁，外交部代理秘书长华蒂奥瓦拉，外交部商务司司长帕姆罗特，芬兰驻华公使孙士敦，芬兰前任驻华公使克诺林，以及工商界、文化界人士二百五十多人。
    出席招待会的还有苏联驻芬兰公使列别杰夫和各人民民主国家驻芬兰使节和商务代表，瑞典和以色列驻芬兰代表，英国驻芬兰使馆外交官员等。
    在招待会上雷任民和吉科宁先后致词。
    招待会在友好热情的气氛中进行。
