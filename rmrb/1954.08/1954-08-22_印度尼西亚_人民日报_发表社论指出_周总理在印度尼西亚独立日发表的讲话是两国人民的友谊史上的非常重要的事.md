Title: Indonesian "People's Daily" Publishes Editorial Pointing Out that Premier Zhou's Speech on Indonesian Independence Day is a Very Important Event in the History of Friendship Between the Two Peoples
Author:
Date: 1954-08-22
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Jakarta, August 21st: Jakarta newspapers prominently published Premier Zhou Enlai's speech at the reception held by the Indonesian Republic's Embassy in China to celebrate the ninth anniversary of Indonesian independence.

	Jakarta's "People's Daily" said in an editorial on the 20th that Premier Zhou Enlai's speech was "a very important event in the history of friendship between the peoples of Indonesia and China, these two neighboring countries," and it also strengthened the cause of peace in Asia and the world.

	The "People's Daily" emphasized that the most noteworthy thing was Premier Zhou Enlai's statement about being willing to cooperate with Indonesia and other countries on the basis of the Five Principles of Peaceful Coexistence. He believes that on the basis of these principles, the friendship between Indonesia and the People's Republic of China, as well as peace in Asia and the world, will be further strengthened. Premier Zhou Enlai also pointed out the important significance of Indonesia's attitude towards the issue of war or peace in Asia.

	The "People's Daily" called on the Indonesian government to make further efforts to oppose any aggressive attempts to split Asia and to consolidate the victory won by the forces of peace at the Geneva Conference.

### 印度尼西亚“人民日报”发表社论指出  周总理在印度尼西亚独立日发表的讲话是两国人民的友谊史上的非常重要的事

1954-08-22
第4版()
专栏：

    印度尼西亚“人民日报”发表社论指出
    周总理在印度尼西亚独立日发表的讲话是两国人民的友谊史上的非常重要的事
    【新华社雅加达二十一日电】雅加达报纸以显著地位刊载周恩来总理在印度尼西亚共和国驻华大使馆举行的庆祝印度尼西亚独立九周年纪念日招待会上的讲话。
    雅加达的“人民日报”二十日在社论中说，周恩来总理的讲话是“印度尼西亚和中国这两个邻近国家的人民的友谊史上的非常重要的事”，它同时也加强了亚洲和世界的和平事业。
      “人民日报”强调说，最值得注意的是周恩来总理的关于愿以五项指导原则为基础和印度尼西亚及其他国家合作的声明。他相信，在这些原则的基础上，印度尼西亚和中华人民共和国之间的友谊以及亚洲和世界的和平将能得到进一步的加强。周恩来总理还指出了印度尼西亚对战争或和平的问题的态度在亚洲的重要意义。
    “人民日报”要求印度尼西亚政府进一步努力，以反对任何想分裂亚洲的侵略性的企图，并巩固和平力量在日内瓦会议上所获得的胜利。
