Title: Indochina International Supervisory and Control Commission Advance Delegation Concludes Work, International Supervisory and Control Commissions Established Separately in Vietnam, etc.
Author:
Date: 1954-08-28
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, New Delhi, August 26th: According to the Indo-New Agency, the advance delegation of the Indochina International Supervisory and Control Commission has completed its work of establishing International Supervisory and Control Commissions in Vietnam, Laos, and Cambodia. The head of the advance delegation, the Indian representative Dutt, returned to New Delhi on the 23rd. The Polish representative Ogrodzinski and the Canadian representative McDonnell, who accompanied him, remained in Hanoi to serve as commissioners of their respective countries on the International Supervisory and Control Commission in Vietnam. The Indian representative Kosla is currently still in Hanoi, temporarily replacing Dutt as the chairman of the International Supervisory and Control Commission in Vietnam. It is reported that Kosla will go to Vientiane to serve as the chairman of the International Supervisory and Control Commission in Laos. The position of chairman of the International Supervisory and Control Commission in Vietnam will be taken over by another Indian representative, Desai. The chairman of the International Supervisory and Control Commission in Cambodia will be the Indian representative Parthasarathi.
	During its half-month of work in Indochina, the advance delegation visited Phnom Penh, Hanoi, Vientiane, and Saigon, and met with relevant personnel from both sides of the three countries.
	Xinhua News Agency, August 26th: According to the Vietnam News Agency, North Vietnam, August 25th: The Indian and Polish representatives of the International Supervisory and Control Commission in Vietnam, accompanied by personnel from the Vietnam People's Army, flew to Lao Cai on the Sino-Vietnamese border on August 23rd to inspect the airport and the offices of the permanent team of the International Supervisory and Control Commission to be established there.
	The representatives of the International Supervisory and Control Commission in Vietnam were warmly welcomed by representatives of local governments, armies, mass organizations, and residents. Before leaving for Hanoi, the representatives expressed their gratitude for the hospitality and assistance of the local government.

### 印度支那国际监督和监察委员会先遣代表团结束工作  越南等三国分别成立国际监督和监察委员会

1954-08-28
第4版()
专栏：

    印度支那国际监督和监察委员会先遣代表团结束工作
    越南等三国分别成立国际监督和监察委员会
    【新华社新德里二十六日电】据印新处报道：印度支那国际监督和监察委员会先遣代表团已完成分别在越南、老挝和柬埔寨三国建立国际监督和监察委员会的工作。先遣代表团团长、印度代表杜特已在二十三日回到新德里。同去的波兰代表奥格罗京斯基和加拿大代表麦克唐奈留在河内，分别担任二国在越南国际监督和监察委员会的委员。印度代表科斯拉现仍留在河内，暂代杜特担任越南国际监督和监察委员会的主席。消息说，科斯拉将去万象担任老挝国际监督和监察委员会主席。越南国际监督和监察委员会主席一职将由另一印度代表德赛担任。柬埔寨国际监督和监察委员会主席将由印度代表帕塔萨拉蒂担任。
    先遣代表团在印度支那工作半月期间，曾先后访问了金边、河内、万象和西贡，与三国双方有关人员会见。
   【新华社二十六日讯】据越南通讯社北越二十五日讯：越南国际监督和监察委员会的印度和波兰代表偕同越南人民军人员在八月二十三日飞往越中边界的老街，视察飞机场和将在那里建立的国际监督和监察委员会的固定小组的办公处。
    越南国际监督和监察委员会的代表受到当地政府、军队、群众团体的代表和居民的热烈欢迎。代表在动身回河内之前，对当地政府的招待和协助表示感谢。
