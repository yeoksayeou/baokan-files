Title: Mr. Attlee's Speech at the Banquet
Author:
Date: 1954-08-17
Page: 1

Mr. Prime Minister, Ladies and Gentlemen:

　　On behalf of the British Labour Party delegation, I would like to thank you for your kind words and friendly hospitality. All of us in the delegation are very pleased to visit your country and get to know you and your ministers.

　　We believe that the greatest need in the world today is friendly cooperation and mutual understanding between the peoples of all countries. The Labour Government, with me as Prime Minister, recognized the People's Government as the effective government of China. We earnestly hope that the Chinese people, after a long period of hardship, will have peace and the opportunity to improve the living standards of the masses.

　　The Labour Party has always believed that there should be the fullest cooperation on an equal basis between the peoples of Asia and elsewhere. We are proud that in the Commonwealth, we are equal partners with India, Pakistan, Ceylon, and countries whose residents are of European origin.

　　We ourselves believe in freedom and democracy, and we hope that others also have the right to arrange their lives in the way they think best. Recognizing the rights of the people of other countries is essential to maintaining peace. The alternative to war is peaceful coexistence.

　　Closer personal contacts and mutually beneficial trade are conducive to this goal.

　　We sincerely hope that our visit can help strengthen mutual understanding and renew and consolidate the long-standing friendship between the Chinese and British people.

　　I propose a toast to world peace, Sino-British friendship, and the health of Premier Zhou Enlai!

### 艾德礼先生在宴会上的讲话

1954-08-17
第1版()
专栏：

    艾德礼先生在宴会上的讲话总理先生、诸位女士、诸位先生：
      我愿意代表英国工党代表团，感谢您的亲切的发言和对我们的友好的招待。我们全体代表都因为来到贵国访问并认识了您和您的部长们，而感到非常高兴。
    我们相信，今天世界上最大的需要是各国人民之间的友好合作和相互了解。由我担任首相的工党政府承认了人民政府是中国的有效政府。我们热望中国人民在经过长久的艰难困苦以后，应该得到和平，并享有提高群众生活水平的机会。
    工党一贯相信亚洲和其他各地人民之间应该在平等的基础上进行最充分的合作。我们感到骄傲的是，在英联邦中，我们和印度、巴基斯坦、锡兰以及居民的原籍为欧洲人的国家都是平等的伙伴。
    我们自己是相信自由与民主的，我们希望别人也有权利按照他们自己认为最好的方式来安排他们的生活。承认其他国家的人民的权利是维持和平所必需的。代替战争的方法是和平共处。
    更密切的个人联系和互利的贸易，是有助于这个目的的。
    我们诚恳地希望，我们的访问可以有助于加强相互的了解，并使中英人民之间长久存在的友谊得到更新和巩固。
    我建议为世界和平、中英友谊和周恩来总理的健康而干杯！
