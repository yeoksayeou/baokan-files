Title: The General Headquarters of the Khmer Resistance Army and the General Headquarters of the Vietnamese People's Army Send a Joint Military Delegation to Cambodia for Negotiations
Author:
Date: 1954-08-13
Page: 4

　　Xinhua News Agency, Hanoi, August 12th: The Vietnam Central Joint Ceasefire Committee issued a communique in Zhongjia on the 11th, stating that the General Headquarters of the Khmer Resistance Army and the General Headquarters of the Vietnamese People's Army have sent a joint military delegation to Cambodia to confer with the General Headquarters of the Royal Cambodian Army. The communique is as follows:
    "In order to implement the Agreement on the Cessation of Hostilities in Cambodia signed in Geneva between representatives of the General Headquarters of the Khmer Resistance Army and the Command of Vietnamese People's Volunteers on the one hand, and representatives of the General Headquarters of the Royal Cambodian Army on the other, the General Headquarters of the Vietnamese People's Army and the General Headquarters of the Khmer Resistance Army have decided to send a military delegation to confer with the General Headquarters of the Royal Cambodian Army on the implementation of the Agreement on the Cessation of Hostilities in Cambodia and the establishment of a Joint Ceasefire Committee in this country. After notifying the General Headquarters of the Royal Cambodian Army and obtaining its formal agreement, the General Command of the French Union Forces in Indochina has decided to provide the necessary tools for the aforementioned military delegation to reach its destination and complete its work quickly. This delegation will leave Gia Lam Airport for Cambodia on August 11, 1954."

### 高棉抗战军队总司令部和越南人民军总司令部  派出联合军事代表团去柬埔寨进行谈判

1954-08-13
第4版()
专栏：

　　高棉抗战军队总司令部和越南人民军总司令部
    派出联合军事代表团去柬埔寨进行谈判
    【新华社十二日讯】越南通讯社北越讯：越南中央联合停战委员会十一日在中稼发表公报说，高棉抗战军队总司令部和越南人民军总司令部已派出一个联合军事代表团前往柬埔寨，同柬埔寨王国军队总司令部会商。公报如下：
    “为了履行高棉抗战军队总司令部和越南人民志愿人员指挥部一方代表同柬埔寨王国军队总司令部一方代表之间在日内瓦签订的柬埔寨停止敌对行动协定，越南人民军总司令部和高棉抗战军队总司令部已决定派遣一个军事代表团，就履行柬埔寨停止敌对行动协定和在这个国家内建立一个联合停战委员会的问题，同柬埔寨王国军队总司令部会商。在通知了柬埔寨王国军队总司令部并获得了它的正式同意以后，在印度支那的法兰西联邦部队总司令部已决定为上述军事代表团提供必要的工具，使它达到目的地并迅速完成它的工作。这个代表团将在一九五四年八月十一日离开嘉林机场前往柬埔寨。”
