Title: Directly Subordinate Organs and Troops of the East China Military Region, Air Force, and Public Security Forces Elect Deputies to the National People's Congress

Author:
Date: 1954-08-20
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, 19th: The East China Military Region of the Chinese People's Liberation Army held a conference of military representatives from the 16th to the 18th. A total of 323 representatives attended the meeting. Among the representatives were cadres, soldiers, heroes, models, meritorious individuals, and ten female comrades.

The meeting heard a report on the election work of the East China Military Region troops and a report on the draft Constitution of the People's Republic of China. All the representatives attending the meeting had a heated discussion on these reports.

The representatives attending the meeting elected Liu Bocheng, Su Yu, Xu Shiyou, Wang Hongkun, Xu Liqing, Zhou Wenjiang (combat hero), Wang Zhaocai (combat hero), and Zhang Ming (combat hero) as deputies to the National People's Congress by secret ballot.

Tang Liang, Deputy Political Commissar of the East China Military Region, congratulated the elected representatives on behalf of the East China Military Region Election Committee. Then, Xu Shiyou delivered a reply speech on behalf of the elected representatives. He said that we must accept everyone's entrustment, attend the National People's Congress meeting with the opinions of all personnel of the East China Military Region, discuss and formulate the Constitution of the People's Republic of China, and elect the people we are most satisfied with to consolidate and strengthen the people's democratic dictatorship in order to ensure the smooth progress of the country's socialist construction.

The meeting unanimously passed a congratulatory telegram to Chairman Mao Zedong and a congratulatory telegram to the National People's Congress meeting. The meeting unanimously passed a resolution endorsing the draft Constitution of the People's Republic of China. The resolution stated: The East China Military Region troops are stationed on the coastal defense front line, facing Taiwan and Kinmen, and bear a major responsibility for liberating our territory Taiwan and other enemy-occupied islands in the southeast coast. All commanders and combatants of our East China Military Region must be mobilized, raise vigilance a hundredfold, strengthen military preparedness, strengthen political and military training, accelerate the modernization of national defense, and strive to liberate Taiwan, defend the motherland, thoroughly eliminate the Chiang Kai-shek traitorous clique, and finally complete the people's liberation cause.

Xinhua News Agency, 19th: The Chinese People's Liberation Army Air Force held a grand conference of military representatives from August 17th to 18th. A total of 133 representatives attended the meeting.

Wang Huiqu, Chairman of the Air Force Election Committee, delivered a report on the election work at the meeting. Wu Faxian, Deputy Political Commissar of the Air Force, delivered a report on the draft Constitution of the People's Republic of China. After a heated discussion, the conference unanimously passed a resolution endorsing the draft Constitution of the People's Republic of China.

All the representatives listened to a speech by Wang Bingzhang, Deputy Commander of the Air Force, on the current tasks. Wang Bingzhang called on all commanders and combatants of the Air Force to strengthen flight and combat training, learn how to coordinate joint operations with various services, and strive to liberate our territory Taiwan.

All the representatives elected Liu Yalou, Liu Shanben, and Zheng Changhua (Air Force combat hero) as deputies to the National People's Congress by secret ballot.

Zheng Changhua spoke on behalf of the elected representatives. He said: We vow to strengthen flight and combat training with a hundredfold effort, actively improve the political, technical, and tactical level of the troops, resolutely defend the sacred and inviolable airspace of the motherland, and contribute everything to liberate Taiwan and defend the socialist construction of our country.

Xinhua News Agency, 19th: The conference of military representatives of the directly subordinate organs and troops of the Public Security Forces of the Chinese People's Liberation Army was held in early August. Sixty-three representatives attended the meeting.

All the representatives listened to a report on the draft Constitution of the People's Republic of China at the meeting and had a heated discussion. The meeting unanimously passed a resolution endorsing the draft Constitution of the People's Republic of China.

The meeting elected Li Tianhuan as a deputy to the National People's Congress by secret ballot. After being elected, Li Tianhuan spoke at the meeting, saying that he must be modest and prudent, study conscientiously, work hard, and resolutely fulfill the entrustment of all personnel. He said: Our public security forces must maintain a high degree of vigilance, continuously improve our skills in fighting against the enemy, and strive to consolidate the people's democratic regime, defend the motherland, and liberate Taiwan.

### 华东军区、空军、公安部队直属机关部队  分别选出全国人民代表大会代表

1954-08-20
第1版()
专栏：

    华东军区、空军、公安部队直属机关部队
    分别选出全国人民代表大会代表
    【新华社十九日讯】中国人民解放军华东军区在十六日到十八日举行军人代表大会会议。出席会议的代表共三百二十三人。代表中有干部、战士、英雄、模范、功臣和十位女同志。
    会议听了华东军区部队普选工作的报告和关于中华人民共和国宪法草案的报告。参加会议的全体代表就这些报告进行了热烈的讨论。
    参加会议的代表以无记名投票方式，选出刘伯承、粟裕、许世友、王宏坤、徐立清、周文江（战斗英雄）、王兆才（战斗英雄）、张明（战斗英雄）八人为全国人民代表大会代表。
    华东军区副政治委员唐亮代表华东军区选举委员会向当选的代表致贺。接着，许世友代表当选代表致答词。他说，我们一定接受大家的委托，带着华东军区全体人员的意见出席全国人民代表大会会议，讨论和制定中华人民共和国宪法，并选举我们最满意的人来巩固和加强人民民主专政，以保证祖国社会主义建设顺利进行。
    会议一致通过了向毛泽东主席致敬电文和给全国人民代表大会会议的贺电。会议一致通过了拥护中华人民共和国宪法草案的决议。决议中说：华东军区部队处在海防前线，面对着台湾、金门，对解放我国领土台湾和东南沿海其他敌占岛屿，负有重大的责任。我们华东军区全体指挥员、战斗员必须动员起来，百倍地提高警惕，加强军事戒备，加强政治、军事训练，加速国防现代化的建设，为解放台湾、保卫祖国、彻底消灭蒋介石卖国集团，最后完成人民解放事业而奋斗到底。
    【新华社十九日讯】中国人民解放军空军军人代表大会会议在八月十七日到十八日隆重举行。出席会议的代表共一百三十三人。
    空军选举委员会主席王辉球在会上作了选举工作的报告。空军副政治委员吴法宪作了中华人民共和国宪法草案的报告。大会经热烈讨论，一致通过了拥护中华人民共和国宪法草案的决议。
    全体代表听取了空军副司令员王秉璋关于当前任务的发言。王秉璋号召空军全体指挥员、战斗员加强飞行和战斗训练，学好协同各兵种的联合作战，为解放我国的领土台湾而奋斗。
    全体代表以无记名的投票方式，选出刘亚楼、刘善本、郑长华（空军战斗英雄）三人为全国人民代表大会代表。
    郑长华代表当选的代表讲话。他说：我们誓以百倍的努力，加强飞行、战斗训练，积极提高部队的政治、技术、战术水平，坚决保卫祖国神圣不可侵犯的领空，为解放台湾、保卫我国的社会主义建设而贡献出自己的一切。
    【新华社十九日讯】中国人民解放军公安部队直属机关、部队军人代表大会会议已在八月上旬举行。出席会议的有六十三名代表。
    全体代表在会上听了关于中华人民共和国宪法草案的报告，并进行了热烈的讨论。会议一致通过了拥护中华人民共和国宪法草案的决议。
    会议以无记名投票方式，选出李天焕为全国人民代表大会代表。李天焕当选后在会上讲话，他表示一定谦虚谨慎、认真学习、努力工作，坚决实现全体人员的委托。他说：我们公安部队要保持高度警惕，不断提高对敌斗争的技能，为巩固人民民主政权、保卫祖国、解放台湾而奋斗到底。
