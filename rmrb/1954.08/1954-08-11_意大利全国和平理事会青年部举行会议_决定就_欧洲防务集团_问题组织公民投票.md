Title: Italian National Peace Council Youth Department Holds Meeting, Decides to Organize Referendum on "European Defense Community" Issue
Author:
Date: 1954-08-11
Page: 4

　　Italian National Peace Council Youth Department Holds Meeting
    Decides to Organize Referendum on "European Defense Community" Issue
   [Xinhua News Agency, Rome, August 10th] According to TASS, Rome, August 9th: The Youth Department of the Italian National Peace Council recently held a meeting and decided to organize a national referendum on the "European Defense Community" issue.
    The Youth Department decided to hold a national referendum in order to allow young people to express their opinions on this issue and other issues related to European security. Then, the results of the referendum will be notified to members of parliament so that they can consider them before discussing the ratification of the "European Defense Community" treaty.

### 意大利全国和平理事会青年部举行会议  决定就“欧洲防务集团”问题组织公民投票

1954-08-11
第4版()
专栏：

　　意大利全国和平理事会青年部举行会议
    决定就“欧洲防务集团”问题组织公民投票
   【新华社十日讯】据塔斯社罗马九日讯：意大利全国和平理事会青年部最近举行会议，决定就“欧洲防务集团”问题组织全国公民投票。
    青年部决定举行全国公民投票，是为了让青年群众对这个问题和有关欧洲安全的其他问题表示意见，然后，将把公民投票的结果通知国会议员以便他们在讨论批准“欧洲防务集团”条约以前考虑。
