Title: Japanese Peace Delegation and Diet Member Delegation Leave Beijing for Home

Author: 
Date: 1954-08-05
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, August 4th—The Japanese Peace Delegation, led by Kenjuro Yanagida, and members Koichi Ohno, Minoru Shirasu, and the Japanese Diet Member Delegation, led by Shotaro Matsuura, Deputy Leader Shigeyoshi Matsumae, and thirteen members, who were invited by the Chinese People's Committee for World Peace and the Chinese People's Association for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries to visit our country, left Beijing for home by plane on the 4th.

### 日本和平代表团和国会议员代表团离京回国

1954-08-05
第4版()
专栏：

    日本和平代表团和国会议员代表团离京回国
   【新华社四日讯】应中国人民保卫世界和平委员会和中国人民对外文化协会邀请来我国访问的日本和平代表团团长柳田谦十郎、团员大野幸一、白水实等三人和日本国会议员代表团团长松浦周太郎、副团长松前重义及团员等十三人在四日乘飞机离京回国。
