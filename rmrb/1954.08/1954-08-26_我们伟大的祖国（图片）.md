Our Great Motherland
The People's Air Force vigorously supports the Huai River flood control work, giving great encouragement to the migrant workers participating in flood control. When the air force fighters constantly overcame the adverse weather and promptly airdropped flood control materials to various dangerous work sections of the Huai River, the migrant workers on the dike applauded and cheered.

### 我们伟大的祖国（图片）

1954-08-26
第1版()
专栏：

    我们伟大的祖国
    人民空军大力支援淮河防汛工作，给参加防汛的民工以极大的鼓舞。当空军战士不断克服恶劣的天气，及时将防汛器材空投到淮河各险工地段时，堤上民工鼓掌欢呼。
