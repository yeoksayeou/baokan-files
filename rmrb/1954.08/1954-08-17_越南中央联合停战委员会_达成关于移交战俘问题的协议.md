Title: Vietnam Central Joint Ceasefire Commission Reaches Agreement on the Transfer of Prisoners of War
Author:
Date: 1954-08-17
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, North Vietnam, August 16th: The two delegations of the Vietnam Central Joint Ceasefire Commission, after reaching two agreements on the establishment of a demilitarized zone at the provisional military demarcation line and on the extension of the deadline for the concentration of Chinese and Vietnamese armed forces, signed a third agreement on August 14 - an agreement on the transfer of prisoners of war. This agreement stipulates that both sides will hand over to each other complete lists of all prisoners of war awaiting transfer. The time and location of the prisoner of war transfer have also been decided.

On the 15th, three groups of liaison officers from the General Command of the Vietnam People's Army left Bach Mai Airport in North Vietnam for the Binh-Tri-Thien region of Central Vietnam, the Fifth Inter-zone in Central South Vietnam, and South Vietnam, to convey the above-mentioned three agreements reached by the Vietnam Central Joint Ceasefire Commission to the Vietnam People's Army Command there. These agreements were notified to the International Supervisory and Monitoring Commission on August 15.

From August 16, the Vietnam Central Joint Ceasefire Commission and its subordinate subcommittees will work at a new location - Phu Luu, North Vietnam.

### 越南中央联合停战委员会  达成关于移交战俘问题的协议

1954-08-17
第4版()
专栏：

    越南中央联合停战委员会
    达成关于移交战俘问题的协议
    【新华社十六日讯】据越南通讯社北越十六日讯：越南中央联合停战委员会中的双方代表团，在达成了关于在临时军事分界线建立非军事区问题和关于延长中越武装部队的集结期限问题的两项协议以后，已在八月十四日签订了第三项协议——关于移交战俘问题的协议。这项协议规定，双方将交给对方关于有待移交的各种战俘的全部名单。移交战俘的时间和地点也已经决定。
    十五日，三批越南人民军总司令部的联络官离开北越白梅机场，前往中越平治天地区、中越南部第五联区和南越，向那里的越南人民军司令部传达越南中央联合停战委员会所达成的上述三项协议。这些协议已在八月十五日通知了国际监督和监察委员会。
    从八月十六日起，越南中央联合停战委员会和所属的小组委员会将在一个新的地点——北越富流进行工作。
