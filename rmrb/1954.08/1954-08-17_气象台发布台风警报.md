Title: Weather Bureau Issues Typhoon Warning
Author: 
Date: 1954-08-17
Page: 2

    Weather Bureau Issues Typhoon Warning
    The Central Meteorological Observatory issued a typhoon warning at 6:00 PM on the 16th:
    At 2:00 PM on the 16th, the center of the typhoon near Okinawa was located 200 kilometers north of Okinawa (i.e., 28 degrees north latitude, 127.4 degrees east longitude). Currently, the typhoon center is moving due north and will gradually turn to the north-northeast. It is expected that in 24 hours (i.e., 2:00 PM on the 17th), the typhoon center will reach the sea area 200 kilometers southwest of Kyushu, Japan (i.e., 31 degrees north latitude, 128.2 degrees east longitude). In the next 24 hours, the wind force in the southern Yellow Sea and the East China Sea will reach level 6 to 9. The wind direction in the Yellow Sea will be northeast or north, and the wind direction in the East China Sea will be northwest or north. The wind force at the Yangtze River estuary and the Qiantang River estuary may reach level 6 to 7, with a northerly wind direction. In the coastal areas from Haizhou, Jiangsu, to Wenzhou, Zhejiang, the wind force may reach level 5 to 6, with a northerly wind direction. (According to Xinhua News Agency)

### 气象台发布台风警报

1954-08-17
第2版()
专栏：

    气象台发布台风警报
    中央气象台十六日下午六时发布台风警报：
    十六日下午二时，冲绳岛附近的台风中心位置在冲绳岛以北二百公里的地方（即北纬二十八度，东经一百二十七点四度）。目前，台风中心正向正北方向移动，并将逐渐转北北东方向移动。预计二十四小时（即十七日下午二时）后，台风中心可到日本九州岛西南二百公里的海面上（即北纬三十一度，东经一百二十八点二度）。未来二十四小时内，我国黄海南部和东海的风力，将达到六至九级，黄海风向东北或北，东海风向西北或北，长江口和钱塘江口风力可达六至七级，风向偏北。自江苏海州到浙江温州之间的沿海地区，风力可达五至六级，风向偏北。　（据新华社讯）
