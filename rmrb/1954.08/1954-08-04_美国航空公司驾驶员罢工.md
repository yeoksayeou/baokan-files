Title: American Airlines Pilots Strike
Author:
Date: 1954-08-04
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, New York, August 3rd: The 1,200 pilots of "American Airlines," the largest airline in the United States, began a strike on July 31 to protest the employer's disregard for the safety of flight personnel and the unreasonable requirement that pilots perform non-stop transcontinental flights exceeding eight hours (U.S. flight regulations originally stipulated that non-stop flights should not exceed eight hours to ensure the safety of flight personnel). According to reports from American news agencies, as of August 2, the strike was still in progress, causing a complete shutdown of the company's flight schedules.

### 美国航空公司驾驶员罢工

1954-08-04
第4版()
专栏：

    美国航空公司驾驶员罢工
    【新华社三日讯】纽约消息：美国最大的航空公司“美洲航空公司”一千二百名驾驶员在七月三十一日开始举行罢工，抗议资方漠视驾驶人员的安全，无理地规定驾驶员必需执行时间超过八小时的横渡大陆的不着陆飞行工作（美国飞行规例本来规定不着陆的飞行不得超过八小时，以保证飞行人员的安全）。据美国通讯社的消息说，到八月二日，罢工仍在进行中，并且使公司的飞行班次完全停顿。
