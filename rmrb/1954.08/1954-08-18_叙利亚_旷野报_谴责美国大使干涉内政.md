Title: Syrian "Al Ayyam" Newspaper Condemns US Ambassador for Interfering in Internal Affairs
Author:
Date: 1954-08-18
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency, Damascus, 17th: U.S. Ambassador to Syria James Moose visited the Syrian Foreign Ministry on the 16th, and he "expressed surprise" that a delegation from the People's Republic of China would be participating in the upcoming Damascus International Fair.

The "Al Ayyam" newspaper on the 16th strongly condemned this diplomatic contact by the U.S. Ambassador, considering it interference in Syria's internal affairs.

### 叙利亚“旷野报”  谴责美国大使干涉内政

1954-08-18
第4版()
专栏：

    叙利亚“旷野报”
    谴责美国大使干涉内政
   【新华社十七日讯】大马士革消息：美国驻叙利亚大使詹姆斯·穆斯十六日拜访了叙利亚外交部，他对中华人民共和国代表团要参加即将开幕的大马士革国际博览会“表示惊奇”。
    “旷野报”十六日严厉谴责美国大使的这种外交接触，认为这是对叙利亚内政的干涉。
