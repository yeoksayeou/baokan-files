Title: Our Great Motherland (Picture)
Author:
Date: 1954-08-18
Page: 1

Our great motherland
After the summer harvest, state-owned farms, agricultural producers' cooperatives, and the vast number of individual farmers actively deliver public grain and sell surplus grain to the state. The picture shows a large fleet of vehicles from the "May 1st" Farm in Shandong transporting dried wheat to the state granary.

### 我们伟大的祖国(图片）

1954-08-18
第1版()
专栏：

    我们伟大的祖国
    国营农场、农业生产合作社和广大个体农民在夏收后，积极交纳公粮，并把余粮卖给国家。图为山东“五一”农场的大车队把晒干的小麦送到国家粮库去。
