Title: Moscow and Berlin Democratic Sector Newspapers Publish Joint Declaration on Liberating Taiwan
Author:
Date: 1954-08-26
Page: 1

    Moscow and Berlin Democratic Sector Newspapers
    Publish Joint Declaration on Liberating Taiwan
   Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, August 24th: "Pravda," "Izvestia," "Trud," and other Moscow newspapers all published the "Joint Declaration of Democratic Parties and People's Organizations of the People's Republic of China for the Liberation of Taiwan" on the 24th.
   Xinhua News Agency, Berlin: Berlin Democratic Sector newspapers all published excerpts from the Joint Declaration of Democratic Parties and People's Organizations of our country for the Liberation of Taiwan on the 24th.

### 莫斯科和柏林民主区各报  刊载我国解放台湾联合宣言

1954-08-26
第1版()
专栏：

    莫斯科和柏林民主区各报
    刊载我国解放台湾联合宣言
   【新华社莫斯科二十四日电】“真理报”、“消息报”、“劳动报”和莫斯科其他报纸，二十四日都刊载了“中华人民共和国各民主党派各人民团体为解放台湾联合宣言”。
   【新华社柏林讯】柏林民主区各报二十四日都登载了我国各民主党派各人民团体为解放台湾联合宣言的摘要。
