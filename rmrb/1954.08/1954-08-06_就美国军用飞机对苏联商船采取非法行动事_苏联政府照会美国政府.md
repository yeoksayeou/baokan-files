Title: On the Illegal Actions of American Military Aircraft Against Soviet Merchant Ships: Soviet Government Note to the U.S. Government
Author: 
Date: 1954-08-06
Page: 4

(Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, August 5th) TASS Moscow, August 5th: The newspaper published the Soviet government's note to the U.S. government regarding the illegal actions taken by American military aircraft against Soviet merchant ships.

On August 3rd, the Soviet Ministry of Foreign Affairs delivered a note to the U.S. Embassy in Moscow. The full text of the note is as follows:

The Union of Soviet Socialist Republics deems it necessary to make the following statement to the Government of the United States of America:

In view of the fact that the Soviet tanker "Tuapse" was illegally detained by a warship in the area of Taiwan Island, in waters controlled by the U.S. Navy, we cannot but draw attention to the fact that U.S. military aircraft have long been systematically circling over Soviet merchant ships navigating in international waters near Taiwan Island. These aircraft often fly very low, approaching Soviet ships to a dangerous extent, sometimes circling above them for as long as an hour.

These provocative actions by U.S. military aircraft clearly violate the freedom of navigation of merchant ships on the high seas and demonstrate the complete disregard of universally recognized norms of international law by the U.S. military authorities.

At the same time, it is quite clear that the purpose of such actions by U.S. military aircraft is also to signal naval vessels to attack Soviet merchant ships. The circumstances of the hijacking of the Soviet tanker "Tuapse" on June 23 in the Taiwan Island area particularly prove this point.

To illustrate the planned and illegal actions of U.S. military aircraft disrupting the navigation of Soviet merchant ships on the high seas, the following recent incidents occurring in the Taiwan Island area can be cited:

    1. On June 11, at 13:03 hours, at 14 degrees 27 minutes North latitude, 115 degrees 27 minutes East longitude in the South China Sea, and at 15:05 hours at 14 degrees 40 minutes North latitude, 115 degrees 38 minutes East longitude, a U.S. military aircraft circled low over the Soviet ship "Mikhail Kutuzov" twice on each occasion. On June 12, at 16 degrees 29 minutes North latitude, 117 degrees 29 minutes East longitude, a U.S. aircraft circled over the same ship several times.

    2. On June 14, at 21 degrees 16 minutes North latitude, 121 degrees 44 minutes East longitude, east-northeast of Taiwan Island, two U.S. military aircraft circled over the Soviet ship "Mikhail Kutuzov" for one hour. The altitude of these two aircraft ranged from 60 meters to 400 meters.

    3. On June 16, at 18:57 hours, at 25 degrees 0 minutes North latitude, 123 degrees 49 minutes East longitude, northeast of Taiwan Island, a U.S. aircraft circled over the Soviet ship "Kemerovo," flying at an altitude of 30 meters.

    4. On June 23, at 10:00 hours, at 8 degrees 0 minutes North latitude, 109 degrees 14 minutes East longitude in the South China Sea, a U.S. aircraft circled very low over the Soviet tanker "Leningrad" several times. On June 24, at 11:30 hours, at 11 degrees 53 minutes North latitude, 112 degrees 58 minutes East longitude, a U.S. aircraft circled over the same ship seven times, flying at an altitude of 60 meters.

    5. On June 24, at 05:30 hours, at 24 degrees 30 minutes North latitude, 127 degrees 0 minutes East longitude in the Taiwan Island area; at 10:50 hours, at 24 degrees 48 minutes North latitude, 127 degrees 05 minutes East longitude, a U.S. aircraft circled very low over the Soviet tanker "Apsheron" several times.

Such incidents of U.S. military aircraft circling over Soviet merchant ships also occur in the seas of the Far East and other areas of the Pacific Ocean.

The Soviet Government calls the attention of the U.S. Government to the fact that such illegal actions by U.S. military aircraft against Soviet merchant ships on the high seas, especially near Taiwan Island, are impermissible, and the Soviet Government hopes that the U.S. Government will take the necessary measures to stop such infringements by U.S. military aircraft on the freedom of navigation of merchant ships on the high seas.

### 就美国军用飞机对苏联商船采取非法行动事  苏联政府照会美国政府

1954-08-06
第4版()
专栏：

    就美国军用飞机对苏联商船采取非法行动事
    苏联政府照会美国政府
      【新华社五日讯】塔斯社莫斯科五日讯：报纸刊载了苏联政府就美国军用飞机对苏联商船采取非法行动致美国政府的照会。
    八月三日，苏联外交部以照会一份交给驻莫斯科的美国大使馆，照会全文如下：
    苏维埃社会主义共和国联盟认为有必要对美利坚合众国政府作如下声明：
    鉴于苏联油船“图阿普斯号”在台湾岛地区，美国海军所控制的海面上被一艘军舰非法扣留，我们不能不提请注意这一事实，即美国军用飞机长期以来一直在有计划地在航行在台湾岛地区公海上的苏联商船上空盘旋。这些飞机往往飞得很低，与苏联船只距离之近到了危险的程度，有时在它们的上空盘旋一小时之久。
    美国军用飞机的这种挑衅行动显然侵犯了商船在公海上航行的自由，并证明了美国军事当局完全无视公认的国际法准则。
    同时，十分清楚，美国军用飞机采取这种行动的目的也是为发出信号，以便海军舰只攻击苏联商船。苏联油船“图阿普斯号”六月二十三日在台湾岛地区被劫走的情形特别证明了这一点。
    为了说明美国军用飞机这种扰乱苏联商船在公海航行的有计划的非法行动，可以举出下面这些最近在台湾岛地区发生的事件。
      一、六月十一日十三时零三分，在南中国海北纬十四度二十七分、东经一百一十五度二十七分的地方，和十五时零五分在北纬十四度四十分、东经一百一十五度三十八分的地方，一架美国军用飞机曾分别在苏联船只“米哈依尔·库图佐夫号”上空低空盘旋两周。六月十二日，在北纬十六度二十九分、东经一百一十七度二十九分的地方，一架美国飞机在同一船只上空盘旋数周。
    二、六月十四日，在台湾岛东南北纬二十一度十六分、东经一百二十一度四十四分的地方，两架美国军用飞机在苏联船只“米哈依尔·库图佐夫号”上空盘旋一小时之久。这两架飞机的飞行高度在六十公尺到四百公尺之间。
    三、六月十六日十八时五十七分，在台湾岛以东北纬二十五度零分、东经一百二十三度四十九分的地方，一架美国飞机在苏联船只“凯梅罗夫号”上空盘旋，这架飞机飞行高度为三十公尺。
    四、六月二十三日十时，在南中国海北纬八度零分、东经一百零九度十四分的地方，一架美国飞机在苏联油船“列宁格勒号”上空很低的地方盘旋数周。六月二十四日十一时三十分，在北纬十一度五十三分、东经一百一十二度五十八分的地方，一架美国飞机在同一船只上空盘旋七周，飞行高度为六十公尺。
    五、六月二十四日五时三十分，在台湾岛地区北纬二十四度三十分、东经一百二十七度零分的地方；十时五十分，在北纬二十四度四十八分、东经一百二十七度零五分的地方，一架美国飞机在苏联油船“阿普塞伦号”很低的上空盘旋数周。
    在远东诸海和太平洋的其他地区也发生这种美国军用飞机在苏联商船上空盘旋的事件。
    苏联政府请美国政府注意，美国军用飞机对在公海上、特别是在台湾岛附近行驶的苏联商船的这种非法行为是不能容许的，苏联政府希望美国政府采取必要的措施来停止美国军用飞机的这种侵犯商船在公海上航行自由的行为。
