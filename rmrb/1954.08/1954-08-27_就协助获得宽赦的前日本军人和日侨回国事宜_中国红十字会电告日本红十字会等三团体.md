Title: On Assisting the Repatriation of Pardoned Former Japanese Soldiers and Japanese Residents: Chinese Red Cross Society Cables to Three Japanese Organizations

Author: 
Date: 1954-08-27
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, August 26th:

    The Chinese Red Cross Society, in order to assist the repatriation of 417 pardoned former Japanese soldiers, including Nishii Kenichi, and individual Japanese residents who have applied to return to their country, cabled the Japanese Red Cross Society, the Japan-China Friendship Association, and the Liaison Office of the Japan Peace Liaison Council on August 26th, requesting them to send a ship between September 15th and 20th to transport the aforementioned former Japanese soldiers and Japanese residents back to Japan.

### 就协助获得宽赦的前日本军人和日侨回国事宜  中国红十字会电告日本红十字会等三团体

1954-08-27
第4版()
专栏：

    就协助获得宽赦的前日本军人和日侨回国事宜
    中国红十字会电告日本红十字会等三团体
    【新华社二十六日讯】中国红十字会为协助获得宽赦的西井建一等前日本军人四百一十七名以及个别申请回国的日本侨民回国，已于八月二十六日电告日本红十字会、日中友好协会和日本和平联络会三团体联络事务局，于九月十五日到二十日之间派船前来，将上述前日本军人和日本侨民接回日本。
