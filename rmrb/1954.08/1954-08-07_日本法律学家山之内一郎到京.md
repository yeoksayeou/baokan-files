Title: Japanese Legal Scholar Ichiro Yamanouchi Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1954-08-07
Page: 4

    Japanese Legal Scholar Ichiro Yamanouchi Arrives in Beijing
    (Xinhua News Agency, 5th) Professor Ichiro Yamanouchi, a member of the Japanese Peace Delegation and a renowned Japanese legal scholar, arrived in Beijing by plane on the 5th at the invitation of the Chinese People's Committee for World Peace, the Chinese People's Association for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries, and the China University of Political Science and Law.

### 日本法律学家山之内一郎到京

1954-08-07
第4版()
专栏：

    日本法律学家山之内一郎到京
    【新华社五日讯】应中国人民保卫世界和平委员会、中国人民对外文化协会及中国政治法律学会邀请来我国访问的日本和平代表团团员、日本著名法律学家山之内一郎教授在五日乘飞机到达北京。
