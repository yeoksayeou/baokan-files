Title: Lao Patriotic Forces Cease Fire in All Battlefields Starting August 6th
Author:
Date: 1954-08-08
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Vientiane, Laos, July 7th: The Lao Patriotic Government's Ministry of National Defense and the Military Headquarters of the Vietnamese People's Volunteer Soldiers in Laos issued a joint communique on the 6th, stating that firing had ceased since 7:00 AM on August 6th. The full text of the communique is as follows:

    In order to faithfully implement the armistice agreement signed at the Geneva Conference, all communication and telecommunications tools have been mobilized to transmit the ceasefire order of the Lao Patriotic Government's Ministry of National Defense and the Military Headquarters of the Vietnamese People's Volunteer Soldiers in Laos.

    Since 7:00 AM on August 6th, all combat units of the Lao People's Liberation Army, including regular and local forces, militias and guerrillas, as well as Vietnamese People's Volunteer Soldiers, have simultaneously implemented a ceasefire on all battlefields in Laos.

### 寮国抗战部队八月六日起在各战场停火

1954-08-08
第1版()
专栏：

    寮国抗战部队八月六日起在各战场停火
    【新华社七日讯】越南通讯社老挝七日讯：寮国抗战政府国防部和在老挝的越南人民志愿人员军事指挥部六日发表联合公报说，自从八月六日上午七时起已经停止射击。公报全文如下：
    为了忠实执行在日内瓦会议上签订的停战协定，一切联络工具和电信工具都已经被动员起来传达寮国抗战政府国防部和在老挝的越南人民志愿人员军事指挥部的停火命令。
    自从八月六日上午七时起，寮国人民解放军的一切战斗部队，其中包括正规军和地方部队，民兵和游击队，以及越南人民志愿人员已同时在老挝各个战场上实行停火。
