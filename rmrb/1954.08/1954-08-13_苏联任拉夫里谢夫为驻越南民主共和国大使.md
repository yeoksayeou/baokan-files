Title: The Soviet Union Appoints Lavrishchev as Ambassador to the Democratic Republic of Vietnam
Author:
Date: 1954-08-13
Page: 1

    Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, August 12th: TASS Moscow, August 12th—The Presidium of the Supreme Soviet of the USSR has appointed Alexander Lavrishchev as the Soviet Union's Ambassador Extraordinary and Plenipotentiary to the Democratic Republic of Vietnam.

### 苏联任拉夫里谢夫为驻越南民主共和国大使

1954-08-13
第1版()
专栏：

    苏联任拉夫里谢夫为驻越南民主共和国大使
    【新华社十二日讯】塔斯社莫斯科十二日讯：苏联最高苏维埃主席团任命亚历山大·拉夫里谢夫为苏联驻越南民主共和国特命全权大使。
