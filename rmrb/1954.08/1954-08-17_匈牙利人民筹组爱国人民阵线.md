Title: Hungarian People Organize Patriotic People's Front
Author:
Date: 1954-08-17
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Prague, August 14th: The Hungarian News Agency reported on the 13th that representatives of various political parties and social, cultural, scientific, and religious organizations in Hungary held a meeting in Budapest. Rakosi, First Secretary of the Central Committee of the Hungarian Working People's Party, attended the meeting.

The meeting, after discussion, adopted a resolution on organizing the Patriotic People's Front. The resolution pointed out: In order to realize the great tasks of people's democracy, a new people's front with a broader foundation than the present one is needed. This new people's front will operate under the name of the Patriotic People's Front. The meeting also elected a preparatory committee responsible for drafting the program and regulations of the Patriotic People's Front, and for preparing for the National Congress of the Patriotic People's Front to be held in October this year.

### 匈牙利人民筹组爱国人民阵线

1954-08-17
第4版()
专栏：

    匈牙利人民筹组爱国人民阵线
   【新华社布拉格十四日电】据匈牙利通讯社十三日讯：匈牙利各政党及社会、文化、科学和教会团体的代表在布达佩斯举行了会议。匈牙利劳动人民党中央委员会第一书记拉科西参加了会议。
    会议经讨论后通过了关于组织爱国人民阵线的决议。决议指出：为了实现人民民主的伟大任务，需要一个比现在的基础更为广泛的新的人民阵线。这个新的人民阵线将以爱国人民阵线为名进行活动。会议并选出了一个筹备委员会，负责草拟爱国人民阵线的纲领和规章，并为将在今年十月间召开的爱国人民阵线全国代表大会进行筹备工作。
