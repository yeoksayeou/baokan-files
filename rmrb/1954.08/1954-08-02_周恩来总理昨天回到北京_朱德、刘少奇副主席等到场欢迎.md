Title: Premier Zhou Enlai Returns to Beijing Yesterday; Vice Chairmen Zhu De and Liu Shaoqi Welcome Him

Author:
Date: 1954-08-02
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, 1st Dispatch: Premier Zhou Enlai of the State Council and Minister of Foreign Affairs of the Central People's Government, the chief representative of the People's Republic of China delegation to the Geneva Conference, returned to Beijing by plane on the afternoon of the 1st after the conclusion of the Geneva Conference and visits to the German Democratic Republic, the Polish People's Republic, the Union of Soviet Socialist Republics, and the Mongolian People's Republic.

Accompanying him on the flight back to Beijing were Wang Jiaxiang, Vice Minister of Foreign Affairs; Wang Bingnan, Secretary-General of the Chinese delegation to the Geneva Conference; and advisors Shi Zhe, Qiao Guanhua, Chen Jiakang, and Huang Hua.

Welcoming him at the airport were: Vice Chairmen of the Central People's Government Zhu De, Liu Shaoqi, and Li Jishen; Secretary-General Lin Boqu; Vice Premiers of the State Council Dong Biwu, Guo Moruo, Huang Yanpei, and Deng Xiaoping; Vice Chairmen of the National Committee of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference (CPPCC) Shen Junru and Chen Shutong; Members of the Central People's Government Committee Li Lisan, Deng Zihui, Xu Teli, Ma Yinchu, Shen Yanbing, Cai Tingkai, Peng Zemin, Li Zhuchen, Zhang Bojun, Zhang Xiruo, Zhang Nanxian, and Long Yun; Members of the State Council Xie Juezai, Zeng Shan, Teng Daiyuan, Chen Shaoxian, Wang Kunlun, Luo Longji, Zhang Naiqi, Shao Lizi, and Huang Shaohong; Standing Committee members of the National Committee of the CPPCC Li Weihan, Yang Xiufeng, Liu Ningyi, Deng Yingchao, Liang Xi, Xu Guangping, Huang Qixiang, Xu Deheng, Jiang Nanxiang, Li Siguang, Li Dequan, Liao Chengzhi, and Wu Yaozong; Deputy Chief of General Staff of the Central People's Government People's Revolutionary Military Commission Zhang Zongxun; Deputy Directors of the General Political Department Fu Zhong, Xiao Hua, and Gan Siqi; Vice Presidents of the Supreme People's Court Zhang Zhirang and Zhang Su; Deputy Chief Procurators of the Supreme People's Procuratorate Gao Kelin; Vice Chairman of the State Planning Commission Jia Tuofu; Deputy Director of the Political and Legal Affairs Committee of the State Council Luo Ruiqing; Deputy Director of the Financial and Economic Committee Ye Jizhuang; Deputy Director of the Cultural and Educational Committee Xi Zhongxun; Deputy Directors of the People's Supervision Committee Pan Zhenya and Wang Han; Vice Ministers of Foreign Affairs Zhang Hanfu and Wu Xiuquan; Vice Mayors of Beijing Zhang Youyu and Wu Han; as well as heads of various commissions, ministries, committees, academies, administrations, and banks of the Central People's Government, senior generals of the Chinese People's Liberation Army, members of the National Committee of the CPPCC in Beijing, heads of units directly under the Central Committee of the CPC, leaders of various democratic parties and people's organizations, non-party figures, representatives of various ethnic minorities, responsible persons and staff members of organs under the Central, North China, and Beijing Municipal governments, responsible persons of Beijing universities, literary and art figures, and representatives of people from all walks of life in Beijing, totaling more than 6,000 people.

Diplomatic envoys from various countries to China who went to the airport to welcome him were: Ambassador of Burma U Hla Maung, Ambassador of India N. Raghavan, Ambassador of Sweden Wistrand, Ambassador of Romania Teodor Rudenco, Ambassador of Indonesia Mononutu, Ambassador of the German Democratic Republic Koenig, Ambassador of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea Choi Il, Minister of Denmark Gregers, Chargé d'Affaires of the United Kingdom Tuvilliam, Chargé d'Affaires ad interim of the Soviet Union Vasikov, Chargé d'Affaires ad interim of Pakistan Sultan Muhammad Khan, Chargé d'Affaires ad interim of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam Chou Liang, Chargé d'Affaires ad interim of Hungary Czetoirdai, Chargé d'Affaires ad interim of Bulgaria Grigorov, Chargé d'Affaires ad interim of Switzerland Trelu, Chargé d'Affaires ad interim of Mongolia Lobsangchultem, Chargé d'Affaires ad interim of Poland Gula, and First Secretary of the Embassy of the Czechoslovak Republic to China Hedlicka, as well as diplomatic officials from various countries' embassies in China.

After Premier Zhou Enlai alighted from the plane, he received flowers from members of the Beijing Young Pioneers. Prolonged applause and cheers erupted at the airport.

### 周恩来总理昨天回到北京  朱德、刘少奇副主席等到场欢迎

1954-08-02
第1版()
专栏：

    周恩来总理昨天回到北京
    朱德、刘少奇副主席等到场欢迎
    【新华社一日讯】出席日内瓦会议的中华人民共和国代表团首席代表、中央人民政府政务院总理兼外交部部长周恩来，在日内瓦会议结束，先后访问德意志民主共和国、波兰人民共和国、苏维埃社会主义共和国联盟、蒙古人民共和国后，已在一日下午乘飞机返抵北京。
    随同飞返北京的，有我外交部副部长王稼祥，我国出席日内瓦会议代表团秘书长王炳南，顾问师哲、乔冠华、陈家康、黄华等。
    前往机场欢迎的，有：中央人民政府副主席朱德、刘少奇、李济深，秘书长林伯渠，政务院副总理董必武、郭沫若、黄炎培、邓小平，中国人民政治协商会议全国委员会副主席沈钧儒、陈叔通，中央人民政府委员会委员李立三、邓子恢、徐特立、马寅初、沈雁冰、蔡廷锴、彭泽民、李烛尘、章伯钧、张奚若、张难先、龙云，政务院政务委员谢觉哉、曾山、滕代远、陈劭先、王昆仑、罗隆基、章乃器、邵力子、黄绍竑，人民政协全国委员会常务委员李维汉、杨秀峰、刘宁一、邓颖超、梁希、许广平、黄琪翔、许德珩、蒋南翔、李四光、李德全、廖承志、吴耀宗，中央人民政府人民革命军事委员会副总参谋长张宗逊，总政治部副主任傅钟、萧华、甘泗淇，最高人民法院副院长张志让、张苏，最高人民检察署副检察长高克林，国家计划委员会副主席贾拓夫，政务院政治法律委员会副主任罗瑞卿，财政经济委员会副主任叶季壮，文化教育委员会副主任习仲勋，人民监察委员会副主任潘震亚、王翰，外交部副部长章汉夫、伍修权，北京市人民政府副市长张友渔、吴晗，以及中央人民政府各委、部、会、院、署、行的负责人，中国人民解放军的高级将领，人民政协全国委员会在京委员，中共中央直属各单位和各民主党派、各人民团体的负责人，无党派人士，各少数民族的代表，中央、华北和北京市所属机关的负责人、工作人员，北京各高等学校的负责人、文学艺术界人士及北京市各界群众代表等共六千多人。
