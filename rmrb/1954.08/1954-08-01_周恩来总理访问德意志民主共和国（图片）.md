Title: Premier Zhou Enlai's Visit to the German Democratic Republic (Pictures)
Author: 
Date: 1954-08-01
Page: 5

    Premier Zhou Enlai's Visit to the German Democratic Republic
    Premier Zhou Enlai in Berlin with members of the Young Pioneers of the German Democratic Republic.
    Premier Zhou Enlai left Geneva on July 23rd and was invited to visit Berlin, the capital of the German Democratic Republic. The picture shows Premier Zhou Enlai and Premier Grotewohl of the German Democratic Republic (right) meeting cordially.
    Humboldt University of Berlin, German Democratic Republic, held a ceremony on the morning of July 25th to award Premier Zhou Enlai with an honorary doctorate in law. The picture shows Premier Zhou Enlai receiving the honorary doctorate certificate.
                    Xinhua News Agency Draft, Berlin Radio Telefax

### 周恩来总理访问德意志民主共和国（图片）

1954-08-01
第5版()
专栏：

    周恩来总理访问德意志民主共和国
    周恩来总理在柏林和德意志民主共和国的少年先锋队队员们。
    周恩来总理在七月二十三日离日内瓦，应邀访问德意志民主共和国首都柏林。图为周总理和德意志民主共和国总理格罗提渥（右）亲切地会见。
    德意志民主共和国柏林胡包特大学在七月二十五日上午举行仪式，授与周恩来总理以名誉法学博士学位。图为周恩来总理接受名誉法学博士学位证书。
                    新华社稿　柏林无线电传真
