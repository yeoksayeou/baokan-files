Title: China's Protest Statement Against US Aircraft Provocations Published to the Press by UN Secretariat
Author:
Date: 1954-08-01
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, New York, July 30th (TASS): The United Nations Secretariat has released to the press a telegram from the Ministry of Foreign Affairs of the People's Republic of China to the Secretary-General of the United Nations, dated July 29th. This telegram included the full text of the protest statement made by Zhang Hanfu, Vice Minister of Foreign Affairs of the People's Republic of China, to the United States government on July 27th regarding the intrusion of US aircraft into Chinese airspace and the downing of two Chinese patrol planes.

The telegram requested the Secretary-General to distribute the full text of this statement to the delegations of all UN member states except for the so-called "delegation" of the Kuomintang clique.

### 我国对美机挑衅的抗议声明  联合国秘书处已向报界发表

1954-08-01
第4版()
专栏：

    我国对美机挑衅的抗议声明  联合国秘书处已向报界发表
   【新华社三十一日讯】塔斯社纽约三十日讯：联合国秘书处向报界发表了中华人民共和国外交部七月二十九日致联合国秘书长的电报。这份电报包括中华人民共和国外交部副部长章汉夫七月二十七日为美国飞机侵入中国领空并击落两架中国巡逻机而向美国政府提出的抗议声明的全文。
    这个电报要求秘书长将这个声明全文分发给除国民党集团的所谓“代表团”以外的联合国各会员国代表团。
