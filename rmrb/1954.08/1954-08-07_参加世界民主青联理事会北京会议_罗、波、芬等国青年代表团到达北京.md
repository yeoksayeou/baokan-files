Title: Youth Delegations from Romania, Poland, Finland, and Other Countries Arrive in Beijing to Attend the World Federation of Democratic Youth Council Meeting

Author:
Date: 1954-08-07
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, August 5th: Youth representatives from various countries are arriving in Beijing one after another to attend the World Federation of Democratic Youth Council meeting in Beijing.

Those who arrived in Beijing by plane on the afternoon of the 5th included: World Federation of Democratic Youth Secretary Demosthenes Lobreau (Brazil), and the head of the Romanian delegation, Monte Dinu, and two members. Youth representatives from eight countries—Argentina, Austria, Bulgaria, Ecuador, Niger, Senegal, French Sudan, and Tunisia—also arrived by plane on the same day.

Prior to this, the Polish and Finnish delegations had arrived in Beijing. The Polish delegation, led by head Jerzy Feliksiak, consisted of four people (including World Federation of Democratic Youth Secretary Jan Klechchi). The Finnish delegation, led by head Joutnana, consisted of two people. They arrived in Beijing by plane and train on the 4th and 3rd afternoons respectively.

Representatives from organizations such as the All-China Youth Federation, the Central Committee of the China New Democratic Youth League, and the Beijing Municipal Youth Federation went to the airport and railway station to welcome these delegates.

### 参加世界民主青联理事会北京会议  罗、波、芬等国青年代表团到达北京

1954-08-07
第4版()
专栏：

    参加世界民主青联理事会北京会议
    罗、波、芬等国青年代表团到达北京
    【新华社五日讯】参加世界民主青年联盟理事会北京会议的各国青年代表，陆续到达北京。
    五日下午乘飞机到达北京的有：世界民主青年联盟书记德莫斯特内·洛勃（巴西）和罗马尼亚代表团团长蒙特·丁努及团员二人。同日乘飞机前来的还有阿根廷、奥地利、保加利亚、厄瓜多尔、尼日尔、塞内加尔、法属苏丹和突尼斯八个国家的青年代表。
    在这以前，波兰和芬兰代表团已先期抵京。波兰代表团在团长叶日·费利克夏克率领下一行四人
  （包括世界民主青年联盟书记扬·克莱什奇），芬兰代表团在团长约特南率领下一行二人，先后在四日和三日下午分别乘飞机和火车到达北京。
    前往机场和车站欢迎这些代表的有：中华全国民主青年联合会、中国新民主主义青年团中央委员会和北京市民主青年联合会等团体的代表。
