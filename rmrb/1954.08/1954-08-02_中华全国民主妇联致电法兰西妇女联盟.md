Title: All-China Women's Federation Sends a Telegram to the French Women's Union
Author:
Date: 1954-08-02
Page: 2

    The All-China Democratic Women's Federation Sends a Telegram to the French Women's Union
    The All-China Democratic Women's Federation sent a telegram of congratulations to the French Women's Union on the agreement reached on the Indochina peace issue. The telegram stated:
    Chinese women warmly welcome the agreement reached at the Geneva Conference on the ceasefire and restoration of peace in Indochina, and extend our heartfelt congratulations to the French women who have persisted in the struggle for peace in Indochina. We also hope that the women of China and France, as well as peace-loving people all over the world, will continue to work together to strive for the thorough implementation of the agreement and safeguard world peace.
                                    (Xinhua News Agency)

### 中华全国民主妇联致电法兰西妇女联盟

1954-08-02
第2版()
专栏：

    中华全国民主妇联致电法兰西妇女联盟
    中华全国民主妇女联合会为印度支那和平问题达成协议致电祝贺法兰西妇女联盟。电文说：
    中国妇女热烈欢迎日内瓦会议就印度支那停战和恢复和平问题达成协议，谨向坚持为争取印度支那和平而斗争的法国妇女致以衷心的祝贺。并望中法两国妇女以及全世界爱好和平的人民共同为争取协议的彻底实现，维护世界和平而继续努力。
                                    （新华社）
