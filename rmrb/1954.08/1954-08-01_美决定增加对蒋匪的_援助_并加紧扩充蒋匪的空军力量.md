Title: U.S. Decides to Increase "Aid" to the Chiang Kai-shek Gang and Intensifies Expansion of the Chiang Kai-shek Gang's Air Force
Author:
Date: 1954-08-01
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, 31st dispatch: New York message: According to American columnist Robert S. Allen, the U.S. authorities have decided to increase the "aid" to the Chiang Kai-shek remnant bandits entrenched in Taiwan from the current approximately 400 million US dollars per year to more than 500 million US dollars.
    U.S. Foreign Aid Administrator Harold Stassen, in private conversations, revealed the U.S. authorities' sinister plot to use the Chiang Kai-shek bandit army to create new tensions and carry out aggression in the Far East. According to Allen, Stassen believes the Indochina armistice is an "unstable truce." Stassen also said: In the near future, Southeast Asia will become the "most active area" for imperialist countries. When Republican Senator Dirksen asked what measures the United States was taking, Stassen replied: "One of the initial things we are doing is increasing military assistance to South Korea and Taiwan. We believe that the armies in both countries should be increased without delay." Stassen added that the puppet armies of South Korea and Taiwan are "two of our (U.S.) largest armies in Asia." Stassen's words blatantly reveal that the U.S. government intends to further use the Chiang Kai-shek remnant army as a tool of aggression.
    Xinhua News Agency, 31st dispatch: After the U.S. authorities deliberately created aircraft provocation incidents in the Hainan Island area, they are stepping up the expansion of the Taiwan Chiang Kai-shek Air Force to expand war activities against the Chinese mainland. A Reuters dispatch from Washington on the 30th reported that "authoritative sources" revealed that the United States will "soon" ship a batch of Sabre jet aircraft to the Taiwan Chiang Kai-shek Air Force. Reuters added that the United States is giving the Chiang Kai-shek bandits this batch of aircraft in order to "increase their (Chiang Kai-shek bandits') attacks on (China's) mainland shipping and islands under Communist control."
    According to a United Press report, a Chiang Kai-shek newspaper in Taiwan also revealed on the 29th that after the Hainan Island incident, the United States will further accelerate the shipment of its military materials to its servants in the Far East, and the United States will ship Sabre jet aircraft to the Chiang Kai-shek Air Force much earlier than scheduled.

### 美决定增加对蒋匪的“援助”  并加紧扩充蒋匪的空军力量

1954-08-01
第4版()
专栏：

    美决定增加对蒋匪的“援助”
    并加紧扩充蒋匪的空军力量
    【新华社三十一日讯】纽约消息：据美国专栏作家罗伯特·斯·艾伦报道，美国当局已决定把给予盘踞在台湾的蒋介石残匪的“援助”，从目前的每年约四亿美元增加到五亿美元以上。
    美国援外事务管理署署长史塔生在私下谈话中透露了美国当局的利用蒋匪军在远东制造新的紧张局势和进行侵略的罪恶阴谋。据艾伦说，史塔生认为印度支那停火是“不稳定的停战”。史塔生并说：在最近的将来，东南亚将成为帝国主义国家活动“最频繁的地区”。当共和党参议员德克森问及美国正在采取什么措施时，史塔生回答说：“我们正在做的初步工作之一是增加对南朝鲜和台湾的军事援助。我们认为两个国家中的军队应当毫不延迟地增加。”史塔生又说，南朝鲜和台湾的傀儡军队是“我们（美国）在亚洲方面两支最大的军队”。史塔生的话露骨地表明：美国政府打算进一步利用蒋匪残余军队作为侵略的工具。
   【新华社三十一日讯】美国当局在海南岛地区蓄意制造飞机的挑衅事件以后，正加紧扩充台湾蒋匪空军力量，以扩大对我国大陆的战争活动。路透社华盛顿三十日电报道，“权威人士”透露，美国“不久”就将把一批佩刀式喷气飞机启运交给台湾蒋匪空军。路透社并说，美国给予蒋匪这批飞机是为了“增加他们（蒋匪）对（中国）大陆航运和共产党控制下的岛屿的袭击”。
    另据合众社报道，台湾一家蒋匪报纸在二十九日也透露，在海南岛事件后，美国要更加加紧把它的军事物资运给它在远东的仆从们，美国要在比预定日期早得多的时候把佩刀式喷气飞机运交给蒋匪空军。
