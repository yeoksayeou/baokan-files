Title: Shenyang No. 1 Machine Tool Plant Actively Prepares to Produce New Model Lathes
Author:
Date: 1954-08-27
Page: 2

The civil engineering and construction work of the Shenyang No. 1 Machine Tool Plant’s reconstruction project is nearing completion, and preparations for producing new model machine tools are actively underway. Workers are participating in training courses to learn and master various modern machinery and equipment; management cadres and technical cadres are enthusiastically learning production technology and management methods for socialist enterprises. To date, the plant has sent dozens of technicians and skilled workers to steel plants, machinery plants, and scientific research institutions in Fushun, Benxi, Shanghai, Harbin, and other places to learn technology. Higher-level authorities have also assigned a group of university and secondary technical school graduates to the plant to participate in production preparation work.

### 沈阳第一机床厂积极准备生产新型车床

1954-08-27
第2版()
专栏：

    沈阳第一机床厂积极准备生产新型车床
    沈阳第一机床厂改建工程中的土木建筑工程已接近完工，正积极进行生产新型机床的准备工作。工人们参加了培训班，学习掌握各种现代化的机器设备；管理干部、技术干部，热心地学习着社会主义企业的生产技术管理方法。到现在为止，该厂已派出数十名技术人员和技术工人分赴抚顺、本溪、上海、哈尔滨等处的钢铁厂、机械厂和科学研究机关去学习技术。上级机关也为该厂派来一批大学和中等技术学校的毕业生，参加生产准备工作。
