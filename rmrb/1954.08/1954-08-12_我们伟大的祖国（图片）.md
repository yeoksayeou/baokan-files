Our Great Motherland
	In the south of our motherland, it is now the season of fruit harvest. State-owned commercial institutions and cooperatives in Guangzhou are purchasing large quantities of bananas, watermelons, pineapples, lychees, etc. from various producing areas to supply the needs of people all over the country. The Guangzhou Municipal Cooperative is purchasing a large number of watermelons and is preparing to transport them to various places.

### 我们伟大的祖国（图片）

1954-08-12
第1版()
专栏：

    我们伟大的祖国
    在祖国的南方，现在正是水果丰收的季节。广州市国营商业机构和合作社向各产区收购大量的香蕉、西瓜、菠萝和荔枝等，供应全国各地人民需要。广州市合作总社收购了大批西瓜，正准备运往各地。
