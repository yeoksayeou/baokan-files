Title: Madame Pandit Delivers Speech at Indonesian Parliament, Emphasizing the Importance of Establishing a Region of World Peace
Author:
Date: 1954-08-21
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Jakarta, August 20th: Madame Pandit delivered a speech at a special session of the Indonesian Parliament on the 18th, emphasizing the importance of establishing a region of world peace.

Madame Pandit said that one very necessary thing is that there should be some regions of peace in the world, in which no one thinks of using war to solve any problems, no matter how difficult and intractable these problems may seem. The more countries that are jointly determined to work towards peaceful solutions without fear or partiality, the wider the scope of these regions of peace will be, until they include the entire world.

She said: "If we hope for world peace, we cannot achieve it through war. All of us should at least have this determination."

### 潘迪特夫人在印度尼西亚国会发表演说  强调建立世界和平地区的重要意义

1954-08-21
第4版()
专栏：

    潘迪特夫人在印度尼西亚国会发表演说
    强调建立世界和平地区的重要意义
    【新华社雅加达二十日电】潘迪特夫人十八日在印度尼西亚国会特别会议上发表演说时强调建立世界和平地区的重要性。
    潘迪特夫人说，很必要的一件事就是：世界上应当有一些和平的地区，在这些地区中，没有人想到用战争的手段来解决任何问题，不论这些问题可能看来是多么困难和多么无法可施。共同决心一道在没有恐惧或偏袒的情况下致力于和平解决的国家越多，那么这些和平地区的范围就越广，一直到包括整个世界。
    她说：“如果我们盼望世界和平，我们不可能用战争来达到目的。我们全体至少应当有这样的决心。”
