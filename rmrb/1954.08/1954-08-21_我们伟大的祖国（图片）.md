Date: 1954-08-21

    Our Great Motherland
    Farmers in various regions are working hard to strengthen the field management of cotton in an effort to overfulfill this year's agricultural production tasks. This is a scene of members of the Majiacun Agricultural Production Cooperative in the Second District of Bin County, Shandong Province, pruning cotton in the fields.

### 我们伟大的祖国（图片）

1954-08-21
第1版()
专栏：

    我们伟大的祖国
    各地农民正在努力加强棉花的田间管理，争取超额完成今年的农业生产任务。这是山东省滨县二区马家村农业生产合作社的社员在田间进行棉花整枝。
