Title: Japanese Peace Representative Professor Hajime Matsuura Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1954-08-28
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, August 25th: Professor Hajime Matsuura, a Japanese peace representative, arrived in Beijing on August 25th at the invitation of the Chinese People's Committee for World Peace and the Chinese People's Association for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries.

### 日本和平代表松浦一教授到京

1954-08-28
第1版()
专栏：

    日本和平代表松浦一教授到京
   【新华社二十五日讯】应中国人民保卫世界和平委员会和中国人民对外文化协会邀请来我国访问的日本和平代表松浦一教授，在八月二十五日到达北京。
