Title: Visiting Delegations from Ningxia and Xinjiang Pastoral Areas Arrive in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1954-08-20
Page: 1

    Xinhua News Agency: The Ningxia Province pastoral area visiting delegation, consisting of eighty-six people, led by delegation leaders Chen Aierdenibaatu (Mongolian), Tawang Jiabu (Mongolian), and Yang Lisheng (Han), arrived in Beijing on the 10th.

    Xinhua News Agency: The Xinjiang pastoral area visiting delegation, consisting of one hundred and one people including Kazakh, Uyghur, Kyrgyz, Mongolian, Uzbek, and Tatar ethnic groups, led by delegation leader Ajiyoufu (Uyghur), deputy leaders Yang Lieguang (Han), and Tielimu Hamaiti (Kazakh), arrived in Beijing on the 16th to visit.

### 宁夏新疆两牧区参观团到京

1954-08-20
第1版()
专栏：

    宁夏新疆两牧区参观团到京
    据新华社讯：宁夏省牧区参观团一行八十六人，由团长陈爱尔德尼巴图（蒙族）、塔旺嘉布（蒙族）、杨力生（汉族）率领，于十日抵达北京。
    据新华社讯：新疆牧区参观团一行一百零一人包括哈萨克、维吾尔、柯尔克兹、蒙、乌兹别克、塔塔尔等民族成份，由团长阿吉由夫（维吾尔族）、副团长杨烈光（汉族）、铁里木哈买提（哈萨克族）率领，于十六日到达北京参观。
