Title: Vice Foreign Minister Li Kenong Returns to Beijing
Author:
Date: 1954-08-07
Page: 1

    Xinhua News Agency, 6th. Li Kenong, representative of the People's Republic of China delegation to the Geneva Conference and Deputy Minister of Foreign Affairs of the Central People's Government, returned to Beijing by train on the afternoon of the 6th, via Moscow, after the Geneva Conference ended and after visiting the Minister of Foreign Affairs of the Czechoslovak Republic on July 26 on behalf of Premier Zhou Enlai.
    Those who went to the station to welcome him included: Zhang Hanfu, Wu Xiuquan, Xiao Xiangrong, Zhu Zaoguan, Zhang Zhen, Yan Kuiyao, Peng Fujiu, Wang Yongjun, Liu Shaowen, Ma Ciqing, Lai Zulie, and many other responsible persons from the Ministry of Foreign Affairs of the Central People's Government and other organs.
    Also at the station to welcome him were: Huang Zhen, Geng Biao, Wang Youping, and Cao Xiangren, our ambassadors to foreign countries who are currently in China.
    Also at the station to welcome him were Šmíd, Chargé d'Affaires ad interim of the Czechoslovak Embassy in China, and other members of the embassy.

### 李克农副外长回到北京

1954-08-07
第1版()
专栏：

    李克农副外长回到北京
    【新华社六日讯】出席日内瓦会议的中华人民共和国代表团代表、中央人民政府外交部副部长李克农，在日内瓦会议结束，七月二十六日代表周恩来总理访问捷克斯洛伐克共和国外交部部长后，经莫斯科在六日下午乘火车回到北京。
    前往车站欢迎的有：中央人民政府外交部等机关的负责人章汉夫、伍修权、萧向荣、朱早观、张震、阎揆要、彭富九、王永浚、刘少文、马次青、赖祖烈等多人。
    前往车站欢迎的还有：现在国内的我驻外使节黄镇、耿飚、王幼平、曹祥仁。
    前往车站欢迎的还有捷克斯洛伐克驻我国大使馆临时代办石密茨及馆员等。
