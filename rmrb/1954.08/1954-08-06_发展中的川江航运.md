Developing Sichuan River Shipping

The Sichuan River has many rapids and swift currents, presenting many difficulties for shipping. In recent years, the People's Government has carried out a series of river regulation projects, reducing the dangers of the rapids, enabling Sichuan's abundant materials to be smoothly transported to various places via inland waterways; construction equipment relying on external supplies can also be transported by ships along the rivers. The development of Sichuan River shipping has played a significant role in promoting industrial and agricultural production and facilitating the exchange of goods. (Attached pictures)

　　Various industrial equipment transported from Anshan, Shenyang, Shanghai and other places are unloaded at the Jiulongpo Wharf in Chongqing, and then transferred to various places via the Chengdu-Chongqing Railway to support construction.
　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　Xinhua News Agency reporter You Yungu

　　Sichuan is one of China's major grain-producing areas. A large amount of surplus grain is being loaded onto ships, ready for transfer to various places.
　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　Xinhua News Agency reporter Sun Zhongjing

　　These are ships sailing on the Sichuan River using the Soviet Union's advanced shipping experience "one-row towing method."
　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　Xinhua News Agency reporter Sun Zhongjing

　　The Chongqing Port Authority of the Yangtze River Navigation Administration has set up various navigation signs on the Sichuan River to ensure smooth sailing of ships.
　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　Xinhua News Agency reporter Sun Zhongjing

### 发展中的川江航运

1954-08-06
第2版()
专栏：

    发展中的川江航运
    川江滩多水急，在航运上有很多困难。几年来，人民政府进行了一系列的河道整修工程，减少了滩险，使四川丰饶的物资能够顺利地经由内河运往各地；依靠外地供应的建设器材，也能经由各江河船只运来。川江航运的发展，对发展工农业生产和沟通物资交流，起了很大作用。（附图片）
    从鞍山、沈阳、上海等地运来的各种工业器材，在重庆九龙坡码头卸货，再经成渝铁路转运各地，支援建设。
                                  新华社记者　游云谷摄
    四川是我国的主要产粮区之一。大批余粮正装上船只，准备转运各地。
                            新华社记者　孙忠靖摄
    这是在川江上用苏联先进的航运经验“一列式拖带法”航行的船只。
                  新华社记者　孙忠靖摄
    长江航运管理局重庆港务局在川江上设置了各种导航标志，保证船只顺利的航行。
                    新华社记者　孙忠靖摄
