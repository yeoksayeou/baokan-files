    Our Great Motherland
    Young students actively respond to Chairman Mao's call for "good health, good study, and good work," and persevere in regular physical exercise. This is a group of students from Dalian Medical College practicing rowing on the coast of Xinghai Park in Lushun-Dalian.

    我们伟大的祖国
