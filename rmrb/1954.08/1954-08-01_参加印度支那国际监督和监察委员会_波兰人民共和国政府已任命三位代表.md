Title: Poland Appoints Three Representatives to Participate in the International Supervisory and Control Commission for Indochina
Author:
Date: 1954-08-01
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, Warsaw, July 31st: The Polish Press Agency, Warsaw, July 30th: The government of the Polish People's Republic, having agreed to participate in the International Supervisory and Control Commission in Indochina, has appointed the following three personnel with the rank of ambassador as representatives: Ogrodziński will participate in the International Supervisory and Control Commission in Vietnam, Graniewski will participate in the International Supervisory and Control Commission in Laos, and Grochi will participate in the International Supervisory and Control Commission in Cambodia.

### 参加印度支那国际监督和监察委员会  波兰人民共和国政府已任命三位代表

1954-08-01
第1版()
专栏：

    参加印度支那国际监督和监察委员会
    波兰人民共和国政府已任命三位代表
   【新华社三十一日讯】波兰通讯社华沙三十日讯：波兰人民共和国政府同意参加在印度支那的国际监督和监察委员会，已任命下列三位大使衔的人员为代表：奥格罗京斯基参加越南国际监督和监察委员会，格兰涅夫斯基参加老挝国际监督和监察委员会，格罗希参加柬埔寨国际监督和监察委员会。
