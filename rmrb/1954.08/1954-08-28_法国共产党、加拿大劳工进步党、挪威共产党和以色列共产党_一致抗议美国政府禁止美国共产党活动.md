Title: French Communist Party, Labor-Progressive Party of Canada, Norwegian Communist Party, and Israeli Communist Party Unanimously Protest U.S. Government's Ban on the Communist Party USA

Author:

Date: 1954-08-28
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, August 27th, TASS Paris, August 26th: The Central Committee of the French Communist Party issued a statement protesting the U.S. government's ban on the activities of the Communist Party USA.

    The statement said that the U.S. reactionary rulers' announcement of the ban on the Communist Party USA makes it clear to the people of all countries the deceptive nature of the so-called "free world."

    The statement said: In order to support the American working people, the French working class and people will do everything possible to expose the United States' false propaganda about the "free world," which aims to conceal plans to enslave the people. The French working class and people will use ever-growing strength to expose the plans of the U.S. rulers to revive German militarism, openly interfere in French affairs, and attempt to force France to ratify the "European Defense Community" treaty.

    Xinhua News Agency, August 24th, TASS, August 23rd: The National Executive Committee of the Labor-Progressive Party of Canada issued a statement to the press protesting the bill banning the Communist Party USA.

    The statement, signed by Tim Buck, called on all progressives in Canada to denounce the bill banning the Communist Party USA and to demand that President Eisenhower veto the Congressional resolution.

    Xinhua News Agency, August 25th, TASS Oslo, August 23rd: "Friheten" published a telegram from the Central Committee of the Norwegian Communist Party to the Communist Party USA. The telegram said: "The persecution of the Communist Party, and thus of all forces for peace and progress, by the reactionaries has aroused indignation among our people. We express our respect for the courage of the Communist Party and believe that no persecution can stop the Communist Party's struggle to unite all forces for peace and progress, for peace, for freedom, and for the happiness of the people."

    Xinhua News Agency, August 27th, TASS Tel Aviv, August 26th: The Political Committee of the Israeli Communist Party passed a resolution strongly protesting the U.S. passage of a bill banning the Communist Party USA.

    The resolution said: "This decision, which violates the U.S. Constitution, was prompted by pressure from the most aggressive imperialist elements who are frantically preparing for war."

    The resolution said: "The Political Committee calls on the working class, the masses of the people, and all patriots of Israel to speak out and intensify the struggle against Israel's 'treasonous acts' bill, modeled on the United States, which attempts to suppress progressive forces fighting for the country's peace and independence, for the freedom and happiness of the people, thereby turning Israel into a police state."

### 法国共产党、加拿大劳工进步党、挪威共产党和以色列共产党  一致抗议美国政府禁止美国共产党活动

1954-08-28
第4版()
专栏：

    法国共产党、加拿大劳工进步党、挪威共产党和以色列共产党
    一致抗议美国政府禁止美国共产党活动
   【新华社二十七日讯】塔斯社巴黎二十六日讯：法国共产党中央委员会发表声明，抗议美国政府禁止美国共产党活动。
    声明说，美国反动统治者宣布禁止美国共产党活动这件事，使世界各国人民清楚地看到所谓“自由世界”的骗人鬼话。
    声明说：法国工人阶级和人民为了声援美国劳动人民，将竭尽一切力量揭穿美国的关于“自由世界”的虚伪宣传，这种宣传的目的是掩饰奴役人民的计划。法国工人阶级和人民将以日益壮大的力量来揭穿美国执政者力图复活德国军国主义、公开干涉法国事务、企图迫使法国批准“欧洲防务集团”条约的计划。
   【新华社二十四日讯】塔斯社二十三日讯：加拿大劳工进步党全国执行委员会向报界发表声明，抗议禁止美国共产党活动的法案。
    由蒂姆·布克签字的声明号召加拿大所有进步人士斥责禁止美国共产党活动的法案，并要求艾森豪威尔总统否决国会的决议。
     【新华社二十五日讯】塔斯社奥斯陆二十三日讯：“自由报”刊载了挪威共产党中央委员会拍给美国共产党的电报。电文说：“反动派对于共产党的、从而也是对所有和平和进步力量的迫害已引起了我国人民的愤慨。我们对共产党的勇敢表示敬意，并且相信，任何迫害都不能阻止共产党为团结一切和平和进步的力量、为争取和平、争取自由和人民幸福的斗争。”
     【新华社二十七日讯】据塔斯社特拉维夫二十六日讯：以色列共产党政治委员会通过决议，坚决抗议美国通过禁止美国共产党活动的法案。
    决议说：“这一个违背美国宪法的决定是由于正在疯狂备战的最富侵略性的帝国主义分子的压力所促成的。”
    决议说：“政治委员会号召工人阶级、人民群众以及以色列所有爱国者大声疾呼和加紧斗争，反对以色列仿照美国制订的‘叛国罪行’法案，这个法案企图镇压为国家的和平和独立、为人民的自由和幸福的前途而斗争的进步力量，从而把以色列变成警察国家。”
