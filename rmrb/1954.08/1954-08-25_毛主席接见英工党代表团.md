Title: Chairman Mao Receives British Labour Party Delegation
Author:
Date: 1954-08-25
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, August 24th: Chairman Mao Zedong of the Central People's Government received the British Labour Party delegation at noon on August 24th. The delegation, invited by the Chinese People's Institute of Foreign Affairs, included Mr. Clement Attlee, Mr. Aneurin Bevan, Mrs. E. Summerskill, Mr. W. Burke, Mr. Morgan Phillips, Mr. S. Watson, Mr. H. Earnshaw, and Mr. H. Franklin. Also present at the reception were Sir Michael Lindsay and his wife, who served as translators for the British Labour Party delegation.

On the Chinese side were: Liu Shaoqi, Zhou Enlai, Chen Yun, Deng Xiaoping, Li Jishen, Zhang Bojun, Huang Yanpei, Guo Moruo, Chen Shutong, and Zhang Xiruo, President of the Chinese People's Institute of Foreign Affairs. (Attached picture)

Above: Chairman Mao Zedong of the Central People's Government receiving the British Labour Party delegation on August 24th, invited by the Chinese People's Institute of Foreign Affairs. The picture shows Chairman Mao shaking hands with Clement Attlee. Xinhua News Agency photo by Lü Houmin

### 毛主席接见英工党代表团

1954-08-25
第1版()
专栏：

    毛主席接见英工党代表团
    【新华社二十四日讯】我中央人民政府毛泽东主席，在二十四日下午接见应中国人民外交学会邀请来我国访问的英国工党代表团克·艾德礼先生、安·比万先生、埃·萨末斯基尔夫人、韦·伯克先生、摩·菲利普斯先生、塞·华生先生、哈·厄恩萧先生、亨·弗兰克林先生。接见时在座的，还有英国工党代表团翻译林迈可爵士夫妇。
    中国方面在座的，有：刘少奇、周恩来、陈云、邓小平、李济深、章伯钧、黄炎培、郭沫若、陈叔通和中国人民外交学会会长张奚若。（附图片）
    上图：我中央人民政府毛泽东主  席在八月二十四日接见应中国人民外交学会邀请来我国访问的英国工党  代表团。图为毛主席和克·艾德礼握手。  新华社稿　吕厚民摄
