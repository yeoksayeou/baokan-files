Title: Vice Premier Pham Van Dong Departs Moscow for Home

Author:
Date: 1954-08-02
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, 1st — TASS reports from Moscow: Pham Van Dong, Vice Premier and Acting Minister of Foreign Affairs of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam, and members of the delegation of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam attending the Geneva Conference, left Moscow for home on July 31st.

Those who saw them off at the airport included: First Deputy Chairman of the Council of Ministers and Minister of Foreign Affairs of the Soviet Union, Molotov, Deputy Minister of Foreign Affairs of the Soviet Union, Zorin, Director of the Southeast Asia Department of the Soviet Foreign Ministry, Novikov, Director of the First European Department of the Soviet Foreign Ministry, Lavrishev, Chairman of the Moscow Soviet, Yasnov, Deputy Director of the Protocol Department of the Soviet Foreign Ministry, Kapustin, Acting Commander of the Moscow City Defense Command, Kovalev, etc.

Also at the airport to see them off were Nguyen Luong Bang, Ambassador of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam to the Soviet Union, and some foreign envoys and diplomatic personnel stationed in the Soviet Union.

### 范文同副总理离莫斯科回国

1954-08-02
第1版()
专栏：

    范文同副总理离莫斯科回国
    【新华社一日讯】据塔斯社莫斯科讯：越南民主共和国副总理兼代理外交部部长范文同和出席日内瓦会议的越南民主共和国代表团人员，在七月三十一日离莫斯科返国。
    到机场欢送的有：苏联部长会议第一副主席兼外交部部长莫洛托夫、苏联外交部副部长佐林、苏联外交部东南亚司司长诺维科夫、苏联外交部第一欧洲司司长拉夫里谢夫、莫斯科市苏维埃主席雅斯诺夫、苏联外交部交际司副司长卡普斯金、莫斯科城防司令部代理司令科瓦列夫等。
    到机场欢送的还有越南民主共和国驻苏大使阮良朋和一些驻苏外国使节和外交人员。
