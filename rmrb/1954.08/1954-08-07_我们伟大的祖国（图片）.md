Our Great Motherland
　　Employees at the Han River Railway Bridge construction site are both reinforcing and raising the dikes along the Han River and completing the riveting and joining of the bridge’s truss girders. The picture shows the workers assembling the steel beams of the Han River Railway Bridge.
