    Our Great Motherland
    The geological drilling work for the piers of the Wuhan Yangtze River Bridge continues under the high water level of the Yangtze River. These workers are installing drill pipes in the 18th hole of the No. 4 pier of the Yangtze River Bridge, preparing to start drilling.
