Title: South, Greece, and Turkey Sign Military Treaty
Author:
Date: 1954-08-12
Page: 4

    Xinhua News Agency, August 11th, Belgrade Dispatch: The foreign ministers of Yugoslavia, Greece, and Turkey signed a "Treaty of Alliance for Political Cooperation and Mutual Military Assistance" in Bled, Yugoslavia, on August 9th. The treaty is valid for a period of twenty years.
    Since Greece and Turkey are members of the North Atlantic Treaty Organization, this military alliance treaty of Greece, Turkey, and Yugoslavia, organized under the planning of the United States, will open the way for Yugoslavia to be linked with the North Atlantic aggressive bloc. Turkish Foreign Minister Köprülü said when he set off for Bled on the 3rd to sign the treaty: "The second stage of the Balkan military alliance is to find a way to link it with the North Atlantic Treaty Organization."

### 南、希、土三国签订军事条约

1954-08-12
第4版()
专栏：

    南、希、土三国签订军事条约
      【新华社十一日讯】贝尔格莱德消息：南斯拉夫、希腊、土耳其三国外交部长八月九日在南斯拉夫的布列德签订了“政治合作和军事援助联盟条约”，条约有效期限是二十年。
    由于希腊和土耳其是北大西洋公约组织成员，这个在美国策划下组织起来的希、土、南三国军事同盟条约就将使南斯拉夫和北大西洋侵略集团发生联系打开了道路。土耳其外交部长柯普路鲁在三日动身到布列德去签订条约时说：“巴尔干军事联盟的第二个阶段就是设法使它和北大西洋公约组织联系起来。”
