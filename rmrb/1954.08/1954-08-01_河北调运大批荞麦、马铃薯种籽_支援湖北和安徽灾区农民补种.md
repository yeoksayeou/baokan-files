Title: Hebei Transfers Large Quantities of Buckwheat and Potato Seeds to Support Farmers in Disaster Areas of Hubei and Anhui in Replanting
Author:
Date: 1954-08-01
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Baoding, 31st -- The Hebei Provincial People's Government has decided to transfer 500,000 jin of buckwheat and about 30,000,000 jin of potato and other late autumn crop seeds to supply farmers in the flood-stricken areas of Hubei and Anhui provinces for replanting after drainage. The buckwheat seeds have now been prepared and the germination rate of the seeds has been checked by technicians, and they will soon be transported to Hubei. Potato seeds are being purchased and will all be transported to Anhui by early August.

### 河北调运大批荞麦、马铃薯种籽  支援湖北和安徽灾区农民补种

1954-08-01
第1版()
专栏：

    河北调运大批荞麦、马铃薯种籽
    支援湖北和安徽灾区农民补种
   【新华社保定三十一日电】河北省人民政府决定调运五十万斤荞麦和三千万斤左右马铃薯等晚秋作物种籽，供给湖北、安徽两省遭受水灾地区的农民排水后重新播种。现在荞麦种已备齐，并经技术人员检查了种籽的发芽率，不久就要运往湖北。马铃薯种正在收购，到八月上旬就可全部运往安徽。
