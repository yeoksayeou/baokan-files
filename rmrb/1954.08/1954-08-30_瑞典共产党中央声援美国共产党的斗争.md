Title: Central Committee of the Communist Party of Sweden Expresses Solidarity with the Struggle of the Communist Party of the United States

Author:

Date: 1954-08-30
Page: 4

    Xinhua News Agency, Stockholm, August 28th: The Swedish newspaper "Ny Tid" reported that the Central Committee of the Communist Party of Sweden sent a telegram to the Communist Party of the United States, expressing solidarity regarding the illegal prohibition of the activities of the Communist Party of the United States by the American government.
    The telegram said: "Dear Comrades, in view of the serious threat posed by the enemies of democracy, we express our fraternal solidarity and pay tribute to all American Communists. We know that you will not be intimidated by these fascist attacks, but will continue and strengthen the struggle for a policy of peace and democracy."
    The telegram emphasized that the defenders of peace and democracy in Sweden expressed their anger at the persecution suffered by the Communist Party of the United States and all forces for peace and democracy.

### 瑞典共产党中央声援美国共产党的斗争

1954-08-30
第4版()
专栏：

    瑞典共产党中央声援美国共产党的斗争
    【新华社二十九日讯】据塔斯社斯德哥尔摩二十八日讯：瑞典“新时代”报报道：瑞典共产党中央委员会就美国政府非法禁止美国共产党活动一事打电报声援美国共产党。
    电报说：“亲爱的同志们，鉴于民主的敌人所造成的严重威胁，我们表示我们兄弟般的声援，并向所有美国共产党员致敬。我们知道：你们不会被这些法西斯进攻吓倒，而将是继续和加强争取和平民主政策的斗争。”
    电报强调指出，瑞典的和平民主捍卫者对美国共产党和所有和平民主力量所遭到的迫害表示愤怒。
