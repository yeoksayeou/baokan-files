Title: Australian Foreign Minister's Remarks Against Including the West Irian Issue on the UN General Assembly Agenda Arouse Strong Dissatisfaction Among Indonesian Politicians
Author:
Date: 1954-08-26
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Jakarta, August 25th: Australian Minister for External Affairs Casey's recent remarks opposing the inclusion of the West Irian issue on the agenda of the United Nations General Assembly have aroused great dissatisfaction in Indonesia.

    Indonesian Foreign Ministry sources said that Casey's remarks are an action that undermines the United Nations Charter and hinders peace, with the aim of forcing Indonesia to accept the so-called "Southeast Asia Collective Defense" and interfering in Indonesia's internal affairs. They pointed out that the countries of the Southeast Asia Treaty Organization want to use West Irian as a military base. If West Irian is returned to Indonesia, their hope will be shattered.

    "Indonesia News" published an editorial saying that Casey's attitude of protecting colonial interests will be strongly opposed by the Indonesian people and all peace-loving and anti-colonialist people, including the Australian people.

### 澳外长表示反对把西伊里安问题列入联大议程的谈话  引起印度尼西亚政界人士的很大不满

1954-08-26
第4版()
专栏：

    澳外长表示反对把西伊里安问题列入联大议程的谈话
    引起印度尼西亚政界人士的很大不满
    【新华社雅加达二十五日电】澳大利亚外交部部长凯西最近表示反对把西伊里安问题列入联合国大会议程的谈话，在印度尼西亚引起了很大的不满。
    印度尼西亚外交部人士说，凯西的谈话是破坏联合国宪章和妨碍和平的行动，目的在于强迫印度尼西亚接受所谓“东南亚集体防御”和干涉印度尼西亚内政。他们指出，东南亚公约组织的国家想把西伊里安作为军事基地。如果西伊里安归还印度尼西亚的话，它们的这种希望就将破灭。
    “印度尼西亚新闻”发表社论说，凯西的这种保护殖民利益的态度将遭到印度尼西亚人民以及包括澳大利亚人民在内的一切爱好和平和反对殖民主义的人民的激烈反对。
