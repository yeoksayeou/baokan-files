Title: Central People's Government Appoints Luo Guibo as Ambassador to Vietnam; Chargé d'Affaires ad interim of Our Embassy in Albania Arrives in Tirana
Author: 
Date: 1954-08-20
Page: 1

    Xinhua News Agency, August 19th—The Central People's Government of the People's Republic of China has appointed Luo Guibo as the Ambassador Extraordinary and Plenipotentiary of the People's Republic of China to the Vietnam Democratic Republic.
    Xinhua News Agency, Prague, August 19th—Tirana dispatch: Wang Dong, Chargé d'Affaires ad interim of the Embassy of the People's Republic of China in Albania, and other embassy staff members arrived in Tirana on the 16th. Upon their arrival, they were welcomed by Albanian Deputy Minister of Foreign Affairs V. Natanaili and other officials from the Ministry of Foreign Affairs.
      "Voice of the People" newspaper published an article entitled "Consolidating Albanian-Chinese Relations and Friendship," welcoming the arrival of Wang Dong and others. The article pointed out that the establishment of diplomatic relations between China and Albania is the first time in Albanian history. This is a great contribution to consolidating the relationship between the Albanian people and the great Chinese people, and at the same time makes an important contribution to maintaining world peace. The Albanian people are very happy to welcome the arrival of the Chinese diplomatic representatives. They were very happy to see the victory of the Chinese revolution, and have been following with great interest the peaceful construction and new life construction work carried out by the people of the new China in the five years since the establishment of the People's Republic of China.

### 中央人民政府任命罗贵波为驻越南大使  我驻阿尔巴尼亚大使馆临时代办到地拉那

1954-08-20
第1版()
专栏：

    中央人民政府任命罗贵波为驻越南大使
    我驻阿尔巴尼亚大使馆临时代办到地拉那
     【新华社十九日讯】中华人民共和国中央人民政府已任命罗贵波为中华人民共和国驻越南民主共和国特命全权大使。
    【新华社布拉格十九日电】地拉那消息：中华人民共和国驻阿尔巴尼亚大使馆临时代办王栋和大使馆其他人员十六日到达地拉那。他们到达时受到了阿尔巴尼亚外交部副部长瓦·纳塔奈利和外交部其他官员的欢迎。
      “人民之声报”以“巩固阿中关系和友谊”为题发表文章，欢迎王栋等的到达。文章指出，中国和阿尔巴尼亚建立外交关系，这在阿尔巴尼亚历史上还是第一次，这对巩固阿尔巴尼亚人民和伟大的中国人民之间的关系具有巨大的贡献，同时对维护世界和平也有重要贡献。阿尔巴尼亚人民十分高兴地欢迎中国外交代表的到达。他们曾十分高兴地看到中国革命的胜利，并一直以极大的兴趣注视着新中国的人民在中华人民共和国建立以来的五年中所从事的和平建设和建设新生活的工作。
