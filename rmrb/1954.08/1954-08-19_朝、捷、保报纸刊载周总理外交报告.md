Title: North Korean, Czechoslovakian, and Bulgarian Newspapers Publish Premier Zhou's Diplomatic Report
Author:
Date: 1954-08-19
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency

    North Korean, Czechoslovakian, and Bulgarian Newspapers Publish Premier Zhou's Diplomatic Report
    [Xinhua News Agency, Pyongyang] Pyongyang newspapers, on the 17th, prominently featured on their international pages the news that the 33rd session of the Central People's Government Committee approved the diplomatic report made by Zhou Enlai, Premier of the State Council and Minister of Foreign Affairs, and the resolution to convene the first session of the First National People's Congress of our country on September 15th.
    [Xinhua News Agency, Prague] Czechoslovakia's "Red Right" and Bulgaria's "Workers' Cause" newspapers, on the 15th and 16th respectively, published the news of the 33rd session of the Central People's Government Committee and the diplomatic report made by Zhou Enlai, Premier of the State Council and Minister of Foreign Affairs, at the meeting.

### 朝、捷、保报纸刊载周总理外交报告

1954-08-19
第1版()
专栏：

    朝、捷、保报纸刊载周总理外交报告
    【新华社平壤讯】平壤各报在十七日以国际版的显著地位，刊登了我中央人民政府委员会第三十三次会议批准我中央人民政府周恩来总理兼外交部部长所作的外交报告和决定在九月十五日召开我国第一届全国人民代表大会第一次会议的决议的消息。
    【新华社布拉格讯】捷克斯洛伐克的“红色权利报”和保加利亚的“工人事业报”，分别在十五日和十六日刊载了我中央人民政府委员会举行第三十三次会议的消息，和周恩来总理兼外交部部长在会上所作的外交报告。
