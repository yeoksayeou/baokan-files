Title: U.S. Plots to Use Air Force to Shield Chiang Kai-shek Bandits
Author:
Date: 1954-08-29
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, August 27th: U.S. authorities are plotting to use U.S. air force power to shield the Chiang Kai-shek traitorous clique. According to a report by Agence France-Presse, senior Chiang Kai-shek bandit officials revealed on the 25th that the U.S. and Chiang Kai-shek bandits have virtually completed a plan stipulating that the U.S. will send "a certain number of U.S. interceptors, completely piloted by American pilots," to be stationed in Taiwan, ready to assist in preventing the Chinese people from liberating Taiwan.

At the same time, according to a report by United Press, air bandit Chennault publicly clamored in the "Look" magazine published on the 23rd to organize a so-called "International Volunteer Air Force" to participate in "combat" against the Chinese people and the people of Asia. He also stated that he had "long drafted the organization and combat plan for this air force."

### 美国策划使用空军庇护蒋匪

1954-08-29
第4版()
专栏：

    美国策划使用空军庇护蒋匪
    【新华社二十七日讯】美国当局正在策划使用美国空军力量来庇护蒋介石卖国集团。据法新社报
  道，蒋匪帮高级官员二十五日透露，美国和蒋匪已经实际上完成了一项计划，规定由美国派遣“一定数量的完全由美国飞行员驾驶的美国截击机”驻在台湾，随时出动协助阻止中国人民解放台湾。
    同时，据合众社报道，空中强盗陈纳德在二十
  三日出版的“展望”杂志上公开叫嚣要组织一支所谓“国际志愿航空队”，参加对中国人民和亚洲人民“作战”，他还表示，他“早已草拟好了这支航
  空队的组织和作战计划”。
