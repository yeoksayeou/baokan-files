Author:
Date: 1954-08-22

Our Great Motherland
Recently, people's congresses in various provinces and cities across the country have convened one after another and elected representatives to the National People's Congress. The First Session of the First People's Congress of Beijing Municipality elected Mao Zedong, Liu Shaoqi, Zhou Enlai and others as representatives to the National People's Congress. Students from the Central Institute for Nationalities are listening to the broadcast here. They are all delighted by this good news.

### 我们伟大的祖国（图片）

1954-08-22
第1版()
专栏：

    我们伟大的祖国
    全国各省市最近相继召开了人民代表大会会议，选出了全国人民代表大会的代表。北京市第一届人民代表大会第一次会议选举了毛泽东刘少奇周恩来等为全国人民代表大会代表。中央民族学院的学员们正在这里收听广播。他们都为这个喜讯而欢欣鼓舞。
