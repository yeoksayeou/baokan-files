Title: Spokesperson for the Swiss Delegation to the Neutral Nations Supervisory Commission in Korea Sternly Denies Fabricated News from American News Agency
Author:
Date: 1954-08-07
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency, Seoul, 6th—According to an Agence France-Presse report from Seoul on the 5th, a spokesperson for the Swiss delegation to the Neutral Nations Supervisory Commission (NNSC) in Korea sternly denied fabricated news from an American news agency. The news agency had reported that the Swiss delegation spokesperson expressed support for US Secretary of State Dulles' remarks at a press conference on the 3rd regarding the disbandment of the NNSC. The Swiss delegation spokesperson stated that the Swiss delegation was neither instructed nor authorized to announce the disbandment of the NNSC. The spokesperson also stated that the Swiss delegation had not received orders from the Swiss government to withdraw from the NNSC.

### 朝鲜中立国监察委员会瑞士代表团发言人  断然否认美国通讯社捏造的消息

1954-08-07
第4版()
专栏：

    朝鲜中立国监察委员会瑞士代表团发言人
    断然否认美国通讯社捏造的消息
    【新华社六日讯】据法新社汉城五日消息，朝鲜中立国监察委员会瑞士代表团的发言人断然否认美国通讯社捏造的消息，这些消息说，瑞士代表团的发言人曾表示支持美国国务卿杜勒斯三日在记者招待会上发表的关于解散中立国监察委员会的谈话。瑞士代表团的发言人表示，瑞士代表团既没有奉命，也没有权力宣布解散中立国监察委员会。发言人还表示，瑞士代表团没有接到瑞士政府的命令，要它退出中立国监察委员会。
