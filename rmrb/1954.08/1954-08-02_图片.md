    Zhou Enlai, Chief Representative of the Delegation of the People's Republic of China to the Geneva Conference, Premier of the State Council and Minister of Foreign Affairs of the Central People's Government, visited Berlin, Warsaw, Moscow, and Ulan Bator after the Geneva Conference. He returned to Beijing by plane on the afternoon of August 1st.
    Above: Premier Zhou Enlai shakes hands with the heads of the Central People's Government who came to welcome him after disembarking. From left to right: Zhou Enlai, Zhu De, Liu Shaoqi, Li Jishen, Shen Junru, Chen Shutong, Lin Boqu, Dong Biwu, Guo Moruo, Huang Yanpei, Deng Xiaoping, Zhang Zongxun. 专栏：

    出席日内瓦会议的中华人民共和国代表团首席代表、中央人民政府政务院总理兼外交部部长周恩来，在日内瓦会议结束后，先后访问了柏林、华沙、莫斯科、乌兰巴托，八月一日下午已乘飞机回到北京。
