Title: Wuhan Guān Yangtze River Water Level Stabilizes, Water Levels Fall in Most Parts of the Upper and Middle Reaches of the Yangtze River
Author:
Date: 1954-08-22
Page: 1

    Xinhua News Agency, Wuhan, August 21st. At 5:00 PM on the 21st, the Yangtze River water level at Wuhan Guān remained at 29.66 meters, the same as at the same time on the 20th, which is 1.38 meters higher than the highest water level in previous years. On the 21st, in the upper and middle reaches of the Yangtze River, except for slight rises in local sections such as Zhijiang and Shashi, water levels fell in most areas. The water level of the entire Han River is falling.
    The persons in charge of the Wuhan Flood Control Headquarters and the Yangtze River Water Conservancy Commission, together with engineering and technical personnel, conducted thorough inspections of the current conditions of the embankments along the Zhanggong Dike and the rivers on the 21st. They are preparing to further reinforce the weak links in each embankment section and prepare to take necessary measures in response to the long-term soaking of the embankments and the new dangers that may occur in the future during the period of high water level decline.

### 武汉关长江水位稳定  长江上中游大部地区水位下落

1954-08-22
第1版()
专栏：

    武汉关长江水位稳定
    长江上中游大部地区水位下落
    【新华社武汉二十一日电】二十一日下午五时，武汉关长江水位仍为二九点六六公尺，和二十日同一时间的水位一样，比历年最高水位高一点三八公尺。二十一日长江上游和中游，除枝江、沙市等局部地段水位微涨外，大部地区水位下落。汉水全线水位都在下落。
    武汉市防汛总指挥部和长江水利委员会的负责人在二十一日同着工程技术人员到张公堤和沿江沿河深入检查目前堤防情况，准备对各个堤段的薄弱环节进一步加固，并准备针对着堤防浸泡过久的情况，以及今后高水位下落时期堤防可能发生的新的险情，采取必要的措施。
