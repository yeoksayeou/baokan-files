    Our Great Motherland
    Shanghai's various oil factories have been systematically learning and promoting the experience of advanced oil factories, improving operational methods such as screening, rolling, and steaming materials before oil extraction, and continuously increasing the oil yield. The state-owned Shanghai Oil Factory No. 1 produced more than 55,700 kilograms of edible oil in the second quarter compared to the same period last year.

### 我们伟大的祖国（图片）

1954-08-04
第1版()
专栏：

    我们伟大的祖国
    上海各油脂工厂有组织地学习和推广先进油厂的经验，改进了榨油前的筛料、轧料和薰煮等操作方法，不断地提高了出油率。国营上海油脂一厂第二季度比去年同期多出五万五千七百余公斤食油。
