Title: Celebrating the Seventh Anniversary of Pakistan's Independence: Pakistani Ambassador Raza Holds Reception; Chairman Mao and Premier Zhou Send Telegrams to Congratulate the Governor-General and Prime Minister of Pakistan
Author:
Date: 1954-08-15
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, August 14th: Pakistani Ambassador to China, Raza, held a reception on the evening of the 14th to celebrate the seventh anniversary of Pakistan's independence.

Those invited to attend the reception included: Zhou Enlai, Premier of the State Council of the Central People's Government of China; Shen Yanbing, Minister of Culture; Li Dequan, Minister of Health; Zhang Hanfu, Vice Minister of Foreign Affairs; Lei Renmin, Vice Minister of Foreign Trade; Yao Yilin, Vice Minister of Commerce; He Changgong, Vice Minister of Geology; Zhou Yang, Vice Minister of Culture; Wei Que, Vice Minister of Education; Hu Yuzhi, Director of the Publishing Administration; and Wu Han, Deputy Mayor of the Beijing Municipal People's Government, among others.

Diplomatic envoys and embassy staff from various countries to China also attended the reception.

Also attending the reception were members of the British Labour Party delegation, who are visiting China at the invitation of the Chinese government: Clement Attlee, Aneurin Bevan, E. Samerskill, W. Burke, Morgan Phillips, S. Watson, H. Earnshaw, and Henry Franklin.

Some members of the Indonesian Art Troupe, led by Mengadas Nasution, also attended the reception.

During the reception, Ambassador Raza and Premier Zhou Enlai delivered speeches (speeches to be issued separately), and after the speeches, the band played the national anthems of China and Pakistan respectively.

The reception took place in a friendly atmosphere.

A dance was held after the reception.

Xinhua News Agency, August 14th: Mao Zedong, Chairman of the Central People's Government of China, and Zhou Enlai, Premier of the State Council, sent telegrams to Ghulam Muhammad, Governor-General of Pakistan, and Muhammad Ali, Prime Minister of Pakistan, respectively, congratulating them on the seventh anniversary of Pakistan's independence.

### 庆祝巴基斯坦独立七周年  巴基斯坦大使罗查举行招待会  毛主席、周总理电贺巴基斯坦总督和总理

1954-08-15
第1版()
专栏：

    庆祝巴基斯坦独立七周年
    巴基斯坦大使罗查举行招待会
    毛主席、周总理电贺巴基斯坦总督和总理
    【新华社十四日讯】巴基斯坦驻华大使罗查在十四日晚举行招待会，庆祝巴基斯坦独立七周年。
    应邀出席招待会的，有：我中央人民政府政务院总理周恩来，文化部部长沈雁冰，卫生部部长李德全，外交部副部长章汉夫，对外贸易部副部长雷任民，商业部副部长姚依林，地质部副部长何长工，文化部副部长周扬，教育部副部长韦悫，出版总署署长胡愈之和北京市人民政府副市长吴晗等。
    各国驻华使节和使馆人员也出席了招待会。
    出席招待会的还有：应邀来我国访问的英国工党代表团克·艾德礼、安·比万、埃·萨末斯基尔、韦·伯克、摩·菲利普斯、塞·华生、哈·厄恩萧、亨·弗兰克林。
    出席招待会的还有以团长孟阿达斯·纳苏申为首的印度尼西亚艺术团部分团员。
    在招待会中罗查大使和周恩来总理先后讲话（讲话另发），并在讲话后乐队分别高奏中国和巴基斯坦两国国歌。
    招待会在友好的空气中进行。
    会后，并举行了舞会。
    【新华社十四日讯】我中央人民政府主席毛泽东、政务院总理周恩来曾分别致电巴基斯坦总督吴拉姆·穆罕默德、总理穆罕默德·阿里祝贺巴基斯坦独立七周年纪念。
