Title: Zhou Enlai Visits Tsedenbal and Sambuu; Tsedenbal, Sambuu, and Damba Return Zhou Enlai's Visit
Author:
Date: 1954-08-02
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Ulaanbaatar, July 31st: Zhou Enlai, Premier of the State Council and Minister of Foreign Affairs of the Central People's Government of the People's Republic of China, visited Yu. Tsedenbal, Prime Minister of the Mongolian People's Republic, at 9:40 PM on July 31st.

Accompanying Premier Zhou Enlai were Wang Jiaxiang, Deputy Minister of Foreign Affairs of the People's Republic of China, and Shi Zhe and Chen Jiakang, advisors to the Delegation of the People's Republic of China to the Geneva Conference. Chen Ying, Chargé d'Affaires of the Embassy of the People's Republic of China in the Mongolian People's Republic, also accompanied him.

Xinhua News Agency, Ulaanbaatar, July 31st: Zhou Enlai, Premier of the State Council and Minister of Foreign Affairs of the Central People's Government of the People's Republic of China, visited Zh. Sambuu, Chairman of the Presidium of the Great People's Hural of the Mongolian People's Republic, at 10:00 PM on July 31st.

Present at the meeting were Dashiin Zeden, Vice Chairman of the Presidium of the Great People's Hural of the Mongolian People's Republic, and Dündibyn Sharav, Secretary.

Accompanying Premier Zhou Enlai were Wang Jiaxiang, Deputy Minister of Foreign Affairs of the People's Republic of China, and Shi Zhe and Chen Jiakang, advisors to the Delegation of the People's Republic of China to the Geneva Conference. Chen Ying, Chargé d'Affaires of the Embassy of the People's Republic of China in the Mongolian People's Republic, also accompanied him.

Xinhua News Agency, Ulaanbaatar, August 1st: Mongolian People's Republic Prime Minister Tsedenbal, Chairman of the Presidium of the Great People's Hural of the Mongolian People's Republic Zh. Sambuu, and First Secretary of the Central Committee of the Mongolian People's Revolutionary Party Damba returned the visit of Zhou Enlai, Premier of the State Council of the Central People's Government of the People's Republic of China, at 8:30 AM on the morning of the 1st. Premier Zhou Enlai accepted the Khata presented by Chairman Zh. Sambuu and horse milk served in a golden bowl (Note—presenting a Khata and horse milk are the most respectful etiquette in Mongolia).

Present were Wang Jiaxiang, Deputy Minister of Foreign Affairs of the Central People's Government of the People's Republic of China, Shi Zhe, advisor to the Delegation of the People's Republic of China to the Geneva Conference, and Chen Ying, Chargé d'Affaires of the Embassy of the People's Republic of China in Mongolia.

### 周恩来拜会泽登巴尔和桑布  泽登巴尔、桑布、达姆巴回拜周恩来

1954-08-02
第1版()
专栏：

    周恩来拜会泽登巴尔和桑布
    泽登巴尔、桑布、达姆巴回拜周恩来
    【新华社乌兰巴托三十一日电】中华人民共和国中央人民政府政务院总理兼外交部部长周恩来于七月三十一日晚间九时四十分拜会了蒙古人民共和国总理尤·泽登巴尔。
    随同周恩来总理前往的有中华人民共和国外交部副部长王稼祥，出席日内瓦会议的中华人民共和国代表团顾问师哲、陈家康。中华人民共和国驻蒙古人民共和国大使馆代办陈应也陪同前往。
      【新华社乌兰巴托三十一日电】中华人民共和国中央人民政府政务院总理兼外交部部长周恩来于七月三十一日晚十时拜会了蒙古人民共和国大人民呼拉尔主席团主席扎·桑布。
    拜会时，在座的有蒙古人民共和国大人民呼拉尔主席团副主席达辛·泽狄布，秘书敦迪宾·沙拉普。
    随同周恩来总理前往的有中华人民共和国外交部副部长王稼祥，出席日内瓦会议的中华人民共和国代表团顾问师哲、陈家康。中华人民共和国驻蒙古人民共和国大使馆代办陈应也陪同前往。
    【新华社乌兰巴托一日电】蒙古人民共和国总理泽登巴尔，蒙古人民共和国大人民呼拉尔主席团主席扎·桑布，蒙古人民革命党中央委员会第一书记达姆巴在一日上午八时三十分回拜了中华人民共和国中央人民政府政务院总理周恩来。周恩来总理接受了扎·桑布主席赠的哈达和用金碗盛的马奶（注——赠哈达和马奶是蒙古最尊敬的礼节）。
    在座的有中华人民共和国中央人民政府外交部副部长王稼祥，出席日内瓦会议的中华人民共和国代表团顾问师哲和中华人民共和国驻蒙古大使馆代办陈应。
