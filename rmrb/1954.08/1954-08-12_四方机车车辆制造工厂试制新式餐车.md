Title: Qingdao Sifang Locomotive and Rolling Stock Factory Trials New Dining Car
Author:
Date: 1954-08-12
Page: 2

    Qingdao Sifang Locomotive and Rolling Stock Factory has begun trial production of a new type of dining car. This newest type of dining car can accommodate forty-eight people dining at the same time. The equipment is very complete, and in the summer it can provide passengers with ice cream and other cold foods, and can also store a large amount of fresh and delicious food for passengers to enjoy. Preparations for the trial production of this dining car began as early as April, and some parts have now been manufactured and will soon leave the factory.

### 四方机车车辆制造工厂试制新式餐车

1954-08-12
第2版()
专栏：

    四方机车车辆制造工厂试制新式餐车
    青岛四方机车车辆制造工厂开始试制新式餐车。这种最新型的餐车，一次能容纳四十八人同时进餐；设备非常完善，夏季可以供给旅客冰淇淋等冷食，还能储备大量新鲜可口的食品供旅客享用。这种餐车的试制准备工作早于四月份开始，目前已有部分配件制成，不久即可出厂。
