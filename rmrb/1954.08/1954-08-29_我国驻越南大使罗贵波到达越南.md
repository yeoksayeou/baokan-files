Title: China's Ambassador to Vietnam, Luo Guibo, Arrives in Vietnam
Author:
Date: 1954-08-29
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, North Vietnam, August 28th: Luo Guibo, the Plenipotentiary Ambassador of the People's Republic of China to the Democratic Republic of Vietnam, arrived in North Vietnam on the 28th.

Those who went to the Sino-Vietnamese border to welcome him included Tran Cong Tuong, the representative of the Government of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam and Deputy Minister of the Ministry of Justice; Major General Chu Van Tan, the representative of the General Command of the Vietnam People's Army; Nguyen Huu Ngo, a member of the Administrative Committee of the Viet Bac Interzone; and Hoang Van Kieu, Chairman of the Lang Son Provincial Administrative Committee.

Le Thanh Nghi, representative of the Central Committee of the Vietnam Workers' Party and member of the Central Committee of the Vietnam Workers' Party, also went to welcome him.

### 我国驻越南大使罗贵波到达越南

1954-08-29
第1版()
专栏：

    我国驻越南大使罗贵波到达越南
    【新华社北越二十八日电】中华人民共和国驻越南民主共和国特命全权大使罗贵波于二十八日到达北越。
    前往中越边界欢迎的有越南民主共和国政府代表、司法部副部长陈公祥，越南人民军总司令部代表朱文晋少将，越北联区行政委员会委员阮友吴，谅山省行政委员会主席黄文乔。
    前往欢迎的还有越南劳动党中央委员会代表、越南劳动党中央委员黎成仪。
