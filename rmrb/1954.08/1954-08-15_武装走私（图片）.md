Title: Armed Smuggling (Picture)
Author: Fang Cheng
Date: 1954-08-15
Page: 6

Column:

    Armed Smuggling
    Made by Fang Cheng
    The Neutral Nations Supervisory Commission has repeatedly revealed that the United States has violated the Korean Armistice Agreement by shipping a large number of reinforcing combat materials into South Korea, and therefore it is regarded as a thorn in the side by the United States. Recently, the United States, on the one hand, clamored to "dissolve" it, and on the other hand, instigated the Syngman Rhee bandit gang to threaten and intimidate it.
