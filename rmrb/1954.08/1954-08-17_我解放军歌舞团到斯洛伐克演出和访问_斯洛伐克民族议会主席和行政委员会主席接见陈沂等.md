Title: The Chinese People's Liberation Army Song and Dance Troupe Performs and Visits Slovakia; Chairman of the Slovak National Council and Chairman of the Administrative Committee Receive Chen Yi and Others
Author:
Date: 1954-08-17
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency, Prague:

    Following their performances and visits in Czech cities such as Prague, Karlovy Vary, Pilsen, České Budějovice, Brno, and Hodonín, the Chinese People's Liberation Army Song and Dance Troupe, currently visiting Czechoslovakia, arrived in Bratislava, the capital of Slovakia, on the 12th, beginning their performances and visits in various parts of Slovakia. They received a warm welcome from the Communist Party, government, army, literary and art circles, and the general public of Bratislava upon their arrival.

    On the evening of the 12th, the Song and Dance Troupe held its first performance in Slovakia on the banks of the Danube River. Those who attended the performance included: First Secretary of the Central Committee of the Slovak Communist Party, K. Bačílek, Chairman of the Slovak National Council, F. Kubáč, Chairman of the Slovak Administrative Committee, R. Strechaj, and other leading figures from the local Party, government, and army, as well as people from Slovak literary and art circles and other sectors, totaling more than 10,000 people.

    Secretary of the Bratislava City Committee of the Slovak Communist Party, Tokar, and the head of the Song and Dance Troupe, Chen Yi, delivered speeches successively. Chen Yi presented a banner signed by all members of the Song and Dance Troupe to the Slovak people, and wished them new achievements in the cause of building socialism.

    The nearly three-and-a-half-hour performance was warmly welcomed by the audience.

    Furthermore: The Song and Dance Troupe's performances and visits in the Czech Republic received high praise from the Czech people. On the eve of the Song and Dance Troupe's departure for Slovakia, the Deputy Prime Minister and Minister of Culture of the Czechoslovak Republic, Kopecký, sent a telegram to the Song and Dance Troupe on the 11th, saying, "I sincerely congratulate all comrades of your Song and Dance Troupe, and congratulate you on your exceptionally great success. We all thank the Song and Dance Troupe for its tremendous cultural and political work in various parts of the Czech Republic. We wish you equally great victories in Slovakia."
   【Xinhua News Agency, Prague, 15th】 Chairman of the Slovak National Council, F. Kubáč, and Chairman of the Slovak Administrative Committee, R. Strechaj, received the head of the Chinese People's Liberation Army Song and Dance Troupe, Chen Yi, and other responsible persons of the Song and Dance Troupe on the 12th, and held a cordial conversation for nearly an hour.

    During the reception, Chen Yi presented gifts on behalf of Commander-in-Chief Zhu De of the Chinese People's Liberation Army to Kubáč and Strechaj.

    Hu Wei, Deputy Military Attaché of the Chinese Embassy in Czechoslovakia, was present at the reception.

### 我解放军歌舞团到斯洛伐克演出和访问  斯洛伐克民族议会主席和行政委员会主席接见陈沂等

1954-08-17
第4版()
专栏：

    我解放军歌舞团到斯洛伐克演出和访问
    斯洛伐克民族议会主席和行政委员会主席接见陈沂等
    据新华社布拉格讯：现在捷克斯洛伐克访问的中国人民解放军歌舞团在布拉格、卡罗维发利、比尔森、布德约维泽、布尔诺、荷图宁等捷克城市演出和访问后，已于十二日到达斯洛伐克首府布拉迪斯拉发，开始了他们在斯洛伐克各地的演出和访问。他们在抵达时受到了布拉迪斯拉发共产党、政府、军队、文艺界和广大群众的热烈欢迎。
    十二日晚间，歌舞团在多瑙河畔举行在斯洛伐克的首次演出。到场观看演出的有：斯洛伐克共产党中央委员会第一书记卡·巴契列克，斯洛伐克民族议会主席弗·库巴奇，斯洛伐克行政委员会主席鲁·斯特雷哈伊和当地其他党、政府和军队的领导人，斯洛伐克文艺界及其他各界人士共一万多人。
    斯洛伐克共产党布拉迪斯拉发市委员会书记托卡尔和歌舞团团长陈沂先后致词。陈沂把有歌舞团全体人员签名的锦旗一面，赠给斯洛伐克人民，并祝他们在建设社会主义的事业中取得新的成就。
    将近三个半小时的演出，受到观众热烈的欢迎。
    又讯：歌舞团这次在捷演出和访问活动，得到捷人民很高的评价。在歌舞团赴斯洛伐克前夕，捷克斯洛伐克共和国副总理兼文化部部长柯别茨基于十一日致电歌舞团说，衷心地祝贺你们歌舞团的全体同志们，祝贺你们异常巨大的成功。我们都感谢歌舞团在捷克各地的巨大的文化和政治工作。我们祝你们在斯洛伐克也得到同样巨大的胜利。
   【新华社布拉格十五日电】斯洛伐克民族议会主席弗·库巴奇和斯洛伐克行政委员会主席鲁·斯特雷哈伊十二日接见了中国人民解放军歌舞团团长陈沂和歌舞团其他负责人，作了将近一小时的亲切谈话。
    接见时，陈沂代表中国人民解放军朱德总司令向库巴奇和斯特雷哈伊赠送礼品。
    接见时在座的有我国驻捷大使馆副武官胡畏。
