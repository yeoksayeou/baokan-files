Title: China Participates in the Damascus International Fair Exhibition; Exhibition Delegation Departs for Syria in Batches
Author:
Date: 1954-08-31
Page: 1

    China Participates in the Damascus International Fair Exhibition; Exhibition Delegation Departs for Syria in Batches
    (Xinhua News Agency, August 30th) The Chinese Council for the Promotion of International Trade's exhibition delegation for the Damascus International Fair, at the invitation of the Syrian government, led by head of the delegation Zhang Xiancheng and deputy head Huo Liu, has departed for Syria in batches.
    The Damascus International Fair is scheduled to be held from September 2nd to October 1st. China's exhibits total approximately 2,000 items, including various new precision machine tools, mining machinery, agricultural machinery, textile machinery, and large-scale steel, seamless steel pipes, and other new heavy industrial products from our country's First Five-Year Plan of economic construction; a variety of daily necessities for the people; various handicrafts; and China's diverse traditional export goods, such as silk, carpets, tea, eggs, grain, oils, minerals, and various industrial raw materials and local specialty products. The exhibition will showcase clothing and utensils of China's ethnic minorities, models of water conservancy projects such as the Huai River project, the Jingjiang River flood diversion project, and the Yellow River irrigation project in Jiwei, as well as numerous photographs and statistical charts reflecting the life and economic construction of the Chinese people.
    Through this exhibition, mutual understanding and friendship between the peoples of China and Syria, and between China and the people of various Middle Eastern countries, will be enhanced, and it will contribute to the development of peaceful and friendly trade between countries on the basis of equality and mutual benefit.

### 我国参加大马士革国际博览会的展览  展览工作团分批前往叙利亚

1954-08-31
第1版()
专栏：

    我国参加大马士革国际博览会的展览
    展览工作团分批前往叙利亚
    【新华社三十日讯】中国国际贸易促进委员会应邀参加叙利亚首都大马士革国际博览会展览工作团，在团长张先成、副团长霍流率领下，已分批启程前往叙利亚。
    大马士革国际博览会原定九月二日到十月一日举行。这次中国参加展出的展品共约两千种，展品中有各种新型的精密的工作母机、矿山机械、农业机械、纺织机械和大型钢材、无缝钢管等我国开始五年计划经济建设以来重工业方面的新产品；有各种人民生活日用品；有各种手工艺品；还有我国多种多样的传统出口物资，如：丝绸、地毯、茶叶、蛋品、粮食、油类、矿产品及各种工业原料和土特产。展览会上将展出我国兄弟民族的服装、用具，治淮、荆江分洪和引黄灌溉济卫等水利工程模型，以及反映我国人民生活及经济建设的照片、统计图表多幅。
    通过这次展览，将增加中、叙两国人民以及中国和中东各国人民间的相互了解与友谊，并将有助于各国在平等互利的基础上发展和平友好的贸易。
