Title: Chilean Delegation Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1954-08-12
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, August 10th: The Chilean delegation, invited by the Chinese People's Association for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries to visit China, arrived in Beijing by plane at 4 p.m. on August 10th, led by the head of the delegation, Salvador Allende.  Those who went to the airport to welcome them included Chu Tunan, President of the Chinese People's Association for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries; Wu Huazhi, Deputy Secretary-General; Ke Bonian, Vice-Chairman of the China Political Science and Law Association; Lei Jieqiong, Deputy Secretary-General; Fang Shishan, Secretary-General of the Chinese Medical Association; Wu Maosun, Secretary-General of the Chinese People's Institute of Foreign Affairs; Zhang Jin, Deputy Director of the Preparatory Committee of the China Building Workers' Union; and representatives from the Architectural Society of China and other organizations.

The list of the Chilean delegation is as follows:

    Head of Delegation: Salvador Allende, Vice President of the Chilean Senate, President of the Popular Front

    Members:
        Professor Dancha Bussi Allende, Historian
        Aida Figueroa Inzunza, Lawyer
        Sergio Inzunza, Lawyer
        Hernan Sanhueza, Doctor
        Isidoro Lifschitz, Doctor
        Alfonso González Dagnino, Doctor
        Alberto Graf, University Professor, Agronomist
        Abraham Shapira, University Professor, Architect
        Raul Arredondo, University Professor, Architect

### 智利访华代表团到京

1954-08-12
第1版()
专栏：

    智利访华代表团到京
   【新华社十日讯】应中国人民对外文化协会邀请来我国访问的智利访华代表团一行十人，在团长萨尔瓦多·阿伦德率领下，在十日下午四时乘飞机到达北京。前往机场欢迎的有：中国人民对外文化协会会长楚图南、副秘书长吴化之，中国政治法律学会副主席柯柏年、副秘书长雷洁琼，中华医学会秘书长方石珊，中国人民外交学会秘书长吴茂荪，中国建筑工会筹备委员会副主任张进和中国建筑学会等团体的代表。
    智利访华代表团名单如下：
    团长：萨尔瓦多·阿伦德　智利参议院副议
