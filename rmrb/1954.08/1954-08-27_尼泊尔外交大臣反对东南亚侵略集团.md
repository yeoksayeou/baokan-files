Title: Nepalese Foreign Minister Opposes Southeast Asia Aggression Bloc
Author:
Date: 1954-08-27
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency, New Delhi, August 25th: Kathmandu dispatch: Nepalese Foreign Minister Regmi said on the 21st that if the purpose of the Southeast Asia treaty organization meeting held in the Philippines is to pit some Asian countries against other Asian countries, then every Asian country must unite and refuse to participate in this scheme. He said that Nepal welcomes the attitude taken by most Colombo countries against the Western-planned Southeast Asian military bloc.
    Regmi said that Nepal cannot send its troops to overseas battlefields, especially when fighting "our Asian brothers."
    Regmi said that he believes that all Southeast Asian countries, including the People's Republic of China, should consider common defense arrangements without being influenced by external forces in any way. This would eliminate any interference by the West in the affairs of the region, and once this is achieved, Southeast Asia can be spared from war.

### 尼泊尔外交大臣反对东南亚侵略集团

1954-08-27
第4版()
专栏：

    尼泊尔外交大臣反对东南亚侵略集团
   【新华社新德里二十五日电】加德满都消息：尼泊尔外交大臣雷格米二十一日说，如果在菲律宾举行的东南亚公约组织会议的目的是使一些亚洲国家和另外一些亚洲国家对立起来的话，每一个亚洲国家必须联合起来，拒绝参加这个勾当。他说，尼泊尔欢迎大多数科伦坡国家采取的反对西方策划的东南亚军事集团的态度。
    雷格米说，尼泊尔不能派自己的军队到海外战场去，尤其是在与“我们的亚洲兄弟”作战的时候。
    雷格米说，他认为所有东南亚国家，包括中华人民共和国在内，应该考虑共同的防御安排，而不在任何方面受外界的影响。这将排除西方对这个地区的事务的任何干预，做到了这一步，东南亚就可以免于战争。
