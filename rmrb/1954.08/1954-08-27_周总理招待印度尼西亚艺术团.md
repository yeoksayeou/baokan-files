Title: Premier Zhou Hosts Indonesian Art Troupe
Author:
Date: 1954-08-27
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, August 26th—Premier Zhou Enlai of the State Council of the Central People's Government held a reception at Huairen Hall on the evening of August 26th to entertain the Indonesian Art Troupe.

The entire Indonesian Art Troupe, headed by团长孟阿达斯·纳苏申, attended the reception.

Also present at the reception were the Indonesian Ambassador to China, Mononutu, and embassy staff.

Present to accompany the guests were Vice Premiers of the State Council of the Central People's Government Guo Moruo and Deng Xiaoping, Secretary-General Xi Zhongxun, Deputy Director of the Cultural and Educational Committee Lu Dingyi, Minister of Culture Shen Yanbing, Vice Ministers Ding Xilin and Zheng Zhenduo, Vice Minister of Foreign Affairs Zhang Hanfu, Mayor of Beijing Peng Zhen, and Deputy Mayor Wu Han.

Also present to accompany the guests were Chu Tunan, President of the Chinese People's Association for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries, Vice Presidents Yang Hansheng and Hong Shen, Vice Chairman of the Chinese People's Committee for World Peace Liao Chengzhi, and famous writers, dancers, musicians, directors, actors, and painters from the capital's literary and artistic circles.

At the reception, Premier Zhou Enlai, Ambassador Mononutu, and Troupe Leader Mengadas Nasution delivered speeches (full texts to be published separately). The hosts and guests continuously toasted to the friendship between the people of China and Indonesia and to the maintenance of Asian and world peace.

  　The reception proceeded in a friendly and warm atmosphere.

### 周总理招待印度尼西亚艺术团

1954-08-27
第1版()
专栏：

    周总理招待印度尼西亚艺术团
    【新华社二十六日讯】中央人民政府政务院总理周恩来二十六日晚在怀仁堂举行酒会，招待印度尼西亚艺术团。
    以孟阿达斯·纳苏申团长为首的印度尼西亚艺术团全体人员出席了酒会。
    出席酒会的，还有印度尼西亚驻华大使莫诺努图和使馆人员。
    出席作陪的，有中央人民政府政务院副总理郭沫若、邓小平，秘书长习仲勋，文化教育委员会副主任陆定一，文化部部长沈雁冰，副部长丁西林、郑振铎，外交部副部长章汉夫，北京市人民政府市长彭真、副市长吴晗。
    出席作陪的，还有中国人民对外文化协会会长楚图南，副会长阳翰笙、洪深，中国人民保卫世界和平委员会副主席廖承志，首都文学艺术界的著名作家、舞蹈家、音乐家、导演、演员和画家等。
    酒会上，周恩来总理、莫诺努图大使、孟阿达斯·纳苏申团长先后讲话（全文另发）。宾主不断为中国和印度尼西亚两国人民之间的友好，为维护亚洲及世界和平而干杯。
  　酒会在友好、热烈的空气中进行。
