Title: Hu Yaobang Hosts Reception for Delegates to the World Federation of Democratic Youth Council Meeting

Author:

Date: 1954-08-18
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, August 17th: Hu Yaobang, Secretary of the Secretariat of the China New Democratic Youth League, held a tea party on the afternoon of the 17th to host youth representatives from various countries attending the Beijing meeting of the World Federation of Democratic Youth Council. The Nepalese representative, who had just arrived in Beijing on the 16th, also attended this tea party.

　　Hu Yaobang first, on behalf of the Chinese youth, invited the representatives attending this meeting to stay in China for visits and to understand how the Chinese people and youth are carrying out peaceful labor to build their great country, and how they are fighting against the backward phenomena left over from the old society. Then he introduced the construction situation of China's industry, agriculture, culture and education, and the situation of Chinese youth. Finally, he also briefly elaborated on the prospects of the happy life of the Chinese people and youth in building socialism. The representatives listened to Hu Yaobang's introduction with joy.

### 胡耀邦招待世界青联理事会会议各国代表

1954-08-18
第1版()
专栏：

    胡耀邦招待世界青联理事会会议各国代表
    【新华社十七日讯】中国新民主主义青年团书记处书记胡耀邦在十七日下午举行茶话会，招待参加世界民主青年联盟理事会北京会议的各国青年代表。十六日刚刚到达北京的尼泊尔代表，也参加了这个茶话会。
    胡耀邦首先代表中国青年邀请参加这次会议的代表留中国参观访问，来了解中国人民和青年是怎样地在进行和平劳动，以建设自己的伟大国家，以及他们是怎样地在和旧社会遗留下来的落后现象作斗争。接着他介绍了中国工业、农业、文化教育等方面的建设情况以及中国青年的情况。最后，他并简明地阐述了中国人民和青年建设社会主义的幸福生活的远景。代表们高兴地听取了胡耀邦的介绍。
