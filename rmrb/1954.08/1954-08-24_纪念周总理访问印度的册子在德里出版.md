Title: Booklet Commemorating Premier Zhou's Visit to India Published in Delhi
Author:
Date: 1954-08-24
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, New Delhi, August 22nd: The People's Publishing House in Delhi, India, has published a booklet commemorating Premier Zhou Enlai's visit to India, entitled "Zhou Enlai in India." The booklet contains multiple photos of Premier Zhou and includes the texts of Premier Zhou's speeches delivered in India, reports on Premier Zhou's activities in India, and the joint statement of the Prime Ministers of China and India.

### 纪念周总理访问印度的册子在德里出版

1954-08-24
第4版()
专栏：

    纪念周总理访问印度的册子在德里出版
    【新华社新德里二十二日电】　印度德里人民出版社出版了一种纪念周恩来总理访问印度的小册子，题目是：“周恩来在印度”。小册子刊载了周总理的照片多幅，并刊载了周总理在印度历次发表的演说，关于周总理在印度的行动的报道和中印两国总理的联合声明。
