Title: National Wheat Production Plan Overfulfilled This Year; Total Output Increased by 20.7 Percent Over Last Year
Author:
Date: 1954-08-05
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, 4th. The national wheat production increase plan for this year has been overfulfilled. According to statistics and estimates from the Ministry of Agriculture of the Central People's Government, the national wheat planting area is more than 400,846,000 mu, an expansion of more than 24 million mu compared to last year; the yield per unit area increased by 13.3 percent compared to last year; and the total output exceeded this year's predetermined plan by 3.9 percent, an increase of 20.7 percent compared to last year. Among them, winter wheat production accounted for 86.4 percent and has been successively harvested; spring wheat production accounted for 13.6 percent, and harvesting is very intensive and will soon be completed.

### 今年国家小麦增产计划超额完成  总产量比去年增加百分之二十点七

1954-08-05
第1版()
专栏：

    今年国家小麦增产计划超额完成
    总产量比去年增加百分之二十点七
    【新华社四日讯】今年国家小麦增产计划超额完成。据中央人民政府农业部的统计和估计，全国小麦种植面积四亿零八百四十六万多亩，比去年扩大二千四百多万亩；单位面积产量比去年增加百分之十三点三；总产量超过今年预定计划百分之三点九，比去年增加百分之二十点七。其中冬小麦产量占百分之八十六点四，已陆续收割完毕；春小麦产量占百分之十三点六，收割正很紧张，不久可以结束。
