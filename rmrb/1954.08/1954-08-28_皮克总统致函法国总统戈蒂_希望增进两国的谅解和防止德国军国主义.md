Title: President Pieck Sends Letter to French President Coty Hoping to Enhance Understanding Between the Two Countries and Prevent German Militarism
Author:
Date: 1954-08-28
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Berlin, August 27th: According to the German News Agency, Berlin, August 26th, Wilhelm Pieck, President of the German Democratic Republic, sent a letter to René Coty, President of the French Republic, hoping to enhance understanding between the peace-loving people of Germany and France, prevent the danger of new German militarism, and promote the solution of the German question in accordance with the interests of lasting peace in Europe.

The letter stated: The German people oppose Adenauer's armament and war preparation policy. The German people do not want war; they want peace and friendship with the French people and the people of other countries.

The letter stated: The people of Germany and France are equally concerned about safeguarding peace in Europe. European peace is of vital importance to Germany and France. There is a path that can provide genuine security for all European countries, and that is to conclude an all-European collective security treaty.

President Pieck finally assured President Coty that the German Democratic Republic will make every effort to strengthen the ideas of friendship and peace among the German people, prevent West German militarists from exerting political pressure on France, and prevent them from preparing new wars of revenge.

### 皮克总统致函法国总统戈蒂  希望增进两国的谅解和防止德国军国主义

1954-08-28
第4版()
专栏：

    皮克总统致函法国总统戈蒂
    希望增进两国的谅解和防止德国军国主义
    【新华社二十七日讯】据德意志通讯社柏林二十六日讯：德意志民主共和国总统威廉·皮克致函法兰西共和国总统雷纳·戈蒂，希望增进德国和法国爱好和平的人民之间的谅解、防止新的德国军国主义的危险和促使按照欧洲持久和平的利益来解决德国问题。
    信中说：德国人民反对阿登纳的武装和备战政策。德国人民不要战争，他们要和平，要与法国人民和其他各国人民的友谊。
    信中说：德法两国人民同样关心保障欧洲和平。欧洲和平和德、法两国利害攸关。有一条道路可以使一切欧洲国家获得真正的安全，这就是订立全欧集体安全条约。
    皮克总统最后向戈蒂总统保证：德意志民主共和国将尽一切努力在德国人民中加强友谊和和平的观念，阻止西德军国主义分子向法国施行政治压力，阻止他们准备新的复仇战争。
