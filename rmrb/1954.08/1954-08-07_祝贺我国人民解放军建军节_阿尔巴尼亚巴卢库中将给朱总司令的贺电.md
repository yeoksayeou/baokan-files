Title: Congratulations on the Anniversary of the Founding of the Chinese People's Liberation Army: Telegram from Lieutenant General Baluku of Albania to Commander-in-Chief Zhu

Author:
Date: 1954-08-07
Page: 4

Congratulations on the Anniversary of the Founding of the Chinese People's Liberation Army
Telegram from Lieutenant General Baluku of Albania to Commander-in-Chief Zhu De

Beijing, Commander-in-Chief of the Chinese People's Liberation Army, Zhu De:

On the occasion of the 27th anniversary of the founding of the Chinese People's Liberation Army, please allow me, on behalf of the Albanian People's Army and myself, to extend to you and the heroic Chinese People's Liberation Army the most heartfelt and fraternal congratulations.

The Albanian People's Army sincerely wishes the great Chinese People's Army further achievements in military training, political education, and combat drills, for the benefit of the brotherly Chinese people and the defense of world peace.

Lieutenant General Beqir Balluku, Minister of National Defense of the People's Republic of Albania
Tirana, July 31, 1954

### 祝贺我国人民解放军建军节  阿尔巴尼亚巴卢库中将给朱总司令的贺电

1954-08-07
第4版()
专栏：

    祝贺我国人民解放军建军节
    阿尔巴尼亚巴卢库中将给朱总司令的贺电北京中国人民解放军总司令朱德：
    值此中国人民解放军建军二十七周年之际，请允许我代表阿尔巴尼亚人民军和我本人向您以及英勇的中国人民解放军致以最衷心的兄弟般的祝贺。
    阿尔巴尼亚人民军衷心地愿伟大中国人民的军队在造福于兄弟的中国人民与保卫世界和平的军事训练、政治教育和战斗教练中取得进一步的成就。
                                阿尔巴尼亚人民共和国国防部部长贝基尔·巴卢库中将
   一九五四年七月三十一日于地拉那
