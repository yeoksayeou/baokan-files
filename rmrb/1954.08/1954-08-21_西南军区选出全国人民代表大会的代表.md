Title: Southwest Military Region Elects Deputies to the National People's Congress
Author:
Date: 1954-08-21
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Chongqing, August 20th. The Southwest Military Region of the Chinese People's Liberation Army convened a meeting of military representative conference from the 15th to the 17th. A total of 151 representatives attended the meeting, including representatives from troops guarding the Yunnan border, troops building the Kangzang Highway, troops undergoing regular training, public security forces, as well as representatives from various positions in the army's literary, medical, and educational work. There were 55 combat heroes, work models, people's meritorious workers, and model Youth League members, 18 ethnic minority cadres and soldiers, and eight female comrades.

The meeting heard the summary report of the Southwest Military Region Election Committee on the election work of the Southwest Military Region troops. The report pointed out that more than 98 percent of the voters in the entire military region participated in the election. Wang Xinting, Deputy Political Commissar of the Southwest Military Region, delivered a report on the draft Constitution of the People's Republic of China at the meeting. All the representatives earnestly discussed the draft Constitution. Combat heroes Wei Xiaotang and Shi Lailiang, representing the Yunnan border defense soldiers, stated in their speeches: "We must strive to defend the border and build the border." Gao Fuyin, a representative of the road construction troops, said: "We must resolutely support the provisions on ethnic groups in the draft Constitution, build the highway to Lhasa as soon as possible, and help the Tibetan people develop their political, economic, and cultural undertakings." Zeng Que Zha, deputy head of the Tibetan People's Troupe, said at the meeting: "From the birth and growth of the Tibetan People's Troupe, I have more deeply realized the correctness and greatness of the Party and Chairman Mao's ethnic policies." The meeting unanimously adopted a resolution in support of the draft Constitution of the People's Republic of China.

The representatives attending the meeting elected He Long, Wang Xinting, He Bingyan, Chen Xilian, Zhang Yingcai (combat hero), and Liu Zilin (combat hero) as deputies to the National People's Congress by secret ballot.

### 西南军区选出全国人民代表大会的代表

1954-08-21
第1版()
专栏：

    西南军区选出全国人民代表大会的代表
   【新华社重庆二十日电】中国人民解放军西南军区在十五日到十七日召开了军人代表大会会议。出席会议的代表共一百五十一人，内有守卫云南边疆的部队、修筑康藏公路的部队、进行正规训练的部队、公安部队以及部队的文艺、医务、教育工作等各个岗位上的代表，有战斗英雄、工作模范、人民功臣、模范青年团员五十五人，兄弟民族干部、战士十八人和八位女同志。
    会议听了西南军区选举委员会关于西南军区部队选举工作的总结报告。报告指出全军区部队百分之九十八以上的选民参加了选举。西南军区副政治委员王新亭在会议上作了关于中华人民共和国宪法草案的报告。全体代表认真地讨论了宪法草案。战斗英雄卫小堂、时来亮代表云南边防战士在发言中表示：要努力保卫边疆、建设边疆。筑路部队的代表高福印说：“我们要坚决拥护宪法草案中关于民族方面的规定，早日把公路修到拉萨，帮助藏族人民发展政治、经济、文化事业。”藏民团副团长曾却札在会上说：从藏民团的诞生和成长，我更加深刻地体会到党和毛主席的民族政策的正确和伟大。会议一致通过了拥护中华人民共和国宪法草案的决议。
    出席会议的代表以无记名的投票方式，选出贺龙、王新亭、贺炳炎、陈锡联、张英才（战斗英雄）、刘子林（战斗英雄）六人为全国人民代表大会代表。
