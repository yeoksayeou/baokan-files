Title: President of the Chinese People's Institute of Foreign Affairs, Zhang Xiruo, Hosts a Banquet for the British Labour Party Delegation
Author:
Date: 1954-08-15
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, August 14th: Zhang Xiruo, President of the Chinese People's Institute of Foreign Affairs, held a reception on August 14th to welcome the British Labour Party delegation led by Mr. Clement Attlee, who are visiting our country at our invitation.

    Attending the reception were delegation members Mr. Aneurin Bevan, Mrs. Eirene White, Mr. Wilfred Burke, Mr. Morgan Phillips, Mr. Stephen Watson, Mr. Harold Earnshaw, Mr. Henry Franklin, and the delegation's translators, Mr. and Mrs. Michael Lindsay.

    Also attending the reception was Mr. Humphrey Trevelyan, the British Chargé d'Affaires to China.

    On the Chinese side, those attending the reception included: Vice Presidents of the Chinese People's Institute of Foreign Affairs Zhou Gengsheng, Chen Hansheng, Hu Yuzhi, Qian Duansheng, Qiao Guanhua, and Secretary-General Wu Maosun, among others.

    Also attending the reception were: Premier of the State Council of the Central People's Government Zhou Enlai, Vice Premiers Guo Moruo and Huang Yanpei, Vice Chairmen of the National Committee of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference Li Jishen and Chen Shutong, Vice President of the Supreme People's Court of the Central People's Government Zhang Zhiran, Secretary-General of the State Council Xi Zhongxun, Political Committee members Xie Juezai, Zeng Shan, Teng Daiyuan, Zhang Bojun, Chen Shaoxian, Shao Lizi, Huang Shaohong, and the heads and deputy heads of the various commissions, ministries, and administrations under it: Ye Jizhuang, Shen Yanbing, Huang Jing, Fu Zuoyi, Li Dequan, Ulanhu, Chen Qiyuan, Lei Renmin, He Changgong, Lü Zhengcao, Li Baohua, Yang Xiufeng, Wei Que, He Cheng, Wei Wenbo, An Ziwen, Ding Xilin, Vice Ministers of Foreign Affairs Zhang Hanfu and Wu Xiuquan, Mayor of Beijing Peng Zhen, and Vice Mayors Zhang Youyu and Wu Han, among others.

    Also attending the reception were Central Committee members of the Communist Party of China Deng Zihui and Liao Chengzhi, representatives of various democratic parties and non-party democratic人士 Cai Tingkai, Luo Longji, Sun Qimeng, Ma Yinchu, Xu Guangping, Peng Zemin, Chen Qiyou, Xu Deheng, Li Chunqing, and Luo Yi, among others.

    Also attending the reception were figures from the scientific and educational communities Li Siguang, Liang Xi, Qian Sanqiang, Hua Luogeng, Chen Da, Jiang Nanxiang, Tang Yongtong, Zhou Peiyuan, Ye Qisun, Jin Yuelin, Chen Daisun, Zhou Binglin, Zhu Guangqian, Liang Sicheng, Fu Zhongsun, Jin Baoshan, Dai Fanglan, Li Zong'en, Zhang Xiaoqian, Yan Jingyao, Lou Bangyan, Pan Guangdan, Mao Yisheng, Lu Zhiwei, and Fang Shishan, figures from the literary and art and news communities Zhou Yang, Lao She, Ouyang Yuqian, Cao Yu, Tian Han, Zhang Geng, Zhao Shuli, Zhao Feng, Ma Ke, Hua Junwu, Liu Kaiqu, Ma Yanxiang, Ye Shengla, Du Jinfang, Deng Tuo, Mei Yi, Wu Wentao, Chen Yongwen, Shen Jiantu, and Pu Xixiu, trade union workers and model workers Liu Ningyi, Zhu Xuefan, Li Jiebo, Liu Zijiu, Zhang Weizhen, Li Yong, Liu Yingyuan, Hao Jianxiu, Hang Peilan, Chen Sulan, Liu Dezhen, Ma Liuha, Guo Shude, Gu Faming, figures from the industrial and commercial sectors Sha Qianli, Wang Zhixin, Le Songsheng, and Ling Qijun, representatives of minority nationalities Pingcuo Wangjie, Pengsike, Ganchunlei, and Ke Jiyi, figures from religious circles Wu Yaozong, Chen Jianzhen, Chen Chonggui, Zhao Zichen, Wang Zizhong, Jiang Yizhen, Zhao Fusan, Da Pusheng, Ma Songting, and Ju Zan, as well as people from all walks of life Qian Junrui, Chu Tunan, Ji Chao Ding, Wu Yunfu, Liu Guanyi, Liang Shuming, Zhang Shizhao, Liu Wang Liming, Qian Changzhao, and heads of various people's organizations, among others.

    The foreign envoys to China attending the reception were: Burmese Ambassador U Hla Maung, Pakistani Ambassador Raza, Indian Ambassador N. Raghavan, Swedish Ambassador Wistrand, Romanian Ambassador Teodor Rudenco, Ambassador of the German Democratic Republic Koenig, Danish Minister Grippenfeldt, Chargé d'Affaires ad interim of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam Zhou Liang, Chargé d'Affaires ad interim of Czechoslovakia Šmíd, Chargé d'Affaires ad interim of Hungary Czatórdai, Chargé d'Affaires ad interim of Bulgaria Grigorov, Chargé d'Affaires ad interim of Finland Seppälä, Chargé d'Affaires ad interim of Switzerland Curchod, Chargé d'Affaires ad interim of Mongolia Lubsanchultem, Chargé d'Affaires ad interim of Poland Gula, Counselor of the Soviet Embassy in China Skachkov, Counselor of the Embassy of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea Seo Cheol, Dutch Negotiation Representative Smits, and Norwegian Negotiation Representative Timmermans.

    Also attending the reception were foreign journalists currently in Beijing.

    More than four hundred people attended the reception.

    The reception proceeded in a friendly atmosphere. (Attached picture)

### 中国人民外交学会会长张奚若  设宴招待英国工党代表团

1954-08-15
第1版()
专栏：

    中国人民外交学会会长张奚若
    设宴招待英国工党代表团
    【新华社十四日讯】中国人民外交学会会长张奚若八月十四日举行酒会，招待应邀前来我国访问的以克·艾德礼先生为首的英国工党代表团。
    出席酒会的，有：代表团团员安·比万先生、埃·萨末斯基尔夫人、韦·伯克先生、摩·菲利普斯先生、塞·华生先生、哈·厄恩萧先生、亨·弗兰克林先生和代表团翻译林迈可夫妇。
    出席欢迎酒会的，还有英国驻我国代办杜维廉。
    中国方面出席酒会的，有：中国人民外交学会副会长周鲠生、陈翰笙、胡愈之、钱端升、乔冠华，秘书长吴茂荪等。
