Title: CPC Central Committee's Message to the Central Committee of the Romanian Workers' Party
Author: 
Date: 1954-08-23
Page: 1

    CPC Central Committee's Message to the Central Committee of the Romanian Workers' Party

    To the Central Committee of the Romanian Workers' Party:

    On the occasion of the tenth anniversary of the liberation of Romania, the Central Committee of the Communist Party of China extends warm, fraternal congratulations to the Romanian Workers' Party and the Romanian people.

    Over the past ten years, under the leadership of the Romanian Workers' Party, the Romanian people have crushed all sabotage plots of domestic and foreign reactionaries, victoriously carried out various democratic reforms and socialist transformation and construction, and achieved brilliant successes. The Chinese people are sincerely pleased with the achievements of the Romanian people in political, economic, and cultural fields and extend fraternal care.

    The Central Committee of the Communist Party of China wishes the Romanian Workers' Party further victories in leading the Romanian people in socialist construction and safeguarding world peace.

                                       The Central Committee of the Communist Party of China
                               August 21, 1954, Beijing

### 中共中央致罗工人党中央电

1954-08-23
第1版()
专栏：

    中共中央致罗工人党中央电罗马尼亚工人党中央委员会：
    值此罗马尼亚解放十周年之际，中国共产党中央委员会谨向罗马尼亚工人党和罗马尼亚人民致以热烈的、兄弟般的祝贺。
    十年来，罗马尼亚人民在罗马尼亚工人党的领导下，粉碎了国内外反动派的一切破坏阴谋，胜利地进行了各种民主改革和社会主义改造与建设，并获得辉煌的成就。中国人民对罗马尼亚人民在政治、经济、文化各方面的成就衷心地感到愉快并寄以兄弟般的关怀。
    中国共产党中央委员会祝罗马尼亚工人党在领导罗马尼亚人民进行社会主义建设和维护世界和平的事业中获得进一步的胜利。
                                       中国共产党中央委员会
                               一九五四年八月二十一日  北京
