Title: Lieutenant General István Bata of Hungary Sends a Telegram to Commander-in-Chief Zhu De
Author:
Date: 1954-08-01
Page: 1

    To Comrade Zhu De, Commander-in-Chief of the Chinese People's Liberation Army:
    On the occasion of the great festival of the Chinese people—the anniversary of the founding of the Chinese People's Liberation Army, please allow me, Comrade Commander-in-Chief, on behalf of all officers and soldiers of the Hungarian People's Army, to extend warm congratulations and greetings to you, and through you to all commanders and fighters of the Chinese People's Liberation Army. At the same time, I would like to personally wish the Chinese army further achievements in safeguarding their motherland and the cause of socialist construction of the great Chinese people.
         Lieutenant General István Bata, Minister of National Defence of the Hungarian People's Republic

### 匈牙利伊斯特万·巴塔中将致电朱总司令

1954-08-01
第1版()
专栏：

    匈牙利伊斯特万·巴塔中将致电朱总司令中国人民解放军总司令朱德同志：
    值此中国人民的伟大节日——中国人民解放军建军节之际，请允许我，总司令同志，以匈牙利人民军全体官兵的名义向您，并通过您向中国人民解放军全体指挥员和战斗员致以热烈的祝贺和敬礼。同时我谨以个人的名义敬祝中国军队在保卫自己祖国和伟大的中国人民的社会主义建设的事业中，获得更进一步的成就。
         匈牙利人民共和国国防部长  伊斯特万·巴塔中将
