Title: World Federation of Democratic Youth Council Issues Communiqué
Author:
Date: 1954-08-17
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, August 16th: The General Secretary of the World Federation of Democratic Youth, Jacques Denis, held a press conference on the afternoon of August 16th. Reporters from Beijing and Tianjin, China, and foreign reporters stationed in Beijing attended. Jacques Denis first thanked the Chinese people for providing favorable conditions for the work of this conference, thus ensuring its smooth progress. Denis then introduced the progress of the conference and its major achievements, and answered questions raised by reporters. Finally, Denis, on behalf of the World Federation of Democratic Youth Council, issued a press communiqué.

Xinhua News Agency, August 16th: Press Communiqué of the World Federation of Democratic Youth Council:

The Council meeting of the World Federation of Democratic Youth, which opened on August 9th, has just concluded its meeting in an atmosphere of indescribable enthusiasm.

The meeting was warmly welcomed in Beijing by the glorious Chinese youth.

Two hundred and sixty-three delegates representing sixty-eight countries participated in this meeting, including fifty-one observers representing various opinions and religious beliefs, and eighteen guests, who participated in the important discussions of the Council.

Regarding the first item on the agenda - the movement of youth in colonial and dependent countries for the rights of youth, national independence, and peace - sixty-five delegates representing fifty-five countries and three international youth organizations spoke. Regarding the second item on the agenda, the convening of the International Rural Youth Conference, thirty delegates representing twenty-eight countries and one international youth organization spoke. Regarding the third item on the agenda - the Fifth World Youth and Student Festival for Peace and Friendship - thirty delegates representing twenty-eight countries spoke.

Twenty-four new organizations from seventeen countries have joined the World Federation of Democratic Youth, thus strengthening our powerful organization and bringing the membership of the World Federation of Democratic Youth to ninety-seven countries, with a membership of eighty-five million.

At the closing session of the meeting, the Council unanimously adopted a declaration for peace and friendship, for easing international tensions, for the peaceful resolution of all outstanding disputes, and for collective security in Europe.

At the same time, an appeal was issued to young men and women of all countries, calling on them to strengthen their struggle and solidarity, to oppose colonialism, to strengthen solidarity with the youth of colonial and dependent countries, and to fight for the true independence of colonial peoples and for the end of colonial wars, such as the war in Malaya. The Beijing meeting of the Council concluded with a moving declaration calling for unity and guaranteeing future victory.

The Council pledged its full support for the International Rural Youth Conference and called for greater efforts in the preparations for the International Rural Youth Conference in the short period before the conference which will be held in Vienna from December 9th to 15th, 1954.

Finally, in a generally warm atmosphere, the Council accepted the enthusiastic invitation of Polish youth to hold the Fifth World Youth and Student Festival for Peace and Friendship in Warsaw. The Council announced that the Fifth Festival will be held in Warsaw at the end of July 1955.

### 世界青联理事会发表公报

1954-08-17
第1版()
专栏：

    世界青联理事会发表公报
   【新华社十六日讯】世界民主青年联盟总书记雅克·德尼在十六日下午举行记者招待会。到会的有中国北京、天津的记者和驻北京的各外国记者。雅克·德尼首先感谢中国人民对这次会议的工作提供了有利条件，从而保证了会议的顺利进行。接着德尼介绍了会议进行的情况和会议所获得的重大成就，并回答了记者们提出的问题。最后，德尼代表世界民主青年联盟理事会发表了新闻公报。
   【新华社十六日讯】世界民主青年联盟理事会新闻公报：
    八月九日召开的世界民主青年联盟理事会刚在难以形容的热烈的气氛中结束了它的会议。
    会议在北京隆重地受到光荣的中国青年的欢迎。
    代表着六十八个国家的二百六十三位代表参加了这次会议，其中有五十一位代表着各种见解和宗教信仰的列席观察员，和十八位来宾，参加了这次理事会的重要讨论。
    关于第一项议程——殖民地和附属国青年为青年的权利、民族独立与和平而斗争的运动的问题，有六十五个代表着五十五个国家和三个国际青年组织的代表发言。关于召开国际农村青年大会的第二项议程有代表着二十八个国家和一个国际青年组织的三十位代表发言。关于第三项议程——第五届世界青年与学生和平友谊联欢节有代表着二十八个国家的三十位代表发言。
    有十七个国家的二十四个新的组织加入了世界民主青年联盟，这样，加强了我们的强大的组织，并使世界民主青年联盟会员国达到九十七个国家，会员人数达八千五百万人。
    在会议的闭幕式上，理事会一致通过一个为和平与友谊，为缓和国际紧张局势，为和平解决所有悬而未决的纠纷和为欧洲的集体安全而斗争的宣言。
    同时也发出了一个告各国青年男女号召书，号召他们加强他们的斗争与团结，来反对殖民主义，加强与殖民地和附属国青年之间的团结，为殖民地人民真正的独立，及结束殖民战争，如在马来亚的战争而斗争。理事会北京会议以号召团结、以保证未来胜利的动人的宣言结束。
    理事会保证以全力支持国际农村青年大会，并号召在一九五四年十二月九日至十五日将在维也纳召开的国际农村青年大会前的短短的时期内，以更大的努力来进行国际农村青年大会的筹备工作。
    最后，在普遍热烈的气氛中，理事会接受了波兰青年热诚的邀请，在华沙举行第五届世界青年与学生和平友谊联欢节，理事会宣告第五届联欢节将在一九五五年七月底在华沙举行。
