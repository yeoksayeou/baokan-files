Title: Mongolian Journalist Deleg Visits Beijing
Author:
Date: 1954-08-27
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency, August 25th: Deleg, the Deputy Editor-in-Chief of the "Truth Daily" of the Mongolian People's Republic, who was invited to visit China according to the 1954 Sino-Mongolian Cultural Cooperation Implementation Plan, began his tour of Beijing on August 21st. He has visited scenic spots such as the Badaling Great Wall and held discussions with responsible persons from the Central People's Government Publishing Administration, People's Daily, and the All-China Democratic Women's Federation. Deleg is about to visit various parts of China.

### 蒙古记者德勒格在京访问

1954-08-27
第4版()
专栏：

    蒙古记者德勒格在京访问
   【新华社二十五日讯】根据中蒙一九五四年文化合作执行计划应邀前来我国访问的蒙古记者本敦扎姆增·德勒格（蒙古人民共和国“真理报”副社长），八月二十一日已开始在京参观访问。他已经参观了八达岭等名胜古迹并和中央人民政府出版总署、人民日报、中华全国民主妇女联合会等单位的负责人进行座谈。德勒格即将前往我国各地访问。
