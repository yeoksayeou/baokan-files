    Our Great Motherland
    The mountains surrounding Anshan have been extensively explored geologically over the past two years, confirming them as a mineral treasure trove. This area has abundant iron ore, dolomite, clay, and silica, minerals essential for the steel industry. This picture shows a surveying team conducting a survey at a certain location in the mining area.

### 我们伟大的祖国（图片）

1954-08-13
第1版()
专栏：

    我们伟大的祖国
    鞍山四周的群山，经过两年来大规模的地质勘探，证实是蕴藏丰富的矿物宝库。这里有大量的钢铁工业不可缺少的铁矿石、白云石、粘土和矽石等矿物。这是测绘工作队在矿区某处进行测绘的情形。
