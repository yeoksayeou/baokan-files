Title: Fortieth Anniversary of World War I: Moscow Newspapers Publish Commemorative Articles
Author:
Date: 1954-08-02
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, August 1st: “People's Daily” devoted an entire page on the 1st to articles about the fortieth anniversary of the First World War. Among them were commemorative articles by German writer Arnold Zweig, British World War I participant Robson, and French General Ernest Petit. “People's Daily” also published Ponomarev's long article: "Lessons of History," and also published photos. Other newspapers such as "Workers’ Daily", "Red Star", and "China Youth Daily" also published commemorative articles.

### 第一次世界大战四十年  莫斯科各报刊载纪念文

1954-08-02
第4版()
专栏：

    第一次世界大战四十年
    莫斯科各报刊载纪念文
    【新华社莫斯科一日电】“真理报”一日以整版篇幅登载了有关第一次世界大战四十年的文章。其中有德国作家阿诺利德·茨维格、英国第一次大战参加者罗布逊和法国将军埃内斯特·佩蒂的纪念文章。“真理报”并刊载了波诺马雷夫的长文：“历史的教训”，还登载了照片。其他“劳动报”、“红星报”、“共青团真理报”等也登载了纪念文章。
