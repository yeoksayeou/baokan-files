Title: Vietnamese People's Army Recovers Towns Including Luc Nam

Author:
Date: 1954-08-14
Page: 4

    Xinhua News Agency, North Vietnam, August 12th: According to the Vietnamese Armistice Agreement, the French Union forces in Indochina have withdrawn from the following towns in the Red River Delta in the past two weeks: Luc Nam City was evacuated on July 28th; Yong'an was evacuated on July 31st; Mang Cai was evacuated on August 1st; Son Tay and Phuc Yen were evacuated on August 3rd; Phu Lang Thuong was evacuated on August 4th; Hung Yen was evacuated on August 5th; and Ninh Giang was evacuated on August 8th.
    The Vietnamese People's Army recovered the above-mentioned towns and was warmly welcomed by the local residents. The policy of the Government of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam towards the newly liberated areas has been announced to the masses and strictly implemented. The People's Administrative Committees in these towns have taken necessary measures to restore the people's lives to normal.

### 越南人民军收复陆南等城镇

1954-08-14
第4版()
专栏：

    越南人民军收复陆南等城镇
    【新华社十二日讯】越南通讯社北越十二日讯：根据越南停战协定，印度支那法兰西联邦部队在过去两周中已自下列的红河三角洲城镇撤退：七月二十八日撤出陆南城；七月三十一日撤出永安；八月一日撤出芒街；八月三日撤出山西和福安；八月四日撤出府谅商；八月五日撤出兴安；八月八日撤出宁江。
    越南人民军收复了上述城镇并受到当地居民的热烈欢迎。越南民主共和国政府对待新解放地区的政策已经向群众宣布并严格执行。这些城镇的人民行政委员会已经采取必要措施使人民的生活恢复正常。
