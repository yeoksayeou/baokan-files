Title: Vice Premier Pham Van Dong Departs from Our Country and Cables Premier Zhou to Express Gratitude
Author:
Date: 1954-08-12
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Munan Pass, 11th: Pham Van Dong, Vice Premier and Acting Minister of Foreign Affairs of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam, arrived at the Sino-Vietnamese border – Munan Pass – at 7:15 AM on the 10th. Accompanying him were: Phan Anh, Minister of Industry and Commerce of the Government of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam, Tran Cong Tuong, Deputy Minister of Justice, and other accompanying members of the delegation.

Accompanied Vice Premier Pham Van Dong were: Wang Tuo, Deputy Director of the Reception Department of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs of the Central People's Government, Xiao Yizhou and Mo Naiqun, Vice Chairmen of the Guangxi Provincial People's Government, Hao Zhongshi, First Deputy Secretary of the Guangxi Provincial Committee of the Chinese Communist Party, and Wang Tan, Director of the Foreign Affairs Office of the Guangxi Provincial People's Government. Liu Wenhua, County Magistrate of Ningming County, Huang Zhiying, Chief of the First District of Ningming County, and Nong Xun, Deputy Secretary of the First District Committee of the Chinese Communist Party of Ningming County, also came to see them off.

The national flags of China and Vietnam were hung high at Munan Pass.

Vice Premier Pham Van Dong and his party were warmly welcomed by the personnel stationed at the pass upon their arrival at Munan Pass.

At 7:20, Vice Premier Pham Van Dong departed from Chinese territory. Upon saying goodbye, he and the well-wishers took a group photo as a memento. The hosts and guests shook hands repeatedly.

Xinhua News Agency, 11th: Premier Zhou Enlai of the State Council of the Central People's Government and concurrently Minister of Foreign Affairs, received a cable from Pham Van Dong, Vice Premier and Acting Minister of Foreign Affairs of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam, on August 10th upon leaving our country. The cable stated: "We have arrived safely at the Sino-Vietnamese border, the journey was smooth, and everyone is in good health. Please accept our heartfelt gratitude."

### 范文同副总理离我国国境并电周总理致谢

1954-08-12
第1版()
专栏：

    范文同副总理离我国国境并电周总理致谢
   【新华社睦南关十一日电】越南民主共和国副总理兼代理外交部部长范文同，在十日晨七时十五分到达中、越国界——睦南关。同行的有：越南民主共和国政府工商部部长潘英、司法部副部长陈公祥，及代表团其他随行人员等。
    陪送范文同副总理的有：中央人民政府外交部交际处副处长王拓，广西省人民政府副主席萧一舟、莫乃群，中共广西省委员会第一副书记郝中士，广西省人民政府外事处处长王谭。宁明县县长刘文华，宁明县第一区区长黄志英，中共宁明县第一区区委副书记农勋等也前来欢送。
    睦南关上高悬着中、越两国国旗。
    范文同副总理等一行到睦南关时，并受到驻关人员的热烈欢迎。
    七时二十分，范文同副总理离开中国国境。告别时，并和欢送者合影留念。宾主一再握手。
    【新华社十一日讯】我中央人民政府政务院周恩来总理兼外交部部长收到了越南民主共和国副总理兼代理外交部部长范文同八月十日在离开我国国境时的来电。电文称：“我们已安抵中越边界，一路顺利，大家身体健康。谨向你表示衷心的感谢。”
