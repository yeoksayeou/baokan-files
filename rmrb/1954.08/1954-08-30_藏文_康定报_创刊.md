Title: Tibetan "Kangding Daily" Launched

Author:
Date: 1954-08-30
Page: 3

Xinhua News Agency

    Tibetan "Kangding Daily" Launched
    The "Kangding Daily," published in Tibetan by the Tibetan Autonomous Region of Xikang Province, was launched on August 23. The Kangding Daily is a four-page newspaper, currently published irregularly. This newspaper will conduct ideological education on ethnic policies, internationalism, and patriotism for the people of the entire region, reflect the construction and development of the autonomous region's politics, economy, and culture, exchange experiences in ethnic work, and guide various tasks in the autonomous region. (Xinhua News Agency)

### 藏文“康定报”创刊

1954-08-30
第3版()
专栏：

    藏文“康定报”创刊
    西康省藏族自治区用藏文出版的“康定报”已在八月二十三日创刊。康定报为四开篇幅，暂为不定期刊。这一报纸将向全区人民进行民族政策、国际主义和爱国主义的思想教育，反映自治区政治、经济、文化的建设和发展情况，交流民族工作经验，指导自治区的各项工作。　（新华社）
