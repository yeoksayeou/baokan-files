Title: Eight Years of Heroic Resistance to Japan Achieve Glorious Victory; Vietnamese People's Army Annihilates More Than 460,000 Enemy Troops
Author:
Date: 1954-08-31
Page: 1

　　Xinhua News Agency, North Vietnam, August 30th: According to an announcement by the General Headquarters of the Vietnamese People's Army, in the past eight years, that is, from December 19, 1946 (the day the nationwide resistance in Vietnam began) to July 21, 1954 (the day the Vietnam armistice agreement was signed), the Vietnamese People's Army annihilated a total of 466,172 troops of the French Union forces.

　　During this period, the Vietnamese People's Army captured 130,670 weapons of various types (including 255 artillery pieces) and 504 vehicles of various types.

　　The Vietnamese People's Army shot down 435 aircraft of various types, 603 ships and motorboats, 337 locomotives, 1,478 carriages, 9,292 vehicles of various types, and 344 artillery pieces, and destroyed tens of thousands of tons of ammunition and tens of millions of liters of petroleum.

### 八年英勇抗战取得光辉胜利  越南人民军共歼敌四十六万多人

1954-08-31
第1版()
专栏：

　  八年英勇抗战取得光辉胜利
    越南人民军共歼敌四十六万多人
    【新华社三十日讯】越南通讯社北越三十日讯：据越南人民军总司令部宣布，过去八年中，即从一九四六年十二月十九日（越南全国抗战开始的日子）到一九五四年七月二十一日（越南停战协定签订的日子），越南人民军共歼灭法兰西联邦部队四十六万六千一百七十二人。
    在这个时期中，越南人民军缴获各种武器十三万零六百七十件（其中包括大炮二百五十五门）和各种车辆五百零四辆。
    越南人民军击毁各种飞机四百三十五架，船舰和汽艇六百零三艘，机车三百三十七辆，车厢一千四百七十八节，各种车辆九千二百九十二辆，大炮三百四十四门，并击毁成万吨弹药和几千万公升的石油。
