Title: Marshal Rokossovsky of Poland Sends Telegram to Commander-in-Chief Zhu, General Porzylowski Sends Telegram to General Nie Rongzhen
Author:
Date: 1954-08-01
Page: 1

Marshal Rokossovsky of Poland Sends Telegram to Commander-in-Chief Zhu

General Porzylowski Sends Telegram to General Nie Rongzhen

To Comrade Zhu De, Commander-in-Chief of the Chinese People's Liberation Army:

	On the occasion of the Army Day of the Chinese People's Liberation Army, I extend my heartfelt congratulations to you and all the servicemen. The Chinese People's Liberation Army, friendly united with the powerful army of the great socialist country—the Soviet Army—and the armies of various people's democratic countries, is firmly defending the independence of its motherland and the peaceful labor of the Chinese people. On behalf of all members of the Polish People's Army, I extend to you my best wishes: I wish you further achievements in strengthening the combat power of the great Chinese people's armed forces.

						Deputy Chairman of the Council of Ministers and Minister of National Defense of Poland, Marshal of Poland
						Konstanty Rokossovsky
						July 27, 1954, Warsaw

To Comrade Nie Rongzhen, Acting Chief of General Staff of the Chinese People's Liberation Army:

	On the occasion of the Army Day of the Chinese People's Liberation Army, please allow me, General Comrade, to extend my heartfelt congratulations to you personally and to all the heroic Chinese soldiers, and wish you further achievements. May the armed forces of the great Chinese people develop further victoriously in order to continuously strengthen the invincible peace camp.

					Deputy Minister of National Defense and Chief of General Staff of the Polish People's Republic
					General Jerzy Porzylowski
					July 27, 1954, Warsaw

### 波兰罗科索夫斯基元帅致电朱总司令  波尔兹洛夫斯基上将致电聂荣臻将军

1954-08-01
第1版()
专栏：

    波兰罗科索夫斯基元帅致电朱总司令
    波尔兹洛夫斯基上将致电聂荣臻将军中国人民解放军总司令朱德同志：
    值此中国人民解放军建军节之际，谨向您及全体军人致以衷心的祝贺。与伟大的社会主义国家的强大军队——苏联军队以及各人民民主国家的军队友好地结合在一起的中国人民解放军，正坚定地保卫着自己祖国的独立和中国人民的和平劳动。我谨代表波兰人民军全体人员向您致以最好的愿望：祝您在加强伟大中国人民的武装力量的战斗威力中获得更进一步成就。
                          波兰部长会议副主席兼国防部长、波兰元帅　康·罗科索夫斯基一九五四年七月二十七日华沙中国人民解放军代总参谋长聂荣臻将军同志：
    欣逢中国人民解放军建军节之际，请允许我，将军同志，向您个人和英勇的中国全体军人同志们致以衷心的祝贺，并祝获得进一步的成就。愿伟大的中国人民的武装力量为了不断加强不可战胜的和平阵营而更进一步胜利发展。
                    波兰人民共和国国防部副部长、总参谋长尤·波尔兹洛夫斯基上将
                        一九五四年七月二十七日华沙
