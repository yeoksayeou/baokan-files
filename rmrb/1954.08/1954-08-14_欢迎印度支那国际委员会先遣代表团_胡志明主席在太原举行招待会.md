Title: Welcome the Advance Delegation of the International Commission for Indochina - Chairman Ho Chi Minh Holds Reception in Thai Nguyen
Author:
Date: 1954-08-14
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, August 13th: According to the Vietnam News Agency, North Vietnam, August 11th: The personnel of the advance delegation of the International Supervisory and Control Commission for Indochina arrived in Zhongjia, North Vietnam, on the morning of the 11th. They were greeted by General Van Tien Dung, Chief Representative of the Vietnam People's Army High Command Delegation to the Vietnam Central Joint Armistice Commission, and General Dechaux, Chief Representative of the French Union Forces High Command Delegation.

On the morning of the same day, the personnel of the advance delegation of the International Supervisory and Control Commission held a meeting with the Vietnam Central Joint Armistice Commission to discuss issues related to the relationship between the Vietnam Central Joint Armistice Commission and the International Supervisory and Control Commission.

The advance delegation of the International Supervisory and Control Commission will return to Zhongjia on August 13th for further talks with the Vietnam Central Joint Armistice Commission.

Xinhua News Agency, North Vietnam, August 13th: Ho Chi Minh, President of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam, received Dute, the Indian Ambassadorial Representative leading the advance delegation of the International Supervisory and Control Commission to Vietnam, Ogrodzinski, the Polish Ambassadorial Representative, and Macdonnell, the Canadian Ambassadorial Representative, as well as other personnel from India, Poland, and Canada in the advance delegation, in Thai Nguyen, the capital of Viet Bac Interzone and Thai Nguyen Province, on the 12th.

Present at the reception were Pham Van Dong, Deputy Prime Minister and Acting Minister of Foreign Affairs of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam, Vo Nguyen Giap, Minister of National Defense and Commander-in-Chief of the People's Army, Ta Quang Buu, Deputy Minister of National Defense, and Van Tien Dung, Chief Representative of the Vietnam People's Army High Command Delegation to the Vietnam Central Joint Armistice Commission, among others.

Xinhua News Agency, August 13th: According to the Vietnam News Agency, North Vietnam, August 13th: Ho Chi Minh, President of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam, held a reception in Thai Nguyen on August 12th to welcome the advance delegation of the International Supervisory and Control Commission for Indochina. The Vietnamese side attending the reception included Pham Van Dong, Chief Representative of the Delegation of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam to the Geneva Conference, Deputy Prime Minister and Acting Minister of Foreign Affairs, General Vo Nguyen Giap, Minister of National Defense and Commander-in-Chief of the Vietnam People's Army, Ta Quang Buu, Deputy Minister of National Defense, General Van Tien Dung, Chief Representative of the Vietnam People's Army High Command Delegation to the Vietnam Central Joint Armistice Commission, Colonel Ha Van Lau, and Phan My, Director of the Prime Minister's Office.

On behalf of the Vietnamese people and the government of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam, President Ho Chi Minh warmly welcomed the advance delegation of the International Supervisory and Control Commission. He said: The people, government, and People's Army of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam guarantee to fully respect and earnestly strive to fulfill the Geneva Agreements. At the same time, they hope that the French government and all relevant parties will also guarantee to faithfully implement the armistice agreement.

President Ho Chi Minh said that the tasks of the International Supervisory and Control Commission will be complex and difficult. However, the Vietnamese people and the government of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam firmly believe that the International Supervisory and Control Commission, upholding the spirit of the resolutions of the Geneva Conference and the Final Declaration of the Geneva Conference, and in the spirit of equality and justice, and relying on the close cooperation and assistance of the relevant parties, will certainly be able to accomplish its important tasks.

President Ho Chi Minh concluded by saying that the Vietnamese people and the government of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam are willing to provide their sincere cooperation and assistance to the International Supervisory and Control Commission, which is composed of people from three different continents.

After President Ho Chi Minh's speech, representatives from India, Canada, and Poland delivered speeches in succession. They expressed their gratitude to President Ho Chi Minh, the government of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam, and the Vietnamese people for the warm hospitality they received at the reception and wherever they went. Everyone then toasted to the health of President Ho Chi Minh and wished the Vietnamese people new achievements in peaceful construction.

### 欢迎印度支那国际委员会先遣代表团  胡志明主席在太原举行招待会

1954-08-14
第4版()
专栏：

    欢迎印度支那国际委员会先遣代表团
    胡志明主席在太原举行招待会
    【新华社十三日讯】据越南通讯社北越十一日讯：印度支那国际监督和监察委员会先遣代表团人员十一日上午自河内到达北越中稼，前往欢迎的有越南中央联合停战委员会的越南人民军总司令部代表团首席代表文进勇将军和法兰西联邦部队总司令部代表团首席代表戴尔特尔将军。
    当日上午，国际监督和监察委员会先遣代表团人员和越南中央联合停战委员会举行了一次会议，讨论了关于越南中央联合停战委员会和国际监督和监察委员会之间关系的问题。
    国际监督和监察委员会先遣代表团将在八月十三日回中稼，再与越南中央联合停战委员会举行会谈。
     【新华社北越十三日电】越南民主共和国胡志明主席十二日在越北联区首府、太原省省会太原，接见率领印度支那国际监督和监察委员会先遣代表团到越南去的印度大使衔代表杜特，和先遣代表团同往越南的波兰大使衔代表奥格罗京斯基、加拿大大使衔代表麦克唐奈，以及先遣代表团中的印度、波兰、加拿大三国的其他人员。
    接见时在座的有：越南民主共和国副总理兼代理外交部部长范文同、越南国防部部长兼人民军总司令武元甲、国防部副部长谢光宝、越南中央联合停战委员会越南人民军总司令部代表团首席代表文进勇等。
     【新华社十三日讯】据越南通讯社北越十三日讯：越南民主共和国胡志明主席八月十二日在太原举行招待会，欢迎印度支那国际监督和监察委员会先遣代表团。越南方面出席招待会的有：越南民主共和国出席日内瓦会议代表团首席代表、副总理兼代理外交部部长范文同，国防部部长兼越南人民军总司令武元甲将军，国防部副部长谢光宝，越南中央联合停战委员会越南人民军总司令部代表团首席代表文进勇将军，何文楼上校和总理府办公厅主任潘美。
    胡志明主席代表越南人民和越南民主共和国政府热烈欢迎国际监督和监察委员会先遣代表团。他说：越南民主共和国人民、政府和人民军保证充分尊重和认真努力履行日内瓦协议。同时，他们希望法国政府和各有关方面也保证忠实履行停战协定。
    胡志明主席说，印度支那国际监督和监察委员会的任务将是复杂和困难的。但是越南人民和越南民主共和国政府坚决相信，国际监督和监察委员会拥护日内瓦会议的各项决议和日内瓦会议最后宣言的精神，本着平等公正的精神，依靠有关方面的密切合作和协助，一定能够完成它的重大任务。
    胡志明主席最后说，越南人民和越南民主共和国政府愿意向从三个不同的大洲来的人们组成的国际监督和监察委员会提供他们衷心的合作和协助。
    在胡志明主席发言后，印度、加拿大和波兰三国代表相继致答词。他们表示感谢胡志明主席、越南民主共和国政府和越南人民在招待会上以及他们所到之处所得到的热情款待。大家然后为胡志明主席的健康干杯，并祝越南人民在和平建设中获得新成就。
