Title: U.S. to Continue Hydrogen Bomb Tests in the Pacific
Author:
Date: 1954-08-10
Page: 4

    Xinhua News Agency, August 9th: Tokyo dispatch: U.S. Ambassador to Japan, Allison, in reply to a letter from several prefectural governors of Kyushu requesting the U.S. to cease hydrogen bomb tests in the Pacific, said that the U.S. will continue to conduct these tests.
    It is clear from Allison's remarks that the U.S. is attempting to disregard the demands of the Japanese people and world public opinion to ban hydrogen bomb tests that cause great harm to the people on some islands in the Pacific.

### 美国将继续在太平洋进行氢弹试验

1954-08-10
第4版()
专栏：

    美国将继续在太平洋进行氢弹试验
    【新华社九日讯】塔斯社八日讯：东京消息：美国驻日本大使艾利逊在答复九州的一些县知事要求美国停止在太平洋试验氢弹的一封信中说，美国将继续进行这些试验。
    从艾利逊的言论中看得很明显，美国企图不理会日本人民以及全世界公众舆论的要求——禁止使太平洋中一些岛屿上的人民受到很大损害的氢弹试验。
