Title: Romanian Microbiology Expert Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1954-08-21
Page: 1
    Xinhua News Agency, August 20th.  At the invitation of the Chinese government, Ștefan Nicolau, member of the Presidium of the National Assembly of the Romanian People's Republic, academician of the Academy of Sciences, and microbiology expert, arrived in Beijing by plane on the afternoon of the 20th for a visit to China.
    Hong Shen, Director of the Foreign Cultural Affairs Liaison Bureau of the Cultural and Educational Committee of the Central People's Government, and others welcomed him at the airport.
    Also present at the airport to welcome him was Anding, Economic Counselor of the Romanian Embassy in China.

### 罗马尼亚微生物学专家到京

1954-08-21
第1版()
专栏：

    罗马尼亚微生物学专家到京
    【新华社二十日讯】应我国政府邀请来我国访问的罗马尼亚人民共和国国民议会主席团委员、科学院院士、微生物学专家斯捷凡·尼古劳在二十日下午乘飞机抵京。
    到机场欢迎的有我中央人民政府政务院文化教育委员会对外文化事务联络局局长洪深等人。
    到机场欢迎的还有罗马尼亚驻我国大使馆经济参赞安定。
