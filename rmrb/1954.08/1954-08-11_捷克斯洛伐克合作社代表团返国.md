Title: Czechoslovak Cooperative Delegation Returns Home
Author:
Date: 1954-08-11
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, August 10th—The Czechoslovak Cooperative Delegation, led by head of delegation Smrčka, departed Beijing by plane on the morning of August 9th to return home. Those who saw them off at the airport included Zhang Qilong and Liang Yao, vice-chairmen of the board of directors of the All China Federation of Supply and Marketing Cooperatives, as well as personnel from the Czechoslovak Embassy in China.

### 捷克斯洛伐克合作社代表团返国

1954-08-11
第4版()
专栏：

    捷克斯洛伐克合作社代表团返国
    【新华社十日讯】捷克斯洛伐克合作社代表团三人在团长斯穆鲁卡率领下在八月九日晨乘班机离京返国。到机场欢送者有中华全国供销合作总社理事会副主任张启龙、梁耀及捷克斯洛伐克驻我国大使馆人员等。
