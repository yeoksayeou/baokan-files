Title: North Korean People's Delegation to China Holds Return Exhibition

Author:
Date: 1954-08-11
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency:

    North Korean People's Delegation to China
    Holds Return Exhibition

    According to Xinhua News Agency: The Return Exhibition of the North Korean People's Delegation to China opened in Pyongyang on the afternoon of the 7th.

    Those who attended the opening ceremony included: Kim Tu-bong, Chairman of the Standing Committee of the Supreme People's Assembly of North Korea; Hong Myung-hee, Vice Premier of the Cabinet; Ha Ang-cheon, Minister of the Propaganda and Agitation Department of the Central Committee of the Workers' Party of Korea; and Kim Ung-gi, head of the North Korean People's Delegation to China. Li Shi, First Secretary of the Chinese Embassy in North Korea, also attended the opening ceremony.

    After Kim Tu-bong cut the ribbon, those present visited the exhibition.

    The exhibition displayed more than 1,000 gifts entrusted by the Chinese people to the North Korean People's Delegation to China for Marshal Kim Il-sung and the Korean people. Among them were silk banners presented by organizations such as the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference and the China People's Anti-American Aggression Support Korea General Association, famous Chinese carvings, embroideries, and other crafts, as well as models of tools created and invented by workers in Anshan, China's steel capital, such as Wang Chonglun's "universal tool tire" model. Among the exhibits were works of art made of ore by workers at the Fushun Coal Mine depicting the Chinese and Korean People's Armies fighting side by side, and gifts from Luo Diekai, father of internationalist fighter Luo Shengjiao, to Choi. These gifts fully demonstrated the deep friendship between the people of China and North Korea.

### 朝鲜人民访华代表团  举办归国展览会

1954-08-11
第4版()
专栏：

    朝鲜人民访华代表团
    举办归国展览会
    据新华社讯：朝鲜人民访华代表团归国展览会七日下午在平壤开幕。
    参加开幕仪式的有：朝鲜最高人民会议常任委员会委员长金枓奉、内阁副首相洪命熹、朝鲜劳动党中央宣传鼓动部部长河仰天和朝鲜人民访华代表团团长金应基。我国驻朝鲜大使馆一等秘书李石也参加了开幕仪式。
    在金枓奉剪彩后，到会的人参观了展览会。
    展览会陈列着我国人民委托朝鲜人民访华代表团带给金日成元帅和朝鲜人民的礼物一千多件。其中有中国人民政治协商会议、中国人民抗美援朝总会等机关团体赠送的锦旗，有我国各地著名的雕刻、刺绣等工艺制品，以及我国钢都鞍山工人们创造发明的工具的模型如王崇伦的“万能工具胎”模型等。展览品中还有抚顺煤矿工人们用矿石塑成的描写中朝人民军队并肩作战的美术品，国际主义战士罗盛教的父亲罗迭开赠送给崔?的礼物等。这些礼物充分表现了中、朝两国人民的深厚友谊。
