Title: Comrade Liu Shaoqi and Comrade Pham Van Dong Hold Talks; Premier Zhou Enlai Pays a Visit to Vice Premier Pham Van Dong
Author:
Date: 1954-08-04
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, August 3rd: Comrade Liu Shaoqi, Secretary of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of China, held talks with Comrade Pham Van Dong, member of the Political Bureau of the Central Committee of the Vietnam Workers' Party, on the afternoon of the 3rd. Present at the meeting were: Zhou Enlai, Secretary of the CPC Central Committee, Deng Xiaoping, Secretary-General of the CPC Central Committee, Wang Jiaxiang, member of the CPC Central Committee, and Yang Shangkun, Director of the General Office of the CPC Central Committee.

Xinhua News Agency, August 3rd: Zhou Enlai, Premier of the State Council and Minister of Foreign Affairs of the Central People's Government, paid a visit to Pham Van Dong, Deputy Prime Minister and Acting Minister of Foreign Affairs of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam, on the afternoon of the 3rd. ### 刘少奇同志和范文同同志会谈  周恩来总理拜会范文同副总理

1954-08-04
第1版()
专栏：

    刘少奇同志和范文同同志会谈
    周恩来总理拜会范文同副总理
    【新华社三日讯】中国共产党中央委员会书记刘少奇在三日下午与越南劳动党中央委员会政治局委员范文同举行会谈。在座的，有：中共中央书记周恩来，中共中央秘书长邓小平，中共中央委员王稼祥，中共中央办公厅主任杨尚昆。
    【新华社三日讯】我中央人民政府政务院总理兼外交部部长周恩来，在三日下午拜会越南民主共和国副总理兼代理外交部部长范文同。（附图片）
    刘少奇同志八月三日和范文同同志会谈。图中自左至右：刘少奇、范文同、翻译员、周恩来。　新华社稿　侯波摄
