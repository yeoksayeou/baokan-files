Title: Responsible Persons of Democratic Parties and People's Organizations in Beijing Issue Statements, Warmly Endorsing the Joint Declaration for the Liberation of Taiwan
Author:
Date: 1954-08-26
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, August 25th: Responsible persons of the local organizations of various democratic parties and people's organizations in Beijing today published written statements in the "Beijing Daily", warmly endorsing the "Joint Declaration of All Democratic Parties and People's Organizations of the People's Republic of China for the Liberation of Taiwan". They unanimously stated that Taiwan is Chinese territory and the Chinese people are determined to liberate Taiwan. This is the steel-like, unshakeable common will of the six hundred million Chinese people. The Chinese people have sufficient strength to completely eliminate the Chiang Kai-shek traitorous clique. They said that the workers, farmers, students, women, and people of all social strata in Beijing must redouble their efforts in production, work hard, heighten vigilance, and strictly prevent enemy sabotage activities, and use practical actions to support the Chinese People's Liberation Army in completing the sacred cause of liberating Taiwan.

The following people issued statements: Jiang Guangnai, convener of the Beijing Branch of the Revolutionary Committee of the Chinese Kuomintang; Wu Han, chairman of the Beijing Branch of the China Democratic League; Pu Jiexiu, chairman of the Beijing Branch of the China Democratic National Construction Association; Feng Binfu, executive director of the Beijing Branch of the China Association for Promoting Democracy; Peng Zemin, chairman of the Beijing Municipal Committee of the Chinese Peasants' and Workers' Democratic Party; Xue Yu, chairman of the Beijing Branch of the Jiusan Society; Zhang Dazhong, secretary of the Beijing Municipal Committee of the Chinese New Democratic Youth League and chairman of the Beijing Democratic Youth Federation; Liu Laifu, vice chairman of the Beijing Federation of Trade Unions; Chai Zemin, chairman of the Beijing Farmers' Association; Zhang Xiaomei, chairman of the Beijing Democratic Women's Federation; Xu Naiming, chairman of the Beijing Students' Federation; Shu Sheyu, chairman of the Beijing Federation of Literary and Art Workers; Mao Yisheng, chairman of the Beijing Branch of the All-China Federation of Natural Science Societies; Qian Chongshu, chairman of the Beijing Association for the Popularization of Science and Technology; Le Songsheng, chairman of the Beijing Federation of Industry and Commerce; and Zhao Shuping, chairman of the Beijing Association of Traditional Chinese Medicine.

### 北京市各民主党派、人民团体负责人发表谈话  热烈拥护为解放台湾联合宣言

1954-08-26
第1版()
专栏：

    北京市各民主党派、人民团体负责人发表谈话
    热烈拥护为解放台湾联合宣言
   【新华社二十五日讯】我国各民主党派北京市地方组织和北京市各人民团体的负责人今日在“北京日报”上分别发表书面谈话，热烈拥护“中华人民共和国各民主党派各人民团体为解放台湾联合宣言”。他们一致表示，台湾是中国的领土，中国人民一定要解放台湾。这是中国六亿人民钢铁一般的、不可动摇的共同意志。中国人民有足够的力量把蒋介石卖国集团彻底消灭。他们说，北京市的工人、农民、学生、妇女、和各社会阶层的人民要加倍努力生产，努力工作，提高警惕，严防敌人的破坏活动，用实际行动支援中国人民解放军完成解放台湾的神圣事业。
    发表谈话的，有：中国国民党革命委员会北京市分部召集人蒋光鼐、中国民主同盟北京市支部主任委员吴晗、中国民主建国会北京市分会主任委员浦洁修、中国民主促进会北京市分会主任理事冯宾符、中国农工民主党北京市委员会主任委员彭泽民、九三学社北京市分社主任委员薛愚、中国新民主主义青年团北京市委员会书记、北京市民主青年联合会主席张大中、北京市总工会副主席刘莱夫、北京市农民协会主席柴泽民、北京市民主妇女联合会主席张晓梅、北京市学生联合会主席徐乃明、北京市文学艺术工作者联合会主席舒舍予、中华全国自然科学专门学会联合会北京分会主任委员茅以升、北京市科学技术普及协会主席钱崇澍、北京市工商业联合会主任委员乐松生、北京市中医学会主任委员赵树屏。
