Title: Northeast Military Region Elects Deputies to the National People's Congress
Author:
Date: 1954-08-09
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Shenyang, August 8th: The Northeast Military Region of the Chinese People's Liberation Army held an election conference from August 3rd to 5th to elect deputies to the National People's Congress from the Northeast Military Region. Two hundred and twenty-nine representatives from various units of the Northeast Military Region attended the conference.

Zhou Huan, Deputy Political Commissar of the Northeast Military Region, addressed the conference. He pointed out the great significance of formulating the Constitution of the People's Republic of China and electing deputies to the National People's Congress, and called on all commanders and fighters of the military region to be a hundred times more vigilant against the aggressive ambitions of American imperialism, actively complete the task of regular training, and strive to liberate Taiwan, defend the integrity of our territorial sovereignty, and defend the socialist construction of the motherland!

At the meeting, the representatives solemnly listened to and discussed the report on the draft Constitution of the People's Republic of China, and unanimously adopted a resolution supporting the draft Constitution of the People's Republic of China.

The conference solemnly elected Luo Ronghuan, Xiao Hua, Zhou Huan, Zhou Shidi, Ma Chunyu (combat hero), Huang Chouhe (combat hero), and other six comrades as deputies to the National People's Congress by secret ballot.

Zhou Huan, on behalf of the elected deputies, spoke at the conference. He expressed that he would definitely live up to the trust of all the representatives and all the commanders and fighters of the military region, stand closely with all comrades in the army and the people of the whole country, and contribute all his strength to consolidating the achievements of the revolution and building our country into a great socialist country.

### 东北军区选出全国人民代表大会的代表

1954-08-09
第1版()
专栏：

    东北军区选出全国人民代表大会的代表
    【新华社沈阳八日电】中国人民解放军东北军区八月三日到五日举行了东北军区出席全国人民代表大会代表的选举大会。出席大会的有东北军区各单位代表二百二十九人。
    东北军区副政治委员周桓向大会讲话。他指出了制定中华人民共和国宪法和选举全国人民代表大会代表的重大意义，并号召全军区指挥员、战斗员百倍警惕美帝国主义的侵略野心，积极完成正规训练任务，为解放台湾，保卫我国领土主权的完整，保卫祖国的社会主义建设而奋斗！
    会上，代表们郑重地听取和讨论了关于中华人民共和国宪法草案的报告，并一致通过了拥护中华人民共和国宪法草案的决议。
    大会以无记名的投票方式，隆重地选出了罗荣桓、萧华、周桓、周士第、马春雨（战斗英雄）、黄丑和（战斗英雄）等六人为出席全国人民代表大会的代表。
    周桓代表当选代表在会上讲话。他表示一定不辜负全体代表和全军区指挥员、战斗员的信任，紧紧地和全军同志与全国人民在一起，为巩固革命胜利成果，把我国建设成一个伟大的社会主义国家而贡献一切力量。
