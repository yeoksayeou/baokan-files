Title: Indochina International Commission Advance Delegation Arrives in Hanoi
Author:
Date: 1954-08-12
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, North Vietnam, August 11th: The advance delegation of the Indochina International Supervision and Control Commission arrived in Hanoi by plane on the 10th.

Welcoming the advance delegation at Gia Lam Airport near Hanoi were: a delegation from the General Headquarters of the Vietnam People's Army headed by Major General Van Tien Dung, a delegation from the General Headquarters of the French Union Forces in Indochina, nearly one hundred Indian residents in Hanoi, and some Vietnamese, French, and foreign journalists.

The advance delegation of the Indochina International Supervision and Control Commission held a half-hour meeting with the two chief representatives of the Vietnam People's Army General Headquarters delegation and the French Union Forces General Headquarters delegation on the future agenda.

On the 11th, some members of the advance delegation went to Trung Gia in North Vietnam to discuss the agenda and working methods with the delegations of the Vietnam People's Army General Headquarters and the French Union Forces General Headquarters.

### 印度支那国际委员会先遣代表团到达河内

1954-08-12
第1版()
专栏：

    印度支那国际委员会先遣代表团到达河内
   【新华社十一日讯】据越南通讯社北越十一日讯：印度支那国际监督和监察委员会的先遣代表团十日乘飞机到达河内。
    在河内附近的嘉林机场欢迎先遣代表团的有：以文进勇少将为首的越南人民军总司令部代表团，印度支那法兰西联邦部队总司令部代表团，还有河内印度侨民近一百人和一些越南、法国及外国记者。
    印度支那国际监督和监察委员会先遣代表团同越南人民军总司令部代表团和法兰西联邦部队总司令部代表团的两位首席代表就未来议程作了半小时的会谈。
    十一日，先遣代表团的部分团员去北越中稼同越南人民军总司令部代表团和法兰西联邦部队总司令部代表团讨论议程和工作方法等问题。
