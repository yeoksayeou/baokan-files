Title: Beijing Mayor Peng Zhen Hosts Reception to See Off British Labour Party Delegation to Visit Shanghai, Premier Zhou Enlai of the Government Administrative Council and Others Attend Reception
Author:
Date: 1954-08-26
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, August 25th: Beijing Municipal People's Government Mayor Peng Zhen hosted a reception in Zhongshan Hall, Beijing, on the evening of the 25th to bid farewell to the British Labour Party delegation who will be leaving Beijing to visit Shanghai and other places.

Attending the reception were: Mr. Clement Attlee of the British Labour Party delegation, Mr. Aneurin Bevan, Mrs. E. Summerskill, Mr. W. Burke, Mr. Morgan Phillips, Mr. Stephen Watson, Mr. Harold Earnshaw, Mr. Henry Franklin, and the delegation's translator, Sir Michael Lindsay and his wife.

Also attending the farewell reception was the British Chargé d'Affaires to China, Mr. D.W.H. MacLean.

On the Chinese side, those attending the reception included: Premier Zhou Enlai of the Central People's Government Administrative Council, Vice Premiers Guo Moruo, Huang Yanpei, and Deng Xiaoping, Vice Chairmen of the National Committee of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference Li Jishen and Chen Shutong, Vice President of the Supreme People's Court of the Central People's Government Zhang Zhiran, Deputy Chief Procurator of the Supreme People's Procuratorate Gao Kelin, Secretary-General of the Administrative Council Xi Zhongxun, Government Council Members Xie Juezai, Zeng Shan, Zhang Bojun, Chen Shaoxian, Luo Longji, Shao Lizi, Huang Shaohong, as well as the heads and deputy heads of the various departments under the Administrative Council, Li Xiannian, Liu Jingfan, Huang Jing, Zhang Naiqi, Zhu Xuefan, Fu Zuoyi, Li Dequan, Chu Tunan, Chen Qiyuan, Xu Zirong, Lei Renmin, He Changgong, Lü Zhengcao, Yang Xiufeng, He Cheng, Ding Xilin, Ye Shengtao, Sa Kongliao, Deputy Ministers of Foreign Affairs Zhang Hanfu and Wu Xiuquan, etc.

Also attending the reception were: Liao Chengzhi, member of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of China, representatives of various democratic parties and non-party democratic人士 Cai Tingkai, Gao Chongmin, Sun Qimeng, Ma Yinchu, Xu Guangping, Peng Zemin, Chen Qiyou, Xu Deheng, Li Chunqing, Luo Yi, etc.

Also attending the reception were: Vice Presidents of the Chinese People's Institute of Foreign Affairs Chen Hansheng, Hu Yuzhi, Qian Duansheng, Qiao Guanhua, Secretary-General Wu Maosun, and heads of central organs of various people's organizations Liu Ningyi, Zhang Yun, Qu Tangliang, etc.

Also attending the reception were: Vice Mayors of Beijing Municipal People's Government Zhang Youyu and Wu Han, Vice Chairman of Beijing Municipal Consultative Committee Liang Sicheng, heads of various people's organizations in Beijing Liu Laifu, Zhang Xiaomei, Zhang Dazhong, Xu Naiming, Lao She, Yue Songsheng, etc.

Also attending the reception were: people from scientific and educational circles Liang Xi, Hua Luogeng, Jiang Nanxiang, Zhou Peiyuan, Jin Yuelin, Chen Daisun, Fu Zhongsun, Zhang Xiaoqian, Yan Jingyao, Lou Bangyan, Mao Yisheng, Lu Zhiwei, Fu Lianzhang, people from literary and art circles Zheng Zhenduo, Cao Yu, Zhao Feng, Xie Bingxin, Hua Junwu, Liu Kaiqu, Ma Yanxiang, Dai Ailian, Li Shaochun, Ye Shengllan, Du Jinfang, Hou Baolin, Lian Kuoru, Tan Fuying, Zhang Junqiu, people from the press circles Deng Tuo, Wang Yunsheng, Shen Jiantu, Fan Jin, Pu Xixiu, labor models Li Yong, Liu Dezhen, Liu Shimei, Hou Decheng, Yin Weichen, people from industrial and commercial circles Sha Qianli, Yin Yukun, Ling Qijun, Sun Fuling, representatives of ethnic minorities Pengsike, Ganchunlei, Ke Jiyi, Abuliezi, religious figures Zhao Zichen, Wang Zizhong, Zhao Fusan, Dapusheng, Ma Songting, Li Shu, Juzan, as well as people from all walks of life Qian Junrui, Wu Yunfu, Liang Shuming, Liu Wang Liming, Qian Changzhao, etc.

Also attending the reception were foreign journalists currently in Beijing.

A total of more than 400 people attended the reception.

The reception proceeded in a friendly atmosphere.

### 北京市彭真市长举行酒会  欢送英国工党代表团去上海访问  政务院总理周恩来等出席酒会

1954-08-26
第1版()
专栏：

    北京市彭真市长举行酒会
    欢送英国工党代表团去上海访问
    政务院总理周恩来等出席酒会
    【新华社二十五日讯】北京市人民政府市长彭真二十五日晚在北京中山堂举行酒会，欢送即将离京去上海等地访问的英国工党代表团。
    出席酒会的，有：英国工党代表团克·艾德礼先生、安·比万先生、埃·萨末斯基尔夫人、韦·伯克先生、摩·菲利普斯先生、塞·华生先生、哈·厄恩萧先生、亨·弗兰克林先生和代表团翻译林迈可爵士夫妇。
