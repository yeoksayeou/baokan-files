Title: Neutral Nations Supervisory Commission Members Jointly Write to Military Armistice Commission Demanding US Guarantees of Working Conditions and Punishment of Guilty Personnel
Author:
Date: 1954-08-05
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Kaesong, August 4th: The Korean-Chinese side of the Military Armistice Commission received a letter on August 4th, jointly addressed to the Military Armistice Commission by the Polish, Czechoslovak, Swedish, and Swiss members of the Neutral Nations Supervisory Commission on August 4th. In the letter, all members of the Neutral Nations Supervisory Commission unanimously expressed deep concern about the series of serious incidents that have occurred and are continuing to occur in South Korea, under US military control, since July 31st, endangering the safety of the personnel of the Neutral Nations Inspection Teams. They unanimously demanded that the US authorities take full and effective measures to ensure the safety of the Neutral Nations Supervisory Commission personnel, the normal working conditions of the Neutral Nations Inspection Teams, and punish those guilty of these incidents.

The full text of the letter jointly addressed to the Military Armistice Commission by all members of the Neutral Nations Supervisory Commission on August 4th is as follows:

From: Neutral Nations Supervisory Commission
To: Military Armistice Commission

The Neutral Nations Supervisory Commission wishes to express its concern regarding reported incidents in Busan and Gunsan endangering the personal safety of personnel of the Neutral Nations Inspection Teams stationed at those ports.

The reported incidents include the following:

1. On July 31, 1954, shots were fired endangering the lives of personnel of the Neutral Nations Inspection Team at Busan port.
2. On August 1, 1954, three grenades or homemade bombs were thrown into the Neutral Nations Inspection Team camp in Gunsan.

The Neutral Nations Supervisory Commission takes note of the statement by the Commander-in-Chief of the United Nations Command and the Senior Member of the United Nations Command side of the Military Armistice Commission, to the effect that the United Nations Command fully recognizes its responsibilities to the Neutral Nations Supervisory Commission and will fulfill them. At the same time, in view of the disturbing news continuing to come from ports under the control of the United Nations Command, the Neutral Nations Supervisory Commission expresses its deep concern and makes a request to the Military Armistice Commission, requesting the United Nations Command to take full and effective measures to ensure the safety of the personnel of the Neutral Nations Supervisory Commission, the normal working conditions of the Neutral Nations Inspection Teams, and hopes that the United Nations Command will punish those guilty.

                                                                    Polish Member
                                                                    General Kseemin (Signed)
                                                                    Czechoslovak Member
                                                                    General Hechko (Signed)
                                                                    Swedish Member
                                                                    General Janda (Signed)
                                                                    Swiss Member
                                                                    General Gross (Signed)
                                                                    August 4, 1954

### 中立国监察委员会全体委员  联名写信给军事停战委员会  要美方保证工作条件并惩罚有罪人员

1954-08-05
第4版()
专栏：

    中立国监察委员会全体委员
    联名写信给军事停战委员会
    要美方保证工作条件并惩罚有罪人员
   【新华社开城四日电】八月四日，军事停战委员会朝中方面收到中立国监察委员会波兰、捷克斯洛伐克、瑞典和瑞士四国委员八月四日联名致军事停战委员会的信。中立国监察委员会全体委员在信里一致对七月三十一日以来，在美方军事控制下的南朝鲜各地所已发生和正在继续发生的危及中立国视察小组人员的安全的一系列的严重事件，表示深切的关怀，并一致要求美方当局采取充分有效措施，保证中立国监察委员会人员的安全以及中立国视察小组的正常工作条件，并惩罚有关这些事件的有罪人员。
    中立国监察委员会全体委员八月四日联名致军事停战委员会的信的全文如下：
    自：中立国监察委员会
    致：军事停战委员会
    中立国监察委员会愿对据报告在釜山与群山发生的危及驻在那些口岸的中立国视察小组人员人身安全的事件表示关怀。
    已经报告的事件包括如下：
    一、一九五四年七月三十一日开枪危害釜山口岸中立国视察小组人员的生命。
    二、一九五四年八月一日有三颗手榴弹或手制炸弹掷入群山中立国视察小组营地。
    中立国监察委员会注意到联合国军代总司令和军事停战委员会联合国军方面首席委员的声明，大意是：联合国军充分认识到它对中立国监察委员会的责任，并将予以履行。同时鉴于令人不安的消息继续自联合国军控制下的各口岸传来，中立国监察委员会深表关怀，并向军事停战委员会提出要求，要求联合国军采取充分有效措施，保证中立国监察委员会人员的安全，以及中立国视察小组的正常工作条件，并希望联合国军惩罚有罪人员。
