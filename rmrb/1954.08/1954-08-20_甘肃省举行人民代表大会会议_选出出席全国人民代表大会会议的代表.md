Title: Gansu Province Holds People's Congress Meeting, Elects Deputies to Attend the National People's Congress Meeting
Author:
Date: 1954-08-20
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, Lanzhou, August 19th: The first session of the First People's Congress of Gansu Province began on August 16th. A total of 443 representatives attended the meeting. The representatives include twelve ethnic groups, including Han, Hui, Tibetan, Mongolian, Dongxiang, Baoan, Salar, Yugu, Kazakh, Uyghur, Tu, and Manchu, as well as all the people's representatives from the former Ningxia Province.

After repeated deliberation and discussion, all the representatives attending the conference elected Zhang Desheng, Zhang Zhongliang, Zhang Zhizhong, Deng Baoshan, Yang Mingxuan, Ma Hongbin (Hui), Huang Zhengqing (Tibetan), Xing Zhaotang, Huo Weide, Sun Diancai, Ma Tengai (Hui), Ma Xiwu, Chen Chengyi, Dali Zaya (Mongolian), Ren Qian, Guo Menghe, Yang Jingren (Hui), Sheng Tongsheng, Wu Hongbin (Hui), Yang Fuxing (Tibetan), Ma Shaowen (Dongxiang), Zhou Youcai, Xue Wanxiang, Zheng Lizhai, and Yang Ziheng, totaling twenty-five people, as representatives of Gansu Province to attend the National People's Congress meeting, by secret ballot.

At the meeting, Deng Baoshan, Chairman of the Gansu Provincial People's Government, made a report on "A Summary of the Work of the Gansu Provincial People's Government over the Past Five Years and Future Work Tasks." Xing Zhaotang, Chairman of the Ningxia Provincial People's Government, made a report on the work of Ningxia Province over the past few years and a report on the election work of Gansu Province.

### 甘肃省举行人民代表大会会议  选出出席全国人民代表大会会议的代表

1954-08-20
第1版()
专栏：

    甘肃省举行人民代表大会会议
    选出出席全国人民代表大会会议的代表
    据新华社兰州十九日电：甘肃省第一届人民代表大会第一次会议在八月十六日开始举行。出席会议的代表共四百四十三人。代表中包括汉、回、藏、蒙、东乡、保安、撒拉、裕固、哈萨克、维吾尔、土、满等十二个民族和原宁夏省的全体人民代表。
    参加大会的全体代表，经过反复酝酿讨论，以无记名投票方式选出张德生、张仲良、张治中、邓宝珊、杨明轩、马鸿宾（回）、黄正清（藏）、邢肇棠、霍维德、孙殿才、马腾霭（回）、马锡五、陈成义、达理札雅（蒙）、任谦、郭孟和、杨静仁
  （回）、盛彤笙、吴鸿宾（回）、杨复兴（藏）、马绍文（东乡）、周有才、薛万祥、郑立斋、杨子恒等二十五人为甘肃省出席全国人民代表大会会议的代表。
    会上，甘肃省人民政府主席邓宝珊作了关于
  “甘肃省人民政府五年来工作概况和今后工作任务”的报告，宁夏省人民政府主席邢肇棠作了关于宁夏省几年来工作的报告和甘肃省选举工作的报告。
