Title: Assisting in the Establishment of International Commissions in Vietnam, Laos, and Cambodia: Advance Delegation Composed of Representatives from India, Poland, and Canada Depart
Author:
Date: 1954-08-09
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, New Delhi, August 8th: The advance delegation of the Indochina International Supervisory and Monitoring Commission left Delhi by plane on the morning of July 7th for Indochina. The task of the advance delegation is, in accordance with the terms stipulated in the respective armistice agreements, to assist in the establishment of international commissions in Vietnam, Laos, and Cambodia respectively, and to study and explore organizational issues and other issues related to the three commissions, in order to submit reports to the governments of India, Poland, and Canada.

The advance delegation includes representatives from the governments of India, Poland, and Canada, and is led by Dutt, Commonwealth Secretary of the Indian Ministry of Foreign Affairs.

Ogrodzinski, the chief representative of the Polish delegation attending the preliminary meeting of the Indochina International Supervisory and Monitoring Commission, and McDonnell, the ambassadorial representative of the Canadian delegation, are also traveling with the advance delegation.

### 协助在越南、老挝和柬埔寨分别建立国际委员会  印、波、加三国代表组成的先遣代表团出发

1954-08-09
第1版()
专栏：

    协助在越南、老挝和柬埔寨分别建立国际委员会
    印、波、加三国代表组成的先遣代表团出发
    【新华社新德里八日电】印度支那国际监督和监察委员会先遣代表团在七日上午乘飞机离开德里到印度支那去。先遣代表团的任务是按照各个停战协定分别规定的条款，协助在越南、老挝和柬埔寨分别建立国际委员会，并研究和探讨同三个委员会有关的组织问题和其他问题，以便向印度、波兰、加拿大三国政府提出报告。
    先遣代表团包括印度、波兰、加拿大三国政府的代表，由印度外交部英联邦秘书杜特率领。
    出席印度支那国际监督和监察委员会预备会议的波兰代表团首席代表奥格罗京斯基和加拿大代表团大使衔代表麦克唐奈也和先遣代表团同行。
