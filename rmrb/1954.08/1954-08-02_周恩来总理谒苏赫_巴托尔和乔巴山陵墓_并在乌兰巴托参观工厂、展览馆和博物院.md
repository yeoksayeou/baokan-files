Title: Premier Zhou Enlai Visits the Mausoleum of Sukhe-Bator and Choibalsan, and Tours Factories, Exhibition Halls, and Museums in Ulaanbaatar
Author:
Date: 1954-08-02
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Ulaanbaatar, July 31st: Premier of the State Council and Minister of Foreign Affairs of the Central People's Government of the People's Republic of China, Zhou Enlai, visited the mausoleum of Sukhe-Bator and Choibalsan at 9:30 PM on the 31st and laid wreaths.

Accompanying him were Wang Jiaxiang, Deputy Minister of Foreign Affairs of the Central People's Government of the People's Republic of China; Wang Bingnan, Secretary-General of the Delegation of the People's Republic of China to the Geneva Conference; advisors Shi Zhe, Qiao Guanhua, Chen Jiakang, Huang Hua, and other personnel.

Chen Ying, Chargé d'Affaires of the Embassy of the People's Republic of China in Mongolia, also accompanied the visit.

Xinhua News Agency, Ulaanbaatar, August 1st: Premier of the State Council of the Central People's Government of the People's Republic of China, Zhou Enlai, accompanied by the Prime Minister of the Mongolian People's Republic, Yu. Tsedenbal; the Chairman of the Presidium of the Great People's Khural of the Mongolian People's Republic, Zh. Sambu; and the First Secretary of the Central Committee of the Mongolian People's Revolutionary Party, Damba, visited the "Choibalsan Industrial Complex," the "Exhibition Hall of Resources of the Mongolian People's Republic," and the "Sukhe-Bator and Choibalsan Museum" from 9:00 AM to 12:00 PM.

Accompanying Premier Zhou Enlai on the visit were Wang Jiaxiang, Deputy Minister of Foreign Affairs of the Central People's Government of the People's Republic of China; Wang Bingnan, Secretary-General of the Delegation of the People's Republic of China to the Geneva Conference; advisors Shi Zhe, Qiao Guanhua, Chen Jiakang, Huang Hua, and other personnel, as well as Chen Ying, Chargé d'Affaires of the Embassy of the People's Republic of China in Mongolia.

### 周恩来总理谒苏赫—巴托尔和乔巴山陵墓  并在乌兰巴托参观工厂、展览馆和博物院

1954-08-02
第1版()
专栏：

    周恩来总理谒苏赫—巴托尔和乔巴山陵墓
    并在乌兰巴托参观工厂、展览馆和博物院
    【新华社乌兰巴托三十一日电】中华人民共和国中央人民政府政务院总理兼外交部部长周恩来，在三十一日下午九时三十分谒苏赫—巴托尔和乔巴山陵墓，并献花圈。
    随同谒陵的有中华人民共和国中央人民政府外交部副部长王稼祥，出席日内瓦会议的中华人民共和国代表团秘书长王炳南，顾问师哲、乔冠华、陈家康、黄华和其他人员。
    中华人民共和国驻蒙古大使馆代办陈应也陪同前往谒陵。
    【新华社乌兰巴托一日电】中华人民共和国中央人民政府政务院总理周恩来在一日上午九时至十二时在蒙古人民共和国总理尤·泽登巴尔，蒙古人民共和国大人民呼拉尔主席团主席扎·桑布和蒙古人民革命党中央委员会第一书记达姆巴陪同下参观了“乔巴山工业联合工厂”，“蒙古人民共和国资源展览馆”和“苏赫·巴托尔、乔巴山博物院”。
    随同周恩来总理前往参观的有中华人民共和国中央人民政府外交部副部长王稼祥，出席日内瓦会议的中华人民共和国代表团秘书长王炳南，顾问师哲，乔冠华，陈家康，黄华和其他人员，以及中华人民共和国驻蒙古大使馆代办陈应。
