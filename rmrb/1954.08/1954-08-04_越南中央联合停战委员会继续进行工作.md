Title: Vietnam Central Joint Ceasefire Commission Continues Work
Author:
Date: 1954-08-04
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, North Vietnam, August 3rd: The chief representatives of both sides in the Vietnam Central Joint Ceasefire Commission and the sub-committees continued their work in Zhongjia, North Vietnam, on August 2nd. The chief representatives of both delegations also held a meeting on the afternoon of the 2nd, and approved the agreement on the organization and methods of work of the Vietnam Central Joint Ceasefire Commission and the local joint ceasefire commissions on various fronts.

### 越南中央联合停战委员会继续进行工作

1954-08-04
第4版()
专栏：

    越南中央联合停战委员会继续进行工作
    【新华社三日讯】越南通讯社北越三日讯：越南中央联合停战委员会中的双方代表团首席代表和各小组委员会八月二日在北越中稼继续进行工作。双方代表团首席代表在二日下午也举行了会议，批准关于越南中央联合停战委员会以及各个战线上的地方联合停战委员会工作的组织和方式问题的协议。
