Title: Premier Kim Il-sung Cables Thanks to the China People's Committee to Resist America and Aid Korea

Author:
Date: 1954-08-05
Page: 1

　　Xinhua News Agency, Pyongyang, 4th. Premier Kim Il-sung of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea cabled a reply on the 2nd to the China People's Committee to Resist America and Aid Korea in response to their congratulatory telegram on the first anniversary of the Korean armistice. The reply stated:

　　"I deeply thank your Committee for the congratulations given on behalf of the fraternal Chinese people on the first anniversary of the signing of the Korean Armistice Agreement.

　　The Korean people, united in striving to rapidly restore and build up the people's economy destroyed by the bestial atrocities of the U.S. imperialists, with the internationalist assistance and support of the Soviet Union, the People's Republic of China, and the people's democracies, as well as peace-loving people all over the world, will fight to the end to strengthen the democratic base of the motherland in every way and to achieve peaceful reunification."

### 金日成首相电谢中国人民抗美援朝总会

1954-08-05
第1版()
专栏：

    金日成首相电谢中国人民抗美援朝总会
   【新华社平壤四日电】朝鲜民主主义人民共和国内阁首相金日成在二日电复中国人民抗美援朝总会在朝鲜停战一周年时的贺电。复电说：
    “深深地感谢贵总会在朝鲜停战协定签订一周年时代表兄弟般的中国人民所给予的祝贺。
    为了迅速恢复和建设被美帝国主义野兽般的暴行所破坏的人民经济而一致奋起的朝鲜人民，在苏联、中华人民共和国和各人民民主国家以及全世界爱好和平人民的国际主义援助和支持下，将为百般地加强祖国的民主基地和争取和平的统一而斗争到底。”
