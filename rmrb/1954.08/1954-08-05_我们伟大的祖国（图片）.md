Author:
Date: 1954-08-05
Page: 1

    Our Great Motherland
    The life of the Miao people in the Taijiang Miao Autonomous Region of Guizhou Province is getting better every year. After last year's bumper harvest, many Miao people added new clothes. This Miao woman, Shi Gefu, put on a piece of clothing embroidered by herself, and the girls all praised her exquisite and meticulous craftsmanship.

### 我们伟大的祖国（图片）

1954-08-05
第1版()
专栏：

    我们伟大的祖国
    贵州省台江苗族自治区的苗族人民生活，一年好似一年。去年丰收后，许多苗族人民都添制了新衣服。这位苗族妇女石格富穿上了一件自己刺绣的衣服，姑娘们都来称赞她的精巧细致的手艺。
