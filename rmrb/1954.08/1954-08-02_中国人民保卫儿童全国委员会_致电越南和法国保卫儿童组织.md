Title: China National Committee for the Defence of Children Cables to Vietnamese and French Child Protection Organizations
Author:
Date: 1954-08-02
Page: 2

    Congratulations on the Agreement Reached on the Indochina Question
    China National Committee for the Defence of Children
    Cables to Vietnamese and French Child Protection Organizations
    To the National Committee for the Protection of Children of Vietnam:
    The Geneva Conference reached an agreement on the cessation of hostilities and restoration of peace in Indochina, ending the bloody war imposed on the Vietnamese people, bringing peace to the people of Indochina and all of Asia, and freeing children from the scourge of war. This is the result of the Vietnamese people's eight years of arduous struggle and the victory of the world's forces for peace. The China National Committee for the Defence of Children extends its warmest congratulations to you.
    In order to protect children, we are determined to work together with the Vietnamese people and all peace-loving people around the world to strive for the thorough implementation of all the agreements of the Geneva Conference and to continue our efforts to consolidate peace in Asia and the world.
                                                    China National Committee for the Defence of Children
      July 29, 1954
                                          (Xinhua News Agency) To the French National Movement for the Protection of Children:
    The Geneva Conference reached an agreement on the cessation of hostilities and restoration of peace in Indochina, ending eight years of brutal war, allowing the people and children of Indochina to regain peaceful lives. This is the result of the unremitting struggle of the French people and all peace-loving people around the world, and a victory for the movement to defend peace and protect children. We would like to express our warm congratulations. In order to protect the rights of children, we are determined to work with the French people and all peace-loving people around the world to strive for the thorough implementation of all the agreements of the Geneva Conference, and to continue to fight for the maintenance and consolidation of peace in Asia and the world.
                                                China National Committee for the Defence of Children
    July 29, 1954
                (Xinhua News Agency)

### 中国人民保卫儿童全国委员会  致电越南和法国保卫儿童组织

1954-08-02
第2版()
专栏：

    祝贺印度支那问题达成协议
    中国人民保卫儿童全国委员会
    致电越南和法国保卫儿童组织越南全国保卫儿童委员会：
    日内瓦会议关于印度支那停战和恢复和平问题达成了协议，停止了强加于越南人民身上的流血战争，给印度支那及全亚洲人民带来了和平，使儿童免受战争的灾难。这是越南人民八年来艰苦奋斗的结果，是全世界和平力量的胜利。中国人民保卫儿童全国委员会谨向您们致以热烈的庆贺。
    为了保卫儿童，我们决心和越南人民以及全世界爱好和平的人民一起，为争取日内瓦会议全部协议的彻底实现，为巩固亚洲与世界的和平继续努力。
                                                    中国人民保卫儿童全国委员会
      一九五四年七月二十九日
                                          （新华社）法国保卫儿童全国运动：
    日内瓦会议关于印度支那停战和恢复和平问题达成协议，停止了八年来的残酷战争，使印度支那的人民和儿童重新获得和平的生活。这是法国人民和全世界爱好和平人民坚持不懈斗争的结果，是保卫和平保卫儿童运动的胜利，特此表示热烈的庆贺。为了保卫儿童的权利，我们决心与法国人民和全世界爱好和平的人民共同争取日内瓦会议全部协议的彻底实现，为维护和巩固亚洲及世界的和平继续奋斗。
                                                中国人民保卫儿童全国委员会
    一九五四年七月二十九日
                （新华社）
