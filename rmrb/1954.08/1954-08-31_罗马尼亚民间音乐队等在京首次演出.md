Title: Romanian Folk Music Ensemble and Others Give First Performance in Beijing

Author: 
Date: 1954-08-31
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, August 29th: The Romanian Folk Music Ensemble and Romanian renowned violinist, Yan Voicu, winner of the Third World Youth and Student Peace Festival, gave their first concert in the capital's Experimental Theater on the evening of the 29th.

Attending the concert were Ding Xilin, Vice Minister of the Ministry of Culture of the Central People's Government; Fan Changjiang, Deputy Secretary-General of the Cultural and Educational Committee of the Government Administration Council; Hong Shen, Director of the Bureau of Foreign Cultural Relations; Chu Tunan, President of the Chinese People's Association for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries; Wu Qing, Special Commissioner of the Soviet and Eastern European Department of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs of the Central People's Government; and figures from the cultural and artistic circles of the capital, including Lü Ji, Zhao Feng, Cai Ruohong, Ding Li, Wu Xiaobang, Ma Ke, Qu Xixian, Sun Shen, Li Ling, Zou Difan, Li Yuanqing, Zha Fuxi, Yang Yinliu, and Zhang Mei.

Also attending the concert were Meng Tiannu, Chargé d'Affaires ad interim of the Romanian Embassy in China, Military Attaché Lieutenant Colonel Dubulin, Romanian microbiology expert Stefan Nicolau, and Romanian renowned director Sekfovlid Volfetel, among others.

At the concert, Ding Xilin, Vice Minister of the Ministry of Culture of the Central People's Government, delivered a welcome speech. He said that the Romanian Folk Music Ensemble possesses Romania's excellent artistic traditions. Since its formation in 1949, under the care and leadership of the Romanian Workers' Party and the government, it has gained a high reputation in Romania and in fraternal countries such as the Soviet Union, Czechoslovakia, Hungary, and Bulgaria. The Romanian Folk Music Ensemble made outstanding contributions at the World Youth and Student Festivals held in Berlin and Bucharest, and achieved great success at the competition in Helsinki. The Romanian Folk Music Ensemble has also become one of the most beloved performance groups of the Romanian people because it frequently performs in factories, construction sites, state farms, and collective farms. Comrade violinist Voicu won first prize in the competition of the Third World Youth and Student Peace Festival due to his extraordinary musical talent.

Ding Xilin said: Your visit to our country will not only give the working people of our country the opportunity to appreciate the excellent musical art of the Romanian people, but also, through your excellent performances, will enable the people of our country to further understand the brilliant achievements of the Romanian people in music and art. I am convinced that your visit and performances will greatly contribute to promoting cultural exchanges between China and Romania and enhancing the brotherly friendship between the peoples of China and Romania. I wish you success and victory in your visit and performances in our country!

Kerzhan Constantin, head of the Romanian Folk Music Ensemble, spoke. He said that from the performance of Romanian famous violinist Yan Voicu tonight, and from the several folk songs played by the Romanian Folk Music Ensemble, you will gain some understanding of our country's music. We will try our best to play these songs authentically, so that you have an impression of the characteristics of the Romanian people. Although we are thousands of miles apart, coming to your country is like coming home. Although we are far apart, we understand each other's lives and our aspirations. We realize not only that we are music performers, but also that we are envoys of friendship. We will do our best to live up to our glorious mission.

Subsequently, the Romanian Folk Music Ensemble and the Romanian renowned violinist Yan Voicu performed many wonderful programs, which were warmly welcomed by the audience.

### 罗马尼亚民间音乐队等在京首次演出

1954-08-31
第1版()
专栏：

    罗马尼亚民间音乐队等在京首次演出
    【新华社二十九日讯】罗马尼亚民间音乐队及罗马尼亚名提琴家、第三届世界青年与学生和平联欢节得奖者扬·沃伊库的首次演奏会，二十九日晚在首都实验剧场举行。
    出席演奏会的，有中央人民政府文化部副部长丁西林，政务院文化教育委员会副秘书长范长江，对外文化联络事务局局长洪深，中国人民对外文化协会会长楚图南，中央人民政府外交部苏联东欧司专员吴青，首都文化艺术界人士吕骥、赵沨、蔡若虹、丁里、吴晓邦、马可、瞿希贤、孙慎、李凌、邹荻帆、李元庆、查阜西、杨荫浏、章枚等人。
    出席演奏会的，还有罗马尼亚驻我国大使馆临时代办孟天努、武官杜布林中校等和罗马尼亚微生物学专家斯捷凡·尼古劳，罗马尼亚名导演色克夫利德·沃尔费捷尔等。
    在演奏会上，中央人民政府文化部副部长丁西林首先致欢迎词。他说，罗马尼亚民间音乐队是具有着罗马尼亚优秀的艺术传统的，自一九四九年组成以来，在罗马尼亚工人党和政府的关怀和领导下，在罗马尼亚国内和在苏联、捷克斯洛伐克、匈牙利、保加利亚等兄弟国家获得了很高的声誉，在柏林和在布加勒斯特举行的世界青年与学生联欢节上，罗马尼亚民间音乐队作了出色的贡献，在赫尔辛基的比赛会上也获得了很大成功。罗马尼亚民间音乐队也由于经常在工厂、工地、国营农场和集体农庄表演，成为罗马尼亚人民最热爱的演奏团体之一。小提琴家沃伊库同志，由于他卓越的音乐演奏天才，在第三届世界青年与学生和平联欢节的比赛中获得一等奖。
    丁西林说：你们这次来到我们的国家，不仅使我国的劳动人民能有机会欣赏罗马尼亚人民优秀的音乐艺术，而且通过你们卓越的演奏将使我国人民更进一步的了解到罗马尼亚人民在音乐艺术方面的光辉成就。我深信，通过你们的访问和演出对于促进中罗两国文化交流，增进中罗两国人民兄弟般的友谊，一定将会有很大的贡献。我预祝你们在我国的访问演出获得成功和胜利！
    罗马尼亚民间音乐队团长克尔然·康斯坦丁讲话。他说，今晚从罗马尼亚著名提琴家扬·沃伊库的演奏，以及从罗马尼亚民间音乐队演奏的几支民间曲子中，你们将获得一些对我国音乐的了解。我们将尽量真实地来演奏这些曲子，以使你们对罗马尼亚人民的特性有一个印象。虽然我们相隔万里，但我们到你们的国家来就像到了自己的家里一样。虽然我们相隔很远，但我们相互了解我们的生活，以及我们的愿望。我们不仅意识到我们是音乐演奏者，同时也意识到我们是友谊的使者。我们将尽力不辜负我们的光荣使命。
    接着，由罗马尼亚民间音乐队及罗马尼亚名提琴家扬·沃伊库演奏了许多精彩节目，受到全场热烈欢迎。
