Title: The Newly Published Volume 26 of the Great Soviet Encyclopedia Details Comrade Mao Zedong and His Works
Author: Lǐ Hé
Date: 1954-08-27
Page: 1

    The Newly Published Volume 26 of the Great Soviet Encyclopedia
    Details Comrade Mao Zedong and His Works
    Our Correspondent in Moscow, Li He, reports: The newly published Volume 26 of the Great Soviet Encyclopedia introduces Comrade Mao Zedong and his works, and publishes a full-page photograph of Comrade Mao Zedong.
    The encyclopedia writes: "Mao Zedong is an outstanding Chinese political and state activist, a great theorist—a Marxist, a tested leader of the Chinese Communist Party and the leader of the Chinese people, the founder of the Chinese People's Democratic State, Chairman of the Central Committee of the Chinese Communist Party, and Chairman of the Central People's Government of the People's Republic of China."
    The encyclopedia systematically narrates Comrade Mao Zedong's activities and writings in various periods of the Chinese revolution. It writes: "The Chinese Communist Party, led by Mao Zedong and his comrades-in-arms, relying on the support of the international democratic and socialist camp headed by the Soviet Union, after more than 20 years of long and arduous struggle, guided the Chinese people to achieve a great victory over imperialist forces and domestic reactionaries. In the process of this struggle, the party's strategies and tactics formulated under the leadership of Mao Zedong are brilliant examples of creatively applying the general principles of Marxism-Leninism to China's specific historical conditions and to the practice of the Chinese revolution."
    The encyclopedia writes: "Mao Zedong's works are a valuable enrichment of Marxist science and have enormous theoretical and practical benefits for all international national liberation and communist movements."
    The newly published Volume 26 of the Great Soviet Encyclopedia introduces Marx, Malenkov, and uses thirty-two pages to introduce Marxism-Leninism.

### 新出版的苏联大百科全书第二十六卷  详细介绍毛泽东同志和他的著作
李何
1954-08-27
第1版()
专栏：

    新出版的苏联大百科全书第二十六卷
    详细介绍毛泽东同志和他的著作
    本报驻莫斯科记者李何报道：新近出版的苏联大百科全书第二十六卷介绍了毛泽东同志和他的著作，并以整页篇幅登载了毛泽东同志的照片。
    大百科全书写道：“毛泽东是杰出的中国政治和国家活动家，伟大的理论家——马克思主义者，经过考验的中国共产党的领导者和中国人民的领袖，中国人民民主国家的创立者，中国共产党中央委员会主席，中华人民共和国中央人民政府主席。”
    大百科全书有系统地叙述了毛泽东同志在中国革命各个时期的活动和著作。它写道：“毛泽东和他的战友们所领导的中国共产党，依靠以苏联为首的国际民主和社会主义阵营的支持，经过二十多年长期艰苦的斗争，引导了中国人民取得对帝国主义势力和国内反动派的伟大胜利。在这个斗争进程中在毛泽东领导下所制订的党的战略和策略，是创造性地运用马克思列宁主义的一般原理到中国具体的历史条件中去，到中国革命实践中去的卓越榜样。”
    大百科全书写道：“毛泽东著作是对马克思主义科学的宝贵的丰富，对于一切国际民族解放和共产主义运动有巨大的理论的和实际的益处。”
    新出版的苏联大百科全书第二十六卷介绍了马克思，马林科夫，并以三十二页介绍了马克思主义—列宁主义。
