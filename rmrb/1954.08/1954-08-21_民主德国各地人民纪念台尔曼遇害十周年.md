Title: People in Various Parts of Democratic Germany Commemorate the Tenth Anniversary of Thalmann's Murder
Author:
Date: 1954-08-21
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency, Berlin, August 20th: People in various parts of the German Democratic Republic held gatherings on August 18th to commemorate the tenth anniversary of the murder of Ernst Thälmann, leader of the German working class and outstanding fighter of the international workers' movement.

    On the morning of August 18th, the Central Committee of the Socialist Unity Party of Germany (SED) and the Erfurt District leadership of the SED held a memorial meeting at Buchenwald near Weimar, where Thälmann was murdered by the Nazi SS. The meeting was attended by Hilde Benjamin and Ölsner, members of the Political Bureau of the SED Central Committee; Rosa Thälmann, Thälmann's widow; Geschke, chairman of the Committee of Anti-Fascist Resistance Fighters; representatives of various democratic parties and mass organizations; and representatives of anti-fascist resistance fighters from European countries.

    Also attending the memorial meeting were diplomatic envoys of the Soviet Union, the People's Republic of China, and other people's democratic countries to the German Democratic Republic.

    Hilde Benjamin, member of the Political Bureau of the SED Central Committee, delivered a report at the meeting commemorating Ernst Thälmann's life. He recounted Thälmann's life and the selfless struggle he waged. He emphasized that the SED would resolutely implement Ernst Thälmann's will, continue to fight for the unity of all German working people, and for the establishment of a unified and democratic Germany on the basis of Marxism-Leninism.

    After the meeting, an unveiling ceremony was held for the "Resistance Movement Museum" established on the site of the former Buchenwald concentration camp.

    On the evening of August 18th, the SED Central Committee and the Berlin organization of the SED also held a grand memorial meeting attended by 3,000 people at the Friedrichstadt-Palast theater in Berlin.

    On this day, memorial meetings were also held in Leipzig, Dresden, Magdeburg, and other towns and villages in the German Democratic Republic.

### 民主德国各地人民纪念台尔曼遇害十周年

1954-08-21
第4版()
专栏：

    民主德国各地人民纪念台尔曼遇害十周年
    【新华社柏林二十日电】德意志民主共和国各地人民于八月十八日纷纷举行集会，纪念德国工人阶级的领袖和国际工人运动的杰出战士恩斯特·台尔曼遇害十周年。
    八月十八日上午，德国统一社会党中央委员会和德国统一社会党欧法特专区领导机构在纳粹党卫军杀害台尔曼的地方——魏玛附近的布痕瓦尔德——举行了纪念大会。参加大会的有德国统一社会党中央委员会政治局委员希德万、厄斯纳，台尔曼的夫人罗萨·台尔曼，反法西斯抵抗战士委员会主席格什克，各民主党派和群众团体的代表和欧洲各国反法西斯抵抗战士的代表。
    参加纪念会的还有苏联、中华人民共和国和其他人民民主国家驻德意志民主共和国的外交使节。
    德国统一社会党中央委员会政治局委员希德万在大会上作了纪念恩斯特·台尔曼生前事迹的报告。他叙述了台尔曼的生平和他所进行的无私的斗争。他强调说：德国统一社会党将坚决执行恩斯特·台尔曼的遗嘱，在马克思—列宁主义的基础上，继续为争取全体德国劳动人民的团结一致、为建立统一和民主的德国而斗争。
    会后，并举行了在布痕瓦尔德集中营旧址建立起来的“抵抗运动博物馆”的揭幕仪式。
    八月十八日晚间，德国统一社会党中央委员会和德国统一社会党柏林组织又在柏林腓特烈宫剧院举行了有三千人参加的盛大纪念会。
    这一天，在来比锡、德累斯顿、马格德堡以及德意志民主共和国的其他城镇和乡村也都举行了纪念会。
