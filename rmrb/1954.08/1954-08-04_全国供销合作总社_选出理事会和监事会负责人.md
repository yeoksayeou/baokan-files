Title: All-China Federation of Supply and Marketing Cooperatives Elects Heads of Council and Supervisory Committee
Author:
Date: 1954-08-04
Page: 2

    All-China Federation of Supply and Marketing Cooperatives
    Elects Heads of Council and Supervisory Committee
    The First Council and Supervisory Committee of the All-China Federation of Supply and Marketing Cooperatives held their first meetings on July 31st, respectively. The Council meeting elected Cheng Zihua as Chairman of the Council, and Zhang Qilong, Yan Guhang, Meng Yongqian, Liang Yao, and Deng Chenxi as Vice-Chairmen. The Supervisory Committee meeting elected Zeng Shan as Chairman of the Supervisory Committee, and Dai Xiaodong and Yu Shude as Vice-Chairmen.
                                  (Xinhua News Agency)

### 全国供销合作总社  选出理事会和监事会负责人

1954-08-04
第2版()
专栏：

    全国供销合作总社
    选出理事会和监事会负责人
    中华全国供销合作总社第一届理事会和监事会在七月三十一日分别举行了第一次会议。理事会会议选出程子华为理事会主任，张启龙、阎顾行、孟用潜、梁耀、邓辰西为副主任。监事会会议选出曾山为监事会主任，戴晓东、于树德为副主任。
                                  （新华社）
