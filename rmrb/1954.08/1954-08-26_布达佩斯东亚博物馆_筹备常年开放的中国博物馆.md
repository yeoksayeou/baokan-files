Title: Budapest East Asia Museum to Prepare a Permanently Open Chinese Museum
Author:
Date: 1954-08-26
Page: 4

    Budapest East Asia Museum
    Preparing a Permanently Open Chinese Museum
   [Xinhua News Agency dispatch from Budapest] The Budapest East Asia Museum is preparing a permanently open Chinese museum. The collection of exhibits in this Chinese museum is the most abundant in Central Europe. It will display the most famous Chinese porcelain, reliefs, bronzes, unearthed antiquities, etc., many of which are very valuable rare items. It will also display many paintings and woodcuts by modern and past Chinese artists, including works by the famous old painter Qi Baishi in 1920 and after liberation.

### 布达佩斯东亚博物馆  筹备常年开放的中国博物馆

1954-08-26
第4版()
专栏：

    布达佩斯东亚博物馆
    筹备常年开放的中国博物馆
   【新华社讯】布达佩斯消息：布达佩斯东亚博物馆正在筹备一个常年开放的中国博物馆。这个中国博物馆所收集的陈列品在中欧是最丰富的。它将陈列最著名的中国瓷器、浮雕、青铜像、出土古物等，其中有许多是很贵重的稀有物品。它还将陈列许多现代和过去的中国艺术家的绘画和木刻，其中有著名老画家齐白石在一九二零年和解放后的作品。
