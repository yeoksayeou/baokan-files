Title: Lebanese Prime Minister and Others Oppose Following the US to Join Aggression Bloc
Author:
Date: 1954-08-04
Page: 4

TASS, Cairo, August 2nd: Arab countries' public opinion opposes joining aggression blocs. "Rouz El Youssef Weekly" wrote that Lebanese Prime Minister Yafi, during his recent visit to Egypt, told reporters: "The governments and people of Arab countries oppose joining the Turco-Pakistan Pact. Joining this pact means following the policies of the United States."

The Saudi Arabian ambassador to Egypt told the "Al-Gomhuria" newspaper that Arab countries are "not prepared to join alliances with Western countries because such alliances harbor imperialist aims."

### 黎巴嫩总理等反对追随美国参加侵略集团

1954-08-04
第4版()
专栏：

    黎巴嫩总理等反对追随美国参加侵略集团
    【新华社三日讯】塔斯社开罗二日讯：阿拉伯国家的舆论界反对参加侵略集团。“鲁兹·优素福周刊”写道，黎巴嫩总理雅非不久以前在埃及访问时曾向记者说：“阿拉伯国家的政府和人民反对参加土巴协定。参加这个协定就是追随美国的政策。”
    沙特阿拉伯驻埃及大使向“共和国报”记者说，阿拉伯国家“不准备参加同西方国家的联盟，因为这样的联盟是抱着帝国主义目的的”。
