Title: Romania Appoints First Deputy Chairman and Deputy Chairmen of the Council of Ministers
Author:
Date: 1954-08-22
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Bucharest, August 19th: TASS reports that the Presidium of the Romanian National Assembly, based on the recommendation of Gheorghe Gheorghiu-Dej, Chairman of the Council of Ministers, has appointed Iosif Chișinevschi, Chivu Stoica, and Alexandru Moghioroș as First Deputy Chairmen of the Council of Ministers; Emil Bodnăraș, Mihail Constantinescu, and Petre Borilă as Deputy Chairmen of the Council of Ministers.

### 罗马尼亚任命部长会议第一副主席和副主席

1954-08-22
第4版()
专栏：

    罗马尼亚任命部长会议第一副主席和副主席
    【新华社二十日讯】塔斯社布加勒斯特十九日讯：罗马尼亚国民议会主席团根据部长会议主席乔治乌—德治的建议，任命约·基辛涅夫斯基、基·斯托伊卡和亚·莫吉奥罗希为部长会议第一副主席；埃·波德纳拉希、米·康斯坦丁尼斯库和彼·波里勒为部长会议副主席。
