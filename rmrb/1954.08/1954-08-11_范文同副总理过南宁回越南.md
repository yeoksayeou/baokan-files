Title: Vice Premier Pham Van Dong Passes Through Nanning on His Way Back to Vietnam
Author:
Date: 1954-08-11
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Nanning, August 10th: Vice Premier and Acting Foreign Minister of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam, Pham Van Dong, accompanied by Wang Tuo, Deputy Director of the Reception Department of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs of the Central People's Government, arrived in Nanning by special train at 4:06 AM on August 9th after passing through Jinan, Shanghai, and other places. He left Nanning by special train at 9:00 PM on the same day to return to his country. Accompanying him were Phan Anh, Minister of Industry and Commerce of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam, Tran Cong Tuong, Deputy Minister of Justice, and other members of the delegation.

When Vice Premier Pham Van Dong and his party arrived in Nanning, Chen Manyuan, Acting Chairman of the Guangxi Provincial People's Government, and many others went to the station to welcome them. After Vice Premier Pham Van Dong alighted from the train, he warmly shook hands with Acting Chairman Chen Manyuan and others, and accepted flowers presented by the Young Pioneers of Nanning City.

In the afternoon of the 9th, Chen Manyuan, Acting Chairman of the Guangxi Provincial People's Government, hosted a banquet in honor of Vice Premier Pham Van Dong. Phan Anh, Minister of Industry and Commerce of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam, and Tran Cong Tuong, Deputy Minister of Justice, also attended the banquet.

Vice Premier Pham Van Dong and his party were accompanied to the Sino-Vietnamese border - Friendship Pass - by Wang Tuo, Deputy Director of the Reception Department of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs of the Central People's Government, Xiao Yizhou and Mo Naiqun, Vice Chairmen of the Guangxi Provincial People's Government, Hao Zhongshi, First Deputy Secretary of the Guangxi Provincial Committee of the Chinese Communist Party, and Wang Tan, Director of the Foreign Affairs Office of the Guangxi Provincial People's Government, totaling five people.

### 范文同副总理过南宁回越南

1954-08-11
第1版()
专栏：

    范文同副总理过南宁回越南
    据新华社南宁十日电：越南民主共和国副总理兼代理外交部部长范文同在我中央人民政府外交部交际处副处长王拓的陪同下，经过济南、上海等地，在八月九日晨四时零六分乘专车到达南宁，并在当天下午九时乘专车离南宁回国。同行的有：越南民主共和国政府工商部部长潘英、司法部副部长陈公祥，及代表团其他随员等。
    范文同副总理等一行到达南宁时，前往车站欢迎的有广西省人民政府代理主席陈漫远等多人。范文同副总理下车后，亲切地和陈漫远代理主席等握手，并接受了南宁市少年先锋队的献花。
    九日下午，广西省人民政府代理主席陈漫远曾设宴招待范文同副总理。越南民主共和国政府工商部部长潘英和司法部副部长陈公祥也参加了宴会。
    范文同副总理等一行由中央人民政府外交部交际处副处长王拓，广西省人民政府副主席萧一舟、莫迺群、中共广西省委员会第一副书记郝中士以及广西省人民政府外事处处长王谭等五人同车陪送至中、越国境——睦南关。
