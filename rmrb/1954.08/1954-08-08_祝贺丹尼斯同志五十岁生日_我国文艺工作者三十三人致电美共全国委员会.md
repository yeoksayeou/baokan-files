Title: Congratulating Comrade Dennis on His Fiftieth Birthday: Thirty-Three Chinese Literary and Art Workers Send a Telegram to the National Committee of the American Communist Party
Author:
Date: 1954-08-08
Page: 4

    Xinhua News Agency, 7th. Thirty-three Chinese literary and art workers sent a telegram to the National Committee of the American Communist Party on August 1st, congratulating Eugene Dennis, General Secretary of the National Committee of the American Communist Party, on his fiftieth birthday.
    The telegram said: "On the occasion of the fiftieth birthday of Comrade Eugene Dennis, General Secretary of the National Committee of the American Communist Party, we, the literary and art workers of China, are pleased to have the opportunity to congratulate and pay tribute to Comrade Dennis. At the same time, we cannot but express our angry protest against the persecution of political prisoners by the American government. We extend our respects to the American people and the American Communist Party, and wish you ever greater achievements in the struggle for peace and democracy!"
    The telegram also included the full text of the congratulatory telegram to Dennis and the protest telegram to Eisenhower.

### 祝贺丹尼斯同志五十岁生日  我国文艺工作者三十三人致电美共全国委员会

1954-08-08
第4版()
专栏：

    祝贺丹尼斯同志五十岁生日
    我国文艺工作者三十三人致电美共全国委员会
    【新华社七日讯】中国文学艺术工作者三十三人在八月一日致电美国共产党全国委员会，祝贺美国共产党全国委员会总书记丹尼斯五十寿辰。
    电文说：值此美国共产党全国委员会总书记尤金·丹尼斯五十寿辰之际，我们，中国的文艺工作者，欣幸能有机会向丹尼斯同志祝寿和致敬。同时，也不能不把我们对美国政府迫害政治犯的愤怒抗议表达出来。我们谨向美国人民和美国共产党致敬，并祝您们在争取和平、民主的运动中获得日益巨大的成就！
    电文并附有致丹尼斯的贺电和致艾森豪威尔的抗议电稿的全文。
