Title: Premier and Foreign Minister Zhou Enlai's Message to Chairman Gheorghe Gheorghiu-Dej and Foreign Minister Bujiqi
Author:
Date: 1954-08-23
Page: 1

To Comrade Gheorghe Gheorghiu-Dej, Chairman of the Council of Ministers of the Romanian People's Republic:
To Comrade Simion Bujiqi, Minister of Foreign Affairs of the Romanian People's Republic:

	On the occasion of the tenth anniversary of the liberation of Romania, please accept my warm congratulations to the government and people of the Romanian People's Republic. I wish the Romanian people greater achievements in the cause of socialist construction. I also wish that the unbreakable friendship between China and Romania will be further developed and consolidated in the common cause of striving for the further relaxation of international tensions and defending world peace.
		Zhou Enlai, August 21, 1954, Beijing

### 周恩来总理兼外长  致乔治乌—德治主席和布吉奇外长电
周恩来
1954-08-23
第1版()
专栏：

    周恩来总理兼外长
    致乔治乌—德治主席和布吉奇外长电罗马尼亚人民共和国部长会议主席乔治乌—德治同志：罗马尼亚人民共和国外交部部长西米昂·布吉奇同志：
    值此罗马尼亚解放十周年之际，请接受我对罗马尼亚人民共和国政府和人民的热烈祝贺。祝罗马尼亚人民在社会主义的建设事业中获得更多的成就。并祝中罗两国牢不可破的友谊在共同争取国际紧张局势的进一步缓和和保卫世界和平的事业中更加发展和巩固。
          周恩来  一九五四年八月二十一日  北京
