Title: Our Country's Observer Delegates Leave Beijing for Moscow to Attend International Aviation Model Competition
Author:
Date: 1954-08-28
Page: 1

    Attending International Aviation Model Competition
    Our Country's Observer Delegates Leave Beijing for Moscow
   [Xinhua News Agency, 24th] Upon invitation from the Central Committee of the Soviet Union's Volunteer Society for Assisting the Army, Navy, and Air Force, the Central National Defense Sports Club sent observer delegates Ren Chao, Li Mao-yun, and translator Wang Yude, a total of three people, to Moscow by plane on the 24th to attend the International Aviation Model Competition to be held from August 26th to September 12th.

### 参加航空模型国际比赛  我国观察代表离京去莫斯科

1954-08-28
第1版()
专栏：

    参加航空模型国际比赛
    我国观察代表离京去莫斯科
   【新华社二十四日讯】应苏联志愿支援陆海空军协会中央委员会的邀请，我中央国防体育俱乐部派观察代表任超、李茂芸和翻译王禹德等三人在二十四日乘飞机前往莫斯科，参加将于八月二十六日至九月十二日举行的航空模型国际比赛。
