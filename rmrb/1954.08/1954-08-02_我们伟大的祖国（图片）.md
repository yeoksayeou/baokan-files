    Our Great Motherland
    Raising the scientific level of agricultural production is an important condition for ensuring the yield and quality of agricultural products. The picture shows the staff of the Zhejiang Provincial Academy of Agricultural Sciences' experimental farm cultivating the late-season japonica rice variety "Laolaiqing" in order to promote it to farmers.
