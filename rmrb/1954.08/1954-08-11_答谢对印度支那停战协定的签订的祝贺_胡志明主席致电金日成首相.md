Title: Chairman Ho Chi Minh Cables Premier Kim Il-sung to Thank Him for Congratulations on the Signing of the Indochina Armistice Agreement
Author:
Date: 1954-08-11
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency, North Vietnam, August 10th: Ho Chi Minh, President of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam, sent a telegram on August 1st to Kim Il-sung, Premier of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea, to thank him for his congratulations on the signing of the Indochina Armistice Agreement. The telegram reads as follows:

	I would like to express my warmest gratitude to the government of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea and to you, Comrade Premier, for your telegram congratulating the signing of the Vietnam Armistice Agreement.

	In the struggle of our two countries to consolidate peace and strive for national reunification, the friendship between the peoples of Korea and Vietnam will grow day by day.

	I wish you, Comrade Premier, good health.

Pham Van Dong, Deputy Prime Minister and Acting Minister of Foreign Affairs of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam, also sent a telegram to Nam Il, Minister of Foreign Affairs of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea, to thank him for his congratulations.

### 答谢对印度支那停战协定的签订的祝贺  胡志明主席致电金日成首相

1954-08-11
第4版()
专栏：

    答谢对印度支那停战协定的签订的祝贺
    胡志明主席致电金日成首相
   【新华社十日讯】据越南通讯社北越十日讯：越南民主共和国胡志明主席在八月一日致电答谢朝鲜民主主义人民共和国金日成首相对印度支那停战协定的签订的祝贺。电文如下：
    我谨向朝鲜民主主义人民共和国政府和您，首相同志，表示我最热烈的谢意，感谢您来电祝贺越南停战协定的签订。
    在我们两国为巩固和平和争取国家统一的斗争中，朝鲜和越南两国人民之间的友谊将与日俱增。
    我敬祝您，首相同志，身体健康。
    越南民主共和国副总理兼代理外交部部长范文同也致电朝鲜民主主义人民共和国外务相南日感谢他的祝贺。
