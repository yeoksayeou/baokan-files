Title: Preparatory Meeting of the Indochina International Supervisory and Control Commission Opens on the 5th, Welcomes Representatives from the Democratic Republic of Vietnam, France, and Other Concerned Countries
Author:
Date: 1954-08-07
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, New Delhi, August 6th: The preparatory meeting of the Indochina International Supervisory and Control Commission was held at 9:30 AM on August 5th to welcome representatives from the Democratic Republic of Vietnam, France, Bao Dai Vietnam, the Kingdom of Laos, and the Kingdom of Cambodia.

The meeting was presided over by India's Krishna Menon, who first gave a welcoming address to the representatives of the concerned countries. The chief representative of the Polish delegation, Ogrodzinski, and the Canadian representative, McDonnell, also spoke, expressing their welcome to the representatives of the concerned countries and their willingness to assist in the smooth execution of the Commission's tasks.

Representatives from the concerned countries also spoke at the meeting. The French representative, French Ambassador to India, Ostrorog, said that France would do its utmost to enable the International Commission to carry out its work smoothly and receive all necessary facilities.

The representative of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam, Hoang Van Hoan, said that the government of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam is willing to do everything possible to strictly abide by the armistice agreement and assist the International Commission in carrying out its tasks, and hopes that all other concerned parties will also be willing to do so. Hoang Van Hoan said that the Democratic Republic of Vietnam will work in a spirit of sincerity to ensure the success of the meeting.

The representative of the Kingdom of Laos, Nosavan, and the representative of the Kingdom of Cambodia, Song Sang, both expressed their willingness to cooperate with the International Commission in their speeches. The representative of Bao Dai Vietnam, Ngo Ngoc Do, also spoke at the meeting.

After this brief meeting, the preparatory meeting of the three countries immediately began to discuss other items on the agenda.

At 4:00 PM, representatives from the Democratic Republic of Vietnam, France, and Bao Dai Vietnam participated in the preparatory meeting to discuss the arrangements for the advance delegation and the International Commission.

### 印度支那国际监督和监察委员会预备会议五日开会  欢迎越南民主共和国、法国等有关国家代表

1954-08-07
第1版()
专栏：

    印度支那国际监督和监察委员会预备会议五日开会
    欢迎越南民主共和国、法国等有关国家代表
    【新华社新德里六日电】印度支那国际监督和监察委员会预备会议在八月五日上午九时半举行会议，欢迎越南民主共和国、法国、保大越南、老挝王国和柬埔寨王国的代表。
    会议由印度的克里希纳·梅农主持，他首先致词欢迎各有关国家的代表，波兰代表团首席代表奥格罗京斯基和加拿大代表麦克唐奈都讲了话，他们对各有关国家代表表示欢迎，并表示愿意帮助顺利地执行委员会的任务。
    各有关国家的代表也都在会上讲话。法国代表、法国驻印度大使奥斯特罗洛格说，法国将尽它力之所及，使国际委员会能顺利地进行工作，并得到一切的便利。
    越南民主共和国代表黄文欢说，越南民主共和国政府愿尽一切力量严格遵守停战的协定，并协助国际委员会执行它的任务，希望所有其他有关方面也愿意这样做。黄文欢说，越南民主共和国将本着真诚的精神进行工作，以保证会议的成功。
    老挝王国代表诺沙凡和柬埔寨王国代表松山在讲话中都表示愿意同国际委员会合作。保大越南代表吴玉玳也在会上讲了话。
    这个简短的会议结束后，三国代表的预备会议立刻又开始讨论议程上的其他项目。
    下午四时，越南民主共和国、法国和保大越南的代表团参加了预备会议，讨论了先遣代表团和国际委员会的安排问题。
