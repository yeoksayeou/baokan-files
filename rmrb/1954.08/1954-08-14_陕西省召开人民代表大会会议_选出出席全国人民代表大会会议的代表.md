Title: Shaanxi Province Convenes People's Congress Meeting, Elects Deputies to the National People's Congress Meeting
Author:
Date: 1954-08-14
Page: 1

(This article comes from a special column)

    [This dispatch from our newspaper, Xi'an, 13th] The first session of the First People's Congress of Shaanxi Province was held in Xi'an from August 5th to 12th. A total of 361 delegates attended the meeting.

    At the meeting, all delegates listened to reports made by Zhao Shoushan, Chairman of the Shaanxi Provincial People's Government, Guo Aizheng, Director of the Shaanxi Provincial People's Government Finance Department, and Shi Yizhi, Secretary-General of the Shaanxi Provincial People's Government, respectively, on "The Work and Future Tasks of the Shaanxi Provincial People's Government in the Past Year and a Half," "The Shaanxi Provincial People's Government's Final Financial Accounts for 1953 and Financial Budget for 1954," and "Continuing to Deepen the Publicity and Discussion of the Draft Constitution of the People's Republic of China to Welcome the Convening of the National People's Congress."

    The meeting seriously examined the work of the Shaanxi Provincial People's Government over the past year and a half, and unanimously agreed on the important tasks currently facing the people of the province as pointed out in the report: supporting the country's industrial construction, especially ensuring the scheduled completion of key basic construction projects and the Baoji-Chengdu Railway construction; further vigorously carrying out the agricultural production increase movement centered on mutual aid and cooperation in rural areas, and striving to overfulfill this year's grain and cotton production increase plans. The meeting also put forward criticisms and suggestions for improvement regarding shortcomings in the government's work.

    During the meeting, the delegates earnestly discussed the Draft Constitution of the People's Republic of China, unanimously expressed their support, and stated that they would work hard to further publicize the basic spirit and content of the Draft Constitution to the masses, in order to greet the convening of the National People's Congress; after the promulgation of the Constitution, they would guarantee to abide by the Constitution in an exemplary manner and strive for the country to move towards socialism.

    During the meeting, a total of 270 proposals were submitted by the delegates. The Proposal Examination Committee carefully examined them one by one and will hand them over to the government for separate handling.

    The meeting elected nineteen deputies to attend the National People's Congress meeting by secret ballot. The elected deputies are: Ma Mingfang, Pan Zili, Zhao Shoushan, Li Fenglian (female), Meng Tianlu, Wang Debiao, Xu Jingzhang, Pu Zhongzhi, Han Xiacun, Cao Guanqun (female), Li Furen, Yang Zhifang (female), Zhang Fenghui, Han Zhaoe, Yu Zhenying, Han Wangchen, Su Zichen, Li Fuqing (female), and An Wenqin.

### 陕西省召开人民代表大会会议  选出出席全国人民代表大会会议的代表

1954-08-14
第1版()
专栏：

    陕西省召开人民代表大会会议
    选出出席全国人民代表大会会议的代表
    【本报西安十三日电】陕西省第一届人民代表大会第一次会议于八月五日到十二日在西安市举行。出席这次会议的代表共三百六十一名。
    会上，全体代表听了陕西省人民政府主席赵寿山、陕西省人民政府财政厅厅长郭艾正、陕西省人民政府秘书长时逸之等分别所作的“陕西省人民政府一年半来的工作和今后任务”、“陕西省人民政府关于一九五三年度财政决算和一九五四年度财政预算”和“继续深入宣传和讨论中华人民共和国宪法草案迎接全国人民代表大会的召开”等报告。
    会议严肃地审查了陕西省人民政府一年半来的工作，并一致同意报告中指出的全省人民当前重要的任务：支援国家的工业建设，特别是保证重要的基本建设和宝成铁路施工的如期完成；在农村中进一步大力开展以互助合作为中心的农业增产运动，全力争取超额完成今年粮、棉增产计划。会议同时对政府工作中的缺点也提出了批评和改进意见。
    会议中，代表们认真地讨论了中华人民共和国宪法草案，一致表示拥护；并表示今后要努力进一步向群众宣传宪法草案的基本精神和内容，以努力工作来迎接全国人民代表大会的召开；在宪法公布后，保证模范地遵守宪法，为国家走向社会主义而奋斗。
    会议期间共收到代表们带来的提案二百七十件。提案审查委员会认真地逐一加以审查，将交给政府分别处理。
    会议以无记名投票方式选出出席全国人民代表大会会议的代表十九人，当选的代表是：马明方、潘自力、赵寿山、李凤莲（女）、孟天禄、王德彪、许敬章、蒲忠智、韩夏存、曹冠群（女）、李敷仁、杨芝芳（女）、张凤翽、韩兆鹗、余振瀛、韩望尘、苏资琛、李馥清（女）、安文钦。
