Title: All-China Youth Federation Standing Committee Holds Meeting, Approves List of Chinese Delegation to World Federation of Democratic Youth Beijing Conference
Author:
Date: 1954-08-05
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, August 4th—The Standing Committee of the All-China Federation of Democratic Youth held a meeting on the evening of the 1st and approved the list of the Chinese delegation to the World Federation of Democratic Youth Council Beijing meeting, headed by Hu Yaobang. The delegation consists of Hu Yaobang, Liao Chengzhi, Liu Daosheng, Qu Tangliang (female), Chen Xuzong, Wu Xueqian, Qian Liren, Li Shoupao, and Qian Dawei, members of the World Federation of Democratic Youth Council, and observers Yu Beichen, Qian Weichang, Ding Cong, Hao Yichun (female), Wang Songsheng, Aysham (female), and Zhu Liang, a total of sixteen people, with Hu Yaobang as the head of the delegation.

### 中华全国民主青联常务委员会举行会议  通过我国参加世界青联北京会议代表团名单

1954-08-05
第1版()
专栏：

    中华全国民主青联常务委员会举行会议
    通过我国参加世界青联北京会议代表团名单
    【新华社四日讯】中华全国民主青年联合会常务委员会在一日晚举行会议，通过了以胡耀邦为首的参加世界民主青年联盟理事会北京会议的中国代表团名单。代表团由世界民主青年联盟理事会理事胡耀邦、廖承志、刘导生、区棠亮（女）、陈绪宗、吴学谦、钱李仁、李寿葆、钱大卫和列席代表于北辰、钱伟长、丁聪、郝诒纯（女）、王松声、阿衣夏木（女）、朱良共十六人组成，并以胡耀邦为团长。
