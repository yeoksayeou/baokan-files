Title: After Attending the Cooperative Representatives Conference of Our Country, Representatives from the Soviet Union and Other Countries Returned Home One After Another
Author:
Date: 1954-08-24
Page: 4

    Xinhua News Agency Dispatch: After being invited to attend the First Representative Conference of the All-China Federation of Cooperatives, the cooperative representatives delegations from the Soviet Union, North Korea, Mongolia, Romania, Bulgaria, and the German Democratic Republic have successively left Beijing and returned to their countries.

### 参加我国合作社代表大会后  苏联等国代表先后回国

1954-08-24
第4版()
专栏：

    参加我国合作社代表大会后
    苏联等国代表先后回国
    据新华社讯：应邀参加中华全国合作社第一次代表大会的苏联、朝鲜、蒙古、罗马尼亚、保加利亚、德意志民主共和国等国合作社代表团，已先后离开北京回国。
