Title: Speech by World Federation of Democratic Youth Secretary-General De Nies at a Banquet
Author: 
Date: 1954-08-17
Page: 1

Premier, dear Chinese friends:

I would like to propose a toast on behalf of the entire council of the World Federation of Democratic Youth. We have gathered at this meeting with a common will to fight for peace.

The achievements of the Geneva Conference, this great event, illuminates our work. Please allow me, Premier, to mention the particularly outstanding role you played. For many of us who were in Western European countries during the negotiations, we could measure this great role; your achievements also mean that everyone understands that the ideas of peaceful consultation and peaceful coexistence as stipulated in the Five Principles are an invincible force.

Therefore, please allow me to toast the cause of peaceful friendship between the people of all countries, the role of the peace-loving forces of the People's Republic of China among the five major powers, and to express warmly my respect to the government, people, and youth of the peace-loving people of China!

### 世界青联总书记德尼在宴会上的讲话

1954-08-17
第1版()
专栏：

    世界青联总书记德尼在宴会上的讲话总理先生、亲爱的中国朋友们：
    我愿代表世界民主青年联盟理事会全体理事向你们举杯祝贺。我们是带着为和平而斗争的共同意志团聚在这次会议中。
    日内瓦会议的成就，这一巨大的事件照耀着我们的工作。在这里请允许我，总理先生，提一提你所起的特别突出的作用。对于那些在谈判期间正在西欧国家的我们中间的很多人来说，都可以衡量出这个伟大的作用；你的成就还意味着使每个人都懂得在五项原则中所规定的和平协商及和平共处的思想是不可战胜的力量。
    因此请允许我为各国人民间的和平友谊的事业，为中华人民共和国和平力量在五大国间所起的作用，为热烈表达向热爱和平的人民中国的政府、人民及青年的敬意而干杯！
