Title: US Steel Production Plummets in First Half of Year
Author:
Date: 1954-08-03
Page: 4

    Xinhua News Agency, New York, 31st. News: The production of steel, the foundation of American heavy industry, has declined severely in the first half of this year.
    According to the American Iron and Steel Institute, American steel production in the first half of this year decreased by more than 13,810,000 net tons compared to the first half of 1953.

### 美国上半年钢铁产量严重下降

1954-08-03
第4版()
专栏：

    美国上半年钢铁产量严重下降
    【新华社三十一日讯】纽约消息：今年上半年美国重工业的基础——钢铁工业的生产量严重下降。
    根据美国钢铁研究所宣布：今年上半年美国钢铁的生产量比一九五三年上半年减少了一千三百八十一万多净吨。
