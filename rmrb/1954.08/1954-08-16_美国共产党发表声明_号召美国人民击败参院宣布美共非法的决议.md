Title: U.S. Communist Party Issues Statement Calling on American People to Defeat Senate Resolution Declaring the U.S. Communist Party Illegal
Author:
Date: 1954-08-16
Page: 4
    Xinhua News Agency, New York, August 14th: TASS reports: The U.S. Communist Party issued a statement regarding the U.S. Senate's vote to declare the Communist Party illegal.

    The statement said: "The Senate's vote on August 12 to declare the Communist Party illegal and undermine labor unions is the illegitimate offspring of McCarthyism and a shameless election-year political conspiracy… This bill was hastily proposed based on a patchwork of motions without any discussion or genuine debate. It is irrational and unconstitutional. This bill attempts to implement anti-worker and union-busting measures under the guise of a Hitlerite anti-communist mask."

    The statement said: "Organized labor and the enlightened public should protest to Congress and the President against this important step toward fascism..."

    The statement said: "The Communist Party will unite with organized workers and freedom-loving Americans to oppose this bill in Congress, in the courts, and before the general public. The Communist Party cannot allow this unconstitutional, anti-American, and pro-fascist resolution to eliminate itself and eliminate the Bill of Rights."

### 美国共产党发表声明  号召美国人民击败参院宣布美共非法的决议

1954-08-16
第4版()
专栏：

    美国共产党发表声明
    号召美国人民击败参院宣布美共非法的决议
    【新华社十五日讯】据塔斯社纽约十四日讯：美国共产党就美参议院表决通过宣布共产党非法的问题发表声明。
    声明说：“八月十二日参议院宣布共产党非法和破坏工会的表决，是麦卡锡主义和选举年的无耻政治阴谋的私生子……这个法案是依靠一些七拼八凑的动议草草提出的，根本没有经过讨论或是真正的辩论，它是没有理性的，违反宪法的。这个法案企图在希特勒式的反共面具掩盖之下，实行仇视工人的破坏工会的办法。”
    声明说：“有组织的劳动人民和开明的公众应向国会和总统提出抗议，反对这个走向法西斯主义的重要步骤……”
    声明说：“共产党将与有组织的工人和爱好自由的美国人民团结一起，在国会、法庭和在全体公众面前反对这个法案。共产党不能听任这个违反宪法的、反美的和亲法西斯的决议来消灭自己，消灭权利法案。”
