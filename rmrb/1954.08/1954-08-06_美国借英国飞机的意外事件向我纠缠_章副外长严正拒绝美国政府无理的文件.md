Title: The U.S. is Pesistently Harassing Us Regarding the Accidental Incident of a British Airplane
Author:
Date: 1954-08-06
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, 5th. Vice Foreign Minister Zhang Hanfu received a letter on the 4th from Mr. Du Weilian, the British Chargé d'affaires, enclosing a document from the U.S. government regarding the accidental incident of a British airplane, with the purpose of unreasonable entanglement. As is well known, the accidental incident of the British airplane is being resolved diplomatically by the Chinese and British governments, and has nothing to do with the U.S. government. Vice Foreign Minister Zhang Hanfu replied to Mr. Du Weilian on the same day, solemnly rejecting this document from the U.S. government.

### 美国借英国飞机的意外事件向我纠缠  章副外长严正拒绝美国政府无理的文件

1954-08-06
第1版()
专栏：

    美国借英国飞机的意外事件向我纠缠
    章副外长严正拒绝美国政府无理的文件
    【新华社五日讯】英国代办杜维廉先生在四日致函我外交部章汉夫副部长，随函附来美国政府提出的目的在于进行无理纠缠的关于英国飞机的意外事件的文件一件。如所周知，英国飞机的意外事件正由中英两国政府经由外交途径解决，与美国政府毫无关系。章汉夫副部长已在当日复函杜维廉先生，严正拒绝了美国政府的这个文件。
