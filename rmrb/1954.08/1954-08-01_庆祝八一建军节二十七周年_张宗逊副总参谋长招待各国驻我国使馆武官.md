Title: Celebrating the 27th Anniversary of the August 1st Army Day, Deputy Chief of General Staff Zhang Zongxun Hosts Military Attaches from Various Countries' Embassies in China
Author:
Date: 1954-08-01
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, July 31st: To celebrate the 27th anniversary of the Chinese People's Liberation Army's "August 1st" Army Day, Zhang Zongxun, Deputy Chief of the General Staff of the Central People's Government's People's Revolutionary Military Commission, held a grand reception on the evening of the 31st to host military attaches from various countries' embassies in China.

Invited to attend the reception were: Soviet Military Attaché Lieutenant General Potrusevsky, Indian Military Attaché Brigadier General Koka, Polish Military Attaché Colonel Monath, Indonesian Military Attaché Lieutenant Colonel Shalin, Romanian Military Attaché Lieutenant Colonel Dubrin, Czechoslovak Acting Military Attaché Captain Muha, and many deputy military attachés from various countries.

Present as hosts were: Fu Zhong, Xiao Hua, and Gan Siqi, Deputy Directors of the General Political Department of the Central People's Government's People's Revolutionary Military Commission; Xu Liqing, Deputy Minister of the General Cadre Department; Xiao Xiangrong, Director of the General Office; and Xu Guangda, Chen Shiju, Su Zhenhua, Wu Faxian, Fu Qiutao, Cheng Jun, Qiu Chuangcheng, Wang Bingzhang, Fang Qiang, Zhu Ming, Lü Zhengcao, Huang Zhiyong, Li Tianhuan, Tan Jiasu, Gu Jingsheng and other senior generals, heads of various departments of the People's Revolutionary Military Commission and various arms of the Chinese People's Liberation Army.

Also present as hosts were: Wu Xiuquan, Deputy Minister of Foreign Affairs of the Central People's Government, and Yu Xinqing, Deputy Director of the General Office of the Central People's Government Committee.

Deputy Chief of General Staff Zhang Zongxun delivered a speech at the meeting. He said that since the founding of the People's Republic of China, the Chinese People's Liberation Army has been charged with the duty of safeguarding the victory of our people's revolution, vigilantly defending our country's territorial waters, airspace, and sacred territory. At the same time, it is closely united with the powerful Soviet army and the armies of various people's democratic countries, and closely united with peace-loving people of all countries, undertaking the lofty mission of defending peace in Asia and the world. Then, he solemnly pointed out that our country's territory of Taiwan is still occupied by American imperialists. Therefore, liberating Taiwan and liberating our compatriots living in Taiwan who are oppressed and enslaved is still the glorious task of our Chinese People's Liberation Army.

At the meeting, Soviet and other countries' military attachés gave speeches congratulating China on its Army Day.

The reception proceeded in a warm and friendly atmosphere.

After the meeting, the combined performing arts troupes of the North China Military Region, the Navy, and the Air Force of the Chinese People's Liberation Army performed wonderful programs to add to the merriment.

### 庆祝八一建军节二十七周年  张宗逊副总参谋长招待各国驻我国使馆武官

1954-08-01
第1版()
专栏：

    庆祝八一建军节二十七周年  张宗逊副总参谋长招待各国驻我国使馆武官
   【新华社三十一日讯】为庆祝中国人民解放军“八一”建军节二十七周年，中央人民政府人民革命军事委员会副总参谋长张宗逊在三十一日晚举行盛大的招待会，招待各国驻我国使馆武官。
    应邀出席招待会的有：苏联武官波特鲁塞夫斯基中将，印度武官郭嘉准将，波兰武官莫纳特上校，印度尼西亚武官沙林中校，罗马尼亚武官杜布林中校，捷克斯洛伐克代理武官慕哈大尉及各国副武官多人。
    出席作陪的有：中央人民政府人民革命军事委员会总政治部副主任傅钟、萧华、甘泗淇，总干部部副部长徐立清，办公厅主任萧向荣，以及人民革命军事委员会各部门、中国人民解放军各兵种首长许光达、陈士榘、苏振华、吴法宪、傅秋涛、成钧、邱创成、王秉璋、方强、朱明、吕正操、黄志勇、李天焕、谭家述、谷景生等高级将领。
    出席作陪的还有：中央人民政府外交部副部长伍修权，中央人民政府委员会办公厅副主任余心清等。
    张宗逊副总参谋长在会上致词。他说，自从中华人民共和国建国以来，中国人民解放军就以保卫我国人民革命的胜利果实为职责，时刻警惕地捍卫着我们祖国的领海、领空和神圣的国土，同时它和强大的苏联军队以及各人民民主国家的军队紧密地团结在一起，和各国爱好和平的人民紧密地团结在一起，担负着保卫亚洲与世界和平的崇高使命。接着，他又庄严指出，目前我国领土台湾仍然被美帝国主义者所侵占，因此，解放台湾，解放被压迫、被奴役的居住在台湾的我国同胞，仍然是我们中国人民解放军的光荣任务。
    会上，苏联和各国武官都先后致词祝贺我国建军节。
    招待会在热烈友好的气氛中进行。
    会后，由中国人民解放军华北军区文工团、海军文工团和空军文工团联合演出精采节目助兴。
