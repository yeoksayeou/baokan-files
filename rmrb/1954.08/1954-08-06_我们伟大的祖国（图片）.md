Title: Our Great Motherland (Picture)
Author:
Date: 1954-08-06
Page: 1

    Our Great Motherland
    Guangdong Province has had a bumper harvest of early rice. Many agricultural producers' cooperatives established this spring have fully demonstrated their superiority in the bumper harvest of early rice. This is a scene of members of the Qianfeng Agricultural Producers' Cooperative in Hesheng Township, Zhongshan County, Guangdong Province, which was established this spring, turning over the new grain in the drying yard.

### 我们伟大的祖国（图片）

1954-08-06
第1版()
专栏：

    我们伟大的祖国
    广东省早稻丰收。许多今春成立的农业生产合作社，在早稻丰收中充分显示了它们的优越性。这是今春成立的广东省中山县合胜乡前锋农业生产合作社社员在晒谷场上翻晒新谷。
