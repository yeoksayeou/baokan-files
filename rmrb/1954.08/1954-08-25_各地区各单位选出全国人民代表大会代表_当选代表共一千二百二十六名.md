Title: Delegates to the National People's Congress Elected in Various Regions and Units; A Total of 1,226 Delegates Elected
Author:
Date: 1954-08-25
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, August 24th: All provinces, the Inner Mongolia Autonomous Region, and centrally-administered municipalities across the country have generally held their first sessions of the first People's Congress and elected delegates to the National People's Congress. The People's Armed Forces and overseas Chinese have also elected delegates to the National People's Congress.

    The election of delegates to the National People's Congress in provinces and municipalities was conducted in accordance with the provisions of the Electoral Law of the National People's Congress and Local People's Congresses of the People's Republic of China. Each province elected one delegate for every 800,000 people; each centrally-administered municipality and industrial city under provincial jurisdiction with a population of over 500,000 elected one delegate for every 100,000 people. As a result of elections in the twenty-five provinces, the Inner Mongolia Autonomous Region, and the fourteen centrally-administered municipalities, a total of 1,124 delegates to the National People's Congress were elected.

    The People's Armed Forces held soldiers' representative conferences and elected 60 delegates to the National People's Congress. At the expanded meeting on overseas Chinese affairs convened by the Overseas Chinese Affairs Commission of the Central People's Government, with the participation of representatives of overseas Chinese, 30 delegates to the National People's Congress who should be elected by overseas Chinese were elected. The 150 delegates to the National People's Congress who should be elected by national minorities have also been elected in various localities.

    In total, 1,226 delegates to the National People's Congress have been produced from various regions, units, and sectors. These delegates include outstanding and necessary figures from all democratic classes and all nationalities. Among the delegates are the great leader of the Chinese people, Mao Zedong, and Mao Zedong's close comrades-in-arms Liu Shaoqi, Zhou Enlai, Zhu De, Chen Yun, and others, the outstanding peace fighter Soong Ching Ling, representatives of various democratic parties, non-party democratic人士（ren shi - people with social standing）, and people's organizations, many famous industrial and agricultural labor models, and workers in culture, education, science, and technology, as well as government workers.

    In addition to conducting elections, the People's Congress meetings held in various localities also universally discussed the draft Constitution of the People's Republic of China, reviewed the work of the people's governments at their respective levels, and, based on the country's general task and current situation, discussed and decided on the local work tasks for the future.

    Through the convening of People's Congress meetings in various localities, the people's democratic system has become more complete, and preparations have been directly made for the convocation of the First National People's Congress.

### 各地区各单位选出全国人民代表大会代表  当选代表共一千二百二十六名

1954-08-25
第1版()
专栏：

    各地区各单位选出全国人民代表大会代表
    当选代表共一千二百二十六名
    【新华社二十四日讯】全国各省、内蒙古自治区和各中央直辖市，普遍举行了第一届人民代表大会第一次会议，选出了全国人民代表大会代表。人民武装部队和国外华侨，也都选出了全国人民代表大会代表。
    各省、直辖市全国人民代表大会代表的选举，是按照中华人民共和国全国人民代表大会及地方各级人民代表大会选举法的规定进行的，各省按人口每八十万人选代表一人；各中央直辖市及人口在五十万以上的省辖工业市按人口每十万人选代表一人。全国二十五个省，内蒙古自治区和十四个中央直辖市选举的结果，共选出全国人民代表大会代表一千一百二十四名。
    人民武装部队都举行了军人代表大会，选出了六十名全国人民代表大会代表。在中央人民政府华侨事务委员会召开的有国外华侨代表参加的侨务扩大会议上，选出了国外华侨应选的全国人民代表大会代表三十名。全国少数民族应选的全国人民代表大会代表一百五十名，也已分别在各地一并选出。
    总计各地区、各单位和各方面所产生的全国人民代表大会代表共有一千二百二十六名。这些代表包括了各民主阶级和各民族的优秀的和必要的人物。代表中有中国人民伟大的领袖毛泽东，有毛泽东同志亲密的战友刘少奇、周恩来、朱德、陈云等，有杰出的和平战士宋庆龄，有各民主党派、无党派民主人士和各人民团体的代表人物，有很多著名的工业、农业劳动模范和文化、教育、科学技术工作者，还有政府工作者。
    各地召开的人民代表大会会议除了进行选举以外，还普遍讨论了中华人民共和国宪法草案，审查了本级人民政府的工作，并根据国家总任务和国家当前的形势，讨论和决定了当地今后的工作任务。
    通过各地人民代表大会会议的召开，人民民主制度已更加完备，并直接为召开第一届全国人民代表大会作好了准备。
