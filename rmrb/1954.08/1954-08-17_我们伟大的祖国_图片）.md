    Our Great Motherland
    The vast West Sichuan Plain is not only a rich agricultural area, but also contains a large number of underground mineral deposits. Now, many new oil drilling rigs have been erected everywhere. These two Sichuan villagers are talking about the wonderful life these drilling rigs will bring them.

### 我们伟大的祖国(图片）

1954-08-17
第1版()
专栏：

    我们伟大的祖国
    辽阔的川西平原不仅是一片富饶的农业区，而且还蕴藏着大量的地下矿产，现在到处都矗立起许多新的石油钻井。这两位四川老乡正在谈论着这些钻井将给他们带来怎样的美好生活。
