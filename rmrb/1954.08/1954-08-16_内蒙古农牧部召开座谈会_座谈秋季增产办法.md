Title: Inner Mongolia Department of Agriculture and Animal Husbandry Holds Symposium to Discuss Autumn Increase Production Methods
Author:
Date: 1954-08-16
Page: 2

Xinhua News Agency
    Inner Mongolia Department of Agriculture and Animal Husbandry Holds Symposium
    Symposium to Discuss Autumn Increase Production Methods

    In order to ensure the overfulfillment of this year's agricultural production increase plan, the Rural and Pastoral Work Department of the Inner Mongolia Branch Bureau of the CPC Central Committee specially invited more than 20 well-known agricultural labor models, directors of agricultural production cooperatives, heads of mutual aid groups, and individual farmers to a symposium on August 5th to discuss methods for increasing production in the autumn. At the meeting, everyone spoke enthusiastically, specifically introducing advanced experiences and methods such as how to prepare for the autumn harvest, continue flood prevention and pest control, grow more autumn vegetables to increase income, and carry out production increase and frugality competitions. They also expressed that after returning, they will take the lead in increasing production, help farmers of all ethnic groups jointly complete the production increase plan, and conscientiously implement the resolutions of the Inner Mongolia Autonomous Region People's Congress.

### 内蒙古农牧部召开座谈会  座谈秋季增产办法

1954-08-16
第2版()
专栏：

    内蒙古农牧部召开座谈会
    座谈秋季增产办法
    为了保证超额完成今年的农业增产计划，中共中央内蒙古分局农村牧区工作部，特地在八月五日邀请了出席内蒙古自治区第一届第一次人民代表大会会议的著名农业劳动模范、农业生产合作社主任、互助组长及单干农民等二十多人，座谈秋季增产办法。会上，大家纷纷发言，具体地介绍了怎样作好秋收准备工作、继续防洪灭虫、多种秋菜增加收入和开展增产节约竞赛等先进经验和办法，他们并表示回去以后将带头增产，帮助各族农民共同完成增产计划，认真贯彻自治区人民代表大会这次会议的决议。
                                  （新华社）
