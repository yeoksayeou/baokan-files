Title: Plenary Session of the Central Committee of the Romanian Workers' Party Held

Author: 
Date: 1954-08-06
Page: 4

(Xinhua News Agency, Bucharest, August 4th) TASS, Bucharest: The Central Committee of the Romanian Workers' Party held a plenary session on August 2nd. The meeting approved the following agenda for the Second Congress of the Romanian Workers' Party, to be held on October 30th:
    I. Summary report of the Central Committee of the Romanian Workers' Party;
    II. Report of the Central Inspection Commission of the Romanian Workers' Party;
    III. Instructions on the development of agriculture in the past two or three years;
    IV. Report on the revision of the Constitution of the Romanian Workers' Party;
    V. Election of the Central Committee and the Central Inspection Commission of the Romanian Workers' Party.
    The plenary session also adopted the draft revision of the Constitution of the Romanian Workers' Party and the draft instructions on the development of agriculture in the past two or three years for the Second Party Congress.

### 罗马尼亚工人党中央举行全体会议

1954-08-06
第4版()
专栏：

    罗马尼亚工人党中央举行全体会议
    【新华社四日讯】塔斯社布加勒斯特讯：罗马尼亚工人党中央委员会在八月二日举行了全体会议。会议通过了将于十月三十日召开的罗马尼亚工人党第二次代表大会的议程如下：
    一、罗马尼亚工人党中央委员会的总结报告；
    二、罗马尼亚工人党中央检查委员会的报告；
      三、关于在最近两三年内发展农业的指示；
    四、关于修改罗马尼亚工人党党章的报告；五、选举罗马尼亚工人党中央委员会和中央检查委员会。
    全体会议并通过了罗马尼亚工人党党章修改草案和第二次党代表大会关于在最近两三年内发展农业的指示草案。
