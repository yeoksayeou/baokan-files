Title: British Labour Party Delegation Sets Off to Visit Our Country
Author:
Date: 1954-08-11
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, August 10th, London: A British Labour Party delegation of eight people, led by Labour Party leader Clement Attlee, departed London by plane on August 9th for a visit to China at the invitation of the Chinese People's Institute of Foreign Affairs. They will travel via Helsinki and Moscow to China. It is reported that the delegation will stay in Moscow for two days and is expected to arrive in Beijing around August 15th.

### 英国工党代表团启程来我国访问

1954-08-11
第1版()
专栏：

    英国工党代表团启程来我国访问
    【新华社十日讯】伦敦消息：应中国人民外交学会的邀请来中国访问的英国工党代表团一行八人，在工党领袖克莱门特·艾德礼先生率领下，已于八月九日乘飞机离开伦敦，取道赫尔辛基和莫斯科前来中国，据悉，代表团将在莫斯科停留两天，可望在八月十五日左右到达北京。
