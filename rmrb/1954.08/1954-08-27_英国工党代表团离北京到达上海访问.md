Title: British Labour Party Delegation Leaves Beijing, Arrives in Shanghai for Visit
Author:
Date: 1954-08-27
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, August 26th: The British Labour Party delegation headed by Clement Attlee left Beijing by plane at 9:00 AM on August 26th, accompanied by Qian Duansheng, Vice President of the Chinese People's Institute of Foreign Affairs, to visit Shanghai and other places.

The delegation's translators, Mr. and Mrs. Michael Lindsay, also left Beijing on the same plane.

Among those who saw them off at the airport were Zhang Xiruo, President of the Chinese People's Institute of Foreign Affairs, and Vice Presidents Zhou Gengsheng, Chen Hansheng, Hu Yuzhi, and Qiao Guanhua, and Secretary-General Wu Maosun.

Also at the airport to see them off were Guo Moruo and Chen Shutong, Vice Chairmen of the National Committee of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference, Xing Xiping, Deputy Secretary-General, and representatives of the Chinese Communist Party, various democratic parties, and people's organizations, including Liao Chengzhi, Luo Longji, Chen Shaoxian, Zhang Naiqi, Wang Shao'ao, Huang Qixiang, Chen Qiyou, Xu Deheng, Li Chunqing, Qu Tangliang, Liu Ningyi, Li Dequan, Chu Tunan, Fu Lianzhang, Yu Beichen, Ji Chaoding, Cao Yu, and Zhao Fusan, as well as Zhang Hanfu, Vice Minister of Foreign Affairs of the Central People's Government, Huan Xiang, Director of the Department of European and African Affairs, Wang Zhuoru, Director of the Protocol Department, Lei Renmin, Vice Minister of Foreign Trade, Ding Xilin, Vice Minister of Culture, and Cao Zhongshu, Head of the Chinese Trade Delegation to Britain, and Peng Zhen, Mayor of the Beijing Municipal People's Government. D.W. Willetts, the British Chargé d'Affaires in China, was also at the airport to see them off.

Xinhua News Agency, Shanghai, August 26th: The British Labour Party delegation, consisting of Clement Attlee, Aneurin Bevan, E.Summerskill, W. Burke, Morgan Phillips, Stephen Watson, Harry Earnshaw, Henry Franklin, and their translators, Mr. and Mrs. Michael Lindsay, who were invited to visit China by the Chinese People's Institute of Foreign Affairs, arrived in Shanghai by special plane from Beijing at approximately 2:00 PM on the 26th, accompanied by Qian Duansheng, Vice President of the Chinese People's Institute of Foreign Affairs.

Among those who welcomed them at the airport were Jin Zhonghua and Xia Yan, directors of the Chinese People's Institute of Foreign Affairs in Shanghai.

Pan Hannian, Deputy Mayor of the Shanghai Municipal People's Government and First Deputy Secretary of the Shanghai Municipal Committee of the Chinese Communist Party, as well as Xu Pingyu, Secretary-General of the Shanghai Municipal People's Government, and Yu Peiwen, Acting Director of the Foreign Affairs Office, were also at the airport to welcome them.

Also welcoming them at the airport were leaders of the Shanghai Municipal Consultative Committee, local organizations of various democratic parties in Shanghai, and people's organizations in Shanghai, as well as people from Shanghai's science, education, culture, arts, and news circles.

After disembarking, members of the delegation received flowers from children and shook hands with the welcomers.

### 英国工党代表团离北京到达上海访问

1954-08-27
第1版()
专栏：

    英国工党代表团离北京到达上海访问
   【新华社二十六日讯】以克·艾德礼为首的英国工党代表团在八月二十六日上午九时乘机离京，由中国人民外交学会副会长钱端升陪同，前往上海等地访问。
    代表团翻译林迈可夫妇也同机离京。
    到飞机场欢送的有中国人民外交学会会长张奚若，副会长周鲠生、陈翰笙、胡愈之、乔冠华，秘书长吴茂荪。
    到机场欢送的还有中国人民政治协商会议全国委员会副主席郭沫若、陈叔通，副秘书长邢西萍，中国共产党和各民主党派、人民团体的代表廖承志、罗隆基、陈劭先、章乃器、王绍鏊、黄琪翔、陈其尤、许德珩、李纯青、区棠亮、刘宁一、李德全、楚图南、傅连暲、于北辰、冀朝鼎、曹禺、赵复三，中央人民政府外交部副部长章汉夫、欧非司司长宦乡、交际处处长王倬如，对外贸易部副部长雷任民，文化部副部长丁西林，中国赴英贸易访问团团长曹中枢等。北京市人民政府市长彭真也到机场欢送。到机场欢送的还有英国驻华代办杜维廉。【新华社上海二十六日电】应中国人民外交学会邀请来我国访问的英国工党代表团克·艾德礼、安·比万、埃·萨末斯基尔、韦·伯克、摩·菲利普斯、塞·华生、哈·厄恩萧、亨·弗兰克林等八人以及代表团翻译林迈可夫妇，由中国人民外交学会副会长钱端升陪同，在二十六日下午二时许由北京乘专机到达上海。
    到机场欢迎的有：中国人民外交学会在上海的理事金仲华、夏衍等。
    上海市人民政府副市长、中国共产党上海市委员会第一副书记潘汉年以及上海市人民政府秘书长徐平羽、外事处代处长俞沛文等也到机场欢迎。
    到机场欢迎的还有：上海市协商委员会和各民主党派上海市地方组织、上海市各人民团体的负责人，以及上海科学、教育、文化艺术和新闻界人士等。
    代表团人员下机后，接受了少年儿童的献花，并与欢迎者握手。
