Title: British Chargé d'Affaires in China Hosts British Labour Party Delegation; Premier Zhou Enlai, Vice Premier Guo Moruo, and Others Attend the Banquet
Author:
Date: 1954-08-25
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, August 24th: The British Chargé d'Affaires in China, Mr. Humphrey Trevelyan, held a banquet on the evening of the 24th to host the British Labour Party delegation headed by Mr. Clement Attlee, which is currently visiting China.

Chinese attendees at the banquet included: Premier of the State Council of the Central People's Government Zhou Enlai, Vice Premier Guo Moruo, Secretary-General Xi Zhongxun, Minister of Culture Shen Yanbing, Vice Minister of Foreign Affairs Zhang Hanfu, Vice Minister of Foreign Trade Lei Renmin, Vice Minister of Culture Ding Xilin, and Mayor of Beijing Peng Zhen, among others.

Also attending from the Chinese side were: President of the Chinese People's Institute of Foreign Affairs Zhang Xiruo, Vice President Qian Duansheng, Qiao Guanhua, and Secretary-General of the China Council for the Promotion of International Trade Ji Chao Ding.

Members of the British Labour Party delegation attending the banquet included: Clement Attlee, Aneurin Bevan, E. Sumerskill, W. Burke, Morgan Phillips, Stephen Watson, Harry Earnshaw, Henry Franklin, and the delegation's translators, Mr. and Mrs. Michael Lindsay.

Also in attendance were: Burmese Ambassador to China U Hla Maung, Pakistani Ambassador to China Raza, and Indian Ambassador to China Raghavan.

The banquet proceeded in a friendly atmosphere.

### 英驻我国代办招待英工党代表团  周恩来总理郭沫若副总理等出席宴会

1954-08-25
第1版()
专栏：

    英驻我国代办招待英工党代表团
    周恩来总理郭沫若副总理等出席宴会
    【新华社二十四日讯】二十四日晚，英国驻我国代办杜维廉举行宴会，招待正在我国访问的以克·艾德礼先生为首的英国工党代表团。
    中国方面出席宴会的有：中央人民政府政务院总理周恩来、副总理郭沫若、秘书长习仲勋，文化部部长沈雁冰，外交部副部长章汉夫，对外贸易部副部长雷任民，文化部副部长丁西林，北京市人民政府市长彭真等。
    中国方面出席宴会的还有：中国人民外交学会会长张奚若、副会长钱端升、乔冠华，中国国际贸易促进委员会秘书长冀朝鼎。
    英国工党代表团出席宴会的有：克·艾德礼、安·比万、埃·萨末斯基尔、韦·伯克、摩·菲利普斯、塞·华生、哈·厄恩萧、亨·弗兰克林和代表团翻译林迈可夫妇。
    出席宴会的还有：缅甸驻华大使吴拉茂、巴基斯坦驻华大使罗查、印度驻华大使赖嘉文。
    宴会在友好的空气中进行。
