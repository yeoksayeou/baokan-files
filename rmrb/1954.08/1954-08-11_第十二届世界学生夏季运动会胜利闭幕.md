Title: The 12th World Student Summer Games Conclude Victoriously
Author: 
Date: 1954-08-11
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Budapest, August 10th: The 12th World Student Summer Games concluded victoriously on August 8th. Two thousand one hundred and eight student athletes from thirty-four countries with different social systems participated in the Games, which played a significant role in promoting student sports and friendship and solidarity among students from various countries.

During the Games, four world records (men's 100-meter butterfly, men's 400-meter medley relay, women's 400-meter medley relay, and featherweight weightlifting) were set, twenty-one World Student Summer Games records were broken, and twenty-five national records of the athletes' respective countries (calculated based on the top six scores in each event) were established.

Chinese athletes ranked second overall in table tennis, six athletes broke five national swimming records, and six track and field events set new Chinese records. The Chinese men's and women's basketball teams both finished fifth, the men's volleyball team finished seventh, and the women's volleyball team finished sixth. Chinese athletes displayed courageous and tenacious spirit and good sportsmanship in all competitions, earning unanimous praise.

In the final day of competition, Chinese long-distance runners Yi Taotege and Shi Qin finished fifth and sixth in the men's 5,000 meters with times of 15 minutes 32.8 seconds and 16 minutes 25.4 seconds, respectively. Yi Taotege's time set a new national record for our country.

### 第十二届世界学生夏季运动会胜利闭幕

1954-08-11
第4版()
专栏：

    第十二届世界学生夏季运动会胜利闭幕
    【新华社布拉格十日电】布达佩斯消息：第十二届世界学生夏季运动会已在八月八日胜利结束。参加运动会的有不同社会制度的三十四个国家的二千一百零八名学生运动员，运动会对于促进各国学生体育运动和各国学生间的友谊和团结有着很大的作用。
    运动会的各项比赛中有四项（男子一百公尺蝶式游泳、男子四百公尺混合姿式接力游泳、女子四百公尺混合姿式接力游泳和毛量级举重）创造了世界纪录，二十一项创造了世界学生夏季运动会的纪录，二十五项（以各项得分前六名计算）创造了运动员本国的全国纪录。
    中国运动员在乒乓球比赛中得总分第二名，有六个运动员突破了五个项目的游泳全国最高纪录，六项田径项目创造了中国的新纪录。中国男子和女子篮球队都得第五名，男子排球队得第七名，女子排球队得第六名。中国运动员在各项比赛中表现了勇敢顽强的精神和良好的体育道德作风，获得了一致的好评。
    在最后一天比赛中，中国长跑选手伊套特格和石勤分别以十五分三十二秒八和十六分二十五秒四的成绩获得男子五千公尺的第五和第六名，伊套特格的成绩创我国新纪录。
