Title: Indonesian Art Troupe Holds Performance Gala; Vice Chairman Liu Shaoqi and Others Receive the Art Troupe and Attend the Gala

Author: 
Date: 1954-08-27
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, August 26th: The Indonesian Art Troupe, invited to visit and perform in our country, held a performance gala at the Huairen Hall in Beijing at 7:30 pm on August 26th.

Those attending the gala included: Vice Chairmen of the Central People's Government Liu Shaoqi and Li Jishen, Premier of the State Council Zhou Enlai, Vice Premiers Guo Moruo, Huang Yanpei, and Deng Xiaoping, President of the Supreme People's Court Shen Junru, Vice Chairman of the People's Revolutionary Military Commission He Long, Secretary-General of the State Council Xi Zhongxun, Deputy Director of the Cultural and Educational Committee Lu Dingyi, Minister of Culture Shen Yanbing, Vice Ministers Zhou Yang, Ding Xilin, and Zheng Zhenduo, Vice Ministers of Foreign Affairs Zhang Hanfu and Wu Xiuquan, Mayor of Beijing Peng Zhen, as well as members of the Central People's Government Committee in Beijing, members of the State Council and heads of various commissions, ministries, committees, academies, administrations, and banks, and heads of various departments of the People's Revolutionary Military Commission.

Also attending the gala were: Vice Chairman of the National Committee of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference Chen Shutong and standing committee members and members in Beijing, Vice Chairman of the Chinese People's Committee for World Peace Liao Chengzhi, President of the Chinese People's Association for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries Chu Tunan, heads of various democratic parties and people's organizations, and well-known figures from the capital's cultural and artistic circles, totaling more than 1,200 people.

Indonesian Ambassador to China Mononutu and embassy staff also attended the gala.

Before the gala performance, Vice Chairmen of the Central People's Government Liu Shaoqi and Li Jishen, Premier of the State Council Zhou Enlai, and Vice Premiers Guo Moruo, Huang Yanpei, and Deng Xiaoping received the head of the Indonesian Art Troupe, Mengoadas Natsushin, the host of the performance, Bakhtiar Effendi, and troupe members Siti Sonati, Mrs. Andi Sapada, and Suharto. Indonesian Ambassador to China Mononutu accompanied them during the meeting. Also present at the meeting were Mayor of Beijing Peng Zhen, Deputy Director of the Cultural and Educational Committee of the State Council Lu Dingyi, Minister of Culture of the Central People's Government Shen Yanbing, and Vice Minister of Foreign Affairs Zhang Hanfu, among others.

At the performance gala, the Indonesian Art Troupe performed many national artistic programs, which were warmly welcomed by all attendees. (See photo attached)

Top photo: Vice Chairman of our Central People's Government Liu Shaoqi and others received the Indonesian Art Troupe on the evening of August 26th. The photo shows Vice Chairman Liu Shaoqi (right) talking with the head of the Indonesian Art Troupe, Mengoadas Natsushin (left).
                        Xinhua News Agency reporter Qi Guanshan.

### 印度尼西亚艺术团举行演出晚会  刘少奇副主席等接见艺术团并出席晚会

1954-08-27
第1版()
专栏：

    印度尼西亚艺术团举行演出晚会
    刘少奇副主席等接见艺术团并出席晚会
    【新华社二十六日讯】应邀来我国访问演出的印度尼西亚艺术团二十六日下午七时半在北京怀仁堂举行演出晚会。
    出席晚会的，有：我中央人民政府副主席刘少奇、李济深，政务院总理周恩来，副总理郭沫若、黄炎培、邓小平，最高人民法院院长沈钧儒，人民革命军事委员会副主席贺龙，政务院秘书长习仲勋，文化教育委员会副主任陆定一，文化部部长沈雁冰，副部长周扬、丁西林、郑振铎，外交部副部长章汉夫、伍修权，北京市人民政府市长彭真，以及中央人民政府委员会在京委员，政务院政务委员和各委、部、会、院、署、行首长，人民革命军事委员会各部门负责人。
    出席晚会的，还有：中国人民政治协商会议全国委员会副主席陈叔通和在京常务委员、委员，中国人民保卫世界和平委员会副主席廖承志，中国人民对外文化协会会长楚图南，各民主党派、各人民团体负责人以及首都文化艺术界著名人士等共一千二百多人。
    印度尼西亚驻华大使莫诺努图和使馆人员也出席了晚会。
    在晚会演出以前，我中央人民政府副主席刘少奇、李济深，政务院总理周恩来，副总理郭沫若、黄炎培、邓小平接见了印度尼西亚艺术团团长孟阿达斯·纳苏申，演出主持人巴克特尔·埃芬迪，团员西蒂·索纳蒂、安迪·萨帕达夫人、苏哈托。陪同进见的，有印度尼西亚驻我国大使莫诺努图。接见时在座的，有北京市人民政府市长彭真，政务院文化教育委员会副主任陆定一，中央人民政府文化部部长沈雁冰，外交部副部长章汉夫等。
    在演出晚会上，印度尼西亚艺术团演出了许多富有民族色彩的文艺节目，受到了到会的热烈欢迎。（附图片）
