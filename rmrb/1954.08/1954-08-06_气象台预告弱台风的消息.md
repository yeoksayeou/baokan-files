Title: Weather Bureau Forecasts Weak Typhoon
Author:
Date: 1954-08-06
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, 5th. The Central Weather Bureau issued a weak typhoon warning at 5:00 PM on the 5th: The tropical depression east of Hainan Island has developed into a weak typhoon. At 2:00 PM today, the center of the weak typhoon was located approximately 100 kilometers south of the Pearl River Estuary (21.2 degrees north latitude, 113.5 degrees east longitude), with the maximum wind speed near the center estimated to be 14-22 meters per second (force 7-9). Currently, the center of the weak typhoon is moving northward at a speed of approximately 12 kilometers per hour. It is expected to make landfall on the coast between Taishan and Yangjiang in Guangdong Province around 10:00 PM tonight (the 5th); after landfall, its intensity will weaken. Tonight, the coastal areas of Guangdong and the northeastern part of the South China Sea will experience strong winds and heavy rain, and the sea will have large waves.

### 气象台预告弱台风的消息

1954-08-06
第1版()
专栏：

    气象台预告弱台风的消息
    【新华社五日讯】中央气象台五日下午五时发布弱台风警报：海南岛以东的热带低压已发展为弱台风。今日下午二时，弱台风中心位置在珠江口以南约一百公里的海面上（北纬二十一点二度、东经一一三点五度处），中心附近最大风速估计为每秒十四—二十二公尺（七—九级）。目前弱台风中心正以每小时约十二公里的速度向北方移动。预计今晚（五日）二十二时前后将在广东省台山与阳江之间海岸登陆；登陆后，强度将趋减弱。今夜广东沿海及南海的东北部有大风和大雨，海面并有大浪。
