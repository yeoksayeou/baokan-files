Title: Romanian Ambassador to China, Guo Zuowen, Holds Reception Celebrating the 10th Anniversary of Romania's Liberation; Vice-Chairmen Liu Shaoqi, Li Jishen, and Premier Zhou Enlai Attend
Author:
Date: 1954-08-24
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, August 23rd: Romanian Ambassador to China, Guo Zuowen, held a grand reception on the evening of the 23rd to celebrate the 10th anniversary of the liberation of Romania.

Invited to the reception were: Vice-Chairmen of the Central People's Government of China, Liu Shaoqi and Li Jishen; Premier of the State Council, Zhou Enlai; Vice-Premier Deng Xiaoping; President of the Supreme People's Court, Shen Junru; Vice-Presidents Zhang Zhiran and Zhang Su; Deputy Chief of the General Staff of the People's Revolutionary Military Commission, Huang Kecheng; Deputy Directors of the General Political Department, Fu Zhong and Gan Siqi; Members of the Central People's Government Committee, Ma Yinchu, Shen Yanbing, and Cai Tingkai; Political Committee Members of the State Council, Xie Juezai, Luo Ruiqing, Zeng Shan, Chen Shaoxian, and Huang Shaohong; Deputy Director of the Cultural and Educational Committee of the State Council, Lu Dingyi; Vice-Ministers of Foreign Affairs, Zhang Hanfu and Wu Xiuquan; Vice-Mayor of Beijing, Wu Han; as well as heads of various commissions, ministries, departments, institutes, administrations, and banks affiliated with the Central People's Government State Council; senior generals of the Chinese People's Liberation Army; heads of various people's organizations; and representatives from the capital's science, education, literature, art, and journalism circles.

The foreign envoys to China invited to the reception were: Burmese Ambassador U La Maung, Pakistani Ambassador Raza, Indian Ambassador R.K. Nehru, Swedish Ambassador Wistrand, Indonesian Ambassador Mononutu, Finnish Minister Sundström, Danish Minister গ্রেgerse, British Chargé d'affaires Du Weilian, Soviet Chargé d'affaires Vasikov, Vietnamese Democratic Republic Chargé d'affaires Zhou Liang, Czechoslovak Chargé d'affaires Šmíd, Hungarian Chargé d'affaires Czator, Bulgarian Chargé d'affaires Grigorov, Swiss Chargé d'affaires Cuelli, Mongolian Chargé d'affaires Lobsangchultem, Polish Chargé d'affaires Gola, Democratic People's Republic of Korea Chargé d'affaires Xu Zhe, Dutch Negotiation Representative Smand, Norwegian Negotiation Representative Timmondeng.

Romanian agricultural experts currently visiting China, Maier Ion and Ilca K. Florin, Romanian microbiology expert Stefan Nicolau, Romanian meritorious artist Ian Voicu, famous director Secfild Volfeghel, and the Romanian folk music team headed by Kerzan Constantin, also attended the reception.

A total of more than 700 people attended the reception.

At the reception, Ambassador Guo Zuowen and Vice-Chairman Liu Shaoqi delivered speeches successively (full text to be published separately), and the audience responded with enthusiastic applause.

The reception proceeded in a warm and friendly atmosphere.

After the banquet, the Romanian folk music team performed brilliant programs, and a dance was held.

### 罗马尼亚驻我国大使郭佐文举行招待会  庆祝罗马尼亚解放十周年  刘少奇、李济深副主席和周恩来总理出席

1954-08-24
第1版()
专栏：

    罗马尼亚驻我国大使郭佐文举行招待会
    庆祝罗马尼亚解放十周年
    刘少奇、李济深副主席和周恩来总理出席
    【新华社二十三日讯】　罗马尼亚人民共和国驻我国大使郭佐文于二十三日晚举行盛大招待会，庆祝罗马尼亚解放十周年。
    应邀出席招待会的，有：我中央人民政府副主席刘少奇、李济深，政务院总理周恩来、副总理邓小平，最高人民法院院长沈钧儒、副院长张志让、张苏，人民革命军事委员会副总参谋长黄克诚、总政治部副主任傅钟、甘泗淇，中央人民政府委员会委员马寅初、沈雁冰、蔡廷锴，政务院政务委员谢觉哉、罗瑞卿、曾山、陈劭先、黄绍竑，政务院文化教育委员会副主任陆定一，外交部副部长章汉夫、伍修权，北京市人民政府副市长吴晗，以及中央人民政府政务院所属各委、部、会、院、署、行的负责人，中国人民解放军高级将领，各人民团体的负责人，首都科学、教育、文学、艺术、新闻界人士等。
    应邀出席招待会的各国驻我国使节有：缅甸大使吴拉茂、巴基斯坦大使罗查、印度大使赖嘉文、瑞典大使魏斯特朗、印度尼西亚大使莫诺努图、芬兰公使孙士敦、丹麦公使格瑞杰生、英国代办杜维廉、苏联临时代办华司考、越南民主共和国临时代办周亮、捷克斯洛伐克临时代办石密茨、匈牙利临时代办察托尔道伊、保加利亚临时代办格利郭洛夫、瑞士临时代办崔礼、蒙古临时代办罗布桑楚勒图姆、波兰临时代办古拉、朝鲜民主主义人民共和国临时代办徐哲、荷兰谈判代表司曼、挪威谈判代表季茂登。
    现正在我国访问的罗马尼亚农业专家马叶尔·伊昂、伊尔卡·克·弗洛林，罗马尼亚微生物学专家斯捷凡·尼古劳，罗马尼亚功勋艺术家扬·沃伊库、名导演色克夫利德·沃尔费捷尔和以克尔然·康斯坦丁为首的罗马尼亚民间音乐队等，也出席了招待会。
    出席招待会的共有七百多人。
    招待会上，郭佐文大使和刘少奇副主席先后致词（全文另发），全场不断报以热烈的掌声。
    招待会在热烈和友好的空气中进行。
    宴会后，罗马尼亚民间音乐队表演了精采节目，并举行了舞会。
