Title: Swiss Legation in China Holds Reception to Celebrate Swiss National Day
Author:
Date: 1954-08-02
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, August 1st. The Chargé d'Affaires ad interim of the Swiss Legation in China, Mr. Cui Li, held a reception in Beijing on the 1st to celebrate Swiss National Day.

Those invited to attend included: Dong Biwu, Vice Premier of the State Council of the Central People's Government; Shen Yanbing, Minister of Culture; Zhang Hanfu, Vice Minister of Foreign Affairs; Li Zheren, Vice Minister of Foreign Trade; Ding Xilin, Vice Minister of Culture; Xiao Xiangrong, Director of the General Office of the People's Revolutionary Military Commission; Wu Han, Deputy Mayor of the Beijing Municipal People's Government, and others.

Diplomatic envoys and embassy personnel from various countries stationed in China were also invited to attend.

The reception was held in a friendly atmosphere.

In other news: Zhou Enlai, Premier of the State Council and Minister of Foreign Affairs of the Central People's Government, sent a telegram to Max Petitpierre, Federal Councillor and Head of the Political Department of the Swiss Confederation, to celebrate Swiss National Day.

### 瑞士驻华公使馆举行招待会庆祝瑞士国庆

1954-08-02
第1版()
专栏：

    瑞士驻华公使馆举行招待会庆祝瑞士国庆
    【新华社一日讯】瑞士驻华公使馆临时代办崔礼一日在北京举行招待会，庆祝瑞士国庆日。
    应邀赴会的有：中央人民政府政务院副总理董必武，文化部部长沈雁冰，外交部副部长章汉夫，对外贸易部副部长李哲人，文化部副部长丁西林，人民革命军事委员会办公厅主任萧向荣，北京市人民政府副市长吴晗等。
    各国驻我国外交使节和使馆人员也应邀赴会。
    招待会在友好的气氛中进行。
    又讯：中央人民政府政务院总理兼外交部部长周恩来曾致电瑞士联邦委员兼政治部部长马克斯·彼蒂彼爱，庆祝瑞士国庆节。
