Title: British Labour Party Delegation Continues Visits and Tours in Beijing
Author: 
Date: 1954-08-20
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, August 19th: Members of the British Labour Party delegation continued their visits, tours, and excursions in Beijing on the 18th and 19th.

On the morning of the 19th, delegation members met separately with Jia Tuofu, Vice Chairman of the State Planning Commission of the Central People's Government; Xi Zhongxun, Deputy Director of the Government Administration Council's Cultural and Educational Committee; He Chengxiang, Director of the Religious Affairs Bureau; Zhang Naiqi, Minister of Food; and Chen Xiyun and Chen Guodong, Vice Ministers, to discuss issues related to China's economic construction, religion, and the supply of grain and other food.

The previous day, delegation members also visited the All-China Federation of Trade Unions and the All-China Democratic Women's Federation. Lai Ruoyu, Chairman of the All-China Federation of Trade Unions, and Vice Chairmen Liu Ningyi and Zhu Xuefan, as well as Xu Guangping and Zhang Yun, Vice Chairwomen of the All-China Democratic Women's Federation, received the delegation members and introduced them to the situation of China's labor movement and women's movement, answering questions raised by the guests.

Over these two days, delegation members also visited Peking University, the state-owned Beijing No. 1 Cotton Textile Factory, the Fengtai Railway Station marshalling yard, the Beijing People's Court Prison, and a nursery. In these places, delegation members had extensive contact with Chinese university professors, young students, workers, government staff, childcare workers, and children.

On the 18th and 19th, delegation members also visited the Summer Palace and the Dong'an Market.

The entire delegation will travel to Northeast China on August 20th for a tour.

### 英国工党代表团人员继续在京访问和参观

1954-08-20
第1版()
专栏：

    英国工党代表团人员继续在京访问和参观
    【新华社十九日讯】英国工党代表团人员十八、十九两日继续在北京分别访问、参观和游览。
    代表团人员在十九日上午分别会见了中央人民政府国家计划委员会副主席贾拓夫，政务院文化教育委员会副主任习仲勋、宗教事务处处长何成湘，粮食部部长章乃器，副部长陈希云、陈国栋，分别就有关我国经济建设、宗教方面的问题以及我国粮食和其他食品的供应问题进行座谈。
    在这前一天，代表团人员还分别访问了中华全国总工会和中华全国民主妇女联合会。中华全国总工会主席赖若愚，副主席刘宁一、朱学范等和中华全国民主妇女联合会副主席许广平、章蕴等分别接待了代表团人员，并向他们介绍了我国工人运动和妇女运动的情况，回答了来宾们提出的问题。
    代表团人员在这两天中还分别参观了北京大学、国营北京第一棉纺织厂、丰台车站车辆调度场、北京市人民法院监狱和一所托儿所，在这些地方，代表团人员和我国大学教授、青年学生、工人、政府工作人员、保育工作者和儿童们进行了广泛的接触。
    十八日、十九日两天，代表团人员还游览了颐和园和东安市场。
    代表团全体人员将在八月二十日到我国东北参观。
