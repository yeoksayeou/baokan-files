Title: People's Liberation Army Navy Servicemen's Representative Conference Elects Delegates to the National People's Congress
Author:
Date: 1954-08-24
Page: 1

    People's Liberation Army Navy Servicemen's Representative Conference
    Elects Delegates to the National People's Congress
    [Xinhua News Agency, 23rd] The People's Liberation Army Navy convened a servicemen's representative conference from August 21st to 22nd. Among the 69 official representatives attending the meeting were Wang Weifu, combat hero of the East China Navy and heroic commander of the torpedo boat in the Toumenshan naval battle, model soldiers and model workers in study, and representatives from naval vessel units, air defense units, and coastal artillery units of the naval forces stationed at the forefront of coastal defense.
    The conference elected Xiao Jingguang and Wang Weifu as delegates to the National People's Congress.
    Su Zhenhua, Deputy Political Commissar of the Navy, delivered a report on the Draft Constitution of the People's Republic of China at the meeting. All the representatives had a heated discussion on Su Zhenhua's report and made a resolution to support the Draft Constitution of the People's Republic of China. The resolution emphasized the need to support the Draft Constitution with practical actions, resolutely implement the government's policies and decrees, closely connect with residents and fishermen in coastal areas, strengthen the modernization and regularization of the Navy, effectively cooperate with the land and air force fraternal units to liberate enemy-occupied islands along the coast, liberate Taiwan, and strive to complete the national liberation cause, defend the socialist construction of the motherland, and defend Asian and world peace.

### 人民解放军海军军人代表大会会议  选出了全国人民代表大会代表

1954-08-24
第1版()
专栏：

    人民解放军海军军人代表大会会议
    选出了全国人民代表大会代表
    【新华社二十三日讯】　中国人民解放军海军在八月二十一日到二十二日召开了军人代表大会会议。出席会议的六十九名正式代表中，有华东海军战斗英雄、头门山海战英雄艇艇长王维福，有练兵模范、学习模范和机关模范工作者，有来自海防前哨海军的舰艇部队、防空部队、海岸炮兵部队的代表。
    会议选出了萧劲光、王维福为全国人民代表大会代表。
    海军副政治委员苏振华在会上作了关于中华人民共和国宪法草案的报告。全体代表对苏振华的报告进行了热烈的讨论，并作出了拥护中华人民共和国宪法草案的决议。决议中强调指出要以实际行动来拥护宪法草案，坚决执行政府的政策法令，密切联系沿海各地的居民、渔民，加强海军现代化、正规化的建设，有效地配合陆、空军兄弟部队解放沿海敌占岛屿，解放台湾，为完成全国解放事业，保卫祖国社会主义建设，保卫亚洲和世界和平而奋斗。
