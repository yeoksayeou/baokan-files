Title: World Federation of Democratic Youth Council Meeting Opens Today; Representatives and Guests from Forty-Nine Countries Arrive in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1954-08-09
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, August 8th: World Federation of Democratic Youth Council, 1954 August Beijing Meeting Press Communiqué: The World Federation of Democratic Youth Council meeting will open at 4:00 PM on August 9, 1954, in the Huai Ren Tang Hall in Beijing. More than 170 attending representatives, observing attendees, and guests from forty-nine countries have already arrived in the capital of the People's Republic of China. Representatives from another thirteen countries are also about to arrive in Beijing.

The agenda of the meeting is as follows:

(1) The youth movement in colonial and underdeveloped countries and the tasks of the World Federation of Democratic Youth.
(2) The participation of the World Federation of Democratic Youth and its member organizations in the International Rural Youth Conference.
(3) Preparation for the Fifth World Youth and Students Festival for Peace and Friendship.

The fact that the World Federation of Democratic Youth Council is holding its meeting in an Asian country for the first time is of great significance to the youth of all countries, and especially profound for the youth of colonial countries who are closely watching the youth movement and people's movement in Asian countries striving for peace and national independence. This meeting also has special significance for rural youth. Peasant youth account for half of the world's youth, and they are seeking a happy future in an environment of peace and friendship by preparing for the International Rural Youth Conference. This council meeting is of great significance to all young men and women, all young athletes, young artists, and young cultural workers, who are eager to maintain close friendly ties with their brothers and sisters in various countries, enhance mutual understanding through cultural and sports exchanges, and promote the consolidation of world peace.

The previous World Youth and Student Festivals achieved brilliant results, which makes us pay more attention to discussing the preparations for the Fifth World Youth and Student Festival for Peace and Friendship.

The President of the World Federation of Democratic Youth, Bruno Bernini, will preside over the opening of the Council meeting. Representatives of Chinese youth will offer congratulations to the participants and welcome them to the Council meeting. Bernini will present a report on the first item on the agenda on behalf of the Executive Committee.

A plenary meeting will be held on the morning of August 10 to discuss the first item on the agenda. The Council meeting will end on August 15, 1954.

### 世界青联理事会会议今天开幕  四十九个国家的代表和来宾等到达北京

1954-08-09
第1版()
专栏：

    世界青联理事会会议今天开幕
    四十九个国家的代表和来宾等到达北京
    【新华社八日讯】世界民主青年联盟理事会一九五四年八月北京会议新闻公报：世界民主青年联盟理事会会议将在一九五四年八月九日下午四时在北京怀仁堂开幕。四十九个国家的一百七十多名出席代表、列席观察员和来宾已经到达中华人民共和国的首都。另有十三个国家的代表也即将到达北京。
    会议议程如下：
    （一）殖民地和落后国家的青年运动和世界民主青年联盟的任务。
    （二）世界民主青年联盟和它的成员组织参加国际农村青年大会的问题。
    （三）筹备第五届世界青年与学生和平友谊联欢节问题。
    世界民主青年联盟理事会首次在亚洲国家举行会议，这对于世界各国青年有重大的意义，而对于密切注视为和平和民族独立而奋斗的亚洲国家青年运动和人民运动的那些殖民地国家青年尤其有深刻的意义。这次会议对农村青年也有特别的意义。农民青年占世界青年的半数，他们正在通过筹备国际农村青年大会来在和平和友谊的环境中求得幸福的前途。这次理事会会议对于一切青年男女、一切男女青年运动员、青年艺术家、青年文化工作者都有重要意义，他们都渴望和各国的兄弟姊妹们维持密切的友谊联系，通过文化和运动的交流增进彼此的谅解而促进世界和平的巩固。
    前几次世界青年与学生联欢节获得了辉煌的成果，因此使我们更加注意讨论第五届世界青年与学生和平友谊联欢节的筹备工作。
    世界民主青年联盟主席布伦诺·柏尼尼将主持理事会会议开幕。中国青年的代表将向参加会议的人表示祝贺并对他们出席理事会表示欢迎。柏尼尼将代表执行委员会做第一项议程的报告。
    八月十日上午开全体会议讨论第一项议程。理事会会议将在一九五四年八月十五日结束。
