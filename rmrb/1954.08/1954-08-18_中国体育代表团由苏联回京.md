Title: Chinese Sports Delegation Returns to Beijing from the Soviet Union
Author:
Date: 1954-08-18
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, 17th. The Chinese sports delegation, consisting of sixteen members led by the head of the delegation, He Long, who had been invited to participate in the 1954 Soviet Sports Festival, returned to Beijing by plane on the afternoon of the 17th.

### 中国体育代表团由苏联回京

1954-08-18
第1版()
专栏：

    中国体育代表团由苏联回京
    【新华社十七日讯】应邀参加一九五四年苏联体育节的中国体育代表团一行十六人，在团长贺龙率领下，已在十七日下午乘飞机回到北京。
