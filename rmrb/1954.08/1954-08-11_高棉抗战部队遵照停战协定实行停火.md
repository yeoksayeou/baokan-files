Title: Khmer Resistance Forces Cease Fire in Accordance with the Armistice Agreement
Author:
Date: 1954-08-11
Page: 1

(Xinhua News Agency, August 10th) All combat troops of the Khmer People's Liberation Army and Vietnamese People's Volunteer soldiers in Cambodia have ceased fire throughout Cambodia on August 7th, in accordance with the Agreement on the Cessation of Hostilities in Cambodia.

The General Headquarters of the Khmer People's Liberation Army and the Command Headquarters of the Vietnamese People's Volunteer soldiers in Cambodia jointly issued a ceasefire order immediately after the Agreement on the Cessation of Hostilities came into effect. The ceasefire order was transmitted to various combat units through all available means, such as radio and delivery by foot. The regular troops, local troops, militia, and guerrillas of the Khmer People's Liberation Army, as well as the Vietnamese People's Volunteer soldiers, faithfully carried out the ceasefire order and completely ceased fire on all fronts in Cambodia on August 7th.

### 高棉抗战部队遵照停战协定实行停火

1954-08-11
第1版()
专栏：

    高棉抗战部队遵照停战协定实行停火
    【新华社十日讯】高棉人民解放军的一切战斗部队和在柬埔寨的越南人民志愿人员遵照柬埔寨停止敌对行动协定，八月七日已在柬埔寨全境实行停火。
    高棉人民解放军总司令部和在柬埔寨的越南人民志愿人员司令部在停止敌对行动协定生效后，立即联合发布了停火命令。停火令通过无线电、步行递送等一切可以利用的方法传达到了各个战斗单位。高棉人民解放军的正规部队、地方部队、民兵和游击队，以及越南人民志愿人员忠实地执行了停火令，八月七日已在柬埔寨各战线上完全停止射击。
