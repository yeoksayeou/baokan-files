Title: Premier Zhou Enlai Hosts a Banquet in Honor of Deputy Prime Minister Pham Van Dong
Author:
Date: 1954-08-03
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, August 2nd: Premier of the State Council and concurrently Minister of Foreign Affairs of the Central People's Government of the People' Republic of China, Zhou Enlai, hosted a banquet on the evening of the 2nd to welcome Pham Van Dong, Deputy Prime Minister and Acting Minister of Foreign Affairs of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam, who came to Beijing at the invitation of our Central People's Government.

Phan Anh, Minister of Industry and Commerce of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam, Tran Cong Tuong, Deputy Minister of Justice, Zhou Liang, Charge d'Affaires ad interim of the Embassy of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam in China, and embassy staff also attended the banquet.

On the Chinese side, the attendees included: Vice Chairmen of the Central People's Government Zhu De, Liu Shaoqi, Li Jishen, Vice Premiers of the State Council Dong Biwu, Guo Moruo, Huang Yanpei, Deng Xiaoping, Vice Chairmen of the National Committee of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference (CPPCC) Shen Junru, Chen Shutong, Members of the Central People's Government Committee Li The diplomatic envoys of various countries to China who attended the banquet included: Burmese Ambassador U Law Maung, Indian Ambassador N. Raghavan, Swedish Ambassador Wistrand, Romanian Ambassador Teodor Rudenco, Indonesian Ambassador Mononutu, Ambassador of the German Democratic Republic Johannes König, Ambassador of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea Choe Il, Danish Minister A. Reporters from various countries stationed in China also attended the banquet.

At the banquet, Premier Zhou Enlai and Deputy Prime Minister Pham Van Dong both delivered speeches (full texts to be published separately).

The banquet proceeded in a warm and friendly atmosphere.

After the banquet, Deputy Prime Minister Pham Van Dong and others attended a welcome soiree.

专栏：

    周恩来总理宴请范文同副总理
    【新华社二日讯】中华人民共和国中央人民政府政务院总理兼外交部部长周恩来，在二日晚设宴招待应我中央人民政府邀请来到北京的越南民主共和国副总理兼代理外交部部长范文同。
