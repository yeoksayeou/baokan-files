Title: Declaration of the World Federation of Democratic Youth Council

Author: 
Date: 1954-08-16
Page: 1

    Declaration of the Council of the World Federation of Democratic Youth
    August 9-15, 1954, Beijing
    We, the participants of the Beijing meeting of the Council of the World Federation of Democratic Youth, on behalf of millions of young people from all over the world, warmly welcome the Geneva Conference which ended the massacre in Indochina. The Geneva Accords are a new victory for the forces of peace over the forces of war.
    In celebrating this victory, we pay heartfelt tribute to the heroic youth of Vietnam, Laos, and Cambodia, and to the glorious French youth who fought to end this dirty war.
    Dear friends! Young men and women of all countries!
    The Geneva Accords once again prove the possibility of peaceful resolution of all international disputes.
    Further relaxation of the international situation and consolidation of peace and security for the people of all countries—regardless of their political and economic systems—based on peaceful coexistence, depend on the activities of you, us, and millions of peace defenders.
    For the bright future of the younger generation, we call on you to fight more actively for peace.
    Resolutely demand the resumption of negotiations for peace in Korea! Resolutely demand a thorough and peaceful settlement of the Indochina issue! Resolutely demand an end to the arms race and the prohibition of atomic weapons, hydrogen weapons, and other weapons of mass destruction!
    Unite! Strive for collective security in Europe, and for the resolution of the German question on the basis of peace and democracy! Oppose military pacts, which by their very nature threaten peace! Resolutely demand that all international issues be resolved through peaceful means!
    Let us unite more firmly in the struggle for peace and a bright future!

### 世界民主青年联盟理事会宣言

1954-08-16
第1版()
专栏：

    世界民主青年联盟理事会宣言
    一九五四年八月九日——十五日。北京
    我们，世界民主青年联盟理事会北京会议的参与者，以世界各国千百万青年的名义，热烈欢迎结束了印度支那屠杀的日内瓦会议。日内瓦的协议是和平力量对战争势力的一次新的胜利。
    在庆贺这一胜利时，我们向越南、老挝、柬埔寨的英勇的青年以及为结束这一肮脏战争而斗争的光荣的法国青年致以衷心的敬礼。
    亲爱的朋友们！各国青年男女们！
    日内瓦的协议再一次证明了和平解决一切国际争端的可能性。
    国际局势的进一步缓和以及在各国——不分其政治和经济制度——和平共处的基础上巩固各国人民的和平及安全，有赖于你们、我们及千百万和平保卫者的活动。
    为了青年一代的光明前途，我们号召你们更加积极地为争取和平而斗争。
    坚决要求重新开始争取朝鲜和平的协商！坚决要求彻底和平解决印度支那问题！坚决要求终止军备竞赛，禁止原子武器、氢武器及其他大规模屠杀的武器！
    团结起来！争取欧洲的集体安全，争取在和平及民主的基础上解决德国问题！反对军事公约，这些公约从本质上就威胁着和平！坚决要求通过和平方式解决一切国际问题！
    让我们在争取和平及光明前途的斗争中，更坚决的团结起来！
