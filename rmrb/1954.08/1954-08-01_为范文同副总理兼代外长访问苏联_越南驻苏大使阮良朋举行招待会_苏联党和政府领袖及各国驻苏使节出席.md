Title: Vice Premier and Acting Foreign Minister Pham Van Dong Visits the Soviet Union; Vietnamese Ambassador to the Soviet Union Nguyen Luong Bang Holds Reception; Soviet Party and Government Leaders and Heads of Diplomatic Missions in the Soviet Union Attend
Author: 
Date: 1954-08-01
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, 31st: TASS Moscow, 31st - The Ambassador of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam to the Soviet Union, Nguyen Luong Bang, held a reception on the 30th to mark the visit of Pham Van Dong, Deputy Prime Minister and Acting Minister of Foreign Affairs of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam, to the Soviet Union.

Those present at the reception included N.A. Bulganin, L.M. Kaganovich, G.M. Malenkov, A.I. Mikoyan, V.M. Molotov, M.G. Pervukhin, M.Z. Saburov, N.S. Khrushchev, N.M. Shvernik, Vice-Chairman of the Council of Ministers of the Soviet Union V.A. Malyschev, Chairman of the Presidium of the Supreme Soviet of the Russian Federation M.P. Tarasov , Chairman of the Council Of Minister of the Russian Federation A.M. Puzanov, Ministers of the Soviet Union: S.A. Akopov, S.F. Antonov, P.Ya. Antropov, B.P. Beshchev, V.D. Vakhrushev, V.P. Yelyutin, V.P. Zotov, N.S. Kazakov, V.D. Kalmykov, A.I. Kozlov, S.N. Kruglov, P.F. Lomako, G.M. Orlov, S.A. Stepanov, S.M. Tikhomirov, Z.A. Shashkov, President of the State Bank of the Soviet Union V.F. Popov, First Deputy Minister of Foreign Affairs of the Soviet Union A.Y. Vyshinsky, President of the Supreme Court of the Soviet Union A.A. Volin, Chairman of the State Construction Committee of the Council of Ministers of the Soviet Union K.M. Sokolov, Marshal of the Soviet Union G.K. Zhukov, Admiral of the Fleet N.G. Kuznetsov, General of the Army I.A. Serov, Director of TASS N.G. Palgunov, Editor-in-Chief of "Pravda" D.T. Shepilov, Editor-in-Chief of "Izvestia" K.A. Gubin, Chairman of the Executive Committee of the Moscow Oblast Soviet A.P. Sheremetev, Deputy Secretary of the Presidium of the Supreme Soviet of the Soviet Union A.F. Gorkin, Marshals of Aviation P.F. Zhigarev and N.S. Skripko, Generals of the Army: A.I. Antonov, V.V. Kurasov and G.K. Malandin, Colonel-General M.A. Shalin, Admirals: N.Ye. Basisty, V.A. Fokin and G.I. Levchenko, Chairman of the Council of the Soviet Society for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries A.I. Denisov, Chairman of the Soviet Peace Committee N.S. The heads of diplomatic missions to the Soviet Union present at the reception included: Hungarian Ambassador Sziklai, Ambassador of the People's Republic of China Zhang Wentian, Ambassador of Burma U Ohn, Ambassador of India K.P.S. Menon, Ambassador of Czechoslovakia Vosahlik, Ambassador of Albania Prifti, Ambassador of Bulgaria Lukanov, Ambassador of Indonesia Subandrio, Ambassador of Romania Rab, Chargé d'Affaires ad interim of France Leroy, Chargé d'Affaires ad interim of the German Democratic Republic Seiz, Chargé d'Affaires ad interim of the Mongolian People's Republic Tobo, Chargé d'Affaires ad interim of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea Park De-hwan, and Chargé d'Affaires ad interim of Poland Maletzki.

The reception took place in a warm and friendly atmosphere.

### 为范文同副总理兼代外长访问苏联  越南驻苏大使阮良朋举行招待会  苏联党和政府领袖及各国驻苏使节出席

1954-08-01
第4版()
专栏：

    为范文同副总理兼代外长访问苏联
    越南驻苏大使阮良朋举行招待会
    苏联党和政府领袖及各国驻苏使节出席
   【新华社三十一日讯】塔斯社莫斯科三十一日讯：越南民主共和国驻苏联大使阮良朋，为越南民主共和国副总理兼代理外交部部长范文同访问苏联，特于三十日举行招待会。
    出席招待会的有尼·亚·布尔加宁、拉·莫·卡冈诺维奇、格·马·马林科夫、阿·伊·米高扬、维·米·莫洛托夫、米·格·别尔乌辛、马·扎·萨布罗夫、尼·谢·赫鲁晓夫、尼·米·什维尔尼克，苏联部长会议副主席维·亚·马利歇夫，俄罗斯联邦最高苏维埃主席团主席麦·普·塔拉索夫，俄罗斯联邦部长会议主席亚·麦·普扎诺夫，苏联各部部长：斯·阿·阿科波夫、谢·费·安东诺夫、彼·雅·安特罗波夫、鲍·巴·别什切夫、弗·德·瓦拉克辛、维·彼·叶留金、瓦·彼·佐托夫、尼·斯·卡扎科夫、维·德·卡尔米科夫、阿·伊·科兹洛夫、谢·涅·克鲁格洛夫、彼·弗·洛马科、格·米·奥尔洛夫、谢·亚·斯捷潘诺夫、谢·米·季霍米罗夫、佐·阿·夏希科夫，苏联国家银行行长维·弗·波波夫，苏联外交部第一副部长安·扬·维辛斯基，苏联最高法院院长安·阿·沃林，苏联部长会议国家建设委员会主席康·米·索科洛夫，苏联元帅格·康·朱可夫，海军元帅纳·格·库兹涅佐夫，陆军上将伊·亚·谢罗夫，塔斯社社长尼·格·帕尔古诺夫，“真理报”总编辑德·特·谢皮洛夫，“消息报”总编辑克·阿·古宾，莫斯科州苏维埃执行委员会主席阿·普·沃尔科夫，莫斯科市苏维埃主席米·阿·雅斯诺夫，苏联各部副部长：斯·阿·波里索夫、阿·维·杜姆拉契夫、瓦·亚·佐林、麦·鲁·库兹明、阿·斯·帕维尔耶夫、克·伊·谢尔盖楚克、伊·阿·斯特里金、阿·维·谢列麦捷夫，苏联最高苏维埃主席团副秘书亚·费·戈尔金，空军元帅帕·弗·日加列夫和纳·斯·斯克里普科，陆军大将：阿·伊·安东诺夫、弗·弗·库拉索夫和格·克·马兰金，陆军上将麦·阿·夏林，海军上将：尼·叶·巴西斯蒂、维·阿·弗金和格·伊·列夫钦科，苏联对外文化协会理事会主席亚·伊·杰尼索夫，苏联拥护和平委员会主席尼·谢·吉洪诺夫，以及苏联外交部、对外贸易部、文化部和国防部的高级官员，群众团体代表、著名的科学和文化界人士及苏联和外国记者。
