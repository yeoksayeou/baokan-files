Title: President Bernini of the World Federation of Democratic Youth Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1954-08-02
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, August 1st—Brenno Bernini, President of the World Federation of Democratic Youth, who came to preside over the Beijing meeting of the Council of the World Federation of Democratic Youth, arrived in Beijing by plane on the afternoon of the 1st.

Those who welcomed him at the airport included: Liao Chengzhi, Chairman of the All-China Federation of Democratic Youth; Liu Daosheng and Qu Tangliang, Vice-Chairmen; Hu Yaobang and Zhang Ze, Secretaries of the Central Committee of the China New Democratic Youth League; Yu Beichen, Secretary-General of the All-China Federation of Democratic Youth; and others.

Also welcoming him at the airport were: Zhang Wenhao, Vice-Chairman of the All-China Students' Federation; Zhang Dazhong, Chairman of the Beijing Federation of Democratic Youth; and others.

Also welcoming him at the airport were: Oskar Fischer (Germany), Secretary of the World Federation of Democratic Youth, who arrived earlier in Beijing; Radnay Fanos, Head of the Administrative Office of the World Federation of Democratic Youth (Hungary); and others.

One hundred young students and Young Pioneers from Beijing also came to the airport to welcome him.

President Bernini stepped off the plane amidst warm applause and shook hands with the welcomers, and the Young Pioneers presented flowers.

Subsequently, Liao Chengzhi delivered a welcome speech. He said, "First of all, let me, on behalf of the All-China Federation of Democratic Youth, extend a warm welcome to President Bernini and all the friends of the World Federation of Democratic Youth for coming to our capital, Beijing." He then said: We believe that, at a time when the struggle of young people around the world to defend peace and strive for national independence is at an unprecedented high, the gathering of young people from various countries will certainly make an important contribution to enhancing the friendship and unity of young people from various countries, and to the world peace movement.

President Bernini then delivered a reply speech. He said that Chinese youth, while engaged in the struggle to build their motherland, have made significant contributions to strengthening world youth's peace and friendship. He expressed his gratitude to the Chinese youth on behalf of the World Federation of Democratic Youth and congratulated them on their new victories in the great cause of building their motherland.

### 世界民主青年联盟主席柏尼尼到京

1954-08-02
第4版()
专栏：

    世界民主青年联盟主席柏尼尼到京
    【新华社一日讯】前来主持世界民主青年联盟理事会北京会议的世界民主青年联盟主席布伦诺·柏尼尼，在一日下午乘机到达北京。
    到机场欢迎的有：中华全国民主青年联合会主席廖承志、副主席刘导生、区棠亮，中国新民主主义青年团中央委员会书记处书记胡耀邦、章泽，中华全国民主青年联合会秘书长于北辰等。
    到机场欢迎的还有：中华全国学生联合会副主席张文浩、北京市民主青年联合会主席张大中。
    到机场欢迎的还有：先期抵京的世界民主青年联盟书记奥斯卡·费希尔（德国）、世界民主青年联盟行政处负责人拉德奈·法诺什（匈牙利）等人。
    北京市青年学生和少年先锋队队员一百人也到机场欢迎。
    柏尼尼主席在热烈掌声中走下飞机和欢迎者握手，少年先锋队队员献花。
    随后廖承志致欢迎词。他说：“首先让我代表中华全国民主青年联合会向以柏尼尼主席为首的世界民主青年联盟的各位朋友来到我们首都——北京，表示热烈欢迎。”接着他说：我们相信，在全世界青年保卫和平与争取民族独立的斗争空前高涨的今天，各国青年会集一堂，对增进各国青年的友谊团结，和对世界和平运动一定会有重要贡献。
    柏尼尼主席接着致答词。他说，中国青年在建设祖国的斗争的同时，对加强世界青年的和平与友谊作了重大贡献。他代表世界民主青年联盟对中国青年们表示感谢，并祝贺中国青年在建设祖国的伟大事业中，获得新的胜利。
