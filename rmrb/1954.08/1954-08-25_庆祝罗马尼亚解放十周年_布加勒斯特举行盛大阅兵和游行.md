Title: Grand Parade and Procession Held in Bucharest to Celebrate the Tenth Anniversary of Romania's Liberation
Author:
Date: 1954-08-25
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency dispatch: Bucharest held a grand military parade and mass procession on Stalin Square, the Romanian capital, on August 23rd, the tenth anniversary of Romania's liberation. Stalin Square was also decked out in festive attire. Standing on the reviewing stand were Romania's advanced workers, renowned scientists and cultural workers, representatives from various people's organizations and the army, as well as diplomatic envoys and foreign guests stationed in Romania from the Soviet Union, China, and other countries.

The leaders of the Romanian Workers' Party and the government, headed by Gheorghe Gheorghiu-Dej, and all members of the Soviet government delegation, headed by Shvernik, ascended the reviewing platform, at which point the venue erupted in enthusiastic applause.

At 8:00 AM, the military parade began. General Bodnaraș, Vice-Chairman of the Council of Ministers and Minister of Armed Forces of the Romanian People's Republic, reviewed the troops standing on the square. He then stepped onto the reviewing platform and delivered a celebratory speech on behalf of the Central Committee of the Romanian Workers' Party and the Council of Ministers. At the conclusion of the speech, cheers of "Hurrah," the sound of celebratory gun salutes, and military music resounded in unison.

The march-past began. Students from the Romanian Military Academy, infantry, naval soldiers, border guard soldiers, cavalry, mechanized troops, artillery, and tank units sequentially marched past the reviewing platform in orderly formations. Transport planes, fighter jets, and jet aircraft continuously flew overhead. The people in the square warmly welcomed the Romanian armed forces who faithfully defend the peaceful construction efforts of the Romanian people.

Next, the mass procession began. The workers' militia arrived at the square. Happy children and Young Pioneers, waving colorful scarves and bouquets, walked past the reviewing platform. Following them were students from labor reserve schools and workers' contingents from various factories in Bucharest. The paraders held aloft portraits of the leaders of the Romanian Workers' Party and the government, portraits of members of the Presidium of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of the Soviet Union, and portraits of leaders of many countries' communist and workers' parties. The paraders also carried many charts and models illustrating the production achievements of various enterprises in the Romanian capital and the achievements Romania has made over the past decade. Slogans and cheers rose and fell in the square.

The military parade and the procession of the working people of Bucharest lasted for more than five hours.

On the evening of the 23rd, the Romanian Council of Ministers held a grand reception.

### 庆祝罗马尼亚解放十周年  布加勒斯特举行盛大阅兵和游行

1954-08-25
第4版()
专栏：

    庆祝罗马尼亚解放十周年
    布加勒斯特举行盛大阅兵和游行
    据新华社讯：在八月二十三日——罗马尼亚解放十周年纪念日，罗马尼亚首都布加勒斯特的斯大林广场上举行了盛大的阅兵和群众游行。斯大林广场也披上了节日的盛装。站在观礼台上的有罗马尼亚先进的工人、著名的科学家和文化工作者、各人民团体和军队的代表，以及苏联、中国和其他国家驻罗马尼亚的外交使节和外宾。
    以乔治乌—德治为首的罗马尼亚工人党和政府的领导人、以什维尔尼克为首的苏联政府代表团全体人员登上检阅台，这时，会场热烈鼓掌欢迎。
    上午八时，阅兵式开始。罗马尼亚人民共和国部长会议副主席兼武装部队部部长波德纳拉希大将检阅了站在广场上的部队。接着，他走上检阅台代表罗马尼亚工人党中央委员会和部长会议发表了庆祝节日的演说。演说毕，“乌拉”声、礼炮声、军乐声齐鸣。
    分列式开始。罗马尼亚军事学院的学生、步兵、海军战士、边防军战士、骑兵、机械化部队、炮兵和坦克部队，相继以整齐的队形走过检阅台。运输机、战斗机和喷气式飞机连接地飞过广场上空。广场上的人们热烈欢迎忠实地捍卫着罗马尼亚人民和平建设劳动的罗马尼亚武装部队。
    接着，群众游行开始。工人自卫队来到了广场。幸福的儿童、少年先锋队队员挥着五颜六色的领巾和花束走过检阅台。往后是劳动后备学校的学生和布加勒斯特各工厂的工人队伍。游行者高举着罗马尼亚工人党和政府的领导人的画像，高举着苏联共产党中央委员会主席团委员的画像，高举着许多国家的共产党和工人党领袖的画像。游行者还拿着许多说明罗马尼亚首都各企业的生产成就和罗马尼亚十年来所取得的成就的图表和模型。广场上口号声和欢呼声此起彼落。
    阅兵和布加勒斯特劳动人民的游行持续了五个多钟头。
    二十三日晚间，罗马尼亚部长会议举行了盛大的招待会。
