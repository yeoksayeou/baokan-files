Title: Lao International Supervisory and Control Commission Advance Team Officially Meets with Representatives of Both Sides of the Lao Joint Armistice Commission
Author:
Date: 1954-08-22
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency, Vientiane, August 21st: According to the Vietnam News Agency, Vientiane, August 20th: The advance team of the Lao International Supervisory and Control Commission officially met with the delegation of the General Command of the Lao Fighting Forces and the Military Command of the Vietnamese People's Volunteers, as well as the delegation of the Command of the Lao Royal Armed Forces and the Command of the French Armed Forces on August 13th.

    At the meeting, Indian Major General Pathania, representing the advance team of the Lao International Supervisory and Control Commission, pointed out that the implementation of the armistice agreement mainly relies on the relevant parties, so the Joint Armistice Commission plays a major role. He guaranteed to do his best to assist both sides in resolving any difficulties that may arise.

    Colonel Deng Wenxin, representing the Lao Fighting Forces and the Vietnamese People's Volunteers, guaranteed to fully and strictly implement all the provisions of the armistice agreement and hoped that the French Federation forces would do the same.

### 老挝国际监督和监察委员会先遣队   正式会见老挝联合停战委员会双方代表

1954-08-22
第4版()
专栏：

    老挝国际监督和监察委员会先遣队
    正式会见老挝联合停战委员会双方代表
    【新华社二十一日讯】据越南通讯社老挝二十日讯：老挝国际监督和监察委员会先遣队在八月十三日正式会见了寮国战斗部队总司令部和越南人民志愿人员军事指挥部的代表团以及老挝王国武装部队司令部和法国武装部队司令部的代表团。
    在会见会上，印度的帕坦尼亚少将代表老挝国际监督和监察委员会先遣队指出，停战协定的履行主要依靠有关的双方，因此联合停战委员会起着主要作用。他保证尽力协助双方解决一切可能发生的困难。
    邓文心上校代表寮国战斗部队和越南人民志愿人员方面保证完全地、严格地履行停战协定中的一切规定，并希望法兰西联邦部队方面也这样做。
