Title: Deputy Prime Minister Pham Van Dong Leaves Beijing for Home; Premier Zhou Enlai and Others See Him Off at the Station
Author:
Date: 1954-08-05
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, August 4th:

    Deputy Prime Minister and Acting Minister of Foreign Affairs of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam, Pham Van Dong, left Beijing by train on the evening of the 4th to return home. Accompanying him were Phan Anh, Minister of Industry and Commerce, and Tran Cong Tuong, Deputy Minister of Justice, both of the Government of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam.
    Those who went to the station to see them off included: Zhou Enlai, Premier of the State Council of the Central People's Government; Vice Premiers Dong Biwu, Guo Moruo, Huang Yanpei, and Deng Xiaoping; Shen Junru and Chen Shutong, Vice Chairmen of the National Committee of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference; Xi Zhongxun, Secretary-General of the State Council of the Central People's Government; Zeng Shan, Teng Daiyuan, Chen Shaoxian, Wang Kunlun, Luo Longji, Shao Lizi, and Huang Shaohong, members of the Government Administration Council; Yang Xiufeng, Shen Yanbing, Liang Xi, Zhang Zhizhong, Ma Yinchu, Huang Qixiang, and Wu Yaozong, Standing Committee members of the National Committee of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference; Luo Ruiqing, Deputy Director of the Political and Legal Committee of the State Council of the Central People's Government; Ye Jizhuang, Deputy Director of the Financial and Economic Committee; Wang Jiaxiang, Zhang Hanfu, and Wu Xiuquan, Vice Ministers of Foreign Affairs; Zhang Zongxun, Deputy Chief of the General Staff of the People's Revolutionary Military Council; Xiao Hua and Gan Siqi, Deputy Directors of the General Political Department; Zhang Youyu and Wu Han, Deputy Mayors of the Beijing Municipal People's Government; Xu Teli, member of the Central Committee of the Chinese Communist Party and Yang Shangkun, Director of the General Office; Cai Tingkai, Standing Committee member of the Central Committee of the Revolutionary Committee of the Chinese Kuomintang, and Liu Fei, member of the Central Committee; Zhang Bojun and Gao Chongmin, Vice Chairmen of the Central Committee of the China Democratic League; Zhang Naiqi, Deputy Director of the General Committee of the China Democratic National Construction Association; Xu Guangping, Secretary-General of the Central Council of the China Association for Promoting Democracy; Peng Zemin, Vice Chairman of the Central Committee of the Chinese Peasants and Workers Democratic Party; Chen Qiyou, Chairman of the China Zhi Gong Party; Xu Deheng, Chairman of the Central Committee of the Jiusan Society; Lin Yun, Director of the Beijing Office of the Taiwan Democratic Self-Government League; Liu Ningyi, Vice Chairman of the All-China Federation of Trade Unions; Liu Daosheng, Vice Chairman of the All-China Youth Federation; Zhang Wenhao, Vice Chairman of the All-China Students' Federation; Chu Tunan, President of the Chinese People's Association for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries; and Ji Chao Ding, Secretary-General of the China Council for the Promotion of International Trade, among others.
    Zhou Liang, Chargé d'affaires ad interim of the Embassy of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam in China, and embassy staff members also went to the station to see them off.
    The diplomatic envoys of various countries to China who went to the station to see them off included: U Ohn Maung, Ambassador of Burma; N. Raghavan, Ambassador of India; Teodor Rudenco, Ambassador of Romania; Mononutu, Ambassador of Indonesia; Johannes König, Ambassador of the German Democratic Republic; Choi Il, Ambassador of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea; V.V. Vaskov, Chargé d'affaires ad interim of the Soviet Union; Smíd, Chargé d'affaires ad interim of Czechoslovakia; Csatordai, Chargé d'affaires ad interim of Hungary; Grigorov, Chargé d'affaires ad interim of Bulgaria; Luvsanchultem, Chargé d'affaires ad interim of Mongolia; Gola, Chargé d'affaires ad interim of Poland, as well as diplomatic officials from various embassies in China.
    The national flags of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam and the People's Republic of China were hung at the station.
    When Deputy Prime Minister Pham Van Dong entered the station, the band played the national anthems of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam and the People's Republic of China. Deputy Prime Minister Pham Van Dong reviewed the guard of honor and accepted flowers presented by members of the Young Pioneers of Beijing. Then, Deputy Prime Minister Pham Van Dong delivered a speech at the station (full text to be published separately). His speech was warmly welcomed.

### 范文同副总理离北京返国  周恩来总理等到车站欢送

1954-08-05
第1版()
专栏：

    范文同副总理离北京返国
    周恩来总理等到车站欢送
   【新华社四日讯】越南民主共和国副总理兼代理外交部部长范文同在四日晚乘火车离开北京返国。同行的，有：越南民主共和国政府工商部部长潘英、司法部副部长陈公祥。
    前往车站欢送的有：中央人民政府政务院总理周恩来、副总理董必武、郭沫若、黄炎培、邓小平，中国人民政治协商会议全国委员会副主席沈钧儒、陈叔通，中央人民政府政务院秘书长习仲勋，政务委员曾山、滕代远、陈劭先、王昆仑、罗隆基、邵力子、黄绍竑，中国人民政治协商会议全国委员会常务委员杨秀峰、沈雁冰、梁希、张治中、马寅初、黄琪翔、吴耀宗，中央人民政府政务院政治法律委员会副主任罗瑞卿，财政经济委员会副主任叶季壮，外交部副部长王稼祥、章汉夫、伍修权，人民革命军事委员会副总参谋长张宗逊，总政治部副主任萧华、甘泗淇，北京市人民政府副市长张友渔、吴晗，中国共产党中央委员会委员徐特立、办公厅主任杨尚昆，中国国民党革命委员会中央常务委员会委员蔡廷锴、中央委员会委员刘斐，中国民主同盟中央委员会副主席章伯钧、高崇民，中国民主建国会总会委员会副主任委员章乃器，中国民主促进会中央理事会秘书长许广平，中国农工民主党中央委员会副主席彭泽民，中国致公党中央委员会主席陈其尤，九三学社中央委员会主席许德珩，台湾民主自治同盟驻京办事处主任林云，中华全国总工会副主席刘宁一，中华全国民主青年联合会副主席刘导生，中华全国学生联合会副主席张文浩，中国人民对外文化协会会长楚图南，中国国际贸易促进委员会秘书长冀朝鼎等。
    越南民主共和国驻我国大使馆临时代办周亮和使馆人员也到车站欢送。
    前往车站欢送的各国驻我国外交使节，有：缅甸大使吴拉茂、印度大使赖嘉文、罗马尼亚大使郭佐文、印尼大使莫诺努图、德意志民主共和国大使柯尼希、朝鲜民主主义人民共和国大使崔一、苏联临时代办华司考、捷克斯洛伐克临时代办石密茨、匈牙利临时代办察托尔道伊、保加利亚临时代办格利郭洛夫、蒙古临时代办罗布桑楚勒图姆、波兰临时代办古拉，以及各国驻我国使馆的外交官员。
    车站上悬有越南民主共和国和中华人民共和国两国国旗。
    当范文同副总理走进车站时，乐队奏起越南民主共和国和中华人民共和国的国歌。范文同副总理检阅了仪仗队，并接受了北京市少年先锋队队员的献花。接着，范文同副总理在车站上发表了谈话（全文另发）。他的讲话，受到了热烈的欢迎。
