Title: US Spy Agency Activities in Taiwan
Author:
Date: 1954-08-22
Page: 4

    Xinhua News Agency, August 21st: According to a report from Hong Kong by journalist Stephen Barber published in the British "News Chronicle" on the 16th, American spy organizations are conducting conspiratorial activities in Taiwan. Barber said, "There are many Americans in Taiwan now. In addition to the official military delegation headed by General William Chase, a more mysterious organization called 'Western Enterprise' has begun to attract attention." Barber said, "It is generally believed that this secret organization is a branch of the American intelligence agency, and its members include some adventurers from the famous 'Flying Tigers'."

### 美国特务组织在台湾活动

1954-08-22
第4版()
专栏：

    美国特务组织在台湾活动
    【新华社二十一日讯】据英国“新闻纪事报”十六日刊载记者斯蒂芬·巴伯尔发自香港的报道，美国的特务组织正在台湾进行阴谋活动。巴伯尔说，“现在很多美国人在台湾。除了以威廉·蔡斯将军为首的正式的军事代表团以外，还有一个叫做‘西方事业’的更为神秘的组织已经开始受人注意了。”巴伯尔说，“一般认为，这个秘密组织是美国谍报机关的一个分支机构，它的成员包括著名的
  ‘飞虎队’中的若干冒险家们。”
