Title: Great Achievements of Peaceful Negotiation (Pictures)
Author:
Date: 1954-08-01
Page: 5

    Xinhua News Agency reporter Liu Dong'ao photographed Zhou Enlai, Chief Representative of the Chinese Delegation to the Geneva Conference, hosting a banquet on June 21 for Pham Van Dong (second from right), head of the delegation of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam, as well as Tep Phan (first from left), head of the delegation of the Kingdom of Cambodia, and Phouang Sananikone (second from left), head of the delegation of the Kingdom of Laos. The picture shows the guests talking after the banquet.
    Xinhua News Agency reporter Liu Dong'ao photographed Soviet Foreign Minister Molotov visiting Foreign Minister Zhou Enlai in Geneva.
    The world-renowned Geneva Conference concluded on July 21, with agreements reached on the cessation of hostilities and restoration of peace in Indochina. This is a great victory for peace over war.
    Xinhua News Agency reporter Liu Dong'ao photographed the Chinese delegation at the meeting hall of the Geneva Conference discussing the issue of peace in Indochina. Seated in the front row center is Zhou Enlai, Chief Representative of the Chinese Delegation, Premier of the State Council and Minister of Foreign Affairs of the Central People's Government.
    Xinhua News Agency reporter Liu Dong'ao photographed British Foreign Secretary Eden visiting Foreign Minister Zhou Enlai in Geneva.
    Xinhua News Agency reporter Liu Dong'ao photographed French Prime Minister and Foreign Minister Mendès France (left) meeting with Foreign Minister Zhou Enlai in Geneva.
    Xinhua News Agency reporter Liu Dong'ao photographed the Soviet delegation at the meeting hall of the Geneva Conference discussing the issue of peace in Indochina. Seated in the front row center is Molotov, Chief Representative of the Soviet Delegation and Minister of Foreign Affairs of the Soviet Union.
    Xinhua News Agency reporter Liu Dong'ao photographed Zhou Enlai, Chief Representative of the Chinese Delegation to the Geneva Conference, receiving Menon, the Indian representative to the United Nations, in Geneva.
    Xinhua News Agency reporter Liu Dong'ao photographed Australian Foreign Minister Casey visiting Foreign Minister Zhou Enlai in Geneva.
    Xinhua News Agency reporter Liu Dong'ao photographed the delegation of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam at the meeting hall of the Geneva Conference discussing the issue of peace in Indochina. Seated in the front row center is Pham Van Dong, Chief Representative of the delegation of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam, Deputy Prime Minister and Acting Minister of Foreign Affairs of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam.
    Xinhua News Agency reporter Qian Sijie photographed people from all walks of life in the capital holding a grand rally on July 23 to warmly celebrate the agreement reached at the Geneva Conference on the cessation of hostilities and restoration of peace in Indochina. The picture shows the adoption of the resolution at the conference.

### 和平协商的巨大成就（图片）

1954-08-01
第5版()
专栏：

    和平协商的巨大成就
    出席日内瓦会议的我国代表团首席代表周恩来，六月二十一日宴请越南民主共和国代表团团长范文同（右起第二人），以及柬埔寨王国代表团团长泰普潘（左第一人）和老挝王国代表团团长冯·萨纳尼空（左起第二人）。图为宴会后宾主谈话。　新华社记者　刘东鳌摄
    苏联外交部部长莫洛托夫在日内瓦访问周恩来外长。新华社记者刘东鳌摄
    举世瞩目的日内瓦会议已于七月二十一日结束了，关于印度支那停战和恢复和平问题的谈判达成协议。这是和平战胜战争的巨大胜利。
    图为在日内瓦会议讨论关于印度支那和平问题会议会场的我国代表团。前排中坐者是我国代表团首席代表、我中央人民政府政务院总理兼外交部部长周恩来。　新华社记者　刘东鳌摄
