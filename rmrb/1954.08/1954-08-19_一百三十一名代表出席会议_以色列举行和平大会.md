Title: 131 Representatives Attend Conference, Israel Holds Peace Conference
Author:
Date: 1954-08-19
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency, Tel Aviv, August 15th: According to TASS, the congress of representatives of peace committees from various districts of Israel was held in Tel Aviv. 131 representatives attended the congress, with an additional 2,000 guests present. The congress was conducted under the following slogans: Oppose the rearmament of Germany; oppose Israel joining aggressive blocs; seek peace between Israel and Arab countries through direct negotiations, without foreign mediators or preconditions.
    Chairman of the Presidium of the Israeli Peace Committee and Member of Parliament Yaari, members of the Peace Committee and Members of Parliament Dubi, Berman, and Bar-Yehuda, as well as Secretary of the Peace Committee Maios and poet Shlensky, all addressed the congress.

### 一百三十一名代表出席会议  以色列举行和平大会

1954-08-19
第4版()
专栏：

    一百三十一名代表出席会议
    以色列举行和平大会
    【新华社十七日讯】据塔斯社特拉维夫十五日讯：以色列各区和平委员会代表大会在特拉维夫举行，出席大会的有一百三十一名代表。另有两千名来宾列席。大会是在下述口号下进行的：反对武装德国；反对以色列加入侵略集团；不要外国调停人也不要先决条件，通过直接谈判谋求以色列和阿拉伯国家的和平。
    以色列和平委员会主席团主席、议会议员雅利，和平委员会委员、议会议员杜比，贝尔曼和巴尔—叶古达，以及和平委员会秘书迈欧斯和诗人希伦斯基，都向大会讲了话。
