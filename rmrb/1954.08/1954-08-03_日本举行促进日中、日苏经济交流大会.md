Title: Japan Holds Conference to Promote Sino-Japanese and Japanese-Soviet Economic Exchange
Author:
Date: 1954-08-03
Page: 4
　
　　Xinhua News Agency, Tokyo, 2nd. A national conference to promote Sino-Japanese and Japanese-Soviet economic exchange was jointly held on the 1st in Hakodate City, Hokkaido, by seven organizations including the "Diet Members' League for Promoting Sino-Japanese Trade" and the "Diet Members' League for Promoting Japanese-Soviet Trade." Approximately 200 representatives from various organizations participated in the conference.

　　The conference passed four resolutions, demanding: (1) the abolition of all trade restrictions between Japan and peaceful democratic countries; (2) the mutual dispatch of economic representatives between Japan and peaceful democratic countries; (3) assistance to Japanese trading companies in signing barter trade contracts with the Soviet Union; (4) the holding of a national conference to promote the movement for free trade with China and the Soviet Union.

### 日本举行促进日中、日苏经济交流大会

1954-08-03
第4版()
专栏：

    日本举行促进日中、日苏经济交流大会
    【新华社二日讯】东京消息：日本“国会议员促进日中贸易联盟”和“国会议员促进日苏贸易联盟”等七个团体一日在北海道的函馆市联合举行了促进日中、日苏经济交流全国大会。参加大会的有各团体代表约二百人。
    大会通过四项决议，要求：（一）取消日本与和平民主国家的一切贸易限制；（二）与和平民主国家互派经济代表；（三）协助日本贸易公司同苏联签订换货贸易合同；（四）举行全国大会，推动和中国、苏联发展自由贸易的运动。
