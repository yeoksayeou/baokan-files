Title: Large Quantities of Turpan Seedless White Grapes Hit the Market
Author:
Date: 1954-08-14
Page: 2
　
    A large quantity of Turpan seedless white grapes, a famous specialty of Xinjiang Province, have been hitting the market continuously.
    This year, in order to meet the needs of supply to all parts of the country and for export trade, Turpan County expanded the cultivation area of ​​seedless white grapes by 307.6 mu, adding 7,448 new grape plants. This year, the county has enjoyed good weather and suitable temperature, and the grapes have not been affected by natural disasters. In addition, with farmers actively improving cultivation and management techniques, the county has had a very good grape harvest.

### 吐鲁番无核白葡萄大量上市

1954-08-14
第2版()
专栏：

    吐鲁番无核白葡萄大量上市
    新疆省著名的特产——吐鲁番无核白葡萄，已经源源上市。
    今年，吐鲁番县为了供应全国各地以及出口贸易的需要，扩大无核白葡萄栽培面积三百零七亩六分，新增加葡萄七千四百四十八墩。今年该县气候良好，温度适宜，葡萄没有受到自然灾害的侵袭，加上农民积极改进栽培管理技术，全县葡萄收成很好。
