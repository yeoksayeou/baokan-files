Title: Chinese Scientists Send Telegram Congratulating Dennis on His Fiftieth Birthday and Telegram to Eisenhower Protesting US Government Persecution of Dennis and Other Democrats
Author:
Date: 1954-08-09
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, August 8th: Forty-six Chinese scientists sent telegrams to the National Committee of the Communist Party of the United States and the General Secretary of the National Committee of the Communist Party of the United States, Dennis, on August 7th, congratulating Dennis on his fiftieth birthday. On the same day, Chinese scientists sent a telegram to US President Eisenhower, protesting the US government's persecution of Dennis and other democrats, and demanding their immediate release.

The Chinese scientists said in their telegram to the National Committee of the Communist Party of the United States that Chinese scientists will fully support the American people's struggle for peace and democracy and extend their sincere respect. The telegram also included the full text of the congratulatory telegram to Dennis on his fiftieth birthday and the protest telegram to Eisenhower.

The telegram to Dennis said: "Dear Comrade Dennis: On the occasion of your fiftieth birthday, we, the Chinese scientists, would like to extend our warm congratulations to you, the outstanding son of the American working class, the heroic fighter for the cause of peace. Now, the peace-loving people of the world are striving for lasting peace and people's democracy with overwhelming force and momentum, while the US ruling clique, which attempts to enslave and exploit the people of the world, is resorting to all means to undermine peace and pursue war; at home, it is even more brutally formulating various laws to suppress the people, and taking personal persecution against you and your comrades and thousands of peace advocates. This despicable behavior has aroused the anger of peace-loving people all over the world, and peace-loving people all over the world will support your just struggle with greater victories in the struggle for peace. Please accept our condolences and respect."

The telegram to Eisenhower protesting the US government's persecution of Dennis and other democrats said: World War II ended with the complete defeat of the German fascist system, but today the United States under your leadership has once again embarked on the road taken by Hitler. The democratic rights of American peace advocates, progressives, workers, working people, and scientists have been wantonly deprived, and many people have been subjected to brutal and unreasonable persecution. We, the Chinese scientists, lodge our most serious protest to you. We demand that the US government immediately stop the political and ideological persecution of the American people, and immediately release Eugene Dennis, the General Secretary of the Communist Party of the United States, who is spending his fiftieth birthday in a dungeon-like prison, and all other persecuted political prisoners.

The telegram was signed by: Guo Moruo, Li Siguang, Liang Xi, Chen Boda, Zhang Jiafu, Tao Menghe, Zhu Kezhen, Wu Youxun, Zeng Zhaolun, Ding Xilin, Hua Luogeng, Jiang Zehan, Duan Xuefu, Rao Yutai, Ye Qisun, Zhou Peiyuan, Zhao Zhongyao, Yan Jici, Qian Sanqiang, Qian Weichang, Zhuang Changgong, Zhao Chenggu, Hou Debang, Huang Ziqing, Tu Changwang, Xie Jiarong, Qian Chongshu, Zhang Jingyue, Dai Fanglan, Liu Chongle, Liu Xianzhou, Mao Yisheng, Li Zongen, Zhong Huilan, Zhang Xiaoqian, Jin Yuelin, Feng Youlan, Ma Yinchu, Zhou Gengsheng, Chen Hansheng, Chen Daisun, Chen Yuan, Pei Wenzhong, Luo Changpei, Fei Xiaotong, Pan Guangdan.

### 我国科学工作者致电祝贺丹尼斯五十寿辰  并致电艾森豪威尔抗议美政府迫害丹尼斯和其他民主人士

1954-08-09
第4版()
专栏：

    我国科学工作者致电祝贺丹尼斯五十寿辰
    并致电艾森豪威尔抗议美政府迫害丹尼斯和其他民主人士
   【新华社八日讯】中国科学工作者四十六人在八月七日分别致电美国共产党全国委员会和美国共产党全国委员会总书记丹尼斯，祝贺丹尼斯五十寿辰。中国科学工作者在同一天致电美国总统艾森豪威尔，抗议美国政府迫害丹尼斯和其他民主人士，并要求立即释放他们。
    中国科学工作者在给美国共产党全国委员会的电文中说，中国科学工作者对美国人民争取和平和民主的斗争将全力支持，并致衷心的敬意。电文并附有祝贺丹尼斯五十寿辰的慰问电和致艾森豪威尔的抗议电的全文。
    给丹尼斯的电文说：“敬爱的丹尼斯同志：值您五十岁寿辰之际，我们中国科学工作者谨向您，美国工人阶级的卓越的儿子，和平事业的英勇战士，致以热烈的祝贺。现在，全世界爱好和平的人民正在以排山倒海、波澜壮阔的力量争取持久和平、人民民主，而企图奴役和剥削全世界人民的美国统治集团竟不择手段来破坏和平、追求战争；在国内更穷凶极恶地制定种种法律来镇压人民，对您和您的战友以及千千万万和平拥护者采取人身迫害。这种卑鄙的行为，已激动了全世界爱好和平人民的愤怒，全世界爱好和平的人民将以争取和平的更大胜利来支持您们的正义斗争。请接受我们的慰问和敬意。”
    在给艾森豪威尔抗议美国政府迫害丹尼斯和其他民主人士的电文说：第二次世界大战是以彻底击溃德国法西斯制度而告终结，但是今天在你领导下的美国却重新踏上了希特勒走过的道路。美国的和平拥护者、进步人士、工人、劳动人民、科学工作者的民主权利横遭剥夺，许多人受到残暴无理的迫害。我们中国科学工作者向你提出最严重的抗议。我们要求美国政府立即停止对美国人民进行政治和思想的迫害，立即释放正在地牢式的监狱里度过他的五十寿辰的美国共产党总书记尤金·丹尼斯和所有其他受到迫害的政治犯。
    在电文上签名的有：郭沫若、李四光、梁希、陈伯达、张稼夫、陶孟和、竺可桢、吴有训、曾昭抡、丁西林、华罗庚、江泽涵、段学复、饶毓泰、叶企孙、周培源、赵忠尧、严济慈、钱三强、钱伟长、庄长恭、赵承嘏、侯德榜、黄子卿、涂长望、谢家荣、钱崇澍、张景钺、戴芳澜、刘崇乐、刘仙洲、茅以升、李宗恩、锺惠澜、张孝骞、金岳霖、冯友兰、马寅初、周鲠生、陈翰笙、陈岱孙、陈垣、裴文中、罗常培、费孝通、潘光旦。
