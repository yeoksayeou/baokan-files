Title: Soviet Swimming Team Arrives in Beijing and Departs for Guangzhou
Author:
Date: 1954-08-24
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, August 22nd: The Soviet swimming team, comprising fifteen members and led by N.N. Bugrov, Deputy Director of the International Liaison Department of the Soviet Council of Ministers' Committee on Physical Culture and Sports, arrived in Beijing by plane on the morning of August 22nd at the invitation of the Physical Culture and Sports Commission of the Central People's Government of China for a visit and friendly competitions.

Those who welcomed the team at the airport included: Zhang Zhihuai, Deputy Director of the Cadre Training Department of the Physical Culture and Sports Commission of the Central People's Government; Zhu Debao, Deputy Director of the Sports Competition Department; Li Zhanwu, Deputy Secretary-General of the Sino-Soviet Friendship Association; Ma Jianxing, Director of the Office of the Physical Culture and Sports Commission of the Beijing Municipal People's Government; and over fifty representatives from the sports community of the capital.

The Soviet swimming team visiting China includes national instructors, Honored Masters of Sports, Masters of Sports, and outstanding young athletes. They will participate in the 1954 National Swimming Competition in Guangzhou for friendly competitions and visit Shanghai and other places.

The Physical Culture and Sports Commission of the Central People's Government hosted a banquet on the evening of August 22nd to welcome the Soviet swimming team.

Xinhua News Agency, August 23rd: The Soviet swimming team, which arrived in China on August 22nd, left Beijing by plane on the morning of August 23rd, led by N.N. Bugrov, Deputy Director of the International Liaison Department of the Soviet Council of Ministers' Committee on Physical Culture and Sports, to participate in the 1954 National Swimming Competition in Guangzhou for friendly competitions.

Those who saw the team off at the airport included Zhang Zhihuai, Deputy Director of the Cadre Training Department of the Physical Culture and Sports Commission of the Central People's Government, and over twenty athlete representatives from the capital.

### 苏联游泳队到北京去广州

1954-08-24
第1版()
专栏：

    苏联游泳队到北京去广州
    【新华社二十二日讯】　应我中央人民政府体育运动委员会邀请来我国访问和作友谊比赛的苏联游泳队共十五人，由苏联部长会议体育运动委员会国际联络部副部长尼·尼·布格罗夫率领，在二十二日上午乘飞机到达北京。
    到机场欢迎的，有：中央人民政府体育运动委员会干部训练司副司长张之槐、运动竞赛司副司长朱德宝、中苏友好协会副总干事李沾吾、北京市人民政府体育运动委员会办公室主任马健行和首都体育界代表等共五十多人。
    这次来我国访问的苏联游泳队，其中有国家指导、功勋运动员、运动健将和青年优秀运动员。他们将到广州参加一九五四年全国游泳竞赛大会作友谊比赛，并到上海等地访问。
    中央人民政府体育运动委员会在二十二日晚设宴欢迎苏联游泳队。
    【新华社二十三日讯】　二十二日来我国访问的苏联游泳队，在苏联部长会议体育运动委员会国际联络部副部长尼·尼·布格罗夫率领下，已在二十三日上午乘飞机离京去广州，参加我国一九五四年全国游泳竞赛大会作友谊比赛。
    到机场欢送的，有中央人民政府体育运动委员会干部训练司副司长张之槐和首都运动员代表等共二十多人。
