Title: Head of German Cooperative Delegation Returns Home; Delegation Representatives from Various Countries Leave Beijing to Visit Shanghai
Author:
Date: 1954-08-03
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency, 28th: The head of the German Democratic Republic Cooperative Delegation, Mr. Loechert, returned to his country by plane from Beijing on the morning of the 28th. Cheng Zihua and Liang Yao, members of the Board of Directors of the All-China Federation of Supply and Marketing Cooperatives, and others went to the airport to see him off. Personnel from the Embassy of the German Democratic Republic in China were also present at the send-off.

The cooperative delegations from the Soviet Union and various People's Democratic countries, accompanied by Meng Yongqian, member of the Board of Directors of the All-China Federation of Supply and Marketing Cooperatives, left Beijing by train on the afternoon of the 28th to visit Shanghai. Zhang Qilong and Liang Yao, members of the Board of Directors of the All-China Federation of Supply and Marketing Cooperatives, and others went to the station to see them off. Personnel from the Romanian Embassy and the Mongolian Embassy also went to the station to see them off.

During their stay in Beijing, the cooperative delegations from various countries not only attended the First Representative Congress of the All-China Cooperatives and held discussions with the All-China Federation of Supply and Marketing Cooperatives, but also extensively visited various grassroots cooperatives. These cooperatives included: urban consumer cooperatives, factory employee consumer cooperatives, suburban supply and marketing cooperatives, and production cooperatives engaged in trades such as needlepoint embroidery, toys, hardware, carved lacquerware, cotton weaving, ivory carving, and carpets. They gained a further understanding of the Chinese cooperative movement, which will greatly help promote friendship between the cooperatives of China and fraternal countries.

### 德国合作社代表团团长返国  各国代表团离京去上海参观

1954-08-03
第4版()
专栏：

    德国合作社代表团团长返国
    各国代表团离京去上海参观
    【新华社二十八日讯】德意志民主共和国合作社代表团团长卢赫特在二十八日晨乘飞机离京回国。中华全国供销合作总社理事会理事程子华、梁耀等到机场欢送。欢送的还有德意志民主共和国驻华使馆人员。
    苏联和各人民民主国家合作社代表团在中华全国供销合作总社理事会理事孟用潜的陪同下，在二十八日下午乘火车离京赴上海参观。前往车站欢送的有中华全国供销合作总社理事会理事张启龙、梁耀等。罗马尼亚大使馆和蒙古大使馆人员也到车站欢送。
    各国合作社代表团这次在京除参加了中华全国合作社第一次代表大会和同中华全国供销合作总社举行座谈外，并曾广泛地访问了各种基层合作社。这些合作社包括：市区消费合作社、工厂职工消费合作社、郊区供销合作社和挑补花、玩具、五金、雕漆、棉织、象牙雕刻、地毯等业的生产合作社。他们对中国合作社运动有了进一步地了解，这对促进中国和各兄弟国家合作社间的友谊将有很大帮助。
