Title: Nehru Opposes Convening a Meeting of Colombo Conference Countries, Points Out That India, Indonesia, and Burma Have Clearly Expressed Opposition to the Southeast Asia Treaty Organization
Author:
Date: 1954-08-13
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, 10th Dispatch from New Delhi: An Indian government spokesperson said on the 9th that Prime Minister Nehru had informed the Prime Minister of Ceylon, Kotelawala, of his opposition to convening any meeting of the Colombo Conference countries to discuss the proposed Southeast Asia Treaty Organization. Nehru believes that there is no reason to convene such a meeting at this stage, because the majority of the Colombo Conference countries, namely India, Indonesia, and Burma, have clearly expressed opposition to the Southeast Asia Treaty Organization.

### 尼赫鲁反对召开科伦坡会议国家的会议  指出印度、印尼和缅甸都已明白表示反对东南亚公约组织

1954-08-13
第4版()
专栏：

　　尼赫鲁反对召开科伦坡会议国家的会议
    指出印度、印尼和缅甸都已明白表示反对东南亚公约组织
    【新华社十日讯】新德里消息：印度政府发言人九日说，尼赫鲁总理已通知锡兰总理科特拉瓦拉表示反对召开任何科伦坡会议国家的会议来讨论拟议中的东南亚公约组织。尼赫鲁认为，在现阶段召开这种会议是没有任何理由的，因为科伦坡会议国家的大多数，即印度、印尼和缅甸都已明白表示反对东南亚公约组织。
