Title: Wuhan People's Representatives Send Telegram to the CCP Central Committee and the Government Council Thanking the Party, Government, and People of the Whole Country for Supporting Wuhan's Flood Control Efforts
Author: 
Date: 1954-08-21
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, Wuhan, August 20th: The entire body of representatives at the First Session of the First People's Congress of Wuhan City unanimously passed a telegram to the CCP Central Committee, the Central People's Government Government Council, and to be forwarded to all provinces, cities, and the people of the entire nation. The telegram is as follows:

To the CCP Central Committee, the Central People's Government Government Council, and to be forwarded to all provinces, cities, and the people of the entire nation:

    We have received the leadership and care of the CCP Central Committee and the Central People's Government, and the concern and support of all provinces, cities, and the people of the entire nation. This has given us direction and strength in the struggle against the flood, and has enabled us to achieve a decisive victory in the first stage of the fifty-five-day struggle, laying the foundation for defeating the flood in the future. Our city's people are filled with confidence and will fight with even more heroic and resourceful strength than they have already shown in the past struggle, to win the final victory and ensure the safety of Wuhan and its people!
               First Session of the First People's Congress of Wuhan City August 20th

### 武汉市全体人民代表电中共中央和政务院  感谢党、政府和全国人民支持武汉防汛斗争

1954-08-21
第1版()
专栏：

    武汉市全体人民代表电中共中央和政务院
    感谢党、政府和全国人民支持武汉防汛斗争
    【新华社武汉二十日电】武汉市第一届人民代表大会第一次会议全体代表一致通过了给中共中央、中央人民政府政务院并转各省各市和全国人民的电文。电文如下：中共中央、中央人民政府政务院并转各省各市和全国人民：
    我们得到了中共中央、中央人民政府的领导和关怀，得到了全国各省各市及全国人民的关心与支持，这使我们在与洪水斗争中获得了方向与力量，使我们在五十五天斗争中，取得了第一阶段的带有决定性的胜利，为今后战胜洪水打下了基础。我们全市人民正充满着信心，以比过去斗争中已经表现出来的更加英勇、机智的力量，为赢得最后胜利、确保武汉市及其人民的安全而奋斗！
               武汉市第一届人民代表大会第一次会议  八月二十日
