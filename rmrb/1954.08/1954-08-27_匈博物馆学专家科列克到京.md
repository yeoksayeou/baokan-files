Title: Hungarian Museology Expert Kolek Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1954-08-27
Page: 4

    Hungarian Museology Expert Kolek Arrives in Beijing
    [Xinhua News Agency, 25th] József Kolek, a Hungarian museology expert invited to visit China under the Sino-Hungarian Cultural Cooperation Agreement, arrived in Beijing on August 25th. Kolek, who is the Director of the Museum Department of the Hungarian Ministry of Culture, will visit China for six weeks to learn about museology, archaeology, and folk art in our country.

### 匈博物馆学专家科列克到京

1954-08-27
第4版()
专栏：

    匈博物馆学专家科列克到京
   【新华社二十五日讯】根据中匈文化合作协定应邀来我国访问的匈牙利博物馆学专家约瑟夫·科列克在八月二十五日到达北京。科列克为匈牙利文化部博物馆司的处长，将在华访问六周，了解我国博物馆学、考古学及民间艺术等。
