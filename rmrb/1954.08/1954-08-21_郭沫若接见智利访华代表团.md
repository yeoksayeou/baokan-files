Title: Guo Moruo Receives Chilean Delegation

Author:

Date: 1954-08-21
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, August 20th: Guo Moruo, Vice Chairman of the National Committee of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference, received the Chilean delegation headed by Salvador Allende, Vice President of the Chilean Senate and Chairman of the Popular Front, on the morning of August 20th. Chilean painter Venturelli and his wife also attended the reception. Those accompanying the reception included Chu Tunan, President of the Chinese People's Association for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries, Chen Zhongjing, Secretary-General, and Xin Zhichao, Deputy Secretary-General of the National Committee of the CPPCC.

### 郭沫若接见智利访华代表团

1954-08-21
第1版()
专栏：

    郭沫若接见智利访华代表团
    【新华社二十日讯】中国人民政治协商会议全国委员会副主席郭沫若在二十日上午接见以智利参议院副议长、人民战线主席萨尔瓦多·阿伦德为首的智利访华代表团。智利画家万徒勒里和他的夫人也参加了此次接见。陪同接见的有：中国人民对外文化协会会长楚图南、秘书长陈忠经，和政协全国委员会副秘书长辛志超。
