Title: Representatives from 26 Countries Including the Soviet Union Arrive in Beijing to Attend the Beijing Meeting of the World Federation of Democratic Youth Council
Author:
Date: 1954-08-08
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency Dispatch: Ninety youth representatives and staff members from twenty-four countries, who are here to attend the Beijing meeting of the World Federation of Democratic Youth Council, arrived in Beijing on the 6th by international train. They came from the following countries: South Africa, Algeria, Germany, Belgium, Brazil, Canada, North Korea, Denmark, Spain, United States, France, United Kingdom, Greece, Hungary, India, Iceland, Israel, Italy, Luxembourg, Martinique, Pakistan, Trinidad, Soviet Union, Vietnam, etc. Among them are: Satoun Mustapha, head of the Algerian delegation (Algerian Democratic Youth League); Werner Lamberz, head of the German delegation (Secretary of the Central Committee of the Free German Youth League); Stanley Levenson, head of the British delegation (Member of the World Federation of Democratic Youth Council, Member of the Executive Committee of the British Communist Youth League, Secretary of the British Youth Festival Committee); Léon Claude Rollin, head of the French delegation (Secretary of the Republican Youth Union of France); among them are Martinique youth representative Auguste Fleron and South African youth representative Albert Sachs; and Lopez Raimundo, a distinguished anti-fascist fighter from Spain who had just been released from prison after three years and is a specially invited representative.
    Romanovsky, head of the Soviet delegation, has also arrived in Beijing.
    At the Beijing Railway Station, more than four hundred young men and women students in beautiful clothes and more than one hundred Young Pioneers with flowers in their hands, as well as many heads of youth organizations, warmly welcomed the youth representatives from many countries who came to attend the meeting.
    Among the heads of youth organizations who welcomed them at the station were: Liu Daosheng, Qian Sanqiang, and Qu Tangliang, vice-chairmen of the All-China Federation of Democratic Youth; Liu Kun, representative of the China New Democratic Youth League; Zhang Wenhao, vice-chairman of the All-China Students' Federation; Guan Shixiong, vice-chairman of the Beijing Federation of Democratic Youth; and Xu Naiming, chairman of the Beijing Students' Federation.
    Denis, Secretary-General of the World Federation of Democratic Youth, and many youth representatives from various countries who had arrived earlier in Beijing also went to the station to welcome them.
    [Xinhua News Agency, 6th] Peruzzi, head of the Italian delegation to the Beijing meeting of the World Federation of Democratic Youth Council, Yadamasuren, head of the Mongolian delegation, and one member, Rudolf Boner, head of the Swiss delegation, and one member, and one Canadian person arrived in Beijing by plane on the afternoon of the 6th.
    Representing the welcoming committee at the airport were: representatives from the All-China Federation of Democratic Youth, the Central Committee of the China New Democratic Youth League, the All–China Student Federation, and the Beijing Federation of Democratic Youth.

### 参加世界民主青联理事会北京会议  苏联等二十六个国家的代表到达北京

1954-08-08
第4版()
专栏：

    参加世界民主青联理事会北京会议
    苏联等二十六个国家的代表到达北京
    据新华社讯：前来参加世界民主青年联盟理事会北京会议的二十四个国家的青年代表和工作人员共九十人，在六日下午乘国际列车到达北京。他们来自下列各国：南非、阿尔及利亚、德国、比利时、巴西、加拿大、朝鲜、丹麦、西班牙、美国、法国、英国、希腊、匈牙利、印度、冰岛、以色列、意大利、卢森堡、马提尼克、巴基斯坦、特立尼达、苏联、越南等。在他们中间有：阿尔及利亚代表团团长萨敦·穆斯塔法（阿尔及利亚民主青年联盟）、德国代表团团长韦尔纳·朗伯兹（自由德国青年联盟中央委员会书记）、英国代表团团长斯坦莱·勒文逊（世界民主青年联盟理事会理事、英国共产主义青年团执行委员会委员、英国青年联欢节委员会书记）、法国代表团团长利昂·克劳德·罗兰奇（法兰西共和青年联盟书记）；在他们中间有马提尼克青年代表奥古斯特·弗伦、南非青年代表艾伯特·萨克斯；还有曾被监禁三年刚从监牢出来的西班牙的杰出的反法西斯战士、特邀代表洛佩兹·雷蒙多。
    苏联代表团团长罗马诺夫斯基也已到达北京。
    在北京车站上，四百多个穿着漂亮衣服的男女青年学生和一百多个手拿着鲜花的少年先锋队队员以及青年团体的许多负责人，热烈欢迎前来参加这次会议的许多国家的青年代表。
    在车站欢迎的各青年团体的负责人中间有：中华全国民主青年联合会副主席刘导生、钱三强、区棠亮，中国新民主主义青年团代表刘昆，中华全国学生联合会副主席张文浩，北京市民主青年联合会副主席关世雄和北京市学生联合会主席徐乃明等。
    世界民主青年联盟总书记德尼和许多先期抵京的各国青年代表也去车站欢迎。
    【新华社六日讯】参加世界民主青年联盟理事会北京会议的意大利代表团团长佩鲁齐，蒙古代表团团长雅达姆苏伦和团员一人，瑞士代表团团长鲁道夫·邦特尔和团员一人以及加拿大代表一人，在六日下午乘飞机到达北京。
    前往机场欢迎的有：中华全国民主青年联合会、中国新民主主义青年团中央委员会、中华全国学生联合会和北京市民主青年联合会等团体的代表。
