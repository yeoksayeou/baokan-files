Title: Vice Premier Pham Van Dong of Vietnam Visits China; Premier Zhou Enlai and Others Welcome Him at Beijing Airport
Author:
Date: 1954-08-03
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, August 2nd:
    Vice Premier and Acting Minister of Foreign Affairs of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam, Pham Van Dong, arrived in Beijing by plane at 3:53 PM on the afternoon of the 2nd, upon the invitation of the Central People's Government of China.

    Accompanying him were Phan Anh and Tran Cong Tuong, representatives of the delegation of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam to the Geneva Conference.

    Those who went to the airport to welcome him included: Zhou Enlai, Premier of the State Council of the Central People's Government; Vice Premiers Dong Biwu, Guo Moruo, Huang Yanpei, and Deng Xiaoping; Chen Shutong, Vice Chairman of the National Committee of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference; Xi Zhongxun, Secretary-General of the State Council of the Central People's Government; Zeng Shan, Teng Daiyuan, Chen Shaoxian, Wang Kunlun, Luo Longji, Shao Lizi, and Huang Shaohong, members of the Government Administration Council; Shen Yanbing, Liang Xi, Ma Yinchu, Huang Qixiang, Jiang Nanxiang, and Liao Chengzhi, Standing Committee members of the National Committee of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference; Luo Ruiqing, Deputy Director of the Political and Legal Committee of the State Council; Ye Jizhuang, Deputy Director of the Financial and Economic Committee; Wang Jiaxiang, Zhang Hanfu, and Wu Xiuquan, Vice Ministers of Foreign Affairs; Zhang Zongxun, Deputy Chief of the General Staff of the People's Revolutionary Military Commission of the Central People's Government; Xiao Hua and Gan Siqi, Deputy Directors of the General Political Department; Zhang Youyu and Wu Han, Deputy Mayors of the Beijing Municipal People's Government; Xu Teli, member of the Central Committee of the Chinese Communist Party; Yang Shangkun, Director of the General Office; Cai Tingkai, Standing Committee member of the Central Committee of the Revolutionary Committee of the Chinese Kuomintang; Zhang Bojun, Vice Chairman of the Central Committee of the China Democratic League; Zhang Naiqi, Deputy Director of the General Committee of the China Democratic National Construction Association; Xu Guangping, Secretary-General of the Central Council of the China Association for Promoting Democracy; Peng Zemin, Vice Chairman of the Central Committee of the Chinese Peasants' and Workers' Democratic Party; Yan Xichun, Standing Committee member of the Central Committee of the China Zhi Gong Party; Xu Deheng, Chairman of the Central Committee of the Jiusan Society; Lin Yun, Director of the Beijing Office of the Taiwan Democratic Self-Government League; Liu Ningyi, Vice Chairman of the All-China Federation of Trade Unions; Li Dequan, Vice Chairman of the All-China Democratic Women's Federation; Liu Daosheng, Vice Chairman of the All-China Youth Federation; Zhou Yang, Vice Chairman of the All-China Federation of Literary and Art Circles; Chu Tunan, President of the Chinese People's Association for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries; Liu Guanyi, Secretary-General of the Chinese People's Committee for World Peace; Ji Chaoding, Secretary-General of the China Council for the Promotion of International Trade; and more than 1,500 representatives from all walks of life in Beijing.

    Zhou Liang, Charge d'Affaires ad interim of the Embassy of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam in China, and embassy staff members also went to the airport to welcome him.

    Diplomatic envoys from various countries stationed in China who went to the airport to welcome him included: U Ohn Maung, Ambassador of Burma; N. Raghavan, Ambassador of India; Teodor Rudenco, Ambassador of Romania; Moekarto Notowidigdo, Ambassador of Indonesia; Johannes König, Ambassador of the German Democratic Republic; Choi Il, Ambassador of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea; A. Gregerssen, Minister of Denmark; Vasikov, Charge d'Affaires ad interim of the Soviet Union; J. Šmíd, Charge d'Affaires ad interim of Czechoslovakia; Csatornay, Charge d'Affaires ad interim of Hungary; Grigorov, Charge d'Affaires ad interim of Bulgaria; Lubsanchultem, Charge d'Affaires ad interim of Mongolia; Gula, Charge d'Affaires ad interim of Poland; Younas, Third Secretary of the Embassy of Pakistan in China, as well as diplomatic officials from various countries' embassies in China.

    The national flags of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam and the People's Republic of China were flying at the airport.

    After Vice Premier Pham Van Dong alighted from the plane, he first shook hands with Premier Zhou Enlai and others. At this time, the band played the national anthems of Vietnam and China. Then, Vice Premier Pham Van Dong reviewed the guard of honor at the airport, and accepted flowers presented by members of the Beijing Young Pioneers, and then delivered a speech to the welcoming crowd (full text to be published separately). His speech was warmly welcomed.

### 范文同副总理到我国访问  周恩来总理等到北京机场欢迎

1954-08-03
第1版()
专栏：

    范文同副总理到我国访问
    周恩来总理等到北京机场欢迎
    【新华社二日讯】越南民主共和国副总理兼代理外交部部长范文同，应我中央人民政府的邀请，在二日下午三时五十三分乘飞机抵达北京。
    随同前来的，有越南民主共和国出席日内瓦会议代表团代表潘英和陈公祥。
    前往机场欢迎的有：中央人民政府政务院总理周恩来，副总理董必武、郭沫若、黄炎培、邓小平，中国人民政治协商会议全国委员会副主席陈叔通，中央人民政府政务院秘书长习仲勋，政务委员曾山、滕代远、陈劭先、王昆仑、罗隆基、邵力子、黄绍竑，中国人民政治协商会议全国委员会常务委员沈雁冰、梁希、马寅初、黄琪翔、蒋南翔、廖承志，政务院政治法律委员会副主任罗瑞卿，财政经济委员会副主任叶季壮，外交部副部长王稼祥、章汉夫、伍修权，中央人民政府人民革命军事委员会副总参谋长张宗逊，总政治部副主任萧华、甘泗淇，北京市人民政府副市长张友渔、吴晗，中国共产党中央委员会委员徐特立，办公厅主任杨尚昆，中国国民党革命委员会中央常务委员会委员蔡廷锴，中国民主同盟中央委员会副主席章伯钧，中国民主建国会总会委员会副主任委员章乃器，中国民主促进会中央理事会秘书长许广平，中国农工民主党中央委员会副主席彭泽民，中国致公党中央常务委员会委员严希纯，九三学社中央委员会主席许德珩，台湾民主自治同盟驻京办事处主任林云，中华全国总工会副主席刘宁一，中华全国民主妇女联合会副主席李德全，中华全国民主青年联合会副主席刘导生，中国文学艺术界联合会副主席周扬，中国人民对外文化协会会长楚图南，中国人民保卫世界和平委员会秘书长刘贯一，中国国际贸易促进委员会秘书长冀朝鼎，以及北京市各界人民代表等共一千五百多人。
    越南民主共和国驻我国大使馆临时代办周亮和使馆人员也到飞机场欢迎。
    前往机场欢迎的各国驻我国外交使节，有：缅甸大使吴拉茂、印度大使赖嘉文、罗马尼亚大使郭佐文、印尼大使莫诺努图、德意志民主共和国大使柯尼希、朝鲜民主主义人民共和国大使崔一、丹麦公使格瑞杰生、苏联临时代办华司考、捷克斯洛伐克临时代办石密茨、匈牙利临时代办察托尔道伊、保加利亚临时代办格利郭洛夫、蒙古临时代办罗布桑楚勒图姆、波兰临时代办古拉、巴基斯坦驻华大使馆三等秘书尤纳斯，以及各国驻我国使馆的外交官员。
    机场上悬有越南民主共和国和中华人民共和国两国国旗。
    范文同副总理下机后，首先和周恩来总理等一一握手。这时，乐队高奏越中两国国歌。接着，范文同副总理在机场上检阅了仪仗队，并接受了北京市少年先锋队队员的献花，然后向欢迎群众致词（全文另发）。他的讲话，受到了热烈的欢迎。
