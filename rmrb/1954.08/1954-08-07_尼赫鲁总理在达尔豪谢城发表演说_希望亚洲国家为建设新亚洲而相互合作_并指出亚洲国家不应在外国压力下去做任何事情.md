Title: Prime Minister Nehru Delivers Speech in Dalhousie, Hoping Asian Countries Will Cooperate to Build a New Asia, and Pointing Out That Asian Countries Should Not Do Anything Under Foreign Pressure
Author:
Date: 1954-08-07
Page: 4

(Xinhua News Agency, 6th) According to news from the Indian Information Service: Indian Prime Minister Nehru said in a speech in Dalhousie, East Punjab Province on the 4th, that Asian countries should not be forced to do anything under any foreign pressure and foreign influence. Nehru emphasized: "I hope that Asian countries will cooperate with each other in building a new Asia."

### 尼赫鲁总理在达尔豪谢城发表演说  希望亚洲国家为建设新亚洲而相互合作  并指出亚洲国家不应在外国压力下去做任何事情

1954-08-07
第4版()
专栏：

    尼赫鲁总理在达尔豪谢城发表演说
    希望亚洲国家为建设新亚洲而相互合作
    并指出亚洲国家不应在外国压力下去做任何事情
    【新华社六日讯】据印度新闻处消息：印度总理尼赫鲁四日在东旁遮普省的达尔豪谢城发表演说时说，亚洲国家不应该被迫在任何外国压力和外国影响下去做任何事情。尼赫鲁强调指出：“我希望亚洲国家在建设新亚洲的事务中相互合作。”
