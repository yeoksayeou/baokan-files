Title: Czechoslovak Chinese Literature Research Expert Dr. Krebsova Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1954-08-02
Page: 4

    Czechoslovak Chinese Literature Research Expert
    Dr. Krebsova Arrives in Beijing
    (Xinhua News Agency, 29th) Berta Krebsova, lecturer at Charles University in Prague, Czechoslovakia, and expert in Chinese literature research, arrived in Beijing by plane on July 29th for a visit to China under the Sino-Czech Cultural Cooperation Agreement. Krebsova has translated and introduced the works of Chinese writers Lu Xun and Guo Moruo. She will continue to collect and study information about the life and works of Lu Xun and Guo Moruo in China.
    Professor Wu Zuxiang, Director of the Chinese Language and Literature Teaching and Research Section of Peking University, writers Ai Wu, Yang Shuo, and representatives of the Bureau for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries of the Cultural and Educational Committee of the Central People's Government went to the airport to welcome her. First Secretary Hedlicka and his wife of the Czechoslovak Embassy in China also went to welcome her.

### 捷克斯洛伐克中国文学研究专家  克列布索娃博士到达北京

1954-08-02
第4版()
专栏：

    捷克斯洛伐克中国文学研究专家
    克列布索娃博士到达北京
    【新华社二十九日讯】根据中捷文化合作协定来中国访问的捷克斯洛伐克布拉格查理士大学讲师、中国文学研究专家别尔塔·克列布索娃博士，七月二十九日乘机到达北京。克列布索娃曾翻译介绍我国文学家鲁迅和郭沫若的著作，她将在中国继续搜集并研究有关鲁迅和郭沫若的生平及创作。
    前往机场欢迎的有北京大学中国语文系教研室主任吴组湘教授，作家艾芜、杨朔，和中央人民政府政务院文化教育委员会对外文化联络事务局代表。捷克斯洛伐克驻我国大使馆一等秘书赫德利奇卡及其夫人也前往欢迎。
