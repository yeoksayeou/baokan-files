Title: Indian "New National Newspaper" Publishes Editorial Pointing Out That All Asian People Will Support China's Efforts to Liberate Taiwan
Author:
Date: 1954-08-22
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, New Delhi, August 21st: An editorial in the August 17th issue of the Indian "New National Newspaper," commenting on the Chinese people's determination to liberate Taiwan, wrote: Taiwan is an integral part of China, and as long as the American enslavement policy executed by the Chiang Kai-shek puppet exists in Taiwan, China's peace and happiness will inevitably be hindered. The editorial said that the Chinese People's Government cannot tolerate any foreign interference in this struggle, just as the Indian people cannot tolerate any foreign interference in their struggle to incorporate foreign colonies on Indian territory into India. China has every right to wage such a struggle. The editorial concluded: "Without a doubt, all Asian people will support China's efforts to liberate Taiwan."

### 印度“新国家报”发表社论指出  所有亚洲人民将支持中国解放台湾的努力

1954-08-22
第4版()
专栏：

    印度“新国家报”发表社论指出
    所有亚洲人民将支持中国解放台湾的努力
    【新华社新德里二十一日电】八月十七日的印度“新国家报”社论在评论中国人民解放台湾的决心时写道：台湾是中国整体的一部分，只要是由蒋介石傀儡执行的美国奴役政策在台湾存在一天，中国的和平与幸福就必然会受到阻碍。社论说，中国人民政府不能容忍任何外国干涉这个斗争，犹如印度人民不能容忍任何外国干涉他们争取把印度国土上的外国殖民地归并印度的斗争一样。中国完全有权利进行这样的斗争。社论最后说：“毫无疑问，所有亚洲人民将支持中国解放台湾的努力。”
