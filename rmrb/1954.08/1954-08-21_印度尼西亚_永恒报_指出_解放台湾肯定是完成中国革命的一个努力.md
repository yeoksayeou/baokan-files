Title: Indonesian "Abadi" Newspaper Points Out that Liberating Taiwan is Definitely an Effort to Complete the Chinese Revolution
Author:
Date: 1954-08-21
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Jakarta, August 20th: The Indonesian Masjumi Party's newspaper "Abadi" published a commentary on August 19th regarding the issue of liberating Taiwan mentioned in Premier Zhou Enlai's diplomatic report. "Abadi" stated that, from the perspective of the People's Republic of China, liberating Taiwan is definitely an effort to complete the Chinese revolution. It is understandable that the People's Republic of China includes this in its current plans.

"Abadi," after citing the United States' protection of Chiang Kai-shek and its non-recognition of the People's Republic of China, pointed out: "All of this is the source of tension." "Abadi" called for efforts to maintain peace and prevent the world from falling into the abyss of war.

### 印度尼西亚“永恒报”指出  解放台湾肯定是完成中国革命的一个努力

1954-08-21
第4版()
专栏：

    印度尼西亚“永恒报”指出
    解放台湾肯定是完成中国革命的一个努力
    【新华社雅加达二十日电】印度尼西亚马斯友美党的报纸“永恒报”十九日就周恩来总理的外交报告中所提到的解放台湾问题发表评论。“永恒报”说，从中华人民共和国的立场来说，解放台湾肯定是完成中国革命的一个努力。中华人民共和国把这一点列在它的当前计划中，这是可以理解的。
    “永恒报”在举出美国庇护蒋介石和不承认中华人民共和国等行为以后指出：“这一切是紧张局势的根源”。“永恒报”要求努力维护和平，使世界不要坠入战争的深渊。
