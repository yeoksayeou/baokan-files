Title: Beijing Municipal People's Congress Opens

Author:
Date: 1954-08-18
Page: 1

    Beijing Municipal People's Congress Opens
    [Our Newspaper] The first session of the First Beijing Municipal People's Congress opened on the 17th at Zhongshan Hall in Zhongshan Park.
    The First Beijing Municipal People's Congress has a total of 564 delegates. Among them, 554 were elected by the people's congresses of various districts, and 10 were elected by the People's Liberation Army units stationed in Beijing.
    The important agenda of this meeting is to elect representatives to attend the National People's Congress, discuss the draft Constitution of the People's Republic of China. The meeting will also hear and review the work report of the Beijing Municipal People's Government, determine future work tasks, and review and approve the report on the municipal government's 1953 fiscal revenue and expenditure final accounts and the 1954 fiscal revenue and expenditure budget.
    Before the opening of the congress, all the delegates held a preparatory meeting at 9:00 a.m. to jointly discuss and determine the list of the presidium of the congress and the agenda, which were submitted to the congress for approval. The meeting also approved the lists of the Credentials Committee, the Proposals Examination Committee, and the Government Work Report Examination Committee. The preparatory work of the plenary session of delegates with such full consultations will be able to better guide the progress of the meeting.
    At 3:00 p.m., the meeting was declared open by Mayor Peng Zhen after the band played the national anthem. He said in his speech that this municipal people's congress was elected by the people's congresses of various districts through universal suffrage. The Beijing Municipal People's Representatives Conference from August 1949 to June 1954 convened a total of four sessions and twelve meetings. The second session of the First People's Representatives Conference, convened in November 1949, exercised the functions and powers of the People's Congress. The convening of the First People's Congress marks the further perfection of our democratic system. The convening of the People's Congress will further promote people's democracy, consolidate the united front, strengthen the unity of all the people, and further promote the socialist construction and socialist transformation in the capital. After the meeting adopted the list of the presidium of the congress, the agenda, and heard the report of the Credentials Committee on the qualifications of the representatives, Wu Han, a member of the Beijing Municipal Constitution Draft Discussion Committee, made a work report on the publicity and discussion of the draft constitution. Then, Zhang Youyu, Deputy Mayor of Beijing Municipality, made a report on the Beijing Municipality's 1953 fiscal revenue and expenditure final accounts and the 1954 fiscal revenue and expenditure budget. The meeting adjourned at 5:30 p.m. on the 17th. The meeting will continue on the 18th.

### 北京市人民代表大会会议开幕

1954-08-18
第1版()
专栏：

    北京市人民代表大会会议开幕
   【本报讯】北京市第一届人民代表大会第一次会议十七日在中山公园中山堂开幕。
    北京市第一届人民代表大会共有代表五百六十四人。其中，五百五十四人是由各区人民代表大会会议选举的，十人是由人民解放军驻京部队选举的。
    这次会议的重要议程是选举出席全国人民代表大会会议的代表，讨论中华人民共和国宪法草案，会议还将听取和审查北京市人民政府的工作报告，确定今后的工作任务，并审查和批准市人民政府一九五三年度财政收支决算和一九五四年度财政收支预算的报告。
    大会开幕前，全体代表曾在上午九时举行了预备会议，共同商定了大会主席团名单和议事日程，提请大会通过。会议并通过了代表资格审查委员会、提案审查委员会和政府工作报告审查委员会的名单。经过这种充分协商的全体代表参加的预备会议的准备工作，将能更好地领导会议的进行。
    下午三时，会议在乐队奏国歌后，由彭真市长宣布大会开幕。他致词说，这次的市人民代表大会是由普选产生的各区人民代表大会会议所选举的。北京市各界人民代表会议从一九四九年八月到一九五四年六月共召开了四届十二次会议。在一九四九年十一月召开的第二届第一次各界人民代表会议就代行了人民代表大会的职权。第一次人民代表大会会议的召开，标志着我们的民主制度更加完备。人民代表大会会议的召开，将更加发扬人民民主，巩固统一战线，加强全体人民的团结，进一步推动首都的各项社会主义建设事业和社会主义改造事业前进。会议通过了大会主席团名单、议事日程和听取了代表资格审查委员会关于代表资格的审查报告后，由北京市宪法草案讨论委员会委员吴晗作了关于宣传、讨论宪法草案的工作报告。接着，由北京市人民政府副市长张友渔作关于北京市一九五三年度财政收支决算和一九五四年度财政收支预算的报告。会议在十七日下午五时半休会。十八日，会议将继续进行。
