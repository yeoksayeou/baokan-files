Title: Northwest Military Region Elects Delegates to the National People's Congress
Author:
Date: 1954-08-15
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Lanzhou, August 14th: The Northwest Military Region of the Chinese People's Liberation Army held a meeting of military representatives from the 7th to the 11th, discussing the draft constitution and electing delegates to the National People's Congress. A total of 170 representatives attended the meeting. The representatives included leading cadres, heroic model figures from various work posts, and cadres and soldiers from ethnic groups such as Han, Uyghur, Hui, and Kazakh.

At the meeting, Peng Shaohui, Deputy Commander of the Northwest Military Region, delivered a speech on behalf of the presidium of the congress. He pointed out: A meeting of this nature has great significance in the political life of our army, and it is a great victorious achievement obtained through the heroic struggle and sacrifices of our army for more than twenty years. We should elect our own representatives with a high sense of responsibility to the country and the people.

At the meeting, the election committee of the Northwest Military Region gave a report on the election work of the Northwest Military Region troops. Xian Henghan, Director of the Political Department of the Northwest Military Region, entrusted by the election committee of the Northwest Military Region, gave a report on the draft constitution of the People's Republic of China at the meeting. The representatives attending the meeting conducted a serious discussion of the draft constitution.

The congress passed a resolution on supporting the draft constitution of the People's Republic of China. The resolution concluded: We will actively unite and lead all commanders and fighters, exemplarily abide by and implement the laws and decrees of the state, strengthen regular training, accelerate the construction of our army into a modern army, and strive to liberate our territory Taiwan, defend and participate in our socialist construction, and defend peace in the Far East and the world.

The entire representatives attending the meeting elected Zhang Zongxun, Gan Siqi, Liao Hansheng, Tao Zhiyue, and Song Zhongfu (combat hero) as delegates to the National People's Congress by means of a secret ballot. Liao Hansheng and Tao Zhiyue spoke at the meeting. They expressed that they would certainly live up to the trust of all the representatives, ensure that they serve the people wholeheartedly, and hoped that everyone would give strict supervision.

### 西北军区选出全国人民代表大会的代表

1954-08-15
第1版()
专栏：

    西北军区选出全国人民代表大会的代表
      【新华社兰州十四日电】中国人民解放军西北军区在七日到十一日举行军人代表大会会议，讨论宪法草案并选举出席全国人民代表大会会议的代表。出席会议的代表共一百七十名。代表中有领导干部，各种工作岗位上的英雄模范人物和汉族、维吾尔族、回族、哈萨克族等民族的干部和战士。
    会上，西北军区副司令员彭绍辉代表大会主席团致词。他指出：这样性质的会议在我军政治生活中具有重大的意义，是我军二十多年来英勇奋斗、流血牺牲所得来的伟大胜利成果，我们应该以高度为国家、为人民负责的态度，选举出自己的代表。
    在会议上，西北军区选举委员会作了关于西北军区部队选举工作的报告。西北军区政治部主任冼恒汉受西北军区选举委员会的委托，在会上作了关于中华人民共和国宪法草案的报告。到会代表对宪法草案进行了认真的讨论。
    大会通过了关于拥护中华人民共和国宪法草案的决议。决议最后说：我们将积极团结与带动全体指战员，模范地遵守和执行国家的法律和法令，加强正规训练，加速把我军建设成为一支现代化的军队，为解放我国领土台湾，保卫和参加我国社会主义建设，保卫远东与世界和平而奋斗。
    参加会议的全体代表以无记名的投票方式，选出张宗逊、甘泗淇、廖汉生、陶峙岳、宋忠福（战斗英雄）五人为出席全国人民代表大会会议的代表。廖汉生和陶峙岳在会上讲了话。他们表示一定不辜负全体代表的信任，保证全心全意地为人民办事，并希望大家给以严格的监督。
