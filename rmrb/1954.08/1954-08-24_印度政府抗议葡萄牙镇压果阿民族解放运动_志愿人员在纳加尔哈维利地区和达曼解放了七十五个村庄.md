Title: Indian Government Protests Portuguese Suppression of Goan National Liberation Movement, Volunteers Liberate Seventy-Five Villages in Nagar Haveli Area and Daman
Author:
Date: 1954-08-24
Page: 4

    Xinhua News Agency, Delhi, 22nd: TASS Delhi reports: The Indian government sent a note to the Portuguese Legation in Delhi on the 20th, protesting the Portuguese army's shooting on August 16th at people participating in the peaceful national liberation movement at Darikhel Castle (in the Portuguese territory of Goa on the west coast of India), during which one person was killed.
    According to newspaper reports, volunteers have liberated seventy-five villages in the Nagar Haveli area and another Portuguese territory in India, Daman.

### 印度政府抗议葡萄牙镇压果阿民族解放运动  志愿人员在纳加尔哈维利地区和达曼解放了七十五个村庄

1954-08-24
第4版()
专栏：

    印度政府抗议葡萄牙镇压果阿民族解放运动
    志愿人员在纳加尔哈维利地区和达曼解放了七十五个村庄
    【新华社二十二日讯】　塔斯社德里讯：印度政府二十日致葡萄牙驻德里公使馆一件照会，抗议葡萄牙军队在八月十六日开枪射击在达里克尔城堡（在印度西海岸的葡萄牙属地果阿）参加和平民族解放运动的人，其中有一人被打死。
    据报纸报道，在纳加尔哈维利地区以及葡萄牙在印度的另一属地——达曼，志愿人员已解放了七十五个村庄。
