Title: Vice Premier Pham Van Dong's Speech at Beijing Railway Station
Author:
Date: 1954-08-05
Page: 1

Vice Premier Pham Van Dong's Speech at Beijing Railway Station

Dear Premier Zhou, Friends, Comrades:

    During the visit of the delegation of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam to the great China, we have received extremely warm, friendly, and grand welcome and hospitality from the Government and people of the People's Republic of China.

    Dear Premier Zhou and many other Vietnamese friends' congratulations and hopes for the victories that the Vietnamese people have already achieved and will achieve in the struggle for peace, unification, independence, and democracy will spread throughout the North and South of Vietnam, and will powerfully encourage us to strive for new victories under new conditions. We will correctly implement the agreements that have been concluded, resolve the remaining issues, and especially achieve the national unification of Vietnam through general elections, and establish a solid and lasting peace in Indochina.

    As we leave the capital of the People's Republic of China, Beijing, to return home, our hearts are filled with excitement, joy, confidence, and strength. This is because we deeply realize that the friendly unity of 600 million kind friends with us is undoubtedly a great encouragement in our struggle to achieve peace, unification, independence, and democracy for our beloved motherland, Vietnam.

    At this moment of parting, I wish the Chinese people brilliant achievements in the cause of peaceful construction.

    Pay respects to dear Premier Zhou Enlai!

    Pay respects to all friends and comrades!

### 范文同副总理在北京车站上的讲话

1954-08-05
第1版()
专栏：

    范文同副总理在北京车站上的讲话亲爱的周总理、诸位朋友、诸位同志：
    越南民主共和国代表团在应邀访问伟大的中国这段时间中，受到了中华人民共和国政府与人民极其热烈、友爱和隆重的欢迎和招待。
    亲爱的周总理和其他许多越南人民的好友对越南人民在争取和平、统一、独立、民主的斗争中已经获得和即将获得的胜利的祝贺与希望，将传遍越南的北方和南方，将有力地鼓励我们在新的条件下争取新的胜利。我们将正确执行已经缔结的协定，解决各项尚存的问题，尤其是经过普选的途径来实现越南的国家统一，在印度支那确立稳固与持久的和平。
      当此离开中华人民共和国的首都北京起程回国时，我们的心中充满了兴奋、愉快、信心和力量。这是因为我们深深地体会到，六万万亲切的朋友与我们的友好团结，在我们为自己亲爱的祖国——越南获得和平、统一、独立与民主的斗争中，无疑是一个极大的鼓舞。
    在此临别之时，我谨祝中国人民在和平建设事业中获得光辉的成就。
    向亲爱的周恩来总理致敬！
    向诸位朋友，诸位同志致敬！
