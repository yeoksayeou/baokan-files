Title: Switzerland to Appoint a Diplomat to Replace Swiss Member of Neutral Nations Supervisory Commission in Korea
Author:
Date: 1954-08-21
Page: 4

(Xinhua News Agency, Kaesong, August 19th) General Li Sang-cho, senior member of the Korean-Chinese side of the Military Armistice Commission, replied on August 19th to General Gross, the Swiss member of the Neutral Nations Supervisory Commission. General Li Sang-cho stated that the Korean-Chinese side had formally taken note of his letter of August 13th. In his letter, General Gross informed the Korean-Chinese side that the Swiss government was considering appointing a diplomat in October to replace him. The new Swiss member would wear a military uniform but without military insignia.

### 瑞士将任命一位外交官  接替朝鲜中立国监察委员会委员

1954-08-21
第4版()
专栏：

    瑞士将任命一位外交官
    接替朝鲜中立国监察委员会委员
    【新华社开城十九日电】军事停战委员会朝中方面首席委员李相朝将军十九日回信给中立国监察委员会瑞士委员格罗斯将军，表示朝中方面已正式注意到他八月十三日的来信。格罗斯将军在他的信中通知朝中方面，瑞士政府考虑在十月份任命一位外交官来接替他，新的瑞士委员将仅着军服而不佩带军阶徽记。
