Title: Negotiations on the Establishment of an International Supervisory and Control Commission in Indochina The Delegation of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam Arrives in New Delhi Representatives from France, Laos, Cambodia, and Bao Dai Vietnam are also sent.
Author:
Date: 1954-08-05
Page: 1

    Xinhua News Agency, New Delhi, August 4th: At the invitation of the Indian government, the government of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam has dispatched a delegation to New Delhi to discuss the establishment of an International Supervisory and Control Commission in Indochina with representatives from India, Poland, and Canada, who are currently holding a preliminary meeting of the Commission in New Delhi. The delegation of five, led by Chief Representative Hoang Van Hoan, arrived in New Delhi by plane from Geneva at 8:30 PM on the afternoon of August 3rd.

    The delegation of the People's Republic of Poland to the preliminary meeting of the International Supervisory and Control Commission in Indochina, led by Chief Representative P. Ogrodzinski, also arrived on the same plane.

    Those present at the airport to welcome them included K.G. Mathur, Deputy Director of the Protocol Division of the Indian Ministry of External Affairs, Soviet Ambassador to India Menshikov, Chinese Ambassador to India Yuan Zhongxian, and Polish Ambassador to India Grudzinski.

    The governments of France, the Kingdom of Laos, the Kingdom of Cambodia, and Bao Dai Vietnam have also been invited by the Indian government to send representatives to New Delhi to discuss the establishment of the International Supervisory and Control Commission.

### 商谈有关在印度支那建立国际监督和监察委员会的问题  越南民主共和国代表团应邀到达新德里  法国、老挝、柬埔寨和保大越南也派去了代表

1954-08-05
第1版()
专栏：

    商谈有关在印度支那建立国际监督和监察委员会的问题
    越南民主共和国代表团应邀到达新德里
    法国、老挝、柬埔寨和保大越南也派去了代表
   【新华社新德里四日电】越南民主共和国政府应印度政府的邀请派遣代表团前往新德里，以便同正在新德里举行印度支那国际监督和监察委员会预备会议的印度、波兰和加拿大三国代表商谈有关在印度支那建立国际监督和监察委员会的问题。这个代表团一行五人在首席代表黄文欢的率领下从日内瓦乘飞机在三日下午八时三十分到达新德里。
    波兰人民共和国出席印度支那国际监督和监察委员会预备会议的代表团首席代表普·奥格罗京斯基等三人也同机到达。
    到机场欢迎的有印度政府外交部交际司副司长克·格·马图尔，苏联驻印度大使曼希科夫，中华人民共和国驻印度大使袁仲贤，波兰驻印度大使格鲁金斯基等人。
    法国、老挝王国、柬埔寨王国和保大越南的政府也同样受印度政府的邀请，派遣了代表来到新德里，商谈有关建立国际监督和监察委员会的问题。
