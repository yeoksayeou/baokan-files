Title: China Islamic Association Holds Banquet to Celebrate Eid al-Adha
Author:
Date: 1954-08-13
Page: 3

    China Islamic Association Holds Banquet to Celebrate Eid al-Adha

    August 10th was the "Eid al-Adha" festival for ten ethnic groups who believe in Islam: Hui, Uyghur, Kazakh, Uzbek, Tatar, Tajik, Kirgiz, Salar, Dongxiang, and Baoan. The China Islamic Association held a banquet on the 10th to celebrate the festival. Attending the banquet were over a hundred people, including members of the China Islamic Association and the China Hui Nationality Culture Advancement Association in Beijing, students from the Central Institute for Nationalities who believe in Islam, and imams from various mosques in Beijing.

    At the meeting, Da Pongsheng, Deputy Director of the China Islamic Association, discussed the achievements of the Geneva Conference, explaining the great significance of the Indochina armistice for safeguarding world peace, and expressed his happiness for China's contribution to the Geneva Conference and the improvement of its international status. When talking about the aggressive acts of American imperialism, Da Pongsheng, on behalf of the people of all ethnic groups in China who believe in Islam, firmly expressed opposition to American imperialism's interference in China's internal affairs. He said: Taiwan is our territory, and our people must liberate Taiwan, and no other country is allowed to obstruct it. The people of all ethnic groups in China who believe in Islam have the firm confidence to participate in this great just struggle together with the people of the whole country. Finally, on behalf of the people of all ethnic groups in China who believe in Islam, he expressed his earnest concern for the brothers of all countries in the world who believe in Islam and who are deeply oppressed by imperialism, sending them sincere "Selam" (sincerely wishing peace), and hoping that they will achieve a peaceful, free, and happy life soon. (Xinhua News Agency)

### 中国伊斯兰教协会举行宴会庆祝古尔邦节

1954-08-13
第3版()
专栏：

    中国伊斯兰教协会举行宴会庆祝古尔邦节
    八月十日是信仰伊斯兰教的回、维吾尔、哈萨克、乌兹别克、塔塔尔、塔吉克、柯尔克兹、撒拉、东乡、保安十个民族的“古尔邦”节。中国伊斯兰教协会在十日举行宴会庆祝佳节。参加宴会的有中国伊斯兰教协会、中国回民文化协进会在京委员、中央民族学院信仰伊斯兰教的学员和北京市各清真寺的阿訇等共百余人。
    会上，中国伊斯兰教协会副主任达浦生就日内瓦会议的成就，说明印度支那停战对保卫世界和平的重大意义，并为我国对日内瓦会议的贡献和国际地位的提高感到高兴。在讲到美帝国主义的侵略行为时，达浦生代表我国信仰伊斯兰教的各族人民坚决表示反对美国帝国主义干涉我国内政。他说：台湾是我国的领土，我国人民一定要解放台湾，不容许别国阻挠。我国信仰伊斯兰教的各族人民有坚强的信心和全国人民一道参加这一伟大的正义斗争。最后他代表中国信仰伊斯兰教的各族人民，殷切关怀深受帝国主义压迫的世界各国信仰伊斯兰教的兄弟们，向他们致亲切的“色兰”（谨祝平安），并望他们早日获得和平、自由、幸福的生活。  （新华社）
