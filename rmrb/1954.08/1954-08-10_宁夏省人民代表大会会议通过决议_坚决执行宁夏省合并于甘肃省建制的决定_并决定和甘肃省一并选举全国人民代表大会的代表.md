Title: Ningxia Provincial People's Congress Meeting Passes Resolution: Firmly Implement the Decision to Merge Ningxia Province into Gansu Province; Decides to Elect Deputies to the National People's Congress Together with Gansu Province
Author:
Date: 1954-08-10
Page: 3

Xinhua News Agency

The first session of the First People's Congress of Ningxia Province was held from July 23rd to 28th. A total of 127 people's representatives of all ethnic groups attended the meeting.

The meeting discussed and approved the report by Xing Zhaotang, Chairman of the Ningxia Provincial People's Government, on the "Basic Summary of Five Years of Work of the Ningxia Provincial People's Government" and the report by Vice Chairman Sun Diancai on the "Work Situation in the First Half of 1954 and Arrangements for Several Key Tasks Currently." The meeting held that since the liberation of Ningxia Province, under the leadership of the Chinese Communist Party, Chairman Mao, and the higher-level People's Government, the province had correctly implemented the central government's various policies and guidelines, and on the basis of establishing and improving the people's democratic system and completing various social reforms, had rapidly restored and developed the national economy. According to the actual figures of industrial, agricultural, and animal husbandry production development in the first half of 1954 and the estimated figures for the second half of the year, the total value of industrial and agricultural production in the province in 1954 can be increased by 90 percent compared with 1950, the total grain output can be increased by 1.13 times compared with 1949, and the number of various livestock such as horses, cattle, sheep, and camels can be increased by 1.8 times compared with 1949. With the development of production and the rise of the people's economic life, the volume of social commodity circulation has also expanded year by year. The total amount of social commodity circulation in the first half of 1954 increased by 2.24 times compared with the whole year of 1950; in the total amount of social commodity circulation, the commodity amount operated by state-owned commerce and cooperatives accounted for 71.22 percent. On the basis of economic recovery and development, Ningxia Province's cultural, educational, and health undertakings have also made great progress. At present, the number of students in ordinary middle schools and secondary technical schools in the province has increased by 2.67 times compared with 1949, and the number of primary school students has increased by about 60 percent. In addition to the establishment of three people's hospitals in the province, people's health centers and health stations have been generally established in various counties and districts, and maternal and child health stations and midwifery stations have been generally established in various cities, towns, and villages. At present, the number of medical and health workers in the province has increased by 5.3 times compared with 1950, and the number of beds has increased by more than 7 times.

The meeting held that Ningxia Province has made remarkable achievements in implementing the policies of ethnic equality, unity, and freedom of religious belief. The political, economic, and cultural construction undertakings in various ethnic minority areas have been greatly developed, the political consciousness of the people of all ethnic groups has been greatly improved, the ideological remnants of Han chauvinism and local nationalism have been gradually overcome, and the unity of the people of all ethnic groups has been unprecedentedly developed and consolidated; on this basis, the rights of the people of all ethnic minorities to be masters of their own affairs have been fully brought into play. Up to now, a total of more than 2,300 ethnic minority cadres have been trained and promoted in the province, and they are undertaking various tasks in state organs. In the general election movement, all ethnic minorities have elected representatives to the people's congresses at all levels in proportion to their population. Of the more than 9,700 representatives to the grassroots people's congresses in the province, 25 percent are Hui people.

The meeting unanimously passed a resolution supporting the decision of the Central People's Government on abolishing the first-level administrative organs of the greater administrative areas and merging several provinces and municipalities. The resolution requires the firm implementation of the central government's decision to "abolish the establishment of Ningxia Province and merge it with Gansu Province into Gansu Province," and to carry out the abolition and merger work in a planned and step-by-step manner. The meeting also decided that all representatives of the current People's Congress will be considered as representatives attending the meeting of the Gansu Provincial People's Congress and will elect representatives to the National People's Congress together with Gansu Province. The meeting also passed a resolution to continue to carry out the agricultural production movement centered on mutual assistance and cooperation on the basis of comprehensively launching the publicity and discussion of the draft Constitution of the People's Republic of China, actively and steadily develop mutual assistance and cooperation organizations, and do a good job in the current production, summer harvest, unified purchase and marketing, and market management.

### 宁夏省人民代表大会会议通过决议  坚决执行宁夏省合并于甘肃省建制的决定  并决定和甘肃省一并选举全国人民代表大会的代表

1954-08-10
第3版()
专栏：

    宁夏省人民代表大会会议通过决议
    坚决执行宁夏省合并于甘肃省建制的决定
    并决定和甘肃省一并选举全国人民代表大会的代表
    宁夏省第一届第一次人民代表大会会议在七月二十三日到二十八日举行。出席会议的各民族人民代表共一百二十七人。
    会议讨论并通过了宁夏省人民政府主席邢肇棠关于“宁夏省人民政府五年工作基本总结”和副主席孙殿才关于“一九五四年上半年工作情况及当前几项主要工作安排”的报告。会议认为宁夏省解放以来，在中国共产党、毛主席和上级人民政府的领导下，正确贯彻执行了中央的各项政策方针，在建立和健全人民民主制度，完成各项社会改革的基础上，迅速恢复和发展了国民经济。根据一九五四年上半年工、农、牧业生产发展的实际数字和下半年的预计数字计算，一九五四年全省工、农业生产总值可比一九五零年提高百分之九十，粮食总产量可比一九四九年增加一点一三倍，马、牛、羊、骆驼等各种牲畜可比一九四九年增加一点八倍。随着生产的发展和人民经济生活的上升，社会商品流转额也逐年扩大。一九五四年上半年的社会商品流转总额比一九五零年全年增加了二点二四倍；在社会商品流转总额中国营商业和合作社经营的商品额占百分之七十一点二二。在经济恢复和发展的基础上，宁夏省文教卫生事业也有很大发展。现在全省普通中学和中等技术学校的学生数比一九四九年增加了二点六七倍，小学的学生数增加了约百分之六十。全省除建立了三个人民医院外，各县、区还普遍建立了人民卫生院和卫生所，各城镇和乡村也普遍建立了妇幼保健站和接生站。现在全省的医疗卫生工作人员比一九五零年增加了五点三倍，病床增加了七倍多。
    会议认为，宁夏省在贯彻执行民族平等团结和宗教信仰自由的政策方面是有显著成绩的。各少数民族地区的政治、经济和文化建设事业有了很大发展，各族人民的政治觉悟大大提高，大民族主义和地方民族主义的思想残余逐渐得到克服，各族人民的团结得到空前的发展和巩固；在此基础上充分发挥了各少数民族当家作主的权利。到现在为止，全省共培养和提拔了二千三百多名民族干部，他们在国家机关中担负着各种工作。在普选运动中各少数民族都选出了和他们人口比例相适应的各级人民代表大会的代表，全省九千七百多名基层人民代表大会的代表中，有百分之二十五的名额是回族。
    会议一致通过了拥护中央人民政府关于撤销大区一级行政机构和合并若干省、市建制的决定的决议，决议要求坚决执行中央关于“宁夏省建制撤销，与甘肃省合并为甘肃省”的决定，并要有计划、有步骤地作好撤销和合并工作。会议并决定本届人民代表大会全体代表，一律作为出席甘肃省人民代表大会会议的代表，和甘肃省一并选举全国人民代表大会的代表。会议还通过决议，要在全面展开宣传和讨论中华人民共和国宪法草案的基础上，继续开展以互助合作为中心的农业生产运动，积极稳步地发展互助合作组织，作好当前的生产、夏收、统购统销和市场管理工作。　　（新华社）
