Title: Our Great Motherland (Picture)
Author:
Date: 1954-08-20
Page: 1

    Our Great Motherland
    The technicians of the flax group of the Special Product Experiment Farm in Hulan County, Heilongjiang Province, have cultivated 35 varieties of flax based on the theories of Soviet biologist Lysenko and have promoted them to rural areas. These two technicians are inspecting the growth of newly planted flax seedlings.

### 我们伟大的祖国（图片）

1954-08-20
第1版()
专栏：

    我们伟大的祖国
    黑龙江省呼兰县特产试验场亚麻组的技术人员，根据苏联生物学家李森科的理论，培育了三十五种亚麻品种，并已向农村推广。这两位技术员正在检查新种亚麻幼苗的生长情况。
