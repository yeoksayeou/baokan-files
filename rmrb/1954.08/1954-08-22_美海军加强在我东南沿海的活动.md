Title: US Navy Strengthens Activities off China's Southeast Coast
Author: 
Date: 1954-08-22
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, August 21st: The U.S. Navy is planning to intensify its provocative activities off the southeast coast of China.

According to Western news agencies, Vice Admiral Pride, commander of the U.S. Seventh Fleet operating in the Taiwan Strait, received operational command on August 21st from Vice Admiral Phillips, commander of the First Fleet, over a fleet operating in the Philippines region and the South China Sea. This fleet includes aircraft carriers, destroyers, and auxiliary warships.

The Seventh Fleet has already stepped up its activities in the Taiwan Strait. The Seventh Fleet's aircraft carrier "Tarawa" sailed to Keelung, Taiwan, a few days ago. Four destroyers of the Seventh Fleet also sailed to Dachen Island off the coast of Zhejiang on the 19th, which is entrenched by the Chiang Kai-shek bandits. The commander in charge of these warships, Garbett, held secret talks with the Chiang bandit military leaders on the island and inspected the Chiang bandit military equipment.

### 美海军加强在我东南沿海的活动

1954-08-22
第4版()
专栏：

    美海军加强在我东南沿海的活动
    【新华社二十一日讯】美国海军正在策划加强在中国东南沿海的挑衅性活动。
    据西方通讯社报道，在台湾海峡一带活动的美国第七舰队司令普赖德在二十一日从第一舰队司令菲利普斯手中，接收了一支在菲律宾地区和南中国海活动的舰队的作战指挥权，这支舰队中包括航空母舰、驱逐舰和辅助军舰。
    第七舰队已经加强了在台湾海峡的活动。第七舰队的一艘航空母舰“塔拉瓦号”在几天以前开往了台湾的基隆港。第七舰队的四艘驱逐舰还在十九日开往了浙江沿海蒋匪盘踞的大陈岛。指挥这些军舰的司令加贝特和岛上的蒋匪军头目进行了秘密会谈，并视察了蒋匪军的军事设备。
