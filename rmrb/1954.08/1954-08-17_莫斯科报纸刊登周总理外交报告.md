Title: Moscow Newspapers Publish Premier Zhou's Diplomatic Report
Author:
Date: 1954-08-17
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, 16th. Moscow's major newspapers on the 15th carried news of the 33rd session of the Central People's Government Committee. The newspapers also published the diplomatic report made by Premier and Foreign Minister Zhou Enlai at the meeting, as well as the resolution of the meeting approving the report.

### 莫斯科报纸刊登周总理外交报告

1954-08-17
第1版()
专栏：

    莫斯科报纸刊登周总理外交报告
   【新华社莫斯科十六日电】莫斯科各主要报纸十五日刊载了我中央人民政府委员会举行第三十三次会议的消息。各报还登载了周恩来总理兼外交部部长在会议上所作的外交报告以及会议批准这个报告的决议。
