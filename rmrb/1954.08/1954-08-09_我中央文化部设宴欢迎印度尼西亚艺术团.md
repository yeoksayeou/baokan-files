Title: My Central Ministry of Culture Hosts Banquet to Welcome Indonesian Art Troupe
Author:
Date: 1954-08-09
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, August 8th: The Central Ministry of Culture gave a banquet on the evening of August 8th to welcome the Indonesian Art Troupe.

    The entire Indonesian Art Troupe, headed by its leader Mengadas Nasution, attended the banquet.

    Also invited to the banquet were: Indonesian Ambassador to China Mononutu and embassy staff, and Zeng Zusen, Dean of the Chinese Studies Department of the University of Indonesia, who is currently visiting China.

    The hosts attending the banquet were: Vice Ministers of the Central Ministry of Culture, Zhou Yang, Ding Xilin, and Zheng Zhenduo.

    Invited to accompany them were: Vice Minister of Foreign Affairs Zhang Hanfu, Director of the Bureau of Foreign Cultural Relations of the Government Administration Council’s Committee of Cultural and Educational Affairs, Hong Shen, and Secretary-General of the Beijing Municipal People's Government, Xue Zizheng.

    Also attending the banquet were: President of the Chinese People's Association for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries, Chu Tunan, heads of the China Federation of Literary and Art Circles, Chinese Writers Association, Chinese Dramatists Association, Chinese Musicians Association, Chinese Artists Association, the preparatory committee of the Chinese Dance Research Association, and heads of various art colleges and art troupes, as well as famous figures in the capital's drama, music, literature, film, and art circles, including Ouyang Yuqian, Jiang Feng, Chen Baichen, Cao Yu, Chen Huangmei, Zhao Feng, Hua Junwu, Xie Bingxin, A Ying, Ma Yanxiang, Ma Shaobo, Zhong Wei, Guo Lanying, and others.

    At the beginning of the banquet, Zhou Yang delivered a welcome speech. He said that the friendship between the people of China and Indonesia has a long history; since the founding of the People's Republic of China, this friendly relationship has continued to develop on a new basis. He pointed out that the arrival of the Indonesian Art Troupe in China provides the Chinese people with an excellent opportunity to appreciate the rich and beautiful Indonesian national art, and opens a new page in the history of Sino-Indonesian cultural exchange. He believes that the art troupe's performances in China will win the welcome and praise of the Chinese people and will contribute to further strengthening the friendly relations between China and Indonesia and to the cause of defending peace in Asia and the world.

    At the banquet, Mengadas Nasution spoke to express his thanks. He said that we received a warm welcome last night, and we have the opportunity to meet with you again tonight, for which we are very grateful. He said that most of the performers in the art troupe are young men and women from colleges and middle schools, and they are working hard to democratize art. He stated that only after Indonesia's founding did art begin to belong to the Indonesian people. Finally, he once again expressed the hope that the art troupe's performances in the People's Republic of China will consolidate the friendship between the people of China and Indonesia.

    Ambassador Mononutu also spoke at the meeting, expressing his gratitude to the Government of the People's Republic of China for inviting the Indonesian Art Troupe to visit and perform in China.

    The banquet proceeded in a warm, friendly, and joyful atmosphere. During the banquet, the guests and hosts constantly toasted to the friendship between the people of China and Indonesia and to the further development of cultural exchanges between the two countries.

    During the meeting, art workers from China and Indonesia performed wonderful programs, which were warmly welcomed.

### 我中央文化部设宴欢迎印度尼西亚艺术团

1954-08-09
第1版()
专栏：

    我中央文化部设宴欢迎印度尼西亚艺术团
   【新华社八日讯】中央人民政府文化部在八日晚设宴欢迎印度尼西亚艺术团。
    以团长孟阿达斯·纳苏申为首的印度尼西亚艺术团全体人员出席了宴会。
    应邀出席宴会的还有：印度尼西亚驻华大使莫诺努图和使馆人员，正在我国访问的印尼大学汉学院院长曾祖森等。
    主人方面出席宴会的，有：中央人民政府文化部副部长周扬、丁西林、郑振铎。
    应邀作陪的，有：外交部副部长章汉夫，政务院文化教育委员会对外文化联络事务局局长洪深，北京市人民政府秘书长薛子正。
    出席宴会的，还有：中国人民对外文化协会会长楚图南，中国文学艺术界联合会、中国作家协会、中国戏剧家协会、中国音乐家协会、中国美术家协会、中国舞蹈艺术研究会筹备委员会和各艺术院校、各艺术团体负责人以及首都戏剧、音乐、文学、电影、美术界著名人士欧阳予倩、江丰、陈白尘、曹禺、陈荒煤、赵沨、华君武、谢冰心、阿英、马彦祥、马少波、仲伟、郭兰英等。
    宴会开始，周扬致欢迎词。他说，中国和印尼两国人民的友谊有着久远的历史；中华人民共和国成立以来，这种友好的关系又在新的基础上不断地获得发展。他指出，现在印度尼西亚艺术团来到我国，这就给了中国人民一个最好的机会来欣赏丰富优美的印尼的民族艺术，而且在中印（尼）两国文化交流的历史上，展开了新的一页。他相信，艺术团在中国的表演将会博得中国人民的欢迎和赞扬，并将对进一步加强中印（尼）两国的友好关系和保卫亚洲及世界和平的事业有所贡献。
    在宴会上，孟阿达斯·纳苏申讲话致谢。他说，昨天晚上我们受到热烈的欢迎，今天晚上又有机会和大家见面，我们非常感谢。他说，艺术团的大多数的演员是高等学校和中学的男女青年，他们都努力于使艺术民主化。他表示说，印尼建国后，艺术才开始属于印尼国民。最后，他再一次希望：艺术团在中华人民共和国的演出能够巩固中印（尼）两国人民的友谊。
    莫诺努图大使也在会上讲话，对中华人民共和国政府邀请印尼艺术团来华访问演出表示感谢。
    宴会在热烈、友好、欢乐的气氛中进行。席间，宾主不断为中印（尼）两国人民的友谊，为两国间的文化交流获得进一步的发展而干杯。
    会上，中印（尼）两国艺术工作者都表演了精采节目，受到热烈欢迎。
