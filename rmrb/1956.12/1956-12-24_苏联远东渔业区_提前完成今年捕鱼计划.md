Title: The Soviet Far East Fishing Region Completes This Year's Fishing Plan Ahead of Schedule
Author:
Date: 1956-12-24
Page: 5

    Soviet Far East Fishing Region
    Completes This Year's Fishing Plan Ahead of Schedule
    (Our Moscow dispatch, December 23rd) The Soviet Union's main fishing region, the Far East, has completed its fishing and fish product plan for this year approximately two weeks ahead of schedule.
    Since the beginning of the year, the Far East's fishery workers and fishing collective farmers have given the state 6.5 million quintals of fish, an increase of several hundred thousand quintals compared to last year. The Far East includes the Primorsky Krai, Sakhalin, Khabarovsk Krai, Kamchatka, and other regions. Primorsky Krai caught 2.5 million quintals of fish, an increase of more than 400,000 quintals compared to last year. Sakhalin caught 1.48 million quintals of fish, an increase of more than 200,000 quintals compared to last year. Khabarovsk Krai caught 710,000 quintals of fish, an increase of more than 100,000 quintals compared to last year. Kamchatka caught 1.84 million quintals of fish.

### 苏联远东渔业区  提前完成今年捕鱼计划

1956-12-24
第5版()
专栏：

    苏联远东渔业区
    提前完成今年捕鱼计划
    本报莫斯科23日电　苏联主要渔业区远东今年提前约半个月完成了捕鱼和鱼制品计划。
    从年初起，远东的渔业工人和捕鱼集体农民给了国家六百五十万公担鱼，比去年增加几十万公担。远东包括滨海边疆、库页岛、伯力边疆、堪察加等地。滨海边疆捕鱼二百五十万公担，比去年增加四十多万公担。库页岛捕鱼一百四十八万公担，比去年增加二十多万公担。伯力边疆捕鱼七十一万公担，比去年增加十多万公担。堪察加捕鱼一百八十四万公担。
