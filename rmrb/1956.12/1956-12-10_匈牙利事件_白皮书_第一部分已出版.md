Title: First Part of Hungarian Event "White Paper" Published
Author:
Date: 1956-12-10
Page: 5

　　First Part of Hungarian Event "White Paper" Published
　　Xinhua News Agency, Budapest, December 8th: The Hungarian People's Republic Government Information Bureau has published the first part of the "White Paper" on the Hungarian event.
　　The first part of the "White Paper" includes documents on the counter-revolutionary forces that participated in the rebellion, and is accompanied by a large number of photographs.

### 匈牙利事件“白皮书”第一部分已出版

1956-12-10
第5版()
专栏：

　  匈牙利事件“白皮书”第一部分已出版
    新华社9日讯　据塔斯社布达佩斯8日讯：匈牙利人民共和国政府新闻局出版了关于匈牙利事件的“白皮书”的第一部分。
    “白皮书”第一部分包括关于参加叛乱的反革命势力的文件，并且附有大量照片。
