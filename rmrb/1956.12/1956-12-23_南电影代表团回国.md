Title: Yugoslav Film Delegation Returns Home
Author:
Date: 1956-12-23
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, 22nd: The entire Yugoslav film delegation, invited to China to participate in the Yugoslav Film Week activities and for a friendly visit, left Beijing by plane today to return home.

    Situ Huimin, Deputy Director of the Film Bureau of the Ministry of Culture, as well as many Chinese film workers and film actors, saw them off at the airport.

### 南电影代表团回国

1956-12-23
第1版()
专栏：

    南电影代表团回国
    新华社22日讯　应邀来中国参加南斯拉夫电影周活动并且进行友好访问的南斯拉夫电影代表团全体人员，今天乘飞机离北京回国。
    文化部电影事业管理局副局长司徒慧敏以及中国电影工作者和电影演员多人到机场送行。
