Title: Mayor of Bombay, Kader, Sets Off to Our Country
Author:
Date: 1956-12-23
Page: 1

    Xinhua News Agency, New Delhi, 22nd -- The Mayor of Bombay, S. A. Kader, at the invitation of the Mayor of Tianjin, flew to China on the 20th.
    Earlier, the Chinese Consul General Zhang Jiping hosted a farewell banquet for Kader.

### 孟买市长卡德尔启程来我国

1956-12-23
第1版()
专栏：

    孟买市长卡德尔启程来我国
    新华社新德里22日电　孟买市长斯·阿·卡德尔应天津市长的邀请，在20日乘飞机前往中国。
    早些时候，中国总领事张吉平曾经设宴为卡德尔饯行。
