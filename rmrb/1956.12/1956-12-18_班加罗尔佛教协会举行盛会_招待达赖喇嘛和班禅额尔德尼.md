Title: Grand Gathering Held by Bangalore Buddhist Association to Welcome the Dalai Lama and Panchen Erdeni
Author:
Date: 1956-12-18
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, New Delhi, December 17th. The Dalai Lama and Panchen Erdeni concluded their three-day visit to Bangalore on the 16th. On the eve of their departure, the Bangalore Buddhist Association held a grand reception. Approximately 5,000 people attended.

In his speech at the reception, the Dalai Lama recalled the historical, religious, and cultural relationship between India and Tibet. He said that during this visit, the Panchen Erdeni and he were deeply convinced that "India has always had a deep friendship with us and the people of our country." He prayed for the progress and success of the Buddhist Association, and for the longevity and happiness of all monks in India.

The Dalai Lama also presented the Buddhist Association with a set of translations of Sanskrit Buddhist scriptures.

In his speech, the Panchen Erdeni said that India has made great efforts to restore Buddhism and has been following a policy of achieving peace. He said, "We must strictly adhere to Panchsheel. We must strengthen the friendship between India and China by adhering to Panchsheel. We must continue our efforts to stop war and build world peace."

The Panchen Erdeni also presented the Buddhist Association with a set of translations of Sanskrit commentaries on Buddhist doctrines by Indian scholars.

The president of the Buddhist Association, Budhrakita, the chairman of the reception committee, Bajaj, and representatives from all walks of life spoke, expressing a very warm welcome to them.

In his speech at the end of the reception, the Chief Minister of Mysore State, Nijalingappa, expressed his gratitude to the Dalai Lama and Panchen Erdeni for attending the reception.

Before attending the reception, the Dalai Lama and Panchen Erdeni also presided over the opening ceremony of the association's library.

On the 15th, the Dalai Lama, Panchen Erdeni, and their entourage visited Mysore, eighty-six miles southeast of Bangalore. There, coconuts were ripening and rice was being harvested. The distinguished guests from the snowy roof of the world visited these places with great interest. They visited the government silk weaving factory, the Sandalwood Oil Factory, a sugar factory, and a dam called Krishnaraja Sagar. This dam is three miles long and one hundred and twenty feet high. Below the dam is a beautiful garden with many fountains and colorful lights. The guests spent the entire evening in this garden.

On the 15th, the Governor of Mysore State, Wadiyar, held a grand formal banquet in Mysore for the two distinguished guests from the Tibetan Plateau.

### 班加罗尔佛教协会举行盛会  招待达赖喇嘛和班禅额尔德尼

1956-12-18
第1版()
专栏：

    班加罗尔佛教协会举行盛会
    招待达赖喇嘛和班禅额尔德尼
    新华社新德里17日电　达赖喇嘛和班禅额尔德尼16日结束了在班加罗尔的为时三天的访问。在他们离开班加罗尔的前夕，班加罗尔的佛教协会举行了一次盛大的招待会。参加这次招待会的约有五千人。
    达赖喇嘛在招待会上讲话时追述了印度同西藏在历史、宗教和文化方面的关系。他说，在这次访问期间，班禅额尔德尼和他都深信“印度一向对我们和我国人民有着深厚的友谊”。他祈祷佛教协会取得进步和成功，并且祈祷印度一切僧众长寿和幸福。
    达赖喇嘛还向这个佛教协会赠送了一套梵文佛经的译本。
    班禅额尔德尼在讲话中说，印度尽了很大的努力来恢复佛法，并且一直在遵循实现和平的政策。他说，“我们必须严格遵守潘查希拉。我们必须通过遵守潘查希拉来加强印度同中国之间的友谊。我们必须继续我们为了制止战争和建立世界和平而作出的努力。”
    班禅额尔德尼也向这个佛教协会赠送了一套印度学者关于佛教教义的梵文注释的译本。
    这个佛教协会的主席布德拉基塔、招待委员会主席巴贾季和各界代表都讲了话，对他们表示非常热烈的欢迎。
    迈索尔邦首席部长尼加林加帕在招待会结束时的讲话中对达赖喇嘛和班禅额尔德尼参加这次招待会表示感谢。
    达赖喇嘛和班禅额尔德尼在参加这次招待会以前还为这个协会的图书馆主持了开馆仪式。
    达赖喇嘛、班禅额尔德尼和随行人员15日曾经到班加罗尔东南八十六英里的迈索尔访问。在那里，椰子成熟了，稻子正在收割。从多雪的世界屋脊来的贵宾们以极大的兴趣访问了这些地方。他们参观了政府的丝织工厂、山达尔石油工厂、制糖工厂和一个名叫克里希纳拉贾·萨加尔的水坝。这个水坝有三英里长，一百二十英尺高。水坝的下方是一个美丽的、有着许多喷泉和五颜六色的灯光的花园。客人们整个晚上都是在这个花园中度过的。
    迈索尔邦邦长瓦迪亚尔15日在迈索尔为这两位来自西藏高原的贵宾举行了一个盛大的正式宴会。
