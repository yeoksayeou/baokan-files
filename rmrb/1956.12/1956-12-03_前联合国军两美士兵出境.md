Title: Two Former UN Army American Soldiers Leave the Country
Author:
Date: 1956-12-03
Page: 5

　　Xinhua News Agency, 2nd. Two former UN Army American soldiers, Pete and Wilson, applied to leave China and have been approved by the relevant Chinese authorities. The Red Cross Society of China has sent personnel to accompany them to Shenzhen, Guangdong, and handed them over to Smith, a representative of the American Red Cross Society, at 12:55 noon on December 2nd.
　　Pete and Wilson were prisoners of war in the Korean War who refused repatriation and applied to live in China through the Red Cross Society of China in February 1954. Before leaving the country, they worked in a paper mill in Jinan, Shandong Province.

### 前联合国军两美士兵出境

1956-12-03
第5版()
专栏：

　　前联合国军两美士兵出境
    新华社2日讯　前联合国军美籍士兵裴特和威尔逊申请离开中国，已经获得中国有关方面的批准。中国红十字会已经派员陪送他们到广东深圳，并且在12月2日中午十二时五十五分把他们交给美国红十字会代表史密斯。
    裴特和威尔逊是在1954年2月间经由中国红十字会申请来中国居住的拒绝遣返的朝鲜战争中的战俘。离境前，他们在中国山东省济南市的一个造纸厂工作。
