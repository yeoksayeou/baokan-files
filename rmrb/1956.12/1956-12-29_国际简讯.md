    International Briefs

    Our Art Troupe's Visit and Performance in Mandalay
    The Chinese Art Troupe gave its first performance in Mandalay, Burma's second largest city, on the evening of the 23rd. More than 10,000 people watched the performance. Every program was warmly welcomed by the people of this ancient Burmese capital.

    China's Children's Art Exhibition Opens in Berlin
    The Chinese Children's Science and Art Works Exhibition opened in Berlin on the 22nd. A total of more than 600 scientific and technological, craft, and art works made by Chinese children aged four to fifteen were displayed at this exhibition. Scientific and technological works included small machinery, industrial products, and architectural models. Craft works included embroidery, carvings, clay sculptures, toys, etc. The clay sculpture "Little Brother Feeding Ducks" made by Zhang Naiying (a descendant of Clay Figure Zhang) from Tianjin, who is thirteen years old, aroused great interest among the visitors.

    German Journalists Leave Beijing for Home
    A delegation of five journalists from the German Democratic Republic, headed by Klaus, member of the editorial board of "Neues Deutschland," left Beijing by plane on the 25th to return home after a 47-day visit to China at the invitation of the All-China Journalists Association. (According to Xinhua News Agency)

### 国际简讯

1956-12-29
第5版()
专栏：国际简讯

    国际简讯
    我国艺术团在曼德勒访问演出
    中国艺术团23日晚在缅甸第二大城市曼德勒作了首次演出。有一万多群众观看了这次表演。演出的每个节目都受到了这个缅甸故都的人民的热烈欢迎。
    我儿童作品展览会在柏林开幕
    中国少年儿童科学艺术作品展览会22日在柏林开幕。在这个展览会上，一共展出了从四岁到十五岁的中国少年儿童们制作的科学技术、工艺、美术三类作品六百多件。科学技术作品包括小型机械、工业制品和建筑模型等。工艺作品包括刺绣、雕刻、泥塑、玩具等。天津市十三岁的张乃英（泥人张的后代）制作的泥塑“小弟弟喂鸭子”引起了参观者的极大兴趣。
    德新闻工作者离北京回国
    以“新德意志报”编委克劳斯为首的德意志民主共和国新闻工作者代表团一行五人，应中国新闻工作者联谊会的邀请在中国作了四十七天的访问以后，25日乘飞机离开北京回国。　（据新华社讯）
