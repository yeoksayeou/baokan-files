Title: Old Revolutionary Base Area - Pingjiang Peasants Busy with Winter Production (Photo)
Author: Ke Shanwen
Date: 1956-12-26
Page: 3

    Old Revolutionary Base Area - Pingjiang Peasants Busy with Winter Production
    Pingjiang County, Hunan Province, is one of China's old revolutionary base areas. The peasants here are engaging in various winter production activities with the fine tradition of hard work to develop production. Xinhua News Agency reporter Ke Shanwen photo
    Left: In early winter, members of the Yanzhou Agricultural Production Cooperative in Pingjiang County are rushing to topdress more than 300 mu of rapeseed.
    Top: Yanzhou Cooperative is building a reservoir that can irrigate more than 1,300 mu of paddy fields. When the project is completed this winter, it can solve most of the water needed for the double-cropping rice fields next year.

### 革命老根据地——平江农民冬季生产忙（图片）
柯善文
1956-12-26
第3版()
专栏：

    革命老根据地——平江农民冬季生产忙
    湖南平江县是我国革命老根据地之一。这里的农民正以辛勤劳动发展生产的优良传统，投入各种冬季生产活动。新华社记者　柯善文摄
    左　在初冬的季节，平江县烟舟农业生产合作社的社员们已赶着给三百多亩油菜施上追肥。
    上　烟舟社正在修建一座可以灌溉一千三百多亩稻田的水库，今冬工程完成后，可解决明年双季稻田需用水的大半。
