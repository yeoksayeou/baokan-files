Author:
Date: 1956-12-21
Page: 5

During the period when the British and French aggressors occupied Port Said, the heroic people of Port Said seized every opportunity and means to fight against the enemy. This is a mass demonstration held in front of the enemy, in which people, regardless of the cruel persecution of the invaders, shouted: "Drive out the enemy!" "Long live beautiful, liberated Egypt!" "Long live Gamal Abdel Nasser!" (Originally published in the Egyptian "Al-Ahram" on December 12)

### 图片

专栏：

    在英法侵略者盘据期间，英勇的塞得港人民利用每一个机会和各种方式同敌人展开了斗争。这是一次在敌人面前举行的群众大示威，人们不顾侵略者的残酷迫害，高呼着：“把敌人赶出去！”“美丽的解放了的埃及万岁！”“加麦尔·阿卜杜勒·纳赛尔万岁！”　（原载12月12日埃及“金字塔报”）
