Title: Chinese Islamic Representatives Conference Concludes
Author:
Date: 1956-12-23
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, December 22nd. The Second Chinese Islamic Representatives Conference concluded this afternoon.

    Burhan Shahidi, Chairman of the Second Committee of the Chinese Islamic Association, said in his closing remarks: "The Second Chinese Islamic Representatives Conference has concluded successfully. As Muslims, we should thank the Chinese Communist Party, the Central People's Government, and Chairman Mao, our beloved leader of all ethnic groups, for their leadership, care, and support."

    Burhan said: "As our country's socialist revolution has achieved basic victory, we in the Islamic community are holding a grand meeting here. Through this meeting, we can see more clearly the bright and happy future of our motherland and ourselves. Happiness should be created by ourselves." He said: "Allah does not send us gold and silver from the sky, but Allah has given us thinking minds and working hands. The people of our country are victoriously carrying out the cause of socialist construction, doing extremely glorious and great undertakings that our predecessors have never done. We Muslims should participate in and support this glorious and great cause with redoubled spirit."

    This morning, the representatives adopted the new articles of association of the Chinese Islamic Association and conducted elections. Burhan Shahidi was elected as the Chairman of the Second Committee of the Chinese Islamic Association, and Nur Muhammad Dapusheng, Yang Jingren, Ma Yuhuai, Ma Zhenwu, Imin Mahsum, Ma Songting, Yan Muliha Mufuti, Zhang Jie, Liu Pinyi and other nine people were elected as Vice Chairmen; An Shiwei and other one hundred and eighty-six people were elected as members of the committee. The total number of committee members is one hundred and eighty-seven, of which one committee member quota not elected at the conference is reserved for Chinese Muslims in Hong Kong. After the closing of the conference, a plenary meeting of all committee members was held, and twenty-two standing committee members were elected, and the work of the Standing Committee was determined.

### 中国伊斯兰教代表会议闭幕

1956-12-23
第1版()
专栏：

    中国伊斯兰教代表会议闭幕
    据新华社22日讯　中国伊斯兰教第二次代表会议，在今天下午闭幕。
    中国伊斯兰教第二届委员会主任包尔汉·夏赫德罕志致闭幕词说：“中国伊斯兰教第二次代表会议胜利闭幕了。作为伊斯兰教徒，我们应当感谢领导、关怀和支持我们的中国共产党、中央人民政府和我们各族人民所敬爱的领袖毛主席。”
    包尔汉说：“当着我们祖国社会主义革命已经取得基本胜利的时候，我们伊斯兰教界在这里举行隆重会议，通过这次会议，我们更清楚地看到了祖国和我们自己的光明幸福前途，幸福应该由我们自己来创造。”他说：“安拉不给我们从天上降下金和银，但是安拉给了我们能思考的头脑和能劳动的双手。我国人民正在胜利地进行着社会主义建设事业，正在做着我们前人从没有做过的极其光荣伟大的事业，我们伊斯兰教徒应该精神百倍地积极参加和支持这一光荣伟大的事业。”
    今天上午，代表们通过了中国伊斯兰教协会新的简章，并且进行了选举。包尔汉·夏赫德罕志当选为中国伊斯兰教协会第二届委员会主任，努尔穆罕默德·达浦生、杨静仁、马玉槐、马震武、伊敏·马合苏木、马松亭、言木力哈木甫提、张杰、刘品一等九人，当选为副主任；安士伟等一百八十六人当选为委员。委员的名额共一百八十七人，其中没有在大会上选举的一个委员名额，是留给香港的中国穆斯林的，在大会闭幕后，接着又举行了全体委员会议，选出了二十二名常务委员，并且确定了常务委员会的工作。
