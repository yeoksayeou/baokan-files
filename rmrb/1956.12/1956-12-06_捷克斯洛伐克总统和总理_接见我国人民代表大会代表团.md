Title: Czechoslovak President and Premier Receive Delegation from China's National People's Congress
Author:
Date: 1956-12-06
Page: 1
　　
Xinhua News Agency, Prague, December 5th: Czechoslovak President Zápotocký and Premier Široký received the delegation from the Chinese National People's Congress and the delegation from the Beijing People's Committee on the morning of December 4th. The receptions were held in a friendly and warm atmosphere.

　　Chinese Ambassador to Czechoslovakia, Cao Ying, was also present at the receptions.

### 捷克斯洛伐克总统和总理　接见我国人民代表大会代表团

1956-12-06
第1版()
专栏：

　  捷克斯洛伐克总统和总理
　　接见我国人民代表大会代表团
    新华社布拉格5日电　捷克斯洛伐克共和国总统萨波托斯基和总理西罗基，12月4日上午分别接见了中国全国人民代表大会代表团和北京市人民委员会代表团。接见是在友好热烈的气氛中进行的。
    接见的时候，中国驻捷克斯洛伐克大使曹瑛也在座。
