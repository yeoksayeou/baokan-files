Title: Our Women's Delegation Visits India
Author:
Date: 1956-12-15
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, December 14th: At the invitation of the Indian government, our government dispatched a Chinese women's delegation to India today by plane for a friendly visit. The list of the delegation is as follows:

　　Head of the delegation: Shi Liang, Minister of Justice
　　Deputy Head of the delegation: Luo Shuzhang, Vice Minister of the Ministry of Food Industry
　　Secretary-General: Cao Mengjun, Secretary of the Secretariat of the All-China Women's Federation
　　Members:
　　Laxida, Member of the National Committee of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference
　　Li Jiansheng, Deputy Director of the Beijing Municipal Health Bureau
　　Yan Renying, Deputy Director and Director of Obstetrics and Gynecology of Peking University Affiliated Hospital
　　Hu Yizai, Deputy Director of Beijing No. 2 State-Owned Cotton Mill
　　Peng Zigang, Editor-in-Chief of "Traveler" magazine
　　Lu Qiongying, Deputy Director of the International Propaganda Department of the All-China Women's Federation

### 我妇女代表团赴印度访问

1956-12-15

    我妇女代表团赴印度访问
