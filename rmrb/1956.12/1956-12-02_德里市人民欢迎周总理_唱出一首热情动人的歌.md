Title: People of Delhi Welcome Premier Zhou: Singing a Passionate and Moving Song
Author:
Date: 1956-12-02
Page: 4
　
Xinhua News Agency, New Delhi, December 1st. At the mass rally held by the people of Delhi on November 30th to welcome Premier Zhou Enlai, the choir sang a moving song. The lyrics were specially prepared by a Delhi poet to welcome Premier Zhou's visit to India. The passionate and moving lyrics welcoming Premier Zhou Enlai said: We have been moving towards peace for generations. China and India are the advocates of the Five Principles of Peaceful Coexistence. Welcome, Mr. Zhou Enlai, the people of China and India are brothers! Millions of Indian people congratulate you! Welcome, Mr. Zhou Enlai, the people of China and India are brothers! On the road to world peace, a new Asia is at the forefront! You are Nehru's friend, and he is the soul of our country. Welcome, Mr. Zhou Enlai, the people of China and India are brothers!

### 德里市人民欢迎周总理　唱出一首热情动人的歌

1956-12-02
第4版()
专栏：

　　德里市人民欢迎周总理
　　唱出一首热情动人的歌
    新华社新德里1日电　在11月30日德里市民欢迎周恩来总理的群众大会上，歌唱队唱出了一首动人的歌，歌词是德里诗人为欢迎周总理访问印度而特别准备的。这首热情欢迎周恩来总理的动人的歌词中说：我们世世代代从来都向着和平迈进。印中两国是五项原则的倡导者。欢迎周恩来先生，印中人民是兄弟！千千万万印度人民向你祝贺！欢迎你，周恩来先生，印中人民是兄弟！在世界和平的生活道路上，新的亚洲走在最前面！你是尼赫鲁的朋友，他是我们国家的灵魂。欢迎你，周恩来先生，印中人民是兄弟！
