Title: Premier Zhou Visits Various Places in India
Author:
Date: 1956-12-02
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, New Delhi, December 1st: Premier Zhou Enlai, Vice Premier He Long, and their entourage left New Delhi today on the Indian President's special plane for Pune, Bombay Province, to begin their visits to various parts of India.

Premier Zhou Enlai arrived at the airport accompanied by Prime Minister Nehru. Those who saw them off included Indian cabinet ministers, members of parliament, senior Indian government officials (both civil and military), representatives of mass organizations, and well-known figures from all walks of life.

The Dalai Lama, the Panchen Erdeni, the Chinese Buddhist Delegation, personnel from the Chinese Embassy in India, and representatives of overseas Chinese also went to the airport to see them off.

Premier Zhou Enlai and Vice Premier He Long warmly shook hands with the people who came to see them off. Premier Zhou Enlai and Vice Premier He Long reviewed the honor guard. The band played the national anthems of China and India.

Prime Minister Nehru saw Premier Zhou Enlai and Vice Premier He Long to the door of the plane. There, they allowed photographers to take pictures.

Accompanying Premier Zhou Enlai on his visits to various parts of India were Pan Zili, the Chinese Ambassador to India, as well as R.K. Nehru, the Indian Ambassador to China, and his wife.

Before boarding the plane, Premier Zhou Enlai met with reporters at the airport and answered questions they raised.

One reporter asked what the nature of his talks with Prime Minister Nehru was. Premier Zhou Enlai said, "Prime Minister Nehru and I have known each other for more than two years. We are old friends; therefore, we can talk about anything."

In response to a question about the Hungarian issue, Premier Zhou Enlai said, "We would like to see the situation improve day by day." A reporter from the United Press asked about the issue of Sino-American relations. Premier Zhou Enlai replied, "The Chinese people have always hoped to improve Sino-American relations. As you know, we have specially invited you American journalists to visit China." Another question raised by the United Press reporter was, "Is there any possibility that the Americans imprisoned in China will be released early?" Premier Zhou Enlai replied, "They always have the opportunity to be released before serving their full sentences if they behave well. But I must remind you that, so far, not a single Chinese person imprisoned in American prisons has been released or repatriated."

### 周总理到印度各地访问

1956-12-02
第4版()
专栏：

　　周总理到印度各地访问
    新华社新德里1日电　周恩来总理、贺龙副总理和他们的随行人员今天乘印度总统的专机离开新德里前往孟买省浦那，开始他们在印度各地的访问。
    周恩来总理在尼赫鲁总理陪同下到达机场。送行的有印度内阁部长们、国会议员们、印度政府的高级文武官员、各国驻印度外交使节，以及群众组织的代表和各界著名人士。
    达赖喇嘛、班禅额尔德尼、中国佛教代表团以及中国驻印度大使馆人员和华侨代表也到机场送行。
    周恩来总理、贺龙副总理同送行的人热情地握手告别。周恩来总理和贺龙副总理检阅了仪仗队。乐队奏了中国和印度的国歌。
    尼赫鲁总理送周恩来总理和贺龙副总理到飞机门口。在那里，他们让摄影记者拍了照片。
    陪同周恩来总理到印度各地访问的有中国驻印度大使潘自力以及印度驻中国大使拉·库·尼赫鲁和他的夫人。
    在上飞机以前，周恩来总理在机场接见了记者并且回答了他们提出的问题。
    有记者问，他和尼赫鲁总理的会谈是什么样的性质。周恩来总理说：“我们，尼赫鲁总理和我，已经相交两年多了。我们是老朋友；因此我们可以无所不谈。”
    在答复关于匈牙利问题的时候，周恩来总理说：“我们愿意看到局势日益好转。”美国合众社记者问到中美关系的问题。周恩来总理回答说：“中国人民一直希望改善中美关系。正如你们所知，我们已经特别邀请你们美国记者来访问中国。”合众社记者提出的另一个问题是：“被囚禁在中国的美国人是否有可能被提早释放？”周恩来总理回答说：“他们总是有机会在服刑期满之前就被释放的，如果他们表现良好的话。但是我必须提醒你，到目前为止，被关在美国监狱里的中国人没有一个被释放或遣送回国。”
