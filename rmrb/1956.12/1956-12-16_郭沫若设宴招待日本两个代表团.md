Title: Guo Moruo Hosts Banquet for Two Japanese Delegations
Author:
Date: 1956-12-16
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, 15th: The Aichi Prefecture Peace Delegation from Japan left Beijing today to visit other places, and the Chiba Prefecture Delegation from Japan will also leave Beijing in the coming days to visit other places.

On the evening of the 13th, Guo Moruo, Chairman of the Chinese People's Committee for World Peace, hosted a banquet for the two delegations.

### 郭沫若设宴招待日本两个代表团

1956-12-16
第1版()
专栏：

    郭沫若设宴招待日本两个代表团
    新华社15日讯　日本爱知县和平代表团在今天离开北京去外地参观访问，日本千叶县代表团也将在日内离开北京去外地参观访问。
    13日晚，中国人民保卫世界和平委员会主席郭沫若曾设宴招待两个代表团。
