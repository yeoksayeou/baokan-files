Title: French Ministry of Defense Admits to Detaining Egyptian Prisoners of War
Author:
Date: 1956-12-22
Page: 6

    Xinhua News Agency, Paris, December 21st: A French Ministry of Defense spokesperson confirmed on the 20th that French forces had detained Egyptian prisoners of war in the Suez Canal area.
    The spokesperson said that reports from London stated that France transported one hundred and seven prisoners of war from Port Said on the 19th, which may be true, but he stated that he himself "did not know."

### 法国防部承认扣留埃战俘

1956-12-22
第6版()
专栏：

    法国防部承认扣留埃战俘
    新华社21日讯　巴黎消息：法国国防部发言人20日证实，法军在苏伊士运河区扣留了埃及战俘。
    这个发言人说，从伦敦来的消息说，法国19日从塞得港运走了一百零七名战俘，这件事可能是真实的，不过他表示他本人“不知道”。
