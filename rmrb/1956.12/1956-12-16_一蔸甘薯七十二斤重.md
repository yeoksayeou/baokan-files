Title: One Sweet Potato Vine Weighs Seventy-Two Jin
Author: Cai Fusheng
Date: 1956-12-16
Page: 3
Agriculture News

    One Sweet Potato Vine Weighs Seventy-Two Jin
    The sweet potatoes cultivated by the Shanku Agriculture Cooperative in Lianyuan County, Hunan Province, have achieved significant increases in production. According to the results of the acceptance inspection on November 13th, the entire cooperative planted 1,936 sweet potato vines, yielding a total of 67,336 jin of sweet potato tubers, averaging more than 34 jin per vine, more than double the average of over 15 jin per vine from the 116 sweet potato vines planted last year. The largest vine weighed seventy-two jin, and the smallest weighed fifteen jin.
    The cooperative has been cultivating sweet potatoes for several years. The seeds they selected are the "Jiangxi Sweet Potato," which grows quickly, has a high yield, and has been cultivated locally for many years. (Cai Fusheng)

### 一蔸甘薯七十二斤重
蔡福生
1956-12-16
第3版()
专栏：农业珍闻

    一蔸甘薯七十二斤重
    湖南涟源县山圹农业社栽培的甘薯，获得了显著增产。据11月13日验收的结果，全社种植的一千九百三十六蔸甘薯，共收薯块六万七千三百三十六斤，平均每蔸三十四斤多，比去年种植的一百一十六蔸甘薯，平均每蔸十五斤多，超过一倍以上。其中最大的一蔸重七十二斤，最小的一蔸也有十五斤重。
    这个社栽培甘薯，已有好几年的历史。他们选用的种子是生长快、产量高、在当地栽培多年的“江西薯”。　　　　（蔡福生）
