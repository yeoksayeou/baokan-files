Title: Kameda Togo and Kaneko Kenta Leave Beijing to Return to Japan
Author:
Date: 1956-12-13
Page: 1

　　Xinhua News Agency, December 12th: Kameda Togo, Deputy Secretary-General of the Asian and Pacific Regions Peace Liaison Committee, and Kaneko Kenta, a member and representative of the Japanese Liaison Bureau to the World Federation of Trade Unions for Asia and Australia, left Beijing on the 11th to return to Japan. On the evening of the 7th, Guo Moruo hosted a banquet to bid them farewell.

### 龟田东伍和金子健太离北京回日本

1956-12-13
第1版()
专栏：

    龟田东伍和金子健太离北京回日本
    据新华社12日讯　亚洲及太平洋区域和平联络委员会副秘书长龟田东伍及委员、日本驻世界工联亚澳联络局代表金子健太，在11日离北京回日本。7日晚，郭沫若曾设宴欢送他们。
