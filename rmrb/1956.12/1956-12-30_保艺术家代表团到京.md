Title: Bulgarian Artists Delegation Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1956-12-30
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, December 29th. The Bulgarian artists' delegation, a group of eight people, arrived in Beijing on the afternoon of the 28th.

The delegation is passing through Beijing on its way to visit the Democratic Republic of Vietnam. They will stay in Beijing for about a week and, at the invitation of the Ministry of Culture, will perform three New Year's performances in succession starting from New Year's Day 1957. After visiting the Democratic Republic of Vietnam, the delegation will also come to China for an official visit.

The head of the delegation is Ma. Pei. Klesterev. The members of the delegation include a ballet dancer, a violinist, a pianist, a bass singer, and a soprano singer.

### 保艺术家代表团到京

1956-12-30
第1版()
专栏：

    保艺术家代表团到京
    新华社29日讯　保加利亚艺术家代表团一行八人28日下午到达北京。
    代表团是在去越南民主共和国访问途中路过北京的，他们将在北京停留一个星期左右，应文化部邀请从1957年元旦起连续作三场新年演出。代表团在访问越南民主共和国以后还将来中国作正式访问。
    代表团的团长是马·佩·克勒斯特夫，团员中有芭蕾舞家、小提琴家、钢琴家、男低音歌唱家和女高音歌唱家。
