Title: Sisal (Picture)
Author: Wang Chuanguo
Date: 1956-12-20
Page: 3

    Sisal
    Sisal is abundant in the Dehong Dai and Jingpo Autonomous Prefecture of Yunnan Province. Sisal fiber is strong and resistant to saltwater corrosion, making it suitable for use as rope on ships. The picture shows local farmers stripping sisal in the field, preparing to sell it to the state to support national construction and increase sideline income.
                                  Xinhua News Agency Reporter Wang Chuanguo Photo

### 剑麻（图片）
王传国
1956-12-20
第3版()
专栏：

    剑麻
    云南省德宏傣族景颇族自治州境内盛产剑麻。剑麻纤维坚韧，不怕咸水侵蚀，适宜于船舰上做缆绳用。图为当地农民在野外剥剑麻，准备卖给国家，支援国家建设，增加副业收入。
                                  新华社记者　王传国摄
