Title: China's Largest Tanker
Author: 
Date: 1956-12-22
Page: 2

    Our Largest Tanker

    (By Our Own Correspondent) On December 17th, workers at the Dalian Shipbuilding Company began construction of China's largest oil tanker, with a displacement of 7,000 tons.

    This large tanker, specifically for transporting oil, is stipulated to be built during China's first Five-Year Plan. It has a deadweight tonnage of 4,500 tons, a length of 110 meters, and a width of 15 meters. The tanker is powered by two 1,200-horsepower diesel engines, with a speed of 12.75 knots per hour.

    The hull of the tanker will be launched in June next year, and it is expected to be fully completed by the end of the year.
                  (According to the Lüda Daily)

### 我国最大的油轮

1956-12-22
第2版()
专栏：

    我国最大的油轮
    本报讯　12月17日，大连造船公司工人动手建造我国最大的排水量七千吨的油轮。
    这艘专门运输油料用的大型油轮，是我国第一个五年计划中规定建造的。它的载重量达四千五百吨，身长一百一十公尺，身宽十五公尺。这艘油轮由两台一千二百马力内燃机推动，每小时的速度达十二点七五海里。
    这艘油轮的船皮将在明年6月下水，年底可全部竣工。
                  （据旅大日报）
