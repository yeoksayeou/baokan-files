Title: All-China Women's Federation and China Democratic National Construction Association Host Women Representatives Attending the All-China Federation of Industry and Commerce Member Representative Conference
Author:
Date: 1956-12-22
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, December 21st—The All-China Women's Federation and the China Democratic National Construction Association held a reception on the 19th to host all the women representatives attending the Second Member Representative Conference of the All-China Federation of Industry and Commerce. More than 100 people attended, including female industrialists and businesswomen, and family members of industrialists and businessmen from various regions.

Li Dequan, Vice-Chairman of the All-China Women's Federation, and Li Zhuchen, Deputy Chairman of the All-China Federation of Industry and Commerce, spoke at the meeting. They encouraged family members of industrialists and businessmen and female industrialists and businesswomen to continue their efforts in the future and make greater contributions to the cause of socialist construction.

Family members of industrialists and businessmen and female industrialists and businesswomen also spoke enthusiastically at the meeting, reporting on their work, study, and ideological transformation processes, as well as their impressions of participating in the Second Member Representative Conference of the All-China Federation of Industry and Commerce.

### 全国民主妇联和民主建国会招待出席工商联会员代表大会的妇女代表

1956-12-22
第1版()
专栏：

    全国民主妇联和民主建国会招待出席工商联会员代表大会的妇女代表
    据新华社21日讯　中华全国民主妇女联合会和中国民主建国会在19日举行招待会，招待出席全国工商业联合会第二届会员代表大会的全体妇女代表。到会的有各地的女工商业者及工商业者家属代表共一百多人。
    全国妇联副主席李德全和全国工商业联合会副主任委员李烛尘在会上先后讲话。他们鼓励工商业者家属和女工商业者今后要继续努力，对社会主义建设事业作出更大的贡献。
    工商业者家属和女工商业者也在会上热烈发言，报告了自己的工作、学习情况和思想改变过程，以及参加全国工商联第二届会员大会的感想。
