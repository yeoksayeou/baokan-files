Title: Beijing Holds Memorial Meeting on the 20th Anniversary of Ostrovsky's Death
Author:
Date: 1956-12-25
Page: 7

Xinhua News Agency report: The Beijing Sino-Soviet Friendship Association Cultural Center held a report meeting on the 23rd to commemorate the 20th anniversary of the death of Nikolai Ostrovsky, the author of the famous Soviet novel "How the Steel Was Tempered."

Go Baoquan, Deputy Secretary-General of the Sino-Soviet Friendship Association, introduced Nikolai Ostrovsky's life and works to the workers, students, and cadres present. Actors from the Beijing People's Art Theater also recited Ostrovsky's works. The Soviet film "They Were the First Komsomol Members" was also screened at the meeting.

### 北京举行奥斯特洛夫斯基逝世二十周年纪念报告会

1956-12-25
第7版()
专栏：

    北京举行奥斯特洛夫斯基逝世二十周年纪念报告会
    据新华社讯　纪念苏联著名小说“钢铁是怎样炼成的”作者尼·奥斯特洛夫斯基逝世二十周年，北京市中苏友好协会文化馆23日举行了报告会。
    中苏友好协会总会副秘书长戈宝权向到会的工人、学生和机关干部介绍了尼·奥斯特洛夫斯基的生平著作。北京人民艺术剧院演员还朗诵了奥斯特洛夫斯基的作品。在会上并放映了苏联影片“他们是第一批共青团员”。
