Title: Japanese Commodity Exhibition Closes in Shanghai
Author:
Date: 1956-12-27
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, Shanghai, December 26th. The Japanese Commodity Exhibition held in Shanghai closed this afternoon.

The exhibition was on display in Shanghai for twenty-six days, with over 1.65 million visitors. During the exhibition, more than 730 Chinese technicians and Japanese technicians held technical exchange seminars on 33 categories including agricultural machinery, textile machinery, electrical appliances, plastics, and solar heat. Representatives from more than 100 Japanese manufacturers in the Japanese Commodity Exhibition Group held more than 900 trade talks with various import and export corporations in Shanghai. In addition, more than 124,000 viewers watched the Japanese films brought by the exhibition group.

At 6:00 p.m., the exhibition held a closing ceremony and closing reception at the Shanghai Sino-Soviet Friendship Building Cinema. Cao Diqiu, Vice Mayor of Shanghai, Li Zhaohuan, Vice Chairman of the Shanghai Municipal Committee of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference, Lu Muyun, Deputy Director of the Shanghai Municipal Committee of the China Council for the Promotion of International Trade, and more than 500 people from all walks of life in Shanghai were invited to attend. Yamamoto Yuichi, Vice President of the Japan International Trade Promotion Association, who arrived in Shanghai this noon, also attended the closing ceremony and reception.

Eiichi Shukutani, head of the Japanese Commodity Exhibition Group, delivered a speech at the closing ceremony. He expressed his gratitude for the friendly support of the Chinese people. He said that the exhibition had achieved great success in promoting trade between the two countries, but the greatest gain was in enhancing mutual understanding between the people of China and Japan. He stated: "After the exhibition group returns home, it is determined to fight with all its strength to promote trade between the two countries and the normalization of diplomatic relations."

Cao Diqiu, Vice Mayor of Shanghai, congratulated the success of the exhibition on behalf of the people of Shanghai. He said: "This success is built on the common aspirations of the people of China and Japan to develop trade and establish normal diplomatic relations." He hoped that this common aspiration and spirit of friendly cooperation could continue to be carried forward in the future.

Yamamoto Yuichi, Vice President of the Japan International Trade Promotion Association, thanked the staff of both China and Japan at the exhibition and the support of the Chinese government on behalf of the Japan International Trade Promotion Association. He said that the success of the exhibition "will also have a beneficial impact on peace in Asia and the world."

After the reception, a Chinese film was also shown.

### 日本商品展览会在上海闭幕

1956-12-27
第1版()
专栏：

    日本商品展览会在上海闭幕
    据新华社上海26日电　在上海举办的日本商品展览会今天下午闭幕。
    展览会在上海展出二十六天，观众达一百六十五万多人。在展览期间，有七百三十多位中国技术人员和日本技术人员举行了有关农业机械、纺织机器、电气、塑胶、太阳热等三十三类技术交流座谈会。日本商品展览团中一百多家日本厂商代表曾和上海市各进出口公司举行了九百多次贸易会谈。还有十二万四千多观众观看了展览团带来的日本电影。
    下午六时，展览会在上海市中苏友好大厦电影院举行闭幕式和闭幕酒会。上海市副市长曹荻秋、中国人民政治协商会议上海市委员会副主席黎照寰、中国国际贸易促进委员会上海市委员会副主任委员陆慕云以及上海市各界人士五百多人应邀出席。今天中午到达上海的日本国际贸易促进协会副会长山本雄一也参加了闭幕式和酒会。
    日本商品展览团团长宿谷荣一在闭幕式上致词。他对中国人民的友好支持表示感谢。他说，展览会在促进两国贸易方面获得很大的成就，但最大的收获是在增进中日两国人民的互相了解方面。他表示：“展览团回国后，决心为促进两国贸易和国交正常化而全力斗争。”
    上海市副市长曹荻秋代表上海人民对展览会的成功表示祝贺。他说：“这种成功是建筑在中日两国人民要求发展贸易和建立正常邦交的共同愿望之上的。”他希望这种共同愿望和友好合作精神能够在今后继续发扬。
    日本国际贸易促进协会副会长山本雄一在讲话中代表日本国际贸易促进协会向展览会中日双方工作人员和中国政府的支持表示感谢。他说，展览会的成功，“对亚洲和世界和平也会有有益的影响”。
    酒会后还放映了中国电影。
