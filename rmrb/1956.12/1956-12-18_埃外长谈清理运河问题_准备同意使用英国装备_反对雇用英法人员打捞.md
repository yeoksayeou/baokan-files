Title: Egyptian Foreign Minister Discusses Canal Clearing Issues: Prepared to Agree to Use British Equipment, Opposes Hiring British and French Personnel for Salvage
Author:
Date: 1956-12-18
Page: 6
    Xinhua News Agency, New York, 17th: Egyptian Foreign Minister Fawzi declared on the 16th that the Egyptian government is prepared to agree to use all equipment and salvage vessels needed to clear obstacles in the Suez Canal, including British equipment and salvage vessels.

    However, the statement said that the Egyptian government still does not agree to hire British and French personnel to engage in salvage work.

### 埃外长谈清理运河问题  准备同意使用英国装备  反对雇用英法人员打捞

1956-12-18
第6版()
专栏：

    埃外长谈清理运河问题
    准备同意使用英国装备  反对雇用英法人员打捞
    新华社17日讯　纽约消息：埃及外交部长法齐16日声明，埃及政府准备同意使用为清理苏伊士运河中的障碍物所需要的一切装备和打捞船只，包括英国的装备和打捞船只在内。
    不过，声明说，埃及政府还是不同意雇用英法人员来从事打捞工作。
