Title: Yokohama City Delegation Arrives in Beijing
Author: 
Date: 1956-12-27
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, 26th. The Japanese-Chinese Citizens Exchange Yokohama City Delegation, headed by Sato Kenji, arrived in Beijing on the evening of the 25th. This delegation is visiting China at the invitation of the Beijing Branch of the Chinese People's Committee for World Peace.

### 横滨市代表团到北京

1956-12-27
第1版()
专栏：

    横滨市代表团到北京
    新华社26日讯　以佐藤贤治为首的日中市民交流横滨市代表团在25日晚上到达北京。这个代表团是应中国人民保卫世界和平委员会北京市分会邀请前来中国访问的。
