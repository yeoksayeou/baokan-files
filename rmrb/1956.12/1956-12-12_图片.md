Title: Photograph
Author: Gao Liang
Date: 1956-12-12
Page: 4

    On December 7th, the Dalai Lama and the Panchen Erdeni visited Sanchi, a sacred Buddhist site in India. Buried on the hill of Sanchi are two disciples of Sakyamuni. The picture shows the two of them visiting the tomb. Xinhua News Agency reporter Gao Liang photographed.
