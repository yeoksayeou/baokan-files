Title: Premier Zhou Leaves Pakistan and Arrives in New Delhi
Author:
Date: 1956-12-31
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, New Delhi, December 30th. Premier Zhou Enlai, Vice Premier He Long, and their entourage arrived in the Indian capital this afternoon by plane from Dhaka, the capital of East Pakistan, after concluding their visit to Pakistan.

Indian Prime Minister Nehru, who had just returned from visits to the United States and Canada, along with Defense Minister Katju, Minister of Engineering, Housing and Supply Singh, Deputy Minister of Foreign Affairs Chanda, Indian Ambassador to China R.K. Nehru and his wife, other Indian government officials, and foreign envoys stationed in Delhi were all at Palam Airport to greet them.

Chinese Ambassador to India Pan Zili and his wife, as well as officials from the embassy, were also at the airport to welcome them.

Premier Zhou Enlai reviewed the guard of honor at the airport and shook hands with the well-wishers one by one.

Premier Zhou answered questions from Indian and other international journalists at the airport. He said that he had visited five South Asian countries and was very pleased to see the friendship of the people of these countries and their love of peace.

Premier Zhou said that he was very grateful to the Nepalese government for making arrangements for his visit. However, he needed to return to Beijing to rest first and then would visit Moscow on January 7th.

Tonight, Premier Zhou Enlai and Prime Minister Nehru will take a special car to visit the famous Bhakra Nangal Dam.

### 周总理离开巴基斯坦到达新德里

1956-12-31
第1版()
专栏：

    周总理离开巴基斯坦到达新德里
    新华社新德里30日电　周恩来总理、贺龙副总理和随行人员在结束了对巴基斯坦的访问后，今天下午从东巴基斯坦首府达卡乘飞机到达印度首都。
    刚从美国、加拿大访问归来的印度总理尼赫鲁和国防部长卡特朱、工程住房和供应部长辛格、外交部副部长钱达，印度驻中国大使拉·库·尼赫鲁夫妇以及印度政府其他官员，驻德里外国使节都到巴兰机场迎接。
    中国驻印度大使潘自力夫妇和大使馆官员也在机场欢迎。
    周恩来总理在机场检阅了仪仗队，并且同欢迎者一一握手。
    周总理在机场回答了印度和其他国家的记者们的问题。他说，他访问了南亚五个国家，他很高兴看到这些国家的人民的友谊和对和平的热爱。
    周总理说，他很感谢尼泊尔政府已经为他的访问作好了安排。但是他需要先回北京休息一下，然后将在1月7日访问莫斯科。
    今天晚上，周恩来总理将和尼赫鲁总理乘专车到著名的巴克拉南加尔水闸参观。
