Title: Premier Zhou Enlai Donates Funds to Aid Burmese Disaster Victims
Author:
Date: 1956-12-14
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Rangoon, December 12th: Premier Zhou Enlai expressed his sympathy on the 11th to the victims of the recent fires in Moulmein and Yangon's Panyang Guidi and donated 40,000 Kyats to assist them.

In a letter to Prime Minister U Ba Swe, Premier Zhou Enlai asked him to forward the funds to the relief organizations in the two disaster areas.

### 周恩来总理捐款救济缅甸灾民

1956-12-14
第1版()
专栏：

    周恩来总理捐款救济缅甸灾民
    新华社仰光12日电　周恩来总理11日对毛淡棉和仰光的潘阳贵地最近遭到火灾的灾民表示同情，并且捐助了四万缅元救济他们。
    周恩来总理在写给吴巴瑞总理的信件中请他把款项转交给两个灾区的救济机关。
