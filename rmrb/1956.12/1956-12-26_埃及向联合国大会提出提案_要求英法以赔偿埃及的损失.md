Title: Egypt Submits Proposal to UN General Assembly, Demanding Britain, France, and Israel Compensate Egypt for Losses

Author: 
Date: 1956-12-26
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, December 25th, New York (TASS): The Egyptian delegation to the United Nations has submitted a proposal to the General Assembly demanding that Britain, France, and Israel compensate Egypt for losses suffered due to aggression.

The proposal calls for the adoption of necessary measures to estimate the losses "caused by this military operation," "particularly the casualties and other losses of Egyptian residents in the Gaza Strip; the damage caused in Gaza, the Sinai Peninsula, Port Said, the Suez Canal, and other areas of Egypt; the damage to the canal and its facilities; the losses incurred due to the stoppage of navigation on the canal, the destruction of ports and airports, the destruction of state property and public utilities, as well as municipal enterprises, the destruction of airports and factories; the losses suffered by Egyptian and foreign private property and private enterprises, and the losses suffered by the Egyptian economy as a whole."

The Eleventh Session of the United Nations General Assembly will resume on January 2, 1957, and will discuss Egypt's proposal.

### 埃及向联合国大会提出提案  要求英法以赔偿埃及的损失

1956-12-26
第1版()
专栏：

    埃及向联合国大会提出提案
    要求英法以赔偿埃及的损失
    新华社25日讯　据塔斯社纽约25日讯：埃及驻联合国代表团向大会提出一项提案，要求英、法、以色列赔偿埃及遭受侵略的损失。
    提案要求采取必要的措施来估计“这次军事行动造成的”损失，“特别是加沙地区埃及居民的伤亡和其他损失；加沙、西奈半岛、塞得港、苏伊士运河和埃及其他地区遭到的破坏；运河及其设施遭到的损害；由于运河通航停顿、港口和机场被破坏、国家财产与公共设施以及市政企业被毁灭、航空港和工厂遭到破坏而造成的损失；埃及和外国私人财产和私人企业遭到的损失以及整个埃及经济遭到的损失。”
    联合国大会第十一届会议在1957年1月2日复会后即将讨论埃及的提案。
