Title: Kádár Visits the Tóthobánya Coal Mining Area; Miners Pledge to Strive to Increase Coal Production

Author:

Date: 1956-12-03
Page: 5

　　Kádár Visits the Tóthobánya Coal Mining Area
　　Miners Pledge to Strive to Increase Coal Production
　　Xinhua News Agency, Budapest, December 2nd. Kádár, Prime Minister of the Hungarian Revolutionary Worker-Peasant Government, visited the Tóthobánya coal mining area on November 30th.

　　He went there at the request of the Tóthobánya Coal Mine Workers' Committee. He held talks with the miners for more than four hours, discussing issues related to the activities of the Workers' Committee and the measures necessary to increase coal production.

　　Prime Minister Kádár told the representatives of the Workers' Committee that the Hungarian Socialist Workers' Party should work in industrial and mining enterprises in a new way and should first of all do a good job in political and ideological education. In industrial and mining enterprises, the government relies and will continue to rely on the assistance of the Workers' Committee. Kádár pointed out that the government plans to strengthen the police force and establish workers' defense teams in factories to protect factories and public order.

　　The miners assured Prime Minister Kádár that they are willing to strive to increase coal production.

### 卡达尔访问托托巴尼奥煤矿区  矿工们保证要为增加采煤量而努力

1956-12-03
第5版()
专栏：

　　卡达尔访问托托巴尼奥煤矿区
　  矿工们保证要为增加采煤量而努力
    新华社布达佩斯2日电　匈牙利工农革命政府总理卡达尔11月30日访问了托托巴尼奥煤矿区。
    他是应托托巴尼奥煤矿工人委员会的请求到那里去的。他同矿工们进行了四个多小时的谈话，讨论了有关工人委员会的活动和为增加采煤量所必需采取的措施等问题。
    卡达尔总理对工人委员会的代表们说，匈牙利社会主义工人党在工矿企业中应该以新的方法来工作，并且首先要做好政治思想教育工作。在工矿企业中，政府在现在和将来都是要依靠工人委员会的协助。卡达尔指出，政府计划加强警察部队，在工厂里成立工人保卫队，保卫工厂和公共秩序。
    矿工们向卡达尔总理保证，他们愿意为增加采煤量而努力。
