Title: Prime Minister Bandaranaike Announces: Britain Agrees to Return Base to Ceylon Next Year
Author:
Date: 1956-12-13
Page: 5

    Prime Minister Bandaranaike Announces
    Britain Agrees to Return Base to Ceylon Next Year
    Xinhua News Agency, Colombo, December 12th: Ceylon Prime Minister Bandaranaike announced in the House of Representatives on the 11th that Britain has agreed to hand over its bases in Ceylon to the Ceylon government next year. The exact date of the handover will be decided after consultations between the two sides.
    Bandaranaike said that Ceylon and Britain will hold talks next month to discuss the issue of Britain's request for "facilities" after these bases are handed over to Ceylon. The issues to be discussed include what "facilities" Britain needs; if these "facilities" are granted to Britain, how long should the term be; and under what conditions the Ceylon government will grant these "facilities".

### 班达拉奈克总理宣布  英国答应明年把基地交还锡兰

1956-12-13
第5版()
专栏：

    班达拉奈克总理宣布
    英国答应明年把基地交还锡兰
    新华社12日讯　科伦坡消息：锡兰总理班达拉奈克11日在众议院宣布，英国已经同意明年把英国在锡兰的基地移交给锡兰政府。移交的确切日期将由双方磋商后决定。
    班达拉奈克说，锡兰和英国下月将举行会谈，讨论关于英国要求在这些基地移交给锡兰后取得“便利”的问题。将讨论的问题包括英国需要什么“便利”；如果给予英国这些“便利”的话，期限要多长；以及在什么条件下锡兰政府给予这些“便利”。
