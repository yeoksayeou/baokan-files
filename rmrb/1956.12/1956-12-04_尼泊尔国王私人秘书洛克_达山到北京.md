Title: Nepal's King's Private Secretary Lok Darshan Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1956-12-04
Page: 1

　　Nepal's King's Private Secretary Lok Darshan Arrives in Beijing
    Xinhua News Agency, December 2nd: Lok Darshan, Chief Private Secretary to the King of Nepal, arrived in Beijing on the afternoon of December 2nd for a visit.
    Chen Shuliang, Deputy Director of the Asian Department of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs, and Wang Yutian, Deputy Director of the Protocol Department, were among those who welcomed him at the airport. Ramamurti, Private Secretary to the Indian Ambassador to China, also welcomed him at the airport.

### 尼泊尔国王私人秘书洛克·达山到北京

1956-12-04
第1版()
专栏：

　　尼泊尔国王私人秘书洛克·达山到北京
    新华社2日讯　尼泊尔国王首席私人秘书洛克·达山在2日下午到达北京访问。
    到机场欢迎的有外交部亚洲司副司长陈叔亮和礼宾司副司长王雨田等人。到机场欢迎的还有印度驻华大使私人秘书拉穆默蒂。
