Title: Soviet Amur Oblast Delegation Returns Home After Concluding Visit to Heilongjiang
Author:
Date: 1956-12-08
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Harbin, December 3rd: The Soviet Amur Oblast Labor People's Delegation, led by Chalobanov, Secretary of the Amur Oblast Committee of the CPSU, returned home on the evening of the 2nd from Harbin after a visit of more than ten days to Heilongjiang Province in our country. The Soviet Amur Oblast Labor People's Delegation successively visited the cities and counties of Harbin, Qiqihar, Jiamusi, Hegang, and Suihua.

### 苏联阿穆尔州代表团结束在黑龙江的访问后回国

1956-12-08
第1版()
专栏：

　　苏联阿穆尔州代表团结束在黑龙江的访问后回国
    据新华社哈尔滨3日电　由苏共阿穆尔州委员会书记查洛巴诺夫率领的苏联阿穆尔州劳动人民代表团一行十三人在我国黑龙江省进行十多天访问活动后，在2日晚离哈尔滨回国。苏联阿穆尔州劳动人民代表团先后访问了哈尔滨、齐齐哈尔、佳木斯、鹤岗、绥化等市、县。
