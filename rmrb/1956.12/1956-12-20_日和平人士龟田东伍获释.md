Title: Japanese Peace Activist Togo Kameda Released
Author:
Date: 1956-12-20
Page: 5
Xinhua News Agency, Yokohama, December 19th: Togo Kameda, Deputy Secretary-General of the Asian and Pacific Regions Peace Liaison Committee and a Japanese peace activist, was released on bail on the 17th by Matsumoto Jiichiro, a member of the Japanese Socialist Party.
Kameda was arrested by the police when he returned to Tokyo on the 14th and was transferred to the Yokohama District Public Prosecutor's Office on the 16th.

### 日和平人士龟田东伍获释

1956-12-20
第5版()
专栏：

    日和平人士龟田东伍获释
    新华社19日讯　横滨消息：亚洲及太平洋区域和平联络委员会副秘书长、日本和平人士龟田东伍已经在17日由日本社会党议员松本治一郎保释。
    龟田是在14日回到东京时被警察逮捕的，并且在16日被移送横滨地方检察厅。
