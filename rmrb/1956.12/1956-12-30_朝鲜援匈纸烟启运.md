Title: Korean Cigarettes Sent to Hungary

Author:

Date: 1956-12-30
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, Pyongyang, 29th — A portion of the aid materials from the Korean people to the Hungarian people—ten tons of cigarettes—were shipped out on the 28th.

　　These first-class "Golden Steel" brand cigarettes were produced by the workers of the Pyongyang Tobacco Factory in a nine-day crash effort, and the workers also guaranteed the good quality of the cigarettes.

### 朝鲜援匈纸烟启运

1956-12-30
第5版()
专栏：

    朝鲜援匈纸烟启运
    新华社平壤29日电　朝鲜人民送给匈牙利人民的援助物资的一部分——十吨纸烟在28日启运。
    这批金钢牌的一级纸烟是平壤烟草工厂的工人们在九天内突击生产出来的，工人们并且保证了纸烟的良好质量。
