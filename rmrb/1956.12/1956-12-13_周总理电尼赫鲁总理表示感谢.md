Title: Premier Zhou Cables Premier Nehru to Express Gratitude
Author:
Date: 1956-12-13
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, New Delhi, December 12th: Premier Zhou Enlai, upon concluding his visit to India, sent a telegram to Indian Prime Minister Nehru, thanking him for his warm welcome and gracious hospitality. Premier Zhou also expressed his gratitude for the warm welcome and gracious hospitality he received from the people wherever he went in the country, and expressed his belief that the friendship and cooperation between the two peoples would be further developed.

In his reply telegram to Premier Zhou Enlai, Nehru said: I am very pleased that you were satisfied with this visit to India, although the visit was short and quite intense. We all welcomed you not only as a friend, but also as a representative of a great nation that has friendly relations with us, which we consider a great pleasure. I am sure that this friendly relationship will last a long time and will benefit our two countries and the cause of world peace.

Nehru also said in his reply telegram: I am very happy to think that you will come to India again, and that we will have more opportunities to meet and have friendly talks.

### 周总理电尼赫鲁总理表示感谢

1956-12-13
第1版()
专栏：

    周总理电尼赫鲁总理表示感谢
    新华社新德里12日电　周恩来总理在结束对印度访问的时候，拍电报给印度总理尼赫鲁，感谢他的热情欢迎和殷勤招待。周总理在电报中还说，对于他不论到这个国家的哪一个地方都受到人民的热烈欢迎和殷勤招待表示感谢，相信两国人民之间的友谊和合作将进一步发展。
    尼赫鲁在给周恩来总理的复电中说：我很高兴你对这次在印度的访问感到满意，虽然访问时间很短促，而且相当紧张。我们大家不仅把你当作一位朋友，而且把你当作同我们有着友好关系的一个伟大的民族的代表来欢迎你，这是我们认为莫大高兴的事，我敢肯定，这种友好关系将会长久存在，并且将会给我们两国以及世界和平事业带来好处。
    尼赫鲁在复电中还说：我想到你将再来印度，而且我们还将有更多的机会会晤和友好会谈，感到十分高兴。
