Title: Soviet Union and Iran Propose to the United Nations: Requesting the Admission of Mongolia and Japan

Author:

Date: 1956-12-10
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, 9th, New York dispatch: The United Nations announced on the 7th that the Soviet Union has submitted a request that the Security Council should admit the Mongolian People's Republic as a member of the United Nations. Prior to the Soviet Union, Iran also requested that the Security Council should discuss Japan's application to become a member of the United Nations as soon as possible.

The United Nations said when announcing this news that the Security Council may hold a meeting on the 12th to discuss these two issues.

### 苏、伊两国向联合国提出　要求接纳蒙古和日本

1956-12-10
第5版()
专栏：

　　苏、伊两国向联合国提出
　　要求接纳蒙古和日本
    新华社9日讯　纽约消息：联合国7日宣布，苏联已经提出了安全理事会应当接纳蒙古人民共和国为联合国会员国的要求。伊朗在苏联之先，也提出安理会应尽快讨论日本申请为联合国会员国的要求。
    联合国在宣布这消息时说，安理会可能在12日举行会议讨论这两个问题。
