Title: Premier Zhou and Vice Premier He's Visit Activities in Calcutta
Author: 
Date: 1956-12-11
Page: 4
　
Xinhua News Agency, Calcutta, December 9th: Premier Zhou Enlai and Vice Premier He Long visited the Indian Statistical Institute and the Calcutta School of Tropical Medicine on the morning of December 9th.

　　Upon arriving at the Statistical Institute, the Chinese guests were warmly welcomed by Professor P.C. Mahalanobis, the director of the institute, and the students.

　　Premier Zhou Enlai planted a mango sapling on the campus. Then, Professor Mahalanobis, who participated in drafting India's Second Five-Year Plan, led the visit to various departments of the institute. Premier Zhou said that China is willing to send experts and students to the institute to study. A delegation headed by Wang Sihua will arrive at the institute in a day or two to discuss the issue of Chinese people studying here with Professor Mahalanobis.

　　During the visit to the School of Tropical Medicine, Premier Zhou Enlai met an American researcher who came to welcome him and shook hands with him. There are also Chinese people engaged in research work at the institute. Premier Zhou Enlai told them to keep in touch with each other and be good friends.

　　The Chinese guests also visited a Buddhist temple this morning. The monks presented them with a sandalwood Buddha statue.

### 周总理贺副总理在加尔各答的参观活动

1956-12-11
第4版()
专栏：

    周总理贺副总理
    在加尔各答的参观活动
    据新华社加尔各答9日电　周恩来总理和贺龙副总理9日上午参观了印度统计学院和加尔各答热带医学院。
    在到达统计学院时，中国客人们受到院长普·钱·马哈拉诺比斯教授和学生们的盛大欢迎。
    周恩来总理在校园里种下了一棵芒果树苗，然后由参与印度第二个五年计划拟订工作的马哈拉诺比斯教授引导参观了学院的各个学系。周总理说，中国愿意派遣专家和学生到这个学院来学习。以王思华为首的一个代表团一两天就要到达这个学院，同马哈拉诺比斯教授商谈中国人在这里研究学习的问题。
    在热带医学院参观的时候，周恩来总理会见了一位美国研究工作者，这位研究工作者前来欢迎他，并且同他握手。另外也有中国人在这个学院从事研究工作。周恩来总理对他们说，要互相经常联系，做好朋友。
    中国客人们今天上午还参观了一所佛教寺院。僧人把一尊檀香木佛像赠送给他们。
