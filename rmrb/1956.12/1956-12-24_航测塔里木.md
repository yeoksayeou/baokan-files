Title: Aerial Survey of Tarim

Author:

Date: 1956-12-24
Page: 2

Xinhua News Agency, Urumqi, December 22nd. The aerial survey of the Tarim Basin concluded on the 20th.

	The area covered by this aerial survey was the western part of the Tarim Basin. The aircraft flew far to both the east and west banks of the Hotan River, conducting measurements in the vast, undulating desert through aerial photography and radar.

	This aerial survey, conducted with the strong assistance of Soviet experts, began in late September and lasted for three months. The amount of work accomplished is equivalent to approximately 200 survey teams working for a year.

### 航测塔里木

1956-12-24
第2版()
专栏：

    航测塔里木
    新华社乌鲁木齐22日电　航空测量塔里木盆地的工作已经在20日结束。
    这次航空测量的地区是塔里木盆地的西部，飞机曾经飞到和阗河东西两岸的远处，借着空中摄影和雷达，在一望无边、沙丘起伏的大沙漠中进行了测量。
    这次航测是在苏联专家大力帮助下进行的，从9月下旬开始，共进行了三个月，测量的效果约等于二百个测量队一年的工作量。
