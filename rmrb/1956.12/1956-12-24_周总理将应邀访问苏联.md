Title: Premier Zhou will visit the Soviet Union at invitation
Author:
Date: 1956-12-24
Page: 1

    Xinhua News Agency, December 23rd. To further strengthen the fraternal friendship and cooperation between the Soviet Union and the People's Republic of China, the Soviet government has invited Zhou Enlai, Premier of the People's Republic of China, to pay a friendly visit to the Soviet Union. Premier Zhou Enlai has accepted this invitation and is scheduled to travel to the Soviet Union in early January 1957.

### 周总理将应邀访问苏联

1956-12-24
第1版()
专栏：

    周总理将应邀访问苏联
    新华社23日讯　为了进一步加强苏联和中华人民共和国之间的兄弟友谊和合作，苏联政府邀请中华人民共和国总理周恩来到苏联进行友好访问。周恩来总理接受了这一邀请，并定于1957年1月初前往苏联。
