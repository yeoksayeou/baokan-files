Title: Xinlan Flour Mill (Photo)
Author: Zhang Jie
Date: 1956-12-17
Page: 2

    Xinlan Flour Mill
    The newly built Xinlan Flour Mill in Lanzhou is an automated flour factory capable of producing approximately 10,000 bags of flour daily (each bag weighing 50 jin). The left photo shows the purification workshop. The right photo shows Huo Changqi, a worker operating an electric hoist, transporting a large quantity of flour to the finished product warehouse.
    Xinhua News Agency reporter Zhang Jie

### 新兰面粉厂（图片）
张杰
1956-12-17
第2版()
专栏：

    新兰面粉厂
    兰州市新建成的新兰面粉厂，是一座自动化的面粉工厂，每天能生产一万袋左右的面粉（每袋是五十斤）。左图是清粉车间。右图是驾着电箱起重机的工人霍长起把大批面粉运往成品库里。　新华社记者　张杰摄
