Title: Si Ji Qing Vegetable Production Cooperative: Vegetables Grow Well in Winter (Picture)
Author: Fu Jun
Date: 1956-12-27
Page: 3

    Si Ji Qing Vegetable Production Cooperative
    Vegetables Grow Well in Winter
    The first batch of tomatoes and cucumbers cultivated in greenhouses by the Si Ji Qing Vegetable Production Cooperative in the suburbs of Beijing, which won the 1955 Agricultural Production Increase Model Unit Award, has already been harvested. The average yield per greenhouse is 40 jin higher than last year.
            Xinhua News Agency Reporter Fu Jun photographed
    Above: Exterior view of the greenhouse
    Right: The first bountiful harvest of cucumbers and tomatoes

### 四季青蔬菜生产合作社  冬天蔬菜长得好（图片）
傅军
1956-12-27
第3版()
专栏：

    四季青蔬菜生产合作社
    冬天蔬菜长得好
    曾获1955年农业增产模范单位奖励的北京市郊四季青蔬菜生产合作社温室培育的第一批西红柿和黄瓜，已经收摘，平均每间温室可以比去年增产四十斤。
            新华社记者　傅军摄
    上　温室外景
    右　第一批丰收的黄瓜和西红柿
