Title: The Happiness of Retired Workers (Picture)
Author: Yuan Hao
Date: 1956-12-04
Page: 2

　　The Happiness of Retired Workers
    Retired workers from the Qinhuangdao Yaohua Glass Factory are using their spare time to participate in environmental greening work. This year they have planted dozens of varieties of precious chrysanthemums. This is them analyzing the varieties of chrysanthemums.
                                    Photo by Yuan Hao (Hebei Daily)

### 退休工人的幸福（图片）
袁浩
1956-12-04
第2版()
专栏：

　　退休工人的幸福
    秦皇岛耀华玻璃工厂退休的老工人，利用空隙时间，参加绿化环境的工作。今年他们栽种了几十种名贵的菊花。这是他们在分析菊花的品种。
                                    袁浩摄（河北日报社稿）
