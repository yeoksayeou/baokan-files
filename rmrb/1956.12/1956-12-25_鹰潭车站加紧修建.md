Title: Yingtan Railway Station Construction Speeding Up
Author:
Date: 1956-12-25
Page: 2

Xinhua News Agency, Nanchang, 24th. To meet the needs of the large transportation volume after the full opening of the Yingtan-Xiamen Railway, the construction project of the new Yingtan Railway Station, the largest railway station in Jiangxi Province, is being accelerated. This station will become a transportation hub connecting the Zhejiang-Jiangxi Railway and the Yingtan-Xiamen Railway. According to the plan, all engineering tasks will be completed by April next year.

### 鹰潭车站加紧修建

1956-12-25
第2版()
专栏：

    鹰潭车站加紧修建
    据新华社南昌24日电　为适应鹰厦铁路全线通车后庞大运输量的需要，江西省最大的一个火车站——新建鹰潭车站的建筑工程正在加速进行中。这个车站将成为联结浙赣铁路和鹰厦铁路的运输枢纽。按照计划，全部工程任务将在明年4 月份完成。
