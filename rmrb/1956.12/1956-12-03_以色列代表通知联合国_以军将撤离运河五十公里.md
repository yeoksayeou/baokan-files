Title: Israeli Representative Notifies UN: Israeli Army to Withdraw 50 Kilometers from Canal

Author:
Date: 1956-12-03
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, 2nd - New York Dispatch: The Israeli delegation to the United Nations notified UN Secretary-General Hammarskjöld on December 1st that, starting from December 3rd, Israeli forces would withdraw from the Suez Canal zone to a distance of 50 kilometers from the canal.

In a letter, Israel's chief representative to the United Nations, Abba Eban, said that the Israeli government had reached an agreement with General Burns, commander of the international forces, on the issue of the United Nations forces being stationed in the area between the canal and the western edge of the "Israeli positions."

### 以色列代表通知联合国　以军将撤离运河五十公里

1956-12-03
第6版()
专栏：

　　以色列代表通知联合国
　　以军将撤离运河五十公里
    据新华社2日讯　纽约消息：以色列驻联合国代表团12月1日通知联合国秘书长哈马舍尔德说，从12月3日开始，以色列军队将从苏伊士运河沿线撤退到离运河五十公里的地方。
    以色列驻联合国首席代表阿巴·伊班在一封信里说，以色列政府同国际部队司令伯恩斯将军就联合国部队驻在运河和“以色列阵地”的西缘之间的一带地区问题达成了协议。
