Title: Former Finnish President Paasikivi Dies
Author:
Date: 1956-12-16
Page: 5

    Former Finnish President
    Paasikivi Dies
    Xinhua News Agency, Helsinki, December 15th: Juho Kusti Paasikivi, a prominent Finnish statesman and former President of the Republic of Finland, passed away in Helsinki on the morning of December 14th due to heart disease. He was 86 years old.

### 芬兰前任总统  巴锡基维逝世

1956-12-16
第5版()
专栏：

    芬兰前任总统
    巴锡基维逝世
    新华社15日讯　赫尔辛基消息：芬兰杰出的政治家、芬兰共和国前总统尤·巴锡基维14日早晨因心脏病在赫尔辛基逝世。他享年八十六岁。
