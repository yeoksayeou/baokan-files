Author: 
Date: 1956-12-07
Page: 4

Reactions to Criticism

    Letter from the First Industrial Bureau of Shenyang: The criticism of our bureau's preparations for sending workers abroad in the article "Do More Concrete Work" published on page 4 of the People's Daily on September 7 is completely correct. In addition to conducting a thorough self-criticism, we guarantee that similar errors will not occur in the future.

### 对批评的反应

1956-12-07
第4版()
专栏：

　　对批评的反应
    沈阳市第一工业局来信：人民日报9月7日第四版所载“多做些具体工作”一文中，对我局输送出国工人准备工作的批评，是完全正确的。我们除作深刻检查外，并保证今后不发生类似错误。
