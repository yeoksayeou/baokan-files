Title: Sino-Egyptian Friendship Association Holds Plenary Council Meeting; Determines Main Work Plan for the Next Three Months
Author:
Date: 1956-12-02
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, 1st Dispatch: The Sino-Egyptian Friendship Association held its second plenary council meeting today. The meeting unanimously approved the Sino-Egyptian Friendship Association's main work plan for the next three months, determining that in the next three months, it will successively publish cartoons, poems, and booklets in Chinese and Arabic supporting Egypt; organize the translation of Egyptian literary books; hold an exhibition of photographs of the Egyptian people's resistance against Anglo-French aggression; and hold Egyptian film evenings and report meetings on the current situation in Egypt.

The meeting unanimously approved the applications of three organizations—the All-China Journalists Association, the China Islamic Cultural Association, and the All-China Federation of Industry and Commerce—accepting them as new group members of the Sino-Egyptian Friendship Association.

The meeting approved the addition of Deng Tuo, Chairman of the All-China Journalists Association; Ma Songting, Vice-Director of the China Islamic Cultural Association; Hu Ziang, Vice Chairman of the All-China Federation of Industry and Commerce; and Jin Jiazhen, professor of the Western Languages Department of Northwest University who once taught at Cairo University, as directors of the Sino-Egyptian Friendship Association.

The meeting also unanimously approved the establishment of a compilation and translation committee to be responsible for compiling and translating works introducing various aspects of China and Egypt, with Directors Zhang Zhirang and Zhu Dan being elected as chairman and vice-chairman respectively.

### 中埃友协举行全体理事会议　确定今后三个月的主要工作计划

1956-12-02
第1版()
专栏：

　　中埃友协举行全体理事会议
　　确定今后三个月的主要工作计划
    新华社1日讯　中埃友好协会今天举行第二次全体理事会议。会议一致通过了中埃友协今后三个月的主要工作计划，确定在今后三个月中将陆续以汉文及阿拉伯文出版我国人民支援埃及的漫画、诗歌、小册子；组织翻译埃及文学书籍；举办埃及人民反对英法侵略的抗战图片展览会；举办埃及电影晚会和有关埃及目前形势的报告会等。
    这次会议一致通过中国新闻工作者联谊会、中国回民文化协进会、中华全国工商业联合会等三个团体的申请，接受它们为中埃友好协会的新的团体会员。
    会议通过增补中国新闻工作者联谊会会长邓拓，中国回民文化协进会副主任马松亭阿訇，中华全国工商业联合会副主任委员胡子昂和曾在开罗大学任教的西北大学西语系教授金家桢等四人为中埃友协理事。
    会议并一致通过建立编译委员会负责编译介绍中埃各方面的著作，理事张志让、朱丹被选为正副主任委员。
