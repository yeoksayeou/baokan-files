Title: The Sixth Session of the Vietnam National Assembly is About to Convene; Ton Duc Thang Discusses the Great Significance of the Meeting; "Nhan Dan" Emphasizes the Importance of Consolidating the People's Democratic System

Author:

Date: 1956-12-29
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, Hanoi, 28th: Ton Duc Thang, Chairman of the Standing Committee of the Vietnam National Assembly, delivered a speech on "Voice of Vietnam" radio today, talking about the significance and content of the Sixth Session of the Vietnam National Assembly. He said that this meeting will review the government's domestic and foreign policy guidelines in 1956 and will discuss the government's work guidelines and lines for 1957, as well as various proposals put forward by the government. The Assembly will pay attention to the struggle for national reunification and the consolidation of the North, and will also propose amendments to the Constitution and improvements to the organization of the National Assembly.

Ton Duc Thang said, "I believe that the resolutions of this conference will have a significant impact on the political and economic life of our people. It will make our people more closely united and consolidate our regime, so as to overcome difficulties, correct mistakes, and develop achievements, advancing towards greater and newer victories in the struggle to consolidate the North and realize national reunification, and making greater contributions to safeguarding world peace."

Xinhua News Agency, Hanoi, 28th: On the eve of the formal opening of the Sixth Session of the National Assembly of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam, "Nhan Dan" published an editorial today saying that in order to complete the national democratic revolution as soon as possible and consolidate the people's democratic dictatorship system in the North, improving and consolidating the regime is a particularly important task at present.

"Nhan Dan" believes that this session of the National Assembly needs to study and do the following work:

	Strengthen the activities of the highest organ of power—the National Assembly—and the activities of the National Assembly deputies. The relationship between the government and the National Assembly and the Standing Committee of the National Assembly must be more clearly defined.

	People's representative conferences and administrative committees at all levels must be re-elected in accordance with the spirit of the Fatherland Front's program, so that the people can increasingly directly manage the country and supervise the regime.

	Many parts of the 1946 Constitution are no longer suitable for the current new environment and need to be supplemented. The current national laws are not perfect enough. In order to guarantee the people's democracy and strengthen the dictatorship over the enemy, the revision of laws must be promoted.

Xinhua News Agency, Hanoi: The Standing Committee of the National Assembly of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam has decided to convene the Sixth National Congress of Representatives at the end of December.

The National Assembly of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam was elected by universal suffrage throughout Vietnam in 1946. The most recent congress was held in September last year.

The representative of the Government Press Office of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam said at a press conference on the morning of the 24th that at this congress, the Standing Committee of the National Assembly will report to the congress on the following issues: a report on the work of the Standing Committee of the National Assembly in the past year and a report on the situation and tasks of the Sixth Session; proposals on improving the organization of the National Assembly; and proposals on amending the Constitution.

At the same time, the Standing Committee of the National Assembly will suggest that the government report to the congress on all the government's work in the past year and the draft of the work for 1957, focusing on: land reform and organizational rectification; financial economy, national planning, budget and improving people's lives; and the struggle for reunification.

### 越南国民大会第六次会议即将召开  孙德胜谈会议的重大意义  “人民报”强调巩固人民民主制度的重要性

1956-12-29
第5版()
专栏：

    越南国民大会第六次会议即将召开
    孙德胜谈会议的重大意义
    “人民报”强调巩固人民民主制度的重要性
    新华社河内28日电　越南国民大会常务委员会委员长孙德胜今天在“越南之声”广播电台发表讲话，谈到越南国民大会第六次会议的意义和内容。他说，这次会议将要检查政府1956年的内政和外交工作的方针和路线，并且将要讨论政府在1957年的工作方针和路线，以及政府所提出的各种提案。大会将注意到实现国家统一的斗争问题以及巩固北方等问题，同时大会将提出修改宪法和健全国民大会组织的问题。
    孙德胜说，我相信，这一次大会的决议，将对我们人民的政治和经济生活有重大的影响。它将使我们人民更加紧密团结，使我们的政权更加巩固，以便克服困难、改正错误、发挥成绩，在巩固北方和实现祖国统一的斗争事业中，向着更伟大、更新的胜利前进，为保卫世界和平作出更大的贡献。
    据新华社河内28日电　在越南民主共和国国民大会第六次会议正式开幕的前夕，“人民报”今天发表社论说，为了尽快完成全国的民主民族革命事业和巩固北方的人民民主专政制度，健全和巩固政权是当前一项特别重要的任务。
    “人民报”认为这一届国民大会会议需要研究和做好以下的工作：
    加强最高权力机关——国民大会的活动和国民大会代表的活动。政府和国民大会以及国民大会常务委员会之间的关系必须得到更加明确的规定。
    各级人民代表会议和行政委员会必须按照祖国战线的纲领精神重行选举，使人民日益能够直接管理国家和监督政权。
    1946年的宪法有许多地方已经不适合目前的新的环境，需要加以补充。目前的国家法律还不够完备。为了保证人民的民主和加强对敌人的专政，修改法律一事必须得到促进。
    据新华社河内电　越南民主共和国国民大会常务委员会决定在12月底召开第六次国民代表大会。
    越南民主共和国国民大会是在1946年由越南全国普选选出的。最近一次大会是在去年9月间召开的。
    越南民主共和国政府报纸处的代表在24日上午的一次记者招待会上说，在这次大会上，国民大会常务委员会将向大会作以下几个问题的报告：关于国民大会常务委员会在过去一年中的工作报告以及第六次会议的情况和任务的报告；关于健全国民大会组织的提案；关于修改宪法问题的提案。
    同时，越南国民大会常务委员会将建议政府向大会报告有关政府在过去一年中的全部工作以及1957年工作的草案，重点是：土地改革和整顿组织问题；财政经济、国家计划、预算和改善人民生活问题；为统一而斗争问题。
