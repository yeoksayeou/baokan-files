Title: Academy of Sciences and Other Institutions Investigate and Research the Social and Historical Conditions of Various Ethnic Groups in Xinjiang

Author:
Date: 1956-12-05
Page: 7

Xinhua News Agency, Urumqi - The Xinjiang Ethnic Social History Investigation Team has begun its investigation and research work in Minfeng, Yutian, and Cele counties of Hotan Prefecture.

This investigation team was recently established in Urumqi and consists of more than 20 professors, researchers, and university students drawn from the Chinese Academy of Sciences, the Central Institute for Nationalities, and relevant units in the Xinjiang Uygur Autonomous Region. The main task of the investigation team is to clarify the social nature of various ethnic groups in Xinjiang before liberation and the modern history of various ethnic groups over the past century within the next four to seven years. At the same time, it will conduct investigations into ancient history, ethnic relations, and archaeology.

This investigation and research will provide materials for future research on the history of various ethnic groups and help the Party and the government to further improve the work in ethnic minority areas.

### 科学院等机构调查研究新疆各族社会历史情况

1956-12-05
第7版()
专栏：

　  科学院等机构调查研究新疆各族社会历史情况
    据新华社乌鲁木齐电　新疆民族社会历史情况调查组开始在和阗专区的民丰、于阗、策勒三县进行调查研究工作。
    这个调查组是最近在乌鲁木齐成立的，由中国科学院、中央民族学院和新疆维吾尔自治区各有关单位抽调的教授、研究人员和大学生二十多人组成。调查组的主要任务，是在今后四年至七年内，弄清解放前新疆各民族的社会性质及各民族的近百年史；同时，进行有关古代史、民族关系和考古等方面考察工作。
    这次调查研究，将为今后研究各民族的历史，帮助党和政府进一步做好民族地区工作提供资料。
