Title: Winter Work Has Begun
Author: Lin Geng
Date: 1956-12-09
Page: 8

　　Winter Work Has Begun
　　Lin Geng
　　
　　Winter's wind blows through the trees
　　You, child of the Siberian cold front
　　Welcome to guests from afar
　　Winter work has begun
　　Clang, clang, the blacksmith's bellows
　　Has become a steel factory
　　How many chimneys stretch towards the sky
　　Where the heavenly wind is forever singing
　　Quiet wind in winter, blow
　　The meteorological bureau's forecast has reached the radio station
　　Gales of force seven or eight, forty or fifty degrees below zero
　　The life of the northern country's winter is unfolding

### 冬季的工作已经开始
林庚
1956-12-09
第8版()
专栏：

　　冬季的工作已经开始
　　林庚严冬的风啊吹过林梢你西伯利亚寒流之子欢迎那远方来的客人冬季的工作已经开始叮当叮当的铁匠风箱已经变成了钢铁工厂多少只烟突伸向天空那里有天风永远在唱冬季里静静的风呀吹起来气象局的预报到了广播台七级风八级风零下四五度北国的冬季天生活正展开
