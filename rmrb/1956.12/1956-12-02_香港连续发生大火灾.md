Title: Hong Kong Experiences Consecutive Major Fires
Author:
Date: 1956-12-02
Page: 4
    Xinhua News Agency, Guangzhou, December 1st: News from Hong Kong: A major fire broke out in the wooden shacks area of Tin Hau Temple Road, Causeway Bay, Hong Kong, on November 29th. It burned for more than an hour, destroying over 200 houses and affecting approximately 400 households, totaling more than 1,700 people. The hillside wooden shacks area of Tin Hau Temple Road also experienced a major fire on November 12th, burning down more than 60 wooden houses and leaving over 350 victims homeless.

### 香港连续发生大火灾

1956-12-02
第4版()
专栏：

　  香港连续发生大火灾
    新华社广州1日电　香港消息：香港铜锣湾天后庙道木屋区11月29日发生大火，燃烧一个多钟头，毁屋二百多间，遭受火灾的约四百户，共一千七百多人。天后庙道的半山木屋区，11月12日也发生过大火，焚毁了六十多间木屋，三百五十多个灾民流离失所。
