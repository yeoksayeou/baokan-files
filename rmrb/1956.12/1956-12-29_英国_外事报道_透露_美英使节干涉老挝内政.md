Title: British "Foreign Affairs Report" Reveals US and British Envoys Interfere in Laotian Internal Affairs
Author:
Date: 1956-12-29
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, December 28th: London News - The British "Foreign Affairs Report" recently revealed that the US and British envoys to Laos are interfering in Laotian internal affairs, plotting to undermine the peaceful unification of Laos.

The magazine said, "Mr. Parsons, the US Ambassador, strongly opposes letting the Lao Patriotic Front participate as ministers before the election." It revealed that Prince Souvanna Phouma, the Prime Minister of the Kingdom of Laos, supports immediately accommodating personnel from the three Lao combat forces into the government. The "Foreign Affairs Report" said, "Since the Americans control the purse strings, their opinions carry weight."

The British magazine also said, "When speaking directly to the Lao people, British Ambassador Mr. Holliday firmly supports the Americans. However, in front of the Americans, the Briton argues that accommodating one or two Lao ministers before the election would not be detrimental, as long as they are not given important positions and Laos firmly guarantees to hand over the two provinces and consolidate their army and regime."

In mentioning French Chargé d'affaires Souli, the "Foreign Affairs Report" said that he "adopted a middle ground" on this matter. Souli believes that "accommodating at most two Lao personnel as ministers as a gesture would do no harm."

The "Foreign Affairs Report" also said that Mr. Sen, the Indian Chairman of the International Commission for Laos, supports Prime Minister Phouma.

### 英国“外事报道”透露  美英使节干涉老挝内政

1956-12-29
第6版()
专栏：

    英国“外事报道”透露
    美英使节干涉老挝内政
    新华社28日讯　伦敦消息：英国“外事报道”最近透露，美国和英国的驻老挝使节干涉老挝内政，阴谋破坏老挝实现和平统一。
    这家杂志说，“美国大使帕森斯先生强烈反对在选举以前让寮国参加进来当大臣。”它透露，老挝王国首相富马是赞成立即容纳三个寮国战斗部队人员参加政府的。“外事报道”说：“由于美国人掌握着钱财大权，所以他们的意见是有分歧的”。
    这个英国杂志又说，“英国大使霍利戴先生在直接对老挝人谈话的时候，他坚决支持美国人。但是，当着美国人的面，这位英国人则争论说，在选举前容纳一两个寮国大臣坏不了什么事，只要不给他们重要职位和寮国坚决保证交出两个省份并且把他们的军队和政权加以归并的话。”
    在提到法国代办苏利的时候，“外事报道”说他对这件事“采取了中间立场”。苏利认为，“容纳最多不过两个寮国人员当大臣做做样子是没有坏处的”。
    “外事报道”还说，老挝国际委员会印度主席森是支持富马首相的。
