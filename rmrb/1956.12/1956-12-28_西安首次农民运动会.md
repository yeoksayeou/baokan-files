Title: Xi'an's First Peasants' Games

Author:

Date: 1956-12-28
Page: 4

Sports News

    Xi'an's First Peasants' Games
    (Xinhua News Agency) Xi'an recently held its first Peasants' Games. Over two hundred male and female peasant athletes competed in basketball and tug-of-war championships for three days from the 22nd to the 24th. The Yanta District and Weiyang District teams won the men's basketball championship and runner-up, respectively. The Baqiao District team won both the men's and women's tug-of-war championships.

### 西安首次农民运动会

1956-12-28
第4版()
专栏：体育新闻

    西安首次农民运动会
    据新华社讯　西安市最近举行第一次农民运动会。两百多名男、女农民运动员，在22日到24日三天里进行了篮球、拔河的锦标赛。雁塔区和未央区的代表队分别获得男子篮球冠军和亚军。男子拔河和女子拔河冠军都由灞桥区代表队获得。
