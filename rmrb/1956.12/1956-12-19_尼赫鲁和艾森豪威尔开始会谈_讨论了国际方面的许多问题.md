Title: Nehru and Eisenhower Begin Talks, Discuss Many International Issues
Author:
Date: 1956-12-19
Page: 6
Xinhua News Agency, Gettysburg, December 18th: Indian Prime Minister Nehru and US President Eisenhower began private talks on December 17th at Eisenhower's farm in Gettysburg, Pennsylvania.
    According to Eisenhower's Press Secretary Hagerty and the Indian Embassy Press Secretary Tandan at the same afternoon press conference, Eisenhower told them that he and Nehru "discussed many international issues," but he did not specify what those issues were. Hagerty estimated that Eisenhower and Nehru had "secret talks" for more than twelve hours on the 17th. No one else attended their talks.

### 尼赫鲁和艾森豪威尔开始会谈  讨论了国际方面的许多问题

1956-12-19
第6版()
专栏：

    尼赫鲁和艾森豪威尔开始会谈
    讨论了国际方面的许多问题
    新华社18日讯　宾夕法尼亚葛底斯堡消息：印度总理尼赫鲁和美国总统艾森豪威尔17日在艾森豪威尔的农庄——葛底斯堡开始私人会谈。
    据艾森豪威尔新闻秘书哈格蒂和印度驻美大使馆新闻秘书坦丹在同一天下午的记者招待会上说，艾森豪威尔曾经对他们说，他和尼赫鲁“讨论过国际方面的许多问题”，但是他没有说明那些问题是什么。据哈格蒂估计，艾森豪威尔和尼赫鲁在17日“秘密会谈”了十二个多小时。没有旁人参加他们的会谈。
