Title: Soviet Leaders Cordially Converse with Chinese Representatives During Meeting with Chinese National People's Congress Delegation and Beijing Municipal People's Committee Delegation; Ambassador Liu Xiao Holds Reception for the Two Delegations' Visit to the Soviet Union
Author:
Date: 1956-12-01
Page: 1

　　Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, 29th: Chairman Voroshilov of the Presidium of the Supreme Soviet of the Soviet Union, Chairman Bulganin of the Council of Ministers of the Soviet Union, and First Secretary Khrushchev of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of the Soviet Union, received all members of the Chinese National People's Congress Delegation and the Beijing Municipal People's Committee Delegation on the afternoon of November 29th. They held three hours of friendly talks.

　　Present at the meeting were Lobanov, Chairman of the Soviet of the Union of the Supreme Soviet of the Soviet Union, Racis, Chairman of the Soviet of Nationalities, Gorkin, Secretary of the Presidium, and Fedrin, Deputy Minister of Foreign Affairs of the Soviet Union. Also present was Liu Xiao, Chinese Ambassador to the Soviet Union.

　　Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, 30th: Chinese Ambassador to the Soviet Union, Liu Xiao, held a reception on November 29th for the visit of the Chinese National People's Congress Delegation and the Beijing Municipal People's Committee Delegation to the Soviet Union.

　　From the Soviet side, those attending the reception included Bulganin, Kaganovich, Mikoyan, Molotov, Khrushchev, Zhukov, and Mukhitdinov, representatives of the Supreme Soviet of the Soviet Union and the Supreme Soviet of the Russian Federal Republic, ministers of various ministries, Marshals and Generals of the Soviet Army, representatives of various social organizations, and representatives of Moscow industrial enterprises.

　　Guests also included diplomatic envoys from various countries stationed in Moscow, as well as Soviet and foreign journalists.

　　The Chinese National People's Congress Delegation and the Beijing Municipal People's Committee Delegation, headed by Peng Zhen, attended the reception.

　　Ambassador Liu Xiao delivered a brief congratulatory speech to those present. Subsequently, Peng Zhen and Khrushchev spoke at the meeting.

　　The reception was conducted in a friendly and sincere atmosphere. (Attached photo)

### 在接见我国人民代表大会代表团和北京市人民委员会代表团的时候　苏联领导人同我国代表亲切交谈　刘晓大使为两代表团访苏举行招待会

1956-12-01
第1版()
专栏：

　　在接见我国人民代表大会代表团和北京市人民委员会代表团的时候
　　苏联领导人同我国代表亲切交谈
　　刘晓大使为两代表团访苏举行招待会
    新华社莫斯科29日电　苏联最高苏维埃主席团主席伏罗希洛夫、苏联部长会议主席布尔加宁和苏联共产党中央委员会第一书记赫鲁晓夫在11月29日下午，接见了中国全国人民代表大会代表团和北京市人民委员会代表团的全体人员。他们进行了三小时的友好谈话。
    接见的时候在座的，有苏联最高苏维埃联盟院主席洛巴诺夫、民族院主席拉齐斯、主席团秘书戈尔金，苏联外交部副部长费德林。在座的还有中国驻苏联大使刘晓。
    新华社莫斯科30日电　中国驻苏联大使刘晓为中国全国人民代表大会代表团和北京市人民委员会代表团访问苏联，在11月29日举行了招待会。
    苏联方面出席招待会的有布尔加宁、卡冈诺维奇、米高扬、莫洛托夫、赫鲁晓夫、朱可夫和穆希金诺夫，苏联最高苏维埃和俄罗斯联邦共和国最高苏维埃的代表，各部部长，苏联元帅和苏军将领，各社会团体的代表和莫斯科工业企业的代表等。
    客人中间还有各国驻莫斯科的外交使节，以及苏联和外国的记者们。
    以彭真为首的中国全国人民代表大会代表团和北京市人民委员会代表团出席了招待会。
    刘晓大使向到会者致简短的祝词。接着，彭真和赫鲁晓夫在会上讲了话。
    招待会是在友好真诚的气氛中进行的。（附图片）
