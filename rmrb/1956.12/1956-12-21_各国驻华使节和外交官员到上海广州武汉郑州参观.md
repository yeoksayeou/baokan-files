Title: Envoys and Diplomatic Officials from Various Countries Stationed in China Visit Shanghai, Guangzhou, Wuhan, and Zhengzhou
Author: 
Date: 1956-12-21
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, 20th: Envoys and diplomatic officials stationed in China, at the invitation of the Chinese Ministry of Foreign Affairs, and accompanied by Vice Minister of Foreign Affairs Ji Pengfei and Director of the West European Department Huang Hua, have returned to Beijing on the 19th after visiting Shanghai, Guangzhou, Wuhan, Zhengzhou, and other places.

The envoys and diplomatic officials invited to visit various locations included: Pakistani Ambassador to China, Ahmed; Ambassador of the German Democratic Republic to China, Gipner, and his wife; Norwegian Ambassador to China, Kløgt-Hansen; Afghan Ambassador to China, Samad; Romanian Ambassador to China, Chioroiu, and his wife; Egyptian Ambassador to China, Hassan Ragab, and his wife; Chargé d'affaires of the Indian Embassy in China, Bahadur Singh, and his wife; Chargé d'affaires of the Bulgarian Embassy in China, Eteropolsky, and his wife; Counselor of the Czechoslovak Embassy in China, Vasec, and his wife; Counselor of the Korean Embassy in China, Li Zhongyi; Counselor of the Indonesian Embassy in China, Anwar Sani; Counselor of the Soviet Embassy in China, Sudarikov; Counselor of the Polish Embassy in China, Dembnicki; First Secretary of the Vietnamese Embassy in China, Pham Binh; First Secretary of the Albanian Embassy in China, Bino; First Secretary of the Mongolian Embassy in China, Damdin Dorji; First Secretary of the Swiss Embassy in China, Rivier; Second Secretary of the Hungarian Embassy in China, Gaond, etc.

The wife of Yugoslav Ambassador to China, Popovic, also participated in this trip.

The envoys and diplomatic officials visited the electrical machinery factory, steam turbine factory, and Shenxin No. 6 Cotton Mill in Shanghai; in Guangzhou, they visited the Peasant Movement Training Institute, the Chinese Export Commodities Fair, Zhongshan University, and the Huangpu Port Wharf, and toured Yuexiu Mountain, the Revolutionary Martyrs' Cemetery, and Huanghuagang. In Wuhan, they visited the Wuhan Iron and Steel Complex and the construction project of the Yangtze River Bridge, and in Zhengzhou, they visited the Textile Machinery Factory and the State-owned No. 3 Cotton Mill. During the visits, the responsible persons of the provinces and cities also introduced the local situation to them. The envoys and diplomatic officials left Beijing for various destinations on December 7th.

### 各国驻华使节和外交官员到上海广州武汉郑州参观

1956-12-21
第1版()
专栏：

    各国驻华使节和外交官员到上海广州武汉郑州参观
    新华社20日讯　各国驻华使节和外交官员应我国外交部邀请，在外交部副部长姬鹏飞和西欧司司长黄华陪同下，在上海、广州、武汉和郑州等地进行参观访问后，已经在19日返回北京。
    应邀到各地参观游览的使节和外交官员有：巴基斯坦驻华大使阿哈默德，德意志民主共和国驻华大使纪普纳和夫人，挪威驻华大使克洛格—亨生，阿富汗驻华大使萨马德，罗马尼亚驻华大使乔罗尤和夫人，埃及驻华大使哈桑·拉加卜和夫人，印度驻华大使馆临时代办巴哈杜尔·辛格和夫人，保加利亚驻华大使馆临时代办埃特罗波尔斯基和夫人，捷克斯洛伐克驻华大使馆参赞瓦塞克和夫人，朝鲜驻华大使馆参赞李忠翼，印度尼西亚驻华大使馆参赞安瓦·萨尼，苏联驻华大使馆参赞苏达利柯夫，波兰驻华大使馆参赞邓布尼茨基，越南驻华大使馆一等秘书范平，阿尔巴尼亚驻华大使馆一等秘书比诺，蒙古驻华大使馆一等秘书达姆丁道尔吉，瑞士驻华公使馆一等秘书芮维约，匈牙利驻华大使馆二等秘书高恩德等。
    南斯拉夫驻华大使波波维奇的夫人也参加了这次旅行。
    使节和外交官员们在上海参观了电机厂、汽轮机厂、申新第六纱厂，在广州参观了农民运动讲习所、中国出口商品展览会、中山大学和黄埔港码头，并游览了越秀山、革命烈士陵园和黄花岗。在武汉参观了武汉钢铁联合企业和长江大桥的建筑工程，在郑州参观了纺织机械厂和国棉三厂。在访问时各地的省、市负责人还向他们介绍了当地情况。使节和外交官员们是在12月7日离京前往各地的。
