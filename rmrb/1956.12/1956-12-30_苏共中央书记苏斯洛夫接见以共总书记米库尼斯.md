Title: Suslov, Secretary of the CPSU Central Committee, Receives Mikunis, General Secretary of the Israeli Communist Party
Author:
Date: 1956-12-30
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, 29th -- TASS reports: Suslov, Secretary of the CPSU Central Committee, received Mikunis, General Secretary of the Central Committee of the Israeli Communist Party, on December 28th.

They held talks in a friendly atmosphere on issues of mutual concern.

### 苏共中央书记苏斯洛夫接见以共总书记米库尼斯

1956-12-30
第5版()
专栏：

    苏共中央书记苏斯洛夫接见以共总书记米库尼斯
    新华社29日讯　据塔斯社莫斯科讯：苏共中央书记苏斯洛夫在12月28日接见了以色列共产党中央委员会总书记米库尼斯。
    他们在友好气氛中就共同关心的问题进行了交谈。
