Title: British Troops Launch Large-Scale Invasion of Southern Yemen
Author:
Date: 1956-12-19
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, Cairo, 18th. Cairo Radio broadcasted on the evening of the 17th that the Yemeni Ambassador to Cairo, Abu Taleb, informed the Arab League Secretariat that Britain had launched a new invasion of southern Yemen on the 17th. He said that British tanks, armored vehicles, and troops had crossed the Yemeni border on a large scale.
    Abu Taleb said that Yemen would lodge a complaint with the United Nations regarding Britain's military build-up in the Aden Protectorate. He said that British jet aircraft were now flying in the airspace over the Yemeni border, and its warships were sailing into Yemeni territorial waters.

### 英军大举侵犯也门南部

1956-12-19
第6版()
专栏：

    英军大举侵犯也门南部
    新华社18日讯　开罗消息：据开罗电台17日晚上广播，也门驻开罗公使阿布塔莱布通知阿拉伯联盟秘书处说，英国17日对也门南部进行了新的侵略。他说，英国的坦克、装甲车和军队大举越过了也门边境。
    阿布塔莱布说，也门将向联合国控诉英国在亚丁保护地进行军事集结。他说，英国的喷气式飞机现在正在也门边境的领空飞行，它的军舰也正在开进也门的领海。
