Title: Japanese Commodity Exhibition Inspection Group Arrives in Shanghai
Author:
Date: 1956-12-18
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, Shanghai, December 17th. The Japanese Commodity Exhibition Inspection Group, composed of Japanese local assembly members and figures from industry and commerce, arrived in Shanghai at noon today aboard the tour ship "Awaji Maru." The inspection group will be visiting, touring, and conducting trade negotiations for a week in Shanghai while the Japanese Commodity Exhibition is on display.

The inspection group is led by the head of the group, Yamaya Hachimansuke, chairman of the Japan Local Assembly Members International Trade Promotion League, and the deputy head of the group, Shimizu Taichiro, vice chairman of the Japan Local Assembly Members International Trade Promotion League. Members of the group include assembly members from various prefectures throughout Japan, chairmen and members of city assemblies, mayors, and representatives of industrial and commercial enterprises participating in the Japanese Commodity Exhibition. The inspection group consists of 161 people.

Representatives from the Shanghai Committee of the China Council for the Promotion of International Trade, the Shanghai Committee of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference, and other circles in Shanghai, as well as Japanese staff members of the Japanese Commodity Exhibition, were at the dock to welcome them.

### 日本商品展览会视察团到上海

1956-12-18
第1版()
专栏：

    日本商品展览会视察团到上海
    新华社上海17日电　由日本地方议员和工商界人士组成的日本商品展览会视察团，已于今天中午搭参观船“淡路丸”抵达上海。视察团乘日本商品展览会在上海展出期间，在这里作为时一周的访问、参观和进行贸易谈判等活动。
    视察团由团长、日本地方议员促进国际贸易联盟会会长山屋八万雄和副团长、日本地方议员促进国际贸易联盟副会长清水太一郎率领。团员中有日本各地都道府县的议会议员，都市的议会议长、议员、市长和参加日本商品展览会的工商企业代表。视察团共有一百六十一人。
    中国国际贸易促进委员会上海市委员会、中国人民政治协商会议上海市委员会等上海各界代表和日本商品展览会的日方工作人员，曾到码头上欢迎。
