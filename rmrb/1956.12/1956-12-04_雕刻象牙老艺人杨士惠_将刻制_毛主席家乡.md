Title: Ivory Carving Veteran Yang Shihui to Carve "Chairman Mao's Hometown"
Author: Bai Yu
Date: 1956-12-04
Page: 2

Ivory Carving Veteran Yang Shihui
　　Will Carve "Chairman Mao's Hometown"
　　(Our newspaper reports) Veteran artist Yang Shihui of the Beijing Ivory Carving Production Cooperative is currently creating a clay model of "Chairman Mao's Hometown."
　　In order to realistically reflect the new and beautiful appearance and labor scenes of "Chairman Mao's Hometown"—the Shaoshan area—Yang Shihui decided to spend some effort creating a "clay draft."
　　This clay sculpture began in early November. Yang Shihui also invited the famous clay figurine artist Lang Shao'an (i.e., "Noodle Man" Lang) to assist him in creating more than a hundred figures for the work. He plans to seek opinions from artists again and then begin carving on the one hundred and eighteen-斤 (jin) piece of ivory in mid-December. (Bai Yu)

### 雕刻象牙老艺人杨士惠　将刻制“毛主席家乡”
白瑀
1956-12-04
第2版()
专栏：

　  雕刻象牙老艺人杨士惠
　　将刻制“毛主席家乡”
    本报讯　北京市雕刻象牙生产合作社老艺人杨士惠，正在制作“毛主席家乡”的泥塑模型。
    为了真实地反映“毛主席家乡”——韶山地区的新的优美面貌和劳动情景。杨士惠决定花费部分精力搞个“泥塑草稿”。
    这件泥塑11月初就开始制作了。杨士惠还邀请了著名的捏人艺人郎绍安（即面人郎）协助他制作作品上的一百多个人物，准备再次征求美术家们的意见后12月中旬开始在一百一十八斤重的象牙料上动手雕刻。　　（白瑀）
