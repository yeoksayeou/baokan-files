Title: Premier Zhou Sends Telegram to Indian President Expressing Gratitude
Author:
Date: 1956-12-12
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, New Delhi, December 11th: Premier Zhou Enlai sent a telegram to Indian President Rajendra Prasad upon his departure from India, expressing his gratitude. The telegram reads as follows:

　　“As my colleagues and I are about to leave your great country, please allow me, on behalf of the Chinese government and people, and in my own name, to express our heartfelt thanks and sincere respect to Your Excellency and the Indian government and people. We are grateful for the warm welcome and gracious hospitality extended to us during our visit to your country. This welcome and hospitality is not only an unforgettable experience for us, but also a vivid demonstration of the fraternal friendship of the Indian people towards the Chinese people. We wish your great nation prosperity and your people happiness. We wish the friendship between China and India will grow day by day.”

### 周总理致电印度总统表示感谢

1956-12-12
第1版()
专栏：

    周总理致电印度总统表示感谢
    新华社新德里11日电　周恩来总理在离开印度时致电印度总统拉金德拉·普拉沙德表示感谢。电文如下：
      “在我的同事和我将要离开你们的伟大国土的时候，请允许我代表中国政府和人民并且以我个人的名义，向阁下及印度政府和人民表示我们衷心的感谢和真挚的敬意。我们感谢你们在我们访问贵国期间对我们的热烈欢迎和殷勤招待。你们的这种欢迎和招待不仅是我们难忘的经历，而且也是印度人民对中国人民的兄弟友谊的鲜明表现。我们祝你们伟大的国家繁荣，祝你们的人民幸福。我们祝中印两国的友谊日益发展。”
