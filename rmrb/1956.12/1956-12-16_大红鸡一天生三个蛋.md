Title: A Big Red Hen Lays Three Eggs in One Day

Author:

Date: 1956-12-16
Page: 3

Agriculture News

    A Big Red Hen Lays Three Eggs in One Day
    A hen raised in the Hushi Second Restaurant in Putian County, Fujian Province, laid three eggs in a row on November 4th. The largest weighed 2.9 taels, the smallest weighed 2.6 taels, and the three eggs together weighed more than half a catty. This hen is a big red hen and weighs six catties. The news caused a sensation for a while, and nearby residents flocked to watch.
                  (According to Fujian Daily)

### 大红鸡一天生三个蛋

1956-12-16
第3版()
专栏：农业珍闻

    大红鸡一天生三个蛋
    福建省莆田县笏石第二饭店养的一只母鸡，在11月4日一天里，一连生了三个蛋。最大的一个重二两九钱，最小的一个重二两六钱，三个蛋共重半斤多。这只母鸡是大红鸡，体重六斤。这个消息曾经轰动一时，附近的居民纷纷前去观看。
                  （据福建日报）
