Title: Clams Can Be Harvested

Author:

Date: 1956-12-19
Page: 3

Column: Response to Criticism

    After the People's Daily published "Can Clams Be Harvested in Yantai Port?" on July 2nd, the Qingdao Port Authority has now notified the Yantai Port Branch to consult with the local aquatic products department to organize the harvesting of clams in the port on the premise of ensuring that the ship operations and port area order are not affected.　CPC Yantai Municipal Committee Office

### 浅蜊可以采捞了

1956-12-19
第3版()
专栏：对批评的反应

    浅蜊可以采捞了
    7月2日人民日报发表了“烟台港内的浅蜊能不能采捞”后，现在青岛港务管理局已通知烟台港务分局会同当地水产部门研究，在切实保证不影响港湾船舶作业和港区秩序的基础上，有组织地在港内采捞浅蜊。　中共烟台市委办公室
