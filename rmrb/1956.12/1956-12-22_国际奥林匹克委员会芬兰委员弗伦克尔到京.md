Title: International Olympic Committee Finnish Member Frenckell Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1956-12-22
Page: 1

    Xinhua News Agency, December 21st. Mr. Von Frenckell, Finnish member of the International Olympic Committee, arrived in Beijing by plane this afternoon for a visit to China at the invitation of the Chinese government. He will be making a short sightseeing visit in Beijing.
    Among those who welcomed him at the airport were Dong Shouyi, Chinese member of the International Olympic Committee, and Huang Zhong, vice chairman of the Chinese Olympic Committee (All-China Sports Federation). The Finnish Ambassador to China, Mr. Sundström, also went to the airport to welcome him.

### 国际奥林匹克委员会芬兰委员弗伦克尔到京

1956-12-22
第1版()
专栏：

    国际奥林匹克委员会芬兰委员弗伦克尔到京
    新华社21日讯　应邀来中国访问的国际奥林匹克委员会芬兰委员冯·弗伦克尔今天下午乘飞机到达北京。他将在北京作短期参观访问。
    到机场欢迎的，有国际奥林匹克委员会中国委员董守义和中国奥林匹克委员会（中华全国体育总会）副主席黄中等人。芬兰驻中国大使孙士敦也到机场欢迎。
