Title: Liaison Delegation of the General Headquarters of the Vietnam People's Army Accuses the United States of Transporting Weapons into South Vietnam
Author: 
Date: 1956-12-23
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, Hanoi, December 22nd: The United States has repeatedly violated the agreement on the cessation of hostilities in Vietnam by continuously transporting weapons and other war materials to South Vietnam.

In a letter sent to the International Commission on December 19th, the Liaison Delegation of the General Headquarters of the Vietnam People's Army accused the United States of these illegal actions. The letter pointed out that on September 23, 1956, the ship "Pierce" delivered nearly five thousand tons of military supplies to Saigon port, including light and heavy machine guns, machine gun bullets, and tanks. On September 26, 1956, the ship "Hong Kong" also unloaded more than three thousand tons of military supplies in Saigon, including one hundred and five 77mm and 75mm caliber cannons, tank parts, combat speedboats, and parts for inland river combat vessels.

The Liaison Delegation of the General Headquarters of the Vietnam People's Army requested the International Commission to investigate these illegal actions and notify the Liaison Delegation of the investigation results and opinions on how to handle these issues.

In another letter to the International Commission on December 14, the Liaison Delegation of the General Headquarters of the Vietnam People's Army pointed out that on October 4, two hundred tons of cannon parts and military aircraft parts were transported to Saigon, marked with the abbreviation "Mutual Defense Assistance Program." The letter accused that this behavior demonstrated that the United States and the South Vietnamese authorities had in fact formed a military alliance.

### 越南人民军总司令部联络代表团  指责美国把武器运入南越

1956-12-23
第5版()
专栏：

    越南人民军总司令部联络代表团
    指责美国把武器运入南越
    新华社河内22日电　据越南通讯社讯：美国接连违反关于在越南停止敌对行动的协定，不断把武器和其他战争物资运交南越。
    越南人民军总司令部联络代表团在12月19日送交给国际委员会的一封信中指责了美国的这种违法行动。信上指出，在1956年9月23日，“皮尔士号”船把将近五千吨军用物资运抵西贡港，其中有轻、重机关枪、机枪子弹和坦克。在1956年9月26日，“香港号”船也在西贡卸下了三千多吨军用物资，其中有一百零五门七七公厘口径和七五公厘口径的大炮，坦克部件，作战汽艇和内河作战船只的零件。
    越南人民军总司令部联络代表团要求国际委员会调查这些违法行动，并且把调查结果和处理这些问题的意见通知联络代表团。
    越南人民军总司令部联络代表团在12月14日给国际委员会的另一封信里指出，10月4日有二百吨大炮部件和军用飞机部件运到西贡，上面标着“互助防御援助计划”的简写字母。信件指责说，这种行为说明美国和南越当局在事实上结成了军事联盟。
