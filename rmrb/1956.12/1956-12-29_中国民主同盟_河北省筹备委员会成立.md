Title: Founding of the Preparatory Committee of the China Democratic League in Hebei Province
Author:
Date: 1956-12-29
Page: 4

    China Democratic League
    Founding of the Preparatory Committee of the China Democratic League in Hebei Province
    Xinhua News Agency, Baoding: The Preparatory Committee of the China Democratic League in Hebei Province was established in Baoding City on the 23rd.
    At the inaugural meeting, Guo Zechen, Standing Committee Member of the Central Committee of the China Democratic League and Director of the Organization Committee of the Central Committee of the China Democratic League, announced on behalf of the Central Committee of the China Democratic League the approval of the establishment of the Preparatory Committee of the China Democratic League in Hebei Province. He also approved Liu Qingyang, Qiao Xinheng, Han Wendong, Yu Mingde, and other twelve people as members. Guo Zechen also introduced to the conference the history of the struggle of the China Democratic League over the past fifteen years, as well as the nature and tasks of the league.
    Yan Dakai, Secretary of the Secretariat of the Hebei Provincial Committee of the Chinese Communist Party, Gao Shuxun, Deputy Governor of Hebei Province, and Qi Biting, Vice Chairman of the Hebei Provincial Committee of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference, also spoke at the meeting. One hundred and twenty-six people attended the inaugural meeting.

### 中国民主同盟  河北省筹备委员会成立

1956-12-29
第4版()
专栏：

    中国民主同盟
    河北省筹备委员会成立
    据新华社保定讯　中国民主同盟河北省筹备委员会23日在保定市成立。
    在成立大会上，民盟中央常务委员、民盟中央组织委员会主任郭则沉代表民盟中央委员会宣布，批准民盟河北省筹备委员会成立。并批准刘清扬、乔辛愥、韩温冬、余明德等十二人为委员。郭则沉还向大会介绍了民盟成立十五年来的斗争历史及盟的性质和任务。
    中共河北省委员会书记处书记阎达开、河北省副省长高树勋、政协河北省委员会副主席齐璧亭等人也在会上讲了话。参加成立大会的有一百二十六人。
