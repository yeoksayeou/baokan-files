Title: Large Roof Panel Pre-Fabrication Plant (Picture)
Author: Guo Leizhen
Date: 1956-12-22
Page: 2

    Large Roof Panel Pre-Fabrication Plant
    On the construction site of the Wuhan Heavy Machine Tool Plant, the "dry-hard" concrete construction method was adopted in the project of making large roof panels, saving more than 40,000 jin of cement. The picture shows a large roof panel pre-fabrication plant.
