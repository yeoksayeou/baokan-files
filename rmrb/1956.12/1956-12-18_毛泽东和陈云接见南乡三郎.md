Title: Mao Zedong and Chen Yun Receive Nango Saburo
Author:
Date: 1956-12-18
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, 17th. Chairman Mao Zedong and Acting Premier Chen Yun received Nango Saburo, Chairman of the Japan-China Import and Export Association, at Qin Zheng Dian in Zhongnanhai on the evening of the 17th, and had a conversation that lasted more than an hour. (Picture attached)
	
	Chairman Mao, Acting Premier Chen Yun, and Nango Saburo (second from left) in a group photo. First from left is Liao Chengzhi. (Photo by Xinhua News Agency reporters Hou Bo and Lü Houmin)

### 毛泽东和陈云接见南乡三郎

1956-12-18
第1版()
专栏：

    毛泽东和陈云接见南乡三郎
    新华社17日讯　毛泽东主席和陈云代总理，17日晚在中南海勤政殿接见了日中输出入协会理事长南乡三郎，进行了一个多小时的谈话。（附图片）
    毛主席、陈云代总理和南乡三郎（左第二人）合影。左第一人为廖承志。　新华社记者　侯波、吕厚民摄
