Yunnan's Largest Thermal Power Plant Breaks Ground

(Our dispatch from Kunming, December 8) Yunnan Province's second automated thermal power plant - the Kunming Power Plant - has fully commenced construction.

　　This power plant is the largest thermal power plant in Yunnan Province. Once completed, this power plant will satisfy the production and lighting needs of newly built and expanded factories and mines in Kunming, Kunyang, and other areas.

　　The complete set of production equipment and architectural design for this power plant are designed and manufactured by the Czechoslovak Republic. To expedite the completion of the plant, the Czechoslovak government will send experts to guide the construction.

　　Currently, various construction materials are being transported from all over the country. Some production equipment has already set off from Prague. (Yunnan Daily Editorial Department)

### 云南最大的火电厂开工

1956-12-10
第2版()
专栏：

　  云南最大的火电厂开工
    本报昆明8日电　云南省第二座自动化火力发电厂——昆明电厂已经全面开工。
    这个电厂是云南省最大的火力发电厂。这个电厂建成以后，将满足昆明、昆阳等地新建、扩建厂矿的生产和照明需要。
    这个电厂的全套生产设备和建筑设计都是由捷克斯洛伐克共和国设计和制造的。为了迅速完成建厂任务，捷克斯洛伐克政府将派专家指导施工。
    目前，各种建筑物资正从全国各地运来。部分生产设备也已经从布拉格启运。（云南日报编辑部）
