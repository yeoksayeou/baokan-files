Title: Our National People's Congress Delegation Leaves Prague for Ostrava
Author:
Date: 1956-12-09
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Prague, December 8th. The Chinese National People's Congress Delegation and the Beijing Municipal People's Committee Delegation left Prague on the evening of December 7th to visit Ostrava.

Prior to their departure for Ostrava, the delegation visited the Sverdlov Stalingrad Machine Manufacturing Factory in Prague on the 5th, toured some of Prague's famous historical sites, and attended a performance of the opera "The Bartered Bride" that evening.

On the 6th, the delegation members traveled by car to Pilsen, a western industrial city, where they visited the massive Lenin Heavy Machinery Manufacturing Plant and the Pilsen Brewery. The delegates returned to Prague that evening.

On the morning of the 7th, a portion of the delegation, led by Deputy Head Li Jishen, visited the "Czech-Chinese Friendship" United Agricultural Cooperative in the village of Vinarice. During the visit, delegation member and Deputy Mayor of Beijing, Le Songsheng, conveyed the greetings of the members of the "Sino-Czech Friendship" Agricultural Production Cooperative of Zhangguozhuang, Beijing, to the members of the cooperative. He also introduced the recent developments and achievements of the "Sino-Czech Friendship" Agricultural Production Cooperative of Zhangguozhuang.

On the same day, another part of the delegation visited the Zverotice United Agricultural Cooperative and some of Prague's famous historical sites. Some delegation members held discussions with members of the Czechoslovak National Assembly to learn about the working experience of Czechoslovak parliamentarians.

### 我全国人民代表大会代表团　离布拉格到俄斯特拉发访问

1956-12-09
第1版()
专栏：

    我全国人民代表大会代表团
　　离布拉格到俄斯特拉发访问
    新华社布拉格8日电　中国全国人民代表大会代表团和北京市人民委员会代表团12月7日晚间离布拉格前往俄斯特拉发访问。
    在赴俄斯特拉发以前，代表团曾经在5日参观了布拉格的斯克德斯大林格勒机器制造工厂，游览了布拉格的一些名胜古迹，并且在当天晚上观看了歌剧“被出卖的新嫁娘”。
    6日，代表团人员乘汽车到西部工业城市比尔森访问，他们在这里参观了巨大的列宁重型机器制造工厂和比尔森啤酒厂。代表们当晚回到布拉格。
    7日上午，一部分团员在副团长李济深率领下访问了维纳尔日采村的“捷中友好”统一农业合作社。在访问过程中，代表团团员、北京市副市长乐松生向这个合作社的社员转达了北京张郭庄“中捷友好”农业生产合作社社员们的问候，并且向他们介绍了张郭庄“中捷友好”农业生产合作社最近的发展和成就。
    这一天，另一部分团员访问了兹维罗提采统一农业合作社和布拉格的一些名胜古迹。有部分团员曾经同捷克斯洛伐克国民议会的议员举行了座谈，了解了捷克斯洛伐克议员的工作经验。
