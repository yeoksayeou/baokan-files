　　Fruit Trees in Mountain Areas
    Since the implementation of mountain area production planning in Dongye Township, Linxian County, Henan Province, agriculture, forestry, animal husbandry, and water conservancy have all greatly developed, bringing great benefits to the farmers. This is Shi Yudian, a nationally renowned tree planting expert from the township, and the fruit trees he has cultivated. (Photo by Qin Hanguang)
