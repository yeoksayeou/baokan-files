Title: Guo Moruo Receives Stansgate and Wife
Author:
Date: 1956-12-20
Page: 1

    Xinhua News Agency, December 18th: Guo Moruo, Vice Chairman of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress, received Lord Stansgate, a member of the British House of Lords, and his wife this morning.
    Present at the reception were Zhang Su and Liao Chengzhi, members of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress, and Qian Duansheng, Vice President, and Wu Maosun, Secretary-General of the Chinese People's Institute of Foreign Affairs.

### 郭沫若接见斯坦斯盖特和夫人

1956-12-20
第1版()
专栏：

    郭沫若接见斯坦斯盖特和夫人
    新华社18日讯　全国人民代表大会常务委员会副委员长郭沫若今天上午接见了英国上议院议员斯坦斯盖特子爵和他的夫人。
    接见时在座的，有全国人民代表大会常务委员会委员张苏、廖承志，中国人民外交学会副会长钱端升、秘书长吴茂荪。
