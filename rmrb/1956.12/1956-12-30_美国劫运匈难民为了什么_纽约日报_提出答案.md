Title: What is the US's Purpose in Abducting Hungarian Refugees? "New York Daily News" Provides the Answer
Author:
Date: 1956-12-30
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, December 28th: According to TASS on December 27th: The "New York Daily News" suggests forming an anti-communist army out of Hungarian refugees, a suggestion that reveals the purpose of the United States in transporting Hungarian refugees to America.

The newspaper stated: "After being trained in the United States, they will join our armed forces. Once a war breaks out, they will become a combat unit of our army."

The "New York Daily News" said, "This force is like a dagger ready to stab the throat of communism and insert itself into Budapest."

### 美国劫运匈难民为了什么  “纽约日报”提出答案

1956-12-30
第5版()
专栏：

    美国劫运匈难民为了什么
    “纽约日报”提出答案
    新华社28日讯　据塔斯社27日讯：“纽约日报”建议把匈牙利难民组成一支反共军队，这个建议透露了美国把匈牙利难民运到美国的目的。
    这家报纸说：“在美国经过训练后，他们将加入我们的武装部队。一旦发生战争，他们便成为我们军队的一支作战部队。”
    “纽约日报”说，“这支部队就像是一把准备刺入共产党咽喉和插入布达佩斯的匕首。”
