Title: Learning Atomic Energy Radioactive Logging Technology
Author:
Date: 1956-12-31
Page: 2

Xinhua News Agency, Lanzhou, December 30th. The first atomic energy radioactive logging training class in China, which opened on October 18, 1956 at the Yumen Oilfield, concluded on December 24th.

Forty people participated in this training class, coming from units such as the Ministry of Geology, the Ministry of Petroleum Industry, the Xinjiang Petroleum Administration Bureau, the Qinghai Petroleum Exploration Bureau, the Yumen Oilfield, the Beijing Petroleum Institute, and the Beijing Geological College. Under the guidance of Soviet atomic energy radioactive logging expert Mr. Mukher, they studied for more than two months and also interned in a number of oil wells, exploration wells, and water injection wells in the Laojunmiao Oilfield. They have initially mastered the basic theory of atomic energy radioactive logging, and most of them can independently operate under normal circumstances.

In 1957, petroleum exploration departments in Xinjiang, Qinghai, and other places will apply this new technology to petroleum exploration work. Recently, the Yumen Oilfield has formulated a specific plan to use this new technology in dozens of oil wells in order to quickly determine the geological conditions underground and deal with problems occurring in oil production in a timely manner.

### 学习原子能放射性测井技术

1956-12-31
第2版()
专栏：

    学习原子能放射性测井技术
    新华社兰州30日电　1956年10月18日在玉门油矿开办的我国第一个原子能放射性测井训练班，在12 月24日结业。
    参加这个训练班学习的有四十人，他们来自地质部、石油工业部、新疆石油管理局、青海石油勘探局、玉门油矿、北京石油学院、北京地质学院等单位。他们在苏联著名的原子能放射性测井专家穆赫尔指导下，经过两个多月的学习，并且在老君庙油田的一批油井、探井、注水井中进行了实习，已经初步掌握了原子能放射性测井的基本理论，有大部分人在正常情况下可以独立进行操作了。
    1957年，新疆、青海等地的石油勘探部门就要把这种新的技术运用到石油勘探工作中去。最近玉门油矿已经制定出具体计划，准备在几十口油井中使用这种新技术，以便迅速探明井下的地质情况和及时处理采油当中发生的问题。
