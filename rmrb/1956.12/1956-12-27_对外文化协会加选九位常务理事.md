Title: Chinese People's Association for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries Adds Nine Executive Directors
Author:
Date: 1956-12-27
Page: 1
　　Xinhua News Agency, 26th. The Standing Council of the Chinese People's Association for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries held its fifth meeting today. Chu Tunan reported on the visit of the Chinese Art Troupe to Chile, Brazil, Uruguay, and Argentina. The meeting also elected Bao Erhan, Ma Yuhuai, Liu Geping, Iminov, Qian Duansheng, Zhou Jianren, Zhou Shujia, Da Punsheng, and Qian Weichang as executive directors.

### 对外文化协会加选九位常务理事

1956-12-27
第1版()
专栏：

    对外文化协会加选九位常务理事
    新华社26日讯　中国人民对外文化协会常务理事会今天举行第五次会议。楚图南在会上报告了中国艺术团访问智利、巴西、乌拉圭、阿根廷等四个国家的情况，会议并加选了包尔汉、马玉槐、刘格平、依敏诺夫、钱端升、周建人、周叔迦、达浦生、钱伟长等九人为常务理事。
