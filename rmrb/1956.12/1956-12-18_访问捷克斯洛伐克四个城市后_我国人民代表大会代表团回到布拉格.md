Title: Chinese National People's Congress Delegation Returns to Prague After Visiting Four Czechoslovak Cities
Author:
Date: 1956-12-18
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Prague, December 17th. After visiting the four cities of Ostrava, Bratislava, Gottwaldov, and Karlovy Vary, the delegation of the National People's Congress of China and the delegation of the Beijing Municipal People's Committee returned to Prague on the 16th.
    The delegation visited the Tosta Textile Factory near Karlovy Vary on the 14th. This factory weaves Silon textiles.
    The delegates were very interested in the beautiful Silon textiles.
    On the 15th, the delegation visited the Karlovy Vary Glass Factory. The delegates admired the exquisite glass artworks manufactured by the factory.

### 访问捷克斯洛伐克四个城市后  我国人民代表大会代表团回到布拉格

1956-12-18
第1版()
专栏：

    访问捷克斯洛伐克四个城市后
    我国人民代表大会代表团回到布拉格
    新华社布拉格17日电　中国全国人民代表大会代表团和北京市人民委员会代表团在访问了俄斯特拉发、布拉迪斯拉发、哥特瓦尔德夫和卡罗维发利四个城市以后，16日回到布拉格。
    代表团14日在卡罗维发利附近参观了托斯塔纺织厂。这个工厂是织造西龙纺织品的。
    代表们对美丽的西龙纺织品感到很大兴趣。
    15日代表团参观了卡罗维发利玻璃厂，代表们赞赏这个工厂所制造的精美的玻璃艺术品。
