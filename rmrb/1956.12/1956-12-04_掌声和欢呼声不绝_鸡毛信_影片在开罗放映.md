Title: Applause and Cheers Never Cease: "Chicken Feather Letter" Film Screened in Cairo
Author:
Date: 1956-12-04
Page: 5
　  Applause and Cheers Never Cease
　　“Chicken Feather Letter” Film Screened in Cairo
    Xinhua News Agency, 3rd Dispatch from Cairo: The Chinese film "Chicken Feather Letter" is being screened in Egypt and has been warmly welcomed by audiences.
    The film began public screenings on November 24th at the "Odeon" cinema in Cairo, with four screenings daily. The final screening on the first day was a special screening for government officials and military officers, with Chinese Ambassador to Egypt Chen Jiakang and embassy staff also invited to attend.
    Approximately one thousand people watched this special screening. The audience's emotions were extremely enthusiastic, with applause, cheers, and sighs erupting in the theater as the plot unfolded. After the film ended, the audience surged towards Ambassador Chen Jiakang amidst thunderous applause to express their congratulations, and shouted slogans such as "Long live Egyptian-Chinese friendship" and "Long live peace."

### 掌声和欢呼声不绝　“鸡毛信”影片在开罗放映

1956-12-04
第5版()
专栏：

　  掌声和欢呼声不绝
　　“鸡毛信”影片在开罗放映
    新华社3日讯　开罗消息：中国影片“鸡毛信”在埃及放映，受到观众的热烈欢迎。
    这部电影从11月24日起在开罗“奥德昂”电影院开始公映，每天放映四场，第一天最后一场招待了政府官员和军官，中国驻埃及大使陈家康和使馆人员也应邀出席。
    观看这场招待映出的共约一千人。观众情绪极为热烈，随着剧情的发展，场内不断发出掌声、欢呼声、叹息声。电影映完后，观众在雷动的鼓掌声中拥向陈家康大使表示祝贺，并且高呼“埃中友好万岁”、“和平万岁”等口号。
