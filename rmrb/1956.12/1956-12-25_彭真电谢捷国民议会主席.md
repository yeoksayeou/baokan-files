Title: Peng Zhen Cables Thanks to Chairman of the Czechoslovak National Assembly

Author: 
Date: 1956-12-25
Page: 1

    Xinhua News Agency, Bucharest, December 24th. Peng Zhen, head of the Chinese National People's Congress delegation, sent a telegram from Bucharest on the 23rd to Mr. Fierlinger, Chairman of the Czechoslovak National Assembly, expressing his heartfelt gratitude for the warm help and cordial reception the delegation received during its visit to Czechoslovakia.

### 彭真电谢捷国民议会主席

1956-12-25
第1版()
专栏：

    彭真电谢捷国民议会主席
    新华社布加勒斯特24日电　中国全国人民代表大会代表团团长彭真23日从布加勒斯特打电报给捷克斯洛伐克国民议会主席费林格，对代表团在捷克斯洛伐克访问期间受到的热情帮助和亲切的接待表示衷心的感谢。
