Title: French Troops Interfere in Tunisian Internal Affairs and Kill Residents; Tunisian Deputy Prime Minister Lodges Strong Protest
Author:
Date: 1956-12-02
Page: 6

　　French Troops Interfere in Tunisian Internal Affairs and Kill Residents
　　Tunisian Deputy Prime Minister Lodges Strong Protest
    Xinhua News Agency, December 1st: TASS reported on November 30th: The Tunisian State Secretariat for Information issued a statement regarding the French troops' forceful establishment of anti-aircraft sentry posts and the shooting deaths of Tunisian people, stating that Tunisian Deputy Prime Minister, Bey Ladgham, has lodged a strong protest with the French representative against this act of "French armed forces once again dangerously interfering in the internal affairs of independent Tunisia."
    The announcement emphasized that the presence of French armed forces in Tunisia poses a threat to the country's sovereignty and security.
    Xinhua News Agency, November 30th: TASS, Paris, November 29th: French newspapers reported that Tunisia issued an official statement saying that residents of Cap Bon and French soldiers clashed on the night of the 28th. The statement said that forty French soldiers attempted to build an anti-aircraft sentry post in Djebel-Draa without the permission of the Tunisian authorities. Local residents opposed the French military's transgression. At that time, "this team of French soldiers, supported by seven military aircraft, opened fire on ordinary residents. As a result, one Tunisian was killed and four were seriously injured, including one woman."

### 法军干涉突尼斯内政并杀害居民　突尼斯副首相提出强硬抗议

1956-12-02
第6版()
专栏：

　  法军干涉突尼斯内政并杀害居民
　　突尼斯副首相提出强硬抗议
    新华社1日讯　据塔斯社11月30日讯：突尼斯新闻国务秘书处就法军强行建立防空哨和开枪打死突尼斯人民这件事发表公告说，突尼斯副首相伯伊·拉德哈姆对“法国武装部队又一次危险地干涉独立的突尼斯的内政”这种行为，已经向法国代表提出坚决的抗议。
    公告强调指出，法国武装部队驻扎在突尼斯，对国家的主权和安全都是一种威胁。
    据新华社30日讯　塔斯社巴黎29日讯：法国报纸报道，突尼斯发表官方公报说，28日夜里波恩海角的居民和法国士兵发生了冲突。公报说，有四十名法国士兵不经突尼斯当局允许就想在杰别里—德拉新这个地方修筑防空哨。当地居民反对法军的越轨行动。就在那个时候，“这队法国士兵在七架军用飞机的支援下对普通居民开了火。结果，一个突尼斯人被打死，四人受重伤，其中有一个妇女”。
