Author: Yu Chang, Shu Lin
Date: 1956-12-18
Page: 4

    The Most Precious Year

    In Lu'an County, Shanxi Province, there was an eligible young man named Chen Shucheng. He submitted five applications to the conscription bureau in a row, and sent his physical examination results and photos to the conscription bureau. One of his application letters read: "I am a member of the Communist Youth League, exactly eighteen years old, four chi five cun tall, and weighs one hundred and five jin, completely meeting the requirements. Please approve my enlistment. 1956 is the most precious year of my life, don't let me miss this opportunity!"
                    (Yu Chang, Shu Lin)

### 最宝贵的一年
余昌  书林
1956-12-18
第4版()
专栏：

    最宝贵的一年
    山西省潞安县有个适龄青年陈树成，他一连向兵役局提出五次申请，并把身体检查的结果和照片，都寄给兵役局。有一次的申请信上写着：我是一个青年团员，整整十八岁，身高四尺五，体重一百零五斤，完全合乎条件，请批准我入伍。1956年是我生命中最宝贵的一年，千万不要让我错过这一机会！
                    （余昌书林）
