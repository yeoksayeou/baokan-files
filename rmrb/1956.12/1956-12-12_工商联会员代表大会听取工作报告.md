Title: Federation of Industry and Commerce Member Representatives' Congress Hears Work Report
Author:
Date: 1956-12-12
Page: 1

    Xinhua News Agency, 11th: The Second Congress of Members Representatives of the All-China Federation of Industry and Commerce continued its meeting this morning.
    Rong Yiren, Vice Chairman of the All-China Federation of Industry and Commerce, delivered a work report to the congress on behalf of the First Executive Committee on the past three years. This report summarized the series of works carried out by the All-China Federation of Industry and Commerce in uniting and educating the broad masses of industrialists and businessmen to actively accept socialist transformation in the three years since its establishment, as well as the experiences gained in these works, examined the shortcomings in the work, and put forward suggestions for future work.
    Gong Tianmin, Vice Chairman of the All-China Federation of Industry and Commerce, and Wu Yunshan, Director of the Finance Committee, also made explanations to the congress on the "Constitution of the Federation of Industry and Commerce" (draft) and the three-year financial report of the All-China Federation of Industry and Commerce, respectively.
    Starting this afternoon, the representatives attending and present at the meeting will conduct group discussions on the reports made to the congress on the 10th by Chen Shutong, Chairman of the All-China Federation of Industry and Commerce, and Bo Yibo, Vice Premier of the State Council.

### 工商联会员代表大会听取工作报告

1956-12-12
第1版()
专栏：

    工商联会员代表大会听取工作报告
    新华社11日讯　中华全国工商业联合会第二届会员代表大会今天上午继续开会。
    中华全国工商业联合会副主任委员荣毅仁代表第一届执行委员会向大会作了三年来的工作报告。这个报告总结了全国工商联成立三年来在团结、教育广大工商业者积极接受社会主义改造方面所进行的一系列工作，以及在这些工作中取得的经验，检查了工作中的缺点，并对今后工作提出了建议。
    全国工商联副主任委员巩天民、财务委员会主任吴韫山也分别向大会作了关于“工商业联合会章程”（草案）的说明和中华全国工商业联合会三年财务报告。
    从今天下午开始，出席和列席会议的代表，就中华全国工商业联合会主任委员陈叔通、国务院副总理薄一波10日向大会作的报告进行分组讨论。
