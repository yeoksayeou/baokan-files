Title: Romanian Government Delegation Leaves Soviet Union for Home

Author:

Date: 1956-12-05
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, 4th Dispatch from TASS: The government delegation of the Romanian People's Republic, headed by Chairman of the Council of Ministers, Chivu Stoica, left Moscow this morning for home.

Those who saw them off at the airport included Chairman of the Council of Ministers of the Soviet Union, Nikolai Bulganin, First Deputy Chairman of the Council of Ministers of the Soviet Union, Anastas Mikoyan and Maxim Saburov, Minister of Foreign Affairs of the Soviet Union, Dmitri Shepilov, Chairman of the Council of Ministers of the Russian Federation, Mikhail Yasnov, and ministers of various ministries.

Also seeing them off at the airport were the Romanian Ambassador to the Soviet Union, Nicolae Dăscălescu, diplomatic envoys from various countries to the Soviet Union, as well as Soviet and foreign journalists.

Stoica delivered a brief speech at the airport.

### 罗政府代表团离苏回国

1956-12-05
第5版()
专栏：

    罗政府代表团离苏回国
    新华社4日讯　据塔斯社莫斯科讯：以部长会议主席斯托伊卡为首的罗马尼亚人民共和国政府代表团今天早晨离开莫斯科回国。
    到机场欢送的有苏联部长会议主席布尔加宁、苏联部长会议第一副主席米高扬和萨布罗夫，苏联外交部长谢皮洛夫，俄罗斯联邦部长会议主席雅斯诺夫和各部部长。
    到机场送行的还有罗马尼亚驻苏联大使达列亚、各国驻苏联的外交使节以及苏联和外国的新闻记者。
    斯托伊卡在机场上发表了简短的讲话。
