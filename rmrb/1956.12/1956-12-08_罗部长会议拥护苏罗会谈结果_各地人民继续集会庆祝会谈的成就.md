Title: Romanian Council of Ministers Endorses Results of Soviet-Romanian Talks; People in Various Regions Continue to Gather and Celebrate the Achievements of the Talks
Author:
Date: 1956-12-08
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, Bucharest, 7th – According to TASS: The Council of Ministers of the Romanian People's Republic held a meeting on the 6th. The Chairman of the Council of Ministers, Stoica, delivered a report on the results of the talks in Moscow between the Romanian government delegation and the Soviet government delegation.

The Romanian Council of Ministers unanimously endorsed the results of the Soviet-Romanian talks. Ministers of the various government departments, when speaking on Stoica's report, emphasized that the joint statement of the Soviet and Romanian government delegations has significant political importance, and the economic assistance that the Soviet Union decided to provide to Romania during this meeting will greatly promote Romania's future development.

At the same time, meetings and mass rallies are continuing to be held throughout Romania to celebrate the successful conclusion of the Soviet-Romanian talks. On the 6th, the working people of Bucharest held a meeting at the Soviet-Romanian Friendship Building in the capital and listened to a report on the Soviet-Romanian talks given by Moghioros, the Deputy Director of the Romanian News Agency. In the city of Constanta, the working people also held a celebration rally. Vlada, a worker from the "Socialist Competition Textile Factory," said at the meeting: The Soviet people have once again expressed their brotherly friendship to us. The Soviet-Romanian joint statement reflects the close cooperation between our two countries, based on equality, respect for national sovereignty, and mutual assistance.

### 罗部长会议拥护苏罗会谈结果　各地人民继续集会庆祝会谈的成就

1956-12-08
第5版()
专栏：

　　罗部长会议拥护苏罗会谈结果
　　各地人民继续集会庆祝会谈的成就
    新华社7日讯　据塔斯社布加勒斯特讯：罗马尼亚人民共和国部长会议在6日举行会议，部长会议主席斯托伊卡在会上作了关于罗马尼亚政府代表团和苏联政府代表团在莫斯科会谈结果的报告。
    罗马尼亚部长会议一致拥护罗苏会谈的结果。政府各部部长在就斯托伊卡的报告发言的时候着重指出，苏联和罗马尼亚两国政府代表团的会谈声明具有重大的政治意义，苏联在这次会谈中决定给予罗马尼亚的经济援助将会大大促进罗马尼亚今后的发展。
    和这同时，罗马尼亚全国各地还在继续举行会议和群众大会，庆祝罗苏会谈完满结束。布加勒斯特的劳动人民6日在首都的罗苏友好大厦举行会议，听了罗马尼亚通讯社副社长莫吉奥罗什作的关于罗苏会谈的报告。在康斯坦萨市，劳动人民也举行了庆祝大会。“社会主义竞赛纺织工厂”的工人符拉达在会上发言说：苏联人民又一次向我们表示了他们的兄弟友情。苏罗联合声明反映了我们两国之间存在着以平等、尊重国家主权和互助为基础的密切合作。
