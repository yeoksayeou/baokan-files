Title: Presidium of the Supreme Soviet of the Soviet Union Appoints Leaders of the State Economic Commission
Author:
Date: 1956-12-27
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, December 25th. TASS reports: According to the resolution of the Council of Ministers of the Soviet Union on the tasks of the State Economic Commission of the Soviet Union and the reorganization of its work, the Presidium of the Supreme Soviet of the Soviet Union made the following decisions:

　　(1) Appoint Belousov, First Deputy Chairman of the Council of Ministers of the Soviet Union, as Chairman of the State Economic Commission of the Soviet Union, and relieve Saburov, First Deputy Chairman of the Council of Ministers of the Soviet Union, from his post as Chairman of the State Economic Commission.

　　(2) Appoint Kosygin and Malyshev as First Deputy Chairmen of the State Economic Commission of the Soviet Union—Ministers of the Soviet Union, and appoint Khrunichev and Kucherenko as Deputy Chairmen of the State Economic Commission of the Soviet Union—Ministers of the Soviet Union; in addition, appoint Matskevich, Minister of Agriculture of the Soviet Union, and Benediktov, Minister of State Farms of the Soviet Union, as Deputy Chairmen of the State Economic Commission of the Soviet Union.

　　Therefore, the Presidium of the Supreme Soviet of the Soviet Union has relieved Kosygin, Malyshev, Khrunichev, Kucherenko, and Matskevich of their positions as Vice-Chairmen of the Council of Ministers of the Soviet Union.

### 苏联最高苏维埃主席团  任命国家经济委员会领导人

1956-12-27
第5版()
专栏：

    苏联最高苏维埃主席团
    任命国家经济委员会领导人
    新华社26日讯　据塔斯社莫斯科25日讯：苏联最高苏维埃主席团根据苏联部长会议关于苏联国家经济委员会的任务和改组其工作的决议，作出了下列决定：
    （一）任命苏联部长会议第一副主席别尔乌辛为苏联国家经济委员会主席，解除苏联部长会议第一副主席萨布罗夫的国家经济委员会主席职务。
    （二）任命柯西金和马利歇夫为苏联国家经济委员会第一副主席——苏联部长，任命赫鲁尼切夫和库切连科为苏联国家经济委员会副主席——苏联部长；此外，任命苏联农业部长马茨凯维奇和苏联国营农场部长别涅吉克托夫为苏联国家经济委员会副主席。
    因此，苏联最高苏维埃主席团解除了柯西金、马利歇夫、赫鲁尼切夫、库切连科和马茨凯维奇的苏联部长会议副主席职务。
