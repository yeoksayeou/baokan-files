Title: According to the Agreement Reached by China and Burma on Boundary Issues, Our Troops Withdraw from the Area West of the 1941 Line
Author:
Date: 1956-12-15
Page: 1

According to the Agreement Reached by China and Burma on Boundary Issues
Our Troops Withdraw from the Area West of the 1941 Line
    Xinhua News Agency, 14th—On November 9th, U Nu, Chairman of the Anti-Fascist People's Freedom League of Burma, and Zhou Enlai, Premier of the State Council of the People's Republic of China, jointly issued a joint press communiqué. On the Sino-Burmese boundary issue, both sides agreed that from the end of November 1956, Chinese troops would withdraw from the area west of the 1941 line, and Burmese troops would withdraw from Panma, Hpimaw, and Gawlum. This troop withdrawal will be completed by the end of 1956.
    Now, the Chinese troops have begun their withdrawal since November 22nd, and by December 12th, they had completely withdrawn from the area west of the 1941 line.

### 根据中缅双方就边界问题取得的协议  我军撤出1941年线以西地区

1956-12-15
第1版()
专栏：

    根据中缅双方就边界问题取得的协议
    我军撤出1941年线以西地区
    新华社14日讯　11月9日缅甸反法西斯人民自由同盟主席吴努同中华人民共和国国务院总理周恩来共同发表联合新闻公报，就中缅边界问题双方同意：从1956年11月底起中国军队将撤出1941年线以西地区，缅甸军队将撤出片马、岗房、古浪三个地方。这项撤军工作将在1956年年底完成。
    现在，中国军队已自11月22日开始撤退，到12月12日已经全部撤出1941年线以西地区。
