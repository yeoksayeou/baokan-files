Title: Autumn-Sown Tomatoes
Author:
Date: 1956-12-10
Page: 3
Xinhua News Agency, Nanchang, December 6th: The autumn-sown tomatoes in the experimental garden of the Jiangxi Provincial Agricultural Science Research Institute have been harvested, with a yield of 6,500 jin per mu.

　　Autumn-sown tomatoes are a scientific experiment conducted this year by the scientific workers of the Horticulture Department of this institute. They used the early-maturing Mayak variety. The successfully tested autumn-sown tomatoes are juicy and delicious, and the fruit color is also very good.

　　Jiangxi Province is accustomed to cultivating tomatoes in spring and summer, so after August, tomatoes are no longer available on the market. Autumn-sown tomatoes can allow people to eat fresh tomatoes from September to November.

### 秋天播种的蕃茄

1956-12-10
第3版()
专栏：

　  秋天播种的蕃茄
    新华社南昌6日电　江西省农业科学研究所试验园圃里秋天播种的蕃茄已经收获，每亩产量有六千五百斤。
    秋天播种蕃茄是这个研究所园艺系的科学工作者们今年做的一项科学试验。他们用的是早熟的马亚克品种。这次试验成功的秋播蕃茄都汁味鲜美，果色也很好。
    江西省习惯于春、夏季栽培蕃茄，所以过了8月，市场上就买不到蕃茄了。秋天播种蕃茄可使人们在9月到11月期间还能吃到新鲜蕃茄。
