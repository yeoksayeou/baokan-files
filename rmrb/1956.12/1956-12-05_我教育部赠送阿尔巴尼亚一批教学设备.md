Title: Ministry of Education of China Donates Teaching Equipment to Albania
Author:
Date: 1956-12-05
Page: 1

　　Xinhua News Agency, Tirana, December 3rd: The Albanian Telegraph Agency reported that a batch of school equipment donated by the Ministry of Education of the People's Republic of China to the Ministry of Education and Culture of the People's Republic of Albania has arrived in Albania, and the rest will be shipped next year.
    This gift includes thirty sets of physics laboratory equipment, thirty sets of chemistry laboratory equipment, thirty sets of biology laboratory equipment, and two hundred microscopes.
    The Albanian Telegraph Agency said that this generous act is another expression of the friendship of the great Chinese people towards the Albanian people.

### 我教育部赠送阿尔巴尼亚一批教学设备

1956-12-05
第1版()
专栏：

　　我教育部赠送阿尔巴尼亚一批教学设备
    新华社4日讯　阿尔巴尼亚通讯社地拉那3日讯：中华人民共和国教育部赠送给阿尔巴尼亚人民共和国教育和文化部的一批供学校用的设备，有一部分已经运到阿尔巴尼亚，其余的将于明年运到。
    这批赠送的礼物包括有三十套物理实验室设备、三十套化学实验室设备、三十套生物实验室设备和二百架显微镜等。
    阿尔巴尼亚通讯社说，这一慷慨举动是伟大的中国人民对阿尔巴尼亚人民的友谊的又一个表示。
