Title: Romania and China Host Book Exhibitions
Author:
Date: 1956-12-07
Page: 7

Xinhua News Agency, December 6th: According to a report by the Romanian News Agency, the Chinese Book Exhibition organized by the Romanian Ministry of Culture opened on the evening of December 5th at the Bucharest Central Bookstore.

Representatives from the Romanian Ministry of Culture, writers, and figures from the literary and artistic circles attended the opening ceremony. Mihal, the secretary of the Romanian Writers Association, delivered the opening speech.

Xinhua News Agency: The Xinhua Bookstore and the International Bookstore have decided to hold a "Romanian People's Republic Book Promotion Week" in the five cities of Beijing, Shanghai, Wuhan, Guangzhou, and Shenyang from December 1st to 10th. The International Bookstores and the Foreign Language Departments of Xinhua Bookstores in the five cities of Beijing and other cities will display and sell over 200 kinds of books published by the Romanian People's Republic in Romanian, Russian, English, French, and German, including social sciences, literature and art books, scientific and technical books on machinery, metallurgy, petroleum, and medicine, as well as children's books and records.

### 罗马尼亚和中国互相举办图书展览

1956-12-07
第7版()
专栏：

　  罗马尼亚和中国互相举办图书展览
    新华社6日讯　据罗马尼亚通讯社报道，罗马尼亚文化部举办的中国图书展览12月5日晚上在布加勒斯特中央书店开幕。
    罗马尼亚文化部的代表、作家和文艺界人士等参加了开幕式。罗马尼亚作家协会书记米哈尔致了开幕词。
    据新华社讯　新华书店和国际书店决定从12月1日到10日，在北京、上海、武汉、广州、沈阳五个城市举办“罗马尼亚人民共和国图书宣传旬”。北京等五个城市国际书店和新华书店外文部都将陈列和出售罗马尼亚人民共和国出版的罗马尼亚文、俄文、英文、法文、德文的社会科学、文学艺术书籍，机械、冶金、石油、医学方面的科学技术书籍，以及儿童读物、唱片等二百多种。
