Title: All-China Federation of Industry and Commerce Holds Enlarged Meeting
Author:
Date: 1956-12-02
Page: 4

　　Xinhua News Agency, December 1st—The First Executive Committee of the All-China Federation of Industry and Commerce held its Third (Enlarged) Meeting today.

　　This meeting will discuss the main documents for the upcoming Second Member Representative Congress of the All-China Federation of Industry and Commerce, as well as the Constitution (Draft) of the All-China Federation of Industry and Commerce, the Provisional Measures for Mutual Aid Funds for the Livelihood of Industrialists and Businessmen (Draft), and the Plan (Draft) for Convening a National Congress of Advanced Producers and Advanced Workers from Industrial and Commercial Circles.

　　Chen Shutong, Chairman of the Executive Committee of the All-China Federation of Industry and Commerce, and Vice-Chairmen Nan Hanchen and Gong Tianmin spoke at the meeting this morning. They reviewed the work of the Federation of Industry and Commerce over the past year. Nan Hanchen also discussed the influence of China's policy of peaceful transformation of capitalist industry and commerce abroad.

### 全国工商联举行扩大会议

1956-12-02
第4版()
专栏：

　　全国工商联举行扩大会议
    据新华社1日讯　中华全国工商业联合会第一届执行委员会今天举行第三次（扩大）会议。
    这次会议将讨论即将召开的中华全国工商业联合会第二届会员代表大会上的主要文件，以及工商业联合会章程（草案）、工商界生活互助金暂行办法（草案）、关于召开全国工商界先进生产者和先进工作者代表会议方案（草案）等。
    全国工商联执行委员会主任委员陈叔通和副主任委员南汉宸、巩天民今天上午在大会上发言。他们对一年来工商联的工作作了回顾。南汉宸并谈述了中国对资本主义工商业实行和平改造的政策在国外所起的影响。
