Title: Qian Junrui Hosts Farewell Banquet for Yugoslavian Film Delegation
Author:
Date: 1956-12-22
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, December 21st. Acting Minister of Culture Qian Junrui met with the entire Yugoslavian film delegation headed by Samujilo Amadaj today and held a banquet to bid them farewell.

The Yugoslav Ambassador to China, Popovic, was invited to attend the banquet.

During the Yugoslav Film Week, the Yugoslav film artists visited Beijing, Nanjing, Shanghai, Hangzhou, and Wuhan, and met with Chinese filmmakers and the general public. After completing their visits to various places, they have now returned to Beijing and will return to their country on the 22nd.

Yugoslav films have been warmly welcomed by Chinese audiences in twenty cities, including Beijing. According to statistics, in the first batch of ten major cities where the Film Week activities have ended, 2.6 million Chinese viewers have seen Yugoslav films.

### 钱俊瑞为南电影代表团饯行

1956-12-22
第1版()
专栏：

    钱俊瑞为南电影代表团饯行
    新华社21日讯　文化部代理部长钱俊瑞今天接见了以萨穆伊洛·阿玛达伊为首的南斯拉夫电影代表团全体人员，并且举行宴会为代表团饯行。
    南斯拉夫驻中国大使波波维奇应邀出席了宴会。
    南斯拉夫电影艺术家们在南斯拉夫电影周期间，访问了北京、南京、上海、杭州、武汉，并且同中国电影工作者和广大观众见了面。他们结束了在各地的访问以后，现已回到北京，将在22日回国。
    南斯拉夫影片在北京等二十个城市中受到中国观众的热烈欢迎，据统计，在第一批已经结束电影周活动的十个大城市中，有二百六十万中国观众看到了南斯拉夫影片。
