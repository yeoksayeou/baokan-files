Title: Japan Completes Ratification Procedures for the Japan-Soviet Joint Declaration; Soviet Deputy Minister of Foreign Affairs Travels to Japan to Exchange Instruments of Ratification
Author:
Date: 1956-12-10
Page: 6

　　Japan Completes Ratification Procedures for the Japan-Soviet Joint Declaration
    Soviet Deputy Minister of Foreign Affairs Travels to Japan to Exchange Instruments of Ratification
    Xinhua News Agency, Tokyo, December 9th: Tokyo dispatch: The Emperor of Japan certified the instrument of ratification for the Japan-Soviet Joint Declaration on the restoration of diplomatic relations between Japan and the Soviet Union yesterday.
    The Japanese Diet and government had already ratified the Japan-Soviet Joint Declaration a few days ago. According to the Japanese Constitution, the Emperor's certification is one of the procedures for ratifying diplomatic documents. Now, Japan has completed all the procedures for ratifying the Japan-Soviet Joint Declaration.
    Xinhua News Agency, December 9th: TASS, Moscow, December 9th: Soviet Deputy Minister of Foreign Affairs Fedrin departed from Moscow for Japan on December 5th. In Tokyo, Fedrin will exchange instruments of ratification with Japan regarding the Soviet-Japan Joint Declaration on the restoration of diplomatic relations between the Soviet Union and Japan.

### 日本完成批准日苏宣言手续  苏联外交部副部长前往日本交换批准书

1956-12-10
第6版()
专栏：

　　日本完成批准日苏宣言手续
    苏联外交部副部长前往日本交换批准书
    据新华社9日讯　东京消息：日本天皇在昨天认证了关于日苏两国恢复邦交的日苏联合宣言的批准书。
    日本国会和政府在前几天已经批准了日苏联合宣言。据日本宪法规定，天皇的认证是批准外交文件的手续之一。现在，日本已完成了批准日苏联合宣言的全部手续。
    新华社9日讯　据塔斯社莫斯科9日讯：苏联外交部副部长费德林已经在12月5日从莫斯科启程去日本。费德林在东京将同日本互换关于苏日恢复邦交的苏日联合宣言批准书。
