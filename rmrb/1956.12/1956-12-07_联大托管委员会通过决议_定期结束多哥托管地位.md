Title: UN Trusteeship Council Passes Resolution to End Togoland Trusteeship on Schedule
Author:
Date: 1956-12-07
Page: 5

　　UN Trusteeship Council Passes Resolution
　　To End Togoland Trusteeship on Schedule
　　Xinhua News Agency, 6th: New York dispatch: The United Nations General Assembly Trusteeship Council passed a resolution on December 5th, deciding to end the trusteeship status of Togoland, currently administered by Britain, on March 6th of next year, and agreed to merge Togoland into the Gold Coast when the Gold Coast gains independence in March of next year.
　　Togoland was a German colony before World War I. After World War I, it became a mandated territory of Britain and France. After World War II, it was placed under the United Nations Trusteeship System and administered by these two colonial countries, Britain and France.
　　The Trusteeship Council's resolution was passed with fifty-eight votes in favor, none against, and eleven abstentions. The original proposal was put forward by eleven countries including India and Burma.

### 联大托管委员会通过决议　定期结束多哥托管地位

1956-12-07
第5版()
专栏：

　  联大托管委员会通过决议
　　定期结束多哥托管地位
    新华社6日讯　纽约消息：联合国大会托管委员会12月5日通过一项决议，决定在明年3月6日结束目前由英国管理的托管领土多哥的托管地位，并且同意在黄金海岸在明年3月获得独立的时候把多哥合并到黄金海岸去。
    多哥在第一次世界大战前是德国的殖民地，在第一次世界大战后变成英法两国的委任统治地，在第二次世界大战后被置于联合国托管制度下，而由英法这两个殖民国家管理。
    托管委员会的决议是以五十八票赞成、没有人反对和十一票弃权的情况下通过的。原提案是由印度和缅甸等十一个国家提出的。
