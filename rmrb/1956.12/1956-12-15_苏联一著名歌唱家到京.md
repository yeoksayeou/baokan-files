Title: A Famous Soviet Singer Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1956-12-15
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, December 14th: Boris Gmyrya, Soviet People's Artist and Stalin Prize laureate, a bass singer, arrived in Beijing this afternoon.

The pianist and piano accompanist, Osilin, and Gmyrya's wife also arrived with him.

They are here for a tour of China. Gmyrya will hold his first concert in China in Beijing on the 18th.

### 苏联一著名歌唱家到京

1956-12-15
第1版()
专栏：

    苏联一著名歌唱家到京
    据新华社14日讯　苏联人民演员、斯大林奖金获得者、男低音歌唱家鲍里斯·格米里亚今天下午来到北京。
    钢琴伴奏、钢琴家奥西林以及格米里亚的夫人也同来。
    他们是来中国巡回演出的。格米里亚18日将在北京举行来中国以后的第一次音乐会。
