Title: Karachi Journalists Association Hosts Chinese Journalists
Author:
Date: 1956-12-26
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, Karachi, 23rd – The Karachi Journalists Association held a reception on the evening of the 22nd to welcome the Chinese journalists who accompanied Premier Zhou Enlai on his visit to Pakistan.

    Gong Peng, Director of the Information Department of the Chinese Ministry of Foreign Affairs, and all the Chinese journalists attended the reception.

    Editors-in-chief of various Karachi newspapers, heads of the Pakistan News Bureau, and journalists attended the reception.

### 卡拉奇新闻记者协会招待我国新闻工作者

1956-12-26
第1版()
专栏：

    卡拉奇新闻记者协会招待我国新闻工作者
    据新华社卡拉奇23日电　卡拉奇新闻记者协会22日晚举行招待会，欢迎随同周恩来总理到巴基斯坦访问的中国新闻工作者。
    参加招待会的有中国外交部新闻司司长龚澎和全体中国记者。
    卡拉奇各报主编、巴基斯坦新闻局负责人和记者参加了招待会。
