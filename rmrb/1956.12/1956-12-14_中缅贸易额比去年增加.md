Title: China-Burma Trade Volume Increases Compared to Last Year
Author: 
Date: 1956-12-14
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, December 13th - A spokesperson for the Ministry of Foreign Trade told our reporter today, "The trade volume between China and Burma in the first ten months of this year has increased by 64% compared to the same period last year. The progress of trade between the two countries is satisfactory."

The spokesperson also announced that China and Burma are about to conduct negotiations for the third annual rice exchange protocol.

He then said, "For the past two years, in addition to importing 150,000 tons of rice annually from Burma, China has also imported a considerable amount of rubber and cotton. China exports hundreds of commodities to Burma, mainly industrial products such as cotton yarn, silk, knitwear, and building materials."

### 中缅贸易额比去年增加

1956-12-14
第1版()
专栏：

    中缅贸易额比去年增加
    新华社13日讯　对外贸易部一位发言人今天对本社记者说，“中国和缅甸的贸易额在今年头十个月中比去年同期增长了64％。两国贸易进展的情况是令人满意的。”
    这位发言人还宣布中缅两国即将进行第三个年度大米换货议定书的谈判。
    他接着说，“两年来，中国除从缅甸每年进口十五万吨大米以外，还进口了相当数量的橡胶、棉花。中国向缅甸出口的商品计有数百种，其中主要是棉纱、丝绸、针织品、建筑器材等工业品。”
