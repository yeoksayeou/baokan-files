Title: Pakistani Political Leaders Call on People to Warmly Welcome Chinese Guests
Author:
Date: 1956-12-21
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Karachi, December 20th: Pakistani political leaders have called on the people to warmly welcome Chinese guests.
West Pakistan's Chief Minister and leader of the Republican Party, Sahib Khan, issued a statement in Lattal on the 18th, welcoming Premier Zhou Enlai's visit to Pakistan.
The statement said: "We and China have very old and brotherly friendly ties. It is our close neighbor, and we share a common border in the north. China and Pakistan can help each other in many ways in pursuing common goals and aspirations. Our two countries face some of the same problems, such as raising the living standards of our people, building our national economies, and contributing to the cause of international peace and justice."
The statement said: "Mr. Zhou Enlai's visit will undoubtedly help promote closer cooperation between our two countries in all aspects."
Sahib Khan said, "I believe that wherever Mr. Zhou Enlai goes, the people of West Pakistan will give him a warm welcome."

Xinhua News Agency, Karachi, December 19th: All parts of Pakistan are preparing to grandly welcome Premier Zhou Enlai. Premier Zhou Enlai will arrive in Karachi tomorrow from Yangon for a ten-day visit to Pakistan.
All central government agencies in Karachi will stop work at 1:00 PM on the 20th to welcome Premier Zhou Enlai's visit to Pakistan.
Inayatullah Hasan, General Secretary of the All Pakistan People's Alliance, issued a statement here on the 18th, requesting the people to warmly welcome Premier Zhou Enlai.
The reception committee established to welcome Premier Zhou Enlai has held a meeting. The meeting studied the work of various sub-committees responsible for planning and preparing the program. The decoration committee has decided to build twenty-one archways in some areas and hang flags and bunting along the roads Premier Zhou Enlai and his party will pass. Residents will also respond to the call to decorate their homes with flags and streamers to show their welcome.

According to newspaper reports, in Lahore, the capital of West Pakistan, arrangements are currently being made to erect archways and hang bunting to welcome Premier Zhou Enlai. A large number of Chinese national flags are being manufactured for decorating streets, buildings, and holding welcome activities.
The government building where the Chinese guests will stay is currently being repainted, and many wonderful welcome activities are being arranged. These activities include a banquet in the famous Shalimar Gardens built during the Mughal era, visits to industrial areas and historical sites, and a state banquet to welcome Zhou Enlai.

Hyderabad and Peshawar are also rapidly making preparations to give Premier Zhou Enlai a grand welcome. In Hyderabad, the entire city is being decorated, and archways are being built.

East Pakistan's Chief Minister Rahman and Bhashani, Chairman of the East Pakistan People's Muslim League, called on the people in Dhaka on the evening of the 17th to give Premier Zhou Enlai a warm and highly disciplined welcome. They called on all those in East Pakistan who can come to participate in the welcome meeting to do so.

### 巴基斯坦政界领袖  号召人民热烈欢迎中国客人

1956-12-21
第1版()
专栏：

    巴基斯坦政界领袖
    号召人民热烈欢迎中国客人
    新华社卡拉奇20日电　西巴基斯坦首席部长、共和党领袖萨希布汗18日在拉台尔发表了一项声明，欢迎周恩来总理访问巴基斯坦。
    声明说：“我们和中国有着非常古老的情同手足的友好联系，它是我们的近邻，我们在北部和它有共同的边界。中国和巴基斯坦在追求共同的目标和愿望上可以在许多方面相互帮助。我们两个国家面临着一些同样的问题，如提高我们人民的生活水准，建设我们的国民经济，对国际和平和正义事业作出贡献等。”
    声明说：“周恩来先生的访问无疑地将会有助于促进我们两国之间在各方面的更密切的合作。”
    萨希布汗说，我相信，无论周恩来先生到那里，西巴基斯坦人民将给予他热烈的欢迎。
    新华社卡拉奇19日电　巴基斯坦各地正准备隆重欢迎周恩来总理。周恩来总理将在明天从仰光到达卡拉奇，对巴基斯坦进行为时十天的访问。
    卡拉奇的各中央政府机关20日下午一时将停止工作，以便欢迎周恩来总理到巴基斯坦来访问。
    全巴基斯坦人民联盟总书记伊纳雅图拉·哈桑18日在这里发表声明，要求人民热烈欢迎周恩来总理。
    为欢迎周恩来总理而设立的招待委员会已经举行了一次会议。会议研究了负责计划和筹备节目的各小组委员会的工作。装饰委员会已经决定在一些地区搭二十一座牌楼，并且在周恩来总理一行经过的道路上挂起旗帜和彩旗。居民也将响应号召用旗帜和花彩来装饰他们的住宅，以示欢迎。
    据报纸报道，在西巴基斯坦首府拉合尔，目前正在安排为欢迎周恩来总理搭牌楼和挂彩旗的事宜，大量的中国国旗正在制造出来，以供装饰街道、建筑物和举行欢迎活动的时候应用。
    目前正在为中国贵宾们住的政府大厦进行粉刷，并且在安排许多精彩的欢迎活动。这些活动包括在著名的莫卧儿时代建造的夏利马尔花园中举行一次宴会，到工业地区和历史胜地去访问，和举行一次欢迎周恩来的国宴。
    海得拉巴和白沙瓦目前也正在迅速进行准备工作，以便给周恩来总理盛大的欢迎。在海得拉巴，整个城市都在进行装饰，并且还在搭牌楼。
    东巴基斯坦首席部长拉赫曼和东巴基斯坦人民穆斯林联盟主席巴沙尼17日晚上在达卡号召人民给周恩来总理以有高度纪律性的热烈欢迎。他们号召东巴基斯坦各地所有能够来参加欢迎会的人都来参加。
