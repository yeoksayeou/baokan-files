Title: Sino-Czech 1957 Cultural Cooperation Plan Signed
Author:
Date: 1956-12-10
Page: 1

　　Xinhua News Agency, Prague, December 9th. The 1957 executive plan for the Sino-Czech Cultural Cooperation Agreement was signed in Prague on the 8th.

　　According to this plan, the two countries will send writers, musicians, artists, journalists, and cultural relic preservation workers to visit each other in 1957. A Chinese National Painting Exhibition, a Chinese Silk Exhibition, and a Dunhuang Mural Exhibition will be held in Czechoslovakia. A Czechoslovak Book and Illustration Exhibition and an Artistic Photography Exhibition will be held in China. China will send graduate students to study in Czechoslovakia, and Czechoslovakia will send young sinologists and sinology graduates to China for observation and internship.

　　Chen Kehan, Vice Minister of Culture of the People's Republic of China, signed on behalf of the Chinese side, and Kahuda, Minister of Education and Culture of Czechoslovakia, signed on behalf of the Czechoslovak side.

### 中捷1957年文化合作计划签字

1956-12-10
第1版()
专栏：

　　中捷1957年文化合作计划签字
    新华社布拉格9日电　中捷文化合作协定1957年执行计划8日在布拉格签字。
    根据这个计划，1957年两国将互派文学家、音乐家、美术家、记者和文物保管工作者等进行访问。在捷克斯洛伐克将举行中国国画展览、中国蚕丝展览和敦煌壁画展览。在中国将举行捷克斯洛伐克书籍和插图展览以及艺术照片展览。中国将派遣研究生到捷克斯洛伐克学习，捷克斯洛伐克将派遣青年汉学家和汉学毕业生到中国考察和实习。
    代表中国方面签字的是中华人民共和国文化部副部长陈克寒，代表捷克斯洛伐克方面签字的是捷教育和文化部部长卡胡达。
