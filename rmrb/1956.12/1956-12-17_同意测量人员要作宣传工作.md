Author:
Date: 1956-12-17
Page: 2
Response to Criticism

    Surveyors Must Do Propaganda Work
    A letter from the Fourth Triangulation Team of the Yangtze River Water Conservancy Commission: After the People's Daily published a letter on August 30th entitled "Surveyors Must Do Propaganda Work," we immediately examined our past work. In the future, we must strengthen our propaganda work and eliminate the masses' concerns.

### 同意测量人员要作宣传工作

1956-12-17
第2版()
专栏：对批评的反应

    同意测量人员要作宣传工作
    长江水利委员会第四三角测量队来信：人民日报8月30日刊载了
  “测量人员要做宣传工作”一信后，我们即对过去的工作进行了检查。今后我们一定要加强宣传工作，消除群众的疑虑。
