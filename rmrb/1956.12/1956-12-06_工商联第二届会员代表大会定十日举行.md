Title: Federation of Industry and Commerce Second Member Representative Congress Scheduled for the 10th
Author:
Date: 1956-12-06
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, 5th December: The Third Plenary Session of the First Executive Committee of the All-China Federation of Industry and Commerce decided to convene the Second Member Representative Congress of the All-China Federation of Industry and Commerce in Beijing on the 10th of this month. This meeting began on the 1st and concluded today. The meeting adopted the documents to be submitted to the Second Member Representative Congress for discussion, and reviewed and approved the work report of the All-China Federation of Industry and Commerce's First Executive Committee since its Second Plenary Session a year ago.

### 工商联第二届会员代表大会定十日举行

1956-12-06
第1版()
专栏：

　　工商联第二届会员代表大会定十日举行
    新华社5日讯　中华全国工商业联合会第一届执行委员会第三次会议决定在本月10日在北京召开中华全国工商业联合会第二届会员代表大会。这次会议从1日开始，在今天结束。会议通过了提请第二届会员代表大会讨论的文件，并审查和通过了全国工商联第一届执委会举行第二次会议以来的一年工作报告。
