Title: Hungarian Government Raises Miners' Wages
Author:
Date: 1956-12-05
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, Budapest, December 4th. According to a report by the Hungarian News Agency on December 3rd, the Hungarian Revolutionary Worker-Peasant Government passed a resolution to increase the wages of miners, effective December 1st of this year. The resolution pointed out that this measure was taken because miners play a particularly important role in the national economy.

### 匈政府提高矿工工资

1956-12-05
第5版()
专栏：

　　匈政府提高矿工工资
    新华社布达佩斯4日电　据匈牙利通讯社3日报道，匈牙利工农革命政府通过了一项决议，决定从今年12月1日起提高采矿工人的工资。决议中指出，采取这个措施是因为采矿工人在国家经济生活中起着特别重要的作用。
