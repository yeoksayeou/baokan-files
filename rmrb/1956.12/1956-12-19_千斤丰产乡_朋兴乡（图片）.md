Title: Thousand-Jin High-Yield Township – Pengxing Township (Picture)
Author: Jia Hui
Date: 1956-12-19
Page: 3

    Thousand-Jin High-Yield Township – Pengxing Township
  　The twelve advanced agricultural production cooperatives in Pengxing Township, Xiaogan County, Hubei Province, achieved a harvest of over 1,000 jin per mu of rice this year. The Heping Cooperative in the township achieved a maximum yield of 1,280 jin per mu of single-season late japonica rice, earning it the title of provincial high-yield model unit this year. The picture shows cooperative members distributing the abundant late japonica rice harvest. Photo by Jia Hui (Hubei Daily)

### 千斤丰产乡——朋兴乡（图片）
贾惠
1956-12-19
第3版()
专栏：

    千斤丰产乡——朋兴乡
  　湖北省孝感县朋兴乡的十二个高级农业生产合作社，今年获得了稻谷亩产千斤的丰收。该乡和平社种植的一季晚粳最高产量达到一千二百八十斤，获得了今年全省丰产模范单位的奖励。这是社员们正在分配丰收的晚粳。　贾惠摄（湖北日报社稿）
