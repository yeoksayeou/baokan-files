    The Yongqing Tibetan Agricultural Production Cooperative in Ge’ka Township, Daofu County, Garzê Tibetan Autonomous Prefecture, Sichuan Province, achieved a bumper harvest this year after its establishment. The cooperative members all received more grain than last year.
    Left picture: Female members of the Yongqing Tibetan Agricultural Production Cooperative threshing grain.
    Right picture: Xiao Zhuyu (right) and other female members of the Yongqing Tibetan Agricultural Production Cooperative are selecting harvested highland barley seeds, striving for an even greater bumper harvest. (Xinhua News Agency reporter Sun Zhongjing photo)
