Title: Port Said Reconstruction Committee Established
Author:
Date: 1956-12-18
Page: 6

    Xinhua News Agency, Cairo, December 17th: The Port Said Reconstruction Committee has been established. The Egyptian government has appointed the members of this committee. The committee is headed by Egyptian Minister of State Planning, Boghdadi.
    The committee has 194 engineers and technical assistants. It has initially determined that 1,000 houses in Port Said were destroyed by bombing. The committee's reconstruction plan includes building 3,200 houses in Port Said.

### 塞得港复兴委员会成立

1956-12-18
第6版()
专栏：

    塞得港复兴委员会成立
    新华社17日讯　开罗消息：塞得港复兴委员会已经成立。埃及政府任命了这个委员会的委员。委员会由埃及国家计划部长波格达迪领导。
    这个委员会拥有一百九十四名工程师和技术助理员。它初步查明塞得港有一千幢房屋被炸毁。委员会的复兴计划包括在塞得港修建三千二百幢房屋。
