Title: Pineau Shouts that France is Not Ready to Withdraw Troops
Author:
Date: 1956-12-02
Page: 6

　　Xinhua News Agency, London, December 1st: French Foreign Minister Pineau stated on November 30th that the French government is not prepared to withdraw its troops from Port Said unconditionally. He said, "If certain political issues are not resolved and certain military precautionary measures are not followed, then (French troops) do not intend to withdraw (from Egypt)."
    Pineau had just held talks with British Foreign Secretary Lloyd. He also said that Britain and France would adopt a common stance on the issue of troop withdrawal and any issues arising from the future situation in the Middle East.
    After making his remarks, Pineau immediately left London for Paris.

### 比诺叫嚣法国不准备撤军

1956-12-02
第6版()
专栏：

　　比诺叫嚣法国不准备撤军
    新华社1日讯　伦敦消息：法国外交部长比诺11月30日表示，法国政府不准备无条件地从塞得港撤军。他说：“要是某些政治问题没有解决，某些军事警戒措施没有遵行，那（法军）就不打算撤离（埃及）。”
    比诺刚刚同英国外交大臣劳埃德进行了会谈。他还说，英法在撤军问题和中东未来局势所引起的任何问题上，都会采取共同的态度。
    比诺在发表谈话后，随即从伦敦动身回到巴黎去。
