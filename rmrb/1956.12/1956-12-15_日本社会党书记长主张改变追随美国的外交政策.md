Title: Japanese Socialist Party Secretary-General Advocates Changing Foreign Policy of Following the United States
Author:
Date: 1956-12-15
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, Tokyo, 14th: Japanese Socialist Party Secretary-General Inejiro Asanuma issued a statement today regarding Tanzan Ishibashi's election as the new president of the Liberal Democratic Party. He requested that Ishibashi revise the past government's policy of following the United States and adjust Japan's relations with China. Asanuma also requested that Ishibashi not consider revising the constitution or implementing rearmament.

### 日本社会党书记长主张改变追随美国的外交政策

1956-12-15
第6版()
专栏：

    日本社会党书记长主张
    改变追随美国的外交政策
    据新华社14日讯　东京消息：日本社会党书记长浅沼稻次郎今天就石桥湛山当选自由民主党新总裁一事发表谈话，要求石桥修改过去的政府所推行的追随美国的政策，调整日本同中国的邦交。浅沼还要求石桥不要考虑修改宪法和实行重整军备。
