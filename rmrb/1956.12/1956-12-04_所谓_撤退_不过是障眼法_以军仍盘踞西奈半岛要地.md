Title: The So-called "Withdrawal" Is Just a Smokescreen; Israeli Troops Still Occupy Key Areas of the Sinai Peninsula
Author:
Date: 1956-12-04
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, 3rd Dispatch. According to the Associated Press from Tel Aviv: Israeli forces still occupy the main central areas of the Sinai Peninsula and the southern tip of the Sinai Peninsula controlling the Gulf of Aqaba.

The cities still in Israeli hands include Abu Aweigila and El Arish, two cities that are important highway and transportation junctions. Israel also occupies the city of Bir Gafgafa in the center of the peninsula on the road to Ismailia, which from a military point of view is the most important city.

An Israeli military spokesman said on December 2 that Israel has so far withdrawn three brigades, or 10,000 to 12,000 people, from the Sinai Peninsula.

The spokesman said that the remaining Israeli troops in the Sinai Peninsula have withdrawn at least 50 kilometers behind the east bank of the Suez Canal.

When the Israeli Ambassador to the United States delivered a speech in Cleveland on December 1, he openly advocated establishing a so-called "buffer zone" in the Sinai Peninsula, Egyptian territory.

### 所谓“撤退”不过是障眼法　以军仍盘踞西奈半岛要地

1956-12-04
第6版()
专栏：

　　所谓“撤退”不过是障眼法
　　以军仍盘踞西奈半岛要地
    新华社3日讯　据美联社从特拉维夫报道：以色列部队仍然占领着西奈半岛的主要中心地区和控制亚喀巴湾的西奈半岛南端。
    仍在以色列手中的城市有很重要的公路和交通交叉地阿布阿韦吉拉和埃尔阿里希两个城市。以色列还占领着半岛中部在通往伊士美利亚的公路上的比尔加菲加法城，这个地方从军事观点看来是最重要的城市。
    以色列军方发言人12月2日说，以色列到目前为止已经从西奈半岛撤退了三个旅，即一万人到一万二千人。
    这个发言人说，在西奈半岛余下的以色列军队已经从苏伊士运河东岸至少向后撤了五十公里。
    以色列驻美国大使12月1日在克利夫兰发表演说时，公开主张在埃及领土西奈半岛建立一个所谓“缓冲地区”。
