Title: Egyptian Undersecretary of Foreign Affairs Receives Chinese Journalists Delegation
Author:
Date: 1956-12-11
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency dispatch: The Chinese Journalists Delegation arrived in Cairo on the 8th. The delegation is visiting Egypt in accordance with the 1956 China-Egypt Cultural Cooperation Agreement.

The delegation members are: Jiang Yuanchun from "People's Daily," Yan Biao from "Xinhua News Agency," Gao Tian from "Guangming Daily," and Che Muqi from "People's China."

The Egyptian Undersecretary of Foreign Affairs, Mustafa Youssef, received the Chinese Journalists Delegation on December 8th.

Youssef warmly welcomed them and promised to provide all conveniences to enable them to obtain comprehensive and truthful information about the Egyptian people's resistance to aggression and construction.

### 埃及外交部次长接见我新闻工作者代表团

1956-12-11
第5版()
专栏：

    埃及外交部次长
    接见我新闻工作者代表团
    据新华社讯　中国新闻工作者代表团已经在8日到达开罗。代表团是执行中国埃及1956年文化合作协定前往埃及进行访问的。
    代表团团员有：“人民日报”的蒋元椿，“新华通讯社”的言彪，“光明日报”的高天和“人民中国”的车慕奇。
    埃及外交部次长穆斯塔法·优素福12月8日接见了中国新闻工作者代表团。
    优素福热烈地欢迎他们，并且答应将提供一切便利来使他们获得关于埃及人民抵抗侵略和建设的全面和真实的情况。
