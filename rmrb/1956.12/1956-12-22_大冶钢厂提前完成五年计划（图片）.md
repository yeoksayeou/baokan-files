    Year-end Triumphant News

    Daye Steel Plant Completes Five-Year Plan Ahead of Schedule
    The Daye Steel Plant has completed its first Five-Year Plan one year and two months ahead of schedule in terms of total output value, pig iron, steel, steel products, and all other indicators. The picture below shows a large batch of steel products ready for shipment from the factory, supplying the needs of construction projects in various regions. (Xinhua News Agency reporter Yan Zhennan)
