Title: Sino-Polish 1957 Cultural Cooperation Agreement Plan Signed
Author:
Date: 1956-12-23
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, Warsaw, December 21st. The 1957 Executive Plan of the Sino-Polish Cultural Cooperation Agreement was signed in Warsaw on the 20th. Chen Kehan, head of the Chinese cultural delegation and Vice Minister of Culture, and Zolkiewski, head of the Polish cultural delegation and Minister of Higher Education of Poland, signed the executive plan on behalf of their respective governments.

The executive plan stipulates that China and Poland will send educational inspection groups to each other, exchange university and graduate students, and send acrobatic troupes, writers, artists, musicians, journalists, etc. to visit or perform this year.

Wang Bingnan, Chinese Ambassador to Poland, held a reception on the 19th for the visit of the Chinese cultural delegation to Poland.

### 中波1957年文化合作协定计划签字

1956-12-23
第1版()
专栏：

    中波1957年文化合作协定计划签字
    新华社华沙21日电　中波文化合作协定1957年执行计划20日在华沙签字。中国文化代表团团长、文化部副部长陈克寒和波兰文化代表团团长、波兰高等教育部长佐基夫斯基代表双方政府在执行计划上签字。
    执行计划中规定：中波将在这年内互派教育考察团，互换大学生和研究生，互派杂技团、作家、美术家、音乐家、记者等访问或演出。
    中国驻波兰大使王炳南在19日为中国文化代表团的访问波兰举行了招待会。
