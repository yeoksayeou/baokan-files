Title: Yugoslav and Polish Party Representatives Begin Talks
Author:
Date: 1956-12-21
Page: 5

　　Xinhua News Agency, Warsaw, December 20th: The Polish News Agency reported that a delegation from the Central Committee of the League of Communists of Yugoslavia, led by Vukmanović, arrived in Warsaw on the 19th.

　　Upon arrival in Warsaw, the delegation immediately held talks with representatives of the Central Committee of the Polish United Workers' Party, Sambrowski, Indrikhovsky, Morawski, and Matwin.

### 南波两党代表开始会谈

1956-12-21
第5版()
专栏：

    南波两党代表开始会谈
    据新华社华沙20日电　据波兰通讯社报道，南斯拉夫共产主义者联盟中央委员会代表团在伏克曼诺维奇率领下于19日到达华沙。
    代表团到达华沙后即同波兰统一工人党中央委员会代表萨姆布罗夫斯基、英德里霍夫斯基、莫拉夫斯基和马特温举行会谈。
