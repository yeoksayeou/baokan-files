Title: National Interest Outweighs Personal Honor: Egyptian Writer Returns French Medal
Author:
Date: 1956-12-19
Page: 6

    National Interest Outweighs Personal Honor
    Egyptian Writer Returns French Medal
    Xinhua News Agency, Cairo, December 17th (TASS): According to newspaper reports, the famous Egyptian writer Dr. Taha Hussein has returned the Legion of Honor medal awarded to him by the French government. He said that he was unwilling to accept a medal from the French government, which was committing a criminal aggression against his homeland.

### 祖国利益胜过个人荣誉  埃名作家退还法国勋章

1956-12-19
第6版()
专栏：

    祖国利益胜过个人荣誉
    埃名作家退还法国勋章
    新华社18日讯　塔斯社开罗17日讯：据报纸报道，埃及著名作家塔加·侯赛尼博士把法国政府奖给他的荣誉军团勋章退还给法国。他说，他不愿意要向他的祖国进行罪恶进攻的法国政府的勋章。
