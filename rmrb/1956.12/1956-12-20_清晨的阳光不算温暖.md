Title: The Morning Sun is Not Warm
Author: Hao Sumin
Date: 1956-12-20
Page: 8

    The morning sun is not warm
    (Mongolian Proverb)
    The morning sun is not warm,
    Momentary ease is not happiness.
    A working fool
    Is stronger than a clever man sleeping in bed;
    A person who can travel around
    Is stronger than a capable person sitting on a worn-out mattress.
    A diligent person only talks about practical work,
    A lazy person is greedy for tea and meals.
    Selected and translated by Hao Sumin

### 清晨的阳光不算温暖
郝苏民
1956-12-20
第8版()
专栏：

    清晨的阳光不算温暖
    （蒙古谚语）
    清晨的阳光不算温暖，
    瞬息的安逸不算幸福。
    工作着的傻子，
    比睡在床上的聪明人强；
    能周游四方的人，
    比坐烂褥子的能干人强。
    勤恳的人只讲实干，
    懒惰的人贪馋茶饭。
    郝苏民选译
