Title: Jordan Again Urges Iraq to Withdraw Troops

Author:

Date: 1956-12-07
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, Damascus, 6th - Newspapers report that the Jordanian government sent a second note to the Iraqi government on December 4th, demanding the immediate withdrawal of Iraqi troops from Jordan.

### 约旦再促伊拉克撤军

1956-12-07
第6版()
专栏：

　  约旦再促伊拉克撤军
    新华社6日讯　大马士革消息：据报纸报道，约旦政府12月4日第二次照会给伊拉克政府，要求伊拉克军队立即撤出约旦。
