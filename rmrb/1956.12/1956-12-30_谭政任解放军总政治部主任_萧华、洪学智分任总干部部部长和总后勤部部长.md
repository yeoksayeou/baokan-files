Title: Tan Zheng Appointed Director of the General Political Department of the PLA, Xiao Hua and Hong Xuezhi Appointed Minister of the General Cadre Department and Minister of the General Logistics Department Respectively
Author:
Date: 1956-12-30
Page: 1

    Xinhua News Agency, December 29th: According to the decision of the 52nd meeting of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress, Chairman of the People's Republic of China Mao Zedong has appointed Tan Zheng as Director of the General Political Department of the Chinese People's Liberation Army, Xiao Hua as Minister of the General Cadre Department of the Chinese People's Liberation Army, and Hong Xuezhi as Minister of the General Logistics Department of the Chinese People's Liberation Army.

### 谭政任解放军总政治部主任  萧华、洪学智分任总干部部部长和总后勤部部长

1956-12-30
第1版()
专栏：

    谭政任解放军总政治部主任
    萧华、洪学智分任总干部部部长和总后勤部部长
    新华社29日讯　中华人民共和国主席毛泽东根据全国人民代表大会常务委员会第五十二次会议决定，任命谭政为中国人民解放军总政治部主任，萧华为中国人民解放军总干部部部长，洪学智为中国人民解放军总后勤部部长。
