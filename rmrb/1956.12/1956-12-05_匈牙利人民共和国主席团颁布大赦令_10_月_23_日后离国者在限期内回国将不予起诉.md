Title: Presidium of the Hungarian People's Republic Issues Amnesty Decree: Those Who Left the Country After October 23 Will Not Be Prosecuted If They Return Within the Deadline

Author:

Date: 1956-12-05
Page: 5

　　Hungarian People's Republic Presidium Issues Amnesty Decree
　　Those Who Left the Country After October 23 Will Not Be Prosecuted If They Return Within the Deadline
　　Xinhua News Agency, Prague, 4th. The Presidium of the Hungarian People's Republic has promulgated a decree announcing an amnesty for all Hungarian citizens who left Hungary for abroad after October 23.
　　The decree states: Those who left Hungary without permission from October 23 until the promulgation of the amnesty decree will not be prosecuted if they voluntarily return to Hungary before March 31 next year.

### 匈牙利人民共和国主席团颁布大赦令　10月23日后离国者在限期内回国将不予起诉

1956-12-05
第5版()
专栏：

　　匈牙利人民共和国主席团颁布大赦令
　　10月23日后离国者在限期内回国将不予起诉
    新华社布拉格4日电　匈牙利人民共和国主席团已经颁布了一项法令，宣布对所有那些在10月23日以后离开匈牙利到国外去的匈牙利公民实行大赦。
    法令说：那些从10月23日到大赦令颁布的时候为止，未经许可而越境离开匈牙利的人，如果他们自动地在明年3月31日以前返回匈牙利，就不对他们起诉。
