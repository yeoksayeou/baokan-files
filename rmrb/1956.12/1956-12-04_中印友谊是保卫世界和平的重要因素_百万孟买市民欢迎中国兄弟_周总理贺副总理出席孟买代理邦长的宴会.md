Title: Sino-Indian Friendship is an Important Factor in Safeguarding World Peace; One Million Bombay Citizens Welcome Chinese Brothers; Premier Zhou and Vice Premier He Attend Banquet Hosted by Acting Governor of Bombay

Author:

Date: 1956-12-04
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Bombay, December 3rd. Premier Zhou Enlai, Vice Premier He Long, and their entourage arrived in Bombay, the largest city on India's west coast, on the afternoon of December 2nd. Over one million Bombay citizens lined the fifteen-mile route from the airport to the Governor's residence, waving Chinese and Indian flags, cheering the Chinese guests, and showering them with countless fragrant flower petals.

Premier Zhou Enlai was warmly welcomed upon arrival at Santa Cruz Airport by the Acting Governor of Bombay State, Chagla, Chief Minister Chavan, and prominent figures from all walks of life in Bombay. Premier Zhou Enlai reviewed a guard of honor composed of the Indian Navy.

Speaking at the welcoming ceremony at the airport, Premier Zhou Enlai said that the friendship between the Chinese and Indian peoples, built on the Five Principles, will continue to develop and strengthen day by day. Both China and India have recently gained freedom from colonial oppression, and both countries hope to engage in peaceful construction. Premier Zhou Enlai pointed out that in this regard, China will learn from India's experience.

Premier Zhou Enlai concluded by shouting "Long live Panchsheel!" "Long live the friendship between the Chinese and Indian peoples!" "Hindi-Chini bhai bhai!" (Indians and Chinese are brothers). The welcomers responded with thunderous cheers.

In his welcoming speech, Bombay Chief Minister Chavan said that India and China both have a major task in the international family – to ensure the happiness and well-being of the people of the world.

After the welcoming ceremony, Premier Zhou Enlai traveled by car to the Governor's residence. The roadsides were lined with Chinese and Indian flags. Colorful welcome arches along the route bore the slogan "Hindi-Chini bhai bhai." The roadsides were densely packed with welcomers, who enthusiastically cheered and threw flowers at the Chinese guests.

Xinhua News Agency, Bombay, December 3rd. On the evening of December 2nd, Premier Zhou Enlai, Vice Premier He Long, and their entourage, at the invitation of the Acting Governor of Bombay, Chagla, attended a pleasant banquet on Malabar Hill in Bombay and watched performances of dance and music by famous Indian artists.

The banquet to entertain the Chinese guests was held in the Bombay State Government Building overlooking the Arabian Sea. Countless colorful electric lights hanging in the trees added enchanting brilliance to the various tropical plants, palm trees, and lawns in the large garden.

After the banquet, Premier Zhou and others were invited into a large hall of white marble illuminated by bright crystal chandeliers. There, Waheeda Rehman, Sitara Devi, and other famous Indian dancers and singers performed and sang moving Indian dances and songs.

Governor Chagla delivered a welcoming speech at the banquet. He pointed out the historical friendship between India and China. He said that although the two countries have different political systems, they have common problems to solve, and they both have much to learn from each other. He believed that there should be constant contact between the two countries in all aspects – cultural, social, and economic. He concluded by saying: "With China and India united in friendship and harmony, Asia can look forward to an era of peace and prosperity with confidence."

Premier Zhou Enlai delivered a reply. He praised Bombay for its important role in India's economic, cultural, and artistic development, as well as in the Indian people's struggle for national independence.

Premier Zhou Enlai said that in recent years, India has made significant progress in many aspects of its construction. China can learn many things from India. He also emphasized: "The friendship between India and China is a very important factor in safeguarding world peace."

Other distinguished guests attending the banquet included the Indian Ambassador to China, R.K. Nehru, and his wife, and the Chinese Ambassador to India, Pan Zili, and his wife.

### 中印友谊是保卫世界和平的重要因素　百万孟买市民欢迎中国兄弟  周总理贺副总理出席孟买代理邦长的宴会

1956-12-04
第1版()
专栏：

    中印友谊是保卫世界和平的重要因素
　　百万孟买市民欢迎中国兄弟
　  周总理贺副总理出席孟买代理邦长的宴会
    新华社孟买3日电　周恩来总理、贺龙副总理和他们的随行人员在12月2日下午到达印度西海岸的最大城市孟买。一百多万孟买市民在他们乘汽车从机场到邦长官邸的十五英里长的道路两旁，挥舞着中印两国的国旗向中国贵宾们欢呼，并且把无数芬芳的花瓣撒在他们的身上。
    周恩来总理在到达圣克卢斯机场时，受到孟买邦代理邦长查格拉、首席部长恰范和孟买各界著名人士的热烈欢迎。周恩来总理检阅了由印度海军组成的仪仗队。
    周恩来总理在机场上举行的欢迎仪式上讲话时说，中印两国人民建立在五项原则的基础上的友谊，将一天天继续发展和加强。中印两国都在最近从殖民主义压迫下取得了自由，两国都希望进行和平建设。周恩来总理指出，在这方面，中国将学习印度的经验。
    周恩来总理最后高呼“潘查希拉万岁！”“中印两国人民的友谊万岁！”“印地秦尼巴依巴依！”（印中人民是兄弟）。欢迎的人报以雷鸣般的欢呼声。
    孟买首席部长恰范在致欢迎词的时候说，印度和中国在国际大家庭中都负有重大的任务——保证世界人民的幸福生活。
    在举行欢迎仪式之后，周恩来总理乘汽车前往邦长官邸。经过的道路两旁到处飘扬着中印两国的国旗。路上彩色的欢迎牌楼上写着“印地秦尼巴依巴依”的口号。路的两旁密密层层地站满了欢迎的人，他们热情地向中国贵宾们欢呼和抛掷鲜花。
    新华社孟买3日电　12月2日晚上，周恩来总理、贺龙副总理和他们的随行人员应印度孟买代理邦长查格拉的邀请，在孟买马拉巴山上参加了愉快的宴会，并且观看了印度著名艺术家们表演的舞蹈和音乐。
    这个招待中国贵宾的宴会是在俯瞰阿拉伯海的孟买邦政府大厦里举行的。挂在树上的无数五彩缤纷的电灯使大花园中的各种热带植物、棕梠树和草坪增加了迷人的光彩。
    宴会之后，周总理等被请到明亮的水晶石灯照耀着的白色大理石的大厅中去。在那里，瓦希达·雷赫曼、西塔拉·德维以及印度其他著名的舞蹈家和歌唱家们表演和歌唱了动人的印度舞蹈和歌曲。
    查格拉邦长在宴会上致欢迎辞。他指出了印度和中国之间的历史性的友谊。他说，虽然两国的政治制度不同，但是它们有共同的问题需要解决，它们彼此都有很多东西可以互相学习。他认为两国之间在各方面——文化、社会和经济方面——应当进行不断的接触。他最后说：“由于中国和印度友好和睦地团结在一起，亚洲可以充满信心地期待着和平和繁荣的时代的到来。”
    周恩来总理致答辞。他赞扬孟买在印度的经济、文化和艺术方面，以及在印度人民争取民族独立的斗争中所起的重大作用。
    周恩来总理说，最近几年来，印度在它的建设的许多方面都获得了重大的进步。中国可以向印度学习许多东西。他还强调说：“印度和中国之间的友谊是保卫世界和平的一个非常重要的因素。”
    出席宴会的其他贵宾有印度驻中国大使拉·库·尼赫鲁和夫人以及中国驻印度大使潘自力和夫人。
