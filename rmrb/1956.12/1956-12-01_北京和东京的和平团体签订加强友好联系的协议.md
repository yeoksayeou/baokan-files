Title: Peace Organizations in Beijing and Tokyo Sign Agreement to Strengthen Friendly Ties
Author:
Date: 1956-12-01
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, 28th - The Beijing Branch of the Chinese People's Committee for World Peace and the visiting delegation of the Tokyo Metropolitan Peace Conference of Japan today signed an agreement on exchanging information and sending delegations for mutual visits.

　　Sun Fuling, Deputy Secretary-General of the Beijing Branch of the Chinese People's Committee for World Peace, and Hachiro Miura, head of the visiting delegation of the Tokyo Metropolitan Peace Conference of Japan, signed the agreement.

　　After signing, Sun Fuling said: The agreement signed today not only expresses the strong desire of the people of Tokyo and Beijing for peace and mutual exchange, but will also promote peace and friendship between the two peoples. Miura Hachiro said: Signing this very meaningful agreement is something we have long desired. The friendship between the Japanese and Chinese people is constantly strengthening. At this auspicious ceremony, I congratulate the further development of friendship between the people of Japan and China.

　　Responsible persons from various departments of the Chinese People's Committee for World Peace, Shu Sheyu, Vice Chairman of the Beijing Branch, standing committee members, committee members, and deputy secretaries-general of the branch attended today's agreement signing ceremony.

　　All members of the visiting delegation of the Tokyo Metropolitan Peace Conference of Japan attended the agreement signing ceremony. Japanese peace figures in Beijing, including Matsumoto Jiichiro, Miyazaki Seimin, and Kameda Togo, also attended the agreement signing ceremony.

　　Xinhua News Agency, 28th - Agreement between the Beijing Branch of the Chinese People's Committee for World Peace and the visiting delegation of the Tokyo Metropolitan Peace Conference of Japan. The full text of the agreement is as follows:

　　The Beijing Branch of the Chinese People's Committee for World Peace and the visiting delegation of the Tokyo Metropolitan Peace Conference of Japan, exchanged views in a cordial atmosphere on issues such as further strengthening friendly ties and exchanging experiences in the future. They believe that the citizens of the capitals of China and Japan, based on the Five Principles of Peaceful Coexistence, increasing mutual understanding, and strengthening economic and cultural exchanges, are not only conducive to promoting friendship between China and Japan and normalizing diplomatic relations, but also to defending peace in Asia and the world.

　　Based on the above views, we have reached the following agreement:

　　1. Exchange of information, including children's works, photographs, artwork, publications, letters, etc.

　　2. Send delegations for mutual visits.

### 北京和东京的和平团体签订加强友好联系的协议

1956-12-01
第1版()
专栏：

　　北京和东京的和平团体
　　签订加强友好联系的协议
    据新华社28日讯　中国人民保卫世界和平委员会北京市分会同日本东京都和平会议访华代表团今天签订了关于交换资料、互派代表团访问的协议。
    在协议上签字的是中国人民保卫世界和平委员会北京市分会副秘书长孙孚凌、日本东京都和平会议访华代表团团长三浦八郎。
    签字后，孙孚凌说：今天签订的这个协议不仅表达了东京和北京两地人民要求和平要求互相往来的强烈愿望，并且将对两国人民的和平和友谊起促进作用。三浦八郎说：签订这个非常有意义的协定，是我们很久以来就想望的事情。日本人民和中国人民的友谊在不断地加强。我在这个可庆幸的仪式上，祝贺日中两国人民友谊更进一步发展。
    参加今天协议签字仪式的有中国人民保卫世界和平委员会各部门负责人，北京市分会副主席舒舍予，分会的常务委员、委员和副秘书长。
    日本东京都和平会议访华代表团全体团员参加了协议签字仪式，日本在京和平人士松本治一郎、宫崎世民、龟田东伍等也参加了协议签字仪式。
    新华社28日讯　中国人民保卫世界和平委员会北京市分会和日本东京都和平会议访华代表团的协议。协议全文如下：
    中国人民保卫世界和平委员会北京市分会与日本东京都和平会议访华代表团，就今后双方进一步加强友好联系和交流经验等问题在融洽的气氛中交换了意见，认为中日两国首都市民，根据和平共处五项原则，增进相互间的了解，加强经济文化交流，不但是有利于中日两国的友谊的促进和邦交的正常化，而且也是有利于保卫亚洲和世界和平的。
    我们根据以上见解，达成如下协议：
    一、交换资料，包括儿童作品、照片、艺术品、刊物信件等。
    二、互派代表团互相访问。
