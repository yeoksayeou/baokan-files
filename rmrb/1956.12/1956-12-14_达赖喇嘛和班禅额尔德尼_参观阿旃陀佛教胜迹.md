Title: The Dalai Lama and Panchen Erdeni Visit the Ajanta Buddhist Monuments
Author:
Date: 1956-12-14
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, New Delhi, December 13th: The Dalai Lama and Panchen Erdeni, along with their entourage, arrived at the famous Ajanta Caves on December 12th from Pune via Jalgaon.

At this Buddhist site, they visited all twenty-six caves carved by Indian artists and laborers from the pre-Christian era to the sixth century AD. These caves contain colorful murals depicting the deeds of Sakyamuni and many exquisite carvings.

In the spacious first cave, the Dalai Lama and Panchen Erdeni, along with their entourage, presented hadas and offered prayers to the Buddha statue.

Prior to this, the Dalai Lama and Panchen Erdeni visited the penicillin factory, the state-run chemical factory, the Indian Institute of Pharmaceuticals, and the Oriental Institute in Pune. At the Oriental Institute, the guests saw some Tibetan Buddhist scriptures and listened to three Indian monks playing music and chanting sutras.

In Pune, they were warmly welcomed by the Municipal Committee and the people, and many people lined the streets to greet them wherever they went.

### 达赖喇嘛和班禅额尔德尼  参观阿旃陀佛教胜迹

1956-12-14
第1版()
专栏：

    达赖喇嘛和班禅额尔德尼
    参观阿旃陀佛教胜迹
    新华社新德里13日电　达赖喇嘛和班禅额尔德尼以及他们的随行人员，12月12日从浦那经过贾尔冈到达了著名的阿旃陀石窟。
    在这个佛教胜迹，他们参观了印度艺术家和劳动人民从公元前到公元六世纪在那里开凿的全部二十六个石窟。这些石窟中，有描述释迦牟尼事迹的丰富多彩的壁画和许多精美的雕刻。
    在宽敞的第一个石窟里，达赖喇嘛和班禅额尔德尼以及随行人员们向佛像敬献哈达和祈祷。
    在这以前，达赖喇嘛和班禅额尔德尼在浦那参观了盘尼西林工厂、国营化学工厂、印度药物研究所和东方研究所。在东方研究所中，客人们看到了一些藏文佛教经典，并且听了三个印度高僧奏乐诵经。
    在浦那，他们受到了市政委员会和人民的热烈欢迎，每到一个地方都有许多人夹道迎接。
