Title: Delegation from the Democratic People's Republic of Korea Education Returns Home
Author:
Date: 1956-12-07
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, December 3rd. The Education Delegation from the Democratic People's Republic of Korea, which arrived in China on October 15th, left Beijing on the 3rd to return home.

　　Those seeing them off at the station included Vice Minister of Education Wei Que, Chen Zenggu, Assistant Minister of Higher Education Ding Hua, Vice Chairman of the National Committee of the Chinese Education Trade Union Fang Ming, and Director of the Beijing Education Bureau Weng Dujian, among others.

　　Kim Kwi-nam, Chargé d'affaires ad interim of the Korean Embassy in China, also saw them off at the station.

### 朝鲜民主主义人民共和国教育考察团回国

1956-12-07
第1版()
专栏：

　　朝鲜民主主义人民共和国教育考察团回国
    据新华社3日讯　10月15日来我国访问的朝鲜民主主义人民共和国教育考察团在3日离北京返国。
    到车站欢送的有教育部副部长韦悫、陈曾固，高等教育部部长助理丁华，中国教育工会全国委员会副主席方明，北京市教育局局长翁独健等。
    朝鲜驻我国大使馆临时代办金贵南也到车站欢送。
