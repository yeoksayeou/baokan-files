Title: The Mountains and Forests are Calling
Author: Chen Shanwen
Date: 1956-12-29
Page: 8

    The Mountains and Forests are Calling
    Chen Shanwen
    I stand on Yao Mountain
    Watching the mountains and valleys
    Red maples and green firs
    White reeds swaying in the evening breeze
    That deep valley, ah
    That endless ancient call
    Like the evening tide lapping the shore
    Like the autumn surge of the Qiantang River
    Like ten thousand arrows shooting away
    Like ten thousand horses galloping through the mountains
    Ancient forests, ah
    Youthful life is calling
          November 24, 1956, on Yao Mountain, Liannan

### 山林在呼喊
陈善文
1956-12-29
第8版()
专栏：

    山林在呼喊
    陈善文
    我站在瑶山上
    看满山满谷
    红枫绿杉
    白芦苇在晚风中摆动
    那深深的山谷啊
    那无穷无尽的古老的呼喊
    像晚潮拍岸
    像钱塘秋涨
    像万箭脱弦而去
    像万马奔过山间
    古老的森林啊
    青春的生命在呼喊
          1956、11、24于连南瑶山上
