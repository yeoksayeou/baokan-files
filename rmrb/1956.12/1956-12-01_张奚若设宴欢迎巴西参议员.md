Title: Zhang Xiruo Hosts Banquet to Welcome Brazilian Senators
Author:
Date: 1956-12-01
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, December 28th—A delegation of ten Brazilian senators, headed by Senator Velasco, arrived in Beijing by plane this afternoon. They are visiting China at the invitation of the Chinese People's Institute of Foreign Affairs after attending the 45th Inter-Parliamentary Union Conference held in Bangkok this month.

Velasco, Vice-President of the Brazilian Socialist Party, is the head of the Brazilian delegation to the 45th Inter-Parliamentary Union Conference. Accompanying him to China are Brazilian Congressmen Mazilli (Social Democratic Party), Braga (Social Democratic Party), and Carlos (Labour Party). The wives of these four congressmen are also visiting China.

Those who welcomed them at the airport today included Hu Yuzhi, Vice-President of the Chinese People's Institute of Foreign Affairs; Wu Maosun, Secretary-General; Zhao Yimin, Vice-President of the Chinese People's Association for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries; Xin Zhichao, Deputy Secretary-General of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress; and Mei Gongbin, Deputy Secretary-General of the National Committee of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference.

Xinhua News Agency, December 30th—Zhang Xiruo, President of the Chinese People's Institute of Foreign Affairs, and his wife hosted a banquet this evening to welcome the four Brazilian congressmen and their wives who are visiting China.

The hosts and guests sat around the table, cordially discussing the friendship between China and Brazil and the cause of world peace, and repeatedly raised their glasses to toast this friendship and peace.

Zhang Xiruo said: Although China and Brazil are geographically far apart, this cannot hinder the growing development of friendship and the increasing mutual understanding between our two countries. He firmly believes that this visit by our Brazilian friends to China will further consolidate and expand our friendship and understanding.

Brazilian Senator Velasco, Vice-President of the Brazilian Socialist Party, spoke at the banquet, expressing his gratitude to the host for the "unforgettable hospitality." He said: We greatly admire the great efforts made by the Chinese government and people to develop the economy and improve the people's living standards. He also said: We have also noticed that the Chinese government is facing great tasks in education and has achieved good results. He also expressed his gratitude to the host for inviting them to visit China, giving them "the opportunity to enhance friendship and understanding between Brazil and China."

### 张奚若设宴欢迎巴西参议员

1956-12-01
第1版()
专栏：

　　张奚若设宴欢迎巴西参议员
    新华社28日讯　巴西参议员维拉斯科等一行十人今天下午乘飞机到达北京。他们是在本月参加在曼谷召开的各国议会联盟第四十五届会议后应中国人民外交学会的邀请来中国访问的。
    巴西社会党副主席维拉斯科是参加各国议会联盟第四十五届会议的巴西代表团团长。和他同来中国的，有巴西众议员马兹利（社会民主党）、布拉加（社会民主党）、卡洛斯（劳工党）。这四位议员的夫人都随同来华访问。
    今天到机场欢迎的，有中国人民外交学会副会长胡愈之、秘书长吴茂荪，中国人民对外文化协会副会长赵毅敏，全国人民代表大会常务委员会副秘书长辛志超，中国人民政治协商会议全国委员会副秘书长梅龚彬等。
    据新华社30日讯　中国人民外交学会会长张奚若和他的夫人，今晚设宴欢迎来华访问的巴西四位议员和他们的夫人。
    宾主们围桌而坐，亲切地叙谈中巴两国友谊和世界和平事业，并且不断起立为这友谊和和平干杯。
    张奚若说：中巴两国虽然在地理上相离很远，但是这并不能阻碍我们两国间友谊的日益发展和相互了解的日益增进。他深信，这次巴西朋友们来中国访问，一定会使我们友谊和了解更加巩固和扩大。
    巴西参议员、巴西社会党副主席维拉斯科在会上讲话，对主人的
  “使人不能忘记的款待”表示感谢。他说：我们非常钦佩中国政府和人民为发展经济和改善人民生活水平而作的伟大的努力。他又说：我们也注意到中国政府在教育方面正面对着伟大的任务和所已取得的很好的成就。他还对主人这次邀请他们来华访问，使他们“能有机会以增进巴中两国间的友谊和了解”表示感谢。
