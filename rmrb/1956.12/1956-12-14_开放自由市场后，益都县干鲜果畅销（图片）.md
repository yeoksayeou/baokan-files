Title: After the Opening of the Free Market, Dry and Fresh Fruits Sell Well in Yidu County (Picture)
Author: Chen Zhiping
Date: 1956-12-14
Page: 3

After Yidu County, Shandong Province, opened the free market under state leadership in early November, the amount of dried and fresh fruits produced in the mountainous areas, such as hawthorns, persimmon cakes, and walnuts, that entered the market and were sold has greatly increased. According to statistics from the Wulipu market, the main distribution center for dried and fresh fruits in Yidu County, from November 1st to 27th, the amount of various dried and fresh fruits entering the market reached more than 450,000 jin, an increase of about three times compared with the same period last year. More than 10,000 jin of walnuts, hawthorns, and hawthorn slices are shipped out of Yidu Station every day.
                                Xinhua News Agency Reporter Chen Zhiping Photo

    On the road from Wulipu to Yidu Station, the transportation line for various dried and fresh fruits is endless every day.

    Mountainous farmers can buy the daily necessities they need after selling various dried and fresh fruits at the Wulipu market. Fang Chongbao (middle) is choosing a new hat for his one-year-old child.

### 开放自由市场后，益都县干鲜果畅销（图片）
陈之平
1956-12-14
第3版()
专栏：

    开放自由市场后，益都县干鲜果畅销
    山东省益都县自11月初开放了国家领导下的自由市场后，山区出产的山楂、柿饼、核桃等干鲜果的上市量和销售量已大大增加。据益都县干鲜果主要集散地五里堡市场统计，从11月1日至27日，各种干鲜果上市量达四十五万多斤，比去年同期增加了三倍左右。每天由益都车站外运的核桃、山楂、楂片有一万多斤。
                                新华社记者　陈之平摄
    从五里堡通往益都车站的道路上，每天搬运各种干鲜果的运输行列络绎不绝。
    山区农民把各种干鲜果在五里堡市场出售后，就可以买到自己需要的日用百货。房崇宝（中）正在为他一周岁的孩子选购新帽子。
