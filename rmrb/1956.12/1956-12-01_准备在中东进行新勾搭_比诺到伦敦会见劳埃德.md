Title: Preparing for New Collusion in the Middle East: Pineau to Meet Lloyd in London
Author:
Date: 1956-12-01
Page: 6

　　Preparing for New Collusion in the Middle East
　　Pineau to Meet Lloyd in London
    Xinhua News Agency, December 30th: London dispatch: British Foreign Secretary Lloyd announced in the House of Commons on the 29th that French Foreign Minister Pineau would arrive in London on the 30th to discuss the Middle East situation with Lloyd. Pineau will return to Paris that evening.

### 准备在中东进行新勾搭　比诺到伦敦会见劳埃德

1956-12-01
第6版()
专栏：

　  准备在中东进行新勾搭
　　比诺到伦敦会见劳埃德
    据新华社30日讯　伦敦消息：英国外交大臣劳埃德29日在下院宣布，法国外交部长比诺将在30日到伦敦，就中东局势同劳埃德进行商谈。比诺将在当天晚上回巴黎。
