Title: China Photographic Society Established in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1956-12-24
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, December 22nd: The China Photographic Society was established in Beijing today. The founding conference began on the 19th and ended on the 22nd.

    The meeting discussed and approved the charter of the China Photographic Society, elected 43 directors and 9 alternate directors to the board, and elected Shi Shaohua as chairman of the board. The meeting also passed a resolution to apply to join the China Federation of Literary and Art Circles as a group member.

    The founding conference was attended by 116 people, including well-known photographers, photojournalists, editors of photographic books and pictorials, and professors from photographic teaching institutions from all over the country.

    The central task of the China Photographic Society proposed at the meeting is to promote the creation of photographic art and improve the creative level of photographic art in our country.

### 中国摄影学会在京成立

1956-12-24
第4版()
专栏：

    中国摄影学会在京成立
    据新华社22日讯　中国摄影学会今天在北京成立。成立大会从19日开始，到22日结束。
    会议讨论和通过了中国摄影学会章程，选出理事会理事四十三人、候补理事九人，由石少华任理事会主席。会议还通过了申请加入中国文学艺术界联合会为团体会员的决议。
    参加成立大会的有各地的著名摄影家、摄影记者，摄影书刊、画报的编辑和摄影教学机构的教授等一百一十六人。
    会议提出的中国摄影学会的中心任务是：繁荣我国的摄影艺术创作和提高摄影艺术的创作水平。
