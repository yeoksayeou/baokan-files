Title: Marshal Zhukov Awarded the Order of Lenin; The Soviet Party and Government Sends Congratulations on His Birthday
Author:
Date: 1956-12-03
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, 2nd dispatch of TASS: To celebrate the sixtieth birthday of the Minister of Defense of the Soviet Union, Hero of the Soviet Union, Marshal of the Soviet Union G.K. Zhukov, and to commend his outstanding contributions to the Communist Party and the Soviet people, the Presidium of the Supreme Soviet of the Soviet Union issued an order on December 1st, awarding him the Order of Lenin and his fourth "Gold Star" medal.

The Central Committee of the Communist Party of the Soviet Union and the Council of Ministers of the Soviet Union wrote a congratulatory letter to Zhukov.

The letter said: "The Central Committee of the Communist Party of the Soviet Union and the Council of Ministers of the Soviet Union warmly congratulate you, the outstanding strategist and prominent activist of the Communist Party of the Soviet Union and the Soviet state, on your sixtieth birthday.

The Communist Party and the Soviet people highly value your contributions to the construction of the armed forces and the defense of the socialist motherland. During the harsh years of the Great Patriotic War, you flexibly and heroically led the Soviet army to carry out decisive battles to defend the freedom and independence of the motherland. In the years of peaceful labor, you have always tirelessly dedicated all your energy and knowledge to the further construction of the Soviet state and the consolidation of national defense.

We sincerely wish you, our friend and comrade, dear Georgy Konstantinovich, good health and a long life, and continue to carry out fruitful activities for the happiness of the Soviet people and for the victory of communism."

### 朱可夫元帅荣获列宁勋章  苏联党和政府致函祝贺他的寿辰

1956-12-03
第5版()
专栏：

　　朱可夫元帅荣获列宁勋章
    苏联党和政府致函祝贺他的寿辰
    新华社2日讯　据塔斯社莫斯科讯：苏联最高苏维埃主席团为了祝贺苏联国防部长、苏联英雄、苏联元帅格·康·朱可夫六十岁寿辰，并且表彰他对共产党和苏联人民的杰出功勋，特在12月1日发布命令，授予他一枚列宁勋章和第四枚“金星”奖章。
    苏联共产党中央委员会和苏联部长会议给朱可夫写了一封贺信。
    贺信说：“苏联共产党中央委员会和苏联部长会议热烈祝贺您，杰出的战略家、苏联共产党和苏维埃国家著名的活动家六十周年寿辰。
    共产党和苏联人民十分珍视您在建设武装部队和保卫社会主义祖国方面所树立的功勋。在伟大的卫国战争的严酷的岁月中，您灵活和英勇地领导苏联军队，为保卫祖国的自由和独立进行决定性的战斗。在和平劳动的年代里，您始终不倦地为进一步建设苏维埃国家，为巩固国防，献出全部精力和知识。
    真诚地祝您、我们的朋友和同志、亲爱的格奥尔基·康斯坦丁诺维奇健康长寿，并且为苏联人民的幸福，为共产主义的胜利继续进行富有成果的活动。”
