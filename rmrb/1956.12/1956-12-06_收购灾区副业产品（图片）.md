Title: Purchasing Sideline Products from Disaster Areas (Picture)
Author: Zhang Yinghua
Date: 1956-12-06
Page: 3

Purchasing Sideline Products from Disaster Areas
    The state-owned commercial departments of Tianjin and the supply and marketing cooperatives in the flood-affected areas of Wen'an and Dacheng counties, Hebei Province, have signed contracts for the purchase of sideline products (totaling more than 12 million yuan). This is a scene of the Wen'an County Supply and Marketing Cooperative transporting reed mats purchased from the disaster area to Tianjin by boat.
　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　Xinhua News Agency Reporter Zhang Yinghua photographed

### 收购灾区副业产品（图片）
张映华
1956-12-06
第3版()
专栏：

　　收购灾区副业产品
    天津市国营商业部门与河北省文安、大城等遭受水灾地区的供销合作社签订了副业产品收购合同（总值一千二百多万元）。这是文安县供销合作社把从灾区收购来的苇席用船运往天津。
　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　新华社记者　张映华摄
