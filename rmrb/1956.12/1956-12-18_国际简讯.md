International Briefs

    Soviet Union and Mongolia Sign Trade Protocol for Next Year
    The Soviet Union and the Mongolian People's Republic signed a trade protocol in Moscow on December 14 for 1957.

    Chinese Acrobatic Troupe Wins Awards at International Competition
    The First International Acrobatic Competition concluded on December 14. The Chinese Acrobatic Troupe won a total of four gold medals, four silver medals, and three bronze medals in this competition, ranking third overall.
    The Soviet Union and Democratic Germany (East Germany) secured first and second places, respectively.

    Chinese Arts and Crafts Exhibition Opens in Dhaka
    The Chinese Arts and Crafts Exhibition opened in Dhaka on the evening of December 16. The opening ceremony was presided over by Ataur Rahman, Chief Minister of East Pakistan. A large number of people visited the exhibition, which was organized by the Pakistan Arts Council of Dhaka. In his address, Chief Minister Rahman said that the exhibition would lead to new and increasingly broad cultural contacts between China and Pakistan.

    Over 10,000 Foreign Students in the Soviet Union
    Xinhua News Agency: There are currently more than 12,000 foreign students studying in the Soviet Union, including students from China and other people's democratic countries. Egypt, Burma, and Indonesia sent students to the Soviet Union for the first time this year.

### 国际简讯

1956-12-18
第5版()
专栏：国际简讯

    国际简讯
    苏蒙签订明年换货议定书
    苏联和蒙古人民共和国14日在莫斯科签订了1957年换货议定书。
    我杂技团在国际会演中获奖
    第一届国际杂技大会演已经在12月14日结束。中国杂技团在这次会演中，一共取得了四个金质奖章，四个银质奖章和三个铜质奖章，总分得第三。
    获得第一和第二的是苏联和民主德国。
    我工艺品展览会在达卡开幕
    中国工艺品展览会16日晚在达卡开幕。开幕式由东巴基斯坦首席部长阿陶尔·拉赫曼主持。大批人参观了这个由达卡的巴基斯坦艺术委员会举办的展览会。首席部长拉赫曼在致词的时候说，这次展览会将导致中国同巴基斯坦之间的新的和日益广泛的文化接触。
    在苏联有万余外国留学生
    包括中国和各人民民主国家的留学生在内，目前在苏联学习的外国留学生共有一万二千多人。埃及、缅甸和印度尼西亚等国今年第一次向苏联派出了留学生。　（新华社）
