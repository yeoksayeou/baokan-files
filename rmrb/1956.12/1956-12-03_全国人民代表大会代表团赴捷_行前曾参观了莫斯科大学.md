Title: NPC Delegation Visits Czechoslovakia, Previously Visited Moscow University
Author:
Date: 1956-12-03
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, December 2nd. The Chinese National People's Congress delegation left Moscow for Prague this morning aboard a "Tu-104" aircraft.

Lobaonov, Chairman of the Soviet of the Union of the Supreme Soviet of the USSR, Lacis, Chairman of the Soviet of Nationalities of the Supreme Soviet of the USSR, Bobrovnikov, Soviet Minister and Chairman of the Executive Committee of the Moscow Soviet, and representatives of Moscow factories and institutions went to the airport to see off the delegation.

Also at the airport to see them off were Liu Xiao, Chinese Ambassador to the Soviet Union, and Vosaglik, Czechoslovak Ambassador to the Soviet Union.

Peng Zhen, the head of the delegation, made a brief speech at the airport. He said: During these days, we have been received everywhere with very warm and brotherly welcome and hospitality. Each of us feels deeply at home. We have witnessed the great achievements of the Soviet people in all aspects and learned a lot of advanced experience. We deeply understand the profound friendship of the Soviet people towards the Chinese people. We will take all this as the most precious gift from the Soviet people and bring it to the Chinese people.

On the eve of his departure from Moscow, Peng Zhen also met with reporters from the Soviet Union and other countries and had a friendly conversation.

The Chinese National People's Congress delegation is visiting Czechoslovakia at the invitation of the Czechoslovak National Assembly.

Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, December 2nd. The Chinese National People's Congress delegation continued its visit in Moscow on December 1st.

In the morning, Vice-Chairman Li Jishen, Hu Zi'ang, and all members of the delegation visited the Lomonosov Moscow State University. They were warmly welcomed by the teachers, students, and Chinese students of the university.

On the same day, Vice-Chairman Zhang Bojun visited the Lenin Library.

### 全国人民代表大会代表团赴捷　行前曾参观了莫斯科大学

1956-12-03
第1版()
专栏：

　　全国人民代表大会代表团赴捷
　　行前曾参观了莫斯科大学
    新华社莫斯科2日电　中国全国人民代表大会代表团今天上午乘“图—一○四式”飞机离开莫斯科前往布拉格。
    苏联最高苏维埃联盟院主席洛巴诺夫、苏联最高苏维埃民族院主席拉齐斯、苏联部长、莫斯科市苏维埃执行委员会主席波布罗夫尼科夫和莫斯科各工厂、机关的代表等到机场欢送代表团。
    到机场欢送的还有中国驻苏联大使刘晓和捷克斯洛伐克驻苏大使沃萨格里克。
    代表团团长彭真在机场发表了简短的讲话。他说：在这些天里，我们到处都受到了十分热烈的兄弟般的欢迎和接待。我们每个人都深深感到像是在自己家里一样。我们亲眼看到了苏联人民在各方面获得的巨大成就，学习了很多先进经验。我们深深地了解到苏联人民对中国人民的深厚友情。我们将把这一切都当作苏联人民的最珍贵的礼品，带给中国人民。
    彭真在离开莫斯科前夕，还接见了苏联和别的国家的记者，进行了友好的谈话。
    中国全国人民代表大会代表团是应捷克斯洛伐克国民议会的邀请前往捷克斯洛伐克访问的。
    据新华社莫斯科2日电　中国全国人民代表大会代表团12月1日继续在莫斯科参观访问。
    上午，副团长李济深、胡子昂和全体团员参观了国立洛蒙诺索夫莫斯科大学。他们受到了大学师生和中国留学生的热烈欢迎。
    同日，副团长章伯钧参观了列宁图书馆。
