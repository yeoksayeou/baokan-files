Title: IOC Finnish Member Returns Home
Author:
Date: 1956-12-27
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, December 26th - Mr. Von Frenckell, the Finnish member of the International Olympic Committee, who was invited to visit China, left Beijing by plane today to return to his country after a four-day tour of Beijing.

### 国际奥委会芬兰委员回国

1956-12-27
第1版()
专栏：

    国际奥委会芬兰委员回国
    据新华社26日讯　应邀来中国访问的国际奥林匹克委员会芬兰委员冯·弗伦克尔在北京参观访问了四天以后，今天乘飞机离北京回国。
