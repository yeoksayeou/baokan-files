Title: The Victory of the Egyptian People is the Victory of the Arab People Arab National Leaders Congratulate the Liberation of Port Said Egyptian Newspapers Call on the People Never to Allow Aggressors to Return

Author: 
Date: 1956-12-26
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, December 25th. TASS, Cairo, December 24th: According to Cairo newspapers, Arab national leaders sent telegrams to Egyptian President Nasser to express their congratulations on the withdrawal of the Anglo-French invading forces from Port Said. King Saud of Saudi Arabia said in his congratulatory telegram that the news of the complete withdrawal of the Anglo-French forces from Port Said filled the hearts of all Arabs with joy. Saud said that this victory is not only a victory for the heroic Egyptian people, but also a victory for all Arabs.
    King Hussein of Jordan emphasized in his congratulatory telegram to President Nasser that the heroic Egyptian people not only defended their own country, but also defended all Arab people.
    Syrian President Kuwatli expressed his firm belief in his congratulatory telegram that the unity of the Arab people will continue to be strengthened, "and all Arab people will strive to realize their national task—unity, freedom and the safeguarding of sovereignty—through close cooperation."
    Sudanese Prime Minister Abdullah Khalil sent a telegram to President Nasser on behalf of the Sudanese government and people to congratulate Egypt on the final withdrawal of Anglo-French forces from Port Said. He also expressed deep satisfaction with the position of the peace-loving people who supported Egypt in this just struggle.
    Xinhua News Agency, Cairo, December 25th. Sadat, editor-in-chief of "Republic" published an article on December 24th, celebrating the recovery of Port Said.
    Sadat said that the Egyptian people will always be loyal to the ideals upheld by the martyrs in Port Said and the Sinai Peninsula, who sacrificed their lives to ensure that Egypt will always fully enjoy freedom and dignity.
    He said that the victory achieved by Egypt is a victory of a small country over two empires.
    Sadat said that the only force that forced Britain to stop its aggression was the serious warning issued by the Soviet Union, which frightened the British.
    The newspaper said that the Egyptian people should swear to mourn the massacre of their compatriots, to stir up enthusiasm, to work resolutely and strive for progress, and never again allow the sacred territory of the motherland to be invaded by enemies.

### 埃及人民的胜利就是阿拉伯人民的胜利  阿拉伯国家领导人祝贺塞得港光复  埃报号召人民永远不让侵略者卷土重来

1956-12-26
第6版()
专栏：

    埃及人民的胜利就是阿拉伯人民的胜利
    阿拉伯国家领导人祝贺塞得港光复
    埃报号召人民永远不让侵略者卷土重来
    新华社25日讯　塔斯社开罗24日讯：据开罗各报报道，在英法侵略军撤离塞得港的时候，阿拉伯国家领导人分别致电埃及总统纳赛尔表示祝贺。沙特阿拉伯国王沙特的贺电说，英法军队完全撤离塞得港的消息使所有阿拉伯人的心中充满了喜悦。沙特说，这个胜利不仅是英勇的埃及人民的胜利，同时也是所有阿拉伯人的胜利。
    约旦国王侯赛尼在拍给纳赛尔总统的贺电中强调指出，英勇的埃及人民不仅保卫了自己的国家，而且也保卫了所有阿拉伯人民。
    叙利亚总统库阿特利在贺电中表示坚信，阿拉伯人民的团结将继续加强，“所有阿拉伯人民在紧密的合作下将力求实现自己的民族任务——团结、自由和保卫主权。”
    苏丹总理阿卜杜拉·哈利勒在拍给纳赛尔总统的电报中代表苏丹政府和人民为英法军队最后撤离塞得港这件事向埃及祝贺。他并且对在这次正义的斗争中支持过埃及的爱好和平的人民的立场表示深深满意。
    据新华社开罗25日电　“共和国报”主编萨达特12月24日发表文章，庆祝塞得港的收复。
    萨达特说，埃及人民将永远忠于在塞得港和西奈半岛的烈士们舍身维护的理想，使埃及永远充分地享受自由和尊严。
    他说，埃及所取得的胜利，是一个小国对两个帝国所取得的胜利。
    萨达特说，迫使英国停止侵略的唯一的力量，是苏联提出的严重警告，这种警告使英国人吓破了胆。
    这家报纸说，埃及人民应该立誓，悼念同胞惨遭屠杀的事件，要鼓起热情，坚决努力，争取进步，永不再使祖国的神圣领土遭受敌人的侵略。
