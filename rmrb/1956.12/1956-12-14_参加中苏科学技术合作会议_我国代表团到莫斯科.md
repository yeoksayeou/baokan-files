Title: Chinese Delegation Arrives in Moscow to Attend Sino-Soviet Scientific and Technological Cooperation Conference
Author:
Date: 1956-12-14
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, 11th dispatch: According to a TASS dispatch from Moscow: The Chinese delegation headed by Li Qiang to attend the Fifth Session of the Sino-Soviet Scientific and Technological Cooperation Committee arrived in Moscow on the 10th.

### 参加中苏科学技术合作会议  我国代表团到莫斯科

1956-12-14
第1版()
专栏：

    参加中苏科学技术合作会议
    我国代表团到莫斯科
    新华社11日讯　据塔斯社莫斯科讯：以李强为首的参加中苏科学技术合作委员会第五届会议的中国代表团已经在10日到达莫斯科。
