Title: Life Begins Again
Author: Yuán Shuǐpāi
Date: 1956-12-14
Page: 5

Life begins again!
	Coming out of the underground station,
	Going to the office, to the market, to the factory,
	Helping children across the square,
	Ah, how familiar is this peaceful life,
	And how much it is hated by the enemy!
	Thinking back to those days,
	Our hearts were also burning:
	Books piled up and burned,
	Patriots hanged from trees.
	The black whirlwind
	Wanted to sweep away socialism in an instant!
	The crimson poisonous tongue wanted to lick dry
	The blood and sweat of the Hungarian people!
	Life begins again!
	Good, dear friends,
	The people have courage,
	The people have confidence,
	Brick by brick,
	Shovel of mud by shovel of mud,
	Rebuild life!
	Repair what is broken,
	Sweep away what is dirty!
	Listen! Washington tolls the death knell (note),
	Because the Hungarian people have already returned to work!

(Note) According to a United Press dispatch from Washington on the 8th, Washington and other cities are tolling the death knell especially for the Hungarian incident. (Attached pictures)

### 生活又开始了
袁水拍
1956-12-14
第5版()
专栏：

    生活又开始了
    袁水拍
    生活又开始了！
    从地下车站走出来，
    上办公室，上市场，上工厂，
    搀着孩子过广场，
    啊，多么熟悉的和平的生活，
    又是多么受敌人仇视的生活啊！
    回想起那些日子，
    我们也心头如焚：
    书本堆起来烧，
    爱国者在树上吊。
    黑色的旋风
    要一下子把社会主义扫空！
    殷红的毒舌要舔干
    匈牙利人民的血汗！
    生活又开始了！
    好啊，亲爱的朋友们，
    人民有的是勇气，
    人民有的是信心，
    一块砖又一块砖，
    一铲泥又一铲泥，
    把生活重新建设起！
    破碎的，补起来，
    肮脏的，扫出去！
    听！华盛顿敲起了丧钟（注），
    因为，匈牙利人民已经复工！
    （注）据合众社华盛顿8日电，华盛顿等城市特为匈牙利事件敲丧钟。（附图片）
    布达佩斯开始了房屋修理工作　（苏联诺维科夫摄）
    布达佩斯地下铁道通车了　（苏联诺维科夫摄）
