Title: After the Implementation of Military Law, Many People in Budapest Surrendered Weapons
Author:
Date: 1956-12-13
Page: 5
Xinhua News Agency, December 12th -- According to a report by the Hungarian News Agency on the 11th, since the Hungarian People's Republic promulgated the decree to implement military law throughout the country on the evening of the 9th, more and more people have handed over weapons and explosives to the police stations. Within twenty-four hours of the promulgation of the decree, the number of weapons handed over to a district police substation in Budapest was equivalent to the number of weapons handed over to police agencies in various districts of the capital in the most recent week.

### 实行军法审判法令后  布达佩斯许多人交出武器

1956-12-13
第5版()
专栏：

    实行军法审判法令后
    布达佩斯许多人交出武器
    新华社12日讯　据匈牙利通讯社11日报道，自匈牙利人民共和国在9日晚颁布在全国实行军法审判的法令以后，越来越多的人把武器和爆炸物交给警察局。在法令公布后的二十四小时内，人们向布达佩斯一个区警察分局所交出的武器数量就相当于最近一星期内向首都各区警察机关所交出的武器。
