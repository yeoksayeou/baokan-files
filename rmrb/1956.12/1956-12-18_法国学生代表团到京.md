Title: French Student Delegation Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1956-12-18
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, December 16th. A French student delegation of fifteen, led by head of delegation Claude Jeisswor, arrived in Beijing by plane today.

This delegation is visiting China at the invitation of the All-China Students' Federation.

### 法国学生代表团到京

1956-12-18
第1版()
专栏：

    法国学生代表团到京
    据新华社16日讯　法国学生代表团一行十五人在团长克劳特·何西沃耳率领下今天乘飞机到北京。
    这个代表团是应中华全国学生联合会的邀请前来我国访问的。
