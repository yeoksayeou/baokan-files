Title: Dalai Lama and Panchen Erdeni Visit Various Parts of India
Author:
Date: 1956-12-04
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, New Delhi, December 3rd. The Dalai Lama and Panchen Erdeni left New Delhi by train on the evening of December 2nd to visit other parts of India.

Those who saw them off at the train station included Prime Minister Nehru, Chairman of the Delhi Municipal Committee Agarwal, and Fu Hao, Counselor of the Chinese Embassy in India.

The places the Dalai Lama and Panchen Erdeni will visit include Bakra Nangal, Agra, Sanchi, the world-renowned Ajanta and Ellora Caves, Madras, Benares, the Buddhist center of Nalanda, Gaya, Sindri, and Calcutta. They are scheduled to leave India on December 30th.

Earlier, the Dalai Lama, Panchen Erdeni, and the Chinese Buddhist delegation attended a banquet hosted by the Working Committee for the 2500th Anniversary of Sakyamuni's Nirvana to entertain representatives from various countries attending the Buddhist symposium.

### 达赖喇嘛和班禅额尔德尼去印度各地访问

1956-12-04
第1版()
专栏：

　　达赖喇嘛和班禅额尔德尼去印度各地访问
    新华社新德里3日电　达赖喇嘛和班禅额尔德尼在12月2日晚上乘火车离开新德里，前往印度的其他一些地方访问。
    到火车站送行的有尼赫鲁总理、德里市政委员会主席阿加瓦尔以及中国驻印度大使馆参赞符浩等。
    达赖喇嘛和班禅额尔德尼将访问的地方包括巴克拉南加尔、亚格拉、桑吉、世界闻名的阿旃陀和爱楼拉的石窟、马德拉斯、贝拿勒斯、佛教中心那烂陀、伽耶、辛德利和加尔各答。他们预定在12月30日离开印度。
    早些时候，达赖喇嘛、班禅额尔德尼和中国佛教代表团出席了释迦牟尼涅槃二千五百周年工作委员会为招待出席佛教座谈会的各国代表所设的宴会。
