　　Anglo-Turkish Conspiracy to Interfere in Syria
    Xinhua News Agency, December 3rd, London Dispatch: Turkish Acting Foreign Minister Menderes left London on December 2nd to return to his country.

    Menderes held three days of talks in London with British Foreign Secretary Lloyd on the issue of opposing Syria. French Foreign Minister Pineau also participated in the talks. Before his departure, Menderes said that they had reached a complete understanding on the issues discussed in the talks.

    The day before, Menderes also said that the Turkish government intends to bring up the so-called issue of Syria purchasing armaments at the North Atlantic Council meeting next week. News has it that Britain, France, and Turkey have decided to have the North Atlantic Group issue a "warning" regarding the fabricated so-called "Soviet Union encouraging Syria to accumulate weapons."

### 英土密谋干涉叙利亚

1956-12-04
第6版()
专栏：

　　英土密谋干涉叙利亚
    新华社3日讯　伦敦消息：土耳其代理外交部长曼德列斯12月2日离开伦敦回国。
    曼德列斯在伦敦曾经同英国外交大臣劳埃德就反对叙利亚的问题进行了三天会谈，法国外交部长比诺也曾经参加会谈。曼德列斯在动身前说，他们在会谈所涉及的问题上彼此完全谅解。
    在前一天，曼德列斯还说过，土耳其政府打算把所谓叙利亚购买军火的问题提到下星期召开的北大西洋联盟理事会上去。有消息说，英法土三国已经决定，要北大西洋集团就虚构的所谓“苏联鼓励叙利亚积聚武器”这件事提出“警告”。
