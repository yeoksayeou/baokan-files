Title: Peng Zhen and Liu Changsheng Arrive in Prague from Rome
Author:
Date: 1956-12-18
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Prague, December 17th: Peng Zhen and Liu Changsheng, representatives of the Central Committee of the Chinese Communist Party attending the Eighth Congress of the Italian Communist Party, arrived in Prague by train from Rome on the evening of the 16th.

### 彭真和刘长胜从罗马到布拉格

1956-12-18
第1版()
专栏：

    彭真和刘长胜从罗马到布拉格
    新华社布拉格17日电　参加意大利共产党第八次代表大会的中国共产党中央委员会代表彭真、刘长胜16日晚乘火车由罗马到达布拉格。
