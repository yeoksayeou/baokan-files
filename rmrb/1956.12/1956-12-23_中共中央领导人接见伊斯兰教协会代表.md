Title: Central Committee Leaders of the Chinese Communist Party Receive Representatives of the Islamic Association

Author:

Date: 1956-12-23
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, December 22nd: Mao Zedong, Chairman of the Central Committee of the Chinese Communist Party, and Vice Chairmen Zhu De and Chen Yun, received the representatives attending the Second Congress of the China Islamic Association and all members of the delegation from various ethnic groups in Heilongjiang Province this afternoon.

### 中共中央领导人接见伊斯兰教协会代表

1956-12-23
第1版()
专栏：

    中共中央领导人接见伊斯兰教协会代表
    新华社22日讯　中国共产党中央委员会主席毛泽东、副主席朱德、陈云，在今天下午接见了出席中国伊斯兰教第二次代表会议的代表和黑龙江省各民族参观团的全体人员。
