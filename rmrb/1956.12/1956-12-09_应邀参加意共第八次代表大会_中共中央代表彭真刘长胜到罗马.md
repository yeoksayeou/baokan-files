Title: In Response to the Invitation to Attend the Eighth Congress of the Italian Communist Party, CCP Central Committee Representatives Peng Zhen and Liu Changsheng Arrive in Rome
Author:
Date: 1956-12-09
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, December 8th. At the invitation of the Central Committee of the Italian Communist Party, the Central Committee of the Chinese Communist Party dispatched Peng Zhen, member of the Political Bureau and Secretary of the Secretariat of the CCP Central Committee, and Liu Changsheng, member of the CCP Central Committee, to Italy to attend the Eighth Congress of the Italian Communist Party, which opened on December 8.
　　Comrades Peng Zhen and Liu Changsheng arrived in Rome by plane from Prague on the evening of the 7th.

### 应邀参加意共第八次代表大会　中共中央代表彭真刘长胜到罗马

1956-12-09
第1版()
专栏：

    应邀参加意共第八次代表大会
　　中共中央代表彭真刘长胜到罗马
    新华社8日讯　中国共产党中央委员会应意大利共产党中央委员会的邀请，派遣了中共中央政治局委员、中央书记处书记彭真和中共中央委员刘长胜前往意大利参加在12月8日开幕的意大利共产党第八次代表大会。
    彭真和刘长胜两同志已在7日晚乘飞机自布拉格到达罗马。
