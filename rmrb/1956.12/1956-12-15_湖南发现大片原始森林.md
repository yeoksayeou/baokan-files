Title: Large Tract of Virgin Forest Discovered in Hunan

Author:

Date: 1956-12-15
Page: 3

Xinhua News Agency, Changsha Dispatch: A large tract of virgin forest has been discovered in the Mangshan area of southern Hunan Province. The total forest area is over 45,000 hectares, with a reserve of approximately 4.5 million cubic meters.

This previously uninhabited forest area was discovered in July this year by an investigation team from the Hunan Provincial Forestry Department. Currently, forestry industry departments are conducting surveying and design work in preparation for developing this forest area.

### 湖南发现大片原始森林

1956-12-15
第3版()
专栏：

    湖南发现大片原始森林
    据新华社长沙电　湖南省南部莽山地区发现大片原始森林。全部林区面积达四万五千多公顷，蓄积量约四百五十多万立方公尺。
    这个一向杳无人迹的林区，是今年7月间湖南省林业厅调查队踏查后发现的。现在，森林工业部门正在勘测设计，准备开发这个林区。
