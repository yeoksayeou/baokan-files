Title: U.S. Government Isolates Itself: Interrupts Government Visits with the Soviet Union
Author:
Date: 1956-12-05
Page: 6

　　U.S. Government Isolates Itself
　　Interrupts Government Visits with the Soviet Union
    Xinhua News Agency, Washington, December 4th: White, the U.S. State Department press officer, announced on the 3rd: "Due to recent developments, planned reciprocal visits of a governmental nature between the United States and the Soviet Union have been interrupted." He also stated that the so-called "recent developments" refer to the failure of the counter-revolutionary riots in Hungary.

### 美国政府自绝于人  中断同苏联的政府性访问

1956-12-05
第6版()
专栏：

　  美国政府自绝于人
　  中断同苏联的政府性访问
    据新华社4日讯　华盛顿消息：美国国务院新闻发布官怀特3日宣布：“由于最近的事态发展，计划中的同苏联之间的政府性质的互相访问已经中断。”他还表示，所谓“最近事态的发展”是指匈牙利反革命暴乱的失败。
