Title: Inner Mongolia Discovers Peat

Author:

Date: 1956-12-15
Page: 2

Column: Exploration Good News

	Inner Mongolia Discovers Peat
	Xinhua News Agency: An integrated exploration team from the Inner Mongolia Autonomous Region's Bureau of Geology of the Department of Industry discovered a large amount of peat resources in the western part of the autonomous region this year. In many places, large-scale peat deposits are found just one meter below the ground. Proven peat reserves this year are over seven million tons.
	Peat is an important raw material for making peat fertilizer.

### 内蒙古发现泥炭

1956-12-15
第2版()
专栏：勘探喜讯

    内蒙古发现泥炭
    据新华社讯　内蒙古自治区工业厅地质局的一个综合勘探队，今年在自治区西部发现大量泥炭资源。许多地方在地面一公尺以下就是大面积的泥炭矿床。今年已经探明的泥炭储量在七百万吨以上。
    泥炭是制造泥炭肥料的重要原料。
