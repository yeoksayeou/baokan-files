Title: Nasser Receives Menon
Author:
Date: 1956-12-30
Page: 6

    Xinhua News Agency, Cairo, December 29th: Egyptian President Nasser received Indian Minister Without Portfolio Menon on the 28th.
    Cairo Radio broadcast that this was Menon's second meeting with Nasser since arriving in Cairo.
    Menon arrived in Cairo by plane from London on the 27th.

### 纳赛尔接见梅农

1956-12-30
第6版()
专栏：

    纳赛尔接见梅农
    新华社29日讯　开罗消息：埃及总统纳赛尔在28日接见了印度不管部长梅农。
    开罗电台的广播说，这是梅农到达开罗后第二次会见纳赛尔。
    梅农在27日从伦敦乘飞机到达开罗。
