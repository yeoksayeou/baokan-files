Title: Soviet and Czechoslovak Newspapers Publish "More on the Historical Experience of the Dictatorship of the Proletariat" Article

Author:
Date: 1956-12-31
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, December 30th: "Pravda" published the full text of the "People's Daily" editorial department's article "More on the Historical Experience of the Dictatorship of the Proletariat" on the 30th. "Izvestia," "Trud," and "Komsomolskaya Pravda" all published abstracts of this article. The Moscow Central Radio Station began broadcasting the full text of this article on the morning of the 30th.

Xinhua News Agency, Sofia, December 30th: The Bulgarian newspapers "Rabotnichesko Delo," "Otechestven Front," "Trud," "Narodna Mladezh," "Zemedelsko Zname," and "Kooperativno Selo" all published abstracts of the "People's Daily" article "More on the Historical Experience of the Dictatorship of the Proletariat" on the 29th.

Xinhua News Agency, Prague, December 30th: Czechoslovakian newspapers "Rude Pravo" and "Lidova Obrana" today published the full text of the "People's Daily" editorial department's article "More on the Historical Experience of the Dictatorship of the Proletariat". All other newspapers published abstracts of this article.

### 苏保捷各报刊载“再论无产阶级专政的历史经验”一文

1956-12-31
第1版()
专栏：

    苏保捷各报刊载“再论无产阶级专政的历史经验”一文
    本报莫斯科30日电　“真理报”30日刊载了“人民日报”编辑部的“再论无产阶级专政的历史经验”一文的全文。“消息报”、“劳动报”、“共青团真理报”都刊载了这篇文章的摘要。莫斯科中央广播台30日上午开始播送这篇文章的全文。
    新华社索非亚30日电　保加利亚“工人事业报”、“祖国阵线报”、“劳动报”，“人民青年报”，“农业旗帜报”，“合作农业报”在29日都摘要刊登了“人民日报”的“再论无产阶级专政的历史经验”一文。
    据新华社布拉格30日电　捷克斯洛伐克的“红色权利报”和“人民防线报”今天都全文刊载了“人民日报”编辑部的文章“再论无产阶级专政的历史经验”。其他所有的报纸都刊载了这篇文章的摘要。
