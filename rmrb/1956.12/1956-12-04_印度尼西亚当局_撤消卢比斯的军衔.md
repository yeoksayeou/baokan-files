Title: Indonesian Authorities Revoke Lubis's Military Rank
Author:
Date: 1956-12-04
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, Jakarta, December 1st: According to Antara News Agency, former Indonesian Army Deputy Chief of Staff Lubis, who recently attempted to stage a coup, has been stripped of his military rank. Approximately fifteen officers in West Java implicated in the conspiracy are being investigated by Army Attorney General Suprapto.

Meanwhile, Jakarta Garrison Commander Major Djuro handed over his duties to his successor on November 30th. He has been transferred to the Third Military Region Command.

According to the Indonesian News Agency, First Military Region Commander Simbolon has agreed to transfer his military authority to the designated officer in the shortest possible time.

### 印度尼西亚当局　撤消卢比斯的军衔

1956-12-04
第6版()
专栏：

　  印度尼西亚当局
　　撤消卢比斯的军衔
    据新华社雅加达1日电　据安塔拉通讯社报道，最近企图发动政变的印度尼西亚前陆军副参谋长卢比斯已经被撤消军衔。大约十五名同这个阴谋有关的在西爪哇的军官已经由陆军检察长苏普拉普托加以调查。
    同时，雅加达卫戌司令朱罗少校在11月30日把职权移交给他的继任者。他已被调往第三军区司令部。
    据印度尼西亚新闻社报道，第一军区司令西姆波朗已经同意在最短期间把他的军权移交给指定的军官。
