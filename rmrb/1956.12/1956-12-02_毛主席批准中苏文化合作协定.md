Title: Chairman Mao Approves Sino-Soviet Cultural Cooperation Agreement
Author:
Date: 1956-12-02
Page: 1

　　Xinhua News Agency, December 1st: Chairman Mao Zedong of the People's Republic of China, in accordance with the decision of the 49th meeting of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress, approved the Cultural Cooperation Agreement between the People's Republic of China and the Union of Soviet Socialist Republics on November 29, 1956.

### 毛主席批准中苏文化合作协定

1956-12-02
第1版()
专栏：

　　毛主席批准中苏文化合作协定
    新华社1日讯　中华人民共和国主席毛泽东根据全国人民代表大会常务委员会第四十九次会议的决定，于1956年11月29日批准了中华人民共和国和苏维埃社会主义共和国联盟文化合作协定。
