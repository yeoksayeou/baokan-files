Title: Liaoning Farmers' Savings Increase
Author:
Date: 1956-12-30
Page: 3

Xinhua News Agency, Shenyang, December 26th. By December 15th, the balance of farmers' savings deposits in the Agricultural Bank of Liaoning Province reached more than 62 million yuan, an increase of more than two and a half times compared with the same period last year. In the most recent distribution by the Nuanchitang Township Agricultural Cooperative in Jinxi County, 30,000 yuan in cash was given to the cooperative members. At that time, 57% of the cooperative members deposited a total of 6,000 yuan in the credit cooperative. Female cooperative members in Batu Yingzi Township, Xinmin County, deposited more than 3,000 yuan in just a few days.

Credit cooperatives in various places have adopted many methods to facilitate farmers' savings deposits, such as establishing farmer savings service stations and hiring savings agents.

### 辽宁农民存款增多

1956-12-30
第3版()
专栏：

    辽宁农民存款增多
    新华社沈阳26日电　到12月15日止，辽宁省农民在农业银行的储蓄存款余额达六千二百多万元，比去年同期增加了两倍半以上，锦西县暖池塘乡农业社最近一次分配给社员三万元现金，当时就有57％的社员把一共六千元存到了信用合作社。新民县巴图营子乡的女社员在几天中就存了三千多元。
    各地信用合作社采用了很多便利农民存款的方法，如成立了农民存款服务站，聘请了存款代办员等。
