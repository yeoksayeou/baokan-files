Title: Syrian Cabinet Resigns
Author:
Date: 1956-12-24
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, December 23rd: The Syrian cabinet of Sa'i al-'Asali announced its resignation on the 22nd. Syrian Prime Minister al-'Asali said on December 17th that he would organize a new cabinet that would be "more active and more harmonious" than the resigning cabinet.

### 叙利亚内阁辞职

1956-12-24
第5版()
专栏：

    叙利亚内阁辞职
    新华社23日讯　叙利亚萨·阿萨利的内阁在22日宣布辞职。叙利亚总理阿萨利曾经在12月17日说，他将要组织一个比辞职的内阁“更积极和更和谐的”新内阁。
