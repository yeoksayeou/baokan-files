Title: Newly Built Dairy Factory in Gannan Tibetan Autonomous Prefecture (Picture)
Author: Ma Yuxing
Date: 1956-12-31
Page: 2

    Newly Built Dairy Factory in Gannan Tibetan Autonomous Prefecture
    A new type of dairy factory is under construction in Gannan Tibetan Autonomous Prefecture, Gansu Province. The civil engineering of this factory has been basically completed, and equipment installation has begun. This factory will be put into production in the second quarter of next year, with a daily production capacity of more than four tons of milk powder.
                                        Photo by Ma Yuxing (Xinhua News Agency Manuscript)

### 甘南藏族自治州新建乳品厂（图片）
马毓兴
1956-12-31
第2版()
专栏：

    甘南藏族自治州新建乳品厂
    甘肃省甘南藏族自治州正在兴建一座新型的乳品厂。这个厂的土建工程已经基本完工，开始安装设备。这个厂将在明年二季度投入生产，每日能生产乳粉四吨多。
                                        马毓兴摄（新华社稿）
