Title: The French Government Hopes to Hold a Summit Meeting of the United States, Britain, and France
Author:
Date: 1956-12-14
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, Paris, December 13th: State Secretary of the French Prime Minister's Office, Gérard Jaquet, told reporters on the 12th that France "very much hopes" that the heads of government of France, Britain, and the United States will hold a meeting.

### 法国政府希望举行美英法首脑会议

1956-12-14
第6版()
专栏：

    法国政府希望
    举行美英法首脑会议
    据新华社13日讯　巴黎消息：法国总理办公厅国务秘书热腊尔·雅克在12日对记者说，法国“非常希望”法、英、美三国政府首脑举行一次会议。
