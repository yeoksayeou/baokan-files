Title: Art Troupe Returning to Beijing After Performance Tour in South America
Author:
Date: 1956-12-08
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, 7th. The Chinese Art Troupe, led by head of the delegation Chu Tunan, returned to Beijing this afternoon after a performance tour in South America.

Zhang Zhixiang, Vice Minister of the Ministry of Culture, Yang Hansheng, Vice President of the Chinese People's Association of Friendship with Foreign Countries, and nearly 300 people including Peking Opera actors welcomed the art troupe at the station.

The ashes of the ten members of the art troupe who sacrificed their lives in a plane crash on the border between Switzerland and West Germany on November 24th will be transported back to Beijing from Prague soon.

### 我赴南美洲访问演出的艺术团回到北京

1956-12-08
第1版()
专栏：

　　我赴南美洲访问演出的艺术团回到北京
    新华社7日讯　赴南美洲访问演出的中国艺术团在团长楚图南率领下，今天下午回到北京。
    文化部副部长张致祥，中国人民对外文化协会副会长阳翰笙，以及京剧演员等近三百人到车站欢迎艺术团。
    11月24日在瑞士和西德边境地方因飞机失事而牺牲的艺术团十位团员的骨灰，最近将由布拉格运回北京。
