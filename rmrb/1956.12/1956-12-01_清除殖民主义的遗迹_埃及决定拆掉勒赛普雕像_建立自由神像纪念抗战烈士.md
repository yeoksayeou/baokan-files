Title: Clearing Colonialist Vestiges: Egypt Decides to Remove de Lesseps Statue and Build Statue of Liberty to Commemorate Anti-War Martyrs
Author:
Date: 1956-12-01
Page: 6

　　Clearing Colonialist Vestiges
　　Egypt Decides to Remove de Lesseps Statue
　　Build Statue of Liberty to Commemorate Anti-War Martyrs
    Xinhua News Agency, Cairo, November 30th: Egyptian officials announced on November 29th that the Egyptian government has decided to remove the statue of Frenchman Ferdinand de Lesseps at the entrance to the Suez Canal in Port Said, and erect a Statue of Liberty in its place.

    The Suez Canal was successfully excavated under the direction of Lesseps using the forced labor of the Egyptian people.

    The new statue that Egypt has decided to build will be used to commemorate those who sacrificed in the war against Anglo-French aggression.

    The Minister of Municipal and Rural Affairs of Egypt said on the 29th that the reconstruction plan for Port Said has been drafted, and work will begin immediately after the withdrawal of the British and French troops.

### 清除殖民主义的遗迹　埃及决定拆掉勒赛普雕像　建立自由神像纪念抗战烈士

1956-12-01
第6版()
专栏：

　　清除殖民主义的遗迹
　　埃及决定拆掉勒赛普雕像
　　建立自由神像纪念抗战烈士
    新华社30日讯  开罗消息：埃及官方11月29日宣布，埃及政府已经决定拆除在塞得港苏伊士运河入口处的法国人费迪南·德·勒赛普的雕像，而另外竖立一座自由神雕像。
    苏伊士运河就是在勒赛普的主持下使用埃及人民的强迫劳动开凿成功的。
    埃及决定建立的新雕像将用以纪念反对英法侵略战争中的牺牲者。
    埃及城乡事务部长29日说，重建塞得港计划已经拟成，英法军队一撤走，工作立即开始。
