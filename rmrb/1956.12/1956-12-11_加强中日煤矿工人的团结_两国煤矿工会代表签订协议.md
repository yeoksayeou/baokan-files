Title: Strengthening the Solidarity of Chinese and Japanese Coal Miners: Representatives of the Two Countries' Coal Mine Unions Sign Agreement
Author:
Date: 1956-12-11
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, December 10th.

    Strengthening the Solidarity of Chinese and Japanese Coal Miners
    Representatives of the Two Countries' Coal Mine Unions Sign Agreement
    Xinhua News Agency, December 10th – The signing ceremony of the agreement between the National Committee of the Chinese Coal Miners' Union and the visiting delegation of the Japanese Coal Miners' Union was held on the evening of the 10th at Ziguang Pavilion, Zhongnanhai, Beijing.
    The Chinese side participating in the signing ceremony included Liu Ningyi, Vice Chairman of the All-China Federation of Trade Unions, Dong Xin, Secretary of the Secretariat, Chen Yu, Deputy Director of the International Liaison Department, and Jin Zhifu, Chairman of the National Committee of the Chinese Coal Miners' Union, among others.
    The Japanese side participating in the signing ceremony included all members of the visiting delegation of the Japanese Coal Miners' Union, headed by Koga Sada.
    At 9:30 PM, representatives from both sides signed the agreement. After signing, both sides raised their glasses in congratulations.
    The full text of the agreement between the National Committee of the Chinese Coal Miners' Union and the visiting delegation of the Japanese Coal Miners' Union is as follows:
    The National Committee of the Chinese Coal Miners' Union and the visiting delegation of the Japanese Coal Miners' Union, through this exchange, based on the rapidly deepened understanding and friendship between each other, in order to establish a truly fraternal unity and cooperative relationship between the coal miners and their families of the two countries, and to strengthen the friendship and amity between the workers and the people of the two countries on the basis of their respective understandings of the current situation, have reached complete agreement on the following:
    1.  Strengthen the exchange of experiences, exchange of various materials, exchange of letters, mutual dispatch of representatives, and strengthen the exchange of workers' technical experiences and cultural activities between the Chinese Coal Miners' Union and its Women Workers and Family Affairs Committee, and the Japanese Coal Miners' Union and the Japanese Coal Mine Housewives' Council.
    2.  While promoting exchanges between coal miners and their families in the two countries, also cooperate with workers in other industries and strive to expand and develop exchange activities in all aspects.
    3.  Work together to solve various problems that hinder mutual understanding between the peoples of the two countries and obstruct friendship and amity between the peoples of the two countries. Currently, strive to restore diplomatic relations and promote trade between the two countries as soon as possible.
    4.  Actively strive to deepen mutual understanding and friendship between Asian workers and their families.

### 加强中日煤矿工人的团结  两国煤矿工会代表签订协议

1956-12-11
第1版()
专栏：

    加强中日煤矿工人的团结
    两国煤矿工会代表签订协议
    新华社10日讯　中国煤矿工会全国委员会和日本煤矿工会访华代表团协议的签字仪式，10日晚在北京中南海紫光阁举行。
    中国方面参加签字仪式的，有中华全国总工会副主席刘宁一、书记处书记董昕、国际联络部副部长陈宇，中国煤矿工会全国委员会主席金直夫等。
    日本方面参加签字仪式的，有以古贺定为首的日本煤矿工会访华代表团全体人员。
    二十一时三十分，双方代表分别在协议上签了字。签字以后双方举杯表示祝贺。
    中国煤矿工会全国委员会和日本煤矿工会访华代表团协议的全文如下：
    中国煤矿工会全国委员会与日本煤矿工会访华代表团，通过这次交流，根据相互间迅速加深了的了解和友谊，为建立两国煤矿工人及其家属之间的真正兄弟般的团结与合作关系，并为在各自对现状理解的基础上加强两国工人和两国人民之间的友好和亲善，对下列协议取得了完全一致的意见。
    一、加强中国煤矿工会及其女工家属工作委员会与日本煤矿工会、日本煤矿主妇协议会之间的经验交流，各种资料的交换，互通书信，互派代表以及加强工人的技术经验和文化活动的交流。
    二、在促进两国煤矿工人及其家属之间的交流的同时，并与其他产业工人相互合作、为扩大发展各个方面的交流活动而努力。
    三、在为解决妨碍两国人民互相了解，阻挠两国人民友好和亲善的各种问题的工作上而共同努力。目前为早日恢复两国邦交和促进贸易而奋斗。
    四、为加深亚洲工人和他们的家属之间的互相了解和友谊而积极努力。
    中国煤矿工会全国委员会
