Title: Highland Sketches
Author: Bā Bō
Date: 1956-12-12
Page: 8

    Highland Sketches
  　Bā Bō
    Releasing the Logs [Note]
    On the summit of high mountains draped with forests,
    Lumberjacks push the timber onto the chutes:
    Like ten thousand horses galloping,
    Like a storm howling,
    Like the thunder of spring,
    Like a salute fired for the motherland;
    It is so joyous,
    It is so leaping,
    Spraying up a river of water droplets,
    Reflecting the sun's glorious rays in myriad directions.
  [Note] Forest workers in northwestern Sichuan build chutes out of wood on the mountains to transport timber. The chute runs from the mountaintop all the way to the riverside. Pushing the timber onto the chute allows it to fall directly into the river, called "releasing the logs."
    Floating the Logs [Note]
    Reefs! Please clear the way!
    This is the cradle of infants,
    This is the pillar of factories,
    This is the rifle stock in the hands of border guards,
    This is the treasure of the motherland's construction...
    Reefs! Please clear the way!
  [Note] Lumberjacks floating timber down the river are said to be "floating the logs."

### 高原小景
巴波
1956-12-12
第8版()
专栏：

    高原小景
  　巴波
    放洪〔注〕
    在披着森林的高山之巅，
    伐木者把木材推向滑道：
    像万马在奔腾，
    像暴风在呼啸，
    像春天的雷声，
    像为祖国射出的礼炮；
    它是那么欢腾，
    它是那么跳跃，
    迸溅起一江水珠，
    映着骄阳光芒万道。
  〔注〕川西北森林工人，为了运送木材，在山上用木料修成滑道。滑道从山顶一直修到江边。把木材推在滑道上，就可直落江中，叫做“放洪”。
    放漂〔注〕
    礁石啊！请让开道路！
    这是婴儿的摇床，
    这是工厂的梁柱，
    这是边防战士手中的枪托，
    这是祖国建设之宝……
    礁石啊！请让开道路！
    〔注〕伐木工人在江水中流送木材叫做“放漂”。
