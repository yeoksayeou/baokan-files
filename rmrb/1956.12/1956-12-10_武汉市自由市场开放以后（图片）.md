Title: Wuhan City's Free Market After Opening (Pictures)
Author: Liu Xinning
Date: 1956-12-10
Page: 3

　　Wuhan City's Free Market After Opening
　　After the opening of the free market under state leadership, transactions in Wuhan's vegetable markets are more active than before, the variety of vegetables has increased, and the quality has also improved.
　　　　　　　　　　　　　　Xinhua News Agency reporter Liu Xinning photo
　　This is a member of the Hongxing Vegetable Production Cooperative in Jiefang Township, a suburb of Wuhan, cutting fat and tender cabbage to supply the city.
　　This is a corner of the Lanling Road vegetable market in Wuhan. Salespersons are preparing all kinds of vegetables for customers.

### 武汉市自由市场开放以后（图片）

专栏：

　　武汉市自由市场开放以后
    在国家领导下的自由市场开放以后，武汉市各蔬菜市场的交易比以前更加活跃，蔬菜的品种增多了，质量也好起来了。
                                  新华社记者　刘馨宁摄
    这是武汉市郊解放乡红星蔬菜生产合作社社员把又肥又嫩的白菜割下来供应城市。
    这是武汉市兰陵路菜市场的一角。售货员在为顾客准备各式各样的蔬菜。
