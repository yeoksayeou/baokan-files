Title: Shanghai Winter Long-Distance Running Exercise
Author:
Date: 1956-12-28
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency Report: This winter, there are far more people participating in long-distance running sports in Shanghai than in previous years, and there are also female long-distance runners.

Every morning, hundreds of young people brave the cold air to run along the Bund of the Huangpu River and the main roads of various districts.

The Shanghai Municipal Sports Commission, the Shanghai Federation of Trade Unions, and the Shanghai Municipal Committee of the Communist Youth League jointly announced: From now until the end of March next year, male athletes who accumulate 25,000 meters in long-distance running and female athletes who accumulate 15,000 meters (running no less than twice a week) will all be given commemorative prizes.

### 上海冬季长跑锻炼

1956-12-28
第4版()
专栏：体育新闻

    上海冬季长跑锻炼
    据新华社讯　今年冬季，上海参加长跑体育运动的人比往年多得多，其中也出现了女子长跑运动员。
    每天清晨，在黄浦江边和各区的干道线上，有数以百计的青年冒着寒冷的空气在奔跑。
    上海市体育运动委员会、上海市工会联合会和青年团上海市委员会联合宣布：从目前起，到明年三月底止，男子运动员长跑路程累计达到二万五千公尺，女子达到一万五千公尺的（每周跑不少于二次），一律发给纪念奖品。
