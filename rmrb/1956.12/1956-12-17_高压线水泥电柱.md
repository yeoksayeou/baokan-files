Title: High-Voltage Line Cement Poles
Author: Zhao Chaozhen
Date: 1956-12-17
Page: 2

New Technology, New Products

    High-Voltage Line Cement Poles
    The Liaoyang Cement Products Factory, under the Ministry of Building Materials Industry, successfully trial-produced a high-voltage line cement pole this year under the commission of the Ministry of Electric Power Industry.
    Each high-voltage line cement pole is 22 meters high, assembled from ten cement poles. This type of pole can replace iron towers for high-voltage transmission lines, saving about 40% of steel and reducing project costs by about 50% compared to iron towers.
                  (Zhao Chaozhen)

### 高压线水泥电柱
赵朝珍
1956-12-17
第2版()
专栏：新技术、新产品

    高压线水泥电柱
    建筑材料工业部所属辽阳水泥制品厂今年接受电力工业部的委托，试制成功一种高压线水泥电柱。
    高压线水泥电柱每架高二十二公尺，由十根水泥电柱装接成。这种电柱可以代替高压输电线路的铁塔，比铁塔节约40％左右的钢材，降低工程造价50％左右。
                  （赵朝珍）
