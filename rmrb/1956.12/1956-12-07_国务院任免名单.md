Title: State Council Appointment and Dismissal List
Author:
Date: 1956-12-07
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, December 5th: The State Council of the People's Republic of China appointed and dismissed a number of personnel from state organs, consuls general stationed abroad, and institutions of higher learning on November 16th. The list is as follows:

Appointments:

	Han Zheyi as Director of the State Council General Bureau of Materials Supply, Wang Fengyuan, Li Gengxin, and Li Sijing as Deputy Directors;
	Zhang Jiping as Consul General of the People's Republic of China in Mumbai, Republic of India;
	Sun Yang, Yu Jianting, Wu Junyang, Fan Muhan, and Gao Yunping as members of the State Planning Commission;
	Jia Tuofu, Sun Zhiyuan, Song Shaowen, Han Zheyi, Gu Mu, Liu Daifeng, Wang Xin’san, Ye Lin, and Xue Zizheng as Deputy Directors of the State Economic Commission; Fang Zhida, Wang Gengjin, Lü Kebai, Li Kaixin, Fan Ruoyi, Zhang Beihua, Min Yifan, Liao Jili, Xie Bei’yi, and Han Zengsheng as members;
	Liu Xing and Li Bin as Deputy Directors of the State Construction Commission, Sun Liyu as Secretary-General;
	Li Yu'ang and Ai Chunan as Assistant Ministers of the Ministry of Finance;
	Zhao Zhigang as Vice Minister of the Ministry of Posts and Telecommunications, Li Yincang, Cheng Anyu, and Kuang Quanj as Assistant Ministers;
	Zhu Min as Assistant Minister of the Ministry of Agriculture;
	Qian Weichang, Chen Shihua, and Zhang Wei as Vice Presidents of Tsinghua University;
	Zhou Peiyuan as Vice President of Peking University;
	Tang Aoqing and Tong Dong as Vice Presidents of Northeast People's University;
	Li Shouheng as Vice President of Zhejiang University;
	Gong Yiqun as Vice President of Zhengzhou University;
	Chen Xujing as Vice President of Sun Yat-sen University;
	Mao Duo as President of the Central School for Political and Legal Cadres, Leng Chu and Xu Ping as Vice Presidents;
	Li Jinbao as Vice President of Beijing Institute of Political Science and Law;
	Peng Qingming as President of Shijiazhuang Normal College, Zhang Yizhi as Vice President;
	Hao Shaoyao, Ji Piliang, Ge Wa, and Temuerbagen as Vice Presidents of Inner Mongolia Normal College;
	Liu Haibin as President of Northwest Institute of Technology;
	Cheng Mingsheng as President of Xi'an Power Institute, Tian Hongbin and An Lequn as Vice Presidents;
	Wu Jizhou as President of Nanjing Aeronautical Institute;
	Zhao Jibin as President of Kaifeng Normal College;
	Li Junfu as President of Xinxiang Normal College, Du Mengmo, Nie Buwu, and Li Xiangyu as Vice Presidents;
	Zhang Jingwu as Vice President of Henan Medical College;
	Xia Jianbai, Jin Tongyin, Chen Yongling, and Liu Suxian as Vice Presidents of Wuhan Technical College of Surveying and Mapping;
	Li Guangdou as President of Zhongnan Institute of Political Science and Law, Lin Shan and Pan Renzhi as Vice Presidents;
	Yu Jiang as Vice President of Hubei Medical College;
	Xu Qizhi and Zheng Fang as Vice Presidents of Chengdu Institute of Technology;
	Wu Liren as President of Chengdu Institute of Radio Engineering, Huang Aimin and Xu Sidu as Vice Presidents.

Dismissals:

	Han Zheyi from the position of Deputy Director of the State Planning Commission, Wang Xin’san, Wang Fengyuan, and Ye Lin from the position of member;
	Sun Zhiyuan and Gu Mu from the position of Deputy Director of the State Construction Commission;
	Zhao Zhigang from the position of Assistant Minister of the Ministry of Posts and Telecommunications;
	Zhang Linchi from the position of Vice Minister of the Ministry of Agriculture;
	Luo Yuchuan, Yong Wentao, and Liu Chengdong from the position of Vice Minister of the Ministry of Forestry;
	Xia Jianbai from the position of Vice President of Tongji University;
	Peng Zhen from the position of President of the Central School for Political and Legal Cadres, Zhang Xiruo, Xie Juezai, Shi Liang, and Tao Xijin from the position of Vice President;
	Peng Qingming and Zhang Yizhi from the position of Vice President of Hebei Normal College;
	Tian Hongbin from the position of President of Northwest Institute of Technology;
	Jin Tongyin from the position of President of Qingdao Institute of Technology, Liu Suxian from the position of Vice President;
	Chen Yongling from the position of Vice President of South China Institute of Technology;
	Zheng Fang from the position of Vice President of Sichuan Chemical Industry College.
