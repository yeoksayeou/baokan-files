Author:

Yingtan-Xiamen Railway
The Yingtan-Xiamen Railway was constructed by the Railway Corps of the Chinese People's Liberation Army and hundreds of thousands of civilian laborers. After two years and one month, the rails have now been laid to Xiamen Island.

	A passenger train passes over the Laizhou Bridge. Photo by Li Wenxiu (Contribution from the PLA Pictorial)

	The train passes through the primitive forest area of Daiyun Mountain, the highest point of the Yingtan-Xiamen Railway and the boundary between southern and northern Fujian.
					Photo by Ye Dakai (Contribution from the Fujian Daily)

	Mr. Su Gongjie, an overseas Chinese from Indonesia returning to China for sightseeing, said to the young people on the train: "Our hope of more than forty years has been realized!"
				Photo by Li Wenxiu (Contribution from the PLA Pictorial)

	Boxes of Fujian's local specialties—lychees and dried longans—are being gathered at the railway station, ready to be transported to other provinces.
	Photo by Ye Dakai (Contribution from the Fujian Daily)

### 鹰厦铁路（图片）

1956-12-16
第2版()
专栏：

    鹰厦铁路
    鹰厦铁路是由中国人民解放军铁道兵和十几万民工负责修建的，经过两年零一个月的时间，现在铁轨已经铺到了厦门岛。
    旅客列车通过莱州大桥。　　　李文秀摄（解放军画报社稿）
    火车通过鹰厦铁路最高点——闽南、闽北交界处戴云山的原始森林区。
                                  叶大开摄（福建日报社稿）
    从印度尼西亚回国观光的华侨苏公杰先生，在列车上对青年们说：“我们四十多年的愿望实现了！”
                        李文秀摄（解放军画报社稿）
    成箱的福建省土特产——荔枝桂元干正往火车站集中，准备运到省外去。
    叶大开摄（福建日报社稿）
