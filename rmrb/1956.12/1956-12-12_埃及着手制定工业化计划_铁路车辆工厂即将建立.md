Title: Egypt Embarks on Industrialization Plan: Railway Vehicle Factory to be Established
Author:
Date: 1956-12-12
Page: 6

　　Xinhua News Agency, December 10th, TASS Cairo, December 10th: According to "Egyptian Progress," the Egyptian Planning Committee, under the direction of Egyptian Minister of Industry Sidqi, is formulating a five-year plan for Egypt's industrialization.

　　The construction of sugar and cement industrial enterprises, for which Egypt has abundant raw material reserves, occupies a major position in the plan.

　　Xinhua News Agency, December 11th, TASS Cairo, December 9th: According to the Middle East News Agency, Egypt will build a factory in Helwan to produce railway vehicles and other equipment. Once this factory is operational, Egypt can save a large amount of foreign exchange currently used to import railway vehicles.

### 埃及着手制定工业化计划  铁路车辆工厂即将建立

1956-12-12
第6版()
专栏：

    埃及着手制定工业化计划
    铁路车辆工厂即将建立
    据新华社10日讯　塔斯社开罗10日讯：据“埃及进步报”报道，埃及计划委员会在埃及工业部长西德基的主持下正在制定埃及工业化的五年计划。
    计划中占主要地位的是建设制糖和水泥工业企业，这些企业所需要的原料在埃及有大量的蕴藏。
    新华社11日讯　塔斯社开罗9日讯：据中东通讯社报道，埃及将在赫卢安建设一座生产铁路车辆和其他装备的工厂。这个厂一开工，埃及就可以节省目前用于进口铁路车辆的大量外汇。
