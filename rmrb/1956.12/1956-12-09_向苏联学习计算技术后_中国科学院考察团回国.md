Title: Chinese Academy of Sciences Delegation Returns After Studying Computing Technology in the Soviet Union
Author:
Date: 1956-12-09
Page: 1

　  Having Studied Computing Technology in the Soviet Union
　　Chinese Academy of Sciences Delegation Returns Home
    Xinhua News Agency Dispatch: The Chinese Academy of Sciences Computing Technology Delegation returned to Beijing from the Soviet Union today.
    The head of the delegation told Xinhua News Agency reporters: Thanks to the assistance provided by the Soviet Union this time, we now have full confidence in establishing a relatively well-equipped Computing Technology Institute in Beijing, and gradually solving the urgent scientific research, product design, and trial production tasks of electronic computers required by our country.
    The delegation consisted of eleven experts. They systematically investigated various aspects of computing technology in the Soviet Union, and also studied the experience of the Soviet Academy of Sciences in establishing its Computing Technology Institute. They also preliminarily discussed issues such as exchanging technical information and sending interns to study in the Soviet Union with the institute.

### 向苏联学习计算技术后　中国科学院考察团回国

1956-12-09
第1版()
专栏：

　  向苏联学习计算技术后
　　中国科学院考察团回国
    据新华社讯　中国科学院计算技术考察团今天全部由苏联回到北京。
    这个考察团的负责人对新华社记者说：由于这次苏联给予我们的帮助，已使我们具有充分的信心在北京建立起一座设备比较完善的计算技术研究所，逐步地解决我国所迫切需要的电子计算机的科学研究、产品设计和试制任务。
    考察团由十一位专家组成，他们在苏联系统地考察了有关计算技术各方面的问题，也学习了苏联科学院计算技术研究所建所的经验，还和这个研究所初步议定了交换技术资料、选派实习生到苏联学习等问题。
