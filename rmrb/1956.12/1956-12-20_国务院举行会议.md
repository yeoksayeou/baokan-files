Title: State Council Holds Meeting
Author: 
Date: 1956-12-20
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, 18th. The 41st plenary session of the State Council was held on the morning of the 18th.

    After hearing an explanation by Vice Premier and Minister of Finance Li Xiannian, the meeting adopted the "Report on the Adjustment and Estimated Implementation of the 1956 National Budget." This report will be submitted to the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress for review.

    After hearing an explanation by Vice Minister of Finance Rong Zihe, the meeting adopted the "Regulations on the 1957 National Economic Construction Bonds (Draft)," and decided to submit the draft regulations to the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress for review.

    The meeting also discussed wage issues.

    The meeting adopted the "Organizational Regulations (Draft) of the People's Congress of the Southeast Guizhou Miao and Dong Autonomous Prefecture," the "Organizational Regulations (Draft) of the People's Committee of the Southeast Guizhou Miao and Dong Autonomous Prefecture," the "Organizational Regulations (Draft) of the People's Congress of the Qiannan Buyi and Miao Autonomous Prefecture," and the "Organizational Regulations (Draft) of the People's Committee of the Qiannan Buyi and Miao Autonomous Prefecture," and approved the "Decision of the State Council on Revoking Jixi County, Heilongjiang Province, and Establishing Jixi City."

    Finally, the meeting approved appointments and removals.

### 国务院举行会议

1956-12-20
第1版()
专栏：

    国务院举行会议
    新华社18日讯　国务院全体会议第四十一次会议在18日上午举行。
    会议听了副总理兼财政部长李先念的说明后，通过了“关于1956年国家预算调整和预计执行情况的报告”。这个报告将提请全国人民代表大会常务委员会审议。
    会议听了财政部副部长戎子和的说明后，通过了“1957年国家经济建设公债条例（草案）”，并决定将条例草案提请全国人民代表大会常务委员会审议。
    会议并就工资问题进行了讨论。
    会议通过了“黔东南苗族侗族自治州人民代表大会组织条例（草案）”、“黔东南苗族侗族自治州人民委员会组织条例（草案）”，“黔南布依族苗族自治州人民代表大会组织条例（草案）”、“黔南布依族苗族自治州人民委员会组织条例（草案）”，批准了“国务院关于撤销黑龙江省鸡西县设立鸡西市的决定”。
    会议最后通过了任免事项。
