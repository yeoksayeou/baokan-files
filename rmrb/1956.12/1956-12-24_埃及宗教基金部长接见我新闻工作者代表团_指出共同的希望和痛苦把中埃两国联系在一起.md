Title: Egyptian Minister of Religious Endowments Receives Chinese Journalists Delegation, Points Out That Shared Hopes and Suffering Connect China and Egypt

Author: 
Date: 1956-12-24
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, Cairo, December 23rd. Egyptian Minister of Religious Endowments, Ahmed Hassan al-Baquri, who had previously visited China, received the Chinese journalists delegation visiting Egypt on the 22nd.

Baquri told the Chinese journalists that shared hopes and suffering connect China and Egypt. He pointed out that Egypt and China are connected by three factors: both countries have civilizations with thousands of years of history; both countries have suffered from imperialist aggression; China has achieved revolutionary victory and is becoming increasingly prosperous, and Egypt has also achieved revolutionary victory.

At the end of the conversation, Baquri asked the Chinese journalists to convey his greetings to the leaders of the Chinese government.

### 埃及宗教基金部长接见我新闻工作者代表团  指出共同的希望和痛苦把中埃两国联系在一起

1956-12-24
第5版()
专栏：

    埃及宗教基金部长接见我新闻工作者代表团
    指出共同的希望和痛苦把中埃两国联系在一起
    新华社开罗23日电　曾经访问过中国的埃及宗教基金部长艾哈迈德·哈桑·巴库里22日接见了中国新闻工作者访问埃及代表团。
    巴库里对中国新闻工作者说，共同的希望和痛苦把中埃两国联系在一起。他指出，埃及和中国是由三个因素联系在一起的：两国都有几千年历史的文明；两国都遭受过帝国主义侵略；中国取得了革命胜利，日益繁荣，埃及也取得了革命胜利。
    巴库里在谈话结束的时候，请中国新闻工作者代他向中国政府领导人致意。
