Title: UN Personnel Overseeing Suez Canal Clearance Arrive in Cairo
Author:
Date: 1956-12-11
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, Cairo, December 10th - TASS Cairo, December 10th: Personnel from the expert committee appointed by UN Secretary-General Hammarskjöld to oversee the clearance of the Suez Canal have arrived in Cairo. The committee consists of nine Americans, six Danes, and five Dutchmen, and is headed by General Raymond Albert Wheeler, advisor to the International Bank for Reconstruction and Development.

### 监督清理运河工作的联合国人员到达开罗

1956-12-11
第6版()
专栏：

    监督清理运河工作的联合国人员到达开罗
    据新华社10日讯　塔斯社开罗10日讯：联合国秘书长哈马舍尔德委派的监督清理苏伊士运河专家委员会的人员已经到达开罗。这个委员会由九个美国人、六个丹麦人和五个荷兰人组成，它的负责人是国际复兴开发银行顾问雷蒙·艾伯特·韦勒将军。
