Title: South Communist Delegation Leaves Warsaw
Author:
Date: 1956-12-31
Page: 5

    Xinhua News Agency, December 30th, Warsaw Dispatch: According to the Polish Press Agency Warsaw dispatch: The delegation of the League of Communists of Yugoslavia left Warsaw on December 29th.

### 南共代表团离华沙

1956-12-31
第5版()
专栏：

    南共代表团离华沙
    据新华社30日讯　据波兰通讯社华沙讯：南斯拉夫共产主义者联盟代表团在12月29日离开华沙。
