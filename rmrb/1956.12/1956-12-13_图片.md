    NPC representative and famous mathematician Professor Hua Luogeng inspected the Xi'an Power Institute in early December. In the students' dormitory, he asked the newly arrived southern students if they were accustomed to the climate of the Northwest and listened to their opinions on their daily lives. Xinhua News Agency reporter Chen Jie photographed.
