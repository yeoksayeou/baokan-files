Title: Mao Zedong, Liu Shaoqi, and Chen Yun Meet with Murata Shozo and Hold Talks

Author: 
Date: 1956-12-11
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, December 10th: Chairman Mao Zedong, Chairman Liu Shaoqi, and Acting Premier Chen Yun met with Murata Shozo, President of the Japanese Commodity Exhibition and Chairman of the Japan International Trade Promotion Association, this afternoon for talks lasting more than an hour. ### 毛泽东、刘少奇、陈云接见村田省藏并进行了谈话

1956-12-11
第1版()
专栏：

    毛泽东、刘少奇、陈云
    接见村田省藏并进行了谈话
    新华社10日讯　毛泽东主席、刘少奇委员长和陈云代总理，今天下午接见日本商品展览会总裁、日本国际贸易促进协会会长村田省藏，进行了一个多小时的谈话。（附图片）
    毛泽东主席和村田省藏握手。　　　新华社记者　吕厚民摄
