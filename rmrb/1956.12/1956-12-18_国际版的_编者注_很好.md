Title: The “Editor's Note” in the International Section is Very Good
Author: Chen Rushu Zheng Shaotang
Date: 1956-12-18
Page: 8

Column: Readers, Authors, Editors

    The “Editor's Note” in the International Section is Very Good
    In the international news on pages 5 and 6 of the December 6th People's Daily, the editors added two annotations, one about Romania welcoming the National Assembly elections at the beginning of next year, and the other about the arrest of Lubis in Indonesia. This is a good practice, and we can gain a more comprehensive understanding of the matter and increase our knowledge of international issues from the editor's introduction. However, in Comrade Gomulka's speech published on December 9th, the "Bydgoszcz Incident" and "Mr. Mikolajczyk" mentioned are not very clear to all readers, and annotations should also be added to explain them.
              (Chen Rushu Zheng Shaotang)

### 国际版的“编者注”很好
陈如舒  郑绍唐
1956-12-18
第8版()
专栏：读者、作者、编者

    国际版的“编者注”很好
    12月6日人民日报第五、六版国际新闻中，编者加上两条注解，一条是关于罗马尼亚迎接明年初国民议会选举的，一条是关于印度尼西亚卢比斯被捕的。这样作很好，我们可以从编者的介绍中，比较全面地了解这件事情，增加一些国际问题的知识。但12月9日刊载的哥穆尔卡同志的讲话中提到的“比得哥熙事件”“米科拉兹柯先生”，读者不是都了解得很清楚的，也应该加注说明一下。
              （陈如舒  郑绍唐）
