Title: Nepalese King's Private Secretary Returns Home
Author:
Date: 1956-12-10
Page: 1

　　Xinhua News Agency, Guangzhou, December 8th: Lok Darshan, the Chief Private Secretary to the King of Nepal, left our country today via Guangzhou after a short visit to Beijing.

### 尼泊尔国王私人秘书回国

1956-12-10
第1版()
专栏：

　　尼泊尔国王私人秘书回国
    新华社广州8日电　尼泊尔国王首席私人秘书洛克·达山在北京作短期访问后，今天已经经广州离开我国。
