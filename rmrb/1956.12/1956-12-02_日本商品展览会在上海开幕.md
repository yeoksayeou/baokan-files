Title: Japanese Commodity Exhibition Opens in Shanghai
Author:
Date: 1956-12-02
Page: 1
　　
Xinhua News Agency, Shanghai, December 1st. The Japanese Commodity Exhibition solemnly opened at 2:00 PM today at the fountain plaza in front of the Shanghai Sino-Soviet Friendship Building.

　　At 3:30 PM, Vice Mayor Cao Diqiu cut the ribbon. Four thousand Chinese and foreign guests entered the various exhibition halls of the Sino-Soviet Friendship Building as the first group of visitors to the exhibition. In the evening, the gilded red star tower on the building shone like crystal. In the Friendship Cinema Hall of the building, President Shigezo Murata and Group Leader Eiichi Suketani held a grand reception to entertain people from all walks of life in Shanghai.

### 日本商品展览会在上海开幕

1956-12-02
第1版()
专栏：

　　日本商品展览会在上海开幕
    据新华社上海1日电　日本商品展览会今天下午二时在上海中苏友好大厦前面的喷泉广场上隆重举行开幕典礼。
    下午三时半，曹荻秋副市长剪彩。四千中外来宾作为展览会的第一批观众进入中苏友好大厦的各个展览馆参观。入晚，大厦上的红星镏金高塔照耀得像水晶一样。大厦中的友谊电影院大厅内，村田省藏总裁和宿谷荣一团长举行盛大酒会，招待上海各界人士。
