Title: Blood Bank
Author: Wang Jingzhi
Date: 1956-12-30
Page: 8

    Blood Bank
    Wang Jingzhi
    South Korean brothers, think about it!
    Who are your relatives? Who are demons and monsters?
    Distinguish between friend and foe, differentiate between kin and enemy.
    Don't mistake your compatriots for enemies,
    Don't mistake the nation's mortal enemy for your benefactor.
    Who started the civil war, forcing you to shed blood for others?
    Who forces you to destroy each other, to annihilate yourselves?
    Forcing you to risk your lives isn't enough,
    They even open blood banks to lure you into selling your blood.
    They want to use your steaming hot blood
    To breed cold, hard gold coins;
    They want to use your fiery red blood
    To build higher mountains of glittering gold;
    They want to use your fresh, living blood
    To irrigate the paradise atop the skyscraper.
    Don't use a drop of blood to strengthen the beastly legions;
    Don't use a drop of blood to feed the invading battleships;
    Don't use pure blood
    To nourish the cruel heart of the munitions king;
    Don't use noble blood
    To add to the licentious rouge in the nightclubs.
    Treasure your own blood,
    Protect your own blood vessels.
    Don't waste a single drop of blood,
    Concentrate its use, use it in the most crucial battle.
    Use the light of blood to eliminate the fascist specter,
    Use white blood cells like they devour bacteria
    To devour America's Hitler—the war criminal!
    Postscript: The People's Daily on December 17th said: The United States and the Syngman Rhee clique have opened a "blood bank" in Seoul, enticing poor, destitute people to sell their blood. Shin Dong-gyun, a student at a higher industrial school in Yeongdeungpo, South Korea, died from excessive blood selling.

### 血液银行
汪静之
1956-12-30
第8版()
专栏：

    血液银行
    汪静之
    南朝鲜兄弟，想想罢！
    谁是亲人？谁是凶神恶煞？
    敌我要认清，亲仇要区别。
    不要把自己的同胞错认作冤家，
    不要把民族的死敌错认作爷。
    谁发动内战逼你们，替人流血？
    谁逼你们自相摧残，自相毁灭？
    逼你们卖命还不算，
    又开了血液银行诱惑你们出卖鲜血。
    要用你们的热腾腾的血
    去繁殖冷冰冰的金元；
    要用你们的红火火的血
    去加高黄粲粲的金山；
    要用你们的活鲜鲜的血
    去灌溉摩天楼上的乐园。
    不要用一滴血去增强野兽兵团；
    不要用一滴血去喂养侵略的炮舰；
    不要用纯洁的血
    去营养军火大王残忍的心肝；
    不要用高尚的血
    去增添夜总会上淫荡的红颜。
    要珍视自己的鲜血，
    要保护自己的血管。
    一滴血也不许浪费，
    要集中使用，用在最重要的一关。
    要用血的光芒消灭掉法西斯的魔影，
    要用白血球像吞灭细菌一般
    吞灭掉美国的希特勒——战争罪犯！
    后记：12月17日人民日报上说：美国和李承晚集团在汉城开设了一个“血液银行”，并诱惑贫苦的无以为生的人去卖血。住在南朝鲜永登浦的高等工业学校学生申东均，因为卖血过多而死。
