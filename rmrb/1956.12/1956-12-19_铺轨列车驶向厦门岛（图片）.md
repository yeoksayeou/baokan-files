Title: Track-laying Train Heads to Xiamen Island (Photo)
Author: Xiang Zhi
Date: 1956-12-19
Page: 2

    Track-laying Train Heads to Xiamen Island
    The track-laying train of the Yingtan-Xiamen Railway passes over the Xiamen Seawall. On the entire seawall, crowds are bustling about, eagerly watching this miracle appearing on the sea. Xinhua News Agency reporter Xiang Zhi photographed
