Title: Menon and Others Leave Hungary
Author:
Date: 1956-12-10
Page: 5
　　
Xinhua News Agency, Budapest, December 9th: Menon, the Indian Ambassador to the Soviet Union and concurrently Minister to Hungary, and Kosla, the Indian Ambassador to Czechoslovakia, left Hungary on December 7th after staying for several days.

　　Before leaving, Kadar, the Prime Minister of the Hungarian Revolutionary Worker-Peasant Government, received Menon.

### 梅农等离开匈牙利

1956-12-10
第5版()
专栏：

　  梅农等离开匈牙利
    新华社布达佩斯9日电　印度驻苏联大使兼驻匈牙利公使梅农同印度驻捷克斯洛伐克大使科斯拉，在匈牙利逗留了数天之后，已经在12月7日离开了匈牙利。
    在离开前，匈牙利工农革命政府总理卡达尔接见了梅农。
