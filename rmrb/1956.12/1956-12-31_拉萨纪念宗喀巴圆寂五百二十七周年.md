Title: Lhasa Commemorates the 527th Anniversary of Tsongkhapa's Passing

Author: 
Date: 1956-12-31
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Lhasa, December 28th. On the evening of the 26th, Lhasa lit millions of butter lamps to commemorate the 527th anniversary of the passing of Tsongkhapa, the founder of the Gelug school of Tibetan Buddhism.

　　All the monasteries in Lhasa held prayers on that day. Officials of the Tibetan local government, in accordance with traditional customs, put on winter clothes and went to the Potala Palace to worship and chant sutras before the stupas of the successive Dalai Lamas.

　　On the night of the 26th, the sounds of Buddhist chants echoed in the monasteries, and the lamas chanted mourning scriptures. Citizens sat around their homes drinking auspicious porridge. The Buddhist lamps burned until late at night.

### 拉萨纪念宗喀巴圆寂五百二十七周年

1956-12-31
第4版()
专栏：

    拉萨纪念宗喀巴圆寂五百二十七周年
    新华社拉萨28日电　26日晚上，拉萨市点起千万盏酥油灯，纪念喇嘛教黄教创始人宗喀巴圆寂五百二十七周年。
    拉萨市各个寺院都在当天举行了祈祷。西藏地方政府的官员们按照传统的习俗，换上冬装到布达拉宫历世达赖喇嘛的灵塔前膜拜和诵经。
    26日夜里，各寺院里佛号长鸣，喇嘛们诵着悼念经文。市民们围坐在自己家里喝着象征吉祥的稀粥。佛灯一直燃点到深夜。
