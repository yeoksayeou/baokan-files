Title: All-China Federation of Industry and Commerce Elects New Leaders
Author:
Date: 1956-12-25
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, December 24th—The Second Executive Committee of the All-China Federation of Industry and Commerce held its first meeting this afternoon.

    The meeting elected Chen Shutong and sixty-seven others as members of the Standing Committee of the Second Executive Committee of the All-China Federation of Industry and Commerce. At the same time, Chen Shutong was elected as the Chairman of the Second Executive Committee of the All-China Federation of Industry and Commerce, and Li Zhuchen, Zhang Naiqi, Xu Dixin, Sheng Pihua, Rong Yiren, Chen Jingshe, Huang Changshui, Hu Ziang, Gong Tianmin, Sha Qianli, Wu Xuezhi, Le Songsheng, Bi Mingqi, Deng Wenzhao, and Han Wangchen were elected as Vice Chairmen. Xiang Shuxiang was elected as Secretary-General. The meeting also approved the candidates for Deputy Secretary-General.
    After discussion, the meeting decided to entrust the Standing Committee with the study and handling of issues such as implementing the resolutions of the conference, specific methods for convening a national conference of advanced producers and advanced workers in industry and commerce, establishing a special committee for mutual aid funds for the livelihood of industrialists and businessmen, and handling proposals from the conference.

### 全国工商联选出新的领导人员

1956-12-25
第1版()
专栏：

    全国工商联选出新的领导人员
    新华社24日讯　中华全国工商业联合会第二届执行委员会今天下午举行第一次会议。
    这次会议选举出陈叔通等六十八人为全国工商联第二届执行委员会常务委员会常务委员。同时，选举陈叔通为全国工商联第二届执行委员会主任委员，李烛尘、章乃器、许涤新、盛丕华、荣毅仁、陈经畬、黄长水、胡子昂、巩天民、沙千里、吴雪之、乐松生、毕鸣岐、邓文钊、韩望尘等十五人为副主任委员，项叔翔为秘书长。会议并通过了副秘书长人选。
    会议经过讨论，决定把关于贯彻大会决议问题、关于召开全国工商界先进生产者和先进工作者代表会议的具体办法问题、关于设立工商界生活互助金专门委员会问题、关于大会提案处理问题等交给常务委员会研究处理。
