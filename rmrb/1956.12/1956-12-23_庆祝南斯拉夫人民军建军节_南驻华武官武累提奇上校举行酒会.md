Title: Yugoslav Military Attaché in China, Colonel Vuletech, Holds Reception to Celebrate the Anniversary of the Founding of the Yugoslav People's Army
Author:
Date: 1956-12-23
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, December 22nd. Colonel Bruno Vuletech, the Yugoslav military attaché in China, and his wife, held a reception at 5 p.m. today to celebrate the 15th anniversary of the founding of the Yugoslav People's Army.

Marshal Peng Dehuai, Minister of National Defense, and Ji Pengfei, Vice Minister of Foreign Affairs, were invited to attend the reception. Also present were senior generals of the Chinese People's Liberation Army, including General Chen Geng, Senior General Li Da, Senior General Xiao Hua, Senior Admiral Wang Hongkun of the Navy, Lieutenant General Wang Bingzhang of the Air Force, and representatives of various military branches and arms.

Yugoslav Ambassador to China, Popovic, and military attachés of various countries' embassies in China also attended today's reception.

Yugoslav Military Attaché to China, Bruno Vuletech, delivered a speech at the reception. After describing the growth of the Yugoslav armed forces and their glorious history during the anti-fascist struggle, he went on to say: The recent mutual visits of Chinese and Yugoslav military delegations have made great contributions to the mutual understanding and the development of friendly cooperation between the two countries' people's armies.

Marshal Peng Dehuai said in his speech: The Yugoslav People's Army is an army with a glorious history of struggle. In the anti-fascist struggle, it experienced extremely arduous roads and fought heroic battles, establishing outstanding merits for the Yugoslav people, and in this struggle, it developed and strengthened itself. All of this has left a very kind and very deep impression on all personnel of the Chinese People's Liberation Army. Peng Dehuai said that with the strengthening of friendly relations between the two peoples, through the efforts of the ambassadors and military attachés of the two countries, and through the mutual visits of the two military delegations, our friendship has obtained a fully expressed and developed foundation. Over the past two years, we have deeply felt that the fraternal friendship between the two peoples and armies is fully in line with the interests of the Chinese and Yugoslav peoples, and is conducive to the unity within the socialist camp. This unity, coupled with the unity of all peace-loving countries, is a powerful guarantee for maintaining world peace.

At the reception, the hosts and guests frequently toasted the sincere friendship between the Chinese and Yugoslav peoples and the two countries' people's armies.

### 庆祝南斯拉夫人民军建军节  南驻华武官武累提奇上校举行酒会

1956-12-23
第1版()
专栏：

    庆祝南斯拉夫人民军建军节
    南驻华武官武累提奇上校举行酒会
    新华社22日讯　今天下午五时，南斯拉夫驻华大使馆武官布鲁诺·武累提奇上校及夫人举行酒会，庆祝南斯拉夫人民军建军十五周年。
    国防部长彭德怀元帅、外交部副部长姬鹏飞应邀出席了酒会。出席酒会的还有中国人民解放军的高级将领陈赓大将、李达上将、萧华上将、王宏坤海军上将、王秉璋空军中将等以及各军种、兵种的代表。
    南斯拉夫驻华大使波波维奇和各国驻华大使馆武官也出席了今天的酒会。
    南斯拉夫驻华大使馆武官布鲁诺·武累提奇在酒会上首先致词。他在叙述了南斯拉夫武装力量的成长过程和在反法西斯斗争时期的光荣历史后，接着说：最近中、南两国军事代表团的互相访问，对于两国人民军队的互相了解和友好合作的发展作出了很大的贡献。
    彭德怀元帅致词说：南斯拉夫人民军是一支具有光荣斗争历史的军队，她在反法西斯的斗争中，经历过极其艰苦的道路，进行过英勇的战斗，为南斯拉夫人民树立过卓越的功绩，并且在这个斗争中使自己发展壮大起来。这一切，都在中国人民解放军的全体人员中，留下了非常亲切和非常深刻的印象。彭德怀说，随着两国人民友好关系的加强，经过两国大使和武官的努力，经过双方军事代表团的互相访问，我们的友谊已经获得充分表达和发展的基础。两年来我们深刻地体会到两国人民和军队之间的兄弟友谊，是完全符合中、南两国人民利益的，是有助于社会主义阵营内部团结的。这种团结加上一切爱好和平国家的团结，就是维护世界和平的有力保证。
    酒会上宾主们频频为中、南两国人民和两国人民军队的诚挚友谊干杯。
