Title: Vietnam People's Army Sends Letter to International Commission Protesting South Vietnam's Violation of Demilitarized Zone
Author:
Date: 1956-12-09
Page: 5
Xinhua News Agency, Hanoi, December 8th: The Vietnam News Agency reports that the Vietnam People's Army delegation wrote to the International Commission's mobile inspection team working in the Demilitarized Zone on December 5th, protesting the illegal entry of South Vietnamese military and administrative personnel into the Demilitarized Zone.

　　More than 250 South Vietnamese military and administrative personnel illegally entered the Demilitarized Zone on December 2nd in 26 vehicles. These personnel approached the temporary military demarcation line, some even standing on the Hien Luong Bridge over the Ben Hai River to take photographs towards the North.

　　In the letter, the Vietnam People's Army delegation requested the mobile inspection team to pay attention to this matter when performing tasks in the area.

### 越人民军致函国际委员会　抗议南越侵犯非军事区

1956-12-09
第5版()
专栏：

　　越人民军致函国际委员会
　　抗议南越侵犯非军事区
    据新华社河内8日电　据越南通讯社讯：越南人民军代表团12月5日写信给在非军事区工作的国际委员会机动视察小组，抗议越南南方军政人员非法进入非军事区。
    二百五十多个越南南方的军政人员在12月2日分乘二十六辆汽车非法进入非军事区。这些人员走近临时军事分界线，有的甚至站在边海河的贤良桥上向北方摄取照片。
    越南人民军代表团在信里要求机动视察小组在到这个地区执行任务时注意这件事情。
