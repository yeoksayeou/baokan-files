Title: French Communist Party Central Committee Member Figuères Released

Author:

Date: 1956-12-21
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, Paris, 20th—Following strong protests from the French people, French Communist Party Central Committee member Figuères, who was arrested by French authorities on the 16th, was released on the 18th. "L'Humanité" pointed out that Figuères' release is a victory for the progressive forces in France.

### 法共中委斐盖尔获释

1956-12-21
第5版()
专栏：

    法共中委斐盖尔获释
    新华社20日讯　巴黎消息：在法国人民的强烈抗议下，16日被法国当局逮捕的法共中央委员斐盖尔已于18日获释。“人道报”指出，斐盖尔获释是法国进步力量的胜利。
