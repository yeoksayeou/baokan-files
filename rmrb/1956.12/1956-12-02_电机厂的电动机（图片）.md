　　Electric Motors from the Motor Factory

　　The first phase of construction at the Harbin Electric Motor Factory has been completed, and it officially began production on November 15th. It is China's first modern and largest electric motor manufacturing plant, capable of producing medium and large-sized hydro-turbine generator units, and various AC and DC motors and generators. This factory has been producing while under construction, and to date, it has manufactured two hundred and forty types, totaling several thousand electric motors and generators, supporting nearly a thousand hydroelectric power stations, factories, and mines across the country. This is a picture of some of the completed electric motors.

                                Photographed by Yu Min (Manuscript from Heilongjiang Daily)

### 电机厂的电动机（图片）
于敏
1956-12-02
第2版()
专栏：

　　电机厂的电动机
    哈尔滨电机厂第一期工程已经完成，11月15日开始正式生产。它是我国第一座近代化的最大的电机制造厂，能够制造中型和大型的水轮发电机组，各式交流、直流电动机和发电机。这个厂是边建设边生产的，到现在为止已经生产了二百四十种、共几千台的电动机和发电机，支援了全国近千个水电站、工厂和矿山。这是已经装成的部分电动机。
                                于敏摄　（黑龙江日报社稿）
