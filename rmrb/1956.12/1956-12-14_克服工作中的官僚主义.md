Title: Overcoming Bureaucracy in Work
Author: 
Date: 1956-12-14
Page: 2

Reaction to Criticism

    Overcoming Bureaucracy in Work
    Letter from the Chinese Communist Party Fushun Steel Plant Third Workshop Branch Committee: After the People's Daily published the article "Why Did Guo Junquan Rise and Fall Three Times?" on September 5th, we immediately conducted a review at the branch committee meeting and believe the criticism is entirely correct. The main reasons for this problem are: we have subjectivist and bureaucratic work styles in our work; we did not adopt patient persuasion and education towards comrades who made mistakes, but wrongly used criticism and wage reductions. In order to learn from the lessons and improve our work, we must strengthen our study of the Party's policies and guidelines in the future, and overcome subjectivism and bureaucracy in our work.

### 克服工作中的官僚主义

1956-12-14
第2版()
专栏：对批评的反应

    克服工作中的官僚主义
    中共抚顺钢厂第三车间支部委员会来信：9月5日人民日报发表了“郭俊全为什么三起三落”一文后，我们即在支委会上进行了检查，认为批评是完全正确的。存在这一问题的主要原因：是我们在工作中存在着主观主义和官僚主义的作风；对待犯错误的同志，不是采取耐心说服教育，而是错误地采用了批评、降薪的办法。为了吸取教训、改进工作，今后我们一定要加强对党的政策方针的学习，克服在工作中的主观主义和官僚主义。
