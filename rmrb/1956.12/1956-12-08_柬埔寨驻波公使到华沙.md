Title: Cambodian Envoy to Poland Arrives in Warsaw
Author:
Date: 1956-12-08
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, Warsaw, December 7th. According to a report by the Polish Press Agency: The first Ambassador of the Kingdom of Cambodia to Poland, Nhiek Tioulong, arrived in Warsaw on the 6th. Edward Bartol, Director of the Protocol Department of the Polish Ministry of Foreign Affairs, with the rank of Minister, welcomed him at the train station.

### 柬埔寨驻波公使到华沙

1956-12-08
第5版()
专栏：

　　柬埔寨驻波公使到华沙
    新华社华沙7日电　据波兰通讯社报道：首任柬埔寨王国驻波兰公使兰·涅特6日到达华沙。波兰外交部公使衔交际司司长爱德华·巴托尔曾到火车站欢迎。
