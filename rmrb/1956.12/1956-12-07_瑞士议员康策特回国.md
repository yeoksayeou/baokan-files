Title: Swiss Parliamentarian Conzett Returns Home
Author:
Date: 1956-12-07
Page: 1
　  
Xinhua News Agency, 6th. Swiss Federal National Councilor Hans Conzett and his wife left Beijing by plane this morning to return to their country.

### 瑞士议员康策特回国

1956-12-07

专栏：

　  瑞士议员康策特回国
