Title: Swiss Parliamentarian Conzett Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1956-12-01
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, 30th - Swiss Federal National Council member Hans Conzett and his wife arrived in Beijing by plane this afternoon. Conzett is visiting China at the invitation of the Chinese People's Institute of Foreign Affairs after attending the 45th Inter-Parliamentary Union Conference held in Bangkok this month.
    Wu Maosun, Secretary-General of the Chinese People's Institute of Foreign Affairs, and Swiss Minister to China, Benoît, and his wife were at the airport to welcome them.

### 瑞士议员康塞特到京

1956-12-01
第1版()
专栏：

　　瑞士议员康塞特到京
    新华社30日讯　瑞士联邦国民议会议员汉斯·康塞特偕夫人在今天下午乘飞机到达北京。康塞特是在参加本月在曼谷召开的各国议会联盟第四十五届会议以后，应中国人民外交学会的邀请来中国访问的。
    中国人民外交学会秘书长吴茂荪，瑞士驻中国公使贝努义和夫人都到机场欢迎。
