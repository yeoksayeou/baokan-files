Title: Zheng Zhenduo and Feng Jiping Host Reception to Bid Farewell to Korean National Art Troupe
Author:
Date: 1956-12-23
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, December 22nd: Zheng Zhenduo, Vice Minister of Culture, and Feng Jiping, Deputy Mayor of Beijing, held a reception at the Beijing Hotel this evening to bid farewell to the National Folk Art Troupe of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea, which is about to leave Beijing.

Kim Kwi-nam, Chargé d'Affaires ad interim of the Embassy of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea in China, was invited to attend the reception.

At the reception, Zheng Zhenduo congratulated the Korean artists on their successful performances in China.

In his speech, Yun Du-heon, head of the art troupe, expressed his gratitude for the warm hospitality received in China and wished for the development of cultural exchanges between Korea and China. A dance party was held after the reception.

### 郑振铎、冯基平举行酒会  欢送朝鲜民族艺术剧团

1956-12-23
第1版()
专栏：

    郑振铎、冯基平举行酒会
    欢送朝鲜民族艺术剧团
    据新华社22日讯　文化部副部长郑振铎、北京市副市长冯基平今天晚上在北京饭店举行酒会，欢送即将离开北京的朝鲜民主主义人民共和国国立民族艺术剧团。
    朝鲜民主主义人民共和国驻中国大使馆临时代办金贵南应邀出席了酒会。
    郑振铎在酒会上，祝贺朝鲜艺术家在中国的成功演出。
    艺术剧团团长尹斗宪在讲话中，对在中国受到的热情招待表示感谢，并祝朝、中两国文化交流的发展。在酒会之后举行了舞会。
