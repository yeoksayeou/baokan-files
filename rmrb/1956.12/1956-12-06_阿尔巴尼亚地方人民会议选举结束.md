Title: Albanian Local People's Assembly Elections Concluded
Author:
Date: 1956-12-06
Page: 5

    Xinhua News Agency, December 5th: According to the Albanian Telegraph Agency, the elections for local People's Assemblies at all levels were held in Albania on December 2nd, and the preliminary results of the elections have been announced.
    The report points out that more than 99% of voters participated in the election on December 2nd, and more than 99% of the voters voted for the candidates of the Democratic Front. Thus, approximately 21,000 candidates nominated by the Democratic Front were elected.
    This election once again demonstrated the people's resolute support for the Albanian Labor Party and the Democratic Front.

### 阿尔巴尼亚地方人民会议选举结束

1956-12-06
第5版()
专栏：

    阿尔巴尼亚地方人民会议选举结束
    据新华社5日讯　据阿尔巴尼亚通讯社报道：12月2日，阿尔巴尼亚举行了各级地方人民会议的选举，选举的初步结果已经公布。
    消息指出，12月2日有99％以上的选民参加了选举，其中有99％以上的选民投票选举民主阵线的候选人。这样就大约有两万一千个由民主阵线提名的候选人当选。
    这次选举再一次显示了人民对于阿尔巴尼亚劳动党和民主阵线的坚决支持。
