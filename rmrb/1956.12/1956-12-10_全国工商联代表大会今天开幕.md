Title: National Federation of Industry and Commerce Congress Opens Today
Author:
Date: 1956-12-10
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, December 9th: The Second Congress of Members of the All-China Federation of Industry and Commerce is scheduled to open in Beijing on the 10th. This meeting will summarize the work of the Federation of Industry and Commerce since the First Congress of Members was held in 1953, and discuss how the industrial and commercial sectors should further play an active role in serving socialist construction in the current situation where the socialist transformation of capitalist industry and commerce has been basically completed. The meeting will exchange experiences of private personnel participating in socialist competitions and working together with public representatives in enterprises, and discuss personnel arrangements and welfare for the industrial and commercial sectors. The meeting will also revise the constitution of the Federation of Industry and Commerce based on the new situation and elect a new leadership body.

A total of 1,400 delegates and attendees will attend this conference, coming from large, medium, and small cities and towns in various provinces, municipalities, and autonomous regions across the country. Representatives from the Tibet region, Hong Kong and Macao industrial and commercial sectors, and overseas Chinese industrialists and businessmen will also attend the conference. Most of the delegates are private personnel working in joint state-private enterprises, as well as representatives from state-owned enterprises, cooperatives, small vendors, representatives of ethnic minority industrialists and businessmen, and specially invited representatives who have contributed to the industrial and commercial sectors.

### 全国工商联代表大会今天开幕

1956-12-10
第1版()
专栏：

　　全国工商联代表大会今天开幕
    新华社9日讯　中华全国工商业联合会第二届会员代表大会定于10日在北京开幕。这次会议将总结自1953年第一届会员代表大会召开以来工商联的工作，讨论在目前资本主义工商业的社会主义改造已经基本完成的形势下，工商界应该进一步发挥积极作用为社会主义建设服务的问题。会议将交流私方人员参加社会主义竞赛和在企业中公私共事的经验，讨论工商界人事安排和生活福利等问题。会议还要根据新的情况修改工商业联合会的章程，选举新的领导机构。
    出席这个大会的代表和列席人员共一千四百人，他们来自全国各省、市、自治区的大、中、小城市和乡镇；西藏地区、港澳工商界和海外华侨也有代表出席了大会。代表中大部分是在公私合营企业中担任工作的私方人员，也有国营企业、合作社的代表、小商贩的代表、少数民族工商业者的代表和对工商界有贡献的特邀代表。
