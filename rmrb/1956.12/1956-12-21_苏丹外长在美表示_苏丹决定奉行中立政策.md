Title: Sudanese Foreign Minister States in the US that Sudan Has Decided to Pursue a Neutral Policy
Author:
Date: 1956-12-21
Page: 5
Xinhua News Agency, New York, 20th. Sudanese Foreign Minister Mohamed Ahmed Mahgoub said at a luncheon on the 19th that the Sudanese government "has decided to pursue a policy of strict neutrality between East and West."
	The Foreign Minister said that Sudan's main alliance is the Arab League, "Our policy within the League is to unite the various forces at play and to ensure a common policy through consultation and deliberation."
	He also said, "We believe that our responsibility is to link the Arab world and Africa together" because they both want to achieve "a fuller and more pleasant friendship."

### 苏丹外长在美表示  苏丹决定奉行中立政策

1956-12-21
第5版()
专栏：

    苏丹外长在美表示
    苏丹决定奉行中立政策
    新华社20日讯　纽约消息：苏丹外交部长穆罕默德·艾哈迈德·马哈古卜19日在一次午餐会上说，苏丹政府“决定奉行东方和西方之间的严格中立的政策”。
    这位外交部长说，苏丹的主要同盟关系是阿拉伯联盟，“我们在联盟内的政策是使各种起作用的力量联合起来，并且通过协商和深思熟虑来保证一种共同的政策”。
    他还说，“我们相信，我们的责任是把阿拉伯世界和非洲联结起来”，因为它们都想达到“一种更圆满的和更愉快的友谊”。
