Title: President Ba U Invites Premier Zhou for Lunch
Author:
Date: 1956-12-12
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Rangoon, December 11th: Burmese President Ba U hosted a luncheon on December 10th for Premier Zhou Enlai, Vice Premier He Long, and Chinese Ambassador to Burma Yao Zhongming and his wife.

　　Following the luncheon, Premier Zhou Enlai and Vice Premier He Long paid a visit to Burmese Prime Minister U Ba Swe.

　　In the evening, Premier Zhou Enlai and Vice Premier He Long had dinner with U Nu, Chairman of the Anti-Fascist People's Freedom League, at U Nu's residence.

### 巴宇总统邀周总理共进午餐

1956-12-12
第1版()
专栏：

    巴宇总统邀周总理共进午餐
    新华社仰光11日电　缅甸总统巴宇12月10日同周恩来总理、贺龙副总理和中国驻缅甸大使姚仲明和夫人共进午餐。
    随后，周恩来总理和贺龙副总理拜会了缅甸总理吴巴瑞。
    晚上，周恩来总理和贺龙副总理在缅甸反法西斯人民自由同盟主席吴努寓所同吴努共进晚餐。
