Title: People in Various Parts of Iraq Hold Large-Scale Demonstrations Demanding the Resignation of the Said Government and Withdrawal from the Baghdad Pact
Author:
Date: 1956-12-01
Page: 6

　　Xinhua News Agency, 30th - According to a TASS dispatch from Damascus on the 29th: Damascus Radio broadcast that the massive demonstration by all residents of Najaf, Iraq, had continued for four days by November 29th. The army and police were unable to suppress the mass demonstrations.
　　Demonstrators demanded the resignation of the current government led by Nuri al-Said, Iraq's immediate withdrawal from the Baghdad Pact, and effective assistance to Egypt in resisting the aggression of Britain, France, and Israel. Bloody clashes occurred between the army and the demonstrators, resulting in approximately one hundred deaths and hundreds of injuries on both sides.
　　To support the residents of Najaf, large-scale marches and demonstrations were held by residents of Baghdad, Amara, Al-Hayy, and Diwaniyah on November 28th, continuing late into the night.
　　According to newspaper reports, many prominent Iraqi politicians wrote to King Faisal II, demanding the immediate resignation of the Nuri al-Said government and Iraq's withdrawal from the Baghdad Pact.
　　Iraqi politicians also demanded guarantees of domestic democratic freedoms, the release of all political prisoners, the establishment of a genuine national government, and the implementation of a policy of Arab national unity against imperialism.

### 要求赛义德政府下台　要求退出巴格达条约　伊拉克各地人民举行大规模示威

1956-12-01
第6版()
专栏：

　　要求赛义德政府下台　要求退出巴格达条约
　　伊拉克各地人民举行大规模示威
    新华社30日讯　据塔斯社大马士革29日讯：据大马士革电台广播，伊拉克那加夫城全体居民的声势浩大的示威到11月29日已经持续了四天。军队和警察镇压不了群众的示威游行。
    示威者要求努里·赛义德领导的现政府下台，要求伊拉克立即退出巴格达条约，要求切实地援助埃及反抗英法以三国的侵略。军队同游行队伍发生了流血冲突，双方共有约一百人死亡，数百人受伤。
    为了声援那加夫市的居民，11月28日巴格达、阿马拉和爱德、迪瓦尼居民举行了大规模的游行示威，一直继续到深夜。
    据报纸报道，伊拉克许多著名的政治家写信给国王费萨尔二世，要求努里·赛义德政府立刻辞职，并且要求伊拉克退出巴格达条约。
    伊拉克的政治家还要求保证国内的民主自由，释放一切政治犯，建立真正的民族政府，并且执行阿拉伯国家团结一致反对帝国主义的政策。
