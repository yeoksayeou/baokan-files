Title: Premier Zhou and Vice Premier He Lay Wreaths at Gandhi's Cremation Site
Author:
Date: 1956-12-01
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, New Delhi, 30th - Premier Zhou Enlai and Vice Premier He Long laid wreaths at Raj Ghat, Gandhi's cremation site, this morning.
    Accompanying them were Mr. Baig, Chief of Protocol of the Indian Ministry of External Affairs, and the wife of Mr. R.K. Nehru, Indian Ambassador to China.
    Also accompanying them were Pan Zili, Chinese Ambassador to India, and Qiao Guanhua, Assistant Minister of Foreign Affairs of China.

### 周总理和贺副总理到甘地火葬场献花圈

1956-12-01
第1版()
专栏：

　　周总理和贺副总理到甘地火葬场献花圈
    新华社新德里30日电　周恩来总理同贺龙副总理今天上午到拉志加特甘地火葬场献了花圈。
    陪同他们去的有印度外交部礼宾司司长巴伊格、印度驻中国大使拉·库·尼赫鲁的夫人。
    陪同他们去的还有中国驻印度大使潘自力、中国外交部部长助理乔冠华。
