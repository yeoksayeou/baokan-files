Title: Soviet and Romanian Leaders Speak at Kremlin Reception: Developing Brotherly Cooperation Between Socialist Countries
Author:
Date: 1956-12-05
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, December 4th: TASS reports that Soviet Council of Ministers Chairman Bulganin held a reception in the Grand Kremlin Palace on December 3rd to entertain the Government Delegation of the Romanian People's Republic.

The Romanian government representatives, headed by Council of Ministers Chairman Stoica, attended the reception.

The Soviet side attending the reception included Comrades Kaganovich, Mikoyan, Molotov, Pervukhin, Saburov, Khrushchev, Zhukov, Brezhnev, Shepilov, Furtseva, Shvernik, Belyaev, Pospelov, as well as Soviet ministers, marshals, generals of the Soviet army, other responsible personnel of the Soviet party and government, and representatives of social organizations.

Romanian Ambassador to the Soviet Union, Dalea, and diplomatic envoys from various countries to the Soviet Union also attended the reception.

Bulganin said in his speech at the reception that the talks between the Soviet and Romanian government delegations, conducted in an atmosphere of sincere friendship and complete understanding, had been successfully completed. Today, we signed a very important document that stipulates the path for further developing economic relations and cooperation between the two socialist brother countries, the Soviet Union and Romania.

Bulganin said that the declaration we signed today proves that the Soviet Union and Romania, these two socialist countries, are building their relations on the basis of complete equality, mutual benefit, and sincere brotherly and selfless assistance. The smooth development of cooperation between socialist countries strengthens the strength of the socialist camp and the foundation of international peace and security.

Stoica replied, saying that our negotiations were conducted in an atmosphere of brotherly friendship, deep understanding of the interests of our people, mutual assistance, and cooperation, which are unique to relations between socialist countries. The results of the negotiations show the unity of our two countries in terms of views and actions, united in the common struggle for progress, peace, and socialism.

Stoica emphasized that in our joint statement, in which we have completely identical views on the assessment of the international situation, the policies of our two governments are reiterated. This policy is to continuously ease international relations, peacefully resolve all disputes through negotiations, further strengthen the unity of socialist countries, oppose the attempts of imperialist blocs to interfere in the internal affairs of other countries and to attack the freedom and independence of all peoples, and to carry out disarmament and ensure security in accordance with the Soviet proposal of November 17.

During our talks, we noted with satisfaction the good results of economic cooperation between the Soviet Union and Romania based on complete equality and mutual benefit. The results of the talks are of particular significance for the further development of this fruitful cooperation. The Romanian people will welcome the results of the two countries' talks with great joy and satisfaction.

The speeches of the leaders of the governments of these two friendly countries won warm applause from those present.

### 苏罗两国领导人在克里姆林宫招待会上讲话　发展社会主义国家间的兄弟合作

1956-12-05
第5版()
专栏：

    苏罗两国领导人在克里姆林宫招待会上讲话
　　发展社会主义国家间的兄弟合作
    新华社4日讯　据塔斯社莫斯科讯：苏联部长会议主席布尔加宁12月3日在大克里姆林宫举行招待会，招待罗马尼亚人民共和国政府代表团。
    以部长会议主席斯托伊卡为首的罗马尼亚政府代表们出席了招待会。
    苏联方面出席招待会的有卡冈诺维奇、米高扬、莫洛托夫、别尔乌辛、萨布罗夫、赫鲁晓夫、朱可夫、勃列日涅夫、谢皮洛夫、福尔采娃、什维尔尼克、别利亚耶夫、波斯伯洛夫等同志，以及苏联的部长、元帅，苏联军队的将领，苏联党和政府的其他负责人员和社会团体代表。
    罗马尼亚驻苏联大使达列亚和各国驻苏联的外交使节也出席了招待会。
    布尔加宁在招待会上致词说，苏罗两国政府代表团在真诚的友谊和完全谅解的情况下进行的会谈已经胜利地完成了，今天我们签订了一个非常重要的文件，它规定了苏联和罗马尼亚两个社会主义兄弟国家之间的进一步发展经济关系和合作的道路。
    布尔加宁说，今天我们所签订的声明证明：苏联和罗马尼亚这两个社会主义国家是在完全平等、互利以及真诚的兄弟和无私的援助的基础之上建立自己的关系的。社会主义各国之间的合作顺利的发展加强了社会主义阵营的实力以及国际和平和安全的基础。
    斯托伊卡致答词说，我们的谈判是在兄弟友好、对我们人民利益的深刻理解、互助合作这些社会主义国家间的关系所特有的气氛中进行的。谈判的结果表明了团结在争取进步、和平和社会主义的共同斗争中的我们两国在观点上和行动上是一致的。
    斯托伊卡强调说，在我们对国际局势的估计观点完全一致的联合声明中，重申了我们两国政府的政策，这个政策就是：不断缓和国际关系，用谈判方式和平解决一切争执问题，进一步加强社会主义国家的团结，反对帝国主义集团干涉其他国家内政、进攻各国人民的自由和独立的企图，并且根据苏联11月17日的建议进行裁军和保障安全。
    在我们会谈的过程中我们满意地指出了苏联和罗马尼亚之间基于完全平等和互利的经济合作的良好成果。会谈的结果对于进一步发展这种富有成果的合作具有特别的意义。罗马尼亚人民将以十分愉快和满意的心情欢迎两国会谈的成果。
    这两个友好国家政府领导人的讲话博得了到会人的热烈鼓掌。
