Title: Shanghai Municipal Committee Calls on Cadres to Celebrate the New Year Frugally
Author:
Date: 1956-12-28
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Shanghai, December 27th. After inspecting the supply of non-staple foods for the New Year and Spring Festival, the Shanghai Municipal Committee of the Communist Party of China called on all cadres in the city to promote a spirit of hard work and plain living, and to do the following two things during the New Year and Spring Festival: 1. No banquets should be held in government offices; 2. Cadres should be as frugal as possible during the New Year and festivals, and buy less New Year goods, in order to meet the needs of the citizens as much as possible.

### 上海市委号召干部  简朴过新年

1956-12-28
第4版()
专栏：

    上海市委号召干部
    简朴过新年
    新华社上海27日电　中共上海市委员会检查了新年、春节副食品供应工作以后，号召全市干部提倡艰苦朴素作风，在过新年和过春节时，能做到以下两点：一、机关中一律不聚餐；二、干部在过年过节时，尽量从简，少买年货，以尽量满足市民的需要。
