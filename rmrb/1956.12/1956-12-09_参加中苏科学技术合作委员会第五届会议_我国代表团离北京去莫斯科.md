Title: Our Delegation Leaves Beijing for Moscow to Attend the Fifth Meeting of the Sino-Soviet Science and Technology Cooperation Committee
Author:
Date: 1956-12-09
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, December 8th. The Fifth Meeting of the Sino-Soviet Science and Technology Cooperation Committee, convened in accordance with the Sino-Soviet Science and Technology Cooperation Agreement, is about to be held in Moscow. The Chinese delegation of nine members participating in this meeting, led by Li Qiang, Acting Chairman of the Chinese Group of the Sino-Soviet Science and Technology Cooperation Committee and Vice Minister of Foreign Trade, left for Moscow by plane today.

### 参加中苏科学技术合作委员会第五届会议　我国代表团离北京去莫斯科

1956-12-09
第1版()
专栏：

　  参加中苏科学技术合作委员会第五届会议
　　我国代表团离北京去莫斯科
    新华社8日讯　根据中苏科学技术合作协定召开的中苏科学技术合作委员会第五届会议即将在莫斯科举行。参加这次会议的我国代表团一行九人，在中苏科学技术合作委员会中国组代理主席、对外贸易部副部长李强率领下今天乘飞机前往莫斯科。
