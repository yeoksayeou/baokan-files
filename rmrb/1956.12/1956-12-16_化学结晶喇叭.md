Title: Chemical Crystal Loudspeaker
Author:
Date: 1956-12-16
Page: 2

Xinhua News Agency dispatch: The Shanghai Joint State-Private Yonggu Electrical Works, in cooperation with private individuals Shen Songlong and Li Huaqi of the Heping Electrical Works, have successfully trial-produced a chemical crystal loudspeaker that can produce sound without using magnets or coils.

This loudspeaker consumes only one-tenth of the electricity of a typical magnet loudspeaker. An ordinary five-tube radio can be connected to twenty of these loudspeakers, allowing simultaneous broadcasting in twenty different locations without the need for expensive radio amplifiers. This is convenient for use in factories, schools, and agricultural production cooperatives.

### 化学结晶喇叭

1956-12-16
第2版()
专栏：

    化学结晶喇叭
    据新华社讯　上海市公私合营永固电工社同和平电工社的私方沈淞龙、李华圻两人合作试制出了一种不用磁钢和线圈而同样能放出声音来的化学结晶喇叭。
    这种喇叭耗电量只有一般磁钢喇叭的十分之一，一台普通的五灯收音机可以接出二十只喇叭，在二十处地方同时播音而不需要贵重的无线电扩大机，便于在工厂、学校和农业生产合作社等地方使用。
