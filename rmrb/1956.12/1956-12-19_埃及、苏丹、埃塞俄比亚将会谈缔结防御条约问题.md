Title: Egypt, Sudan, and Ethiopia to Discuss Concluding a Defense Treaty
Author:
Date: 1956-12-19
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, December 18th: Cairo dispatch: The weekly "Rose al-Yūsuf" reported on the 17th that Sudanese Prime Minister Abdullah Khalil said that Egypt, Sudan, and Ethiopia will hold talks on concluding a defense treaty.

### 埃及、苏丹、埃塞俄比亚将会谈缔结防御条约问题

1956-12-19
第6版()
专栏：

    埃及、苏丹、埃塞俄比亚
    将会谈缔结防御条约问题
    据新华社18日讯　开罗消息：“鲁兹·优素福”周刊17日报道，苏丹总理阿卜杜拉·哈利勒说，埃及、苏丹和埃塞俄比亚三国将举行缔结防御条约的会谈。
