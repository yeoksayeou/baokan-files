Title: Afghan King and Prime Minister Send Telegram to Nasser Congratulating Egyptian People on Forcing Aggressors to Withdraw Troops
Author:
Date: 1956-12-27
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, Kabul, December 25th. The Afghan Eastern News Agency reported today that Afghan King Mohammed Zahir Shah and Prime Minister Daud Khan sent a telegram to Egyptian President Nasser to congratulate him on the occasion of the aggressive forces being forced to withdraw from Egyptian territory.

In the telegram, the King and Prime Minister expressed their joy that violence had succumbed to the just stance of the heroic Egyptian people, and wished the Egyptian brothers prosperity and success.

The telegram pointed out that due to the admirable heroic actions and spirit of sacrifice of the Egyptian people, "the imperialist and illegal attempts of the aggressive forces have failed and they have been forced to withdraw from Egyptian territory."

### 阿富汗国王和首相致电纳赛尔  祝贺埃及人民迫使侵略者撤军

1956-12-27
第5版()
专栏：

    阿富汗国王和首相致电纳赛尔
    祝贺埃及人民迫使侵略者撤军
    新华社喀布尔25日电　据阿富汗东方通讯社今天报道，阿富汗国王穆罕默德·查希尔·沙阿和首相达乌德汗在侵略军队被迫从埃及领土撤退的时候打电报给埃及总统纳赛尔表示祝贺。
    国王和首相在电报中对于暴力在英勇的埃及人民的正当立场面前屈服表示庆幸。并祝埃及兄弟繁荣和成功。
    贺电指出，由于埃及人民值得钦佩的英雄行为和牺牲精神，使得“侵略军队的帝国主义的非法企图失败并且被迫撤离埃及领土。”
