Title: British MP Stansgate Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1956-12-16
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, December 15th: Viscount Stansgate, a member of the British House of Lords, and his wife arrived in Beijing by plane this morning. Stansgate is visiting China at the invitation of the Chinese People's Institute of Foreign Affairs for a friendly visit, after attending the 45th Conference of the Inter-Parliamentary Union held in Bangkok in November this year.
    Greeting them at the airport today were Qian Duansheng, Vice President of the Chinese People's Institute of Foreign Affairs, Wu Maosun, Secretary-General, and Zhang Su, Deputy Secretary-General of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress. Mr. A.T. Lamb, Counsellor of the British Chargé d'Affaires Office in China, was also at the airport to welcome them.

### 英议员斯坦斯盖特到京

1956-12-16
第1版()
专栏：

    英议员斯坦斯盖特到京
    新华社15日讯　英国上议院议员斯坦斯盖特子爵和他的夫人，今天上午乘飞机到达北京。斯坦斯盖特是在参加今年11月间在曼谷举行的各国议会联盟第四十五届会议之后，应中国人民外交学会的邀请，来中国作友好访问的。
    今天到机场欢迎的，有中国人民外交学会副会长钱端升、秘书长吴茂荪，全国人民代表大会常务委员会副秘书长张苏等。英国驻华代办处参赞艾惕思也到机场欢迎。
