Author: Yan Zhennan, Li Jichun
Date: 1956-12-21

    Accelerating the Construction of the Wuhan Yangtze River Bridge
    Right picture: The bridge pier foundation work has been basically completed.
  　Xinhua News Agency reporter Yan Zhennan photo
    Bottom picture: While the steel beams are being erected, the construction of the bridge's road surface is also proceeding rapidly; the steel structure has been completed for more than two spans, and the bridge deck for the road surface has also been laid to the No. 2 pier. A huge crane is hoisting the bridge deck. Photo by Li Jichun (Manuscript from Hubei Daily)
