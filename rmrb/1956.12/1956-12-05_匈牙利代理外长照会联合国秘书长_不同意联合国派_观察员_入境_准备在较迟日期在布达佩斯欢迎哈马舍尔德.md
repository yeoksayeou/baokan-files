Title: Hungarian Acting Foreign Minister Sends Note to UN Secretary-General: Disagrees with UN Sending "Observers" to Hungary; Prepares to Welcome Hammarskjöld in Budapest at a Later Date
Author:
Date: 1956-12-05
Page: 5

　　Xinhua News Agency, December 4th, New York dispatch: Hungarian Acting Foreign Minister Szebes on December 3rd sent a note to UN Secretary-General Hammarskjöld, reiterating Hungary's disagreement with the UN sending "observers" to Hungary.

　　Szebes stated in the note: "The Hungarian government maintains its earlier position that the events that have occurred in Hungary since October 23, 1956, are entirely internal affairs of Hungary and do not fall within the jurisdiction of any international organization, including the United Nations Organization."

　　"Therefore, the Hungarian government, as before, believes that allowing UN observers to enter Hungarian territory would undermine Hungarian sovereignty and violate the principles of the UN Charter."

　　The note also stated: "In the hope that Your Excellency (Hammarskjöld) may personally obtain satisfactory information about the situation in Hungary, the Hungarian government remains willing, as it has previously indicated, to send representatives to Rome or New York without delay to discuss matters with Your Excellency."

　　"In order to make it possible for Your Excellency to have direct consultations with the Hungarian government, the Hungarian People's Republic Worker-Peasant Revolutionary Government is prepared to welcome Your Excellency in Budapest at a later date that is mutually convenient."

### 匈牙利代理外长照会联合国秘书长　不同意联合国派“观察员”入境  准备在较迟日期在布达佩斯欢迎哈马舍尔德

1956-12-05
第5版()
专栏：

    匈牙利代理外长照会联合国秘书长
　　不同意联合国派“观察员”入境
    准备在较迟日期在布达佩斯欢迎哈马舍尔德
    新华社4日讯　纽约消息：匈牙利代理外交部长谢贝什3日照会联合国秘书长哈马舍尔德，再次表明匈牙利不同意联合国派遣“观察员”进入匈牙利。
    谢贝什在照会中说：“匈牙利政府保持它早先的态度，就是自从1956年10月23日以来在匈牙利所发生的事件完全是匈牙利的内部事务，并不属于包括联合国组织在内的任何国际组织的权限的范围之内。
    “因此，匈牙利政府和以前一样认为，允许联合国观察员进入匈牙利的领土将破坏匈牙利的主权并且将违反联合国宪章的原则。”
    照会还说：“为了希望使阁下
  （哈马舍尔德）可以亲身获得关于匈牙利局势的满意的情报，匈牙利政府仍然像它以前所表示的一样，愿意派代表毫不延迟地在罗马或纽约与阁下商谈。
      “为了使阁下有可能与匈牙利政府进行直接的商谈，匈牙利人民共和国工农革命政府准备在对双方都适当的较迟的日期在布达佩斯欢迎阁下。”
