Title: The Dalai Lama and the Panchen Erdeni Depart Madras; Madras State Governor Held a Grand Reception for Them
Author:
Date: 1956-12-22
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, New Delhi, 20th - The Dalai Lama and the Panchen Erdeni have concluded their three-day visit to Madras and departed for Bezwada on the evening of December 19th.

On the evening of December 19th, the Governor of Madras State, John, held a grand reception for the Dalai Lama and the Panchen Erdeni in the gardens of the Government House. Approximately three hundred prominent figures from all walks of life attended the reception. The Governor, on behalf of the State Government, presented two large bronze oil lamps to the Dalai Lama and the Panchen Erdeni.

On the morning of the 19th, accompanied by Chief Administrator Sadagopan, the guests visited a vehicle assembly plant, where crowds of workers, housewives, and children came to wave and cheer for the guests. The hosts also presented two vehicle models to the Dalai Lama and the Panchen Erdeni.

Prior to this, the Dalai Lama and the Panchen Erdeni visited a large textile factory called Buckingham and Carnatic Mills. They also visited the Kalakshetra art school.

To welcome the Dalai Lama and the Panchen Erdeni, the Madras Buddhist Association held a reception on the evening of December 18th, attended by approximately one thousand people.

### 达赖喇嘛和班禅额尔德尼离开马德拉斯  马德拉斯邦长曾为他们举行了盛大的招待会

1956-12-22
第1版()
专栏：

    达赖喇嘛和班禅额尔德尼离开马德拉斯
    马德拉斯邦长曾为他们举行了盛大的招待会
    据新华社新德里20日电　达赖喇嘛和班禅额尔德尼已经结束了在马德拉斯的三天访问，12月19日晚上，他们动身前往贝兹瓦达。
    马德拉斯邦邦长约翰12月19日晚上在政府大厦的花园里为达赖喇嘛和班禅额尔德尼举行了一个盛大的招待会，大约有三百个各界著名人士出席了招待会。邦长代表邦政府把两盏巨大的青铜油灯赠送给达赖喇嘛和班禅额尔德尼。
    19日上午，客人们在首席行政长官萨达戈潘的陪同下参观了车辆装配工厂，成群结队的工人、家庭主妇和孩子走来向客人们挥手和欢呼。主人还把两辆车辆模型送给达赖喇嘛和班禅额尔德尼。
    在这以前，达赖喇嘛和班禅额尔德尼参观了一个叫做白金汉和卡纳提克工厂的大纺织工厂。他们还参观了卡腊克希特腊艺术学院。
    马德拉斯的佛教协会为了欢迎达赖喇嘛和班禅额尔德尼，曾经在12月18日傍晚举行了大约有一千人参加的招待会。
