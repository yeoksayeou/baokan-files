Title: Chen Jingkai Sets New World Record Again with 135.5 kg Clean and Jerk
Author:
Date: 1956-12-01
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Shanghai, 30th – Chinese Olympic weightlifter Chen Jingkai set a new world record of 135.5 kg in the clean and jerk in the Bantamweight class on the evening of the 29th in Shanghai.

Chen Jingkai set the world record for this event on June 7th in Shanghai at 133 kg. This record was broken by South Korean athlete Yuk Young Ho on November 23rd at the 16th Olympic Games with a result of 135 kg. However, Yuk Young Ho's record did not last even a week before being broken again by Chen Jingkai. Chen Jingkai’s clean and jerk performance during an exhibition match held not long ago in Wuhan reached 137.5 kg.

Chen Jingkai's total score on the evening of the 29th was 320 kg, equal to the score of the fourth-place finisher in this class at the current Olympic Games. His press was 90 kg, and his snatch was 95 kg.

Chen Jingkai weighed 56 kg on the evening of the 29th, meeting the weight standard for bantamweight weightlifters.

### 双手挺举135.5公斤　陈镜开再创世界新纪录

1956-12-01
第4版()
专栏：

    双手挺举135.5公斤
　　陈镜开再创世界新纪录
    新华社上海30日电　中国奥林匹克举重选手陈镜开29日晚上在上海创造了最轻量级双手挺举一百三十五点五公斤的世界新成绩。
    陈镜开今年6月7日在上海创造的这项比赛的世界纪录是一百三十三公斤，这个纪录在11月23日被南朝鲜运动员玉英何在第十六届奥林匹克运动会上以一百三十五公斤的成绩所打破。但是，玉英何的纪录还没有保持到一个星期，就又被陈镜开打破了。陈镜开不久以前在武汉举行表演赛时，双手挺举的成绩曾经达到一百三十七点五公斤。
    陈镜开29日晚上的总成绩是三百二十公斤，同这一届奥林匹克运动会这级比赛第四名的成绩相等。他推举的成绩是九十公斤，抓举是九十五公斤。
    陈镜开29日晚上的体重是五十六公斤，合乎最轻量级举重运动员的体重标准。
