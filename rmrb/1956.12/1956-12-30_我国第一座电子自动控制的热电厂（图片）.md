Title: China's First Electronically Controlled Thermal Power Plant (Picture)
Author: Xu Zhicheng
Date: 1956-12-30
Page: 2

China's First Electronically Controlled Thermal Power Plant

The Jilin Thermal Power Plant has completed its annual construction plan. The second turbine was officially put into production in mid-December, and began supplying steam and electricity needed by industry in the Jilin area. The picture above shows the exterior of the Jilin Thermal Power Plant. (Xinhua News Agency reporter Xu Zhicheng)
