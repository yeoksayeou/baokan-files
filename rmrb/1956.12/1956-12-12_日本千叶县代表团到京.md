Title: Japanese Chiba Prefecture Delegation Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1956-12-12
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, 11th. The Japanese Chiba Prefecture Delegation, headed by Fujiwara Toyojiro, consisting of four members, arrived in Beijing on the 10th at the invitation of the Chinese People's Committee for World Peace.

### 日本千叶县代表团到京

1956-12-12
第1版()
专栏：

    日本千叶县代表团到京
    新华社11日讯　应中国人民保卫世界和平委员会邀请来中国访问的以藤原丰次郎为首的日本千叶县代表团一行四人，已经在10日到达北京。
