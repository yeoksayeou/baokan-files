Title: Guo Moruo Hosts Banquet for Guests Attending Commemoration of World Cultural Figures
Author: 
Date: 1956-12-12
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, 11th -- Foreign guests invited to China to participate in the commemoration activities for world cultural figures, American scientist and political theorist Benjamin Franklin, French scientist Pierre Curie, and his wife, Polish scientist Marie Curie, have arrived in Beijing successively.

Guo Moruo, President of the Chinese Academy of Sciences, Chairman of the Chinese 1956 Committee for Commemorating World Cultural Figures, and Chairman of the Chinese People's Committee for World Peace, hosted a banquet tonight to welcome them.

The foreign guests who have arrived in Beijing include: Pierre Curie and Marie Curie's granddaughter, Hélène Langevin-Joliot, and her husband, French nuclear physicist Michel Langevin; Polish physicist and professor at the University of Warsaw, Złotowski, and Marie Curie University professor Teskai; and American writer Abe Capek.

Among those present tonight were Honorary Chairman of the All-China Federation of Natural Science Societies Wu Yuzhang, Vice President of the Chinese People's Association for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries Zhao Yimin, Vice President of the Chinese Academy of Sciences Wu Youxun, members of the Presidium of the China Federation of Literary and Art Circles Lao She, Ouyang Yuqian, as well as physicist Zhou Peiyuan, aerodynamicist Qian Xuesen, and others.

### 郭沫若宴请参加纪念世界文化名人的来宾

1956-12-12
第1版()
专栏：

    郭沫若宴请参加纪念世界文化名人的来宾
    新华社11日讯　应邀来中国参加世界文化名人美国科学家及政论家本杰明·富兰克林、法国科学家皮埃尔·居里及其夫人波兰科学家玛丽·居里纪念活动的外国来宾已经先后到达北京。
    中国科学院院长、中国1956年纪念世界文化名人委员会主席、中国人民保卫世界和平委员会主席郭沫若今天晚上设宴欢迎他们。
    现已到达北京的外宾有：皮埃尔·居里、玛丽·居里的外孙女海伦娜·郎出万和她的丈夫、法国核子物理学家密歇尔·郎出万；波兰物理学家、华沙大学教授兹沃托夫斯基和玛丽·居里大学教授泰斯开；美国作家阿贝·察佩克。
    今天晚上出席作陪的有中华全国自然科学专门学会联合会名誉主席吴玉章，中国人民对外文化协会副会长赵毅敏，中国科学院副院长吴有训，中国文学艺术界联合会主席团委员老舍、欧阳予倩，以及物理学家周培源，空气动力学家钱学森等多人。
