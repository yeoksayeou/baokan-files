Title: Ambassador Chen Zhifang calls on Syrian Prime Minister al-Assali
Author:
Date: 1956-12-24
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, Damascus, December 23rd: Chen Zhifang, Ambassador of the People's Republic of China to Syria, called on Syrian Prime Minister al-Assali on the 22nd. Ambassador Chen and Prime Minister al-Assali had a cordial and friendly conversation. Ambassador Chen conveyed Premier Zhou Enlai's greetings to him and presented Premier Zhou Enlai's gift to Prime Minister al-Assali.

### 陈志方大使拜会叙利亚总理阿萨利

1956-12-24
第1版()
专栏：

    陈志方大使拜会叙利亚总理阿萨利
    新华社23日讯　大马士革消息：中华人民共和国驻叙利亚大使陈志方在22日拜会了叙利亚总理阿萨利。陈大使和阿萨利总理进行了亲切友好的谈话，并且向他转达了周恩来总理的问候，还转交了周恩来总理赠给阿萨利总理的礼物。
