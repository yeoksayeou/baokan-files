Title: Italian Communist Party Central Committee Elects New Leadership, Togliatti Reelected as General Secretary
Author:
Date: 1956-12-19
Page: 5
Xinhua News Agency, Rome, December 17th: According to TASS, the Central Committee of the Italian Communist Party, at its plenary session held on the 17th, elected the party's leadership and secretariat. Palmiro Togliatti was unanimously reelected as General Secretary of the Party Central Committee, and Luigi Longo was elected as Deputy General Secretary.

The members elected to the party's leadership were: Palmiro Togliatti, Luigi Longo, Mario Alicata, Giorgio Amendola, Arturo Colombi, Giuseppe Vittorio, Giuseppe Dozza, Pietro Ingrao, Girolama Li Causi, Augustino Novella, Gian Carlo Pajetta, Giacomo Pellegrini, Antonio Roasio, Luciano Romagnoli, Emilio Sereni, Velio Spano, Umberto Terracini, and Mauro Scoccimarro, who was elected Chairman of the Central Control Commission.

Elected as secretaries of the secretariat were Togliatti, Longo, Amendola, Ingrao, Pajetta, and Enrico Berlinguer, Paolo Bufalini.

### 意共中央选出新的领导机构  陶里亚蒂重新当选总书记

1956-12-19
第5版()
专栏：

    意共中央选出新的领导机构
    陶里亚蒂重新当选总书记
    新华社18日讯　据塔斯社罗马17日讯：意大利共产党中央委员会在17日举行的全体会议上选出了党的领导机构和书记处。陶里亚蒂在一致同意之下重新当选为党中央委员会总书记，路易吉·隆哥当选为副总书记。
    当选为党的领导机构成员的有：帕尔米罗·陶里亚蒂、路易吉·隆哥、马里奥·阿利卡塔、乔治·阿门多拉、阿尔图罗·科隆比、朱塞佩·维多里奥、朱塞佩·多萨、彼特罗·英格拉奥、吉罗拉马·里·考西、奥古斯丁诺、诺维拉、贾恩·卡洛·巴叶塔、贾科摩·贝勒格林尼、安东尼奥·罗西欧、卢契安诺·罗曼奥利、埃米略·塞伦尼、维利奥·斯巴诺、翁伯托·特拉齐尼和被选为中央监察委员会主席的毛罗·斯科奇马罗。
    当选为书记处书记的有陶里亚蒂、隆哥、阿门多拉、英格拉奥、巴叶塔、和恩里科·波纳齐、保罗·布法利尼。
