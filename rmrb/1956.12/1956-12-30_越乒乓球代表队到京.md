Title: Vietnamese Table Tennis Team Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1956-12-30
Page: 1

    Xinhua News Agency, December 29th - The Vietnamese Democratic Republic table tennis team, a group of nine, arrived in Beijing by train tonight.

    This Vietnamese table tennis team, invited to visit China and conduct friendly matches, consists of five male athletes and two female athletes. The best player among them is Huang Degguang, a machine worker from Hai Phong City. The team leader, Mei Weiyang, is a former Indochina doubles champion.

### 越乒乓球代表队到京

1956-12-30
第1版()
专栏：

    越乒乓球代表队到京
    据新华社29日讯　越南民主共和国乒乓球代表队一行九人今晚乘火车到达北京。
    这个应邀来中国访问和进行友谊比赛的越南乒乓球代表队中，有五名男运动员和两名女运动员，其中打得最好的是海防市的机器工人黄德光。代表队的领队梅维养是前印度支那双打冠军之一的选手。
