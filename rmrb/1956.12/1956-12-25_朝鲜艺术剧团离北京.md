Title: Korean Art Troupe Leaves Beijing
Author:
Date: 1956-12-25
Page: 7
Xinhua News Agency, December 23rd. The entire staff of the National Folk Art Troupe of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea left Beijing by train this afternoon for Shenyang.

The art troupe will hold its last performances in China in Shenyang and Yanji before returning home.

Over the past two months, more than 70,000 Chinese audiences in Beijing, Shanghai, Hangzhou, Guangzhou, and Wuhan have seen the Korean opera "The Tale of Chun香" and "The Tale of Shim Chong" performed by the art troupe.

### 朝鲜艺术剧团离北京

1956-12-25
第7版()
专栏：

    朝鲜艺术剧团离北京
    新华社23日讯　朝鲜民主主义人民共和国国立民族艺术剧团全体人员今天下午坐火车离开北京前往沈阳。
    艺术剧团将在沈阳、延吉举行访问中国的最后几场演出，然后回国。
    两个月以来，在北京、上海、杭州、广州、武汉共有七万多中国观众看到了艺术剧团演出的朝鲜唱剧“春香传”和“沈清传”。
