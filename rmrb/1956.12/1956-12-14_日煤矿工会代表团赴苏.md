Title: Japanese Coal Miners' Union Delegation to USSR
Author:
Date: 1956-12-14
Page: 1

    Xinhua News Agency, 11th. The Japanese Coal Miners' Union delegation, consisting of thirty members, who came to China at the invitation of the Chinese Coal Miners' Union, left Beijing by plane on the morning of the 11th for a visit to the Soviet Union after visiting various parts of China. Jin Zhifu, Chairman of the Chinese Coal Miners' Union, Chen Yu, Deputy Director of the International Liaison Department of the All-China Federation of Trade Unions, and others were at the airport to see them off.

### 日煤矿工会代表团赴苏

1956-12-14
第1版()
专栏：

    日煤矿工会代表团赴苏
    新华社11日讯　应中国煤矿工会邀请来我国访问的日本煤矿工会代表团一行三十人，在我国各地参观访问后，已经在11日晨乘飞机离开北京前往苏联访问。到机场欢送的有中国煤矿工会主席金直夫，中华全国总工会国际联络部副部长陈宇等人。
