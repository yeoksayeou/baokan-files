Title: Compulsory Purchase of Civilian Housing at Reduced Prices Has Been Dealt With
Author:
Date: 1956-12-14
Page: 2

Response to Criticism

    Compulsory Purchase of Civilian Housing at Reduced Prices Has Been Dealt With
    A letter from the Meihekou Locomotive Depot of the Jilin Railway Administration: After the People's Daily published criticism on October 28 regarding our depot's inappropriate compulsory purchase of civilian housing at reduced prices, we have carefully convened relevant personnel for research and have taken action.

### 压价收买民房已作处理

1956-12-14
第2版()
专栏：对批评的反应

    压价收买民房已作处理
    吉林铁路管理局梅河口机务段来信：人民日报10月28日发表了对我段不该压价强收民房的批评后，我们已慎重地召集了有关人员进行了研究，作了处理。
