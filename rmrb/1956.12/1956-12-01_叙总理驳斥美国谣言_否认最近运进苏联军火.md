Title: Syrian Premier Refutes American Rumors, Denies Recent Shipment of Soviet Arms
Author:
Date: 1956-12-01
Page: 6

　　Syrian Premier Refutes American Rumors
　　Denies Recent Shipment of Soviet Arms
Xinhua News Agency, Damascus, 30th -- Syrian Premier Sabri al-Asali denied on November 28th that Syria had recently received a shipment of Soviet arms.

Al-Asali told reporters, "This is completely unfounded."

Al-Asali's denial was in response to recent rumors fabricated by American officials. U.S. State Department spokesman White said that Syria had recently received a large quantity of arms from the Soviet Union.

### 叙总理驳斥美国谣言　否认最近运进苏联军火

1956-12-01
第6版()
专栏：

　　叙总理驳斥美国谣言
　　否认最近运进苏联军火
    新华社30日讯　大马士革消息：叙利亚总理萨布里·阿萨利在11月28日否认叙利亚最近收到了一批苏联军火。
    阿萨利对记者说：“这是完全没有根据的”。
    阿萨利的否认是针对美国官方最近的造谣而发的。美国国务院发言人怀特说，叙利亚最近接到从苏联运去的大量军火。
