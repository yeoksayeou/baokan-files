Title: Budapest Workers' Central Committee Calls for Halt to Activities Inciting Strikes and Riots
Author:
Date: 1956-12-04
Page: 5
Xinhua News Agency, Budapest, December 3rd: "People's Free News" reported on the 2nd that the Budapest Central Workers' Committee issued a communique calling on workers' committees across the country and honest working people to stop activities attempting to incite strikes and riots.

    The communique said: "Recently, there are still people in the capital and many parts of the country distributing leaflets calling for strikes and armed activities. We firmly oppose these activities because they do not serve the interests of the Hungarian people."

### 布达佩斯工人中央委员会　号召制止煽动罢工和暴动的活动

1956-12-04
第5版()
专栏：

    布达佩斯工人中央委员会
　　号召制止煽动罢工和暴动的活动
    新华社布达佩斯3日电　“人民自由报”2日报道：布达佩斯中央工人委员会发表公报，号召全国各工人委员会和正直的劳动人民制止企图煽动罢工和暴动的活动。
    公报说：“最近仍有人在首都和全国很多地方散发号召罢工和武装活动的传单，我们坚决反对这种活动，因为这种活动不是为匈牙利人民的利益服务的。”
