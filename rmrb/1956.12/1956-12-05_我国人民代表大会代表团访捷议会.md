Title: Chinese National People's Congress Delegation Visits Czechoslovak Parliament
Author:
Date: 1956-12-05
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, Prague, December 4th: The Chinese National People's Congress delegation and the Beijing Municipal People's Committee delegation, accompanied by Cao Ying, the Chinese Ambassador to Czechoslovakia, visited the Czechoslovak National Assembly on the morning of December 3rd.

　　Chairman Fierlinger, Vice-Chairpersons Hodinova-Spurna and Polansky of the Czechoslovak National Assembly, chairpersons of various committees of the National Assembly, and some members of parliament warmly received the delegation and held a symposium with the delegation in a cordial atmosphere.

　　The hosts introduced the organization and working methods of the National Assembly and its role in the construction of state power to the Chinese guests, and answered some questions raised by the delegation members.

　　Afterwards, the delegation laid wreaths at the Tomb of the Unknown Soldier and the Gottwald Mausoleum.

　　At noon, Prague Mayor Svoboda hosted a banquet for the delegation.

　　After the luncheon, some members of the delegation visited the Oriental Institute of the Academy of Sciences. The delegation members were very interested in the work of this institute.

### 我国人民代表大会代表团访捷议会

1956-12-05
第1版()
专栏：

　　我国人民代表大会代表团访捷议会
    新华社布拉格4日电　中国全国人民代表大会代表团和北京市人民委员会代表团，12月3日上午在中国驻捷克斯洛伐克大使曹瑛陪同下访问了捷克斯洛伐克国民议会。
    捷克斯洛伐克国民议会主席费林格、副主席霍迪诺娃—斯普尔娜和波兰斯基，国民议会各委员会主席和一些议员热情地接待了代表团，并且同代表团在亲切的气氛中举行了座谈。
    主人们向中国客人介绍了国民议会的组织、工作方法以及在政权建设中所起的作用，并且答复了代表团人员所提出的一些问题。
    随后，代表团到无名烈士墓和哥特瓦尔德陵墓前献了花圈。
    中午，布拉格市市长斯沃波达设宴招待代表团。
    午宴以后，代表团部分人员到科学院东方研究所访问，团员们对这个研究所的工作感到很大兴趣。
