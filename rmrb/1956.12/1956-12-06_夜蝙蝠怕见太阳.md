Title: Night Bats Fear the Sun

Author: 
Date: 1956-12-06
Page: 8

　　Night Bats Fear the Sun
    (Mongolian Proverb) The wicked find it hard to go far, night bats fear the sun. All venomous snakes are dangerous regardless of size, treacherous people cannot be differentiated by distance. If you don't kill the venomous snake, the venomous snake will bite you.
       Edited and translated by Hao Sumin

### 夜蝙蝠怕见太阳

1956-12-06
第8版()
专栏：

　　夜蝙蝠怕见太阳
    （蒙古谚语）坏心人难走四方，夜蝙蝠怕见太阳。凡毒蛇没有粗细之说，是奸人没有远近之分。不杀毒蛇，毒蛇咬人。
       郝苏民辑译
