Title: Soviet and Cambodian Experts Hold Meeting, Draft Measures to Strengthen Economic and Cultural Relations
Author:
Date: 1956-12-18
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, Phnom Penh, December 17th: According to the Cambodian News Agency, Soviet and Cambodian experts have held two meetings and established three working groups to draft practical measures to strengthen economic and cultural relations between Cambodia and the Soviet Union.

The Soviet expert delegation arrived in Phnom Penh on December 4th. The head of the delegation, Degtyar, paid an official visit to the Cambodian Minister of Public Health, Yeam Sokhon, on the 13th and inspected the site of a 500-bed hospital to be built with Soviet assistance.

### 苏柬两国专家举行会议  拟定加强经济文化关系措施

1956-12-18
第5版()
专栏：

    苏柬两国专家举行会议
    拟定加强经济文化关系措施
    新华社金边17日电　据柬埔寨新闻社报道，苏联专家们和柬埔寨专家们已经举行了两次会议，成立了三个工作小组来拟定加强柬埔寨和苏联之间的经济和文化关系的实际措施。
    苏联专家代表团是在12月4日到达金边的。这个代表团的负责人杰格佳尔在13日正式访问了柬埔寨王国公共卫生大臣严叔沈，并且视察了将在苏联援助下建设的一个有五百张床位的医院的地址。
