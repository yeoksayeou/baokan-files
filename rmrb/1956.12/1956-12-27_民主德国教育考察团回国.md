Title: Democratic German Education Delegation Returns Home
Author:
Date: 1956-12-27
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, December 26th: The four-member education delegation from the German Democratic Republic, after conducting an inspection visit of more than a month in various parts of China, left Beijing by plane this morning to return home.

The head of the education delegation, Isold Oschmann, said to the Chinese education community before leaving that she hopes to continue to improve the national education of both countries through the exchange of educational experiences in the future, and at the same time, enhance and deepen the friendship between the educators of the two countries.

### 民主德国教育考察团回国

1956-12-27
第1版()
专栏：

    民主德国教育考察团回国
    据新华社26日讯　德意志民主共和国教育考察团四人，在中国各地进行了一个多月的考察访问以后，今天上午乘飞机离开北京回国。
    教育考察团团长依苏尔德·奥希曼在临别前向中国教育界说，她希望今后能继续通过两国教育经验的交流，使两国的国民教育事业办得更好，同时也可以增强和加深两国教育家们的友谊。
