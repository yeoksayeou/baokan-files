Title: Chinese and Burmese Premiers Leave Mangshi for Burma; Border People of Both Countries Give Warm Send-Off
Author:
Date: 1956-12-18
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Mangshi, December 17th: After attending the Sino-Burmese Border People's Friendship Gathering, Burmese Prime Minister U Ba Swe left Mangshi this morning to return to his country.

	Premier Zhou Enlai accompanied Prime Minister U Ba Swe to Burma to continue his friendly visit.

	Ten thousand people of various ethnic groups from the Sino-Burmese border region gathered in Mangshi Square before dawn, waiting to bid farewell to the two Premiers. At 7:30 a.m., Premier Zhou Enlai accompanied Premier U Ba Swe to the square. After the two Premiers reviewed the honor guard, they shook hands and bid farewell to the well-wishers. At this moment, more than thirty gorgeously dressed young women from various ethnic groups once again presented fragrant flowers to the two Premiers.

	At 8:00 a.m., the two Premiers boarded their cars and left Mangshi.

	The colleagues of the two Premiers also left Mangshi this morning.

	Liu Minghui, Acting Governor of Yunnan Province, and other leaders of Yunnan Province, accompanied the two Premiers by car to Wanting Town.

### 中缅总理离芒市去缅甸  两国边境人民热烈欢送

1956-12-18
第1版()
专栏：

    中缅总理离芒市去缅甸
    两国边境人民热烈欢送
    新华社芒市17日电　缅甸联邦总理吴巴瑞在参加了中缅两国边境人民联欢大会以后，今天早晨离开芒市回国。
    周恩来总理同吴巴瑞总理一起去缅甸，继续进行友好访问。
    中缅两国边境各族人民一万人，在黎明前就聚集在芒市广场上，等候着欢送两国总理。七时半，周恩来总理陪同吴巴瑞总理来到了广场。两国总理检阅了仪仗队以后，和欢送的人群握手道别。这时，三十多个各民族的盛装少女，再一次把芬芳的鲜花献给两国总理。
    八时，两国总理登上汽车，离开了芒市。
    两国总理的同行人员，今晨也同时离开了芒市。
    云南省代省长刘明辉和云南省的其他领导人，陪送两国总理乘车到畹町镇。
