Title: Part of the Delegation of the Beijing Municipal People's Committee Returns to Beijing from Moscow
Author:
Date: 1956-12-14
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, Beijing.

    Part of the Delegation of the Beijing Municipal People's Committee Returns to Beijing from Moscow
    According to Xinhua News Agency, a group of nine members of the delegation of the Beijing Municipal People's Committee, led by Deputy Head Zhang Youyu, returned to Beijing by plane from Moscow on the 8th. Before the delegation's departure, the Moscow City Soviet Executive Committee held a grand banquet to bid them farewell.
    Before leaving, Deputy Head Zhang Youyu said to those who came to see them off, "Without a doubt, this visit has strengthened the friendly ties between Moscow and Beijing. Everywhere we went, we encountered truly brotherly receptions. This reception shows that the friendship between the people of the Soviet Union and China is very deep and unbreakable. After learning about the work of your municipal organs, we have gained many beneficial things."
    This part of the delegation visited various departments of the Moscow City Soviet, learned about their work, and conducted other visits.

### 北京市人民委员会代表团部分人员从莫斯科回京

1956-12-14
第1版()
专栏：

    北京市人民委员会代表团部分人员从莫斯科回京
    据新华社讯　北京市人民委员会代表团部分团员一行九人，由副团长张友渔率领，已于8日乘飞机从莫斯科返抵北京。代表团临行前，莫斯科市苏维埃执行委员会举行盛大宴会欢送。
    临行前张友渔副团长对欢送他们的人说，毫无疑问，我们这次访问加强了莫斯科和北京的友好联系。我们到处都遇到真正兄弟般的接待。这种接待表明，苏中两国人民的友谊是非常深厚和牢不可破的。我们了解了你们市政机关的工作以后，得到了许多有益的东西。
    代表团的这一部分人员访问了莫斯科市苏维埃各部门，了解它们的工作，并进行了其他访问。
