Title: Menon Leaves Egypt for Lebanon
Author:
Date: 1956-12-31
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, Cairo, 30th - Indian Minister Without Portfolio Menon left Cairo on the 29th for Beirut, the capital of Lebanon, after a three-day visit to the Egyptian capital.

During his stay in Cairo, Menon held talks with Egyptian President Nasser.

### 梅农离埃及去黎巴嫩

1956-12-31
第6版()
专栏：

    梅农离埃及去黎巴嫩
    新华社30日讯　开罗消息：印度不管部长梅农在埃及首都访问了三天以后，在29日离开开罗前往黎巴嫩首都贝鲁特。
    梅农在开罗期间同埃及总统纳赛尔进行了会谈。
