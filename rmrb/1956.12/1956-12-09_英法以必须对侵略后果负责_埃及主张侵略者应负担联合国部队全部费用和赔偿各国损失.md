Title: Britain, France and Israel Must Be Held Responsible for the Consequences of Aggression; Egypt Argues that Aggressors Should Bear All Expenses of UN Forces and Compensate Losses of All Countries
Author:
Date: 1956-12-09
Page: 1

　　Britain, France and Israel Must Be Held Responsible for the Consequences of Aggression
　　Egypt Argues that Aggressors Should Bear All Expenses of UN Forces and Compensate Losses of All Countries

　　Xinhua News Agency, 7th dispatch from New York: The Egyptian representative said at the United Nations General Assembly Budget Committee on the 6th: Britain, France and Israel should bear all the expenses of the United Nations Emergency Force, and should also compensate the losses of those countries that have suffered economic damage due to the blockage of the Suez Canal.

　　He condemned the three countries for carrying out aggression, and pointed out that Israel's attack on the Sinai Peninsula was "the action of bandits."

　　Xinhua News Agency, 8th dispatch from Tass, Beirut, 7th: Jordanian newspapers demanded that Britain, France and Israel compensate Egypt for the losses it suffered as a result of their aggression.

　　"Struggle Daily" wrote: "The solidarity of countries around the world that condemned the armed intervention of Britain, France, and Israel proves that the aggressors should bear this responsibility and pay compensation fees.

### 英法以必须对侵略后果负责　埃及主张侵略者应负担联合国部队全部费用和赔偿各国损失

1956-12-09
第1版()
专栏：

　　英法以必须对侵略后果负责
　　埃及主张侵略者应负担联合国部队全部费用和赔偿各国损失
    新华社7日讯　纽约消息：埃及代表6日在联合国大会预算委员会上说：英国、法国和以色列应当负担联合国紧急部队的全部费用，而且应当赔偿那些由于苏伊士运河堵塞而在经济上受到损害的国家的损失。
    他谴责这三国进行了侵略，并且指出，以色列进攻西奈半岛是
  “匪徒的行动”。
    新华社8日讯　塔斯社贝鲁特7日讯：约旦报纸要求英国、法国和以色列赔偿埃及由于遭受它们的侵略而蒙受的损失。
    “奋斗报”写道：“指责英法以武装干涉者的世界各国的声援，证明了侵略者应该担当这一责任，并且支付赔偿费。
