Title: New Workshop of Shanghai Turbine Factory (Picture)
Author: Lu Yongzhen
Date: 1956-12-07
Page: 2

The New Workshop of the Shanghai Turbine Factory

The expansion project of the state-owned Shanghai Turbine Factory, China's first factory to manufacture steam turbines, has been basically completed. After the expansion, the factory's building area and machinery equipment have increased four and a half times compared to the original. This is a corner of the large component section of the newly built steam turbine workshop.

　　　　　　　　　　　　　　Photo by Lu Yongzhen (Draft from Shanghai Labor Newspaper)

### 上海汽轮机厂的新建车间（图片）
陆永桢
1956-12-07
第2版()
专栏：

　　上海汽轮机厂的新建车间
　　我国第一个制造汽轮机的工厂——国营上海汽轮机厂的扩建工程，已经基本完成。扩建以后，厂区的建筑面积和机器设备比原来的都增加了四倍半。这是新建的汽轮机车间大件工段的一角。
　　　　　　　　　　　　　　陆永桢摄（上海劳动报社稿）
