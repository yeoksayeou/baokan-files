Title: Mayor of Bombay Visits Beijing After Visiting Tianjin
Author:
Date: 1956-12-31
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, December 30th: The Mayor of Bombay, India, Mr. Kader, arrived in Beijing this morning by train from Tianjin for a friendly visit. This evening, the Vice Mayor of Beijing, Zhang Youyu, hosted a banquet to welcome Mr. Kader. During his four-day visit to Tianjin, Mr. Kader visited the Tianjin Bicycle Factory, Knitting Factory, Central Hospital, Second Workers' Sanatorium, and Workers' Cultural Palace. Responsible persons from relevant departments of the Tianjin Municipal People's Committee also briefed the guest on the municipal construction, culture, education, and health.

### 孟买市长在天津参观访问后来到北京

1956-12-31
第1版()
专栏：

    孟买市长在天津参观访问后来到北京
    据新华社30日讯　印度孟买市市长卡德尔今天上午乘火车离开天津来到北京，进行友好访问。今晚，北京市副市长张友渔设宴欢迎卡德尔。卡德尔在天津的四天访问中，曾经参观了天津自行车厂、针织厂、中心医院、第二工人疗养院和工人文化宫。天津市人民委员会各有关部门的负责人并向客人介绍了市政建设和文教卫生等各方面的情况。
