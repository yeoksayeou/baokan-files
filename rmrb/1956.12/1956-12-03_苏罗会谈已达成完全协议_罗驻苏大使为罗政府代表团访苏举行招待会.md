Title: Soviet-Romanian Talks Reach Full Agreement; Romanian Ambassador to the Soviet Union Holds Reception for Romanian Government Delegation's Visit

Author:

Date: 1956-12-03
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, 2nd: According to TASS, negotiations between the Soviet government delegation and the Romanian People's Republic government delegation continued on December 1st in the Kremlin. As before, the negotiations proceeded in a practical, sincere, and fully mutually understanding manner.

	The Soviet side participating in the talks included Bulganin, Mikoyan, and Saburov, among others. The Romanian side participating in the talks included Stoica, Bîrlădeanu, among others.

	The two delegations discussed the suggestions made by the advisors regarding the issues of common concern to the Soviet and Romanian governments. They reached complete agreement on the issues discussed. The two delegations have agreed to issue a statement regarding the negotiations.

Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, 2nd: According to TASS, the Romanian Ambassador to the Soviet Union, Dalea, held a reception in Moscow on November 30th for the visit of the Romanian government delegation to the Soviet Union.

	The head of the Romanian government delegation, Chairman of the Council of Ministers Stoica, and delegation members including Vice-Chairman of the Council of Ministers Bîrlădeanu, Chairman of the State Planning Committee Marin, Minister of Finance Manescu, Minister of Foreign Trade Popescu, and others attended the reception.

	The Soviet side attending the reception included Bulganin, Mikoyan, Molotov, Pervukhin, Khrushchev, Zhukov, Furtseva, as well as ministers from various government departments, marshals of the Soviet Union, and representatives from all walks of life.

	Among the guests were Liu Xiao, Chinese Ambassador to the Soviet Union, Zhang Bojun and Hu Ziang, Deputy Heads of the Chinese National People's Congress delegation currently visiting Moscow, diplomatic envoys from various countries, and Soviet and foreign journalists.

	Stoica and Bulganin both spoke at the reception. The reception proceeded in a warm and friendly atmosphere.

	On the evening of the 30th, members of the Romanian government delegation, accompanied by Bulganin and others, went to the Moscow Grand Theater to watch the opera "The Decembrists."

### 苏罗会谈已达成完全协议　罗驻苏大使为罗政府代表团访苏举行招待会

1956-12-03
第5版()
专栏：

　　苏罗会谈已达成完全协议
　  罗驻苏大使为罗政府代表团访苏举行招待会
    新华社2日讯　据塔斯社莫斯科讯：苏联政府代表团和罗马尼亚人民共和国政府代表团之间的谈判12月1日在克里姆林宫继续进行。和以前一样，谈判是在切实、诚恳、完全相互谅解的情况下进行的。
    苏联方面参加会谈的有布尔加宁、米高扬和萨布罗夫等。罗马尼亚方面参加会谈的有斯托伊卡、伯尔拉德亚努等。
    两国代表团讨论了顾问们就苏联和罗马尼亚政府共同关心的问题所提出的建议。并且就讨论的问题达成完全的协议。两国代表团已经商定发表有关谈判的声明。
    新华社2日讯　据塔斯社莫斯科讯：罗马尼亚驻苏联大使达列亚为罗马尼亚政府代表团的访问苏联，在11月30日在莫斯科举行了招待会。
    罗马尼亚政府代表团团长、部长会议主席斯托伊卡，团员：部长会议副主席伯尔拉德亚努、国家计划委员会主席马林、财政部长曼尼斯库、对外贸易部长波佩斯库等出席了招待会。
    苏联方面参加招待会的有布尔加宁、米高扬、莫洛托夫、别尔乌辛、赫鲁晓夫、朱可夫、福尔采娃以及政府各部部长、苏联元帅和各界代表人士。
    在座的客人中还有中国驻苏联大使刘晓、正在莫斯科访问的中国全国人民代表大会代表团副团长章伯钧和胡子昂、各国外交使节以及苏联和外国的新闻记者。
    斯托伊卡和布尔加宁都在招待会上讲了话。招待会是在热烈、友好的气氛中进行的。
    30日晚间，罗马尼亚政府代表团人员在布尔加宁等陪同下到莫斯科大剧院观看了歌剧“十二月党人”。
