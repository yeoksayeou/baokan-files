Title: Mayor Qader of Bombay, India, Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1956-12-26
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, December 25th. Mayor Qader of Bombay, India, arrived in Beijing today at the invitation of Huang Huoqing, the Mayor of Tianjin, to visit Tianjin. He will go to Tianjin tomorrow.

Wu Han, the Deputy Mayor of Beijing, and others welcomed him at the airport.

Badarchinka and Ramu, the First Secretaries of the Indian Embassy, also welcomed him at the airport.

### 印度孟买市长卡德尔到京

1956-12-26
第1版()
专栏：

    印度孟买市长卡德尔到京
    新华社25日讯　应天津市市长黄火青的邀请到天津访问的印度孟买市市长卡德尔今天抵达北京。他将在明天去天津。
    到机场欢迎的有北京市副市长吴晗等。
    印度大使馆一等秘书巴德钦卡和拉穆也到机场欢迎。
