Title: President Mirza Presents Souvenirs to Premier Zhou
Author:
Date: 1956-12-24
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, Karachi, December 23rd. President Mirza of the Islamic Republic of Pakistan and his wife presented souvenirs to Premier Zhou Enlai, Vice Premier He Long, and all accompanying personnel on the evening of the 22nd. The souvenirs were exquisitely carved Pakistani silverware – tea sets, inkwells, cigarette boxes, etc., some of which were inlaid with gemstones. Mrs. Mirza also presented Premier Zhou Enlai with an ivory carving of a colored portrait of Premier Zhou Enlai. That evening, the Chinese guests were also invited to watch a cultural performance.

### 米尔扎总统赠纪念品给周总理

1956-12-24
第1版()
专栏：

    米尔扎总统赠纪念品给周总理
    新华社卡拉奇23日电　巴基斯坦伊斯兰共和国总统米尔扎和夫人22日晚上赠送纪念品给周恩来总理、贺龙副总理和全体随行人员。纪念品是雕刻得很精细的巴基斯坦银器——茶具、墨水壶、香烟盒等，其中有些镶着宝石。米尔扎夫人还赠给周恩来总理象牙雕刻的周恩来总理的彩色肖像。当天晚上，中国客人还应邀观看了文艺表演。
