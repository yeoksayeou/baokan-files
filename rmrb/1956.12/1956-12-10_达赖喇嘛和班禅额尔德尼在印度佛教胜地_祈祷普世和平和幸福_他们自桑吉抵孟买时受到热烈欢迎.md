Title: The Dalai Lama and Panchen Erdeni Pray for Universal Peace and Happiness at Buddhist Holy Sites in India; Warmly Welcomed Upon Arrival in Bombay from Sanchi
Author:
Date: 1956-12-10
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, New Delhi, December 9th: The Dalai Lama and Panchen Erdeni, along with their entourage, received a warm welcome at the railway station when they arrived in Bombay from Sanchi on December 8th by special train.

　　Bombay station was decorated with colored flags and red carpets. The Dalai Lama and Panchen Erdeni were adorned with many garlands. Welcoming the guests at the station were the Speaker of the Bombay State Legislative Assembly, the Deputy Chief Minister of Bombay State, and the Mayor of Bombay. Zhang Jiping, the Chinese Consul General in Bombay, and overseas Chinese also came to welcome them.

　　The Bombay Goodwill Cultural Association subsequently held a grand reception at the Taj Mahal Hotel. Two hundred prominent figures from all walks of life in Bombay attended the reception. The Vice-Chairman of the Association, Ismail, delivered a brief welcome speech. The Dalai Lama and Panchen Erdeni expressed their gratitude for the welcome they received and expressed their hope that the friendly relations between China and India would be further developed. Famous artists specially performed programs for the guests, including temple dances and prayer dances.

　　The Governor of Bombay State, Chagla, hosted a banquet in honor of the Dalai Lama and Panchen Erdeni.

　　When the Dalai Lama and Panchen Erdeni arrived in Sanchi, Central Province on December 7th, they were welcomed by the Governor of Central Province. As the Dalai Lama and Panchen Erdeni traveled by car from the railway station into the city, hundreds of people along the way paid their respects to them.

　　Sanchi is a Buddhist holy site, where the relics of Shariputra and Maha Maugalyayana, disciples of Buddha Shakyamuni, are enshrined. The relics were once moved to the London Museum by the British government, but they were returned to the Indian government some time ago.

　　The Dalai Lama and Panchen Erdeni offered prayers and golden lamps at the temple where the relics are enshrined.

　　They also visited the museum and viewed the many ancient sculptures on display there.

　　In the afternoon, the Indian Buddhist Association held a grand public reception to welcome them. Under an exquisite tent, the Dalai Lama and Panchen Erdeni sat on gilded chairs. They were garlanded and received the cheers of the ten thousand people attending the reception.

　　When the Dalai Lama spoke at the meeting, he expressed his gratitude to the Indian people and their leaders for regaining these relics through heroic struggle, giving him the opportunity to come and pay homage. He prayed for peace and happiness throughout the world, and that names like war, disease, and famine would never be heard again.

　　Panchen Erdeni said that he was very happy to see these relics. He offered prayers for lasting world peace. He said: "We must strive to cooperate with others to build world peace."

　　After the meeting, many lights on the trees were lit, and fireworks were set off to welcome the guests.

　　The Dalai Lama and Panchen Erdeni arrived in Sanchi from Agra, where they visited some famous historical sites, including the famous Taj Mahal.

### 达赖喇嘛和班禅额尔德尼在印度佛教胜地　祈祷普世和平和幸福　他们自桑吉抵孟买时受到热烈欢迎

1956-12-10
第1版()
专栏：

　　达赖喇嘛和班禅额尔德尼在印度佛教胜地
　　祈祷普世和平和幸福
　　他们自桑吉抵孟买时受到热烈欢迎
    新华社新德里9日电　达赖喇嘛和班禅额尔德尼和他们的随行人员在12月8日乘专车从桑吉到达孟买的时候，在火车站上受到了热烈的欢迎。
    孟买车站装饰着采色的旗帜，铺着红色的地毯。达赖喇嘛和班禅额尔德尼给戴上了许多花环。在车站上迎接客人的有孟买邦立法议会议长，孟买邦副首席部长，孟买市长。中国驻孟买总领事张吉平和华侨也到车站欢迎。
    孟买亲善文化协会随后在华丽大饭店举行了盛大的招待会。孟买各界著名人士二百人参加这次招待会。协会的副主席伊斯梅尔致简短的欢迎词。达赖喇嘛和班禅额尔德尼对所受到的欢迎表示感谢，并且表示希望中印两国之间的友好关系获得进一步的发展。著名的艺术家们特别为客人们演出了节目，其中包括寺院舞和祈祷舞。
    孟买邦邦长查格拉设宴招待了达赖喇嘛和班禅额尔德尼。
    当12月7日达赖喇嘛和班禅额尔德尼到达中央省的桑吉的时候，他们受到了中央省省长的欢迎。达赖喇嘛和班禅额尔德尼从火车站乘汽车进入市内时，路上有数以百计的人们向他们致敬。
    桑吉是佛教的胜地，释迦牟尼的弟子奢利补多罗和摩呵目加略的遗骨就供奉在这里。遗骨曾经被英国政府移往伦敦博物馆，但是一些时候以前已经被印度政府运回来了。
    达赖喇嘛和班禅额尔德尼在供奉遗骨的庙宇里作了祈祷，并且供上金灯。
    他们还参观了博物馆，观看了那里陈列的许多古代雕刻。
    下午，印度佛教协会举行了盛大的公众招待会，欢迎他们。在精致的帐篷下面，达赖喇嘛和班禅额尔德尼坐在镀金的椅子上。他们被戴了花环，接受参加招待会的一万人的欢呼。
    达赖喇嘛在会上讲话的时候，表示感谢印度人民和他们的领袖们通过英勇的斗争夺回了这些遗骨，使他有机会前来参拜。他祈祷普天之下得到和平和幸福，战争、疾病和饥荒等名称将永远不再听到。
    班禅额尔德尼表示他很高兴看到这些遗骨。他为世界永久和平作了祷告。他说：“我们必须努力同别人合作来建立世界和平。”
    会后，树上的许许多多的灯都点燃了，还放了焰火，欢迎客人们。
    达赖喇嘛和班禅额尔德尼是从亚格拉到达桑吉的，他们在亚格拉参观了一些名胜古迹，包括著名的泰吉·玛哈尔陵。
