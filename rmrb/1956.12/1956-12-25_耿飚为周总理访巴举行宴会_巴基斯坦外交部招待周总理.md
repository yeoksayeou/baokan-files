Title: Geng Biao Holds Banquet for Premier Zhou's Visit to Pakistan; Pakistan Foreign Ministry Hosts Premier Zhou

Author:
Date: 1956-12-25
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Karachi, December 24th: Geng Biao, the Chinese Ambassador to Pakistan, held a banquet at the new location of the Chinese Embassy on the evening of the 23rd to celebrate Premier Zhou Enlai and Vice Premier He Long's visit to Pakistan.

Among those attending the banquet were Pakistani President Mirza, Prime Minister Suhrawardy, Speaker of the National Assembly Wahab Khan, Foreign Minister Noon, government ministers, other senior officials, military generals, and prominent figures from various circles, as well as diplomatic envoys from various countries to Pakistan.

The new location of the Chinese Embassy is in the city center. On this evening, the embassy was illuminated with colorful lights and decorated very lavishly. The spacious lawn was crowded with guests.

At the banquet, Premier Zhou Enlai suggested a toast to President Mirza's health, Pakistan's prosperity, China-Pakistan friendship, and world peace.

President Mirza proposed a toast to Chairman Mao Zedong's health and the prosperity of the People's China.

Premier Zhou Enlai freely interacted with the guests and had cordial conversations with them. Many guests asked Premier Zhou Enlai for autographs, and many others asked to take photos with Premier Zhou.

Xinhua News Agency, Karachi, December 24th: The Pakistani Foreign Ministry held a reception on the evening of the 23rd to host Premier Zhou Enlai and his entourage.

### 耿飚为周总理访巴举行宴会  巴基斯坦外交部招待周总理

1956-12-25
第1版()
专栏：

    耿飚为周总理访巴举行宴会
    巴基斯坦外交部招待周总理
    新华社卡拉奇24日电　中国驻巴基斯坦大使耿飚为周恩来总理和贺龙副总理访问巴基斯坦，23日晚上在中国使馆新址举行宴会。
    出席宴会的有巴基斯坦总统米尔扎、总理苏拉瓦底、国民议会议长瓦哈布汗、外交部长努恩、政府各部部长、其他高级官员、军事将领和各界名流以及各国驻巴基斯坦外交使节。
    中国大使馆新址位于市中心。这天晚上大使馆内照耀着五彩的灯光，装饰得非常华丽。宽敞的草地上挤满了客人。
    在宴会上，周恩来总理建议为米尔扎总统的健康、巴基斯坦的繁荣、中巴友谊和世界和平干杯。
    米尔扎总统建议为毛泽东主席的健康和人民中国的繁荣干杯。
    周恩来总理不拘束地同客人们接触，同他们亲切地交谈。许多客人要求周恩来总理签名，还有许多客人要求同周总理一起照像。
    新华社卡拉奇24日电　巴基斯坦外交部23日晚间举行招待会，招待周恩来总理一行。
