Title: CPPCC National Committee Standing Committee Holds Meeting; Approves Draft Regulations on 1957 National Construction Bonds
Author:
Date: 1956-12-27
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, December 26th – The Standing Committee of the National Committee of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference held the thirty-second meeting on the afternoon of the 26th.

After hearing the explanation from Vice Minister of Finance Rong Zihe, the meeting discussed and approved the draft regulations on the 1957 National Economic Construction Bond. This draft regulation shall be discussed and approved by the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress.

After hearing the explanation from Zheng Zhenduo, the head of the Cultural Group of the CPPCC National Committee, the meeting discussed the issue of improving the journal of the National Committee of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference, and passed a decision on improving the editorial work of the CPPCC National Committee's journal. This decision pointed out that the journal's task is to report on the activities of the CPPCC National Committee, the CPPCC local committees, and the democratic parties and people's organizations, and to exchange work experience; to reflect the problems and opinions of all aspects of the united front, and to conduct free discussions, in order to consolidate and expand the people's democratic united front, and to implement the policy of long-term coexistence and mutual supervision of the democratic parties and the policy of letting a hundred flowers bloom. The content of the journal includes: important documents of the CPPCC National Committee, important documents of democratic parties and people's organizations related to the united front (including historical materials and other research materials); activity reports and work experience introductions of various work units of the CPPCC National Committee, CPPCC local committees, democratic parties, and people's organizations; treatises on united front policy issues; reflections and discussions on issues and opinions from all aspects of the united front; reports, communications, essays, literary works, and other manuscripts reflecting the lives of people of all strata, all ethnic minorities, and overseas Chinese. The meeting also approved the list of the journal's editorial committee.

### 政协全国委员会常委会举行会议  同意1957年建设公债条例草案

1956-12-27
第1版()
专栏：

    政协全国委员会常委会举行会议
    同意1957年建设公债条例草案
    新华社26日讯　中国人民政治协商会议全国委员会常务委员会在26日下午举行第三十二次会议。
    会议在听了财政部副部长戎子和的说明后，讨论并同意1957年国家经济建设公债条例草案。这个条例草案将经全国人民代表大会常务委员会讨论批准。
    会议在听了政协全国委员会文化组组长郑振铎的说明后，讨论了关于改进中国人民政治协商会议全国委员会会刊问题，通过了关于改进政协全国委员会会刊编辑工作的决定。这个决定指出，会刊的任务，是报道政协全国委员会、政协各地方委员会和各民主党派、人民团体的活动情况，交流工作经验；反映统一战线各方面的问题和意见，开展自由讨论，以巩固和扩大人民民主统一战线，贯彻各民主党派长期共存、互相监督的方针和百家争鸣的政策。会刊的内容包括：政协全国委员会的重要文件，各民主党派、各人民团体有关统一战线的重要文件（包括历史资料和其他研究资料在内）；政协全国委员会各工作单位、政协各地方委员会、各民主党派和人民团体的活动情况报道和工作经验介绍；关于统一战线政策问题的论著；关于统一战线各方面的问题和意见的反映和讨论；反映各阶层人民、各少数民族和华侨生活的报告、通讯、杂感、文艺作品和其他文稿。会议还通过了会刊编辑委员会名单。
