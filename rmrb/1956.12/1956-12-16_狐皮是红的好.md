Title: Fox Fur Looks Best Red
Author: Hao Sumin
Date: 1956-12-16
Page: 8

Fox Fur Looks Best Red
(Mongolian Proverb)
	Language is truly good,
	Fox fur looks best red.
	Where does belly fat come from? – From eating well,
	Where does a lesson come from? – From experiencing setbacks.
			Compiled and translated by Hao Sumin

### 狐皮是红的好
郝苏民
1956-12-16
第8版()
专栏：

    狐皮是红的好
    （蒙古谚语）
    语言是真的好，
    狐皮是红的好。
    肚油从何来——吃的肥，
    教训从何来——吃过亏。
              郝苏民辑译
