Title: Founding Congress of the French Communist Youth League

Author: 
Date: 1956-12-30
Page: 6

    Xinhua News Agency Draft: The Founding Congress of the French Communist Youth League was held in Paris from December 14th to 17th, where the leadership was elected, and the league's program and charter were adopted.

专栏：

    法兰西共产主义青年联盟从12月14日到17日在巴黎举行了成立大会，并且选出了领导机构，通过了联盟的纲领和章程。（新华社稿）
