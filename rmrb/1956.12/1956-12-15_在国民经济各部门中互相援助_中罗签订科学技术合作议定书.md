Title: Mutual Assistance in Various Sectors of the National Economy: China and Romania Sign Scientific and Technological Cooperation Protocol
Author:
Date: 1956-12-15
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, December 14th: The fourth session of the Sino-Romanian Joint Commission on Scientific and Technological Cooperation signed a protocol. The fourth session was held in Beijing from November 24th to December 12th this year.

    The protocol stipulates that the two sides should provide mutual assistance in various sectors of the national economy. The People's Republic of China will provide the Romanian People's Republic with technical data related to the chemical, pharmaceutical, food, textile, pulp and paper industries, handicrafts, etc., as well as seeds and planting materials of medicinal plants and crops, and will send printing technicians to Romania to assist in Chinese printing work.

    The Romanian People's Republic will provide the People's Republic of China with technical data related to the petroleum industry, chemical industry, building materials, railways, textiles, leather making, medicine, etc., as well as crop seeds, and will accept Chinese experts to Romania for medical research.

    The Romanian People's Republic has dispatched experts to China to provide technical assistance in petroleum and chemical engineering.

    According to the decision of the Joint Commission, the corresponding scientific research institutions of the two countries will maintain direct contact to facilitate mutual exchange and cooperation in scientific research work.

    The Romanian delegation left Beijing by plane on the 14th to return home.

### 在国民经济各部门中互相援助  中罗签订科学技术合作议定书

1956-12-15
第1版()
专栏：

    在国民经济各部门中互相援助
    中罗签订科学技术合作议定书
    据新华社14日讯　中罗科学与技术合作联合委员会第四届会议签订了议定书。第四届会议是今年11月24日到12月12日在北京举行的。
    在议定书中规定了双方在国民经济各部门中要互相援助。中华人民共和国将供给罗马尼亚人民共和国有关化学、医药、食品、纺织、纸浆造纸工业、手工艺等技术资料，以及供给药用植物及农作物的种子和种植材料，并且将派遣印刷技术工人赴罗马尼亚协助中文的印刷工作。
    罗马尼亚人民共和国将供给中华人民共和国有关石油工业、化学工业、建筑材料、铁路、纺织、制革、医药等技术资料以及农作物种子，并将接受中国专家赴罗马尼亚考察医学。
    罗马尼亚人民共和国已派遣了专家到中国进行石油、化工方面的技术援助工作。
    根据联合委员会的决议，两国相应的科学研究机构将保持直接联系，以便科学研究工作的互相交流和合作。
    罗马尼亚代表团已在14日乘飞机离开北京回国。
