Title: Italian Fascist Bandits Attack Local Italian Communist Party Organizations
Author:
Date: 1956-12-04
Page: 6

　　Xinhua News Agency, Rome, December 30th, TASS dispatch: Newspapers reported that Italian Fascist bandits attacked two local organizations of the Italian Communist Party in Genoa. These Fascist elements set fire to the office buildings of these two organizations, but the fire was extinguished in time.

### 意大利法西斯匪徒　袭击意共地方组织

1956-12-04
第6版()
专栏：

　  意大利法西斯匪徒
　　袭击意共地方组织
    据新华社30日讯　塔斯社罗马29日讯：报纸报道：意大利的法西斯匪徒袭击了意大利共产党在热那亚的两个地方组织。这些法西斯分子放火烧着了这两个组织的办公楼房，但是火被及时地扑灭了。
