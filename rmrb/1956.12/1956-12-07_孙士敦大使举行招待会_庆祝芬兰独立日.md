Title: Ambassador Sundström Holds Reception to Celebrate Finnish Independence Day
Author:
Date: 1956-12-07
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, December 6th. Finnish Ambassador to China, Mr. Sundström, held a reception at the Beijing Hotel this evening to celebrate Finnish Independence Day.
　　
    Chen Yun, Acting Premier of the State Council, Vice Premier Bo Yibo, Vice Chairmen of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress, Shen Junru and Chen Shutong, and Acting Foreign Minister Zhang Wentian attended the reception.
　　
    At the reception, Ambassador Sundström proposed a toast to the prosperity of the Chinese people and the health of Chairman Mao Zedong. The Ambassador also congratulated the government of the People's Republic of China on its new achievements in its cause of benefiting the Chinese people.
　　
    In his speech, Acting Premier Chen Yun extended congratulations to the Republic of Finland on behalf of the Chinese people and government. He proposed a toast to the prosperity of the Republic of Finland and the happiness of the Finnish people, to the development and strengthening of friendly cooperation between China and Finland, and to the health of Finnish President Kekkonen.
　　
    The reception lasted for two hours. Among the three hundred guests were heads of various departments of Chinese state organs, people from all walks of life in Beijing, and diplomats from various countries stationed in China.

### 孙士敦大使举行招待会　庆祝芬兰独立日

1956-12-07
第1版()
专栏：

　　孙士敦大使举行招待会
　　庆祝芬兰独立日
    新华社6日讯　芬兰共和国驻中国大使孙士敦今晚在北京饭店举行招待会，庆祝芬兰独立日。
    国务院陈云代总理、薄一波副总理，全国人民代表大会常务委员会副委员长沈钧儒、陈叔通，外交部代部长张闻天都出席了招待会。
    孙士敦大使在招待会上提议为中国人民的繁荣和毛泽东主席的健康干杯。大使还祝贺中华人民共和国政府在为中国人民谋福利的事业中取得新的成就。
    陈云代总理在致词中代表中国人民和政府向芬兰共和国表示祝贺。他提议为芬兰共和国的繁荣和芬兰人民的幸福，为中芬两国友好合作的发展和加强，为芬兰吉科宁总统的健康干杯。
    招待会共历时两小时。在三百名客人中有中国国家机关各部门的负责人，北京社会各界人士和各国驻中国的外交官。
