Title: Pre-fabricated Components Using Bamboo Templates (Picture)
Author: Guo Leizhen
Date: 1956-12-27
Page: 2
Actively Seeking New Raw Materials

    Pre-fabricated Components Using Bamboo Templates
    The Second Construction Site of the Concrete Company of Wuhan Metallurgical Construction Corporation is using bamboo templates extensively to replace wooden templates for pre-fabricating various building components. For the pre-fabrication of column components alone, they have saved more than 2,400 yuan. The picture shows the workers using bamboo templates. (Photo by Guo Leizhen, contributed by Changjiang Daily)

### 用竹模板预制构件（图片）
郭雷震
1956-12-27
第2版()
专栏：积极寻找新原料

    用竹模板预制构件
    武汉冶金建筑总公司混凝土公司第二工地，大量利用竹模板代替木模板来预制各种建筑构件，仅预制柱子构件一项就节约了二千四百多元。这是工人们正在利用竹模板工作。　郭雷震摄（长江日报社稿）
