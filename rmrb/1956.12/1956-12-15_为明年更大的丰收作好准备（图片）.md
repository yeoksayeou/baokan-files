Page: 3

Preparing for a Bigger Harvest Next Year
    The Xinsheng Agricultural Cooperative in Suihua County, Heilongjiang Province, achieved a bumper harvest this year. The average yield of grain from more than 1,300 hectares of field crops was over 4,000 jin per hectare. Now the entire cooperative is preparing to strive for an even bigger harvest next year. Photo by Yu Min (Xinhua News Agency)
  Left: The Seventh Production Team of the Xinsheng Agricultural Cooperative is collecting manure. The entire cooperative has already dug out enough fertilizer for 600 hectares of land.
  Above: This year, the Xinsheng Agricultural Cooperative plans to buy eight strong horses. This is a newly purchased dark red horse with big eyes and a wide mouth.

### 为明年更大的丰收作好准备（图片）
于敏
1956-12-15
第3版()
专栏：

    为明年更大的丰收作好准备
    黑龙江省绥化县新胜农业社今年获得了丰收。一千三百多公顷大田作物，平均每公顷收粮食四千多斤。现在全社正在为争取明年更大的丰收作准备。　于敏摄（新华社稿）
  左　新胜农业社第七生产队正在起粪。全社已刨出能上六百公顷地的肥料。
  上　今年新胜农业社计划买八匹壮马。这是新买来的大眼睛宽嘴巴的枣红马。
