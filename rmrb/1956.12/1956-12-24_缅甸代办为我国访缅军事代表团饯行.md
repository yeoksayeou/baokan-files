Title: Burmese Chargé d'Affaires Hosts Farewell Banquet for Chinese Military Delegation to Burma
Author:
Date: 1956-12-24
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, December 22nd: The Chinese Military Goodwill Delegation to Burma, headed by Marshal Ye Jianying, is about to depart. On the afternoon of the 21st, the Chargé d'Affaires of the Burmese Embassy in China, U Ba Maung, and his wife held a reception to bid farewell to the delegation.

    Marshal Ye Jianying, head of the Chinese Military Goodwill Delegation to Burma, and all members of the delegation attended the reception.

    Vice Minister of Foreign Affairs Zhang Hanfu, President of the Sino-Burmese Friendship Association Zheng Zhenduo, and Vice President Qian Weichang also attended the reception.

    Senior generals of the Chinese People's Liberation Army, including General Chen Geng, Senior General Xiao Hua, and Lieutenant General Wang Bingzhang of the Air Force, also attended the reception.

    Military attachés of various countries in China were also invited to attend the reception.

    At the reception, Chargé d'Affaires U Ba Maung and Delegation Leader Ye Jianying delivered speeches. They unanimously believed that this visit will surely promote the further development of friendly relations between the people and armies of China and Burma.

    The Chinese Military Goodwill Delegation to Burma was invited by the Burmese government.

### 缅甸代办为我国访缅军事代表团饯行

1956-12-24
第1版()
专栏：

    缅甸代办为我国访缅军事代表团饯行
    新华社22日讯　以叶剑英元帅为首的中国军事友好访缅代表团即将启程，21日下午，缅甸驻华大使馆临时代办吴巴茂及夫人举行酒会为代表团饯行。
    中国军事友好访缅代表团团长叶剑英元帅和全体团员都出席了酒会。
    外交部副部长章汉夫，中缅友好协会会长郑振铎、副会长钱伟长也出席了酒会。
    出席酒会的还有中国人民解放军的高级将领陈赓大将、萧华上将、王秉璋空军中将等。
    各国驻华武官也应邀出席了酒会。
    在酒会上，吴巴茂代办和叶剑英团长相互致词，他们一致认为，这次访问必将促进中缅两国人民和军队之间友好关系的进一步发展。
    中国军事友好访缅代表团是应缅甸政府邀请的。
