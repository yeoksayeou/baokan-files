Title: Chairman Mao Receives Mrs. Martins of Brazil
Author:
Date: 1956-12-09
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, December 8th: Chairman Mao Zedong received Brazilian sculptor Mrs. Martins and the personnel who accompanied her on her visit to China this evening.

Guo Moruo and Zhang Xiruo were also present at the reception.

### 毛主席接见巴西马丁斯夫人

1956-12-09
第1版()
专栏：

　　毛主席接见巴西马丁斯夫人
    新华社8日讯　毛泽东主席今晚接见了巴西雕塑家马丁斯夫人和同她一起来华访问的人员。
    接见的时候，郭沫若和张奚若也在座。
