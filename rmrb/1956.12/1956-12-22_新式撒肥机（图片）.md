Title: New-style Fertilizer Spreader (Photo)
Author: Yang Putao
Date: 1956-12-22
Page: 3

    New-style Fertilizer Spreader
    The Shanghai Public-Private Joint New China Iron Factory recently successfully trial-produced a tractor-towed fertilizer spreader modeled after Soviet products. This fertilizer spreader can be used to spread various granular and powdered fertilizers. Each machine can complete the fertilizer spreading work on 1.8 hectares of land per hour.　Xinhua News Agency reporter Yang Putao photo

### 新式撒肥机（图片）
杨溥涛
1956-12-22
第3版()
专栏：

    新式撒肥机
    上海公私合营新中华铁工厂最近仿照苏联产品试制成功一种拖拉撒肥机。这种撒肥机可以用来撒各种颗粒肥料和粉状肥料，每一台每小时可以完成一点八公顷土地的撒肥工作。　新华社记者　杨溥涛摄
