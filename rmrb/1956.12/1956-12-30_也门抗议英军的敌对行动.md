Title: Yemen Protests British Military Hostilities
Author:
Date: 1956-12-30
Page: 6

    Xinhua News Agency, London, December 29th. TASS, London, December 29th: The Yemeni Legation in London issued the following announcement on the 28th:

    On December 25th, British troops attacked a residential area near Qataba. Many residents were injured. A fire broke out that burned for eight hours.

    In addition, on the 25th and 26th, British troops strafed the city of Qataba and its suburbs with machine guns and other weapons, destroying several houses.

    The Yemeni Chargé d'Affaires in London has protested to the British Foreign Office, demanding that British troops cease their hostile actions against Yemen.

### 也门抗议英军的敌对行动

1956-12-30
第6版()
专栏：

    也门抗议英军的敌对行动
    新华社29日讯　塔斯社伦敦29日讯：也门驻伦敦公使馆28日发表了下列公告：
    12月25日英国军队攻击了卡塔巴近郊的一个居民点。居民中有许多人受伤。发生了连烧八个小时的火灾。
    此外，英国军队在25日和26日用机枪等武器扫射卡塔巴市和市郊，毁坏了好几座房子。
    也门驻伦敦代办向英国外交部提出抗议，要求制止英国军队对也门的敌对行动。
