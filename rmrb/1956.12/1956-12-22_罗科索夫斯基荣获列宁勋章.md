Title: Rokossovsky Awarded the Order of Lenin
Author:
Date: 1956-12-22
Page: 5

    Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, 21st. TASS reports: The Presidium of the Supreme Soviet of the USSR issued an order on the 20th, awarding the Order of Lenin to Marshal of the Soviet Union, Konstantin Konstantinovich Rokossovsky, to celebrate his sixtieth birthday and to commend him for his meritorious service to the Soviet army and the Soviet state.

### 罗科索夫斯基荣获列宁勋章

1956-12-22
第5版()
专栏：

    罗科索夫斯基荣获列宁勋章
    新华社21日讯　据塔斯社莫斯科讯：苏联最高苏维埃主席团20日发布命令，把一枚列宁勋章授给苏联元帅康·康·罗科索夫斯基，以祝贺他诞生六十周年和表彰他为苏联军队和苏维埃国家所建立的功勋。
