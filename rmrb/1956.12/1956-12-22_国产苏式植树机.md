Title: Domestic Soviet-style Tree Planter
Author: Li Yingchang
Date: 1956-12-22
Page: 2

    Domestic Soviet-style Tree Planter
    (Reported by this newspaper) The Shenyang Public-Private Joint Venture Zhendong Iron Factory successfully imitated the Soviet imported ПY-1 tree planter.
    This tree planter is simple to operate and inexpensive, suitable for planting broad-leaved trees, shrubs and trees. It can be used on flat or sloping ground. Each tree planter is equipped with two workers and can plant 3.75 hectares per day, which is 17.5 times more efficient than manual afforestation. (Li Yingchang)

### 国产苏式植树机
李应昌
1956-12-22
第2版()
专栏：

    国产苏式植树机
    本报讯　沈阳市公私合营的震东铁工厂仿造苏联进口的ПY—1植树机成功。
    这种植树机操作简便，造价很低，适于营造阔叶树乔灌木。在平地或斜坡地均可使用。每台植树机配备两名工人，一日可以造三点七五公顷，比人工造林提高十七点五倍。　　（李应昌）
