Title: Large Numbers of East German Workers and Youth Join the Socialist Unity Party of Germany
Author:
Date: 1956-12-03
Page: 5

　　Large Numbers of East German Workers and Youth
　　Join the Socialist Unity Party of Germany
    Xinhua News Agency, 2nd. According to the German News Agency on the 1st: A large number of East German workers and youths have joined the Socialist Unity Party of Germany in recent months.
    In the Karl-Marx-Stadt district, more than 3,000 people have joined the Socialist Unity Party since the beginning of August, the vast majority of whom are workers and young people; during the same period, in the Wismut mining area, the party organization accepted 1,600 miners as candidate members.

### 大批民主德国工人和青年　参加德国统一社会党

1956-12-03
第5版()
专栏：

　　大批民主德国工人和青年
　　参加德国统一社会党
    新华社2日讯　据德意志通讯社1日讯：大批民主德国的工人和青年在最近几个月中参加了德国统一社会党。
    在卡尔·马克思斯塔特专区，从8月初到现在，有三千多人参加了统一社会党，其中绝大多数是工人和青年；同一时期，在维斯穆特矿区，党组织接受了一千六百名矿工作为候补党员。
