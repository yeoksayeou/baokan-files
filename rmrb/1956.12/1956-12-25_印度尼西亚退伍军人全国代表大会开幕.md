Title: Indonesian Veterans' National Congress Opens
Author:
Date: 1956-12-25
Page: 5
Xinhua News Agency, Jakarta, December 24th: Indonesian Prime Minister Sastroamidjojo stated at the opening ceremony of the Indonesian Veterans' National Congress on the evening of the 22nd that the integrity of the democratic Republic of Indonesia is currently being harmed and threatened by actions that violate the laws of the country. He said that the shortcomings and errors of the government should not be used by anyone as an excuse to take illegal actions. The government fully understands the current difficulties and aspirations of the people and is making every effort to overcome them and satisfy them.

President Sukarno also spoke, calling on all veterans to abandon partisan interests and organize a national veterans' organization.

### 印度尼西亚退伍军人全国代表大会开幕

1956-12-25
第5版()
专栏：

    印度尼西亚退伍军人全国代表大会开幕
    据新华社雅加达24日电　印度尼西亚总理沙斯特罗阿米佐约22日晚在印度尼西亚退伍军人全国代表大会开幕式上说，目前民主的印度尼西亚共和国的完整受到一些违反国法的行动的危害和威胁。他说，政府的缺点和错误不应被任何人当作采取违法行动的借口。政府充分了解现在人民的困难和愿望，并且正在竭力加以克服和给以满足。
    苏加诺总统也讲了话，他号召所有退伍军人抛弃党派利益，组织一个全国性的退伍军人组织。
