Title: Sudanese Prime Minister Arrives in Cairo
Author:
Date: 1956-12-14
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, Cairo, December 13th: Sudanese Prime Minister Abdallah Khalil, accompanied by Interior Minister Ali Abdel Rahman, arrived in Cairo today for an official visit.

### 苏丹总理到达开罗

1956-12-14
第6版()
专栏：

    苏丹总理到达开罗
    新华社13日讯　开罗消息：苏丹总理阿卜杜拉·哈利勒在内政部长阿里·阿卜杜勒·拉赫曼的陪同下，今天到达开罗作正式访问。
