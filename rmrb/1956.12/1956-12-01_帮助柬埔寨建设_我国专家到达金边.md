Title: Helping Cambodia with Construction, Chinese Experts Arrive in Phnom Penh
Author:
Date: 1956-12-01
Page: 1

　  Helping Cambodia with Construction
　  Chinese Experts Arrive in Phnom Penh
    Xinhua News Agency, Phnom Penh, 30th - Eight Chinese experts dispatched to the Kingdom of Cambodia to assist in construction under the Sino-Cambodian Economic Aid Agreement arrived in Phnom Penh on the morning of the 29th.
    The Chinese experts will collect relevant data based on the plans to assist Cambodia in establishing textile mills, cement plants, plywood factories, and paper mills, in order to carry out design work.

### 帮助柬埔寨建设  我国专家到达金边

1956-12-01
第1版()
专栏：

　  帮助柬埔寨建设
　  我国专家到达金边
    新华社金边30日电　根据中柬经济援助协定派来柬埔寨王国帮助建设的中国专家八人已经在29日上午到达金边。
    中国专家们将根据援助柬埔寨建立纺织厂、水泥厂、胶合板厂和造纸厂的计划收集有关资料，以便进行设计工作。
