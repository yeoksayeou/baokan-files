Title: Hungarian Security Forces Arrest a Group of Armed Bandits
Author:
Date: 1956-12-30
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, Budapest, 29th: According to TASS, Hungarian security forces have recently arrested a large number of counter-revolutionary bandits in the vicinity of Budapest. These bandits, in the final days of October, engaged in mass shootings, robbery, and theft of state property. They also kidnapped many upright citizens and murdered many of them.

The bandits also committed several acts of sabotage. One of those arrested, Béla Nyihmond, led a gang that attempted to sabotage trains and blow up railway bridges.

A large quantity of weapons and ammunition was found in the homes of those arrested. The state prosecutor's office is investigating the cases of these counter-revolutionary bandits.

### 匈警卫部队逮捕一批武装匪徒

1956-12-30
第5版()
专栏：

    匈警卫部队逮捕一批武装匪徒
    新华社29日讯　据塔斯社布达佩斯讯：匈牙利警卫部队最近几天在布达佩斯附近逮捕了大批反革命匪徒。这些匪徒在10月末的几天中曾经分批进行枪杀、抢劫和盗窃国家财产的活动。他们还绑架了许多正直的人，并且杀害了他们当中的许多人。
    匪徒们还犯了几起破坏罪行。这次被捕的贝洛·尼赫蒙德曾经领导过一帮匪徒，企图破坏火车和炸毁铁路桥梁。
    在被捕者的住所内查出了大批武器和弹药。国家检察机关正在审理这批反革命匪徒的案件。
