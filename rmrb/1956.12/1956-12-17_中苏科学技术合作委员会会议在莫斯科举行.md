Title: Sino-Soviet Scientific and Technological Cooperation Committee Meeting Held in Moscow
Author:
Date: 1956-12-17
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, December 15th: According to TASS, the fifth session of the Sino-Soviet Scientific and Technological Cooperation Committee opened in Moscow on the 14th.

    The Soviet leader of the committee, First Deputy Chairman of the Soviet State Economic Committee, Siluyanov, said at the meeting that the Soviet people are watching China's great achievements in economic development with great satisfaction, and Sino-Soviet scientific and technological cooperation plays a significant role in this regard. The foundation of the committee's work is the eternal friendship between the Chinese and Soviet peoples. No conspiracy of the enemy can shake it.

    Li Qiang, the head of the Chinese delegation attending the meeting, said in his speech that in the two years since the signing of the Sino-Soviet Scientific and Technological Cooperation Agreement, this cooperation has rapidly developed and consolidated. The Soviet government's declaration on developing and strengthening the foundation of friendship and cooperation between the Soviet Union and other socialist countries has been warmly responded to in China. This declaration contributes to the general economic development of all socialist countries and helps strengthen the struggle of the people of these countries against imperialist aggression.

    This session of the Sino-Soviet Scientific and Technological Cooperation Committee will discuss the transfer of technical design data for the construction of television centers, building materials factories, film studios, etc. to China, and will also discuss issues related to the exchange of scientific and technological achievements and production experience, and the mutual dispatch of scientific and technical personnel.

### 中苏科学技术合作委员会会议在莫斯科举行

1956-12-17
第1版()
专栏：

    中苏科学技术合作委员会会议在莫斯科举行
    新华社15日讯　据塔斯社莫斯科讯：中苏科学技术合作委员会第五届会议14日在莫斯科开幕。
    委员会苏联方面的领导者、苏联国家经济委员会第一副主席西卢亚诺夫在会上说，苏联的人们极其满意地注视着中国在经济发展中的伟大成就，中苏两国的科学技术合作在这方面起着不小的作用。委员会工作的基础是中苏两大民族的永恒的友谊。敌人的任何阴谋诡计都不可能动摇它。
    参加这次会议的中国代表团团长李强在会上发言时说，在签订中苏科学技术合作协定以来的两年期间，这种合作很快地得到了发展和巩固。苏联政府关于发展和加强苏联和其他社会主义国家之间的友谊和合作的基础的宣言在中国得到了热烈的响应。这个宣言有助于各个社会主义国家普遍的经济发展、有助于加强这些国家人民反对帝国主义的侵略的斗争。
    中苏科学技术合作委员会的这届会议将讨论把建设电视中心、建筑材料工厂、电影制片厂等技术设计资料转交给中国的问题，还要讨论有关交流科学技术上的成就和生产经验，互相派遣科学技术人员等问题。
