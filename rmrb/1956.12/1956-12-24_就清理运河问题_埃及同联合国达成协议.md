Title: Egypt and the United Nations Reach Agreement on Clearing the Canal
Author:
Date: 1956-12-24
Page: 5

    Egypt and the United Nations Reach Agreement on Clearing the Canal
    Xinhua News Agency, December 23rd, Cairo dispatch: Cairo Radio broadcast on the 22nd that the Egyptian government and the United Nations have reached an agreement on the issue of clearing the Suez Canal.
    The radio station said that the Italian and Danish salvage ships currently in Port Said are ready to begin work. A West German salvage fleet will also begin work rapidly.

### 就清理运河问题  埃及同联合国达成协议

1956-12-24
第5版()
专栏：

    就清理运河问题
    埃及同联合国达成协议
    新华社23日讯　开罗消息：开罗电台22日广播说，埃及政府和联合国方面已经就清理苏伊士运河问题达成协议。
    这家电台说，现在在塞得港的意大利和丹麦的打捞船已经做好了准备工作。一支西德打捞船队也将迅速开始工作。
