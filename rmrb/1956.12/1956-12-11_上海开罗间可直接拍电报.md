Title: Direct Telegrams Can Be Sent Between Shanghai and Cairo
Author: 
Date: 1956-12-11
Page: 1

    Xinhua News Agency, 10th. The direct wireless telegraph circuit between Shanghai and Cairo has been successfully tested. After consultation between the telecommunications authorities of China and Egypt, the telegraph service will be officially opened from December 10th; from the same day, the telegraph rates between the two countries will be reduced by about a quarter compared to the past when routed through other countries.

### 上海开罗间可直接拍电报

1956-12-11
第1版()
专栏：

    上海开罗间可直接拍电报
    新华社10日讯　上海—开罗间的直达无线电报电路已经试验成功。经中埃两国电信主管机关的协商，从12月10日起正式开放电报业务；从同一天开始，两国的电报价目比过去经过其他国家接转的时候降低约四分之一。
