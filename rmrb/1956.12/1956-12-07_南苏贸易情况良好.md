Title: Good Trade Situation Between Yugoslavia and the Soviet Union
Author: 
Date: 1956-12-07
Page: 5

　　Xinhua News Agency Dispatch: The Yugoslav-Soviet Commission, reviewing the results of trade between Yugoslavia and the Soviet Union this year, signed a protocol in Belgrade on December 4th. The commission expressed satisfaction with the overall trade situation between the two countries.

### 南苏贸易情况良好

1956-12-07
第5版()
专栏：

　  南苏贸易情况良好
    据新华社讯　审查今年南斯拉夫和苏联间贸易的结果的南苏委员会12月4日在贝尔格莱德签订了一项议定书。委员会对两国间总的贸易情况表示满意。
