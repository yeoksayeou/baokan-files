Title: Comrade Mao Zedong's Annotation on the Article "Our Cooperative Must Raise 20,000 Pigs"
Author:
Date: 1956-12-23
Page: 1

Comrades responsible for each province, city, and district, please note: If you agree, please print and distribute this article to all agricultural cooperatives for reference and emulation. You should know that Yanggu County is the hometown of the tiger-fighting hero Wu Song, but this area does not have a habit of raising pigs. This cooperative changed this habit and started raising pigs. It failed in the first year, succeeded in the second year, developed in the third year, and greatly developed in the fourth year, with an average of about two pigs per person, totaling 20,000 pigs. This cooperative can do this, so why can't other cooperatives do it?

Mao Zedong
November 9, 1956

### 毛泽东同志对“我们一个社要养猪两万头”一文的批语

1956-12-23
第1版()
专栏：

    毛泽东同志对“我们一个社要养猪两万头”一文的批语
    请各省市区负责同志注意：如果你们同意的话，就把这篇文章印发一切农业合作社，以供参考，并且仿照办理。要知道，阳谷县是打虎英雄武松的故乡，可是这一带没有喂猪的习惯。这个合作社改变了这种习惯，开始喂猪。第一年失败，第二年成功，第三年发展，第四年大发展，平均每人约有猪二头，共计二万头。这个合作社可以这样做，为什么别的合作社不可以这样做呢？
                          毛泽东
                                      1956年11月9日
