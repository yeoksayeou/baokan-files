Title: Jilin Fertilizer Plant Under Construction (Picture)
Author: Xu Zhicheng
Date: 1956-12-26
Page: 2

The first phase of the large-scale Jilin Fertilizer Plant is nearing completion, and some workshops are already undergoing trial operation. All employees are striving to put it into production in the first half of next year, nine months ahead of schedule. The picture above shows the exterior of the fertilizer plant during winter construction. Xinhua News Agency reporter Xu Zhicheng.

The completed 22,000 cubic meter large gas storage tank with air pressure.

Various process pipelines, stretching 180 kilometers, are under construction.

### 吉林肥料厂在建设中（图片）
胥志成
1956-12-26
第2版()
专栏：

    吉林肥料厂在建设中
    规模很大的吉林肥料厂第一期工程即将竣工，部分车间已进行试运转。全厂职工争取提前九个月在明年上半年投入生产。上图是肥料厂冬季施工外景。　新华社记者　胥志成摄
    已经建成的二万二千立方公尺气压的大型气体蓄藏柜。
    长达一百八十公里的各种工艺管道正在施工。
