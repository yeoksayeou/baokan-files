Title: Chen Yun, Acting Premier, Receives Yugoslav Economic Delegation
Author:
Date: 1956-12-21
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, 20th December — Acting Premier of the State Council, Chen Yun, received the Yugoslav Economic Delegation, headed by Kiro Gligorov, and all members of the delegation this morning.

    Present at the reception was the Yugoslav Ambassador to China, Popović.

    Also present at the reception was Kong Yuan, Vice Minister of Foreign Trade.

### 陈云代总理接见南经济代表团

1956-12-21
第1版()
专栏：

    陈云代总理接见南经济代表团
    新华社20日讯　国务院代总理陈云今天上午接见了南斯拉夫经济代表团团长基罗·格里戈罗夫和代表团全体人员。
    接见时在座的有南斯拉夫驻中国大使波波维奇。
    接见时，对外贸易部副部长孔原也在座。
