Title: Nehru Departs for Visit to the United States
Author:
Date: 1956-12-15
Page: 6
Xinhua News Agency, New Delhi, December 14th: Indian Prime Minister Nehru left here by plane this morning, via London, for Washington. He is visiting the United States at the invitation of US President Eisenhower. Accompanying him were his daughter, Mrs. Indira Gandhi, and Secretary-General of the Ministry of External Affairs, N. R. Pillai.

President Prasad of India, Vice President Radhakrishnan, cabinet ministers, foreign envoys, senior Indian government officials, members of parliament, and many well-known figures from society went to Palam Airport to see him off.

According to the official itinerary released by the Indian Information Bureau, Nehru will arrive in Washington on December 16. After staying in Washington for four days, Nehru will also visit New York and Ottawa, the capital of Canada, for two days each, and then return to London on December 24.

### 尼赫鲁启程赴美访问

1956-12-15
第6版()
专栏：

    尼赫鲁启程赴美访问
    新华社新德里14日电　印度总理尼赫鲁今天早晨乘飞机离开这里取道伦敦前往华盛顿。他是应美国总统艾森豪威尔的邀请前往美国访问的。随同他前往的有他的女儿英迪拉·甘地夫人和外交部秘书长纳·鲁·皮莱。
    到巴兰飞机场送行的有印度总统普拉沙德、副总统拉达克里希南、内阁阁员、外国使节、印度政府高级官员、议会议员和许多社会知名人士。
    根据印度新闻局公布的正式访问日程，尼赫鲁将在12月16日到达华盛顿。尼赫鲁在华盛顿逗留四天以后，还要到纽约和加拿大首都渥太华各访问两天，然后在12月24日回到伦敦。
