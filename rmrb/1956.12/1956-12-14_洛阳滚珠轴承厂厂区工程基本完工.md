Title: Basic Completion of the Construction Project at the Luoyang Ball Bearing Factory

Author: 
Date: 1956-12-14
Page: 2

Xinhua News Agency, Luoyang, December 13th. The construction project at the newly built, modern, large-scale Luoyang Ball Bearing Factory has been basically completed. Currently, the factory's production workshops are installing machinery and equipment, while the auxiliary workshops began production three months ago.

Once this factory is fully operational, its designed production capacity will allow it to produce enough ball bearings to equip more than 100,000 tractors annually.

This factory, equipped with first-class equipment and a high degree of mechanization and automation in its production process, will be capable of producing standard-grade ball bearings.

The factory's construction project commenced in September 1955, and now, the civil engineering construction has been basically completed. Based on the current actual conditions, this factory can start production in the first quarter of next year.

### 洛阳滚珠轴承厂厂区工程基本完工

1956-12-14
第2版()
专栏：

    洛阳滚珠轴承厂厂区工程基本完工
    新华社洛阳13日电　新建的一座现代化的大型滚珠轴承厂——洛阳滚珠轴承厂，厂区建筑工程已经基本完成。目前，这个厂的生产车间正在安装机器设备，辅助车间在三个月以前已经开始投入生产。
    这个厂全面投入生产以后，按照它的设计能力，一年生产出来的滚珠轴承，可以装配十多万辆拖拉机。
    这个拥有头等设备，生产过程高度机械化和自动化的工厂，它将能够生产标准级的滚珠轴承。
    这个厂的厂区建筑工程是在1955年9月份开始施工的，现在，土木建筑工程已经基本完成。根据目前的实际条件，这个厂在明年第一季度就能开始投入生产。
