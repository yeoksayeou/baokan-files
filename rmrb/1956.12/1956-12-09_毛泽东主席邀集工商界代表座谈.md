Title: Chairman Mao Zedong Invites Representatives from Industry and Commerce for a Discussion
Author:
Date: 1956-12-09
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, 8th: Chairman Mao Zedong invited the heads of delegations from various provinces and cities attending the Second National Congress of the All-China Federation of Industry and Commerce for a discussion on the evening of the 8th, regarding how to further leverage the positive role of industry and commerce in serving socialist construction.

Chairman Mao Zedong spoke at the meeting about the current domestic and international political situation and domestic economic issues.

Chen Shutong, Chairman of the All-China Federation of Industry and Commerce, Vice Chairmen Li Zhuchen, Xu Dixing, Sheng Pihua, Rong Yiren, Chen Jingyu, Huang Changshui, Hu Zi'ang, and Gong Tianmin, Chairman of the China Democratic National Construction Association Huang Yanpei, and Vice Chairmen Shi Fuliang, Hu Juewen, and Sun Qimeng were invited to attend the forum.

Also present at the forum were Vice Chairmen of the CPC Central Committee Liu Shaoqi, Chen Yun, Alternate Member of the Political Bureau of the CPC Central Committee Lu Dingyi, Bo Yibo, and heads of relevant departments of the Central Committee and various people's organizations.

At the meeting, Chen Shutong and representatives of industry and commerce from various provinces and cities delivered speeches.

### 毛泽东主席邀集工商界代表座谈

1956-12-09
第1版()
专栏：

　　毛泽东主席邀集工商界代表座谈
    新华社8日讯　毛泽东主席在8日晚上邀集了出席中华全国工商业联合会第二届会员代表大会的各省市代表团负责人，座谈如何进一步发挥工商界的积极作用，为社会主义建设服务问题。
    毛泽东主席在会上就目前国内外政治形势和国内经济问题讲了话。
    中华全国工商业联合会主任委员陈叔通，副主任委员李烛尘、许涤新、盛丕华、荣毅仁、陈经畬、黄长水、胡子昂、巩天民，中国民主建国会主任委员黄炎培，副主任委员施复亮、胡厥文、孙起孟等应邀出席了座谈会。
    出席座谈会的，还有中共中央副主席刘少奇、陈云、中共中央政治局候补委员陆定一、薄一波以及中央各有关部门、各人民团体的负责人。
    在会上，陈叔通和各省市工商界代表发了言。
