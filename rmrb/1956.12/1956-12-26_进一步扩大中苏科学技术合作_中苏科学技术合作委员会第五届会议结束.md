Title: Further Expansion of Sino-Soviet Scientific and Technological Cooperation - Fifth Session of the Sino-Soviet Scientific and Technological Cooperation Committee Concluded
Author:
Date: 1956-12-26
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, December 25th: The fifth session of the Sino-Soviet Scientific and Technological Cooperation Committee held in Moscow concluded on the 24th, and the two sides signed a protocol at the meeting.

At this session, the two sides exchanged information on the implementation of the resolutions of the previous session and considered the current issues of further exchanging production experience and scientific and technological achievements. The Chinese representatives made reports on technological innovations in China's ferrous metal sector and new varieties of Chinese crops.

The protocol signed by the two sides stipulates further expansion of scientific and technological cooperation between the two countries. A major sign of expanded cooperation is that departments and competent authorities with similar production characteristics can directly establish contact.

According to the protocol between the two sides, the Soviet side will transfer to China technical data on the construction of television centers and the development of television broadcasting, as well as technical data on the construction of water conservancy projects and building materials industrial enterprises, drawings for manufacturing various electrical machines, transformers, and batteries, and technical data on manufacturing automobile tires, rubber products, and insulating materials.

The Chinese side will transfer to the Soviet side technical data on zinc production, drawings for underwater concrete pouring equipment used in the construction of the Yangtze River Bridge, and other materials.

The two countries will mutually receive experts from each other to investigate specific issues.

The protocol also determined that several international scientific and technological conferences with the participation of Chinese and Soviet experts will be held in 1957.

### 进一步扩大中苏科学技术合作  中苏科学技术合作委员会第五届会议结束

1956-12-26
第1版()
专栏：

    进一步扩大中苏科学技术合作
    中苏科学技术合作委员会第五届会议结束
    新华社莫斯科25日电　在莫斯科举行的中苏科学技术合作委员会第五届会议已经在24日结束，会上双方已签订了议定书。
    在这届会议上双方交换了关于完成上届会议决议的情况，考虑了当前进一步交换生产经验和科学技术成就的问题。中国代表在会上作了关于中国黑色金属部门的技术革新、中国农作物新品种的报告。
    双方签订的议定书规定进一步扩大两国间的科学技术合作，扩大合作的主要标志是双方生产性质相同的部和主管机关可以直接建立联系。
    按照双方议定书的规定，苏联方面将转交给中国关于建设电视中心和发展电视事业的技术资料，以及关于建设水利工程、建筑材料工业企业的技术资料，制造各种电力机器、变压器、蓄电池的图纸和关于制造汽车轮胎、橡胶制品、绝缘器材方面的技术资料。
    中国方面将转交给苏联方面关于锌的生产的技术资料，关于在建设长江大桥时采用的在水中灌水泥的装备的图纸，以及其它。
    两国将互相接待对方专家对专门问题的考察。
    议定书中还确定在1957年举行若干中苏专家参加的国际科学技术会议。
