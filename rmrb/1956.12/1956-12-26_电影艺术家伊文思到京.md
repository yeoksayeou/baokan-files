Title: Film Artist Joris Ivens Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1956-12-26
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, December 25th. Mr. Joris Ivens, the Dutch director of the film "Song of the Rivers," a world-renowned film artist and winner of the International Peace Prize, arrived in Beijing this afternoon with his wife at the invitation of the Ministry of Culture for a visit to China.
	Situ Huimin, Deputy Director of the Film Bureau of the Ministry of Culture, and more than 30 artists, including directors and cinematographers from the August First Film Studio and the Central Newsreel and Documentary Film Studio, welcomed them at the airport.

### 电影艺术家伊文思到京

1956-12-26
第1版()
专栏：

    电影艺术家伊文思到京
    新华社25日讯　应文化部邀请来我国访问的国际和平奖金获得者、国际著名电影艺术家、影片“激流之歌”导演荷兰约里斯·伊文思先生及其夫人，今天下午到京。
    文化部电影事业管理局副局长司徒慧敏，八一电影制片厂和中央新闻纪录电影制片厂的导演、摄影等艺术工作者三十多人到机场欢迎。
