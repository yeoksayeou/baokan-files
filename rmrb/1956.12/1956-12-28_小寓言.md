Title: Small Fables
Author: Kǎléi'ěr·qiàpéikè
Date: 1956-12-28
Page: 8

Small Fables
Czechoslovakia　Karel Capek
The Wolf and the Goat
Let us sign an agreement on the basis of economy: I will not eat your grass, and you will voluntarily supply me with your meat.
The Rooster
The day has not yet dawned, because I have not given the signal.
The Fox
Creatures can be divided into three categories: enemies, competitors, and spoils.
The Eagle
What, cruel? My friend, the struggle for survival is always legitimate.
The Spider Who Wove a Net
Waiting is also a difficult task.
The Wolf
If no one hunted us wolves, there would be peace in the world.
The Flock of Sheep
If we do not defend ourselves, at least the wolves' stomachs would have been full long ago.
The Thorns
Is this garden desolate? I don't think so.
The Withered Branch
I have relied on my own strength.
The Maggot
Long live war!
The Smoke
You are all slaves to gravity. Only I and the clouds are free.
The Philanthropist
What misfortune! I just gave ten copper coins to a beggar.
Tyrants and Scholars
I will take action, and you just need to find the arguments for this action.
Colonial War
Now, we must protect those who are still alive like a loving father.
(Selected and translated by Huang Xianjun from the German "Sunday Weekly" 1956, No. 39) Postscript: The author, Karel Capek (1890-1938), was one of the great writers of Czechoslovakia in the 20th century. Before World War II, he had already become a heroic fighter in the anti-fascist struggle. He has many works, including the allegorical novel "War with the Newts", the plays "Mother" and "The White Plague", as well as political essays.

### 小寓言
卡雷尔·恰彼克
1956-12-28
第8版()
专栏：

    小寓言
    捷克斯洛伐克　卡雷尔·恰彼克
    狼和山羊
    让我们在节约的基础上签订一项协定：我不吃你的草，而你要自愿地把你的肉供给我。
    公鸡
    天还没有亮，因为我还没有发出信号来。
    狐狸
    生物可分作三类：仇敌、竞争者和卤获物。
    鹰
    什么，残酷吗？我的先生，谋取生存的斗争总是合法的。
    织好网的蜘蛛
    等待也是一桩困难的工作。
    狼
    如果没有人猎取我们狼的话，世界上就有了和平。
    羊群
    假若我们不自卫的话，至少狼的肚子早就饱了。
    荆棘
    这座花园难道很荒凉吗？我可不这么说。
    枯枝
    我已经依靠自己的力量。
    蛆虫
    战争万岁！
    烟
    你们大家都是万有引力的奴隶。只有我和云是自由的。
    博爱者
    是怎样的不幸呵？我刚施舍给叫化子十个铜元。
    暴君和学者
    我来采取行动，而你们只要去寻找这个行动的论据就行。
    殖民战争
    现在，我们要像慈父般地保护那些还活着的人们。
    （黄贤俊选译自德国“星期日周报1956年第39期）附记：作者卡雷尔·恰彼克KarelCapek（1890—1938）是捷克斯洛伐克二十世纪伟大的作家之一。在第二次世界大战以前，他已经成了反法西斯斗争中的一位英勇的战士。他的作品很多，有寓言小说“反火蛇战争”、剧本“母亲”和“白色的瘟疫”，以及政论等。
