Title: Another Victory in the Anti-Colonialist Struggle: French Troops Withdraw from Libya and Return Bases

Author:

Date: 1956-12-04
Page: 6

Another Victory in the Anti-Colonialist Struggle
	French Troops Withdraw from Libya and Return Bases

	Xinhua News Agency, 3rd dispatch from Tripoli, Libya: According to the Franco-Libyan treaty signed in July of this year, a ceremony was held in the Fezzan province on December 1st to hand over French military bases to the Libyan army. The Libyan flag has been raised over these military bases.

	With France returning the military bases to Libya, French troops have completely withdrawn from Libya. French troops have been stationed in Libya since Libya was liberated from Italian occupation during World War II.

### 反殖民主义斗争又一胜利　法军撤出利比亚并交还基地

1956-12-04
第6版()
专栏：

　  反殖民主义斗争又一胜利
　　法军撤出利比亚并交还基地
    新华社3日讯　利比亚的黎波里消息：根据今年7月签订的法国利比亚条约，12月1日在费赞省举行了法国军事基地移交给利比亚军队的仪式。这些军事基地上已经升起了利比亚的国旗。
    随着法国把军事基地交还给利比亚，法国部队已经全部撤出了利比亚。自从第二次世界大战中利比亚从意大利占领下解放以来，法国部队一直驻在利比亚。
