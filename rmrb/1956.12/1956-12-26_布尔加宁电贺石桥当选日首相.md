Title: Bulganin Sends Congratulations to Ishibashi on Election as Japanese Prime Minister
Author:
Date: 1956-12-26
Page: 6

    Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, December 25th: According to TASS, Soviet Council of Ministers Chairman Bulganin yesterday sent a telegram to Tanzan Ishibashi, congratulating him on his election as Japanese Prime Minister.
    In the telegram, Bulganin expressed his conviction that the normal diplomatic relations already established between the Soviet Union and Japan will be consolidated and developed in a mutually promoting manner, in the spirit of friendship and sincere trust between the two peoples.

### 布尔加宁电贺石桥当选日首相

1956-12-26
第6版()
专栏：

    布尔加宁电贺石桥当选日首相
    新华社25日讯　据塔斯社莫斯科讯：苏联部长会议主席布尔加宁昨天打电报给石桥湛山，祝贺他当选为日本首相。
    布尔加宁在电报中表示确信：业已建立起来的苏联和日本之间的正常外交关系，将会本着两国人民友好和真诚信任的精神在互相促进下得到巩固和发展。
