Title: Preparatory Committee of the Guangzhou Branch of the Jiusan Society Established
Author:
Date: 1956-12-01
Page: 4

    Preparatory Committee of the Guangzhou Branch of the Jiusan Society Established
    Xinhua News Agency, Guangzhou, 27th December. The preparatory committee of the Guangzhou branch of the Jiusan Society was established on the 25th. Xu Xiangong is the chairman, and Peng Guangqin and Huang Youmou are the vice-chairmen.

### 九三学社广州分社筹委会成立

1956-12-01
第4版()
专栏：

    九三学社广州分社筹委会成立
    新华社广州27日电　九三学社广州分社筹备委员会在25日成立。主任委员是徐贤恭，副主任委员是彭光钦、黄友谋。
