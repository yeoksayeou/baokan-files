Title: Chinese Ambassador Presents Credentials to Syrian President
Author:
Date: 1956-12-22
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, Damascus, 21st: Chen Zhifang, the Ambassador Extraordinary and Plenipotentiary of the People's Republic of China to Syria, accompanied by all diplomatic personnel from the embassy and the Chief of Protocol of the Syrian Ministry of Foreign Affairs, presented his credentials to Shukri al-Quwatli, the President of the Syrian Republic, at the Presidential Palace at 11:00 AM on the 19th.

    On the Syrian side, those present included Foreign Minister Bitar, the Secretary-General of the Presidential Palace, and the Master of Ceremonies of the Presidential Palace.

    Ambassador Chen Zhifang delivered a eulogy to President Quwatli, expressing on behalf of the Chinese government and people complete sympathy and support for the Syrian people's struggle to safeguard national independence and oppose colonialism, and wished Syria and its people prosperity and happiness.

    In his reply, the President welcomed the Ambassador's arrival and expressed his delight at the profound friendship between China and Syria.

    After the presentation of credentials, the President and the Ambassador had a cordial and friendly conversation. Ambassador Chen Zhifang also presented President Quwatli with a gift from Chairman Mao Zedong.

### 我国大使向叙利亚总统递国书

1956-12-22
第1版()
专栏：

    我国大使向叙利亚总统递国书
    新华社21日讯　大马士革消息：中华人民共和国驻叙利亚特命全权大使陈志方19日上午十一时偕大使馆全体外交人员由叙利亚外交部礼宾司长陪同到总统府，向叙利亚共和国总统舒克里·库阿特利递交国书。
    叙利亚方面在场的有外交部长比塔尔、总统府秘书长和总统府典礼长。
    陈志方大使向库阿特利总统致颂词，代表中国政府和人民对叙利亚人民维护民族独立、反对殖民主义的斗争表示完全同情和支持，并且祝叙利亚国家和人民繁荣幸福。
    总统在答词中表示欢迎大使的来到，并且对叙中两国深厚的友谊表示庆幸。
    递交国书后，总统和大使进行了亲切友好的谈话。陈志方大使还转交了毛泽东主席送给库阿特利总统的礼物。
