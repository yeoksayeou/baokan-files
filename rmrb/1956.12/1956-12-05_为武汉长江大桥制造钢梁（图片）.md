Title: Manufacturing Steel Beams for the Wuhan Yangtze River Bridge (Picture)
Author: Yuan Hao
Date: 1956-12-05
Page: 2

Manufacturing Steel Beams for the Wuhan Yangtze River Bridge

The workers of the Shanhaiguan Bridge Factory, who completed the first Five-Year Plan one year and three months ahead of schedule, are striving to complete the manufacturing of steel beams for the Wuhan Yangtze River Bridge by the end of this year. This is the second span of steel beams for the Wuhan Yangtze River Bridge that the workers are test-assembling.

　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　Photo by Yuan Hao (Xinhua News Agency draft)

### 为武汉长江大桥制造钢梁（图片）
袁浩
1956-12-05
第2版()
专栏：

　　为武汉长江大桥制造钢梁
　　提前一年零三个月完成了第一个五年计划的山海关桥梁工厂的工人，正在争取在今年年底全部完成武汉长江大桥钢梁的制造工作。这是工人们在试装武汉长江大桥钢梁的第二联。
　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　袁浩摄　（新华社稿）
