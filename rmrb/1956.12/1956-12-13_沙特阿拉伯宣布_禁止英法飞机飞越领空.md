Title: Saudi Arabia Announces Ban on British and French Aircraft from Overflying its Airspace
Author:
Date: 1956-12-13
Page: 5

    Saudi Arabia Announces
    Ban on British and French Aircraft from Overflying its Airspace
    Xinhua News Agency, December 12th, Cairo dispatch: According to a broadcast from Cairo Radio on the 11th, Saudi Arabia has banned British and French aircraft from flying over its airspace.

### 沙特阿拉伯宣布  禁止英法飞机飞越领空

1956-12-13
第5版()
专栏：

    沙特阿拉伯宣布
    禁止英法飞机飞越领空
    新华社12日讯　开罗消息：据开罗电台11日广播，沙特阿拉伯已经禁止英国和法国的飞机飞越它的领空。
