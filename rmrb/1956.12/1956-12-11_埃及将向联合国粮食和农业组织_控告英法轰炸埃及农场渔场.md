Title: Egypt to Sue the UN Food and Agriculture Organization Against British and French Bombing of Egyptian Farms and Fishing Grounds
Author:
Date: 1956-12-11
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, December 10th, TASS, Cairo, December 9th: "Al-Ahram" reported that Egypt is preparing to raise the issue of agricultural and fisheries losses caused by the British and French aggression against Egypt to the United Nations Food and Agriculture Organization. According to the newspaper, in its report to the above-mentioned organization, Egypt will list facts proving that British and French aircraft barbarically bombed Egyptian farms and fishing grounds.

### 埃及将向联合国粮食和农业组织  控告英法轰炸埃及农场渔场

1956-12-11
第6版()
专栏：

    埃及将向联合国粮食和农业组织
    控告英法轰炸埃及农场渔场
    新华社10日讯　塔斯社开罗9日讯：“金字塔报”报道，埃及准备向联合国粮食和农业组织提出英法侵略给埃及造成农业和渔业上的损失的问题。据报纸报道，埃及在向上述组织提出的报告中将列举事实，证明英法飞机野蛮地轰炸埃及的农场和捕鱼场所。
