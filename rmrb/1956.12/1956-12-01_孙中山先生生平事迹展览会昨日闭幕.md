Title: Exhibition of Sun Yat-sen's Life and Deeds Closes Yesterday
Author:
Date: 1956-12-01
Page: 4

　　Exhibition of Sun Yat-sen's Life and Deeds Closes Yesterday
    Xinhua News Agency, 30th. The "Exhibition of Sun Yat-sen's Life and Deeds" closed today. Since its opening on November 12th, a total of 101,300 people have visited the exhibition. Among them were workers, students, cadres, and visitors from Shenyang, Hunan, Tianjin and other places.
    Representatives from Burma, North Korea, Mongolia, and other countries who were invited to China to attend the 90th anniversary of Sun Yat-sen's birth also visited the exhibition. More than 200 foreign guests visited the exhibition.
    Many people wrote down their thoughts in the guestbook after visiting. Some even contributed materials on Sun Yat-sen's life and deeds that were not included in the exhibition. Xu Wenyu sent a copy of the publication "National Destiny," which contained Sun Yat-sen's speeches and photos of his revolutionary activities. Sun Chaopeng also sent a photo of Sun Yat-sen with his family. After visiting the exhibition, an old worker, Wu Jintang, presented a wood carving of Sun Yat-sen that he had made to the exhibition.

### 孙中山先生生平事迹展览会昨日闭幕

1956-12-01
第4版()
专栏：

　　孙中山先生生平事迹展览会昨日闭幕
    新华社30日讯　“孙中山先生生平事迹展览会”已经在今天闭幕。这个展览会从11月12日开幕以来，共有十万一千三百多人前往参观。其中有工人、学生、干部等，还有来自沈阳、湖南、天津等地的观众。
    应邀来我国参加孙中山先生诞辰九十周年纪念大会的缅甸、朝鲜、蒙古等国的代表也参观了展览会。参观展览会的外宾有二百多人。
    许多人参观后在留言簿上写下了感想。有的还献出了展览会上所没有的孙中山先生生平事迹资料。徐文玉送的“国运”刊物一本，里面有孙中山先生的讲演稿和先生从事革命活动的照片。孙超朋也送了孙中山先生和家人合照的照片。一位老工人吴锦棠在参观展览会后，把自己刻的孙中山先生木刻像送给了展览会。
