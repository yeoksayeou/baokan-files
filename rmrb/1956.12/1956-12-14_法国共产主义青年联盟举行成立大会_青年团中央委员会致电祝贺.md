Title: French Communist Youth League Holds Founding Congress; Central Committee of the Communist Youth League Sends Congratulatory Telegram
Author: 
Date: 1956-12-14
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, December 13th. The founding congress of the French Communist Youth League will be held in Paris on the 14th. The Central Committee of the Chinese New Democratic Youth League sent a telegram to express its congratulations.

The telegram stated that the Central Committee of the Chinese New Democratic Youth League, on behalf of 120 million Chinese youth, extends warm and fraternal congratulations to the founding congress of the French Communist Youth League, and through you, sends sincere respect to French youth friends who love peace, democracy, and progressive causes.

The telegram stated that French youth are enthusiastically responding to the call of the French Communist Party and establishing the French Communist Youth League. We firmly believe that under the leadership of the French Communist Party, the French Communist Youth League and other communist youth organizations in France will further expand and consolidate their ties with the broad masses of French youth, thereby achieving greater success in the struggle to safeguard peace, defend and develop democracy and freedom, realize their demands and rights, and promote the realization of socialism in France.

The telegram stated that at a time when imperialist reactionary forces are attempting to expand their heinous anti-communist and anti-Soviet activities, the close unity of all peace-loving and just youth in the world is of paramount importance. We, the Chinese youth, will work with you to further strengthen our internationalist solidarity.

### 法国共产主义青年联盟举行成立大会  青年团中央委员会致电祝贺

1956-12-14
第1版()
专栏：

    法国共产主义青年联盟举行成立大会
    青年团中央委员会致电祝贺
    新华社13日讯　法国共产主义青年联盟成立大会将于14日在巴黎举行。中国新民主主义青年团中央委员会致电表示祝贺。
    电报说，中国新民主主义青年团中央委员会谨代表一亿二千万中国青年向法国共产主义青年联盟成立大会致以热烈的兄弟般的祝贺，并通过你们向爱好和平民主和进步事业的法国青年朋友致以真挚的敬意。
    电报说，法国青年热烈地响应法国共产党的号召，成立法国共产主义青年联盟。我们深信，在法国共产党领导下，法国共产主义青年联盟和法国其他共产主义的青年组织，必将更加扩大和巩固同广大法国青年群众的联系，从而在为维护和平，为保卫和发展民主自由，为实现自己的要求和权利，为促使社会主义在法国实现的斗争中，将取得更大的成就。
    电报说，在帝国主义反动势力企图扩大反共反苏的罪恶活动的时候，全世界一切爱好和平和正义的青年的紧密团结是有着头等重要的意义的。我们中国青年将和你们一起为更加加强我们之间的国际主义的团结而努力。
