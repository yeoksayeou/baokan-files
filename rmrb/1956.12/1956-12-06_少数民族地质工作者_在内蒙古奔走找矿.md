Title: Minority Geologists Prospect for Minerals in Inner Mongolia
Author:
Date: 1956-12-06
Page: 2

Xinhua News Agency, Hohhot, December 4th: More than fifty geologists of Mongolian, Daur, Hui, Manchu, and other ethnic minorities are constantly traversing the vast fields of Inner Mongolia, searching for various mineral deposits alongside many Han geologists.

These minority geologists are all working in various exploration teams under the Geological Bureau of the Inner Mongolia Autonomous Region's Department of Industry. Mongolian staff in a geological survey team working on the Ulanqab League grasslands established a close friendship with local herders and, based on leads from mineral reports provided by the Mongolian herders, discovered a promising coalfield in the United Middle and Rear Banner. Mongolian geological team leader Ebolartu has led a geological team for the past year, traversing the mountains, deserts, and grasslands of the Ikh Chao League and Pingdiquan Administrative District, and has discovered more than ten mineral deposits, including coal, apatite, and peat.

While working in the field, these minority geologists also persisted in daily cultural studies, and their educational levels have greatly improved compared to when they first started working.

### 少数民族地质工作者  在内蒙古奔走找矿

1956-12-06
第2版()
专栏：

　  少数民族地质工作者
    在内蒙古奔走找矿
    据新华社呼和浩特4日电　五十多名蒙古、达呼尔、回、满等民族的地质工作者，长年奔走在内蒙古辽阔的原野上，和许多汉族地质工作者一道寻找各种矿藏。
    这些少数民族的地质工作者，都在内蒙古自治区工业厅地质局所属的各个勘探队里工作。在乌兰察布盟草原上进行工作的一个地质普查队中的蒙古族职工，由于和当地牧民建立了亲密的友谊，根据蒙古族牧民报矿的线索，在中后联合旗发现了一处很有希望的煤田。蒙古族的地质组长额博拉尔图，一年来带领着一个地质组，踏遍了伊克昭盟、平地泉行政区的丛山峻岭、沙漠和草原，已经发现了煤、磷灰石和泥炭等十多处矿藏。
    这些少数民族的地质工作者，在野外工作中，还坚持了每天的文化学习，文化水平比参加工作的时候都大大地提高了。
