Title: Kundulun Zhao in Baotou (Picture)
Author: Yuan Ruxun
Date: 1956-12-20
Page: 2

    Baotou's Kundulun Zhao
    Baotou's Kundulun Zhao originally had only a solitary lama temple. Due to the construction of the Baotou Iron and Steel Base, it will become a railway hub for the Bao (Baotou) - Bai (Baiyunebo) line, the Bao (Baotou) - Shi (Shiguaizi) line, and the ring road line.

　　　　　　　　Xinhua News Agency reporter Yuan Ruxun photographed.

### 包头的昆独仑召（图片）
袁汝逊
1956-12-20
第2版()
专栏：

    包头的昆独仑召
    包头昆独仑召原来只有一座孤零零的喇嘛庙，由于包头钢铁基地的建设，它将变成包（头）白（云鄂博）线、包（头）石（拐子）线和环城线等的铁路枢纽站。　　
　　　　　　　　新华社记者　袁汝逊摄
