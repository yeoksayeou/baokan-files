Title: Japanese Medical Delegation for Schistosomiasis Control Returns Home
Author:
Date: 1956-12-06
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, 5th. The Japanese medical delegation for schistosomiasis control left Beijing this morning to return home.

The delegation arrived in China at the end of September. The medical experts in the delegation, together with Chinese medical experts, conducted clinical experiments and research work on schistosomiasis control in the schistosomiasis-infected areas of Wuxi. After concluding their visit to Wuxi, they came to Beijing at the end of November and held academic report meetings and symposiums.

Guo Moruo, President of the Chinese Academy of Sciences, received the delegation on the evening of December 2nd and listened to the Japanese medical experts' opinions on schistosomiasis control.

Li Dequan, Honorary President of the Chinese Medical Association, hosted a farewell banquet for the delegation on the evening of the 4th.

### 日本防治血吸虫病医学代表团回国

1956-12-06
第1版()
专栏：

　　日本防治血吸虫病医学代表团回国
    新华社5日讯　日本防治血吸虫病医学代表团今天早晨离北京回国。
    代表团是9月底来到中国的。代表团中的医学家曾经在无锡血吸虫病感染地区同中国医学家共同进行了防治血吸虫病的临床实验和研究工作。他们结束了在无锡的访问以后，于11月底来到北京，并且举行了学术报告会和座谈会。
    中国科学院院长郭沫若在12月2日晚上接见了代表团，并且听了日本医学家关于防治血吸虫病的意见。
    中华医学会名誉会长李德全在4日晚上设宴为代表团饯行。
