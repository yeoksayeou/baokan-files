Title: Intrigues to Interfere in Hungary's Internal Affairs Emerge Endlessly; The United States Submits Another Inflammatory Proposal to the UN General Assembly
Author:
Date: 1956-12-10
Page: 5

　　Xinhua News Agency, 9th dispatch from New York: The United States and twelve other countries submitted a new proposal to the United Nations General Assembly on the 8th to interfere in Hungary's internal affairs, and requested the United Nations General Assembly to discuss the proposal on December 10th.

　　The proposal requests the General Assembly to declare that the Soviet Union "crushed Hungary's political independence," condemns the Soviet Union for allegedly "violating the United Nations Charter," and demands that the Soviet Union make arrangements to withdraw its troops from Hungary under so-called "United Nations observation." In addition to the United States, the countries signing the proposal include Argentina, Australia, Belgium, Denmark, El Salvador, Ireland, Italy, the Netherlands, Norway, Pakistan, Sweden, and Thailand.

　　This proposal was put forward after representatives of the United States and the above-mentioned twelve countries, plus Britain, France, Cuba, and Peru, held a secret meeting lasting three hours that day. Representatives participating in this seventeen-nation secret meeting said that the U.S. representative even advocated "canceling or temporarily suspending" the credentials of the Hungarian delegation if the Hungarian government firmly refused to accept United Nations observers.

### 干涉匈牙利内政的阴谋层出不穷　美国又向联大提出煽动性提案

1956-12-10
第5版()
专栏：

    干涉匈牙利内政的阴谋层出不穷
　　美国又向联大提出煽动性提案
    新华社9日讯　纽约消息：美国和另外十二个国家8日向联合国大会提出一项干涉匈牙利内政的新提案，并且要求联合国大会在12月10日讨论这项提案。
    提案要求大会宣布苏联“粉碎了匈牙利的政治独立”，谴责苏联所谓“违反联合国宪章”，并且要求苏联作好安排，在所谓“联合国的观察下”把它的军队撤出匈牙利。签署这项提案的除美国以外，还有阿根廷、澳大利亚、比利时、丹麦、萨尔瓦多、爱尔兰、意大利、荷兰、挪威、巴基斯坦、瑞典和泰国。
    这项提案是美国同上述十二个国家外加英国、法国、古巴、秘鲁等四国代表在当天举行了长达三小时的秘密会议之后提出来的。参加这个十七国秘密会议的代表说，美国代表甚至主张“取消或者暂时停止”匈牙利代表团的资格，如果匈牙利政府坚决不接纳联合国观察员的话。
