Title: Iraqi Troops Withdraw from Jordan
Author:
Date: 1956-12-10
Page: 6

　　Iraqi Troops Withdraw from Jordan
    Xinhua News Agency, Baghdad, December 9th: The Iraqi government announced on the evening of December 8th that Iraq has withdrawn its troops from Jordan.
    Iraqi troops entered Jordan under the pretext of assisting Jordan after Britain, France and Israel launched their war of aggression against Egypt.
    A communiqué issued by the Iraqi government said that at the request of the Jordanian government, Iraq decided to withdraw its troops. The communiqué also said that these withdrawn troops are now stationed within Iraq, but in a location close to the Jordanian border.

### 伊拉克军撤出约旦

1956-12-10
第6版()
专栏：

　　伊拉克军撤出约旦
    新华社9日讯　巴格达消息：伊拉克政府8日晚间宣布，伊拉克已经从约旦撤退了它的军队。
    伊拉克军队是在英法和以色列发动侵略埃及战争后以援助约旦为名义进入约旦的。
    伊拉克政府发表的一项公报说，在约旦政府的要求下，伊拉克决定把军队撤出。公报又说，这些撤出的军队现在驻在伊拉克境内，但是在靠近约旦边境的地方。
