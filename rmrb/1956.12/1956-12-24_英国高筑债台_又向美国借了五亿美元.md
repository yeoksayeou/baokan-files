Title: Britain Builds a Towering Debt, Borrows Another $500 Million from the United States
Author:
Date: 1956-12-24
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, December 22nd dispatch from Washington: The United States Export-Import Bank announced on the 21st that it would grant Britain a $500 million loan in order to enable it to obtain "the dollars needed to pay for American goods and services, including the dollars needed to purchase oil."

Prior to this, Britain had already borrowed $1.3 billion from the International Monetary Fund. Adding this loan means that Britain has increased its debt by $1.8 billion in less than half a month.

### 英国高筑债台  又向美国借了五亿美元

1956-12-24
第5版()
专栏：

    英国高筑债台
    又向美国借了五亿美元
    据新华社22日讯　华盛顿消息：美国进出口银行21日宣布给英国五亿美元贷款，以便使它获得“支付美国商品和劳务所需要的美元，其中包括购买石油所需要的美元”。
    在这以前，英国已经向国际货币基金借了十三亿美元的贷款，加上这笔贷款，使得英国在不到半个月的时间内，又增加了十八亿美元的债务。
