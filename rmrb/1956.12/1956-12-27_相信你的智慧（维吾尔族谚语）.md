Believe in Your Wisdom
(Uyghur Proverb)

Rather than trusting your money,
　　You should trust your wisdom.
Rather than searching for money,
　　You should search for wisdom.
A fool does not know the worth of the wise,
　　A healthy person does not know the suffering of the sick.
To boast of oneself is to be a fool,
　　To boast of one's wife is to be doubly foolish.
　　　　　　Zhao Shijie, selected and translated

### 相信你的智慧（维吾尔族谚语）

1956-12-27
第8版()
专栏：

    相信你的智慧
    （维吾尔族谚语）
    与其相信你的金钱，
    倒不如相信你的智慧。
    你与其寻找金钱，
    倒不如寻找智慧。
    糊涂虫哪儿知道贤明者的宝贵，
    健康人哪儿知道病患者的痛苦。
    夸耀自己是一个糊涂虫，
    夸耀妻子是两个糊涂虫。
            赵世杰选译
