Title: Chinese National People's Congress Delegation and Beijing Municipal People's Committee Delegation Leave Prague for Bucharest Visit
Author:
Date: 1956-12-22
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, Prague, December 21st: After an eighteen-day visit to Czechoslovakia, the delegation of the National People's Congress of China and the delegation of the Beijing Municipal People's Committee left Prague by train on the afternoon of the 20th for a visit to Bucharest.

    Chairman Fierlinger of the Czechoslovak National Assembly said in his farewell speech at the station: "We bid farewell to you as if we were bidding farewell to our own relatives. Please tell the Chinese people that we thank them for sending such an important delegation to visit our country."

    Peng Zhen said in his reply that he was sincerely grateful for the warm and comradely reception the delegation had received in Czechoslovakia, an advanced socialist industrial country. He said that the delegation had learned a lot of advanced experience in Czechoslovakia.

### 我人民代表大会代表团和北京市人民委员会代表团  离布拉格赴布加勒斯特访问

1956-12-22
第1版()
专栏：

    我人民代表大会代表团和北京市人民委员会代表团
    离布拉格赴布加勒斯特访问
    新华社布拉格21日电　中国全国人民代表大会代表团和北京市人民委员会代表团在捷克斯洛伐克访问了十八天以后，20日下午乘火车离开布拉格前往布加勒斯特访问。
    捷克斯洛伐克国民议会主席费林格在车站上致欢送词说，我们同你们告别就像同亲人告别一样。请告诉中国人民，我们感谢他们派你们这样一个重要的代表团来访问我国。
    彭真在答词中对于代表团在捷克斯洛伐克所受到的亲切的、同志式的荣誉的接待表示衷心感谢。他说，代表团在捷克斯洛伐克这样一个先进的社会主义工业国中学到了很多先进经验。
