Title: Reasonable Plan to be Developed for Embroidery Workers' Wages
Author:
Date: 1956-12-17
Page: 2
Response to Criticism

Reasonable Plan to be Developed for Embroidery Workers' Wages
    Letter from the Suzhou Branch of the Jiangsu Provincial Silk Company: In response to the letter "Don't Let Embroidery Girls Scrape White Mud" published in the People's Daily on November 16th, regarding the wage issue of embroidery workers, our company is organizing a team to investigate the situation. After the investigation, a reasonable wage plan will be formulated.

### 刺绣女工工资将制定合理方案

1956-12-17
第2版()
专栏：对批评的反应

    刺绣女工工资将制定合理方案
    江苏省丝绸公司苏州分公司来信：11月16日人民日报登载的“莫让刺绣姑娘刮白泥”一信中，提到有关刺绣女工的工资问题，我公司正在组织力量进行了解，待了解后将制定合理的工资方案。
