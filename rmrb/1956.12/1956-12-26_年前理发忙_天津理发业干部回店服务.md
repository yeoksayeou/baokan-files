Title: Busy Hairdressing Before the New Year: Tianjin Hairdressing Industry Cadres Return to Shops to Serve

Author:

Date: 1956-12-26
Page: 3

Xinhua News Agency, Tianjin -- In the days leading up to the New Year, barbershops are especially busy. Forty-two barbers in Tianjin's Welfare Company, who have many years of hairdressing experience, have recently returned to the shops one after another to directly serve customers. These barbers were all transferred to leadership organizations at various levels at the beginning of this year during the high tide of the public-private partnership of the entire industry, serving as managers, deputy managers, section chiefs and other administrative management positions.

### 年前理发忙  天津理发业干部回店服务

1956-12-26
第3版()
专栏：

    年前理发忙
    天津理发业干部回店服务
    据新华社天津电　新年到来的前几天，正是理发店生意更忙的时候。天津市福利公司具有多年理发经验的四十二位理发师，最近又陆续回到店里重新直接为顾客服务。这些理发师都是在今年年初全行业公私合营高潮时期被调到各级领导机构担任经理、副经理和股长等行政管理职务的。
