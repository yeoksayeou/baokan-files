Title: Egyptian Foreign Minister Sends Letter to Hammarskjöld Protesting British Aircraft Attacking Egyptian Positions
Author: 
Date: 1956-12-02
Page: 6
Xinhua News Agency, New York, December 1st: Egyptian Foreign Minister Fawzi sent a written protest to United Nations Secretary-General Hammarskjöld on November 30 regarding the British aircraft's violation of the ceasefire and attack on Egyptian positions on the 29th. He said, "I hardly need to emphasize the importance of this issue, the seriousness of this violation of the ceasefire, the circumstances of British fighter planes flying over the ceasefire line, and the severity of the consequences that may arise from this violation of the ceasefire."
    He requested that this letter be circulated among all United Nations member states.

### 埃外长致函哈马舍尔德　抗议英机攻击埃及阵地

1956-12-02
第6版()
专栏：

    埃外长致函哈马舍尔德
　　抗议英机攻击埃及阵地
    新华社1日讯　纽约消息：埃及外交部长法齐11月30日就英国飞机29日破坏停火、攻击埃及阵地事向联合国秘书长哈马舍尔德提出书面抗议。他说：“我几乎不必着重指出这个问题的重要性，这种违反停火的行为的严重性和英国战斗机飞越停火线的情况以及由于这种违反停火行为可能产生的后果的严重性。”
    他要求把这封信在所有的联合国会员国中传阅。
