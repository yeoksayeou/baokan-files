Title: Central Committee Leaders of the Chinese Communist Party Receive Representatives to the Congress of the All-China Federation of Industry and Commerce and Receive Representatives to Three Advanced Workers Conferences
Author:
Date: 1956-12-19
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, 18th Dispatch: Mao Zedong, Chairman of the Central Committee of the Chinese Communist Party, and Vice-Chairmen Liu Shaoqi, Zhu De, and Chen Yun, received all the representatives attending the Second Congress of the All-China Federation of Industry and Commerce this afternoon.

Xinhua News Agency, 18th Dispatch: Mao Zedong, Chairman of the Central Committee of the Chinese Communist Party, and Vice-Chairmen Liu Shaoqi and Zhu De, received representatives attending the First National Advanced Workers Representative Conference of the Customs, representatives attending the Advanced Workers Conference of Units Directly Under the General Logistics Department and the General Finance Department of the Chinese People's Liberation Army, and representatives attending the First Advanced Teaching Workers Conference of the Political Academy of the Chinese People's Liberation Army, one after another this afternoon.

### 中共中央领导人  接见工商联会员代表大会代表  并接见三个先进工作者会议的代表

1956-12-19
第1版()
专栏：

    中共中央领导人
    接见工商联会员代表大会代表
    并接见三个先进工作者会议的代表
    新华社18日讯　中国共产党中央委员会主席毛泽东、副主席刘少奇、朱德、陈云，在今天下午接见了出席中华全国工商业联合会第二届会员代表大会的全体代表。
    新华社18日讯　中国共产党中央委员会主席毛泽东、副主席刘少奇、朱德，在今天下午先后接见了出席全国海关第一次先进工作者代表会议的代表、出席中国人民解放军总后勤部和总财务部直属单位先进工作者会议的代表和出席中国人民解放军政治学院第一届先进教学工作者会议的代表。
