Title: NPC Standing Committee Holds Meeting, Approves Regulations on Economic Construction Bonds for Next Year
Author:
Date: 1956-12-30
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, December 29th — The 52nd meeting of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress was held on the afternoon of the 29th.

    The meeting heard a report by Li Xiannian, Vice Premier of the State Council and Minister of Finance, on the adjustment and execution of the 1956 state budget. The meeting approved the adjustments made by the State Council to the 1956 state budget.

    After hearing explanations from Li Xiannian, Vice Premier of the State Council and Minister of Finance, the meeting approved the Regulations on National Economic Construction Bonds for 1957.

    The meeting also approved a list of appointments and removals.

### 全国人民代表大会常委会举行会议  通过明年经济建设公债条例

1956-12-30
第1版()
专栏：

    全国人民代表大会常委会举行会议
    通过明年经济建设公债条例
    新华社29日讯　全国人民代表大会常务委员会第五十二次会议在29日下午举行。
    会议听取了国务院副总理兼财政部长李先念关于1956年国家预算调整和预算执行情况的报告。会议同意国务院对1956年国家预算所作的调整。
    会议在听了国务院副总理兼财政部长李先念的说明后，通过了1957年国家经济建设公债条例。
    会议还通过了决定任免名单。
