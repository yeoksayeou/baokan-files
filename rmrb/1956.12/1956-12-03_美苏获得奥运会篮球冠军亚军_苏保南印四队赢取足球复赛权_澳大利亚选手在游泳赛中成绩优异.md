Title: USA and USSR Win Olympic Basketball Gold and Silver Medals; USSR, Bulgaria, South Africa, and India Qualify for Football Quarterfinals; Australian Athletes Excel in Swimming Events
Author:
Date: 1956-12-03
Page: 6

　　Xinhua News Agency, Melbourne, December 2nd: The Olympic basketball final took place last night. The USA team defeated the Soviet team by a score of 89 to 55, winning the gold medal for this Olympiad. The Soviet team won the silver medal.

　　The bronze medal for this Olympiad was won by Uruguay, which also won third place in the previous Olympiad. The French team came in fourth (eighth in the previous Olympiad). Bulgaria, which came in seventh in the previous Olympiad, defeated Brazil by a score of 64 to 52 and took fifth place. Brazil took sixth place. The Philippines took seventh place. Chile took eighth place.

　　Xinhua News Agency, Melbourne, December 2nd: The semi-quarterfinals of the Olympic football tournament have concluded. In the semi-quarterfinals, Yugoslavia defeated the United States by a score of 9 to 1. Bulgaria defeated Great Britain by a score of 6 to 1, India defeated Australia by a score of 4 to 2, and the Soviet team defeated Indonesia by a score of 4 to 0. The four winning teams have qualified for the quarterfinals.

　　Xinhua News Agency, Melbourne, December 2nd: Five finals have already taken place in the Olympic swimming events.

　　The top three places in the women's 100-meter freestyle were all won by Australian athletes. First place went to Fraser, and second place went to world record holder Crapp. Their times were 62 seconds and 62.3 seconds, respectively, both breaking the world record of 62.4 seconds.

　　The top three places in the men's 100-meter freestyle were also won by Australian athletes. Their times all broke the Olympic record of 57.1 seconds.

　　The first place in the women's 200-meter breaststroke was won by a 31-year-old German housewife, Happ, with a time of 2 minutes 53.1 seconds.

　　The men's 200-meter butterfly was a new event added to this year's swimming competition. The world record holder, Yossik of the United States, won the championship. His time in the final was 2 minutes 19.3 seconds.

### 美苏获得奥运会篮球冠军亚军  苏保南印四队赢取足球复赛权  澳大利亚选手在游泳赛中成绩优异

1956-12-03
第6版()
专栏：

　　美苏获得奥运会篮球冠军亚军
　  苏保南印四队赢取足球复赛权
    澳大利亚选手在游泳赛中成绩优异
    据新华社2日讯　墨尔本消息：奥林匹克运动会篮球赛昨天晚上进行决赛。美国队以八十九分比五十五分战胜苏联队，获得这一届的冠军。苏联队获得亚军。
    获得这一届第三名的是乌拉圭队，上一届它也是第三名。法国队获得第四名（上届第八名）。上届第七名的保加利亚队以六十四比五十二分战胜巴西队而获得了第五名。第六名是巴西队。第七名是菲律宾队。第八名是智利队。
    据新华社2日讯　墨尔本消息：奥林匹克运动会的足球赛的半复赛已经结束。在半复赛中南斯拉夫队以九比一击败美国队。保加利亚以六比一击败英国队，印度以四比二击败了澳大利亚队，苏联队以四比○击败了印度尼西亚队。四个胜利队取得了复赛权。
    据新华社2日讯　墨尔本消息：奥林匹克运动会的游泳赛已经进行了五项决赛。
    女子一百公尺自由泳的前三名都被澳大利亚运动员所取得。第一名是弗雷塞，第二名是世界纪录保持者克拉普，她们的成绩分别为六十二秒和六十二·三秒，都打破了六十二·四秒的世界纪录。
    男子一百公尺自由泳的前三名也是澳大利亚运动员。他们的成绩都打破了五七·一秒的奥林匹克纪录。
    获得女子二百公尺蛙泳第一名的是一位已经三十一岁的德国家庭妇女哈普，成绩是二分五三·一秒。
    男子二百公尺蝶泳是这一届游泳赛中新增加的项目。世界纪录创造者美国的约赛克获得冠军。他在决赛中的成绩是二分十九·三秒。
