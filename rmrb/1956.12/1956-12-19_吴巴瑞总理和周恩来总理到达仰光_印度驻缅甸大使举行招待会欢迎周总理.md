Title: Prime Minister U Ba Swe and Premier Zhou Enlai Arrive in Rangoon, Indian Ambassador to Burma Holds Reception to Welcome Premier Zhou

Author: 
Date: 1956-12-19
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Rangoon, 18th - Prime Minister U Ba Swe and Premier Zhou Enlai arrived in Rangoon at 5:30 PM on the 17th from Mangshi.

Deputy Prime Ministers of Burma, U Kyaw Nyein and U Ba Swe, Chinese Deputy Premier He Long, Kachin State Chief Minister of Burma U Zanhta Sin, Burmese Ambassador to China U Hla Maung, and Chinese Ambassador to Burma Yao Zhongming arrived at the same time.

Greeting the two Prime Ministers at the airport were high-ranking Burmese government officials, Indian Ambassador to Burma, R.K. Nehru, and officials from the Chinese Embassy in Burma.

Tens of thousands of Rangoon citizens stood along the road from the airport to the Presidential Palace to welcome the two Prime Ministers, who had successfully participated in the China-Burma border people's joint celebration.

Xinhua News Agency, Rangoon, 18th - The Indian Ambassador to Burma, R.K. Nehru, held a reception on the evening of the 17th to welcome Premier Zhou Enlai. The reception was attended by 500 guests, including prominent figures in Rangoon and diplomatic envoys from various countries to Burma.

The beautiful gardens of the Indian Embassy in Burma were decorated with colorful lights, and beautiful Indian music was played.

Premier Zhou Enlai happily met with the Speaker of the Burmese Chamber of Nationalities, Sao Hkun Hkio, Daw Khin Kyi (Aung San's wife), Karen State Affairs Minister Saw Luthu, and high-ranking officials from the Burmese government and armed forces. Premier Zhou Enlai told them that he enjoyed his visit to Upper Burma, and he was very touched by the warm welcome given to him by the people of Upper Burma.

When the Indian Ambassador introduced the leaders of the Indian community in Burma to Premier Zhou Enlai, Premier Zhou said to the Indian Ambassador that he was very grateful for the warm welcome given to him by the Indian community during his visit to Rangoon and Upper Burma.

Pakistani Ambassador Rehrman and his wife expressed their welcome to Premier Zhou to visit Pakistan and hoped that the Premier would have a pleasant time during his visit to their country. Premier Zhou happily accepted their kind intentions.

Premier Zhou happily met with the Egyptian Minister and his wife at the reception. The Egyptian Minister's wife said with emotion that the Egyptian people are very grateful for the help given by the Chinese people in Egypt's recent struggle against foreign aggression.

Two American guests attending the reception approached Premier Zhou and told him that they were very happy to see the Premier in Burma. They hoped to be able to visit China in the near future.

Wherever Premier Zhou Enlai went at the reception, he was surrounded by many people who greeted him and talked with him warmly and with smiles. The reception ended at 8:00 PM in a warm atmosphere.

On the evening of the 17th, Premier Zhou Enlai and Deputy Premier He Long also attended a banquet hosted by Burmese Chief of Staff General Ne Win.

### 吴巴瑞总理和周恩来总理到达仰光  印度驻缅甸大使举行招待会欢迎周总理

1956-12-19
第1版()
专栏：

    吴巴瑞总理和周恩来总理到达仰光
    印度驻缅甸大使举行招待会欢迎周总理
    新华社仰光18日电　吴巴瑞总理和周恩来总理在17日下午五时三十分从芒市到达仰光。
    缅甸副总理藻昆卓、吴觉迎，中国副总理贺龙，缅甸克钦邦邦长吴赞塔信，缅甸驻中国大使吴拉茂和中国驻缅甸大使姚仲明同时到达。
    到机场欢迎中缅两国总理的有缅甸政府高级官员，印度驻缅甸大使拉·梅罗特拉和中国驻缅甸大使馆官员。
    成千上万的仰光市民站在从机场到总统府的道路两旁，欢迎成功地参加了中缅两国边境人民联欢大会的两国总理。
    新华社仰光18日电　印度驻缅甸大使拉耳季·梅罗特拉在17日晚间举行招待会，欢迎周恩来总理。出席招待会的有仰光的著名人士和各国驻缅甸使节等五百位来宾。
    印度驻缅甸大使馆的美丽的花园里装饰着彩灯，并且播送着美妙的印度音乐。
    周恩来总理愉快地会见了缅甸民族院议长萧恢塔，昂山夫人，克伦邦事务部长苏拉吞以及缅甸政府和缅甸武装部队的高级官员。周恩来总理对他们说，他在上缅甸的访问很愉快，上缅甸人民给予他的热烈欢迎使他很受感动。
    在印度大使把在缅甸的印度侨民领袖们介绍给周恩来总理的时候，周总理对印度大使说，他非常感谢印度侨民在他访问仰光和上缅甸期间所给予他的热烈欢迎。
    巴基斯坦大使雷赫曼和他的夫人表示他们欢迎周总理到巴基斯坦去访问，并且希望总理在他们国家访问期间将能够过得很愉快。周总理愉快地接受了他们的好意。
    周总理在招待会上高兴地会见了埃及公使和他的夫人。埃及公使夫人感动地说，埃及人民非常感激中国人民在埃及最近反对外国侵略的斗争中所给予的帮助。
    出席招待会的两位美国客人走到周总理面前对总理说，他们能够在缅甸见到总理感到非常高兴。他们希望在不久的将来能够去访问中国。
    在招待会上，周恩来总理不论走到那里，都有许多人热情而微笑着把他围起来，向他致意和同他交谈。招待会在晚上八点钟在热烈的气氛中结束。
    17日晚，周恩来总理和贺龙副总理还应邀出席了缅甸国防军参谋总长奈温将军的宴会。
