Title: Grotewohl Emphasizes at the Council of Ministers of the German Democratic Republic: The German Democratic Republic Will Continue to Strengthen Brotherly Friendship with Czechoslovakia and Poland; Meeting Passes Draft Law on Shortening Working Hours
Author:
Date: 1956-12-09
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, Berlin, December 8th: According to a report in "Neues Deutschland," the Council of Ministers of the German Democratic Republic held a meeting on the 6th. Prime Minister Grotewohl gave a report on the current political situation at the meeting.

　　In his report, Grotewohl spoke about the current international situation and the Soviet disarmament proposals, as well as the issue of German reunification. He emphasized that the German Democratic Republic will continue to strengthen and consolidate its brotherly cooperation with its two neighboring countries, Czechoslovakia and Poland. He denounced Western imperialism and West Germany for plotting to sow discord in the friendly relations between the German Democratic Republic and Poland, and stated that the government and people of the German Democratic Republic are always ready to jointly defend the inviolability of the Oder-Neisse border with the People's Republic of Poland.

　　At this meeting, a draft law was also passed to shorten the weekly working hours to 45 hours in all enterprise sectors starting from February next year. This bill will be submitted to the People's Chamber for discussion.

### 格罗提渥在民主德国部长会议上强调　民主德国将继续加强同捷波两国兄弟友谊　会议通过关于缩短劳动时间的法律草案

1956-12-09
第5版()
专栏：

　　格罗提渥在民主德国部长会议上强调
　　民主德国将继续加强同捷波两国兄弟友谊
　　会议通过关于缩短劳动时间的法律草案
    据新华社柏林8日电　据“新德意志报”报道，德意志民主共和国部长会议6日举行会议。格罗提渥总理在会上就目前政治形势作了报告。
    格罗提渥在报告中谈到了目前国际形势和苏联裁军建议以及德国的统一问题。他强调了民主德国将继续加强和巩固同捷克斯洛伐克和波兰这两个邻国的兄弟合作。他斥责西方帝国主义和西德阴谋挑拨民主德国同波兰的友好关系，并且表示民主德国政府和人民随时准备同波兰人民共和国共同保卫奥得—尼斯河边界的不可侵犯性。
    在这次会议上，还通过了一项从明年2月起在各企业部门中把每周劳动时间缩短为四十五小时的法律草案。这个法案将提交人民议院讨论。
