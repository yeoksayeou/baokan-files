Title: Vice Chairman Zhu Watches Performance by Korean Art Troupe
Author:
Date: 1956-12-21
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, 20th -- Vice Chairman Zhu De watched the Korean Changgeuk "The Story of Chunhyang" performed by the National Folk Art Troupe of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea at the Tianqiao Theater in Beijing tonight.
    During the intermission, Vice Chairman Zhu De received the head of the art troupe, Yoon Doo-heon, the deputy head, Lee Won-bae, the general director, Ahn Young-il, and the main actors.
    Kim Kwi-nam, Chargé d'affaires ad interim of the Embassy of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea in China, accompanied him to watch the performance. (Attached picture)
    The situation when Vice Chairman Zhu De talked with Yoon Doo-heon, head of the Korean National Folk Art Troupe.

### 朱副主席观看朝艺术剧团演出

1956-12-21
第1版()
专栏：

    朱副主席观看朝艺术剧团演出
    新华社20日讯　朱德副主席今晚在北京天桥剧场观看了朝鲜民主主义人民共和国国立民族艺术剧团演出的朝鲜唱剧“春香传”。
    朱德副主席在演出休息的时候，接见了艺术剧团团长尹斗宪、副团长李源培，总导演安英一以及主要演员。
    陪同观剧的有朝鲜民主主义人民共和国驻中国大使馆临时代办金贵南。（附图片）
    朱德副主席和朝鲜国立民族艺术剧团尹斗宪团长谈话时情形。
                         新华社记者　冀连波摄
