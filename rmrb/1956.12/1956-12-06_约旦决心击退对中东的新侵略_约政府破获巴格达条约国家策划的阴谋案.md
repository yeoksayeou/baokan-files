Title: Jordan Determined to Repel New Aggression Against the Middle East; Jordanian Government Uncovers Conspiracy Case Masterminded by Baghdad Pact Nations
Author:
Date: 1956-12-06
Page: 6
　　
Jordan Determined to Repel New Aggression Against the Middle East
Jordanian Government Uncovers Conspiracy Case Masterminded by Baghdad Pact Nations
Xinhua News Agency, 4th dispatch from Amman: A Jordanian government spokesperson said today that Jordan considers an attack by any party on Syria or other free Arab nations to be an attack on Jordan itself. He said that Jordan will do everything in its power to repel such an attack.

Xinhua News Agency, 4th dispatch from Cairo: The "Egyptian Gazette" (afternoon edition) published on December 3rd a dispatch from Amman, the capital of Jordan, stating that the Jordanian government had uncovered a conspiracy case plotted by the Baghdad Pact nations against Syria and Jordan.

The newspaper said that the documents seized by the Jordanian government "prove that the conspiracy against Syria is part of a larger conspiracy including opposition to Jordan."

The newspaper said that the Jordanian authorities have arrested those involved in the conspiracy and seized a large number of weapons.

### 约旦决心击退对中东的新侵略  约政府破获巴格达条约国家策划的阴谋案

1956-12-06
第6版()
专栏：

　　约旦决心击退对中东的新侵略
    约政府破获巴格达条约国家策划的阴谋案
    新华社4日讯　安曼消息：约旦政府发言人今天说，约旦认为任何方面对叙利亚或其他自由的阿拉伯国家发动的进攻就是对约旦本身的进攻。他说，约旦将尽它的一切力量来击退这种进攻。
    据新华社4日讯　开罗消息：“埃及人报”（下午版）12月3日刊载从约旦首都安曼来的消息说：约旦政府破获了巴格达条约国家策划的反对叙利亚和约旦的阴谋案。
    这家报纸说，约旦政府缴获的文件“证明反对叙利亚的阴谋是包括反对约旦在内的大阴谋的一部分”。
    报纸说，约旦当局逮捕了参与阴谋的人，并且缴获了大批武器。
