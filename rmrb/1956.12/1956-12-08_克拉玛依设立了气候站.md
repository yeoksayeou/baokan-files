Title: Karamay Establishes Weather Station
Author:
Date: 1956-12-08
Page: 7

　　Karamay Establishes Weather Station
    Xinhua News Agency, Karamay. The newly built Karamay Weather Station began operations on December 1st. On that day, the station's staff made the first scientific records of various meteorological conditions in the Karamay area.

    This weather station was established by the Xinjiang Uygur Autonomous Region Meteorological Bureau at the request of the Karamay Mining Bureau. It will be dedicated to serving the oilfield's construction and production, providing scientific meteorological data for design work. The weather station will be expanded in the future to undertake weather forecasting work.

### 克拉玛依设立了气候站

1956-12-08
第7版()
专栏：

　　克拉玛依设立了气候站
    据新华社克拉玛依讯　新建的克拉玛依气候站已在12月1日开始工作。当天，气候站的工作人员第一次对克拉玛依地区的种种气象情况作了科学的纪录。
    这个气候站是新疆维吾尔自治区气象局应克拉玛依矿务局的请求而设立的。它将专为这里的油田建设和生产服务，为设计方面提供科学的气象根据。气象站今后还要扩建，以便担负起气象预报工作。
