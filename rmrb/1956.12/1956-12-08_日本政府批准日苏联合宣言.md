Title: Japanese Government Approves Japan-Soviet Joint Declaration
Author:
Date: 1956-12-08
Page: 5

　　Xinhua News Agency, Tokyo, December 7th: The Japanese government today formally approved the Japan-Soviet Joint Declaration regarding the restoration of diplomatic relations between Japan and the Soviet Union at a cabinet meeting.
    The Japanese government also approved at the cabinet meeting the protocol between Japan and the Soviet Union on developing trade and granting each other most-favored-nation treatment.
    These agreements were recently approved by the Japanese Diet. Japanese Foreign Minister Mamoru Shigemitsu and Soviet Deputy Foreign Minister Nikolai Fedorenko will exchange ratification instruments in Tokyo next week.

### 日本政府批准日苏联合宣言

1956-12-08
第5版()
专栏：

　　日本政府批准日苏联合宣言
    新华社7日讯　东京消息：日本政府今天在内阁会议上正式批准了关于日苏两国恢复外交关系的日苏联合宣言。
    日本政府在内阁会议上还批准了日苏两国关于发展贸易和互相给予最惠国待遇的议定书。
    这些协议最近刚被日本国会批准。日本外务相重光葵和苏联外交部副部长费德林将在下星期在东京互换批准书。
