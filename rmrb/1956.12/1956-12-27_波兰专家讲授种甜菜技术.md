Title: Polish Experts Lecture on Sugar Beet Planting Techniques
Author: Zou Banghuan, Xu Ping
Date: 1956-12-27
Page: 2

Polish Experts Lecture on Sugar Beet Planting Techniques

(Our Newspaper) Recently, the Ministry of Food Industry held a sugar beet technology training class, inviting Polish experts to impart advanced experience in sugar beet planting. Participants included technical cadres engaged in sugar beet production and factory directors, section chiefs, and station chiefs from sugar factories in Heilongjiang, Jilin, Inner Mongolia, Xinjiang and other regions, totaling seventy-five people.

The Polish experts said: "We will dedicate all our strength to further enhance the deep friendship between the people of China and Poland."

### 波兰专家讲授种甜菜技术
邹邦郇　许平
1956-12-27
第2版()
专栏：

    波兰专家讲授种甜菜技术
    本报讯　最近食品工业部举办了一个甜菜技术讲习班，聘请波兰专家来传授种植甜菜的先进经验。参加学习的有黑龙江、吉林、内蒙古、新疆等地区从事甜菜生产的技术干部和各糖厂厂长、科长、站长等共七十五个人。
    波兰专家说：“我们将献出自己所有的力量，以进一步增强中波两国人民的深厚友谊”。
              （邹邦郇　许平）
