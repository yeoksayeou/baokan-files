Title: League of Communists of Yugoslavia Delegation Travels to Warsaw to Visit Polish United Workers' Party
Author:
Date: 1956-12-19
Page: 5

    League of Communists of Yugoslavia Delegation Travels to Warsaw to Visit Polish United Workers' Party
    Xinhua News Agency, Belgrade, 18th – According to the Yugoslav News Agency, Belgrade: A delegation from the League of Communists of Yugoslavia left Belgrade on the 17th to visit the Polish United Workers' Party in Poland.
    The delegation is composed of Vukmanović (head of the delegation), a member of the Executive Committee of the Central Committee of the League, and seven other leaders of the League.
    This visit by the delegation is a return visit to the visit made by the Polish United Workers' Party delegation to Yugoslavia at the end of August this year. The delegation will stay in Poland for approximately ten days.

### 南共代表团前往华沙访问波兰统一工人党

1956-12-19
第5版()
专栏：

    南共代表团前往华沙访问波兰统一工人党
    新华社18日讯　据南斯拉夫通讯社贝尔格莱德讯：南斯拉夫共产主义者联盟代表团17日离开贝尔格莱德前往波兰访问波兰统一工人党。
    代表团由联盟中央委员会执行委员会委员伏克曼诺维奇（团长）和联盟的另外七位领导人组成。
    代表团的这次访问是对波兰统一工人党代表团今年8月底访问南斯拉夫的答谢访问。代表团大约将在波兰逗留十天。
