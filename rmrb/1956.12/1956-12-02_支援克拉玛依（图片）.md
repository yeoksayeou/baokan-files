Author: Zhang Lizhong

　　Supporting Karamay
　　The Xinjiang Transportation Bureau and many transportation units across the country are supporting Karamay. A motorcade fully loaded with oil pipelines is heading towards the oil region. Xinhua News Agency reporter Zhang Lizhong photographed.
