Title: Soviet Society for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries Delegation Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1956-12-31
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, December 30th. A nine-member delegation from the Soviet Society for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries arrived in Beijing by plane this afternoon. The delegation is visiting China at the invitation of the Sino-Soviet Friendship Association.

The head of the delegation is Academician Kairov, President of the Academy of Educational Sciences of the Russian Republic. The members include writer Katayev, painter Vileysky, Chairman of the Kazakh Republic Society for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries Jianggalin, Ostrovskaya, the wife of Ostrovsky, the author of the novel "How the Steel Was Tempered," writer Tendryakov, Chinese literature researcher Adlin, Chairman of the Stalin Collective Farm of the Belarusian Republic Sarovarov, and Deputy Director of the Oriental People's Republics Department of the Soviet Society for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries, Aldabayev.

### 苏联对外文化协会代表团到达北京

1956-12-31
第1版()
专栏：

    苏联对外文化协会代表团到达北京
    据新华社30日讯　苏联对外文化协会代表团一行九人今天下午坐飞机到达北京。代表团是应中苏友好协会总会邀请来中国进行友好访问的。
    代表团团长是俄罗斯共和国教育科学院院长凯洛夫院士。团员有作家卡达耶夫，画家威列依斯基，哈萨克共和国对外文化协会主席江加林，小说“钢铁是怎样炼成的”的作者奥斯特洛夫斯基的夫人奥斯特洛夫斯卡娅，作家田德里亚柯夫，中国文学研究家艾德林，白俄罗斯共和国斯大林集体农庄主席萨罗瓦罗夫，苏联对外文化协会东方人民共和国部副主任阿尔达巴耶夫。
