Title: Sino-U.S. Ambassadorial Talks Continue

Author: 
Date: 1956-12-14
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Geneva, December 13th: The Sino-U.S. ambassadorial talks held their 63rd meeting from 10:00 to 12:50 on December 13th. As the New Year holidays are approaching, both sides agreed that the next meeting will be held on January 19th next year.

### 中美大使级会谈继续举行

1956-12-14
第1版()
专栏：

    中美大使级会谈继续举行
    新华社13日讯　日内瓦消息：中美两国大使级会谈12月13日十时到十二时五十分举行了第六十三次会议。由于新年假日即将到来，双方同意下次会议将在明年1月19日举行。
