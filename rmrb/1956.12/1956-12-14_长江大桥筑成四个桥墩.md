Title: Four Bridge Piers of the Yangtze River Bridge Completed
Author: Qiang Yi
Date: 1956-12-14
Page: 2

Year-End Good News

    Four Bridge Piers of the Yangtze River Bridge Completed
    (Our Wuhan dispatch) The Wuhan Yangtze River Bridge overfulfilled its annual construction and installation plan one month ahead of schedule. By the end of November, it had completed 103.9% of the annual plan. Currently, four bridge piers of the Yangtze River Bridge have been completed. The steel beams on the Hanyang side have crossed pier No. 2 and are approaching pier No. 3. The steel beams on the Wuchang side are also extending from in front of the Yellow Crane Tower towards pier No. 8. Pier No. 5 in the middle of the river was originally planned to be built to an elevation of 20 meters by the end of the year, but it has now been built to an elevation of 25 meters. The steel beam riveting project was originally planned to complete 4,300 tons by the end of the year, but it has now completed 4,500 tons.
                        (Qiang Yi)

### 长江大桥筑成四个桥墩
强仪
1956-12-14
第2版()
专栏：年终捷报

    长江大桥筑成四个桥墩
    本报武汉电　武汉长江大桥提前一个月超额完成了全年建筑安装计划。截至11月底止，共完成全年计划103.9％。现在，长江大桥已经建成了四个桥墩，汉阳岸的钢梁已经跨过二号墩，逼近三号墩；武昌岸的钢梁也正由黄鹤楼前向八号墩伸延。江心的五号桥墩原来计划到年底才筑到二十公尺标高，现在已经筑到二十五公尺标高。钢梁铆合工程原来计划到年底完成四千三百吨，现在已经完成四千五百吨。
                        （强仪）
