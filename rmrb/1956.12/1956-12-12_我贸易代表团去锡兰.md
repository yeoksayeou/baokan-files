Title: Our Trade Delegation to Ceylon
Author:
Date: 1956-12-12
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, Kunming, December 11th. The People's Republic of China government trade delegation, led by the Minister of Foreign Trade, Ye Jizhuang, left Kunming by plane this afternoon for Colombo.
The delegation is visiting Ceylon at the invitation of the Ceylonese government. During the visit, the delegation will hold discussions with the Ceylonese government on issues related to trade and technical cooperation.

### 我贸易代表团去锡兰

1956-12-12
第1版()
专栏：

    我贸易代表团去锡兰
    新华社昆明11日电　由对外贸易部部长叶季壮率领的中华人民共和国政府贸易代表团，在今天下午从昆明乘飞机前往科伦坡。
    代表团是应锡兰政府的邀请去锡兰进行友好访问的。在访问期间，代表团将就有关贸易和技术合作等问题，同锡兰政府进行商谈。
