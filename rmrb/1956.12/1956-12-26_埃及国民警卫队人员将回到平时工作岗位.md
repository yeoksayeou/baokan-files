Title: Egyptian National Guard Personnel to Return to Regular Jobs
Author:
Date: 1956-12-26
Page: 6

    Xinhua News Agency, December 25th: Cairo - The commander of the Egyptian National Liberation Army, Hussein, said on the 24th that Egyptian National Guard personnel who had been called up for duty would return to their regular jobs.
    Hussein said that, strictly speaking, this is not demobilization. He pointed out that the National Liberation Army is composed of the People's Resistance Organization, the National Guard, and the Youth Camp. The People's Resistance Organization will continue its activities because its mission is closely related to national defense.

### 埃及国民警卫队人员将回到平时工作岗位

1956-12-26
第6版()
专栏：

    埃及国民警卫队人员将回到平时工作岗位
    据新华社25日讯　埃及民族解放军司令侯赛尼24日说，应召入伍的埃及国民警卫队人员将回到他们平时的工作岗位上去。
    侯赛尼说，严格地说，这不是复员。他指出，民族解放军是由人民抵抗组织、国民警卫队和青年营组成的。人民抵抗组织将继续活动，因为它的任务是同国防密切相关的。
