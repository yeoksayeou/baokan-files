Title: Nasser Hosts Jordanian Delegation to Present Award

Author:
Date: 1956-12-30
Page: 6

　　Xinhua News Agency, Cairo, December 29th: According to Cairo newspapers on the 28th, President Nasser hosted a banquet on the evening of the 27th for the Jordanian delegation sent by King Hussein of Jordan to present the Jordanian Supreme Renaissance Medal to Port Said. This medal is usually only awarded to heads of state in Jordan.

### 纳赛尔宴约旦授勋代表团

1956-12-30
第6版()
专栏：

    纳赛尔宴约旦授勋代表团
    据新华社开罗29日电　据开罗报纸28日报道，纳赛尔总统27日晚上设宴招待约旦国王侯赛尼派来把约旦最高复兴勋章授给塞得港市的约旦代表团。这种勋章在约旦通常只赠给国家元首。
