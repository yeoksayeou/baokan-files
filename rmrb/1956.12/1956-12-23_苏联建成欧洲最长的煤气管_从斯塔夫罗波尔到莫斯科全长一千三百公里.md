Author:
Date: 1956-12-23
Page: 5

    The Soviet Union Completes Europe's Longest Gas Pipeline
    From Stavropol to Moscow, 1,300 Kilometers Long
    (Our Moscow dispatch, December 21st) Europe's longest Stavropol-Moscow gas pipeline project has now been completed one year ahead of schedule. This gas pipeline is 1,300 kilometers long, covering an area of four million square meters, passing through the Ukrainian and Russian Federation Republics and more than a dozen states. Construction began two years ago and was originally scheduled for completion in 1957. This gas pipeline will directly supply Moscow with three to four million cubic meters of gas annually without passing through compressor stations. Residents and enterprises in Moscow will receive more cheap gas. Dozens of cities and villages along the gas pipeline will also receive gas.
    This long gas pipeline passes through countless rivers, pools, valleys. The builders have overcome more than 400 natural and man-made obstacles.
    The second Stavropol-Moscow gas pipeline will begin construction next year. By the end of the Sixth Five-Year Plan, the amount of gas fuel supplied to Moscow by the Stavropol-Moscow gas pipeline will exceed the fuel amount produced by the thirty-five new mines being built in Donbass. ### 苏联建成欧洲最长的煤气管  从斯塔夫罗波尔到莫斯科全长一千三百公里

1956-12-23
第5版()
专栏：

    苏联建成欧洲最长的煤气管
    从斯塔夫罗波尔到莫斯科全长一千三百公里
    本报莫斯科21日电　欧洲最长的斯塔夫罗波尔—莫斯科煤气管工程现在提前一年建成。这条煤气管全长一千三百公里，占地面积四百万平方公尺，通过乌克兰和俄罗斯联邦两个加盟共和国和十几个州，两年以前就开始建设，原定1957年完成。这条煤气管每年将不经过压气站直接供给莫斯科三百万到四百万立方公尺的煤气。莫斯科的居民和企业将得到更多的廉价煤气的供应。沿煤气管两旁大小数十个城市和村庄也将得到煤气。
    这条漫长的煤气管通过无数的河流、池沼、谷地。建设者们先后克服了四百多处自然和人工障碍。
    第二条斯塔夫罗波尔—莫斯科的煤气管将在明年开始建设。到第六个五年计划结束时，斯塔夫罗波尔—莫斯科煤气管供应莫斯科的煤气燃料量，将超过正在顿巴斯建设的三十五个新矿井产煤的燃料量。（附图片）
