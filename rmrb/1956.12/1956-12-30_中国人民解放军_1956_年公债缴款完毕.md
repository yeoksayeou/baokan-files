Title: Chinese People's Liberation Army Completes Payment of 1956 National Bonds
Author:
Date: 1956-12-30
Page: 4

    Xinhua News Agency, 26th: The Chinese People's Liberation Army has completed its payment for the 1956 National Economic Construction Bonds. According to preliminary statistics, the entire army paid a total of 30,088,200 yuan, exceeding the quota assigned to officers and soldiers by more than 50%.

### 中国人民解放军1956年公债缴款完毕

1956-12-30
第4版()
专栏：

    中国人民解放军1956年公债缴款完毕
    新华社26日讯　中国人民解放军认购的1956年度国家经济建设公债，已经缴款完毕。据初步统计，全军共缴款三千零八十八万二千元，超过国家分配给部队官兵认购的数字50％以上。
