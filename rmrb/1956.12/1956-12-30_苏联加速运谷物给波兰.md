Title: Soviet Union Accelerates Grain Delivery to Poland
Author:
Date: 1956-12-30
Page: 5

    Xinhua News Agency, Warsaw, December 29th: According to a report by the Polish Press Agency on the 28th, Poland has received approximately 70,000 tons of grain from the Soviet Union ahead of schedule. According to the agreement between the two countries, this batch of grain was originally scheduled to be delivered after the New Year.
    The head of the Polish grain organization, Bor, told reporters from the Polish Press Agency: The Soviet Union will deliver the 1.4 million tons of grain that we should receive under the Soviet-Polish agreement within the first six months of next year, which is very beneficial to us.

### 苏联加速运谷物给波兰

1956-12-30
第5版()
专栏：

    苏联加速运谷物给波兰
    据新华社华沙29日电　据波兰通讯社28日报道：波兰已提前收到了从苏联运来的七万吨左右的谷物。根据两国缔结的协定，这批谷物本来要在新年过后才开始交运的。
    波兰粮食机构的负责人波耳对波兰通讯社记者说：我们根据波苏协定应该接到的一百四十万吨谷物，苏联将在明年头六个月内运交完毕，这对我们是非常有利的。
