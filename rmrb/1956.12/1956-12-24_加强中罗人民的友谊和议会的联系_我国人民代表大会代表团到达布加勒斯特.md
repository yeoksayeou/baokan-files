Title: Strengthening the Friendship Between the Chinese and Romanian People and the Ties Between the Parliaments: Delegation of the National People's Congress of China Arrives in Bucharest
Author:
Date: 1956-12-24
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Bucharest, December 23rd: The delegation of the National People's Congress of China and the delegation of the Beijing Municipal People's Committee arrived in Bucharest, the capital of Romania, by special train on the evening of the 22nd. At the station, which was decorated like a festival with lights as bright as day, more than a thousand people warmly welcomed them.

Present at the station to welcome the delegation were Sadoveanu, Vice-Chairman of the Presidium of the Romanian National Assembly; Apostol, Chairman of the Central Council of Romanian Trade Unions; Constantinescu and Mogioroş, Vice-Chairmen of the Romanian Council of Ministers; ministers of various ministries of the government; Denilă, First Secretary of the Bucharest Municipal Committee of the Romanian Workers' Party; and Floredoiu, Chairman of the Bucharest Municipal People's Council, among others. Children presented the guests with beautiful bouquets, and the welcoming crowd continuously shouted "Long live the friendship between the Romanian and Chinese peoples!"

Sadoveanu delivered a welcoming speech at the station. He pointed out that the delegation's visit to Romania would further strengthen the friendship between the people of China and Romania. He said, "We welcome the envoys of the great Chinese people, our brothers, with boundless excitement. Through this visit, you will gain a deeper understanding of the profound friendship that our people have for our great brothers and friends, the Chinese people."

Peng Zhen, head of the delegation of the National People's Congress of China, delivered a reply. He first extended cordial greetings and high respect to the welcomers and all the working people of Romania, and thanked them for their warm welcome to the delegation. Peng Zhen said, "The Chinese people have always been concerned about the socialist construction cause of the fraternal Romanian people. In the twelve years since liberation, under the leadership of the glorious Romanian Workers' Party and the government, the Romanian people have achieved brilliant successes and accumulated a lot of valuable experience in various struggles. This visit is to strengthen mutual understanding, learn from your advanced experiences in various fields, strengthen the ties between our two parliaments, and further strengthen the friendship and cooperation between the people of our two countries."

Sergeanu, member of the Presidium of the Romanian National Assembly, and Bunaciu, secretary of the Presidium, and Ke Bonian, the Chinese ambassador to Romania, took a special train to the Romanian-Soviet border to greet the delegation, and accompanied the delegation to Bucharest.

### 加强中罗人民的友谊和议会的联系  我国人民代表大会代表团到达布加勒斯特

1956-12-24
第1版()
专栏：

    加强中罗人民的友谊和议会的联系
    我国人民代表大会代表团到达布加勒斯特
    新华社布加勒斯特23日电　中国全国人民代表大会代表团和北京市人民委员会代表团22日晚乘专车到达罗马尼亚首都布加勒斯特。在灯光如昼的装饰得像节日一样的车站上，有一千多人热烈地欢迎他们。
    罗马尼亚国民议会主席团副主席萨多维亚努，罗马尼亚工会中央理事会主席阿波斯托尔，罗马尼亚部长会议副主席康斯坦丁尼斯库、莫吉奥罗希、政府各部部长，罗马尼亚工人党布加勒斯特市委员会第一书记德尼拉克和布加勒斯特市人民会议主席弗勒多尤等都到车站欢迎代表团。儿童们把美丽的花束献给客人，欢迎的人群不断地高呼“罗中人民友谊万岁！”
    萨多维亚努在车站上致欢迎词。他指出，代表团到罗马尼亚访问，将使中罗两国人民之间的友谊更加密切。他说，我们以无限兴奋的心情来欢迎兄弟一般的伟大中国人民的使者。通过这一次访问，你们将会更深刻地了解我国人民对于伟大的兄弟和朋友、对中国人民所抱的深厚友情。
    中国全国人民代表大会代表团团长彭真致答词。他首先向欢迎者和罗马尼亚全体劳动人民表示亲切的问候和崇高的敬意，并且感谢他们对代表团的热烈欢迎。彭真说：“中国人民一向关心兄弟的罗马尼亚人民的社会主义建设事业。罗马尼亚人民在解放以来的十二年中，在光荣的罗马尼亚工人党和政府的领导下，在各种斗争中已经取得了辉煌的成就，积累了很多宝贵的经验。我们这次来访问是为了加强相互间的了解，学习你们各方面的先进经验。加强我们两国议会之间的联系，进一步加强两国人民之间的友谊和合作。
    罗马尼亚国民议会主席团委员塞尔杰亚努和主席团的秘书布纳丘和中国驻罗马尼亚大使柯柏年乘专车到罗苏边境迎接代表团，并陪同代表团一道到达布加勒斯特。
