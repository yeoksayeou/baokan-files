Title: Negotiations to Commence on Agreement Regarding the Status of Soviet Troops in Poland; Shepilov and Zhukov Arrive in Warsaw
Author: 
Date: 1956-12-18
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, December 17th: According to TASS, Soviet Foreign Minister Shepilov and Soviet Defense Minister Zhukov left Moscow by plane for Warsaw on December 17th.

Shepilov and Zhukov's trip to Warsaw is in accordance with the decision made during talks between the delegation from the Central Committee of the Communist Party of the Soviet Union and the Soviet government and the delegation from the Central Committee of the Polish United Workers' Party and the Polish government in November of this year, to negotiate with the Polish government on signing an agreement concerning the legal status of Soviet troops in Poland.

According to a report from the Polish Press Agency: Shepilov and Zhukov arrived in Warsaw on the 17th, where they were greeted at the airport by Polish Foreign Minister Rapacki, Defense Minister Spychalski, and others.

### 商谈签订关于苏军在波地位的协定  谢皮洛夫和朱可夫到达华沙

1956-12-18
第5版()
专栏：

    商谈签订关于苏军在波地位的协定
    谢皮洛夫和朱可夫到达华沙
    新华社17日讯　据塔斯社莫斯科讯：苏联外交部长谢皮洛夫和苏联国防部长朱可夫12月17日乘飞机离开莫斯科前往华沙。
    谢皮洛夫和朱可夫这次到华沙去，是按照苏联共产党中央委员会和苏联政府代表团同波兰统一工人党中央委员会和波兰政府代表团今年11月间会谈的决定，同波兰政府商谈签订一项关于苏联军队在波兰的法律地位的协定的。
    另据波兰通讯社报道：谢皮洛夫和朱可夫已经在17日到达华沙，到机场欢迎他们的有波兰外交部长拉帕茨基、国防部长斯彼哈尔斯基等人。
