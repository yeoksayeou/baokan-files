Title: Premier Zhou Calls on Overseas Chinese to "Everyone Learn from Xuanzang" During Meeting with Overseas Chinese Representatives in Calcutta
Author:
Date: 1956-12-11
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Calcutta, 10th - Premier Zhou Enlai and Vice Premier He Long met with more than 150 representatives of Overseas Chinese residing in Calcutta and nearby states in the garden of the West Bengal State Government Building on the afternoon of December 9th.

In his speech, Premier Zhou Enlai called on Overseas Chinese to "Everyone Learn from Xuanzang," work hard to learn the language of their host country, and strengthen unity. He said that every overseas Chinese compatriot must respect the laws and customs of their host country and maintain good relations with the people of their host country, and must not allow relations to deteriorate. He also said that to unite well with the Indian people, the Chinese must first unite themselves. Differences in political views are unavoidable, but regardless of differences of opinion, they should unite under the banner of patriotism.

Among the Overseas Chinese received were Overseas Chinese and Tibetan compatriots from Darjeeling, Assam, and Bihar, veteran Kuomintang member Xiong Fendan, Tan Yunshan, Dean of the Chinese Academy of International University, Xie Yingrui, Deputy to the National People's Congress of China, and Ye Heting, Member of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference.

Overseas Chinese representatives presented a banner to Premier Zhou Enlai, and Tibetan compatriots presented Hada scarves.

Mukerjee, Speaker of the West Bengal Legislative Council and President of the Calcutta Branch of the Sino-Indian Friendship Association, and Ghosh, Mayor of Calcutta, were also invited to attend the meeting.

Pan Zili, Chinese Ambassador to India, and Liu Yufeng, Consul General in Calcutta, also attended the meeting.

### 在加尔各答接见华侨代表时  周总理号召华侨“人人学玄奘”

1956-12-11
第4版()
专栏：

    在加尔各答接见华侨代表时
    周总理号召华侨“人人学玄奘”
    新华社加尔各答10日电　周恩来总理和贺龙副总理12月9日下午在印度西孟加拉邦政府大厦花园接见了旅居加尔各答和附近各邦的华侨代表一百五十多人。
    周恩来总理在讲话时号召华侨“人人学玄奘”，努力学习所在国语言，并且加强团结。他说，每一个侨胞都要很好地尊重所在国的法律和生活习惯，同所在国人民要把关系搞好，不许搞坏。他还说，要和印度人民团结好，首先要中国人自己团结起来。政治见解有差别是免不了的，但是不管有什么不同意见，应该在爱国主义的旗帜下团结起来。
    受接见的华侨中，有来自大吉岭、阿萨姆邦、比哈尔邦的华侨和藏族侨胞，老同盟会员熊奋南，国际大学中国学院院长谭云山，中国全国人民代表大会代表谢应瑞，中国人民政治协商会议委员叶鹤汀等。
    华侨代表向周恩来总理献了锦旗，藏族侨胞代表献了哈达。
    西孟加拉邦立法会议议长、印中友好协会加尔各答分会会长慕克吉、加尔各答市长高士等也应邀参加了会见。
    中国驻印度大使潘自力和驻加尔各答总领事柳雨峰也参加了会见。
