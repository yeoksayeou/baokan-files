Title: Blatant Violation of the Ceasefire Resolution: British Air Force Attacks Egyptian Positions
Author:
Date: 1956-12-01
Page: 6

　　Blatant Violation of the Ceasefire Resolution
　　British Air Force Attacks Egyptian Positions
    Xinhua News Agency, 30th - Cairo Dispatch: Two British aircraft attacked Egyptian positions near Ismailia in the Suez Canal area on November 29th. Egyptian anti-aircraft fire returned fire.
    A United Nations spokesman in Cairo announced at a press conference on November 29th that Egypt had filed a complaint with the United Nations authorities regarding Britain's violation of the United Nations ceasefire resolution.
    The spokesman read to reporters an official Egyptian complaint to the United Nations authorities. The complaint stated that two British Venom aircraft bombed Egyptian military vehicles and anti-aircraft gun positions in the Suez Canal and one mile east of Ismailia on November 29th.

### 明目张胆破坏停战决议　英国空军袭击埃及阵地

1956-12-01
第6版()
专栏：

　　明目张胆破坏停战决议
　　英国空军袭击埃及阵地
    新华社30日讯　开罗消息：两架英国飞机在11月29日袭击了苏伊士运河地区伊士美利亚附近的埃及阵地。埃及高射炮火予以还击。
    联合国在开罗的一位发言人11月29日在一次记者招待会上宣布，埃及已经就英国破坏联合国停战决议的行为向联合国当局提出控告。
    这位发言人向记者宣读了埃及给联合国当局的一份正式控告书。这份控告书说，两架英国毒液式飞机在11月29日轰炸了苏伊士运河和伊士美利亚以东一英里处埃及的军用汽车和高射炮阵地。
