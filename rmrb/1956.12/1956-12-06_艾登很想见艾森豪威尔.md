Title: Eden Eager to Meet Eisenhower
Author:
Date: 1956-12-06
Page: 6

　　Xinhua News Agency, December 5th, London dispatch: British Acting Prime Minister Butler stated in the House of Commons on December 4th that Prime Minister Eden is very eager to meet with U.S. President Eisenhower.

　　Labour MP Henderson asked Butler whether Eden would arrange to meet with Eisenhower before returning home from Jamaica.

　　Butler said: "I know the Prime Minister values these meetings highly. No arrangements have been made yet, but the Prime Minister and the government are keeping the possibility of a meeting firmly in mind."
      "However, the timing of the meeting must be decided by the heads of the two governments."

　　Earlier reports from Washington said that Eisenhower currently has no intention of holding talks with the leaders of the British and French governments.

### 艾登很想见艾森豪威尔

1956-12-06
第6版()
专栏：

　　艾登很想见艾森豪威尔
    新华社5日讯　伦敦消息：英国代理首相巴特勒12月4日在下院回答议员说，艾登首相很想同美国总统艾森豪威尔会晤。
    工党议员汉德逊问巴特勒，艾登是否将安排在从牙买加返国以前同艾森豪威尔会晤。
    巴特勒说：“我知道首相很珍视这些会晤。现在还没有作出任何安排，但是首相和政府牢惦着进行一次会谈的可能性。”
      “但是会晤的时间必须由两国政府首脑决定。”
    早些时候来自华盛顿的消息说，艾森豪威尔目前无意同英法的政府领导人进行会谈。
