Title: Superphosphate Chemical Fertilizer
Author: 
Date: 1956-12-17
Page: 2

    New Technology, New Products

    The local state-owned Hengyang Chemical Factory has successfully trial-produced a chemical fertilizer – superphosphate. It is planned to produce 6,000 tons before the spring rice transplanting next year to support agricultural production.

    Superphosphate is suitable for various crops in both paddy and dry fields. It can help the roots of crops develop and grow, and enhance the ability of crops to resist diseases and pests. (According to Xinhua News Agency)

### 过磷酸钙化学肥料

1956-12-17
第2版()
专栏：新技术、新产品

    过磷酸钙化学肥料
    地方国营衡阳化工厂试制成功了一种化学肥料——过磷酸钙，计划在明年春季插秧以前要生产出六千吨来支援农业生产。
    过磷酸钙适用于水、旱田各种农作物，能帮助农作物的根部发育生长，增强农作物抵抗病、虫害的能力。　（据新华社讯）
