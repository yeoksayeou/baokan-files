Title: Negotiating Aid to Jordan: Jordanian Prime Minister to Visit Syria, Egypt, and Saudi Arabia

Author:

Date: 1956-12-04
Page: 6

　　Xinhua News Agency, 3rd dispatch from Beirut: According to a broadcast by Beirut Radio on the evening of December 2nd, Jordanian Prime Minister Nabulsi will soon lead a delegation to the capitals of Syria, Egypt, and Saudi Arabia to discuss the issue of providing financial assistance to Jordan with the governments of these three countries.

　　Nabulsi announced on November 27th that the Jordanian government had decided to abolish the British-Jordanian treaty. In a later conversation, he explained that Jordan could not abolish the British-Jordanian treaty until Jordan receives financial assistance from Egypt, Syria, and Saudi Arabia to replace that from Britain.

### 商谈给约旦援助问题　约旦首相将访叙埃沙三国

1956-12-04
第6版()
专栏：

　  商谈给约旦援助问题
　　约旦首相将访叙埃沙三国
    新华社3日讯　贝鲁特消息：据贝鲁特电台2日晚上广播说，约旦首相纳布西不久将率领一个代表团到叙利亚、埃及和沙特阿拉伯三国的首都，同这三个国家的政府商谈给予约旦财政援助的问题。
    纳布西已经在11月27日宣布约旦政府决定废除英国—约旦条约。他在后来的一次谈话中说明，在约旦从埃及、叙利亚和沙特阿拉伯得到代替英国的财政援助以前，约旦不能够废除英国—约旦条约。
