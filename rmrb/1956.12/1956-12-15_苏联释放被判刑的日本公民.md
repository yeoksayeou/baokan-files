Title: Soviet Union Releases Sentenced Japanese Citizens
Author:
Date: 1956-12-15
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, December 14th -- TASS reported: The Presidium of the Supreme Soviet of the USSR issued an order on December 13th to pardon and release Japanese citizens sentenced in the Soviet Union. This decision was made by the Soviet Union based on humanitarian principles, considering that the state of war between the Soviet Union and Japan had ended and peaceful relations had been established.

The order stipulates the release of all sentenced Japanese citizens from their places of detention and permits all Japanese citizens released from their places of detention to return to Japan.

### 苏联释放被判刑的日本公民

1956-12-15
第5版()
专栏：

    苏联释放被判刑的日本公民
    新华社14日讯　据塔斯社莫斯科讯：苏联最高苏维埃主席团13日颁布了赦免和释放在苏联被判刑的日本公民的命令。这是苏联鉴于苏联和日本之间战争状态已经结束、和平关系已经建立，根据人道原则而决定的。
    命令决定从监禁地点释放所有被判刑的日本公民，并且允许所有从监禁地点释放的日本公民返回日本。
