Title: Average Lifespan of Soviet People Nearly Doubled Compared to Pre-Revolution

Author:

Date: 1956-12-31
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, 30th - Soviet newspapers report that the average lifespan of Soviet people has nearly doubled compared to pre-revolution, increasing from thirty-four years to sixty-four years. The overall morbidity rate of the Soviet people is continuously decreasing. The mortality rate has decreased by more than 70% since 1913. During the Fifth Five-Year Plan, the Soviet population increased by a total of sixteen million three hundred thousand.

　　The Soviet Union currently has more than 150,000 health institutions. Almost all agricultural regional centers have comprehensive prevention and treatment hospitals. Some prevention and treatment hospitals are also equipped with medical aircraft to treat residents in remote and isolated areas.

　　The number of Soviet doctors is more than 300,000. In terms of the ratio of doctors to the population, the Soviet Union is the highest in the world. In addition, the Soviet Union has 850,000 intermediate-level medical workers. The Soviet Union's existing 70 medical colleges train 20,000 to 25,000 new doctors each year.

　　The Soviet Union's more than 2,100 sanatoriums and more than 900 rest homes have played a significant role in improving the health of the Soviet people. This year alone, they have received more than five million people.

### 苏联人民平均寿命比革命前延长近一倍

1956-12-31
第5版()
专栏：

    苏联人民平均寿命比革命前延长近一倍
    新华社莫斯科30日电　据苏联报纸报道：苏联人民现在的平均寿命比革命前延长了近一倍，已经从三十四岁延长到六十四岁。苏联人民总的生病率不断地下降。死亡率从1913年以来降低了70％以上。在第五个五年计划中，苏联人口一共增加了一千六百三十万。
    苏联目前有十五万个以上的卫生机构。几乎在所有农业地区中心都有综合防治医院。有些防治医院还备有医疗飞机，以便给遥远的和偏僻地区的居民治病。
    苏联医生的数目有三十多万，按照医生和居民人口的比率来说，苏联是世界上最高的。此外苏联还有八十五万中级医务工作者。苏联现有的七十个医学院，每年要培养出二万到二万五千个新医生。
    苏联的二千一百多个疗养院和九百多个休养所对增进苏联人民的健康发挥了很大的作用。单在今年一年中，它们就接待了五百多万人。
