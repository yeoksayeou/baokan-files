Title: Feng Xuan Deposits Instruments of Ratification for Four Geneva Conventions with the Swiss Government
Author:
Date: 1956-12-31
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, 30th. Feng Xuan, Ambassador Extraordinary and Plenipotentiary of the People's Republic of China to the Swiss Confederation, on December 28th, on behalf of the Government of the People's Republic of China, deposited with the Swiss Federal Government the instruments of ratification by the Chairman of the People's Republic of China on November 5, 1956, for the four Geneva Conventions of August 12, 1949: for the Amelioration of Condition of the Wounded and Sick in Armed Forces in the Field, for the Amelioration of the Condition of Wounded, Sick and Shipwrecked Members of Armed Forces at Sea, relative to the Treatment of Prisoners of War, and relative to the Protection of Civilian Persons in Time of War, and submitted a note to Mr. Petitpierre, Head of the Swiss Federal Political Department. Ambassador Feng Xuan and Mr. Dellemolle, Head of the International Division of the Swiss Federal Political Department, signed a protocol on the deposit of the instruments of ratification on behalf of their respective governments.

### 冯铉向瑞士政府交存四个日内瓦公约批准书

1956-12-31
第1版()
专栏：

    冯铉向瑞士政府交存四个日内瓦公约批准书
    新华社30日讯　中华人民共和国驻瑞士联邦特命全权大使冯铉，12月28日代表中华人民共和国政府向瑞士联邦政府交存了中华人民共和国主席在1956年11月5日批准的1949年8月12日改善战地武装部队伤者病者境遇、改善海上武装部队伤者病者及遇船难者境遇、关于战俘待遇和关于战时保护平民四个日内瓦公约的批准书，并递交了致瑞士联邦政治部部长彼蒂彼爱的照会一份。冯铉大使和瑞士联邦政治部代表、国际司司长德拉模分别代表两国政府签署了关于交存批准书的议定书。
