Title: Chinese People's Congress Delegation Visits Slovakia
Author:
Date: 1956-12-12
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, Prague, December 10th: The delegation of the National People's Congress of China and the delegation of the Beijing Municipal People's Committee left Ostrava by special train on the evening of the 8th and arrived in Bratislava, the capital of Slovakia, on the morning of the 9th.

　　Before going to Bratislava, the delegation visited Ostrava for a day.

　　In the morning, the delegation visited the New Gottwald Iron and Steel Plant and the Tatra Automobile Factory in groups. In the afternoon, the delegation visited the new workers' residential area in the suburbs.

　　On this day, the chairman of the Ostrava State People's Committee, the secretary of the State Party Committee, and the mayor of Ostrava hosted a banquet for the delegation and presented gifts to the delegation.

### 我国人民代表大会代表团访斯洛伐克

1956-12-12
第1版()
专栏：

    我国人民代表大会代表团访斯洛伐克
    据新华社布拉格10日电　中国全国人民代表大会代表团和北京市人民委员会代表团8日晚乘专车离开俄斯特拉发，在9日晨到达斯洛伐克首府布拉迪斯拉发。
    在赴布拉迪斯拉发以前，代表团曾经在俄斯特拉发进行了一天的访问。
    上午，代表团分组参观了新哥特瓦尔德钢铁厂和塔特拉汽车厂。下午，代表团访问了市郊的新工人住宅区。
    这一天，俄斯特拉发州人民委员会主席、州党委会书记和俄斯特拉发市长设宴招待代表团，并且向代表团赠送了礼物。
