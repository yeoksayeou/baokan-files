Title: Malik Awarded Order of the Red Banner of Labor
Author:
Date: 1956-12-21
Page: 5

    Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, December 19th – TASS reports: The Presidium of the Supreme Soviet of the Soviet Union issued a decree awarding the Order of the Red Banner of Labor to Ya. A. Malik, Soviet Ambassador Extraordinary and Plenipotentiary to the United Kingdom, in honor of his fiftieth birthday and in recognition of his achievements in diplomatic work.

### 马立克荣获劳动红旗勋章

1956-12-21
第5版()
专栏：

    马立克荣获劳动红旗勋章
    新华社20日讯　据塔斯社莫斯科19日讯：苏联最高苏维埃主席团发布了一项命令，授给苏联驻英国特命全权大使雅·亚·马立克劳动红旗勋章，以祝贺他的五十岁生日，并且表扬他在外交工作中的功绩。
