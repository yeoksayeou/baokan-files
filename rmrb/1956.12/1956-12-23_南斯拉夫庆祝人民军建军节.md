Title: Yugoslavia Celebrates People's Army Day
Author:
Date: 1956-12-23
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, December 22nd—According to the Yugoslav News Agency: December 22nd is the 15th anniversary of the founding of the Yugoslav People's Army. On the eve of the festival, a grand performance gala was held in Belgrade.

    Those present at the evening party included Yugoslav President and Supreme Commander of the Armed Forces Tito, State Secretary for National Defense Gošnjak, and people from all walks of life in Belgrade.

    On the same day, various celebrations were also held in other cities in Yugoslavia.

### 南斯拉夫庆祝人民军建军节

1956-12-23
第5版()
专栏：

    南斯拉夫庆祝人民军建军节
    新华社22日讯　据南斯拉夫通讯社讯：22日是南斯拉夫人民军建军15周年纪念日。在节日前夕，贝尔格莱德举行了隆重的演出晚会。
    出席晚会的有南斯拉夫总统和武装部队最高统帅铁托、国防国务秘书长戈什尼亚克等人和贝尔格莱德的各界人士。
    同一天，南斯拉夫的其他城市也举行了各种庆祝活动。
