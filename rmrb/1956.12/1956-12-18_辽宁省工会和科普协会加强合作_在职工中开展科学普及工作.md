Title: Liaoning Provincial Trade Union and Science Popularization Association Strengthen Cooperation to Carry Out Scientific Popularization Work Among Workers
Author:
Date: 1956-12-18
Page: 7

Xinhua News Agency: Liaoning Provincial Trade Union and the Science Popularization Association Strengthen Cooperation to Carry Out Scientific Popularization Work Among Workers

Xinhua News Agency report: At the first Liaoning Provincial Worker Scientific and Technological Popularization Work Conference held from the 10th to the 12th, the Liaoning Provincial Federation of Trade Unions and the Liaoning Provincial Association for Science and Technology Popularization decided to further cooperate in worker scientific and technological popularization work. The two departments have now drafted a cooperation plan.

The meeting pointed out that Liaoning Province has many industrial cities, many key enterprises, and factories and enterprises are developing rapidly, with a continuous increase in new workers. Doing a good job in the province's worker scientific and technological popularization work is of great significance to rapidly improving the scientific and technological level of workers.

The meeting determined that in the future, production technology propaganda and education should be conducted for the vast number of workers based on the main technical problems in factory and mine production and construction, and propaganda work should be carried out on basic knowledge of natural science, hygiene knowledge, safe production, and other knowledge as appropriate.

### 辽宁省工会和科普协会加强合作  在职工中开展科学普及工作

1956-12-18
第7版()
专栏：

    辽宁省工会和科普协会加强合作
    在职工中开展科学普及工作
    新华社讯　在10日到12日举行的辽宁省第一次职工科学技术普及工作会议上，辽宁省工会联合会和辽宁省科学技术普及协会决定在职工科学技术普及工作中进一步协作。现在，这两个部门已经制定出协作计划草案。
    这次会议指出，辽宁省工业城市多，重点企业多，工厂企业发展快，新工人不断增加。搞好全省职工科学技术普及工作，对迅速提高职工的科学技术水平有重大意义。
    会议确定，今后应根据厂矿生产和建设中的主要技术问题，向广大职工进行生产技术宣传教育，并适当进行自然科学基础知识、卫生知识、安全生产等知识的宣传工作。
