Title: Soviet Soloist Alenichenko Holds Concert in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1956-12-10
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, December 9th—Soviet soprano soloist Alenichenko, first prize winner of the singing competition at the 4th World Youth and Students Peace and Friendship Festival and the 1956 All-Soviet Soloist Competition, enchanted Beijing audiences tonight with her solo performances of world-renowned songs.
	Alenichenko and her accompanying pianist, Rasiko, arrived in Beijing on the 6th. Tonight, the Ministry of Culture held their first concert in China at the Tianqiao Theater in Beijing.
	Alenichenko is a soloist at the Shevchenko National Opera and Ballet Theatre in Kiev, Ukraine. She can sing more than one hundred famous Russian and Western European songs. Tonight, at the concert lasting one hundred minutes, she continuously sang fourteen of her best songs, with a round and consistent tone, eliciting enthusiastic applause continuously.

### 苏联独唱家阿列尼琴柯在京举行音乐会

1956-12-10
第1版()
专栏：

　　苏联独唱家阿列尼琴柯在京举行音乐会
    据新华社9日讯　苏联女高音独唱家、第四届世界青年与学生和平友谊联欢节歌唱比赛和1956年全苏独唱家比赛会的一等奖获得者阿列尼琴柯，9日晚上以她独唱的世界名曲陶醉了北京的听众。
    阿列尼琴柯和为她伴奏的钢琴家拉西柯是6日来到北京的。今天晚上，文化部在北京天桥剧场举行了他们访问中国的第一次音乐会。
    阿列尼琴柯是乌克兰基辅舍甫琴柯国立歌剧和芭蕾舞剧院的独唱家，她能唱一百首以上的俄罗斯和西欧的名曲。今天晚上，她在历时一百分钟的音乐会上，连续独唱了最拿手的十四个歌，音色圆润，始终不变，不断引起热烈的掌声。
