Title: German and Polish Party Delegations Hold Talks
Author:
Date: 1956-12-14
Page: 5
Xinhua News Agency, Warsaw, December 13th: According to a report from the Polish Press Agency: The delegation of the Politburo of the Central Committee of the Socialist Unity Party of Germany, composed of Mattern, Ebert, and Heinrich Rau, held talks in Warsaw on December 11th with the delegation of the Politburo of the Central Committee of the Polish United Workers' Party, composed of Gomulka, Jedrychowski, and Rapacki.
    The talks covered issues of concern to both parties. The talks were conducted in a sincere, friendly, and mutually understanding atmosphere. The talks showed that the two parties' views were basically consistent on all issues discussed.

### 德波两党代表团举行会谈

1956-12-14
第5版()
专栏：

    德波两党代表团举行会谈
    新华社华沙13日电　据波兰通讯社报道：由马特恩、艾伯特、海因里希·劳所组成的德国统一社会党中央委员会政治局代表团同由哥穆尔卡、英德里霍夫斯基、拉帕茨基组成的波兰统一工人党中央委员会政治局代表团，12月11日在华沙进行了会谈。
    会谈的内容是两党所关心的问题。会谈是在诚恳、友好和相互谅解的气氛中进行的。会谈表明，对所讨论的一切问题两党的观点基本上是一致的。
