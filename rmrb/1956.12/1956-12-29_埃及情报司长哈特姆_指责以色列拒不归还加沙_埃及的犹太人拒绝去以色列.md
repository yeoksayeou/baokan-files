Title: Egyptian Intelligence Director Hatem Accuses Israel of Refusing to Return Gaza; Egyptian Jews Refuse to Go to Israel
Author:
Date: 1956-12-29
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, Cairo, December 28th: Egyptian Intelligence Director Hatem said here on the evening of the 26th that Israeli Prime Minister Ben-Gurion's statement on the 25th regarding the refusal to return the Gaza Strip to Egypt blatantly violated the resolutions passed by the United Nations General Assembly on November 2nd, 4th, and 7th.

The aforementioned resolutions of the United Nations General Assembly demanded that Israel immediately withdraw its troops behind the 1949 armistice line.

Hatem said: "What Ben-Gurion said is an open challenge to the United Nations." He said that Egypt is willing to maintain peace and demands the thorough implementation of the resolutions passed by the United Nations General Assembly. Only in this way can there be peace and security in the Middle East.

Khaled Mohieddin, editor of "Al Messa," wrote in "Al Messa" on the 27th that the United Nations should insist that Israeli troops quickly and completely withdraw from the Sinai Peninsula and the Gaza Strip.

Xinhua News Agency, December 28th: TASS, Cairo, December 27th: A representative of the Egyptian government announced to reporters that Egyptian Jews refuse to go to Israel. Therefore, the Egyptian government believes that the kind of demand made by Israel is unacceptable. Israel demanded to exchange Egyptian citizens in Gaza, who were treated as prisoners by the Israeli authorities, for an equal number of Jews residing in Egypt. The representative concluded: "Egypt treats Egyptian Jews the same as it treats Muslims and Copts."

### 埃及情报司长哈特姆  指责以色列拒不归还加沙  埃及的犹太人拒绝去以色列

1956-12-29
第6版()
专栏：

    埃及情报司长哈特姆
    指责以色列拒不归还加沙
    埃及的犹太人拒绝去以色列
    新华社开罗28日电　埃及情报司长哈特姆26日晚上在这里说，以色列总理本—古里安25日发表的关于拒绝把加沙地带归还埃及的声明，明目张胆地违反了联合国大会11月2日、4日和7日所通过的决议。
    联合国大会的上述决议要求以色列立即把军队撤退到1949年的停战线后面去。
    哈特姆说：“本—古里安所说的话是对联合国的公开挑战。”他说，埃及愿意维护和平，埃及要求彻底履行联合国大会所通过的决议。只有这样，中东才有和平和安全。
    “晚报”编辑哈立德·毛希丁27日在“晚报”上写道，联合国应该坚持要以色列军队迅速地全部撤出西奈半岛和加沙地带。
    新华社28日讯　塔斯社开罗27日讯：埃及政府的代表向记者宣布，埃及的犹太人拒绝到以色列去。因此埃及政府认为，以色列提出的那种要求是不能接受的，以色列要求把在加沙被以色列当局作为俘虏看待的埃及公民交换住在埃及境内的同等数量的犹太人。这位代表最后说：“埃及对待埃及的犹太人跟对待穆斯林、哥普特人一样。”
