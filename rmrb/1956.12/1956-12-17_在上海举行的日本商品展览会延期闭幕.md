Title: Japanese Commodity Exhibition in Shanghai Postpones Closing

Author:
Date: 1956-12-17
Page: 1

    Xinhua News Agency, Shanghai, December 14th. The Japanese Commodity Exhibition held in Shanghai announced today that due to the enthusiastic number of visitors, the originally scheduled three-week exhibition period will be extended by five days, to close on December 26th.
    Since the exhibition opened two weeks ago, 900,000 people have visited. Chinese and Japanese technical personnel have already held seventeen technical exchange seminars, and extensive trade negotiations are also actively underway.

### 在上海举行的日本商品展览会延期闭幕

1956-12-17
第1版()
专栏：

    在上海举行的日本商品展览会延期闭幕
    新华社上海14日电　在上海举行的日本商品展览会今天宣布，由于参观者踊跃，决定把预定三周的展期延长五天，到本月26日闭幕。
    展览会开幕两周以来，已经有九十万人前往参观。中日双方技术人员已经举行了十七次技术交流座谈会，同时广泛的贸易谈判也在积极进行中。
