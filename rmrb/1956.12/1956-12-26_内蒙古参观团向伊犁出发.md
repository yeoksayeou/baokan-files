Title: Inner Mongolia Visiting Group Departs for Ili
Author:
Date: 1956-12-26
Page: 4
    
    The Inner Mongolia Autonomous Region visiting group, consisting of twenty-nine people, departed from Urumqi on the 22nd to visit the Ili Kazakh Autonomous Prefecture. There, they will go deep into the pastoral areas to investigate and understand the situation regarding livestock production and various tasks in the pastoral areas.
    This visiting group is the first group sent by the Inner Mongolia Autonomous Region to Xinjiang for a visit. They arrived in Urumqi on the 16th. The visiting group will exchange experiences with the people of all ethnic groups in the Xinjiang Uygur Autonomous Region in industry, agriculture, animal husbandry, and cultural and health undertakings.

### 内蒙古参观团向伊犁出发

1956-12-26
第4版()
专栏：

    内蒙古参观团向伊犁出发
    内蒙古自治区参观团一行二十九人，22日从乌鲁木齐出发到伊犁哈萨克族自治州去参观访问。在那里，他们将深入到牧区，调查了解有关畜牧业生产和牧区各项工作情况。
    这个参观团是内蒙古自治区第一次派到新疆参观访问的团体，他们在16日到达乌鲁木齐。参观团将和新疆维吾尔自治区各族人民交流工业、农业、畜牧业和文化卫生事业等方面的经验。
