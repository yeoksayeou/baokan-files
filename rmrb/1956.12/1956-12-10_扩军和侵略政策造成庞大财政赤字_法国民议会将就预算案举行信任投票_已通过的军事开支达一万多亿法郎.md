Title: Expansion of Armaments and Aggressive Policies Cause Huge Fiscal Deficit; French National Assembly to Hold Vote of Confidence on Budget; Approved Military Spending Reaches Over One Trillion Francs
Author:
Date: 1956-12-10
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, Paris, 9th - Paris news: French Prime Minister Mollet requested the National Assembly on the 8th to hold a vote of confidence on the government's proposed 1957 national budget draft. The vote will be held on the afternoon of the 10th.

According to an Associated Press report: Mollet's budget is "a deficit-ridden document." According to this budget, expenditures for 1957 will reach 4.5 trillion francs, not including expenses in Algeria, which may add another 310 billion francs. Thus, it is estimated that there will be a deficit of 990 billion francs in France's 1957 national budget.

Xinhua News Agency, Paris, 8th - Paris news: After twelve hours of debate, the French National Assembly approved the military budget portion of the government's proposed 1957 national budget draft on the morning of the 7th. One hundred and fifty members of the Communist Party and the Republican Union for Progress voted against it.

Dorelle, the general rapporteur of the National Assembly's Defense Committee on the military budget, revealed to the parliament that due to the situations in Algeria and the Middle East, France has to maintain an army of 1,230,000 men. This makes the total military expenditure for next year reach 1.01 trillion francs, 60 billion francs more than this year. However, this number does not include military spending in Algeria or the cost of armed intervention in Egypt.

The Defense Committee rapporteur also suggested manufacturing atomic weapons.

Communist Party member General Malleret-Joinville spoke during the discussion, pointing out that the expenditures stipulated in the government's budget draft are not the entire military expenses. Even without calculating military operations in Algeria and military spending on the invasion of Egypt, the total military expenditure actually exceeds 1.5 trillion francs.

### 扩军和侵略政策造成庞大财政赤字　法国民议会将就预算案举行信任投票  已通过的军事开支达一万多亿法郎

1956-12-10
第6版()
专栏：

  　扩军和侵略政策造成庞大财政赤字
　　法国民议会将就预算案举行信任投票
　  已通过的军事开支达一万多亿法郎
    据新华社9日讯　巴黎消息：法国总理摩勒8日要求国民议会就政府提出的1957年度国家预算草案举行信任投票。投票将于10日下午举行。
    据美联社消息说：摩勒的预算是“一个人不敷出的文件”。按照这个预算，1957年度的支出达到四万五千亿法郎，这还不包括阿尔及利亚的开支在内，后者可能要再加三千一百亿法郎。这样，据估计，在法国1957年度的国民预算中将有九千九百亿法郎的赤字。
    据新华社8日讯　巴黎消息：法国国民议会经过十二小时的辩论后，在7日早上批准了政府提出的1957年度国家预算草案中的军事预算部分。共产党和进步共和联盟议员一百五十人在表决时投了反对票。
    国民议会国防委员会军事预算总报告人多列向议会作报告的时候透露，由于阿尔及利亚和中东的局势，法国不得不维持一支一百二十三万人的军队。这就使得明年的军事开支总额达一万零十亿法郎，比今年多六百亿法郎。可是这个数目并不包括在阿尔及利亚的军事开支或是在埃及进行武装干涉的花费。
    国防委员会报告员还建议制造原子武器。
    共产党议员马勒雷—儒恩维将军在讨论中发言，他指出，政府的预算草案中所规定的开支还不是全部的军事费用。即使不把在阿尔及利亚的军事行动和侵略埃及的军事开支计算在内，全部军事费用实际上超过一万五千亿法郎。
