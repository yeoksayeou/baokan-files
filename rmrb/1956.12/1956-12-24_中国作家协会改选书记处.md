Title: Chinese Writers Association Re-elects Secretariat

Author:

Date: 1956-12-24
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, December 22nd: The Presidium of the Chinese Writers Association, at a recent meeting, re-elected the Secretariat. Mao Dun was elected First Secretary. Lao She, Shao Quanlin, Liu Baiyu, Cao Yu, Zang Kejia, Wu Zuxiang, Zhang Jin, Zhang Guangnian, Chen Baichen, and Yan Wenjing were elected Secretaries.

### 中国作家协会改选书记处

1956-12-24
第1版()
专栏：

    中国作家协会改选书记处
    新华社22日讯　中国作家协会主席团在最近举行的一次会议上改选了书记处。新的书记处由茅盾任第一书记。老舍、邵荃麟、刘白羽、曹禺、臧克家、吴组缃、章靳以、张光年、陈白尘和严文井任书记。
