Title: Two New Mechanized Farms Established in Tibet
Author:
Date: 1956-12-06
Page: 3

　　Xinhua News Agency, Lhasa, December 3rd. Two more state-invested mechanized farms have begun construction on the banks of the fertile Nyang Chu River in Tibet. A group of cadres in charge of establishing the farms have already gone to the sites, and the farms' water conservancy projects are under construction.

　　According to the person in charge of the Agriculture and Forestry Department of the Preparatory Committee for the Tibet Autonomous Region, these two farms have a total of more than 20,000 mu of arable land, and 4,000 mu of land will be put into production next spring.

### 西藏新建两个机耕农场

1956-12-06
第3版()
专栏：

　　西藏新建两个机耕农场
    新华社拉萨3日电　又有两个由国家投资的机耕农场，开始在西藏富饶的年楚河边建立。一批建场干部已经前往场地，农场的水利工程正在兴建。
    据西藏自治区筹备委员会农林处负责人说，这两个农场共拥有两万多亩可耕地，明年春天就将有四千亩土地投入生产。
