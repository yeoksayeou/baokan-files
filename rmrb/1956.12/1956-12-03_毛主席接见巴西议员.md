Title: Chairman Mao Receives Brazilian Parliamentarians
Author: 
Date: 1956-12-03
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, 2nd December. Chairman Mao Zedong received four Brazilian parliamentarians and their wives this evening. When receiving them, Chairman Liu Shaoqi and Vice-Chairmen Guo Moruo and Chen Shutong were also present.

### 毛主席接见巴西议员

1956-12-03
第1版()
专栏：

　　毛主席接见巴西议员
　　新华社2日讯　毛泽东主席今晚接见了巴西四位议员和他们的夫人。接见的时候，刘少奇委员长和郭沫若、陈叔通副委员长也在座。
