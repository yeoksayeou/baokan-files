Title: Presidium of the Supreme Soviet of the USSR Approves the Soviet-Japanese Joint Declaration and Protocol
Author:
Date: 1956-12-11
Page: 6

    Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, 10th Dispatch from TASS: The Presidium of the Supreme Soviet of the USSR issued an order on December 8th, approving the Soviet-Japanese Joint Declaration and the Soviet-Japanese Protocol on the Development of Trade and Mutual Granting of Most-Favored-Nation Treatment, signed in Moscow on October 19th of this year, which had already been adopted by the Council of Ministers of the USSR.

### 苏联最高苏维埃主席团  批准苏日联合宣言和议定书

1956-12-11
第6版()
专栏：

    苏联最高苏维埃主席团
    批准苏日联合宣言和议定书
    新华社10日讯　据塔斯社莫斯科讯：苏联最高苏维埃主席团12月8日发布命令，批准已被苏联部长会议通过的今年10月19日在莫斯科签署的苏联和日本的联合宣言、苏联和日本关于发展贸易和互相给予最惠国待遇的议定书。
