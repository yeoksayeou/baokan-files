Title: Xinjiang Uyghur Autonomous Region Industry Pre-fulfills This Year's Production Plan
Author:
Date: 1956-12-24
Page: 2
Xinjiang Daily, Urumqi, December 23rd: As of December 21st, the industrial and transportation departments of the Xinjiang Uyghur Autonomous Region have overfulfilled the 1956 annual production plan. In industry, the total output value of seventy-three relatively large state-owned, locally state-owned, and joint state-private factories and mines has pre-fulfilled the annual production plan. By the end of November, twenty-nine factories and mines had completed their annual output value plans. Among thirty major products, kerosene, pig iron, double-wheeled double-shared plows, cultivators, light leather, heavy leather, printed matter, refractory bricks and eight other kinds of products, have completed or overfulfilled the annual output plan one month ahead of schedule. Up to now, the total output value of twenty-one factories and mines in the entire autonomous region has reached the level of 1957. According to the statistics of fifty-two factories and mines in the local state-owned industrial system, the total output value this year can reach 129.77% of the annual production target; in the output of seventeen major products, ten products including electricity, coal, pig iron, steel ingots, steel, refractory bricks, low-voltage electrical porcelain, edible vegetable oil, leather, and agricultural implements have reached the 1957 output plan indicators. In terms of transportation, this year's production plan has also been completed.

### 新疆维吾尔自治区工业  提前完成了本年度生产计划

1956-12-24
第2版()
专栏：

    新疆维吾尔自治区工业
    提前完成了本年度生产计划
    本报乌鲁木齐23日电　到12月21日止，新疆维吾尔自治区工业、交通部门已经超额完成了1956年度的生产计划。在工业方面，七十三个国营、地方国营、公私合营较大型的厂矿、总产值已经提前完成年度生产计划。在11月底，就有二十九个厂矿完成了年度产值计划。三十种主要产品中，提前一个月完成和超额完成年度产量计划的，有煤油、生铁、双轮双铧犁、中耕器、轻革、重革、印刷品、耐火砖等八种。到目前为止，全自治区有二十一个厂矿的生产总值已经达到1957年的水平。根据地方国营工业系统五十二个厂矿的统计，今年生产总值可以达到年生产指标的129.77％；在十七种主要产品的产量上，电力、煤炭、生铁、钢锭、钢材、耐火砖、低压电瓷、食用植物油、皮革和农具等十种产品，已经达到了1957年的产量计划指标。在交通运输方面，也都完成今年的生产计划。
                  （新疆日报）
