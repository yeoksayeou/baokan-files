Title: North Korean Dredger Helps Dredge Dagu Estuary Channel
Author:
Date: 1956-12-04
Page: 2

　　Xinhua News Agency, Tianjin, December 3rd: The "Construction" dredger from the Democratic People's Republic of Korea began helping China dredge the Dagu Estuary channel today. It is the first foreign dredger to come to support China's shipping industry at the invitation of the Ministry of Communications, and it can dredge 400 cubic meters of mud per hour.
　　When the "Construction" arrived in Tianjin Port, it was warmly welcomed by all the staff of the Dredging Company of the Ministry of Communications.

### 朝鲜一艘挖泥船  帮助疏浚大沽口航道

1956-12-04
第2版()
专栏：

    朝鲜一艘挖泥船
　  帮助疏浚大沽口航道
    据新华社天津3日电　朝鲜民主主义人民共和国“建设”号挖泥船，今天开始在大沽口帮助我国疏浚航道。它是应交通部之请前来支援我国航运事业的第一艘外国挖泥船，每小时能挖净泥四百立方公尺。
    当“建设”号到天津港时，受到交通部疏浚公司全体职工热烈的欢迎。
