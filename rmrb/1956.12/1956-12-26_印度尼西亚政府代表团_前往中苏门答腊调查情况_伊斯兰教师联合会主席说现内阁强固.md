Title: Indonesian Government Delegation Goes to Central Sumatra to Investigate Situation; Chairman of the Islamic Teachers Association Says Current Cabinet is Strong

Author:
Date: 1956-12-26
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, Jakarta, December 25th: An Indonesian central government delegation went to Central Sumatra on the 24th to conduct an on-the-spot investigation of the situation there.

Indonesia News Agency quoted a Padang radio station report on the 24th saying that Lieutenant Colonel Hussein, chairman of the so-called Wild Bull Division Committee, which took over the administrative power of Central Sumatra last week, said that he is willing to negotiate with the central government, including the central government delegation from Jakarta.

Xinhua News Agency, Jakarta, December 25th: According to a report by Antara News Agency, the congress of the Indonesian Islamic Teachers Association still opened in Medan on the evening of the 23rd as originally planned.

Dahlan, chairman of the Central Committee of the Indonesian Islamic Teachers Association, said in his opening speech that the current cabinet is the strongest cabinet and has never violated its program and the results of the general election.

Medan is the location of the First Military Region Command, where Simbolon launched the coup.

Xinhua News Agency, Jakarta, December 25th: Leaders of the Islamic Teachers Association established an emergency executive committee in Jakarta on the evening of the 23rd, headed by Ilyas, the Minister of Religious Affairs in the cabinet.

The committee will carry out the daily tasks of the party's leaders until the party leaders who are attending the party congress in Medan, the capital of North Sumatra, return from there.

### 印度尼西亚政府代表团  前往中苏门答腊调查情况  伊斯兰教师联合会主席说现内阁强固

1956-12-26
第6版()
专栏：

    印度尼西亚政府代表团
    前往中苏门答腊调查情况
    伊斯兰教师联合会主席说现内阁强固
    据新华社雅加达25日电　印度尼西亚中央政府的代表团24日前往中苏门答腊，以便对那里的情况进行实地调查。
    印度尼西亚新闻社24日援引巴东电台的消息说，上星期接管了中苏门答腊的行政权的所谓雄牛师委员会的主席侯赛尼中校表示，他愿意同中央政府、包括来自雅加达的中央政府代表团在内，进行谈判。
    据新华社雅加达25日电　据安塔拉通讯社报道，印度尼西亚伊斯兰教师联合会代表大会仍然按照原定计划于23日晚在棉兰开幕。
    印度尼西亚伊斯兰教师联合会中央委员会主席达兰在致开幕词时说，现内阁是最强固的内阁，而且从来不曾违反它的纲领和普选结果。
    棉兰是第一军区司令部的所在地，西姆波朗就在那个地方发动了政变。
    据新华社雅加达25日电　伊斯兰教师联合会领袖们23日晚在雅加达成立了以内阁宗教事务部长伊利雅斯为首的紧急执行委员会。
    委员会将执行党的领导人物的日常任务，直到正在北苏门答腊首府棉兰参加党代表大会的党的领袖们从那里回来的时候为止。
