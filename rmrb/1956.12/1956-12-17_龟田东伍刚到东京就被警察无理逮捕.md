Title: Kameda Tokuo Arrested Unjustly by Police Upon Arrival in Tokyo
Author:
Date: 1956-12-17
Page: 6

    Xinhua News Agency, Tokyo, December 15th: Kameda Tokuo, Deputy Secretary-General of the Asian and Pacific Regions Peace Liaison Committee and a Japanese peace activist, was unjustly arrested by police last night upon returning to Tokyo's Haneda Airport by plane.

    He was taken to the Hodogaya Police Station in Kanagawa Prefecture for questioning at 11:30 last night and detained. The police station will conduct a formal interrogation today.

    Kaneko Kenta, a member of the Asian and Pacific Regions Peace Liaison Committee who returned to the country on the same flight as Kameda Tokuo, has returned to his home in Tokyo.

### 龟田东伍刚到东京就被警察无理逮捕

1956-12-17
第6版()
专栏：

    龟田东伍刚到东京就被警察无理逮捕
    新华社15日讯　东京消息：亚洲及太平洋区域和平联络委员会副秘书长、日本和平人士龟田东伍在昨天晚上乘飞机回到东京羽田机场后被警察无理逮捕。
    他在昨天晚上十一时半被带到神奈川县保土谷警察署进行了讯问，并且被扣押。警察署将在今天进行正式审讯。
    同龟田东伍同机回国的亚洲及太平洋区域和平联络委员会委员金子健太已经回到他在东京的家里。
