Title: French Parliament Holds Vote of Confidence, Mollet Government Barely Survives
Author:
Date: 1956-12-12
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, Paris, 11th - The French Mollet government received a vote of confidence in the National Assembly on the evening of the 10th regarding the proposed 1957 national budget. The vote tally was 215 to 184. This was the sixth time the Mollet cabinet allowed the parliament to hold a vote of confidence on the budget issue.

Prior to this vote, the parliament had held five votes of confidence on the current cabinet regarding issues such as agricultural machinery and farmer subsidies, increased criminal fines, postal budget, and veterans' appropriations. The veterans' appropriations issue passed with 225 votes to 220, with the government obtaining a dangerously slim majority of five votes.

### 法国议会举行信任投票  摩勒政府侥幸过关

1956-12-12
第6版()
专栏：

    法国议会举行信任投票
    摩勒政府侥幸过关
    据新华社11日讯　巴黎消息：法国摩勒政府10日晚上就它所提出的1957年国家预算在国民议会中得到了信任票，投票比数是二百一十五对一百八十四。这是摩勒内阁让议会在预算问题上进行的第六次信任投票。
    在这次投票以前，议会还分别就农业机器和农户津贴、增加刑事罚款、邮政预算、退伍军人拨款等问题对现内阁举行了五次信任投票。其中退伍军人拨款问题是以二百二十五票对二百二十票通过的，政府仅获得危险的五票的多数。
