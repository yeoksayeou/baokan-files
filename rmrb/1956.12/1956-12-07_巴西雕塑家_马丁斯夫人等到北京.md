Title: Brazilian Sculptor Mrs. Martins Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1956-12-07
Page: 1
　
　Xinhua News Agency, December 4th: Brazilian sculptor Mrs. Martins and two others arrived in Beijing by plane this afternoon. Mrs. Martins is in China for a visit at the invitation of the Chinese People's Institute of Foreign Affairs, after attending the UNESCO conference in New Delhi.

### 巴西雕塑家  马丁斯夫人等到北京

1956-12-07
第1版()
专栏：

    巴西雕塑家
　  马丁斯夫人等到北京
    新华社4日讯　巴西雕塑家马丁斯夫人等三人，今天下午乘飞机到达北京。马丁斯夫人是在参加在新德里召开的联合国教育、科学及文化组织会议以后，应中国人民外交学会的邀请来中国访问的。
