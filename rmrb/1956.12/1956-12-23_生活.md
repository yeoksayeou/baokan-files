Title: Life
Author: Ali·Baluji
Date: 1956-12-23
Page: 8

    Life
    Egypt　Ali·Baluji
    I have experienced the torment of ruthless years, and I have tasted the sweetness of joyful times.
    However, in this world, I have never found anything more precious than free labor.
              (Translated by Zhi Tao from "Selected Egyptian Poems")

### 生活
阿里·巴鲁基
1956-12-23
第8版()
专栏：

    生活
    埃及　阿里·巴鲁基
    我经历过无情岁月的煎熬，我也尝受过欢乐时光的甜蜜。
    但是，在这个世上我从未找到比自由的劳动更可贵的东西。
              （智涛译自“埃及诗选”）
