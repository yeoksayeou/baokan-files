Title: Attempting to Undermine the Security of the Near East: Turkey and the United States Collude

Author: 
Date: 1956-12-24
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, Washington, December 22nd: Turkish Ambassador to the United States, Haydar Görk, held talks on the 21st with U.S. Assistant Secretary of State William Rountree on Near East issues.

According to United Press reports, during the meeting, the Turkish Ambassador requested the U.S. State Department to "continue to pay attention" to the following issues: such as Turkey's hope to establish a connection between the North Atlantic Treaty Organization and the Baghdad Pact, Turkey's views on "the security of the eastern Mediterranean and the Near East," and the question of "whether it is possible" for the United States to participate in the Baghdad Pact, etc.

### 企图破坏近东的安全  土耳其和美国勾勾搭搭

1956-12-24
第5版()
专栏：

    企图破坏近东的安全
    土耳其和美国勾勾搭搭
    据新华社22日讯　华盛顿消息：土耳其驻美大使海达尔·高尔克21日同美国助理国务卿威廉·朗特里就近东问题举行会谈。
    据合众社报道，在这次会谈中，土耳其大使要求美国国务院“继续注意”下面这样一些问题：例如土耳其希望在北大西洋集团与巴格达条约之间建立联系、土耳其对“地中海东部和近东安全”的看法以及美国参加巴格达条约“是否可能”的问题等等。
