Title: Premier Zhou and Vice Premier He Lay Wreaths at Aung San's Tomb
Author:
Date: 1956-12-13
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Rangoon, December 12th: Premier Zhou Enlai and Vice Premier He Long visited Aung San's Mausoleum at noon on the 11th to lay wreaths to this Burmese national hero. U Khin Maung, Chief of Protocol of the Burmese Ministry of Foreign Affairs, accompanied Premier Zhou.

Premier Zhou and Vice Premier He Long had lunch with Prime Minister U Ba Swe at the Burmese Prime Minister's residence on the 11th. Chinese Ambassador Yao Zhongming was also present.

### 周总理和贺副总理向昂山墓献花圈

1956-12-13
第1版()
专栏：

    周总理和贺副总理向昂山墓献花圈
    据新华社仰光12日电　周恩来总理和贺龙副总理在11日中午到昂山陵墓向这位缅甸民族英雄献了花圈。陪同周总理去的有缅甸外交部礼宾司司长吴庆貌。
    周总理和贺龙副总理11日在缅甸总理的官邸同吴巴瑞总理同进午餐。中国大使姚仲明也在座。
