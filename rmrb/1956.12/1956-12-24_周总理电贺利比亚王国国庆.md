Title: Premier Zhou Sends Congratulations on Libyan Kingdom's National Day
Author:
Date: 1956-12-24
Page: 1

Premier Zhou Cables Congratulations on the National Day of the Kingdom of Libya

To His Excellency Mostafa Halim, Prime Minister of the United Kingdom of Libya:

On the occasion of the fifth anniversary of the founding of the United Kingdom of Libya, I extend my warm congratulations to the Government of the United Kingdom of Libya, the Libyan people, and to Your Excellency, on behalf of the Government of the People's Republic of China and the Chinese people, and in my own name.

I am confident that the friendly relations established between our two countries during the Asian-African Conference will be developed further. I wish the United Kingdom of Libya prosperity and the Libyan people happiness.

            Zhou Enlai, Premier of the State Council of the People's Republic of China, December 23, 1956

### 周总理电贺利比亚王国国庆

1956-12-24
第1版()
专栏：

    周总理电贺利比亚王国国庆利比亚联合王国首相摩斯塔法·哈利姆阁下：
    当利比亚联合王国成立五周年的时候，我谨代表中华人民共和国政府和中国人民，并以我个人的名义向利比亚联合王国政府，利比亚人民和阁下表示热烈的祝贺。
    我相信我们两国在亚非会议期间建立起来的友好关系将会获得进一步的发展。祝利比亚联合王国繁荣，利比亚人民幸福。
            中华人民共和国国务院总理　周恩来　1956年12月23日
