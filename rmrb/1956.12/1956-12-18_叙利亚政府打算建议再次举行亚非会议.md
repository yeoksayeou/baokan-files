Title: Syrian Government Plans to Propose Another Asian-African Conference

Author: 
Date: 1956-12-18
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, Cairo, December 17th: According to Cairo Radio, Syrian Prime Minister al-Asali told Cairo Radio reporters on the 16th that the Syrian government plans to propose holding another Asian-African conference soon. He said that Asian and African countries must "permanently eradicate colonialism from Asia and Africa."

### 叙利亚政府打算建议再次举行亚非会议

1956-12-18
第6版()
专栏：

    叙利亚政府打算建议再次举行亚非会议
    新华社17日讯　开罗消息：据开罗电台广播，叙利亚总理阿萨利16日对开罗电台的记者说，叙利亚政府打算建议不久再举行一次亚非国家会议。他说，亚非国家必须“永远铲除亚非两洲的殖民主义”。
