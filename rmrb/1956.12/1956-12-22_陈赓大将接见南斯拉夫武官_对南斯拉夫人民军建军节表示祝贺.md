Title: General Chen Geng Meets with Yugoslav Military Attaché to Congratulate on the Anniversary of the Founding of the Yugoslav People's Army
Author:
Date: 1956-12-22
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, December 21st—Tomorrow, December 22nd, marks the 15th anniversary of the founding of the Yugoslav People's Army. At 3:00 PM today, General Chen Geng, Acting Chief of the General Staff of the Chinese People's Liberation Army, met with Colonel Bruno Vuletic, Military Attaché of the Yugoslav Embassy in China.

During the meeting, General Chen Geng extended his congratulations on the anniversary of the founding of the Yugoslav People's Army. Colonel Vuletic, on behalf of the General Staff of the Yugoslav People's Army and General Vuchkovich, Chief of the General Staff, presented gifts to General Chen Geng.

### 陈赓大将接见南斯拉夫武官  对南斯拉夫人民军建军节表示祝贺

1956-12-22
第1版()
专栏：

    陈赓大将接见南斯拉夫武官
    对南斯拉夫人民军建军节表示祝贺
    新华社21日讯　22日是南斯拉夫人民军十五周年建军节，今天下午三时，中国人民解放军代总参谋长陈赓大将接见了南斯拉夫驻华大使馆武官布鲁诺·武累提奇上校。
    接见时，陈赓大将对南斯拉夫人民军建军节表示祝贺，武累提奇上校代表南斯拉夫人民军总参谋部和总参谋长伏奇科维奇上将向陈赓大将赠送了礼品。
