Title: Japanese Diet Members Haruji Tahara and Yoshio Tomomori Arrive in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1956-12-26
Page: 1

    Japanese Diet members Haruji Tahara and Yoshio Tomomori Arrive in Beijing
    Xinhua News Agency, December 25th: Japanese Socialist Party Diet members Haruji Tahara and Yoshio Tomomori arrived in Beijing by plane today at the invitation of the Chinese People's Institute of Foreign Affairs and the Chinese Medical Association, respectively.

### 日本国会议员田原春次和堂森芳夫抵京

1956-12-26
第1版()
专栏：

    日本国会议员田原春次和堂森芳夫抵京
    新华社25日讯　日本社会党国会议员田原春次和堂森芳夫分别应中国人民外交学会和中华医药会的邀请在今日乘机抵达北京。
