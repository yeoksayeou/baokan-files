Title: Iranian Foreign Minister Says United States Will Join Baghdad Pact
Author:
Date: 1956-12-03
Page: 6

　　Iranian Foreign Minister Says
　　United States Will Join Baghdad Pact
    Xinhua News Agency, December 2nd -- Tehran Dispatch: Iranian Foreign Minister Ali Gholi Ardalan said on December 1st that the United States is about to join the Baghdad Pact.
    He said this after the United States recently issued a statement reaffirming its support for this pact.
    Ardalan said, "Iran deeply appreciates the measures taken by the United States in strengthening and endorsing the Baghdad Pact."

### 伊朗外交大臣说　美国将加入巴格达条约

1956-12-03
第6版()
专栏：

　  伊朗外交大臣说
　　美国将加入巴格达条约
    新华社2日讯　德黑兰消息：伊朗外交大臣阿里·戈利·阿达兰在12月1日说，美国即将加入巴格达条约。
    他是在美国最近发表声明重申支持这个条约以后这样说的。
    阿达兰说，“伊朗深深地感谢美国在加强和赞同巴格达条约方面所采取的措施。”
