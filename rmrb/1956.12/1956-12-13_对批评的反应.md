Date: 1956-12-13

Responses to Criticism

    A letter from the office of the Anshan Municipal People's Committee: The reader's letter "Only 'I' am the exception" in the People's Daily on September 3, which criticized the Anshan Municipal People's Committee for not complying with the system, is basically consistent with the facts. Based on the spirit of this criticism, we have examined and corrected the shortcomings in this regard.

### 对批评的反应

1956-12-13
第4版()
专栏：对批评的反应

    对批评的反应
    安东市人民委员会办公室来信：9月3日人民日报“唯‘我’例外”的读者来信中，批评安东市人民委员会不遵守制度的问题，基本上是符合事实的。我们根据这一批评的精神，检查并纠正这一方面的缺点。
