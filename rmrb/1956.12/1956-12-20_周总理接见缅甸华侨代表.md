Title: Premier Zhou Receives Representatives of Overseas Chinese in Burma
Author:
Date: 1956-12-20
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, Rangoon, December 19th: Premier Zhou Enlai and Vice Premier He Long received thirty-seven representatives of Overseas Chinese from various parts of Burma at the Chinese Embassy in Burma at 5 p.m. on the 18th.

    Xu Simin, a representative of Overseas Chinese in Burma to the National People's Congress, introduced these Overseas Chinese representatives to Premier Zhou Enlai and Vice Premier He Long. Premier Zhou Enlai and Vice Premier He Long cordially inquired about their work and living conditions. The Overseas Chinese representatives thanked Premier Zhou Enlai and Vice Premier He Long for receiving them and for their concern.

### 周总理接见缅甸华侨代表

1956-12-20
第1版()
专栏：

    周总理接见缅甸华侨代表
    新华社仰光19日电　周恩来总理和贺龙副总理18日下午五时在中国驻缅甸大使馆接见了来自缅甸各地的华侨代表三十七人。
    全国人民代表大会缅甸华侨代表徐四民向周恩来总理和贺龙副总理介绍了这些华侨代表。周恩来总理和贺龙副总理亲切地向代表们询问了他们的工作和生活情况。华侨代表们感谢周恩来总理和贺龙副总理的接见和对他们的关怀。
