Title: Egyptian President and Sudanese Prime Minister End Talks Discussing Strengthening Cooperation Between the Two Countries
Author:
Date: 1956-12-18
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, December 17th, TASS dispatch from Cairo, December 16th: The talks between Egyptian President Nasser and Sudanese Prime Minister Khalil in Cairo have concluded.

    According to newspapers, a total of three meetings were held, during which both sides discussed further strengthening cooperation in various fields between the two countries.

    It is reported that President Nasser, on behalf of the Egyptian people, thanked the Sudanese government and people for their support of Egypt in the struggle against the British, French, and Israeli aggression.

### 埃及总统和苏丹总理会谈结束  讨论了加强两国合作问题

1956-12-18
第6版()
专栏：

    埃及总统和苏丹总理会谈结束
    讨论了加强两国合作问题
    新华社17日讯　塔斯社开罗16日讯：埃及总统纳赛尔和苏丹总理哈利勒在开罗的会谈已经结束。
    据报纸报道，会谈一共举行了三次，双方讨论了两国进一步加强各方面合作的问题。
    据报道，纳赛尔总统代表埃及人民感谢苏丹政府和人民在埃及反击英、法、以色列侵略的斗争中对埃及的支持。
