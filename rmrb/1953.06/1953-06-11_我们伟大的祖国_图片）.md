Author:
Date: 1953-06-11
Page: 1

    Our Great Motherland
    In order to transform nature and ensure agricultural production, the people in the northwest of our country, from Yulin in Shaanxi in the east to Youquan in Gansu in the west, along a 3,000-li-long sand control line, are extensively building windbreak and sand-fixing forests. This 3,000-li-long line will gradually be connected into a green "Great Wall." Here, two-year-old willow trees are already eight or nine feet tall and one or two inches thick in stem. Technicians are studying the growth of the trees.

### 我们伟大的祖国(图片）

1953-06-11
第1版()
专栏：

    我们伟大的祖国
    我国西北区东起陕西榆林，西至甘肃酉泉长约三千里防沙线上的人民为了改造自然，保障农业生产，正广泛地营造防风防沙林。这三千里长的沿线将逐渐连接成一座绿的“长城”。这是二年生的柳树已高达八九尺，茎粗一二寸，技术人员们正在研究树木生长的情况。
