Title: Philippine People's Liberation Army Attacks Over 100 Times in May
Author:
Date: 1953-06-05
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency, Manila, 4th. The Quirino authorities in the Philippines admit that the Philippine People's Liberation Army launched one hundred and four attacks in May. According to the puppet army's obviously reduced figures, the People's Liberation Army annihilated forty-four puppet soldiers in May, killing twenty-six and wounding eighteen.

### 菲律宾人民解放军  五月份出击百余次

1953-06-05
第4版()
专栏：

    菲律宾人民解放军
    五月份出击百余次
    【新华社四日讯】马尼拉消息：菲律宾季里诺当局承认，菲律宾人民解放军在五月份出击一百零四次。据傀儡军公布的显然缩小了的数字，人民解放军在五月份共歼灭了四十四名傀儡军，其中打死二十六名，打伤十八名。
