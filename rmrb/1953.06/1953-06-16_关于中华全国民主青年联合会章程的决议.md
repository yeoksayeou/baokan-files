Title: Resolution on the Constitution of the All-China Youth Federation
Author: 
Date: 1953-06-16
Page: 1

The Second National Congress of the All-China Youth unanimously approved the "Report on the Revision of the Constitution of the All-China Federation of Democratic Youth" made by Vice-Secretary-General Qian Weichang on behalf of the Presidium, and unanimously adopted the proposed "Constitution of the All-China Federation of Democratic Youth" as the official constitution of the Federation.

### 关于中华全国民主青年联合会章程的决议

1953-06-16
第1版()
专栏：

    关于中华全国民主青年联合会章程的决议
    中华全国青年第二次代表大会一致同意钱伟长副秘书长代表主席团所作的“关于修改中华全国民主青年联合总会简章的报告”，并一致通过所提的“中华全国民主青年联合会章程”即为本会的正式章程。
