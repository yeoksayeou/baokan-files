    World Peace Council Budapest Meeting
    Presidium of the World Peace Council meeting that opened on June 15, 1953.
    On June 15, 1953, Guo Moruo, Vice-Chairman of the Standing Committee of the World Peace Council and Chairman of the Chinese People's Committee for World Peace, delivered a speech entitled "Korean Armistice and Peaceful Settlement of the Far East Issue" at the World Peace Council meeting.
