Title: Denmark Refuses to Continue Accepting "U.S. Aid"
Author:
Date: 1953-06-20
Page: 4

    Xinhua News Agency, Copenhagen, 18th: According to a Reuters Copenhagen dispatch: The Danish government formally notified the U.S. Embassy in Copenhagen on June 10th that Denmark "no longer needs Marshall Plan aid."

### 丹麦拒绝继续接受“美援”

1953-06-20
第4版()
专栏：

    丹麦拒绝继续接受“美援”
    【新华社十八日讯】据路透社哥本哈根消息：丹麦政府于六月十日正式通知在哥本哈根的美国使馆说，丹麦“不再需要马歇尔计划援助”。
