Title: Swedish Cultural Delegation Leaves Beijing for Home

Author:
Date: 1953-06-11
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, June 10th: The Swedish cultural delegation headed by Karl-Erik Meckquist left Beijing by plane this morning to return home.

Those who went to the airport to see them off included: Zhang Xiruo, President of the Chinese People's Institute of Foreign Affairs, and Sha Kofu, Secretary-General of the All-China Federation of Literary and Art Circles, and others.

### 瑞典文化代表团离京返国

1953-06-11
第4版()
专栏：

    瑞典文化代表团离京返国
    【新华社十日讯】以卡尔—艾力克·梅克奎斯特为首的瑞典文化代表团于十日晨乘飞机离北京返国。
    到机场欢送的有：中国人民外交学会会长张奚若，中华全国文学艺术界联合会秘书长沙可夫等人。
