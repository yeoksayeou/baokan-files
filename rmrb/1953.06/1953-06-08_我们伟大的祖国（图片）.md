    Our Great Motherland
    The Jiangsu Sericulture College, with a history of more than forty years, has undergone a series of reforms since liberation and has adopted advanced Soviet teaching methods, gradually becoming China's first new sericulture college. This is a scene of students feeding silkworms in the silkworm rearing room.

### 我们伟大的祖国（图片）

1953-06-08
第1版()
专栏：

    我们伟大的祖国
    有四十余年历史的江苏蚕丝专科学校，解放后进行了一系列的改革工作，并采用了苏联的先进教学法，已逐步成为我国第一个新的蚕丝专科学校。这是学生们在蚕房里喂蚕的情形。
