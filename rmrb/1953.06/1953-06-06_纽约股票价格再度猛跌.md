Title: New York Stock Prices Plummet Again
Author:
Date: 1953-06-06
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, New York, June 2nd (TASS dispatch): On June 1st, stock prices on the New York Stock Exchange fell sharply again. The prices of many companies' stocks fell by more than one to three dollars. Companies whose stock prices fell included General Motors, Chrysler, and Bethlehem Steel. It is well known that these companies are all engaged in the production of war materials.

### 纽约股票价格再度猛跌

1953-06-06
第4版()
专栏：

    纽约股票价格再度猛跌
    【新华社五日讯】塔斯社纽约二日讯：六月一日纽约股票交易所的股票价格再度激烈下跌。许多公司的股票的价格下跌了一元至三元以上。股票跌价的公司包括通用汽车公司，克莱斯勒汽车公司与伯利恒钢铁公司。大家知道，这些公司都是从事战争物资生产的公司。
