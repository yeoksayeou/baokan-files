Title: Soviet Leninist Young Communist League Delegation Arrives in Beijing to Attend the Second National Congress of the Chinese Communist Youth League; Free German Youth League and Italian Youth Representatives Also Arrive in Beijing One After Another
Author:
Date: 1953-06-19
Page: 1

    Xinhua News Agency, June 18th: The delegation of the Soviet Leninist Young Communist League, invited to China to attend the Second National Congress of the Chinese New Democratic Youth League, arrived in Beijing by plane on the afternoon of the 18th.
    Present at the airport to welcome them were: Hu Yaobang, Li Chang, Rong Gaotang, and Luo Yi, Secretaries of the Central Committee Secretariat of the Chinese New Democratic Youth League; Qu Tangliang, Yang Shu, Zhang Ze, and Hu Keshi, Alternate Secretaries; Kang Nai'er, Secretary-General; Zhang Dazhong, Secretary of the Beijing Municipal Committee of the Youth League; Chen Xuzong, President of "China Youth Daily"; Zhu Yujin, Deputy Director of the China Youth Publishing House; Zhang Zhen, Deputy Director of the Liaison Department of the Sino-Soviet Friendship Association.
    Li Haiqiu, Counselor of the Soviet Embassy in China, Second Secretary Safonov, Ivanov, and A. Slavnov, Vice-Chairman of the Soviet Youth Anti-Fascist Committee, who was invited to China to attend the Second National Congress of the All-China Youth Federation, also welcomed them at the airport.
    The delegation list is as follows:
    Head: V.E. Semichastny (Secretary of the Central Committee of the Soviet Leninist Young Communist League)
    Members: L.A. Chavyalova (Secretary of the Ivanovo Oblast Committee of the Soviet Leninist Young Communist League)
     R.M. Mukimyanova (Secretary of the Central Committee of the Uzbek Republic of the Soviet Leninist Young Communist League)
     K.D. Kenzhebayev (Secretary of the Central Committee of the Kazakh Republic of the Soviet Leninist Young Communist League)
     A.I. Adzhubei (Editor of "Komsomolskaya Pravda")
    Xinhua News Agency, June 16th: Johannes Korsch, representative of the Free German Youth League and member of the Presidium of the Central Committee of the Free German Youth League, and Gerhard Seiler, a youth labor model, arrived in Beijing by plane on the afternoon of the 16th. They will attend the upcoming Second National Congress of the Chinese New Democratic Youth League.
    Liu Daosheng, Secretary of the Central Committee Secretariat of the Chinese New Democratic Youth League, Qu Tangliang, Alternate Secretary and Director of the International Liaison Department, and Wu Xueqian, Deputy Director of the International Liaison Department, went to the airport to welcome them.
    Xinhua News Agency, June 18th: Hugo Pizzoli, Italian youth representative and Secretary of the National Secretariat of the Italian Communist Youth Federation, arrived in Beijing by plane on the afternoon of the 18th, having been invited to China to attend the Second National Congress of the Chinese New Democratic Youth League.
    Yang Shu, Zhang Ze, and Hu Keshi, Alternate Secretaries of the Central Committee Secretariat of the Chinese New Democratic Youth League, and others went to the airport to welcome them. (Attached photo)
    Young Pioneers present flowers to Semichastny, Secretary of the Central Committee of the Soviet Leninist Young Communist League. (Photo by Zou Jiandong, Xinhua News Agency reporter)

### 应邀参加我国青年团第二次全国代表大会  苏联列宁共产主义青年团代表团抵京  自由德国青年联盟和意大利青年代表也先后抵京

1953-06-19
第1版()
专栏：

    应邀参加我国青年团第二次全国代表大会
    苏联列宁共产主义青年团代表团抵京
    自由德国青年联盟和意大利青年代表也先后抵京
    【新华社十八日讯】应邀前来我国参加中国新民主主义青年团第二次全国代表大会的苏联列宁共产主义青年团代表团于十八日下午乘飞机抵达北京。
    到机场欢迎的有：中国新民主主义青年团中央委员会书记处书记胡耀邦、李昌、荣高棠、罗毅，候补书记区棠亮、杨述、章泽、胡克实，秘书长康乃尔，青年团北京市委员会书记张大中，“中国青年报”社社长陈绪宗，中国青年出版社副社长朱语今，中苏友好协会总会联络部副主任张震等。
    苏联驻我国大使馆参赞李海秋，二等秘书萨福隆、伊凡诺夫及应邀前来我国参加中华全国青年第二次代表大会的苏联青年代表、苏联青年反法西斯委员会副主席亚·斯拉夫诺夫也到机场欢迎。
    代表团名单如下：
    团长：弗·埃·谢米察斯特尼（苏联列宁共产主义青年团中央委员会书记）
    团员：勒·阿·查维雅洛娃（苏联列宁共产主义青年团伊凡诺佛州委员会书记）
     赖·姆·穆基姆雅诺娃（苏联列宁共产主义青年团乌兹别克共和国中央委员会书记）
     克·德·肯热巴耶夫（苏联列宁共产主义青年团哈萨克共和国中央委员会书记）
     阿·伊·阿朱别伊（“青年团真理报”编辑）
    【新华社十六日讯】自由德国青年联盟代表、自由德国青年联盟中央委员会主席团委员约翰纳斯·柯希和青年劳动模范盖尔哈特·赛莱尔两人于十六日下午乘飞机到达北京。他们将参加即将召开的中国新民主主义青年团第二次全国代表大会。
    赴机场欢迎的有：中国新民主主义青年团中央委员会书记处书记刘导生、候补书记兼国际联络部部长区棠亮和国际联络部副部长吴学谦等人。
    【新华社十八日讯】应邀前来我国参加中国新民主主义青年团第二次全国代表大会的意大利青年代表、意大利共产主义青年团全国书记处书记雨果·彼齐奥利于十八日下午乘飞机抵达北京。
    前往机场欢迎的有：中国新民主主义青年团中央委员会书记处候补书记杨述、章泽、胡克实等。（附图片）
