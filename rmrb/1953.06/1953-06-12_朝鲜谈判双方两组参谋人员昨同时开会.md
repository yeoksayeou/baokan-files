Title: Korean Negotiation: Both Sides' Two Groups of Staff Officers Met Simultaneously Yesterday
Author:
Date: 1953-06-12
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Kaesong, June 11th: The Korean Armistice Negotiation Delegation of the Chinese and Korean sides issued a communique on June 11th as follows:
    On June 11th, both sides' two groups of staff officers held administrative meetings simultaneously. The two groups of staff officers agreed to continue the meeting at 11:00 AM on the 12th.

### 朝鲜谈判双方两组参谋人员昨同时开会

1953-06-12
第1版()
专栏：

    朝鲜谈判双方两组参谋人员昨同时开会
    【新华社开城十一日电】朝鲜停战谈判朝中代表团六月十一日发表公报如下：
    六月十一日双方两组参谋人员同时举行了行政性会议，两组参谋会议定于十二日上午十一时继续开会。
