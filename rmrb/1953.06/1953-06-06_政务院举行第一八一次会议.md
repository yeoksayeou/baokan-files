Title: Government Administration Council Holds 181st Meeting
Author:
Date: 1953-06-06
Page: 1
    Xinhua News Agency, 5th — The 181st meeting of the Central People's Government Administration Council was held on the afternoon of June 5th. The meeting heard a statement from Rong Zihe, Acting Minister of the Central People's Government Ministry of Finance, regarding instructions on agricultural tax work in 1953, and then approved the "Instructions of the Government Administration Council of the Central People's Government on Agricultural Tax Work in 1953." The meeting also approved various appointment and removal lists.

### 政务院举行第一八一次会议

1953-06-06
第1版()
专栏：

    政务院举行第一八一次会议
    【新华社五日讯】中央人民政府政务院第一百八十一次政务会议在六月五日下午举行。会议听取了中央人民政府财政部戎子和代部长关于一九五三年农业税工作的指示的说明后，通过了“中央人民政府政务院关于一九五三年农业税工作的指示”。会议并通过了各项任免名单。
