Title: Resolution of the Second National Congress of the All-China Youth Federation on the Report on Striving to Defend and Build the Motherland
Author:
Date: 1953-06-16
Page: 1

    The Second National Congress of the All-China Youth Federation, after listening to and discussing Comrade Liao Chengzhi's report "Striving to Defend and Build the Motherland," fully agrees with and supports the content of this report, and calls on the youth of the whole country to unite and resolutely strive to defend the motherland, build the motherland, and safeguard world peace.

### 中华全国青年第二次代表大会  关于为保卫祖国和建设祖国而奋斗的报告的决议

1953-06-16
第1版()
专栏：

    中华全国青年第二次代表大会
    关于为保卫祖国和建设祖国而奋斗的报告的决议
    大会在听取和讨论了廖承志同志“为保卫祖国和建设祖国而奋斗”的报告后，完全同意和拥护这个报告的内容，并号召全国青年，团结一致，坚决为保卫祖国、建设祖国和保卫世界和平而奋斗。
