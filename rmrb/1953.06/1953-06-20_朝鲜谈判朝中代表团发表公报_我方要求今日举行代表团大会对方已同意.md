Author:
Date: 1953-06-20
Page: 1

    Korean Negotiations: Korean-Chinese Delegation Issues Communiqué
    Our Side Requests Delegation Meeting Today, Counterpart Agrees
    [Xinhua News Agency, Kaesong, 19th] The Korean-Chinese delegation to the Korean Armistice Negotiations issued the following communiqué on June 19th:
    On June 19th, our side's chief delegate, General Nam Il, notified the opposing side through our side's chief liaison officer that, due to important matters, our side requested a meeting of both delegations be held at 11:00 AM on June 20th. The opposing side has indicated their agreement.

### 朝鲜谈判朝中代表团发表公报  我方要求今日举行代表团大会对方已同意

1953-06-20
第1版()
专栏：

    朝鲜谈判朝中代表团发表公报
    我方要求今日举行代表团大会对方已同意
    【新华社开城十九日电】朝鲜停战谈判朝中代表团六月十九日发表公报如下：
    六月十九日我方代表团首席代表南日大将经由我方首席联络官通知对方，因有重要事件我方要求在六月二十日上午十一时举行双方代表团大会，对方表示同意。
