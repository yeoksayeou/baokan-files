Title: The U.S. Side Injured Another of Our Captured Personnel
Author:
Date: 1953-06-02
Page: 1

　　Xinhua News Agency, Kaesong, 1st – On May 29th, the U.S. side on Geoje Island, using the pretext of “attempting to escape,” again injured one of our captured personnel.

### 美方又打伤我被俘人员一名

1953-06-02
第1版()
专栏：

    美方又打伤我被俘人员一名
    【新华社开城一日电】五月二十九日，美方在巨济岛又在“企图逃走”的藉口下打伤我方被俘人员一名。
