    Our Great Motherland
    The first phase of the Futzeling Reservoir project on the Huai River has been completed. Currently, five of the eleven sluice gates on the arch dam have been installed. This photo shows Shanghai workers working diligently on the Futzeling Reservoir construction site. They are installing a sluice gate.

### 我们伟大的祖国(图片）

1953-06-19
第1版()
专栏：

    我们伟大的祖国
    淮河佛子岭水库第一期工程已告一段落。现在，连拱坝上的十一道闸门有五道已经安装好了。这是在佛子岭水库工地上辛勤工作的上海工人。他们正在安装一道闸门。
