Title: British Negotiation Representative Holds Reception to Celebrate Queen Elizabeth II's Coronation; Premier Zhou Enlai Sends Telegram to Churchill to Express Congratulations
Author:
Date: 1953-06-03
Page: 1

    British Negotiation Representative Holds Reception to Celebrate Queen Elizabeth II's Coronation
    Premier Zhou Enlai Sends Telegram to Churchill to Express Congratulations
    [Xinhua News Agency, June 2nd] To celebrate the coronation of Queen Elizabeth II of England, the British government's negotiation representative, Mr. Raine, held a reception in Beijing at 5:00 PM on the 2nd.
    Those invited to attend included: Zhang Hanfu, Deputy Minister of Foreign Affairs of the Central People's Government; Wang Bingnan, Director of the General Office; Yan Baohang, Deputy Director; Huan Xiang, Director of the Department of Western European and African Affairs; Wang Zhuoru, Director of the Protocol Department; and Qin Lizhen, Director of the Visa Office; Lin Haiyun, Director of the General Office of the Ministry of Foreign Trade; Lu Xuzhang, Manager of the China Import and Export Company; and Ji Chao Ding, Secretary-General of the China Council for the Promotion of International Trade. Also attending the reception were: Liu Ningyi, head of the Chinese People's Delegation that visited Britain in 1950, and Yuan Yongxi, head of the Student Delegation of the People's Republic of China that visited Britain in 1952, and others.
    Diplomatic envoys of various countries to China were also invited to attend the reception.
    In addition, Premier Zhou Enlai of the State Council of the Central People's Government sent a telegram to Sir Winston Churchill, Prime Minister of the Government of the United Kingdom of Great Britain and Northern Ireland, expressing congratulations.

### 英谈判代表举行招待会庆祝英女王加冕  我政务院总理周恩来电邱吉尔表示祝贺

1953-06-03
第1版()
专栏：

    英谈判代表举行招待会庆祝英女王加冕
    我政务院总理周恩来电邱吉尔表示祝贺
    【新华社二日讯】为庆祝英国女王伊丽莎白二世加冕，英国政府谈判代表蓝来讷于二日下午五时在北京举行招待会。
    应邀参加者有我中央人民政府外交部副部长章汉夫，办公厅主任王炳南，副主任阎宝航，西欧非洲司司长宦乡，交际处处长王倬如，签证处处长秦力真；对外贸易部办公厅主任林海云，中国进出口公司经理卢绪章，中国国际贸易促进委员会秘书长冀朝鼎。参加招待会的还有：一九五○年访问英国的中国人民代表团团长刘宁一，一九五二年访问英国的中华人民共和国学生代表团团长袁永熙等。
    应邀参加招待会的还有各国驻华使节。
    又讯：我中央人民政府政务院总理周恩来曾致电大不列颠及北爱尔兰联合王国政府首相邱吉尔爵士，表示祝贺。
