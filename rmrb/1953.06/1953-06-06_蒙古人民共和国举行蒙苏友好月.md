Title: Mongolian People's Republic Holds Month of Mongolian-Soviet Friendship
Author:
Date: 1953-06-06
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Ulaanbaatar, June 5th: According to TASS, the Central Committee of the Mongolian-Soviet Cultural Association and people's organizations held a conference on June 4th to formally announce the beginning of the Month of Mongolian-Soviet Friendship. The purpose of the Month of Mongolian-Soviet Friendship is to further strengthen the friendship between the people of Mongolia and the Soviet Union, and to widely study and apply the rich experience of the Soviet Union.

The presidium of the conference included Yumjaagiin Tsedenbal, Prime Minister of the Mongolian People's Republic and General Secretary of the Central Committee of the Mongolian People's Revolutionary Party; Damba, Suranzhab, Zhembaldorzh, and Shilin Dib, members of the Political Bureau of the Central Committee of the Mongolian People's Revolutionary Party. Soviet Ambassador to Mongolia G.I. Ivannikov and diplomatic representatives from China, North Korea, and Czechoslovakia also attended the conference. After the Secretary-General of the Central Committee of the Mongolian-Soviet Cultural Association, Dugsuren, delivered the opening speech, everyone stood up to pay respects to the great leader of the working people of the world, Joseph Stalin, and the outstanding leader of the international workers' movement, Klement Gottwald.

The Deputy Prime Minister of the Mongolian People's Republic and Chairman of the Central Committee of the Mongolian-Soviet Cultural Association, Shilin Dib, made a report on "Further Strengthening the Indestructible Brotherly Friendship between the Mongolian People and the Great Soviet People." G.I. Ivannikov also spoke at the conference.

Lectures and reports on the achievements of the Soviet Union in building communism are being held throughout Mongolia, and many exhibitions have been organized. A photo exhibition entitled "The Soviet People Advance to Communism" opened at the "Eldev-Ochir" cinema in Ulaanbaatar. The opening ceremony was attended by the leaders of the Mongolian People's Revolutionary Party and the government, headed by Tsedenbal, as well as diplomatic representatives from the Soviet Union, China, North Korea, and Czechoslovakia. During the opening ceremony, the Vice Chairman of the Central Committee of the Mongolian-Soviet Cultural Association, Sambu, gave a speech on the great friendship between the Mongolian and Soviet peoples.

During the Month of Mongolian-Soviet Friendship, there will be exhibitions of Soviet films, a Soviet Book Week, and a Soviet Culture Week. Dozens of pamphlets written by Soviet advanced producers have been published in Mongolian. In order to learn from the achievements of the Soviet people, Mongolia has established hundreds of Russian language training classes and study groups. Stakhanov schools have been organized in factories of all sizes.

The working people of Mongolia are participating in socialist competitions, mastering the technology of advanced Soviet workers, in order to complete the 1953 national economic plan ahead of schedule, improve labor productivity, improve product quality, and increase the number of livestock, the country's main wealth.

### 蒙古人民共和国举行蒙苏友好月

1953-06-06
第1版()
专栏：

    蒙古人民共和国举行蒙苏友好月
    【新华社五日讯】据塔斯社乌兰巴托讯：六月四日，蒙苏文化协会中央委员会和人民团体主办了一个大会，宣布蒙苏友好月正式开始。蒙苏友好月的目的是进一步加强蒙古和苏联两国人民之间的友谊，广泛学习和应用苏联的丰富经验。
    大会主席团有蒙古人民共和国总理、蒙古人民革命党中央委员会总书记泽登巴尔，蒙古人民革命党中央委员会政治局委员达姆巴、苏兰扎布、热姆巴尔多日和锡林迪布。苏联驻蒙古大使格·伊·伊凡尼科夫和中国、朝鲜、捷克斯洛伐克的外交代表也参加了大会。蒙苏文化协会中央委员会秘书长杜格苏伦致开幕词后，全体起立为全世界劳动人民伟大领袖约·维·斯大林和国际工人运动卓越领袖克利门特·哥特瓦尔德志哀。
    蒙古人民共和国副总理、蒙苏文化协会中央委员会主席锡林迪布作了“关于进一步加强蒙古人民和伟大的苏联人民之间的牢不可破的兄弟友谊”的报告。在大会上发言的还有格·伊·伊凡尼科夫。
    蒙古全国各地正在举办关于苏联建设共产主义所获得的成就的演讲会和报告会，并组织了许多展览会。“苏联人民向共产主义迈进”的图片展览，在乌兰巴托的一个以“爱尔德布·奥齐尔”命名的电影院开幕。参加展览会开幕礼的有以泽登巴尔为首的蒙古人民革命党和政府的首长，苏联、中国、朝鲜、和捷克斯洛伐克的外交代表。在举行开幕礼时，蒙苏文化协会中央委员会副主席桑布就蒙古和苏联两国人民的伟大的友谊发表了演说。
    在蒙苏友好月期间，将举办苏联电影展览、苏联图书周和苏联文化周。苏联先进生产者写的小册子已用蒙古文出版了数十种。为了学习苏联人民的成就，蒙古成立了数以百计的俄语训练班和学习小组。在各大小工厂里都组织了斯大哈诺夫学校。
    蒙古的劳动人民正参加社会主义竞赛，掌握苏联先进工人的技术，以便提前完成一九五三年的国民经济计划，提高劳动生产力，改进产品质量以及增加国家的主要财富——牲畜的头数。
