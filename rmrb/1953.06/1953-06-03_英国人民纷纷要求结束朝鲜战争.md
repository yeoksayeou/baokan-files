Title: British People Widely Demand an End to the Korean War
Author:
Date: 1953-06-03
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, London, May 30th (TASS): Since the Peace Action Congress was held in Manchester on May 17th, messages demanding an end to the Korean War and the holding of a five-power conference have been pouring into the British Peace Committee.

	The Aberdeen Peace Committee has collected 800 signatures for a petition calling for peace in Korea and a five-power conference. The first batch of signatures has been sent to Prime Minister Churchill. The signature collection continues.

	The West Wales and Bedford Peace Committees have launched a petition calling for an end to the Korean War.

	The Halifax Peace Committee is also launching a petition movement demanding the withdrawal of British troops from Korea if the United States continues to obstruct the achievement of an agreement in the armistice negotiations.

	Meetings are being held all over Britain demanding peace and negotiated solutions to international problems.

	Duncan Jones, Secretary-General of the British Peace Committee, said: "It is quite clear that the Manchester Conference has had a great influence in inspiring this action. We are calling for even larger-scale action across the country."

### 英国人民纷纷要求结束朝鲜战争

1953-06-03
第4版()
专栏：

    英国人民纷纷要求结束朝鲜战争
    【新华社讯】塔斯社伦敦三十日讯：自从五月十七日在曼彻斯特举行了和平行动大会以后，要求结束朝鲜战争、要求举行五大国会议的消息纷纷传到英国和平委员会去。
    亚伯丁和平委员会已经为要求在朝鲜实现和平和举行五大国会议的呼吁书征集了八百个签名。第一批签名已经送交邱吉尔首相。征集签名的工作在继续进行中。
    西威尔士和培德福德的和平委员会发动了要求结束朝鲜战争的请愿。
    哈利德克斯和平委员会也在发动一个请愿运动，要求从朝鲜撤退英国军队，如果美国在停战谈判中继续阻挠达成协议的话。
    英国全国各地纷纷集会，要求和平，要求谈判解决国际问题。
    英国和平委员会秘书长邓肯·琼斯说：“十分明显，曼彻斯特大会在鼓舞这种行动方面有很大影响。我们要求在全国有规模更大的行动。”
