Title: Chinese-Soviet Friendship Association Delegation Visits Moscow

Author:
Date: 1953-06-06
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, June 5th: TASS Moscow, June 5th—The Chinese-Soviet Friendship Association delegation to the Soviet Union, headed by Li Furén, is currently visiting Moscow. The guests visited the Kremlin and the city of Moscow, as well as the Tretyakov Art Gallery, the Museum of Technology, and the Ordzhonikidze Machine-Building Plant. They also visited the Tchaikovsky Concert Hall, where they met with I. Moiseyev, the head of the Soviet National Folk Dance Ensemble, and attended a performance of the ensemble's artistic program.

The representatives will also continue to visit other museums and exhibitions in Moscow, and visit the Timiryazev Agricultural Academy.

### 中苏友好协会总会赴苏参观团  在莫斯科参观访问

1953-06-06
第4版()
专栏：

    中苏友好协会总会赴苏参观团
    在莫斯科参观访问
    【新华社五日讯】塔斯社莫斯科五日讯：以李敷仁为首的中苏友好协会总会赴苏参观团，正在莫斯科参观访问。来宾们参观了克里姆林宫和莫斯科的市容，并参观了特列甲科夫艺术陈列馆、技术博物馆和奥尔忠尼启泽机器制造厂。他们还访问了柴可夫斯基音乐厅，在那里和苏联国家歌舞团的负责人伊·莫伊谢耶夫晤谈，并出席了表演会观看歌舞团表演艺术节目。
    代表们还将在莫斯科继续参观其他的博物馆和展览会，并参观季米里雅泽夫农业科学院。
