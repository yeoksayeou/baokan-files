Title: Soviet, British, and Vietnamese Representatives Arrive in Beijing to Attend the Second National Congress of the Chinese Youth

Author:

Date: 1953-06-07
Page: 1

    Xinhua News Agency, 6th - At the invitation of the All-China Federation of Democratic Youth, Soviet representatives, Y. Slavnov, Vice Chairman of the Soviet Youth Anti-Fascist Committee, and M. Khertiev, First Secretary of the Moscow Oblast Committee of the Lenin Communist Youth League, arrived in Beijing by plane on the afternoon of the 6th to attend the Second National Congress of the Chinese Youth. British representative, I. Hall, Vice Chairman of the British Communist Youth League, also arrived on the same plane.
    Those who went to the airport to welcome them included: Wu Han, Secretary-General of the All-China Federation of Democratic Youth; Yu Beichen, Deputy Secretary-General; Liu Daosheng, Luo Yi, Qu Tangliang, Zhang Ze, and Hu Keshi, Secretaries of the Central Committee of the China New Democratic Youth League; Kang Nai'er, Secretary-General; and Wu Xueqian, Deputy Director of the International Liaison Department, among others.
    Vietnamese representatives, Le Duc Chinh, Executive Member of the Central Committee of the Vietnam National Salvation Youth League, and Cao Viet Bao, National Model Worker, also arrived in Beijing on the 4th of this month.

### 参加我全国青年第二次代表大会  苏联、英国和越南代表到达北京

1953-06-07
第1版()
专栏：

    参加我全国青年第二次代表大会
    苏联、英国和越南代表到达北京
    【新华社六日讯】应中华全国民主青年联合总会邀请前来参加中华全国青年第二次代表大会的苏联代表、苏联青年反法西斯委员会副主席亚·斯拉夫诺夫、和列宁共产主义青年团莫斯科州委员会第一书记米·赫尔捷耶夫已于六日下午乘机到达北京。英国代表、英国共产主义青年团副主席伊·哈尔亦随机到达。
    前往机场欢迎的有：中华全国民主青年联合总会秘书长吴晗、副秘书长于北辰、中国新民主主义青年团中央委员会书记处书记刘导生、罗毅、候补书记区棠亮、章泽、胡克实、秘书长康乃尔、国际联络部副部长吴学谦等人。
    越南代表、越南救国青年团中央执行委员黎德整和全国劳动模范高曰保亦于本月四日到京。
