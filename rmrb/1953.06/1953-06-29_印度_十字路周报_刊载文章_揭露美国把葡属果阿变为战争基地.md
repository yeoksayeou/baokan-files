Title: Indian "Crossroads Weekly" Publishes Article Exposing US Transformation of Portuguese Goa into War Base
Author:
Date: 1953-06-29
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, New Delhi, 28th—The latest issue of "Crossroads Weekly" in India published an article entitled "America Turns Goa into a War Base." The article describes the situation of the United States, under the cover of the North Atlantic Treaty Organization's shield, intruding into Portuguese Goa, a territory on the Indian Peninsula. The article states that under the planning of American technicians, a series of military base construction projects are being actively carried out in Goa. The United States has control over Goa's propaganda agency, "Goa Radio Station," and directly transports two important strategic materials produced in the Goa region, iron ore and manganese ore, to the United States and Japan, which is controlled by the United States. The article emphasizes that Goa, under American control, has in fact become one of the American war bases in Asia.

### 印度“十字路周报”刊载文章  揭露美国把葡属果阿变为战争基地

1953-06-29
第4版()
专栏：

    印度“十字路周报”刊载文章
    揭露美国把葡属果阿变为战争基地
    【新华社新德里二十八日电】最近一期“十字路周报”刊载了题为“美国变果阿为战争基地”的文章。文章叙述了美国在北大西洋公约盾牌的掩护下，侵入葡萄牙在印度半岛上的属地果阿的情况。文章说：在美国技术人员的策划下，果阿正积极进行一系列的军事基地建设工作。美国控制了果阿的宣传机构“果阿广播电台”，并且把果阿地区出产的两项重要战略物资铁矿和锰矿直接运到美国和美国控制下的日本。文章着重指出，在美国控制下的果阿，实际上已经变成美国在亚洲的战争基地之一。
