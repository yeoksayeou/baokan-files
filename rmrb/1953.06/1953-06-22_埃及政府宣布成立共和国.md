Title: The Egyptian Government Announces the Establishment of a Republic
Author:
Date: 1953-06-22
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Cairo, June 21st: The Egyptian government announced on the 18th that Egypt had abolished the monarchy and established a republic. The reorganized government is headed by Naguib, the former Prime Minister and Commander-in-Chief of the Armed Forces, who serves as the first President and Prime Minister of the Egyptian Republic. Other key positions that have been appointed are also held by members of the "Revolutionary Committee" led by Naguib, which seized power in a coup last July: Lieutenant Colonel Gamal Abdel Nasser, Vice-Chairman of the "Revolutionary Committee," serves as Deputy Prime Minister and Minister of the Interior; Major General Abdel Hakim Amer, Naguib's former Chief of Staff, serves as Commander-in-Chief of the Armed Forces; Major Salah Salem serves as Minister of National Guidance; and Lieutenant Colonel Abdel Latif Baghdadi of the Air Force serves as Minister of the Army and Navy.

### 埃及政府宣布成立共和国

1953-06-22
第4版()
专栏：

    埃及政府宣布成立共和国
    【新华社二十一日讯】开罗消息：埃及政府十八日宣布，埃及废除君主政体，成立了共和国。改组后的政府由原任首相兼武装部队总司令纳吉布担任埃及共和国第一任总统兼总理，其他已任命的主要职位，也都由去年七月间发动政变从而夺取了政权的以纳吉布为首的“革命委员会”的委员们担任：“革命委员会”副主席加麦尔·阿卜杜勒·纳赛尔中校担任副总理兼内政部部长；原任纳吉布的参谋长阿卜杜勒·哈基姆·阿密尔少校担任武装部队总司令；赛拉·萨勒姆少校担任国家指导部部长；阿卜杜勒·拉迪夫·巴格达迪空军中校担任陆、海军部部长。
