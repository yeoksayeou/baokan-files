Title: Korean Armistice Negotiations Continue with Administrative Meetings
Author:
Date: 1953-06-07
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, Kaesong, 6th—The Korean Armistice Negotiation Korean-Chinese delegation released the following communiqué on June 6th: On June 6th, both sides' delegations continued to hold administrative meetings. The next meeting is scheduled for June 7th at 11:00 AM.

### 朝鲜停战谈判继续举行行政性会议

1953-06-07
第1版()
专栏：

    朝鲜停战谈判继续举行行政性会议
    【新华社开城六日电】朝鲜停战谈判朝中代表团六月六日发表公报如下：六月六日双方代表团继续举行行政性会议，下一次会议定于六月七日上午十一时举行。
