Title: Mongolian Newly Appointed Ambassador to Our Country, Ochirbat, Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1953-06-21
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, June 20th—The newly appointed Ambassador of the Mongolian People's Republic to our country, Ochirbat, arrived in Beijing on the afternoon of the 20th.
　　Those who went to the airport to welcome him were: Zhang Hanfu, Deputy Minister of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs of the Central People's Government, Wang Bingnan, Director of the General Office, Chen Jiakang, Director of the Asian Department, Wang Zhuoru, Director of the Protocol Department, Wu Han, Deputy Mayor of the Beijing Municipal People's Government, Zeng Mei, Deputy Chief of Staff of the Beijing-Tianjin Garrison Command, and others.
　　Foreign envoys and embassy staff stationed in China also went to the airport to welcome him. S. Lkhamsüren, Chargé d'Affaires ad interim of the Embassy of the Mongolian People's Republic, and all members of the embassy also went to welcome him.

### 蒙古新任驻我国大使奥其尔巴特抵京

1953-06-21
第1版()
专栏：

    蒙古新任驻我国大使奥其尔巴特抵京
    【新华社二十日讯】蒙古人民共和国新任驻我国大使奥其尔巴特在二十日下午到达北京。
    前往机场欢迎的有：中央人民政府外交部副部长章汉夫、办公厅主任王炳南、亚洲司司长陈家康、交际处处长王倬如，北京市人民政府副市长吴晗，京津卫戍司令部副参谋长曾美等。
    前往机场欢迎的还有外国驻华使节和使馆人员。蒙古人民共和国大使馆临时代办斯林道尔吉和全体馆员也前往欢迎。
