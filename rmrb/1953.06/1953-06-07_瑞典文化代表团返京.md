Title: Swedish Cultural Delegation Returns to Beijing
Author: 
Date: 1953-06-07
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, 6th. The Swedish Cultural Delegation returned to Beijing by train from Wuhan on the evening of the 6th.

　　In the space of a month, the delegation visited major cities such as Shenyang, Nanjing, Shanghai, Hangzhou, Guangzhou, Changsha, and Wuhan, as well as Shaoshan Township, the hometown of Chairman Mao in Xiangtan, Hunan.

### 瑞典文化代表团返京

1953-06-07
第1版()
专栏：

    瑞典文化代表团返京
    【新华社六日讯】瑞典文化代表团于六日晚间乘火车自武汉返抵北京。
    代表团在一个月的时间内访问了沈阳、南京、上海、杭州、广州、长沙、武汉等各大城市及湖南湘潭毛主席的故乡——韶山乡。
