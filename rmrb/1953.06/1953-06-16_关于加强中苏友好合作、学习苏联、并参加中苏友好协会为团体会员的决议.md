Title: Resolution on Strengthening Sino-Soviet Friendship and Cooperation, Learning from the Soviet Union, and Joining the Sino-Soviet Friendship Association as a Group Member
Author:
Date: 1953-06-16
Page: 1

    Strengthening the friendly cooperation between the Chinese and Soviet peoples and learning from the Soviet Union is a major political task for the entire nation. The Congress unanimously agrees that the All-China Youth Federation and its affiliated organizations should join the Sino-Soviet Friendship Association as group members; and should closely cooperate with the Sino-Soviet Friendship Association, combining it with their central tasks, to vigorously strengthen Sino-Soviet friendship and cooperation and promote the task of learning from the Soviet Union among the youth.

### 关于加强中苏友好合作、学习苏联、并参加中苏友好协会为团体会员的决议

1953-06-16
第1版()
专栏：

    关于加强中苏友好合作、学习苏联、并参加中苏友好协会为团体会员的决议
    加强中苏两国人民的友好合作和学习苏联，是全国人民的重大政治任务。大会一致同意中华全国民主青年联合会及所属各组织参加中苏友好协会为团体会员；并应与中苏友好协会密切配合，结合自己的中心工作，在青年群众中大力加强中苏友好合作和推动学习苏联的任务。
