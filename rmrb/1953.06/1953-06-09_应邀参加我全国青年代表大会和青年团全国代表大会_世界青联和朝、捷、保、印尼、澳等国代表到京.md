Title: Representatives from the World Federation of Democratic Youth and from Korea, Czechoslovakia, Bulgaria, Indonesia, Australia and other countries arrive in Beijing to attend the National Youth Congress and the National Congress of the Communist Youth League of China
Author:
Date: 1953-06-09
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, 8th. Piero Pieralli, Secretary of the World Federation of Democratic Youth, and Marco Zamorano, Chilean Youth Representative to the World Federation of Democratic Youth, arrived in Beijing by plane on the afternoon of the 8th, having been invited to our country to participate in the Second National Congress of the All-China Youth Federation and the Second National Congress of the China New Democratic Youth League. Also arriving on the same flight were Czech youth representatives, namely, Ja. Herna, Secretary of the Central Committee of the Czechoslovak Youth League, and St. Holy, a member of the Central Committee; and Bulgarian youth representatives, namely, Ma. Zaharieva, Secretary of the Bulgarian Dimitrov People's Youth League, and St. Simeonov, Deputy Editor-in-Chief of "People's Youth Daily."

Those who went to the airport to welcome them were: Liu Daosheng, Secretary of the Secretariat of the Central Committee of the China New Democratic Youth League; alternate secretaries Qu Tangliang and Zhang Ze; Secretary-General Kang Niaoer; Wu Han, Secretary-General of the All-China Democratic Youth Federation; Deputy Secretaries-General Qian Weichang and Yu Beichen; Tian Demin, Chairman of the All-China Students' Federation; Sun Fuling, Vice-Chairman of the Beijing Democratic Youth Federation; and Chen Xuzong, President of China Youth Daily, among others.

Korean youth representatives, Li Yan, Vice-Chairman of the Central Committee of the Korean Democratic Youth League, and Zhang Xingjun, Deputy Director of the Democratic Youth League Guidance Department of the General Political Bureau of the Korean People's Army; Indonesian youth representatives, Sukatno, First Secretary of the Indonesian People's Youth League, and Australian youth representatives, K. Smurk, National Chairman of the Australian Eureka Youth League, also arrived in Beijing by train on the 7th of this month.

### 应邀参加我全国青年代表大会和青年团全国代表大会  世界青联和朝、捷、保、印尼、澳等国代表到京

1953-06-09
第1版()
专栏：

    应邀参加我全国青年代表大会和青年团全国代表大会
    世界青联和朝、捷、保、印尼、澳等国代表到京
    【新华社八日讯】应邀前来我国参加中华全国青年第二次代表大会和中国新民主主义青年团第二次全国代表大会的世界民主青年联盟代表、世界民主青年联盟书记皮埃罗·皮埃拉利和驻世界民主青年联盟的智利青年代表马·萨莫拉诺两人已在八日下午乘飞机到达北京。同机到达的还有捷克斯洛伐克青年代表、捷克斯洛伐克青年联盟中央委员会书记雅·赫纳和中央委员斯·霍利，保加利亚青年代表、保加利亚季米特洛夫人民青年联盟书记玛·札哈里耶娃和“人民青年报”副总编辑斯·西米昂诺夫。
    前往机场欢迎的有：中国新民主主义青年团中央委员会书记处书记刘导生，候补书记区棠亮、章泽，秘书长康乃尔、中华全国民主青年联合总会秘书长吴晗，副秘书长钱伟长、于北辰，中华全国学生联合会主席田德民，北京市民主青年联合会副主席孙孚凌，中国青年报社社长陈绪宗等人。
    朝鲜青年代表、朝鲜民主青年同盟中央委员会副委员长李焰和朝鲜人民军总政治局民主青年同盟指导部副部长张性骏，印度尼西亚青年代表、印尼人民青年团第一书记苏卡特诺和澳大利亚青年代表、澳大利亚攸利卡青年团全国主席克·斯马克亦已于本月七日乘火车到达北京。
