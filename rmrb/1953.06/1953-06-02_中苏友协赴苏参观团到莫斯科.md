Title: Sino-Soviet Friendship Association Delegation Arrives in Moscow
Author:
Date: 1953-06-02
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, June 1st: The delegation of the Sino-Soviet Friendship Association, headed by delegation leader Li Furen, arrived in Moscow on the 1st.  Greeting them at the station were the Chairman of the Soviet Society for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries, A. Denisov, Vice Chairman V. Yakovlev, and many representatives from Soviet cultural circles. Also welcoming them was Charge d'Affaires ad interim of the Chinese Embassy in the Soviet Union, Ge Baoquan, and others. The Soviet Society for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries sent N. Yerofeyev, Director of the Oriental Department, to the border to meet the delegation, and he accompanied the delegation to Moscow.

### 中苏友协赴苏参观团到莫斯科

1953-06-02
第1版()
专栏：

    中苏友协赴苏参观团到莫斯科
    【新华社莫斯科一日电】中苏友好协会总会赴苏参观团由李敷仁团长率领一日到达莫斯科。到车站欢迎的有苏联对外文化协会主席亚·杰尼索夫、副主席维·雅科夫列夫和苏联文化界代表多人。欢迎的还有我国驻苏联大使馆临时代办戈宝权等。苏联对外文化协会曾派东方部主任纳·叶罗菲耶夫到边境迎接，他陪同参观团来莫斯科。
