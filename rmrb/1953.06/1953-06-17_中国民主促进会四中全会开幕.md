Title: Fourth Plenary Session of the China Association for Promoting Democracy Opens
Author: 
Date: 1953-06-17
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency report: The Fourth Plenary Session of the Third Central Council of the China Association for Promoting Democracy (CAPD) opened in Beijing on the 16th. The central content of this meeting is to summarize the work experience since the Third Plenary Session of the Third Central Council, discuss and determine the working principles for implementing the country's three major tasks this year, and consider convening the Second National Congress and revising the constitution.

The opening ceremony of the meeting was held at 10:00 am on the 16th. Present were 18 central council members, and those attending as observers included alternate central council members, members of the headquarters' organization, propaganda, and culture and education committees, heads of local organizations, and specially invited representatives, totaling 55 people. Chen Shutong, Vice Chairman of the National Committee of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference (CPPCC), Yu Yifu, Deputy Minister of the United Front Work Department of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of China (CPC), Li Jishen, Chairman of the Revolutionary Committee of the Chinese Kuomintang (RCCK), Zhang Bojun, Vice Chairman of the Central Committee of the China Democratic League (CDL), Huang Yanpei, Chairman of the China Democratic National Construction Association (CDNCA), Huang Qixiang, Secretary-General of the Central Executive Bureau of the Chinese Peasants' and Workers' Democratic Party (CPWDP), Xu Deheng, Chairman of the Jiusan Society, Yan Xichun, representative of the China Zhi Gong Party, and Lin Yun, representative of the Taiwan Democratic Self-Government League (TDSL), were invited to attend the opening ceremony.

Ma Xulun, Chairman of the CAPD, first paid high tribute on behalf of the CAPD to the CPC and the great leader of the Chinese people, Chairman Mao, in his opening speech. Ma Xulun said: Our country is currently facing a new historical period, and the three major central tasks of the country this year are also the current political tasks of the CAPD. This meeting is to unify our ideological understanding on the basis of unity and progress, determine our future working principles according to the requirements of the three major tasks, and better mobilize all members and the masses they are connected with to actively implement the three major tasks with practical actions. Ma Xulun pointed out: The most central task of the CAPD is to actively participate in national construction and consolidate and expand the people's democratic united front. The vast majority of the CAPD members are cultural and educational workers and government employees, mainly middle and primary school teachers and staff. We should further exert our organizational role, help and encourage them to step up their study of Marxism-Leninism and Mao Zedong Thought, step up their study of the advanced experience, science and technology of the Soviet Union, integrate theory with practice, transform their thinking, improve their political and professional level, and give full play to their initiative and creativity, working intensively and accurately to meet the needs of national construction and ensure the completion of the tasks assigned by the state. Chen Shutong, Vice Chairman of the National Committee of the CPPCC, delivered a speech at the meeting. He expressed his warm congratulations on the convening of this meeting. After pointing out the responsibilities of the CAPD in the country's large-scale construction, especially in cultural and educational construction, he encouraged all members of the CAPD to study hard, carry out ideological transformation, and unite with each other, using the weapon of criticism and self-criticism to improve themselves and help others. Li Jishen, Chairman of the RCCK, Zhang Bojun, Vice Chairman of the Central Committee of the CDL, and Huang Yanpei, Chairman of the CDNCA, successively delivered speeches at the meeting. They unanimously pointed out that the members of the CAPD bear certain responsibilities in the country's cultural and educational construction work, and after this meeting, they will be able to mobilize themselves better and drive the masses they are connected with to play their due role in cultural and educational construction.

### 中国民主促进会四中全会开幕

1953-06-17
第1版()
专栏：

    中国民主促进会四中全会开幕
    据新华社讯：中国民主促进会第三届中央理事会第四次全体会议十六日在北京开幕。这次会议的中心内容是总结第三届中央理事会第三次全体会议以来的工作经验，讨论并确定执行国家今年三大任务的工作方针，考虑召开第二次全国代表大会和修改会章问题。
    会议开幕式在十六日上午十时举行。出席的有中央理事十八人，列席的有中央候补理事、总部所属组织、宣传、文教等委员会委员、各地方组织负责人以及特邀代表等共五十五人。中国人民政治协商会议全国委员会副主席陈叔通、中共中央统一战线工作部副部长于毅夫、中国国民党革命委员会主席李济深、中国民主同盟中央委员会副主席章伯钧、中国民主建国会主任委员黄炎培、中国农工民主党中央执行局秘书长黄琪翔、九三学社主席许德珩、中国致公党代表严希纯、台湾民主自治同盟代表林云等应邀出席开幕式。
    中国民主促进会主席马叙伦在致开幕词中首先代表中国民主促进会向中国共产党和中国人民伟大的领袖毛主席致崇高的敬礼。马叙伦说：目前我们国家正面临着一个新的历史时期，今年国家的三大中心任务，也就是民主促进会当前的政治任务。这次会议就是要在团结进步的基础上，统一我们的思想认识，根据三大任务的要求，确定我们今后的工作方针，更好地动员全体会员和所联系的群众，以实际行动积极贯彻三大任务。马叙伦指出：中国民主促进会最中心的任务，就是积极参加国家建设，巩固和扩大人民民主统一战线。民主促进会绝大部分是文教工作者和机关工作者，其中主要是中、小学教职员，我们应当进一步发挥组织作用，帮助和鼓励他们加紧学习马克思列宁主义、毛泽东思想，加紧学习苏联的先进经验和科学技术，结合实际，改造思想，提高政治水平和业务水平，发挥最大的积极性和创造性，紧张地准确地进行工作，以适应国家建设的需要，保证完成国家所赋予的任务。中国人民政治协商会议全国委员会副主席陈叔通在会上致词。他对这次会议的召开表示热烈的祝贺。他指出中国民主促进会在国家大规模建设中特别是文教建设中的责任以后，勉励中国民主促进会的全体会员要认真学习，进行思想改造，并且要互相团结，用批评与自我批评的武器来提高自己，帮助别人。中国国民党革命委员会主席李济深、中国民主同盟中央委员会副主席章伯钧、中国民主建国会主任委员黄炎培相继在会上致词。他们一致指出：中国民主促进会的成员，在国家文教建设工作中均负有一定的责任，在这次会议以后，必能更好地动员起来，并带动其所联系的群众，在文教建设方面发挥应有的作用。
