Title: Oyama Ikuo Arrives in Moscow
Author:
Date: 1953-06-29
Page: 4

    Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, June 28th: TASS, Moscow, reported: Oyama Ikuo, Chairman of the Japanese Peace Supporters Committee, distinguished scientist and political leader, and winner of the Stalin International Peace Prize "For Strengthening International Peace," and his wife, arrived in Moscow on the 27th. He was welcomed at the airport by representatives of Soviet people's organizations.
    Oyama Ikuo said that visiting Moscow has been a long-held dream of mine. Now this dream has come true. The light of Moscow illuminates the path to peace for all countries.

### 大山郁夫到达莫斯科

1953-06-29
第4版()
专栏：

    大山郁夫到达莫斯科
    【新华社二十八日讯】据塔斯社莫斯科讯：日本拥护和平委员会主席、杰出的科学家和政治领袖、“加强国际和平”斯大林国际奖金得奖人大山郁夫和他的夫人，于二十七日到达莫斯科。他在机场上受到苏联人民团体代表的欢迎。
    大山郁夫说，访问莫斯科是我长久以来的梦想。现在这个梦想成为现实了。莫斯科的光芒照亮了所有国家走向和平的大道。
