Title: The Beijing Municipal Committee of the Communist Youth League Holds its Second Congress
Author:
Date: 1953-06-09
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency Report: The Beijing Municipal Committee of the Chinese New Democratic Youth League held its Second Congress from the 5th to the 7th. The meeting summarized the work of the League over the past two and a half years and determined that the task of the Beijing Municipal Committee of the Youth League during the period of national economic construction is to unite the city's youth more closely under the leadership of the Beijing Municipal Committee of the Chinese Communist Party, and actively participate in the country's construction and the struggle to resist U.S. aggression and aid Korea, and defend world peace. To this end, the meeting required the League organizations throughout the city: 1. To educate young people to become conscious builders of the new life of the motherland. 2. Should help young people learn political, cultural, scientific, and technological knowledge, and pay special attention to learning advanced Soviet experience in all aspects.

During the meeting, Peng Zhen, Secretary of the Beijing Municipal Committee of the Chinese Communist Party, and Hu Keshi, Alternate Secretary of the Central Secretariat of the Chinese New Democratic Youth League, attended and gave instructions. Peng Zhen reported to the representatives on the current general tasks of the Party and the state and called on all Youth League members in the city to serve the people unconditionally under the leadership of the Party and to strive to realize the country's industrialization and the gradual transition to socialism.

Finally, the meeting elected the members of the Second Committee of the Beijing Municipal Committee of the Chinese New Democratic Youth League, and elected Hu Yaobang, Zhang Dazhong, and other twelve people as representatives to attend the Second National Congress of the Chinese New Democratic Youth League.

### 青年团北京市委员会  召开第二届代表大会

1953-06-09
第1版()
专栏：

    青年团北京市委员会
    召开第二届代表大会
    据新华社讯：中国新民主主义青年团北京市委员会于五日到七日召开了第二届代表大会。会议总结了两年半来团的工作，确定了在祖国经济建设时期中，北京市青年团的任务是在中国共产党北京市委员会的领导下，更加密切地团结全市青年，积极参加祖国的建设事业和抗美援朝、保卫世界和平的斗争。为此，会议要求全市团的组织：一、要教育青年成为祖国新生活的自觉建设者。二、应当帮助青年学习政治和文化科学技术，特别注意学习苏联各方面的先进经验。
    会议进行期间，中国共产党北京市委员会书记彭真、中国新民主主义青年团中央书记处候补书记胡克实都曾到会指示。彭真向代表们报告了目前党和国家的总的任务，并号召全市青年团员在党的领导下，无条件地为人民服务，为实现国家工业化和逐步过渡到社会主义的任务而奋斗。
    最后，会议选出了中国新民主主义青年团北京市委员会第二届委员会委员，并选举胡耀邦、张大中等十二人为出席中国新民主主义青年团第二次全国代表大会的代表。
