Title: Bolivia Establishes "Anti-Imperialist Youth People's Front"
Author:
Date: 1953-06-05
Page: 4

    Xinhua News Agency, Buenos Aires: TASS reported: The "Anti-Imperialist Youth People's Front" has recently been established in Bolivia. This youth organization calls on all patriotic youth in Latin American countries to fight for independence and against imperialism, and demands that the government nationalize the railways, restore free trade in tin, and implement other measures to ensure Bolivia's economic independence.

### 玻利维亚成立“反帝国主义青年人民阵线”

1953-06-05
第4版()
专栏：

    玻利维亚成立“反帝国主义青年人民阵线”
    【新华社二日讯】塔斯社布宜诺斯艾利斯讯：玻利维亚新近成立了“反帝国主义青年人民阵线”。这个青年组织号召拉丁美洲国家的所有爱国青年进行争取独立和反对帝国主义的斗争，并要求政府把铁路收归国有，恢复锡的自由贸易，以及实行其它确保玻利维亚的经济独立的措施。
