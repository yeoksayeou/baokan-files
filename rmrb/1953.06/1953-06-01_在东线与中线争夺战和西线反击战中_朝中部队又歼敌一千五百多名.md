Title: On the Eastern and Central Fronts' battle for strategic points and the Western Front's counterattack, Korean and Chinese forces eliminated over 1,500 enemy troops
Author: 
Date: 1953-06-01
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Korean Front, May 31st. The Korean and Chinese People's forces continue to achieve new victories in the fierce battle for strategic points on the Eastern and Central Fronts and the counterattack launched on the Western Front. According to preliminary statistics, over 1,560 enemy troops have been eliminated, including American invading forces, the Li Seung-man puppet army, and the British and Turkish mercenary troops.

After capturing several enemy positions east of the Bukhan River and near Kimhwa on the night of the 27th, our forces on the Eastern and Central Fronts engaged in fierce battles for the newly occupied positions. The fighting was most intense on the position south of Chestnut Ridge, southeast of Kimhwa. On the 28th, the weather in this area was terrible, but our heroic artillery and mortar gunners overcame the difficulties of strong winds and heavy fog, and with powerful artillery fire, they coordinated with the infantry to repel twelve consecutive counterattacks by five companies to two battalions of enemy troops. After our army repelled a counterattack by four enemy companies, our observers discovered more than 170 enemy corpses on the southeast side of the position. By 7:00 PM on the 29th, our army had annihilated more than 700 soldiers of the Li Seung-man puppet army in the battle for the hills near Chestnut Ridge and Jiulongli, southwest of Kimhwa. On "Quadrilateral Hill" and the surrounding hills east of the Bukhan River, our army also continuously repelled many frantic counterattacks by the enemy from the 28th to the evening of the 29th, annihilating nearly 500 soldiers of the Li Seung-man puppet army.

On the evening of the 28th, our forces on the Western Front also launched a counterattack in the rain against the three enemy positions on the southeast hill of Maehyun-ri, west hill of Matap-ri, and small south hill of Pyeongchon, northwest of Goranpo, and captured these positions in one fell swoop. ### 在东线与中线争夺战和西线反击战中  朝中部队又歼敌一千五百多名

1953-06-01
第1版()
专栏：

    在东线与中线争夺战和西线反击战中
    朝中部队又歼敌一千五百多名
    【新华社朝鲜前线三十一日电】朝中人民部队在东线和中线激烈争夺战和西线展开的反击战中继续取得新的胜利。据初步统计，被歼敌军又达一千五百六十余名，其中除美国侵略军和李承晚伪军外，还有英国和土耳其的帮凶军。
    东线和中线我军在二十七日夜攻克北汉江以东和金城附近多处敌军阵地以后，即在刚占领的阵地上与敌人展开激烈的争夺战。战斗打得最激烈的是在金城东南栗洞南山的阵地上。二十八日，这一带气候恶劣，英勇的我军重炮和迫击炮手们克服了大风大雾的困难，以强大炮火配合步兵击溃了敌军五个连到两个营的兵力的十二次连续反扑。在我军打退敌军四个连的一次反扑以后，我军观察员就在阵地的东南侧发现了一百七十多具敌军尸体。到二十九日下午七时止，我军在栗洞南山和金城西南九龙里附近的山头争夺战中已歼灭了李承晚伪军七百多名。在北汉江以东“方形山”及其周围山头上，我军自二十八日到二十九日傍晚，也连续打退了敌军许多次疯狂反扑，歼灭李承晚伪军近五百名。
    二十八日傍晚，西线我军也冒雨向高浪浦里西北的梅岘里东南山、马踏里西山和坪村南小山三个敌军阵地发起反击，一举攻克了这些阵地。但直到我军将守山的两个多排土耳其帮凶军全部歼灭以后，美国军队才姗姗赶来，又遭到我军的迎头痛击。在这次战斗中，我军先后毙伤土耳其帮凶军和美国侵略军共二百多名。
    反击坪村南小山的我军部队在不到一小时的激战中，即全部占领敌军地面工事。接着，又攻克敌人据以顽抗的三条坑道，将守敌英联邦师第二十九旅惠灵顿公爵团第四连一百六十多名全部歼灭。然后，我军主动撤离了这个阵地。
