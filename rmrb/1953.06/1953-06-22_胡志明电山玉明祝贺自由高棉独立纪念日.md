Title: Ho Chi Minh Sends Telegram to Son Ngoc Minh to Congratulate Free Khmer Independence Day
Author:
Date: 1953-06-22
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, 21st: According to the Vietnam News Agency, Ho Chi Minh, Chairman of the Government of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam, sent a telegram to Son Ngoc Minh, Chairman of the Central People's Liberation Committee of Khmer, to congratulate the Independence Day of Free Khmer on June 19th.

The telegram said: Under the leadership of Chairman Son Ngoc Minh and the Central People's Liberation Committee of Khmer, the Khmer people have already achieved many glorious victories in the war of resistance. The victory of the Khmer people is also a common victory for the Vietnamese people and the Vietnam-Khmer-Laos People's Alliance in the joint struggle to drive out the common enemy, the French colonialists and American interventionists.

On behalf of the Government of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam and the Vietnamese people, Chairman Ho Chi Minh extended warm congratulations to Chairman Son Ngoc Minh, the Central People's Liberation Committee of Khmer, and the Khmer people.

### 胡志明电山玉明祝贺自由高棉独立纪念日

1953-06-22
第4版()
专栏：

    胡志明电山玉明祝贺自由高棉独立纪念日
    【新华社二十一日讯】据越南通讯社讯：越南民主共和国政府主席胡志明，打电报给高棉中央人民解放委员会主席山玉明，祝贺六月十九日自由高棉独立纪念日。
    电文说：高棉人民在山玉明主席和高棉中央人民解放委员会的领导下，已经在抗战中取得了许多光荣的胜利。高棉人民的胜利，也是越南人民和越南—高棉—寮国人民联盟在驱逐共同敌人法国殖民者和美国干涉者的联合斗争中的共同胜利。
    胡志明主席代表越南民主共和国政府和越南人民，向山玉明主席、高棉中央人民解放委员会和高棉人民致热烈的祝贺。
