Title: India and Bulgaria Sign Trade Agreement
Author:
Date: 1953-06-20
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency, New Delhi, 19th. The Indian government and the trade delegation of the People's Republic of Bulgaria signed a trade agreement in New Delhi on the 17th. This is the first trade agreement between India and Bulgaria. The agreement is effective from June 1953 to the end of 1954.

The agreement stipulates that India will export tea, spices, dyes, volatile oils, castor oil, raw wool, raw cotton, shellac, and electrical equipment to Bulgaria. Bulgaria will export fruits, tiles, calcium carbide, urea, thermocouples, insulating materials, threshing machines, pneumatic hammers, electric motors, universal milling machines, concrete mixers, and other goods to India.

According to this agreement, the two governments are responsible for encouraging direct trade between the two countries as much as possible.

### 印度和保加利亚签订贸易协定

1953-06-20
第4版()
专栏：

    印度和保加利亚签订贸易协定
    【新华社新德里十九日电】印度政府和保加利亚人民共和国贸易代表团于十七日在新德里签订了一项贸易协定。这是印度和保加利亚之间的第一个贸易协定。这个协定的有效期限是从一九五三年六月起到一九五四年年底止。
    协定中规定印度向保加利亚输出茶叶、香料、染料、挥发油、蓖麻油、生羊毛、原棉、虫胶和电工器材等货物。保加利亚向印度输出水果、瓷砖、碳化钙、尿素、热电计、绝缘器材、打谷机、气锤、电动机、万能铣床、混凝土拌搅机等货物。
    根据这项协定，两国政府有责任鼓励两国之间尽量进行直接贸易。
