Title: Our Great Motherland
Author:
Date: 1953-06-25
Page: 1

    Our Great Motherland
    In the struggle to jointly oppose aggression, defend their homelands, and defend peace with the Korean people, the Chinese people have increasingly consolidated the fighting friendship forged with blood and flesh. This is a situation on a certain coast in Korea where commanders of the Chinese People's Volunteers and the Korean People's Army are jointly deploying coastal defense.

### 我们伟大的祖国

1953-06-25
第1版()
专栏：

    我们伟大的祖国
    中国人民在和朝鲜人民共同反对侵略、保卫祖国、保卫和平的斗争中，日益巩固了用血肉结成的战斗友谊。这是在朝鲜的某个海岸，中国人民志愿军和朝鲜人民军的指挥员正在共同部署海防的情况。
