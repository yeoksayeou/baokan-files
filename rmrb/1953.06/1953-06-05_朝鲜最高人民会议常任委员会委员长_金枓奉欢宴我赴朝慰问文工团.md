Title: Kim Tu-bong, Chairman of the Standing Committee of the Supreme People's Assembly of North Korea, Hosts a Banquet for the Chinese Art Troupe Visiting North Korea to Express Sympathy

Author:

Date: 1953-06-05
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Pyongyang, June 4th. Kim Tu-bong, Chairman of the Standing Committee of the Supreme People's Assembly of North Korea, held a banquet in Pyongyang on the evening of the 1st to receive all members of the first delegation of the Chinese People's Committee to Resist America and Aid Korea Art Troupe.

Korean officials accompanying the delegation included: Vice Premiers of the North Korean Cabinet, Heo Ga-i, Choi Chang-ik, and Jeong Il-ryong; Secretaries of the Central Committee of the Korean Workers' Party, Park Jeong-ae and Park Chang-ok, as well as cabinet ministers, senior generals and combat heroes of the Korean People's Army, and leaders of democratic parties and social organizations, and artists.

Soviet, Chinese, and other people's democratic countries' ambassadors and embassy staff in North Korea were also invited to attend.

Chairman Kim Tu-bong said at the beginning of the banquet: "In my own name and on behalf of the Standing Committee of the Supreme People's Assembly of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea, I warmly welcome the comrades of the Art Troupe. The Korean people see with infinite gratitude and joy the noble art of the great Chinese people performed by the comrades. The work of the comrades has made a great contribution to promoting the splendid cultural exchange between the people of Korea and China." He concluded by proposing a toast to the health of Chairman Mao Zedong.

Zhang Yunxi, head of the first delegation of the Art Troupe, said in his speech: "The friendship between the people of China and North Korea, forged in blood, is higher than the mountains and deeper than the sea. Our artists are willing to do everything in their power to further consolidate the friendship between the two peoples and defend lasting peace in Asia and the world." He raised his glass to wish the Korean people greater victories and Marshal Kim Il-sung good health.

During the banquet, the hosts and guests repeatedly raised their glasses to the internationalist unity of the peace and democracy camp led by the Soviet Union, and to the Chinese People's Volunteers and the heroic Korean people.

Xinhua News Agency, Pyongyang, June 3rd. The first delegation of the Chinese People's Committee to Resist America and Aid Korea Art Troupe, led by head Zhang Yunxi, arrived in Pyongyang from the Korean front on the morning of May 29 to perform for the Korean people and the officers and soldiers of the Korean People's Army. Under the auspices of the North Korean Cabinet's Ministry of Culture and Propaganda, the Art Troupe performed five shows continuously from May 29 to June 1, each including wonderful programs such as singing, music, dance, acrobatics, magic, and Peking Opera, and received a warm welcome and praise from all walks of life in North Korea. The delegation left Pyongyang on the 2nd.

When the Art Troupe performed on the evening of May 30, Kim Tu-bong, Chairman of the Standing Committee of the Supreme People's Assembly of North Korea, Vice Premiers of the Cabinet Heo Ga-i and Choi Chang-ik, Secretaries of the Central Committee of the Korean Workers' Party Park Jeong-ae and Park Chang-ok, as well as senior generals of the Korean People's Army, leaders of various democratic parties and social organizations, and representatives from all walks of life in Pyongyang attended the performance. Also in attendance were Soviet, Chinese, and other people's democratic countries' ambassadors and embassy staff in North Korea. Before the performance that day, a welcoming ceremony was held.

Ki Seok-bok, Vice Minister of Culture and Propaganda of North Korea, delivered a welcoming speech at the ceremony. He said: "From the Art Troupe and the Comfort Delegation comrades sent by our Chinese brothers, we deeply feel the love of our 500 million Chinese brothers for us. Therefore, we feel even more deeply how profound the sincere concern of Chairman Mao Zedong, the beloved leader of the Chinese people, is for our Korean people; this further strengthens our confidence in victory and our fighting will." Head Zhang Yunxi delivered a reply. He expressed his respect to the heroic Korean people. He said: "Through the cultural exchanges between our two countries, the fighting friendship between the people of our two countries will be further strengthened."

Korean artists and representatives of workers and women in the Pyongyang area presented flags and flowers to the Chinese People's Committee to Resist America and Aid Korea Art Troupe. The Art Troupe also presented flags to the Korean people as a tribute. The welcoming ceremony was filled with an atmosphere of unity and friendship.

### 朝鲜最高人民会议常任委员会委员长  金枓奉欢宴我赴朝慰问文工团

1953-06-05
第1版()
专栏：

    朝鲜最高人民会议常任委员会委员长
    金枓奉欢宴我赴朝慰问文工团
    【新华社平壤四日电】朝鲜最高人民会议常任委员会委员长金枓奉，一日晚在平壤市举行宴会，招待中国人民抗美援朝总会赴朝慰问文艺工作团第一团全体人员。
    朝鲜方面出席作陪的有：朝鲜内阁副首相许嘉谊、崔昌益、郑一龙，朝鲜劳动党中央委员会书记朴正爱、朴昌玉，以及内阁各相，朝鲜人民军高级将领和战斗英雄，各民主政党和社会团体的负责人、文艺工作者等。
    苏联、中国和各人民民主国家驻朝使节和使馆人员也应邀参加。
    金枓奉委员长在宴会开始时说：我以朝鲜民主主义人民共和国最高人民会议常任委员会和我个人的名义，热烈地欢迎文艺工作团的各位同志。朝鲜人民以无限的感激和欢欣，看到同志们演出伟大的中国人民的高贵艺术。同志们的工作对于促进朝中两国人民灿烂的文化交流作了很大的贡献。他最后建议为毛泽东主席的健康而干杯。
    文艺工作团第一团团长张云溪致词说：中朝两国人民以鲜血结成的友谊比山更高，比海更深。我们文艺工作者愿以一切力量促使两国人民的友谊更加巩固，保卫亚洲和世界的持久和平。他举杯祝朝鲜人民获得更大的胜利，祝金日成元帅健康。
    席间，宾主一再为以苏联为首的和平民主阵营的国际主义团结，为中国人民志愿军和英雄的朝鲜人民举杯。
    【新华社平壤三日电】中国人民抗美援朝总会赴朝慰问文艺工作团第一团在团长张云溪率领下，在五月二十九日晨从朝鲜前线到达平壤，向朝鲜人民和朝鲜人民军将士进行慰问演出。在朝鲜内阁文化宣传省的主持下，文艺工作团从五月二十九日到六月一日的四天中，连续演出五场，每场都包括歌唱、音乐、舞蹈、杂技、魔术、京剧等精彩节目，受到朝鲜各界热烈的欢迎和赞扬。该团已于二日离开平壤。
    文艺工作团在五月三十日晚间演出时，朝鲜最高人民会议常任委员会委员长金枓奉，内阁副首相许嘉谊、崔昌益，朝鲜劳动党中央委员会书记朴正爱、朴昌玉等，以及朝鲜人民军高级将领、各民主党派和社会团体的负责人、平壤市各界人民代表都到场观看。到会的还有苏联、中国和各人民民主国家驻朝使节和使馆工作人员。在这一天的演出开始前，举行了欢迎会。
    朝鲜文化宣传省副相奇石福在欢迎会上致欢迎词。他说：我们从中国兄弟们派来的文艺工作团和慰问团同志们的身上，深深地感受到中国五万万兄弟们对我们的热爱。因此，我们更深地感到了中国人民敬爱的领袖毛泽东主席对我们朝鲜人民诚挚的关怀是多么深厚；这也更加增强了我们的胜利信心和斗争意志。团长张云溪致答词。他向英勇的朝鲜人民表示敬意。他说：通过我们两国的文化交流，两国人民的战斗友谊必将进一步加强。
    朝鲜艺术工作者和平壤地区的劳动人民代表、妇女代表在会上向中国人民赴朝慰问文艺工作团献旗献花。赴朝慰问文艺工作团也向朝鲜人民献旗致敬。欢迎会充满了团结友爱的气氛。
