Title: First Batch of Specific Barter Contracts Signed; Majority of Members of French Trade Delegation Return Home
Author:
Date: 1953-06-18
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, 17th. To implement the Sino-French trade agreement signed on June 5th, the China National Import and Export Corporation and various export corporations, after eleven days of intense work with the French industrial and commercial trade delegation, signed the first batch of specific barter contracts, totaling more than 110, on the 16th. The total value of imports and exports on both sides is 8.2 million pounds each (to be paid in Francs).

On the 17th, the majority of the members of the French industrial and commercial trade delegation, led by head of delegation Depra and deputy head of delegation Dumont, left Beijing by plane to return to their country. Those who came to the airport to see them off included Ji Chao-ding, Secretary-General of the China Council for the Promotion of International Trade; Xie Xiao Nai, Director of the Liaison Department; Lu Xu-zhang, Manager of the China National Import and Export Corporation; Cao Zhong-shu and Li Ying-ji, Deputy Managers; and Zhan Wu, Deputy Manager of the Bank of China.

### 签订了第一批具体易货合同  法贸易代表团大部团员返国

1953-06-18
第1版()
专栏：

    签订了第一批具体易货合同
    法贸易代表团大部团员返国
    【新华社十七日讯】为执行六月五日签订的中法贸易协定，中国进出口公司及各出口公司与法国工商业贸易代表团经过十一天紧张工作，已于十六日签订了第一批具体易货合同一百一十余份。双方进出口总值各八百二十万英镑（以法郎付款）。
    十七日，法国工商业贸易代表团大部分团员在团长德普拉、副团长杜孟率领下，乘飞机离北京返国。到机场欢送的，有中国国际贸易促进委员会秘书长冀朝鼎、联络部部长谢筱迺，中国进出口公司经理卢绪章、副经理曹中枢、李应吉，中国银行副经理詹武等人。
