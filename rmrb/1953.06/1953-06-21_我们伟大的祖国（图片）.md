Author:
Date: 1953-06-21
Page: 1

    Our Great Motherland
    The China Welfare Institute Children's Theater Troupe was founded by Mr. Soong Ching Ling. This troupe has 79 members, with an average age of 16. Their performances are enthusiastically welcomed by the children of Shanghai. This is the 14-year-old violinist Huang Weier and the 13-year-old young pianist Zheng Ming. They are performing a song together.

### 我们伟大的祖国（图片）

1953-06-21
第1版()
专栏：

    我们伟大的祖国
    中国福利会儿童剧团是宋庆龄先生创办的。这个剧团有团员七十九人，他们的平均年龄是十六岁。他们演出的节目，受到上海儿童们热烈的欢迎。这是十四岁的小提琴手黄伟儿和十三岁的少年钢琴家郑明。他们正在合奏歌曲。
