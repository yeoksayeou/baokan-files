Title: Tibetan Representatives Arrive in Beijing to Attend the Inaugural Meeting of the Chinese Buddhist Association

Author:

Date: 1953-06-03
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency:

    Eleven representatives from Tibet, led by Gendun Delek Rinpoche (Gendun Delek Hutuktu), arrived in Beijing on May 29th after departing from Lhasa on April 3rd and flying from Chongqing. Those who welcomed them at the airport included He Chengxiang, Director of the Religious Affairs Department of the Cultural and Educational Committee of the Government Administration Council; Zhao Puchu, Director of the Preparatory Office of the Chinese Buddhist Association; and initiators of the Chinese Buddhist Association such as Galasa, Lobsang Basang, Neng Hai, Jujan, Zhou Shujia, and Guo Peng; as well as Ranba, Director of the Dalai Lama's Office in Beijing; Luowang, Acting Director of the Panchen Lama's Office in Beijing; and more than 30 Tibetan representatives who came to Beijing to attend the inaugural meeting of the Chinese Buddhist Association, including Awang Gyatso, Vice Chairman of the Tibetan Autonomous Region People's Government of Xikang Province; Xia Ricang Living Buddha, a member of the Qinghai Provincial Consultative Committee; and Ulan Living Buddha, Abbot of Qinghai's Ta'er Monastery. Representatives from the State Ethnic Affairs Commission also went to welcome them.

    Before the eleven representatives from Tibet departed from Lhasa, the headquarters of the Chinese People's Liberation Army's Tibet Military Region held a banquet to see them off. The Dalai Lama also met with them, pointing out that the establishment of the Chinese Buddhist Association is a great joyous event for the Chinese Buddhist community and encouraging them to hold a successful meeting.

### 出席中国佛教协会成立大会  西藏代表十一人到达北京

1953-06-03
第1版()
专栏：

    出席中国佛教协会成立大会
    西藏代表十一人到达北京
    【新华社讯】西藏出席中国佛教协会成立大会的代表十一人，在公德林·晋美吉村（公德林呼图克图）的率领下，于四月三日从拉萨出发，五月二十九日自重庆飞抵北京。到机场欢迎的有政务院文化教育委员会宗教事务处处长何成湘，中国佛教协会筹备处主任赵朴初，中国佛教协会发起人噶喇藏、罗桑巴桑、能海、巨赞、周叔迦、郭朋，达赖喇嘛驻京办事处处长然巴，班禅驻京办事处代处长罗旺以及来京出席中国佛教协会成立大会的藏族代表：西康省藏族自治区人民政府副主席阿旺嘉错、青海省协商委员会委员夏日仓活佛、青海塔尔寺法台乌兰活佛等三十多人。中央民族事务委员会亦派代表前往欢迎。
    西藏的十一位代表从拉萨出发前，中国人民解放军西藏军区司令部曾设宴欢送。达赖喇嘛并召见他们，指出中国佛教协会的成立是中国佛教界的一件大喜事，勉励他们开好会议。
