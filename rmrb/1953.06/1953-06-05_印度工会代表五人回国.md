Title: Five Indian Trade Union Representatives Return Home
Author: 
Date: 1953-06-05
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Guangzhou, June 4th: Five Indian trade union representatives, Yousuf, Satyapriya Banerjee, Vitalrao, Sudin Kumar Pramanik, and Trivedi Parmanand, who were invited to our country to participate in the "May Day" International Labor Day celebrations and the Seventh National Congress of the Trade Union of China, left Guangzhou at 9:00 AM on the 4th, via Hong Kong, to return to their country. More than 30 people, including Wu Jiemin, Vice Chairman of the Guangzhou Municipal Federation of Trade Unions, went to the station to see them off.

The representatives visited revolutionary sites such as the Guangzhou Commune, the Peasant Movement Training Institute, Sanyuanli, and Huanghuagang in Guangzhou, as well as railway worker dormitories and clubs. They also held symposiums with judicial and educational workers respectively.

### 印度工会代表五人回国

1953-06-05
第4版()
专栏：

    印度工会代表五人回国
    【新华社广州四日电】应邀前来我国参加“五一”国际劳动节庆祝典礼和中国工会第七次全国代表大会的印度工会代表优索福、萨蒂亚普里雅·班纳吉、维塔拉奥、苏丁·库马尔·普拉马尼克、特里维迪·帕马南德等一行五人在四日上午九时离广州取道香港返国。到车站欢送的有广州市总工会副主席吴介民等三十多人。
    代表们在广州曾参观了广州公社、农民讲习所、三元里、黄花岗等革命故迹以及铁路工人宿舍和俱乐部，还分别和司法、教育工作者举行了座谈会。
