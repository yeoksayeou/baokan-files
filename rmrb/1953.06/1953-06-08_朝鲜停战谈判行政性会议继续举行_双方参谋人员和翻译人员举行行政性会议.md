Title: Continued Administrative Meeting of Korean Armistice Negotiations; Staff and Translators from Both Sides Hold Administrative Meeting
Author:
Date: 1953-06-08
Page: 1

    Xinhua News Agency, Kaesong, July 7th: The Korean-Chinese delegation to the Korean Armistice Negotiations issued the following communiqué on June 7th:
    At 11:00 AM on June 7th, the two delegations continued to hold an administrative meeting. The next meeting is scheduled for 11:00 AM on June 8th.
    On the afternoon of June 7th, staff members and translators from both sides held administrative meetings successively.

### 朝鲜停战谈判行政性会议继续举行  双方参谋人员和翻译人员举行行政性会议

1953-06-08
第1版()
专栏：

    朝鲜停战谈判行政性会议继续举行
    双方参谋人员和翻译人员举行行政性会议
    【新华社开城七日电】朝鲜停战谈判朝中代表团六月七日发表公报如下：
    六月七日上午十一时双方代表团继续举行行政性会议，下一次会议定于六月八日上午十一时举行。
    六月七日下午双方参谋人员和翻译人员先后举行行政性会议。
