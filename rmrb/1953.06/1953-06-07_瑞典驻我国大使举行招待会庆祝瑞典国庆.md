Title: Swedish Ambassador to China Holds Reception to Celebrate Swedish National Day
Author: 
Date: 1953-06-07
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, June 6th: The Swedish Ambassador to China, Mr. Wistrand, held a reception in Beijing at 7:00 PM on the 6th to celebrate Swedish National Day. Those invited to attend the reception included: Zhang Hanfu, Vice Minister of Foreign Affairs of the Central People's Government; Lei Renmin, Vice Minister of Foreign Trade; Ding Xilin, Vice Minister of Culture; and Wu Han, Deputy Mayor of the Beijing Municipal People's Government.

Diplomatic envoys from various countries to China were also invited to attend the reception.

The reception proceeded in a friendly atmosphere.

### 瑞典驻我国大使举行招待会庆祝瑞典国庆

1953-06-07
第1版()
专栏：

    瑞典驻我国大使举行招待会庆祝瑞典国庆
    【新华社六日讯】瑞典驻我国大使魏斯特朗于六日下午七时在北京举行招待会庆祝瑞典国庆日。应邀出席招待会的有：中央人民政府外交部副部长章汉夫，对外贸易部副部长雷任民，文化部副部长丁西林，北京市人民政府副市长吴晗等。
    各国驻我国外交使节也应邀出席了招待会。
    招待会在友好的气氛中进行。
