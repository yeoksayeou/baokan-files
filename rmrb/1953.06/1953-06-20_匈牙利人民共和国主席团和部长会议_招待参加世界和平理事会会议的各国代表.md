Title: Presidium and Council of Ministers of the Hungarian People's Republic Host Representatives of Various Countries Attending the World Peace Council Meeting
Author:
Date: 1953-06-20
Page: 1
　　
    Xinhua News Agency, Budapest, 19th: The Presidium of the Hungarian People's Republic and the Council of Ministers of the Hungarian People's Republic held a grand reception in the National Assembly Building on the evening of the 18th to host representatives from various countries attending the World Peace Council meeting held here.
    István Dobi, Chairman of the Presidium of the Hungarian People's Republic, and Ernő Gerő and Imre Nagy, Deputy Chairmen of the Hungarian Council of Ministers, personally attended the reception for the guests.
    Council members of the World Peace Council and representatives invited to attend the meeting participated in the reception.

### 匈牙利人民共和国主席团和部长会议  招待参加世界和平理事会会议的各国代表

1953-06-20
第1版()
专栏：

    匈牙利人民共和国主席团和部长会议
    招待参加世界和平理事会会议的各国代表
    【新华社十九日讯】塔斯社布达佩斯十九日讯：匈牙利人民共和国主席团和匈牙利人民共和国部长会议于十八日晚在国民议会大厦举行盛大招待会，招待出席在此间举行的世界和平理事会会议的各国代表。
    匈牙利人民共和国主席团主席伊斯特万·道比、匈牙利部长会议副主席埃诺·格罗和伊姆雷·纳吉亲自出席招待来宾。
    参加招待会的有世界和平理事会理事和应邀参加这次会议的代表。
