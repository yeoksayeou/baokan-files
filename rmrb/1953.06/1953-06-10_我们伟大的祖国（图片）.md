    Our Great Motherland
    The "Michurin Agriculture Plant Selection and Breeding Training Class" organized by the Ministry of Agriculture of the Central People's Government has gathered agricultural scientists from all over the country to systematically teach Michurin's advanced agricultural scientific theories and methods. This is a scene of Soviet expert Ivanov giving a lecture to the trainees at the training class upon invitation.

### 我们伟大的祖国（图片）

1953-06-10
第1版()
专栏：

    我们伟大的祖国
    中央人民政府农业部举办的“米丘林农业植物选种及良种繁育讲习班”，集中了全国各地的农业科学工作者，系统地讲授米丘林先进的农业科学理论和方法。这是苏联专家依万诺夫应邀向讲习班学员们讲演的情形。
