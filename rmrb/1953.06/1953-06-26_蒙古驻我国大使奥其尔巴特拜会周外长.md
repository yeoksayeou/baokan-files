Title: Mongolian Ambassador to China, Ochirbat, calls on Foreign Minister Zhou

Author: 
Date: 1953-06-26
Page: 1

    Xinhua News Agency, June 25th: Ochirbat, the newly appointed Ambassador Extraordinary and Plenipotentiary of the Mongolian People's Republic to China, accompanied by Second Secretary Ailin Qingsu Nuomu, paid a visit to Foreign Minister Zhou Enlai at the Ministry of Foreign Affairs on the afternoon of the 25th to discuss matters relating to the presentation of his credentials. Also present were Wang Bingnan, Director of the General Office of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs, Chen Jiakang, Director of the Asian Department, and Wang Zhuoru, Director of the Protocol Department.

### 蒙古驻我国大使奥其尔巴特拜会周外长

1953-06-26
第1版()
专栏：

    蒙古驻我国大使奥其尔巴特拜会周外长
    【新华社二十五日讯】蒙古人民共和国新任驻我国特命全权大使奥其尔巴特，在二十五日下午，由二等秘书艾林清苏诺木陪同，到外交部拜会周恩来部长，商谈呈递国书事宜。在座的还有：外交部办公厅主任王炳南、亚洲司司长陈家康和交际处处长王倬如。
