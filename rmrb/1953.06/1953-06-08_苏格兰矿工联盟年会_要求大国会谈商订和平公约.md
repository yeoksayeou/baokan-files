Title: Scottish Mineworkers' Union Annual Meeting Demands Great Power Talks to Negotiate Peace Pact
Author:
Date: 1953-06-08
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency, London, June 7th (TASS dispatch): The Scottish Mineworkers' Union held its annual meeting in Aberdeen, Scotland, and passed a resolution expressing the miners' desire for peace.

    Union Chairman, Abe Moffat, in his speech, called for a Great Power conference to negotiate a peace pact.

    One of the resolutions calls for a significant reduction in military spending. The meeting also unanimously passed a resolution demanding a reduction in the term of compulsory military service.

    Moffat, in supporting the resolution demanding increased wages, spoke about the issue of foreign trade. He pointed out that the reduction in British coal exports is due to rearmament and the trade restrictions imposed on Britain by the United States.

### 苏格兰矿工联盟年会  要求大国会谈商订和平公约

1953-06-08
第4版()
专栏：

    苏格兰矿工联盟年会
    要求大国会谈商订和平公约
    【新华社七日讯】塔斯社伦敦六日讯：苏格兰矿工联盟在苏格兰亚伯丁地方举行年会，通过了表示矿工和平愿望的决议。
    联盟主席阿比·莫法特在演说中要求举行大国会议商订和平公约。
    决议之一要求大量削减军备开支。会议并一致通过要求缩短强迫军役期限的决议。
    莫法特在支持要求增加工资的决议时谈到对外贸易问题。他指出：英国出口煤的减少，是由于重整军备和美国加于英国的贸易限制。
