Title: Massive Rallies Held in Thirty-Six French Provinces, Demanding an End to the War and a Peaceful Resolution to the German Question
Author:
Date: 1953-06-25
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, June 23rd dispatch from Paris (TASS): According to a report in the French newspaper "l'Humanité": Under the slogans of striving for the cessation of the Korean and Indochina wars and striving for a peaceful resolution of the German question, thirty-six French provinces held grand rallies on the 21st. Altogether, tens of thousands of people participated in the rallies.

    In Vence-Loubet, Alpes-Maritimes province, farmers held a 4,000-person rally. Waldeck Rochet, a member of the Political Bureau of the Central Committee of the French Communist Party, spoke at the meeting, calling on farmers to strengthen the struggle to defend peace and French agriculture. His speech was warmly welcomed by the meeting.

### 法国三十六省好几万人分别举行盛大集会  要求停止战争及和平解决德国问题

1953-06-25
第4版()
专栏：

    法国三十六省好几万人分别举行盛大集会
    要求停止战争及和平解决德国问题
    【新华社二十三日讯】据塔斯社巴黎讯：法国“人道报”报道：在争取停止朝鲜和印度支那战争、争取和平解决德国问题的口号下，法国三十六省在二十一日分别举行了盛大集会。参加集会的，一共有好几万人。
    在阿尔卑斯海滨省的维聂夫—卢别，农民们举行了四千人的大会。法国共产党中央委员会政治局委员瓦德克·罗歇在会上演说，号召农民们加强保卫和平和法国农业的斗争。他的演说受到大会的热烈欢迎。
