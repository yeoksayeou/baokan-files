Title: Pyongyang City Commemorates the Third Anniversary of the Fatherland Liberation War with a Report Meeting, Sends a Telegram of Respect to Chairman Mao Zedong
Author:
Date: 1953-06-27
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Pyongyang, June 26th. The Pyongyang City commemorative report meeting for the third anniversary of the Fatherland Liberation War sent a telegram of respect to Chairman Mao Zedong. The telegram stated: As the entire Korean people welcome the third anniversary of the just war waged to defend the freedom and independence of their fatherland and peace in Asia and the world, against the armed aggression of US imperialism, we express our heartfelt and warm gratitude to you, the Chinese Communist Party you lead, and the fraternal Chinese people for your assistance to our Fatherland Liberation War.

The telegram stated: In the course of the war, the Korean people and its true armed forces - the People's Army, with the spiritual and material support of the people's democratic countries headed by the great Soviet Union and all progressive mankind, and with the armed assistance of the fraternal Chinese People's Volunteers, heroically defended the independence and honor of their fatherland.

The fighting friendship and unity between the Korean People's Army and the Chinese People's Volunteers are becoming increasingly consolidated. In the common just struggle, the immortal feats and achievements of the heroic commanders and fighters of the Chinese People's Volunteers will forever live in the hearts of us Korean people for generations to come.

The telegram stated: We are further raising our vigilance against the enemy, and at the same time, we are filled with the firm determination that no matter what difficulties and obstacles may arise, under the leadership of the beloved leader and steel commander of the Korean Workers' Party and the Korean people, and Supreme Commander of the People's Army, Marshal Kim Il-sung, we will further closely coordinate with the fraternal Chinese People's Volunteers and fight to the end to defend the freedom and independence of our fatherland. In this just struggle, we, the Korean people, will further strengthen our internationalist friendship and unity with the people of the People's Republic of China and all people's democratic countries headed by the great Soviet Union, and with all freedom-loving people of the world.

### 平壤市纪念祖国解放战争三周年报告大会  电毛泽东主席致敬

1953-06-27
第1版()
专栏：

    平壤市纪念祖国解放战争三周年报告大会
    电毛泽东主席致敬
    【新华社平壤二十六日电】平壤市纪念祖国解放战争三周年报告大会打电报给毛泽东主席致敬。电文说：全朝鲜人民迎接为维护自己祖国自由及独立和亚洲及世界和平、反对美帝国主义武装侵略而奋起进行的正义战争三周年，谨向援助我们祖国解放战争的您和您所领导的中国共产党及兄弟般的中国人民，表示衷心和热烈的感谢。
    电文说：朝鲜人民和它的真正的武装力量——人民军，在战争过程中，在以伟大的苏联为首的各人民民主国家和全体进步人类的精神上和物质上的支援之下，在兄弟般的中国人民志愿军的武力援助之下，英雄地维护了自己祖国的独立和荣誉。
    朝鲜人民军和中国人民志愿军的战斗友谊和团结，越来越巩固。在正义的共同斗争中，英雄的中国人民志愿军指挥员和战斗员们树立的不朽功勋和业绩，将千秋万代地永远活在我们朝鲜人民的心中。
    电文说：我们正进一步提高着对敌人的警惕性，同时充满着坚强决心：不管任何困难和障碍，都要在朝鲜劳动党和朝鲜人民的敬爱领袖和钢铁统帅、人民军最高司令官金日成元帅的领导下，与兄弟般的中国人民志愿军进一步紧密地协同一致，为保卫我们祖国的自由和独立而奋斗到底。我们朝鲜人民在正义斗争中，要进一步加强与以伟大的苏联为首的中华人民共和国及各人民民主国家的人民、和全世界爱好自由的人民的国际主义的友谊团结。
