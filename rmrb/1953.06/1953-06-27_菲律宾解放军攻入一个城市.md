Title: Philippine People's Liberation Army Enters a City
Author:
Date: 1953-06-27
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, 22nd dispatch from Manila: The Philippine People's Liberation Army has recently launched successive attacks near Manila, the capital of the Philippines. On June 13th, the People's Liberation Army attacked a city 22 miles from Manila and held a mass meeting in the city. On the evening of June 19th, the People's Liberation Army attacked an area four miles from the center of Manila, near the Quirino government's Ministry of National Defense headquarters. According to the Associated Press, the attackers were all in uniform and well-armed; it said that this attack was the closest the Philippine People's Liberation Army had been to Manila in its seven years of fighting.

### 菲律宾解放军攻入一个城市

1953-06-27
第4版()
专栏：

    菲律宾解放军攻入一个城市
    【新华社二十二日讯】马尼拉消息：菲律宾人民解放军最近连续向菲律宾首都马尼拉附近出击。六月十三日，人民解放军袭入离马尼拉二十二英里的一个城市，并在城里召开了群众大会。六月十九日晚，人民解放军又袭击了离马尼拉市中心四英里的靠近季里诺政府国防总部的地区。据美联社透露，袭击者都穿着军服，武装齐备；并说，这次袭击是菲律宾人民解放军七年来作战最靠近马尼拉的一次攻击。
