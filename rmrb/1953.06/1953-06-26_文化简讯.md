　　Cultural Briefs
　　Ministry of Posts and Telecommunications Issues Commemorative Stamps for the Seventh National Congress of Chinese Trade Unions
　　To commemorate the historic Seventh National Congress of Chinese Trade Unions held in May this year, the Ministry of Posts and Telecommunications of the Central People's Government has issued a commemorative stamp. The design of this stamp represents the Chinese working class uniting more closely and striving for the industrialization of the motherland under the leadership of the Chinese Communist Party. The stamp has two denominations: 800 yuan (red) and 400 yuan (blue), both are vertical rectangles, and sales began on June 25.
　　Han Cadres in Qinghai Golog Region Achieve Success in Learning the Tibetan Language
　　Han cadres in the Golog region of Qinghai Province have achieved good results in learning the language of the Tibetan people. Since the Han cadres in the Golog region began learning the Tibetan language at the end of last year, by the end of March this year, the average cadre had learned more than 600 Tibetan sentences and more than 700 individual characters; 40 percent of the cadres were able to apply the Tibetan language they had learned to their work. In order to help cadres learn the Tibetan language, the Golog Working Committee of the Chinese Communist Party also organized three Tibetan language teaching groups for teaching, textbook writing, and phonetic notation, and helped various learning units establish inspection and reward systems.

    文化简讯
    邮电部发行中国工会第七次全国代表大会纪念邮票
    中央人民政府邮电部为纪念今年五月召开的历史性的中国工会第七次全国代表大会，特发行纪念邮票一种。这种邮票的图案表示中国工人更紧密地团结起来，在中国共产党领导下，为祖国的工业化而奋斗。邮票的面值分八百元（红色）、四百元（蓝色）两种，都是竖长方形，六月二十五日已开始发售。
    青海果洛地区汉族干部学习藏族语文有成绩
    青海省果洛地区的汉族干部，学习藏族人民的语文获得良好成绩。果洛地区的汉族干部，自去年年底开始学习藏族语文后，到今年三月底为止，一般干部都学会六百多个藏语单句和七百多个单字；其中百分之四十的干部，能把学会的藏语运用到工作中去。中国共产党果洛工作委员会，为帮助干部学习藏族语文，还组织了教学、教材编写、注音等三个藏语教学组，并帮助各学习单位建立了检查制度和奖励制度。
