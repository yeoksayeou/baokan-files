Title: Central Meteorological Observatory Issues Weak Typhoon Announcement
Author:
Date: 1953-06-30
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, June 29th: The Central Meteorological Observatory issued a weak typhoon announcement at 12:00 noon on the 29th.

At 8:00 AM on the 29th, the center of the weak typhoon was estimated to be approximately 260 kilometers south of Hong Kong (20 degrees North latitude, 115 degrees East longitude). It is expected that within 24 hours, wind speeds on the sea between Shantou and Guangzhou Bay will be approximately 10 meters per second to 15 meters per second (level 6 or 7). On the coastal land in the aforementioned areas, wind speeds may reach 8 meters per second to 10 meters per second (around level 5). Relevant departments are requested to prepare for prevention.

### 中央气象台发布弱台风消息

1953-06-30
第1版()
专栏：

    中央气象台发布弱台风消息
    【新华社二十九日讯】中央气象台二十九日上午十二时发布弱台风消息。
    二十九日上午八时弱台风中心估计约在香港以南二百六十公里处（北纬二十度，东经一百一十五度）。预计二十四小时内，汕头至广州湾之间的海面上，风速约在每秒十公尺到每秒十五公尺之间
    （六、七级）。上述地区的沿海陆地上，风速可达每秒八公尺到十公尺（五级左右）。希有关方面准备预防。
