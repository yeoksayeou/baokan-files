Title: Representatives of Some U.S. Groups Advocating Peace and Ending the Korean War Gather, Demanding the U.S. Government Take Measures to Establish Peace
Author:
Date: 1953-06-29
Page: 4

　　Xinhua News Agency, New York, June 28th: TASS, New York, June 28th: Representatives of some U.S. groups advocating peace, an end to the "Cold War," and the Korean War held a meeting in Washington. Participants included union members from the automobile, steel, textile, fur, paper, and electrical equipment industries, people who lost relatives and friends in Korea, and representatives from the American "Peace Crusade" organization.

　　Former Minnesota Democratic-Farmer-Labor Party Congressman Bernard called on the representatives at the meeting to demand the acceleration of Korean armistice negotiations and the prompt negotiation of a five-power peace pact.

　　The meeting wrote a letter to Eisenhower, urging him to fulfill the long-held wishes of the American people and to take measures to establish peace.

### 美国一些主张和平和结束朝鲜战争的团体的代表集会  要求美国政府采取建立和平的措施

1953-06-29
第4版()
专栏：

    美国一些主张和平和结束朝鲜战争的团体的代表集会
    要求美国政府采取建立和平的措施
    【新华社二十八日讯】塔斯社纽约二十八日讯：美国一些主张和平、主张结束“冷战”和朝鲜战争的团体的代表在华盛顿举行了一次会议。参加会议的有汽车、钢铁、纺织、毛皮、造纸、电气器材等工业的工会会员，在朝鲜失去了亲属朋友的人和美国“和平十字军”组织的代表。
    众议院前明尼苏达州民主农工党议员伯纳德，在会上号召代表们要求加速朝鲜停战谈判，从速进行缔结五大国和平公约的谈判。
    会议写信给艾森豪威尔，要求他实现美国人民长久以来的愿望，并采取建立和平的措施。
