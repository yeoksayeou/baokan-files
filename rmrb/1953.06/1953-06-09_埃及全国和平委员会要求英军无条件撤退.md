Title: Egyptian National Peace Committee Demands Unconditional Withdrawal of British Troops
Author:
Date: 1953-06-09
Page: 4

    Xinhua News Agency, August 8th: According to the "For Lasting Peace, For People's Democracy!" newspaper on May 29th, the Egyptian National Peace Committee recently issued two statements regarding the Egyptian people's heroic struggle to force the withdrawal of British troops, to fight for national liberation, and to oppose aggressive military pacts. The Peace Committee demanded that the Egyptian government "insist on the immediate and unconditional withdrawal of foreign troops, and never allow these troops or other foreign troops to be stationed in our country."
    The Peace Committee also warned to be vigilant against the possibility of imperialists carrying out provocative actions like those organized in Cairo in January 1952, in order to strangle the Egyptian people's national liberation movement.
    The Peace Committee pointed out: "In order to defend our national liberation struggle, it is first necessary to fully guarantee people's freedom, lift the ban on newspapers that oppose the 'Middle East Command,' especially the ban on the Peace Defenders' journal 'Writers' Monthly,' release political prisoners holding any views, and launch a resolute struggle against traitors, spies, and saboteurs."

### 埃及全国和平委员会要求英军无条件撤退

1953-06-09
第4版()
专栏：

    埃及全国和平委员会要求英军无条件撤退
    【新华社八日讯】据“争取持久和平，争取人民民主！”报五月二十九日报道，埃及全国和平委员会最近发表了两个关于埃及人民争取使英国军队撤退、争取民族解放和反对侵略性军事公约的英勇斗争的声明。和平委员会要求埃及政府“坚持要外国军队立即无条件撤退，永远不准许这些军队或其他外国军队驻在我们国家”。
    和平委员会并提出警告，要警惕帝国主义者可能进行像一九五二年一月间在开罗所组织的那样的挑拨行动，以绞杀埃及人民的民族解放运动。
    和平委员会指出：“为了卫护我们的民族解放斗争，首先必须充分保证人民自由，解除对于反对‘中东司令部’的报纸的禁令，特别是对于和平拥护者的机关刊‘作家月刊’的禁令，释放抱着任何见解的政治犯，并发动反对卖国贼、间谍和破坏分子的坚决斗争。”
