Title: Korean Negotiation Both Sides Delegation Conference Held Administrative Meeting Yesterday
Author:
Date: 1953-06-05
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, Kaesong, June 4th. The Korean armistice negotiations' Korean-Chinese delegation issued the following communiqué on June 4th: After the resumption of the plenary session of both sides' delegations at 11:00 am on June 4th, the administrative meeting continued. The other side suggested that the plenary session of both sides' delegations be adjourned for one day and resumed at 11:00 am on June 6th. Our side agreed.

### 朝鲜谈判双方代表团大会昨开行政性会议

1953-06-05
第1版()
专栏：

    朝鲜谈判双方代表团大会昨开行政性会议
    【新华社开城四日电】朝鲜停战谈判朝中代表团六月四日发表公报如下：双方代表团大会六月四日上午十一时复会后，继续举行行政性会议。对方建议双方代表团大会休会一天，到六月六日上午十一时复会，我方同意。
