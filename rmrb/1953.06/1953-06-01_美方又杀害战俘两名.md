Title: The US side again kills two prisoners of war
Author:
Date: 1953-06-01
Page: 1

    Xinhua News Agency, Kaesong, 29th - On May 24th, the US side, in the Busan No. 2 prisoner of war camp, again killed one of our captured personnel under the pretext of "escape."
    Xinhua News Agency, Kaesong, 30th - On May 28th, the US side cruelly killed another prisoner of war in the Busan prisoner of war camp. This prisoner of war was hacked to death with a knife.

### 美方又杀害战俘两名

1953-06-01
第1版()
专栏：

    美方又杀害战俘两名
    【新华社开城二十九日电】五月二十四日，美方在釜山第二号战俘营又以“逃走”为藉口打死我方被俘人员一名。
    【新华社开城三十日电】五月二十八日，美方在釜山战俘营又残忍地杀害战俘一名。这名战俘是被用刀斩死的。
