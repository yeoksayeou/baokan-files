Title: Moscow Newspapers Publish Communiqué on Prisoner of War Repatriation Agreement
Author:
Date: 1953-06-10
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, June 9th. Moscow newspapers on June 9th all published the full text of the communiqué from the Korean Armistice Negotiation's Chinese and Korean delegation regarding the Prisoner of War Repatriation Agreement. Moscow Radio also broadcast this news on June 9th.

### 莫斯科各报  登载关于战俘遣返协议公报

1953-06-10
第1版()
专栏：

    莫斯科各报
    登载关于战俘遣返协议公报
    【新华社莫斯科九日电】莫斯科各报九日都登载了朝鲜停战谈判朝中代表团关于战俘遣返问题的协议公报全文。莫斯科广播电台九日也广播了这条消息。
