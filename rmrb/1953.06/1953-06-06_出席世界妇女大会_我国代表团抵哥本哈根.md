Title: Attending the World Women's Congress, Chinese Delegation Arrives in Copenhagen
Author:
Date: 1953-06-06
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Copenhagen, 5th. The Chinese delegation to the World Women's Congress, led by head of delegation Li Dequan, arrived in Copenhagen on the morning of the 4th. After leaving Berlin on the 3rd, the delegation arrived by ship at the port of Korsør in Denmark at midnight. Representatives of the Danish Women's Association and the Danish-Chinese Friendship Association made a special trip from Copenhagen to welcome them. During their journey from Berlin to Denmark, the Chinese women's representatives were also warmly received by representatives from organizations such as the Democratic Women's League of Germany.

### 出席世界妇女大会  我国代表团抵哥本哈根

1953-06-06
第4版()
专栏：

    出席世界妇女大会
    我国代表团抵哥本哈根
    【新华社哥本哈根五日电】出席世界妇女大会的中国代表团在团长李德全率领下，在四日晨到达哥本哈根。代表团在三日离开柏林后乘轮船在午夜到达丹麦的科索尔港，丹麦妇女协会和丹麦中国友好协会的代表特由哥本哈根专程抵达该地欢迎。中国妇女代表们在从柏林赴丹麦的旅途中，并受到德国民主妇女联合会等团体代表的亲切招待。
