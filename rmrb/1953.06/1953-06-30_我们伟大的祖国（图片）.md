    Our Great Motherland
    The Wanshiyan Reservoir in Xiamen has been completed. This reservoir, which can store 150,000 cubic meters of water, not only has the function of flood control, but also can flush sewers and irrigate nearby farmland. This is a corner of the Wanshiyan Reservoir.

### 我们伟大的祖国（图片）

1953-06-30
第1版()
专栏：

    我们伟大的祖国
    厦门市万石岩水库已经建筑完成。这个蓄水十五万公方的水库，不仅有拦洪的作用，并且能够冲刷下水道和灌溉附近的农田。这是万石岩水库的一角。
