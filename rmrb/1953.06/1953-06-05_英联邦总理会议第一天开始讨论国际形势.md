Title: Commonwealth Prime Ministers' Conference Begins First Day Discussing International Situation
Author:
Date: 1953-06-05
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, June 4th: London dispatch: The Commonwealth Prime Ministers' Conference opened in London on the 3rd. The conference is attended by British Prime Minister Churchill, Indian Prime Minister Nehru, Pakistani Prime Minister Muhammad Ali, Ceylonese Prime Minister Dudley Senanayake, Australian Prime Minister Robert Menzies, Canadian Prime Minister Louis St. Laurent, New Zealand Prime Minister Sidney Holland, South African Prime Minister Malan, and Southern Rhodesian Prime Minister Huggins.

According to Reuters, the conference will last for a week to discuss international situation and various issues of federal diplomacy, military affairs and economy, including the Korean armistice agreement, the possibility of talks between East and West, the Tripartite Conference of the United States, Britain and France, the North Atlantic Treaty Organization and the Pacific "Defense" issues, as well as "researching" the results of the "plan to remedy the economic difficulties of the Sterling area" drafted at the last Federal Prime Ministers' Conference, which failed due to the refusal of the United States to cooperate.

After the meeting on the 3rd, 10 Downing Street issued a press release saying that the Prime Ministers began a general discussion on the international situation. According to Reuters, quoting "authoritative sources," the Prime Ministers support Churchill's proposal for a conference of major powers.

### 英联邦总理会议第一天开始讨论国际形势

1953-06-05
第4版()
专栏：

    英联邦总理会议第一天开始讨论国际形势
    【新华社四日讯】伦敦消息：英联邦总理会议三日在伦敦开幕。参加会议的有英国首相邱吉尔、印度总理尼赫鲁、巴基斯坦总理穆罕默德·阿里、锡兰总理杜德莱·沈纳那亚克、澳大利亚总理罗伯特·孟席斯、加拿大总理圣劳伦、新西兰总理悉尼·赫兰、南非联邦总理马兰及南罗得西亚总理哈金斯。
    据路透社消息，会议将历时一周，讨论国际形势以及联邦外交、军事及经济各项问题，包括朝鲜停战协定、东西方会谈的可能性、美英法三国会议、北大西洋公约和太平洋“防务”问题，以及“研究”上次联邦总理会议拟定的、由于美国拒绝合作而落空的“补救英镑地区的经济困难的计划”的结果。
    三日会议结束后，唐宁街十号发表会议公报说，总理们开始了关于国际形势问题的一般讨论。据路透社引“权威人士”透露的消息，总理们支持邱吉尔关于召开大国会议的建议。
