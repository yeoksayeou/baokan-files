Title: Moscow Circles Gather to Commemorate China's Great Poet Qu Yuan
Author:
Date: 1953-06-18
Page: 4

    Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, 17th. Soviet people commemorate the 2230th anniversary of the death of the great Chinese poet Qu Yuan. On the evening of the 15th, the Soviet Writers' Union, the Soviet Peace Committee, and the Gorky Institute of World Literature of the Soviet Academy of Sciences held a memorial meeting at the Tchaikovsky Concert Hall in Moscow. Present were famous Soviet writers, Sinologists, social activists, composers, painters, actors, workers, staff, students, and others. Ge Baoquan, Chargé d'Affaires ad interim of the Embassy of the People's Republic of China in the Soviet Union, and embassy staff also attended the memorial meeting.
    On the rostrum of the meeting hall hung a huge portrait of Qu Yuan, with flowers placed at the foot of the portrait.
    The meeting was presided over by I. Anisimov, Director of the Gorky Institute of World Literature. In his opening remarks, he pointed out that progressive people all over the world, in response to the call of the World Peace Council, are commemorating Qu Yuan. Qu Yuan is not only a great representative of Chinese literature, but also of world literature.
    Soviet Sinologist and Doctor of Linguistics, Fedelin, who studies Qu Yuan, gave a memorial report at the meeting entitled "Qu Yuan and his Works." He said: Qu Yuan's name is among the immortal poets and writers of the world. His writings have stood the test of time. The depth of his works, the novelty and beauty of his language, have been admired by readers in the past and present. Qu Yuan dreamed of a happy life for his motherland and his people. He sincerely struggled for true freedom and justice. Qu Yuan's legacy belongs not only to the Chinese people, but also to all progressive mankind who are defending peace, culture, and national independence, and to all honest people who advocate the ideas of friendship and cooperation among all peoples.
    Subsequently, Ge Baoquan was invited to address the meeting, pointing out that the Soviet Union's commemoration of this day would help strengthen the cultural ties between the Chinese and Soviet people.
    After the meeting, a musical performance was held. Actors and musicians from Moscow participated in the performance. The program included Qu Yuan's poems and works by Chinese composers. The Yermolova Theatre performed two sections from Guo Moruo's historical drama "Qu Yuan" at the concert.

### 莫斯科各界集会纪念我国伟大诗人屈原

1953-06-18
第4版()
专栏：

    莫斯科各界集会纪念我国伟大诗人屈原
    【新华社莫斯科十七日电】苏联人民纪念中国伟大诗人屈原逝世二千二百三十周年。十五日晚，苏联作家协会、苏联拥护和平委员会、苏联科学院高尔基世界文学研究所，假莫斯科柴可夫斯基音乐厅举行了纪念会。到会的有苏联著名作家、汉学家、社会活动家、作曲家、画家、演员、工人、职员、学生等。中华人民共和国驻苏联大使馆临时代办戈宝权和大使馆人员也参加了纪念会。
    会场的讲台上挂着巨幅屈原画像，像脚摆满着鲜花。
    会议由高尔基世界文学研究所所长伊·阿尼西莫夫主持。他在致开幕词时指出，全世界进步人类根据世界和平理事会的号召，都在纪念屈原。屈原不仅是中国文学、而且是世界文学的伟大代表。
    苏联研究屈原的汉学家、语言学博士费德林在会上作了纪念报告，题目是“屈原及其创作”。他说：屈原的名字列在世界上不朽的诗人和作家的前列。他的著作通过了时间的考验。他的作品的深刻性，他的语言的新颖和美丽，过去和现在都一直受着读者的赞美。屈原曾梦想使他的祖国和他的人民过幸福的生活。他真诚地为争取真正的自由和公正而奋斗。屈原的遗产不仅属于中国人民，而且属于正在保卫和平、文化和民族独立的全体进步人类，属于拥护各国人民的友好和合作思想的全体正直人民。
    接着，戈宝权应邀在会上致辞，他指出，苏联对这个日子的纪念，有助于加强中苏两国人民的文化联系。
    会后举行了音乐演奏会。莫斯科的演员和音乐家参加了表演。节目包括屈原的诗歌和中国作曲家的作品。叶尔莫洛娃剧院在演奏会上演出了郭沫若的历史剧“屈原”中的两节。
