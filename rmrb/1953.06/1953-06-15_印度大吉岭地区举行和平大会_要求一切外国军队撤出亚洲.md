Title: Peace Conference Held in Darjeeling, India, Demands Withdrawal of All Foreign Troops from Asia
Author:
Date: 1953-06-15
Page: 4

    Xinhua News Agency, Delhi, 12th (TASS): “Freedom Daily” reports that several peace fighter conferences have been held in the foreign tea plantation area of Darjeeling. Active peace fighter and veteran of the Indian National Congress, Sundar Lal, spoke at these conferences.
    The conferences passed resolutions demanding the withdrawal of all foreign troops from Korea, Vietnam, Malaya, and other Asian countries, and the conclusion of a peace pact among the great powers.
    Similar resolutions were also passed at a peace rally held in Lucknow.

### 印度大吉岭地区举行和平大会  要求一切外国军队撤出亚洲

1953-06-15
第4版()
专栏：

    印度大吉岭地区举行和平大会
    要求一切外国军队撤出亚洲
    【新华社十三日讯】塔斯社德里十二日讯：“自由报”报道，大吉岭的外国茶园地区举行了几次和平战士大会。积极的和平战士、印度国大党元老森德拉尔在这些大会上讲了话。
    这些大会通过决议，要求从朝鲜、越南、马来亚和其他亚洲国家撤退一切外国军队，并缔结大国和平公约。
    在勒克瑙举行的和平集会也通过了类似的决议。
