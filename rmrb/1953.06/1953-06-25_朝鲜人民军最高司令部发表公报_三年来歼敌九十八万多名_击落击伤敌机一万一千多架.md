Title: The Supreme Command of the Korean People's Army Issues Communiqué: Annihilated Over 980,000 Enemy Troops in Three Years; Shot Down or Damaged Over 11,000 Enemy Aircraft
Author:
Date: 1953-06-25
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, Pyongyang, June 24th: The Supreme Command of the Korean People's Army issued the following communiqué on the comprehensive achievements of the Korean People's Army and the Chinese People's Volunteers fighting side-by-side for three years:

The results achieved by the Korean People's Army and the Chinese People's Volunteers in fighting against the US and British invading armies and the Syngman Rhee puppet army for three years are as follows:

Killed, wounded, or captured 989,391 enemy troops, including 380,773 American soldiers, 580,644 Syngman Rhee puppet soldiers, and 27,974 others.

Captured combat equipment included: 327 tanks, 8,987 vehicles, 145 armored vehicles, 10 aircraft, 5,879 artillery pieces of various types, 110,238 sniper weapons of various types, 4,648 telephones and radios, over 300,000 artillery shells of various types, tens of millions of rounds of ammunition of various types, and 150,000 hand grenades.

Destroyed or damaged equipment included: 2,565 tanks, 3,825 vehicles, 1,188 artillery pieces of various types. Sank 163 enemy ships and damaged 93 enemy ships.

Shot down 5,431 enemy aircraft and damaged 6,033 enemy aircraft.

### 朝鲜人民军最高司令部发表公报  三年来歼敌九十八万多名  击落击伤敌机一万一千多架

1953-06-25
第1版()
专栏：

    朝鲜人民军最高司令部发表公报
    三年来歼敌九十八万多名
    击落击伤敌机一万一千多架
    【新华社平壤二十四日电】朝鲜人民军最高司令部二十四日发表朝鲜人民军和中国人民志愿军并肩作战的三年综合战绩公报如下：
    朝鲜人民军和中国人民志愿军三年来在和美、英侵略军和李承晚伪军作战中所取得的战果如下：
    毙伤俘敌九十八万九千三百九十一名，其中美军三十八万零七百七十三名，李承晚伪军五十八万零六百四十四名，其他二万七千九百七十四名。
    缴获的战斗器材有：坦克三百二十七辆，汽车八千九百八十七辆，装甲车一百四十五辆，飞机十架，各种炮五千八百七十九门，各种狙击武器十一万零二百三十八挺，电话机及电台四千六百四十八部，各种炮弹三十万余发，各种弹药数千万发，手榴弹十五万余颗。
    击毁和击伤的器材有：坦克二千五百六十五辆，汽车三千八百二十五辆，各种炮一千一百八十八门。击沉敌舰船一百六十三艘，击破敌舰船九十三艘。
    击落敌机五千四百三十一架，击伤敌机六千零三十三架。
