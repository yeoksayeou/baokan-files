Title: British "Veterans' Movement for Peace" Holds Meeting, Demanding Immediate Cessation of All Wars and Great Power Negotiations
Author:
Date: 1953-06-23
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, London, June 15th: TASS, London, June 15th: The second national representative conference of the British "Veterans' Movement for Peace" was held in London. Forty representatives and observers from the association's local branches, other peace organizations, and trade unions attended the meeting. The conference unanimously adopted a program statement calling for an immediate cessation of all ongoing wars and the commencement of great power negotiations to ease the tense international situation. The statement urged the British government to oppose artificial restrictions on international trade, and it also condemned the rearmament of West Germany and Japan. George Cross, the acting secretary of the "Veterans' Movement for Peace," delivered the main report at the meeting. When speaking about the role of the Soviet Union in World War II, Cross said: "We should not forget that we are grateful to the Soviet people." Cross said that British veterans would never forget the Battle of Stalingrad. Edgar Young spoke during the discussion, calling for the expansion of trade between East and West. He said that the U.S. Battle Act was hindering trade between Britain and half the world. Phyllis West, the secretary of the Labour Party organization in Swindon (Wiltshire), emphasized the part of the conference program statement that condemned the stationing of foreign troops in Britain. She said that the pilots of the U.S. Air Force base in the Swindon area were corrupting the city's youth.

### 英国“退伍军人保卫和平运动协会”开会  要求立刻停止一切战争举行大国谈判

1953-06-23
第4版()
专栏：

    英国“退伍军人保卫和平运动协会”开会
    要求立刻停止一切战争举行大国谈判
    【新华社十七日讯】塔斯社伦敦十五日讯：英国“退伍军人保卫和平运动协会”第二次全国代表会议在伦敦举行。出席会议的有该协会的地方分会以及其他保卫和平组织和工会的四十名代表和观察员。会议一致通过一项纲领声明，号召立刻停止目前正在进行的一切战争，开始大国谈判以缓和紧张的国际局势。声明敦促英国政府反对人为的国际贸易限制，声明还谴责重新武装西德和日本。“退伍军人保卫和平运动协会”代理书记乔治·克罗斯在会议上作了主要报告。克罗斯讲到苏联在二次世界大战时所起的作用时说：“我们不应当忘记，我们是感谢苏联人民的。”克罗斯说，英国退伍军人永远不会忘记斯大林格勒保卫战。埃德加·杨格在讨论时发言，号召扩展东西方之间的贸易。他说，美国巴特尔法阻碍着英国和半个世界进行贸易。斯温顿（尉尔特郡）工党组织书记菲利斯·韦斯特强调指出会议纲领声明中关于谴责外国军队驻留英国的部分。她说，斯温顿区的美国空军基地飞行员把这个城市的青年们都给腐化了。
