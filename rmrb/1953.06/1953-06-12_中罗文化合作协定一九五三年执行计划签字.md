Title: Signing of the 1953 Implementation Plan of the Sino-Romanian Cultural Cooperation Agreement
Author: 
Date: 1953-06-12
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, June 11th: The 1953 implementation plan of the Cultural Cooperation Agreement between the People's Republic of China and the Romanian People's Republic was signed in Bucharest on June 9th. The two sides issued a joint communiqué as follows:

The joint Sino-Romanian meeting held from June 2nd to 9th to formulate the 1953 implementation plan of the Sino-Romanian Cultural Cooperation Agreement has completed the work of formulating the implementation plan. The participants in the joint meeting were: the delegation headed by Wang Youping, Ambassador Extraordinary and Plenipotentiary of the People's Republic of China to the Romanian People's Republic, and the delegation headed by Mihail Roșianu, Chairman of the Broadcasting Committee of the Council of Ministers of the Romanian People's Republic. The joint meeting unanimously agreed that, according to the Sino-Romanian Cultural Cooperation Agreement signed in Beijing in December 1951, cultural exchange work was successfully carried out in 1952. The formulation of this year's implementation plan was carried out entirely in a friendly and mutually understanding atmosphere.

The signatories to the implementation plan were Wang Youping, Ambassador Extraordinary and Plenipotentiary of the People's Republic of China to the Romanian People's Republic, Zhao Zhengyi, Second Secretary of the Embassy, and other embassy staff on the Chinese side; and Mihail Roșianu, Chairman of the Broadcasting Committee of the Council of Ministers of the Romanian People's Republic, Murgulescu, Minister of Higher Education, Ion Nistor, Minister of Education, Nicolae Cioroiu, Deputy Minister of Foreign Affairs, Markovits, Chairman of the Foreign Cultural Association, Babu, Vice-Chairman, Șerban, Secretary of the Writers' Union, and relevant staff members of the Romanian Foreign Cultural Association on the Romanian side.

The signing of the 1953 implementation plan of the Sino-Romanian Cultural Cooperation Agreement will help develop cultural relations between the two countries, promote the exchange of experiences in science, art, and culture, and deepen the friendship between the peoples of China and Romania.

### 中罗文化合作协定一九五三年执行计划签字

1953-06-12
第1版()
专栏：

    中罗文化合作协定一九五三年执行计划签字
    【新华社十一日讯】中华人民共和国和罗马尼亚人民共和国文化合作协定一九五三年执行计划，已于六月九日在布加勒斯特签字。双方发表公报如下：
    为拟定中罗文化合作协定一九五三年执行计划而在六月二日至九日举行的中罗联席会议，已完成了拟定执行计划的工作。参加联席会议的有：以中华人民共和国驻罗马尼亚人民共和国特命全权大使王幼平为首的代表团，和以罗马尼亚人民共和国部长会议广播委员会主席米哈伊尔·罗山亚努为首的代表团。联席会议一致认为，根据一九五一年十二月在北京所签订的中罗文化合作协定，在一九五二年内已顺利地执行了文化交流工作。本年度执行计划的拟定，完全是在友好及互相谅解的情况下进行的。
    参加执行计划的签字仪式者，中国方面为中华人民共和国驻罗马尼亚人民共和国特命全权大使王幼平，大使馆二等秘书赵政一，以及大使馆其他人员；罗马尼亚人民共和国部长会议广播委员会主席米·罗山亚努，高等教育部部长穆尔古莱斯库，教育部部长扬·尼斯托尔，外交部副部长尼·丘罗尤，对外文化协会主席马科威、副主席巴布，作家联盟秘书谢尔马鲁，以及罗马尼亚对外文化协会有关工作人员。
    中罗文化合作协定一九五三年执行计划的签订，将有助于两国文化关系的发展，及两国在科学艺术文化方面经验交流的发展，并加深中罗两国人民间的友谊。
