    Our Great Motherland
    The Lian Gong Dam of the Fozi Ridge Reservoir on the Huai River is the highest dam in the country. Visitors all praise this dam project. Young Pioneers from nearby elementary schools often come here to visit. They say, "This dam is growing really fast!"

### 我们伟大的祖国（图片）

1953-06-15
第1版()
专栏：

    我们伟大的祖国
    淮河佛子岭水库的连拱坝，是全国最高的一座水坝。参观的人都赞美这个水坝工程。工地附近小学的少年儿童队员时常来这里参观。他们说：“这水坝长得真快！”
