Title: Our Great Motherland (Picture)
Author:
Date: 1953-06-02
Page: 1

    Our Great Motherland
    In the past three years, the large number of children's books published in our country have been increasingly welcomed by the majority of children. This is a shepherd boy from Shuangzha Township in the suburbs of Nanjing. They bought their favorite children's books from the "cultural peddler" and read while herding cattle.

### 我们伟大的祖国（图片）

1953-06-02
第1版()
专栏：

    我们伟大的祖国
    三年来我国出版的大量少年儿童读物，日益受到广大少年儿童的欢迎。这是南京市郊区双闸乡的牧童。他们在“文化挑”上买到他们心爱的儿童读物，一边放牛，一边阅读。
