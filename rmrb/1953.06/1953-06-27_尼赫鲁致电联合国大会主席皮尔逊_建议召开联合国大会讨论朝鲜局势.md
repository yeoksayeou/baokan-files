Title: Nehru Calls on UN General Assembly President Pearson Suggesting a UN General Assembly Meeting to Discuss the Korean Situation
Author:
Date: 1953-06-27
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, June 26th: According to foreign news agencies, Indian Prime Minister Nehru, who is currently in Cairo, sent a telegram to UN General Assembly President Pearson on the 25th, suggesting that the UN General Assembly be convened to discuss the Korean situation caused by the Syngman Rhee government's opposition to the armistice.

Nehru said at a press conference held in Cairo on the same day that Syngman Rhee's "release" of prisoners of war was a "violation of the POW agreement." Nehru asked: "Who controls the situation in Korea - Syngman Rhee? Or the United Nations Command?" He believes that "if the United Nations cannot control the situation in Korea, this is the United Nations admitting its weakness." He said, "These very important issues cannot be resolved by the Korean military leaders, and the UN General Assembly must meet immediately."

### 尼赫鲁致电联合国大会主席皮尔逊  建议召开联合国大会讨论朝鲜局势

1953-06-27
第1版()
专栏：

    尼赫鲁致电联合国大会主席皮尔逊
    建议召开联合国大会讨论朝鲜局势
    【新华社二十六日讯】据外国通讯社报道：正在开罗的印度总理尼赫鲁在二十五日致电联合国大会主席皮尔逊，建议召开联合国大会，以讨论因李承晚政府反对实现停战而造成的朝鲜局势。
    尼赫鲁在同一天在开罗举行的记者招待会上说，李承晚“释放”战俘是“破坏战俘协议”。尼赫鲁问道：“谁控制着朝鲜的局势——李承晚？还是联合国军司令部？”他认为，“如果联合国不能控制朝鲜形势，这就是联合国承认了自己脆弱。”他说，“这些非常重要的问题不是朝鲜军事领袖所能解决的，联合国大会必须马上开会。”
