Title: Government Administration Council Holds 182nd Meeting
Author:
Date: 1953-06-13
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, June 12th: The 182nd meeting of the Central People's Government Administration Council was held on the afternoon of the 12th. The meeting listened to and approved the report by Vice Minister of the First Ministry of Machine Building, Wang Daohan, on the current situation of state-owned machine industry and the main points of future work arrangements. The meeting also passed various appointment and removal lists.

### 政务院举行第一八二次会议

1953-06-13
第1版()
专栏：

    政务院举行第一八二次会议
    【新华社十二日讯】中央人民政府政务院第一百八十二次政务会议在十二日下午举行。会议听取和批准了中央人民政府第一机械工业部汪道涵副部长所作的关于目前国营机械工业的情况及今后工作布署的要点的报告。会议并通过了各项任免名单。
