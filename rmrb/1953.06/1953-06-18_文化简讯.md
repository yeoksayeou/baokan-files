Cultural Briefs

    The Pharmacopoeia of the People's Republic of China is Completed
    The Pharmacopoeia of the People's Republic of China has been completed and will be published by the Commercial Press in mid-August. Xinhua Bookstores and China Book Distribution Company in various regions have been accepting pre-orders since June 5th (deadline: June 20th).
    This pharmacopoeia was compiled by the Chinese Pharmacopoeia Compilation Committee, established by the Ministry of Health of the Central People's Government in 1950, over a period of more than two years. It includes a total of 531 items, including crude drugs, chemical drugs, biological products, and preparations, divided into two parts: the main text and the appendix. The main text provides detailed regulations for each drug's properties, identification, inspection, content, efficacy, storage, and dosage. The appendix contains general testing methods, reagents, test solutions, dosage tables, and lists of toxic drugs related to the main text. The entire book contains approximately one million words.

### 文化简讯

专栏：文化简讯

    文化简讯
    中华人民共和国药典编纂完成
    中华人民共和国药典已编纂完成，将在八月中旬由商务印书馆出版。各地新华书店和中国图书发行公司已自六月五日起（二十日截止）办理预定。
    这部药典是由中央人民政府卫生部在一九五○年成立的中国药典编纂委员会经过两年多的时间编纂成功的。全部收载了生药、化学药品、生物学制品和制剂等共五百三十一种，分正文附录两部分。正文中对于每种药品的性状、鉴别、检查、含量和效用、贮藏、剂量等都有详细的规定。附录记载着和正文中有关的一般检验法试药、试液以及剂量表、毒剧药表等。全书约一百万字左右。
