Title: Prevent Typhoons in the Southeast Coast
Author:
Date: 1953-06-05
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, June 4th. The Central Meteorological Observatory issued the following typhoon warning at 12:00 noon on June 4th:

    At 8:00 AM on June 4th, the center of the typhoon was located 200 kilometers northeast of Manila, at 17.2 degrees north latitude and 121.3 degrees east longitude. The maximum wind speed near the center was approximately 50 meters per second, and the wind speed 90 kilometers from the center was approximately 25 meters per second. The typhoon center is currently moving northwest at a speed of 16 kilometers per hour. It is expected that within 24 hours, the maximum wind speed in the southern Taiwan Strait to within 300 kilometers southeast of Dongsha Island will reach 15 to 20 meters per second, with northeasterly winds and showers. The above areas must take precautions, and the southern Fujian coast and eastern Guangdong coast should also prepare for prevention. It is expected that at 2:00 PM on June 5th, the typhoon center will be located 150 kilometers southeast of Dongsha Island, at 20.3 degrees north latitude and 118.2 degrees east longitude.

### 东南沿海要预防台风

1953-06-05
第1版()
专栏：

    东南沿海要预防台风
    【新华社四日讯】中央气象台四日上午十二时发布台风警报如下：
    六月四日上午八时台风中心位置在马尼剌东北方二百公里处，即北纬十七点二度，东经一百二十一点三度。中心附近最大风速约为每秒五十公尺，距中心九十公里处，风速约为每秒二十五公尺。目前台风中心以每小时十六公里速度向西北方向移动。预计二十四小时内，台湾海峡南部至东沙岛东南三百公里以内的地区，最大风速可达每秒十五至二十公尺，风向东北，并有阵雨。以上地区须做预防，福建南部沿海及广东东部沿海也要做预防准备。预计五日下午二时，台风中心位置将在东沙岛东南方一百五十公里的海上，即北纬二十点三度，东经一百十八点二度的地方。
