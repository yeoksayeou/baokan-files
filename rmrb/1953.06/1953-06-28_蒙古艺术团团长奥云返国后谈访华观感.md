Title: Mongolian Art Troupe Leader Oyun Discusses Impressions of China After Returning Home
Author:
Date: 1953-06-28
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, June 21st, Ulaanbaatar: The Art Troupe of the Mongolian People's Republic, which visited and performed in various parts of China for one and a half months, returned to the Mongolian capital Ulaanbaatar on June 10th. On June 16th, the head of the art troupe, Oyun, met with a reporter from the Mongolian "Truth Daily" and shared her impressions of her visit to China. She first spoke about the warm welcome the Mongolian Art Troupe received from the Chinese people.

Oyun said: Everywhere in China, we saw the brotherly friendship between the Mongolian and Chinese peoples and the sincere feelings of the Chinese people towards our people. She particularly recalled the meeting between Chairman Mao and the members of the art troupe, saying: This meeting was deeply imprinted in the hearts of each of us. Chairman Mao received us so kindly and warmly.

Oyun praised the growth of new Chinese art and the development of Chinese ethnic minority cultures and arts. She said: Thanks to the wise leadership of the People's Government, the culture and art of Chinese ethnic minorities have achieved tremendous accomplishments and brilliantly reflect the new life of the people. This time, our meeting with the artistic workers of our fraternal Inner Mongolian people left an indelible impression on us.

Oyun also vividly described the Chinese people's boundless love for their great leader Chairman Mao, the Chinese Communist Party, and the People's Government, as well as the brilliant achievements the Chinese people have made in construction. She said: The outstanding achievements of the Chinese people in all aspects will be a great encouragement to us, and the 500 million great Chinese people are a huge force in the camp of peace, democracy, and socialism.

### 蒙古艺术团团长奥云返国后谈访华观感

1953-06-28
第4版()
专栏：

    蒙古艺术团团长奥云返国后谈访华观感
    【新华社二十一日讯】乌兰巴托消息：曾在中国各地访问和演出一个半月的蒙古人民共和国艺术团，已经在六月十日回到蒙古首都乌兰巴托。艺术团团长奥云在十六日接见蒙古“真理报”记者，发表了她的访华观感。她首先谈到蒙古艺术团受到中国人民热烈欢迎的情形。
    奥云说：在中国，我们到处都看到蒙中两国人民的兄弟般的友谊和中国人民对我国人民的真挚的感情。她特别追述了毛主席接见艺术团人员的情况说：这次会晤深深地印入了我们每个人的心坎。毛主席是那么和霭和亲切地接待了我们。
    奥云赞扬新中国艺术的成长，和中国少数民族文化、艺术的发展。她说：由于人民政府的英明领导，中国少数民族的文化和艺术获得了极大的成就，并且光辉地反映了人民的新生活。这次，我们和兄弟般的内蒙古人民的艺术工作者的会见，给我们留下了不可磨灭的印象。
    奥云还生动地叙述了中国人民对自己的伟大领袖毛泽东主席、对中国共产党和人民政府的无限爱戴，以及中国人民在建设中所获得的辉煌成就。她说：中国人民各方面的卓越的成就对我们将是莫大的鼓舞，而五万万伟大的中国人民在和平民主和社会主义阵营中是一支巨大的力量。
