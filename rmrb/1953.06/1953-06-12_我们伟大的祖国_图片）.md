    Our Great Motherland
    With the rapid development of the Northwest Railway Trunk Line, a Sanqiao Railway Factory capable of meeting the needs of railway construction is being built. This railway factory is the largest and most modern railway vehicle repair factory in Northwest China. Most of its construction work is done by mechanized means.

### 我们伟大的祖国(图片）

1953-06-12
第1版()
专栏：

    我们伟大的祖国
    随着西北铁路干线的迅速发展，一座能够适应铁路建设需要的三桥铁路工厂正在建造起来。这个铁路工厂是我国西北最大的和最新式的铁路车辆修理工厂。它的建厂工程大部分是用机械化施工的。
