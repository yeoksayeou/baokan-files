Title: Guo Moruo and Others Arrive in Budapest and Receive Warm Welcome
Author:
Date: 1953-06-14
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Budapest, June 13th: Guo Moruo, Vice-Chairman of the World Peace Council Standing Committee and Chairman of the Chinese People's Committee for World Peace, Mao Dun, member of the World Peace Council Standing Committee, Council members Cai Tingkai, Liu Ningyi, Wu Yaozong, Guo Fang, specially invited representatives Liu Guanyi, Xu Baoju, and staff members arrived in Budapest by plane on the 12th to attend the World Peace Council meeting to be held here on the 15th of this month. Those who welcomed them at the airport included István Déghy, Chairman of the Hungarian National Peace Council, Sándor Rónai, Chairman of the Hungarian National Assembly, József Darvas, Chairman of the Hungarian Writers' Association, Archbishop János Péter, István Rusznyák, President of the Hungarian Academy of Sciences, György Nonn, Deputy Minister of Culture of Hungary, Secretaries of the World Peace Council Secretariat Guryayev (Soviet Union), and Mr.Goll (United Kingdom), as well as Xiao San and Li Yimang, Chinese members of the World Peace Council, and others. Huang Zhen, Ambassador of the People's Republic of China to Hungary, led embassy staff to the airport to welcome them. István Rusznyák, President of the Hungarian Academy of Sciences, made a short speech, welcoming Guo Moruo and others on behalf of the Hungarian people and the Hungarian National Peace Council, and wishing the World Peace Council meeting a complete success. Guo Moruo delivered a reply, expressing his gratitude to the Hungarian National Peace Council for its welcome, and paying tribute to the citizens of Budapest and the peace-loving Hungarian people on behalf of the Chinese people. Guo Moruo said: The people of the world need peace, and the power of peace is gradually rising. We must make further efforts to ensure that peace achieves final victory. Finally, Guo Moruo wished for the further development of the fraternal friendship between the Chinese and Hungarian peoples.

### 郭沫若等到达布达佩斯受到热烈欢迎

1953-06-14
第1版()
专栏：

    郭沫若等到达布达佩斯受到热烈欢迎
    【新华社布达佩斯十三日电】世界和平理事会常务委员会副主席、中国人民保卫世界和平委员会主席郭沫若，世界和平理事会常务委员会委员茅盾，理事蔡廷锴、刘宁一、吴耀宗、郭芳，特邀代表刘贯一、许宝驹及工作人员等一行于十二日乘飞机抵达布达佩斯，参加行将于本月十五日在此举行的世界和平理事会会议。到机场欢迎的有匈牙利全国和平理事会主席艾泽伯特·安弟奇，匈牙利国民议会主席桑多·罗奈伊，匈牙利作家协会主席约瑟夫·达尔瓦斯、雅诺希·彼得大主教，匈牙利科学院院长伊斯特万·鲁斯尼雅克，匈牙利文化部副部长乔治·农，世界和平理事会书记处书记顾里亚耶夫（苏联）、戈尔（英国）及世界和平理事会中国理事萧三、李一氓等人。中国驻匈牙利人民共和国大使黄镇率领使馆人员亦到机场欢迎。匈牙利科学院院长伊斯特万·鲁斯尼雅克发表简短谈话，代表匈牙利人民和匈牙利全国和平理事会对郭沫若等表示欢迎，并预祝世界和平理事会会议圆满成功。郭沫若致答词，对匈牙利全国和平理事会的欢迎表示谢意，并代表中国人民向布达佩斯市民向爱好和平的匈牙利人民致敬；郭沫若说：全世界人民需要和平，和平的力量正逐渐高涨，我们要进一步努力使和平获得最后胜利。郭沫若最后祝中匈两国人民的兄弟友谊进一步发展。
