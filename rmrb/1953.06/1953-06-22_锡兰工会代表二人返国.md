Title: Two Ceylonese Union Representatives Return Home
Author: 
Date: 1953-06-22
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency, Guangzhou. The head of the Ceylonese trade union delegation, Mandis, and member Damapriya, who were invited to China to participate in the "May Day" International Labor Day celebrations and the Seventh National Congress of Chinese Trade Unions, arrived in Guangzhou from Beijing on the evening of the 10th. On the morning of the 16th at 9:00 AM, they left Guangzhou via Hong Kong to return to their country. Those who saw them off at the station included Tan Zhuofen, director of the Guangzhou Federation of Trade Unions office, and many others.

During their stay in Guangzhou, the representatives visited and toured the site of the Guangzhou Commune, Huanghuagang, and Yuexiu Mountain. On the evening of the 11th, they attended a film reception hosted by the Guangzhou Federation of Trade Unions.

### 锡兰工会代表二人返国

1953-06-22
第4版()
专栏：

    锡兰工会代表二人返国
    【新华社广州电】应邀来我国参加“五一”国际劳动节庆祝典礼和中国工会第七次全国代表大会的锡兰工会代表团团长曼迪斯、团员达马普里雅，在十日晚由北京到达广州，十六日上午九时离广州取道香港返国。到车站欢送的有广州市总工会办公室主任谭卓芬等多人。
    代表们在广州期间，参观和游览了广州公社故址、黄花岗和越秀山。十一日晚，出席了广州市总工会举办的电影招待晚会。
