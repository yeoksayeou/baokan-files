Title: Celebrating "June 1st" International Children's Day: Capital Children Hold Celebration Meeting
Author:
Date: 1953-06-01
Page: 1

    Celebrating "June 1st" International Children's Day
    Capital Children Hold Celebration Meeting
    [Xinhua News Agency, May 31st] The children of the capital held a celebration meeting on May 31st to celebrate "June 1st" International Children's Day. More than 600 representatives of children from primary and secondary schools in the capital attended the meeting, as well as more than 100 children from various countries' embassies in China. Wu Han, Deputy Mayor of the Beijing Municipal People's Government, also attended the meeting. The children were all dressed in beautiful new clothes, singing songs with the children from various countries, and joyfully entered the venue.
    After the meeting began, Deputy Mayor Wu Han spoke. He said: In our great motherland, Chairman Mao, the Communist Party, and the People's Government are carrying out large-scale construction for the bright future of our country's children. They express their sincere concern for the children's education, upbringing and development prospects, and have created a happy life and a good learning environment for the children. There are now nearly 300,000 children in elementary and junior high schools in the capital, and 80,000 children have become members of the glorious Young Pioneers. Children's health care and welfare are developing in a planned way. There are now 190 nurseries and childcare stations, as well as hospitals, swimming pools, stadiums and opera schools specially set up for children. Deputy Mayor Wu earnestly hoped that the children would study hard, exercise their bodies, learn the internationalism and patriotism of the Volunteer Army uncles, and prepare to serve the motherland in the future.
    In warm applause and melodious music, representatives of Beijing's children presented flowers to Deputy Mayor Wu Han. Then, the Korean children present presented flowers to the presidium of the meeting. At this time, the Chinese children and Korean children in the presidium shook hands warmly and held on for a long time, causing long and exciting applause from the audience. The children of Beijing also presented flowers to the children of various countries' embassies in China. Finally, a congratulatory letter to Chairman Mao and a congratulatory letter to the Chinese People's Volunteer Army were read out.
    After the meeting, the Art Troupe of the General Political Department of the Central People's Government Revolutionary Military Commission performed wonderful artistic programs for the children. The Korean children also enthusiastically participated in the performance.

### 庆祝“六一”国际儿童节  首都儿童举行庆祝大会

1953-06-01
第1版()
专栏：

    庆祝“六一”国际儿童节
    首都儿童举行庆祝大会
    【新华社三十一日讯】首都儿童三十一日举行庆祝“六一”国际儿童节大会。到会的有首都各中小学儿童代表六百多人，还有各国驻我国大使馆的小朋友一百多人。北京市人民政府副市长吴晗也出席大会。小朋友们个个都穿着漂亮的新衣，和各国小朋友一齐唱着歌，喜气洋洋地走进会场。
    大会开始后，吴晗副市长讲话。他说：在我们伟大的祖国，毛主席、共产党和人民政府正为着我国少年儿童的美好的将来，进行大规模的建设。他们对儿童们的教养和发展前途表示亲切的关怀，为儿童们创造了幸福的生活和良好的学习环境。现在首都有将近三十万少年儿童在小学和初级中学读书，八万儿童成为光荣的少年儿童队队员。儿童的保健和福利事业正在有计划的发展。现在已经有一百九十个托儿所和托儿站，并且有了专门为儿童设置的医院、游泳池、体育场和戏曲学校。吴副市长热切希望小朋友们努力学习、锻炼身体、学习志愿军叔叔们的国际主义和爱国主义精神，准备将来为祖国服务。
    在热烈的掌声和悠扬的乐声中，北京市少年儿童代表向吴晗副市长献花。接着，到会的朝鲜小朋友向大会主席团献花，这时，主席团中的中国小朋友和朝鲜小朋友亲热地握着手，久久不放，引起全场长久的激动的掌声。北京市少年儿童又向各国驻我国使馆的小朋友献花。最后，宣读了大会给毛主席的致敬信和给中国人民志愿军的致敬信。
    会后，中央人民政府人民革命军事委员会总政治部文艺工作团为小朋友演出精彩的文艺节目，朝鲜的小朋友也热情地参加了表演。
