Title: All India Peace Council Calls for Commemoration of Korean Day on the Third Anniversary of the Korean War
Author:
Date: 1953-06-04
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, New Delhi, 3rd—As the third anniversary of the Korean War approaches, the All India Peace Council is calling on the Indian people to prepare to commemorate Korean Day on June 25th, demanding an immediate end to the Korean War.

In its appeal, the All India Peace Council stated: "People from all walks of life in India hope for an immediate end to the Korean War. It is necessary for the Indian peace movement to organize this unified desire into a broad movement, which should become an irresistible force."

The appeal also emphasized: "The main preparatory work for commemorating Korean Day should be to win the support of leaders of various factions and representatives of various organizations who have not yet participated in the peace movement."

### 全印和平理事会号召在朝鲜战争三周年举行纪念朝鲜日

1953-06-04
第4版()
专栏：

    全印和平理事会号召在朝鲜战争三周年举行纪念朝鲜日
    【新华社新德里三日电】朝鲜战争的三周年快到了，全印和平理事会号召印度人民准备在六月二十五日举行纪念朝鲜日，要求立刻停止朝鲜战争。
    全印和平理事会在这个号召中说：“印度各阶层人民都希望立刻停止朝鲜战争。印度和平运动有必要把这种一致的愿望组织成为广泛的运动，这种运动应当成为不可抗拒的力量。”
    这个号召并着重指出：“纪念朝鲜日的主要准备工作，应该是争取目前还没有参加和平运动的各种派别的领袖和各种组织的代表们的支持。”
