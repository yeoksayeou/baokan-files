Title: India Decides to Close its Legation in Portugal
Author:
Date: 1953-06-13
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency, Delhi, June 11th: TASS reported from Delhi on the 11th that the Indian government has decided to close its legation in Lisbon starting June 11th. The Indian government made this decision because the Portuguese government refused to discuss the issue of returning Portuguese colonies in India to India on May 15th of this year. However, the Portuguese government still maintains its legation in India.

### 印度决定关闭驻葡公使馆

1953-06-13
第4版()
专栏：

    印度决定关闭驻葡公使馆
    【新华社十二日讯】塔斯社德里十一日讯：印度政府决定自六月十一日起关闭驻里斯本的公使馆。印度政府作出这个决定，是由于葡萄牙政府今年五月十五日拒绝讨论关于把葡萄牙在印度的殖民地交还给印度的问题。但是，葡萄牙政府仍保持着驻印度的公使馆。
