Title: Strong Winds in the Huai River Basin and Shandong Province
Author:
Date: 1953-06-14
Page: 1
　
　　Xinhua News Agency, June 13th: The Central Meteorological Observatory issued a gale warning at 12:00 PM on June 13th as follows:

　　On the afternoon of the 13th, a strong wind area of force seven was detected in the Huai River basin, and the range is gradually expanding. It is expected that from 10:00 AM on the 14th, there will be force six to seven gales in the middle and lower reaches of the Huai River basin, with southerly winds; Shandong, Hebei, and northern Jiangsu will also have force six to seven gales, with southerly winds. From 12:00 noon, southern Northeast China, the Inner Mongolia Autonomous Region, the Yellow Sea, and the Bohai Sea will also have gales of force six or above, with southerly winds.

### 淮河流域和山东等地有大风

1953-06-14
第1版()
专栏：

    淮河流域和山东等地有大风
    【新华社十三日讯】中央气象台六月十三日晚十二时发布大风警报如下：
    十三日下午，淮河流域发现有大风区域，风力七级，范围正在逐渐扩大中。预计十四日十时起，我国淮河流域中、下游将有六、七级大风，风向偏南；山东、河北及江苏北部亦将有六、七级大风，风向偏南。中午十二时起，东北的南部、内蒙自治区及黄海、渤海，亦将有六级以上大风，风向偏南。
