Title: Many Countries' Public Opinion Welcomes the Agreement on the Repatriation of Prisoners of War
Author:
Date: 1953-06-11
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, June 10th: Public opinion and officials in many countries have welcomed the agreement reached by both sides of the Korean armistice negotiations on the basis of the eight-point proposal of the Korean-Chinese side regarding the issue of prisoner of war repatriation.

　　In Britain, the first editions of London evening newspapers on the 8th all carried this news, and readers eagerly snapped up the papers. British and Commonwealth spokesmen unanimously welcomed the agreement on the repatriation of prisoners of war, considering it an important step towards peace in Korea.

　　In France, Paris newspapers all published the news on the front page with large headlines, and expressed the hope that the agreement on this issue would lead to the signing of an armistice agreement soon. French diplomatic circles welcomed the agreement on the repatriation of prisoners of war.

　　A spokesman for the Dutch Foreign Ministry said that the Dutch government considered it very satisfactory that the final obstacle to the armistice had been removed. He hoped that the war would end soon.

　　According to Agence France-Presse, Indian Prime Minister Nehru, who was attending the Commonwealth Prime Ministers' Conference in Britain, told reporters on the 8th that the news from Korea greatly increased the hope of an armistice. He also said that now was the time to seek peace not only for Korea but also for the whole world.

　　Pakistani Foreign Minister Zafrulla Khan commented on the agreement on the issue of prisoners of war, saying: "As it stands, this agreement is satisfactory. Hopefully, an armistice agreement will be signed in the future, thereby preparing for discussions on other issues that need to be resolved, so that it can be said that the foundation of peace has been laid in the Far East."

　　Ceylon government officials welcomed the agreement on the repatriation of prisoners of war.

　　Indonesian Foreign Ministry officials said on the 9th that the agreement on the repatriation of prisoners of war was a "joyful event."

　　Tokyo newspapers in Japan on the 9th unanimously welcomed the agreement on the repatriation of prisoners of war.

　　Although the Syngman Rhee gang opposed the agreement on the issue of prisoner of war repatriation, the South Korean people still warmly welcomed the agreement. According to a Kyodo News reporter, a Japanese who had just returned to Japan from South Korea said that the South Korean people felt heartfelt joy at the prospect of the imminent end of the war.

　　Reporters from British and American news agencies reported that American soldiers in Korea danced for joy when they heard the news of the agreement on the prisoner of war issue. A Sergeant First Class named Frank Rolt said: "This news is too good to be believed. Now my wife doesn't have to worry about the imminent birth of our child." Soldier Chamov Chakes said: "I want to see an armistice so we can go home." British soldiers in Korea also warmly welcomed the agreement on the issue of prisoners of war. Soldier Ronald Chee said: "Let's hope this means we can go home sooner and people will no longer be killed."

### 许多国家舆论欢迎遣返战俘问题达成协议

1953-06-11
第1版()
专栏：

    许多国家舆论欢迎遣返战俘问题达成协议
    【新华社十日讯】许多国家的舆论和官方人士欢迎朝鲜停战谈判双方在朝中方面八点方案基础上达成的关于战俘遣返问题的协议。
    在英国，伦敦八日第一批发行的晚报都刊载了这个消息，读者热切地抢购报纸。英国和英联邦的发言人一致对遣返战俘问题取得协议表示欢迎，认为这是走向朝鲜和平的重要的一步。
    在法国，巴黎各报都以大字标题在第一版登载这个消息，并希望这个问题的协议会使停战协定很快签字。法国外交界人士欢迎遣返战俘问题取得协议。
    荷兰外交部一个发言人说，荷兰政府认为停战的最后障碍得以消除是非常令人满意的。他希望战争很快就会结束。
    据法新社报道，正在英国参加英联邦总理会议的印度总理尼赫鲁在八日接见记者时说，朝鲜来的消息大大提高了停战的希望。他又说，现在时机不但应该为朝鲜、而且应该为全世界谋取和平。
    巴基斯坦外交部长查弗鲁拉汗评论战俘问题的协议说：“就目前而言，这个协定是令人满意的。希望将来签订一个停战协定，从而准备讨论其他需要解决的问题，这样才能说，和平的基础已在远东奠定。”
    锡兰政府官员对于遣返战俘问题取得协议表示欢迎。
    印尼外交部人士在九日说，关于战俘遣返问题的协议是一件“喜事”。
    日本东京九日的报纸一致欢迎关于战俘遣返问题的协议。
    虽然李承晚匪帮反对战俘遣返问题的协议，南朝鲜人民仍然对这个协议热烈欢迎。据共同社记者报道，一个刚从南朝鲜回到日本的日本人说，南朝鲜人民对于战争即将结束的前景，感到衷心的快慰。
    英、美通讯社的记者报道说，在朝鲜的美军士兵听到战俘问题取得协议的消息时，高兴得跳起舞来了。一个名叫弗兰克·罗尔特的上士说：“这个消息太好了，简直不能使人相信。现在我的妻子可以不再为即将临盆的孩子担心了。”士兵查莫夫查凯斯说：“我愿意看到停战，那末我们就可以回家了。”在朝鲜的英国士兵也热烈欢迎关于战俘问题的协议。士兵罗纳尔德·奇说：“让我们希望这将意味着我们能更快地回家，人们不再被杀。”
