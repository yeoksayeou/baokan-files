Title: Central Committee of the Communist Youth League Holds Enlarged Fifth Plenary Session
Author:
Date: 1953-06-24
Page: 1

    The Central Committee of the Chinese New Democratic Youth League convened its enlarged Fifth Plenary Session on the morning of June 23rd. Attending the meeting were over 120 people, including members and alternate members of the Central Committee of the League, the first and second secretaries of the committees of major administrative regions and key provinces and cities, and responsible comrades from various departments of the Central Committee of the League. The meeting discussed and approved the "Work Report (Draft) at the Second National Congress of the Chinese New Democratic Youth League" and the "Report (Draft) on Amending the Constitution of the League." The meeting also decided that Hu Yaobang and Li Chang, secretaries of the Secretariat of the Central Committee of the League, would represent the First Central Committee in making the above two reports to the Second National Congress of the Communist Youth League.

### 青年团中央召开五中全会扩大会议

1953-06-24
第1版()
专栏：

    青年团中央召开五中全会扩大会议
    【本报讯】中国新民主主义青年团中央委员会于六月二十三日上午召开第五次委员会扩大会议。出席会议的有团中央委员、候补中央委员、各大行政区、主要省市委员会正副书记及团中央各部门负责同志等共一百二十余人。会议讨论并通过了“在中国新民主主义青年团第二次全国代表大会上的工作报告（草稿）”及“关于修改团章的报告（草稿）”。会议并决定由团中央书记处书记胡耀邦、李昌代表第一届中央委员会分别向青年团第二次全国代表大会作上述两个报告。
