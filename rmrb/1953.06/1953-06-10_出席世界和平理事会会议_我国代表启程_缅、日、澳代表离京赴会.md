Title: Chinese Representatives Depart to Attend World Peace Council Meeting; Burmese, Japanese, and Australian Representatives Leave Beijing for the Meeting
Author:
Date: 1953-06-10
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, June 9th:

    To Attend the World Peace Council Meeting
    Chinese Representatives Depart
    Burmese, Japanese, and Australian Representatives Leave Beijing for the Meeting
    [Xinhua News Agency, June 9th] The World Peace Council will hold a meeting on June 15th in Budapest, the capital of Hungary. The Chinese members attending this meeting include Guo Moruo, Vice-Chairman of the World Peace Council; Mao Dun, member of the Council and member of the Standing Committee of the World Peace Council; Cai Tingkai, Liu Ningyi, Wu Yaozong, Guo Fang, all members of the Council; and specially invited representatives Liu Guanyi and Xu Baoju, a group of eight people. They departed from Beijing by plane on the 9th, heading to the Soviet Union to attend the meeting.
    The other three Chinese members who will attend the meeting are: Li Dequan, Xiao San, and Li Yimang (member of the Standing Committee of the World Peace Council), and the other five specially invited representatives: Ulan (Mongolian), Ciren Namu (Tibetan), Rashidah (Tatar), Zheng Senyu, and Chen Dingmin are already abroad.
    Peng Zhen, Vice-Chairman of the China Peace Committee, and others saw them off at the airport.
    [Xinhua News Agency, June 9th] The Burmese representatives, Tekin Kodomai and Tekin Chimau, who were passing through Beijing to attend the World Peace Council meeting to be held on June 15th in Budapest, left Beijing by plane on the 9th to attend the meeting.
    Japanese representatives, Toogo Kameta and Kumaichi Yamamoto, also left Beijing on the 8th to attend the meeting.
    Australian representatives William Morrow and John Westman passed through our country and left Beijing on the 6th to attend the meeting.

### 出席世界和平理事会会议  我国代表启程  缅、日、澳代表离京赴会

1953-06-10
第1版()
专栏：

    出席世界和平理事会会议
    我国代表启程
    缅、日、澳代表离京赴会
    【新华社九日讯】世界和平理事会将于六月十五日在匈牙利首都布达佩斯举行会议。出席这次会议的中国理事、世界和平理事会副主席郭沫若，理事、世界和平理事会常务委员会委员茅盾，理事蔡廷锴、刘宁一、吴耀宗、郭芳，以及特邀代表刘贯一、许宝驹一行八人，已在九日乘飞机离北京取道苏联去出席会议。
    将出席会议的另三个中国理事：李德全、萧三、李一氓（世界和平理事会常务委员会委员）和另五个特邀代表：乌兰（蒙族）、策仁娜姆（藏族）、拉希达（塔塔尔族）、郑森禹、陈定民已在国外。
    到机场送行的有：中国人民保卫世界和平委员会副主席彭真等。
    【新华社九日讯】路过北京去参加将于六月十五日在布达佩斯举行的世界和平理事会会议的缅甸代表德钦哥都迈和德钦漆貌二人，于九日乘飞机离北京赴会。
    参加会议的日本代表龟田东伍和山本熊一二人，也已在八日离开北京。
    澳大利亚代表威廉·毛罗和约翰·威斯特曼二人，取道我国，在六日离北京去出席会议。
