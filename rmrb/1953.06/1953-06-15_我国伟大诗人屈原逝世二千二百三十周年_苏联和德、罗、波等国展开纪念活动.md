Title: 2230th Anniversary of the Death of China's Great Poet Qu Yuan: Soviet Union and Germany, Romania, Poland and other countries launch commemorative activities
Author:
Date: 1953-06-15
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, 14th. The Institute of Oriental Studies of the Soviet Academy of Sciences held an expanded academic conference on the 12th to commemorate the 2230th anniversary of the death of the great Chinese poet Qu Yuan. At the meeting, Master Edlin reported on Qu Yuan's life and works, and Master Diman reported on Qu Yuan's era. The speakers unanimously praised Qu Yuan as a great patriotic poet of the Chinese nation and introduced Qu Yuan's glorious contribution to the history of Chinese literature.
    Xinhua News Agency, 14th. The people of the German Democratic Republic, Romania, and Poland commemorated the 2230th anniversary of the death of China's great poet Qu Yuan.
    In the German Democratic Republic, Berlin's "Neues Deutschland" published Qu Yuan's famous poem "Guoshang" and Guo Moruo's article on Qu Yuan in May. The "Berliner Abendpost" published an article entitled "Qu Yuan Lives Forever in the Hearts of the People," describing Qu Yuan's life and works. The article also excerpted Qu Yuan's famous poem "Ai Ying" and lines from other poems. In addition, the "Berliner Zeitung," "Die Morgenpost," and "Neue Zeit" also published articles commemorating Qu Yuan. Berlin Radio broadcast a special program commemorating Qu Yuan.
    Romania's "Scînteia" also published an article commemorating Qu Yuan in May. The article emphasized that the anniversary of Qu Yuan's death is a good opportunity for the Romanian people to better understand the ancient culture of the great Chinese brother people. After describing Qu Yuan's patriotic activities as a politician and writer, the article pointed out that this Chinese poet ardently longed for a better life and a happy future during his lifetime. Now, under the leadership of the Chinese Communist Party and Chairman Mao Zedong, the Chinese people have broken the shackles of feudalism and imperialism and are moving towards an increasingly better and happier life.
    Polish orientalists held a special meeting to commemorate the great Chinese poet Qu Yuan during the 12th Congress held in Warsaw from June 7th to 8th.

### 我国伟大诗人屈原逝世二千二百三十周年  苏联和德、罗、波等国展开纪念活动

1953-06-15
第4版()
专栏：

    我国伟大诗人屈原逝世二千二百三十周年
    苏联和德、罗、波等国展开纪念活动
    【新华社莫斯科十四日电】苏联科学院东方学研究所十二日举行学术会议的扩大会议，纪念中国伟大诗人屈原逝世二千二百三十周年。会上，爱德林硕士报告了屈原的生平和创作，迪曼硕士报告了屈原的时代。报告人一致称颂屈原是伟大的中国民族的爱国诗人，并介绍了屈原的作品在中国文学史上的辉煌贡献。
    【新华社十四日讯】德意志民主共和国、罗马尼亚和波兰三国人民纪念我国伟大诗人屈原逝世二千二百三十周年。
    在德意志民主共和国，柏林的“新德意志报”曾在五月间登载了屈原的名诗“国殇”和郭沫若关于屈原的文章。“柏林晚邮报”以“屈原永远活在人民的心里”为题发表论文，叙述了屈原的生平和作品。论文并摘引了屈原的名诗“哀郢”和其他诗篇中的诗句。此外，“柏林日报”、
    “晨报”和“新时报”也登载了纪念屈原的文章。柏林广播电台播送了纪念屈原的特别节目。
    罗马尼亚的“火花报”也在五月间刊载了一篇纪念屈原的文章。文章着重指出，屈原的逝世纪念日对于罗马尼亚人民来说，是更好地了解伟大中国兄弟人民的古代文化的一个好机会。文章在叙述了作为政治家和文学家的屈原的爱国活动之后指出，这位中国诗人生前热诚地想望一种美好的生活和幸福的未来。现在，中国人民在中国共产党和毛泽东主席的领导下，打碎了封建主义和帝国主义的枷锁，正在向着日益美好和幸福的生活前进。
    波兰的东方学家们从六月七日到八日在华沙举行第十二次代表大会期间，曾举行了纪念中国伟大诗人屈原的特别会议。
