Title: Austrian People Demand the Conclusion of a Five-Power Peace Pact
Author: 
Date: 1953-06-03
Page: 4

    Austrian People Demand the Conclusion of a Five-Power Peace Pact
    [Xinhua News Agency, June 1st dispatch from Vienna] The movement of the Austrian people demanding the conclusion of a Five-Power Peace Pact is growing stronger.
    According to the "Voice of the Austrian People," the workers of the Egger-Union factory wrote to Austrian Chancellor Julius Raab, asking him to immediately come forward to advocate the conclusion of a peace pact. The Peace Committee of the Vienna Locomotive Factory wrote a letter to the Vienna Municipal Council, requesting support for the World People's Peace Congress's proposal on the conclusion of a Five-Power Peace Pact. The Peace Committee of the Austrian Fiat Automobile Factory also wrote a similar letter to the Austrian Municipal Council.

### 奥地利人民  要求缔结五大国和平公约

1953-06-03
第4版()
专栏：

    奥地利人民要求缔结五大国和平公约
    【新华社一日讯】塔斯社维也纳讯：奥地利人民要求缔结五大国和平公约的运动正日益壮大。
    据“奥地利人民之声报”说，埃格—尤尼翁工厂的工作人员曾写信给奥地利总理尤利乌斯·拉布，要求他立刻出面主张缔结和平公约。维也纳机车工厂的和平委员会写了一封信给维也纳市政委员会，要求支持世界人民和平大会关于缔结五大国和平公约的建议。奥地利菲亚特汽车工厂和平委员会也写了一封同样的信给奥地利市政委员会。
