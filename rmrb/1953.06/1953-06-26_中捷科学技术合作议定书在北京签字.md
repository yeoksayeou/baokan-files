Title: Sino-Czech Scientific and Technological Cooperation Protocol Signed in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1953-06-26
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, June 24th: The protocol of the first meeting of the Joint Committee for Scientific and Technological Cooperation between the People's Republic of China and the Czechoslovak Republic was signed in Beijing on the 24th. Signing on behalf of the Chinese side was Jia Tuofu, Chairman of the Chinese Group of the Sino-Czech Joint Committee for Scientific and Technological Cooperation and Deputy Director of the Financial and Economic Committee of the Central People's Government of the People's Republic of China. Signing on behalf of the Czechoslovak Republic was Vlasák, Chairman of the Czechoslovak Group of the Joint Committee and Deputy Director of the Czechoslovak State Planning Office.
    Attending the signing ceremony on the Chinese side were: Wu Xiuquan, Deputy Minister of Foreign Affairs; Wang Daohan, Member of the Chinese Group of the Joint Committee and Deputy Minister of the First Ministry of Machine Building of the Central People's Government; Xu Yixin, Director of the Soviet Union and Eastern European Affairs Department of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs; Shen Ping, Deputy Director of the Protocol Department; and Li Zhuozhi, Member of the Chinese Group and Commercial Counselor of the Chinese Embassy in Czechoslovakia. On the Czech side were: Kousal, Czechoslovak Ambassador to China; Finzl, Member of the Czechoslovak Group of the Joint Committee and Deputy Minister of the Czechoslovak Construction Industry; and Šal, Commercial Counselor of the Czechoslovak Embassy in China, and others.

### 中捷科学技术合作议定书在北京签字

1953-06-26
第1版()
专栏：

    中捷科学技术合作议定书在北京签字
    【新华社二十四日讯】中华人民共和国和捷克斯洛伐克共和国科学与技术合作联合委员会第一届会议之议定书已于二十四日在北京签订。代表中国方面签字的为中捷科学与技术合作联合委员会中国组主席、中华人民共和国中央人民政府政务院财政经济委员会副主任贾拓夫，代表捷克斯洛伐克共和国方面签字的为该联合委员会捷克斯洛伐克组主席、捷克斯洛伐克国家计划局副局长伏拉萨克。参加签字典礼的，我方有：外交部副部长伍修权，该联合委员会中国组委员、中央人民政府第一机械工业部副部长汪道涵，外交部苏联东欧司司长徐以新，交际处副处长沈平及中国组委员、我国驻捷克斯洛伐克大使馆商务参赞李琢之，捷方有：捷克斯洛伐克驻华大使康萨拉，该联合委员会捷克斯洛伐克组委员：捷克斯洛伐克建筑工业部副部长芬茨尔及捷克斯洛伐克驻华大使馆商务参赞沙尔等。
