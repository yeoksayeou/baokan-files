Title: Mongolian Art Troupe Departs for Home After Concluding Performances in Our Country
Author:
Date: 1953-06-03
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Harbin, June 2nd: The Art Troupe of the Mongolian People's Republic, after a visit to our country of more than forty days, departed from Harbin for home on the 2nd. Those who went to the station to see them off included Bai Wei, Director of the Cultural Bureau of the Songjiang Provincial People's Government, Zhu Fan, Deputy Director of the Cultural Bureau of the Harbin Municipal People's Government, and more than 250 representatives from cultural and artistic circles in Songjiang Province and Harbin City.

After arriving in Harbin on May 31st, Qiang Xiaochu, Chairman of the Songjiang Provincial People's Government, and Lü Qi'en, Mayor of the Harbin Municipal People's Government, jointly hosted a banquet for all members of the art troupe on the same day.

The Art Troupe of the Mongolian People's Republic stayed in Harbin for two days and performed three shows. The art troupe performed many wonderful programs, displaying the Mongolian people's brave and tenacious national style and the scenes of free and happy life, which were warmly welcomed and appreciated by tens of thousands of audiences in Harbin. During their stay in Harbin, the art troupe visited China's modern linen factory and toured the scenic area along the Songhua River.

Before leaving Harbin, the Art Troupe of the Mongolian People's Republic held a closing ceremony to mark the end of their performance tour in our country. Those who attended the closing ceremony included Qiang Xiaochu, Chairman of the Songjiang Provincial People's Government, Lü Qi'en, Mayor of the Harbin Municipal People's Government, and more than 2,400 representatives from various agencies and organizations in Songjiang Province and Harbin City. At the beginning of the closing ceremony, Yu Lin, Director of the Cultural and Educational Committee of the Songjiang Provincial People's Government, first delivered a thank-you speech on behalf of the Ministry of Culture of our Central People's Government. He said: The visit and performance of the Art Troupe of the Mongolian People's Republic in nine cities including Beijing, Shanghai, and Hangzhou have greatly enhanced and developed the profound friendship between the people of China and Mongolia. The head of the art troupe, Oyun, said in his farewell speech: After returning to our country, we will definitely convey the great achievements of the new China in various construction projects and the profound friendship expressed by the great Chinese people to the Mongolian people.

### 蒙古艺术团结束在我国的演出后启程返国

1953-06-03
第1版()
专栏：

    蒙古艺术团结束在我国的演出后启程返国
    【新华社哈尔滨二日电】来我国访问四十多天的蒙古人民共和国艺术团，已于二日离哈尔滨返国。前往车站欢送的有松江省人民政府文化局局长白韦、哈尔滨市人民政府文化局副局长朱繁及松江省与哈尔滨市文化艺术界代表二百五十余人。
    蒙古人民共和国艺术团于五月三十一日到达哈尔滨后，松江省人民政府主席强晓初与哈尔滨市人民政府市长吕其恩曾在当天联合设宴招待艺术团全体人员。
    蒙古人民共和国艺术团在哈尔滨停留两天，演出三场。艺术团表演了许多精彩节目，显示出蒙古人民勇敢、顽强的民族风格和自由幸福生活的情景，受到哈尔滨市上万观众的热烈欢迎和赞赏。艺术团在哈尔滨市期间，曾参观了我国现代化装置的亚麻工厂，游览了松花江边的风景区。
    蒙古人民共和国艺术团在离开哈尔滨前，曾举行了结束在我国演出访问的闭幕式。出席闭幕式的有松江省人民政府主席强晓初、哈尔滨市人民政府市长吕其恩等及松江省与哈尔滨市各机关团体代表两千四百余人。闭幕式开始，首先由松江省人民政府文化教育委员会主任于林代表我中央人民政府文化部致谢词。他说：蒙古人民共和国艺术团在我国北京、上海、杭州等九个城市的访问和表演，大大地增强和发展了中蒙两国人民的深厚友谊。艺术团团长奥云在告别词中说：我们回国后，一定把新中国的各项伟大建设成就和伟大的中国人民对蒙古人民所表示的深厚友情，传达给蒙古人民。
