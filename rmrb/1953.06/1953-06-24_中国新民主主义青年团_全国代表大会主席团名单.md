Title: List of Presidium Members of the National Congress of the China New Democratic Youth League
Author:

    Wang Wei, Wang Yuhui, Wang Zonghuai, Tian Demin, Zhu Yujin, Keli Geng (Mongolian), Du Ping (female), Du Qian, Li Chang, Li Chao, Gu Faming, Wu Qunbo (female), Song Yangchu, Zhou Ke, Hu Keshi, Hu Yaobang, Zha Bo Ping, Hao Jianxiu (female), Zhu Huai Shun, Kang Naier, Zhang Dazhong, Huang Jiafu, Chen Lian (female), Chen Xuzong, Zhang Ze, Xiang Nan, Qu Tangliang (female), Jia Shouyi, Yang Shu, Yang Haibo, Liao Chengzhi, Rong Gaotang, Jiang Nanxiang, Liu Daosheng, Lai Dachao, Han Xiacun, Luo Yi.
