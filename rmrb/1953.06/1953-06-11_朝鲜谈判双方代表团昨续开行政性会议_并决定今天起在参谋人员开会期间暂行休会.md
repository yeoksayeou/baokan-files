Title: Korean Negotiation: Both Sides' Delegation Continued Administrative Meeting Yesterday and Decided to Suspend Meetings from Today During Staff Meetings
Author: 
Date: 1953-06-11
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Kaesong, June 10th. The Korean armistice negotiation's Korean-Chinese delegation released the following communique on June 10th:

　　The delegations of both sides continued to hold an administrative meeting on June 10th, and decided that the plenary sessions of the delegations would be temporarily suspended from June 11th while the staff members held meetings, and would reconvene when necessary.

　　After the plenary session of the delegations of both sides on June 10th, the staff members of both sides held an administrative meeting.

### 朝鲜谈判双方代表团昨续开行政性会议  并决定今天起在参谋人员开会期间暂行休会

1953-06-11
第1版()
专栏：

    朝鲜谈判双方代表团昨续开行政性会议
    并决定今天起在参谋人员开会期间暂行休会
    【新华社开城十日电】朝鲜停战谈判朝中代表团六月十日发表公报如下：
    双方代表团六月十日继续举行行政性会议，并决定自六月十一日起参谋人员举行会议期间代表团大会暂行休会，在必要时复会。
    在六月十日双方代表团大会后双方参谋人员举行了行政性会议。
