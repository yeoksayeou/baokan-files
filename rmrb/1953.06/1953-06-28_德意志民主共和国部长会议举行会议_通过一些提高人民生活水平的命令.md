Title: Meeting of the Council of Ministers of the German Democratic Republic Held; Adoption of Orders to Improve the People's Living Standards
Author:
Date: 1953-06-28
Page: 4

    Xinhua News Agency, Berlin, June 27th (TASS): The Council of Ministers of the German Democratic Republic held a meeting in Berlin on June 25th. According to the German News Agency, a communique issued by the Press Office of the Prime Minister of the German Democratic Republic stated that the Council of Ministers adopted a number of orders aimed at improving the living standards of the people.
    These orders include: canceling the method of calculating wages after the decision to increase labor quotas was made; further improving the food supply to the people; improving the supply of work clothes for workers; further improving the supply of industrial products to the people; increasing investment by 600 million marks to build and repair housing; improving the supply of building materials to the people; reducing the amount of compulsory grain quotas to increase farmers' production; and increasing pensions and social insurance annuities.
    The Council of Ministers also adopted other orders to improve the living standards of the working people, and put forward specific measures for implementing these orders.
    Minister of Public Security Zaisser reported on the provocations and riots created by fascist agents and their accomplices, as well as the victims of this incident.
    The Council of Ministers mentioned the bravery and loyalty shown by the People's Police in this act of provocation by imperialist agents and expressed its willingness to help the families of those who sacrificed their lives in this provocative incident.

### 德意志民主共和国部长会议举行会议  通过一些提高人民生活水平的命令

1953-06-28
第4版()
专栏：

    德意志民主共和国部长会议举行会议
    通过一些提高人民生活水平的命令
    【新华社二十七日讯】塔斯社柏林二十六日讯：德意志民主共和国部长会议于六月二十五日在柏林举行会议。据德意志通讯社消息，德意志民主共和国总理新闻处发表的公报说，部长会议通过了一些旨在于提高人民生活水平的命令。
    这些命令计有：取消提高劳动定额的决定之后计算工资的办法；更进一步改善对人民的食品供应；改进工人工作服的供应；更进一步改进对人民的工业制品供应；增加投资六亿马克以新建和修缮住宅；改善对人民的建筑材料供应；减低义务公粮数额，以提高农民的生产；增加抚恤金和社会保险的年金。
    部长会议还通过了另一些提高劳动人民生活水平的命令，并提出了实行这些命令的具体办法。
    公安部长蔡塞尔报告了法西斯特务及其同谋者所制造的挑衅和骚乱以及这次事件的受害者的情况。
    部长会议提到人民警察在这次帝国主义特务挑衅行动中所表现的勇敢和忠诚，并表示愿意帮助在这次挑衅事件中牺牲的人的家属。
