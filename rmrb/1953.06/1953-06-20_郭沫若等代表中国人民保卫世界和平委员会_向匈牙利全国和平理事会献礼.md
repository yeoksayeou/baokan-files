Title: Guo Moruo and Others, Representing the Chinese People's Committee for World Peace, Present Gifts to the Hungarian National Peace Council
Author:
Date: 1953-06-20
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Budapest, June 19th: Guo Moruo, Vice-Chairman of the Standing Committee of the World Peace Council, Chairman of the Chinese People's Committee for World Peace, Cai Tingkai, a member of the World Peace Council, and Liu Guanyi, Special Representative and Secretary-General of the Chinese People's Committee for World Peace, who attended the World Peace Council meeting held in Budapest, presented gifts to the Hungarian National Peace Council on the 18th on behalf of the Chinese People's Committee for World Peace. Andics, Chairman of the Hungarian National Peace Council and Deputy Minister of Higher Education of Hungary, and Bánki, Secretary-General of the Hungarian National Peace Council, accepted the gifts on behalf of the Hungarian National Peace Council. The gifts included an ivory boat, a painting by the famous Chinese painter Qi Baishi, and cloisonné vases, etc.

　　Guo Moruo addressed the audience amidst warm applause. He said: Hungary and China are brotherly countries and two strong fortresses in the peace and democracy camp headed by the Soviet Union. Our goals in the peace movement are the same. The Hungarian National Peace Council has made achievements in the cause of peace. The current World Peace Council meeting in Budapest is a recognition by the people of the world of the achievements of the Hungarian people in peace. We are now presenting the achievements of the Chinese working people to you to express our love and respect for the Hungarian people.

　　Andics then delivered a reply. She said: It is an honor for the Hungarian people to be able to fight for peace together with the great Chinese people. She expressed her belief that the current World Peace Council meeting will surely bring greater achievements to the cause of world peace, and that this cause is inseparable from the efforts of the great Chinese people. Finally, Andics presented a movie projector and other gifts to Guo Moruo and other Chinese representatives to express the Hungarian people's love and respect for the Chinese people.

### 郭沫若等代表中国人民保卫世界和平委员会  向匈牙利全国和平理事会献礼

1953-06-20
第1版()
专栏：

    郭沫若等代表中国人民保卫世界和平委员会
    向匈牙利全国和平理事会献礼
    【新华社布达佩斯十九日电】出席在布达佩斯召开的世界和平理事会会议的世界和平理事会常务委员会副主席、中国人民保卫世界和平委员会主席郭沫若，世界和平理事会理事蔡廷锴，特邀代表、中国人民保卫世界和平委员会秘书长刘贯一于十八日代表中国人民保卫世界和平委员会向匈牙利全国和平理事会献礼。匈牙利全国和平理事会主席、匈牙利高等教育部副部长安迪奇，匈牙利全国和平理事会秘书长班吉代表匈牙利全国和平理事会接受礼品。礼品包括象牙船一只，中国名画家齐白石作品一幅，景泰蓝花瓶等。
    郭沫若在热烈的掌声中致词。他说：匈牙利和中国是兄弟国家，是以苏联为首的和平民主阵营中的两个坚强堡垒。我们在和平运动中的目标是一致的。匈牙利全国和平理事会在和平事业中是有成就的。目前世界和平理事会在布达佩斯召开，正是世界人民对匈牙利人民和平成就的公认。我们现在把中国劳动人民的成果献给你们，以表达我们对匈牙利人民的敬爱。
    安迪奇接着致答词。她说：匈牙利人民能够和伟大的中国人民为和平而共同战斗，这是匈牙利人民的光荣。她表示相信目前的世界和平理事会会议一定会给世界和平事业带来更大的成就，而这个事业是和伟大中国人民的努力分不开的。安迪奇最后把电影机一架及其他礼品多件赠给郭沫若和其他中国代表，以表达匈牙利人民对中国人民的敬爱。
