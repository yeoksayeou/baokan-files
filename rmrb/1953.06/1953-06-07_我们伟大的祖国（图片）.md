Author:
Date: 1953-06-07

    Our Great Motherland
    The No. 321 Geological Exploration Team of the Ministry of Geology of the Central People's Government has completed its assigned geological exploration tasks in the past year and has also trained a large number of geological drilling personnel. The picture shows the team members using a compass to measure the strike and dip of the strata.

### 我们伟大的祖国（图片）

1953-06-07
第1版()
专栏：

    我们伟大的祖国
    中央人民政府地质部三二一地质勘探队，一年来已完成了规定的各项地质勘探任务，同时还培养了大批的地质钻探人员。这是队员们正在用罗盘测量地层的走向及倾斜的情形。
