Title: U.S. Labor, Religious, and Cultural Leaders Initiate Establishment of American Grassroots Movement for Peace Organization
Author:
Date: 1953-06-21
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Prague, June 19th: The New York "Daily Worker" reports that the National Grassroots Movement for Peace Organization, initiated by leaders from American labor, religious, professional, and cultural circles, is scheduled to hold a full-day meeting in Washington on June 25th – the third anniversary of the Korean War. Participating representatives will include peace representatives from various national organizations, who will visit congressmen, senators, and government officials in the capital.

A spokesperson for the Grassroots Movement for Peace Organization stated at the New York headquarters: "The American people need and desire peace, and their rising peace sentiment can accelerate the peaceful resolution of issues in Korea and Asia." He said that the establishment of this Washington Grassroots Movement for Peace Organization is to counter the support of pro-war government and Congress for "the 'China Lobby' of sympathizers with Chiang Kai-shek and pro-fascist elements like 'Asia Firsters'." He added: "If the people do not resolutely express their wishes, this group of warmongers may destroy the great opportunity for peace that is now unfolding before us."

Recently, some prominent social leaders have joined the list of initiators of the Grassroots Movement for Peace Organization. These include: Bishop Arthur W. Moulton of Salt Lake City; Reverend J.E. Bowen of Harlan, Kentucky; Reverend J.K. Thornton of Manyfic, Arkansas; and William Harrison of Boston.

### 美国劳工、宗教、文化各界领袖  发起成立美国院外运动和平组织

1953-06-21
第4版()
专栏：

    美国劳工、宗教、文化各界领袖
    发起成立美国院外运动和平组织
   【新华社布拉格十九日电】纽约“工人日报”报道，美国劳工界、宗教界、自由职业者和文化界的领袖们所发起的全国院外运动和平组织，定于六月二十五日——朝鲜战争第三周年——在华盛顿举行一个整天的会议。与会代表将包括有各种全国性组织的和平代表，他们将访问首都的众议员、参议员和政府官员。
    院外运动和平组织发言人在纽约总部谈称：“美国人民需要并且希望和平，他们把高涨的和平情绪表现出来，可以在朝鲜和亚洲加速问题的和平解决。”他说，成立这个华盛顿院外运动和平组织，是为了还击主张战争的政府和国会对于“同情蒋介石的‘运动援华院外集团’和‘亚洲第一论者’之类亲法西斯分子”的支持。他又说：“如果人民不坚决表示他们的愿望，这一群主张战争的人就有可能摧毁现在已经展开在我们面前的争取和平的大好机会。”
    最近又有些著名的社会领袖参加了院外运动和平组织发起人的名单，这些人是：盐湖城的阿瑟·吴·墨尔顿主教；肯塔基州哈兰地方的约·埃·鲍恩牧师；阿肯色州曼尼菲的约·克·桑顿牧师；波士顿的威廉·哈里逊。
