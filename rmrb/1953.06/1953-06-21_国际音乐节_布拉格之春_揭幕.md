Title: International Music Festival "Prague Spring" Opens

Author: 
Date: 1953-06-21
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Prague: The international music festival "Prague Spring" opened in Prague on the 15th, with the first concert held in the Smetana Hall. This music festival is a major event in international cultural life, and will review the achievements of progressive music from around the world.

Famous musicians and singers from the Soviet Union, Hungary, Bulgaria, Romania, Poland, Chile, and France have arrived in Prague to participate in the music festival. Chinese musicians He Luting, Meng Bo, and Shi Lemeng will participate in the music festival as observers.

### 国际音乐节“布拉格之春”揭幕

1953-06-21
第4版()
专栏：

    国际音乐节“布拉格之春”揭幕
    【新华社十六日讯】据塔斯社布拉格讯：国际音乐节“布拉格之春”于十五日在布拉格揭幕，首次演奏会在斯米塔娜大厅举行。这次音乐节是国际文化生活中的一件大事，在音乐节中将检阅全世界进步音乐的成就。
    苏联、匈牙利、保加利亚、罗马尼亚、波兰、智利和法国等国的著名音乐家和歌唱家，已到达布拉格参加音乐节。中国音乐工作者贺绿汀、孟波和时乐蒙三人将以列席的身份参加音乐节。
