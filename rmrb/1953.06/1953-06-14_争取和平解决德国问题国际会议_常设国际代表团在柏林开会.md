Title: International Conference for Peaceful Resolution of the German Question: Standing International Delegation Meets in Berlin
Author:
Date: 1953-06-14
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Berlin, June 13th: TASS, Berlin, June 12th: The German News Agency published the following statement from the Standing International Delegation of the International Conference for the Peaceful Resolution of the German Question:

    On June 11, 1953, the Standing International Delegation held a meeting in the German Academy of Sciences building in Berlin. This delegation was elected at the International Conference for the Peaceful Resolution of the German Question last November.

    Prominent public figures from East Germany, West Germany, Austria, Belgium, Denmark, Finland, France, Great Britain, Italy, Norway, Poland, Sweden, Switzerland, and Czechoslovakia participated in the meeting.

    The first meeting was chaired by Dr. Dieckmann, Chairman of the People's Chamber of the German Democratic Republic. The presidium included the secretary of the Standing International Delegation, Mr. Erlfes, former Mayor of Munich-Gräfelfing, “L’Esprit” editor Jean-Marie Domenach, and Paris attorney Michel Bruguière.

    British journalist Gordon Schaffer, Jean-Marie Domenach, and Otto Buchwitz delivered reports at the meeting.

    The meeting is continuing.

### 争取和平解决德国问题国际会议  常设国际代表团在柏林开会

1953-06-14
第4版()
专栏：

    争取和平解决德国问题国际会议
    常设国际代表团在柏林开会
    【新华社柏林十三日电】塔斯社柏林十二日电：德意志通讯社发表了争取和平解决德国问题国际会议常设国际代表团的声明如下：
    一九五三年六月十一日，常设国际代表团在柏林德国科学院大厦举行会议，这个代表团是去年十一月在争取和平解决德国问题国际会议上选出来的。
    参加会议的有东德、西德、奥地利、比利时、丹麦、芬兰、法国、英国、意大利、挪威、波兰、瑞典、瑞士和捷克斯洛伐克的著名公众人士。
    第一次会议由德意志民主共和国人民议院主席狄克曼博士任主席。在主席团中有常设国际代表团书记——前慕尼黑—格拉巴什市长艾尔费斯先生、“灵报”编辑让·玛丽·多门纳克、巴黎法庭律师密歇尔·布吕吉埃。
    英国新闻记者戈登·夏菲尔、让·玛丽·多门纳克和奥托·布赫威茨在会上作了报告。
    会议正继续进行中。
