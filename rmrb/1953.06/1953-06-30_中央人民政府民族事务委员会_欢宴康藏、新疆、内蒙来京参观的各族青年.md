Title: The Nationalities Affairs Commission of the Central People's Government Hosts a Banquet for Youths of Various Nationalities from Kang Tibetan Areas, Xinjiang, and Inner Mongolia Visiting Beijing
Author:
Date: 1953-06-30
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, June 29th:

The Nationalities Affairs Commission of the Central People's Government hosted a banquet in the capital on the 29th for all members of the Tibetan Youth Visiting Group, the Qamdo Youth Visiting Group, the Cultural Work Troupe of the Political Department of the Fifth Army of the Chinese People's Liberation Army, and the Inner Mongolia Normal College Visiting Group. Attending the banquet were Liu Geping, Wang Feng, Liu Chun, and Zhang Zhiyi, vice chairmen of the Nationalities Affairs Commission of the Central People's Government, and committee members in Beijing. General Xiao Hua, Deputy Director of the General Political Department of the Central People's Revolutionary Military Commission of the Central People's Government, General Zhang Guohua, Commander of the Tibet Military Region of the Chinese People's Liberation Army, Deputy Political Commissar General Fan Ming, Yu Beichen, Minister of the United Front Work Department of the Central Committee of the Chinese New Democratic Youth League and Secretary-General of the All-China Youth Federation, and Ranba Nangji Wangdui, Director of the Dalai Lama's Office in Beijing, and Luo Wang, Acting Director of the Panchen's Office in Beijing, were invited to attend.

Liu Geping, Vice Chairman of the Nationalities Affairs Commission of the Central People's Government, delivered a welcoming speech. He said that all of you present are the younger generation on various construction posts in the motherland, coming from the remote border areas of the motherland. Today's gathering in Beijing symbolizes the further consolidation of the friendship and unity of all nationalities in our motherland. He hoped that after the visit, everyone would convey the construction situation of the motherland and the greatness and loveliness of the motherland to people from all walks of life, and strive to build a big family of friendly cooperation among all nationalities.

During the banquet, youths of all nationalities frequently toasted to the health of Chairman Mao, the great leader of all nationalities, and to the great unity of all nationalities.

In the evening, a song and dance party was held.

### 中央人民政府民族事务委员会  欢宴康藏、新疆、内蒙来京参观的各族青年

1953-06-30
第1版()
专栏：

    中央人民政府民族事务委员会
    欢宴康藏、新疆、内蒙来京参观的各族青年
    【新华社二十九日讯】中央人民政府民族事务委员会二十九日在首都欢宴西藏地区青年参观团、昌都地区青年参观团、中国人民解放军第五军政治部文艺工作团和内蒙古师范学院参观团等全体团员。出席宴会的有中央民族事务委员会副主任委员刘格平、汪锋、刘春、张执一和在京委员。应邀作陪参加宴会的有中央人民政府人民革命军事委员会总政治部副主任萧华将军、中国人民解放军西藏军区司令员张国华将军、副政治委员范明将军，中国新民主主义青年团中央委员会统一战线工作部部长、中华全国民主青年联合会秘书长于北辰，西藏达赖喇嘛驻京办事处处长然巴·囊吉旺堆、班禅驻京办事处代处长罗旺等人。
    中央人民政府民族事务委员会副主任委员刘格平致欢迎词。他说，在座各位都是在祖国建设各个岗位上的青年一代，来自祖国的遥远边疆，今天在北京欢聚，象征了我们祖国各民族友爱团结的更加巩固。希望各位参观后，将祖国的建设情况和祖国的伟大可爱，向各界人民传达，为建设各族人民友好合作的大家庭而努力。
    席间，各民族青年频频为各族人民的伟大领袖毛主席的健康干杯，为各民族的大团结干杯。
    晚上，举行了歌舞联欢晚会。
