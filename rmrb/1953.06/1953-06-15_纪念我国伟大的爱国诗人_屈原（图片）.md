	During the Warring States period, the Chu culture in China's Yangtze River basin had already developed to a high degree. From the unearthed Chu cultural relics, including bronzes, jades, woodware, and especially the abundant lacquerware patterns, we can see a free, unrestrained, lively, and fresh style. In particular, the discovery of Chu silk paintings, lacquer shields, boats, chariots, measuring tools, weights, and many silk and hemp textiles and other daily necessities provides important new information for China's history and history of arts and crafts. The works of the great patriotic poet Qu Yuan grew up in this social and cultural background.

    战国时代我国长江流域的楚文化已经发展到很高的程度。我们从出土的楚文物中的铜器、玉器、木器等各种器物和特别丰富的漆器花纹上面，可以看到一种自由奔放、活泼清新的风格。特别是楚人的绢帛画、漆盾、舟、车、量、衡、以及许多丝、麻织品和其他日用品的发现，为我国历史和工艺美术史提供了重要的新资料。伟大的爱国诗人屈原的作品，就是在这种社会文化背景中生长起来的。
