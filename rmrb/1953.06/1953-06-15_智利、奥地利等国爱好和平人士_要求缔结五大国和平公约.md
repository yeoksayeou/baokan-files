Title: Peace-loving People in Chile, Austria, and Other Countries Demand the Conclusion of a Five-Power Peace Pact
Author: 
Date: 1953-06-15
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Buenos Aires, June 12th (TASS dispatch): Chilean newspapers report that the Chilean National Peace Committee has issued a declaration calling on all Chilean citizens and public organizations to demand the conclusion of a five-power peace pact. The committee pointed out that 875,000 Chilean people already support this peace initiative and called on all citizens and public organizations to promote the urgent need for mutual understanding among the five major powers and to strive for government support for the proposal to conclude a peace pact.

Xinhua News Agency, Vienna, June 12th (TASS dispatch): Austria is holding a "Peace Week" under the slogan of striving for the conclusion of a five-power peace pact. "Voice of the Austrian People" reported that in the first three days of "Peace Week," hundreds of gatherings have already been held in various districts of Vienna and other Austrian cities. Participants in the meetings unanimously demanded that the Austrian government support the declaration of the World People's Peace Congress on the conclusion of a five-power peace pact.

　　In Sankt Pölten, thousands of local residents participated in the "Peace Festival."

　　Workers at the Vienna locomotive manufacturing factory wrote to Austrian Chancellor Raab, asking him to support the World People's Peace Congress's declaration on the conclusion of a five-power peace pact.

　　A delegation of workers from the Pau-Montag factory went to the Austrian Parliament to deliver a letter to the leaders of various parties, asking the parliamentarians to support the conclusion of a five-power peace pact.

### 智利、奥地利等国爱好和平人士  要求缔结五大国和平公约

1953-06-15
第4版()
专栏：

    智利、奥地利等国爱好和平人士
    要求缔结五大国和平公约
    【新华社十四日讯】塔斯社布宜诺斯艾利斯十二日讯：智利报纸报道，智利全国和平委员会发表宣言，号召智利全体公民和公共团体要求缔结五大国和平公约。委员会指出已有八十七万五千智利人民支持这个和平倡议，并号召全体公民和公共团体宣传五大国彼此谅解的迫切需要，并争取政府支持缔结和平公约的建议。
    【新华社十四日讯】塔斯社维也纳十二日讯：在争取缔结五大国和平公约的口号下，奥地利正在举行“和平周”。“奥地利人民之声报”报道：在“和平周”头三天内，维也纳各区及其它奥地利城市已经举行了好几百个集会。参加会议的人一致要求奥地利政府支持缔结五大国和平公约。
    在圣波尔顿，数千当地居民参加了“和平联欢节”。
    维也纳机车制造工厂的职工写信给奥地利总理拉布，要求他支持世界人民和平大会关于缔结五大国和平公约的宣言。
    保—蒙达奇工厂职工的一个代表团到奥地利议会去，交了一封信给各党派的领袖，要求议员们支持缔结五大国和平公约。
