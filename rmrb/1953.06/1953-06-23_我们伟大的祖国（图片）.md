Our Great Motherland
    This year, Xingtang County, Hebei Province, has had a bumper wheat harvest. The Qiu Laoyun and Gai Genshan agricultural producers' cooperatives implemented "narrow-row close planting," and the wheat grew particularly well. These are the members of the Gai Genshan agricultural producers' cooperative. They are using carts to transport the cut wheat to the threshing ground.

### 我们伟大的祖国（图片）

1953-06-23
第1版()
专栏：

    我们伟大的祖国
    河北省行唐县今年小麦丰收。仇老云和盖根山两个农业生产合作社实行了“窄行密植”，小麦长得特别好。这是盖根山农业生产合作社的社员们。他们正把割下的麦子用大车运到压麦场去。
