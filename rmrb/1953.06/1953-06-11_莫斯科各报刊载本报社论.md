Title: Moscow Newspapers Publish This Newspaper's Editorial
Author:
Date: 1953-06-11
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, 10th -- Moscow newspapers today all published the People's Daily editorial of June 9th: "Opening the Road to a Peaceful Resolution of the Korean Question." The newspapers also published news of American planes continuing to bomb peaceful cities and villages in Korea. The newspapers also carried reports of reactions from the US and Britain.

### 莫斯科各报刊载本报社论

1953-06-11
第1版()
专栏：

    莫斯科各报刊载本报社论
    【新华社莫斯科十日电】莫斯科各报十日都登载了人民日报九日的社论：“打开和平解决朝鲜问题的道路”。各报并登载美机继续轰炸朝鲜和平城市和乡村的消息。各报也登载了美英方面的反应。
