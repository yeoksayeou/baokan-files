Title: North Korean and Chinese Delegations, via Our Government, Formally Invite Five Neutral Nations to Participate in the Neutral Nations Repatriation Commission
Author:
Date: 1953-06-12
Page: 1

    Xinhua News Agency, June 11th: The Ministry of Foreign Affairs of the Central People's Government of the People's Republic of China delivered copies of the agreement on the repatriation of prisoners of war to the ambassadors and envoys of the five neutral nations designated to participate in the Neutral Nations Repatriation Commission—Poland, Czechoslovakia, Sweden, Switzerland, and India—on June 8th.
    On June 10th, Vice Minister of Foreign Affairs Zhang Hanfu received the ambassadors of Czechoslovakia and India, the ambassadors of Sweden, the minister of Switzerland, and the chargé d'affaires ad interim of Poland, respectively. He explained that the Korean armistice talks' North Korean and Chinese delegation had agreed with the United Nations Command delegation on the 10th that both negotiating parties would separately extend invitations to the five neutral nations participating in the Neutral Nations Repatriation Commission. The delegation of the Korean People's Army and the Chinese People's Volunteers at the armistice talks are willing, via the Government of the People's Republic of China, to formally invite Poland, Czechoslovakia, Sweden, Switzerland, and India to agree to participate in the work of the Neutral Nations Repatriation Commission in accordance with the terms of the agreement on the repatriation of prisoners of war, and hope to learn the opinions of their respective governments. The five ambassadors and envoys all indicated that they would immediately report to their respective governments.

### 朝中代表团经由我国政府  正式邀请五个中立国家参加中立国遣返委员会

1953-06-12
第1版()
专栏：

    朝中代表团经由我国政府
    正式邀请五个中立国家参加中立国遣返委员会
    【新华社十一日讯】中华人民共和国中央人民政府外交部于六月八日将关于战俘遣返问题的协议的抄本交给协议中规定的参加中立国遣返委员会的五个中立国家：波兰、捷克斯洛伐克、瑞典、瑞士和印度的驻华使节。
    六月十日我外交部章汉夫副部长分别接见了捷克斯洛伐克大使、印度大使、瑞典大使、瑞士公使及波兰临时代办，说明朝鲜停战谈判朝中代表团已于十日与联合国军代表团协议，由谈判双方分别向参加中立国遣返委员会的五个中立国家提出邀请；朝鲜人民军与中国人民志愿军停战谈判代表团愿意经由中华人民共和国政府正式邀请波兰、捷克斯洛伐克、瑞典、瑞士和印度同意按照战俘遣返问题协议条款参加中立国遣返委员会的工作，并希望得知各该国政府的意见。五国使节均表示当立即报告本国政府。
