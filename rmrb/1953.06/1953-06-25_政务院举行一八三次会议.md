Title: The Government Administration Council Holds its 183rd Meeting
Author:
Date: 1953-06-25
Page: 1

    Xinhua News Agency: The 183rd meeting of the Central People's Government Administration Council was held on the afternoon of the 19th. The meeting listened to and approved the report by Li Shucheng, Minister of Agriculture of the Central People's Government, on the state of agricultural production in the spring of 1953.

### 政务院举行一八三次会议

1953-06-25
第1版()
专栏：

    政务院举行一八三次会议
    【新华社讯】中央人民政府政务院第一百八十三次政务会议在十九日下午举行。会议听取和批准了中央人民政府农业部李书城部长关于一九五三年春季农业生产情况的报告。
