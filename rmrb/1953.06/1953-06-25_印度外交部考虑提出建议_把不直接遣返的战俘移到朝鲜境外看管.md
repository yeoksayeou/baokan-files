Title: Indian Foreign Ministry Considering Proposal to Move POWs Not Directly Repatriated Outside of Korea for Custody
Author:
Date: 1953-06-25
Page: 1
    
    Xinhua News Agency, June 24th, New Delhi: In light of Syngman Rhee's deliberate sabotage of the Korean armistice, Indian Foreign Ministry officials are considering a plan to move POWs who are not directly repatriated outside of Korea for custody after the armistice.
    According to reports, Indian Foreign Ministry officials believe that the current situation has created new responsibilities regarding the issue of guarding POWs, because if the recovered Korean POWs remain near Syngman Rhee's troops, the Syngman Rhee group will conspire to further secretly or openly "release" the POWs.
    Sources say that other countries participating in the Neutral Nations Repatriation Commission may soon make a joint decision. They may recommend sending POWs detained by the US side outside of Korea, but not too far from the Korean border.

### 印度外交部考虑提出建议  把不直接遣返的战俘移到朝鲜境外看管

1953-06-25
第1版()
专栏：

    印度外交部考虑提出建议
    把不直接遣返的战俘移到朝鲜境外看管
    【新华社二十四日讯】新德里消息：印度外交部人士鉴于李承晚蓄意破坏朝鲜停战，正在考虑在停战以后把不直接遣返的战俘移到朝鲜以外来加以看管的办法。
    据消息说，印度外交部人士认为：目前的时局已使看管战俘问题产生了新的责任，因为被追回的朝鲜战俘假如仍然处于李承晚军队附近的话，李承晚集团将阴谋进一步暗中或公开地“释放”战俘。
    有消息说：其他建议中的参加中立国遣返委员会的国家可能不久作出共同决定。它们可能建议把美方拘留的战俘送到朝鲜境外去，但不应离朝鲜国境太远。
