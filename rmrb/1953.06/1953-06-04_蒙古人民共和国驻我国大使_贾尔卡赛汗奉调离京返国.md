Title: Mongolian People's Republic Ambassador to China, Jalkhaajav, Departs Beijing Upon Recall

Author:

Date: 1953-06-04
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, June 3rd:

    Jalkhaajav, the first Ambassador Extraordinary and Plenipotentiary of the Mongolian People's Republic to China, departed Beijing on June 3rd upon recall.  Those who went to the airport to see him off included: Zhang Hanfu, Vice Minister of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs of the Central People's Government; Wang Bingnan, Director of the General Office; Chen Jiakang, Director of the Asian Affairs Department; and Wang Zhuoru, Director of the Protocol Department. Diplomatic envoys of various countries to China and S. Luvsandorj, Chargé d'Affaires ad interim of the Mongolian People's Republic Embassy in China, and all the embassy staff members also went to see him off.

### 蒙古人民共和国驻我国大使  贾尔卡赛汗奉调离京返国

1953-06-04
第1版()
专栏：

    蒙古人民共和国驻我国大使
    贾尔卡赛汗奉调离京返国
    【新华社三日讯】蒙古人民共和国首任驻华特命全权大使贾尔卡赛汗在三日奉调离京返国。前往机场送行的有：中央人民政府外交部副部长章汉夫，办公厅主任王炳南，亚洲司司长陈家康，交际处处长王倬如等。各国驻华使节和蒙古人民共和国驻华大使馆临时代办斯林道尔吉暨全体馆员也前往送行。
