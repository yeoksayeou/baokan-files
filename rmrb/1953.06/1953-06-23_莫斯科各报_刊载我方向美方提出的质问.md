Title: Moscow Newspapers Publish Our Side's Questions to the U.S. Side
Author:
Date: 1953-06-23
Page: 1

    Moscow Newspapers
    Publish Our Side's Questions to the U.S. Side
    [Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, 22nd] Moscow newspapers have been carrying reports for days about the provocative actions taken by the Syngman Rhee gang with the tacit consent of the United States. On the 21st, the newspapers published the Xinhua News Agency report of the questions posed by Marshal Kim Il-sung and General Peng Dehuai to Clark on the 19th, and Harrison's letter to General Nam Il on the 18th.

### 莫斯科各报  刊载我方向美方提出的质问

1953-06-23
第1版()
专栏：

    莫斯科各报
    刊载我方向美方提出的质问
    【新华社莫斯科二十二日电】莫斯科各报连日登载李承晚匪帮在美国默许下采取的挑衅行动。各报在二十一日登载了新华社报道的金日成元帅和彭德怀将军十九日向克拉克提出的质问，和哈利逊十八日给南日大将的信。
