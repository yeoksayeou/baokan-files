Title: I Sign Trade Agreement with French Industrial and Commercial Trade Delegation
Author:
Date: 1953-06-07
Page: 1

    Xinhua News Agency, June 6th: The China National Import and Export Corporation and the French Industrial and Commercial Trade Delegation, led by Mr. Depla, have reached an agreement after amicable negotiations in a short period of time, based on the principle of equality and mutual benefit. Both parties held a signing ceremony in Beijing on June 5th. The agreement stipulates that the total trade volume for both sides is 10 million pounds each (payable in French francs). The goods sold by the French side to the Chinese side mainly include various production equipment, including various steel products, machinery, automobiles, medicine, medical equipment, instruments, and chemical raw materials. The goods sold by the Chinese side to the French side include tea, silk, vegetable oilseeds, bristles, casings, feathers, and other products. The specific transaction conditions for the export goods of both parties will be stipulated in separate specific transaction contracts signed by both parties.
    Signing on behalf of the China National Import and Export Corporation was Lu Xuzhang, the manager of the company. Signing on behalf of the French side were Depla, head of the French Industrial and Commercial Trade Delegation, Deputy Head Dument, and members Hirson, Morand, Rossier, Aubrat, Bouvier, Lachaud, Marcolan, Picaud, Demoran, Demontant, Fourcade, Bourquin, Felix, and Frohoff.
    Attending the signing ceremony were Nan Hanchen, Chairman of the China Council for the Promotion of International Trade, Ji Chao Ding, Secretary-General, Xie Xiaonai, Director of the Liaison Department, Zhan Wu, Deputy General Manager of the Bank of China, Cao Zhongshu and Li Yingji, Deputy Managers of the China National Import and Export Corporation, and more than ten managers from various export companies.

### 我与法国工商业贸易代表团签订贸易协定

1953-06-07
第1版()
专栏：

    我与法国工商业贸易代表团签订贸易协定
    【新华社六日讯】中国进出口公司与以德普拉为团长的法国工商业贸易代表团，根据平等互利的原则，在短时间内，经过融洽的谈判，已经达成协议，双方并于六月五日在北京举行了签字仪式。该协定规定贸易总金额双方各为一千万英镑（用法郎支付）。法国方面售给中国方面的商品主要为各种生产器材，包括各种钢材、机器、汽车、医药、医疗器械、仪器和化工原料等。中国方面售给法国方面的商品包括茶叶、蚕丝、植物油料、猪鬃、肠衣、羽毛及其他产品。双方出口货的具体交易条件将由双方另签定具体交易合同规定之。
    代表中国进出口公司签字的是该公司经理卢绪章。法国方面签字的为法国工商业贸易代表团团长德普拉、副团长杜孟及团员伊尔逊、摩兰、罗西埃、奥布拉、布维埃、拉萧、马尔古朗、皮卡、德摩兰、德蒙丹当、弗尔卡德、布尔坎、费利克斯、佛罗浩夫。
    参加签字仪式的有中国国际贸易促进委员会主席南汉宸、秘书长冀朝鼎、联络部部长谢筱乃、中国银行副总经理詹武、中国进出口公司副经理曹中枢、李应吉及各出口公司经理等十余人。
