Title: Third Anniversary of the Korean Fatherland Liberation War Photo Exhibition Opens in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1953-06-26
Page: 1

    Korean Fatherland Liberation War Third Anniversary
    Photo Exhibition Opens in Beijing
    [Xinhua News Agency, June 25th] The "Photo Exhibition on the Third Anniversary of the Korean People's Fatherland Liberation War" hosted by the Embassy of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea in China opened on June 25th at the Water Pavilion in Zhongshan Park, Beijing. The opening ceremony was presided over by Xu Zhe, Chargé d'Affaires ad interim of the Korean Embassy in China.
    Those invited to attend the opening ceremony included: Chen Shutong, Vice Chairman of the Chinese People's Committee to Resist America and Aid Korea; Zhang Xiruo, Peng Zemin, and Luo Longji, Standing Committee members; Zhang Hanfu, Deputy Minister of Foreign Affairs of the Central People's Government; Ding Xilin, Deputy Minister of Culture; Zeng Zhaolun, Deputy Minister of Higher Education; Zhu Zaoguan, Deputy Director of the General Office of the Central People's Revolutionary Military Commission; Chen Jiakang, Director of the Asian Department of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs of the Central People's Government; Hong Shen, Director of the Bureau of Foreign Cultural Relations of the Cultural and Educational Committee of the Government Administration Council; Wu Han, Deputy Mayor of the Beijing Municipal People's Government; and 200 people from all walks of life in Beijing.
    Diplomatic envoys and diplomats from various countries to China were also invited to attend.
    Xu Zhe, Chargé d'Affaires ad interim of the Korean Embassy in China, and Chen Shutong, Vice Chairman of the Chinese People's Committee to Resist America and Aid Korea, delivered speeches at the ceremony successively. Finally, Chen Shutong cut the ribbon to open the exhibition.

### 朝鲜祖国解放战争三周年  图片展览会在京开幕

1953-06-26
第1版()
专栏：

    朝鲜祖国解放战争三周年
    图片展览会在京开幕
    【新华社二十五日讯】朝鲜民主主义人民共和国驻华大使馆举办的“朝鲜人民祖国解放战争三周年图片展览会”，于六月二十五日在北京中山公园水榭开幕。开幕典礼由朝鲜驻华大使馆临时代办徐哲主持。
    应邀参加开幕典礼的有：中国人民抗美援朝总会副主席陈叔通，常务委员张奚若、彭泽民、罗隆基，中央人民政府外交部副部长章汉夫，文化部副部长丁西林，高等教育部副部长曾昭抡，中国人民革命军事委员会办公厅副主任朱早观，中央人民政府外交部亚洲司司长陈家康，政务院文化教育委员会对外文化联络事务局局长洪深，北京市人民政府副市长吴晗，以及北京各界人士共二百人。
    应邀参加的还有各国驻华使节和外交官。
    朝鲜驻华大使馆临时代办徐哲和中国人民抗美援朝总会副主席陈叔通相继在会上致词。最后由陈叔通剪彩开幕。
