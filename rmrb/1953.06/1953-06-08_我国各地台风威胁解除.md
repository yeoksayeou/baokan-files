Title: Typhoon Threat to Various Parts of China Lifted
Author:
Date: 1953-06-08
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, July 7th: The Central Meteorological Observatory issued a typhoon cancellation warning at 6:00 AM on the 7th: The typhoon that moved from east of Taiwan towards Kyushu, Japan, on the 6th, had reached the sea 400 kilometers northeast by north of Okinawa Island at 2:00 AM this morning, at 29.6 degrees north latitude and 128.6 degrees east longitude. The maximum wind speed of the typhoon is approximately 35 meters per second; at sea 260 kilometers from the center, the wind speed reaches 18 meters per second. Currently, the typhoon center is accelerating northeastward at a speed of approximately 60 kilometers per hour. It is expected that within the next 24 hours, the typhoon center will move to northern Honshu, Japan. The typhoon threat to various parts of China has been lifted.

### 我国各地台风威胁解除

1953-06-08
第1版()
专栏：

    我国各地台风威胁解除
    【新华社七日讯】中央气象台七日上午六时发布台风解除警报：六日自台湾以东向日本九州进行的台风，七日晨二时中心已抵达冲绳岛东北偏北四百公里的海面上，即北纬二十九点六度、东经一百二十八点六度。台风最大风速约每秒三十五公尺；距中心二百六十公里的海上，风速达每秒十八公尺。目前，台风中心约以每小时六十公里的速度，向东北方向加速前进。预计未来二十四小时内，台风中心将移到日本本州北部。我国各地所受此次台风威胁都已解除。
