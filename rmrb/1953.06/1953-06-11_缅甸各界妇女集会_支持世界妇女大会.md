Title: Burmese Women from All Walks of Life Gather to Support the World Women's Congress
Author: 
Date: 1953-06-11
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Rangoon, 9th. More than a thousand women representatives from all walks of life in Burma held a gathering in Rangoon on the 5th to warmly support the World Women's Congress held in Copenhagen. The gathering was presided over by female representative Du Yue Lai, and unanimously adopted the "Report from Burmese Women to the World Women's Congress," as well as resolutions to support the World Women's Congress, strive for women's due rights, and demand that the five major powers conclude a peace pact.

    In her speech at the gathering, Du Yue Lai said: The World Women's Congress was convened to stop wars and protect the welfare of women and children, and these demands also represent the demands of Burmese women. Therefore, Burmese women firmly support the convening of the World Women's Congress. She said: The greatest hope of Burmese women is to carry out peaceful construction and improve their lives, but the imperialists want to use bacteriological bombs and atomic bombs to slaughter the women and their children of the world. For the sake of the peaceful life of the Burmese people, Burmese women wholeheartedly support the demands of the World Women's Congress. Burmese women demand that the five major powers conclude a peace pact and oppose the war conspiracies of the imperialists.

### 缅甸各界妇女集会  支持世界妇女大会

1953-06-11
第4版()
专栏：

    缅甸各界妇女集会
    支持世界妇女大会
    【新华社九日讯】仰光消息：缅甸各界妇女代表千余人于五日在仰光举行集会，热烈支持在哥本哈根举行的世界妇女大会。这次集会由妇女代表都约莱主持，一致通过了“缅甸妇女致世界妇女大会报告书”，以及拥护世界妇女大会、争取妇女应有的权利、要求五大国缔结和平公约等项决议。
    都约莱在集会上致词说：世界妇女大会是为了制止战争、保卫妇女和儿童的福利而召开的，这些要求也代表了缅甸妇女的要求，因此缅甸妇女坚决拥护世界妇女大会的召开。她说：缅甸妇女最大的希望是能够进行和平建设，改善自己的生活，但帝国主义却想用细菌弹、原子弹来屠杀世界的妇女和她们的儿女们。为了缅甸人民的和平生活，缅甸妇女衷心支持世界妇女大会的要求，缅甸妇女要求五大国缔结和平公约，反对帝国主义的战争阴谋。
