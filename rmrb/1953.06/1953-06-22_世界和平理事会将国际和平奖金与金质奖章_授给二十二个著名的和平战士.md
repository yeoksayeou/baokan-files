Title: World Peace Council Awards International Peace Prizes and Gold Medals to Twenty-Two Prominent Peace Fighters
Author:
Date: 1953-06-22
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, June 21st dispatch from Budapest: The World Peace Council, at its afternoon meeting on the 19th, unanimously adopted the decision of the International Peace Prize and Gold Medal Evaluation Committee, proposed by Mrs. Bronka Fejallo, member of the Standing Committee of the World Peace Council.

The International Peace Prize and Gold Medal Evaluation Committee held meetings on December 10, 11, and 16, 1952, chaired by Pierre Cot. Participants included Vanda Wasilewska, Mao Dun, Gabriel D’Arboussier, Jorge Amado, Hewlett Johnson, Artur Lundkvist, Jan Mukarovsky, and Eriberto Jara.

The committee decided to posthumously award an honorary Peace Prize to the Bulgarian poet and national hero Nikola Vaptsarov, who died in the anti-fascist struggle.

The committee decided that the recipients of the International Peace Prize are: French poet Paul Éluard (for his works and activities in striving for peace); American scientist William Du Bois (for his activities in striving for peace); German filmmakers Martin Hellberg, Kurt, and Jeanne Stern (for producing the film "Fighting Countryside"); Icelandic novelist Halldór Laxness (for his literary activities in striving for peace); Mexican artist Leopoldo Méndez and his assistants in the "Popular Graphics Workshop" (for artistic works striving for peace); Indian writer Mulk Raj Anand (for works contributing to the cause of peace).

Each of these six prizes is worth 2.5 million French francs.

The committee decided to award gold medals to the following individuals: French cartoonist Jean Effel; Czechoslovak poet Vítězslav Nezval (for his poem "Song of Peace"); British writer James Aldridge (for his novel "The Diplomat"); Brazilian musician Cláudio Santoro (for his symphony "Song of Love and Peace"); Argentine writer Maria Rosa Oliver (for her activities in striving for peace); Japanese painters Toshi Maruki and Iri Maruki (for "The Hiroshima Panels"); French sculptor Georges Salendre (for artistic works promoting peace); Iranian writer Ali-Avi Bozorg (for his work "Nameha"); Romanian sculptor Jowassy (for the woodcut "Koreans"); Venezuelan poet Carlos Augusto León (for poems advocating peace); Colombian Doctor of Law Luis Carlos Pérez (for peace activities); Finnish sculptor Wäinö Aaltonen (for the sculpture "Peace").

The World Peace Council meeting also unanimously adopted a resolution on the composition of the Evaluation Committee and the regulations for the future awarding of the International Peace Prize, proposed by World Peace Council member Alfredo Varela (Argentina). The resolution stated:

"The World Peace Council decides to award the International Peace Prize annually, in three categories, to reward those who have made the most effective contributions to the consolidation of international peace through works and creative activities in the fields of literature, science, art, and liberal professions.

The study of these works and activities, as well as the awarding of prizes, will be entrusted to a permanent International Evaluation Committee.

The World Peace Council approves a number of former members of the Evaluation Committee as members of this committee, including: Pietro Nenni, Pierre Cot, Mao Dun, Vanda Wasilewska, Jan Mukarovsky, Damodar Kosambi, Hewlett Johnson, Artur Lundkvist, Eriberto Jara, Mrs. Weigel-Brecht, Mohammed Kamel Bindari, Jerzy Putrament, Gabriel D'Arboussier, and Pablo Picasso.

The World Peace Council decides that the Standing Committee will, at its first meeting following this Council meeting, nominate other well-known figures from the fields of literature, science, and art to join the Evaluation Committee."

### 世界和平理事会将国际和平奖金与金质奖章  授给二十二个著名的和平战士

1953-06-22
第1版()
专栏：

    世界和平理事会将国际和平奖金与金质奖章
    授给二十二个著名的和平战士
    【新华社二十一日讯】据塔斯社布达佩斯讯：世界和平理事会十九日下午会议一致通过了世界和平理事会常务委员会委员布朗卡·费雅洛夫人提出的国际和平奖金与金质奖章评议委员会的决定。
    国际和平奖金与金质奖章评议委员会曾在一九五二年十二月十日、十一日和十六日举行会议，由皮埃尔·戈特担任主席。参加会议的有：凡达·华西列夫斯卡、茅盾、加布里尔·达波赛、若热·亚马多、休勒特·约翰逊、阿图尔·伦德奎斯特、扬·穆卡罗夫斯基、艾里伯尔托·哈拉。
    委员会决定追授给在反法西斯斗争中牺牲的保加利亚诗人、民族英雄尼古拉·瓦普察洛夫以名誉和平奖金。
    委员会决定国际和平奖金得奖人为：法国诗人保罗·埃吕阿（因争取和平的作品与活动得奖）；美国科学家威廉·杜波依斯（因争取和平的活动得奖）；德国电影制片人马丁·赫勒伯、库尔特、和让娜·史特恩（因摄制影片“战斗的乡村”得奖）；冰岛小说家霍尔德·拉克尼斯（因争取和平的文学活动得奖）；墨西哥艺术家利奥波多·孟德斯及他的“通俗图画社”中的助手们（因争取和平的艺术作品得奖）；印度作家穆尔克·拉志·阿南德（因有助于和平事业的作品得奖）。
    这六笔奖金每一笔计二百五十万法郎。
    委员会决定金质奖章颁发给下列各人：法国漫画家让·艾飞；捷克斯洛伐克诗人维捷斯拉夫·奈兹瓦尔（因他的诗“和平之歌”得奖）；英国作家詹姆斯·阿尔德里奇（因小说“外交官”得奖）；巴西音乐家克劳迪奥·桑托罗（因交响曲“爱与和平之歌”得奖）；阿根廷女作家玛丽亚·罗莎·奥利弗（因争取和平的活动得奖）；日本画家赤松俊子和丸木位里（因“原子弹之画”得奖）；法国雕刻家乔治·萨朗德（因促进和平的艺术作品得奖）；伊朗作家阿利阿维·波佐格（因作品“纳麦哈”得奖）；罗马尼亚雕刻家乔瓦西（因木刻“朝鲜人”得奖）；委内瑞拉诗人卡洛斯·奥古斯特·利昂（因拥护和平的诗歌得奖）；哥伦比亚的法律博士路易斯·卡罗斯·佩雷兹（因和平活动得奖）；芬兰雕刻家瓦伊诺·阿尔托宁（因雕刻品“和平”得奖）。
    世界和平理事会会议并一致通过了世界和平理事会理事艾尔弗雷多·瓦雷拉（阿根廷）提出的关于评议委员会的组成和将来颁发国际和平奖金的章程的决议案。决议案说：
    “世界和平理事会决定每年颁发国际和平奖金，奖金分三种，以奖励在文学、科学、艺术和自由职业方面对巩固国际和平提供了最有效的贡献的作品和创造性活动的人。
    研究这些作品和活动以及颁发奖金的工作，将委托常设的国际评议委员会来进行。
    世界和平理事会通过一批前评议委员会的委员为这个委员会的委员，这些人是：彼特罗·南尼、皮埃尔·戈特、茅盾、凡达·华西列夫斯卡、扬·穆卡罗夫斯基、高善必、休勒特·约翰逊、阿图尔·伦德奎斯特、艾里伯尔托·哈拉、魏格尔—布列希特夫人、穆罕默德·卡迈尔·宾达里、叶日·普特拉曼特、加布里尔·达波赛和帕布洛·毕加索。
    世界和平理事会决定常务委员会在本届理事会会议以后的第一次会议上，提名文学、科学和艺术界的其他知名人士参加评议委员会。”
