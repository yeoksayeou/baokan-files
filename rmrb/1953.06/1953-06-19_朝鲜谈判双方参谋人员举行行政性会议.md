Title: Korean Armistice Talks: Staff Officers of Both Sides Hold Administrative Meeting
Author:
Date: 1953-06-19
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, Kaesong, June 18th: The Korean Armistice Talks delegation from the Korean and Chinese side released the following communique on June 18th:
	On June 18th, staff officers, liaison officers, and translators from both sides held separate administrative meetings.

### 朝鲜谈判双方参谋人员举行行政性会议

1953-06-19
第1版()
专栏：

    朝鲜谈判双方参谋人员举行行政性会议
    【新华社开城十八日电】朝鲜停战谈判朝中代表团六月十八日发表公报如下：
    六月十八日，双方参谋人员、联络官和译员分别举行了行政性会议。
