Author:

    Our Great Motherland
    The construction projects of our great motherland greatly educate the children and strengthen their patriotic ideas. The Young Pioneers of the Dongdan District Central Elementary School in Beijing took advantage of their team day to visit the basic construction site in the eastern suburbs. They are cheering to a worker on a water tower under construction.

### 我们伟大的祖国（图片）

1953-06-01
第1版()
专栏：

    我们伟大的祖国
    我们伟大祖国的建设事业，给儿童们以很大的教育，加强了他们的爱国主义思想。北京市东单区中心小学的少年儿童队员们利用队日到东郊参观基本建设工地。他们正向一个在建筑中的水塔上的工人欢呼。
