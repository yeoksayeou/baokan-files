Title: Tibetan Youth Delegation and Qamdo Region Youth Delegation Arrive in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1953-06-08
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, June 7th: The Tibetan Youth Delegation and the Qamdo Region Youth Delegation, a group of ninety people, led by the head of the delegation Sokang Kelang Wangjue and the head of the delegation Losang Langji, arrived in Beijing, the capital, on the evening of June 7th.

Those who went to the station to welcome them included: Liu Geping, Deputy Director of the Central Nationalities Affairs Commission, Kang Naier, Secretary-General of the Central Committee of the China New Democratic Youth League, Yu Beichen, Deputy Secretary-General of the All-China Federation of Democratic Youth, as well as students from the Nationalities Class of the Central Institute for Nationalities and the Youth League Central Committee's League School, and youth representatives from government agencies, more than 200 people in total.

Also at the station to welcome them were: representatives of General Zhang Guohua, Commander of the Tibet Military Region of the Chinese People's Liberation Army, and Deputy Political Commissar General Fan Ming, as well as Ranba, Director of the Dalai Lama's Office in Beijing, and Luowang, Acting Director of the Panchen's Office in Beijing.

After the members of the two delegations got off the train, they warmly shook hands and embraced the youth of all fraternal nationalities who had come to welcome them. Everyone joyfully sang "Unity is Strength."

The Tibetan Youth Delegation and the Qamdo Region Youth Delegation include youth representatives from Lhasa, Shigatse, Gyantse, Nagqu, Ngari, as well as Qamdo, Bomi, and the Thirty-Nine Tribes regions. Along the way, the two delegations were warmly welcomed by youth from all walks of life in major cities such as Chongqing, Wuhan, Shanghai, Hangzhou, and Nanjing, and they visited factories, schools, cultural palaces, children's palaces, scenic spots, and famous temples.

The two delegations will visit the capital and attend the upcoming Second National Congress of the All-China Youth Federation.

### 西藏青年参观团和昌都地区青年参观团抵京

1953-06-08
第1版()
专栏：

    西藏青年参观团和昌都地区青年参观团抵京
    【新华社七日讯】西藏青年参观团和昌都地区青年参观团一行九十人，在团长索康·克郎旺角与团长洛桑·郎吉率领下，于六月七日晚到达首都北京。
    前往车站欢迎的有：中央民族事务委员会副主任委员刘格平、中国新民主主义青年团中央委员会秘书长康乃尔、中华全国民主青年联合总会副秘书长于北辰以及中央民族学院和青年团中央团校民族班的学员、机关青年代表二百余人。
    到站欢迎的还有：中国人民解放军西藏军区司令员张国华将军、副政治委员范明将军的代表及达赖喇嘛驻京办事处处长然巴、班禅驻京办事处代处长罗旺。
    两参观团团员下车后，与到站欢迎的各兄弟民族青年热烈握手和拥抱，大家欢唱“团结就是力量”。
    西藏青年参观团和昌都地区青年参观团包括拉萨、日喀则、江孜、黑河、阿里及昌都、波密、三十九族等地区的青年代表人物。两参观团沿途在重庆、武汉、上海、杭州、南京等大城市，受到各界青年的热烈欢迎，并参观了工厂、学校、文化宫、儿童宫、名胜古迹和著名寺院。
    两参观团将在首都参观，并将列席即将召开的中华全国青年第二次代表大会。
