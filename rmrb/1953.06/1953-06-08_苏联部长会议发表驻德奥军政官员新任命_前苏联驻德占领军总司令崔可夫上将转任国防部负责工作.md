Title: Soviet Council of Ministers Announces New Appointments for Military and Political Officials in Germany and Austria; Former Commander-in-Chief of the Soviet Occupation Forces in Germany, General Chuikov, Transferred to Work Responsible for the Ministry of Defense

Author:
Date: 1953-06-08
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, 7th dispatch: TASS, Moscow, 7th dispatch: The Soviet Council of Ministers has relieved General Chuikov of his post as Commander-in-Chief of the Soviet Occupation Forces in Germany, so that he may transfer to responsible work in the Soviet Ministry of Defense.

The Soviet Council of Ministers has appointed Lieutenant General A.A. Grechko as Commander-in-Chief of the Soviet Occupation Forces in Germany.

Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, 7th dispatch: TASS, Moscow, 7th dispatch: The Soviet Council of Ministers considers it inappropriate for one person to concurrently hold the two positions of Soviet High Commissioner in Austria and Commander-in-Chief of the Soviet Forces. Therefore, the Council of Ministers has relieved the Commander-in-Chief of the Soviet Forces in Austria of his concurrent position as High Commissioner; his activities will be limited to commanding the Soviet Forces in Austria.

Ambassador I.I. Ilyichev has been appointed as Soviet High Commissioner in Austria.

### 苏联部长会议发表驻德奥军政官员新任命  前苏联驻德占领军总司令崔可夫上将转任国防部负责工作

1953-06-08
第4版()
专栏：

    苏联部长会议发表驻德奥军政官员新任命
    前苏联驻德占领军总司令崔可夫上将转任国防部负责工作
    【新华社七日讯】塔斯社莫斯科七日讯：苏联部长会议已经解除了崔可夫上将的苏联驻德占领军总司令的职务，俾使他转任苏联国防部的负责工作。
    苏联部长会议已任命阿·阿·格列奇科中将为苏联驻德占领军总司令。
    【新华社七日讯】塔斯社莫斯科七日讯：苏联部长会议认为不宜再由一个人来兼任苏联驻奥地利高级专员和苏联军队总司令这两个职务。因此，部长会议已解除苏联驻奥地利军队总司令所兼任的高级专员职务，他的活动限于统辖苏联驻奥地利军队。
    伊·伊·伊利切夫大使已被任命为苏联驻奥地利高级专员。
