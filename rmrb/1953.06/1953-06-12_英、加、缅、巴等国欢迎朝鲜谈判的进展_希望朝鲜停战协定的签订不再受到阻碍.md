Title: Britain, Canada, Burma, Pakistan, etc., Welcome Progress in Korean Negotiations; Hope Armistice Agreement Will No Longer Be Obstructed

Author:

Date: 1953-06-12
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, June 11th, London Dispatch: According to London newspapers, Prime Minister Churchill, in answering a question in the House of Commons on June 9th, issued a statement regarding the signing of the prisoner of war repatriation agreement at Panmunjom. Churchill said: "Besides administrative arrangements, there should now be nothing to hinder the signing of the armistice agreement. And I hope that the administrative arrangements can be completed soon."

Xinhua News Agency, June 11th, TASS Ottawa Dispatch: "The News Daily" reported that Canadian Foreign Minister Pearson, in a statement to the press, pointed out that reaching an agreement on the exchange of prisoners of war was a pleasant and hopeful event, which would certainly enable the armistice agreement to be concluded within a few days.

Xinhua News Agency, June 11th: Burma and Pakistan welcomed the agreement reached on the prisoner of war issue in the Korean armistice negotiations.

According to Agence France-Presse: A Burmese Foreign Ministry spokesman said: The Burmese government "welcomes the signing of the Korean prisoner of war agreement and hopes that this agreement will pave the way for lasting peace." The spokesman believes that the position taken by Syngman Rhee is hopeless.

According to Reuters: Pakistani Prime Minister Muhammad Ali, in a radio address in London, expressed satisfaction with recent international changes and progress. He said: "We welcome the Soviet Union's peace initiatives, and we hope to use this opportunity to resolve as many of the causes of conflict as possible." He believes that the Korean War is finally coming to an end.

### 英、加、缅、巴等国欢迎朝鲜谈判的进展  希望朝鲜停战协定的签订不再受到阻碍

1953-06-12
第1版()
专栏：

    英、加、缅、巴等国欢迎朝鲜谈判的进展
    希望朝鲜停战协定的签订不再受到阻碍
    【新华社十一日讯】伦敦消息：据伦敦报纸报道：六月九日，邱吉尔首相在下院答复问题时就板门店签订战俘遣返协议事发表声明。邱吉尔说：“除了行政上的安排之外，现在应该没有什么东西来阻碍停战协定的签订了。而且我希望，行政上的安排不久就可以完成。”
    【新华社十一日讯】塔斯社渥太华十一日讯：“新闻日报”报道说，加拿大外交部长皮尔逊在他向报界发表的声明中指出，达成交换战俘协议是一件愉快而充满希望的事件，这一定会使停战协定能在几天之内缔结。
    【新华社十一日讯】缅甸、巴基斯坦对朝鲜停战谈判在战俘问题上取得协议表示欢迎。
    据法新社报道：缅甸外交部发言人说：缅甸政府“欢迎朝鲜战俘协议的签订，并希望这个协议将为持久和平铺平道路。”这位发言人认为，李承晚所采取的立场是没有希望的。
    据路透社报道：巴基斯坦总理穆罕默德·阿里在伦敦发表广播演说，对国际方面最近的变化和进展表示满意。他说：“我们欢迎苏联的和平行动，我们希望能利用这个机会来尽量解决冲突的许多原因。”他认为，朝鲜战争终于快结束了。
