Title: Thirty-seven Buddhist Monasteries in Burma Hold Peace Prayer Meeting
Author:
Date: 1953-06-27
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Rangoon, June 26th: Thirty-seven Buddhist monasteries in Burma jointly and solemnly held a peace prayer meeting at the Ba Yuyi Temple in Rangoon on June 20th. The meeting was attended by high monks from the thirty-seven monasteries and representatives from the Burmese Committee for the Defense of World Peace.

    The meeting began with prayers from the high monks for the victory of the cause of world peace, for the achievements of the World Peace Council, and for the health of peace fighters throughout the world and throughout Burma. Then, Wu Rui Biao, a representative of the Burmese Committee for the Defense of World Peace, addressed the meeting, calling on the Burmese people and Burmese Buddhists to unite and strive for peace. Wu Suti Ka, an 89-year-old high monk from the Suo Da Ye Ma Temple, said in his speech that the cause of peace cannot be achieved by prayer alone, and that the most effective way to promote world peace at present is to demand the conclusion of a Five-Power Peace Pact.

    After the speeches, thirty-nine high monks from the thirty-seven monasteries took the lead in signing the appeal for the conclusion of a Five-Power Peace Pact. The peace prayer meeting also unanimously decided to send a telegram to the World Peace Council, expressing full support for the world peace movement.

### 缅甸三十七个佛教寺院  举行和平祈祷大会

1953-06-27
第4版()
专栏：

    缅甸三十七个佛教寺院
    举行和平祈祷大会
    【新华社二十六日讯】仰光消息：缅甸三十七个佛教寺院于六月二十日在仰光的巴宇意寺联合隆重举行和平祈祷大会。参加大会的有三十七个寺院的高僧和缅甸保卫世界和平委员会的代表。
    大会首先由各寺高僧为世界和平事业的胜利、为世界和平理事会的成就、为全世界和全缅甸和平战士的健康而祈祷。接着，缅甸保卫世界和平委员会代表吴瑞彪在大会上致词，号召缅甸人民和缅甸佛教徒团结起来，为争取和平而奋斗。索达耶玛寺八十九岁的高僧吴素堤卡在讲话中说：和平事业不能单靠祈祷来实现，目前促成世界和平实现的有力办法是要求缔结五大国和平公约。
    大会发言结束之后，三十七个寺院的三十九名高僧率先在要求缔结五大国和平公约的呼吁书上签名。和平祈祷大会并一致决定致电世界和平理事会，表示全力支持世界和平运动。
