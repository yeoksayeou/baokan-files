Title: Second National Committee of the All-China Youth Federation Holds Meeting, Elects Chairman, Vice-Chairmen, and Secretary-Generals and Deputy Secretary-Generals
Author:
Date: 1953-06-17
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, June 16th: The Second National Committee of the All-China Youth Federation held its first meeting today, attended by eighty-six committee members. According to the Charter of the All-China Youth Federation passed by the Second National Congress of the All-China Youth, the meeting elected the Chairman, Vice-Chairmen, Standing Committee members, Secretary-General, and Deputy Secretary-Generals of the All-China Youth Federation.

The election results are as follows:

Liao Chengzhi was elected Chairman of the All-China Youth Federation.

Liu Daosheng, Qian Sanqiang, Wu Han, and Qu Tangliang were elected Vice-Chairmen of the All-China Youth Federation.

Ding Cong, Yu Beichen, Fang Liang, Wang Guangying, Tuerxun, Tian Demin, Quelo Basang, Wu Maosun, Wu Han, Li Shu, Shi Ruzhang (female), Ma Feng, Ma Tengai, Sun Fuling, Wuleji Naren, Hao Yichun (female), Qu Tangliang (female), Xuekang Tudeng Nima, Ye Zhishan, Liao Chengzhi, Liu Xiyuan, Liu Liangmo, Liu Daosheng, Jiang Yi, Qian Sanqiang, Qian Weichang, and Guan Shixiong, a total of twenty-seven people, were elected as Standing Committee members of the All-China Youth Federation. (In order of the number of strokes in the surname)

Yu Beichen was elected Secretary-General of the All-China Youth Federation.

Qian Weichang, Ding Cong, Sun Fuling, Guan Shixiong, and Qian Liren were elected Deputy Secretary-Generals of the All-China Youth Federation.

### 全国青联第二届全国委员会举行会议  选出主席、副主席及正副秘书长

1953-06-17
第1版()
专栏：

    全国青联第二届全国委员会举行会议
    选出主席、副主席及正副秘书长
    【新华社十六日讯】中华全国民主青年联合会第二届全国委员会于十六日举行第一次会议，出席委员八十六人。根据中华全国青年第二次代表大会通过的中华全国民主青年联合会章程，推选中华全国民主青年联合会主席、副主席、常务委员和秘书长、副秘书长。
    选举结果如下：
    廖承志当选为中华全国民主青年联合会主席。
    刘导生、钱三强、吴晗、区棠亮当选为中华全国民主青年联合会副主席。
    丁聪、于北辰、方亮、王光英、吐尔逊、田德民、曲洛·巴桑、吴茂荪、吴晗、李恕、施如璋（女）、马烽、马腾霭、孙孚凌、乌勒吉那仁、郝诒纯（女）、区棠亮（女）、雪康·土登尼玛、叶至善、廖承志、刘西元、刘良模、刘导生、蒋毅、钱三强、钱伟长、关世雄等二十七人当选为中华全国民主青年联合会常务委员。（以姓氏笔划为序）
    于北辰当选为中华全国民主青年联合会秘书长。
    钱伟长、丁聪、孙孚凌、关世雄、钱李仁为中华全国民主青年联合会副秘书长。
