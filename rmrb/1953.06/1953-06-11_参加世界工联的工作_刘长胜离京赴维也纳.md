Title: Liu Changsheng Leaves Beijing for Vienna to Participate in the Work of the World Federation of Trade Unions
Author:
Date: 1953-06-11
Page: 4

    Participating in the Work of the World Federation of Trade Unions
    Liu Changsheng Leaves Beijing for Vienna
    Xinhua News Agency, June 10th: Liu Changsheng, Vice-Chairman of the All-China Federation of Trade Unions and Secretary of the Secretariat of the World Federation of Trade Unions, left Beijing by train on June 9th with his staff to participate in the work of the World Federation of Trade Unions in Vienna.

### 参加世界工联的工作  刘长胜离京赴维也纳

1953-06-11
第4版()
专栏：

    参加世界工联的工作
    刘长胜离京赴维也纳
    【新华社十日讯】中华全国总工会副主席、世界工会联合会书记处书记刘长胜偕同工作人员于六月九日乘火车离京，前往维也纳参加世界工联的工作。
