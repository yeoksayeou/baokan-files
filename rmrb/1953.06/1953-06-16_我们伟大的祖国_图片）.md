Our Great Motherland
The Northwest Yellow River Engineering Bureau of the Yellow River Conservancy Commission is carrying out soil and water conservation work in the wheat-producing area of eastern Gansu Province to prevent soil erosion in the Northwest Loess Plateau and flooding in the lower reaches of the Yellow River. This is the scene of surveyors taking measurements.

### 我们伟大的祖国(图片）

1953-06-16
第1版()
专栏：

    我们伟大的祖国
    黄河水利委员会西北黄河工程局正在甘肃省东部麦产区进行水土保持工作，以防止西北黄土高原的水土流失和黄河下游的汜滥。这是测量队员正在测量的情形。
