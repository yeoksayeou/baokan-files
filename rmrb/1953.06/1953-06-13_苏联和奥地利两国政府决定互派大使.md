Title: Soviet and Austrian Governments Decide to Exchange Ambassadors
Author:
Date: 1953-06-13
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, June 11th: TASS reported from Moscow on the 11th that the Soviet government and the Austrian government have agreed to convert the Soviet Political Mission in Austria and the Austrian Political Mission in the Soviet Union into embassies and to exchange ambassadors.
    The Presidium of the Supreme Soviet of the USSR has appointed I. I. Ilyichev, the Soviet High Commissioner to Austria, as the Soviet Ambassador Extraordinary and Plenipotentiary to Austria.

### 苏联和奥地利两国政府决定互派大使

1953-06-13
第4版()
专栏：

    苏联和奥地利两国政府决定互派大使
    【新华社十二日讯】塔斯社莫斯科十一日讯：苏联政府和奥地利政府双方同意将苏联驻奥地利政治代表团和奥地利驻苏联政治代表团改为大使馆，并互派大使。
    苏联最高苏维埃主席团已任命苏联驻奥地利高级专员伊·伊·伊利切夫为苏联驻奥地利特命全权大使。
