Title: Meteorological Observatory Issues Typhoon Emergency Alert
Author:
Date: 1953-06-06
Page: 1

    Xinhua News Agency, 5th. The Central Meteorological Observatory issued the following typhoon emergency warning at 6:00 PM on June 5th:
    At 2:00 PM on June 5th, the center of the typhoon was located 170 kilometers south of Kaohsiung, Taiwan, at 21.5 degrees north latitude and 120.2 degrees east longitude. The maximum wind speed was approximately 40 meters per second, and the wind speed was 14 meters per second at 220 kilometers from the center. Currently, the typhoon center is moving north-northeast at a speed of approximately 18 kilometers per hour. It is expected that within 12 hours, the maximum wind speed in the Taiwan Strait area of ​​China will reach 20 to 25 meters per second, with a northeast wind direction; the wind speed in the coastal areas of Fujian will reach more than 12 meters per second, with a northerly wind direction, accompanied by large waves and heavy rain, requiring urgent prevention. Within 24 hours, the maximum wind speed in the southern part of the East China Sea may reach 30 meters per second, with an easterly wind direction; the coastal areas south of Sanmen Bay in Zhejiang and the northern part of the Taiwan Strait will experience wind speeds of more than 10 meters per second, with the wind direction changing from northeast to northwest, accompanied by heavy rain, requiring prevention. It is expected that at 8:00 PM on June 6th, the center of the typhoon will be located 400 kilometers east of Fuzhou, at 26 degrees north latitude and 123 degrees east longitude.

### 气象台发布台风紧急警报

1953-06-06
第1版()
专栏：

    气象台发布台风紧急警报
    【新华社五日讯】中央气象台五日下午六时发布台风紧急警报如下：
    六月五日下午二时台风中心位置在台湾高雄以南一百七十公里处，即北纬二十一点五度，东经一百二十点二度。最大风速约为每秒四十公尺，距中心二百二十公里处风速达每秒十四公尺。目前台风中心约以每小时十八公里的速度向北逐渐转为东北偏北方向移动。预计十二小时内我国台湾海峡一带地区，最大风速达每秒二十至二十五公尺，风向东北；福建沿海一带地区风速可达每秒十二公尺以上，风向偏北，并有大浪及大雨，须作紧急预防。二十四小时内，我国东海南部最大风速可达每秒三十公尺，风向偏东；浙江三门湾以南沿海及台湾海峡北部，风速达每秒十公尺以上，风向东北转西北，并有大雨，须作预防。预计六日下午八时台风中心位置将在福州以东四百公里处，即北纬二十六度，东经一百二十三度。
