Title: Korean Armistice Talks: Both Sides Continue to Hold Administrative Meetings
Author: 
Date: 1953-06-10
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Kaesong, June 9th: The Korean Armistice Talks delegation of the Chinese and Korean sides issued a communique on June 9th as follows:

　　On June 9th, representatives of both sides to the armistice talks and staff officers of both sides held administrative meetings successively.

　　The next meeting of the full delegations of both sides is scheduled to be held at 11:00 a.m. on June 10th.

### 朝鲜停战谈判双方继续举行行政性会议

1953-06-10
第1版()
专栏：

    朝鲜停战谈判双方继续举行行政性会议
    【新华社开城九日电】朝鲜停战谈判朝中代表团六月九日发表公报如下：
    六月九日双方停战谈判代表和双方参谋人员先后举行了行政性会议。
    双方代表团大会下一次会议定于六月十日上午十一时举行。
