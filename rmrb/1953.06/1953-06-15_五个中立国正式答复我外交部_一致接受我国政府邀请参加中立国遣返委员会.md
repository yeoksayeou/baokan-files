Title: Five Neutral Nations Officially Reply to Our Ministry of Foreign Affairs – Unanimously Accept Our Government's Invitation to Participate in the Neutral Nations Repatriation Commission
Author:
Date: 1953-06-15
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, June 14th: The five neutral nations that are to participate in the Neutral Nations Repatriation Commission according to the Prisoner of War Repatriation Agreement – India, Czechoslovakia, Sweden, Switzerland, and Poland – formally replied to the Ministry of Foreign Affairs of the Central People's Government of the People's Republic of China on June 11th, 12th, and 13th, expressing their agreement to participate in the work of the Neutral Nations Repatriation Commission stipulated in the Prisoner of War Repatriation Agreement.

June 11th, the Indian government replied in writing: “The Indian government welcomes the agreement reached in Panmunjom and is happy to serve all parties concerned to promote the cause of peace in Korea. Therefore, the Indian government is prepared to appoint a member to participate in the work of the Neutral Nations Repatriation Commission. The Indian government assumes that all parties concerned will fully guarantee cooperation with the work of the commission and guarantee a peaceful environment in Korea for this purpose."

June 12th, Czechoslovak Ambassador Kounzala sent a note to the Chinese Ministry of Foreign Affairs, stating that the Czechoslovak government agreed to send a representative to participate in the Neutral Nations Repatriation Commission. The ambassador stated in the note: "The Czechoslovak government is proud that it will be able to practically assist in resolving the issue of prisoner of war repatriation and enable them to return to peaceful construction labor."

On the same day, the Swedish Embassy sent a note to the Chinese Ministry of Foreign Affairs, expressing its agreement to send personnel to participate in the work of the Neutral Nations Repatriation Commission.

On June 12th and 13th, the Swiss Minister and the Polish Chargé d'Affaires ad interim verbally notified the Chinese Ministry of Foreign Affairs that they agreed to participate in the Neutral Nations Repatriation Commission.

### 五个中立国正式答复我外交部  一致接受我国政府邀请参加中立国遣返委员会

1953-06-15
第1版()
专栏：

    五个中立国正式答复我外交部
    一致接受我国政府邀请参加中立国遣返委员会
    【新华社十四日讯】根据战俘遣返问题协议参加中立国遣返委员会的五个中立国家：印度、捷克斯洛伐克、瑞典、瑞士及波兰在六月十一日、十二日、十三日先后正式答复中华人民共和国中央人民政府外交部，表示同意参加战俘遣返问题协议中的中立国遣返委员会的工作。
    六月十一日印度政府书面答复称：“印度政府欢迎在板门店达成的协议，并乐于为有关各方效劳以促进朝鲜和平的事业。因此，印度政府准备指派一委员参加中立国遣返委员会的工作。印度政府假定：有关各方将充分保证对委员会的工作给予合作，并保证为此目的在朝鲜将有和平的环境。”
    六月十二日捷克斯洛伐克大使康萨拉照会中国政府外交部，表示捷克斯洛伐克政府同意派遣代表参加中立国遣返委员会。大使在照会中并声称：“捷克斯洛伐克政府引以为荣幸的是，它将能实际地协助解决遣返战俘问题，并使他们重返和平建设的劳动。”
    同日，瑞典大使馆照会中国政府外交部，表示同意派员参加中立国遣返委员会的工作。
    六月十二日、十三日，瑞士公使及波兰临时代办先后口头通知中国政府外交部表示同意参加中立国遣返委员会。
