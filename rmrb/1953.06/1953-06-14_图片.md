Title: Photo
Author: Li Jiuling
Date: 1953-06-14
Page: 4

    Zhu Shilun, Deputy Director of the Propaganda Department of the China Resist America Aid Korea General Association, presents a letter of condolence on behalf of the China Resist America Aid Korea General Association and the people of the whole country to the representatives of the sick and wounded志愿军 (Volunteer Army) personnel who have been repatriated.
    The repatriated sick and wounded Chinese People's Volunteer Army soldiers, taking advantage of their free time, built their own small flower garden on the open space, adorned with the national flags of China, the Soviet Union, and Korea, to express their love for the motherland and their great internationalist spirit. (Xinhua News Agency reporter Li Jiuling)

### 图片
李九龄
1953-06-14
第4版()
专栏：

    抗美援朝总会宣传部副部长朱世纶代表抗美援朝总会和全国人民把慰问信交给被遣返回国的志愿军病伤人员的代表。
    回到祖国的被遣返的中国人民志愿军病伤员，利用空暇时间，在空地上修造自己的小花圃，上面缀有中国、苏联、朝鲜三国的国旗，表示他们对祖国的热爱和伟大的国际主义精神。（新华社记者李九龄摄）
