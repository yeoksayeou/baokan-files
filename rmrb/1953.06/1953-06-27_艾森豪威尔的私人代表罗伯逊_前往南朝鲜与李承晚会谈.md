Title: Eisenhower's Personal Representative, Robertson, Goes to South Korea for Talks with Syngman Rhee
Author:
Date: 1953-06-27
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, June 25th: US Assistant Secretary of State Robertson, acting as the personal representative of Eisenhower and Dulles to hold talks with Syngman Rhee, left Tokyo for South Korea on the 25th.

Robertson arrived in Tokyo on the 24th. Accompanying him were US Army Chief of Staff Collins, Assistant Secretary of State for Public Affairs McCardle, and Director of the State Department's Northeast Asian Affairs Office, Young. Upon arriving in Tokyo, Robertson announced that he brought a letter from Dulles to Syngman Rhee, but he could not disclose the contents of the letter.

During his stay in Tokyo, Robertson consulted with Clark, who had just held talks with Syngman Rhee, on the Korean issue.

### 艾森豪威尔的私人代表罗伯逊  前往南朝鲜与李承晚会谈

1953-06-27
第1版()
专栏：

    艾森豪威尔的私人代表罗伯逊
    前往南朝鲜与李承晚会谈
    【新华社二十五日讯】作为艾森豪威尔和杜勒斯的私人代表去和李承晚进行会谈的美国助理国务卿罗伯逊，已经在二十五日由东京前往南朝鲜。
    罗伯逊是在二十四日到达东京的。和他一同来的有美国陆军参谋长柯林斯，负责公共事务的助理国务卿麦卡德尔和国务院东北亚洲司司长杨格。罗伯逊在到达东京时宣称，他带来杜勒斯给李承晚的一封信，但是他不能透露这封信的内容。
    罗伯逊在东京期间，曾经和刚和李承晚举行过会谈的克拉克商谈朝鲜问题。
