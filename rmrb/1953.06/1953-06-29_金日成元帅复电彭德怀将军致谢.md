Title: Marshal Kim Il-sung Replies to General Peng Dehuai with Thanks
Author:
Date: 1953-06-29
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Pyongyang, June 28th: Marshal Kim Il-sung, Supreme Commander of the Korean People's Army, replied to General Peng Dehuai, Commander of the Chinese People's Volunteer Army, with thanks. The telegram reads as follows:

To General Peng Dehuai, Commander of the Chinese People's Volunteer Army:

On the occasion of the third anniversary of the Korean War of Liberation, I extend my heartfelt congratulations and regards to you, the Chinese people, and the Korean People's Army. On behalf of the entire Korean people, all officers and soldiers of the Korean People's Army, and in my own name, I extend my sincere gratitude to you and all commanders and fighters of the Chinese People's Volunteer Army, who have worked hard in the struggle against imperialist aggression and in defense of peace and freedom. I firmly believe that the fighting friendship and solidarity between the Chinese and Korean peoples and the Chinese and Korean People's forces will be further consolidated in the future, and will surely smash any enemy conspiracy, and make great contributions to the cause of defending the freedom and security of both China and Korea and to peace in Asia and the world.

	Marshal Kim Il-sung
	Supreme Commander of the Korean People's Army
		June 28, 1953

### 金日成元帅复电彭德怀将军致谢

1953-06-29
第1版()
专栏：

    金日成元帅复电彭德怀将军致谢
    【新华社平壤二十八日电】朝鲜人民军最高司令官金日成元帅复电中国人民志愿军司令员彭德怀将军致谢。电文如下：中国人民志愿军司令员彭德怀将军：
    对于您在朝鲜解放战争三周年纪念给我和朝鲜人民以及朝鲜人民军的热烈祝贺和慰问，我代表全体朝鲜人民、朝鲜人民军全体将士，并以我个人的名义，谨向在反对帝国主义侵略战争和保卫和平与自由的斗争中劳苦功高的您和中国人民志愿军全体指挥员、战斗员，表示诚挚的感谢。我深信朝中人民和朝中人民部队的战斗的友谊团结力量，今后将愈臻巩固，必能粉碎敌人的任何阴谋，在保卫朝中两国的自由、安全和亚洲及世界和平事业中必有极大贡献。
       朝鲜人民军最高司令官  元帅金日成
            一九五三年六月二十八日
