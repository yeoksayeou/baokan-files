Title: Vietnam Committee for the Defense of World Peace Calls for the Conclusion of a Five-Power Peace Pact
Author:
Date: 1953-06-17
Page: 4
    Vietnam Committee for the Defense of World Peace
    Requests the Conclusion of a Five-Power Peace Pact
    [Xinhua News Agency Dispatch] According to Vietnam News Agency: The Vietnam Committee for the Defense of World Peace issued a communique on the 9th, endorsing the resolution of the World People's Peace Congress regarding the demand for the five major powers to conclude a peace pact. The communique stated that the Vietnamese people consistently adhere to a policy of defending national independence, democracy, and peace. Over ten million out of the 23 million Vietnamese people have already signed the declaration demanding the five major powers to conclude a peace pact. The entire nation is actively implementing the resolutions of the World People's Peace Congress and the Asian and Pacific Regions Peace Conference. The Vietnamese people wholeheartedly welcome the peaceful attitude of the Soviet Union and China, and request that the governments of the United States, Britain, and France begin consultations with the Soviet Union and China in order to conclude a peace pact.

### 越南保卫世界和平委员会  要求缔结五大国和平公约

1953-06-17
第4版()
专栏：

    越南保卫世界和平委员会
    要求缔结五大国和平公约
    【新华社讯】据越南通讯社讯：越南保卫世界和平委员会在九日发表文告，拥护世界人民和平大会关于要求五大国缔结和平公约的决议。文告说，越南人民始终奉行保卫民族独立、民主与和平的政策。越南二千三百万人民中已有一千万人以上在要求五大国缔结和平公约的宣言上签名。全国人民正在积极执行世界人民和平大会和亚洲及太平洋区域和平会议的决议。越南人民衷心欢迎苏联和中国的和平态度，并要求美国、英国和法国政府着手和苏联、中国进行协商，以便缔结一个和平公约。
