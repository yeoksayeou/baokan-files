Title: All-China Youth Federation Second Congress: Congratulatory Telegram to the World Federation of Democratic Youth
Author:
Date: 1953-06-16
Page: 1

    All-China Youth Federation Second Congress
    Congratulatory Telegram to the World Federation of Democratic Youth
    World Federation of Democratic Youth:
    The Second Congress of the All-China Youth Federation has successfully concluded today. We would like to extend our warmest greetings to the World Federation of Democratic Youth on behalf of the youth of all ethnic groups in China!
    Our congress was held as our country enters a new historical period of planned economic construction. It defined the tasks of Chinese youth during the great construction period of the motherland. It calls on the youth of the whole country to unite more closely, follow the direction pointed out by our country's great leader Chairman Mao Zedong, actively participate in various national constructions, and strive to gradually realize national industrialization and create a better and happier life.
    Chinese youth deeply understand that all their achievements are inseparable from the cause of the people and youth of the world striving for peace. We warmly support the upcoming 3rd World Youth Congress and the 4th World Youth and Students Festival for Peace and Friendship, and have already elected the Chinese youth delegation to attend the 3rd World Youth Congress at the congress. We believe that through this congress and festival, the youth of the world will unite more widely and take active actions to defend peace and the vital rights of youth. We, the Chinese youth, will always be loyal to the cause of world peace, will unite more closely with the youth of the world, and contribute our strength under the banner of the World Federation of Democratic Youth.
    All-China Youth Federation Second Congress
    June 15, 1953

### 中华全国青年第二次代表大会  向世界民主青年联盟致敬电

1953-06-16
第1版()
专栏：

    中华全国青年第二次代表大会
    向世界民主青年联盟致敬电世界民主青年联盟：
    中华全国青年第二次代表大会已于今天胜利闭幕。我们谨代表中国各民族的青年，向世界民主青年联盟致以热烈的敬礼！
    我们的大会是在我国进入有计划经济建设的新的历史时期召开的。它确定了在祖国伟大的建设时期中国青年的任务。它号召全国青年更紧密地团结起来，遵循我国人民伟大的领袖毛泽东主席所指出的方向，积极参加国家的各种建设，为逐步实现国家工业化与创造更加美好幸福的生活而斗争。
    中国青年深知自己的一切成就，是与全世界人民和全世界青年争取和平的事业分不开的。我们热烈拥护即将召开的第三届世界青年代表大会与第四届世界青年与学生和平友谊联欢节，并已在大会上选出了出席第三届世界青年代表大会的中国青年代表团。我们相信，通过这次大会与联欢节，全世界青年将更广泛地团结起来，用积极行动来保卫和平与青年的切身权利。我们中国青年永远忠实于世界和平事业，将更紧密地与全世界青年团结在一起，在世界民主青年联盟的旗帜下贡献自己的力量。
    中华全国青年第二次代表大会
    一九五三年六月十五日
