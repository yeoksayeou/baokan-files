    Cultural Briefs
    Overseas Chinese Team from Indonesia to Perform in Shenyang
    The Overseas Chinese team from Indonesia, a group of fifty people led by team leaders Fang Dingxun and Ding Zicai, took a train from the capital to Shenyang on the afternoon of May 30th for performance matches. Over sixty people went to the station to see them off, including Chen Manyun, Deputy Director of the General Office of the Overseas Chinese Affairs Commission of the Central People's Government, representatives from the All-China Sports Federation, and representatives of returned Overseas Chinese students.
    Beijing People's Art Theater to Perform "Spring Flowers and Autumn Fruits" in Tianjin
    Starting in April, the Beijing People's Art Theater performed the drama "Spring Flowers and Autumn Fruits," written by Lao She and reflecting the "Five-Anti" movement, for a total of forty-seven performances at the Youth Palace and the Central Academy of Drama Experimental Theater, receiving a warm welcome from the audience. Recently, the Beijing People's Art Theater accepted an invitation from the Tianjin First Workers' Cultural Palace and decided to perform in Tianjin starting on June 4th.

    文化简讯
    印尼华侨归国球队去沈阳作表演赛
    印尼华侨归国球队一行五十人，由领队方定壎、丁子才率领，在五月三十日下午由首都乘火车去沈阳作表演赛。前往车站欢送的有中央人民政府华侨事务委员会办公厅副主任陈曼云、中华全国体育总会代表、华侨归国学生代表等六十多人。
    北京人民艺术剧院去天津演出“春华秋实”
    北京人民艺术剧院四月起在京演出了老舍编剧的反映“五反”运动的话剧“春华秋实”，在青年宫和中央戏剧学院实验剧场两处共演出四十七场，受到了观众的欢迎。最近，北京人民艺术剧院接受天津第一工人文化宫的邀请，决定从六月四日起在天津演出。
