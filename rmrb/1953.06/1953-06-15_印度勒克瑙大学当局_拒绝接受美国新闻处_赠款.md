Title: Indian Lucknow University Authorities Refuse to Accept USIS "Grant"
Author:
Date: 1953-06-15
Page: 4

    Indian Lucknow University Authorities
    Refuse to Accept USIS "Grant"
    [Xinhua News Agency] According to the Indian "Crossroads Weekly" report: Lucknow University refused to accept 625,000 rupees donated by the United States Information Service. It is said that the USIS donated the money to open a "psychological experimental station." The university authorities, in refusing the "grant," said that accepting the help of the USIS would encourage interference with the freedom of Lucknow University.
    The Ambala "Tribune" commented on the matter, saying: "Indian public opinion will support the Lucknow University Board of Directors' decision not to accept the grant."

### 印度勒克瑙大学当局  拒绝接受美国新闻处“赠款”

1953-06-15
第4版()
专栏：

    印度勒克瑙大学当局
    拒绝接受美国新闻处“赠款”
    【新华社讯】据印度“十字路周报”报道：勒克瑙大学拒绝接受美国新闻处赠给它的六十二万五千卢比。据说美国新闻处赠送这笔钱是为了开办一个“心理实验站”。大学当局在拒绝这笔“赠款”的时候说：接受美国新闻处的帮助将助长对勒克瑙大学的自由进行干涉。
    翁巴拉“论坛报”评论这件事说：“印度公众舆论将支持勒克瑙大学董事会不接受这笔赠款的决定”。
