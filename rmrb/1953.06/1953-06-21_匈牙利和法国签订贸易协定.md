Title: Hungary and France Sign Trade Agreement
Author:
Date: 1953-06-21
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Budapest, June 18th: TASS reported from Budapest on June 16th: The Hungarian News Agency reported: Hungary and France have signed a trade and settlement agreement in Budapest. The agreement is effective from June 1, 1953, to June 1, 1954. France will supply Hungary with light and heavy industrial products and colonial goods, and Hungary will ship industrial and agricultural products to France.

### 匈牙利和法国签订贸易协定

1953-06-21
第4版()
专栏：

    匈牙利和法国签订贸易协定
    【新华社十八日讯】塔斯社布达佩斯十六日讯：匈牙利通讯社报道：匈牙利和法国在布达佩斯签订了贸易与结算协定。协定有效期限从一九五三年六月一日起，到一九五四年六月一日止。法国要把轻、重工业产品以及殖民地的货物供给匈牙利，匈牙利要把工业与农业产品运给法国。
