Title: Attending the World Peace Council Meeting: Leaders of Peace Movements from Various Countries Arrive in Budapest One After Another
Author:
Date: 1953-06-16
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency, Budapest, June 15th: Leaders of peace movements from various countries are arriving in Budapest one after another to attend the World Peace Council meeting that opened in Budapest on the 15th.

The members of the World Peace Council who have already arrived in Budapest include: Tikhonov, Ehrenburg, Vasilyevskaya, Obalin and Archbishop Nikolai from the Soviet Union, Professor Mukarovsky from Czechoslovakia, and Guo Moruo and Xiao San from China. Others who have already arrived include: World Peace Council Secretary-General Lafitte and French public figure Laurent Casanova, President of the Women's International Democratic Federation, Mrs. Gordon, German female writer Anna Seghers, and Canadian peace movement leader James Gareth Endicott.

The World Peace Council has also invited some people to participate in this meeting, including: Pastor Martin Niemöller, Superintendent of the Lutheran Church in Hesse, West Germany, Major General S.S. Sokhey (India), former Deputy Director-General of the World Health Organization of the United Nations, West German manufacturer Hans Teextor, Generals Buxbaum and Cavalcanti, active participants in the Brazilian peace movement, Pastor Uffe Hansen from Denmark, and Colonel de Armasetti, a Chilean military officer.

The Hungarian delegation, composed of many scientists, writers, artists, advanced workers in industry and agriculture, has also been invited to participate in the meeting.

### 出席世界和平理事会会议  各国和平运动领袖陆续到达布达佩斯

1953-06-16
第4版()
专栏：

    出席世界和平理事会会议
    各国和平运动领袖陆续到达布达佩斯
    【新华社十五日讯】据塔斯社布达佩斯十五日讯：各国和平运动的领袖正陆续来到布达佩斯，出席十五日在布达佩斯开幕的世界和平理事会会议。
    已经到达布达佩斯的世界和平理事会理事有：苏联的吉洪诺夫、爱伦堡、华西列夫斯卡、奥巴林和尼古拉大主教，捷克斯洛伐克的穆卡罗夫斯基教授，中国的郭沫若和萧三。其他已经到达的人有：世界和平理事会总书记拉斐德和法国公众领袖洛朗·加桑诺瓦，国际民主妇女联合会主席戈登夫人，德国女作家安娜·西格斯，加拿大和平运动领袖文幼章等。
    世界和平理事会还邀请了一些人参加这次会议，被邀请的人中有：西德黑森州信义福音会监督马丁·尼穆勒牧师，前联合国世界卫生组织副主席斯·斯·索克少将（印度），西德制造商汉斯·泰克斯脱，巴西和平运动的积极参加者布希鲍姆将军和卡瓦尔康蒂将军，丹麦的乌斐·汉森牧师，智利军人德阿迈斯蒂上校。
    由许多科学家、作家、艺术家、工业和农业中的先进工作者组成的匈牙利代表团，也被邀请参加会议。
