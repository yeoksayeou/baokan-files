Title: International Student Union and Chinese People's Committee for World Peace Send Congratulations to the Second National Congress of the All-China Youth Federation

Author: 
Date: 1953-06-12
Page: 1

    International Student Union and Chinese People's Committee for World Peace
    Send Congratulations to the Second National Congress of the All-China Youth Federation
    [Xinhua News Agency, June 10] The International Student Union sent a telegram to the All-China Youth Federation, congratulating it on the opening of the Second National Congress of the All-China Youth Federation. The telegram is as follows: All-China Youth Federation, Beijing:
    On the occasion of the opening of your Second National Youth Congress, the Secretariat of the International Student Union extends to you its warm fraternal congratulations and wishes the Congress success in further mobilizing Chinese youth to strive for the construction of your great motherland and the fight for lasting peace. We firmly believe that Chinese youth and students will make great contributions to the 4th World Youth and Student Peace and Friendship Festival and the 3rd World Student Congress. International Student Union
    [Xinhua News Agency] The Chinese People's Committee for World Peace sent a telegram congratulating the Second National Congress of the All-China Youth Federation. The telegram is as follows: Second National Congress of the All-China Youth Federation:
    On the occasion of the opening of the Second National Congress of the All-China Youth Federation, we extend our warm congratulations to the Congress and all the representatives!
    The Second National Congress of the All-China Youth Federation will stipulate the important tasks of Chinese youth in the new historical period of the country. We firmly believe that this conference will be able to widely mobilize the youth of the whole country to join the people of the whole country and plunge into the construction upsurge of our great motherland, and strengthen the struggle to defend peace in the Far East and the world. We wish the conference a successful success.
    Chinese People's Committee for World Peace, June 10

### 国际学联和中国人民保卫世界和平委员会  电贺中华全国青年第二次代表大会

1953-06-12
第1版()
专栏：

    国际学联和中国人民保卫世界和平委员会
    电贺中华全国青年第二次代表大会
    【新华社十日讯】国际学生联合会致电中华全国民主青年联合总会，祝贺中华全国青年第二次代表大会的召开。电文如下：北京中华全国民主青年联合总会：
    兹值你们第二次青年代表大会召开之际，国际学生联合会书记处谨向你们致以热烈的兄弟的祝贺，并祝大会在进一步动员中国青年为建设你们伟大祖国和争取持久和平而奋斗的工作中获得成就。我们深信中国青年和学生将对第四届世界青年与学生和平与友好联欢节以及第三届世界学生代表大会作出伟大的贡献。国际学生联合会
    【新华社讯】中国人民保卫世界和平委员会致电祝贺中华全国青年第二次代表大会的召开。电文如下：中华全国青年第二次代表大会：
    兹值中华全国青年第二次代表大会开幕之际，我们谨向大会及全体代表们致以热烈的祝贺！
    全国青年第二次代表大会将规定中国青年在国家进入新的历史时期的重大任务。我们深信：此次大会必能广泛动员全国青年和全国人民在一起，投入我们伟大祖国的建设热潮，加强保卫远东与世界和平的斗争。我们预祝大会的胜利成功。
    中国人民保卫世界和平委员会  六月十日
