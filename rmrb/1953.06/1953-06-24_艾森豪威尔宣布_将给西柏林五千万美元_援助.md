Title: Eisenhower Announces $50 Million in "Aid" to West Berlin
Author:
Date: 1953-06-24
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Berlin, June 20th (TASS): U.S. President Eisenhower announced in Washington on June 18th that the United States will provide West Berlin with a so-called additional $50 million in aid, supposedly to further strengthen West Berlin's economy.

Berliners believe that granting this new dollar donation is a foolish attempt to encourage those American agents in West Berlin who have engaged in provocations and failed.

This blatant American interference has opened the eyes of many.

### 艾森豪威尔宣布  将给西柏林五千万美元“援助”

1953-06-24
第4版()
专栏：

    艾森豪威尔宣布
    将给西柏林五千万美元“援助”
    【新华社讯】塔斯社柏林二十日电：美国总统艾森豪威尔于六月十八日在华盛顿宣布，美国将给予西柏林五千万美元的所谓额外援助，据说这是为了更进一步加强西柏林的经济。
    柏林人士认为，给予这笔新的美元赠款是一种鼓励西柏林那些进行挑衅而失败了的美国特务的愚蠢企图。
    美国这种蛮横的干涉已使许多人睁开了眼睛。
