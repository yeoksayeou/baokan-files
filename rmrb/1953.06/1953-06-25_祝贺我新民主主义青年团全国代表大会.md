Title: Congratulating the National Congress of the New Democratic Youth League of China
Author:
Date: 1953-06-25
Page: 3

    Congratulations to the National Congress of the New Democratic Youth League of China
    Telegram of Congratulations from the Central Committee of the Romanian Labor Youth Union
    The Central Committee of the Romanian Labor Youth Union sent a telegram congratulating the Second National Congress of the New Democratic Youth League of China. The telegram reads as follows: The New Democratic Youth League of China:
    On behalf of the young builders of socialism in the Romanian People's Republic, the Central Committee of the Romanian Labor Youth Union sends warm, fighting, fraternal greetings to the Second National Congress of the New Democratic Youth League of China, and salutes the New Democratic Youth League of China, the advanced organization of Chinese youth. The New Democratic Youth League of China is now learning from the rich experience of the glorious Lenin and Stalin Communist Youth League, mobilizing millions of young people to struggle for the completion of the first five-year plan of national construction, for the realization of cultural revolution, and for the construction of a happy future. Romanian youth feel sincerely happy for the victories won by the Chinese youth brothers and the Chinese people under the leadership of the Chinese Communist Party headed by Comrade Mao Zedong in the struggle for the prosperity of New Democratic China and the defense of peace. We wish the Congress great success, the success of which will help consolidate the People's Republic of China and help the cause of defending peace among the people of all countries. With love and fraternal feelings, we await the messengers of Chinese youth to come to Bucharest to participate in the Third World Youth Congress and the Fourth World Youth and Students Peace and Friendship Festival.
                               (Xinhua News Agency)
    Telegram of Congratulations from the Vietnam Youth League and the Vietnam National Salvation Youth Group
    Nguyen Lam, member of the Standing Committee of the Vietnam Youth League and Secretary of the Vietnam National Salvation Youth Group, sent the following telegram of congratulations to the Second National Congress of the New Democratic Youth League of China: Central Committee of the New Democratic Youth League of China:
    On behalf of all the youth of Vietnam, we extend our warm congratulations to the Second National Congress of the New Democratic Youth League of China. We wish this Congress complete success, and we wish the Chinese youth, under the leadership of Chairman Mao and the Chinese Communist Party, to successfully realize the five-year construction plan.
    Under the leadership of President Ho Chi Minh and the Vietnam Workers' Party, the Vietnamese youth will always be united with the Chinese youth, and we assure you that we will participate more actively in mobilizing the masses to implement the policies of rent reduction and interest reduction put forward by President Ho, the Party, the government, and the National United Front, develop production, and wage a resolute struggle against the enemy's vicious policy of "using war to support war and using Vietnamese to fight Vietnamese," so as to eliminate the French colonialists and American interventionists and their running dogs, in order to strive for national independence and defend world peace. At present, we are using enthusiastic responses to participate in the Third World Youth Congress, the Fourth World Youth and Students Peace and Friendship Festival, and the Third World Student Congress as practical actions to express our love for the democratic youth of the world.
                               (Xinhua News Agency)
    Telegram of Congratulations from the Central Committee of the Burmese People's Youth League
    The telegram of congratulations from the Central Committee of the Burmese People's Youth League to the Second National Congress of the New Democratic Youth League of China reads as follows: Central Committee of the Youth League in Beijing:
    We extend our warm congratulations to the delegates to the Congress and all the youth, and hope that the Congress will be a complete success. (Xinhua News Agency)

### 祝贺我新民主主义青年团全国代表大会

1953-06-25
第3版()
专栏：

    祝贺我新民主主义青年团全国代表大会
    罗马尼亚劳动青年联盟中央委员会的贺电
    罗马尼亚劳动青年联盟中央委员会致电祝贺我国新民主主义青年团第二次全国代表大会，电文如下：中国新民主主义青年团：
    罗马尼亚劳动青年联盟中央委员会代表罗马尼亚人民共和国正在建设着社会主义的青年建设者们，向中国新民主主义青年团第二次全国代表大会致以热烈的、战斗的兄弟敬礼，并向中国青年的先进组织中国新民主主义青年团致敬。中国新民主主义青年团现在正在学习着光荣的列宁、斯大林共产主义青年团的丰富经验，动员千百万青年为完成国家建设第一个五年计划、为实现文化革命以及建设幸福的将来而斗争。罗马尼亚青年对中国青年兄弟们和中国人民在以毛泽东同志为首的中国共产党的领导下进行的，争取新民主主义中国的繁荣与保卫和平的斗争中所获得的胜利，感到衷心的快乐。谨祝代表大会得到巨大的成功，大会的成功将有助于中华人民共和国的巩固，有助于保卫各国人民间的和平的事业。我们怀着热爱和兄弟的感情等候着中国青年的使者前来布加勒斯特参加第三届世界青年代表大会和第四届世界青年与学生和平友谊联欢节。
                               （新华社）
    越南青年联盟和越南救国青年团的贺电
    越南青年联盟常务委员会委员、越南救国青年团书记阮蓝致中国新民主主义青年团第二次全国代表大会贺电如下：中国新民主主义青年团中央委员会：
    我们谨代表越南全体青年，向中国新民主主义青年团第二次全国代表大会致以热烈的祝贺。敬祝这个大会圆满成功，敬祝中国青年，在毛主席和中国共产党的领导下，胜利地实现五年建设计划。
    越南青年在胡志明主席和越南劳动党的领导下，永远和中国青年团结在一起，谨向你们保证：我们将更积极地参加发动群众，以贯彻实现胡主席、党、政府和民族统一战线所提出的减租减息的政策，发展生产，向敌人的“以战养战，用越南人打越南人”的恶毒政策作坚决的斗争，从而消灭法国殖民者和美国干涉者及其走狗们，以争取民族的独立，保卫世界的和平。目前我们正在用热烈地响应参加第三届世界青年代表大会，第四届世界青年与学生和平友谊联欢节和第三届世界学生代表大会的实际行动来表示我们对全世界民主青年的爱戴。
                               （新华社）
    缅甸人民青年联盟中央委员会的贺电
    缅甸人民青年联盟中央委员会给中国新民主主义青年团第二次全国代表大会的贺电，文如下：北京青年团中央：
    谨向大会代表及全体青年致以热烈的祝贺，并希望大会胜利成功。　（新华社）
