Title: A Glorious Example of Resisting Aggression and Defending Peace - Commemorating the First Anniversary of the Korean People's War Against Aggression (Pictures)
Author:
Date: 1953-06-28
Page: 5

A Glorious Example of Resisting Aggression and Defending Peace
Commemorating the First Anniversary of the Korean People's War Against Aggression
The Korean People's Army and the Chinese People's Volunteers have achieved a great victory in the war against aggression. This is a scene of the victorious meeting of the Korean People's Army and the Chinese People's Volunteers in a certain place.
Marshal Kim Il-sung, Supreme Commander of the Korean People's Army (right), and General Peng Dehuai, Commander of the Chinese People's Volunteers (left), together.
Railway workers from China and Korea are studying and improving their work together.
The Chinese People's Volunteers and the Korean people have established a deep friendship. They use breaks in the fighting to help Korean farmers with their work.
Representatives of all ethnic groups from the Second Chinese People's Delegation to Korea are with the heroes of the Volunteers and the Korean People's Army.
Peace-loving people around the world are actively supporting the Korean people's just war. This is a scene of staff from the Romanian medical team supporting Korea and Korean doctors examining soldiers of the Korean People's Army.
The young and valiant Korean People's Army, tempered by three years of great struggle, has grown into a new type of people's army.
For three years, Korean workers and peasants have persisted in production amidst the intense war environment, actively supporting the front lines. This is a scene of workers installing machines in an underground workshop of a textile factory.
Korean farmers enthusiastically hand over public grain to support the front lines.
The Korean people love their heroic troops. They often present gifts and banners to the People's Army heroes stationed nearby, expressing their heartfelt love and gratitude.
Korean educators persist in their work amidst the arduous war environment, cultivating outstanding future generations for the motherland. This is a scene from a class at Pyongyang No. 3 Girls' School.

### 反抗侵略保卫和平的光荣榜样  纪念朝鲜人民反侵略战争一周年（图片）

1953-06-28
第5版()
专栏：

    反抗侵略保卫和平的光荣榜样
    纪念朝鲜人民反侵略战争一周年
    朝鲜人民军和中国人民志愿军，在反侵略战争中获得了伟大的胜利。这是朝鲜人民军和中国人民志原军在某地胜利会师的情形
    朝鲜人民军最高司令官金日成元帅（右）和中国人民志原军司令员彭德怀将军（左）在一起。
    朝中两国铁道工人在一起研究改进工作。
    中国人民志愿军战士们和朝鲜人民已建立起深厚的友谊。他们利用战斗空隙时间，帮助朝鲜农民们工作。
    中国人民第二届赴朝慰问团各族人民代表和志愿军及朝鲜人民军的英雄们在一起。
    全世界爱好和平的人们都积极支援朝鲜人民的正义战争。这是罗马尼亚支援朝鲜的医疗队的工作人员和朝鲜医师在给朝鲜人民军战士检查身体时的情形。
    年青英勇的朝鲜人民军，经过整整三年的伟大斗争的锻炼，已经成长为一支新型的人民部队。
    朝鲜的工人和农民们，三年来，在激烈的战争环境中，坚持着生产，积极支援前线。这是工人们在一个织布厂的地下车间安装机器的情形。
    朝鲜的农民们踊跃缴纳公粮，支援前线。
    朝鲜人民热爱自己的英雄部队，他们常常向附近驻地的人民军英雄们献礼献旗，表示衷心的热爱和感激。
    朝鲜的教育工作者们，在艰苦的战争环境中，坚持工作，为祖国培育着优秀的后一代。这是平壤市第三女子学校上课时的情形。
