Title: The Seventh Plenary (Enlarged) Meeting of the Central Committee of the China Democratic League - Telegram of Respect to Chairman Mao Zedong
Author: 
Date: 1953-06-10
Page: 1

To the respected Chairman Mao:
    As our country enters a new period of large-scale construction under your wise leadership, the Seventh Plenary (Enlarged) Meeting of the Central Committee of the China Democratic League is held in Beijing. We would like to express our highest respect to you on behalf of all members of the China Democratic League.
    Our meeting summarized the work of the League since the Sixth Plenary Session; discussed the current international and domestic situation, as well as the work of our League in welcoming the three major tasks of the country under the favorable domestic and international situation; revised the League's charter; and strengthened the League's central leadership.
    The meeting has successfully concluded today. We resolutely assure you: Under your leadership and the leadership of the Chinese Communist Party, all members of our Democratic League will follow the resolutions of this meeting, continue to carry out ideological remolding, improve ideological education on distinguishing between ourselves and the enemy, strengthen work, strengthen unity, strengthen learning, actively participate in national construction, and focus on participating in cultural and educational construction; strive to complete the three major tasks, thoroughly realize the Common Program, build a New Democratic country, and advance towards socialism!
The Seventh Plenary (Enlarged) Meeting of the Central Committee of the China Democratic League
          June 8, 1953

### 中国民主同盟中央委员会第七次全体（扩大）会议  给毛泽东主席的致敬电

1953-06-10
第1版()
专栏：

    中国民主同盟中央委员会第七次全体（扩大）会议
    给毛泽东主席的致敬电敬爱的毛主席：
    正当我们的国家在您的英明领导下进入大规模建设的新时期，我们中国民主同盟中央委员会第七次全体（扩大）会议在北京举行。我们谨代表中国民主同盟全体盟员向您致以最崇高的敬礼。
    我们的会议总结了六中全会以来的盟务工作；讨论了当前的国际国内形势，以及本盟在国内外有利的形势下迎接国家三大任务的工作；修改了本盟的盟章；加强了本盟的中央领导机构。
    会议已在今天胜利闭幕，我们向您坚决保证：我们民主同盟全体盟员在您和中国共产党的领导下，遵照这次会议的决议，继续进行思想改造，提高分清敌我的思想教育，加强工作，加强团结，加强学习，积极参加国家建设，并以参加文教建设为中心工作；为完成三大任务，彻底实现共同纲领，建设新民主主义国家并向着社会主义前进而奋斗！中国民主同盟中央委员会第七次全体（扩大）会议    
          一九五三年六月八日
