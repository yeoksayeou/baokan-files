Title: Korean Negotiation Both Sides Staff Officers Continue to Hold Administrative Meetings
Author:
Date: 1953-06-13
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, Kaesong, June 12th: The Korean Armistice Negotiation Korean-Chinese Delegation issued the following communique on June 12th:

　　On June 12th, the two groups of staff officers from both sides held administrative meetings simultaneously. The two staff meetings are scheduled to continue at 11:00 AM on the 13th.

### 朝鲜谈判双方参谋人员继续开行政性会议

1953-06-13
第1版()
专栏：

    朝鲜谈判双方参谋人员继续开行政性会议
    【新华社开城十二日电】朝鲜停战谈判朝中代表团六月十二日发表公报如下：
    六月十二日双方两组参谋人员同时举行了行政性会议，两组参谋会议定于十三日上午十一时继续开会。
