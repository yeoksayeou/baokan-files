Title: Soviet People's Subscription to Public Bonds Exceeds Quota; Ministry of Finance Issues Circular to All Regions Nationwide to Cease Further Subscription to Public Bonds
Author:
Date: 1953-06-29
Page: 4

    Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, 28th: TASS Moscow, 28th: The Soviet Ministry of Finance announced:
    As of June 27th, the subscription amount for the 1953 public bond for the development of the Soviet national economy, originally set at 15 billion rubles, has reached 15.343 billion rubles.
    Upon the instructions of the Council of Ministers of the Soviet Union, the Soviet Ministry of Finance has ordered that all regions nationwide cease further subscription to public bonds from June 28th, 1953.
      A. Zverev (signed), Minister of Finance of the Soviet Union

### 苏联人民认购公债数超过定额  财政部通令全国各地不再继续认购公债

1953-06-29
第4版()
专栏：

    苏联人民认购公债数超过定额
    财政部通令全国各地不再继续认购公债
    【新华社二十八日讯】塔斯社莫斯科二十八日电：苏联财政部公告：
    截至六月二十七日为止，原定发行额为一百五十亿卢布的一九五三年发展苏联国民经济公债的认购数额，已达一百五十三亿四千三百万卢布。
    苏联财政部奉苏联部长会议的指示，已下令自一九五三年六月二十八日起全国各地不再继续认购公债。
      苏联财政部部长阿·兹维列夫（签署）
