Title: Swiss Peace Organization Supports the Conclusion of a Five-Power Peace Pact
Author:
Date: 1953-06-14
Page: 4

    Swiss Peace Organization
    Supports the Conclusion of a Five-Power Peace Pact
    [Xinhua News Agency, Bern, 10th] TASS, Bern, 10th: The Swiss Peace Committee wrote an open letter to the Swiss Federal Council, requesting it to declare its support for the conclusion of a Five-Power Peace Pact.
    The letter stated: The Swiss people will welcome such a declaration from the Federal Council, because they know that today's problems should be resolved not by force, but through consultation and mutual understanding between countries.

### 瑞士和平组织  拥护缔结五大国和平公约

1953-06-14
第4版()
专栏：

    瑞士和平组织
    拥护缔结五大国和平公约
    【新华社讯】塔斯社伯尔尼十日电：瑞士和平委员会写给瑞士联邦政务委员会一封公开信，要求它声明赞成缔结五大国和平公约。
    这封信说：瑞士人民将欢迎联邦政务委员会作这样的声明，因为他们知道，不应该以武力，而应该通过协商和各国间的相互了解来解决今天的问题。
