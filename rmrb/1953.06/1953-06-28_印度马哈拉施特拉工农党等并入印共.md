Title: Maharashtra Peasants and Workers Party of India and others merge into the Communist Party of India
Author:
Date: 1953-06-28
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, June 27th: According to the "For Lasting Peace, For People's Democracy!" published on the 12th, the Maharashtra Peasants and Workers Party of India held a meeting from May 14th to 16th in Lanvad village near Nasik. More than 120 representatives from Berar, Poona, Nasik and other counties of Maharashtra attended the meeting. The meeting unanimously approved the decision of the Executive Committee of the Peasants and Workers Party to merge into the Communist Party of India. All county organizations of the Peasants and Workers Party have expressed their support for this decision. At more than twenty conferences, attended by more than 500,000 peasant masses, this decision was also endorsed.

Prior to this, the Left Socialist Party and the Social Revolutionary Party of Northern Province had already merged into the Communist Party of India.

Note: Maharashtra includes the central part of Bombay Province, the southwestern part of the Central Province, and the northwestern part of Hyderabad State.

### 印度马哈拉施特拉工农党等并入印共

1953-06-28
第4版()
专栏：

    印度马哈拉施特拉工农党等并入印共
    【新华社二十七日讯】据十二日出版的“争取持久和平，争取人民民主！”报道：印度马哈拉施特拉工农党，于五月十四日到十六日在纳西克附近的兰瓦德村举行会议。参加会议的有贝刺尔、浦那、纳西克和马哈拉施特拉的其他县的一百二十多个代表。会议一致通过了工农党执行委员会的关于工农党并入印度共产党的决定。工农党的所有县组织都已经表示拥护这个决定。在二十多次大会上，参加这些大会的五十万以上的农民群众，也对这个决定表示赞同。
    在这以前，左派社会主义党和北方省的社会革命党等已经并入印度共产党。
    按：马哈拉施特拉包括孟买省中部、中央省西南部和海得拉巴邦西北部地区。
