    Our Great Motherland
    After the Dujiangyan annual maintenance project was completed, more than three million mu of fertile land in the western Sichuan Plain were fully irrigated. This is a view of the Zouma River diversion gate, completed this spring, releasing water to irrigate farmland.

### 我们伟大的祖国（图片）

1953-06-29
第1版()
专栏：

    我们伟大的祖国
    都江堰岁修工程完成后，川西坝子的三百多万亩良田得到了充分的灌溉。这是今年春季完工的走马河节制闸，正在放水灌溉农田的情形。
