Title: U.S. Side Injures Four More of Our Captured Personnel

Author:
Date: 1953-06-05
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Kaesong, 4th—The U.S. side has successively injured four more of our captured personnel. According to the U.S. side, on May 29th, in the Busan POW camp, one prisoner of war was injured by falling rocks while being forced to do dangerous hard labor. On May 30th, in Geoje Island, one prisoner of war was beaten and injured. On June 1st, the U.S. side injured two more prisoners of war on Geoje Island.

### 美方又打伤我被俘人员四名

1953-06-05
第1版()
专栏：

    美方又打伤我被俘人员四名
    【新华社开城四日电】美方又接连打伤我方被俘人员四名。据美方宣布，五月二十九日，在釜山战俘营中有一名战俘在被迫从事危险的苦工时，被落下的岩石打伤。五月三十日，在巨济岛上有一名战俘被殴打成伤。六月一日，美方又在巨济岛上打伤战俘二名。
