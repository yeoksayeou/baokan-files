Title: 5,000 People Hold Rally in Sao Paulo, Brazil, Demanding End to Korean War
Author:
Date: 1953-06-06
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Montevideo, 5th: Brazilian labor unions, cultural organizations, and other groups, as well as prominent public figures, are all demanding an immediate peaceful resolution to the Korean question.

    According to the "People's Daily" newspaper, a rally attended by 5,000 people was held in Sao Paulo, where participants demanded an early ceasefire in Korea. In Rio de Janeiro, the Brazilian Press Association hosted a rally protesting American aggression in Korea. The conference pointed out that a Korean armistice is the universal desire of the Brazilian people and the people of the world.

### 巴西圣保罗五千人举行大会  要求停止朝鲜战争

1953-06-06
第4版()
专栏：

    巴西圣保罗五千人举行大会
    要求停止朝鲜战争
    【新华社五日讯】塔斯社蒙得维的亚讯：巴西工会、文化团体和其他组织以及著名的公众领袖，都要求以和平的方式立刻解决朝鲜问题。
    据“人民报”报道：在圣保罗举行了一个有五千人参加的大会，大家要求早日在朝鲜停火。在里约热内卢，巴西新闻协会主持了一个抗议美国侵略朝鲜的大会。大会指出：朝鲜停战是巴西和全世界人民的普遍愿望。
