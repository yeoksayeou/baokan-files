Title: 154th Anniversary of Pushkin's Birth: Commemorative Meeting Held in Beijing

Author:

Date: 1953-06-06
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, June 5th: To commemorate the 154th anniversary of the birth of the great Russian poet Pushkin on June 6th, the All-China Federation of Literary Workers, the Beijing Sino-Soviet Friendship Association, and the Soviet Society for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries jointly held a commemorative meeting on the 5th. More than 900 people from literary and art circles, student circles, and government cadres in the capital attended.

T.F. Terekhov, representative of the Soviet Society for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries in China, and Soviet friends also attended the commemorative meeting.

The commemorative meeting was presided over by Lao She, member of the National Committee of the All-China Federation of Literary and Art Circles and Vice President of the Beijing Sino-Soviet Friendship Association. At the meeting, the poet Tian Jian made a report entitled "Ode to Pushkin." Then, actors from the Beijing People's Art Theater and the China Youth Art Theater recited Pushkin's poems: "To Siberia," "The Tale of the Fisherman and the Goldfish," and "Exegi Monumentum."

Finally, the films "Pushkin and Moscow" and "Dubrovsky," adapted from Pushkin's novel, were screened.

### 普希金诞生一百五十四周年  北京各界举行纪念会

1953-06-06
第1版()
专栏：

    普希金诞生一百五十四周年
    北京各界举行纪念会
    【新华社五日讯】六月六日是俄罗斯伟大诗人普希金诞生一百五十四周年纪念日，中华全国文学工作者协会、北京市中苏友好协会和苏联对外文化协会在五日联合举办纪念会。到会有首都文学艺术界、学生界，以及机关干部九百多人。
    苏联对外文化协会驻我国代表季列也夫和苏联友人也参加了纪念会。
    纪念会由中华全国文学艺术界联合会全国委员会委员、北京市中苏友好协会副会长老舍主持。会上，诗人田间做了题为“普希金颂”的报告。接着由北京人民艺术剧院和中国青年艺术剧院演员朗诵普希金的诗作：“致西伯利西”、“渔夫和金鱼的故事”和“纪念碑”。
    最后并放映电影：“普希金与莫斯科”和根据普希金的小说改编的电影：“杜布罗夫斯基”。
