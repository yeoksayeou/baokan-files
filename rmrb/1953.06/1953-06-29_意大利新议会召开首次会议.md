Title: Italian New Parliament Holds First Meeting
Author:
Date: 1953-06-29
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency, Rome, 28th. The Italian Parliament's upper and lower houses held their first meetings on the 25th. The majority elected Cesare Merzagora of the Christian Democratic Party as Speaker of the Upper House and Giovanni Gronchi as Speaker of the Lower House. Nenni, the General Secretary of the Socialist Party, submitted to the parliament a bill signed by Socialist Party members, demanding the repeal of the electoral system "reform" law passed by the previous parliament under government pressure and the restoration of the 1948 election law. Vittorio, General Secretary of the Italian General Confederation of Labor, and other communist and socialist parliamentarians proposed to the parliament that the government should abolish measures prohibiting civil servants from participating in strikes.

### 意大利新议会召开首次会议

1953-06-29
第4版()
专栏：

    意大利新议会召开首次会议
    【新华社二十八日讯】罗马消息：意大利议会上、下两院已于二十五日召开第一次会议，会议多数选出天主教民主党的契萨雷·梅查戈拉为上院议长，乔瓦尼·格隆基为下院议长。社会党总书记南尼向议会提出了一项由社会党议员签字的法案，要求取销上届议会在政府压力下通过的选举制“改革”法，恢复一九四八年的选举法，意大利总工会总书记维多里奥和其他共产党、社会党的议员向议会提议，要求政府取销禁止公务人员参加罢工的措施。
