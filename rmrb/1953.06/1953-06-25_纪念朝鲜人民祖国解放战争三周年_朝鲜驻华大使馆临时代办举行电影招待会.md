Title: Commemorating the Third Anniversary of the Fatherland Liberation War of the Korean People: Korean Embassy in China Chargé d'Affaires Holds Film Reception
Author: 
Date: 1953-06-25
Page: 1
　　
    To commemorate the third anniversary of the Korean People's Fatherland Liberation War on June 25th, the Chargé d'Affaires of the Embassy of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea in China, Xu Zhe, held a film reception in Beijing on the 24th, showing the Korean film "People Who Defend Their Hometowns."
    Those invited to attend included: Chen Shutong, Vice Chairman of the China People's Committee to Resist US Aggression and Aid Korea; Zhang Xiruo, Minister of Education of the Central People's Government; Zhang Hanfu, Vice Minister of Foreign Affairs; Li Zheren, Vice Minister of Foreign Trade; Ding Xilin, Vice Minister of Culture; Xiao Hua, Deputy Director of the General Political Department of the Chinese People's Revolutionary Military Commission; Wu Yuzhang, President of the Renmin University of China; Hong Shen, Director of the Bureau of Foreign Cultural Relations of the Cultural and Educational Committee of the Central People's Government; and people from all walks of life in Beijing.
    Also invited to attend were diplomatic envoys and officials from various countries stationed in China.
    The screened Korean film was warmly welcomed.

### 纪念朝鲜人民祖国解放战争三周年  朝鲜驻华大使馆临时代办举行电影招待会

1953-06-25
第1版()
专栏：

    纪念朝鲜人民祖国解放战争三周年
    朝鲜驻华大使馆临时代办举行电影招待会
    【新华社二十四日讯】为纪念六月二十五日朝鲜人民祖国解放战争三周年，朝鲜民主主义人民共和国驻华大使馆临时代办徐哲，二十四日在北京举行电影招待会，放映朝鲜影片“保卫家乡的人们”。
    应邀参加的有：中国人民抗美援朝总会副主席陈叔通，中央人民政府教育部部长张奚若，外交部副部长章汉夫，对外贸易部副部长李哲人，文化部副部长丁西林，中国人民革命军事委员会总政治部副主任萧华，中国人民大学校长吴玉章，中央人民政府政务院文化教育委员会对外文化联络事务局局长洪深，以及北京各界人士。
    应邀参加的还有各国驻华使节和外交官员。
    放映的朝鲜影片，受到热烈的欢迎。
