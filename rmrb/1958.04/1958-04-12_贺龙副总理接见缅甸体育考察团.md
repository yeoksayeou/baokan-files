Title: Vice Premier He Long Receives Burmese Sports Delegation
Author:
Date: 1958-04-12
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency, April 11th: Vice Premier of the State Council He Long received the head of the Burmese Sports Delegation, U Zaw Weik, and all members of the delegation in Beijing today.
    Huang Zhong, Deputy Director of the Physical Culture and Sports Commission, was also present at the reception.

### 贺龙副总理接见缅甸体育考察团

1958-04-12
第4版()
专栏：

    贺龙副总理接见缅甸体育考察团
    新华社11日讯　国务院副总理贺龙今天在北京接见了缅甸体育考察团团长吴佐韦和考察团全体人员。
    接见时，体育运动委员会副主任黄中也在座。
