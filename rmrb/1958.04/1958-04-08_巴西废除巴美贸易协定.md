Title: Brazil Abolishes Brazil-U.S. Trade Agreement
Author:
Date: 1958-04-08
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, Montevideo, 3rd dispatch: Rio de Janeiro News: Brazil has formally announced the abolition of the trade agreement signed with the United States in 1955.

### 巴西废除巴美贸易协定

1958-04-08
第6版()
专栏：

    巴西废除巴美贸易协定
    新华社讯　塔斯社蒙得维的亚3日消息：里约热内卢消息，巴西已经正式宣布废除1955年和美国签订的贸易协定。
