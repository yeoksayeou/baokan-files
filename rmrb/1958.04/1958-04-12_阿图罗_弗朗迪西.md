Personage Introduction

    Arturo Frondizi

    Argentine President Arturo Frondizi worked as a lawyer in his early years and entered Congress in 1946. In January 1954, he was elected Chairman of the National Committee of the "Civic Radical Union."

    In February of this year, Frondizi was elected president as the candidate of the "Intransigent Civic Radical Union." Because he put forward some political propositions in line with national interests during this campaign, he won the support of all democratic parties and the Communist Party in Argentina.

    The "Intransigent Civic Radical Union" advocates guaranteeing democratic freedom, protecting national resources, developing national industry, establishing diplomatic and trade relations with all countries in the world, and pursuing an independent and peaceful foreign policy in its campaign platform.

### 阿图罗·弗朗迪西

1958-04-12
第6版()
专栏：人物介绍

    阿图罗·弗朗迪西
    阿根廷总统阿图罗·弗朗迪西早年从事律师职业，1946年开始进入国会。1954年1月，他当选为“公民激进联盟”全国委员会主席。
    今年2月间，弗朗迪西以“不妥协公民激进联盟”的候选人资格当选总统。由于他在这次竞选时提出了一些符合民族利益的政治主张，因此得到了阿根廷一切民主党派和共产党的支持。
    “不妥协公民激进联盟”在竞选纲领中主张保障民主自由，保护国家资源，发展民族工业，同世界各国建立外交和贸易关系以及奉行独立的和平外交政策。
