Title: Soviet Delegation Arrives in Argentina to Attend New President's Inauguration
Author:
Date: 1958-04-29
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, Buenos Aires, 27th April—TASS reported: Argentine newspapers are paying great attention to the news that the Soviet government delegation, which has come to attend the inauguration ceremony of the new President of the Republic, Frondizi, has arrived here. All newspapers today published detailed accounts of the arrival of the Soviet government delegation, and pointed out the warm welcome they received at the airport. "La Prensa," "Democracia," and other newspapers published photographs of the delegation members and the statement made by the head of the delegation, Tarasov, Vice Chairman of the Presidium of the Supreme Soviet of the Soviet Union.

Frondizi's personal representative, Ortiz de Rosas, visited the Soviet government delegation on the evening of the 27th, and the two sides held friendly talks.

### 苏联代表团到阿根廷参加新总统就职典礼

1958-04-29
第5版()
专栏：

    苏联代表团到阿根廷参加新总统就职典礼
    新华社28日讯　据塔斯社布宜诺斯艾利斯27日讯：阿根廷报纸十分重视前来参加共和国新总统弗朗迪西就职典礼的苏联政府代表团到达这里的消息。今天所有报纸都刊登了苏联政府代表团到达的详细情况，并且指出了他们在飞机场所受到的热烈欢迎。“新闻报”、“民主报”以及其他报纸刊登了代表团团员的照片和代表团团长、苏联最高苏维埃主席团副主席塔拉索夫发表的声明。
    弗朗迪西的私人代表奥尔蒂斯·德·罗萨斯27日晚间访问了苏联政府代表团，双方进行了友好的谈话。
