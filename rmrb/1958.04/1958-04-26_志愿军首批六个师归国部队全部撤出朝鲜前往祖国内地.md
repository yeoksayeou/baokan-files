Title: The First Six Divisions of the Chinese People's Volunteer Army Withdraw from Korea to the Motherland

Author:

Date: 1958-04-26
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Pyongyang, April 25th:
    The Headquarters of the Chinese People's Volunteer Army announced: From March 15th to April 25th, the first group of six divisions, totaling 80,000 personnel, of the Chinese People's Volunteer Army, have all withdrawn from Korea and returned to the motherland.

Xinhua News Agency, Andong, April 25th:
    As of today, the first group of returning troops of the Chinese People's Volunteer Army have all passed through Andong on their way to the interior of the motherland.

    Since March 16th, more than 100,000 people in Andong City, regardless of day or night, braving wind and rain, have gone to the station to welcome the victorious return of the "most beloved people," and have held nearly a hundred discussion meetings with the Volunteer Army officers and soldiers, mutually introducing the victory of defeating the American aggressors and the great achievements of the socialist construction of the motherland. The "Friendship Flower" wall newspaper on the platform of Andong Station continuously published a large number of letters of condolence from people from all walks of life across the country.

    The returning heroes wrote a large number of letters of thanks to the people of Andong City, thanking their relatives for their warm welcome.

### 志愿军首批六个师归国部队全部撤出朝鲜前往祖国内地

1958-04-26
第1版()
专栏：

    志愿军首批六个师归国部队全部撤出朝鲜前往祖国内地
    新华社平壤25日电　中国人民志愿军总部撤军公报：从3月15日到4月25日，中国人民志愿军第一批六个师八万人，已经全部撤出朝鲜返回祖国。
    新华社安东25日电　到今天为止，中国人民志愿军首批归国部队已经全部经过安东前往祖国内地。
    从3月16日以来，安东市人民共有十多万人不分昼夜、不避风雨地到车站欢迎了“最可爱的人”胜利归来，并且和志愿军官兵们进行了近百次座谈会，互相介绍了击败美国侵略军的胜利和祖国社会主义建设的伟大成就。在安东车站站台上的“友谊之花”墙报，连续刊载了来自祖国各地的各界人民的大批慰问信。
    归国英雄们给安东市人民写了大批感谢信，感谢亲人们对他们的热情欢迎。
