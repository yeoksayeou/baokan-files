Title: Serving Production, Integrating with Reality: Academy of Sciences Sends Personnel for Large-Scale Soil Survey
Author: Guo Huanzhong
Date: 1958-04-05
Page: 7

    Serving Production, Integrating with Reality
    Academy of Sciences Sends Personnel for Large-Scale Soil Survey

    (Our Newspaper) Beijing - The Soil Team of the Chinese Academy of Sciences will dispatch sixty researchers to the Yangtze and Fen River basins this year to conduct large-scale soil surveys and research.

    The forty-eight personnel going to the Yangtze River will cooperate with the Soil Team of the Yangtze River Basin Planning Office, forming twenty-four working groups to conduct surveys in the Jianghan Plain and the Poyang Lake area. The plan is to complete 52,000 square kilometers this year. The twelve researchers going to the Fen River basin will cooperate with the Shanxi Provincial Water Conservancy Bureau, forming seven groups, striving to complete 12,000 square kilometers in the Jinan and Shushui areas before the rainy season arrives in Shanxi (August-September).

    No one has conducted such a large-scale survey in these areas before. These surveyors will not only solve production problems but also collect a large amount of original data for scientific research. The researchers will depart for the two locations around mid-April. (Guo Huanzhong)

### 服务生产　结合实际  科学院派人进行大规模土壤调查
郭焕忠
1958-04-05
第7版()
专栏：

    服务生产　结合实际
    科学院派人进行大规模土壤调查
    本报讯　中国科学院土壤队今年有六十位研究人员将赴长江、汾河两流域进行大规模的土壤调查研究工作。
    到长江去的四十八人将与长江流域规划办公室土壤队合作，组成二十四个工作组，在江汉平原和鄱阳湖一带进行调查。计划今年完成五万二千平方公里的任务。到汾河流域去的十二位研究人员将与山西省水利局合作，组成七个组，争取在山西雨季来临前（8、9月），完成晋南和涑水地区的一万二千平方公里的任务。
    上述地区，过去还没有人进行过这样大面积的调查工作。这些调查人员不但要解决生产问题，而且要收集大量供科学研究的原始资料。研究人员将于4月中旬前后出发，分赴两地。　（郭焕忠）
