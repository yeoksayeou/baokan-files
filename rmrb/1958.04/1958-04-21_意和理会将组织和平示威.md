Title: Italian Peace Council to Organize Peace Demonstration
Author:
Date: 1958-04-21
Page: 5
Xinhua News Agency, 20th: The Italian National Peace Council will organize a peace demonstration in Genoa on May 4th against missile bases and to strive for the cessation of nuclear weapons testing.

### 意和理会将组织和平示威

1958-04-21
第5版()
专栏：

    意和理会将组织和平示威
    据新华社20日讯　意大利全国和平理事会将在5月4日在热那亚组织一次反对导弹基地、争取停止试验核武器的和平示威。
