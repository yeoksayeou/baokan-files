Title: Colombian Foreign Minister Advocates for Complete Disarmament
Author:
Date: 1958-04-11
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, Buenos Aires, April 10th: According to a report in the Colombian magazine "Semana," Colombian Foreign Minister Carlos Sanz de Santamaria advocates reaching an agreement on complete disarmament.

### 哥伦比亚外长主张全面裁军

1958-04-11
第6版()
专栏：

    哥伦比亚外长主张全面裁军
    据新华社10日讯　据塔斯社布宜诺斯艾利斯9日讯：据哥伦比亚“一周”杂志报道，哥伦比亚外交部长卡洛斯·桑斯·德圣塔马里亚主张就全面裁军达成协议。
