Title: Celebrating the Fifth Anniversary of the Establishment of Friendship with the Czech-Chinese Friendship Agricultural Cooperative: A Gathering is Held at the Sino-Czech Friendship Agricultural Cooperative in the Suburbs of Beijing
Author:
Date: 1958-04-26
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency, April 24th: Members of the Sino-Czech Friendship Agricultural Cooperative in Zhang Guozhuang Township, Beijing, held a meeting today to celebrate the fifth anniversary of the establishment of friendship between them and the Czech-Chinese Friendship Agricultural Cooperative in Vinařice Village, Czechoslovakia.
    When Václavík, Chargé d'Affaires ad interim of the Czechoslovak Embassy in China, and embassy staff, accompanied by Zhang Youyu, Deputy Mayor of Beijing, and responsible persons from relevant districts and townships in Beijing and the Ministry of Foreign Affairs, arrived at the meeting place, they were warmly welcomed by the cooperative members.
    Guo Rong, Director of the Sino-Czech Friendship Agricultural Cooperative, expressed his gratitude on behalf of all the cooperative members to the Party, government, people of Czechoslovakia, and the brothers and sisters of Vinařice Village for their care and assistance to the cooperative.
    Václavík conveyed the friendly greetings of the Czechoslovak people and the members of the Czech-Chinese Friendship Agricultural Cooperative in his speech.

### 和捷中友好农业社建立友谊五周年  京郊中捷友好农业社集会庆祝

1958-04-26
第4版()
专栏：

    和捷中友好农业社建立友谊五周年
    京郊中捷友好农业社集会庆祝
    据新华社24日讯　北京市张郭庄乡中捷友好农业社社员今天举行了庆祝他们和捷克斯洛伐克维纳尔日采村捷中友好农业社建立友谊五周年大会。
    当捷克斯洛伐克驻华大使馆临时代办瓦塞克和使馆人员在北京市副市长张友渔，外交部和北京市有关区、乡负责人陪同下到达会场时，受到社员们的热烈欢迎。
    中捷友好农业社主任郭荣在大会上代表全体社员，向捷克斯洛伐克的党、政府、人民和维纳尔日采村的兄弟姐妹们对这个社的关怀、帮助表示感谢。
    瓦塞克在讲话中转达了捷克斯洛伐克人民和捷中友好农业社社员们的友谊问候。
