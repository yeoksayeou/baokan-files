Title: Romanian Government Delegation Returns to Beijing Accompanied by Premier Zhou Enlai; Hubei Governor Zhang Tixue Hosts Banquet for All Distinguished Guests
Author:
Date: 1958-04-07
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, April 6th: The Romanian government delegation, headed by Chairman Chivu Stoica, concluded its visit to Wuhan and returned to Beijing by special plane this evening, accompanied by Premier Zhou Enlai and others.

Those who welcomed them at the airport included Vice Premier Chen Yi, Deputy Secretary-General of the State Council Qi Yanming, and Director of the Department of Socialist Countries of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs Wang Yutian, among others.

Accompanying the distinguished guests to Beijing were also Vice Minister of Foreign Affairs Zeng Yongquan, China's Ambassador to Romania Ke Bonian, and Director of the Protocol Department of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs Yu Peiwen.

Xinhua News Agency, Wuhan, April 6th: Hubei Province Governor Zhang Tixue and Wuhan City Mayor Liu Huinong held a banquet at noon today to entertain all the distinguished guests of the Romanian government delegation headed by Chairman Chivu Stoica.

Premier Zhou Enlai, Vice Minister of Foreign Affairs Zeng Yongquan, China's Ambassador to Romania Ke Bonian, as well as leading figures from various fields of the Party, government, and military of Hubei Province and Wuhan City attended the banquet.

At the banquet, the hosts and guests repeatedly and enthusiastically congratulated the ever-growing eternal friendship between the Chinese and Romanian peoples, and celebrated the great unity of the socialist camp headed by the Soviet Union.

This morning, the distinguished guests, accompanied by Premier Zhou Enlai and Governor Zhang Tixue, arrived at Shanhe Township in Wuchang County, fifty kilometers from Wuhan, and visited an experimental plot cultivated by Wu Caisheng, the Party Committee Secretary of the district, in the Jinxiu Agricultural Cooperative of the township, as well as a small reservoir recently built by the farmers of Shanhe Township and the agricultural exhibition of the township. During the visit, the distinguished guests were warmly welcomed by the local farmers.

At 3:00 p.m., the distinguished guests left Wuhan for Beijing by special plane. Zhang Tixue, Liu Huinong, and other leading figures of the Party, government, and military of Hubei Province and Wuhan City went to the airport to see them off.

### 在周恩来总理陪同下  罗政府代表团回到北京  湖北省长张体学曾设宴招待全体贵宾

1958-04-07
第4版()
专栏：

    在周恩来总理陪同下
    罗政府代表团回到北京
    湖北省长张体学曾设宴招待全体贵宾
    新华社6日讯　以基伏·斯托伊卡主席为首的罗马尼亚政府代表团结束在武汉市的参观访问后，由周恩来总理等陪同乘专机在今天傍晚回到北京。
    到机场迎接的，有陈毅副总理，国务院副秘书长齐燕铭，外交部社会主义国家司司长王雨田等人。
    陪同贵宾到达北京的，还有外交部副部长曾涌泉，我国驻罗马尼亚大使柯柏年和外交部礼宾司司长俞沛文。
    新华社武汉6日电　湖北省省长张体学和武汉市市长刘惠农今天中午举行宴会，招待以基伏·斯托伊卡主席为首的罗马尼亚政府代表团全体贵宾。
    周恩来总理，外交部副部长曾涌泉，我国驻罗马尼亚大使柯柏年，以及湖北省和武汉市党政军各方面的负责人出席作陪。
    宴会上，宾主双方不断地热烈祝贺中罗人民永恒友谊的日益发展，祝贺以苏联为首的社会主义阵营的伟大团结。
    今天上午，贵宾们由周恩来总理和张体学省长等人陪同到达距武汉市五十公里的武昌县山河乡，参观了中共区委书记吴才生在这个乡的锦绣农业合作社种的一块试验田，以及山河乡农民最近兴修的一座小型水库和这个乡的农业展览会。参观时，贵宾们受到了当地农民的热烈欢迎。
    下午三时，贵宾们乘专机离开武汉前往北京。张体学、刘惠农等湖北省和武汉市的党政军负责人都到机场欢送贵宾。
