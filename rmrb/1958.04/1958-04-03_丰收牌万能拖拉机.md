Title: Harvest Brand Universal Tractor
Author:
Date: 1958-04-03
Page: 3

New Products

    Harvest Brand Universal Tractor

    The first "Harvest Brand" universal tractor in Zhejiang Province was successfully trial-produced at the Hangzhou Power Machinery Factory on the 1st.

    This tractor has six horsepower, is small, light, and can plow 1.5 mu of land per hour. It can be used in paddy fields, dry land, and larger terraced fields on hillsides. With attachments, it can also be used for irrigation, flour milling, rice husking, and power generation for lighting.

    This year, the Hangzhou Power Machinery Factory decided to manufacture 200 "Harvest Brand" tractors.
                  (According to Xinhua News Agency)

### 丰收牌万能拖拉机

1958-04-03
第3版()
专栏：新产品

    丰收牌万能拖拉机
    浙江省第一台“丰收牌”万能拖拉机1日在杭州动力机厂试制成功。
    这台拖拉机有六马力，小巧，轻便，每小时能耕地一点五亩；水田、旱地、山坡上较大的梯田都能使用。加上附件以后，它还能用来灌溉、磨粉、砻谷和发电照明。
    今年杭州动力机厂决定制造“丰收牌”拖拉机二百台。
                  （据新华社讯）
