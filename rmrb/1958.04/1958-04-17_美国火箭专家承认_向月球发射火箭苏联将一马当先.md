Title: American Rocket Expert Admits: Soviet Union Will Be First to Launch Rocket to the Moon
Author:
Date: 1958-04-17
Page: 6

    American Rocket Expert Admits
    Soviet Union Will Be First to Launch Rocket to the Moon
    Xinhua News Agency, Washington, 16th: American Army rocket expert Braun said on the 15th that in the race to launch a rocket to the moon, the Soviet Union will very likely defeat the United States, just as it preceded the United States in launching artificial satellites.

### 美国火箭专家承认  向月球发射火箭苏联将一马当先

1958-04-17
第6版()
专栏：

    美国火箭专家承认
    向月球发射火箭苏联将一马当先
    据新华社16日讯　据华盛顿消息：美国陆军火箭专家布劳恩15日说，在向月球发射火箭的竞赛中，苏联非常可能会战胜美国，就像它在发射人造地球卫星方面走在美国的前头一样。
