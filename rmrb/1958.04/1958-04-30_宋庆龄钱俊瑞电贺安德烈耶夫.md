Title: Song Qingling and Qian Junrui Send Congratulations to Andreyev
Author: 
Date: 1958-04-30
Page: 2

Xinhua News Agency, April 29th. Song Qingling, President of the Sino-Soviet Friendship Association, and Qian Junrui, Secretary-General, sent a telegram to Andreyev, Chairman of the Council of the Soviet-Chinese Friendship Association, to congratulate him on the "May Day" International Labor Day. The full text is as follows: Dear Comrade Andreyev,

    On the occasion of the "May Day" International Labor Day, on behalf of all members of the Sino-Soviet Friendship Association, I would like to extend warm congratulations to you, and through you to all members of the Soviet-Chinese Friendship Association, and to the great Soviet people. I wish the Soviet people greater victories in the great cause of building communism and striving for lasting world peace.

    Long live the eternal and unbreakable friendship between the peoples of China and the Soviet Union!
                  Song Qingling, President of the Sino-Soviet Friendship Association
                  Qian Junrui, Secretary-General of the Council of the Sino-Soviet Friendship Association
                                  Beijing, April 28, 1958

### 宋庆龄钱俊瑞电贺安德烈耶夫

1958-04-30
第2版()
专栏：

    宋庆龄钱俊瑞电贺安德烈耶夫
    新华社29日讯　中苏友好协会总会会长宋庆龄、秘书长钱俊瑞，致电苏中友好协会理事会主席安德烈耶夫，祝贺“五一”国际劳动节。全文如下：亲爱的安德烈耶夫同志：
    值此“五一”国际劳动节来临之际，谨代表中苏友好协会全体会员，向你，并通过你向苏中友好协会的全体会员、向伟大的苏联人民致以热烈的祝贺。祝苏联人民在建设共产主义和争取世界持久和平的伟大事业中，获得更大的胜利。
    中苏两国人民永恒的牢不可破的友谊万岁！
                  中苏友好协会总会会长　宋庆龄
                  中苏友好协会总会理事会秘书长　钱俊瑞
                                  1958年4月28日于北京
