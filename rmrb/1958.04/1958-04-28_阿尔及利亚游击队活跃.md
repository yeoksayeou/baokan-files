Title: Algerian Guerrillas Active
Author:
Date: 1958-04-28
Page: 6

    Xinhua News Agency, April 27th, Paris, April 26th (TASS): Algerian guerrillas have launched active military operations in recent days.
    Near Médéa in the department of Algiers, guerrillas overturned a French military vehicle, killing six French soldiers. In an ambush on a road in Greater Kabylia, guerrillas destroyed a French military truck, killing seven French soldiers on board. At the same time, the Algerian National Liberation Army engaged in fierce fighting with French colonial troops in the mountains near Sétif.

### 阿尔及利亚游击队活跃

1958-04-28
第6版()
专栏：

    阿尔及利亚游击队活跃
    据新华社27日讯　据塔斯社巴黎26日讯：阿尔及利亚游击队连日来展开了活跃的军事行动。
    在阿兰省美地亚附近，游击队使一辆法军汽车翻倒，六名法国兵丧命。游击队在内卡比利亚的公路伏击战中，炸毁了法国一辆军用卡车，击毙了车上的七名法国兵。同时，阿尔及利亚民族解放军在塞提夫附近山区和法国殖民军进行了激战。
