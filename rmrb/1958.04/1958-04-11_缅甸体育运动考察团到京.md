Title: Burmese Sports Delegation Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1958-04-11
Page: 2

    Xinhua News Agency, April 10th. The Burmese Sports Delegation, a group of five, arrived in Beijing this afternoon by train from Chongqing.
    This delegation, led by U Zaw Weik, Chairman of the Burmese National Fitness Committee, is visiting China at the invitation of the State Physical Culture and Sports Commission.
    Li Keru, Director of the General Office of the State Physical Culture and Sports Commission, and Zhang Zhihuai, Director of the Ball Games Department, were at the station to welcome them.
    Before arriving in Beijing, the delegation visited Kunming and Chongqing. After staying in Beijing for a considerable period, they will also visit Shanghai and other cities.

### 缅甸体育运动考察团到京

1958-04-11
第2版()
专栏：

    缅甸体育运动考察团到京
    新华社10日讯　缅甸体育运动考察团一行五人，今天下午由重庆乘火车到达北京。
    这个由缅甸国民健身委员会主席吴佐韦率领的代表团是应体育运动委员会邀请来中国进行访问的。
    体育运动委员会办公厅主任李克如和球类司司长张之槐曾到车站欢迎。
    代表团在抵达北京以前，曾经访问了昆明和重庆。在北京逗留相当时期以后，他们还要到上海和其他城市访问。
