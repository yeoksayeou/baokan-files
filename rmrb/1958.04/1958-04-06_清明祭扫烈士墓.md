Title: Qingming Festival Sweeping Tombs of Martyrs

Author: 
Date: 1958-04-06
Page: 2
Xinhua News Agency, Pyongyang, April 5th: General Yang Yong, Commander of the Chinese People's Volunteer Army, and General Wang Ping, Political Commissar, led more than 2,500 officers and soldiers of the Volunteer Army headquarters and local Korean people today, on the Qingming Festival, to sweep the tombs at the Cemetery of the Volunteer Army Martyrs.

Xinhua News Agency, Shenyang, April 5th: More than 500 people, including leading members of the Liaoning Province, Shenyang City Party, government, and military organizations, and representatives from all walks of life, went to the Shenyang Martyrs' Cemetery today to sweep the tombs of the martyrs.

Kim Yong-bin, the North Korean Consul General in Shenyang, and consulate staff also participated in the tomb sweeping.

After the memorial ceremony, leaders of the Party, government, and military, and representatives from all walks of life presented wreaths to the tombs of heroes such as Huang Jiguang, Yang Gensi, Sun Zhanyuan, Qiu Shaoyun, and Yang Lian'di, who gloriously sacrificed their lives in the War to Resist U.S. Aggression and Aid Korea.

Xinhua News Agency, Shanghai, April 5th: More than 10,000 workers, students, state personnel, and writers in Shanghai braved the drizzle today to sweep the tombs of the martyrs who sacrificed their lives in the liberation of Shanghai and the struggle to defend the motherland's coastal defense, as well as the tomb of Lu Xun.

Xinhua News Agency, Nanjing, April 5th: All the delegates who are attending the Second Session of the First Nanjing Municipal Congress of the Communist Party of China, and more than 300 responsible cadres from various bureaus and offices of the Nanjing Municipal People's Committee, braved the rain today to go to Yuhuatai to sweep the tombs of the martyrs.

Xinhua News Agency, Urumqi, April 5th: Wang Enmao, First Secretary of the Xinjiang Uygur Autonomous Region Committee of the Communist Party of China, Saifuddin, Secretary and Chairman of the Autonomous Region, and representatives from all walks of life in the Autonomous Region and Urumqi City, went to the Yan'erwo Revolutionary Martyrs Cemetery in the southern suburbs this morning to sweep the tombs and pay their respects to Chen Tanqiu, one of the founders of the Communist Party of China, and Mao Zemin, Qiao Guozhen, Lin Jilu, Wu Maolin, and other outstanding Communist Party members and revolutionary martyrs.

### 清明祭扫烈士墓

1958-04-06
第2版()
专栏：

    清明祭扫烈士墓
    据新华社平壤5日电　中国人民志愿军司令员杨勇上将和政治委员王平上将，在今天清明节日，率领志愿军领导机关官兵与驻地朝鲜人民一起共二千五百多人，到志愿军烈士陵园扫墓。
    据新华社沈阳5日电　辽宁省、沈阳市党、政府和军队负责人及各界代表五百多人，今天到沈阳市烈士陵园祭扫烈士陵墓。
    朝鲜驻沈阳总领事金龙彬和领事馆人员，也参加了祭扫。
    举行祭扫仪式以后，党、政府和军队首长和各界代表先后向为抗美援朝而光荣牺牲的黄继光、杨根思、孙占元、丘少云、杨连第等烈士墓前敬献花圈。
    据新华社上海5日电　上海市工人、学生、国家工作人员、作家等一万多人，今天冒着细雨分别在解放上海、保卫祖国海防斗争中牺牲的烈士墓以及鲁迅墓前祭扫。
    据新华社南京5日电　正在参加中共南京市第一届代表大会第二次会议的全体代表，南京市人民委员会各局、处负责干部共三百多人，今天冒雨前往雨花台祭扫烈士墓。
    据新华社乌鲁木齐5日电　中共新疆维吾尔自治区委员会第一书记王恩茂，书记、自治区主席赛福鼎和自治区、乌鲁木齐市各界代表，今天上午到南郊燕儿窝革命烈士陵园扫墓，吊念中国共产党创始人之一陈潭秋和中共优秀党员毛泽民、乔国桢、林基路、吴茂林等革命烈士。
