Title: Brief News

Author:

Date: 1958-04-24
Page: 6

△ A delegation from the Soviet Veterans Committee, headed by writer Boris Polevoi, arrived in Washington on the 22nd to attend the 13th anniversary celebration of the meeting of Soviet and American troops at the Elbe River on April 25, 1945.
△ The Prime Minister of the British colony of Malta, Dom Mintoff, and his government officials resigned on the 21st due to disagreements with the British government over economic aid issues.

### 简讯

1958-04-24
第6版()
专栏：简讯

    △以作家波列伏依为首的苏联退役军人委员会代表团于22日到达华盛顿，准备参加1945年4月25日苏美军队易北河会师十三周年庆祝大会。
    △英国殖民地马耳他岛总理明托夫和他的政府人员，21日因为同英国政府在经济援助问题上发生意见分歧而辞职。
