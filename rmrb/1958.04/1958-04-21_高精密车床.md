Title: High-Precision Lathe
Author: Yao Ganshen
Date: 1958-04-21
Page: 3
Column: New Products

    High-Precision Lathe

    The Nanjing Machine Tool Plant recently successfully trial-produced the high-precision single-spindle automatic lathe – Model [10]A.

    The [10]A lathe has a maximum speed of 6,300 revolutions per minute and can process a part in as little as 1.7 seconds. The entire process is automated and has complete safety protection devices.

    Compared with similar lathes from other countries, our precision structure has surpassed that of the German Factory 652, and partially surpassed that of the Swiss P7 factory. (Yao Ganshen)

### 高精密车床
姚淦深
1958-04-21
第3版()
专栏：新产品

    高精密车床
    南京机床厂最近把高精密的单轴纵切自动车床—〔10〕A机床试制成功了。
    〔10〕A机床最高转度为六千三百转／分，最快一点七秒钟就可以加工一个零件，整个的工作都是自动化的，并且还有完善的安全保护装置。
    这台机床与世界各国同类型机床相比较，我们的精度结构方面已经超过了德国的652厂，并部分超过了瑞士P7厂。　（姚淦深）
