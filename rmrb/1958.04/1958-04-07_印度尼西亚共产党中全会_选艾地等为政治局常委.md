Title: Indonesian Communist Party Central Committee Plenum Elects Aidit and Others as Members of the Politburo Standing Committee
Author:
Date: 1958-04-07
Page: 6
    Xinhua News Agency, Jakarta, April 4th. The Central Committee of the Indonesian Communist Party, at its sixth plenary session held from March 31st to April 3rd, elected Aidit, Lukman, Njoto, and Sudisman as members of the Standing Committee of the Central Political Bureau. The meeting also elected Sudisman, Jusuf Adjitorop, Peris Pardede, Siswojo, Anwar Sanusi, Anwar Kadir, and Djoko Sudjono as secretaries of the Central Committee Secretariat. The meeting also decided that all alternate members of the Central Committee be promoted to full members. These members are: S. Utajo, Tjugito, Anwar Sanusi, Anwar Kadir, and Ruski Kamaruddin.

    Alternate member of the Politburo, Jusuf Adjitorop, was promoted to full member of the Politburo.

    The Central Committee meeting of the Indonesian Communist Party pointed out that the above adjustments were made because "the party's tasks are more important than before, in order to make the party's leadership organs correctly adapt to the development of the situation."

### 印度尼西亚共产党中全会  选艾地等为政治局常委

1958-04-07
第6版()
专栏：

    印度尼西亚共产党中全会
    选艾地等为政治局常委
    新华社雅加达4日电　印度尼西亚共产党中央委员会在从3月31日到4月3日举行的第六次全体会议上选举了艾地、鲁克曼、约多和苏蒂斯曼为中央政治局常务委员会委员。会议还选举了苏蒂斯曼、优素福·阿吉托罗普、佩里斯·帕尔德德、西斯沃约、安瓦尔·萨努西、安瓦尔·卡迪尔和佐科·苏佐诺为中央委员会书记处书记。会议还决定所有中央委员会的候补委员升任为正式委员。这些委员是：斯·乌塔约、朱义多、安瓦尔·萨努西、安瓦尔·卡迪尔和鲁斯基·卡马路丁。
    政治局候补委员优素福·阿吉托罗普升任为政治局正式委员。
    印度尼西亚共产党中央委员会会议指出，上述调整是由于“党的任务比以前更重大了，是为了使党的领导机构正确地适应局势的发展”。
