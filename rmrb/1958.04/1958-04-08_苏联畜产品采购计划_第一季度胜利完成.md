Title: Soviet Union Successfully Completes First Quarter Procurement Plan for Livestock Products
Author:
Date: 1958-04-08
Page: 5

    Xinhua News Agency, 6th. TASS reported on the 5th: Materials published by the Central Statistical Bureau of the Soviet Council of Ministers indicate that, as of April 1st, the Soviet Union had completed 104% of its first quarter meat procurement plan, 103% of its milk procurement plan, and 129% of its egg procurement plan. A total of 570,000 tons of meat, 2,864,400 tons of milk, and 1,004,500,000 eggs were purchased and acquired nationwide.
    Compared with the same period last year, collective farms and state farms sold and sold to the state 100,100 tons more meat and 351,100 tons more milk in the first quarter of this year. The number of livestock on collective farms and state farms has also increased.

### 苏联畜产品采购计划  第一季度胜利完成

1958-04-08
第5版()
专栏：

    苏联畜产品采购计划
    第一季度胜利完成
    新华社6日讯　据塔斯社5日报道：苏联部长会议中央统计局公布的材料说明，截至4月1日为止，苏联第一季度肉类采购计划完成了104％，奶类完成了103％，蛋类完成了129％。全国总计征购和收购了五十七万吨肉，二百八十六万四千吨奶类，十亿零四千五百万个鸡蛋。
    同去年同期相比，今年第一季度集体农庄和国营农场向国家交售和出售的肉类增加了十万零一千吨，牛奶增加了三十五万一千吨。集体农庄和国营农场的牲畜头数也有了增加。
