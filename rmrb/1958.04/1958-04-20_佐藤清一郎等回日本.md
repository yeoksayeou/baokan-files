    Sato Seiichiro and Others Return to Japan

    Xinhua News Agency, Tianjin, 19th. The Japanese ship "Hakusan Maru" set sail from Tianjin New Port on the 19th to return to Japan. Representatives from the Japanese Red Cross Society, the Japan-China Friendship Association, and the Japan Peace Liaison Council, along with the eighth Japanese delegation to escort the remains of martyrs who died in China, headed by Sato Seiichiro, returned to Japan on the ship. On the 19th, those who saw them off at the New Port wharf included Peng Yan and Ni Feijun, Deputy Secretaries-General of the Red Cross Society of China, Ji Feng, Director of the International Liaison Department of the Red Cross Society of China, and Wu Shifa, President of the Tianjin Red Cross Society.

    Also returning to Japan on the same ship on the 19th were 422 repatriated Japanese nationals in the sixteenth group. The 2,309 sets of Japanese ashes collected from various parts of China were handed over by the Red Cross Society of China to the three Japanese organizations to be sent back to Japan on the same ship; in addition, there were ashes of three Japanese who died of illness while participating in China's liberation and construction endeavors. Beside the ashes of Anzai Masao, who died of illness while participating in China's liberation cause, were wreaths presented by the General Logistics Department Health Department of the Chinese People's Liberation Army and the Red Cross Society of China.

    Also returning to Japan on the same ship were the fifth group of eight former Japanese war criminals who had completed their sentences or were granted early release by the Chinese people. These eight individuals had been waiting in Tianjin for a ship back to Japan since their release in September of last year.

### 佐藤清一郎等回日本

1958-04-20
第2版()
专栏：

    佐藤清一郎等回日本
    新华社天津19日电　日本轮船“白山丸”19日从天津新港启碇回日本。日本红十字会、日本友好协会、日本和平联络会三团体代表和以佐藤清一郎为首的日本第八批中国殉难烈士遗骨护送团随船回日本。19日在新港码头上欢送他们的有中国红十字会总会副秘书长彭炎、倪斐君，中国红十字会总会国际联络部部长纪锋和天津市红十字会会长吴士法等。
    19日同船回日本的还有第十六批归国日侨四百二十二名。由中国红十字会交由日本三团体同船送回日本的，有在中国各地收集到的二千三百零九具日本人骨灰；此外还有参加中国解放事业和建设事业病故的三名日本人的骨灰。在参加中国解放事业中病故的安西正夫的骨灰旁，有中国人民解放军总后勤部卫生部和中国红十字会献的花圈。
    同船回日本的还有第五批服刑期满或被中国人民提前宽大释放的八名前日本战争犯罪分子。这八个人在去年9月份被释放后一直在天津候船回日本。
