Title: Finnish News Delegation Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1958-04-03
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, April 2nd. At the invitation of the All-China Journalists Association, the Finnish journalist delegation of ten people arrived in Beijing by plane from Moscow this afternoon.

The head of the Finnish journalist delegation is Olavi Lähdesmäki, Deputy Director of the Political Department and Head of the Press Section of the Finnish Ministry for Foreign Affairs. The delegation members include chairmen, editors-in-chief, and editorial secretaries of nine Finnish newspapers, radio stations, and news agencies, namely: Sundström, Sourvali, Simola, Pohjama, Kureniemi, Leskinen, Kullaa, Mattila, Stenbäck, etc.

Wang Yunsheng, Vice President of the All-China Journalists Association, Wang Yi, Deputy Editor-in-Chief of the People's Daily, Wen Jize, Deputy Director of the Broadcasting Bureau, and many others went to the airport to welcome them.

Nevanlinna, First Secretary of the Finnish Embassy in China, also went to the airport to welcome them.

### 芬兰新闻代表团到北京

1958-04-03
第4版()
专栏：

    芬兰新闻代表团到北京
    新华社2日讯　应中华全国新闻工作者协会的邀请到我国访问的芬兰新闻工作者代表团一行十人，今天下午由莫斯科乘飞机到北京。
    芬兰新闻工作者代表团团长是芬兰外交部政治司副司长兼新闻科科长奥尔科密斯，代表团团员包括芬兰九家报纸、广播电台、通讯社的董事长、总编辑、编辑秘书，他们是：孙士敦、索尔瓦利、辛波拉、波赫雅玛、库仁沙里、列斯基宁、库洛凡拉、马迪拉、斯特堡等。
    中华全国新闻工作者协会副会长王芸生，人民日报副总编辑王揖，广播事业局副局长温济泽等多人前往机场欢迎。
    芬兰驻华大使馆一等秘书耐瓦莱宁也到机场欢迎。
