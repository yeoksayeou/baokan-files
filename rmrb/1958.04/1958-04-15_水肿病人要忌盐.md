Title: Edema Patients Should Avoid Salt

Author: 

Date: 1958-04-15
Page: 8

Why?

    Edema Patients Should Avoid Salt
    There are many causes of edema, common ones being heart disease and kidney disease. But regardless of the cause, patients must eat little or no salt. This is because the ingredient of salt is sodium chloride, and sodium and water retention in the body will form or promote edema, which is like adding fuel to the fire.
    Of course, a salt-free diet is unpalatable. Therefore, scientists have invented some sodium-free but salty-tasting things to replace salt, such as the "sodium-free soy sauce" available on the market.
    Is it necessary to never eat salt? Not necessarily. When the condition changes, appropriate adjustments should be made. Because some patients who abstain from salt for a long time will have too little sodium in their body, which will produce "hyponatremia syndrome".
              (Lei)

### 水肿病人要忌盐

1958-04-15
第8版()
专栏：为什么

    水肿病人要忌盐
    水肿的原因很多，常见的是心脏病、贤脏病引起的。但不管是什么原因引起水肿，病人都必须少吃或不吃盐。这是因为食盐的成分为氯化钠，其中钠与水分在身体的潴留会形成或促进水肿，这就不啻是火上加油了。
    忌盐的饮食当然难吃的。因此，科学家们就发明了一些不含钠而又有咸味的东西来代替食盐，这就是市面上的“无钠酱油”这类东西。
    是否永远不吃盐，也不是。当病情有改变时，就要作适当调整。因为有些病人长期戒盐，体内的钠太少就会产生“低钠症候群”。
              （雷）
