Title: The People of Andong Warmly Welcome the Volunteer Army Tank Units
Author:
Date: 1958-04-02
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Andong, April 1st: The people of Andong City today warmly welcomed the tank units of the first group of Chinese People's Volunteer Army troops returning home.

At 3:30 this afternoon, as the train carrying the heroic tank units passed through the "Arch of Triumph" at the Yalu River bridgehead and entered Andong Station, the cheers and applause of the crowd on the platform, the deafening gongs and drums, and the sound of the "Volunteer Army War Song" intertwined into a scene of joyous sound, vibrating this border city of the motherland.

### 安东人民热烈欢迎志愿军坦克部队

1958-04-02
第4版()
专栏：

    安东人民热烈欢迎志愿军坦克部队
    据新华社安东1日电　安东市人民今天热烈欢迎中国人民志愿军首批归国部队中的坦克部队。
    下午三时半，载着英雄的坦克部队的列车通过鸭绿江桥头的“凯旋门”进入安东车站时，站台上人群的欢呼声、鼓掌声、震耳的锣鼓和“志愿军战歌”声，交织成一片欢腾的音响，振荡着这个祖国边城。
