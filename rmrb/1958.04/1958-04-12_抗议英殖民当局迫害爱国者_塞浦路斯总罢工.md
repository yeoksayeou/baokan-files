Title: Cyprus General Strike Protests Persecution of Patriots by British Colonial Authorities
Author:
Date: 1958-04-12
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, Nicosia, 11th dispatch: A 24-hour general strike was held throughout Cyprus on the 10th to protest the British colonial authorities' imprisonment of Cypriot patriots. After the strike began, factories stopped work, transportation was suspended, and shops closed their doors throughout Cyprus.

On this day, Cypriot patriots imprisoned in British prisons held a 24-hour hunger strike to protest the persecution by British colonialists.

The day before, the parents of these national movement participants gathered in St. John's Cathedral in Nicosia to hold a 24-hour fast to protest the British colonialists' detention of their children.

At the same time as the general strike began, the mayors of many cities in Cyprus sent telegrams to the British Governor of Cyprus, Foot, demanding that he immediately release all imprisoned Cypriot patriots and abolish the emergency law.

### 抗议英殖民当局迫害爱国者  塞浦路斯总罢工

1958-04-12
第6版()
专栏：

    抗议英殖民当局迫害爱国者
    塞浦路斯总罢工
    据新华社11日讯　尼科西亚消息：塞浦路斯全岛在10日举行了二十四小时的总罢工，抗议英国殖民当局监禁塞浦路斯爱国者。在罢工开始后，塞浦路斯各地的工厂停工、交通停顿，商店也关了门。
    在这一天，被监禁在英国监狱的塞浦路斯爱国者举行了二十四小时的绝食，抗议英国殖民者对他们的迫害。
    在前一天，这些民族运动参加者的父母集合在尼科西亚圣约翰大教堂中，为抗议英国殖民者拘留他们的儿女举行了二十四小时绝食。
    在总罢工开始的同时，塞浦路斯许多城市的市长们打电报给英国驻塞浦路斯总督福特，要求他立刻释放一切被监禁的塞浦路斯爱国者和废除紧急法。
