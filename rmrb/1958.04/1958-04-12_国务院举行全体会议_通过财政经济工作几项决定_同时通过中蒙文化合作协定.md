Title: State Council Holds Plenary Session, Adopts Several Decisions on Financial and Economic Work, and Approves Sino-Mongolian Cultural Cooperation Agreement
Author:
Date: 1958-04-12
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, 11th - The State Council held its 75th Plenary Session this afternoon.

　　The meeting discussed and adopted the following: 1. Several Decisions of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of China and the State council on the Decentralization of Industrial Enterprises. 2. Several Provisions of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of China and the State Council on the Limits of Authority for Price Management and Related Commercial Management Systems. 3. The State Council's Notice on Changing the Scope of Revenue and Expenditure, Revenue Items, and Sharing Ratios of Local Finances to Basically Fixed for Five Years. 4. Several Provisions of the State Council on Improving the Grain Management System.

　　The meeting approved the State Council's Provisions on Improving the Tax Revenue Management System (Draft), and decided to submit this draft to the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress for deliberation and approval.

　　The meeting approved the Cultural cooperation agreement between the Government of the People's Republic of China and the Government of the Mongolian People's Republic, and decided to submit it to the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress for deliberation and approval.

　　The meeting passed a resolution: to establish Baiyin City in parts of Gaolan and Jingyuan Counties, Gansu Province, centered on Haojiachuan. The city will cover an area of approximately 700 square kilometers, with a population of more than 50,000. The city's People's Committee will be stationed in Haojiachuan. To establish Huidong County in the eastern part of Huiyang County, Guangdong Province. The county will have jurisdiction over twenty townships and three towns, with a population of 298,700. The County People's Committee will be stationed in Pingshan Town.

　　The meeting also decided to abolish Wukang County in Zhejiang Province, and incorporate its original jurisdiction into Deqing County; and to abolish Xuanping County in Zhejiang Province, and divide its original jurisdiction into Wuyi and Lishui counties respectively.

　　Finally, the meeting approved the list of appointments and removals.

### 国务院举行全体会议  通过财政经济工作几项决定  同时通过中蒙文化合作协定

1958-04-12
第1版()
专栏：

    国务院举行全体会议
    通过财政经济工作几项决定
    同时通过中蒙文化合作协定
    新华社11日讯　国务院在今天下午举行了第七十五次全体会议。
    会议讨论和通过了以下几项文件：一、中共中央和国务院关于工业企业下放的几项决定。二、中共中央和国务院关于物价管理权限和有关商业管理体制的几项规定。三、国务院关于地方财政的收支范围、收入项目和分成比例改为基本上固定五年不变的通知。四、国务院关于改进粮食管理体制的几项规定。
    会议通过了国务院关于改进税收管理体制的规定（草案），并且决定将这个草案提请全国人民代表大会常务委员会审议批准。
    会议通过了中华人民共和国政府和蒙古人民共和国政府文化合作协定，并决定提请全国人民代表大会常务委员会审议批准。
    会议通过决定：将甘肃省皋兰、靖远两县所属以郝家川为中心的部分地区设立白银市，全市面积约七百多平方公里，有五万多人口，市人民委员会驻郝家川。将广东省惠阳县的东部地区设立惠东县，这个县共辖二十个乡三个镇，有二十九万八千七百多人，县人民委员会驻平山镇。
    会议还通过决定，撤销浙江省武康县，将其原辖区并入德清县；撤消浙江省宣平县，将其原辖区分别划归武义、丽水两县。
    会议最后通过了任免名单。
