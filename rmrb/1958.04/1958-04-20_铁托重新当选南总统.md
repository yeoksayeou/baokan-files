Title: Tito Re-elected as President of Yugoslavia
Author:
Date: 1958-04-20
Page: 3

Xinhua News Agency, Belgrade, April 19th: At the session of the Fourth Federal People's Assembly of Yugoslavia held today, Tito was once again elected as President of the Federal People's Republic of Yugoslavia.

### 铁托重新当选南总统

1958-04-20
第3版()
专栏：

    铁托重新当选南总统
    新华社贝尔格莱德19日电　在今天举行的南斯拉夫第四届联邦国民议会会议上，铁托再一次当选为南斯拉夫联邦人民共和国总统。
