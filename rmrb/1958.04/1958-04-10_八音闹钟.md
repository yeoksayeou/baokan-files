Title: Eight-Sound Alarm Clock
Author:
Date: 1958-04-10
Page: 3

New Products

    Eight-Sound Alarm Clock
    An eight-sound alarm clock that can play beautiful melodies such as "The East is Red" has recently been successfully trial-produced at the Chongqing Jialing Machinery Factory and will soon be mass-produced.
    All materials for this high-quality alarm clock are standardized, with high hardness and wear resistance. It is not afraid of high and low temperatures and the effects of vibration. Keeps time normally. One winding lasts thirty-six to forty-eight hours.

### 八音闹钟

1958-04-10
第3版()
专栏：新产品

    八音闹钟
    一种能弹奏“东方红”等优美曲子的八音闹钟，最近在重庆嘉陵机器厂试制成功，不久就可成批生产。
    这种高级闹钟的全部材料都是标准化，硬度高，耐磨性强，不怕高低温和震动的影响。走时正常。上条一次能走三十六到四十八小时。
