Title: Hungarian Leaders Receive Awards
Author: 
Date: 1958-04-06
Page: 3
Xinhua News Agency, Budapest, April 5th: According to Hungarian newspapers, the Presidium of the Hungarian People's Republic awarded the "For the Workers' and Peasants' Power" medal to many leaders of the Hungarian Socialist Workers' Party and the government.

The recipients included Munnich Ferenc, Premier of the Hungarian Revolutionary Worker-Peasant Government; Dobi István, Chairman of the Presidium of the Hungarian People's Republic; Apró Antal, First Deputy Premier of the Worker-Peasant Revolutionary Government; Rónai Sándor, Speaker of the National Assembly, and other leaders.

On January 28th of this year, Kádár János, First Secretary of the Central Committee of the Socialist Workers' Party, also received the same award.

### 匈一批领导人受奖

1958-04-06
第3版()
专栏：

    匈一批领导人受奖
    新华社布达佩斯5日电  据匈牙利报纸报道，匈牙利人民共和国主席团授予匈牙利社会主义工人党和政府许多领导人以“为了工农政权”奖章。
    受奖的人包括匈牙利工农革命政府总理明尼赫，匈牙利人民共和国主席团主席道比，工农革命政府第一副主席奥普罗，国民议会主席罗奈伊及其他领导人。
    在今年1月28日，社会主义工人党中央委员会第一书记卡达尔曾荣获同样的奖励。
