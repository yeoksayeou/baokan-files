Title: Hammarskjöld Welcomes New Soviet Decision
Author:
Date: 1958-04-11
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, April 10th: New York dispatch: United Nations Secretary-General Hammarskjöld said at a press conference in New York on the 8th that he welcomed the Soviet decision to halt nuclear weapons testing.

### 哈马舍尔德欢迎苏联新决定

1958-04-11
第6版()
专栏：

    哈马舍尔德欢迎苏联新决定
    据新华社10日讯　纽约消息：联合国秘书长哈马舍尔德8日在纽约记者招待会上说，他欢迎苏联停止试验核武器的决定。
