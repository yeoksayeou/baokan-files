Title: U Nu Sends Telegram to Khrushchev Congratulating Him and Welcoming the Soviet Union's Cessation of Nuclear Testing
Author:
Date: 1958-04-08
Page: 5
Xinhua News Agency, Rangoon, 7th – Burmese Prime Minister U Nu sent a telegram of congratulations to the Chairman of the Council of Ministers of the Soviet Union, Khrushchev, for the Soviet Union's cessation of nuclear weapons testing.

U Nu said in the telegram that he believed this courageous step would advance the resolution of the disarmament issue by a major step.

This telegram was announced by the official Burmese news agency on the evening of the 6th.

### 吴努电贺赫鲁晓夫  欢迎苏联停止核试验

1958-04-08
第5版()
专栏：

    吴努电贺赫鲁晓夫
    欢迎苏联停止核试验
    新华社仰光7日电　缅甸总理吴努为苏联停止核武器试验，打了一个贺电给苏联部长会议主席赫鲁晓夫。
    吴努在电报中说，他相信，这个勇敢的步骤将使裁军问题的解决前进一大步。
    这封电报是缅甸官方新闻发布机关在6日晚间公布的。
