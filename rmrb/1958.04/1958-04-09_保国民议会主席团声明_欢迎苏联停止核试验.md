Title: Bulgarian National Assembly Presidium Statement Welcomes Soviet Suspension of Nuclear Tests
Author:
Date: 1958-04-09
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, Sofia, April 7th: The Bulgarian Telegraph Agency reported that the Presidium of the Bulgarian National Assembly issued a statement today, expressing its full support for the decision of the Supreme Soviet of the Soviet Union to unilaterally suspend atomic and hydrogen weapon tests.

The statement said that if the United States and Britain, which produce atomic and hydrogen weapons, also followed the Soviet Union's example, it would be a major step forward in stopping the atomic arms race and would create preconditions for easing international tensions. The statement concluded by expressing full support for the appeal issued by the Supreme Soviet of the Soviet Union to the parliaments of all countries on the issue of an immediate cessation of atomic and hydrogen weapon tests. The statement pointed out that the Presidium of the Bulgarian National Assembly hopes that the parliaments and peoples of all countries in the world will support this noble effort by the Soviet Union and work towards the rapid achievement of a universal cessation of atomic and hydrogen weapon tests and the consolidation of world peace.

### 保国民议会主席团声明  欢迎苏联停止核试验

1958-04-09
第5版()
专栏：

    保国民议会主席团声明
    欢迎苏联停止核试验
    据新华社索非亚7日电　据保加利亚通讯社报道，保加利亚国民议会主席团今天发表声明，表示完全支持苏联最高苏维埃关于单方面停止原子武器和氢武器试验的决议。
    声明说，如果生产原子武器和氢武器的美国和英国也仿效苏联的榜样，那么在停止原子军备竞赛方面将前进一大步，并将为缓和国际紧张局势创造前提条件。声明最后还表示完全支持苏联最高苏维埃就立即停止原子武器和氢武器试验问题向各国议会发出的呼吁书。声明指出，保加利亚国民议会主席团希望世界各国议会和人民支持苏联的这一崇高的努力，并为迅速实现普遍停止原子武器和氢武器试验和巩固世界和平而努力。
