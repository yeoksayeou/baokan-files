Going to the Workers, Peasants, and Soldiers

In the surging tide of the socialist revolution, the broad masses of literary and art workers, in order to transform their thinking and better serve the workers, peasants, and soldiers, have been going to factories, rural areas, and grassroots levels, wanting to take root and settle down among the masses. Writer Zhou Libo (left) is participating in labor training in a rural area of Yiyang County, Hunan Province. He eats, lives, and labors with the local peasants. Zhou Libo has come here several times between 1954 and 1957. Xinhua News Agency reporter Wang Ping photo

The famous artist Gu Yuan is participating in agricultural labor in Dazhai Village, Zunhua County, Hebei Province. This is him instructing Zhang Shuqin (left) of the rural club art group to learn painting during a break from labor. Xinhua News Agency reporter Shi Panqi photo

Fan Fang (right), director of the China Peking Opera Theatre, has been sent to Houmaozhuang Village in Zunhua County, Hebei Province. While actively participating in labor, he also takes the time to help the village's amateur opera troupe rehearse Peking Opera and carry out cultural and recreational activities. Xinhua News Agency reporter Shi Panqi photo

Famous singer and Head of the Vocal Music Department of the Southwest Conservatory of Music, Lang Yuxiu, is performing for the masses at the "Flower Fair" in Chengdu, accompanied by Chang Sumin, Vice Chairman of the Sichuan Federation of Literary and Art Circles and President of the Southwest Conservatory of Music. Xinhua News Agency reporter You Yungu photo

### 到工农兵中去（图片）

1958-04-06
第2版()
专栏：

    到工农兵中去
    在汹涌澎湃的社会主义革命高潮中，广大的文艺工作者为了改造自己的思想，更好地为工农兵服务，纷纷走向工厂、农村、基层，要在群众中生根、落户。作家周立波（左）正在湖南益阳县农村劳动锻炼。他和当地的农民同吃、同住、同劳动。周立波在1954到1957年间曾数度来到这里。　新华社记者　王平摄
    著名的美术家古元在河北遵化县大寨村参加农业劳动。这是他在劳动后休息时指导农村俱乐部美术小组的张淑勤（左）学习画画。	新华社记者　时盘棋摄
    中国京剧院导演樊放（右），下放在河北遵化县后毛庄，他一面积极参加劳动，一面抽时间帮助村里业余剧团排练京剧，开展文娱活动。　新华社记者　时盘棋
    摄名歌唱家、西南音乐专科学校声乐系系主任郎毓秀，在成都的“花会”上为群众演唱，伴奏者为四川文联副主席、西南音乐专科学校校长常苏民。	新华社记者　游云谷摄
