Title: Saillant Calls on All Trade Union Organizations to Mobilize, Turn May Day into a Day of Peaceful Demonstration
Author:
Date: 1958-04-02
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, Budapest, April 1st: Louis Saillant, General Secretary of the World Federation of Trade Unions, made a report on March 31st at the meeting of the Executive Committee of the World Federation of Trade Unions on the development of the struggle for peace and the urgent tasks of the trade unions in strengthening unity.

In his report, Saillant emphasized that the most important task of the World Federation of Trade Unions at present is to organize concerted action by the working people of the world and strengthen the movement of the people of all countries to strive for peace, achieve disarmament, peaceful coexistence, and international cooperation.

Saillant pointed out that all trade union organizations should mobilize to turn May 1, 1958, into a powerful demonstration to strive for the unity of the working class, to strive for the realization of specific demands, to strive for the easing of international tensions, and to strive for peaceful coexistence.

Lazaro Pena, Secretary of the World Federation of Trade Unions, made a report on the work of the trade unions in the struggle against colonialism and strengthening international solidarity according to the second agenda item. In his report, Pena called on all working people to strengthen their support for the people of Indonesia, Cuba, Tunisia, Morocco, Cyprus, South Africa, and other countries.

### 赛扬号召一切工会组织动员起来  把“五一”变成和平示威的日子

1958-04-02
第5版()
专栏：

    赛扬号召一切工会组织动员起来
    把“五一”变成和平示威的日子
    据新华社布达佩斯1日电　世界工会联合会总书记路易·赛扬3月31日在世界工联执行委员会会议上作了关于和平斗争的发展情况和工会在加强团结方面的迫切任务的报告。
    赛扬在报告中着重指出，目前世界工联最重要的任务就是组织全世界劳动人民一致行动，加强各国人民争取和平、实现裁军、和平共处和国际合作的运动。
    赛扬指出，一切工会组织都应当动员起来，把1958年5月1日变成争取工人阶级团结、争取实现具体要求、争取缓和国际紧张局势和争取和平共处的强大示威。
    世界工联书记拉萨罗·培尼亚按照第二项议程作了关于工会在反对殖民主义和加强国际团结斗争中的工作报告。培尼亚在报告中号召全体劳动人民加强对印度尼西亚、古巴、突尼斯、摩洛哥、塞浦路斯、南非和其他国家人民的支援。
