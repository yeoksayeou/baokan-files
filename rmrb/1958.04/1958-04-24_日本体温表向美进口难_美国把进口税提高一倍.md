Title: Japanese Thermometers Face Difficulties Importing to the US; US Doubles Import Taxes
Author:
Date: 1958-04-24
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, Tokyo, 23rd dispatch: The US government approved on the 21st the recommendation of the US Tariff Commission to increase the import tariff rate on Japanese thermometers from the current 42.5% to 85%. This decision has aroused dissatisfaction from the Japanese side.

Kondo Shinichi, Director of the Public Information and Cultural Affairs Bureau of the Japanese Ministry of Foreign Affairs, said that this US measure is not only a major blow to the relevant manufacturers who rely heavily on exports to the United States, but also inevitably makes people in other export industries feel very uneasy.

The Japanese Jiji Press reported that Japanese manufacturers are worried that the Japanese government will take measures to forcibly control exports, including thermometers.

In 1956 and 1957, half of Japan's exported thermometers were shipped to the United States for sale.

### 日本体温表向美进口难  美国把进口税提高一倍

1958-04-24
第6版()
专栏：

    日本体温表向美进口难
    美国把进口税提高一倍
    新华社23日讯　东京消息：美国政府在21日批准了美国关税委员会关于把日本体温表的进口关税率从目前的42.5％提高到85％的建议。这个决定引起了日本方面的不满。
    日本外务省情报文化局局长近藤晋一说，美国这个措施不仅对在很大程度上有赖于向美国出口的有关厂商是个极大的打击，而且不免使其它出口工业界人士感到很大的不安。
    日本时事通讯社说，日本制造商担心日本政府将会采取措施强行控制包括体温表的出口。
    日本出口的体温表在1956和1957年，有一半是运往美国销售的。
