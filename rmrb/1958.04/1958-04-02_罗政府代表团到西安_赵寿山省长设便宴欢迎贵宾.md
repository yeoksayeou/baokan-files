Title: Romanian Government Delegation Arrives in Xi'an; Governor Zhao Shoushan Hosts Banquet to Welcome Distinguished Guests
Author:
Date: 1958-04-02
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, Xi'an, April 1st. The government delegation of the Romanian People's Republic, headed by Chairman of the Council of Ministers, Chivu Stoica, arrived in Xi'an at 5:40 PM today by special plane. Vice Foreign Minister Zeng Yongquan and Chinese Ambassador to Romania Ke Bonian accompanied them from Kunming.

Governor of Shaanxi Province, Zhao Shoushan, Secretary of the Shaanxi Provincial Committee of the Chinese Communist Party, Zhao Boping, and responsible persons from all aspects of the Party, government, army, and people of Shaanxi Province and Xi'an City went to the airport in the rain to welcome them.

In the evening, Governor Zhao Shoushan hosted a banquet to welcome the distinguished guests.

Xinhua News Agency, Kunming, April 1st. The government delegation of the Romanian People's Republic, headed by Chairman Chivu Stoica, left Kunming for Xi'an by special plane at noon today.

Vice Foreign Minister Zeng Yongquan and Chinese Ambassador to Romania Ke Bonian accompanied the distinguished guests. Governor of Yunnan Province Yu Yichuan, as well as responsible persons from the Party, government, and army, all went to the airport to see them off.

This morning, the distinguished guests visited Yuantong Mountain in Kunming City.

### 罗政府代表团到西安  赵寿山省长设便宴欢迎贵宾

1958-04-02
第1版()
专栏：

    罗政府代表团到西安
    赵寿山省长设便宴欢迎贵宾
    新华社西安1日电　罗马尼亚人民共和国部长会议主席基伏·斯托伊卡率领的政府代表团，今天下午五时四十分乘专机到达西安。我国外交部副部长曾涌泉和中国驻罗马尼亚大使柯柏年由昆明陪同前来。
    陕西省省长赵寿山、中共陕西省委书记赵伯平、陕西省、西安市党和政府、军队各方面的负责人冒雨前往机场欢迎。
    晚间，赵寿山省长设便宴欢迎贵宾。
    新华社昆明1日电　以基伏·斯托伊卡主席为首的罗马尼亚人民共和国政府代表团一行，今天中午乘专机离开昆明去西安。
    外交部副部长曾涌泉、我国驻罗马尼亚大使柯柏年陪同贵宾前往。云南省省长于一川以及党和政府、军队负责人都到机场欢送。
    今天上午，贵宾们游览了昆明市的圆通山。
