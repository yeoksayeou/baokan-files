Title: Brussels World's Fair Opens
Author:
Date: 1958-04-19
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, Brussels, 17th: The large-scale 1958 World's Fair opened in Brussels, the capital of Belgium, on the 17th. This is the first world's fair since World War II. More than fifty countries and international organizations are participating. The expo will not close until October 19.

　　On the 17th, Heysel Park was crowded and exceptionally lively.

　　At the opening ceremony, fifty airplanes flew into the sky, and 20,000 colorful balloons also rose into the air.

　　King Baudouin of Belgium presided over the opening of the Expo amidst cheers. He said that the purpose of the Expo is to create an atmosphere of mutual understanding and peace.

　　At the center of the Expo, a novel three-dimensional building made of steel and aluminum, the "Atomium," which is 102 meters high, shone brightly in the bright spring sunshine. It is a symbol of the coming atomic age.

　　Among the pavilions of various countries, the pavilions of the Soviet Union, the United States, Czechoslovakia, Hungary, France, and the United Kingdom are particularly prominent in terms of scale and architectural form.

### 布鲁塞尔世界博览会开幕

1958-04-19
第6版()
专栏：

    布鲁塞尔世界博览会开幕
    据新华社17日讯　布鲁塞尔消息：规模巨大的1958年世界博览会17日在比利时首都布鲁塞尔开幕。这是第二次世界大战以后第一次世界性的博览会。参加的国家和国际组织有五十多个。博览会到10月19日才闭幕。
    17日这一天，海塞尔公园万头攒动，分外热闹。
    开幕时，五十架飞机飞上天空，两万个五彩气球也凌空而起。
    比利时国王博杜安在欢呼声中主持博览会开幕。他说，博览会的目的是创造相互了解和和平的气氛。
    博览会的中心，有一个钢铝合制的高达一百零二公尺的新颖立体建筑物——“原子之光”馆在明媚的春阳下闪闪发光，它是即将来到的原子世纪的象征。
    在各国的展览馆中规模和建筑形式特别突出的要算苏联、美国、捷克斯洛伐克、匈牙利、法国、英国等国家的展览馆。
