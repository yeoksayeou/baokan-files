Title: Soviet-Arab Friendship Association Established in Moscow
Author:
Date: 1958-04-23
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, April 22nd: According to TASS, the Soviet-Arab Countries Friendship and Cultural Relations Association was established in Moscow on the 21st. The Deputy Minister of Culture of the Soviet Union, Kaftanov, was elected as the chairman of the association's council.
    This association was established at the initiative of many well-known Soviet scientists, social figures, workers and collective farmers.

### 苏联阿拉伯友协在莫斯科成立

1958-04-23
第5版()
专栏：

    苏联阿拉伯友协在莫斯科成立
    据新华社22日讯　据塔斯社莫斯科讯：苏联—阿拉伯东方国家友好和文化联络协会21日在莫斯科成立。苏联文化部副部长卡弗坦诺夫当选为协会理事会主席。
    这个协会是根据苏联许多知名的科学家、社会人士、工人和集体农民的倡议成立的。
