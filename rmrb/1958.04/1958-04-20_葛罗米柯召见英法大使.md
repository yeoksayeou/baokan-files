Title: Gromyko Summons British and French Ambassadors
Author:
Date: 1958-04-20
Page: 3

Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, 19th -- TASS reports: Soviet Foreign Minister Gromyko invited the British Ambassador to the Soviet Union, Reilly, and the French Ambassador, Dejean, to the Soviet Foreign Ministry on the 18th, one after the other, to hold talks with them on issues related to the preparation for a summit meeting.

### 葛罗米柯召见英法大使

1958-04-20
第3版()
专栏：

    葛罗米柯召见英法大使
    新华社19日讯　据塔斯社莫斯科讯：苏联外交部长葛罗米柯18日先后邀请驻苏联的英国大使赖利和法国大使德让到苏联外交部，就有关最高级会议的准备工作问题同他们进行了交谈。
