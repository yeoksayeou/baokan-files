Title: Locust Bean Pods
Author: 
Date: 1958-04-26
Page: 2
Wild Plant Resources

    Locust bean pods are the fruit of the pagoda tree. Pagoda trees are distributed in North China, Northwest China, and other regions. According to tests and trials in Tianjin, the outer skin of locust bean pods contains 17% glucose, about 20% sophorin (i.e., sophora yellow), and about 1% rutin. The outer skin of locust bean pods can be used to extract industrial maltose, caramel blocks, edible sugar, sophorin (dye raw material), and rutin (valuable medicine). The locust beans inside the pod contain 21% protein, 33.75% starch, and a small amount of fat, and can be used to make soy sauce, sauces, brew wine, and as feed. At present, Hebei, Shandong, Tianjin and other places have been purchasing and utilizing locust beans in large quantities.

### 槐豆角

1958-04-26
第2版()
专栏：野生植物资源

    槐豆角
    槐豆角是土槐树的子实。槐树分布在我国华北、西北等地区。据天津市化验和试制，槐豆角的外皮含有17％的葡萄糖，20％左右的黄碱素（即槐黄），1％左右的路丁。利用槐豆角外皮可提制工业用饴糖、糖色块、食用糖、黄碱素（染料原料），路丁（贵重药品）。槐豆角中的槐豆，含有21％的蛋白质，33.75％的淀粉和少量脂肪，可以制酱油、酱料、酿酒和作饲料等。目前河北、山东、天津等地已大量收购和利用槐豆。
