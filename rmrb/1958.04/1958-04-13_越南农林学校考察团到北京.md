Title: Vietnamese Agricultural and Forestry School Delegation Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1958-04-13
Page: 2

Xinhua News Agency, 12th — According to the 1958 implementation plan of the Sino-Vietnamese Cultural Agreement, a four-member Vietnamese Agricultural and Forestry School delegation, led by delegation head Liang Tingwen, arrived in Beijing on the 10th. The delegation will visit various places to inspect the teaching work of higher and secondary agricultural and forestry schools.

### 越南农林学校考察团到北京

1958-04-13
第2版()
专栏：

    越南农林学校考察团到北京
    新华社12日讯　根据中越文化协定1958年执行计划，越南农林学校考察团四人，在团长梁庭文率领下，已在10日下午到达北京。考察团将要到各地考察高等和中等农林业学校的教学工作。
