Title: Kyodo News Poll Shows Kishi Nobusuke Unpopular

Author:

Date: 1958-04-23
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, Tokyo, April 22nd: According to the results of a Kyodo News poll conducted on the upcoming general election, only 16.9% of people expressed a desire for the current Prime Minister Nobusuke Kishi to remain in office. Kyodo News said that this result "clearly shows that Prime Minister Kishi is unpopular."

The results of this survey also show that the ruling Liberal Democratic Party is becoming increasingly unpopular. In this survey, only 35.6% of people expressed support for the Liberal Democratic Party, while 59.2% of people expressed support for this party in the last Kyodo News poll.

The Japanese cabinet decided today to dissolve the Diet on April 25. The general election will be held on May 22.

### 共同社民意调查表明  岸信介不得人心

1958-04-23
第6版()
专栏：

    共同社民意调查表明
    岸信介不得人心
    新华社22日讯　东京消息：据共同社就即将举行的大选进行的民意调查的结果，仅仅有16.9％的人表示希望现任首相岸信介继续执政。共同社说，这个结果“明确地表现出岸首相不得人心”。
    这次调查的结果同时也显示出执政的自由民主党也愈来愈不得人心。在这次调查中，仅有35.6％的人表示支持自由民主党，而共同社在上次进行民意调查时，曾经有59.2％的人表示支持这个党。
    日本内阁已经在今天决定在4月25日解散国会。大选将在5月22日举行。
