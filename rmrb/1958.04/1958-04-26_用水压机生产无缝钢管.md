Title: Production of Seamless Steel Pipes Using Hydraulic Presses
Author: 
Date: 1958-04-26
Page: 3

    Producing Seamless Steel Pipes Using Hydraulic Presses
    Xinhua News Agency, Harbin, April 25th: Workers at the state-owned Hua'an Machinery Factory have boldly innovated technically, using hydraulic presses and other equipment to create a new method for producing seamless steel pipes – the hot extrusion method.
    Using the hot extrusion method to replace rolling mills in the production of seamless steel pipes is a pioneering achievement in China's steel rolling industry. This method can produce various shapes of steel pipes such as square pipes and inner-round outer-square pipes, and can also produce high-grade alloy steel, stainless steel, and other metal pipes with low hot plasticity. A hydraulic press with a pressure of 2,000 tons, if used to produce seamless steel pipes, can produce 20,000 tons per year.

### 用水压机生产无缝钢管

1958-04-26
第3版()
专栏：

    用水压机生产无缝钢管
    新华社哈尔滨25日电　国营华安机械厂职工大胆革新技术，利用水压机等设备创造出一种生产无缝钢管的新方法——热压挤法。
    用热压挤法代替轧管机生产无缝钢管，是我国轧钢工业上的创举。这种方法能够生产出方管、内圆外方等各种形状的钢管，并且能生产高级合金钢、不锈钢等热塑性低的金属管材。一台二千吨压力的水压机，如果用来生产无缝钢管，一年能够生产出两万吨。
