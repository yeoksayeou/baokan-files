Title: Conference of Independent African States Decides to Establish Informal Coordinating Body
Author:
Date: 1958-04-22
Page: 7

    Xinhua News Agency, Accra, April 20th: According to a communique released here, the Conference of Independent African States decided on April 20th to establish an informal body composed of the permanent representatives to the United Nations of all participating countries. This body will be responsible for all coordination work and for preparing for future conferences of independent African states.

    The conference also decided to hold a conference of independent African states at least once every two years, and, based on Ethiopia's proposal, decided that the next conference would be held in Addis Ababa. Special meetings of foreign ministers or experts to deal with specific issues may be held when necessary.

    The Secretary-General of the conference told reporters that a permanent secretariat would not be established.

### 非洲独立国家会议决定建立非正式协调机构

1958-04-22
第7版()
专栏：

    非洲独立国家会议决定建立非正式协调机构
    新华社阿克拉20日电　据这里发表的公报透露，非洲独立国家会议20日决定建立一个由所有参加会议的国家常驻联合国代表组成的非正式机构，这个机构将负责一切协调工作，并且为以后的非洲独立国家会议进行准备。
    会议还决定每两年至少举行一次非洲独立国家会议，并且根据埃塞俄比亚的建议决定下次会议在亚的斯亚贝巴举行。处理具体问题的外交部长或专家特别会议可以在必要时举行。
    会议秘书长告诉记者说，将不设立常设秘书处。
