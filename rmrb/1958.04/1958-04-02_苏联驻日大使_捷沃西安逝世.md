Title: Soviet Ambassador to Japan, Tevosyan, Dies
Author:
Date: 1958-04-02
Page: 5

    Xinhua News Agency, April 1st: TASS, Moscow, March 31st: The Central Committee of the Communist Party of the Soviet Union and the Council of Ministers of the USSR issued an obituary announcing that Ivan Tevosyan unfortunately passed away in Moscow on March 30th after a long illness. Ivan Tevosyan was a member of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of the Soviet Union, a representative of the Supreme Soviet of the Soviet Union, and the Soviet Ambassador to Japan, at the age of fifty-six.

### 苏联驻日大使  捷沃西安逝世

1958-04-02
第5版()
专栏：

    苏联驻日大使
    捷沃西安逝世
    据新华社1日讯　据塔斯社莫斯科3月31日讯：苏联共产党中央委员会和苏联部长会议发表了关于伊凡·捷沃西安在长期重病后不幸于3月30日在莫斯科逝世的讣告。伊凡·捷沃西安是苏联共产党中央委员会委员、苏联最高苏维埃代表、苏联驻日本大使，享年五十六岁。
