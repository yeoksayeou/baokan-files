Title: Launch a Scientific Advance and Welcome the Technological Revolution: Inner Mongolia Establishes Science Work Committee; Yunnan Prepares to Build Seven Scientific Research Institutes
Author:
Date: 1958-04-06
Page: 2

    Launch a Scientific Advance and Welcome the Technological Revolution:
    Inner Mongolia Establishes Science Work Committee; Yunnan Prepares to Build Seven Scientific Research Institutes

    This newspaper reports: According to the Inner Mongolia Daily, in order to adapt to the urgent need for scientific research and technological reform in the great leap forward in production and construction, the Inner Mongolia Autonomous Region Science Work Committee was formally established on March 26.

    At the meeting, Hu Zhaoheng, Deputy Director of the Inner Mongolia Autonomous Region Science Work Committee, delivered a report on "Achieving a Great Leap Forward in Science to Ensure a Great Leap Forward in Production."

    Hurqin, Deputy Director of the Inner Mongolia Autonomous Region Science Work Committee, conveyed the spirit of Guo Moruo, President of the Chinese Academy of Sciences and Head of the Chinese Science and Technology Delegation to the Soviet Union, on his summary report on his visit to the Soviet Union, "Strengthening Sino-Soviet Scientific Cooperation to Fight for a Great Leap Forward in Scientific Undertakings," and Chen Boda, Vice President of the Chinese Academy of Sciences, at the Fifth Enlarged Meeting of the State Council Science Planning Commission, on the spirit of the report "Emphasizing the Present over the Past, Learning While Doing."

    More than twenty scientific and technical personnel spoke at the meeting. They unanimously expressed: resolutely implement and execute the basic policy of scientific research serving the great leap forward in production; strive for self-reform, turn left step by step, give our hearts to the Party and the people, become scientific and technical workers who are "both red and expert," "red through and expert deep," and welcome the upcoming technological revolution, to better serve the socialist construction of the motherland and the autonomous region.

    Xinhua News Agency, Kunming, 5th: Yunnan Province is preparing to build seven scientific research institutes. Together with the original seven scientific research institutions, a new scientific research center will be formed in this remote province of our country.

    In order to unify the organization of scientific research forces to serve the great leap forward in frontier production, the Yunnan Office of the Chinese Academy of Sciences and the Yunnan Provincial Science and Technology Commission have been established.

    The seven scientific research institutes that are being prepared are mostly based on the scientific and technical forces of relevant units in Yunnan Province, and are established according to Yunnan's natural characteristics and the needs of industrial and agricultural development. They are the Tropical Crops Research Institute, the Non-ferrous Metals Research Institute, the Medicinal Plant Research Institute, the Tropical Disease Infectious Disease Research Institute, the Agricultural Science Research Institute, the Local Industry Science Research Institute, and the Comprehensive Research Institute of Philosophy and Social Sciences.

### 发动科学进军　迎接技术革命  内蒙古成立科学工作委员会　云南筹建七个科学研究所

1958-04-06
第2版()
专栏：

    发动科学进军　迎接技术革命
    内蒙古成立科学工作委员会　云南筹建七个科学研究所
    本报讯　内蒙古日报消息：为了适应生产建设大跃进对科学研究工作和技术改革工作的迫切需要，内蒙古自治区科学工作委员会于3月26日正式成立。
    会上由内蒙古自治区科学工作委员会副主任胡昭衡做了关于“实现科学大跃进保证生产大跃进”的报告。
    内蒙古自治区科学工作委员会副主任胡尔钦传达了中国科学院院长、中国访苏科学技术代表团团长郭沫若关于“加强中苏科学合作为促进科学事业的大跃进而战斗”的访苏总结报告和中国科学院副院长陈伯达在国务院科学规划委员会第五次扩大会议上所作的“厚今薄古，边干边学”报告的精神。
    有二十多位科学技术工作人员在大会上发了言。他们一致表示：坚决贯彻和执行科学研究工作为生产大跃进服务的基本方针；努力自我改造，步步向左转，把心交给党交给人民，成为“又红又专”“红透专深”的科学技术工作者，迎接即将到来的技术革命，更好的为祖国和自治区的社会主义建设服务。
    新华社昆明5日电　云南省正在筹建七个科学研究所，加上原有的七个科学研究机构，一个新兴的科学研究中心就要在我国这个边远省份形成。
    为了统一组织科学研究力量为边疆生产大跃进服务，中国科学院云南办事处和云南省科学技术委员会已经成立。
    正在筹建的七个科学研究所，大多是以云南省内各有关单位的科学技术力量为基础，根据云南的自然特点和工农业发展需要而设立的。它们是热带作物研究所、有色金属研究所、药用植物研究所、热带病传染病研究所、农业科学研究所、地方工业科学研究所和哲学社会科学的综合研究所。
