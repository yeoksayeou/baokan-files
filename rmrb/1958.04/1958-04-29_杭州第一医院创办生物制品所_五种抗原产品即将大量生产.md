Title: Hangzhou First Hospital Establishes Biological Products Institute; Five Types of Antigen Products to be Mass-Produced
Author: Shi Dasen
Date: 1958-04-29
Page: 7

    Hangzhou First Hospital Establishes Biological Products Institute
    Five Types of Antigen Products to be Mass-Produced

    (Our report) Hangzhou First Hospital staff, based on existing equipment, have established a hospital-affiliated Biological Products Institute. They have already produced five products: Schistosomiasis skin test antigen, Wright's antigen, Cephalin-cholesterol antigen, Kahn antigen, and Widal's antigen. Among them, the Schistosomiasis skin test antigen is a drug that capitalist countries are currently unable to manufacture. Currently, the products made by this institute can meet the testing needs of 350,000 people.

    These five products will soon be mass-produced to meet the needs of various regions.
                                                (Shi Dasen)

### 杭州第一医院创办生物制品所  五种抗原产品即将大量生产
石大森
1958-04-29
第7版()
专栏：

    杭州第一医院创办生物制品所
    五种抗原产品即将大量生产
    本报讯　杭州第一医院的职工在现有设备的基础上，创办了一个医院附属生物制品所。现在，他们已经生产了血吸虫病皮内试验抗原、华氏抗原、脑磷脂抗原、康氏抗原和肥达氏抗原等五种产品。其中血吸虫病皮内试验抗原是目前资本主义国家还不能制造的药品。现在，这个所制成的产品就可供三十五万人试验的需要。
    这五种产品，将在最近大量生产，以供应各地需要。
                                                （石大森）
