Title: West German Social Democratic Party Decides to Launch Anti-Atomic Weapons Campaign
Author:
Date: 1958-04-28
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, Berlin, 26th: Bonn dispatch: The Executive Committee of the West German Social Democratic Party held an expanded meeting in Bonn on the evening of the 25th. The meeting unanimously adopted a decision to further promote the movement against atomic death throughout West Germany.

Social Democratic Party Chairman Ollenhauer said at the meeting that equipping the West German army with atomic weapons is incompatible with the interests of easing tensions in Europe and the reunification of Germany. Therefore, the Social Democratic Party will continue to call on West German residents to oppose the Adenauer government's atomic armament policy "by all legal means."

Xinhua News Agency, Tokyo, 27th: World-renowned scientist and Nobel Prize winner Dr. Albert Schweitzer has called on the governments of the United States and Britain to hold top-level talks with the Soviet Union to sign a declaration to halt nuclear weapons testing.

### 西德社会民主党决定  开展反对原子武器运动

1958-04-28
第5版()
专栏：

    西德社会民主党决定
    开展反对原子武器运动
    据新华社柏林26日电　波恩消息：西德社会民主党执行委员会25日晚在波恩举行扩大会议。会上一致通过了一项决定，要在西德全国进一步开展反对原子死亡运动。
    社会民主党主席奥伦豪尔在会议上说：用原子武器装备西德军队是同缓和欧洲紧张局势和德国的重新统一的利益不相容的。因此社会民主党将继续号召西德居民“用一切合法手段”来反对阿登纳政府的原子武装政策。
    据新华社27日讯　东京消息：世界著名科学家、诺贝尔奖金获得者艾伯特·施维泽尔博士呼吁美英两国政府同苏联举行最高级会谈，以签署一项停止核武器试验的宣言。
