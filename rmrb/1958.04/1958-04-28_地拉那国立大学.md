Title: Tirana State University
Author:
Date: 1958-04-28
Page: 5

Tirana State University in the People's Republic of Albania was established on the basis of five higher education institutions and a scientific research institute. It is Albania's first university and opened last September. The university includes fifteen departments in liberal arts, law, science, engineering, and medicine. It is very large, with well-equipped showrooms and laboratories, and a library with a collection of 300,000 books.

Tirana University has more than 3,300 students, and more than 90% of the students enjoy state scholarships. There are 250 professors, scholars, and scientists teaching at the university.

The scientific research work of Tirana University is closely related to socialist construction. For example, teachers and students in the Industrial Department are designing construction plans for two hydropower stations with power generation capacities of 15,000 kilowatts and 20,000 kilowatts, which are the first projects designed domestically in Albania.

### 地拉那国立大学

1958-04-28
第5版()
专栏：兄弟国家文化生活

    地拉那国立大学
    阿尔巴尼亚人民共和国的地拉那国立大学，是在五个高等学院和一个科学研究院的基础上建立起来的。它是阿尔巴尼亚第一所大学，去年9月开学。这所大学包括文、法、理、工、医等科的十五个专业，规模很大，陈列室和实验室等设备都很周全，还有一个藏书三十万册的图书馆。
    地拉那大学有三千三百多名学生，而且90％以上的学生都享有国家助学金。有二百五十位教授、学者和科学工作者在大学任教。
    地拉那大学的科学研究工作同社会主义建设密切相联。例如，工业系的师生们正在设计发电能力为一万五千瓩和两万瓩的两个水电站的建筑计划，这是阿尔巴尼亚国内自己第一次设计的项目。
