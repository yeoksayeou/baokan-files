Title: International Broadcasting Organization Asian Member National Folk Music Broadcasting Conference Begins

Author:

Date: 1958-04-26
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, April 24th: The International Broadcasting Organization Asian Member National Folk Music Broadcasting Conference began today in Beijing.

Attending today's meeting were representatives and observers from eleven Asian countries.

The opening ceremony was presided over by Wen Jize, Vice Chairman of the International Broadcasting Organization Council. In his opening remarks, he said that the purpose of this conference is to introduce the achievements of Asian countries in folk music broadcasting, exchange experiences, enhance mutual understanding and friendship, and strengthen mutual ties and cooperation.

Zhang Xiruo, Director of the Commission for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries of the People's Republic of China, delivered a congratulatory speech. He extended a warm welcome to the representatives and friends from various countries attending the conference, and congratulated the conference on achieving success in developing folk music broadcasting work in various countries and promoting the exchange of folk music among Asian countries.

Wang Yuanfang, Deputy Secretary-General of the Chinese Musicians Association, delivered a congratulatory speech on behalf of Chinese music workers.

U Khin Zaw, Director of Burma Broadcasting Station, and Ilin, Deputy Director of the Broadcasting Administration Bureau of the USSR State Broadcasting and Television Committee, also spoke at the opening ceremony.

The Asian member states of the International Broadcasting Organization that sent representatives to this conference are the Soviet Union, the Mongolian People's Republic, the Democratic People's Republic of Korea, the Democratic Republic of Vietnam, and China. Afghanistan, Burma, India, Indonesia, Japan, and Pakistan sent observers to participate in this conference. The United Arab Republic will also send observers to participate in the conference.

The conference is scheduled to end on the 30th.

In addition, the Ministry of Culture of the People's Republic of China, the Commission for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries, the Broadcasting Affairs Bureau, and the Chinese Musicians Association jointly held a reception on the evening of the 24th to celebrate the opening of this conference, and to entertain the representatives and observers from various countries attending the conference.

### 国际广播组织亚洲会员国民间音乐广播会议开始举行

1958-04-26
第4版()
专栏：

    国际广播组织亚洲会员国民间音乐广播会议开始举行
    新华社24日讯　国际广播组织亚洲会员国民间音乐广播会议，今天开始在北京举行。
    参加今天会议的有来自亚洲十一个国家的代表和观察员。
    开幕式由国际广播组织理事会副主席温济泽主持。他在开幕词里说，这次会议的目的是介绍亚洲国家的民间音乐广播的成就，交流经验，增进彼此间的了解和友谊，加强相互间的联系和合作。
    中华人民共和国对外文化联络委员会主任张奚若致祝词。他对参加会议的各国代表和朋友表示热诚的欢迎，并且祝贺会议在发展各国民间音乐广播工作和促进亚洲国家民间音乐的交流方面获得成就。
    中国音乐家协会副秘书长王元方代表中国音乐工作者致了祝词。
    缅甸广播电台台长吴钦藻和苏联国家广播和电视委员会广播总局副局长伊林也在开幕式上讲了话。
    派代表出席这次会议的国际广播组织亚洲会员国有苏联、蒙古人民共和国、朝鲜民主主义人民共和国、越南民主共和国和中国；派观察员参加这次会议的有阿富汗、缅甸、印度、印度尼西亚、日本和巴基斯坦。阿拉伯联合共和国也将派观察员参加会议。
    会议预定在30日结束。
    又讯　中华人民共和国文化部、对外文化联络委员会、广播事业局和中国音乐家协会为庆祝这次会议的召开，24日晚联合举行酒会，欢宴出席会议的各国代表和观察员。
