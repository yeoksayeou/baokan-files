    Forest Resources of the Greater Khingan Range
    The Greater Khingan Range is one of China's largest forest areas, with forest reserves accounting for approximately 21% of the country's total. The Dahurian larch produced here is hard in texture, highly decay-resistant, and has straight trunks, making it ideal for mine props, railway sleepers, utility poles, etc.

### 大兴安岭森林资源

1958-04-21
第2版()
专栏：小资料

    大兴安岭森林资源
    大兴安岭是我国最大林区之一，森林蓄积量约占全国森林总蓄积量的21％。这里出产的质地坚硬、耐腐力强和树干通直的落叶松，最适于作矿柱、枕木、电柱等。
