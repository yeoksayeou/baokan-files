Title: China Establishes Committee for Commemorating World Cultural Figures; Guan Hanqing Commemorative Activities to be Held in Late June
Author:
Date: 1958-04-24
Page: 7
Xinhua News Agency, 23rd – The China Committee for Commemorating World Cultural Figures, a permanent body in charge of domestic annual commemorations of world cultural figures, was recently established in Beijing. Guo Moruo is the chairman of the committee, and Chu Tunan is the vice chairman.
    The World Peace Council has decided that the world cultural figures to be commemorated in 1958 are the Chinese playwright Guan Hanqing, the Persian poet Saadi, the Italian physicist Torricelli, the Japanese painter Ogata Kōrin, the French painter Daumier, the English poet Milton, and the Swedish female writer Lagerlöf.
    The committee has decided to adopt professional forms of commemorative activities, such as academic report meetings, discussion meetings, symposiums, exhibitions, and theatrical performances. This year's organizing units of the committee are the Chinese People's Committee for World Peace, the Chinese People's Association for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries, the Chinese Academy of Sciences, the All-China Federation of Natural Science Societies, the China Federation of Literary and Art Circles, the Chinese Writers Association, the Chinese Artists Association, and the Chinese Dramatists Association.
    The commemorative activities for the 700th anniversary of the Chinese Guan Hanqing's dramatic creation will be held in Beijing in late June. Currently, the Chinese Dramatists Association and relevant units are actively carrying out preparatory work. Commemorative activities for the other six world cultural figures will be held in the second half of this year.

### 中国纪念世界文化名人委员会成立  六月下旬将举行关汉卿纪念活动

1958-04-24
第7版()
专栏：

    中国纪念世界文化名人委员会成立
    六月下旬将举行关汉卿纪念活动
    新华社23日讯　主持国内每年进行的世界文化名人纪念活动的常设机构——中国纪念世界文化名人委员会，最近在北京成立。委员会由郭沫若任主席、楚图南任副主席。
    世界和平理事会决定1958年纪念的世界文化名人是中国戏剧家关汉卿、波斯诗人萨迪、意大利物理学家托里拆利、日本画家尾形光琳、法国画家多米埃、英国诗人米尔顿、瑞典女作家拉耶勒夫。
    委员会决定采取专业的纪念活动形式，如学术性报告会、讨论会、座谈会、展览会、戏剧演出等来进行纪念。委员会今年的筹组单位是中国人民保卫世界和平委员会、中国人民对外文化协会、中国科学院、中华全国自然科学专门学会联合会、中国文学艺术界联合会、中国作家协会、中国美术家协会、中国戏剧家协会等八个单位。
    关于中国关汉卿戏剧创作七百年的纪念活动将在6月下旬在北京举行。目前中国戏剧家协会和有关单位正在积极进行筹备工作。其他六位世界文化名人的纪念活动将在今年下半年举行。
