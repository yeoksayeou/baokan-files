Title: Soviet Scientists Discuss "Stationary" Satellites: Artificial Satellites Can Be Cleverly Arranged as Convenient Stopover Points and Observatories

Author: 
Date: 1958-04-30
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, 29th: According to TASS, Academician Barabashov of the Ukrainian Academy of Sciences published an article in "Worker's Daily" stating that launching "stationary" Earth satellites fixed above a certain area is already a realistic matter.

    He said that by launching a satellite into space above the equator and making it travel from west to east after entering orbit, with a speed exactly equivalent to the angular velocity of the Earth's rotation around its own axis, it would be possible to create a satellite that appears to be stationary from the ground. The linear speed of such a satellite's flight would be 3,076 meters per second. Calculations show that the satellite should be located 35,810 kilometers above the equator. Barabashov pointed out that when stratospheric flight reaches a certain level of development, fixed satellites launched into interstellar space may become very convenient stopover points. Generally, using non-stationary satellites as stopover points requires waiting for the satellite to move to a certain relative position with the launch site on the ground before it can be reached from the ground. Using fixed satellites as stopover points, on the other hand, makes it possible to fly to this stopover point at any time.

    If three fixed satellites are launched so that the angle between adjacent satellites and the center of the Earth is exactly 120 degrees, then, because the positions of the three satellites are all on the equatorial plane, it would be possible to observe all areas of the Earth except for the polar regions from the satellites.

### 苏联科学家谈“不动的”卫星  人造卫星能够巧安排  充当十分方便的中途站和观测所

1958-04-30
第5版()
专栏：

    苏联科学家谈“不动的”卫星
    人造卫星能够巧安排
    充当十分方便的中途站和观测所
    新华社29日讯　据塔斯社讯：乌克兰科学院院士巴拉巴绍夫在
  “工人报”上发表文章说，发射固定在某一地区上空的“不动的”地球卫星，已经是一件现实的事情。
    他说，把卫星发射到赤道上空的宇宙空间，使它在进入轨道后从西向东运行，运行的速度恰好相当于地球绕自己的轴自转的角速度，这样就能造成一个从地面上看来位置固定不动的卫星。这种卫星飞行的线速度每秒钟为三千零七十六公尺。计算表明，卫星应当位于赤道之上三万五千八百一十公里的高空中。巴拉巴绍夫指出，同温层飞行发展到一定水平时，发射到星际空间的固定卫星就可能成为一个十分方便的中途站。一般利用位置不固定的卫星做中途站，必须等待卫星运行到同设在地面上的起飞场具有某种相对位置的时候，才能从地面飞上去。而利用固定卫星做中途站，在任何时刻都可以飞到这个中途站上。
    发射三个固定卫星，使相邻的卫星同地心的夹角都正好成一百二十度，那末，由于三个卫星的位置都在赤道平面上，从卫星上就可以观察到地球上除两极地区以外的所有地区。
