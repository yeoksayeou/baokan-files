Title: Chilean Socialist Senator Opposes Latin America Following the US in Expanding Armaments; Argentine President Advocates Latin American Economic Unification
Author:
Date: 1958-04-12
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, April 11th: According to a TASS report from Buenos Aires on the 10th: According to the Chilean newspaper "El Imparcial," Chilean Socialist Senator Ampuero expressed opposition to Latin American countries expanding their armaments.

He condemned the United States' policy of military "aid" to Latin American countries, because this aid brought "extremely serious consequences." He also condemned the United States' attempt to incorporate the "Organization of American States" into the North Atlantic Treaty Organization. He said, "We believe that only by adopting a neutral stance and maintaining the principle of peaceful coexistence can we make a good start in implementing a disarmament policy in Latin America."

Xinhua News Agency, April 11th: Rio de Janeiro dispatch: Argentine President-elect Frondizi emphasized before leaving Sao Paulo, Brazil, on the 10th to return to his country that Latin American economic unification is very important. Only by developing the enormous economic potential of Latin America can political freedom be guaranteed.

Frondizi, who will take office as President of Argentina on May 1st, visited Brazil for three days from the 8th to the 10th. During the visit, he held meetings with Brazilian President Kubitschek. It is reported that the issue of economic unification between Argentina and Brazil was the subject of the talks.

### 智利社会党参议员  反对拉丁美洲追随美国扩军备战  阿根廷总统主张拉丁美洲经济统一

1958-04-12
第6版()
专栏：

    智利社会党参议员
    反对拉丁美洲追随美国扩军备战
    阿根廷总统主张拉丁美洲经济统一
    据新华社11日讯　据塔斯社布宜诺斯艾利斯10日讯：据智利“报信者报”报道，智利社会党参议员安普埃罗表示反对拉丁美洲国家扩张军备。
    他谴责美国给拉丁美洲国家军事“援助”的政策，因为这种援助带来了“极其严重的后果”。他还谴责美国想把“美洲国家组织”并入北大西洋公约组织。他说，我们认为，只有采取中立的立场和维护和平共处原则的立场才能够成为在拉丁美洲实行裁军政策的良好开端。
    新华社11日讯　里约热内卢消息：阿根廷当选总统弗朗迪西在10日离开巴西的圣保罗回国之前强调说，拉丁美洲的经济统一是非常重要的。只有发展拉丁美洲巨大的经济潜力，才能保证政治上的自由。
    将在5月1日就职阿根廷总统的弗朗迪西从8日到10日在巴西访问了三天。访问期间，他同巴西总统库比契克举行了会议。据报道，阿根廷和巴西的经济统一问题是会谈的主题。
