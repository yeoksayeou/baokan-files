Title: UAE Military Friendship Delegation Visits an Aviation School
Author:
Date: 1958-04-08
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency, April 7th: The entire United Arab Republic Military Friendship Delegation, accompanied by Air Force Lieutenant General Chang Qiankun and our country's Military Attaché to the United Arab Republic, Colonel Li Dazhong, today traveled by special plane to a certain location to visit a certain aviation school of the Chinese People's Liberation Army. The distinguished guests also watched a flight performance during the visit.

At the end of the visit, delegation leader Mohammed Ibrahim, on behalf of the United Arab Republic Armed Forces Aviation School, presented the PLA aviation school with a school flag, a photo album, and five school badges. Commander Ibrahim also presented a school badge to the principal of the aviation school on behalf of the president of the Air Force School. He said that these gifts demonstrate the profound friendship between the air force schools of China and the Arab countries. Zhao Qun, Colonel and head of the Chinese People's Liberation Army aviation school, presented the delegation with two self-made jet aircraft models on behalf of all the officers and soldiers of the school.

### 阿联军事友好访华代表团参观某航空学校

1958-04-08
第4版()
专栏：

    阿联军事友好访华代表团参观某航空学校
    新华社7日讯　阿拉伯联合共和国军事友好访华代表团全体人员，今天在常乾坤空军中将和我国驻阿拉伯联合共和国武官李大中上校等陪同下，乘专机赴某地参观了中国人民解放军某航空学校。贵宾们在参观中还观看了飞行表演。
    参观结束时，穆罕默德·易卜拉欣团长代表阿拉伯联合共和国武装部队空军学校，将一面校旗、一本图片纪念册和五枚校徽赠给解放军某航空学校。易卜拉欣团长还代表空军学校校长赠给这所航空学校校长一枚校徽。他说，这些礼物显示着中阿两国空军学校的深厚友谊。中国人民解放军某航空学校负责人赵群上校代表全校官兵，将该校自制的两架喷气式飞机模型赠给了代表团。
