Title: Truly a Servant of the People (Picture)
Author: Zhou Shuming, Zhang Ruihua
Date: 1958-04-10
Page: 3

Column: Glorious Labor, Glorious People

    Truly a Servant of the People
    Xiao Funian (first from left), a "peddler" from the Zong'ai Township Supply and Marketing Cooperative in Shouyang County, Shanxi Province, uses various methods to make it easier for the masses to buy goods. For the elderly, he delivers goods to their beds. The masses say he is "truly a servant of the people."
    Xinhua News Agency reporter Zhou Shuming and Zhang Ruihua photographed.

    真是人民的勤务员
    山西寿阳县宗艾乡供销社“货郎”萧富年（左第一人）采用各种方法，便利群众购买。对老年人，他就把货送到炕头上。群众说他“真是人民的勤务员”。
