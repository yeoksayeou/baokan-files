Title: UAR Military Delegation Visits Nanjing, Then Goes to Shanghai
Author:
Date: 1958-04-13
Page: 2

Xinhua News Agency, Nanjing, April 12th. The military friendship delegation of the United Arab Republic (UAR) concluded its three-and-a-half-day visit in Nanjing. This morning, accompanied by Lieutenant General Xiao Xiangrong, Senior Colonel Zhu Kaiyin, and our military attaché to the United Arab Republic, Senior Colonel Li Dazhong, they left Nanjing by special plane to visit Shanghai and other places. Leading members of the Chinese People's Liberation Army (PLA) stationed in Nanjing, as well as government officials from Jiangsu Province and Nanjing City, were at the airport today to see off the distinguished guests.

During their stay in Nanjing, the distinguished guests visited the PLA Military Academy, an engineering corps school, and the Yongli Ning Factory. They also paid respects at the Sun Yat-sen Mausoleum, laid wreaths at the Rain Flower Terrace Revolutionary Martyrs Cemetery, and toured scenic spots in Nanjing. They attended a banquet and soiree hosted by the leading members of the PLA stationed in Nanjing.

On the eve of their departure from Nanjing, the delegation held a farewell banquet. Before the banquet began, the delegation and the leading members of the Nanjing troops exchanged gifts. Lieutenant General Zhang Caiqian, the leading member of the Nanjing troops, also presented a command knife, captured from the invading Japanese army during the War of Resistance Against Japan, to团长 (Head of Delegation) Mohammed Ibrahim as a memento of friendship.

While visiting an engineering corps school, Senior Colonel Gu Hong, the person in charge of the school, presented a model of a military low-water bridge to the delegation on behalf of all the officers and soldiers of the school. Head of Delegation Mohammed Ibrahim presented a military flag of the engineering corps of the armed forces of the United Arab Republic and other gifts to the engineering corps school.

### 阿联军事代表团在南京参观后去上海

1958-04-13
第2版()
专栏：

    阿联军事代表团在南京参观后去上海
    新华社南京12日电　阿拉伯联合共和国军事友好访华代表团结束了在南京历时三天半的参观访问后，今天上午在萧向荣中将、朱开印大校和我国驻阿拉伯联合共和国武官李大中上校等陪同下，乘专机离开南京，前往上海等地参观访问。中国人民解放军驻南京部队首长和江苏省、南京市的政府负责人员，今天都到机场欢送贵宾。
    贵宾们在南京期间，参观访问了中国人民解放军军事学院、某工程兵学校和永利宁厂，晋谒了中山陵，凭吊了雨花台人民革命烈士墓和游览了南京的风景名胜；出席了中国人民解放军驻南京部队首长举行的宴会和晚会。
    代表团在离开南京的前夕，曾举行告别宴会。宴会开始前，代表团和南京部队首长互相赠送了礼品。南京部队首长张才千中将还把在抗日战争时期从日本侵略军队手中缴获的一柄指挥刀赠给了穆罕默德·易卜拉欣团长，作为友谊纪念。
    代表团在参观某工程兵学校时，学校负责人顾鸿大校曾代表全校官兵把一座军用低水桥梁模型赠给了代表团。穆罕默德·易卜拉欣团长把阿拉伯联合共和国武装部队工程兵部队的一面军旗和其它礼物赠给了这所工程兵学校。
