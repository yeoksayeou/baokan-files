Title: Chairman Chivu Stoica Pays Respects to Vice Chairman Zhu De and Chairman Liu Shaoqi; Premier Zhou Enlai Hosts Banquet for Distinguished Guests
Author:
Date: 1958-04-04
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, April 3rd: Chairman of the Romanian Council of Ministers, Chivu Stoica, paid respects to Vice Chairman Zhu De and Chairman Liu Shaoqi this afternoon. Accompanying him were members of the Romanian government delegation: Vice Chairman of the Romanian Council of Ministers and Minister of Transportation and Post and Telecommunications, Emil Bodnăraș; Minister of Foreign Affairs, Avram Bunaciu; and Romanian Ambassador to China, Teodor Rudenco.

Present at the meeting were Vice Premiers of the State Council Chen Yun and Li Fuchun; Vice Chairmen of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress, Li Jishen, Guo Moruo, and Cheng Qian; Vice Minister of Foreign Affairs, Zeng Yongquan; Chinese Ambassador to Romania, Ke Bonian; Director of the Department of Socialist Countries of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs, Wang Yutian; and Director of the Protocol Department, Yu Peiwen.

Xinhua News Agency, April 3rd: Premier Zhou Enlai hosted a luncheon today to entertain all the distinguished guests of the Romanian government delegation headed by Chairman Chivu Stoica. Chairman Chivu Stoica, Vice Chairman Bodnăraș, Foreign Minister Bunaciu, Ambassador Rudenco, and other accompanying members of the delegation attended the banquet.

Present to accompany them were Vice Premiers He Long and Chen Yi, Vice Minister of Foreign Affairs Zeng Yongquan, Vice Minister of Foreign Trade Li Zheren, and our country's Ambassador to Romania Ke Bonian, among others. (Attached picture)

Below: Chairman Chivu Stoica pays respects to Vice Chairman Zhu and Chairman Liu; Vice Chairman Zhu and Chairman Liu bid farewell to Chairman Chivu Stoica.

Xinhua News Agency reporter Zou Jiandong.

### 基伏·斯托伊卡主席  拜会朱副主席刘委员长  周总理设宴招待贵宾

1958-04-04
第1版()
专栏：

    基伏·斯托伊卡主席
    拜会朱副主席刘委员长
    周总理设宴招待贵宾
    新华社3日讯罗马尼亚部长会议主席基伏·斯托伊卡在今天下午拜会了朱德副主席和刘少奇委员长。随同拜会的有罗马尼亚政府代表团团员：罗马尼亚部长会议副主席兼交通和邮电部长埃米尔·波德纳拉希，外交部长阿夫拉姆·布纳丘，罗马尼亚驻中国大使特奥多尔·鲁登科。
    拜会时在座的有国务院副总理陈云、李富春，人大常委会副委员长李济深、郭沫若、程潜，外交部副部长曾涌泉，中国驻罗马尼亚大使柯柏年，外交部社会主义国家司司长王雨田，礼宾司司长俞沛文。
    新华社3日讯　周恩来总理今天中午举行便宴，招待以基伏·斯托伊卡主席为首的罗马尼亚政府代表团全体贵宾。基伏·斯托伊卡主席，波德纳拉希副主席，布纳丘外交部长，鲁登科大使和代表团的其他随行人员都应邀出席。
    出席作陪的，有贺龙、陈毅副总理，外交部副部长曾涌泉，对外贸易部副部长李哲人和我国驻罗马尼亚大使柯柏年等人。（附图片）

            新华社记者　邹健东摄
