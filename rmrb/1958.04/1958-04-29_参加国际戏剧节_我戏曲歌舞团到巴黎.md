Title: Our Traditional Opera and Dance Troupe Arrives in Paris to Participate in International Theater Festival
Author:
Date: 1958-04-29
Page: 5
Xinhua News Agency, Paris, April 28th: The Chinese Traditional Opera and Dance Troupe, led by its head Wu Han, arrived in Paris on the evening of the 27th to participate in the Paris International Theater Festival. They were warmly welcomed at the station by people from the French theater community, French friends, and overseas Chinese in France, and received bouquets of flowers.

This traditional opera and dance troupe, comprising more than eighty members, is composed of famous performing artists from the Shanghai Peking Opera Company, the Central Experimental Opera House Dance Troupe, and other units. Among them are outstanding Southern Kunqu artist Yu Zhenfei, and famous Peking Opera actors Li Yuru, Yan Huizhu, and Zhang Meijuan. The Chinese Traditional Opera and Dance Troupe is scheduled to officially begin its performances in France on the 29th.

### 参加国际戏剧节  我戏曲歌舞团到巴黎

1958-04-29
第5版()
专栏：

    参加国际戏剧节
    我戏曲歌舞团到巴黎
    新华社巴黎28日电　参加巴黎国际戏剧节的中国戏曲歌舞团在团长吴晗率领下27日晚到达巴黎。他们在车站受到法国戏剧界人士、法国友人以及中国旅法侨胞的热烈欢迎，并接受了献花。
    这个包括八十多人的戏曲歌舞团是由上海京剧院、中央实验歌剧院舞剧团等单位的一些有名的表演艺术家组成的。其中有南方昆曲杰出艺术家俞振飞、著名京剧演员李玉茹、言慧珠和张美娟等。中国戏曲歌舞团定29日起在法国正式演出。
