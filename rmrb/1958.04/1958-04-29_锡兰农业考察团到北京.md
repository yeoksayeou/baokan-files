Title: Ceylon Agricultural Delegation Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1958-04-29
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency, April 28th: The Ceylon agricultural delegation, consisting of four members led by Mr. Perera, arrived in Beijing by plane at 4:00 PM on April 27th and 28th respectively, to conduct an agricultural study in our country. Xi Fengzhou, Director of the Foreign Liaison Bureau of the Ministry of Agriculture, and others were at the airport to greet them.
　　The delegation will travel to various parts of our country in the coming days to study agricultural production.

### 锡兰农业考察团到北京

1958-04-29
第4版()
专栏：

    锡兰农业考察团到北京
    新华社28日讯　前来我国考察农业的锡兰农业考察团一行四人，在团长彼瑞拉率领下，先后于4月27、28日下午四时乘飞机到达北京。农业部对外联络局局长席凤洲等人曾到机场迎接。
    考察团日内将前往我国各地考察农业生产。
