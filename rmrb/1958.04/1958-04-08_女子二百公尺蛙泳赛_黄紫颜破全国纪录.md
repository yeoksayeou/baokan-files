Title: Women's 200m Breaststroke Race: Huang Ziyan Breaks National Record
Author:
Date: 1958-04-08
Page: 7

Xinhua News Agency, Guangzhou, April 7th. Guangzhou female swimmer Huang Ziyan broke the national record in the women's 200-meter breaststroke at the Guangzhou City Swimming Trials on the 6th. Her new time is 3 minutes 10.4 seconds, breaking the national record of 3 minutes 12.2 seconds held by Cai Meiling.

Huang Ziyan is the first athlete to break a national swimming record this year.

### 女子二百公尺蛙泳赛  黄紫颜破全国纪录

1958-04-08
第7版()
专栏：

    女子二百公尺蛙泳赛
    黄紫颜破全国纪录
    新华社广州7日电　广州市女子游泳选手黄紫颜，6日在广州市游泳测验会中，刷新了女子二百公尺蛙泳的全国纪录。她的新成绩是三分十秒四，打破了蔡美玲保持的三分十二秒二的全国纪录。
    黄紫颜是今年第一个打破全国游泳纪录的运动员。
