Title: Science Community Activity Center, Guangdong Science Hall, Opens

Author: 
Date: 1958-04-16
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Guangzhou, April 15th: The Guangdong Science Hall, a center for scientific activities in Guangdong Province, grandly opened on the 15th. After Zhu Kezhen, Vice President of the Chinese Academy of Sciences, cut the ribbon, the first Guangdong Provincial Scientific Work Conference was held in the Science Hall.

The Guangdong Science Hall is a place specifically for scientific workers to discuss papers, exchange experiences, hold academic lectures and gatherings, and conduct scientific research. This I-shaped building with a national architectural style has a construction area of nearly eight thousand square meters. It is located at the foot of Yuexiu Mountain, with a very elegant environment. At the center of the hall is a large auditorium that can accommodate more than 800 people. The hall also has eight lecture rooms, six research rooms, as well as reception rooms, lounges, and social halls.

On the fourth floor of the Science Hall is a spacious and well-equipped reading room. This reading room is part of the Guangzhou Central Library and already has more than three thousand kinds of Chinese and foreign scientific journals and more than one hundred and twenty thousand volumes of Chinese and foreign scientific books, of which more than sixty thousand volumes are ancient Chinese books. This reading room will regularly collect and store various scientific books, materials, and information for scientific workers to study and read.

The Guangzhou Artificial Satellite Observation Station is also located in the Science Hall. This hall has twelve “wide-angle telescopes” donated by the Soviet Union and an astronomical clock.

Zhu Kezhen, Vice President of the Chinese Academy of Sciences, and Fan Changjiang, Secretary-General of the State Council Science Planning Commission, traveled from Beijing to attend today's opening ceremony.

Tao Zhu, First Secretary of the Guangdong Provincial Committee of the Communist Party of China, Qu Mengjue, Secretary, Chen Yu, Governor of Guangdong Province and Director of the Guangdong Provincial Science Work Committee, and Zhu Guang, Mayor of Guangzhou, also attended the opening ceremony.

The internationally renowned Soviet scientist Academician Tamm, who is currently lecturing in Guangzhou, was also invited to attend the opening ceremony. More than 800 scientific workers from various parts of Guangdong Province and scientific workers from provinces such as Guangxi, Hunan, and Hubei also attended the opening ceremony.

### 科学界活动中心广东科学馆开幕

1958-04-16
第1版()
专栏：

    科学界活动中心广东科学馆开幕
    新华社广州15日电　广东省科学界的活动中心——广东科学馆15日隆重开幕，由中国科学院副院长竺可桢剪彩后，随即在科学馆里举行了广东省第一次科学工作会议。
    广东科学馆是专供科学工作者们进行讨论论文、交流经验、举行学术讲演和集会，以及进行科学研究的场所。这一座工字形的具有民族风格的大厦，建筑面积近八千平方公尺。它座落在越秀山麓的南边，环境十分幽雅。馆的中央是一个可容八百多人的大礼堂。馆内还设有八个报告室、六个研究室以及接待室、休息室、交谊厅等。
    科学馆的四楼有一个地方宽敞、设备讲究的阅览室。这个阅览室是广州中心图书馆的一部分，现在已有三千多种中、外文的科学期刊和十二万多册中、外文科学书籍。其中六万多册是中文古籍。这个阅览室将经常收集和收藏各种科学图书、资料和情报，供给科学工作者研究和阅读。
    广州人造卫星观测站也设在科学馆里。这个馆有十二架苏联赠送的“广角望远镜”，一座天文钟。
    中国科学院副院长竺可桢，国务院科学规划委员会秘书长范长江从北京赶来参加了今天的开幕式。
    中共广东省委第一书记陶铸、书记区梦觉，广东省省长、广东省科学工作委员会主任陈郁，广州市市长朱光等都参加了开幕式。
    正在广州讲学的国际著名的苏联科学家达姆院士也应邀参加了开幕式。参加开幕式的还有广东省各地的科学工作者和来自广西、湖南、湖北等省的科学工作者共八百多人。
