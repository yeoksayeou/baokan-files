Title: Cambodian Parliament Approves the Sim Var Cabinet
Author: 
Date: 1958-04-25
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, Phnom Penh, April 24th: After a lengthy debate, the Cambodian National Assembly today approved the new Cambodian cabinet headed by Sim Var as Prime Minister by a vote of thirty-six in favor, two against, and fifteen abstentions. Seven members of parliament who have been listed as cabinet members did not participate in the vote.

### 柬议会批准沈法内阁

1958-04-25
第6版()
专栏：

    柬议会批准沈法内阁
    据新华社金边24日讯　柬埔寨国民议会经过了长时间的辩论以后，在今天下午以三十六票赞成、两票反对和十五票弃权批准了以沈法为首相的柬埔寨新内阁。七名议员已经列为内阁阁员，没有参加这次投票。
