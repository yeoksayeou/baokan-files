    Using Biogas for Welding Metal
    The Biogas Research Group of the Guiyang Municipal Industrial Bureau recently succeeded in using biogas to replace acetylene for welding lead plates, aluminum plates, and copper plates.
    According to Yuan Shengting and Zhou Bingkun, welders from the Guiyang Chemical Plant who participated in the welding experiment, using biogas for welding has many advantages over using acetylene. It can reduce oxygen consumption by three-quarters and eliminate the need for calcium carbide altogether. The welding temperature can be adjusted arbitrarily, making it easier to ensure welding quality. Acetylene welding requires highly precise welding torches, while biogas welding can use ordinary welding torches.
                  (Xinhua News Agency dispatch)

### 利用沼气焊接金属

1958-04-09
第3版()
专栏：

    利用沼气焊接金属
    贵阳市工业局沼气研究组，最近试验利用沼气代替乙炔焊接铅板、铝板和铜板成功。
    据参加这次焊接试验的贵阳化工厂焊接工袁胜庭和周炳坤谈，用沼气焊接比用乙炔焊接有很多优点。它可以少用四分之三的氧气，并可以全部不用电石；焊接温度可以任意调节，易于保证焊接质量；乙炔焊接需要精密度很高的焊枪，而沼气焊接使用一般焊枪即可。
                  （据新华社讯）
