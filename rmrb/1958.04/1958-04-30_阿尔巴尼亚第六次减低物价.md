Title: Albania's Sixth Price Reduction

Author:

Date: 1958-04-30
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, Tirana, 28th - The Central Committee of the Albanian Labor Party and the Council of Ministers today issued a resolution on reducing retail prices of food and industrial products, announcing a reduction in retail prices of food and industrial products from April 29th. The price reductions range from 10% to 33%.

The prices of food in public canteens will also be reduced accordingly.

Prior to this year, Albania had implemented five price reductions since liberation. Including this price reduction, residents will benefit 4.4 billion leks annually.

### 阿尔巴尼亚第六次减低物价

1958-04-30
第6版()
专栏：

    阿尔巴尼亚第六次减低物价
    据新华社地拉那28日电　阿尔巴尼亚劳动党中央委员会和部长会议今天发布关于减低食品和工业品零售价格的决议，宣布从4月29日起减低食品和工业品的零售价格。减价的幅度从10％到33％不等。
    公共食堂的食品的价格，也要相应减低。
    在今年以前，阿尔巴尼亚在解放以来已经实行了五次减低物价。连同这一次减价可以使居民在一年中获益四十四亿里克。
