Title: Poem and Painting
Author: Fei Shengfu
Date: 1958-04-23
Page: 8

    Poem and Painting
    Left Sea Poem
    Fei Shengfu Painting
    Spring arrives, branches eager to blossom,
    Sent down to the mountain village, happily making home.
    A handful of ever-flowing stream by the brook,
    Blessings to the old landlady.

### 一诗一画（图片）
左海  费声福
1958-04-23
第8版()
专栏：

    一诗一画
    左海　诗
    费声福  画
    春到枝头欲着花，
    山村下放喜安家。
    溪边一掬长流水，
    祝福房东老大妈。
