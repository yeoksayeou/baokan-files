Title: Spring in the Suburbs of Beijing (Picture)
Author: Liu Changzhong
Date: 1958-04-12
Page: 2

    ——Spring in the Suburbs of Beijing—— Iron oxen replace yellow oxen, plowing a hundred mu a day, meticulous cultivation and careful work, ensuring a bumper harvest this year. The spring warmth brings the flowers to bloom, and the farm work follows, regardless of drought or flood, guaranteeing high yields. Liu Changzhong

### 北京郊区的春天（图片）
刘长忠
1958-04-12
第2版()
专栏：

    ——北京郊区的春天——铁牛代黄牛，一天百亩头，精耕又细作，今年保丰收。春暖花又开，农活跟着来，不管旱和涝，保证产量高。		刘长忠
