Title: Belgian-Chinese Friendship Association Executive Bureau Vice-Chairman Yahmed and Others Arrive in Beijing
Author: 
Date: 1958-04-03
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency, April 2nd. Yahmed, Vice-Chairman of the Executive Bureau of the Belgian-Chinese Friendship Association, and Mrs. Degen, Secretary-General, arrived in Beijing this afternoon at the invitation of the Chinese People's Association for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries.

### 比中友协执行局副主席亚梅德等到北京

1958-04-03
第4版()
专栏：

    比中友协执行局副主席亚梅德等到北京
    新华社2日讯　应中国人民对外文化协会邀请来中国访问的比利时中国友好协会执行局副主席亚梅德和秘书长德根夫人，已经在今天下午到达北京。
