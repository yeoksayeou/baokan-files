Title: "Oppose Returning to the Hungry Thirties!" More Than 10,000 Scottish Workers Demonstrate
Author:
Date: 1958-04-11
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, London, April 9th. More than 10,000 Scottish workers stopped work this afternoon and held a grand demonstration in Dundee to protest the increase in unemployment in Britain and the closure of some factories.

    A resolution was passed at a rally held by the demonstrators, demanding that the British government stop the growth of unemployment. After the meeting, the demonstrators held a parade. They held up placards with slogans such as "Oppose Returning to the Hungry Thirties!" and "We Demand Full Employment!"

### “反对回到饥饿的三十年代！”  苏格兰万余工人示威

1958-04-11
第5版()
专栏：

    “反对回到饥饿的三十年代！”
    苏格兰万余工人示威
    新华社伦敦9日电　一万多名苏格兰工人今天下午停止了工作，在敦提举行了一次盛大的示威，抗议英国失业人数的增加和一些工厂的停闭。
    在示威群众举行的集会上通过了一项决议，要求英国政府制止失业的增长。会后示威者举行了游行。他们举着标语牌，上面写着：“反对回到饥饿的三十年代！”“我们要求全部就业！”
