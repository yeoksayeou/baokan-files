Title: The First Batch of Sichuan Central Petroleum Has Been Refined (Picture)
Author: Liu Shilin
Date: 1958-04-28
Page: 1

The crude oil from the central Sichuan oil region has been refined into gasoline, kerosene, and diesel oil at the Chongqing Refinery, and the quality is excellent. More than ten tons have been refined, and these products will soon be supplied to the market by the Petroleum Corporation.
　  Xinhua News Agency reporter Liu Shilin

### 第一批川中石油炼出来了（图片）
刘诗临
1958-04-28
第1版()
专栏：

    第一批川中石油炼出来了
    四川中部石油区的原油已在重庆炼油厂炼制成了汽油、煤油和柴油，质量都很优良。现已炼出十多吨，这些产品即将由石油公司供应市场需要。
　  新华社记者　刘诗临摄
