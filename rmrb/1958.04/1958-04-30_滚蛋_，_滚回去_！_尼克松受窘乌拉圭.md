Title: “Get Out!” “Go Back!” Nixon Embarrassed in Uruguay
Author:
Date: 1958-04-30
Page: 6

    “Get Out!” “Go Back!”
    Nixon Embarrassed in Uruguay

    Xinhua News Agency, April 29th dispatch from Montevideo: U.S. Vice President Nixon encountered shouts of "Nixon go back!" upon his arrival in the Uruguayan capital on the 28th.

    Nixon will be in Uruguay for a two-day visit. This is the first stop of his eighteen-day visit to Latin American countries.

    As Nixon's motorcade drove from the airport to his residence, Uruguayan students holding demonstrations shouted slogans at him such as "Nixon go back" and "Nixon get out."

    Leaflets distributed by the students read: "Nixon is a representative of American oppression in Latin America because he supports and encourages dictatorial rule."

### “滚蛋”，“滚回去”！  尼克松受窘乌拉圭

1958-04-30
第6版()
专栏：

    “滚蛋”，“滚回去”！
    尼克松受窘乌拉圭
    新华社29日讯  蒙得维的亚消息：美国副总统尼克松在28日到达乌拉圭首都时遇到一片“尼克松滚回去”的呼声。
    尼克松将在乌拉圭进行两天的访问。这是他将要对拉丁美洲国家进行的为期十八天的访问中的第一站。
    当尼克松一行的车队从飞机场驶往寓所时，举行示威的乌拉圭学生向他高呼“尼克松滚回去”、“尼克松滚蛋”等口号。
    学生们散发的传单上写着：“尼克松是美国在拉丁美洲进行压迫的代表，因为他支持和鼓励独裁统治”。
