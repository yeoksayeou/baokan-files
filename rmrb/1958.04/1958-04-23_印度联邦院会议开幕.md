Title: Opening of the Indian Council of States Session
Author: 
Date: 1958-04-23
Page: 6
Xinhua News Agency, New Delhi, April 22nd - The twenty-first session of the Indian Council of States opened this morning. One-third of the members of the Council of States are newly elected in the recent biennial elections of the state legislative assemblies. This election has brought about some changes in the strength of the various parties in the Council of States, with the opposition parties gaining the twelve seats lost by the Congress Party. The Communist Party of India increased from eleven to fourteen seats, and the People's Socialist Party increased from three to eight seats.

### 印度联邦院会议开幕

1958-04-23
第6版()
专栏：

    印度联邦院会议开幕
    据新华社新德里22日电　印度联邦院第二十一次会议今天上午开幕。联邦院三分之一的议员都是在最近举行的两年一度的各邦立法会议选举中新产生的。这次选举使得联邦院中各党派的力量发生了一些变化，反对党派获得了国大党失去的十二个席位。印度共产党从十一席增加到十四席，人民社会党从三席增加到八席。
