Title: National Master-Level Gymnastics Competition Begins
Author:
Date: 1958-04-24
Page: 7

Xinhua News Agency, April 22nd: The 1958 National Master-Level Gymnastics Championships began tonight at the Beijing Gymnasium.

　　The competition events for men include six compulsory exercises and six optional exercises in floor exercise, horizontal bar, parallel bars, rings, pommel horse, and vault. The competition events for women include four compulsory exercises and four optional exercises in floor exercise, uneven bars, balance beam, and vault. The compulsory exercises for both men's and women's competitions this time are quite difficult and are the master-level compulsory exercises announced by the State Physical Culture and Sports Commission this year, which are also the compulsory exercises for the 1958 World Gymnastics Championships.

　　This championship will last for four days. The compulsory exercises for men and women will be held on the 22nd and 23rd, and the optional exercises will be held on the 24th and 26th.

　　Twelve well-known gymnasts, including Yang Jianmin, Wang Huize, Qi Yufang, and Lan Yalan, did not participate in this championship in order to prepare for the competition with the Russian men's and women's gymnastics teams that will be visiting our country soon.

　　Ninety-seven male and female gymnasts from fourteen units, including Beijing, Tianjin, Shanghai, Jiangsu, Shandong, Liaoning, Sichuan, Heilongjiang, Hubei, Shanxi, Hebei, Guangdong, Inner Mongolia Autonomous Region, and the Air Force of the Chinese People's Liberation Army, are participating in this competition. Among them, except for one Master of Sport, the other ninety-six are all first-class athletes. They will strive to obtain the title of Master of Sport in this competition.

### 全国健将级体操赛开始

1958-04-24
第7版()
专栏：

    全国健将级体操赛开始
    新华社22日讯　1958年全国健将级体操锦标赛今晚在北京体育馆开始。
    这次比赛项目，男子有自由体操、单杠、双杠、吊环、鞍马、纵跳马等六项规定动作和六项自选动作。女子有自由体操、高低杠、平衡木、横跳马等四项规定动作和四项自选动作。这次男子和女子比赛的规定动作难度较大，都是体育运动委员会今年公布的健将级规定动作，也就是1958年世界体操锦标赛的规定动作。
    这次锦标赛将进行四天，22日和23日进行男子和女子规定动作比赛，24日和26日两天举行自选动作比赛。
    著名体操选手杨健民、王辉泽、戚玉芳、蓝亚兰等十二人，为了准备同日内即将来我国访问的俄罗斯男女体操队竞赛，他们都没有参加这次锦标赛。
    参加这次比赛的有来自北京、天津、上海、江苏、山东、辽宁、四川、黑龙江、湖北、山西、河北、广东、内蒙古自治区、中国人民解放军空军部队等十四个单位的九十七名男、女体操选手，其中除了一名运动健将外，其他九十六名都是一级运动员。他们将在这次比赛中争取获得运动健将的称号。
