Title: State Council Appointment and Removal List
Author:
Date: 1958-04-21
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, 19th – The 75th plenary meeting of the State Council held on April 11th passed a list of appointments and removals of personnel from foreign embassies and universities as follows:

　　Appointed Zheng Tuobin as Commercial Counselor of the Embassy of the People's Republic of China in the Union of Soviet Socialist Republics;
　　Li Fanru as Commercial Counselor of the Embassy of the People's Republic of China in the Democratic People's Republic of Korea.

　　Removed Ming Ke from the post of Commercial Counselor of the Embassy of the People's Republic of China in the Union of Soviet Socialist Republics;
　　Liu Ruoming from the post of Commercial Counselor of the Embassy of the People's Republic of China in the People's Republic of Poland;
　　Xue Chunpu from the post of Commercial Counselor of the Embassy of the People's Republic of China in the Democratic People's Republic of Korea;
　　Huo Shilian from the post of President of Zhejiang University.

### 国务院任免名单

1958-04-21
第4版()
专栏：

    国务院任免名单
    新华社19日讯　国务院4月11日举行的第七十五次全体会议通过任免一批驻外使馆、高等学校工作人员，名单如下：
    任命郑拓彬为中华人民共和国驻苏维埃社会主义共和国联盟大使馆商务参赞；
    李范如为中华人民共和国驻朝鲜民主主义人民共和国大使馆商务参赞。
    免去明克的中华人民共和国驻苏维埃社会主义共和国联盟大使馆商务参赞职务；
    刘若明的中华人民共和国驻波兰人民共和国大使馆商务参赞职务；
    薛春圃的中华人民共和国驻朝鲜民主主义人民共和国大使馆商务参赞职务；
    霍士廉的浙江大学校长职务。
