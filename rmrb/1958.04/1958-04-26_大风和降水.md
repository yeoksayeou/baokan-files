Title: Strong Winds and Precipitation
Author:
Date: 1958-04-26
Page: 2

Xinhua News Agency, April 25th. The Central Meteorological Research Institute released a forecast of strong winds and precipitation on the 25th:

    It is expected that in the next twenty-four hours, the entire Northeast region, central and eastern Inner Mongolia, Bohai Sea, and northern Yellow Sea will experience strong winds of force six to eight. There will be heavy rain in the Northeast, with some areas experiencing torrential rain. Eastern Inner Mongolia, northern and western Heilongjiang will have heavy snow. Northern Hebei, northern Shanxi, and northern Liaoning will experience frost.

### 大风和降水

1958-04-26
第2版()
专栏：气象台

    大风和降水
    据新华社25日讯　中央气象科学研究所于25日发布大风和降水预告：
    预计未来二十四小时内，东北全区、内蒙古中部和东部、渤海、黄海北部将有六——八级的大风。东北将有大雨，部分地区并有暴雨。内蒙古东部、黑龙江北部和西部将有大雪。河北北部、山西北部和辽宁北部将有霜冻。
