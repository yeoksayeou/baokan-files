Title: Romanian Government Delegation Leaves Our Country
Author:
Date: 1958-04-11
Page: 2

Xinhua News Agency, Shenyang, 10th -- The Romanian government delegation headed by Chairman Chivu Stoica concluded its visit to China and left Shenyang by plane at 8:30 this morning to visit the Democratic People's Republic of Korea.

Vice Minister of Foreign Affairs Li Dong-geon of the Korean Ministry of Foreign Affairs, who came to Shenyang to welcome the Romanian People's Republic government delegation, accompanied them.

A grand send-off ceremony was held at Dongta Airport. Du Zheheng, Governor of Liaoning Province, delivered a farewell speech, representing the people of the province in wishing the distinguished guests success in their future visit and wishing them good health and a safe journey. Then, Chairman Chivu Stoica delivered a farewell speech to the thousands of people who came to see them off (to be issued separately).

After Chairman Chivu Stoica arrived at the airport, accompanied by Du Zheheng, Governor of Liaoning Province, he reviewed the honor guard and shook hands with the Chinese and foreign people who came to see them off. He then walked to the front of the crowd and waved to them. The crowd waved hundreds of colored flags, and the sounds of gongs and drums and cheers rang out. Before boarding the plane, Chairman Chivu Stoica once again said goodbye to the heads of the provincial and municipal party, government, and military.

Those who went to the airport to see them off included Wang Zheng, Secretary of the Liaoning Provincial Committee of the Communist Party of China, Zhang Like, Deputy Mayor of Shenyang, Xu Shaofu, Secretary of the Shenyang Municipal Committee of the Communist Party of China, heads of the Shenyang garrison, and heads of provincial and municipal party and government organs, people's organizations, and democratic parties.

Vice Minister of Foreign Affairs Zeng Yongquan, Chinese Ambassador to Romania Ke Bonian, and Director of the Protocol Department of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs Yu Peiwen also went to the airport to see them off.

Also at the airport to see them off were Kim Yong-bin, Consul General of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea in Shenyang, and consulate staff.

### 罗政府代表团离我国

1958-04-11
第2版()
专栏：

    罗政府代表团离我国
    新华社沈阳10日电　以基伏·斯托伊卡主席为首的罗马尼亚政府代表团结束了在中国的访问，今天八时三十分乘飞机离开沈阳去朝鲜民主主义人民共和国访问。
    来沈阳迎接罗马尼亚人民共和国政府代表团的朝鲜外务省副相李东健陪同前往。
    在东塔机场举行了隆重的欢送仪式。辽宁省省长杜者蘅致欢送词，他代表全省人民预祝贵宾们在未来的访问中获得成就，并且祝贵宾们身体健康，一路平安。接着，基伏·斯托伊卡主席向千多名欢送的群众致告别词（另发）。
    基伏·斯托伊卡主席到达机场后，在辽宁省省长杜者蘅陪同下检阅了仪仗队，并且同前来欢送的中外人士一一握手告别，又走到欢送群众的面前招手致意。欢送群众挥动着数百面彩旗，锣鼓声和欢呼声响成一片。基伏·斯托伊卡主席登机以前，又再一次向省、市党政军负责人道别。
    到机场欢送的有，中共辽宁省委书记王铮，沈阳市副市长张力克，中共沈阳市委书记徐少甫，沈阳驻军首长和省市各党政机关、各人民团体、各民主党派负责人。
    我国外交部副部长曾涌泉，中国驻罗马尼亚大使柯柏年，外交部礼宾司司长俞沛文，也到机场欢送。
    到机场欢送的还有朝鲜驻沈阳总领事金龙彬和领事馆人员。
