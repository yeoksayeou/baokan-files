Title: State Council Appointments and Removals

Author:

Date: 1958-04-15
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency dispatch: The State Council, at its 74th plenary meeting held on April 4th, approved the appointment and removal of a number of personnel from state organs, universities and colleges, and embassies abroad. The list is as follows:

　　Appointed:

　　Tong Xiaopeng as Director of the Premier's Office of the State Council;

　　Zhang Yongxiang as Vice President of Zhejiang Medical College.

　　Removed:

　　Zhang Ce from the post of Deputy Secretary-General of the State Council;

　　Qi Yanming from the post of Director of the Premier's Office of the State Council, and Li Qi and Zhang Yan from the posts of Deputy Directors;

　　Xue Zizheng from the post of Deputy Director of the State Economic Commission;

　　Ye Ke from the post of Cultural Counselor of the Embassy of the People's Republic of China in the German Democratic Republic.

### 国务院任免名单

1958-04-15
第4版()
专栏：

    国务院任免名单
    新华社讯　国务院在4月4日举行的第七十四次全体会议通过任免一批国家机关、高等学校和驻外使馆工作人员，名单如下：
    任命童小鹏为国务院总理办公室主任；
    张永祥为浙江医学院副院长。
    免去张策的国务院副秘书长职务；
    齐燕铭的国务院总理办公室主任职务，李琦、张彦的副主任职务；
    薛子正的国家经济委员会副主任职务；
    叶克的中华人民共和国驻德意志民主共和国大使馆文化参赞职务。
