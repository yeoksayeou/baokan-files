Brief News

    Chinese Postal and Telecommunications Workers' Union Delegation Arrives in Tokyo
    Tokyo dispatch: The Chinese Postal and Telecommunications Workers' Union delegation of five, led by Xiang Minghua, Vice Chairman of the National Committee of the Chinese Postal and Telecommunications Workers' Union, arrived in Tokyo by plane on the evening of the 31st.

    China and Romania Sign This Year's Trade Agreement
    Bucharest dispatch: The 1958 goods exchange and payment agreement between China and Romania was signed in Bucharest on the 30th.
    The agreement stipulates that the variety of goods supplied to each other by the two countries this year will be greatly increased, and the trade volume will have a larger increase than last year.

    Czechoslovakia Builds Largest Synthetic Fiber Factory
    Prague dispatch: Czechoslovakia's largest synthetic fiber factory is under construction in Humenie (in the Rzeszów region) in eastern Slovakia. The factory will be put into production in the second half of next year, and is expected to produce 2,500 tons of Capron annually.

### 简讯

1958-04-01
第5版()
专栏：简讯

    简讯
    我邮电工会访日代表团抵东京
    东京讯：由中国邮电工会全国委员会副主席向明华率领的中国邮电工会访日代表团一行五人，在31日晚间乘飞机到达东京。
    中罗签订今年贸易协定
    布加勒斯特讯：中国和罗马尼亚两国1958年换货和付款协定30日在布加勒斯特签字。
    协定规定今年两国相互供应的货物的品种要大大增加，贸易额将比去年有较大的增长。
    捷兴建最大合成纤维工厂
    布拉格讯：捷克斯洛伐克一家最大的合成纤维工厂正在斯洛伐克东部的胡门尼厄（在鲁累索夫州）建设。这个工厂将在明年下半年投入生产，预计每年能够生产出两千五百吨卡普隆。
