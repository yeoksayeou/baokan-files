Title: New Milk Production Record for Dairy Cows (Picture)
Author: Xu Wen
Date: 1958-04-23
Page: 2

    New Milk Production Record for Dairy Cows
    On April 1st, "No. 8" dairy cow at the Jurong Agricultural Technical School Farm in Jiangsu Province produced 140 jin and 2 liang of milk, surpassing the Shanghai Dairy Company's "No. 400" dairy cow's daily output of 135 jin and a half, setting a new national record. The main reason for the farm's high milk production is feeding the cows more succulent fodder. The picture shows the herdsman Zhuang Guangshun milking the cow.
                                  Xinhua News Agency reporter Xu Wen

### 乳牛产奶新纪录（图片）
徐文
1958-04-23
第2版()
专栏：

    乳牛产奶新纪录
    江苏省句容农业技术学校农场“八号”乳牛4月1日产乳一百四十斤二两，超过上海乳牛公司“四百号”乳牛日产一百三十五斤半的乳量，创全国最高纪录。这个农场乳牛乳量高产的原因主要是多吃多汁食料。图为牧工庄广顺在挤奶。
                                  新华社记者  徐文摄
