Title: Brief News

Author:
Date: 1958-04-10
Page: 6

    Brief News

    "Soviet-British Association" Established in Moscow
    Moscow Dispatch: The "Soviet-British Association" was established in Moscow on the 8th. The famous Soviet writer Surkov was elected as the chairman of the association.

    Soviet Folk Dance Troupe to Perform in the United States
    Moscow Dispatch: The Soviet National Folk Dance Troupe, led by artistic director Moiseyev, left Moscow by plane on the 8th for a tour of the United States.

    Soviet Circus Performs in Damascus
    Damascus Dispatch: The Soviet Circus gave its first performance in Damascus on the evening of the 8th and achieved great success.

### 简讯

1958-04-10
第6版()
专栏：简讯

    简讯
    “苏英协会”在莫斯科成立
    莫斯科讯：“苏联—英国协会”8日在莫斯科成立。苏联著名作家苏尔科夫当选为协会主席。
    苏联民间舞蹈团赴美演出
    莫斯科讯：苏联国家民间舞蹈团由艺术指导莫伊谢耶夫率领，8日乘飞机离莫斯科前往美国进行巡回演出。
    苏联马戏团在大马士革演出
    大马士革讯：苏联马戏团8日晚间在大马士革举行了第一次演出，并且获得很大的成功。
