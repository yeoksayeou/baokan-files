Title: Indian-Chinese Friendship Association Delegation Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1958-04-29
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, April 28th: The ten-member delegation of the Indian-Chinese Friendship Association, led by the head of the delegation and Secretary-General of the Indian-Chinese Friendship Association, Brajesh Prasad, arrived in Beijing by plane today at the invitation of the Chinese-Indian Friendship Association for a visit to our country. Ding Xilin, President of the Chinese-Indian Friendship Association, and others went to the airport to welcome them.

### 印中友协代表团到京

1958-04-29
第4版()
专栏：

    印中友协代表团到京
    新华社28日讯　应中印友好协会邀请来我国访问的印中友协代表团一行十人，由团长、印中友协秘书长布腊姆·普腊卡什率领，已经在今天乘飞机到达北京。前往机场欢迎的有中印友好协会会长丁西林等。
