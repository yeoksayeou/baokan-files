    ·Amazing Facts·
    》The male blue bird-of-paradise hangs upside down on a branch, spreads its wings, and shows the beauty of its feathers to the opposite sex.
    》The hummingbird is the only bird that can hover in the air. It can even fly backwards.

### 奇妙的事实

1958-04-29
第8版()
专栏：

    ·奇妙的事实·
    》雄的蓝色极乐鸟会把自己倒悬在树枝上，张开翅膀，向异性显示自己羽毛的美丽。
    》峰鸟是唯一能在空中呆立不动的鸟，它甚至还会倒飞。
