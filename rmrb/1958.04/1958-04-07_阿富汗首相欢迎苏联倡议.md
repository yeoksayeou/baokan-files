Title: Afghan Prime Minister Welcomes Soviet Initiative
Author:
Date: 1958-04-07
Page: 5
Xinhua News Agency, Kabul, April 6th: According to a report from the Eastern News Agency, Afghan Prime Minister Daoud, in response to a question from an Eastern News Agency reporter on the 5th, stated that the Afghan government is pleased with the resolution passed by the Supreme Soviet of the Soviet Union regarding the unilateral cessation of atomic and nuclear weapons testing and considers it to be one of the important measures conducive to ensuring peace and easing international tensions.

Daoud also said: The Afghan government has always opposed the production, testing, and use of nuclear weapons.

### 阿富汗首相欢迎苏联倡议

1958-04-07
第5版()
专栏：

    阿富汗首相欢迎苏联倡议
    新华社6日讯　据塔斯社喀布尔6日讯：据东方通讯社报道，阿富汗首相达乌德5日在回答东方通讯社记者的问题的时候指出，阿富汗政府对苏联最高苏维埃通过关于苏联单方面停止试验原子武器和核武器的决议感到满意，并且认为这是有益于保证和平和缓和国际紧张局势的重要措施之一。
    达乌德还说：阿富汗政府一向反对生产、试验和使用核武器。
