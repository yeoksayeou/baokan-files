Title: NATO Defense Ministers Meeting Again Beats the Drum of "Nuclear Deterrence"; Dulles Asks Congress to Amend the US Atomic Energy Act
Author:
Date: 1958-04-19
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, April 18th: The North Atlantic Treaty Organization Defense Ministers' Meeting concluded a three-day meeting here on the 17th.

    A communiqué issued after the meeting reiterated that the "basic strategy" of the North Atlantic Treaty Organization is to establish a so-called "nuclear deterrent force," and it also emphasized the need to "gradually modernize the forces of the North Atlantic Treaty Organization." The communiqué also mentioned that the defense ministers also discussed the plan adopted at the Paris meeting last year to establish missile bases in Western Europe.

    News released after the meeting also revealed that the Defense Ministers' Meeting did not make a final decision on where to establish missile bases. NATO officials said that the United States is still negotiating with France, Turkey, Italy, and the Netherlands on the establishment of missile bases.

    Xinhua News Agency, April 18th: Washington dispatch: Dulles requested the US Joint Congressional Committee on Atomic Energy on the 17th to amend the 1954 US Atomic Energy Act in order to allow US "allies" to follow the United States in a nuclear arms race, and to implement the decision made at the North Atlantic Treaty Organization summit in December last year to stockpile nuclear warheads on the territory of the group's member states.

    Dulles also said that if the United States is unwilling to provide its "allies" with more nuclear knowledge, it may cause "serious consequences." They may pursue a policy of "neutrality or non-participation" in the group, thereby "greatly increasing the burden on the United States."

### 北大西洋集团国防部长会议  又在敲打“核威慑”的破锣  杜勒斯要求国会修改美国原子能法

1958-04-19
第5版()
专栏：

    北大西洋集团国防部长会议
    又在敲打“核威慑”的破锣
    杜勒斯要求国会修改美国原子能法
    据新华社18日讯　北大西洋集团国防部长会议17日在这里结束了一次历时三天的会议。
    会后所发表的一项公报重申北大西洋集团的“基本战略”是建立所谓“核威慑力量”，它同时强调必须“逐步把北大西洋公约组织的部队现代化”。公报还提到，国防部长们并且讨论去年巴黎会议通过的关于在西欧建立导弹基地的计划。
    会后透露的消息还表明，国防部长会议也没有就在哪里建立导弹基地的问题作出最后决定。北大西洋集团的官方人士说，目前美国还在同法国、土耳其、意大利和荷兰就建立导弹基地的问题进行谈判中。
    据新华社18日讯　华盛顿消息：杜勒斯17日在美国两院原子能联席小组委员会要求修改1954年的美国原子能法，以便使美国的“盟国”跟着美国进行核军备竞赛，实现去年12月北大西洋集团首脑会议关于在这个集团的成员国领土上储备核弹头的决定。
    杜勒斯还说，如果美国不愿意把核知识更充分地供给它的“盟国”，可能会引起“严重的后果”。它们有可能奉行“中立或者不参加”集团的政策，从而“使美国的负担大大加重”。
