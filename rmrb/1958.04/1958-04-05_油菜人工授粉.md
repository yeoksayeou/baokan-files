Title: Rapeseed Artificial Pollination
Author: Zhou Banwen
Date: 1958-04-05
Page: 2

    Rapeseed Artificial Pollination
    The Qianfeng Agricultural Cooperative in Wan'an Township, Shiqian County, Guizhou Province, has universally implemented artificial pollination on its 641 mu of rapeseed. Last year, the rapeseed that underwent artificial pollination yielded 12%-15% more than ordinary rapeseed. It is estimated that this year, the rapeseed implementing artificial pollination can reach a yield of 220 jin per mu (last year's average yield was 174 jin per mu). (Zhou Banwen)

### 油菜人工授粉
周班文
1958-04-05
第2版()
专栏：

    油菜人工授粉
    贵州石阡县万安乡前丰农业社种植的六百四十一亩油菜，普遍进行了人工授粉。去年他们进行人工授粉的油菜，比一般油菜增产12％—15％。据估计，今年实行人工授粉的油菜，单位面积产量可达到二百二十斤（去年平均每亩产量是一百七十四斤）。　（周班文）
