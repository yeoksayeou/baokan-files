Title: Japanese Agricultural Technology Delegation Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1958-04-16
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, April 15th - The Japanese Agricultural Technology Delegation, headed by delegation leader Tera Tada Kenichi, arrived in Beijing from Guangzhou this morning.  More than twenty people, including Xi Fengzhou, Deputy Secretary-General of the Chinese Society of Agronomy, greeted them at the station.

Today, Yang Xiandong, Director of the Chinese Society of Agronomy, met with the Japanese Agricultural Technology Delegation and hosted a banquet in their honor.

### 日本农业技术团到达北京

1958-04-16
第4版()
专栏：

    日本农业技术团到达北京
    新华社15日讯　以寺田填一团长为首的日本农业技术团一行三十五人，于今天早晨由广州抵达北京。中国农学会副秘书长席凤洲等二十多人前往车站欢迎。
    今天，中国农学会理事长杨显东接见了日本农业技术团，并设宴招待。
