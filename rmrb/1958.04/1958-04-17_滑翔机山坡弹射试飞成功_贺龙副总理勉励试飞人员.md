Title: Glider Hillside Catapult Flight Test Successful; Vice Premier He Long Encourages Test Pilots
Author:
Date: 1958-04-17
Page: 7

Glider Hillside Catapult Flight Test Successful
Vice Premier He Long Encourages Test Pilots

(Our dispatch) The Aviation Cadre Training Class of the All-China National Defense Sports Association successfully conducted a glider hillside catapult flight test on April 15 in Zhangjiakou.

Because of thorough research and preparation beforehand, both test flights went very well. The first catapult resulted in a 180-degree spot landing on the hillside. After the second catapult, pilot Wu Yingcheng used the updraft dynamic lift to fly in the air for an hour, then accurately glided to Yulin Airport and landed according to plan.

Vice Premier He Long, Vice Director Lin Kai of the All-China National Defense Sports Association, and others personally visited and observed. After the successful test flight, Vice Premier He Long met with test pilots Sun Kejin, Wu Yingcheng, and the comrades leading this experiment, giving them encouragement and instructions.

Hillside catapulting is an innovation in our country. At the same time, in the process of glider training, it does not require the consumption of airplanes or winch vehicles to tow the glider, nor does it occupy arable land. Therefore, it is an effective method of achieving more with less in the gliding cause.

### 滑翔机山坡弹射试飞成功  贺龙副总理勉励试飞人员

1958-04-17
第7版()
专栏：

    滑翔机山坡弹射试飞成功
    贺龙副总理勉励试飞人员
    本报讯　中国人民国防体育协会航空干部训练班于4月15日在张家口进行滑翔机山坡弹射试飞成功。
    由于事先经过了充分的研究和准备，所以两次试飞情况都很好。第一次弹射，完成了一百八十度定点着陆在山坡上；第二次弹射出去后，由驾驶员吴英诚利用迎风的动力上升气流，在空中飞翔达一小时之久，然后按照计划准确地滑飞至榆林机场降落。
    贺龙副总理、中国人民国防体育协会林恺副主任等都亲临参观。试飞成功后，贺龙副总理接见了试飞员孙克进、吴英诚和领导这项试验工作的同志，给以鼓励和指示。
    山坡弹射在我国是一项创举，同时它在滑翔训练的过程中，由于没有飞机、绞盘车牵引滑翔机所付出的消耗，也不占用可耕土地，因此是滑翔事业中少花钱多办事的一项有效办法。
