Title: Yan'an Circles Commemorate "April 8th" Martyrs
Author:
Date: 1958-04-09
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency, Yan'an, 8th - Representatives from all circles in Yan'an, more than two thousand people, swept the tombs of the "April 8th" martyrs today to commemorate the twelfth anniversary of the martyrdom of Wang Ruofei, Qin Bangxian, Deng Fa, Ye Ting, Huang Qisheng and other martyrs.

At 11 a.m., the memorial ceremony began, presided over by Bai Zhiming, member of the Shaanxi Provincial Committee of the Chinese Communist Party. After the band played mournful music, the presiding person and others offered wine, wreaths, and observed silence in front of the martyrs' tombs, and read a eulogy. Subsequently, all the representatives lined up and circled the tomb, and added soil to the martyrs' graves.

### 延安各界纪念“四八”烈士

1958-04-09
第4版()
专栏：

    延安各界纪念“四八”烈士
    新华社延安8日电　延安各界代表两千多人今天为“四八”烈士扫墓，纪念王若飞、秦邦宪、邓发、叶挺、黄齐生等烈士殉难十二周年。
    上午十一时，祭奠仪式开始，由中共陕西省委委员白志明主祭。乐队奏哀乐后，主祭人等在烈士陵前奠酒、献花圈、默念，并读祭文。随后，全体代表列队绕墓一周，并为烈士坟墓添土。
