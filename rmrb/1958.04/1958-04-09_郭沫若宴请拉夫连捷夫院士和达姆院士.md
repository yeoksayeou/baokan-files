Title: Guo Moruo Hosts Banquet for Academician Lavrentyev and Academician Tamm
Author:
Date: 1958-04-09
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, April 8th: Guo Moruo, President of the Chinese Academy of Sciences, hosted a banquet this evening to welcome Mikhail A. Lavrentyev, Vice President of the Soviet Academy of Sciences, Director of the Siberian Branch, and renowned Soviet mathematician and mechanic, and his wife, as well as Igor Yevgenyevich Tamm, the renowned Soviet theoretical physicist.

Present at the banquet were Zhang Jingfu, Zhu Kezhen, Wu Youxun, Vice Presidents of the Academy of Sciences, and several other prominent scientists.

Sudarikov, Cultural Counselor of the Soviet Embassy in China, was also invited to attend the banquet.

### 郭沫若宴请拉夫连捷夫院士和达姆院士

1958-04-09
第4版()
专栏：

    郭沫若宴请拉夫连捷夫院士和达姆院士
    新华社8日讯　中国科学院院长郭沫若今晚设宴欢迎苏联科学院副院长、西伯利亚总分院院长、著名的苏联数学、力学家米·阿·拉夫连捷夫院士及夫人，和著名的苏联理论物理学家依·叶·达姆院士。
    出席作陪的有科学院副院长张劲夫、竺可桢、吴有训和著名科学家多人。
    苏联驻华大使馆文化参赞苏达利柯夫也应邀出席了宴会。
