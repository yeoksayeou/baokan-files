Title: China's Largest Sea Salt Field - Nanbu Salt Field Irrigation Pond Salting

Author:
Date: 1958-04-26
Page: 3

Construction Blossoms

    China's Largest Sea Salt Field
    -- Nanbu Salt Field Irrigation Pond Salting
    (Our correspondent from Hangu, April 25th) The first phase of construction of the Nanbu Salt Field in the Changlu Salt Area, China's largest sea salt production base, was basically completed one year ahead of schedule on the 23rd and officially started irrigating the ponds for salt production. Construction of this salt field began at the end of 1956. The effective production area of the salt fields is more than 2,370,000 mu. When fully completed in 1959, it can produce 1.68 million tons of sea salt annually, with a maximum annual output of 3.5 million tons, more than double the planned output of the existing five salt fields in the Changlu Hangu Salt Area this year. The sea salt produced by this salt field each year can supply chemical fertilizer plants with more than 6.46 million tons of ammonium chloride chemical fertilizer. The completion of the Nanbu Salt Field provides a solid material foundation for the great development of China's chemical industry, especially the chemical fertilizer industry.

### 我国最大的海盐场——南堡盐场灌池晒盐

1958-04-26
第3版()
专栏：建设的花朵

    我国最大的海盐场
    ——南堡盐场灌池晒盐
    本报汉沽25日电　我国最大的海盐晒制场——长芦盐区南堡盐场第一期建筑工程，已在23日提前一年基本完成，正式灌池晒盐。这个盐场是在1956年底动工兴建的，盐田的有效生产面积二百三十七万多公亩，到1959年全部建成，每年可以生产海盐一百六十八万吨，最高年产量能达到三百五十万吨，比长芦汉沽盐区现有五个盐场今年的计划产量多一倍。这个盐场每年生产的海盐可供化肥厂制造六百四十六万多吨氯化铵化学肥料。南堡盐场的建成，给我国化学工业，特别是化学肥料工业的大发展，提供了充实的物质基础。
