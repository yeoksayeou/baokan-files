Title: Trade Union Delegations from the Soviet Union, France, and Eight Other Countries Arrive in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1958-04-29
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency, April 28th: At the invitation of the All-China Federation of Trade Unions, trade union delegations from nine more countries (or regions) arrived in Beijing on the 27th and 28th by train or plane to attend the May 1st International Labor Day celebrations. They are: the Soviet trade union delegation headed by Pikaliev, Chairman of the Central Committee of the Soviet Aviation and Defense Industry Trade Union; the French General Confederation of Labor delegation headed by Louis Saillant, Secretary of the French General Confederation of Labor; the Korean trade union delegation headed by Zhu Ren-shun, Chairman of the Central Committee of the Korean Chemical and Electrical Industry Workers' Trade Union; the delegation of the General Union of Workers of Black Africa headed by Palanse Pierre; the Burmese trade union delegation headed by U Ba Swe, Chairman of the Trade Union Congress (Burma); the Bulgarian trade union delegation headed by T. Madanov; the German trade union delegation headed by A. Nawi, Secretary of the Free German Trade Union Federation; and representatives from the Ceylon Federation of Trade Unions, Xin A. Pi. Wimalasiri, representatives from the Ceylon Labor Federation, Kai Pa. Nair, representatives from the Ceylon Independent Industrial and Commercial Workers' Union, Ke. An. Fernando, Chairman of the Ceylon Agricultural Trade Union, Su. A. Xi. Pereira, representatives from the Ceylon National Teachers' Union, Dang Xi. Ahapuarachige, Chairman of the All Ceylon Teachers' League, A. Sai. Kannangaratnam, Vice Chairman of the Ceylon Port and Dock Workers' Union, Wei. Arnoldis, representatives from the Central Cooperative Conference of Indonesian Government Employees, Makmur Sugen and two others, and representatives from the Indonesian Trade Union Federation, Basharudin.
　　
Dong Xin, Kang Yonghe, Xi Zhanyuan and others from the Secretariat of the All-China Federation of Trade Unions went to the railway station and airport to welcome them.

### 苏联、法国等九个工会代表团到京

1958-04-29
第4版()
专栏：

    苏联、法国等九个工会代表团到京
    新华社28日讯　应中华全国总工会邀请，27日、28日又有九个国家（或地区）的工会代表团分别乘火车或飞机到达北京，前来参加“五一”国际劳动节观礼。他们是：以苏联航空与国防工会中央委员会主席皮卡列夫为首的苏联工会代表团，以法国总工会书记路·治阿亚为首的法国总工会代表团，以朝鲜化学电气工业劳动者职业同盟中央委员会委员长朱仁舜为首的朝鲜工会代表团，以帕兰司·彼爱尔为首的黑非洲工人总联合会代表团，以工会大会（缅甸）主席吴巴瑞为首的缅甸工会代表团，以托·马达诺夫为首的保加利亚工会代表团，以自由德国工会联合会书记阿·挪维为首的德国工会代表团，以及锡兰工会联合会代表辛·阿·皮·威玛拉锡里，锡兰劳工联合会代表凯·帕·奈尔，锡兰独立工商工人工会代表科·安·费南多，锡兰农业工会主席苏·阿·西·佩雷拉，锡兰全国教师工会代表当·西·阿哈普阿拉契格，全锡兰教师同盟主席阿·赛·康纳加拉特南，锡兰港口码头工人工会副主席威·阿诺利斯，印度尼西亚政府公务员中央合作会议代表玛克穆尔·苏根等三人和印度尼西亚工会联合会代表巴沙鲁汀。
    分别前往车站和机场欢迎的有中华全国总工会书记处书记董昕、康永和、郗占元等。
