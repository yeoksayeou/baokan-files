    Brief News
    Tito to Lead Party and Government Delegation to Visit Poland
    Belgrade Dispatch: President Tito will lead a Yugoslav party and state delegation to Poland for a visit in late spring this year.
    Soviet Cultural Delegation to Visit UAR
    Moscow Dispatch: A Soviet cultural delegation headed by Soviet Minister of Culture Mikhailov, at the invitation of the National Guidance Department of the United Arab Republic, departed from here on the 2nd for a visit to the United Arab Republic.

    铁托将率党政  代表团访波
    贝尔格莱德讯：铁托总统将在今年春末率领南斯拉夫党和国家代表团到波兰进行访问。
    苏文化代表团赴阿联访问
    莫斯科讯：由苏联文化部长米哈伊洛夫率领的苏联文化工作者代表团，应阿拉伯联合共和国国家指导部的邀请，2日从这里动身前往阿拉伯联合共和国进行访问。
