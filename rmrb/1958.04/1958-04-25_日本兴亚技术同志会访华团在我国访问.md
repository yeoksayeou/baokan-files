Title: Japanese Kōa Technical Comrades Association Delegation Visits China
Author:
Date: 1958-04-25
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, April 24th: The Japanese Kōa Technical Comrades Association Delegation, invited by the Chinese Society of Electrical Engineering, left Beijing on the 24th and will return to their country via Shanghai and Guangzhou. The head of the delegation, Hidekata Aida, will also stay in Beijing for a short period.

This delegation arrived in China on March 20th. During their visit, they toured factories, mines, and schools in Dalian, Anshan, Shenyang, Fushun, Changchun, Harbin, and Beijing, and exchanged technical experiences with Chinese engineers and technicians in various locations. They also extensively exchanged views on the development of industrial and technological exchanges between the two countries.

### 日本兴亚技术同志会访华团在我国访问

1958-04-25
第4版()
专栏：

    日本兴亚技术同志会访华团在我国访问
    新华社24日讯　应中国电机工程学会邀请，来中国访问的日本兴亚技术同志会访华团，已于24日离京，将经上海、广州返国。代表团团长相田秀方还将在北京作短期逗留。
    这个代表团是3月20日到中国访问的，在访问期间，参观了大连、鞍山、沈阳、抚顺、长春、哈尔滨和北京等地的工矿、学校，并在各地同我国工程技术人员交流了技术经验，同时就发展两国工业技术交流问题广泛地交换了意见。
