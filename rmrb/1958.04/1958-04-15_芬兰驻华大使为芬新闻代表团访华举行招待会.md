Title: Finnish Ambassador to China Holds Reception for Finnish News Delegation's Visit

Author:
Date: 1958-04-15
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, April 14th: The Finnish Ambassador to China, Mr. G.J. Sundström, held a reception on the evening of the 13th for the Finnish journalists' delegation's visit to China. Invited to the reception were Deng Tuo, President of the All-China Journalists Association, Wang Yunsheng, Vice President, Huang Hua, Director of the Department of West European Affairs of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs, Xu Huang, Deputy Director of the Information Department, Wen Jize, Deputy Director of the Broadcasting Bureau, and members of the press in the capital.

### 芬兰驻华大使为芬新闻代表团访华举行招待会

1958-04-15
第4版()
专栏：

    芬兰驻华大使为芬新闻代表团访华举行招待会
    新华社14日讯　芬兰驻华大使孙士敦13日晚为芬兰新闻工作者代表团访问中国，举行招待会。应邀出席招待会的，有中华全国新闻工作者协会会长邓拓、副会长王芸生，外交部西欧司司长黄华、新闻司副司长徐晃，广播事业局副局长温济泽以及首都新闻界人士。
