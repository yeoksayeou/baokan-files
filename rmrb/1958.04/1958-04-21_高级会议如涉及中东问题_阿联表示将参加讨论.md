Title: If High-Level Conference Involves Middle East Issues, Arab United Republic Expresses Willingness to Participate in Discussions
Author:
Date: 1958-04-21
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, Cairo, April 19th: According to TASS, the Director of the Information Bureau of the Arab United Republic, Saad Afra, in response to a question from a Cairo Radio reporter regarding the possibility of the Arab United Republic participating in a high-level conference, stated that if the conference discusses issues involving the Middle East, then the Arab United Republic will send representatives to participate in the discussion of these issues. He pointed out that the era of holding meetings to resolve regional issues without the participation of representatives of the Middle Eastern people is over. The policy of dividing spheres of influence in the world and colonialist arguments such as filling "vacuums" are outdated.

### 高级会议如涉及中东问题  阿联表示将参加讨论

1958-04-21
第5版()
专栏：

    高级会议如涉及中东问题
    阿联表示将参加讨论
    据新华社20日讯　据塔斯社开罗19日讯：阿拉伯联合共和国新闻局局长萨德·阿弗拉在回答开罗电台记者提出的关于阿拉伯联合共和国参加高级会议的可能性问题时说，如果会议讨论涉及中东的问题，那么阿拉伯联合共和国将派代表参加这些问题的讨论。他指出，没有中东人民代表参加就开会解决这个地区问题的时代已经过去了。划分世界势力范围的政策和填补“真空”这类殖民主义论调已经过时了。
