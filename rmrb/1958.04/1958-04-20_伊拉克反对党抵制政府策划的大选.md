Title: Iraqi Opposition Parties Boycott Government-Orchestrated Elections
Author:
Date: 1958-04-20
Page: 3
    Xinhua News Agency, Cairo, 18th - According to the "People's Daily," the Iraqi people and opposition parties are boycotting the parliamentary elections announced by the Said government for May 5th, which violate the will of the people. The opposition parties' call for a boycott of the elections is having a significant effect.
    The newspaper pointed out that Said is engaging in contacts with various parties in an attempt to form a new parliament completely under his control, in order to extend the life of the Baghdad Pact.

### 伊拉克反对党抵制政府策划的大选

1958-04-20
第3版()
专栏：

    伊拉克反对党抵制政府策划的大选
    据新华社开罗18日电　据“人民报”报道，伊拉克人民和反对党正在抵制赛义德政府违反人民意志而宣布在5月5日举行的议会选举。反对党发出的抵制选举的呼吁产生很大效果。
    这家报纸指出，赛义德正在同各方面进行接触，企图组成一个完全置于他的控制之下的新议会，以便延长巴格达条约的寿命。
