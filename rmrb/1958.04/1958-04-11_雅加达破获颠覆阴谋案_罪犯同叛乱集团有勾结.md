Title: Jakarta Uncovers Subversion Plot: Criminals Colluded with Rebel Groups
Author:
Date: 1958-04-11
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, Jakarta, April 8th: TASS reported from Jakarta on the 8th that Indonesian state organs have uncovered a case of rebels plotting to create chaos in Jakarta and overthrow the Juanda cabinet. State organs have arrested a total of twelve people, including three senior officials and seven employees of foreign companies.

Documents confiscated by Indonesian state organs demonstrate the connections of these conspirators with rebels in North Sulawesi and Central Sumatra, as well as with bandits from the "Islamic State Movement."

### 雅加达破获颠覆阴谋案  罪犯同叛乱集团有勾结

1958-04-11
第5版()
专栏：

    雅加达破获颠覆阴谋案
    罪犯同叛乱集团有勾结
    新华社9日讯　据塔斯社雅加达8日讯：印度尼西亚国家机关破获了一起叛乱分子阴谋在雅加达制造混乱和推翻朱安达内阁的案件。国家机关一共逮捕了十二人，其中有三名高级官员和七名外国公司的雇员。
    被印度尼西亚国家机关没收的文件说明了这些阴谋分子同北苏拉威西和中苏门答腊的叛乱分子以及同“伊斯兰教国运动”的匪徒们的关系。
