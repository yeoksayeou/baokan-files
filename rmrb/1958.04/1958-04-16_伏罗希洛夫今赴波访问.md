Title: Voroshilov to Visit Poland Today
Author:
Date: 1958-04-16
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, 14th—TASS reported from Moscow on the 14th that Voroshilov, Chairman of the Presidium of the Supreme Soviet of the Soviet Union, will depart from Moscow on April 16th for a visit to Poland at the invitation of the Polish government.

Accompanying Voroshilov on his visit are: Yekaterina Furtseva, Deputy of the Supreme Soviet of the USSR, Member of the Presidium of the Central Committee of the CPSU, and Secretary of the Central Committee; Kirill Mazurov, Member of the Presidium of the Supreme Soviet of the USSR, Alternate Member of the Presidium of the Central Committee of the CPSU, and First Secretary of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of Belarus; Trofim Kirilenko, Chairman of the Credentials Committee of the Soviet Union of Nationalities of the Supreme Soviet of the USSR, and Secretary of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of Ukraine; Arvīds Pelše, Member of the Commission on Foreign Affairs of the Soviet of the Union of the Supreme Soviet of the USSR, and First secretary of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of Latvia; Vyacheslav Yelyutin, Minister of Higher Education of the USSR; Nikolai Patolichev, Deputy of the Supreme Soviet of the USSR, and First Deputy Minister of Foreign Affairs of the USSR; and Averky Aristov, Soviet Ambassador to Poland.

### 伏罗希洛夫今赴波访问

1958-04-16
第6版()
专栏：

    伏罗希洛夫今赴波访问
    新华社15日讯　据塔斯社莫斯科14日讯：苏联最高苏维埃主席团主席伏罗希洛夫应邀将在4月16日由莫斯科启程前往波兰访问。
    随同伏罗希洛夫前往访问的有：苏联最高苏维埃代表、苏共中央主席团委员、中央书记福尔采娃，苏联最高苏维埃主席团委员、苏共中央主席团候补委员、白俄罗斯共产党中央第一书记马祖罗夫，苏联最高苏维埃民族院资格审查委员会主席、乌克兰共产党中央委员会书记契尔文年科，苏联最高苏维埃联盟院外交委员会委员、拉脱维亚共产党中央委员会第一书记斯?奇库斯，苏联高等教育部长叶留金，苏联最高苏维埃代表、苏联外交部第一副部长帕托利切夫，苏联驻波兰大使阿勃拉西莫夫。
