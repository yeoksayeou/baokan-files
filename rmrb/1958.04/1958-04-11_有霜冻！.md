Title: Frost!
Author:
Date: 1958-04-11
Page: 3
Weather Forecast

    Frost!
    This newspaper reports that the Central Meteorological Science Research Institute issued a gale and frost warning at 8:00 PM on April 10th.
    It is expected that within the next thirty-six hours, there will be northwesterly winds of force six to seven in central and western Inner Mongolia, northern Shanxi, northern Hebei, the Bohai Sea, the Yellow Sea, and the Liaodong Peninsula. There will be northerly winds of about force six in central and southern Hebei, northern Shaanxi, central Shanxi, Shandong, and northeastern Henan. There will be northerly winds of force five to six in the Huai River basin and southern Shanxi.
    It is expected that on the morning of the 12th, there will be frost in northern and central Shanxi, northern and central Hebei, northern Shandong, and Liaoning. On the morning of the 13th, there will be frost in some areas of southern Shanxi, southern Hebei, southern Shandong, northern Henan, northern Anhui, and northern Jiangsu.

### 有霜冻！

1958-04-11
第3版()
专栏：气象台

    有霜冻！
    本报讯  4月10日下午8时中央气象科学研究所发布大风和霜冻预告。
    预计未来三十六小时内，内蒙古中部、西部，山西北部，河北北部，渤海、黄海，辽东半岛将有六、七级的西北风，河北中部、南部、陕西北部，山西中部，山东，河南东北部将有六级左右的偏北风，淮河流域，山西南部将有五、六级偏北风。
    预计12日早晨，山西北部、中部，河北北部、中部，山东北部，辽宁将有霜冻。13日早晨，山西南部，河北南部，山东南部，河南北部，安徽北部，江苏北部等地的部分地区将有霜冻。
