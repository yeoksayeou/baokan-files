Title: Soviet Trade Delegation Returns Home
Author:
Date: 1958-04-28
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, 27th: The Soviet government trade delegation headed by I.G. Kabanov, Minister of Foreign Trade of the Soviet Union, returned home by plane on the 27th.

　　My Minister of Foreign Trade Ye Ji Zhuang, Deputy Ministers Li Zheren and Jiang Ming, and Zhang Huadong, Commercial Counselor of our embassy in the Soviet Union, went to the airport to see them off.

　　Also at the airport to see them off were Antonov, Charge d'Affaires ad interim of the Soviet Embassy in China, and Yeremin, the Soviet Commercial Representative in China, and others.

### 苏联贸易代表团回国

1958-04-28
第1版()
专栏：

    苏联贸易代表团回国
    新华社27日讯　以苏联对外贸易部部长伊·格·卡巴诺夫为首的苏联政府贸易代表团于27日乘机回国。
    我对外贸易部部长叶季壮，副部长李哲人、江明，我国驻苏大使馆商务参赞张化东等前往机场送行。
    到机场送行的还有苏联驻华大使馆临时代办安东诺夫，苏联驻华商务代表叶烈敏等。
