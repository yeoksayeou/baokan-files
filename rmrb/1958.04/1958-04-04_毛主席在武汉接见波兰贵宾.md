Title: Chairman Mao Receives Polish Guests in Wuhan
Author:
Date: 1958-04-04
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Wuhan, April 3rd: Chairman Mao Zedong received Jaroszewicz, head of the Polish government delegation and Vice-Chairman of the Council of Ministers of the Polish People's Republic, and all members of the delegation in Wuhan on the evening of the 2nd. Present at the reception were Yang Yingjie, Deputy Director of the State Planning Commission of China, Wang Renzhong, First Secretary of the Hubei Provincial Committee of the Chinese Communist Party, and Liu Huinong, Mayor of Wuhan. The Polish Ambassador to China, Kirylok, was also present at the reception. (Attached picture)
Chairman Mao receives the Polish government delegation in Wuhan. The first person on the right is Vice-Chairman Jaroszewicz.

### 毛主席在武汉接见波兰贵宾

1958-04-04
第1版()
专栏：

    毛主席在武汉接见波兰贵宾
    新华社武汉3日电　毛泽东主席2日晚在武汉接见了波兰政府代表团团长、波兰人民共和国部长会议副主席雅罗谢维奇和代表团全体团员。接见时在座的，有我国国家计划委员会副主任杨英杰，中共湖北省委第一书记王任重和武汉市市长刘惠农。接见时，波兰驻我国大使基里洛克也在座。（附图片）
