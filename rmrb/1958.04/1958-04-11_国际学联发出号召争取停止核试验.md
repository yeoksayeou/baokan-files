Title: International Student Union Calls for Efforts to Stop Nuclear Testing
Author:
Date: 1958-04-11
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, Prague, April 10th: The Secretariat of the International Student Union recently wrote to various student organizations regarding students' activities in the struggle for peace and against preparations for atomic war.

The ISU letter welcomes the Soviet Union's decision to unilaterally cease nuclear testing and calls on student organizations to make further contributions to securing a halt to nuclear testing by all countries.

### 国际学联发出号召争取停止核试验

1958-04-11
第6版()
专栏：

    国际学联发出号召争取停止核试验
    新华社布拉格10日电　国际学生联合会书记处最近就学生在争取和平和反对准备原子战争中的活动问题写信给各个学生组织。
    国际学联的信欢迎苏联单方面停止核试验的决定，并要求各个学生组织对求得各国停止核试验作出进一步的贡献。
