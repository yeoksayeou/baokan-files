	Brief News
	Bulgarian Party and Government Delegation to Visit East Germany
		According to a report by the German News Agency: At the invitation of the Central Committee of the Socialist Unity Party of Germany (SED), and the government the German Democratic Republic, the Bulgarian Communist Party and the government of the People's Republic of Bulgaria will send a delegation to the German Democratic Republic this month for a friendly visit.
	Chinese Youth Delegation Visiting the United Arab Republic Hold a Get-Together with Students of Syrian University
		Damascus dispatch: The Chinese Youth Delegation held a grand get-together with students of Syrian University on the evening of the 5th. Nearly two thousand Syrian students, artists, and prominent figures from the cultural and educational fields attended the get-together held in the auditorium of Syrian University.
	Soviet Union Issues Gorky Commemorative Stamp
		According to TASS: The Soviet Union has issued a commemorative stamp to mark the 90th anniversary of Gorky's birth. There is only one stamp in this set.
		In the center of this colored photogravure forty kopeck stamp, there is a portrait of Gorky, with the writer's birth and death years "1868-1936" printed below.

### 简讯

1958-04-07
第5版()
专栏：简讯

    简讯
    保加利亚党政代表团将访问民主德国
    据德意志通讯社报道：应德国统一社会党中央委员会和德意志民主共和国政府的邀请，保加利亚共产党和保加利亚人民共和国政府将在本月分派代表团到德意志民主共和国进行友好访问。
    访问阿联的我青年代表团同叙利亚大学学生联欢
    大马士革讯：中国青年代表团在5日晚间同叙利亚大学学生举行了盛大的联欢会。参加在叙利亚大学礼堂中举行的联欢会的，有叙利亚学生、艺术家和文化教育界的著名人士近两千人。
    苏发行高尔基纪念邮票
    据塔斯社讯：苏联发行了纪念高尔基诞生九十周年的纪念邮票。这套邮票一共只有一种。
    在这枚彩色透印的四十戈比邮票的中心，印着高尔基的肖像，下面印有作家诞生和逝世的年份“1868—1936”的字样。
