Title: Where Do the Rebel Weapons Come From? “American” Provides the Answer
Author:
Date: 1958-04-10
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, April 9th, New York Dispatch: "American" magazine published a dispatch from Bukittinggi today, saying that the rebel groups in central Sumatra have obtained many American weapons. The author of the dispatch, Beech, said that he personally saw the weapons there and they were undoubtedly American weapons.

He said that some observers believe that the airdropping of these weapons to the rebels was done by Chennault, a former U.S. Air Force personnel operating from a base in Taiwan.

### 叛军武器何来？  “美国人”作了回答

1958-04-10
第5版()
专栏：

    叛军武器何来？
    “美国人”作了回答
    新华社9日讯　纽约消息：“美国人”杂志今天发表一篇武吉丁宜通讯说，中苏门答腊的叛乱集团获得很多美国武器。通讯作者比奇说，他亲眼看到那里的武器确实无误地是美国武器。
    他说，一些观察家认为，给叛军空投这些武器，是以台湾为活动基地的前美国空军人员陈纳德所干的事。
