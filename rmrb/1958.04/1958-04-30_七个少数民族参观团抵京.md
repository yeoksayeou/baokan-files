Title: Seven Minority Nationalities Visiting Delegations Arrive in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1958-04-30
Page: 2

Xinhua News Agency, April 29th. The seven minority nationalities visiting delegations participating in this year's "May 1st" International Labor Day celebrations have arrived in Beijing one after another. They come from Sichuan, Yunnan, Gansu, Guizhou, Guangdong, Hunan, and the Guangxi Zhuang Autonomous Region, including more than 30 ethnic groups such as Mongolian, Hui, Tibetan, Yi, Zhuang, Miao, and Yao, totaling more than 300 people.

### 七个少数民族参观团抵京

1958-04-30
第2版()
专栏：

    七个少数民族参观团抵京
    新华社29日讯　参加今年“五一”国际劳动节观礼的七个少数民族参观团，已先后抵京。他们来自四川、云南、甘肃、贵州、广东、湖南和广西僮族自治区，包括蒙古、回、藏、彝、僮、苗、瑶等三十多个民族成分，共三百余人。
