Title: Mitigating the Impact of the US Economic Crisis: Governor of Bank Indonesia Says to Find New Markets
Author:
Date: 1958-04-30
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, Jakarta, April 28th: Lukman Hakim, Governor of the Bank of Indonesia, said in a written reply to questions raised by Antara News Agency today that Indonesia has inevitably been affected by the US economic recession. However, the extent of this impact is not yet known.

He said that most of Indonesia's exports to the United States are rubber, followed by oil, and then local produce. Taking rubber as an example, if the average international market price of rubber falls by 10% in 1958, and the United States reduces its rubber imports from Indonesia by 10%, Indonesia's rubber exports in 1958 will decrease by about 500 million rupiah. If the US economic recession continues for some time and spreads to other regions, the impact on countries exporting raw materials will be even greater.

He said that in order to avoid the continued impact of the US economic recession, the only effort Indonesia can currently make is to sell export commodities to other countries and find new markets.

### 减少美国经济危机的影响  印度尼西亚银行行长表示要找新市场

1958-04-30
第6版()
专栏：

    减少美国经济危机的影响
    印度尼西亚银行行长表示要找新市场
    据新华社雅加达28日电　印度尼西亚银行行长卢克曼·哈基姆今天在就安塔拉通讯社提出的问题所作的书面答复中说，印度尼西亚不可避免地受到了美国经济衰退的影响。但是现在还不知道这种影响有多大。
    他说，印度尼西亚向美国出口的物资大部分是橡胶，其次是石油，再次是土产品。以橡胶为例，如果1958年国际市场的橡胶价格平均下降10％，再加上美国从印度尼西亚进口的橡胶减少10％，印度尼西亚在1958年的橡胶出口将会减少五亿盾左右。如果美国的经济衰退再持续一些时间，并且扩展到其他地区，那么对出口原料国家的影响将会更大。
    他说，为了避免美国经济衰退继续发生影响，印度尼西亚目前能够作的唯一的努力是向其他国家出售出口物资，并且寻找新市场。
