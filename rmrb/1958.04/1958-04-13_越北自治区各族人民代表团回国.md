Title: Delegation of People from All Ethnic Groups in the Viet Bac Autonomous Region Returns Home

Author:

Date: 1958-04-13
Page: 2

Xinhua News Agency, Nanning, April 12th. The delegation of people from all ethnic groups in the Viet Bac Autonomous Region of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam, after visiting Wuhan, Nanjing, Shanghai, Hangzhou, Guilin and other cities, returned home by special train from Nanning this morning.

    Wei Guoqing, Chairman of the People's Committee of the Guangxi Zhuang Autonomous Region, delivered a farewell speech at the station. He said: Your visit to attend the founding conference of the Guangxi Zhuang Autonomous Region and to visit various places has further enhanced the profound friendship between the people of China and Vietnam and given us great encouragement. On behalf of the people of all ethnic groups in Guangxi, I would like to express my gratitude to you once again. Wei Guoqing said that he firmly believed that the people of all ethnic groups in Vietnam would surely achieve even more brilliant achievements on the road to the great socialist advance, and would surely achieve complete victory in their unremitting struggle for the unification of their motherland.

    Next, the head of the delegation, Zhu Wenjin, delivered a reply. He said that we would certainly take your deep feelings for the people of Viet Bac and all the Vietnamese people, the good impressions of the establishment of the Guangxi Zhuang Autonomous Region and the complete success of the conference, and the brilliant achievements and rich experience you have gained in the Great Leap Forward, as the most precious gifts to the people of all ethnic groups in the Viet Bac Autonomous Region.

    Those who came to the station to see them off included Wu Jinnan, Secretary of the Guangxi Zhuang Autonomous Region Committee of the Communist Party of China, and Vice-Chairmen of the Autonomous Region, Qin Yingji, Mo Naiqun, and more than 300 people from all ethnic groups and all walks of life.

    Nguyen Mai, Acting Consul of the Vietnamese Consulate in Nanning, also went to the station to see them off.

### 越北自治区各族人民代表团回国

1958-04-13
第2版()
专栏：

    越北自治区各族人民代表团回国
    新华社南宁12日电　越南民主共和国越北自治区各族人民代表团，在武汉、南京、上海、杭州、桂林等城市参观访问后，在今天早晨从南宁乘专车回国。
    广西僮族自治区人民委员会主席韦国清在车站上致欢送词。他说：你们这次应邀来参加广西僮族自治区成立大会，并赴各地参观访问，进一步增进了中越两国人民的深厚友谊，给了我们很大的鼓舞。我谨代表广西各族人民再一次向你们表示感谢。韦国清说，我深信越南各族人民在向伟大的社会主义前进道路上，一定获得更加辉煌的成就；在为统一祖国的不懈斗争中一定得到完全的胜利。
    接着，代表团朱文晋团长致答词。他说，我们一定把你们对越北人民和全体越南人民的深厚感情，把广西僮族自治区成立的良好印象和大会的完满成功，把你们在大跃进中取得的辉煌成就和丰富经验，当作最珍贵的礼物，带给越北自治区各族人民。
    到车站欢送的还有中共广西僮族自治区委员会书记伍晋南，自治区副主席覃应机、莫乃群以及各族各界人民三百多人。
    越南驻南宁领事馆代领事阮梅也到车站送行。
