Title: Vice Chairman Jaroszewicz Presents a Tractor to Vice Premier Chen Yi
Author:
Date: 1958-04-08
Page: 4

    Vice Chairman Jaroszewicz Presents a Tractor to Vice Premier Chen Yi
    Xinhua News Agency, 7th April. Polish People's Republic Council of Ministers Vice Chairman Jaroszewicz, on behalf of the Polish government, today presented a Polish-made "Mazur" D-40 tractor to State Council Vice Premier Chen Yi as a gift to Chinese agriculture.
    Vice Chairman Jaroszewicz wrote to Vice Premier Chen Yi today saying: "On behalf of the government of the Polish People's Republic, I am honored to present to you, esteemed Vice Premier Comrade, a Polish-made 'Mazur' D-40 tractor as a gift to Chinese agriculture, which has made great achievements and has great tasks in the socialist construction of the countryside.
    Let this action be an expression of the friendship and respect of the Polish people towards you and towards the great people of your country.
    Most esteemed Vice Premier Comrade, please accept my highest regards to you."

### 雅罗谢维奇副主席赠陈毅副总理一台拖拉机

1958-04-08
第4版()
专栏：

    雅罗谢维奇副主席赠陈毅副总理一台拖拉机
    新华社7日讯　波兰人民共和国部长会议副主席雅罗谢维奇今天代表波兰政府将一台波兰生产的“玛祖尔”D—40型拖拉机赠送给国务院副总理陈毅，作为向中国农业的献礼。
    雅罗谢维奇副主席今天写信给陈毅副总理说：“我代表波兰人民共和国政府荣幸地将波兰生产的‘玛祖尔’D—40型的拖拉机一台赠送给您，敬爱的副总理同志，作为向中国农业的献礼，中国农业在农村社会主义建设中有着巨大成就和任务。
    让这一行动作为波兰人民向您和向贵国伟大人民的友好和尊敬的表示。
    最敬爱的副总理同志，请接受我对您的崇高敬礼。”
