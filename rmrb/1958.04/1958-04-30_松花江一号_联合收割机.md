Title: "Songhua River No. 1" Combine Harvester
Author:
Date: 1958-04-30
Page: 3

New Product

    "Songhua River No. 1" Combine Harvester
    The jointly owned public-private Harbin No. 2 Machinery Factory trial-produced a grain combine harvester on the 28th, named "Songhua River No. 1". This combine harvester has fifty-five horsepower, a harvesting width of nearly ten feet, and can harvest about fifteen acres of crops per hour. It consists of harvesting, threshing, and cleaning sections, and can harvest wheat and barley. With slight adjustments, it can also harvest soybeans. During the agricultural off-season, it can also be used as a power machine for power generation, rice milling, etc.

### “松花江一号”联合收割机

1958-04-30
第3版()
专栏：新产品

    “松花江一号”联合收割机
    公私合营哈尔滨第二机械厂28日试制出一台谷物联合收割机，命名为“松花江一号”。这台联合收割机有五十五马力，收割宽度将近一丈，每小时能收割庄稼十五亩左右。它由收割、脱谷、筛净几部分组成，能收割小麦、大麦，稍加调整还能收割大豆；农闲时，它还能作发电、碾米等动力机械用。
