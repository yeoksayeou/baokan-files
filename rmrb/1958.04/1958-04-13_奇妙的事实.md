    Curious Facts
    △The Indian "archerfish" can squirt water at insects hovering above the water, causing them to fall in and become its food.
    △The deep-sea anglerfish has a luminous antenna growing from its head, which it uses to lure other fish, then devours them with its large mouth. (Xi)

    奇妙的事实
    △印度的“射水鱼”能从水中向在水面上飞旋的昆虫喷水，使它掉进水里，充作自己的食物。
    △深海琵琶鱼头上长出发光的长角，用来引诱其它鱼类，然后用大嘴巴吞噬它们。（熙）
