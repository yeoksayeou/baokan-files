    ·Wonderful Facts·
    △ There is a hotel in France that never sees sunlight throughout the year. It is located on a cliff 210 meters high behind a waterfall. On sunny days, beautiful rainbows often shimmer in front of the windows, forming a spectacular sight.
    △ In the tomb of Queen Hatshepsut of Egypt, the oldest bread was found. Its "age" has reached more than 3,500 years.

### 奇妙的事实

1958-04-06
第4版()
专栏：

    ·奇妙的事实·
    △法国有一家终年照不到太阳光的旅馆，它位于瀑布后面高二百一十公尺的悬壁上。晴天则窗前经常闪耀着绮丽的采虹，蔚成奇观。
    △在埃及哈沙普苏脱皇后墓中，发现了一只最古老的面包。它的“年龄”已达三千五百岁以上。
