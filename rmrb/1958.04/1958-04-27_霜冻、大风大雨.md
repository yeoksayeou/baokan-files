Title: Frost, Gale, and Heavy Rain
Author:
Date: 1958-04-27
Page: 2

    Xinhua News Agency, 26th -- The Central Meteorological Research Institute issued a frost, gale, and heavy rain forecast at 12:00 noon on the 26th:

    It is expected that on the morning of the 27th, there will be frost in Gansu, northern Shaanxi, central and northern Shanxi, northern Hebei, and Liaoning. In the next 24 hours, there will be northerly winds of about force 6 in northern Xinjiang, Hubei, Hunan, and Jiangxi; there will be northerly winds of force 5-6 in Zhejiang, northern Guangxi, and northern Guangdong; there will be northerly winds of force 6-7 in the East China Sea; and there will be heavy rain in the southern part of the middle and lower reaches of the Yangtze River, with local areas experiencing torrential rain.

### 霜冻、大风大雨

1958-04-27
第2版()
专栏：

    霜冻、大风大雨
    新华社26日讯  中央气象科学研究所26日中午十二时发布霜冻大风及大雨预告：
    预计27日早晨，甘肃、陕西北部、山西中部北部、河北北部和辽宁，将有霜冻。未来二十四小时内，新疆北部、湖北、湖南、江西将有六级左右偏北风；浙江、广西北部、广东北部将有五、六级偏北风；东海将有六、七级偏北风；长江流域中下游的南部地区将有大雨，局部地区有暴雨。
