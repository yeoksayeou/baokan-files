Title: First Round of National Football League Concludes
Author:
Date: 1958-04-15
Page: 7
Sports Movement

    Xinhua News Agency, April 13th: The twenty-two matches of the first stage of the first round of the 1958 National Football League Division A have all concluded. The results of the final two matches held today in the Beijing and Tianjin competition zones are as follows: the Beijing football team and the "August 1st" team drew one to one in Beijing; in Tianjin, the Tianjin football team defeated the Chengdu Institute of Physical Education team with a score of eight to zero.
    In the first stage, a total of six matches were held in the Beijing and Tianjin competition zones. The wins and losses of the four Division A football teams participating in this competition zone are: the Beijing team and the Tianjin team both had one win and two draws in three matches, and the "August 1st" team had three draws in three matches. The Chengdu Institute of Physical Education team had one draw and two losses in three matches.
    The sixteen matches in the Shanghai and Nanjing competition zones, involving eight Division A football teams, concluded as early as the 10th. The wins and losses of each team are: the Shanghai Institute of Physical Education team had three wins and one loss in four matches; the China Red Flag Sports Association team, the Shenyang Military Region team, the China Railway Sports Association team, and the Shenyang Institute of Physical Education team all had two wins, one draw, and one loss in four matches; the Changchun team had one win, one draw, and two losses in four matches; the China First Machine Sports Association team had one win and three losses in four matches; the Nanjing Military Region team had one draw and three losses in four matches. In the twenty-two matches of the first stage, there were seven draws, and weak teams defeated strong teams in six matches.

### 全国足球赛第一回合结束

1958-04-15
第7版()
专栏：体育运动

    全国足球赛第一回合结束
    据新华社13日讯　1958年全国足球甲级队联赛第一循环第一阶段的二十二场比赛已经全部结束。今天在北京、天津竞赛区举行的最后两场比赛的结果是：北京足球队同“八一”队在北京以一比一踢成平局；在天津，天津足球队以八比○胜成都体育学院队。
    第一阶段北京、天津竞赛区共举行了六场比赛，参加这个竞赛区比赛的四个甲级足球队的胜负情况是：北京队和天津队都是三战一胜二平，“八一”队三战三平。成都体育学院队三战一平二负。
    有八个甲级足球队参加的第一阶段上海、南京竞赛区的十六场比赛早在10日结束，各队胜负情况是：上海体育学院队四战三胜一负；中国红旗体育协会队、沈阳部队队、中国火车头体育协会队、沈阳体育学院队都是四战二胜一平一负；长春队四战一胜一平二负；中国第一机械体育协会队四战一胜三负；南京部队队四战一平三负。在第一阶段的二十二场比赛中，双方踢成平局的有七场，弱队打败强队的有六场。
