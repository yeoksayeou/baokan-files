Title: Austrian President Schärf Welcomes Soviet Cessation of Nuclear Tests
Author: 
Date: 1958-04-05
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, April 4th, TASS, April 4th: At the request of a "Pravda" reporter, Austrian President Dr. Adolf Schärf expressed his personal opinion on the Soviet Union's unilateral decision to stop nuclear weapons testing.

Dr. Schärf said: Everyone should sincerely welcome steps to prevent the endless abuse of atomic science for military purposes and to prevent the situation from further developing in this threatening direction. The prerequisite for achieving this is not to take temporary measures, but to take permanent measures and to make such a firm decision: whenever possible, the desire for peace should be demonstrated as clearly as the Soviet Union has done.

### 奥地利总统谢尔夫  欢迎苏联停止核试验

1958-04-05
第5版()
专栏：

    奥地利总统谢尔夫
    欢迎苏联停止核试验
    据新华社4日讯据塔斯社4日讯：奥地利总统阿道夫·谢尔夫博士应“真理报”记者的要求，对苏联单方面停止核子武器试验的决定发表他个人的意见。
    谢尔夫博士说：每个人都应当竭诚欢迎阻挠无止境地滥用原子科学于军事目的和阻止事态进一步朝这个有威胁性的方向发展的步骤。做到这一地步的先决条件，不是采取暂时的，而是采取经常性的措施，并作出这样一种坚决的决定：只要一有可能就应继苏联之后同样明确地证明爱好和平的愿望。
