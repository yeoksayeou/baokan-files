Title: Indonesian Newspaper Reveals US Diplomat Funded Hussein's Rebellion
Author:
Date: 1958-04-04
Page: 6

    Indonesian Newspaper Reveals
    US Diplomat Funded Hussein's Rebellion
    Xinhua News Agency, Jakarta, April 3rd - "Dongxing Daily" reported today that when Cummings was the US ambassador to Indonesia, the US embassy provided 2.5 million rupiah to Indonesian rebels under Hussein, as expenses to enable Hussein to carry out separatist activities to put the Riau region of East Sumatra under his control. This news was revealed by Hussein's confidant.
    Hussein's confidant also said that Cummings had visited the Riau region twice for activities, one of which was immediately after the Masjumi Party branch meeting in the Riau region had just ended.

### 印度尼西亚报纸揭露  美外交官资助侯赛尼造反

1958-04-04
第6版()
专栏：

    印度尼西亚报纸揭露
    美外交官资助侯赛尼造反
    新华社雅加达3日电　“东星报”今天报道，当柯明斯任美国驻印度尼西亚大使期间，美国大使馆曾经向印度尼西亚叛乱分子侯赛尼提供二百五十万盾，作为使侯赛尼进行把东苏门答腊廖内地区置于他的控制之下的分裂活动的开支。这个消息是由侯赛尼的亲信透露的。
    侯赛尼的这个亲信还说，柯明斯曾经两次到廖内地区去活动，其中一次是在廖内地区的马斯友美党支部会议刚刚结束时去的。
