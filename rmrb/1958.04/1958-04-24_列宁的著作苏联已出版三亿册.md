Title: Lenin's Works in Soviet Union Have Been Published 300 Million Copies
Author:
Date: 1958-04-24
Page: 6

    Xinhua News Agency, Moscow: According to statistics from the Soviet Central Book Chamber, from November 7, 1917, to April 1 this year, the Soviet Union has published 300 million copies of Lenin's works in eighty-eight languages.
    The Central Book Chamber's statistics also indicate that in 1956 alone, a total of 331 works by Lenin were translated and published in countries around the world.

### 列宁的著作苏联已出版三亿册

1958-04-24
第6版()
专栏：

    列宁的著作苏联已出版三亿册
    据新华社23日讯　据塔斯社莫斯科讯：根据苏联中央书库的统计，从1917年11月7日到今年4月1日为止，苏联已经用八十八种语文出版了三亿册列宁的著作。
    中央书库的统计还表明，在1956年一年，世界各国总共翻译和出版了三百三十一种列宁的著作。
