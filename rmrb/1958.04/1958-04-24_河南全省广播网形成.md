Title: Formation of Henan Province-Wide Broadcasting Network
Author: Li Lianyou
Date: 1958-04-24
Page: 7

    Formation of Henan Province-Wide Broadcasting Network

    (Our report) The broadcasting undertaking in Henan Province is experiencing a great leap forward. Currently, 104 counties and 8 suburban districts in the province have established broadcasting stations, installing 65,000 loudspeakers, thereby forming a province-wide broadcasting network. Twenty-six counties and suburban districts in the province have achieved or basically achieved radio broadcasts reaching every commune, fulfilling the task stipulated in the National Agricultural Development Program ten years ahead of schedule. (Li Lianyou)

### 河南全省广播网形成
李连友
1958-04-24
第7版()
专栏：

    河南全省广播网形成
    本报讯　河南省的广播事业在大跃进。目前，全省已有一百零四个县和八个市郊区建立了广播站，安装了六万五千多只喇叭，已经形成全省规模的广播网。该省有二十六个县和市郊区达到或基本达到社社通广播，提前十年实现了农业发展纲要规定的任务。　（李连友）
