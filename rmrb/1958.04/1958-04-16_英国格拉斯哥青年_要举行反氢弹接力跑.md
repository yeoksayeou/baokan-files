Title: Glasgow Youth in Britain to Hold Anti-Hydrogen Bomb Relay Race
Author:
Date: 1958-04-16
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, London, April 15th: Some young people in Glasgow, Britain, have decided to hold an anti-hydrogen bomb relay race from northern Scotland to London. They plan to have the last person participating in the relay race arrive in London before the British Parliament's summer recess.

A committee composed of more than one hundred representatives from many youth groups held a meeting on this matter last weekend. They unanimously passed a resolution demanding that: Britain cease conducting hydrogen bomb tests; no rocket bases be established in Britain.

### 英国格拉斯哥青年  要举行反氢弹接力跑

1958-04-16
第5版()
专栏：

    英国格拉斯哥青年
    要举行反氢弹接力跑
    据新华社伦敦15日电　英国格拉斯哥的一些青年人决定举行一次从苏格兰北部到伦敦的反对氢弹的接力赛跑。他们计划使参加接力赛跑的最后一个人在英国议会夏季休会以前到达伦敦。
    由许多青年团体的一百多名代表组成的一个委员会上周末就这件事举行了会议，他们一致通过了一项决议，要求：英国不再继续进行氢弹试验；不在英国建立火箭基地。
