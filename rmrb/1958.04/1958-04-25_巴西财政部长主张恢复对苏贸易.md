Title: Brazilian Finance Minister Advocates Restoring Trade with the Soviet Union
Author:
Date: 1958-04-25
Page: 6
Xinhua News Agency, Buenos Aires, April 19th (TASS dispatch): According to newspaper reports, Brazilian Finance Minister Arckimim has once again advocated expanding the Brazilian coffee sales market. He advocates restoring trade relations with the Soviet Union.

    Speaking at the National Student Union, the Finance Minister emphasized, "If anyone now wants to propose selling Brazilian coffee to Russia, they would be doing Brazil a great service."

    "El Clarin" pointed out that Brazilian domestic opinion considers Arckimim's speech to be of "great significance."

### 巴西财政部长主张恢复对苏贸易

1958-04-25
第6版()
专栏：

    巴西财政部长主张恢复对苏贸易
    新华社讯　塔斯社布宜诺斯艾利斯19日讯：据报纸报道，巴西财政部长阿尔基明再次主张扩大巴西咖啡销售市场。他主张恢复同苏联的贸易关系。
    财政部长在全国学生联合会讲话时强调指出，“如果谁现在想提出把巴西咖啡卖给俄国的建议，谁就给巴西作了一件大事。”
    “号角报”指出，巴西国内认为阿尔基明的讲话“有重大意义”。
