Title: "Red Satellite Wreaks Havoc in Heaven" Premieres in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1958-04-05
Page: 7

    "Red Satellite Wreaks Havoc in Heaven" Premieres in Beijing
    The new play "Red Satellite Wreaks Havoc in Heaven," rehearsed by the Fourth Troupe of the China Peking Opera Theater, and full of romantic color, premiered in Beijing on the 3rd.
    This play emphasizes both martial and civil aspects, and also features traditional performance arts such as the "Fire Meteor."
                      (Xinhua News Agency)

### “红色卫星闹天宫”已在京公演

1958-04-05
第7版()
专栏：

    “红色卫星闹天宫”已在京公演
    中国京剧院四团排练的富有浪漫主义色彩的新戏“红色卫星闹天宫”3日在北京公演。
    这个戏文武并重，还有“火流星”等传统表演艺术。
                      （新华社）
