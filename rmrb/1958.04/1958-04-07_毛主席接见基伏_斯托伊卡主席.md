Title: Chairman Mao Receives Chairman Chivu Stoica
Author:
Date: 1958-04-07
Page: 1

    Xinhua News Agency, Wuhan, 6th. Chairman Mao Zedong received the Chairman of the Council of Ministers of Romania, Chivu Stoica, in Wuhan on the 5th. Also received were members of the government delegation of the Romanian People's Republic: Emil Bodnăraș, Vice-Chairman of the Council of Ministers and Minister of Transport and Post and Telecommunications; Avram Bunaciu, Minister of Foreign Affairs; and Teodor Rudenco, Romanian Ambassador to China.
    Present at the reception were: Premier Zhou Enlai, Wang Renzhong, First Secretary of the Hubei Provincial Committee of the Chinese Communist Party, Zhang Tixue, Governor of Hubei Province, Zeng Yongquan, Vice Minister of Foreign Affairs, Liu Huinong, Mayor of Wuhan, and Ke Bonian, Chinese Ambassador to Romania.

### 毛主席接见基伏·斯托伊卡主席

1958-04-07
第1版()
专栏：

    毛主席接见基伏·斯托伊卡主席
    新华社武汉6日电　毛泽东主席5日在武汉市接见了罗马尼亚部长会议主席基伏·斯托伊卡。同时被接见的有罗马尼亚人民共和国政府代表团团员、部长会议副主席兼交通和邮电部长埃米尔·波德纳拉希，外交部部长阿夫拉姆·布纳丘和罗马尼亚驻中国大使特奥多尔·鲁登科。
    接见时在座的有：周恩来总理，中共湖北省委第一书记王任重，湖北省省长张体学，外交部副部长曾涌泉，武汉市市长刘惠农，中国驻罗马尼亚大使柯柏年。
