Title: The Czechoslovak Government Issues a Statement Calling on the US and UK to Halt Nuclear Testing
Author:
Date: 1958-04-06
Page: 3
Xinhua News Agency, Prague, April 5th: The Czechoslovak News Agency reported on the 4th that the Czechoslovak government issued a statement saying that the entire population of Czechoslovakia joyfully welcomes the resolution of the Supreme Soviet of the Soviet Union on unilaterally ceasing the testing of atomic and hydrogen weapons.

The Czechoslovak government urgently calls on the governments of the United States and the United Kingdom to also cease the testing of atomic and hydrogen weapons.

The statement emphasizes that, in accordance with the wishes of the people of all countries around the world, governments of all countries (including large and small countries) have a responsibility to make the prohibition of nuclear weapons testing an obligation for all nations.

### 捷政府发表声明  呼吁美英停止核试验

1958-04-06
第3版()
专栏：

    捷政府发表声明
    呼吁美英停止核试验
    新华社布拉格5日电　据捷克斯洛伐克通讯社4日报道，捷克斯洛伐克政府发表声明说，捷克斯洛伐克全国人民兴高采烈地欢迎苏联最高苏维埃关于单方面停止试验原子武器和氢武器的决议。
    捷克斯洛伐克政府迫切地呼吁美国和英国政府也停止试验原子武器和氢武器。
    声明强调指出，本着全世界各国人民的愿望，各国（包括大国和小国）政府有责任促使禁止核武器试验成为一切国家的义务。
