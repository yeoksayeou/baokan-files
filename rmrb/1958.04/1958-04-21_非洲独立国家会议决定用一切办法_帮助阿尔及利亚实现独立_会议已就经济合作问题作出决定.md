Title: African Independent States Conference Decides to Use All Means to Help Algeria Achieve Independence Conference Has Made Decisions on Economic Cooperation Issues
Author:
Date: 1958-04-21
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, Accra, April 19th: According to a communique published here, the African Independent States Conference made some decisions on economic cooperation issues at today's plenary meeting and concluded discussions on cultural exchange issues. Economic cooperation and cultural exchange issues were the second and third items on the conference's agenda.

The conference's Economic Committee and Cultural Committee are scheduled to hold their first meetings this afternoon to draft resolutions on these issues.

The economic issues discussed included: the United Nations Economic Commission for Africa, the issue of permanent cooperation institutions, market issues, and intra-African transportation issues. The communique said that it might be recommended to establish an economic research committee to implement the recommendations submitted to the conference.

The Political Committee held a full-day meeting to complete the drafting of resolutions on the first item on the agenda. The first item on the agenda was to exchange views on issues such as foreign policy, the future of non-independent African countries, Algeria, racial issues, and measures to protect independent African countries.

Tunisian delegation head and Foreign Minister Mokaddem told the press that the conference had decided to use all means to help the Algerian people realize their legitimate aspirations for independence and freedom and demanded that France end the war in Algeria. He also condemned France for exerting various pressures on the Tunisian government.

### 非洲独立国家会议决定用一切办法  帮助阿尔及利亚实现独立  会议已就经济合作问题作出决定

1958-04-21
第5版()
专栏：

    非洲独立国家会议决定用一切办法
    帮助阿尔及利亚实现独立
    会议已就经济合作问题作出决定
    据新华社阿克拉19日电　据这里发表的公报说，非洲独立国家会议在今天的全体会议上就经济合作问题作出了一些决定，并且结束了关于文化交流问题的讨论。经济合作和文化交流问题是这次会议的第二项和第三项议程。
    会议的经济委员会和文化委员会准备在今天下午举行第一次会议，就这些问题草拟决议。
    讨论过的关于经济方面的问题中包括：联合国非洲经济委员会问题、合作常设机构问题、市场问题、非洲内部交通问题。公报说，可能建议成立经济研究委员会来执行向会议提出的各项建议。
    政治委员会举行了整天会议，以便完成草拟关于第一项议程的决议的工作。第一项议程是就外交政策、非洲未独立国家前途、阿尔及利亚、种族问题和保卫非洲独立国家措施等问题交换意见。
    突尼斯代表团团长莫卡德姆外交部长对报界说，会议已经决定用一切办法帮助阿尔及利亚人民实现他们争取独立和自由的合法愿望，要求法国结束阿尔及利亚战争。他还谴责法国对突尼斯政府施加种种压力。
