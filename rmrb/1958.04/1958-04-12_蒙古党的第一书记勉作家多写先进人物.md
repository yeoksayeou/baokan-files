Title: Mongolian Party First Secretary Encourages Writers to Write More About Advanced Figures
Author:
Date: 1958-04-12
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, Ulaanbaatar, April 10th: The First Secretary of the Mongolian People's Revolutionary Party, Damba, told Mongolian writers that writers should describe more of the new achievements and new creations in life in their works. Advanced figures who create wealth should become the protagonists in their works.

Damba made these remarks when he met with Mongolian writers in Ulaanbaatar, together with members of the Political Bureau of the Central Committee of the Mongolian People's Revolutionary Party.

Damba encouraged writers to go to factories, farms and pastoral areas to live and create, and to share joys and sorrows with the working people.

### 蒙古党的第一书记勉作家多写先进人物

1958-04-12
第5版()
专栏：

    蒙古党的第一书记勉作家多写先进人物
    据新华社乌兰巴托10日电  蒙古人民革命党中央委员会第一书记达姆巴对蒙古作家们说，作家应当在作品里多多描写生活中的新成就和新创造。创造财富的先进人物应当成为作品中的主人翁。
    达姆巴是在同蒙古人民革命党中央政治局委员们一起，在乌兰巴托会见蒙古作家时说这番话的。
    达姆巴勉励作家们到工厂、农场和牧区去生活和创作，和劳动人民同甘苦。
