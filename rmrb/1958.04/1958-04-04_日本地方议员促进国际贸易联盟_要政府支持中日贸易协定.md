Title: Japan Local Assembly Members’ League for Promoting International Trade Demands Government Support for Sino-Japanese Trade Agreement
Author:
Date: 1958-04-04
Page: 6
Xinhua News Agency, Tokyo, 3rd -- The Japan Local Assembly Members’ League for Promoting International Trade passed a resolution at its summit meeting, which concluded on the 2nd, demanding that the Japanese government express its support for the Fourth Sino-Japanese Trade Agreement as soon as possible and without conditions.
	Prior to this, the Tokyo Metropolitan Assembly Members’ League for Promoting Trade had submitted a request to Japanese Prime Minister Nobusuke Kishi on March 29th, demanding that the Japanese government express its support for the Fourth Sino-Japanese Trade Agreement as soon as possible. The request criticized the Japanese government for deliberately adopting a delaying attitude on the issue of supporting the Sino-Japanese trade agreement.

### 日本地方议员促进国际贸易联盟  要政府支持中日贸易协定

1958-04-04
第6版()
专栏：

    日本地方议员促进国际贸易联盟
    要政府支持中日贸易协定
    新华社3日讯　东京消息：日本地方议员促进国际贸易联盟在2日结束的首脑会议上通过一项决议，要求日本政府尽早无条件地表示支持第四次日中贸易协定。
    在这以前，东京都议员促进贸易联盟曾经在3月29日向日本首相岸信介提出一份要求书，要求日本政府尽早表示支持第四次日中贸易协定。要求书指责日本政府在支持日中贸易协定问题上故意采取拖延的态度。
