Title: Surabaya University Students Rally in Front of US Consulate to Protest US Aid to Rebel Groups
Author:
Date: 1958-04-01
Page: 6

    Xinhua News Agency, Jakarta, March 31st: Several hundred students from Surabaya, under the leadership of the Surabaya University Student Committee Against American Intervention, rallied in front of the US Consulate in Surabaya this morning. They delivered a protest letter to the US Consulate, protesting US aid to Indonesian rebel groups and demanding that Dulles retract his fallacious statements slandering the patriotic actions of Indonesian students.
    The committee also issued a declaration today, demanding that the Philippine government prohibit Indonesian rebels from operating in that country and demanding that the Philippine government expel them.
    It is reported that the "Ching Kwong Daily" of the Chiang Kai-shek Kuomintang clique and the signboards of some schools controlled by Chiang Kai-shek Kuomintang elements have been smeared off by angry Surabaya people.

### 泗水大学生在美领事馆前集会  抗议美国援助叛乱集团

1958-04-01
第6版()
专栏：

    泗水大学生在美领事馆前集会
    抗议美国援助叛乱集团
    据新华社雅加达31日电　泗水几百名大学生在泗水大学学生反对美国干涉委员会的领导下，今天上午在美国驻泗水领事馆前面集会，向美国领事馆递交了一份抗议书，抗议美国援助印度尼西亚的叛乱集团，并且要求杜勒斯收回他诬蔑印度尼西亚学生爱国行动的谬论。
    这个委员会今天还发表了一项宣言，要求菲律宾政府禁止印度尼西亚的叛乱分子在这个国家活动，并且要求菲律宾政府把他们赶走。
    据悉，蒋介石国民党集团的“清光日报”和一些为蒋介石国民党分子把持的学校的招牌被愤怒的泗水人民抹掉了。
