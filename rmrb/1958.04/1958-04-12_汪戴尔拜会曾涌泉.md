Title: Wang Dairo Visits Zeng Yongquan
Author:
Date: 1958-04-12
Page: 1

    Xinhua News Agency, April 11th: Wang Dairo, the newly appointed Ambassador Extraordinary and Plenipotentiary of the German Democratic Republic to China, and his wife arrived in Beijing by plane on the morning of April 11th.

    Wang Yutian, Director of the Department of Socialist Countries of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs, and Zhang Shijie, Deputy Director of the Protocol Department, were at the airport to welcome them. Mr. Pirbach, Chargé d'Affaires ad interim of the Embassy of the German Democratic Republic in China, and all the embassy staff also went to the airport to greet them.

    Chen Gaocheng, Chargé d'Affaires ad interim of the Embassy of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam in China, was also at the airport to welcome them.

    Xinhua News Agency, April 11th: Wang Dairo, the newly appointed Ambassador of the German Democratic Republic to China, paid a visit to Zeng Yongquan, Vice Minister of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs, on the afternoon of April 11th to discuss matters related to the presentation of his credentials.

    Present at the meeting were Wang Yutian, Director of the Department of Socialist Countries of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs, and Zhang Shijie, Deputy Director of the Protocol Department.

### 汪戴尔拜会曾涌泉

1958-04-12
第1版()
专栏：

    汪戴尔拜会曾涌泉
    新华社11日讯　德意志民主共和国新任驻华特命全权大使汪戴尔和他的夫人11日上午乘飞机到达北京。
    前往机场欢迎的有外交部社会主义国家司司长王雨田，礼宾司副司长张世杰。德意志民主共和国驻华大使馆临时代办皮尔巴赫和全体使馆人员也前往机场迎接。
    到机场欢迎的还有越南民主共和国驻华大使馆临时代办陈高诚。
    新华社11日讯　德意志民主共和国新任驻华大使汪戴尔11日下午拜会我外交部副部长曾涌泉，商谈递交国书事宜。
    在座的有外交部社会主义国家司司长王雨田和礼宾司副司长张世杰。
