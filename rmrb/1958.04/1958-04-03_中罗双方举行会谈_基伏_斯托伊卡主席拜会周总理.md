Title: Sino-Romanian Talks Held; Chairman Chivu Stoica Calls on Premier Zhou

Author:
Date: 1958-04-03
Page: 1

    Sino-Romanian Talks Held
    Chairman Chivu Stoica Calls on Premier Zhou
    Xinhua News Agency, April 2nd: The Chairman of the Council of Ministers of the Romanian People's Republic, Chivu Stoica, held talks with Premier Zhou Enlai this afternoon.
    Participating in the talks on the Romanian side were Emil Bodnăraș, Vice-Chairman of the Council of Ministers and Minister of Transport and Telecommunications; Avram Bunaciu, Minister of Foreign Affairs; and Teodor Rudenco, Romanian Ambassador to China.
    Participating in the talks on the Chinese side were Vice-Premier Chen Yi, Vice-Minister of Foreign Affairs Zeng Yongquan, Vice-Minister of Foreign Trade Li Zheren, Chinese Ambassador to Romania Ke Bonian, and Wang Yutian, Director of the Department of Socialist Countries of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs.
    Xinhua News Agency, April 2nd: The Chairman of the Council of Ministers of the Romanian People's Republic, Chivu Stoica, called on Premier Zhou Enlai this afternoon.
    Accompanying him were members of the Romanian government delegation: Emil Bodnăraș, Vice-Chairman of the Council of Ministers and Minister of Transport and Telecommunications; Avram Bunaciu, Minister of Foreign Affairs; and Teodor Rudenco, Romanian Ambassador to China.
    Present at the meeting were Vice-Premier Chen Yi, Vice-Minister of Foreign Affairs Zeng Yongquan, Vice-Minister of Foreign Trade Li Zheren, Chinese Ambassador to Romania Ke Bonian, Wang Yutian, Director of the Department of Socialist Countries of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs, and Yu Peiwen, Director of the Protocol Department. (Attached picture)
    Above: Chairman Chivu Stoica calls on Premier Zhou Enlai and holds talks. From left to right: Zhou Enlai, Chivu Stoica, Emil Bodnăraș, Avram Bunaciu. Photo by Păuşată Anghel (Xinhua News Agency draft)

### 中罗双方举行会谈  基伏·斯托伊卡主席拜会周总理

1958-04-03
第1版()
专栏：

    中罗双方举行会谈
    基伏·斯托伊卡主席拜会周总理
    新华社2日讯　罗马尼亚人民共和国部长会议主席基伏·斯托伊卡今天下午同周恩来总理举行了会谈。
    罗马尼亚方面参加会谈的，有部长会议副主席兼交通和邮电部长埃米尔·波德纳拉希，外交部长阿夫拉姆·布纳丘和罗马尼亚驻中国大使特奥多尔·鲁登科。
    中国方面参加会谈的，有陈毅副总理，外交部副部长曾涌泉，对外贸易部副部长李哲人，中国驻罗马尼亚大使柯柏年，外交部社会主义国家司司长王雨田。
    新华社2日讯　罗马尼亚人民共和国部长会议主席基伏·斯托伊卡今天下午拜会了周恩来总理。
    随同拜会的，有罗马尼亚政府代表团团员：部长会议副主席兼交通和邮电部长埃米尔·波德纳拉希，外交部长阿夫拉姆·布纳丘和罗马尼亚驻中国大使特奥多尔·鲁登科。
    拜会时在座的，有陈毅副总理，外交部副部长曾涌泉，对外贸易部副部长李哲人，中国驻罗马尼亚大使柯柏年，外交部社会主义国家司司长王雨田和礼宾司司长俞沛文。（附图片）
    上图：基伏·斯托伊卡主席拜会周恩来总理，并进行了会谈。自左至右：周恩来、基伏·斯托伊卡、埃米尔·波德纳拉希、阿夫拉姆·布纳丘。　珀萨特·安盖尔摄（新华社稿）
