Title: Current Transformer
Author: Zhu Ying
Date: 1958-04-08
Page: 3

Current Transformer

	China's first independently designed 220,000-volt ultra-high-voltage current transformer has been successfully trial-produced at the Shenyang Transformer Factory. This transformer can convert high-voltage, high-current electricity into low-current electricity, and can also isolate high voltage from low voltage, making it convenient for industrial electricity use. Its successful trial production, together with power transformers, high-voltage switches, and voltage transformers, forms a complete set of main equipment, marking a rapid improvement in China's motor manufacturing level.

	Xinhua News Agency reporter Zhu Ying (attached picture)

### 电流互感器
朱瑛
1958-04-08
第3版()
专栏：新产品

    电流互感器
    我国第一台自行设计的二十二万伏特超高压电流互感器，在沈阳变压器厂试制成功。这种互感器可以将高压的大电流变为小电流，也可以将高压与低压隔离，便于工业用电。它的试制成功，与电力变压器、高压开关、电压互感器一起组成一套完整的主要设备，标志着我国电机制造水平有迅速提高。
    新华社记者   朱瑛摄（附图片）
