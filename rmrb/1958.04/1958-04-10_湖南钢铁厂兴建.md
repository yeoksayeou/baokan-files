Title: Hunan Iron and Steel Plant Under Construction
Author: Chen Zhonghu
Date: 1958-04-10
Page: 3
Construction's Flowers

    Hunan Iron and Steel Plant Under Construction
    The Hunan Iron and Steel Plant, with an annual output of 600,000 tons, commenced construction at the end of March.
    The construction tasks for this factory this year are substantial. The civil engineering portion of the metal products factory must be fully completed, and some equipment must be installed in preparation for handover for production by April 1st of next year. The wire rod mill, 600 meters long and 30 meters wide, must also be put into production in the second quarter of next year. This year, a portion of prefabricated components and structural parts, as well as most of the auxiliary projects, such as machine repair, carpentry, and coal gasification stations, must be completed.
                                               (Chen Zhonghu)

### 湖南钢铁厂兴建
陈中虎
1958-04-10
第3版()
专栏：建设的花朵

    湖南钢铁厂兴建
    年产六十万吨的湖南钢铁厂已在3月底动工兴建。
    这个厂今年的建设任务很大，金属制品厂的土建部分要全部完成，还要安装一部分设备，以备明年4月1日交付生产。全长六百公尺、宽三十公尺的线材厂也要在明年第二季度投入生产，今年要完成一部分预制构件及结构部分，和大部分附属工程。如机修、木工、煤气发生站等。
                                               （陈中虎）
