Title: Premier Zhou Expresses at Hungarian Ambassador's Reception Support for Soviet Union's Cessation of Nuclear Testing; Firmly Believes the United States and Britain Should Adopt Similar Measures
Author:
Date: 1958-04-05
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, April 4th: Premier Zhou Enlai, in his speech at the Hungarian Liberation Thirteenth Anniversary reception tonight, emphasized that the Chinese people warmly support the Soviet Union's momentous initiative to cease nuclear weapons testing, and firmly believe that the United States and Britain should also adopt similar measures.

While addressing this issue, Premier Zhou also said: "This significant action by the Soviet Union demonstrates the Soviet people's peaceful intentions and their confidence in the forces of world peace, representing yet another major contribution to the cause of world peace and human progress." Premier Zhou affirmed: "The great cause of hundreds of millions of people around the world safeguarding peace is bound to be victorious."

Premier Zhou's solemn statement garnered prolonged and enthusiastic applause at the grand reception attended by over four hundred Chinese and foreign guests.

### 周总理在匈牙利大使的招待会上表示  支持苏联停止核试验  坚决认为美国英国应采取同样措施

1958-04-05
第1版()
专栏：

    周总理在匈牙利大使的招待会上表示
    支持苏联停止核试验
    坚决认为美国英国应采取同样措施
    新华社4日讯　周恩来总理今晚在匈牙利解放十三周年招待会上的讲话中强调指出，中国人民热烈支持苏联决定停止核武器试验的重大创举，并且坚决认为美国和英国也应该采取同样的措施。
    周总理在谈到这个问题时还说：“苏联的这一重大行动显示了苏联人民的和平意志，显示了苏联人民对世界和平力量的信心，这对于世界和平和人类进步的事业是又一个重大的贡献。”周总理肯定地认为：“世界亿万人民维护和平的伟大事业是一定要胜利的。”
    周总理的这一庄严的表示，在有四百多位中外人士参加的盛大招待会上，博得了长时间的热烈掌声。
