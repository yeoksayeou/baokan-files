Title: Central Committee Leaders of the Chinese Communist Party Receive All Representatives of the Youth Congress
Author:
Date: 1958-04-14
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, April 13th: Comrades Liu Shaoqi, Zhou Enlai, Chen Yun, Deng Xiaoping, Lin Boqu, Dong Biwu, Peng Zhen, and He Long, members of the Political Bureau of the Central Committee of the Chinese Communist Party, and Comrade Lu Dingyi, alternate member, received all the representatives attending the Third National Congress of the All-China Youth Federation at 4:00 PM today and took a group photo with the representatives.

(Attached picture)
Comrades Liu Shaoqi and Zhou Enlai shaking hands with the representatives one by one. Xinhua News Agency reporter Zou Jiandong took the photo.

### 中共中央领导人接见青年代表大会全体代表

1958-04-14
第1版()
专栏：

    中共中央领导人接见青年代表大会全体代表
    新华社13日讯　中共中央政治局委员刘少奇、周恩来、陈云、邓小平、林伯渠、董必武、彭真、贺龙和候补委员陆定一，今日下午四时接见了出席中华全国青年第三次代表大会的全体代表，并和代表们一起合影。
