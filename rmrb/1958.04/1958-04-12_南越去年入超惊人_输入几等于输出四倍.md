Title: South Vietnam's Imports Exceeded Exports Hugely Last Year - Imports Nearly Four Times Exports
Author:
Date: 1958-04-12
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, Hanoi, April 10th. According to Saigon newspapers, a report released by the South Vietnam Import and Export Association reveals that South Vietnam's imports in 1957 were almost four times its exports. The total value of imports reached over 10 billion piastres, exceeding 1956 by 2 billion. The total value of exports was only a little over 2.7 billion piastres.

The report stated that 90% of the imports were U.S. "aid" materials provided in the form of "counterpart funds." These materials are currently the most difficult to sell in the South Vietnamese market.

### 南越去年入超惊人  输入几等于输出四倍

1958-04-12
第6版()
专栏：

    南越去年入超惊人
    输入几等于输出四倍
    新华社河内10日电　据西贡报纸消息，南越进出口工会发表的一个报告透露，南越在1957年的输入额几乎等于输出额的四倍。输入总值达一百亿皮阿斯特以上，比1956年多二十亿。输出总值只有二十七亿皮阿斯特多一点。
    消息说，90％的输入是以“对等基金”形式提供的美“援”物资。这些物资目前在南越市场上最没有销路。
