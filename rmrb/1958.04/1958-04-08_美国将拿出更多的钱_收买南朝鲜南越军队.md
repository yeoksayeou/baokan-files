Title: The United States Will Provide More Money to Buy Off the South Korean and South Vietnamese Armies
Author: 
Date: 1958-04-08
Page: 6

    Xinhua News Agency, 7th dispatch from Washington: The U.S. House Foreign Affairs Committee revealed on the 5th that the United States plans to give the South Korean Syngman Rhee clique $240 million in "military aid" in the next fiscal year, and the South Vietnamese Ngo Dinh Diem clique $194 million in "defense support and technical assistance" and $24 million in "direct military aid."

### 美国将拿出更多的钱  收买南朝鲜南越军队

1958-04-08
第6版()
专栏：

    美国将拿出更多的钱
    收买南朝鲜南越军队
    据新华社7日讯　华盛顿消息：美国众议院外交委员会5日透露，美国打算在下一个财政年度给南朝鲜李承晚集团二亿四千万美元的“军事援助”，给南越吴庭艳集团一亿九千四百万美元的“国防支援和技术援助”以及二千四百万美元的“直接军事援助”。
