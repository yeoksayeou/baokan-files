Title: Further Strengthening the Friendly Relations Between the Soviet Union and the United Arab Republic: The Soviet People Warmly Welcome Nasser
Author:
Date: 1958-04-30
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, April 29th: At the invitation of the Presidium of the Supreme Soviet of the Soviet Union, the President of the United Arab Republic, Nasser, arrived in Moscow at 11:35 this morning (Moscow time) aboard a "Tu-104" special plane escorted by nine jet fighters.

The members of the delegation accompanying President Nasser to the Soviet Union included: Vice Presidents Boghdadi and Hourani, Foreign Minister Fawzi, Education Minister Hussein, Minister of State for Presidential Affairs Sabri, and Minister of Urban and Rural Affairs of the Syrian Region, Karim. The Soviet Ambassador to the United Arab Republic, Kiselev, accompanied Nasser on the same flight to Moscow.

Welcoming President Nasser at the airport were Soviet party and government leaders Voroshilov, Khrushchev, Kirichenko, Kozlov, Mukhitdinov, Kosygin, etc., Soviet government ministers, marshals, generals, as well as diplomatic envoys from various countries to the Soviet Union and hundreds of representatives from all walks of life in Moscow.

As President Nasser and his entourage stepped off the plane, applause and cheers from the welcoming crowd immediately resounded at the airport.

Subsequently, Voroshilov delivered a welcome speech. He first extended his greetings to the freedom-loving and heroic Arab people. He said that the Arab people had raised the banner of striving for freedom and independence and for the unity of the Arabs in the anti-imperialist struggle throughout the Arab East. The Soviet people clearly know that the peaceful foreign policy pursued by the government of the United Arab Republic is a great contribution to the cause of universal peace. The Soviet people are deeply pleased with the achievements of the people of the United Arab Republic in consolidating national independence and state sovereignty and in eradicating the consequences of colonialism.

Voroshilov expressed his firm belief that the profound friendship and sincere cooperative relations existing between the United Arab Republic and the Soviet Union will, after this visit, become even closer brotherly relations.

Nasser delivered a reply speech. He said that our people know that the Soviet people have made great efforts to strive for universal peace throughout the world. Our people know that the Soviet people have assisted the Arab countries, and we express our deep gratitude for this assistance.

Nasser said, I am deeply convinced that our visit will enable our two countries to understand each other better and further consolidate our relations. I believe that our people, together with your people, will make greater efforts to strive for universal peace and for the safe life of all peoples in the world.

Afterwards, President Nasser, accompanied by Soviet leaders, traveled by car to his residence in the Kremlin. On the way into the city towards the Kremlin, many people lined the streets to welcome the distinguished guests.

### 进一步加强苏联和阿联友好关系  苏联人民热烈欢迎纳赛尔

1958-04-30
第5版()
专栏：

    进一步加强苏联和阿联友好关系
    苏联人民热烈欢迎纳赛尔
    据新华社莫斯科29日电　应苏联最高苏维埃主席团邀请前来苏联访问的阿拉伯联合共和国总统纳赛尔，今天上午十一时三十五分（莫斯科时间）乘“图一一○四”专机，在九架喷气式歼击机护送下到达莫斯科。
    随同纳赛尔前往苏联访问的代表团成员包括：副总统波格达迪和胡拉尼，外交部长法齐，教育部长侯赛尼，负责总统事务的国务部长萨布里，叙利亚地区城乡事务部长卡里姆。苏联驻阿拉伯联合共和国大使基谢廖夫陪同纳赛尔同机前往莫斯科。
    到机场欢迎纳赛尔总统的有苏联党和政府领导人伏罗希洛夫、赫鲁晓夫、基里钦科、科兹洛夫、穆希金诺夫、柯西金等，苏联政府部长们、元帅们、将军们以及各国驻苏联的外交使节和莫斯科各界代表数百人。
    当纳赛尔总统和他的随行人员走下飞机时，机场上立刻响起了欢迎群众的鼓掌欢呼声。
    接着，伏罗希洛夫致欢迎词。他首先向爱好自由的、英勇的阿拉伯人民致意。他说，阿拉伯人民在整个阿拉伯东方举起了争取自由和独立、争取阿拉伯人在反帝国主义斗争中团结一致的旗帜。苏联人民很清楚地知道，阿拉伯联合共和国政府执行的和平外交政策是对普遍和平事业的巨大贡献。苏联人民为阿拉伯联合共和国人民在巩固民族独立和国家主权以及在铲除殖民主义后果方面所取得的成就感到深深的高兴。
    伏罗希洛夫表示深信，阿拉伯联合共和国和苏联之间现存的深厚友谊和真诚的合作关系在这一次访问以后，将成为更加密切的兄弟般的关系。
    纳赛尔致答词。他说，我国人民知道，苏联人民尽了很大的努力争取全世界的普遍和平。我国人民知道，苏联人民援助了阿拉伯各国人民，我们对这种援助表示深深的感谢。
    纳赛尔说，我深信，我们的访问将使我们两国更加了解，使我们的关系更进一步巩固。我相信，我国人民同贵国人民一道，将尽更大的努力争取普遍和平，争取全世界各国人民安全地生活。
    随后，纳赛尔总统在苏联领导人陪同下乘车前往克里姆林宫寓所。在进入市区驶往克里姆林宫的途中，街道两旁有许多群众夹道欢迎贵宾。
