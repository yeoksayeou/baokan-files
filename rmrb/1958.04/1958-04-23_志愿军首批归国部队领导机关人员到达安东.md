Title: The First Group of Returning Chinese People's Volunteer Army Leadership Arrives in Andong
Author:
Date: 1958-04-23
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Andong, 22nd: The train carrying the leadership of the first group of returning Chinese People's Volunteer Army troops triumphantly arrived at Andong Station at 6:10 PM. Amidst cheers, the welcoming crowd carried the returning heroes into the gathering venue. Workers and students from Andong City performed programs for the returning heroes, and the Chinese People's Volunteer Army song and dance troupe also gave a thank-you performance to their motherland compatriots. At 10:30 PM, the train carrying the returning heroes left Andong and headed towards the interior of the motherland.

### 志愿军首批归国部队领导机关人员到达安东

1958-04-23
第4版()
专栏：

    志愿军首批归国部队领导机关人员到达安东
    据新华社安东22日电　凯旋归国的中国人民志愿军首批归国部队领导机关人员乘坐的列车于下午六点十分驶进安东车站。在一片欢呼声中，欢迎的群众把归国英雄抬入联欢场地。安东市的职工和学生为归国英雄表演了节目，志愿军部队文工团也给祖国亲人们进行了答谢演出。晚十点三十分，载着归国英雄的列车离开安东开往祖国内地。
