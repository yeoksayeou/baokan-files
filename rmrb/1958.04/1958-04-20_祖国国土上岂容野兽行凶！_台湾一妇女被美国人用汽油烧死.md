Title: How Can Beasts Be Allowed to Run Wild on Our Motherland's Soil! Taiwanese Woman Burned to Death with Gasoline by American

Author:
Date: 1958-04-20
Page: 2

Xinhua News Agency, April 19th, Taipei Dispatch: On April 12th, a Taiwanese woman was burned alive in the residence of Thomas, a member of the U.S. Military Assistance Advisory Group in Taiwan. This is the second known case of Americans committing murder in Taiwan within the second week of April. The first case was when U.S. soldier Greenwell severely beat and injured Chiang Kai-shek's army Major Zhang Youde in Taipei City.

According to Taiwan newspapers, Chen Hua, a 32-year-old woman from Taichung City, worked as a maid in Thomas's residence in Culture New Village (an American residential area) in Taichung City. Before dawn on the 12th, she was tied to a sofa chair by the American, doused with gasoline, and burned alive. The body was charred black, her clothes and hair were burned off, all five fingers of her left hand were destroyed, and her face was so burned that it was unrecognizable. The death was gruesome.

### 祖国国土上岂容野兽行凶！  台湾一妇女被美国人用汽油烧死

1958-04-20
第2版()
专栏：

    祖国国土上岂容野兽行凶！
    台湾一妇女被美国人用汽油烧死
    新华社19日讯　台北消息：4月12日，一个台湾妇女在美国驻台湾军事援助顾问团人员汤姆斯的住宅中被活活烧死。这是4月份第二个星期内台湾发生的第二件被公众知道的美国人行凶案件。第一件是美军士兵格林威在台北市把蒋军少校张有德殴打重伤。
    据台湾报纸透露，台中市三十二岁的妇女陈花，在台中市文化新村（美国人住宅区）汤姆斯的住宅中充当女佣。12日天亮以前，她被美国人绑在沙发椅上，浇上汽油活活烧死。尸首烧得全身焦黑，衣服头发都被烧光，左手五指全毁，面部已烧焦到不能辨认，死状很惨。
