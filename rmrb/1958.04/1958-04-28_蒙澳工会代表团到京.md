Title: Mongolian and Australian Trade Union Delegations Arrive in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1958-04-28
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, April 26th: The Mongolian Trade Union Delegation, led by Alga, member of the Presidium of the Central Council of Trade Unions of the Mongolian People's Republic, and the Australian Trade Union Delegation, led by Chamberlain, Executive Member of the Australian Council of Trade Unions, arrived in Beijing by plane this afternoon. Both delegations were invited by the All-China Federation of Trade Unions to come to China to attend the "May Day" celebrations.

Zhu Xuefan, Vice-Chairman of the All-China Federation of Trade Unions, and Li Zaiwen, Secretary of the Secretariat, went to the airport to welcome them.

### 蒙澳工会代表团到京

1958-04-28
第1版()
专栏：

    蒙澳工会代表团到京
    新华社26日讯　以蒙古人民共和国工会中央理事会主席团委员阿尔格为首的蒙古工会代表团和以澳大利亚工会理事会执行委员张伯伦为首的澳大利亚工会代表团，分别在今天下午乘飞机到达北京。这两个代表团都是应中华全国总工会的邀请来我国参加“五一”节观礼的。
    中华全国总工会副主席朱学范、书记处书记栗再温分别到机场欢迎。
