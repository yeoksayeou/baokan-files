Title: Cambodian Prime Minister Welcomes Soviet Peace Proposals
Author:
Date: 1958-04-13
Page: 3

Xinhua News Agency, Phnom Penh, April 12th: According to a communique issued by the Cambodian Ministry of Foreign Affairs, Cambodian Prime Minister and Minister of Foreign Affairs, Penn Nouth, welcomed the Soviet Union's decision to suspend nuclear weapons testing.

After receiving the Soviet government's decision in this regard on April 7th, Prime Minister Penn Nouth told the Soviet Ambassador to Cambodia, Alexander Anikin, that the Royal Cambodian Government and people welcome all decisions and initiatives taken by all countries to ease international tensions and thus promote world peace.

### 柬埔寨首相欢迎苏联和平主张

1958-04-13
第3版()
专栏：

    柬埔寨首相欢迎苏联和平主张
    新华社金边12日电　根据柬埔寨外交部发表的公报，柬埔寨首相兼外交大臣宾努对苏联宣布停止核武器试验的决定表示欢迎。
    宾努首相在4月7日收到了苏联政府关于这方面的决定以后，曾经对苏联驻柬埔寨大使亚历山大·安尼金说，柬埔寨王国政府和人民对各国采取的为了和缓国际紧张局势从而促进世界和平的一切决定和倡议都表示欢迎。
