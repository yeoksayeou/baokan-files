Title: Romanian Council of Ministers Vice-Chairman Visits Vice-Premier Peng Dehuai
Author: 
Date: 1958-04-05
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, April 4th — Emil Bodnăraș, member of the Government Delegation of the Romanian People's Republic, Vice-Chairman of the Council of Ministers and Minister of Transport and Post and Telecommunications, paid a visit to Vice-Premier Marshal Peng Dehuai this afternoon.

Present at the meeting were General Huang Kecheng, Senior General Zhang Zongxun, Senior General Gan Siqi, and others.

### 罗部长会议副主席拜会彭德怀副总理

1958-04-05
第4版()
专栏：

    罗部长会议副主席拜会彭德怀副总理
    新华社4日讯　罗马尼亚人民共和国政府代表团团员、部长会议副主席兼交通和邮电部长埃米尔·波德纳拉希今日下午拜会了副总理彭德怀元帅。
    拜会时在座的有，黄克诚大将、张宗逊上将、甘泗淇上将等。
