Title: France Can No Longer Be America's Errand Boy - Professor Lavelle Expresses Indignation
Author:
Date: 1958-04-28
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, Paris, 26th (TASS dispatch) - "International Forum" weekly published an article by Professor Bernard Lavelle sharply criticizing the United States' policy toward France.

The author recalled that the United States "once forced France to continue the Indochina War in order to squeeze France out of South Vietnam." The Americans are now adopting the same tactics in North Africa.

Lavelle pointed out that the United States treats its "Atlantic allies," especially France, like errand boys. The Americans' "treacherous" actions "have become unbearable for us." He said, "France has had enough of being a vassal of the United States," and it must "gain political independence."

Lavelle condemned the foreign policy of the United States. He emphasized that the Americans' fabricated claim that the Soviet Union has aggressive intentions is the root cause of many errors in American diplomacy. He said that the Soviet Union has never wanted and will never want armed conflict. "Therefore, it is absurd to force France to spend 600 billion or 800 billion francs each year on the completely useless military expenditure of the North Atlantic Treaty Organization."

### 法国不能再做美国的小伙计  拉维尔?教授发出不平之鸣

1958-04-28
第6版()
专栏：

    法国不能再做美国的小伙计
    拉维尔?教授发出不平之鸣
    据新华社27日讯　据塔斯社巴黎26日讯：“国际论坛”周刊发表的伯纳·拉维尔?教授的一篇文章尖锐地批评了美国对法国的政策。
    作者回顾说，美国“曾经迫使法国继续进行印度支那战争，以便把法国从南越排挤出去”。美国人现在在北非又采取了同样的策略。
    拉维尔?指出，美国对待自己的“大西洋盟友”，特别是对待法国，就像对待小伙计一样。美国人“背信弃义”的行动“使我们再也受不住了”。他说，“法国当美国附庸的味道已经尝够了”，它必须“获得政治独立”。
    拉维尔?谴责美国的对外政策。他强调说，美国人无中生有地硬说苏联有侵略意图，是美国外交上许多错误的根源。他说，苏联从来不希望、现在也决不希望发生武装冲突。“因此，强迫法国每年花费六千亿或八千亿法郎用在完全无益的北大西洋公约组织的军费上，是很荒谬的”。
