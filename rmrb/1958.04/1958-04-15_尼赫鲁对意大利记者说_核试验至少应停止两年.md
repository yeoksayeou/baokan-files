Title: Nehru Tells Italian Journalist: Nuclear Tests Should Be Halted for at Least Two Years
Author:
Date: 1958-04-15
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, Rome, April 14th: Indian Prime Minister Nehru told a journalist from the Italian newspaper "La Notte" that nuclear weapons testing should be halted for at least two years. He also said that he supports the Polish proposal to establish a nuclear-free zone in Central Europe.

"La Notte" published its journalist's interview with Nehru on the 12th. In this interview, Nehru reiterated his above-mentioned attitude.

### 尼赫鲁对意大利记者说  核试验至少应停止两年

1958-04-15
第5版()
专栏：

    尼赫鲁对意大利记者说
    核试验至少应停止两年
    新华社14日讯　罗马消息：印度总理尼赫鲁对意大利“国家晚报”记者说，核武器试验至少应该停止两年。他还说，他赞成波兰提出的关于在中欧建立无原子武器区的计划。
    “国家晚报”在12日刊登了它的记者对尼赫鲁的访问记。在这篇访问记中，尼赫鲁重申了他的上述态度。
