Title: Yang Shengxiu breaks national record in 10km race walk
Author:
Date: 1958-04-07
Page: 7
Xinhua News Agency, Xi'an, 6th: Youth athlete of Grade One, Yang Shengxiu, broke the national record of 52 minutes 11.6 seconds for the 10km race walk, set by Guan Shaoying in 1957, with a time of 52 minutes 7.8 seconds at the track and field meet held at Xi'an Institute of Physical Education from April 3rd to 5th.

### 十公里竞走杨省修破全国纪录

1958-04-07
第7版()
专栏：体育新闻

    十公里竞走杨省修破全国纪录
    新华社西安6日电　青年一级运动员杨省修在西安体育学院4月3日至5日举行的田径运动会上，以五十二分七秒八的成绩，打破了1957年关绍英所创造的十公里竞走五十二分十一秒六的全国纪录。
