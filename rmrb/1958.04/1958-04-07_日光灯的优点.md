    Advantages of Fluorescent Lamps
    Fluorescent lamps have three advantages: First, they consume less electricity and emit more light. A 60-watt light bulb emits 506 lumens (note), while a 15-watt fluorescent lamp emits 510 lumens, consuming about one-quarter of the electricity of a 60-watt light bulb. Second, the light of a fluorescent lamp is similar to moonlight. Colors can be accurately distinguished under fluorescent light. Moreover, the light is soft and not dazzling. Third, fluorescent lamps have a long lifespan. A light bulb can be used for about 1,000 hours, while a fluorescent lamp can be used for 2,500 hours or more.
  First Commercial Department Transportation Electrical and Engineering Materials Trading Bureau
                          Zhang Ziqiang
    Note: Lumen is a unit of measurement for light quantity

### 日光灯的优点
张自强
1958-04-07
第3版()
专栏：消费者的话

    日光灯的优点
    日光灯有三个优点：一、耗电量小而发光量大。一只耗电量六十瓦的灯泡，所发的光有五百零六流明（注），而一只耗电量十五瓦的日光灯，所发的光就有五百一十流明，耗电量为六十瓦灯泡的四分之一左右。二、日光灯的光和月光相似。在日光灯下能准确辨别颜色。并且光线柔和，不眩目。三、日光灯使用期限长。一个电灯泡约可使用一千个小时，而日光灯管则能使用二千五百个小时以上。
  第一商业部交通电工器材贸易局
                          张自强
    注：流明系光量的计算单位
