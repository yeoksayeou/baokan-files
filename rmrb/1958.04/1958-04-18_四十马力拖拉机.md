Title: Forty Horsepower Tractor
Author:
Date: 1958-04-18
Page: 3

New Product

    The first forty-horsepower wheeled diesel tractor made in China was successfully trial-produced at the Tianjin Tractor Factory on the 10th.
    This tractor is the TT-240 model. All of the more than 3,000 parts on the tractor are made of domestic raw materials. The tractor is equipped with five forward gears and one reverse gear, with speeds ranging from 4.56 kilometers per hour to 12.95 kilometers per hour. Its maximum traction force is 1,400 kilograms, while the fuel consumption per horsepower per hour is only 220 grams. This tractor can cultivate 10 hectares of land every eight hours. It can be used not only for farmland operations such as plowing, harrowing, sowing, harvesting, inter-tillage, and pest control, but also as a power source to drive waterwheels, gin cotton, extract oil, generate electricity, transport goods, and build roads.

### 四十马力拖拉机

1958-04-18
第3版()
专栏：新产品

    四十马力拖拉机
    我国第一台四十马力轮胎式柴油拖拉机，10日在天津拖拉机制造厂试制成功。
    这种拖拉机为TT——二四○型，拖拉机上的三千多个零件全部是用国产原材料制成的。拖拉机上装有五种前进变速装置和一种后退变速装置，速度每小时四点五六公里到十二点九五公里。它的最大牵引力是一千四百公斤，而每马力每小时的耗油量只需二百二十克。这种拖拉机每八小时能耕地十公顷，不但能作耕地、耙地、播种、收割、中耕、治虫等农田作业，还可以用来作动力带水车、轧棉花、榨油、发电、运输、筑路等。
