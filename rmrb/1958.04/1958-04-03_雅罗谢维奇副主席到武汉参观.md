Title: Vice Chairman Jaroszewicz Visits Wuhan
Author:
Date: 1958-04-03
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency, Wuhan, April 2nd: The Polish government delegation headed by Vice Chairman Jaroszewicz arrived in Wuhan by special plane this morning from Hangzhou. Yang Yingjie, Deputy Director of the State Planning Commission of our country, accompanied them.

Welcoming the distinguished guests today were Zhang Tixue, Secretary of the Hubei Provincial Committee of the Chinese Communist Party and Governor of Hubei Province, Liu Huinong, Mayor of Wuhan, Li Erzhong, Secretary of the Wuhan Municipal Committee of the Chinese Communist Party, and Wu Shian, Major General of the garrison, among others.

In the evening, Zhang Tixue, Liu Huinong, and others accompanied the distinguished guests to dinner.

### 雅罗谢维奇副主席到武汉参观

1958-04-03
第4版()
专栏：

    雅罗谢维奇副主席到武汉参观
    新华社武汉2日电　以雅罗谢维奇副主席为首的波兰政府代表团，今天上午乘专机由杭州到达武汉。我国家计划委员会副主任杨英杰陪同前来。
    今天前来欢迎贵宾的，有中共湖北省委书记、湖北省省长张体学，武汉市市长刘惠农，中共武汉市委书记李尔重，驻军首长吴世安少将等。
    晚间，张体学、刘惠农等陪同贵宾共进晚餐。
