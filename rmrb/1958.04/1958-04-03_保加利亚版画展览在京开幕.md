Title: Bulgarian Print Exhibition Opens in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1958-04-03
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, 2nd. The Bulgarian Print Exhibition, held according to the Sino-Bulgarian Cultural Agreement, opened this morning at the Daoning Zhai in Beihai Park.

    Those who attended the opening ceremony included: Ding Xilin, Vice-Chairman of the Commission for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries; Liu Kaiqu and Ye Qianyu, Vice-Chairmen of the Chinese Artists Association; art circles in the capital; Professor Veselin Staykov, member of the Presidium of the Bulgarian Artists Association, Honored Artist, and Dimitrov Prize winner, and Professor Dechko Uzunov, who are currently visiting China; Bulgarian Ambassador to China, Negalkov and his wife, and embassy staff; and diplomatic officials from various countries stationed in China.

    Professor Veselin Staykov introduced the achievements of Bulgarian printmaking art and the significance of this exhibition to the guests. He said that this exhibition will further promote and strengthen cultural exchanges between Bulgaria and China, two socialist brother countries.

    The exhibition includes 137 prints, including excellent old and modern Bulgarian print works. These works reflect the life, struggles, and labor of the Bulgarian people from many aspects with vivid artistic images, and depict the beautiful natural scenery of Bulgaria.

### 保加利亚版画展览在京开幕

1958-04-03
第4版()
专栏：

    保加利亚版画展览在京开幕
    新华社2日讯　根据中保文化协定举办的保加利亚版画展览今日上午在北海公园道宁斋开幕。
    在展览会开幕的时候，前来参加的有：对外文化联络委员会副主任丁西林，中国美术家协会副主席刘开渠、叶浅予，首都的美术界人士；正在我国访问的保加利亚美术家协会主席团委员、功勋艺术家、季米特洛夫奖金获得者维赛林·斯达耶柯夫教授和德奇科·乌祖诺夫教授；保加利亚驻华大使涅加尔科夫和夫人及使馆人员，各国驻华外交官员。
    维赛林·斯达耶柯夫教授在会上向来宾介绍了保加利亚版画艺术的成就和这次展出的意义。他说，这次展出将会进一步促进和加强保加利亚和中国这两个社会主义兄弟国家之间的文化交流工作。
    这次展出的版画共有一百三十七幅，包括有保加利亚古老的和现代的优秀版画作品。这些作品以鲜明的艺术形象从多方面反映了保加利亚人民的生活、斗争和劳动，以及描绘了保加利亚美丽大自然的风景。
