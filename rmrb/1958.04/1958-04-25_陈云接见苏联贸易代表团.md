Title: Chen Yun Receives Soviet Trade Delegation
Author: 
Date: 1958-04-25
Page: 1

    Xinhua News Agency, April 24th: Vice Premier Chen Yun received the entire Soviet government trade delegation, headed by Soviet Minister of Foreign Trade I.G. Kabanov, on the afternoon of the 23rd. Soviet Chargé d'affaires ad interim to China, Antonov, participated in the meeting.
    Also present at the meeting were Vice Minister of Foreign Trade Jiang Ming, and China's Commercial Counselor to the Soviet Union, Zhang Huadong, and others.

### 陈云接见苏联贸易代表团

1958-04-25
第1版()
专栏：

    陈云接见苏联贸易代表团
    新华社24日讯　陈云副总理在23日下午接见了以苏联对外贸易部部长伊·格·卡巴诺夫为首的苏联政府贸易代表团全体人员。苏联驻华临时代办安东诺夫参加了这次接见。
    接见时，对外贸易部副部长江明、我国驻苏联商务参赞张化东等人也在座。
