Title: Ceylon's "Daily Sun" Says People of All Countries Welcome the Soviet Decision
Author:
Date: 1958-04-07
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, Colombo, April 5th: According to TASS, the Ceylon newspaper "Daily Sun" published an editorial on the Soviet decision to unilaterally cease nuclear weapons testing. The editorial is titled "Nuclear Weapons Testing Must Be Stopped Immediately."

The newspaper writes that, regrettably, the United States is trying to diminish the significance of this bold Soviet decision. If the United States and Britain truly hope to eliminate the horror of nuclear war, then they should not adopt such a cold attitude towards the Soviet decision. The newspaper says that regardless of whether the United States and Britain stop nuclear weapons testing, it is clear that people all over the world welcome the Soviet decision. If other countries give due importance to the Soviet Union's sincerity for peace, then not only can testing be stopped, but an agreement can be reached on the issue of eliminating all stockpiles of nuclear weapons.

### 锡兰“每日太阳报”说  各国人民都欢迎苏联的决定

1958-04-07
第5版()
专栏：

    锡兰“每日太阳报”说
    各国人民都欢迎苏联的决定
    新华社6日讯　据塔斯社科伦坡5日讯：锡兰报纸“每日太阳报”的社论谈到苏联关于单方面停止试验核武器的决定。这个社论的标题是“必须立即停止核武器试验”。
    报纸写道，遗憾的是，美国力图缩小苏联的这一大胆决定的意义。如果美国和英国真正希望消除核战争的恐怖，那末它们就不应该对苏联的决定采取如此冷淡的态度。报纸说，不管美国和英国是否停止核武器试验，很明显的是，全世界人民都欢迎苏联的决定。如果其他国家对苏联的和平诚意给予应有的重视，那么就不仅可以停止试验，而且可以就取消核武器的一切储备问题达成协议。
