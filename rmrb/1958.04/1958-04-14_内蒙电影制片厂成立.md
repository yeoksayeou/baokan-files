Title: Inner Mongolia Film Studio Established
Author:
Date: 1958-04-14
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Hohhot, April 13th: The Inner Mongolia Film Studio was recently established in Hohhot. Mongolian and Han cinematographers will begin filming in mid-April.

    The main tasks of this film studio are to produce newsreels and dubbed films (translating Chinese films into Mongolian). This year, it will produce three medium-length documentaries and six Inner Mongolia briefings.

### 内蒙电影制片厂成立

1958-04-14
第1版()
专栏：

    内蒙电影制片厂成立
    据新华社呼和浩特13日电　内蒙古电影制片厂最近在呼和浩特市成立。蒙古族、汉族摄影人员在4月中旬就将开始拍摄工作。
    这个制片厂的主要任务是摄制新闻纪录片和翻译片（从汉语片翻成蒙古语片）。今年将要摄制三部中型纪录片和六本内蒙古简报。
