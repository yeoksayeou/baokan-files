Title: Joint Statement Issued by North Korean and Romanian Governments: American Troops Should Withdraw from South Korea as Soon as Possible; Romanian Government Delegation Arrives in Mongolia for a Visit
Author:
Date: 1958-04-14
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, Pyongyang, 12th - Premier of the Cabinet of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea, Kim Il-sung, and Chairman of the Council of Ministers of the Romanian People's Republic, Chivu Stoica, signed a joint statement between the two governments in Pyongyang at 7 p.m. today.

The statement said that during the visit of the Romanian People's Republic government delegation to Korea, the two government delegations held talks, and the two sides exchanged views on expanding and developing friendly cooperative relations between the two countries, strengthening the unity between socialist camp countries, and on the current international situation, and reached completely unanimous opinions.

The statement said that both sides fully support the Soviet government's proposal to convene a summit conference of major powers. Both sides warmly welcome the Soviet government's decision to unilaterally cease nuclear weapon testing and demand that the United States and the United Kingdom also adopt the same decision as soon as possible.

The statement said that the Romanian People's Republic government believes that the proactive measures taken by the Chinese and North Korean governments regarding the withdrawal of the Chinese People's Volunteers from Korea have opened up new realistic possibilities for the peaceful reunification of Korea and have made a significant contribution to easing tensions in the Far East. The statement said that the United States and other countries that have troops stationed in South Korea should withdraw their troops from Korea as soon as possible.

The statement said that the government of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea fully supports the initiative of the Romanian People's Republic government to convene a summit conference of Balkan countries.

The statement said that both sides once again emphasized the great significance of the Moscow Meeting and the Moscow Declaration, and stated that they will make every effort to further strengthen the unity of the socialist camp countries led by the Soviet Union in the future.

The statement emphasized that the legitimate status of the People's Republic of China in the United Nations should be restored. The statement said that both sides sympathize with and care for the Algerian people and the Asian and African people who oppose colonialism and fight for freedom and independence. Both sides condemned all attempts by foreign countries to interfere in Indonesia's internal affairs and support the heroic struggle of the Indonesian people to defend their national sovereignty.

Xinhua News Agency, Pyongyang, 13th - The government delegation of the Romanian People's Republic, led by Chairman of the Council of Ministers Chivu Stoica, left Pyongyang by plane this morning to visit the Mongolian People's Republic.

Xinhua News Agency, Ulan Bator, 13th - The Romanian government delegation, invited to visit Mongolia and led by Chairman of the Council of Ministers Chivu Stoica, arrived in Ulan Bator by plane this afternoon.

Mongolian party and government leaders Tsedenbal, Damba, Sambu, Tsende, Surenzhab, Baljinyam, Damdin, Dugersuren, and others, as well as diplomatic envoys from various countries to Mongolia, were at the airport to welcome the distinguished Romanian guests.

Tsedenbal accompanied Chivu Stoica in reviewing the guard of honor and then introduced him to the welcoming crowd.

When the Romanian government delegation entered the city by car, the citizens lined the streets and warmly welcomed the guests.

### 朝罗两国政府发表联合声明指出  美国军队应尽速撤出南朝鲜  罗政府代表团到达蒙古访问

1958-04-14
第5版()
专栏：

    朝罗两国政府发表联合声明指出
    美国军队应尽速撤出南朝鲜
    罗政府代表团到达蒙古访问
    据新华社平壤12日电　朝鲜民主主义人民共和国内阁首相金日成和罗马尼亚人民共和国部长会议主席基伏·斯托伊卡今天下午7时在平壤签署了两国政府的联合声明。
    声明说，在罗马尼亚人民共和国政府代表团访问朝鲜期间，两国政府代表团举行了会谈，双方就扩大和发展两国间的友好合作关系、加强社会主义阵营国家间的团结和关于目前国际形势等问题交换了意见，并且取得了完全一致的意见。
    声明说，双方完全支持苏联政府提出的关于召开大国首脑会议的建议。双方热烈欢迎苏联政府关于单方面停止试验核武器的决定，并且要求美国和英国也尽速通过同样的决定。
    声明说，罗马尼亚人民共和国政府认为，朝中两国政府关于从朝鲜撤出中国人民志愿军的主动措施，为和平统一朝鲜开拓了新的现实的可能性，并且对缓和远东紧张局势作出了重大贡献。声明说，美国和其他派有军队在南朝鲜的国家应当尽速从朝鲜撤出自己的军队。
    声明说，朝鲜民主主义人民共和国政府完全支持罗马尼亚人民共和国政府关于召开巴尔干半岛各国政府首脑会议的倡议。
    声明说，双方再一次强调莫斯科会议和莫斯科宣言的巨大意义，并且表示，今后要尽一切努力，进一步加强以苏联为首的社会主义阵营国家的团结。
    声明强调，应该恢复中华人民共和国在联合国的合法地位。声明说，双方同情和关怀反对殖民主义、为自由和独立而斗争的阿尔及利亚人民和亚非人民。双方谴责外国干涉印度尼西亚内政的一切企图，并且支持印度尼西亚人民保卫国家主权的英雄斗争。
    据新华社平壤13日电　以部长会议主席基伏·斯托伊卡为首的罗马尼亚人民共和国政府代表团今天早晨乘飞机离开平壤前往蒙古人民共和国访问。
    据新华社乌兰巴托13日电　应邀到蒙古访问的罗马尼亚政府代表团在部长会议主席基伏·斯托伊卡率领下，今天下午坐飞机到达乌兰巴托。
    到飞机场欢迎罗马尼亚贵宾的有蒙古党政领导人泽登巴尔、达姆巴、桑布、曾德、苏伦扎布、巴尔吉尼亚姆、达姆丁、杜格苏伦等人以及各国驻蒙古的外交使节。
    泽登巴尔陪同基伏·斯托伊卡检阅了仪仗队，随后介绍他同欢迎的人群见面。
    罗马尼亚政府代表团乘车进入市区时，市民夹道热烈欢迎客人。
