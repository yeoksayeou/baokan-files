Title: The Soviet Union Selflessly Promotes Peaceful Use of Atomic Energy and is Willing to Further Assist Underdeveloped Countries
Author:
Date: 1958-04-04
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, Vienna, March 3rd: According to TASS, the Deputy Representative of the Soviet Union to the International Atomic Energy Agency (IAEA), Zamyatin, announced on the 2nd that the Soviet Union is willing to provide further scientific and technological assistance to less developed countries within the IAEA.

Zamyatin handed a letter to the Director-General of the IAEA, Cole, stating that the Soviet Union is prepared, upon request, to send twenty to thirty experts on a temporary basis to member states of the IAEA to assist them in compiling scientific and technological plans for the peaceful use of atomic energy. The salaries of these experts will be fully borne by the Soviet government.

The Soviet Union is also willing to help member states of the IAEA train atomic energy experts. It is prepared to receive forty to forty-five university students from IAEA member states into the Soviet Union for five to six years of study in atomic energy related fields in the 1958-1959 academic year. It is also prepared to receive fifty scientists and experts from IAEA member states to the Soviet Union for advanced study lasting three to six months.

The Soviet government is willing to bear all expenses for the advanced studies in the Soviet Union of twenty-five students and twenty experts.

### 苏联和平利用原子能大公无私  愿意进一步援助不发达国家

1958-04-04
第5版()
专栏：

    苏联和平利用原子能大公无私
    愿意进一步援助不发达国家
    新华社3日讯　据塔斯社维也纳讯：苏联驻国际原子能机构副代表扎米阿金2日宣布，苏联愿意对国际原子能机构中不大发达的国家进行进一步的科学技术援助。
    扎米阿金交给国际原子能机构总干事柯尔一封信，这封信说，苏联准备应邀派遣二三十名专家临时到这个机构的成员国，帮助它们编制和平利用原子能的科学技术计划。这些专家的工资完全由苏联政府负担。
    苏联还愿意帮助这个机构的成员国培养原子能专家。它准备在1958—1959年学年中接收四十至四十五名大学生到苏联，从事五年到六年的原子能专业学习。它还准备接收国际原子能机构成员国的五十名科学家和专家到苏联晋修三个月到半年。
    苏联政府愿意负担其中二十五名学生和二十名专家在苏联深造的全部费用。
