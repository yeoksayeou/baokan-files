Title: Trade Union Representatives from Twenty-One Countries Arrive in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1958-04-30
Page: 2

    Trade Union Representatives from Twenty-One Countries Arrive in Beijing
    Xinhua News Agency, April 29th: At the invitation of the All-China Federation of Trade Unions, trade union delegations and trade union representatives from various countries who are coming to attend the May 1st International Labor Day celebrations have mostly arrived in Beijing.
    This year, forty-six trade union organizations from thirty-three countries in Asia, Africa, Europe, and Australia have been invited to participate in the May 1st International Labor Day celebrations in our country. As of April 29th, representatives from more than thirty trade union organizations from twenty-one countries have arrived in Beijing. Representatives of trade union organizations from the remaining twelve countries are also on their way to Beijing.
    The trade union representatives from various countries who arrived in our country earlier have visited factories, schools, and rural areas in Beijing and other places in the past few days, and have also visited some trade union organizations and workers. They are very interested in the situation of the all-round great leap forward in socialist construction in our country, which has left them with a deep impression.
    Xinhua News Agency, April 29th: At the invitation of the All-China Federation of Trade Unions, trade union delegations from seven more countries (regions) arrived in Beijing by train or plane on the 29th to participate in the May 1st International Labor Day celebrations in our country. They are the Indonesian Workers' Unified Delegation composed of the All-Indonesia Central Labor Union, the Central Labor Union of the Republic of Indonesia, the All-Indonesia Muslim Labor Union, the All-Indonesia Workers' Congress, the Central Cooperation Conference of Indonesian Government Civil Servants, and the Indonesian Foreign Investment Labor Union Cooperation Conference, represented by Kadis Markono and others; the Japanese Labor Delegation to Visit the People's Republic of China, headed by Makoto Ichikawa, Chairman of the All-Japan Garrison Forces Labor Union; the Moroccan Trade Union Delegation, headed by Bouchaib Krafla, Director of the Moroccan Labor Union; the Italian General Confederation Of Labor Delegation, headed by Angelo Di Gioia, Deputy Secretary of the Italian General Confederation Of Labor; Gabby, General Secretary of the New Zealand Railway Artisans' Association; Nai Bajata Sasenshin, representative of the Thai Free Labor Union; and Diallo Bubacar, Deputy Secretary-General of the French Sudan Postal and Telecommunications Union, representative of the General Federation of Black African Workers.
    Dong Xin, Secretary of the Secretariat of the All-China Federation of Trade Unions, and heads of various industrial trade unions, etc., went to the railway station and airport to welcome them.

### 二十一国工会代表到京

1958-04-30
第2版()
专栏：

    二十一国工会代表到京
    新华社29日讯　应中华全国总工会邀请，前来参加“五一”国际劳动节观礼的各国工会代表团和工会代表，现在大部分到达北京。
    今年应邀来我国参加“五一”国际劳动节观礼的有亚、非、欧、澳四洲三十三个国家的四十六个工会组织。到29日为止，已到达北京的有二十一个国家三十多个工会组织的代表。其余十二个国家中的工会组织的代表，也正在来京途中。
    早先来到我国的各国工会代表们，这几天来分别在北京等地参观了工厂、学校和农村，并访问了一些工会组织和工人。他们对我国社会主义建设全面大跃进的情况感到极大兴趣，给他们留下了深刻的印象。
    新华社29日讯　应中华全国总工会邀请，前来我国参加“五一”国际劳动节观礼的各国工会代表团，29日又有七个国家（区域）的工会代表团乘火车或飞机到达北京。他们是由全印度尼西亚中央职工会、印度尼西亚共和国中央职工会、全印度尼西亚穆斯林工会、全印度尼西亚工人大会、印度尼西亚政府公务员中央合作会议和印度尼西亚外资工会合作会议组成的印度尼西亚工人统一代表团代表卡迪斯·玛尔柯诺等八人；以日本全驻留军工会主席市川诚为首的日本访问中华人民共和国劳动代表团，以摩洛哥劳工联合会理事布夏依勒·克拉法为首的摩洛哥工会代表团，以意大利总工会副书记安杰罗·第·乔亚为首的意大利总工会代表团，新西兰铁路工匠协会总书记盖比，泰国自由劳工联合会代表乃巴扎·沙盛信，黑非工人总联合会代表法属苏丹邮电工会副总书记狄阿洛·布巴加尔。
    前往车站和机场欢迎的有中华全国总工会书记处书记董昕和各产业工会负责人等。
