Title: Wenzhou Iron Factory Produces Steel

Author: 
Date: 1958-04-26
Page: 3

Column: Flowers of Construction

    Wenzhou Iron Factory Produces Steel
    The small steelmaking furnace of Wenzhou Iron Factory produced its first batch of steel on the evening of April 21st. This steelmaking furnace has a capacity of 800 jin per batch and can produce 10 tons of steel per day. This year, it will produce 350 tons of steel ingots, steel blocks, and steel castings.

### 温州铁工厂出钢

1958-04-26
第3版()
专栏：建设的花朵

    温州铁工厂出钢
    温州铁工厂的小型炼钢炉于4月21日晚上炼出了第一炉钢铁。这座炼钢炉每炉容量八百斤，每天能炼钢十吨，今年将生产钢锭、钢块及钢铸件三百五十吨。
