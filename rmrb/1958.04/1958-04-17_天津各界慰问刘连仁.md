Title: Tianjin All Circles Extend Condolences to Liu Lianren
Author:
Date: 1958-04-17
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Tianjin, 16th. Responsible persons of the Tianjin Municipal People's Committee and representatives from all walks of life this afternoon expressed their sincere condolences to Liu Lianren and presented him with gifts of sympathy.
    In the living room of the Tianjin Hotel, Liu Lianren, together with his wife Zhao Yulan, son Liu Huanxin, and fourth younger brother Liu Liandeng, met with representatives from all walks of life and accepted the gifts of sympathy presented by them. Liu Lianren expressed his great gratitude for the concern shown by people from all walks of life.
    This afternoon, Ni Feijun, Deputy Secretary-General of the Red Cross Society of China, presented Liu Lianren with a consolation payment of 200 yuan on behalf of the Red Cross Society of China. The Tianjin Municipal Committee of the Communist Party of China and the Tianjin Municipal People's Committee sent two wardrobes and a tea set. The Tianjin Municipal Committee of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference, the Tianjin Branch of the Chinese People's Committee for World Peace, the Tianjin Federation of Trade Unions, the Tianjin Women's Federation, the Tianjin Branch of the Red Cross Society of China, the Tianjin Municipal Committee of the Communist Youth League, the Tianjin Municipal Federation of Democratic Youth, the Tianjin Federation of Industry and Commerce, and religious circles in Tianjin all sent gifts of sympathy to Liu Lianren and his family.

### 天津各界慰问刘连仁

1958-04-17
第4版()
专栏：

    天津各界慰问刘连仁
    新华社天津16日电　天津市人民委员会负责人和各界代表今天下午向刘连仁表示亲切的慰问，并且分别向刘连仁赠送了慰问品。
    在天津饭店的客厅里，刘连仁和他的妻子赵玉兰、儿子刘焕新、四弟刘连登一起同各界代表见面，接受了各界赠送的慰问品。刘连仁对各界人士的关怀非常感谢。
    今天下午，中国红十字会总会副秘书长倪斐君代表中国红十字会赠给刘连仁慰问金人民币二百元，中共天津市委员会和天津市人民委员会送来两个衣箱和一套茶具，中国人民政治协商会议天津市委员会、中国人民保卫世界和平委员会天津分会、天津市工会联合会、天津市妇女联合会、中国红十字会天津分会、共青团天津市委员会、天津市民主青年联合会、天津市工商业联合会、天津宗教界等，都给刘连仁和他的家属送了慰问品。
