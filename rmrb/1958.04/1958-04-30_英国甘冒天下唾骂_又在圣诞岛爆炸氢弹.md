Title: Braving Universal Condemnation, Britain Again Explodes Hydrogen Bomb on Christmas Island
Author:
Date: 1958-04-30
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, London, April 29th: A statement issued by the British Ministry of Supply on the evening of April 28th announced that Britain had detonated a "nuclear device" at high altitude over Christmas Island in the Pacific on that day. According to Western news agencies, this may have been a megaton-class hydrogen bomb.

In less than a year, Britain has already conducted four hydrogen bomb tests in the Christmas Island area. After the Soviet Union announced a unilateral moratorium on nuclear testing, Britain disregarded widespread opposition from world public opinion and conducted this nuclear test again.

### 英国甘冒天下唾骂  又在圣诞岛爆炸氢弹

1958-04-30
第6版()
专栏：

    英国甘冒天下唾骂
    又在圣诞岛爆炸氢弹
    新华社29日讯　伦敦消息：英国供应部4月28日晚上发表的一项声明宣布，英国在这一天在太平洋中的圣诞岛高空爆炸了一个“核装置”。据西方通讯社报道，这可能是一个百万吨级的氢弹。
    英国在不到一年的时间里，已经在圣诞岛区域举行过四次氢弹试验。在苏联宣布了单方面停止核试验以后，英国不顾世界舆论广泛的反对，又举行了这次核试验。
