Title: Rangoon University Students Welcome Soviet Peace Initiative
Author:
Date: 1958-04-14
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, Rangoon, April 12th: According to a report in the "Burma Daily" on the 12th, students at Rangoon University recently sent a telegram to the Chairman of the Council of Ministers of the Soviet Union, Khrushchev, warmly welcoming the Soviet Union's decision to unilaterally cease nuclear weapons testing.

The telegram said, "This courageous decision by the Soviet Union will make a significant contribution to easing international tensions and world peace."

The telegram expressed the hope that "other countries possessing nuclear weapons will also take the same measures" without insisting on any unnecessary conditions.

### 仰光大学学生欢迎苏联和平倡议

1958-04-14
第5版()
专栏：

    仰光大学学生欢迎苏联和平倡议
    新华社仰光12日电　据“缅甸人报”12日报道，仰光大学的学生最近打电报给苏联部长会议主席赫鲁晓夫，热烈欢迎苏联关于单方面停止核武器试验的决定。
    电报说，“苏联这一勇敢的决定对缓和国际紧张局势和世界和平都将作出巨大的贡献。”
    电报表示希望，“拥有核武器的其他国家也采取同样的措施”，而不硬要提出什么不必要的条件。
