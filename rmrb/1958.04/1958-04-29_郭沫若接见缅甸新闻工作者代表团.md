Title: Guo Moruo Receives Burmese Journalists Delegation
Author:
Date: 1958-04-29
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, April 28th - Guo Moruo, Vice Chairman of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress, received the Burmese Journalists Delegation headed by U Ba Than at his residence today. Lu Minggu, Deputy Secretary-General of the All-China Journalists Association, was present at the meeting.

The Burmese Journalists Delegation came to China for a visit at the invitation of the All-China Journalists Association. After arriving in Beijing on March 30, the delegation visited Beijing, Shanghai, Hangzhou, Wuhan, Guangzhou and other places.

### 郭沫若接见缅甸新闻工作者代表团

1958-04-29
第4版()
专栏：

    郭沫若接见缅甸新闻工作者代表团
    新华社28日讯　全国人民代表大会常务委员会副委员长郭沫若，今天在寓所接见了以吴巴丹为首的缅甸新闻工作者代表团。接见时在座的有中华全国新闻工作者协会副秘书长卢鸣谷。
    缅甸新闻工作者代表团是应中华全国新闻工作者协会的邀请来中国访问的。代表团于3月30日到达北京后，曾经在北京以及到上海、杭州、武汉、广州等地进行了访问。
