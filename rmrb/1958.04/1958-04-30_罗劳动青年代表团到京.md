Title: Romanian Labor Youth Delegation Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1958-04-30
Page: 2

Xinhua News Agency, April 28th—The Romanian Labor Youth Union delegation of six people, led by Alexandru Capandi, Secretary of the Central Committee of the Romanian Labor Youth Union, arrived in Beijing by plane today at the invitation of the Central Committee of the Chinese Communist Youth League for a visit to our country.

### 罗劳动青年代表团到京

1958-04-30
第2版()
专栏：

    罗劳动青年代表团到京
    新华社28日讯　应中国共产主义青年团中央委员会邀请来我国访问的罗马尼亚劳动青年联盟代表团一行六人，在团长、罗马尼亚劳动青年联盟中央委员会书记亚历山德鲁·卡潘迪率领下，已经在今天乘飞机到达北京。
