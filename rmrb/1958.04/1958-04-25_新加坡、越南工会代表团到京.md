Title: Singapore, Vietnamese Trade Union Delegations Arrive in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1958-04-25
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, April 24th. At the invitation of the All-China Federation of Trade Unions, Lee You Cheng, Vice-Chairman of the Singapore Trades Union Congress, and the Vietnamese General Confederation of Labour delegation headed by Nguyen Huu Giap, arrived in Beijing by train today to attend the May 1st International Labour Day celebrations.

Xi Zhanyuan, Secretary of the Secretariat of the All-China Federation of Trade Unions, and others went to the station to welcome them.

### 新加坡、越南工会代表团到京

1958-04-25
第4版()
专栏：

    新加坡、越南工会代表团到京
    新华社24日讯　应中华全国总工会邀请，前来我国参加“五一”国际劳动节观礼的新加坡职工总会副主席李友成，和以阮友捷为首的越南总工会代表团已经在今天乘火车先后到达北京。
    前往车站欢迎的有中华全国总工会书记处书记郗占元等。
