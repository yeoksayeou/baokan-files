Title: What is a halftone screen
Author: Zhang Lijun
Date: 1958-04-10
Page: 3

Industrial Common Knowledge

    What is a halftone screen
    "A halftone screen is the texture in a photographic printing plate. A 300-line screen has 300 lines per square inch, totaling 90,000 dots. A 510-line screen has 510 lines per square inch, totaling 260,100 dots. The more halftone lines on a photographic plate, the more beautiful the printed material. (Zhang Lijun)

### 什么叫网线
张立俊
1958-04-10
第3版()
专栏：工业常识

    什么叫网线
    “网线是照相印版中的底纹，三百线，就是每平方吋有三百根网线，九万个点子。五百一十线，就是每平方吋有五百一十根网线，二十六万零一百个点子。照相版上网线越多，印刷品就越漂亮。　（张立俊）
