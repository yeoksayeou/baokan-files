Title: 150-Ton Bridge Crane
Author:
Date: 1958-04-08
Page: 3

    150-Ton Bridge Crane
    A 150-ton large bridge crane has recently been successfully trial-produced at the Taiyuan Heavy Machinery Plant. This is the largest bridge crane that China is currently capable of manufacturing.
    This crane weighs 230 tons, is 34 meters long, and 5 meters high. It can lift objects weighing 150 tons to a height of 21 meters. Its auxiliary hook can also lift objects weighing 75 tons. (Xinhua News Agency)

### 150吨桥式起重机

1958-04-08
第3版()
专栏：新产品

    150吨桥式起重机
    一台一百五十吨大型桥式起重机，最近在太原重型机器厂试制成功。这是目前我国能够制造的最大的桥式起重机。
    这台起重机体重二百三十吨、长三十四公尺、高五公尺。它能将一百五十吨的物件吊到二十一公尺的高度。它的副钩也能起重七十五吨的物件。（新华社）
