Title: Chinese Trade Union Delegation Goes to the Soviet Union to Participate in the "May Day" Parade

Author:

Date: 1958-04-28
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, 27th. At the invitation of the All-Union Central Council of Trade Unions, the Chinese Trade Union Delegation, led by Yang Zhuhua, member of the Presidium of the All-China Federation of Trade Unions, left Beijing by plane today for Moscow to participate in the "May Day" International Labor Day celebrations.

### 中国工会代表团赴苏参加“五一”观礼

1958-04-28
第4版()
专栏：

    中国工会代表团赴苏参加“五一”观礼
    新华社27日讯　应全苏工会中央理事会邀请，前往苏联参加“五一”国际劳动节观礼的中国工会代表团在团长、中华全国总工会主席团委员杨之华率领下，已在今天乘飞机离开北京去莫斯科。
