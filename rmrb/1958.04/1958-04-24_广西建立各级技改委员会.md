Title: Guangxi Establishes Technical Improvement Committees at All Levels
Author:
Date: 1958-04-24
Page: 2

Guangxi Establishes Technical Improvement Committees at All Levels

(From our column)

    This newspaper reports: The Guangxi Zhuang Autonomous Region Party Committee of the Chinese Communist Party has decided to universally establish industrial and agricultural technical improvement committees throughout the region to vigorously support mass activities in reforming and creating production tools.

    Currently, this autonomous region has extensively launched tool reform. Some areas have achieved cartization. In order to maximize the concentration of mass creativity and inventions and to help the masses overcome difficulties in the tool reform movement, the Autonomous Region Party Committee has decided to establish industrial and agricultural technical reform committees at the autonomous region, special district, county, and township levels under the leadership of Party committees at all levels.

    In addition, this newspaper reports: The Guangxi Zhuang Autonomous Region has decided to hold a region-wide agricultural technical reform exhibition in Nanning to exchange experiences, promote new tools, and accelerate the advancement of the tool reform movement.

### 广西建立各级技改委员会

1958-04-24
第2版()
专栏：

    广西建立各级技改委员会
    本报讯　中共广西僮族自治区党委决定在全区普遍建立工农业技术改革委员会，大力支持群众改革和创造生产工具的活动。
    目前，这个自治区已广泛开展了工具改革，有些地区实现了车子化，为了最大限度地集中群众的创造发明，帮助群众克服改革工具活动中的困难，自治区党委决定在各级党委的领导下成立自治区的、专区的、县的、乡的工、农技术改革委员会。
    又讯　广西僮族自治区决定在南宁举办全区性的农业技术改革展览会，以交流经验，推广新工具，加速工具改革运动向前发展。
