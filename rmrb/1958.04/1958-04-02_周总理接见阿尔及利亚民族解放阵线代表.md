Title: Premier Zhou Receives Representatives of the Algerian National Liberation Front
Author:
Date: 1958-04-02
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, April 1st -- Premier Zhou Enlai received Brahim Ghafa, representative of the Algerian National Liberation Front, and Adef Daniel, representative of the Arab People's Congress, this afternoon.

Present at the reception were Bao Erhan, Director of the China Islamic Association, and Liao Chengzhi, Vice Chairman of the Chinese Committee for Asian Solidarity. (Attached picture)

Premier Zhou Enlai warmly shakes hands with Brahim Ghafa, representative of the Algerian National Liberation Front. Xinhua News Agency reporter Zheng Zhensun Photo

### 周总理接见阿尔及利亚民族解放阵线代表

1958-04-02
第1版()
专栏：

    周总理接见阿尔及利亚民族解放阵线代表
    新华社1日讯　周恩来总理今天下午接见了阿尔及利亚民族解放阵线代表卜拉欣·加法和阿拉伯人民大会代表阿德夫·达尼尔。
    接见时在座的有中国伊斯兰教协会主任包尔汉和中国亚洲团结委员会副主席廖承志等人。（附图片）
