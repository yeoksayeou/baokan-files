Title: In the Lesser Khingan Mountains (Picture)
Author:
Date: 1958-04-21
Page: 2

    In the Lesser Khingan Mountains
    To save timber, chainsaw workers peel back the snow and saw at 15 centimeters below the surface of the soil at the base of the trees.
    Tractors are using double cable skidding to transport lumber.
    Young lumberjack Sun Zhenxi gathers several pounds of pine cones in the forest every day. Four pounds of pine cones can cultivate two thousand saplings.

### 在小兴安岭里（图片）

1958-04-21
第2版()
专栏：

    在小兴安岭里
    电锯工人为了节约原木，扒开积雪在树腿以下十五公分贴地皮下锯。
    拖拉机正用双索套集运原木法拖运木材。
    青年伐木工人孙桢西每天在林中拾几斤松树子。四斤松树子即能培育出二千棵幼树。
