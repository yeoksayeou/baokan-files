Title: Anti-Imperialism, Patriotism, Unity, Progress: Build the Border Region of the Great Motherland - Tibet. The Dalai Lama and Panchen Lama Send Greetings to Chairman Mao and the State Council
Author: 
Date: 1958-04-23
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, Lhasa, April 22nd: The congratulatory telegrams from the Dalai Lama and Panchen Erdini to Chairman Mao and the State Council. The full text is as follows:

                  The Dalai Lama's Congratulatory Telegram
    Respected Chairman Mao and the State Council:
    The Preparatory Committee for the Tibet Autonomous Region has been established for two years. As Lhasa and major cities in Tibet are holding enthusiastic celebrations, I would like to extend my highest respects to the Chinese Communist Party, you, and the State Council.
    In the past year, thanks to the correct leadership of the Chinese Communist Party, you, and the State Council, various tasks in Tibet have achieved certain successes, especially in the unity of Han and Tibetan nationalities and the unity within the Tibetan nationality, which has been strengthened more than before.
    In the future, we will continue to work hard under the banner of anti-imperialism and patriotism, contribute all our strength to consolidating the unification of the motherland, building the prosperous and magnificent border region of our great motherland - Tibet, and striving for the early establishment of a unified Tibet Autonomous Region!
    Respectfully,
    Dalai Lama, April 22, 1958

    Panchen Erdini's Congratulatory Telegram
    Respected Chairman Mao and the State Council:
    On the occasion of the second anniversary of the establishment of the Preparatory Committee for the Tibet Autonomous Region, I extend my cordial respects to the Chinese Communist Party, you, and the State Council with great joy and gratitude.
    In the past year, under the correct and wise leadership of the Central Government and you, with the direct assistance of the Tibet Working Committee of the CPC, and with the united and concerted efforts of the Dalai Lama, myself, and all staff, the various tasks of the Preparatory Committee for the Tibet Autonomous Region have developed to a certain extent and have achieved considerable success.
    In the future, we will continue to follow the central government's guiding principles of anti-imperialism, patriotism, unity, and progress, further strengthen the close unity between the Han and Tibetan nationalities and within the Tibetan nationality, continue to promote the spirit of anti-imperialism and patriotism, strive to learn from the advanced work experience of all fraternal nationalities, gradually improve and enhance our various tasks, further strengthen and consolidate the unification of our great motherland, and strive to realize the unified Tibet Autonomous Region as soon as possible and build a prosperous and happy new Tibet!
    Respectfully,
    Panchen Erdini, April 22, 1958

### 反帝　爱国　团结　进步  建设伟大祖国的边疆——西藏  达赖和班禅电毛主席和国务院致敬

1958-04-23
第1版()
专栏：

    反帝　爱国　团结　进步
    建设伟大祖国的边疆——西藏
    达赖和班禅电毛主席和国务院致敬
    新华社拉萨22日电　达赖喇嘛和班禅额尔德尼给毛主席和国务院的致敬电。全文如下：
                  达赖喇嘛的致敬电敬爱的毛主席和国务院：
    西藏自治区筹备委员会成立已经两周年了。在拉萨和西藏各大城镇正在进行热烈庆祝之际，我谨向中国共产党、您和国务院致以崇高的敬礼。
    在过去的一年中，由于中国共产党、您和国务院的正确领导，西藏的各项工作均获得了一定的成绩，尤其在汉藏族团结和藏族内部的团结方面比以前更加强了。
    今后，我们将在反帝爱国的旗帜下继续努力前进，为巩固祖国的统一，为建设我们伟大祖国的富饶壮丽的边疆——西藏，为争取早日成立统一的西藏自治区而贡献出一切力量！
    达赖喇嘛　1958年4月22日敬上
    班禅额尔德尼的致敬电敬爱的毛主席和国务院：
    值此西藏自治区筹备委员会成立两周年之际，我怀着十分愉快和感激的心情，向中国共产党、您和国务院致以亲切的敬意。
    一年来在中央和您的正确英明领导下，在中共西藏工委的直接帮助下，在达赖喇嘛和我个人以及全体工作人员团结一致共同努力下，西藏自治区筹备委员会的各项工作有了一定的发展，并取得了不少的成绩。
    今后，我们将继续遵循着中央指示的反帝、爱国、团结、进步的方针，进一步加强汉藏族和藏族内部的亲密团结，继续发扬反帝爱国精神，努力学习各兄弟民族的先进工作经验，逐步改进和提高我们的各项工作，进一步加强和巩固我们伟大祖国的统一，为争取尽早的实现统一的西藏自治区和建设繁荣幸福的新西藏而努力奋斗！
    班禅额尔德尼　1958年4月22日敬上
