Title: People of Sakdong-ri, North Korea, Commemorate Martyr Luo Shengjiao
Author:
Date: 1958-04-01
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, Pyongyang, March 31st. The people of Sakdong-ri, Songchon County, North Korea, solemnly commemorated the internationalist fighter Luo Shengjiao on March 27th. Choi ?, a model soldier of a certain unit of the Korean People's Army who was rescued from an ice cave by Martyr Luo Shengjiao during his lifetime, also specially requested leave to come from afar to pay his respects.

    The memorial ceremony was conducted in accordance with Korean customs. People placed sacrifices in front of the martyr's tomb, presented wreaths, and stood in silence with their hats removed for a long time in front of the martyr's tomb, expressing their deep condolences. Then, Choi ? offered wine to Luo Shengjiao's brother in tribute.

    After the memorial ceremony, the students of Luo Shengjiao Primary School planted evergreens and cypresses at the martyr's cemetery.

### 朝鲜朔仑里人民  祭奠罗盛教烈士

1958-04-01
第5版()
专栏：

    朝鲜朔仑里人民
    祭奠罗盛教烈士
    新华社平壤31日电　朝鲜成川郡朔仓里人民在3月27日隆重祭奠国际主义战士罗盛教。被罗盛教烈士生前从冰窟里救起来的朝鲜人民军某部模范战士崔?也特地请假远道赶来祭奠。
    祭奠仪式按照朝鲜的风俗习惯进行。人们在烈士墓前摆了祭品，敬献了花圈，并长时间地脱帽站立在烈士墓前，致以深切的悼念。接着，崔?向罗盛教哥哥敬酒致奠。
    祭奠结束后，罗盛教小学的学生们在烈士墓地栽植了苍松翠柏。
