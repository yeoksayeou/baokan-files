Title: Deng Tuo Hosts a Banquet for the Finnish Journalists Delegation
Author:
Date: 1958-04-04
Page: 4

    Xinhua News Agency, April 3rd. Deng Tuo, President of the All-China Journalists Association, hosted a banquet this evening to welcome the Finnish Journalists Delegation, headed by Olko Mis, and all its members.

    Among those present at the banquet this evening were Wang Yunsheng, Vice President of the All-China Journalists Association, Xu Huang, Deputy Director of the Information Department of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs, and members of the press in the capital.

    Nevalainen, Chargé d'Affaires ad interim of the Finnish Embassy in China, and his wife also attended the banquet.

### 邓拓宴请芬兰新闻工作者代表团

1958-04-04
第4版()
专栏：

    邓拓宴请芬兰新闻工作者代表团
    新华社3日讯　中华全国新闻工作者协会会长邓拓今天晚上设宴招待芬兰新闻工作者代表团团长奥尔科密斯和全体团员。
    今天晚上出席宴会的有，中华全国新闻工作者协会副会长王芸生，外交部新闻司副司长徐晃以及首都新闻界人士。
    芬兰驻华大使馆临时代办耐瓦莱宁和夫人也出席了宴会。
