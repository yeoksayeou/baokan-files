Title: Xu Yixin Appointed as China's Ambassador to Norway
Author: 
Date: 1958-04-03
Page: 1

    Xinhua News Agency, April 2nd: Chairman of the People's Republic of China, Mao Zedong, has appointed Xu Yixin as the Ambassador Extraordinary and Plenipotentiary of the People's Republic of China to the Kingdom of Norway.
    Chairman of the People's Republic of China, Mao Zedong, has relieved Wang Youping of his position as Ambassador Extraordinary and Plenipotentiary of the People's Republic of China to the Kingdom of Norway.

### 徐以新任我国驻挪威大使

1958-04-03
第1版()
专栏：

    徐以新任我国驻挪威大使
    新华社2日讯　中华人民共和国主席毛泽东任命徐以新为中华人民共和国驻挪威王国特命全权大使。
    中华人民共和国主席毛泽东免去王幼平的中华人民共和国驻挪威王国特命全权大使的职务。
