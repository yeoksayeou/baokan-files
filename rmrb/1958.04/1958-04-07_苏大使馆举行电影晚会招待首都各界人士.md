Title: Soviet Embassy Hosts Film Evening for People from All Walks of Life in the Capital
Author: 
Date: 1958-04-07
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, April 5th—The Soviet Embassy in China held a film evening tonight at the Capital Cinema to entertain responsible persons from various departments of our government and people from all walks of life in the capital.

Also invited to the evening were workers from the capital, members of suburban agricultural cooperatives, teachers and students from universities and colleges, and staff members from various institutions and organizations, totaling more than 1,100 people.

After a welcome speech by Soviet Embassy in China Counselor Sudarikov, the Soviet color feature film "The Communist" was screened.

During the evening, the color film "Festival of the New World," which recorded the grand celebrations of the 40th anniversary of the Great October Socialist Revolution by people from various countries, was also screened.

### 苏大使馆举行电影晚会招待首都各界人士

1958-04-07
第4版()
专栏：

    苏大使馆举行电影晚会招待首都各界人士
    据新华社5日讯　苏联驻华大使馆今晚在首都电影院举行电影晚会，招待我国政府各部门负责人和首都各界社会人士。
    应邀参加晚会的还有首都的工人、郊区农业社社员、高等学校的师生和各机关、团体的工作人员，共一千一百多人。
    在苏联驻华大使馆参赞苏达利柯夫致欢迎词后，放映了苏联彩色艺术片“共产党员”。
    晚会上，还放映了纪录各国人民庆祝伟大十月社会主义革命四十周年盛况的彩色影片“新世界的节日”。
