Title: Shanxi Hejin Xusheng Cooperative: Dryland Wheat Exceeds 1,000 Jin
Author:
Date: 1958-04-26
Page: 2
Agriculture High Yield Record

    Shanxi Hejin Xusheng Cooperative
    Dryland Wheat Exceeds 1,000 Jin
    In 1957, the Xusheng Agricultural Cooperative in Xiaoliang Township, Hejin County, Shanxi Province, had three mu of dryland winter wheat with an average yield of 1,426 jin per mu, setting the highest wheat yield per unit area record in Shanxi Province. These three mu of land were flooded once in 1956. Before sowing on the high-yield land, 12,000 jin of base fertilizer were applied per mu in layers and in multiple applications; after the wheat seedlings emerged, top dressing was applied three times according to different stages of development. Measures such as deep plowing and close planting were also adopted.

### 山西河津旭升社  旱地小麦过千斤

1958-04-26
第2版()
专栏：农业高产志

    山西河津旭升社
    旱地小麦过千斤
    山西省河津县小梁乡旭升农业社，1957年有三亩旱地冬小麦平均亩产一千四百二十六斤，创造山西全省小麦单位面积产量最高纪录。这三亩地1956年被洪水漫灌过一次。丰产地在播种以前，每亩地分层分次施入底肥一万二千斤；麦苗出土以后，又根据不同的发育阶段，施了三次追肥。还采用了深耕、密植等措施。
