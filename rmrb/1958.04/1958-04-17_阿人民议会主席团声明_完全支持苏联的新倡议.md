Title: Statement by the Presidium of the Albanian People's Assembly: Fully Supporting the Soviet Union's New Initiative
Author:
Date: 1958-04-17
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, Tirana, April 16th: According to the Albanian Telegraph Agency, the Presidium of the Albanian People's Assembly issued a statement on the 15th, saying that the Presidium of the People's Assembly, representing the people of the entire country, fully supports the decision of the Supreme Soviet of the Soviet Union to unilaterally cease nuclear weapons testing and supports the appeal issued by the Supreme Soviet of the Soviet Union to the parliaments of all countries in March of this year regarding the cessation of nuclear weapons testing.

The statement expressed its belief that the parliaments, governments, and people of all peace-loving countries will support this decision by the Soviet Union.

### 阿人民议会主席团声明  完全支持苏联的新倡议

1958-04-17
第6版()
专栏：

    阿人民议会主席团声明
    完全支持苏联的新倡议
    据新华社地拉那16日电　据阿尔巴尼亚通讯社报道，阿尔巴尼亚人民议会主席团15日发表声明说，人民议会主席团代表全国人民完全支持苏联最高苏维埃关于苏联单方面停止核武器试验的决议，并且支持苏联最高苏维埃今年3月间就停止试验核武器问题致各国议会的呼吁书。
    声明表示相信所有爱好和平的国家的议会、政府和人民将会支持苏联这项决议。
