Title: Comrade Chivu Stoica
Author:
Date: 1958-04-02
Page: 1

    Comrade Chivu Stoica, who leads the Romanian government delegation to visit our country at the invitation of our government, is the Chairman of the Council of Ministers of the Romanian People's Republic and one of the outstanding leaders of the Romanian state and party.
    Comrade Chivu Stoica was born in 1908 into a poor peasant family. He once worked in the "Central Railway Repair Workshop" in Bucharest, where he participated in the labor movement and organized trade union activities.
    In 1931, Comrade Chivu Stoica joined the Romanian Communist Party. During 1932-1933, Comrade Chivu Stoica worked alongside Comrade Gheorghe Gheorghiu-Dej and was one of the organizers and leaders of the railway workers' and oil workers' strikes. He organized and led the railway workers and oil workers in their struggle to improve living conditions, fight for democratic freedoms, and resist fascist aggression against Romania. Because of his struggle activities, he was arrested by the reactionary government and sentenced to twelve years of hard labor. He was imprisoned in many places, and later he and other political prisoners were imprisoned in the Târgu Jiu concentration camp. In 1944, Comrade Chivu Stoica was one of the leaders of the struggle to overthrow the fascist dictatorship in his country and liberate Romania from Hitler's enslavement.
    In October 1945, Comrade Chivu Stoica was elected as a member of the Central Committee of the Romanian Communist Party at the National Congress of the Romanian Communist Party. After the merger of the Romanian Communist Party and the Social Democratic Party into the Romanian Workers' Party in 1948, Comrade Chivu Stoica has been continuously elected as a member of the Central Committee and a member of the Political Bureau of the Central Committee of the Workers' Party.
    In the parliamentary elections of November 1946 and the elections of deputies to the Grand National Assembly in 1948, 1952, and 1957, Comrade Chivu Stoica was continuously elected as a deputy.
    From 1945 to 1946, Comrade Chivu Stoica served as Vice Chairman of the Romanian General Confederation of Labor, in 1947 as Director General of the Railway Administration, and from 1948 to 1952 as Minister of Industry and Commerce, and Minister of Metallurgical and Chemical Industries.
    In March 1950, the Presidium of the Romanian Grand National Assembly appointed him as Vice Chairman of the Council of Ministers, and in August 1954 as First Vice Chairman of the Council of Ministers.
    In October 1955, the Presidium of the Grand National Assembly elected him as Chairman of the Council of Ministers, and he has held this position until now.
    Due to his long-term and successful activities for the motherland and the working people, and for the peace and friendship of the people of the world, the government of the Romanian People's Republic has awarded him numerous medals and awards. (Attached image)

### 基伏·斯托伊卡同志

1958-04-02
第1版()
专栏：

    基伏·斯托伊卡同志
    应我国政府邀请率领罗马尼亚政府代表团来我国访问的基伏·斯托伊卡同志，是罗马尼亚人民共和国部长会议主席，是罗马尼亚国家和党的杰出领导人之一。
    基伏·斯托伊卡同志1908年生在一个贫苦农民的家里。他曾经在布加勒斯特“中央铁路修配工场”作工，在这个时候他参加了工人运动和组织工会的活动。
    1931年基伏·斯托伊卡同志加入了罗马尼亚共产党。1932—1933年期间，基伏·斯托伊卡同志和乔治乌—德治同志并肩工作，是铁路工人和石油工人大罢工的组织者和领导人之一。他组织和领导了铁路工人和石油工人进行为争取改善生活条件、争取民主自由权利和反抗法西斯主义侵入罗马尼亚的斗争。因为他的斗争活动，他遭到反动政府的逮捕，并且被判处了十二年的苦工。他被关在许多地方的监狱里，后来他和其他政治犯被关在堤尔久乌集中营里。1944年，基伏·斯托伊卡同志是推翻本国法西斯独裁政权和从希特勒奴役下解放罗马尼亚的斗争的领导者之一。
    1945年10月，基伏·斯托伊卡同志在罗马尼亚共产党全国代表大会上当选为罗共中央委员。1948年罗马尼亚共产党和社会民主党合并成为罗马尼亚工人党后，基伏·斯托伊卡同志一直当选为工人党中央委员和中央政治局委员。
    在1946年11月的议会选举、1948、1952和1957年的大国民议会议员选举中，基伏·斯托伊卡同志都连续当选为议员。
    1945—1946年，基伏·斯托伊卡同志任罗马尼亚劳动联合总会副主席，1947年任铁路管理总局局长，1948—1952年任工业和商务部部长，冶金与化学工业部部长等职。
    1950年3月，罗马尼亚大国民议会主席团任命他为部长会议副主席，1954年8月任部长会议第一副主席。
    1955年10月，大国民议会主席团选举他为部长会议主席，直到现在。
    由于他为祖国和劳动人民，为世界人民的和平与友谊所作的长期而富有成就的活动，罗马尼亚人民共和国政府曾经授予他多次勋章和奖章。（附图片）
