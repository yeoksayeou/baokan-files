Title: Kardelj, Ranković, and Others Elected Vice-Presidents of the Yugoslav Federal Executive Council
Author:
Date: 1958-04-23
Page: 5

    Xinhua News Agency, Belgrade, April 20th - The Yugoslav Federal Executive Council held its first meeting on the 19th. Following President Tito's proposal, the meeting elected Edvard Kardelj, Aleksandar Ranković, Rodoljub Čolaković, and Mijalko Todorović as Vice-Presidents of the Federal Executive Council, and Veljko Zeković as Secretary of the Federal Executive Council.

### 卡德尔、兰科维奇等当选南联邦执委会副主席

1958-04-23
第5版()
专栏：

    卡德尔、兰科维奇等当选南联邦执委会副主席
    据新华社贝尔格莱德20日电　南斯拉夫联邦执行委员会在19日举行第一次会议。根据铁托总统的提议，会议选举爱德华·卡德尔、亚历山大·兰科维奇、罗多柳布·乔拉柯维奇和米雅尔科·托多罗维奇为联邦执行委员会副主席，维尔科·泽科维奇为联邦执行委员会秘书。
