Title: Osaka Citizens' Rally Demands the Establishment of Diplomatic Relations between China and Japan; Nineteen Japanese Manufacturers Accuse the Government of Obstructing the Sino-Japanese Trade Agreement
Author:
Date: 1958-04-21
Page: 5

    Xinhua News Agency, 19th dispatch from Tokyo: More than one thousand Osaka citizens held a rally to restore Sino-Japanese diplomatic relations, passing a declaration demanding the swift restoration of normal diplomatic relations with China.
    The declaration criticized the Japanese government for ignoring the demands of the Japanese people and adopting a pro-American attitude, obstructing the implementation of the Fourth Sino-Japanese Trade Agreement. The declaration strongly demanded that the Japanese government take measures as soon as possible to hold negotiations with China to discuss the restoration of Sino-Japanese diplomatic relations. The declaration stated that during the upcoming general election, the citizens of Osaka would stand at the forefront and actively launch a movement to restore Sino-Japanese diplomatic relations.
    This rally was held on the 16th. Nakasaki Satoshi, Chairman of the Special Committee for the Restoration of Sino-Japanese Diplomatic Relations of the Socialist Party, Shiga Yoshio, Member of the Central Standing Committee of the Japanese Communist Party, Kawakatsu Den, Vice Chairman of the Japan International Trade Promotion Association, and representatives from the Osaka Prefectural Assembly, industry, trade, and worker, farmer, youth, women, and student groups spoke at the meeting, all demanding the swift restoration of Sino-Japanese diplomatic relations.
    Prior to this, according to a report in the "Akahata," ten representatives from six organizations, including the National Council for the Restoration of Sino-Japanese Diplomatic Relations, the Tokyo Federation of the Japan-China Friendship Association, the Tokyo Regional Council of the General Council of Trade Unions of Japan, the Liaison Association of Returnees from China, the League of Local Assembly Members Promoting International Trade, and the Japan Trade Union Council for the Promotion of International Trade, met on the 12th to discuss methods for launching a signature campaign aimed at promoting the restoration of Sino-Japanese diplomatic relations. The meeting decided to establish a center for the signature campaign in Tokyo.
    Xinhua News Agency, 19th dispatch from Tokyo: Representatives of nineteen Japanese manufacturers with trade relations with China held a meeting in Tokyo on the 18th to discuss the current situation of Sino-Japanese trade. The meeting was presided over by the Japan-China Trade Association. Representatives from manufacturers such as Toyo Cotton Company and Keimei Trading Company participated.
    The meeting decided to appeal to other manufacturers involved in Sino-Japanese trade to jointly demand that the Japanese government and the Liberal Democratic Party fully implement the Fourth Sino-Japanese Trade Agreement, and to request that the three Sino-Japanese trade organizations continue their efforts to resolve the Sino-Japanese trade agreement issue.

### 大阪市民集会要求中日建交  日本十九家厂商指责政府阻挠中日贸易协定

1958-04-21
第5版()
专栏：

    大阪市民集会要求中日建交
    日本十九家厂商指责政府阻挠中日贸易协定
    新华社19日讯　东京消息：大阪市民一千多人举行的恢复日中邦交大会上，通过了一项宣言，要求尽速同中国恢复正常邦交。
    宣言指责日本政府忽视日本人民的要求，采取从属美国的态度，阻挠第四次日中贸易协定的执行。宣言强烈要求日本政府尽速采取措施同中国举行谈判，商谈恢复日中邦交问题。宣言表示，在即将举行的大选期间，大阪市民将站在前列，积极展开恢复日中邦交的运动。
    这次大会是在16日举行的。社会党恢复日中邦交特别委员会委员长中崎敏、日本共产党中央常任干部会委员志贺义雄、日本国际贸易促进协会副会长川胜传和大阪府议会、产业、贸易界代表以及工人、农民、青年、妇女和学生团体代表在会上讲了话，他们都要求尽速恢复日中邦交。
    在这以前，据“赤旗报”报道，恢复日中邦交国民会议、日中友好协会东京都联合会、日本工会总评议会东京地方评议会、从中国回国者联络会、地方议员促进国际贸易联盟、日本工会促进国际贸易协议会等六个团体的十位代表在12日开会讨论了今后展开旨在促进恢复日中邦交的签名运动的方法。会议决定设立东京都的签名运动的中心。
    据新华社19日讯　东京消息：日本十九家同日中贸易有关系的厂商代表18日在东京举行会议，讨论日中贸易的目前情况。会议由中日贸易会主持。参加的有东洋棉花公司和启明贸易公司等厂商的代表。
    会议决定吁请其他和日中贸易有关的厂商联合要求日本政府和自由民主党完全实施第四次日中贸易协定，并要求日中贸易三团体为解决日中贸易协定问题而继续努力。
