Title: Khrushchev Leads Delegation to Visit Hungary
Author:
Date: 1958-04-02
Page: 5

    Khrushchev Leads Delegation to Visit Hungary
    Xinhua News Agency, April 1st - TASS Moscow reports: The Central Committee of the Communist Party of the Soviet Union and the Council of Ministers of the Soviet Union have accepted the invitation and appointed a party and government delegation to visit Hungary. The delegation will depart from Moscow on the 2nd to visit Hungary.
    The head of the delegation is Khrushchev, Chairman of the Council of Ministers of the Soviet Union. Members of the delegation include Kozlov, First Deputy Chairman of the Council of Ministers of the Soviet Union; Gromyko, Minister of Foreign Affairs of the Soviet Union; Grishin, Chairman of the All-Union Central Council of Trade Unions; Shelest, First Secretary of the Kiev Oblast Committee of the Communist Party of Ukraine; Boikova, Second Secretary of the Leningrad City Committee of the Soviet Union; and Gromov, Soviet Ambassador to Hungary.

### 赫鲁晓夫率代表团访匈

1958-04-02
第5版()
专栏：

    赫鲁晓夫率代表团访匈
    据新华社1日讯　据塔斯社莫斯科讯：苏联共产党中央委员会和苏联部长会议已经接受邀请任命了访问匈牙利的党和政府代表团。代表团将在2日由莫斯科启程前往匈牙利访问。
    代表团团长是苏联部长会议主席赫鲁晓夫，团员有苏联部长会议第一副主席科兹洛夫，苏联外交部长葛罗米柯，全苏工会中央理事会主席格里申，乌克兰共产党基辅州委员会第一书记谢列斯特，苏联列宁格勒市委员会第二书记鲍伊科娃和苏联驻匈牙利大使格罗莫夫。
