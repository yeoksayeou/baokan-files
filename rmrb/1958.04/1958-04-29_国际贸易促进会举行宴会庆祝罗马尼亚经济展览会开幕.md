Title: China Council for the Promotion of International Trade Hosts Banquet to Celebrate Opening of Romanian Economic Exhibition
Author:
Date: 1958-04-29
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, 28th: The China Council for the Promotion of International Trade held a banquet this evening to celebrate the opening of the Romanian Economic Exhibition.

    Ji Chao-ding, Vice-Chairman of the China Council for the Promotion of International Trade, said at the banquet that we were deeply impressed after visiting the Romanian Economic Exhibition this afternoon. I believe that the achievements and experiences of Romania's socialist construction will promote our country's socialist great leap forward.

    Anna Toma, head of the Romanian government delegation, attended the banquet and delivered a speech. She hoped that the Chinese people would learn more about the efforts made by the Romanian people in socialist construction through the exhibition.

    Also invited to the banquet today were Romanian Ambassador to China Rudenko, all members of the Romanian government delegation, and all staff members of the Romanian Economic Exhibition.

### 国际贸易促进会举行宴会庆祝罗马尼亚经济展览会开幕

1958-04-29
第1版()
专栏：

    国际贸易促进会举行宴会庆祝罗马尼亚经济展览会开幕
    新华社28日讯　中国国际贸易促进委员会今晚举行宴会，庆祝罗马尼亚经济展览会开幕。
    中国国际贸易促进委员会副主席冀朝鼎在宴会上说，我们在今天下午参观了罗马尼亚经济展览会以后，留下了深刻的印象。我相信罗马尼亚社会主义建设的成就和经验，对我国的社会主义大跃进将会起促进作用。
    罗马尼亚政府代表团团长安娜·托马出席了宴会，并且讲了话。她希望中国人民通过展览会能更多地了解罗马尼亚人民在社会主义建设中所作的努力。
    应邀参加今天宴会的还有罗马尼亚驻我国大使鲁登科、罗马尼亚政府代表团全体团员以及罗马尼亚经济展览会的全体工作人员。
