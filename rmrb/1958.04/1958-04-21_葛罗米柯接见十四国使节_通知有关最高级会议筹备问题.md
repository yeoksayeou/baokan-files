Title: Gromyko Receives Envoys from Fourteen Countries, Informs Them of Preparations for Top-Level Meeting
Author:
Date: 1958-04-21
Page: 5

　　Xinhua News Agency, April 20th: TASS reported that Soviet Foreign Minister Gromyko received Chinese Ambassador to the Soviet Union Liu Xiao and ambassadors and chargés d'affaires from thirteen other countries on the 19th. Gromyko informed them of matters related to the preparations for a top-level meeting.

　　These thirteen countries are: Albania, Bulgaria, Hungary, Democratic Republic of Vietnam, German Democratic Republic, Democratic People's Republic of Korea, Mongolia, Poland, Czechoslovakia, Romania, Yugoslavia, India, and Sweden.

### 葛罗米柯接见十四国使节  通知有关最高级会议筹备问题

1958-04-21
第5版()
专栏：

    葛罗米柯接见十四国使节
    通知有关最高级会议筹备问题
    新华社20日讯　据塔斯社报道：苏联外交部长葛罗米柯在19日接见了中国驻苏联大使刘晓和其它十三个国家的大使和临时代办。葛罗米柯把有关筹备最高级会议的问题通知了他们。
    这十三个国家是：阿尔巴尼亚、保加利亚、匈牙利、越南民主共和国、民主德国、民主朝鲜、蒙古、波兰、捷克斯洛伐克、罗马尼亚、南斯拉夫、印度、瑞典。
