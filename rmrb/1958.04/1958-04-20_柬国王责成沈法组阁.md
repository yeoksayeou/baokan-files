Title: Cambodian King Entrusts Sim Var with Forming Cabinet
Author: 
Date: 1958-04-20
Page: 3

Xinhua News Agency, Phnom Penh, 19th: Cambodian King Suramarit has entrusted Sim Var, President of the Royal High Council and former Prime Minister, with the task of forming a cabinet. Sim Var accepted the task of forming a cabinet on the 18th.

Sim Var served as Prime Minister and Interim Prime Minister before the King dissolved the National Assembly in January of this year.

### 柬国王责成沈法组阁

1958-04-20
第3版()
专栏：

    柬国王责成沈法组阁
    据新华社19日讯　金边消息：柬埔寨国王苏拉玛里特已经责成皇廷最高会议议长、前首相沈法组阁，沈法在18日已经接受组阁任务。
    沈法在今年1月国王解散国民议会以前曾经担任首相和临时首相。
