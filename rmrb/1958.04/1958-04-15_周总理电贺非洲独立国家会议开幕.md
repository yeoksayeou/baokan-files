Title: Premier Zhou Sends Congratulatory Message on the Opening of the African Independent States Conference
Author:
Date: 1958-04-15
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, April 14th: Premier Zhou Enlai today sent a telegram to Ghanaian Prime Minister Nkrumah, congratulating him on the opening of the African Independent States Conference. The telegram reads as follows:
Ghana, Accra
To Prime Minister Nkrumah,
Transferred to the African Independent States Conference,

    On behalf of the Chinese government and the Chinese people, I extend warm congratulations on the opening of the African Independent States Conference, and wish the conference success in its efforts to oppose colonialism, strive for the national independence of African countries, oppose racial discrimination, enhance friendly cooperation among the people of African countries, and safeguard world peace.
                             Zhou Enlai, Premier of the State Council of the People's Republic of China
    				    April 14, 1958, Beijing

### 周总理电贺非洲独立国家会议开幕

1958-04-15
第1版()
专栏：

    周总理电贺非洲独立国家会议开幕
    新华社14日讯　周恩来总理今天致电加纳总理恩克鲁玛，祝贺非洲独立国家会议的开幕。电文如下：加纳，阿克拉恩克鲁玛总理转非洲独立国家会议，
    我谨代表中国政府和中国人民热烈地祝贺非洲独立国家会议的开幕，祝会议在反对殖民主义、争取非洲国家的民族独立、反对种族歧视、增进非洲各国人民友好合作和维护世界和平的事业上取得成功。
                             中华人民共和国国务院总理　周恩来
    				    一九五八年四月十四日于北京
