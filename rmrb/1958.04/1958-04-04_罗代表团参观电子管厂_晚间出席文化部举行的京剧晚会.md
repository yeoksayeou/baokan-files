Title: Romanian Delegation Visits Electron Tube Factory; Attends Peking Opera Performance Held by the Ministry of Culture in the Evening
Author:
Date: 1958-04-04
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency, April 3rd: The Chairman of the Romanian Council of Ministers, Chivu Stoica, visited the state-owned Beijing Electron Tube Factory this morning. Accompanying him were Foreign Minister Bungaţianu, Ambassador to China Rudenko, and other members of the Romanian government delegation.

The Vice-Chairman of the Council of Ministers and Minister of Transport and Post and Telecommunications, Emil Bodnăraş, accompanied by Major General Wang Zhenxiang, Major General Wang Huanru, and Director of the Protocol Department of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs Yu Peiwen, visited a military school of the Chinese People's Liberation Army this morning.

During the visit to the Electron Tube Factory, the distinguished guests, accompanied by Vice Minister of the First Ministry of Machine Building Cao Xiangren and Chinese Ambassador to Romania Ke Bonian, walked through the various workshops of the factory, carefully observed the production process of electron tubes, and inquired about many questions regarding production methods and equipment efficiency.

After the visit, Chairman Chivu Stoica said to the many male and female workers who gathered in the square to welcome the distinguished guests: "You have a very good factory that is of great significance to the development of the national economy. You have already cultivated many engineering and technical personnel and skilled workers here. I wish you greater victories in your future production and work."

Xinhua News Agency, April 3rd: The Ministry of Culture of the People's Republic of China held a Peking Opera performance this evening to entertain all the distinguished guests of the Romanian government delegation headed by the Chairman of the Romanian Council of Ministers, Chivu Stoica.

During the evening, famous Chinese Peking Opera actors Du Jinfang, Li Shaochun, Yuan Shihai, and Ye Sheng Lan, among others, performed "The White-Haired Girl."

Premier Zhou Enlai and Vice Chairman Peng Zhen accompanied the distinguished guests to watch the performance.

Also accompanying the performance were Xi Zhongxun, Wang Shoudao, Zhang Xiruo, Luo Guibo, Liu Zhiming, and Chinese Ambassador to Romania Ke Bonian, who are in charge of relevant departments of the Chinese government.

After the performance, Chairman Chivu Stoica and other Romanian distinguished guests, accompanied by Premier Zhou Enlai and Vice Chairman Peng Zhen, went on stage to meet the actors. The distinguished guests presented the actors with flower baskets. Chairman Chivu Stoica is visiting the large tube workshop of the Beijing Electron Tube Factory.

### 罗代表团参观电子管厂  晚间出席文化部举行的京剧晚会

1958-04-04
第4版()
专栏：

    罗代表团参观电子管厂
    晚间出席文化部举行的京剧晚会
    据新华社3日讯　罗马尼亚部长会议主席基伏·斯托伊卡今天上午参观了国营北京电子管厂。同往参观的还有外交部长布纳丘、驻中国大使鲁登科和罗马尼亚政府代表团的其他随行人员。
    部长会议副主席兼交通和邮电部长埃米尔·波德纳拉希由王振祥少将、王焕如少将和外交部礼宾司长俞沛文等人陪同，今天上午参观了中国人民解放军的一所军事学校。
    在参观电子管厂时，贵宾们由我国第一机械工业部副部长曹祥仁和我国驻罗马尼亚大使柯柏年等人陪同，走过工厂的各个车间，仔细地参观了电子管的生产过程，并且询问了许多有关生产方法和设备效能的问题。
    基伏·斯托伊卡主席参观后对聚集在广场上欢迎贵宾的许多男女工人说：“你们有着一个对发展国民经济具有重要意义的很好的工厂。你们已经在这里培养了许多工程技术人员和技术工人，我祝你们在今后的生产和工作中获得更大的胜利。”
    新华社3日讯　中华人民共和国文化部在今天晚间举行京剧晚会，招待以罗马尼亚部长会议主席基伏·斯托伊卡为首的罗马尼亚政府代表团的全体贵宾。
    晚会上，由中国著名的京剧演员杜近芳、李少春、袁世海和叶盛兰等演出了“白毛女”。
    周恩来总理和彭真副委员长陪同贵宾观剧。
    陪同观剧的还有我国政府有关部门的负责人习仲勋、王首道、张奚若、罗贵波、刘芝明和中国驻罗马尼亚大使柯柏年。
    演出结束后，基伏·斯托伊卡主席和罗马尼亚的其他贵宾由周恩来总理和彭真副委员长等陪同，登上舞台同演员们会面。贵宾们向演员们赠送了花篮。基伏·斯托伊卡主席正在参观北京电子管厂大型管车间。
                                  新华社记者　刘东鳌摄
