Title: Nasser to Visit Soviet Union at the End of the Month
Author:
Date: 1958-04-12
Page: 5
Xinhua News Agency, Cairo, April 10th. According to an official announcement today, President Nasser of the United Arab Republic, at the invitation of the Presidium of the Supreme Soviet of the Soviet Union, will visit the Soviet Union at the end of April.

The statement said that during the visit, Nasser will meet with Soviet leaders and will visit various parts of the Soviet Union to learn about the lives of the Soviet people and the achievements the Soviet Union has made in various fields.

### 纳赛尔月底访苏

1958-04-12
第5版()
专栏：

    纳赛尔月底访苏
    新华社开罗10日电　据官方今天宣布，阿拉伯联合共和国总统纳赛尔应苏联最高苏维埃主席团的邀请，将在4月底前往苏联访问。
    声明说，在访问期间，纳赛尔将同苏联领导人会见，并且将访问苏联各地，了解苏联人民的生活和苏联在各方面所获得的成就。
