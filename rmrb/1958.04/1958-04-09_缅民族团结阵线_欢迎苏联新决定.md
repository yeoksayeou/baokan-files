Title: Burmese National United Front Welcomes New Soviet Decision
Author:
Date: 1958-04-09
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, Rangoon, April 7th: The Burmese National United Front issued a statement today saying that the Soviet Union's decision to unilaterally cease nuclear weapons testing is the most encouraging event for the people of the world in current international affairs. The statement said that the people of the whole world unanimously demand the prohibition of testing atomic and hydrogen weapons that endanger the safety of the people of the world. The statement called on the United States and Britain to cease nuclear weapons testing as the Soviet Union has done.

### 缅民族团结阵线  欢迎苏联新决定

1958-04-09
第5版()
专栏：

    缅民族团结阵线  欢迎苏联新决定
    据新华社仰光7日电　缅甸民族团结阵线今天发表声明说，苏联决定单方面停止核武器试验是目前国际事务中使世界人民最感鼓舞的事情。声明说，全世界人民一致要求禁止试验危害世界人民安全的原子武器和氢武器。声明要求美国和英国像苏联一样地停止核武器试验。
