Title: Jilin Thermal Power Plant Phase I Project Completed
Author:
Date: 1958-04-01
Page: 3

Construction Bloom

    Jilin Thermal Power Plant Phase I Project Completed
    The last boiler of the first phase of the Jilin Thermal Power Plant was officially put into operation on the 28th. Thus far, the first phase of the plant has been fully completed.
    Construction of this thermal power plant began in January 1955. With the needs of Jilin's industrial development, the plant will continue to be expanded. The second phase of expansion began in February this year and is planned to be completed ahead of schedule this year. It is expected that the increase in power generation and heating capacity of this plant this year will be equal to the capacity built in the past three years. (Xinhua News Agency)

### 吉林热电厂第一期工程建成

1958-04-01
第3版()
专栏：建设的花朵

    吉林热电厂第一期工程建成
    吉林热电厂第一期工程的最后一台锅炉，在28日正式投入运行。至此，这个厂的第一期工程已全部建成。
    这座热电厂是在1955年1月动工建设的。随着吉林地区工业发展的需要，这个厂还将继续进行扩建。二期扩建工程已从今年2月开始，计划今年提前完成。预计这个厂今年一年增加的发电、供热能力，将等于过去三年建设的能力。　（新华社）
