Title: President Nasser Receives Ambassador Chen Jiakang
Author:
Date: 1958-04-03
Page: 5

    Xinhua News Agency, Cairo, April 1st: President Nasser of the United Arab Republic received Chinese Ambassador Chen Jiakang at his residence on the afternoon of April 1st. Chen Jiakang had recently returned to China for consultations and recently returned to Cairo. He conveyed the respects of Chairman Mao Zedong and Premier Zhou Enlai to President Nasser, and expressed the Chinese people's support for the Arab people's aspirations for unity and independence.

    Nasser discussed with Chen Jiakang various measures to strengthen economic and technical cooperation between China and the United Arab Republic, and exchanged views on the current situation in the Middle East and the international situation.

    They talked for two and a half hours. This was Chen Jiakang's first meeting with Nasser since returning to Cairo.

### 纳赛尔总统接见陈家康大使

1958-04-03
第5版()
专栏：

    纳赛尔总统
    接见陈家康大使
    新华社开罗1日电　阿拉伯联合共和国总统纳赛尔1日下午在他自己的寓所接见了中国大使陈家康。陈家康在不久前回国述职，最近回到开罗。他转达了毛泽东主席和周恩来总理对纳赛尔总统表示的敬意，并且表示中国人民支持阿拉伯人民争取统一和独立的愿望。
    纳赛尔同陈家康讨论了加强中国和阿拉伯联合共和国两国之间的经济和技术合作的各项措施，并且就当前的中东局势和国际局势交换了意见。
    他们交谈了两个半小时。这是陈家康回到开罗以后第一次同纳赛尔会晤。
