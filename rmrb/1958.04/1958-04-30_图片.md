Author: Xu Yonghui

    During the busy rice transplanting season, Pingyang County, Zhejiang Province, has widely adopted "rice transplanting boats" this year. This is a female commune member of the Zhanjiakeng Agricultural Cooperative transplanting rice while sitting on a rice transplanting boat. (Xinhua News Agency photo)
