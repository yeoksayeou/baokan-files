Title: Bulgarian Communist Party Central Committee Announces Draft of Third Five-Year Plan: Industrial Products to Increase by 60%, Agricultural Products to Increase by 35% Compared to Last Year
Author: 
Date: 1958-04-10
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, Sofia, 8th: According to a report by the Bulgarian Telegraph Agency today, the draft guidelines for Bulgaria's Third Five-Year Plan (1958-1962) stipulate that during the Five-Year Plan period, industrial products will increase by at least 60%, the total output of agricultural products will increase by approximately 35% compared to 1957, and national income will increase by approximately 50%.

The draft points out that the basic political and economic task of the new Five-Year Plan is to continue building a socialist society, expand the material production base, further consolidate socialist relations of production, and raise the socialist consciousness of the working people.

This draft was discussed and approved at the plenary meeting of the Central Committee of the Bulgarian Communist Party held on April 4th. The Central Committee plenary session decided to publish this draft in newspapers and periodicals for nationwide discussion.

Finally, this draft will be submitted to the Seventh Congress of the Bulgarian Communist Party, to be held on June 2nd, for discussion and adoption, and then submitted to the Council of Ministers as a resolution of the Congress for implementation.

The draft guidelines state that during the Third Five-Year Plan period, it is necessary to continue implementing the policy of national industrialization, prioritizing the development of heavy industry, ensuring a new upsurge in all sectors of the national economy based on the adoption of new technologies, better utilization of existing production capacity, and continuous improvement of labor productivity, and on this basis, ensuring a further improvement in the living and cultural standards of the people.

The draft stipulates that the basic tasks of the industrial sector during the new Five-Year Plan period are: to further develop electricity, coal mining, non-ferrous metallurgy, machine manufacturing, building materials, and other industries, and to pay special attention to the development of the ferrous metallurgy industry and the chemical industry. Over the five years, the production of means of production will increase by approximately 77%. The production of consumer goods will increase by approximately 50%. Over the five years, industrial production costs will be reduced by 12.5%.

The basic tasks in agriculture are: to utilize the possibilities created by the victory of the socialist cooperative system in the countryside, to greatly increase agricultural products, reduce production expenditures per unit product, and achieve stable high yields per unit area and high livestock productivity through further improving crop production, more rational utilization of land, and development of mechanization, fertilization, and irrigation.

The draft points out that with the increase in national income, the actual income of urban and rural working people will increase by at least 30% by the end of 1962 compared to 1957.

The draft also stipulates the basic tasks regarding transportation, basic construction investment, improving labor productivity, and practicing thrift. The draft also points out that based on improving labor productivity, the transition to a seven-hour workday will begin.

A portion of the draft describes the tremendous achievements of Bulgaria's national economy during the Second Five-Year Plan period. Currently, in Bulgaria's national economy, socialist components account for 98% of total industrial output, 87% of total agricultural output, and more than 99% of retail trade.

### 保共中央公布第三个五年计划草案  工业产品将增长60％　农业产品比去年增长35％

1958-04-10
第6版()
专栏：

    保共中央公布第三个五年计划草案
    工业产品将增长60％　农业产品比去年增长35％
    新华社索非亚8日电　据保加利亚通讯社今天报道，保加利亚第三个五年计划（1958—1962年）指示草案中规定：在五年计划期间，工业产品至少增长60％，农业产品总量将比1957年增长约35％，国民收入将增加约50％。
    草案指出，新五年计划的基本政治和经济任务是：继续建设社会主义社会，扩大物质生产基地，进一步巩固社会主义生产关系和提高劳动人民的社会主义觉悟。
    这个草案是在保加利亚共产党中央委员会4月4日举行的全体会议上讨论通过的。中央全会决定在报刊上发表这一草案，以供全民讨论。
    最后，这个草案将提交6月2日召开的保共第七次代表大会讨论和通过，然后作为大会的决议提交部长会议执行。
    指示草案说，在第三个五年计划期间，必须继续执行国家工业化的方针，优先发展重工业，在采用新技术、更好地利用现有生产能力和不断提高劳动生产率的基础上保证国民经济一切部门的新高涨，并在这一基础上保证进一步提高人民的生活和文化水平。
    草案规定新五年计划期间工业部门的基本任务是：进一步发展电力、采煤、有色冶金、机器制造、建筑材料等工业，并特别注意发展黑色冶金业和化学工业。五年中，生产资料的生产将增长约77％。消费品生产将增长约50％。五年中，工业生产成本将降低12.5％。
    农业方面的基本任务是：利用社会主义合作制在农村中的胜利所创造的可能性，通过进一步提高农作物生产、更加合理地利用土地、发展机械化、施肥、灌溉等措施，大大增加农产品，减少单位产品的生产支出，达到单位面积的稳定的丰收和高额的畜产品生产率。
    草案指出，随着国民收入的增加，到1962年年底城乡劳动人民的实际收入将比1957年至少增加30％。
    草案还规定了关于交通运输、基本建设投资、提高劳动生产率、厉行节约等方面的基本任务。草案还指出，将在提高劳动生产率的基础上，开始向七小时工作制过渡。
    草案有一部分叙述了保加利亚国民经济在第二个五年计划期间所取得的巨大成就。目前，在保加利亚国民经济中，社会主义成分已占工业总产量的98％，占农业总产量的87％，占零售贸易额的99％以上。
