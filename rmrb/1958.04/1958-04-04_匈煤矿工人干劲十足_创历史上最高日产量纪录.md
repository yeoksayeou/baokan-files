Title: Hungarian Coal Miners Work with Great Enthusiasm, Creating the Highest Daily Output Record in History
Author:
Date: 1958-04-04
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, Budapest, April 2nd: On the eve of the Hungarian National Day on April 4th, Hungarian coal miners have created the highest daily coal output record in Hungarian history - 89,700 tons.

    Starting from March 24th, many miners launched a week-long labor competition to welcome the National Day. During this week, the national daily coal output increased rapidly every day, from 82,146 tons to 89,700 tons.

### 匈煤矿工人干劲十足  创历史上最高日产量纪录

1958-04-04
第5版()
专栏：

    匈煤矿工人干劲十足
    创历史上最高日产量纪录
    据新华社布达佩斯2日电　在4月4日匈牙利国庆节的前夕，匈牙利煤矿工人已经创造了匈牙利历史上煤的日产量的最高纪录——八万九千七百吨。
    从3月24日起，许多矿工为迎接国庆节展开了为期一周的劳动竞赛。在这一周里，全国煤的日产量每天都在迅速上升，从八万二千一百四十六吨一直增加到八万九千七百吨。
