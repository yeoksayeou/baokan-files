Title: Beautiful Porcelain Plates (Picture)
Author: Yang Tingliang
Date: 1958-04-05
Page: 3

    Beautiful Porcelain Plates
    Workers at the Xi'an People's Enamel Factory burned paintings by famous artists onto porcelain plates in order to beautify their products. The picture on the left shows shrimp painted by Qi Baishi, and the picture on the right shows magpies painted by Xu Beihong. Yang Tingliang photographed (Xinhua News Agency draft)

### 美丽的瓷盘（图片）
杨廷良
1958-04-05
第3版()
专栏：

    美丽的瓷盘
    西安人民搪瓷厂职工为了美化产品，把一些名画家的画烧在了瓷盘上。图左是齐白石画的虾，图右是徐悲鸿画的喜鹊。	　杨廷良摄（新华社稿）
