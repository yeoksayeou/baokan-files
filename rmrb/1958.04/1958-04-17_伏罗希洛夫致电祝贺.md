Title: Voroshilov Sends Congratulatory Telegram

Author:

Date: 1958-04-17
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, 16th: According to TASS, Moscow, 16th, the Chairman of the Presidium of the Supreme Soviet of the Soviet Union, Voroshilov, sent a telegram on April 15th to the Chairman of the Conference of Independent African States, congratulating him on the convening of the conference.

The telegram said, "For centuries, the people of the great African continent have been engaged in a heroic struggle against the oppression of colonialism. Now, a series of African countries have won their independence. Their free development and the great achievements they have made are an inspiring example to all other African countries. We believe that no force can stop the African people's desire for independence and freedom."

Voroshilov also expressed his hope in the telegram that the conference would successfully complete the tasks it faces in order to benefit the African people and consolidate world peace.

At the end of the opening session, congratulatory telegrams from many countries, including China, were read.

The Conference Adopts Five Agenda Items

On the same day in the afternoon, the Conference of Independent African States unanimously adopted the conference agenda at a closed meeting.

According to a communique issued by the conference secretariat, the agenda includes the following five points:

I. Exchange of views on the following issues: foreign policy, especially foreign policy related to Africa, the future of places in Africa that are not independent, the Algerian question, racial issues, and measures to protect the independence of African countries.

II. Study ways to promote economic cooperation between African countries, which will be based on the exchange of information on technology, science, and education, with special attention to industrial planning and agricultural development.

III. Formulate specific recommendations regarding the mutual dispatch of government and non-governmental delegations among countries in the area of cultural exchange.

IV. Study issues of international peace and adherence to the United Nations Charter and reaffirm the principles of the Bandung Conference.

V. Establish a permanent body after the conference ends.

At the afternoon meeting, it was also decided that Ghana's Prime Minister Nkrumah would serve as the chairman of the conference.

### 伏罗希洛夫致电祝贺

1958-04-17
第5版()
专栏：

    伏罗希洛夫致电祝贺
    新华社16日讯　据塔斯社莫斯科16日讯：苏联最高苏维埃主席团主席伏罗希洛夫在4月15日打电报给非洲独立国家会议主席，对会议的召开表示祝贺。
    电报说，“几百年来，伟大的非洲大陆人民为反对殖民主义的压迫进行着英勇的斗争。现在已经有一系列的非洲国家争取到了自己的独立。它们的自由发展和它们所取得的大量成就对其他一切非洲国家是一个鼓舞的榜样。我们相信，没有任何力量可以阻止非洲人民争取独立和自由的愿望。”
    伏罗希洛夫在电报中还表示希望，会议将能顺利完成它所面临的任务，以造福非洲人民，巩固世界和平。
    在开幕会议结束时宣读了从包括中国在内的许多国家发来的贺电。
    会议通过五项议程
    同一天下午，非洲独立国家会议在秘密会议上一致通过了会议议程。
    据会议秘书处发表的公报说，议程包括如下五点：
    一、就下列问题交换意见：外交政策，特别是有关非洲的外交政策，非洲没有独立的地方的前途，阿尔及利亚问题，种族问题和保卫非洲独立国家的措施。
    二、研究促进非洲国家之间的经济合作的办法，这种合作将以交换关于技术、科学和教育方面的情报为基础，特别注意工业计划和农业发展。
    三、在文化交流方面拟订关于各国互派政府代表团和非政府代表团的具体建议。
    四、研究国际和平和遵守联合国宪章的问题并且重申万隆会议的原则。
    五、在会议结束后成立一个常设机构。
    下午会议上还决定由加纳总理恩克鲁玛担任会议主席。
