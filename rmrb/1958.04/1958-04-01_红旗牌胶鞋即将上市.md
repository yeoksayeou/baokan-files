Title: Red Flag Brand Rubber Shoes to be Launched Soon
Author: Zhou Dezai
Date: 1958-04-01
Page: 3

Column: Blossoms of Construction

    Red Flag Brand Rubber Shoes to be Launched Soon
    The first new-type rubber factory in Nanchang City—the local state-owned Nanchang Rubber Factory—partially commenced production on March 18. The first batch of Red Flag brand Yuanbao rubber shoes produced by this factory, after inspection, fully meets the design standards in terms of quality.
    After the factory is fully put into production, it is expected to produce about three million pairs of various rubber shoes this year (including Yuanbao rubber shoes, light boots, various colored sneakers, and Lishi shoes).

### 红旗牌胶鞋即将上市

1958-04-01
第3版()
专栏：建设的花朵

    红旗牌胶鞋即将上市
    南昌市第一座新型橡胶工厂——地方国营南昌橡胶厂，已于3月18日部分投入生产。这个厂第一批生产的红旗牌元宝胶鞋，经过检验，质量完全符合设计标准。
    这个工厂全部投入生产后，预计今年就可生产各种胶鞋（包括元宝胶鞋，轻便靴、各色长球鞋、力士鞋）三百万双左右。（周德载）
