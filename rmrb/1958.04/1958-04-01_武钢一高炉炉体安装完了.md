Title: The Wuhan Iron and Steel Corporation (WISCO) No. 1 Blast Furnace Body Installation Completed
Author:
Date: 1958-04-01
Page: 3

Construction Flowers

    Xinhua News Agency, Wuhan, March 31st. The installation project for the WISCO No. 1 blast furnace body was completed today. The metal shell installation of the three hot blast stoves of this blast furnace has also been completed recently. Now, when people go to the WISCO construction site, they can see the tall figures of these buildings from miles away.

    In order to meet the needs of starting production ahead of schedule on October 1 this year, the production preparation work of WISCO No. 1 blast furnace is also being accelerated. In addition, WISCO is rushing to repair the water terminal and pig iron warehouse to meet the needs of transportation and pig iron storage after the No. 1 blast furnace is put into production.

### 武钢一高炉炉体安装完了

1958-04-01
第3版()
专栏：建设的花朵

    武钢一高炉炉体安装完了
    据新华社武汉31日电　武钢一号高炉炉体安装工程今天竣工。这座高炉的三座热风炉的金属外壳安装工程也在最近先后完成了。现在人们到武钢工地去，在几里以外就能看到这些建筑物的高大的身躯。
    为了适应提前在今年10月1日开工生产的需要，武钢一号高炉的生产准备工作也在快马加鞭地进行着。此外，武钢正在赶修水上码头和生铁仓库，以适应一号高炉生产后的运输和生铁堆放的需要。
