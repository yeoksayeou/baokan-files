Title: Want to Know Where the Wire is Faulty?
Author:
Date: 1958-04-17
Page: 3

New Products

    Want to know where the wire is faulty?
    An instrument that can check the location of faults in wires within 350 kilometers in five minutes has recently been successfully trial-produced by the Tianjin Public-Private Joint Electronic Instrument Factory. This instrument, the Type 732 Line Fault Pulse Tester, has been tested by relevant units organized by the General Administration of Long Distance Telecommunications of the Ministry of Posts and Telecommunications. The results show that it has good performance, high sensitivity and accuracy.

### 想知道电线在哪里出毛病吗？

1958-04-17
第3版()
专栏：新产品

    想知道电线在哪里出毛病吗？
    五分钟就检查出三百五十公里内电线发生障碍的地点的一种仪器，最近已在天津市公私合营电子仪器厂试制成功。这种仪器——七三二型线障脉冲测试器，经邮电部长途电信总局组织有关单位试验的结果，性能良好，灵敏度和准确度都很高。
