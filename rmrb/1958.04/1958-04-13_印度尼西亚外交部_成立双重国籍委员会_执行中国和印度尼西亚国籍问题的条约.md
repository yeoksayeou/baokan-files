Title: Indonesian Foreign Ministry Establishes Dual Nationality Committee to Implement Treaty on Nationality Issues Between China and Indonesia
Author:
Date: 1958-04-13
Page: 3
	
    Indonesian Foreign Ministry
    Establishes Dual Nationality Committee
    To Implement Treaty on Nationality Issues Between China and Indonesia
    Xinhua News Agency, Jakarta, April 12th. The Indonesian Foreign Ministry established a "Dual Nationality Committee" on the 12th to handle matters related to the implementation of the treaty between China and Indonesia on dual nationality issues.
    According to Antara News Agency, the committee, headed by Sujono of the Foreign Ministry, has a total of fifteen members, composed of representatives from the Cabinet's foreign affairs, justice, internal affairs, intelligence, and defense departments, as well as representatives from immigration, procuratorate, and public security organs.

### 印度尼西亚外交部  成立双重国籍委员会  执行中国和印度尼西亚国籍问题的条约

1958-04-13
第3版()
专栏：

    印度尼西亚外交部
    成立双重国籍委员会
    执行中国和印度尼西亚国籍问题的条约
    新华社雅加达12日电　印度尼西亚外交部12日成立一个“双重国籍委员会”来处理有关执行中国和印度尼西亚关于双重国籍问题的条约。
    据安塔拉通讯社报道，这个以外交部的苏佐诺为首的委员会一共有十五个委员，由内阁的外交、司法、内政、情报和国防等部门的代表以及移民、检察和公安机关的代表组成。
