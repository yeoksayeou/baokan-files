Title: Norwegian Labour Party Member of Parliament Finn Moe Leaves Beijing for Moscow
Author:
Date: 1958-04-24
Page: 4

    Xinhua News Agency, April 22nd: Finn Moe, a Norwegian Labour Party member of parliament and chairman of the Parliamentary Foreign Affairs and Constitution Committee, and his wife, who were invited to visit China by the Chinese People's Institute of Foreign Affairs, left Beijing for Moscow by plane today.

### 挪威工党议员芬·穆离京去莫斯科

1958-04-24
第4版()
专栏：

    挪威工党议员芬·穆离京去莫斯科
    据新华社22日讯　应中国人民外交学会邀请来我国访问的挪威工党议员，议会外交、宪法委员会主席芬·穆和他的夫人，已经在今天乘飞机离开北京去莫斯科。
