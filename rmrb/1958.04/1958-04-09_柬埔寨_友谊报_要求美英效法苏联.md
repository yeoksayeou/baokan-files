Title: Cambodia's "Friendship News" Demands US and Britain Follow the Example of the Soviet Union
Author:
Date: 1958-04-09
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, Phnom Penh, April 8th: Cambodia's "Friendship News" published an editorial on the 7th, welcoming the Soviet Union's decision to halt nuclear weapons testing.

The editorial said that now, disregarding the indifferent attitude of the United States and Britain on this issue, the Soviet Union has decided to unilaterally halt nuclear weapons testing. The Cambodian people wholeheartedly welcome this action by the Soviet Union. The editorial demanded that the United States and Britain follow the example of the Soviet Union and halt nuclear weapons testing to safeguard human security and progress.

### 柬埔寨“友谊报”  要求美英效法苏联

1958-04-09
第5版()
专栏：

    柬埔寨“友谊报”
    要求美英效法苏联
    据新华社金边8日电　柬埔寨“友谊报”在7日发表社论，欢迎苏联关于停止核武器试验的决定。
    社论说，现在，苏联不顾美国和英国在这个问题上采取的置之不理的态度，决定苏联单方面停止核武器试验。柬埔寨人民无限欢迎苏联的这一行动。社论要求美英两国效法苏联，停止核武器试验，以保障人类的安全和进步。
