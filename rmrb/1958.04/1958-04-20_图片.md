    In order to avoid delaying planting due to drought, the peasants of Wushan County, Gansu Province, have launched a mass campaign to "bring water up the mountains" and "compete with the Wei River for land." In areas where canals cannot be built, they are using high-line water transport to plant crops, ensuring that spring sowing is not delayed. The picture shows members of the Xingguang Agricultural Cooperative using high-line to transport water. Xinhua News Agency reporter Li Feng.
