Title: Polish Guests Visit Guangzhou
Author:
Date: 1958-04-06
Page: 2
Xinhua News Agency, Guangzhou, April 5th. The Polish government delegation headed by Vice Chairman Jaroszewicz, accompanied by Guangzhou Deputy Mayor Sun Leyi, visited Huangpu Port in the suburbs of Guangzhou this morning, visited the Polish merchant ship "Kosciuszko" docked in Huangpu Port, and finally took a small motorboat from Huangpu to the Guangzhou city area.

In the afternoon, the guests also visited China's Export Commodities Exhibition Hall. The guests greatly appreciated China's export commodities.

In the evening, Guangdong Province Governor Chen Yu hosted a banquet for the guests.

### 波兰贵宾在广州参观

1958-04-06
第2版()
专栏：

    波兰贵宾在广州参观
    新华社广州5日电　以雅罗谢维奇副主席为首的波兰政府代表团今天上午由广州市副市长孙乐宜陪同游览了广州郊区的黄埔港，访问了停泊在黄埔港内的波兰商船克修斯戈号，最后乘小汽船从黄埔漫游到广州市区。
    下午，贵宾们又参观了我国出口商品陈列馆，贵宾们对我国的出口商品很赞赏。
    晚上，广东省省长陈郁设宴招待贵宾。
