Title: Temperature Drop Tomorrow Morning
Author:
Date: 1958-04-06
Page: 2
Weather Forecast Station

Temperature Drop Tomorrow Morning

(Our newspaper) News dispatch: The Central Meteorological Science Research Institute released a temperature drop forecast at 8:00 AM on the 5th:

It is expected that on the morning of the 7th, the lowest temperature in southern Shanxi, the Hebei Plain, and Shandong will drop to 3°C-5°C, and some areas will have frost.

In the next 24 hours, the Northeast will have northwesterly winds of force 7-8, and the Bohai Sea, the northern Yellow Sea, and the Shandong Peninsula will have northerly winds of about force 6.

### 明晨降温

1958-04-06
第2版()
专栏：气象台

    明晨降温
    本报讯　中央气象科学研究所5日八时发布降温预报：
    预计7日早晨，山西南部、河北平原、山东，最低气温将下降到3℃—5℃，部分地区将有霜冻。
    未来二十四小时内，东北将有七、八级的西北风，渤海、黄海北部、山东半岛，将有六级左右的偏北风。
