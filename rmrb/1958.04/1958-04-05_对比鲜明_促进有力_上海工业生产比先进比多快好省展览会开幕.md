Title: Sharp Contrasts, Powerful Promotion: Shanghai Industrial Production Competition for Advanced, More, Faster, Better, and More Economical Exhibition Opens

Author: 
Date: 1958-04-05
Page: 3

Xinhua News Agency Dispatch: The Shanghai Industrial Production Competition for Advanced, More, Faster, Better, and More Economical Exhibition opened on the 1st at the Sino-Soviet Friendship Building in Shanghai. The exhibition focuses on showcasing advanced products and advanced experiences from various industries and factories. It also displays products that were originally advanced but later became backward, and products that were originally backward but later became advanced, along with their development and changes. Through the sharp contrast between the advanced and backward in product quality and production costs, the exhibition will play a timely and powerful role in encouraging and promoting current production.

### 对比鲜明　促进有力  上海工业生产比先进比多快好省展览会开幕

1958-04-05
第3版()
专栏：

    对比鲜明　促进有力
    上海工业生产比先进比多快好省展览会开幕
    据新华社讯　上海市工业生产比先进比多快好省展览会，1日在上海中苏友好大厦开幕。展览会集中地展出了各行业各厂的先进产品、先进经验；也展出了那些原来是先进的、后来落后了，原来是落后的、后来又先进了的产品，及其发展变化的情况。展览会通过各种产品质量之间、生产成本之间先进与落后的鲜明对比，将对当前生产起到及时的有力的鼓励和促进作用。
