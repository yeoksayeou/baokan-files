Title: Chinese People's Organizations Donate to Help the Algerian People
Author: 
Date: 1958-04-09
Page: 5
Xinhua News Agency, 8th. The Chinese Committee for Asian Solidarity, the All-China Federation of Trade Unions, and the China Islamic Association, responding to the call of the Asian-African People's Solidarity Council, expressed the Chinese people's sympathy and support for the Algerian people's struggle for national independence, and decided to donate 500,000 yuan in Renminbi. This donation will be transferred to the Algerian National Liberation Front through the Permanent Secretariat of the Asian-African People's Solidarity Council in Cairo.

### 我国人民团体捐款帮助阿尔及利亚人民

1958-04-09
第5版()
专栏：

    我国人民团体捐款帮助阿尔及利亚人民
    新华社8日讯　中国亚洲团结委员会、中华全国总工会和中国伊斯兰教协会响应亚非人民团结理事会的号召，表示中国人民对阿尔及利亚人民争取民族独立斗争的同情和支持，决定捐助人民币五十万元。这一项捐款将经开罗亚非人民团结理事会常设秘书处转交阿尔及利亚民族解放阵线。
