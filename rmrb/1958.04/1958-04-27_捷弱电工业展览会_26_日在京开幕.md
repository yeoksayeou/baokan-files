Title: Czechoslovak Weak Current Electrical Industry Exhibition Opens in Beijing on the 26th
Author:
Date: 1958-04-27
Page: 2

Xinhua News Agency, April 26th. The Czechoslovak Weak Current Electrical Industry Exhibition opened in Beijing this morning.

The exhibition was organized by the Commercial Office of the Czechoslovak Embassy in China.

At the opening ceremony, Mr. Ševčík, Commercial Counselor of the Czechoslovak Embassy in China, introduced the development of Czechoslovakia's weak current electrical industry to the visitors. He expressed his hope that the exhibition would further strengthen technological cooperation between China and Czechoslovakia.

The exhibition features more than fifty exhibits, including several types of radio and television receivers, electronic measuring instruments, microwave measuring equipment, radio transmitters, and industrial television equipment.

The Commercial Office of the Czechoslovak Embassy in China will also hold various reports and technical seminars introducing new products in the field of Czechoslovak weak current electrical industry during the exhibition.

The exhibition will close on May 17th.

### 捷弱电工业展览会26日在京开幕

1958-04-27
第2版()
专栏：

    捷弱电工业展览会26日在京开幕
    新华社26日讯　捷克斯洛伐克弱电工业展览会今天上午在北京开幕。
    这次展览会是由捷克斯洛伐克驻华大使馆商务处举办的。
    捷驻华大使馆商务参赞舍夫契克在开幕式上向前来参观的人介绍了捷弱电工业发展的情况。他希望通过展览会进一步加强中捷两国的技术合作。
    展览会上展出了几种类型的广播电视接收机、电子测量仪器、测量微波设备、无线电发报机和工业电视设备等共五十多件展品。
    捷驻华大使馆商务处在展览期间还将举行各种介绍捷弱电工业方面新产品的报告会和技术座谈会。
    展览会将在5月17日结束。
