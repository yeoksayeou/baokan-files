Title: Bolivian University Students Hold Anti-American Demonstration
Author:
Date: 1958-04-25
Page: 5
    Xinhua News Agency, April 24th - According to a TASS dispatch from Buenos Aires on April 23rd: According to the "Democracia" newspaper, on the 22nd, university students in La Paz, the capital of Bolivia, held an anti-American demonstration to protest the transfer of the country's largest oil-producing area to an American company. After the demonstration, the students held a mass rally near the government building.

### 玻利维亚大学生举行反美示威游行

1958-04-25
第5版()
专栏：

    玻利维亚大学生举行反美示威游行
    据新华社24日讯　据塔斯社布宜诺斯艾利斯23日讯：据“民主报”报道，22日玻利维亚首都拉巴斯的大学生举行反美示威游行，抗议把全国最大的石油产地转让给美国公司。大学生在游行以后在政府大厦附近举行了群众大会。
