Title: Statement by the Prime Minister of Saudi Arabia: Pursuing a Policy of Active Neutrality
Author: 
Date: 1958-04-21
Page: 5
Xinhua News Agency, Cairo, April 19th: Saudi Arabian Prime Minister Prince Faisal broadcast a statement on Mecca Radio on the evening of the 18th, affirming that his country pursues a policy of active neutrality and opposes joining any foreign alliance.

    The statement said that the Saudi Arabian government will continue to maintain friendly relations with all countries that do not take hostile actions against it. The statement said that the establishment of alliances must be initiated by the Arab countries themselves.

    Regarding relations with Eastern countries, the statement said that the existing relations between Saudi Arabia and these countries are based on the Charter of the United Nations.

    The statement said that the Saudi Arabian government regrets the United States' attitude on the issue of the Gulf of Aqaba. The statement believes that the United States' attitude supports the aggression of Egypt by Britain, France, and Israel, and expresses the hope that the United States will change its attitude on this issue.

    The statement mentioned the conditions for Saudi Arabia to restore relations with Britain and France: Britain agrees to resolve the dispute over the Buraimi Oasis and other territorial disputes through arbitration; France agrees to grant Algeria independence.

    The statement said that the only way to resolve the Israeli issue is to restore the rights of the Palestinian Arabs and compensate them for the losses they suffered due to the aggression.

    The statement concluded by saying that the Saudi Arabian government will assume the obligations and responsibilities of the Arab League Charter and related agreements. The Saudi Arabian government will make its best efforts to restore harmonious relations between Arab countries.

### 沙特阿拉伯首相声明  奉行积极中立政策

1958-04-21
第5版()
专栏：

    沙特阿拉伯首相声明
    奉行积极中立政策
    据新华社开罗19日电　沙特阿拉伯首相费萨尔王储18日晚上在麦加电台广播一项声明，肯定他的国家奉行积极中立和反对参加任何外国联盟的政策。
    这项声明说，沙特阿拉伯政府将继续同一切不对它采取敌对行动的国家保持友好关系。声明说，建立联盟必需由阿拉伯国家自己发起。
    关于同东方国家的关系，声明说，沙特阿拉伯同这些国家现在存在的关系是以联合国宪章为依据的。
    声明说，沙特阿拉伯政府对美国在亚喀巴湾问题上的态度表示遗憾。声明认为美国的态度是支持英国、法国和以色列对埃及的侵略，并且表示希望美国改变它在这个问题上的态度。
    声明提到沙特阿拉伯同英国和法国恢复关系的条件：英国同意通过仲裁来解决布赖米绿洲和其它领土纠纷；法国同意给予阿尔及利亚独立。
    声明说，解决以色列问题的唯一办法是恢复巴勒斯坦阿拉伯人的权利，赔偿他们因侵略而受到的损失。
    声明最后说，沙特阿拉伯政府将承担对阿拉伯国家联盟公约和有关协议的义务和责任。沙特阿拉伯政府将尽最大的努力恢复阿拉伯国家之间的融洽关系。
