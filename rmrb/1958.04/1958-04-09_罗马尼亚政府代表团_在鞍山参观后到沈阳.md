Title: Romanian Government Delegation Visits Anshan and Arrives in Shenyang
Author:
Date: 1958-04-09
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency, Anshan, 8th April. The Romanian government delegation, headed by Chairman Chivu Stoica, arrived in Anshan by special plane from Beijing at 10:45 this morning for a visit.

    Li Weimin, Mayor of Anshan, Wang Yixin, Deputy Mayor, Yang Shijie, Secretary of the Anshan Municipal Committee of the Chinese Communist Party, Ma Bin and Liu Jingqing, Deputy Managers of Anshan Iron and Steel, welcomed the distinguished guests at the airport.

    After listening to Ma Bin, Deputy Manager of Anshan Iron and Steel, at the Dongshan Hotel, about the production and construction of Anshan Iron and Steel since liberation, Chairman Chivu Stoica and other members of the delegation took a car to visit the Anshan Iron and Steel Ironmaking Plant, the No. 2 Steelmaking Plant, the Large Rolling Mill, and the Seamless Steel Tube Plant.

    The distinguished guests were warmly welcomed by the workers in front of the open hearth furnace of the No. 2 Steelmaking Plant. The No. 1 open hearth furnace – the Youth Solidarity Furnace – which was tapping steel, aroused great interest among the distinguished guests, and they watched the pouring of the steel ingots. Chairman Chivu Stoica frequently shook hands with the workers and took photos with them in front of the open hearth furnace.

    After the distinguished guests left the No. 2 Steelmaking Plant, they were welcomed by hundreds of workers gathered in front of the gate. Chairman Chivu Stoica said excitedly to the steelworkers: I bring you warm greetings from the Romanian steelworkers. Chairman Chivu Stoica and Vice-Chairman Emil Bodnăraş inquired about production in the ironmaking plant and other places, and introduced the situation of the steel industry in Romania.

    After the visit, the Romanian guests accepted a souvenir presented by Ma Bin, Deputy Manager of Anshan Iron and Steel Company on behalf of the company – a pattern symbolizing the worker-peasant alliance made of various steel materials produced by Anshan Iron and Steel.

    At 5 p.m., the distinguished guests left Anshan by special plane for Shenyang.

    Accompanying the distinguished guests today were Zeng Yongquan, Vice Minister of Foreign Affairs, and Ke Bonian, Chinese Ambassador to Romania.

    Xinhua News Agency, Shenyang, 8th April. The Romanian government delegation, headed by Chairman Chivu Stoica, arrived in Shenyang by special plane from Anshan at 5:30 this afternoon for a visit.

    As the distinguished guests stepped off the plane, they shook hands with Du Zheheng, Governor of Liaoning Province, Wang Zheng, Secretary of the Liaoning Provincial Committee of the Chinese Communist Party, Zhang Like, Deputy Mayor of Shenyang, Xu Shaofu, Secretary of the Shenyang Municipal Committee of the Chinese Communist Party, and Lieutenant General Cheng Shicai, Commander of the Shenyang Garrison, who were waiting for them at the airport, and accepted flowers presented by the Young Pioneers of Shenyang.

### 罗马尼亚政府代表团  在鞍山参观后到沈阳

1958-04-09
第4版()
专栏：

    罗马尼亚政府代表团
    在鞍山参观后到沈阳
    新华社鞍山8日电　以基伏·斯托伊卡主席为首的罗马尼亚政府代表团，今天上午十时四十五分乘专机由北京来鞍山参观。
    鞍山市市长李维民，副市长王一新、中共鞍山市委书记杨士杰，鞍钢副经理马宾、刘镜清等人在机场欢迎贵宾。
    基伏·斯托伊卡主席和其他团员们在东山宾馆听取了鞍钢副经理马宾对鞍钢解放以来的生产建设情况的介绍以后，乘车到厂区参观了鞍钢炼铁厂、第二炼钢厂、大型轧钢厂和无缝钢管厂。
    贵宾们在第二炼钢厂平炉前，受到了工人们的热烈欢迎。正在出钢的一号平炉——青年团结炉引起了贵宾们的很大兴趣，他们观看了倾注钢锭的情况。基伏·斯托伊卡主席频频和工人握手，并且在平炉前和工人们合影。
    贵宾们走出第二炼钢厂后，又受到了齐集门前的上百个工人的欢迎。基伏·斯托伊卡主席兴奋地对钢铁工人们说：我从罗马尼亚钢铁工人那里给你们带来了热情的问候。基伏·斯托伊卡主席和埃米尔·波德纳拉希副主席在炼铁厂等地询问了生产情况，并且介绍了罗马尼亚国内钢铁工业的情况。
    参观后，罗马尼亚贵宾们接受了鞍钢公司副经理马宾代表鞍钢公司赠送的一件纪念品——一个用鞍钢生产的各种钢材缀成的象征工农联盟的图案。
    午后五点，贵宾们乘专机离鞍山赴沈阳。
    今天陪同贵宾前来的，有我外交部副部长曾涌泉、中国驻罗马尼亚大使柯柏年。
    据新华社沈阳8日电　以基伏·斯托伊卡主席为首的罗马尼亚政府代表团，今天下午五时三十分由鞍山乘专机到达沈阳访问。
    贵宾们走下飞机，同在机场迎候他们的辽宁省省长杜者蘅、中共辽宁省委书记王铮、沈阳市副市长张力克、中共沈阳市委书记徐少甫和沈阳驻军首长程世才中将等一一握手，并接受了沈阳市少先队员们的献花。
