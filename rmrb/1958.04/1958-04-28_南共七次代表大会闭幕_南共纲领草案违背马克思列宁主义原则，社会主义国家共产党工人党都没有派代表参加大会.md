Title: Seventh Congress of the League of Communists of Yugoslavia Concludes: Draft Program of the League of Communists of Yugoslavia Violates Marxist-Leninist Principles; Communist and Workers' Parties of Socialist Countries Did Not Send Representatives to the Congress
Author:
Date: 1958-04-28
Page: 5
Xinhua News Agency, Ljubljana, 26th—The Seventh Congress of the League of Communists of Yugoslavia concluded here this afternoon.

    Today's congress passed resolutions approving the work of the Central Committee of the League of Communists of Yugoslavia from the Sixth to the Seventh Congress; resolutions approving the various reports made at the congress; the program of the League of Communists of Yugoslavia, which was revised and supplemented based on discussions at the congress and prior to the congress; revisions and supplements to the Statute of the League of Communists of Yugoslavia; and resolutions on the current tasks of the League of Communists of Yugoslavia.

    After the congress elected a new Central Committee and Central Inspection Committee, Tito made a brief speech at the meeting.

    The newly elected Central Committee met today and elected a Central Executive Committee composed of fifteen members (one of whom was elected for the first time) and a Secretariat composed of five members. Tito was elected General Secretary. In addition to Tito, the members of the Secretariat are Kardelj, Ranković, Vukmanović, and Gošnjak.

    Because many of the arguments put forward in the draft program of the League of Communists of Yugoslavia are contrary to the spirit of the declaration of the Moscow Meeting of Representatives of Communist and Workers' Parties of Socialist Countries and violate the fundamental principles of Marxism-Leninism, the Communist and Workers' Parties of all socialist countries did not send delegations to this congress of the League of Communists of Yugoslavia. With the exception of the communist parties of three countries, the communist and workers' parties of capitalist countries also did not send delegations or representatives to the congress of the League of Communists of Yugoslavia. ### 南共七次代表大会闭幕  南共纲领草案违背马克思列宁主义原则，社会主义国家共产党工人党都没有派代表参加大会

1958-04-28
第5版()
专栏：

    南共七次代表大会闭幕
    南共纲领草案违背马克思列宁主义原则，社会主义国家共产党工人党都没有派代表参加大会
    新华社卢布尔雅那26日电　南斯拉夫共产主义者联盟第七次代表大会，今天下午在这里闭幕。
    今天的大会通过决议，批准了南共中央委员会从第六次到第七次代表大会期间的工作；通过了关于批准在代表大会上所作的各项报告的决议；通过了根据代表大会和大会以前的讨论作了修改、补充的南共纲领；通过了对南共章程的修改和补充；通过了关于南共当前任务的决议。
    在代表大会选举了新的中央委员会和中央检查委员会之后，铁托在会上作了简短讲话。
    新选出的中央委员会今天开会，选出了由十五人组成（其中一人是第一次当选）的中央执行委员会和由五人组成的书记处。铁托当选为总书记。书记处的成员除铁托外，还有卡德尔、兰科维奇、伏克曼诺维奇和戈什尼亚克。
    由于南共纲领草案中所提出的许多论点，同莫斯科社会主义国家共产党和工人党代表会议的宣言的精神相对立，同马克思列宁主义的根本原则相违背，因此所有社会主义国家的共产党和工人党都没有派遣代表团参加南共的这次代表大会。资本主义国家的共产党和工人党，除了三个国家的共产主义政党外，也都没有派遣代表团或代表参加南共的这次代表大会。保加利亚共产党、匈牙利社会主义工人党、越南劳动党、德国统一社会党、中国共产党、蒙古人民革命党、波兰统一工人党、罗马尼亚工人党、苏联共产党、印度尼西亚共产党、意大利共产党、突尼斯共产党和冰岛统一社会党，都只派观察员列席了代表大会。
    但是，社会党方面派遣代表团或代表参加大会的，有亚洲社会党会议、日本社会党、以色列统一劳工党、智利社会党和意大利社会党。
    此外，阿尔及利亚民族解放阵线、摩洛哥独立党、喀麦隆人民联盟派代表团或代表出席了大会。阿拉伯联合共和国民族联盟派观察员列席了大会。
