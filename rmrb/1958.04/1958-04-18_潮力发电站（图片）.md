Title: Tidal Power Station (Picture)
Author: Zhao Su-fang
Date: 1958-04-18
Page: 3

Tidal Power Station
Our country's first tidal power station is under construction in Xinglin Port, Xiamen. This power station relies on the tidal flow of high and low tides to drive generators to generate electricity. After its completion this year, it will have 500 kilowatts of power to supply local towns and agricultural cooperatives. This is the dam foundation of the hydroelectric power station under construction. Photo by Zhao Su-fang (Xinhua News Agency manuscript)

### 潮力发电站（图片）
赵肃芳
1958-04-18
第3版()
专栏：

    潮力发电站
    我国第一座潮力发电站，正在厦门杏林港兴建。这座发电站是依靠涨潮和退潮的水流动力，来推动发电机发电的。今年建成后将有五百瓩的电源供应当地乡镇和农业社用。这是正在施工的水电站坝基。		　赵肃芳摄（新华社稿）
