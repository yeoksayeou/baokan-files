Title: Nie Rongzhen Receives the Vice-President of the Soviet Academy of Sciences
Author:
Date: 1958-04-27
Page: 2

    Nie Rongzhen Receives the Vice-President of the Soviet Academy of Sciences
    Xinhua News Agency, April 26th. Vice Premier of the State Council Nie Rongzhen received today afternoon the Vice-President of the Soviet Academy of Sciences, the President of the Siberian Branch, and famous expert in mathematical mechanics, Academician M.A. Lavrentyev.
    Present at the meeting were Pei Lisheng, Secretary-General of the Chinese Academy of Sciences, Wu Heng, Deputy Secretary-General of the Science Planning Commission of the State Council, Wang Shuntong, and others.
    In the evening, Guo Moruo, President of the Chinese Academy of Sciences, hosted a banquet to bid farewell to Vice-President Lavrentyev and his wife, as well as the famous Soviet theoretical physicist Academician I.E. Tamm.
    Also attending were Wu Youxun, Vice-President of the Chinese Academy of Sciences, Pei Lisheng, Secretary-General, and renowned scientists Qian Xuesen, Qian Sanqiang, Hua Luogeng, and many others.

### 聂荣臻接见苏联科学院副院长

1958-04-27
第2版()
专栏：

    聂荣臻接见苏联科学院副院长
    新华社26日讯　国务院副总理聂荣臻今天下午接见苏联科学院副院长、西伯利亚总分院院长、著名的数学力学专家米·阿·拉夫连捷夫院士。
    接见时在座的有中国科学院秘书长裴丽生、国务院科学规划委员会副秘书长武衡、王顺桐等。
    晚间，中国科学院郭沫若院长设宴欢送拉夫连捷夫副院长及夫人和苏联著名理论物理学家依·叶·达姆院士。
    出席作陪的有中国科学院副院长吴有训、秘书长裴丽生和著名科学家钱学森、钱三强、华罗庚等多人。
