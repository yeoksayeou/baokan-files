Title: People's Party of Iran Agrees with Moscow Meeting Declaration
Author:
Date: 1958-04-26
Page: 6
Xinhua News Agency, Paris, 23rd - According to a report in the French newspaper "l'Humanité" on April 1st, the Central Committee of the People's Party of Iran recently held its Fifth Plenary Session. The meeting unanimously adopted a resolution expressing full agreement with the "Peace Manifesto" and the "Declaration of the Representatives of the Communist and Workers' Parties of the Socialist Countries" adopted at the Moscow meeting.

    The meeting determined the future political line of the People's Party of Iran, which aims to guarantee the realization of national independence, peace, democracy, progress, and the improvement of the welfare of the Iranian people.

    The meeting also determined the basic points of the program of the People's Party of Iran and the party's tasks in the current situation to fight against US-British imperialism and its plans for enslavement and aggression.

    The meeting heard two reports from the party's executive committee: (1) the program of the People's Party of Iran and the party's tasks in the struggle for national independence, peace, democracy, and the protection of the rights of working people; (2) the conference of representatives of Communist and Workers' parties of all countries.

### 伊朗人民党同意莫斯科会议宣言

1958-04-26
第6版()
专栏：

    伊朗人民党同意莫斯科会议宣言
    新华社23日讯　巴黎消息：据法国“人道报”4月1日报道，伊朗人民党中央委员会不久以前举行了第五次全体会议。会议一致通过决议表示完全同意莫斯科会议通过的“和平宣言”和“社会主义国家共产党和工人党代表会议宣言”。
    会议确定了伊朗人民党今后的政治路线，这一路线是以保证实现民族独立、和平、民主、进步和增进伊朗人民福利为目标的。
    会议还确定了伊朗人民党党纲的基本要点和党在当前情况下为反对美英帝国主义及其奴役和侵略计划进行斗争的任务。
    会议曾经听取了党的执行委员会的两个报告：（一）伊朗人民党的纲领以及党在争取民族独立、和平、民主和保护劳动人民的权利的斗争中的任务；（二）各国共产党和工人党代表会议。
