Title: Liao Chengzhi Hosts a Banquet for the Japanese Aichi Prefecture Peace Delegation
Author:
Date: 1958-04-21
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, April 20th. Liao Chengzhi, Vice-Chairman of the Chinese People's Committee for World Peace, hosted a banquet this evening to welcome the head of the Japanese Aichi Prefecture Peace Delegation, Ken Mori, and all members of the delegation.

The Aichi Prefecture Peace Delegation will travel to the Northeast by train on the 21st to continue their tour and visits.

### 廖承志欢宴日本爱知县和平代表团

1958-04-21
第4版()
专栏：

    廖承志欢宴日本爱知县和平代表团
    新华社20日讯　中国人民保卫世界和平委员会副主席廖承志今天晚上设宴欢迎日本爱知县和平代表团团长森健和全体团员。
    爱知县和平代表团将于21日乘车赴东北继续进行参观访问。
