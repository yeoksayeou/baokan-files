Title: Ceylon Football Team Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1958-04-28
Page: 7

    Ceylon Football Team Arrives in Beijing
    (Our newspaper dispatch) The Ceylon National Football Team, a group of twenty, arrived in Beijing by plane on the 26th, led by the team leader and Honorary Secretary of the Ceylon Football Association, Henry Kross, after concluding their visits to Kunming and Chongqing.

### 锡兰足球队到京

1958-04-28
第7版()
专栏：

    锡兰足球队到京
    本报讯　锡兰国家足球队一行二十人，结束了在昆明和重庆的访问以后，26日在领队、锡兰足球协会名誉秘书长亨利库斯率领下乘飞机到达北京。
