Title: Alarm Clock with Calendar and Radio
Author:
Date: 1958-04-30
Page: 3

    Alarm Clock with Calendar and Radio
    The Chongqing Jialing Machine Factory recently successfully trial-produced a new alarm clock with a calendar and radio. It can function as a regular alarm clock and also automatically report the date. The alarm clock is equipped with a small radio that can receive programs from local and central broadcasting stations. The clock has a refined and beautiful appearance.
    This clock was designed and manufactured by the staff of Chongqing Jialing Machine Factory to welcome the "May Day" holiday.

### 带日历和收音机的闹钟

1958-04-30
第3版()
专栏：新产品

    带日历和收音机的闹钟
    重庆嘉陵机器厂最近试制成功一种带有日历和收音机的新闹钟。它能起一般闹钟的作用，还能自动报告日期；闹钟里面装有小型收音机，能够收到地方和中央广播电台的节目，钟的外形精致美观。
    这种钟是重庆嘉陵机器厂职工为迎接“五一”节设计制成的。
