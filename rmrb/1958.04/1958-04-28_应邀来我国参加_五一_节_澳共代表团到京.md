Title: Australian Communist Party Delegation Arrives in Beijing to Attend "May Day" Celebrations at the Invitation of Our Country
Author:
Date: 1958-04-28
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, April 27th: An eight-member delegation of the Communist Party of Australia, led by Frank Johnson, member of the Political Bureau of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of Australia, arrived in Beijing today at the invitation of our country to attend the May 1st International Labor Day celebrations.

Those who greeted them at the airport included Wang Jiaxiang, Secretary of the Secretariat of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of China, Yang Shangkun, Alternate Secretary, Liu Ningyi, member of the Central Committee, and Li Qixin and other responsible staff members of the central organs.

### 应邀来我国参加“五一”节  澳共代表团到京

1958-04-28
第1版()
专栏：

    应邀来我国参加“五一”节
    澳共代表团到京
    新华社27日讯　应邀来我国参加“五一”国际劳动节观礼的澳大利亚共产党代表团一行八人，在澳共中央政治局委员弗兰克·约翰逊率领下于今日到达北京。
    到机场迎接的有：中国共产党中央书记处书记王稼祥、候补书记杨尚昆、中央委员刘宁一和中央机关负责工作人员李启新等。
