Title: "Poems of Beijing" Revised Edition
Author: Jing Dong
Date: 1958-04-29
Page: 8

New Bookshelf

    "Poems of Beijing" Revised Edition
    The poetry collection "Poems of Beijing," which includes works by more than forty poets such as Guo Moruo, Zang Kejia, and Tian Jian, will be revised and republished.

    The revised "Poems of Beijing" will include selected works by poets He Qifang, Yuan Shuipai, Gong Mu, and some young poets. At the same time, some works will also be deleted.

    In these poems, the poets praise the socialist construction of the capital, praise the capital people's heroic labor enthusiasm, praise the capital's beautiful and charming scenery and the festive jubilant scenes, and express the people's boundless love for the great motherland. (Qing)

### “北京的诗”增订本
京侗
1958-04-29
第8版()
专栏：新书架

    “北京的诗”增订本
    包括诗人郭沫若、臧克家、田间等四十余人的作品的诗集“北京的诗”，将重新修订后再版。
    增订后的“北京的诗”，将增选诗人何其芳、袁水拍、公木和一些青年诗人的作品，同时，也将删去一些作品。
    在这些诗篇里，诗人们歌颂了首都的社会主义建设，歌颂了首都人民豪迈的劳动热情，歌颂了首都美妙迷人的景色和节日的欢腾景象，表达出了人民对于伟大祖国的无限热爱。　（青）
