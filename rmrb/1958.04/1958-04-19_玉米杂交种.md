Glossary

    Hybrid Corn Varieties
    The hybrid corn varieties referred to here are seeds obtained by hybridizing two different corn varieties. The first generation of this hybrid has stronger advantages than its parent varieties—stronger vitality, higher yield, and better quality.

### 玉米杂交种

1958-04-19
第2版()
专栏：名词解释

    玉米杂交种
    这里所指的玉米杂交种是：两个不同的玉米品种进行杂交后所获得的种子。这种杂交种的第一代具有比其父本母本都强的优势——生活力强、产量高、品质好等。
