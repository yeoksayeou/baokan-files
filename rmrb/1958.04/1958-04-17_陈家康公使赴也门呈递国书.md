Title: Chen Jiakang, Envoy to Yemen, to Present Credentials

Author: 
Date: 1958-04-17
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, Cairo, April 15th: Chen Jiakang, the Chinese envoy to Yemen, left Cairo by plane on the morning of the 15th for Yemen to present his credentials to King Ahmad.

Chen Jiakang is the Chinese ambassador to the United Arab Republic, and he concurrently serves as the envoy to Yemen.

### 陈家康公使赴也门呈递国书

1958-04-17
第6版()
专栏：

    陈家康公使赴也门呈递国书
    据新华社开罗15日电　中国驻也门公使陈家康15日上午从开罗乘飞机前往也门，向国王艾哈迈德递交国书。
    陈家康是中国驻阿拉伯联合共和国大使，他兼任驻也门公使。
