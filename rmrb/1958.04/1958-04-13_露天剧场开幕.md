Title: Open-Air Theater Opening
Author: Qiming
Date: 1958-04-13
Page: 4

    Open-Air Theater Opening
    Starting today, the Wanfo Pavilion Open-Air Theater in Beihai Park will begin performance activities, with the Xinsheng and Rongguan Pingju Troupes jointly performing "Ma Widow's Shop."
                          (Qiming)

### 露天剧场开幕
岐鸣
1958-04-13
第4版()
专栏：看看听听玩玩

    露天剧场开幕
    北海公园万佛楼露天剧场自今天开始演出活动，由新生、荣冠两个评剧团联合演出“马寡妇开店”。
                          （岐鸣）
