Title: Ji Fang Acting Chairman of the Central Committee of the Chinese Peasants and Workers Democratic Party
Author:
Date: 1958-04-05
Page: 4

    This newspaper reports that the Central Committee of the Chinese Peasants and Workers Democratic Party held the 35th (enlarged) meeting of the Central Executive Bureau in Beijing on the 4th of this month. At the meeting, everyone believed that in order to meet the needs of the work, Ji Fang was unanimously elected as the acting chairman of the Central Committee to lead the work of the whole party.
    More than 20 people attended and attended this meeting, including members of the Central Executive Bureau, members of the Central Committee and alternate members of the Central Committee in Beijing, and members and members of the Central Rectification Leadership Committee in Beijing.

### 季方代理农工党中央委员会主席

1958-04-05
第4版()
专栏：

    季方代理农工党中央委员会主席
    本报讯　农工民主党中央于本月4日在京召开了中央执行局第三十五次会议（扩大），在会上，大家认为为了适应工作的需要，一致推选季方为中央委员会代理主席，领导全党工作。
    出席和列席这次会议的有中央执行局委员，在京中央委员、候补中央委员，中央整风领导委员会在京委员、组员，等二十多人。
