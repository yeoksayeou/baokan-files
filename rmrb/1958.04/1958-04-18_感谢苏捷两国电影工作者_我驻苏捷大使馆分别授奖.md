Title: In Appreciation of Soviet and Czech Filmmakers: Awards Presented by Our Embassies in the Soviet Union and Czechoslovakia

Author:

Date: 1958-04-18
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, 16th. The Chinese Embassy in the Soviet Union held an award ceremony on the 16th. Ambassador Liu Xiao, on behalf of the Chinese Ministry of Culture, presented medals and certificates of merit for outstanding films from 1949-1955 to the Soviet film studios, directors, and cinematographers who participated in the production of the films "The Victory of the Chinese People" and "Liberated China." The Moscow Central Documentary Film Studio and the Gorky Film Studio received honorary certificates from the Chinese Ministry of Culture. Directors Varlamov, Gerasimov, and the cinematographers of the two films, a total of fifteen people, received medals and letters of appreciation.

Xinhua News Agency, Prague, 16th. Ge Buhai, Counselor of the Chinese Embassy in Czechoslovakia, today presented honorary certificates, individual medals, and gifts on behalf of the Chinese Ministry of Culture to the Czechoslovakian filmmakers who participated in the production of the color documentary "People's Hearts Are One." Those awarded were Vo. Jasný, director of the Czechoslovak Army Film Studio, cinematographer Ka. Kašina, and composers S. Havelka and M. Vacek.

### 感谢苏捷两国电影工作者  我驻苏捷大使馆分别授奖

1958-04-18
第6版()
专栏：

    感谢苏捷两国电影工作者
    我驻苏捷大使馆分别授奖
    据新华社莫斯科16日电　中国驻苏联大使馆16日举行授奖仪式，由刘晓大使代表中国文化部，将1949—1955年优秀影片的奖章和奖状等授予参加“中国人民的胜利”和“解放了的中国”两部影片的摄制工作的苏联制片厂、导演和摄影师。获得中国文化部荣誉奖状的有莫斯科中央文献纪录电影制片厂和高尔基电影制片厂。获得奖章和感谢信的有导演瓦尔拉莫夫、格拉西莫夫以及两片的摄影师等共十五人。
    据新华社布拉格16日电　中国驻捷克斯洛伐克大使馆参赞葛步海今天代表中国文化部把荣誉奖状、个人奖章和礼品授予参加彩色纪录片“人民心一条”摄制工作的捷克斯洛伐克的电影工作者。得奖的有捷克斯洛伐克陆军电影制片厂导演沃·亚斯尼、摄影师卡·卡希纳、作曲者斯·哈维耳卡和米·瓦策克。
