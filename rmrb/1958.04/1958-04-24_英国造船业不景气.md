Title: British Shipbuilding Industry in Recession
Author:
Date: 1958-04-24
Page: 6

    British Shipbuilding Industry in Recession
    Xinhua News Agency, London, 23rd. The "Financial Times" reported today on the recession in the British shipbuilding industry. In the first quarter of this year, the British shipbuilding industry did not receive any new foreign orders. Normally, foreign orders account for about 20% of the orders received by the British shipbuilding industry.
    In the first quarter of this year, some customers also cancelled their original orders. The "Financial Times" pointed out that due to competition from foreign shipbuilding industries, the threat faced by the British shipbuilding industry will not be short-term but long-term. In addition, the tonnage of ships laid up in British ports in March reached the highest peak since the 1930s.

### 英国造船业不景气

1958-04-24
第6版()
专栏：

    英国造船业不景气
    据新华社伦敦23日电  “金融时报”今天报道了英国造船业不景气的状况。在今年第一季度，英国造船业没有接到任何新的外国订货。通常，外国订货在英国造船业所接到的订货中占20％左右。
    在今年第一季度，还有一些主顾取消了他们原来的订货。“金融时报”指出，由于外国造船业的竞争，英国造船业所面临的威胁将不是短期而是长期的。此外，3月份在英国各港口搁置不用的船只的吨数达到了1930年代以来的最高峰。
