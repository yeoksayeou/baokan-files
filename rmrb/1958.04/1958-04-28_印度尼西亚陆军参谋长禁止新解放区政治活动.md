Title: Indonesian Army Chief of Staff Forbids Political Activities in Newly Liberated Areas
Author:
Date: 1958-04-28
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, Jakarta – Lieutenant Colonel Piengaadi, Director of the Indonesian Army Press Office, said on the evening of the 25th that Army Chief of Staff Major General Nasution had issued orders to the commanders of military operations in West Sumatra, the Riau region, and Central and North Sulawesi, requiring them to prohibit any form of political activity in the newly liberated areas of the aforementioned locations.

Piengaadi explained on the 26th that this ban was solely for the purpose of restoring security and stability in the liberated areas. It is temporary and does not mean the abolition of political parties.

### 印度尼西亚陆军参谋长禁止新解放区政治活动

1958-04-28
第6版()
专栏：

    印度尼西亚陆军参谋长禁止新解放区政治活动
    据新华社雅加达讯　印度尼西亚陆军新闻处处长皮恩加迪中校在25日晚上说，陆军参谋长纳苏蒂安少将已经向西苏门答腊、廖内地区以及中和北苏拉威西指挥军事行动的司令们发出命令，要求他们禁止在上述地方的新解放的地区中进行任何形式的政治活动。
    皮恩加迪在26日解释说，这个禁令完全是为了恢复解放地区的治安和稳定。它是暂时性的，并不意味着取消政党。
