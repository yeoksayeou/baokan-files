Title: Two Central Committee Members of the Indonesian Women's Movement Association Arrive in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1958-04-29
Page: 4
　
　　Xinhua News Agency, April 28th. Mrs. Danti Aidit and Mrs. Sudasi, members of the Central Committee of the Indonesian Women's Movement Association, arrived in Beijing by plane this afternoon. They were invited by the All-China Women's Federation of the People's Republic of China to visit China and participate in the "May Day" celebrations.

### 印度尼西亚妇女运动协会两位中央委员到京

1958-04-29
第4版()
专栏：

    印度尼西亚妇女运动协会两位中央委员到京
    新华社28日讯　印度尼西亚妇女运动协会中央委员丹蒂·艾地夫人和苏打西夫人，今天下午乘飞机到达北京。她们是应中华人民共和国全国妇女联合会邀请来我国参观访问并参加“五一”节观礼的。
