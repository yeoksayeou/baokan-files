Title: United Arab Republic Military Attaché in China Holds Reception for Visit of UAR Military Delegation to China
Author:
Date: 1958-04-06
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, April 5th. The Military Attaché of the United Arab Republic (UAR) in China, Colonel Montaki, and his wife, held a reception this evening for the visit of the Military Friendship Delegation of the United Arab Republic to China.

    Vice Premier of the State Council and Minister of National Defense, Marshal Peng Dehuai, Vice Minister of Foreign Affairs Zhang Hanfu, President of the Sino-Arab Friendship Association Bao Erhan, and senior generals of the Chinese People's Liberation Army, General Li Da, General Zhang Zongxun, General Gan Siqi, General Guo Tianmin, Lieutenant General Xiao Xiangrong, Vice Admiral Luo Shunchu, Lieutenant General Wang Bingzhang of the Air Force, Lieutenant General Chang Qiankun of the Air Force, Lieutenant General Liu Shaowen, Lieutenant General Wang Zheng, Lieutenant General Rao Zhengxi, and our country's Military Attaché to the United Arab Republic, Colonel Li Dazhong, were invited to attend the reception.

    Also attending the reception were the head of the Military Friendship Delegation of the United Arab Republic to China, Lieutenant General Mohammed Ibrahim, and all members of the delegation.

    In his speech at the reception, Colonel Montaki said that the visit of the Military Friendship Delegation of the United Arab Republic to friendly China will undoubtedly be a sincere expression of the sincere and lofty feelings of the army and people of the United Arab Republic towards the Chinese army and the great Chinese people. It is also to repay and thank the Chinese government and people for the resolute and historic attitude they have taken towards the Arab people during the tense international situation, the deepening crisis, and the imperialist aggression against our peaceful people.

    Colonel Montaki said: As long as imperialism exists in the world, our peace-loving people, who are sincerely committed to consolidating peace, should have a strong army to protect this peace. He said that history will record the great contribution made by the armies of our two countries to consolidating world peace.

    Vice Premier of the State Council and Minister of National Defense, Marshal Peng Dehuai, raised his glass at the reception to toast the sincere friendship and cooperation between the peoples and armies of China and the UAR, the friendship and unity established by the people of Asia and Africa in the struggle to safeguard national independence and oppose colonialism, and lasting world peace.

    The Chargé d'Affaires ad interim of the Embassy of the United Arab Republic in China, Xie Binni, and military attachés from various countries in China also attended the reception tonight.

### 为阿联军事代表团访华  阿联驻华武官举行招待会

1958-04-06
第1版()
专栏：

    为阿联军事代表团访华
    阿联驻华武官举行招待会
    新华社5日讯　阿拉伯联合共和国驻华武官蒙大奇上校和夫人，今天晚上为阿拉伯联合共和国军事友好代表团访问中国举行招待会。
    国务院副总理兼国防部长彭德怀元帅，外交部副部长章汉夫，中阿友好协会会长包尔汉，中国人民解放军高级将领李达上将、张宗逊上将、甘泗淇上将、郭天民上将、萧向荣中将、罗舜初海军中将、王秉璋空军中将、常乾坤空军中将、刘少文中将、王诤中将、饶正锡中将、我国驻阿拉伯联合共和国武官李大中上校等应邀出席了招待会。
    出席招待会的，还有阿拉伯联合共和国军事友好访华代表团团长穆罕默德·易卜拉欣中将和全体团员。
    蒙大奇上校在招待会上致词说，阿拉伯联合共和国军事友好代表团对友好的国家中国的访问，无疑地将是真诚表达了阿拉伯联合共和国军队和人民对中国军队和伟大的中国人民所怀的真挚和崇高的感情，同时也是报答和感谢中国政府和人民在国际局势紧张、危机加深和帝国主义对我们和平的人民发动侵略的时候对阿拉伯人民所采取的果断和具有历史性的态度。
    蒙大奇上校说：只要世界上还存在着帝国主义，我们爱好和平和忠诚致力于巩固和平的人民，就应有一支强大的军队来保护这个和平。他说，历史将要记载下我们两国军队对巩固世界和平所作的巨大贡献。
    国务院副总理兼国防部长彭德怀元帅在招待会上举杯祝酒，为中阿两国人民和军队之间的真诚友谊和合作，为亚非人民在维护民族独立、反对殖民主义斗争中所建立的友谊和团结，为世界的持久和平干杯。
    阿拉伯联合共和国驻华大使馆临时代办谢宾尼，各国驻华武官也出席了今晚的招待会。
