Title: Budyonny Awarded the Order of Lenin
Author:
Date: 1958-04-27
Page: 3

    Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, April 25th: According to TASS, the Presidium of the Supreme Soviet of the Soviet Union has awarded Marshal of the Soviet Union Budyonny the Order of Lenin on the occasion of his seventy-fifth birthday, in recognition of his contributions to the Soviet state in the building of the Soviet army.

### 布琼尼荣获列宁勋章

1958-04-27
第3版()
专栏：

    布琼尼荣获列宁勋章
    新华社25日讯　据塔斯社莫斯科24日讯：苏联最高苏维埃主席团在苏联元帅布琼尼七十五岁诞辰的时候，授予他列宁勋章，以表彰他在苏军建设上对苏维埃国家的功绩。
