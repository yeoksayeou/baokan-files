Title: Robeson Sends Greetings to the Chinese People
Author:
Date: 1958-04-20
Page: 3
Xinhua News Agency, April 19th—The Committee for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries of the People's Republic of China received yesterday evening a letter from the famous American black singer Paul Robeson, sent from the United States on the occasion of his sixtieth birthday, conveying his greetings to the Chinese people. Enclosed with the letter were photographs of Robeson himself and his family, as well as a copy of Robeson's book "Here I Stand," published this year in New York.
The letter said: To the people of the People's Republic of China, I extend to you all my warmest and heartfelt respects. My family and I express our deep gratitude to you.
	As you well know, we closely follow the momentous events taking place in your great country, one of the cradles of human civilization, and one of the great nations of today and the future.
	It has always been a special honor and eternally fascinating for me to learn a little of your beautiful language, to listen often to your beautiful ancient and modern music, and to sing some of your beautiful folk songs.
	I am deeply grateful for the rich contributions your writers, poets, and musicians have made to world culture.
	Thank you to your heroic leaders, and thank you to all the Chinese people for your contributions to socialism and your defense of peace.
	I hope that one day soon I will be able to greet you on Chinese soil, which would surely be a joyous day.
	Gratefully, your faithful friend, Paul Robeson (attached picture)
	A photo of Robeson's family. From left: daughter-in-law, granddaughter, wife, Robeson, grandson, son. The text is: "We extend to you our greetings and congratulations, may peace and friendship spread throughout the world."
（Xinhua News Agency draft）

### 罗伯逊向我国人民致意

1958-04-20
第3版()
专栏：

    罗伯逊向我国人民致意
    新华社19日讯　中华人民共和国对外文化联络委员会昨晚收到美国著名黑人歌唱家保罗·罗伯逊在他六十岁寿辰之际从美国发来的向中国人民致意的信。随同这封信一起寄来的有罗伯逊本人和他的家庭的照片以及今年在纽约出版的罗伯逊的一本著作“我站在这里”。
    这封信说：中华人民共和国的人民，我向你们全体致以最热烈的衷心的敬意。我和我的家庭向你们表示深切的感谢。
    像你们所清楚知道的，我们密切地关心你们伟大的国家所发生的重大事情，你们伟大的国家是人类文明的摇篮之一，当今和未来的伟大国家之一。
    学一点你们美丽的语言，经常倾听你们优美的古代和现代音乐，唱一些你们美丽的民歌，这些一直使我感到格外的光荣，并且使我感到永远是引人入胜的。
    我深深地感激你们的作家、诗人、音乐家对世界文化作出的丰富贡献。
    感谢你们英勇的领导者们，感谢全体中国人民对社会主义的贡献和对和平的保卫。
    我希望不久将有一天在中国土地上向你们致意，那肯定会成为一个愉快的日子。
    感激你们的忠实于你们的朋友 　保罗·罗伯逊（附图片）
　　罗伯逊的家庭的照片。左起：儿媳，孙女，夫人，罗伯逊，孙儿，儿子。文字是：“我们谨向你们致意和祝贺，愿和平与友谊普及全世界。”
　（新华社稿）
