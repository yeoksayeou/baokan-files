Title: Eliminating the Last Stronghold of the Rebel Troops in Da Ban Nu Li: Government Forces Capture Da Lu Dong
Author:
Date: 1958-04-29
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, Jakarta, April 28th: Indonesian government forces recaptured Da Lu Dong, the last stronghold of the rebel troops in Da Ban Nu Li, on the 27th. At the same time, government forces advancing towards the rebel group's den, Bukit Tinggi, have reached the vicinity of two important rebel strongholds.

According to a broadcast by Radio Republic Indonesia on the evening of the 27th, government forces encountered no resistance when they recaptured Da Lu Dong on the morning of the 27th. Government forces only engaged in combat with rebel troops while sweeping the outskirts of Da Lu Dong, and the rebels fled after suffering losses. With the recapture of Da Lu Dong, all important cities in the Da Ban Nu Li area are now under the control of government forces.

Government forces seized documents belonging to the rebel group's "Foreign Minister," Simbolon, in Da Lu Dong. These documents mentioned a conspiracy to "arm local residents with weapons airdropped."

According to Antara News Agency, government forces have reached the outskirts of Padang Panjang and Sawahlunto in the West Sumatra region. Padang Panjang is about twenty kilometers south of Bukit Tinggi, the location of the rebel group's "government"; Sawahlunto is an important transportation hub west of Lake Singkarak, about eighty kilometers northeast of the liberated Padang. Lieutenant Colonel Piengadie, a spokesman for the Indonesian Army, announced in Jakarta that about sixty rebel troops in Sawahlunto had revolted against the Hussein rebel group, demanding that government forces provide assistance quickly.

### 拔除打板奴里叛军最后据点  政府军攻克打鲁栋

1958-04-29
第6版()
专栏：

    拔除打板奴里叛军最后据点
    政府军攻克打鲁栋
    新华社雅加达28日电　印度尼西亚政府军在27日收复了叛军在打板奴里的最后据点打鲁栋。同时，正在向叛乱集团的巢穴武吉丁宜挺进的政府军已经进抵叛军两个重要据点的周围。
    据印度尼西亚共和国广播电台27日晚间广播，政府军在27日清晨收复打鲁栋时，没有遇到抵抗。政府军只是在扫荡打鲁栋郊区时，同叛军发生了战斗，叛军在遭受损失后逃走。打鲁栋收复后，打板奴里地区的全部重要城市已经都在政府军控制之下。
    政府军在打鲁栋缴获了叛乱集团的“外交部长”西姆波朗的文件，这些文件中谈到“用空投武器武装当地居民”的阴谋。
    另据安塔拉通讯社报道，政府军部队已经进抵西苏门答腊地区的巴东班让和沙哇伦多两市的近郊。巴东班让在叛乱集团“政府”所在地武吉丁宜南面约二十公里；沙哇伦多是辛卡腊克湖西面的重要交通枢纽，在已解放的巴东东北约八十公里。据印度尼西亚陆军发言人皮恩加迪中校在雅加达宣布，在沙哇伦多，有叛军约六十人起义反对侯赛尼叛乱集团，他们要求政府军迅速给予援助。
