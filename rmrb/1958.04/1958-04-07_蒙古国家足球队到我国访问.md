Title: Mongolian National Football Team Visits Our Country
Author:
Date: 1958-04-07
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, April 4th: The Mongolian People's Republic National Football Team, a group of nineteen people, arrived in Beijing by train on the evening of the 4th.

This football team, led by Qi. Halteerhuqiao, Minister of the Motorcycle Sports Department of the Sports Commission under the Council of Ministers of the Mongolian People's Republic, is visiting our country at the invitation of the Sports Commission of the People's Republic of China. They are scheduled to visit Beijing and Tianjin for a month and a half.

### 蒙古国家足球队到我国访问

1958-04-07
第4版()
专栏：

    蒙古国家足球队到我国访问
    据新华社4日讯　蒙古人民共和国国家足球队一行十九人，4日晚乘火车到达北京。
    这个由蒙古人民共和国部长会议体育运动委员会摩托运动部部长契·哈勒特尔胡乔率领的足球队，是应中华人民共和国体育运动委员会的邀请来我国作友好访问的。他们预定在北京、天津访问一个半月。
