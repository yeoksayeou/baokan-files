Title: Call on the Whole Party to Study and Disseminate the Moscow Declaration; Welcome the Soviet Decision to Cease Nuclear Weapon Testing
Author:
Date: 1958-04-04
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, Jakarta, April 3rd: According to the Indonesian Communist Party's central organ newspaper, "People's Daily," the Sixth Plenary Session of the Central Committee of the Indonesian Communist Party unanimously adopted a resolution at its meeting on the 2nd, expressing support for the two declarations issued by the Moscow meeting of Communist and Workers' Parties of all countries. The resolution requires party committees and organizations at all levels, as well as party members and probationary members, to actively study and explain these two documents to the broad masses, and to take them as practical guides in the sacred struggle for national independence, democracy, peace, and socialism.

The meeting also passed a resolution welcoming the Soviet Union's decision to unilaterally cease nuclear weapon testing. The resolution pointed out that the Soviet government's decision proves the Soviet Union's sincerity and its struggle for world peace. The resolution calls on the Indonesian government and the people of the whole country to express their support for this decision, and demands that the United States and Britain follow the Soviet Union's example and immediately cease nuclear weapon testing.

The meeting also decided to send a delegation to submit the resolutions of the plenary session to the President, Prime Minister, and Army Chief of Staff of Indonesia. The delegation will be composed of Aidit, Lukman, and Njoto.

### 号召全党学习和传播莫斯科宣言  欢迎苏联停止试验核武器的决定

1958-04-04
第6版()
专栏：

    号召全党学习和传播莫斯科宣言
    欢迎苏联停止试验核武器的决定
    据新华社雅加达3日电　据印度尼西亚共产党中央机关报“人民日报”报道，印度尼西亚共产党中央委员会第六次全体会议在2日举行的会议上一致通过决议，表示支持世界各国共产党和工人党莫斯科会议所发表的两项宣言。决议要求党的各级委员会和组织以及党员和预备党员积极学习和向广大群众解释这两个文件，并且把它们作为争取民族独立、民主、和平和社会主义的神圣斗争中的实际指针。
    会议还通过决议，欢迎苏联关于单方面停止核武器试验的决定。决议指出，苏联政府的这个决定证明了苏联的诚意和为实现世界和平而斗争。决议要求印度尼西亚政府和全国人民表示支持这个决定，并且要求美国、英国效法苏联，立即停止核武器试验。
  会议还决定派遣代表团把全体会议的决议递交给印度尼西亚总统、总理和陆军参谋长。代表团将由艾地、鲁克曼和约多组成。
