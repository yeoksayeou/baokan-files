Title: Yang Yumin Sets New Women's Long Jump Record
Author:
Date: 1958-04-27
Page: 2

Xinhua News Agency, Shanghai, April 26th: Yang Yumin, the national record holder for women's long jump, was invited to participate in a performance competition at the student track and field and weightlifting games held by Shanghai First Medical College today. She broke her own national long jump record with a jump of 5.55 meters. The national record last year was 5.54 meters.

### 杨玉敏创女子跳远新纪录

1958-04-27
第2版()
专栏：

    杨玉敏创女子跳远新纪录
    新华社上海26日电　女子跳远全国纪录保持者杨玉敏，今天应邀参加上海第一医学院举行的学生田径举重运动大会作表演比赛，以五公尺五十五公分的成绩打破了她自己保持的跳远全国纪录。去年的全国纪录是五公尺五十四公分。
