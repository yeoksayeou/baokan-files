Title: Algerian Liberation Army Widely Attacks French Troops
Author:
Date: 1958-04-23
Page: 5

    Xinhua News Agency, Algiers, 22nd: It was announced here yesterday that the Algerian National Liberation Army and the French colonial army had engaged in fierce fighting in the mountains of the Oran province. According to the French military, seventeen French soldiers were killed and eighteen wounded.
    In the suburbs of Philippeville, the Algerian National Liberation Army clashed with the French colonial army three times on the evening of the 20th. On the same day, a French transport convoy was ambushed by the Algerian National Liberation Army in Plaxbourg, about fifteen miles from Philippeville. In addition, a French mobile unit was also ambushed in Villebourg on the 19th. Near Menerville in central Algeria, and in the western part of Algeria near the Moroccan border, the Algerian National Liberation Army and the French army also engaged in combat on the 17th.

### 阿解放军广泛袭击法军

1958-04-23
第5版()
专栏：

    阿解放军广泛袭击法军
    据新华社22日讯　阿尔及尔消息：昨天这里宣布，阿尔及利亚民族解放军同法国殖民军在阿兰省的山峦里发生了激烈战斗。据法军方面承认，法军有十七人死亡，十八人受伤。
    在菲利普维尔市郊，阿尔及利亚民族解放军同法国殖民军在20日晚上就发生了三次冲突。在这一天，离菲利普维尔约十五英里的普拉克斯布格地方还有一队法国运输队遭到阿尔及利亚民族解放军伏击。此外，法军机动部队19日在维尔堡也遭到了伏击。在阿尔及利亚中部的梅内尔维尔附近以及在阿尔及利亚西边邻近摩洛哥的边境地方，阿尔及利亚民族解放军同法军在17日也发生了战斗。
