Author:
Date: 1958-04-28
Page: 4

　　570,000 Items of Revolutionary Historical Archives Collected Nationwide

　　A vigorous campaign to collect revolutionary historical archives is underway across the country. According to incomplete statistics, twenty-three provinces, cities, and autonomous regions, including Shanxi, Henan, and Hebei, have collected over 570,000 items, 15,900 kilograms, and 20,000+ volumes of revolutionary historical archives. In addition, central government departments have collected over 7,000 items.

　　Among the large quantity of archives collected from various regions, many are extremely valuable, such as the 1920 Communist Group documents, autobiographies of some Central Committee members of the Communist Party during the First Revolutionary Civil War, documents and proclamations of the Chinese Soviet Republic, documents of the CCP Northern Bureau, documents of the CCP Jiangsu Provincial Committee around 1930, records of the first Jiangxi Southwest Party Congress, documents of the Zhejiang Provincial Committee and Western Zhejiang Special Committee in 1928, the 1931 "Resolution of the CCP Central Committee on the Struggle Against Counter-Revolutionary Groups in the Soviet Area," "Summary of Two Years of Operations of the Chinese Workers' and Peasants' Red Army," and manuscripts of revolutionary leaders and responsible comrades of the CCP Central Committee.

　　The CCP committees of all provinces, cities, and autonomous regions attach great importance to the collection of revolutionary historical archives. People from all walks of life, the broad masses of cadres, and the masses have also actively supported this work. Guo Moruo, President of the Chinese Academy of Sciences, sent a handwritten inscription by Comrade Zhu De; Shu Xincheng from Shanghai contributed the 1923 handwritten documents of the revolutionary martyr Yun Daiying; Wang Shaoqiu from Hainan Island sent seventy photos from the Yan'an period; a farmer from Rongjiang County, Guizhou, contributed a handwritten letter from Comrade He Long, preserved for decades; a farmer from Danyang County, Jiangsu, preserved over one hundred documents from the CCP underground Jiangsu Provincial Committee and Danyang County Committee; and Wu Jianli, a Beijing citizen, sent two copies of the "Guide" weekly newspaper. (Xinhua News Agency)

### 全国已收集革命历史档案资料五十七万件

1958-04-28
第4版()
专栏：资料

    全国已收集革命历史档案资料五十七万件
    全国各地正在大力开展革命历史档案资料的征集工作。据不精确的统计，山西、河南、河北等二十三个省、市、自治区共收集到革命历史档案资料五十七万余件、一万五千九百公斤又二万余卷。另外，中央机关收集到七千余件。
    各地收集到的大批档案资料当中，有许多是十分珍贵的，如1920年共产主义小组文件，第一次国内革命战争时期一些共产党中央委员的自传，中华苏维埃共和国的文件、布告，中共中央北方局的文件，1930年前后中共江苏省委的文件，赣西南第一次党代会记录，1928年浙江省委、浙西特委的文件，1931年“中共中央关于苏区内向反革命团体斗争的决议”，“中国工农红军两年作战总结”，以及革命领袖和中共中央负责同志的手稿等。
    中共各省、市、自治区委员会对革命历史档案资料的征集工作都很重视。全国各界人士、广大干部和群众也都积极支持这一工作。中国科学院院长郭沫若送来朱德同志的亲笔题词；上海舒新城献出革命先烈恽代英同志1923年的亲笔文件；海南岛王绍裘寄来七十张延安时期的照片；贵州榕江县一位农民，献出保存数十年之久的一封贺龙同志的亲笔信件；江苏丹阳县一位农民，保存了中共地下江苏省委、丹阳县委的文件一百余件；北京市民伍建莅送来“向导”周报两份。　（新华社）
