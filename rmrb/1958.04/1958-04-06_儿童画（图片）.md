Children's Drawings

    Here, we publish drawings by several kindergarten children. Although these drawings lack artistic skill and profound ideological content, they fully exude the rich imagination of children on these naive canvases. Look! How adorable is this little bear, and the "red moon" and red sun hang high in the sky, and the goldfish in the river swim so freely... The new generation of our motherland loves the things around them in this way.

    Red Sun and "Red Moon" Gao Xing (7 years old)
    Big Ship Cheng Jia'en (6 years old)
    Good Little Bear Ma Zhiyang (5 and a half years old)
    Goldfish Swimming Lan Yicai (6 years old)

### 儿童画（图片）

1958-04-06
第4版()
专栏：儿童画

    儿童画
    这里，发表了几位幼儿园的小朋友画的画。这些画里，虽然没有什么艺术的技巧和高度的思想内容，但这带着稚气的画面上，却充分洋溢着孩子们丰富的想像。看！这只小熊多么可爱，那个“红月亮”和红太阳高挂在天空，河里的金鱼游得多么自由自在……。我们祖国新的一代就这样热爱着周围的事物。
    红太阳和“红月亮”　高兴（七岁）
    大轮船　　程加恩（六岁）
    小熊乖乖    马志扬（五岁半）
    金鱼游泳　　蓝义才（六岁）
    讲故事　纳一摄
