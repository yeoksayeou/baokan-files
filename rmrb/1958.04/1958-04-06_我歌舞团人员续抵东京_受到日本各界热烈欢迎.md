Title: My Song and Dance Troupe Personnel Continue to Arrive in Tokyo, Receiving Warm Welcome from All Circles in Japan
Author: 
Date: 1958-04-06
Page: 3
    Xinhua News Agency, Tokyo, 5th: The second group of personnel from the Chinese Song and Dance Troupe, a party of fifty-nine people led by the head of the troupe, Lü Ji, arrived in Tokyo on the evening of the 4th.

### 我歌舞团人员续抵东京  受到日本各界热烈欢迎

1958-04-06
第3版()
专栏：

    我歌舞团人员续抵东京
    受到日本各界热烈欢迎
    据新华社5日讯　东京消息：中国歌舞团第二批人员一行五十九人由团长吕骥率领，在4日晚上到达东京。
