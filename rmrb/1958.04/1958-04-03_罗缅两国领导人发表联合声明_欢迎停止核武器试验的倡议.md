Title: Romanian and Burmese Leaders Issue Joint Statement Welcoming Initiative to Halt Nuclear Weapons Testing
Author:
Date: 1958-04-03
Page: 5
Xinhua News Agency, Yangon, April 2nd: A joint statement signed here on March 31st by the Chairman of the Romanian Council of Ministers, Chivu Stoica, and the Prime Minister of Burma, U Nu, stated today that the cessation of nuclear and thermonuclear explosion tests is a welcome first step towards banning the use of atomic energy for destructive purposes and resolving the issue of disarmament.

    The two leaders, in the statement, both welcomed "the proposal to convene a summit meeting in order to work out practical ways to solve the outstanding and most pressing international problems." They also expressed hope that measures would be taken to hold such a meeting without delay.

    The two leaders favored the abolition of military blocs and believed that military blocs should be replaced by a collective security system.

    The joint statement emphasized that the two leaders strongly felt that recognizing the legitimate position of the People's Republic of China in the United Nations would not only greatly strengthen the role of this world organization, but also help restore stability and peace in Asia and the world. Therefore, the two leaders will work to ensure that the People's Republic of China obtains its legitimate position in the United Nations.

    The two leaders also welcomed the action of the Chinese People's Volunteers withdrawing from Korea, believing that this contributes to the cause of peace in Asia and the world. The two leaders agreed that the Geneva Agreements on Vietnam must be implemented to promote the reunification of Vietnam.

    The two leaders also pledged in the statement to do their best to further develop the economic, technological, scientific and cultural relations between the two countries.

    The joint statement said that Burmese Prime Minister U Nu had gladly accepted Chivu Stoica's invitation to visit the Romanian People's Republic.

### 罗缅两国领导人发表联合声明  欢迎停止核武器试验的倡议

1958-04-03
第5版()
专栏：

    罗缅两国领导人发表联合声明
    欢迎停止核武器试验的倡议
    新华社仰光2日电　今天发表的罗马尼亚部长会议主席基伏·斯托伊卡和缅甸总理吴努3月31日在这里签署的一项联合声明说，停止核和热核爆炸试验是禁止原子能用于破坏目的和解决裁军问题的值得欢迎的第一步。
    两国领导人在声明中都欢迎“召开最高级会议的建议，以便订出解决悬而未决的和最迫切的各种国际问题的实际办法”。他们并且希望采取措施，使这样的会议毫不延迟地举行。
    两国领导人赞成废除军事集团，并且认为，军事集团应该由一个集体安全的体系来代替。
  联合声明强调说，两国领导人强烈地感到，承认中华人民共和国在联合国的合法地位，不但将在很大程度上加强这个世界组织的作用，还有助于恢复亚洲和全世界的稳定与和平。因此，两国领导人将努力保证，使中华人民共和国在联合国取得合法地位。
    两国领导人还欢迎中国人民志愿军从朝鲜撤出的行动，认为这有助于亚洲和世界和平事业。两国领导人一致认为，必须执行关于越南问题的日内瓦协议，以促使越南重新统一。
    两国领导人在声明中还保证，尽力进一步发展两国的经济、技术、科学和文化关系。
    联合声明说，缅甸总理吴努已经欣然接受了基伏·斯托伊卡请他访问罗马尼亚人民共和国的邀请。
