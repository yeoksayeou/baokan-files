Title: Storm Clouds Gathering in Iraq: Nearly Two Hundred Officers Arrested
Author:
Date: 1958-04-17
Page: 5
　
　　Xinhua News Agency, Beirut, 16th - TASS Beirut, 16th: According to "Orient News," Iraqi authorities have arrested 192 officers of the Iraqi Army on charges of plotting a coup.

　　The newspaper pointed out that Iraqi authorities are continuing to arrest patriots in large numbers.

### 伊拉克山雨欲  来近二百名军官被捕

1958-04-17
第5版()
专栏：

    伊拉克山雨欲
    来近二百名军官被捕
    新华社16日讯　塔斯社贝鲁特16日讯：据“东方报”报道，伊拉克当局逮捕了伊拉克陆军的一百九十二名军官，罪名是策划政变。
    报纸指出，伊拉克当局正在继续大批逮捕爱国人士。
