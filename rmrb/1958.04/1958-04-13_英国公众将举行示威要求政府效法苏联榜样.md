Title: British Public to Hold Demonstrations Demanding Government Follow Soviet Example
Author:
Date: 1958-04-13
Page: 3
Xinhua News Agency, London, April 11th: The British public will hold a mass demonstration in Trafalgar Square in London on the 13th to further demand that the British government follow the Soviet Union in halting nuclear weapons testing.

Morgan Phillips, General Secretary of the Labour Party, announced in a letter published in the "Daily Worker" today that Labour Party leaders will issue an urgent appeal to the government on that day. He wrote in the letter: If Britain follows the Soviet Union in announcing a temporary cessation of hydrogen bomb tests, then "it would make reaching an agreement between each other much closer."

British trade union leaders are now calling on the people to support this mass demonstration.

### 英国公众将举行示威要求政府效法苏联榜样

1958-04-13
第3版()
专栏：

    英国公众将举行示威要求政府效法苏联榜样
    据新华社伦敦11日电　英国公众将于13日在伦敦特拉法加广场举行群众示威，进一步要求英国政府效法苏联停止核武器试验。
    工党总书记摩根·菲利普斯今天在“工人日报”上发表的一封信中宣布，工党领袖们将在那一天向政府发出紧急呼吁。他在信中写道：如果英国追随苏联宣布暂时停止氢弹试验，那么，“就会使得达成彼此之间的协议大大地接近”。
    英国工会领袖们现在正在号召人民支持这一群众示威。
