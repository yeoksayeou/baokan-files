Title: Guangzhou Overseas Chinese Sugar Mill Starts Production (Picture)
Author: Cai Zhongzhi
Date: 1958-04-07
Page: 3

Xinhua News Agency, Guangzhou, April 6th: The Guangzhou Overseas Chinese Sugar Mill, with a daily crushing capacity of 2,000 tons of sugarcane, officially started production on the 5th. The entire production process of this sugar mill is mechanized and automated.

    广州华侨糖厂开工
