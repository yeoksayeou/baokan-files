Title: Kim Il-sung and Pham Van Dong Send Congratulatory Telegrams
Author:
Date: 1958-04-18
Page: 5

　　Kim Il-sung and Pham Van Dong Send Congratulatory Telegrams
　　Xinhua News Agency, Pyongyang, April 17th. Kim Il-sung, Premier of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea, on behalf of the Korean government and people, sent a telegram to Ghanaian Prime Minister Nkrumah to congratulate him on the convening of the Conference of Independent African States. Kim Il-sung expressed in his congratulatory telegram his hope that this conference will greatly contribute to the cause of opposing colonialism, consolidating national independence, and safeguarding peace.
　　Xinhua News Agency, Hanoi, April 16th. According to the Vietnam News Agency, Premier Pham Van Dong sent a telegram today on behalf of the government and people of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam to congratulate the convening of the Conference of Independent African States.
　　Premier Pham Van Dong expressed in his congratulatory telegram his hope that this conference will achieve great success in strengthening unity among African countries in order to promote the national liberation movements of these countries, to oppose colonialism, racial discrimination, and war, and to defend peace in Africa, Asia, and the world.

### 金日成和范文同致电祝贺

1958-04-18
第5版()
专栏：

    金日成和范文同致电祝贺
    据新华社平壤17日电　朝鲜民主主义人民共和国首相金日成代表朝鲜政府和人民，打电报给加纳总理恩克鲁玛对非洲独立国家会议的召开表示祝贺。金日成在贺电中表示希望这个会议将大大有助于反对殖民主义、巩固国家独立和维护和平的事业。
    新华社河内16日电　据越南通讯社报道，范文同总理今天代表越南民主共和国政府和人民打电报祝贺非洲独立国家会议的召开。
    范文同总理在贺电中表示希望这次会议在非洲各国之间为了促进这些国家的民族解放运动，为了反对殖民主义、种族歧视和战争以及为了保卫非洲、亚洲和世界的和平而加强团结方面，取得巨大成就。
