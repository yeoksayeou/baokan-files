Title: Hungarian National Table Tennis Team Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1958-04-12
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, April 11th. The Hungarian National Table Tennis Team, a group of twelve people, arrived in Beijing today by Tu-104 aircraft at the invitation of our country.

The Hungarian National Table Tennis Team will hold three friendly matches here with the Beijing Table Tennis Team and the Beijing-Guangzhou Table Tennis Joint Team. The first match is scheduled for the evening of the 12th at the Beijing Gymnasium, consisting of four men's singles matches, four women's singles matches, and one men's doubles match against the Beijing team. The remaining two matches will be held on the evenings of the 13th and 15th, respectively.

The Hungarian National Table Tennis Team is led by Bognár and Boldoczki, Vice Presidents of the Hungarian Table Tennis Association, as the head and deputy head of the team, respectively. The team includes four male and four female athletes: Berczik, Jettvi, Földi, and Bubonyi (the above four are male players), and Kóczián, Mossóczi, Kerekes, and Lantos (the above four are female players). Famous player, 1953 World Men's Singles Champion Sidó, did not come to China due to a foot injury.

### 匈牙利国家乒乓球队到达北京

1958-04-12
第4版()
专栏：

    匈牙利国家乒乓球队到达北京
    新华社11日讯　应邀来我国访问的匈牙利国家乒乓球队一行十二人，今天乘图一○四飞机到达北京。
    匈牙利国家乒乓球队在这里将同北京乒乓球队和北京、广州乒乓球联队举行三次友谊比赛。第一次比赛已经确定12日晚在北京体育馆同北京队举行四场男子单打、四场女子单打和一场男子双打比赛。其余两次比赛将分别在13日和15日晚举行。
    匈牙利国家乒乓球队由匈牙利乒乓球协会副主席鲍格纳尔和鲍尔多齐分别任领队和副领队。全队包括有男、女运动员各四名。他们是：别尔切克、杰特维、福尔迪、布尔尼（以上四名是男选手）、高基安、莫沙齐、克雷克斯、兰托斯（以上四名是女选手）。著名选手、1953年世界男子单打冠军西多因脚伤没有来中国。
