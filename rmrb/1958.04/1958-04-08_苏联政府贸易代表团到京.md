Title: Soviet Government Trade Delegation Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1958-04-08
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, 7th April. The Soviet Government Trade Delegation, led by I.G. Kabanov, Minister of Foreign Trade of the Soviet Union, arrived in Beijing by plane this afternoon.

Those who went to the airport to welcome the delegation included Ye Jizhuang, Minister of Foreign Trade, Vice Ministers Li Zheren and Jiang Ming, Li Huichuan, Deputy Director of the Department of Socialist Countries of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs, and relevant responsible persons of the Ministry of Foreign Trade.

Yeremin, Soviet Commercial Representative to China, and E.G. Sudarikov, Counselor of the Soviet Embassy in China, I.V. Arkhipov, Economic Cooperation Counselor, B.N. Vyryashekin, Counselor, and relevant responsible persons of the Soviet Embassy and the Soviet Commercial Representative Office also went to the airport to greet them.

### 苏联政府贸易代表团到京

1958-04-08
第4版()
专栏：

    苏联政府贸易代表团到京
    新华社7日讯　苏联政府贸易代表团在团长、苏联对外贸易部部长伊·格·卡巴诺夫率领下，今天下午乘飞机到达北京。
    前往机场欢迎的，有对外贸易部部长叶季壮、副部长李哲人、江明，外交部社会主义国家司副司长李汇川和对外贸易部的有关负责人等。
    苏联驻华商务代表叶烈敏和苏联驻华大使馆参赞恩·格·苏达利柯夫，经济合作参赞依·伏·阿希波夫，参赞伯·恩·魏立夏金以及苏联大使馆和苏联商务代表处的有关负责人员也到机场迎接。
