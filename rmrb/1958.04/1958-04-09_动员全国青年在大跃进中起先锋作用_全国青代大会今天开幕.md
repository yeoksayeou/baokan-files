Title: Mobilize the Nation's Youth to Play a Pioneer Role in the Great Leap Forward; National Youth Congress Opens Today
Author:
Date: 1958-04-09
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, 8th. The Third National Congress of the All-China Youth Federation is scheduled to open in Beijing on the 9th. This congress will further mobilize Chinese youth, inspire revolutionary vigor, play a pioneer role in the Socialist Great Leap Forward, and act as a promoter of socialist construction.

The Second National Committee of the All-China Democratic Youth Federation held its fifth meeting on the 7th, and adopted the report to be submitted to the congress entitled "Go All Out, Aim High, and Play a Pioneer Role in the Socialist Great Leap Forward," as well as the new Constitution (draft) of the All-China Youth Federation to be submitted to the congress for discussion, and an explanation of this draft constitution.

A total of 600 delegates are participating in the congress, and 568 have registered as of today. They come from all regions of the country, including youth representatives from all ethnic groups, classes, different occupations, and different religious beliefs; female youth representatives account for more than 20%. The vast majority of these representatives are advanced figures who have emerged in socialist transformation and socialist construction, including labor models, activists, and advanced producers in industry and agriculture; soldiers of the Chinese People's Liberation Army and the Chinese People's Volunteer Army; outstanding young workers in drama, music, and dance, film and drama actors, athletes, referees, young teachers, doctors, nurses, scientific and technological workers, government cadres, and journalists; young industrialists and businessmen who are actively accepting socialist transformation; believers in Buddhism, Islam, Christianity, Catholicism, and Taoism who love the motherland; young neighborhood committee directors and neighborhood public security committee members who are actively engaged in street work; and overseas Chinese students and youth who have returned from abroad.

### 动员全国青年在大跃进中起先锋作用  全国青代大会今天开幕

1958-04-09
第4版()
专栏：

    动员全国青年在大跃进中起先锋作用
    全国青代大会今天开幕
    新华社8日讯　中华全国青年第三次代表大会定9日在北京开幕。这次大会将要进一步动员中国青年，鼓足革命干劲，在社会主义大跃进中起先锋作用，作社会主义建设事业的促进派。
    中华全国民主青年联合会第二届全国委员会在7日举行第五次会议，通过了将要向大会作的题为“鼓足干劲，力争上游，在社会主义大跃进中起先锋作用”的报告，和将要交给大会讨论的新的全国青联章程（草案）以及关于这个章程草案的说明。
    参加大会的代表共六百人，到今天为止已经有五百六十八人报到。他们来自全国各个地区，包括各民族、各阶级出身、不同职业和不同宗教信仰的青年代表；女青年代表占20％多。这些代表绝大多数是在社会主义改造和社会主义建设中涌现出来的先进人物，其中有工农业劳动模范、积极分子和先进生产者；有中国人民解放军和中国人民志愿军战士；有优秀的青年戏剧、音乐、舞蹈工作者，电影和话剧演员，体育运动员、裁判员，青年教师，医生、护士，科学技术工作者，机关干部和新闻工作者；有积极接受社会主义改造的青年工商业者；有热爱祖国的佛教、伊斯兰教、基督教、天主教、道教的教徒；有积极从事街道工作的青年居民委员会主任、街道治安保卫委员；有从海外归国的华侨学生和青年。
