Title: Gromyko Summons U.S. Ambassador to Discuss Summit Meeting Issues
Author: 
Date: 1958-04-19
Page: 5
Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, April 18th: TASS Moscow dispatch: Soviet Foreign Minister Gromyko invited U.S. Ambassador Thompson to the Soviet Foreign Ministry on April 17th to confer with him on issues related to preparations for a summit meeting.

### 葛罗米柯召见美大使  筹商最高级会议问题

1958-04-19
第5版()
专栏：

    葛罗米柯召见美大使
    筹商最高级会议问题
    新华社18日讯　塔斯社莫斯科讯：苏联外交部长葛罗米柯4月17日邀请美国大使汤普逊到苏联外交部，就有关筹备最高级会议的问题同他进行了交谈。
