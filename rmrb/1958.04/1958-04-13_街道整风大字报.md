Street Rectification Big-Character Posters

Editor's Note: The residents of Xi'an City, during the street rectification campaign, have created many big-character posters resembling wall poems. The content is vivid and moving. Here are some selections:

　　Challenge
　　Plan to save rice and flour by cooking,
　　Dismantle and alter, flip and re-pad to save cloth and cotton,
　　Sift ash and mix with water to save coal,
　　Improve cooking to save oil and salt,
　　Tighten expenses to save pocket money,
　　I challenge the whole courtyard,
　　Please sign up quickly to compete.
(Li Xiuzhen, 19 Baishulin Street)

　　Conservation is a Treasure
　　Two hands,
　　Truly skillful,
　　Making fine white rice and noodles well,
　　Making just enough,
　　Making it well,
　　Enough to fill the children's bellies,
　　No spilling rice,
　　No dropping noodles,
　　Don't take it lightly,
　　Grain is truly a treasure,
　　Accumulate a little to make a lot.
　　(Wang Fenxia, Fuxue Lane)

　　Eradicating Illiteracy
　　Since the Foolish Old Man could move mountains,
　　Why worry that illiteracy cannot be eradicated?
　　Today I am determined,
　　To study culture diligently,
　　To learn five characters every night,
　　One hundred and fifty characters a month,
　　After a year of hard study,
　　I will be able to read newspapers and books.
　　(Xie Xiulan, 10 Baishulin Street)

　　Lazy Woman
　　Lazy woman,
　　Truly filthy,
　　Spends the whole day just washing clothes.
　　Wanders to the east house and then to the west house,
　　Chatting and laughing the day away.
　　Others tell her to pay attention to hygiene,
　　Her answer is truly awful,
　　"It's my business, don't interfere,
　　If something happens, I won't complain."
　　 (Zhu Jingxian)

### 街道整风大字报

1958-04-13
第4版()
专栏：

    街道整风大字报
    编者按：西安市的居民，在街道整风中，创作了许多像墙头诗一样的大字报。内容生动感人。现在选择一些刊登在这里。挑战书计划做饭省米面，拆改翻弹省布棉，筛灰拌水省煤炭，改进烹调省油盐，紧缩开支省零钱，我向全院来挑战，请快报名比比看。
  （柏树林街十九号李秀珍）节约宝中宝两只手，真正巧，白米细面做的好，做的少，做的好，娃娃吃了刚刚饱，不撒米，不掉面，不要把它看的淡，粮食本是宝中宝，积少成多不得了。
  （府学巷王芬霞）
  扫文盲既然愚公能移山，何愁文盲扫不完，今天我要下决心，学习文化要认真，每晚认得五个字，一月就认一百五，努力学习一年后，能看报来能读书。
  （柏树林街十号谢秀兰）
  懒婆娘懒婆娘，真正脏，一天光怕洗衣裳。东家串来西家转，说说笑笑过一天。别人叫她讲卫生，她的回答真难听，“我的事，你别管，出了事儿我不怨。”
          （朱静娴）
