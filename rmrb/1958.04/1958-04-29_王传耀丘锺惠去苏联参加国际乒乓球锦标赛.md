Title: Wang Chuanyao and Qiu Zhonghui Go to the Soviet Union to Participate in the International Table Tennis Championships
Author:
Date: 1958-04-29
Page: 7
　　
Xinhua News Agency, 28th April: China's famous table tennis players Wang Chuanyao and Qiu Zhonghui, at the invitation of the Soviet Union's Committee for Physical Culture and Sports under the Council of Ministers, today flew from Beijing to the Soviet Union to participate in the International Table Tennis Championships.

　　This International Table Tennis Championship, hosted by the Soviet Union, is scheduled to begin on May 4th in Moscow. The competition events will include men's and women's singles, doubles, and mixed doubles. Chinese players Wang Chuanyao and Qiu Zhonghui will participate in the men's and women's singles competitions respectively, and will also participate in the mixed doubles competition.

　　The Soviet Union has invited table tennis players from Yugoslavia, Czechoslovakia, Romania, Hungary, Austria and other five countries to participate in this International Table Tennis Championship.

### 王传耀丘锺惠去苏联参加国际乒乓球锦标赛

1958-04-29
第7版()
专栏：体育新闻

    王传耀丘锺惠去苏联参加国际乒乓球锦标赛
    新华社28日讯　中国著名乒乓球选手王传耀和丘锺惠应苏联部长会议体育运动委员会邀请，今天由北京乘飞机去苏联参加国际乒乓球锦标赛。
    这个由苏联举办的国际乒乓球锦标赛定5月4日开始在莫斯科举行。比赛项目将有男子和女子的单打、双打和男女混合双打。中国选手王传耀和丘锺惠除分别参加男子和女子的单打比赛外，还将参加男女混合双打比赛。
    苏联邀请参加这次国际乒乓球锦标赛的，还有南斯拉夫、捷克斯洛伐克、罗马尼亚、匈牙利、奥地利等五个国家的乒乓球选手。
