Title: Grand Gathering in Warsaw Celebrates 13th Anniversary of the Sino-Soviet Treaty
Author:
Date: 1958-04-23
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, Warsaw, April 21st: A celebration rally for the 13th anniversary of the signing of the Sino-Soviet Treaty of Friendship, Alliance and Mutual Assistance and Post-War Cooperation was held today at the Palace of Culture and Science here.

Attending the celebration rally were Polish United Workers' Party and state leaders Gomulka, Zawadzki, Cyrankiewicz, and 4,000 representatives from all walks of life in Poland.

Invited to participate in the rally were Voroshilov, Chairman of the Presidium of the Supreme Soviet of the Soviet Union, who is currently visiting Poland, Furtseva, member of the Presidium of the Central Committee of the CPSU and Secretary of the Central Committee, as well as diplomatic envoys from various countries to Poland.

First Secretary of the Central Committee of the Polish United Workers' Party, Gomulka, delivered the opening speech, first expressing a warm welcome to Voroshilov and his entourage.

Gomulka evaluated the significance of the Sino-Soviet Treaty. He said that the Polish people need to further consolidate the traditional friendship with the Soviet people and strengthen solidarity with the socialist camp.

Gomulka condemned West Germany for violating the will of the European people, accelerating the revival of militarism, and arming the West German army with American atomic weapons. He pointed out that Poland should strengthen its vigilance.

Next, Chairman of the Council of Ministers of Poland, Cyrankiewicz, gave a report on the 13th anniversary of the Sino-Soviet Treaty.

Finally, Voroshilov delivered a speech at the meeting. He said that the people of the Soviet Union and Poland have been, are, and will always be brothers. No enemy can destroy our friendship. He said that we will never let you fall into misfortune. You should not let us fall into misfortune either. Our two countries should unite and support each other on the principles of Marxism and internationalism.

Voroshilov's speech was warmly welcomed.

### 华沙盛会庆祝苏波友好条约十三周年

1958-04-23
第5版()
专栏：

    华沙盛会庆祝苏波友好条约十三周年
    据新华社华沙21日电　今天，这里的文化科学宫举行了苏波友好、互助与战后合作条约签订十三周年庆祝大会。
    出席庆祝大会的有：波兰统一工人党和国家的领导人哥穆尔卡、萨瓦茨基、西伦凯维兹和波兰各界代表四千人。
    应邀参加大会的有：正在波兰访问的苏联最高苏维埃主席团主席伏罗希洛夫、苏共中央主席团委员、中央书记福尔采娃，以及各国驻波兰外交使节。
    波兰统一工人党中央委员会第一书记哥穆尔卡致开会词，他首先向伏罗希洛夫和他的随行人员表示热烈欢迎。
    哥穆尔卡评价了苏波条约的意义。他说，波兰人民需要更加巩固同苏联人民的传统友谊，更加加强同社会主义阵营的团结。
    哥穆尔卡谴责西德违反欧洲人民的意志，加速恢复军国主义，用美国原子武器武装西德军队。他指出，波兰应该加强警惕。
    接着，波兰部长会议主席西伦凯维兹作了关于苏波条约十三周年的报告。
    最后，伏罗希洛夫在会上发表了讲话。他说，苏波两国人民过去、现在和将来永远是兄弟。任何敌人也不能破坏我们的友谊。他说，我们任何时候也不让你们落入不幸。你们也不要让我们落入不幸，我们两国要在马克思主义和国际主义原则上互相团结，互相支持。
    伏罗希洛夫的讲话受到了热烈的欢迎。
