Title: Resolution Made by Chinese Writers Association and Chinese Artists Association: Removal of Ding Ling, Feng Xuefeng, and Jiang Feng from Vice Chairmanships

Author:

Date: 1958-04-17
Page: 7

    Chinese Writers Association, Chinese Artists Association Make Resolutions
    Removal of Ding Ling, Feng Xuefeng, and Jiang Feng from Vice Chairmanships
    (People's Daily News) The Chinese Writers Association Council, based on the proposal of the Presidium, decided to remove the rightists Ding Ling and Feng Xuefeng from their positions as Vice Chairmen of the Chinese Writers Association.
    The 12th Standing Council Meeting of the Chinese Artists Association also made a resolution to remove the rightist Jiang Feng from his positions as Vice Chairman and Standing Council Member of the Chinese Artists Association.

### 中国作家协会、中国美术家协会作出决议  撤销丁玲、冯雪峰、江丰副主席职务

1958-04-17
第7版()
专栏：

    中国作家协会、中国美术家协会作出决议
    撤销丁玲、冯雪峰、江丰副主席职务
    本报讯　中国作家协会理事会根据主席团的建议决定撤销右派分子丁玲、冯雪峰的中国作家协会副主席的职务。
    中国美术家协会第十二次常务理事会也作出决议，撤销了右派分子江丰的中国美术家协会副主席和常务理事的职务。
