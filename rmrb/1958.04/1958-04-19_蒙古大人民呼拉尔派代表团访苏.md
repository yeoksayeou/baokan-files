Title: Mongolian Great People's Hural Delegation Visits Soviet Union
Author:
Date: 1958-04-19
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, April 18th: The delegation of the Great People's Hural of the Mongolian People's Republic arrived in Moscow today. The delegation is visiting the Soviet Union at the invitation of the Supreme Soviet of the Soviet Union. The head of the delegation is Surenjav, Chairman of the Great People's Hural of Mongolia.

### 蒙古大人民呼拉尔派代表团访苏

1958-04-19
第6版()
专栏：

    蒙古大人民呼拉尔派代表团访苏
    据新华社莫斯科18日电　蒙古人民共和国大人民呼拉尔代表团今天到达莫斯科。代表团是应苏联最高苏维埃的邀请到苏联访问的。代表团团长是蒙古大人民呼拉尔主席苏伦扎布。
