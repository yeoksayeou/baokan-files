Title: Vice Premier Chen Yi Receives Hungarian Economic and Technical Delegation
Author:
Date: 1958-04-06
Page: 2
Xinhua News Agency, 5th -- Vice Premier of the State Council Chen Yi received all members of the Hungarian Economic and Technical Delegation headed by Hungarian Minister of Foreign Trade, Jenő Incze, this morning.

Present at the reception were: Minister of Foreign Trade Ye Ji-zhuang, Deputy Director of the Department of Socialist Countries of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs Chen Boqing, and Director of the Second Bureau of the Ministry of Foreign Trade Jia Shi, and others.

Also present at the reception was the Hungarian Ambassador to China, Nogradi.

### 陈毅副总理接见匈经济技术代表团

1958-04-06
第2版()
专栏：

    陈毅副总理接见匈经济技术代表团
    新华社5日讯　国务院副总理陈毅今天上午接见了以匈牙利对外贸易部部长英切·耶诺为首的匈牙利经济技术代表团全体人员。
    接见时在座的有：对外贸易部部长叶季壮、外交部社会主义国家司副司长陈伯清、对外贸易部二局局长贾石等。
    接见时，匈牙利驻中国大使诺格拉第也在座。
