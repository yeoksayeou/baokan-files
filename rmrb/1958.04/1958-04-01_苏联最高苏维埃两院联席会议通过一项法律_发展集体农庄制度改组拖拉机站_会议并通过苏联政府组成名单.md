Title: The Supreme Soviet of the Soviet Union Jointly Passes a Law: Developing the Collective Farm System and Reorganizing Tractor Stations; The Meeting Also Approved the List of the Soviet Government's Composition
Author:
Date: 1958-04-01
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, March 31st. Our correspondent reports: The joint session of the Soviet Supreme Soviet's Union Council and National Council was held at 3:00 PM (Moscow time) today in the Great Kremlin Palace.

The chairman of the meeting announced that the two houses had concluded the discussion on the further development of the collective farm system and the reorganization of machine tractor stations, and that the Chairman of the Council of Ministers and First Secretary of the Central Committee of the CPSU, Khrushchev, would now make a concluding speech on the discussion of this issue.

Then, Comrade Khrushchev made a concluding speech.

After Khrushchev's speech, the meeting unanimously passed the law on further developing the collective farm system and reorganizing machine tractor stations.

Khrushchev then, amidst warm applause, presented the list of the government's composition, including: First Deputy Chairman of the Council of Ministers, Frol Romanovich Kozlov and Anastas Ivanovich Mikoyan; Deputy Chairman of the Council of Ministers, Alexei Nikolaevich Kosygin, Alexander Feodorovich Zasyadko, Dmitry Feodorovich Ustinov, Joseph Iosifovich Kuzmin (concurrently Chairman of the Soviet State Planning Committee); Foreign Minister A. A. Gromyko, Finance Minister A. G. Zverev, Defense Minister R. Ya. Malinovsky, Interior Minister N. P. Dudorov, Health Minister M. D. Kovrigina, Culture Minister N. A. Mikhailov, Agriculture Minister V. V. Matskevich, Higher Education Minister V. P. Yelyutin, Chairman of the Board of the State Bank N. A. Bulganin, and Chairman of the State Security Committee I. A. Serov.

The representatives unanimously approved the list of the government's composition proposed by Khrushchev.

The meeting then moved to the next item on the agenda, with Soviet Foreign Minister Gromyko delivering a statement on the cessation of testing atomic and hydrogen weapons.

Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, March 31st. Our correspondent reports at 7:30 PM (Moscow time) today: The joint session of the Soviet Supreme Soviet's Union Council and National Council, after listening to Comrade Gromyko's statement, launched a discussion on the statement. The representatives unanimously approved the new initiative of the Soviet government proposed by Gromyko in the discussion.

The meeting then unanimously passed the resolution on the Soviet Union's unilateral cessation of testing atomic and hydrogen weapons.

The meeting passed documents such as a letter to the US Congress, a letter to the British Parliament, and letters to other countries' parliaments on the Soviet Union's unilateral cessation of testing atomic and hydrogen weapons.

At this point, the entire agenda of the Soviet Supreme Soviet meeting was completed, and it was declared victoriously closed.

### 苏联最高苏维埃两院联席会议通过一项法律  发展集体农庄制度改组拖拉机站  会议并通过苏联政府组成名单

1958-04-01
第1版()
专栏：

    苏联最高苏维埃两院联席会议通过一项法律
    发展集体农庄制度改组拖拉机站
    会议并通过苏联政府组成名单
    新华社莫斯科31日电　本社记者报道：苏联最高苏维埃联盟院和民族院的联席会议今天下午三时（莫斯科时间）在大克里姆林宫举行。
    大会主席宣布，两院已经结束对关于进一步发展集体农庄制度和改组机器拖拉机站问题的讨论，现在由部长会议主席、苏共中央第一书记赫鲁晓夫作有关这一问题讨论的结束发言。
    接着，赫鲁晓夫同志作了结束发言。
    赫鲁晓夫发言后，会议一致通过了关于进一步发展集体农庄制度和改组机器拖拉机站的法律。
    赫鲁晓夫接着在热烈掌声中提出了政府组成的名单，其中包括：部长会议第一副主席弗罗尔·罗曼诺维奇·科兹洛夫和阿纳斯塔斯·伊凡诺维奇·米高扬；部长会议副主席阿列克谢·尼古拉耶维奇·柯西金，亚历山大·费奥多罗维奇·扎霞迪科，德米特里·费奥多罗维奇·乌斯季诺夫，约瑟夫·约西弗维奇·库兹明（兼苏联国家计划委员会主席）；外交部长安·安·葛罗米柯，财政部长阿·格·兹维列夫，国防部长罗·亚·马利诺夫斯基，内务部长尼·帕·杜多罗夫，卫生部长玛·德·科夫里金娜，文化部长尼·亚·米哈伊洛夫，农业部长弗·弗·马茨凯维奇，高等教育部长维·彼·叶留金，国家银行管理委员会主席尼·亚·布尔加宁，国家安全委员会主席伊·亚·谢罗夫。
    代表们一致通过了赫鲁晓夫提出的政府组成名单。
    会议随后进入下一议程，由苏联外交部长葛罗米柯发表关于停止试验原子武器和氢武器的声明。
    新华社莫斯科31日电　本社记者今天下午十九时三十分（莫斯科时间）报道：苏联最高苏维埃联盟院和民族院联席会议在听取了葛罗米柯同志的声明以后，就这个声明展开了讨论。代表们在讨论中一致赞成葛罗米柯提出的苏联政府的新倡议。
    会议接着一致通过了关于苏联单方面停止试验原子武器和氢武器问题的决议。
    会议通过了关于就苏联单方面停止试验原子武器和氢武器问题告美国国会书、告英国议会书，以及告其他国家国会书等文件。
    至此，苏联最高苏维埃会议全部议程进行完毕，宣告胜利闭幕。
