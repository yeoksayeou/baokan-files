Title: Preparatory Committee for Participation in the Leipzig International Friendly Army Sports Games Established in Beijing; General Li Da Appointed as Chairman
Author:
Date: 1958-04-15
Page: 7

Xinhua News Agency, 14th — The Preparatory Committee of the Chinese People's Liberation Army for the First International Friendly Army Sports Games in Leipzig was recently established in Beijing. General Li Da, Deputy Minister of the General Training Department of the Chinese People's Liberation Army, was appointed as Chairman of the Preparatory Committee. Lieutenant General Liu Zhijian, Deputy Director of the General Political Department, and Rong Gaotang, Deputy Director of the Physical Culture and Sports Commission of the People's Republic of China, were appointed as Vice Chairmen.

At its first preparatory meeting recently held, the Preparatory Committee decided that the selection of athletes would be based on designated events and record standards, with selection as candidates arrive and no separate sports meet held. All selections are to be completed before May 20.

### 参加国际友军来比锡体育运动会  筹备委员会在京成立  李达上将任主任委员

1958-04-15
第7版()
专栏：

    参加国际友军来比锡体育运动会
    筹备委员会在京成立
    李达上将任主任委员
    新华社14日讯　中国人民解放军第一届国际友军来比锡体育运动大会筹备委员会，最近在北京成立。筹备委员会由中国人民解放军训练总监部副部长李达上将任主任委员，总政治部副主任刘志坚中将、中华人民共和国体育运动委员会副主任荣高棠任副主任委员。
    筹备委员会在最近举行的第一次筹备会议上，决定运动员的选拔方式采取按指定项目和纪录标准，随到随选，不另举行运动会，在5月20日前全部选拔完毕。
