Title: Vice Premier Chen Yi Receives Brahim Ghafa

Author:

Date: 1958-04-03
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, April 2nd: Vice Premier of the State Council and Minister of Foreign Affairs Chen Yi received Brahim Ghafa, representative of the Algerian National Liberation Front, who came to China to participate in the "Support Algeria Day" activities, this morning.

Present at the reception were Liao Chengzhi, Vice Chairman of the Chinese Asian Solidarity Committee, and Ke Hua, Director of the West Asia and Africa Department of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs, among others.

### 陈毅副总理接见卜拉欣·加法

1958-04-03
第4版()
专栏：

    陈毅副总理接见卜拉欣·加法
    新华社2日讯　国务院副总理兼外交部长陈毅今天上午接见了前来我国参加“声援阿尔及利亚日”活动的阿尔及利亚民族解放阵线代表卜拉欣·加法。
    接见时在座的有中国亚洲团结委员会副主席廖承志和外交部西亚非洲司司长柯华等人。
