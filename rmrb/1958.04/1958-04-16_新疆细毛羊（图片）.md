Title: Xinjiang Fine Wool Sheep (Picture)
Author: Shasha
Date: 1958-04-16
Page: 2

    Xinjiang Fine Wool Sheep
    The Xinjiang Fine Wool Sheep is a wool-and-meat breed of sheep developed by crossbreeding Soviet Caucasian rams with Kazakh native ewes. Xinjiang Fine Wool Sheep have a high wool yield, and their wool is fine and strong, making it a high-quality raw material for the wool textile industry. (Photo by Shasha)
    A flock of sheep at the Gongnais Sheep Breeding Farm in Xinjiang.
    Breeding rams.

### 新疆细毛羊（图片）
莎沙

专栏：

    新疆细毛羊
    新疆细毛羊是以苏联卡夫卡斯种公羊与哈萨克土种母羊杂交育成的一种毛肉兼用的细毛羊。新疆细毛羊的剪毛量高，羊毛细、拉力强，是高级的毛纺原料。　莎沙摄
    新疆巩乃斯种羊场的羊群。
