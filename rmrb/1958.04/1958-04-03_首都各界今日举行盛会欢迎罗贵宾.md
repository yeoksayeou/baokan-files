Title: All Circles in the Capital Hold Grand Meeting Today to Welcome Distinguished Guest Luo
Author:
Date: 1958-04-03
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency, April 2nd. People from all circles in the capital will hold a grand rally on the afternoon of the 3rd to welcome the Romanian government delegation headed by Chivu Stoica, Chairman of the Romanian Council of Ministers. The Central People's Broadcasting Station will broadcast the live proceedings of the welcome rally on the first program starting at 4:25 pm on the 3rd.

### 首都各界今日举行盛会欢迎罗贵宾

1958-04-03
第4版()
专栏：

    首都各界今日举行盛会欢迎罗贵宾
    新华社2日讯　首都各界人民将在3日下午举行盛大集会，欢迎以罗马尼亚部长会议主席基伏·斯托伊卡为首的罗马尼亚政府代表团。中央人民广播电台将从3日下午四点二十五分开始，在第一种节目中转播欢迎大会的实况。
