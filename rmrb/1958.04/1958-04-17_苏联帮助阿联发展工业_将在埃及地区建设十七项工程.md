Title: Soviet Union to Assist the United Arab Republic in Developing Industry; Seventeen Projects to be Constructed in the Egyptian Region
Author:
Date: 1958-04-17
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, Cairo, April 15th: According to the economic and technical cooperation agreement between the two countries, the Soviet Union will provide technical assistance to the United Arab Republic in the construction and expansion of seventeen industrial projects in the Egyptian region. The two sides signed a contract in Cairo today in this regard.

The projects in the contract include factories for the production of steel, coking coal, aluminum, cables, non-ferrous metals, medical equipment, and milk.

A contract was also signed today for the establishment of ten vocational training centers in the Egyptian region and the training of skilled Egyptian industrial workers by Soviet experts.

### 苏联帮助阿联发展工业  将在埃及地区建设十七项工程

1958-04-17
第6版()
专栏：

    苏联帮助阿联发展工业
    将在埃及地区建设十七项工程
    据新华社开罗15日电　根据两国经济和技术合作协定，苏联将给阿拉伯联合共和国在埃及地区修建和扩建的十七项工业工程提供技术援助。双方今天已经在开罗签订了这方面的合同。
    合同中的工程包括生产钢铁、焦煤、铝、电缆、有色金属、医药器材、牛奶的工厂。
    今天还签订了在埃及地区建立十个职业训练中心和由苏联专家训练埃及熟训产业工人的合同。
