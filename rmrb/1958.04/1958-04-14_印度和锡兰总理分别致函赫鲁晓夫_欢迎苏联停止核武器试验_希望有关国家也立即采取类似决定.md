Title: Indian and Ceylonese Prime Ministers Send Letters to Khrushchev Welcoming the Soviet Union's Cessation of Nuclear Weapons Testing, Hoping Relevant Countries Will Take Similar Decisions Immediately

Author:

Date: 1958-04-14
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, April 12th: According to TASS, Indian Prime Minister Nehru wrote a letter to the Chairman of the Council of Ministers of the Soviet Union, Khrushchev, on April 9th, stating: "The Indian government and the Indian people welcome and support the Soviet government's decision to cease testing nuclear weapons and thermonuclear weapons."

The letter stated: "As you know, India has firmly advocated for the cessation of these tests for several years. Therefore, your government's initiative makes us particularly happy."

The letter stated: "This decision is an important step in easing international tensions, and I hope that other countries related to this issue can also take similar decisions immediately."

Nehru said in the letter: "Ordinary people all over the world expect major powers to reach an agreement. Because in this way, the Earth's atmosphere will never be polluted again. I have no doubt that such an agreement will create favorable conditions for resolving other international issues that make the world uneasy."

Xinhua News Agency, April 13th: According to the Indian Press Trust on April 12th, Ceylonese Prime Minister Bandaranaike welcomed the Soviet Union's unilateral decision to stop nuclear weapons testing, believing it was a legitimate step beneficial to world peace.

Bandaranaike made these remarks in a reply letter to the Chairman of the Council of Ministers of the Soviet Union, Khrushchev, on April 11th. He also said in the letter that he expressed the hope that other countries in the world possessing atomic and hydrogen weapons would take the same steps in order to save the world from the consequences of disaster.

### 印度和锡兰总理分别致函赫鲁晓夫  欢迎苏联停止核武器试验  希望有关国家也立即采取类似决定

1958-04-14
第5版()
专栏：

    印度和锡兰总理分别致函赫鲁晓夫
    欢迎苏联停止核武器试验
    希望有关国家也立即采取类似决定
    新华社13日讯　据塔斯社莫斯科12日讯：印度总理尼赫鲁4月9日写信给苏联部长会议主席赫鲁晓夫说：“印度政府和印度人民欢迎和支持苏联政府关于停止试验核武器和热核武器的决定。”
    信中说：“您知道，几年来我们印度一直坚决主张停止这些试验。因此，贵国政府的倡议使我们感到特别高兴。”
    信中说：“这一决定是缓和国际紧张局势的一个重要步骤，我希望，同这个问题有关的其他国家，也能立即采取类似的决定。”
    尼赫鲁在信中说：“全世界的普通人们都期待各大国之间达成协议。因为这样一来，地球大气就永远不会再被染污了。我毫不怀疑，这样的协定将为解决其他使世界感到不安的国际问题创造有利条件。”
    据新华社13日讯　据印度报业托辣斯4月12日报道，锡兰总理班达拉奈克表示欢迎苏联单方面停止核武器试验的决定，认为这是有益于世界和平的正当步骤。
    班达拉奈克是在4月11日给苏联部长会议主席赫鲁晓夫的回信上说这番话的。他在信上还说，他表示希望，世界上其他拥有原子武器和氢武器的国家也采取同样的步骤，以便把世界从灾难的后果中拯救出来。
