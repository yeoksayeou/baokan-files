Title: The Capable Automobile
Author: Gao Guoquan
Date: 1958-04-09
Page: 3

    The "Gong Nong" brand all-purpose agricultural automobile, successfully trial-produced by the Shenyang Automobile Assembly Plant, can be used for power generation, driving water pumps, flour mills, logging machines, sprayers, threshers, chaff cutters, and dozens of other agricultural machinery, in addition to carrying goods and people. It can also replace tractors for plowing, harrowing, and sowing.

    Top Image: ① Filling up with water, preparing for a test drive. ② The automobile undergoes a water pumping irrigation test. If a seven-inch pipe is used, it can pump more than 200 tons of water per hour. ③ The automobile drives a chaff cutter to cut grass, capable of cutting more than 600 kilograms per hour. ④ The automobile drives an electric saw, capable of cutting wood. ⑤ The automobile drives a rice mill, capable of milling 200 catties of rice per hour. ⑥ The automobile can also automatically unload cargo, requiring only one person to operate the unloading.
          Xinhua News Agency reporter Gao Guoquan (with pictures)

### 能干的小汽车
高国权
1958-04-09
第3版()
专栏：

    能干的小汽车
    沈阳汽车装配厂试制成功的工农牌万能农业小汽车，除载货、拉人外，可以用来发电、带动抽水机、磨粉机、伐木机、喷雾机、打谷机、铡草机等几十种农业机械工作，还可以代替拖拉机进行耕地、耙地、播种。
    上图：①把水灌足，准备出车试验。②小汽车作抽水灌溉试验。如用七寸管子，每小时可抽水二百多吨。③小汽车带动铡草机铡草，每小时可铡六百多公斤。④小汽车带动电锯，可以切开木材。⑤小汽车带动磨米机，每小时可磨米二百斤。⑥小汽车还能自动卸车，卸车的时候只要一个人操纵。
          新华社记者　高国权摄（附图片）
