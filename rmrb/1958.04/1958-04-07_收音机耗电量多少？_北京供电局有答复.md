Title: How much electricity does a radio consume? Beijing Power Supply Bureau responds

Author:

Date: 1958-04-07
Page: 3

Letter to the Editor:

How much electricity does a radio consume?
Beijing Power Supply Bureau responds

To the Family Committee of the Fifth Construction Engineering Company:

Regarding the standard electricity consumption of various radios, we have the following explanation from Comrade Fang Zenghong of the Beijing Power Supply Bureau:

A typical three-tube or four-tube radio consumes the same amount of electricity as a 25-watt light bulb. A five-tube radio consumes the same amount of electricity as a 40-watt light bulb. A six-tube radio consumes the same amount of electricity as a 60-watt light bulb. Based on the above consumption standards, a three-tube or four-tube radio consumes one kilowatt-hour of electricity after 40 hours of use, and 0.025 kilowatt-hours of electricity after one hour of use. Other radios can be estimated similarly. Editor

### 收音机耗电量多少？  北京供电局有答复

1958-04-07
第3版()
专栏：来信

    收音机耗电量多少？
    北京供电局有答复
    第五建筑工程公司家属委员会：
    关于各种收音机耗电量标准问题，我们请北京供电局方增宏同志解答如下：
    一般三灯及四灯收音机，等于一个二十五瓦灯泡耗电量。五灯收音机等于一个四十瓦灯泡耗电量。六灯收音机等于一个六十瓦灯泡耗电量。根据以上耗电标准，一个三灯或四灯收音机，使用四十个小时才耗电一度，使用一小时耗电零点零二五度。其他收音机可类推。	编者
