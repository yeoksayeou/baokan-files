Title: China-Vietnam Aid Protocol Signed in Beijing: Assistance for the Construction and Renovation of Eighteen Industrial Enterprises Over Four Years
Author:
Date: 1958-04-01
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, March 31st—The protocol between the Chinese and Vietnamese governments regarding the People's Republic of China's aid to the Democratic Republic of Vietnam in 1958, and the agreement on the People's Republic of China's aid to the Democratic Republic of Vietnam for the construction and renovation of eighteen industrial enterprises, were signed in Beijing today.

    These two documents stipulate that, from 1958 to 1961, the Chinese side will use a portion of the 800 million RMB yuan in grant aid to Vietnam to help Vietnam build and renovate eighteen industrial enterprise projects, namely: Viet Tri Paper Mill, Co Dinh Chromite Mine, Shanxi Sugar Mill, Yi An Sugar Mill, Rubber Products Factory, Hanoi Cultural and Educational Supplies Factory, Haiphong Enamel Factory, Bac Giang Ceramics Factory, Soap and Toothpaste Factory, Hanoi Cigarette Factory, Hanoi Knitting Factory, Haiphong Battery Factory, Van Dien Dry Battery Factory, Haiphong Plastic Products Factory, Viet Tri Thermal Power Plant, Hanoi Foundry Workshop, Giang Cao Water Diversion Project Gates, Viet Tri Electrolytic Salt Factory, etc.; as well as providing Vietnam with free supplies of cotton, cotton yarn, paper, steel, wires and cables, various machines, chemical raw materials, and daily necessities, etc.

    At the same time, the Chinese and Vietnamese governments also signed an agreement on the mutual supply of goods and payment for the year 1958. According to the agreement, China will supply Vietnam with cotton cloth, flour, factory and mining equipment, industrial raw materials, medicines and medical equipment, etc. Vietnam will supply China with apatite, chromite sand, cement, rice, corn, live pigs, cardamom, coffee, timber, raw lacquer, etc. The trade volume between China and Vietnam this year has increased compared to last year.

    Ye Jizhuang, Minister of Foreign Trade of the People's Republic of China, and Phan Anh, Minister of Commerce of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam, signed the above-mentioned documents on behalf of their respective governments.

### 中国援助越南议定书在京签字  四年内援助建设和改建十八项工业企业

1958-04-01
第1版()
专栏：

    中国援助越南议定书在京签字
    四年内援助建设和改建十八项工业企业
    新华社31日讯　中越两国政府关于1958年中华人民共和国援助越南民主共和国的议定书和关于中华人民共和国援助越南民主共和国建设和改建十八个工业企业项目的协定，今天在北京签字。
    这两个文件规定，中国方面将自1958年起到1961年内，用无偿援助越南的八亿元人民币援款中的一部分，帮助越南新建和改建十八个工业企业项目，即：越池造纸厂，古定铬铁矿，山西糖厂，宜安糖厂，橡胶制品厂，河内文教用品厂，海防搪瓷厂，北江陶瓷厂，肥皂牙膏厂，河内卷烟厂，河内针织厂，海防蓄电池厂，文典干电池厂，海防塑料制品厂，越池热电厂，河内铸工车间，江高引水工程闸门，越池电解食盐厂等；以及无偿供应越南棉花、棉纱、纸张、钢材、电线电缆、各种机器、化工原料和日用百货等。
    同时，中越两国政府还签订了关于1958年度互相供应货物和付款的协定。根据协定的规定，中国将供给越南棉布、面粉、厂矿设备、工业用原料、医药及医疗器械等。越南将供应中国磷灰石、铬矿砂、水泥、大米、玉米、生猪、砂仁、咖啡、木材、生漆等。今年中越两国的贸易额比去年有所增加。
    代表两国政府在上述文件上签字的，中华人民共和国方面是对外贸易部部长叶季壮，越南民主共和国方面是商业部部长潘英。
