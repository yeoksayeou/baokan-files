Title: West Germany and Atomic Armament: Ollenhauer Advocates Referendum; West German, French, and Dutch Youth Suggest a European Youth Conference

Author:

Date: 1958-04-15
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, April 14th, Bonn Dispatch: The chairman of the West German Social Democratic Party, Ollenhauer, stated at a press conference held in Bremen, West Germany, on the evening of April 12th that if the Federal Parliament rejects the Social Democratic Party's proposal for a national referendum on the issue of atomic armament, the Social Democratic Party will submit proposals in the state parliaments of West Germany, suggesting that referendums be held separately in each state.

Ollenhauer reiterated in Hanover on April 13th that taking warning strikes or general strikes as a means of struggle against the atomic armament of the West German army is "completely constitutional." He emphasized that the Federal Parliament's adoption of the resolution on atomic armament on March 25th does not mean the end of the matter, but only marks the beginning of a mass protest movement.

Xinhua News Agency, April 13th, Berlin Dispatch from TASS: According to a report by the German News Agency, youth representatives from West Germany, France, and the Netherlands held a meeting in Friedrichshafen, West Germany. The participants in the meeting suggested to all youth organizations in Europe that a European Youth Conference be convened to discuss the issue of fighting against atomic armament.

The youth representatives from the three countries welcomed the plan to establish nuclear-weapon-free zones in Europe, believing that this is the first feasible step towards easing international tensions.

### 西德要不要原子武装  奥伦豪尔主张公民投票  西德法荷青年建议召开欧洲青年会议

1958-04-15
第5版()
专栏：

    西德要不要原子武装
    奥伦豪尔主张公民投票
    西德法荷青年建议召开欧洲青年会议
    新华社14日讯　波恩消息：西德社会民主党主席奥伦豪尔12日晚上在西德不来梅市举行的记者招待会上说，如果联邦议院拒绝社会民主党所提出的关于就原子武装问题举行全国公民投票的议案，社会民主党就要在西德各州议会中提出议案，建议在各州分别举行公民投票。
    奥伦豪尔13日在汉诺威重申，采取警告性罢工或总罢工的斗争方式来反对西德军队配备原子武器是“完全合乎宪法的”。他强调说，联邦议院3月25日通过有关原子武装的决议并不意味着事情的了结，而只是标志着群众性抗议运动的开始。
    新华社13日讯　据塔斯社柏林13日讯：据德意志通讯社报道，西德、法国、荷兰三国青年代表在西德腓特烈港举行了会见。会见参加者向欧洲一切青年组织建议召开欧洲青年会议，讨论为反对原子军备而斗争的问题。
    三国青年代表欢迎在欧洲建立无原子武器地区的计划，认为这是缓和国际局势的第一个可能实现的步骤。
