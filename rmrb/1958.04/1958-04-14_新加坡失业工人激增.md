Title: Sharp Increase in Unemployment in Singapore

Author:
Date: 1958-04-14
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, Singapore, 12th – According to statistics released by the Singapore Ministry of Labor, the number of unemployed workers registered at employment agencies had reached 8,926 by the end of March.

The statistics show that a total of 1,492 unemployed workers registered at employment agencies in March, an increase of 648 compared to February, while only 163 people obtained employment.

### 新加坡失业工人激增

1958-04-14
第6版()
专栏：

    新加坡失业工人激增
    新华社12日讯　新加坡消息：据新加坡劳工部发表的统计数字，到3月底为止，在职业介绍所登记的失业工人已经达到八千九百二十六人。
    统计表明，在3月份前往职业介绍所登记的失业工人共有一千四百九十二人，比2月份增加了六百四十八人，而获得职业的只有一百六十三人。
