Title: Eleven Overseas Chinese Returned from Japan

Author:

Date: 1958-04-16
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Tianjin, April 15th. Eleven overseas Chinese returned to the motherland today on the Japanese ship "Hakusan Maru." Chen Rengui, a representative of the Tokyo Overseas Chinese Association, and others accompanied them to Tianjin New Port.

### 十一名旅日华侨回国

1958-04-16
第4版()
专栏：

    十一名旅日华侨回国
    新华社天津15日电　十一名旅日华侨今天乘日本轮船“白山丸”回到祖国。陪同他们到达天津新港的还有东京华侨总会的代表陈仁贵等人。
