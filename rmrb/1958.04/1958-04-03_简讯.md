Title: Brief News

Author:

Date: 1958-04-03
Page: 5

    Brief News
    Chinese Youth Delegation Leaves Cairo for Damascus
    Cairo dispatch: After a month-long visit to various parts of Egypt, the Chinese Youth Delegation left here by plane on the morning of the 2nd for Damascus.
    Finnish President Kekkonen to Visit Soviet Union in May
    Moscow dispatch: Finnish President Kekkonen, at the invitation of Voroshilov, Chairman of the Presidium of the Supreme Soviet of the Soviet Union, will visit the Soviet Union on May 22 this year.

专栏：简讯

    简讯
    我青年代表团离
    开罗赴大马士革
    开罗讯：中国青年代表团在埃及各地访问一个月后，2日上午乘飞机离开这里前往大马士革。
    芬总统吉科宁五月访苏
    莫斯科讯：芬兰共和国总统吉科宁应苏联最高苏维埃主席团主席伏罗希洛夫的邀请，将在今年5月22日到苏联访问。
