Title: Czechoslovak Power Workers' Union Delegation Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1958-04-22
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, April 21st: At the invitation of the National Committee of the Chinese Water Conservancy and Electric Power Workers' Union, the Czechoslovak Power Workers' Union Delegation, consisting of four members led by Chairman Josef Irašek, arrived in Beijing by plane yesterday. Huang Minwei, Chairman of the National Committee of the Chinese Water Conservancy and Electric Power Workers' Union, and Vice Chairman Zhang Jin were at the airport to welcome them.

### 捷动力工会代表团到京

1958-04-22
第4版()
专栏：

    捷动力工会代表团到京
    新华社21日讯　应中国水利电力工会全国委员会的邀请前来我国访问的捷克斯洛伐克动力工会代表团一行四人，在团长约瑟夫·伊拉谢克率领下昨天乘飞机到达北京。到机场欢迎的有中国水利电力工会全国委员会主席黄民伟、副主席张金等。
