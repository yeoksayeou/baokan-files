Title: CPC Central Committee Sends Congratulatory Message to CPI Special Congress
Author: 
Date: 1958-04-06
Page: 1

    Xinhua News Agency, 5th: The Central Committee of the Communist Party of China sent a congratulatory message to the Special Congress of the Communist Party of India. The full text is as follows: To the Central Committee of the Communist Party of India, and to all comrades of the Special Congress of the Communist Party of India:
    The Central Committee of the Communist Party of China, on behalf of all members of the Communist Party of China, extends warm fraternal congratulations to your Special Congress and wishes your Special Congress complete success.
    The great Indian people have waged unremitting struggles to consolidate national independence, develop the national economy, strive for social progress, oppose colonialism, and safeguard world peace. In this struggle, the Communist Party of India has made significant contributions.
    We firmly believe that this special congress of your party will play a new and outstanding role in defending world peace, consolidating India's independence, promoting national construction, and striving for social progress.
               The Central Committee of the Communist Party of China, April 5, 1958

### 中共中央电贺印共特别代表大会

1958-04-06
第1版()
专栏：

    中共中央电贺印共特别代表大会
    新华社5日讯　中国共产党中央委员会致印度共产党特别代表大会的贺电，全文如下：印度共产党中央委员会转印度共产党特别代表大会全体同志们：
    中国共产党中央委员会谨代表中国共产党全体党员向你们的特别代表大会致以热烈的兄弟般的祝贺，祝你们的特别代表大会获得圆满的成功。
    伟大的印度人民为巩固民族独立，发展民族经济，争取社会进步，反对殖民主义和维护世界和平进行了坚持不懈的斗争。在这个斗争中，印度共产党作出了重大的贡献。
    我们坚决相信，你们党的这次特别代表大会对于保卫世界和平，巩固印度的独立，促进国家建设和争取社会进步的事业，必将发挥新的卓越的作用。
               中国共产党中央委员会　1958年4月5日
