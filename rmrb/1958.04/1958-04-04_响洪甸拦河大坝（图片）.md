Title: Xianghongdian Dam (Picture)
Author: Wang Yongjia
Date: 1958-04-04
Page: 3

    Xianghongdian Dam
    The Xianghongdian Dam, being built in Jinzhai County, Anhui Province, is under intense construction. The dam has an arc length of 361 meters and a height of 84.5 meters, and is expected to be completely poured by the end of May. Photo by Wang Yongjia

### 响洪甸拦河大坝（图片）
王永嘉
1958-04-04
第3版()
专栏：

    响洪甸拦河大坝
    在安徽省金寨县境内兴建的响洪甸拦河大坝，正在紧张施工。这个坝弧长三百六十一公尺，高八十四公尺半，预计在五月底可以全部浇筑完成。    王永嘉摄
