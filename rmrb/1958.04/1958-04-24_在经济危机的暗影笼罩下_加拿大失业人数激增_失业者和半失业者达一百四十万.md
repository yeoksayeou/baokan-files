Title: Under the Shadow of the Economic Crisis, Canada's Unemployment Soars: 1.4 Million Unemployed and Underemployed
Author: 
Date: 1958-04-24
Page: 6

    Under the Shadow of the Economic Crisis
    Canada's Unemployment Soars
    Unemployed and Underemployed Reach 1.4 Million
    Xinhua News Agency, Winnipeg, 23rd: According to materials released by the Canadian Worker Employment Bureau on the 22nd, as of March 20th, the number of registered unemployed had reached 883,660, accounting for 15% of Canada's total labor force.

    In addition, 519,000 people (or 8.8% of the total labor force) only had less than thirty-five hours of work per week, resulting in greatly reduced wage income.

    Canada has not seen such a high unemployment rate since the crisis of the 1930s.

### 在经济危机的暗影笼罩下  加拿大失业人数激增  失业者和半失业者达一百四十万

1958-04-24
第6版()
专栏：

    在经济危机的暗影笼罩下
    加拿大失业人数激增
    失业者和半失业者达一百四十万
    新华社23日讯　据塔斯社温尼伯讯：根据加拿大工人雇佣局22日发表的材料，到3月20日为止，已经登记的失业人数达到八十八万三千六百六十人，占加拿大劳动力总数的15％。
    此外，有五十一万九千人（即占劳动力总数的8.8％）每星期只有不到三十五个小时的工作可做，因而工资收入大大减少。
    加拿大自从三十年代的危机以来还没有出现过这样高的失业比例。
