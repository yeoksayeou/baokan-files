Title: Four Thousand Teachers and Students from Eight Agricultural and Forestry Schools in Shaanxi Go Up to the Mountains and Down to the Countryside

Author:

Date: 1958-04-08
Page: 7

Shaanxi's Eight Agricultural and Forestry Schools
Four Thousand Teachers and Students Go Up to the Mountains and Down to the Countryside

(Our dispatch) Recently, more than 4,200 teachers and students from eight secondary agricultural and forestry schools in Shaanxi Province have gone down to the countryside and up to the mountains to help farmers achieve a great leap forward in production technology. They will work in the countryside for ten months. After arriving in the mountainous areas of Shenmu, Mizhi, and Dingbian, more than 390 teachers and students from Yulin Agricultural School have already helped local agricultural cooperatives formulate livestock development plans and plans to teach technology to farmers, which are very popular with the farmers.

### 陕西八所农林学校  四千师生上山下乡

1958-04-08
第7版()
专栏：

    陕西八所农林学校
    四千师生上山下乡
    本报讯　陕西省八所中等农业和林业专科学校的师生四千二百多人，最近先后下乡上山，帮助农民实现生产技术大跃进。他们将在农村工作十个月。榆林农业学校师生三百九十多人到达神木、米脂、定边等山区后，已经帮助当地农业社订出了畜牧发展规划和向农民传授技术的计划，很受农民欢迎。
