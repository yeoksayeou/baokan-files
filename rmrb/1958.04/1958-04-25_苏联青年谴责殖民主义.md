Title: Soviet Youth Condemns Colonialism
Author:
Date: 1958-04-25
Page: 5

    Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, 23rd. TASS reported: The Soviet Youth Organizations Committee issued a statement on Youth Anti-Colonialism Day (April 24), strongly condemning colonialism.
    The statement said that Soviet youth, like all progressive mankind, expressed deep indignation at the bloody wars provoked by the colonialists in Algeria and Cameroon, and at the imperialists' conspiracies in Indonesia. Soviet youth firmly demanded an end to the wars in Algeria and Cameroon, and fully supported the Indonesian youth's struggle for national independence and national unity.
    The statement stated that Soviet youth support the just struggle of the people in colonial and dependent countries and stand with them.

### 苏联青年谴责殖民主义

1958-04-25
第5版()
专栏：

    苏联青年谴责殖民主义
    新华社24日讯　据塔斯社莫斯科23日讯：苏联青年组织委员会就青年反殖民主义日（4月24日）发表声明，坚决谴责殖民主义。
    声明说，苏联青年同全体进步人类一样，对殖民主义者在阿尔及利亚和喀麦隆所挑起的血腥战争，对帝国主义者在印度尼西亚的阴谋深表愤慨。苏联青年坚决要求停止阿尔及利亚和喀麦隆的战争，全力支持印度尼西亚青年争取本国独立和全国统一的斗争。
    声明表示，苏联青年支持殖民地和附属国人民的正义斗争，并同他们站在一起。
