Title: Indonesian University Student Organizations Call for the Elimination of Colonialism
Author:
Date: 1958-04-25
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, Jakarta, April 24th: Several Indonesian university student organizations, including the Jakarta University Student Movement and the Indonesian National University Student Union, issued a joint statement today – “Youth Anti-Colonialism Day” – strongly condemning colonialism.

The statement pointed out that colonialism still exists in parts of the world, such as Algeria and other places. This unreasonable phenomenon should be eliminated. The statement said that the existence of various forms of colonialism also violates the provisions on fundamental human rights in the United Nations Charter.

The statement called on all countries in the world to spare no effort to eliminate colonialism and demanded that the Indonesian government take the initiative to adopt various measures to eliminate colonialism. Indonesian youth and student organizations are scheduled to hold a gathering at the State Palace on the evening of the 24th to commemorate "Youth Anti-Colonialism Day." President Sukarno will attend the gathering.

### 印度尼西亚大学生组织呼吁消除殖民主义

1958-04-25
第5版()
专栏：

    印度尼西亚大学生组织呼吁消除殖民主义
    新华社雅加达24日电　包括雅加达大学学生运动和印度尼西亚全国大学学生联合会在内的一些印度尼西亚大学学生组织在今天——“青年反殖民主义日”发表联合声明，强烈谴责殖民主义。
    声明指出，在世界部分地区像阿尔及利亚和其他一些地方，仍然存在着殖民主义。应该消除这种不合理的现象。声明说，各种形式的殖民主义的存在还违反联合国宪章中关于基本人权的规定。
    声明呼吁世界所有国家不遗余力地消灭殖民主 义，并且要求印度尼西亚政府主动采取消灭殖民主义的各项措施。印度尼西亚的青年和学生组织定在24日晚间在国家宫举行集会，纪念“青年反殖民主义日”。苏加诺总统将参加这次集会。
