Title: Nepalese King Presents China with a Rhinoceros
Author:
Date: 1958-04-30
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, New Delhi, April 29th: King Mahendra of Nepal will present the Chinese government with a six-month-old one-horned rhinoceros. The rhinoceros will be handed over to a representative of the Chinese government on the India-Nepal border. The one-horned rhinoceros is a specialty of the India-Nepal border and the sub-Himalayan region of Assam.

### 尼泊尔国王赠我国一头犀牛

1958-04-30
第5版()
专栏：

    尼泊尔国王赠我国一头犀牛
    据新华社新德里29日电　尼泊尔国王马亨德拉将送给中国政府一头六个月的独角犀牛。这头犀牛将在印度—尼泊尔边境交给一位中国政府代表。独角犀牛是印度—尼泊尔边境和阿萨姆这个亚喜马拉雅山地区的特产。
