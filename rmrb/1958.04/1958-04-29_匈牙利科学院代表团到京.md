Title: Hungarian Academy of Sciences Delegation Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1958-04-29
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, April 28th: At the invitation of the Chinese Academy of Sciences, the Hungarian Academy of Sciences delegation, which is here to negotiate cooperation matters between the Chinese and Hungarian Academies of Sciences, arrived in Beijing by plane on the afternoon of the 28th. The delegation included the head of the delegation, Ferenc Erdei, Secretary-General of the Hungarian Academy of Sciences, Academician Imre Szabó, and Tivadar Siklós, Head of the Foreign Affairs Department of the Hungarian Academy of Sciences. Another member of the delegation, Academician Lajos Jánossy, will arrive in Beijing on May 1st.

Also arriving in Beijing on the 28th, at the invitation of China, were Professor Bolesław Świętochowski, an agronomist from the Polish Academy of Sciences, and Dr. Stanisław Kowaliński, a soil scientist.

### 匈牙利科学院代表团到京

1958-04-29
第4版()
专栏：

    匈牙利科学院代表团到京
    据新华社28日讯　应中国科学院邀请，前来我国谈判中匈两国科学院合作事项的匈牙利科学院代表团的团长、匈牙利科学院秘书长埃尔德·费伦茨和团员萨波·伊姆雷院士，团员、匈牙利科学院外事处处长西克洛什·提瓦达等一行三人，于28日下午乘飞机抵京。代表团另一位团员雅诺西·拉洛斯院士将在5月1日到京。
    28日同时到北京的，还有应邀来我国访问的波兰科学院耕作学家波莱斯瓦夫·希温托霍夫斯基教授和土壤学家斯坦尼斯瓦夫·科伐林斯基博士。
