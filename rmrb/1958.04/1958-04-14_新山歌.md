Title: New Folk Song
Author:
Date: 1958-04-14
Page: 8

New folk song:
　　Looking at the moon's beginning, the river flows west.
　　Looking down at mid-month, the river reaches the fields.
　　Looking up at the end of the month, the river crosses the high hills.
　　Men and women work together to achieve a thousand catties of grain.
　　Spring plowing songs fill the sky,
　　People are busy with production all over the fields.
　　In spring, compete for strength, in autumn, compete for yield,
　　See who becomes a heroic general.
　　　　　　（Jiangsu Yancheng Special District）

### 新山歌

1958-04-14
第8版()
专栏：新山歌

    新山歌月初垫脚望，河水在西荡，月半低头看，河水到田岸，月底抬头望，河水过高岗，男女齐心干，实现千斤粮。春耕号子满天响，遍野人群生产忙，春天比劲秋比产，看谁做个英雄将。
          （江苏盐城专区）
