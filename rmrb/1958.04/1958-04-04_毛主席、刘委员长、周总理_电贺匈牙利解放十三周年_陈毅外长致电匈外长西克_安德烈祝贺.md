Title: Mao Zedong, Chairman Liu, Premier Zhou Send Congratulations on the 13th Anniversary of Hungary's Liberation; Foreign Minister Chen Yi Sends Congratulations to Hungarian Foreign Minister Sik Andrei

Author:
Date: 1958-04-04
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, April 3rd—Chairman Mao Zedong, Chairman Liu Shaoqi, and Premier Zhou Enlai today sent a telegram to István Dobi, Chairman of the Presidium of the Hungarian People's Republic, János Kádár, First Secretary of the Central Committee of the Hungarian Socialist Workers' Party, and Ferenc Münnich, Prime Minister of the Hungarian Revolutionary Worker-Peasant Government, on behalf of the Chinese government, the Central Committee of the Chinese Communist Party, and the Chinese people, to congratulate them on the 13th anniversary of Hungary's liberation. The telegram reads as follows:

To Comrade István Dobi, Chairman of the Presidium of the Hungarian People's Republic,
To Comrade János Kádár, First Secretary of the Central Committee of the Hungarian Socialist Workers' Party,
To Comrade Ferenc Münnich, Prime Minister of the Hungarian Revolutionary Worker-Peasant Government of the Hungarian People's Republic:

On the occasion of the 13th anniversary of Hungary's liberation, we, on behalf of the Government of the People's Republic of China, the Central Committee of the Communist Party of China, and the Chinese people, extend our heartfelt and warm congratulations to you, and through you, to the Revolutionary Worker-Peasant Government of the Hungarian People's Republic, the Central Committee of the Hungarian Socialist Workers' Party, and the Hungarian people.

Over this past year, under the leadership of the Hungarian Socialist Workers' Party and the Revolutionary Worker-Peasant Government, the Hungarian people have achieved great successes in the struggle to consolidate the Hungarian People's Republic, and have also made important contributions to strengthening the solidarity of the socialist camp headed by the Soviet Union and to safeguarding peace in Europe and the world. The Chinese people are delighted and encouraged by these achievements of the fraternal Hungarian people. We wish the eternal friendship and fraternal cooperation between the Chinese and Hungarian peoples to be further consolidated and developed.

       Mao Zedong
       Chairman of the People's Republic of China
       Chairman of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of China
       Liu Shaoqi
       Chairman of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress of the People's Republic of China
       Zhou Enlai
       Premier of the State Council of the People's Republic of China
       April 3, 1958

Xinhua News Agency, April 3rd—Foreign Minister Chen Yi today sent a telegram to Andrei Sik, Foreign Minister of the Hungarian People's Republic, congratulating him on the 13th anniversary of Hungary's liberation. The telegram reads as follows:

To Comrade Andrei Sik, Foreign Minister of the Hungarian People's Republic:

On the occasion of the 13th anniversary of Hungary's liberation, I extend to you my warm congratulations. I wish the fraternal Hungarian people greater achievements in the struggle to build socialism and safeguard peace in Europe and the world.

       Chen Yi
       Foreign Minister of the People's Republic of China
       April 3, 1958, in Beijing

### 毛主席、刘委员长、周总理  电贺匈牙利解放十三周年  陈毅外长致电匈外长西克·安德烈祝贺

1958-04-04
第1版()
专栏：

    毛主席、刘委员长、周总理
    电贺匈牙利解放十三周年
    陈毅外长致电匈外长西克·安德烈祝贺
    新华社3日讯　毛泽东主席、刘少奇委员长、周恩来总理今天致电匈牙利人民共和国主席团主席道比·伊斯特万，匈牙利社会主义工人党中央委员会第一书记卡达尔·亚诺什，匈牙利工农革命政府总理明尼赫·费伦茨，代表中国政府、中共中央和中国人民，祝贺匈牙利解放十三周年。电文如下：匈牙利人民共和国主席团主席道比·伊斯特万同志：匈牙利社会主义工人党中央委员会第一书记卡达尔·亚诺什同志：匈牙利人民共和国工农革命政府总理明尼赫·费伦茨同志：
    当匈牙利解放十三周年的时候，我们谨代表中华人民共和国政府、中国共产党中央委员会和中国人民，向你们，并且通过你们向匈牙利人民共和国工农革命政府，匈牙利社会主义工人党中央委员会和匈牙利人民表示衷心的、热烈的祝贺。
    一年来，匈牙利人民在匈牙利社会主义工人党和工农革命政府的领导下，在巩固匈牙利人民共和国的斗争中获得了巨大的成就，同时对加强以苏联为首的社会主义阵营的团结和维护欧洲和世界的和平也作出了重要的贡献。中国人民对兄弟的匈牙利人民的这些成就感到欢欣鼓舞。祝中匈两国人民间的永恒友谊和兄弟合作更加巩固和发展。
       中华人民共和国主席
       中国共产党中央委员会主席  毛泽东
       中华人民共和国全国人民代表大会常务委员会委员长　刘少奇
       中华人民共和国国务院总理　周恩来　1958年4月3日
    新华社3日讯　外交部长陈毅今天致电匈牙利人民共和国外交部长西克·安德烈，祝贺匈牙利解放十三周年。电文如下：匈牙利人民共和国外交部长西克·安德烈同志：
    在匈牙利解放十三周年的时候，我谨向你表示热烈的祝贺。祝兄弟的匈牙利人民在建设社会主义和维护欧洲和世界和平的斗争中取得更大的成就。
            中华人民共和国外交部长　陈　毅　1958年4月3日于北京
