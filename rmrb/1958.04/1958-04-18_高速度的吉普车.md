Title: High-Speed Jeep

Author: Sun Zhongjing

Date: 1958-04-18

Page: 3

New Product

    High-Speed Jeep
    The Changjiang (Yangtze River) brand Type 46 Jeep has been successfully trial-produced by the Chongqing Wangjiang Machinery Factory. It is suitable for transportation in the wild, such as geological and forest exploration, agricultural and forestry transportation, etc. When fully loaded, its maximum speed reaches 120 kilometers per hour. (Xinhua News Agency reporter Sun Zhongjing photo)

### 高速度的吉普车
孙忠靖
1958-04-18
第3版()
专栏：新产品

    高速度的吉普车
    长江牌四六型吉普车，由重庆望江机器厂试制成功。它适用于野外的交通条件，如地质、森林勘探、农林运输等。满载时，最大速度每小时达一百二十公里。　新华社记者　孙忠靖摄
