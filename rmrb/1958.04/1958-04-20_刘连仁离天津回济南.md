Title: Liu Lianren Leaves Tianjin to Return to Jinan
Author: 
Date: 1958-04-20
Page: 2

Xinhua News Agency, Tianjin, April 19th: Liu Lianren, along with his wife Zhao Yulan, fourth brother Liu Liandeng, and son Liu Huanxin, accompanied by representatives from the Shandong Provincial Red Cross Society and the Tianjin Municipal Red Cross Society, left Tianjin by train on the 19th for Shandong, returning to his hometown after fourteen years of separation.

    At the station, representatives from the Tianjin Municipal Committee of the Chinese Communist Party, the Tianjin Municipal People's Committee, the Tianjin Municipal Committee of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference, the Tianjin Garrison Command of the Chinese People's Liberation Army, the Tianjin Municipal Red Cross Society, and various people's organizations came to see them off. Before boarding the train, Liu Lianren excitedly shook hands with the representatives from all walks of life who came to see him off, thanking everyone for their care and help.

    Xinhua News Agency, Jinan, April 19th: Compatriot Liu Lianren and his wife Zhao Yulan, brother Liu Liandeng, and son Liu Huanxin arrived in Jinan at 5:50 PM today from Tianjin.

    Over two hundred representatives from the Shandong Provincial and Jinan Municipal People's Committees, the Jinan Municipal Red Cross Society, and various people's organizations welcomed them at the station.

    In his speech, Liu Lianren first said: "I'm back! How did I get back? It is all thanks to the great fortune brought by Chairman Mao and the Communist Party, and the great power of the Chinese people!" He poured out his suffering in Japan for fourteen years to the people who welcomed him.

    After the welcome meeting, Liu Lianren, accompanied by the representatives, went to the Shandong Hotel to rest. Tomorrow, people from all walks of life in Shandong Province and Jinan City will hold a welcome and consolation meeting for Liu Lianren.

### 刘连仁离天津回济南

1958-04-20
第2版()
专栏：

    刘连仁离天津回济南
    据新华社天津19日电　刘连仁同他的妻子赵玉兰、四弟刘连登和儿子刘焕新19日由山东省红十字会和天津市红十字会的代表陪同，乘火车从天津去山东，返回他离别了十四年的家乡。
    在车站上，中共天津市委员会、天津市人民委员会、中国人民政治协商会议天津市委员会、中国人民解放军天津警备区司令部、天津市红十字会和各人民团体的代表都前来欢送。在上车以前，刘连仁激动的同欢送他的各界代表紧紧的握手，感谢大家对他的关怀和帮助。
    据新华社济南19日电　难胞刘连仁和他的妻子赵玉兰、弟弟刘连登、儿子刘焕新在今天下午五时五十分由天津到达济南。
    山东省和济南市人民委员会、济南市红十字会及各人民团体代表共二百多人到车站欢迎。
    刘连仁在讲话中首先说：“我回来啦！是怎么回来的？是多亏毛主席共产党的洪福大，是中国人民的力量大！”他向欢迎他的人民倾诉了在日本十四年的苦难。
    欢迎会结束后，刘连仁在代表们陪同下到山东宾馆休息。明天山东省和济南市各界人民将为刘连仁举行欢迎和慰问大会。
