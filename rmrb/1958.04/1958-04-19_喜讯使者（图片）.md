Title: Messenger of Good News (Photo)
Author: Chen Jinxin
Date: 1958-04-19
Page: 3

    Messenger of Good News
    Wu Xuezhong, a rural postman in Minxian County, Gansu Province, is the most trusted friend of the local farmers. Regardless of wind, rain, cold winter, or hot summer, he delivers letters and newspapers on time. Recently, he has also taken the initiative to deliver letters directly to the fields. (Xinhua News Agency, Chen Jinxin)

### 喜讯使者（图片）
陈金新

专栏：

    喜讯使者
    甘肃省岷县的乡邮员武学忠是当地农民最知心的朋友。不管刮风下雨，寒冬盛夏，信件和书报都按时送到。最近他又主动把信件直接送到田间。　（新华社陈金新摄）
