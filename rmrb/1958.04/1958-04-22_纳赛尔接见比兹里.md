Title: Nasser Receives Bizri
Author:
Date: 1958-04-22
Page: 7

    Xinhua News Agency, Cairo, April 20th: "Al-Ahram" reported today that the President of the United Arab Republic, Nasser, received Bizri, a member of the National Planning Committee, on the 19th. Bizri then left for Damascus to spend Eid al-Fitr. He will return to Cairo to take up his new post after Eid al-Fitr.

### 纳赛尔接见比兹里

1958-04-22
第7版()
专栏：

    纳赛尔接见比兹里
    新华社开罗20日电　“金字塔报”今天报道，阿拉伯联合共和国总统纳赛尔在19日接见了国家计划委员会委员比兹里。比兹里随后就前往大马士革度开斋节。他将在度完开斋节后回到开罗就任新职。
