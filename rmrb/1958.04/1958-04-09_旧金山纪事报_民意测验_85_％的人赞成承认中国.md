Title: "San Francisco Chronicle" Poll: 85% Favor Recognizing China
Author:
Date: 1958-04-09
Page: 6

    "San Francisco Chronicle" Poll
    85% Favor Recognizing China
    Xinhua News Agency, San Francisco, 8th dispatch: The "San Francisco Chronicle" conducted a poll among its readers regarding US policy toward China, and the results showed that 85% of the people favored the United States recognizing the People's Republic of China.
    When the newspaper announced the poll results on the 7th, it said that the US government's policy toward China "completely violates the somewhat informed opinions of the vast majority of residents in the San Francisco area."

### “旧金山纪事报”民意测验  85％的人赞成承认中国

1958-04-09
第6版()
专栏：

    “旧金山纪事报”民意测验
    85％的人赞成承认中国
    新华社8日讯　旧金山消息：“旧金山纪事报”就美国对华政策向它的读者举行了民意测验，结果有85％的人赞成美国承认中华人民共和国。
    这家报纸7日在宣布测验结果的时候说，美国政府对中国的政策是“完全违反了旧金山地区绝大多数居民的多少是了解情况的意见”的。
