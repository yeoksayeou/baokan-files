Title: Chinese Youth Delegation Returns to Beijing
Author:
Date: 1958-04-22
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, April 21st - The Chinese Youth Delegation, led by head of delegation Wu Xueqian, returned to Beijing by train today after visiting the United Arab Republic.

The Chinese Youth Delegation, a group of thirty-eight people, left Beijing on February 26th at the invitation of the Cairo University Federation to visit the United Arab Republic. The delegation visited the Egyptian and Syrian regions of the United Arab Republic for thirty-six days and made contact with people from all walks of life. The "August 1st" basketball team in the delegation held six friendly matches with the basketball team of the United Arab Republic, and all of them were won. The seven young artists in the delegation performed a total of fifteen shows for an audience of 7,500 people, and were warmly welcomed.

### 中国青年代表团回到北京

1958-04-22
第4版()
专栏：

    中国青年代表团回到北京
    新华社21日讯　去阿拉伯联合共和国访问的中国青年代表团在团长吴学谦率领下，今天乘火车回到北京。
    中国青年代表团一行三十八人，是应开罗大学联合会的邀请在2月26日离开北京去阿拉伯联合共和国的。代表团在阿拉伯联合共和国的埃及地区和叙利亚地区访问了三十六天，和各界人士进行了接触。代表团中的“八一”篮球队与阿拉伯联合共和国的篮球队举行了六场友谊比赛，都取得了胜利。代表团中的七位青年艺术工作者共演出了十五场，观众有七千五百人，受到了热烈的欢迎。
