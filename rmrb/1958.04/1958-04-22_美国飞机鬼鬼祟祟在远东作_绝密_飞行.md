Title: US Planes Sneakily Conduct "Top Secret" Flights in the Far East
Author:
Date: 1958-04-22
Page: 7

Xinhua News Agency, April 21st, Singapore Dispatch: Six American Sabre jet fighters passed through Singapore on the 20th. According to Agence France-Presse, these U.S. military aircraft were completing a "highly secret flight mission."

The destination of these U.S. military aircraft, which refueled in Singapore, was not disclosed. There are conflicting reports about their departure point. According to one American pilot, the planes took off earlier from South Korea.

It is reported that this American pilot said that these aircraft are conducting a top-secret "exercise flight" in the Far East.

### 美国飞机鬼鬼祟祟在远东作“绝密”飞行

1958-04-22
第7版()
专栏：

    美国飞机鬼鬼祟祟在远东作“绝密”飞行
    新华社21日讯　新加坡消息：有六架美国佩刀式喷气战斗机在20日经过新加坡。据法新社透露，这些美军飞机是在完成一次“非常秘密的飞行任务”。
    这些在新加坡加油的美军飞机的目的地没有透露。它们的起飞地点也说法不一。据一个美国飞行员说，这些飞机是早些时候从南朝鲜起飞的。
    据报道，这个美国飞行员说，这些飞机正在远东地区进行一次绝密的“演习飞行”。
