Column: Rectification is infinitely good, the street scene is new

    In Xueyuan Hutong, Xidan District, Beijing, there are many preschool children, but it is difficult to find empty houses to set up nurseries. The Sixth Residents' Committee set up a children's listening station to allow preschool children to come and listen to the Central People's Broadcasting Station's children's radio programs every day.
    Xinhua News Agency reporter Zou Jiandong photographed.

专栏：整风无限好  街头气象新
