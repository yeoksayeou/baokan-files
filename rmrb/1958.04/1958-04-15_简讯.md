    Brief News

    First Radio Conversation Between Soviet and American Antarctic Scientific Stations
    TASS reports: On the 13th, "Komsomolskaya Pravda" reported the first wireless telephone conversation between the Soviet "Mirny" scientific station and the American "Little America" Antarctic station.

    "Mirny" and "Little America" agreed to communicate with each other by radio once a week in the future in order to further expand contact.

    Czechoslovak Trade Delegation Arrives in Jakarta
    Jakarta Dispatch: According to an Antara News Agency report, the Czechoslovak trade delegation arrived in Jakarta on the afternoon of the 13th. The delegation will hold negotiations with the Indonesian government on expanding economic and trade cooperation between the two countries.

### 简讯

1958-04-15

专栏：简讯

    简讯
    苏、美南极科学站第一次无线电通话
    据塔斯社报道：13日“共青团真理报”报道了苏联“和平村”科学站同美国“小亚美利加”南极站之间的第一次无线电话交谈。
    “和平村”和“小亚美利加”协议，今后每星期彼此间通过无线电联系一次，以便进一步扩大接触。
    捷贸易代表团到雅加达
    雅加达讯：据安塔拉通讯社报道，捷克斯洛伐克贸易代表团在13日下午到达雅加达。代表团将就扩展两国之间的经济和贸易合作问题同印度尼西亚政府进行谈判。
