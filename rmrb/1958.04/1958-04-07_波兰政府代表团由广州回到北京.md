Title: Polish Government Delegation Returns to Beijing from Guangzhou
Author:
Date: 1958-04-07
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Guangzhou, April 6th: The Polish government delegation, headed by Vice-Chairman Jaroszewicz, left Guangzhou by special plane this afternoon to return to Beijing, accompanied by Yang Yingjie, Deputy Director of the State Planning Commission.

Xinhua News Agency, April 7th: The government delegation of the Polish People's Republic, headed by Vice-Chairman Jaroszewicz, returned to Beijing at 1:10 PM on the 7th after visiting various places in southern China.

Those who greeted them at the airport included: Minister of Foreign Trade Ye Jizhuang, Vice Minister Li Zheren, Vice Minister of Foreign Affairs Ji Pengfei, and Polish Ambassador to China Kiryluk.

### 波兰政府代表团由广州回到北京

1958-04-07
第4版()
专栏：

    波兰政府代表团由广州回到北京
    据新华社广州6日电　以雅罗谢维奇副主席为首的波兰政府代表团，今天下午在国家计划委员会副主任杨英杰陪同下乘专机离开广州返北京。
    新华社7日讯　以雅罗谢维奇副主席为首的波兰人民共和国政府代表团在我国南方各地参观后，已在7日一时十分回到北京。
    到机场迎接的有，对外贸易部部长叶季壮、副部长李哲人，外交部副部长姬鹏飞，波兰驻中国大使基里洛克。
