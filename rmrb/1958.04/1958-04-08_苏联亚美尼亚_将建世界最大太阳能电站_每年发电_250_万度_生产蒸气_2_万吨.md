Title: Soviet Armenia to Build World's Largest Solar Power Plant, Generating 2.5 Million Kilowatt-hours of Electricity and Producing 20,000 Tons of Steam Annually
Author:
Date: 1958-04-08
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, April 7th - TASS reports that Soviet scientists have completed the design for the world's largest solar power plant. This plant, utilizing mirrors with a total area of two hectares, will generate 2.5 million kilowatt-hours of electricity and produce 20,000 tons of steam annually.

    The main part of this power plant, planned for the Ararat Basin in Armenia, consists of 1,300 mirrors mounted on specially made carts. These mirrors can automatically move along twenty-three concentric closed tracks. A steam boiler is planned to be installed on a forty-meter-high tower, where the sunlight reflected by the mirrors will be concentrated.

    The Soviet Union has made great progress in utilizing solar thermal energy. Currently, the Soviet Union has more than 10,000 square meters of solar flat-plate heaters. The largest domestic solar flat-plate hot water installation is under construction in Crimea.

    Moldavia is one of the best regions in the Soviet Union for utilizing solar thermal energy based on its geographical conditions. Here, 1,300 solar energy installations will be set up for the food and light industries in the next three years. According to Moldavian scientists, the total solar irradiation energy received by the entire republic is 20 billion kilowatts.

### 苏联亚美尼亚  将建世界最大太阳能电站  每年发电250万度　生产蒸气2万吨

1958-04-08
第5版()
专栏：

    苏联亚美尼亚
    将建世界最大太阳能电站
    每年发电250万度　生产蒸气2万吨
    据新华社7日讯　据塔斯社报道，苏联科学家已经完成了一个世界上最大的太阳能发电站的设计工作。这个发电站利用总面积达两公顷的镜子每年可发出二百五十万度电，生产两万吨蒸气。
    这个预计设在亚美尼亚的阿拉拉特盆地的发电站的主要部分，是装在特制的小车上的一千三百面镜子，这些镜子可以自动地沿着二十三条同心闭合轨道移动。蒸气锅炉预定安装在四十公尺高的塔上，镜子所反射的太阳光就集中在这个蒸气锅炉上。
    苏联在利用太阳光热能方面有很大进展。目前，苏联已有一万多平方公尺太阳能平面加热器。在克里米亚正在建造一个国内最大的太阳能平面热水装置。
    摩尔达维亚是苏联利用太阳热能地理条件最好的地区之一。这里最近三年内将为食品工业和轻工业装设一千三百个太阳能装置。据摩尔达维亚科学家计算，共和国全境受到的太阳照射能量有二百亿瓩。
