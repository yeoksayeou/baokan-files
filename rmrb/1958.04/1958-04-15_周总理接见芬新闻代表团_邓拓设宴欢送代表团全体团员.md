Title: Premier Zhou Receives Finnish News Delegation; Deng Tuo Hosts Banquet to See Off All Members of the Delegation
Author:
Date: 1958-04-15
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency, April 14th: Premier Zhou Enlai received the Finnish journalists' delegation headed by Olkoomis this afternoon and had a conversation with them for more than an hour.

Present at the reception were Wang Yunsheng, Vice President of the All-China Journalists Association, Lu Minggu, Deputy Secretary-General, and Xu Huang, Deputy Director of the Information Department of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs.

Xinhua News Agency, April 14th: Deng Tuo, President of the All-China Journalists Association, hosted a banquet tonight to bid farewell to Olkoomis, head of the Finnish journalists' delegation, and all members of the delegation.

Nevalainen, First Secretary of the Finnish Embassy in China, and his wife also attended the banquet.

### 周总理接见芬新闻代表团  邓拓设宴欢送代表团全体团员

1958-04-15
第4版()
专栏：

    周总理接见芬新闻代表团
    邓拓设宴欢送代表团全体团员
    新华社14日讯　周恩来总理在今天下午接见了以奥尔科密斯为首的芬兰新闻工作者访华代表团全体人员，进行了一个多小时的谈话。
    接见时在座的，有中华全国新闻工作者协会副会长王芸生、副秘书长卢鸣谷，外交部新闻司副司长徐晃。
    据新华社14日讯　中华全国新闻工作者协会会长邓拓今天晚上设宴欢送芬兰新闻工作者代表团团长奥尔科密斯和全体团员。
    芬兰驻华大使馆一等秘书耐瓦莱宁和夫人也出席了宴会。
