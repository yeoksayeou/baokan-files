Title: The First Giant I-Beam

Author:

Date: 1958-04-08
Page: 3

New Product

    The First Giant I-Beam
    The Anshan Iron and Steel Works' Large Rolling Mill successfully trial-rolled China's first No. 55 giant I-beam on April 3rd. This is an important new product that China's iron and steel industry has successfully trial-rolled since the beginning of this year.
    The No. 55 giant I-beam is a giant steel material necessary for manufacturing freight car beams and constructing large factories and bridges. (According to Xinhua News Agency)

### 第一根巨型工字钢

1958-04-08
第3版()
专栏：新产品

    第一根巨型工字钢
    鞍钢大型轧钢厂在4月3日试轧出我国第一根五五号巨型工字钢。这是我国钢铁工业今年以来试轧成功的一项重要的新产品。
    五五号巨型工字钢，是制造货车大梁和建筑大型厂房、桥梁必需的巨型钢材。　　　（据新华社讯）
