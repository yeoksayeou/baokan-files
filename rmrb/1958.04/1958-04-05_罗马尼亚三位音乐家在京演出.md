Title: Three Romanian Musicians Perform in Beijing

Author: 
Date: 1958-04-05
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, April 4th. Romanian musicians Stella Simonetti, Vasili Jianu, and Radu Negrenescu, who are visiting China, gave a musical performance at the Beijing Theater this evening.

	During the nearly two-hour performance, the three musicians sang and played seventeen pieces, including works by world-renowned musicians Tchaikovsky and Mozart, as well as famous Romanian musicians and Romanian folk songs. Each performance received long and enthusiastic applause from the audience.

	Chu Tunan, Deputy Director of the Commission for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries, and people from all walks of life in the capital, as well as officials from the Romanian Embassy in China, attended tonight's concert.

	When Chu Tunan met with the three musicians during the intermission, he said that we were especially happy to be able to enjoy the wonderful performances of the Romanian musicians at a time when the people of Beijing were joyfully welcoming the Romanian People's Republic government delegation. After the performance, Chu Tunan went on stage and warmly shook hands with the three musicians repeatedly, and presented flowers to congratulate them. (Attached picture)

### 罗马尼亚三位音乐家在京演出

1958-04-05
第4版()
专栏：

    罗马尼亚三位音乐家在京演出
    新华社4日讯　来我国访问的罗马尼亚人民共和国音乐家斯黛拉·西蒙乃迪、瓦西里·日安努和拉都·奈格连努今日晚间在北京剧场举行音乐演出会。
    三位音乐家在近两小时的演出中，演唱和演奏了世界著名音乐家柴可夫斯基、莫扎特和罗马尼亚著名音乐家的作品以及罗马尼亚民歌共十七个节目。每一个节目的演出都博得了全场听众长时间的热烈掌声。
    对外文化联络委员会副主任楚图南和首都各界人士，罗马尼亚驻中国大使馆官员出席了今晚的音乐会。
    楚图南在演出休息期间同三位音乐家见面的时候说，正当北京人民兴高彩烈地欢迎罗马尼亚人民共和国政府代表团的时候，能够欣赏罗马尼亚音乐家们的精彩演出，我们感到格外高兴。演出后，楚图南上台和三位音乐家一再热烈握手，并献花致贺。（附图片）
