Title: Scenery of Wenshan (Picture)
Author: Xǔ Zhìdá
Date: 1958-04-02
Page: 4

    Scenery of Wenshan
    The newly established Wenshan Zhuang and Miao Autonomous Prefecture in Yunnan Province is located in the southeastern corner of Yunnan, including the eight counties of Wenshan, Yanshan, Xichou, Qiubei, Maguan, Guangnan, Funing, and Malipo. It borders the Guangxi Zhuang Autonomous Region to the east and the friendly neighbor Vietnam to the south. On this land of more than 30,000 square kilometers, people of seven ethnic groups, including Zhuang, Miao, Han, Yi, Yao, Hui, and Bai, live, among which the Zhuang people number more than 450,000, accounting for 32.2% of the total population. The Miao people number more than 150,000, accounting for 10.9% of the total population. Located in the subtropical zone, it is rich in products, and it is now the flourishing spring season with flowers blooming everywhere.

### 文山风光（图片）
许智达
1958-04-02
第4版()
专栏：

    文山风光
    新成立的云南省文山僮族苗族自治州位于云南的东南角，包括文山、砚山、西畴、丘北、马关、广南、富宁、麻栗坡八个县。东与广西僮族自治区相连，南与友好邻邦越南接壤。在这三万多平方公里的土地上，居住着僮、苗、汉、彝、瑶、回、白等七个民族的人民，其中僮族四十五万多人，占总人口的32.2％。苗族十五万多人，占总人口的10.9％。这里地处亚热带，物产丰饶，目前正是遍地花开的盛春时节。
              许智达摄
