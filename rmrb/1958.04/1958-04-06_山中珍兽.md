Title: Rare Animals in the Mountains
Author: Li Jilu, Tong Deyin
Date: 1958-04-06
Page: 2

    Rare Animals in the Mountains
    In the mountains of the Wandashan range on the southeastern border of Heilongjiang Province, there is a state-run wildlife breeding farm that raises local precious animals: sable (also known as Z貂), raccoon dog, red fox, and sika deer.
    The sable in this photo is a precious specialty of the original forests of Heilongjiang, and one of the three treasures of Northeast China that people praise.　Xinhua News Agency reporter
        Li Jilu, Tong Deyin (with picture)

### 山中珍兽
李基禄  佟德印
1958-04-06
第2版()
专栏：

    山中珍兽
    在黑龙江省东南边境完达山脉的群山中，有一个国营野牲饲养场，饲养着当地名贵的小兽——黑貂（别名紫貂）、北方狢、赤狐、梅花鹿。
    这张照片里的黑貂，是黑龙江原始大森林里的名贵特产，也是人们所称道的东北三宝之一。　新华社记者
        李基禄、佟德印摄（附图片）
