Title: "Red Satellite Wreaks Havoc in Heaven"
Author: 
Date: 1958-04-05
Page: 8

Column: Stage and Screen

    In China's rich repertoire of classical dramas, many are adapted from deeply rooted folk legends. The Peking opera "Red Satellite Wreaks Havoc in Heaven" is one such example. It personifies the Soviet Union's launched artificial satellite, interweaving it into the stories of Chang'e and the Weaver Girl, and having it participate in a righteous struggle. This kind of legendary imagination aligns with the audience's desires.
    In the new society, there are countless new legends to tell, requiring today's artists to process and create. The creation and performance of "Red Satellite Wreaks Havoc in Heaven" is commendable. (Lan)

### “红色卫星闹天宫”

1958-04-05
第8版()
专栏：舞台和银幕

    “红色卫星闹天宫”
    在我国丰富的古典戏剧创作中，有不少是根据深入人心的民间传说编成的。京剧“红色卫星闹天宫”也是这样。它把苏联发射的人造卫星人格化了，穿插在嫦娥和织女的故事中，并且使他参与了一场正义的斗争。这种传奇式的想像是符合观众的意愿的。
    新社会里有说不尽的新传奇，需要今天的艺术家来加工创造，“红色卫星闹天宫”的创作和演出是可喜的。　（兰）
