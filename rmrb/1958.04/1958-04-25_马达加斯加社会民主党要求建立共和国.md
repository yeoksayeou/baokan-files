Title: Madagascar Social Democratic Party Demands the Establishment of a Republic
Author:
Date: 1958-04-25
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, April 24th: TASS, Paris, April 23rd: The congress of the Social Democratic Party of Madagascar (a French territory in Africa) concluded in Tananarive. The congress passed a resolution demanding that France grant Madagascar "autonomous status, which should stipulate the future establishment of an independent republic."

### 马达加斯加社会民主党要求建立共和国

1958-04-25
第5版()
专栏：

    马达加斯加社会民主党要求建立共和国
    新华社24日讯　据塔斯社巴黎23日讯：马达加斯加（法国在非洲的属地）社会民主党代表大会在塔那那利佛闭幕。大会通过决议，要求法国给予马达加斯加“以自治地位，这种自治地位应该规定将来建立独立共和国”。
