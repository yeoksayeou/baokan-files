Title: The 7th World Youth Festival to be Held in Vienna Next July
Author:
Date: 1958-04-20
Page: 3

Xinhua News Agency, Vienna, 19th: TASS, Vienna, 18th: The 7th World Youth and Student Festival has been confirmed to be held in Vienna, the capital of Austria, from July 26th to August 4th, 1959. The standing committee for preparing the festival will be located in Vienna.

This was stated in a working communique issued by the standing committee for preparing the festival in Vienna. The communique said that the members of the standing committee, during the ten days after their arrival in Vienna, held talks with Austrian state and municipal authorities, cultural and sports organizations and institutions on specific issues related to organizing the festival, and visited many cultural institutions, sports venues, theaters, stadiums, and clubs. They have reached corresponding agreements with various Austrian government ministries, state and municipal authorities on the conditions for holding the festival in Vienna.

### 第七届世界青年联欢节  明年七月在维也纳举行

1958-04-20
第3版()
专栏：

    第七届世界青年联欢节
    明年七月在维也纳举行
    据新华社19日讯　据塔斯社维也纳18日讯：第七届世界青年与学生联欢节已经确定在1959年7月26日至8月4日在奥地利首都维也纳举行。筹备联欢节的常务委员会将设在维也纳。
    这是筹备联欢节的常务委员会在维也纳发表的工作公报中说的。公报中说，常务委员会的委员们在到达维也纳后的十天中，同奥地利国家机关和市政机关、文化体育团体和机关就组织联欢节的具体问题进行了会谈，并且访问了许多文化机构、运动场所、剧院、体育场、俱乐部。他们已经同奥地利政府各部、国家和市政当局就有关在维也纳举行联欢节的条件达成了相应的协议。
