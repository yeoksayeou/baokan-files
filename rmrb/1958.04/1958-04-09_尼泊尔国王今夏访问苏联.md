Title: Nepalese King to Visit Soviet Union This Summer
Author:
Date: 1958-04-09
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, April 8th. TASS reported on April 8th that King Mahendra Bir Bikram of Nepal has accepted the invitation from Voroshilov, Chairman of the Presidium of the Supreme Soviet of the Soviet Union, to visit the Soviet Union this summer.

### 尼泊尔国王今夏访问苏联

1958-04-09
第5版()
专栏：

    尼泊尔国王今夏访问苏联
    新华社8日讯　据塔斯社8日报道，尼泊尔国王马亨德拉·比尔·比克拉姆接受苏联最高苏维埃主席团主席伏罗希洛夫的邀请，将在今年夏天访问苏联。
