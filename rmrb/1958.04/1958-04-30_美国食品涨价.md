Title: U.S. Food Prices Rise
Author: 
Date: 1958-04-30
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, April 29th, Washington dispatch: According to an announcement by the U.S. Department of Agriculture on the 28th, the cost for American housewives to purchase food in the first quarter of 1958 rose to a peak of an average of $1,054 per year, an increase of 7% compared to the first quarter of 1957. This increase was caused by rising prices of all products except for fats, oils and cooking oils. Approximately three-quarters of this increase was caused by rising prices of meat, fruits, and vegetables.

### 美国食品涨价

1958-04-30
第6版()
专栏：

    美国食品涨价
    新华社29日讯　华盛顿消息：据美国农业部28日宣布，美国家庭主妇购买食品的费用在1958年第一季度上升到平均每年得花一千零五十四美元的高峰，比1957年第一季上涨了7％。这种上涨是由于除了油脂和食油之外的全部产品价格的上涨而引起的。总计这种上涨的四分之三左右是肉类、水果和蔬菜价格上涨造成的。
