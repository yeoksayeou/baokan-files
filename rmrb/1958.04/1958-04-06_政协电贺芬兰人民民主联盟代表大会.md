Title: CPPCC Sends Congratulations to the Finnish People's Democratic League Congress
Author:
Date: 1958-04-06
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, 5th. The National Committee of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference sent a telegram on the 4th to the Council of the Finnish People's Democratic League, congratulating them on the opening of the Fifth Congress of the Finnish People's Democratic League. The telegram reads as follows: To the Council of the Finnish People's Democratic League in Helsinki and to all delegates to the Fifth Congress of the Finnish People's Democratic League:
　　On the occasion of the opening of the Fifth Congress of the Finnish People's Democratic League, I wish the Congress a complete success.
　　　　　　　　　　　　The National Committee of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference, April 4, 1958

### 政协电贺芬兰人民民主联盟代表大会

1958-04-06
第1版()
专栏：

    政协电贺芬兰人民民主联盟代表大会
    新华社5日讯　中国人民政治协商会议全国委员会4日致电芬兰人民民主联盟联盟理事会，祝贺芬兰人民民主联盟第五次代表大会的召开。电文如下：赫尔辛基芬兰人民民主联盟联盟理事会并转芬兰人民民主联盟第五次代表大会全体代表：
    欣逢芬兰人民民主联盟召开第五次代表大会之际，谨祝大会圆满成功。
              中国人民政治协商会议全国委员会　1958年4月4日
