    Glorious Labor  Glorious People
    Niu Fengxia, a young female worker at the Baoding City Knitting Factory in Hebei Province, overfulfilled her plan in February by knitting 236 dozen and 8 pairs of socks, and knitted more than 300 dozen in March.
