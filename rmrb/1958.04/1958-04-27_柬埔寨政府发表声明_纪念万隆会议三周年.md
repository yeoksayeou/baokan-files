Title: Cambodian Government Issues Statement Commemorating the Third Anniversary of the Bandung Conference
Author:
Date: 1958-04-27
Page: 3

    Cambodian Government Issues Statement
    Commemorating the Third Anniversary of the Bandung Conference
    Xinhua News Agency, Phnom Penh, April 26th - According to a report by the Cambodian News Agency today, the Cambodian government issued a statement on April 25th, the third anniversary of the Bandung Conference, saying that if all countries adhere to the resolutions of the Bandung Conference, especially the principles of peaceful coexistence, countries with different ideologies will be able to maintain friendly relations based on the principles of equality, honor, and dignity. This will contribute to the establishment of an era of peace that encompasses the entire world, which is in line with the spirit of the United Nations Charter and the ardent desire of all countries in the world.

### 柬埔寨政府发表声明  纪念万隆会议三周年

1958-04-27
第3版()
专栏：

    柬埔寨政府发表声明
    纪念万隆会议三周年
    新华社金边26日电　据柬埔寨通讯社今天报道，柬埔寨政府就4月25日万隆会议三周年发表声明说，如果所有国家都恪守万隆会议的决议，特别是恪守和平共处的原则，不同意识形态的国家就能够依据平等、光荣和尊严的准则保持友好关系，并且有助于建立普及全世界的和平时代，而这是符合联合国宪章的精神和世界各国的热切愿望的。
