Title: Guiyang to Build a Fertilizer Plant
Author:
Date: 1958-04-20
Page: 2

Xinhua News Agency, Guiyang, 19th -- The Guiyang Fertilizer Plant, which will provide a large amount of chemical fertilizer for the barren mountainous areas of Guizhou, began construction on the outskirts of Guiyang City on the 17th. According to the plan, this factory will partially go into production in the third quarter of next year and will be fully operational in 1961. When fully operational, it will be able to produce 600,000 tons of chemical fertilizers such as ammonium sulfate and superphosphate per year.

In the construction of this factory, it has received strong support from relevant departments. Many factories in the province have transferred technicians and cadres to help with the construction. The railway department has also decided to build a dedicated railway line to Guiyang City for them.

### 贵阳兴建化肥厂

1958-04-20
第2版()
专栏：

    贵阳兴建化肥厂
    新华社贵阳19日电　一座将为贵州贫瘠山区提供大量化学肥料的贵阳化肥厂，17日在贵阳市郊动工兴建。按照计划，这个工厂将在明年第三季度部分投入生产，1961年全部投入生产。全部投入生产后，每年可以生产硫酸铵、重过磷酸钙等化肥六十万吨。
    这个厂在建设中，得到有关部门的大力支持。省内许多工厂都抽调技工和干部前来帮助建设。铁道部门还决定为他们修建通往贵阳市的铁路专用线。
