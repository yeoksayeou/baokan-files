Title: Ministry of National Defense Holds Song and Dance Evening to Welcome UAE Military Delegation
Author:
Date: 1958-04-05
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, April 4th: The Ministry of National Defense held a song and dance evening tonight to welcome the Military Friendship Delegation of the United Arab Republic to China.

Lieutenant General Mohammed Ibrahim, head of the delegation, and all members of the delegation attended the evening.

Attending the evening as hosts were: Lieutenant General Liu Zhijian, Lieutenant General Xiao Xiangrong, Vice Admiral Luo Shunchu, Lieutenant General Chang Qiankun, and other senior generals of the Chinese People's Liberation Army. More than 700 officers from the Army, Navy, and Air Force also attended the evening.

The song and dance troupe of the General Political Department of the Chinese People's Liberation Army performed musical and dance programs for the distinguished guests. The performers sang Egyptian songs such as "Egypt - My Mother" in Arabic, which were warmly applauded and welcomed by the distinguished guests.

At the end of the evening, Chief of Delegation Mohammed Ibrahim and all members of the delegation went on stage, presented flower baskets to the performers, and took a group photo.

### 国防部举行歌舞晚会　欢迎阿联军事代表团

1958-04-05
第4版()
专栏：

　　国防部举行歌舞晚会　欢迎阿联军事代表团
    新华社4日讯　国防部今天晚上举行歌舞晚会，欢迎阿拉伯联合共和国军事友好访华代表团。
    代表团团长穆罕默德·易卜拉欣中将和全体团员，出席了晚会。
    出席晚会作陪的有：刘志坚中将、萧向荣中将、罗舜初海军中将、常乾坤空军中将等中国人民解放军高级将领。参加晚会的还有陆海空军军官七百多人。
    中国人民解放军总政治部文工团为贵宾们演出了音乐舞蹈节目。演员们用阿拉伯语演唱了“埃及——我的母亲”等埃及歌曲。受到贵宾热烈鼓掌欢迎。
    晚会结束时，穆罕默德·易卜拉欣团长和全体团员走上舞台，向演员们赠送了花篮，并一起摄影留念。
