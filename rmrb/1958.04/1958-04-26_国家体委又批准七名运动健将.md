Title: State Physical Culture and Sports Commission Approves Seven More Master Athletes
Author:
Date: 1958-04-26
Page: 7

Xinhua News Agency, April 24th. The State Physical Culture and Sports Commission of the People's Republic of China recently approved seven more master athletes. They are:

　　Weightlifting: Qu Bingyu, Xu Lishui.

　　Track and Field: Wang Yi (female), Liu Xingyu (female).

　　Mountaineering: Xu Jing, Chen Rongchang.

　　Swimming: Liu Furan.

　　Based on reports from relevant departments, the State Physical Culture and Sports Commission recently stripped the rightist element and counter-revolutionary element Li Rongguo of his title as track and field master athlete.

　　To date, there are 202 male and female master athletes in our country, of which 69 are female.

### 国家体委又批准七名运动健将

1958-04-26
第7版()
专栏：体育运动

    国家体委又批准七名运动健将
    新华社24日讯　中华人民共和国体育运动委员会最近又批准七名运动健将，他们是：
    举重：曲炳瑜、许丽水。
    田径：王毅（女）、刘兴玉（女）。
    爬山：许竞、陈荣昌。
    游泳：刘复然。
    体育运动委员会根据有关部门的报告，在最近剥夺了右派分子、反革命分子李荣国田径运动健将的称号。
    到目前为止，我国男女运动健将已有二百零二人，其中女运动健将六十九人。
