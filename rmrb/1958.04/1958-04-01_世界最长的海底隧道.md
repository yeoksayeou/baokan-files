	Odds and Ends
	
	World's Longest Undersea Tunnel
	
	The tunnel linking Japan's Honshu and Kyushu islands was completed on March 9th. This is currently the world's longest tunnel. It is 3,460 meters long, with an undersea section of 780 meters. The project lasted twenty-one years and cost 5.6 billion yen. (Attached picture)

### 世界最长的海底隧道

1958-04-01
第6版()
专栏：花絮

    世界最长的海底隧道
    联结日本本州和九州二岛的隧道已于3月9日完工。这是目前世界上最长的隧道。它全长三千四百六十公尺，海底部分长七百八十公尺。这项工程延续了二十一年，建筑费用达五十六亿日圆。（附图片）
