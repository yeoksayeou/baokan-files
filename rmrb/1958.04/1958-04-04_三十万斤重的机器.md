Title: Machine Weighing 300,000 Jin

Author: 
Date: 1958-04-04
Page: 3

New Products

	A machine weighing 300,000 Jin
	The Shenyang Heavy Machinery Plant successfully trial-produced a large multi-chamber tube mill on March 29th, a type of machine that has never been produced in China before. This machine weighs 154 tons and has a cylinder diameter of 2.2 meters, involving relatively complex technology. It is a machine for grinding limestone, marl, sintered blocks, coal, chemical and ceramic raw materials, etc., and is an indispensable piece of equipment for cement plants.

	The successful manufacturing of this machine signifies the improvement of China's heavy machinery manufacturing level.
	Xinhua News Agency reporter Zhu Ying (with photo)

### 三十万斤重的机器

1958-04-04
第3版()
专栏：新产品

    三十万斤重的机器
    沈阳重型机器厂在3月29日试制成功一种我国从未生产过的大型多室管磨机。这台机器重一百五十四吨，筒体直径为二点二公尺，技术相当复杂。它是磨石灰石、泥灰岩、烧结块、煤、化学和陶磁原料等的机器，也是水泥厂不可缺少的设备。
    这种机器制造成功，标志着我国重型机器制造水平的提高。
    新华社记者朱　瑛摄（附图片）
