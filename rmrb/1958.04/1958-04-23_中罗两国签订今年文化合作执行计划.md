Title: China and Romania Sign This Year's Cultural Cooperation Implementation Plan
Author: 
Date: 1958-04-23
Page: 5

    China and Romania Sign This Year's Cultural Cooperation Implementation Plan
    Xinhua News Agency Dispatch from Bucharest—The 1958 implementation plan for the Sino-Romanian Cultural Cooperation Agreement was signed on the 12th in Bucharest, the capital of Romania. The implementation plan stipulates that the two countries will strengthen cooperation in science, education, culture, and sports this year.

### 中罗两国签订今年文化合作执行计划

1958-04-23
第5版()
专栏：

    中罗两国签订今年文化合作执行计划
    据新华社讯　中罗文化合作协定1958年执行计划12日在罗马尼亚首都布加勒斯特签字。执行计划规定，两国今年将在科学、教育、文化和体育等方面加强合作。
