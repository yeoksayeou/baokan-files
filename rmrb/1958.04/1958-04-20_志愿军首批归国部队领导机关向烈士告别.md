Title: Leading Organ of First Group of Returning Volunteer Army Bids Farewell to Martyrs
Author:
Date: 1958-04-20
Page: 2

Xinhua News Agency, Pyongyang, 19th -- The leading organ of the first group of returning Chinese People's Volunteer Army units, along with all officers and soldiers, solemnly bid farewell to the martyrs at a Volunteer Army Martyrs' Cemetery in a certain area this morning.

　　Following the presentation of wreaths to the martyrs by the unit commander, Major General Zhong Guochu, and others amidst mournful music, Lieutenant Colonel Lei Feng, a representative of the officers and soldiers, delivered a sorrowful farewell address. Subsequently, the officers and soldiers swept the tombs of the martyrs and circled the cemetery once to express their condolences.

### 志愿军首批归国部队领导机关向烈士告别

1958-04-20
第2版()
专栏：

    志愿军首批归国部队领导机关向烈士告别
    新华社平壤19日电　中国人民志愿军首批归国部队领导机关全体官兵，今天上午到某地志愿军烈士陵园庄严告别。
    部队首长钟国楚少将等在哀乐声中向烈士们敬献花圈后，官兵代表雷烽中校沉痛地致了告别词。接着，官兵们为烈士扫了墓，并且绕陵一周志哀。
