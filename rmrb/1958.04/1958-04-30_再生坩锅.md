    Reborn Crucible

    Did you know? A refractory material used in the steel industry—the crucible—generally costs more than 20 yuan apiece. However, the "reborn crucible" produced by the Hardware Smelting Group of the Dianan Residents' Committee in Xuzhou only costs a little over 2 yuan apiece for the same size.

    How exactly is the reborn crucible reborn? New crucibles used in steel factories for melting steel and iron can generally no longer be used for melting steel and iron after seven or eight uses. They buy these crucibles to melt copper, lead, and tin. After about eight or nine more uses, they can't even melt copper, lead, or tin anymore. Then, they crush the crucibles into powder, add some firebricks and fireclay, and then they can make reborn crucibles.

### 再生坩锅

1958-04-30
第4版()
专栏：

    再生坩锅
    你知道吗？钢铁工业上的一种耐火器材——坩锅，一般要二十多元一只，然而在徐州市电南居民委员会五金熔炼小组生产的同样大小的“再生坩锅”，只要二元多一只。
    再生坩锅究竟是怎样再生出来的？钢铁厂里用作化钢化铁的新坩锅，一般用过七、八次以后，就不能再化钢化铁了，他们就把这种坩锅买来，化铜、化铅、化锡，大约再用八、九次以后，连铜、铅、锡都不能化了，就把坩锅压碎成粉末，再加一些耐火砖、耐火土，便可制成再生坩锅。
