    A solar eclipse can be seen across the country on April 19th. This is Li Ping, a lecturer in the Physics Department of Beijing Normal University, who has been sent to the Hongfeng Agricultural Cooperative on the outskirts of Beijing, using a "Moon-Earth Movement Instrument" to explain the cause of the solar eclipse to the members, helping farmers understand this astronomical phenomenon. Xinhua News Agency reporter Chu Ying
