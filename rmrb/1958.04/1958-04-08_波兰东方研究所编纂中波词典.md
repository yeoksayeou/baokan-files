Title: Polish Oriental Institute Compiles Chinese-Polish Dictionary
Author: 
Date: 1958-04-08
Page: 5

    Xinhua News Agency, Warsaw, 7th. The Polish News Agency reports that the Polish Academy of Sciences' Oriental Studies Institute is compiling a Chinese-Polish dictionary. This dictionary will be the first Chinese-Polish dictionary published in Poland.

### 波兰东方研究所编纂中波词典

1958-04-08
第5版()
专栏：

    波兰东方研究所编纂中波词典
    据新华社华沙7日电　据波兰通讯社报道，波兰科学院东方学研究所正在编纂一部中波词典。这部词典将是波兰出版的第一部中波词典。
