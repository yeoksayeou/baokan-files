Title: Premier Zhou Enlai Receives Military Delegation from the United Arab Republic
Author: 
Date: 1958-04-08
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, 7th. Premier Zhou Enlai received the military friendly delegation from the United Arab Republic led by Lieutenant General Muhammed Ibrahim and all members of the delegation this afternoon.

    Present at the reception were General Zhang Zongxun, Lieutenant General Xiao Xiangrong, Deputy Director of the West Asia and Africa Department of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs He Gongkai, and Colonel Li Dazhong, military attache of our country to the United Arab Republic.

    Charge d'affaires ad interim of the Embassy of the United Arab Republic in China, Xie Binni, was also present.

    During the reception, Head of Delegation Muhammed Ibrahim presented Premier Zhou Enlai with the gifts from President Nasser. Afterwards, Premier Zhou Enlai and the distinguished guests took a group photo together.

### 周总理接见阿联军事代表团

1958-04-08
第1版()
专栏：

    周总理接见阿联军事代表团
    新华社7日讯　周恩来总理今天下午接见阿拉伯联合共和国军事友好访华代表团团长穆罕默德·易卜拉欣中将和全体团员。
    接见时在座的有：张宗逊上将、萧向荣中将、外交部西亚非洲司副司长何功楷和我国驻阿拉伯联合共和国武官李大中上校。
    阿拉伯联合共和国驻我国大使馆临时代办谢宾尼也在座。
    接见时，穆罕默德·易卜拉欣团长将纳赛尔总统赠送给周恩来总理的礼品，当面交给了周恩来总理。随后，周恩来总理和贵宾们一起合影留念。
