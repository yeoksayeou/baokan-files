Title: A Scene in New York (Picture)
Author:
Date: 1958-04-16
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency Draft:
    A Scene in New York
    This is a street in New York, the largest city in the United States, which is hailed as "Paradise". Rubble is everywhere, and it is extremely dirty. The person in the photo is unemployed due to the American economic crisis and is currently making a meager living by picking up garbage. This photo was taken in March 1958.

### 纽约一景（图片）

1958-04-16
第5版()
专栏：

    纽约一景
    这就是号称“天堂”的美国第一大城纽约的一条街道。瓦砾满地，肮脏不堪。照片上的这个人因为美国的经济危机而失业，目前依靠拾垃圾勉强维持生活。这张照片是在一九五八年三月拍摄的。　新华社稿
