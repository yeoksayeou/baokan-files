Title: China and Germany Sign This Year's Trade Agreement
Author:
Date: 1958-04-25
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, Berlin, April 24th: The 1958 agreement on the exchange of goods and payments between the People's Republic of China and the German Democratic Republic was signed in Berlin on the afternoon of the 23rd.

This agreement was reached after negotiations between the trade delegations of the two countries in an atmosphere of friendship and understanding.

### 中德签订今年贸易协定

1958-04-25
第5版()
专栏：

    中德签订今年贸易协定
    据新华社柏林24日电　中华人民共和国和德意志民主共和国1958年度交换货物和付款协定23日下午在柏林签订。
    这项协定是两国贸易代表团在友好和谅解气氛中进行谈判后达成的。
