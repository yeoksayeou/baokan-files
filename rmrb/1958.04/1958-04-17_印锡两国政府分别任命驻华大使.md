Title: Indian and Ceylonese Governments Appoint Ambassadors to China Respectively
Author:
Date: 1958-04-17
Page: 6
Xinhua News Agency, April 15th -- The government of Ceylon has appointed Mr. William Gopallawa as Ambassador Extraordinary and Plenipotentiary to China.

Xinhua News Agency, April 16th -- The government of the Republic of India has appointed Mr. Gopalaswami Parthasarathi as Ambassador Extraordinary and Plenipotentiary to China.

### 印锡两国政府分别任命驻华大使

1958-04-17
第6版()
专栏：

    印锡两国政府分别任命驻华大使
    新华社15日讯　锡兰政府已经任命威廉·高伯拉瓦先生为驻华特命全权大使。
    新华社16日讯　印度共和国政府已经任命高伯拉斯瓦米·帕塔萨拉蒂先生为驻华特命全权大使。
