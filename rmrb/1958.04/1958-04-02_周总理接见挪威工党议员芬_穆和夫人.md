Title: Premier Zhou Enlai Receives Norwegian Labour Party MP Finn Moe and Wife
Author:
Date: 1958-04-02
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, April 1st. Premier Zhou Enlai received today Finn Moe, a Norwegian Labour Party Member of Parliament and Chairman of the Parliamentary Foreign Affairs and Constitutional Committee, and his wife.

Present at the reception were: Zhang Xiruo, President of the Chinese People's Institute of Foreign Affairs; Wu Maosun, Secretary-General; Zhang Yan, Deputy Director of the Foreign Affairs Office of the State Council; and Song Zhiguang, Deputy Director of the Department of West European Affairs of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs.

Norwegian Ambassador to China, Mr. Krog-Hansen, was also present.

### 周总理接见挪威工党议员芬·穆和夫人

1958-04-02
第1版()
专栏：

    周总理接见挪威工党议员芬·穆和夫人
    新华社1日讯　周恩来总理今天接见了挪威工党议员，议会外交、宪法委员会主席芬·穆和夫人。
    接见时在座的有：中国人民外交学会会长张奚若、秘书长吴茂荪，国务院外事办公室副主任张彦，外交部西欧司副司长宋之光。
    挪威驻华大使克洛格—亨生也在座。
