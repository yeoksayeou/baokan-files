Title: USSR and UAR Strengthen Cultural Exchange; Both Countries to Send Professional Delegations for Visits
Author:
Date: 1958-04-12
Page: 5
Xinhua News Agency, Cairo, April 11th: The United Arab Republic and the Soviet Union signed a plan in Cairo on the 10th for cultural exchanges this year.

According to the plan, the two countries will send delegations of scientists and artists to visit each other. The United Arab Republic will invite Soviet scientists in physics, petroleum, agriculture, and chemistry to help the United Arab Republic, and will also send its own scientists to study in the Soviet Union. It will also invite Soviet orientalists and archaeologists to visit. The two countries will hold film festivals and exchange radio programs and books.

The plan was signed by Soviet Minister of Culture Mikhailov and United Arab Republic Minister of National Guidance Radwan, based on the cultural agreement concluded by the two countries last year. Mikhailov led a Soviet cultural delegation to Cairo for a visit on April 3.

### 苏联和阿联加强文化交流  两国将互派专业代表团进行访问

1958-04-12
第5版()
专栏：

    苏联和阿联加强文化交流
    两国将互派专业代表团进行访问
    据新华社开罗11日电　阿拉伯联合共和国和苏联10日在开罗签订了今年度文化交流的计划。
    根据这个计划，两国将互派科学家和艺术家代表团进行访问。阿拉伯联合共和国将邀请苏联在物理、石油、农业、化学方面的科学家来帮助阿拉伯联合共和国，同时将派出自己的科学家到苏联去留学。它还将邀请苏联的东方学家和考古学家前来访问。两国将互相举行电影节，交换广播节目和书籍。
    这个计划是由苏联文化部长米哈伊洛夫和阿拉伯联合共和国国家指导部长拉德温根据去年缔结的两国文化协定签订的。米哈伊洛夫在4月3日率领苏联文化代表团到达开罗访问。
