Title: American and International Progressives Celebrate Robeson's Sixtieth Birthday
Author:
Date: 1958-04-09
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, April 8th: TASS, New York, April 8th: Progressives in the United States and around the world are preparing to hold various activities on April 9th to widely celebrate the sixtieth birthday of the famous American Black singer Paul Robeson.
    Social figures from all over the world have been sending telegrams to Robeson, congratulating the famous artist and outstanding fighter for peace and the freedom of colored peoples, wishing him good health and success in his work. A celebration party will be held in Chicago, USA, on April 9th. Before going to Chicago, Robeson expressed his gratitude to Soviet journalists for the love of the Soviet people for him, saying that this love has encouraged him during these difficult years.

### 美国和各国进步人士  庆祝罗伯逊六十寿辰

1958-04-09
第5版()
专栏：

    美国和各国进步人士
    庆祝罗伯逊六十寿辰
    据新华社8日讯　据塔斯社纽约8日讯：美国和全世界进步人士都准备在4月9日举行各种活动，广泛庆祝美国著名黑人歌唱家保罗·罗伯逊的六十寿辰。
    世界各地的社会人士纷纷拍电报给罗伯逊，祝贺这位著名艺术家，为和平、为争取有色民族的自由而斗争的杰出战士身体健康，工作顺利。4月9日，美国芝加哥将举行庆祝晚会。罗伯逊在去芝加哥前对苏联记者表示感谢苏联人民对他的热爱，他说，这种热爱使他在这些年艰难的日子里得到了鼓舞。
