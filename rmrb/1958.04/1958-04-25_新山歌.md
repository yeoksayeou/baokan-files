Title: New Mountain Song
Author: Zhang Daohong
Date: 1958-04-25
Page: 8

New mountain songs split cliffs and carve out rivers,
Flying springs are erected on the east and west mountains,
The flowing water rushes through the air,
Like a fairy playing silk strings.
    Zhang Daohong, Anlu County, Hubei
