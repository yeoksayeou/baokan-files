Title: Cuban Rebel Forces Still Fighting Nationwide
Author:
Date: 1958-04-18
Page: 5
Xinhua News Agency, Havana, 17th: Cuban armed rebel forces continue to wage guerrilla warfare against government troops in vast areas throughout the country.
    In Oriente Province, the main base of the guerrillas, armed rebels continue to attack government troops' highway and railway transportation. The capital of the province, Santiago de Cuba, remains cut off from most other provinces.
    The guerrillas also launched an attack on the city of Samanilla, near the U.S. naval base at Guantanamo. Telephone communications between Havana and the city have been interrupted.
    At the same time, rebels are actively waging guerrilla warfare in Las Villas Province in the central part of the country and Pinar del Rio Province in the westernmost part. Recently, a force of armed rebels landed on the north coast of Pinar del Rio Province to establish a western front.
    Armed rebel leader Castro delivered a radio address, stating that he would never negotiate a truce or make any compromises with the Batista dictatorship.

### 古巴起义部队仍在全国奋战

1958-04-18
第5版()
专栏：

    古巴起义部队仍在全国奋战
    据新华社17日讯　哈瓦那消息：古巴武装起义部队继续在全国广大地区对政府军进行游击战。
    在游击队的主要根据地奥连特省，武装起义者继续攻击政府军的公路和铁路运输。这个省的首府圣地亚哥古巴德同其他大部分省份的联系，仍然处在断绝状态。
    游击队还对美国海军基地关塔那摩附近的撒马尼拉城发动进攻。哈瓦那同这个城市之间的电话交通已经中断。
    和这同时，起义者在中部的拉斯维利亚省和最西部的比那尔德里奥省展开了积极的游击战。最近有一支武装起义者在比那尔德里奥省的北海岸登陆以建立西部战线。
    武装起义者领袖卡斯特罗发表广播演说，表示决不同巴蒂斯塔独裁政权谈判停战或者作任何妥协。
