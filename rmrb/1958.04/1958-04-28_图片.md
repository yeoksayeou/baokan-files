    The Glass Ceramics Research Institute and the Glass Industry Design Institute used to squabble over trivial matters, but the situation has now changed dramatically. Recently, the Design Institute designed a gas station for the Research Institute, and Li Zeyan (right), an engineer from the Research Institute, went to the Civil Engineering Department of the Design Institute to check the design draft with the technicians in the department.
