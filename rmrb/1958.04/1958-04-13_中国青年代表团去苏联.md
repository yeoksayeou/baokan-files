Title: Chinese Youth Delegation to the Soviet Union
Author:
Date: 1958-04-13
Page: 2

Xinhua News Agency, April 12th: The Chinese Youth Delegation, consisting of five members and led by Hu Keshi, Secretary of the Secretariat of the Central Committee of the Communist Youth League of China, departed from Beijing by plane today for Moscow to attend the 13th Congress of the All-Union Leninist Young Communist League of the Soviet Union. Zhang Shuguang, Standing Committee Member of the Central Committee of the Communist Youth League, and others were at the airport to see them off. Wu Xueqian, a member of the delegation and Standing Committee Member of the Central Committee of the Communist Youth League, had arrived in Moscow earlier.

### 中国青年代表团去苏联

1958-04-13
第2版()
专栏：

    中国青年代表团去苏联
    新华社12日讯　应邀参加苏联列宁共产主义青年团第十三次代表大会的中国青年代表团一行五人，在团长、中国共产主义青年团中央委员会书记处书记胡克实率领下，今天乘飞机离开北京赴莫斯科。去机场欢送的有共青团中央常务委员张曙光等人。代表团团员、共青团中央常务委员吴学谦已先期到达莫斯科。
