Title: Romanian Government Delegation Arrives in Kunming; Yunnan Governor Yu Yichuan Hosts Banquet

Author: 
Date: 1958-04-01
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Kunming, March 31st. When Romanian People's Republic Council of Ministers Chairman Chivu Stoica arrived in Kunming from Yangon today, he said that the visit of the Romanian government delegation to China and the talks with Chinese government leaders would certainly help deepen the friendship between Romania and China and the multifaceted cooperation between the two countries, thereby deepening the unity of the socialist camp, which is a strong guarantee of world peace.

The Romanian government delegation arrived in Kunming by special plane at 2:00 pm today. The distinguished guests received a warm welcome at Kunming Airport from responsible persons from all aspects of the Yunnan Province Party, government, and military, as well as people of all ethnic groups in Kunming. Vice Foreign Minister Zeng Yongquan and Chinese Ambassador to Romania Ke Bonian specially rushed from Beijing to Kunming to greet them.

In his speech at the airport, Chairman Chivu Stoica warmly paid tribute to the fraternal Chinese people on behalf of the Romanian people, and he wished the Chinese people new achievements in the great work of building a great socialist industrial country.

Chairman Chivu Stoica, despite traveling a long distance to visit India, Vietnam, and Myanmar, was still in high spirits when he arrived in China's first city today. Facing the gentle breeze, Chairman Chivu Stoica excitedly said to the more than one thousand welcoming people standing at the airport: The warm welcome of the people of Kunming marks the close brotherly friendship formed between the people of Romania and China in the common goal of building socialism.

Chairman Chivu Stoica's enthusiastic speech was met with prolonged and warm applause from the welcoming crowd.

Yunnan Governor Yu Yichuan, on behalf of the Yunnan Provincial People's Committee and the people of all ethnic groups in the province, expressed a warm welcome to the distinguished guests. He said that the people of Yunnan, like the people of the whole country, cherish our fraternal friendship with the great Romanian people and are pleased with the growing development of this friendship.

Today, the national flags of China and Romania fluttered in the wind at Kunming Airport. When the special plane carrying the distinguished guests slowly landed, representatives of more than twenty ethnic groups in Kunming waved colorful bouquets and five-colored new silk, cheering in unison, and the entire airport was filled with applause and the sound of gongs and drums. At this time, men and women of the Axi, Dai, and Lahu ethnic groups sang and danced to welcome the distinguished guests.

At the beginning of the welcoming ceremony, the band played the national anthems of Romania and China. Chairman Chivu Stoica, accompanied by Governor Yu Yichuan, reviewed the guard of honor, and Young Pioneers presented flowers to the distinguished guests.

Finally, the distinguished guests, accompanied by Yu Yichuan and others, went around the airport to meet the welcoming crowd. Young people of various ethnic groups, dressed in festive costumes, constantly presented bouquets of bright red azaleas and camellias to the distinguished guests in the welcoming crowd.

Also present at the airport today to welcome the distinguished guests were Yunnan Vice Governor Liu Minghui, Kunming Mayor Pan Shuoduan, and Kunming garrison commander Lieutenant General Chen Kang, among others.

Myanmar Consul General in Kunming U Maung Maung Toe and Consul of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam in Kunming Le Tung Son also came to the airport to welcome them.

Xinhua News Agency, Kunming, March 31st. Yunnan Governor Yu Yichuan hosted a banquet this evening to welcome the Romanian People's Republic government delegation headed by Chairman Chivu Stoica.

At the banquet, Chairman Chivu Stoica and Vice Chairman Emil Bodnăraș, Foreign Minister Avram Bunaciu, and all members of the delegation repeatedly congratulated each other on friendship and health with their Chinese hosts.

Attending the banquet as escorts were Vice Foreign Minister Zeng Yongquan, Chinese Ambassador to Romania Ke Bonian, and responsible persons from all circles of the Yunnan Provincial Party, government, and military, among others.

Myanmar Consul General in Kunming U Maung Maung Toe and Consul of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam in Kunming Le Tung Son also attended the banquet.

After the banquet, the Yunnan Provincial People's Committee held an evening party to welcome the distinguished guests.

This afternoon, the distinguished guests, accompanied by Yu Yichuan and others, toured the Xishan Scenic Area in the suburbs of Kunming.

Xinhua News Agency, Yangon, March 31st. Romanian Council of Ministers Chairman Chivu Stoica left Yangon by plane this morning for a visit to China.

Chairman Chivu Stoica visited Myanmar for three days at the invitation of the government of the Union of Burma. Before leaving Yangon, Chairman Chivu Stoica and Burmese Prime Minister U Nu signed a joint statement.

Present at the airport today to see off Chairman Chivu Stoica were Burmese Prime Minister U Nu, Deputy Prime Minister U Kyaw Nyein, cabinet ministers, and about five hundred people from Yangon.

Chairman Chivu Stoica held a reception yesterday evening, which was attended by Prime Minister U Nu and other senior Burmese government officials. On the same day at noon, Prime Minister U Nu held a banquet for Chairman Chivu Stoica.

### 罗马尼亚政府代表团到达昆明  云南省省长于一川设宴招待

1958-04-01
第1版()
专栏：

    罗马尼亚政府代表团到达昆明
    云南省省长于一川设宴招待
    新华社昆明31日电　罗马尼亚人民共和国部长会议主席基伏·斯托伊卡今天由仰光到达昆明时说，罗马尼亚政府代表团在中国的访问和同中国政府领导人的会谈，必将有助于加深罗中两国的友谊和两国间多方面的合作，从而加深作为世界和平的有力保证的社会主义阵营的团结。
    罗马尼亚政府代表团在今天下午二时乘专机到达昆明。贵宾们在昆明机场受到了云南省党、政府、军队各方面负责人和昆明市各族人民的热烈欢迎。外交部副部长曾涌泉和中国驻罗马尼亚大使柯柏年特地由北京赶到昆明迎接。
    基伏·斯托伊卡主席在机场上的讲话中热情地代表罗马尼亚人民向兄弟的中国人民致敬，他并且祝中国人民在建设一个伟大社会主义工业国的巨大工作中获得新的成就。
    基伏·斯托伊卡主席虽然长途跋涉访问了印度、越南和缅甸三个国家，但他在今天到达中国的第一个城市时仍然精神焕发。基伏·斯托伊卡主席迎着拂面的和风向伫立在机场上的一千多名欢迎群众激动地说：昆明市人民对他们的盛情欢迎，标志着罗中两国人民在建设社会主义的共同目标中结成了紧密的兄弟友谊。
    基伏·斯托伊卡主席的热情讲话获得了欢迎群众长时间的热烈掌声。
    云南省省长于一川代表云南省人民委员会和全省各族人民向贵宾表示热烈欢迎，他说，云南人民和全国人民一样，十分珍视我们和伟大的罗马尼亚人民的兄弟般的友谊，并且为这种友谊的日益发展而感到高兴。
    今天，昆明机场迎风飘扬着中罗两国国旗。当贵宾们乘坐的专机徐徐着陆时，昆明市二十多个民族的代表们挥舞着鲜艳的花束和五色新绢齐声欢呼，整个机场上掌声和锣鼓声响成一片。这时，阿细族、傣族和拉祜族的男女载歌载舞欢迎贵宾。
    欢迎仪式开始时，乐队高奏罗中两国国歌，基伏·斯托伊卡主席由省长于一川陪同检阅了仪仗队，少先队员们向贵宾们献了鲜花。
    最后，贵宾们由于一川等陪同绕机场一周，同欢迎群众见面。欢迎队伍中不断有身穿节日服装的各族青年，把鲜红的杜鹃花和山茶花束献给贵宾。
    今天到机场欢迎贵宾的还有云南省副省长刘明辉、昆明市市长潘朔端和昆明驻军首长陈康中将等多人。
    缅甸驻昆明总领事吴茂茂铎、越南民主共和国驻昆明领事黎松山也到机场欢迎。
    新华社昆明31日电　云南省省长于一川今天晚间举行宴会，欢迎以基伏·斯托伊卡主席为首的罗马尼亚人民共和国政府代表团。
    宴会上，基伏·斯托伊卡主席和埃米尔·波德纳拉希副主席、外交部长阿夫拉姆·布纳丘以及代表团的全体人员，一再地同中国主人们互相祝贺友谊和健康。
    出席宴会作陪的，有我国外交部副部长曾涌泉，我国驻罗马尼亚大使柯柏年以及云南省党、政府和军队各界负责人多人。
    缅甸驻昆明总领事吴茂茂铎、越南民主共和国驻昆明领事黎松山也参加了宴会。
    宴会后，云南省人民委员会举行晚会欢迎贵宾。
    今天下午，贵宾们在于一川等陪同下，游览了昆明市郊西山风景区。
    新华社仰光31日电　罗马尼亚部长会议主席基伏·斯托伊卡在今天上午乘飞机离开仰光，前往中国进行访问。
    基伏·斯托伊卡主席应缅甸联邦政府的邀请，在缅甸进行了为时三天的访问。在离开仰光以前，基伏·斯托伊卡主席同缅甸总理吴努签署了一项联合声明。
    今天到机场欢送基伏·斯托伊卡主席的，有缅甸总理吴努、副总理吴觉迎、内阁部长们以及仰光的约五百名群众。
    基伏·斯托伊卡主席昨天晚间举行了招待会，吴努总理和缅甸政府的其他高级官员出席了招待会。同一天中午，吴努总理为基伏·斯托伊卡主席举行了宴会。
