Title: China and Hungary Sign This Year's Cultural Cooperation Agreement
Author:
Date: 1958-04-05
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, Budapest, April 3rd: The 1958 Executive Plan for the Sino-Hungarian Cultural Cooperation Agreement was signed here today. According to the agreement, a scientific cooperation agreement between the two countries will be signed this year.

### 中匈两国签订今年文化合作协定

1958-04-05
第5版()
专栏：

    中匈两国签订
    今年文化合作协定
    据新华社布达佩斯3日电　中匈文化合作协定1958年执行计划今天在此间签字。根据协定，今年将签定两国科学合作协定。
