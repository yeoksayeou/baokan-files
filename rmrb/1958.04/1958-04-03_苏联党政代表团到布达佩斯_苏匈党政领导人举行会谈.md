Title: Soviet Party and Government Delegation Arrives in Budapest, Soviet and Hungarian Party and Government Leaders Hold Talks
Author:
Date: 1958-04-03
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, Budapest, April 2nd: A Soviet Communist Party and Government delegation headed by Khrushchev arrived in Budapest by plane this morning.

Welcoming the delegation at the airport were Hungarian Socialist Workers' Party and state leaders Kádár, Münnich, Dobi, representatives from all walks of life, and diplomatic envoys from various countries stationed in Hungary.

Khrushchev spoke at the airport. He pointed out that in the year since the issuance of the joint declaration by the Soviet and Hungarian governments, the friendship between the two countries has been further strengthened, and the economic, political, and cultural ties between the two countries have greatly developed. Despite the attempts of enemies to undermine or even weaken the friendship between the peoples of the Soviet Union and Hungary, the fraternal cooperation between the two countries has been further strengthened.

Khrushchev emphasized that a close friendship has been established between the Soviet Union and Hungary, and among all socialist countries. They are united by common interests, unity of thought, and a shared ultimate goal in the struggle for socialism and communism. Khrushchev said that the unity of these countries is something no one can ever shake. As long as we are united, we will not fear any enemy conspiracy.

This afternoon, the Soviet Communist Party and Government delegation held talks with the Hungarian Socialist Workers' Party and the Revolutionary Worker-Peasant Government delegation.

Participating in the talks on the Soviet side were Khrushchev, Kozlov, Gromyko, Grishin, etc.; on the Hungarian side were Kádár, Münnich, Dobi, Marosán, etc.

### 苏联党政代表团到布达佩斯  苏匈党政领导人举行会谈

1958-04-03
第5版()
专栏：

    苏联党政代表团到布达佩斯
    苏匈党政领导人举行会谈
    据新华社布达佩斯2日电　以赫鲁晓夫为首的苏联共产党和政府代表团今天上午搭飞机到达布达佩斯。
    到机场欢迎代表团的有匈牙利社会主义工人党和国家领导人卡达尔、明尼赫、道比等，各界代表，以及各国驻匈牙利的外交使节。
    赫鲁晓夫在机场上讲了话。他指出，在苏匈两国政府联合宣言发表以来的一年中，两国的友谊得到了进一步的加强，两国的经济、政治和文化联系有了很大的发展。尽管敌人企图破坏或者那怕是削弱苏匈两国人民的友谊，但是，两国的兄弟合作是进一步加强了。
    赫鲁晓夫强调指出，苏联和匈牙利之间、一切社会主义国家之间已经建立起了亲密的朋友关系，它们是由于共同的利益、思想的一致和在争取社会主义和共产主义的斗争中抱有共同的最终目标而团结起来的。赫鲁晓夫说，我们这些国家之间的团结一致，是永远没有人动摇得了的。只要我们团结一致，我们就不怕敌人的任何阴谋。
    今天下午，苏联共产党和政府代表团同匈牙利社会主义工人党和工农革命政府代表团举行了会谈。
    参加会谈的，苏联方面是赫鲁晓夫，科兹洛夫、葛罗米柯、格里辛等；匈牙利方面是卡达尔、明尼赫、道比、马罗山等。
