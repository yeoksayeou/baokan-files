Title: Indonesian Foreign Minister Warns Chiang Gang Elements: No Sabotage in Indonesia! Syngman Rhee Clique Yells Support for Rebels

Author:

Date: 1958-04-03
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, Jakarta, 2nd: Antara News Agency reports that Indonesian Foreign Minister Subandrio warned Kuomintang elements from Taiwan here on the 1st that unless their groups in Indonesia cease participating in political activities, these groups will be banned.

When asked if foreign political parties are allowed to exist in Indonesia, Subandrio said, "They are indeed not allowed." When asked if the government would ban the existence of Kuomintang groups from Taiwan in Indonesia, he said that if the activities of these groups are not limited to purely social affairs and they participate in politics, they will certainly be banned.

Xinhua News Agency, Pyongyang, 1st: Seoul dispatch: Oh Jae-kyung, spokesman for the Syngman Rhee clique and Director of the South Korean Public Information Office, openly advocated in a statement today giving the Indonesian rebel group "full support."

Syngman Rhee, when meeting with American reporter Lucas, also stated that he was willing to send South Korean troops to Indonesia to help the rebel group oppose the Indonesian government.

Xinhua News Agency, Jakarta, 2nd: After last night's Indonesian cabinet meeting, reporters asked the Indonesian Minister of Information for his opinion on Syngman Rhee's clamor to send troops to aid the Sumatran rebels. Sudibjo replied, "The clamoring of the Syngman Rhee ilk is not surprising."

### 印度尼西亚外长警告蒋帮分子  不准在印度尼西亚搞鬼  李承晚集团叫嚣也要支持叛军

1958-04-03
第6版()
专栏：

    印度尼西亚外长警告蒋帮分子
    不准在印度尼西亚搞鬼
    李承晚集团叫嚣也要支持叛军
    据新华社雅加达2日电　据安塔拉通讯社报道，印度尼西亚外交部长苏班德里约1日在这里警告台湾国民党分子说，除非他们在印度尼西亚的团体不再参加政治活动，否则这些团体将予禁止。
    有人问苏班德里约，外国政党是否允许在印度尼西亚存在，他说：“的确是不允许的。”有人问，政府是否将禁止在印度尼西亚的台湾国民党团体存在，他说，如果这些团体的活动不限于纯粹的社会事务，而且参预政治的话，它们当然要受到禁止。
    据新华社平壤1日电　汉城消息：李承晚集团的发言人、南朝鲜公报室室长吴在璟在今天发表的谈话中，公然主张给予印度尼西亚叛乱集团以“充分的支持”。
    李承晚在接见美国记者卢卡斯时也曾经表示，他愿意派遣南朝鲜军队前往印度尼西亚，帮助叛乱集团反对印度尼西亚政府。
    据新华社雅加达2日电　在昨晚的印度尼西亚内阁会议以后，记者们请印度尼西亚政府情报部长就李承晚叫嚣愿派遣军队援助苏门答腊叛军一事发表意见，苏迪比约答复说：“李承晚之流的叫嚣是不足为奇的”。
