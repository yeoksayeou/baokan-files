Which Factory Produces the Most Durable Sneakers?

    Rubber factories test and compare the quality of their rubber shoes. The Ministry of Light Industry recently compared the wearing life of sneakers from sixteen rubber factories, and Shanghai Da Zhonghua Rubber Factory came out on top. The sneakers produced by this factory lasted an average of 170.7 days when worn by students. Second place went to Shanghai Jin Gang Rubber Factory, with an average of 165.6 days. Sneakers from Shanghai Zhengtai Rubber Factory lasted an average of 161.4 days, but this factory used 30 more jin of rubber per thousand pairs of shoes than Shanghai Jin Gang Rubber Factory.

    The shortest wearing time was for shoes from Tianjin Dalu Rubber Factory and Tianjin Yu Tai Rubber Factory, averaging only 140-plus days.

### 哪个厂生产的球鞋最耐穿？

1958-04-04
第3版()
专栏：比一比

    哪个厂生产的球鞋最耐穿？
    橡胶厂生产的胶鞋，产品质量好坏，都做试穿比较。轻工业不久前把十六个橡胶厂球鞋穿用寿命作了比较，还是上海大中华橡胶厂占先。这个厂生产的球鞋经过学生试穿，平均穿一百七十点七天。其次是上海金钢橡胶厂，平均穿一百六十五点六天。上海正泰橡胶厂的球鞋平均穿一百六十一点四天，而这个厂每生产一千双胶鞋的用胶量却比上海金钢橡胶厂还多三十斤。
    穿着天数最少的是天津的大陆橡胶厂和天津裕太橡胶厂，平均只能穿一百四十几天。
