Title: Everyone Saves, Every Household Invests (Picture)
Author: Yu Chengjian
Date: 1958-04-30
Page: 3

Everyone Saves, Every Household Invests
Party members and cadres in Xinqiao Township, Tianmen County, Hubei Province, have taken the lead in saving and investing in the establishment of fertilizer plants. This is at the township's Party member meeting, where Ma Nengyin (right), the Party branch secretary of Tuanjie Cooperative, representing all the cooperative's cadres, presents their past savings to invest in the fertilizer plant. Xinhua News Agency reporter Yu Chengjian.
