Title: Brief News

Author:

Date: 1958-04-28
Page: 5

    △The Central Committee of the Mongolian People's Revolutionary Party and the Council of Ministers decided to donate 15,000 draught horses to China to support the great leap forward in China's agricultural production.
    △The Coking Chemical Production Group of the Standing Committee on Ferrous Metallurgy of the Council for Mutual Economic Assistance of the Soviet Union and the Eastern European People's Democratic Countries concluded its five-day meeting in Kharkov on the 25th.
    △The Soviet government delegation, invited by the Argentine government to attend the inauguration ceremony of the new Argentine President Frondizi, arrived in Buenos Aires on the 25th.
    △Poland and Japan concluded a five-year trade treaty and a one-year trade agreement in Tokyo.

### 简讯

1958-04-28
第5版()
专栏：简讯

    △蒙古人民革命党中央和部长会议决定赠送给中国一万五千匹耕马，支援中国农业生产的大跃进。
    △苏联和东欧人民民主国家八国经济互助委员会所属的黑色金属常设委员会焦炭化学生产小组，在哈尔科夫举行五天会议后，已经在25日闭幕。
    △应阿根廷政府的邀请前来参加阿根廷新总统弗朗迪西就职典礼的苏联政府代表团，25日到达布宜诺斯艾利斯。
    △波兰同日本在东京缔结了一个为期五年的通商条约和一个为期一年的贸易协定。
