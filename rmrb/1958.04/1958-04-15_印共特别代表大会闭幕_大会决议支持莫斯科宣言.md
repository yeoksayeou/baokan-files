Title: CPI Special Congress Concludes; Resolution Supports Moscow Declaration
Author:
Date: 1958-04-15
Page: 6

    CPI Special Congress Concludes
    Congress Resolution Supports Moscow Declaration
    Xinhua News Agency, New Delhi, April 14th: The Indian Communist Party's Special Congress concluded on the evening of April 13th in Amritsar after electing 101 members to the National Committee. All thirty-nine members of the previous Central Committee were elected to the National Committee. Before closing, the Special Congress also elected a Supervisory Committee chaired by S.V. Ghate.
    The National Committee elected a Central Executive Committee consisting of twenty-five members. The following were elected as Secretaries of the Secretariat: Ajoy Ghosh, Sohan Singh Joshi, P.T. Ranadive, Basava Punnaiah, S.A. Dange, A.K. Gopalan, Bhupesh Gupta, Z.A. Ahmad. One more secretary remains to be elected.
    On the closing day of the Special Congress, a grand parade was held in Amritsar, attended by 25,000 residents, including 1,000 women, as well as the delegates attending the Special Congress. April 13th is the Indian New Year, and countless residents from nearby gathered in Amritsar on this day to celebrate the festival in the traditional way. Tens of thousands of people crowded the roadsides along the parade route, cheering the CPI leaders participating in the parade.
    The Indian Communist Party's Special Congress concluded with a grand rally attended by more than 100,000 people. Indian Communist Party leaders elaborated on the resolutions passed by the Special Congress at the rally.
    Xinhua News Agency, New Delhi, April 14th: Late news from Amritsar: The Indian Communist Party's Special Congress passed a resolution supporting the "Peace Manifesto" and the "Declaration of the Conference of Representatives of Communist and Workers' Parties of Socialist Countries."
    The resolution stated that the Congress pledged to do its utmost to mobilize the people to strive for the maintenance of world peace and to transform the noble goals in the Peace Manifesto into the reality of life.
    The resolution stated: The Declaration of the Conference of Representatives of Communist and Workers' Parties of Socialist Countries "has opened up a new stage in the solidarity of the international workers' movement and has given this movement invaluable weapons to move forward at a greater pace."
    The resolution concluded by saying that the Special Congress pledged to demonstrate to the people that communism is a noble cause and to play its due role in serving the people of India and realizing the goals of our present era—socialism and communism.

### 印共特别代表大会闭幕  大会决议支持莫斯科宣言

1958-04-15
第6版()
专栏：

    印共特别代表大会闭幕
    大会决议支持莫斯科宣言
    据新华社新德里14日电　印度共产党特别代表大会13日晚上在阿姆利则选出了一百零一名全国委员会委员以后宣告闭幕。上届中央委员会的三十九位委员全部被选入全国委员会。特别代表大会在闭幕前，还选出了由斯·维·加特担任主席的监察委员会。
    全国委员会选出了由二十五人组成的中央执行委员会。当选为书记处书记的有：阿约艾·高士、索汉·辛格、乔希、布·特·兰那地夫、巴萨瓦·彭尼亚、什·阿·丹吉、阿·克·高普兰、布佩希·古普塔、兹·阿·艾哈迈德。还有一位书记尚待选出。
    在特别代表大会闭幕这一天，阿姆利则城举行了盛大游行，参加游行的有包括一千名妇女在内的二万五千名居民，以及出席特别代表大会的代表们。4月13日是印度历新年，附近无数居民在这一天云集阿姆利则用传统的方式庆祝这个节日。成千上万的人拥挤在游行队伍通过的道路两旁，向参加游行的印共领袖们欢呼。
    印度共产党这次特别代表大会最后以举行一个有十万余人参加的盛大集会结束。印度共产党领导人在大会上阐述了这次特别代表大会所通过的决议。
    新华社新德里14日电　阿姆利则迟到消息：印度共产党特别代表大会通过了支持“和平宣言”和“社会主义国家共产党和工人党代表会议宣言”的决议。
    决议说，代表大会保证竭尽全力动员人民为维护世界和平而努力，并且把和平宣言中的高尚目标变为生活的现实。
    决议说：社会主义国家共产党和工人党代表会议宣言“已经在国际工人运动的团结方面开辟了一个新阶段，并且给予了这个运动以无法估价的武器使能以更大的步伐向前迈进”。
    决议最后说，特别代表大会保证要向人民证明共产主义是高尚的事业，并且要在为印度人民服务和实现我们现时代的目标——社会主义和共产主义方面发挥应有的作用。
