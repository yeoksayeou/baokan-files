Title: Academician Lavrentyev, Vice-President of the Soviet Academy of Sciences, Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1958-04-08
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, April 7th. Academician M. A. Lavrentyev, Vice-President of the Soviet Academy of Sciences, Director of the Siberian Branch, and a famous Soviet mathematician and mechanic, and his wife, arrived in Beijing by plane today at the invitation of Guo Moruo, President of the Chinese Academy of Sciences, for a visit to China.

Among those who went to the airport to welcome them were Wu Youxun, Vice-President of the Chinese Academy of Sciences, and his wife, as well as many other prominent scientists.

### 苏科学院副院长拉夫连捷夫院士到京

1958-04-08
第4版()
专栏：

    苏科学院副院长拉夫连捷夫院士到京
    新华社7日讯　苏联科学院副院长、西伯利亚总分院院长、著名的苏联数学、力学家米·阿·拉夫连捷夫院士及夫人应中国科学院院长郭沫若的邀请来我国参观访问，已经在今天乘飞机到达北京。
    前往机场欢迎的有中国科学院副院长吴有训和夫人以及其他著名科学家等多人。
