Title: Chairman Mao Meets with Wuhan Science and Technology Workers
Author:
Date: 1958-04-07
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Wuhan, 6th — Chairman Mao Zedong met with science and technology workers in Wuhan this afternoon.

　　This meeting took place during a 5,300-person Great Leap Forward rally held by the scientific community in the Wuhan area. In the center of the venue, Chairman Mao Zedong frequently waved to the attendees. Chairman Mao Zedong shook hands with each member of the presidium of the congress, and scientists reported to him their determination to undergo ideological transformation and strive for a leap forward in scientific endeavors.

　　Today, accompanying the meeting were Li Xiannian, a member of the Political Bureau of the CPC Central Committee, Ke Qingshi, First Secretary of the Shanghai Municipal Committee of the CPC, Wang Renzhong, First Secretary of the Hubei Provincial Committee of the CPC, and Song Kanfu, First Secretary of the Wuhan Municipal Committee of the CPC, among others. ### 毛主席会见武汉科学技术界人士

1958-04-07
第1版()
专栏：

    毛主席会见武汉科学技术界人士
    新华社武汉6日电　毛泽东主席6日下午会见了武汉科学技术工作者。
    这次会见是在武汉地区科学界举行的五千三百人跃进誓师大会中间进行的。在会场中央，毛泽东主席向到会人员频频招手致意。毛泽东主席和大会主席团成员一一握手相见，科学家向他汇报了进行思想改造和争取科学事业跃进的决心。
    今天陪同会见的有中共中央政治局委员李先念、中共上海市委第一书记柯庆施、中共湖北省委第一书记王任重、中共武汉市委第一书记宋侃夫等。（附图片）
