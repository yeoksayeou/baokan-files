Title: Weightlifting Middleweight Competition: Qiu Rongshan Breaks National Record
Author:
Date: 1958-04-10
Page: 7

Xinhua News Agency, Chongqing, April 8th: Qiu Rongshan, a nineteen-year-old weightlifter from the People's Liberation Army, broke the national record in the middleweight division of the National 25 Units Weightlifting Championships tonight at 9:23 PM with a two-hand press of 118 kilograms, surpassing the previous record of 117.5 kilograms.

### 举重中量级比赛  丘荣山破全国纪录

1958-04-10
第7版()
专栏：

    举重中量级比赛
    丘荣山破全国纪录
    据新华社重庆8日电　十九岁的解放军青年举重运动员丘荣山，今晚二十一时二十三分在全国二十五单位举重锦标赛中量级比赛中，以双手推举一百一十八公斤的成绩打破了一百一十七点五公斤的全国纪录。
