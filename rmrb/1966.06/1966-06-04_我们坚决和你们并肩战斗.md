Title: We resolutely fight shoulder to shoulder with you
Author: 
Date: 1966-06-04
Edition: 3

    Comrades Nie Yuanzi and others: You bravely broke through the various controls of the revisionist elements and posted a revolutionary big-character poster, exposing a major conspiracy of the "Three-Family Village" black gang. With high revolutionary spirit, you ignited the revolutionary fire at Peking University. We firmly support your revolutionary actions of daring to struggle, fearing nothing, and being invincible. We vow to fight shoulder to shoulder with you, wielding the golden cudgel of Mao Zedong Thought to smash all monsters and demons, resolutely, thoroughly, cleanly, and completely annihilate all Khrushchev-style counter-revolutionary revisionist elements, and carry the socialist cultural revolution through to the end.
          All Students of the Philosophy Department
          Fudan University, Shanghai

**Named Entities**

聂元梓	Nie Yuanzi

社会主义文化大革命	Shehuizhuyi wenhua da geming (Socialist Cultural Revolution)

### 我们坚决和你们并肩战斗

1966-06-04
第3版()
专栏：

    我们坚决和你们并肩战斗
    聂元梓等同志：你们勇敢地冲破修正主义分子的种种控制，贴出了一张革命的大字报，揭穿了“三家村”黑帮分子的一个大阴谋，以高昂的革命精神，在北京大学点燃了革命的烈火。我们坚决支持你们敢于斗争无所畏惧、所向披靡的革命行动。我们誓与你们并肩战斗，挥起毛泽东思想的千钧棒，砸碎一切牛鬼蛇神，坚决彻底干净全部地歼灭一切赫鲁晓夫式反革命修正主义分子，把社会主义文化大革命进行到底。
          上海复旦大学
          哲学系全体学生
