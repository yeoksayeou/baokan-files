Title: Bucharest Hosts Song and Dance Soiree for Chinese Party and Government Delegation; Our Party and Government Delegation Visits "Fundulea" Institute of Grain and Technical Crop Science
Author:
Date: 1966-06-20
Edition: 6

    Xinhua News Agency, Bucharest, June 19th. The Romanian State Committee for Culture and Art held a grand song and dance performance at the Bucharest Opera and Ballet Theatre on the evening of the 18th to entertain the Chinese Party and Government Delegation headed by Comrade Zhou Enlai, Vice Chairman of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of China and Premier of the State Council.

    When Comrade Zhou Enlai entered the theater in the company of Comrade Nicolae Ceaușescu, General Secretary of the Romanian Communist Party, Comrade Chivu Stoica, Member of the Executive Committee of the Romanian Communist Party Central Committee, Member of the Standing Presidium and Chairman of the State Council, and Comrade Ion Gheorghe Maurer, Member of the Executive Committee of the Romanian Communist Party Central Committee, Member of the Standing Presidium and Chairman of the Council of Ministers of Romania, the entire audience stood up and cheered for a long time.

    Zhao Yimin, Qiao Guanhua, and Zeng Yongquan, members of the Chinese Party and Government Delegation, also attended the song and dance soiree.

    The magnificent revolutionary songs and rich and colorful folk dances performed by Romanian artists were warmly welcomed. When they sang Chinese songs such as "The Working Class Has Strength" in Chinese and performed the Chinese waist drum dance, the audience applauded enthusiastically. The theater was filled with a warm atmosphere of unity and friendship between the Chinese and Romanian peoples.

    After the performance, the Chinese Party and Government Delegation presented flower baskets to the Romanian artists. At this time, the entire audience stood up and applauded thunderously for a long time. When Comrade Zhou Enlai walked out of the theater accompanied by Comrades Nicolae Ceaușescu, Chivu Stoica, and Ion Gheorghe Maurer, the audience standing on both sides of the theater corridor chanted rhythmically and enthusiastically.

    Also accompanying Comrade Zhou Enlai and other Chinese guests to watch the performance were Comrades Gheorghe Apostol, Alexandru Bârlădeanu, and Emil Bodnăraș, Members of the Executive Committee of the Romanian Communist Party Central Committee, First Deputy Chairmen of the Council of Ministers of Romania, as well as Comrade Alexandru Drăghici, Member of the Executive Committee of the Romanian Communist Party Central Committee, Member of the Standing Presidium and Central Secretary. Diplomatic officials from the Chinese Embassy in Romania and diplomatic envoys from various countries to Romania also watched the performance.

    Xinhua News Agency, Bucharest, June 19th. The Chinese Party and Government Delegation led by Comrade Zhou Enlai, Vice Chairman of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of China and Premier of the State Council, visited the Romanian Crop Research and Experiment Center – the "Fundulea" Institute of Grain and Technical Crop Science on the afternoon of the 18th.

    Accompanying the Chinese Party and Government Delegation on the visit was Comrade Emil Bodnăraș, Member of the Executive Committee of the Romanian Communist Party Central Committee, First Deputy Chairman of the Council of Ministers of Romania, and others.

    The institute is located about forty kilometers east of Bucharest, with more than two thousand scientific research workers, technicians, and agricultural workers, and owns about six thousand hectares of land. Since its establishment in 1952, it has made contributions to improving crop varieties, training technical personnel, and promoting the development of agricultural production in Romania.

    At 6:00 p.m., when the Chinese guests arrived at the institute, Comrade Nicolae Giosan, Member of the Romanian Communist Party Central Committee and Chairman of the Supreme Agricultural Council, Comrade Gheorghe Nicolae, Member of the Romanian Communist Party Central Committee and First Secretary of the Bucharest State Committee of the Romanian Communist Party, Comrade Vasile Matei, Alternate Member of the Romanian Communist Party Central Committee and Chairman of the Executive Committee of the Bucharest State People's Council, and Mr. Titus Mureșan, Director of the "Fundulea" Institute of Grain and Technical Crop Science, greeted the guests by the experimental fields, went forward and warmly shook hands with Comrade Zhou Enlai and other Chinese guests.

    Accompanied by Director Titus Mureșan, the distinguished Chinese guests visited the experimental fields of crops such as sugar beets, wheat, and corn before arriving at the institute's office building. Director Titus Mureșan then warmly introduced the work of the institute to the Chinese guests.

    Director Titus Mureșan presented Comrade Zhou Enlai with improved crop varieties and photo collections cultivated by the institute. Comrade Zhou Enlai also presented gifts to the institute.

    Before the end of the visit, Comrade Zhou Enlai, at the invitation of the hosts, wrote an inscription in the institute's guestbook, wishing all the staff of the institute to continue to make new contributions in promoting the development of socialist agriculture in Romania.

    During Comrade Zhou Enlai's visit to the institute, he was warmly welcomed by the staff wherever he went.

    The men, women, young, and old in the towns and villages along the way of Comrade Zhou Enlai and Comrade Emil Bodnăraș's motorcade were dressed in festive attire, waving Chinese and Romanian national flags and flowers, lining the roadside to warmly salute the Chinese guests. The members of the agricultural cooperative in the town of Brănești also built welcome arches on the road with green branches and freshly picked flowers, inscribed with "Long Live Romanian-Chinese Friendship!" in both Romanian and Chinese. (Photo attached)

    On the afternoon of June 18th, the Chinese Party and Government Delegation led by Comrade Zhou Enlai visited the Romanian Crop Research and Experiment Center – the "Fundulea" Institute of Grain and Technical Crop Science. Xinhua News Agency reporter Du Xiuxian (faxed photo)

### 布加勒斯特举行歌舞晚会招待中国党政代表团  我党政代表团参观“丰杜拉”粮食和技术作物科学研究所

1966-06-20
第6版()
专栏：

    布加勒斯特举行歌舞晚会招待中国党政代表团
    我党政代表团参观“丰杜拉”粮食和技术作物科学研究所
    新华社布加勒斯特十九日电　罗马尼亚国家文化艺术委员会十八日晚在布加勒斯特歌剧和芭蕾舞剧院举行盛大歌舞演出，招待以中共中央副主席、国务院总理周恩来同志为首的中国党政代表团。
    当周恩来同志在罗共中央总书记尼·齐奥塞斯库、罗共中央执委、中央常设主席团委员、国务委员会主席基伏·斯托伊卡和罗共中央执委、中央常设主席团委员、罗马尼亚部长会议主席扬·格·毛雷尔等同志陪同下进入剧场时，全场起立，长时间欢呼。
    中国党政代表团团员赵毅敏、乔冠华、曾涌泉也出席了歌舞晚会。
    罗马尼亚艺术家表演的雄壮的革命歌曲和丰富多采的民间舞蹈受到了热烈欢迎。当他们用中国话演唱《咱们工人有力量》等中国歌曲和跳中国腰鼓舞时，全场热烈鼓掌。剧院里充满着中罗两国人民团结友好的热烈气氛。
    演出结束后，中国党政代表团向罗马尼亚艺术家赠送了花篮。这时全场起立，响起雷鸣般的长时间的掌声。当周恩来同志在尼·齐奥塞斯库、基伏·斯托伊卡和扬·格·毛雷尔等同志陪同下走出剧院时，站在剧院走廊两旁的观众有节奏地热烈欢呼。
    陪同周恩来同志和其他中国客人观看演出的还有罗共中央执委、中央常设主席团委员、罗马尼亚部长会议第一副主席：格·阿波斯托尔，亚·伯尔拉德亚努和埃·波德纳拉希，以及罗共中央执委、中央常设主席团委员、中央书记亚·德勒吉奇等。观看演出的还有中国驻罗马尼亚大使馆外交官员和各国驻罗马尼亚外交使节。
    新华社布加勒斯特十九日电　由中国共产党中央委员会副主席、国务院总理周恩来同志率领的中国党政代表团十八日下午参观了罗马尼亚农作物研究和实验中心——“丰杜拉”粮食和技术作物科学研究所。
    陪同中国党政代表团前往参观的有罗马尼亚共产党中央执行委员会委员、中央常设主席团委员、罗马尼亚部长会议第一副主席埃·波德纳拉希等。
    这个研究所位于布加勒斯特以东约四十公里，共有二千多名科学研究工作人员、技术人员和农业工人，拥有约六千公顷土地。它从一九五二年成立以来，在改良农作物品种，培养技术人员，促进罗马尼亚农业生产发展方面作出了贡献。
    下午六点，当中国客人抵达研究所时，在试验田田边迎候客人的罗共中央委员、最高农业委员会主席尼·季奥桑，罗共中央委员、罗共布加勒斯特州委员会第一书记格·尼古拉，罗共中央候补委员、布加勒斯特州人民会议执行委员会主席瓦·马特耶斯库，“丰杜拉”粮食和技术作物科学研究所所长蒂·穆列森等迎上前去，同周恩来同志和其他中国客人热情握手。
    中国贵宾在蒂·穆列森所长陪同下，参观了甜菜、小麦、玉米等农作物的试验田之后，来到研究所办公大楼。接着，蒂·穆列森所长向中国客人热情地介绍了研究所的工作情况。
    蒂·穆列森所长向周恩来同志赠送了研究所培育出来的农作物良种和照片集。周恩来同志也向研究所赠送了礼品。
    参观结束前，周恩来同志应主人的邀请在研究所的贵宾留言簿上题词，祝研究所的全体职工在促进罗马尼亚的社会主义农业的发展方面，不断作出新的贡献。
    周恩来同志在研究所参观的过程中，所到之处都受到职工们的热烈欢迎。
    周恩来和埃·波德纳拉希等同志所乘坐的汽车在前往研究所的途中，所经过的村镇的男女老少，都穿着节日盛装，挥动着中罗两国国旗和鲜花，排列在公路两旁热情地向中国客人致意。布勒奈什蒂镇的农业合作社社员们还在公路上用碧绿的树枝和刚摘下的鲜花搭了欢迎牌坊，上面用罗中两国文字写着：“罗中友谊万岁！”（附图片）
    六月十八日下午，周恩来同志率领的中国党政代表团，参观了罗马尼亚农作物研究和实验中心——“丰杜拉”粮食和技术作物科学研究所。　新华社记者　杜修贤摄（传真照片）
