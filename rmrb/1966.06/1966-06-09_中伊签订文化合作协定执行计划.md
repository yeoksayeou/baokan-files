Title: China and Iraq Sign Cultural Cooperation Agreement Implementation Plan
Author:
Date: 1966-06-09
Edition: 5

    Xinhua News Agency, Baghdad – The 1966-1967 annual implementation plan of the China-Iraq Cultural Cooperation Agreement and the China-Iraq Radio and Television Cooperation Protocol were signed on the morning of the 4th in Baghdad, the capital of Iraq.
    The implementation plan was signed by Li Chang, head of the Chinese government's cultural friendship delegation and Vice-Minister of the Commission for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries, and Iraqi Minister of Education Khidr Abdul Ghafur. The Radio and Television Cooperation Protocol was signed by Li Chang and Shakir Ahmed Shaleel, Undersecretary of the Iraqi Ministry of Culture and National Guidance.

### 中伊签订文化合作协定执行计划

1966-06-09
第5版()
专栏：

    中伊签订文化合作协定执行计划
    新华社巴格达电　中国和伊拉克文化合作协定一九六六年——一九六七年度执行计划和中国—伊拉克广播电视合作议定书四日上午在伊拉克首都巴格达签字。
    执行计划是由中国政府文化友好代表团团长、对外文委副主任李昌和伊拉克教育部长希德尔·阿卜杜勒·加福尔签署的。广播电视合作议定书是由李昌和伊拉克文化和国家指导部次长沙克尔·艾哈迈德·沙勒尔签署的。
