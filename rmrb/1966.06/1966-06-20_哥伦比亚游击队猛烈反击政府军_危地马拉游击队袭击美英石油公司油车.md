Title: Colombian Guerrillas Fiercely Counterattack Government Troops; Guatemalan Guerrillas Attack US-British Oil Company Tankers
Author:
Date: 1966-06-20
Edition: 6

    Xinhua News Agency, Bogota dispatch: Colombian guerrillas have recently continued to fight government troops, killing and wounding a number of them.
    The Colombian government troops' recent offensive against the El Pato guerrilla area has met with fierce counterattacks from the guerrillas. According to news released by the government troops, in a battle near the city of Neiva, one government soldier was killed and six were wounded. In another battle that occurred in an area under the jurisdiction of the city of Colombia, three government soldiers were wounded, with no guerrilla casualties.
    In southern Tolima province, guerrillas are also constantly attacking government troops conducting "mopping-up" operations in the area.
    Xinhua News Agency, Guatemala City dispatch: A Guatemalan anti-government guerrilla force, the "Edgar Ibarra Guerrilla Front," recently attacked several oil tankers belonging to the American Esso Oil Company and the British Shell Oil Company, causing $50,000 in losses to these two imperialist enterprises.
    It is reported that guerrillas controlled a section of the highway from the capital, Guatemala City, to Puerto Barrios on the Atlantic, near Río Hondo in the department of Zacapa. They used machine guns to strafe the oil tankers traveling on this highway, causing all the gasoline to leak out. Due to the guerrillas' action, traffic on this major highway in Guatemala was completely cut off. (Photo attached)
    Angolan patriotic armed forces are constantly growing and developing in the fight against Portuguese colonialists. The picture shows the heroic fighters of the Angolan patriotic armed forces. Xinhua News Agency reporter Gao Liang.

### 哥伦比亚游击队猛烈反击政府军  危地马拉游击队袭击美英石油公司油车

1966-06-20
第6版()
专栏：

    哥伦比亚游击队猛烈反击政府军
    危地马拉游击队袭击美英石油公司油车
    新华社讯　波哥大消息：哥伦比亚游击队最近继续同政府军进行战斗，打死打伤政府军多人。
    哥伦比亚政府军最近对埃尔帕托游击地区发动的进攻，遭到了游击队的猛烈反击。据政府军方面公布的消息说，在内瓦城附近的一次战斗中，政府军被打死一人，打伤六人。另一次在哥伦比亚市管辖的地区内发生的一次战斗中，政府军又被打伤三人，游击队无一伤亡。
    在托利马省南部，游击队也不断袭击正在这个地区内进行“清剿”活动的政府军。
    据新华社讯　危地马拉城消息：危地马拉的一支反政府游击队“埃德加尔·伊瓦拉游击战线”不久以前袭击了美国埃索石油公司和英国壳牌石油公司的几辆运油卡车，给这两家帝国主义企业造成了五万美元的损失。
    据报道，游击队员们在萨卡帕省里约翁多市附近控制了从首都危地马拉城通向大西洋巴里奥斯港的一段公路，他们用机关枪向行驶在这条公路上的运油卡车扫射，车上的汽油全部漏失。由于游击队员的这一行动，危地马拉的这条主要的公路全线交通中断。（附图片）
