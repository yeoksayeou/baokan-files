Title: Moroccan Audience Warmly Welcomes Chinese Lantern, New Year Picture, and Paper-Cut Exhibition; Chairman Mao Leads Chinese People to Achieve Great Successes
Author:
Date: 1966-06-12
Edition: 4

    Xinhua News Agency, Rabat, 9th dispatch: A Chinese lantern, New Year picture, and paper-cut exhibition opened in Rabat, the capital of Morocco, on the 7th.
    Moroccan Deputy Secretary of State for Education Nasser al-Fassi, as well as government officials, figures from the cultural, artistic, and journalistic circles, and foreign envoys to Morocco, totaling over two hundred people, attended the opening ceremony.
    The exhibition was warmly welcomed by the audience.
    They enthusiastically praised the great achievements made by the Chinese people in socialist construction under the leadership of Chairman Mao. They wrote in the guestbook: "I am deeply moved by the political, economic, and social progress made by the great Chinese people under the leadership of Chairman Mao," "The exhibition shows the progress of China and the happy life of the Chinese people," "This exhibition reflects the face of the great New China, reflecting the superb talents and unremitting labor of the Chinese people, which is amazing and admirable."

### 摩洛哥观众热烈欢迎我国灯彩年画剪纸展览  毛主席领导中国人民取得重大成就

1966-06-12
第4版()
专栏：

    摩洛哥观众热烈欢迎我国灯彩年画剪纸展览
    毛主席领导中国人民取得重大成就
    新华社九日讯　拉巴特消息：中国灯彩、年画、剪纸展览七日在摩洛哥首都拉巴特开幕。
    摩洛哥教育副国务大臣纳赛尔·法西以及政府官员、文化、艺术、新闻界人士和各国驻摩洛哥使节等二百多人参加了开幕式。
    展览受到了观众们的热烈欢迎。
    他们热情赞扬中国人民在毛主席的领导下在社会主义建设中所取得的重大成就。他们在留言簿上写道：“伟大的中国人民在毛主席的领导下所取得的政治、经济和社会方面的进步使我深受感动”，“从展览中可以看出中国的进步与中国人民的幸福生活”，“这个展览反映了伟大的新中国的面貌，反映了中国人民的高超的才能与坚持不懈的劳动，令人惊奇和钦佩”。
