Author: 
Date: 1966-06-09

    Mao Zedong Quotation
    Our educational policy should enable those who receive education to develop in moral, intellectual and physical aspects, and become workers with socialist consciousness and culture.
                      "On the Correct Handling of Contradictions Among the People"
